incommunicability:/ɪnkəˌmjuːnɪkəˈbɪləti/
n.|不可沟通性，无法表达|The state of being unable to communicate effectively; the quality of being difficult or impossible to understand or share with others.|His emotional incommunicability made it hard for him to maintain close relationships.  他情感上的不可沟通性使他难以维持亲密关系。|incommunicableness, uncommunicativeness|communicability, expressiveness|noun


incommunicable:/ɪnkəˈmjuːnɪkəbl/
adj.|无法表达的，不可沟通的|Not able to be communicated to others; too great, deep, or subtle to be expressed.|Her feelings of grief were so intense as to be almost incommunicable. 她悲伤的情感如此强烈，几乎无法表达。|ineffable, inexpressible, unutterable|communicable, expressible|adjective
