multivalve:/ˌmʌltiˈvælv/ | adj. n. |多瓣的；多阀门的；多壳瓣的；多瓣动物|Having or involving multiple valves.  A mollusk having more than two shell valves.|
The multivalve engine offers increased performance.  这款多气门发动机提高了性能。|
The chiton is a multivalve mollusk.  石鳖是一种多壳瓣的软体动物。|
近义词:polyvalve|反义词:bivalve|词性:adjective, noun


Etymology:
From multi- + valve.  Multi- is a combining form meaning "many," "multiple," from Latin multus "much, many." Valve comes from Latin valva "leaf of a folding door," also used of other swinging or sliding parts.
