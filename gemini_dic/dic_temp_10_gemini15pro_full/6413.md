amphitheater:/ˈæmfɪˌθiːətər/ (US) /ˈæmfɪˌθɪətə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 圆形剧场；露天剧场 | An oval or circular building with rising tiers of seats around an open space or arena, used for performances and public spectacles. | The concert was held in a large amphitheater. 这场音乐会是在一个大型圆形剧场举行的。|  The gladiators fought in the amphitheater.  角斗士在圆形剧场里搏斗。 | synonyms: arena, stadium, coliseum | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps small enclosed theater) | noun
