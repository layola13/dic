nonflyable:/ˌnɒnˈflaɪəbəl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈflaɪəbl/ (UK)
adjective
不能飞的；不宜飞行的
Not suitable or able to fly.
The plane was deemed nonflyable due to mechanical issues.  这架飞机由于机械问题被认为无法飞行。
The weather conditions made the helicopter nonflyable.  天气状况使直升机无法飞行。
近义词: unflyable, grounded
反义词: flyable, airworthy
词性: adjective
