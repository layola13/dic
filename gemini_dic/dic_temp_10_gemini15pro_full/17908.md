beerier:/ˈbɪəriər/| adj.|更像啤酒的；啤酒味更浓的|Having a stronger flavor or aroma of beer; more resembling beer in taste or smell.|This new IPA is beerier than the last one we tried.  这款新的IPA比我们上次尝试的啤酒味更浓。|The aroma wafting from the brewery was distinctly beerier. 从啤酒厂飘来的香味明显更像啤酒。|近义词：maltier, hoppier, more alcoholic|反义词: less beer-like, weaker, milder|词性:形容词
Etymology: beer + -y + -er (comparative suffix).  From "beer," referring to the alcoholic beverage, plus the adjective-forming suffix "-y," indicating possession of a quality, and the comparative suffix "-er," indicating a greater degree of that quality.
