fortifiers:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪərz/
noun
防御工事; 设防者; 防御者
People who or things that fortify something, making it stronger or more resistant to attack.
The engineers acted as fortifiers, strengthening the city's defenses against the impending siege.  工程师们充当了设防者，加强了城市防御即将到来的围攻。
New technologies have emerged as powerful fortifiers of national security. 新技术已成为国家安全的强大保障。
Synonyms: defenders, protectors, strengtheners, reinforcers
Antonyms: weakeners, saboteurs, destroyers
