hool:/huːl/
n.
喧闹; 狂欢;
A noisy, boisterous celebration or activity.
The football fans went on a hool after their team won the championship.  足球迷们在他们的球队赢得冠军后举行了狂欢。
近义词：celebration, revelry, spree, carousal
反义词：mourning, grief

词性: 名词
