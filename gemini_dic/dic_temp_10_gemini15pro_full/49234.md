delegitimization:/diːləˌdʒɪtɪmaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 使丧失合法性，非合法化 | The act of making something or someone seem illegitimate or invalid. | The delegitimization of the government led to widespread protests. 政府的非合法化导致了广泛的抗议。|  The constant attacks on her character were a clear attempt at delegitimization. 对她性格的不断攻击显然是为了使其丧失合法性。| 近义词：invalidation, discrediting, undermining | 反义词：legitimization, validation, authorization | 词性：名词

Etymology: From de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + legitimization (from legitimize, from French légitimer, from Latin legitimus "lawful").
