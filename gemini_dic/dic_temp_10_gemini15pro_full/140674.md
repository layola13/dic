omer:/ˈoʊmər/| n. |俄梅珥（古代以色列的容量单位，约为2.2升）|An omer is an ancient Hebrew unit of dry measure, approximately 2.2 liters.  It is mentioned in the Torah in relation to the offering of barley at Passover.|The priest offered an omer of barley. 祭司献上了一俄梅珥的大麦。|近义词:measure, unit|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|noun
