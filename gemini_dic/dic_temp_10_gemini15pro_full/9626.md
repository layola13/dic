antistick:/ˌæntiˈstɪk/| adj.|不粘的，防粘的|Preventing something from sticking to a surface.  Designed to prevent food from sticking during cooking.|例句：I bought an antistick pan for making omelets. 我买了一个不粘锅来做煎蛋卷。|近义词：nonstick, |反义词:sticky, adhesive|形容词

antistick:/ˌæntiˈstɪk/| n.|不粘材料，不粘涂层|A substance or coating that prevents sticking.|例句：The antistick on this pan is starting to wear off.  这个锅的不粘涂层开始脱落了。|近义词：nonstick coating, |反义词:adhesive, glue|名词
