lungless:/ˈlʌŋləs/| adj.|无肺的|Lacking lungs; respiring through the skin or other means.|Some salamanders are lungless and breathe entirely through their skin. 一些蝾螈没有肺，完全通过皮肤呼吸。|例句：The lungless salamander thrived in the damp environment.  无肺蝾螈在潮湿的环境中茁壮成长。

例句：Lungless frogs are highly susceptible to changes in water quality. 无肺蛙对水质变化高度敏感。|近义词：apulmonic|反义词：lunged|词性: 形容词
