bedlamite:/ˈbedləˌmaɪt/| n.|疯子；精神病人|A person who is insane or behaves in a wildly irrational manner.  A former inmate of the Hospital of St. Mary of Bethlehem, a London insane asylum.|The streets were filled with bedlamites during the festival, their costumes and antics adding to the chaotic atmosphere.|近义词：lunatic, madman, maniac, insane person|反义词:sane person|词性:noun

Etymology: From Bedlam, the popular name for the Hospital of St. Mary of Bethlehem, a London insane asylum, + -ite.  The word "Bedlam" itself is a corruption of "Bethlehem."
