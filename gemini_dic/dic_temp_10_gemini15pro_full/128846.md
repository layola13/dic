nigrine:/ˈnaɪɡraɪn/ | n. |黑锰矿，软锰矿|A black mineral consisting mainly of manganese dioxide. It is an important ore of manganese. |The miners discovered a rich vein of nigrine. 矿工们发现了一条富含黑锰矿的矿脉。|Synonym:  pyrolusite,  wad |Antonym: N/A (lacks a direct opposite as it refers to a specific mineral.)|noun
