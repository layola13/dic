edge:/ɛdʒ/ | v. | 徐徐移动；使锋利 |To move gradually or cautiously; to sharpen| The car edged forward into traffic. 汽车慢慢地驶入车流。| synonyms: inch, creep, sharpen, hone | antonyms: retreat, dull | verb
