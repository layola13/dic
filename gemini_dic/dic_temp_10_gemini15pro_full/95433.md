incing:/ˈɪnsɪŋ/
v.
煽动; 激起
inciting someone to do something 激励某人做某事
The speaker was accused of inciting the crowd to violence. 演讲者被指控煽动群众实施暴力。
近义词:  provoking, instigating, agitating, fomenting
反义词:  calming, pacifying, appeasing,  mollifying
词性: 动词 (present participle of "incite")


inciting:/ɪnˈsaɪtɪŋ/
adj.
煽动的; 刺激的
an inciting incident 一个煽动性的事件
The inciting words of the leader sparked a riot. 领导人煽动性的话语引发了骚乱。
近义词: provocative, inflammatory, stimulating
反义词: calming, soothing,  pacifying
词性: 形容词
