overdedicates:/ˌoʊvərˈdɛdɪkeɪts/
v.
过度奉献，过分投入
dedicates something excessively.  Commits too much time, energy, or resources to something.
He overdedicates himself to his work, often neglecting his family. 他过度投入于工作，经常忽略家人。
The company overdedicates resources to marketing, leaving little for research and development.  这家公司过度投入资源于市场营销，导致研发资金不足。
overcommits, overextends
undercommits, neglects
