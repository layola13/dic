navigabilities:/ˌnævɪɡəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可航性；可通航性|The navigabilities of the river are affected by the tides.  河流的可航性受潮汐影响。|例句：The navigabilities of these waterways have been greatly improved. 这些水道的可航性已得到极大改善。

The recent dredging project has increased the river's navigabilities. 最近的疏浚工程增加了河流的可航性。|近义词：navigability, seaworthiness |反义词: impassability |词性: 名词(复数)

Etymology:  From "navigable" + "-ities" (suffix forming plural nouns). "Navigable" comes from Late Middle English, from Old French "navegable," from Latin "navigabilis," from "navigare" ("to sail, navigate"), from "navis" ("ship") + "-abilis" (suffix meaning "able to be").
