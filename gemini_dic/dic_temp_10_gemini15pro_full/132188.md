nondances:/ˌnɒnˈdɑːnsɪz/
noun
非舞蹈；不跳舞的人（或事物）
People who don't dance or things that aren't dances.
The party was full of nondances, making it difficult to get the dance floor going.  派对上到处都是不跳舞的人，很难让舞池热闹起来。

Synonyms: wallflowers, nonparticipants
Antonyms: dancers, performers
