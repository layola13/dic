oosperm:/ˈoʊəˌspɜːrm/
n.
受精卵
fertilized egg; a zygote, especially of an animal.  The diploid cell resulting from the fusion of a sperm and an ovum.
The oosperm develops into an embryo. 受精卵发育成胚胎。
zygote, fertilized ovum
