canter:/ˈkæntər/ (US), /ˈkæntə(r)/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|慢跑，缓驰；使慢跑|To canter is to ride or cause a horse to canter.  慢跑是指骑马或使马慢跑。|He cantered his horse around the field. 他骑着马绕着田野慢跑。|近义词：lope, jog|反义词：gallop, sprint|词性:名词, 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Canterbury gallop, a pace used by pilgrims riding to Canterbury Cathedral. 源自坎特伯雷疾驰，这是朝圣者骑马前往坎特伯雷大教堂时使用的一种速度。
