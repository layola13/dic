foldaway:/ˈfəʊldəweɪ (UK), ˈfoʊldəweɪ (US)/
adjective|可折叠的，可收起的|Designed to be folded away out of sight when not in use.|This table is foldaway, which is very convenient for small spaces.  这张桌子是可折叠的，对于小空间来说非常方便。|collapsible, portable, compact|fixed, bulky
noun|折叠式物品|An object designed to be folded away for storage.|The apartment came with a foldaway bed. 公寓配有一张折叠床。|collapsible item, folding item, space-saver|fixed item, bulky item


词性: adjective, noun
词源:  "fold" (verb) + "away" (adverb).  "Fold" comes from Old English "fealdan," meaning "to double over." "Away" comes from Old English "onweg," meaning "on the way," from "on" (on) + "weg" (way).  The combination creates the sense of something that can be folded and put away.
