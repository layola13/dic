opinioned:/əˈpɪnjənd/
adjective
固执己见的；武断的
Having strong opinions, especially ones that are inflexible or based on prejudice.
He was a very opinioned man, and it was difficult to change his mind. 他是一个非常固执己见的人，很难改变他的想法。
dogmatic, assertive, insistent, biased
open-minded, receptive, flexible
词性: 形容词
