calcic:/ˈkælsɪk/| adj.|钙的；含钙的|Relating to or containing calcium.|Calcic minerals are essential for healthy bones. 钙质矿物质对骨骼健康至关重要.|近义词：calcium-containing, calcareous|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑缺乏钙的表达，例如 calcium-deficient)|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *calx* (lime) + *-ic* (suffix forming adjectives).
