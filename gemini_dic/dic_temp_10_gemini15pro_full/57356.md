dowsing: /ˈdaʊzɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdaʊzɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|探测（地下水源、矿藏等）|The practice of searching for underground water, minerals, or other hidden objects using a forked stick, rods, or a pendulum.|He learned dowsing from his grandfather. 他从他祖父那里学会了探测术。|近义词：divining, water witching|反义词: None|词性: noun

dowse: /daʊz/ (US), /daʊz/ (UK)| v.|探测（地下水源、矿藏等）|To search for underground water, minerals, or other hidden objects using a forked stick, rods, or a pendulum.|She dowsed for water in her backyard. 她在后院探测水源。|近义词：divine, water witch|反义词: None|词性: verb
