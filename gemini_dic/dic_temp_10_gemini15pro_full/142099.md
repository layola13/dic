orientates:/ˈɔːriənˌteɪts/ /ˈɔːrɪənˌteɪts/| v.| 使适应；确定方向；使朝向| To adjust or align oneself to a particular situation, direction, or set of values.  To familiarize someone with new surroundings or circumstances. | The compass orientates the ship towards north. 指南针使船朝向北方。|  synonyms: align, adapt, adjust, familiarize, acclimate | antonyms: disorient, confuse, lose | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *oriēntāre, from Latin oriēns (“rising”), present participle of orīrī (“to rise”).  Relates to the rising sun being used for navigation and direction.
