out-blossomed:/ˌaʊtˈblɑsəmd/
Verb
胜过；开花更盛于
To blossom more profusely or beautifully than; surpass in blossoming.
The cherry tree out-blossomed all the others in the orchard. 这棵樱桃树比果园里所有其他树开花都茂盛。
Surpass, excel, outdo, outstrip
Lag behind, fall short of
词性:动词

out-blossomed:/ˌaʊtˈblɑsəmd/
Adjective
开花更盛的
Having blossomed more profusely or beautifully than another.
The out-blossomed branches drooped with the weight of the flowers.  开花更盛的枝条因为花的重量而下垂。
Blooming, flourishing, thriving
Withered, faded
词性:形容词
