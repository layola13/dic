dwindle:/ˈdwɪndl/| vi.|减少，缩小，衰落|To become smaller and smaller; to decrease gradually in size, amount, or strength.|The town's population has dwindled to a mere few hundred.  这个城镇的人口已经减少到只有几百人。|The company's profits dwindled in the second quarter.  这家公司的利润在第二季度减少了。|近义词：decrease, diminish, shrink, decline|反义词:increase, grow, expand|动词
