leadman:/ˈliːdmən/| n. |领班，工头|A worker who leads or supervises a group of other workers, often in a factory or on a construction site.|The leadman instructed the crew on the day's tasks. 工头指示工人当天需要完成的任务。|The leadman ensured the project stayed on schedule. 领班确保项目按计划进行。|近义词：foreman, supervisor, team leader|反义词: subordinate, worker|noun

leadman:/ˈliːdmən/| n. |主角，领衔主演（尤指歌舞表演）|The principal performer in a show, especially a musical or dance performance.|The leadman captivated the audience with his powerful voice. 男主角用他浑厚的嗓音迷住了观众。|The leadman's dance moves were breathtaking. 领舞的舞步令人叹为观止。|近义词：star, principal, lead|反义词: supporting actor, extra|noun
