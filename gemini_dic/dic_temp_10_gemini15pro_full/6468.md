ampulla:/æmˈpʌlə/ /æmˈpʊlə/
n.
壶腹; 安瓿;
A dilated sac or cavity, such as the dilations of the semicircular canals of the ear or the terminal expansions of the lactiferous ducts in the mammary gland.
例句：The ampulla of Vater is where the pancreatic duct and common bile duct enter the duodenum.  壶腹是胰管和胆总管进入十二指肠的地方。

例句：The surgeon carefully examined the ampulla of the fallopian tube.  外科医生仔细检查了输卵管的壶腹。

近义词：sac, cavity, vesicle
反义词：duct, tube
词性：noun
