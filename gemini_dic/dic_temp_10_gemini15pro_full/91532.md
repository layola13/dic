hypallage:/haɪˈpælədʒiː/
n.
换置, 移位修饰法, 转移修辞格
A figure of speech in which an adjective or participle grammatically qualifies a noun other than the person or thing it is actually describing.
The poet used hypallage to describe the "weary road" instead of the "weary traveler." 诗人用换置的修辞手法描述“疲惫的道路”，而不是“疲惫的旅行者”。

The rustling of the silk dress filled the room. (The dress isn't rustling, the silk is.)  丝绸长裙的沙沙声充满了房间。(沙沙作响的是丝绸，而不是裙子。)
A sleepless night of worry. (The person, not the night, is sleepless.) 一个充满担忧的不眠之夜。(不眠的是人，而不是夜晚。)
近义词:transferred epithet, metonymy
