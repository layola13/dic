parses:/ˈpɑːrzɪz/ | v. | 解析；分析；理解 |  To analyze (a sentence) into its parts and describe their syntactic roles. To examine or analyze something in a methodical way. |  She parses the sentence to understand its grammatical structure. 她分析句子以理解其语法结构。 The computer parses the data to extract relevant information.  计算机解析数据以提取相关信息。 |  analyzes, interprets, decodes | synthesizes, confuses | verb
