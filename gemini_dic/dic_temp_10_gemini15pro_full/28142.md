capitulate:/kəˈpɪtʃʊleɪt/
verb
投降，屈服
to cease resisting an opponent or demand : surrender
例句：The enemy was forced to capitulate. 敌人被迫投降。
例句：The company capitulated to the union's demands. 公司屈服于工会的要求。
近义词：surrender, yield, submit, give in
反义词：resist, fight, oppose
词性:v.

词源:
From Latin capitulatus, past participle of capitulare "to draw up in chapters, arrange conditions," from capitulum "chapter heading," diminutive of caput "head."  The original sense was "to draw up terms of agreement," hence "to surrender on terms."
