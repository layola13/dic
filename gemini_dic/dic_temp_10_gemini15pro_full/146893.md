overinsisted: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈsɪstɪd/
verb
过度坚持；过分强调
having insisted too much or too strongly
He overinsisted on his point and alienated his audience. 他过分强调自己的观点，疏远了听众。
近义词: overemphasized, belabored
反义词: downplayed, understated
词性: verb (past tense and past participle of overinsist)
