continent:/ˈkɒntɪnənt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntɪnənt/ (US)| n. adj.|大陆，洲；自制的|A continent is one of the Earth's seven main continuous expanses of land: Africa, Antarctica, Asia, Australia, Europe, North America, and South America.  As an adjective, it means exercising self-restraint, especially sexually.|例句：Asia is the largest continent. 亚洲是最大的洲。|近义词：mainland, landmass (n.) chaste, abstinent (adj.)|反义词：incontinent (adj.)|词性: 名词, 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *continent, from Latin continentem (nominative continens), present participle of continēre "to hold together, enclose, or contain," from com- "together" + tenēre "to hold."
