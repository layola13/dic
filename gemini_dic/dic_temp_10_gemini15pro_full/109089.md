legatura:/leɡəˈtʊərə/ /ˌleɡəˈtʊrə/ | n. |连音；连字；连奏；装订 |A slur or tie in music, indicating that notes are to be played smoothly and connected; a ligature used in printing or writing to connect two letters; the binding of a book. |The soprano held the high note with a beautiful legatura. 女高音用优美的连音保持着高音。|Synonyms: slur, tie, ligature, binding |Antonyms: staccato, separation| noun
