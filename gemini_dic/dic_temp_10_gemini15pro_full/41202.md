coquettish:/kɒˈkɛtɪʃ/ /koʊˈkɛtɪʃ/| adj.|卖弄风情的，娇媚的，轻佻的|Characteristic of a coquette; flirtatious.|She gave him a coquettish glance. 她娇媚地瞥了他一眼。|近义词：flirtatious, playful, teasing|反义词：serious, modest, reserved|词性: adjective

词源: From French *coquet*, diminutive of *coq* (cock, rooster).  Originally referred to a woman who flirts like a rooster struts.
