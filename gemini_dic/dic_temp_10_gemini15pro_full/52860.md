dimetric:/daɪˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
二量体的；二聚的
Relating to or characterized by dimorphism in which the two forms are crystallographically distinct.  Having two axial lengths equal and the third unequal.
The dimetric projection gives a pictorial representation of an object.  二测投影给出了一个物体的图形表示。
近义词: anisotropic
反义词: isometric
词性: adjective


dimetric:/daɪˈmɛtrɪk/
noun
二聚体
A crystal having two axes of equal length and the third a different length. A chemical compound formed by the combination of two molecules of the same kind.
The chemist studied the properties of the newly discovered dimetric.  化学家研究了新发现的二聚体的特性。
近义词: dimer
反义词: monomer
词性: noun
