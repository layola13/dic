amok:/əˈmʌk/ /əˈmɑːk/| adv. n.| 狂乱地，杀人狂，|A state of murderous frenzy, originally observed in Malay culture, characterized by a sudden outburst of violent, uncontrollable behavior, often leading to indiscriminate killing.|He ran amok with a machete. 他挥舞着砍刀疯狂地砍人。|近义词：berserk, frenzied, wild, uncontrolled|反义词:calm, peaceful, controlled|词性: adverb, noun

Etymology: From Malay *āmok* "frenzied," "rushing in a frenzy."  Ultimately perhaps from Sanskrit *ā-muc*  "to release," "let loose."
