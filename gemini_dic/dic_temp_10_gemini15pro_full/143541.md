outgushes:/ˈaʊtɡʌʃɪz/| v.|涌出，迸发|A sudden and forceful flow of liquid, emotion, or words.  The emotion outgushes from her heart.|例句：Tears of joy outgushed from her eyes. 喜悦的泪水从她的眼中涌出。

例句：A geyser outgushes hot water and steam. 间歇泉喷出热水和蒸汽。|近义词：erupts, bursts, gushes, streams, pours, flows|反义词:trickles, seeps, oozes|词性:动词
