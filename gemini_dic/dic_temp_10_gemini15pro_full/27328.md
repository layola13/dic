camaieu:/kæˈmaɪ.uː/
n.
单色画, 浮雕, 同色调
A painting or carving done in shades of a single color, especially in grayish or brownish tones. Also, a cameo.
The artist specialized in camaieu, creating stunning landscapes using only shades of gray. 这位艺术家专攻单色画，仅用灰色调就创作出令人惊叹的风景。
