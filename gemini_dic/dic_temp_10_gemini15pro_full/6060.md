amenorrhoea: /əˌmeɪnəˈriːə/ (UK) /əˌmenəˈriːə/ (US)| n. |闭经，无月经|Absence of menstruation, typically for more than three months in women who have previously menstruated, or the absence of menstruation by age 16 in girls who have not yet menstruated.|例句：The doctor investigated the cause of her amenorrhoea. 医生调查了她闭经的原因。

例句：Amenorrhoea can be a symptom of a variety of medical conditions. 闭经可能是多种疾病的症状。|近义词：menostasis|反义词：menstruation, menses|词性: noun
