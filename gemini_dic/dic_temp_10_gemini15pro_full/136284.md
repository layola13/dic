nonreprehensible:/ˌnɒnˌrɛprɪˈhɛnsɪbl/ | adj. |无可指责的，无懈可击的|Not deserving of criticism or blame; blameless.|Her conduct throughout the ordeal was entirely nonreprehensible. 她在整个考验中的行为完全无可指责。|Synonyms: blameless, irreproachable, impeccable, faultless, unblameable|Antonyms: reprehensible, blameworthy, culpable, guilty|adjective

Etymology->
From non- (not) + reprehensible (deserving censure or condemnation). Reprehensible comes from Late Latin reprehensībilis, from reprehendere (to blame, censure), from re- (back) + prehendere (to grasp, seize).
