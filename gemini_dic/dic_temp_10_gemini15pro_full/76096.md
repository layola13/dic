furrowing:/ˈfɜːroʊɪŋ/ (US) /ˈfʌrəʊɪŋ/ (UK)| n. v.|犁沟，皱纹，起皱|The act of making furrows or wrinkles, or the furrows/wrinkles themselves.  This can refer to the act of plowing a field, or the lines formed on a person's face due to age or expression.|
She concentrated, furrowing her brow. 她集中精神，皱起了眉头。
The farmer was furrowing the field with his tractor. 农夫正在用拖拉机犁地。|近义词：creasing, wrinkling, grooving, plowing|反义词:smoothing, flattening|词性:noun, present participle of *furrow (verb)*
