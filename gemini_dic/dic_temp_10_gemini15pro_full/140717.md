omnify:/ˈɒmnɪfaɪ/ (US) /ˈɒmnɪfaɪ/ (UK)| v. |使无所不能；赋予全能；涵盖一切|To make universal or all-encompassing; to extend to or include everything.|The new software platform aims to omnify the user experience, offering a seamless transition between devices.|近义词：universalize, encompass, include, integrate|反义词：restrict, limit, exclude|词性:动词

Etymology:  From Latin *omni-* (all) + *-ficare* (to make), related to *facere* (to do).
