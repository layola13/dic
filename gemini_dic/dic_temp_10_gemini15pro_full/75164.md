fritzed-out:/ˈfrɪtstˌaʊt/
adjective
崩溃的，失灵的，坏了的
Broken, malfunctioning, especially of electronic equipment; also, exhausted, overwhelmed, or burnt out.  Relates to the sound or appearance of something failing electrically.
My old computer completely fritzed-out after the power surge. 我的旧电脑在电压激增后完全坏了。
She felt completely fritzed-out after working 70 hours that week.  那一周工作70小时后，她感到筋疲力尽。
Synonym: broken, malfunctioning, exhausted, burnt out, frazzled
Antonym: working, functional, energetic, refreshed
词性: 形容词
