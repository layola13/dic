commonwealth:/ˈkɒmənˌwelθ/ /ˈkɑːmənˌwelθ/| n.|共和国，联邦，联合体|A nation, state, or other political entity founded on law and united by a compact of the people for the common good.  A group of sovereign states and their dependencies associated by their own free choice and linked by historical ties, common interests, and ideals, especially the Commonwealth of Nations, which is headed by the British monarch.|The British Commonwealth is made up of many former British colonies. 英联邦由许多前英国殖民地组成。|synonyms: federation, confederation, union, alliance, association, league, coalition, bloc, partnership|antonyms: division, separation, disunity|noun
