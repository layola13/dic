cismontane:/ˌsɪzmɒnˈteɪn/ | adj. | 山这边的，位于山脉这边的 | Situated on the side of a mountain range nearer to the speaker or a place of reference. |  The cismontane regions are known for their fertile valleys. 山这边的地区以其肥沃的山谷而闻名。| The cismontane vineyards produce excellent wines. 山这边的葡萄园出产优质葡萄酒。| 近义词: hither | 反义词: ultramontane, transmontane | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Italian *cismontano*, from Latin *cis* (on this side of) + *mons*, *montis* (mountain).
