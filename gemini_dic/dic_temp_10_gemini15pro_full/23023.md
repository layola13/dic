bouldering: /ˈboʊldərɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbəʊldərɪŋ/ (UK)| n.| 抱石；攀岩的一种形式|The act of climbing on large rocks or artificial rock walls without ropes or harnesses, typically close to the ground, focusing on difficult moves and problem-solving.|Example: I went bouldering with my friends last weekend.  我上周末和朋友们去抱石了。

Example: Bouldering is a great way to build strength and improve your climbing technique. 抱石是增强力量和提高攀岩技巧的好方法。|近义词：rock climbing, free climbing|反义词: (No direct opposite, potentially abseiling/rappelling - descending with ropes)|词性: Noun
