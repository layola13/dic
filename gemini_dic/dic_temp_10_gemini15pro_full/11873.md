arrives:/əˈraɪvz/
verb
到达
comes to a place, reaches a destination
The train arrives at 8:00 am. 火车早上8点到达。
The package arrived yesterday. 包裹昨天到达了。
近义词: reaches, comes, appears
反义词: departs, leaves
词性: verb
