overmystified: /ˌoʊvərˈmɪstɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 过度神秘化的，使过于困惑的 | Made too mysterious or confusing; excessively mystified. | The plot of the movie was so overmystified that I left the theater more confused than when I entered. 这部电影的情节过于神秘，以至于我离开电影院时比进去时更困惑。| 近义词：perplexed, bewildered, confused | 反义词：clear, straightforward, lucid | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "mystified" (past participle of "mystify," from "mystify"  from French "mystifier," from Late Latin "mystificare" "to make mystical," from Latin "mysticus" "mystic" + "-ficare" (from "facere" "to make").)
