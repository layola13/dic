blueness:/ˈbluːnɪs/
n.
蓝，蓝色; 忧郁
the quality or state of being blue.
The blueness of the sky was breathtaking. 天空的湛蓝令人叹为观止。
blue, azure, cerulean
yellowness, redness
noun
