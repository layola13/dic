ballooner:/bəˈluːnər/| n.|热气球驾驶员；乘热气球的人|A person who operates or travels in a hot-air balloon. |例句：The ballooners prepared for takeoff as the sun began to rise.  热气球驾驶员们准备起飞，这时太阳开始升起。

例句：Experienced ballooners know how to navigate using wind currents.  经验丰富的热气球驾驶员知道如何利用风向航行。|近义词：aeronaut, pilot|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ballooner:/bəˈluːnər/| v. |乘坐热气球；像气球一样膨胀|To travel by hot-air balloon; To swell or puff out like a balloon.|例句：We ballooned over the Napa Valley at sunrise. 我们在日出时乘坐热气球飞越纳帕谷。

例句：Her dress ballooned out in the wind.  她的裙子在风中鼓了起来。|近义词：float, inflate, expand|反义词:deflate, shrink|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From balloon + -er.  Balloon comes from French ballon, from Italian ballone "large ball," augmentative of balla "ball."
