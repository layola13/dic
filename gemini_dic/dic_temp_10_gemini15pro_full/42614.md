counterrallies:/ˈkaʊntərˌræliz/ | n. |反集会，对抗性集会 | A demonstration or organized gathering in opposition to another rally. |  The government organized counterrallies to disrupt the opposition's protests. 政府组织了反集会来扰乱反对派的抗议活动。| Synonyms: counterdemonstrations, counterprotests | Antonyms: rallies, demonstrations, protests | noun

counterrally:/ˈkaʊntərˌræli/ | v. |举行反集会，进行对抗性集会 | To organize or participate in a counterrally. |  Supporters counterrallied against the proposed cuts to education. 支持者们举行反集会，反对拟议的教育削减计划。| Synonyms: counterdemonstrate, counterprotest | Antonyms: rally, demonstrate, protest | verb
