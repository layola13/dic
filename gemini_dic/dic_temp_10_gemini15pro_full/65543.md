/ˈjuːθəˌnaɪz/ | v. | 安乐死 | To kill (a person or animal) painlessly, especially to relieve suffering from an incurable illness. | The vet euthanized the dog to end its suffering. 兽医对这只狗实施了安乐死以结束它的痛苦。|  The family made the difficult decision to euthanize their beloved pet. 这家人艰难地决定对他们心爱的宠物实施安乐死。 | synonyms: put down, put to sleep | antonyms: resuscitate, revive | verb

/ˈjuːθəˌnaɪzd/ | adj. | 被安乐死的 |  Killed painlessly, especially to relieve suffering from an incurable illness. |  The euthanized animal was cremated. 被安乐死的动物被火化了。 | The shelter takes in many abandoned and sometimes euthanized animals.  收容所接收许多被遗弃的，有时是被安乐死的动物。 | synonyms: put down, put to sleep | antonyms: living, alive | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
