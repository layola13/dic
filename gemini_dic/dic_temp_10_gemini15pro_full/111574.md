loblolly:/ˈlɑːblɑːli/ | n. | 火炬松；泥浆；稀粥；傻瓜 | A fast-growing pine of the southeastern US, having long needles in clusters of three and a reddish-brown bark. Also, a thick gruel or porridge, or a mudhole or mire.  Informally, it can refer to a foolish person. |  The loblolly pines grew tall and straight. 火炬松长得又高又直。| 近义词：pine, gruel, porridge, mire, fool | 反义词: genius | 词性: noun

loblolly:/ˈlɑːblɑːli/ | adj. | 傻的 | Foolish; stupid |  He made a loblolly decision to invest all his money in that failing company. 他做了一个愚蠢的决定，把所有的钱都投资在那家快要倒闭的公司。| 近义词：foolish, silly, stupid | 反义词: wise, intelligent | 词性: adjective
