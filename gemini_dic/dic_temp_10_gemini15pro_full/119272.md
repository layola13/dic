mickey-taking: /ˈmɪkiˌteɪkɪŋ/ | n. | 嘲笑；戏弄；取笑 | The act of teasing or mocking someone in a playful or ironic way.  It often involves gentle mockery and good-natured teasing, rather than outright insults or malice. | "He's always mickey-taking, but he doesn't mean any harm." 他总是爱开玩笑，但并没有恶意。 | 近义词: teasing, mocking, ribbing, banter, joking | 反义词: praising, flattering | 词性: noun
