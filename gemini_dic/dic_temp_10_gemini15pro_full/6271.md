amor:/ˈɑːmɔːr/ (US), /ˈeɪmɔːr/ (UK)| n.|爱，爱情；爱神（通常指丘比特）|A feeling of strong or constant affection and devotion.|例句：Their amor for each other was evident. 他们彼此的爱显而易见。

例句：The statue of Amor stood in the garden. 爱神雕像矗立在花园里。|近义词：love, affection, passion|反义词：hate, indifference|词性:名词
