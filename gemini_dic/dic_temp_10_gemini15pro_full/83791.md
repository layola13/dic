hairlessnesses:/ˈhɛərləsnɪsɪz/
noun
无毛，秃头，脱毛状态
The state or quality of being hairless; absence of hair.
The Sphynx cat is known for its hairlessnesses.  斯芬克斯猫以其无毛而闻名。
The hairlessnesses of the newborn surprised the doctors.  新生儿的无毛状态让医生们感到惊讶。
近义词:baldness, glabrousness
反义词:hairiness, hirsuteness
词性:noun

Etymology:
From hairless + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  Hairless comes from Old English *hǣrlēas, from hǣr (hair) + -lēas (without).
