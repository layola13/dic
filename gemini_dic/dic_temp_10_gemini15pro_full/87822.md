hickory:/ˈhɪkəri/ (US), /ˈhɪkəri/ (UK)| n. |山胡桃木；山胡桃树；山胡桃木棍|A North American tree of the walnut family that produces edible nuts and hard, strong wood.  The wood itself is also called hickory.|The carpenter used hickory to make the axe handle.  木匠用山胡桃木做了斧柄。|Synonyms: pecan, walnut (for the nut), ash, oak, maple (for the wood)|Antonyms: N/A|noun


hickory:/ˈhɪkəri/ (US), /ˈhɪkəri/ (UK)| adj. |山胡桃木制的|Made of hickory wood.|She has a beautiful hickory rocking chair. 她有一把漂亮的山胡桃木摇椅。|Synonyms: wooden|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
