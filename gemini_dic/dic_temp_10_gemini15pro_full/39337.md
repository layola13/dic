conjoin:/kənˈdʒɔɪn/ | v. |结合，连接，联合|To join together; to combine.|The two rivers conjoin at the city center. 这两条河在市中心汇合。|近义词：unite, combine, merge, link|反义词：separate, divide, disjoin|词性:动词
conjoin:/kənˈdʒɔɪn/ | adj. |结合的，连接的|Joined together; combined.|The conjoin efforts of the two teams resulted in a successful project. 两个团队的共同努力使项目取得了成功。|近义词：united, combined, joint|反义词：separate, divided, disjointed|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin conjungere, from con- ‘together’ + jungere ‘to join.’
