dividuous:/dɪˈsɪdʒuəs/| adj.| 落叶的|Shedding foliage at the end of the growing season.| The oak tree is deciduous and loses its leaves in autumn. 橡树是落叶树，秋天会落叶。| deciduous trees are common in temperate climates. 落叶树在温带气候中很常见。|近义词：deciduate|反义词：evergreen|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *deciduus, from decidere "to fall off," from de- "down" + cadere "to fall."
