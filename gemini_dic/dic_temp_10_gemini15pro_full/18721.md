beretta:/bəˈrɛtə/ | n. | 贝雷帽 | (Often lowercase) A soft, round, flat-crowned cap, usually of felt or wool. | He wore a stylish beretta. 他戴着一顶时髦的贝雷帽。| synonym: cap, beret | antonym: helmet | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Italian word "Beretta," the name of the Beretta family, founders of the firearms company, and also a type of cap.  The origin of the family name is uncertain, possibly from a Lombardic word meaning "cap."
