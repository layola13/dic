overstraightly:/ˌoʊvərˈstreɪtli/
adverb|过于正直地，过于直接地|Too righteously or too directly; in an excessively straightforward manner.|He interpreted the rules overstraightly, missing the nuances of the situation. 他对规则的解释过于刻板，忽略了情况的细微差别。|例句：She spoke overstraightly, causing offense unintentionally. 她说话过于直接，无意中冒犯了别人。|近义词：rigidly, inflexibly, unbendingly|反义词：flexibly, leniently, loosely|词性: 副词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "straight" (Old English streht "straight," from Proto-Germanic *strakhtaz "straight") + "-ly" (Old English -lice, adverbial suffix).  The prefix "over-" indicates excess or going beyond a normal limit.
