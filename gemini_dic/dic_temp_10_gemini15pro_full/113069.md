lumps:/lʌmps/| n.|块，肿块，疙瘩|A compact mass of something, typically one without a definite or regular shape.  |The batter was full of lumps. 面糊里有很多疙瘩。|Synonyms: chunks, clots, masses, clumps|Antonyms: particles, grains, fragments|noun


lumps:/lʌmps/| v.|把…归并在一起，(使)结块|To group or categorize together indiscriminately. |Don't lump all the expenses together. 不要把所有的费用都混在一起。|Synonyms: combine, group, classify, categorize|Antonyms: separate, divide, distinguish|verb
