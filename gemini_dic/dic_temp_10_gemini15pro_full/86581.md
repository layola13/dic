heliocentricism:/ˌhiːliːoʊˈsɛntrɪsɪzəm/| n. |日心说|The astronomical model in which the Earth and planets revolve around the Sun at the center of the Solar System.| Copernicus's theory of heliocentricism revolutionized astronomy. 哥白尼的日心说理论彻底改变了天文学。|近义词: heliocentric theory|反义词: geocentricism|词性:noun

Etymology:
From helio- "sun" (from Greek hēlios "sun") + -centric (from Latin centrum "center") + -ism (doctrine, belief).
