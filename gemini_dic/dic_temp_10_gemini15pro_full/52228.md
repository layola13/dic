diatonically:/ˌdaɪəˈtɑːnɪkli/ | adv. | 全音阶地； 音阶式地 |  In music, relating to or based on the diatonic scale (a scale of seven notes with characteristic intervals).  This means using only the notes within a key, without chromatic alterations. | The melody is diatonically simple, yet emotionally powerful. 这旋律全音阶式地简单，却情感丰富。| The composer used diatonically correct harmonies throughout the piece.  作曲家在整首曲子中都使用了全音阶正确的和声。| 近义词: stepwise, scale-wise | 反义词: chromatically | 词性: 副词
