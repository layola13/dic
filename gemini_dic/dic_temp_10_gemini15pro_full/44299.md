crooked:/ˈkrʊkɪd/ (US), /ˈkrʊkɪd/ (UK)| adj.|弯曲的，歪的；不诚实的，欺诈的|Bent or twisted out of shape or out of a straight line; dishonest or illegal.|The picture hung crooked on the wall.  这幅画歪歪斜斜地挂在墙上。 He was involved in crooked business deals. 他参与了不正当的商业交易。|近义词：bent, twisted, dishonest, corrupt, fraudulent|反义词：straight, honest, upright|词性:形容词

crooked:/ˈkrʊkɪdli/ (US), /ˈkrʊkɪdli/ (UK)| adv.|弯曲地，歪斜地；不诚实地|In a bent or twisted way; dishonestly.|He smiled crookedly. 他咧嘴一笑。  The mirror was hung crookedly on the wall. 镜子歪歪斜斜地挂在墙上。|近义词：awry, askew, dishonestly|反义词：straight, honestly|词性:副词
