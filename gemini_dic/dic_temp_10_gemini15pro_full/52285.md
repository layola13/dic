dichroiscope: /daɪˈkrɔɪskoʊp/ | n. | 二向色镜，二色镜 | An instrument used to examine the dichroism of crystals, which is the property of exhibiting different colors when viewed in different directions of polarized light.  It utilizes a calcite crystal to separate and compare the two polarized rays. |  The gemologist used a dichroiscope to determine the dichroism of the tourmaline. 宝石学家用二向色镜来测定碧玺的多色性。 | The dichroiscope revealed the subtle pleochroism of the iolite. 二向色镜揭示了堇青石微妙的多向色性。 |  polariscope, spectroscope | N/A | noun
