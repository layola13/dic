fooling-around:/ˈfuːlɪŋ əˈraʊnd/| n./v.|闲逛；胡闹；鬼混|The act of engaging in playful, often silly or unproductive activities.  It can refer to physical play, messing around with objects, or simply wasting time.|例句：The children were fooling around in the backyard. 孩子们在后院里玩耍嬉闹。

例句：Stop fooling around and get to work! 别再胡闹了，开始工作吧！|近义词：playing around, messing around, goofing off, dawdling|反义词：working, studying, being serious|词性：名词/动词
