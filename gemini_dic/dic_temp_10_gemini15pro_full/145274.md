overbookishness:/ˌoʊvərˈbʊkɪʃnəs/
n.
过度书生气，迂腐
Excessive fondness for or preoccupation with books and learning; sometimes implying pedantry or an impractical approach to life.
He displayed a certain overbookishness, quoting obscure poets at inappropriate times. 他表现出某种过度书生气，在不恰当的时候引用一些晦涩的诗人。
近义词: pedantry, bookishness, scholarly, intellectualism
反义词: practicality, worldliness, ignorance, philistinism
