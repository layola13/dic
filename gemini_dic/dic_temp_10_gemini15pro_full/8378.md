anti-radiation:/ˌæntiˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən/
anti-radiation| adj.|抗辐射的，防辐射的|Designed to protect against the harmful effects of radiation.  This describes something that blocks or reduces radiation exposure.|例句：She bought an anti-radiation phone case. 她买了一个防辐射手机壳。|近义词：radiation-proof, radioprotective|反义词: radioactive|词性:形容词
