describer:/dɪˈskraɪbər/| n.|描述者，形容者|A person or thing that describes.  Someone or something that gives a description of something.|The travel writer is a skilled describer of landscapes.|近义词: narrator, depicter, expounder, interpreter|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "describe" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Describe" comes from the Latin "describere" (to write down, copy, represent), from "de-" (down) + "scribere" (to write).
