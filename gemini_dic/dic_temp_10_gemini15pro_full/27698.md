candling:/ˈkændlɪŋ/| n.| 烛光检查，透光检查|The process of examining an egg by holding it to a light to see if it is fertile or contains defects.|例句：Candling is a traditional method used to check the quality of eggs. 透光检查是用来检查鸡蛋质量的传统方法。

例句：They used candling to determine the viability of the chicken eggs. 他们用透光检查来确定鸡蛋的受精情况。|近义词：testing, examining, inspecting|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From candle + -ing.  The process involves holding an egg up to a bright light source, traditionally a candle.
