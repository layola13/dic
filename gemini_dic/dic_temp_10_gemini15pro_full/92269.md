hypervigilantnesses:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɪdʒɪlənsnɪsɪz/| n. |过度警觉，高度警惕|The state of being hypervigilant, characterized by an excessive awareness of potential dangers or threats.|Her hypervigilantnesses made it difficult for her to relax. 她的过度警觉让她难以放松。|近义词：watchfulness, alertness, vigilance, cautiousness|反义词：carelessness, negligence, inattentiveness|词性:名词

Etymology: From hypervigilance + -ness.  Hyper- comes from Greek, meaning "over, above, beyond." Vigilance comes from Latin *vigilantia, from vigilans "watchful," present participle of vigilare "to keep awake, watch," from vigil "awake, watchful."
