ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *blason (“shield, coat of arms”), from Frankish *blaso (“horn”), from Proto-Germanic *blasaną (“to blow, blast”). The sense development is from hunting horns, often carried as trophies and later incorporated into coats of arms.
