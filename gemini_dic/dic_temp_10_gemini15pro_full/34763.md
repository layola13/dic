cliffside:/ˈklɪfsaɪd/| n. |悬崖边，崖壁|The house was perched precariously on the cliffside. 这房子危险地坐落在悬崖边上。|例句：The climber carefully traversed the cliffside. 登山者小心翼翼地穿过崖壁。|近义词：cliff face, precipice, bluff|反义词:valley, plain|noun

cliffside:/ˈklɪfsaɪd/| adj. |悬崖边的|The cliffside path offered breathtaking views. 悬崖边的小路提供了令人叹为观止的景色。|例句：They built a cliffside cabin. 他们在悬崖边建了一间小屋。|近义词：cliff-hanging, precipitous|反义词:lowland|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'cliff' + 'side'. 'Cliff' comes from Old English 'clif' meaning "cliff, steep rock, promontory," related to Middle Dutch 'klippe', 'clif' "rock, cliff." 'Side' comes from Old English 'sīde' meaning "side, flank,"  related to Old Frisian 'side', Old Saxon 'sida', Old High German 'sīta', Old Norse 'síða', Gothic 'seiths'.
