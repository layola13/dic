miaowing:/miˈaʊɪŋ/| adj.|喵喵叫的，（猫）哀鸣的|The sound a cat makes, especially when it is complaining or in distress.  A plaintive, drawn-out meow.|例句：The kitten was miaowing pitifully because it was hungry. 小猫饿了，可怜地喵喵叫着。

例句：I heard a faint miaowing coming from the bushes. 我听到灌木丛中传来微弱的喵喵声。|近义词：mewing, caterwauling (for loud, unpleasant meows)|反义词: purring (猫发出愉悦的呜呜声)|词性: adjective
