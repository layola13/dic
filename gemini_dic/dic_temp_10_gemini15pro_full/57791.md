ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dreggen*, from Old English *dragan*, *dregan* (“to draw, pull, drag”), from Proto-Germanic *draganą* (“to draw, pull, drag”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰregʰ- (“to pull, draw”). Cognate with Dutch *dragen* (“to carry, wear”), German *tragen* (“to carry, wear”), Danish *drage* (“to draw, pull”), Swedish *draga* (“to draw, pull”), Icelandic *draga* (“to draw, pull”).
