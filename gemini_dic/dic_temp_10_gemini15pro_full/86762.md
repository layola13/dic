helplessness:/ˈhɛlpləsnəs/| n. |无助，无力，无能为力|The state of being unable to help oneself; lack of power or ability to act effectively.|She felt a deep sense of helplessness as she watched the fire spread. 她看着火势蔓延，感到深深的无助。|近义词：powerlessness, inability, impotence, vulnerability|反义词:power, ability, control, competence|词性:noun

helpless:/ˈhɛlplɪs/| adj. |无助的，无力的，无能为力的|Unable to help oneself; lacking power or ability to act effectively.|The injured bird was completely helpless. 受伤的小鸟完全无助。|近义词：powerless, dependent, vulnerable|反义词:powerful, capable, independent, competent|词性:adjective
