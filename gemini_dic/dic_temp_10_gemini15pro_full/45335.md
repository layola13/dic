cumu:/ˈkjuːmjʊlətɪv/ | adj. | 累积的，渐增的 | increasing by successive additions. | The cumulative effect of the small changes was significant. 小变化的累积效应是显著的。|  The medicine has a cumulative effect. 这种药有累积作用。| 近义词:  accumulative, increasing, progressive, aggregate | 反义词: decreasing, diminishing | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *cumulātīvus*, from cumulātus (past participle of cumulāre ‘to heap up’), from cumulus ‘heap’.
