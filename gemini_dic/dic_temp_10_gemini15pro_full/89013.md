hollowest:/ˈhɒləʊɪst/ /ˈhɑːloʊɪst/
adjective
最空心的，最空洞的
having the largest cavity or being most empty inside.
The hollowest tree in the forest became home to a family of owls. 森林里最空心的树成了猫头鹰的家。
The politician's promises rang hollowest of all.  这位政客的承诺听起来最空洞。
近义词: emptiest, most vacant, most concave
反义词: fullest, solidest, densest
词性: adjective

词源:
From Old English holh + -est (superlative suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hulwą.
