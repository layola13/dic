midwinterly:/ˌmɪdˈwɪntərli/| adj.|仲冬的，隆冬的|Characteristic of or occurring in the middle of winter.  It describes something that is typical of the deepest part of winter.|例句：The midwinterly landscape was bleak and frozen. 仲冬的景色荒凉而冰冻。

例句：We braved the midwinterly chill to go ice-skating. 我们冒着隆冬的严寒去滑冰。|近义词：wintry, hibernal|反义词：midsummerly|词性：形容词
