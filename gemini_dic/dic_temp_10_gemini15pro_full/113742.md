macrograph:/ˈmækrəɡræf/ | n. |宏观照片；放大照片 | A photograph of an object taken at low magnification, typically showing its overall structure or details visible to the naked eye or with slight magnification. | Example 1: The macrograph clearly showed the cracks in the metal. 宏观照片清楚地显示了金属上的裂纹。

Example 2:  A macrograph of the circuit board revealed a faulty connection.  电路板的宏观照片显示出一个错误的连接。 | Synonym: photomacrograph, close-up (photograph) | Antonym: micrograph | noun
