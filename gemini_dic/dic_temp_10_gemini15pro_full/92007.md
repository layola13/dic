hyperorganic:/ˌhaɪpərɔːrˈɡænɪk/
adjective
超有机的，极度有机的
Characterized by extreme or excessive organic qualities; appearing excessively natural or biological, sometimes to the point of being grotesque or unnatural.
The artist's hyperorganic sculptures, dripping with simulated bodily fluids, evoked a mixture of fascination and revulsion.  艺术家超有机的雕塑，滴着模拟体液，唤起了一种既着迷又厌恶的混合感觉。
The jungle setting was hyperorganic, with plants and vines twisting into impossible shapes.  丛林的环境极度有机，植物和藤蔓扭曲成不可能的形状。
近义词:  excessively organic, grotesquely organic, surrealistically organic
反义词: inorganic, artificial, synthetic
词性: adjective
词源:  hyper- (prefix meaning "over, excessive") + organic
