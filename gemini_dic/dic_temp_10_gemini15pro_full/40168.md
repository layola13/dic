contentable:/kənˈtɛntəbəl/
adjective
易满足的，知足的
Easily satisfied or pleased; content.
She leads a simple, contentable life in the countryside. 她过着简朴而知足的乡村生活。
synonyms: content, satisfied, cheerful, happy, easygoing
antonyms: discontented, unsatisfied, restless, unhappy, demanding
词性: 形容词

(由于contentable较少用于词组搭配，以下列出其近义词content的常用搭配)
content with:  对...感到满足  He is content with his life. 他对自己的生活感到满足。
content to:  满足于...  She was content to stay at home. 她满足于待在家里。
to one's heart's content:  尽情地；心满意足地  He ate to his heart's content. 他尽情地吃。
content oneself with:  满足于...  He contented himself with a simple meal. 他满足于一顿简单的饭菜。
find content in: 在...中找到满足感 She finds content in gardening. 她在园艺中找到了满足感。
