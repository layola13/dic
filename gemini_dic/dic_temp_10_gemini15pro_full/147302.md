overmodifying:/ˌōvərˈmɑːdɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 过度修饰 | Using too many modifiers (adjectives, adverbs, etc.) in a sentence, making it sound cluttered and unnatural. | The writer's prose was criticized for overmodifying simple sentences, making them unnecessarily complex.  作者的散文因过度修饰简单的句子而受到批评，使句子不必要地复杂。 |  She was constantly overmodifying her essays, adding adjectives upon adjectives until the meaning was lost. 她不断地过度修饰她的文章，一个接一个地添加形容词，直到意思丢失。 | overusing, overdoing, embellishing excessively | understating, simplifying | verb
