deauthorizes:/diːˈɔːθəˌraɪzɪz/
|v.|撤销授权，取消授权|To remove the authorization or permission previously granted to someone or something.|The administrator deauthorized the user's access to the confidential files.  管理员撤销了用户访问机密文件的权限。
|revoke, withdraw, cancel, rescind|authorize, grant, empower|verb
