bouncingly:/ˈbaʊnsɪŋli/
adverb
有弹性地，活泼地
in a bouncing manner; with a springing or rebounding movement.
The ball bounced bouncingly down the stairs.  球蹦蹦跳跳地滚下楼梯。
近义词:resiliently, springily, elastically
反义词:stiffly, rigidly
词性:adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From bounce + -ing + -ly.  Bounce comes from Middle French bondir "to leap, jump".
