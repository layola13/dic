disarranger:/dɪsəˈreɪndʒər/
noun
扰乱者；混乱制造者
someone or something that disarranges or throws into disorder
The wind was a disarranger of the neatly piled papers. 风把整齐堆放的纸吹乱了。
Synonyms: disturber, disruptor
Antonyms: organizer, arranger
词性: 名词
