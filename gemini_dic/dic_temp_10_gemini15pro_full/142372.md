orthopoda:/ɔːrˈθɒpədə/| n. |鳥腳亞目|A diverse group of ornithischian dinosaurs characterized by a "bird-like hip" structure, including both bipedal and quadrupedal herbivores.|Ornithopods such as Iguanodon were common during the Cretaceous period. 鳥腳類恐龍，如禽龍，在白堊紀時期很常見。|近义词：ornithischian|反义词: N/A | noun
