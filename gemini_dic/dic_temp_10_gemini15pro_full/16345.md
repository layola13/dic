barbarous: /ˈbɑːrbərəs/ (US) /ˈbɑːbərəs/ (UK)| adj.|野蛮的，残暴的；粗俗的，未开化的| savagely cruel; exceedingly brutal; uncivilized; primitive in customs and culture |  The barbarous acts of the dictator shocked the world.  独裁者的野蛮行径震惊了世界。

The prison guards engaged in barbarous treatment of the inmates. 监狱看守对犯人进行了野蛮的虐待。

|近义词：savage, brutal, cruel, inhuman, uncivilized, primitive|反义词：civilized, humane, refined, cultured|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *barbare*, from Latin *barbarus*, from Greek *βάρβαρος* (barbaros), originally referring to anyone who did not speak Greek (imitating the unintelligible sounds "bar-bar").  The meaning later shifted to refer to those considered uncivilized or savage.
