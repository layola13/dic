graptolitic:/ˌɡræptəˈlɪtɪk/ | adj. | 笔石的；与笔石有关的 | Relating to or containing graptolites, extinct marine colonial organisms. | The graptolitic shales are a rich source of fossils.  笔石页岩是丰富的化石来源。| Near synonyms:  fossiliferous (containing fossils) | Antonyms:  abiotic (non-living) | 词性: adjective

graptolitic:/ˌɡræptəˈlɪtɪk/ | n. | 笔石化石 | A fossil graptolite. |  The museum displayed several graptolitics from the Ordovician period.  博物馆展出了几个奥陶纪的笔石化石。| Near synonyms:  fossil | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "living organism") | 词性: noun
