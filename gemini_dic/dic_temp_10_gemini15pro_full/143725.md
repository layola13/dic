outlived:/ˌaʊtˈlɪvd/
verb
比...活得长; 经历...而幸存
To live longer than someone or something else; to survive or endure beyond something.
He outlived his wife by ten years. 他比妻子多活了十年。
The old house has outlived many storms. 这栋老房子经历了许多暴风雨而幸存下来。
survive, outlast
predecease
