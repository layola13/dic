hetairism:/ˈhɛtərɪzəm/
n.
娼妓制度;  公开姘居;
The state or practice of concubinage. A system of social organization in which a man may have several female partners.  A more general term than concubinage, often used to describe systems where women have some degree of autonomy and social standing, unlike typical concubinage which implies a lower status.
Hetairism was a common practice in ancient Greece. 古希腊盛行娼妓制度。
近义词: concubinage, promiscuity
反义词: monogamy, chastity
词性: noun
