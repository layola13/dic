disrespecter:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspɛktər/
n.
不尊重者，失礼者
Someone who is disrespectful; a person who shows a lack of respect.
He is a disrespecter of traditions. 他不尊重传统。
近义词：insulter, offender, slight-er
反义词：admirer, respecter
