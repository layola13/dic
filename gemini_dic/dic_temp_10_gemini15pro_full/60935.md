noun
高架铁路；高架道路
raised structures, typically for transportation, above ground level.
The city's elevateds offer a convenient way to travel.  这座城市的高架铁路提供了一种便捷的出行方式。
The elevateds provide a scenic view of the city. 高架道路提供了城市的美景。
synonyms: overpasses, viaducts, railways, platforms
antonyms: subways, undergrounds, tunnels
