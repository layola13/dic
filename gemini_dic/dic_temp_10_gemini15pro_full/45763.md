cushionlike:/ˈkʊʃənlaɪk/| adj.|像垫子一样的，柔软的|Resembling a cushion, especially in softness or shape.  Something that is soft and yielding, like a cushion.|The seats were cushionlike and comfortable. 座位像垫子一样柔软舒适。|近义词：soft, padded, cushiony, yielding, springy|反义词：hard, firm, rigid|词性:adjective
词源: cushion + like.  "Cushion" comes from Old French "coissin" (modern French "coussin"), ultimately from Latin "coxa" (hip).  "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
