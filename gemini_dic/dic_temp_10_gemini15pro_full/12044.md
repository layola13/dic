articulates:/ɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪts/ | v. |清晰地表达；连接；形成关节|expresses fluently and coherently; joins together; forms a joint|She articulates her thoughts clearly and concisely. 她清晰而简洁地表达了她的想法。|The bones in the shoulder articulate with the collarbone. 肩膀的骨头与锁骨相连。|synonyms: enunciates, expresses, pronounces, connects, joins|antonyms: mumbles, slurs, separates, disconnects|verb
