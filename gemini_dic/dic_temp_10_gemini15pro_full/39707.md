consignatory:/kənˌsaɪnəˈtɔːri/ /kənˈsaɪɡnətɔːri/
n.|收货人；受托人|A person or organization to whom something is consigned, especially goods or documents.|The consignatory will receive the shipment within three business days.  收货人将在三个工作日内收到货物。|synonyms: consignee, agent, trustee |antonyms: consignor, shipper, sender
word origin: Late Middle English: from late Latin *consignatarius, from consignare ‘consign’.

consignatory:/kənˌsaɪnəˈtɔːri/ /kənˈsaɪɡnətɔːri/
n.|签署国；签约方|A party that signs and is bound by a treaty or agreement.|All consignatories of the treaty are obligated to uphold its terms. 所有签署该条约的国家都有义务遵守其条款。|synonyms: signatory, contracting party, ratifier |antonyms: non-signatory
