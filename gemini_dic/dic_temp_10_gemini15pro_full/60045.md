educations:/ˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
教育（通常用单数形式）
The process of receiving or giving systematic instruction, especially at a school or university.  Although grammatically correct, "educations" is rarely used; "education" is generally preferred.  It could refer to multiple systems or types of education.
He received a good education. 他接受了良好的教育。
Her educations in both music and science allowed her to excel in both fields. 她在音乐和科学方面的教育使她能够在两个领域都表现出色。
近义词：schooling, training, instruction, upbringing
反义词：ignorance, illiteracy
词性：名词
