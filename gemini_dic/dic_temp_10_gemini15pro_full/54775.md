dispelling:/dɪˈspɛlɪŋ/
verb
驱散，消除
the act of making something go away, especially a feeling or belief
The rising sun dispelled the mist.  冉冉升起的太阳驱散了薄雾。
synonyms: eliminating, removing, banishing, clearing
antonyms: gathering, accumulating, collecting
