go-places:/ˈɡoʊˌpleɪsɪz/ | adj. |  有前途的，成功的，注定要成功的 | Likely to be successful and achieve great things. |  She's a bright young woman, she's definitely going places. 她是个聪明的年轻女性，她肯定前途无量。|  Example: He's got a lot of talent; he's going places. 他很有天赋，他将来一定会有所成就。| 近义词: promising, successful, destined for success | 反义词: unsuccessful, mediocre, stagnant | 词性: 形容词

词源:  This phrase originates from the idea of traveling to different and exciting locations, implying progress and achievement. It became metaphorical for career and personal advancement.
