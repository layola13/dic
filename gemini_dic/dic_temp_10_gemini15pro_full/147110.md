overlisten: /ˌoʊvərˈlɪsən/ | v. |过度聆听；听得过久|To listen to something for too long or too intently, often to the point of fatigue or obsession.|I think I overlistened to that album; I'm kind of sick of it now. 我想我过度聆听了那张专辑；我现在有点厌倦它了。|He overlistened to the news reports and became overly anxious. 他过度聆听了新闻报道，变得过分焦虑了。|近义词: obsess over, dwell on|反义词: ignore, neglect|词性: 动词


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "listen" (verb meaning "to pay attention to sound")
