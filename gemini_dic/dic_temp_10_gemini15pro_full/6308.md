amortizable:/əˈmɔːrtɪˌzeɪbl/ /əˈmɔːrtəˌzaɪbl/
adjective
可摊销的
Able to be amortized;  gradually written off over a period of time.
The cost of new equipment is amortizable over five years. 新设备的成本可以在五年内摊销。
Synonyms: depreciable, deductible, expensable
Antonyms: non-amortizable, non-depreciable
