litigating:/ˈlɪtɪɡeɪtɪŋ/
verb
提起诉讼；进行诉讼
engaging in a legal dispute; taking legal action
The company is litigating a patent infringement case.  该公司正在提起专利侵权诉讼。
They are litigating against their former employer. 他们在对他们的前雇主提起诉讼。
Synonyms: suing, contesting, disputing
Antonyms: mediating, arbitrating, settling, compromising
v.
