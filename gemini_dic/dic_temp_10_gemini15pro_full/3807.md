agnosias:/æɡˈnoʊziəz/| n.|失认症|Loss of the ability to recognize objects, persons, sounds, shapes, or smells while the specific sense is not defective nor is there any significant memory loss.|The patient exhibited visual agnosias, unable to recognize familiar faces. 病人表现出视觉失认症，无法识别熟悉的面孔。|近义词:inability to recognize, sensory deficit|反义词:recognition, perception|noun
