cremaillere:/krɛˈmɑːjɛər/ |n.| 齿条，齿轨 |A toothed rack, especially one engaging with a pinion to produce linear motion.| The cog railway used a cremaillere system to climb the steep mountain.  齿轨铁路使用齿条系统爬上陡峭的山坡。|Synonyms: rack, cogwheel railway, rack railway|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps smooth incline?)| noun

cremaillere:/krɛˈmɑːjɛər/ |n.| (指宴会上)逐步增加来宾人数| A formal dinner where guests are invited in successive groups.| The ambassador hosted a cremaillere, gradually introducing new diplomats throughout the evening. 大使举办了一场逐步增加来宾人数的宴会，整个晚上陆续介绍新外交官。|Synonyms: staged dinner, progressive dinner (loosely)|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps single-seating dinner?)| noun
