disheveled: /dɪˈʃevəld/ /dɪˈʃɛvəld/ | adj. | 乱蓬蓬的，凌乱的 |  (of a person's hair, clothes, or appearance) untidy; disordered.  | Her hair was disheveled and her clothes were torn. 她的头发乱蓬蓬的，衣服也破了。|  untidy, messy, rumpled, disordered, tousled, unkempt | tidy, neat, orderly, groomed | adjective

dishevel: /dɪˈʃevəl/ /dɪˈʃɛvəl/ | vt. | 使蓬乱，使凌乱 | To make (someone's hair or clothes) untidy. | The wind disheveled her hair. 风吹乱了她的头发。|  disorder, ruffle, tousle, mess up | tidy, neaten, arrange | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *descheveler, from des- (expressing reversal) + chevel (hair), from Latin capillus (hair).
