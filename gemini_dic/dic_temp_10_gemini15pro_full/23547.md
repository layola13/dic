brainlessnesses:/ˈbreɪnləsnəsɪz/
n.
愚蠢; 无脑; 缺乏智慧
The state of being brainless; extreme stupidity or foolishness.
His brainlessnesses led to a series of poor decisions. 他的愚蠢导致了一系列糟糕的决定。
近义词：stupidities, follies, idiocies
反义词：intelligence, wisdom, brilliance
词性：名词


词源：brainlessness (n.) + -es (plural suffix)
brainless (adj.) from brain (n.) + -less (suffix indicating absence)
