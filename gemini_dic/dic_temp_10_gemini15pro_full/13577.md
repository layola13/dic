attent:/əˈtɛnt/| adj.|专心的，注意的|Paying close attention to something; heedful; observant.|例句：She was very attent during the lecture. 她上课很专心。|近义词：attentive, heedful, observant|反义词：inattentive, negligent, careless|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *attentus*, past participle of *attendere* "to stretch toward, give heed to," from *ad-* "to" + *tendere* "to stretch."
