maxillae: /mækˈsɪliː/ (pl.)
noun
上颌骨（复数）
The jawbones of vertebrates, specifically the upper jaw in most.  In humans, the two fused maxillae form the upper jaw.
The surgeon repaired the patient's fractured maxillae.  外科医生修复了病人断裂的上颌骨。
maxilla (singular), jawbone, jaw
mandible (lower jaw)
noun (plural)
