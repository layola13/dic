folklorists:/ˈfoʊklɔːrɪsts/ | n. |民俗学家|Scholars who study folklore, which includes traditional customs, beliefs, stories, songs, and dances of a people.|Folklorists often travel to remote areas to collect stories and songs. 民俗学家经常前往偏远地区收集故事和歌曲。|Synonyms:  ethnologists,  anthropologists|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
