modus:/ˈmoʊdəs/ (US), /ˈməʊdəs/ (UK)| n. |方式，方法，模式|A particular way of doing something; a method or procedure.|例句：The modus operandi of the thief was to distract the shopkeeper while his accomplice stole the money.  小偷的作案手法是分散店主的注意力，而他的同伙则偷钱。

例句：Their preferred modus operandi is to threaten their victims online. 他们惯用的伎俩是在网上威胁受害者。

近义词：method, manner, way, mode, approach, procedure, process, technique, strategy, system|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
