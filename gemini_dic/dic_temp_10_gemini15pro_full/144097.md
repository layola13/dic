outsatisfies:/ˌaʊtsætɪsˈfaɪz/
verb
比...更令人满意
to satisfy more than someone or something else.
例句：The new phone outsatisfies the old one with its longer battery life.  这款新手机的电池续航时间比旧手机更令人满意。

例句：Her performance outsatisfied all the judges' expectations. 她的表演超出了所有评委的预期，让他们非常满意。

近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, outperform
反义词：disappoint, underperform
词性：动词


词源：
"out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding; surpassing") + "satisfy" (verb meaning "to meet the expectations or needs of")
