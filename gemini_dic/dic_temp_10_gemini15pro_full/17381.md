beadsnake:/ˈbiːdˌsneɪk/ | n. | 珠蛇，链蛇 (指某些鳞片排列似串珠的蛇) | A snake with scales that resemble beads. |  The herpetologist identified the reptile as a type of beadsnake. 爬虫学家将这种爬行动物鉴定为一种珠蛇。
