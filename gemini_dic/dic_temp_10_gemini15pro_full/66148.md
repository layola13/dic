exceptless:/ɪkˈsɛptləs/
adj.
无例外的，无一例外的
Without exception; admitting of no exception.
Her performance was exceptless, a true masterpiece. 她的表演无懈可击，是一部真正的杰作。
The rule is exceptless. 这条规则没有例外。
近义词：unfailing, flawless, impeccable, perfect
反义词：faulty, imperfect, flawed
词性：形容词

词源：
From "except" (meaning excluding) + "-less" (suffix indicating absence).  "Except" ultimately derives from the Latin "excipere" (to take out, exclude).
