marai:/ˈmɑːraɪ/ /məˈraɪ/ | n. | 沼泽，湿地 | A swamp or wetland, especially one dominated by reeds or papyrus.  It is often used in New Zealand English and refers to a type of wetland found there. | The marai provided a habitat for many native birds.  沼泽为许多本地鸟类提供了栖息地。

The pukeko thrives in the marai.  紫水鸡在沼泽地里繁衍生息。 | 近义词: swamp, marsh, wetland | 反义词: desert, upland | 词性: noun

词源: From Māori *marai, "swamp".
