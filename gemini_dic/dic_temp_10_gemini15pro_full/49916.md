denary:/ˈdiːnəri/| adj. n.|十进制的，十进位的；十进制数|Relating to or expressed in the base-ten number system, which uses the digits 0 through 9.|Computers operate using the binary system, but humans typically use the denary system.|近义词:decimal|反义词:binary|词性:adjective, noun

denary:/ˈdiːnəri/| n.|十进制数|A number expressed in the base-ten system.|The denary number 10 is equivalent to the binary number 1010.|近义词:decimal number|反义词:binary number|词性:noun
