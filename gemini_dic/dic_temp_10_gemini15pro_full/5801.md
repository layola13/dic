amaro:/əˈmɑːroʊ/ | n. | 阿玛罗，一种意大利苦味利口酒 | An Italian herbal liqueur, generally bitter or bittersweet, drunk as a digestif. |  I enjoy a glass of amaro after dinner. 我喜欢在晚餐后喝一杯阿玛罗。 |  例句：This amaro has notes of citrus and herbs.  这款阿玛罗带有柑橘和草本的味道。 | 近义词: digestif, liqueur | 反义词:  N/A (no direct antonym) | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Italian *amaro*,  masculine singular of adjective meaning "bitter,"  from Latin *amarus*.
