conceptionist:/kənˈsɛpʃənɪst/
n.|概念主义者；观念艺术家|A conceptionist is an artist or writer who emphasizes the idea or concept behind the work rather than the finished product.|The conceptionist explained that the meaning of the piece was more important than its aesthetic qualities.|近义词: conceptualist, ideologue|反义词: formalist|词性: noun
