antiskepticism:/ˌæntiˈskɛptɪsɪzəm/
|n.|反怀疑论|The philosophical position that opposes skepticism, arguing for the possibility of knowledge or justified belief.|His book is a powerful defense of antiskepticism. 他的这本书强有力地捍卫了反怀疑论。|近义词:dogmatism, foundationalism|反义词:skepticism|词性:名词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposing (from Greek)
skepticism (noun): a doubting or questioning attitude or state of mind; doubt concerning basic religious or philosophical principles (from Greek "skeptikos" - "inquiring")
-ism (suffix):  forming nouns denoting action, practice, system, doctrine, etc.
