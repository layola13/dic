amphiscii:/æmˈfɪskiˌaɪ/
noun
两回归线之间居民
People dwelling between the tropics, whose shadows fall north at noon for one part of the year, and south for the other.
The amphiscii experience both northward and southward shadows throughout the year due to the sun's changing position.
近义词:  tropical dwellers
反义词:  periscii, heteroscii


Etymology->
From Greek ἀμφίσκιοι (amphískioi), from ἀμφί (amphí, “on both sides”) + σκιά (skiá, “shadow”).
