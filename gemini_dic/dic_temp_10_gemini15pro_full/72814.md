folding-in:/ˈfoʊldɪŋ ɪn/ | n./v. | 折叠；混合 | The act of gently combining a light mixture into a heavier one, often used in baking. | Gently fold in the whipped cream.  轻轻地把打发的奶油拌进去。| Synonyms: incorporate, blend, mix | Antonyms: separate, divide | noun/verb

folding-in:/ˈfoʊldɪŋ ɪn/ | n./v. | 内折; 向内折叠 | The act of folding something inwards. | The origami instructions called for folding in the corners. 折纸说明要求把角向内折叠。| Synonyms: tuck, turn in | Antonyms: unfold, open out | noun/verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "fold" (verb) + "in" (adverb). "Fold" comes from Old English *fealdan, from Proto-Germanic *falþaną. "In" comes from Old English "in," from Proto-Germanic *in.
