hamburgs:/ˈhæmbɜːrɡz/
noun
汉堡包（复数）
Hamburgers (plural).  Ground beef patties, usually served in a bun with various toppings.
I ordered two hamburgs with extra cheese. 我点了两个加额外芝士的汉堡包。
synonyms: burgers, hamburger patties
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a food item.  Could consider healthier alternatives like salads or veggie burgers)
词性: 名词
