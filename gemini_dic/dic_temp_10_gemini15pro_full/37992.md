complice:/ˈkɑːmplɪs/ /ˈkʌmplɪs/| n. |共犯，帮凶|A person who helps another commit a crime or wrongdoing.|例句：He was arrested as an accomplice to the murder.  他因作为谋杀案的帮凶而被捕。

例句：She was his unwitting accomplice in the robbery.  她在抢劫案中成了他不知情的帮凶。

近义词：accessory, abettor, collaborator, partner in crime|反义词:opponent, adversary|词性:noun
