drawstrings:/ˈdrɔːstrɪŋz/
noun
束带，拉绳，抽绳
Cords or strings threaded through a casing or hem, used to tighten or close an opening, typically in clothing or bags.
The sweatpants have drawstrings at the waist. 这条运动裤的腰部有束带。
synonyms: tie-strings, cords, laces
antonyms: zippers, buttons
n.

drawstring:/ˈdrɔːstrɪŋ/
adjective
有抽绳的
Having a drawstring.
This is a drawstring bag. 这是一个有抽绳的袋子。
synonyms: tie-string, laced
antonyms: zippered, buttoned
adj.


ETYMOLOGY->

From "draw" (meaning to pull or tighten) + "string" (a thin cord). The word refers to the action of drawing the string to close or tighten something.
