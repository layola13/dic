hopefulness:/ˈhoʊpfəlnəs/| n. |希望；乐观|The quality of being full of hope; expectation of something desired.|Her hopefulness was infectious, inspiring everyone around her. 她充满希望的精神很有感染力，鼓舞了周围的每一个人。|近义词：optimism, positivity, expectancy|反义词：pessimism, despair, hopelessness|词性:名词
Etymology: From "hopeful" + "-ness". "Hopeful" comes from "hope" (from Old English "hopian") + "-ful" (a suffix meaning "full of").
