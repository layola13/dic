curvaceously: /kɜːrˈveɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 曲线优美地，丰满地 | Having a full, rounded figure with prominent curves. | She walked curvaceously across the room, drawing everyone's attention. 她婀娜多姿地走过房间，吸引了所有人的目光。|  The dress accentuated her curvaceous figure. 这条裙子突出了她优美的曲线。 | 近义词: voluptuously, full-figured | 反义词: slimly, thinly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From curve + -aceous + -ly.  "Curve" originates from the Latin "curvus" meaning "bent" or "crooked." "-aceous" is a suffix meaning "of the nature of" or "resembling," and "-ly" is an adverbial suffix.
