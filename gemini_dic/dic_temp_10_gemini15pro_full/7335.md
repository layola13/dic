anharmonic:/ˌæn.hɑːrˈmɒn.ɪk/ | adj. |非谐的，非调和的 |Deviating from harmonic progression or motion; not harmonic.  Relating to or characterized by anharmonicity. |The anharmonic oscillator is a more realistic model than the simple harmonic oscillator for describing the vibrations of atoms in a molecule. 分子中原子振动的非谐振子模型比简谐振子模型更真实。|The anharmonic overtones contributed to the richness of the instrument's sound. 非谐泛音丰富了乐器的声音。|近义词:inharmonic, nonlinear|反义词:harmonic|词性:形容词

词源:
From an- (not) + harmonic.  Ultimately from Greek an- (not) + harmonikos (harmonic).
