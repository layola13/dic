chapelet:/ˈʃæplɪt/ /ˈtʃæplət/| n. |念珠，玫瑰经，一连串|A string of beads, especially one used for counting prayers, like a rosary; also, a sequence or series of similar things.|She fingered the chapelet as she prayed. 她祈祷时拨动着念珠。|近义词：rosary, beads, string, chain, series, sequence|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "disunity" if referring to a series)
词性: 名词
