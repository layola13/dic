decelerate: /diːˈsɛləreɪt/ (US) /diːˈsɛləreɪt/ (UK)| v. | 减速，降低速度 | To reduce the speed of something; to slow down. | The car decelerated as it approached the traffic light. 汽车在接近红绿灯时减速了。| The company's growth has decelerated in recent months. 公司的增长近几个月来有所放缓。| synonyms: slow down, brake, retard | antonyms: accelerate, speed up | verb
