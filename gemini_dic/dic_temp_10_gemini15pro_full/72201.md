flora:/ˈflɔːrə/ (US), /ˈflɔːrə/ (UK)| n. |植物群，植物区系|Plants of a particular region or period.|The flora of the Amazon rainforest is incredibly diverse. 亚马逊雨林的植物群极其多样。|The flora of this island is unique.  这个岛的植物群很独特。|近义词：vegetation, plant life|反义词:fauna|noun
