livingly:/ˈlɪvɪŋli/| adv.|活泼地，生动地|In a manner full of life and vitality; vividly.|She described the scene livingly, bringing it to life for her listeners. 她生动地描述了那个场景，使之在听众面前栩栩如生。|近义词：vividly, animatedly, spiritedly|反义词：dully, lifelessly|词性:副词

词源: From "living" (adjective) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Old English "libban" (to live).
