corruptors:/kəˈrʌptərz/| n. |腐败者，堕落者，贿赂者|People who engage in corrupt practices, such as bribery, extortion, or embezzlement.  These individuals use their power or influence to gain an unfair advantage, often at the expense of others or the public good. |The corruptors were brought to justice and sentenced to prison. 这些腐败分子被绳之以法并被判处监禁。|Synonyms:  bribers, grafters, defrauders, swindlers, criminals|Antonyms: reformers, philanthropists, altruists|Noun
