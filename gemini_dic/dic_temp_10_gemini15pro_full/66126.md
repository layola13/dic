except:/ɪkˈsept/ (US) /ɪkˈsɛpt/ (UK)| prep. conj. v. |除了，除…之外|  Not including; other than. |例句：Everyone went except me. 除了我，大家都去了。|近义词：but, besides, excluding, barring, save, other than|反义词:including|词性:介词，连词，动词

except:/ɪkˈsept/ (US) /ɪkˈsɛpt/ (UK)| prep. conj. v. |除外; 反对|To object to; to take exception to. |例句：He excepted to the judge's ruling. 他反对法官的裁决。|近义词：object to, oppose, dispute, challenge|反义词:accept, approve, agree|词性:介词，连词，动词

PHRASE->
except for = 除了…之外
except that = 除此之外
except when = 除了…的时候

no exceptions = 没有例外

词源：From Old French excepter, from Latin exceptāre (“to take out, exclude”), from excipere (“to take out”), from ex (“out of”) + capere (“to take”).
