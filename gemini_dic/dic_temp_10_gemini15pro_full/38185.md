comptroller:/kənˈtroʊlər/ (US), /kəmˈtrəʊlə(r)/ (UK)| n. |主计长，审计官，财务总监|A person who checks the financial accounts of a company or government organization.|The comptroller reported a significant increase in profits this quarter. 本季度主计长报告利润大幅增长。|The city comptroller is responsible for overseeing the city's finances. 市财务总监负责监督市政财政。|Synonyms: auditor, controller, accountant|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
