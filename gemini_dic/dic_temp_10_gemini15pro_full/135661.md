nonpreferences:/ˌnɑːnˈprɛfrənsɪz/
n.
非偏好，不偏好，无偏好
Lack of preference; the state of not having a preference between options.
Consumers expressed their nonpreferences for artificial sweeteners. 消费者表达了他们对人造甜味剂的非偏好。
近义词:indifference, apathy
反义词:preferences, favorites
词性:名词
