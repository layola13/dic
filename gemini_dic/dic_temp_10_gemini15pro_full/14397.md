autosuggestible:/ˌɔːtəsəˈdʒɛstəbəl/ | adj. | 易受暗示的，易被他人意见左右的 | Readily accepting suggestions from others; easily influenced. |  例句：Hypnosis works best on autosuggestible people.  催眠对易受暗示的人最有效。

例句：He's so autosuggestible that he'll believe anything you tell him. 他很容易受暗示，你跟他说什么他都信。


近义词：suggestible, gullible, impressionable, susceptible | 反义词：resistant, skeptical, critical | 词性：形容词

词源: auto- (self) + suggestible (capable of being influenced by suggestion)
