misdelivered:/ˌmɪsdɪˈlɪvərd/
|adj.|投递错误的，送错的|Delivered to the wrong place or person.|The package was misdelivered to the wrong address. 包裹被误送到错误的地址了。|
Synonyms: misdirected, wrongly delivered, incorrectly addressed|Antonyms: correctly delivered, properly addressed|Adjective


misdelivered:/ˌmɪsdɪˈlɪvərd/
|v.|投递错误，送错 (past participle of misdeliver)|To deliver something to the wrong place or person.|The postman misdelivered our mail to the neighbor's house. 邮递员把我们的邮件错送到邻居家了。|
Synonyms: misdirect, wrongly deliver, incorrectly address|Antonyms: correctly deliver, properly addressed|Verb (past participle)
