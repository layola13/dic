modish:/ˈmoʊdɪʃ/ (US), /ˈməʊdɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|时髦的，流行的|Conforming to or following what is currently fashionable and stylish.  Characterized by being in the current fashion; stylish. |Her modish attire turned heads at the party. 她的时髦装扮在派对上引人注目。|Synonyms: fashionable, stylish, trendy, chic, contemporary|Antonyms: unfashionable, outdated, old-fashioned, antiquated, passé|adjective
