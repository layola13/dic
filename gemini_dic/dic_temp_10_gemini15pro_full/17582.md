beauteousness:/ˈbjuːtiəsnəs/| n. |美丽，漂亮|The quality of being beautiful; loveliness.|例句：The beauteousness of the sunset captivated everyone. 夕阳的美丽迷住了所有人。

例句：She was struck by the beauteousness of the landscape. 她被景色的美丽所震撼。


近义词：beauty, loveliness, attractiveness, handsomeness, prettiness|反义词：ugliness, unattractiveness, homeliness|词性：名词

词源: From Middle English *beauteousnes*, from beauteous + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin bellus "beautiful".
