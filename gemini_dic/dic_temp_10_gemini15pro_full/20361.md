bisque:/bɪsk/| n.|浓汤，贝类浓汤|A thick, creamy soup, typically made with shellfish.|例句：The lobster bisque was rich and flavorful. 龙虾浓汤味道浓郁。

例句：She ordered the tomato bisque as a starter. 她点了一份番茄浓汤作为开胃菜。|近义词：soup, chowder, potage|反义词:|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bisque*, perhaps from *Biscaye* (Bay of Biscay), though the connection is uncertain.  Another theory suggests it's related to the word "bis-cuire" (twice cooked), referring to the way shellfish are sometimes cooked twice in making bisque.
