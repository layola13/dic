partnership:/ˈpɑːrtnərʃɪp/ | n. | 伙伴关系，合作，合伙企业 |  A relationship between two or more people, organizations, or countries who agree to work together. |  We formed a partnership to develop the new software. 我们建立了合作关系来开发新的软件。| synonyms: collaboration, alliance, association, cooperation | antonyms: rivalry, competition, conflict | noun


partnership:/ˈpɑːtnərʃɪp/ | n. | 合伙 | The state of being a partner. |  He dissolved the partnership after a disagreement.  在意见不合后，他解除了合作关系。| synonyms: union, affiliation | antonyms: separation, disunion | noun
