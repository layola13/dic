ETYMOLOGY->
From 'make' (Old English 'macian' - to make, construct) + 'believe' (Old English 'belīefan' - to believe).  The compound word emerged in the late 16th century, initially as a verb meaning "to feign, pretend." The noun and adjective forms developed later.
