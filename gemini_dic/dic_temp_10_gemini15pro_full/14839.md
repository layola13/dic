axised:/ˈæksɪzd/
adjective
有轴的，以轴为中心的
Having an axis or multiple axes; centered around an axis or axes.
The axised design of the machine allows for precise rotations.  这台机器的轴心设计允许精确旋转。
The telescope is axised to track the movement of celestial bodies.  望远镜以轴为中心追踪天体的运动。
近义词: axial, centered, aligned
反义词: off-center, unaligned
词性: adjective
词源: From "axis" + "-ed" suffix, indicating having or characterized by.
