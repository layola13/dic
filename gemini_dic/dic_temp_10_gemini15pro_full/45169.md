cuffed:/kʌft/
adjective
戴着手铐的; 袖口有边的
restrained with handcuffs. having cuffs.
The police officer led the cuffed suspect to the police car.  警察把戴着手铐的嫌疑人带到了警车里。
The model wore a stylish cuffed shirt.  模特穿着一件时髦的带袖口的衬衫。
Synonyms: manacled, shackled, restrained
Antonyms: unrestrained, free, liberated
adjective

cuffed:/kʌft/
verb (past tense and past participle of *cuff*)
(用手掌)轻拍，轻打
hit (someone) lightly with an open hand.
He playfully cuffed his friend on the shoulder.  他开玩笑地拍了拍朋友的肩膀。
She cuffed the dog gently when it misbehaved.  当狗行为不端时，她轻轻地拍了一下它。
Synonyms: slap, pat, strike
Antonyms: caress, stroke, pet
verb
