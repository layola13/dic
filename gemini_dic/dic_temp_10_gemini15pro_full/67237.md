expugnable:/ɪkˈspʌɡnəbl/
adjective
可攻克的，可征服的，可被驱逐的
able to be taken by force; conquerable.
例句：The castle, once thought impregnable, proved expugnable after a long siege.  曾经被认为坚不可摧的城堡，经过长时间的围攻后，证明是可以攻克的。

例句：The general believed the enemy's defenses were expugnable with the right strategy. 将军相信，只要有正确的策略，敌人的防御是可以攻克的。

近义词：conquerable, vulnerable, assailable
反义词：invincible, impregnable, unassailable
词性：adjective
词源：From Late Latin expugnabilis, from expugnare ‘to take by assault’, from ex- ‘out’ + pugnare ‘to fight’.
