nonfragileness:/ˌnɒnfrəˈdʒɪlətnəs/ (US) /ˌnɒnfrəˈdʒaɪlnəs/ (UK)| n. |非脆弱性，反脆弱性|The quality of not being fragile; the ability to thrive and improve under stress or disorder.  This goes beyond resilience, which implies bouncing back to the original state. Nonfragileness suggests getting stronger and better in the face of adversity.|The book "Antifragile" explores the concept of nonfragileness in depth.  《反脆弱》这本书深入探讨了非脆弱性的概念。|Synonyms: robustness, resilience, antifragility|Antonyms: fragility, vulnerability, weakness|Noun
