nonauthenticity:/ˌnɒnˌɔːθenˈtɪsɪti/ /ˌnɑːnˌɔːθenˈtɪsəti/
noun
非真实性，不真实，伪造
The state or quality of not being authentic or genuine.
The painting was deemed a fake due to its nonauthenticity.  这幅画由于其非真实性被认为是赝品。
The nonauthenticity of the document led to its rejection.  这份文件的非真实性导致它被拒绝。
falsity, spuriousness, inauthenticity, ungenuineness
authenticity, genuineness
