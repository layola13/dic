democratising:/dɪˈmɒkrətaɪzɪŋ/
v.
使民主化，使大众化
making something accessible to everyone, removing restrictions based on class, wealth, etc.
The internet has democratised access to information. 互联网使获取信息大众化。
synonyms: popularizing, making accessible, opening up, spreading, broadening
antonyms: restricting, limiting, monopolizing, controlling
verb

democratisation:/dɪˌmɒkrətɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
民主化，大众化
the action of making something accessible to everyone, removing restrictions based on class, wealth, etc.
The democratisation of education is a key goal for many developing countries. 教育民主化是许多发展中国家的主要目标。
synonyms: popularization, accessibility, liberalization
antonyms: restriction, limitation, control
noun
