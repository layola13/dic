dolomite:/ˈdɒləmaɪt/ (UK) /ˈdoʊləˌmaɪt/ (US)| n.|白云石；白云岩|A carbonate mineral having the chemical composition CaMg(CO3)2.  A sedimentary rock composed primarily of the mineral dolomite.|The Dolomites are a mountain range in northeastern Italy. 白云石山脉是意大利东北部的一个山脉。|Synonyms: dolostone, magnesium limestone|Antonyms: N/A|noun


dolomitic:/ˌdɒləˈmɪtɪk/ (UK) /ˌdoʊləˈmɪtɪk/ (US)| adj.|白云质的；含白云石的|Of, relating to, or consisting of dolomite.|The soil in this region is dolomitic. 这个地区的土壤是白云质的。|Synonyms: magnesian|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
