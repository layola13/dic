affeeror:/əˈfiːərər/| n.|估价人；仲裁人；鉴定人|An affeeror was a medieval official who moderated the amercement imposed by a court leet.|The affeeror reduced the fine to a more reasonable sum. 估价人将罚款减少到更合理的数额。|近义词：arbitrator, assessor, valuer|反义词: N/A|noun
Etymology->
From Anglo-French *affereur, from affeurer "to fix the value of, assess," from a- "to" + feur "price, value," from Latin forum "marketplace."
