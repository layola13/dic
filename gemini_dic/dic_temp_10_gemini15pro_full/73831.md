formidable:/ˈfɔːrmɪdəbl/ (US) /ˈfɔːmɪdəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|令人敬畏的；可怕的；艰难的|Inspiring fear or respect through being impressively large, powerful, intense, or capable. |例句：They faced a formidable opponent. 他们面对一个强大的对手。

例句：Climbing Mount Everest is a formidable challenge. 攀登珠穆朗玛峰是一项艰巨的挑战。|近义词：daunting, intimidating, challenging, impressive, powerful|反义词：weak, easy, insignificant, manageable|词性:形容词
