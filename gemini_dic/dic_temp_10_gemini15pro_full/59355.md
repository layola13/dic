earmark: /ˈɪərmɑːrk/ (US) /ˈɪəmɑːk/ (UK)
| vt.| 指定…作特定用途 |To designate (something, typically funds or resources) for a particular purpose.|  The committee earmarked the funds for disaster relief. 委员会将这些资金指定用于赈灾。 | They earmarked the profits for research and development. 他们将利润指定用于研发。 | synonyms: allocate, designate, reserve, set aside | antonyms: withhold, retain, general fund | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "ear mark," originally referring to a mark cut into the ear of an animal to identify its owner.  Later, the term broadened to mean any distinguishing characteristic and then to funds or resources set aside for a specific purpose.
