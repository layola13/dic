freightless:/ˈfreɪtləs/
adjective
无货物的；空载的
Carrying no freight; without freight.
The freightless ship returned to port.  空载的船返回了港口。
The truck made a freightless journey back to the depot. 卡车空载返回了仓库。
近义词:empty, unloaded, unladen
反义词:laden, loaded, full
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From freight + -less.  Freight (meaning cargo) comes from Middle Dutch *vrecht, from vreden "to load, equip".  The suffix -less is a common English suffix meaning "without".
