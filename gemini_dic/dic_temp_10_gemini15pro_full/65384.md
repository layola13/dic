eupatorine:/juːpəˈtɔːriːn/ | n. | 蛇鞭菊素;泽兰素 | A flavone found in certain plants of the genus Eupatorium.  It is a crystalline substance that is used in some traditional medicines. |  Eupatorine has been isolated from leaves of  *Eupatorium cannabinum*.  蛇鞭菊素已从大麻叶中分离出来。| synonyms: ayapin;6-methoxyapigenin | antonyms: None | noun
