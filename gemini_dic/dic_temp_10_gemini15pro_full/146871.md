overinflations: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度膨胀；恶性通货膨胀 | The act of inflating something excessively, especially the currency of a country.  It refers to a period of rapid and uncontrolled increase in the general price level of goods and services in an economy. |  The Weimar Republic experienced crippling overinflations after World War I.  魏玛共和国在第一次世界大战后经历了严重的恶性通货膨胀。 | hyperinflation, runaway inflation | deflation, disinflation | noun
