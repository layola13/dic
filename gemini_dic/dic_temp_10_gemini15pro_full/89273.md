homocercy:/ˌhɒməˈsɜːsi/ /ˌhoʊməˈsɜːrsi/| n. |同尾性|The condition of having a symmetrical tail fin, where the upper and lower lobes are equal in size and shape, typical of most teleost fish.|Most bony fishes exhibit homocercy.  大多数硬骨鱼表现出同尾性。|Synonyms: homocercal tail|Antonyms: heterocercy|noun
