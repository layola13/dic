moky:/ˈmoʊki/| adj.|烟雾弥漫的，冒烟的|filled with or smelling of smoke.  Resembling smoke, especially in color or texture.|The room was moky after the fire. 房间里火灾后烟雾弥漫。|近义词：smoky, hazy, foggy|反义词：clear, bright|词性:形容词

词源:  From Middle English *moky, from Old English *mociġ, from Proto-Germanic *maukaz. Cognate with West Frisian mokich (“smoky”), Dutch mokerig (“muggy, sultry”), German muckig (“musty, fusty”). Related to muck.
