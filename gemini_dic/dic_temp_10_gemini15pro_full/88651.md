hoarders:/ˈhɔːrdərz/ (US), /ˈhɔːdəz/ (UK)| n.|囤积者|People who compulsively accumulate and save possessions, often to the point of excess and clutter.|The house was filled with junk, revealing that the owners were hoarders.|近义词：collectors, accumulators, savers|反义词: spenders, minimalists|Noun
