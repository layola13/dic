overthrowers:/ˌoʊvərˈθroʊərz/
n. |推翻者，颠覆者|People or groups who overthrow a government or leader. |The overthrowers quickly took control of the capital city.| 近义词: rebels, revolutionaries, insurgents, usurpers | 反义词: loyalists, supporters, defenders |词性:名词

overthrow:/ˌoʊvərˈθroʊ/
v. |推翻，颠覆|To remove forcibly from power. |The rebels overthrew the government. |近义词: topple, depose, unseat | 反义词: establish, support, uphold |词性:动词
