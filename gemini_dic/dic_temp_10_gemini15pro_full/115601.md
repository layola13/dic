marbling:/ˈmɑːrblɪŋ/ /ˈmɑːrbəlɪŋ/| n.|大理石花纹，大理石纹理，(书边等)洒金，(肉类)脂肪纹理|The marbling in the steak was exquisite, a sign of high quality.  牛排的大理石花纹非常漂亮，表明它品质很高。|Synonyms: veining, mottling, striation|Antonyms: plainness, uniformity|noun

marbling:/ˈmɑːrblɪŋ/ /ˈmɑːrbəlɪŋ/| adj.|大理石花纹的，大理石纹理的|(Rarely used as an adjective)  很少用作形容词|Synonyms: marbled, veined, mottled|Antonyms: plain, uniform|adjective
