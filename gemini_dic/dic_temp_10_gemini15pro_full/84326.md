halfclearly: /ˌhæfˈklɪrli/ | adv. | 半清晰地，不太清楚地 |  A combination of "half" and "clearly," meaning not fully or completely clear. It suggests something is expressed or perceived with some ambiguity or lack of full understanding. | The instructions were halfclearly written, leading to confusion.  说明书写得半清晰，导致了混乱。| 近义词: vaguely, obscurely, indistinctly | 反义词: clearly, distinctly, lucidly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
half: From Old English *healf, from Proto-Germanic *halbaz.
clearly: From clear + -ly. Clear from Old French cler from Latin clarus.
