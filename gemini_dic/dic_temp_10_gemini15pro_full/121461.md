misjudger:/ˌmɪsˈdʒʌdʒər/
n.
误判者，判断错误的人
A person who misjudges; one who forms a wrong or unfair estimate or opinion.
He proved to be a misjudger of character, trusting someone who later betrayed him. 他证明了自己对性格判断有误，相信了一个后来背叛他的人。

近义词:
misinterpreter, blunderer, estimator
反义词:
good judge, discerner, interpreter

词性:
noun
