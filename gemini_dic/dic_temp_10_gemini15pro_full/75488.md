frustum:/ˈfrʌstəm/| n. |平截头体；截头锥体；圆台|The frustum of a cone or pyramid is the part that remains when a top section is cut off by a plane parallel to the base. |例句：The lampshade was shaped like the frustum of a cone.  灯罩的形状像一个圆台。

例句：The architect incorporated a frustum into the building's design.  建筑师将一个平截头体融入建筑设计中。|近义词：section, part, portion|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *frustum*, neuter of *frustus*  "piece, bit, fragment,"  past participle of *frangere* "to break."
