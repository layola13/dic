dehumidifier:/ˌdiːhjuːˈmɪdɪfaɪər/ | n. | 除湿机 | A device that removes excess moisture from the air. |  The dehumidifier helped to dry out the damp basement. 除湿机帮助弄干了潮湿的地下室。

The dehumidifier is essential during the humid summer months.  除湿机在潮湿的夏季是必不可少的。| 近义词: dehumidifying machine | 反义词: humidifier | 词性: noun
