cementitious:/ˌsɪmənˈtɪʃəs/
adjective
水泥的；胶结的
Relating to or resembling cement; having cement-like properties.
The cementitious material used in this project is a blend of Portland cement and fly ash.  这个项目中使用的水泥质材料是波特兰水泥和粉煤灰的混合物。
The mixture had a cementitious quality, binding the other materials together. 这种混合物具有胶结性，可以将其他材料粘合在一起。
Synonyms: adhesive, binding, cohesive
Antonyms: non-binding, loose
