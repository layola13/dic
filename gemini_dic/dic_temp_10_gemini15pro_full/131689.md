noncondemnations:/ˌnɑːnkɑːndɛmˈneɪʃənz/ | n. | 不谴责，不责备 | The absence of condemnations; a state of not being condemned or judged negatively. |  His speech was notable for its noncondemnations of the opposing party. 他的演讲因没有谴责反对党而引人注目。|  His policy of noncondemnations was seen as weakness by some. 他不谴责的政策被一些人视为软弱。| Near synonyms: forgiveness, pardon, absolution, exoneration, acquittal | Antonyms: condemnation, censure, reproach, reprimand, rebuke | noun
