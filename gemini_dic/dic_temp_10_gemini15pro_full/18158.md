beingness:/ˈbiːɪŋnɪs/
n.
存在；存在状态；本质
The state or quality of having existence; the fact of existing.
She radiated a sense of calm beingness. 她散发着一种平静的存在感。
existence, presence, essence
nonexistence, absence
