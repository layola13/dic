boronic:/bəˈrɒnɪk/
adjective|硼的；含有硼酸基的|Relating to or containing boronic acid or its derivatives.
Boronic acids are important in organic synthesis. 硼酸在有机合成中很重要。
Boronic esters are often used in Suzuki coupling reactions. 硼酸酯常用于铃木偶联反应。
