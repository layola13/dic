hydropsy:/ˈhaɪdrɒpsi/ (US) /ˈhaɪdrɑːpsi/ (UK)| n. | 水肿，积水|An abnormal accumulation of serous fluid in the tissues or in a body cavity.| The doctor diagnosed the patient with hydropsy. 医生诊断病人患有水肿。|The horse suffered from hydropsy and its legs were swollen. 这匹马患有水肿，腿部肿胀。 |近义词：edema, swelling|反义词: dehydration|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *hydrops*, from Ancient Greek ὕδρωψ (húdōps, “dropsy”), from ὕδωρ (húdōr, “water”) + ὤψ (ṓps, “face, appearance”). Literally "watery appearance".
