banquetings:/ˈbæŋkwɪtɪŋz/
noun
宴会, 宴席
The act of feasting; a grand meal or feast.  Specifically, the plural form suggests multiple instances of feasting or elaborate meals.
The king held several banquetings throughout the year to celebrate various victories. 国王一年中举行了多次宴会来庆祝各种胜利。
The banquetings were legendary for their extravagance. 这些宴会以其奢华而闻名。
近义词: feasts, celebrations, festivities
反义词: fasts, deprivations
词性: Noun (plural)

Etymology:
From Middle English *banquetyng, from Old French *banqueter, from banc "bench, table."  Originally referred to a small meal eaten on a bench, but evolved to signify a lavish feast.
