genesises: /ˈdʒɛnɪsiːz/
n.
起源，创始；创世纪
The genesises of the universe is still a mystery. 宇宙的起源仍然是一个谜。
The book of Genesis tells the story of creation.  创世纪讲述了创造的故事。
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin genesis, from Greek, ‘origin’, ‘creation’.
Synonyms: origins, beginnings, inceptions, commencements
Antonyms: ends, conclusions, terminations
noun
