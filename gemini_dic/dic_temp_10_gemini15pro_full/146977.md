overjoyfulnesses: /ˌoʊvərˈdʒɔɪfəlnəsɪz/
noun
极度喜悦，狂喜
The state of being extremely joyful; overwhelming happiness.
Her overjoyfulnesses at the news were evident in her beaming smile and excited chatter. 她对这个消息欣喜若狂，这在她灿烂的笑容和兴奋的喋喋不休中显而易见。
The reunion with her family after so many years brought on waves of overjoyfulnesses. 多年后与家人团聚，她欣喜若狂。
Synonyms: ecstasies, raptures, transports
Antonyms: miseries, sorrows, despairs
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + joyfulness + -es (plural suffix). Over- is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." Joyfulness is derived from joyful, which comes from joy + -ful (suffix meaning "full of"). Joy ultimately comes from Old French joie, from Latin gaudia.
