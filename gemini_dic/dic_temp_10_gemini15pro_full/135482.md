nonphilosopher:/ˌnɒn fɪˈlɒsəfər/ /ˌnɑːn fɪˈlɑːsəfər/
n.
非哲学家；不研究哲学的人
Someone who is not a philosopher; a person who does not study or practice philosophy.
Many nonphilosophers find the subject intimidating. 许多非哲学家觉得这门学科令人生畏。
layperson, non-specialist
