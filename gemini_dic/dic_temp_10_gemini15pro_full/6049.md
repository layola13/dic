amendment:/əˈmendmənt/ | n. | 修改，修正，修正案 |  A change or addition made to a law, document, or constitution. | The First Amendment to the US Constitution guarantees freedom of speech. 美国宪法第一修正案保障言论自由。| synonyms: alteration, modification, revision | antonyms:  preservation, retention | noun

amendment:/əˈmendmənt/ | n. | 改进，改善 |  An improvement or correction. |  The amendment to the policy improved employee satisfaction.  这项政策的修改提高了员工满意度。 | synonyms: improvement, betterment, enhancement | antonyms: worsening, deterioration | noun
