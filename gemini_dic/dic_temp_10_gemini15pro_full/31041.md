chamberlet: /ˈtʃeɪmbərˌlɛt/
n.
小房间，小卧室
A small chamber, especially a bedroom.
The chamberlet was cozy and perfect for a single guest.  小房间很舒适，非常适合一位客人入住。
The prince retired to his chamberlet for the night. 王子回到他的小卧室过夜。

近义词: cubicle, small room, closet
反义词: hall, large room
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *chambrelette, diminutive of chambre ("chamber"), from Latin camera ("vault, arched roof").
