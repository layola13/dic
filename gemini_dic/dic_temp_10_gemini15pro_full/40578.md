conundrum:/kəˈnʌndrəm/| n.|难题，谜题|A confusing and difficult problem or question.|例句：The scientists were faced with a conundrum they couldn't solve. 科学家们面临着一个他们无法解决的难题。

例句：She was in a conundrum about whether to accept the job offer or not. 她对于是否接受这份工作邀请感到很为难。

近义词：puzzle, riddle, enigma, problem, dilemma|反义词:solution, answer|词性:noun
