blowing:/ˈbloʊɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbləʊɪŋ/ (UK)| v. adj.|吹，刮，吹气，爆炸|The wind is blowing hard. 风刮得很大。|近义词：gusting, puffing, blasting|反义词:sucking, inhaling|verb/adjective

blowing:/ˈbloʊɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbləʊɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|吹，刮，吹气|The blowing of the wind was constant. 风不停地吹。|近义词：gust, puff, blast|反义词:suction, inhalation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *blāwian, from Proto-Germanic *blēaną, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰlē- (“to blow, swell”). Cognate with Dutch blazen (“to blow”), German blasen (“to blow”), Latin flare (“to blow”).

blow up:  爆炸；充气 (The bomb blew up. / He blew up the balloons.)

blow away:  吹走; 使大为惊讶 (The wind blew away the leaves. / I was blown away by his performance.)
blow one's mind:  令人兴奋不已 (The concert blew my mind.)
