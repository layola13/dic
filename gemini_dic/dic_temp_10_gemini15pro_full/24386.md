brinishnesses:/ˈbraɪnɪʃnəsɪz/
n.
咸度；含盐量
the quality or degree of being brinish; saltiness.
The brinishnesses of the different sea samples varied considerably. 不同海水样本的咸度差异很大。
The brinishnesses of the Dead Sea is remarkably high. 死海的盐度非常高。
salinity, saltiness
freshness, blandness
