electro-oculography:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˌɒkjʊˈlɒɡrəfi/ | n. |眼电图描记法；眼动电流描记法 | The technique of recording eye movements by measuring the corneo-retinal potential difference.  It is a method used to assess eye movement and diagnose certain eye conditions. | Electro-oculography is used to diagnose retinal diseases. 眼电图描记法用于诊断视网膜疾病。 | Electro-oculography helped detect early signs of macular degeneration in the patient. 眼电图描记法帮助发现了病人黄斑变性的早期迹象。 | synonym: EOG | antonym: N/A | noun
