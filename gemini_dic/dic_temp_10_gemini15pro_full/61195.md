elutriation:/ɪˌluːtriˈeɪʃən/
n.
淘析；洗净；漂洗
The process of separating particles based on their size, shape, and density by washing them with a stream of fluid.
The archaeologist used elutriation to separate the tiny pottery shards from the soil. 考古学家使用淘析法将微小的陶器碎片从土壤中分离出来。
The sample was cleaned by elutriation to remove fine dust particles.  样品通过淘析法清洗以去除细小的灰尘颗粒。
separation, washing, purification
contamination
noun
