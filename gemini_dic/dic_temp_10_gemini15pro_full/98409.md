insomnious:/ɪnˈsɒmniəs/ /ɪnˈsɑːmniəs/| adj.|失眠的，患失眠症的|Characterized by or suffering from insomnia; sleepless.|He spent another insomnious night tossing and turning. 他又度过了一个辗转反侧、难以入眠的夜晚。|近义词：sleepless, restless, wakeful|反义词：sleepy, somnolent|词性:形容词

词源: Late 16th century: from Latin insomnis ‘sleepless’ (from in- ‘not’ + somnus ‘sleep’) + -ous.
