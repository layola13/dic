anti-idealistic:/ˌæntiˌaɪdiːəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
反理想主义的
Opposed to or rejecting idealism; characterized by a focus on practical considerations and skepticism about noble or elevated goals.
His anti-idealistic approach to politics focused on achieving tangible results rather than pursuing abstract principles. 他反理想主义的政治方法侧重于取得实际成果，而不是追求抽象的原则。

Her anti-idealistic worldview led her to believe that people are motivated primarily by self-interest. 她反理想主义的世界观使她相信，人们的动机主要是出于自身利益。

近义词: pragmatic, realistic, cynical, disillusioned
反义词: idealistic, utopian, romantic, optimistic
词性: adjective
