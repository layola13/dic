nonslanderous:/ˌnɒnˈslændərəs/ | adj. | 非诽谤性的，不构成诽谤的 | Not containing or constituting slander; not slanderous.  |  The lawyer argued that the statement was nonslanderous and protected by free speech. 律师辩称该声明不构成诽谤，受言论自由的保护。 |  Example:  The article presented a nonslanderous account of the events. 这篇文章对事件进行了非诽谤性的描述。 | Synonyms:  inoffensive, harmless, innocuous | Antonyms:  slanderous, defamatory, libelous | adjective

词源:

non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non.
slanderous (adjective):  From Old French esclandre (scandal), ultimately from Late Latin scandalum (stumbling block, offense).
