asthenopia:/ˌæsθɪˈnoʊpiə/ | n. |眼疲劳，视力疲劳|Eye strain,  tired eyes, characterized by pain, discomfort, and visual disturbances. |Example 1:  After hours of staring at the computer screen, she experienced asthenopia.  长时间盯着电脑屏幕后，她感到眼睛疲劳。

Example 2: The doctor diagnosed his asthenopia as being caused by an uncorrected refractive error. 医生诊断他的眼疲劳是由未矫正的屈光不正引起的。|Synonym: eye strain, visual fatigue, eye fatigue|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider "rested eyes" or "visual comfort")|noun
