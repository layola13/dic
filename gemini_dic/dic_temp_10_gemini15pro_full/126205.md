mwah:/mwɑː/|/mwɑː/| int. n.|亲吻的声音；飞吻|A kissing sound, often written to represent a kiss.  It can also be used as a noun representing the kiss itself.| Mwah! See you later!  飞吻！回头见！| 近义词: kiss, smooch | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a sound, but conceptually: insult, rejection) | 词性: interjection, noun
