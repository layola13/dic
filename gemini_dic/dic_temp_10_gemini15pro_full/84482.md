halterlike:/ˈhɔːltərlaɪk/| adj.| 像halter的，halter形的 |Resembling a halter; halter-shaped. A halter is a rope or strap placed around the head of a horse or other animal, used for leading or tethering it. Halterlike describes something that looks or functions like a halter. |The dress had a halterlike top, leaving her shoulders bare. 这件连衣裙有一个像halter一样的上衣，露出了她的肩膀。| The dog's collar was tight and almost halterlike, restricting its movement. 狗的项圈很紧，几乎像halter一样，限制了它的活动。 |Similar: halter-style, strappy, sleeveless (for tops)| Antonym: full-coverage, long-sleeved (for tops)|adjective
