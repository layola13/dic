assimilative:/əˈsɪməˌleɪtɪv/
adjective
同化的;吸收的
Taking in and fully understanding information or ideas.
例句：Children are generally assimilative and quickly pick up languages. 孩子们通常善于吸收，能很快学会语言。

例句：The assimilative power of the human mind is truly remarkable. 人类大脑的吸收能力确实非凡。

近义词：absorptive, receptive, integrative, incorporating
反义词：resistant, rejecting, exclusive

词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin assimilativus, from assimilare (see assimilate).
