bombazet:/ˌbɒmbəˈzɛt/
n.
轻薄棉布,棉织物
A thin, usually glazed cotton fabric.
This dress is made of bombazet. 这条裙子是用轻薄棉布做的。

近义词: cotton, muslin, cambric
