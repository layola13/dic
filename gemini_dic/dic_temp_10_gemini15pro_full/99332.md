intercutaneous:/ˌɪntərkuˈteɪniəs/| adj.|皮内的，皮间的|Existing or done between the layers of the skin.|The doctor administered an intercutaneous injection for the allergy test.|近义词：intradermal|反义词：subcutaneous|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *inter* (between) + *cutis* (skin) + -aneous (forming adjectives).
