brown-bag:/ˈbraʊn bæɡ/| n. adj. v.|自带午餐；自带酒水；|A simple lunch, typically one taken to work or school in a brown paper bag.  Also used as a verb, meaning to bring one's own lunch. Sometimes hyphenated. |例句：I brown-bag it most days to save money. 我大多数日子都自带午餐以省钱。|近义词：packed lunch, sack lunch|反义词: eat out, dine out|词性:noun, adjective, verb
brown-bag:/ˈbraʊn bæɡ/|adj.|自带午餐的；|Describing an event, especially a meeting or seminar, where attendees bring their own lunch. |例句：We're having a brown-bag lunch meeting tomorrow. 明天我们有一个自带午餐的会议。|近义词：informal, casual|反义词:formal, catered|词性:adjective
brown-bag:/ˈbraʊn bæɡ/|v.|自带午餐；自带酒水|To bring one's own lunch, typically in a brown paper bag. |例句：She brown-bagged it to the park for a picnic. 她自带午餐去公园野餐了。|近义词：pack a lunch, bring your own|反义词:eat out, dine out|词性:verb
