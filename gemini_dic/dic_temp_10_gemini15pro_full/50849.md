deseeds:/diːˈsiːdz/
verb
去除种子
removes the seeds from (fruit or vegetables)
The chef deseeds the peppers before stuffing them.  厨师在填充辣椒之前去除了辣椒籽。
deseeds the tomatoes and chops them roughly.  将西红柿去籽并粗略切碎。
Synonyms: seed, pit, core
Antonyms: plant, sow
词性: verb (动词)
