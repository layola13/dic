defused:/diːˈfjuːzd/| v. |拆除(炸弹的)引信; 缓和, 平息|To remove the fuse from (a bomb or other explosive device) and make it harmless; to make a situation less tense or dangerous.|The bomb squad successfully defused the explosive device. 爆破小组成功拆除了爆炸装置。|近义词：deactivated, neutralized, disarmed|反义词：armed, activated, ignited|词性:动词

词源: de- (Latin prefix meaning "from, down, away, off") + fuse (from Old French "fus," meaning "spindle")
