oxshoe:/ˈɒksʃuː/| n.|牛蹄铁|A U-shaped metal plate or pair of plates nailed to the underside of an ox's hoof to protect it from wear or injury.  It is similar to a horseshoe, but designed for oxen.|The blacksmith carefully fitted the oxshoes to the oxen's hooves.|近义词:ox-shoe, shoe (for oxen)|反义词:None|词性:noun
