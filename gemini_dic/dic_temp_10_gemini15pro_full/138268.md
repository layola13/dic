notable:/ˈnoʊtəbl/| adj. n.|显著的；著名的；值得注意的；名人|Deserving of attention or notice; remarkable. Also, a person who is distinguished or famous.|The city is notable for its beautiful architecture. 这座城市以其美丽的建筑而闻名。|Synonyms: remarkable, outstanding, distinguished, prominent, important, significant|Antonyms: insignificant, unremarkable, ordinary, common, obscure|adjective
notable:/ˈnoʊtəbl/| n.|名人；显要人物|A famous or important person.|Several notables attended the gala. 一些名流参加了盛会。|Synonyms: celebrity, dignitary, luminary, personage|Antonyms: nobody, unknown|noun
