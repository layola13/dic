britishnesses:/ˈbrɪtɪʃnəsɪz/
noun
英国性，英国气质
The qualities or characteristics that are considered typically British.
She embodies several classic Britishnesses, such as a love of queuing and a dry sense of humor. 她体现了几种典型的英国气质，例如热爱排队和冷幽默。
Synonyms: Britishness (singular), British traits, British characteristics
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could consider qualities associated with other nationalities, e.g., Americanness, Frenchness)
