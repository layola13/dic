n. |垃圾；废物；碎屑|Refuse, rubbish, or odds and ends, especially left after some work or process has been done. |The attle of the workshop covered the floor. 车间的垃圾覆盖了地板。|Synonyms: debris, detritus, scrap, waste, rubble |Antonyms: valuables, treasure |Noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *attel, possibly from Old English *ættol, related to eat.  The original sense likely referred to something gnawed or chewed upon, hence scraps or leftovers.  The exact etymology is uncertain.
