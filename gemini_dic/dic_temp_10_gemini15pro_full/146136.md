overemploys:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈplɔɪz/
|v.|过度雇佣，人员过剩|To employ more people than are necessary or efficient.|The company overemploys administrative staff.  这家公司行政人员过剩。|overstaff, overman|underemploy, understaff
|verb|


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + employ.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Employ" comes from the Old French "emploier," meaning "to make use of."
