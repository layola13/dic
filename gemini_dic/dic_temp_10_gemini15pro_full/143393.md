outfished:/ˌaʊtˈfɪʃt/
verb
捕鱼胜过；比…捕到更多鱼
to catch more fish than someone else
He outfished his brother. 他比他兄弟捕到的鱼多。
近义词:outdo, surpass
反义词: be outdone by, lose to
词性:verb
