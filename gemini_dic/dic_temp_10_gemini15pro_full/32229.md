chichling:/ˈtʃɪklɪŋ/| n.|小鸡；乳臭未干的小孩；年轻人|A young chicken; a naive, inexperienced young person.|例句：The farmer protected the chichlings from the hawk. 农民保护小鸡免受老鹰的伤害。

例句：He's just a chichling in the business world. 他在商界只是个乳臭未干的小孩。|近义词：chick, youngster, fledgling, newbie, novice|反义词：veteran, expert, old-timer|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chikeling, equivalent to chick +‎ -ling. Cognate with Dutch kieken (chicken) + -ling (diminutive suffix), German Küchlein (chick, pullet).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kiukinam.
