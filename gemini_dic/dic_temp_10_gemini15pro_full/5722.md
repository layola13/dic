alveary: /ˈælviəri/ (US), /ˈælviəri/ (UK)
noun
蜂房，养蜂场
A place where bees are kept, especially a collection of hives.
The beekeeper inspected the alveary, ensuring each hive was thriving.  养蜂人检查了蜂房，确保每个蜂箱都欣欣向荣。
The old oak tree housed a natural alveary, buzzing with activity.  这棵老橡树里有一个天然的蜂房，嗡嗡作响，一片繁忙。
Synonyms: apiary, beehive, bee yard
Antonyms: None (lack of a direct antonym)
