dejection:/dɪˈdʒɛkʃən/
n.
沮丧，灰心
A state of low spirits caused by loss, disappointment, or failure.
She felt a deep sense of dejection after losing her job. 她失业后感到深深的沮丧。
despondency, depression, gloom, sadness
elation, joy, happiness
noun
