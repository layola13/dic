multimillennial: /ˌmʌlti.mɪˈlɛni.əl/ | adj. | 千禧一代的；跨越多个千年的 | Spanning or relating to multiple millennia; lasting for several thousand years. | The multimillennial history of human civilization is full of fascinating stories.  人类文明跨越数千年的历史充满了引人入胜的故事。 | The multimillennial evolution of the Earth has shaped its current form. 地球数千年来的演变塑造了它现在的形态。 | 近义词: age-long, ancient, long-term | 反义词: short-term, recent, contemporary | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From multi- + millennial (from Latin mille, "thousand" + annus, "year").
