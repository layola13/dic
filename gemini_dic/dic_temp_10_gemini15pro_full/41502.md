corny:/ˈkɔːrni/ (US), /ˈkɔːni/ (UK)| adj.|老套的，陈腐的，庸俗的|Sentimental, overly emotional, or cliché, often in a way that is embarrassing or silly.  It can refer to jokes, stories, music, or gestures.|例句：His jokes were so corny that nobody laughed. 他的笑话太老套了，没人笑。

例句：She rolled her eyes at his corny pick-up lines. 她对他老套的搭讪语翻了个白眼。|近义词：cheesy, clichéd, hackneyed, banal, trite|反义词：original, fresh, innovative, sophisticated|词性:形容词
