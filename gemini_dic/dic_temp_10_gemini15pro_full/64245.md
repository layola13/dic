equipedal:/ˌiːkwɪˈpiːdəl/
adjective
等足的，同足的
having feet or legs of equal length
The equipedal statue of the mythical creature fascinated the visitors.  这尊神话生物的等足雕像吸引了游客。
Horses are equipedal animals. 马是同足动物。
synonym:isopedal
antonym:anisopedal
adjective
