orpine:/ˈɔːrpaɪn/| n.|景天; 费菜; 八宝景天|A succulent plant of the genus Sedum, especially Sedum telephium, having fleshy leaves and clusters of small pink or purplish flowers.  It was traditionally used in folk medicine.|The orpine in my garden is blooming beautifully. 我花园里的景天开得很漂亮。|近义词：sedum, livelong, stonecrop|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
