eddyless:/ˈɛdi.ləs/| adj.|无涡流的，平稳的|Characterized by the absence of eddies; smooth and undisturbed.|The eddyless flow of the river made for a peaceful canoe ride. 河流平稳的流动使独木舟之旅宁静祥和。|近义词：smooth, calm, still, tranquil|反义词:turbulent, swirling, agitated|词性:形容词
词源: eddy (n.) + -less (suffix).  "Eddy" comes from Old Norse "iða" meaning "whirlpool."  "-less" is a suffix of Old English origin meaning "without" or "lacking."
