PHRASE->
facial palsies = 面瘫
Bell's palsies =  贝尔氏麻痹 (面瘫的一种)
ocular palsies = 眼肌麻痹
progressive palsies = 进行性麻痹
cerebral palsies = 脑瘫


词源:  From Late Latin *paralysis, from Greek *paralusis, from paraluein "to disable on one side," from para- "beside" + lyein "to loosen."


注意事项: 注意区分单数palsy和复数palsies的用法。  Palsy通常指单一部位的麻痹，而palsies指多个部位的麻痹。
