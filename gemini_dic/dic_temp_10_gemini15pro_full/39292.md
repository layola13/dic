conical:/ˈkɒnɪkəl/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnɪkl/ (US)| adj.|圆锥形的；圆锥体的|Having the shape of a cone; cone-shaped.|

例句：The roof of the tower was conical. 塔的屋顶是圆锥形的。

例句：The ice cream was served in a conical wafer. 冰淇淋盛在锥形威化筒里。


近义词：cone-shaped, tapered, pointed|
反义词:flat, level|
词性:adjective|

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cōnicus*, from Latin cōnus (“cone”) + -icus (adjective-forming suffix).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek κῶνος (kônos, “cone, pine cone”).
