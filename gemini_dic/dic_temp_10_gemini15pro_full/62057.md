encased:/ɪnˈkeɪst/| adj.|被包住的，被包裹的| Enclosed or covered completely, as if in a case. |The jewels were encased in a velvet-lined box. 珠宝被放在一个天鹅绒衬里的盒子里。|The mummy was encased in bandages. 木乃伊被绷带包裹着。|近义词：enclosed, covered, enveloped, surrounded, sheathed|反义词：exposed, uncovered, open, bare|词性:形容词
