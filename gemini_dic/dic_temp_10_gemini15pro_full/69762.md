feringee:/fəˈrɪŋɡiː/| n.|外国人（尤指欧洲人）; 外来事物|A term used in South Asia and parts of East Africa to refer to a foreigner, especially a European. Often carries a connotation of otherness or exoticism.  Sometimes used derogatorily.|例句：The locals called all the European tourists "feringhees". 当地人称所有欧洲游客为“外国人”。

例句：The feringee brought unfamiliar customs to the village.  这个外国人给村庄带来了不熟悉的习俗。|近义词：foreigner, outsider, alien|反义词:native, local|词性:noun
