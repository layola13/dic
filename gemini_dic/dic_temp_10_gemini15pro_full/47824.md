decategorization:/diːˌkætɪɡərɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. |去类别化，消除类别|The process of removing or blurring the boundaries between categories, often with the aim of promoting inclusivity or challenging traditional classifications.  It can refer to social categories (like race or gender), information categories (in databases), or other types of classifications.|例句：Decategorization in mental health aims to reduce stigma by focusing on individual experiences rather than diagnostic labels. 去类别化在精神健康领域旨在通过关注个人经历而不是诊断标签来减少污名。

例句：The decategorization of data in the database allowed for more flexible searches. 数据库中数据的去类别化允许更灵活的搜索。|近义词：declassification, blurring, integration|反义词: categorization, classification|词性:noun
