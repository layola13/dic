cyclotron:/ˈsaɪklətrɒn/ (UK), /ˈsaɪkloʊtrɑːn/ (US)| n. |回旋加速器|A type of particle accelerator in which charged particles spiral outwards from the centre as they are accelerated by an alternating electric field and a constant magnetic field.|The cyclotron was invented by Ernest O. Lawrence in 1930.  回旋加速器是由欧内斯特·O·劳伦斯于1930年发明的。
