murdered:/ˈmɜːdəd/ (UK) /ˈmɜːrdərd/ (US)| v. adj.|被谋杀的，遭杀害的|Killed unlawfully and with premeditation.  Past participle of "murder".|例句：The businessman was murdered in his office.  这位商人在他办公室里被谋杀了。

|例句：She found the murdered body in the basement. 她在地下室发现了被谋杀的尸体。

|近义词：killed, slain, assassinated|反义词:rescued, saved|词性:动词过去分词，形容词
