archconfraternity: /ˌɑːrkɒnfrəˈtɜːrnɪti/ /ˌɑːrkɑːnfrəˈtɜːrnəti/
n.
主要宗教联谊会；大善会
A large and important religious confraternity.  A confraternity is a lay association of men and women in the Roman Catholic Church or certain Anglican churches, devoted to a particular form of religious worship or a charitable cause.  An archconfraternity is typically a larger or more influential confraternity.

The Archconfraternity of the Most Precious Blood was founded in Rome.  大宝血善会创立于罗马。

The archconfraternity organized pilgrimages to holy sites.  大善会组织了前往圣地的朝圣。

Synonyms: brotherhood, fellowship, association, society, league, guild, union

Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider)  isolation, division, disunity

词性: noun
