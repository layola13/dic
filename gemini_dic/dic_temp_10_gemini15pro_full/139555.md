obturation:/ˌɒbtjʊˈreɪʃn/ /ˌɑːbtʃəˈreɪʃn/
noun
阻塞；闭塞；填塞
the act of closing or blocking a passage or opening, or the state of being closed or blocked.
The obturation of the root canal is the final step in root canal therapy. 根管阻塞是根管治疗的最后一步。
closure, blockage, sealing, occlusion
opening, unblocking
n.
