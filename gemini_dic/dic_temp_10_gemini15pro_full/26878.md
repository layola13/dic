cakelet: /ˈkeɪklɪt/
n.
小蛋糕，迷你蛋糕
A small cake, often individually sized.
She baked a batch of delicious chocolate cakelets for the party. 她为派对烤了一批美味的巧克力小蛋糕。
The bakery specializes in cakelets decorated with intricate designs. 这家面包店专门制作装饰精美的小蛋糕。
Synonyms: cupcake, mini cake, petit four
Antonyms: large cake, layer cake
