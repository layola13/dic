overfilm:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪlm/| v.|（在…上）覆以薄膜|To cover (something) with a thin film.|They overfilmed the wood to protect it from the elements. 他们在木头上覆了一层薄膜以保护它不受风雨侵蚀。|Synonyms: coat, cover, laminate|Antonyms: expose, uncover|verb
