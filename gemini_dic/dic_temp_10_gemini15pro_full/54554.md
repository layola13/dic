dislocate: /ˈdɪsləkeɪt/ /dɪsˈloʊkeɪt/
| vt.|使脱位，使错位；扰乱，打乱|To move a bone out of its normal position in a joint; to disrupt the normal arrangement or connection of something.|The fall dislocated his shoulder. 这次摔倒导致他肩膀脱臼。|近义词：disjoint, luxate, disturb, disrupt|反义词：locate, order, organize|verb

dislocate: /ˈdɪsləkeɪt/ /dɪsˈloʊkeɪt/
| adj.|脱臼的，错位的|Moved out of its normal place, especially a bone in a joint.|His shoulder was dislocated. 他的肩膀脱臼了。|近义词：disjointed, luxated|反义词：located, ordered, organized|adjective
