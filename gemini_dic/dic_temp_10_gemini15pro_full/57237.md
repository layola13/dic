downhill:/ˌdaʊnˈhɪl/| adj. adv.|下坡的；走下坡路的；每况愈下的|Going downhill means descending a slope. It can also figuratively mean declining or deteriorating.|例句：The road is downhill from here. 从这里开始，路是下坡的。|近义词：descending, declining, worsening|反义词: uphill, ascending, improving|词性:形容词, 副词
downhill:/ˌdaʊnˈhɪl/| n.|下坡|A downhill is a downward slope.|例句：The skiers raced down the downhill. 滑雪者沿着下坡赛跑。|近义词：descent, slope|反义词: ascent, incline|词性:名词

词源: From "down" (Old English "dūn," meaning "from a higher place") + "hill" (Old English "hyll," meaning "a mound of earth").
