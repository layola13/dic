nuclear-force:/ˈnjuːklɪə(r) fɔːs/ | n. | 核力 | The force that holds protons and neutrons together within the nucleus of an atom. | The strong nuclear force is one of the four fundamental forces in nature.  核力是自然界中的四种基本力之一。

The nuclear force is what prevents the nucleus from flying apart. 核力是防止原子核分裂的原因。| 近义词: strong force, strong interaction | 反义词: (No direct antonym, but conceptually: electromagnetic force - which causes repulsion between protons) | 词性: noun
