defix:/ˈdiːfɪks/| v. |使固定；牢记；钉住；专注|To fasten or fix securely, especially by piercing or impaling.|They defixed the notice to the board with a tack. 他们用图钉把通知钉在板上。|近义词：fix, fasten, attach, secure, implant|反义词：detach, remove, unfasten|词性：动词

defix:/ˈdiːfɪks/| n. |符咒；诅咒；钉子|A curse tablet in ancient Roman and Greek magic, often inscribed on lead and pierced with nails, intended to inflict harm or misfortune on someone.|Archaeologists unearthed a defix in the ruins of the Roman forum. 考古学家在罗马广场的废墟中挖掘出一个符咒。|近义词：curse, hex, jinx, charm|反义词：blessing|词性：名词
