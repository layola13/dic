omniparient:/ˌɒmniˈpɛəriənt/ /ˌɑːmniˈpɛəriənt/| adj.|无所生育的，遍生万物|Producing or capable of producing all things; all-creating.  Referring to a deity or divine force believed to be the source of all creation.|例句：The omniparient goddess brought forth the universe from chaos. 无所不能的女神从混沌中创造了宇宙。

例句：Ancient myths often depict an omniparient force responsible for the existence of all life. 古代神话通常描绘了一种无所不能的力量，它负责所有生命的存在。|近义词：omnipotent, all-creating, all-generating|反义词: barren, sterile|词性:adjective
