butterflied:/ˈbʌtərˌflaɪd/| adj. v. (past participle)|蝴蝶状的，剖开的|Cut (a piece of meat or fish) open and spread it out flat like a butterfly.|例句：The chef butterflied the chicken breast before grilling it.  厨师在烤鸡胸肉之前把它剖开了。

例句：We ordered butterflied prawns for the barbecue. 我们点了剖开的对虾来烧烤。|近义词：split, opened, spatchcocked|反义词:whole, intact|词性:形容词，动词(过去分词)
