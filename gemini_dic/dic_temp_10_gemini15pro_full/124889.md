muddle-headed:/ˈmʌd(ə)lˌhedɪd/
| adj. |糊涂的，头脑混乱的|Confused and unable to think clearly.|He made a muddle-headed decision and regretted it later. 他做了一个糊涂的决定，后来后悔了。|Example: She was so muddle-headed this morning that she put salt in her coffee instead of sugar. 她今天早上头脑昏昏沉沉的，竟然把盐而不是糖放进了咖啡里。|Synonyms: confused, bewildered, addle-brained, befuddled|Antonyms: clear-headed, lucid, sharp, astute|Adjective
