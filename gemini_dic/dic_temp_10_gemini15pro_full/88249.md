hindustani:/ˌhɪndʊˈstɑːni/ | n. adj.| 印度斯坦语；印度斯坦的 |  A language of northern India, based on Khariboli, belonging to the Indo-Aryan branch of the Indo-European family, and having the same colloquial basis as Urdu. Also: a person who speaks Hindustani; relating to Hindustani or the area where it is spoken. |  She is fluent in Hindustani. 她能说流利的印度斯坦语。| synonyms: Hindi-Urdu, Urdu-Hindi | antonyms: N/A | noun, adjective
