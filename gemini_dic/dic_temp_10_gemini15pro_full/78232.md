gerbilles:/dʒɜːˈbɪlz/
n.
沙鼠；
Small rodents, typically kept as pets, characterized by long hind legs and a long tail. They are social animals and live in burrows in the wild.
My daughter loves her pet gerbilles.  我的女儿很喜欢她的宠物沙鼠。
Gerbilles are desert animals. 沙鼠是沙漠动物。

近义词: rodents, mice (loosely)
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider predators like snakes or owls)
词性: noun


gerbil:/ˈdʒɜːrbɪl/
n.
沙鼠；
A small rodent with long hind legs and a long tail, often kept as a pet.
The gerbil escaped from its cage. 沙鼠从笼子里逃跑了。
A gerbil is a popular small pet. 沙鼠是一种受欢迎的小宠物。
近义词: rodent, mouse (loosely)
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider predators like snakes or owls)
