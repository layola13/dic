implementing:/ˈɪmplɪmentɪŋ/
verb
实施，执行
putting a plan or system into effect
The company is implementing a new marketing strategy. 公司正在实施新的营销策略。
The government is implementing new regulations to protect the environment. 政府正在实施新的规章制度来保护环境。
synonyms: carrying out, enacting, applying, executing
antonyms: neglecting, abandoning, halting, ceasing
verb
