cuttingness:/ˈkʌtɪŋnɪs/
n.
尖刻; 刻薄; 辛辣; 刺骨
The quality of being sharp, incisive, or hurtful, especially in speech or manner.
Her words had a cuttingness that stung even after she left the room. 她的言语尖刻，即使在她离开房间后，依然令人感到刺痛。
sharpness, incisiveness, acrimony, mordancy
gentleness, kindness, mildness
noun
