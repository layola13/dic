harpylike:/ˈhɑːrpiˌlaɪk/
|adj.|
|像鹰身女妖的；凶恶的；贪婪的|
|Resembling or characteristic of a harpy, a mythical creature with the head and torso of a woman and the wings and talons of a bird, often depicted as cruel, predatory, and rapacious.|
|Her harpylike laughter echoed through the halls.|她那鹰身女妖般的笑声回荡在大厅里。|
|The CEO's harpylike demands for increased profits left the employees demoralized.|首席执行官对增加利润的贪婪要求让员工们士气低落。|
|近义词：rapacious, predatory, avaricious, grasping, shrewish|
|反义词：generous, benevolent, kind, compassionate|
|词性: adjective|


harpy:/ˈhɑːrpi/
|n.|
|鹰身女妖；泼妇；凶恶的女人|
|In Greek mythology, a harpy was a winged monster with the face of a woman and the body of a bird. They were known for their viciousness and greed.|
|The old woman was a real harpy, constantly nagging and complaining.|那个老妇人真是个泼妇，不停地唠叨和抱怨。|
|The harpies descended upon the feast, snatching the food and leaving a trail of destruction.|鹰身女妖突袭了宴会，抢走了食物，留下一片狼藉。|
|近义词：shrew, virago, termagant, hag, witch|
|反义词：angel, saint|
|词性: noun|
