hooraying:/hʊˈreɪɪŋ/
| n. |欢呼，喝彩|The hooraying of the crowd was deafening.  人群的欢呼声震耳欲聋。|
|The hooraying continued long after the game ended.  比赛结束后，欢呼声持续了很久。|
|近义词：cheering, applauding, acclaiming|
|反义词：booing, jeering, hissing|
|词性: 动名词 (gerund) 源自动词 hooray|
