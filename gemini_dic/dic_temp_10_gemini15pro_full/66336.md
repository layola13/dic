exculpable:/ɪkˈskʌlpəbl/ | adj. | 可免责的，可原谅的 | Free from blame or guilt; excusable. |  The court found the defendant exculpable due to insufficient evidence. 法庭因证据不足而认定被告无罪。 | Example: His actions, though regrettable, were ultimately exculpable. 他的行为虽然令人遗憾，但最终还是可以原谅的。 | Synonyms: blameless, innocent, guiltless, forgivable, pardonable | Antonyms: culpable, guilty, blameworthy | adjective
