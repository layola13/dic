non-objective:/ˌnɒn əbˈdʒɛktɪv/ | adj. | 非客观的，非具象的 | Not representing or based on external reality; relating to or denoting art that does not attempt to represent reality. |  Her non-objective paintings focused on color and form rather than depicting recognizable objects. 她的非具象绘画侧重于色彩和形式，而不是描绘可识别的物体。

| Example: The museum's exhibit of non-objective art featured works by Kandinsky and Mondrian.  博物馆的非具象艺术展览展出了康定斯基和蒙德里安的作品。| Synonym: abstract, nonrepresentational | Antonym: objective, representational | adjective
