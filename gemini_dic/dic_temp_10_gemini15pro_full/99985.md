interparish:/ˌɪntərˈpærɪʃ/
adjective
堂区间的；教区间的
Between or involving different parishes.
例句：Interparish rivalries have existed for centuries.  堂区间的竞争已经存在了几个世纪。

例句：The interparish competition was fiercely contested.  教区间的比赛竞争激烈。

近义词：interdenominational, interchurch
反义词：intraparish (within a single parish)
词性：adjective
