enome:/ˈiːnoʊm/| n. |基因组；整套基因|The entire set of genes in an organism.  It includes all the genetic material found in an organism's chromosomes.|Scientists are studying the human enome to better understand diseases. 科学家们正在研究人类基因组以便更好地了解疾病。|Synonym: genome|Antonym: N/A|Noun
