outseeks:/aʊtˈsiːks/
verb
超越，胜过
To surpass or exceed in seeking or striving for something.
He outseeks all others in his dedication to his craft. 他对他的工艺的奉献精神超过了所有其他人。
She outseeks her rivals in ambition and drive. 她在雄心和动力上胜过她的竞争对手。
近义词:surpass, exceed, outdo, outstrip
反义词:lag behind, fall short of
词性:v
