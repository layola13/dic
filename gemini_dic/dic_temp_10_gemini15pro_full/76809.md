gamogenesis:/ˌɡæməˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
有性生殖
Sexual reproduction, specifically the formation of gametes and their fusion to form a zygote.
Gamogenesis is the most common form of reproduction in animals. 有性生殖是动物中最常见的繁殖方式。
The process of gamogenesis involves meiosis and fertilization. 有性生殖的过程包括减数分裂和受精。
近义词: sexual reproduction
反义词: asexual reproduction
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *γαμεῖν (gameîn, “to marry”) + γένεσις (génesis, “origin, creation”).
