nonponderousness:/ˌnɒnpɒnˈdərəsnəs/
|n.|轻盈；不笨重；|The nonponderousness of the new aircraft made it highly maneuverable.  这架新飞机的轻盈使其具有很高的机动性。|English Explanation: The quality or state of not being ponderous; lack of weightiness or heaviness, both literally and figuratively.  It can refer to physical lightness or a lack of intellectual or emotional heaviness.|例句：The dancer moved with an effortless nonponderousness.  舞者以毫不费力的轻盈舞动着。

|近义词：lightness, airiness, agility, nimbleness|反义词：ponderousness, heaviness, clumsiness, awkwardness|词性: noun


Etymology:
From non- + ponderousness.  Ponderousness comes from ponderous + -ness.  Ponderous derives from Late Latin ponderōsus "weighty," from Latin pondus "weight."
