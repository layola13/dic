palateless:/ˈpælətləs/ | adj. | 无味的；平淡的；乏味的 | Lacking flavor; bland; insipid; not stimulating to the palate.  | The soup was thin and palateless. 这汤太淡而无味了。 | Synonyms: bland, insipid, tasteless, unappetizing, unflavored | Antonyms: flavorful, tasty, savory, delicious, palatable | adjective

palateless:/ˈpælətləs/ | adj. | 不受欢迎的；不令人喜欢的 | Uninteresting; not enjoyable. | He gave a palateless speech. 他做了一个乏味的演讲。 | Synonyms: uninteresting, dull, boring, tedious, vapid | Antonyms: interesting, engaging, captivating, stimulating, exciting | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From palate + -less.  "Palate" comes from Old French "palat" ultimately from Latin "palatum" meaning "roof of the mouth, taste."
