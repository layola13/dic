ceraceous:/sɪˈreɪʃəs/| adj.|蜡质的，像蜡的|Resembling wax in appearance, consistency, or properties.|The fungus had a smooth, ceraceous surface.  这种真菌表面光滑，呈蜡质。|近义词：waxy, waxlike|反义词: non-waxy, non-ceraceous|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Latin *ceraceus*, from *cera* "wax".
