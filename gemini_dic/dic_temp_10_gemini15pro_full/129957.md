non-Mediterranean:/ˌnɒnˌmedɪtəˈreɪniən/
adjective
非地中海的；非地中海气候的
Not of or characteristic of the Mediterranean Sea, its coasts, or their peoples.
The non-Mediterranean climate of the region makes it unsuitable for growing olives.  这个地区非地中海型气候使得它不适合种植橄榄。
Many non-Mediterranean plants thrive in cooler climates. 许多非地中海植物在较冷的气候中茁壮成长。
Near synonyms: extra-Mediterranean, non-mediterranean
Antonyms: Mediterranean

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Old French non-, Latin non "not," from PIE *ne- "not."
Mediterranean: From Latin Mediterraneus "midland," literally "in the middle of the land," from medius "middle" + terra "land, earth."
