counterclaimed:/ˌkaʊntərˈkleɪmd/
verb
反诉，反驳
to assert a claim in opposition to another claim, especially in a legal context.
The company counterclaimed, alleging breach of contract.  该公司提起反诉，声称对方违反合同。
The defendant counterclaimed for damages.  被告反诉要求赔偿损失。
rebut, retort, oppose
claim, accept
