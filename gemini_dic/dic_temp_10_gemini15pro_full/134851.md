nonmodernistic:/nɒnˌmɒdəˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
非现代主义的
Not characteristic of or related to modernism, especially in art, literature, or architecture.  Rejecting or opposing modernism.
His nonmodernistic approach to painting focused on classical techniques. 他非现代主义的绘画方法注重古典技巧。
The architect's nonmodernistic designs incorporated traditional elements.  这位建筑师的非现代主义设计融合了传统元素。
Synonyms: traditional, conventional, classic, conservative
Antonyms: modernistic, modernist, avant-garde, contemporary
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non, meaning "not."  Indicates negation or absence.
modernistic (adjective): Derived from modernism + -istic. Modernism refers to a style or movement in the arts that aims to break with classical and traditional forms.  -istic is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "relating to" or "characteristic of."
