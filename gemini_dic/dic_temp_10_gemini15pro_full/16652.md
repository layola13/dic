barracking:/ˈbærəkɪŋ/ /ˈbærɪkɪŋ/| n.|起哄，喝倒彩；加油，助威|The constant barracking from the opposing team's fans was distracting.|对方球队球迷不断的起哄声让人分心。|
barracking:/ˈbærəkɪŋ/ /ˈbærɪkɪŋ/| n.| (Australian, informal)  Teasing; good-natured ribbing|They gave him some good-natured barracking about his new haircut.|他们善意地取笑他的新发型。|
barracking:/ˈbærəkɪŋ/ /ˈbærɪkɪŋ/| n.| (chiefly British)  Loud cheering or applause|The team was greeted with enthusiastic barracking from the home crowd.|球队受到了主场观众热烈的欢呼和掌声。|


近义词: cheering, jeering, heckling, rooting, supporting, teasing, ribbing|
反义词: supporting (when barracking is used negatively), silence|
词性: noun|

词源: From the French word "baraque", meaning "hut, shed." Originally referred to the noisy, crowded living quarters of soldiers or laborers.
