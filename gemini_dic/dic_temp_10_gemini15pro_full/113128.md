lungoor:/lʌŋˈɡʊər/ | n. | 印度叶猴，长尾叶猴 | A long-tailed, arboreal monkey (Semnopithecus entellus) of India, having a slender build, long limbs, and often a sacred status. |  例句：We saw a troop of lungoors swinging through the trees. 我们看到一群长尾叶猴在树间荡来荡去。| 近义词：monkey, primate | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 predator 捕食者) | 词性: 名词


词源: From Hindi langūr, ultimately from Sanskrit laṅgūlin, meaning “long-tailed.”
