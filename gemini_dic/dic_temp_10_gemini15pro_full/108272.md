laundering:/ˈlɔːndərɪŋ/ | n. | 洗钱; 洗涤;  | the practice of concealing the source of illegally obtained money by transferring it through a series of transactions. Also refers to the washing of clothes, etc. | The authorities are investigating a complex money laundering scheme. 当局正在调查一个复杂的洗钱计划。|He put his dirty clothes in the basket for laundering. 他把脏衣服放进篮子里准备洗。 | synonyms: washing, cleaning, cleansing (for literal washing);  money cleaning, sanitizing (for illegal activity) | antonyms: soiling, dirtying (for literal washing);  none (for illegal activity) | noun

laundering:/ˈlɔːndərɪŋ/ | v. | 洗（衣物）; 洗（钱） | present participle of launder |  The company is accused of laundering drug money. 这家公司被指控洗钱。 | She was laundering sheets when the phone rang.  她正在洗床单时电话响了。 | synonyms: washing, cleaning | antonyms: soiling, dirtying | verb
