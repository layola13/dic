distributed: /dɪˈstrɪbjuːtɪd/ /dɪˈstrɪbjətɪd/ | adj. | 分布式的，分散的 | spread out over a wide area or among many people | The company has a distributed network of offices across the country.  这家公司在全国各地拥有分布式的办事网络。 | synonyms: spread, scattered, dispersed | antonyms: concentrated, centralized | adjective

distributed: /dɪˈstrɪbjuːtɪd/ /dɪˈstrɪbjətɪd/ | v. (past participle of distribute) | 分布，分发（distribute的过去分词） |  Having been divided and shared out |  The leaflets were distributed to the crowd. 传单分发给了人群。（这是distribute的用法，distributed在这里是过去分词作谓语的一部分）| synonyms: allocated, apportioned, dispensed | antonyms: collected, gathered, amassed | verb (past participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *distributen*, from Latin distrībūtus, past participle of distrībūere (“to distribute”), from dis- (“apart, asunder”) + tribuere (“to allot, assign, bestow, grant, give, divide, distribute”).
