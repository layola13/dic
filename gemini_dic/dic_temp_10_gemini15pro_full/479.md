abnet:/ˈæbnet/| n.| 腹网; 腹带;  |A supporting band, bandage, or netting worn around the abdomen.|  The surgeon applied an abnet after the operation. 手术后，外科医生使用了腹带。| 近义词: abdominal bandage, belly band, binder | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "loosening garment") | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *abdomen* (abdomen) + *-et* (diminutive suffix).  Essentially meaning "small covering for the abdomen."
