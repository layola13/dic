misreference:/ˌmɪsˈrɛfərəns/ | n. | 错误引用；误称 | An act of referring to something incorrectly or inaccurately. |  He made a misreference to the author's name in his essay. 他在他的文章中错误地引用了作者的名字。 | synonym: miscitation, inaccurate reference | antonym: accurate reference, correct citation | noun

misreference:/ˌmɪsˈrɛfərəns/ | vt. | 错误地引用；误称 | To refer to something incorrectly or inaccurately. | The lawyer misreferenced a key piece of evidence, weakening his case.  律师错误地引用了一个关键证据，削弱了他的论点。 | synonym: miscite, misquote | antonym: cite correctly, quote accurately | verb
