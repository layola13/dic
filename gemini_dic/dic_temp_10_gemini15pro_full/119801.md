migrators:/ˈmaɪɡreɪtərz/
n.
迁徙者，移民
People or animals that migrate.
The park is a haven for migrating birds. 这个公园是候鸟的天堂。
近义词:emigrants, immigrants, travelers, nomads
反义词:residents, settlers
词性:noun


migrators:/ˈmaɪɡreɪtərz/
n.
迁移者，移动者
Things that migrate.
Software migrators help companies move their data to new systems. 软件迁移工具帮助公司将数据迁移到新系统。
近义词:movers, shifters, transferers
反义词:stayers
词性:noun
