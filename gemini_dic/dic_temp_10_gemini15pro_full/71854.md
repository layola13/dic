fleshen:/ˈflɛʃən/ (US), /ˈflɛʃn/ (UK)| v. |充实，具体化；使丰满|to give more substance or detail to something; to make something more complete or realistic|例句：The author fleshed out the characters in his novel by providing detailed descriptions of their past experiences. 作者通过详细描述人物的过去经历，充实了他小说中的人物。

例句：The skeletal proposal was fleshed out with specific budget figures and timelines.  这个框架提案通过具体的预算数字和时间表得到了充实。


近义词：elaborate, expand, develop, enhance, augment|反义词: abridge, condense, summarize, shorten|词性:动词
