cloggier:/ˈklɑːɡiər (US), ˈklɒɡiər (UK)| adj.|更堵塞的，更不通畅的|More clogged; more obstructed or blocked.  Describes something that is more difficult to move through or along than before, due to an accumulation of something thick or sticky.|The pipes are even cloggier now than they were last week.  管道现在比上周更堵塞了。|The traffic is cloggier today because of the accident. 今天的交通因为事故更加拥堵。|近义词：blocked, obstructed, congested|反义词:clear, open, free-flowing|词性:形容词

词源: From "clog" + "-ier" (comparative suffix). "Clog" comes from Middle English "cloggen," of uncertain origin, possibly related to a word meaning "lump" or "clod."
