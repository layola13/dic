pargasite:/ˈpɑːrɡəsaɪt/| n. |帕加石|A complex inosilicate mineral of the amphibole group, typically dark green to black in color, containing sodium, calcium, magnesium, iron, aluminum, and hydroxyl. It is found in metamorphic rocks, particularly those formed under high pressure.|The geologist identified the mineral as pargasite due to its distinct cleavage and color.|近义词：amphibole, hornblende|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific mineral) |词性:noun
