conclusory:/kənˈkluːsəri/
adjective
结论性的；总结性的
Relating to or resembling a conclusion; expressing a conclusion without providing adequate supporting evidence.
The report offered a conclusory statement without presenting the data.  这份报告提供了一个结论性的陈述，但没有提供数据。
His conclusory remarks left many questions unanswered.  他总结性的发言留下许多问题没有解答。
synonyms:  summary, final, concluding, definitive, decisive
antonyms:  preliminary, introductory, initial, opening, inconclusive, uncertain
词性: adjective
