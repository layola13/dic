nonmelodically:/ˌnɒn.məˈlɒd.ɪk.li/ /ˌnɑːn.məˈlɑː.dɪk.li/
adverb
非旋律地，不和谐地
without melody; in a way that is not melodic
The piece was played nonmelodically, focusing on rhythm and texture.  这首曲子演奏得没有旋律，注重节奏和质感。
The avant-garde composer often used instruments nonmelodically, creating unusual soundscapes.  这位先锋派作曲家经常不按旋律使用乐器，创造出不同寻常的声音景观。
Synonym:  unmelodiously, discordantly, inharmoniously
Antonym: melodically, harmoniously
词性: 副词
