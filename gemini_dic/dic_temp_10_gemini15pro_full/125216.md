multiconic:/ˌmʌltɪˈkɒnɪk/ | adj. | 多圆锥投影的 | Relating to or involving multiple cones, especially in the context of map projections. A multiconic projection is a map projection in which the surface of the Earth is projected onto a series of cones, each tangent to a different parallel of latitude. |  Multiconic projections are often used for mapping large areas with an east-west orientation. 多圆锥投影常用于绘制东西方向的大面积区域。

 The multiconic projection minimizes distortion along the central meridian. 多圆锥投影使沿中央子午线的变形最小。 | 近义词: polyconic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps 'single-cone' in a specific context) | 词性: adjective
