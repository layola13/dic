choice:/tʃɔɪs/| n.|选择,抉择,选择权；供选择的东西(或人),入选者；精选品|The choice is yours. 选择权在你手里。|Synonyms: option, alternative, selection, preference|Antonyms: obligation, necessity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *chois, past participle of choisir (“to choose”), from Frankish *kausjan, from Proto-Germanic *kausjaną (“to choose, test, taste”).  Cognate with Old English cēosan (“to choose”).
