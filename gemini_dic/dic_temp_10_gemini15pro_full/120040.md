millipede:/ˈmɪlɪpiːd/ | n. |千足虫，马陆|A small creature with a long body and many legs, belonging to the class Diplopoda.  It is not an insect, but a myriapod.|例句：I saw a millipede crawling on the forest floor. 我看到一只千足虫在森林地面上爬行。

|例句：Millipedes are detritivores, meaning they eat decaying plant matter. 千足虫是食腐动物，这意味着它们吃腐烂的植物。|近义词：myriapod |反义词: N/A |词性:noun


millipede: /ˈmɪlɪpiːd/ | n. |千足虫，马陆|From Latin *millepeda, from mille "thousand" + pes "foot".|例句：The millipede curled up into a tight spiral when I touched it. 我碰到它时，千足虫蜷缩成一个紧密的螺旋形。

|例句：Some species of millipedes can secrete a defensive fluid that irritates the skin. 有些种类的千足虫可以分泌一种刺激皮肤的防御性液体。|近义词：diplopod |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
