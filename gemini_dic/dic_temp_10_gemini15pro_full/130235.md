non-skid:/ˌnɒnˈskɪd/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈskɪd/ (US)| adj.|防滑的|Designed to prevent skidding or slipping.  Relating to or denoting a surface, material, or device designed to reduce or prevent slipping or sliding.|The non-skid surface of the bath mat prevented any slips or falls. 浴垫的防滑表面防止了任何滑倒或跌倒。|Synonyms: anti-slip, slip-resistant, grip, traction|Antonyms: slippery, slick, smooth|adjective

non-skid:/ˌnɒnˈskɪd/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈskɪd/ (US)| n.|防滑材料，防滑装置|A material or device designed to prevent skidding.|The tires had excellent non-skid. 轮胎有很好的防滑性能。  (Note: This noun usage is less common than the adjective.)|Synonyms: anti-slip material, traction device|Antonyms: lubricant|noun
