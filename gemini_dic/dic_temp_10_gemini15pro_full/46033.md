cyborgs:/ˈsaɪbɔːrɡz/
|n.|半机械人；电子人；改造人|A fictional or hypothetical person whose physical abilities are extended beyond normal human limitations by mechanical elements built into the body.|例句：In the movie, cyborgs are created to fight in wars. 在电影中，半机械人被创造出来是为了参加战争。

例句：Many science fiction stories feature cyborgs with enhanced strength and abilities. 许多科幻小说都以拥有增强力量和能力的半机械人为特色。|近义词：bionic humans, androids|反义词:humans, naturals|Noun
