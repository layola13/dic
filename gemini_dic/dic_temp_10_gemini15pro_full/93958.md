immuring:/ɪˈmjʊərɪŋ/| v. |禁闭，把…砌入墙内，封闭|The act of enclosing or confining someone within walls, often as punishment or to prevent escape. |例句：The king immured his enemies alive within the castle walls. 国王把他的敌人活活地砌在城堡的墙里。

例句：The monk immured himself in his cell for prayer and meditation.  这位僧侣把自己关在小房间里祈祷和冥想。|近义词：confine, imprison, incarcerate, entomb|反义词:liberate, free, release|词性:动词
