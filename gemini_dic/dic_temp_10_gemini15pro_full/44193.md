croaking:/ˈkroʊkɪŋ/| n. v.|呱呱叫；抱怨；|The croaking of frogs filled the night air. 青蛙的呱呱叫声充满了夜空。|近义词：ribbiting, complaining, grumbling|反义词: praising, applauding|词性:名词/动词
Etymology: From Middle English *croken, from Old English *crācian (“to croak”), of imitative origin.|
