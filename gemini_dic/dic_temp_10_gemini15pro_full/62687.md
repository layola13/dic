engenderment:/ɪnˈdʒɛndərmənt/ | n. | 产生，造成，引起 |The engenderment of trust is crucial for a successful relationship.  建立信任对于一段成功的恋情至关重要。|  The policy engendered controversy. 这项政策引起了争议。 | 近义词：creation, generation, production, causation | 反义词：prevention, suppression, hindrance | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *engendren, from Old French engendrer, from Latin ingenerāre (“to implant, engender, beget”), from in- + generāre (“to beget, produce”).
