bismillah:/bɪsˈmɪləh/
phrase
奉真主之名
In the name of God (an invocation used by Muslims at the beginning of an undertaking).
He began his speech with "Bismillah." 他以“奉真主之名”开始了他的演讲。
近义词: (No direct English equivalent, conceptually similar to "In God's name" or "God willing")
反义词:  (No antonym)
词性: phrase
