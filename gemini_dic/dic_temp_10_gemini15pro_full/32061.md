chert:/tʃɜːrt/ | n. | 燧石；角岩 | A hard, fine-grained sedimentary rock consisting of microcrystalline or cryptocrystalline quartz. | The arrowheads were made of chert. 箭头是用燧石做的。|  The geologist identified the rock as chert. 地质学家将这块岩石鉴定为燧石。 | synonyms: flint, jasper, chalcedony | antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *chiert, probably from Late Latin *carcedonius, a variant of chalcedonius (“chalcedony”).
