iguanian:/ɪˈɡwɑːniən/
adjective|鬣蜥类的；美洲鬣蜥科的|Relating to or belonging to the Iguanidae, a family of lizards that includes iguanas.|The Galapagos Islands are home to several unique iguanian species. 加拉帕戈斯群岛是几种独特的鬣蜥物种的家园。|近义词:lizard-like|反义词:None|词性:adjective

iguanian:/ɪˈɡwɑːniən/
noun|鬣蜥类动物|A lizard belonging to the Iguanidae family.|Marine iguanas are a fascinating example of an iguanian adapted to life by the sea.  海鬣蜥是鬣蜥适应海洋生活的迷人例子。|近义词:iguana, lizard|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology: From Modern Latin *Iguana* + -ian.  *Iguana* itself comes from the Taíno word *iwana*.
