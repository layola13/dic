luxuriations:/lʌɡˌʒʊəriˈeɪʃənz/
|n.|奢侈, 豪华, 骄奢淫逸|The act of indulging in luxury; luxurious behavior or lifestyle. An instance of luxurious behavior or lifestyle.|He spent his days in idle luxuriations, lounging by the pool and sipping cocktails. 他终日无所事事地享受着奢华的生活，在泳池边闲逛，啜饮着鸡尾酒。|近义词：indulgences, extravagances, excesses|反义词：austerities, privations, abstinences|词性:noun

词源: From Late Latin *luxuriationem,* accusative singular of *luxuriatio,*  "rankness," from Latin *luxuriare* "to be luxuriant," from *luxuria* "luxury, abundance," related to *luxus* (see luxury).
