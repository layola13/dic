overripened:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪpənd/ | adj. | 过熟的 | Excessively ripe; past its prime for eating.  | The overripened bananas were too mushy to eat. 过熟的香蕉太软了，不能吃。 | The overripened fruit attracted swarms of flies. 过熟的水果引来了成群的苍蝇。 | 近义词: overripe, rotten | 反义词: unripe, green | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "ripened" (past participle of "ripen," from Old English "ripian")
