act upon:/ækt əˈpɒn/ (US) /ækt əˈpɑːn/| phrasal verb | 对…起作用；按照…行事；根据…行动 |  To take action because of something or as instructed by someone. | The committee decided to act upon the recommendations of the report. 委员会决定根据报告中的建议采取行动。

The medicine acts upon the infected area to reduce inflammation.  这种药作用于感染区域以减少炎症。 | 近义词: act on, follow, implement, carry out | 反义词: ignore, disregard, neglect | 词性: 动词短语

act on:/ækt ɑːn/ (US) /ækt ɒn/| phrasal verb | 对…起作用；按照…行事；根据…行动 | To take action because of something or as instructed by someone. |  I will act on your advice. 我会按照你的建议去做。

We need to act on this information immediately. 我们需要立即根据这条信息采取行动。 | 近义词: act upon, follow, implement, carry out | 反义词: ignore, disregard, neglect | 词性: 动词短语


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Stress acts upon the immune system. = 压力对免疫系统有影响。
The government acts upon the advice of its scientific advisors. = 政府根据其科学顾问的建议采取行动。

一般过去时:
They acted upon the information they received. = 他们根据收到的信息采取了行动。
The doctor acted upon his best judgment. = 医生根据他的最佳判断采取了行动。

现在完成时:
The company has acted upon the complaints of its customers. = 公司已根据客户的投诉采取了行动。
We have acted upon the recommendations of the report. = 我们已根据报告的建议采取了行动。

过去完成时:
They had acted upon the warning before the storm hit. = 他们在暴风雨来临之前就根据警告采取了行动。
She had acted upon her lawyer's advice. = 她已经按照律师的建议行事了。


将来时:
We will act upon any new information as it becomes available. = 我们将在任何新信息可用时立即采取行动。
The board will act upon the recommendations at its next meeting. = 董事会将在下次会议上根据建议采取行动。

被动语态:
The recommendations were acted upon immediately. = 这些建议立即得到了采纳。
The new policy is being acted upon by all departments. = 所有部门都在执行新政策。
