borderless:/ˈbɔːdələs/ | adj. | 无边界的；无国界的 | Having no borders or boundaries; not limited by national borders. |  例句：The internet has created a borderless world for information sharing. 互联网为信息共享创造了一个无国界的世界。

例句：The company envisions a borderless future for its operations. 该公司设想其运营的未来是无国界的。


近义词：unbounded, limitless, international, global, transnational | 反义词：bounded, limited, national, domestic | 词性：形容词
