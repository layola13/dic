barricade: /ˌbærɪˈkeɪd/ | n. vt. |路障，障碍；设路障，阻隔|A barrier hastily constructed, as in a street, to obstruct the passage of traffic or troops. | The protesters erected barricades to block the street. 抗议者设置路障封锁街道。|  The police barricaded the area. 警察用路障封锁了该地区。 | synonym: barrier, obstacle, blockade | antonym: opening, passage | noun, verb
