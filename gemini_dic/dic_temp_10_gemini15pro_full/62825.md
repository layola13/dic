eniac:/ˈɛniæk/ |n.|埃尼阿克（世界上第一台通用电子数字计算机）|ENIAC, an acronym for Electronic Numerical Integrator and Computer, was the first programmable, electronic, general-purpose digital computer. It was Turing-complete, and able to solve "a large class of numerical problems" through reprogramming.|The ENIAC was a groundbreaking machine, marking the beginning of the computer age. 埃尼阿克是一台具有突破性的机器，标志着计算机时代的开始。| synonyms: computer, first general-purpose computer | antonyms: abacus, slide rule | noun
Etymology: Acronym from Electronic Numerical Integrator And Computer.
