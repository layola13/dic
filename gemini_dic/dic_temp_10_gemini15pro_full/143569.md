outhold:/ˈaʊtˌhəʊld/ (UK) /ˈaʊtˌhoʊld/ (US)| n.|外堡; 外围防御工事; 支撑物|A fortified position outside or away from a main defensive structure, often used as a watchtower or lookout post; also, anything that supports or strengthens something else.|例句：The soldiers manned the outhold to watch for approaching enemies. 士兵们驻守在外堡，监视逼近的敌人。

例句：The outer walls of the castle served as an outhold against attacks. 城堡的外墙是抵御攻击的外部防御工事。|近义词：outpost, stronghold, bulwark, support, prop|反义词:center, core|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->

From Old English *ūt-hold* (unattested), from *ūt* (out) + *hold* (stronghold, fortress).
