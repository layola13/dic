autopsying:/ˈɔːtɑːpsiɪŋ/ /ˈɔːtɒpsiɪŋ/
verb
解剖；验尸
Performing an autopsy; conducting a postmortem examination.
The medical examiner was autopsying the body to determine the cause of death. 法医正在解剖尸体以确定死因。
The pathologist was autopsying the victim of the car accident. 病理学家正在解剖车祸受害者的尸体。
synonyms: examining post-mortem, dissecting, necropsying
antonyms: reviving, resuscitating
verb


Etymology: From autopsy + -ing. Autopsy comes from the Greek autopsia, "seeing with one's own eyes," from autos, "self," and opsis, "sight."
