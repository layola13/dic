dentiphone:/ˈdɛntɪˌfoʊn/| n. |齿音送话器，牙齿送话器|A device that transmits speech sounds through vibrations of the teeth and jawbone, used especially in noisy environments.|The pilot used a dentiphone to communicate clearly despite the loud engine noise. 飞行员使用齿音送话器以便在嘈杂的引擎噪音中清晰地交流。|近义词：throat microphone, bone conduction microphone|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "conventional microphone")|词性:noun
