exemplifier:/ɪɡˈzɛmplɪfaɪər/ | n. | 例证者；示例者 |  A person or thing that exemplifies something; an illustration or example. | The teacher used several exemplifiers to illustrate the concept of gravity. 老师用几个例子来说明重力的概念。 | synonyms: example, illustration, instance, specimen | antonyms: counterexample | noun

exemplify:/ɪɡˈzɛmplɪfaɪ/ | v. |  举例说明；例证；是…的典型 | To illustrate or clarify by giving an example.  |  Her early work exemplifies her talent. 她早期的作品是她才华的例证。 | synonyms: illustrate, demonstrate, show, represent, typify | antonyms: obfuscate, confuse | verb
