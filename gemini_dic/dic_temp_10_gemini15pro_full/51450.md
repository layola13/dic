dethronize:/diːˈθroʊnaɪz/ | vt. | 废黜；取代；使失去重要地位 | To remove a king or queen from power; to remove someone from a position of authority or dominance. | The rebels dethroned the king and established a republic. 反叛者废黜了国王，建立了共和国。

The new smartphone app has dethroned the previous market leader.  这款新的智能手机应用取代了之前的市场领导者。 | 近义词：oust, depose, unseat, displace, supplant | 反义词：enthrone, crown, install | 词性：动词
