antiunemployment:/ˌæntiʌnɪmˈplɔɪmənt/
adj.
反失业的，防止失业的
Relating to or designed to prevent unemployment.
The government introduced several antiunemployment measures. 政府推出了几项反失业措施。
Synonyms: counter-unemployment, job-creating, employment-generating
Antonyms: unemployment-inducing, job-destroying
词性: 形容词
