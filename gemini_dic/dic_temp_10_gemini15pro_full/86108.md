heartbeat:/ˈhɑːrtbiːt/ (US) /ˈhɑːtbiːt/ (UK)| adj.|核心的；重要的|Heartbeat issues are central to the campaign.  核心问题是这场运动的关键。|This area is the heartbeat of the city's nightlife. 这个地区是城市夜生活的中心。|近义词：central, essential, core|反义词:peripheral, unimportant|adjective
