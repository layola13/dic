nonobscene:/ˌnɒnəbˈsiːn/ | adj. |非淫秽的; 不猥亵的 |Not obscene; decent.  | Example: The movie was rated PG because it contained nonobscene nudity.  这部电影被评为PG级，因为它包含非淫秽的裸体。| Example:  His language, though colorful, was entirely nonobscene. 他的语言虽然丰富多彩，但完全不猥亵。 | Synonyms: decent, clean, wholesome, inoffensive, unobjectionable | Antonyms: obscene, indecent, lewd, vulgar, pornographic | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + obscene.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "obscene" comes from the Latin "obscenus," meaning "unlucky, ill-omened," later developing the sense of "offensive to modesty or decency."
