hiked:/haɪkt/| v.|徒步旅行过；远足过|past tense and past participle of "hike"|We hiked for miles through the forest. 我们在森林里徒步旅行了好几英里。|近义词：trekked, rambled, walked, backpacked|反义词: rested, stayed, drove|词性:动词

词源: From hike + -ed, the past tense and past participle marker. Hike itself likely originated as a variant of hitch, meaning "to move with a jerk," and later acquired the sense of a long walk.
