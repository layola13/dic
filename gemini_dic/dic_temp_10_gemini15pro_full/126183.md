muzzier:/ˈmʌzɪər/ | adj. | 更模糊的，更昏沉的 | Comparative form of muzzy: more muzzy. muzzy:/ˈmʌzi/ | adj. | 模糊的，昏沉的 |Mentally confused; lacking sharpness of focus.
