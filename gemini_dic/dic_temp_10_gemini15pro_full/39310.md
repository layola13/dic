coniform:/ˈkoʊnɪfɔːrm/
adjective
锥形的，圆锥形的
Having the shape of a cone.
The volcano has a distinctly coniform shape. 这座火山有着明显的锥形形状。
The artist created a coniform sculpture out of clay. 艺术家用粘土创作了一个锥形雕塑。
conical, cone-shaped
--
adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *coniformis, from conus (“cone”) + -formis (“-form”).
