outnumber:/ˌaʊtˈnʌmbər/
verb
以数量胜过，比……多
be more numerous than
例句：
The demonstrators outnumbered the police.
示威者人数超过了警察。

Women outnumber men in the nursing profession.
护理行业女性人数超过男性。

近义词：exceed, surpass, top
反义词：be outnumbered by
词性：v.
