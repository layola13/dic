babushkas:/bəˈbuːʃkəz/ /bəˈbʊʃkəz/
|n.|巴布什卡, 俄羅斯方巾，頭巾，老婦人|A headscarf tied under the chin, typically worn by Russian peasant women. Also, an elderly woman, especially one perceived as being old-fashioned or overprotective.|The babushkas sat on the park bench, gossiping.  那些老太太坐在公园的长椅上闲聊。|Near synonyms: headscarf, scarf, shawl, kerchief; (for elderly woman) grandmother, granny, old woman|Antonym: (for headscarf) None; (for elderly woman) young woman|Noun
