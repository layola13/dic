gearings:/ˈɡɪərɪŋz/
n.
传动装置，齿轮装置; (公司)资本与负债的比率; 杠杆比率
The system of gears in a machine. The ratio of a company's capital to its debt.
The car's gearings were damaged in the accident. 这辆车的传动装置在事故中损坏了。
The company's high gearings made it vulnerable to a downturn in the market.  这家公司的高杠杆比率使其容易受到市场低迷的影响。
近义词: gears, leverage, gearing ratio
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以根据语境选择例如：low leverage 低杠杆)
词性: 名词 (通常用复数形式)


词源:  From "gear" + "-ing" (suffix forming nouns from verbs, indicating the result or product of an action, or the means of performing an action). "Gear" itself comes from Old Norse "gervi" meaning "preparation, equipment."
