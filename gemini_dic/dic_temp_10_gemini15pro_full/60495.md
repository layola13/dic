eke-out:/ikˈaʊt/
verb
补充；勉强维持；延长
to make a supply of something such as money or food last longer by using it very carefully
We had to eke out our meager supplies. 我们不得不精打细算地用我们少得可怜的补给。
stretch, supplement, spin out
squander, waste
词性:动词
