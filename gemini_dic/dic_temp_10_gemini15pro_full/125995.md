muskrats:/ˈmʌskˌræts/ | n. |麝鼠|A large, semi-aquatic rodent with a musky odor, native to North America and introduced to Europe and Asia. It has a long, scaly tail and webbed hind feet. |Muskrats build lodges in marshes and ponds. 麝鼠在沼泽和池塘里筑巢。| Muskrat houses are made of vegetation. 麝鼠的房子是用植物做的。 |近义词:musquash (主要用于加拿大)|反义词: 无|词性:名词
