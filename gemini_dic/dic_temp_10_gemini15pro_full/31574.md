chasm: /ˈkæzəm/ (US) /ˈkæzəm/ (UK)| n. |深渊，裂隙，巨大分歧|A deep fissure in the earth, rock, or another surface. Also, a profound difference of opinion, feeling, etc.|例句：The earthquake created a chasm in the road. 地震在路上造成了一个裂缝。

例句：There is a growing chasm between the rich and the poor. 贫富差距越来越大。|近义词：gulf, abyss, rift, divide, gap|反义词：closure, bridge, connection|词性: noun
