lochage:/ˈlɒkeɪdʒ/| n.|住宿；宿营地；兵舍；驻扎的军队|A lochage is a place where soldiers are lodged or quartered.  It can also refer to the troops themselves stationed at a particular place.|The lochage was situated near the river.  这个兵营位于河边附近。|The general inspected the lochage. 将军视察了驻扎的军队。|synonyms: accommodation, barracks, quarters, troops, garrison|antonyms:  N/A|noun
