nonclimbing:/ˌnɒnˈklaɪmɪŋ/
adjective
非攀爬的，不攀爬的
Not able to or designed for climbing.
The zoo features a nonclimbing enclosure for the pandas.  这个动物园为熊猫建造了一个非攀爬的围栏。
The fence is nonclimbing.  这个栅栏是无法攀爬的。
Near Antonyms: climbing, ascendable, scalable
Antonyms: unclimbable
