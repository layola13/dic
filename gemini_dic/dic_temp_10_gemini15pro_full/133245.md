nonexecutive:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪv/ | n. adj.|非执行人员，非执行的|A nonexecutive director is a member of a company's board of directors who is not part of the executive team.  非执行董事是公司董事会成员，不属于执行团队。|The company appointed several new nonexecutives to the board. 公司任命了几名新的非执行董事进入董事会。|近义词：non-executive director, outside director|反义词：executive, manager|词性:名词，形容词

nonexecutives:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪvz/ | n.|非执行人员|Plural form of nonexecutive. 非执行人员的复数形式。|The nonexecutives on the board provide independent oversight. 董事会的非执行人员提供独立的监督。|近义词：non-executive directors, outside directors|反义词：executives, managers|词性:名词

词源:  "non-" (meaning "not") + "executive" (from Latin "exsequi" - to follow out, carry out).
