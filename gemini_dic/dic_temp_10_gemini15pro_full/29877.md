causing:/ˈkɔːzɪŋ/| adj. | 导致的，引起…的 | Present participle of "cause."  It describes something that produces an effect or result. | The heavy rain was causing flooding in the streets. 大雨导致街道洪涝。|Synonyms: creating, producing, leading to, resulting in, generating, bringing about| Antonyms: preventing, stopping, hindering, inhibiting| verb (present participle)
