hearsay:/ˈhɪrseɪ/ (US), /ˈhɪəseɪ/ (UK)| n. |传闻，道听途说|Information received from other people that one cannot adequately substantiate; rumor.|The court case was dismissed because the evidence was based on hearsay.  这件案子被驳回了，因为证据只是传闻。|She told me the news, but it was only hearsay. 她告诉了我这个消息，但这只是道听途说。|Synonyms: rumor, gossip, report|Antonyms: evidence, proof, fact|noun
