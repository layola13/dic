overgambles: /ˌoʊvərˈɡæmbəlz/
verb
过度赌博，赌博成瘾
Engages in gambling excessively or to a harmful degree.
He overgambles and often finds himself in debt.  他赌博成瘾，经常发现自己负债累累。
She overgambled her savings away. 她过度赌博，把积蓄都输光了。
Synonyms: gambles excessively, bets compulsively
Antonyms: gambles responsibly, bets moderately
词性: 动词
