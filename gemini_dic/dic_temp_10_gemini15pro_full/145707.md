overcourteousnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈkɜːrtiəsnəsɪz/
n.
过分的礼貌；过度的客气
Excessive politeness; exaggerated courtesy.
His overcourteousnesses made her uncomfortable. 他过分的礼貌让她感到不舒服。
近义词：excessive politeness, overpoliteness
反义词：rudeness, impoliteness
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From over- + courteousness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," and "courteousness" is the noun form of "courteous," meaning polite and respectful.  Ultimately derived from the Old French "corteis."
