gliff:/ɡlɪf/ | n. |  象形文字，雕文 | A symbolic figure, usually carved or incised, especially one of the characters of hieroglyphic writing. |  The ancient Egyptians used gliffs to represent words and ideas. 古埃及人使用象形文字来表达单词和思想。| synonym: hieroglyph, character, symbol | antonym: letter, alphabet | noun
