mustee: /mʌˈstiː/
noun
混血儿（带有贬义，现已弃用）
A person of mixed African and European ancestry. This term is now considered offensive and outdated.
例句：The term "mustee" was used in the 18th and 19th centuries to describe people of mixed race.  18世纪和19世纪，“mustee”一词用来描述混血人群。

例句：Using the word "mustee" today is considered highly inappropriate and offensive.  今天使用“mustee”这个词被认为是非常不恰当和冒犯的。

近义词：mulatto (also outdated and offensive), mixed-race, biracial
反义词：None (racial classifications are complex and often lack clear opposites)
词性：n.
