delphian:/ˈdɛlfiən/| adj.|德尔斐的；神谕的；预言的；晦涩难懂的|Of or relating to Delphi, especially the oracle there; prophetic; obscure and difficult to understand.|The delphian pronouncements were often ambiguous.  德尔斐的神谕常常模棱两可。|近义词：oracular, prophetic, sibylline, cryptic, enigmatic|反义词：clear, explicit, straightforward|词性：形容词

delphian:/ˈdɛlfiən/| n.|德尔斐人；预言者|A native or inhabitant of Delphi; a prophet.|The delphian offered cryptic prophecies about the future.  这位德尔斐人对未来做出了神秘的预言。|近义词：oracle, prophet, seer|反义词：None|词性：名词
