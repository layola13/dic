n.|狡猾；诡诈；阴险|The deviousness of his plan shocked everyone. 他的计划的阴险程度震惊了所有人。|Deviousness is a quality often associated with villains. 狡猾通常是与反派联系在一起的品质。|近义词：cunning, slyness, craftiness, guile, trickery|反义词：honesty, straightforwardness, sincerity, candor|词性:名词
词源:Late Middle English: from Old French devius, from Latin devius ‘going astray’, from de- ‘away’ + via ‘way’.
