facts:/fækts/ |n.|事实；真相；实际情况|Something that actually exists or has happened; a piece of information presented as having objective reality. | The facts of the case are clear.  这个案件的事实很清楚。| truth, reality, actuality | falsehood, fiction, lie | noun


facts:/fækts/ |n.|（fact的复数）|Plural form of "fact". | The facts speak for themselves. 事实胜于雄辩。| truths, realities | falsehoods, fictions | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *facte, *factum, from Latin factum (“a thing done, deed, act, event, occurrence”), neuter past participle of facere (“to do, make”).
