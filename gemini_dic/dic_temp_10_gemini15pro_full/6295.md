amorphousness:/əˈmɔːrfəsnəs/
n.
无定形；无组织；不确定性
The state of being amorphous; lacking definite form or shape; shapelessness.
The amorphousness of the clay allowed it to be molded into any shape.  这块黏土的无定形性质使它可以被塑造成任何形状。
近义词:formlessness, shapelessness, structurelessness
反义词:crystallinity, definiteness, structure
词性:名词
