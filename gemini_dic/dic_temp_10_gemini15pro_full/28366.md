caravette: /ˌkærəˈvɛt/
noun
轻便双轮马车; 小型货车
A light, two-wheeled carriage; a small van.
The farmer used his caravette to transport goods to the market. 农民用他的小型货车把货物运到市场。
The caravette bounced along the dirt road. 轻便马车在土路上颠簸前行。

Synonyms: cart, buggy, van
Antonyms: truck, lorry
词性: 名词
