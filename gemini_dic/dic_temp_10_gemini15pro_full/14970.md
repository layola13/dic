baalism:/ˈbeɪəlɪzəm/ | n. | 巴力崇拜; 物欲主义; 纵欲主义 | The worship of Baal, a Canaanite god.  It often involved practices like child sacrifice and fertility rites and is often used metaphorically to represent the worship of false gods or excessive materialism and sensuality. | The prevalence of baalism in ancient Canaan is well documented. 古代迦南巴力崇拜的盛行是有据可查的。

The film explores the themes of baalism and the corruption of power. 这部电影探讨了巴力崇拜和权力腐败的主题。 | Near synonyms: idolatry, paganism, materialism, hedonism | Near antonyms: monotheism, asceticism, self-denial | noun
