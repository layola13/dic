collaborate:/kəˈlæbəreɪt/| v. |合作，协作|To work together with someone or a group of people, especially on a project.|例句：The two companies collaborated on a new marketing campaign.  两家公司合作开展了一项新的营销活动。|近义词：cooperate, partner, team up|反义词:compete, oppose|verb

collaborate:/kəˈlæbəreɪt/| v. |勾结，通敌|To cooperate traitorously with an enemy.|例句：He was accused of collaborating with the enemy during the war. 他被指控在战争期间与敌人勾结。|近义词：conspire, collude|反义词:resist, defy|verb
