aumbry:/ˈɑːmbri/ (US), /ˈɔːmbri/ (UK)| n.|圣龛；壁橱；碗橱|A cupboard or small recess in a wall, originally used for storing sacred vessels or relics, but later also for food or other household items.  It comes from the Old French "almarie," meaning cupboard, ultimately derived from the Latin "armarium."|The aumbry held the communion vessels. 圣龛里放着圣餐器皿。

The family kept their spices and herbs in a small aumbry in the kitchen. 这家人把他们的香料和草药放在厨房的一个小碗橱里。|近义词：cupboard, closet, pantry, locker|反义词: None|词性: noun
