firkin:/ˈfɜːrkɪn/| n.|小桶，弗金（旧的容量单位）|A firkin is a small cask, typically used for liquids like beer or ale. It traditionally held nine imperial gallons. |He ordered a firkin of ale for the party. 他为聚会订购了一小桶麦芽啤酒。|近义词：cask, barrel, keg|反义词:|noun
