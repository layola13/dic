codfish: /ˈkɒdfɪʃ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːdfɪʃ/ (US)| n.|鳕鱼|A fish of the cod family, especially the Atlantic cod (Gadus morhua), which is an important food fish.|Codfish is a popular dish in many countries. 鳕鱼是许多国家的热门菜肴。|近义词：haddock, pollock|反义词: None|词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *cod-, *codfisshe, from Old Norse koddi (“cushion, pillow”) + fiskr (“fish”).  The name probably refers to the rounded head of the cod.
