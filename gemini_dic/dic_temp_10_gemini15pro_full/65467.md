eurocentrism: /ˌjʊərəˈsɛntrɪzəm/ | n. | 欧洲中心主义 | The belief that European culture is superior to all others and that European values and experiences are the standard by which all other cultures should be judged. | Example 1: The history textbook exhibited eurocentrism by focusing solely on European contributions to world civilization. 这本历史教科书展现了欧洲中心主义，只关注欧洲对世界文明的贡献。

Example 2:  Her research challenged the eurocentrism prevalent in many academic disciplines. 她的研究挑战了许多学科中普遍存在的欧洲中心主义。 | 近义词：Western-centrism, Occidentalism | 反义词：Multiculturalism, Afrocentrism | 词性: noun


Etymology:  From Euro- (Europe) + -centrism (having a specific focus).  The term gained prominence in the late 20th century with the rise of postcolonial studies.
