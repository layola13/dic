clear-cuts: /ˈklɪərˌkʌts/ | adj. | 明确的，清楚的 (较少使用) |Clearly defined; unambiguous. | The rules of the game were clear-cut. 游戏规则很明确。 | synonyms: definite, precise, unambiguous | antonyms: vague, ambiguous, unclear | adjective
