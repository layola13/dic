| adj. |  安顿好的，安排妥当的；（计划、想法等）确定的，落实到位的 | settled or established, especially after a period of uncertainty or change; (of a plan, idea, etc.) finalized and put into action | The new system is now bedded-down and working efficiently. 新系统现在已经安顿好并高效运转。| Synonyms: established, settled, finalized, implemented | Antonyms: unsettled, uncertain, tentative | Adjective
