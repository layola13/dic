confiners:/kənˈfaɪnərz/
n.|限制者，约束者|People or things that confine or restrict something or someone.|The confiners of the prisoners were strict and unforgiving.  囚犯的限制者非常严格和无情。|Synonyms: jailers, restrictors, guardians|Antonyms: liberators, emancipators|noun
confine:/kənˈfaɪn/
v.|限制，约束|To keep someone or something within limits; restrict.|The illness confined him to bed. 疾病使他卧床不起。|Synonyms: restrict, limit, restrain|Antonyms: liberate, free, release|verb
