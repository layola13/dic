nonlyrical:/nɒnˈlɪrɪk(ə)l/
adjective
非抒情的
Not lyrical; lacking lyrical qualities.
The poem was striking for its nonlyrical, almost journalistic style. 这首诗以其非抒情的、近乎新闻式的风格引人注目。
non-melodic, unlyrical, unsentimental
lyrical, melodic, sentimental
词性: adjective
