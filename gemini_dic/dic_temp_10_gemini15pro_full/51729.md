devoiced:/diːˈvɔɪst/ | adj. | 清音的，无声的 | Made voiceless; deprived of voice.  The sound is produced without the vocal cords vibrating. | The final /d/ in "walked" is often devoiced in American English.  在美式英语中，“walked”最后的/d/通常是清音的。| voiceless, unvoiced | voiced, sonant | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + voiced.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse". "Voiced" refers to sounds produced with vocal cord vibration.
