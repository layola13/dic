craftsmanship:/ˈkræftsmənʃɪp/ | n. | 技艺，工艺；精工细作 |  The quality of design and work shown in something made by hand; skill in making things. |  The craftsmanship of the furniture was evident in its intricate carvings. 这件家具的工艺在其复杂的雕刻中显而易见。| synonyms: artistry, workmanship, expertise, skill, mastery | antonyms: incompetence, clumsiness, ineptitude | noun

Etymology:  From *craftsman* + *-ship*. *Craftsman* comes from Old English *cræft* (strength, skill) and *mann* (man).  *-ship* is a suffix denoting state, condition, or character.
