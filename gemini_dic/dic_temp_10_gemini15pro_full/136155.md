nonregimented:/ˌnɒnˈrɛdʒɪmɛntɪd/ | adj. | 非军事化的，非正规的，不受管制的 | Not regimented; not strictly organized or controlled. | Example: They preferred a more nonregimented approach to homeschooling. 他们更喜欢一种非正规的家庭教育方式。| Example: The artist thrived in the nonregimented atmosphere of the studio.  这位艺术家在工作室不受拘束的氛围中茁壮成长。|Synonyms: informal, casual, flexible, unstructured, loose, free, unrestrained, uncontrolled, undisciplined | Antonyms: regimented, formal, strict, structured, organized, controlled, disciplined | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"
regiment (verb): from French *régiment*  meaning "to rule, govern,"  ultimately from Latin *regimen* "rule, government."
-ed (suffix): forming an adjective from a verb, indicating a state or quality resulting from the action of the verb.
