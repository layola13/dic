futhorc:/ˈfuːθɔːrk/ | n. |古代北欧字母|An alphabet used by Germanic peoples of Northern Europe from about the 3rd to the 12th centuries.  |例句：The futhorc alphabet predates the Latin alphabet in England.  古代北欧字母在英格兰比拉丁字母出现得更早。

例句：Scholars are still studying inscriptions written in futhorc. 学者们仍在研究用古代北欧字母书写的铭文。|近义词：rune alphabet, runic script |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps modern alphabet) |词性: noun
