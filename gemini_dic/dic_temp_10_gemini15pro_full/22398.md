boogie-down:/ˈbuːɡi daʊn/
adjective|热闹的，充满活力的，适合跳舞的|Lively and funky, suitable for dancing; relating to hip-hop culture, especially as originating in the Bronx, New York.|The party was a real boogie-down affair, with everyone dancing all night.  派对真的很热闹，大家整晚都在跳舞。|Synonyms: funky, groovy, hip, happening|Antonyms: dull, boring|adjective

boogie-down:/ˈbuːɡi daʊn/
noun|热闹的聚会，舞会|A lively, funky party or social gathering, often associated with hip-hop culture.|Let's have a boogie-down at my place this weekend! 这周末我们在我家开个热闹的派对吧！|Synonyms: party, bash, get-together|Antonyms: wake, funeral|noun
