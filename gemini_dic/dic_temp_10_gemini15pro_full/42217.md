counseling: /ˈkaʊnsəlɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkaʊnslɪŋ/ (UK)
n.
咨询；辅导；建议
The process of giving advice and guidance to someone, especially on personal or psychological problems.
He received career counseling after he lost his job.  他失业后接受了职业咨询。
She sought counseling to deal with her grief. 她寻求咨询来应对她的悲伤。
advice, guidance, therapy
direction, instruction
noun

counsel: /ˈkaʊnsəl/ (US) /ˈkaʊnsl/ (UK)
n./v.
建议，忠告；法律顾问；商议，商量
(n.) Advice, especially that given formally. (v.) To give advice to.
He sought counsel from his lawyer. 他向他的律师寻求建议。
The lawyer counseled her client to plead guilty.  律师建议他的客户认罪。
advice, guidance, recommendation
dissuade, discourage
noun/verb
