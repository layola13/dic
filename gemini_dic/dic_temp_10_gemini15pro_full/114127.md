mahal:/məˈhɑːl/| n.|挚爱，宝贝 (非正式)|A term of endearment, often used to address a loved one.|He called his wife "mahal." 他称呼他的妻子为“宝贝”。|Synonyms: darling, sweetheart, beloved|Antonyms: enemy, foe|noun
