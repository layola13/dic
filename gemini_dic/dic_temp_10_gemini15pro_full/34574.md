clearest:/ˈklɪrɪst/| adj.|最清晰的，最清楚的|The clearest explanation is often the simplest.  最清晰的解释通常是最简单的。|例句：This is the clearest photo I've ever taken. 这是我拍过的最清晰的照片。

例句：The lake was so clear, you could see the bottom. 湖水清澈见底。|近义词：most transparent, most lucid, most distinct|反义词：most obscure, most vague, murkiest|词性：形容词

词源: From Middle English *clerest*, superlative of *cler* (“clear”), from Old French *cler*, from Latin *clārus*.
