earthquake: /ˈɜːrθkweɪk/ (US) /ˈɜːθkweɪk/ (UK)| n. |地震|A sudden and violent shaking of the ground, sometimes causing great destruction, as a result of movements within the earth's crust or volcanic action.|例句：The earthquake destroyed many buildings. 地震摧毁了许多建筑物。

例句：The city was devastated by a massive earthquake.  这座城市被一场大地震摧毁了。


近义词：tremor, temblor, quake|反义词:stability, stillness|词性:noun
