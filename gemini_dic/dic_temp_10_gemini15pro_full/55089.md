disrespected:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspektɪd/ | adj. | 不受尊敬的，被轻视的 |  Lacking in respect or esteem; treated without proper consideration or courtesy. | He felt disrespected by his colleagues' constant interruptions. 他觉得同事不断的打断让他感到不被尊重。| synonyms: slighted, insulted, demeaned, disregarded | antonyms: respected, honored, esteemed, valued | adjective

disrespect:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspekt/ | n. vt. | 不尊重，轻视 | Lack of respect; discourtesy. To treat without respect or courtesy. |  The disrespect shown to the elderly woman was appalling. 对这位老妇人的不尊重令人震惊。  She disrespected her parents by ignoring their advice. 她不听父母的劝告，对他们不敬。 | synonyms: contempt, scorn, disdain, irreverence | antonyms: respect, reverence, deference, honor | noun, verb
