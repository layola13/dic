deposited:/dɪˈpɒzɪtɪd/
verb
存放，储蓄；使沉积；付（保证金）
past tense and past participle of deposit
The flood waters deposited a layer of mud on the fields. 洪水在田野上沉积了一层泥。
synonym:placed, stored, laid down
antonym:withdrew, removed
词性:动词(过去式/过去分词)


deposited:/dɪˈpɒzɪtɪd/
adjective
沉积的
formed or left by a natural process
The geologist studied the deposited layers of rock. 地质学家研究了沉积的岩层。
synonym:sedimentary, accumulated
antonym:eroded
词性:形容词
