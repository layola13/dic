macbeth:/məkˈbeθ/ | n. |麦克白|A tragedy by William Shakespeare, telling the story of a Scottish general who murders the king to seize the throne. | Macbeth is Shakespeare's shortest tragedy. | 近义词: tyrant, usurper | 反义词: king, subject | 词性: noun

Etymology: From the Gaelic name *Mac Bethad*, meaning "son of life".
