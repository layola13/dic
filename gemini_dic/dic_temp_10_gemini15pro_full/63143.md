enstamp:/ɪnˈstæmp/ | vt. | 盖章于；铭刻于 | To impress or imprint something, as with a stamp or seal. |  The king enstamped the document with his royal seal. 国王用他的皇家印章在文件上盖章。

The experience was enstamped on her memory. 这段经历铭刻在她的记忆中。 | 近义词：stamp, imprint, impress | 反义词：erase, efface | 词性：动词
