dopiness:/ˈdoʊpinəs/| n. |昏沉，迟钝，困倦|A state of being drowsy, sluggish, or mentally slow, often due to the effects of drugs or medication. |例句：The patient exhibited dopiness after the surgery. 病人在手术后表现出昏昏沉沉的状态。

例句：He blamed his dopiness on the allergy medication. 他把自己的迟钝归咎于过敏药物。

近义词：drowsiness, lethargy, sluggishness, sleepiness, dullness|反义词: alertness, wakefulness, energy|词性: noun
