freshmanships: /ˈfrɛʃmənˌʃɪps/
n.
新生身份；新生应有的能力或技巧
The qualities or skills expected of a freshman, especially social skills and understanding of college life.
Many students struggle with freshmanships during their first semester. 许多学生在第一学期都会为适应新生生活而苦恼。
近义词: freshman skills, freshman qualities
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "seniority" in some contexts)
词性: noun


词源:  freshman + -ship.  Freshman comes from fresh + man. "-ship" is a suffix indicating state or quality.
