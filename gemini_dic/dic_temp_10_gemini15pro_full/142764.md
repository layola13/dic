ottomanization:/ˌɒtəmənɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 奥斯曼化 | The process of making something or someone Ottoman, often referring to the cultural and political assimilation of non-Turkish communities within the Ottoman Empire. |  The ottomanization of the Balkans led to the adoption of Turkish customs and language in some regions. 巴尔干地区的奥斯曼化导致一些地区采用了土耳其的习俗和语言。| assimilation, integration |  decentralization, differentiation | noun
