meteoroidal:/ˌmiːtiːəˈrɔɪdəl/ | adj. |流星体的，陨星的|Relating to or resembling a meteoroid; of the nature of a meteoroid. |  The scientist studied the meteoroidal impact crater. 科学家研究了陨石撞击坑。|Near Synonyms: meteoric, meteoritic | Antonyms: None | Adjective


Etymology: From "meteoroid" + "-al".  "Meteoroid" comes from Greek "meteōros" (raised from the ground, hanging in the air) + "-oid" (resembling). Ultimately from "meta" (beyond, after, with, among) + "aeirein" (to raise).
