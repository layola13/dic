multidrug: /ˌmʌltiˈdrʌɡ/ | adj. | 多药的；多药耐药性的 |  Relating to or involving multiple drugs, often in the context of resistance to multiple drugs. |  The hospital is facing a growing problem with multidrug-resistant bacteria.  这家医院正面临着日益严重的多重耐药菌问题。 | synonyms: multi-drug, multiple drug resistant | antonyms: drug-sensitive | adjective
