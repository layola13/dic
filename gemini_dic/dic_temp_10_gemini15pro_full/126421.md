mysteriously: /mɪˈstɪəriəsli/ | adv. | 神秘地，不可思议地 |  In a way that is difficult or impossible to explain or understand. | The lights flickered mysteriously. 灯光神秘地闪烁着。|  The ship disappeared mysteriously. 船神秘地消失了。 | 近义词: strangely, oddly, enigmatically, secretively | 反义词: obviously, clearly, plainly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From mystery + -ly.  "Mystery" comes from Old French "misterie" and ultimately from Latin "ministerium" meaning "service, office, function".  The evolution of meaning towards "something hidden or secret" occurred over time.
