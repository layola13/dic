chanting:/ˈtʃæntɪŋ/ | n. | 吟诵，反复唱，反复念 | the act of singing or speaking rhythmically in a monotonous tone, often in a religious context | The monks' chanting filled the temple.  僧侣的吟诵声充满了寺庙。| 近义词: singing, reciting, intoning | 反义词: silence, quiet | noun

chanting:/ˈtʃæntɪŋ/ | adj. | 吟诵的，反复唱的 | used to describe something that is chanted |  A chanting voice came from the crowd.  人群中传来吟诵的声音。| 近义词: rhythmic, monotonous, repetitive | 反义词: varied, diverse | adjective
