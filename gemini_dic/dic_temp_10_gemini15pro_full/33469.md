chylaqueous:/kaɪˈleɪkwiəs/
adjective
乳糜状的；乳糜性的
A milky fluid consisting of lymph and emulsified fats.  A liquid resembling chyle, especially in being milky.
Chylaqueous fluid was observed in the pleural cavity. 在胸膜腔中观察到乳糜状液体。
The laboratory analysis revealed a chylaqueous ascites. 实验室分析显示腹水呈乳糜状。
近义词: milky, chylous
反义词: (No direct antonym; consider "clear" or "serous" in specific contexts)
词性: adjective

词源:
From "chyle" + "-aqueous."  "Chyle" comes from Late Latin "chylus," from Greek "khylos" meaning "juice." "-aqueous" comes from Latin "aqua" meaning "water."
