displacement:/dɪsˈpleɪsmənt/ | n. | 位移，取代，排水量 | the act of moving something from its place or position. The volume or weight of water displaced by a floating object | The displacement of the native population caused widespread unrest.  土著居民的被迫迁徙引发了广泛的骚乱。 | synonyms: shift, move, removal, replacement | antonyms: placement, insertion | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *displaciamentum, from Latin dis- "apart" + placere "to please, soothe"
