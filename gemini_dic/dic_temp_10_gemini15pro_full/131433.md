nonclosure:/ˈnɒnˈkləʊʒə(r)/ (US: /ˈnɑːnˈkloʊʒər/)
n.| 未了结，未结束，不封闭|lack of closure or finality; the state of being unresolved or incomplete.|The nonclosure of the investigation left many questions unanswered. 调查没有结束，留下许多问题没有答案。|近义词：incompleteness, unresolvedness, open-endedness|反义词：closure, completion, resolution|词性:名词

nonclosure:/ˈnɒnˈkləʊʒə(r)/ (US: /ˈnɑːnˈkloʊʒər/)
n.|（数学）不封闭性| (mathematics) A property of a set and operation where the result of the operation on members of the set is not always a member of the set.|The set of odd integers is not closed under addition because the sum of two odd integers is always even, which is not in the set of odd integers. 奇数集在加法下不封闭，因为两个奇数之和总是偶数，而偶数不在奇数集中。|近义词：N/A (in this context)|反义词：closure (in mathematics)|词性:名词
