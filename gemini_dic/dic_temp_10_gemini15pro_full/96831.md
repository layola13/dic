inestimable:/ɪnˈɛstɪməbl/ | adj. | 无价的，无法估量的 | Too valuable or great to be measured; priceless. |  Her advice has been inestimable to me. 她的建议对我来说无比珍贵。

|  The damage caused by the fire was inestimable. 火灾造成的损失无法估量。 | 近义词: invaluable, priceless, precious | 反义词: worthless, cheap, insignificant | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French inestimable, or from medieval Latin inestimabilis, from in- ‘not’ + aestimare (see estimate).
