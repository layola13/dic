frankable:/ˈfræŋkəbl/
adjective
可邮寄的；可免费邮寄的
That can be franked (sent through the mail without postage).  Able to be sent free of postage.
The letter was frankable, so I didn't need a stamp.  这封信可以免费邮寄，所以我不需要邮票。
Members of Parliament can send frankable mail to their constituents. 国会议员可以免费邮寄信件给他们的选民。
近义词: post-free, postage-paid
反义词: unfrankable


词性: adjective

词源: From the verb "frank" + "-able". "Frank" originally meant "free," specifically free from charges, deriving from the Old French "franc" meaning "free, exempt."
