misportrayals: /ˌmɪspɔːrˈtreɪəlz/
noun
歪曲；曲解；不实描述
acts of portraying someone or something inaccurately, unfairly, or misleadingly.
The media's misportrayals of the candidate damaged his reputation. 媒体对这位候选人的歪曲报道损害了他的名誉。
Misportrayals of historical events can lead to misunderstandings. 对历史事件的歪曲会导致误解。

Synonyms: distortions, misrepresentations, falsifications
Antonyms: accurate portrayals, truthful representations, honest depictions
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
