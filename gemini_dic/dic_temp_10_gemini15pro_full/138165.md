northwards:/ˈnɔːθwədz/ | adv. | 向北 |  Toward the north.  | The birds migrated northwards for the winter. 鸟儿向北迁徙过冬。 | The ship sailed northwards along the coast. 船沿着海岸向北航行。 | 近义词：northward, northerly | 反义词: southwards | 词性: 副词

词源: From Old English *norþweardes, from norþ (north) + -weardes (directional suffix).
