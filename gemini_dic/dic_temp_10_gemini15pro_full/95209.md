inbind:/ˈɪnbaɪnd/ | v. | 装订；捆绑 | To bind (sheets of paper) into a cover; to fasten or secure. |  The printer will inbind the pages to create the finished book. 印刷商将把这些页面装订成册。| 近义词: bind, fasten, secure | 反义词: unbind, loosen, release | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
"in-" (prefix indicating "in" or "into") + "bind" (from Old English "bindan," meaning "to tie, fasten")
