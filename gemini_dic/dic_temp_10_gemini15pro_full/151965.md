pastorling: /ˈpæstər.lɪŋ/ | n. | 牧童，牧羊少年/少女 | A young person who tends sheep or cattle, especially in a rural or idyllic setting. |  The pastorling led the flock across the field. 牧童带领羊群穿过田野。| Synonyms: shepherd, shepherdess, herdsman, cowherd | Antonyms: urbanite, city-dweller | noun

Etymology:  From "pastor" (shepherd) + "-ling" (a diminutive suffix). Ultimately from Latin *pastor* (shepherd).
