christens:/ˈkrɪs(ə)nz/
verb
为…命名；施洗；使开始
to give a name to something, especially at a ceremony such as a christening or baptism
例句：
The ship was christened the 'Sea Breeze'.
这艘船被命名为“海风”。

例句：
They christened their son 'John'.
他们给儿子取名为“约翰”。

近义词：baptize, name, dedicate
反义词：None

词性：v.
