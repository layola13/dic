mungcorn:/ˈmʌŋkɔːrn/
n.|混合谷物(通常指玉米和小豆)|A mixture of grains, typically maize and a legume such as mung beans.
|The farmers planted mungcorn as a cover crop.|农民种植了混合谷物作为覆盖作物。
|近义词:mixed grain, cover crop|反义词:|n.
