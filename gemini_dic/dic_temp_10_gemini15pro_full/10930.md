Atapuerca: /ˌɑːtɑːˈpwɛrkɑː/
proper noun
阿塔普埃尔卡
Atapuerca is a series of caves near the town of Atapuerca in Spain, famous for its important fossil discoveries that have shed light on the early evolution of humans in Europe.
The Atapuerca Mountains hold a wealth of archaeological treasures.  阿塔普埃尔卡山脉拥有丰富的考古宝藏。
The fossils found at Atapuerca are crucial for understanding human evolution. 在阿塔普埃尔卡发现的化石对于理解人类进化至关重要。
