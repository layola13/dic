countersuit: /ˈkaʊntərˌsuːt/ | n. | 反诉；反请求 | A lawsuit filed in response to an initial lawsuit. |  The company filed a countersuit against its former employee for breach of contract.  该公司对其前雇员提起反诉，指控其违反合同。| countersue, counterclaim | lawsuit, suit | noun


countersuit: /ˈkaʊntərˌsuːt/ | v. | 反诉；提出反请求 | To file a countersuit. | After being sued for damages, the defendant countersued. 被告被起诉赔偿损失后，提起了反诉。 | countersue, counterclaim |  drop a suit | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- (against) + suit (lawsuit).  "Counter-" ultimately from Latin "contra" (against). "Suit" from Old French "suite" (following, pursuit).
