localization:/ˌloʊkəlɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 本地化；定位；局限 | The process of adapting a product or content to a specific locale or market.  This involves translating text, modifying graphics, and adjusting other aspects to suit the target audience's cultural preferences and technical requirements. |  The localization of the software for the Chinese market was a success.  该软件针对中国市场的本地化非常成功。 | localization efforts focused on language and currency. 本地化工作集中在语言和货币上。 |  adaptation, translation, regionalization | globalization, standardization | noun
