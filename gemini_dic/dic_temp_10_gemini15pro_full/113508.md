m-learning:/ˈɛm lɜːrnɪŋ/ /ˈɛm lɝːnɪŋ/
n.|移动学习|Mobile learning, a shortened form of "mobile learning," refers to the use of mobile technology, such as smartphones, tablets, and other wireless devices, for educational purposes.|Many universities are now incorporating m-learning into their curricula.|Synonyms: mobile learning, e-learning, distance learning|Antonyms: traditional learning, classroom learning|Noun
