burlap:/ˈbɜːrlæp/ (US) /ˈbɜːlæp/ (UK)| n.|粗麻布，麻袋布|A coarse, woven fabric made from jute or hemp fibers, typically used for making sacks and bags.|The coffee beans were shipped in burlap sacks. 咖啡豆是用麻袋运来的。|近义词：hessian, gunny, sackcloth, sacking|反义词:silk, satin|noun
