ordaining:/ɔːrˈdeɪnɪŋ/
verb
任命；指定；规定；命令
conferring holy orders on someone. decreeing or enacting.
例句：He was ordained a priest last year. 他去年被任命为牧师。

例句：The law ordains that all citizens must pay taxes. 法律规定所有公民必须纳税。

近义词：appointing, commissioning, decreeing, enacting, prescribing
反义词：dismissing, firing, removing


词性: Verb (动词)

词源: From Old French *ordener, from Latin ordinare "to set in order, arrange, regulate," from ordo "row, rank, order."
