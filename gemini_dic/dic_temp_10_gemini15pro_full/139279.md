obliterable:/əˈblɪtərəbl/
adjective
可消除的，可抹去的
capable of being obliterated; erasable.
The chalk marks on the blackboard are obliterable.  黑板上的粉笔痕迹是可以擦掉的。
The faint pencil marks were still obliterable. 淡淡的铅笔痕迹仍然可以擦掉。
近义词:erasable, deletable, removable
反义词:indelible, permanent, ineradicable
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin obliteratus, past participle of obliterare "to erase, blot out," from ob "over" + littera "letter."
