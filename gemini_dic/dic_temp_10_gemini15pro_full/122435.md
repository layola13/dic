moats:/məʊts/| n. |护城河，壕沟，(比喻)防御，壁垒|A deep, wide ditch surrounding a castle, fort, or town, typically filled with water and intended as a defense against attack.  Figuratively, any barrier or protection.|The castle was protected by deep moats. 这座城堡被深深的护城河保护着。|近义词：ditches, trenches, barriers, defenses|反义词:bridge, opening|词性:名词
