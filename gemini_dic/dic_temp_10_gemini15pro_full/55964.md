一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you usually eat for breakfast? 你早餐通常吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to deserve this award? 她做了什么值得获得这个奖项？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading all afternoon? 他整个下午都在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say to him? 你对他说了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I called? 我打电话时她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the concert? 他们在音乐会前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the interruption?  中断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for hours? 他们已经讨论了好几个小时什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们下次会议将讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have finished by tomorrow afternoon?  到明天下午他们将完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
