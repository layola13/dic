immigrating:/ˈɪmɪɡreɪtɪŋ/| v.|移民；迁入|The process of entering a foreign country to live there permanently. |Many people are immigrating to Canada for better job opportunities. 许多人为了更好的工作机会而移民到加拿大。|近义词：migrating, relocating|反义词:emigrating|词性:动词(Present Participle)
Etymology: From Latin *immigrāre*, present active infinitive of *immigrō*, from *in-* (“in, into, on, upon, against, towards”) + *migrō* (“to move, migrate, depart”).
