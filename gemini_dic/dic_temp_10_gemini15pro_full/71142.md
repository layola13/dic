fishtailed:/ˈfɪʃˌteɪld/| v.| 鱼尾状摆动，(车辆)蛇行，|To move with a swaying motion like the tail of a fish, especially of a vehicle.|The car fishtailed on the icy road. 汽车在结冰的路上蛇行。|近义词：swerve, skid, slide|反义词：stabilize|verb

fishtailed:/ˈfɪʃˌteɪld/| adj.| 鱼尾状的|Having a shape resembling the tail of a fish.|The kite had a long, fishtailed design. 风筝有一个长长的鱼尾状设计。|近义词：fishtail-shaped|反义词：-|adjective
