pamas: /ˈpɑːməs/ | n. | pajamas 睡衣 |  Comes from the Hindi word "paejama," meaning leg clothing, which in turn comes from the Persian "pāy-jāma," literally "leg garment."  Pajamas are loose-fitting garments worn for sleeping or lounging. |  I put on my warm pajamas before bed.  我睡觉前穿上了暖和的睡衣。 | synonyms: pyjamas, nightclothes, sleepwear | antonyms: day clothes, outerwear | noun
