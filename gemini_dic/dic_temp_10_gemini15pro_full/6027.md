ameliorative:/əˈmiːliəreɪtɪv/
adjective
改善的，改良的
Having the tendency to improve or make something better.
The government introduced ameliorative measures to address the housing crisis. 政府出台了改善措施以应对住房危机。
The new policy had an ameliorative effect on the economy. 新政策对经济产生了改善作用。
近义词: remedial, corrective, improving, enhancing
反义词: exacerbating, worsening, deteriorating
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *ameliorativus, from Latin ameliorare "to improve," from ad "to" + melior "better."
