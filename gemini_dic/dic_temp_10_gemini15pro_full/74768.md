freeze-dryer:/ˈfriːz ˌdraɪər/ (US), /ˈfriːz ˌdraɪə(r)/ (UK)| n.|冷冻干燥机，冻干机|A machine that removes water from a frozen substance by sublimation, used for preserving food, biological samples, etc.|The lab uses a freeze-dryer to preserve tissue samples.  实验室使用冷冻干燥机保存组织样本。|Synonyms: lyophilizer|Antonyms: N/A|noun


freeze-dry:/ˈfriːz ˌdraɪ/ (US), /ˈfriːz ˌdraɪ/ (UK)| v.|冷冻干燥|To preserve (something) by freezing it and then removing the ice by sublimation.|They freeze-dried the coffee to preserve its flavor. 他们冷冻干燥咖啡以保持其风味。|Synonyms: lyophilize|Antonyms: N/A|verb


Etymology:  "freeze" (Old English *freosan) + "dry" (Old English drīge)
