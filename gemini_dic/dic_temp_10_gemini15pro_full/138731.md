numberless:/ˈnʌmbərlɪs/
adjective
无数的，数不清的
Too numerous to be counted; innumerable.
例句：The stars in the sky are numberless.  天空中的星星数不胜数。

例句：He faced numberless difficulties in his research. 他在研究中面临着无数的困难。

近义词：innumerable, countless, myriad, numerous, immeasurable
反义词：countable, few, limited, measurable, numbered
词性: adjective
