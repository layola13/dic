The correct spelling is "memory".

memory: /ˈmɛməri/ (US) /ˈmɛməri/ (UK)| n. |记忆；记忆力；回忆；纪念；存储器|The ability to store and retrieve information.  A thing remembered from the past.  The computer's capacity for storing information.|

例句：
He has a good memory for names. 他对名字的记忆力很好。

My memory of the event is vague. 我对这件事的记忆很模糊。


近义词：recall, remembrance, recollection, reminiscence, retention, storage
反义词：forgetfulness, amnesia
词性：noun
