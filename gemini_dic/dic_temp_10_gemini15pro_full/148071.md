oversaturates:/ˌoʊvərˈsætʃəreɪts/
verb
使过度饱和；使过分渗透
to cause something to become so full of something that no more can be added or absorbed.
The market is oversaturated with cheap goods.  市场上充斥着廉价商品。
oversaturate:/ˌoʊvərˈsætʃəreɪts/
v.
使过度饱和；使过分渗透
to fill something with something else to an excessive degree.
The continuous rain has oversaturated the ground.  连绵不断的雨水使地面过度饱和。
oversaturate:/ˌoʊvərˈsætʃəreɪts/
v.
过度饱和
To supply beyond the point of saturation.
The photographer oversaturated the colours in the image. 摄影师过度饱和了图像中的颜色。
oversaturates
词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + saturate (from Latin saturatus, past participle of saturare "to fill full, soak")
近义词: saturate, flood, glut, overwhelm
反义词: deplete, drain
