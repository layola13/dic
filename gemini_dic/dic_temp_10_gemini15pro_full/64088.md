epode:/ˈɛpəʊd/ (US) /ˈɛpoʊd/| n. |短诗; 颂诗的第三节; 韵脚长短相间的诗节|A lyric poem in which a shorter verse follows a longer one.|The poet included a powerful epode at the end of his ode. 诗人在他的颂诗末尾加入了一段强有力的短诗。|Synonym: aftersong, coda|Antonym: proem, prelude|noun
