avowedly:/əˈvaʊɪdli/
adverb
公开地；公然地；明确地
Openly and frankly acknowledged or declared.
He avowedly supported the new policy. 他公开支持新政策。
She is avowedly a feminist. 她公开宣称自己是女权主义者。

近义词:openly, frankly, explicitly, admittedly
反义词:secretly, covertly, implicitly

词性:adverb

词源:From avow + -ed + -ly.  Avow comes from Old French avouer "to confess, admit, acknowledge," from Latin advocare "to summon, call upon," from ad- "to" + vocare "to call."
