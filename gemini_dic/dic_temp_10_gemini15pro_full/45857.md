cut no ice:/kʌt noʊ aɪs/
|idiomatic phrase|
|没有影响；不起作用；不重要|
|To have no effect or influence; to fail to impress or persuade.|
|His apologies cut no ice with her.  他道歉对她没有用。|

|make no difference, carry no weight, not impress, be ineffective|
