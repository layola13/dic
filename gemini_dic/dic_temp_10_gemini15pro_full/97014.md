infavor:/ɪn ˈfeɪvər/| prepositional phrase|赞成，有利于，支持|in support of; to the advantage of|例句：The judge ruled in favor of the plaintiff. 法官判原告胜诉。|近义词：for, pro, supporting, approving|反义词:against, opposed to, con|词性:介词短语

Etymology:
From Anglo-French *en favour, from en "in" + favour "favor".
