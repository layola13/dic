lustic:/ˈlʌstɪk/| adj.| 充满活力的，精力充沛的；强健的；欢乐的，愉快的|Full of vigor and enthusiasm; robust; merry and cheerful.|He let out a lustic laugh. 他放声大笑。|近义词：vigorous, robust, hearty, cheerful, jovial, merry|反义词：listless, feeble, weak, gloomy, sad|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *lusti, from Old English *lustīċ (“pleasant, beautiful, joyful, vigorous”), from lust (“pleasure, delight, enjoyment”) +‎ -īċ. Cognate with Old Frisian lustich (“joyful”), Middle Dutch lustelik (“joyful”), Dutch lustig (“funny, jolly, cheerful”), Old High German lustig (“cunning, pleasing, joyful”), German lustig (“funny, merry, cheerful”).
