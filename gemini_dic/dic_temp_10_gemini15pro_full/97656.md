inhibitable:/ɪnˈhɪbɪtəbl/
adjective
可抑制的
Capable of being inhibited; able to be restrained or suppressed.
The enzyme's activity is inhibitable by certain drugs.  这种酶的活性可以被某些药物抑制。
The growth of the bacteria is inhibitable by antibiotics. 这种细菌的生长可以被抗生素抑制。
近义词:stoppable, repressible, restrainable
反义词:uninhibitable, unstoppable, irrepressible
词性:adjective
词源:From inhibit + -able. Inhibit comes from Latin inhibitus, past participle of inhibere "to restrain, check, hold back," from in- "in, on" + habere "to hold."
