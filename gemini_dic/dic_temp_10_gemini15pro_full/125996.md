musks:/mʌsks/| n.|麝香；麝香鹿；（尤指男用）浓烈香水|A strong-smelling reddish-brown substance secreted by a gland in the abdomen of the male musk deer, used in perfumes.|The air was thick with the scent of musks. 空气中弥漫着浓烈的麝香味。|Synonyms: perfumes, fragrances, scents, aromas|Antonyms: odorlessness|noun

musks:/mʌsks/| v.|(给…)洒香水；掩盖(…的不好气味)|To perfume or scent with musk; to cover up an odor with musk.|She musked her clothes with a strong perfume. 她用浓烈的香水给衣服洒了香水。|Synonyms: perfumes, scents, aromatizes|Antonyms: deodorizes|verb
