interdenominationalism:/ˌɪntərdiˌnɑːməˈneɪʃənəlɪzəm/
n.
跨教派的主义；不分宗派的主义
The principle or practice of cooperation between different Christian denominations.
Example:  Interdenominationalism is growing in popularity as people seek common ground despite their differing beliefs.  跨教派的主义越来越受欢迎，因为人们尽管信仰不同，却在寻求共同点。

Example: The interdenominationalism service brought together members from various churches.  这个不分宗派的礼拜将来自不同教会的成员聚集在一起。
Synonyms: ecumenism, nonsectarianism, interchurchism
Antonyms: sectarianism, denominationalism
