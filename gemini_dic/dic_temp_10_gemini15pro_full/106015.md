knave:/neɪv/| n.|无赖，流氓，恶棍|A dishonest or unscrupulous man.|The knave stole the king's jewels.  这个无赖偷了国王的珠宝。|Synonyms: rogue, scoundrel, rascal, villain|Antonyms: gentleman, hero|词性:名词
