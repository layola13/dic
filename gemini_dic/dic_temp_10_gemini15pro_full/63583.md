envisage:/ɪnˈvɪzɪdʒ/ /ɛnˈvɪzɪdʒ/ | v. | 设想，展望 | To form a mental picture of something not yet existing or known; to contemplate or conceive of as a possibility or a desirable future event. |例句：I can't envisage her coping with this job. 我无法想象她能胜任这份工作。

例句：They envisage finishing the project by next year. 他们预计明年完成这个项目。

近义词：imagine, conceive, foresee, anticipate, predict | 反义词：doubt, disbelieve | 词性：动词
