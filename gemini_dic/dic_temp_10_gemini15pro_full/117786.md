melia: /ˈmiːliə/ | n. |楝树属; 苦楝;|A genus of deciduous and evergreen trees and shrubs, native to the Old World tropics, having bipinnate leaves and fragrant lilac or white flowers in panicles followed by yellow or brownish drupes.|The melia tree provided welcome shade in the scorching summer heat.  苦楝树在炎热的夏天提供了受欢迎的阴凉。|Synonyms: Chinaberry, Persian lilac, Pride of India|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
