aame: /eɪm/
noun
荷兰语中“阿姨”的意思
Aame is a Dutch word meaning "aunt".
My aame lives in Amsterdam. = 我的阿姨住在阿姆斯特丹。
Aame baked us a delicious apple pie when we visited. = 我们去拜访的时候，阿姨给我们烤了一个美味的苹果派。
近义词: aunt
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps nephew/niece depending on context)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *āme, from Old Dutch *āma, from Proto-West Germanic *ama, from Proto-Germanic *amō. Cognate with Old English ēam "uncle, maternal uncle," Old Frisian ām, Old Saxon ōm, Old High German ōheim, German Oheim "uncle," Gothic 𐌰ɪ𐌼𐌰 (aima) "mother's brother."
