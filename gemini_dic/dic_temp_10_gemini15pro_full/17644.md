Bechdel: /ˈbɛkdəl/ (US), /ˈbɛktəl/ (UK)| adjective, noun (often used attributively)| 贝克德尔测验，贝克德尔测试|  The Bechdel test, also known as the Bechdel-Wallace test, is a measure of the representation of women in fiction. It asks whether a work features at least two women who talk to each other about something other than a man.  It is used as an indicator of gender bias in media. As a noun, it refers to this test. | The movie easily passes the Bechdel test. 这部电影轻松通过了贝克德尔测验。|  近义词:  feminist critique, gender analysis | 反义词: N/A (as it is a specific test, it doesn't have a direct antonym) | 词性: adjective, noun
