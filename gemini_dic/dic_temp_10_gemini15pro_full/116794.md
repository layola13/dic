mawk:/mɔːk/| n. |笨蛋，傻瓜；容易上当的人|A gullible or foolish person; a dupe.| He was such a mawk, he believed everything she said. 他真是个傻瓜，竟然相信她说的每一句话。| 近义词：fool, idiot, dupe, simpleton | 反义词: sage, genius |词性:名词

mawk:/mɔːk/| v. |嘲弄，愚弄|To make fun of; to tease or fool.| The older boys mawk the younger ones mercilessly.  那些大男孩无情地嘲弄小男孩们。| 近义词：tease, mock, taunt, ridicule | 反义词: praise, compliment |词性:动词
