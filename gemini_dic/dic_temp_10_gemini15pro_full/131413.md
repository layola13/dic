nonclearances:/ˌnɒnˈklɪərənsɪz/
n.|未清算项，未批准事项|Items or matters that have not been cleared or approved.  Specifically refers to instances where necessary approvals or permissions have not been obtained.|The project was delayed due to several nonclearances from the regulatory body.  该项目由于监管机构的几项未批准事项而被推迟。|The team is working to resolve the remaining nonclearances before the launch date. 团队正在努力解决剩余的未清算项，以便在发布日期之前完成。|unapproved items, pending approvals, outstanding issues|clearances, approvals|noun
