monostrophe:/ˈmɒnəˌstrəʊfiː/ /ˈmɑːnəˌstrəʊfiː/| n.|单节诗; 独段诗|A poem or strophe consisting of only one stanza.  A poem not divided into stanzas.|The poet composed a moving monostrophe for the occasion.|Synonym: stanza, verse|Antonym: polystrophe|noun
