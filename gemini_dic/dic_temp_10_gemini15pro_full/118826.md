metantimonate:/ˌmɛtəˈæntɪməneɪt/
| n. |偏锑酸盐|A salt of metantimonic acid.  A compound containing the SbO₃⁻ anion.|Sodium metantimonate is used in some types of glass. 偏锑酸钠用于某些类型的玻璃。|
| synonym: antimonate | antonym: N/A | noun |

metantimonic:/ˌmɛtəæntiˈmɒnɪk/
| adj. |偏锑的|Relating to or containing antimony in its +5 oxidation state, specifically referring to metantimonic acid (HSbO₃) or its derivatives.|Metantimonic acid is a strong oxidizing agent. 偏锑酸是一种强氧化剂。|
| synonym: antimonic(V) | antonym: N/A | adjective |
