convocative:/kənˈvɒkətɪv/ /kənˈvɑːkətɪv/| adj.|召集的；召唤的；引起…的|Tending to convoke or summon, especially tending to summon or evoke something unpleasant or unwelcome.|Her provocative dress was convocative of unwanted attention. 她那件挑逗性的连衣裙招来了不必要的关注。|近义词：summoning, evocative, suggestive, redolent|反义词：dispelling, dissuasive,  repellent|词性:形容词

convocative:/kənˈvɒkətɪv/ /kənˈvɑːkətɪv/| n.|召集令|A call to assemble; a summons.|The king issued a convocative for all the lords of the realm. 国王向王国的所有领主发出了召集令。|近义词：summons, call, invitation|反义词：dismissal|词性:名词 (罕见)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *convocativus, from Latin convocatus (past participle of convocare “to call together”), from con- (“together”) + vocare (“to call”).
