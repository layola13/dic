adultly:/əˈdʌltli/
adverb
成年地；成熟地
in a manner befitting an adult; maturely.
She handled the situation very adultly. 她非常成熟地处理了这种情况。
He spoke to them adultly about the dangers of drugs. 他成熟地和他们谈论了毒品的危害。
近义词: maturely, responsibly, soberly
反义词: childishly, immaturely, irresponsibly
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From adult + -ly.  "Adult" comes from the Latin "adultus," the past participle of "adolescere" which means "to grow up."  The suffix "-ly" is commonly used to form adverbs from adjectives.
