hillstone:/ˈhɪlˌstoʊn/| n.|山石，页岩，板岩|A type of finely grained sedimentary rock that splits easily into thin layers.|The house was built with hillstone. 这座房子是用山石建造的。|Synonyms: shale, slate, flagstone|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a type of rock)|noun
