heteracanth: /ˌhɛtərəˈkænθ/ | adj. n. |异棘的，异棘鱼 | Having different types of spines or fin rays, particularly referring to fish. Also refers to a fish possessing such spines. | Example 1: The heteracanth fish displayed a variety of spine lengths along its dorsal fin.  这种异棘鱼的背鳍上显示出各种长度的刺。

Example 2:  Heteracanth morphology is a key characteristic used in classifying certain fish species. 异棘的形态是某些鱼类分类的关键特征。 | Near synonyms:  spiny, acanthoid | Antonyms: smooth, spineless | Word type: Adjective, Noun
