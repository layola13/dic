modularity:/ˌmɒdʒʊˈlærɪti/ | n. | 模块化，模块性 | The degree to which a system's components may be separated and recombined. |  The modularity of the furniture allows for various configurations.  这套家具的模块化设计允许多种配置。| synonyms: flexibility, adaptability, decomposability | antonyms: inflexibility, rigidity, monolithic | noun
