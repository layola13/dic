indictees:/ɪnˌdaɪkˈtiːz/ | n. | 被控告者；被告 | People who have been formally accused of a crime by a grand jury.  | The indictees pleaded not guilty to all charges. 被告对所有指控拒不认罪。|  The indictees were brought before the judge for arraignment. 被告被带到法官面前接受传讯。 | synonyms: accused, defendants | antonyms: accusers, plaintiffs | noun
