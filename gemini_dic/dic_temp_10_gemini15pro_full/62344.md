endenize:/ˈɛndənaɪz/
verb
使本土化; 使地方化; 使适应当地环境
To adapt to a local environment; to establish a plant or animal in a new habitat or environment where it is not native.
The government is trying to endenize the education system. 政府正在努力使教育系统本土化。
The introduced species has become fully endenized. 引进的物种已经完全本土化了。
acclimatize, naturalize, localize
alienate, denationalize
