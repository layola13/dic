disseminators:/dɪˈsɛmɪneɪtərz/ | n. |传播者；散布者 |  People or things that spread information, ideas, or something else widely. | The internet has become one of the most effective disseminators of information. 互联网已成为最有效的信息传播者之一。 |  promulgators, distributors, spreaders | suppressors, concealers | noun

disseminate:/dɪˈsɛmɪneɪt/ | v. |传播；散布 | To spread or disperse something, especially information, widely. | The organization disseminates information about the disease through pamphlets and public service announcements. 该组织通过小册子和公共服务公告传播有关这种疾病的信息。| promulgate, distribute, spread, circulate | suppress, conceal, withhold | verb
