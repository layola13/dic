discontent:/dɪskənˈtɛnt/ | n. | 不满，不满足 | a lack of satisfaction with one's circumstances |  The workers voiced their discontent over the new policy. 工人们表达了对新政策的不满。 | dissatisfaction, unhappiness, restlessness | contentment, satisfaction | noun

discontent:/dɪskənˈtɛnt/ | adj. | 不满的，不满足的 | not content or satisfied; dissatisfied | He seemed discontent with his job. 他似乎对自己的工作不满意。| dissatisfied, unhappy, restless | content, satisfied, happy | adjective

discontent:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛnt/ | vt. | 使不满 | to make discontented | The long hours discontented the workers. 长时间的工作让工人们感到不满。 | displease, dissatisfy | please, satisfy | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French descontentement, from des- (expressing negation) + contentement (contentment). Ultimately from Latin contentus (content).
