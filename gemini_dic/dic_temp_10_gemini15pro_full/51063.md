desoldering:/diːˈsɒldərɪŋ/ /diːˈsɑːdərɪŋ/
n.|去焊，拆焊|The process of removing solder and a component from a circuit board.|He's skilled in desoldering and reworking circuit boards. 他擅长电路板的去焊和返工。|synonyms: unsoldering|antonyms: soldering
词性:名词


desolder:/diːˈsɒldər/ /diːˈsɑːdər/
v.|去焊，拆焊|To remove solder and a component from a circuit board.|I need to desolder this capacitor. 我需要拆掉这个电容器。|synonyms: unsolder|antonyms: solder
