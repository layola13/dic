dromaeognathous: /ˌdroʊmiːˈɒɡnəθəs/
adjective
奔龙类的，驰龙类的
Having a palate structure resembling that of the Dromaeosauridae, a group of theropod dinosaurs.  This structure involves certain modifications of the bones in the roof of the mouth.
The *Velociraptor*, a dromaeognathous dinosaur, was a fearsome predator.  迅猛龙，一种奔龙类恐龙，是一种可怕的捕食者。
Near synonyms:  raptor-like, theropod
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, as it's a specific anatomical classification)
word type: adjective
