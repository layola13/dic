instills:/ɪnˈstɪlz/| v. |灌输，逐渐 instill 的第三人称单数|gradually establishes (an idea or attitude, especially a desirable one) in a person's mind.|例句：The coach instills confidence in his players. 教练向他的球员灌输信心。|例句：She instilled a love of reading in her children. 她逐渐培养了孩子们对阅读的热爱。|近义词：implant, infuse, inculcate, introduce, inspire|反义词：eradicate, remove, uproot|词性:动词

词源:
From Latin instillare "to put in drop by drop," from in- "in" + stillare "to drip," from stilla "drop."
