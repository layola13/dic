mammal:/ˈmæməl/ | n. | 哺乳动物 | A warm-blooded vertebrate animal of a class that is distinguished by the possession of hair or fur, the secretion of milk by females for the nourishment of the young, and (typically) the birth of live young. |  Bats are the only mammals capable of true flight.  蝙蝠是唯一能真正飞行的哺乳动物。

Blue whales are the largest mammals on Earth. 蓝鲸是地球上最大的哺乳动物。| 近义词: vertebrate, creature, animal | 反义词: invertebrate | 词性: noun
