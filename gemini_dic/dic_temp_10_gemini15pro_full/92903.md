idealogues:/ˈaɪdiəlɔːɡz/ | n. |意识形态拥护者；理论家|A person who strongly believes in a particular set of ideas, especially political ones and tries to persuade others to accept them.| The idealogues of the party were determined to implement their vision. 该党的理论家决心实施他们的愿景。|  synonyms: theorist, doctrinaire, zealot | antonyms: pragmatist, moderate | noun
