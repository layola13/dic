coatrooms:/ˈkoʊtruːmz/ /ˈkəʊtruːmz/
n.
衣帽间，存放外套的房间
A room, as in a theater or restaurant, for checking coats and other belongings.
The guests left their coats in the coatrooms before entering the main hall.  客人们在进入大厅之前把外套放在衣帽间。
The theater has several coatrooms for the convenience of patrons.  剧院设有几个衣帽间，方便顾客。
synonyms: cloakroom, checkroom
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "open area" or "hallway" in certain contexts, but these aren't true opposites)
noun
