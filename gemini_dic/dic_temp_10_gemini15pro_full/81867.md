grief-stricken:/ˈɡriːfˌstrɪkən/| adj.|极度悲伤的，悲痛欲绝的|Overwhelmed by grief; heartbroken.  Deeply affected by sorrow.|She was grief-stricken after the death of her husband. 她丈夫去世后她悲痛欲绝。|近义词：bereaved, heartbroken, devastated, inconsolable|反义词：joyful, happy|词性:形容词
词源: From grief + stricken (past participle of strike). Originally meant literally struck by grief, as if by a physical blow.
