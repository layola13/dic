hemlock:/ˈhɛmlɒk/ (US) /ˈhɛmlɑːk/ (UK)| n. |铁杉；毒芹；由毒芹提取的毒药|A highly poisonous European plant of the parsley family, with a ribbed stem, small white flowers, and a characteristic smell. Also, a poisonous drink made from this plant.  It is infamous for being the poison that Socrates was forced to drink.|Socrates died after drinking hemlock. 苏格拉底喝了毒芹后死了。|synonyms: poison, toxin, venom|antonyms: antidote, remedy|noun


hemlock:/ˈhɛmlɒk/ (US) /ˈhɛmlɑːk/ (UK)| v. |用毒芹毒死|To poison with hemlock.|The prisoner was hemlocked. 那名囚犯被毒芹毒死了。|synonyms: poison, kill, execute|antonyms: cure, heal|verb
