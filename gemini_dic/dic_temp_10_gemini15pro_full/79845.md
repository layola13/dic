glyptodon:/ˈɡlɪptədɒn/ (US) /ˈɡlɪptəˌdɑːn/| n. |雕齿兽|An extinct, heavily armored mammal, resembling a giant armadillo, that lived in the Americas during the Pleistocene epoch.|The museum displayed a complete glyptodon skeleton.  博物馆展出了一具完整的雕齿兽骨架。|Synonym: none (due to its unique nature, no direct synonyms exist, though one might say "giant armadillo" for a general comparison)|Antonym: none|noun


glyptodont:/ˈɡlɪptədɒnt/ (US) /ˈɡlɪptəˌdɑːnt/| adj. |雕齿兽的|Relating to or characteristic of the glyptodon.|The glyptodont shell was incredibly strong. 雕齿兽的壳非常坚固。|Synonym: none|Antonym: none|adjective
