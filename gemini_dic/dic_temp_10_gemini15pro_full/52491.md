dietitians:/ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃənz/
n.
营养师；营养学家
A dietitian is an expert in food and nutrition.
例句：The dietitians recommend eating a balanced diet. 营养师建议均衡饮食。

例句：Many hospitals employ registered dietitians. 很多医院雇佣注册营养师。

近义词：nutritionists, dieticians
反义词: 无
词性: 名词(plural)

词源: From "dietitian,"  from "diet" (from Old French "diete," from Latin "diaeta," from Greek "diaita" meaning "manner of living") + "-ician" (a suffix indicating a specialist).
