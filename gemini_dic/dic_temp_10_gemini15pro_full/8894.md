antigambling: /ˌæntiˈɡæmblɪŋ/ | adj. | 反赌博的，反对赌博的 | Opposed to gambling.  |  The antigambling movement gained momentum in the early 20th century.  反赌博运动在20世纪初获得了发展势头。 |  近义词: against gambling | 反义词: progambling | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix) + gambling (noun)

"anti-" comes from Greek, meaning "against" or "opposite of".
"gambling" comes from the Old English word "gamenian," meaning "to play games."
