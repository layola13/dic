archetypically:/ˌɑːrkɪˈtɪpɪkli/ | adv. |典型地；作为原型地|in a way that is typical of an original model or an ideal example |例句：She is archetypically English. 她典型地具有英国人的特征。|例句：The hero is archetypically brave and selfless.  这位英雄典型地展现了勇敢和无私的精神。|近义词：typically, classically, representatively |反义词: atypically, unconventionally |词性: 副词

词源:
From archetype + -ical + -ly.  "Archetype" comes from the Greek "arkhetupos" meaning "original pattern."
