cubists:/ˈkjuːbɪsts/
n.
立体主义艺术家，立体派画家
Artists who paint in the style of Cubism, which emphasizes abstract geometric shapes.
Picasso and Braque were the leading Cubists of the early 20th century.  毕加索和布拉克是20世纪早期主要的立体主义艺术家。
近义词:  modern artists, avant-garde artists 现代艺术家，先锋派艺术家
反义词:  traditional artists, realist artists 传统艺术家，现实主义艺术家
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cubiste*, from *cube* ("cube") + *-iste* (suffix forming agent nouns).  Ultimately from Latin *cubus*. 源自法语 *cubiste*，由 *cube*（“立方体”）+ *-iste*（构成施动者名词的后缀）组成。最终源自拉丁语 *cubus*。
