estaminet: /ɛˈstæmɪneɪ/ /eɪˈstæmɪneɪ/
| n. |小酒馆，小咖啡馆|A small cafe or bar, especially one in France or Belgium, typically serving alcohol, simple food, and sometimes offering lodging. | We stopped at a charming estaminet for lunch during our cycling tour of Belgium. 我们在比利时自行车旅行期间，在一间迷人的小酒馆停下吃午饭。|Synonyms: cafe, bar, bistro, pub, tavern | Antonyms:  None (lacks a direct opposite) | noun
