gunneries:/ˈɡʌn(ə)riz/
noun
炮术；炮兵；炮兵部队
The science or art of managing guns; the emplacement, working, or firing of guns. Also: the guns themselves, or a body of artillery.
The ship's gunneries were impressive. 这艘船的炮术令人印象深刻。
Synonyms: artillery, ordnance, cannons
Antonyms: disarmament, peace
词性: 名词
