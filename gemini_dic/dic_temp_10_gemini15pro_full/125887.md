muscovado:/ˌməskəˈvɑːdoʊ/ | n. | 赤砂糖，黑糖 | Unrefined cane sugar with a strong molasses flavor and dark brown color. | 例句：The muscovado sugar added a rich, caramel-like flavor to the cake.  赤砂糖给蛋糕增添了浓郁的焦糖味。

例句：She prefers muscovado to regular white sugar for its complex taste. 她因为黑糖复杂的风味而更喜欢它胜过普通的精制白糖。

| 近义词：brown sugar, raw sugar, jaggery | 反义词: refined sugar, white sugar | 词性: noun
