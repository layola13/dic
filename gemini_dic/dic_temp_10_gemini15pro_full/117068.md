me-tooer:/ˌmiːˈtuːər/ |n.|我也是（指控性骚扰或性侵犯的人）|Someone who comes forward with an allegation of sexual harassment or assault, especially in the wake of similar accusations against a prominent figure.  These allegations are often part of a wider social movement against such behavior. |例句：Many me-tooers came forward after the accusations against the Hollywood producer. 许多在好莱坞制片人被指控后，也站出来指控自己遭受性骚扰或性侵犯。

例句：Some people are skeptical of me-tooers, while others see them as brave survivors. 一些人对“我也是”运动的参与者表示怀疑，而另一些人则将他们视为勇敢的幸存者。|近义词：accuser, victim, survivor|反义词: perpetrator, abuser |词性:noun
