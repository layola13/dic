chromism:/ˈkroʊmɪzəm/| n. |变色现象，颜色变化|The property of exhibiting chromism, i.e., a reversible change in color of a substance.|The chameleon exhibits chromism to blend in with its surroundings. 变色龙表现出变色现象以融入周围环境。|Synonyms: color change, discoloration|Antonyms: colorfastness|noun
