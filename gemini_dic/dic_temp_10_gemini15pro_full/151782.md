pass-for:/ˈpæs fɔːr/ | v. | 被认为是；冒充；充当 | To be accepted or regarded as something, often when not genuinely so. |  He tried to pass for a doctor, but his lack of medical knowledge soon gave him away. 他试图冒充医生，但他缺乏医学知识很快就暴露了。

He could pass for twenty years old, even though he's closer to thirty.  虽然他快三十岁了，但他看起来像二十岁。 | 近义词：pose as, masquerade as, be mistaken for | 反义词：be exposed as, be revealed as | 词性: 动词
