certified:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 被证明的；合格的；持有证书的 | Officially recognized as possessing certain qualifications or meeting certain standards. |  She is a certified teacher. 她是一位持有证书的教师。 | The diamond is certified as genuine. 这颗钻石被证明是真品。 | 近义词：accredited, approved, licensed, qualified | 反义词：uncertified, unqualified | 词性: adjective

词源:  From the past participle of *certify*, from French *certifier*, from Late Latin *certificare* "to make certain," from Latin *certus* "settled, sure" + *-ficare*,  a combining form meaning "to make, to do."
