brachycephalous: /ˌbrækiˈsɛfələs/ | adj. | 短头型的 | Having a short, broad head, typically with a cephalic index of 81 or higher.  This term is often used in anthropology and zoology. |  The bulldog is a brachycephalous breed.  斗牛犬是一种短头型犬种。 | short-headed | long-headed, dolichocephalous | adjective


Etymology->
From Greek *βραχύς (brakhús, “short”) + *κεφαλή (kephalḗ, “head”).
