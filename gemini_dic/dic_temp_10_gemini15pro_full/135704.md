nonpresumptively:/ˌnɒnprɪˈzʌmptɪvli/
adv.
非推定地，不臆断地
without making presumptions; in a way that does not assume something to be true before it has been proven.
The committee nonpresumptively reviewed the evidence before reaching a decision. 委员会在做出决定之前不带任何臆断地审查了证据。
The doctor nonpresumptively diagnosed the patient based solely on the presented symptoms. 医生仅根据出现的症状，不带任何臆断地诊断了病人。
Near Synonyms: cautiously, carefully, judiciously, objectively, impartially
Antonyms: presumptively, rashly, hastily, prejudicially, biasedly
