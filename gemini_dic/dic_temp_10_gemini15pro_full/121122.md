misdeclares:/ˌmɪsdɪˈkleərz/
verb
错误地申报；虚报
To declare something incorrectly, especially on official documents.
He misdeclared his income to the tax authorities. 他向税务机关虚报了收入。
misrepresent, falsify
declare correctly, report accurately
词性: 动词
