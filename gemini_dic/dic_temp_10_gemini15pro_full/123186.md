monocarbonate:/ˌmɒnəʊˈkɑːbənət/ |n.| 一碳酸盐 |A carbonate containing only one carbonate group per molecule.  A salt of carbonic acid in which only one of the two replaceable hydrogen atoms has been replaced by a metal atom. |Sodium bicarbonate is a monocarbonate. 小苏打是一种一碳酸盐。| bicarbonate | - | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
mono- (one) + carbonate (from carbonic acid)
