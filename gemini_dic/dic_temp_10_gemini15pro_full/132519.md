nondeterministic:/ˌnɒndɪˌtɜːmɪˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非确定性的 | Not determined or predictable; random.  Relating to a type of computation in which the next state is not uniquely determined by the current state and the input. |  A nondeterministic algorithm may produce different results on different runs with the same input.  非确定性算法在使用相同输入的不同运行中可能会产生不同的结果。 | Nondeterministic finite automata are used in theoretical computer science. 非确定性有限自动机用于理论计算机科学。 | probabilistic, stochastic | deterministic | adjective
