impoverishments:/ɪmˈpɒvərɪʃmənts/| n. |贫困化，贫穷化；削弱；损耗|The process of becoming poor or the state of being poor; a depletion or reduction in quality or strength.|The war led to widespread impoverishments of the population. 战争导致了人口普遍贫困化。|Synonyms: deprivations, depletions, reductions, weakenings|Antonyms: enrichments, improvements, enhancements|noun
