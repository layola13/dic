overmagnify: /ˌoʊvərˈmæɡnɪfaɪ/
verb
过度放大；过分夸大
to magnify something excessively; to exaggerate
The media tends to overmagnify the dangers of air travel. 媒体往往过分夸大航空旅行的危险。
His failures were overmagnified by his critics.  他的失败被批评者们过分夸大了。

近义词: exaggerate, overstate, amplify, inflate
反义词: understate, minimize, downplay
词性: verb
