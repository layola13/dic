encompassing:/ɪnˈkʌmpəsɪŋ/| adj.|包含的，包罗万象的|including everything or having a very wide range|例句：The book encompasses a wide range of topics. 这本书涵盖了广泛的主题。|例句：Her research encompasses the social and economic aspects of the issue. 她的研究涵盖了这个问题的社会和经济方面。|近义词：comprehensive, inclusive, all-embracing|反义词：limited, narrow, restricted|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin encompassare "to encircle," from Latin in- "in" + compassare "to go around."
