infuriatingly:/ɪnˈfjʊəriˌeɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人愤怒地，极其恼人地 |  in a manner that causes intense anger or frustration.  It describes something that is done in a way that is very annoying or frustrating. |  The website was infuriatingly slow.  这个网站慢得让人抓狂。 |  The traffic was moving infuriatingly slowly.  交通慢得令人发狂。|  annoyingly, exasperatingly, maddeningly | calmingly, soothingly | adverb

infuriate:/ɪnˈfjʊərieɪt/ | vt. | 激怒，使大怒 | To make someone extremely angry and impatient. | His condescending attitude infuriated me. 他那居高临下的态度激怒了我。 |  The constant noise from the construction site infuriated the residents. 建筑工地持续不断的噪音激怒了居民。 | enrage, anger, incense | calm, soothe, placate | verb
