ghee:/ɡiː/| n.| 酥油，印度黄油|Clarified butter made from buffalo or cow's milk, with a nutty flavor. It is widely used in South Asian and Middle Eastern cuisine.|I used ghee to fry the onions for the curry. 我用酥油炸洋葱做咖喱。|Synonyms: clarified butter, anhydrous milk fat|Antonyms: regular butter, oil|noun
