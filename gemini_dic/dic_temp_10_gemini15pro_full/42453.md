countereducational:/ˌkaʊntərˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃənl/ | adj. | 反教育的，阻碍教育的 | Hindering or opposing education; going against the principles or goals of education. |  Some argue that excessive screen time can be countereducational, hindering the development of critical thinking skills.  有些人认为过多的屏幕时间可能适得其反，阻碍批判性思维能力的发展。

| Example:  Censorship of certain books can be countereducational, limiting students' access to diverse perspectives. 审查某些书籍可能适得其反，限制学生接触不同观点。 | Synonyms:  anti-educational,  uneducative,  miseducative | Antonyms: educational,  instructive,  enlightening | word type: adjective
