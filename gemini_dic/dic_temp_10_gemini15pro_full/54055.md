disdaining:/dɪsˈdeɪnɪŋ/
Verb
轻蔑；鄙视；不屑
present participle of disdain, meaning to regard or treat with contempt; to consider beneath oneself.
He was disdaining the offer of help, believing he could manage on his own. 他不屑于接受帮助，认为自己可以应付。
She was disdaining to answer his rude questions. 她不屑于回答他粗鲁的问题。
Synonyms: scorning, spurning, snubbing, slighting
Antonyms: admiring, respecting, appreciating
