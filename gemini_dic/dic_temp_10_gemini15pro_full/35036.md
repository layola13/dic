close-mouthed:/ˌkloʊsˈmaʊðd/ /ˌkləʊsˈmaʊθt/| adj.| 沉默寡言的，嘴巴紧的| unwilling to speak; taciturn.  Someone who is close-mouthed does not readily reveal information or express their feelings. | He's a close-mouthed man; he doesn't talk about his private life. 他是个沉默寡言的人，不谈论他的私生活。| 近义词: reticent, taciturn, tight-lipped| 反义词: talkative, loquacious, garrulous|词性: 形容词

close-mouthed:/ˌkloʊsˈmaʊðd/ /ˌkləʊsˈmaʊθt/| adj.| 守口如瓶的|Keeping secrets well; discreet. Someone who is close-mouthed can be trusted not to reveal confidential information.|  She was close-mouthed about the surprise party. 她对惊喜派对守口如瓶。| 近义词: discreet, secretive| 反义词: indiscreet, blabbermouth|词性: 形容词


词源:  Compound of 'close' (meaning shut) + 'mouthed'.  The meaning developed from the literal sense of keeping one's mouth closed to the figurative sense of not revealing information.
