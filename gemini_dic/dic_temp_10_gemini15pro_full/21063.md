blinding:/ˈblaɪndɪŋ/| adj.| 耀眼的，使人炫目的；使人眼花缭乱的；使人困惑的 |  (of light) so bright as to make it difficult or impossible to see; extremely intense; overpowering; (informal) used for emphasis, especially of something unpleasant | The sun was blinding. 太阳很刺眼。| synonyms: dazzling, glaring, overwhelming, intense | antonyms: dim, faint, subtle | adjective

blinding:/ˈblaɪndɪŋ/| n.|  失明 | the process of becoming blind |  The blinding snow made it impossible to drive.   blinding的大雪使得开车无法进行。| synonyms: loss of sight | antonyms: sight, vision | noun
