outstaying:/ˌaʊtˈsteɪɪŋ/
|v.|停留过久；超过…期限|Staying beyond the permitted or expected time. |例句：He outstayed his welcome by a couple of hours. 他多待了几个小时，让人觉得他待得太久了。|近义词：overstaying, lingering, remaining|反义词:leaving, departing|verb
