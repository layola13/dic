fettering:/ˈfɛtərɪŋ/
n.|束缚，羁绊|The act of restraining or hindering someone or something.|The fettering of the press is a threat to democracy. 对新闻界的束缚是对民主的威胁。|
近义词:restricting, chaining, binding, confining, shackling
反义词:liberating, freeing, releasing
词性:Noun (Gerund or present participle of 'fetter')

fetter:/ˈfɛtər/
n.|枷锁，束缚|A chain or shackle placed on the feet.|His imagination was fettered by the strict rules.  他的想象力被严格的规则所束缚。|
近义词:chain, shackle, restraint, constraint
反义词:freedom, liberty
词性:Noun

fetter:/ˈfɛtər/
vt.|束缚，限制|To restrain with chains or shackles; to restrict or hinder.|Bureaucracy fettered the project. 官僚主义束缚了这个项目。|
近义词:restrict, restrain, hinder, confine, shackle
反义词:liberate, free, release
词性:Verb


词源: From Old English *feter (plural *fetru, *fetere), from Proto-Germanic *fetra- (compare Old Norse fetill, Old Frisian feter, Dutch veter, German Fessel "fetter"), from PIE root *ped- "foot."
