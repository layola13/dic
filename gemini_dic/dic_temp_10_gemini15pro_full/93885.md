immorally:/ɪˈmɔːrəli/
adverb
不道德地
in a way that is not moral; wickedly.
He acted immorally by accepting bribes. 他接受贿赂的行为是不道德的。
近义词: wrongly, wickedly, sinfully, unethically
反义词: morally, ethically, righteously
词性: adverb

词源:
From im- (not) + moral + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *moralis "concerning character or custom," from mos (genitive moris) "custom, manner, habit, way of life."
