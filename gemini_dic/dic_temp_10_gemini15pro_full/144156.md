outshining: /ˌaʊtˈʃaɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 超越，胜过 | Surpassing in splendor, achievement, or excellence; eclipsing. |  Her talent for singing outshined everyone else in the choir. 她的歌唱天赋胜过合唱团里的所有人。

The new company is outshining its competitors in terms of innovation. 这家新公司在创新方面胜过其竞争对手。


Synonyms: excelling, surpassing, eclipsing, outdoing, outperforming, overshadowing

Antonyms: underperforming, lagging, failing, paling, being overshadowed

Word type: Verb (present participle)

Etymology:  From "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "shine" (to emit light; to be brilliant or distinguished).
