mullers:/ˈmʌlərz/| n.|研磨机，研磨器;  (尤指) 穆勒氏管|A muller is a heavy, flat tool, usually made of stone or glass, used for grinding pigments or other substances to a fine powder, especially in a muller and slab.  Specifically, it can refer to Müllerian ducts.|例句：The artist used a muller to grind the pigment for her painting.  艺术家用研磨机研磨她画作的颜料。

例句：In embryology, the Müllerian ducts develop into the female reproductive system. 在胚胎学中，穆勒氏管发育成女性生殖系统。|近义词：pestle, grinder|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
