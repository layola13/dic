asperifolious:/ˌæspərɪˈfəʊliəs/
adj.

Having rough leaves, often covered with short, stiff hairs.
The plant is asperifolious, with leaves that are unpleasant to touch.  这种植物糙叶的，叶子摸起来不舒服。
