dromatherium:/ˌdrəʊməˈθɪəriəm/
n.|跑兽；奔兽；一种已灭绝的早期哺乳动物|An extinct genus of early mammal, considered to be one of the earliest known members of the mammal lineage.|Dromatherium is known from fossils found in the Late Triassic of North America.|近义词:Adelobasileus, Sinoconodon|反义词:N/A|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek δρομάς (dromás, “runner”) + θηρίον (thēríon, “wild animal”).
