oligocene:/ˈɑːlɪɡoʊˌsiːn/ | n. | 渐新世 |A geologic epoch of the Paleogene Period, following the Eocene Epoch and preceding the Miocene Epoch, dating from about 33.9 to 23 million years ago.|The Oligocene saw the rise of many modern mammal groups. |近义词: Tertiary Period (更广泛的时期) |反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑前后相邻的地质时期 Eocene 或 Miocene) |词性:名词
