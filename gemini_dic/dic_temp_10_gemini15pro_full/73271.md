forearmed:/fɔːrˈɑːrmd/
| adj. | 预先准备的，预先武装的，有准备的 | Prepared in advance, especially for danger or difficulty; having foreknowledge that allows one to be prepared. | Example: Forearmed with the knowledge of the upcoming storm, they boarded up their windows.  预先知道即将到来的暴风雨，他们用木板封住了窗户。

Example:  Going into the negotiation forearmed with the facts gave her an advantage.  带着事实进入谈判给了她一个优势。
| 近义词: prepared, ready, braced, equipped, informed | 反义词: unprepared, unaware, vulnerable, defenseless | 词性: 形容词
