cark:/kɑːrk/ | n. | 忧虑；焦虑；烦恼 | A burden; a worry or anxiety. |  The cark of finding enough money to pay the bills weighed heavily on her.  她为了支付账单而四处筹钱的焦虑压得她喘不过气。 | 近义词: worry, anxiety, burden, care, concern | 反义词: peace, tranquility, calmness | 词性: 名词

cark:/kɑːrk/ | v. | (使)烦恼；(使)忧虑 | To cause worry or anxiety. | The constant deadlines were carking him.  不断的截止日期让他焦虑不安。 | 近义词: worry, trouble, distress, burden | 反义词: soothe, comfort, reassure | 词性: 动词


词源:
From Middle English *cark*, from Old North French *carquer*, probably of imitative origin.
