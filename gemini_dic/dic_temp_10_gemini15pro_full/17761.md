bedheads:/ˈbedˌhɛdz/| n.|乱蓬蓬的头发；睡醒后头发凌乱的样子|A person's messy hair after waking up.|例句：She woke up with a serious case of bedhead. 她醒来时头发乱糟糟的。

例句：I can't go out looking like this – I have terrible bedheads. 我不能这个样子出去——我的头发太乱了。|近义词：messy hair, tangled hair, tousled hair|反义词:combed hair, styled hair|词性:名词

词源:Compound word from "bed" and "head," literally referring to the state of one's hair after sleeping in bed.
