oopack:/ˈuːpæk/ | n. | 包装，包裹 |  A bundle, package, or parcel.  Likely a  blend of "ooh" (expressing surprise or delight) and "pack".  Its usage suggests a special or carefully prepared package. |  She received an oopack filled with goodies from her grandmother. 她收到祖母寄来的一个装满好东西的包裹。 | synonyms: package, parcel, bundle | antonyms: unpack, unwrap | noun
