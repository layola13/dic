angustura:/æŋɡəˈstʊərə/ | n. | 安古斯图拉树皮, 安古斯图拉苦酒 |The bark of a South American tree ( *Galipea officinalis* or *Cusparia febrifuga* ) of the rue family (Rutaceae), formerly used as a febrifuge and tonic, and also as the source of a bitter aromatic extract used as a flavoring agent and as an ingredient in bitters. Also called *cusparia bark*.  A type of aromatic bitters made from the angustura bark and other ingredients, commonly used in cocktails. | I added a dash of angustura bitters to my Old Fashioned. 我在我的老式鸡尾酒中加了一点安古斯图拉苦酒。| 近义词: bitters | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "sweetener") | noun


angustura:/æŋɡəˈstʊərə/ | adj. | 安古斯图拉的，产于安古斯图拉的 | Relating to or derived from the angustura tree or its bark. | The angustura bitters added a complex flavor to the drink.  安古斯图拉苦酒为饮料增添了复杂的风味。| 近义词: cusparia | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | adjective
