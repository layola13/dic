nonmelodiousness:/ˌnɒnˌmɛˈləʊdiəsnəs/ /ˌnɑːnˌmɛˈloʊdiəsnəs/
n.
不悦耳, 不和谐
lack of melodiousness; the quality of not being melodious.
The nonmelodiousness of the beginner's violin practice made it hard to concentrate. 初学者的小提琴练习不悦耳，让人难以集中注意力。
近义词:discordance, cacophony, dissonance
反义词:melodiousness, euphony, harmony
词性:名词
