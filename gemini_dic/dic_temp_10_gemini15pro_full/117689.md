mela:/ˈmeɪlə/ | n. | 集市，庙会，节日 | A large gathering or fair, often with religious connotations, held in India and other parts of South Asia. |  Example: The annual mela attracts thousands of pilgrims.  年度庙会吸引了成千上万的朝圣者。| Example: We went to the mela and rode on the Ferris wheel. 我们去了庙会，坐了摩天轮。 | Synonyms: fair, festival, carnival | Antonyms: None | Noun
