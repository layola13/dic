outwit:/ˌaʊtˈwɪt/| vt.| 智胜，瞒骗 | To defeat or trick (someone) by being more intelligent or clever. | He outwitted his opponents by anticipating their next move. 他预料到了对手的下一步行动，从而智胜了他们。

She outwitted the police by dyeing her hair and fleeing the country. 她把头发染了色，逃离了这个国家，从而瞒过了警察。

|近义词：outsmart, outfox, trick, deceive|反义词：be outwitted, be fooled|词性：动词|

词源：

From out- + wit.  "Out-" prefix meaning "to surpass or exceed."  "Wit" meaning intelligence, cleverness, or resourcefulness.  Thus, "outwit" literally means to surpass someone in wit or cleverness.
