一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I eat breakfast every day. = 我每天吃早餐。
She works at a bank. = 她在银行工作。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am eating breakfast now. = 我现在正在吃早餐。
She is working from home today. = 她今天在家工作。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have eaten breakfast already. = 我已经吃过早餐了。
She has worked at this company for five years. = 她在这家公司工作了五年。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been eating breakfast for an hour. = 我已经吃了一个小时的早餐了。
She has been working on this project all week. = 她整个星期都在做这个项目。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I ate breakfast yesterday. = 我昨天吃了早餐。
She worked late last night. = 她昨晚工作到很晚。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was eating breakfast when the phone rang. = 我正在吃早餐时电话响了。
She was working on the report when the power went out. = 她在写报告时停电了。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had eaten breakfast before I went to work. = 我上班前已经吃过早餐了。
She had worked at the company for two years before she got promoted. = 她升职前在这家公司工作了两年。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been eating breakfast for an hour when the doorbell rang. = 我已经吃了一个小时的早餐了，这时门铃响了。
She had been working on the project for months before it was finally finished. =  她在项目上工作了几个月，最终才完成。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will eat breakfast tomorrow. = 我明天会吃早餐。
She will work from home next week. = 她下周会在家工作。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be eating breakfast at 8 am tomorrow. = 我明天早上8点会正在吃早餐。
She will be working on the presentation all day tomorrow. = 她明天一整天都会在做演示。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have eaten breakfast by the time you arrive. = 你到达之前我就已经吃过早餐了。
She will have worked here for ten years by next June. = 到明年六月，她在这里工作就满十年了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
I will have been eating breakfast for an hour by the time you arrive. = 到你到达时，我将已经吃了一个小时的早餐。
She will have been working on this project for six months by the end of the year. = 到年底，她将在这个项目上工作了六个月。
