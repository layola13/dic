danava:/ˈdɑːnəvə/| n.|达纳瓦，恶魔，阿修罗|A class of demonic beings in Hinduism, similar to the Asuras, often described as powerful and malevolent.|Example 1: In Hindu mythology, the danavas constantly battled the devas. 在印度神话中，达纳瓦不断与提婆作战。

Example 2: The danava was depicted as a fearsome creature with immense strength.  这个达纳瓦被描绘成一个拥有巨大力量的可怕生物。|Synonym: asura, demon, rakshasa|Antonym: deva, god|Noun
