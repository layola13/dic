disinterring:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtɜːrɪŋ/
v.
挖掘，发掘（尤指尸体）; 重新发现，重新提出（旧事）
The bodies were disinterred for forensic examination.  这些尸体被挖出来进行法医检验。
The historian disinterred a forgotten manuscript from the archives.  这位历史学家从档案馆里挖掘出一份被遗忘的手稿。
exhume, unearth, dig up
bury, inter
verb
