hydropult:/ˈhaɪdrəˌpʌlt/ | n. |弹射器；水力弹射器|A catapult that uses the force of high-pressure steam or water to launch aircraft from the deck of a ship. | 例句：The hydropult launched the jet fighter smoothly from the carrier.  水力弹射器将喷气式战斗机平稳地从航母上弹射出去。

例句：The navy is developing a new, more powerful hydropult system. 海军正在开发一种新的、更强大的弹射器系统。|近义词：catapult, launcher |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
