cob:/kɒb/ (UK) /kɑːb/ (US)| n.|玉米棒子；雄天鹅；矮壮的马；圆块|A cob is the central cylindrical part of an ear of corn (maize) on which the kernels grow. It can also refer to a male swan, a short-legged, strong horse, or a rounded lump of something like coal.|The farmer shucked the corn and discarded the cob. 农民剥了玉米，扔掉了玉米棒子。|近义词：core (for corn), swan (for male swan), pony (for horse)|反义词: (No direct antonym) |noun

cob:/kɒb/ (UK) /kɑːb/ (US)| vt.|用泥块涂抹|To cob is to build something using lumps of clay, mud, and straw.|They cobbed the walls of their traditional house. 他们用泥块涂抹了他们传统房屋的墙壁。|近义词：daub, plaster|反义词: (No direct antonym) |verb
