beverages:/ˈbevərɪdʒɪz/
noun
饮料
Drinks, usually other than water.
例句：We offer a wide variety of beverages, including coffee, tea, and juice. 我们提供各种各样的饮料，包括咖啡、茶和果汁。

例句：Please feel free to help yourself to the beverages in the refrigerator.  请随意取用冰箱里的饮料。

近义词：drinks, refreshments, potions
反义词:foods, solids
词性:n
