accomplishable:/əˈkʌmplɪʃəbl/
adjective
可完成的；可达成的
That goal is certainly accomplishable with a little hard work. =  那个目标通过一些努力肯定是可以实现的。
This task is easily accomplishable within a week. = 这项任务可以轻松在一周内完成。
近义词: achievable, attainable, feasible, realizable
反义词: impossible, unattainable, unachievable
词性: adjective
词源: From accomplish + -able. Accomplish comes from Old French acomplir, from Latin ad- "to" + complere "to fill up, complete."
