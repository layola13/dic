gilo:/ˈɡiːloʊ/
n.
灯笼椒，青椒 (a type of large, sweet pepper)
Gilos are commonly used in Caribbean cuisine. 灯笼椒常用于加勒比海菜肴。
例句：I added some gilos to the stew for extra flavor. 我在炖菜里加了些灯笼椒提味。

例句：Gilos are a staple ingredient in many Jamaican dishes. 灯笼椒是许多牙买加菜肴的主要成分。

近义词：pepper, bell pepper
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could be contrasted with a hot pepper like scotch bonnet)
词性: noun
