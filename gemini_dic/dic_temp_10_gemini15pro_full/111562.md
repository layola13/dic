lobeless:/ˈloʊbləs/| adj.|无裂片的，无耳垂的|Lacking lobes; without lobes.  This refers to a structure, typically a leaf or other plant part, that does not have lobes (rounded projections). It can also refer to the lack of earlobes.|The lobeless leaves of the plant made it easy to identify.  这种植物无裂片的叶子使它很容易识别。|Near synonyms: entire, undivided, unlobed|Antonyms: lobed, divided|adjective
