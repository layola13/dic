outseeks:/ˌaʊtˈsiːks/
verb
超越，胜过
To surpass or exceed in seeking or searching for something.
Tesla outseeks its competitors in developing innovative battery technology.  特斯拉在开发创新电池技术方面胜过其竞争对手。
She outseeks everyone in her dedication to finding a cure for the disease. 她对寻找治愈这种疾病的方法的奉献精神超过了所有人。
近义词:surpass, outstrip, exceed, outdo
反义词:lag behind, fall short of
词性:v.
