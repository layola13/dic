boorishnesses:/ˈbʊərɪʃnɪsɪz/
|n.|粗鲁，粗野，缺乏教养|The boorishnesses of the guests embarrassed the host.|客人粗鲁的行为让主人很尴尬。|
|近义词：rudeness, incivility, crudeness, vulgarity, coarseness|
|反义词：politeness, courtesy, refinement, civility, sophistication|
|词性：名词|
|词源：From boorish + -ness. Boorish comes from Middle English *burisch*, from Old French *boire*, "rustic, peasant." Ultimately from Late Latin *būrus*, "farmer, peasant."|
