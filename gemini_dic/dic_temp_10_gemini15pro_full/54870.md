displeasingly:/dɪsˈpliːzɪŋli/ /dɪsˈpliːzɪŋli/
adverb|令人不快地，讨厌地|in a way that is not pleasing; unpleasantly|Her behavior was displeasingly childish. 她的行为幼稚得令人讨厌。|The food was displeasingly bland.  这食物淡而无味，令人不悦。|近义词:unpleasantly, objectionably, offensively, disagreeably|反义词:pleasingly, agreeably, delightfully|词性:副词

displeasing:/dɪsˈpliːzɪŋ/ /dɪsˈpliːzɪŋ/
adjective|令人不快的，讨厌的|not pleasing; offensive|His comments were very displeasing to her. 他的评论让她很不高兴。|The smell was displeasing.  这气味令人不快。|近义词:unpleasant, objectionable, offensive, disagreeable|反义词:pleasing, agreeable, delightful|词性:形容词

displease:/dɪsˈpliːz/ /dɪsˈpliːz/
verb|使不高兴，使生气|to cause someone to be annoyed or upset|His actions displeased his parents. 他的行为让他的父母不高兴。|It displeases me when people are late.  人们迟到让我很不高兴。|近义词:annoy, irritate, offend, upset|反义词:please, delight|词性:动词
