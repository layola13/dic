brandling:/ˈbrændlɪŋ/| n.|红纹蚯蚓; 鱼饵|A small reddish-brown earthworm (Eisenia foetida), often used as fishing bait. |例句：The angler dug up some brandlings for bait. 钓鱼者挖了一些红纹蚯蚓作为鱼饵。

例句：Brandlings are a popular choice for fishing bait because of their strong smell. 红纹蚯蚓因为它们强烈的气味而成为一种受欢迎的鱼饵选择。|近义词：redworm, tiger worm|反义词: None|词性:noun
