impest:/ɪmˈpɛst/| vt.|使成灾，侵扰，骚扰|To infest or overrun destructively; plague.|Locusts impested the fields, devouring all the crops.  蝗虫侵扰了田野，吞噬了所有庄稼。|近义词：infest, plague, overrun, beset|反义词：clear, cleanse, purify|词性:动词

词源:  From Latin *impestus, from impetere "to attack," from in- "into, in, on, upon" + petere "to go to, seek, aim at, attack."
