exerts:/ɪɡˈzɜːrts/ /ɛɡˈzɝts/| v.|运用，施加，发挥|Applies or brings to bear (a force, influence, or quality).|The government exerts considerable influence over the media. 政府对媒体施加相当大的影响力。|He exerted all his strength to move the rock. 他使出全身力气搬动那块石头。|近义词：apply, wield, employ, utilize, use|反义词：refrain, withhold|词性:动词

词源:
From Old French *eserter, from Latin exertus, past participle of exserere “to put forth, thrust out,” from ex- “out” + serere “to join, link, bind.”
