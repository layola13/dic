nonguidance:/nɒnˈɡaɪdəns/ | n. | 非指导，无指导 | Absence of guidance or direction.  It implies a situation where individuals are left to their own devices, without external instruction or control. |  The students appreciated the nonguidance approach to learning, as it allowed them to explore their own interests.  学生们很欣赏这种非指导性的学习方法，因为它允许他们探索自己的兴趣。| synonyms: autonomy, independence, self-direction | antonyms: guidance, direction, instruction | noun
