confute: /kənˈfjuːt/ | vt. |驳倒，反驳|To prove a statement or theory to be wrong or false; disprove.| He confuted the argument with compelling evidence. 他用令人信服的证据驳倒了这个论点。 | synonyms: refute, rebut, disprove | antonyms: confirm, corroborate, substantiate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin confutare "to check, restrain, suppress," from Latin com-, intensive prefix + -futare (of uncertain origin).
