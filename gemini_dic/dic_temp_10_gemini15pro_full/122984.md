monamore:/ˌmɒnəˈmɔːr/ /ˌmɑːnəˈmɔːr/
noun
我的爱人；甜心；宝贝
A term of endearment, equivalent to "my love," "sweetheart," or "darling."
My monamore, you are the sunshine of my life. 我的爱人，你是我生命中的阳光。
Synonyms: sweetheart, darling, beloved, love, honey
Antonyms: enemy, foe
