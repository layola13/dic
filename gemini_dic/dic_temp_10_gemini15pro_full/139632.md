occludent:/əˈkluːdənt/ | n. adj.| 闭塞音，闭塞的 |A consonant sound made by completely blocking the flow of air somewhere in the vocal tract, such as /p/, /b/, /t/, /d/, /k/, and /g/.  (A sound or letter representing it.) Also used as an adjective to describe such sounds. | Example (n.):  The sounds /p/, /b/, /t/, /d/, /k/, and /g/ are all occludents.  闭塞音 /p/、/b/、/t/、/d/、/k/ 和 /g/ 都是闭塞音。

Example (adj.):  An occludent consonant is formed by blocking airflow. 闭塞辅音是通过阻塞气流形成的。 | 近义词：(n.) stop, plosive | 反义词: (n.) fricative, approximant | 词性: noun, adjective

occlude:/əˈkluːd/ | vt.| 闭塞，遮挡 | To stop, close up, or obstruct (an opening, orifice, or passage).| Example: The thrombus occluded the artery. 血栓阻塞了动脉。 The dentist applied sealant to occlude the fissures in the molars. 牙医用密封剂封闭了臼齿的裂缝。 | 近义词：block, obstruct, close, cover | 反义词: open, uncover | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *occludere*, from *ob- “against” + claudere “to shut, close”.
