liplet:/ˈlɪplət/|n.|小唇；唇瓣|A small lip, especially a fleshy lobe or projection resembling a lip.  It can refer to a part of a flower or a small, lip-like structure in an animal.|The orchid's liplet was a vibrant purple. 这朵兰花的唇瓣是鲜艳的紫色。|synonyms: labellum, lip|antonyms: N/A|noun

liplet:/ˈlɪplət/|n.|小嘴唇（尤指婴儿或儿童）|A small lip, especially of a baby or child.|The baby's liplet trembled as he started to cry.  婴儿的小嘴唇在他开始哭的时候颤抖着。|synonyms:  lip|antonyms: N/A|noun
