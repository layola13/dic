infantilises:/ɪnˈfæntɪlaɪzɪz/
verb
使幼稚化；把…当作小孩对待
treat (someone) as a child or in a way that denies their maturity.
He accused her of infantilising him. 他指责她把他当小孩看待。
近义词:patronize, condescend, demean
反义词:empower, respect, mature
词性:v.
