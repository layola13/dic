nonevadible:/ˌnɒnɪˈveɪdəbl/
adj.
不可避免的，无法逃避的
Unable to be evaded or avoided.
His fate seemed nonevadible. 他看起来命运难逃。
近义词:inevitable, unavoidable, inescapable
反义词:avoidable, escapable, evadable
词性:形容词

词源:
"non-" (not) + "evadible" (capable of being evaded). "Evadible" comes from "evade" (escape or avoid), which ultimately derives from the Latin "evadere" (to go out, escape).
