excisable:/ɛkˈsaɪzəbl/
adjective
可征收消费税的
Subject to or liable to excise duty.  Relating to goods or commodities on which an excise tax is levied.
Cigarettes and alcohol are excisable products.  香烟和酒精是应税产品。
Synonyms: taxable, dutiable, levyable
Antonyms: non-taxable, duty-free, exempt

词性: adjective
