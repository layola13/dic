all-American:/ɔːl əˈmɛrɪkən/ | adj. n. | 全美明星的；典型的美国人的；全美明星运动员 |  Relating to or characteristic of an ideal or exemplary type of American, often associated with wholesome values, athleticism, and patriotism.  Can also refer to a person selected for an all-American team. | He was an all-American boy, with a bright smile and a love for baseball. 他是一个典型的美国男孩，笑容灿烂，热爱棒球。 |  She was named an all-American in her senior year of college.  她在大学四年级时被评为全美明星运动员。|  Synonyms: exemplary, ideal, quintessential, representative, star | Antonyms: atypical, unconventional, non-representative | adjective, noun

all-Americans:/ɔːl əˈmɛrɪkənz/ | n. | 全美明星运动员（复数）| Plural form of "all-American," referring to a group of athletes selected for an all-American team. | The all-Americans were honored at a special ceremony. 全美明星运动员们在一个特别的仪式上受到了表彰。 | The coach was proud of his team's three all-Americans. 教练为他的队里的三名全美明星运动员感到自豪。 | Synonyms: star athletes, top performers | Antonyms: average players, mediocre athletes | noun
