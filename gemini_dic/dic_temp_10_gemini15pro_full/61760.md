empathise with:/ˈempəθaɪz wɪð/ | v. |同情，感同身受，理解|To understand and share the feelings of another person.|I can empathise with his situation, having been through something similar myself. 我能理解他的处境，因为我自己也经历过类似的事情。|Synonyms: sympathize with, relate to, understand, share the feelings of|Antonyms: disregard, ignore, be indifferent to|verb

ETYMOLOGY->

From em- + -pathy.  "pathy" comes from the Greek word "pathos" meaning "feeling" or "suffering". The prefix "em-" intensifies the meaning, suggesting a deep entering into the feeling.
