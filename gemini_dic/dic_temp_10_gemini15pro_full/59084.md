dyadic:/daɪˈædɪk/ | adj.| 二元的，二体的，双元组成的 | Relating to a dyad, a group of two; characterized by two elements or parts interacting. |  The dyadic relationship between a mother and her child is fundamental to human development. 母子之间的二元关系是人类发展的基础。

The therapist focused on the dyadic interaction between the couple. 治疗师专注于这对夫妇之间的二元互动。 | 近义词：dual, binary, twofold | 反义词：monadic, singular | 词性: adjective

dyadic:/daɪˈædɪk/ | n.| 二元组，二元关系 | A group of two, especially two people interacting; a dyad. | The research focused on several dyadics within the community. 这项研究的重点是社区内的几个二元群体。

The dyadic formed by the two business partners proved highly successful. 由两位商业伙伴组成的二元组非常成功。 | 近义词：pair, duo, twosome | 反义词：monad | 词性: noun


词源:  From Greek *δυαδικός (duadikos), from δυάς (duas, “two”) + -ικός (-ikos, adjectival suffix).
