megalopolistic:/ˌmɛɡəˌlɒpəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 大都市的，特大城市的 | Relating to or characteristic of a megalopolis (a very large, heavily populated city or urban complex). |  The megalopolistic sprawl of the Northeast Corridor stretches from Boston to Washington, D.C. 美国东北部走廊的大都市蔓延从波士顿延伸到华盛顿特区。| synonyms: metropolitan, urban, cosmopolitan | antonyms: rural, provincial | adjective

megalopolis: /ˌmɛɡəˈlɒpəlɪs/ | n. | 特大城市，大都市带 | A very large, heavily populated city or urban complex. | The Pearl River Delta megalopolis in China is one of the world's largest. 中国的珠江三角洲大都市区是世界上最大的城市之一。 | synonyms: metropolis, conurbation, megacity | antonyms: village, hamlet | noun
