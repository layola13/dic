homestead:/ˈhoʊmsted/ (US), /ˈhəʊmsted/ (UK)| n. | 宅地，家园；农庄 |A house, especially a farmhouse, and the outbuildings and land belonging to it. |例句：They built a homestead on the prairies. 他们在草原上建造了一个家园。

例句：She grew up on her family's homestead in the countryside.  她在乡下她家的农庄长大。

近义词：farm, ranch, property, estate, residence|反义词:apartment, city|词性:noun
