lamellated:/ˈlæməleɪtɪd/
adjective
薄片状的，层状的
composed of thin layers or plates
The lamellated structure of the rock made it easy to split.  这块岩石的层状结构使它很容易分裂。
layered, stratified, laminar
homogeneous, monolithic
adjective
