confessions: /kənˈfɛʃənz/ (US) /kənˈfɛʃənz/ (UK)| n.|忏悔; 告白; 供认; 坦白|a statement admitting or acknowledging something (usually bad or wrong)|例句：His confessions shocked the community. 他的忏悔震惊了整个社区。

例句：The priest heard the confessions of his parishioners.  神父聆听了教区居民的忏悔。

近义词：admissions, acknowledgements, disclosures, avowals|反义词：denials, concealments|词性: 名词 (plural of confession)
