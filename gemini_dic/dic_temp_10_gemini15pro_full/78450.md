get-go:/ˈɡɛtɡoʊ/
noun
开始; 一开始
the very beginning
例句：From the get-go, I knew this project would be challenging.  从一开始，我就知道这个项目会很有挑战性。

例句：She was against the idea from the get-go. 她从一开始就反对这个主意。

近义词：beginning, start, outset, onset, inception
反义词：end, finish, conclusion
词性：noun
