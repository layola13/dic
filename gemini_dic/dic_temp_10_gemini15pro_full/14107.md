authenticatable:/ɔːˈθɛntɪkeɪtəbl/
adjective
可验证的，可认证的
Capable of being authenticated; able to be proven genuine.
Users must provide authenticatable credentials.  用户必须提供可验证的凭证。
The system requires an authenticatable user ID and password. 系统需要可验证的用户ID和密码。
近义词: verifiable, confirmable, provable
反义词: unverifiable, unprovable
词性: adjective
词源: From authenticate + -able.  Authenticate comes from Late Latin authenticāre, from Latin authenticus "genuine, trustworthy," from Greek authentikos "acting with one's own authority."
