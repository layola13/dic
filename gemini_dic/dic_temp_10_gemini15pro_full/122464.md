mobilizer:/ˈmoʊbəˌlaɪzər/ (US) /ˈməʊbɪˌlaɪzə(r)/ (UK)| n.|动员者；动员器|A person or thing that mobilizes something or someone.|The union leader was a powerful mobilizer of public opinion.  工会领导人是强大的舆论动员者。|近义词：activist, organizer, catalyst|反义词：pacifier, suppressor|noun

mobilize:/ˈmoʊbəˌlaɪz/ (US) /ˈməʊbɪˌlaɪz/ (UK)| v.|动员；调动|To organize and prepare (something, such as a group of people) for a particular purpose.|The government mobilized the army for war. 政府动员军队准备战争。|近义词：assemble, rally, marshal|反义词：demobilize, disband|verb
