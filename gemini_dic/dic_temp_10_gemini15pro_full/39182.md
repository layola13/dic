congener:/ˈkɒndʒɪnə(r)/ /ˈkɑːndʒənər/
noun
同类物，同源物，同族元素
A thing of the same kind or origin.  Specifically, in chemistry, an element belonging to the same group in the periodic table.
例句：
Methanol and ethanol are congeners.
甲醇和乙醇是同系物。


例句：
The researchers studied the effects of various congeners of PCBs on wildlife.
研究人员研究了多氯联苯的各种同系物对野生动物的影响。

近义词：analogue, counterpart, relative
反义词:  None (The concept of "opposite" doesn't apply to congeners).
词性: n.
