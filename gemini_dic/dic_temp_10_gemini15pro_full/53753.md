discombobulate: /ˌdɪskəmˈbɑːbəleɪt/ /ˌdɪskəmˈbɒbjʊleɪt/
adjective
使困惑的，使混乱的
To confuse or disorient someone; to upset or frustrate.
The sudden change in plans left me completely discombobulated.  这突如其来的计划改变让我完全不知所措。
The long flight and jet lag left her feeling discombobulated. 长途飞行和时差让她感到晕头转向。
近义词: bewildered, confused, disoriented, flustered, rattled
反义词: calm, composed, oriented, organized

词性: verb
使困惑，使混乱

词源: Origin uncertain, possibly a humorous pseudo-Latin formation. First recorded in the mid-19th century in American English.
