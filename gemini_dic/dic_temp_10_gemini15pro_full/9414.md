antiradiating:/ˌæntiˈreɪdieɪtɪŋ/
adjective
防辐射的
Designed to prevent the emission or propagation of radiation, especially electromagnetic radiation.
This special suit is antiradiating, protecting the worker from harmful gamma rays.  这件特殊的防护服是防辐射的，可以保护工人免受有害伽马射线的伤害。
The bunker has antiradiating properties.  这个掩体具有防辐射特性。
近义词: radiation-proof, radioprotective
反义词: radiating
