article:/ˈɑːrtɪkl/ (US) /ˈɑːtɪkəl/ (UK)| n.|文章；条款；物品；冠词|A piece of writing included with others in a newspaper, magazine, or other publication; a particular item or object; a separate clause or paragraph of a legal document or agreement; a, an, the|

例句：
I read an interesting article in the newspaper today.
我今天在报纸上读到一篇有趣的文章。

This article of clothing is very expensive.
这件衣服很贵。

近义词：piece, item, object, story, report, essay, editorial, commentary
反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on the specific meaning)

词性: noun
