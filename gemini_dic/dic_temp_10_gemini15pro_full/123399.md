monopetalous:/ˌmɒnəˈpɛtələs/ | adj. | 单瓣的 | Having a corolla composed of a single petal, or of petals united into one piece. | The flower is monopetalous, with a bell-shaped corolla.  这朵花是单瓣的，有着钟形的花冠。| Example 2: Monopetalous flowers are common in the family Asteraceae. 菊科植物中单瓣花很常见。 | Synonym: gamopetalous, sympetalous | Antonym: polypetalous | adjective
