nonconnivances:/ˌnɑːnkəˈnaɪvənsɪz/
n.
不串通；不默许；不知情
lack of connivance;  absence of secret or tacit understanding or cooperation.
The investigator found nonconnivance between the parties involved. 调查员发现相关各方之间没有串通。
近义词:  noncooperation, innocence, unawareness
反义词: connivance, collusion, complicity
词性: 名词 (plural)

Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + connivance (from Old French conniver, "to close the eyes," from Latin connivere, "to wink at").
