overmanaged:/ˌoʊvərˈmænɪdʒd/
adjective
管理过度的，过度管理的
Excessive or overly detailed management, often to the detriment of efficiency and morale.
The overmanaged team felt stifled and lacked motivation.  这个被过度管理的团队感到窒息，缺乏动力。
The company became overmanaged and bureaucratic, hindering innovation.  这家公司变得管理过度且官僚主义，阻碍了创新。
近义词:micromanaged, overcontrolled
反义词:undermanaged, loosely managed
词性:adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "managed" (past participle of "manage")  "over-"（前缀，表示“过度”）+ “managed”（“manage”的过去分词）
