declaim:/dɪˈkleɪm/
verb
慷慨陈词；朗诵；演讲；高谈阔论
To speak rhetorically; to make a formal speech.
The actor declaimed his lines with passion. 演员充满激情地朗诵他的台词。
近义词:orate, recite, pronounce, deliver
反义词:whisper, murmur, mumble
词性:v.

declaim:/dɪˈkleɪm/
词源:From Latin declamare (“to speak loudly, declaim”), from de- (“down, completely”) + clamare (“to cry out, shout”).
词性:v.
