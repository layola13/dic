galosh:/ɡəˈlɒʃ/ (UK), /ɡəˈlɑːʃ/ (US)| n.|套鞋，橡胶雨鞋|A waterproof overshoe, typically made of rubber.|例句：I put on my galoshes before going out in the rain. 我在下雨前穿上了套鞋。

例句：She bought a new pair of galoshes for the winter. 她买了一双新的冬季套鞋。|近义词：overshoe, rubber, wellie (UK)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps sandals or flip-flops for contrasting footwear) |词性:noun
