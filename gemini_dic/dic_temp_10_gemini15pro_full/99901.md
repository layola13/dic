internationalises:/ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəlaɪzɪz/ | /ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 使国际化 | To make something international; to adapt something for use in different countries. | The company internationalised its operations to expand into new markets.  该公司将其业务国际化，以扩展到新的市场。|  globalizes, expands | localizes, restricts | verb

internationalises:/ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəlaɪzɪz/ | /ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 使国际化 | To give something an international character or outlook. |  The university internationalised its curriculum to attract students from all over the world. 这所大学将其课程国际化，以吸引来自世界各地的学生。 | broadens, diversifies | narrows, limits | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From international + -ise/-ize. "International" comes from Late Latin "internationalis,"  from "inter" (between) + "nationalis" (national).
