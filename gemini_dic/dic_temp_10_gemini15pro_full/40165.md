contends:/kənˈtɛndz/
verb
竞争; 争辩; 声称
asserts something as a position in an argument
He contends that the judge was wrong. 他声称法官错了。
contends:/kənˈtɛndz/
verb
竞争; 争辩; 声称
struggles or competes, especially in a long or difficult way
Several teams are contending for the championship. 几支队伍正在争夺冠军。
contend:/kənˈtɛnd/
verb
竞争; 争辩; 声称
deal with a difficult situation
Emergency services are still contending with the aftermath of the hurricane. 紧急服务部门仍在应对飓风的善后工作。
synonym: argue, assert, compete, vie, fight, struggle, grapple
antonym: yield, surrender, acquiesce, agree, concede
词性: verb
