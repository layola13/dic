maa: /mɑː/ | n. | 妈妈 (非正式，方言) |  An informal or dialectal term for mother. | Example: My maa always makes the best cookies.  我妈妈总是做最好吃的饼干。| Example:  Maa said I could go to the party. 妈妈说我可以去参加派对。 | 近义词: mom, mum, mother | 反义词: (无直接反义词) | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely a variant of "mama," a common childish word for mother found in many languages.  It may be influenced by regional dialects.
