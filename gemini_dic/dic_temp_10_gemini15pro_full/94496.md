importers:/ɪmˈpɔːrtərz/
|n.|进口商；进口公司|A person or company that imports goods from another country.  A business that brings goods into a country from abroad for sale.|China is one of the largest importers of oil. 中国是最大的石油进口国之一。|synonyms: buyer, trader, dealer, merchant, purchaser|antonyms: exporters|noun
