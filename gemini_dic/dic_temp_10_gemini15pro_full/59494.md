easterliness:/ˈiːstərlɪnɪs/
noun
东向性；东风；偏东方向
The prevailing easterliness of the winds brought much-needed rain. 盛行的东风带来了急需的降雨。
easterliness:  The quality or state of being easterly; an easterly character or position.  It can refer to the direction of wind or a general eastward tendency.
Example: The easterliness of the trade winds influenced the sailors' route.  信风的东向性影响了水手的航线。
Example: The easterliness of the current carried the debris towards the shore. 水流的东向性将碎片带向海岸。
Synonyms: eastwardness, easting
Antonyms: westliness, westing
