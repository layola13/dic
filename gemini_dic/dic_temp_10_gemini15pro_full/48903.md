defray:/dɪˈfreɪ/ | v. |支付，承担（费用）| To cover the expenses of something; to pay for something.| The company will defray the costs of travel and accommodation. 公司将承担旅行和住宿的费用。| The scholarship defrays the cost of tuition. 奖学金支付了学费。 | 近义词: pay, cover, bear, meet | 反义词: charge, bill | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *desfraier "to break up, reduce, diminish," from des- "out, away" + fraier "to break, rub."  The original sense was "to break down expenses," hence "to pay."
