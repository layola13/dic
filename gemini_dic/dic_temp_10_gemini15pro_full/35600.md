coarser:/ˈkɔːrsər/ (US), /ˈkɔːsə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更粗糙的，更粗俗的|Comparative form of coarse.  Rougher or less refined in texture, quality, or appearance.  Can also refer to coarser language or behavior.|

例句：
The coarser sand irritated my skin.
这粗糙的沙子刺激了我的皮肤。

The fabric was coarser than I expected.
这种布料比我预想的要粗糙。


近义词：rougher, rough, cruder, coarser-grained|
反义词：finer, smoother, softer, refined|
词性: adjective
