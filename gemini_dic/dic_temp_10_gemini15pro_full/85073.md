hard-nosed:/ˌhɑːrdˈnoʊzd/ | adj. |顽强的，不妥协的，注重实际的|  Practical and determined, not influenced by emotions.  Showing an unwillingness to compromise.  |  He's a hard-nosed businessman who always gets what he wants. 他是一个顽强的商人，总是能得到他想要的东西。|  The team took a hard-nosed approach to negotiations. 该团队在谈判中采取了强硬的态度。| 近义词: tough, uncompromising, pragmatic, realistic, shrewd | 反义词: soft, sentimental, flexible, idealistic | 词性: 形容词

词源:  This compound adjective originated in the early 20th century, likely from the world of boxing. "Hard" refers to toughness, while "nosed" suggests someone who has taken a lot of punches to the nose and is thus unflinching.
