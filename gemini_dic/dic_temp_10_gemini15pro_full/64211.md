equiform:/ˈiːkwɪfɔːrm/
adjective
相同的，一致的
Having the same form or shape.
The two triangles are equiform, meaning they have the same shape but not necessarily the same size. 这两个三角形是等形的，意味着它们形状相同但不一定大小相同。
The architect designed two equiform buildings on opposite sides of the street.  建筑师在街道的两侧设计了两座形状相同的建筑。
近义词:similar, congruent, identical, alike
反义词:different, dissimilar, incongruent
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *aequiformis, from aequus (“equal”) + forma (“form, shape”).
