deployments:/dɪˈplɔɪmənts/| n. |部署，调度|The act of deploying something, especially troops or resources.  The strategic positioning or arrangement of something.|例句：The company announced the deployment of new software.  公司宣布部署新软件。|例句：The deployment of troops to the region was swift. 部署到该地区的部队行动迅速。|近义词：positioning, arrangement, stationing, allocation|反义词：withdrawal, retrieval, recall|词性:noun

词源: From deploy + -ment, ultimately from Old French despleier "to unfold, unfurl"
