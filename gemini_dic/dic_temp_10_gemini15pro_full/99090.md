interbreeding:/ˌɪntərˈbriːdɪŋ/
n.|杂交繁殖，异种交配|The interbreeding of different species can lead to unpredictable results.|不同物种之间的杂交繁殖可能会导致不可预知的结果。|
例句：The interbreeding of wolves and dogs has created a variety of hybrid breeds.|狼和狗的杂交繁殖创造了各种各样的杂交品种。

例句：Excessive interbreeding within a small population can lead to genetic defects.|在一个小群体中过度近亲繁殖会导致基因缺陷。

近义词：crossbreeding, hybridization|
反义词：outbreeding|
词性：名词


interbreeding:/ˌɪntərˈbriːdɪŋ/
adj.|杂交繁殖的|Interbreeding populations are more susceptible to disease.|杂交繁殖的种群更容易感染疾病。|
例句：The interbreeding populations of birds showed a decline in genetic diversity.|杂交繁殖的鸟类种群显示出遗传多样性的下降。

例句：Interbreeding varieties of plants can produce new and interesting characteristics.|杂交繁殖的植物品种可以产生新的有趣的特征。

近义词：hybrid, crossbred|
反义词：purebred|
词性：形容词


词源：
From inter- + breeding.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "breeding" refers to the production of offspring.
