anatomizer:/əˈnætəˌmaɪzər/| n.|解剖学家；分析者；批评家|Someone who dissects bodies or analyzes something closely and critically.|The anatomizer carefully examined the specimen.  解剖学家仔细检查了标本。|The literary anatomizer dissected the novel's themes and symbolism.  这位文学分析家仔细分析了这部小说的主题和象征意义。|近义词：analyst, dissector, critic, interpreter|反义词:synthesizer, compiler|noun

anatomize:/əˈnætəˌmaɪz/| v.|解剖；分析；批评|To dissect a body or analyze something in detail, often critically.|The biologist anatomized the frog to study its organs.  生物学家解剖了青蛙来研究它的器官。|The critic anatomized the film's plot and characters.  评论家仔细分析了这部电影的情节和人物。|近义词：analyze, dissect, examine, criticize|反义词:synthesize, combine|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *anatomizare, from Ancient Greek ἀνατέμνειν (anatémnein, “to dissect”), from ἀνά (aná, “up”) + τέμνειν (témnein, “to cut”).
