nonliquidations:/ˌnɒnlɪkwɪˈdeɪʃənz/
n.
不清理，不结清（通常指公司或债务）
The nonliquidations of these assets have tied up a significant amount of capital. 这些资产的不清算占用了大量资金。
nonliquidation is the state of not being liquidated, often referring to assets or debts.  Nonliquidation can occur when a company avoids bankruptcy or when a specific asset is held rather than sold.
例句：The nonliquidations of the company's assets allowed them to continue operating. 该公司资产的不清算使他们能够继续运营。

例句：The nonliquidation of the debt resulted in accumulating interest.  这笔债务的不清算导致利息累积。

近义词：non-settlement, non-closure
反义词：liquidations, settlement
词性：名词
