fibrinogen:/faɪˈbrɪnədʒən/ /faɪˈbrɪnəˌdʒɛn/ | n. | 纤维蛋白原 | A soluble protein present in blood plasma, from which fibrin is produced by the action of the enzyme thrombin. |  Fibrinogen levels are often elevated during pregnancy. 纤维蛋白原水平在怀孕期间通常会升高。|  High levels of fibrinogen can increase the risk of blood clots. 高水平的纤维蛋白原会增加血栓的风险。 | synonyms: clotting factor I | antonyms: None | noun
