consecrated: /ˈkɒnsɪkreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 神圣的，奉献的，被祝圣的 | Dedicated to a sacred purpose; made holy; devoted solemnly to a particular purpose. | The consecrated ground was treated with reverence. 这片被祝圣的土地受到了人们的崇敬。 | Synonyms: hallowed, sacred, blessed, sanctified | Antonyms: profane, secular, unconsecrated | adjective

consecrate: /ˈkɒnsɪkreɪt/ | vt. | 使神圣，奉献，祝圣 | To declare or make (something, usually a church or other religious place) sacred; to dedicate solemnly to a particular purpose. | The bishop consecrated the new church. 主教祝圣了这座新教堂。| Synonyms: hallow, sanctify, bless, dedicate | Antonyms: desecrate, profane | verb
