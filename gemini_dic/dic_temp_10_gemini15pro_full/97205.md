inflammably:/ɪnˈflæməbli/ | adv. | 易燃地，可燃地 | In a way that is easily set on fire. | The material is highly inflammable, so keep it away from open flames. 这种材料非常易燃，所以要远离明火。|  例句：The old wooden houses were inflammably close together. 这些老木屋彼此之间的距离非常近，很容易着火。| 近义词：combustibly, readily ignited | 反义词：nonflammable, fire-resistant | 词性: 副词

inflammable:/ɪnˈflæməbl/ | adj. | 易燃的，可燃的 | Easily set on fire. |  例句：Gasoline is a highly inflammable liquid. 汽油是一种高度易燃的液体。| 近义词：combustible, flammable | 反义词：nonflammable, fire-resistant | 词性: 形容词


词源: From Late Latin *inflammabilis, from Latin inflammare (“to set on fire”), from in- (“in, into, on, upon, against”) + flammare (“to flame”), from flamma (“flame”).
