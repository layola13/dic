infights:/ˈɪnˌfaɪts/ | n. | 内讧；内部斗争 | A struggle for power or advantage within a group or organization. |  The party leadership was weakened by bitter infights.  该党的领导层因激烈的内讧而被削弱。 | Example: The company was plagued by infights between different departments. 公司受到不同部门之间内讧的困扰。 | Synonyms:  internal conflict, internal strife, power struggle, squabble, bickering | Antonyms:  cooperation, collaboration, unity, harmony | noun

infight:/ˈɪnˌfaɪt/ | v. | 进行内讧；参与内部斗争 | To engage in a struggle for power or advantage within a group or organization. |  The two factions infighted for control of the party.  这两个派系为争夺党的控制权而内讧。 | Example:  Rival gangs infighted for control of the drug trade. 敌对帮派为争夺毒品交易的控制权而内讧。| Synonyms:  clash, contend, feud, quarrel | Antonyms:  cooperate, collaborate, unite | verb
