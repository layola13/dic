nonharmoniously:/ˌnɒnhɑːrˈməʊniəsli/ (US) /ˌnɒnhɑːˈməʊniəsli/ (UK)| adv.|不和谐地，不协调地|Not in a harmonious way; discordantly.|The instruments played nonharmoniously, creating a cacophony of sound.  这些乐器演奏得很不和谐，发出刺耳的声音。|The different factions within the government worked nonharmoniously, leading to political gridlock. 政府内部的不同派系不协调地工作，导致政治僵局。|近义词: discordantly, inharmoniously, dissonantly|反义词: harmoniously, concordantly, harmonically|词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix): not, opposite of  (来自拉丁语)
harmonious (adjective): exhibiting or characterized by harmony (来自希腊语 harmonia "agreement, concord")
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
