dovish:/ˈdʌvɪʃ/| adj.|鸽派的，爱好和平的|Favoring peace or conciliation; opposed to war.  Characterized by or suggestive of a dove, especially in gentleness.|例句：The president took a dovish stance on the conflict, advocating for a diplomatic solution. 总统在冲突中采取了鸽派立场，主张通过外交途径解决。

例句：Her dovish nature made her a natural mediator. 她爱好和平的天性使她成为一个天生的调解人。|近义词：pacific, peaceful, conciliatory, appeasing|反义词：hawkish, belligerent, aggressive|词性:形容词
