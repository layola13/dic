parasitize:/ˈpærəsaɪtaɪz/ | v. | 寄生; 利用...自肥 | To live as a parasite on or in another organism; to exploit or leech off of someone or something. | Example 1: The wasp larvae parasitize the caterpillar.  黄蜂幼虫寄生在毛毛虫身上。

Example 2:  He seems to parasitize off his wealthy friends. 他似乎在利用他富有的朋友自肥。 |  Synonyms: exploit, leech, sponge, freeload | Antonyms: support, sustain, nourish | verb
