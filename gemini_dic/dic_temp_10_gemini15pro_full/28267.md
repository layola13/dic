captivate:/ˈkæptɪveɪt/| vt.| 迷住，使着迷|to attract and hold the interest and attention of someone by being entertaining, pretty, etc.|Her singing captivated the audience. 她的歌声迷住了观众。|近义词：enchant, fascinate, charm, mesmerize|反义词：bore, repel, repulse|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin captivatus, past participle of captivare (“to take or hold captive”), from Latin captivus (“captive”).
