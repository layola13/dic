congrue:/kənˈɡruː/ /ˈkɑːŋɡruː/
verb
一致；符合；相符
To agree, correspond, or coincide; to be in harmony or agreement.
The data from the two studies congrue with each other. 这两项研究的数据彼此一致。
His actions do not congrue with his words. 他的言行不一。
近义词:accord, agree, coincide, conform, correspond, harmonize
反义词:conflict, disagree, differ, diverge
词性:verb

congruent:/ˈkɒŋɡruənt/ /ˈkɑːŋɡruənt/
adjective
全等的；一致的；适合的
Corresponding or agreeing; similar in character or function.
The two triangles are congruent. 这两个三角形全等。
Her behavior was congruent with her values. 她的行为与她的价值观一致。
近义词:accordant, agreeing, coincident, conformable, consonant, corresponding, harmonious, identical, similar
反义词:conflicting, different, disagreeing, dissimilar, divergent, incongruous, unlike
词性:adjective

congruence:/ˈkɒŋɡruəns/ /ˈkɑːŋɡruəns/
noun
一致；适合；全等
Agreement, harmony, or correspondence.
There is a congruence between the two sets of data. 这两组数据之间存在一致性。
The congruence of their goals made them natural allies. 他们目标的一致使他们成为天然的盟友。
近义词:accord, agreement, coincidence, conformity, correspondence, harmony
反义词:conflict, disagreement, difference, divergence, incongruity
词性:noun

词源: From Latin congruere "to agree, come together, correspond," from com "together" (see com-) + -gruere, related to ruere "to rush, fall"
