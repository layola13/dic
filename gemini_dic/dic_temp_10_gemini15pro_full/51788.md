devourers:/dəˈvaʊərərz/
noun
吞噬者；贪婪的消费者
People or animals that consume large quantities of something.
Locusts are notorious devourers of crops. 蝗虫是臭名昭著的庄稼吞噬者。
The flames were like ravenous devourers, consuming everything in their path.  火焰就像贪婪的吞噬者，吞噬着沿途的一切。
consumers, predators
preservers
