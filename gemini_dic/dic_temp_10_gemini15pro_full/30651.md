cerite: /ˈsɛraɪt/ | n. | 硅铈石 | A brownish-red mineral containing cerium, lanthanum, and other rare earth elements.  It is a silicate mineral and a source of these elements. | Cerite is found in certain igneous rocks. 硅铈石发现于某些火成岩中。|  Scientists extracted cerium from cerite. 科学家从硅铈石中提取铈。 |Synonyms: allanite, bastnäsite | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
