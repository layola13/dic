excused: /ɪkˈskjuːzd/ | adj. | 免除的；被原谅的；有理由的 |  freed from an obligation or duty; forgiven for a fault or offense; having a valid reason |  He was excused from jury duty because of his illness. 他因病被免除陪审团义务。 | synonyms: forgiven, pardoned, absolved, exempted, released | antonyms:  blamed, accused, condemned, obligated, required | adjective

excused: /ɪkˈskjuːzd/ | v. | 原谅；免除 | to forgive someone for a minor offense or release someone from an obligation |  Please excuse my lateness. 请原谅我的迟到。 | synonyms: forgive, pardon, absolve, exempt, release | antonyms: blame, accuse, condemn, obligate, require | verb
