ichnography: /ɪkˈnɒɡrəfi/ /ɪkˈnɑːɡrəfi/
ichnography: n.
ichnography: 平面图；水平投影图
ichnography: A drawing or diagram of a building's ground plan or layout; the representation of a horizontal section.
ichnography: The architect presented the ichnography of the new museum, clearly showing the arrangement of the galleries.  建筑师展示了新博物馆的平面图，清楚地展示了画廊的布局。

ichnography:  The ichnography revealed the complex network of tunnels beneath the ancient city.  平面图揭示了古城下方复杂的隧道网络。
ichnography: 近义词: ground plan, floor plan, layout, plan, blueprint
ichnography: 反义词: elevation, section
ichnography: 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ichnographia*, from Ancient Greek ἰχνογραφία (ikhnographía, “ground plan”), from ἴχνος (íkhnos, “trace, track, footprint”) + -γραφία (-graphía, “writing, drawing, description”).
