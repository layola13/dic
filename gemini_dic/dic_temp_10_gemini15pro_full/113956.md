magdaleon: /ˌmæɡdəˈliːɒn/ | n. | 玛格达拉人，抹大拉的马利亚 (Mary Magdalene) 的追随者 | A follower of Mary Magdalene, sometimes specifically a member of a gnostic sect believing she possessed secret teachings of Jesus. |  In some early Christian traditions, the Magdaleon were believed to have special knowledge. 在一些早期的基督教传统中，玛格达拉人被认为拥有特殊的知识。 |  The Gospel of Mary presents a Magdaleon perspective on the resurrection.  《马利亚福音》展现了抹大拉的信徒对复活的观点。| synonym:  disciple, follower | antonym: opponent, detractor | noun
