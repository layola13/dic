neuralgia:/nʊˈræl.dʒə/ /nʊˈræl.dʒi.ə/ | n. | 神经痛 |  A sharp, stabbing pain or burning sensation that occurs along a nerve.  It can be caused by a variety of factors, including injury, infection, or disease.|  She suffers from trigeminal neuralgia, which causes excruciating pain in her face. 她患有三叉神经痛，导致脸部剧烈疼痛。 | Synonyms: nerve pain, stabbing pain | Antonyms: analgesia, pain relief | noun

neuralgias:/nʊˈræl.dʒəz/ /nʊˈræl.dʒi.əz/ | n. | 神经痛（复数）| Plural form of neuralgia, referring to multiple instances of nerve pain. |  He experiences various neuralgias throughout his body due to diabetes. 由于糖尿病，他全身经历各种神经痛。 | Synonyms: nerve pains, stabbing pains | Antonyms:  analgesias, pain relief | noun
