oculist: /ˈɒkjəlɪst/ (British) /ˈɑːkjəlɪst/ (American)| n.|眼科医生|A doctor who specializes in the treatment of diseases and disorders of the eye. |Example: My grandmother had to visit an oculist when she started having trouble with her vision. 我的祖母视力出现问题时不得不去看眼科医生。

Example:  The oculist prescribed new glasses for my nearsightedness. 眼科医生为我的近视开了新眼镜。

近义词: ophthalmologist, eye doctor|反义词: None|词性: noun
