lien:/ˈliːən/ | n. |留置权；扣押权|A right to keep possession of property belonging to another person until a debt owed by that person is discharged.|The bank had a lien on the property until the loan was repaid. 银行对该财产拥有留置权，直到贷款还清为止。|Synonym: charge, security, encumbrance|Antonym: release|noun
