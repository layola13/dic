bend-over:/ˌbɛnd ˈoʊvər/ | n. | 弯腰，俯身 | An act of bending at the waist, often to pick something up or look at something closely.  |  He did a quick bend-over to tie his shoe. 他迅速弯腰系鞋带。 |  stoop, crouch | stand up, straighten up | noun

bend-over:/ˌbɛnd ˈoʊvər/ | v. | 弯腰，俯身 | To bend at the waist. |  I had to bend over to pick up the coin. 我不得不弯腰去捡硬币。 | stoop, crouch | stand up, straighten up | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "bend" (Old English *bendan, to curve, stretch) and the adverb "over" (Old English ofer, above, beyond).
