fibular:/ˈfɪbjʊlə(r)/| n.|腓骨|The outer and usually smaller of the two bones between the knee and the ankle in vertebrates.|A fracture of the fibula can be a painful injury. 腓骨骨折可能是一种痛苦的损伤。|The surgeon repaired the damaged fibula with a plate and screws.  外科医生用钢板和螺钉修复了受损的腓骨。|近义词:calf bone, peroneal bone|反义词:tibia|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *fibula, diminutive of Latin fibula "brooch, clasp," so called from its resemblance to a clasp. The Latin word is related to figere "to fasten."
