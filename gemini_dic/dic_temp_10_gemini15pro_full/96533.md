indoline:/ˈɪndəˌlinː/ |n.|吲哚啉|A colorless, oily liquid, C₈H₉N, that is the parent compound of many alkaloids and is used in the manufacture of dyes and pharmaceuticals.  It is a heterocyclic compound containing a benzene ring fused to a pyrrole ring without the double bond in the pyrrole ring.|Indoline is a key intermediate in the synthesis of many pharmaceuticals. 吲哚啉是许多药物合成中的关键中间体。|Indoline derivatives exhibit interesting biological activities. 吲哚啉衍生物表现出有趣的生物活性。|近义词: dihydroindole|反义词: indole|词性: noun
