beers:/bɪərz/ | n. | 啤酒（复数） | An alcoholic drink made from yeast-fermented malt flavored with hops. |  He ordered two beers. 他点了两杯啤酒。| Synonyms: ales, brews | Antonyms: None | noun


Etymology: From Old English *bēor (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *beuzą (“beer”). Cognate with Old Norse bjór, Old High German bior, Old Saxon bior. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰeusóm.
