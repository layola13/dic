catapetalous: /ˌkætəˈpɛtələs/
adjective
下瓣的；花瓣下垂的
Having petals directed downwards.
The *Clerodendrum thomsoniae* has beautiful catapetalous, crimson flowers.  红萼Clerodendrum thomsoniae有美丽的下垂的深红色花朵。
The botanist identified the rare orchid by its distinctive catapetalous blooms. 植物学家通过其独特的下垂花朵识别出这种稀有的兰花。
近义词:  pendulous, drooping, reflexed
反义词:  erect, upright
