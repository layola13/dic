crev:/krɛv/ | n. | 缝隙，裂缝 | A narrow opening, a crack or fissure. |例句：Water seeped through the crev in the rock. 水从岩石的缝隙中渗出。

例句：The climber found a handhold in a crev in the cliff face. 攀登者在悬崖峭壁的裂缝中找到了一个支撑点。| 近义词：crack, fissure, crevice, gap, chink | 反义词：seal, closure | 词性：名词

词源:  Likely a shortened form of "crevice," which comes from Old French "crevaisse" (crack, fissure), ultimately from Latin "crepare" (to crack, break, burst).
