agoraphobes:/ˌæɡərəˈfoʊbz/ | n. | 恐旷症患者 | People who suffer from agoraphobia, a fear of open or crowded spaces. |  Many agoraphobes find it difficult to leave their homes. 许多恐旷症患者发现很难离开家。|  Agoraphobes often experience panic attacks in crowded places. 恐旷症患者经常在拥挤的地方经历恐慌症发作。| synonyms: sufferers of agoraphobia | antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "sociable people" but this isn't a true opposite) | noun
