deconglomerates:/diːkənˈɡlɒmərˌeɪts/ | v. | 分解，拆分（企业集团）|  To separate a large business organization into smaller, more independent companies. |  The company deconglomerates its media holdings to focus on its core business.  该公司将其媒体资产拆分，以专注于其核心业务。 | synonyms:  divest, break up, dismantle, separate,  | antonyms:  merge, consolidate, combine, amalgamate | verb

deconglomerate:/diːkənˈɡlɒmərət/ | n. |  分解，拆分（后的企业）| The act or process of separating a large business organization into smaller, more independent companies, or a company that has been separated in this way. | The deconglomerate is now a more agile and competitive player in the market.  拆分后的公司现在在市场上更加灵活和具有竞争力。 | synonyms:  division, spin-off, subsidiary | antonyms:  conglomerate, corporation | noun

词源：
de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + conglomerate (from Latin conglomerare, "to roll together")
