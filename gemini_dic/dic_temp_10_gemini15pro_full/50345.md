deplume:/dɪˈpluːm/ | v. | 剥夺（羽毛）；使丧失（名誉、权威等） | To strip of feathers; to deprive of something valuable, as of honor or reputation. | The scandal deplumed him of his authority. 这桩丑闻剥夺了他的权威。 | Synonyms: strip, divest, deprive, rob | Antonyms: adorn, endow, invest | verb

deplume:/dɪˈpluːm/ | v. | 拔毛；剥光 | To pluck or strip of feathers. | The chef deplumed the chicken before roasting it.  厨师在烤鸡之前把鸡的毛拔光了。 | Synonyms: pluck, feather | Antonyms:  implant | verb
