as-a-matter-of-fact:/æz ə ˈmætər əv fækt/
adverbial phrase|事实上，其实，说真的|Used to introduce a statement that contradicts what someone else has said or thought, or to add emphasis to what you are saying.|例句：I thought he was from Canada, but as a matter-of-fact, he's American. 我以为他来自加拿大，但事实上他是美国人。|近义词：in fact, actually, really, indeed|反义词:theoretically, hypothetically|词性: 副词短语

as-a-matter-of-fact:/æz ə ˈmætər əv fækt/
adverbial phrase|事实上，其实，说真的|Used to introduce a statement that provides additional information that is relevant to the topic being discussed.|例句：He’s quite tall. As a matter of fact, he’s the tallest in his class. 他很高。事实上，他是班上最高的。|近义词：in fact, actually, really, indeed|反义词:theoretically, hypothetically|词性: 副词短语
