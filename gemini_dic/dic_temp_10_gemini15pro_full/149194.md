oxygenations:/ˌɒksɪdʒəˈneɪʃənz/ | n. | 氧合；充氧 |The process of adding oxygen to something, typically blood. |例句：The patient's blood oxygenations improved after receiving supplemental oxygen. 病人在接受补充氧气后，血液氧合情况有所改善。

例句：Proper oxygenations of the blood is essential for survival. 血液的适当氧合对于生存至关重要。


|近义词：oxygenation, aeration |反义词: deoxygenation |词性: 名词(plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From oxygen + -ation.  Oxygen from French oxygène,  from Greek oxys "sharp, acid" + -gène "producing,"  from gignesthai "to be born, become."
