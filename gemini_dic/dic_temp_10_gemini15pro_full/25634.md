bunchily:/ˈbʌntʃɪli/
adjective
成群地，成束地，簇拥地
Growing or arranged in bunches; clustered together.
The flowers were bunchily arranged in the vase.  花瓶里的花簇拥在一起。
The grapes hung bunchily from the vine. 葡萄成串地挂在藤蔓上。
Synonym: clustered, grouped, bunched
Antonym: sparsely, scattered, individually
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From bunch + -ly (adverb/adjective suffix).  "Bunch" comes from  Middle English *bunche, probably from a Scandinavian source, related to Norwegian *bunke "heap, pile".
