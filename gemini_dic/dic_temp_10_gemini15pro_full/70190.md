fibrinogenous:/ˌfaɪbrɪˈnɒdʒɪnəs/ | adj. | 产生纤维蛋白的；由纤维蛋白形成的 | Relating to or producing fibrin.  Fibrinogen is a soluble protein present in blood plasma, from which fibrin is produced by the action of the enzyme thrombin. |  The fibrinogenous reaction is crucial for blood clotting. 纤维蛋白原反应对血液凝固至关重要。 | Example 2:  Fibrinogenous activity increases after injury. 受伤后纤维蛋白原活性会增加。 | synonyms: fibrin-forming, fibrinoplastic | antonyms: fibrinolytic | Adjective
