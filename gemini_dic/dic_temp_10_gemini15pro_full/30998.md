chalker:/ˈtʃɔːkər/ (US), /ˈtʃɔːkə(r)/ (UK)| n.|粉笔使用者；涂粉笔的人；（尤指）台球杆的巧克粉涂抹器|A person who uses chalk, especially one who chalks a billiard cue. |The chalker carefully applied the chalk to the tip of his cue.|Synonyms: cue-tipper (for billiards), marker|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider 'eraser' in a specific context)|noun
