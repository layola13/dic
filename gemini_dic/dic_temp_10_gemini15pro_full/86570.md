heliconian:/ˌhɛlɪˈkoʊniən/ | adj. n. |  赫利孔山的；缪斯的；(Heliconian)蝴蝶的；赫利孔蝴蝶 | Relating to Mount Helicon in Boeotia, Greece, sacred to the Muses; also, relating to or resembling a Heliconian butterfly. | The Heliconian butterflies are known for their bright colors.  赫利孔蝴蝶以其鲜艳的颜色而闻名。|  She wrote a Heliconian poem, inspired by the Muses. 她写了一首受缪斯女神启发的赫利孔诗。 | 近义词：Pierian, Muse-inspired (for the adjective); Heliconiid (for the butterfly) | 反义词：None | 词性：adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *Ἑλικώνιος (Helikṓnios) "of Helicon," from Ἑλικών (Helikṓn), name of a mountain in Boeotia, sacred to Apollo and the Muses. The butterfly sense is a modern taxonomic name, referring to their habitat in the Neotropics.
