garreted:/ˈɡærɪtɪd/
adjective
设有阁楼的；住在阁楼的
Furnished with a garret; inhabiting a garret.
He lived in a garreted room. 他住在一个有阁楼的房间里。
Near synonyms: attic, lofted
Antonyms: basement, ground-floor
词性: 形容词
