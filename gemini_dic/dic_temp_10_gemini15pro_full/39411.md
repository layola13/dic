conmen:/ˈkɒnmɛn/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnmɛn/ (US)| n. |骗子；诈骗犯|A man who cheats or tricks people by gaining their trust and persuading them to believe something that is not true.|The conmen tricked elderly people out of their savings. 这些骗子骗取了老人们的积蓄。|近义词：fraudster, swindler, cheat, trickster|反义词: honest person, law-abiding citizen|Noun
