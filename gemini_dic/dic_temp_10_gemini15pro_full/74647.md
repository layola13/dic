free-for-all:/ˌfriː fər ˈɔːl/ | n. | 混乱；混战；自由竞争 | A disorganized or unrestricted situation or contest in which everyone competes with everyone else. | The Black Friday sale was a complete free-for-all. 黑色星期五的促销完全是一场混战。|  melee, brawl, scrum, dogfight | order, organization | noun

free-for-all:/ˌfriː fər ˈɔːl/ | adj. |  自由参与的；混战的 | Open to everyone; unrestricted. | It was a free-for-all buffet, so everyone could eat as much as they wanted.  这是一个自由参与的自助餐，所以每个人都可以想吃多少就吃多少。 | unrestricted, open, chaotic | restricted, organized | adjective
