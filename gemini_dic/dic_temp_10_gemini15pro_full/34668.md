clerical: /ˈklerɪkəl/ (US), /ˈklerɪkl/ (UK)| adj.| 文书的；办事员的；牧师的|Relating to office work, especially routine documentation and administrative tasks; also relating to clergy. |例句：She found the clerical work tedious. 她觉得文书工作很乏味。|例句：He wore a clerical collar. 他戴着牧师的衣领。|近义词：administrative, secretarial, pastoral|反义词：managerial, executive|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French clerc 'clerk' or late Latin clericalis, from clericus 'clergyman.'
