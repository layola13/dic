heliochromy:/ˌhiːliˈɒkrəmi/| n.|彩色摄影; 彩色照相术; 天然彩色照相术|A process of color photography in which the colors are produced by the direct action of light on a specially prepared plate. |Example 1: Heliochromy was a significant advancement in photography, allowing for the capture of true color images. 彩色照相术是摄影技术的一项重大进步，它能够捕捉真实的彩色图像。

Example 2:  The museum showcased early examples of heliochromy, demonstrating the vibrant hues achievable with this technique. 博物馆展示了早期的彩色照相作品，展示了用这种技术可以实现的鲜艳色彩。|近义词：color photography, chromophotography|反义词: monochrome photography|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hēlios* (sun) + *chrōma* (color)
