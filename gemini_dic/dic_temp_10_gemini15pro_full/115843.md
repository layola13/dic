markings:/ˈmɑːrkɪŋz/
n.|标记，记号，符号；(动物或植物的)斑纹，花纹|Marks or other features on a surface or body, often indicating something.|The markings on the butterfly's wings were intricate and beautiful. 蝴蝶翅膀上的花纹错综复杂，非常美丽。|signs, symbols, patterns, lines, spots|blankness, plainness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *markinge*, derived from the verb *marken* (to mark), which ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *markōną* (to mark, border, limit).  The plural form "markings" reflects the standard English pluralization of nouns ending in "-ing."
