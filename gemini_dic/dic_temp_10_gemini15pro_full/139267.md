obligor:/ˈɑːblɪɡɔːr/ (US), /əˈblɪɡɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.|债务人，义务人|A person or entity who is legally or contractually obligated to provide something to another person or entity (the obligee).|例句：The obligor is required to make monthly payments to the obligee. 债务人必须每月向债权人付款。

例句：Under the terms of the contract, the obligor is responsible for all repairs. 根据合同条款，债务人负责所有维修。|近义词：debtor, payer|反义词：obligee, creditor|词性:名词
