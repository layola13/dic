outliving:/ˌaʊtˈlɪvɪŋ/
Verb
比...活得长;  幸免于;  经受住
Living longer than someone or something else. Surviving or enduring something.
She outlived her husband by twenty years. 她比她丈夫多活了二十年。
The company outlived the recession. 这家公司挺过了经济衰退。
survive, outlast
predecease
