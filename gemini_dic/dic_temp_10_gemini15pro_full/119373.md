microcosmography: /ˌmaɪkrəʊkɒzˈmɒɡrəfi/ /ˌmaɪkroʊkɑːzˈmɑːɡrəfi/ | n. |  微观世界描述；缩影 | A description of a small world, especially the world of human beings, seen as a representation of the larger world. | John Earle's *Microcosmography* is a witty and insightful portrayal of 17th-century London life.  约翰·厄尔的《微观世界描述》是对17世纪伦敦生活机智而深刻的刻画。| synonym: portrayal, depiction, sketch, vignette | antonym: macrocosm | noun
