impartation:/ˌɪmpɑːrˈteɪʃn/ | n. |传授，赋予，给予|The impartation of knowledge is the primary function of a teacher.  知识的传授是教师的主要职责。|例句：The impartation of his wisdom to his followers was invaluable.  他将智慧传授给他的追随者是无价的。

例句：The ritual involved the impartation of spiritual power.  这个仪式包括赋予精神力量。|近义词：communication, transmission, bestowal, conveyance|反义词：withholding, concealment|词性：名词

impart:/ɪmˈpɑːrt/ | vt. |传授，赋予，告知|He imparted his knowledge to his students. 他把知识传授给学生。|例句：The teacher imparted valuable lessons to her students. 老师给学生们传授了宝贵的课程。

例句：She imparted the secret to her closest friend. 她把这个秘密告诉了她最亲密的朋友。|近义词：communicate, convey, transmit, disclose|反义词：withhold, conceal|词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *impartacioun, from Late Latin impartātiō (“communication, imparting”), from Latin impartō (“I share, bestow, communicate”), from in- (“in, into”) + pars (“part, share”).
