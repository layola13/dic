frumpishly:/ˈfrʌmpɪʃli/
adverb
邋遢地；古板地；不修边幅地
In a frumpy or dowdy manner; shabbily and unstylishly.
She dressed frumpishly in an old cardigan and worn-out shoes. 她穿着老旧的开襟羊毛衫和破旧的鞋子，显得邋遢。
Near Synonyms: dowdily, drably, sloppily, unstylishly
Near Antonyms: smartly, stylishly, fashionably, elegantly
Part of Speech: adverb
