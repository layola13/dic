例句：Ancient myths often feature ignivomous creatures, symbolizing destruction and power.  古代神话中经常出现喷火的生物，象征着毁灭和力量。|近义词：fire-breathing, fire-spewing|反义词: non-flammable, fire-resistant|词性: adjective
