in-the-black:/ɪn ðə blæk/
adjective
盈利；赢利；赚钱；顺差
Having made a profit; not in debt.
Our company has been in the black for three consecutive quarters. 我们公司连续三个季度盈利。
The business is finally in the black after years of struggle.  经过多年的努力，这家公司终于盈利了。
近义词: profitable, solvent, creditworthy
反义词: in-the-red, insolvent, bankrupt
词性: adjective

词源: This idiom originates from accounting practices. Traditionally, accountants used black ink to record profits and red ink to record losses. Therefore, "in the black" signifies a positive balance, while "in the red" signifies a negative balance.
