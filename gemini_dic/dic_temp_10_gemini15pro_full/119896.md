militarization:/ˌmɪlɪtərɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
军事化
The process of organizing something or someone on a military basis; the act of equipping and training for war, or the act of increasing the military capacity of a country.
The militarization of the police force has been a controversial issue. 警察部队的军事化一直是一个有争议的问题。
militarisation (British English)
armament, buildup
demilitarization, disarmament
noun
