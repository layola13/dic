epiboly:/ɪˈpɪbəli/| n. |外包，上包，包被|The spreading or extension of one part of a developing embryo over another part.|The epiboly of the ectoderm is a crucial step in gastrulation. 外胚层的包被是原肠形成的关键步骤。|近义词：envelopment, overgrowth|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly invagination depending on context) |词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek *epibolḗ* "a throwing on or over," from *epibállein* "to throw upon," from *epi-* "upon" + *bállein* "to throw."
