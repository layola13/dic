adequately: /ˈædɪkwɪtli/
adverb
充分地；足够地
To a satisfactory or acceptable extent.
He was adequately prepared for the exam. 他为考试做了充分的准备。

The job was not adequately done. 这项工作做得不够充分。

近义词: sufficiently, competently, satisfactorily
反义词: inadequately, insufficiently, poorly
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adaequātus, past participle of adaequāre (“to make equal to”), from ad- (“to”) + aequāre (“to make equal”), from aequus (“equal, level, just”).
