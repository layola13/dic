abye:/əˈbaɪ/
verb
赎罪; 忍受; 承受
to experience something unpleasant or to suffer for something that you have done wrong
He will abye the consequences of his actions. 他将承受他行为的后果。
近义词:suffer, endure, bear, experience
反义词:rejoice, celebrate
词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *abien*, from Old English *ābycgan* (“to buy back, redeem, expiate”), from *ā-* (“on, in, at, to”)+ *bycgan* (“to buy”).  Cognate with German *erbeigen*.  The original sense was "to pay the penalty for," hence "to atone for," later "to endure, suffer."
