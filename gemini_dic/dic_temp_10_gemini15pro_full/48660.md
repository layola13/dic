defensibly:/dɪˈfɛnsɪbli/
adverb
可辩护地，有理由地
in a way that can be defended or justified
Her actions, though controversial, were defensibly motivated by her desire to protect her family. 她的行为虽然有争议，但出于保护家人的愿望，是可以辩护的。

Her actions were defensibly motivated. 她的行为是有理由的。

justifiably, reasonably, legitimately
indefensibly, unjustifiably, unreasonably
adverb
