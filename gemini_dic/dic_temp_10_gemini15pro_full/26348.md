buzzwords:/ˈbʌzwɜːrdz/
n.
流行语；时髦词语
Fashionable words or phrases, often used in business, politics, or technology, that sound impressive but may have little real meaning.
Example: "Synergy" and "paradigm shift" are common corporate buzzwords.
“协同效应”和“模式转变”是常见的企业流行语。

Example:  He sprinkled his presentation with buzzwords to impress the executives.
他在演讲中穿插了一些流行语，以给高管们留下深刻印象。

Synonyms: jargon, clichés, trendy terms, catchphrases, vogue words
Antonyms: standard vocabulary, plain language, simple terms
