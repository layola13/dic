eglandulose: /iːˈɡlændʒʊləs/ | adj. | 无腺的；缺乏腺体的 | Lacking glands; not glandular. | The eglandulose skin of reptiles helps them conserve water. 爬行动物无腺的皮肤有助于它们保存水分。 | The botanist identified the plant as an eglandulose species. 植物学家将这种植物鉴定为无腺物种。 | 近义词: aglandular | 反义词: glandular | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "e-" (without) + "glandulose" (having glands).  The prefix "e-" comes from Greek, meaning "out of" or "without."  "Glandulose" comes from "glandula" (Latin for "little acorn," referring to the shape of some glands) + "-ose" (a suffix indicating possession or resemblance).
