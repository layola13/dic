depositable:/dɪˈpɒzɪtəbl/
adjective
可存的，可存放的
able to be deposited, especially of money in a bank account.
The money is depositable into your savings account.  这笔钱可以存入你的储蓄账户。
Checks made out to the company are depositable.  开给公司的支票是可以存入银行的。
近义词:bankable
反义词:non-depositable
词性:adjective
