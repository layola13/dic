mussolinis:/ˌmʊsəˈliːniːz/ | n. | 墨索里尼们（指贝尼托·墨索里尼及其追随者或具有类似独裁风格的人）|  A term referring to Benito Mussolini, the Italian fascist dictator, and often used to describe people who share his authoritarian and nationalistic views, or similar dictatorial styles. |  History books often discuss the rise of the Mussolinis in Europe. 历史书经常讨论墨索里尼主义在欧洲的兴起。 The term "Mussolinis" can be used pejoratively to describe modern-day autocrats.  “墨索里尼们”这个词可以用来贬义地描述当代的独裁者。| 近义词: dictators, fascists, autocrats | 反义词: democrats, liberals | 词性: 名词

词源:  Derived from the surname of Benito Mussolini (1883-1945), the Italian dictator.  源自意大利独裁者贝尼托·墨索里尼（1883-1945）的姓氏。
