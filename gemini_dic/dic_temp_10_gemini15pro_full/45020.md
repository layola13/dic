cta:/ˈsiː tiː ˈeɪ/ | n. |  行动号召；行动呼吁 | Call to action. A piece of content intended to induce a viewer, reader, or listener to perform a specific act, typically taking the form of an instruction or directive (e.g. "Click here", "Subscribe now"). |  The website's CTA button is bright red and says "Buy Now".  该网站的行动号召按钮是亮红色的，上面写着“立即购买”。 | synonyms: prompt, invitation, solicitation | antonyms: deterrence, discouragement | noun
