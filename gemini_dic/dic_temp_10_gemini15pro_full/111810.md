logarithmetical:/ˌlɒɡərɪðˈmɪk(ə)l/ /ˌlɑːɡərɪðˈmɪk(ə)l/| adj.|对数的；用对数表示的|Relating to or expressed in terms of logarithms.|The slide rule is based on logarithmetical principles. 计算尺是基于对数原理的。|The engineer used a logarithmetical scale. 工程师使用了对数刻度。|近义词: logarithmic|反义词: linear, exponential|词性: adjective

logarithm:/ˈlɒɡərɪðəm/ /ˈlɑːɡərɪðəm/| n.|对数|A quantity representing the power to which a fixed number (the base) must be raised to produce a given number.|The logarithm of 100 to the base 10 is 2.  以10为底100的对数是2。|Students learn about logarithms in algebra class. 学生在代数课上学习对数。|近义词: exponent, power|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: noun
