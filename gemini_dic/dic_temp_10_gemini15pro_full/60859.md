eleemosynary: /ˌɛlɪˈmɒsɪnəri/ /ˌiːlɪˈmɒsɪˌnɛri/ | adj. | 慈善的；施舍的；依靠施舍的 | Relating to or dependent on charity; charitable.  | The eleemosynary institution provided food and shelter for the homeless.  这个慈善机构为无家可归者提供食物和住所。 |  The wealthy businessman made an eleemosynary contribution to the local hospital.  这位富有的商人向当地医院捐了一笔善款。 | 近义词: charitable, philanthropic, benevolent | 反义词: selfish, greedy, stingy | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin eleemosynarius, from Greek eleēmosunē ‘alms’, from eleos ‘pity’.
