mateless:/ˈmeɪtlɪs/| adj.|无伴侣的，无配偶的，独身的|Without a mate or partner; single, unpaired.|例句：The mateless bird sang a mournful song.  这只没有伴侣的鸟唱着一首悲伤的歌。|例句：Feeling mateless and lonely, she decided to join a dating app. 感到孤独无伴，她决定加入一个约会软件。|近义词：single, unmated, lone, solitary, partnerless|反义词：mated, paired, married, coupled|词性:形容词
Etymology: From mate + -less.  "Mate" ultimately comes from Old High German "gimazzo" meaning "companion."  The suffix "-less" indicates a lack or absence of something.
