delirifacient:/dɪˌlɪəriˈfeɪʃənt/
adjective
causing delirium; deliriant
A drug or substance that causes delirium.
The shaman used a delirifacient plant to induce visions.  萨满使用一种致谵妄的植物来引发幻觉。
Synonyms:  psychotomimetic, hallucinogenic, psychedelic
Antonyms:  sedative, tranquilizer
词性: adjective


delirifacient:/dɪˌlɪəriˈfeɪʃənt/
noun
A drug or substance that causes delirium.
Some delirifacients can be found in common household products. 一些常见的家用产品中也含有致谵妄物质。
Synonyms:  hallucinogen, psychedelic
Antonyms:  sedative, tranquilizer
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dēlīrifaciens*, present participle of *dēlīrifacere* "to make delirious," from *dēlīrus* "crazy, insane" + *facere* "to make."
