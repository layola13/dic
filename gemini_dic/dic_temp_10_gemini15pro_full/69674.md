fendered:/ˈfɛndəd/
adjective
有挡泥板的；装有护舷的
equipped or protected with fenders.
The boat was fendered to prevent damage to its hull. 这艘船装有护舷以防止船体损坏。
The car's fendered wheels protected it from mud and debris. 汽车装有挡泥板的轮子保护它免受泥浆和碎屑的侵害。
近义词:protected, shielded
反义词:unprotected, exposed
词性:adjective

词源:
From fender + -ed.  "Fender" itself comes from "defend" (via the now obsolete "defender"), ultimately from Latin "defendere".
