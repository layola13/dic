monophthongization:/ˌmɒnəfθɒŋɡaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌmɑːnəfθɑːŋɡaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |单元音化|The process by which a diphthong (a vowel sound that changes quality within a single syllable) becomes a monophthong (a single, unchanging vowel sound).|The Great Vowel Shift in English involved a significant amount of monophthongization.| 近义词: vowel simplification | 反义词: diphthongization | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From mono- (one) + Greek phthongos (sound) + -ization (forming nouns of process).
