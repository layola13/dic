all-embracing:/ɔːl-ɪmˈbreɪsɪŋ/
adjective
包罗万象的;无所不包的
Including everything or everyone; comprehensive.
例句：The book offers an all-embracing account of the artist's life and work. 这本书全面介绍了这位艺术家的生活和工作。

例句：The new policy is an all-embracing solution to the problem.  这项新政策是对这个问题的包罗万象的解决方案。

近义词：comprehensive, inclusive, all-inclusive, universal
反义词：limited, restricted, narrow, exclusive
词性: adjective
词源: From all + embracing (present participle of embrace, from Old French embracer, from en- 'in' + brace 'arms').
