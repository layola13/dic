hereditarily:/hɪˌredɪˈtɛrəli/ | adv. | 遗传地；世袭地 | In a way that is passed from parent to offspring through genes. |  Hemophilia is a sex-linked genetic disorder that is hereditarily transmitted. 血友病是一种遗传性疾病，通过基因遗传。

Her eye color is hereditarily determined. 她的眼睛颜色是由遗传决定的。 | 近义词：genetically, innately | 反义词：acquired, environmentally | 词性: 副词
