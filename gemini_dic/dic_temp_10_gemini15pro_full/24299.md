brighteners:/ˈbraɪtnərz/ | n. | 增白剂，光亮剂 | Substances added to materials to make them appear brighter or whiter by absorbing ultraviolet light and emitting visible blue light. |  The detergent contains optical brighteners to make clothes appear whiter.  这种洗涤剂含有荧光增白剂，使衣服看起来更白。 | synonyms:  whiteners, bleaching agents | antonyms: dulling agents, darkening agents | noun


brightener:/ˈbraɪtnər/ | n. | 增白剂，光亮剂 | A substance that brightens something. | The baker added a brightener to the icing to make it more vibrant. 面包师在糖霜中添加了一种增亮剂，使其更加鲜亮。 | synonyms: enhancer, improver | antonyms: duller, darkener | noun
