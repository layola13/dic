calypso:/kəˈlɪpsoʊ/ (US) /kəˈlɪpsəʊ/ (UK)| n.|卡里普索；西印度群岛的一种民歌|A type of West Indian (Caribbean) music in syncopated rhythm, typically with improvised lyrics on topical or satirical subjects.|
The band played a lively calypso that got everyone dancing. 乐队演奏了一首活泼的卡里普索，让每个人都跳起舞来。|
The steel drums created a distinctive calypso sound. 钢鼓创造了独特的卡里普索声音。|
Synonyms: Caribbean music, West Indian music, soca, reggae |Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but could consider contrasting genres like classical music, heavy metal) |noun
