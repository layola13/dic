nonaerated:/ˌnɒnˈeəreɪtɪd/
adjective
未充气的，不含气的
Not having air or carbon dioxide added.
The flat cola was nonaerated and tasted awful.  这瓶可乐没气了，尝起来很糟糕。
Near synonyms: flat, still, uncarbonated
Antonyms: aerated, carbonated, fizzy
词性: 形容词
