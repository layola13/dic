interfiltrating:/ɪnˌtərˈfɪltrɪtɪŋ/
| adj. |互相渗透的，互相渗入的|mutually infiltrating; permeating each other|The interfiltrating roots of the two trees made them difficult to separate. 两棵树互相渗透的根系使它们难以分开。|例句：Interfiltrating rivers created a complex network of waterways.  互相渗透的河流形成了一个复杂的水道网络。|近义词：interpenetrating, permeating, intermingling, interwoven|反义词：separate, distinct, isolated|词性:形容词

interfiltrate:/ˌɪntərˈfɪltreɪt/
| v. |互相渗透，互相渗入|(of two or more things) penetrate or permeate each other|The two cultures interfiltrated over the centuries.  两个文化在几个世纪里互相渗透。|例句：The armies interfiltrated each other's lines during the chaotic battle. 在混乱的战斗中，两军互相渗透到对方的防线。|近义词：interpenetrate, permeate, intermingle, infiltrate|反义词：separate, segregate, isolate|词性:动词

词源:
From inter- + filtrate.  "Inter-" signifies "between" or "among," and "filtrate" means to filter or pass through. Thus, "interfiltrate" describes the action of passing through or into each other.
