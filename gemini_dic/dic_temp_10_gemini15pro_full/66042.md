exarc:/ˈɛksɑːrk/ | n. | 总督；地方官；外省总督 | A governor of a province or district in the Byzantine Empire, especially one with extensive civil and military powers. |  The exarch governed a large territory and held considerable power.  这位总督管辖着大片领土，拥有相当大的权力。|  The exarch collected taxes and administered justice. 总督征税并执行司法。| synonym: governor, viceroy, prefect | antonym: subject, citizen | noun
