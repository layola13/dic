dowy:/ˈdaʊi/| adj.|露湿的，带露水的；像露珠的；新鲜的，朝气蓬勃的|Covered with or resembling dew; fresh and youthful.|The dewy grass glistened in the morning sun. 晨曦中，带露水的草地闪闪发光。|近义词：dewy, moist, fresh|反义词：dry, arid|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *dewy, from Old English dēawig (“dewy”), equivalent to dew +‎ -y. Cognate with Dutch dauwig (“dewy”), German tauig (“dewy”).
