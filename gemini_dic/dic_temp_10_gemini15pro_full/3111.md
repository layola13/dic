aerostatics:/ˌɛroʊˈstætɪks/| n. |空气静力学|The branch of physics that deals with the equilibrium of gaseous fluids and of solid bodies immersed in them.  It is concerned with the properties of gases at rest, especially with the buoyancy of bodies in air. |例句：Aerostatics explains how hot air balloons can float. 空气静力学解释了热气球是如何漂浮的。

例句：He studied aerostatics to understand the principles of lighter-than-air flight. 他学习空气静力学以了解轻于空气的飞行原理。|近义词：pneumatics|反义词:aerodynamics|词性:noun
