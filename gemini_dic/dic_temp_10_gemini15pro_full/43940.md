cri:/kriː/ | n. | 哭，喊叫 | A loud, sharp, high-pitched sound, especially one uttered by a bird or a baby.  | Example: The baby's cri pierced the silence. 婴儿的哭声划破了寂静。 | Synonym: cry, shriek, wail, scream | Antonym: whisper, murmur | noun


cri:/kriː/ | v. | 哭，喊叫 | To utter a loud, sharp, high-pitched sound, especially as an expression of pain, fear, or grief. | Example:  The seagulls cried overhead. 海鸥在头顶鸣叫。 | Synonym: cry, shriek, wail, scream | Antonym: whisper, murmur | verb


Etymology: From Old French *crier, from Latin quiritare "to shriek, scream," related to Quirites "Roman citizens" (whose appeal for help was a shout).
