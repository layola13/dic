crispy:/ˈkrɪspi/| adj.|酥脆的，脆的|Pleasantly firm and brittle.  Characterized by a pleasant crackling sound when bitten or crushed.|The fried chicken was perfectly crispy.|近义词：crunchy, brittle, flaky|反义词:soggy, chewy|词性:形容词
词源:From the Middle English *crisp*, from Old English *crips* ("curly, wrinkled, winding"), from Proto-Germanic *krispaz ("to curl, shrink, wrinkle"). Related to Old Norse *kriusp* ("brittle") and Dutch *krip* ("wrinkle").  The sense of "brittle and easily broken" developed later.
