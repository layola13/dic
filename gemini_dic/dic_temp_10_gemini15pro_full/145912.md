overdignifying: /ˌoʊvərˈdɪɡnɪfaɪɪŋ/
| adj. | 过分抬举的，过分尊重的 |  Attributing excessive dignity or importance to something or someone; treating something trivial as if it were grand or significant. |  He was overdignifying his minor role in the project, acting as if he were the CEO.  他过分抬举了他在项目中的次要角色，表现得好像他是首席执行官一样。 |  exaggerating, aggrandizing, overestimating, inflating |  underestimating, downplaying, belittling | adjective


overdignify: /ˌoʊvərˈdɪɡnɪfaɪ/
| vt. | 过分抬举，过分尊重 | To treat something or someone with more dignity or importance than is warranted. | She overdignified the situation by wearing a ball gown to the casual picnic. 她穿着舞会礼服去参加轻松的野餐，把场面搞得太隆重了。 |  exaggerate, aggrandize, overestimate, inflate | underestimate, downplay, belittle | verb
