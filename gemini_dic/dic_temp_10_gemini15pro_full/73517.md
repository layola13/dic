foreship:/ˈfɔːrʃɪp/ | n. | 前甲板，船头 | The foreship was badly damaged in the storm.  船头在暴风雨中严重受损。| The captain stood on the foreship, looking out at the sea. 船长站在船头，望着大海。 | 近义词: bow, prow | 反义词: stern, aftership | noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *foreschip, from Old English *for(e)scip (“fore-ship”), equivalent to fore- +‎ ship. Cognate with Middle Low German vōrschip, vōrskep, Dutch voorschip, German Vorschiff.
