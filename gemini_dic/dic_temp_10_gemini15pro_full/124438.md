motorist:/ˈməʊtərɪst/ (UK) /ˈmoʊtərɪst/ (US)| n. |汽车驾驶员；驾车旅行的人|A person who drives a car or other motor vehicle, especially regularly.|例句：The motorist was fined for speeding.  这位驾车者因超速行驶被罚款。

例句：Many motorists were stranded on the highway due to the snowstorm.  由于暴风雪，许多驾车者被困在高速公路上。

近义词：driver, car driver |反义词:pedestrian, cyclist|词性:noun
