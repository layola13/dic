kapok:/ˈkeɪpɒk/ (UK) /ˈkeɪpɑːk/ (US)| n.|木棉; 爪哇木棉的丝状纤维|A silky fibre obtained from the fruit of the ceiba tree, used for stuffing cushions, mattresses, etc. |The life jacket was filled with kapok.  救生衣里填充的是木棉。|Synonyms:  cotton, down, floss, silk cotton|Antonyms: N/A|noun
