noninstructiveness:/ˌnɒnɪnˈstrʌktɪvnəs/
noun
无益性，无启发性
Lack of providing instruction or useful information.
The noninstructiveness of the lecture left the students confused and bored. 这场讲座毫无启发性，让学生们感到困惑和无聊。
Synonyms: unhelpfulness, uselessness, futility
Antonyms: helpfulness, usefulness, instructiveness
