fossiliferous:/ˌfɒsɪˈlɪfərəs/ /ˌfɑːsɪˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
含化石的；多化石的
Containing fossils; rich in fossils.
The cliffs are fossiliferous, revealing numerous ancient marine creatures.  这些悬崖富含化石，展现了无数古代海洋生物。
The expedition discovered a fossiliferous limestone bed. 探险队发现了一个含化石的石灰岩层。

近义词: fossil-rich, fossil-bearing
反义词: unfossiliferous, barren


词性: adjective
