centage: /ˈsɛntɪdʒ/ | n. | 百分比，百分率 |  A rate, number, or amount in each hundred.  Derived by multiplying a fraction by 100 and adding the percent sign (%). |  The percentage of students passing the exam was 90%.  考试及格的学生比例为 90%。 | synonym: proportion, rate, ratio | antonym: whole, entirety | noun
