detacher:/dɪˈtætʃər/ | n. | 分离器，脱离器 |A device or mechanism that detaches something. | The detacher quickly separated the two parts of the machine.  分离器迅速地将机器的两部分分开。 | 近义词: separator, disconnector | 反义词: attacher, connector | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *détacher*, from *dé-* (prefix indicating separation) + *attacher* (to attach). Ultimately from Frankish *stakka* (stake).
