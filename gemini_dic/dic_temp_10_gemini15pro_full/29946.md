caveats:/ˈkæviˌæts/ (US), /ˈkæviæt/ (UK)| n.|警告，注意事项，附加说明|A caveat is a warning or proviso of specific stipulations, conditions, or limitations.|例句：He accepted the offer with a few caveats. 他接受了这个提议，但有一些附加条件。

例句：There are a few caveats to this approach.  这种方法有一些注意事项。

近义词：warnings, cautions, provisos, stipulations, conditions, restrictions|反义词: guarantees, assurances |词性: 名词 (Noun)
