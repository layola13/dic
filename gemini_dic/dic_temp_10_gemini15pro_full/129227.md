例句：She was a known noctambulist and her family had to take precautions to ensure her safety. 她是一个已知的梦游症患者，她的家人不得不采取预防措施以确保她的安全。|近义词：sleepwalker, somnambulist|反义词:|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *noctambulus*, from *noct-*, stem of *nox* (“night”) + *-ambulus* (akin to *ambulō* (“I walk”)).
