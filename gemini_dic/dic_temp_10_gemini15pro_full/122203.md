misuser:/ˌmɪsˈjuːzər/ | n. |误用者；滥用者|Someone who misuses something; someone who uses something incorrectly or for a wrong purpose. | The misuser of company funds was fired. 公司资金的滥用者被解雇了。|  The misuser of the software caused the system to crash.  这个软件的误用者导致系统崩溃。 |近义词：abuser, exploiter |反义词: proper user, responsible user | noun
