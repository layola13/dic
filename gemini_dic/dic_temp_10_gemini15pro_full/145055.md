overallegiances: /ˌoʊvərəˈliːdʒənsɪz/
n.
过度忠诚；过分效忠
Excessive loyalty or devotion, often to multiple entities, potentially causing conflict.
His overallegiances to both his family and his company created a difficult situation. 他对家庭和公司的过度忠诚造成了一个困境。

The athlete's overallegiances to various sponsors caused controversy.  这位运动员对多个赞助商的过分效忠引发了争议。

Near Synonyms:  excessive loyalty, undue devotion, conflicting loyalties
Antonyms: disloyalty, apathy, indifference
Word Type: Noun


Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "allegiance" (from Old French "alegeance," meaning "loyalty or obligation")
