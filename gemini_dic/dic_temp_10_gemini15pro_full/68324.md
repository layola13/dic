fall-back-on:/ˌfɔːl ˈbæk ɒn/ (British) /ˌfɔːl ˈbæk ɑːn/ (American)| phrasal verb|依靠，求助于，退而求其次|To rely on something or someone, especially in a difficult situation, or to use something as a last resort.|例句：If the business fails, he has his savings to fall back on. 如果生意失败了，他还有积蓄可以依靠。|近义词：resort to, rely on, depend on|反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider)  forge ahead, be independent|词性:Phrasal Verb
