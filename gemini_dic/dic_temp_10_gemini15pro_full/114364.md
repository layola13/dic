majorette:/ˌmeɪdʒəˈrɛt/ | n. | 女鼓乐队指挥，女乐队指挥 | A woman or girl who twirls a baton and leads a marching band. |  The majorettes led the parade with their dazzling batons. 女鼓乐队指挥们用她们耀眼的指挥棒引领着游行队伍。| synonyms: baton twirler, drum majorette | antonyms: N/A | noun

majorette:/ˌmeɪdʒəˈrɛt/ | n. | (非正式)女啦啦队长| (informal) A female cheerleader. | The majorettes cheered enthusiastically for the football team. 啦啦队员们热情地为足球队加油。 | synonyms: cheerleader, pom-pom girl | antonyms: N/A | noun
