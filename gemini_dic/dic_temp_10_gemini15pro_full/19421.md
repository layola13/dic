bickerer:/ˈbɪkərər/ | n. | 争吵者，爱吵架的人 | A person who bickers; someone who frequently engages in petty quarrels or arguments. |  The two children were known as the neighborhood bickerers, constantly arguing over toys. 这两个孩子是邻居中有名的争吵者，经常为玩具争吵。

My roommates are such bickerers; they argue about everything. 我的室友们真是爱吵架，他们什么都吵。| 近义词: arguer, quarreler, squabbler | 反义词: peacemaker, mediator | 词性: noun
