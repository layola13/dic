honky-tonk: /ˈhɒŋki tɒŋk/ (UK) /ˈhɑːŋki tɑːŋk/ (US)| n. adj.|喧闹的酒吧; 廉价酒吧; 乡村音乐; 喧闹的|A honky-tonk is a cheap, noisy bar, often with live music, typically country music. As an adjective, it describes something related to or characteristic of such a bar or its music.|

例句：
We spent the night dancing at a honky-tonk bar.
我们在一家喧闹的酒吧里跳了一晚上的舞。

The band played a honky-tonk version of the classic song.
乐队演奏了这首经典歌曲的乡村音乐版本。


近义词：bar, saloon, dive, club, roadhouse |反义词: upscale bar, sophisticated club |词性: noun, adjective
