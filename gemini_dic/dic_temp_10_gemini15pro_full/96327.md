indignant:/ɪnˈdɪɡnənt/| adj.|愤慨的，义愤填膺的|Feeling or showing anger or annoyance at what is perceived as unfair treatment.|例句：She was indignant at the suggestion that she had cheated. 她对关于她作弊的暗示感到愤慨。|例句：He became indignant when accused of lying. 当他被指责撒谎时，他变得很愤慨。|近义词：angry, annoyed, offended, resentful, furious|反义词：calm, pleased, content, satisfied|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin indignus (“unworthy”), from in- (“not”) + dignus (“worthy”).
