cofinanced:/ˌkoʊˈfaɪnænst/ | v. | 共同出资；联合融资 | financed together by two or more parties. | The project was cofinanced by the government and several private companies. 這個項目是由政府和幾家私營公司共同出資的。|
cofinanced:/ˌkoʊˈfaɪnænst/ | adj. | 共同出资的 | jointly financed. | The cofinanced project is expected to create hundreds of jobs. 这个共同出资的项目预计将创造数百个就业机会。| 近义词: jointly funded, jointly financed | 反义词: solely funded, independently financed | 词性: v., adj.
cofinanced: /ˌkoʊˈfaɪnænst/ | Etymology: co- (together) + financed (past participle of finance) |  Origin: From the prefix "co-" meaning "together" and the verb "finance". |
