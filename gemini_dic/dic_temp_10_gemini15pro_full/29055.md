carucage:/ˈkærəkɪdʒ/| n.|犁地税；耕地税|A medieval tax levied on each plough or ploughland.|The carucage was a significant source of revenue for the crown in the 12th and 13th centuries.  犁地税是12世纪和13世纪王室的重要收入来源。|Synonyms: land tax, property tax|Antonyms: tax exemption, tax credit|noun
