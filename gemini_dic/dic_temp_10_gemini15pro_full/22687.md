bordrag:/ˈbɔːrdrɑːɡ/ | n. |Contribution; levy; tax (especially in Scandinavian countries)| A payment, often mandatory, made to a fund or organization.  Primarily used in Scandinavian languages and contexts. |  The bordrag for healthcare is deducted directly from your salary.  医疗保健的税费直接从你的工资中扣除。|  levy, tax, contribution, duty, fee | exemption | noun
Etymology->From Old Norse *borðdráttr, from borð ("table") + dráttr ("drawing, pulling"). Originally referred to provisions brought to the table or a communal feast.
