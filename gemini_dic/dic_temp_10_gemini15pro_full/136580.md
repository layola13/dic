nonsalutation:/ˌnɒnˌsæljʊˈteɪʃən/
n.
非致敬; 不打招呼
Lack of a salutation or greeting.  The act of not offering a greeting.
He began his email with a nonsalutation, simply launching into the business at hand. 他没有寒暄，开头就直接切入正题。
Near Synonyms:  omission of greeting, lack of greeting,  absence of salutation, no greeting
Antonyms: salutation, greeting, acknowledgment
noun
