originalist:/əˈrɪdʒənəlɪst/| n.|原旨主义者；宪法原旨主义者|A person who believes that the meaning of a legal text, especially the Constitution, should be interpreted based on the original intentions of those who wrote it.|例句：The originalist argued that the Second Amendment protects only the right to bear arms in a well-regulated militia.  这位原旨主义者认为，第二修正案只保护在管理良好的民兵组织中持有武器的权利。

例句：Some originalists believe that the Constitution should be interpreted according to the understanding of the average person at the time it was written.  一些原旨主义者认为，宪法应该根据当时普通人的理解来解释。|近义词：textualist|反义词:living constitutionalist|词性:noun
