cosseting:/ˈkɒsətɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːsətɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|娇惯的，溺爱的|Treating someone with excessive indulgence and attention; pampering.|She resented her cosseting parents, who never let her make her own decisions. 她憎恨她溺爱的父母，他们从不让她自己做决定。|近义词：pampering, indulging, spoiling, coddling|反义词：neglecting, ignoring|词性:形容词

cosset:/ˈkɒsɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːsɪt/ (US)| vt.|宠爱，娇养|To treat someone with excessive indulgence and attention; to pamper.|She cosseted her youngest child. 她溺爱她最小的孩子。|近义词：pamper, indulge, spoil, coddle|反义词：neglect, ignore|词性:动词
