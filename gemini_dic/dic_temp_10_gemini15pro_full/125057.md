muley:/ˈmjuːli/ /ˈmʌli/| adj. n.|无角的，无角的动物（尤指鹿或牛）|Having no horns, or having lost them; especially of deer or cattle.  A hornless animal, especially a deer or cow.|The muley cow grazed peacefully in the pasture. 那无角的母牛在牧场上平静地吃草。|The hunter stalked a large muley buck. 猎人跟踪着一只大型无角雄鹿。|近义词：hornless, polled|反义词: horned|词性:形容词，名词
