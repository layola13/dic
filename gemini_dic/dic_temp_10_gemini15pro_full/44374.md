cross-cultural:/ˌkrɒsˈkʌltʃərəl/ /ˌkrɔːsˈkʌltʃərəl/| adj.|跨文化的|Relating to different cultures or comparison between them.|例句：Cross-cultural communication can be challenging. 跨文化交流可能具有挑战性。

例句：Cross-cultural understanding is essential in today's globalized world. 在当今全球化的世界中，跨文化理解至关重要。

近义词：intercultural, multicultural|反义词: monocultural|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "cross-" (meaning "across, between") + "cultural" (relating to culture).  "Cross-" comes from Old Norse "kross," ultimately from Latin "crux" (cross). "Cultural" comes from "culture," from Latin "cultura" (cultivation, care).
