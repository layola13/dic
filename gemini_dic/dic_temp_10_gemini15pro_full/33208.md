chronicle: /ˈkrɒnɪk(ə)l/ (US) /ˈkrɑːnɪk(ə)l/ (UK)| n. vt.| 编年史，记录；把…载入编年史|A chronicle is a record of historical events kept in chronological order.|Example: The book is a chronicle of the city's history. 这本书是这座城市的历史编年史。|Synonyms: record, history, annals, account|Antonyms: fiction, fabrication|Noun
