assistantly:/əˈsɪstəntlɪ/
adv.
辅助地，助理地
Acting in a supporting role; helping someone.
She worked assistantly to the project manager, taking notes and scheduling meetings. 她担任项目经理的助手，负责记录笔记和安排会议。
近义词: supportively, helpfully
反义词: independently, autonomously
词性: 副词

词源: assist (v.) + -ant (suffix forming nouns and adjectives) + -ly (suffix forming adverbs)
From Latin assistere "to stand by, attend, assist," from ad- "to" (see ad-) + sistere "to cause to stand," from PIE root *sta- "to stand, make or be firm."
