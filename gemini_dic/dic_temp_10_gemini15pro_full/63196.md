entasis:/ˈɛntəsɪs/| n.|柱身凸度，(柱身)收分|A slight convex curve in the shaft of a column, introduced to correct the optical illusion of concavity produced by a straight shaft.|The entasis of the columns gave the temple a sense of strength and stability.  柱子的收分使神庙显得雄伟稳固。|Near synonyms: swelling, curve, bulge|Near antonyms: concavity, hollowness|noun
