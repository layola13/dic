disbursement:/dɪsˈbɜːrsmənt/ | n. |支付，支出，拨款|The act of paying out money, especially from a fund set aside for a specific purpose.  |The government approved the disbursement of funds for the new bridge project. 政府批准了新桥梁项目的资金支出。|  The company makes regular disbursements to its shareholders. 公司定期向股东支付款项。 | synonyms: payment, expenditure, outlay | antonyms: receipt, income, deposit | noun

disburse:/dɪsˈbɜːrs/ | vt. |支付，支出，拨款|To pay out (money), especially from a fund. | The charity disbursed £1 million in aid last year.  去年该慈善机构支付了100万英镑的援助款。| The treasurer disbursed the funds for the project.  财务主管支付了该项目的资金。| synonyms: pay out, expend, distribute | antonyms: collect, receive, gather | verb
