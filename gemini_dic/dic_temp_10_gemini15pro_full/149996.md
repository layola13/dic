palmist:/ˈpɑːmɪst/ | n. | 手相家，掌纹家 | A person who claims to be able to tell your future by looking at the lines on your palm. |  I visited a palmist who told me I would travel the world. 我拜访了一位手相家，他告诉我我会环游世界。| fortune teller, soothsayer |  | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *palmiste*, from palme (“palm of the hand”) + -iste.  Ultimately from Latin palma ("palm tree, palm of the hand").
