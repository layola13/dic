metropole:/məˈtrɒpəlɪs/ (US) /ˈmetrəʊpəlɪs/ (UK)| n. |大都市；首府；母国|A major city, especially the chief city of a country, region, or state; the parent state of a colony. |例句：London is a bustling metropole. 伦敦是一座繁华的大都市。

例句：The metropole exerted considerable influence over its colonies. 母国对其殖民地施加了相当大的影响力。|近义词：megalopolis, metropolis, capital, city |反义词:province, countryside, rural area |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *métropole*, from Ancient Greek *μητρόπολις* (mētrópolis, “mother city; capital of a country”), from *μήτηρ* (mḗtēr, “mother”) + *πόλις* (pólis, “city”).
