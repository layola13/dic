exiled:/ˈeksaɪld/ | adj. v. | 流亡的，放逐的；被流放者 |  forced to live in a foreign country, especially for political reasons; a person who is exiled | The exiled king lived the rest of his life in Switzerland.  这位流亡的国王在瑞士度过了余生。 | synonyms: banished, deported, outcast | antonyms: repatriated, returned | adjective, verb

exile:/ˈeksaɪl/ | n. v. | 流放，放逐；被流放者；流亡 | the state of being barred from one's native country, typically for political or punitive reasons;  expel and bar (someone) from their native country, typically for political or punitive reasons;  a person who lives away from their native country, either from choice or compulsion |  He lived in exile for many years. 他流亡多年。 | synonyms: banishment, deportation, expulsion | antonyms: repatriation, return | noun, verb
