demobilised:/diːˈməʊbɪlaɪzd/
| adj. |复员的，退伍的|Discharged from military service.|The demobilised soldiers returned home to their families. 退伍士兵回到家中与家人团聚。|The demobilised soldier struggled to adapt to civilian life.  退伍士兵难以适应平民生活。|近义词：discharged, mustered out|反义词：mobilised, enlisted|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning 'reverse' or 'remove') + mobilise (meaning 'to prepare for war or other emergency').  The word "mobilise" comes from French "mobiliser," ultimately from Latin "mobilis" meaning "movable."
