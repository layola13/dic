massasauga:/ˌmæsəˈsɔːɡə/ | n. |响尾蛇,  巨蝮蛇的一种|A small, venomous rattlesnake ( *Sistrurus catenatus*) found in the eastern and midwestern United States and parts of Canada. It is characterized by its relatively small size and dark blotches on its back.|  The massasauga is a shy snake and rarely bites humans unless provoked. 巨蝮蛇是一种胆小的蛇，很少咬人，除非被激怒。|  Massasaugas are found in a variety of habitats, including prairies, marshes, and forests. 巨蝮蛇栖息在各种各样的栖息地，包括草原、沼泽和森林。| synonym: rattlesnake, pygmy rattlesnake, viper | antonym:  (no direct antonym for a specific snake species) | noun
