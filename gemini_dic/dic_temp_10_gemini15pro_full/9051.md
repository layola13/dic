antimedication:/ˌæntiˌmedɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
反对药物治疗；药物治疗反对论
opposition to the use of medication, especially psychiatric medication.
Some people hold an antimedication stance due to concerns about side effects.  有些人因为担心副作用而反对药物治疗。
The rise of the antimedication movement has raised concerns among medical professionals. 反药物治疗运动的兴起引起了医疗专业人士的担忧。
近义词: drug aversion, antipsychiatry
反义词: pro-medication, medication adherence
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
medication (noun): the administration of medication or the medication itself (from Latin medicatio)
