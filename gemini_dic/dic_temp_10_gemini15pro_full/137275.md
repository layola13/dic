nonsympathizing:/ˌnɒnsɪmpəθaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 缺乏同情心的，不同情的 | Lacking sympathy or showing no sympathy. |  He cast a nonsympathizing glance at the beggar. 他冷漠地瞥了乞丐一眼。

She remained nonsympathizing throughout his ordeal. 在他的苦难中，她始终无动于衷。

| 近义词：unsympathetic, uncaring, callous, indifferent, cold | 反义词：sympathetic, compassionate, caring, empathetic, warm | 词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + sympathizing (present participle of sympathize).  Sympathize comes from the Greek "sympatheia" meaning "fellow-feeling, community of feeling,"  from "sym-" (with, together) + "pathos" (feeling).
