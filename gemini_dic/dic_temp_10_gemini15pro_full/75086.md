frillers:/ˈfrɪlərz/| n.|褶边装饰，荷叶边，(尤指鸟类)有饰毛的鸟|People who wear or like frilly clothes, especially women.  Those who are fond of elaborate ornamentation, especially in dress.  Birds with ornamental plumage, especially around the neck.|She wore a dress with frillers at the neck and sleeves. 她穿着一件领口和袖口有荷叶边的连衣裙。|近义词：ruffles, flounces, ornaments, decorations|反义词:plainness, simplicity|词性:名词

词源：From the noun "frill" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting things having the specified feature or performing the specified action).  "Frill" comes from the Middle English "frillen" (to curl, ripple), probably of imitative origin.
