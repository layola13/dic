cantonalisms:/kænˈtɒnəlɪzəmz/ | n. | 地方主义，州主义 | The characteristic features of a canton, especially its dialect or customs.  A focus on the interests of a canton over those of a nation as a whole. | Switzerland is known for its cantonalisms, with each canton having its own unique traditions. 瑞士以其地方主义而闻名，每个州都有自己独特的传统。

The politician's speech was full of cantonalisms, appealing to local pride.  这位政客的演讲充满了地方主义色彩，以迎合当地人的自豪感。 | 近义词: regionalisms, provincialisms, localisms | 反义词: nationalism, centralism, universalism | 词性: 名词
