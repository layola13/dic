outfoxes:/ˌaʊtˈfɒksɪz/ /ˌaʊtˈfɑːksɪz/
verb
以智取胜，胜过，智胜
To outsmart someone; to be more cunning or clever than someone else.
The young chess player outfoxed his more experienced opponent.  年轻的棋手以智取胜了他更有经验的对手。
The spy outfoxed the guards and escaped. 间谍智胜了守卫，逃跑了。
近义词: outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver, circumvent
反义词: be outwitted, be outsmarted
