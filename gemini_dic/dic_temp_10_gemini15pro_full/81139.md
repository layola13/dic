gran:/ɡræn/ | n. | grandmother; granule | a child's word for grandmother; a small compact particle of a substance. |  My gran always bakes me cookies. 我的奶奶总是给我烤饼干。|The sand was composed of fine grans. 沙子是由细小的颗粒组成的。| synonyms: grandma, granny (for grandmother); particle, speck, grain (for granule) | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for gran.  Could consider "grandchild" as a generational opposite for the grandmother meaning) | noun
