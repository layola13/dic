ahriman:/ˈɑːrɪmən/| n. |阿里曼; 恶神; |In Zoroastrianism, Ahriman is the destructive spirit, the source of all evil in the universe. |例句：Ahriman is the antithesis of Ahura Mazda, the supreme god of good. 阿里曼是阿胡拉·马兹达（善的至高神）的对立面。

例句：The ancient Persians believed that Ahriman was responsible for all the suffering and misfortune in the world. 古代波斯人认为阿里曼是世界上所有苦难和不幸的根源。|近义词：devil, Satan, Angra Mainyu|反义词：Ahura Mazda, God|词性: 名词
