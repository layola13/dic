mechanised: /ˈmɛkənaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈmɛkəˌnaɪzd/ (US)| adj.|机械化的，机动的|Equipped with machinery, especially to replace human or animal labour.|例句：The farm was fully mechanised.  这个农场完全机械化了。

例句：The army has become increasingly mechanised in recent years. 近年来，军队越来越机械化了。

近义词：automated, motorized, mechanized|反义词: manual, unmechanised|词性:形容词
