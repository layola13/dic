entangled:/ɪnˈtæŋɡld/ | adj. | 缠绕的，纠缠的；复杂的，难解的 | Involved or twisted together in a complicated way; difficult to separate or understand. |  The kite string became entangled in the branches of the tree.  风筝线缠绕在树枝上。

| 近义词: tangled, intertwined, involved, complex | 反义词: disentangled, separated, simple, clear | 词性: 形容词

entangled:/ɪnˈtæŋɡld/ | v. |  使缠绕，使纠缠；使卷入，使陷入 |  Cause to become twisted together in a complicated way; involve (someone) in a difficult situation. | The ropes were entangled, making it difficult to untie them. 绳子缠在一起，很难解开。 | 近义词:  tangle, intertwine, involve, complicate | 反义词: disentangle, separate, simplify, clarify | 词性: 动词
