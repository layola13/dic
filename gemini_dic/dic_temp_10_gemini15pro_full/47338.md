de-shelling:/diːˈʃɛlɪŋ/ |n. |去壳，脱壳|The process of removing the outer shell or covering from something, such as nuts, seeds, or shellfish.|例句：The de-shelling of peanuts is done by a machine. 花生的去壳是由机器完成的。

例句：The de-shelling of the prawns was a messy but necessary task.  剥虾壳是一项凌乱但必要的任务。|近义词：husking, shelling, peeling|反义词：shelling, encasing|词性：名词 (derived from verb)
