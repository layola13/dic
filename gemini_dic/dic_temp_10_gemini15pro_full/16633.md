baroque's:/bəˈroʊks/| adj. n.|巴洛克式的，巴洛克风格的；巴洛克艺术(作品) |Belonging to or characteristic of the Baroque style of art and architecture; the Baroque style or period itself.  It's the possessive form of "baroque".|The palace's decor was undeniably baroque's, with its ornate carvings and gilded details. 这座宫殿的装饰无疑是巴洛克式的，有着华丽的雕刻和镀金的细节。|The baroque's grandeur is evident in the paintings of Rubens. 巴洛克的宏伟在鲁本斯的绘画中显而易见。| 近义词: ornate, elaborate, flamboyant | 反义词: simple, plain, austere |词性: 形容词，名词 (用作属性)
