mandolinists:/ˌmændəˈlɪnɪsts/
|n.|曼陀林演奏者|A person who plays the mandolin.|The mandolinists played a lively tune. 曼陀林演奏者们演奏了一首活泼的曲子。|Synonyms: mandolin player, musician|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider non-musician)|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *mandolino* (mandolin) + *-ist* (a suffix forming agent nouns).  *Mandolino* is a diminutive of *mandola*, from Greek *pandoura* (a three-stringed lute).
