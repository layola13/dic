conventical: /kənˈvɛntɪkəl/
adjective
秘密聚会的；非法集会的
A conventicle is a secret or illegal religious meeting.
例句：The authorities raided the conventicle and arrested several participants.  当局突袭了这个秘密宗教集会，逮捕了几名参与者。

例句：During times of religious persecution, conventicles offered a safe haven for worshippers. 在宗教迫害时期，秘密集会为信徒提供了一个安全的避风港。
近义词：assembly, gathering, meeting
反义词：official gathering, public meeting
词性：adjective


词源：From Late Latin *conventiculum, diminutive of Latin conventus (“meeting, assembly”).  源自晚期拉丁语 *conventiculum，是拉丁语 conventus（“会议，集会”）的缩小词。
