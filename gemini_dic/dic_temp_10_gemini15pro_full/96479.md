individuation:/ɪndɪˌvɪdʒuˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 个体化；个性化 | The process of becoming an individual; the process of differentiating oneself from others and developing a unique identity. |  Jung believed individuation is the central process of human development.  荣格认为个体化是人类发展的核心过程。


例句：
The individuation process is often challenging but ultimately rewarding.
个体化过程通常具有挑战性，但最终会有回报。


Her art reflects her journey of individuation and self-discovery.
她的艺术反映了她个体化和自我发现的旅程。


近义词：development, differentiation, self-realization, self-discovery
反义词：conformity, assimilation, standardization
词性：名词
