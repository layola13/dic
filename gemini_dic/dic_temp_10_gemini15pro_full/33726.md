circles: /ˈsɜːrkəlz/ (British) /ˈsɜːrkəlz/ (American)| v.|盘旋；环绕；包围；|To move in a circle, especially in the air. |A hawk circled high above. 一只鹰在高空盘旋。|synonyms: revolve around, rotate around, orbit, encircle, surround, encompass |antonyms: straighten, unwind| verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French cercle, from Latin circulus, diminutive of circus (“ring, circular racecourse”).
