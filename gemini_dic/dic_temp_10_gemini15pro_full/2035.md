acuition: /ˌækjuˈɪʃən/
noun
获得,习得,收购
The acquisition of language is a complex process. 语言的习得是一个复杂的过程。
The museum announced its recent acquisition of a rare painting.  博物馆宣布最近获得了一幅珍贵的画作。
synonyms: attainment, procurement, purchase, gaining
antonyms: loss, disposal, sale
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *acquisitio, from Latin acquīsīre "to acquire," from ad- "to" + quaerere "to seek, gain."

USAGE->
The company's latest acquisition is a small software firm. = 这家公司最近的收购是一家小型软件公司。

The acquisition of new skills is essential for career advancement. = 获取新技能对于职业发展至关重要。

Her acquisition of a second language opened up new opportunities. = 她学会了第二语言，这为她开辟了新的机会。

The museum is proud of its recent acquisition of a rare artifact. =  博物馆为最近获得一件稀有文物而感到自豪。

PHRASE->
the acquisition of knowledge = 知识的获取
