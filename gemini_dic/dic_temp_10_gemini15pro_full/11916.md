arschin:/ɑːrˈʃɪn/ | n. |俄尺，阿尔申（俄罗斯的长度单位，等于0.71米）|A Russian unit of linear measure equal to 28 inches (0.71 meter).|The fabric was sold by the arschin.  这种布是按俄尺出售的。|The carpet was three arschins long. 这块地毯有三俄尺长。|近义词:cubit, ell (旧制长度单位)|反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑 millimeter, centimeter 等更小单位)|词性:noun
Etymology->
From Russian аршин (aršin), from Turkish arşın, ultimately from Persian ارش (arš, “cubit”).
