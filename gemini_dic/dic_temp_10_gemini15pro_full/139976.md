odoriferous:/ˌoʊdəˈrɪfərəs/
adjective
有气味的；散发气味的
Having a strong or distinctive odor, especially an unpleasant one.
The odoriferous durian filled the air with its pungent aroma.  榴莲浓烈的气味弥漫在空气中。
The locker room was odoriferous after the game.  比赛结束后，更衣室里气味难闻。
fragrant, aromatic, pungent, smelly
odorless, scentless
adjective
