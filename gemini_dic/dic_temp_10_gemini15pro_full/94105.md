impeachers:/ɪmˈpiːtʃərz/ | n. | 弹劾者；检举者；控告者 |  A person who impeaches someone, especially a public official.  This refers to bringing formal charges against a government official. | The impeachers presented compelling evidence against the president. 弹劾者提交了针对总统的有力证据。| accusers, prosecutors | defenders, supporters | noun
