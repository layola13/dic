nonrecurrent:/ˌnɒn rɪˈkɜːrənt/ /ˌnɑːn rɪˈkɜːrənt/
adjective
非重复发生的，不复发的
Not occurring again or repeatedly.
This is a nonrecurrent cost. 这是一笔非经常性开支。
The doctor assured him that the pain was nonrecurrent. 医生向他保证疼痛不会复发。
近义词:nonrecurring, single, one-time, isolated
反义词:recurrent, recurring, repeated, frequent, chronic
词性:adjective
