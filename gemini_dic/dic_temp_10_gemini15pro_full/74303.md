fractioning:/ˈfrækʃənɪŋ/
noun
分馏，分级，分步分离
The process of separating a mixture into fractions by a physical process such as distillation or crystallization.
Crude oil undergoes fractioning to separate it into different components like gasoline and kerosene. 原油经过分馏将其分离成汽油和煤油等不同成分。
fractionation, separation, dividing
combination, unification
n.
