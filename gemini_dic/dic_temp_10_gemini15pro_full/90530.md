huffed:/hʌft/
|v.|生气地说；气呼呼地说；吹嘘|to say something in an annoyed or offended way, often because you feel you have been treated unfairly; to breathe loudly and in an annoyed way because you are tired or have been doing something strenuous|She huffed and puffed as she climbed the stairs. 她气喘吁吁地爬上楼梯。|"I never get to choose," he huffed. “我从来没机会选择，”他气呼呼地说。|近义词：snorted, puffed, complained, grumbled|反义词：accepted, agreed|词性: 动词

huffed:/hʌft/
|adj.|气恼的；恼火的|annoyed or offended|She gave a huffed reply and stormed off. 她恼怒地回答了一声，然后怒气冲冲地走开了。|近义词：annoyed, offended, irritated, indignant|反义词：pleased, happy|词性: 形容词
