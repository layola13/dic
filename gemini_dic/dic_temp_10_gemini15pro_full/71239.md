fitting:/ˈfɪtɪŋ/ (US), /ˈfɪtɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. n.|合适的，恰当的；装配，配件|Something that is fitting is suitable or appropriate. A fitting is a small part that you connect to something.|The dress was a fitting tribute to her elegance. 这件衣服是对她优雅气质的恰当体现。|近义词：appropriate, suitable, proper, apt, relevant, apposite, meet, befitting, convenient, right, correct, apt, applicable, pertinent, congruous, fit, commensurate|反义词：inappropriate, unsuitable, improper, unfitting, unbecoming|词性:形容词

fitting:/ˈfɪtɪŋ/ (US), /ˈfɪtɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|装配，安装；试衣；配件|The act of putting something together, or a small part that is used to connect things.|The fitting of the new kitchen will take two days. 新厨房的安装将需要两天时间。|近义词：installation, assembly, fixture, attachment, connection|反义词：removal, dismantling|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "fitt" (a division, section, canto), from Proto-Germanic "*fitiz" (a web, something woven), related to Old Norse "fitja" (to knit together). The sense of "suitable" developed later, likely from the idea of something being "woven" or "put together" properly.
