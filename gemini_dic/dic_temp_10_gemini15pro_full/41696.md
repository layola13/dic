corrigibility:/ˌkɔːrɪdʒɪˈbɪləti/
noun
可纠正性，可改正性
The quality of being corrigible; capable of being corrected, amended, or reformed.
His corrigibility and willingness to learn made him a good student.  他的可教性和学习意愿使他成为一名好学生。
correctability, amendability, reformability
incorrigibility, obstinacy
n.
