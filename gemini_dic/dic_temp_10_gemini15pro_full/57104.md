doulocracy:/duːˈlɒkrəsi/
n.
奴隶统治；奴隶政体
A government or social system controlled by slaves.
The historian described the unusual doulocracy that existed briefly on the island.  这位历史学家描述了岛上短暂存在的不同寻常的奴隶统治。
The doulocracy was short-lived, overthrown within a year. 这个奴隶政权很短命，一年之内就被推翻了。
Synonyms: slaveocracy
Antonyms: democracy, republic
