descrying:/dɪˈskraɪɪŋ/
verb
发现；看出；辨别出
Catching sight of, especially something distant or obscure.
From a distance, she descried two figures walking towards her. 从远处，她看到两个人影朝她走来。
synonyms: spotting, perceiving, discerning, noticing, observing
antonyms: overlooking, missing, ignoring
