on-balance:/ˌɒn ˈbæləns/ /ˌɑːn ˈbæləns/| adv. adj.|总的来说，总而言之|Considering all aspects or factors; overall.|On balance, the decision was a good one. 总的来说，这个决定是正确的。|近义词：overall, all things considered, taking everything into account|反义词：partially, in some respects|词性: 副词, 形容词

词源:  From the accounting term "balance," referring to the final sum or overall state of an account.  The phrase "on balance" extends this meaning to a more general assessment of a situation.
