amphopeptone:/ˌæmfoʊˈpɛptoʊn/
n.
两性胨
A mixture of peptides derived from the partial hydrolysis of animal protein, containing both acidic and basic groups, used in culture media for microorganisms.
Amphopeptone is a common ingredient in bacterial growth media. 两性胨是细菌生长培养基中的常见成分。
Amphopeptone provides a source of nitrogen for microbial growth. 两性胨为微生物生长提供氮源。
近义词:peptone, protein hydrolysate

词性:noun
