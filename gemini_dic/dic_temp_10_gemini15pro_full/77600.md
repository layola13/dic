geekinesses:/ˈɡiːkɪnəsɪz/
noun
极客行为；书呆子气；怪癖
The quality or state of being geeky; multiple instances of geekiness.  A collection of geeky traits or behaviors.
He embraced his geekinesses, proudly displaying his collection of vintage comics. 他欣然接受自己的怪癖，自豪地展示他收藏的复古漫画。
synonyms: eccentricities, quirks, idiosyncrasies, peculiarities
antonyms: normalcy, conventionality
