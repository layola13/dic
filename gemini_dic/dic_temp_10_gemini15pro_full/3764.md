agitated:/ˈædʒɪteɪtɪd/| adj.|激动的，焦虑的，不安的|Feeling or appearing troubled or nervous.|She was visibly agitated by the news. 她明显被这个消息弄得很不安。|近义词：disturbed, upset, anxious, restless, nervous, frantic, flustered|反义词：calm, composed, tranquil, serene|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *agitatus*, past participle of *agitare* "to set in motion, drive, stir up," frequentative of *agere* "to drive, lead, act, do."
