cogging:/ˈkɒɡɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːɡɪŋ/ (US)| n. v.|齿槽效应；装齿轮；作弊；欺骗|The act of furnishing with cogs, or teeth; hence, the state of being cogged.  Also, cheating or manipulating something, often a game of chance. |The old clock needed cogging to work properly.  这台旧钟需要装上齿轮才能正常工作。

The gambler was caught cogging the dice.  这个赌徒被发现操控骰子作弊。|近义词：cheating, manipulating, rigging, gearing|反义词：fair play, honesty|noun, verb
