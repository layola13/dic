listlessnesses:/ˈlɪstləsnəsɪz/
n.
无精打采；倦怠；冷漠
A state of lacking energy and enthusiasm.
The long illness resulted in a period of listlessnesses.  长期疾病导致了一段时间的无精打采。
He showed a marked listlessness in his work. 他在工作中表现出明显的倦怠。

近义词:lethargy, apathy, inertia, languor, lassitude
反义词:energy, enthusiasm, vigor, liveliness, animation
词性: 名词(plural)
