immaterial:/ˌɪməˈtɪəriəl/
adjective
无关紧要的；非物质的；精神上的
Not important or relevant; not made of matter; spiritual.
例句：The cost is immaterial if it saves lives.  如果能挽救生命，成本就无关紧要。

例句：His personal feelings are immaterial to his job performance. 他的个人感受与他的工作表现无关。

近义词：insignificant, irrelevant, unimportant, inconsequential, negligible, trifling, trivial
反义词：important, relevant, significant, material, substantial, essential
词性: adjective
