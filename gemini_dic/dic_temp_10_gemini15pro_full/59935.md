edgier:/ˈɛdʒiər/
adjective
更前卫的，更尖锐的，更急躁的
More unconventional, daring, or irritable than previously; more intense or provocative.
Her new music is edgier than her previous work. 她的新音乐比她以前的作品更前卫。
synonyms: sharper, more intense, more provocative, more daring, more unconventional, more cutting-edge, riskier
antonyms: milder, softer, tamer, more conventional, more traditional
词性: 形容词
