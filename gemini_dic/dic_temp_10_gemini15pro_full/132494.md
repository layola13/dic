nondesignate:/nɒnˈdɛzɪɡneɪt/
adjective
非指定的，未指定的
Not specifically designated or appointed to a particular role or purpose.
The nondesignate hitter batted first. 非指定的击球手第一个击球。
The meeting will be held in a nondesignate location. 会议将在一个未指定的地点举行。
近义词: undesignated, unspecified, unappointed
反义词: designated, appointed, specified
词性: adjective

词源: non- + designate.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "designate" comes from the Latin word "designare," meaning "to mark out, indicate, choose, appoint."
