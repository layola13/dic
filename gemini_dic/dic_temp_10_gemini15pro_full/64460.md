eristical:/ˌɛrˈɪstɪkəl/
adjective
争论的，诡辩的
Prone to controversy; disputatious, argumentative, often with the intention of winning the argument rather than finding the truth.
Her eristical nature made it difficult to have a productive conversation. 她好争辩的性格使人难以和她进行富有成效的对话。
近义词:argumentative, contentious, disputatious, polemical
反义词:conciliatory, agreeable, amicable, cooperative
词性:adjective
词源:From Greek *eris* "strife" + -ikos, adjectival suffix. Related to *erizein* "to contend, wrangle".
