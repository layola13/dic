over-charities:/ˈoʊvərˈtʃærɪtiz/ | n. | 过度慈善，过度捐赠 (通常指捐赠过多或捐赠给不值得的机构) |  The act of giving excessively to charities, often to the point of detriment to oneself or to more deserving causes.  It can also refer to a situation where there are too many charities operating in a particular area or field. |  His over-charities left him with little money for his own family. 他的过度慈善让他自己的家庭所剩无几。

His over-charities were directed towards animal shelters, neglecting other important causes. 他的过度慈善都给了动物收容所，忽略了其他重要事业。 | 近义词: excessive philanthropy, over-giving | 反义词: underfunding, neglect | 词性: 名词
