glowed:/ɡloʊd/| v.|发光，发出光辉；容光焕发|The embers glowed in the fireplace. 余烬在壁炉里发出暗淡的光。|近义词：shone, beamed, radiated|反义词:darkened, dimmed|verb

Etymology: From Middle English *glowen, from Old English glōwan (“to glow”), from Proto-Germanic *glōaną (“to glow”). Cognate with Dutch gloeien (“to glow”), German glühen (“to glow”), Old Norse glóa (“to glow”).
