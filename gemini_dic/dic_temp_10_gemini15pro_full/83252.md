gymnoblastic:/ˌdʒɪmnəˈblæstɪk/| adj.|裸芽的，裸生殖的|Having a hydroid colony in which the polyps are not protected by a chitinous or horny sheath.|Gymnoblastic hydroids are common in shallow marine environments. 裸芽水螅常见于浅海环境。|近义词: athecate |反义词: calyptoblastic|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Greek *gymnos* (naked) + *blastos* (bud, sprout)
