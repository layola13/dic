hangability:/ˌhæŋəˈbɪləti/
n.
可绞杀性，可被吊死的状态
The hangability of the rope was questioned. 绳子的可绞杀性受到了质疑。
The judge determined the hangability of the convicted murderer. 法官确定了被判有罪的凶手的可绞刑性。
n.
可悬挂性
The hangability of the painting depended on its weight and the strength of the hook. 这幅画的可悬挂性取决于它的重量和挂钩的强度。
The engineer tested the hangability of the new lighting fixture. 工程师测试了新灯具的可悬挂性。
近义词:  susceptibility to hanging (针对人), suitability for hanging (针对物体)
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider) unsuitability for hanging, resistance to hanging
词性: noun
