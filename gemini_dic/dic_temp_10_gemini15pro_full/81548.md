grayest:/ˈɡreɪɪst/| adj.|最阴郁的，最沉闷的|Figuratively, most depressing or gloomy.|It was the grayest day of the year, both literally and figuratively.  这是今年最阴沉的一天，字面上和比喻上都是。|Synonyms: dreariest, most depressing, most somber|Antonyms: happiest, most cheerful, most joyful|adjective
