gildale: /ˈɡɪldeɪl/ | n. | 行会; 同业公会; 协会; 互助会;  |A guildale is an association of people for mutual aid or the pursuit of a common goal.  |The merchants formed a gildale to protect their interests. 商人们组成了一个行会来保护他们的利益。| synonym: guild, association, society, union | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "individual," "independent operator" in certain contexts) | noun

etymology: Middle English *gildale*, from *gild* (guild) + *ale* (alehouse, meeting place).  The word originally referred to a feast or social gathering held by a guild, but later came to mean the guild itself. | etymology
