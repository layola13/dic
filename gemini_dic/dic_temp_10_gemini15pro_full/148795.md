overvaluation:/ˌoʊvərˌvæljuˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 高估，估值过高 | An excessively high estimation of worth or value. | The overvaluation of the company's stock led to a sharp decline after the earnings report. 公司股票的估值过高导致盈利报告发布后股价暴跌。|Synonyms: inflation, exaggeration, overestimate, overpricing | Antonyms: undervaluation, underestimate | noun

overvalue:/ˌoʊvərˈvæljuː/ | vt. | 高估，过高评价 | To estimate the value of something too highly. |  Many people overvalue the importance of material possessions. 很多人高估了物质财富的重要性。 | Synonyms: overrate, overestimate | Antonyms: undervalue, underestimate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + valuation. "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Valuation" comes from the Late Latin word "valuatio," meaning "estimation of worth."
