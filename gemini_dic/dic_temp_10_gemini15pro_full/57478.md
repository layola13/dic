draftsmanship:/ˈdræftsmənʃɪp/
n.
制图术;绘图技巧;草拟(物)的技巧
The skill of making drawings, especially technical drawings, or the quality of a drawing.
The architect's draftsmanship was impeccable, capturing every detail with precision.  这位建筑师的绘图技巧无可挑剔，精确地捕捉到了每一个细节。
His draftsmanship is second to none. 他的绘图技巧首屈一指。
近义词:drawing, sketching, rendering
反义词:N/A
词性:noun

词源: From draftsman +‎ -ship.  来自 draftsman（绘图员） + -ship （名词后缀，表示技巧、状态）。
