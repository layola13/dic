axon:/ˈæksɒn/ (UK) /ˈæksɑːn/ (US)| n. |轴突|A long, slender projection of a nerve cell that typically conducts electrical impulses away from the neuron's cell body.|The axon transmits signals to other neurons, muscles, or glands.|
The axon of this neuron is unusually long.  这个神经元的轴突异常的长。

The signal travels down the axon to the synapse. 信号沿着轴突向下传送到突触。

|近义词:nerve fiber|反义词:dendrite|词性:noun
