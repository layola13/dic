delirium's:/dɪˈlɪriəmz/
n.|谵妄，精神错乱|A state of mental confusion and excitement characterized by disorientation, hallucinations, and incoherence.  It is often caused by illness, fever, or drug withdrawal.|The patient's delirium's made it difficult for him to communicate. 病人的谵妄使他难以沟通。|近义词:confusion, madness, insanity|反义词: lucidity, sanity|词性:名词 (所有格)


词源:
From Late Latin *dēlīrium, from Latin dēlīrāre (“to go off the furrow, be crazy”), from dē (“off, away from”) + līra (“furrow, ridge between furrows”).
