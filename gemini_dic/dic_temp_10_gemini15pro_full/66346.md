excurse:/ɪkˈskɜːrs/ /ɪkˈskɜːrz/| vi. n.| 离题；远足； экскурс (俄语，意为“digression”, “excursus”)|To deviate from the main subject in speech or writing; to make a digression.  A short journey or trip, especially one taken for pleasure.  A detailed discussion of a particular point in a book, etc.|例句：The professor was known for his fascinating, if sometimes lengthy, excurses on obscure historical events.  教授以其引人入胜的，尽管有时冗长的、关于晦涩历史事件的离题而闻名。

例句：We took a pleasant excursus into the countryside. 我们到乡下进行了一次愉快的短途旅行。|近义词：digress, deviate, ramble, wander, excursion, trip, outing|反义词:focus, concentrate|词性:verb, noun
