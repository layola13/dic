ornately:/ɔːrˈneɪtli/ | adv. |华丽地，装饰精美地 | In a highly decorated and elaborate manner; with rich ornamentation. | The palace was ornately decorated with gold and precious jewels. 宫殿用黄金和珍贵的珠宝装饰得富丽堂皇。 | The actress wore an ornately embroidered gown.  这位女演员穿着一件绣工精美的礼服。 | 近义词：elaborately, lavishly, richly | 反义词：simply, plainly, austerely | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Late Latin *ornatus, from Latin ornare (“to adorn, equip, furnish, embellish”) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  最终源自 Proto-Indo-European *hor- (“to desire, excite”).
