incage:/ɪnˈkeɪdʒ/
verb
关入笼中；囚禁
To confine in or as if in a cage.
The captured bird was incaged.  这只被捕获的鸟被关进了笼子。
Synonyms: imprison, confine, restrain
Antonyms: liberate, free, release
词性: 动词
