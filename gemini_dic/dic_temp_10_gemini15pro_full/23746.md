bravadoes:/brəˈvɑːdoʊz/
noun
虚张声势，逞能
A show of boldness intended to impress or intimidate.
He masked his insecurity with a false bravado. 他用虚假的逞强来掩饰自己的不安全感。

bravado:/brəˈvɑːdoʊ/
noun
虚张声势，逞能
A show of boldness intended to impress or intimidate.
Despite his bravado, I could tell he was nervous. 尽管他装作勇敢，但我看得出他很紧张。

近义词: swagger, bluster, braggadocio, ostentation
反义词: timidity, fear, apprehension, modesty
词性: noun
