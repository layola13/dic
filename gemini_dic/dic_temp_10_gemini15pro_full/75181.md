frizzed:/frɪzd/
adjective
卷曲的，毛躁的
formed small, tight curls, usually by heat or dryness.
Her hair frizzed in the humidity. 她的头发在潮湿的空气中变得卷曲毛躁。
synonyms:curly, kinky, fuzzy, bushy
antonyms:straight, smooth, sleek
词性:形容词


frizz:/frɪz/
verb
使卷曲，使毛躁
to form small, tight curls, usually by heat or dryness.
The heat frizzed her hair.  高温使她的头发卷曲毛躁。
synonyms:curl, kink, crinkle
antonyms:straighten, smooth
词性:动词


frizz:/frɪz/
noun
卷曲，毛躁
small, tight curls in hair, usually caused by heat or dryness.
She tried to tame the frizz in her hair. 她试图抚平头发上的毛躁。
synonyms:curl, kink, crinkle
antonyms:straightness, smoothness
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the earlier word "frizzle," meaning to fry or scorch, suggesting the appearance of hair damaged by heat.  Ultimately from Old French "fresir," meaning to roast or fry.
