columbary: /ˈkɒləmbəri/ (UK) /ˈkɑːləmbɛri/ (US)| n. |鸽舍，鸽房|A structure, especially a tower, with compartments for pigeons to nest in.  A dovecote.|The old manor house had a columbary in the garden, a testament to the estate's history.|Synonyms: dovecote, pigeon house|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps aviary for birds in general) |noun


columbary: /ˈkɒləmbəri/ (UK) /ˈkɑːləmbɛri/ (US)| adj. |(rare) of or relating to pigeons or doves|Relating to, or resembling, a dove or pigeon.|Its columbary plumage shimmered in the sunlight. (This usage is quite rare.)|Synonyms: dove-like, pigeon-like|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym) |adjective
