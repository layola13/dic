overlabored:/ˌoʊvərˈleɪbərd/| adj.|过度劳累的；（文体）过分雕琢的；不自然的|Worked on or elaborated too much; showing signs of excessive effort and lacking spontaneity.|The prose was dense and overlabored, making it a chore to read. 这篇文章的散文写得又浓又累，读起来很费劲。|His overlabored explanation only confused the students further. 他过分费力的解释反而让学生们更加困惑了。|近义词：strained, forced, contrived, artificial, labored, overworked|反义词：natural, effortless, spontaneous, simple|词性:形容词
词源:  From over- + labored, past participle of labor ("to work").  Essentially meaning "worked on too much".
