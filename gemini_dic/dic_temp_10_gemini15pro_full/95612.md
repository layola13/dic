incommutable:/ˌɪnkəˈmjuːtəbl/ | adj. | 不可交换的，不可替代的 | Not capable of being commuted or exchanged; unchangeable. |  The judge imposed an incommutable sentence of life imprisonment. 法官判处了不可减刑的无期徒刑。| Synonyms: unchangeable, irreversible, fixed, unalterable | Antonyms: changeable, reversible, alterable, flexible | Adjective

incommutable:/ˌɪnkəˈmjuːtəbl/ | adj. | (指事物)不能转换的 | Not capable of being changed into something else. | This form of energy is incommutable into any other form.  这种能量无法转换成任何其他形式。| Synonyms: untransformable, inconvertible | Antonyms: transformable, convertible | Adjective
