germlike:/ˈdʒɜːrmlaɪk/| adj.|像细菌的；似病菌的|Resembling or characteristic of a germ; carrying or likely to carry germs.  Likely to cause disease.|The damp, dark room had a distinctly germlike smell.  潮湿阴暗的房间里有一股明显的像细菌的味道。|Synonyms: germy, infectious, contagious, unhygienic, insalubrious|Antonyms: sterile, hygienic, clean, sanitary|adjective
