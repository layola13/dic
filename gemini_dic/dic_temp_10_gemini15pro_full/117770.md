melchite:/ˈmɛlkaɪt/| n. adj.|梅尔凯特教徒，梅尔凯特派的|A member of an Eastern Christian church, especially of the Melkite Greek Catholic Church, that is in communion with Rome and uses a Byzantine liturgy.|例句：The Melchite community maintains its rich traditions. 梅尔凯特社区保持着其丰富的传统。|例句：The Melchite priest celebrated the Divine Liturgy. 梅尔凯特神父举行了神圣礼仪。|近义词：Greek Catholic, Byzantine Catholic|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑其他基督教派别，例如：Protestant, Orthodox)|词性：名词，形容词
