arachnological: /əˌræknəˈlɒdʒɪk(ə)l/ /əˌræknəˈlɑːdʒɪk(ə)l/
adj. |蜘蛛学的；蜘蛛学上的|Relating to the study of arachnids (spiders, scorpions, mites, etc.). |She had an arachnological question about the type of spider she found in her garden. 她有一个关于她在花园里发现的蜘蛛类型的蜘蛛学问题。| The museum's arachnological collection is one of the largest in the world.  这家博物馆的蜘蛛学藏品是世界上最大的藏品之一。|近义词:  spider-related |反义词: N/A |词性: adjective

arachnology: /əˌræknɒlədʒi/ /əˌræknɑːlədʒi/
n. |蜘蛛学|The scientific study of arachnids. |He specialized in arachnology, fascinated by the diversity of spiders. 他专攻蜘蛛学，对蜘蛛的多样性着迷。| Arachnology is a branch of zoology. 蜘蛛学是动物学的一个分支。|近义词:  study of arachnids|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
