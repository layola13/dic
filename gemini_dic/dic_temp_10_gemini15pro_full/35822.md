cocking:/ˈkɒkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkɪŋ/ (US)| adj. | 翘起的; 倾斜的 |Tilted or turned upwards. |He gave her a cocking glance. 他斜了她一眼。|近义词:tilted, slanted, inclined|反义词:level, horizontal|词性: adjective

词源:  From the verb "cock," which originally meant "to raise" or "to set upright."  Ultimately from Middle Dutch *kokken "to crow" (of a rooster), referring to the raising of its comb.
