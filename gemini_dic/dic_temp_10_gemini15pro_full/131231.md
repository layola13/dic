noncapitalist:/ˌnɒnˈkæpɪtəlɪst/
adjective
非资本主义的
Not of, relating to, or practicing capitalism.  Characterized by a system other than that of capitalism.
The commune experimented with a noncapitalist economic model.  这个公社尝试了一种非资本主义的经济模式。
The author presented a noncapitalist critique of the global economy. 作者提出了一个非资本主义的全球经济批判。
Synonyms: socialist, communist, Marxist, collectivist
Antonyms: capitalist
