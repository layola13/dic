bergschrund:/ˈbɛərɡʃrʊnd/ /ˈbɜːrɡʃrʊnd/
|n.|冰斗裂缝，冰川裂隙|A large crevasse that forms at the head of a glacier where the moving ice separates from the stationary ice or firn above.|The climbers cautiously navigated the bergschrund.|Synonyms: crevasse, chasm, fissure|Antonyms: closure, bridge (in the context of a crevasse)|noun
