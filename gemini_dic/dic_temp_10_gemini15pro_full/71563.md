flashed:/flæʃt/ | v. | 闪光，闪现；突然出现；（使）快速移动；闪现，掠过 |  A light flashed in the distance. 远处闪过一道光。 |  shine, gleam, glitter | fade, dim | verb
flashed:/flæʃt/ | adj. | 闪光的，闪现的 |  a flashed image  闪现的图像 |  bright, shining, gleaming | dull, dim | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *flashen,  frequentative of  flasken  (“to splash, dash”),  from Old Norse  *flaska  (“to splash, dash”).  Cognate with  Swedish  flaska  (“to gush, spout”),  Danish  flaske  (“bottle”), and  Icelandic  flaska  (“bottle, flask”). The noun is derived from the verb.
