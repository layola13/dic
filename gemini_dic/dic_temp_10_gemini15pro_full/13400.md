atomises:/ˈætəmaɪzɪz/
verb
使…雾化; 使…原子化; 将…分裂成原子
To reduce to atoms or to an extremely fine spray or powder; to divide into minute particles or fragments.
The perfume atomises into a fine mist. 香水雾化成细小的薄雾。
Synonyms: vaporize, spray, pulverize, disintegrate
Antonyms: condense, solidify, combine, unite
词性: 动词
