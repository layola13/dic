embezzler:/ɪmˈbezlər/ | n. | 盗用者，挪用公款者 | A person who illegally takes money that they are responsible for managing, often from their employer. | The embezzler was sentenced to five years in prison.  挪用公款者被判处五年监禁。 | Synonyms: thief, swindler, fraudster | Antonyms: donor, philanthropist | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From embezzle + -er.  Embezzle comes from the Old French embesiller "to make away with," from the prefix en- "in" + besiller "to plunder."
