eulogization:/juːlədʒɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 颂扬，赞颂，悼词 |  The act of praising someone or something highly, typically in a formal speech or written tribute. |  The eulogization of the fallen soldiers was deeply moving.  阵亡士兵的悼词令人深感动容。| synonyms: praise, commendation, tribute, encomium, laudation | antonyms: criticism, condemnation, censure, denunciation | noun
