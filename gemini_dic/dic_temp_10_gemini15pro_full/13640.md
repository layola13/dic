attitudinizer:/ˈætɪtjuːdɪnaɪzər/| n. |装腔作势的人；故作姿态的人|A person who adopts affected attitudes or poses.|He's such an attitudinizer, always trying to impress everyone with his fake sophistication. 他真是个装腔作势的人，总是试图用他虚假的世故来给每个人留下深刻印象。|近义词：poseur, posturer, affected person|反义词：genuine person, sincere person|词性:noun

attitudinize:/ˈætɪtjuːdɪnaɪz/| v. |装腔作势；故作姿态|To behave in an affected or artificial way; to strike a pose.|She attitudinized with her new handbag, pretending it was a designer brand. 她拿着她的新手提包装腔作势，假装它是一个名牌。|近义词：pose, posture, affect|反义词：behave naturally, be sincere|词性:verb
