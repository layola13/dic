eleventh:/ɪˈlev(ə)nθ/
adjective|第十一的；十一人组成的；|Relating to the number eleven in a series; coming after ten others.
|例句：This is the eleventh time I've told you. 这是我第十一次告诉你了。
|例句：She finished in eleventh place. 她获得了第十一名。
|近义词：final, last (in a series of eleven)
|反义词：first
|词性：形容词

eleventh:/ɪˈlev(ə)nθ/
noun|第十一；十一人；十一分之一|The ordinal number matching the number eleven in a series; one of eleven equal parts of a whole.
|例句：Today is the eleventh of the month. 今天是这个月的十一号。
|例句：An eleventh of the profits will go to charity.  利润的十一分之一将捐给慈善机构。
|近义词：one-eleventh (as a fraction)
|反义词：None (as a noun)
|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *endlefta (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *ainlifta-, from *ainliban "eleven" + *-ta- ordinal suffix.  The modern spelling reflects influence from "seven" and "ninth."
