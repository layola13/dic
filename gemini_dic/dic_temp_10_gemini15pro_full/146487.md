overgamble:/ˌoʊvərˈɡæmbəl/ | v. | 过度赌博，赌博成瘾 | To gamble excessively, often to the point of financial or personal detriment. | He overgambled and lost his life savings. 他过度赌博，输光了毕生积蓄。|  She had a tendency to overgamble when stressed.  她压力大的时候有过度赌博的倾向。 | 近义词: gamble excessively,  compulsive gambling | 反义词: gamble responsibly, abstain from gambling | 词性: 动词

Etymology:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "gamble" (from Old English *gamenian "to play, amuse oneself")
