centromedian:/ˌsɛntroʊˈmiːdiən/| adj.|中心中值的|Relating to or situated at the centroid and median of a geometric figure.|The centromedian point of a triangle is the intersection of its medians.|近义词:midpoint, center|反义词:peripheral, extreme|词性:形容词

Etymology:
centro- (from Latin *centrum*, from Greek *kéntron* "center, point") + median (from Late Latin *medianus* "of the middle")
