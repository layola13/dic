hare-brained:/ˈhɛərˌbreɪnd/| adj.|轻率的；愚蠢的；不切实际的|Foolish and impractical.|例句：That hare-brained scheme of yours is never going to work. 你那愚蠢的计划永远不会成功。

例句：Investing all your savings in that company was a hare-brained decision. 把你所有的积蓄都投资在那家公司是一个轻率的决定。


近义词：foolish, silly, reckless, rash, impulsive, ill-conceived, impractical|反义词:sensible, wise, prudent, practical|词性:形容词


词源:  From "hare" (the animal known for its quick, impulsive movements) + "brained" (having a brain of a certain quality).  The implication is that someone who is hare-brained acts as impulsively and thoughtlessly as a hare.
