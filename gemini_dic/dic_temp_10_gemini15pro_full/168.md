abate:/əˈbeɪt/| v. |减轻，减少，缓和|to become less strong; to make something less strong|The storm/wind/rain has started to abate.  暴风雨/风/雨开始减弱了。

The pain in his leg abated after he took some medicine. 他腿上的疼痛在服药后减轻了。|近义词：subside, lessen, diminish, decrease, decline, wane|反义词:increase, intensify, escalate, aggravate|词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The noise abates after midnight. = 午夜过后噪音减弱了。
Heavy rain often abates the heat. =  暴雨通常会减轻炎热。


一般过去时:
The storm abated quickly. = 暴风雨很快就减弱了。
Her fever abated overnight. = 她的烧夜里退了。


现在完成时:
The floodwaters have finally abated. = 洪水终于消退了。
The pain has abated somewhat. = 疼痛已经有所减轻。


过去完成时:
The wind had abated before they set sail. = 他们在起航前风已经减弱了。
The fever had abated by the time the doctor arrived. =  医生到达时，烧已经退了。


将来时:
The storm will abate soon. = 暴风雨很快就会减弱。
The traffic congestion will abate after rush hour. = 交通拥堵将在高峰时段后缓解。


虚拟语气:
If the wind were to abate, we could go sailing. = 如果风减弱了，我们就可以去航行了。
I wish the noise would abate. = 我希望噪音能减弱。


被动语态:
The problem was abated by the new policy. = 新政策缓解了这个问题。
The risk of infection is being abated by the vaccination program. =  疫苗接种计划正在降低感染风险。


疑问句:
Does the pain abate after taking the medication? = 服药后疼痛会减轻吗？
When will the storm abate? = 暴风雨什么时候会减弱？


否定句:
The noise doesn't abate at all. = 噪音一点也没有减弱。
The fever didn't abate overnight. =  烧夜里没有退。



PHRASE->
abate the nuisance =  减少滋扰

abate the storm = 平息风暴
