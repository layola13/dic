morose:/məˈroʊs/ /məˈrəʊs/| adj.|闷闷不乐的；阴郁的；脾气不好的|Characterized by or expressing gloom.  Having a sullen and gloomy disposition.|He's been morose and withdrawn ever since he lost his job.  他自从失业以来一直闷闷不乐，郁郁寡欢。|Synonyms: sullen, gloomy, glum, sulky, dour, saturnine|Antonyms: cheerful, happy, jovial, merry|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mōrōsus* (“peevish, fastidious”), from *mōs* (“manner, custom, habit”) + *-ōsus*.
