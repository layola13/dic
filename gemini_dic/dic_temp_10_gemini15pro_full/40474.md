contributing:/kənˈtrɪbjʊtɪŋ/
adjective
贡献的，起作用的
Playing a part in bringing something about.
例句：He made a contributing factor to the team's success. 他对团队的成功做出了贡献。

例句：Stress is a contributing factor to many illnesses. 压力是许多疾病的诱因。

近义词：contributive,  instrumental,  helpful, conducive
反义词：detrimental, hindering, obstructive
词性: adjective

contribute:/kənˈtrɪbjuːt/
verb
贡献，捐献；投稿
To give something, especially money or goods, to help achieve or provide something.
例句：He contributed a large sum of money to the charity. 他向慈善机构捐了一大笔钱。

例句：She regularly contributes articles to the magazine. 她定期向杂志投稿。

近义词：donate, give, provide, supply, furnish
反义词：withdraw, withhold, detract
词性: verb

contribution:/ˌkɒntrɪˈbjuːʃn/
noun
贡献，捐款；稿件
Something that you contribute or do to help produce or achieve something together with other people, or to help make something successful.
例句：Her contribution to the project was invaluable. 她对这个项目的贡献是无价的。

例句：He made a significant contribution to the field of medicine. 他对医学领域做出了重大贡献。

近义词：donation, gift, offering, input
反义词：deduction, withdrawal
词性: noun
