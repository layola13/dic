antienvironmental:/ˌæntiɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəl/
adjective
反对环境保护的；不利于环境的
Opposed to environmentalism; harmful to the environment.
The company's antienvironmental policies led to protests. 该公司反对环境保护的政策引发了抗议。
The factory's waste disposal practices were antienvironmental. 这家工厂的废物处理方法对环境有害。
近义词: environmentally damaging, ecologically harmful
反义词: pro-environmental, environmentally friendly, green
词性: adjective
