include:/ɪnˈkluːd/ (US), /ɪnˈkluːd/ (UK)| v. |包含，包括|To contain something as a part of something else, or to make something part of something else.|The price includes breakfast. 价格包含早餐。|synonyms: contain, comprise, encompass, incorporate|antonyms: exclude, omit|verb

include:/ɪnˈkluːd/ (US), /ɪnˈkluːd/ (UK)| n. |包含，包括（名词形式较少见）|The act of including something.|The include file caused the error.  包含文件导致了错误。|synonyms: inclusion, incorporation|antonyms: exclusion, omission|noun
