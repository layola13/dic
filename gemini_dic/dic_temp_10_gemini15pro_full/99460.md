interfacing:/ˈɪntərˌfeɪsɪŋ/| n. |接口，交界面；连接；互动|The act of connecting or interacting, especially between two systems or pieces of equipment.  Also refers to the hardware or software that enables this connection.|The interfacing between the two computer systems was seamless. 两个计算机系统之间的接口是无缝的。|synonyms: connection, interaction, link, interface|antonyms: separation, disconnection|noun

interfacing:/ˈɪntərˌfeɪsɪŋ/| adj. |连接的；交互的|Serving as a connection or interaction between two systems or things.|Interfacing modules are crucial for the proper functioning of the device. 连接模块对于设备的正常运行至关重要。|synonyms: connecting, interacting, linking|antonyms: separating, disconnecting|adjective
