artificialize:/ˌɑːrtɪˈfɪʃəlaɪz/
verb
使人工化，使人为化
To make artificial; to give an artificial appearance or character to.
The company artificialized the flavoring in their products.  这家公司人工合成了他们产品中的调味剂。
They artificialized the environment to conduct the experiment. 他们在人工环境下进行实验。
近义词:synthesize, fabricate, contrive
反义词:naturalize
词性:verb
