cerago:/sɪˈreɪɡoʊ/ /sɛˈreɪɡoʊ/
noun
蜂蜡；耳垢
Beeswax; earwax.  A mixture of beeswax, propolis, and pollen used by bees to seal cracks in the hive.  Sometimes also used to refer to earwax, due to its similar appearance.
The beekeeper harvested the cerago to make candles. 养蜂人收获蜂蜡来制作蜡烛。
He cleaned the cerago from his ears. 他清理了耳朵里的耳垢。
Synonyms: beeswax, earwax, propolis (partially)
Antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词
