high-pressure:/ˌhaɪˈprɛʃər/| adj.|高压的，高压力的|Characterized by or involving intense pressure, stress, or urgency.|Working in a high-pressure sales environment can be very stressful. 在高压力的销售环境中工作压力会很大。|The hospital's high-pressure oxygen chamber is used to treat divers with decompression sickness. 医院的高压氧舱用于治疗患有减压病的潜水员。|Synonyms: stressful, demanding, intense, urgent|Antonyms: low-pressure, relaxed, easygoing|adjective


high-pressure:/ˌhaɪˈprɛʃər/| n.|高压|A state of high or intense pressure.|He couldn't handle the high-pressure of the job and resigned. 他无法承受这份工作的巨大压力而辞职了。|The high-pressure in the pipeline caused it to burst. 管道中的高压导致它爆裂。|Synonyms: stress, strain, tension|Antonyms: low-pressure, relaxation|noun
