macrophytic:/ˌmækroʊˈfɪtɪk/ | adj. | 大型植物的，肉眼可见的水生植物的 |  Relating to or consisting of macrophytes; large enough to be seen with the naked eye (especially of aquatic plants). | The lake was choked with macrophytic growth.  湖里长满了大型水生植物。 | synonyms: macroscopic, visible | antonyms: microscopic, invisible | adjective
