assassin: /əˈsæsɪn/ | n. | 刺客，暗杀者 | A person who murders an important person for political or religious reasons. |  John Wilkes Booth was the assassin of Abraham Lincoln.  约翰·威尔克斯·布斯是亚伯拉罕·林肯的刺客。| synonyms: murderer, killer, executioner | antonyms: protector, guardian, savior | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *assassin*, from Arabic *ḥaššāšīn* "hashish-eaters," plural of *ḥaššāš* "hashish user," from *ḥašīš* "hashish."  The original Assassins were a Nizari Ismaili sect of Shia Islam, reputed to use hashish before carrying out assassinations.
