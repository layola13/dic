disguster:/dɪsˈɡʌstər/| n.|厌恶，憎恶；令人厌恶的人或事物|Something that causes a strong feeling of dislike or disgust. |The smell of rotten eggs filled me with disguster. 腐烂鸡蛋的气味让我充满厌恶。|近义词：revulsion, abhorrence, loathing, disgust|反义词: admiration, liking, pleasure|Noun

Etymology:
From Middle French *desgouster*, from Old French desgouster (“to displease, disgust”), from des- (expressing reversal) + gouster (“to taste”). Ultimately from Latin gustus (“taste”).
