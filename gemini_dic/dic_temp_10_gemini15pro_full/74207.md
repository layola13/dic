foursome: /ˈfɔːrsəm/ | n. |四人一组；四人赛；四人性交|A group of four people, especially one formed for a particular purpose, such as playing golf or bridge. |例句：We played a foursome of golf this afternoon. 我们今天下午打了一场四人高尔夫球赛。

例句：They made up a foursome for bridge.  他们组成了一个四人桥牌组。|近义词：quartet, group of four |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: noun
