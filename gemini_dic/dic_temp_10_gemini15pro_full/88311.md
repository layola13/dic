hippered:/ˈhɪpərd/ | adj. | 时髦的，熟悉内情的，消息灵通的 |  Aware of or informed about the latest trends or developments, especially in popular culture.  Having or showing awareness of or involvement in something fashionable or unconventional. |  例句：She's very hippered and always knows the latest trends. 她非常时髦，总是知道最新的潮流。

例句：He's a hippered musician who knows all the cool new bands. 他是一位消息灵通的音乐家，知道所有很酷的新乐队。| 近义词：trendy, stylish, fashionable, hip, cool, in, informed, aware, knowledgeable, savvy | 反义词：unfashionable, out-of-date, outdated, square, unhip, unaware, ignorant | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From "hip" (meaning "fashionable") + "-ered" (a suffix forming adjectives).  "Hip" itself has uncertain origins, possibly related to West African languages.
