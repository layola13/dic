maverick: /ˈmævərɪk/ (US) /ˈmævərɪk/ (UK) | n. adj.|特立独行者；持不同意见者；不墨守成规的人；标新立异的；独立的| An independent person who does not go along with a group or party.  A person who refuses to follow the customs or rules of a group.|例句：He was a maverick in the field of physics, challenging established theories. 他是物理学领域的独行侠，挑战既有的理论。|近义词：nonconformist, individualist, rebel, dissenter, iconoclast|反义词：conformist, follower, traditionalist|词性:名词、形容词
