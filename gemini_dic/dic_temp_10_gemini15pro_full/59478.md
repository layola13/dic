PHRASE->
ease up (on someone/something): 放松，减轻 (对某人/某事的) 压力  Ease up on him, he's new to the job.  对他宽容些，他是新来的。
ease into something: 慢慢适应  She's easing into her new role as manager. 她正在慢慢适应她作为经理的新角色。
at ease:  轻松自在  I felt at ease in her company. 和她在一起我感到很自在。
ill at ease:  局促不安  He seemed ill at ease at the party. 他在聚会上似乎很不自在。
ease off:  减弱，缓和  The rain eased off in the afternoon. 下午雨势减弱了。
