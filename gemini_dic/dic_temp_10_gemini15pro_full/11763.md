ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *arousen*, from Anglo-Norman *aruser*,  from Old French *esruser*, *ahuser* "to startle, rouse (a deer)," from *a-* (from Latin *ad-*) + *huser* "to shout, hoot," of Germanic origin.
