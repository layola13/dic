canters:/ˈkæntərz/ (US), /ˈkæntəz/ (UK)| n. v.|慢跑，轻快地跑；|A canter is a three-beat gait of a horse or other quadruped, intermediate in speed between a trot and a gallop.|The horse cantered across the field. 马轻快地跑过田野。|近义词：lope, trot|反义词:gallop, sprint|词性:名词,动词

canter:/ˈkæntə(r)/ (UK), /ˈkæntər/ (US)| v.|慢跑，轻快地跑|To ride or cause (a horse) to canter.|He cantered his horse along the beach. 他骑着马沿着海滩慢跑。|近义词：lope, trot|反义词:gallop, sprint|词性:动词
