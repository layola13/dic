necrotomy: /nɪˈkrɒtəmi/ | n. | 尸体解剖，剖尸 | The act of dissecting a dead body, especially for determining the cause of death. |  A necrotomy was performed to determine the cause of death.  尸体解剖是为了确定死因。 | necropsy, autopsy |  vivisection (活体解剖) | noun
etymology-> From Greek nekros "dead body" + -tomy "cutting."
