fleckier:/ˈflɛkiər/| adj.|更多斑点的，斑驳的|Covered with more small marks or spots of a different color or texture than the background.  More spotted or speckled than something else.|The fleckier coat of the older dog made it look distinguished.  这只老狗斑驳的毛皮使它看起来很尊贵。|近义词：spotted, speckled, mottled, dappled|反义词：plain, uniform, unblemished|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From fleck + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *flakō ("spot, piece").
