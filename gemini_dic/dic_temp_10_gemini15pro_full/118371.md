meritorious:/ˌmɛrɪˈtɔːriəs/| adj.|值得称赞的；有功的|Deserving praise, reward, or honor because of exceptional quality, service, or achievement.  Based on merit.|Her meritorious service to the community was recognized with an award. 她对社区的功绩得到了奖励。|近义词：praiseworthy, commendable, laudable, deserving|反义词：blameworthy, reprehensible, discreditable|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin meritorius, from Latin meritum ‘merit’.
