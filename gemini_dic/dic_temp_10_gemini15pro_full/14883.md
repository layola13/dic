ayesha:/ɑːˈjeɪʃə/ | n. | 艾莎 | A female given name, of Arabic origin, meaning "life" or "alive".  It is a variant transliteration of Aisha, the name of the Prophet Muhammad's wife. | My friend Ayesha is a talented artist. 我的朋友艾莎是一位才华横溢的艺术家。 | Ayesha is a common name in many Muslim communities. 艾莎在许多穆斯林社区是一个常见的名字。 |  近义词: Aisha, Asha | 反义词: N/A | noun
