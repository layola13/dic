harmost:/ˈhɑːrmɒst/| n.|地方长官，行政长官|An administrator or governor of a district or region.|The harmost was responsible for collecting taxes and maintaining order. 地方长官负责收税和维持秩序。|Synonyms: administrator, governor, official, ruler|Antonyms: subordinate, subject, civilian|noun
