half-conscious:/ˌhæfˈkɒnʃəs/ | /ˌhæfˈkɑːnʃəs/ | adj. | 半清醒的；神志不清的 | Partly conscious; not fully aware of one's surroundings. |  The patient was half-conscious after the surgery.  手术后病人处于半昏迷状态。

The boxer was half-conscious after the knockout punch. 拳击手被击倒后处于半昏迷状态。


近义词:  semiconscious, dazed, groggy, sleepy, drowsy | 反义词:  conscious, alert, awake, aware | 词性: adjective

词源:  "half-" (Old English healf) + "conscious" (from Latin conscius "knowing with others, aware," from com "with, together" + scire "to know")
