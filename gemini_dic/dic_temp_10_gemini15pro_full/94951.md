in relation to:/ɪn rɪˈleɪʃən tuː/
preposition
关于；涉及；与…相比
Concerning; with reference to; compared to.
The committee will review the matter in relation to the budget. 委员会将结合预算审查此事。
The house is quite small in relation to the others on the street.  这房子与街上其他的房子相比很小。
近义词:regarding, concerning, with respect to, compared with/to
反义词:irrespective of, regardless of
词性:介词 (preposition)
