indecimable:/ˌɪndɪˈsaɪdəbl/| adj.|不可判定的，无法决定的|Not capable of being decided or proven; (in logic and mathematics) not having an algorithm that can determine whether a given statement is true or false.|The halting problem is a classic example of an indecimable problem in computer science.  计算机科学中的停机问题是不可判定问题的经典例子。|synonyms:  unsolvable, unprovable, indeterminable|antonyms: decidable, solvable, provable|adjective
