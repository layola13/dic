assailing:/əˈseɪlɪŋ/
verb
攻击，抨击
Attacking, criticizing strongly and aggressively.
The journalist was assailing the politician with tough questions.  记者用尖锐的问题攻击这位政治家。
The enemy troops were assailing the city walls. 敌军正在猛攻城墙。
synonyms: attacking, assaulting, bombarding, berating, lambasting
antonyms: defending, protecting, supporting, praising, commending
v.
