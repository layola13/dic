contemporaneously: /kənˌtɛmpəˈreɪniəsli/ | adv. | 同时地，同期地 | happening or existing at the same time | The two events occurred contemporaneously. 这两件事同时发生。 | Examples:
The two artists worked contemporaneously on separate murals.  两位艺术家同时创作不同的壁画。

The rise of social media occurred contemporaneously with the development of smartphones. 社交媒体的兴起与智能手机的发展同时发生。


|Synonyms: simultaneously, concurrently, at the same time | Antonyms: separately, at different times, asynchronously | Word Type: Adverb
