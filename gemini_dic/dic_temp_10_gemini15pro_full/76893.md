gangway:/ˈɡæŋweɪ/ | n. | 通道，舷梯，（人群中的）通道 | A passage through which to enter or leave a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle; a raised platform or walkway providing access to or from a ship or aircraft.  A clear passage through a crowd. |  例句：The passengers boarded the ship via the gangway. 乘客通过舷梯登上了船。

例句：Please make a gangway for the paramedics. 请给医护人员让条路。| 近义词：passage, walkway, aisle, corridor | 反义词：barrier, obstruction | 词性：名词


gangway:/ˈɡæŋweɪ/ | int. | 让开！借过！| Used to demand that people move out of one's way. | 例句：Gangway! Coming through! 借过！借过！

例句：Gangway! There's a fire! 让开！着火了！ | 近义词：excuse me, make way, move aside | 反义词：N/A | 词性：感叹词


ETYMOLOGY->

From Old Norse *gangvegr "gangway," from *gangr "going" + *vegr "way."
