digestively:/daɪˈdʒɛstɪvli/
adv.|消化地；关于消化的|Relating to or aiding digestion.
The food was digestively processed.  食物被消化地处理了。
Certain enzymes aid digestively. 某些酶有助于消化。
近义词:gastrically, intestinally
反义词:indigestibly
词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "digestive" + "-ly".  "Digestive" comes from Late Latin *digestivus*, from Latin digestus, past participle of digerere "to separate, distribute, dissolve, digest," from dis- "apart" + gerere "to carry, bear."
