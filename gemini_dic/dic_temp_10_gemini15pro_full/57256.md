downloading:/ˌdaʊnˈloʊdɪŋ/ | n. | 下载；下载中 | The act of transferring data from a remote computer system to one's own computer. | I am currently downloading a large file. 我现在正在下载一个大文件。 | Synonyms: transferring, retrieving | Antonyms: uploading | noun

downloading:/ˌdaʊnˈloʊdɪŋ/ | adj. | 下载的；下载中 |  Being in the process of being downloaded. | The downloading file is quite large. 正在下载的文件相当大。 | Synonyms: incoming, transferring | Antonyms: uploading | adjective

词源:  From "download" + "-ing" suffix. "Download" itself originated from the concept of transferring data "down" from a central computer or server.  The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb "download" into a present participle (used in continuous tenses) or a gerund (noun form).
