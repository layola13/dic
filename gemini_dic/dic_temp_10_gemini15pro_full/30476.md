centripetence:/ˌsɛntrɪˈpiːtəns/ | n. | 向心力，向心倾向 | The tendency to move or be directed toward a center. |  The centripetence of the gravitational force keeps the planets in orbit around the sun. 向心引力使行星绕太阳运行。| Synonyms:  centripetal force, inward drive, centralizing tendency | Antonyms: centrifugal force, centrifugal tendency | noun

centripetal:/ˌsɛnˈtrɪpɪtl/ | adj. | 向心的 | Directed or moving toward a center. | The centripetal force pulls objects towards the center of a circle. 向心力将物体拉向圆心。 | Synonyms:  inward, converging | Antonyms: centrifugal, diverging | adjective
