macc:/mæk/ | n. |  Media Access Control address (MAC address) | A unique identifier assigned to a network interface controller (NIC) for use as a network address in communications within a network segment. | Every device on a network has a unique MAC address. 每个网络设备都有一个唯一的MAC地址。| synonym: physical address, hardware address | antonym: N/A | noun
