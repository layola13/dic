atonalist: /ˌeɪˈtəʊnəlɪst/ | n. | 无调性音乐家；无调性音乐作曲家 | A composer who writes atonal music, music that avoids traditional harmonies and key signatures. |  Schoenberg was a pioneering atonalist. 勋伯格是一位无调性音乐的先驱。| synonym: twelve-tone composer, serialist | antonym: tonal composer | noun


atonal: /eɪˈtəʊnl/ | adj. | 无调性的 | Lacking a tonal center or key; characterized by the avoidance of traditional harmonies and key signatures. | The atonal composition was challenging for many listeners. 这首无调性的作品对许多听众来说具有挑战性。 | synonym: nontonal, twelve-tone, serial | antonym: tonal | adjective
