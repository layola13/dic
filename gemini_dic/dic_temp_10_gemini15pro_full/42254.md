counter-accusation:/ˌkaʊntərˌækjʊˈzeɪʃn/| n.|反控告，反指控|An accusation made in response to a previous accusation.|例句：The defendant's counter-accusation surprised the prosecution.  被告的反控告让控方感到惊讶。

例句：She responded to the allegations with a counter-accusation of her own. 她用自己的反指控回应了这些指控。|近义词：countercharge, counterclaim|反义词: admission (of guilt)|词性: noun
