denationalised:/ˌdiːˈnæʃnəlaɪzd/
adjective
非国有的，私有化的
Having been changed from being owned and controlled by the government to being privately owned and controlled.
The denationalised railway system has seen significant investment.  私有化的铁路系统获得了大量投资。
The government denationalised the airline in the 1990s.  政府在20世纪90年代将这家航空公司私有化了。
Synonyms: privatized, deregulated
Antonyms: nationalized, state-owned
詞性: adjective
