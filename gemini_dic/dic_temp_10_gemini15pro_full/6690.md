analemma: /ˌænəˈlɛmə/ | n. | 日行迹，八字曲线 | A curve representing the Sun's apparent position in the sky at the same mean solar time, as viewed from a fixed location on Earth, throughout the year. It is often shown on globes and sundials. | The analemma's shape is like a figure eight, with the top loop smaller than the bottom. 日行迹的形状像一个数字8，上面的环比下面的环小。
