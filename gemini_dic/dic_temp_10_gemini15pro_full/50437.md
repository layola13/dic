deprecate:/ˈdɛprɪkeɪt/ | v. | 反对；轻视；抨击；不赞成 | To express disapproval of something. |  The teacher deprecated the student's disruptive behavior. 老师批评了那个学生的捣乱行为。

The government deprecated the violence and called for calm. 政府谴责了暴力行为，呼吁保持冷静。


近义词: criticize, condemn, deplore, denounce, censure
反义词: praise, commend, approve, endorse, laud
词性: 动词
