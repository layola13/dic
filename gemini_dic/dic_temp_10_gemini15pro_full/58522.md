duetting:/duˈɛtɪŋ/ /duːˈɛtɪŋ/
n. |二重唱，二重奏|The act of performing a duet.  A duet is a musical piece for two performers.  Therefore, duetting is the act of those two performers playing or singing together. |The two singers were duetting beautifully. 这两位歌手的二重唱优美动听。| 近义词:harmonizing, collaborating musically|反义词:soloing|词性:名词
