antheriform:/ˈænθərɪfɔːrm/
adjective
花药状的； resembling an anther
Resembling or having the form of an anther, the pollen-bearing part of a stamen.
The antheriform appendage on the orchid's labellum attracts pollinating insects.  兰花唇瓣上的花药状附属物吸引授粉昆虫。
Near Antonyms: petaloid, sepaloid
Etymology: From Greek *anthēros* (blooming) + Latin *-formis* (shaped)
