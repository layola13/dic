enargite:/ˈɛnɑːrdʒaɪt/| n. | 硫砷铜矿 |A dark gray or black metallic mineral, a copper arsenic sulfide, Cu3AsS4, an important ore of copper. |The mine is a significant source of enargite. 这个矿山是硫砷铜矿的重要来源。| Enargite is often found alongside other copper minerals like chalcopyrite and bornite. 硫砷铜矿常与其他铜矿物如黄铜矿和斑铜矿一起发现。|Synonyms:  luzonite (polymorph) | Antonyms: N/A | noun
