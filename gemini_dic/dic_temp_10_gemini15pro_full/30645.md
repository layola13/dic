ceriferous:/sɪˈrɪfərəs/
adjective
产蜡的；分泌蜡的
Producing or secreting wax.
The ceriferous glands of the honeybee secrete beeswax. 蜜蜂的蜡腺分泌蜂蜡。
近义词:wax-producing, wax-secreting
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) non-waxy, wax-deficient
词性: adjective
