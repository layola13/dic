go's: /ɡoʊz/
noun
(单数形式go的第三人称单数现在式)去；进行；尝试；(机器等)运转；(事情)进展；(时间)流逝；(机会)失去；(比赛等)平局
He goes to school every day. 他每天去上学。
The engine goes smoothly. 引擎运转平稳。
近义词: proceeds, functions, fares, continues
反义词: stops, halts, ceases

He goes to work by bus. 他乘公共汽车去上班。
She goes to the gym every morning. 她每天早上都去健身房。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He is going to the store now. 他现在正要去商店。
She is going home after work. 她下班后要回家。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has gone to the library. 他去图书馆了。(他现在不在此处)
She has gone to Paris for vacation. 她去巴黎度假了。(她现在不在此处)

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He went to the park yesterday. 他昨天去了公园。
She went to the concert last night. 她昨晚去了音乐会。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
He was going to the store when he saw his friend. 他正要去商店时，看到了他的朋友。
She was going home when it started to rain. 她正要回家时，开始下雨了。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had gone to the bank before he went to work. 他在上班之前去了银行。
She had gone to the doctor before she went on vacation. 她去度假之前去看过医生。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
He had been going to the gym regularly before he got injured. 他受伤前一直定期去健身房。
She had been going to that coffee shop every day for a year. 她每天都去那家咖啡店，已经一年了。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
