airbag: /ˈɛəˌbæɡ/ (US) /ˈɛːˌbæɡ/ (UK)| n. |安全气囊|A safety device in a vehicle consisting of a bag that inflates rapidly on collision to prevent injury to the occupants.|例句：The airbag deployed on impact.  安全气囊受到撞击时展开了。

|例句：The airbag saved his life in the crash. 安全气囊在车祸中救了他的命。

|近义词：air cushion, safety cushion|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "seatbelt" as an alternative safety measure) |词性:noun
