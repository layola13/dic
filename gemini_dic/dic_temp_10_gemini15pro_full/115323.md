manikin:/ˈmænɪkɪn/ | n. |人体模型；时装模特儿；侏儒|A jointed model of the human body, used especially by artists, and also a small-scale anatomical model of the human body used for teaching. Also, a person, especially a woman or girl, employed to model clothes. Can sometimes refer to a dwarf. |The artist used a wooden manikin to study human proportions. 艺术家用一个木制人体模型来研究人体比例。

The fashion designer dressed the manikin in her latest creation.  时装设计师给她最新创作的服装穿在人体模型上。|Synonyms: dummy, model, lay figure, mannequin, form|Antonyms: giant|Noun


mannikins:/ˈmænɪkɪnz/ | n. |人体模型（manikin的复数）；时装模特儿（manikin的复数）；侏儒（manikin的复数）|Plural form of manikin.  Refers to multiple jointed models of the human body, multiple people employed to model clothes, or multiple dwarfs. |The store window displayed several mannikins wearing the new fall collection.  商店橱窗里展示了几个穿着新款秋季系列服装的人体模型。


The art school purchased a dozen new mannikins for the drawing classes.  艺术学校为绘画课购买了一打新的人体模型。|Synonyms: dummies, models, lay figures, mannequins, forms|Antonyms: giants|Noun
