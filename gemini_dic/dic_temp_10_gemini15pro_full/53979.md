discreteness's:/dɪˈskriːtnəsɪz/| n. |独立性，分离性，离散性|The quality or state of being discrete; separateness.  It refers to something being individually separate and distinct.|The discreteness's of the data points allowed for easy analysis.  数据点的离散性使得分析变得容易。|Synonyms: separateness, distinctness, individuality, detachment|Antonyms: continuity, connectedness, integration|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *discrete* + *-ness*.

*Discrete* comes from Latin *discretus*, past participle of *discernere* "to separate, distinguish," from *dis-* "apart" + *cernere* "to sift, separate, distinguish."
