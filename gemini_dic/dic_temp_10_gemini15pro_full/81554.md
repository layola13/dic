grayling:/ˈɡreɪlɪŋ/| n. |灰鳟;茴鱼|A freshwater fish (Thymallus thymallus) of the salmon family, found in clear, cold streams in Europe and North America. It is popular as a game fish.|例句：The grayling leaped from the water to catch the fly. 灰鳟跃出水面捕食苍蝇。

例句：Grayling are known for their large dorsal fins. 灰鳟以其巨大的背鳍而闻名。|近义词：trout, char|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑其他鱼类，例如carp 鲤鱼)|词性:noun
