hourlier:/ˈaʊərliər/ | adj. | 更频繁地，每小时地 | Happening more frequently every hour; occurring at shorter intervals each succeeding hour. |  The pain became hourlier, making it hard to sleep. 疼痛变得越来越频繁，难以入睡。|  The updates were becoming hourlier as the situation developed. 随着情况的发展，更新变得越来越频繁。|近义词：more frequent, more often | 反义词：less frequent, less often | 词性: 形容词

词源: 由 "hourly" (每小时的) + "-er" (比较级后缀) 构成。虽然“-er”通常用于比较级，但在这里表示程度的递增，而非直接的比较。
