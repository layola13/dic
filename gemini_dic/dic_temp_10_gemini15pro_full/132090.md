noncreditably:/ˌnɒnˈkrɛdɪtəbli/
adv.
不值得称道地，不光彩地
in a manner that does not bring credit or honor; disreputably.
He behaved noncreditably in the affair. 他在这件事中表现得不光彩。
近义词:disgracefully, shamefully, dishonorably
反义词:creditably, honorably, respectably
词性:副词

noncreditably:/(ˌ)nɒnˈkredɪtəbli/
Etymology: non- + creditably
non-: prefix meaning "not"
creditably: adverb form of "creditable," meaning "deserving of credit or honor"
词源:non- + creditably
non-: 前缀，意为“不”
creditably: "creditable"的副词形式，意为“值得称赞的”
