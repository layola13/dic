gastrology:/ɡæˈstrɒlədʒi/ | n. | 占星术;  美食占星术(一种将占星术与食物和饮料选择联系起来的做法) |  A system that supposes a connection between the positions of the stars or planets and events on earth, specifically relating it to food and drink choices.  It is not based on scientific evidence and is considered a pseudoscience. |  "She consulted a gastrologer to determine the best foods to eat based on her astrological sign." 她咨询了一位美食占星术家，根据她的星座来决定吃什么食物最好。 |  Near synonyms:  astrology,  culinary astrology (though less common) | Antonyms: astronomy, science | noun
