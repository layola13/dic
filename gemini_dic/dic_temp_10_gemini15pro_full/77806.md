generalness:/ˈdʒɛnərəlnəs/
n.
普遍性；一般性；概括性
The state or quality of being general; lack of specialization or specificity.
The generalness of the statement made it applicable to a wide range of situations.  该声明的普遍性使其适用于各种情况。
generality, broadness, universality
specificity, particularity
noun
