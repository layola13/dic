mascot's:/ˈmæskɑːts/| n.|吉祥物（的）|The possessive form of "mascot," referring to something belonging to or associated with a mascot.|The mascot's costume was torn during the game. 吉祥物的服装在比赛中被撕破了。|近义词:symbol's, emblem's|反义词:None|词性:名词所有格

ETYMOLOGY->
From French "mascotte," meaning "lucky charm," originally from Provençal "mascoto," meaning "witchcraft," "sorcery."  Ultimately derived from "masco," meaning "witch."
