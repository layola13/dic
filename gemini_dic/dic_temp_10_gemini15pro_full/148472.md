overstrengthening: /ˌoʊvərˈstreŋθənɪŋ/ /ˌəʊvəˈstreŋθənɪŋ/
|v.|过度强化 (用作动词的情况较少)|Overstrengthening the metal can make it brittle.|过度强化金属会使其变脆。|
|近义词: overreinforce, excessively strengthen|
|反义词: weaken, understrengthen|
|词性: 动词|


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "strengthening" (from "strengthen" -  from "strength" + "-en")

英文解析:  The act of making something excessively strong, often to the point of detriment.  This usually implies that the increased strength comes at the cost of other desirable properties, such as flexibility or ductility.
