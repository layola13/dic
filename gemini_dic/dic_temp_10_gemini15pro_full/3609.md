Etymology:
From agedness + -'s (possessive suffix). Agedness is derived from aged + -ness (suffix forming abstract nouns). Aged comes from Middle English aged, from Old French aage (age) + -ed (suffix indicating a state or condition). Ultimately from Latin aetas (age).
