hypergluon:/ˈhaɪpərˌɡluːɒn/| n. |超胶子|A hypothetical particle analogous to a gluon but mediating interactions between hypothetical hyperquarks.  It is a theoretical particle proposed in some extensions of the Standard Model of particle physics.|Physicists are searching for evidence of hypergluons. 物理学家正在寻找超胶子的证据。|近义词: (No direct synonyms due to its hypothetical nature)  Perhaps related concepts like "gauge boson" could be considered. |反义词:  (None, as it's a hypothetical particle)|名词
