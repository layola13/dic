medusiform:/məˈdjuːsɪfɔːrm/
adjective
伞状的，水母形的
Shaped like a medusa or jellyfish; umbrella-shaped.
The Portuguese man-of-war is a medusiform creature.  葡萄牙战舰是一种伞状的生物。
The cloud had a medusiform appearance. 这朵云呈现出水母的形状。
Synonyms: umbrella-shaped, bell-shaped, jellyfish-like
Antonyms: amorphous, shapeless
