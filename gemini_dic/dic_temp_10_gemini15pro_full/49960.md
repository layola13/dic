denial:/dɪˈnaɪəl/| n.| 否认，拒绝，否定|The defendant's denial of the charges was unconvincing.  被告否认指控，但并不令人信服。|例句：His denial of responsibility angered everyone involved. 他否认承担责任激怒了所有相关人员。

例句：Her denial that she had taken the money was weak. 她否认拿了钱，但语气很弱。


近义词：refusal, rejection, repudiation|反义词:acceptance, admission, acknowledgment|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *denial, from denier "to deny," from Latin denegare "to refuse, deny, say no," from de- "away" + negare "to deny."
