Etymology:

From "mascot" + "-ry," a suffix forming nouns denoting a domain, condition, or practice.  "Mascot" itself comes from the Provençal word "mascoto," meaning "sorcerer," "witch," or "charm," ultimately from the Latin "masca," meaning "mask" or "spectre."
