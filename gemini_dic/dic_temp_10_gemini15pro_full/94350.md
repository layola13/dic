impiously:/ɪmˈpaɪəsli/| adv.|不敬地，亵渎地|In an impious manner; lacking reverence for God or sacred things.  Showing disrespect or irreverence towards something considered sacred or holy.|He impiously mocked the religious beliefs of others. 他不敬地嘲弄了别人的宗教信仰。|例句：She spoke impiously of the church. 她对教会的言论很不敬。

例句：He impiously cursed the heavens. 他亵渎地咒骂上天。


近义词：irreverently, profanely, blasphemously|反义词：reverently, piously, devoutly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impiōsus, from Latin impius (“irreligious, undutiful”), from in- (“not”) + pius (“dutiful, religious”).
