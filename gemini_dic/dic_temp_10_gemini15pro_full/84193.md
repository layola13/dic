half-rebelliously:/ˌhæf rɪˈbɛliəsli/
|adv.|半叛逆地，略带叛逆地|Showing a slight degree of resistance or defiance against authority or convention. |She smiled half-rebelliously, a spark of defiance in her eyes. 她半叛逆地笑了笑，眼中闪过一丝反抗的神色。|He half-rebelliously kicked at a stone as he walked. 他边走边半叛逆地踢着石头。|Near synonyms: defiantly, resisting, opposing, somewhat rebelliously|Near antonyms: obediently, submissively, compliantly|adverb
