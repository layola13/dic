entailed:/ɪnˈteɪld/ | v. | 使必要；需要；承担 |  To involve something that cannot be avoided. | Building a new house entails a lot of work.  建造新房子需要大量的工作。| synonyms: involve, necessitate, require, demand | antonyms: exclude, preclude | verb

entailed:/ɪnˈteɪld/ | adj. | 限制继承的；预定的 |  (of property) Inheritable only by a specified heir or line of heirs, so that the owner cannot sell or give it away. | The entailed estate has been in the family for centuries. 这片限定继承的房产已经在这个家族传承了几个世纪。| synonyms: inherited, bequeathed, hereditary | antonyms: unentailed, disposable | adjective
