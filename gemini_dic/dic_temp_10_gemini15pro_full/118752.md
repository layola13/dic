metalinguistic:/ˌmetəˌlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk/
adjective
元语言的
Relating to the study of language and its relationship to culture and thought, or to the ability to reflect on and analyze language as a system.
Children's metalinguistic awareness develops gradually as they mature.  孩子们对元语言的意识随着他们的成熟而逐渐发展。
Metalinguistic skills are crucial for effective communication. 元语言技能对于有效沟通至关重要。
近义词: linguistic, meta-linguistic
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "unreflective of language" as a contrasting concept)
词性: adjective
