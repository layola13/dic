一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What happens every day?  每天都会发生什么？
Nothing much happens around here.  这里没什么大事发生。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What is happening right now?  现在正在发生什么？
Something strange is happening outside.  外面正在发生一些奇怪的事情。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What has happened since I left?  自从我离开后发生了什么事？
Has anything interesting happened today?  今天发生了什么有趣的事情吗？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What has been happening here all day?  这里一整天都在发生什么？
I've been wondering what has been happening with him.  我一直想知道他发生了什么事。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What happened yesterday?  昨天发生了什么事？
Nothing much happened at the meeting.  会议上没发生什么大事。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What was happening when you arrived?  你到达时正在发生什么？
I was sleeping when the earthquake happened.  地震发生时我正在睡觉。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had happened before the police arrived?  警察到达之前发生了什么事？
I knew something had happened because he looked upset.  我知道发生了一些事，因为他看起来很沮丧。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had been happening before you got here?  你来之前一直在发生什么？
They had been arguing for hours before I intervened.  在我介入之前，他们已经争吵了几个小时。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will happen next?  接下来会发生什么？
I don't know what will happen tomorrow.  我不知道明天会发生什么。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will be happening this time next week?  下周这个时候会发生什么？
I'll be traveling when the event happens.  活动发生时我将在旅行。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will have happened by the end of the year?  到年底会发生什么？
By next year, many things will have happened.  到明年，很多事情都会发生。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing when it happens?  它发生的时候你将一直在做什么？
By the time we arrive, they will have been working for hours.  当我们到达时，他们将已经工作了几个小时。
