effectual:/ɪˈfɛktʃuəl/
adjective
有效的，奏效的
producing or capable of producing a desired effect
例句：The medicine was effectual in relieving her pain.  这种药有效地缓解了她的疼痛。

例句：They took effectual measures to prevent the spread of the disease. 他们采取了有效的措施来防止疾病的传播。

近义词：effective, efficient, potent, powerful
反义词：ineffectual, ineffective, useless, futile
词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *effectualis, from Latin effectus (“effect, accomplishment, result”) (from efficere (“to work out, accomplish, perform, complete, make, cause, bring about”), from ex- (“out”) + facere (“to do, make”)) + -alis (adjective suffix).
