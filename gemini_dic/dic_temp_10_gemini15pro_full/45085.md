cubited:/ˈkjuːbɪtɪd/
adj.
以肘为单位的；肘长的
Measured in cubits (an ancient unit of length based on the forearm).
The ark was three hundred cubits long, fifty cubits wide and thirty cubits high. 方舟长三百肘，宽五十肘，高三十肘。
Near synonyms:  forearm-length
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider: short, miniature, tiny)
Word origin: From Late Latin *cubitatus, from Latin cubitum ‘elbow’.
ETYMOLOGY-> From Late Latin *cubitatus, from Latin cubitum ‘elbow’.
