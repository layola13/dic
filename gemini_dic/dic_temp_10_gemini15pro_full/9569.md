antislavery:/ˌæntiˈsleɪvəri/
| n. | 反奴隶制 | The principle or practice of opposing slavery. |  Antislavery was a major political issue in the United States before the Civil War.  在美国内战前，反奴隶制是一个主要的政治问题。| synonyms: abolitionism | antonyms: proslavery | noun

Etymology:
anti- (prefix) + slavery (noun)

"anti-" comes from Greek "anti" meaning "against" or "opposite".

"slavery" comes from Old French "esclavage" from Medieval Latin "sclavus" meaning "Slav".  This is because many Slavs were enslaved during the Middle Ages.
