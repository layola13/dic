norma:/ˈnɔːrmə/ | n. | 规范，标准，准则 | A standard, model, or pattern; especially, an average standard of development or achievement. | The norma for blood pressure is around 120/80.  血压的标准值大约是 120/80。| synonym: standard, rule, criterion | antonym: anomaly, exception | noun

norma:/ˈnɔːrmə/ | n. | 定额，指标 | A prescribed or expected amount, quantity, or rate. |  The factory workers had to meet a daily norma. 工厂工人必须达到每日的生产定额。 | synonym: quota, target, allocation | antonym: surplus, excess | noun
