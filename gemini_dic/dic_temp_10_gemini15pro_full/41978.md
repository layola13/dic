cost-accountants:/kɒst əˈkaʊntənts/ | n. | 成本会计师 |  Professionals responsible for tracking, analyzing, and understanding the costs associated with running a business. | The cost-accountants prepared a detailed report on the company's manufacturing expenses. 成本会计师准备了一份关于公司制造成本的详细报告。|  The cost-accountants advised the management on strategies to reduce overhead costs. 成本会计师就降低管理费用的策略向管理层提供建议。 |Synonyms: management accountants, cost analysts | Antonyms:  N/A | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
"cost" (from Old French "cost" meaning "price, value") + "accountant" (from Old French "accompter" meaning "to count, reckon")
