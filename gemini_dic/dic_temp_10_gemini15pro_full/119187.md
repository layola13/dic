mezzotint: /ˈmetsəʊtɪnt/ | n. | 铜版雕刻(一种)；(用铜版雕刻法制成的)版画 | A method of engraving on copper or steel by scraping or burnishing a roughened surface to produce light and shade. A print made by this method. | The museum has a fine collection of 18th-century mezzotints.  这家博物馆收藏了大量18世纪的铜版画。| synonyms: engraving, etching, print | antonyms: None | noun

mezzotinter: /ˌmetsəˈtɪntər/ | n. | 铜版雕刻师 | An engraver who uses the mezzotint method. | The mezzotinter was renowned for his detailed portraits.  这位铜版雕刻师以其精细的肖像画而闻名。| synonyms: engraver, etcher, printmaker | antonyms: None | noun
