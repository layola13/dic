affixment:/əˈfɪksmənt/| n.|词缀添加，附加，贴上|The affixment of "-ed" to a verb usually indicates past tense.  在动词后加上“-ed”通常表示过去时。|The affixment of a label to the product is the final step.  将标签贴到产品上是最后一步。|近义词：attachment, addition, appending|反义词：removal, detachment|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From affix + -ment.  来自 affix（词缀）+ -ment（名词后缀，表示行为或结果）。
