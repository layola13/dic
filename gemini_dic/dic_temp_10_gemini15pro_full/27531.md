campylospermous:/ˌkæmpɪloʊˈspɜːrməs/
adjective
弯籽的；弯种子的
Having seeds with a curved embryo.
The campylospermous ovules are a distinctive feature of this plant family.  这种植物科的弯曲胚珠是一个显著特征。
This genus is characterized by its campylospermous seeds.  这个属的特征是其弯曲的种子。

近义词:curved, incurved
反义词:straight

词性:adjective

词源: From Greek *kampylos* (curved) + *sperma* (seed).
