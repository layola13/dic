caffeine:/ˈkæfiːn/ /kæˈfiːn/| n.|咖啡因；咖啡碱|A crystalline compound that is found especially in tea and coffee plants and is a stimulant of the central nervous system.|I need a cup of coffee to get my caffeine fix in the morning. 我早上需要一杯咖啡来补充咖啡因。|Synonyms: stimulant, energizer|Antonyms: depressant, sedative|noun
