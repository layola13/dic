nettier:/ˈnɛtiər/| adj.|更整洁的，更干净的|Comparative form of "netty": more neat and tidy, especially in appearance.  Relating to or resembling a net.  (Often used in a metaphorical sense to describe something intricate or complex.)|Her handwriting is nettier than mine. 她的字迹比我的整洁。 The nettier solution avoided unnecessary complexity. 更简洁的解决方案避免了不必要的复杂性。|近义词：tidier, neater, cleaner, more organized|反义词:messier, dirtier, more disorganized|词性:形容词

词源: From "net" (referring to a mesh fabric) + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives). The comparative form "nettier" is formed by adding "-er".
