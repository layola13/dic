commonership:/ˈkɒmənəʃɪp/ /ˈkɑːmənərʃɪp/| n. |共同所有权，共同拥有|The state of jointly owning something with others.  This implies shared rights, responsibilities, and benefits associated with the ownership.|例句：They established a commonership of the land, sharing both the profits and the upkeep costs. 他们建立了土地的共同所有权，共同承担利润和维护费用。

例句：Commonership of the family business caused tension among the siblings.  家族企业的共同所有权在兄弟姐妹之间造成了紧张关系。|近义词：joint ownership, co-ownership, shared ownership|反义词:sole ownership, individual ownership|词性:noun
