barnstormed:/ˈbɑːrnstɔːrmd/
verb
巡回演出，巡回演讲，到处游说
travelled around an area making speeches or performing, especially as part of a political campaign.
例句：The candidate barnstormed across the state, hoping to win votes. 候选人在全州巡回演讲，希望能赢得选票。

例句：The theatrical troupe barnstormed through the countryside, putting on plays in small towns.  剧团在乡下巡回演出，在小镇上演戏剧。

近义词：toured, campaigned, canvassed
反义词：stayed put
