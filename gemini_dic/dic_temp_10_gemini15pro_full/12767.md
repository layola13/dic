assignee:/əˌsaɪˈniː/| n.|受让人，被指派人|A person to whom a right or liability is legally transferred.|The assignee will receive all rights and obligations under the contract.  受让人将获得合同项下的所有权利和义务。|近义词：recipient, transferee|反义词: assignor|noun

assignee:/əˌsaɪˈniː/| n.|代理人，受托人|A person appointed to act on behalf of another.|The assignee managed the property during the owner's absence. 代理人在业主不在期间管理房产。|近义词：agent, representative|反义词: principal|noun
