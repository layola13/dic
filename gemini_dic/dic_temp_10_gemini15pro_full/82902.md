gumbo:/ˈɡʌmboʊ/| n.|秋葵浓汤；秋葵；混合物；混乱|A stew thickened with okra pods, often containing meat or seafood and vegetables.  A mixture of many things.|例句：She made a delicious gumbo for dinner. 她晚饭做了一份美味的秋葵浓汤。

例句：The situation was a real gumbo of conflicting opinions.  情况是一团乱麻，各种意见相互冲突。|近义词：stew, mix, medley, jumble|反义词:order, organization|noun
