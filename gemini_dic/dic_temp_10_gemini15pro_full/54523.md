disk-braked:/ˈdɪsk breɪkt/
| adj. | 盘式制动的 | Equipped with disc brakes.  A system where the brakes are applied to a disc rather than a drum. |  The disk-braked car stopped quickly.  那辆盘式制动汽车很快就停了下来。 | Example: Many modern bicycles are disk-braked for better stopping power. 许多现代自行车都配备了盘式制动器，以获得更好的制动力。| Synonyms: disc brake equipped,  | Antonyms: drum-braked | adjective
