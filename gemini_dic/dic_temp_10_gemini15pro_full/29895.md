cauterization:/ˌkɔːtərɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. | 烧灼；烙术 |The process of burning tissue to stop bleeding or prevent infection.  |The doctor performed a cauterization to seal the wound. 医生进行了烧灼来封闭伤口。|Synonyms: cautery, sear, branding|Antonyms: healing, regeneration|noun

cauterize:/ˈkɔːtəraɪz/| vt. | 烧灼；烙 |To burn tissue with a hot iron, electric current, or caustic substance. |The surgeon cauterized the blood vessel to stop the bleeding.  外科医生烧灼血管以止血。|Synonyms: sear, burn, brand|Antonyms: heal, soothe|verb
