aleck:/ˈælɪk/| n.|自作聪明的人，爱出风头的人|A person, typically a man, who is regarded as clever in a smug, irritating way.  Someone who is annoyingly and ostentatiously clever or witty.  A conceited, know-it-all person.|例句：He's such an aleck, always showing off how much he knows. 他真是个自作聪明的人，总是炫耀他知道多少东西。

例句：Don't be such an aleck; nobody likes a know-it-all.  别那么自作聪明；没人喜欢自以为是的人。|近义词：smart aleck, wise guy, know-it-all, show-off|反义词:humble person, modest person|词性:noun
