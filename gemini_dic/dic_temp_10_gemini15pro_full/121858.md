misrepresenters:/ˌmɪsˌrɛprɪˈzɛntərz/
|n.|歪曲事实者；误传者；作不实陈述者
|People who misrepresent facts, often deliberately, are called misrepresenters.  那些歪曲事实的人，通常是故意的，被称为歪曲事实者。
|The advertisement was misleading, and the company was accused of being misrepresenters of their product. 这则广告具有误导性，该公司被指控对其产品进行虚假陈述。
|近义词: liars, deceivers, falsifiers, distorters
|反义词: truth-tellers, honest reporters
