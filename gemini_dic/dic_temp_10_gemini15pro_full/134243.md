noninfectious:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfekʃəs/| adj.|非传染性的，非感染性的|Not capable of being transmitted through infection; not caused by infection.|The common cold is infectious, but arthritis is noninfectious.|近义词：non-contagious, noncommunicable|反义词:infectious, contagious|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- + infectious. "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "infectious" comes from the Latin "inficere," meaning "to taint, to infect."
