overtruthfulness:/ˌoʊvərˈtruːθfʊlnəs/
n.
过度诚实，过分坦率
The state of being excessively truthful, often to the point of being blunt or insensitive.
His overtruthfulness sometimes offended people, even though he meant no harm. 他的过度诚实有时会冒犯别人，即使他没有恶意。
近义词:candor, frankness, honesty
反义词:dishonesty, deception, evasiveness
词性:noun


Etymology:
"over-" (prefix) + "truthfulness"
"over-" comes from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, across."
"truthfulness" comes from "truthful" + "-ness."
"truthful" comes from "truth" + "-ful."
"truth" comes from Old English "trīewþ," meaning "faith, fidelity, loyalty."
"-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
"-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state, quality, or condition.
