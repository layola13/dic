morrice:/ˈmɒrɪs/ (UK) /ˈmɔːrɪs/ (US)| n.|莫里斯舞；一种古代的英国民间舞蹈|A traditional English folk dance involving rhythmic stepping and the clashing of sticks or swords, often performed by men with blackened faces.|Morris dancers were a common sight at village fetes in the past. 过去，莫里斯舞者是乡村节日的常见景象。|Synonyms: folk dance, traditional dance||noun
morrice:/ˈmɒrɪs/ (UK) /ˈmɔːrɪs/ (US)| v.|跳莫里斯舞|To perform the morris dance.|They morriced through the streets, bells jingling on their legs. 他们跳着莫里斯舞穿过街道，腿上的铃铛叮当作响。|Synonyms: dance, perform||verb
