grammarless:/ˈɡræmərləs/| adj.|无语法规则的，不通顺的|Lacking proper grammar or following conventional grammatical rules; ungrammatical.|His grammarless sentences were difficult to understand. 他的不通顺的句子很难理解。|The email he sent was completely grammarless and unprofessional. 他发的邮件完全不通顺，很不专业。|近义词：ungrammatical, incoherent, flawed|反义词: grammatical, coherent, correct|词性:形容词

词源: "grammar" + "-less"  "grammar" 来自古法语 grammaire，最终源自希腊语 grammatikē (tekhnē)  "art of letters,"  "-less" 是一个古英语后缀，表示“没有”或“缺乏”。
