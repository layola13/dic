interjangle:/ˌɪntərˈdʒæŋɡl/
|v.|使纠缠；使混乱|To mix up or entangle in a confused or disordered manner.|The headphone wires were hopelessly interjangled. 耳机线完全缠在一起了。|近义词：tangle, entwine, interweave, intertwine|反义词：untangle, disentangle, separate|词性：动词

interjangle:/ˌɪntərˈdʒæŋɡl/
|n.|混乱；纠缠|A confused or disordered mass; a tangle.|The room was an interjangle of clothes and books. 房间里衣服和书乱成一团。|近义词：tangle, mess, jumble, muddle|反义词：order, neatness|词性：名词

词源:  A blend of *inter-* (meaning "between, among") and *jangle* (meaning "to make a harsh, discordant sound," also "to irritate, upset").  The sense of confusion likely arises from the idea of things clashing or grating against each other.
