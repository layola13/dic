leave-taking:/ˈliːvˌteɪkɪŋ/
n.
告别，告辞
The act of saying goodbye; a farewell.
We watched their emotional leave-taking at the airport. 我们在机场看着他们依依惜别的场面。

近义词:farewell, goodbye, parting, valediction
反义词:arrival, greeting, welcome
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English leve-takyng, from leve, "permission to depart" (from Old English lēaf "permission") + taking,  "the action of laying hold of something" (from Old English takung).
