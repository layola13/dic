cholesteric:/kəˌlɛstəˈrɪk/| adj.|胆甾型的，胆甾醇的|Relating to or characteristic of cholesterols; containing or resembling cholesterol.  A type of liquid crystal that exhibits a helical structure.|Cholesteric liquid crystals are used in some types of displays. 胆甾型液晶用于某些类型的显示器。|The cholesteric phase is a special case of the nematic phase. 胆甾相是向列相的一种特殊情况。|近义词:cholesterol-containing, liquid crystalline|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-cholesteric)|词性:adjective
