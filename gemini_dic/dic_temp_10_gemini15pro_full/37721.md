PHRASE->
compare to = 比作；与…相比
compare with = 与…比较；与…匹敌
compare notes = 交换意见；核对笔记
beyond compare = 无与伦比
compare favorably/unfavorably with =  比…好/不好

词源：From Middle English comparen, from Old French comparer, from Late Latin comparare, present active infinitive of comparo (“I couple, match, compare”), from com- (“together”) + paro (“I prepare”).

注意事项:  "compare to" 强调相似性，而 "compare with" 强调相似性和差异性。
