词源:
From Middle English *naked, *nakid, from Old English nacod (“naked, bare, nude”), from Proto-Germanic *nakwaða (“naked”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)nogʷʰ-os (“naked”). Cognate with West Frisian neaken (“naked”), Dutch naakt (“naked”), German nackt (“naked”), Danish nøgen (“naked”), Swedish naken (“naked”), Icelandic nakinn (“naked”).
