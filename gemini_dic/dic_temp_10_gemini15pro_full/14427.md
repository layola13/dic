autumnity: /ˌɔːˈtʌmnɪti/
n.
秋天的特性；秋天的气氛；秋天的景色
The quality or state of being autumnal; the characteristics of autumn.
The air held a distinct autumnity, crisp and cool. 空气中弥漫着明显的秋天气息，清爽而凉爽。
近义词:  autumnalness, fall
反义词: springiness, vernality
词性: 名词
