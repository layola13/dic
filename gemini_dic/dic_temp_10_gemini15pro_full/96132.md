indemnifying:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪfaɪɪŋ/
verb
补偿；赔偿
present participle of indemnify, meaning to compensate for harm or loss.  Protecting someone from financial loss.
The company is indemnifying its employees against legal action.  该公司正在保障其员工免受法律诉讼的影响。
compensating, securing, protecting
prosecuting, penalizing
v.
