firmity:/ˈfɜːrmɪti/ /ˈfɝːmɪti/| n.|（古）(尤指老年人身体) 虚弱；不坚固|His firmity prevented him from travelling. 他身体虚弱，无法旅行。|近义词：infirmity, weakness, frailty|反义词: strength, robustness, health|noun
