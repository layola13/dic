chairlifts:/ˈtʃɛərˌlɪfts/ | n. | 吊椅式缆车，滑雪缆车 | A type of aerial lift used to transport skiers and snowboarders uphill, consisting of a series of chairs suspended from a moving cable. |  We rode the chairlift to the top of the mountain. 我们乘坐吊椅缆车到达山顶。| synonyms: ski lift, gondola, cable car | antonyms: None | noun
