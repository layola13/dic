laurits:/ˈlaʊrɪts/| n. |劳里茨|A Scandinavian given name, a variant of Lawrence, meaning "from Laurentum" (a city in ancient Italy).  Ultimately derived from the Latin "laurus," meaning "laurel."|Laurits is a popular name in Denmark. 劳里茨在丹麦是一个很受欢迎的名字。| Laurits won the science fair. 劳里茨赢得了科学展览会。| 近义词:Lawrence, Lars|反义词: N/A| Noun
