batching:/ˈbætʃɪŋ/
n.
批量处理，分批处理
The act or process of grouping similar tasks or items together for processing or handling as a single unit.
Batching emails can save you time.  批量处理电子邮件可以节省你的时间。
The bakery does all its cake batching on Wednesdays. 这家面包店每星期三批量制作蛋糕。
grouping, bundling, collecting
individualizing, separating
noun
