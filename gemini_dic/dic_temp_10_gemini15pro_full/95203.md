例句：He sensed an inauthenticity in her forced smile. 他感觉到她强颜欢笑中的虚伪。|近义词：falseness, artificiality, phoniness, insincerity |反义词：authenticity, genuineness, sincerity|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + authentic + -ity (noun suffix).  "Authentic" comes from Late Latin authenticus, from Greek authentikos "original, genuine," from authentes "one acting on one's own authority," from autos "self" + hentes "doer, being."
