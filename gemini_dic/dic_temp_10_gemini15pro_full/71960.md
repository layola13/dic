flightinesses:/ˈflaɪtinɪsɪz/
n.
轻浮；反复无常；易变
The state of being flighty; capriciousness.
Her flightinesses made it difficult to rely on her. 她的反复无常让人难以信赖她。
Synonyms: capriciousness, fickleness, volatility, whimsicality, levity, frivolity
Antonyms: steadiness, stability, constancy, seriousness, gravity
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

flightiness:/ˈflaɪtinɪs/
n.
轻浮；反复无常；易变
The state of being flighty; capriciousness.
His flightiness made him a poor choice for a leadership role. 他的轻浮使他成为领导角色的不二人选。
Synonyms: capriciousness, fickleness, volatility, whimsicality, levity, frivolity
Antonyms: steadiness, stability, constancy, seriousness, gravity
词性: 名词 (noun)

flighty:/ˈflaɪti/
adj.
轻浮的；反复无常的；易变的
Given to capricious or whimsical behavior; fickle.
She's a bit flighty and changes her mind constantly. 她有点轻浮，经常改变主意。
Synonyms: capricious, fickle, volatile, whimsical, frivolous, giddy
Antonyms: steady, stable, constant, serious, grounded
词性: 形容词 (adjective)
