awkward:/ˈɔːkwərd/ (US) /ˈɔːkwəd/ (UK)| adj.|笨拙的；尴尬的；棘手的；难处理的|Lacking grace or skill in movement or behavior; causing embarrassment or inconvenience; difficult to handle or deal with.|例句：He felt awkward dancing with such a tall partner. 他觉得自己和这么高的舞伴跳舞很笨拙。|例句：There was an awkward silence after his inappropriate joke.  在他讲了个不合时宜的笑话之后，现场陷入了尴尬的沉默。|近义词：clumsy, ungainly, embarrassing, uncomfortable, tricky|反义词：graceful, elegant, comfortable, convenient, easy|词性:形容词
