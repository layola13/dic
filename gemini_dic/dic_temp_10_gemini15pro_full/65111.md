etherification:/iːθərɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 醚化（作用） | The process of forming an ether, typically by reacting an alcohol with another alcohol or a phenol in the presence of an acid catalyst. |  Ethyl ether is produced by the intermolecular etherification of ethanol. 乙醚是由乙醇的分子间醚化反应生成的。

The Williamson ether synthesis is a common method of etherification. 威廉姆森醚合成是醚化反应的常用方法。 | 近义词: alkylation | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
