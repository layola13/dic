disproof's:/dɪsˈpruːfs/
noun
反驳；反证
The act of proving something to be false or wrong.
The scientist offered a disproof of the theory.  科学家提出了该理论的反证。
synonyms: refutation, rebuttal, contradiction, negation
antonyms: proof, confirmation, verification, validation
n.
