museums:/mjuːˈziːəmz/
noun
博物馆（museum的复数）
Buildings or institutions dedicated to the acquisition, conservation, study, exhibition, and educational interpretation of objects having scientific, historical, or artistic value.
We visited several museums during our trip to Europe.  我们在欧洲之旅中参观了几个博物馆。
The city's museums are a major tourist attraction.  这座城市的博物馆是一个主要的旅游景点。
synonyms: galleries, collections, archives
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a place, could consider concepts like ignorance, destruction)
