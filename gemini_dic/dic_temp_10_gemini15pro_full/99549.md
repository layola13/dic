intergrave:/ɪnˈtɜːrɡreɪv/ | v. | 埋葬；雕刻；铭记 | To bury (a corpse) in a grave; to carve (an inscription or design) deeply into a surface; to impress deeply (as on the memory). |  He intergraved his wife's name on her tombstone. 他将妻子的名字刻在了她的墓碑上。

The experience was intergraved on his memory. 那次经历深深地铭刻在他的记忆中。 | 近义词: bury, engrave, inscribe, imprint | 反义词: exhume, erase | 词性: 动词
