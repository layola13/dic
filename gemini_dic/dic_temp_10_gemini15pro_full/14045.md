austere:/ɔːˈstɪə(r)/ /ɑːˈstɪr/
adjective
朴素的，简朴的；严厉的；苦行的
Severe or strict in manner, attitude, or appearance.  Characterized by or suggesting a lack of comfort and luxury; unadorned.
例句：The monk lived an austere life, devoid of luxury.  这位僧侣过着简朴的生活，没有任何奢侈品。

例句：Her austere office had only a desk, a chair, and a computer. 她简朴的办公室里只有一张桌子、一把椅子和一台电脑。

近义词：stern, strict, severe, harsh, ascetic, simple, plain, unadorned
反义词：luxurious, lavish, ornate, indulgent, lenient
词性: adjective
