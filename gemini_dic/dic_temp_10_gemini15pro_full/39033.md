conflicted:/kənˈflɪktɪd/ | adj. | 矛盾的，冲突的 | Experiencing conflicting emotions or desires; torn between opposing impulses. | She felt conflicted about leaving her job, even though it was stressful. 她对离开自己的工作感到矛盾，尽管这份工作压力很大。| conflicted:/kənˈflɪktɪd/ | adj. | 矛盾的，冲突的 | He was conflicted about whether to tell the truth or protect his friend. 他很矛盾，不知道是该说实话还是保护他的朋友。 | 近义词: ambivalent, torn, uncertain, hesitant | 反义词: resolute, decisive, certain | 词性: adjective

词源: From the verb "conflict" + "-ed."  "Conflict" comes from the Latin "conflictus," meaning "a striking together," from "confligere," meaning "to strike together," from "com-" (together) + "fligere" (to strike).
