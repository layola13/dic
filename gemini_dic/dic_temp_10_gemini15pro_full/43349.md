cram:/kræm/| v. | 塞满；填鸭式学习；临时抱佛脚 | to force a lot of things into a small space; to study hard in a short time just before an exam | I crammed all my clothes into the suitcase. 我把所有的衣服都塞进了手提箱。

She's cramming for her history exam. 她正在为历史考试临时抱佛脚。 | synonyms: stuff, pack, jam, swot (British), grind | antonyms: empty, unpack | verb
