atrophy:/ˈætrəfi/ (US) /ˈætrəfi/ (UK)| n. v.|萎缩，衰退|A wasting away or diminution.  A decrease in size of an organ or tissue.|例句：Muscle atrophy can occur after a prolonged period of inactivity.  长时间不活动后肌肉会萎缩。|近义词：degeneration, decline, deterioration, wasting|反义词：growth, development, strengthening|词性: noun, verb

atrophy:/ˈætrəfi/ (US) /ˈætrəfi/ (UK)| v. |萎缩，衰退|(of body tissue or an organ) waste away, typically due to the degeneration of cells, or become vestigial during evolution.|例句：Her artistic skills atrophied from lack of use. 由于缺乏使用，她的艺术技能退化了。|近义词：wither, shrink, decay|反义词：grow, develop, flourish|词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *atrophie*, from Late Latin *atrophia*, from Greek *atrophia* "a wasting away," from *atrophes* "ill-fed, without nourishment," from *a-* "without" + *trophe* "nourishment."
