in your wheelhouse:/ɪn jər ˈwiːlhaʊs/| idiom |在你的能力范围内，你的擅长领域|Something that is "in your wheelhouse" is something you are good at or have experience with. It refers to tasks or situations that are within your comfort zone and skill set.|例句：Negotiating deals is definitely in his wheelhouse.  谈判交易绝对是他的强项。

例句：This type of project is right in my wheelhouse. 这种类型的项目正是我擅长的。|近义词：forte, specialty, strength, area of expertise|反义词: weakness, shortcoming|phrase
