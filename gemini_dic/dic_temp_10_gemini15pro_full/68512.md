fanaticism:/fəˈnætɪsɪzəm/
n.
狂热; 盲信
excessive enthusiasm or zeal; extreme devotion to a belief or cause
His fanaticism for the cause bordered on obsession. 他对这项事业的狂热近乎痴迷。
近义词：zealotry, extremism, bigotry
反义词：moderation, tolerance, apathy
词性：名词

fanaticisms:/fəˈnætɪsɪzəmz/
n.
狂热行为（fanaticism的复数形式）
instances of excessive enthusiasm or zeal; extreme devotions to beliefs or causes
The various fanaticisms of the 20th century caused untold suffering.  20世纪的各种狂热行为造成了难以言喻的苦难。
近义词：zealotries, extremisms, bigotries
反义词：moderations, tolerances, apathies
词性：名词
