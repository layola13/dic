begoning:  This is not a correctly spelled English word.  It's likely a misspelling of "beginning."  Therefore, the following analysis is for "beginning."

/bɪˈɡɪnɪŋ/ | n. adj.| 开始，开端; 起初的，开始的 |The beginning of a story is often the most important part.  故事的开头通常是最重要的部分。|例句：The beginning of the movie was a bit slow.  这部电影的开头有点慢。|近义词：start, commencement, outset, onset, inception |反义词: end, conclusion, finish, termination |词性: noun, adjective

Etymology: From Middle English beginninge, equivalent to begin + -ing.
