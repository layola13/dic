enascent:/ɪˈnæsənt/ /iˈneɪsənt/ | adj. | 初生的，新兴的 | Beginning to exist or develop; inchoate. |  The conference highlighted the nascent electric vehicle industry.  这次会议重点介绍了新兴的电动汽车行业。

The nascent democracy is still fragile.  这个新生的民主仍然很脆弱。 | 近义词：emerging, budding, developing, incipient, burgeoning | 反义词：declining, waning, mature, established, dying | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *nascens, present participle of nāscor (“to be born”).
