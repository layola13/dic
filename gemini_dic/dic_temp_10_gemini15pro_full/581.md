abounding:/əˈbaʊndɪŋ/
adjective
丰富的，大量的
Present participle of abound, meaning to exist in large numbers or amounts. Characterized by great plenty; plentiful.
The river is abounding with fish. = 这条河里鱼很多。
The garden was abounding in beautiful flowers. = 花园里开满了美丽的花朵。
synonyms: plentiful, abundant, copious, profuse, teeming, overflowing, replete, rife, swarming, awash
antonyms: scarce, scant, meager, sparse, deficient, lacking
词性: 形容词

USAGE->
例句1: The forest is abounding with wildlife. = 森林里充满了野生动物。
例句2: Opportunities are abounding in this growing city. = 在这个不断发展的城市里，机会比比皆是。


PHRASE->
abounding in = 富含...
abounding with = 充满...
a region abounding in natural resources = 一个自然资源丰富的地区
a life abounding in happiness = 幸福的生活
an area abounding with opportunities = 一个充满机会的地区
