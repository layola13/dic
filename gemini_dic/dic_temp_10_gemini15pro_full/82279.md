groundsman:/ˈɡraʊndzmən/ (UK), /ˈɡraʊndzmæn/ (US)| n. |场地管理员，园丁|A person who takes care of a sports ground, park, or other outdoor area.  This includes maintaining the grass, preparing playing surfaces, and general upkeep.|The groundsman was busy mowing the cricket pitch. 场地管理员正忙着修剪板球场。|Synonyms: groundskeeper, caretaker, custodian|Antonyms: vandal, saboteur|noun
