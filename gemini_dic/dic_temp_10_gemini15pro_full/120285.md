miniaturize:/ˈmɪnɪətʃəraɪz/ /ˈmɪnɪətʃəˌraɪz/| vt.|使小型化；微型化|To make something much smaller than its usual size.|Scientists are working to miniaturize electronic devices. 科学家们正在努力使电子设备小型化。|The company miniaturized the camera to fit inside a pen.  这家公司把相机微型化以适应笔的内部。|reduce, shrink, compress|enlarge, expand|verb
Etymology->
From miniature + -ize.
