|unfazed, unaffected, untouched|

cut-to-the-quick:/kʌt tə ðə kwɪk/
|adjective|
|伤透了心的，深深地刺痛的 (较少用作形容词，通常作为idiom)|
|Deeply hurt or wounded emotionally.|
|She felt cut to the quick by his betrayal.|他的背叛让她感到伤透了心。|
|deeply hurt, deeply wounded, devastated, heartbroken|
|unfazed, unaffected, untouched|
|phrase|


ETYMOLOGY->
From the "quick" of the fingernail or toenail, which is the sensitive flesh underneath.  Cutting into this area causes sharp pain. The phrase was then extended to emotional pain.
