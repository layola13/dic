counterargument:/ˈkaʊntərˌɑːrɡjʊmənt/ | n. | 反驳；抗辩 | An argument made to oppose another argument. |  He presented a strong counterargument to the proposal. 他对这项提议提出了强有力的反驳。 |  rebuttal, objection, confutation | argument, support, confirmation | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- (against, opposing) + argument.  The prefix "counter-" comes from the Old French "contre-" ultimately derived from the Latin "contra" meaning "against".
