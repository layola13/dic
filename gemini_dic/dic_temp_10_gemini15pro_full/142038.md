organoid:/ˈɔːrɡənɔɪd/| n. |类器官|A miniature, simplified version of an organ produced in vitro in three dimensions that shows realistic micro-anatomy. |Scientists are growing brain organoids to study neurological diseases. 科学家正在培养大脑类器官来研究神经系统疾病。|
organoid:/ˈɔːrɡənɔɪd/| n. |类器官|A structure that resembles an organ. |The tumor formed an organoid mass. 肿瘤形成了一个类似器官的肿块。|
organoid:/ˈɔːrɡənɔɪd/| n. |类器官|Resembling an organ in structure or function. |The organoid tissue displayed similar cellular activity to a real kidney. 这种类器官组织表现出与真实肾脏相似的细胞活动。|
Synonyms: organ-like, mini-organ, tissue construct
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps "disorganized tissue" or "cell culture" in some contexts)
