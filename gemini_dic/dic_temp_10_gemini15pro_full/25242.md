buffability:/ˌbʌfəˈbɪləti/
n.
可增益性，可强化性
The ability of a character or item in a game to be improved or enhanced through buffs.
This new character has incredible buffability, making her a top choice for experienced players.  这个新角色有着惊人的可增益性，使她成为经验丰富玩家的首选。
Near Synonyms:  enhanceability, improvability, upgradability
Antonyms:  unimprovability, inflexibility, rigidity
noun
