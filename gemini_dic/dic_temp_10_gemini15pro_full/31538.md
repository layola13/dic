chartering:/ˈtʃɑːrtərɪŋ/ | n. | 包租；租赁（尤指飞机、船舶等）；特许；给予特权 | The act of hiring or renting something, especially a plane, ship, or bus, for exclusive use. |  The company specializes in chartering private jets for business executives.  这家公司专门为企业高管包租私人飞机。| synonyms: hiring, renting, leasing | antonyms: selling, returning | noun

chartering:/ˈtʃɑːrtərɪŋ/ | v. | 包租；租赁；特许；给予…特权 |  To hire or rent something, especially a plane, ship, or bus, for exclusive use. | They chartered a bus to take them to the concert. 他们租了一辆巴士送他们去音乐会。 | synonyms: hire, rent, lease | antonyms: sell, return | verb
