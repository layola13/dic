hyperpureness:/ˌhaɪpərˈpjʊrnəs/ | n. |极度纯洁，超级纯净|  The state or quality of being extremely pure. |  The scientist was impressed by the hyperpureness of the sample. 科学家对样本的极度纯净印象深刻。| 近义词: extreme purity, absolute purity | 反义词: impurity, contamination | 词性: 名词

词源: hyper- (prefix meaning "over, excessive") + pureness (noun form of "pure")  "hyper-" 源自希腊语 "hyper," 意思是 "over" 或 "above." "pureness" 来自古法语 "pur," 最终源自拉丁语 "purus."
