depreciatory:/dɪˈpriːʃəˌtɔːri/ /dɪˈpriːsiəˌtɔːri/
adjective
贬低的，轻视的
Expressing disapproval; tending to diminish or disparage.
Her depreciatory remarks about his work made him feel discouraged. 她对他工作的贬低性评论使他感到气馁。
synonyms: disparaging, belittling, derogatory, demeaning
antonyms: complimentary, praising, laudatory
词性: adjective
