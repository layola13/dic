eternalist:/ɪˈtɜːrnəlɪst/ | n. | 永恒主义者；永恒论者 |  Someone who believes in eternalism, the philosophical view that all points in time are equally real. |  The eternalist argued that the past, present, and future all exist simultaneously.  永恒主义者认为过去、现在和未来同时存在。| 近义词:perennialist (比较偏重教育方面) | 反义词:presentist | 词性:名词

eternalist:/ɪˈtɜːrnəlɪst/ | adj. | 永恒主义的；永恒论的 | Relating to eternalism. |  He holds an eternalist view of time. 他持有永恒主义的时间观。 | 近义词:perennial (比较偏重教育方面) | 反义词:presentist | 词性:形容词
