illacrymable:/ˌɪləˈkraɪməbəl/
adjective
不易落泪的; 不催人泪下的
not causing or tending to cause tears.
The movie was surprisingly illacrymable, despite its tragic subject matter. 这部电影出奇地不催人泪下，尽管它的主题很悲惨。
近义词: unemotional, unmoving, unsentimental
反义词: tearjerking, moving, sentimental
词性: adjective

词源:
From in- (not) + Latin lacrima (tear) + -able (capable of being).  Essentially meaning "not able to be cried over".
