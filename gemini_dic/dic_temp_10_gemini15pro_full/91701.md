hyperconscientiousness:/ˌhaɪpərˌkɒnʃiˈɛnʃəsnəs/ | n. | 过度认真；过度尽责 |  The state of being excessively conscientious, characterized by extreme attention to detail, meticulousness, and a strong sense of duty or obligation, often to the point of being detrimental to one's well-being. |  Her hyperconscientiousness made it difficult for her to delegate tasks. 她过度认真，很难把任务委托给别人。 |  scrupulousness, meticulousness, punctiliousness | carelessness, negligence | noun
