cure-alls:/ˈkjʊrɔːlz/
noun
万能药; 万灵丹
A cure-all is a medicine or other remedy that is claimed to cure all diseases or solve all problems.
Many people consider vitamin C to be a cure-all, but scientific evidence doesn't support this claim. 很多人认为维生素C是万能药，但科学证据并不支持这种说法。
synonyms: panacea, remedy, elixir, nostrum
antonyms: placebo, ineffective treatment
