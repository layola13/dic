disgraceful:/dɪsˈɡreɪsfəl/
adjective
丢脸的，可耻的
Bringing shame or disgrace. Causing shame or disgrace.
His behavior was disgraceful. 他的行为很丢脸。
近义词: shameful, dishonorable, ignominious, scandalous
反义词: honorable, respectable, creditable
词性: adjective

词源:
From dis- + graceful.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of." "Graceful" comes from "grace," meaning elegance or refinement, ultimately from Latin "gratia."  Therefore, "disgraceful" literally means "not graceful" or "lacking grace."
