coloboma:/ˌkɒləˈbəʊmə/ (UK), /ˌkɑːləˈboʊmə/ (US)| n. |缺损，先天性裂|A congenital defect or fissure, especially of the eye, but also other parts of the body, typically involving the iris, eyelid, or retina.|
The child was born with a coloboma of the iris.  这个孩子出生时患有虹膜缺损。

The coloboma affected his vision significantly.  这个缺损严重影响了他的视力。

|近义词：defect, fissure, malformation|反义词: normalcy, wholeness|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *κολοβός (kolobós, “docked, curtailed, mutilated”) +‎ -oma. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gel- (“cold, to congeal”).
