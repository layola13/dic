overtheatrical: /ˌoʊvərθiˈætrɪkəl/
adjective
过于戏剧化的；夸张的
Excessively theatrical; exaggerated or melodramatic in style or behavior.
Her reaction to the news was overtheatrical, complete with gasping and clutching her chest. 她对这个消息的反应过于戏剧化，又是喘气又是捂住胸口。
He delivered his lines in an overtheatrical manner, which made the audience laugh. 他用一种过于戏剧化的方式念台词，这使得观众大笑。
近义词: histrionic, melodramatic, exaggerated, affected
反义词: understated, subtle, restrained, natural
词性: adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "theatrical" (adjective relating to the theater)
