nondemobilization:/ˌnɒndɪˌmoʊbɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 非复员，不遣散 | The state of not being demobilized; the continued maintenance of armed forces at a high level of readiness after the cessation of hostilities.  The nondemobilization of troops led to increased military spending. |  The government's policy of nondemobilization kept the army at full strength.  非复员政策使军队保持了满员状态。| Synonyms:  retention, maintenance,  continuance | Antonyms: demobilization, discharge, disbandment | noun
