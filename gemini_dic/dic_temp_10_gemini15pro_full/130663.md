nonadventurously:/ˌnɒnədˈventʃərəsli/ | adv. | 不冒险地，缺乏冒险精神地 |  In a way that avoids risks or excitement; in a safe and conventional manner. |  She lived her life nonadventurously, preferring routine and familiarity. 她一生过得缺乏冒险精神，更喜欢常规和熟悉的事物。 |  cautiously, timidly, conservatively | adventurously, daringly, boldly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + adventurously.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of."  "Adventurously" is the adverb form of "adventurous," which comes from the Old French "aventure" (adventure) ultimately deriving from the Latin "adventura" (a thing about to happen).
