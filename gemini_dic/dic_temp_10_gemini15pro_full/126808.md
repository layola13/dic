nap's:/næps/| n.|小睡，午睡|It's nap time for the baby. 这是婴儿的小睡时间。|例句：The cat is enjoying its nap in the sun. 猫在阳光下享受着它的午睡。|近义词：sleep, doze, siesta|反义词:wakefulness|词性:名词
词源: Contraction of "nap is" or "nap has".  Ultimately from Old English *hnappian, "to doze, slumber."
