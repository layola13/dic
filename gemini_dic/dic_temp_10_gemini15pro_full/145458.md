overcivilize: /ˌoʊvərˈsɪvəlaɪz/
| v. |  过度文明化，过分教化 | To make excessively refined or sophisticated, often implying a loss of naturalness or vigor. | Don't overcivilize your children; let them experience the outdoors. 不要过度教化你的孩子；让他们体验户外活动。| refine excessively, overcultivate |  neglect,  ignore | verb
