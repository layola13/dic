hive:/haɪv/| n. v.|蜂房，蜂巢；忙碌的场所；人群|A structure built by bees for storing honey and raising young; a place swarming with activity; a large number of people.|Bees store honey in their hive. 蜜蜂将蜂蜜储存在它们的蜂巢中。|The market was a hive of activity. 市场熙熙攘攘，非常热闹。|近义词：apiary, nest, colony, swarm|反义词:|n. v.

Etymology:
From Old English *hīfe, from Proto-Germanic *hīwō, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)key- (“to cut, split”). Cognate with Dutch bijkorf (“beehive”), German Bienenkorb (“beehive”).  The verb sense developed later, from the noun.
