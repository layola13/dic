nonbeauties:/nɒnˈbjuːtiz/
noun
丑陋的人或事物; 不美的東西
lacking in beauty; not beautiful things or people.
The artist deliberately chose to depict nonbeauties in his work, challenging conventional notions of aesthetics.  这位艺术家特意选择在他的作品中描绘不美的事物，挑战了传统的审美观念。
Near synonyms: uglies, eyesores, plain things
Near antonyms: beauties, stunners, marvels
word type: plural noun
