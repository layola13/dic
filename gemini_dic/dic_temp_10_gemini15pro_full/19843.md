bind-over: /ˈbaɪnd ˌoʊvər/ | n. | 保释，具结，责令待审 | A legal obligation, imposed by a judge, requiring a person to appear in court at a later date or to behave in a certain way. | The judge placed him under a bind-over to keep the peace. 法官责令他具结保证遵守治安。 | synonyms: recognizance, surety, bail | antonyms: release, discharge | noun

bind-over: /ˈbaɪnd ˌoʊvər/ | v. | 使具结，责令待审 | To legally obligate someone to appear in court at a later date or to behave in a certain way. | The magistrate bound him over to appear at the Crown Court. 地方法官责令他到刑事法庭出庭。 | synonyms: obligate, compel, constrain | antonyms: release, discharge | verb
