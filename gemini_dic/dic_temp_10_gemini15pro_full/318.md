abetted: /əˈbɛtɪd/
verb
教唆；煽动；怂恿；支持
encouraged or assisted (someone) to do something wrong, in particular, to commit a crime or other offense.
He was abetted in the deception by his wife. = 他行骗受到了他妻子的怂恿。

The group was accused of abetting the terrorists. = 该组织被指控唆使恐怖分子。

近义词:  instigate, incite, encourage, aid, assist, support
反义词: deter, discourage, hinder, obstruct
词性: verb

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He abets his friend in skipping school. = 他怂恿他的朋友逃学。
She abets the crime by providing the getaway car. = 她通过提供逃跑车辆来教唆犯罪。

一般过去时:
The lawyer abetted his client in lying to the court. = 律师教唆他的客户向法庭撒谎。
They abetted the thief in stealing the jewels. = 他们怂恿小偷偷珠宝。

现在完成时:
He has abetted several criminals in their escape. = 他已经帮助几个罪犯逃脱。
She has abetted her brother in his illegal activities. = 她一直怂恿她哥哥从事非法活动。


过去完成时:
He had abetted the rebellion before the army arrived. = 在军队到来之前，他一直在煽动叛乱。
They had abetted the fraud for years before they were caught. = 他们在被抓之前已经进行多年的欺诈活动。

将来时:
If you abet this crime, you will go to jail. = 如果你教唆这起犯罪，你就会进监狱。
He will abet his friend's escape plan if he can. = 如果可以的话，他会帮助他的朋友实施逃跑计划。

虚拟语气:
If he were to abet the criminals, he would be arrested. = 如果他怂恿罪犯，他就会被逮捕。
I wish I hadn't abetted him in his wrongdoing. = 我希望我没有怂恿他做错事。

被动语态:
The escape was abetted by the prison guard. = 逃跑是由狱警协助的。
The crime was abetted by several individuals. = 犯罪是由几个人怂恿的。


疑问句:
Did he abet the robbery? = 他教唆抢劫了吗？
Who abetted the criminals? = 谁怂恿了罪犯？

否定句:
He did not abet the crime. = 他没有教唆犯罪。
She will not abet his illegal activities. = 她不会怂恿他的非法活动。


PHRASE->
abet in =  怂恿...做...

abet the commission of a crime = 怂恿犯罪
