chalked:/ˈtʃɔːkt/ | adj. | 用粉笔写的；用粉笔画的；记下的；<非正式>确定的，板上钉钉的 | marked, written, or drawn with chalk; <informal> certain, fixed, or definite |  The game plan was chalked up on the blackboard.  比赛计划用粉笔写在黑板上。 | 近义词: drawn, written, sketched, recorded, certain, definite | 反义词: uncertain, indefinite | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the noun "chalk" + "-ed" suffix, indicating a past participle or adjective form. Ultimately from Old English *cealc, from Latin calx "lime, limestone."
