leechery:/ˈliːtʃəri/| n. |好色；荒淫；纵欲|Excessive or offensive sexual desire; lustfulness.|His reputation was ruined by accusations of lechery and corruption. 他的名声因被指控好色和腐败而毁于一旦。|The king's lechery was legendary. 国王的荒淫是传奇性的。|近义词：lust, debauchery, lasciviousness, lewdness|反义词：chastity, abstinence, purity|词性:noun
词源:From Middle English *lecherie, from Old French lecherie, from lecheor (“glutton, lecher”), from lecher (“to lick”), of uncertain origin.
