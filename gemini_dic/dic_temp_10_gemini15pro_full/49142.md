deja:/ˈdeɪʒɑː/ (US), /ˈdeɪʒɑː/ (UK)| n.|似曾相识|The feeling that one has already lived through the present situation.|I had a strong sense of deja vu when I visited that town. |我访问那个小镇时，有一种强烈的似曾相识的感觉。|近义词：paramnesia|反义词: jamais vu|词性:名词

词源: French, from the past participle of voir ‘to see’.
