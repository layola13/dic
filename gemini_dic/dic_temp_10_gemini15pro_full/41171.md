copycatted:/ˈkɒpikætɪd/ |v.|模仿的，抄袭的|Copied or imitated the actions or work of another person.  Derived from "copycat" (verb) + "-ed" (past participle suffix). "Copycat" itself originates from the tendency of kittens to mimic their mother's actions.|He copycatted my design for the science project. 他抄袭了我科学项目的设计。|Synonyms: imitated, emulated, duplicated, mirrored|Antonyms: originated, innovated, pioneered|Verb (past participle)

copycat:/ˈkɒpikæt/ |n. v.|模仿者，抄袭者；模仿，抄袭|A person who copies another's behavior, work, etc. |The company was accused of being a copycat and stealing ideas from its competitors. 这家公司被指责抄袭并窃取其竞争对手的创意。|He copycats everything I do. 他模仿我做的每一件事。|Synonyms: imitator, impersonator, mimic|Antonyms: originator, innovator, pioneer|Noun, Verb
