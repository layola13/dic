hearselike:/ˈhɜːrslaɪk/| adj.|像灵车的；阴沉的；不祥的|Resembling a hearse, often in a gloomy or ominous way. Suggesting death or mourning.|The house had a hearselike quality, dark and imposing.|近义词：funereal, somber, gloomy, morbid|反义词：cheerful, lively, joyful|词性:形容词
Etymology:  'Hearse' (from Old French 'herse', a harrow-like frame for candles placed over a coffin) + '-like' (suffix indicating resemblance).
