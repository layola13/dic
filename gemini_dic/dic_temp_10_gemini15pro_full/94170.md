impenitentnesses:/ɪmˈpɛnɪtəntnɪsɪz/
|n.|不悔悟，顽固不化|The state of being impenitent; lack of remorse or regret for wrongdoing.|His impenitentnesses for his crimes shocked the judge. 他对罪行的顽固不化震惊了法官。|近义词：unrepentance, remorselessness, obduracy|反义词：penitence, remorse, contrition|词性:名词

Etymology:
From impenitent + -ness.  Impenitent from im- (not) + penitent (feeling or showing sorrow and regret for having done wrong). Penitent comes from Old French *penitent (from Latin paenitentem, present participle of paenitere "to repent").
