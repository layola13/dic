improviser:/ˈɪmprəvaɪzər/| n.|即兴演奏者，即兴创作的人|A person who improvises, especially in music or drama. |He's a talented improviser on the piano. 他是一位才华横溢的钢琴即兴演奏家。| synonyms: ad-libber, extemporizer, off-the-cuffer | antonyms: planner, preparer | noun

improvise:/ˈɪmprəvaɪz/| v.|即兴创作，临时凑合|To create and perform (music, drama, or verse) spontaneously or without preparation.|The musicians improvised a bluesy tune. 音乐家们即兴演奏了一首布鲁斯曲调。|We had to improvise a shelter from branches. 我们不得不临时用树枝搭建一个避难所。|synonyms: ad-lib, extemporize, wing it|antonyms: plan, prepare, rehearse|verb
