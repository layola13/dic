fahr:/ˈfɑːrənhaɪt/| adj. n.|华氏温度的；华氏温度计；华氏温标|Relating to, based on, or denoting a scale of temperature on which water freezes at 32° and boils at 212° under standard conditions.|The temperature is 80 degrees Fahr. 温度是80华氏度。|近义词:Celsius, centigrade|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement, but could consider) Kelvin, Celsius|词性:形容词, 名词
