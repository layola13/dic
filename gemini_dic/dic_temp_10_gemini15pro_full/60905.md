elenctic:/ɪˈlɛŋktɪk/
adjective
驳斥的，反驳性的
Refuting or refutational; serving to confute or disprove.
Elenctic arguments are designed to disprove an opponent's position.  驳斥性论证旨在反驳对方的立场。
The philosopher presented an elenctic argument against the existence of free will. 这位哲学家提出了一个反驳自由意志存在的论证。
近义词:refutatory, disproving, contradictory
反义词:supporting, confirmatory, corroborative
词性:adjective
