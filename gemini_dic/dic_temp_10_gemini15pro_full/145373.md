overcapability:/ˌoʊvərˌkeɪpəˈbɪləti/
n.
过度能力，过剩能力
The state of having more capability than is needed or desired.
Their overcapability in manufacturing led to a surplus of goods. 他们在制造方面的过度能力导致了商品过剩。
Synonyms: excess capacity, surplus capability, overcapacity
Antonyms: incapacity, inability, deficiency
noun
