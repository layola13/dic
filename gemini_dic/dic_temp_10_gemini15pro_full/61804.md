emphatics:/ɪmˈfætɪks/ | n. |强调词;加强语气词|Words or phrases used to emphasize a point or feeling. | "Really," "truly," and "absolutely" are common emphatics. |例句：He really wants to go. 他真的想去。

例句：She was absolutely furious. 她绝对是暴怒了。|近义词：intensifiers, boosters|反义词：downtoners, minimizers|词性:noun
