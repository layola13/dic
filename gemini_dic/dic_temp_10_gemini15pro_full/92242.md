hypertrophically:/ˌhaɪpərˈtrɒfɪkli/| adv.|肥大地，过度生长地|In a way that exhibits hypertrophy; excessively enlarged or overgrown.|The patient's heart muscle had grown hypertrophically due to the long-standing high blood pressure. 由于长期高血压，病人的心肌肥大。|Example 1: The scar tissue developed hypertrophically, making it raised and unsightly.  疤痕组织过度生长，使其隆起且难看。

Example 2:  The bodybuilder's muscles developed hypertrophically due to intense training. 健美运动员的肌肉由于高强度训练而过度发达。|Synonyms: excessively, abnormally, overly|Antonyms:  atrophically, under-developed|Word type: Adverb
