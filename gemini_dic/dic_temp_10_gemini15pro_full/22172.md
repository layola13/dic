bombast:/ˈbɒmbæst/ (US) /ˈbɒmbɑːst/ (UK)| n. |夸大的言辞；浮夸的语言|High-sounding language with little meaning, used to impress people.| The politician's speech was full of bombast, but lacked any real substance.  这位政治家的演讲充满了夸夸其谈，但缺乏实质内容。| synonyms: bluster, rhetoric, grandiloquence | antonyms: understatement, simplicity, conciseness | noun

bombastic:/bɒmˈbæstɪk/ (US) /bɒmˈbæstɪk/ (UK) | adj. |夸大的；言过其实的|Using high-sounding but empty language.|  His bombastic pronouncements were met with derision. 他夸夸其谈的声明遭到了嘲笑。| synonyms: pompous, inflated, overblown | antonyms: understated, simple, modest | adjective
