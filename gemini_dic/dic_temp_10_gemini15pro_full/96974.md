infantical: /ɪnfænˈtɪkəl/
adjective
幼稚的，孩子气的
Resembling or characteristic of an infant, especially in being childish or immature.
He threw an infantical tantrum when he didn't get his way.  他因为没有达到目的而像小孩一样大发脾气。
近义词: childish, immature, infantile, puerile
反义词: mature, adult, grown-up
词性: adjective
