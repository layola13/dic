overjoyed:/ˌəʊvəˈdʒɔɪd/| adj.|欣喜若狂的，狂喜的|extremely happy; filled with great joy|例句：He was overjoyed at the birth of his son. 他对儿子的出生欣喜若狂。|近义词：elated, joyous|反义词：dejected, heartbroken|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + joyed, past participle of joy.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "to a great degree".  The word "joy" ultimately comes from the Old French "joie," deriving from the Latin "gaudia."
