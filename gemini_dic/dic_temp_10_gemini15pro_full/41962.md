cosponsoring:/koʊˈspɑːnsərɪŋ/
verb
共同赞助，联合举办
The two companies are cosponsoring a charity event.
两家公司共同赞助了一场慈善活动。
例句：Several local businesses are cosponsoring the music festival.  几家当地企业共同赞助了这场音乐节。

例句：The senator is cosponsoring a bill to improve education. 参议员正在共同发起一项改善教育的法案。

近义词：co-hosting, jointly sponsoring, supporting together
反义词： (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑：solely sponsoring, independently funding)
词性：v.
