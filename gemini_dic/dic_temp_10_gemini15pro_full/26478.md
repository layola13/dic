c-sections:/siː ˈsekʃənz/| n.|剖腹产|A surgical operation for delivering a baby by cutting through the wall of the mother's abdomen and uterus.  Short for Cesarean sections.|She had to have an emergency c-section. 她不得不进行紧急剖腹产。|Synonym: Cesarean section, Cesarean delivery|Antonym: vaginal delivery|Noun
