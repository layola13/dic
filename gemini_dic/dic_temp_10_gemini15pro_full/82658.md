guerreros:/ɡeˈreɾos/| n. |战士们，勇士们| Spanish plural of guerrero, meaning "warrior." It comes from the Spanish word "guerra," meaning "war," which ultimately derives from the Germanic word "werra," also meaning "war."|The guerreros fought bravely. 勇士们英勇作战。|近义词：soldiers, fighters, warriors|反义词:civilians, pacifists|Noun
