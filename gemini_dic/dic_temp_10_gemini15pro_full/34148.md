clairvoyance:/ˌklɛərˈvɔɪəns/ | n. | 透视力；洞察力 | The ability to see beyond the range of ordinary perception.  The supposed faculty of perceiving things or events in the future or beyond normal sensory contact. | She claims to have the gift of clairvoyance. 她声称拥有透视的能力。| Near Synonyms:  precognition, second sight, prescience, intuition, insight | Antonyms:  obliviousness, ignorance | noun
