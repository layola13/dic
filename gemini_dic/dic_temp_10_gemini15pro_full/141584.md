oppressively:/əˈprɛsɪvli/
adverb|压抑地；沉重地；暴虐地|In a way that feels oppressive; in a way that is unjustly harsh or tyrannical. |The heat hung oppressively in the air. 空气中闷热得令人窒息。|Synonym: tyrannically, harshly, cruelly, brutally, ruthlessly |Antonym: kindly, gently, mercifully, leniently|词性: 副词

oppressive:/əˈprɛsɪv/
adjective|压迫的；压抑的；沉重的|Burdensome, unjustly harsh, or tyrannical. |The atmosphere at work was oppressive. 工作氛围令人压抑。|Synonym: tyrannical, harsh, cruel, brutal, ruthless |Antonym: kind, gentle, merciful, lenient|词性: 形容词

oppress:/əˈprɛs/
verb|压迫；压制；使（心情）沉重|Keep (someone) in subservience and hardship, especially by the unjust exercise of authority.  |A small elite oppressed the majority of the population. 少数精英压迫大多数民众。|Synonym: tyrannize, persecute, repress, subdue, subjugate |Antonym: liberate, free, emancipate|词性: 动词

词源:  From Late Latin *oppressīvus, from Latin oppressus, past participle of opprimere “to press against, suppress,” from ob- “against” + premere “to press.”
