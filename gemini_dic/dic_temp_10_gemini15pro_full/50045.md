denotive:/dɪˈnəʊtɪv/| adj.|表示的，指示的|Expressing or indicating something clearly and directly; explicit.|The denotive meaning of "home" is a place where one lives, but it can also have connotative meanings of comfort and family.|近义词：explicit, literal, indicative, signifying|反义词：connotative, suggestive, implicit|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *denotativus*, from Latin denotare ‘denote’, from de- ‘completely’ + notare ‘to mark’.
