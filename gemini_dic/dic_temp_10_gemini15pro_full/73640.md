forfeiture:/ˈfɔːrfɪtʃər/| n. |没收，丧失，罚金|The forfeiture of his driving licence meant he lost his job.  他的驾照被没收意味着他失去了工作。|Example: He suffered the forfeiture of his property. 他遭受了财产被没收的损失。|Synonyms: loss, surrender, penalty, confiscation|Antonyms: gain, acquisition, retention|noun
forfeiture:/ˈfɔːrfɪtʃər/| n. |(因犯规、违约、失职等造成的)丧失|His constant lateness resulted in the forfeiture of his job. 他经常迟到导致他失去了工作。|Example: The forfeiture of her scholarship was a devastating blow.  失去奖学金对她来说是一个毁灭性的打击。|Synonyms: deprivation, disqualification|Antonyms: award, entitlement|noun
