dame:/deɪm/ | n. | 女士；贵妇人；（旧时的）女人 | A woman; a lady (often used in a slightly old-fashioned way). |  She acted like a grand dame. 她表现得像个贵妇人。| 近义词: lady, woman, matron | 反义词: gentleman, sir | 词性: 名词

dame:/deɪm/ | n. | (英国)女爵士（授予女性的爵位）| (in Britain) the title given to a woman who has been given a knighthood |  Dame Judi Dench is a famous actress. 朱迪·丹奇女爵士是一位著名的女演员。| 近义词: knight (for men) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词


词源: From Old French "dame," meaning "lady," which itself comes from Latin "domina," meaning "mistress of a household."
