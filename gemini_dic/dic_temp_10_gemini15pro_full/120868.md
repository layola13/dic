misbestowed:/ˌmɪsbɪˈstəʊd/ | /ˌmɪsbɪˈstoʊd/ | adj. | 错误地给予的；不适当地授予的 |  Incorrectly or inappropriately conferred or given.  It describes something, usually an honor, gift, or privilege, that has been given to someone who doesn't deserve it or for whom it's not suitable. |  His knighthood was considered by many to be misbestowed. 很多人认为他的爵位授予不当。 | misplaced, misapplied, misused, ill-bestowed | appropriate, suitable, deserved, fitting | adjective
