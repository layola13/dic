nereites:/ˈnɪərɪˌaɪtiːz/| n. |涅柔斯虫，海生蠕虫化石|A genus of fossil marine worms, typically found in Paleozoic rocks, known for their distinctive, meandering trails preserved on bedding planes.  These trails are often complex and can reveal information about the behavior and environment of these ancient organisms.|Example: The nereites trace fossils found in the sandstone indicate a shallow marine environment.  在砂岩中发现的涅柔斯虫遗迹化石表明这是一个浅海环境。|Example:  Researchers are studying the nereites fossils to understand the evolution of early marine life. 研究人员正在研究涅柔斯虫化石以了解早期海洋生物的进化。|近义词: trace fossil, ichnofossil |反义词: (None - it's a specific genus of fossil)|词性: noun
