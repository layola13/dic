argentous:/ɑːrˈdʒɛntəs/
adjective
银(I)的；亚银的
Containing silver, especially in the +1 oxidation state.
Silver chloride is an example of an argentous compound. 氯化银是亚银化合物的一个例子。
近义词:silver(I)
反义词:argentic
词性:adjective
