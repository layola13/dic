inquisitiveness:/ɪnˈkwɪzətɪvnəs/| n. |好奇心，求知欲|A strong desire to learn or know more about something.|Her inquisitiveness led her to explore every corner of the museum. 她的好奇心驱使她探索了博物馆的每一个角落。|curiosity, inquiring mind|apathy, indifference|noun


inquisitive:/ɪnˈkwɪzətɪv/| adj. |好奇的，爱打听的|Given to inquiry, research, or asking questions; eager for knowledge.|Children are naturally inquisitive. 孩子们天生就好奇。|curious, inquiring|apathetic, indifferent|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin inquisitīvus (“inquiring, inquisitive”), from Latin inquīsītus, perfect passive participle of inquīrō (“inquire into, investigate”), from in (“in, into, on, upon, against”) + quaerō (“seek, ask”).
