homilite:/ˈhoʊməˌlaɪt/| n. |硅硼钙石|A rare borosilicate mineral containing calcium, iron, and sometimes sodium, typically occurring as brown or black prismatic crystals.|Homilite is a relatively rare mineral found in igneous and metamorphic rocks. 硅硼钙石是一种相对稀有的矿物，发现于火成岩和变质岩中。|Synonyms: None (as it's a specific mineral name) |Antonyms: None |Noun
