continuate:/kənˈtɪnjuːeɪt/ | v. | 继续，延续 | To continue without interruption; to extend or prolong | The doctor continuated the treatment for another week. 医生继续治疗了一个星期。|  He continuated his work despite the distractions. 尽管有干扰，他还是继续工作。 | 近义词: continue, persist, persevere, extend, prolong | 反义词: discontinue, cease, stop, interrupt | 词性: 动词

词源: From Late Latin *continuatus, past participle of continuare ("to join together, connect, make continuous"), from Latin continuus ("continuous").
