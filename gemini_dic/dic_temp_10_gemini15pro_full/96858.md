inexcusably:/ˌɪnɪkˈskjuːzəbli/
adverb
不可原谅地，inexcusably
without excuse or justification
He behaved inexcusably. 他的行为不可原谅。
近义词: unforgivably, unpardonably
反义词: excusably, justifiably
词性: adverb
