frisking:/ˈfrɪskɪŋ/| n. v.|搜身，轻快地蹦跳|The act of quickly running your hands over someone's body, usually to search for hidden weapons or other illegal items.  Also, the act of playfully skipping or leaping.|The police officer was frisking the suspect. 警察正在搜查嫌疑犯的身。|The lambs were frisking in the field. 羊羔在田野里欢快地蹦跳。|近义词：searching, patting down, gamboling, frolicking|反义词：ignoring, neglecting|词性: noun (mostly), verb (less common)

词源: From the verb "frisk," which comes from the Old French word "frisque," meaning lively or brisk.
