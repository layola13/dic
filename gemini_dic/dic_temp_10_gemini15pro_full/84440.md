hallway:/ˈhɔːlweɪ/ (US) /ˈhɔːlweɪ/ (UK)| n.|走廊，过道|A passage in a building that provides access to rooms.|例句：The children were playing in the hallway. 孩子们在走廊里玩耍。

例句：She walked down the long hallway to her office. 她沿着长长的走廊走到她的办公室。|近义词：corridor, passage, passageway|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps room or chamber if contrasting enclosed space vs. passage)|词性:noun
