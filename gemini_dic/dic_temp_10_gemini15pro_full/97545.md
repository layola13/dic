ingratiating:/ɪnˈɡreɪʃieɪtɪŋ/
adjective
讨好的；奉承的；迎合的
Intended to gain approval or favor; sycophantic.
例句：He had a particularly ingratiating manner. 他有一种特别讨人喜欢的方式。

例句：She flashed him an ingratiating smile. 她向他露出讨好的笑容。

近义词：charming, flattering, fawning, obsequious, sycophantic
反义词：off-putting, repellent, abrasive

词性: adjective
