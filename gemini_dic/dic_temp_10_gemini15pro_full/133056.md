nonepisodic:/ˌnɒnˈɛpɪˈsɒdɪk/
adjective
非偶发的，非间歇的，连续的
Not occurring in episodes; continuous or sustained.
Her pain was nonepisodic, a constant dull ache. 她的疼痛是持续性的，一阵钝痛。
Synonyms: continuous, sustained, constant, unremitting, persistent, perpetual, incessant
Antonyms: episodic, intermittent, sporadic, occasional
词性: 形容词
