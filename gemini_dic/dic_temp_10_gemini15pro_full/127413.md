nearsightedly:/nɪrˈsaɪtɪdli/| adv.|近视地；目光短浅地|In a manner lacking foresight or long-term perspective.  Describes actions or thinking that focuses only on the immediate present and fails to consider future consequences.|He nearsightedly focused on short-term profits, ignoring the long-term sustainability of the business. 他目光短浅地只关注短期利润，而忽略了企业的长期可持续发展。|Synonyms: shortsightedly, myopically|Antonyms: farsightedly, presciently|adverb
