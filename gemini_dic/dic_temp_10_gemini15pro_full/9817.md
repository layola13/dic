anybody's-guess:/ˈɛnɪbɒdiz ɡɛs/ | n. |难以预料的事情，未知数|Something that is unknown or unpredictable.  It implies that no one can know the answer with certainty. |It's anybody's guess who will win the election.  谁会赢得选举还不确定。|synonyms:  uncertain, unpredictable, unknown, open question, toss-up | antonyms: certain, predictable, known, foregone conclusion | noun
