discreetness:/dɪˈskriːtnəs/| n.|谨慎，审慎，不引人注目|The discreetness of the inquiry ensured that nobody suspected anything.  调查的谨慎性确保了没有人怀疑任何事情。|例句：She handled the sensitive issue with great discreetness. 她非常谨慎地处理了这个敏感问题。

例句：The discreetness of his manner made him a trusted confidant. 他谨慎的态度使他成为值得信赖的知己。|近义词：tact, prudence, circumspection, diplomacy|反义词：indiscretion, carelessness, recklessness|词性:名词

词源: From Middle French *discretnesse*, from *discret* (see discreet).
