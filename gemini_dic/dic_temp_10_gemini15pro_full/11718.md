arnicas:/ˈɑːrnɪkəz/
noun
山金车属植物；山金车属植物的提取物
A genus of perennial herbs of the composite family, native to mountainous regions of northern temperate regions.  Extracts of the plant are used in herbal medicine.
She used arnica cream on her bruised knee. 她在她擦伤的膝盖上涂了山金车膏。
synonyms:leopard's bane, wolf's bane
