outthwack: /ˌaʊtˈθwæk/
| v. |用力击打，痛打|To strike or hit forcefully; to thrash.|He outthwacked his opponent in the final round of the boxing match. 他在拳击比赛的最后一轮痛打了他的对手。|The knight outthwacked the dragon with his mighty sword. 骑士用他的强大的宝剑痛击了恶龙。|近义词：thrash, clobber, wallop, bash, pummel|反义词：caress, stroke|词性：动词
