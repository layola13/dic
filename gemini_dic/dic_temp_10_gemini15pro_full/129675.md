non-departmental:/ˌnɒnˌdɪpɑːtˈmɛntl/ /ˌnɑːnˌdiːpɑːrtˈmɛntl/| adj.|非部门的，跨部门的|Not belonging to or affiliated with any particular department, often referring to a government body or organization.| The non-departmental public body is responsible for regulating the industry.  这个非部门公共机构负责监管该行业。|Synonyms: independent, autonomous, cross-departmental, interdepartmental|Antonyms: departmental|adjective

non-departmental:/ˌnɒnˌdɪpɑːtˈmɛntl/ /ˌnɑːnˌdiːpɑːrtˈmɛntl/| n.|非部门机构|A government body that is not part of a ministry or department.|The non-departmental is funded directly by the government. 这个非部门机构由政府直接资助。|Synonyms: independent agency, autonomous body, quango (quasi-autonomous non-governmental organization)|Antonyms: government department, ministry|noun
