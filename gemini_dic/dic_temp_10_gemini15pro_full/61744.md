empanelling:/ɪmˈpænəlɪŋ/ | n. | 陪审团遴选，选任陪审团 | The empanelling of the jury took longer than expected. |  The process of selecting a jury for a trial. | Example: The empanelling of the jury took several hours.  陪审团的遴选花了几个小时。 | Example: The judge oversaw the empanelling process carefully. 法官仔细监督了陪审团的遴选过程。 | Synonyms: jury selection, impaneling | Antonyms: dismissal of jury | noun

empanel:/ɪmˈpænəl/ | vt. | 选任（陪审团）| The judge empanelled a jury of twelve people. | To enroll a list of jurors. | Example:  They empanelled a jury for the murder trial. 他们为谋杀案审判选任了陪审团。 | Example: The court clerk will empanel the jury tomorrow. 法院书记员明天将选任陪审团。 | Synonyms:  swear in, constitute, enroll | Antonyms: discharge, dismiss | verb
