PHRASE->
1. autophagic flux (自噬流):  The complete process of autophagy, from the formation of autophagosomes to their fusion with lysosomes and degradation of their contents.

2. autophagic degradation (自噬降解): The breakdown of cellular components through autophagy.

3. autophagic pathway (自噬途径): The series of molecular events that lead to autophagy.

4. autophagic activity (自噬活动): The level or rate of autophagy occurring in a cell or tissue.

5. autophagic dysfunction (自噬功能障碍): Impaired or abnormal autophagy.
