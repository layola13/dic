benefactrice:/ˌbɛnɪˈfæktriːs/
noun
女施主，女恩人
A woman who confers a benefit; a female benefactor.
She was a benefactrice of the arts, donating generously to the local museum. 她是一位艺术赞助人，慷慨地向当地博物馆捐款。
The hospital was built thanks to the generous benefactrice. 这家医院的建成多亏了那位慷慨的女捐助人。
近义词:patroness, donor
反义词:malefactor
词性:n.
