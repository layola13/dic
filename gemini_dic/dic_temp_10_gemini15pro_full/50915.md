deservingly:/dɪˈzɜːrvɪŋli/
adv.
应得地，理所当然地
in a way that is deserved; justly.
She was deservingly praised for her hard work. 她因努力工作而理所当然地受到表扬。
近义词: justly, rightfully, appropriately
反义词: undeservedly, unfairly, unjustly
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From deserve + -ing + -ly.  "Deserve" comes from Old French "deservir"  (to deserve, be worthy of), from Latin "deservire" (to serve well, deserve), from "de-" (completely) + "servire" (to serve).
