bribeability:/ˌbraɪbəˈbɪləti/
n.
可贿赂性, 受贿的可能性
The ease with which someone can be bribed.
The bribeability of officials is a serious problem in some countries.  一些国家的官员腐败是一个严重的问题。
近义词: corruptibility, venality
反义词: incorruptibility, integrity
词性: noun

Etymology:
From bribe + -able + -ity.  "Bribe" comes from Old French "bribe" meaning "a piece of bread given to a beggar," later "a gift given to influence conduct."
