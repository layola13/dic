conciliatoriness:/kənˌsɪliəˈtɔːrɪnəs/
|n.|安抚性，调和性|The quality of being conciliatory; inclination to conciliate.|Her conciliatoriness helped to resolve the conflict peacefully. 她的调解性帮助和平解决了冲突。|
|近义词：peacefulness, diplomacy, appeasement, compromise, accommodation|
|反义词：aggression, hostility, belligerence, antagonism, defiance|
|词性: noun|
|词源：From conciliatory + -ness. Conciliatory comes from Late Latin conciliatorius, from conciliare "to bring together, win over," from concilium "council."|
