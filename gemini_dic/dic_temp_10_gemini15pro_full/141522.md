oppidan:/ˈɒpɪdən/ (UK), /ˈɑːpɪdən/ (US)| n.|城镇居民；寄宿生|A resident of a town, especially as distinguished from a rural dweller.  In some English public schools, a student who lives in the town, as opposed to a boarder.|He was an oppidan, unfamiliar with the ways of the countryside.|近义词：townsman, citizen, resident|反义词：rustic, countryman|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *oppidanus*, from *oppidum* ("town").
