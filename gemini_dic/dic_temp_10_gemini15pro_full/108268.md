launderer:/ˈlɔːndərər/ (US), /ˈlɔːndrər/ (UK)| n.|洗涤者，洗衣工；洗钱者|A person or business that launders things, especially clothes, or illegally launders money.|The launderer charged high prices for his services. 洗衣工的服务收费很高。|The government is cracking down on launderers who try to conceal the source of illegal funds. 政府正在打击试图隐瞒非法资金来源的洗钱者。|synonyms: cleaner, washerman/woman, money launderer|antonyms: none (for the clothing sense); perhaps 'investigator', 'prosecutor' (for the money laundering sense)|noun
