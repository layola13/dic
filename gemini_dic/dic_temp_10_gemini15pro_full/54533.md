disleaf:/dɪsˈliːf/ | v. |  落叶；使落叶 | To cause the leaves of a plant to fall off. | The harsh winter disleafed the trees earlier than usual. 严冬使树木比往常更早落叶。| The strong winds disleafed the branches.  强风吹落了树枝上的叶子。| 近义词: defoliate | 反义词: leaf | 词性: 动词
