distinct:/dɪˈstɪŋkt/
adjective
明显的，清晰的
clearly different or separate; readily distinguishable
例句：There's a distinct smell of gas. 有一股明显的煤气味。
例句：The two languages are quite distinct from each other. 这两种语言截然不同。
近义词：different, separate, clear, obvious, noticeable
反义词：similar, indistinct, vague, blurred

distinct:/dɪˈstɪŋkt/
adjective
独特的，显著的
easily noticeable; unmistakable
例句：She has a distinct advantage over other candidates. 她比其他候选人有明显的优势。
例句：This wine has a distinct fruity flavor.  这种酒有一种独特的水果味。
近义词：unique, characteristic, special, individual
反义词：common, ordinary, usual


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin distinctus, past participle of distinguere "to separate, distinguish," from dis- "apart" + -stinguere "to prick, mark" (related to instigate).
