meionite:/ˈmaɪəˌnaɪt/| n. |钙霞石|A tetragonal mineral, a calcium aluminosilicate of the scapolite group.  A member of the scapolite group, a feldspathoid mineral.|
例句：Meionite is a common mineral found in metamorphic rocks.  钙霞石是一种常见的变质岩矿物。

例句：The meionite crystals were well-formed and transparent. 钙霞石晶体形态良好且透明。

近义词：scapolite, marialite|
反义词: N/A|
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek "μειών" (meiōn, "less"), because its pyramids are less acute than those of vesuvianite, a mineral with which it was originally confused.
