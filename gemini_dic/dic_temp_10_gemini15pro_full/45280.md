cultural:/ˈkʌltʃərəl/| adj.|文化的；教养的|Relating to the ideas, customs, and social behaviour of a society.|例句：The city has a rich cultural heritage. 这座城市有着丰富的文化遗产。|例句：Cultural differences can sometimes lead to misunderstandings. 文化差异有时会导致误解。|近义词：artistic, civilizing, educational|反义词：natural, uncultured|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *culturalis*, from cultura (“cultivation, agriculture; care, culture”), from cultus, past participle of colere (“to till, cultivate, worship”).
