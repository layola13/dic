outflaming: /aʊtˈfleɪmɪŋ/
Verb
燃烧得更旺盛；胜过
Burning more brightly or intensely than; surpassing in brilliance or intensity.
The bonfire was outflaming all the others.  篝火比所有其他的都要旺盛。
Her passion for art outflamed his. 她对艺术的热情胜过他。
Synonyms: outshining, outdazzling, excelling, surpassing
Antonyms: dimming, paling, fading, inferior
词性: 动词
