discretive:/dɪˈskriːtɪv/| adj.|离散的，分立的|Relating to or involving discrete quantities or variables; not continuous.|The study uses discretive data, such as the number of children in each family.|近义词：separate, distinct, individual|反义词:continuous, connected|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *discrētus*, past participle of *discernere* "to separate, distinguish," from *dis-* "apart" + *cernere* "to sift, separate, distinguish."
