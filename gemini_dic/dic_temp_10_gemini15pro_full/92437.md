hypoparathyroidism:/ˌhaɪpoʊˌpærəˈθaɪrɔɪdɪzəm/
|n.|甲状旁腺功能减退症|A condition in which the parathyroid glands do not produce enough parathyroid hormone, leading to low levels of calcium in the blood.|The patient was diagnosed with hypoparathyroidism after experiencing muscle cramps and seizures.|近义词: tetany (in severe cases)|反义词: hyperparathyroidism|词性: noun

hypoparathyroid:/ˌhaɪpoʊˌpærəˈθaɪrɔɪd/
|adj.|甲状旁腺功能减退的|Relating to or characterized by hypoparathyroidism; having insufficient parathyroid function.|The hypoparathyroid patient required regular calcium supplements.|近义词: None (specific medical term)|反义词: hyperparathyroid|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
hypo- (Greek: under, below) + para- (Greek: beside, near) + thyroid (Greek: thyreoeidēs, shield-shaped, referring to the thyroid cartilage)
