doomsday: /ˈduːmzdeɪ/ (UK) /ˈduːmzdeɪ/ (US)| n.|世界末日；末日审判；灾难性的一天|The day of final judgment; the end of the world.  A time or event of crisis or great danger.|例句：Many people feared the Y2K bug would bring about doomsday. 很多人害怕千年虫会带来世界末日。

例句：The doomsday clock is a symbolic representation of the world's vulnerability to catastrophe. 末日时钟象征性地代表了世界容易遭受灾难的程度。|近义词：apocalypse, armageddon, the end of the world, day of reckoning|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
