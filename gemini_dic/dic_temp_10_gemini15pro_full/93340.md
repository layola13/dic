illaqueable:/ˌɪləˈkweɪəbəl/
adjective
无法攻击的，无懈可击的
Not capable of being attacked, questioned, or disputed.  Impregnable, irrefutable.
His logic was illaqueable, leaving no room for argument. 他的逻辑无懈可击，不容置疑。
The fortress, high on the mountain, was illaqueable.  位于高山之巅的堡垒坚不可摧。
synonyms:  unassailable, impregnable, invulnerable, irrefutable, indisputable
antonyms: vulnerable, assailable, refutable, disputable
word type: adjective
