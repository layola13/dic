leadenness:/ˈlɛdənɪs/| n.|沉闷；迟钝；呆滞|The leadenness of his prose made it difficult to read. 他的散文沉闷难读。|例句：The leadenness of the atmosphere oppressed everyone. 沉闷的气氛压抑着每个人。

例句：She felt a leadenness in her limbs after the long journey.  长途旅行后，她感到四肢沉重无力。

近义词：dullness, heaviness, inertia, sluggishness, torpor|反义词：liveliness, energy, vibrancy|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From leaden + -ness.  Leaden itself comes from Old English *lēaden, from lēad (lead).  The metaphorical sense of "heavy, dull" developed from the physical properties of lead.
