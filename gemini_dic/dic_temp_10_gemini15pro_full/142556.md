ostentatious: /ˌɒstənˈteɪʃəs/ /ˌɑːstənˈteɪʃəs/ | adj. | 炫耀的，卖弄的 | characterized by vulgar or pretentious display; designed to impress or attract notice |  He drives an ostentatious sports car. 他开着一辆炫耀的跑车。 | synonyms: showy, pretentious, flamboyant, flashy | antonyms: modest, humble, understated, unassuming | adjective

ostentation: /ˌɒstənˈteɪʃən/ /ˌɑːstənˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 炫耀，卖弄 | pretentious and vulgar display, especially of wealth and possessions, intended to impress or attract notice. | The ostentation of the wedding was breathtaking. 婚礼的奢华令人叹为观止。 | synonyms: showiness, pretentiousness, flamboyance, flashiness | antonyms: modesty, humility, understatement | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ostentationem*, accusative singular of *ostentatio* (“a showing, display, exhibition”), from *ostentare* (“to display, show”), frequentative of *ostendere* (“to show, display, exhibit”).  The verb *ostendere* is from *obs-* (variant of *ob-*) + *tendere* (“to stretch, extend”).
