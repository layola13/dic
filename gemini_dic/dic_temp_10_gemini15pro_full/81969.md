grindability:/ˌɡraɪndəˈbɪləti/
n.|可磨性，研磨性|The grindability of a material refers to the ease with which it can be ground into smaller particles.  一种材料的可磨性是指将其研磨成更小颗粒的容易程度。|The grindability of the clinker is a crucial factor in cement production. 熟料的可磨性是水泥生产中的一个关键因素。|friability, pulverizability|resistance, hardness|noun
