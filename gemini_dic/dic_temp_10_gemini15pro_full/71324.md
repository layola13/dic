fjordic:/fjɔːrˈdɪk/ | adj. | 峡湾的；峡湾特有的 | Relating to or characteristic of a fjord. | The rugged, fjordic coastline of Norway is breathtaking. 挪威崎岖的峡湾海岸线令人叹为观止。 |  |近义词：coastal, maritime | 反义词：inland | 词性:形容词

fjordic:/fjɔːrˈdɪk/ | n. | 峡湾地区的人或语言 | A person from a fjord region or the language spoken there. (Rare usage) |  He spoke a fjordic dialect I couldn't understand. 他说一种我听不懂的峡湾方言。(罕见用法) | 近义词：None (usage is too specific) | 反义词：None (usage is too specific) | 词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From fjord + -ic. Fjord comes from Old Norse *fjǫrðr, related to faran "to travel, fare." The suffix -ic forms adjectives meaning "of or relating to."
