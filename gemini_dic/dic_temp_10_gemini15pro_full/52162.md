diaphote:/ˈdaɪəˌfoʊt/| n. |透射正片; 透明正片|A photographic transparency on a glass plate, especially one for use in a magic lantern or projector. |例句：The diaphote projected a vivid image of the cathedral onto the screen.  透射正片将大教堂的鲜明图像投射到屏幕上。

例句：He carefully placed the diaphote in the projector, eager to share his travel photos. 他小心翼翼地把透射正片放进投影仪里，急切地想分享他的旅行照片。|近义词：transparency, slide|反义词: negative |词性: noun
