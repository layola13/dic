flexitime:/ˈfleksɪtaɪm/| n.|弹性工作时间；灵活工作时间制|A system of working a set number of hours with the starting and finishing times chosen within agreed limits by the employee.|Many companies now operate flexitime systems. 许多公司现在实行弹性工作时间制。|Example:  I appreciate the flexitime system because it allows me to pick up my kids from school. 我很感激弹性工作时间制，因为它允许我去接我的孩子放学。

Example: Flexitime gives employees more autonomy over their schedules. 弹性工作制赋予员工对其日程安排更大的自主权。|Synonyms: flexible working hours, flextime, flexi-time|Antonyms: fixed hours, nine-to-five|noun
