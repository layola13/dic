papery:/ˈpeɪpəri/| adj.|薄如纸的，像纸一样的|Resembling paper in thinness, dryness, or brittleness; flimsy.|The old woman's skin was papery and translucent. 老妇人的皮肤薄如纸，而且透明。|The leaves of the plant were dry and papery.  这种植物的叶子干燥易碎，像纸一样。|近义词：thin, flimsy, fragile, brittle|反义词：thick, strong, robust|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From paper + -y (suffix forming adjectives meaning "having the qualities of," "full of," "covered with," "somewhat," etc.).  Ultimately from Latin papyrus.
