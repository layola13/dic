overplanted:/ˌoʊvərˈplæntɪd/
adjective
种植过密的，过度种植的
planted with too many plants, especially trees.  The market is overplanted with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价的进口商品。
The vineyard was overplanted, resulting in smaller grapes.  这个葡萄园种植过密，导致葡萄变小。
Synonyms: overcrowded, congested, saturated
Antonyms: underplanted, sparse
词性: adjective
