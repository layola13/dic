dictamen:/dɪkˈteɪmən/
n.
pronouncement; decree; dictate; opinion; verdict
An authoritative statement; a formal pronouncement, decree, or judgment.
The committee issued its dictamen on the proposed law. 委员会发布了关于拟议法律的正式声明。
synonyms:  pronouncement, decree, edict, judgment, ruling, verdict, decision, order, directive, mandate, injunction, dictate, assertion, declaration, affirmation, statement, dictum
antonyms:  request, plea, appeal, petition, entreaty, supplication, solicitation
noun
