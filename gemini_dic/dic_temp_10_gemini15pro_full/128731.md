nigerianization:/naɪˌdʒɪəriənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 尼日利亚化 |The process of adapting something to Nigerian norms, customs, or practices.  The act of giving something a Nigerian character. |  The nigerianization of the curriculum was met with mixed reactions.  尼日利亚化课程受到了褒贬不一的反应。

The government's policy of nigerianization led to the appointment of more Nigerian citizens to key positions. 政府的尼日利亚化政策导致更多尼日利亚公民被任命到关键职位。| 近义词: localization, indigenization | 反义词: internationalization, globalization | 词性: noun
