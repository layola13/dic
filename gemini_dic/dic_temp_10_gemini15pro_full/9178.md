antinomian:/ˌæntɪˈnoʊmiən/| adj. n.|反律法的，违反道德律的；反律法主义者|Rejecting or opposing the moral law, especially the moral law of the Old Testament.|The preacher was accused of antinomian heresy.|近义词：lawless, immoral, dissolute, libertine|反义词:moral, lawful, ethical|adjective, noun


antinomian:/ˌæntɪˈnoʊmiən/| n.|反律法主义者|A person who rejects or opposes the moral law, especially the moral law of the Old Testament.|Some antinomians believe they are exempt from moral obligations.|近义词：heretic, dissenter, nonconformist|反义词:conformist, traditionalist|noun
