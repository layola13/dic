circlers:/ˈsɜːrk(ə)lərz/
n.
盘旋者，绕圈者
People or things that move in circles.
The circlers above the field were hawks, searching for prey.  田野上空盘旋的是老鹰，它们在寻找猎物。
The vultures were patient circlers, waiting for the inevitable. 秃鹫耐心地盘旋着，等待着不可避免的事情发生。
synonyms: orbiters, revolvers, rotators
antonyms:  non-movers, statics
