ciphers:/ˈsaɪfərz/ | n. | 密码，暗号；零，无足轻重的人或物 | A secret or disguised way of writing; a zero or something of no value. |  The spies used complex ciphers to communicate.  间谍们使用复杂的密码进行交流。| synonyms: code, cryptogram, zero, nullity | antonyms: plaintext, solution, something, somebody | noun

ciphers:/ˈsaɪfərz/ | v. |  计算；用密码写 |  To calculate; to write in a secret code. |  He ciphered the message using a simple substitution code. 他用简单的替换密码对信息进行了加密。| synonyms: calculate, compute, encode, encrypt | antonyms: decode, decrypt | verb
