leiotrichan:/ˌlaɪoʊˈtrɪkən/ | adj. | 光滑的，无毛的 |  Having smooth hair; lacking guard hairs.  Derived from Greek *leios* "smooth" + *thrix* "hair". |  The leiotrichan skin of the dolphin felt incredibly smooth.  海豚的光滑皮肤摸起来非常光滑。 | Synonyms: smooth, glabrous | Antonyms: hairy, hirsute | adjective

leiotrichy:/ˌlaɪoʊˈtrɪki/ | n. | 光滑，无毛 | The condition of having smooth hair; the absence of guard hairs.  Derived from Greek *leios* "smooth" + *thrix* "hair". |  Leiotrichy is a characteristic of certain marine mammals. 光滑是某些海洋哺乳动物的特征。| Synonyms: smoothness, glabrousness | Antonyms: hairiness, hirsuteness | noun
