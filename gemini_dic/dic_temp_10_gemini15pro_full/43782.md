cremating:/krɪˈmeɪtɪŋ/
v.
火化
The process of burning a dead body to ashes.
They are cremating the body tomorrow.  他们明天将火化遗体。
The family chose to cremate him rather than bury him.  这家人选择火化他而不是土葬。
近义词: incinerating
反义词: burying
词性: 动词(present participle of cremate)


cremate:/krɪˈmeɪt/
v.
火化
To reduce (a dead body) to ashes by fire.
His remains were cremated and scattered at sea. 他的遗体被火化并撒入大海。
They decided to cremate her body. 他们决定火化她的遗体。
近义词: incinerate
反义词: bury
词性: 动词

词源: From Latin *cremāre, present active infinitive of cremō (“I burn”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱer- (“to burn”).
