contradiction:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪkʃən/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪkʃən/| n. |矛盾，反驳，否认|A combination of statements, ideas, or features of a situation that are opposed to one another.|He seemed oblivious to the contradiction in his own argument. 他似乎没有注意到自己论点中的矛盾之处。|Synonyms: inconsistency, discrepancy, conflict, paradox, incongruity|Antonyms: agreement, consistency, harmony, accord|noun

contradiction:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪkʃən/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪkʃən/| n. |矛盾，反驳，否认|The act of saying something that is the opposite of what someone else has said.|Her statement was in direct contradiction to his testimony. 她的陈述与他的证词直接矛盾。|Synonyms: denial, refutation, rebuttal|Antonyms: confirmation, corroboration, support|noun
