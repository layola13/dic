changeability:/ˌtʃeɪndʒəˈbɪləti/ | n. |可变性，易变性 |The changeability of the weather makes it difficult to plan a picnic. |例句：The changeability of his moods made him difficult to work with.  他的情绪多变让他很难共事。

例句：The changeability of the stock market requires investors to be cautious. 股票市场的变幻莫测要求投资者谨慎行事。|近义词：mutability, variability, fluidity, instability |反义词：stability, constancy, permanence |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From changeable + -ity.  Changeable comes from change (v.) + -able. Change ultimately derives from the Latin word "cambiare" meaning "to barter or exchange."
