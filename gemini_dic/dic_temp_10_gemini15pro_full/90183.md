hotshots:/ˈhɒtʃɒts/ (US) /ˈhɒtʃɒts/ (UK)| n.|高手；能人；精英；热门人物|A person or group of people who are exceptionally skilled or successful in a particular field.  A "hot shot" can also refer to something exciting, new, and popular.|The new CEO brought in a team of hotshots to turn the company around.  新任首席执行官聘请了一批高手来扭转公司的局面。|Synonyms: experts, aces, stars, whizzes, high-fliers|Antonyms: amateurs, beginners, novices|Noun

hotshot:/ˈhɒtʃɒt/ (US) /ˈhɒtʃɒt/ (UK)| adj.|高手的；能人的；精英的；热门的|Exceptionally skilled, successful, or important.|He's a hotshot lawyer in New York. 他是纽约的一位王牌律师。|Synonyms: expert, ace, star, top-notch, first-rate|Antonyms: amateur, beginner, novice|Adjective
