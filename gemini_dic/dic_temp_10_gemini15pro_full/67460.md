extollers:/ɪkˈstəʊlərz/
n.
赞美者；称颂者
People who praise something or someone highly.
The extollers of the king's wisdom were many. 国王的智慧有很多赞美者。

The extollers of the new policy believe it will revitalize the economy.  新政策的支持者认为它将 revitalize 经济。

Synonyms: praisers, lauders, eulogizers, celebrators
Antonyms: critics, detractors, denouncers, disparagers
词性: noun (plural)
