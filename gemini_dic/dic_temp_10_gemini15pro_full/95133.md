inapplicablenesses:/ɪnˌæplɪkəˈbɪlɪtɪz/
|n.|不适用性；不适用之处|The inapplicablenesses of the old law to modern society are clear. 旧法律对现代社会的不适用性显而易见。|例句：The lawyer pointed out the inapplicablenesses of the regulation to this specific case.  律师指出了该规定对这一具体案例的不适用性。

The inapplicablenesses of this theory in practical situations are numerous.  这个理论在实际情况中的不适用之处很多。|近义词：irrelevance, unsuitability, inappropriateness|反义词：relevance, suitability, appropriateness|词性：名词

inapplicable:/ˌɪnəˈplɪkəbl/
|adj.|不适用的；不切题的|This rule is inapplicable in this situation. 这条规则在这种情况下不适用。|例句：The old regulations are inapplicable to the new circumstances. 旧的规章制度不适用于新的情况。

His argument was inapplicable to the topic at hand.  他的论点与当前的主题不切题。|近义词：irrelevant, unsuitable, inappropriate|反义词：relevant, suitable, appropriate|词性：形容词

apply:/əˈplaɪ/
|v.|应用；申请|Please apply this cream to the affected area. 请将此药膏涂抹于患处。|例句：You should apply for this scholarship. 你应该申请这项奖学金。

He applied his knowledge to solving the problem. 他运用他的知识来解决问题。|近义词：use, utilize, employ, request|反义词：ignore, neglect, withdraw|词性：动词
