beatifies:/biˈætɪfaɪz/
|v.|使圣洁；使快乐；祝福；册封…为真福品/圣品|To declare (a deceased person) to be blessed, as a first step toward canonization to sainthood.  To make supremely happy.|The Pope beatified Mother Teresa in 2003. 教皇于2003年将特蕾莎修女册封为真福。|近义词：sanctify, consecrate, exalt, bless|反义词：condemn, curse, vilify|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *beatificare, from Latin beatus (“blessed”) + -ificare (“to make”).
