benedictines:/ˌbɛnɪˈdɪktɪnz/
n.
本笃会修士；本笃会修女
Members of a Roman Catholic monastic order following the rule of Saint Benedict.
例句：The Benedictines are known for their scholarship and hospitality. 本笃会修士以其学术和好客而闻名。

例句：The Benedictines established a monastery on the hill.  本笃会在山上建立了一座修道院。

近义词：monks, nuns （更广义的）
反义词:  (无直接反义词，可以考虑世俗人士 laity)
词性: noun
