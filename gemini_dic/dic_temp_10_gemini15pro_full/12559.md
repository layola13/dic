aspiratory:/ˈæspərətɔːri/ (US), /əˈspaɪrətri/ (UK)| adj. n.|吸气的；送气的；促吸的；(医)吸入器|Relating to breathing in; pronounced with a puff of air; a device used for inhaling medicine.|例句：The aspiratory muscles contract during inhalation. 吸气肌在吸气过程中收缩。|例句：He used an aspiratory to administer the medication. 他用吸入器给药。|近义词：inhalant, inspiratory|反义词：expiratory|词性：形容词, 名词

aspirate:/ˈæspərət/ (US), /ˈæspɪrət/ (UK)| n. v.|吸入物；抽出；送气；|To draw (fluid, etc) by suction from a vessel or cavity; pronounced with a puff of air (phonetics).|例句：The doctor aspirated the fluid from the patient's lung. 医生从病人的肺部抽出液体。|例句：The 'p' in 'pit' is aspirated. “pit”中的“p”是送气的。|近义词：inhale, suck, withdraw|反义词：exhale, blow|词性：名词, 动词
