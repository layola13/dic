andropetalous:/ˌændrəˈpɛtələs/
adjective
雄蕊瓣化的，雄蕊瓣状的
Having stamens that resemble petals or are petal-like.  Stamens are transformed into petaloid structures.
The andropetalous flower had showy, colorful stamens that looked like petals, attracting pollinators.  这种雄蕊瓣状的花有着艳丽多彩、看起来像花瓣的雄蕊，吸引着传粉者。
Synonym: petaloid, petaliferous
Antonym: apetalous
词性: adjective
