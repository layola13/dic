hornbeak:/ˈhɔːrnˌbiːk/| n.|角喙兽|A large, magical creature resembling a griffin with a steel beak and talons, native to mountainous regions.  It is known for its fierce loyalty and its ability to tear through almost any substance with its beak.|Buckbeak, later renamed Witherwings, was a hippogriff, often mistakenly called a hornbeak, in the Harry Potter series.|近义词:griffin, hippogriff|反义词: (No direct antonym exists for a mythical creature)|名词
