gibber:/ˈdʒɪbər/| v. n.|快速而不清楚地说话，胡言乱语；(动物)吱吱叫；无意义的话|To speak rapidly and unintelligibly; to chatter unmeaningly. Also, the act of speaking in such a manner, or the unintelligible speech itself.|The monkeys gibbered excitedly at the sight of food. 猴子们看到食物兴奋地吱吱叫。|近义词：babble, chatter, prattle|反义词:articulate, enunciate|词性:动词，名词

gibberish:/ˈɡɪbərɪʃ/| n.|胡言乱语，乱语|Unintelligible or nonsensical talk or writing.|He was talking complete gibberish. 他说的完全是胡言乱语。|近义词：nonsense, babble, drivel|反义词:sense, meaning|词性:名词
