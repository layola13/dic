greenest:/ˈɡriːnɪst/| adj.|最绿的，最环保的|Superlative form of "green," meaning having the most intense green color, or being the most environmentally friendly.|例句：The greenest valley I've ever seen. 我见过最绿的山谷。|近义词：most eco-friendly, most sustainable|反义词：brownest, most polluting|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Old English "grēne," meaning "green, growing, verdant," related to "grow."  The "-est" suffix is a superlative ending.
