fluoroscopy:/flʊrˈɒskəpi/ | n. |荧光透视；荧光检查 | Examination of a part of the body by passing X-rays through it onto a fluorescent screen, so that the structure can be seen and its movements studied. | The doctor used fluoroscopy to guide the catheter into the correct position. 医生使用荧光透视引导导管进入正确位置。| Fluoroscopy was performed to assess the swallowing function.  进行了荧光透视检查以评估吞咽功能。 | 近义词: radioscopy | 反义词: None | noun
