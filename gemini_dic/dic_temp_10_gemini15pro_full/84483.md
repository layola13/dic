halter:/ˈhɔːltər (US), ˈhɔːltə(r) (UK)| n. |缰绳；笼头；（女装的）露背上衣|A rope or strap placed around the head of a horse or other animal, used for leading or tethering it. Also, a woman's top that ties behind the neck, leaving the back and shoulders bare. |例句：The horse's halter broke, and it ran away. 马的缰绳断了，它跑了。

例句：She wore a stylish halter top to the beach. 她穿着一件时髦的露背上衣去了海滩。|近义词：bridle, headstall (for animals); top, tank top (for clothing)|反义词: N/A |词性:noun


halter:/ˈhɔːltər (US), ˈhɔːltə(r) (UK)| vt. |用缰绳拴住|To put a halter on (an animal).|例句：He haltered the cow before leading it to the barn. 他给母牛套上缰绳，然后把它牵到谷仓。

例句：The stable boy haltered all the horses for the night. 马厩的男孩晚上把所有的马都拴好了。|近义词：tie, tether|反义词:untie, release|词性:verb
