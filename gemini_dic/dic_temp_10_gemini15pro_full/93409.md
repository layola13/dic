illiterate: /ɪˈlɪtərət/ (US), /ɪˈlɪtərɪt/ (UK)| n. | 文盲 | A person who is unable to read or write. | 例句：The government is working to reduce the number of illiterates in the country. 政府正在努力减少该国文盲的数量。 | 近义词：unlettered person | 反义词: literate person |词性: 名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Latin illiteratus ‘uneducated’, from in- ‘not’ + literatus (see literate).
