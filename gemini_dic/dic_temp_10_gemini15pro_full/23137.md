bouro:/ˈbʊəroʊ/ | n. |  A type of large wild ox |  A large, heavily built wild ox with a shaggy mane, found in parts of Southeast Asia.  It is closely related to the gaur. | The bouro is a powerful animal and can be dangerous if provoked. |  Synonyms: wild ox, banteng (sometimes used interchangeably, but technically a different species) | Antonyms: N/A (no direct opposite for an animal species) | Noun
