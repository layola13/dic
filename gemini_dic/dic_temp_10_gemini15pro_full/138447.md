novaculite:/ˌnoʊvəˈkjuːlaɪt/| n.|硅质岩，磨刀石|A hard, dense, fine-grained siliceous rock, used for sharpening tools.|  Novaculite is a popular material for sharpening knives because of its fine grit. 硅质岩因其细密的纹理而成为磨刀的常用材料。

Novaculite whetstones are highly prized by woodworkers. 木工们非常珍视硅质岩磨刀石。


|近义词：chert, flint, whetstone|反义词:|noun
