admire:/ədˈmaɪər(UK) / /ədˈmaɪr(US)/| vt.|钦佩，赞赏，欣赏|To regard with wonder, pleasure, and approval.|I admire your courage. 我钦佩你的勇气。

I admire her dedication to her work. 我钦佩她对工作的奉献精神。|近义词：respect, esteem, appreciate, value|反义词：despise, scorn, loathe|verb

admire:/ədˈmaɪər(UK) / /ədˈmaɪr(US)/| n.|钦佩，赞赏，欣赏 (较少用作名词)|A feeling of wonder, pleasure, and approval.|He spoke of her with admiration. 他钦佩地谈到她。

She felt a deep admiration for his talent. 她对他的才华深感钦佩。|近义词：respect, esteem, appreciation|反义词：disdain, contempt, scorn|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *admirer*, from Latin admirari "to wonder at," from ad- "to" + mirari "to wonder."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I admire his talent. = 我欣赏他的才华。
She admires people who are kind and generous. = 她钦佩善良慷慨的人。

一般过去时:
We admired the beautiful sunset. = 我们欣赏美丽的日落。
They admired his bravery in the face of danger. = 他们钦佩他在危险面前的勇敢。

现在完成时:
I have always admired her work ethic. = 我一直很钦佩她的职业道德。
He has admired her paintings for years. = 多年来，他一直很欣赏她的画作。

过去完成时:
She had admired him from afar for a long time before they met. = 在他们相遇之前，她仰慕他很久了。
They had admired the view for a while before continuing their hike. = 他们在继续徒步旅行之前欣赏了一会儿风景。

将来时:
I will admire anyone who achieves that goal. = 我会钦佩任何实现这一目标的人。
We will admire the scenery when we get to the top of the mountain. = 当我们到达山顶时，我们将欣赏风景。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would admire his honesty. = 如果我是你，我会钦佩他的诚实。
I wish I had the skills to do something so admirable. = 我希望我有能力做一些如此令人钦佩的事情。

被动语态:
Her dedication is admired by everyone. = 每个人都钦佩她的奉献精神。
His paintings are admired for their vibrant colors. = 他的画作因其鲜艳的色彩而受到赞赏。

疑问句:
Do you admire his work? = 你欣赏他的作品吗？
What do you admire most about her? = 你最钦佩她什么？

否定句:
I don't admire people who are dishonest. = 我不钦佩不诚实的人。
She doesn't admire his arrogance. = 她不欣赏他的傲慢。


PHRASE->
admire greatly = 非常钦佩
admire for = 因...而钦佩
secretly admire = 偷偷钦佩
openly admire = 公开钦佩
much admired = 备受赞赏
