mactra:/ˈmæktʃrə/
noun
鸟蛤，槽螺
A genus of marine bivalve mollusks, commonly known as trough shells or duck clams.  They have a characteristic triangular shape and are found in sandy or muddy substrates.
The beach was littered with mactra shells after the storm.  暴风雨过后，海滩上到处都是鸟蛤壳。
近义词: clam, bivalve
词性: noun
