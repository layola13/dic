chiselers:/ˈtʃɪzələrz/
n.
骗子；诈骗者；敲诈者
People who cheat or swindle others, especially in small amounts of money.
Those chiselers were caught trying to scam elderly people out of their savings. 那些骗子试图诈骗老年人的积蓄时被抓住了。
synonyms: swindlers, fraudsters, cheats, con artists, scammers
antonyms: honest people, philanthropists

That chiseler tried to shortchange me at the store. 那个骗子试图在商店少找我钱。
