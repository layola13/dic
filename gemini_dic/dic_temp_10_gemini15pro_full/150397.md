panoramical:/ˌpænərˈæmɪkəl/ | adj. | 全景的；全景式的 | Providing or relating to a wide, unbroken view. |  From a high vantage point, we had a truly panoramical view of the city. 从一个高的有利位置，我们看到了城市的真正全景。 | synonyms: panoramic, wide, sweeping, comprehensive | antonyms: limited, restricted, narrow | adjective

panoramical:/ˌpænərˈæmɪkəl/ | n. | 全景照片；全景画 | A photograph or picture representing a panorama. | The artist unveiled his latest panoramical of the Grand Canyon.  这位艺术家公布了他最新的科罗拉多大峡谷全景画。| synonyms: panorama, vista, landscape | antonyms: close-up, detail | noun
