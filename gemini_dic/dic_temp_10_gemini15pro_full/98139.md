insanitation:/ɪnˌsænɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. |不卫生，卫生条件差 |The state of being insanitary; lack of sanitation.  This refers to extremely poor hygiene or a generally unhealthy environment, especially relating to the disposal of sewage and garbage.|The insanitation in the refugee camp was appalling.  难民营的卫生状况令人震惊。|近义词:unhygienic conditions, filth, squalor|反义词:sanitation, hygiene, cleanliness|词性:名词
Etymology: in- (not) + sanitation (the systems for taking dirty water and other waste products away from buildings in order to protect people's health).
