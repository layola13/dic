nonadversarial:/ˌnɒnˌædvərˈseəriəl/ | adj. | 非对抗性的，非敌对的 | Characterized by the absence of conflict or opposition.  Relating to or denoting a method or approach in which parties work together rather than against each other. |  The two companies engaged in nonadversarial negotiations. 这两家公司进行了非对抗性谈判。 |  The research team adopted a nonadversarial approach to data sharing.  该研究团队采取了非对抗性的数据共享方法。 | cooperative, collaborative, conciliatory | adversarial, antagonistic, combative | adjective

Etymology->
From non- + adversarial.  "Adversarial" comes from the Latin "adversarius," meaning "opponent."
