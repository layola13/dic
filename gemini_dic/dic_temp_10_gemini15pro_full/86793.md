hemadynamometer:/ˌhiːməˌdaɪnəˈmɒmɪtər/ /ˌhiːməˌdaɪnəˈmɑːmɪtər/
noun
血压计
An instrument for measuring blood pressure.
The doctor used a hemadynamometer to check the patient's blood pressure. 医生用血压计检查病人的血压。
Synonyms: sphygmomanometer, blood pressure monitor, blood pressure gauge
Antonyms: None (Instruments don't typically have opposites.)
