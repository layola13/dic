churme:/tʃɜːrm/ | n. | 喧闹声，嘈杂声；一群人 | A confused noise or murmur; a crowd or throng. |  The churme of the marketplace was overwhelming. 市场上的喧闹声震耳欲聋。| 近义词：hubbub, din, clamor | 反义词：silence, quiet | 词性：名词

词源:  Middle English *churmen, probably from Old French charmer, to enchant, subdue with charms. The sense development appears to be from 'enchanting murmur' to 'confused noise'.

例句：
The churme of the children playing filled the air.
孩子们玩耍的嘈杂声充满了空气。


The sudden churme of the crowd startled the horses.
人群突然的喧闹声惊吓了马匹。
