optometrist:/ˌɒpˈtɒmɪtrɪst/ /ˌɑːpˈtɑːmɪtrɪst/| n.|验光师；配镜师|A healthcare professional who examines the eyes for visual defects and prescribes corrective lenses or other treatments.|例句：I need to schedule an appointment with my optometrist. 我需要预约我的验光师。

例句：The optometrist said I need a stronger prescription for my glasses. 验光师说我需要更强的眼镜处方。|近义词：eye doctor, ophthalmologist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *optométriste*, from Ancient Greek ὄπτομαι (óptomai, “I see”) + μέτρον (métron, “measure”).
