interaulic: /ˌɪntərˈɔːlɪk/
adjective
宫廷内的，朝廷间的
Occurring within a royal court or between royal courts.
The interaulic intrigue and power struggles were legendary. 宫廷内的阴谋和权力斗争是传奇性的。
The interaulic correspondence revealed the tensions between the two kingdoms. 朝廷间的通信揭示了两国之间的紧张关系。
近义词: courtly, royal, regal
反义词: public, common, plebeian
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *interaulicus*, from inter- "between" + aula "court, hall".
