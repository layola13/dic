chamberlain:/ˈtʃeɪmbərlɪn/| n.|侍从，管家；（市政当局的）财务主管|A chamberlain is an officer who manages the household of a monarch or other high-ranking person.  A chamberlain can also be a treasurer of a city or town.|The king's chamberlain announced the arrival of the queen. 国王的侍从官宣布女王驾到。

The city chamberlain is responsible for managing the city's finances. 市财务主管负责管理市财政。|Synonyms: steward, attendant, treasurer|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
