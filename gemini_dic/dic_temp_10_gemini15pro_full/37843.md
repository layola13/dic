competing:/kəmˈpiːtɪŋ/
adjective
竞争的，相互矛盾的
striving to win or gain something from (someone) by defeating or establishing superiority over them in a contest or race.
例句：There are five competing theories to explain the phenomenon. 有五种相互矛盾的理论来解释这一现象。

例句：The competing teams were fierce rivals. 互相竞争的队伍是激烈的对手。

近义词：rival, vying, contending, opposing
反义词：cooperative, collaborating, harmonious
词性: adjective
