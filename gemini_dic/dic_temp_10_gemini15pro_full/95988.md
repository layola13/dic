incurable:/ɪnˈkjʊrəbl/| adj.|无法治愈的，不可救药的|Not able to be cured or healed.|例句：He has an incurable disease. 他患有不治之症。|例句：Her optimism seemed incurable. 她的乐观似乎是不可救药的。|近义词：irremediable, terminal, hopeless|反义词：curable, treatable|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from in- ‘not’ + curable.
