packfong:/ˈpækfɒŋ/| n. | 白铜，镍银，洋白铜 |A copper-nickel-zinc alloy, also known as nickel silver or German silver. It resembles silver in appearance but does not contain silver. |例句：The cutlery was made of packfong. 这套餐具是用白铜做的。

例句：Packfong was a popular material for decorative items in the 19th century. 白铜是19世纪流行的装饰品材料。|近义词：nickel silver, German silver, alpacca, electrum |反义词: silver (in terms of composition, not appearance)|词性:noun
