availingly:/əˈveɪlɪŋli/
adverb|有效地，有益地|in a way that is helpful or effective. |He tried availingly to persuade her, but she wouldn't listen. 他努力有效地说服她，但她就是不听。|例句：She spoke availingly about the importance of education. 她有效地讲述了教育的重要性。|近义词：effectively, usefully, beneficially|反义词：uselessly, ineffectively, futilely|词性:副词
词源: From avail + -ing + -ly. Avail comes from Old French "availlir" (to be of use, to profit), from Latin "advalere" (to be strong, to be worth).
