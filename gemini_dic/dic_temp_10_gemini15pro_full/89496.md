honeystone:/ˈhʌnistəʊn/ (UK) /ˈhʌnistoʊn/ (US)| n. |蜜石, 黄金石|A kind of yellow jasper, also known as golden jasper. It's opaque and often has a waxy luster.  |例句：The artisan crafted a beautiful pendant from a piece of honeystone. 工匠用一块蜜石制作了一个漂亮的吊坠。

例句：Honeystone is often used in jewelry because of its warm, golden color. 蜜石因其温暖的金色而常用于珠宝制作。|近义词：golden jasper, yellow jasper|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
