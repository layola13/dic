indycars:/ˈɪndikɑːrz/| n.|印地赛车|Open-wheel race cars specifically designed and built for the IndyCar Series, a premier level of open-wheel, formula racing in North America.  The term is a shortened, pluralized form of "Indy car" referring to the Indianapolis 500, the most famous race in the series.|The indycars roared around the track.|Synonyms: formula cars, race cars|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
