belies:/bɪˈlaɪz/
verb
掩饰；与…不符；使…失望
to show something to be false, or to hide something such as an emotion
Her calm face belied the terror she was feeling. 她平静的脸上掩饰着内心的恐惧。
Synonyms: contradict, mask, disguise
Antonyms: reveal, expose, show, confirm
词性: 动词
