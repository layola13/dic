meeter:/ˈmiːtər/| n.| 会见者；迎接者；相逢者 |Someone who meets or greets another; one who encounters another. |例句：The official meeter at the airport welcomed the diplomats.  机场的官方迎接人员对外交官们表示了欢迎。

例句：The hotel employed a friendly meeter to greet guests upon arrival.  酒店雇佣了一位友好的迎宾员在客人到达时迎接他们。|近义词：greeter, welcomer, recipient |反义词:avoider, shunner |词性:noun

Etymology: From the verb "meet" + "-er" (agent suffix).  The verb "meet" has Old English origins, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *motjanan.
