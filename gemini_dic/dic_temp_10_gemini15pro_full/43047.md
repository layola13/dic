cowing:/ˈkaʊɪŋ/| adj. |使人畏缩的，吓唬人的|Causing someone to feel intimidated or fearful.|The dictator's cowing presence silenced the crowd. 独裁者令人生畏的出现让全场鸦雀无声。|The cowing stare of the teacher made the student nervous. 老师吓人的凝视让学生很紧张。|近义词：intimidating, frightening, daunting, menacing|反义词：emboldening, encouraging, reassuring|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "cow," meaning to intimidate or frighten.  "Cow" itself comes from Old Norse *kúga, meaning "to oppress."
