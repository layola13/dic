overcoolly:/ˌoʊvərˈkuːlli/
adverb|过于冷静地；冷漠地|in a manner that is excessively calm and composed, often suggesting indifference or detachment.|He overcoolly dismissed her concerns, making her feel insignificant. 他过于冷漠地打消了她的顾虑，让她觉得自己微不足道。|例句：She overcoolly accepted the news of her job loss. 她对失业的消息反应过于冷静。|近义词：calmly, indifferently, apathetically, dispassionately|反义词：excitedly, emotionally, passionately|词性: 副词
词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + coolly (adverb from cool, meaning "calm and composed")
