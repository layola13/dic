descendant:/dɪˈsendənt/ | n. | 后裔；子孙；派生物 | A person who is related to someone who lived a long time ago; something that has developed from something else. | She is a descendant of Queen Victoria. 她是维多利亚女王的后裔。|  The modern computer is a descendant of the abacus. 现代计算机是算盘的派生物。 | synonyms: offspring, heir, successor, progeny | antonyms: ancestor, forefather | noun
