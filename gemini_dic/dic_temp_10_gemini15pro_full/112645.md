词源:  "lowball" originated in the game of poker, referring to a deceptive tactic of betting low early in a hand to lure other players in, then raising the stakes later. This meaning was extended to other contexts in the mid-20th century.  "lowball"源于扑克游戏，指的是一手牌早期下注很低以吸引其他玩家，然后在后期提高赌注的欺骗策略。这种含义在20世纪中期扩展到其他语境。
