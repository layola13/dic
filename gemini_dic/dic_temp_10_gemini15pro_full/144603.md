outweep:/ˌaʊtˈwiːp/
verb
胜过…哭泣，比…哭得厉害
to weep more or more intensely than
She outwept all the other mourners at the funeral. 她比葬礼上所有其他哀悼者哭得都厉害。
近义词: outcry, lament, bewail
反义词: rejoice, celebrate
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *outwepen*, from Old English *ūtweopan* (“to weep out, bewail, lament”), from *ūt* (“out”) + *wēpan* (“to weep”). Cognate with Dutch *uitwenen*, German *ausweinen*.
