bracken:/ˈbrækən/| n. |蕨菜；欧洲蕨|A large, coarse fern that grows abundantly on hillsides and in woods. |The hills were covered in bracken.  这些山丘上长满了蕨菜。|  The fire spread rapidly through the dry bracken. 大火迅速蔓延过干燥的蕨类植物。| synonyms: fern, brake | antonyms: N/A | noun
