alliums:/ˈæliəmz/
n.
葱属植物
A genus of plants in the amaryllis family, characterized by a strong onion or garlic odor.
Onions, garlic, leeks, and chives are all alliums. 洋葱、大蒜、韭菜和细香葱都是葱属植物。
synonyms: onion family, Allium species
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, could list non-allium vegetables like carrots, potatoes)
noun
