fondon:/ˈfɒndɒn/ | n. | 翻糖，软糖 | A thick, creamy sugar paste used for icing cakes and making sweets. | The cake was decorated with elaborate fondon flowers. 蛋糕上装饰着精致的翻糖花。|  She learned how to make fondon roses. 她学会了如何制作翻糖玫瑰。| 近义词: icing, frosting | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词


词源: From French *fondant*, present participle of *fondre* "to melt," from Latin *fundere* "to pour, melt."
