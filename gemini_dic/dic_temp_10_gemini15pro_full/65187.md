ethology:/iːˈθɒlədʒi/ /iˈθɑːlədʒi/| n. |动物行为学|The scientific and objective study of animal behaviour, usually with a focus on behaviour under natural conditions, and viewing behaviour as an evolutionarily adaptive trait.|例句：Ethology has provided valuable insights into animal communication. 动物行为学为了解动物交流提供了宝贵的见解。

例句：Her research in ethology focuses on primate social structures. 她的动物行为学研究重点是灵长类动物的社会结构。|近义词：animal behavior, zoology|反义词: None|词性: noun
