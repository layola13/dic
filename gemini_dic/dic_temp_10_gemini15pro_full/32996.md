choriamb:/ˈkɔːriˌæmb/ | n. | 长短格;扬抑抑格 | A metrical foot consisting of a long syllable followed by two short syllables and then another long syllable (– ᴗ ᴗ –). |  The poet skillfully employed choriambs in his verse.  诗人巧妙地在诗歌中运用了长短格。 | 近义词: (No direct synonym, but related terms include) metrical foot, choree, iamb | 反义词: N/A | 词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek *χωρίαμβος (khōríambos), from χόριος (khórios, “trochaic”) + ἴαμβος (íambos, “iamb”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰer- (“to grasp, enclose”).
