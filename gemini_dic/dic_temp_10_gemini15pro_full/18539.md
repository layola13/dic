beneficial:/ˌbɛnɪˈfɪʃəl/| adj.|有益的，有利的|advantageous; resulting in good; helpful|例句：Regular exercise is beneficial to your health.  规律的运动对你的健康有益。|近义词：helpful, advantageous, profitable, salutary|反义词：harmful, detrimental, damaging|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin beneficialis, from beneficium ‘benefit’.
