bemoaner:/bɪˈmoʊnər/
n.|悲叹者，哀悼者|A person who expresses grief or disappointment about something.|She was a constant bemoaner of her fate. 她总是哀叹自己的命运。|Synonyms: complainer, lamenter, griever|Antonyms: celebrator, rejoicer|noun

bemoan:/bɪˈmoʊn/
vt.|悲叹，哀悼，抱怨|To express grief or disappointment about something.|He bemoaned the loss of his job. 他哀叹失去了工作。|Synonyms: lament, bewail, deplore|Antonyms: celebrate, rejoice|verb
