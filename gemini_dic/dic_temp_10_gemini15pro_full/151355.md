parkish:/ˈpɑːrkɪʃ/| adj.|公园似的，类似公园的|Resembling or characteristic of a park, especially in being spacious, pleasant, and green.|例句：The grounds of the mansion were extensive and parkish.  这栋豪宅的庭院广阔，像公园一样。

例句：The hotel had a parkish area for guests to relax in. 这家酒店有一个类似公园的区域供客人放松。|近义词：parklike, spacious, leafy, green, verdant|反义词：cramped, confined, urban, built-up|词性:形容词
