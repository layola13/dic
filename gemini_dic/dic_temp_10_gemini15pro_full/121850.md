misreports:/ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔːrts/
|v. (third-person singular simple present misreports, present participle misreporting, simple past and past participle misreported)|错误地报告；不如实报告|To report incorrectly or falsely; to give a wrong account of.|The media misreported the facts of the case. 媒体错误地报道了案件的事实.|Synonyms: falsify, distort, misrepresent, misstate|Antonyms: report accurately, represent truthfully|verb

misreport:/ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔːrt/
|n.|错误的报告|An incorrect or false report.|The witness later admitted to giving a misreport. 证人后来承认作了虚假陈述。|Synonyms:  falsehood, lie, fabrication, misrepresentation|Antonyms:  truth, accurate report|noun
