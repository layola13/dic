driven:/ˈdrɪvn/ | adj. | 驱动的，有动力的，执着的 | motivated or influenced by something |  He is a driven man, determined to succeed. 他是一个有进取心的人，决心要成功。|近义词: motivated, ambitious, determined |反义词: lazy, apathetic, unmotivated |词性:形容词

词源: Past participle of drive (verb), from Old English *drīfan, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch drijven and German treiben.
