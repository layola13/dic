dovetail:/ˈdʌvteɪl/| n. v.|燕尾榫;吻合;|A dovetail is a type of joint used in woodworking.  燕尾榫是一种用于木工的接合方式。|The two pieces of wood were dovetailed together.  这两块木头用燕尾榫连接在一起。|近义词：interlock, mesh, coincide|反义词：clash, conflict|n.
dovetail:/ˈdʌvteɪl/| n. v.|吻合; 协调;|Their plans dovetailed perfectly with ours. 他们的计划与我们的计划完全吻合。|The schedules dovetailed nicely, allowing us to attend both events.  时间安排非常吻合，让我们可以参加两个活动。|近义词：harmonize, agree, correspond|反义词：clash, conflict|v.
dovetail:/ˈdʌvteɪl/| n. v.|用燕尾榫接合;使…吻合; 使…协调;|He dovetailed the two pieces of wood together. 他用燕尾榫把两块木头连接在一起。|We need to dovetail our efforts with theirs. 我们需要使我们的努力与他们的努力相协调。|近义词：interlock, join, mesh|反义词：separate, disconnect|v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From "dove's tail," referring to the resemblance of the joint to a dove's spread tail feathers.
