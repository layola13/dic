monotonist:/məˈnɒtənɪst/ | n. | 单调主义者；单调乏味的人 | A person who speaks or acts in a monotonous way; a person who advocates monotony or sameness. |  He was a monotonist, always droning on about the same topics. 他是一个单调乏味的人，总是喋喋不休地谈论着相同的话题。 | synonyms: bore, drone, dullard | antonyms: enthusiast, lively person | noun

monotonous:/məˈnɒtənəs/ | adj. | 单调的；乏味的 | Dull, tedious, and repetitious; lacking in variety and interest. | The monotonous sound of the dripping tap drove me crazy. 水龙头滴水单调的声音让我抓狂。 | synonyms: tedious, boring, dull, repetitive, unvaried | antonyms: varied, interesting, exciting, stimulating | adjective
