hierarchical:/ˌhaɪəˈrɑːrkɪkl/  /ˌhaɪˈrɑːrkɪkl/
adjective|等级的，分层的|Arranged in order of rank or importance.|例句：The company has a hierarchical structure, with the CEO at the top.  这家公司有一个等级森严的结构，首席执行官位居顶端。

例句：Feudal society was rigidly hierarchical. 封建社会等级森严。
近义词：stratified, ranked, graded, ordered, layered|反义词：non-hierarchical, egalitarian|词性: adjective
