clef:/klɛf/
n.
谱号
A symbol placed on a musical staff to indicate the pitch of the notes.
The treble clef is used for higher notes. 高音谱号用于较高的音符。
The bass clef is used for lower notes. 低音谱号用于较低的音符。
sign, symbol
None
noun
