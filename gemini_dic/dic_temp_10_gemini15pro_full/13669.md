attraction:/əˈtrækʃn/| n.|吸引力；吸引人的事物；景点|Something that attracts or is intended to attract people by appealing to their desires and tastes.|The Eiffel Tower is a popular tourist attraction. 埃菲尔铁塔是一个很受欢迎的旅游景点。|近义词：appeal, lure, charm, magnetism|反义词：repulsion, aversion|noun

attraction:/əˈtrækʃn/| n.|吸引；引力|The force by which one object attracts another.|The attraction between the two magnets was strong. 两块磁铁之间的吸引力很强。|近义词：pull, gravitation, magnetism|反义词：repulsion|noun
