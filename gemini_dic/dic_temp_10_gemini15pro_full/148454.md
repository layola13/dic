overstraight:/ˌoʊvərˈstreɪt/
adjective
过于正直的；过分拘谨的；过分一本正经的
Excessively or rigidly straight, especially in moral or social attitudes.  Characterized by excessive adherence to rules or conventions, often to the point of being prudish or inflexible.
He was known for his overstraight demeanor, never cracking a joke or showing any emotion. 他以过分拘谨的举止而闻名，从不开玩笑，也不表露任何情感。

The overstraight community shunned her for her unconventional lifestyle.  过分拘谨的社区因为她不落俗套的生活方式而排斥她。
近义词:priggish, prudish, puritanical, strait-laced
反义词:liberal, permissive, unconventional
词性:adjective
