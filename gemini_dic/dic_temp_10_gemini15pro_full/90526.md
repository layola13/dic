huerta:/ˈwer.tɑː/ (US), /ˈwɛər.tə/ (UK)| n.|菜园，果园，种植园|A piece of irrigated land, generally enclosed, used for growing vegetables, fruits, or other crops.  It often implies a smaller-scale operation than a plantation or farm.|My grandmother has a beautiful huerta where she grows tomatoes and peppers. 我的祖母有一个美丽的菜园，在那里她种植西红柿和辣椒。|Synonyms: garden, orchard, vegetable garden|Antonyms: wasteland, desert|noun
