anthozoa:/ænθəˈzoʊə/ | n. | 珊瑚虫纲 |A large class of sedentary marine coelenterates that includes the sea anemones and corals.  They exist exclusively as polyps and are characterized by radial symmetry, a central mouth surrounded by tentacles, and a saclike internal cavity.|  Coral reefs are built by anthozoans. 珊瑚礁是由珊瑚虫纲动物建造的。 |Synonyms: coral, sea anemone |Antonyms: None | Noun

anthozoan:/ænθəˈzoʊən/ | adj. | 珊瑚虫纲的；属于珊瑚虫纲的 | Relating to or belonging to the class Anthozoa. | The anthozoan polyps create stunning underwater structures. 珊瑚虫息肉创造了令人惊叹的水下结构。 | Synonyms: corallian | Antonyms: None | Adjective
