outswim:/ˌaʊtˈswɪm/
verb
游得比…快；游得比…远
to swim faster or further than someone or something
He can outswim anyone on the team. 他比队里任何人都游得快。
近义词: outpace, surpass, beat
反义词: lose to, be outswum by
词性: 动词
