outjinx:/ˌaʊtˈdʒɪŋks/
verb
胜过；比...更厉害
To surpass someone in the use of jinxes or curses; to outdo someone in bringing bad luck or misfortune upon another.
She outjinxed him by wishing him a worse fate. 她诅咒他遭遇更悲惨的命运，从而胜过他。
近义词: outhex, outcurse
反义词: bless, protect
词性: verb
