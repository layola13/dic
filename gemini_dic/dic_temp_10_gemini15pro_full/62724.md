England.  This form is now considered archaic, poetic, or simply incorrect.  It implies multiple Englands which doesn't make geographical sense.
Of all the Englands I have seen, this is the fairest. (Archaic/poetic usage)
Synonym: England
Antonym:  (None -  as a proper noun denoting a place, it doesn't have a direct opposite)
