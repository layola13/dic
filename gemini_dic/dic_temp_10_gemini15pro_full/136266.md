nonrepentantly:/ˌnɒnrɪˈpɛntəntli/ | adv. | 无悔地，不后悔地 |  In a way that shows no regret or remorse for one's actions.  | He nonrepentantly admitted to his mistakes. 他毫不后悔地承认了自己的错误。 |  He nonrepentantly continued his disruptive behavior. 他继续着他破坏性的行为，毫无悔意。 | 近义词: unrepentantly, impenitently | 反义词: repentantly, remorsefully | 词性: 副词

词源:
From non- + repentant + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "repentant" comes from the Old French "repentant" (present participle of "repentir" meaning "to repent"), which ultimately derives from the Latin "repaenitere" meaning "to feel remorse."  "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
