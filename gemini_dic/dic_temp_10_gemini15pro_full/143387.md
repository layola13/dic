outfights:/ˌaʊtˈfaɪts/
verb
战胜，击败
defeats someone by fighting better
He outfought his opponent in the final round. 他在最后一轮击败了对手。
overpower, best, conquer, defeat, vanquish
lose to, surrender to, submit to
v.
