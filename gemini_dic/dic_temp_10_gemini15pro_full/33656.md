cinemograph: /ˈsɪnɪməɡræf/ | n. | 电影照片，动态照片 | A still photograph in which a minor and repeated movement occurs; created by taking a series of photographs or a video recording, and, using image editing software, compositing the photographs or the video frames into a seamless loop of animation. |  Example 1: The cinemograph showed the waterfall cascading down the rocks, while the rest of the scene remained still.  动态照片展示了瀑布倾泻而下，而场景的其余部分保持静止。

Example 2:  She created a beautiful cinemograph of a blinking eye, making it seem almost lifelike. 她创作了一幅美丽的眨眼动态照片，看起来栩栩如生。 | 近义词: animated GIF, living photo, cinemagraph | 反义词: still photo, photograph | 词性: noun
