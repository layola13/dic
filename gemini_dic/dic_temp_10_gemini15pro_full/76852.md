ganger:/ˈɡæŋər/| n.|工头；领班；一组工人；暴徒|A person who leads or is a member of a gang, especially one of workers.  A foreman or overseer, especially of a group of laborers or convicts.  A member of a violent criminal gang.|The ganger directed the workers to start laying the track. 工头指示工人开始铺设轨道。

The ganger was responsible for the safety of his crew.  领班负责其队员的安全。

The street gang was led by a ruthless ganger.  这个街头帮派由一个残忍的暴徒领导。|近义词：foreman, overseer, supervisor, leader, boss, thug, hoodlum|反义词:subordinate, follower|noun
