have-a-word:/hæv ə wɜːrd/ |v. | 谈谈；与…说几句话（通常指私下谈话或警告）| to speak briefly to someone, typically in private, especially to reprimand or warn them |"I need to have a word with you about your performance." 我需要和你谈谈你的表现。| synonym: chat, speak, discuss, talk | antonym: ignore, avoid | verb

have-a-word:/hæv ə wɜːrd/ |n. |  简短的谈话 | a brief conversation | "Can I have a word with you later?" 我可以稍后和你谈谈吗？| synonym: conversation, chat, discussion | antonym: silence | noun
