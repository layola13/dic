drab:/dræb/| adj.|单调的，乏味的；土褐色的；浅褐色的|Dull and uninteresting; lacking brightness or color.|The landscape was drab and gray.  这片景色单调乏味，一片灰蒙蒙的。|The walls were painted a drab brown. 墙壁被漆成了单调的棕色。|近义词:dull, dreary, monotonous, colorless, lackluster|反义词:bright, colorful, vibrant, exciting, interesting|词性:形容词

词源:From Middle English *drabbe, of uncertain origin. Possibly from a Scandinavian source (compare Icelandic drabbi dregs, Norwegian drav brewer's grains) or from Old French drap cloth, drapery.
