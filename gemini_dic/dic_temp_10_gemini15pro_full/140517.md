oleander:/ˌoʊliˈændər/ | n. |夹竹桃|An evergreen shrub of the dogbane family, with clusters of fragrant white, pink, or red flowers.  It is native to the Mediterranean region and is widely cultivated as an ornamental. All parts of the plant are poisonous. | The oleanders in the garden were in full bloom. 花园里的夹竹桃盛开了。| synonyms: rosebay, nerium | antonyms: N/A | noun


oleanders:/ˌoʊliˈændərz/ | n. |夹竹桃（复数）|Plural form of oleander. Refers to more than one oleander shrub. |  The oleanders lining the driveway were a vibrant pink.  沿着车道的夹竹桃是鲜艳的粉红色。| synonyms: rosebays, neriams | antonyms: N/A | noun
