disgustednesses:/dɪsˈɡʌstɪdnəsɪz/
n.
厌恶，憎恶
The state of being disgusted; feelings of strong dislike or revulsion.
His disgustednesses at the sight of the rotting food was evident.  他看到腐烂的食物显露出明显的厌恶。
The sheer disgustednesses of the situation overwhelmed her.  情况的极度令人作呕让她不知所措。
近义词:revulsion, repugnance, aversion, nausea
反义词:attraction, liking, pleasure
词性:名词

词源: From "disgusted" (adjective) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality). "Disgusted" comes from the verb "disgust," which originates from the Old French "desgouster" (to displease the taste), from "des-" (expressing reversal) + "gouster" (to taste).
