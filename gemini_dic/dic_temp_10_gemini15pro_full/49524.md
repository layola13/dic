demandress:/dɪˈmændrɛs/
n.
女原告；女债权人
a woman who demands something, especially payment of a debt or fulfillment of a claim.
She acted as demandress in the lawsuit against the company. 她在这场针对公司的诉讼中担任原告。
synonyms: claimant, plaintiff, petitioner
antonyms: defendant, respondent
noun
