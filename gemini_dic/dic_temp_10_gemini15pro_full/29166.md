cashabilities:/ˌkæʃəˈbɪlətiz/| n. | 变现能力；兑现能力 |The ability of an asset to be converted into cash quickly and without significant loss of value.  This refers to the ease with which assets can be turned into ready cash. |  The company's cashabilities were strong, allowing them to weather the economic downturn.  这家公司的变现能力很强，使他们能够安然度过经济低迷时期。 |  liquid assets, liquidity, convertibility | illiquidity, frozen assets | noun
