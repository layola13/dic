contagion:/kənˈteɪdʒən/| n.|传染，蔓延，感染; 接触传染病|The contagion spread rapidly through the school.  这种传染病在学校迅速蔓延。|例句：The doctor worried about the contagion spreading to other patients. 医生担心这种传染病会传染给其他病人。

例句：Fear is a contagion that can spread quickly through a crowd. 恐惧是一种会在人群中迅速蔓延的传染病。

近义词：infection, disease, contamination, spread|反义词:immunity, resistance|词性:noun

contagion:/kənˈteɪdʒən/| n.|有害的影响，不良情绪的蔓延|The contagion of violence spread throughout the city. 暴力蔓延到整个城市。|例句：The contagion of negativity can be detrimental to team morale.  消极情绪的蔓延会对团队士气有害。

例句：Her enthusiasm was a contagion, inspiring everyone around her. 她的热情很有感染力，激励了她周围的每个人。

近义词：influence, diffusion, proliferation|反义词:suppression, containment|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *contagio (“a touching, contact”), from Latin contingere (“to touch closely, seize upon, pollute”), from com- (“together”) + tangere (“to touch”).
