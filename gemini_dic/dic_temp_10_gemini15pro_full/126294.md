myochrome:/ˈmaɪəkroʊm/| n. |肌红蛋白|A red protein containing heme, which carries and stores oxygen in muscle cells. |Myoglobin releases oxygen to the muscles when they need it for activity. 肌红蛋白在肌肉需要活动时向肌肉释放氧气。|近义词: myohaemoglobin |反义词: None |noun

myochrome:/ˈmaɪəkroʊm/| adj. |肌红色的|Having the color of muscle. |The myochrome tissue was clearly visible under the microscope. 肌红色组织在显微镜下清晰可见。|近义词: flesh-colored, reddish-brown |反义词: None |adjective
