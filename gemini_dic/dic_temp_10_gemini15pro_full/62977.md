enodis:/ˈiːnəʊdɪs/| adj.|无节的，光滑的|Smooth, without knots or joints.  Relating to a stem or other plant part that lacks nodes. |The enodis stem of the plant provided little support.  这种植物的光滑茎干几乎无法提供支撑。|近义词：smooth, even, unjointed|反义词:nodose, knotted, jointed|词性:形容词
