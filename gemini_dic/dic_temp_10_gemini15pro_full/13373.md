atmosphere:/ˈætməsfɪr/ (US) /ˈætməsfɪə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 大气；气氛；环境|The earth's atmosphere is about 300 miles thick. 地球的大气层大约有300英里厚。|The atmosphere in the room was tense. 房间里的气氛很紧张。|近义词：air, ambiance, mood, environment|反义词:vacuum|noun

atmospheres:/ˈætməsfɪrz/ (US) /ˈætməsfɪəz/ (UK)| n.| 大气层（复数）；气氛；环境（复数）|The atmospheres of Mars and Earth are very different. 火星和地球的大气层非常不同。|The different atmospheres of the two parties made it difficult to reach an agreement.  两个党派截然不同的气氛使得很难达成协议。|近义词：airs, ambiances, moods, environments|反义词:vacuums|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *atmosphère*, from Ancient Greek *ἀτμός* (atmós, “vapor, steam”) + *σφαῖρα* (sphaîra, “sphere, globe”).
