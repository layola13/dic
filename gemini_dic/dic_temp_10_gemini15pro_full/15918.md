大胆，魄力，胆识 (plural of ballsiness)
Audacity; aggressive self-confidence; guts.  It refers to a quality of being bold, assertive, and willing to take risks.
Example:  She demonstrated remarkable ballsiness in starting her own business during a recession.  她在经济衰退期间创办了自己的公司，展现了非凡的胆识。

Example: The CEO admired the ballsinesses of his young employees who weren't afraid to challenge the status quo. 首席执行官欣赏他年轻员工的大胆，他们不怕挑战现状。

Synonyms: nerve, audacity, chutzpah, guts, grit, boldness, courage
Antonyms: timidity, cowardice, fearfulness, apprehension
