ominousnesses:/ˈɒmɪnəsnəsɪz/ | n. | 不祥之兆，凶兆 | The quality of being ominous; the state of foreboding evil.  A collection of ominous signs or feelings. | The ominousnesses surrounding the abandoned house made the children afraid to approach.  围绕着这栋废弃房屋的种种不祥之兆让孩子们不敢靠近。 | synonyms: forebodings, portents, premonitions | antonyms: auspiciousness, hopefulness | noun

ominous:/ˈɒmɪnəs/ | adj. | 不祥的，预兆的 | Giving the impression that something bad or unpleasant is going to happen; threatening; inauspicious. |  Dark clouds are often seen as ominous.  乌云通常被视为不祥之兆。 The silence was ominous.  寂静是不祥的。| synonyms: threatening, menacing, foreboding, portentous | antonyms: auspicious, promising, encouraging | adjective
