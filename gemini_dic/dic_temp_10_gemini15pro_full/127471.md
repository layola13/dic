necessitarian:/nəˌsesɪˈtɛəriən/
n.|必然论者；决定论者|A person who believes in necessitarianism; a determinist.|He argued against the necessitarian view of history, emphasizing the role of individual choice. 他反对历史必然论的观点，强调个人选择的作用。|Synonyms: determinist, fatalist|Antonyms: libertarian, voluntarist|noun
necessitarian:/nəˌsesɪˈtɛəriən/
adj.|必然论的；决定论的|Relating to or characterized by necessitarianism; deterministic.|His necessitarian philosophy left no room for free will. 他的必然论哲学没有给自由意志留下任何空间。|Synonyms: deterministic, fatalist|Antonyms: libertarian, voluntarist|adjective
