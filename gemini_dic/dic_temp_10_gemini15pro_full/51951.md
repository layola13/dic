diacatholicon:/ˌdaɪəkəˈθɒlɪkən/
noun
万能药；包治百病的药；(尤指中世纪的)解毒剂
A universal remedy; a panacea, especially an antidote (chiefly in the Middle Ages).
The alchemist claimed to have discovered a diacatholicon that would cure any illness. 炼金术士声称发现了一种可以治愈任何疾病的万能药。
synonyms: panacea, cure-all, elixir, catholicon
antonyms: poison, toxin
