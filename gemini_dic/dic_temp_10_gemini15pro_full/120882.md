misbrewed:/ˌmɪsˈbruːd/
adjective
酿造失败的；调制失败的
Improperly or unsuccessfully brewed.  The beer tasted strange; it seemed to be misbrewed. 这啤酒味道很怪，好像酿坏了。

例句：
The misbrewed potion caused unexpected side effects.
酿造失败的药水引起了意想不到的副作用。

The misbrewed coffee was too bitter to drink.
这杯煮坏的咖啡太苦了，没法喝。


近义词：badly brewed, poorly brewed, spoiled
反义词：well-brewed, properly brewed

词性：adjective
