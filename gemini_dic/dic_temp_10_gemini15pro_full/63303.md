enthymematical: /ˌɛnθaɪˈmɛmətɪkəl/ /ˌɛnθaɪˈmætɪkəl/
adjective
隐含的，不完全表达的
Based on or involving an enthymeme, a syllogism in which one of the premises is not explicitly stated.
Her argument was enthymematical, relying on unspoken assumptions shared by her audience. 她论点是隐含的，依赖于听众共有的未明说的假设。

The politician's speech was full of enthymematical reasoning, leaving much to the audience's interpretation.  这位政治家的演讲充满了隐含的推理，留给听众很多解释的空间。
