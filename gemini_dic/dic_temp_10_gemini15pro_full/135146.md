nonoily:/nɒnˈɔɪli/ | adj. | 不油腻的，无油的 | Not oily; free from oil. |  This moisturizer is nonoily and perfect for my skin. 这款保湿霜不油腻，非常适合我的皮肤。|  The chicken was cooked perfectly, nonoily and juicy.  这只鸡做得非常好，不油腻而且多汁。 |近义词: greaseless, non-greasy | 反义词: oily, greasy | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
"non-" (prefix) + "oily" (adjective).  "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not," and "oily" comes from the Old English word "ælig."
