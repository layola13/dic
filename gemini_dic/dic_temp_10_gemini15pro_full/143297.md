outdressing: /ˌaʊtˈdrɛsɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的穿着；比…穿得更漂亮
surpassing someone in elegance or stylishness of dress
例句：She outdressed everyone at the party. 她在派对上比任何人都穿得漂亮。
例句：He tried to outdress his rival. 他试图在穿着上胜过他的对手。

近义词：outshining, eclipsing
反义词：underdressing

词性：v.
