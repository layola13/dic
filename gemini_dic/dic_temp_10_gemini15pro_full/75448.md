fruition:/fruːˈɪʃən/| n.|实现; 完成; 结果实; 成熟|The project finally came to fruition after years of hard work.  这个项目经过多年的努力终于实现了。|The plans have come to fruition sooner than expected. 这些计划比预期更早地实现了。|近义词：realization, accomplishment, fulfillment, completion|反义词:failure, frustration|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French fruicion or Latin fruitio(n-), from frui ‘enjoy’.
