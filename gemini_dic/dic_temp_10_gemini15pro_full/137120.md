nonsubordinating:/ˌnɒnsəˈbɔːdɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非从属的；并列的
Not subordinating; coordinating.  A nonsubordinating conjunction connects two independent clauses.
He used a nonsubordinating conjunction like "and" or "but" to join the two sentences. 他用像“和”或“但”这样的并列连词连接了两个句子。
The two clauses were joined by a nonsubordinating conjunction, creating a compound sentence.  这两个分句由一个并列连词连接，形成了一个复合句。
synonyms: coordinating, connecting, joining, linking
antonyms: subordinating, dependent
词性: adjective
