chipolata:/ˌtʃɪpəˈlɑːtə/| n.|小香肠，脆皮小香肠|A small, thin sausage, often served grilled or fried. Chipolatas are popular in British and Irish cuisine, often served with breakfast or as part of a roast dinner.|We had chipolatas with our scrambled eggs. 我们早餐吃了脆皮小香肠和炒蛋。|The chipolatas were perfectly cooked, crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside. 脆皮小香肠做得恰到好处，外皮酥脆，内里多汁。|近义词:sausage, link|反义词:None|词性:noun

词源: From French chipolata, from Italian cipollata "onion sauce," from cipolla "onion," from Latin *cēpulla, diminutive of cēpa "onion." The sausages were originally flavored with onions, hence the name.  虽然名字来源于洋葱，但现代的chipolata香肠通常不含洋葱。
