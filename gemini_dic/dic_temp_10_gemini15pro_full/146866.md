overinflated:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪtɪd/
adjective
过度膨胀的；充气过多的
inflated beyond normal or desirable limits; exaggerated.
The tires were overinflated, making the ride bumpy. 轮胎充气过多，导致行驶颠簸。
The CEO's ego was overinflated by years of constant praise. 多年的不断赞扬让这位CEO的自负心膨胀过度。
Synonyms: inflated, overblown, exaggerated, pompous
Antonyms: deflated, understated, modest
词性: 形容词
