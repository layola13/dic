medullary:/ˈmɛdəˌlɛri/ (US), /ˈmɛdjʊləri/ (UK)| adj.|髓质的，骨髓的，延髓的|Relating to, or consisting of, marrow or medulla; resembling marrow or medulla.|The medullary cavity of the bone contains marrow.  骨骼的髓腔包含骨髓。|The doctor examined the patient's medullary reflexes. 医生检查了病人的延髓反射。|近义词：central, inner, core|反义词：cortical, peripheral|词性:形容词

medullary:/ˈmɛdəˌlɛri/ (US), /ˈmɛdjʊləri/ (UK)| n.|髓质|The inner, soft part of an organ, such as the kidney or adrenal gland.|Damage to the renal medullary can lead to kidney failure. 肾髓质的损伤会导致肾衰竭。|The adrenal medullary produces adrenaline. 肾上腺髓质产生肾上腺素。|近义词：marrow, pith|反义词：cortex|词性:名词
