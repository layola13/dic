feoffee:/ˈfiːfiː/
n.
受封者，封臣
A person who is granted a feoffment (an estate in land)
The feoffee held the land in trust for the original grantor. 受封者为原授予人代管土地。
The feoffee was responsible for managing the estate. 受封者负责管理庄园。
近义词:grantee, beneficiary
反义词:feoffor, grantor
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French feffé, past participle of feffer "to enfeoff," from Medieval Latin feoffare, of Germanic origin.
