erratical:/ɪˈrætɪkəl/
adj.
古怪的，反复无常的，不稳定的
deviating from the usual or proper course in conduct or opinion; eccentric; queer
Her erratic behavior worried her parents. 她的古怪行为使她的父母担心。
Synonyms: unpredictable, inconsistent, capricious, volatile, fickle
Antonyms: consistent, steady, stable, predictable, regular
词性: 形容词
