mendacity:/mɛnˈdæsɪti/
n.
不诚实，谎言，虚伪
The quality or state of being mendacious; untruthfulness; tendency to lie.
His mendacity was breathtaking; he seemed to lie effortlessly. 他的谎言令人震惊；他似乎毫不费力地撒谎。
近义词：dishonesty, falsehood, untruthfulness, deceit, fabrication, prevarication, lying
反义词：honesty, truthfulness, veracity
词性：名词

mendacious:/mɛnˈdeɪʃəs/
adj.
撒谎的，虚假的，不真实的
Given to lying; habitually dishonest.
Politicians are often seen as mendacious. 政客常常被视为虚伪的。
近义词：lying, dishonest, untruthful, deceitful, false
反义词：honest, truthful, veracious
词性：形容词
