breakpoint:/ˈbreɪkpɔɪnt/| n.|断点；临界点；突破点；转折点|A point in a program at which execution can be halted for debugging purposes.  A point at which something changes significantly.|The negotiators reached a breakpoint in the talks.|近义词：turning point, critical point, threshold, milestone|反义词: continuation, standstill|noun
