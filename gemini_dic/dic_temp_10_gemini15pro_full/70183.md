fibrillation:/ˌfɪbrɪˈleɪʃən/ /ˌfaɪbrɪˈleɪʃən/| n. |颤动，纤维性颤动|A rapid, irregular, and unsynchronized contraction of muscle fibers.|Atrial fibrillation is a common heart rhythm disorder. 心房颤动是一种常见的心律失常。|近义词：tremor, quivering, flutter|反义词：stability, stillness|词性：名词

fibrillation:/ˌfɪbrɪˈleɪʃən/ /ˌfaɪbrɪˈleɪʃən/| n. |细微震颤，原纤颤动|A quivering or tremor, often referring to muscle fibers.|Ventricular fibrillation is a life-threatening heart rhythm disorder. 心室颤动是一种危及生命的心律失常。|近义词：twitching, palpitation|反义词：rest, calm|词性：名词
