come-to-no-good:/ˌkʌm tə ˈnoʊ ˌɡʊd/
adjective
没有出息的;  注定要失败的
likely to fail or become bad
He was always getting into trouble, and everyone said he would come to no good.  他总是惹麻烦，每个人都说他不会有出息。
近义词: ill-fated, doomed, headed for trouble
反义词: promising, successful, fortunate
词性: adjective

come-to-no-good:/ˌkʌm tə ˈnoʊ ˌɡʊd/
noun
没出息的人；坏蛋
a person who is likely to fail or become bad
The gang was made up of come-to-no-goods who terrorized the neighborhood.  这帮派是由一群在附近制造恐怖的坏蛋组成的。
近义词: miscreant, scoundrel, rogue
反义词: role model, paragon, saint
词性: noun
