endogenous:/ɛnˈdɒdʒɪnəs/ /ɛnˈdɑːdʒɪnəs/| adj.|内生的，内源性的|Growing or originating from within an organism or system.|例句：Endogenous growth theory emphasizes innovation as the driver of economic growth. 内生增长理论强调创新是经济增长的驱动力。

例句：The patient's depression was thought to be endogenous, rather than caused by external factors. 患者的抑郁症被认为是内源性的，而不是由外部因素引起的。|近义词：internal, inherent, innate|反义词：exogenous, external|词性:形容词
