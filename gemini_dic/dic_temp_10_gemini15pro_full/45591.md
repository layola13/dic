/ˈkɜːrənsiz/ | n. |流通，流行|The fact that something is used or accepted by many people.|Example: The rumour gained wide currency.  这个谣言流传甚广。|Synonym: prevalence, circulation, popularity|Antonym: rarity, scarcity|noun
