bustees:/bʌsˈtiːz/
n.
贫民窟，棚户区
Slums, shantytowns; specifically, densely populated, impoverished areas often found in Indian cities.
The bustees of Kolkata are overcrowded and lack basic amenities. 加尔各答的贫民窟人满为患，缺乏基本设施。
The documentary shed light on the harsh realities of life in the bustees. 这部纪录片揭示了贫民窟生活的残酷现实。
slums, shantytowns
(No direct antonym)
