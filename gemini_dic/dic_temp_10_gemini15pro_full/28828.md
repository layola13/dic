carp-entered:/ˈkɑːrpˌɛntərd/
adjective
木工制作的；木结构的
constructed by a carpenter; made of wood
The carp-entered cabin was sturdy and well-built. 木结构的小屋坚固且建造精良。
carp-entered:/ˈkɑːrpˌɛntərd/
adjective
粗糙的；拙劣的；笨拙的
crudely or clumsily made
His carp-entered attempt at a birdhouse fell apart in the wind. 他笨手笨脚地做的鸟舍在风中散架了。

近义词:wooden, timbered, rough, crude, clumsy
反义词:sophisticated, refined, elegant

词性:adjective
