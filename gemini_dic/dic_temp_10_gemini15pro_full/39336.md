conjecturing:/kənˈdʒɛktʃərɪŋ/| n.| 推测，猜想 |The act of forming an opinion or supposition about something without proof or sufficient evidence. |  Scientists are still conjecturing about the origin of the universe. 科学家们仍在推测宇宙的起源。|近义词：guessing, speculating, hypothesizing, surmising|反义词：knowing, proving, confirming| v.
conjecture: /kənˈdʒɛktʃər/ | v. n.|推测，猜想|To form an opinion or supposition about (something) on the basis of incomplete information.| I can only conjecture about his reasons for leaving. 我只能推测他离开的原因。|近义词：guess, speculate, hypothesize, surmise|反义词：know, prove, confirm| v. n.
