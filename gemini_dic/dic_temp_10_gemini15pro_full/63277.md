enthralling:/ɪnˈθrɔːlɪŋ/| adj.|迷人的，引人入胜的|Capturing and holding the attention; fascinating.|The enthralling performance captivated the audience. 这场引人入胜的表演吸引了观众。|近义词：captivating, fascinating, mesmerizing, gripping|反义词：boring, dull, tedious, uninteresting|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From en- + thrall (n.) "bondage, captivity," from Old Norse *thrallr "slave, serf," from Proto-Germanic *thrallaz.  The original sense was "to enslave," later "to hold spellbound, fascinate."
