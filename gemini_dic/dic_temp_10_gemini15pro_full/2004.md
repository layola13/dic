actuality: /ˌæktʃuˈæləti/ | n. | 现实；实际情况；事实 | The actuality of the situation was far worse than they had imagined.  现实情况比他们想象的要糟糕得多。|  In actuality, the project is much more complex. 事实上，这个项目要复杂得多。 | synonyms: reality, fact, truth | antonyms: fantasy, illusion, unreality | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: actualite, from Old French actualité, from late Latin actualitas, from actualis ‘acting, active’ (see actual).
