obsessionally:/əbˈseʃənəli/
adverb
痴迷地，着魔地
In a manner characterized by obsession; compulsively.
He obsessionally checked his phone for messages. 他不停地查看手机上的信息。
She cleaned the house obsessionally. 她疯狂地打扫房子。
近义词:compulsively, fixatedly, fanatically
反义词:casually, indifferently, moderately
词性:副词

obsession:/əbˈseʃən/
noun
痴迷，着魔
A persistent, intrusive, and unwanted thought or idea that causes anxiety or distress; an excessive preoccupation with something.
He has an obsession with cleanliness. 他有洁癖。
Her obsession with him is unhealthy. 她对他的迷恋是不健康的。
近义词:fixation, preoccupation, mania
反义词:indifference, apathy
词性:名词

obsess:/əbˈses/
verb
使着迷，使困扰
To preoccupy the mind of excessively; to haunt or trouble persistently.
The idea obsessed him all day. 这个想法整天困扰着他。
She's obsessed with losing weight. 她一心想着减肥。
近义词:preoccupy, haunt, consume
反义词:ignore, neglect
词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin *obsessio*, from Latin obsessus, past participle of obsidere “to besiege,” from ob- “against, toward” + sedēre “to sit.”
