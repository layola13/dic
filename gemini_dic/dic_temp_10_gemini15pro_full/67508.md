extractable:/ɪkˈstræktəbl/| adj.|可提取的，可抽出的|Able to be extracted or removed.|The metal is extractable from the ore. 这种金属可以从矿石中提取出来.|近义词：removable, obtainable, separable|反义词:non-extractable, inseparable|词性:形容词
词源: From extract + -able.  "extract" comes from Latin extractus, past participle of extrahere "to draw out," from ex- "out" + trahere "to draw."
