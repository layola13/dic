discord:/ˈdɪskɔːrd/ (US), /ˈdɪskɔːd/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|不和，纷争；冲突；不一致|lack of agreement or harmony between people or things; disagreement; conflict|The constant discord between the two roommates made living together impossible. 两人之间持续不断的纷争使得他们无法继续合住。|近义词：conflict, strife, disagreement, disharmony, contention|反义词：harmony, agreement, concord, unity|词性:名词, 动词

discord:/dɪˈskɔːrd/ (US), /dɪˈskɔːd/ (UK)| vi. vt.|不一致，不协调；冲突|to disagree or clash|His actions discord with his words. 他的行为与言辞不符。|近义词：clash, conflict, disagree|反义词：harmonize, agree, accord|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French descord (“disagreement, discord”), from Latin discordāre (“to disagree”), from dis- (“apart, away”) + cordāre (“to be of one heart”), from cor (“heart”).
