hotspot:/ˈhɒtˌspɒt/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtˌspɑːt/ (US)| n. |热点，热区，危险地区，热门地点|A place of significant activity, danger, or controversy.  A public place where a wireless local area network (WLAN) is accessible.|
例句：This cafe is a popular hotspot for students.  这家咖啡馆是学生们的热门地点。

例句：The border region has become a hotspot for violence. 边境地区已成为暴力事件频发的热点地区。

近义词：focal point, center, hub, trouble spot, flashpoint|
反义词：backwater, quiet zone|
词性：noun
