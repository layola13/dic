Himmler:/ˈhɪmlər/| n.|海因里希·希姆莱|Heinrich Himmler (1900-1945) was a leading member of the Nazi Party and one of the most powerful men in Nazi Germany. He was Reichsführer of the Schutzstaffel (SS), a military organization under Adolf Hitler and the Geheime Staatspolizei (Gestapo), the secret state police.|Himmler was responsible for the Holocaust.|近义词:Nazi leader, SS chief|反义词:None (due to the nature of the person)|词性:名词
