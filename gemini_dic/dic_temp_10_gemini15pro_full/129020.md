-ism:/ɪzəm/
n.
主义，学说，制度，行为，特征
A distinctive practice, system, or philosophy, typically a political ideology or an artistic movement.
例句：
Capitalism is an economic system. 资本主义是一种经济制度。

Consumerism is rampant in modern society. 消费主义在现代社会非常盛行。

近义词：doctrine, philosophy, ideology, theory
反义词：(Often context-dependent, no single perfect antonym)
