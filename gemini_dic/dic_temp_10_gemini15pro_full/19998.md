biohazard: /ˈbaɪoʊˌhæzərd/ (US) /ˈbaɪəʊˌhæzərd/ (UK)| n.|生物危害|A biological agent or condition that constitutes a threat to humans, especially in the context of infectious diseases or contaminated materials.|The lab was sealed off due to a biohazard.  实验室因生物危害被封锁了。|Synonyms: biological threat, bio-risk, contagion|Antonyms: safety, security|noun

biohazard: /ˈbaɪoʊˌhæzərd/ (US) /ˈbaɪəʊˌhæzərd/ (UK)| adj.|生物危害的|Describing something that poses a biological threat.|The biohazard symbol was clearly displayed on the container.  容器上清楚地标示了生物危害符号。|Synonyms: dangerous, contaminated, infectious|Antonyms: safe, harmless|adjective
