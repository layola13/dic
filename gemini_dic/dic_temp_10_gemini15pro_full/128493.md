newspeaks:/ˈnjuːzspiːks/ | n. |  新闻腔; 新闻用语;  |  Language, especially that used in official statements or broadcasts, that is designed to impress rather than to inform clearly. |  The politician's speech was full of newspeaks and empty promises.  这位政客的演讲充满了新闻腔和空洞的承诺。 | synonyms: jargon, doublespeak, propaganda, officialese | antonyms: plain language, straightforward language | noun
