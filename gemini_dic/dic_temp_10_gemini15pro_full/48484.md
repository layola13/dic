deepavali:/ˌdiːpəˈvɑːli/ /ˌdiːpəˈwɑːli/| n.|排灯节，印度教节日|Deepavali, also known as Diwali, is one of the most important festivals celebrated by Hindus, Jains, Sikhs and some Buddhists, notably Newar Buddhists.  排灯节，也称为光明节，是印度教徒、耆那教徒、锡克教徒和一些佛教徒，特别是尼瓦尔佛教徒庆祝的最重要的节日之一。|We celebrate Deepavali with lights and fireworks. 我们用灯和烟火庆祝排灯节。|The family gathered for a special meal on Deepavali. 家人们聚在一起吃排灯节的特殊餐点。|近义词：Diwali, Festival of Lights|反义词: None|词性:noun

deepavali:/ˌdiːpəˈvɑːli/ /ˌdiːpəˈwɑːli/| n.|灯节|Deepavali literally means "row of lights" and symbolizes the spiritual "victory of light over darkness, good over evil, and knowledge over ignorance". 排灯节的字面意思是“一排灯”，象征着精神上的“光明战胜黑暗，善良战胜邪恶，知识战胜无知”。|During Deepavali, people light lamps and decorate their homes. 在排灯节期间，人们点燃灯笼并装饰他们的家。|Many businesses offer Deepavali sales and promotions. 许多商家提供排灯节的促销活动。|近义词：Diwali, Festival of Lights|反义词: None|词性:noun
