parodist: /ˈpærədɪst/ | n. | 滑稽者;拙劣的模仿者;歪曲者| A person who produces parodies.  Someone who imitates the style of someone or something else in a humorous or satirical way. |  The parodist cleverly mimicked the politician's distinctive voice and mannerisms.  这位滑稽模仿者巧妙地模仿了这位政客独特的嗓音和举止。 |  imitator, satirist, mocker | antagonist, original artist | noun
