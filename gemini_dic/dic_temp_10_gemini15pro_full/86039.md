healthier:/ˈhelθiər/ (US), /ˈhelθɪər/ (UK)| adj.|更健康的|Comparative form of "healthy," indicating a greater degree of health.  It suggests being in a better state of physical or mental well-being than something else or a previous state.|例句：Eating more vegetables is a healthier option.  多吃蔬菜是一个更健康的选择。

例句：He looks healthier after his vacation.  他度假回来后看起来更健康了。

近义词：fitter, stronger, well, robust|反义词:unhealthier, sicker, weaker|词性:adjective
