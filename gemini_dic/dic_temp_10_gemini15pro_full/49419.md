dells:/dɛlz/
n.
小峡谷，溪谷
Small valleys, usually wooded.
We hiked through the shady dells. 我们徒步穿过阴凉的小峡谷。
近义词: valleys, glens, ravines
反义词: peaks, summits
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *delle*, from Old English *dell* (“valley”), from Proto-Germanic *daliz* (“valley”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰolh₁- (“valley, hollow”). Cognate with Dutch *dal* (“valley”), German *Tal* (“valley”), Icelandic *dalur* (“valley”).
