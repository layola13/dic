disharmonious: /ˌdɪshɑːrˈməʊniəs/ (US) /ˌdɪshɑːˈməʊniəs/ (UK)| adj.|不和谐的，不协调的|Lacking harmony; discordant. Characterized by conflict or disagreement.| The disharmonious sounds of the construction site made it difficult to concentrate. 建筑工地的不和谐声音使人难以集中注意力。|The meeting ended in a disharmonious atmosphere. 会议在不和谐的气氛中结束。|近义词: discordant, inharmonious, incongruous, clashing, conflicting|反义词: harmonious, concordant, agreeable, compatible, consistent|词性: adjective
词源: From dis- (expressing negation) + harmonious, from harmony, from Old French harmonie, from Late Latin harmonia, from Greek harmonia "agreement, concord of sounds," from harmos "joint, fastening."
