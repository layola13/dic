fatboy:/ˈfætˌbɔɪ/| n.|胖子；懒人；豆袋椅|A beanbag chair, or a person perceived as lazy or overweight.|He sank into the comfortable fatboy. 他陷入了舒适的豆袋椅里。|近义词：beanbag chair, couch potato, slob|反义词: hard-worker, athlete|名词

fatboy:/ˈfætˌbɔɪ/| adj.|（非正式）肥胖的；懒惰的|Pertaining to or resembling a fatboy, particularly in terms of laziness or size. (Often considered derogatory.) |He had a rather fatboy lifestyle, spending most of his time playing video games. 他过着相当懒散的生活，大部分时间都在玩电子游戏。|近义词：lazy, obese, overweight|反义词: active, slim, thin|形容词
