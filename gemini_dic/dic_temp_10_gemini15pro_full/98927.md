intentively:/ɪnˈtɛntɪvli/| adv.|专心地，集中地，专注地|With close attention; purposefully.|She listened intently to the lecture. 她专心地听着讲座。|He studied the map intently, trying to find the hidden path. 他专心致志地研究地图，试图找到隐藏的小路。|近义词：attentively, carefully, closely, earnestly, diligently|反义词：inattentively, carelessly, distractedly|词性:副词
词源: From Late Latin *intentīvus, from Latin intentus (“stretched out, strained, intent”), past participle of intendere (“to stretch out, strain, intend”).
