floggers:/ˈflɒɡəz/ (UK) /ˈflɑːɡərz/ (US)| n. |鞭打者；笞刑者；严厉批评者|A person who flogs someone or something, especially as a punishment; someone who criticizes harshly.|The slave floggers were notorious for their cruelty. 奴隶鞭打者以其残忍而臭名昭著。|The critics were real floggers of the new play.  评论家们对这部新剧进行了严厉的批评。|Synonyms: whipper, scourger, critic, castigator|Antonyms: protector, defender|Noun
