overorganizing:/ˌōvərˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪzɪŋ/
|v.|过度组织；过分安排|The act of organizing something excessively, often to the point of inefficiency or inflexibility.|She was overorganizing the event, creating unnecessary stress for everyone.|近义词: overplanning, micromanaging|反义词: underorganizing, neglecting|动词

overorganized:/ˌōvərˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪzd/
|adj.|过度组织的；过分安排的|Excessively organized, often to the point of inefficiency or inflexibility.|Her overorganized schedule left no room for spontaneity.|近义词: overplanned, micromanaged|反义词: underorganized, neglected|形容词
