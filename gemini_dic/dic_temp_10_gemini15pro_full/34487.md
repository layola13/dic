claymore:/ˈkleɪmɔːr/| n.|双手阔剑，阔刃大剑，反步兵地雷|A large two-edged sword formerly used by Scottish Highlanders.|The warrior swung his claymore with deadly force.  战士挥舞着阔刃大剑，力量惊人。|Synonyms: broadsword, greatsword|Antonyms: dagger, knife|noun

claymore:/ˈkleɪmɔːr/| n.|反步兵地雷 (通常指M18A1)|An anti-personnel mine.|The claymore was set to detonate on a tripwire.  反步兵地雷被设定为绊线引爆。|Synonyms: mine, landmine|Antonyms: N/A|noun
