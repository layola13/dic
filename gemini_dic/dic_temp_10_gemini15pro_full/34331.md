clashers:/ˈklæʃərz/
noun
冲突者，发生冲突的人
People who clash or are involved in a clash.  Those who participate in conflicts, disagreements, or battles.
The clashers were separated by the police. 警察把冲突者们分开了。
The online game pitted clashers against each other in a virtual arena.  这个网络游戏让冲突者们在一个虚拟竞技场中相互对抗。
synonyms: combatants, rivals, adversaries, opponents, fighters
antonyms: allies, partners, collaborators, teammates
