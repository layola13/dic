misprogrammed:/ˌmɪsˈproʊɡræmd/ | adj. | 错误编程的，程序编写错误的 | Incorrectly programmed; having a program with errors. | The robot malfunctioned because it was misprogrammed.  这个机器人发生故障是因为它被错误地编程了。| The misprogrammed software caused the system to crash.  错误编程的软件导致系统崩溃。 |Synonyms: malfunctioning, faulty, buggy, flawed | Antonyms: correctly programmed, functioning, working | adjective

misprogram:/ˌmɪsˈproʊɡræm/ | vt. |  错误地编程 | To program incorrectly; to write a program with errors. |  I accidentally misprogrammed the thermostat, and the house got too cold. 我不小心错误地设置了恒温器的程序，导致房子太冷了。| He misprogrammed the drone, causing it to fly in the wrong direction. 他错误地设置了无人机的程序，导致它朝错误的方向飞行。 | Synonyms: miscode, misconfigure | Antonyms: program correctly, configure correctly | verb
