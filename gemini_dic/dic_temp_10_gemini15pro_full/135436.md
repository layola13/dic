nonpersonages:/ˌnɒnˈpɜːsənɪdʒɪz/ | n. | 无名之辈；非人物；小人物 | People deemed unimportant or insignificant, often in a political or historical context; those not considered historically relevant individuals.  |  The history books often overlook the nonpersonages who nevertheless contributed to society.  这些历史书籍经常忽略那些对社会有贡献的小人物。 |  The dictator declared his political opponents nonpersonages, effectively erasing them from public life.  独裁者宣布他的政敌为无名之辈，有效地将他们从公共生活中抹去。 | synonyms:  nobodies, insignificant people, commoners, the masses | antonyms:  VIPs, dignitaries, notables, celebrities | noun
