overbrowse:/ˌoʊvərˈbraʊz/ | v. | 过度放牧，过度啃食 | To graze so heavily that the vegetation is damaged. |  The deer overbrowsed the young trees, stunting their growth. 鹿过度啃食了幼树，阻碍了它们的生长。

The goats have overbrowsed the hillside, leaving it barren. 山羊过度放牧了山坡，使其变得荒芜。 | 近义词: overgraze | 反义词: undergraze | 词性:动词
