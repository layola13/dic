conscious-of:/ˈkɒnʃəs əv/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnʃəs əv/ (US)| adj.|意识到，注意到|Aware of; mindful of; sensitive to something.|She was very conscious of being the only woman in the room. 她很清楚自己是房间里唯一的女性。|例句：He was conscious of his heart beating faster. 他意识到自己的心跳加快了。|近义词：aware of, mindful of, alert to|反义词：unaware of, oblivious to|词性：形容词
