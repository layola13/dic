badaud:/bɑːˈdoʊ/ | n. | 闲逛者，漫步者，(尤指)好奇的旁观者 | A person who strolls about idly, especially one who gapes curiously. |  He was a badaud, always stopping to watch street performers. 他是一个闲逛者，总是停下来观看街头艺人的表演。| 近义词: onlooker, bystander, gawker, loafer, idler | 反义词: worker, participant | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From French *badaud*, from Old French *bader* "to gape, yawn," of uncertain origin.
