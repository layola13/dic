disjointed:/dɪsˈdʒɔɪntɪd/ | adj. | 脱节的，不连贯的，杂乱无章的 | lacking connection or consistency; disconnected | The novel felt disjointed, as if the chapters had been randomly assembled. 这部小说感觉脱节，好像章节是随意拼凑的。 |  Her speech was disjointed and difficult to follow. 她的演讲杂乱无章，难以理解。 | 近义词: disconnected, incoherent, rambling, fragmented, illogical | 反义词: coherent, connected, unified, consistent, logical | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From dis- + joint (v.) + -ed.  The prefix "dis-" signifies negation or reversal, meaning "not" or "apart."  "Joint" refers to the point of connection. Therefore, "disjointed" literally means separated at the joints.
