at the hands of:/æt ðə hændz ɒv/ | prepositional phrase |出自…之手；由…造成| Meaning "by the action of," especially indicating mistreatment or suffering inflicted by someone.  It emphasizes the agency of the person or group responsible. | The refugees suffered greatly at the hands of their oppressors.  这些难民在压迫者手中遭受了巨大的苦难。| Synonyms: because of, due to, from, by| Antonyms: by oneself, independently | phrase
