bleaches:/ˈbliːtʃɪz/
bleaches:v.|漂白, 变白, 使褪色|The sun bleaches the clothes. 太阳把衣服晒白了。|近义词：whitens, fades, lightens|反义词：darkens, colors|词性:动词(第三人称单数)
bleaches:/ˈbliːtʃɪz/
bleaches:n.|漂白剂|She uses bleaches to clean the bathroom. 她用漂白剂清洁浴室。|近义词：whitener, disinfectant|反义词：dye, colorant|词性:名词(复数)

词源:  From Middle English *blechen, from Old English blǣċan (“to bleach, whiten, fade”), from Proto-Germanic *blaikijaną (“to bleach”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰleg- (“to burn, shine”). Cognate with Dutch bleken (“to bleach”), German bleichen (“to bleach”), Swedish bleka (“to bleach”).
