oblongata:/ˌɒblɒŋˈɡɑːtə/ /ˌɑːblɔːŋˈɡɑːtə/
noun
延髓
The lowest part of the brainstem, continuous with the spinal cord; it controls vital functions such as breathing and heart rate.
The medulla oblongata is responsible for regulating many vital functions.  延髓负责调节许多重要的生命功能。
Damage to the medulla oblongata can be fatal.  延髓的损伤可能是致命的。
