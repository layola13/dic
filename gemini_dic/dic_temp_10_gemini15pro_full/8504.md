antibromic:/ˌæntiˈbroʊmɪk/
adjective
抗臭的; 防臭的
Preventing or counteracting foul odors.
Antibromic agents are used in some deodorants.  一些除臭剂中使用了抗臭剂。
The shoe inserts had antibromic properties.  鞋垫具有抗臭特性。
synonyms: deodorant, anti-odor, odor-fighting
antonyms:  malodorous, smelly, stinky
