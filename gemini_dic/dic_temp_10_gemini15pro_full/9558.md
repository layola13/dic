antishark:/ˈæntiʃɑːrk/
adjective
防鲨的；抗鲨的
Designed to repel or protect against sharks.
例句：
They developed an antishark wetsuit.
他们开发了一种防鲨潜水衣。

例句：
The resort invested in antishark measures to protect swimmers.
这个度假村投资了防鲨措施来保护游泳者。

近义词：shark-repellent, shark-proof
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective


词源:
"anti-" (against) + "shark"
