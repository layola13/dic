ante-room:/ˈæntiˌruːm/ /ˈæntiˌrʊm/
noun
前厅，接待室
A small room leading to a larger or more important one.
The guests waited in the ante-room before being admitted to the main hall. 客人们在被允许进入大厅之前在前厅等候。

The secretary showed me into the ante-room. 秘书把我带进了接待室。

近义词: waiting room, lobby, foyer, vestibule
反义词: main room, principal room
词性: noun
