disadjust:/ˌdɪsəˈdʒʌst/
verb
失调，混乱，使失调
To put out of adjustment; to disturb the adjustment or balance of.
The sudden change in temperature disadjusted the delicate instrument.  温度的突然变化使精密仪器失调。
近义词: disorder, derange, upset, disarrange, disorganize
反义词: adjust, regulate, order, organize
词性: verb

disadjust:/ˌdɪsəˈdʒʌst/
noun
失调，混乱
The state of being disadjusted.
The economic disadjust caused social unrest. 经济失调引发了社会动荡。
近义词: disorder, derangement, upset, disarrangement, disorganization
反义词: adjustment, regulation, order, organization
词性: noun
