nonreciprocities:/ˌnɒnˌrɛsɪˈprɒsɪtiz/
noun
非互惠性；非对等性
lack of reciprocity; absence of mutual exchange or action.
The trade nonreciprocities between the two countries led to an escalating trade war.  两国之间的贸易非互惠性导致了贸易战升级。
Nonreciprocities in international relations can create tension and mistrust. 国际关系中的非互惠性会造成紧张和不信任。
Synonyms:  asymmetry, imbalance, inequality, disproportion
Antonyms: reciprocity, mutuality, symmetry, balance
n.
