oleaginousnesses:/ˌoʊliˈædʒɪnəsnəsɪz/| n. |油腻；油滑；谄媚|The quality of being oleaginous; excessive oiliness, greasiness, or unctuousness, often in a figurative sense to describe insincere flattery or excessive praise.|His oleaginousnesses made everyone at the meeting uncomfortable. 他的油滑让会议上的每个人都感到不舒服。|近义词：unctuousness, oiliness, greasiness, smarm, slickness|反义词:sincerity, genuineness, honesty|Noun

词源:
From Latin *oleāginōsus* (oily), from *oleāgin-* (of oil) + *-ōsus* (full of).  Ultimately from *oleum* (oil).
