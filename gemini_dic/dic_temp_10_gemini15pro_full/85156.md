hardtop:/ˈhɑːrdtɒp/ (US) /ˈhɑːrdtɑːp/ (UK)| n. adj.|硬顶，硬顶汽车，硬顶敞篷车|A car with a rigid, non-removable roof, or such a roof itself.  Originally referring to a convertible car with a detachable solid roof, now also often used for cars with fixed roofs styled to resemble convertibles.|例句：He bought a classic hardtop sports car. 他买了一辆经典的硬顶跑车。

例句：The hardtop provided better insulation than the soft top.  硬顶比软顶提供了更好的隔热效果。|近义词：coupe, fixed-roof, closed car|反义词：convertible, soft-top|词性: noun, adjective

词源:  Compound of *hard* and *top*.
