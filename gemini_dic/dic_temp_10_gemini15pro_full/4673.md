alembic:/əˈlɛmbɪk/ | n. | 蒸馏器；蒸馏瓶 | An apparatus used for distillation, consisting of a vessel in which a substance is heated, a condenser for cooling and condensing the resulting vapor, and a receiver for collecting the condensed liquid. | The alchemist used an alembic to distill the essential oils.  炼金术士用蒸馏器提炼精油。 | synonyms: still, retort, distiller | antonyms: N/A | noun

alembic:/əˈlɛmbɪk/ | adj. (比喻) |  炼金术的; 升华的; 精炼的 | Relating to or suggestive of alchemy or transformation. | The poet's alembic imagination transformed everyday experiences into art. 诗人那炼金术般充满想象力的大脑将日常的经历转化为了艺术。 | synonyms: transformative, refining, alchemical | antonyms: stagnant, unchanging | adjective
