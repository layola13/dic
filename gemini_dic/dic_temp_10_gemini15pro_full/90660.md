humbag:/ˈhʌmbʌɡ/| n.|骗局，恶作剧；冒牌货|something that is not what it seems to be and is designed to trick people; a fake|例句：The weight-loss pills were a total humbug. 这些减肥药完全是骗人的。|例句：He pretended to be a doctor, but he was a humbug. 他假装是医生，但他是个骗子。|近义词：hoax, fraud, sham, fake|反义词：genuine article, real McCoy|词性:noun
