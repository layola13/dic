dumbfounderment:/dʌmˈfaʊndərmənt/ | n. | 惊愕，目瞪口呆 | The state of being dumbfounded; astonishment. |  The magician's sudden disappearance left the audience in utter dumbfounderment. 魔术师突然消失，让观众目瞪口呆。| 近义词: astonishment, amazement, bewilderment, stupefaction | 反义词: composure, calmness | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From dumbfounded + -ment. Dumbfounded comes from dumb (unable to speak) + confound (to confuse or perplex).
