nicotinic:/ˌnɪkəˈtɪnɪk/| adj.|尼古丁的；烟碱的|Relating to or resembling nicotine, especially in its physiological effects.|Nicotinic receptors in the brain are activated by nicotine. 大脑中的烟碱受体被尼古丁激活。|近义词: nicotine-related, cholinergic (specifically referring to the nicotinic subtype)|反义词: muscarinic (referring to the other main subtype of cholinergic receptors)|词性: adjective

nicotinic:/ˌnɪkəˈtɪnɪk/| n.|烟碱药; 尼古丁受体激动剂|A drug that acts on nicotinic receptors.|Some nicotinics are used to treat Alzheimer's disease. 一些烟碱药用于治疗阿尔茨海默病。|近义词: nicotinic agonist|反义词: nicotinic antagonist|词性: noun
