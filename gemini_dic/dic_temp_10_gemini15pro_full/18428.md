beltway:/ˈbeltweɪ/| n.|环城公路，环状道路|A highway that encircles an urban area.|例句：Traffic on the beltway was heavy during rush hour.  环城公路在高峰时段交通拥堵。

例句：They took the beltway to avoid driving through the city center. 他们走环城公路以避免开车穿过市中心。|近义词：ring road, bypass, loop|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "city center route")|词性:noun
