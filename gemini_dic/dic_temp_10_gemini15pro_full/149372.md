pacificatory:/pəˈsɪfɪkətɔːri/ /pəˈsɪfɪkeɪtɔːri/
adjective
抚慰的；调解的；息事宁人的
Tending to make peace; conciliatory.
例句：
The government issued a pacificatory statement in an attempt to calm public fears. 政府发表了一份抚慰人心的声明，试图平息公众的恐惧。

Her pacificatory tone helped to diffuse the tense situation. 她息事宁人的语气帮助缓和了紧张的局势。

近义词：conciliatory, appeasing, mollifying, propitiatory
反义词：provocative, antagonistic, inflammatory, belligerent
词性: adjective

pacificatory:/pəˈsɪfɪkətɔːri/ /pəˈsɪfɪkeɪtɔːri/
noun
调解人；调解物
A person or thing that pacifies.  (Less common usage)
例句：
He acted as a pacificatory between the warring factions. 他在交战派别之间充当调解人。(较少用)

The treaty served as a pacificatory after years of conflict. 该条约在多年的冲突后起到了息事宁人的作用。(较少用)

近义词：peacemaker, mediator, conciliator
反义词：agitator, instigator, warmonger
词性: noun
