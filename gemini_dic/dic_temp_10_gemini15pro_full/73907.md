forsooth:/fɔːrˈsuːθ/
|adv.|真的；确实；诚然|Indeed; truly (archaic or humorous).|例句：Forsooth, I know not. 诚然，我不知道。|例句：He declared, forsooth, that he was innocent. 他竟然宣称自己是无辜的。|近义词：indeed, truly, verily|反义词：falsely|词性：副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *forsothe*, from Old English *for sōþe* ("for truth"), equivalent to *for* + *sooth*. Cognate with Dutch *voorzeker* (“certainly”).
