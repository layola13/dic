easy-going:/ˈiːziˈɡəʊɪŋ/ /ˈiːziˈɡoʊɪŋ/| adj.|随和的，好相处的|Relaxed and tolerant in approach or manner.|例句：He's such an easy-going guy; he gets along with everyone. 他是个很随和的人，和谁都相处得来。

例句：She has a very easy-going personality and doesn't get stressed easily. 她性格随和，不容易感到压力。|近义词：relaxed, laid-back, amiable, affable|反义词：uptight, tense, demanding, picky|词性:形容词

词源:  Compound of *easy* and *going*, present participle of *go*.  The original sense referred to a horse with a smooth gait.  The figurative sense, referring to a person's temperament, developed in the 19th century.
