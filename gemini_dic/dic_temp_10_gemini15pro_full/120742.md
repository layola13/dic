misanalyzing: /ˌmɪsˈænəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
错误地分析；分析不当
Analyzing something incorrectly or reaching a flawed conclusion.
He is misanalyzing the data and drawing the wrong conclusions. 他错误地分析了数据，得出了错误的结论。
The report misanalyzing the market trends led to a poor investment decision.  这份对市场趋势分析错误的报告导致了一项糟糕的投资决策。
misinterpreting, misconstruing
correctly analyzing, interpreting accurately
verb
