flouters:/ˈflaʊtərz/
noun
蔑视者，藐视法律者
Those who treat rules or conventions with disdain or contempt.
The flouters of the law were brought to justice. 藐视法律的人被绳之以法。
synonyms: scoffers, mockers, scorners, deriders, jeerers
antonyms: conformists, adherents, followers
