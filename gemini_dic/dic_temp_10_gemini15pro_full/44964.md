crystalling:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
结晶的; 正在形成晶体的
The process of forming crystals.  The solution is crystalling as it cools.
The honey is crystalling in the jar.  罐子里的蜂蜜正在结晶。
Synonyms: solidifying, hardening, congealing
Antonyms: melting, dissolving, liquefying
word class: adjective

crystalling:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
结晶; 使...具体化; 使...明确;
The act of forming crystals. The sugar is crystalling on the sides of the pan.
His ideas began to crystallize into a solid plan. 他的想法开始具体化为一个可靠的计划。
Synonyms: solidifying, hardening, congealing, taking shape
Antonyms: melting, dissolving, liquefying, blurring
word class: verb
