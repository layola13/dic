fez:/fɛz/ | n. | 平顶圆锥形红色毡帽；土耳其毡帽 | A brimless, cone-shaped red felt hat, formerly the Turkish national headdress. | He wore a bright red fez. 他戴着一顶鲜红色的土耳其毡帽。| synonyms: tarboosh | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific type of hat) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Turkish *fes*, from Italian *fesso*, ultimately from Medieval Latin *festa*, "feast" (referring to the city of Fez in Morocco, where the dye used for the hats was produced, and where a festival was held).
