going-around:/ˈɡoʊɪŋ əˈraʊnd/ | adj. | 流行的；普遍的 | The going-around price for this type of car is about $20,000.  这种车的普遍价格大约是2万美元。|例句：That's the going-around fashion these days. 这是最近流行的时尚。|近义词：current, popular, prevalent |反义词: uncommon, rare | adjective
