papillate:/ˈpæpɪˌleɪt/ /ˈpɑːpɪˌleɪt/ | adj. | 乳头状的，有小突起的 | Covered with papillae or small nipple-shaped projections. |  The surface of the tongue is papillate. 舌头表面是乳头状的。 | The leaf's texture is rough and papillate.  这片叶子的质地粗糙且有小突起。 |近义词：papillose, mammillate, granular |反义词: smooth |词性: adjective

papilla:/pəˈpɪlə/ /pəˈpɪlʌ/ | n. | 乳头，小突起 | A small, nipple-like projection or elevation, as on the tongue or skin. | The taste buds are located on the papillae of the tongue. 味蕾位于舌头的乳头上。|  The flower petals have tiny papillae that give them a velvety texture.  花瓣上有微小的乳突，使它们具有天鹅绒般的质感。 |近义词：projection, protuberance, nipple |反义词: depression |词性: noun
