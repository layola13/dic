dissipate:/ˈdɪsɪpeɪt/| v.|消散，驱散，浪费|To disperse or scatter; to cause to disappear or vanish; to squander or waste.|The fog began to dissipate as the sun rose. 雾随着太阳升起而开始消散。|Synonyms: disperse, scatter, vanish, evaporate, squander, waste|Antonyms: gather, collect, accumulate, conserve|verb

dissipate:/ˈdɪsɪpeɪt/| adj. (rare)|消散的，分散的|Scattered, dispersed. |His dissipate attention made it hard for him to focus on any one task.  他分散的注意力使他很难集中精力于任何一项任务。|Synonyms: dispersed, scattered, diffused|Antonyms: concentrated, focused|adjective
