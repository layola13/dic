corporeally:/kɔːrˈpɔːriəli/
adverb
肉体地；身体上地

The shaman claimed to be able to corporeally transport himself to the spirit world.  萨满声称能够将自己的身体传送到精神世界。
He was corporeally punished for his crimes. 他因犯罪而受到肉体惩罚。
近义词:physically, bodily, carnally
反义词:spiritually, mentally
词性:adverb
