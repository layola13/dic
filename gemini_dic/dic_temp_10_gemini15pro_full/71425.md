flaker:/ˈfleɪkər/| n. |片状物; 薄片;  (尤指) 古怪的人; 不可靠的人; 放鸽子的人|A person who agrees to do something but doesn't follow through.  Someone unreliable, especially one who cancels plans at the last minute.|He's such a flaker; he always cancels plans at the last minute. 他真是个不靠谱的人，总是最后一刻取消计划。|Synonyms: no-show, dropout, unreliable person |Antonyms: reliable person, stand-up guy/gal|noun
