basto:/ˈbæstoʊ/| n.|巴斯托牌（一种西班牙纸牌）, 棍棒（纸牌花色）|A card game popular in Spain and Latin America, also referring to the club suit in such games. |He played the basto, winning the hand. 他打出了棍棒，赢得了这一局。|近义词：club (in card games)|反义词: None|noun

basto:/ˈbæstoʊ/| adj.|粗糙的，粗野的|Rough, coarse, or unrefined (primarily used in Spanish and Portuguese).  This usage is less common in English. |The fabric had a basto texture. 这块布料的质地很粗糙。|近义词：coarse, rough, crude|反义词: smooth, fine, refined|adjective
