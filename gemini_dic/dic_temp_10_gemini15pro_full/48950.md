degage:/deɪˈɡɑːʒeɪ/ /deɪˈɡɑːʒ/
verb
脱离; 离开; 走开
to leave or go away, especially when someone tells you to
Example: "Please, degage," she said coldly. “请你走开，”她冷冷地说。

Example: The officer told the crowd to degage. 警官命令人群离开。

近义词: leave, depart, go away, withdraw, retreat, clear out
反义词: arrive, stay, remain, approach

词性: verb
