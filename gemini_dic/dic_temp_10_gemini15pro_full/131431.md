noncloser:/ˈnɑːnˌkləʊzər/ /ˈnɑːnˌkloʊzər/| n.|未完成者；未结束者；（尤指销售）无法完成交易的人|Someone who is unable to close a deal, especially in sales.  A person who fails to achieve a desired outcome or complete a task. |例句：He was labeled a noncloser after failing to secure several key contracts.  他因未能获得几份关键合同而被贴上“无法完成交易的人”的标签。

例句：Despite her initial enthusiasm, she proved to be a noncloser when it came to actually finishing projects. 尽管她最初充满热情，但在实际完成项目时，她被证明是一个无法完成的人。|近义词：underachiever, failure, also-ran|反义词: closer, achiever, winner|词性:noun
