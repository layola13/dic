overcorruption:/ˌoʊvər kəˈrʌpʃən/ | n. | 过度腐败；极度腐败 | Excessive corruption; corruption that is pervasive and deeply ingrained. | The overcorruption in the government led to widespread public unrest. 政府的过度腐败导致了广泛的公众骚乱。| The overcorruption within the organization made it impossible to conduct business ethically.  该组织内部的极度腐败使得无法进行合乎道德的业务。 | 近义词：extreme corruption, rampant corruption | 反义词: integrity, honesty | 词性: noun

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much") + "corruption" (from Latin "corruptus," past participle of "corrumpere," meaning "to break up, destroy, spoil, bribe").
