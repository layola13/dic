aphaniptera:/ˌeɪfənˈɪptərə/ | n. |蚤目; 蚤类 |An order of wingless insects, the fleas, that are parasitic on warm-blooded animals. |Fleas, belonging to the order Aphaniptera, are known for their jumping abilities. 跳蚤，属于蚤目，以其跳跃能力而闻名。| 近义词: Siphonaptera (另一个科学名称，同义) | 反义词:  无 | 词性: 名词
