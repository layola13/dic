insufferable:/ɪnˈsʌfərəbl/ | adj. | 难以忍受的，令人厌恶的 |  Not to be endured; intolerable; extremely annoying or unpleasant.  |  His arrogance was insufferable. 他的傲慢令人无法忍受。 |  unbearable, intolerable, obnoxious, dreadful |  tolerable, bearable, pleasant, agreeable | adjective

insufferable:/ɪnˈsʌfərəbl/ | adj. |  无法容忍的 | Unable to be tolerated or endured. | Her constant complaining was insufferable. 她不停的抱怨让人无法忍受。 | unbearable, intolerable |  tolerable, acceptable | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *insufferabilis, from in- "not" + sufferabilis "tolerable," from Latin sufferre "to endure."
