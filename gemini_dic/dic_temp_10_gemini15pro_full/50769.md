desaturates:/diːˈsætʃəreɪts/
|v.|（使）降低饱和度，（使）去饱和|Reduces the saturation of a color, making it less vivid and more muted.  It can also refer to removing a substance from something, like removing oxygen from blood. |The artist desaturated the image to give it a vintage feel. 艺术家降低了图像的饱和度，使其具有复古感。|synonyms: dull, mute, bleach, fade, deoxygenate|antonyms: saturate, brighten, enhance|verb
