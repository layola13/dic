miscarry:/mɪˈskæri/ /mɪsˈkæri/ | vi. | 流产；失败；未送达 | To fail to achieve the intended purpose; to end prematurely and unsuccessfully.  If a pregnant woman miscarries, her baby dies and she gives birth to it before it is fully developed. | The project miscarried due to lack of funding.  这个项目由于缺乏资金而失败了。

She miscarried at six months. 她在怀孕六个月时流产了。 | 近义词：fail, abort, collapse | 反义词：succeed, thrive | 词性: 动词

miscarriage:/ˈmɪskærɪdʒ/ /mɪsˈkærɪdʒ/ | n. | 流产；失败；失误 | The spontaneous or induced expulsion of a nonviable fetus; a failure. |  The miscarriage of justice led to his wrongful imprisonment.  司法失误导致他被冤枉入狱。

She had a miscarriage in her first trimester. 她在妊娠早期流产了。 | 近义词：failure, abortion, fiasco | 反义词：success, triumph | 词性: 名词
