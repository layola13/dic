coextension:/ˌkoʊɪkˈstɛnʃən/ | n. |共同外延，共外延|Having the same extension (referring to properties or predicates that apply to the same set of individuals or objects).| The properties of "being a triangle" and "being a three-sided polygon" are coextensional, as they apply to the same set of shapes.  三角形和三边多边形的性质是共外延的，因为它们适用于同一组形状。

The predicates "being an equilateral triangle" and "being an equiangular triangle" are coextensional. 等边三角形和等角三角形的谓词是共外延的。| 近义词: equivalence, identity, synonymy | 反义词: non-coextension, difference |词性: noun
