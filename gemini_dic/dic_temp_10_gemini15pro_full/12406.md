asiento:/ɑːˈsjɛntoʊ/ (US), /əˈsjɛntoʊ/ (UK)| n.|座位; 席位; (尤指飞机、火车或剧院的)预订座位; (尤指正式协议中的)职位, 席位; (旧时奴隶贸易中)被贩卖的人数, 配额|A place to sit, especially a seat in a vehicle, theater, etc., or a reservation for a seat; a position or place, especially in a formal agreement; (historical) the number of enslaved people allowed to be imported, especially under a license from the Spanish government.|

例句：
I booked an aisle asiento on the plane.
我在飞机上订了一个靠过道的座位。

例句：
The treaty guaranteed him an asiento on the council.
该条约保证了他在理事会中的席位。


近义词：seat, place, position, berth, accommodation
反义词: standing (room)
词性: noun
