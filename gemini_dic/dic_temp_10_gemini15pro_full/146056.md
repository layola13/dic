overdubs: /ˈoʊvərˌdʌbz/ (US) /ˈəʊvəˌdʌbz/ (UK)| n. |配音;叠录;附加录音| An additional recording mixed with an original recording. | The song features several overdubs, including backing vocals and a guitar solo. 这首歌有很多叠录，包括背景人声和吉他独奏。 |  The band used overdubs to enhance the sound of their instruments.  这支乐队用叠录来增强乐器的声音。 | synonyms: layering, dubbing, additional recording | antonyms: original recording, live recording | noun

overdub: /ˌoʊvərˈdʌb/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈdʌb/ (UK)| v. |配音;叠录;附加录音| To record (sound, music, or speech) over an existing recording. | They overdubbed the singer's voice to correct some pitch issues. 他们给歌手的声音叠录，以修正一些音高问题。 | He overdubbed a guitar solo onto the track. 他在音轨上叠录了一段吉他独奏。 | synonyms: layer, dub, rerecord | antonyms: record live | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over" + "dub" (meaning to copy or make a recording of something). "Dub" originates from the shortening of "double" or "duplicate."
