interferes:/ˌɪntərˈfɪərz/ | v. | 干扰，干涉，妨碍 | To come into collision or opposition; to meddle; to clash.  |  The noise interferes with my concentration. 噪音干扰了我的注意力。 | Synonyms: meddle, intervene, obstruct, hinder, impede | Antonyms: aid, assist, facilitate, help, support | verb
