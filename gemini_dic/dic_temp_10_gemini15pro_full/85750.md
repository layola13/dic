hawksby:/ˈhɔːksbi/ | n. |霍克斯比| A surname; a habitational name from Hawksby in the North Riding of Yorkshire.  It is derived from the Old Norse personal name *Haukr + the Old Norse word 'býr', meaning 'farm' or 'settlement'. Thus, Hawksby means "Haukr's farm/settlement". |  There are several families named Hawksby in Yorkshire. | 近义词: (As a surname, it doesn't have direct synonyms.
