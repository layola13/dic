nanotechnologies:/ˌnænəʊtekˈnɒlədʒiz/| n. |纳米技术|The branch of technology that deals with dimensions and tolerances of less than 100 nanometers, especially the manipulation of individual atoms and molecules.|Researchers are exploring the potential of nanotechnologies in medicine. 研究人员正在探索纳米技术在医学领域的潜力。|Synonyms: nanotechnology, nanoscience, nanotech|Antonyms: macrotechnology |noun
