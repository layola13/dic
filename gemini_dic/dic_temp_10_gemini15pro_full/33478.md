chylopoetic:/ˌkaɪləʊpɔɪˈɛtɪk/
adjective
乳糜生成的，与乳糜形成有关的
Relating to the formation of chyle. Chyle is a milky bodily fluid consisting of lymph and emulsified fats, formed in the small intestine during digestion.
例句：The chylopoetic system plays a crucial role in absorbing fats from the diet. 乳糜生成系统在从饮食中吸收脂肪方面起着至关重要的作用。

例句：Damage to the lymphatic system can disrupt the chylopoetic process. 淋巴系统的损伤会扰乱乳糜生成过程。
近义词：chyle-producing, lacteal
反义词: None
词性: adjective
