colonializes:/ˈkɒlənɪˌlaɪzɪz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlənɪˌlaɪzɪz/ (US)| v. |殖民化，将…变成殖民地| To establish a colony or colonies in (a territory).  To settle among and establish political control over (the indigenous people of a place).  To appropriate (a place or domain) for one's own use.| European powers colonialized much of Africa in the 19th century. 欧洲列强在19世纪殖民了非洲的大部分地区。

The company is trying to colonialize the online gaming market.  这家公司正试图在网络游戏市场建立殖民地。|近义词：settle, conquer, occupy, dominate, subjugate|反义词：liberate, free, decolonize|词性:动词
