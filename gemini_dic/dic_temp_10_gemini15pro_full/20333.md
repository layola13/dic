bisi:/ˈbiːsi/| n.|大米，食物 (Specifically a Yoruba word)|A staple food in West Africa, particularly among the Yoruba people, made from fermented maize.|Example: Bisi is a popular dish in Nigeria.  尼日利亚的特色菜肴。|Synonym:  food, meal, dish (general terms, not precise)|Antonym: None (lack of a direct opposite for a specific food)|noun
bisi:/ˈbiːsi/| adj.|腐烂的，发霉的 (Can also mean this in some dialects)|Rotten, moldy, spoiled (typically used for food).|Example: The bisi fruit had to be thrown away.  腐烂的水果不得不扔掉。|Synonym: rotten, spoiled, moldy, decayed|Antonym: fresh, unspoiled|adjective
