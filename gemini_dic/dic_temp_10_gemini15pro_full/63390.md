entosthoblast:/ɛn'tɒsθəˌblæst/
n.
内骨胚层细胞，成骨细胞
A cell from which bone develops; an osteoblast.
例句：Entosthoblasts play a crucial role in bone formation. 内骨胚层细胞在骨骼形成中起着至关重要的作用。

例句：The activity of entosthoblasts is essential for skeletal growth. 内骨胚层细胞的活动对骨骼生长至关重要。

近义词：osteoblast, bone-forming cell
反义词: osteoclast (bone-resorbing cell)
词性: noun
