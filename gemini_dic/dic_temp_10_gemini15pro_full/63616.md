enwreathe:/ɪnˈriːð/
verb
环绕；围绕；用…环绕
To encircle or adorn with a wreath or garland.
The bride's hair was enwreathed with flowers.  新娘的头发上环绕着鲜花。
The columns were enwreathed with ivy.  柱子上缠绕着常春藤。
近义词: garland, festoon, adorn, decorate, wreathe, crown, drape, surround, encompass
反义词: uncover, strip, denude
词性: verb
