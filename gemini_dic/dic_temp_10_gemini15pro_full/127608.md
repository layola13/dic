needies:/ˈniːdiz/
n.| 穷人，需要帮助的人 |People who are in need, often financially.  Those who require assistance or support. | The charity provides food and shelter for the needies.  这个慈善机构为穷人提供食物和住所。|近义词：the poor, the disadvantaged, the needy |反义词: the rich, the wealthy |词性: 名词

词源:  "needies" is a colloquial and often considered derogatory plural form of "needy," derived from the noun "need."  "Need" comes from Old English "nēad," meaning "necessity, compulsion, distress."
