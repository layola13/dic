interclash: /ˌɪntərˈklæʃ/
interclash: n. |冲突，碰撞，抵触|A violent clash or conflict between two or more things, especially groups of people.|The interclash between the two rival gangs resulted in several injuries.|近义词：conflict, clash, collision, encounter, friction|反义词：harmony, agreement, peace, concord|词性: 名词

interclash: v. |（使）冲突，碰撞，抵触|To clash or conflict violently, especially of groups of people.|The protesters interclashed with the police.|近义词：clash, conflict, collide, encounter|反义词：harmonize, agree, cooperate|词性: 动词
