anthropomorphical:/ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfɪkəl/| adj.|拟人的，赋予人性的|Attributing human characteristics or behavior to a god, animal, or object.|例句：The children's book had anthropomorphical animals who talked and wore clothes.  这本童书里有会说话、穿衣服的拟人化动物。

例句：It's tempting to anthropomorphize pets and give them human qualities.  人们很容易把宠物拟人化，赋予它们人类的品质。|近义词：humanlike, personified|反义词: zoomorphic|词性: adjective
