overlikings: /ˌoʊvərˈlaɪkɪŋz/
noun
过分的喜爱；过度的偏爱
Excessive fondness or partiality.
He showed overlikings for sweet food, much to the detriment of his health. 他对甜食表现出过分的喜爱，这对他的健康很不利。
近义词:  predilections, preferences, fondness, partiality
反义词:  aversion, dislike, antipathy
词性: n.


Etymology:
From 'over-' (meaning excessive) + 'liking' (noun form of 'like', meaning fondness).  The suffix '-ing' transforms the verb 'like' into a gerund/noun.  'Over-' is a common prefix used to indicate an excess or surplus.
