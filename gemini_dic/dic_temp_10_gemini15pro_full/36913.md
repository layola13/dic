colorizing:/ˈkʌləraɪzɪŋ/
verb
着色，彩色化
The process of adding color to a black and white image or movie.
Old black and white movies are often colorized for modern audiences.  老黑白电影经常被着色以适应现代观众。
tinting, painting
bleaching, fading
v.
