noneducations:/ˌnɒnˌedʒʊˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非教育，缺乏教育 |  A state of lacking formal education or the process of not receiving education.  It refers to the absence of schooling or structured learning.  It can also imply a lack of knowledge or understanding in a particular area. |  His noneducation made it difficult for him to find a well-paying job. 他的缺乏教育使他很难找到一份高薪工作。

The cycle of poverty is often perpetuated by noneducation. 贫困的循环往往因缺乏教育而持续。 | Near synonyms: ignorance, illiteracy, unlearnedness | Antonyms: education, learning, schooling | Noun
