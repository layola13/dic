ambustion:/æmˈbʌstʃən/ | n. | 燒傷; 灼傷 |  injury caused by burning or scalding, typically one involving the skin. | The child suffered ambustion from the hot stove.  孩子被热炉子烫伤了。|  burn, scald, sear | healing, recovery | noun
etymology: From Late Latin ambustio (“a burning”), from amburere (“to burn around”), from ambi- (“around”) + urere (“to burn”).
