parenchymatous:/pəˈrɛŋkɪmətəs/
adj.
薄壁组织的
Relating to or consisting of parenchyma (the functional tissue of an organ as distinguished from the connective and supporting tissue).
The stem of the plant is largely parenchymatous.  这种植物的茎大部分是薄壁组织。

The fruit has a soft, parenchymatous flesh.  这种水果的果肉柔软，是薄壁组织。

近义词:soft, spongy
反义词:sclerotic, fibrous
词性:adjective
