confucianism: /kənˈfjuːʃənɪzəm/ | n. | 儒家思想，儒学 | A system of philosophical and ethical teachings founded by Confucius and developed by Mencius. |  Confucianism has deeply influenced Chinese culture for centuries. 儒家思想深刻地影响了中国文化数个世纪。

Confucianism emphasizes filial piety and social harmony. 儒家强调孝道和社会和谐。| 近义词: Confucian doctrine, Confucian philosophy | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Confucius + -ism.  Confucius is the Latinized form of  Kǒng Fūzǐ (孔夫子), meaning "Master Kong".  The suffix "-ism" denotes a doctrine, belief, or system.
