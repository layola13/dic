nonhabitation:/ˌnɒnhæbɪˈteɪʃən/
n.
无人居住；无人居住的状态
The state of not being inhabited; absence of inhabitants.
The prolonged nonhabitation of the island led to the deterioration of the few buildings that remained.  这座岛长期无人居住，导致残存的几座建筑物破败不堪。
The city declared the building unsafe for nonhabitation. 该市宣布该建筑不适合居住。
unoccupancy, desertion, emptiness
habitation, occupancy, inhabitance
