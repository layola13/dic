incorporealism:/ˌɪnkɔːrˈpɔːriəlɪzəm/
n.
非物质性；非实体性；唯心论
The doctrine that there are no material substances, only minds and ideas.  The belief that spirits or souls have no physical form.
His philosophy leaned towards incorporealism, believing that true reality existed beyond the physical world. 他信奉唯心论，认为真正的实在存在于物质世界之外。
The ancient philosopher espoused a form of incorporealism, denying the existence of a material world. 这位古代哲学家信奉一种非物质性学说，否认物质世界的存在。

近义词: immaterialism, spiritualism, idealism
反义词: materialism, physicalism
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *incorporeus "bodiless," from in- "not" + corporeus "bodily, corporeal." + -ism "doctrine, belief."
