anapnograph:/ˈænəpnəɡrɑːf/
n.
呼吸描记器，呼吸运动记录器
An instrument for recording the movements of the chest in respiration.
The doctor used an anapnograph to monitor the patient's breathing. 医生用呼吸描记器监测病人的呼吸。
近义词: pneumograph, respirograph
反义词: None
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἀνά (aná, “up, upon”) + πνέω (pnéō, “I breathe”) + -graph.
