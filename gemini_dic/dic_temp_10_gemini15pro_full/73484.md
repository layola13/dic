forerib:/ˈfɔːrɪb/ (US), /ˈfɔːrɪb/ (UK)| n. |前肋排（牛肉）|The forerib is a cut of beef from the front part of the rib section, usually containing ribs six through ten.  It is a flavorful and tender cut, often used for roasting.|We had a delicious roast forerib for Sunday dinner. 我们星期天晚餐吃了美味的烤前肋排。|The butcher prepared a beautiful forerib for us. 肉贩为我们准备了一块漂亮的前肋排。|近义词：rib roast, prime rib|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially lean cuts like sirloin if contrasting based on fat content)|名词
