dunbar: /ˈdʌnbɑːr/ (US), /ˈdʌnbɑː(r)/ (UK)| n. | 邓巴 | Dunbar is a town in Scotland. It is also a surname. The name is best known for its association with the Dunbar number, a suggested cognitive limit to the number of people with whom one can maintain stable social relationships. | Dunbar is a beautiful coastal town.  邓巴是一个美丽的沿海小镇。|  Robin Dunbar proposed the Dunbar number. 罗宾·邓巴提出了邓巴数。| 近义词: town, name, surname | 反义词:  (No direct antonym)| 名词
