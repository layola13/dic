arenga:/əˈrɛŋɡə/ | n. | 糖棕;  糖棕树;  西谷椰子;  糖棕的汁液;  用糖棕的汁液制成的糖;  (尤指慷慨激昂的) 演说，演讲 | A type of palm tree (Arenga pinnata) native to tropical Asia, cultivated for its sap which is used to make sugar, as well as for its fibers and fruit. Also refers to the sap itself, or the sugar derived from it, and figuratively to a long, pompous, or bombastic speech. |  The arenga tree is a valuable source of sugar in some parts of Asia.  糖棕树在亚洲一些地区是重要的糖源。

The politician delivered a fiery arenga to the crowd.  这位政治家向群众发表了慷慨激昂的演说。 | 近义词：harangue, oration, address, speech, palm, sugar palm | 反义词： (For the speech meaning) silence, reticence | 词性: noun
