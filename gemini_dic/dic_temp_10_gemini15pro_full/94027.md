imparidigitate:/ɪmˌpærɪˈdɪdʒɪteɪt/
adjective
奇数羽状的；单数羽状的
Having an odd number of digits, usually one, on a limb, such as a horse's hoof.
A horse is an imparidigitate animal. 马是一种奇数羽状动物。
近义词: perissodactyl
反义词: artiodactyl, paridigitate
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *impari-digitatus*, from impar (“unequal, odd”) + digitatus (“having fingers or toes”).
