coalite:/ˈkoʊlaɪt/| n.|科莱特（一种无烟煤）|A smokeless fuel produced by the low-temperature carbonization of coal.|Coalite burns with less smoke than ordinary coal. 科莱特比普通煤燃烧产生的烟雾少。|Near synonyms: smokeless fuel, anthracite|Antonyms: bituminous coal|noun
