anacardiaceous:/ˌænəkɑːrdiˈeɪʃəs/ | adj. |漆树科的；漆树科植物的|Belonging to or resembling the Anacardiaceae family of plants, which includes cashew, mango, poison ivy, and sumac.  These plants often have resinous or milky sap.|Cashews and mangoes are anacardiaceous fruits. 腰果和芒果是漆树科的水果。|Synonyms: cashew family, sumac family|Antonyms: N/A|adjective


anacardiaceous:/əˌnækɑːrdiˈeɪʃəs/ | n. |漆树科植物|A plant belonging to the Anacardiaceae family. |The garden contains several anacardiaceous species, including a stately staghorn sumac.  这个花园里有几种漆树科植物，包括一棵雄伟的鹿角漆。|Synonyms: cashew family member, sumac family member|Antonyms: N/A|noun
