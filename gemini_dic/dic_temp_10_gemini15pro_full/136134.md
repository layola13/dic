nonreflectivenesses:/ˌnɒnˌriːflɛkˈtɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
无反射性；不反光性
The state or quality of being nonreflective.  This refers to surfaces that do not reflect light or other radiation.
The nonreflectivenesses of the matte black paint made it ideal for the stealth aircraft.  哑光黑色油漆的无反射性使其成为隐形飞机的理想选择。
The artist experimented with the nonreflectivenesses of various materials.  这位艺术家尝试了各种材料的无反射性。
近义词:non-reflectivity, opacity, dullness
反义词:reflectivity, glossiness, shininess
词性:noun
