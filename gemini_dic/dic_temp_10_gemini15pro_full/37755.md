compassionate:/kəmˈpæʃənɪt/| adj.|富有同情心的，表示怜悯的|Feeling or showing sympathy and concern for others.|She was a compassionate and caring nurse. 她是一位富有同情心和爱心的护士。|例句：The compassionate judge reduced his sentence.  富有同情心的法官减轻了他的刑罚。

例句：He felt compassionate towards the homeless man. 他对这个无家可归的人感到同情。|近义词：sympathetic, empathetic, kind, caring, merciful|反义词：cruel, heartless, indifferent, callous, apathetic|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *compassio, from Latin compassus, past participle of compati, "to suffer with, feel sympathy for."
