capriccio:/kəˈpriːtʃoʊ/ /kəˈprɪtʃɪəʊ/| n.|奇想，狂想曲，反复无常|A sudden, impulsive, and seemingly unmotivated change of mind or action; a musical composition, often free in form and brilliant in style.|She had a sudden capriccio to dye her hair pink. 她突然心血来潮想把头发染成粉红色。|
Synonyms: whim, fancy, impulse, notion
Antonyms: plan, calculation, deliberation
Part of Speech: noun

capriccio:/kəˈpriːtʃoʊ/ /kəˈprɪtʃɪəʊ/| n.|奇想，狂想曲，反复无常|The architect designed the building on a capriccio, ignoring all practical considerations.  这位建筑师一时兴起设计了这座建筑，忽略了所有实际的考虑。|
Synonyms: quirk, vagary, caprice
Antonyms: constancy, stability
Part of Speech: noun
