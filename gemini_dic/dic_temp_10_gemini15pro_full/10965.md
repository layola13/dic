arbitrarily: /ˌɑːrbɪˈtrerəli/ /ˌɑːrbɪˈtrɛːrɪli/ | adv. | 随意地，任意地 | Based on random choice or personal whim, rather than any reason or system. |  He chose the colors arbitrarily.  他随意地选择了颜色。|  The books were arranged arbitrarily on the shelves. 书架上的书是随意摆放的。 | synonyms: randomly, capriciously, at will, haphazardly | antonyms: systematically, methodically, rationally | adverb
