overintensifies: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈtɛnsɪˌfaɪz/
verb
过度强化；过分加剧
To intensify something to an excessive degree.  It implies going beyond what is necessary or desirable, resulting in a negative or exaggerated effect.
She overintensifies her emotions, making a small problem seem like a catastrophe. 她过度强化自己的情绪，使一个小问题看起来像一场灾难。
The director overintensifies the violence in the film, making it gratuitous and unpleasant. 导演过度强化了电影中的暴力，使其变得毫无必要且令人不快。
overemphasize, exaggerate, overdo
understate, downplay
