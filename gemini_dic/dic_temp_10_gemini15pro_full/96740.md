ineligible:/ɪnˈɛlɪdʒəbəl/
adjective
不合格的，没有资格的
Not qualified or entitled to something.
He was declared ineligible to compete because of his age. 他因年龄原因被宣布没有参赛资格。
ineligible for membership  没有会员资格
synonyms: disqualified, unsuitable, unfit, not entitled
antonyms: eligible, qualified, suitable, fit
词性: 形容词
