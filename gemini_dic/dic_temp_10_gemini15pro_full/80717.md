gormand:/ˈɡɔːrmənd/ /ˈɡɔːrmɑːnd/| n.|贪吃者；暴食者|A person who eats greedily or to excess; a glutton.|He was a known gormand, capable of finishing a whole roast chicken in one sitting. 他是个有名的贪吃鬼，能一口气吃完一整只烤鸡。|近义词：glutton, gourmand, epicure, foodie|反义词: ascetic|词性: noun
