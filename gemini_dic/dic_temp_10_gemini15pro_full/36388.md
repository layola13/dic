coinvent:/ˌkoʊɪnˈvɛnt/| v.|共同发明，联合发明|To invent something jointly with another person or group.|Bell and Gray are said to have coinvented the telephone. 贝尔和格雷据说共同发明了电话。|近义词：collaborate on an invention, jointly invent|反义词：copy, steal, plagiarize|词性:动词

Etymology: co- (together) + invent (from Latin invenire, "to come upon, find")
