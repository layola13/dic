coily: /ˈkɔɪli/ | adj. | 卷曲的，盘绕的 | Having a lot of coils or curls; spirally twisted. | Her hair is naturally coily. 她的头发天生卷曲。| The snake lay coily in the grass. 蛇盘绕在草丛里。 |  近义词: curly, kinky, spiralled, coiled | 反义词: straight, uncoiled | 词性: 形容词

词源: From coil + -y.  From the noun "coil," meaning a series of loops or rings, with the suffix "-y" added to form an adjective indicating a characteristic of having coils.
