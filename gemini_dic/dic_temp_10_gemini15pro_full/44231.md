crocodile: /ˈkrɒkədaɪl/ (UK) /ˈkrɑːkədaɪl/ (US) | n. | 鳄鱼 | A large, carnivorous reptile with a long snout, powerful jaws, and a thick, scaly hide. It lives in tropical rivers and swamps. |  The crocodile lurked in the murky water. 鳄鱼潜伏在浑浊的水中。

The Nile crocodile is one of the largest species. 尼罗河鳄鱼是最大的物种之一。| 近义词: alligator, gavial | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an animal) | 词性: noun
