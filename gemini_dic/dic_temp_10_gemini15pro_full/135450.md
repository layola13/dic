nonpertinency:/ˌnɒnpɜːtɪnənsi/ | n. | 无关紧要；不相关；不相干 | Lack of relevance or connection to the matter at hand.  |  The judge struck the witness's testimony from the record, deeming it a nonpertinency. 法官认为证人的证词无关紧要，将其从记录中删除。 | Synonyms: irrelevance, immateriality, extraneousness | Antonyms: relevance, pertinence, germaneness | noun

nonpertinencies:/ˌnɒnpɜːtɪnənsiz/ | n. | 无关紧要的事物；不相关的事物；不相干的事物 (nonpertinency的复数形式) |  Plural form of nonpertinency, referring to multiple instances of irrelevance. | The report was filled with nonpertinencies and failed to address the core issue. 报告充满了无关紧要的内容，未能解决核心问题。 | Synonyms: irrelevancies, immaterialities | Antonyms: relevancies, pertinences | noun
