一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他靠什么谋生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you listening to? = 你在听什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? = 你最近学到了什么？
What has he accomplished this year? = 他今年完成了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been reading this week? = 她这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had happened before I arrived? = 在我到达之前发生了什么事？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the interruption? = 打断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for hours? = 他们几个小时一直在讨论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will happen next year? = 明年会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? = 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by next month? = 到下个月你将完成什么？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做什么五年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？
