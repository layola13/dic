angusticlave:/æŋˈɡʌstɪkleɪv/| n. |狭窄的房间，密闭空间|A small, narrow, or confined space, often implying a sense of claustrophobia or oppression.  Derived from "angustus" (Latin for "narrow") and "clave" (Latin for "key," suggesting a locked or enclosed space).|The laboratory felt like an angusticlave, with its cramped quarters and lack of windows.|Synonyms: cubicle, closet, cell, chamber|Antonyms: expanse, open area|noun
