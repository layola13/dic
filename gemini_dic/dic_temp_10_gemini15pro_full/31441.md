charity: /ˈtʃærəti/ (US) /ˈtʃærɪti/ (UK)| n. |慈善; 慈善机构; 施舍; 仁慈|The act of giving money, food, or other kinds of help to people who are poor, sick, etc.; organizations that do this|例句：She donates a significant portion of her income to charity. 她将收入的很大一部分捐给了慈善机构。

例句：The charity provides food and shelter for the homeless.  这个慈善机构为无家可归的人提供食物和住所。

近义词：benevolence, philanthropy, altruism|反义词：selfishness, greed, cruelty|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "charité"  (love, affection, charity, alms), from Latin "caritas" (dearness, high price, affection, love, esteem), from "carus" (dear, costly, precious, loved, beloved).
