counter-agency:/ˈkaʊntərˈeɪdʒənsi/ | n. | 对抗机构；反机构 | An organization or group that acts in opposition to another, often a government or established institution. |  The counter-agency worked tirelessly to expose the government's corruption. 对抗机构不知疲倦地揭露政府的腐败行为。 | Counter-agency operatives infiltrated the rival organization. 反机构特工渗透进了敌对组织。 | synonyms: opposition, resistance, counter-force | antonyms: cooperation, collaboration, support | noun
