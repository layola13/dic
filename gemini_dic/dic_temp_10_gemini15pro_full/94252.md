imperiwigged:/ɪmˌpɛrɪˈwɪɡd/
adj.
戴假发的，戴着假发的
Wearing a periwig (an old-fashioned wig).  The term evokes a sense of formality and old-fashioned style.
The imperwigged judge entered the courtroom.  戴着假发的法官走进了法庭。
The painting depicted an imperwigged gentleman of the 18th century. 这幅画描绘了一位18世纪戴着假发的绅士。
Near Synonyms: wigged, bewigged
Antonyms: unwagged, hairless (in the context of head hair)
Word Family: periwig (n.), wig (n.)
