monographic:/ˌmɒnəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 专著的；专题的 | Of or relating to a monograph; pertaining to a single subject or area of study. | This is a monographic study of the poet's early work.  这是对这位诗人早期作品的专题研究。 |  She wrote a monographic dissertation on Shakespeare's sonnets. 她写了一篇关于莎士比亚十四行诗的专题论文。 |  specialized, single-subject, in-depth, exhaustive, comprehensive | general, broad, overview, cursory, superficial | adjective
