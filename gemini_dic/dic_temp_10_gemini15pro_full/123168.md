monkish:/ˈmʌŋkɪʃ/| adj.|僧侣般的，像僧侣的；禁欲的，苦行的|Resembling or characteristic of a monk, especially in being austere, solitary, or devoted to religious contemplation.|He led a monkish existence, dedicating his life to prayer and study. 他过着僧侣般的生活，将生命奉献给祈祷和学习。|例句：His room was sparsely furnished, almost monkish in its simplicity. 他的房间陈设简陋，几乎像僧侣的房间一样简单。

例句：She admired his monkish devotion to his craft. 她钦佩他对技艺的僧侣般的奉献精神。|近义词：ascetic, austere, monastic|反义词：worldly, hedonistic|词性:形容词

词源:
From monk + -ish.  Ultimately from Greek monakhos "monk, solitary person," from monos "alone, single, solitary."
