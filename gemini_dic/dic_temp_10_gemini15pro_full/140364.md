oil-painter:/ˈɔɪl ˌpeɪntər/ | n. | 油画画家 | A person who paints pictures using oil paints. |  Example 1: The oil-painter carefully blended the colors on his palette. 油画家小心翼翼地在他调色板上混合颜色。

Example 2:  My aunt is a talented oil-painter and her work is displayed in several galleries.  我的姑妈是一位才华横溢的油画家，她的作品在几个画廊展出。 | Synonym: artist, painter | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who doesn't paint or creates art in a different medium) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From oil + painter.  "Oil" refers to the medium of oil paint, and "painter" denotes someone who paints. "Painter" comes from the Old French word "peintre" and ultimately from the Latin "pingere" meaning "to paint."
