allowable:/əˈlaʊəbl/
adj.
可允许的；容许的
Permitted; acceptable.
例句：The allowable baggage weight is 20kg.  允许的行李重量是20公斤。

例句：Is this expense allowable under company policy?  根据公司政策，这笔费用是允许的吗？

近义词：permissible, acceptable, legitimate, tolerated
反义词：unacceptable, forbidden, prohibited, disallowed
词性: adjective


词源：From "allow" + "-able".  "Allow" comes from Old French "alouer" meaning "to praise, commend, approve," later "to grant, permit." "-able" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being," "worthy of being," or "tending to."
