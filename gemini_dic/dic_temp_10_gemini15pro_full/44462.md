cross-wind:/ˈkrɒsˌwɪnd/ (UK) /ˈkrɑːsˌwɪnd/ (US)| n. adj.|侧风，横风|A wind blowing across or at right angles to the course of something, especially an aircraft or ship.|The plane encountered a strong cross-wind during landing.  飞机着陆时遇到了强劲的侧风。|
cross-wind:/ˈkrɒsˌwɪnd/ (UK) /ˈkrɑːsˌwɪnd/ (US)| adj.|侧风的，横风的|Exposed to or affected by a cross-wind.|The cross-wind landing was challenging for the pilot.  侧风着陆对飞行员来说很有挑战性。|
cross-wind: |词源: cross (adv., prep., n., adj., v.) + wind (n.)|  "cross" 表示“穿过，交叉”，"wind"表示“风”。|
cross-wind:| 近义词: side wind | 反义词: headwind, tailwind |

cross-wind:| adj.| 侧风的|
