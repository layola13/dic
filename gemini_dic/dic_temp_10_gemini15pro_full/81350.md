grapholite:/ˈɡræfəˌlaɪt/| n. |石墨板，石墨片，书写板|A compact form of pyrolytic graphite, used especially for making seals and bearings.|Grapholite seals are used in high-temperature applications. 石墨板密封件用于高温应用。|Synonyms: graphite sheet, pyrolytic graphite|Antonyms: None|Noun
