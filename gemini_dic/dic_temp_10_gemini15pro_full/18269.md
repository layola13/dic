belittlements: /bɪˈlɪt(ə)lmənts/
n.
轻视，贬低；轻蔑的言行
the act of making someone or something seem unimportant
His constant belittlements made her feel insecure. 他不断的贬低让她感到没有安全感。

近义词: disparagements, denigrations, deprecations, put-downs
反义词: praise, compliments, flattery, exaltation
词性: noun (plural)
