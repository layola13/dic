cotenants:/ˌkoʊˈtɛnənts/ | n. |共同租户，合租人 |One who rents property jointly with another.  People who share ownership or rental of a property. | The cotenants shared the responsibility of paying the rent. 共同租户共同承担支付租金的责任。

The cotenants had a disagreement about decorating the living room.  这些合租人对客厅的装修意见不一。 |近义词：joint tenants, co-lessees |反义词: landlord, lessor | noun
