add:/æd/ | v. | 加，增加，补充说 | To combine (numbers or amounts) to get a total; to include something extra. | I added some salt to the soup. 我在汤里加了一些盐。

Please add my name to the list. 请把我的名字添加到名单上。 | 近义词: increase, augment, append, supplement, sum | 反义词: subtract, deduct, remove | 词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *addere*, from *ad-* (“to”) + *dare* (“to give”).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I add sugar to my coffee every morning. = 我每天早上都在咖啡里加糖。
She adds a personal touch to all her projects. = 她为她所有的项目都增添了个人色彩。

一般过去时:
He added some extra spices to the dish. = 他在菜里加了一些额外的香料。
They added a new wing to the building. = 他们在建筑物上加盖了一个新侧翼。

现在完成时:
We have added more security measures. = 我们已经增加了更多的安全措施。
The company has added several new products to its line. = 公司在其产品线中增加了几款新产品。

过去完成时:
After she had added the finishing touches, the painting was complete. = 她添加了最后的润色之后，这幅画就完成了。
They had already added the tax to the bill. = 他们已经在账单上加上了税款。


将来时:
I will add your name to the guest list. = 我会把你的名字添加到客人名单上。
We will add more details later. = 我们稍后会添加更多细节。

虚拟语气:
If I were to add anything, it would be more cheese. = 如果我要添加任何东西，那就是更多的奶酪。
I wish I could add more hours to the day. = 我希望我能给一天增加更多的时间。

被动语态:
More information will be added to the website soon. = 网站很快就会添加更多信息。
A new feature was added to the software. = 软件中添加了一项新功能。

疑问句:
Should I add more milk? = 我应该再加些牛奶吗？
What do you want me to add to the shopping list? = 你想让我在购物清单上添加什么？

否定句:
Don't add any more salt. = 不要再加盐了。
I didn't add anything to the mixture. = 我没有在混合物中添加任何东西。


PHRASE->
add up = 加起来
add up to = 总计达
add to = 增加，增添
add in = 包括在内
add on = 附加，加上
