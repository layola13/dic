cartographical:/ˌkɑːrtəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
制图的；地图制作的
Relating to the production of maps.
The book contains detailed cartographical information about the region. 这本书包含该地区的详细地图信息。
Cartographical skills are essential for geographers.  制图技能对地理学家至关重要。
近义词:map-making, mapping
反义词: (No direct antonym, but one could consider "non-cartographical")
