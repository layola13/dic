blatantly:/ˈbleɪtəntli/
adverb
公然地；明目张胆地
in a blatant way
He blatantly lied to the police. 他公然对警察撒谎。

近义词:openly, obviously, flagrantly, conspicuously
反义词:subtly, secretly, discreetly
词性:adverb

词源:
From late Latin *blatire "to babble, chatter," of imitative origin.  The original sense was "noisy, clamorous," later "offensively conspicuous."
