lumpen:/ˈlʌmpən/| n. adj.|流氓无产者；社会渣滓；笨拙的；粗俗的|A person, especially a criminal, who is regarded as belonging to the lowest social class;  clumsy; boorish. |The novel portrays a group of lumpens living on the fringes of society. 这部小说描绘了一群生活在社会边缘的流氓无产者。|近义词：riffraff, vagrant, outcast, clumsy, awkward|反义词：aristocrat, elite, refined, graceful|词性:名词，形容词
词源: From German *Lumpen* (“rags”), plural of *Lump* (“rag, scrap”).
