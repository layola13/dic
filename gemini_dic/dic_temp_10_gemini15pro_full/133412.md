nonfaltering:/nɒnˈfɔːltərɪŋ/ | adj. | 坚定的，毫不动摇的 |  Not faltering; firm and steady.  Showing or characterized by unwavering determination or resolution; steadfast. |  She displayed nonfaltering courage in the face of danger.  她在危险面前展现出毫不动摇的勇气。| 近义词: resolute, unwavering, steadfast, firm, determined | 反义词: hesitant, wavering, faltering, uncertain, irresolute | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "falter" (verb meaning "to hesitate or stumble").  The word "falter" comes from the Old Norse "faltrask," meaning "to stumble."
