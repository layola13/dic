misoccupied:/ˌmɪsˈɒkjupaɪd/
adjective
心不在焉的，专注于错误事物的
preoccupied with something inappropriate or unimportant; distracted
She seemed misoccupied, staring out the window during the entire meeting. 她似乎心不在焉，整个会议期间都盯着窗外。
He was misoccupied with video games and neglected his studies. 他沉迷于电子游戏而荒废了学业。
Synonyms: distracted, preoccupied, inattentive, absent-minded
Antonyms: attentive, focused, concentrated
adjective
