exposes:/ɪkˈspoʊzɪz/
| v.|暴露，揭露，使曝光；使面临，使遭受|The report exposes the corruption within the company. 这份报告揭露了公司内部的腐败。|近义词：reveals, uncovers, discloses|反义词：conceals, hides, protects|词性:动词 (verb)

exposes:/ɪkˈspoʊzɪz/
| n.|暴露，揭露|The newspaper published several exposes on political scandals. 这家报纸发表了几篇关于政治丑闻的揭露文章。|近义词：revelations, disclosures|反义词：cover-ups, concealments|词性:名词 (noun)
