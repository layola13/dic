The architect's antimodern design incorporated traditional elements.  这位建筑师的反现代设计融合了传统元素。

| 近义词: traditional, conservative, reactionary, nostalgic | 反义词: modern, contemporary, progressive, avant-garde | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + modern.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to."  "Modern" comes from Old French "moderne," ultimately from Late Latin "modernus," meaning "of the present mode or fashion."
