blacklist:/ˈblækˌlɪst/ /ˈblækˌlɪst/| n. vt.|黑名单，禁止往来户；将…列入黑名单|A list of people or things that are disapproved of or are to be avoided or punished.|例句：The company blacklisted him after he was caught stealing.  这家公司在他被抓到偷窃后把他列入了黑名单。

例句：His name is on the blacklist. 他的名字在黑名单上。|近义词：blocklist, prohibit list, deny list|反义词：whitelist|词性：名词，动词

blacklist:/ˈblækˌlɪst/ /ˈblækˌlɪst/| vt.|将…列入黑名单|To put someone or something on a blacklist.|例句：Several websites have been blacklisted by the government.  政府将几个网站列入了黑名单。

例句：They blacklisted him for his political activities. 他们因为他参与政治活动而把他列入了黑名单。|近义词：ban, bar, exclude|反义词：whitelist|词性：动词
