eponymic:/ˌɛpəˈnɪmɪk/ | adj. |同名的，以...命名的| Of, relating to, or being an eponym; derived from a person's name. | The disease is eponymic, named after the doctor who first described it. 这种疾病是以第一个描述它的医生命名的。| synonymous: named after, namesake | antonymous: anonymous | adjective

eponym:/ˈɛpənɪm/ | n. | 名祖，同名者| A person after whom a discovery, invention, place, etc., is named or thought to be named. |  The Earl of Sandwich is the eponym of the sandwich.  三明治伯爵是三明治的命名来源。 | synonymous: namesake | antonymous: anonymous | noun
