honeyest: /ˈhʌnɪɪst/ | adj. | 最诚实的，最甜蜜的 (non-standard, often humorous or dialectal) |  A superlative form of "honey" used informally to mean "sweetest" or, by extension, "dearest, kindest, most wonderful," often implying a degree of playful exaggeration. It can also be interpreted as a humorous or dialectal form of "honest." | Example: She's the honeyest girl I've ever met. 她是我见过的最可爱的女孩。| Example: He gave me the honeyest smile. 他给了我一个最甜蜜的微笑。| Near synonyms: sweetest, dearest, kindest, most wonderful, honest (in some contexts) | Antonyms: meanest, cruelest, worst | word type: adjective
