embassador:/æmˈbæsədər/| n.|大使；使节；代表|A diplomat of the highest rank sent by one country as its long-term representative to another.  A person who acts as a representative or promoter of a specified activity.| The French ambassador to the United States. 法国驻美国大使。|近义词：envoy, diplomat, legate, representative, agent|反义词:rebel, dissident|noun
