nonredemptible:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈdiːməbəl/ | adj. | 不可赎回的，不可兑现的 | Not able to be redeemed, especially of stocks, bonds, or other financial instruments.  This also extends to intangible things like promises or reputations. |  The company issued nonredemptible bonds.  这家公司发行了不可赎回的债券。 | The damage to his reputation was nonredemptible. 对他名誉的损害是不可挽回的。 | irredeemable, nonredeemable, unredeemable | redeemable | adjective
