coked:/koʊkt/| adj.| 兴奋的；充满活力的；（因吸食可卡因）亢奋的；|Under the influence of cocaine; high.  受可卡因的影响；很兴奋。|He was so coked he could barely stand. 他嗑药嗑high了，几乎站不稳。|近义词：high, wired, strung out, intoxicated, stimulated|反义词：sober, straight, down|词性:形容词

coked:/koʊkt/| adj.| 焦化的；焦结的；|Covered with coke (the fuel). 被焦炭覆盖。|The walls of the furnace were coked. 炉壁结焦了。|近义词：charred, burned|反义词：clean, clear|词性:形容词
