derive:/dɪˈraɪv/ /dəˈraɪv/| v. |获得，取得；源于，衍生|To obtain something from a specified source. To come from something.|例句：He derived great pleasure from painting. 他从绘画中获得了巨大的乐趣。|例句：The word "derive" is derived from Latin. “derive”这个词源于拉丁语。|近义词：obtain, acquire, glean, originate, stem|反义词:forfeit, lose|词性:动词

derive:/dɪˈraɪv/ /dəˈraɪv/| adj. |衍生的，派生的 (较少使用)|Formed or developed from something else.|例句：a derived unit  一个派生单位|例句：This is a culturally derived custom.  这是一个文化衍生的习俗。|近义词：derivative|反义词:original, primary|词性:形容词
