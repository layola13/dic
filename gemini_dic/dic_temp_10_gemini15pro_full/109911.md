libation:/laɪˈbeɪʃən/
n.
奠酒，祭酒；（尤指酒的）饮料
An act of pouring a liquid as a sacrifice (as to a deity); (also figurative) a drink, especially an alcoholic one.
例句：The priest made a libation to the gods. 祭司向众神献上了奠酒。

例句：We made a libation of wine before the meal. 我们在饭前斟了一杯酒。

近义词：drink, beverage, offering, toast
反义词：(No direct antonym)
词性：noun
