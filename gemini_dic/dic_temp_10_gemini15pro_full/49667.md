demises:/dɪˈmaɪziz/ /dɪˈmiːziz/| n.|死亡，终止，转让|A formal or legal term referring to death, especially of a person who holds a lease; or the transfer of property or a lease by death.|His demise was unexpected. 他的去世出乎意料。|Synonyms: death, decease, passing, expiration, termination|Antonyms: birth, beginning, commencement|noun


demises:/dɪˈmaɪziz/ /dɪˈmiːziz/| n.|财产(尤指租賃權)的轉讓|The conveyance or transfer of property or a lease by will or by operation of law.|The demise of the lease to the new tenant was finalized last week. 上周租賃權轉讓給新租戶的手續完成了。|Synonyms: transfer, conveyance, assignment|Antonyms: acquisition, retention|noun
