neossology:/ˌniːɒˈsɒlədʒi/ | n. |幼鸟学|The study of young birds, specifically nestlings.| Ornithologists specializing in neossology study the development and behavior of birds in the nest.  鸟类学家专门研究幼鸟学，研究鸟类在巢中的发育和行为。| 近义词: nidiology | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps adult ornithology) | 词性: noun
