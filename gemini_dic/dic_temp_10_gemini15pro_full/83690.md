haggis:/ˈhæɡɪs/| n.|羊杂碎；羊肚包饭|A Scottish dish consisting of a sheep's or calf's offal mixed with suet, oatmeal, and seasoning and boiled in a bag, traditionally one made from the animal's stomach.|例句：Haggis is a traditional Scottish dish. 羊杂碎是苏格兰传统菜肴。

例句：She tried haggis for the first time on her trip to Scotland. 她在苏格兰旅行时第一次尝试了羊杂碎。|近义词：offal, pluck|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle English *hagws*, *hagwys*, from Old North French *haguez*, *aguetz* (plural), "entrails, pluck," from a Germanic source; akin to Old English *hogg* "hog, pig," and Middle Low German *haggen* "to chop, hack."
