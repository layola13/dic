pacificator:/pəˈsɪfɪkeɪtər/
noun
调解人；平息者；安抚者
a person who pacifies or appeases someone or something.
例句：The skilled pacificator brought the two warring factions together.  这位熟练的调解人把两个交战派别召集到一起。

例句：She acted as a pacificator between her arguing parents.  她充当了争吵的父母之间的调解人。

近义词：peacemaker, mediator, conciliator, arbitrator
反义词：agitator, instigator, warmonger
