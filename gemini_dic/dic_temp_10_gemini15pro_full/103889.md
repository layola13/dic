ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *judaicus, from Latin Judaeus (“Jew”), from Ancient Greek Ἰουδαῖος (Ioudaîos), from Hebrew יְהוּדִי‎ (y'hūḏī). Ultimately from Hebrew יְהוּדָה‎ (Y'hūḏā, “Judah”).
