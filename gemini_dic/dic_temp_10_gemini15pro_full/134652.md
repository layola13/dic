nonmanagerial:/ˌnɒnˌmænəˈdʒɪəriəl/
adjective
非管理的
Not involved in management; not having the rank of manager.
The company offers excellent benefits to both managerial and nonmanagerial staff. 公司为管理人员和非管理人员提供优厚的福利。
non-executive, nonsupervisory, rank-and-file
managerial, executive
