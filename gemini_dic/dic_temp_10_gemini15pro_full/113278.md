luteic:/luːˈtiːɪk/ | adj. | 黄体化的，黄体期的 | Relating to the corpus luteum, a temporary endocrine structure in female ovaries that is involved in the production of relatively high levels of progesterone and moderate levels of estradiol and inhibin A. |  The luteic phase of the menstrual cycle follows ovulation. 月经周期的黄体期在排卵之后。| Synonyms:  progestational | Antonyms: follicular | adjective
