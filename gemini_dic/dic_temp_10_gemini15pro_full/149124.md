oxidant:/ˈɒksɪdənt/ (UK) /ˈɑːksɪdənt/ (US)| n.|氧化剂|A substance that oxidizes another substance; a substance that accepts electrons in an oxidation-reduction reaction.|Antioxidants protect the body from the damaging effects of oxidants. 抗氧化剂保护身体免受氧化剂的损害。|Synonyms: oxidizing agent, oxidizer|Antonyms: reductant, reducing agent|noun

oxidant:/ˈɒksɪdənt/ (UK) /ˈɑːksɪdənt/ (US)| adj.|氧化的|Relating to or causing oxidation.|The oxidant properties of chlorine make it a powerful disinfectant. 氯的氧化特性使其成为一种强力消毒剂。|Synonyms: oxidizing|Antonyms: reducing|adjective
