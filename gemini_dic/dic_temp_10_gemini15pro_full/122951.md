mummery:/ˈmʌməri/| n.|滑稽古怪的举动；虚伪的仪式；装腔作势|A performance or ceremony that is ridiculous or insincere.|The elaborate mummery of a state funeral. 国葬的繁文缛节。|Synonyms: charade, pretense, farce, sham|Antonyms: sincerity, genuineness|noun
