brought:/brɔːt/
verb
带来，拿来，引起，导致
Past tense of bring.  Carried or conveyed something to a place or person. Caused something to happen.
She brought a cake to the party. 她带了个蛋糕来参加聚会。
The rain brought much-needed relief to the drought-stricken area. 这场雨给遭受旱灾的地区带来了急需的缓解。
近义词: carried, fetched, delivered, caused, produced
反义词: took, removed
