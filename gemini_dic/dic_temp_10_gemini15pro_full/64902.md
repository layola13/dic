essentially:/ɪˈsɛnʃəli/ | adv. |本质上；根本上；基本上| At its heart, or in its basic nature.  |The book is essentially a collection of recipes. 这本书本质上是一本食谱集。| essentially means in its most basic form. This is a simple explanation of a complex process. 这本书基本上是对一个复杂过程的简单解释。| 近义词:basically, fundamentally, primarily, chiefly, mainly | 反义词:superficially, incidentally | 词性:副词
