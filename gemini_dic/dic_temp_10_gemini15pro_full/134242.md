noninfectious:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfɛkʃəs/
adjective
非传染性的
Not capable of being transmitted through infection.
This type of pneumonia is noninfectious. 这种肺炎是非传染性的。
Synonyms: noncommunicable, noncontagious
Antonyms: infectious, contagious
词性: 形容词
