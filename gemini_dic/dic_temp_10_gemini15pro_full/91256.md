hydrofluorocarbon: /ˌhaɪdrəʊˈflʊərəʊˌkɑːrbən/ (UK) /ˌhaɪdroʊˈflʊrəʊˌkɑːrbən/ (US)
n.|氢氟烃|A compound containing only hydrogen, fluorine, and carbon atoms. These gases are potent greenhouse gases and are used as refrigerants and in other industrial applications.|Hydrofluorocarbons are used in air conditioning systems. 氢氟烃用于空调系统。|Synonyms: HFC, refrigerant|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one might consider natural refrigerants like propane or ammonia)|noun
