clasping:/ˈklɑːspɪŋ/ /ˈklæspɪŋ/
adjective
紧握的，扣住的
holding tightly; gripping firmly.
She held the child's hand in a clasping grip. 她紧紧地握着孩子的手。

近义词: gripping, grasping, holding, clutching
反义词: releasing, unclasping, letting go
词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *claspen, from Old English *clæpsen, frequentative of *clappan (“to clap, beat”), from Proto-Germanic *klappaną (“to clap, beat”), from Proto-Indo-European *glep- (“to stick, adhere”).
