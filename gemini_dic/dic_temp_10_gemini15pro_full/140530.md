-olent: /ˈoʊlənt/ | adj. | 充满...的，有...气味的 | characterized by a specified smell, often a strong or unpleasant one | The air was redolent of pine needles. 空气中充满了松针的气味。|  The room was redolent with the smell of stale cigarette smoke. 房间里充满了难闻的香烟味。 |近义词: fragrant, aromatic, perfumed |反义词: odorless, scentless | 词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *olent- (stem of olēns), present participle of olēre “to smell.”
