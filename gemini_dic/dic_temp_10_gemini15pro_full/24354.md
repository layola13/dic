brindled:/ˈbrɪnd(ə)ld/| adj.|有斑纹的，有斑点的，灰褐色的|Having dark streaks or flecks on a gray or tawny background.|例句：The brindled cat sat in the sun.  这只灰褐色带斑纹的猫坐在阳光下。

例句：We saw a brindled cow in the field. 我们在田野里看到了一头带有斑纹的母牛。|近义词：streaked, mottled, tabby, spotted|反义词: solid, uniform|词性:形容词

词源: From brindled, past participle of brindle, from brinded, variant of brinned, past participle of brin "to burn," from Old Norse bruna "to burn." The original sense was probably "burned brown."
