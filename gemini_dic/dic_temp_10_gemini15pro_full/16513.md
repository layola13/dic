baritonal:/ˌbærɪˈtoʊnl/ | adj. |男中音的; 像男中音的| Having the quality of a baritone voice; resembling the range of a baritone. | His baritonal voice filled the hall. 他的男中音歌声充满了整个大厅。| The actor delivered his lines in a deep, baritonal voice.  这位演员用低沉的男中音嗓音念台词。 |近义词: deep, rich, resonant |反义词: high-pitched, soprano, tenor |词性: adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *baritonale*, from *baritono* (baritone) + *-ale* (suffix forming adjectives). Ultimately from Greek *βαρύτονος* (barútonos, “deep-sounding”), from *βαρύς* (barús, “heavy”) + *τόνος* (tónos, “tone”).
