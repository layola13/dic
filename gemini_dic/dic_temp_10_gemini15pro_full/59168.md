dysenteries:/ˈdɪsənˌtɛriz/
n.
痢疾（复数）
Infection of the intestines resulting in severe diarrhea with the presence of blood and mucus in the feces.
Outbreaks of dysenteries are common in areas with poor sanitation.  痢疾的爆发在卫生条件差的地区很常见。
The refugee camp was struggling with an epidemic of dysenteries.  难民营正与痢疾流行作斗争。

近义词:diarrhea, flux
反义词: (No direct antonym for a disease)

词性: noun (plural)
