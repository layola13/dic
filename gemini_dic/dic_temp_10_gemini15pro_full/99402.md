interdisciplinarity:/ˌɪntərˌdɪsəplɪˈnærəti/| n. |跨学科性，跨学科研究|The increasing complexity of the modern world demands interdisciplinarity in research.  现代世界的日益复杂性要求研究的跨学科性。|例句：The program encourages interdisciplinarity by bringing together scholars from different fields.  该项目通过汇集不同领域的学者来鼓励跨学科研究。

例句：Interdisciplinarity is crucial for tackling complex global challenges like climate change. 跨学科性对于应对气候变化等复杂的全球挑战至关重要。|近义词：cross-disciplinarity, multidisciplinarity|反义词: disciplinarity, specialization|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (between) + disciplinary + -ity (noun suffix).  "Disciplinary" comes from discipline, from Latin disciplina "instruction, knowledge," from discipulus "pupil, student."
