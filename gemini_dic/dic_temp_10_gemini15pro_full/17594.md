beautifies:/ˈbjuːtɪˌfaɪz/
verb
美化；使变美
to make something beautiful or more beautiful.
The flowers beautify the garden. 这些花美化了花园。
近义词: embellishes, adorns, enhances, decorates
反义词: uglifies, mars, spoils, defaces
词性: verb

词源:
From Middle English *beautifien, from Old French beautifier, from beau (“beautiful”) + -fier (from Latin -ficāre, from facere (“to make, to do”)).
