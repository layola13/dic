munchers:/ˈmʌntʃərz/
n.
大吃的人；吃东西很响的人
People who eat enthusiastically and often noisily.
The munchers were happily devouring the snacks.  那些大吃的人正津津有味地吃着零食。
近义词：eaters, diners, gourmands
反义词： (没有直接的反义词，可用anorexics厌食症患者表示对比)
词性：名词(复数)

munch:/mʌntʃ/
v.
大声咀嚼
To eat something steadily and often noisily.
He munched on his apple. 他大声地咀嚼着他的苹果。
近义词：chew, champ, gnaw
反义词：nibble
词性：动词

词源:  From the verb "munch," which is imitative of the sound of chewing.  来源于动词"munch"，是模仿咀嚼的声音。
