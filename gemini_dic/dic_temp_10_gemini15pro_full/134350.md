noninteracting:/ˌnɒnɪntərˈæktɪŋ/ | adj. | 非相互作用的，不相互影响的 | Not interacting; not influencing or affecting each other. | The particles in an ideal gas are assumed to be noninteracting. 理想气体中的粒子被假定为非相互作用的。|  These two systems are noninteracting and can be considered separately. 这两个系统互不影响，可以单独考虑。 | 近义词: independent, separate, isolated | 反义词: interacting, interdependent, coupled | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "interacting" (present participle of "interact," from "inter-" (prefix meaning "between") + "act" (verb meaning "to do something")).
