grout: /ɡraʊt/ | n. v. | 水泥浆，灌浆；填缝；用灰浆涂抹 | A thin mortar used to fill cracks and crevices, especially between tiles. As a verb, it means to fill with grout. |  例句：
The tiler used white grout for the bathroom floor.
瓷砖工用白色水泥浆铺浴室地板。

He grouted the tiles after they were laid.
他铺完瓷砖后用水泥浆填缝。

| 近义词：cement, mortar, filler | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun, verb

grout: /ɡraʊt/ | v. | 用灰浆涂抹 | To fill with grout. | 例句：
I need to grout the kitchen tiles.
我需要给厨房瓷砖填缝。

The workers are grouting the wall.
工人们正在给墙抹灰浆。

| 近义词：seal, caulk, fill | 反义词: N/A | 词性: verb

词源：From Old French *grote, from Frankish *grūt, from Proto-Germanic *grūtaz (“grit, groats”). Cognate with Middle Dutch gruut (“groats, grouts”), German Grütze (“groats, porridge”). More at groats. Initially meant "coarse meal," then "sediment," then "thin mortar."
