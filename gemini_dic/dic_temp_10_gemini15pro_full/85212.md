harkener:/ˈhɑːrkənər/ (US) /ˈhɑːkənə(r)/ (UK)| n.|倾听者，听众|A person who listens attentively; a listener.|例句：The harkener was captivated by the storyteller's vivid descriptions. 听众被讲故事人生动的描述所吸引。

例句：She was a keen harkener, always eager to hear new perspectives. 她是一位敏锐的倾听者，总是渴望听到新的观点。|近义词：listener, auditor, audience, earwitness|反义词:speaker, talker|词性:noun

词源: From Middle English *herkener, from herkenen (“to listen”), equivalent to hark +‎ -ener.
