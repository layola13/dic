bleakest:/ˈbliːkɪst/ | adj. | 最荒凉的，最凄凉的，最黯淡的 |  Superlative form of bleak; most lacking in warmth, hope, or cheer. | The bleakest winter I've ever experienced was in 2018.  我经历过的最凄凉的冬天是2018年。|  desolate, dreary, dismal, grim | promising, hopeful, cheerful | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bleik, from Old Norse *bleikr (“pale, wan, bleached”), from Proto-Germanic *blaikaz (“pale, shining, white”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰleg- (“to shine, flash, burn”). Cognate with Old English blāc (“pale, wan, livid”). More at bleak.  -est is the superlative suffix.
