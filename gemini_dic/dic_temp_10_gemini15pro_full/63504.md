enumerability:/ɪˌnjuːmərəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可枚举性，可列举性 | The property of a set whose elements can be put into one-to-one correspondence with the natural numbers or a subset thereof.  A set is enumerable if it is either finite or countably infinite. | The enumerability of rational numbers is a fundamental concept in set theory. 有理数的可枚举性是集合论中的一个基本概念。|  The enumerability of this infinite set surprised many mathematicians.  这个无限集的可枚举性让许多数学家感到惊讶。 | countability, listability | uncountability, non-enumerability | noun
