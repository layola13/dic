afforestation:/əˌfɔːrɪˈsteɪʃən/
n.|造林，植树造林|The act or process of establishing a forest, especially on land not previously forested.|例句：Afforestation projects are crucial for combating climate change.  植树造林项目对对抗气候变化至关重要。

例句：The government has invested heavily in afforestation programs. 政府已大力投资植树造林项目。
|近义词：reforestation, forestation, planting|反义词:deforestation
