globalism:/ˈɡloʊbəlɪzəm/ | n. |全球主义，全球化 |The policy of placing the interests of the entire world above those of individual nations.  The operation or planning of economic and foreign policy on a global basis. |  The protests highlighted growing unease about globalization. 这些抗议突显了人们对全球化日益增长的不安。| Globalization has led to increased interconnectedness between countries. 全球化导致国家间的相互联系日益增强。 | synonyms: internationalism, mundialization, globalization | antonyms: nationalism, localism, regionalism | noun
