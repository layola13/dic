dunner:/ˈdʌnər/| n.|催债人; 讨债人|A person, especially a hired thug, who tries to collect a debt by intimidation.|The dunner showed up at his door demanding payment.|近义词：debt collector, enforcer|反义词: debtor, borrower|词性:名词

dunner:/ˈdʌnər/| v.|催债; 讨债|To make persistent demands for payment.|He was constantly being dunned by creditors.|近义词：badger, harass, pester|反义词: pay, reimburse|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *dunnen "to roar, make a din," of imitative origin.  The sense of "demanding payment of a debt" arose from the noisy and persistent manner of debt collectors.
