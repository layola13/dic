ostentation:/ˌɑːstenˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 炫耀，卖弄 |  A pretentious and vulgar display, especially of wealth and luxury, intended to impress or attract notice. |  He lived a life of ostentation, driving expensive cars and wearing designer clothes. 他过着炫耀的生活，开着豪车，穿着名牌服装。 |  showiness, pretentiousness, flamboyance, extravagance | modesty, humility, simplicity, unpretentiousness | noun

ostentatious:/ˌɑːstenˈteɪʃəs/ | adj. | 炫耀的，卖弄的 | Characterized by vulgar or pretentious display; designed to impress or attract notice. |  Her ostentatious jewelry made it clear she came from a wealthy family. 她炫耀的珠宝清楚地表明她来自一个富裕的家庭。| showy, pretentious, flamboyant, extravagant | modest, humble, simple, unpretentious | adjective
