bathymetric:/ˌbæθɪˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
水深的；测深的
relating to the measurement of the depths of water in oceans, seas, or lakes.
例句：Bathymetric surveys are essential for safe navigation.  水深测量对于安全航行至关重要。

例句：The bathymetric map showed a deep trench near the coast. 水深图显示海岸附近有一条深沟。
近义词：depth-related,  hydrographic
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "topographic" as referring to land elevation)
词性: adjective
