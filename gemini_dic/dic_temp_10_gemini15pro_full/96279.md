indie:/ˈɪndi/ | adj. n. | 独立的，独立制作的；独立音乐，独立电影 | Characteristic of or produced by independent artists or companies, especially in music or film.  |  Example: I really like the indie music scene in this city. 我真的很喜欢这个城市的独立音乐景象。

| Example:  She runs an indie record label. 她经营一家独立唱片公司。 | 近义词: independent, alternative | 反义词: mainstream, commercial | 词性: 形容词, 名词

词源:  Shortened form of *independent*.  源于 *independent* 的缩写形式.
