herbal:/ˈɜːrbl/ (US) /ˈhɜːbl/ (UK)| adj.| 草本的；药草的|Relating to or made from herbs.|例句：She takes an herbal remedy for her cold. 她服用草药来治疗感冒。|例句：Herbal teas are becoming increasingly popular. 花草茶越来越受欢迎了。|近义词：botanical, plant-based|反义词：synthetic, artificial|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Middle English *erbal, from Old French erbal, from Medieval Latin herbālis, from Latin herba (“herb, grass”).
