ETYMOLOGY->

From mis- + use.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English and Old French origin meaning "wrong," "bad," or "wrongly." "Use" comes from the Old French "user" and ultimately from the Latin "usus," meaning "use," "practice," or "custom."
