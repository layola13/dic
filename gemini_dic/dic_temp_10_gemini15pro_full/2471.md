adjustmental: /əˈdʒʌstmənt(ə)l/
adjective
调整的; 调节的; 适应的
Relating to or involving adjustment, especially psychological adjustment.  The process of adapting or becoming used to a new situation.
He experienced some adjustmental difficulties after moving to a new city.  他搬到新城市后经历了一些适应困难。

She demonstrated remarkable adjustmental skills in coping with the loss of her job. 她应对失业展现了非凡的适应能力。

近义词: adaptive, accommodating, flexible
反义词: maladaptive, inflexible, rigid
词性: adjective

词源:  From "adjustment" + "-al".  "Adjustment" comes from the verb "adjust", which derives from Old French "ajuster" (to arrange, put in order).
