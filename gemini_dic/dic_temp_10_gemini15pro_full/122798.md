moldery:/ˈməʊldəri/ (US) /ˈməʊldəri/ (UK)| adj.|发霉的；腐朽的；陈腐的|Covered with or smelling of mold; decaying or crumbling; stale or outdated.|The old books in the attic were moldery and smelled damp.  阁楼里的旧书发霉了，闻起来很潮湿。|The abandoned house had a moldery smell. 这栋废弃的房子有一股霉味。|近义词: musty, mildewy, decaying, rotting, stale|反义词: fresh, new, clean|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *moldery, from mold + -ery.  Ultimately from Old English molde (“dust, earth, loam, soil”).
