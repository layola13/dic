downshifted:/ˈdaʊnˌʃɪftɪd/
adjective|降档的；放慢生活节奏的|Relating to a lower gear or a simpler lifestyle. |例句：They embraced a downshifted lifestyle, moving to a small town. 他们拥抱了慢节奏的生活方式，搬到了一个小镇。|近义词： simpler, slower-paced, less stressful|反义词: fast-paced, stressful, upscale|词性:adj.
