halal:/həˈlɑːl/ | adj. n. | 清真的，符合伊斯兰教法的；清真食品 | Permitted or required by Islamic law; (as a noun) food that is permitted by Islamic law. |  This restaurant serves halal meat. 这家餐厅供应清真肉类。 | Synonyms: permissible, lawful | Antonyms: haram, forbidden | adjective, noun

halals:/həˈlɑːlz/ | n. | 清真食品（halal 的复数形式）| Plural form of "halal" when used as a noun, referring to multiple halal foods or items. | They offer a wide variety of halals at the market.  他们在市场上提供各种各样的清真食品。 | Synonyms: permissible foods, lawful foods | Antonyms: haram foods, forbidden foods | noun
