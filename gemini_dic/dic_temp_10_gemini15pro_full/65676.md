even-up:/ˈiːvən ˈʌp/
verb
使相等；拉平；扯平
to make a situation or competition equal, usually by giving an advantage to the weaker side
The extra time allowed them to even up the score.  加时赛让他们有机会扳平比分。
equalize, balance
-
verb
报复；清算
to get revenge on someone
He swore he'd even up with the man who'd cheated him. 他发誓要报复那个骗他的人。
settle the score, get even
