beavered:/ˈbiːvərd/ | adj. | 忙得不可开交的；像海狸一样忙碌的 |  Resembling or characteristic of a beaver, especially in diligent or persistent work.  | He's been beavered away at that project for weeks.  他几周来一直忙于那个项目。|  busy, industrious, diligent, engaged  | lazy, idle, indolent | adjective

Etymology:
From *beaver* (n.) + *-ed* (suffix forming adjectives).  The word originates from the observed industrious nature of beavers.
