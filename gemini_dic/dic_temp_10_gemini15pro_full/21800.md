bobolink:/ˈbɑːbəˌlɪŋk/ | n. | 燕雀, 巴尔的摩金莺 | A North American migratory songbird (Dolichonyx oryzivorus) of the blackbird family, the male of which has striking black and white plumage in the breeding season. | 例句1：The bobolink's song is a cheerful, bubbling melody. 燕雀的歌声欢快而活泼。

例句2：Bobolinks migrate long distances between their breeding grounds in North America and their wintering grounds in South America. 燕雀在北美的繁殖地和南美的越冬地之间进行长途迁徙。 | 近义词：blackbird, songbird | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源：From the Spanish word *bobo* (meaning "fool, stupid") + *link*, imitative of the bird's song. The name refers to the male bird's striking plumage, which was thought to resemble the motley costume of a jester.
