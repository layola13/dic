gombo:/ˈɡʌmboʊ/ (US), /ˈɡɒmbəʊ/ (UK)| n.|秋葵汤|A stew or soup thickened with okra pods, often containing meat or shellfish, vegetables, and rice.|例句：My grandmother makes the best gumbo. 我祖母做的秋葵汤最好吃。
