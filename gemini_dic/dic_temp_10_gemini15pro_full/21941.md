bogginesses:/ˈbɒɡɪnɪsɪz/
n.
沼泽地，困境，泥潭
A state of being stuck in a bog, either literally or figuratively; a difficult or awkward situation.
The car got stuck in the bogginesses after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，汽车陷入了泥沼。
The project became mired in a series of bogginesses. 这个项目陷入了一系列困境。
近义词:mire, quagmire, swamp, morass, predicament, difficulty
反义词:ease, simplicity, clarity
