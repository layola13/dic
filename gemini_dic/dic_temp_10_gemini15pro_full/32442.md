chimes:/tʃaɪmz/ | n. | 钟声，和谐的声音 | a set of bells, especially in a church tower, or the musical sound they make | The chimes of Big Ben rang out across London.  大本钟的钟声响彻伦敦。| synonyms: bells, peals, ringing | antonyms: silence, quiet | noun

chimes:/tʃaɪmz/ | v. | (钟)鸣响，和谐一致 | to make a clear ringing sound, or to make something produce this sound | The clock chimed six. 时钟敲响了六点。| synonyms: ring, toll, clang | antonyms: be silent, hush | verb
