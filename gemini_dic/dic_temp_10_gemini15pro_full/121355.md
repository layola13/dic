misgraded:/ˌmɪsˈɡreɪdɪd/
Verb
错误评分的，评分不当的
incorrectly graded or assessed.
The teacher misgraded several of the exams. 老师错误地给几份试卷打了分。
近义词: wrongly graded, incorrectly assessed
反义词: correctly graded, accurately assessed
词性:Verb

词源：
mis- + graded.  "mis-" is a prefix indicating something done wrongly or incorrectly. "Graded" comes from "grade," meaning to assign a rank or value.
