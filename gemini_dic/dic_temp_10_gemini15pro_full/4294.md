ais:/eɪˈɪz/ |n. (plural)| aisles |A passage between rows of seats in a building such as a church or theater, an airplane, or train, or between rows of shelves in a supermarket.| Example: The bride walked down the aisle.  新娘沿着通道走过来。| Example: I spent hours wandering the aisles of the supermarket. 我在超市的过道里逛了好几个小时。| Synonym: passageway, corridor, gangway | Antonym: wall, barrier | noun

ais:/eɪˈɪz/ |n. (plural)| ails |(old-fashioned) Problems or difficulties.| Example:  The doctor asked, "What ails you?" 医生问：“你哪里不舒服？”| Example:  She confided in him about all the ails that beset her. 她向他倾诉了困扰她的一切病痛。| Synonym: troubles, difficulties, woes | Antonym: advantages, benefits | noun
