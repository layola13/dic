eurhipidurous:/jʊərɪˈpɪdjʊrəs/
adjective
爱好欧里庇得斯作品的；对欧里庇得斯作品狂热的
Having or showing great enthusiasm for the works of Euripides.
例句：His eurhipidurous nature led him to quote the playwright in every conversation.  他狂热喜爱欧里庇得斯，以至于每次谈话都引用这位剧作家的作品。

例句：She amassed a eurhipidurous collection of books and memorabilia related to the Greek tragedian. 她收集了大量与这位希腊悲剧作家相关的书籍和纪念品，展现出她对欧里庇得斯的热爱。

近义词：enthusiastic, devoted, passionate
反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic
词性：adjective
词源：From Euripides (name of the Greek tragedian) + -ous (suffix forming adjectives).
