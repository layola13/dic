emu:/ˈiːmjuː/ (US), /ˈiːmjuː/ (UK)| n.|鸸鹋|A large, flightless Australian bird similar to an ostrich but smaller. |例句：We saw an emu at the zoo. 我们在动物园看到了一只鸸鹋。

例句：The emu is the second-largest living bird by height, after its ratite relative, the ostrich. 鸸鹋是现存第二高的鸟类，仅次于它的近亲鸵鸟。|近义词：ostrich, rhea|反义词:|noun
