overcloak:/ˈoʊvərˌkloʊk/
n.
掩饰；伪装；幌子
Something that conceals or disguises something else.
The charity worked as an overcloak for his illegal activities.  这个慈善机构是他非法活动的幌子。
His apparent calm was an overcloak for his anxiety. 他表面上的平静掩盖了他的焦虑。
近义词: disguise, cover, facade, pretense, cloak
反义词: revelation, exposure

词性: noun
