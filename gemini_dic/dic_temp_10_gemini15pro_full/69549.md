fellowess:/ˈfɛloʊɛs/
n.
女同伴，女同事，女会员
A female fellow; a woman who shares a particular activity or status with another.
Mary was elected as a fellowess of the Royal Society. 玛丽被选为皇家学会的女会员。
The fellowesses of the college gathered for a reunion. 学院的女会员们聚在一起举行聚会。
Synonyms: colleague, peer, associate, companion
Antonyms: opponent, rival, adversary
noun
