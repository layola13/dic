broken-reed:/ˈbroʊkən riːd/| adj. |脆弱的，靠不住的，易受伤害的|Someone who is a broken reed is someone you cannot rely on for support in times of trouble or difficulty.  It refers to something fragile and easily broken, thus unreliable.|He was a broken reed, offering no support when I needed it most. 他靠不住，在我最需要帮助的时候没有提供任何支持。|Synonyms: unreliable, untrustworthy, undependable, weak, fragile|Antonyms: reliable, trustworthy, dependable, strong, resilient|adjective

broken-reed:/ˈbroʊkən riːd/| n. |脆弱的人，靠不住的人|A broken reed refers to a person who is unreliable and cannot be depended on, especially in times of difficulty.|She proved to be a broken reed when the project faced challenges. 当项目面临挑战时，她被证明是靠不住的。|Synonyms: unreliable person, untrustworthy person, undependable person, weak person|Antonyms: reliable person, trustworthy person, dependable person, strong person|noun
