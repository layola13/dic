oversprinkle: /ˌoʊvərˈsprɪŋkəl/
verb
过度撒；撒得过多
To sprinkle something excessively.
She oversprinkled the cake with sugar, making it too sweet. 她往蛋糕上撒了太多的糖，使它太甜了。
The baker oversprinkled the donuts with powdered sugar. 面包师在甜甜圈上撒了过多的糖粉。

Synonym:  douse, saturate, overpour
Antonym: undersprinkle, skimp
词性: 动词
