interchangeability:/ˌɪntərˌtʃeɪndʒəˈbɪləti/| n.|可互换性，互换性|The interchangeability of parts is crucial in modern manufacturing.  零件的可互换性在现代制造业中至关重要。|Parts designed for interchangeability simplified the manufacturing process. 为可互换性而设计的零件简化了制造过程。|近义词：substitutability, replaceability, exchangeability|反义词：incompatibility, non-interchangeability|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From interchangeable + -ity.  Interchangeable is from inter- + changeable.  "Change" comes from Old French "changier" and ultimately from Latin "cambiare."
