oratorian:/ˌɔːrəˈtɔːriən/ | n. | 雄辩家；奥拉托利会会员 | A person skilled in oratory; a member of the Oratorian order, a Roman Catholic religious congregation. |  The young oratorian delivered a moving sermon.  这位年轻的雄辩家发表了一篇感人的布道。| Synonyms: rhetorician, speaker, preacher | Antonyms: listener, audience | Noun

oratorian:/ˌɔːrəˈtɔːriən/ | adj. | 雄辩的；奥拉托利会的 | Relating to or characteristic of orators or oratory; of or relating to the Oratorian order. |  He gave an oratorian performance, captivating the entire audience. 他进行了一场雄辩的表演，吸引了所有的观众。| Synonyms: eloquent, rhetorical, articulate | Antonyms: inarticulate, hesitant, tongue-tied | Adjective
