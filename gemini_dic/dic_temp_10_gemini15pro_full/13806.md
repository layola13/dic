audit:/ˈɔːdɪt (UK) / ˈɑːdɪt (US)| n. vt.|审计，查账；旁听|An official inspection of an organization's accounts, typically by an independent body.|The company's accounts are audited annually. 这家公司的账目每年都会被审计。|synonyms: inspection, examination, review|antonyms: disregard, neglect|noun, verb

audit:/ˈɔːdɪt (UK) / ˈɑːdɪt (US)| vt.|旁听（大学课程）|Attend (a class) as an auditor.|She audited a course in Spanish literature. 她旁听了西班牙文学的课程。|synonyms: attend, sit in on|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "skip," "avoid")|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "audite," from Latin "auditus," past participle of "audire" (to hear). Originally referred to a hearing or examination of accounts.
