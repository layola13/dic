oversourness:/ˌoʊvərˈsaʊərnəs/| n. |过酸，酸味过重|The oversourness of the lemonade made me pucker. 柠檬水太酸了，让我嘴都 puckered 起来了。|例句：The oversourness of the unripe fruit was unpleasant.  未成熟水果的酸味令人不悦。|近义词：acidity, tartness, sourness|反义词: sweetness, mildness|词性:名词

词源：over (prefix meaning "excessive") + sourness (noun form of "sour")
