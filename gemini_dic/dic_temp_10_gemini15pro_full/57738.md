dream:/driːm/ | n. vi. vt. | 梦; 梦想; 理想; |A series of thoughts, images, and sensations occurring in a person's mind during sleep.  A cherished aspiration, ambition, or ideal.| I had a strange dream last night. 我昨晚做了一个奇怪的梦。| synonyms: vision, fantasy, aspiration | antonyms: reality, actuality | noun

dream:/driːm/ | vi. vt. | 做梦; 梦想; 想到; |To experience dreams during sleep. To indulge in daydreams or fantasies. To conceive of something as possible or desirable.| I dream of traveling the world. 我梦想环游世界。| synonyms: fantasize, envision, imagine | antonyms: awaken | verb
