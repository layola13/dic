demonstrationist:/ˌdemənˈstreɪʃənɪst/ | n. | 示威者，游行者 | A person who participates in a demonstration. |  Example 1: The demonstrationists marched peacefully through the streets. 示威者和平地穿过街道游行。

Example 2:  Several demonstrationists were arrested after the protest turned violent.  几名示威者在抗议演变成暴力事件后被捕。 | Synonym:  protester, demonstrator, marcher, picketer | Antonym:  conformist, bystander | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From demonstration + -ist.  "Demonstration" comes from Late Latin demonstrationem, meaning "a showing, manifestation."  The suffix "-ist" denotes a person who practices or engages in something.
