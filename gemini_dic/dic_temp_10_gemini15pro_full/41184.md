copyright: /ˈkɒpiˌraɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpiˌraɪt/ (US)| n. | 版权；著作权 |The exclusive legal right, given to an originator or an assignee to print, publish, perform, film, or record literary, artistic, or musical material, and to authorize others to do the same. | The book is protected by copyright. 这本书受版权保护。 | synonym: intellectual property, ownership | antonym: public domain | noun

copyright: /ˈkɒpiˌraɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpiˌraɪt/ (US)| vt. | 保护版权；获得…的版权 | To secure a copyright on a work. | He copyrighted his novel as soon as it was finished.  他小说一完成就获得了版权。| synonym: register, protect | antonym: plagiarize, infringe | verb
