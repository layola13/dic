overfreedom:/ˌoʊvərˈfriːdəm/
n.
过度自由
Excessive freedom; a state where an individual has so much freedom that it becomes overwhelming or detrimental.
Some argue that too many choices can lead to overfreedom and paralysis.  有些人认为过多的选择会导致过度自由和瘫痪。
synonyms: excessive freedom, unlimited freedom, boundless liberty
antonyms: restriction, constraint, limitation
noun
