carrageen:/ˈkærəɡiːn/| n.|角叉菜；卡拉胶|Carrageen is a type of red seaweed used as a gelling agent in food. |The pudding was thickened with carrageen. 布丁用角叉菜增稠。|Synonyms: Irish moss, red seaweed|Antonyms: None|Noun
