brass:/bræs/
n.|黄铜；铜管乐器；黄铜制品；厚脸皮|A yellow alloy of copper and zinc.  Also, wind instruments made of brass. Can also refer to high-ranking military officers or officials. |The trumpets and trombones are brass instruments. |近义词：bronze, nerve, audacity|反义词：modesty, shyness|词性:noun

brass:/bræs/
adj.|黄铜制的；铜管乐器的|Made of brass. |The doorknob was made of solid brass. |近义词：bronze, brazen|反义词：-|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bræs, from Proto-Germanic *brasa-. Related to Old High German bras (German Erz "ore").
