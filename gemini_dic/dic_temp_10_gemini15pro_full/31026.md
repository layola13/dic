chalybeate: /kəˈlɪbiət/ /ˈkæliˌbiːɪt/
adjective
含铁的；铁质的
(of water or other liquid) impregnated with iron salts.  Containing iron (especially of medicinal springs).
例句：The doctor recommended a stay at a chalybeate spring for his health. 医生建议他为了健康去含铁的温泉疗养。

例句：The chalybeate waters are said to be good for anemia.  据说含铁的水对贫血有好处。

近义词：ferruginous, iron-rich
反义词： (No direct antonym, could consider "iron-deficient" in context)
词性：adjective


chalybeate: /kəˈlɪbiət/ /ˈkæliˌbiːɪt/
noun
铁质矿泉；含铁的
A mineral spring containing iron salts.
例句：The town was famous for its chalybeate and attracted visitors from all over.  这个小镇以其铁质矿泉而闻名，吸引了来自各地的游客。

例句：He filled a bottle from the chalybeate, hoping it would improve his condition. 他从铁质矿泉装满一瓶水，希望它能改善他的状况。

近义词：iron spring, mineral spring
反义词： (No direct antonym)
词性：noun
