lend-a-hand:/ˌlend ə ˈhænd/ | v. | 帮助；帮忙；伸出援手 | To offer help or assistance. | Example: Can you lend a hand with these boxes?  你能帮我搬一下这些箱子吗？

Example:  He lent a hand to his elderly neighbor with her groceries. 他帮他的年迈邻居提杂货。 | 近义词: help, assist, aid, support | 反义词: hinder, obstruct, impede | 词性: verb (phrase)

lend:/lend/ | v. | 借出；贷与；给予（帮助、支持等） | To give something to someone for a short period of time, expecting it to be returned.  Also, to provide help or support. | Example: Could you lend me your pen? 你能把你的笔借给我吗？

Example: The bank refused to lend him any more money. 银行拒绝再借给他钱。 | 近义词: loan, give, provide | 反义词: borrow, take | 词性: verb

hand:/hænd/ | n. | 手；帮助；指针；工人 | The part of the body at the end of the arm. Also can refer to assistance, a pointer on a clock, or a worker. | Example: Please wash your hands before dinner.  饭前请洗手。

Example:  All hands on deck! 全体船员到甲板上！ | 近义词: assistance, help, pointer, worker | 反义词: (No direct antonym for the physical "hand") | 词性: noun
