deliriousness:/dɪˈlɪriəsnəs/
n.
神志不清，谵妄
A state of mental confusion and excitement characterized by disorientation, hallucinations, and incoherent speech.
The patient's deliriousness made it difficult for him to communicate. 病人的神志不清使得他难以沟通。
近义词:confusion, disorientation, incoherence, madness, frenzy
反义词:lucidity, clarity, coherence, sanity
词性:noun

deliriousness:/dɪˈlɪriəsnəs/
noun
谵妄；精神错乱
The state of being delirious.
His deliriousness was caused by a high fever. 他的谵妄是由高烧引起的。
近义词:hysteria, mania, insanity
反义词:sanity, lucidity
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēlīriōsus, from Latin dēlīrāre (“to be crazy, rave”), from dē- (“from, away”) + līra (“furrow, track”).  Meaning "state of being delirious" from 17th century.
