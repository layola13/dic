bulletproofs:/ˈbʊlɪtpruːfs/
n.
防弹的；防弹物；防弹装置；Bulletproofs (一种加密货币技术)
Bulletproofs are cryptographic proofs that are short, non-interactive zero-knowledge proofs.  Bulletproofs是一种简洁的、非交互式的零知识证明的密码学证明。
Bulletproofs are used in cryptocurrency transactions to enhance privacy.  Bulletproofs被用于加密货币交易中以增强隐私性。
Synonyms: tamper-proof, impenetrable
Antonyms: vulnerable, penetrable
noun
