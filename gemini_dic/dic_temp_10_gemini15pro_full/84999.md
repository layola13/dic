haradrim:/ˈhærədrɪm/| n.|哈拉德林人，哈拉德人|A fictional race of men from the south of Middle-earth in J.R.R. Tolkien's fantasy world. They are described as swarthy-skinned and often allied with the forces of Mordor.|The Haradrim rode great oliphaunts into battle. 哈拉德人骑着巨大的猛犸象作战。|Near synonyms: Southrons, Easterlings (in the context of Tolkien's work)|Antonyms:  Men of the West (in the context of Tolkien's work)|noun
