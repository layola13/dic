numismatician:/ˌnjuːməˈtɪʃən/ /ˌnuːməˈtɪʃən/| n.|钱币学家，钱币收藏家|A person who studies or collects coins, medals, paper money, etc.|例句：The numismatician was thrilled to find a rare coin from the Roman Empire.  这位钱币学家很高兴找到一枚稀有的罗马帝国时期的硬币。

例句：She is a renowned numismatician with a vast collection of ancient coins.  她是一位著名的钱币学家，收藏了大量的古钱币。|近义词：coin collector, philatelist (for stamps)|反义词: None|词性: Noun
