forestall:/fɔːrˈstɔːl/ /fɔːrˈstɑːl/
verb
预防，阻止
To prevent or obstruct (an anticipated event or action) by taking action ahead of time.
The company took steps to forestall a hostile takeover.  公司采取措施以防止恶意收购。
Heavy rains in the spring forestalled the drought. 春天的大雨阻止了旱灾的发生。
近义词:prevent, preclude, obviate, avert, anticipate
反义词:precipitate, encourage, promote
词性:verb
