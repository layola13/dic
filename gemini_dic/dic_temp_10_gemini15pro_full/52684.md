dignifiedness:/ˈdɪɡnɪfaɪdnəs/| n. |庄重，尊严，高贵|The quality of being dignified; stateliness and nobility of manner.|She accepted the criticism with dignifiedness. 她庄重地接受了批评。|近义词：dignity, nobility, stateliness, grandeur, majesty|反义词：humility, insignificance, lowliness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From dignified + -ness.  Dignified comes from the Latin dignus meaning "worthy" + -fied (from French -fié, ultimately from Latin -ficare "to make").
