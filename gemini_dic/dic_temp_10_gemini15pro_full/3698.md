aggregating:/ˈæɡrɪɡeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 聚集的，累积的，加重的 | Combining several elements into a single whole; increasing the severity of something. |  The company is aggregating data from various sources.  这家公司正在汇总来自不同来源的数据。

The aggregating effect of these small changes was significant. 这些微小变化的累积效应是显著的。 |  cumulative, collective, combined, amassed, total  |  dispersing, separating, dividing, individual, diminishing | adjective


aggregating:/ˈæɡrɪɡeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 聚集，累积，加重 |  Present participle of "aggregate".  |  He is aggregating the data into a single report. 他正在将数据汇总到一份报告中。

She found his constant complaining aggregating. 她发现他不断的抱怨越来越严重。 | accumulating, amassing, collecting, combining, totaling | dispersing, separating, dividing, decreasing, lessening | verb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *aggregatus*, past participle of aggregare "to add to a flock," from ad- "to" + grex "flock, herd."
