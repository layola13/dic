anticynics:/ˌæntiˈsɪnɪks/
|n.|反愤世嫉俗者；反犬儒主义者|People who actively oppose or reject cynicism, believing in sincerity, goodness, or the positive aspects of humanity.  Those who have hope and believe in the good in people and society, often acting against cynical viewpoints.|Example: The anticynics in the group continued to believe in the project despite the setbacks.  这群反愤世嫉俗者尽管遇到挫折，仍然相信这个项目。

Example:  Her anticynics rallied around her, offering support and encouragement. 她的反犬儒主义支持者们团结在她周围，给予支持和鼓励。|近义词: optimist, idealist, believer, humanist|反义词: cynic, pessimist, misanthrope|词性:noun
