glycoluril:/ˌɡlaɪkəˈlʊərɪl/ | n. |乙内酰脲|A bicyclic organic compound with the formula C4H6N4O2. It is a white crystalline solid that is slightly soluble in water.  It is used in the production of various polymers, resins, and coatings, as well as in some personal care products. |Glycoluril is a key ingredient in some high-performance coatings. 乙内酰脲是一些高性能涂料的关键成分。

Glycoluril derivatives are often used as crosslinking agents. 乙内酰脲衍生物常用作交联剂。 |近义词:tetrahydroimidazo[4,5-d]imidazole-2,5(1H,3H)-dione (IUPAC name) |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
