immensely:/ɪˈmɛnsli/| adv.|非常地，极其地|To a very great extent; extremely.|The task was immensely difficult, but they managed to complete it. 这项任务极其困难，但他们还是设法完成了。|近义词：extremely, greatly, highly, enormously, vastly|反义词：slightly, marginally, insignificantly|词性:副词
词源: From immense + -ly.  "Immense" comes from the French "immense," ultimately from the Latin "immensus," meaning "immeasurable," from "in-" (not) + "mensus" (measured).
