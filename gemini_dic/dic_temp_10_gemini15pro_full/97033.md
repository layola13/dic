infectiveness:/ɪnˈfɛktɪvnəs/| n.|传染性，感染力|The infectiveness of a disease is determined by how easily it spreads from one person to another. 一种疾病的传染性取决于它从一个人传播到另一个人的容易程度。|例句：The new variant of the virus has a higher infectiveness. 这种病毒的新变种具有更高的传染性。|近义词：contagiousness, transmissibility|反义词:resistance|词性:名词
