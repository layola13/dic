atheism:/ˈeɪθiɪzəm/ (US), /ˈeɪθiɪzəm/ (UK)| n.|无神论|Disbelief or lack of belief in the existence of God or gods.|例句：Atheism is on the rise in many Western countries.  许多西方国家的无神论正在兴起。

例句：He openly declared his atheism. 他公开宣布自己是无神论者。|近义词：non-belief, unbelief, skepticism, irreligion|反义词：theism, belief, faith|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *athéisme*, from Ancient Greek *ἄθεος* (átheos, “without a god”), from *ἀ-* (a-, “without”) + *θεός* (theós, “god”).  The English word atheism is first attested in the late 16th century.
