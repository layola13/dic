brave:/breɪv/| adj.|勇敢的，无畏的|Showing courage and resolution in the face of danger or difficulty.|例句：The brave firefighter rescued the child from the burning building. 勇敢的消防员从燃烧的建筑物中救出了孩子。|例句：It was a brave decision to start her own business.  开始自己的事业是一个勇敢的决定。|近义词：courageous, valiant, fearless, bold, daring|反义词：cowardly, timid, fearful|词性:形容词

brave:/breɪv/| v.|勇敢地面对|To face or endure with courage.|例句：She braved the storm to get home. 她冒着暴风雨回家。|例句：He braved the criticism and continued his work.  他不顾批评，继续他的工作。|近义词：confront, face, endure, withstand|反义词：avoid, evade, shun|词性:动词
