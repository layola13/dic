ineffervescible:/ˌɪnɪfərˈvɛsəb(ə)l/
adjective
沉闷的，无生气的，不兴奋的
Lacking effervescence; not bubbly or lively.  Not showing enthusiasm or excitement.
He delivered the news in an ineffervescible monotone. 他用一种沉闷的单调语气宣布了这个消息。
Synonyms: dull, flat, listless, phlegmatic, apathetic, unexcitable
Antonyms: effervescent, lively, bubbly, vivacious, enthusiastic, dynamic
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From in- (not) + effervescent (bubbly, lively) + -ible (capable of being).  Ultimately from Latin ex- (out) + fervere (to boil).
