hagged:/ˈhæɡɪd/| adj.|憔悴的，像巫婆的|Looking tired and worn, as if by worry or suffering; having a wild or disheveled appearance.  Resembling a hag, especially in being ugly or frightening.|She looked haggard after her sleepless night. 她一夜没睡，看起来很憔悴。|近义词：worn, gaunt, drawn, careworn, exhausted, weary|反义词：youthful, refreshed, radiant|词性:形容词

hagged:/ˈhæɡɪd/| n.|老丑的女人（罕用）|An ugly old woman, especially one thought to have evil powers.|The old hagged glared at the children. 那个老巫婆怒视着孩子们。|近义词：hag, witch, crone|反义词：beauty, maiden|词性:名词 (罕用)
