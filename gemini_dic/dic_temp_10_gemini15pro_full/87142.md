herbescent:/hɜːrˈbɛsənt/ | adj. |草本的，草绿色的 | Becoming herbaceous; resembling a herb in having a soft stem. |  The herbescent border of the garden was filled with fragrant herbs. 花园里草绿色的边界种满了芳香的草本植物。| The artist captured the herbescent hues of the meadow in his painting.  艺术家在他的画作中捕捉到了草地草绿色的色调。| 近义词: herbaceous, grassy, leafy | 反义词: woody, ligneous | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *herbescens*, present participle of *herbescere* "to become grassy," from *herba* "herb"
