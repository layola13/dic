词源：From Old French baptiser, from Late Latin baptizare, from Greek baptizein "to immerse, dip, plunge," from baptos "dipped, dyed," verbal adjective of baptein "to dip, dye."


注意事项:  "baptising" is the British English spelling. The American English spelling is "baptizing."
