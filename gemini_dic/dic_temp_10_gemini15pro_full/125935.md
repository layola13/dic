mushroom:/ˈmʌʃruːm/ (US), /ˈmʌʃrʊm/ (UK)| n. vi.|蘑菇；迅速增长；|A mushroom is a fleshy, spore-bearing fruiting body of a fungus, typically produced above ground on soil or on its food source.|Example: We picked wild mushrooms in the forest. 我们在森林里采摘野生蘑菇。|Example: The city's population mushroomed after the gold rush.  淘金热之后，该市的人口迅速增长。|近义词：fungus, toadstool (for poisonous varieties)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "萎缩/shrink" for the verb form)|词性: noun, verb
