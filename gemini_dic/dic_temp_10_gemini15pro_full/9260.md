antipatriotic:/ˌæntiˌpeɪtriˈɑːtɪk/
adjective
不爱国的，反爱国的
Opposed to one's country; lacking or expressing opposition to patriotic feeling.
His antipatriotic speeches caused outrage. 他的不爱国言论引起了众怒。
近义词: unpatriotic, disloyal, treasonous, seditious
反义词: patriotic, loyal, nationalist
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (against) + patriotic (inspired by love for one's country).  The word "patriotic" ultimately comes from the Greek "patriotes" meaning "fellow countryman."
