nonimpositions:/ˌnɒnɪmpəˈzɪʃənz/
n.
不强加；不施加压力；不打扰
The state of not imposing something, especially taxes, rules, or one's presence.
The artist appreciated the nonimposition of deadlines, allowing him to work at his own pace. 艺术家很感激没有截止日期的压力，这让他可以按照自己的节奏工作。
noninterference, abstention
imposition, intrusion
