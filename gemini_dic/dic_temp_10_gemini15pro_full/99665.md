interlabelling:/ˌɪntərˈleɪbəlɪŋ/
|n.|相互标记，互相标注|The process of assigning labels to multiple items or entities in a way that establishes connections or relationships between them.  It involves marking or tagging items with corresponding labels to indicate their association or interdependence.  This can be useful for tracking relationships, organizing data, or coordinating activities across different elements.|The interlabelling of the samples allowed researchers to track the flow of genetic material between different cell populations.|近义词：cross-referencing, linking, correlating|反义词:isolating, separating|词性:名词
