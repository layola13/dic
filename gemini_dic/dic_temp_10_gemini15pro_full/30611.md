cerebrin: /ˈsɛrɪbrɪn/ /ˈsɛrəbrɪn/
noun
脑苷脂
A fatty substance found in brain tissue.
Cerebrin is a component of the myelin sheath that insulates nerve fibers. 脑苷脂是构成髓鞘的成分之一，髓鞘可以隔离神经纤维。
Cerebrin plays a role in nerve impulse transmission. 脑苷脂在神经冲动传递中发挥作用。
近义词: cerebroside
反义词: (No common antonym)
词性: n.

Etymology:
From Latin cerebrum ("brain") + -in (chemical suffix indicating a neutral substance).
