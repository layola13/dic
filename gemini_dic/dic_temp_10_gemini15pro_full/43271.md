craftiness:/ˈkræftinəs/
n.|狡猾，诡诈|The fox's craftiness allowed it to steal the chickens. 狐狸的狡猾让它偷走了鸡。|例句：He achieved his goals through sheer craftiness. 他完全靠狡猾实现了目标。|近义词：cunning, slyness, guile, deviousness|反义词：honesty, sincerity, forthrightness|词性：名词

词源: From Middle English *craftienes*, from Old English *cræftiga*, equivalent to crafty +‎ -ness.
