directorate:/daɪˈrɛktərət/, /dɪˈrɛktərət/ | n. | Directorate (also refers to the position or tenure of a director) | The directorate lasted for five years.  指导部的任期为五年。 | Synonyms:  leadership, administration, directorship | Antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun


Etymology:  From Late Middle English, from Medieval Latin *directoratus, from Latin director “director” + -atus (suffix forming nouns of office or function).
