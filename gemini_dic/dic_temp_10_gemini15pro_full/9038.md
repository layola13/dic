antimaterialists:/ˌænti məˈtɪəriəlɪsts/
|n.|反物质主义者|People who oppose or reject materialism, prioritizing spiritual or ethical values over material possessions.| Many antimaterialists believe that consumerism is detrimental to society.  许多反物质主义者认为消费主义对社会有害。|近义词:spiritualists, ascetics, idealists|反义词:materialists, consumerists|Noun


antimaterialist:/ˌænti məˈtɪəriəlɪst/
|adj.|反物质主义的|Opposed to or rejecting materialism; prioritizing spiritual or ethical values over material possessions.|  They live an antimaterialist lifestyle, focused on experiences rather than acquiring things. 他们过着反物质主义的生活方式，专注于体验而不是获取物品。|近义词:spiritual, ascetic, idealistic|反义词:materialistic, consumerist|Adjective
