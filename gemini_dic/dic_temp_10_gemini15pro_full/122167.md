mistruths:/ˌmɪsˈtruːθs/
noun
不实之言，谎言
false statements, lies
Many politicians spread mistruths during election campaigns. 许多政治家在竞选期间散布不实之言。
The article was full of mistruths and distortions. 这篇文章充满了不实之言和歪曲。
synonyms: lies, falsehoods, fabrications, untruths, fallacies
antonyms: truths, facts, realities
n.
