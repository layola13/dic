exceed:/ɪkˈsiːd/
verb
超过，超出
to be greater than a number or amount, or to go past an allowed limit
The final cost should not exceed $5,000.  最终费用不应超过 5,000 美元。

surpass, excel, outstrip, transcend
fall short of, be less than, lag behind
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *exceder, from Latin excedere ("to go out, go beyond, surpass, exceed"), from ex- ("out of, from") + cedere ("to go, yield").
