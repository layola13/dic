bugginesses:/ˈbʌɡɪnɪsɪz/
|n.| 烦扰，恼怒，麻烦事（通常用复数形式）|A state of being bothered, annoyed, or preoccupied with petty annoyances or anxieties. It often implies a feeling of being overwhelmed by minor problems.|
例句：His constant bugginesses were starting to wear on everyone's nerves. 他不断的小麻烦开始让每个人都心烦意乱。

例句：She had a lot of bugginesses on her mind. 她心里有很多烦心事。
|近义词：worries, anxieties, troubles, hassles, annoyances|
反义词：calm, peace, tranquility|
词性:名词


词源:
Derived from "bug," meaning to annoy or bother, with the suffix "-iness" added to form a noun indicating a state or quality.  The plural form "bugginesses" emphasizes the multiplicity of minor annoyances.
