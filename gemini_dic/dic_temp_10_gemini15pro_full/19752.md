billeting:/ˈbɪlɪtɪŋ/
n.
住宿安排；提供住宿；被安排住宿
The act of assigning lodging to military personnel or others.
The soldiers were billeted in local homes. 士兵们被安排住在当地居民家中。

billeting:/ˈbɪlɪtɪŋ/
n.
住宿；被安排的住处
Lodging assigned to military personnel or others.
The billeting was basic but adequate. 住宿条件简陋但足够了。


ETYMOLOGY->
From billet (n.) + -ing (suffix forming nouns from verbs).  The verb "billet" comes from the French "billet," meaning a small stick or note, which was used as a lodging assignment.

SYNONYMS->
accommodation, lodging, housing, quartering

ANTONYMS->
eviction, displacement, homelessness
