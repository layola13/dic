convection:/kənˈvekʃən/ | n. | 对流，传送 | The transfer of heat through a fluid (liquid or gas) caused by molecular motion. | Heat is transferred from the baseboard heater to the room by convection.  热量通过对流从踢脚板加热器传递到房间。| synonyms: circulation, transmission | antonyms: conduction, radiation | noun

convective:/kənˈvektiv/ | adj. | 对流的 | Relating to, or characterized by convection. | Convective clouds often bring heavy rain. 对流云经常带来暴雨。| synonyms: circulatory | antonyms: conductive, radiative | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *convectiōn- (stem of convectiō), from Latin convect-, past participle stem of convehere "to carry or bring together," from com- "together" + vehere "to carry."
