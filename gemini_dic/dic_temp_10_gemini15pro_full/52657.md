digitiform:/ˈdɪdʒɪtɪfɔːrm/
adjective
指状的，指形的
Resembling a finger or toe in form.
The digitiform leaves of the plant looked like tiny green fingers reaching for the sun.  这种植物的指状叶看起来像伸向太阳的绿色小手指。
The aye-aye has a digitiform middle finger that it uses to extract insects from wood. 指猴有一根指状的中指，用来从木头中取出昆虫。
近义词:finger-shaped, fingerlike
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider words describing other shapes, e.g., ovate, lanceolate)
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *digitātus 'having fingers', from digitus 'finger'.
