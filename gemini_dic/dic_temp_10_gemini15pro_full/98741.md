insurrectionism:/ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃənɪzəm/
n.
暴乱主义；起义主义；叛乱主义
The act of engaging in insurrection or rebellion against an established government or authority.
The government accused the group of insurrectionism and treason. 政府指控该组织暴乱和叛国。
insurgency, rebellion, revolt, uprising
obedience, loyalty, submission
noun
