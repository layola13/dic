anthropolite:/ˈænθrəpəˌlaɪt/
n.
人造石，人造宝石
A manufactured gem resembling a natural gem.
This ring features a beautiful anthropolite that sparkles like a diamond. 这枚戒指上有一颗美丽的人造宝石，像钻石一样闪闪发光。

Near Synonyms:
simulant, imitation, synthetic gem, artificial gem

Antonyms:
natural gem, genuine gem

Etymology:
From Greek *anthrōpo- (human) + -lite (stone).

Word Type: Noun
