nonexculpations:/ˌnɒnˌɛksˌkʌlˈpeɪʃənz/
|n.|
|无开脱；不免罪；不辩解|
|The absence of exculpations, or acts of clearing someone from blame or guilt.  It refers to a situation where no excuses or justifications are offered or accepted.|


例句：
The lack of nonexculpations led to his conviction.  缺少免罪的证据导致了他的定罪。

His nonexculpations regarding the incident were unconvincing. 他对事件的辩解 unconvincing.


近义词：
incriminations, accusations, condemnations
|n.|
|控告；指责；谴责|

反义词：
exculpations, apologies, justifications
|n.|
|开脱；道歉；辩护|

词性:
noun (plural)
