disturbing:/dɪˈstɜːrbɪŋ/| adj.|令人不安的，令人烦恼的，扰乱的|Causing anxiety; worrying.  Derived from the verb "disturb," which comes from Old French "destourber" (to hinder, disturb), ultimately from Latin "disturbare" (to throw into disorder).|The news report about the war was deeply disturbing.  战争的新闻报道令人深感不安。

The disturbing images kept flashing back in her mind. 那些令人不安的画面不断在她脑海中闪现。|近义词：unsettling, troubling, worrying, alarming, upsetting|反义词：calming, soothing, reassuring, comforting|词性:形容词
