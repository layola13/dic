archaeal:/ɑːrˈkiːəl/| adj.|古细菌的；古菌的|Of or relating to archaea, a domain of single-celled microorganisms.|Archaea are single-celled organisms that are similar to bacteria in size and simplicity of structure but radically different in molecular organization.  古细菌是单细胞生物，其大小和结构的简单性与细菌相似，但在分子组织上却根本不同。|Archaea thrive in extreme environments, such as hot springs, where archaeal life forms predominate.  古细菌在极端环境中茁壮成长，例如温泉，在那里古细菌生命形式占主导地位。|近义词: prokaryotic|反义词: eukaryotic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *Archaeum* (referring to the domain Archaea) + *-al* (suffix forming adjectives).  *Archaeum* is from Ancient Greek *ἀρχαῖος (arkhaîos)* meaning "ancient, primeval," as these organisms were thought to represent a very ancient form of life.
