glaucescent:/ɡlɔːˈsɛsənt/
adj.
略带白霜的，稍被白粉的
Covered with a pale bloom; slightly glaucous.
The glacescent leaves of the plant shimmered in the sunlight. 这种植物略带白霜的叶子在阳光下闪闪发光。
近义词: glaucous, frosted
反义词: shiny, glossy
词性: 形容词
