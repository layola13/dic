oarsweed:/ˈɔːrzwiːd/ | n. | 桨草，一种生长在水中的植物 | A type of seaweed, especially one that resembles oars or paddles. It is often found attached to rocks or other submerged objects in coastal areas. |  Example: The oarsweed swayed gently in the current. 桨草在水流中轻轻摇摆。 Example:  We found a large clump of oarsweed washed up on the beach. 我们发现一大丛桨草被冲上了海滩。 | 近义词: seaweed, kelp | 反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑 terrestrial plant 陆生植物) | 词性: noun


词源:  "oarsweed" is a compound word formed from "oars" and "weed."  "Oars" refers to the resemblance of the seaweed's shape to the blades of oars. "Weed" in this context refers to any plant growing in water, especially seaweed.
