nonmythologically:/ˌnɒnˌmɪθəˈlɒdʒɪkəli/
adv.
非神话地，不涉及神话地
In a way that does not involve or relate to mythology.
He explained the origins of the universe nonmythologically, focusing on scientific evidence. 他以非神话的方式解释了宇宙的起源，重点是科学证据。
The historian approached the subject nonmythologically, relying on historical documents.  这位历史学家以非神话的方式研究这个主题，依靠的是历史文献。
近义词: realistically, factually, empirically
反义词: mythically, legendarily, fabulously
词性: adverb
