bluer:/ˈbluːər/ | adj. | 更蓝的 |  The comparative form of "blue," meaning more blue than something else.  | The sky is bluer today than yesterday. 今天的天空比昨天更蓝。|  The ocean looks bluer from the top of the cliff. 从悬崖顶上看，大海显得更蓝。 | 近义词：deeper blue, more azure, more cerulean | 反义词：paler, lighter, less blue | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Old English *blǣw, from Proto-Germanic *blēwaz, meaning "blue, livid, lead-colored."  The "-er" suffix is a comparative ending.
