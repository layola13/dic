non-symmetrical:/ˌnɒn sɪˈmetrɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːn sɪˈmetrɪkəl/ | adj. | 非对称的，不对称的 | Not symmetrical; having parts on either side of a central line or plane that do not correspond in size, shape, or position. | The artist deliberately used non-symmetrical patterns in her work. 这位艺术家在她的作品中特意使用了不对称的图案。|  The building's non-symmetrical facade made it stand out from its surroundings.  这座建筑不对称的正面使其在周围环境中脱颖而出。 | asymmetrical, irregular, unbalanced, uneven | symmetrical, balanced, even, regular | adjective
