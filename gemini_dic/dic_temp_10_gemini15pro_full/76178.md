fussers:/ˈfʌsərz/| n.|大惊小怪的人；小题大做的人；过于讲究的人|People who make a big deal out of minor things; those who are overly concerned with unimportant details. |例句：The fussers complained about the placement of the napkins. 这些大惊小怪的人抱怨餐巾纸的摆放。

例句：Don't be such a fusser; it's just a small scratch. 别这么小题大做，只是一个小划痕而已。|近义词：worriers, nitpickers, perfectionists|反义词:easy-going people, carefree people|词性:noun (plural)
