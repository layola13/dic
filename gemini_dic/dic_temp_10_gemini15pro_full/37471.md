commissionnaire:/kəmˌɪʃəˈnɛːr/ | n. | 服务员，门房，行李员 | A uniformed attendant, typically one employed in a hotel or theater, who performs errands for guests or patrons. |  The commissionnaire opened the door for us with a flourish.  门房殷勤地为我们开了门。| Synonyms: porter, attendant, doorman, concierge | Antonyms: guest, patron | noun

commissionnaire:/kəmˌɪʃəˈnɛːr/ | n. | (尤指前军人的)信差，跑腿的人 | A person, often a former soldier, employed to run errands, deliver messages, etc. | The company employed a commissionnaire to deliver important documents. 公司雇佣了一名信差递送重要文件。| Synonyms: messenger, courier, runner | Antonyms: recipient, receiver | noun
