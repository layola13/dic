circumnavigatory:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈnævɪɡətɔːri/ | adj. | 环球航行的，环绕航行的 | Relating to, or involving circumnavigation; going around the world, especially by water. | Magellan's circumnavigatory voyage proved the Earth was round. 麦哲伦的环球航行证明了地球是圆的。 | The circumnavigatory expedition was a resounding success. 这次环球航行探险取得了巨大的成功。 | 近义词：round-the-world, global | 反义词：local, regional | 词性：形容词

circumnavigate:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈnævɪɡeɪt/ | v. | 环球航行，绕...航行 | To sail or travel all the way around (something, especially the world). | They circumnavigated the globe in a small yacht. 他们乘坐一艘小型游艇环游世界。 |  The team plans to circumnavigate the island.  这个团队计划环绕岛屿航行。 | 近义词：sail around, go around | 反义词：stay put | 词性：动词

词源: From Latin circumnavigatus, past participle of circumnavigare (“to sail around”), from circum (“around”) + navigare (“to sail, navigate”).
