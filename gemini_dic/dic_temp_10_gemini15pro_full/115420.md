mantilla:/mænˈtɪlə/ | n. |  曼蒂利亚（一种西班牙妇女戴的蕾丝或丝绸头巾） | A light scarf or veil worn over the head and shoulders, especially by Spanish women. |  The bride wore a beautiful lace mantilla.  新娘戴着漂亮的蕾丝曼蒂利亚。

She wore a black mantilla to the funeral. 她戴着黑色的曼蒂利亚参加葬礼。 | 近义词: veil, scarf, shawl | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *mantilla*, diminutive of *manta* ("blanket"), from Latin *manta* (“shaggy cloth, blanket”). Ultimately of uncertain origin, possibly Pre-Roman.
