crownless:/ˈkraʊnləs/
adjective
无冕的，未加冕的，没有王冠的
Without a crown; not crowned.
The crownless king wandered the streets, unrecognized.  无冕之王在街上游荡，无人认出。
He played a crownless king in the play.  他在剧中扮演一位没有加冕的国王。
近义词:uncrowned, unkinged
反义词:crowned, regal
词性:adjective

crownless:/ˈkraʊnləs/
adjective
(比喻)缺乏权威或尊严的
Lacking authority or dignity; deprived of power or status.
The former champion, now crownless, watched the match with a wistful expression. 曾经的冠军，如今失去了王冠，带着渴望的表情看着比赛。
She felt crownless and powerless after the scandal.  丑闻之后，她感到自己失去了权威和力量。
近义词:powerless, dethroned, humbled
反义词:powerful, authoritative, respected
词性:adjective
