fagotto:/fəˈɡɒtəʊ/ (UK), /fəˈɡɑːtoʊ/ (US)| n. |巴松管，低音管|A double-reed woodwind instrument with a long conical tube doubled back on itself, having a deep, mellow tone.|The fagotto solo in the second movement was particularly moving.  巴松管在第二乐章的独奏尤其动人。|Synonyms: bassoon|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a musical instrument)|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *fagotto* "bundle of sticks," from *faggot* "bundle of sticks" (from Late Latin *facus*, variant of *fascis* "bundle"), so called from the instrument's resemblance to a bundle of sticks.
