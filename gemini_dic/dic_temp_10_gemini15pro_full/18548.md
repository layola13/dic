benefit:/ˈbenɪfɪt/ /ˈbɛnəfɪt/| n. vt. vi.|利益，好处；有益于|Something that is advantageous or good; an advantage.|例句：The benefit of regular exercise is undeniable.  规律运动的好处是不可否认的。|近义词：advantage, profit, gain|反义词：disadvantage, detriment, loss|词性:名词

benefit:/ˈbenɪfɪt/ /ˈbɛnəfɪt/| vt. vi.|有益于，受益|To be helpful or advantageous to; to receive an advantage.|例句：The new policy will benefit low-income families. 这项新政策将使低收入家庭受益。|近义词：help, aid, assist|反义词：harm, hinder|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French bienfet (modern French bienfait), literally "good deed," from bien ("well") + fait ("deed, fact").  Ultimately from Latin bene ("well") and factum ("deed").
