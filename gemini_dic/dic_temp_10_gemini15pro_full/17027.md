bated:/ˈbeɪtɪd/| adj.| 屏息的，抑制的 |  Reduced or moderated; especially : held in restraint : SUSPENDED.  Characterized by bated breath : in anxious suspense |  They waited with bated breath for the results of the election. 他们屏息等待着选举结果。 | 近义词: suppressed, restrained, hushed, muted | 反义词: unrestrained, uninhibited | 词性:形容词

词源:
From the verb "bate," meaning "to reduce the force or intensity of," which comes from the Old French "abattre" (to beat down).  Ultimately related to the word "abate."
