kettin:/ˈkɛtɪn/ | n. | 毛毡, 厚呢, 粗毯 | A heavy, coarse fabric made of matted wool or other fibers. | The shepherd wrapped the newborn lamb in a warm kettin. 牧羊人用温暖的毛毡包裹着新生的羔羊。 |  The old woman wore a kettin cloak to protect her from the cold. 老妇人披着厚呢斗篷御寒。 | 近义词: felt, baize, blanket | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

词源: From Scots *kettin*, of uncertain origin. Possibly from Old Norse *köttr* (flesh, meat) + *-inn* (suffix forming adjectives). This would suggest a connection to a type of felt originally made from animal hair.
