long-serving:/ˌlɒŋˈsɜːvɪŋ/ | /ˌlɔːŋˈsɜːrvɪŋ/ | adj. | 长期服务的；任职时间长的 | Having served for a long time, especially in a particular job or role. |  She is a long-serving member of the committee. 她是一位在委员会任职很久的成员。 |  He is a long-serving employee of the company. 他是这家公司的资深员工。 | 近义词: veteran, experienced, established | 反义词: short-term, new, recent | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Compound of "long" and "serving," the present participle of "serve."  "Long" traces back to Old English "lang," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*langaz." "Serve" comes from Old French "servir," ultimately from Latin "servire" meaning "to be a slave, serve."
