coverlid: /ˈkʌvər.lɪd/ (US) /ˈkʌvə.lɪd/ (UK)
n.
床罩，被罩
A decorative outer covering for a bed, often quilted or padded.
She pulled the coverlid up to her chin and snuggled into the pillows. 她把床罩拉到下巴，依偎在枕头里。
The hotel provided a clean, white coverlid for each bed. 酒店为每张床都提供了一条干净的白色床罩。

近义词: quilt, comforter, bedspread, duvet
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
