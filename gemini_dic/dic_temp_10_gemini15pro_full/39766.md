consolidation:/kənˌsɒlɪˈdeɪʃən/ /kənˌsɑːlɪˈdeɪʃən/| n.|巩固，加强，合并|The company is undergoing a period of consolidation.  这家公司正在经历一个整合期。|例句：The consolidation of power in the hands of a few people is a dangerous trend.  权力集中在少数人手中是一个危险的趋势。

例句：The government's consolidation of several departments saved taxpayers millions of dollars. 政府合并几个部门为纳税人节省了数百万美元。


近义词：strengthening, reinforcement, merging, combination|反义词: weakening, division, separation|词性:名词
