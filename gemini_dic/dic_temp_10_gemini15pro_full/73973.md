fortissimo:/fɔːrˈtɪsɪmoʊ/ (US) /fɔːˈtɪsɪməʊ/ (UK)| adj. adv. n.|极强音，很响亮地|Extremely loud; as loud as possible.  It's a musical term indicating a very high volume.|The orchestra played the final chord *fortissimo*.  管弦乐队以极强的音量演奏了最后一个和弦。|近义词：loudly, powerfully|反义词：pianissimo, softly|词性: adjective, adverb, noun
Etymology: From Italian *fortissimo*, superlative of *forte* (strong).
