moistly: /ˈmɔɪstli/ | adv. |潮湿地；湿润地 | In a moist manner; slightly wet. | The cake was moistly baked, retaining a delightful texture.  蛋糕被烤得湿润润的，保持着令人愉悦的口感。|  The dew clung moistly to the grass. 露水湿漉漉地粘在草上。| 近义词: damply, humidly | 反义词: dryly, aridly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From moist + -ly.  "Moist" comes from Middle English *moiste, from Old French moiste, ultimately from Latin mucidus "moldy, musty."  The suffix "-ly" forms adverbs from adjectives.
