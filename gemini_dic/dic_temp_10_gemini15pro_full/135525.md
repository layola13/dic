nonpleading:/ˌnɒnˈpliːdɪŋ/
adjective
不抗辩的，未作答辩的
A nonpleading defendant is one who has not filed a plea in response to a lawsuit.
The nonpleading defendant was found liable by default.  未作答辩的被告被缺席判决败诉。
The court entered a default judgment against the nonpleading party. 法院对未作答辩的当事人作出了缺席判决。
Synonyms: unresponsive, defaulting
Antonyms: pleading, responsive
词性: 形容词
