nontrials:/ˌnɒnˈtraɪəlz/
noun
非试验，非审判，非测试
Cases resolved outside of a formal trial setting, such as through settlements or plea bargains.
Many legal disputes are resolved through nontrials, saving time and resources. 许多法律纠纷都通过非庭审方式解决，节省了时间和资源。
synonyms: settlements, resolutions, out-of-court settlements
antonyms: trials, litigations, court proceedings
n.
