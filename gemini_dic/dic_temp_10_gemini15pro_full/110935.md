lippier:/ˈlɪpiər/| adj.|更爱顶嘴的，更爱抬杠的|Comparative form of lippy: more lippy.  Meaning having or showing a tendency to be impertinent or disrespectful, especially in a cheeky or insolent way.|She's gotten a lot lippier since she started high school.  自从她上了高中后，变得更加爱顶嘴了。|Example: He was much lippier than usual this morning. 他今天早上比平时更爱抬杠。|Synonyms: cheeky, impudent, insolent, sassy, disrespectful|Antonyms: respectful, polite, obedient, submissive|adjective
