hyperparoxysms:/ˌhaɪpərˈpærəkˌsɪzəmz/| n. |阵发性加剧; 超阵发|A sudden intensification of paroxysms (sudden attacks or recurrences of a disease).  A spasm or convulsion of extreme violence.|The patient experienced hyperparoxysms of coughing fits throughout the night.|近义词：spasms, convulsions, fits, exacerbations|反义词:remission, abatement|noun
