croquet:/kroʊˈkeɪ/ (US), /ˈkrəʊkeɪ/ (UK)| n. |槌球游戏；槌球运动|A lawn game in which players hit wooden or plastic balls through small hoops (wickets) using mallets. |We played croquet on the lawn all afternoon. 我们整个下午都在草坪上打槌球。|例句：The croquet set was left out in the rain.  槌球用具被落在雨里了。|近义词：ground billiards|反义词: N/A |noun
