miserable: /ˈmɪzərəbl/ (US) /ˈmɪzrəbl/ (UK)| adj.| 悲惨的，痛苦的，可怜的；令人难受的；糟糕的| Extremely unhappy or uncomfortable; causing unhappiness or discomfort|  I felt miserable after failing the exam.  我考试不及格后感到很痛苦。

The weather was miserable - cold, wet, and windy. 天气很糟糕——又冷又湿又刮风。


近义词: unhappy, wretched, sorrowful, pathetic, awful, terrible

反义词: happy, joyful, cheerful, pleasant, wonderful

词性: adjective

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French miserable, from late Latin miserabilis, from miserari ‘pity’, from miser ‘wretched’.
