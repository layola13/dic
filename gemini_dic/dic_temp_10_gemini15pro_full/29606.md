categories:/ˈkætəɡɔːriz/ | n. |类别，种类|Classes or divisions of people or things sharing common characteristics. |We offer a wide range of products in different categories. 我们提供各种不同类别的产品。| synonyms: classes, types, sorts, kinds, groups, divisions, classifications | antonyms: individuals, specifics, particulars | noun

categories:/ˈkætəɡəraɪz/ | v. |分类，归类|To place in a category or categories; classify. |It's difficult to categorize this particular artist.  很难将这位艺术家归类。| synonyms: classify, group, sort, type, pigeonhole, label, compartmentalize | antonyms: individualize, separate, distinguish | verb
