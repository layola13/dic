nonheinousness:/ˌnɑːnˈheɪnəsnəs/
n.
无罪恶；非暴行
the quality or state of not being heinous.
The defense argued for the nonheinousness of the crime, citing the defendant's mental state.  辩方以被告的精神状态为由，辩称该罪行并非十恶不赦。
近义词：innocence, harmlessness, inoffensiveness
反义词：heinousness, atrocity, wickedness
词性：名词
