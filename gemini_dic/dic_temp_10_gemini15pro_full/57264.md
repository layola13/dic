downpipe:/ˈdaʊnpaɪp/| n.|落水管；排气管|A pipe that carries rainwater down from a roof or exhaust gases down from an engine.|Rainwater gurgled down the downpipe. 雨水沿着落水管汩汩流下。|The downpipe on my car needs replacing. 我车上的排气管需要更换。|Synonym: drainpipe, exhaust pipe|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "uptake pipe" in specific contexts)|noun
