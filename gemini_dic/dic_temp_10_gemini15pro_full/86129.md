heartening:/ˈhɑːrtnɪŋ/ | adj. |鼓舞人心的；令人振奋的 | encouraging, cheering, giving someone confidence or hope. | The news of the successful surgery was heartening.  手术成功的消息令人鼓舞人心。| 近义词: encouraging, uplifting, inspiring, cheering | 反义词: disheartening, discouraging, depressing | 词性: adjective

词源:  From hearten + -ing.  From heart + -en.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hartô.
