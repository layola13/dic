outweigh:/ˌaʊtˈweɪ/
verb
胜过，超过
to be greater or more important than something else
The benefits of this treatment far outweigh any risks. 这种治疗的好处远远大于任何风险。
synonyms:exceed, surpass, override, prevail over
antonyms: be outweighed by, lose to
词性:动词
