embattle:/ɪmˈbæt(ə)l/ | vt.|使备战，使战斗，投入战斗|to prepare for battle, especially by arming or fortifying|The king embattled his troops along the border. 国王沿边界部署军队备战。|The city was embattled against the invaders. 这座城市与侵略者展开了战斗。|近义词：arm, fortify, prepare, equip|反义词：disarm, demilitarize|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From en- + battle, from Old French bataillier "to fight," from bataille "battle," from Late Latin battualia "military exercise, fencing," from battuere "to beat, strike."
