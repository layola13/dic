absurdest: /əbˈsɜːrdɪst/ /əbˈsɜːrdəst/
adjective
最荒谬的
most absurd; most unreasonable, illogical, or inappropriate.
That's the absurdest thing I've ever heard! = 那是我听过的最荒谬的事情！
His suggestion was the absurdest of all. = 他的建议是最荒谬的。
近义词: ridiculous, preposterous, ludicrous, nonsensical, inane
反义词: sensible, rational, logical, reasonable
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French absurde, from Latin absurdus (“out of tune, discordant, incongruous, foolish”), from ab- (“away from”) + surdus (“deaf, dull, mute”).  The superlative form "absurdest" is created by adding "-est" to "absurd."
