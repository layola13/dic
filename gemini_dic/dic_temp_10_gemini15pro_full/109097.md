legendary:/ˈledʒənderi/ | adj. | 传奇的；传说的；著名的 |  Celebrated or described in legend.  |  例句：The legendary musician Jimi Hendrix. 传奇音乐家吉米·亨德里克斯。 | 近义词：fabled, mythical, famous, renowned | 反义词：unknown, obscure, ordinary | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French legendaire, from late Latin legendarius ‘to be read’, from Latin legenda (see legend).
