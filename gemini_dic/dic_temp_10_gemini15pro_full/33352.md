chumming:/ˈtʃʌmɪŋ/ | adj. | 友好的，亲密的 | Characterized by friendly intimacy.  (This usage is less common and usually refers to the act of chumming as a shared activity that fosters closeness.)| They spent the afternoon chumming around, reminiscing about old times.  他们一下午都亲密地在一起，回忆往事。 | 近义词：friendly, close, intimate | 反义词: unfriendly, distant |词性: 形容词 (adjective - less common usage)
