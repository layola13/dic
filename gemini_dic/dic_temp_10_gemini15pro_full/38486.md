conchologist:/kɒŋˈkɒlədʒɪst/ /kɑːnˈkɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |贝类学家|A person who studies shells, especially mollusks' shells.|例句：The conchologist identified the rare shell as a species from the Pacific.  这位贝类学家将这个稀有贝壳鉴定为来自太平洋的物种。

例句：My grandfather, a keen conchologist, has a vast collection of shells from around the world. 我祖父是一位热衷于贝壳学的专家，收藏了来自世界各地的各种贝壳。|近义词：malacologist|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek κόγχη (kónkhē, “shellfish, mussel”) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”).
