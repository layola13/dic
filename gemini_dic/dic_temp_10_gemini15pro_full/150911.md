parallelepipedic:/ˌpærəˌlɛlɪˈpɪpɪdɪk/ | adj. | 平行六面体的 |  Having the form of a parallelepiped; relating to or shaped like a parallelepiped.  A parallelepiped is a three-dimensional figure formed by six parallelograms (where opposite sides are parallel and equal in length). | The crystals were parallelepipedic in shape. 这些晶体呈平行六面体形状。|  The box was remarkably parallelepipedic.  这个盒子非常像一个平行六面体。 |  cuboid, rectangular, prismatic | N/A | adjective
