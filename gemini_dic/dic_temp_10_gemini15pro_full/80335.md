goldfinch:/ˈɡoʊldˌfɪntʃ/| n. |金翅雀|A small, brightly colored finch with yellow markings on its wings and tail. |例句：I saw a goldfinch perched on the sunflower. 我看到一只金翅雀栖息在向日葵上。

例句：The goldfinch's song is a cheerful melody. 金翅雀的歌声是一曲欢快的旋律。

近义词：bird, finch|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
