desiccation:/ˌdesɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |干燥，脱水|the state of extreme dryness, or the process of extreme drying|The desiccation of the landscape was evident after months without rain.  几个月没有下雨后，地貌的干燥显而易见。|Synonyms: dehydration, drying, parching|Antonyms: hydration, moistening|noun

desiccate:/ˈdesɪkeɪt/ | v. |使干燥，使脱水|to remove the moisture from something, typically in order to preserve it|The hot sun desiccates the exposed earth.  炎热的太阳使裸露的土地干燥。|Synonyms: dry, dehydrate, parch|Antonyms: hydrate, moisten|verb
