localizable:/ˈloʊkəˌlaɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可本地化的，可定位的 | Able to be adapted to local conditions or languages.  This often refers to software or content that can be translated and adapted for different regions or markets. |  The software is localizable into multiple languages.  这个软件可以本地化成多种语言。 | The app developer ensured the app was localizable to reach a wider audience.  应用程序开发者确保该应用程序可本地化，以覆盖更广泛的受众。 |  translatable, adaptable | non-localizable, untranslatable | adjective
