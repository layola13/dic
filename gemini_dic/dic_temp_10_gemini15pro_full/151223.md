pared:/pɛrd/ | adj. |削皮的；削减的；减少的 |Reduced or diminished by or as if by paring. | The company pared its workforce down to a skeleton crew.  公司将员工削减到只剩骨干人员。| The budget has been pared to the bone. 预算已被削减到最低限度。 |近义词:trimmed, reduced, diminished, curtailed |反义词: augmented, increased, expanded |词性:形容词

Etymology: From the verb "pare," from Old French "parer" (to prepare, trim, adorn), from Latin "parare" (to prepare, make ready).
