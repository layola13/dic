logicalness:/ˈlɒdʒɪklnəs/ /ˈlɑːdʒɪklnəs/| n. |逻辑性，合理性|The logicalness of his argument was undeniable. 他的论证的逻辑性是不可否认的。|例句：The logicalness of her deduction impressed the jury. 她的推论的逻辑性给陪审团留下了深刻的印象。  The scientist questioned the logicalness of the theory.  这位科学家质疑该理论的逻辑性。|近义词：reasonableness, rationality, validity, soundness|反义词:illogicality, absurdity, irrationality|词性:名词
Etymology: From logical + -ness.  Logical comes from the Late Latin logicus, from Greek logikos "of or pertaining to speech or reason," from logos "reason, word, speech."  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives, meaning "the quality or state of being."
