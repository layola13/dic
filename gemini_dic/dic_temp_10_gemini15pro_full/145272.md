overbookish: /ˌoʊvərˈbʊkɪʃ/
adjective
书呆子气的, 过于学究的
Too devoted to books or reading; excessively scholarly or pedantic.
He had an overbookish style, using obscure words and complex sentences. 他有一种过于学究的风格，使用晦涩的词语和复杂的句子。
近义词: pedantic, bookish, scholarly, academic, intellectual
反义词: unintellectual, simple, straightforward, practical
