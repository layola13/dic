adventuresome: /ədˈvɛntʃərˌsəm/ /ədˈvɛntʃərˌsʌm/
adjective
爱冒险的；大胆的
Willing to take risks or try new things; adventurous.
She has always been an adventuresome traveler, eager to explore unfamiliar territories. 她一直是一个爱冒险的旅行者，渴望探索陌生的领域。

He's an adventuresome cook, always trying out new recipes and ingredients. 他是一位爱冒险的厨师，总是尝试新的食谱和食材。

近义词: adventurous, daring, bold, intrepid, venturesome
反义词: cautious, timid, fearful, apprehensive, risk-averse
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From adventure + -some.  The suffix "-some" forms adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "given to."  Therefore, "adventuresome" literally means "characterized by adventure."
