nals: /nɑːlz/ | n. (plural) | 记录，纪要；(通常指)会议记录 |  A record of events or business transacted, especially a formal record of the proceedings of a meeting. | The secretary took detailed nals of the meeting. 秘书详细记录了会议的纪要。|  minutes, records, proceedings | reports, accounts| noun


Etymology:
From Late Middle English *nales*, plural of *nale*, from Old French *nal*, *nail*, a notch cut in a tally stick, from Old Norse *nǫl* ‘needle’. The sense of a record comes from the practice of keeping accounts by cutting notches on a tally stick.
