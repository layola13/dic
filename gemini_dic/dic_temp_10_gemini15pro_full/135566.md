nonpoliticization:/ˌnɒnpəˌlɪtɪsaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.|非政治化|The act or process of making something nonpolitical, or the state of being nonpolitical.  The removal of something from the realm of political influence or controversy. |例句：The nonpoliticization of humanitarian aid is crucial. 人道主义援助的非政治化至关重要。

例句：They called for the nonpoliticization of the judicial system. 他们呼吁司法系统非政治化。|近义词：depoliticization, neutrality, objectivity|反义词：politicization, partisanship|词性:名词
