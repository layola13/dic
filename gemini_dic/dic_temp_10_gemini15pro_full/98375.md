insobriety:/ˌɪnsəˈbraɪəti/
n.
lack of sobriety; drunkenness
The state of being drunk; intoxication.
His insobriety led to a car accident. 他的醉酒导致了一场车祸。
synonyms: drunkenness, intoxication, inebriation, tipsiness
antonyms: sobriety, temperance, abstinence
