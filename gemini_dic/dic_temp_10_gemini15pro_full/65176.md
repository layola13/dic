ethnolinguists: /ˌɛθnoʊˈlɪŋɡwɪsts/ | n. | 民族语言学家 |  Experts who study the relationship between language and culture. | Ethnolinguists often conduct fieldwork in diverse communities. 民族语言学家经常在不同的社区进行实地考察。|  Ethnolinguists analyze how language reflects and shapes cultural values. 民族语言学家分析语言如何反映和塑造文化价值观。 | synonyms: anthropological linguists, sociolinguists | antonyms: N/A | noun
