mollifier:/ˈmɒlɪfaɪər/ (US) /ˈmɑːlɪfaɪər/ (UK)| n.|缓和剂；调解人；润滑剂| something that softens or soothes; a person who appeases or conciliates| The negotiator acted as a mollifier between the two warring factions.  谈判代表在两个交战派别之间充当了调解人。|Synonyms: softener, mitigator, appeaser, mediator|Antonyms: irritant, aggravator|noun

mollify:/ˈmɒlɪfaɪ/ (US) /ˈmɑːlɪfaɪ/ (UK)| v.|缓和；平息；抚慰| appease the anger or anxiety of (someone)|  A warm bath mollified her tense muscles.  热水澡舒缓了她紧张的肌肉。|Synonyms: appease, soothe, pacify, placate|Antonyms: aggravate, provoke, irritate|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin mollificāre, from Latin mollis (“soft”) + -ficāre (“-fy”).
