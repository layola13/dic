inbreeding's:/ˈɪnbrɪːdɪŋz/ | n. |近亲繁殖的（后果）| The negative effects resulting from inbreeding.  It refers to the deteriorated traits or consequences caused by the breeding of closely related individuals. | The inbreeding's impact on the royal family's health was a topic of much discussion. 近亲繁殖对皇室家族健康的影响是一个被广泛讨论的话题。

The cheetah population suffers from the inbreeding's detrimental effects on genetic diversity.  猎豹种群遭受近亲繁殖对遗传多样性的不利影响。 | 近义词: consanguinity's effects, inbreeding depression | 反义词: outbreeding's effects, hybrid vigor | 词性: 名词 (Noun), specifically a possessive form indicating the consequences or effects of inbreeding.

Etymology: From "inbreeding" + "-'s" (possessive suffix). "Inbreeding" is formed from "in-" (meaning "within") + "breeding" (the act of producing offspring).  "Breed" comes from Old English "brēdan," meaning "to cherish, nourish, keep warm."
