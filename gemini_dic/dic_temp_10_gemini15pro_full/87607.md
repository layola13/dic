heterotropous:/ˌhɛtərɒˈtrɒpəs/| adj.|异养的，倒生的|An ovule is heterotropous when the micropyle is close to the funiculus.|The heterotropous ovule is characteristic of most flowering plants.|近义词：anatropous (similar in orientation, but technically different)|反义词：orthotropous, atropous|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *heteros* (ἕτερος) "other, different" + *tropos* (τρόπος) "turn, direction, way".
