noncurdling:/ˌnɑːnˈkɜːrdlɪŋ/
adjective
不凝结的
Not curdling; remaining smooth and homogenous, especially of milk or cream when heated or treated with acid.
The recipe called for a noncurdling milk substitute for those who are lactose intolerant.  这食谱要求对乳糖不耐受的人使用不凝结的牛奶替代品。
The special additive ensured a noncurdling sauce, even after hours on the stove.  这种特殊添加剂确保了酱汁即使在炉子上放几个小时也不会凝结。
Near Antonyms: curdling, coagulating
Antonyms: thickening (in a different way)
