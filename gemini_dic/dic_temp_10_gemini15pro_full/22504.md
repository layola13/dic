boomed:/buːmd/
verb
发出隆隆声；快速发展；(生意)兴隆；(声音)洪亮地发出
to make a deep, resonant sound; to experience a period of rapid growth; to prosper; to say something in a loud, resonant voice
The cannons boomed across the battlefield.  大炮在战场上隆隆作响。

The business boomed during the holiday season. 假日期间生意兴隆。

His voice boomed across the hall. 他的声音在整个大厅回荡。

近义词:resounded, thundered, flourished, thrived, prospered
反义词:whispered, declined, failed, struggled

词源:From Middle English *boumen, from Old Norse bauma (“to resound”)

词性:v
