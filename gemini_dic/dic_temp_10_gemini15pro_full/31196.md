changes:/ˈtʃeɪndʒɪz/
n.|变化，改变|Alterations or modifications in a thing or situation. |The changes in the weather have been dramatic this year. 今年的天气变化很大。|synonyms: alterations, modifications, variations, transformations|antonyms: constants, consistencies|noun


change:/tʃeɪndʒ/
v.|改变，变化|Make or become different. |He changed his mind about going to the party. 他改变了去参加派对的主意。|synonyms: alter, modify, vary, transform|antonyms: maintain, preserve|verb


change:/tʃeɪndʒ/
n.|改变，变化|An act or process through which something becomes different. |A change of scenery would do you good.  换个环境对你有好处。|synonyms: alteration, modification, variation, transformation|antonyms: constancy, consistency|noun


Etymology: From Middle English *chaungen*, from Old French *changier*, from Late Latin *cambiare*, from Latin *cambire* ("to barter, exchange"), of uncertain origin.
