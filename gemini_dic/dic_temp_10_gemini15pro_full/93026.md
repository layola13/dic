idiocy:/ˈɪdiəsi/ | n. | 愚蠢，白痴 | extreme stupidity or foolishness | His decision was pure idiocy. 他的决定纯粹是愚蠢。| synonyms: stupidity, foolishness, folly, absurdity | antonyms: wisdom, intelligence, brilliance | noun

idiotic:/ˌɪdiˈɒtɪk/ | adj. | 愚蠢的，白痴的 | extremely stupid or foolish | That's an idiotic idea! 那是个愚蠢的主意！| synonyms: stupid, foolish, silly, absurd | antonyms: wise, intelligent, sensible | adjective

idiotry:/ˈɪdiətri/ | n. | 愚蠢行为，极端愚蠢 | extremely foolish or stupid behaviour |  The government's handling of the crisis was sheer idiotry. 政府处理这场危机的方式完全是愚蠢至极。| synonyms: stupidity, foolishness, folly, absurdity | antonyms: wisdom, intelligence, brilliance | noun
