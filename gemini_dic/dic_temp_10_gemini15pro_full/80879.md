governablenesses:/ˌɡʌvərˈnəbəlnəsəz/
n.
可治理性（复数）
The quality or state of being governable.
The governablenesses of the newly formed nations are yet to be seen.  这些新成立国家的可治理性还有待观察。
The report analyzed the governablenesses of different regions. 报告分析了不同地区的可治理性。
Synonym: manageability, controllability, tractability
Antonym: ungovernability, unruliness, intractability
