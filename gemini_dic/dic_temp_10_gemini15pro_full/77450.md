gawped:/ɡɔːpt/ | v. | 呆呆地看，瞠目结舌 | to stare at something or someone with your mouth open, especially because you are surprised or shocked |  He just gawped at me when I told him the news. 他听到这个消息后只是呆呆地看着我。 |  The tourists gawped at the magnificent view. 游客们目瞪口呆地欣赏着壮丽的景色。 | stare, gape, gaze | glance | verb
