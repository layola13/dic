一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think about this? 你觉得这个怎么样？
What does she usually eat for breakfast? 她早餐通常吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? 你在看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? 她用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? 你这整个星期都在忙什么？
What has he been thinking about lately? 他最近一直在想什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had happened before the police arrived? 警察到达之前发生了什么事？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you?  在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么长时间？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后会做什么？
What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting tomorrow? 明天会议上他们会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将会取得什么成就？
What will have changed by the time we get back? 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
