madrigalesque: /ˌmædrɪɡəˈlɛsk/ | adj. | 田园诗般的，牧歌式的，类似小夜曲的 | Suggestive of or resembling a madrigal, especially in being light, elegant, or intricate in style.  |  Her music has a madrigalesque quality, light and airy. 她的音乐有一种小夜曲般的特质，轻盈而飘逸。 | The intricate melody had a madrigalesque charm.  这首复杂的旋律有一种小夜曲般的魅力。| 近义词: lyrical, melodic, pastoral, idyllic | 反义词: cacophonous, discordant | 词性: adjective

词源: From Italian *madrigale* (madrigal) + *-esque* (in the style of).
