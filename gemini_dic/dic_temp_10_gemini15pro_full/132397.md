nondemonstrative:/ˌnɒndɪˈmɒnstrətɪv/
adjective
不爱表达感情的；内向的
Not given to or marked by outward display of emotion; reserved.
He's a quiet, nondemonstrative man. 他是一个安静、不爱表达感情的男人。
近义词:reserved, undemonstrative, unemotional, inhibited
反义词:demonstrative, affectionate, expressive, emotional
词性:adjective
