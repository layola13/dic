backbite:/ˈbækbaɪt/ | v. | 诽谤，背后说坏话 | To speak spitefully and falsely about someone who is not present. | He was known to backbite his colleagues to gain favor with the boss. 他为了讨好老板而对同事说三道四。|  She backbites about everyone she knows. 她对她认识的每个人都在背后说坏话。| 近义词: slander, defame, malign, gossip | 反义词: praise, commend, laud | 词性: 动词

backbiting: /ˈbækbaɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 诽谤，背后说坏话 | The act of speaking spitefully and falsely about someone who is not present. |  The backbiting in the office created a toxic atmosphere. 办公室里的诽谤制造了有毒的气氛。| Their constant backbiting damaged their friendship. 他们不断的诽谤损害了他们的友谊。| 近义词: slander, defamation, maligning, gossip | 反义词: praise, commendation | 词性: 名词


词源:
From Middle English *bakbiten*, from *bak* (“back”) + *biten* (“to bite”).  Literally, "to bite in the back."
