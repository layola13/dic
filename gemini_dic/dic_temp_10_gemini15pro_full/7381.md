animalism:/ˈænɪməlɪzəm/
n.
兽性; 动物性; 粗野
The doctrine that humans are merely animals with no spiritual dimension.  The belief that humans are motivated purely by animal instincts.
The novel explored the dangers of unchecked animalism. 这部小说探讨了不受控制的兽性的危险。
He displayed a raw animalism in his pursuit of power. 他在追求权力时表现出赤裸裸的兽性。
brutishness, bestiality, savagery
humanity, spirituality, civility
noun
