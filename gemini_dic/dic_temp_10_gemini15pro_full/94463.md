impoliticly:/ˌɪmpəˈlɪtɪkli/
| adv. |不策略地，不明智地，失策地|In an impolitic manner; unwisely; without prudence or tact.  This means acting in a way that is likely to cause offense or harm, especially to one's own interests.|He impoliticly criticized his boss in front of the entire team. 他在整个团队面前不策略地批评了他的老板。|Synonyms: unwisely, imprudently, indiscreetly, tactlessly|Antonyms: wisely, prudently, discreetly, tactfully
|词性:副词
