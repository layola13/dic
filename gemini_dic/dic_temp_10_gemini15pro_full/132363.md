nondeliberations:/ˌnɒndɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃənz/
noun
非审议；未审议事项
Absence of deliberation; matters not deliberated upon.
The minutes reflected the nondeliberations of the committee, simply listing topics that were tabled.  会议记录反映了委员会的非审议事项，仅仅列出了被搁置的议题。
The period of nondeliberations lasted for several months, causing frustration among stakeholders.  非审议期持续了几个月，引起了利益相关者的不满。
近义词:inaction, standstill, inactivity
反义词:deliberations, discussions, consultations
词性:noun (plural)

Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + deliberations (plural of deliberation, from Late Middle English deliberacioun, from Old French deliberacion, from Latin dēlīberātiō, from dēlīberāre “to consider, weigh,” from de- "from, out of" + lībra "balance, scales.")
