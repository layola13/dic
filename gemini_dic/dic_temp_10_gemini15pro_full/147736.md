overprocesses:/ˌəʊvəˈprəʊsɪsɪz/ | v. | 过度处理，过度加工 | To subject to excessive processing or analysis, often to the point of inefficiency or counterproductivity. |  The data was overprocessed, leading to a loss of valuable information. 数据过度处理，导致丢失了有价值的信息。 |  The company overprocesses its new employees with unnecessary paperwork and training. 这家公司对新员工进行了不必要的文书工作和培训，使其过度劳累。| overanalyze, overdo | underprocess | verb
