aphetism:/ˈæfɪtɪzəm/ | n. | 禁忌语; 讳言 | The use of a substitute word or phrase for one considered offensive or taboo.  It's a form of euphemism specifically related to avoiding direct mention of sacred names or things. |  The tribe used an aphetism when speaking of their deity, fearing to offend by using the true name.  这个部落在谈到他们的神时使用了讳言，害怕使用真名会冒犯神灵。| synonym: euphemism, circumlocution, periphrasis | antonym:  blasphemy, profanity | noun
