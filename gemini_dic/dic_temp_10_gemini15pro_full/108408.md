lawman:/ˈlɔːmən/| n. |执法人员，警官|A law enforcement officer, especially a sheriff or marshal in the American West. |The old Western movies often portray the lawman as a stoic hero.  那些老西部片经常把执法人员描绘成坚忍不拔的英雄。

The lawman apprehended the outlaw after a lengthy chase.  经过长时间的追捕，执法人员逮捕了歹徒。|近义词：police officer, peace officer, sheriff, marshal, constable|反义词：outlaw, criminal, lawbreaker|词性:noun

Etymology: From Old English *lagu-mann "man of law,"  composed of lagu "law" (from Proto-Germanic *lagu-, from Proto-Indo-European *legh- "to lay, lie, put") + mann "man" (from Proto-Germanic *mann-).
