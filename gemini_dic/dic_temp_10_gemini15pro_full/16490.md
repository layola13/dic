bargee:/ˈbɑːrdʒiː/
noun
驳船夫；驳船工人
A person employed to work on or operate a barge.
The bargee steered the barge skillfully through the narrow canal.  驳船夫熟练地驾驶驳船穿过狭窄的运河。
Synonyms: bargeman, boatman, lighterman
Antonyms: landsman
