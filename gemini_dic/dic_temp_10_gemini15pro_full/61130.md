elongate:/iˈlɒŋɡeɪt/ | v. |  伸长，延长 | To make something longer. |  He elongated the rope by tying another piece to it. 他通过系上另一根绳子来延长绳子。 | She elongated the vowels in her speech. 她拉长了她讲话中的元音。 | synonyms: lengthen, extend, stretch, protract | antonyms: shorten, contract, compress | verb

词源：From Late Latin elongatus, past participle of elongare "to lengthen," from Latin e- "ex-," here probably meaning "out" + longus "long."
