oversimplicity:/ˌōvərˌsɪmˈplɪsɪti/ | n. | 过度简化，过于简单化 |  The oversimplicity of the explanation made it misleading.  |  例句：The oversimplicity of his argument made it unconvincing. 他的论点过于简化，因此没有说服力。

例句：Reducing the complex issue to a simple dichotomy is an oversimplicity. 把这个复杂的问题简化成简单的二分法是一种过度简化。 | 近义词：simplification, reductionism | 反义词：complexity, nuance, sophistication | 词性: 名词

oversimplify:/ˌōvərˈsɪmplɪˌfaɪ/ | vt. | 过度简化，过于简单化 | To represent something in a way that makes it seem less complex than it really is. | 例句：The media often oversimplifies complex political issues. 媒体经常过度简化复杂的政治问题。

例句：Oversimplifying the history of the conflict can lead to misunderstandings. 过度简化冲突的历史会导致误解。 | 近义词：simplify excessively, reduce too much | 反义词：complicate, elaborate, nuance | 词性: 动词

oversimplified:/ˌōvərˈsɪmplɪˌfaɪd/ | adj. | 过度简化的，过于简单化的 |  Characterized by excessive simplification. |  例句：The oversimplified explanation failed to address the key nuances of the issue.  这个过于简单的解释未能触及问题的关键细微之处。

例句：His oversimplified view of the economy ignored important factors. 他对经济的过度简化的看法忽略了重要因素。 | 近义词：excessively simplified, reductive | 反义词：complex, nuanced, sophisticated | 词性: 形容词
