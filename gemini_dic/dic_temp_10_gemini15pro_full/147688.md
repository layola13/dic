overpractice: /ˌoʊvərˈpræktɪs/ | v. | 过度练习 | To practice something excessively, often to the point of diminishing returns or causing harm. |  He overpracticed the piano piece and ended up making more mistakes. 他过度练习了钢琴曲，结果反而错误更多了。

He overpracticed his tennis serve and injured his shoulder. 他过度练习了网球发球，伤到了肩膀。| 近义词: overdo, overtrain | 反义词: underpractice | 词性: 动词
