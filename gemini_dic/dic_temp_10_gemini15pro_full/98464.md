inspirit:/ɪnˈspɪrɪt/
verb
鼓舞，激励
to fill (someone) with courage, spirit, or enthusiasm
例句：
Her speech inspirited the crowd. 她的演讲鼓舞了人群。

The coach's words inspirited the team to victory. 教练的话激励了球队走向胜利。

近义词：encourage, motivate, stimulate, invigorate, animate, hearten, uplift, inspire
反义词：discourage, dispirit, demoralize, deject, depress
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inspirare, from in- (“in”) + spirare (“to breathe”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *sp(e)i- (“to prosper, thrive”).
