hydantoin:/haɪˈdæntoʊɪn/ |n.|乙内酰脲|A crystalline heterocyclic organic compound with the formula C3H4N2O2. It is an oxidized derivative of imidazolidine. Hydantoins are used in pharmaceuticals and agriculture.|Hydantoins are important intermediates in the synthesis of various amino acids.|近义词:glycolylurea|反义词: (No direct antonym exists for a specific chemical compound like hydantoin.)|词性:noun
