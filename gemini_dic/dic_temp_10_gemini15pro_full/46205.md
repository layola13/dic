cyrillic: /sɪˈrɪlɪk/ | adj. n.|西里尔字母的，西里尔字母|Relating to the Cyrillic alphabet, a writing system used for various languages, especially Slavic languages like Russian and Bulgarian.  Also, the Cyrillic alphabet itself. |The Cyrillic alphabet is used to write Russian.  西里尔字母用于书写俄语。| The inscription was in Cyrillic. 碑文是用西里尔字母写的。|近义词: Slavic (when referring to the languages that use it) |反义词: Latin, Roman (referring to alphabets) |词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: Named after Saint Cyril (/ˈsɪrəl/), a 9th-century Byzantine monk who, along with his brother Methodius, created the Glagolitic alphabet, from which Cyrillic was later derived.
