over-react:/ˌoʊvərriˈækt/ | vi. |反应过度；过分反应|To respond more emotionally or intensely than is warranted.|She overreacted to his innocent comment and started yelling. 她对他的无心之言反应过度，开始大喊大叫。|例句：He tends to overreact to criticism. 他容易对批评反应过度。|近义词：overdo, exaggerate|反义词：underreact|词性:动词
