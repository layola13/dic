multipotent:/ˌmʌlˈtiˈpəʊtənt/
adjective
多能的; 多潜能的
Having the ability to develop into more than one cell type, but not all cell types.
例句：Multipotent stem cells can differentiate into various types of blood cells. 多能干细胞可以分化成各种类型的血细胞。

例句：Bone marrow contains multipotent stem cells. 骨髓中包含多能干细胞。

近义词：pluripotent, multipotential
反义词：unipotent, totipotent
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From multi- (many) + potent (powerful). "Multi-" comes from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many".  "Potent" comes from Latin "potens" meaning "powerful, able".
