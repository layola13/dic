egyptologists:/ˌiːdʒɪpˈtɒlədʒɪsts/
n.
埃及学家
A person who studies ancient Egyptian history, language, and culture.
Egyptologists are still uncovering new tombs in the Valley of the Kings. 埃及学家们仍在帝王谷中发现新的陵墓。
Egyptologists have deciphered many of the hieroglyphs found in ancient tombs. 埃及学家们已经破译了许多在古墓中发现的象形文字。
synonyms: archaeologist specializing in Egypt, scholar of ancient Egypt
antonyms: (No direct antonym, but possibly) amateur historian, layman
