hesitant:/ˈhezɪtənt/| adj.|迟疑的，犹豫的|Unsure or slow in acting or speaking, often due to reluctance or uncertainty.|She was hesitant to answer the question. 她犹豫要不要回答这个问题。|近义词:uncertain, reluctant, dubious, wavering|反义词:decisive, confident, resolute|词性:形容词
hesitancy:/ˈhezɪtənsi/| n.|犹豫，迟疑|The state of being hesitant; reluctance or uncertainty.|Her hesitancy to commit to the project was understandable. 她对这个项目犹豫不决是可以理解的。|近义词:indecision, reluctance, doubt|反义词:decisiveness, confidence, resolution|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *haesitant-*, present participle of *haesitare* "to stick fast, hesitate," frequentative of *haerere* "to stick, adhere, cling."
