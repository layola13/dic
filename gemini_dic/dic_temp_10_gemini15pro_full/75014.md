PHRASE->
Not applicable.  "Friendlily" is an adverb and doesn't typically form set phrases.  It modifies verbs, describing *how* an action is performed.  While you can construct sentences like "She friendlily offered help," there are no common, fixed expressions using "friendlily" the way there are with some other words.
