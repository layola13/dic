epuration:/ˌɛpjʊˈreɪʃən/ | n. |净化，清除，整肃|The act of purifying or cleansing; specifically, the purging of a political party, government, etc., of undesirable members.|The epuration of the party after the scandal left it significantly weakened.  丑闻发生后，该党进行了整肃，实力大为削弱。|近义词：purge, cleansing, purification|反义词:contamination, pollution|词性:名词

词源: From French *épuration*, from *épurer* (“to purify”), from Latin *depūrāre* (“to cleanse, purify”).
