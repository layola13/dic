establisher:/ɪˈstæblɪʃər/
noun
建立者；创立者
One who establishes something; a founder.
例句：He was the establisher of the company. 他是这家公司的创始人。

例句：She is recognized as the establisher of modern dance techniques. 她被公认为是现代舞蹈技巧的创始人。

近义词：founder, creator, originator, architect
反义词：destroyer, demolisher


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "establish," which comes from the Old French "establir" (meaning "to establish, fix, settle"), derived from the Latin "stabilire" (meaning "to make stable"), from "stabilis" (meaning "stable, steadfast").
