overtempt:/ˌōvərˈtempt/ | vt. | 过度诱惑；诱惑过度 | To tempt someone excessively, beyond what is reasonable or appropriate.  |  He overtempted his employees with exorbitant bonuses, leading to unethical behavior. 他用过高的奖金过度诱惑他的员工，导致了不道德的行为。

|  The delicious smell of the freshly baked cookies overtempted me, and I ate five!  新鲜出炉的饼干的香味让我无法抗拒，我吃了五个！ |  Near synonyms: entice excessively, seduce unduly, lead astray | Near antonyms: dissuade, discourage, deter | Verb
