frightenable:/ˈfraɪtənəbl/| adj.|易受惊的，胆小的|Capable of being frightened.  Able to be made afraid.|The child is very frightenable and doesn't like loud noises. 这个孩子很胆小，不喜欢大声喧哗。|近义词：timid, fearful, apprehensive, skittish|反义词：brave, courageous, fearless|词性:形容词
词源: From "frighten" (to scare) + "-able" (suffix indicating capability).  "Frighten" comes from the Old English "fyrhtan," meaning to frighten.
