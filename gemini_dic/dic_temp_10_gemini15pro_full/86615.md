heliotropism:/ˌhiːliˈɒtrəpɪzəm/ | n. | 向日性 | The tendency of a plant or other organism to turn or grow toward the sun. |  Sunflowers exhibit heliotropism, following the sun's movement across the sky.  向日葵表现出向日性，跟随太阳在天空中移动。

The leaves of the plant showed strong heliotropism, always facing the sunlight.  这种植物的叶子表现出强烈的向日性，总是面向阳光。 | 近义词: phototropism | 反义词: apheliotropism (背日性) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hēlios* (sun) + *trope* (turning) + *-ism* (suffix forming nouns of action, state, or condition).
