everichon:/ˈevərɪtʃən/ | n. |每个人，所有的人 | Everyone means every single person. |例句：Everyone is welcome to join the party. 欢迎大家来参加聚会。

例句：Everyone in the class passed the exam. 班上的每个人都通过了考试。|近义词：everybody, all |反义词: nobody, no one |词性: pronoun

词源：From Middle English *everichone, from Old English *æfre ælc ān (“ever each one”). Equivalent to every +‎ one.
