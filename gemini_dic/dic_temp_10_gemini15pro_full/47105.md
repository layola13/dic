daughterlike: /ˈdɔːtərlaɪk/ | adj. | 像女儿一样的；孝顺的 | Resembling or characteristic of a daughter; showing the qualities of a good daughter, such as affection, obedience, and respect. | She has a daughterlike affection for her teacher. 她对她的老师有一种像女儿一样的爱。|  Her daughterlike concern for him was touching. 她像女儿一样关心他，真是感人。 | 近义词: filial, dutiful, devoted | 反义词: disobedient, rebellious, disrespectful | 词性: 形容词

词源:  daughter + like.  "Daughter" comes from Old English "dohtor," ultimately from Proto-Germanic *dukhter. "Like" comes from Old English "gelic," meaning "similar," from Proto-Germanic *galikaz.
