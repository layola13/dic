anthropogeography:/ˌænθrəpoʊdʒiˈɑːɡrəfi/ | n. |人文地理学|The study of the relationship between human societies and their environment.  It explores how human activities influence the Earth's surface and how environmental factors shape human cultures and societies.|例句：Anthropogeography helps us understand the distribution of languages across the globe. 人文地理学帮助我们理解全球语言的分布。|近义词：human geography|反义词: physical geography|词性:noun
