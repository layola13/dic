beatify:/biˈætɪfaɪ/ /biˈætəˌfaɪ/| vt.|使幸福；使快乐；宣布…列入真福品级；为…增添美感|To make supremely happy.  In the Roman Catholic Church, to declare that a deceased person is among the blessed and therefore entitled to limited public religious veneration.|Mother Teresa was beatified in 2003.  特蕾莎修女于2003年被列入真福品级。|近义词：bless, sanctify, exalt, glorify|反义词：condemn, curse|verb
