crankily: /ˈkræŋkɪli/ | adv. | 暴躁地，坏脾气地 | In a bad-tempered or irritable way. | He answered crankily, clearly not in the mood for conversation. 他暴躁地回答，显然没有心情谈话。| She stomped her foot crankily and refused to move. 她生气地跺了跺脚，拒绝移动。 | 近义词：grumpily, irritably, peevishly | 反义词：cheerfully, pleasantly, good-naturedly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From the adjective "cranky" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Cranky" originates from the earlier "crank," referring to a person with strange or eccentric ideas, later evolving to mean irritable or bad-tempered.
