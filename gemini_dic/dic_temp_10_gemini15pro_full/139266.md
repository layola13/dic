obligingnesses: /əˈblaɪdʒɪŋnəsɪz/
noun

He showed great obligingnesses to his guests, ensuring they had everything they needed.  他對待客人非常殷勤，確保他們所需的一切。
The staff were known for their obligingnesses and willingness to help.  员工以乐于助人、愿意帮忙而闻名。
synonyms: kindness, courtesy, helpfulness, accommodation, amiability
antonyms: rudeness, unkindness, hostility, unhelpfulness

obliging: /əˈblaɪdʒɪŋ/
adjective
乐于助人的；恳切的
Willing to do favors; helpful or accommodating.
He was very obliging and offered to carry my bags. 他非常乐于助人，主动提出帮我拿行李。
The hotel staff were extremely obliging and made our stay very enjoyable. 酒店员工非常殷勤，使我们住得很愉快。
synonyms: helpful, accommodating, kind, courteous, amiable
antonyms: unhelpful, unaccommodating, unkind, rude
词性: 形容词

oblige: /əˈblaɪdʒ/
verb
迫使；施恩惠于
To require or constrain someone to do something; to do a favor for someone.
I felt obliged to help her, as she had helped me in the past. 我觉得有义务帮助她，因为她以前帮过我。
Could you oblige me by closing the window? 你能帮我关一下窗户吗？
synonyms: compel, force, require, favor, accommodate
antonyms: release, free, refuse, hinder
词性: 动词



ETYMOLOGY ->

From Old French *obliger (from Latin obligāre, "to bind, tie up, put under obligation"), from ob- ("towards") + ligāre ("to bind"). The sense of "to do a favor" developed in the 17th century.
