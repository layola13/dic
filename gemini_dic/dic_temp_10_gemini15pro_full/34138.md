claimants:/ˈkleɪmənts/| n.|索赔人，申请人，要求者|A person who makes a claim, especially in a legal context; someone who asserts a right or demand.|例句：The claimants are seeking compensation for their losses. 索赔人正在寻求损失赔偿。

例句：The insurance company is processing the claims of the claimants. 保险公司正在处理索赔人的索赔。|近义词：applicant, petitioner, candidate|反义词：defendant, respondent|词性:noun
