incensed:/ɪnˈsenst/| adj.|激怒的，愤怒的| feeling or showing extreme anger|例句：He was incensed by the accusations of corruption. 他被腐败的指控激怒了。

例句：The incensed customer demanded a refund.  愤怒的顾客要求退款。

近义词：enraged, furious, indignant, outraged, livid|反义词:calm, pleased, peaceful|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *ensensen*, from Anglo-Norman *encenser*, from Late Latin *incensāre*, present active infinitive of *incendō* (“to set on fire, kindle, inflame”), from *in-* + *candō* (“to glow, shine”).  Doublet of *incense* (verb).
