ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *lēthargicus, from Ancient Greek λήθαργος (lēthargos, “forgetfulness, drowsiness”), from λήθη (lēthē, “forgetfulness, oblivion”) + ἀργός (argos, “idle, inactive”).  Doublet of litargio.
