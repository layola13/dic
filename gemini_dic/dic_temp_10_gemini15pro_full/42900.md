cousinhood:/ˈkʌzənˌhʊd/| n.|堂/表兄弟姐妹关系；亲族关系|The state or relationship of being cousins. A group of cousins.|例句：There's a strong sense of cousinhood among the members of the extended family.  这个大家庭的成员之间有着强烈的亲族关系。

例句：Growing up, we had a large and lively cousinhood.  从小到大，我们有一大群活泼的堂/表兄弟姐妹。|近义词：kinship, family, relatives|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps estrangement or alienation if referring to the breakdown of the relationship) |词性: Noun
