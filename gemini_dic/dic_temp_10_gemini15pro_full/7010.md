andrology:/ænˈdrɒlədʒi/ /ænˈdrɑːlədʒi/
n.
男性医学，男科学
The branch of medicine concerned with the male reproductive system and its disorders.
Andrology is a relatively new medical specialty.  男科学是一门相对较新的医学专业。
He's a specialist in andrology. 他是男科专家。
Synonyms:  male reproductive medicine
Antonyms:  gynecology
