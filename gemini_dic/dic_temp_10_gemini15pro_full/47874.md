decelerates:/diːˈsɛləˌreɪts/
verb
减速
reduces speed; slows down
The car decelerates as it approaches the red light. 汽车在接近红灯时减速。
The train decelerated as it pulled into the station. 火车进站时减速了。
synonyms: slows, brakes, retards
antonyms: accelerates, speeds up, quickens
词性: 动词
