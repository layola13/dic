guiltylike:/ˈɡɪltiˌlaɪk/
adjective
像犯了罪似的，内疚的
Resembling or characteristic of guilt; appearing or feeling as if one has done something wrong.
He cast a guiltylike glance at his mother after breaking the vase.  他打碎花瓶后，心虚地看了妈妈一眼。
近义词:guilty, ashamed, sheepish, remorseful
反义词:innocent, unashamed, brazen
词性:adjective
