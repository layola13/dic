coleaders:/ˈkoʊˌliːdərz/
n.
共同领导人，联合领导人
People who share leadership responsibilities.
The project had two coleaders who were responsible for different aspects of its development.  这个项目有两个共同领导人，他们分别负责其发展的不同方面。
The coleaders worked well together, complementing each other's strengths. 两位共同领导人合作良好，优势互补。
synonyms: co-leaders, joint leaders
antonyms: follower, subordinate
noun
