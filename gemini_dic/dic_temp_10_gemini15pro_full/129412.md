nominatable:/ˈnɒmɪneɪtəbl/
adjective
可提名的
able to be nominated.
例句：He was nominatable for the award. 他有资格被提名该奖项。

例句：Is she nominatable for the position?  她有资格被提名那个职位吗？


近义词：eligible, qualified
反义词：ineligible, unqualified
词性: adjective

词源: From nominate + -able.  "nominate" comes from the Latin "nominatus," the past participle of "nominare" (to name).
