libelists: /ˈlaɪbəlɪsts/ | n. |诽谤者；造谣者|  A person who publishes or spreads libels.  Someone who defames another by writing or publishing false or malicious statements. | The libelists were sued for damaging the reputation of the politician. 诽谤者因损害政治家的名誉而被起诉。|  The newspaper was accused of being a tool of libelists.  这家报纸被指控是诽谤者的工具。 | Synonyms: defamers, slanderers, calumniators | Antonyms: apologists, defenders, supporters | Noun
