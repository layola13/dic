nonvalue:/ˈnɒnˌvæljuː/ (US) /ˈnɑːnˌvæljuː/| n. |非价值，无价值|Something that does not contribute to the overall worth or utility.  It often refers to activities or processes in a business context that do not directly add value to the product or service being offered to the customer.|The consultant identified several nonvalue adding steps in the manufacturing process.|近义词：waste, inefficiency, superfluity|反义词: value, asset, worth|noun
