donkeyish:/ˈdɒŋkiɪʃ/ (US) /ˈdɑːŋkiɪʃ/| adj.|像驴的；愚蠢固执的；倔强的|Resembling or characteristic of a donkey, especially in being stubborn or obstinate.|He displayed a donkeyish refusal to compromise. 他表现出驴子般的拒绝妥协。|Synonyms: stubborn, obstinate, mulish, intractable, pigheaded|Antonyms: compliant, amenable, docile, agreeable|attribute

ETYMOLOGY->
From donkey + -ish. The suffix "-ish" is used to form adjectives from nouns, meaning "like" or "having the characteristics of."
