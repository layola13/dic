alleluia's:/ˌælɪˈluːjəz/
noun
赞美诗，哈利路亚
A plural form of "alleluia," an exclamation of praise or joy.
The choir sang powerful alleluias during the Easter service.  唱诗班在复活节仪式上高唱强有力的赞美诗。
The air was filled with joyful alleluias. 空气中充满了喜悦的赞美声。
Synonyms: praises, hymns, hallelujahs
Antonyms: lamentations, sorrows
