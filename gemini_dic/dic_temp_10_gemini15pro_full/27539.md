camwood:/ˈkæmwʊd/| n. |非洲红木，染料木|A red wood from the Baphia nitida tree, native to West Africa, used as a dye and in traditional medicine.  It yields a red dye and is also used medicinally.|The tribe used camwood to create vibrant body paint for their ceremonies.|近义词：barwood|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific type of wood)|名词
