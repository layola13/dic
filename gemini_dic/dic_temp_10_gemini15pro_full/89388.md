homoplastic:/ˌhɒməˈplæstɪk/ /ˌhoʊməˈplæstɪk/| adj.|同塑的，同塑型的|  (of organs or other bodily structures) similar in form and function but not homologous, i.e. not derived from the same ancestral structure.|The wings of bats and birds are homoplastic, having evolved independently for flight.|近义词：analogous, convergent|反义词：homologous|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *homos* (same) + *plastos* (molded, formed)
