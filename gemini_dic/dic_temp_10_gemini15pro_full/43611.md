creaseless:/ˈkriːslɪs/| adj.|无皱的，不起皱的|Without creases; smooth and unwrinkled.|例句：She wore a creaseless white shirt. 她穿着一件不起皱的白色衬衫。|例句：The fabric is advertised as being completely creaseless. 这种织物被宣传为完全不起皱。|近义词：smooth, unwrinkled, ironed, uncreased|反义词:creased, wrinkled, rumpled|词性:形容词
词源: crease + -less.  "Crease" comes from the Old North French "cresser" meaning "to break, crush". The suffix "-less" means "without".
