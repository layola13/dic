amnesia:/æmˈniːʒə/ /æmˈniːziə/| n. |失忆，健忘症|A partial or total loss of memory.|例句：He suffered from amnesia after the accident. 事故发生后，他患上了失忆症。

例句：The trauma caused her to experience a period of amnesia.  这次创伤使她经历了一段时间的失忆。|近义词：forgetfulness, memory loss|反义词: remembrance, recollection|词性: noun
