outburst:/ˈaʊtbɜːrst/| n.|爆发，迸发；(感情的)突然发作，(情绪的)突发|A sudden, intense expression of emotion or energy. |例句：Her outburst of anger surprised everyone. 她突然的愤怒爆发让每个人都感到惊讶。

例句：There was an outburst of cheering when the team scored. 当球队得分时，爆发出一阵欢呼声。

近义词：eruption, explosion, burst, fit, spasm|反义词:calm, peace, suppression|词性:noun
