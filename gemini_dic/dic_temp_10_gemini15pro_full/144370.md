outstinging:/ˌaʊtˈstɪŋɪŋ/
verb
胜过，超过（在唱歌方面）
surpassing in singing; singing better than.
The nightingale was outstinging all the other birds in the forest.  夜莺的歌声胜过森林里所有其他的鸟。
She outstinged her rivals in the singing competition. 在歌唱比赛中，她的歌声胜过了她的对手们。
近义词: outsinging, surpassing, excelling
反义词: losing to, being inferior to
词性: Verb

词源:
"out-" (prefix meaning "surpassing, exceeding") + "sting" (from Old English *stingan, related to Old Norse stinga "to pierce, prick," originally referring to the sharp, piercing quality of some sounds) + "-ing" (present participle suffix)
