dace:/deɪs/ /dæs/ | n. |欧亚鲌;雅罗鱼;鲦鱼|A small freshwater fish of the carp family, found in rivers and streams in Europe and Asia.  It is silvery in color and has a forked tail.|The dace darted through the clear water.  雅罗鱼在清澈的水中飞快地游动。| The fisherman caught a dace in the river. 渔夫在河里钓到了一条雅罗鱼。|近义词：chub, minnow, bleak|反义词:|n.
