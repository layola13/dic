low-brow:/ˈloʊbraʊ/| adj.|低俗的，庸俗的，文化水平低的|Not intellectually demanding; appealing to the masses and lacking in refinement.  Characterized by or appealing to popular taste; lacking in intellectual or cultural sophistication.|例句：They dismissed the movie as low-brow entertainment. 他们认为这部电影是低俗的娱乐。|例句：He considers reality TV to be low-brow. 他认为真人秀节目很低俗。|近义词：vulgar, tasteless, crude, unsophisticated, philistine|反义词：high-brow, sophisticated, refined, intellectual, cultured|词性:形容词
