fabricates:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪts/
verb
制造，捏造，编造
to invent or produce something false in order to deceive someone
The evidence was fabricated to make him look guilty.  证据是捏造的，目的是让他看起来有罪。
fabricate:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪts/
verb
制造，建造，装配
to construct or manufacture from various parts
The company fabricates steel components for bridges.  这家公司制造桥梁用的钢制部件。

synonyms:construct, manufacture, forge, invent, devise, concoct, fake
antonyms:demolish, destroy, dismantle, uncover, expose
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fabricātus, past participle of fabricāre (“to fashion, construct”), from Latin fabrica (“workshop, construction”).
