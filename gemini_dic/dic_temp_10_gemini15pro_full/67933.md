faceting:/ˈfæsɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 刻面，琢面；方面 | The process of cutting facets into a gem or other material to enhance its brilliance.  The act of considering something from multiple angles or perspectives. |  The faceting of the diamond gave it exceptional sparkle.  钻石的刻面赋予了它非凡的光彩。  We must consider all faceting of the problem before making a decision.  在做决定之前，我们必须考虑问题的各个方面。 | synonyms: aspect, side, phase, angle | antonyms: whole, entirety | noun

faceting:/ˈfæsɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 多方面的 | Having multiple facets or aspects. | This is a faceting issue with no easy solutions. 这是一个多方面的问题，没有简单的解决方案。 | synonyms: multifaceted, complex | antonyms: simple, straightforward | adjective
