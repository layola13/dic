daphne:/ˈdæfni/ | n. | 瑞香；达芙妮（女子名） | A flowering shrub, of a genus which includes the spurge laurel.  A girl's name, from Greek mythology. |  The daphne in my garden is blooming beautifully. 我花园里的瑞香开得很漂亮。 |  My friend Daphne is a talented musician. 我的朋友达芙妮是一位才华横溢的音乐家。 | synonym: laurel, bay tree (for the plant);  no direct synonym for the name | antonym: N/A | noun
