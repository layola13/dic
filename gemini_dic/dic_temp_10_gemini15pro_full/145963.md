overdistorting:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈstɔːrtɪŋ/ |adj.|过度扭曲的，过度失真的|Exaggerating or misrepresenting something to a degree that significantly alters its true nature or meaning.|The media was accused of overdistorting the facts of the case. 媒体被指责过度歪曲了案件的事实。|Synonyms: exaggerating, misrepresenting, twisting, warping|Antonyms: accurately representing, faithfully depicting|Word Type: Adjective

overdistorting:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈstɔːrtɪŋ/ |v.|过度扭曲，过度失真 (present participle)|The act of exaggerating or misrepresenting something to a degree that significantly alters its true nature or meaning.|The artist is overdistorting the image to create a surreal effect.  这位艺术家正在过度扭曲图像以创造一种超现实的效果。|Synonyms: exaggerating, misrepresenting, twisting, warping|Antonyms: accurately representing, faithfully depicting|Word Type: Verb (present participle)

词源：
"Over-" (prefix) + "distort" (verb)
"Over-" intensifies the meaning of "distort."  "Distort" comes from the Latin "distorquere," meaning "to twist awry."
