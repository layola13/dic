defailance:/dɪˈfeɪləns/
n.
不履行，违约，失职
failure to perform a duty or obligation; neglect
The defailance of the contractor led to significant delays in the project.  承包商的违约导致项目严重延误。
Synonyms: default, nonperformance, negligence, breach, lapse
Antonyms: performance, fulfillment, compliance, adherence
noun

Etymology:
From Old French *defaillance, from defaillir "to fail, be lacking," from de- "down, away" + faillir "to fail," from Latin fallere "to deceive, disappoint."
