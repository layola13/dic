doom-laden:/ˈduːmˌleɪdn/
adjective
充满厄运的，不祥的
filled with a sense of impending doom or disaster
The doom-laden atmosphere of the abandoned town was unsettling.  这个废弃小镇弥漫着不祥的气氛，令人不安。
The prophet delivered a doom-laden prophecy about the future of the kingdom.  先知对王国的未来做出了一个充满厄运的预言。
synonyms: ominous, portentous, inauspicious, foreboding
antonyms: promising, auspicious, hopeful, optimistic
adjective
