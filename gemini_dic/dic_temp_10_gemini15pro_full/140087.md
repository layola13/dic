off /ɒf/ (UK), /ɔːf/ (US) | adv. adj. prep. | 离开，关闭，休息，减价 | away from a place or position; not operating; not at work or not functioning; deducted from a price | The lights are off. 灯关了。| 近义词: away, disconnected, absent | 反义词: on, connected, present | 词性: adverb, adjective, preposition

off /ɒf/ (UK), /ɔːf/ (US) | adj. | 停止的，取消的，腐败的 | not operating; cancelled; no longer fresh | The milk is off. 牛奶坏了。| 近义词: cancelled, stale, bad | 反义词: on, fresh, good | 词性: adjective

off /ɒf/ (UK), /ɔːf/ (US) | prep. | 从...离开，脱离 | down or away from; situated away from | He fell off the ladder. 他从梯子上摔下来了。 | 近义词: from, away from | 反义词: on, onto | 词性: preposition
