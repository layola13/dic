ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *fraudulentnes*, from Old French fraudulenteté, from Latin fraudulentus (“deceitful, fraudulent”), from fraus (“deceit, fraud”) + -lentus (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'full of, prone to'). Equivalent to fraudulent +‎ -ness.
