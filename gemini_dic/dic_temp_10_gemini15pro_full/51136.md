despoiler:/dɪˈspɔɪlər/| n. |掠夺者；劫掠者；破坏者|Someone who steals or violently removes something from a person or place; someone who plunders or ravages. |The despoilers ransacked the village, leaving nothing but ruins. 劫掠者洗劫了村庄，只留下一片废墟。

The museum was ransacked by despoilers who stole priceless artifacts.  博物馆被掠夺者洗劫一空，他们偷走了无价的文物。

|近义词：plunderer, looter, raider, pillager, ravager|反义词：protector, guardian, defender|词性: noun
