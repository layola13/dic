estiferous:/ɛsˈtɪfərəs/| adj.|炎热的，夏季的|Characterized by or suggestive of summer; bringing on summer; pertaining to summer.|例句：The estiferous weather made us crave ice cream. 炎热的天气让我们想吃冰淇淋。|例句：The estiferous afternoon was perfect for a swim. 炎热的下午很适合游泳。|近义词：summery, hot, sultry, torrid|反义词：wintry, cold, frigid|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *aestifer*, from *aest-*, stem of *aestus* ("heat, summer heat") + *-fer* ("bearing, bringing").
