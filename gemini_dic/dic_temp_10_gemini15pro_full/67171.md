expostulator:/ɪkˈspɒstjʊleɪtər/ /ɛkˈspɑːstʃəleɪtər/| n. |抗议者；劝诫者；责备者|A person who reasons earnestly with someone against something that person intends to do or has done; someone who remonstrates.|She acted as an expostulator, trying to convince him to change his mind. 她充当劝诫者的角色，试图说服他改变主意。|Example: The expostulator argued vehemently against the proposed policy.  这位抗议者强烈反对拟议的政策。|Synonyms: remonstrator, protester, objector, critic, reprover|Antonyms: supporter, advocate, approver|noun
