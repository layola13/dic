centrists:/ˈsɛntrɪsts/| n.|中间派，温和派|People who hold moderate political views, advocating a position between the extremes of the left and right wings.  They often seek compromise and pragmatism over ideology.|例句：The centrists in the party played a crucial role in brokering a compromise between the liberal and conservative factions.  该党中的中间派在促成自由派和保守派之间的妥协方面发挥了关键作用。

例句：Centrists are often seen as more pragmatic and less ideologically driven than those on the political extremes.  中间派通常被认为比政治极端分子更务实，意识形态色彩更淡。|近义词：moderates, middle-of-the-roaders|反义词:extremists, radicals|词性:noun
