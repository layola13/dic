musicals:/ˈmjuːzɪkəlz/
n.
音乐剧；音乐片
stage productions or films that involve singing and dancing as well as acting
I love going to see musicals on Broadway. 我喜欢去百老汇看音乐剧。
近义词：musical comedies, musical plays, musical theatre
反义词:plays, dramas
