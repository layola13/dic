confounding:/kənˈfaʊndɪŋ/ | adj.| 令人困惑的；混淆的；惊慌失措的 | Causing surprise or confusion, especially by acting against their expectations. |  The confounding results of the experiment made the scientists question their hypothesis.  这个实验令人困惑的结果使科学家们对他们的假设产生了疑问。 | Synonyms: bewildering, perplexing, puzzling, baffling | Antonyms: clarifying, illuminating, enlightening | adjective

confound:/kənˈfaʊnd/ | vt.| 使困惑；使惊讶；证明…有错 | To cause surprise or confusion in (someone), especially by acting against their expectations. | The magician's tricks confounded the audience. 魔术师的戏法使观众感到困惑。| Synonyms: amaze, astonish, surprise, bewilder | Antonyms: clarify, explain, enlighten | verb
