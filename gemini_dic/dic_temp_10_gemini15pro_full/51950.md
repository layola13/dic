diacap:/daɪˈækæp/| n.|盖层；覆盖层；保护层|A protective layer or covering, especially one of rock or sediment.|The diacap prevented the underlying rock from eroding.  盖层阻止了下层岩石的侵蚀。|Synonyms: covering, layer, cap, mantle|Antonyms: base, foundation|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
"Diabase cap."  A shortened form of "diabase cap," where "diabase" is a type of igneous rock, and "cap" refers to a covering layer.
