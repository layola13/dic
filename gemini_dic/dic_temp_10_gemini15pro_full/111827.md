loggerhead:/ˈlɒɡəhed/ (British), /ˈlɔːɡərˌhed/ (American)| n.|红海龟；笨蛋；迟钝的人|A large marine turtle with a reddish-brown shell found in tropical and subtropical waters. Also used informally to refer to a stupid or slow-witted person. |例句：The loggerhead turtle is an endangered species. 红海龟是一种濒危物种。

例句：He's such a loggerhead; he never understands anything. 他真是个笨蛋，什么都不懂。|近义词：blockhead, dunce, numskull (for the informal meaning)|反义词:genius, mastermind (for the informal meaning) |词性:noun


loggerhead:/ˈlɒɡəhed/ (British), /ˈlɔːɡərˌhed/ (American)| adj.|顽固的；执拗的|Stubborn, obstinate. |例句：They were at loggerheads over the new policy. 他们在新政策上意见不合。

例句：She has a loggerhead attitude and refuses to compromise. 她态度顽固，拒绝妥协。|近义词：stubborn, obstinate, headstrong|反义词:compliant, amenable|词性:adjective
