extremeless:/ɪkˈstriːmlɪs/| adj. |没有极端的，不极端的|Lacking extremes; moderate; temperate.  Not characterized by excess or intensity. |The climate there is extremeless, rarely getting too hot or too cold. 那里的气候不极端，很少太热或太冷。|近义词:moderate, temperate, mild, balanced|反义词:extreme, radical, intense, immoderate|词性:形容词

词源:  Formed by adding the negative prefix "ex-" (meaning "out of, from") and the suffix "-less" (meaning "without") to the word "extreme."  Therefore, it literally means "without extremes."
