interfraternity:/ˌɪntərfrəˈtɜːrnəti/| adj.|兄弟会之间的，跨兄弟会的|Existing or occurring between fraternities.|The interfraternity council organized a Greek Week competition.|近义词：intercollegiate|反义词：intrafratenity|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From inter- + fraternity.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," derived from Latin. "Fraternity" comes from the Latin "fraternitas," meaning "brotherhood."
