enriches:/ɪnˈrɪtʃɪz/
verb
使富足；使丰富；使充实；增益
makes something better in quality, more valuable, or more interesting
Traveling enriches the mind. 旅行使心灵充实。
Synonyms: enhances, improves, augments, supplements
Antonyms: depletes, impoverishes, diminishes
词性: 动词
