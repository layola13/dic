organza:/ɔːrˈɡænzə/ /ɔːrˈɡɑːnzə/
n.
薄纱，柯根纱
A thin, stiff, transparent fabric of silk or nylon.
例句：She wore a beautiful organza dress to the wedding. 她穿着一件漂亮的柯根纱礼服参加婚礼。

例句：The curtains were made of a delicate organza. 窗帘是用精致的薄纱制成的。

近义词：chiffon, voile, gauze
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps something like "heavy fabric" or "opaque fabric")
词性: noun
