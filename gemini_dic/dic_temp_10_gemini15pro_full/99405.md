interdisciplinary:/ˌɪntərˈdɪsɪplɪnəri/ | adj. | 交叉学科的，跨学科的 | Combining or involving two or more academic disciplines or fields of study. | Example: The university offers an interdisciplinary program in environmental studies. 这所大学提供环境研究的跨学科课程。

Example: Interdisciplinary research is becoming increasingly important in addressing complex global challenges.  跨学科研究在应对复杂的全球挑战中变得越来越重要。


|近义词：cross-disciplinary, multidisciplinary |反义词: disciplinary, specialized |词性: adjective|
