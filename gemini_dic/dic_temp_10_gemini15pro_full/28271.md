captivating:/ˈkæptɪveɪtɪŋ/| adj.|迷人的，有魅力的|Having the power to attract and hold the interest; fascinating.|Her captivating smile made everyone feel welcome. 她迷人的微笑让每个人都感到宾至如归。|近义词：enthralling, fascinating, mesmerizing, enchanting|反义词：uninteresting, boring, dull, repellent|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "captivate," from Late Latin "captivatus," past participle of "captivare" "to take captive," from Latin "captivus" "captive," from "captus," past participle of "capere" "to take, seize."
