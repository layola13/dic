conterranean:/ˌkɒntəˈreɪniən/ /ˌkɑːntəˈreɪniən/
adjective
共同的；同时代的；属于同一时代的
existing or occurring at or during the same time
例句：Conterranean fossils can help date the rock layer.  同时代的化石可以帮助确定岩层的年代。

例句：They were conterranean writers, influencing each other's work. 他们是同时代的作家，互相影响着彼此的作品。
近义词：contemporary, coeval, synchronous
反义词：non-contemporary, asynchronous
词性：adjective
词源：From Latin *conterraneus, from con- 'together' + terra 'earth'.
