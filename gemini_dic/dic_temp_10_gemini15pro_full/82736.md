guidingly:/ˈɡaɪdɪŋli/
adv.
引导地，指导地
in a guiding manner; so as to guide.
She guidingly placed his hand on the railing. 她引导性地把他的手放在栏杆上。
近义词:directively, instructively
反义词: aimlessly, randomly
词性:副词

词源: From guide + -ing + -ly.  "Guide" comes from Old French "guider," ultimately from Frankish "*witan," meaning "to show the way."
