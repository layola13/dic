goswami:/ɡɔːˈswɑːmiː/ /ɡoʊˈswɑːmiː/
n.
大师，导师，上师，（尤指印度教的）宗教领袖
A Hindu religious teacher, especially one of elevated spiritual status.
He is a revered goswami in the community. 他是社区里受人尊敬的导师。
The goswami gave a discourse on the Bhagavad Gita.  这位上师就《薄伽梵歌》发表了演讲。
Synonyms: guru, master, teacher, preacher, spiritual leader
Antonyms: disciple, student, follower
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Sanskrit *gosvāmin*, meaning "master of cows," later signifying "master of the senses" and thus a spiritual leader.  The word is composed of *go* ("cow," also symbolizing the senses) and *svāmin* ("master," "lord," or "owner").
