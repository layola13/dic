ineffable:/ɪnˈɛfəbəl/ | adj. | 不可言喻的，难以表达的 | Too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words. | The beauty of the sunset was ineffable.  日落的美丽难以言喻。| synonyms: indescribable, inexpressible, unutterable, unspeakable | antonyms: expressible, describable, utterable | adjective

ineffable:/ɪnˈɛfəbəl/ | adj. | 神圣的，崇高的 | Sacred; not to be uttered. |  The ineffable name of God.  上帝的圣名。| synonyms: holy, sacred, divine | antonyms: profane, secular | adjective
