dauntlessness:/ˈdɔːntləsnəs/
noun
无畏，勇敢
The quality of being fearless and resolute.
She showed remarkable dauntlessness in the face of danger. 她面对危险表现出非凡的勇气。
courage, bravery, fearlessness, intrepidity
timidity, cowardice, fearfulness
