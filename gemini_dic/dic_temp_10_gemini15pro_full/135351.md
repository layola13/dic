nonperceptibility:/ˌnɑːnpərˌsɛptɪˈbɪləti/
n.|不可感知性；不被察觉的性质|The nonperceptibility of the changes made it difficult to detect the forgery. 这些变化的不可感知性使得很难发现伪造。|
nonperceptibility refers to the quality or state of being impossible or very difficult to perceive through the senses.  It describes something that is too subtle or faint to be noticed, often referring to changes, differences, or effects.| The nonperceptibility of the signal made communication impossible.  信号的不可察觉性使得通讯变得不可能。 |Synonyms: imperceptibility, invisibility, undetectability|Antonyms: perceptibility, visibility, detectability|noun
