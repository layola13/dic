hyperphalangy:/ˌhaɪpərˈfæləndʒi/
n.
多指（趾）症
A congenital anomaly characterized by the presence of extra phalanges in the digits.
Polydactyly is a form of hyperphalangy.  多指症是多指（趾）症的一种形式。
Whales exhibit hyperphalangy in their flippers. 鲸鱼的鳍状肢表现出多指（趾）症。
Synonyms: polydactyly, supernumerary digits
Antonyms: oligodactyly, ectrodactyly
