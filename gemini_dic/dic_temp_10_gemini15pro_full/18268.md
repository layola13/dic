belittlement:/bɪˈlɪtlmənt/ | n. | 轻视，贬低 | the act of making someone or something seem unimportant |  His constant belittlement of her achievements made her lose confidence. 他不断贬低她的成就，使她失去了信心。| synonyms: disparagement, denigration, depreciation, minimization | antonyms: praise, commendation, exaltation | noun

belittle:/bɪˈlɪtl/ | v. | 轻视，贬低 | to make someone or something seem unimportant | He belittled her efforts to learn French. 他轻视她学习法语的努力。| synonyms: disparage, denigrate, depreciate, minimize | antonyms: praise, commend, exalt | verb
