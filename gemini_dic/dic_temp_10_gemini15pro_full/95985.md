incur:/ɪnˈkɜːr/ (US), /ɪnˈkɜː(r)/ (UK)| v. |招致，引起，遭受|To experience something unpleasant or unwelcome as a result of one's actions or circumstances. |例句：The company incurred heavy losses due to the economic downturn.  公司由于经济低迷而遭受了严重的损失。

例句：He incurred the wrath of his boss by being late again. 他因为再次迟到而招致了老板的愤怒。


近义词：suffer, experience, sustain, attract, bring on|反义词: avoid, prevent, avert|词性:动词
