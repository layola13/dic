eulogises:/ˈjuːlədʒaɪzɪz/
verb
赞扬，称颂
praises; speaks or writes in high praise of
The author eulogises the beauty of the natural world in his latest book.  这位作者在他的新书中赞美了自然世界的美丽。
synonyms: praises, extols, lauds, celebrates, commends
antonyms: criticizes, condemns, denounces
词性:动词
