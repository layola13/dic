censurers:/ˈsɛnʃərərz/
noun
批评者，指责者
People who express strong disapproval or condemnation of something or someone.
The censurers of the new policy were very vocal.  新政策的批评者们表达了强烈的不满。
The artist faced harsh censurers for her controversial exhibit.  这位艺术家因其备受争议的展览而面临严厉的批评。
critics, detractors, faultfinders
supporters, admirers, advocates
n.
