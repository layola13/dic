owning:/ˈoʊnɪŋ/| adj.|拥有的；承认的|Being in possession of something; acknowledging something as one's own.|The owning of a pet comes with responsibilities. 拥有宠物意味着责任。|近义词：possessing, having|反义词:lacking, disowning|adjective

owning:/ˈoʊnɪŋ/| n.|拥有；承认|The act, state, or right of possessing something.|The owning of property is a fundamental right. 拥有财产是一项基本权利。|近义词：possession, ownership|反义词:dispossession, renunciation|noun
