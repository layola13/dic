beaders:/ˈbiːdərz/
n.
串珠者; 珠饰工人; 珠饰艺术家
People who make beaded items, such as jewelry, clothing, or decorations.
The beaders skillfully crafted intricate designs using tiny glass beads.  串珠者巧妙地用微小的玻璃珠制作出复杂的图案。
The beaders' market showcased a vibrant array of handcrafted jewelry.  串珠者市场展示了一系列充满活力的手工珠宝。
近义词: beadworkers, bead-stringers
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "non-crafters")
词性: Noun


beaders:/ˈbiːdərz/
n.
（用珠子装饰的）饰边; 珠饰
Decorative edges or trims made of beads.
The dress was adorned with delicate beaders along the neckline.  这件连衣裙的领口装饰着精致的珠饰。
The beaders on the curtains added a touch of elegance to the room. 窗帘上的珠饰为房间增添了一丝优雅。
近义词: beading, beadwork
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: Noun
