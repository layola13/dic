foresees:/fɔːrˈsiːz/
verb
预见，预知
to see or know something that will happen in the future.
The report foresees a significant increase in demand.  该报告预测需求将大幅增长。
The company foresees a rise in profits next year.  公司预计明年利润将增长。
synonym: predict, anticipate, envisage
antonym: misunderstand, misinterpret
v.
