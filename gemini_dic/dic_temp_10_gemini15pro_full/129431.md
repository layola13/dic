nomography:/nəˈmɒɡrəfi/
n.
列线图解法;诺谟图学
A graphical technique for representing the relationship between three or more variables by means of a chart or diagram, often consisting of aligned scales.
Nomography allows engineers to quickly solve complex equations without tedious calculations.  列线图解法使工程师能够快速求解复杂的方程式，而无需繁琐的计算。
The nomography provided a quick solution to the engineering problem. 这张诺谟图提供了一个工程问题的快速解决方案。

近义词: alignment chart, nomogram
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "numerical calculation")
