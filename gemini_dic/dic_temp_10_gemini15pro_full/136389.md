nonrestriction:/ˌnɒnriˈstrɪkʃən/ | n. |无限制，非限制|Absence of restriction; the state of being unrestricted. |例句：The nonrestriction on the number of applicants allowed more people to apply.  申请人数没有限制，允许更多人申请。|例句：The nonrestriction on trade between the two countries boosted their economies. 两国之间贸易的无限制促进了它们的经济发展。 |近义词：freedom, liberty, license, deregulation |反义词: restriction, limitation, constraint |词性:名词

Etymology:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + restriction (from Latin *restrictionem, accusative of *restrictio, from restringere "to restrain, bind back," from re- "back" + stringere "to draw tight").
