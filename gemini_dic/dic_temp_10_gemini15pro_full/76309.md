gabeler:/ˈɡeɪblər/ | n. |  叉子；餐叉；（尤指）鱼叉 | A dining utensil with two or more prongs, used for eating. |  He used a gabeler to eat his salmon. 他用叉子吃他的鲑鱼。 | 近义词: fork, trident (especially for fishing) | 反义词: spoon | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *gabeler, from gabele "fork," from Frankish *gabila, from Proto-Germanic *gabulō. Cognate with Old High German gabala, German Gabel, Dutch gaffel, Old Norse gafl.
