finjan:/ˈfɪndʒæn/| n.|芬姜，一种带把手的小咖啡杯 (尤指阿拉伯地区使用)|A small cup with a handle for drinking coffee, especially in Arab countries.|He sipped his strong, bitter coffee from a delicate finjan. 他用一个精致的芬姜小口啜饮着浓烈苦涩的咖啡。|Near synonyms: cup, demitasse|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "mug" for a larger, less delicate cup)|noun
