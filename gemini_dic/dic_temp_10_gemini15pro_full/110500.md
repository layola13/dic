limax:/ˈlaɪmæks/| n. |蛞蝓；鼻涕虫|A terrestrial gastropod mollusk without a shell or with a very small internal shell.  A slug. |The garden was infested with limax. 花园里到处都是蛞蝓。|近义词：slug, gastropod|反义词: None.| noun
