parietes:/pəˈraɪəˌtiːz/
noun
壁；腔壁；(尤指)腹壁
The walls of a cavity or hollow organ, especially the abdominal cavity.
The surgeon examined the patient's abdominal parietes.  外科医生检查了病人的腹壁。
The parietes of the heart were thickened. 心脏壁增厚了。
Synonyms: walls, lining, membrane
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps cavity, lumen)
