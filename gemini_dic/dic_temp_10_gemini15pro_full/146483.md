overfurnishing: /ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrnɪʃɪŋ/ | n. | 过度装饰；家具过多 | The act of furnishing a room or house with too much furniture, making it cluttered and cramped. |  The overfurnishing of the small apartment made it feel stuffy.  这间小公寓家具太多，感觉很闷。| 近义词: cluttering, overcrowding | 反义词: minimalism, sparseness | 词性: 名词

overfurnish: /ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrnɪʃ/ | vt. | 过度装饰；放置过多家具 | To furnish a room or house with too much furniture. |  Be careful not to overfurnish the living room; it will look cramped.  小心不要在客厅里放太多家具，它会显得拥挤。 | 近义词: overcrowd, clutter | 反义词: underfurnish | 词性: 动词
