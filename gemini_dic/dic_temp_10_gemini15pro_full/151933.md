pastier:/ˈpeɪstiər/ (US), /ˈpɑːstiər/ (UK)| adj.|更像糊状的，黏糊糊的|More paste-like or sticky than something else.|The dough was much pastier than I expected, making it difficult to roll out.  这面团比我预想的要黏糊糊的多，很难擀开。| The sauce became pastier after simmering for a while.  酱汁炖了一会儿后变得更稠了。|近义词：stickier, gooier, thicker|反义词：runnier, thinner|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From paste + -ier (comparative suffix). Ultimately from Latin *pasta "dough."
