de-anglicized:/diːˈæŋɡləsaɪzd/
adjective
去英语化的；非英语化的
Reduced or eliminated English influence, especially in language or culture.
The government promoted a de-anglicized version of the Irish language. 政府推广了一个去英语化的爱尔兰语版本。
The school's name was de-anglicized to reflect the local culture. 学校的名字被去英语化以反映当地文化。
