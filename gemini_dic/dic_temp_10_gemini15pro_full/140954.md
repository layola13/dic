one-sidednesses:/ˌwʌnˈsaɪdɪdnəsɪz/| n. |片面性；单方面性|The one-sidednesses of the report led to a distorted view of the situation.  这份报告的片面性导致了对情况的歪曲理解。|This agreement displays a marked one-sidedness in favor of the landlord. 这份协议明显偏袒房东。|近义词：bias, partiality, prejudice|反义词：impartiality, objectivity, fairness|词性:noun
Etymology:  From "one-sided" + "-ness". "One-sided" originates from "one" + "side" + "-ed".  "Side" comes from Old English "sīde".  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns expressing state, quality, or condition.
