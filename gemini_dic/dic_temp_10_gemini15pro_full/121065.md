misconstruable:/ˌmɪskənˈstruːəbl/
adjective
可被误解的
Open to misinterpretation; easily misinterpreted.
His statement was deliberately misconstruable. 他的声明故意说得模棱两可，容易被误解。
The contract is worded in a way that is intentionally misconstruable.  合同的措辞故意写得容易引起误解。
Synonyms: ambiguous, vague, equivocal, misleading, open to interpretation
Antonyms: clear, unambiguous, precise, unequivocal
词性: 形容词
