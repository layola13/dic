conoidal:/kəˈnɔɪdəl/
adjective
圆锥形的；近似圆锥体的
Resembling a cone; somewhat conical.
The shell has a conoidal shape.  这个贝壳呈圆锥形。
近义词: conical, cone-shaped
反义词:  flat, planar
词性: adjective
