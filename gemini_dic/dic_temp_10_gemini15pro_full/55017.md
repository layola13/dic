disputers:/dɪˈspjuːtərz/
n.
争论者，辩论者，质疑者
People who argue or debate a point, or who challenge the validity of something.
The disputers argued for hours about the meaning of the contract.  争论者们就合同的含义争论了几个小时。
The disputers challenged the scientific evidence presented by the expert. 质疑者对专家提出的科学证据提出了质疑。
Synonyms: arguers, debaters, challengers, questioners, contestants
Antonyms: agreers, supporters, endorsers
