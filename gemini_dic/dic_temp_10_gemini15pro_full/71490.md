flanched:/flæntʃt/
adjective
法兰连接的；带凸缘的
Having a flange or flanges. A flange is a projecting flat rim, collar, or rib on an object, serving to strengthen or attach or (on a wheel) to maintain position on a rail.
The pipe was flanched to the wall.  这根管子用法兰连接到墙上。
The flanched wheels kept the train on the track. 带凸缘的轮子使火车保持在轨道上。
近义词:flanged, rimmed
反义词:unflanched, flangeless
词性:adjective

词源:
From the verb "to flange," which comes from the French word "flanche" meaning "flank" or "side."
