outfishing: /ˌaʊtˈfɪʃɪŋ/
|v.|过度捕捞；比...捕鱼捕得多|The act of catching fish at a faster rate than they can reproduce, leading to a decline in fish populations.|Commercial fleets are outfishing local fishermen.|近义词: overfishing|反义词: underfishing|词性: 动词
outfish: /ˌaʊtˈfɪʃ/
|v.|比...捕鱼捕得多|To catch more fish than someone else.|He outfished his brother on their fishing trip.|近义词: surpass, beat|反义词: be outfished by|词性: 动词
