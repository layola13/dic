oversweetness:/ˌoʊvərˈswiːtnəs/| n. | 过甜，甜度过高 | Excessive sweetness; a taste that is too sweet. | The oversweetness of the dessert made it unpalatable. 这道甜点太甜了，让人难以下咽。|  The oversweetness of the candy gave me a headache.  这糖果太甜了，让我头疼。| 近义词：sickliness, cloyingness | 反义词: tartness, sourness | 词性: noun

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "sweetness" (noun form of "sweet")
