disbranch:/dɪsˈbrɑːntʃ/
verb
去除枝条；截去旁枝
To remove branches from; to prune.
The gardener disbranched the overgrown tree. 园丁修剪了过度生长的树木。
近义词:prune, trim, lop
反义词:graft, append
词性:v.


disbranch:/dɪsˈbrɑːntʃ/
noun
去除枝条的行为；截枝
The act of removing branches; pruning.
The disbranching of the trees made the street look wider.  修剪树木使街道看起来更宽了。
近义词:pruning, trimming, lopping
反义词:grafting, appending
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + branch (a division or subdivision).  The prefix "dis-" indicates removal or separation, while "branch" refers to the limb of a tree. Thus, "disbranch" literally means to remove a branch.
