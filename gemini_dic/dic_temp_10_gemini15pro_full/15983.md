balustraded: /ˈbæləstreɪdɪd/ | adj. | 有栏杆的，有围栏的 | Having a balustrade; furnished with a balustrade. | The balustraded terrace offered stunning views of the valley.  有栏杆的露台提供了山谷的壮丽景色。 |  近义词: railed, fenced | 反义词: open, unenclosed | 词性: adjective

词源: From balustrade + -ed.  Balustrade comes from Italian *balaustrata (attested from 1593 in the form balaustrata), from balaustro "baluster," from balaustra "wild pomegranate flower," from Late Latin balaustium, from Greek balaustion "flower of the wild pomegranate tree."
