miraa:/ˈmɪərɑː/| n.|卡特（一种兴奋剂植物）|A shrub (Catha edulis) native to East Africa and the Arabian Peninsula, the leaves of which are chewed as a stimulant.|People in some cultures chew miraa for its stimulating effects.  一些文化中的人们咀嚼卡特以获得其刺激作用。|近义词：khat, qat|反义词: 無 (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑镇静剂sedative)|词性:noun
