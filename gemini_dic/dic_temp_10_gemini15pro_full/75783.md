fumage:/fuˈmɑːʒ/| n.|烟熏画法；烟熏艺术|The creation of an image by passing a candle or other flame over a surface, such as paper or canvas, to deposit soot and create patterns.  通过将蜡烛或其他火焰在纸张或画布等表面上移动来沉积烟灰并创作图案的图像创作方法。|The artist created a haunting fumage landscape.  这位艺术家创作了一幅令人难忘的烟熏风景画。|近义词：smoke art, soot drawing|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "clear painting")|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *fumer* (to smoke), from Latin *fumus* (smoke). 源自法语 *fumer*（吸烟），来自拉丁语 *fumus*（烟）。
