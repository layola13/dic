cobnut:/ˈkɒbnʌt/ (UK), /ˈkoʊbnʌt/ (US)| n.|榛子；榛树|A round, hard-shelled nut that grows on a hazel tree.|We gathered cobnuts in the autumn. 我们在秋天收集榛子。|Synonyms: hazelnut, filbert|Antonyms: None|Noun

cobnut:/ˈkɒbnʌt/ (UK), /ˈkoʊbnʌt/ (US)| adj.|榛子色的；淡褐色的|Having a light brown color, like that of a cob nut.|She wore a cobnut-colored sweater. 她穿着一件榛子色的毛衣。|Synonyms: hazel, light brown, tan|Antonyms: dark, black|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle English *cobnut*, from *cob* (a rounded lump or head) + *nut*.
