meager:/ˈmiːɡər/ (US) /ˈmiːɡə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|贫乏的，不足的，瘦弱的|Lacking in quantity or quality; deficient.  Thin or emaciated.|例句：The refugees had meager rations of food and water. 难民的食物和水配给不足。|例句：He survived on a meager salary. 他靠微薄的薪水生存。|近义词：scanty, sparse, paltry, insufficient, inadequate, lean, thin|反义词：abundant, plentiful, ample, generous, substantial, plump|词性:形容词
词源:From Old French *maigre (Modern French maigre), from Latin macer "lean, thin."
