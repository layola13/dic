mufti:/ˈmʌfti/| n.|便服，便衣；伊斯兰教法典注释家|A civilian dress worn by someone who normally wears a uniform, especially a military uniform.  A Muslim legal expert who is empowered to give rulings on religious matters.|The soldiers were allowed to go out in mufti. 士兵们被允许穿便服外出。 The Grand Mufti issued a fatwa against smoking. 大穆夫提发布了禁止吸烟的宗教法令。|Synonyms: civilian clothes, plain clothes (for dress); jurist, scholar, expert (for legal expert)|Antonyms: uniform|noun
