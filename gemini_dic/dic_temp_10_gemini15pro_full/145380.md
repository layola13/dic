overcarelessly: /ˌoʊvərˈkɛrləsli/
| adv. | 过于粗心地，太不小心地 | Doing something with excessive carelessness; in a very careless manner. | He overcarelessly tossed the valuable vase onto the sofa, and it shattered. 他非常粗心地把那个珍贵的花瓶扔到沙发上，结果摔碎了。|  She overcarelessly left the front door unlocked when she went out. 她出门时太不小心，忘了锁前门。| 近义词: negligently, recklessly, thoughtlessly | 反义词: carefully, cautiously, meticulously | 词性: 副词
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "carelessly" (adverb form of "careless," from "care" + "-less")
