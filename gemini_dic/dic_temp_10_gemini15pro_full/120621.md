mis-analyzing: /ˌmɪsˈænəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
Verb
错误分析；分析错误
Incorrectly analyzing something; analyzing something in a wrong way.
He was mis-analyzing the data and came to the wrong conclusion. 他错误地分析了数据，得出了错误的结论。
misinterpreting, misconstruing
correctly analyzing, interpreting accurately
verb
