obstructives: /əbˈstrʌktɪvz/
| n. | 阻塞物；障碍物 |Something that obstructs or hinders passage or progress; an obstacle.| The doctor removed the obstructives from the patient's airway. 医生清除了病人呼吸道中的阻塞物。| synonyms: obstacles, impediments, barriers | antonyms: aids, facilitators | noun

obstructive: /əbˈstrʌktɪv/
| adj. | 阻塞的；阻碍的 |Causing or tending to cause obstruction.| The obstructive behavior of the protesters caused traffic jams throughout the city. 抗议者的阻挠行为造成了全市交通堵塞。| synonyms: hindering, impeding, blocking | antonyms: helpful, facilitating | adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *obstructivus, from Latin obstructus (past participle of obstruere ‘build in front of, block up’), from ob- ‘against’ + struere ‘build, pile up’.
