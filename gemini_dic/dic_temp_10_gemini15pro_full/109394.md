lentil:/ˈlɛntɪl/ (US), /ˈlɛntl/ (UK)| n.|扁豆，小扁豆|A type of small, round legume with a flat shape, used for food.|Example 1:  I made lentil soup for dinner. 我晚饭做了扁豆汤。

Example 2: Lentils are a good source of protein. 扁豆是良好的蛋白质来源。|近义词：pulse, legume|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps meat as a contrasting protein source)|词性:noun
