daubingly:/ˈdɔːbɪŋli/| adv.|涂抹地，污秽地|In a manner of smearing or applying something roughly or carelessly.  It implies a lack of skill or precision.|He daubingly applied the paint, leaving streaks and uneven patches. 他涂抹油漆的方式很粗糙，留下了条纹和不均匀的斑块。|近义词：smearingly, messily, clumsily|反义词: neatly, carefully, precisely|词性:副词

daub:/dɔːb/| v. n.|涂抹，涂料|To smear or coat with a soft adhesive substance.|He daubed the wall with mud. 他用泥巴涂抹墙壁。|近义词: smear, plaster, coat|反义词: clean, wipe|词性:动词/名词
