def:/dɛf/| adj.| 肯定的；明确的；果断的|Certain; clearly defined; unmistakable.|例句：He gave a def answer. 他给出了肯定的答复。|近义词：certain, definite, positive, absolute, explicit|反义词：indefinite, uncertain, vague, ambiguous|词性:形容词

def:/dɛf/| n.| 默认值；缺省值|A value automatically assigned to a variable or parameter if no other value is specified.|例句：The def setting for the display is 1024x768. 显示器的默认设置为 1024x768。|近义词：default, preset|反义词：custom, user-defined|词性:名词

def:/dɛf/| adv.| 肯定地；明确地；果断地|In a definite or certain manner.|例句：He spoke def about his plans. 他明确地谈了他的计划。|近义词：definitely, certainly, positively, absolutely, explicitly|反义词：indefinitely, uncertainly, vaguely, ambiguously|词性:副词
