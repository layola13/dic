leader:/ˈliːdər/ (US) /ˈliːdə(r)/ (UK)| n.|领导者，领袖，指挥者|A person who guides or directs a group or organization.|The team leader motivated everyone to work harder.  团队领导激励每个人更加努力工作。

|近义词：chief, head, guide, director, boss, manager, superintendent, supervisor|反义词：follower, subordinate|词性: noun


leader:/ˈliːdər/ (US) /ˈliːdə(r)/ (UK)| adj.(较少使用)|领先的，主要的|Being ahead of others; principal.|The leader brand in the market is facing fierce competition.  市场上的领先品牌正面临激烈的竞争。

|近义词：leading, foremost, primary, chief, principal|反义词：trailing, lagging, last|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *lædere (unattested), from *lædan "to lead," of Germanic origin; related to Dutch leider and German Leiter.
