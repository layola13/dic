alienator:/ˈeɪliəˌneɪtər/ | n. |疏远者；离间者|Someone who alienates another person or people.  The alienator poisoned the relationship between the two friends.|例句：The alienator spread rumors to drive a wedge between the siblings. 离间者散布谣言，挑拨兄弟姐妹之间的关系。

例句：He became an alienator within his own family, pushing everyone away. 他成了自己家庭中的一个疏远者，把每个人都推开了。|近义词：estranger, isolator|反义词：unifier, reconciler|词性:noun
