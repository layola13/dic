leather-like: /ˈleðərˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像皮革的，似皮革的 | Resembling or having the appearance and texture of leather. | This leather-like material is actually made from recycled plastic.  这种类似皮革的材料实际上是由回收塑料制成的。| The bag is made of a leather-like fabric. 这个包是由一种类似皮革的织物制成的。 |  Near synonyms: leathery, leatherish, faux leather, imitation leather | Antonyms: cloth-like, fabric-like | adjective
