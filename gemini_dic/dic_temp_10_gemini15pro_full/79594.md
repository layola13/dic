gloriation:/ˌɡlɔːriˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 颂扬，赞美，荣耀 | The act of glorifying; extolling or praising highly; the state of being glorified. |  The gloriation of violence in the media is a serious concern. 媒体对暴力的颂扬是一个严重的问题。| 近义词: praise, exaltation, celebration, commendation | 反义词: condemnation, criticism, denigration | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *gloriātiōn- (stem of gloriātiō), from past participle stem of Latin gloriārī (“to boast, glory”), from glōria (“glory”).
