exhilarate:/ɪɡˈzɪləreɪt/ /ɛɡˈzɪləreɪt/ | vt. | 使高兴；使兴奋；使振奋 | To make someone feel very happy, animated, or elated. | The news of their victory exhilarated the entire team.  他们的胜利消息让整个团队兴奋不已。

The roller coaster ride exhilarated the children. 过山车让孩子们兴奋不已。


近义词: thrill, excite, stimulate, invigorate, enliven | 反义词: depress, discourage, dishearten, sadden | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin exhilaratus, past participle of exhilarare ("to gladden, cheer"), from Latin ex- ("out, from") + hilarare ("to make cheerful"), from hilaris ("cheerful").
