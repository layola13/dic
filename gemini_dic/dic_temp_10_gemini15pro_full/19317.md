例句：He turned into a real bible-basher after his conversion, quoting scripture at every opportunity. 他皈依后变成了一个真正的圣经死磕者，一有机会就引用经文。|近义词：hypocrite, sanctimonious, zealot|反义词：freethinker, skeptic, agnostic|词性:名词(复数)
