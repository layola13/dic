monkhood:/ˈmʌŋkhʊd/ | n. |僧侣身份，僧侣生活|The state or condition of being a monk.  A monastic life. |He entered monkhood at a young age. 他年轻时就入了僧侣之门。| Near Synonyms: monasticism, religious life, clergy | Antonyms: laity, secular life | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *munuchād (unattested), from munuc "monk" (from Late Latin monachus, from Greek monakhos "solitary," from monos "alone") + -hād "-hood."
