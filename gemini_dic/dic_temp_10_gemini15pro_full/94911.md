in-group:/ˈɪn ɡruːp/| n.| 内群体；自己人 |A social group to which a person psychologically belongs, and with which he or she identifies.  A group of people sharing a sense of belonging, mutual respect, and loyalty.|She felt comfortable and accepted within her close-knit in-group of friends. 她感到在亲密的内群体朋友中很舒服，也很被接受。|The company's in-group often made decisions without consulting other employees.  公司的小圈子经常不咨询其他员工就做出决定。|近义词：clique, circle, inner circle|反义词：out-group|词性：名词
