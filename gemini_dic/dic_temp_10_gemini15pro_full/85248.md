harmfully:/ˈhɑːmfəli/| adv.|有害地，不利地|In a way that causes harm or damage.  Acting or speaking in a way that causes damage or injury; detrimentally.|例句：Smoking affects your health harmfully. 吸烟对你的健康有害。|近义词：detrimentally, adversely, negatively, damagingly, destructively|反义词：beneficially, helpfully, positively, advantageously|词性:副词
词源: From harm + -ful + -ly.  "Harm" comes from Old English "hearm," meaning injury or grief.
