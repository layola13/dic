malacostomous:/ˌmæləˈkɒstəməs/| adj.|软口的，软嘴的|Having a soft mouth or lips; tender-mouthed.  This term is often used in zoology to describe certain fish or invertebrates.|The malacostomous fish gently sucked in the plankton.  这种软嘴鱼轻轻地吸入了浮游生物。|Synonyms: soft-mouthed, tender-mouthed|Antonyms: hard-mouthed|adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *malakos* (soft) + *stoma* (mouth)
