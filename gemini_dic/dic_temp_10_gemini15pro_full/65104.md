etherealizing:/ɪˈθɪəriəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使空灵化；使飘渺；使超凡脱俗
The process of making something ethereal; giving something a delicate and otherworldly quality.
The artist etherealized the landscape in his painting, giving it a dreamy, otherworldly feel. 艺术家在他的画作中使风景空灵化，赋予它一种梦幻般的、超凡脱俗的感觉。
spiritualizing, sublimating, refining
materializing, coarsening, solidifying
词性: 动词

ethereal:/ɪˈθɪəriəl/
adjective
空灵的；飘渺的；超凡脱俗的
Extremely delicate and light in a way that seems too perfect for this world.
Her ethereal beauty captivated everyone in the room. 她空灵的美丽吸引了房间里所有的人。
airy, celestial, heavenly
earthy, mundane, substantial
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From ethereal + -ize.  Ethereal comes from Late Latin aethereus, from Greek aithérios "of the upper air," from aithēr "upper air; bright, pure air; the sky."
