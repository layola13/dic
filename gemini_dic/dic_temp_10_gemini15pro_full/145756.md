overcultivation: /ˌoʊvərˌkʌltɪˈveɪʃən/
noun
过度耕作
Excessive cultivation of land, leading to depletion of soil nutrients and reduced fertility.
The overcultivation of the fields led to a decrease in crop yields.  过度耕作导致农作物产量下降。
Overcultivation has degraded the soil. 过度耕作使土壤退化。

近义词: overfarming, overuse
反义词: undercultivation, sustainable agriculture
词性: noun
