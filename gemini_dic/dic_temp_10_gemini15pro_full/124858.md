mucosity: /mjuːˈkɒsɪti/ /mjuːˈkɑːsɪti/
noun
粘性；粘液性
The state or quality of being mucous or viscous; sliminess.
The syrup had a high mucosity.  这种糖浆粘性很高。
Synonyms: sliminess, viscosity, stickiness, glutinousness, gumminess
Antonyms: dryness, fluidity, thinness
词性: 名词
