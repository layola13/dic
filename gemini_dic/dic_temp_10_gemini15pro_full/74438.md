francolite:/ˈfræŋkəˌlaɪt/
noun
磷灰石
A carbonate-rich variety of apatite, a phosphate mineral.
Francolite is a common constituent of phosphorites. 磷灰石是磷矿的常见成分。
Francolite often contains significant amounts of fluorine. 磷灰石通常含有大量的氟。
Synonym:apatite, phosphorite
Antonym: None (lacks a direct opposite as it is a specific mineral name)
