nightmares:/ˈnaɪtˌmerz/ (US), /ˈnaɪtmeəz/ (UK)| n. |噩梦|A frightening or unpleasant dream.|例句：I had a terrible nightmare last night. 我昨晚做了一个可怕的噩梦。

例句：She suffers from recurring nightmares about the accident. 她一直反复做关于那场事故的噩梦。

近义词：bad dreams, night terrors|反义词:pleasant dreams, sweet dreams|词性:noun
