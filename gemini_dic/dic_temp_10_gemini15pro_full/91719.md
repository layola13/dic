hypercritically:/ˌhaɪpərˈkrɪtɪkli/ | adv. | 过分批判地，吹毛求疵地 |  In an excessively critical manner, finding fault with everything. | He reviewed the play hypercritically, pointing out even the smallest flaws. 他对这部戏剧进行了吹毛求疵的评论，甚至指出了最小的缺陷。 | She hypercritically examined every detail of the contract. 她对合同的每一个细节都进行了吹毛求疵的审查。 |  overcritical, faultfinding, captious, nitpicking | uncritical, accepting, tolerant | adverb

Etymology:
From "hyper-" (Greek: ὑπέρ, hupér, "over, beyond") + "critical" (from Late Latin criticus, from Greek kritikós, "able to discern, judge," from krínein, "to separate, decide").
