overcondensations:/ˌoʊvərˌkɑːndenˈseɪʃənz/
|n.|过度冷凝；过度压缩|The process of condensation beyond a normal or desirable level, often resulting in a dense, compact, or overly thick substance.|Overcondensations in the manuscript made it difficult to read.  手稿过度压缩，难以阅读。|Overcondensation of the milk resulted in a thick, almost solid, mass.  牛奶过度浓缩，变成浓稠的、几乎固态的块状物。|Synonyms: thickening, compacting, densification|Antonyms: thinning, expansion, rarefaction|noun
