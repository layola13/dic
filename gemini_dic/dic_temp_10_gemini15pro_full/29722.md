CATIA:/kəˈtiːə/| n.|卡蒂亚|CATIA (Computer Aided Three-dimensional Interactive Application) is a multi-platform CAD/CAM/CAE commercial software suite developed by Dassault Systèmes. It is used by leading organizations in multiple industries, from aerospace to automotive, for designing, simulating, and manufacturing products.|例句：The engineers used CATIA to design the new aircraft. 工程师们使用CATIA设计了这架新飞机。|例句：CATIA offers powerful tools for 3D modeling and simulation. CATIA 提供了强大的3D建模和仿真工具。|近义词：CAD software, design software, engineering software|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
