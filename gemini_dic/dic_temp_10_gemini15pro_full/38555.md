conclusively:/kənˈkluːsɪvli/
adverb
最终地；决定性地
In a way that proves something is true, or ending any doubt.
The evidence conclusively proves his guilt.  证据确凿地证明他有罪。
The study conclusively demonstrated the link between smoking and lung cancer. 这项研究最终证明了吸烟和肺癌之间的联系。
近义词: definitively, decisively, finally, ultimately
反义词: inconclusively, doubtfully, ambiguously
词性: adverb
词源: From Late Latin *conclusivus, from Latin concludere (to conclude).
