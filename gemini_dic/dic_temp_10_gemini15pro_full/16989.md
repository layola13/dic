bastille:/bæˈstiːl/ | n. | 巴士底狱 | A fortress in Paris, built in the 14th century and used as a state prison. It was stormed by the populace on July 14, 1789, marking the start of the French Revolution. | The storming of the Bastille symbolized the end of tyranny. 巴士底狱的攻占象征着暴政的结束。

The Bastille was a symbol of royal oppression. 巴士底狱是皇权压迫的象征。 | 近义词：fortress, prison, jail | 反义词: freedom, liberty | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bastide "fortified building," from Provençal bastida, from the verb bastir "to build," from Late Latin *bastire.
