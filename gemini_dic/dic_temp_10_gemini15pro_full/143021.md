outbitching: /ˌaʊtˈbɪtʃɪŋ/
verb
胜过(某人)的刻薄; 比(某人)更爱抱怨
Surpassing someone in complaining or being critical; behaving in a more unpleasant or spiteful way than another person.
She was outbitching everyone at the party with her constant complaints about the food and the music.  她在聚会上不断抱怨食物和音乐，比任何人都更爱抱怨。
The two rivals were outbitching each other, trying to gain sympathy from their colleagues.  这两个竞争对手互相诋毁，试图赢得同事们的同情。
近义词: outdoing, outmaneuvering, surpassing, excelling in negativity or complaining
反义词: being supportive, being kind, being complimentary
词性: Verb (present participle)
