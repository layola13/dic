paracone: /ˈpærəkəʊn/ (UK) /ˈpærəkoʊn/ (US)| n. |前锥|The main cusp of an upper molar tooth.  It is the cusp situated furthest lingually and mesially.|The paracone is a key feature in identifying different mammalian species. 前锥是鉴定不同哺乳动物物种的关键特征。|The paleontologist examined the paracone of the fossil tooth. 古生物学家检查了化石牙齿的前锥。|近义词: cusp (牙尖), protocone (原锥)|反义词:  N/A|noun
