fly-bys:/ˈflaɪ baɪz/ | n. | 飞越；掠过；顺道访问 | A flight past a point, especially a close approach to a planet or other celestial body by a spacecraft in order to obtain scientific information. Also, a brief visit to a place. | The spacecraft made a fly-by of Jupiter to collect data.  这艘宇宙飞船飞越木星以收集数据。

The president made a quick fly-by of the disaster area. 总统对灾区进行了快速的顺道访问。 | 近义词: flypast, pass, visit | 反义词: landing, stay | 词性: 名词
