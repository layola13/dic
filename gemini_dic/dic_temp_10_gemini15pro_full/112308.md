lordliest:/ˈlɔːrdliɪst/ | adj. | 最尊贵的，最高贵的 | Superlative form of lordly; most lordly.  Characterized by or befitting a lord; haughty and arrogant. |  He strode into the room with the lordliest air imaginable. 他以最尊贵的姿态大步走进房间。 | The lordliest of the guests demanded the finest wine. 最尊贵的客人要求最好的酒。 | 近义词：grandest, noblest, haughtiest, most majestic | 反义词：humblest, lowliest, meekest | 词性: 形容词

词源: From lordly + -est (superlative suffix). Ultimately from Old English *hlāford (loaf-ward, "bread keeper")
