noncovetous:/nɒnˈkʌvɪtəs/ | adj. |不贪婪的，不觊觎的 | Not desiring something that belongs to another.  Free from avarice or covetousness. |  She led a noncovetous life, content with what she had. 她过着不贪婪的生活，满足于她所拥有的一切。|  He was known for his noncovetous nature, never envying the success of others.  他以不贪婪的天性而闻名，从不嫉妒别人的成功。| 近义词: content, satisfied, unambitious, unselfish | 反义词: covetous, greedy, avaricious, envious, grasping | 词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + covetous.  "Covetous" comes from Old French *coveitos, from Latin cupidus "desirous," from cupere "to desire."
