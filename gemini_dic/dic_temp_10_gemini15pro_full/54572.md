disloyalists:/dɪsˈlɔɪəlɪsts/
n.
不忠者，背叛者
People who are not loyal to a person, cause, or government.
During the American Revolution, the disloyalists remained loyal to the British crown.  在美国独立战争期间，不忠诚者仍然忠于英国王室。
The disloyalists faced persecution after the war.  战后，不忠诚者面临迫害。
synonyms: traitors, rebels, defectors, deserters
antonyms: loyalists, patriots, adherents
