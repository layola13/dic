dissolvabilities:/dɪˌzɒlvəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |溶解性，可溶性|The plural form of dissolvability, referring to the capacity of multiple substances to dissolve.  It indicates the degree to which a substance can dissolve in a solvent to form a solution.  It's often used in scientific contexts.|The dissolvabilities of the different salts were tested in the experiment.|近义词：solubilities|反义词: insolubilities|词性:名词
