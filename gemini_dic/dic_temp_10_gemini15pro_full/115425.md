mantissa:/mænˈtɪsə/| n.|尾数，假数|The mantissa of a logarithm is the fractional part. 对数的尾数是小数部分。|例句：The logarithm of 20 is 1.3010, where 1 is the characteristic and .3010 is the mantissa. 20的对数是1.3010，其中1是首数，.3010是尾数。

例句：In scientific notation, the mantissa is the part of the number that represents the significant digits. 在科学计数法中，尾数是数字中表示有效数字的部分。|近义词：significand|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mantissa "makeweight, addition," of unknown origin.  Borrowed into English in the late 16th century.
