derrick:/ˈdɛrɪk/| n.|起重机，井架|A derrick is a tall framework over a deep hole, such as an oil well, that supports the machinery used for drilling or for raising and lowering heavy objects.|The derrick loomed over the oil field. 井架耸立在油田上。|The workers used a derrick to lift the heavy equipment. 工人们用起重机吊起重型设备。|近义词：crane, hoist|反义词:|noun
