inheriting:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtɪŋ/
verb
继承，遗传
the process of receiving something (property, title, or genetic characteristics) from one's parents or predecessors.
She is inheriting her grandmother's house. 她将继承她祖母的房子。
The prince is inheriting the throne. 王子将继承王位。
Synonyms: receiving, acquiring, obtaining
Antonyms: forfeiting, relinquishing, losing
词性: 动名词 (gerund) or 现在分词 (present participle)
