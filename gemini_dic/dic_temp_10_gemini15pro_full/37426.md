commigration:/ˌkɑːmɪˈɡreɪʃən/ | n. |共同迁徙，共栖|The commigration of different species of birds to the same summer breeding grounds is a fascinating phenomenon. 不同种类的鸟类共同迁徙到同一个夏季繁殖地是一个迷人的现象。|例句：The commigration of the two tribes led them to a new fertile valley.  两个部落的共同迁徙使他们来到了一个新的肥沃的山谷。

例句：Researchers studied the commigration patterns of butterflies and their symbiotic relationship with certain plants.  研究人员研究了蝴蝶的共同迁徙模式及其与某些植物的共生关系。


|近义词：migration, joint migration|反义词:emigration, immigration|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin com- (together) + migratio (migration)
