parenchymal:/pəˈrɛŋkɪməl/ | adj. | 实质的，薄壁组织的 | Relating to or affecting the parenchyma of an organ or tissue. parenchyma: /pəˈrɛŋkɪmə/ | n. | 实质，薄壁组织 | The functional tissue of an organ as distinguished from the connective and supporting tissue. | The liver parenchyma is damaged in cirrhosis. 肝硬化损害了肝实质。 | 近义词: tissue | 反义词: stroma | 词性: noun
