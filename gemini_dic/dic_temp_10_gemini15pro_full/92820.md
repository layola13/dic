iconographist:/ˌaɪkənˈɒɡrəfɪst/ | /ˌaɪkəˈnɑːɡrəfɪst/ | n. | 图像学家；图像研究者 | A specialist in the study and interpretation of iconography (the visual images and symbols used in a work of art). | The iconographist explained the hidden meaning behind the symbols in the painting. 图像学家解释了这幅画中符号背后的隐藏含义。

| The iconographist meticulously researched the historical context of the sculpture.  图像学家仔细研究了这座雕塑的历史背景。 |  Synonyms: art historian, symbologist, image analyst | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
