inconfusion:/ˌɪnkənˈfjuːʒən/ | n. | 混乱，困惑 |A state of being confused or bewildered; disorder. |The sudden change left everyone in confusion. 突如其来的变化让每个人都陷入了混乱。|近义词：disorder, chaos, bewilderment, perplexity |反义词：order, clarity, certainty |词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from in- ‘in’ + Old French confusion or late Latin confusio(n-), from confundere ‘pour together, confuse’ (see confound).
