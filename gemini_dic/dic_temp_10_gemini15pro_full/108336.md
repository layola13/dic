lavas:/ˈlɑːvəz/ /ˈlævəz/
n.
熔岩流；火山岩
Molten rock that erupts from a volcano or fissure, or the solid rock resulting from cooling of this.
The lavas flowed down the mountainside. 熔岩流沿着山坡流下。


The volcano erupted, sending lavas and ash into the air. 火山爆发，将熔岩和火山灰喷向空中。

lava flow
volcanic rock

solidified rock


n.
词源: From Italian *lava*, from Latin *labes* ("a fall, sinking, sliding").
用法: Plural of lava.  Used to refer to multiple flows or types of lava.
