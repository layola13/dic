anti-Israeli:/ˌæntiɪzˈreɪli/
adjective
反以色列的
Opposed to the state of Israel or its policies.  This term can be considered controversial and potentially loaded, as it can be used in contexts ranging from legitimate criticism of Israeli government actions to expressions of hostility towards Israel's existence.
He was accused of making anti-Israeli remarks. 他被指控发表反以色列的言论。
近义词：anti-Zionist (although not perfectly synonymous, as Zionism refers to the movement for a Jewish state, while Israel refers to the state itself)
反义词：pro-Israeli
词性:adjective
