overthwartness:/ˌoʊvərˈθwɔːrtnəs/
noun
顽固; 故意刁难; 故意作对
The state of being overthwart; perverseness, contrariness.  A deliberate and stubborn opposition to what is desired or expected.
His overthwartness made it impossible to reach a compromise. 他固执的敌对态度使得双方无法达成妥协。
synonyms: perversity, contrariness, stubbornness, obstinacy, intractability, recalcitrance, defiance, resistance
antonyms: compliance, acquiescence, cooperation, docility, submissiveness
n.
