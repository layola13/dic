cartels:/kɑːrˈtɛlz/
n.
卡特尔；同业联盟；垄断联盟
An association of manufacturers or suppliers with the purpose of maintaining prices at a high level and restricting competition.
The drug cartels control much of the cocaine trade.  这些贩毒集团控制着大部分可卡因交易。

The international oil cartel agreed to reduce production. 国际石油卡特尔同意减产。


近义词:syndicate, combine, monopoly, trust
反义词:competition
词性:名词
