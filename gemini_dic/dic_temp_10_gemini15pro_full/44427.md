cross-posted:/ˌkrɔːsˈpoʊstɪd/ | adj. | 交叉发布的，跨平台发布的 | Posted in more than one online forum or newsgroup.  This means the same message is shared across multiple platforms or groups. | Example: My question was cross-posted to several related subreddits. 我的问题被交叉发布到几个相关的subreddit上。

Example: The job advertisement was cross-posted on LinkedIn and Indeed.  这个招聘广告在领英和Indeed上都发布了。 | 近义词: syndicated, duplicated, reposted | 反义词: original post (arguably) | 词性: adjective
