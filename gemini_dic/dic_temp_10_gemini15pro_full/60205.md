effloresce:/ˌɛfləˈrɛs/ | v. |  开花；风化；成熟；繁荣 | To lose water of crystallization and become powdery; to blossom or flourish.  | The crystals effloresced in the dry air. 这些晶体在干燥的空气中风化了。|Synonyms: bloom, blossom, flourish, thrive | Antonyms: wither, decay, decline | Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin efflorescere, from ex- (out) + florescere (to begin to flower), from flos (flower).
