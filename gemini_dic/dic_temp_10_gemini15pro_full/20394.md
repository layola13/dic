bitchily: /ˈbɪtʃɪli/
| adv. |刻薄地，恶毒地|In a bitchy manner; spitefully or maliciously.  Characterized by a nasty, spiteful, or unpleasant attitude, especially in a petty or nagging way.|She responded bitchily to his innocent question. 她刻薄地回答了他无辜的问题。|近义词：nastily, spitefully, cattily, meanly|反义词：kindly, nicely, sweetly|词性: 副词

bitchily: /ˈbɪtʃɪli/
|Etymology:| From bitch + -ily.  "Bitch" originally referred to a female dog, but evolved to become a derogatory term for a woman, often implying malice or unpleasantness.  The suffix "-ily" transforms the noun or adjective into an adverb.
|词源：| 源自 bitch + -ily。“Bitch”最初指的是母狗，但后来演变成对女性的贬义词，通常暗示恶意或令人不快。后缀“-ily”将名词或形容词转换为副词。
