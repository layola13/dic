compostable:/kɒmˈpəʊstəbl/ | adj. | 可堆肥的 | Able to be decomposed and used as compost. |  Food scraps and yard waste are compostable.  厨余和庭院垃圾是可堆肥的。

Compostable packaging is becoming increasingly popular. 可堆肥包装正变得越来越流行。 | synonyms: biodegradable, decomposable | antonyms: non-biodegradable, non-decomposable | adjective
