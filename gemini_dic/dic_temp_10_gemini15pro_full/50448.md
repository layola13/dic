depreciating:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪtɪŋ/
adjective
贬值的；跌价的
decreasing in value over time
例句：The depreciating value of the car made it difficult to sell. 这辆汽车贬值，难以出售。

例句：Depreciating assets can be a tax advantage.  贬值的资产可以是一种税收优势。


近义词：declining, diminishing, lessening, reducing, falling
反义词：appreciating, increasing, rising
词性：adjective (形容词)
