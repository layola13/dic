opisthocoelian: /ˌɒpɪsθəˈsiːliən/ | adj. | 后凹的 | Having a centrum (the main body of a vertebra) that is concave at the posterior end and convex at the anterior end. |  Opisthocoelian vertebrae are found in some amphibians and reptiles. 后凹椎骨存在于一些两栖动物和爬行动物中。| 近义词: concave posteriorly | 反义词: procoelian | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Greek *opisthen* (behind, at the back) + *koilos* (hollow).
