goethite:/ˈɡɜːθaɪt/ | n. | 针铁矿，褐铁矿 | A common iron oxyhydroxide mineral, FeO(OH), occurring in radiating, acicular, or fibrous masses, in concretionary or stalactitic forms, and as an earthy coating.  It is an important ore of iron. | Goethite is a common constituent of lateritic soils. 针铁矿是红土土壤的常见成分。| Synonym:limonite, hydrated iron oxide | Antonym: N/A | noun
