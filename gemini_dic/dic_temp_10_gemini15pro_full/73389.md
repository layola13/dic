forehand:/ˈfɔːrˌhænd/| n. adj. adv.|正手（球拍运动中）|A stroke played with the palm of the hand facing forward.  The dominant side of the body (right for a right-handed person).|He hit a powerful forehand down the line. 他打了一个强有力的正手直线球。|近义词: front hand |反义词: backhand|noun, adjective, adverb

forehand:/ˈfɔːrˌhænd/| n. adj. adv.|正面的；前面的|Situated in front or at the front.|The forehand side of a ship. 船的前部。|近义词: front, forward |反义词: rear, back|noun, adjective, adverb
