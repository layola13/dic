beefer:/ˈbiːfər/| n.|抱怨者，爱发牢骚的人|Someone who complains, especially about insignificant things.  A person who gripes or finds fault.|例句：He's such a beefer, always complaining about the food. 他真是个爱抱怨的人，总是抱怨食物不好吃。

例句：Don't be a beefer; just get on with the job.  别抱怨了，赶紧干活吧。|近义词：complainer, grumbler, whiner|反义词:praiser, optimist|Noun
