overappraising: /ˌoʊvərəˈpreɪzɪŋ/
Verb
高估
The act of assigning too high a value or importance to something.
He was overappraising his abilities and failed the exam. 他高估了自己的能力，考试不及格。
She was overappraising the value of the antique vase. 她高估了古董花瓶的价值。
Synonyms: overestimating, overvaluing, exaggerating
Antonyms: undervaluing, underestimating, downplaying
