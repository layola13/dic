derogatory: /dɪˈrɒɡətəri/ (US) /dɪˈrɒɡətri/ (UK)| adj.| 贬低的，贬损的，诽谤的|Expressive of low opinion; disparaging.  Intended to detract or diminish the importance, value, or standing of something or someone.| He made some derogatory remarks about her appearance. 他对她的外貌发表了一些贬损的言论。|近义词：disparaging, belittling, denigrating, demeaning|反义词：complimentary, flattering, praising|词性: adjective

Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin *derogatorius, from Latin derogare ‘repeal (part of a law),’ from de- ‘away, down’ + rogare ‘ask.’  The original sense was ‘detracting from,’ later ‘disparaging.’
