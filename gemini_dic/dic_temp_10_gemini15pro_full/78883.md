ginned:/dʒɪnd/
adjective|轧过的，(棉花)去籽的|Separated from the seeds, as cotton.|The ginned cotton was ready to be spun into yarn. 轧过的棉花准备纺成纱。|cleaned, processed|unprocessed, raw|adjective

Etymology->
From past participle of *gin*, from a shortened form of *engine*. Ultimately from Latin *ingenium* ("natural capacity, talent, intelligence, clever invention").
