excusablenesses:/ɪkˈskjuːzəblənəsɪz/
|n.|可原谅性；可辩解性|The excusablenesses of his lateness due to the traffic jam. 由于交通堵塞，他迟到是可以原谅的。|His excusablenesses for forgetting her birthday was accepted. 他忘记她生日的可原谅性被接受了。|近义词：forgivability, justifiability|反义词：unforgivability, inexcusability|词性:名词

excusable:/ɪkˈskjuːzəbl/
|adj.|可原谅的；可辩解的|It is excusable that he was late because of the heavy rain. 因为他下大雨迟到了，这是可以原谅的。|Her mistake is excusable under the circumstances. 在这种情况下，她的错误是可以原谅的。|近义词：forgivable, justifiable, pardonable|反义词：unforgivable, inexcusable, unpardonable|词性:形容词

excuse:/ɪkˈskjuːz/
|n. vt.|借口；理由；原谅；免除|He made an excuse for his lateness. 他为迟到找了个借口。|Please excuse my interruption. 请原谅我的打扰。|近义词：reason, justification, apology, pardon|反义词：accusation, blame|词性:名词；动词
