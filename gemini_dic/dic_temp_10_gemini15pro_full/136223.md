nonremission:/ˌnɑːnriˈmɪʃən/
n.
无缓解，未减轻，未赦免
The nonremission of her symptoms worried the doctors. 她的症状没有缓解让医生们很担心。
nonremission of sins  罪孽未得到赦免
The nonremission of the loan caused financial hardship.  贷款未得到免除造成了经济困难。

近义词: continuation, persistence,  unabatement
反义词: remission, abatement, alleviation


词性: 名词 (noun)

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + remission (from Latin "remissionem," meaning "a sending back, relaxation")
