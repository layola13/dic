cabassou:/kəˈbæsˌuː/ /kəˈbæsuː/
noun
卡巴苏，卡巴苏棕榈
A South American palm tree (Orbignya phalerata syn. O. speciosa, family Arecaceae) with hard-shelled nuts yielding an edible oil. Also called babassu, cusi.
The cabassou palm provides food, fuel, and building materials. 卡巴苏棕榈提供食物、燃料和建筑材料。
The oil extracted from cabassou nuts is used in cosmetics and cooking. 从卡巴苏坚果中提取的油用于化妆品和烹饪。
Synonyms: babassu, cusi
