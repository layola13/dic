parisyllabic:/ˌpærɪsɪˈlæbɪk/
adjective
多音节的；同音节数的
Having the same number of syllables.  Relating to or characterized by parisyllabism.
The poem employs numerous parisyllabic rhymes.  这首诗用了很多同音节数的韵。
polysyllabic, multisyllabic
monosyllabic
adjective
