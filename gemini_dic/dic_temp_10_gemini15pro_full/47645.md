debituminize: /diːbɪˈtʃuːmɪnaɪz/
verb

To remove bitumen from.
The process debituminizes the sand, making it reusable.  这个过程去除沙子中的沥青，使其可重复使用。
The refinery debituminizes heavy crude oil to extract valuable components.  炼油厂脱除重质原油中的沥青以提取有价值的成分。
