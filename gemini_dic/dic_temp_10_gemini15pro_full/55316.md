dissolvers:/dɪˈzɒlvərz/
n.|溶解器，溶解装置|A machine or apparatus used for dissolving substances, typically solids in liquids.|The dissolvers in the lab are used to prepare chemical solutions.|近义词:solvents, dissolving agents|反义词:precipitants, coagulants|词性:名词
dissolver:/dɪˈzɒlvər/
n.|溶解者，溶解剂|A substance that dissolves another substance.|Sugar is a dissolver in water.|近义词:solvent|反义词:precipitant|词性:名词
