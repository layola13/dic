castorin:/ˈkæstərɪn/| n.|蓖麻毒蛋白|A highly toxic protein, a lectin, found in the seeds of the castor bean plant (Ricinus communis). It is one of the most poisonous naturally occurring substances known.|例句：Castorin is extremely dangerous even in small amounts. 蓖麻毒蛋白即使少量也极其危险。

例句：The symptoms of castorin poisoning can include nausea and vomiting. 蓖麻毒蛋白中毒的症状可能包括恶心和呕吐。|近义词：ricin|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific toxin)|词性:noun
