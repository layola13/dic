incentivizing: /ɪnˈsɛntɪvaɪzɪŋ/
verb
激励化；刺激；以物质刺激鼓励
The act of providing incentives to encourage a particular behavior or action.
The government is incentivizing companies to invest in green technology. 政府正在激励企业投资绿色技术。
The company is incentivizing its employees to meet their sales targets. 公司正在激励员工完成销售目标。

近义词: motivating, encouraging, stimulating, prompting
反义词: discouraging, deterring, hindering

词性: v. (present participle of incentivize)
词源: From incentivize + -ing. Incentivize is a back-formation from incentive. Ultimately from Latin incentivus (“inciting, setting on fire”), from incinere (“to kindle, set on fire”).
