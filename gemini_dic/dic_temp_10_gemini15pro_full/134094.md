nonimitative:/ˌnɒnˈɪmɪtətɪv/
adjective
非模仿的，非拟声的
Not imitative; not copying or based on something else.
Her style of painting is nonimitative and highly original. 她的绘画风格是非模仿的，非常有创意。

The child's use of language was nonimitative, suggesting a natural talent.  这个孩子对语言的使用是非模仿性的，暗示了一种天赋。

synonym: original, innovative, creative, unique
antonym: imitative, derivative, copied, unoriginal
词性: adjective
