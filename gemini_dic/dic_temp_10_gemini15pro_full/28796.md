carollers:/ˈkærələrz/ (US), /ˈkærələz/ (UK)| n.|颂歌者；唱圣诞歌的人|A group of people who sing carols, especially at Christmas.|例句：The carollers went from house to house, spreading Christmas cheer. 颂歌者挨家挨户地唱着圣诞歌，传播着圣诞节的欢乐。

例句：We listened to the carollers singing in the snow. 我们听着颂歌者在雪中唱歌。|近义词：carol singers, Christmas singers|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "non-singers" or "silent observers")|词性: Noun
