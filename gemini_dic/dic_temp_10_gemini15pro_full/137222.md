nonsurety:/ˌnɑːnˈʃʊrəti/ | adj. | 非担保的；无担保的 | Not of, relating to, or backed by a surety or guarantee.  Relating to a loan or other financial obligation that is not backed by collateral or a guarantor. | Example 1: The company offers nonsurety bonds for certain types of projects.  这家公司为某些类型的项目提供非担保债券。

Example 2:  He took out a nonsurety loan with a high interest rate. 他申请了一笔高利率的无担保贷款。 | Near Synonyms: unsecured, unguaranteed | Antonyms: secured, guaranteed | Adjective
