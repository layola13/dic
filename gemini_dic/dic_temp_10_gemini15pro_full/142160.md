ork:/ɔːrk/ | n. | 逆戟鲸; 虎鲸 | A toothed whale that lives in small groups in all oceans. It is black and white and the male has a very tall dorsal fin. |  Example: We saw a pod of orcas breaching off the coast. 我们看到一群虎鲸在海岸边跃出水面。

Example: The orca is an apex predator. 虎鲸是顶级掠食者。 | Synonym: killer whale | Antonym:  (Orcas don't have a direct antonym, but perhaps "prey" in the context of the food chain) | noun
