coeditors:/ˌkoʊˈɛdɪtərz/
n.
共同编辑；合编者；助理编辑
People who edit something together, sharing the responsibility.
Example: The coeditors worked tirelessly to ensure the journal's quality. 共同编辑们不知疲倦地工作以确保期刊的质量。
Example:  The book has two coeditors, both experts in their field. 这本书有两个共同编辑，都是各自领域的专家。
Synonyms: joint editors, associate editors, contributing editors
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps) sole editor, single editor
词性: noun
