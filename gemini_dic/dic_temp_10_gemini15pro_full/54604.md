dismay:/dɪsˈmeɪ/| n. |沮丧，惊慌 | A feeling of unhappiness and disappointment. | She watched in dismay as her carefully laid plans fell apart. 她沮丧地看着自己精心制定的计划破灭了。 | 近义词: discourage, disheartened, alarm, appall | 反义词: encourage, hearten, reassure | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old French *desmayer (modern French démayer) "lose courage, give up", from des- "away" + -mayer "to hammer, beat".
