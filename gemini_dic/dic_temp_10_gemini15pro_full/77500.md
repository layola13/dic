gazette:/ɡəˈzɛt/ | n. | 公报；报纸 | An official journal or newspaper, especially one publishing official notices or legal news. | The results of the election were published in the government gazette.  选举结果刊登在政府公报上。| synonyms: journal, newspaper, periodical, publication, bulletin, newsletter | antonyms:  None (Gazette doesn't have a direct opposite) | noun

gazettes:/ɡəˈzɛts/ | n. | 公报（gazette的复数） | Plural form of gazette. | The local gazettes reported the mayor's resignation. 各地方报报道了市长的辞职。| synonyms: journals, newspapers, periodicals, publications, bulletins, newsletters | antonyms: None | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "gazette," originally a Venetian coin worth a small amount, the price of a small news sheet published in Venice in the 16th century.  The name transferred from the coin to the publication itself.
