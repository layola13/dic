exhaled:/ɛksˈheɪld/
verb
呼出, 吐气
having breathed out
例句：She exhaled slowly and tried to relax. 她慢慢地呼气并试图放松。

例句：He exhaled a cloud of smoke. 他吐出一团烟雾。

近义词：breathed out, expelled
反义词：inhaled
词性：verb
