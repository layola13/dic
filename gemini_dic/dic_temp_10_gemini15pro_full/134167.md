nonimputativenesses:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpjuːtətɪvnɪsɪz/
|n.|非归罪性，非归因性|The state or quality of not being imputable or attributable.  This word refers to the absence of assigning blame or responsibility for something.|The nonimputativenesses of the errors led to a broader investigation of the system, rather than focusing on individual blame.|近义词:  non-attribution, blamelessness, innocence|反义词: culpability, imputability, responsibility|词性: Noun
