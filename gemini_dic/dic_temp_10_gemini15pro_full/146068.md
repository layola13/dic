overeasy: /ˌoʊvərˈiːzi/ | adj. | 煎一面半熟的; 煎两面略熟的 | Fried on both sides, but with the yolk still runny. | I'd like my eggs overeasy, please. 我想要我的鸡蛋煎一面半熟。 |  I always order my eggs overeasy with bacon. 我总是点煎两面略熟的鸡蛋配培根。 | 近义词: over light, sunny-side up (partially) | 反义词: over hard, over well | 词性: 形容词

overeasy: | Etymology:  Compound of "over" and "easy."  "Over" in this context refers to flipping the egg over in the pan.  "Easy" refers to the short cooking time and runny yolk. |  |  |  |  |  |
