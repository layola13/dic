albuminosis:/ˌælbjʊmɪˈnoʊsɪs/
n.
蛋白尿；白蛋白尿
Presence of albumin in the urine, typically a symptom of kidney disease.
The doctor diagnosed albuminosis after finding high levels of protein in the patient's urine sample. 医生在病人的尿液样本中发现高蛋白后诊断为蛋白尿。

词性:noun

albuminosis:/ˌælbjʊmɪˈnoʊsɪs/
词源:From albumin + -osis. Albumin from Latin albus "white" + -in (chemical suffix). -osis from Greek -osis, denoting a condition or process, often abnormal.
中文翻译:蛋白尿；白蛋白尿
英文解析:A condition characterized by the presence of albumin in the urine, usually indicative of kidney disease.
