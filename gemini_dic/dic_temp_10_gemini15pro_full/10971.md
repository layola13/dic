arbitrated: /ˈɑːrbɪtreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 仲裁的；裁定的 | Decided by a judge or arbiter. |  The dispute was arbitrated by a neutral third party. 这场纠纷是由一个中立的第三方仲裁的。| Synonyms: judged, adjudicated, mediated, umpired | Antonyms: unmediated, unresolved, contested | adjective

arbitrate: /ˈɑːrbɪtreɪt/ | v. | 仲裁；裁决 | To act as a judge in a dispute; to settle a dispute between two parties. | The union and management agreed to arbitrate their differences. 工会和管理层同意将他们的分歧提交仲裁。 | Synonyms: adjudicate, judge, mediate, referee, umpire | Antonyms:  ignore, neglect, exacerbate | verb

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin arbitrat- ‘judged, considered’, past participle of arbitrari, from arbiter ‘judge, witness’.
