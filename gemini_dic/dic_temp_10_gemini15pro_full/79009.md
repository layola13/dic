gitano: /xiˈtano/ (Spanish), /dʒɪˈtɑːnoʊ/ (English approximation)| n. adj.|吉普赛人，吉普赛的|A member of a traditionally nomadic people originating in northern India, now living chiefly in southern Europe, the Middle East, and North Africa; Romany. Also used as an adjective to describe things related to Romani people or culture.|例句：The gitano danced with passion and fire. 吉普赛人热情奔放地跳舞。
