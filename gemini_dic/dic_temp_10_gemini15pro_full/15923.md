ballyhooed:/bəˈliˌhuːd/
adjective
大肆宣传的；大肆吹嘘的
Extensively advertised or publicized, often excessively and with exaggerated claims.
The ballyhooed new product turned out to be a disappointment.  这个大肆宣传的新产品结果令人失望。
The much ballyhooed movie was a flop at the box office. 这部被大肆宣传的电影票房惨败。
synonyms: hyped, publicized, promoted, touted
antonyms: understated, downplayed
词性: adjective
