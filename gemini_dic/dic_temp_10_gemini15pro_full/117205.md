measure:/ˈmeʒər/ (US), /ˈmɛʒə(r)/ (UK)| n. v. | 测量；措施；程度；尺寸；计量单位 |  A standard unit used to express the size, amount, or degree of something.  Also, an action taken to achieve a particular purpose. | The tailor took my measures for a new suit.  裁缝为我量尺寸做一套新西装。 | synonyms:  step, action, procedure, process, means, expedient, course, policy | antonyms: inaction, inactivity | noun

measure:/ˈmeʒər/ (US), /ˈmɛʒə(r)/ (UK)| v. | 测量；估量；衡量 | To determine the size, amount, or degree of something. |  He measured the room with a tape measure. 他用卷尺测量了房间。 | synonyms: gauge, calculate, estimate, assess | antonyms: guess, estimate (when used as an imprecise measurement) | verb
