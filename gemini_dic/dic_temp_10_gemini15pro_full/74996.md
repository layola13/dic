Friederich: /ˈfriːdərɪk/ | n. | 弗里德里希 | A German male given name, equivalent to Frederick in English. It is derived from the Germanic elements *frithu- "peace" and *rīks "ruler, mighty". | Example: Friederich Nietzsche was a German philosopher. 弗里德里希·尼采是一位德国哲学家。
Example: My grandfather's name is Friederich. 我的祖父名叫弗里德里希。 | 近义词: Frederick | 反义词: N/A | 词性: Noun
