deadliest:/ˈdedliɪst/| adj.|最致命的，死亡人数最多的|causing the most deaths; most likely to cause death|例句：The deadliest animal in the world is the mosquito. 世界上最致命的动物是蚊子。|例句：The earthquake was the deadliest in the country's history. 这次地震是该国历史上死亡人数最多的。|近义词：most lethal, most fatal, most dangerous|反义词：safest, least harmful|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dēadlīc (attested as dēadlic) "subject or liable to death, mortal; causing death, deadly, fatal," from dēad "dead" + -līc "-ly."  The superlative form "-liest" developed in Middle English.
