demonstrational:/ˌdemənˈstreɪʃənəl/
adjective
示范性的；论证性的；公开表露感情的
serving as or intended for demonstration; characterized by demonstration; openly expressive of emotions
例句：The teacher gave a demonstrational lecture on how to use the new software. 老师做了一次示范性讲座，讲解如何使用新软件。

例句：The child's demonstrational affection for his mother was heartwarming. 孩子对母亲公开表露的爱令人感到温暖。

近义词：exhibitory, illustrative, expressive, affectionate
反义词：unexpressive, reserved, inhibited
词性: adjective

demonstration:/ˌdemənˈstreɪʃən/
noun
示范；证明；游行示威
an act of showing or explaining how something works or is done; proof; a public march or meeting expressing political views
例句：The salesperson gave a demonstration of the new vacuum cleaner. 销售员演示了新的吸尘器。

例句：The protesters held a demonstration outside the government building. 抗议者在政府大楼外举行了示威游行。
近义词：exhibition, display, proof, protest, march
反义词：concealment, suppression
词性: noun

demonstrate:/ˈdemənˌstreɪt/
verb
证明；示范；表露；游行示威
clearly show the existence or truth of (something) by giving proof or evidence; show or explain how something works or is done; openly express (a feeling or quality); take part in a public demonstration
例句：The scientist demonstrated the theory with a series of experiments. 科学家用一系列实验论证了这个理论。

例句：She demonstrated her anger by slamming the door. 她摔门以示愤怒。
近义词：prove, show, exhibit, display, express, protest
反义词：conceal, hide, suppress
词性: verb
