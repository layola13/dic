blennioid:/ˈblɛniɔɪd/| n. adj.|鳚鱼；鳚科的|A fish of the family Blenniidae, characterized by a long, slender body and a continuous dorsal fin.  They are typically found in shallow marine waters, often clinging to rocks or seaweed. |Example 1: The blennioid darted into a crevice in the rock. 这条鳚鱼飞快地钻进了岩石的缝隙里。

Example 2:  The researcher studied the unique camouflage abilities of the blennioid. 研究人员研究了鳚鱼独特的伪装能力。|近义词：blenny|反义词: None|词性: noun, adjective
