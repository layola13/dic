givens:/ˈɡɪvənz/ | n. | 给定值；前提；已知条件 | Something that is assumed to be true or that is offered as a basis for argument or discussion. | The givens of the problem are that we have a limited budget and a tight deadline.  这个问题的已知条件是我们预算有限，期限很紧。 | synonyms: facts, data, premises, assumptions | antonyms: unknowns, variables | noun

givens:/ˈɡɪvənz/ | n. | 馈赠；礼物 | Something that is bestowed voluntarily and without compensation. (Less common usage) | The givens of nature are abundant and beautiful. 大自然的馈赠是丰富而美丽的。 | synonyms: gifts, presents, donations | antonyms: takings, seizures | noun
