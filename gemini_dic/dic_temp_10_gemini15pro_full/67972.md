facings:/ˈfeɪsɪŋz/
n.
饰面，覆面；（尤指衣服或家具的）贴面，镶边
a covering, especially of cloth or other material, that is put on something to make it look better or to protect it
The chair has attractive leather facings. 这把椅子的皮革饰面很漂亮。
covering, lining, veneer, overlay
backing, underside
