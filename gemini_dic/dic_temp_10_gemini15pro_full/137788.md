nonvenally:/nɒnˈviːnəli/
adv.
非静脉地；不通过静脉注射
not administered or occurring through a vein
The medication was administered nonvenally.  这种药物是非静脉注射的。
He received nonvenal nutrition for several days. 他接受了几天非静脉营养。
近义词: orally, intramuscularly, subcutaneously
反义词: intravenously, venally
