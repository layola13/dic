orthopedist: /ˌɔːrθəˈpiːdɪst/ (US) /ˌɔːθəˈpiːdɪst/ (UK)| n. |骨科医生|A medical doctor who specializes in the branch of medicine concerned with the correction or prevention of deformities, disorders, or injuries of the skeleton and associated structures (such as tendons and ligaments).|例句：I went to see an orthopedist about my knee pain. 我去看骨科医生治疗我的膝盖疼痛。

例句：The orthopedist recommended physical therapy for my injured shoulder. 骨科医生建议我为受伤的肩膀进行物理治疗。|近义词：orthopedic surgeon, bone doctor|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

词源：From ortho- +‎ -pedist, from Greek orthos ("straight", "correct") + pais ("child").  Initially the specialty focused on correcting skeletal deformities in children.  Over time, the scope broadened to include all musculoskeletal conditions in people of all ages.
