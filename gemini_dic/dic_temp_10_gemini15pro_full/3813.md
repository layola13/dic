agnosticism:/æɡˈnɑːstɪsɪzəm/ | n. |不可知论 |The view that the existence of God, of the divine or the supernatural is unknown or unknowable.| He admitted to agnosticism, doubting the existence of God. 他承认自己是不可知论者，怀疑上帝的存在。|  近义词: skepticism, doubt | 反义词: gnosticism, faith | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Ancient Greek ἀ- (a-, 'not, without') + γνῶσις (gnôsis, 'knowledge') + -ισμός (-ismós). Coined by Thomas Henry Huxley in 1869.
