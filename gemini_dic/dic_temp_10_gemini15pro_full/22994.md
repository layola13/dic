bouffe:/buːf/ (US), /bʊf/ (UK)| n.|食物; 伙食 (informal, often derogatory)|Food, often implying it is of poor quality or plentiful but unsophisticated.|例句：The bouffe at the cafeteria was barely edible.  自助餐厅的食物简直难以下咽。

例句：They served up some cheap bouffe at the party.  他们在派对上提供了一些廉价的食物。|近义词：grub, chow, food, eats|反义词:gourmet food, delicacy|词性:noun
词源:From French "bouffer" (to puff, to eat greedily).
