foveola:/fəˈviːələ/
n.
小凹，小窝，（尤指）视网膜中央凹
A small pit, cavity, or depression, especially the central pit in the retina.
The foveola is responsible for sharp central vision. 中央凹负责清晰的中央视觉。
The image of an object is focused on the foveola. 物体的图像聚焦在中央凹上。
