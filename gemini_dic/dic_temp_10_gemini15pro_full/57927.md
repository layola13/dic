drink:/drɪŋk/ | n. v. | 饮料，喝酒 |  A liquid that is swallowed to quench thirst or for enjoyment; to swallow a liquid. |  I need a drink of water. 我需要喝点水。/  He drank the whole bottle of wine. 他喝了整瓶酒。|  近义词: beverage, sip, gulp, swallow |  反义词: abstain | 词性: 名词, 动词

drink:/drɪŋk/ | v. | 喝，饮 | To consume liquid. | She drinks coffee every morning. 她每天早上喝咖啡。/ The plants need to drink water to survive. 植物需要喝水才能生存。 | 近义词: imbibe, consume, swallow | 反义词: abstain | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old English *drincan, from Proto-Germanic *drenkananą.


注意事项:  "drink" 作为名词时可数或不可数，取决于语境。 例如，"two drinks" 指的是两杯饮料，而 "drink" 泛指饮料时不可数。
