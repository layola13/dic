cross-questioning:/ˌkrɔːsˈkwɛstʃənɪŋ/ | n. | 交叉讯问，盘问 | The lawyer's cross-questioning of the witness revealed inconsistencies in her testimony. 律师对证人的交叉讯问揭示了她证词中的矛盾之处。 |  The detective's rigorous cross-questioning elicited a confession from the suspect.  侦探的严厉盘问套出了嫌疑人的供词。 | 近义词: interrogation, examination, grilling | 反义词: N/A | noun

cross-question:/ˌkrɔːsˈkwɛstʃən/ | v. | 交叉讯问，盘问 | The defense attorney cross-questioned the prosecution's key witness.  辩护律师交叉盘问了控方的主要证人。 | The police cross-questioned the suspect for hours. 警察盘问了嫌疑人几个小时。 | 近义词: interrogate, examine, grill | 反义词: N/A | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From "cross-" (meaning "across, transversely") + "questioning" (the act of asking questions). The "cross-" prefix suggests the questioning goes back and forth, challenging or seeking clarification.
