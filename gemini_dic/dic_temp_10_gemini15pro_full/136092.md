nonrecollective:/ˌnɒnˌrɛkəˈlɛktɪv/ | adj. | 非回忆性的，不涉及回忆的 | Characterized by the absence of conscious recollection or remembrance.  Relating to memory processes that do not involve conscious retrieval of past experiences. | The study focused on nonrecollective familiarity, a feeling of knowing without remembering the specific source. 这项研究的重点是非回忆性熟悉，即一种知道但记不起具体来源的感觉。

| The nonrecollective memory test assessed implicit learning.  这个非回忆性记忆测试评估了内隐学习。 | implicit, unconscious, automatic | explicit, recollective, conscious | adjective
