erratum:/ɪˈreɪtəm/ (plural: errata /ɪˈrɑːtə/)
|n.|正误表；勘误表；错误|An error in printing or writing that is later corrected. A list of errors and their corrections appended to a book or published separately.|The book contains a few errata which have been corrected in the second edition. 这本书有一些错误，已在第二版中更正。|
|近义词：correction, mistake, error, misprint, typo|
|反义词：accuracy, correctness|
|词性：名词|
