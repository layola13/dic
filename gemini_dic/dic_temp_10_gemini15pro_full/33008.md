choristic:/kɔːˈrɪstɪk/
adjective
分离的，空间分异的
Relating to or characterized by chorisis, the spatial separation of related organisms or biological phenomena.
Choristic species exhibit distinct geographical distributions.  这些空间分异的物种呈现出不同的地理分布。
The study focused on the choristic distribution of the two subspecies.  这项研究的重点是两个亚种的空间分异分布。
近义词: allopatric, disjunct
反义词: sympatric
词性: adjective
