multiplexor:/ˈmʌltɪˌpleksər/ (US), /ˈmʌltɪˌpleksə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 多路复用器，多路转换器 | A device that selects one of several analog or digital input signals and forwards the selected input into a single line. |  The multiplexor routes data from several sources to one destination.  多路复用器将来自多个源的数据路由到一个目的地。

|  This network uses a multiplexor to combine multiple data streams onto a single fiber optic cable.  该网络使用多路复用器将多个数据流组合到一根光纤电缆上。| synonym: mux, data selector | antonym: demultiplexer, demux | noun
