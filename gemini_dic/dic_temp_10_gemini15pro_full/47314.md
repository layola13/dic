de-litter:/diːˈlɪtər/
verb
清除垃圾; 清理废弃物
To remove litter from (a place).
The volunteers de-littered the park after the festival.  志愿者们在节日后清理了公园的垃圾。
The council has started a campaign to de-litter the streets.  市议会已经开始了一项清理街道垃圾的活动。
clean up, tidy up
litter, pollute
