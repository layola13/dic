arguably:/ˈɑːrɡjuəbli/ | adv. | 可以说是；大概；很有可能 |  Used to say that something is possible or likely, although it may not be certain.  | He is arguably the best player in the world. 他可以说是世界上最好的球员了。| 近义词: possibly, probably, conceivably | 反义词:  undeniably, indisputably | 词性: 副词

词源:  From the verb "argue" + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin "arguere" (to make clear, prove, accuse).
