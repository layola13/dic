hyracoidea:/ˌhaɪrəˈkɔɪdiə/ | n. |蹄兔目|An order of small, thickset, herbivorous mammals native to Africa and the Middle East. They are also known as dassies.| Hyraxes are the closest living relatives to elephants and manatees, despite their rodent-like appearance. 蹄兔是非洲和中东地区的一种小型、矮胖的食草哺乳动物。它们也被称为岩狸。 |近义词:dassies|反义词: N/A |名词
