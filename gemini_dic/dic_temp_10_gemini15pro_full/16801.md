baseborn:/ˈbeɪsbɔːrn/
adjective
私生的；出身卑贱的
Born out of wedlock; of humble or low birth.
He was taunted for being baseborn and having no claim to the throne. 他因私生子身份而被嘲笑，没有资格继承王位。
近义词:illegitimate, lowborn, common
反义词:legitimate, noble, highborn
词性:adjective

词源:
From Middle English *baseborn*, equivalent to base +‎ born.  The element *base* here carries the older meaning of "lowly" or "of low status."
