ashoka:/əˈʃoʊkə/ |n.|阿育王；无忧树|Asoka, also known as Ashoka the Great, was an Indian emperor of the Maurya Dynasty who ruled almost all of the Indian subcontinent from c. 268 to 232 BCE.  The ashoka tree is a plant species in the subfamily Caesalpinioideae of the legume family Fabaceae. |例句：Asoka is considered one of India's greatest emperors. 阿育王被认为是印度最伟大的皇帝之一。

例句：The ashoka tree is prized for its beautiful flowers. 无忧树因其美丽的花朵而备受珍视。|近义词：emperor (for Asoka as a person), tree (for ashoka as a plant)|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun
