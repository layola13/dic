dummying:/ˈdʌmiɪŋ/
v.
假装；装傻；模拟；用虚拟数据填充
Creating a simplified version of something, often for testing or practice.  Making something seem less intelligent or effective.
He was dummying up, pretending he didn't know anything. 他在装傻，假装他什么都不知道。
They dummied up a prototype for the new car. 他们制作了新车的原型。
近义词:simulating, faking, feigning, pretending
反义词:actualizing, realizing
词性:动词(v.)
