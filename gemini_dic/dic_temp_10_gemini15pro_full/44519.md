crosspiece:/ˈkrɒspiːs/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːspiːs/ (US)| n. | 横木，横档，横梁 | A horizontal piece in a structure, such as a rail between the legs of a table or a bar connecting two upright posts. | The carpenter secured the crosspiece to the uprights.  木匠把横木固定在立柱上。

The crosspiece of the ladder broke under his weight. 梯子的横档在他的重量下断了。| synonyms: crossbar, rung, transom, rail | antonyms: upright, post, vertical | noun
