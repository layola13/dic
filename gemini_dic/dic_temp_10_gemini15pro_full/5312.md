allow:/əˈlaʊ/ (US) /əˈlaʊ/ (UK)| vt.|允许，准许|To give permission for someone to do something, or to not prevent something from happening.|例句：My parents don't allow me to stay out late.  我的父母不允许我熬夜。

例句：Smoking is not allowed in the restaurant.  餐厅内禁止吸烟。

|近义词：permit, authorize, enable, let|反义词:forbid, prohibit, ban|词性:动词
