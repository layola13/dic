nonignitability:/ˌnɒnɪɡˌnaɪtəˈbɪləti/ | n. |不可燃性，不燃性 |The quality or state of being nonignitable; resistance to ignition.  The material's nonignitability makes it safe for use in high-temperature environments. 这种材料的不可燃性使其可以安全地用于高温环境。|例句：The nonignitability of the building materials is a crucial safety feature.  建筑材料的不可燃性是一项至关重要的安全特性。

例句：They tested the nonignitability of various fabrics.  他们测试了各种织物的不可燃性。|近义词：fire resistance, incombustibility |反义词：flammability, ignitability |词性:名词

nonignitable:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈnaɪtəbl/ | adj. |不可燃的，不燃的|Not capable of being ignited; not flammable.  Nonignitable materials are essential for fire safety. 不可燃材料对于防火安全至关重要。|例句：The nonignitable material prevented the fire from spreading. 不可燃材料阻止了火势蔓延。
