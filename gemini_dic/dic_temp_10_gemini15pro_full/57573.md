dramatize: /ˈdrɑːməˌtaɪz/  /ˈdræməˌtaɪz/
verb
戏剧化；改编成戏剧
To present or represent in a dramatic or theatrical manner.
The author dramatized his experiences in the war.  作者将他在战争中的经历戏剧化地呈现出来。

The novel was dramatized for television. 这部小说被改编成电视剧。

近义词: theatricalize, stage, adapt, exaggerate
反义词: downplay, minimize, understate
词性: verb
