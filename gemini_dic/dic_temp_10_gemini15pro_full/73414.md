forel:/ˈfɔːrəl/ | n. |红点鲑; 欧白鲑|A freshwater fish of the salmon family, found in lakes in Europe and North America.  It is valued as a food and game fish.|例句：We caught several forel in the lake this morning.  我们今天早上在湖里钓了几条红点鲑。

例句：Forel are popular among anglers for their fighting spirit. 红点鲑因其顽强的斗志而受到垂钓者的喜爱。|近义词：trout, char |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
