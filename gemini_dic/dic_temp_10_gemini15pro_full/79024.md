ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *given, yeven, yiven, from Old English giefan, geofan (“to give, bestow, grant, allot, commit, entrust, devote, give up, deliver”), from Proto-Germanic *gebaną (“to give”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰebʰ- (“to give, take”). Cognate with Scots gie (“to give”), West Frisian jaan (“to give”), Dutch geven (“to give”), Low German geven (“to give”), German geben (“to give”), Danish give (“to give”), Swedish giva (“to give”), Icelandic gefa (“to give”), Gothic 𐌲𐌹𐌱𐌰𐌽 (giban).
