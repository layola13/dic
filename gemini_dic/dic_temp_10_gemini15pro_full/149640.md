painterliness:/ˈpeɪntərlɪnɪs/
n.
绘画性；笔触感；绘画风格
The quality of looking like a painting, typically achieved through visible brushstrokes and a focus on the process of painting rather than precise representation.
The painterliness of the artwork gave it a unique texture and vibrancy. 这幅艺术品的绘画性赋予它独特的质感和活力。
The artist's rejection of traditional techniques resulted in works of remarkable painterliness.  这位艺术家对传统技法的摒弃，创作出了具有非凡绘画性的作品。
synonyms:  brushwork, texture, impasto, artistry, expressiveness
antonyms:  precision, realism, detail, smoothness, meticulousness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "painterly" + "-ness". "Painterly" comes from "painter" (from Old French "peintre") + "-ly". Ultimately from Latin "pingere" (to paint).
