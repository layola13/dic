half-tearful:/ˌhɑːfˈtɪərfəl/ /ˌhæfˈtɪrfl/
adjective
半含泪水的；快要哭出来的
Being almost tearful; on the verge of tears.
She gave him a half-tearful smile. 她给了他一个半含泪水的微笑。
Near synonyms: teary, tearful, close to tears
Near antonyms:  joyful, cheerful
词性: 形容词
