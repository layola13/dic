hythe:/haɪθ/
n.
港口，码头；登陆处
A landing place; a small harbor.
The hythe was bustling with activity as the fishing boats returned.  码头上随着渔船的返回而熙熙攘攘。
The ancient hythe was no longer in use, silted up and forgotten. 古老的港口已经不再使用，淤塞了，也被遗忘了。

近义词: harbor, port, wharf, dock
反义词: (无明确的反义词)
词性: 名词
