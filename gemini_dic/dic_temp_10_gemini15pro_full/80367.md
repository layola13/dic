goldylocks: /ˈɡoʊldiˌlɑːks/ | n. adj.| 金发丽儿; 恰到好处; 适中|Refers to the fairy tale character "Goldilocks and the Three Bears" or something that is "just right," not too extreme in any way.  Derived from the golden curls of the character. | The porridge was goldilocks, not too hot and not too cold.  这粥的温度刚刚好，不冷不热。 |  Near synonyms: perfect, ideal, just right | Antonyms: extreme, excessive, insufficient | noun, adjective

goldilocks: /ˈɡoʊldiˌlɑːks/ | adj.| 恰到好处的; 适中的 |  Describes something that is not too extreme, but just right.  | This chair is goldilocks – it's perfectly comfortable. 这把椅子正合适——非常舒服。 | Near synonyms:  optimal, moderate | Antonyms:  extreme, immoderate | adjective
