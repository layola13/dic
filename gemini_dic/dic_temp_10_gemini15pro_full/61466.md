embracers:/ɪmˈbreɪsərz/ | n. | 拥抱者；信奉者；接受者 | People who embrace something (an idea, a belief, a change, etc.) are embracers. |  Those who readily embraced the new technology found themselves ahead of the curve. 那些迅速接受新技术的人发现自己走在了潮流的前面。

The early embracers of electric cars faced many challenges.  电动汽车的早期拥护者面临许多挑战。 | 近义词：adopters, supporters, proponents, advocates | 反义词：opponents, resisters, detractors | 词性: noun
