dimmy:/ˈdɪmi/| adj.|昏暗的，不明亮的|Something that is dimmy is not very bright or clear.  It's often used to describe light or a place with low light.  It can also describe something that is difficult to see or understand.|The room was dimmy, lit only by a single candle. 房间里很昏暗，只有一支蜡烛照明。|The old photograph was dimmy and faded.  这张老照片昏暗褪色。|近义词：dark, faint, obscure, gloomy|反义词:bright, clear, luminous|词性:形容词
