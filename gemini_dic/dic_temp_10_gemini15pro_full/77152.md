gashful:/ˈɡæʃfʊl/
adjective
容易受伤的；易受惊吓的；胆怯的
prone to being gashed or startled; timid; shy
The gashful deer darted into the woods at the sound of the gunshot.  那只胆怯的鹿听到枪声就冲进了树林。
近义词:skittish, timid, shy, fearful, apprehensive
反义词:bold, brave, courageous, daring
词性:adjective

词源:
From "gash" (a deep cut) + "-ful" (suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by").  由“gash”（一道深长的伤口）+“-ful”（后缀，意为“充满”或“以…为特征”）组成。
