cauterize:/ˈkɔːtəraɪz/| vt.| 烧灼，使麻木|To burn the skin or flesh of (a wound) with a heated instrument or caustic substance, typically to stop bleeding or prevent infection. |The doctor cauterized the wound to stop the bleeding. 医生烧灼伤口以止血。|Synonyms: burn, sear, singe|Antonyms: heal, soothe|verb

cauterized:/ˈkɔːtəraɪzd/| adj.| 烧灼的，麻木的|Having been treated by cauterization. |The cauterized wound began to heal. 烧灼后的伤口开始愈合。|Synonyms: burned, seared, singed|Antonyms: healed, soothed|adjective
