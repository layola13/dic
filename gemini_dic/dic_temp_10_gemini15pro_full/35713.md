cocaptain:/ˈkoʊˌkæptɪn/| n.|联合队长，副队长|A cocaptain shares the responsibilities of leadership with another person, typically in a sports team.|Example:  She was elected cocaptain of the basketball team. 她被选为篮球队的联合队长。

Example: The cocaptains led the team onto the field. 两位联合队长带领球队进入赛场。|近义词：assistant captain, joint captain|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "subordinate" in a hierarchical sense)|词性:noun
