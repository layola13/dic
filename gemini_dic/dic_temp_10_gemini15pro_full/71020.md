firmest:/ˈfɜːrmɪst/| adj.|最坚定的，最牢固的，最坚决的|Superlative adjective of firm.  Meaning the most resolute, most stable, or most solid. |The coach delivered his firmest warning yet to the team about their lack of discipline. 教练就团队缺乏纪律性向他们发出了迄今为止最严厉的警告。

The firmest foundations are built on trust and respect. 最牢固的基础是建立在信任和尊重的基础上的。|近义词：strongest, most resolute, most stable, most solid|反义词：weakest, most unstable, most irresolute|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *fermest, from Old English *fyrmest, superlative of *fyrme (“firm”), from Proto-Germanic *firmiz (“fixed, firm”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰerǵʰ- (“high, elevated”). Cognate with West Frisian firmst, Dutch stevigst, German fest (“firm”).
