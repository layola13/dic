inauspiciously: /ˌɪnɔːˈspɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 不祥地，不吉利地 |  Not conducive to success; unpromising.  Characterized by or producing bad omens. | The trip began inauspiciously with a flat tire.  这次旅行一开始就不顺利，爆胎了。| The play opened inauspiciously to a half-empty theater. 这部戏的开场很不吉利，剧院里只有一半的观众。| 近义词: ominously, unfortunately, unluckily | 反义词: auspiciously, fortunately, luckily | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Latin *inauspiciosus*, from in- "not" + auspiciosus "auspicious," ultimately from *avispicium* "divination by observing the flight of birds," from avis "bird" + specere "to look at."
