词源: From Middle English *herin, from Old English hæring, from Proto-Germanic *ḫaringaz, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ker- (“to jump, spring”). Cognate with Dutch haring, German Hering. The word ultimately relates to the fish's leaping movements.
