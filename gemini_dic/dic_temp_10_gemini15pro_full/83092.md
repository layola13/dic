gushingly:/ˈɡʌʃɪŋli/
adv.
滔滔不绝地，热情洋溢地
in a gushing manner; effusively.
She gushingly praised his performance. 她热情洋溢地称赞了他的表演。
近义词：effusively, enthusiastically, exuberantly
反义词：reservedly, reticently, coldly
词性: 副词

gush:/ɡʌʃ/
v.
涌出，喷出；滔滔不绝地说
(of a liquid) flow out in a rapid and plentiful stream; speak or write effusively.
Water gushed from the broken pipe. 水从破裂的管道中喷涌而出。
She gushed about how much she loved the movie. 她滔滔不绝地讲述她有多么喜欢这部电影。
近义词：spout, pour, effuse
反义词：trickle, drip
词性: 动词

gush:/ɡʌʃ/
n.
涌出，喷射；迸发
a sudden rapid outflow of liquid; an unrestrained expression of feeling.
There was a sudden gush of blood from the wound.  伤口突然涌出大量鲜血。
Her letter was full of gushes of sentimentality. 她的信充满了感伤的迸发。
近义词：outpouring, surge, burst
反义词：trickle, drip
词性: 名词

词源:  From gush (v.), perhaps of imitative origin.
