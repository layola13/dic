hesitative:/ˈhezɪtətɪv/| adj.|犹豫的，迟疑的|inclined to hesitate; uncertain or slow in acting or speaking|She gave a hesitative smile. 她犹豫地笑了笑。|近义词：uncertain, tentative, reluctant, hesitant|反义词：decisive, confident, resolute|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *haesitativus, from Latin haesitatus (past participle of haesitare “to stick fast, hesitate”), from haerere “to stick.”
