crystallized:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪzd/ /ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪzd/ | adj. v. | 结晶的；明确的；定型的 |  Formed into crystals; having a definite and well-established form.  | The honey crystallized in the jar.  蜂蜜在罐子里结晶了。| The plan crystallized in her mind.  这个计划在她脑海中逐渐清晰。| 近义词：solidified, concretized, defined, formed | 反义词: amorphous, fluid, vague, nebulous |词性: 形容词 或 动词(过去式/过去分词)

crystallize:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪz/ /ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 结晶；使明确；使定型 | To cause to form crystals or assume a crystalline form; to make or become definite and clear. |  Cool the solution to crystallize the compound. 冷却溶液使化合物结晶。|  His vague ideas began to crystallize into a concrete plan. 他模糊的想法开始形成一个具体的计划。| 近义词：solidify, concretize, define, form | 反义词: dissolve, melt, liquefy |词性: 动词
