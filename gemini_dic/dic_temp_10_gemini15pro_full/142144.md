originator:/əˈrɪdʒəneɪtər/ | n. | 发起人，创始人，创作者 | A person who creates or initiates something. |例句：He was the originator of the project. 他是这个项目的创始人。

例句：She is recognized as the originator of this new style of painting. 她被公认为这种新绘画风格的创始人。

近义词：founder, creator, initiator, inventor, pioneer | 反义词：follower, imitator | 词性：noun

originate:/əˈrɪdʒəneɪt/ | v. |起源于，发起，创立 | To begin; to create or initiate something. |例句：The idea originated with him. 这个想法是他提出的。

例句：The custom originated in the Middle Ages.  这个习俗起源于中世纪。


近义词：begin, start, initiate, create, invent, derive | 反义词：end, finish, conclude, terminate, copy | 词性：verb
