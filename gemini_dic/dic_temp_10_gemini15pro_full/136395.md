nonresurrections:/ˌnɑːnˌrɛzəˈrɛkʃənz/
noun
非复活，未复活（复数）
The absence of resurrections; instances where something or someone has not been brought back to life or restored after death or destruction.
The museum showcased artifacts from civilizations lost to time, testaments to countless nonresurrections.  博物馆展示了失落文明的文物，证明了无数的未复活。
The theory posited a universe of endless cycles of creation and destruction, with most beings experiencing nonresurrections.  该理论假设了一个由无尽的创造和毁灭循环组成的宇宙，大多数生命体都经历了未复活。
Synonym: non-revivals
Antonym: resurrections
