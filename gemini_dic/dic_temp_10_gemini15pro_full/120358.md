minimized:/ˈmɪnɪmaɪzd/| v. |最小化；使减到最小|To reduce something to the smallest possible amount or degree. |The company minimized its losses by selling off the failing division. 公司通过出售亏损部门将损失降到最低。|

The new design minimized the risk of mechanical failure. 新的设计最大限度地降低了机械故障的风险。|近义词：reduced, decreased, lessened, curtailed|反义词: maximized, increased, amplified, expanded |verb (past tense)


minimized:/ˈmɪnɪmaɪzd/| adj. |最小化的|Reduced to the smallest possible amount or degree. |The program runs in a minimized window. 程序在一个最小化的窗口中运行。|

The impact of the storm was minimized by timely evacuations.  暴风雨的影响由于及时的疏散而被最小化了。|近义词：small, tiny, minimal, reduced|反义词: maximized, large, huge, expanded |adjective


词源: From the verb minimize, derived from the adjective minimal, ultimately from Latin *minimus*, superlative of *minus* ("less").
