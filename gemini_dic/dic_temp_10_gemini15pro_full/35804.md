cockeye:/ˈkɑːkaɪ/| adj. n.|斜视的，斜视眼|Having one eye that looks in a different direction than the other; a squinting eye.  A person with a squinting eye.|He had a noticeable cockeye. 他有一只很明显的斜视眼。|近义词：squint, cross-eyed|反义词: straight-eyed|词性:形容词, 名词

cockeye:/ˈkɑːkaɪ/| v.|斜视|To look at with a squinting eye.|He cockeyed the stranger suspiciously. 他怀疑地斜视着那个陌生人。|近义词：squint, leer|反义词: stare directly|词性:动词
