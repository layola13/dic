dungiest:/ˈdʌndʒiɪst/
adjective
最肮脏的
Superlative form of dungy: most dungy. Characterized by filth and squalor; extremely dirty.
The orphan lived in the dungiest corner of the city. 这个孤儿住在城市里最肮脏的角落。
The dungiest alley in the city was surprisingly home to a thriving community of street artists.  城市里最肮脏的小巷竟然是一个欣欣向荣的街头艺术家社区的所在地。
Synonyms:filthy, grimy, squalid, sordid
Antonyms:clean, pristine, spotless
词性:形容词
