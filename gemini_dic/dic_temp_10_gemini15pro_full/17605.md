beauts:/ˈbjuːts/ /bjuːts/| n.|美人;美好的事物;极好的东西|Informal plural of beauty.  Something excellent or pleasing, especially a beautiful woman.|例句：Those vintage cars are real beauts. 那些老爷车真是漂亮。

例句：She's one of the beauts of the campus. 她是校园里的美女之一。|近义词：beauties, stunners, knockouts|反义词：uglies, eyesores|词性:名词
