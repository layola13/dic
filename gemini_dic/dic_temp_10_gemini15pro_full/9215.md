antioxygenation:/ˌæntiˌɑːksɪdʒəˈneɪʃən/ |n.|抗氧化，去氧|The process of removing oxygen or preventing oxidation. This term is less common than "antioxidation".|Antioxygenation is important in food preservation to prevent spoilage.  抗氧化在食品保存中很重要，可以防止腐败变质。|Antioxygenation helps maintain the color and flavor of certain foods. 抗氧化有助于保持某些食物的颜色和味道。|Near synonyms: antioxidation, deoxygenation|Opposite: oxidation|noun
