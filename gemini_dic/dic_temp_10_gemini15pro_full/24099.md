breviped:/ˈbrɛvɪpɛd/| adj. n.|短脚的，短脚动物|Having short legs; an animal with short legs. |The breviped penguin is known for its short legs. 短脚企鹅以其短腿而闻名。|The museum displayed the skeleton of a prehistoric breviped. 博物馆展出了一个史前短脚动物的骨骼。|近义词：short-legged|反义词:long-legged|词性:adjective, noun
Etymology: From Latin *brevis* (short) + *pes, pedis* (foot).
