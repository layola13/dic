misfeeding:/ˌmɪsˈfiːdɪŋ/
|n.| 喂养不当；营养不良|The misfeeding of infants can lead to serious health problems. 婴儿喂养不当会导致严重的健康问题。|Misfeeding can stunt a child's growth. 喂养不当会阻碍孩子的生长。|malnutrition, underfeeding, overfeeding|proper feeding, adequate nutrition|noun

misfeed:/ˌmɪsˈfiːd/
|vt.|  喂养不当；（机器）供料不当|The farmer misfed his livestock, resulting in illness. 农民喂养牲畜不当，导致牲畜生病。|The printer misfed the paper, causing a jam. 打印机供纸不当，导致卡纸。|malnourish, undernourish, overfeed|nourish properly, feed adequately|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (meaning "wrong" or "badly") + feed (meaning "to give food to").  The prefix "mis-" is of Old English origin, while "feed" comes from Old English *fēdan.
