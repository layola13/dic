Houthis: /ˈhuːθiz/ | n. | 胡塞武装；胡塞运动 |  A Shiite Muslim group from northern Yemen, officially called Ansar Allah (Partisans of God), that has been fighting against the Yemeni government. | The Houthis have controlled Sanaa, Yemen's capital, since 2014.  胡塞武装自2014年以来一直控制着也门首都萨那。|  Synonyms: Ansar Allah, Houthi rebels | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider opposing forces like the Saudi-led coalition) | Noun
