malign:/məˈlaɪn/ | adj.  v. |恶意的，恶性的；诽谤，中伤 |  (adj.) speaking about someone in a spitefully critical manner; (v.) to speak harmful untruths about;  | 例句：He made malign comments about his competitor. 他对他的竞争对手发表了恶毒的评论。 | 近义词：evil, harmful, malicious, slander, defame | 反义词：benevolent, benign, praise | 词性：形容词，动词
malign:/məˈlaɪn/ | v. |诽谤，中伤 | to speak harmful untruths about; | 例句：She was maligned by the press. 她被媒体诽谤了。| 近义词：slander, defame, vilify | 反义词：praise, commend | 词性：动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *malignen, from Old French malignier, from Late Latin malignare (“to act wickedly”), from Latin malignus (“wicked, bad”), from male (“badly”) + genus (“kind, sort”).
