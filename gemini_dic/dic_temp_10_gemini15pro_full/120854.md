misbehaved:/ˌmɪsbɪˈheɪvd/
adjective
行为不端的，举止失礼的
Having behaved badly.
The misbehaved child was sent to his room. 这个行为不端的孩子被送到他的房间去了。
Near Synonyms: naughty, unruly, disobedient, badly behaved, troublesome, wayward
Antonyms: well-behaved, obedient, good
词性: 形容词
