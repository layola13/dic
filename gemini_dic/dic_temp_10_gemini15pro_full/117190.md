measure up:/ˈmeʒər ʌp/ | phrasal verb | 符合标准；达到预期；与…相匹敌 | to be good enough, or as good as someone or something else |  The new software didn't measure up to our expectations.  这款新软件没有达到我们的预期。| 近义词：meet expectations, fulfill requirements, match up, compare favorably, be equal to | 反义词：fall short, fail to meet expectations, be inferior to | 词性：动词短语

measure up:/ˈmeʒər ʌp/ | phrasal verb | 测量；估量 | to find the size, length, or amount of something | We need to measure up the room to see how much carpet we need. 我们需要测量房间的大小，看看我们需要多少地毯。| 近义词：measure, gauge, assess, evaluate | 反义词：N/A | 词性：动词短语
