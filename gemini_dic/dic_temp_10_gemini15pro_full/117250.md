mechanical:/məˈkænɪkl/| adj.|机械的；力学的；呆板的；无意识的|Relating to machines or machinery; operated or produced by a machine or machinery; done without thought; automatic.|例句：The car had a mechanical failure. 这辆车发生了机械故障。|近义词：automatic, motorized, involuntary|反义词：manual, deliberate, spontaneous|词性:形容词

mechanical:/məˈkænɪkl/| n.|机械部件；机修工|A mechanic; a machine component.|例句：He's a whiz with mechanicals. 他是个机械高手。|近义词：machine, apparatus, mechanism|反义词：N/A|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mechanicus, from Greek mēkhanikos (“of or belonging to machines, inventive”), from mēkhanē (“machine, contrivance, device”).
