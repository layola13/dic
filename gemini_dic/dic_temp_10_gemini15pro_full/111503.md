loamiest:/ˈloʊmiɪst/| adj.|最肥沃的，壤土最多的|Having the most loam; consisting of or resembling loam to the greatest extent. |The loamiest soil in the garden produced the largest vegetables. 花园里最肥沃的土壤种植出了最大的蔬菜。|近义词：richest, most fertile, most productive|反义词：leanest, poorest, barren|词性:形容词
词源：From loam + -y + -est.  Loam comes from Old English *lām, of uncertain origin.
