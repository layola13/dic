hypogamous:/haɪˈpɒɡəməs/
adjective
下娶的，嫁给社会地位较低者的
Marrying a person of lower social standing or status.
She made a hypogamous marriage, choosing a partner from a less privileged background than her own. 她下嫁了，选择了一个比她出身低微的伴侣。
近义词:  marrying down
反义词: hypergamous
词性: adjective
