nonluminously:/ˌnɒnˈluːmɪnəsli/ | adv. | 非发光地，不发光地 | Not emitting light; in a way that does not produce or reflect light. | The objects moved nonluminously across the night sky.  这些物体在夜空中非发光地移动。| 近义词: dimly, faintly, obscurely | 反义词: brightly, luminously | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  not, absence of  (来自拉丁语 non)
luminous (adjective): emitting light  (来自拉丁语 luminosus,  来自 lumen "light")
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
