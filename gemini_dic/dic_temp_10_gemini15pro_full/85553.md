hatchettite:/ˈhætʃɪtaɪt/
n.
斧石
A monoclinic-prismatic mineral containing arsenic, calcium, and oxygen.
Hatchettite is a rare mineral found in igneous rocks. 斧石是一种罕见的火成岩矿物。
Hatchettite was first discovered in Scotland. 斧石最早在苏格兰被发现。
Synonym:calcium arsenate hydrate
Antonym: N/A
词性: noun
