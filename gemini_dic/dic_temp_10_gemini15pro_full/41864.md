coscenarist: /koʊˈsiːnərɪst/ /kəʊˈsiːnərɪst/
noun
共同编剧, 合著者
A coscenarist is a person who writes a screenplay with another writer or writers.
The coscenarist accepted the award for the film.  这位共同编剧接受了这部电影的奖项。
He worked as a coscenarist on several successful films. 他在几部成功的电影中担任共同编剧。
Synonyms: co-writer, collaborator
Antonyms: sole writer
词性: 名词
