The English word "infinite" is a direct borrowing from the Latin *infinitus*.|The universe seems infinito./宇宙似乎是无限的。 The concept of infinito is fascinating./无限的概念很吸引人。|近义词：boundless, endless, limitless, immeasurable|反义词:finite, limited, bounded|adjective, noun
