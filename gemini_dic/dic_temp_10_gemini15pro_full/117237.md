mebibit:/ˈmɛbɪbɪt/
noun
兆位元(Mibit)
A mebibit is a unit of information or computer storage equal to 2^20 bits, or 1,048,576 bits.  It is a member of the set of binary prefixes defined by the International Electrotechnical Commission (IEC).
My internet connection has a download speed of 10 mebibits per second. 我的网速是每秒10兆位元。
近义词:megabit (though not precisely equivalent)
反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)
词性:n.
