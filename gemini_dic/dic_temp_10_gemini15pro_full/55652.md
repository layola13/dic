dithionic:/ˌdaɪθaɪˈɒnɪk/
adjective|二硫的；含二硫酸根的|Relating to or containing dithionic acid (H2S2O6), which contains a sulfur-sulfur bond.  Characterized by the S2O62− anion.|Dithionic acid is unstable at room temperature. 二硫酸在室温下不稳定。|Near Synonyms: disulfuric, disulfurous |Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "monosulfuric" if context allows)
