nonvenomousnesses:/ˌnɒnˈvɛnəməsnəsɪz/
noun
无毒性(复数)
The state or quality of being nonvenomous; lack of venom.
Snakes can be categorized based on their nonvenomousnesses.  蛇可以根据它们的无毒性来分类。
The guide pointed out the nonvenomousnesses of several local snake species.  导游指出了几种当地蛇类的无毒性。
Synonym: nontoxicity, harmlessness
Antonym: venomousness, toxicity
