button:/ˈbʌtn/ (US) /ˈbʌtn/ (UK)| n. v.|按钮，纽扣；扣上|A small, usually round object that is sewn onto clothing and used for fastening, or that is pushed to operate a device.|She pressed the button to call the elevator. 她按下按钮叫电梯。|近义词：switch, knob, fastener|反义词: (As a verb) unbutton, undo|noun, verb
