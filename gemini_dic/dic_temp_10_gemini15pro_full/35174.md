cloy:/klɔɪ/
verb
使腻;使过饱;使厌烦
to make someone feel that they have had too much of something pleasant, especially something sweet
The sweetness of the dessert began to cloy. 这道甜点的甜味开始让人发腻。
sicken, surfeit, satiate
stimulate, excite
