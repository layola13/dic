outrebound:/ˌuːtrɪˈbaʊnd/ | adj. | 外跳的，向外的 |  Traveling or moving outward or away from a central point, especially in basketball, referring to a rebound secured by a player on the offensive team. | The outrebound allowed the team to maintain possession and score. 外跳的篮板球让球队保持了控球并得分。| 近义词: offensive rebound | 反义词: defensive rebound | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "outrebound" is a combination of "out-" (meaning outside or beyond) and "rebound" (the act of a ball bouncing back after hitting a surface).  It's a relatively modern term, primarily used in basketball.
