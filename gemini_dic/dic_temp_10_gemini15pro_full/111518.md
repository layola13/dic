loanword:/ˈloʊnˌwɜːrd/ /ˈləʊnˌwɜːd/ | n. |外来词| A word adopted from one language and incorporated into another language without translation. | Example: "Karaoke" is a loanword from Japanese.  “卡拉OK”是一个日语外来词。

Example:  The English word "algebra" is a loanword from Arabic.  英语单词“algebra”（代数）是一个阿拉伯语外来词。| 近义词: borrowing, adopted word | 反义词: native word | 词性: noun
