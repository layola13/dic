cabochon:/ˈkæbəʃɒn/ (US) /ˈkæbəʃɒn/ (UK)| n. |凸圆形宝石|A polished gem or bead with a rounded convex top and a flat or slightly convex base.|例句：She wore a ring with a large cabochon ruby. 她戴着一枚镶有凸圆形大红宝石的戒指。

例句：The cabochon emerald caught the light beautifully. 这颗凸圆形祖母绿捕捉光线非常漂亮。

|近义词：dome, bead, gem|反义词:|noun
