overclaimed:/ˌoʊvərˈkleɪmd/
verb
过度索赔，索赔过高
to have claimed or requested more than is justified or accurate.  To demand excessive compensation or credit.
The contractor overclaimed for materials and labor. 承包商对材料和人工过度索赔。
The insurance company suspected the claimant had overclaimed for the damage to their car. 保险公司怀疑索赔人对汽车损坏的索赔过高。
近义词: exaggerated, inflated, padded
反义词: underclaimed
