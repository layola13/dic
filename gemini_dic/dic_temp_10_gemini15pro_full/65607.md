evanescent:/ˌɛvəˈnɛsənt/ | adj. | 短暂的，转瞬即逝的 | Quickly fading or disappearing; lasting for only a short time. | The evanescent beauty of the rainbow captivated everyone. 彩虹转瞬即逝的美丽吸引了所有人。

| The fireworks created an evanescent display of light and color. 烟花创造了转瞬即逝的光与色的展示。 | 近义词: fleeting, ephemeral, transient, momentary | 反义词: permanent, enduring, lasting, perpetual | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *evanescentem*, present participle of *evanescere* "to vanish," from *ex-* "out" + *vanescere* "to vanish," related to *vanus* "empty."
