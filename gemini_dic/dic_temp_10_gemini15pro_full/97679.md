inhospitablenesses:/ˌɪnhɑːspɪˈtæbəlnəsɪz/
|n.|不友好；冷淡；荒凉；不适宜居住|The state or quality of being inhospitable; multiple instances of inhospitability.  指不友好的状态或性质；多次出现的不友好的情况。|The inhospitablenesses of the Arctic deterred many explorers. 北极的恶劣环境阻挡了许多探险家。|近义词：unfriendliness, hostility, bleakness, barrenness|反义词：hospitability, friendliness, warmth, fertility|词性: 名词

inhospitable:/ˌɪnhɑːˈspɪtəbl/
|adj.|不友好的；冷淡的；荒凉的；不适宜居住的|Not welcoming or generous to guests; providing no shelter or sustenance; (of a place) difficult to live in because of the climate or terrain.|The villagers were inhospitable to strangers. 村民对陌生人不友好。|近义词：unfriendly, hostile, bleak, barren|反义词：hospitable, friendly, welcoming, fertile|词性: 形容词
