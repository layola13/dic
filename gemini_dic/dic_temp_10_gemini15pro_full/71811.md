fleeciest:/ˈfliːsiɪst/| adj.|最羊毛似的，最柔软的|Superlative form of "fleecy," meaning covered with or resembling fleece; very soft and woolly.|The fleeciest blanket I own is perfect for cold winter nights. 我最柔软的毯子最适合寒冷的冬夜。|近义词：softest, woolliest, fluffiest|反义词:coarsest, roughest|词性:形容词
词源:From "fleece" (羊毛), from Old English *flys, from Proto-Germanic *fleusą.  The "-y" suffix makes it an adjective (fleecy), and "-est" creates the superlative form (fleeciest).
