gamophyllous:/ˌɡæməˈfɪləs/| adj.|合瓣的；合生的|Having the petals united, at least at the base, forming a tube or funnel.  The petals are fused together to form a single unit.|Example: The morning glory flower is gamophyllous, its petals forming a distinct funnel shape. 牵牛花是合瓣的，它的花瓣形成了一个独特的漏斗形状。

Example:  Gamophyllous flowers are often seen in plants belonging to the Asteraceae family.  在菊科植物中经常可以看到合瓣花。|近义词：sympetalous|反义词：polypetalous|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *γαμο- (gamo-) "marriage, union,"  + *φύλλον (phyllon) "leaf."  Thus, "united leaves" (referring to petals).
