adeptist:/ˈæd.əp.tɪst/ | n. |  行家，专家，熟练者 | A person who is skilled or proficient in something. | He's an adeptist at playing the piano. 他是一位钢琴演奏的行家。

He is an adeptist in the art of negotiation. 他是谈判艺术的专家。

| 近义词: expert, proficient, skilled, master, virtuoso | 反义词: novice, beginner, amateur | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *adept*, from Latin *adeptus* "having attained, achieved," past participle of *adipisci* "to attain," from *ad* "to" + *apisci* "to reach."
