glycerin:/ˈɡlɪsərɪn/ /ˈɡlɪsərɔːl/| n.|甘油；丙三醇|A sweet, syrupy liquid obtained from animal fats and oils or by the fermentation of glucose. It is used in medicines, explosives, and antifreeze.|Glycerin is commonly used in skincare products. 甘油常用于护肤品。|Synonyms: glycerol, glycerine|Antonyms: None|Noun

glycerol:/ˈɡlɪsərɔːl/| n.|甘油；丙三醇|The technical or chemical term for glycerin.|Glycerol is a trihydric alcohol. 甘油是一种三元醇。|Synonyms: glycerin, glycerine|Antonyms: None|Noun
