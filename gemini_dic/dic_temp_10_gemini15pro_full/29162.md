cash: /kæʃ/ | n. v. | 现金；兑现 | Money in the form of coins or notes, as distinct from checks, money orders, or credit.  To exchange a check, etc. for cash. | Example: I need some cash for the bus. 我需要一些现金坐公共汽车。| Example:  I need to cash this check. 我需要兑现这张支票。 | Synonyms: money, currency, funds | Antonyms: credit, debt | noun, verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *casse (box, money chest), from Latin capsa (box).
