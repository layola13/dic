inscriptible:/ɪnˈskrɪptɪbl/
adjective
可刻写的，可题写的
able to be inscribed or written on, especially suitable for bearing an inscription.
The monument was made of inscriptible marble. 这座纪念碑由可刻字的大理石制成。

non-inscriptible
