multipropellants:/ˌmʌltiprəˈpɛlənts/| n. |多组元推进剂|A rocket propellant consisting of two or more substances fed separately to a combustion chamber.|The new rocket utilizes multipropellants for increased efficiency.|Synonyms: composite propellants|Antonyms: monopropellants|noun
