bondable:/ˈbɒndəbl/| adj.|可担保的，可抵押的|Suitable to be bonded; capable of being bonded.  Relating to someone who is considered trustworthy and reliable enough to be offered a surety bond, especially in a position handling money or valuables.|The company requires all employees handling cash to be bondable. 这家公司要求所有处理现金的员工都要有担保。|近义词：reliable, trustworthy, dependable|反义词：untrustworthy, unreliable, risky|词性:形容词
