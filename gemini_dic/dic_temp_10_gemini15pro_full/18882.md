besiegement: /bɪˈsiːdʒmənt/
n.
围攻，包围；困扰
The city was under siege for months.  这座城市被围困了数月。
The castle was subjected to a long and brutal besiegement. 这座城堡遭受了漫长而残酷的围攻。
English explanation: The state of being besieged; the act of besieging.  A prolonged period of being surrounded by armed forces aiming to capture a place or force its surrender. Also refers to a persistent feeling of being overwhelmed or harassed.
Example: The constant demands of her job left her feeling under a besiegement of stress. 她工作的持续高要求让她感到压力重重。
Synonyms: siege, blockade, beleaguerment
Antonyms: liberation, relief, rescue
词性: 名词
