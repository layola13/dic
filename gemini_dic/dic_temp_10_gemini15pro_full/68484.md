familyhood:/ˈfæmɪlihʊd/
n.
家庭状态，家庭关系
The state or condition of being a family.
例句：They embraced the joys and challenges of familyhood. 他们拥抱了家庭生活的快乐和挑战。

例句：Familyhood brings with it a unique set of responsibilities. 家庭生活带来了一系列独特的责任。

近义词：kinship, family life
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly isolation, solitude)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From family + -hood.  "Family" comes from Old French "famille" (household), ultimately from Latin "familia" (household, servants). "-hood" is an Old English suffix denoting "state, condition, quality."
