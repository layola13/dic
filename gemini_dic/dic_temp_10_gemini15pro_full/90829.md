hunched:/hʌntʃt/| adj.| 驼背的，弓着身的|Curved or bent over, especially of the back and shoulders.|The old woman sat hunched over her knitting. 老妇人弓着背坐在那里织毛衣。|近义词：stooped, bent, crouched|反义词:straight, erect|词性:形容词
hunched:/hʌntʃt/| v.| 耸肩，弓背|To raise (one's shoulders) and bend the top of one's body forward.|He hunched his shoulders against the cold wind. 他耸起肩膀抵御寒风。|近义词：slouch, stoop, crouch|反义词:straighten|词性:动词
