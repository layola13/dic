audio-visuals: /ˌɔːdiəʊˈvɪʒuəlz/ /ˌɑːdioʊˈvɪʒuəlz/ | n. |视听教材，视听设备| Materials such as film, video, and recordings used in education and presentations, or the equipment used to present them. | The teacher used audio-visuals to make the lesson more engaging. 老师使用视听教材使课程更具吸引力。| synonyms: multimedia, audiovisual aids | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "text-based materials") | noun


audio-visual: /ˌɔːdiəʊˈvɪʒuəl/ /ˌɑːdioʊˈvɪʒuəl/ | adj. |视听的| Relating to both hearing and sight. | The conference room had state-of-the-art audio-visual equipment.  会议室配备了最先进的视听设备。| synonyms:  aural-visual | antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From audio + visual. "Audio" comes from Latin "audire" (to hear), and "visual" comes from Latin "videre" (to see).
