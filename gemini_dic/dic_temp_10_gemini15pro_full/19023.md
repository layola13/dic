beta-carotene:/ˈbeɪtə ˈkærəˌtiːn/ | n. | β-胡萝卜素 | An orange-red pigment found in fruits and vegetables, especially carrots, that is converted to vitamin A in the body. |  Example: Beta-carotene is an important antioxidant. β-胡萝卜素是一种重要的抗氧化剂。

Example:  Many fruits and vegetables are rich in beta-carotene. 许多水果和蔬菜富含β-胡萝卜素。 | Synonym: provitamin A | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound) | noun
