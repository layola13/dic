mutes:/mjuːts/
n.|静音; 弱音; 弱音器; 闭口音|People playing instruments with mutes produce a softer sound. 使用弱音器演奏乐器的人会发出更柔和的声音。|近义词: silences, dampers, hushes |反义词:  amplifiers, loudspeakers|词性:名词

mutes:/mjuːts/
v.|使静音; 减弱…的声音; 抑制; 缓和|He muted the television during the phone call. 他在打电话时把电视静音了。|近义词: silences, softens, quiets|反义词: amplifies, increases|词性:动词
