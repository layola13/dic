disequilibrium's:/ˌdɪsɪkwɪˈlɪbriəmz/ | n. | 不平衡，失衡 |A state of imbalance or instability; lack of equilibrium.  It refers to a situation where forces or factors are not balanced, resulting in instability or change. | The sudden drop in demand created a disequilibrium in the market. 需求的骤降造成了市场的不平衡。 | The government's intervention aimed to restore equilibrium to the disrupted economy. 政府的干预旨在恢复混乱经济的平衡。| imbalance, instability, disproportion | equilibrium, balance, stability | noun
