packs:/pæks/
n.|包裹，背包，一群，一副|A collection of items packaged together; a rucksack; a group of animals. |She brought three packs of cookies to the party. 她带了三包饼干来参加聚会。|bundles, sets, groups|individuals, singles
词性:名词

packs:/pæks/
v.|打包，塞满，挤满|(third-person singular simple present packs, present participle packing, simple past and past participle packed) To put things into a container or bag; to fill a space tightly. |He packed his suitcase for the trip. 他为旅行打包行李。|fills, crams, loads|empties, unpacks
词性:动词
