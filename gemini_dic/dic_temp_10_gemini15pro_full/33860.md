circumscribe:/sɜːrkəmˈskraɪb/ /sɝːkəmˈskraɪb/| vt.|限制；画…的外接圆；在…周围画线|To draw a line around; to restrict something within limits.|The king's power was circumscribed by the constitution. 国王的权力受宪法限制。|The circle circumscribes the square.  这个圆外接这个正方形。|近义词：restrict, limit, confine, define, encompass|反义词：expand, broaden, enlarge|动词

circumscribed:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌskraɪbd/ /ˈsɝːkəmˌskraɪbd/| adj.|受限制的；外接的|Restricted or limited; drawn around a geometric figure so as to touch as many points as possible.|His life was circumscribed by poverty and illness. 他的生活受到贫困和疾病的限制。|A circumscribed circle is drawn around a polygon. 一个外接圆画在一个多边形周围。|近义词：limited, restricted, confined|反义词：unlimited, unrestricted, broad|形容词
