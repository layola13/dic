nonfluenter:/nɒnˈfluːəntər/ | adj. | 不流利的 | Not fluent; lacking fluency, especially in speaking or writing a language. |  He is a nonfluenter speaker of English. 他英语说得不流利。| 近义词: halting, faltering, hesitant | 反义词: fluent, eloquent | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "fluent" (from Latin "fluentem," present participle of "fluere" meaning "to flow") + "-er" (suffix forming a comparative adjective, although in this case used to indicate a person with the quality).
