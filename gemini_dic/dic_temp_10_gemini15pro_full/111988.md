lone:/loʊn/| adj.|孤独的，单独的，独自的|Being the only one; solitary.|He was a lone voice of dissent. 他是唯一一个持异议的人。|近义词：solitary, single, alone, isolated|反义词：accompanied, together, multiple|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *lone, *al one (“all one, single”), equivalent to all + one. Cognate with Scots alane (“alone, only”), German allein (“alone”). More at all, one.
