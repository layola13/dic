outrogue: /ˌaʊtˈroʊɡ/
verb
超过，胜过（在恶行或大胆方面）
To surpass or exceed in roguery or daring.
He outrogued even the most seasoned criminals with his audacious plan. 他那大胆的计划，甚至让那些老练的罪犯都自叹不如。
The young spy outrogued her rivals by infiltrating the enemy's headquarters.  这位年轻的间谍通过潜入敌军总部，在狡猾程度上胜过了她的对手。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel, outwit
反义词: be outdone, be surpassed

词性: v.

词源:  "out-" (meaning "exceeding") + "rogue" (a dishonest or unprincipled person).  由表示“超过”的前缀“out-”和表示“流氓”或“无赖”的名词“rogue”组成。
