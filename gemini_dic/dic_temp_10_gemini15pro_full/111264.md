litigiousness:/lɪˈtɪdʒəsnəs/ | n. |好诉讼，爱打官司 |The tendency to engage in lawsuits or legal disputes; readiness or inclination to sue. |The company's litigiousness has created a hostile work environment.  这家公司的好诉讼风气制造了一种充满敌意的工作环境。|The lawyer was known for his litigiousness, taking on even the smallest cases.  这位律师以好诉讼而闻名，即使是最小的案件他也会接。|Synonyms: disputatiousness, quarrelsomeness, contentiousness |Antonyms: peacefulness, agreeableness, amicability |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From litigious + -ness.  Litigious comes from the Latin word *litigiōsus*, meaning "quarrelsome, disputatious".
