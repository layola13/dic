disguisers:/dɪsˈɡaɪzərz/
n.
伪装者；掩饰者
People who disguise themselves or things that disguise something.
The disguisers were so skilled that no one recognized them. 伪装者们如此熟练，以至于没有人认出他们。
The thieves used sophisticated disguisers to avoid detection. 小偷们使用精密的伪装工具来躲避侦查。
synonyms: camouflagers, maskers, concealers
antonyms: revealers, exposers
noun
