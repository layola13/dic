exsufflicate: /ˌɛksˈsʌflɪkeɪt/
verb
使…蔑视，轻视；使…贬低；使…变得无价值
to deprive of value or importance; to make contemptible or insignificant.
The constant criticism from her boss exsufflicated her and made her question her abilities. 老板不断的批评使她感到自己被轻视，并开始怀疑自己的能力。
His plagiarism exsufflicated his academic work. 他的剽窃行为使他的学术成果变得一文不值。
近义词: disparage, belittle, denigrate, depreciate
反义词: exalt, elevate, praise, commend
词性: verb
