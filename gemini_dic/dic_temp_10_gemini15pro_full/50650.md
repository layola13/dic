deritualizing:/diːˈrɪtʃuəlaɪzɪŋ/
| v. |去仪式化，使非仪式化|The process of removing the ritualistic aspects of something, making it less formal or ceremonial.  |Modern society is deritualizing many traditional customs, such as formal courtship.  现代社会正在使许多传统习俗去仪式化，例如正式的求爱。| The museum is deritualizing the display of artifacts, focusing on their social context rather than their sacred significance.  博物馆正在使文物的展示去仪式化，重点关注它们的社会背景，而不是它们的神圣意义。|desacralizing, secularizing|ritualizing|verb
