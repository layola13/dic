augmentatively:/ɔːɡmɛnˈteɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 增大地，增强地 | In a way that augments or increases something; so as to augment. | He spoke augmentatively about the company's future. 他夸大地谈论了公司的未来。| 近义词: increasingly, expansively | 反义词: diminishingly, decreasingly | 词性: 副词

augmentatively:/ɔːɡmɛnˈteɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 增强地，扩大地 |  Making something greater by adding to it; enlarging. |  She described her accomplishments augmentatively, emphasizing every detail. 她添油加醋地描述了她的成就，强调了每一个细节。| 近义词: exaggeratively, hyperbolically | 反义词: restrictively, reductively | 词性: 副词

词源:  From Late Latin *augmentativus, from Latin augmentatus, past participle of augmentare "to increase," from augmentum "increase," from augere "to increase."
