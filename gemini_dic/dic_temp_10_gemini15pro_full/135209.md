nonostensibly:/ˌnɒnɒˈstɛnsɪbli/
adverb
不明显地，不公开地
Not obviously or openly; in a way that is not apparent or readily perceived.
He nonostensibly slipped the note into her hand. 他不露痕迹地把纸条塞到她手里。
Synonyms:  inconspicuously, subtly, discreetly, covertly, secretly
Antonyms:  ostensibly, openly, visibly, noticeably, conspicuously
词性: 副词
