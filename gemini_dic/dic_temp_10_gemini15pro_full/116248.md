mass-produced:/ˌmæs prəˈduːst/ | adj. | 大量生产的；批量生产的 | Produced in large quantities, typically by machinery. |  例句：Mass-produced cars are generally cheaper than hand-built ones. 大量生产的汽车通常比手工制造的汽车便宜。

例句：The factory mass-produced toys for the holiday season. 这家工厂为假期大量生产玩具。

近义词：factory-made, manufactured, bulk-produced | 反义词：hand-made, handcrafted, bespoke, custom-made | 词性: 形容词

mass-produce:/ˌmæs prəˈdjuːs/ | v. | 大量生产；批量生产 | To manufacture (a product) in large quantities, typically by machinery. | 例句：The company mass-produces clothing for several major retailers. 这家公司为几家主要的零售商大量生产服装。

例句：They plan to mass-produce this new type of computer. 他们计划大量生产这种新型计算机。

近义词：manufacture, produce in bulk, churn out | 反义词：handcraft, custom-make | 词性: 动词
