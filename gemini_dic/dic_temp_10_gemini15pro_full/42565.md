counterpart:/ˈkaʊntərˌpɑːrt/ | n. |对应的人或物；副本；极相似的人或物|a person or thing that has the same function or position as another in a different place or situation|The US Secretary of State met his Chinese counterpart in Geneva. 美国国务卿在日内瓦会见了中国外交部长。|Synonyms: equivalent, peer, parallel, match, twin|Antonyms: opposite, contrast|Noun
