expensively:/ɪkˈspɛnsɪvli/
adverb
昂贵地
In a way that costs a lot of money.
She dresses expensively. 她穿着昂贵。
The house was expensively decorated. 房子装修得很豪华。
Synonyms:  dearly, lavishly, richly
Antonyms: cheaply, economically, inexpensively
词性: 副词
