gladdener:/ˈɡlædnər/ | n. | 使人高兴的人或事物 | Something or someone that brings joy or happiness. |  Her smile was a gladdener to all who saw it. 她的笑容使所有看到的人都感到高兴。|  The flowers were a welcome gladdener in the hospital room.  这些花在病房里受到了欢迎，令人感到高兴。 | synonyms: cheerer, comforter, delight | antonyms: depressant, downer | noun
