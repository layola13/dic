home-grown:/ˌhoʊmˈɡroʊn/
adjective
自家种植的；本土的；国产的
grown at home or in one's own country; not foreign or imported
例句：We only use home-grown vegetables in our restaurant. 我们餐厅只使用自家种植的蔬菜。
例句：The company relies on home-grown talent.  这家公司依赖本土人才。
近义词：local, domestic, indigenous
反义词：foreign, imported, exotic
词性: adjective
