overutilized: /ˌoʊvərˈjuːtəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 过度使用的，过度利用的 |  Used too much; excessively utilized.  It describes something that has been used more than is necessary or desirable, often leading to inefficiency or strain. | The overutilized server crashed due to the heavy load.  服务器因负载过重而崩溃。|  The company's overutilized workforce was suffering from burnout.  公司过度使用的员工正遭受倦怠之苦。| overused, overworked | underutilized, underused | adjective
