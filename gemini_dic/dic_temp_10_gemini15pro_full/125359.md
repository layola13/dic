multilocular:/ˌmʌltiˈlɒkjʊlə(r)/ /ˌmʌltiˈlɑːkjələr/| adj.|多室的，多房的|Having or divided into many small chambers or compartments.|The multilocular cyst was identified through ultrasound. 通过超声波识别出多房囊肿。|Synonyms: multicellular, polylocular|Antonyms: unilocular|adjective
multilocular:/ˌmʌltiˈlɒkjʊlə(r)/ /ˌmʌltiˈlɑːkjələr/|词源: From Latin multi- 'many' + loculus 'small place, compartment', diminutive of locus 'place'.|词性: 形容词
