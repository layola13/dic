hikers:/ˈhaɪkərz/| n.|徒步旅行者；远足者|People who go for long walks in the countryside or mountains, especially for pleasure or exercise.|例句：The hikers enjoyed the scenic views from the mountaintop. 徒步旅行者们从山顶欣赏风景。

例句：Many hikers carry backpacks with essential supplies. 许多徒步旅行者背着装有必需品的背包。|近义词：walkers, ramblers, trekkers|反义词:couch potatoes, homebodies|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hike + -er (agent suffix).  "Hike" likely originated from an earlier form "hich," meaning to hoist or lift, suggesting the action of lifting one's feet while walking a long distance.
