nontheosophic:/ˌnɒnˌθiːəˈsɒfɪk/
adj.
非神智学的, 不属于神智学的
Not relating to or characteristic of theosophy; not theosophical.
His nontheosophic beliefs led him down a different spiritual path. 他的非神智学信仰引领他走上了一条不同的精神道路。
The group's discussions were strictly nontheosophic, focusing on practical matters.  小组的讨论严格地非神智学，专注于实际问题。
近义词:non-theosophical
反义词:theosophic, theosophical


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + theosophic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "theosophic" refers to theosophy, a religious philosophy and movement originating in the late 19th century.  Theosophy itself comes from the Greek "theos" (god) and "sophia" (wisdom).
