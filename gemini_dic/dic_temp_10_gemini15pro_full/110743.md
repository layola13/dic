lingering:/ˈlɪŋɡərɪŋ/| adj.|挥之不去的，持续的，流连的|remaining for a long time; slow to disappear|例句：A lingering smell of smoke filled the room. 屋子里弥漫着一股挥之不去的烟味。|例句：She gave him a lingering kiss. 她给了他一个缠绵的吻。|近义词：remaining, persistent, enduring, abiding|反义词：fleeting, transient, ephemeral|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *lingren, from Old Norse *lengja (“to lengthen, prolong”), from langr (“long”). Cognate with Danish længe (“long”), Swedish länga (“to long, yearn”).
