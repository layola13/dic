chemiglyphic: /ˌkɛmɪˈɡlɪfɪk/
adjective
化学雕刻的; 化学蚀刻的
relating to or denoting a process of etching using chemicals
Chemiglyphic processes are used in the production of microchips.  化学蚀刻工艺用于生产微芯片。
Chemiglyphic techniques are crucial for creating intricate designs on metal surfaces. 化学雕刻技术对于在金属表面创建复杂设计至关重要。
近义词: chemical etching, etched
反义词:  None (缺乏直接的反义词)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "chemi-" (combining form of "chemical") + "glyphic" (from Greek *gluphē* meaning "carving").
