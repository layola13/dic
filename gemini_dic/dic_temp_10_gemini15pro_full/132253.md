nondeclaratory:/ˌnɒndɪˈklærətɔːri/
adjective
非声明式的，不作声明的
Not making or constituting a declaration; not declaratory.
The agreement contained a nondeclaratory provision regarding future investments. 该协议包含一项关于未来投资的非声明性条款。
The court issued a nondeclaratory judgment. 法院发布了一项非声明性判决。
Near Synonyms:  nondisclosing, tacit, implicit, unexpressed
Antonyms: declaratory, explicit, expressed
词性: adjective
