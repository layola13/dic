ceded:/ˈsiːdɪd/ | v. |割让，放弃，让与|to give something such as power, land, or a right to someone else, especially unwillingly or because you are forced to do so|Mexico ceded California to the US in 1848. 墨西哥在1848年把加利福尼亚割让给了美国。|synonyms: relinquish, surrender, yield, concede | antonyms: retain, keep, acquire, gain | verb
