horticulturalist: /ˌhɔːrtɪˈkʌltʃərəlɪst/ /ˌhɔːrtɪˈkʌltʃərəlɪst/
n.
园艺家；园艺师
A person who practices horticulture; a gardener, especially one skilled in the science of horticulture.
The horticulturalist expertly pruned the rose bushes.  园艺师熟练地修剪了玫瑰丛。

近义词: gardener, plantsman, cultivator
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who neglects or destroys plants)
