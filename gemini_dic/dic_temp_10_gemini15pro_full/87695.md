hexametrize:/hɛkˈsæmɪtraɪz/| v. |使成六音步, 以六音步写|To write or compose in hexameter, a line of verse containing six metrical feet.|He hexametrized the ancient Greek poem. 他将古希腊的诗歌改写成了六音步诗。|She hexametrized her own English translation of the Iliad. 她将她自己翻译的英文版《伊利亚特》改写成了六音步诗。|synonyms: versify, compose in hexameter|antonyms: N/A|verb

hexametrization:/hɛkˌsæmɪtraɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |六音步化, 以六音步写|The act of writing or composing in hexameter.|The hexametrization of the poem gave it a stately rhythm.  这首诗的六音步化赋予了它庄重的节奏。|The hexametrization of English verse is a challenging task. 将英文诗歌改写成六音步是一项具有挑战性的任务。|synonyms: versification|antonyms: N/A|noun
