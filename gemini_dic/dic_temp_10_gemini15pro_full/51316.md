detainees:/ˌdiːteɪˈniːz/
|n.|被拘留者；在押人员|People held in custody, typically by the authorities.|The detainees were held without charge for several weeks.  被拘留者们未经指控就被关押了几个星期。|Synonyms: prisoners, captives, inmates|Antonyms: liberators, rescuers|Noun

detainee:/ˌdiːteɪˈniː/
|n.|被拘留者；在押人员|A person held in custody, typically by the authorities.|The detainee was allowed one phone call.  这名被拘留者被允许打一个电话。|Synonyms: prisoner, captive, inmate|Antonyms: liberator, rescuer|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "detain," which comes from Old French "detenir," and ultimately from Latin "detinere" (to hold back, keep).
