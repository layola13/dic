colour-coded:/ˈkʌlə(r) ˌkəʊdɪd/ | adj. | 颜色编码的，用颜色标记的 |  Marked or distinguished by different colours, often to represent different categories or values.  This system allows for quick visual identification and organization of information. | The wires are colour-coded to make them easier to identify. 电线是用颜色编码的，以便更容易识别。

The map was colour-coded to show different elevation levels.  地图用颜色编码来显示不同的海拔高度。 | synonyms: color-keyed, color-differentiated | antonyms: unlabeled, unmarked | adjective
