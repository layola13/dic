anti-reducing:/ˌænti rɪˈd(j)uːsɪŋ/ | adj. | 抗还原的，阻止还原的 | Preventing or inhibiting reduction (a chemical reaction involving the gain of electrons).  This term describes substances or conditions that prevent other substances from being reduced. |  The anti-reducing agent prevented the formation of the desired product.  抗还原剂阻止了所需产物的形成。

The solution was treated with an anti-reducing buffer to maintain the oxidized state of the compound.  该溶液用抗还原缓冲液处理以保持化合物的氧化态。 | Near synonyms:  antioxidant, reducing inhibitor | Antonyms: reducing, reductant | Adjective
