inflicted:/ɪnˈflɪktɪd/
verb
施加，使遭受，造成
to cause (something unpleasant or painful) to be suffered by someone or something
例句：The typhoon inflicted severe damage on the coastal areas. 台风给沿海地区造成了严重的破坏。

例句：He inflicted his anger on his family. 他把怒气发泄在了家人身上。

近义词：impose, cause, wreak
反义词：alleviate, soothe, relieve
词性：v.
