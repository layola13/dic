over-loving:/ˈoʊvərˌlʌvɪŋ/
adj.
过度溺爱的，过分疼爱的
showing or expressing too much love, often to a degree that is harmful or detrimental.
She was an over-loving mother, which made her children dependent. 她是一位过度溺爱的母亲，这使得她的孩子们很依赖她。
Over-loving grandparents showered their grandchildren with gifts. 过度疼爱的祖父母给孙子孙女们送了很多礼物。

近义词:overprotective, smothering, doting
反义词:neglectful, uncaring
词性:adjective


词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive, too much") + "loving" (present participle of "love")
