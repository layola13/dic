nucleoidioplasma:/ˌnjuːkliːɔɪdiˈoʊˌplæzəm/ | n. | 核质体基质；拟核基质 | The nucleoidioplasma is the substance within the nucleoid of a prokaryotic cell, similar to the cytoplasm of a eukaryotic cell but containing the genetic material.  | The bacterial nucleoidioplasma contains the chromosome and associated proteins. 细菌的核质体基质包含染色体和相关的蛋白质。 | The composition of the nucleoidioplasma is crucial for gene regulation. 核质体基质的组成对于基因调控至关重要。 | 近义词: bacterial cytoplasm, protoplasm | 反义词: cytoplasm (in eukaryotes) | 词性: noun
