epipubic:/ˌɛpɪˈpjuːbɪk/ | adj. |耻骨上的，位于耻骨上的| Situated on or relating to the epipubis, a pair of small bones projecting forward from the anterior margin of the pubis in certain mammals. | The epipubic bones are present in marsupials and monotremes.  有袋动物和单孔目动物都存在耻骨上骨。|  Epipubic bones provide support for the abdominal pouch in marsupials.  耻骨上骨为有袋动物的育儿袋提供支撑。 |  suprapubic |  - | adjective
epipubis:/ˌɛpɪˈpjuːbɪs/ | n. | 耻骨上骨 | A pair of small bones projecting forward from the anterior margin of the pubis in certain mammals. |  The epipubis is a characteristic feature of marsupials. 耻骨上骨是有袋动物的一个特征。 | The epipubis helps support the abdominal pouch. 耻骨上骨有助于支撑腹袋。|  prepubic bone | - | noun
