ensconce:/ɪnˈskɒns/ (UK), /ɪnˈskɑːns/ (US)| vt.|安置；使…舒适地坐定；隐藏|To settle securely or snugly; to place or hide securely.|She ensconced herself in a comfortable armchair with a good book. 她舒适地坐在扶手椅上，手里拿着一本好书。|The thief ensconced the stolen jewels in a secret compartment. 小偷把偷来的珠宝藏在一个秘密隔间里。|近义词：settle, nestle, install, tuck, shelter, conceal|反义词：expose, dislodge, remove|词性：动词
