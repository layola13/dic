bawled:/bɔːld/ | v. | 放声痛哭，大声叫喊 | to cry or shout loudly | The child bawled when his toy broke. 孩子玩具坏了就放声大哭。|  The coach bawled instructions to the players. 教练大声喊叫着给球员们发出指示。 | 近义词: cried, sobbed, yelled, shouted | 反义词: whispered, murmured | verb

bawl:/bɔːl/ | v. |  大声叫喊，放声痛哭 | to shout loudly, to cry noisily | "Get out!" he bawled. “滚出去！”他大吼道。 | The baby bawled all night. 婴儿哭了一整夜。 | 近义词: yell, shout, scream, cry, weep | 反义词: whisper, murmur | verb

词源:
From Middle English *baullen*, of imitative origin.  Related to Old Norse *baula* (“to bellow”).
