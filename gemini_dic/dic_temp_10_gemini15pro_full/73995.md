fortuitousness:/fɔːrˌtuːɪtəsnəs/
n.
偶然性；意外性
The quality of being fortuitous; accidental occurrence.
The fortuitousness of the meeting surprised them both. 这次会面的偶然性让两人都感到惊讶。
近义词：accidentality, chance, randomness, serendipity
反义词：intentionality, design, purposefulness
词性: 名词

fortuitous:/fɔːrˈtuːɪtəs/
adj.
偶然的；意外的；幸运的
Happening by accident or chance rather than design.
It was a fortuitous meeting that led to their collaboration.  一次偶然的相遇促成了他们的合作。
近义词：accidental, chance, lucky, serendipitous
反义词：intentional, deliberate, planned
词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *fortuitus, from Latin forte 'by chance', from fors 'chance'.
