atrocha:/ɑːˈtroʊtʃə/ |n.|小路，小径; (尤指拉美国家) 狭窄的土路|A narrow trail or path, especially in Latin American countries. |We followed the atrocha through the dense jungle. 我们沿着这条小路穿过茂密的丛林。|The atrocha led us to a hidden waterfall. 这条小路带我们来到了一个隐秘的瀑布。|近义词:path, trail, track, lane|反义词:highway, road|词性:名词


词源:  Spanish, from Vulgar Latin *ruptura, from Latin rumpere ‘to break’.  Originally referring to a breach or opening in a wall or fence, it later came to mean a small path or track.
