castrel:/ˈkæstrəl/ | n. |红隼，茶隼|A small falcon (Falco tinnunculus) common in Europe, Asia, and Africa, known for its habit of hovering in the air while hunting. |The castrel hovered over the field, searching for prey.  红隼在田野上空盘旋，寻找猎物。|Synonyms:  kestrel, windhover |Antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a specific animal) | Noun
