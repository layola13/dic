copple:/ˈkɒpəl/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpəl/ (US)| n. |碗状物，小碗，顶，圆锥形物|A bowl-shaped or rounded top or tip, often conical.  A copple can also refer to a small bowl or vessel.|The copple of the hill offered a stunning view. 山顶呈现出一幕壮丽的景色。|近义词：top, summit, peak, apex, bowl, cup|反义词:bottom, base|词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *copel, from Old English *copel, a diminutive of *cupp (“cup”).  Related to cup.
