evocatively:/ɪˈvɑːkətɪvli/
adverb
唤起地；引起回忆地；使人想起地
in a way that brings strong images, memories, or feelings to mind.
The music was evocatively beautiful, stirring deep emotions within the listeners. 这音乐优美动人，激起了听众内心深处的情感。
She evocatively described her childhood in the countryside. 她生动地描述了她在乡村的童年。
Synonyms: suggestively, reminiscently, poignantly, vividly
Antonyms: blandly, uninterestingly, flatly
词性: 副词
