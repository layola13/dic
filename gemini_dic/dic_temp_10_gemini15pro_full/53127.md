diploic:/dɪˈpləʊɪk/ | adj. |板障的，颅骨板的| Relating to the diploe, the spongy layer of bone between the inner and outer tables of the skull. | The diploic veins drain blood from the skull.  板障静脉排出颅骨中的血液。|  近义词:  cranial, bony | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

diploë:/ˈdɪpləʊiː/ | n. |板障，颅骨板| The spongy layer of bone between the inner and outer compact layers of the skull. |  The diploë contains bone marrow and provides structural support. 板障包含骨髓并提供结构支撑。| 近义词: spongy bone, cancellous bone | 反义词: compact bone | 词性: noun
