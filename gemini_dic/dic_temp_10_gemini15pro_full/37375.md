commentable:/ˈkɒmɛntəbəl/ (US) /kɑːˈmɛntəbl/| adj.|可评论的，值得评论的|Something that is commentable is suitable for or worthy of comment. |The blog post was highly commentable, sparking a lively discussion. 这篇博文非常值得评论，引发了热烈的讨论。|The judge's decision is commentable. 法官的判决值得评论。|近义词:discussable,remarkable, noteworthy|反义词:unremarkable, insignificant|词性:形容词

comment:/ˈkɒment/ (US) /ˈkɑːment/| n. v.|评论，注释；评论，发表意见|A comment is a written or spoken remark expressing an opinion or reaction.|He made no comment on the allegations. 他对这些指控没有发表任何评论。|She commented on the beauty of the scenery. 她评论了景色的美丽。|近义词:remark, observation, note|反义词:silence|词性:名词，动词

-able:/əbl/| suffix.|可…的，能…的|A suffix that forms adjectives meaning "able to be," "capable of being," "suitable for being," or "tending to."|This chair is foldable. 这把椅子是可折叠的.|The situation is manageable. 情况是可控制的。|近义词:capable, possible|反义词:incapable, impossible|词性:后缀
