okaying:/oʊˈkeɪɪŋ/
v.
批准，认可
Giving approval or authorization to something.
The manager is okaying the budget proposal. 经理正在批准预算提案。
He okayed the project to proceed. 他批准了该项目的进行。
近义词: approving, authorizing, sanctioning, endorsing
反义词: disapproving, rejecting, vetoing, denying
词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "okay" + "-ing" (present participle suffix). "Okay" itself has a disputed etymology, with one popular theory suggesting it originated as an abbreviation of "oll korrect" (all correct) in the 19th century.
