meadowy:/ˈmedəʊi/ (UK) /ˈmedəʊwi/ (US)| adj.|草地般的，像草地的|Resembling or characteristic of a meadow; having the qualities of a meadow, such as being grassy, open, and peaceful.|The air was sweet with the scent of wildflowers in the meadowy valley. 空气中弥漫着草地上野花的芬芳。|Synonyms: grassy, meadow-like, pastoral, verdant|Antonyms: barren, arid, desolate|adjective
