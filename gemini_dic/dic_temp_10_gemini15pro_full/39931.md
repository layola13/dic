constricts:/kənˈstrɪkts/| v. |收缩，压缩，限制|Makes something narrower or tighter; restricts. |The snake constricts its prey. 蛇收缩身体缠住猎物。|The government's new policy constricts free speech. 政府的新政策限制了言论自由。|近义词：compress, squeeze, tighten, restrict|反义词：expand, dilate, widen, relax|词性:动词


词源: From Latin *constringere*, from *com-* (together) + *stringere* (to draw tight).
