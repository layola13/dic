liquation:/lɪˈkweɪʃən/
n.
熔解;液化;熔化
The process of separating components of a mixture by partial melting.
The liquation of the metal allowed for the removal of impurities.  金属的熔化可以去除杂质。
melting, fusion, dissolving
solidification, freezing
