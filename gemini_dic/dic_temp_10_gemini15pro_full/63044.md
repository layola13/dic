enrich:/ɪnˈrɪtʃ/ /ɛnˈrɪtʃ/| vt. |使富足，使丰富，充实，强化|To improve or enhance the quality or value of something by adding something to it.|例句：Reading enriches the mind. 阅读能充实头脑。|近义词：enhance, improve, augment, supplement|反义词：impoverish, deplete|verb

enrich:/ɪnˈrɪtʃ/ /ɛnˈrɪtʃ/| vt. |浓缩，加浓|To increase the proportion of a particular isotope in a material.|例句：The uranium was enriched to weapons-grade levels. 铀浓缩到武器级别。|近义词：concentrate, fortify|反义词：dilute|verb
