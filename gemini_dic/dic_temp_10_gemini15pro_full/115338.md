mano-a-mano:/ˌmɑːnoʊ ə ˈmɑːnoʊ/ | adj. adv.| 肉搏的；一对一的 |  Spanish, literally "hand to hand." Describing a confrontation or contest conducted between two people, especially one involving direct physical contact or a close, intense rivalry. | The two rivals faced each other in a mano-a-mano duel. 这两位对手进行了一场一对一的决斗。|近义词：one-on-one, head-to-head, face-to-face |反义词: group, collective | 词性: 形容词，副词
