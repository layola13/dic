costarred:/kəʊˈstɑːd/ | v. | 共同主演 |  Acted together in a film or play.  | Example 1:  She costarred with Tom Hanks in the movie. 她和汤姆·汉克斯共同主演了这部电影。

Example 2: He has costarred with several famous actresses throughout his career.  在他的职业生涯中，他与几位著名的女演员合作演出。 | 近义词: acted alongside, shared the screen with | 反义词: solo performance |词性: 动词
