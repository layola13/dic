impaling:/ɪmˈpeɪlɪŋ/
| n. |刺穿，穿透|The act of piercing something with a sharp pointed object.|The impaling of the insect on the thorn was a gruesome sight.  昆虫被刺穿在荆棘上，景象十分可怕。|近义词：piercing, spearing, transfixing|反义词:extricating, removing|noun


impale:/ɪmˈpeɪl/
| vt. |刺穿，穿透|To pierce with a sharp pointed object; to fix on a sharp stake.|The knight impaled the dragon with his lance.  骑士用他的长矛刺穿了龙。|近义词：pierce, spear, transfix|反义词:extricate, remove|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *empaler*, from en- "in, on" + pal "stake," from Latin pālus.
