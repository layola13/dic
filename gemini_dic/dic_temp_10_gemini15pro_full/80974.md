graciosity:/ɡrəˈsɪsəti/ /ɡreɪˈʃiːəsəti/| n.| 小费；赏钱；恩惠；好处| A tip given to someone for a service; a small sum of money given as a token of appreciation, especially to someone providing a service.|  The waiter received a generous graciosity. 服务员收到了一笔丰厚的小费。|  He accepted the graciosity with a smile. 他笑着收下了赏钱。|Synonyms: tip, gratuity, baksheesh, largess|Antonyms: stinginess, miserliness|Noun
