neurotubule: /ˌnʊəroʊˈtjuːbjuːl/ /ˌnjʊərəʊˈtjuːbjuːl/ | n. | 神经微管 | A microscopic tubular structure present in the cytoplasm of neurons, composed of tubulin, and involved in intracellular transport and maintaining cell shape. | Neurons rely on microtubules for transporting essential molecules and organelles. 神经元依靠微管来运输必需的分子和细胞器。

Microtubule dysfunction can lead to various neurological disorders.  微管功能障碍会导致各种神经系统疾病。


近义词: None (highly specific term) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源: neuro- (from Greek *neuron* nerve) + tubule (from Latin *tubulus* small pipe)
