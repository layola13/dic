non-metal:/ˈnɒnˌmɛt(ə)l/ (US) /ˈnɒnˌmetl/ (UK)| n. |非金属|An element that doesn't have the characteristics of a metal (such as hardness, luster, or the ability to conduct heat or electricity).|Oxygen is a non-metal. 氧气是一种非金属。| Argon is a chemically inert non-metal. 氩气是一种化学惰性非金属。|Synonyms: nonmetallic element|Antonyms: metal|noun
