counterplot:/ˈkaʊntərplɒt/ (US) /ˈkaʊntəplɒt/ (UK)| v.|反计，对抗阴谋，密谋反对|To secretly plan against another plan.|They counterplotted to overthrow the dictator. 他们密谋推翻独裁者。|Synonyms: scheme, plot, intrigue, conspire|Antonyms: cooperate, collaborate|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- (against) + plot (a secret plan).  The word "plot" comes from Old French "complot," of uncertain origin, possibly related to "pelote" (ball).
