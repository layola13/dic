listeria:/lɪˈstɪəriə/| n. |李斯特菌|A genus of rod-shaped bacteria that are parasites of warm-blooded animals and can cause infections such as listeriosis. |Example 1: Listeria can cause serious illness in pregnant women. 李斯特菌会导致孕妇患上严重的疾病。

Example 2: The contaminated food was found to contain listeria. 受污染的食物被发现含有李斯特菌。|近义词: bacterium (in a broader sense)|反义词: None (as it's a specific genus of bacteria)|词性: noun
