intercommissions:/ˌɪntərkəˈmɪʃənz/
n.
相互委托；相互佣金；相互间的佣金
The intercommissions between the two companies ensured a steady flow of business. 两家公司之间的相互佣金确保了业务的稳定流动。
The complex system of intercommissions between various departments made accounting difficult. 各部门之间复杂的相互佣金系统使会计工作变得困难。
Synonym: cross-commissions, mutual commissions
Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider "independent commissions")
