fre:/friː/ | adj. | 自由的，免费的 | Free means not under the control or in the power of another; able to act or be done as one wishes. |  The bird was set free. 这只鸟被放生了。| synonyms: independent, liberated, gratis, complimentary | antonyms: captive, restricted, costly, expensive | adjective

fre:/friː/ | adv. | 自由地，免费地 | Free means without cost or payment. | The drinks were free. 饮料是免费的. | synonyms: freely, gratuitously, complimentary | antonyms: expensively | adverb
