cebo:/ˈseɪboʊ/ (US), /ˈsiːbəʊ/ (UK)| n.|饵;诱饵;诱骗|A piece of food or other lure used to attract and catch fish or animals.  It can also refer to anything used to entice or tempt someone.|例句：The fisherman used a worm as cebo for the fish. 渔夫用虫子作鱼饵。

例句：The con artist used a promise of easy money as cebo to lure in his victims. 骗子用轻松赚钱的承诺来引诱受害者。|近义词：bait, lure, decoy, enticement|反义词: deterrent, repellent|词性:noun
Etymology: From Spanish *cebo*, from Late Latin *cibum* (food), from Latin *cibus* (food).
