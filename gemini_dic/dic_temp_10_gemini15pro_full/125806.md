murk:/mɜːrk/ | n. |黑暗; 阴暗; 晦暗; 浑浊; 污秽; 费解; 难以理解 |darkness or thick mist that makes it difficult to see; something that is difficult to understand| The boat disappeared into the murk. 船消失在黑暗中。 | synonyms: darkness, gloom, obscurity, fog, haze | antonyms: brightness, clarity, light | noun

murk:/mɜːrk/ | v. |使变暗; 使模糊; 使费解 | to make something dark, unclear, or difficult to understand |  The scandal murked his reputation.  这桩丑闻玷污了他的名誉。 | synonyms: obscure, confuse, muddle | antonyms: clarify, illuminate, elucidate | verb
