drudgingly:/ˈdrʌdʒɪŋli/
adverb
辛苦地，沉闷地
in a way that involves hard, boring work
He drudgingly went back to work after his vacation.  他假期结束后，沉闷地回去工作。
She drudgingly completed the tedious task assigned to her. 她辛苦地完成了分配给她的繁琐任务。
近义词: laboriously, tediously, toilsomely
反义词: effortlessly, easily, readily
词性: adverb

词源: From the verb "drudge" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Drudge" comes from Middle English "druggen," of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old Norse "draga" (to draw, pull, drag).
