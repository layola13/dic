arrestation: /ˌærɪˈsteɪʃən/
n.
逮捕，拘留
The act of arresting someone; the state of being arrested.
The police made the arrestation quickly and efficiently. 警方迅速有效地进行了逮捕。
The arrestation of the suspect marked a turning point in the investigation. 嫌疑人的被捕标志着调查的转折点。
近义词: apprehension, capture, detention, seizure
反义词: release, liberation, discharge
