booster-dose:/ˈbuːstər doʊs/ (US) /ˈbuːstə dəʊs/ (UK)| n.|加强剂量；补充剂量|An extra dose of a vaccine given to boost the immune response, especially after the initial dose(s) have lost some of their effectiveness.|I went to the clinic to get my COVID-19 booster dose. 我去诊所接种了新冠疫苗加强针。|

The doctor recommended a booster dose of the vaccine six months after the initial dose. 医生建议在初次接种疫苗六个月后接种加强针。|近义词:booster shot, follow-up dose|反义词:initial dose|词性:名词
