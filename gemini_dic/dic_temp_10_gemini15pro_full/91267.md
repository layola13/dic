hydrogen-like:/ˈhaɪdrədʒənˌlaɪk/| adj.|类氢的|Resembling hydrogen in structure or properties, especially having a single electron orbiting a nucleus.|例句：Hydrogen-like atoms have only one electron. 类氢原子只有一个电子。

例句：The spectrum of a hydrogen-like ion is similar to that of hydrogen. 类氢离子的光谱与氢的光谱相似。|近义词：hydrogenic|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词
