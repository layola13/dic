committable:/kəˈmɪtəbl/
adjective
可承诺的；可托付的
Capable of being committed or entrusted.
This project is committable, we have the resources and the manpower. 这个项目是可以承诺的，我们有资源和人力。
The information is highly sensitive and not committable to just anyone.  这些信息高度敏感，不能随便托付给任何人。
近义词:reliable, trustworthy, dependable
反义词:unreliable, untrustworthy, undependable
词性:adjective
词源:From "commit" + "-able". "Commit" comes from the Latin "committere", meaning "to join together, entrust, commit".  "-able" is a suffix that forms adjectives meaning "capable of being".
