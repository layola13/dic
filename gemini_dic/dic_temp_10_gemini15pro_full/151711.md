parturifacient: /ˌpɑːrtjʊrɪˈfeɪʃənt/ /ˌpɑːrtʃʊrɪˈfeɪʃənt/
adjective, noun
分娩促进的；催产剂
Causing or accelerating childbirth; an agent that induces childbirth.
例句：The doctor administered a parturifacient drug to induce labor. 医生使用了催产药物来引产。

例句：Certain herbs are believed to have parturifacient properties.  某些草药被认为具有催产特性。

近义词：oxytocic, abortifacient (at higher doses)
反义词：tocolytic
词性：adjective, noun

词源：From Latin parturiēns, parturient- ‘being in labour’, from the verb parturire, from partus ‘birth’.
