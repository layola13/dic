humbled:/ˈhʌmbəld/| adj.|谦逊的，虚心的|Feeling or showing a modest or low estimate of one's own importance.|Her award humbled her.  她的获奖让她感到谦卑。|I felt very humbled to meet the president.  见到总统让我感到非常荣幸而谦逊。|近义词：modest, unassuming, meek|反义词：arrogant, proud, haughty|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "humble," from Old French "humbler," ultimately from Latin "humilis" (low, lowly, humble), related to "humus" (earth).
