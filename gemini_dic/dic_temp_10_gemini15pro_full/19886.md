binocular:/bɪˈnɒkjʊlə(r)/ /baɪˈnɑːkjələr/ | adj. n. |双筒的；双目的；双目望远镜 | relating to, using, or adapted for both eyes; a device, typically a pair of prisms with magnifying lenses, designed to give a separate magnified view to each eye |  He looked through his binoculars at the distant ship. 他用双筒望远镜看着远处的船。 | synonyms: field glasses, opera glasses | antonyms: monocular | adjective, noun

binoculars:/bɪˈnɒkjʊləz/ /baɪˈnɑːkjələrz/ | n. |双筒望远镜 | an optical instrument designed for simultaneous use by both eyes |  She brought her binoculars to the bird watching expedition. 她带着双筒望远镜参加观鸟探险。 | synonyms: field glasses, opera glasses | antonyms: monocular | noun
