lyndon:/ˈlɪndən/| n. |林登， Lyndon B. Johnson的昵称|A male given name, a transferred use of the surname, originally a habitational name from any of several places so called, from Old English *lind ‘lime tree’ + dūn ‘hill’. Lyndon B. Johnson, often referred to as LBJ, served as the 36th president of the United States.|Lyndon B. Johnson was the 36th president of the United States. 林登·B·约翰逊是美国第36任总统。|近义词: (as a name) no direct synonyms, but other presidential names like Kennedy, Nixon, Carter |反义词: N/A|词性:noun
