fructescence:/ˌfrʌktˈɛsəns/ | n. |结果实；果期；果序|The process of fruiting; the fruiting period; a fruiting or infructescence. The arrangement of fruits on a stem. | The fructescence of the mango tree is typically in the summer months. 芒果树的果期通常在夏季。| The botanist studied the fructescence of the rare plant.  植物学家研究了这种稀有植物的果序。 | synonyms: fruiting, fruit bearing, infructescence | antonyms:  barrenness, sterility | noun
