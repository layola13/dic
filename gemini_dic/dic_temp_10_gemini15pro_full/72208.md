floran:/ˈflɔːrən/ | adj. n. | 植物的；植物群的；植物区系的；植物群落成员 | Relating to plants or plant life; a member of a plant community. |  The floran diversity of the rainforest is astounding. 雨林的植物多样性令人震惊。 | The botanist cataloged the various floran species in the region.  植物学家对该地区不同的植物种类进行了分类。 | 近义词：botanical, vegetative, floral | 反义词：faunal | 词性：形容词, 名词

词源: From Latin "flora" (goddess of flowers) + "-an" (suffix forming adjectives and nouns). 源自拉丁语 "flora"（花神）+ "-an"（构成形容词和名词的后缀）。
