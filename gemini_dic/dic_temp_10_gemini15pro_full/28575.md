careen:/kəˈriːn/
verb
倾斜，摇晃;  高速疾驰
to lean, sway, or tip to one side while in motion; to move forward rapidly, often in an uncontrolled manner.
The car careened around the corner. 汽车摇晃着转过街角。

The bus careened down the hill. 公车沿着山坡飞驰而下。
近义词:tilt, lurch, sway, speed, rush
反义词:steady, stabilize, slow
词性:verb

careen:/kəˈriːn/
verb
(船)倾斜, (为…)倾侧(船身)以便维修
(of a ship or boat) to heel over; to cause (a ship or boat) to heel over for cleaning or repair.
They careened the ship to repair the hull. 他们倾斜船身以修理船体。
The ship was careened for maintenance. 这艘船被倾斜以便进行维护。
近义词:heel, tip, list
反义词:right, upright
词性:verb
