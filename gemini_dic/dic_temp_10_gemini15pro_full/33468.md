chylaceous:/kaɪˈleɪʃəs/
adjective
乳糜的；乳汁状的
Resembling chyle; milky.  Of the nature of chyle.
The chylaceous fluid was observed under a microscope. 乳糜状液体在显微镜下被观察。
The patient's vomit had a chylaceous appearance. 病人的呕吐物呈乳糜状。
近义词: milky, chyle-like
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider antonyms for "milky" like "clear," "transparent")
词性: adjective
