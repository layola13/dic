hawks:/hɔːks/ | n. |鹰; 鹰派人物; 骗子; 掠夺者 |Birds of prey, typically having a short, hooked beak, strong claws, and keen eyesight, known for their hunting skills. Also refers to people who advocate aggressive political policies or are perceived as predatory. |例句：The hawks circled overhead, searching for prey. 老鹰在头顶盘旋，寻找猎物。

例句：The hawks in the government pushed for military intervention. 政府中的鹰派人物推动军事干预。|近义词：falcons, eagles, predators, hardliners|反义词：doves|词性：名词
