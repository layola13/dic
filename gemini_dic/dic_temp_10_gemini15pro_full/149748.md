palatality:/ˌpæləˈtælɪti/ | n. | 腭音化；腭音性 |The quality of being palatal; the degree to which a sound is articulated with the tongue against or near the hard palate.  This often results in a "y" sound. |The palatality of the "y" sound in "yes" is very clear.  "yes" 中 "y" 音的腭音性十分明显。|近义词: palatalization |反义词: velarization |词性: noun

Etymology:  From Late Latin *palatalitatem, from palatalis (“of the palate”), from palatum (“palate”).
