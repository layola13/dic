chairmans:/ˈtʃɛərmənz/ | n. |主席们；董事长们|  Plural form of "chairman," referring to multiple individuals holding the position of chairman. | The chairmans of the various committees met to discuss the budget. 各个委员会的主席们开会讨论预算。|The two chairmans disagreed on the best course of action. 两位主席对最佳行动方案意见不一。| synonyms: presidents, heads, leaders | antonyms: subordinates, followers | noun
