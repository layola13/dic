account for:/əˈkaʊnt fɔːr/
verb
解释，说明；占…比例
To explain or give a reason for something; to constitute a specified amount or proportion of something.
The poor weather may account for the small crowd.  糟糕的天气可能是人少的原因。

Recent pressure at work may account for his behavior. 最近的工作压力可以解释他的行为。

近义词:explain, justify, constitute, comprise, make up
反义词:obscure, confuse
词性:verb

USAGE->

His job accounts for most of his time. = 他的工作占据了他大部分时间。
This factor accounts for the difference in results. =  这个因素解释了结果的差异。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
