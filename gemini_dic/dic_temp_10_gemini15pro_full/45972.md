cyanided:/ˈsaɪənaɪdɪd/
verb|用氰化物处理过的，被氰化的|Treated with cyanide.
The ore was cyanided to extract the gold.|矿石用氰化物处理以提取黄金。
近义词:poisoned, contaminated
反义词:purified, cleansed
词性:v.

cyanided:/ˈsaɪənaɪdɪd/
adjective|用氰化物处理过的，被氰化的|Treated with cyanide.
The cyanided waste posed a significant environmental threat.|被氰化物处理过的废物构成了重大的环境威胁。
近义词:poisoned, contaminated
反义词:purified, cleansed
词性:adj.


词源: From cyanide + -ed.  Cyanide itself comes from the Greek word *kyanos* meaning "dark blue," referring to Prussian blue, a pigment from which cyanide can be derived.
