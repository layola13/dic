harrumphing:/hɑːˈrʌmfɪŋ/
|n.|不满的哼哼声；表示不悦的清嗓子声|An act of clearing the throat noisily as a sign of disapproval or impatience. |He responded to the suggestion with a loud harrumphing. 他对这个建议大声地哼了一声以表示不满。|近义词：grunting, groaning, grumbling|反义词：approving, agreeing|词性:名词

harrumph:/hɑːˈrʌmf/
|vi. & vt.|(发出)不满的哼哼声；(发出)表示不悦的清嗓子声|To clear the throat noisily as a sign of disapproval or impatience. |"Nonsense!" he harrumphed. “胡说！”他哼了一声。|近义词：grunt, groan, grumble|反义词：approve, agree|词性:动词
