bluefin:/ˈbluːfɪn/| n. |蓝鳍金枪鱼|A tuna, especially the Atlantic bluefin (Thunnus thynnus), highly valued as a food fish.|例句：Bluefin tuna are prized for their rich, fatty meat. 蓝鳍金枪鱼因其肥美的肉质而备受推崇。

例句：Overfishing has drastically reduced bluefin populations. 过度捕捞使蓝鳍金枪鱼的数量急剧减少。|近义词：tuna|反义词:|词性:noun
