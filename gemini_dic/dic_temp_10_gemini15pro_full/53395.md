disallowable:/ˌdɪsəˈlaʊəbl/
adjective
不可允许的，不允许的
Not allowable; that cannot be allowed.
Expenses deemed disallowable by the tax authorities.  被税务机关视为不允许的费用。
The claim was disallowable because it was filed too late.  这项索赔因为提交太晚而不被允许。
近义词:unacceptable, inadmissible, impermissible, invalid
反义词:allowable, permissible, acceptable, valid
词性:adjective

词源:
dis- (prefix):  expressing negation or opposition; not. 源自拉丁语 dis-，表示否定或相反。
allowable:  that can be allowed.  来自动词 allow（允许）加上后缀 -able（可...的）。
