inscape:/ˈɪnskeɪp/| n.|内景，内心世界，个体特有的内在形式|The unique inner nature of a person or object, especially as perceived as an aesthetic quality.|Gerard Manley Hopkins's poetry is full of vivid inscapes.  杰拉德·曼利·霍普金斯的诗歌充满了生动的内景描写。|近义词：inner landscape, inner world, subjectivity|反义词：exterior, outside, surface|词性:noun

Etymology:  Coined by the English poet Gerard Manley Hopkins in the late 19th century. From in- + -scape (as in landscape).
