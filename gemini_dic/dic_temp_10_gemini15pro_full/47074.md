dative:/ˈdeɪtɪv/| adj. n.|与格的，与格|Relating to or denoting a case of nouns, pronouns, and adjectives (typically marked by inflection) used to indicate the indirect object of a verb.  In some languages, also used for other grammatical functions.|She gave him the book. (In this sentence, "him" is the indirect object and would be in the dative case in languages with such a case.) 他把书给了他。（在这个句子中，“him”是间接宾语，在有与格的语言中会用与格。）|近义词:indirect object|反义词:nominative, accusative|词性:adjective, noun
