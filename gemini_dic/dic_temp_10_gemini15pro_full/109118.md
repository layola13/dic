leghorn:/ˈlɛɡɔːrn/ | n. |  莱ghorn鸡；莱ghorn草帽 | A breed of domestic chicken, originally from Tuscany, Italy, known for its high egg production. Also refers to a hat made from a fine, plaited straw of the same name. |  例句：The farmer raises leghorns for their eggs.  这位农民饲养莱ghorn鸡是为了获取鸡蛋。

例句：She wore a stylish leghorn hat to the garden party. 她戴着一顶时髦的莱ghorn草帽参加了花园派对。 | 近义词：(for chicken) hen, layer (for hat) straw hat, panama hat | 反义词: (no direct antonym) | 词性:noun
