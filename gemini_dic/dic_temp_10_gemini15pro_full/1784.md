acranial: /eɪˈkreɪniəl/
adjective
无颅的，没有头盖骨的
Lacking a cranium or skull.  Relating to the parts of the body other than the head.
The acranial part of the embryo developed normally. 胚胎的无颅部分发育正常。
The acranial invertebrates exhibit a diverse range of body plans. 无颅无脊椎动物展现出多种多样的身体结构。

近义词: headless, acephalous
反义词: cranial
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (without) + cranial (relating to the skull).  "Cranial" ultimately derives from the Greek "kranion" (skull).
