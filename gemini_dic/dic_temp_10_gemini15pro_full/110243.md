lifequake:/ˈlaɪfˌkweɪk/| n.|生活大地震，人生巨变|A significant disruption or upheaval in someone's life, often causing a fundamental shift in their values, beliefs, or lifestyle. It can be triggered by major life events such as job loss, divorce, illness, or the death of a loved one.|例句：The loss of his job triggered a lifequake that forced him to re-evaluate his priorities.  他失去工作引发了一场人生巨变，迫使他重新评估自己的优先事项。

例句：Experiencing a lifequake can be an opportunity for personal growth and transformation.  经历人生巨变可以成为个人成长和转变的机会。|近义词：upheaval, crisis, turning point, epiphany, transformation|反义词:stability, normalcy, routine|词性:noun
