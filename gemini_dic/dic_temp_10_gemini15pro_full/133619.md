nonforbearance: /ˌnɒnfɔːrˈbɛərəns/ | n. | 不宽容，不忍耐；拒绝延期偿还 | Lack of forbearance; refusal to grant a delay in repaying a debt or fulfilling an obligation. | The bank's nonforbearance on the loan led to the foreclosure of their home. 银行对贷款的不宽容导致他们的房子被取消赎回权。

The creditor's nonforbearance put the struggling business in jeopardy. 债权人的不宽容使这家苦苦挣扎的企业陷入了危险。 | Near synonyms: intolerance, impatience, intransigence, refusal | Antonyms: forbearance, tolerance, patience, leniency | noun
