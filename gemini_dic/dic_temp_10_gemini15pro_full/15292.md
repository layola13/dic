backlot: /ˈbækˌlɒt/ (US) /ˈbækˌlɒt/ (UK)| adj. |摄影棚外景的；在摄影棚外围空地拍摄的|Relating to or characteristic of a backlot.|The backlot set was designed to look like a small town.|Synonyms: outdoor, exterior|Antonyms: indoor, interior|Adjective
