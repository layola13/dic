misfashions:/ˌmɪsˈfæʃənz/
| n. | 过时的式样；不合时宜的服装 | Outdated or inappropriate styles of clothing or dress. |  Many of the clothes in my grandmother's attic are now considered misfashions.  我祖母阁楼里的许多衣服现在都被认为是过时的式样。| 近义词: outmoded styles, outdated fashions | 反义词: current fashions, latest trends | 词性: 名词

misfashion:/ˌmɪsˈfæʃən/
| vt. | 把…做成不合时宜的式样; (rare) to fashion poorly or inappropriately | To design or create (clothing or other items) in an outdated or unsuitable style. | The designer misfashioned the dress, making it look like something from a bygone era.  设计师把这件衣服做得不合时宜，看起来像过去时代的产物。 | 近义词: misdesign, misstyle | 反义词: fashion, style | 词性: 动词
