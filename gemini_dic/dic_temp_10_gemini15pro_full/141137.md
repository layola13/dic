onychomancy: /ˈɒnɪkəˌmænsi/ | n. |指甲占卜|Divination by means of examining the fingernails.  It involves observing the reflections, lines, and spots on the fingernails to predict the future or diagnose illnesses.|Example:  She practiced onychomancy, believing she could foresee events by studying her nails. 她练习指甲占卜，相信通过研究自己的指甲可以预见未来。

Example:  Although onychomancy is an ancient practice, it is not scientifically proven. 虽然指甲占卜是一种古老的做法，但它没有科学依据。|近义词：nail reading, divination, fortune-telling|反义词:  None (lack of a specific antonym for this specialized term)|词性: noun
