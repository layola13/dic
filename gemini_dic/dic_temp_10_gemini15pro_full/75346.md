frostweed:/ˈfrɔːstwiːd/| n. |霜草，冰草|A plant that exudes water in freezing weather, forming ice crystals.  A North American plant (Helianthemum canadense) of the rockrose family, whose stems exude water in late autumn that freezes into beautiful ribbons of ice. |例句：The frostweed was covered in delicate ice ribbons. 霜草上覆盖着精致的冰带。

例句：We found frostweed growing along the trail. 我们发现小路边生长着霜草。|近义词：ice plant, rockrose |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From frost + weed.  "Frost" from Old English "forst" meaning "frost, freezing cold". "Weed" from Old English "wēod" meaning "plant, herb".
