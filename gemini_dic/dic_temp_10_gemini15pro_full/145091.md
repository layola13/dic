overapplauds:/ˌōvərəˈplôdz/| v. |过度鼓掌，鼓掌过久|To applaud excessively or for too long.|The audience overapplauded the mediocre performance out of politeness.  观众出于礼貌对平庸的表演鼓掌过久。|The singer was overapplauded after his final song. 歌手最后一首歌后被过度鼓掌。|近义词：overpraise, acclaim excessively|反义词: underapplaud, barely clap|词性:动词
