elysian: /ɪˈlɪziən/ /ɪˈliʒən/
adjective
极乐世界的；天堂般的
Relating to or characteristic of Elysium, a paradise or state of perfect happiness.
例句：The garden was an elysian retreat, filled with fragrant flowers and the sound of birdsong.  这个花园是一个天堂般的静修地，充满了芬芳的花朵和鸟鸣声。

例句：She described the experience as an elysian moment of pure joy and contentment. 她将这段经历描述为一个充满纯粹喜悦和满足的极乐时刻。

近义词：heavenly, blissful, paradisiacal, idyllic
反义词：hellish, infernal, dystopian
词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin Elysius, from Latin Elysii campi (“Elysian fields”), from Ancient Greek Ἠλύσιον πεδίον (Ēlúsion pedíon).
