man-made:/ˌmænˈmeɪd/ | adj. | 人造的，人工的 | Made by humans rather than occurring naturally. |例句：The dam is a man-made structure.  这个大坝是人造建筑。


例句：Many man-made materials are now replacing natural materials. 许多人造材料现在正在取代天然材料。


近义词：artificial, synthetic, manufactured | 反义词：natural, organic | 词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From man + made (past participle of make). First recorded in the late 19th century.  由“man”（人）和“made”（make的过去分词）组成。最早记录于19世纪后期。
