conjugational:/ˌkɑːndʒəˈɡeɪʃənəl/ |adj.| 共轭的；结合的 | Relating to conjugation, especially in grammar (the variation of the form of a verb in an inflected language such as Latin, Greek, or English, such as "be, am, is, are, was, were".) or in biology (the temporary union of two bacteria or unicellular organisms for the exchange of genetic material). | The conjugational patterns of irregular verbs can be challenging for language learners. 不规则动词的词形变化模式对语言学习者来说可能具有挑战性。

The conjugational transfer of genetic material between bacteria can lead to antibiotic resistance.  细菌之间遗传物质的结合转移会导致抗生素耐药性。 |近义词: inflectional, combinative |反义词: uninflected, simple |词性: adjective
