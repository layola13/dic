disoccupy:/ˌdɪsˈɒkjupaɪ/ /ˌdɪsˈɑːkjupaɪ/
verb
使闲置，使无人居住；撤离
to cause (a place) to be no longer occupied; to leave or cause (someone) to leave a place
The workers were disoccupied after the factory closed. 工厂关闭后，工人们都失业了。
The army disoccupied the town after the war. 战后军队撤离了该镇。
近义词: vacate, evacuate, abandon
反义词: occupy, inhabit
词性: verb
