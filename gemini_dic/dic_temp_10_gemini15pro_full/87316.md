hernia:/ˈhɜːrniə/ (US) /ˈhɜːniə/ (UK)| n. |疝气；突出|A condition in which part of an organ is displaced and protrudes through the wall of the cavity containing it.|He had a hernia operation last year. 他去年做了疝气手术。|The doctor diagnosed an umbilical hernia. 医生诊断出脐疝。|近义词：rupture, protrusion|反义词: (None, as it's a medical condition)|noun

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
A hernia occurs when an organ pushes through an opening in the muscle or tissue that holds it in place.  当器官穿过将其固定到位的肌肉或组织的开口时，就会发生疝气。

Hernias are usually caused by a combination of muscle weakness and strain. 疝气通常是由肌肉无力和拉伤共同引起的。
