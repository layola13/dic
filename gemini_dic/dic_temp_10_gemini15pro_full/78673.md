gift-wrap:/ˈɡɪftˌræp/ (US), /ˈɡɪftˌræp/ (UK)| n. v.|礼品包装；包装礼品|The act of wrapping a gift, or the materials used to do so.  It can refer to the paper, ribbon, bows, and other decorative elements used to make a present look attractive.|She carefully gift-wrapped the present for her friend. 她仔细地为朋友包装礼物。|近义词:wrap, package|反义词:unwrap|词性:noun, verb
