decahedron:/ˌdɛkəˈhiːdrən/| n. |十面体|A solid figure with ten plane faces.|A regular decahedron has ten congruent faces, each of which is an equilateral pentagon. 正十面体有十个全等的正五边形面。|The dice were shaped like small decahedrons. 骰子形状像小十面体。|polyhedron, ten-sided figure| |noun
Etymology->
From Ancient Greek δέκα (déka, “ten”) + ἕδρα (hédra, “seat, base, face”).
