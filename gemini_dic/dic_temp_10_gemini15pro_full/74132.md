foundries:/ˈfaʊndriz/
noun
铸造厂；翻砂车间
A workshop or factory for casting metal.
Molten metal is poured into moulds in foundries to create various shapes. 熔化的金属在铸造厂被倒入模具中以创造各种形状.
synonyms: casting works, metalworks, ironworks, steelworks
antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a place of manufacturing)
n.
