decommission:/ˌdiːkəˈmɪʃən/
| vt. | 解除职务；使退役；关闭|To officially take something out of service, especially military equipment or a large machine such as a nuclear reactor.| The navy decommissioned several of its older ships.  海军让几艘旧军舰退役了。|
decommission:/ˌdiːkəˈmɪʃən/
| n. | 退役；解除职务|The act of officially taking something out of service.| The decommissioning of the power plant is scheduled for next year.  这家发电厂计划于明年退役。


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning 'reverse' or 'remove') + commission (meaning 'to authorize or put into service').

SYNONYMS->
retire, withdraw, deactivate, dismantle

ANTONYMS->
commission, activate, launch
