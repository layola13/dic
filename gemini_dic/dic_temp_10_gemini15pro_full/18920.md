bespake:/bɪˈspeɪk/| adj.|定制的，定做的|Made to a particular customer's order; custom-made. |Example: He wore a bespoke suit. 他穿着一套定制的西装。|Example:  She ordered a bespoke wedding dress. 她订购了一件定制的婚纱。|近义词：custom-made, tailored, made-to-order, bespoke|反义词: ready-made, off-the-rack, ready-to-wear|词性:形容词

bespeak:/bɪˈspiːk/| vt.|预订，预示，表明|1. Order or reserve (something) in advance. 2. Be a sign or indication of; suggest.|Example:  Their impeccable manners bespoke good breeding. 他们无可挑剔的举止表明他们出身良好。|Example:  The dark clouds bespeak a coming storm. 乌云预示着暴风雨即将来临。|近义词：indicate, suggest, signify, imply, order, reserve|反义词: cancel, countermand|词性:动词
