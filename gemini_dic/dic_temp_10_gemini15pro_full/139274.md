oblique:/əˈbliːk/ /oʊˈbliːk/| adj. n.|倾斜的，间接的；斜线；倾斜物|A muscle that is neither parallel nor perpendicular to the long axis of a body or limb.  Something slanting or sloping; not straightforward or direct.|The architect used many oblique lines in her design. 建筑师在她的设计中使用了许多斜线。|synonyms: slanting, sloping, inclined, angled, diagonal, indirect, evasive, implicit|antonyms: straight, direct, perpendicular, explicit|adjective

oblique:/əˈbliːk/ /oʊˈbliːk/| n.|倾斜物；斜肌|A muscle that is neither parallel nor perpendicular to the long axis of a body or limb. |The external oblique muscle is located on the sides of the abdomen. 外斜肌位于腹部两侧。|synonyms: slant, slope, inclination, angle, diagonal|antonyms: straight, direct, perpendicular|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *obliquus, from Latin obliquus (“slanting, sideways, oblique”), from ob (“against, towards”) + liquis (“slanting”).
