delightedly:/dɪˈlaɪtɪdli/
adverb
高兴地，欣喜地
In a manner expressing great pleasure or delight.
She clapped her hands delightedly when she saw the puppy. 她看到小狗高兴地拍手。
近义词: joyfully, happily, gladly, merrily
反义词: sadly, unhappily, gloomily
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From delight + -ed + -ly. Delight comes from Old French deleiter, from Latin delectare "to allure, delight," frequentative of delicere "to entice."
