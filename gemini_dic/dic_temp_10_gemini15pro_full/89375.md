homophobe:/ˈhɒməfəʊb/ (UK) /ˈhoʊməfoʊb/ (US)| n.| 恐同者，恐同症患者 |A person who has an extreme and irrational aversion to homosexuality and homosexual people. |He was accused of being a homophobe after making derogatory remarks about gay people. 他因发表贬低同性恋者的言论而被指责为恐同者。|近义词：bigot, prejudiced person|反义词：ally, supporter|词性: Noun

Etymology:
homo- (from Greek homós, meaning "same") + -phobe (from Greek phobos, meaning "fear")
