arterial:/ɑːrˈtɪəriəl/| adj.|动脉的；干线的|Relating to or resembling an artery; being a major route.|例句：Arterial blood is bright red. 动脉血是鲜红色的。

例句：The city's arterial roads are congested during rush hour. 城市的干道在高峰时段很拥堵。|近义词：main, principal, key|反义词：venous, capillary|词性：形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *arterialis*, from Latin *arteria* ("artery").
