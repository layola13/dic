freewheel:/ˈfriːwiːl/| v. n.|自由旋转，滑行，放任自流|To move forward in a vehicle without applying power or effort, or to act without restraint or planning.|例句：He freewheeled down the hill. 他顺着山坡滑行下去。|近义词：coast, drift, glide|反义词：pedal, strive, control|verb
freewheel:/ˈfriːwiːl/| n.|飞轮，自由轮|A mechanism, typically on a bicycle, that allows the driven wheel to rotate independently of the pedals.|例句：The bicycle's freewheel needs to be replaced. 这辆自行车的飞轮需要更换。|近义词：coaster brake, ratchet|反义词：fixed gear|noun
