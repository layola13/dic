mantled:/ˈmæntəld/ | adj. | 覆盖的，披风的 | Covered with a mantle; cloaked. | The mountains were mantled in snow. 群山被雪覆盖。 | The queen, mantled in velvet, addressed her subjects. 女王披着天鹅绒斗篷，向她的臣民讲话。 | 近义词: covered, draped, cloaked, enveloped | 反义词: bare, exposed, uncovered | 词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *mantelen, from Anglo-Norman manteler, from Old French manteler, from mantel ("mantle").
