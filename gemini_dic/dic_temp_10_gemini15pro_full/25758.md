burbliest:/ˈbɜːrbliɪst/
adjective
最多泡的；最冒泡的；最欢腾的
Most bubbly; producing or characterized by the most bubbles.
The burbliest champagne I've ever tasted. 我尝过最冒泡的香槟。
Near synonyms: fizziest, frothiest, foamiest
Near antonyms: flattest, stillest
词性: 形容词
