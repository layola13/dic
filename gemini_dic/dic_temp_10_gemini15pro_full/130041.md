The internship was non-paying, but it provided valuable experience.  这份实习没有报酬，但提供了宝贵的经验。 | 近义词:unpaid, free, gratuitous, complimentary | 反义词: paying, remunerative, compensated | 词性:形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "paying" (present participle of the verb "pay")
