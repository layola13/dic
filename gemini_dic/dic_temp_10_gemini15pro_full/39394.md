conjurement:/ˈkɒndʒəmənt/ (US) /ˈkɑːndʒərmənt/| n. |魔咒，咒语，魔法|The act of conjuring; invocation of spirits, or the performance of magical rites or incantations.|She tried a conjurement to bring back her lost love. 她尝试用咒语找回失去的爱人。|Near synonyms:  incantation, spell, charm, invocation, sorcery, witchcraft|Near antonyms:  prayer, benediction|noun
