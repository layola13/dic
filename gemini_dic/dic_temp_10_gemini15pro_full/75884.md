fundamental:/ˌfʌndəˈmentl/ | adj. n. | 基本的；根本的；基础的；基本原理；基本原则 | Forming a necessary base or core; of central importance. |例句：A fundamental change in the structure of the organization. 该组织结构的根本性变化。|近义词：basic, essential, primary, elementary |反义词：superficial, secondary, trivial |词性:adjective

fundamental:/ˌfʌndəˈmentl/ | n. | 基本原理；基本原则 | A central or primary rule or principle on which something is based. |例句：The fundamentals of economics. 经济学的基本原理。|近义词：basic, principle, rule, essential |反义词：detail, triviality |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fundamentale, from Latin fundamentum (“foundation”), from fundare (“to found, lay the bottom, establish”), from fundus (“bottom, base”).
