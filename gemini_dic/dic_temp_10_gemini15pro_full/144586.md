outwaters:/ˈaʊtˌwɔːtərz/ | n. |外海，远海；沿海水域以外的水域| The ship sailed into the outwaters, leaving the coast behind. 这艘船驶入外海，把海岸线抛在身后。|例句：The fishing boat ventured into the outwaters in search of larger catches.  渔船冒险进入远海去捕捞更大的鱼。

|例句：Strong currents are common in these outwaters.  这些外海水域常有强劲的水流。 |近义词：open sea, high seas, offshore waters |反义词:inshore waters, coastal waters |词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From 'out' (meaning outside, beyond) + 'waters' (plural of water, referring to a body of water).  由“out”（意为外面，远处）+“waters”（water的复数形式，指水域）组成。
