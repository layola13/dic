averts:/əˈvɜːrts/| v. |避免，防止，转移|to turn away (one's eyes or thoughts); to prevent something bad from happening|例句：He averted his eyes from the accident scene. 他把目光从事故现场移开。

例句：The company averted a financial crisis by cutting costs. 公司通过削减成本避免了金融危机。|近义词：prevents, avoids, thwarts, obviates|反义词：causes, incites, provokes|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Latin *avertere*, from *ab-* (“away from”) + *vertere* (“to turn”).
