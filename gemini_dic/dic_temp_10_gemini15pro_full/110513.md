limberly:/ˈlɪmbərli/ | adv. | 灵活地，柔软地 | In a limber and flexible manner.  |She danced limberly across the stage. 她轻盈地在舞台上舞动。 | 近义词：flexibly, supplely, agilely | 反义词：stiffly, rigidly | 词性：副词

词源:  From "limber" (adjective meaning flexible) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  The word "limber" comes from Middle English *limber*, probably from Old English *limp*.
