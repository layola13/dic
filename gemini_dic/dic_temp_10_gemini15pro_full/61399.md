emblement:/ˈembləmənt/| n. |收获物; 年作物; 土地收益|Annual crops produced by labor, such as grains, vegetables, and fruit, that are considered personal property even if the land they grow on has changed ownership.  It is also the right of a tenant farmer to return and harvest crops planted before the termination of the tenancy.|The farmer was granted the emblements of his fields even after he sold the land.|近义词：crop, harvest, yield, produce, fruit|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *emblament (Modern French emblavement, meaning "sowing, seeding"),  from emblaer ("to sow"), from en- ("in") + blaer ("to sow with grain"), from Late Latin bladum ("grain," "crop").
