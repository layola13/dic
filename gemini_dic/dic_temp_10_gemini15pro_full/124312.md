noun
母性行为，慈母般的关怀; (尤指动物的)育幼行为; (母鸡)孵蛋
Acts of a mother; maternal care, especially the brooding or protective care of animals (especially of a hen) over her young.
The hen's motherings kept her chicks warm and safe. 母鸡的孵育让小鸡们感到温暖和安全。
