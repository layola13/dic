category:/ˈkætəɡɔːri/ /ˈkætəɡəri/ | n. | 类别，种类 | A group or set of things or people having something in common; a division within a system of classification. |  This book belongs to the category of fiction. 这本书属于小说类。| synonyms: class, type, sort, kind, genre, group, division | antonyms: individual, singularity | noun

category:/ˈkætəɡɔːri/ /ˈkætəɡəri/ | vt. | 分类，归类 | To place in a category. | The librarian categorized the new books. 图书管理员将新书分类。 | synonyms: classify, group, sort, rank, grade, rate | antonyms: randomize, disorganize | verb
