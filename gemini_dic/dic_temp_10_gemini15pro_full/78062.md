geocentrical:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈsɛntrɪkəl/
adjective
地心的；以地球为中心的
Having or representing the earth as the center, as of the solar system.
The ancient Greeks held a geocentrical view of the universe. 古希腊人持有地心说的宇宙观。

The Ptolemaic system is a geocentrical model of the universe. 托勒密体系是一个地心说的宇宙模型。

Synonym: earth-centered
Antonym: heliocentric
词性: adjective
