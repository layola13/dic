bedrolls:/ˈbedˌroʊlz/| n. |睡袋，铺盖卷|A thick, padded blanket or quilt, often used as a portable bed, especially outdoors.|The cowboys unrolled their bedrolls under the starry sky. 牛仔们在星空下铺开了他们的睡袋。|近义词：sleeping bags, blankets, bedding|反义词:(None -  lack of a direct opposite.  Perhaps "mattress" for a more permanent sleeping arrangement.)|noun
