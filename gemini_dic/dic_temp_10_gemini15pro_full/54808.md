dispermous:/daɪˈspɜːməs/
adjective
双籽的；二分果的
Having two seeds, or splitting into two one-seeded portions.
The dispermous fruit split open, revealing two tiny seeds.  双籽果实裂开，露出两颗小种子。
The plant produces dispermous capsules. 这种植物会结出双籽的蒴果。
近义词: two-seeded
反义词: polyspermous (多籽的)
词性: adjective

词源:
From Greek *dispermos*, from *di-* (two) + *sperma* (seed).
