liberality: /ˌlɪbəˈrælɪti/
n.
慷慨，大方；宽宏大量
the quality of being liberal; generosity
He treated them with liberality. 他对他们很慷慨。

近义词: generosity, munificence, bounty, largesse
反义词: stinginess, meanness, parsimony, miserliness
词性: noun
