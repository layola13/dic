harp:/hɑːrp/ | n. vi. | 竖琴；喋喋不休 | A large, stringed musical instrument played by plucking the strings with the fingers.  To talk or write persistently and tediously on a particular topic. | She played a beautiful melody on the harp. 她用竖琴演奏了一曲优美的旋律。| He keeps harping on about his problems. 他不停地唠叨他的问题。 | synonyms: lyre, zither (for n.); dwell, reiterate (for v.) | antonyms:  (no direct antonym for the noun);  (for the verb: be silent, drop it) | noun, verb
