chulan:/ˈtʃuːlɑːn/ | n. | 阁楼 | An attic or loft space. (Less common usage) |  They stored old furniture in the chulan. 他们把旧家具存放在阁楼里。| synonym: attic, loft | antonym:  basement, cellar | noun
