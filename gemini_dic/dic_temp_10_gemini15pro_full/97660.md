inhibitor:/ɪnˈhɪbɪtər/ | n. |抑制剂，阻止者|A substance that slows down or prevents a particular chemical reaction or other process, or that reduces the activity of a particular enzyme.|The drug acted as an inhibitor, preventing the virus from replicating.|Synonyms: suppressant, retarder, blocker|Antonyms: activator, catalyst, stimulant|Noun

inhibitors:/ɪnˈhɪbɪtərz/ | n. |抑制剂，阻止者（复数）|Substances that slow down or prevent particular chemical reactions or other processes, or that reduce the activity of particular enzymes.|Many enzyme inhibitors are used in medicine.|Synonyms: suppressants, retarders, blockers|Antonyms: activators, catalysts, stimulants|Noun
