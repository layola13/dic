broadest:/ˈbrɔːdɪst/| adj.|最广泛的，最宽阔的|Superlative form of "broad," meaning having the largest extent from side to side; widest; most extensive or inclusive.|The Amazon River has the broadest mouth of any river in the world. 亚马逊河的河口是世界上最宽阔的。|The museum has the broadest collection of antique toys I've ever seen. 这家博物馆收藏的古董玩具是我见过的最广泛的。|近义词：widest, most extensive, most comprehensive|反义词：narrowest, smallest, most limited|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Old English *brād, from Proto-Germanic *braidaz, from Proto-Indo-European *mrēh₂tós.  Cognates include Old High German breit, German breit, Old Norse breiðr, and Gothic braids.  The "-est" suffix is the superlative ending.
