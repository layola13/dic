brainier:/ˈbreɪniər/| adj.|更聪明的，更机智的|More intelligent and clever. Having or showing greater intelligence.|例句：She's always been the brainier of the two sisters. 她一直是两姐妹中更聪明的那一个。|近义词：smarter, more intelligent, brighter, sharper, wiser|反义词：less intelligent, dumber, stupider|词性:形容词
词源: brain (n.) + -ier (comparative suffix).  From "brain" meaning the organ of thought, with "-ier" added to form the comparative adjective.
