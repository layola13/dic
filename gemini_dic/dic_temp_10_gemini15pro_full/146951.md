overinvesting: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɛstɪŋ/
|n.|过度投资|The act of investing too much money, resources, or time in something, often to a point where the return is less than the investment.|They worried about overinvesting in the stock market.|近义词: overspending, overcommitting|反义词: underinvesting|noun
