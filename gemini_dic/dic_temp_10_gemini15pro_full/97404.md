infrequently:/ɪnˈfriːkwəntli/
adverb
不经常地；很少地
not often; rarely
She visits her parents infrequently, perhaps only twice a year. 她很少去看望父母，可能一年只有两次。
The bus runs infrequently on Sundays. 周日公交车很少运行。

近义词:rarely, seldom, occasionally
反义词:frequently, often, regularly
词性:adverb
