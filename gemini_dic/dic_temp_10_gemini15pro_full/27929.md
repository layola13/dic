cantilena:/ˌkæntɪˈliːnə/ | n. | 歌唱；旋律；滑音；拖长的音节 |A smooth, flowing melody; a song, especially a slow, lyrical one.  A sustained note or melodic phrase, often with a smooth, gliding quality. | The soprano's cantilena in the aria was breathtaking. 女高音在咏叹调中的旋律令人叹为观止。|  The violinist played the cantilena with a beautiful, sustained tone. 小提琴家用优美而持续的音调演奏了这段旋律。 | synonyms: melody, tune, air, song, theme, aria, chant | antonyms: dissonance, cacophony | noun
