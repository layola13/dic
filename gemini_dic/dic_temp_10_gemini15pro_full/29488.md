/ˈkætəˌpleksi/ | n. | 猝倒，肌无力 | A sudden loss of muscle tone, often triggered by strong emotions such as laughter or excitement. |  She experienced cataplexy after hearing the surprising news. 她听到令人惊讶的消息后，经历了猝倒。| Synonym: atony, weakness | Antonym: tonicity, strength | noun

/ˈkætəˌpleksi/ | n. | 猝倒，突然的肌肉无力 | A sudden and transient episode of muscle weakness accompanied by full conscious awareness, typically triggered by emotions such as laughter, fear, anger, or excitement. | The comedian's jokes caused the man to experience cataplexy, causing him to collapse to the floor.  喜剧演员的笑话导致该男子猝倒，倒在地上。| Synonym: muscle weakness, atony | Antonym: muscle strength, tonicity | noun
