managements:/ˈmænɪdʒmənts/| n.|管理；经营；管理部门；管理人员|The act or process of managing; the application of skill and intelligence in directing or controlling something. Also, the people who manage a business or organization.|The company's management team is highly effective.  这家公司的管理团队非常高效。|Synonyms: administration, direction, supervision|Antonyms:  mismanagement, neglect|noun
