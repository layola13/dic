off-white:/ˌɔfˈwaɪt/ /ˌɑːfˈhwaɪt/
adjective
灰白色，米白色
A color that is a very light shade of white, slightly tinged with yellow or grey.
She wore an off-white dress to the party. 她穿着一件灰白色的连衣裙去参加聚会。
The walls were painted a calming off-white.  墙被漆成了令人平静的米白色。
Near synonyms: cream, ivory, beige, eggshell
Antonyms:  pure white, bright white, jet black
词性: adjective
