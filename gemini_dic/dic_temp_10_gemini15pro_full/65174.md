ethnolinguistics: /ˌɛθnoʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪks/ | n. | 民族语言学 | The study of the relationships between language and culture, and how they mutually influence each other. | Example 1: Ethnolinguistics explores how language reflects a culture's worldview. 民族语言学探讨语言如何反映一种文化的 worldview。

Example 2: Her research in ethnolinguistics focuses on the language of a remote Amazonian tribe. 她在民族语言学方面的研究集中在一个偏远的亚马逊部落的语言上。 | Synonym: anthropological linguistics, linguistic anthropology | Antonym: N/A | noun
