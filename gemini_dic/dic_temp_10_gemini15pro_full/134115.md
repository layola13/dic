nonimpairment:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpeəmənt/
n.|无损伤；未受损害|The nonimpairment of the environment is crucial for sustainable development.  环境的未受损害对于可持续发展至关重要。|The contract guaranteed the nonimpairment of their rights.  合同保证了他们的权利不受损害。|近义词：intactness, integrity, soundness|反义词：impairment, damage, harm|词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + impairment (from impair, meaning "to weaken or damage")
