overclamour: /ˌōvərˈklæmər/ | v. | 盖过…的声音；大声疾呼压倒 | To shout down or drown out by making a louder noise. | The protesters overclamoured the speaker. 抗议者们的声音盖过了演讲者。 | The crowd overclamoured the announcement. 人群的喧闹声盖过了公告。 | 近义词: drown out, outshout, overwhelm | 反义词: be quiet, listen | 词性: 动词

overclamour: /ˌōvərˈklæmər/ | n. | 喧闹声；吵闹声 | A loud and insistent clamour or outcry, often used to drown out other sounds. | The overclamour of the market made it hard to hear. 市场的喧闹声使人难以听到。 |  The overclamour from the opposing fans was deafening. 对立球迷的喧闹声震耳欲聋。 | 近义词: uproar, din, commotion | 反义词: silence, quiet | 词性: 名词
