egocentrism: /ˌiːɡoʊˈsɛntrɪzəm/ | n. | 自我中心主义；利己主义 | The inability to see a situation from another person's point of view. The assumption that others share one's perceptions, thoughts, and feelings. |  Example: Her egocentrism prevented her from understanding her friend's feelings. 她的自我中心主义使她无法理解朋友的感受。

Example: The child's egocentrism was evident in his belief that the world revolved around him.  这个孩子的自我中心主义体现在他认为世界围着他转的信念上。 | 近义词: self-centeredness, narcissism, egoism | 反义词: altruism, empathy, selflessness | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From ego + centric + -ism.  Ego is from Latin, meaning "I". Centric refers to being centered or focused.  -ism is a suffix forming nouns of action, state, condition, doctrine, etc.
