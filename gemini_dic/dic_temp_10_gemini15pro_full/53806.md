disconfidence:/dɪsˈkɒnfɪd(ə)ns/
noun
不信任，缺乏信心
Lack of confidence; distrust.
He expressed disconfidence in the new manager's abilities. 他对新经理的能力表示不信任。
近义词:mistrust, doubt, suspicion, skepticism
反义词:confidence, trust, belief, faith
词性:n.


disconfidence:/dɪsˈkɒnfɪd(ə)ns/
noun
不自信，缺乏自信心
Lack of confidence in oneself; self-doubt.
Her disconfidence prevented her from applying for the promotion. 她的不自信使她没能申请晋升。
近义词:self-doubt, insecurity, hesitancy, timidity
反义词:self-confidence, assurance, self-assurance, boldness
词性:n.

词源:  "dis-" (expressing negation) + "confidence" (from Latin "confidentia," meaning "trust, reliance, assurance").
