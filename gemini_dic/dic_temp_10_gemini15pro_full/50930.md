desiccatory:/ˈdɛsɪkeɪtəri/ /ˈdɛsɪkətɔːri/ | adj. n. | 干燥的；干燥剂；干燥器 | causing or tending to cause dryness |  The arid climate had a desiccatory effect on the land.  干旱的气候对土地有干燥作用。| 近义词: drying, dehydrating, parching | 反义词: moisturizing, hydrating, humidifying | 词性: adjective, noun

desiccatory:/ˈdɛsɪkeɪtəri/ /ˈdɛsɪkətɔːri/ | adj. n. | 干燥的；干燥剂；干燥器 | a closed container containing a drying agent, used for keeping specimens or chemicals dry | Store the silica gel in a desiccatory. 将硅胶储存在干燥器中。| 近义词: drier, dehydrator | 反义词: humidifier | 词性: adjective, noun


词源: From Latin *desiccatus*, past participle of desiccare (“to dry up”), from de- (“thoroughly”) + siccare (“to dry”), from siccus (“dry”).
