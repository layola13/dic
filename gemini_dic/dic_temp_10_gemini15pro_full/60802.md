electrons:/ɪˈlektrɒnz/ (UK) /ɪˈlektrɑːnz/ (US)| n. |电子|A stable subatomic particle with a charge of negative electricity, found in all atoms and acting as the primary carrier of electricity in solids.|The flow of electrons through a wire creates an electric current. 电子在导线中的流动形成了电流。|Electrons orbit the nucleus of an atom. 电子绕原子核运行。|synonyms:  negative particle, beta particle|antonyms: proton, positron|noun
