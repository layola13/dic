clergywomen: /ˈklɜːrdʒɪˌwɪmɪn/ | n. | 女教士；女牧师 |  A woman who is ordained as a minister, priest, or other religious leader. |  例句：The clergywomen of the church worked tirelessly to help the community.  教堂的女教士们不知疲倦地帮助社区。

例句：Several clergywomen spoke at the interfaith conference.  几位女教士在跨宗教会议上发言。

近义词：priestess, minister, pastor, reverend, chaplain | 反义词: None (lack of a direct opposite, perhaps laity could be considered a broad contrast) | 词性: noun
