blamable:/ˈbleɪməbl/| adj.|应受责备的，可责怪的|Deserving blame; culpable.  Worthy of blame or censure.|The driver was blamable for the accident.  司机应对这起事故负责。|近义词：culpable, guilty, responsible, accountable, answerable|反义词：blameless, innocent, faultless|词性:形容词

词源: From blame + -able.  Ultimately from Old French blasmer.
