abductors:/əbˈdʌktərz/| n.|绑架者，拐骗者|  Muscles that draw a part away from the midline of the body or from another part.  Those who abduct someone. |The abductors demanded a ransom for the safe return of the child. 绑匪要求赎金以保证孩子的安全返回。

The abductors were caught and brought to justice. 绑匪被抓获并绳之以法。|近义词：kidnappers, snatchers|反义词: rescuers, liberators|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abductor*, from *abducere* "to lead away," from *ab* "away" + *ducere* "to lead."

USAGE->
The abductors were apprehended by the police. = 警察逮捕了绑架者。

The abductors' motives were unclear. = 绑架者的动机尚不清楚。
