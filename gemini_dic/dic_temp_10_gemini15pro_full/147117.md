overliveliness:/ˌoʊvərˈlaɪvlɪnɪs/
|n.|过度活跃，过分活泼|The overliveliness of the children made it difficult to concentrate. 孩子们过分的活泼让人难以集中注意力。|Overliveliness can sometimes be misinterpreted as disruptive behavior. 过度活跃有时会被误解为破坏性行为。|近义词：exuberance, boisterousness, restlessness|反义词：lethargy, apathy, inactivity|词性:名词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + liveliness (noun from lively + -ness)  由前缀"over-"（意为“过度”）和名词"liveliness"（由形容词"lively"加后缀"-ness"构成）组成。
