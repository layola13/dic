beanie:/ˈbiːni/| n.|无檐小便帽，针织帽|A small, close-fitting hat, typically knitted from wool.|He always wears a beanie in winter. 他冬天总是戴着针织帽。|近义词：cap, hat, toboggan|反义词:|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From American slang, possibly a shortening of *bean*, meaning "head".  The exact origin is uncertain.
