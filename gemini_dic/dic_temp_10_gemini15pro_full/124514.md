mounjaro:/ˌmaʊnˈdʒɑːroʊ/| n. |蒙扎罗|A brand name for tirzepatide, a medication used to treat type 2 diabetes. It works by mimicking the effects of two natural hormones, GLP-1 and GIP, which help regulate blood sugar levels and can also lead to weight loss.|Mounjaro helped her control her blood sugar levels significantly. 蒙扎罗显著地帮助她控制了血糖水平。|

Mounjaro allowed him to lose weight without feeling constantly hungry.  蒙扎罗让他在没有持续饥饿感的情况下减肥。|近义词:tirzepatide, Ozempic (similar mechanism but different drug), Rybelsus (oral semaglutide)|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
