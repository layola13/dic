noun
反无神论
opposition to antiatheism (the opposition to atheism).  It represents a defense of atheism against attacks or criticisms.
Antiatheisms are becoming more common as new arguments against atheism are proposed.  反无神论变得越来越普遍，因为新的反对无神论的论点被提出来了。
His book explored various antiatheisms and their philosophical underpinnings. 他的书探讨了各种反无神论及其哲学基础。

antonyms:  antiatheism, criticisms of atheism
