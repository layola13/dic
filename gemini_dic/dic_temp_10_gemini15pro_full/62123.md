enchorial:/ɛnˈkɔːriəl/
adjective
地方性的，本地的；通俗的，民间的；（古埃及文字）通俗体的
Relating to or used in a particular country or area; popular, common; (of ancient Egyptian script) demotic.
The Rosetta Stone contains hieroglyphic, demotic, and enchorial scripts. 罗塞塔石碑包含象形文字、通俗文字和地方文字。
Synonyms: local, indigenous, vernacular, demotic
Antonyms: foreign, exotic, esoteric
adjective

enchorial:/ɛnˈkɔːriəl/
noun
（古埃及的）通俗体文字
The demotic script of ancient Egypt.
The enchorial script was used for everyday writing in ancient Egypt. 通俗体文字在古埃及用于日常书写。
Synonyms: demotic
Antonyms: hieroglyphic
noun
