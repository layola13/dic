fixatives:/ˈfɪksətɪvz/
n.
固定剂；定影剂
Substances used to preserve biological specimens, tissues, or cells by stabilizing their structure and preventing decay.
The scientist used fixatives to prepare the tissue samples for microscopic examination. 科学家使用固定剂来制备用于显微镜检查的组织样本。
preservatives, stabilizers
