fibroid:/ˈfaɪbrɔɪd/| n. |纤维瘤；子宫肌瘤|A benign tumor composed mainly of fibrous connective tissue.  A fibroid is a noncancerous growth that develops from the muscle tissue of the uterus.|She had surgery to remove uterine fibroids. 她做了手术切除子宫肌瘤。|近义词：myoma, leiomyoma|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，因为它是指一种特定类型的肿瘤)|词性:noun
