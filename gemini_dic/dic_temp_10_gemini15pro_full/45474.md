curber:/ˈkɜːrbər/ | n. | 路边石；抑制者；马路牙子 | Someone or something that restrains or controls.  A stone or concrete edging to a raised path, road, or street. | The city installed new curbers along Main Street. 城市沿着主街安装了新的路缘石。|  The government acted as a curber on spending. 政府控制了开支。 | synonyms: restrainer, check, curb, limit | antonyms: promoter, encourager, instigator | noun
