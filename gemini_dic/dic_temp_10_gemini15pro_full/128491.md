newspapery:/ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpəri/
adjective
像报纸的；有报纸特色的
resembling or characteristic of a newspaper, especially in style or content.
The writing style was rather newspapery, lacking literary flair.  这写作风格很像报纸，缺乏文学色彩。
His report was very factual and newspapery. 他的报告非常写实，就像报纸报道一样。

近义词:journalistic, tabloid
反义词:literary, academic
词性:adjective
