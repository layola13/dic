nonconveyance:/ˌnɑːnkənˈveɪəns/
n.| 未运输；未转让；未传达|The nonconveyance of the property led to a legal dispute. |财产的未转让导致了一场法律纠纷。|
n.|未转让| The nonconveyance of the deed caused complications. |契约的未转让导致了复杂的情况。|
近义词:nondelivery, nontransmission, withholding|
反义词:conveyance, transfer, delivery|
词性:noun


词源:
From non- + conveyance.  Conveyance itself comes from Old French conveiance, ultimately from Latin *conviare "to accompany on the way."
