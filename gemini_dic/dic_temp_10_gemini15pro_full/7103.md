anesthetizing:/əˈnɛsθəˌtaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |麻醉；使麻木|Rendering insensible to pain or other sensation, typically by administering an anesthetic. |The doctor is anesthetizing the patient before the surgery. 医生正在手术前麻醉病人。| The dentist anesthetized my gums before pulling the tooth. 牙医在拔牙前麻醉了我的牙龈。 |synonyms: numbing, desensitizing, benumbing | antonyms: stimulating, sensitizing, awakening | verb (present participle)

anesthesia:/ˌænəsˈθiːʒə/ | n. |麻醉；麻木|Loss of feeling or awareness, especially of pain, induced by drugs or other agents.| The patient was under general anesthesia during the operation. 病人在手术期间处于全身麻醉状态。| Local anesthesia numbed the area before the stitches were put in.  在缝合之前，局部麻醉使该区域麻木。| synonyms: numbness, insensibility, narcosis | antonyms: sensation, feeling, awareness | noun
