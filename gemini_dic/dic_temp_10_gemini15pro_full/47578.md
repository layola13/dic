debasedness:/dɪˈbeɪsɪdnəs/
n.
堕落；卑鄙；贬值
The state of being debased; moral or artistic degradation.
The debasedness of the currency caused economic chaos.  货币贬值导致了经济混乱。
近义词:degradation, depravity, corruption, vileness
反义词:nobility, virtue, purity, integrity
词性:名词

debase:/dɪˈbeɪs/
v.
降低…的价值（或质量）；贬低；贬损
To reduce the quality or value of something.
He debased himself by lying. 他因为撒谎而降低了自己的身份。
近义词:degrade, corrupt, cheapen, demean
反义词:elevate, enhance, ennoble, improve
词性:动词
