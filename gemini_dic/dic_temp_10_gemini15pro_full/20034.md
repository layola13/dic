biometrics:/ˌbaɪəʊˈmetrɪks/ | n. | 生物识别技术；生物统计学 | The study and application of statistical analysis to biological data.  Also, the measurement and analysis of unique physical or behavioral characteristics (such as fingerprint or voice patterns) especially as a means of verifying personal identity. | Biometrics is becoming increasingly important in security systems. 生物识别技术在安全系统中变得越来越重要。| Biometric authentication is now used in many smartphones. 生物特征认证现在用于许多智能手机。 | synonyms:  biometric authentication, biometric identification, biometric technology, bioscience | antonyms: N/A | noun

biometric:/ˌbaɪəʊˈmetrɪk/ | adj. | 生物测定的；生物统计的 |  Relating to biometrics or using biometrics. |  A biometric scanner is used to verify the identity of employees. 生物识别扫描仪用于验证员工的身份。 | Biometric data is considered sensitive personal information. 生物识别数据被认为是敏感的个人信息。 | synonyms: biological, physiological | antonyms: N/A | adjective
