domiciled: /ˈdɒmɪsaɪld/ (UK) /ˈdɑːməsaɪld/ (US)| adj.|居住的，定居的|Having a domicile; residing.  Legally having one's permanent home in a particular place.|He is domiciled in France, although he works in Germany. 他在法国定居，尽管他在德国工作。|近义词: resident, inhabiting, dwelling, living|反义词: non-resident, homeless|词性:形容词
Etymology: From French *domicilié*, past participle of *domicilier* "to domicile," from Latin *domicilium* "dwelling, residence," from *domus* "house" + *-ilium* (suffix forming nouns of place).
