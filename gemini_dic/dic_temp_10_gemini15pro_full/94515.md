importuning:/ˌɪmpɔːrˈtjuːnɪŋ/
| adj. | 恳求的，再三要求的 |  Characterized by persistent and often troublesome requests or demands.  | The child's importuning requests for candy finally wore down his mother.  孩子不停地要糖果，最终使他母亲屈服了。 |  近义词：beseeching, pleading, insistent, persistent | 反义词：unobtrusive, discreet, reserved | 词性:形容词

importune:/ˌɪmpɔːrˈtjuːn/
| vt. | 恳求，再三要求 | To beg persistently and urgently. | He importuned the king for a pardon.  他恳求国王赦免他。 | 近义词：beseech, implore, entreat, solicit | 反义词：demand, command | 词性:动词

词源:
From Late Latin *importunare, from Latin importunus “unfit, inconvenient, troublesome,” from in- “not” + portus “harbor, haven.”  The original sense was "to be troublesome to," later developing into the meaning of "to make an insistent request."
