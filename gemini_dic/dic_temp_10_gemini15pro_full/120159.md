mind-blowing:/ˈmaɪndˌbloʊɪŋ/| adj.|令人兴奋的；极其令人印象深刻的；令人感到震撼的|Overwhelmingly impressive, exciting, or shocking.|The special effects in the movie were mind-blowing. 这部电影的特效令人叹为观止。|近义词：amazing, astonishing, incredible, phenomenal, stunning|反义词：boring, dull, mundane, unimpressive|词性:形容词
词源:mind (n.) + blow (v.) + -ing (suffix).  "Mind" refers to the intellect or thoughts, and "blow" evokes the image of something being explosively impressive, as if it "blows your mind."
