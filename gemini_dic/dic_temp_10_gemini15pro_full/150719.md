paracelsian:/ˌpærəˈsɛlsiən/
adjective
帕拉塞尔苏斯的；帕拉塞尔苏斯学说的
Relating to or characteristic of Paracelsus or his doctrines.
Paracelsian medicine emphasized the importance of chemical remedies. 帕拉塞尔苏斯医学强调化学疗法的重要性。
Near synonyms: alchemical, hermetic
Antonyms: conventional, orthodox
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Paracelsus + -ian.  Paracelsus (1493-1541), Swiss physician and alchemist, born Philippus Aureolus Theophrastus Bombastus von Hohenheim, adopted the name Paracelsus, meaning "beyond Celsus," a Roman physician.
