always:/ˈɔːlweɪz/ /ˈɑːlweɪz/
adverb|总是，一直，永远|always means at all times; on every occasion.|例句：He always arrives early. 他总是早到。|近义词：constantly, invariably, perpetually|反义词:never, rarely|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle English *alway, from Old English *ealne weg (“all the way,” literally “all way”), equivalent to all + way.
