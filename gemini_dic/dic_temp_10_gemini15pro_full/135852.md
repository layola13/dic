nonprospect:/ˌnɒnˈprɒspekt/ | n. | 非潜在客户，没有希望的销售对象 |  A person or entity that is not considered a likely customer or client. | The marketing team filtered out the nonprospects from their contact list. 营销团队从他们的联系人列表中过滤掉了非潜在客户。|  The company decided not to waste resources on nonprospects. 公司决定不在没有希望的销售对象上浪费资源。 | 近义词: non-buyer, unlikely customer | 反义词: prospect, lead, potential customer | 词性: 名词

Etymology: "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "prospect" (from Latin "prospectus," past participle of "prospicere" meaning "to look forward").
