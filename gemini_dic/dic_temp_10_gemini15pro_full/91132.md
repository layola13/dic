hybridist:/ˈhaɪbrɪdɪst/
n.
杂交者，杂交育种家
A person who produces hybrids, especially of plants or animals.
Luther Burbank was a famed hybridist who developed new varieties of fruits and vegetables.  路德·伯班克是一位著名的杂交学家，培育出了许多新品种的水果和蔬菜。

The hybridist crossed two different species of orchids.  这位杂交学家杂交了两种不同的兰花。
近义词：crossbreeder, hybridizer
反义词：(No direct antonym, potentially "purebreeder")
词性：noun
