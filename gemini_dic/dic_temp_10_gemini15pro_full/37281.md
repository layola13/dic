commandeer:/ˌkɑːmənˈdɪr/ | v. | 征用，强占 | To seize (something, such as a vehicle or building) for military or official use; to take arbitrarily or by force. | The soldiers commandeered the farmer's truck to transport supplies. 士兵们征用了农民的卡车来运送物资。

The government commandeered the building for use as a temporary hospital. 政府征用了这栋建筑物作为临时医院。 | synonyms: seize, confiscate, appropriate, expropriate | antonyms: return, release, give back | verb
