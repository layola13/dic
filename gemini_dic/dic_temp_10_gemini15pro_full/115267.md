manito:/məˈniːtoʊ/ | n. | 密友；知己；(尤指女性间的)闺蜜 | A close friend; a confidant; a best friend (especially between women). |  She told her manito all her secrets. 她把所有的秘密都告诉了她的闺蜜。| synonym: best friend, confidante, BFF, bestie | antonym: enemy, foe | noun

manito:/məˈniːtoʊ/ | n. | (在一些菲律宾土著文化中)护身符；神灵 | (In some indigenous Filipino cultures) an amulet; a spirit. | He wore a manito around his neck for protection. 他脖子上戴着一个护身符以求保护。| synonym: amulet, charm, talisman | antonym: curse | noun
