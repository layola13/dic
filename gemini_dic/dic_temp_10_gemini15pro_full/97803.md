injuriously:/ɪnˈdʒʊəriəsli/
adverb
有害地；伤害地
in a way that causes injury or harm.
The rumors affected her injuriously. 这些谣言对她有害。
Synonyms: harmfully, detrimentally, damagingly, destructively
Antonyms: beneficially, helpfully, advantageously
词性: 副词
