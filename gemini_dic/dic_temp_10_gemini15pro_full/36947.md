colourful:/ˈkʌləfʊl/ (British) /ˈkʌlərfl/ (American)| adj.|丰富多彩的；鲜艳的|Having many different and bright colours.|例句：The birds have colourful feathers. 这些鸟有着色彩斑斓的羽毛。|例句：She wore a colourful dress to the party. 她穿了一件色彩鲜艳的裙子去参加派对。|近义词：bright, vivid, vibrant|反义词：dull, drab, monochrome|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From 'colour' + '-ful'.  'Colour' comes from Old French 'colour' (and ultimately Latin 'color'). '-ful' is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by".
