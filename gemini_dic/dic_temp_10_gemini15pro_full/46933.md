darn:/dɑːrn/| v. n.|缝补，织补；诅咒；(用于加强语气)该死；破洞|To mend a hole in cloth by weaving yarn across it; A mild oath.|She darned the hole in her sock. 她织补了袜子上的洞。|近义词：mend, repair, fix, damn|反义词：tear, rip|词性：动词，名词

darn:/dɑːrn/| adj.| (informal) 该死的，讨厌的|Used to express mild annoyance or surprise.|This darn button keeps falling off!  这该死的纽扣一直掉！|近义词：cursed, confounded|反义词：blessed|词性：形容词
