casbah:/ˈkæzbɑː/ (US), /ˈkɑːzbɑː/ (UK)| n. | 城堡，城区，（尤指北非城市的）旧城区 |A citadel, or the older part of a North African city.  It typically refers to a walled fortress or fortified quarter within a city, often characterized by narrow, winding streets and traditional architecture.  The word evokes a sense of history, mystery, and exoticism.| We wandered through the casbah, fascinated by the vibrant marketplace. 我们在旧城区漫步，被充满活力的市场所吸引。

The casbah offered a glimpse into the city's rich past.  旧城区让我们得以一窥这座城市丰富的历史。|Synonym: citadel, fortress, old town, walled city | Antonym:  new city, suburb | Noun
