aniline: /ˈænɪlɪn/ (US), /ˈænɪliːn/ (UK)| n.|苯胺|A colorless oily liquid, C6H5NH2, derived from benzene and used in making dyes, explosives, and pharmaceuticals.  It is a primary aromatic amine.|Aniline is used in the manufacture of many drugs and dyes.|近义词: aminobenzene, phenylamine|反义词: None|noun
