astarte:/əˈstɑːrti/ /æˈstɑːrti/| n.|阿斯塔特; 阿斯塔蒂;|Astarte is the name of the principal female deity of the ancient Phoenicians, often associated with love, fertility, and war. She is considered the equivalent of Ishtar in Mesopotamian mythology and Aphrodite in Greek mythology.|Astarte was worshipped throughout the Phoenician world. 阿斯塔特在整个腓尼基世界都被崇拜。|The temple of Astarte was a magnificent structure. 阿斯塔特的寺庙是一座宏伟的建筑。|近义词：Ishtar, Aphrodite, Inanna|反义词:None|词性:noun
