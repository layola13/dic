-aine (This is a suffix, not a standalone word) /eɪn/
suffix
-ane 的变体,用于某些化学名称|
英文解析：A variant of -ane, used in some chemical names, especially for alkaloids.  It indicates saturation of the carbon atoms in the molecule.
例句：Cocaine is a powerful stimulant. 可卡因是一种强效兴奋剂。
例句：Morphine is a potent pain reliever. 吗啡是一种强效的止痛药。
