anthophyllite:/ænˈθɒfɪlaɪt/
n.
直闪石
A metamorphic mineral, a variety of amphibole, usually occurring in fibrous or columnar masses.
Anthophyllite is a common mineral in metamorphic rocks. 直闪石是变质岩中常见的矿物。
Anthophyllite asbestos is considered less hazardous than other forms of asbestos. 直闪石石棉被认为比其他形式的石棉危害更小。
