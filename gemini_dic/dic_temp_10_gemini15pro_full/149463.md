paddles:/ˈpædlz/ (US), /ˈpædəlz/ (UK)| n. v.|桨，拍子，划桨|A short oar with a wide blade, used without a rowlock.|He dipped his paddles into the water and pushed the canoe forward. 他把桨伸进水里，推动独木舟前进。|近义词：oars, sculls|反义词: None|词性: Noun, Verb

paddles:/ˈpædlz/ (US), /ˈpædəlz/ (UK)| v.|划桨，戏水，涉水|To move through shallow water.|The children paddled in the stream. 孩子们在小溪里戏水。|近义词：wade, dabble|反义词: None|词性: Verb

词源: From Old French *padle, from Vulgar Latin *patella, diminutive of Latin patina "pan, dish."
