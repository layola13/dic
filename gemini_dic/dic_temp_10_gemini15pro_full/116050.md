martialists:/ˈmɑːrʃəlɪsts/
noun
武术家；尚武主义者
People who practice martial arts or advocate for the importance of military strength.
The martialists gathered for the annual tournament. 武术家们聚集在一起参加年度锦标赛。
The government was influenced by a group of martialists. 政府受到一群尚武主义者的影响。
Synonyms: warriors, fighters, soldiers, militarists
Antonyms: pacifists, doves
