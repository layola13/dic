overdecorations:/ˌoʊvərˌdɛkəˈreɪʃənz/| n. |过度装饰；过分装饰| Excessive or superfluous decorations; ornamentation that is too elaborate or cluttered. |The overdecorations in the living room made it feel cramped and stuffy. 客厅里的过度装饰让它感觉狭小而闷热。| The Christmas tree, laden with overdecorations, nearly toppled over.  挂满过多装饰品的圣诞树几乎要倒了。| 近义词：excess, superfluity, clutter, ornamentation| 反义词:simplicity, minimalism| 词性: noun


overdecorate:/ˌoʊvərˈdɛkəˌreɪt/| v. |过度装饰；过分装饰| To decorate excessively; to adorn with too much ornamentation. |Be careful not to overdecorate the cake, or it will look gaudy. 小心不要把蛋糕装饰得太过火，否则会显得俗气。|  She overdecorated her apartment with knick-knacks and souvenirs. 她把自己的公寓用小摆设和纪念品装饰得过于繁琐。| 近义词：overdo, embellish excessively| 反义词:underdecorate, simplify| 词性: verb
