alpinist: /ˈælpɪnɪst/ | n. | 登山运动员；登山家 | A person who climbs mountains, especially as a sport. |  The alpinist reached the summit after days of arduous climbing.  这位登山运动员经过数天的艰苦攀登，终于到达了顶峰。| Synonyms: climber, mountaineer | Antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *alpiniste*, from *alpin* (“alpine”) + *-iste* (agent suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *Alpēs* (“the Alps”).
