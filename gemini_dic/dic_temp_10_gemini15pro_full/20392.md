bitchier:/ˈbɪtʃiər (US), ˈbɪtʃɪə(r) (UK)| adj.|更贱的，更恶毒的|Comparative form of "bitchy," meaning more spiteful, malicious, or unpleasant in a way that is typically associated with women.|例句：Her comments were getting bitchier as the night went on. 随着夜幕降临，她的评论变得越来越刻薄。

例句：She's always been a bit bitchy, but lately she's become even bitchier. 她一直都有些刻薄，但最近变得更刻薄了。|近义词：cattier, meaner, nastier, more malicious|反义词：kinder, nicer, sweeter, more pleasant|词性: 形容词

词源:
From bitch + -y + -er.  "Bitch" itself has a complex etymology, ultimately deriving from Old Norse "bikkja" meaning "female dog."  The suffix "-y" forms adjectives, and "-er" creates the comparative form.
