paranoia:/ˌpærəˈnɔɪə/ | n. |偏执狂，妄想症| A mental condition characterized by delusions of persecution, unwarranted jealousy, or exaggerated self-importance, typically elaborated into an organized system. | He suffers from paranoia and believes everyone is out to get him. 他患有偏执狂，认为每个人都想陷害他。| Her paranoia led her to believe that her phone was tapped. 她的偏执狂让她相信她的电话被窃听了。| synonyms: delusion, suspicion, distrust, mistrust | antonyms: trust, confidence, faith | noun
