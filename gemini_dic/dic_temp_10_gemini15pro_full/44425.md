cross-pollination: /ˌkrɒs ˌpɒləˈneɪʃn/ (UK) /ˌkrɔːs ˌpɑːləˈneɪʃn/ (US)
| n. |异花授粉；互相影响；融合|Cross-pollination between different varieties of apple can lead to new and improved flavors. 不同品种苹果之间的异花授粉可以带来新的和改良的风味。|The cross-pollination of ideas from different cultures can lead to innovation. 不同文化思想的融合可以带来创新。|Synonyms: cross-fertilization, hybridization, interbreeding|Antonyms: self-pollination, inbreeding|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From cross- + pollinate, from pollen.  "Cross-" indicates interaction between different entities, while "pollinate" refers to the transfer of pollen, leading to fertilization in plants.  The metaphorical meaning of exchanging ideas or influences developed from the original botanical sense.
