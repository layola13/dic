effigiation: /ˌefɪdʒiˈeɪʃən/
n.
肖像, 雕像; 描绘, 刻画
The act of making an effigy or representation of something, especially of a person.
The effigiation of the king was placed in the town square. 国王的雕像被放置在城镇广场上。
The artist's effigiation of the queen was remarkably lifelike.  艺术家对女王的描绘非常逼真。
