nontheatrical:/ˌnɑːnθiˈætrɪkəl/
adjective
非戏剧的；非剧场的；非供剧场放映的
Not related to or intended for the theater or stage; specifically, referring to films or other performances not intended for public exhibition in commercial theaters.
The company specialized in nontheatrical distribution of documentaries.  这家公司专门从事纪录片的非影院发行。
The film was made for nontheatrical exhibition, primarily for schools and community groups. 这部电影是为了非影院放映而制作的，主要面向学校和社区团体。
近义词:non-commercial, private
反义词:theatrical
