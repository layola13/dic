misreporters:/ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔːrtərz/ | n. | 误报者；错误报道者 |  People who report something incorrectly, either intentionally or unintentionally.  They may misrepresent facts, distort information, or provide inaccurate accounts of events. | The misreporters were criticized for spreading false information. 误报者因散布虚假信息而受到批评。|  The newspaper retracted the story after discovering the misreporters had fabricated evidence.  在发现误报者捏造证据后，报纸撤回了这篇报道。 | synonyms: fabricators, distorters, liars | antonyms: accurate reporters, journalists, truth-tellers | noun
