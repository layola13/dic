nonbearing:/ˌnɒnˈbeərɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈberɪŋ/
adjective
非承重的，不产生收益的
Not designed to support weight or producing income.
The nonbearing walls in the house can be easily removed.  这房子里的非承重墙可以很容易地拆除。
This investment is currently nonbearing. 这项投资目前没有收益。
近义词:unproductive, barren, infertile (for land), non-income-producing
反义词:bearing, load-bearing, productive, profitable
词性:adjective
