dilutely:/daɪˈluːtli/
|adv.|稀释地，淡薄地|in a dilute form; with a low concentration.|The paint was dilutely mixed with water.  这颜料用水稀释了。|Synonyms: weakly, thinly, faintly|Antonyms: strongly, concentratedly, thickly
|词性: 副词

dilute:/daɪˈluːt/
|v. |稀释，冲淡|To make a liquid thinner or weaker by adding water or another solvent.|She diluted the juice with water. 她用水稀释了果汁。|Synonyms: weaken, thin, water down|Antonyms: concentrate, strengthen, thicken
|词性: 动词

dilute:/daɪˈluːt/
|adj.|稀释的，淡薄的|Made thinner or weaker by adding water or another solvent.|The dilute solution was not effective.  这种稀释的溶液无效。|Synonyms: weak, thin, watery|Antonyms: strong, concentrated, thick
|词性: 形容词
