beebread:/ˈbiːbrɛd/| n.|蜂粮|A mixture of pollen and honey or nectar collected by worker bees and deposited in honeycomb cells as food for the larvae.|Worker bees produce beebread to feed the developing larvae. 工蜂生产蜂粮来喂养正在发育的幼虫。|近义词：pollen bread, ambrosia|反义词: None|noun
