greeze:/ɡriːz/ /ɡriz/| n.|油脂，润滑脂|A thick oily substance, especially as used as a lubricant.|The mechanic applied some greeze to the gears. 机械师给齿轮涂了一些润滑脂。|近义词：lubricant, oil, grease|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "cleaner," "degreaser")|noun
