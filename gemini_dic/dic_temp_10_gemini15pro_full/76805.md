gammoners:/ˈɡæmənəz/
n.
骗子; 哄骗者; 愚弄者
People who deceive, cheat, or hoax others.
He called the salesmen a bunch of gammoners, trying to sell faulty goods. 他称那些销售员是一群骗子，试图出售有缺陷的商品。
Synonyms:  cheats, swindlers, fraudsters, hoaxers, tricksters
Antonyms: honest people, truthful individuals
