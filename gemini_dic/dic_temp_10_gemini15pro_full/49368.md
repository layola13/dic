deliquescing:/ˌdelɪˈkwesɪŋ/ | adj. | 潮解的，溶解的 | Becoming liquid by absorbing moisture from the air. |  The deliquescing crystals soon dissolved into a puddle. 这些潮解的晶体很快溶解成一滩液体。|  dissolving, liquefying | solidifying, drying | adjective

deliquesce:/ˌdelɪˈkwes/ | v. | 潮解，溶解 | (of a solid) become liquid by absorbing moisture from the air. |  The salt deliquesced in the humid air.  盐在潮湿的空气中潮解了。| dissolve, liquefy | solidify, dry | verb

词源:
From Latin *deliquescere, present active infinitive of *deliquesco (“melt away”), from de- (“down, away”) + liquesco (“become liquid”), from liquidus (“liquid”).
