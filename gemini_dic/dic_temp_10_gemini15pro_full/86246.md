heave-ho:/ˌhiːv ˈhoʊ/
interjection
加油；用力拉；起锚
An exclamation used to encourage effort, especially when hauling or lifting.
例句：
Heave-ho! And over she goes! 加油！翻过来了！

例句：
With a mighty heave-ho, they lifted the heavy box onto the truck.  他们喊着“用力拉”，把沉重的箱子抬到了卡车上。


近义词：heave, haul, pull
反义词:None
词性:interjection


heave-ho:/ˌhiːv ˈhoʊ/
noun
用力拉；起锚
The action of heaving or hauling something heavy.
例句：
It took several heave-hos to get the boat onto the trailer.  费了好大劲才把船弄到拖车上。


例句：
The sailors gave a final heave-ho and the anchor was raised. 水手们最后喊了一声“起锚”，锚就升起来了。


近义词：heave, haul, pull
反义词:None
词性:noun
