extollments:/ɪkˈstəʊlmənts/ | n. |赞美；称颂；颂扬| Acts of praising something or someone highly. |The king received many extollments on his bravery. 国王因其勇敢而受到许多赞扬。|  His latest book was filled with extollments of the natural world. 他的最新著作充满了对自然世界的赞美。|Synonyms: praises, accolades, commendations, tributes|Antonyms: criticisms, condemnations, denunciations| Noun
