haan:/hɑːn/ | interj. | 是，是的 (Informal, chiefly South Asian) | An informal way of saying "yes," used primarily in South Asian countries. | Example: "Haan, I understand." 是的，我明白了。| Synonym: yes, yeah, yup | Antonym: no, nah | interjection

haan:/hɑːn/ | n. | 是 (Informal, chiefly South Asian) |  A noun form, less common, meaning "yes" or agreement. Primarily used in South Asian countries. | Example: "My answer is a haan." 我的答案是肯定的。| Synonym: yes, agreement, affirmation | Antonym: no, disagreement, refusal | noun
