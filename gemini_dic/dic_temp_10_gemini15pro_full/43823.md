creolistics:/ˌkriːəˈlɪstɪks/ | n. | 克里奥尔语研究，克里奥尔语学 | The study of creole languages, their formation, structure, and social context. | Creole linguistics is a fascinating field, exploring the development of new languages from the mixture of existing ones. 克里奥尔语语言学是一个引人入胜的领域，探索从现有语言的混合中发展新语言。| 近义词: creole studies | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From French *créolistique*, from *créole* ("creole") + *-istique* (suffix forming adjectives and nouns relating to a science or art). Ultimately from Portuguese *crioulo* (“a servant born in the master's house”), from *criar* (“to raise, bring up”), from Latin *creāre* (“to create”).
