coxcomb:/ˈkɒksˌkəʊm/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkskoʊm/ (US)| n.| 花花公子；纨绔子弟；鸡冠花|A conceited and foolish dandy, often vain about his appearance and accomplishments.  A coxcomb is also a jester's hat, resembling a rooster's comb, or the comb itself.| He strutted around like a coxcomb, showing off his expensive new suit. 他像个花花公子一样昂首阔步，炫耀他昂贵的新西装。|The rooster proudly displayed his bright red coxcomb.  这只公鸡骄傲地展示着它鲜红色的鸡冠。|Synonyms: dandy, fop, popinjay, peacock|Antonyms: modest person, wallflower|Noun
