obnoxiousness:/əbˈnɑːkʃəsnəs/ |n.|令人讨厌; 讨厌的行为; 惹人厌烦|The obnoxiousness of his behavior made everyone uncomfortable.|他的行为令人讨厌，让每个人都感到不舒服。|Synonyms: offensiveness, unpleasantness, repulsiveness|Antonyms: pleasantness, agreeableness, attractiveness|noun

obnoxiously:/əbˈnɑːkʃəsli/ |adv.|令人讨厌地; 惹人生气地|He obnoxiously interrupted the speaker several times.|他令人讨厌地多次打断演讲者。|Synonyms: offensively, unpleasantly, rudely|Antonyms: pleasantly, agreeably, politely|adverb

obnoxious:/əbˈnɑːkʃəs/ |adj.|讨厌的; 令人不快的; 惹人讨厌的|His obnoxious behavior made it difficult to like him.|他令人讨厌的行为让人很难喜欢他。|Synonyms: offensive, unpleasant, repulsive|Antonyms: pleasant, agreeable, attractive|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin obnoxius ("exposed to harm, liable, subject, submissive"), from ob- ("towards, against") + noxa ("harm, injury, damage").
