commies:/ˈkɑːmiz/| n.|共产党员，共产党同情者 (derogatory)|A derogatory and informal term for communists or those sympathetic to communism.  It is considered offensive and should be avoided in formal or respectful contexts.|Example: He called them a bunch of commies. 他称他们是一群共产党员。

Example:  She dismissed his ideas as commie propaganda. 她把他的想法斥为共产党宣传。


|近义词：communists, reds, Marxists (These are more neutral terms, but "reds" can also be derogatory)|反义词:capitalists, anti-communists|词性:noun (plural)

commie:/ˈkɑːmi/| n.|共产党员，共产党同情者 (derogatory)|A derogatory and informal term for a communist or someone sympathetic to communism. It is considered offensive and should be avoided in formal or respectful contexts.|Example: He was labeled a commie during the Cold War. 在冷战期间，他被贴上了共产党员的标签。

Example: Don't call people commies, it's disrespectful. 不要叫别人共产党员，这是不尊重的。


|近义词：communist, red, Marxist (These are more neutral terms, but "red" can also be derogatory)|反义词:capitalist, anti-communist|词性:noun (singular)
