claimable:/ˈkleɪməbl/| adj.|可要求的；可认领的；可索赔的|That which can be claimed; suitable or eligible to be claimed.  Able to be claimed. |The prize is claimable within 30 days of the draw.  该奖品在开奖后30天内可以领取。|Synonyms: retrievable, obtainable, collectible, due, payable|Antonyms: unclaimable, forfeited, lost|adjective
