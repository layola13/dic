incrediblenesses: /ɪnˈkrɛdəbəlˌnɛsɪz/ | n. | 不可思议，难以置信 |  The state or quality of being incredible; incredibility.  It refers to multiple instances or aspects of incredibility.  | The incrediblenesses of his story made it hard to believe.  他故事的种种不可思议之处让人难以相信。| Synonyms: incredibilities, marvels, wonders, miracles | Antonyms: believabilities, plausibilities, ordinariness | Noun

Etymology: From incredible + -ness.  "Incredible" comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin incredibilis, from in- ‘not’ + credibilis (from credere ‘believe’). The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, meaning "the state or quality of being".
