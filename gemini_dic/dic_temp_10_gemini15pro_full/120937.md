miscellaneously:/ˌmɪsəˈleɪniəsli/
adv.
杂乱地，各种各样地
in a miscellaneous manner; in a variety of ways.
The books were miscellaneously piled on the desk. 书杂乱地堆在桌子上。
Synonyms: diversely, variously, assortedly
Antonyms: uniformly, homogeneously
词性: 副词
