indelicate:/ˌɪnˈdɛlɪkɪt/ | adj. |不雅的，粗俗的，不得体的|Lacking good taste or refinement.|It was indelicate of him to mention her divorce.  他提起她的离婚是很不得体的。|Synonyms: improper, coarse, vulgar, gauche|Antonyms: delicate, refined, tactful, proper|adjective
