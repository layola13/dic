breadless:/ˈbredlɪs/
adjective
无面包的；缺面包的
without bread; lacking bread
The breadless family struggled to survive the harsh winter.  无面包的家庭挣扎着度过严冬。
The refugees were breadless and hungry.  难民们没有面包，饥肠辘辘。
synonyms: impoverished, destitute, penniless
antonyms: wealthy, affluent, prosperous
词性: adjective
