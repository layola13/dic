debrief:/diːˈbriːf/ | v. n. |汇报，询问，听取汇报；简报会|To question (someone, typically a soldier, astronaut, or diplomat) about a completed mission or undertaking.  To give a summary of information to someone. |The pilot was debriefed after every flight. 飞行员每次飞行后都要进行汇报。| Synonyms: interrogate, question, interview, examine, quiz, pump, grill; inform, brief, update, fill in | Antonyms: conceal, withhold | verb

debrief:/diːˈbriːf/ | n. |简报会，汇报会|A meeting for giving and receiving information or instructions.|The team held a debrief after the project was completed. 项目完成后，团队举行了一次汇报会。| Synonyms: briefing, meeting, conference, discussion | Antonyms: N/A | noun
