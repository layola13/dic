breakfasting: /ˈbrɛkfəstɪŋ/
| n./ v.|吃早餐|The act of eating breakfast.  It can also refer to the meal itself.|
|例句：We were breakfasting on the patio when the phone rang. 我们正在露台上吃早餐时，电话响了。|
|例句：The hotel offers breakfasting from 7am to 10am.  这家酒店从早上7点到10点提供早餐。|
|近义词：having breakfast, eating breakfast|
|反义词: fasting|
|词性:  Noun/Verb (present participle of "breakfast")|
|词源：From "breakfast" + "-ing" suffix. "Breakfast" comes from "break" (ending a period of fasting) and "fast" (abstinence from food).|
