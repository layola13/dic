inconvertibleness:/ˌɪnkənˈvɜːrtəbəlnəs/

例句：The inconvertibleness of his beliefs made it difficult to reason with him.  他信念的不可转变性使人很难与他讲道理。|近义词：non-exchangeability, immutability, inflexibility|反义词：convertibility, exchangeability, flexibility|词性: noun
