hyperoestrogenism: /ˌhaɪpərˈiːstrədʒənɪzəm/
| n. |雌激素过多症|A condition characterized by excessive levels of estrogen in the body.|Hyperestrogenism can cause a variety of symptoms, including irregular periods and breast tenderness. 雌激素过多症会导致各种症状，包括月经不调和乳房胀痛。|近义词: estrogen excess|反义词: hypoestrogenism|词性: noun

Etymology:
hyper- (Greek: over, excessive) + estrogen + -ism (suffix forming nouns of condition or process)
