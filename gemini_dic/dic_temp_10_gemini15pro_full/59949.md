edibility:/ˌedɪˈbɪləti/
n.
可食性
the quality of being safe or fit to be eaten.
例句：The edibility of wild mushrooms should always be checked by an expert. 野生蘑菇的可食性应该始终由专家检查。

例句：The chef questioned the edibility of the strangely colored fruit.  厨师质疑这种颜色奇怪的水果的可食性。

近义词：consumability, palatability
反义词：inedibility, toxicity
词性：noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *edibilitatem, from Latin edibilis ("eatable")  + -ity.  Edibilis is from edere ("to eat").
