architecturally:/ˌɑːrkɪˈtektʃərəli/
adjective
建筑上地，关于建筑地
In terms of architecture; with regard to the principles or style of architecture.
The building is architecturally significant. 这座建筑在建筑方面意义重大。
The house is architecturally stunning.  这栋房子建筑风格令人惊叹。
Synonyms: structurally, constructionally, design-wise
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider conceptually, aesthetically if contrasting the focus)
词性: adjective
