intactable:/ɪnˈtæktəbəl/
adjective
不可改变的；难以处理的；倔强的
not capable of being changed or influenced; difficult to deal with or manage
例句：His intractable cough kept him awake all night. 他顽固的咳嗽让他整夜无法入睡。

例句：The government faces an intractable problem of high unemployment. 政府面临着高失业率这一棘手的问题。

近义词：unyielding, inflexible, stubborn, obstinate, adamant
反义词：flexible, malleable, tractable, amenable
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *intactābilis, from in- + Late Latin tactābilis (“tangible”), from Latin tangō (“touch”). Equivalent to intact +‎ -able.
