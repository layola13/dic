bluntly:/ˈblʌntli/| adv.|直率地，坦白地，率直地|In a direct and honest way, often seeming rude or insensitive.|He told her bluntly that she was wrong. 他直截了当地告诉她，她错了。|近义词：frankly, directly, candidly, plainly|反义词: subtly, indirectly, tactfully|词性: 副词
blunt:/blʌnt/| adj. vt.|钝的，迟钝的；使钝|Having a dull edge or point; not sharp.  To make something less sharp.|The knife was too blunt to cut the meat. 这把刀太钝了，切不了肉。 He bluntly refused her request. 他断然拒绝了她的请求。|近义词：(adj.) dull, obtuse (vt.) weaken, deaden |反义词: (adj.) sharp, keen (vt.) sharpen, hone|词性: 形容词，动词
