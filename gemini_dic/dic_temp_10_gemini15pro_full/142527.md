ossified:/ˈɒsɪfaɪd/ /ˈɑːsəfaɪd/| adj.|僵化的；骨化的；硬化的|Having become rigid or inflexible, especially in attitudes or beliefs; changed into bone.|例句：His political views had ossified over the years. 他的政治观点多年来已经僵化了。|例句：The cartilage gradually ossifies into bone. 软骨逐渐骨化成骨。|近义词：rigid, inflexible, hardened, fixed, set, entrenched|反义词：flexible, adaptable, malleable, fluid|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *ossificāre*, from *os* (genitive *ossis*) "bone" + *-ficāre* (from *facere* "to make").
