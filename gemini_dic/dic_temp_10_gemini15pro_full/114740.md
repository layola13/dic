malnutritions:/ˌmælnuˈtrɪʃənz/ | n. | 营养不良 |  The state of being poorly nourished; inadequate nutrition.  Lack of proper nutrition, caused by not having enough to eat, not eating enough of the right things, or being unable to use the food that one does eat. |  Malnutritions are a serious problem in many developing countries. 营养不良是许多发展中国家的一个严重问题。

Malnutritions can lead to a weakened immune system. 营养不良会导致免疫系统减弱。 | 近义词: undernourishment, malnutrition | 反义词: nourishment,  good nutrition | 词性: noun
