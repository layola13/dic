counterwheel:/ˈkaʊntərˌwiːl/
noun|对轮，平衡轮|A wheel or roller placed so as to oppose or balance another; specifically, a wheel in a timepiece that regulates its speed.|The counterwheel in this old clock is worn down.  这个老式钟表里的对轮磨损了。|Synonyms: balance wheel, regulating wheel|Antonyms: N/A|n.
