beseechment:/bɪˈsiːtʃmənt/
n.
恳求; 乞求
The act of beseeching; earnest or urgent request.
The king ignored her beseechment for mercy. 国王无视了她恳求宽恕的请求。
近义词：supplication, plea, entreaty, petition
反义词：demand, command, order
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *besechement, equivalent to beseech + -ment.  Beseech itself comes from Old English besēcan "to seek, implore, ask eagerly," from be- "thoroughly" + sēcan "to seek."
