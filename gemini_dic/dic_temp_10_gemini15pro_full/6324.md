amounts:/əˈmaʊnts/| n. v.|数量，金额；总计，等于|The total amounts to $500.  总金额达500美元。|Synonyms: totals, quantity, sum |Antonyms: lack, shortage |noun

amounts:/əˈmaʊnts/| v.|总计，等于|His reply amounts to a refusal. 他的回答等于拒绝。|Synonyms: equals, adds up to, signifies |Antonyms: subtracts from, decreases |verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *amunter "to ascend, rise, mount," from amont "upward," from a mont "to the mountain," from Latin ad montem "to the mountain." The financial sense developed from the idea of "adding up to."
