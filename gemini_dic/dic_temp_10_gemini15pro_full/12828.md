associableness:/əˌsoʊʃəbəlˌnɛs/
n.| 社交性，合群，友善 | The quality of being associable; sociability.  The ability to be friendly and mix with others easily. | Her associableness made her a popular member of the team. 她的社交能力使她成为团队中受欢迎的成员。| 近义词：sociability, gregariousness, affability | 反义词：unsociability, aloofness, introversion | 词性: 名词

词源: From associable + -ness.  Associable comes from associate (verb) + -able.  Ultimately from Latin *associare*, from ad- 'to' + socius 'companion'.
