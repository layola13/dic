out-of-square:/ˌaʊt əv ˈskwɛər/ | adj. | 不成直角的，不正直的，不正当的 | Not at right angles; not straight, honest, or fair. | The carpenter realized the frame was out-of-square and had to rebuild it.  木匠意识到框架不成直角，不得不重新建造。 | The businessman's out-of-square dealings eventually landed him in jail.  这位商人的不正当交易最终使他入狱。 | 近义词: crooked, askew, awry, unfair, dishonest | 反义词: square, straight, honest, fair | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "out of" + "square". "Out of" indicates deviation from a standard, and "square" refers to a right angle or something honest and fair.  来源于"out of" + "square"。"Out of"表示偏离标准，"square"指直角或诚实公平的事物。
