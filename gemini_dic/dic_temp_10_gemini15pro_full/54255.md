disgraceful:/dɪsˈɡreɪsfəl/| adj.|可耻的，丢脸的，不光彩的|Bringing shame or disgrace; dishonorable.  Characterized by a lack of grace or refinement; unbecoming.|His behavior was disgraceful. 他的行为很可耻。|近义词：shameful, dishonorable, ignominious, discreditable|反义词：honorable, respectable, creditable|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from disgrace + -ful.
