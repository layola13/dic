blench:/blɛntʃ/ /blɛntʃ/ | vi. | 畏缩，退缩；变白 | To flinch or shrink back from something unpleasant or frightening; to turn pale. |  The sight of the blood made him blench.  看到血他吓得脸都白了。

He blenched at the thought of going to the dentist. 他一想到要去看牙医就畏缩了。 | 近义词: flinch, recoil, wince, blanch | 反义词:  embrace, welcome, confront | 词性: 动词

blench:/blɛntʃ/ /blɛntʃ/ | vt. | 使变白 | To make something white or pale.  | The harsh chemicals blenched the fabric.  强烈的化学物质使织物褪色变白了。

The sun blenched the vibrant colors of the painting.  阳光使画作鲜艳的色彩变白了。 | 近义词: blanch, whiten, bleach | 反义词: darken, color | 词性: 动词
