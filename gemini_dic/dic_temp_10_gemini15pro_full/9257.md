antipatriarchs:/ˌæntiˈpeɪtriɑːrks/ |n.| 反父权制者，反对父权制的人|A person who opposes patriarchy, a social system in which men hold primary power and predominate in roles of political leadership, moral authority, social privilege and control of property.|  The antipatriarchs protested against the all-male panel. 反父权制者抗议全由男性组成的专家组。|  She identifies as an antipatriarch and actively works to dismantle systems of male dominance. 她认为自己是一个反父权制者，并积极致力于瓦解男性主导的体系。| 近义词: feminist, egalitarian | 反义词: patriarch, traditionalist |词性: 名词
