cross-current:/ˈkrɒsˌkʌrənt/ (US) /ˈkrɒsˌkʌrənt/ (UK)| n. |交叉水流；反向潮流；逆流|A current flowing across another current, often creating turbulence or difficulties.  A conflicting trend or influence.|The strong cross-currents made it difficult to swim straight.  强劲的交叉水流使人难以直线游动。|synonyms: counter-current, undercurrent, riptide|antonyms: main current, mainstream|noun

cross-current:/ˈkrɒsˌkʌrənt/ (US) /ˈkrɒsˌkʌrənt/ (UK)| adj. |交叉的；反向的；逆流的|Running counter or across; opposing.|Cross-current winds made landing the plane tricky.  逆向风使飞机降落变得棘手。|synonyms: opposing, conflicting, contrary|antonyms: supporting, agreeing, parallel|adjective
