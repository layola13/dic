orphaline: /ˈɔːrfəlɪn/ | n. | 孤儿行；诗中长度不足一行的诗行 | A short line of verse, especially one at the end of a stanza that does not rhyme with the others. |  The poem ended with a poignant orphaline. 这首诗以一行令人心酸的孤儿行结尾。 | synonym: short line, incomplete line | antonym: full line | noun

orphaline: /ˈɔːrfəlɪn/ | adj. | 孤儿般的；长度不足一行的 | Resembling or characteristic of an orphan; (of a line of verse) shorter than the surrounding lines. | The orphaline line gave the stanza a sense of incompleteness.  这行孤儿行使诗节有一种不完整的感觉。| synonym: orphaned, incomplete | antonym: complete, full | adjective
