armaturing:/ˈɑːrmətʃərɪŋ/ | n. | 装配钢筋;  armadura; 绕组; 电枢; 衔铁;  |The process of providing or fitting with an armature, especially in reinforced concrete construction. The steel framework used to reinforce concrete structures. The moving part of an electrical machine (e.g., a motor or generator), which rotates within a magnetic field. |  The workers were busy armaturing the new bridge. 工人们正忙着给新桥装配钢筋.  The armature of the motor was damaged. 电机的电枢损坏了. | Synonyms: reinforcement, framework, winding | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps: weakening, dismantling) | noun
