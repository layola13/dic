hardcovered:/ˌhɑːrdˈkʌvərd/| adj.|精装的；硬皮书的|A book that is bound with a stiff cover, typically of cardboard covered with cloth, leather, or other material. |例句：I prefer hardcovered books because they are more durable. 我更喜欢精装书，因为它们更耐用。

例句：She bought a hardcovered first edition of her favorite novel. 她买了一本精装的初版她最喜欢的小说。|近义词：hardback, casebound|反义词：paperback, softcover|词性:形容词

词源:
From "hard" + "cover" + "-ed".  "Hard" meaning firm and rigid, and "cover" referring to the outer protective layer.  The "-ed" suffix transforms the noun "cover" into an adjective describing something that *has* a hard cover.
