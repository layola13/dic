overconcentrations: /ˌoʊvərˌkɑːnsənˈtreɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度集中；过分集中 |  Excessive concentrations, often referring to resources, investments, or industries within a limited area or sector.  This can lead to vulnerability and instability. | The overconcentrations of power in a few large companies stifles competition.  几家大公司权力过度集中扼杀了竞争。

Overconcentrations of farming in certain areas can deplete the soil.  某些地区农业过度集中会导致土壤枯竭。 | near synonyms:  clusters, accumulations, centralization, monopolies | near antonyms: distribution, dispersal, diversification | noun
