idiopathic:/ˌɪdiəˈpæθɪk/| adj.|特发性的，原因不明的|Relating to or denoting any disease or condition that arises spontaneously or for which the cause is unknown.|The doctor diagnosed the patient with idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis. 医生诊断该病人患有特发性肺纤维化。|Synonyms: essential, primary, spontaneous|Antonyms: secondary, acquired|adjective

idiopathic:/ˌɪdiəˈpæθɪk/| adj.|自发的|Occurring spontaneously or without apparent cause.|Idiopathic seizures can be frightening and require medical attention. 自发性癫痫发作可能很可怕，需要就医。|Synonyms: innate, inherent, congenital|Antonyms: acquired, induced|adjective
