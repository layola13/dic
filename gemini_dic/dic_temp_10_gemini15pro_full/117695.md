melainotype: /məˈleɪnəˌtaɪp/ /məˈleɪnəˌtaɪp/ | n. | 黑色照片，铁版照相 |A photographic process that produces a positive image on a blackened iron plate. |  Example: The melaintype, with its rich detail and matte surface, was a popular photographic method in the 19th century.  铁版照相以其丰富的细节和磨砂表面，是19世纪流行的摄影方法。

Example:  He found a collection of old melaintypes in his grandmother's attic. 他在他祖母的阁楼里发现了一批古老的铁版照片。 | 近义词: ferrotype, tintype | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider photographic processes like daguerreotype or ambrotype which are different processes) | 词性: noun
