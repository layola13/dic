doze:/doʊz/| vi. n.|打瞌睡，小睡； doze off 打盹|To sleep lightly and briefly.  |例句：I dozed off during the lecture. 我在讲座期间打瞌睡了。

例句：The cat dozed in a patch of sunlight.  猫在一片阳光下打盹。|近义词：nap, slumber, drowse|反义词:awaken, stay awake|词性:verb, noun
