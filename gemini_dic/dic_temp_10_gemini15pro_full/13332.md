atishoo:/ætɪˈʃuː/ | int. | 打喷嚏（拟声词） |  The sound made when sneezing.  |  Example: Atishoo! Excuse me, I think I'm coming down with a cold. 打喷嚏！不好意思，我觉得我感冒了。

Example:  He let out a loud "atishoo!" and grabbed a tissue. 他大声地打了个喷嚏，然后抓起一张纸巾。| 近义词: achoo, kerchoo | 反义词:  (无，拟声词通常没有反义词) | 词性: interjection
