berhyme:/bɪˈraɪm/
verb
使押韵；用韵诗表达
to put into rhyme; to express in rhymed verse
The poet berhymed "love" with "dove." 诗人将“爱”与“鸽”押韵。
rhyme, versify
- (no common antonym)
