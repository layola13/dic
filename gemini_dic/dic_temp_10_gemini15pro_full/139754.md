ocrea: /ˈəʊkriə/ /ˈɒkriə/ | n. |托叶鞘，膜状鞘|A sheath formed from two stipules that partially or wholly surrounds the stem above the node, characteristic of the Polygonaceae (buckwheat family).|The ocrea of the buckwheat plant is quite distinctive.  荞麦植物的托叶鞘非常独特。

The ocrea protects the delicate new growth of the stem. 托叶鞘保护茎上娇嫩的新生部分。|近义词:sheath, stipular sheath |反义词: (No direct antonym exists for this specialized botanical term) |词性:noun
