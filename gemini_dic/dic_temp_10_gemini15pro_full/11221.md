archival:/ɑːrˈkaɪvəl/
adjective
档案的；档案馆的
Relating to or suitable for archives; relating to or constituting historical records.
The museum has an extensive archival collection of photographs.  博物馆有一个广泛的照片档案收藏。
The documents are archival and should be preserved. 这些文件是档案性质的，应该妥善保存。
Synonyms: record, historic, historical
Antonyms: contemporary, modern
词性: 形容词
