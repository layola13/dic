It was extremely influential in the 1980s and 1990s, but declined in importance in the 2000s and eventually ceased operation. | COMDEX was the place to be for new product announcements in the 1990s.  在20世纪90年代，COMDEX是新产品发布的重要场所。|  synonyms:  computer show, trade fair, expo | antonyms: N/A | noun
