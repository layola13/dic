mechanography:/ˌmɛkəˈnɒɡrəfi/ /ˌmɛkəˈnɑːɡrəfi/
n.
机械制图，打字
The process or art of writing or typing by means of a machine, especially a typewriter.
Mechanography played a crucial role in office work before the advent of computers.  机械制图在计算机出现之前在办公室工作中起着至关重要的作用。
Typing, keyboarding
Handwriting, calligraphy
noun
