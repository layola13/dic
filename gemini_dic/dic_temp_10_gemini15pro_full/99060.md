interassure:/ˌɪntərəˈʃʊr/ | v. |互相保证；互相担保| To give each other mutual assurance or confidence.  | Example 1: The two parties interassured each other that the deal would be beneficial for both. 双方互相保证这笔交易对彼此都有利。

Example 2:  They interassured one another of their continued support during the difficult times. 他们在困难时期互相保证会继续支持对方。 | 近义词: reassure, guarantee, promise | 反义词:  doubt, mistrust | 词性: 动词


词源:  "inter-" (between, among) + "assure" (to give confidence to). The prefix "inter-" indicates a reciprocal action between two or more parties.
