absorbabilities:/əbˌzɔːrbəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |吸收率，吸收能力|The absorbabilities of different materials vary greatly. 不同材料的吸收率差异很大。|例句：The researchers compared the absorbabilities of various types of sponges. 研究人员比较了各种类型海绵的吸收率。

The high absorbabilities of these paper towels make them ideal for cleaning spills. 这些纸巾的高吸收性使它们成为清洁溢出物的理想选择。|近义词：absorption capacities, uptake rates|反义词：impermeabilities|词性：名词(plural)

ETYMOLOGY->
From "absorbable" + "-ity" (suffix forming nouns of quality or state) + "-ies" (plural suffix).  "Absorb" comes from Latin "absorbēre" (to suck up, swallow down), from "ab-" (from, away) + "sorbēre" (to suck in).
