metapodiale: /ˌmɛtəˈpoʊdiˌeɪl/ | n. | 跖骨 | A bone of the metapodium, which in humans comprises the metatarsals of the foot and the metacarpals of the hand. |  The fracture involved the distal end of the third metapodiale. 第三跖骨远端骨折。 | 近义词: metatarsal (in the foot), metacarpal (in the hand) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *μεταπόδιον (metapodion) "after the foot,"  from μετά (meta, “after, among, with”) + πούς (pous, "foot").
