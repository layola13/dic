objectify: /əbˈdʒɛktɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 使客观化；使具体化；物化；将…视为物体 | To treat someone as an object, especially a sexual object, rather than as a person with feelings and thoughts. |  She felt objectified by the photographer's lingering gaze. 她觉得摄影师的目光把她物化了。 |  He objectified women, seeing them only as potential conquests. 他物化女性，只把她们看作是潜在的征服对象。| synonyms: dehumanize, depersonalize, materialize, reify | antonyms: personalize, humanize, subjectify | verb
