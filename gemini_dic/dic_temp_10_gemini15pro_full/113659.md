machinima:/məˈʃiːnɪmə/ /məˈʃɪnɪmə/| n. |机械动画；游戏动画|A genre of filmmaking that uses real-time computer graphics engines, often from video games, to create animated films.|Example:  The machinima used footage from the popular game "World of Warcraft." 这部机械动画使用了热门游戏《魔兽世界》的画面。

Example:  Creating machinima can be a cost-effective way to produce animated content. 制作机械动画可以是一种经济有效的动画内容制作方式。|近义词:computer animation, CGI|反义词:traditional animation|词性:noun
