inergetic:/ˌɪnərˈdʒɛtɪk/
adjective
缺乏能量的，不活泼的
Lacking energy or vitality; inactive.
例句：He felt inergetic after a long day of work.  他工作了一整天后感到浑身无力。

例句：The inergetic economy showed little sign of recovery.  死气沉沉的经济几乎没有复苏的迹象。

近义词：lethargic, sluggish, listless, apathetic, inactive, passive
反义词：energetic, active, dynamic, vigorous, lively
词性：adjective
词源：From Latin *inergēticus, from iners (idle, inactive) + -ēticus (suffix forming adjectives).
