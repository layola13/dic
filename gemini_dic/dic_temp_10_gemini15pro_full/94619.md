impregnators:/ɪmˈprɛɡneɪtərz/
n.
浸渍器；使充满者；使怀孕者
A person or device that impregnates something.  The process of impregnating involves saturating or filling something with a substance.
The wood impregnators applied a sealant to protect the deck from the elements. 木材浸渍器涂了一种密封剂来保护甲板免受自然因素的影响。
The company specializes in manufacturing impregnators for the textile industry. 这家公司专门生产纺织业用的浸渍器。
synonyms: saturators, fillers, infusers, injectors
antonyms: drainers, emptiers, extractors
noun
