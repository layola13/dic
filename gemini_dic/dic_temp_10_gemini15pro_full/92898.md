idealnesses:/aɪˈdiːəlnɪsɪz/
|n.|理想状态；完美性|The state or quality of being ideal; perfection.|His pursuit of idealnesses in his art eventually led to frustration. 他对艺术完美性的追求最终导致了挫败感。|近义词：perfection, ideality|反义词：imperfection, flawedness|词性: 名词

Etymology: From ideal + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  Derived ultimately from Latin *idealis*, from idea "form, pattern," from Greek idea "form, pattern, mental representation."
