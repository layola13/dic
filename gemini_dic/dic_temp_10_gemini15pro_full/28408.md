carbonations:/ˌkɑːrbəˈneɪʃənz/ | n. |碳酸化作用；充碳作用|The process of dissolving carbon dioxide in a liquid, typically water, to produce a carbonated beverage.|The carbonation in the soda gives it a fizzy taste. 苏打水中的碳酸化作用使其具有嘶嘶的口感。| 近义词: effervescence, fizz | 反义词: flatness | noun
