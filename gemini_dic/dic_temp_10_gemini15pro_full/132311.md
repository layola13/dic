nondefinable:/ˌnɒndɪˈfaɪnəbl/ | adj. | 不可定义的，无法下定义的 | Not able to be defined or explained clearly. |  This concept is so abstract that it's practically nondefinable.  这个概念太抽象了，几乎无法定义。 |
Example: The feeling of love is often described as nondefinable.  爱的感觉通常被描述为无法定义的。|
Synonyms: indefinable, inexpressible, indescribable | Antonyms: definable, describable, explicable | word property: adjective
