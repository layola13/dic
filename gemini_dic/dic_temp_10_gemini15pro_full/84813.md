handy:/ˈhændi/| adj.|方便的，手边的，灵巧的|Convenient to use or handle; readily available; skillful with one's hands.|例句：A handy little tool. 一件方便的小工具。|近义词：useful, convenient, practical, skillful, deft|反义词:inconvenient, awkward, clumsy|词性:形容词
Etymology: From hand + -y.
