misauthorizes:/ˌmɪsˈɔːθəˌraɪzɪz/
verb
未经授权；错误授权
to authorize wrongly or improperly; to give incorrect or improper authorization to.
The clerk misauthorized the return and the customer received a full refund for stolen merchandise.  店员错误地授权了退货，顾客偷来的商品获得了全额退款。
The junior officer misauthorized the airstrike, leading to a tragic loss of civilian life.  这位低级军官错误地授权了空袭，导致平民的悲惨伤亡。
synonyms: misapprove, disallow,  forbid, prohibit,  veto
antonyms: authorize, approve, permit, sanction
词性: 动词
