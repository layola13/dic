email-bombs:/ˈiːmeɪl bɒmz/ (US) /ˈiːmeɪl bɒmz/ (UK)| n.|邮件炸弹|The act of sending a massive amount of emails to an email address, often with the intent to overload the server or inbox and disrupt service.|The activist group launched an email-bomb against the company to protest its environmental policies.  活动家团体向该公司发送邮件炸弹，抗议其环境政策。|Synonyms: mail bombing, email flooding|Antonyms: None|Noun


email-bombs:/ˈiːmeɪl bɒmz/ (US) /ˈiːmeɪl bɒmz/ (UK)| v.|用邮件炸弹攻击|To overwhelm an email address with a large number of messages.|They email-bombed the server until it crashed.  他们用邮件炸弹攻击服务器直到它崩溃。|Synonyms: mail bomb, email flood|Antonyms: None|Verb
