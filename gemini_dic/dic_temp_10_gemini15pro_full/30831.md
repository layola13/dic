cha-ching:/ˌtʃɑːˈtʃɪŋ/ | interjection, onomatopoeia | 赚钱的声音，收银机的声音 |  Imitates the sound of a cash register or coins, indicating a sale or the making of money. | Cha-ching!  Another sale!  / 叮！又一笔交易！ | 近义词: ker-ching, jingle | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider sounds of loss like a sigh or groan) | 词性:感叹词,象声词
