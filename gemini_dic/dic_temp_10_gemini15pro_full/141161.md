oophytic: /ˌoʊ.əˈfɪt.ɪk/
adjective
附生的，卵生的
Growing on or among eggs, especially insect eggs.  Relating to a plant that grows on another plant but is not parasitic, like an orchid.
Epiphytic orchids thrive in the humid rainforest canopy. 附生兰花在潮湿的雨林冠层中茁壮成长。
The oophytic fungus developed on the surface of the insect eggs.  这种卵生的真菌在昆虫卵的表面生长。
近义词: epiphytic
反义词: terrestrial, parasitic
词性: adjective

词源:  From Greek *oon* (egg) + *phyton* (plant), relating to the growth habit of certain fungi. The term is also sometimes used in botany for epiphytes.
