fellfare:/ˈfelˌfeər/| n.|野鹟|A thrush (Turdus pilaris) that breeds in northern Eurasia and winters south to North Africa and the Middle East, often in large flocks.|We saw a large flock of fieldfares in the field. 我们在田野里看到一大群野鹟。|近义词：thrush, redwing|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific bird species)|noun
