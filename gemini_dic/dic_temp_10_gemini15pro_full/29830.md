caulkings:/ˈkɔːkɪŋz/
| n. |填缝；填隙材料；填缝剂|A sealant used to fill gaps and prevent leaks, typically made of silicone, acrylic, or other flexible materials. |例句：The caulkings around the window are cracked and need to be replaced. 窗户周围的填缝料裂开了，需要更换。

例句：Apply fresh caulkings to prevent drafts.  涂上新的填缝剂以防止漏风。|近义词：sealant, filler, putty|反义词: N/A |词性:noun


caulking:/ˈkɔːkɪŋ/
| n. |填缝；填隙；填隙材料|The act of sealing a gap or seam with a flexible material. |例句：The caulking around the bathtub needs to be redone.  浴缸周围的填缝需要重做。

例句：Proper caulking can prevent water damage. 正确的填缝可以防止水损坏。|近义词：sealing, filling|反义词: N/A |词性:noun


词源: From "caulk," from Middle English "calken," from Old North French "cauquer," meaning "to press, tread down," of uncertain origin.
