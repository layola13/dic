amissibility:/əmɪsəˈbɪləti/
n.
amissibility (rare) 可接纳性；可允许性；可容许性
The amissibility of evidence in court.  法庭上证据的可采性。
The amissibility of certain types of evidence is often challenged in legal proceedings.  某些类型证据的可采性在法律诉讼中经常受到质疑。
近义词: admissibility, acceptability, allowability
反义词: inadmissibility, unacceptability
词性: noun

词源:  From admissible + -ity.  由 admissible（可接纳的）+ -ity（名词后缀）构成。
