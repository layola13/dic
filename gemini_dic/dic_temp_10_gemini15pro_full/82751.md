guiled:/ɡaɪld/
|v. (past tense)|欺骗；诱骗|to persuade someone to do something wrong or foolish, usually by deceiving them. |例句：She was guiled into revealing confidential information. 她被诱骗泄露了机密信息。

例句：He guiled the old lady out of her savings. 他骗走了老太太的积蓄。

|近义词：deceived, tricked, cheated, misled, fooled|反义词：informed, enlightened, undeceiving|词性：动词
