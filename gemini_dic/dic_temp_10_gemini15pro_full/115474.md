lanugo:/ləˈnuːɡoʊ/
noun
胎毛，毳毛
fine, soft hair, especially that which covers the body of a newborn baby or fetus.
The premature baby was covered in lanugo.  这个早产儿身上覆盖着胎毛。
synonyms: down, fuzz, hair
antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider "baldness," "smoothness" in specific contexts)
