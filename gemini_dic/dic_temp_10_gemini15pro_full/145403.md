overcautiousnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈkɔːʃəsnɪsɪz/
|n.|过度谨慎，过分小心|The state of being excessively cautious.|His overcautiousness prevented him from taking the necessary risks to succeed. 他过分的谨慎使他无法承担必要的风险来获得成功。|
|近义词: timidity, cautiousness, wariness|
|反义词: recklessness, boldness, daring|
|词性: noun|

overcautious:/ˌoʊvərˈkɔːʃəs/
|adj.|过度谨慎的，过分小心的|Excessively cautious; too careful.|She was overcautious about crossing the street, looking both ways several times.  她在过马路时过分小心，反复看了好几次两边。|
|近义词: timid, wary, apprehensive|
|反义词: reckless, bold, daring|
|词性: adjective|

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "cautious" (adjective from Latin "cautus," meaning "wary" or "careful") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).
