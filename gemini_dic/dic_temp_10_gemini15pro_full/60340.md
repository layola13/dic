egomaniac:/ˌiːɡoʊˈmeɪniæk/ | n. | 自我中心者；极端利己主义者 | A person who is obsessively egotistical or self-centered. |  He's such an egomaniac; he only cares about himself. 他真是个极端利己主义者，他只关心自己。 |  egotist, narcissist | altruist, humanitarian | noun


egomaniacs:/ˌiːɡoʊˈmeɪniəks/ | n. | 自我中心者；极端利己主义者（复数）| Plural form of "egomaniac," referring to multiple people who are obsessively egotistical or self-centered. | Those egomaniacs are always competing for attention. 那些极端利己主义者总是争着抢着要引起别人的注意。 | egotists, narcissists | altruists, humanitarians | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek ego (I) + maniac (mad, insane).  The term implies an excessive preoccupation with oneself and one's own importance.
