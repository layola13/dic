meniscuses: /məˈnɪskəˌsiːz/ (US), /məˈnɪskəˌsiːz/ (UK)
n.
弯月面，半月板
The curved upper surface of a liquid in a tube. Also, a crescent-shaped fibrous cartilage structure in the knee joint.
The meniscuses in his knees were damaged from years of running.  他的膝盖半月板由于多年的跑步而受损。
Synonyms: crescent, menisci
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a physical shape)
noun
