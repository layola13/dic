bantingism:/ˈbæntɪŋɪzəm/
n.
班廷减肥法; 低碳水化合物饮食
A weight-loss diet, popularized by William Banting, that involves drastically reducing the intake of carbohydrates, especially sugars and starches, and increasing the intake of fats.
William Banting popularized a weight-loss method, which is now called Bantingism.  威廉·班廷推广了一种减肥方法，现在被称为班廷减肥法。
He followed Bantingism strictly and lost a significant amount of weight. 他严格遵循班廷减肥法，体重明显减轻。
diet, low-carb diet, ketogenic diet
high-carb diet
