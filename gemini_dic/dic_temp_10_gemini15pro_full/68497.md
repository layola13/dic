famously:/ˈfeɪməsli/| adv.|著名地，出名地| in a way that is well known; publicly.|例句：The restaurant is famously expensive. 这家餐厅以昂贵闻名。|近义词：notably, remarkably, prominently|反义词: obscurely, anonymously|词性:副词
词源: From famous + -ly. Ultimately from Latin famosus.
