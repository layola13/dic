bantine:/ˈbæntaɪn/
adjective
斑驳的，杂色的，有斑点的
Marked or stained with different colors; mottled.
The old book had bantine pages, stained with age and water damage. 这本旧书的书页斑驳，因年代久远和水渍而留下污迹。
近义词: mottled, variegated, piebald, dappled
反义词: uniform, monochrome, solid


词性
adjective


词源
From French *bantin*, from Italian *bantino*, diminutive of *banto*, "striped cloth," of uncertain origin.
