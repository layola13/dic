passives:/ˈpæsɪvz/| n.|被动语态; 被动式; 被动语态的动词形式|The sentence "The ball was kicked by the boy" uses the passive voice. |句子“球被男孩踢了”使用被动语态。| synonyms: inactives,  | antonyms: actives | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *passifes, from Old French passif, from Latin passivus (“capable of suffering or feeling; submissive”), from passus, past participle of patior (“suffer, endure, undergo”).
