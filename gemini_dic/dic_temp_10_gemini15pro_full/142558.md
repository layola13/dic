ostentatiously:/ˌɒstɛnˈteɪʃəsli/ /ˌɑːstɛnˈteɪʃəsli/
adverb
炫耀地；招摇地
Characterized by vulgar or pretentious display; designed to impress or attract notice.
She ostentatiously displayed her expensive jewelry. 她炫耀地展示她昂贵的珠宝。
ostentatiously flipped open his gold-plated phone. 他炫耀地打开他的镀金手机。

近义词:showily, flamboyantly, pretentiously
反义词:modestly, humbly, discreetly
词性:adverb
