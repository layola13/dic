lumination:/ˌluːmɪˈneɪʃən/ |n.| 照明，照亮；启发 |The illumination of the streets made them safer at night. 街道的照明使它们在夜间更安全。|例句：The festival of lights is a beautiful illumination. 灯光节是一场美丽的照明展示。 The teacher's explanation provided illumination on the difficult subject. 老师的解释阐明了这个难题。|近义词：lighting, brightness, enlightenment|反义词：darkness, obscurity|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *illuminatio*, from Latin illuminare "to light up, enlighten," from in- "in" + luminare "to light," from lumen "light."
