cartouche:/kɑːrˈtuːʃ/| n. |涡卷形装饰; (古埃及象形文字的)椭圆框;  弹药筒; |An oval or oblong figure, as in Egyptian hieroglyphics, enclosing characters that represent the name of a sovereign or deity.  A decorative tablet or panel in the shape of a scroll with rolled-up ends. A cartridge case.|The pharaoh's name was written inside a cartouche. 法老的名字写在椭圆框里。|Synonyms:  ornament, decoration, emblem, insignia, cartridge |Antonyms: plainness, simplicity|Noun

cartouches:/kɑːrˈtuːʃɪz/| n. |涡卷形装饰(复数); (古埃及象形文字的)椭圆框(复数);  弹药筒(复数); |Plural form of cartouche.  |The cartouches on the tomb were intricately carved. 墓上的椭圆框雕刻精美。|Synonyms:  ornaments, decorations, emblems, insignias, cartridges |Antonyms: plainness, simplicity|Noun
