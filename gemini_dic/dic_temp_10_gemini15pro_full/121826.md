misrelating:/ˌmɪsrɪˈleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 错误地联系；错误地叙述；不恰当地关联 | Representing or narrating incorrectly; establishing an inappropriate connection between things. | She was misrelating the events of the night, confusing the order in which they happened. 她错误地叙述了当晚的事件，混淆了事件发生的顺序。

misrelating:/ˌmɪsrɪˈleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |  错误地联系的；错误地叙述的；不恰当地关联的  | Incorrectly connected or narrated; inappropriately related. |  His misrelating account of the incident caused further confusion. 他对事件的错误叙述导致了进一步的混乱。


misrelating: /ˌmɪsrɪˈleɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 错误的联系；错误的叙述；不恰当的关联 | The act of misrelating; an instance of misrelating. | The misrelating of these two historical events is a common misconception. 将这两个历史事件错误地联系起来是一个常见的误解。


词源:  mis- (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + relating (from relate, meaning "to connect or narrate")

近义词: misrepresenting, misinterpreting, distorting, falsifying

反义词: accurately representing, correctly interpreting, clarifying

词性:  verb, adjective, noun (depending on context)
