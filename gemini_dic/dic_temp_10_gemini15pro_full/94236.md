imperilled:/ɪmˈpɛrɪld/ | adj. | 处于危险中的，受到威胁的 |  Put in danger; endangered. Exposed to peril or harm.  | The rising floodwaters imperilled the lives of the villagers. 上涨的洪水危及村民的生命。| synonyms: endangered, jeopardized, threatened, menaced | antonyms: safe, secure, protected | adjective

imperil:/ɪmˈpɛrɪl/ | v. | 危及，使处于危险 | To put in peril or danger; endanger. | The toxic waste imperils the local ecosystem. 有毒废物危及当地生态系统。| synonyms: endanger, jeopardize, threaten, menace | antonyms: protect, safeguard, secure | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *emperi(l)ler, from en- + perill(i)er "to put in peril," from peril "danger," from Latin periculum "danger, risk, trial."
