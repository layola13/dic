nontransitively:/ˌnɒntrænˈzɪtɪvli/ /ˌnɑːntrænˈzɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 非及物地 |  In a way that does not take a direct object.  | The verb "sleep" is used nontransitively in the sentence "I slept well."  我睡得很好。 (动词“sleep”在这个句子中是非及物动词。) | intransitively | transitively | adverb
