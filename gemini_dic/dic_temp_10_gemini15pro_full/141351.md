operas:/ˈɒpərəz/ (UK) /ˈɑːpərəz/ (US)| n. |歌剧（复数）|A dramatic work in one or more acts, set to music for singers and instrumentalists.|

例句：
Many famous operas were composed in Italy.
很多著名的歌剧是在意大利创作的。

例句：
She enjoys attending operas and ballets.
她喜欢观看歌剧和芭蕾舞。
