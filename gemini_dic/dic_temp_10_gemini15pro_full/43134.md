coypu:/ˈkɔɪpuː/ (US), /ˈkɔɪpjuː/ (UK)| n.|海狸鼠，河狸鼠|A large, semiaquatic South American rodent with webbed hind feet, valued for its fur.|Coypus are native to South America but have been introduced to other parts of the world. 海狸鼠原产于南美洲，但已被引入世界其他地区。

Coypus can cause significant damage to crops and riverbanks. 海狸鼠会对农作物和河岸造成重大损害。|近义词：nutria, swamp beaver|反义词:None|词性:noun


coypu:/ˈkɔɪpuː/ (US), /ˈkɔɪpjuː/ (UK)| n.|海狸鼠皮，河狸鼠皮毛|The fur of the coypu.|Coypu fur is used to make coats and hats. 海狸鼠皮用于制作外套和帽子。

The demand for coypu fur has decreased in recent years. 近年来，对海狸鼠皮的需求有所下降。|近义词：nutria fur|反义词:None|词性:noun
