dehortatory:/dɪˈhɔːrtətɔːri/
adjective
劝阻的，反对的
Trying to dissuade someone from doing something, expressing strong disapproval.
The teacher's dehortatory speech discouraged students from cheating on the exam. 老师苦口婆心的劝诫使学生们不敢在考试中作弊。
Near Synonyms: dissuasive, admonitory, cautionary
Antonyms: hortatory, encouraging, exhortative
词性: adjective
