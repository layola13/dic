mortary:/ˈmɔːrtəri/| adj.|砂浆的，用砂浆砌成的；像砂浆的| Relating to, resembling, or consisting of mortar (a mixture of cement, sand, and water used in building).|The mortary mixture was thick and difficult to spread.  这种砂浆混合物很稠，难以铺开。|The old wall had a rough, mortary texture.  老墙的表面粗糙，像砂浆一样。|近义词：cementlike, cementitious|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *mortari*, from Old French *mortier* (mortar), from Latin *mortārium* (mortar, trough for pounding), derived from *mortārius* (pounding).
