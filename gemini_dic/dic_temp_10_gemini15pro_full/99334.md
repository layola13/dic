intercutting:/ˌɪntərˈkʌtɪŋ/
n.
交切，穿插，交错剪辑
The film uses intercutting to build suspense. 这部电影运用交错剪辑来制造悬念。
The intercutting of scenes from past and present created a powerful emotional impact.  过去和现在场景的交错剪辑创造了强大的情感冲击力。
montage, cross-cutting
n/a
noun

intercut:/ˌɪntərˈkʌt/
v.
交切，穿插，交错剪辑
The director intercut scenes of the battle with scenes of the family at home. 导演将战斗场景与家人在家里的场景交错剪辑。
They intercut shots of the city with interviews of its residents. 他们将城市的镜头与居民的采访交错剪辑。
interweave, splice
n/a
verb
