monomerous:/məˈnɒmərəs/| adj.|单体的，单节的，单数的|Consisting of a single part or unit, especially of a single mer.  Relating to or characterized by having only one part or member.  (Biology) Having flowers with parts in ones, not in multiples of threes, fours, or fives.|The monomerous flower had a single petal.  这种单体花只有一个花瓣。|single, solitary, individual|polymerous|adjective
