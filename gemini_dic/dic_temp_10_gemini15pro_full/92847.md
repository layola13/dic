icteritious:/ˌɪktəˈrɪʃəs/
adjective
黄疸的，患黄疸的
Affected by jaundice, having a yellowish discoloration of the skin, whites of the eyes, and mucous membranes, due to an excess of bilirubin in the blood.
The patient's skin appeared icteritious, indicating a possible liver problem. 病人的皮肤显得黄疸，表明可能存在肝脏问题。

The newborn displayed icteritious skin and sclera.  新生儿表现出黄疸的皮肤和巩膜。

jaundiced, yellowed
healthy, normal
