embalmings:/ɪmˈbɑːmɪŋz/
|n.|防腐处理；尸体保存|The process of preserving a corpse from decay.|The embalmings were carried out with the utmost care. 这些尸体都经过极其仔细的防腐处理。|近义词: preservation, mummification|反义词: decomposition, decay|词性:名词

Etymology:
From embalm + -ing (present participle suffix) + -s (plural marker).  Embalm comes from Middle French embaumer, from en- (in) + baume (balm).  Ultimately from Greek βάλσαμον (balsamon, "balsam").
