despoilments:/dɪˈspɔɪlmənts/
noun
掠夺物；破坏；赃物
The act of despoiling; plunder; pillage. The violent emptying of a place of its valuables.
The museum was filled with the despoilments of ancient tombs.  博物馆里摆满了从古墓中掠夺来的物品。
synonyms:plunder, pillage, loot, spoils, robbery
antonyms:restoration, restitution, reparation
