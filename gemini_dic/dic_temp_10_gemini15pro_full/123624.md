monthly: /ˈmʌnθli/ | adj. adv. n. | 每月的；每月一次地；月刊 | Happening, done, or produced every month. |  We pay a monthly rent of $1,000. 我们每月支付 1000 美元的租金。

|  The magazine is published monthly. 这本杂志每月出版一次。

|  She receives a monthly salary. 她每月领取薪水。

| 近义词: every month, each month | 反义词: annually, yearly | 词性: adjective, adverb, noun


monthly: /ˈmʌnθli/ | n. | 月刊 | A magazine published once a month. | She subscribes to several monthlies. 她订阅了几种月刊。

|  The monthly arrived in the mail today. 月刊今天通过邮件寄到了。
