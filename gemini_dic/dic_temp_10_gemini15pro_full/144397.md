outstrip:/ˌaʊtˈstrɪp/
verb
超过，胜过
To go faster or further than someone or something else; to be more successful.
例句：
The company's profits have outstripped all expectations.
这家公司的利润超出了所有预期。

例句：
He soon outstripped his rivals in the race.
他在比赛中很快就超过了他的对手。

近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, outpace, overtake
反义词：lag behind, fall behind
词性：v.
