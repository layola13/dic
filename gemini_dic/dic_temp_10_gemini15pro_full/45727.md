curvier:/ˈkɜːrviər/ /ˈkɜːrvier/| adj.|更弯曲的，曲线更美的|Having more curves; more shapely.  More pronounced curves, especially of a woman's body.|She looks even curvier in that dress. 她穿那件衣服看起来曲线更美了。|近义词：shaplier, more voluptuous, fuller-figured|反义词:straighter, thinner, less curvy|词性:adjective

Etymology:  From curve + -y + -er (comparative suffix). Ultimately from Latin *curvus*
