nonseizures:/nɒnˈsiːʒəz/
noun
无癫痫发作; 非癫痫发作
Absence of seizures.  A period of time during which a person with epilepsy does not experience any seizures.
The patient reported nonseizures for six months following the start of the new medication.  该患者报告在新药开始使用后的六个月内没有癫痫发作。
近义词: seizure freedom, remission (of seizures)
反义词: seizures, epileptic episodes
词性: noun
