cartographer:/kɑːrˈtɑːɡrəfər/
n.
制图师，制图员
A person who makes maps.
The cartographer spent years perfecting the map of the ancient world.  这位制图师花了数年时间完善古代世界的地图。
mapmaker, chart maker
