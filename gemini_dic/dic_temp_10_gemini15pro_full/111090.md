lit-up:/lɪt ˈʌp/| adj.|点亮的，照亮的，兴奋的|Brightly illuminated; also, excited or exhilarated. |The lit-up Christmas tree sparkled in the night.  点亮的圣诞树在夜里闪闪发光。|Synonyms: illuminated, bright, radiant, glowing, exhilarated, excited |Antonyms: dark, dim, extinguished, dull, depressed|adjective

lit-up:/lɪt ˈʌp/| v.|点亮，照亮|(past tense and past participle of light up) to illuminate something; also, to become or cause someone to become excited or exhilarated. |The fireworks lit up the sky. 烟花照亮了天空。|Synonyms: illuminate, brighten, irradiate, animate, excite |Antonyms: darken, dim, extinguish, dull, depress|verb
