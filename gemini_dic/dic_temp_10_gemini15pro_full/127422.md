neater:/ˈniːtər/ /ˈniːtər/| adj.|更整洁的，更整齐的|comparative form of neat: more tidy and in order.|Her handwriting is much neater than mine. 她的字迹比我的整洁得多。|近义词：tidier, cleaner, more orderly|反义词：messier, dirtier, more disorganized|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *netere, comparative of net (“neat”), from Old English nett (“shining, bright, clean, pure, elegant”), from Proto-Germanic *nattijaz (“net, clean”), from Proto-Indo-European *ned- (“to sew, to net, to twist, to tie”). Cognate with West Frisian netter (“neater”), Dutch netter (“neater”), German netter (“neater”).
