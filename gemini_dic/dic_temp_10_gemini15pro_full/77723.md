gemmiparity:/ˌdʒɛmɪˈpærɪti/ | n. | 芽殖，芽生 | Reproduction by budding, in which a new organism develops from an outgrowth or bud due to cell division at one particular site.  The new organism remains attached as it grows, separating from the parent organism only when it is mature, leaving behind scar tissue. | Hydra reproduce by gemmiparity. 水螅通过芽殖繁殖。| 近义词: budding | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin *gemma* ("bud") + *paritas* ("parity, equality"), from *par* ("equal").
