bathybius:/bəˈθɪbiəs/
n.
原生质团，海床原生质
A supposed primordial slime or protoplasm formerly believed to exist on the ocean floor and to be the source of all life.
The scientists dredged up samples of what they thought was bathybius, the primordial ooze.  科学家们挖出了他们认为是原生质团的样本，即原始软泥。
The theory of bathybius as the source of life was eventually disproven.  原生质团是生命起源的理论最终被证明是错误的。
近义词: protoplasm
反义词: None
词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *βαθύς (bathús, “deep”) + βίος (bíos, “life”). Coined by Thomas Henry Huxley in 1868.
