mesocuniform:/ˌmɛzoʊˈkjuːnɪfɔːrm/
adjective
中楔形的
Relating to or denoting a simplified form of cuneiform writing, intermediate between the complex early forms and the later, more abstract forms.  Derived from "meso-" (middle) + "cuneiform" (wedge-shaped writing).
The mesocuniform script was easier to learn than the older, more complex version.  中楔形文字比旧的、更复杂的版本更容易学习。
Near synonyms:  intermediate cuneiform
No real antonyms exist, as it describes a specific stage in the development of cuneiform.
word type: adjective
