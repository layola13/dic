muffler:/ˈmʌflər (US), ˈmʌflə (UK)| n.|消音器，围巾，头巾|A device used to deaden the sound of escaping gases, as on an automobile or other internal combustion engine. Also, a scarf or wrap worn around the neck for warmth.|The car needs a new muffler.|近义词：silencer (for exhaust systems), scarf, shawl (for clothing)|反义词:amplifier (opposite in function for exhaust systems)|noun

muffle:/ˈmʌfl/| vt.|使声音降低，裹住，抑制|To make a sound quieter or less distinct; to wrap or cover for warmth or protection.|The thick fog muffled the sound of the foghorn.|近义词：mute, deaden, soften, stifle, suppress|反义词:amplify, increase|verb
