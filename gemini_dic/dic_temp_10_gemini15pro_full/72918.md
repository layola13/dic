fondant:/ˈfɑːndənt/ (US) /ˈfɒndənt/ (UK)| n. | 翻糖，软糖 |A thick, creamy sugar paste used for icing cakes and pastries, or a sweet made from this.|  The wedding cake was covered in smooth white fondant. 婚礼蛋糕上覆盖着光滑的白色翻糖。| synonyms: icing, frosting | antonyms: N/A | noun

fondant:/ˈfɑːndənt/ (US) /ˈfɒndənt/ (UK)| adj. | 融化的，熔化的 |Melting; dissolving.| The fondant chocolate flowed over the ice cream. 融化的巧克力流在冰淇淋上。| synonyms: melting, dissolving | antonyms: solid, frozen | adjective
