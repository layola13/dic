hazarder:/ˈhæzərdər/
n.|冒险者; 敢于冒险的人|Someone who takes risks; a gambler.|He was a hazarder, always willing to bet on a long shot. 他是个冒险家，总是愿意赌一把冷门。|近义词: risk-taker, gambler, adventurer, speculator|反义词: cautious person, conservative|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From the verb "hazard" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Hazard" comes from Old French "hasard" (chance, game of dice), probably of Arabic origin.
