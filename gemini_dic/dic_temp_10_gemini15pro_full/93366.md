illegitimateness:/ˌɪlɪˌdʒɪtɪmətˈnɛs/
n.
非法性；私生；不正当
The state of being illegitimate, especially of a child born to parents not married to each other.  This can also refer to something not authorized by law or custom.
The illegitimateness of his claim to the throne was widely debated.  他的王位继承权的非法性受到了广泛的争议。
illegality, unlawfulness, invalidity
legitimacy, validity, lawfulness
