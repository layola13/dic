gung-ho:/ˌɡʌŋˈhoʊ/| adj.|热情高涨的；充满热情的；积极的|Extremely enthusiastic and eager to do something.|例句：The new recruits were gung-ho and ready for action. 新兵们热情高涨，随时准备行动。

例句：She's really gung-ho about the new project. 她对这个新项目真的充满热情。|近义词：enthusiastic, eager, zealous, keen, avid|反义词：apathetic, unenthusiastic, indifferent, reluctant|词性:形容词

词源：From Chinese 工合 (gōnghé), short for 工业合作社 (gōngyè hézuòshè, “industrial cooperative society”), the name of a Chinese industrial cooperative organization founded in 1938.  The term was adopted by US Marines during World War II.  It originally meant "work together" but evolved to mean "enthusiastic".
