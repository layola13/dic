emphysematous:/ˌɛmfɪˈsiːmətəs/ | adj. | 肺气肿的，气肿的 | Affected by, or resembling, emphysema; characterized by abnormal distension of tissue by air or gas. |  The patient's lungs showed emphysematous changes. 病人的肺部显示出肺气肿的病变。|  The X-ray revealed emphysematous bullae in the upper lobes of the lungs. X光片显示肺上叶有肺气肿性大疱。 | synonymous: inflated, swollen, distended | antonymous: deflated, compressed | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *emphysematosus, from Greek emphysema (inflation)  + -ous.  Emphysema from  en- (in) + physan (to blow)
