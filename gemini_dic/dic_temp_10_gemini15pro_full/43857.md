crescendo:/krəˈʃendoʊ/ (US), /krəˈʃendəʊ/ (UK)| n. |渐强，高潮|a gradual increase in loudness, or the peak of this.|The music built to a powerful crescendo.  音乐逐渐增强到一个强有力的高潮。|近义词：climax, culmination, peak|反义词: diminuendo, decrescendo| noun
