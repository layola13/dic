parseeism:/ˈpɑːrsiˌɪzəm/ | n. |祆教; 拜火教;|The religion of the Parsees, characterized by worship of Ahura Mazda and the belief in good and evil spirits. | Zoroastrianism is sometimes referred to as Parseeism because it was preserved by the Parsis in India.| 近义词:Zoroastrianism | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Parsis + -ism.  Parsis refers to the Zoroastrians who emigrated from Persia to India.  Ultimately derived from Pars (Persia).
