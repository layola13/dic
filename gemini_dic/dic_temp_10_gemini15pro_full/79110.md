gladdens:/ˈɡlædnz/
verb
使高兴，使快乐
To make someone feel happy or joyful.
The sunshine gladdens my heart. 阳光使我心情愉悦。
The news of their arrival gladdened us all. 他们抵达的消息让我们所有人都很高兴。
Synonyms: cheers, delights, pleases
Antonyms: saddens, depresses
词性:动词
