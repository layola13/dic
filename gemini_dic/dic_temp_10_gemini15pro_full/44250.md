crofted:/ˈkrɒftɪd/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːftɪd/ (US)| adj.|耕作小块土地的，拥有小块耕地的|Relating to or characteristic of a croft (a small piece of agricultural land, especially in Scotland).  Specifically, it refers to land tenure by customary law.  The tenant of a croft is known as a crofter.|例句：They live in a crofted cottage. 他们住在一个耕作小块土地的村舍里。

例句：The crofted land provides a modest income. 这块耕作地提供了一份微薄的收入。|近义词：smallholding, homesteading|反义词:industrial, urban|词性:adjective
词源: From the noun "croft" + "-ed". "Croft" comes from Old English "croft" meaning "small enclosed field".
