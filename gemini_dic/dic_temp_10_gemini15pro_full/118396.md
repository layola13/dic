meropidan:/ˌmɛrəˈpaɪdən/ | adj. | 蜂虎科的 | Belonging to the Meropidae family of birds, characterized by brightly colored plumage, slender bodies, and elongated central tail feathers.  These birds are known for their aerial agility and insect-catching abilities. |  The European bee-eater is a meropidan bird with vibrant plumage.  欧洲蜂虎是一种羽毛鲜艳的蜂虎科鸟类。 |  Synonyms:  bee-eater (often used interchangeably), jacamaralcyon (a less common synonym referring specifically to extinct members of the family) | Antonyms: N/A (There is no direct antonym for a taxonomic classification.) | taxonomy
