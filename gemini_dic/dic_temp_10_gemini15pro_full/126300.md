myogalid:/ˈmaɪoʊɡælɪd/ | n. | 肌联蛋白 | A protein that forms filaments that hold together the bundles of myofibrils that make up striated muscle fibers. | Myogalid plays a crucial role in muscle contraction. 肌联蛋白在肌肉收缩中起着至关重要的作用。| synonym: myomesin | antonym: N/A | noun
