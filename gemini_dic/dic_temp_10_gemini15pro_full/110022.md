librettos:/lɪˈbrɛtoʊz/
n.
歌剧，音乐剧等的脚本；歌词；剧本
the text of an opera or other long vocal work.
The librettos of many famous operas were written by Lorenzo Da Ponte. 许多著名歌剧的脚本都是由洛伦佐·达·庞特创作的。
synonyms: script, text, book, words, lyrics
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object.  One could consider "improvisation" an antonymous concept.)
noun
