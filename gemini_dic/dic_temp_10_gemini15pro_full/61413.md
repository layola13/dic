embody:/ɪmˈbɑːdi/ /ɛmˈbɒdi/| v. |包括，包含|to contain or include something as a constituent part| The new constitution embodies the ideals of democracy and freedom. 新宪法包含了民主和自由的理念。| synonyms: incorporate, comprise, encompass| antonyms: exclude, omit| verb
