diureticalnesses:/ˌdaɪjʊˈrɛtɪkəlnəsɪz/
| n. |利尿性；利尿作用|The state or quality of being diuretic; the capacity to increase the volume of urine produced.|Due to the diureticalnesses of the medication, she needed to drink more water. 由于药物的利尿性，她需要喝更多的水。|近义词：diuretic properties, diuretic effects|反义词：antidiuretic properties|词性:名词

Etymology:
From diuretical + -ness.  Diuretical from  diuret(ic) + -al. Diuretic from French *diurétique, from Greek *diouretikos, from *diourein 'to urinate,' from dia- 'through' + ouron 'urine'.
