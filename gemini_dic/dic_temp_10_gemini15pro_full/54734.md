disparage:/dɪˈspærɪdʒ/| vt.|贬低，轻视，诋毁|To belittle or speak slightingly of; to undervalue. |例句：He disparaged his opponent's record. 他贬低了对手的记录。

例句：She disparaged her own achievements out of modesty. 她出于谦虚贬低了自己的成就。

|近义词：belittle, denigrate, depreciate, detract from, minimize, underestimate, underrate|反义词：praise, commend, extol, laud, admire, respect, appreciate|词性:动词
