go one better:/ɡəʊ wʌn ˈbɛtər/
|v.| 更胜一筹；做得更好|to do something even better than someone else has already done| She baked a cake, and he went one better by making a three-tiered wedding cake. 她烤了一个蛋糕，而他更胜一筹，做了一个三层结婚蛋糕。|outdo, surpass, exceed|  fall short, lag behind|verb

go one better:/ɡəʊ wʌn ˈbɛtər/
|idiom| 更胜一筹；做得更好|to surpass someone's achievement or action by doing something more impressive|He bought his girlfriend a diamond ring, but his brother went one better and bought her a car.  他给女朋友买了一枚钻戒，但他的兄弟更胜一筹，给她买了一辆车。|outdo, surpass, exceed| be outdone, be surpassed|idiom

词源：该短语起源于19世纪晚期的纸牌游戏，特别是桥牌。在游戏中，“go one better”指的是出一张比对手更高的牌。后来，这个短语的含义扩展到更普遍的竞争和超越的语境。
