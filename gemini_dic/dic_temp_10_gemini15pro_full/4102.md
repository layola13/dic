aimful:/ˈeɪmfʊl/
adjective
有目标的，有目的的
Having a specific goal or purpose; directed or intended.
She made an aimful shot at the target. 她瞄准目标，精准地射了一箭。
近义词:purposeful, determined, focused, intentional
反义词:aimless, purposeless, random, undirected
词性:adjective

Etymology: From aim + -ful. Aim from Old French "esmer" meaning "to estimate" or "to aim."  -ful from Old English "-ful" meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
