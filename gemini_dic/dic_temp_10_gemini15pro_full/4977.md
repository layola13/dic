alkalis: /ˈælkəlaɪz/ | n. | 碱; 碱金属 | Substances that have a pH greater than 7 and can neutralize acids. They are often slippery to the touch and can react with acids to form salts and water. |  Sodium hydroxide and potassium hydroxide are common alkalis. 氢氧化钠和氢氧化钾是常见的碱。| synonyms: bases | antonyms: acids | noun
