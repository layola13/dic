over-chilled:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɪld/
adj.
过度冷却的
Cooled to an excessive degree.
The over-chilled wine lost some of its flavor. 这瓶过度冰镇的葡萄酒失去了一些风味。
近义词:over-refrigerated, too cold
反义词:warm, overheated
词性:形容词
