lupuses:/ˈluːpəsiːz/
n.
狼疮（复数）
plural of lupus;  Systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE), is an autoimmune disease in which the body's immune system mistakenly attacks healthy tissue in many parts of the body.
The doctor diagnosed her with lupus. 医生诊断她患有狼疮。
Lupuses can affect multiple organ systems. 狼疮会影响多个器官系统。

近义词: (No direct synonyms for the plural form.  Synonyms for lupus (singular) would include autoimmune diseases, connective tissue disorders)
反义词: (No antonyms)
词性: noun (plural)
