outscheming:/ˌaʊtˈskiːmɪŋ/
verb
用计谋胜过, 智取
defeating someone by being more clever or cunning in devising schemes or plans.
He outsmarted his opponents by outscheming them in the negotiations. 他在谈判中智胜了对手，用计谋胜过了他们。
synonyms: outmaneuvering, outwitting, outsmarting
antonyms: being outsmarted, being outmaneuvered
v.
