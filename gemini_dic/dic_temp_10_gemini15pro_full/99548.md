intergrappling:/ˌɪntərˈɡræplɪŋ/ | n. |  互相扭打，互相格斗 | The act of two or more people grappling with each other. |  The intergrappling between the two wrestlers was intense. 两个摔跤运动员之间的扭打非常激烈。 | 近义词：grappling, wrestling, struggling | 反义词: yielding, surrendering | 词性: 名词

词源： "inter-" (prefix meaning "between, among") + "grappling" (from "grapple," meaning "to seize and hold firmly")
