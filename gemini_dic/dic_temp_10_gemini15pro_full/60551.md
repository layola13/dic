elapse:/ɪˈlæps/| v. |消逝，流逝|To pass or go by (said of time).|Three months elapsed before I heard from her again. 三个月过去了，我才再次收到她的来信。|近义词：pass, go by, slip away|反义词:stand still|词性:动词

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Time elapses quickly when you're having fun.  时间过得很快，当你玩得开心的时候。
A considerable amount of time elapses between each stage of the process.  这个过程的每个阶段之间都会流逝相当长的时间。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Time is elapsing, and we need to make a decision soon.  时间在流逝，我们需要尽快做出决定。
As we speak, precious time is elapsing.  就在我们说话的时候，宝贵的时间正在流逝。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Five years have elapsed since we last met.  自从我们上次见面以来，已经过去了五年。
Too much time has elapsed for us to change the plan now.  现在改变计划已经太晚了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Time has been elapsing quickly while we've been working on this project. 我们一直在做这个项目，时间过得真快。
Months have been elapsing without any significant progress.  几个月过去了，没有任何重大进展。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Several weeks elapsed before he received a reply.  几个星期过去了，他才收到回复。
Only a few minutes elapsed before the alarm went off.  几分钟后，警报就响了。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
While they were arguing, valuable time was elapsing.  他们在争论的时候，宝贵的时间正在流逝。
As he was waiting, time was elapsing slowly and painfully.  他等待的时候，时间过得缓慢而痛苦。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
A year had elapsed before they met again. 一年过去了，他们才再次见面。
Many years had elapsed since the incident.  自那次事件以来，已经过去了许多年。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Years had been elapsing without any contact between them.  多年来，他们一直没有联系。
Days had been elapsing with no news from the search party.  几天过去了，搜救队没有任何消息。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
More time will elapse before we see the results.  我们需要更多时间才能看到结果。
A significant amount of time will elapse before the project is completed.  该项目完成还需要相当长的时间。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
This time next week, a whole month will have been elapsing since the incident.  下周这个时候，距离事件发生就整整一个月了。 (用将来完成时更合适)
By the time we finish, several hours will have elapsed.  到我们完成的时候，几个小时就过去了。(用将来完成时更合适)


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By the end of the month, two years will have elapsed since she started working here.  到月底，她在这里工作就满两年了。
By next year, a decade will have elapsed since the company was founded.  到明年，公司成立就十年了。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By the time the project is finished, we will have been working on it for over a year. (更常见的用法，强调持续进行的动作) 到项目完成时，我们将已经为它工作了一年多。
By next summer, she will have been living in London for five years. 到明年夏天，她将在伦敦生活五年了。
