misheard: /ˌmɪsˈhɜːrd/ (US) /ˌmɪsˈhɜːd/ (UK)| v. | 误听; 听错 | To hear incorrectly; understand wrongly what someone has said. |  I misheard what he said and thought he was leaving tomorrow. 我听错了他的话，以为他明天就要离开了。

I misheard the announcement and went to the wrong gate. 我听错了广播，去了错误的登机口。

近义词: misinterpret, misconstrue, misunderstand | 反义词: hear correctly, understand correctly | 词性: 动词
