mystique:/mɪˈstiːk/| n.|神秘感，神秘气氛；难以理解的魅力|An aura of mystique surrounds the actress.  这位女演员周身环绕着一种神秘的光环。|例句：The mystique of the ancient ruins drew tourists from all over the world. 古老遗址的神秘感吸引了来自世界各地的游客。

例句：She cultivated an air of mystique, rarely giving interviews or appearing in public. 她营造了一种神秘的氛围，很少接受采访或公开露面。|近义词：aura, charisma, allure, magic, enigma|反义词：clarity, openness, transparency|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mystique*, from Late Latin *mysticus* (“mystical”), from Ancient Greek *μυστικός (mustikós)*, from *μύστης (mústēs)* (“initiate”).
