cassation:/kəˈseɪʃən/ | n. | 撤销，废除；（尤指法国的）最高法院 |  The act of annulling or making void; specifically, the court of highest appeal in France. | The lawyer filed for a cassation of the judgment.  律师申请撤销判决。| synonyms: annulment, abrogation, revocation, repeal | antonyms: ratification, confirmation, approval | noun

cassation:/kəˈseɪʃən/ | n. | （乐曲的）终止，休止 | A ceasing; specifically, a musical cadence. | The symphony ended with a quiet cassation. 交响乐以一个安静的休止结束。| synonyms: conclusion, termination, ending | antonyms: beginning, commencement, start | noun
