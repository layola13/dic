come-hither:/kʌmˈhɪðər/
adjective
挑逗性的；勾引人的；引诱的
Suggestive of sexual invitation; alluring.
She gave him a come-hither look. 她向他抛了个媚眼。
近义词: seductive, alluring, inviting, tempting
反义词: uninviting, repulsive, off-putting
词性: adjective

词源:  From the phrase "come hither," meaning "come here," used as an imperative to attract someone, often with romantic or sexual connotations.  The usage as an adjective developed from the implied meaning of the phrase.
