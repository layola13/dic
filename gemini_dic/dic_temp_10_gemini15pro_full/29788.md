/kɔːz/ | n. | 原因，理由; 事业，目标 | a reason for something; a principle, aim, or movement to which one is committed | The police are still trying to establish the cause of the fire. 警方仍在试图确定火灾原因。| reason, motive, grounds | effect, result | noun
