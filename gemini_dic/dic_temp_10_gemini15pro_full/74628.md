free: /friː/
adjective|自由的，免费的|Not subject to constraint or control; not captive or enslaved. Costing nothing.|The bird was set free. 这只鸟被放生了。| 近义词:liberated, independent, complimentary, gratuitous|反义词: captive, restricted, costly, expensive
adjective|空闲的，未被占用的|Not occupied or busy.|Are you free this evening? 你今晚有空吗？|近义词:available, unoccupied, spare|反义词:busy, occupied, engaged
verb|释放，使自由|To release from captivity or constraint.|They freed the hostages. 他们释放了人质。|近义词:release, liberate, emancipate|反义词:confine, imprison, restrict
adverb|免费地|Without cost.|Children under five travel free. 五岁以下儿童免费旅行。|近义词:gratis, gratuitously|反义词:expensively
