inchoate:/ɪnˈkoʊeɪt/ /ɪnˈkəʊeɪt/| adj.|初步的，未完成的，不成熟的|Just beginning and not fully formed or developed; rudimentary.|The country's inchoate democracy is still fragile.  这个国家初生的民主制度仍然很脆弱。|Synonyms: rudimentary, nascent, incipient, embryonic, undeveloped, immature|Antonyms: developed, mature, complete, finished|Adjective


inchoate:/ɪnˈkoʊeɪt/ /ɪnˈkəʊeɪt/| n.|开始，初期|The beginning of something, typically something unpleasant.|The inchoate stages of a revolution. 一场革命的初期阶段。|Synonyms: beginning, start, outset, commencement|Antonyms: end, finish, completion, conclusion|Noun (rarely used)
