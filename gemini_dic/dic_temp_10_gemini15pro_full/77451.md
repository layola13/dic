gawper:/ˈɡɔːpər/ (British English)| n.| 傻傻地盯着看的人；呆呆地看的人 | Someone who stares rudely or stupidly. |  The tourists were gawpers, staring at the locals with open mouths. 那些游客像个呆子，张大嘴巴盯着当地人看。| synonyms: onlooker, spectator, rubbernecker, ogler | antonyms: none | noun

gawper:/ˈɡɔːpər/ (British English)| n.| 观看者 | spectator, viewer | He's a regular gawper at football matches. 他是足球比赛的常客。 | synonyms: viewer, watcher, spectator  | antonyms: participant | noun
