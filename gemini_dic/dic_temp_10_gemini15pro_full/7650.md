annum:/ˈænəm/| n.|年; 每年|A period of one year.  It is often used in financial contexts.|例句：The company's profits increased by 10% per annum. 该公司的利润每年增长10%。

例句：My salary is £30,000 per annum. 我的年薪是3万英镑。|近义词：year|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
