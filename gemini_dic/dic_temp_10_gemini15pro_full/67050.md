explicable:/ɪkˈsplɪkəbl/ /ɛkˈsplɪkəbl/
adjective
可解释的；可说明的
capable of being explained
Her behavior is perfectly explicable. 她的行为完全可以解释。

The mystery is now explicable. 这个谜团现在可以解释了。

synonym: understandable, explainable, comprehensible, justifiable
antonym: inexplicable, incomprehensible, unaccountable, mysterious
词性: adjective
