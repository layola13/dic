de-integrated:/ˌdiːˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪd/
adj.
去整合的，分解的，反向整合的
Reversed or undone the integration of something, especially of a system or organization.
The company de-integrated its manufacturing process, choosing to outsource certain components.  该公司对其制造流程进行了去整合，选择外包某些组件。
The report recommended de-integrating the large, unwieldy conglomerate. 报告建议将这个庞大而笨拙的企业集团进行分解。
近义词:  disintegrated, separated, decoupled
反义词: integrated, combined, unified
词性:  形容词
