criminalize:/ˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪz/| vt.|使…成为犯罪；宣布…为非法|To make something illegal; to declare something a crime.|The government criminalized the possession of certain drugs. 政府将持有某些毒品定为犯罪。|近义词：outlaw, prohibit, ban|反义词:legalize, decriminalize|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From crime + -ize.  "Crime" comes from the Latin "crimen" meaning "accusation, charge, crime."  The suffix "-ize"  forms verbs meaning “to make or cause to be,” “to subject to,” or “to become like.”
