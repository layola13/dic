overnormally:/ˌoʊvərˈnɔːrməli/| adv.| 过度正常地，异常正常地| Excessively or unusually normal; beyond what is considered typical or average normal behavior.  This word is often used ironically or humorously to describe someone who appears overly conformist or excessively conventional.| He behaved overnormally after the incident, almost as if nothing had happened. 事件发生后，他的行为异常正常，就好像什么都没发生过一样。|She was overnormally polite, which made me suspicious. 她异常有礼貌，这让我起了疑心。| 近义词: excessively normal, unusually conventional, unnaturally normal | 反义词: abnormal, eccentric, unusual | 词性: 副词
