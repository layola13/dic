nonpathologically:/ˌnɒnpæθəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ /ˌnɑːnpæθəˈlɑːdʒɪkli/| adv.|非病理性地，非病态地|Not in a pathological way; in a healthy or normal manner.  Relating to or characteristic of conditions that are not indicative of disease or pathology.|Her slight forgetfulness was considered nonpathologically age-related. 她轻微的健忘被认为是非病理性的与年龄相关的。|Synonym: normally, healthily, naturally|Antonym: pathologically, abnormally|adverb
