abstracter: /æbˈstræktər/
n.
摘要者，提取者，抄录员
A person who makes and prepares abstracts, especially of legal documents.
The abstracter prepared a summary of the land deed. 摘要员准备了土地契约的摘要。

abstracter: /æbˈstræktər/
n.
题录员，文摘员
A person who writes abstracts of academic papers or other written works.
The abstracter summarized the key findings of the scientific article. 题录员总结了这篇科学文章的主要发现。


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "abstract" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Abstract" comes from Late Latin "abstractus," past participle of "abstrahere" meaning "to draw away," from "abs-" (from, away from) + "trahere" (to draw).


SYNONYMS->
summarizer, epitomizer, condenser,  reporter (for legal documents)


ANTONYMS->
elaborator, expander


USAGE->
The abstracter diligently summarized the lengthy legal document.  摘要员认真地总结了冗长的法律文件。

The company hired an abstracter to create concise summaries of their research reports.  公司聘请了一位摘要员来为他们的研究报告撰写简洁的摘要。


PHRASE->
a certified abstracter = 一位认证的摘要员
an experienced abstracter = 一位经验丰富的摘要员
the abstracter's summary = 摘要员的总结
work as an abstracter = 做摘要员的工作
hire an abstracter = 聘请一位摘要员
