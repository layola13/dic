carmelite: /ˈkɑːməlaɪt/ | n. adj. | 加尔默罗会的修士/修女；加尔默罗会的 | A member of the Roman Catholic mendicant order founded on Mount Carmel in the 12th century. Also used as an adjective to describe things related to the Carmelite order. |例句：She is a Carmelite nun. 她是一位加尔默罗会的修女。|例句：The Carmelite monastery is located on a hilltop. 加尔默罗会的修道院坐落在山顶上。|近义词：monk, nun, friar (for male members) |反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider secular or lay person) |词性: noun, adjective
