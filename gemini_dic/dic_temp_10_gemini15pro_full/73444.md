foremostly:/ˈfɔːrmoʊstli/| adv.|首先，最主要地|primarily, chiefly, principally;  placed at the front or beginning.  It emphasizes something as the most important or primary consideration.|Foremostly, I want to thank my family for their support. 首先，我要感谢我的家人的支持。|近义词：primarily, chiefly, principally, mainly, above all|反义词: lastly, finally|词性:副词
词源: From "foremost" + "-ly." "Foremost" comes from Old English "foremest," the superlative of "fore," meaning "before," "in front."
