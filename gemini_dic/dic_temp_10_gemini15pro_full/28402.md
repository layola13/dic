carbonation:/ˌkɑːrbəˈneɪʃn/ | n. |碳酸化作用; 碳化作用| The process of dissolving carbon dioxide in a liquid, often under pressure, to produce a bubbly or fizzy drink.  It also refers to the reaction of carbon dioxide with calcium hydroxide to form calcium carbonate, a process involved in the hardening of cement and concrete, and the formation of cave formations.|

例句：
The carbonation in the soda makes it fizzy.
苏打水中的碳酸化作用使它充满气泡。

The carbonation of concrete increases its strength.
混凝土的碳化作用增加了它的强度。


近义词：effervescence (for drinks), calcification (for hardening)|反义词: decarbonation|词性: noun
