easier:/ˈiːziər/| adj.|更容易的，更简单的|Comparative form of easy.  Meaning "less difficult; posing less of a problem."|Learning Spanish is easier than learning Russian. 学习西班牙语比学习俄语更容易。|It's easier said than done. 说起来容易做起来难。|近义词：simpler, less difficult, more convenient|反义词：harder, more difficult, tougher|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *esier, *esyer, from Old English *ēaðīre, comparative form of ēaðe (“easy”). Cognate with Dutch eerder (“earlier, rather”), German eher (“rather, sooner”).
