antifascist: /ˌæntiˈfæʃɪst/ | n. adj. | 反法西斯主义者；反法西斯的 | A person or group who opposes fascism.  Also describes actions or ideologies opposed to fascism. |  The antifascist protesters marched through the streets. 反法西斯抗议者走上街头。| 近义词: anti-Nazi, anti-racist | 反义词: fascist, Nazi | 词性: 名词, 形容词


antifascists: /ˌæntiˈfæʃɪsts/ | n. | 反法西斯主义者（复数） | Plural form of "antifascist," referring to multiple people or groups who oppose fascism. | The antifascists organized a rally against hate speech. 反法西斯主义者组织了一场反对仇恨言论的集会。| 近义词: anti-Nazis, anti-racists | 反义词: fascists, Nazis | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + fascist. "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," derived from Greek. "Fascist" comes from Italian "fascista," ultimately from "fascio" meaning "bundle" or "group," referring to the symbol of the fasces (rods bundled around an axe) used in ancient Rome.
