governmentalizing:/ˌɡʌvərnˈmɛntəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使政府化，使官僚化
The process of making something more governmental or bureaucratic; increasing government control or influence.
The opposition party accused the ruling party of governmentalizing the education system. 反对党指责执政党使教育系统官僚化。
The new regulations are governmentalizing the healthcare industry. 新的规章制度正在使医疗保健行业官僚化。
Synonyms: bureaucratizing, nationalizing, centralizing
Antonyms: privatizing, deregulating, decentralizing
词性: 动词
