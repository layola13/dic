negrita:/neˈɡriːtə/ | n. |  黑体字，粗体字 | A bold typeface. |  The title of the article was printed in **negrita**.  这篇文章的标题是用粗体印刷的。 | 近义词: bold, boldface | 反义词: light, regular | 词性: 名词

词源: From Spanish *negrita*, feminine of *negrito* (“small black one”), diminutive of *negro* (“black”).
