middleman:/ˈmɪdəlmæn/| n. |中间人；经纪人|A person who buys goods from producers and sells them to retailers or consumers, or who acts as an intermediary between two people or groups.|The farmer sells his produce through a middleman. 这个农民通过中间人出售他的农产品。|近义词：intermediary, broker, agent, go-between|反义词:producer, consumer|noun

middleman:/ˈmɪdəlmæn/| n. |中间商|A person who buys goods from producers and sells them to retailers or consumers, or who acts as an intermediary between two parties.|Many businesses use middlemen to distribute their products. 许多企业使用中间商来分销他们的产品。|近义词：distributor, wholesaler, retailer|反义词:producer, manufacturer|noun
