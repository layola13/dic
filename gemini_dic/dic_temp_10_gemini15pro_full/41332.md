ETYMOLOGY->
From Corfu + -ote. Ultimately from Medieval Greek *Κορφοί (Korphoí), from Ancient Greek Κορυφοί (Koryphoí, “peaks”), plural of κορυφή (koryphḗ, “summit, peak”).
