aspic:/ˈæspɪk/| n.|肉冻，冻肉汤|A savory jelly made with meat stock, set in a mold and used to contain pieces of meat, fish, or vegetables.|例句：The aspic was shimmering on the buffet table.  自助餐桌上的肉冻闪闪发光。

例句：She carefully unmolded the aspic onto a serving platter. 她小心翼翼地把肉冻从模具中倒在餐盘上。|近义词：jelly, gelatin|反义词:|noun
