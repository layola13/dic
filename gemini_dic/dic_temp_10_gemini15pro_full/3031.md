aerobic:/ɛˈroʊbɪk/ /əˈrəʊbɪk/| adj.|有氧的，需氧的|Relating to, involving, or requiring free oxygen.|Regular aerobic exercise can improve cardiovascular health.  规律的有氧运动可以改善心血管健康。

Aerobic respiration is essential for most life forms. 有氧呼吸对大多数生命形式至关重要。|近义词:oxidative, oxygen-requiring|反义词:anaerobic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *aēr* (air) + *bios* (life).  源自希腊语 *aēr*（空气）+ *bios*（生命）。


USAGE->
Attributive Usage (作定语):

Aerobic exercises are good for your heart. 有氧运动对你的心脏有好处。
She prefers aerobic workouts to anaerobic ones. 她更喜欢有氧运动而不是无氧运动。

Predicative Usage (作表语):

This type of bacteria is aerobic.  这种细菌是需氧的。
The process of decomposition is largely aerobic. 分解的过程主要是需氧的。


PHRASE->
aerobic exercise = 有氧运动
aerobic respiration = 有氧呼吸
aerobic bacteria = 需氧菌
aerobic capacity = 有氧能力
aerobic training = 有氧训练
