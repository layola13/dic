bata:/ˈbɑːtə/ | n. | 巴塔鞋，帆布鞋 | A type of canvas shoe, often used as a generic term for any simple canvas shoe. Originates from the Czech company Bata Shoes. |  He's wearing a pair of bata. 他穿着一双巴塔鞋。|  My bata are comfortable for everyday wear. 我的巴塔鞋适合日常穿着。| synonyms: plimsolls, sneakers, tennis shoes | antonyms:  boots, dress shoes | noun
