nonsuggestion:/ˌnɒn səˈdʒɛstʃən/ | n. | 非暗示; 非建议 | The therapist's approach was one of nonsuggestion, allowing the patient to lead the session.  治疗师采用非暗示的方法，让病人主导治疗过程。 |  Her silence was a form of nonsuggestion. 她的沉默是一种非暗示的形式。 | 近义词: neutrality, non-intervention | 反义词: suggestion, prompting | 词性: noun

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + suggestion (from Latin suggerere "to furnish, supply, prompt")
