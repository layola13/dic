overinsistence:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈsɪstəns/| n.|过度坚持，过分强调|The overinsistence on following the rules can stifle creativity. 过分强调遵守规则会扼杀创造力。|例句：His overinsistence on the topic made everyone uncomfortable. 他对这个话题的过分强调让每个人都感到不舒服。

例句：Her overinsistence on organic food was bordering on obsession. 她对有机食品的过分强调近乎痴迷。|近义词：persistence, insistence, tenacity|反义词:acquiescence, compliance, submissiveness|词性:名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much") + "insistence" (from "insist," derived from Latin "insistere" meaning "to stand upon, persist").
