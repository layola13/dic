ephemeral:/ɪˈfɛmərəl/| adj.|短暂的，朝生暮死的|Lasting for a very short time.|例句：The ephemeral beauty of a flower.  花的短暂之美。

例句：Youth is ephemeral. 青春是短暂的。|近义词：transient, fleeting, short-lived, momentary|反义词：permanent, enduring, lasting, perpetual|词性:形容词
ephemeral:/ɪˈfɛmərəl/| n.|只生存一天的事物|Something that lasts for only a short time.|例句：Ephemerals bloom and die in a single day. 朝生暮死之物一天之内开花凋谢。

例句：These insects are ephemerals, living for only a few hours. 这些昆虫生命短暂，只存活几个小时。|近义词：Mayfly|反义词：Perennial|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ephemeralis*, from Greek *ephēmeros* "lasting only a day," from *epi* "on, upon" + *hēmera* "day."
