holding-on:/ˈhəʊldɪŋ ɒn/ (UK) /ˈhoʊldɪŋ ɑːn/ (US)| n.|坚持，抓紧，不放手|The act of gripping something tightly or refusing to let go.  It can also refer to persisting in a difficult situation.|He demonstrated remarkable holding-on during the storm, clinging to the mast of the boat.|近义词：grip, grasp, tenacity, persistence|反义词: letting go, release, surrender|noun

holding-on:/ˈhəʊldɪŋ ɒn/ (UK) /ˈhoʊldɪŋ ɑːn/ (US)| adj.|紧握的，持续的|Describing something that is being held tightly or a situation that persists.|His holding-on grip prevented him from falling.|近义词： firm, tenacious, persistent|反义词: loose, weak, fleeting|adjective
