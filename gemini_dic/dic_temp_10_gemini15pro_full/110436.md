likely:/ˈlaɪkli/| adj. adv.|可能的，很可能的；或许，大概|Something that is likely will probably happen or is probably true.  It can also be used as an adverb meaning "probably".|例句：It is likely that she will win the race. 她很可能会赢得比赛。|近义词：probable, possible, expected|反义词：unlikely, improbable|词性:形容词/副词
Etymology: From Middle English *likli*, from Old Norse *líkligr* (probable), from *líkr* (like, similar) + *-ligr* (suffix forming adjectives).
