moistest:/ˈmɔɪstɪst/| adj.|最潮湿的|Superlative form of moist: most moist.  Having the most water or moisture.|The cake was incredibly moistest, practically melting in my mouth.|近义词：dampest, wettest, soggiest|反义词：driest, aridest|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *moiste, from Old French moiste (modern French moite), from Vulgar Latin *muscidus (“moldy, musty”), from Latin muscus (“moss”).
