gargoyle: /ˈɡɑːrɡɔɪl/ | n. |滴水嘴，怪兽状滴水嘴，（指人）面貌丑陋的人|A carved stone figure, usually grotesque, projecting from the gutter of a building to throw rainwater clear of the wall. Also, a person with a grotesque appearance. | The cathedral is adorned with numerous gargoyles.  这座大教堂装饰着许多滴水嘴。|  He looked like a gargoyle perched on the rooftop. 他看起来像个蹲在屋顶上的怪兽。 | synonyms: grotesque, chimera, monster | antonyms: beauty, angel | noun
