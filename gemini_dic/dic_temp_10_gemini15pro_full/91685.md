hypercompetitive:/ˌhaɪpər kəmˈpɛtɪtɪv/
adjective
过度竞争的，极度竞争的
Excessively competitive; marked by cutthroat competition.
The tech industry is a hypercompetitive environment. 科技行业是一个竞争激烈的环境。
Silicon Valley fosters a hypercompetitive atmosphere. 硅谷营造了一种高度竞争的氛围。

近义词: cutthroat, fiercely competitive, ruthlessly competitive, ultracompetitive
反义词: cooperative, collaborative, noncompetitive

词性: adjective
