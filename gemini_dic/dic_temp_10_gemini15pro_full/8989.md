antiliberalness:/ˌæntiˈlɪbərəlnəs/
|n.| 反自由主义，非自由主义 |The state of being opposed to liberal principles or policies. |His antiliberalness manifested in his disdain for free speech and individual rights.  他的反自由主义体现在他对言论自由和个人权利的蔑视。|Synonyms: illiberalism, intolerance, authoritarianism |Antonyms: liberalism, tolerance, open-mindedness|noun
