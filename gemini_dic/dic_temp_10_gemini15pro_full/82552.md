guanine:/ˈɡwɑːniːn/
n.|鸟嘌呤|A purine base, C5H5N5O, one of the fundamental components of DNA and RNA, in which it forms base pairs with cytosine.|Guanine is one of the four main nucleobases found in the nucleic acids DNA and RNA.|Guanine pairs with cytosine in DNA and RNA. |近义词: nucleobase, purine |反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑其互补碱基cytosine)|词性: noun
