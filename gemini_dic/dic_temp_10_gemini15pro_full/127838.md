nemertid: /nɛˈmɜːrtɪd/ | n. | 纽形动物 | A type of ribbon worm, belonging to the phylum Nemertea, characterized by a long, slender, unsegmented body and a proboscis used for capturing prey. |  Nemerteans, also called ribbon worms or proboscis worms, are found in marine, freshwater, and terrestrial environments. | 近义词: ribbon worm, proboscis worm | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

nemertean: /nɛˈmɜːrtiən/ | adj. n. | 纽形动物的；纽形动物 | Relating to or belonging to the phylum Nemertea, a group of ribbon worms.  Also can be used as a noun to refer to a nemertean worm. | The nemertean stretched out its proboscis to capture a small crustacean. | 近义词: nemertine | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective, noun

词源: From New Latin *Nemertes (genus name), from Greek Νημερτής (Nēmertēs), the name of a sea nymph.
