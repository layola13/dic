bench-pressed:/ˈbɛntʃ prɛst/ | adj. |卧推的|Relating to or achieved by bench pressing.|  His bench-pressed weight is impressive. 他的卧推重量令人印象深刻。| synonyms: lifted, pressed | antonyms: lowered, dropped | adjective
