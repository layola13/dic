navbar:/ˈnævˌbɑːr/ (US), /ˈnavˌbɑː/ (UK)| n. |导航栏|A horizontal or vertical bar at the top or side of a website or application that contains links to the main sections of the site or app.|The website's navbar makes it easy to navigate between different sections.  这个网站的导航栏使在不同部分之间导航变得容易。|synonyms: navigation bar, menu bar, navigation menu|antonyms: N/A|noun

navbar:/ˈnævˌbɑːr/ (US), /ˈnavˌbɑː/ (UK)| n. |导航条|A horizontal or vertical bar, typically at the top of a computer screen, that displays menus and icons.|The navbar provides quick access to frequently used functions. 导航条提供对常用功能的快速访问。|synonyms: navigation bar, menu bar, navigation menu|antonyms: N/A|noun
