counter-question:/ˈkaʊntərˌkwestʃən/| n.|反问; 反诘|A question asked in response to another question, often to avoid answering the original question or to shift the focus of the conversation.| "When I asked him about his alibi, he responded with a counter-question about my motives." 当我问他不在场证明时，他反问我我的动机是什么。|Synonym: retort, comeback|Antonym: answer, reply|noun


counter-question:/ˈkaʊntərˌkwestʃən/| v.|反问; 反诘|To ask a question in response to another question, often to avoid answering the original question or to shift the focus of the conversation.|He counter-questioned me about my own experiences instead of answering my original question. 他反问我自己的经历，而不是回答我最初的问题。|Synonym: challenge, question back|Antonym: answer, respond|verb
