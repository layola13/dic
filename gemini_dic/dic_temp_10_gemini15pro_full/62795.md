engrossment:/ɪnˈɡroʊsmənt/ | n. |全神贯注，专注；正式文本；垄断 |The state of being completely absorbed or occupied; the act of engrossing or absorbing all one's attention or interest; a fair copy of a legal document. |His engrossment in the video game made him oblivious to his surroundings. 他对电子游戏的全神贯注使他对周围的环境浑然不觉。| 近义词: absorption, preoccupation, immersion, captivation | 反义词: distraction, inattention | 词性: noun

engrossment:/ɪnˈɡroʊsmənt/ | n. | engrossed 的名词形式 | The act of engrossing or the state of being engrossed.  | The lawyer prepared the final engrossment of the contract. 律师准备了合同的最终正式文本。 | 近义词:  transcription, final copy | 反义词: draft | 词性: noun


词源:
From Old French engrossir "make thick, enlarge," from en- "make, put in" + grossir "make big or large," from gros "big, large, thick."
