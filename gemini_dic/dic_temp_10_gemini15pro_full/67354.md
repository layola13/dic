extensibility:/ɪkˌsten.səˈbɪl.ə.ti/ | n. | 可扩展性，延展性 |The quality of being extensible; capable of being extended.  The ability of a system to be easily modified to accommodate new functionality or requirements. | This software's extensibility allows developers to add new features easily.  这个软件的可扩展性允许开发者轻松添加新功能。 | 近义词: flexibility, scalability, adaptability | 反义词: inflexibility, rigidity | 词性: noun

词源:  From extend + -ibility.  Ultimately from Latin extendere "to stretch out."
