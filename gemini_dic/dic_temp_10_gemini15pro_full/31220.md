channelling:/ˈtʃæn(ə)lɪŋ/ | n. |引导，传导；(尤指)通灵 |The act of directing something into a particular course or medium.  It can also refer to the claimed ability to receive messages from spirits or other non-physical entities.|She believes she is channelling the spirit of her deceased grandmother. 她相信她正在引导她已故祖母的灵魂。|Synonyms: directing, conveying, transmitting, communicating (with spirits) |Antonyms: blocking, obstructing| noun

Etymology:
