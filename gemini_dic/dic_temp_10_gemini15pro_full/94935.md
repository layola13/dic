in-operation:/ɪn ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃən/ | adj. | 运行中的，操作中的，生效的 | in operation describes something that is currently functioning or being used. It indicates that a system, machine, plan, or law is actively working or having an effect. | The new system is now in operation. 新系统现已投入运行。

The factory has been in operation for over 50 years. 这家工厂已经运营了50多年。| synonyms: functioning, working, active, effective, operational | antonyms: out of operation, inactive, defunct, non-functional | adjective
