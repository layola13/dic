overdiscouraged:/ˌoʊvər dɪsˈkɜːrɪdʒd/
adjective
过度沮丧的，极度灰心的
Excessively discouraged; deeply disheartened.  Feeling a loss of hope or confidence to an overwhelming degree.
He felt overdiscouraged after failing the exam for the third time. 他在第三次考试不及格后感到极度灰心。
The team was overdiscouraged by their recent losing streak.  球队最近的连败让他们非常沮丧。
Synonym: disheartened, demoralized, dejected, despondent
Antonym: encouraged, hopeful, optimistic
词性: 形容词
