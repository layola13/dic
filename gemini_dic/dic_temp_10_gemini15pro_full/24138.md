briariest:/ˈbraɪəriɪst/
adjective
最多荆棘的；最棘手的
covered with or full of briars; most thorny; most difficult or troublesome.
The briariest path led to the hidden waterfall.  最多荆棘的小路通向隐藏的瀑布。
The lawyer faced the briariest case of her career.  这位律师面临着她职业生涯中最棘手的案件。
近义词: thorniest, prickliest, most difficult, most challenging
反义词: easiest, smoothest, simplest
词性: adjective
词源: From briar + -iest (superlative suffix).  Briar comes from Old English brēr, related to Dutch braam and German Brombeere.
