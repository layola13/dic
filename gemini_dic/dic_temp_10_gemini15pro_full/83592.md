haematoidin:/ˌhiːmətˈɔɪdɪn/ | n. | 血红素，胆红素 | A crystalline, iron-free pigment found in old blood clots, identical with bilirubin. |  Example 1: The haematoidin in the bruise indicated it was several days old.  瘀伤中的血红素表明它已经好几天了。

 Example 2:  Researchers analyzed the haematoidin to determine the age of the hemorrhage. 研究人员分析了血红素以确定出血的年龄。| 近义词: bilirubin | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *haîma* (blood) + *-oeidēs* (like, resembling) + *-in* (a suffix forming names of chemical substances).
