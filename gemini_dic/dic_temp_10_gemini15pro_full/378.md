ability:/əˈbɪləti/ /əˈbɪlɪti/| n.|能力，才能|The ability to learn is essential.  学习的能力是至关重要的。|例句：She has the ability to speak French fluently. 她能够流利地说法语。|近义词：capacity, capability, skill, talent, aptitude|反义词：inability, incompetence, weakness|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *abilité*, from Latin *habilitās*, from *habilis* ("able, apt, fit"), from *habēre* ("to have, hold").  最终源自原始印欧语 *gʰabʰ-*.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
His ability allows him to solve complex problems. = 他的能力使他能够解决复杂的问题。
Her musical ability is truly impressive. = 她的音乐才能真的令人印象深刻。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:
He has shown great ability in mathematics throughout his academic career. = 在他的整个学术生涯中，他在数学方面展现了非凡的才能。
She has developed her ability to paint over the years. = 多年来，她一直在提升自己的绘画能力。

过去完成时:
He had already demonstrated his ability before he was promoted. = 在他晋升之前，他已经展示了他的能力。
She had honed her ability through years of practice before the competition. = 在比赛之前，她通过多年的练习磨练了自己的技能。

将来时:

Her ability will be invaluable to the team. = 她的能力对团队来说将是无价的。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

否定句:
