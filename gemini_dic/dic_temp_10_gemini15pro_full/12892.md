Simple Present:
Assuming it rains tomorrow, we'll stay indoors. 假设明天下雨，我们将待在室内。
He speaks fluent French, assuming he hasn't forgotten it. 假设他没有忘记，他讲一口流利的法语。


Present Continuous:
I'm assuming you're coming to the party. 我假设你会来参加派对。
We are assuming he's telling the truth. 我们假设他说的是实话。


Present Perfect:
I have been assuming that you would agree. 我一直假设你会同意。
They have been assuming she’ll be late. 他们一直假设她会迟到。


Present Perfect Continuous:
We've been assuming he'd change his mind. 我们一直假设他会改变主意。
They have been assuming the project will be finished on time. 他们一直假设项目会按时完成。


Simple Past:
We assumed he was coming, but he didn't show up. 我们以为他会来，但他没有出现。
They assumed she knew the answer. 他们以为她知道答案。


Past Continuous:
I was assuming you'd be here by now. 我以为你现在会在这里。
They were assuming the meeting would be cancelled. 他们以为会议会被取消。


Past Perfect:
We had assumed the problem was solved. 我们原以为问题已经解决了。
They had assumed she would accept the offer. 他们原以为她会接受这个提议。


Past Perfect Continuous:
I had been assuming he would come until he called and cancelled. 我一直以为他会来，直到他打电话取消。
They had been assuming the project would be successful.  他们一直以为这个项目会成功。


Simple Future:
I will assume you agree unless I hear otherwise. 除非我听到其他消息，否则我将假定你同意。
Assuming the weather is good, we'll have a picnic. 假设天气好，我们将野餐。


Future Continuous:
I will be assuming responsibility for the project next month. 我下个月将承担该项目的责任。（此处 assume 为动词“承担”的意思）
They will be assuming he's coming to the meeting. 他们会假设他会来参加会议。


Future Perfect:
By next week, we will have assumed control of the company. 到下周，我们将已经控制了公司。（此处 assume 为动词“控制”的意思）
By then, they will have assumed he’s changed his mind. 到那时，他们会假设他已经改变了主意。



Future Perfect Continuous:
By next year, they will have been assuming this for a decade. 到明年，他们就假设这件事十年了。
By the time she graduates, she will have been assuming this role for two years. 到她毕业的时候，她就已经担任这个角色两年了。


Subjunctive Mood:
If I were to assume that, it would be wrong. 如果我假设那样，那就错了。
I wish I could assume everything would be alright. 我希望我可以假设一切都会好起来。


Passive Voice:
It was assumed that he was guilty.  人们认为他有罪。
It is being assumed that the project will be a success.  人们认为这个项目会成功。


Question:
Assuming you win the lottery, what will you do? 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
Are you assuming I'm going to pay for this? 你是假设我会为此付钱吗？


Negation:
I'm not assuming anything. 我什么都没假设。
They didn't assume she knew the answer. 他们没有假设她知道答案。
