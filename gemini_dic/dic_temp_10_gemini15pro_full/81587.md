greaser: /ˈɡriːzər/ (US), /ˈɡriːsə/ (UK)| n. |油脂工；涂油脂的人；<俚>穿着皮衣，头发抹油的男子，(尤指20世纪50年代的)阿飞|A young man, typically from the 1950s, characterized by greased-back hair, leather jackets, and often rebellious behavior.  Often considered a derogatory term.|例句：He looked like a greaser with his slicked-back hair and leather jacket. 他头发抹得油光锃亮，穿着皮夹克，看起来像个阿飞。
