monopodial:/ˌmɒnəˈpəʊdiəl/ | adj. | 单轴的，单顶的 |  (of a plant) having a single main stem or axis that grows vertically and produces lateral branches or flowers. |  Example: Monopodial orchids grow upward from a single stem.  单轴兰花从一根茎向上生长。
Example: The monopodial growth habit is characteristic of many conifers.  单轴生长习性是许多针叶树的特征。 |  Synonyms: acrotonic, racemose | Antonyms: sympodial | Word type: adjective
