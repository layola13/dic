crayonists:/ˈkreɪənɪsts/
noun
蜡笔画家；蜡笔艺术家
A person who uses crayons, especially as a medium for artistic expression.
The exhibition showcased the vibrant work of young crayonists.  这个展览展示了年轻蜡笔画家的活力作品。
The crayonists' bold use of color was impressive. 蜡笔画家大胆的用色令人印象深刻。
Synonyms: crayon artist, crayon user
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider artists working in other mediums, like painters, sculptors)
