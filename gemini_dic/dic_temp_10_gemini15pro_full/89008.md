holloa:/həˈləʊ/ (UK), /həˈloʊ/ (US)| n. vi. vt. int.|喊叫，呼喊；喂|A loud shout or cry to attract attention. Also used as a greeting or to express surprise.|例句：He let out a holloa to his friends across the field. 他向田野另一边的朋友们大声喊叫。

例句： "Holloa!" he cried, "Is anyone there?"  “喂！”他喊道，“有人在吗？”

近义词：shout, yell, cry, call, hail|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:名词，动词，感叹词
