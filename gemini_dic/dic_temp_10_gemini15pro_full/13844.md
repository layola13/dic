aufklärung:/ˈaʊfˌklɛːrʊŋ/| n.|启蒙运动，启蒙，教化，说明，澄清|The Enlightenment, a European intellectual and philosophical movement of the 17th and 18th centuries emphasizing reason and individualism rather than tradition. It also refers to the process of clarifying or explaining something.|The German Aufklärung was a period of great intellectual ferment. 德国启蒙运动时期是一个思想非常活跃的时期。|近义词：enlightenment, elucidation, clarification|反义词：obscurantism, mystification|词性:noun

Etymology: From German *Aufklärung*, from *aufklären* (“to clear up, enlighten”), from *auf-* (up) + *klären* (to clarify), related to *klar* (clear). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *klaraz* (“clear”).
