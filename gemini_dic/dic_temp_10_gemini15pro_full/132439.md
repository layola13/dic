nondepletive:/ˌnɒndɪˈpliːtɪv/
adjective
非消耗性的,非递减的
Not reducing the amount or supply of something.  It refers to a process or action that does not use up or permanently diminish a resource.
例句：Nondepletive energy sources, such as solar and wind power, are becoming increasingly popular.  非消耗性能源，例如太阳能和风能，正变得越来越受欢迎。

例句：The study focused on nondepletive methods of timber harvesting. 这项研究的重点是非消耗性木材采伐方法。
近义词：renewable, sustainable, inexhaustible
反义词：depletive, consumptive, exhaustible
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + depletive.  "Depletive" comes from the Latin "deplere" meaning "to empty out."  The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning.
