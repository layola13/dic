mimesis:/mɪˈmiːsɪs/, /maɪˈmiːsɪs/ | n. | 拟态 | The close external resemblance of an animal or plant (or part of one) to another animal, plant, or inanimate object. | The insect's mimesis of a leaf was incredibly convincing. 这种昆虫对叶子的拟态非常逼真。 | synonyms: camouflage, mimicry | antonyms: difference, distinction | noun
