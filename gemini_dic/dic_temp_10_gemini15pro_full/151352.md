parkin:/ˈpɑːrkɪn/ | n. | 帕金蛋糕；生姜甜饼 | A soft, sticky gingerbread cake, usually made with oatmeal and treacle or golden syrup. | My grandmother's parkin is the best I've ever tasted. 我祖母做的帕金蛋糕是我吃过最好吃的。| synonyms: gingerbread, spice cake | antonyms: None | noun

parkin:/ˈpɑːrkɪn/ | n. | 帕金森病 | Parkinson's disease, a progressive nervous system disorder that affects movement. |  He was diagnosed with parkin a few years ago. 他几年前被诊断出患有帕金森病。 | synonyms: Parkinson's disease, PD | antonyms: None | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *parkyng, likely related to perk, meaning "brisk" or "lively," perhaps referring to the spices used in the cake which were thought to have invigorating properties.  The connection to Parkinson's disease is entirely coincidental.
