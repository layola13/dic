half-jokingly:/ˌhɑːf ˈdʒoʊkɪŋli/
adverb
半开玩笑地
In a way that is partly serious and partly not serious.
"He half-jokingly suggested they should elope."  他半开玩笑地建议他们私奔。
"She half-jokingly threatened to quit her job." 她半开玩笑地威胁要辞职。
Synonyms: playfully, teasingly, partly seriously
Antonyms: seriously, earnestly
词性: 副词
