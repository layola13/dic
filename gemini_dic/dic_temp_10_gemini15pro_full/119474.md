micromillimetre:/ˌmaɪkrəˈmɪlɪmiːtər/| n. |微毫米，微微米|A unit of length equal to one millionth of a millimetre (10^-9 metres), also called a nanometre.|The wavelength of visible light is measured in micromillimetres. 可见光的波长是用微毫米测量的。|Synonym: nanometre, nm|Antonym: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement, but one could consider larger units like millimetre, centimetre, metre, kilometre)|noun
