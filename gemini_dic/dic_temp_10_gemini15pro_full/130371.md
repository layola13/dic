non-universal:/ˌnɒn juːnɪˈvɜːsəl/ | adj. | 非普遍的，非通用的 | Not applicable to all cases, situations, or people; not general. | This theory is non-universal and only applies to specific contexts.  这个理论并非普遍适用，只适用于特定情况。 | Example: The principle of free speech is not non-universal, as some countries have restrictions on what can be said. 言论自由的原则并非普遍适用，因为有些国家对可以说什么有限制。 | Synonyms: particular, specific, restricted, limited, local | Antonyms: universal, general, global, widespread, common | 词性: 形容词
