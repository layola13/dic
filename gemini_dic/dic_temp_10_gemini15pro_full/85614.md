haughty: /ˈhɔːti/ (US) /ˈhɔːti/ (UK)| adj. |傲慢的，自大的|  Having or showing arrogant superiority to and disdain of those one views as unworthy.  Characterized by a high opinion of oneself and a low opinion of others.| She gave him a haughty look and turned away. 她傲慢地看了他一眼，然后转身离开了。| He spoke in a haughty tone that made everyone dislike him. 他用傲慢的语气说话，让每个人都不喜欢他。| 近义词: arrogant, disdainful, supercilious, condescending | 反义词: humble, modest, unassuming |词性: 形容词
