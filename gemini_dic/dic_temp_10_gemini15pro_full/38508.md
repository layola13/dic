conciliating:/kənˈsɪliˌeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
调解的；安抚的；和解的
Intended or likely to placate or pacify.
例句：She had a conciliating manner. 她有一种安抚人的态度。

例句：He spoke in a conciliating tone. 他以和解的语气说话。

近义词：appeasing, pacifying, placatory, mollifying, soothing, calming
反义词：antagonizing, provoking, irritating, agitating
词性: adjective


conciliate:/kənˈsɪliˌeɪt/
verb
调解；安抚；平息
To stop (someone) from being angry or discontented; placate; pacify.
例句：The company conciliated the workers by offering a pay raise. 公司通过提供加薪来安抚工人。

例句：She tried to conciliate her angry friend. 她试图安抚她愤怒的朋友。

近义词：appease, placate, pacify, mollify, soothe, calm
反义词：antagonize, provoke, irritate, agitate
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin conciliatus, past participle of conciliare (“to bring together, unite, win over”), from Latin concilium (“council, meeting”), from com- (“together”) + calare (“to call, summon”).
