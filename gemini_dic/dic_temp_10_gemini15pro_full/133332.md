nonexpressivenesses:/ˌnɒnɪkˈsprɛsɪvnəsɪz/ /ˌnɑːnɪkˈsprɛsɪvnəsɪz/
n.
缺乏表情，无表情
lack of expressiveness; the quality or state of being unexpressive.
Her nonexpressiveness made it difficult to know what she was thinking. 她的无表情让人很难知道她在想什么。
近义词：impassiveness, inexpressiveness, reserve, reticence
反义词：expressiveness, expressivity, demonstrativeness, effusiveness
词性：名词

词源：
non- (prefix) + expressive + -ness (suffix)
"non-" 表示否定，"expressive" 表示表达的，"-ness" 将形容词变为名词，表示状态或性质。
