hud:/hʌd/
noun|平视显示器；挡风玻璃上的显示屏|A transparent display that presents data without requiring users to look away from their usual viewpoints. |The pilot checked the hud for altitude and airspeed. 飞行员查看了平视显示器上的高度和空速。|head-up display| |noun
Etymology->From *head-up display*, shortened to *HUD*.
