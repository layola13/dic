nonretentive: /ˌnɑːn.rɪˈten.tɪv/ | adj. | 无记忆的；记性差的；不持久的 | Not retaining or remembering information easily; unable to hold or store something. |  She has a nonretentive memory and often forgets things. 她记性不好，经常忘事。|  The soil is sandy and nonretentive of moisture. 这种土壤是沙质的，不保水。 | Synonyms: forgetful, oblivious, unretentive | Antonyms: retentive, mindful | adjective

Etymology:
From non- + retentive.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of." "Retentive" comes from the Latin "retentus," the past participle of "retinere," meaning "to hold back" or "retain."
