hmm:/hm/, /hʌm/ | interj. | 嗯，哼，唔 | An interjection used to express hesitation, thoughtfulness, or doubt. | Example: Hmm, I'm not sure about that. 嗯，我不确定。| Example: Hmm, let me think about it. 嗯，让我想想。 | Synonym: uh, um, er | Antonym:  (Interjections typically don't have antonyms) | interjection
