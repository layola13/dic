blurred:/blɜːrd/ | adj. | 模糊的，看不清的 |  (of an image) unable to be seen clearly; indistinct. | The photograph was blurred, making it difficult to identify the subject. 照片很模糊，难以辨认拍摄对象。 | hazy, indistinct, fuzzy, out of focus | clear, sharp, distinct, focused | adjective

blurred:/blɜːrd/ | v. | (使)模糊，(使)看不清 |  make or become unclear or indistinct. |  Tears blurred her vision. 眼泪模糊了她的视线。| obscure, cloud, dim, confuse | clarify, sharpen, focus, define | verb
