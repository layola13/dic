fraudulence:/ˈfrɔːdʒələns/| n.|欺诈，欺骗，诈骗|The act of deceiving or misrepresenting; trickery or deception.|The investigation uncovered widespread fraudulence in the company's accounting practices. 这项调查揭露了该公司会计行为中普遍存在的欺诈行为。|近义词：deception, dishonesty, trickery, deceit, cheating, duplicity, guile, chicanery|反义词：honesty, integrity, truthfulness|词性:名词

fraudulent:/ˈfrɔːdʒələnt/| adj.|欺诈的，欺骗的，骗人的|Characterized by, based on, or done by fraud; deceitful.|The fraudulent activities of the company cost investors millions of dollars. 该公司的欺诈活动使投资者损失了数百万美元。|近义词：dishonest, deceptive, deceitful, crooked, shady, unscrupulous|反义词：honest, legitimate, truthful|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *fraudulence*, from Old French *fraulence*, from Latin *fraudulentia* ("deceit, trickery"), from *fraudulentus* ("deceitful"), from *fraus* ("deceit, fraud, damage").
