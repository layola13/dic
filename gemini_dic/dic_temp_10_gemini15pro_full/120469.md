minnow:/ˈmɪnoʊ/ (US), /ˈmɪnəʊ/ (UK)| n. |米诺鱼；小鱼；小人物；无足轻重的人|A small freshwater fish of the carp family.  Figuratively, it can also refer to an unimportant person or organization.|例句：The child caught a minnow in the stream.  孩子在小溪里捉到了一条米诺鱼。

例句：Compared to the industry giants, his company was just a minnow. 与行业巨头相比，他的公司不过是个小虾米。|近义词：tidler, small fry, minnow, sardine|反义词：whale, giant, behemoth|词性:noun
