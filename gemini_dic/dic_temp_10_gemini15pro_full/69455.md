feign:/feɪn/
|v.|假装；装作；佯作|To pretend to feel or be affected by (something).|He feigned illness to avoid going to school. 他装病不去上学。|近义词：pretend, simulate, sham|反义词：be genuine, be sincere|verb

feigner:/ˈfeɪnər/
|n.|假装者；装腔作势的人|A person who feigns. |He's such a feigner, always pretending to be busy. 他真是个装腔作势的人，总是假装很忙。|近义词：pretender, dissembler, impostor|反义词：genuine person|noun
