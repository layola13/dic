hoarse:/hɔːrs/ /hɔːrs/| adj.|嘶哑的|Having a rough, grating voice, often as a result of a sore throat or shouting.|例句：Her voice was hoarse from cheering at the game. 她的声音因为在比赛中欢呼而变得嘶哑。

例句：He woke up hoarse after sleeping with the window open. 他开着窗户睡觉后醒来嗓子嘶哑了。|近义词：raspy, husky, gravelly|反义词：clear, smooth, melodious|词性:形容词
