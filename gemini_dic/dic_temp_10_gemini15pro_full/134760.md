nonmembership:/ˌnɒnˈmɛmbəʃɪp/ /ˌnɑːnˈmɛmbərʃɪp/
n.
非会员身份，不属于会员
The state of not being a member of a particular group or organization.
His nonmembership in the club meant he couldn't attend the exclusive events.  由于他不是俱乐部的会员，所以无法参加专属活动。
近义词：exclusion, non-affiliation
反义词：membership
词性：名词
