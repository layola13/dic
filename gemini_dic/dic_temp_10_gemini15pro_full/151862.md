passionless:/ˈpæʃənləs/| adj.|缺乏激情的，冷淡的，无动于衷的|Lacking passion or emotion; indifferent.|He delivered a passionless speech that failed to move the audience. 他发表了一场缺乏激情的演讲，未能打动观众。|Synonyms: apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic, emotionless, spiritless|Antonyms: passionate, enthusiastic, fervent, ardent, zealous|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From passion + -less.  "Passion" comes from Old French "passion"  and ultimately from Latin "passio," meaning "suffering" or "endurance," derived from the verb "pati," meaning "to suffer" or "to endure." The suffix "-less" is an Old English suffix meaning "without."
