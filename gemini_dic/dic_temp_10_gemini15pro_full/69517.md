fellable:/ˈfeləbl/
adjective
可砍伐的，可击倒的
capable of being felled (cut down).
The tree was deemed fellable due to its disease. 这棵树由于生病被认为可以砍伐。
Large, mature trees are fellable for timber.  这些高大成熟的树木可以砍伐作为木材。
近义词:cuttable,  loggable
反义词:unfellable


词性: adjective

词源: From "fell" (verb, meaning to cut down) + "-able" (suffix meaning capable of being).  "Fell" ultimately derives from Old English *fællan*, from Proto-Germanic *fallaną*.
