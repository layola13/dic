amphibiousness:/æmˈfɪbiəsnəsnɪs/
|n.|两栖性，水陆两用性|The amphibiousness of the vehicle allowed it to travel on both land and water.  这种车辆的两栖性使其能够在陆地和水上行驶。|The expedition required equipment designed for amphibiousness.  这次探险需要为水陆两用设计的设备。|versatility, adaptability|inability, restriction|noun

Etymology:

From amphibious + -ness.  Amphibious comes from the Greek ἀμφίβιος (amphíbios), meaning "living in both," from ἀμφί (amphí, "both, on both sides") + βίος (bíos, "life").
