mashes:/ˈmæʃɪz/| n.|糊状物，土豆泥，麦芽浆|A soft, pulpy mass produced by crushing something.|The chef prepared creamy mashed potatoes as a side dish. 厨师准备了奶油土豆泥作为配菜。|近义词：puree, pulp, mush|反义词:chunks, pieces|词性:名词

mashes:/ˈmæʃɪz/| v.|捣碎，压碎|To crush or beat (something) into a soft, pulpy mass.|He mashed the bananas for the bread. 他把香蕉捣碎做面包。|近义词：crush, pulverize, squash|反义词:solidify, harden|词性:动词
