flowerful:/ˈflaʊərfəl/| adj.|富含花卉的；华丽的；辞藻华丽的|Full of flowers; elaborately ornate; using many flowery words.|The garden was flowerful in the spring. 春天，花园里开满了鲜花。|The writer's flowerful prose was a bit much for my taste.  这位作家的华丽辞藻有点不合我的胃口。|近义词：florid, flowery, ornate, embellished|反义词：plain, simple, unadorned|词性:形容词
