glead:/ɡliːd/| n.|红鹰; 红鸢|A bird of prey (Milvus milvus) of the hawk family, with a deeply forked tail and reddish-brown plumage. It is found in Europe, North Africa, and western Asia.|例句：The glead soared high above the valley. 红鸢在山谷上空翱翔。

例句：We observed a glead hunting for voles in the field. 我们观察到一只红鸢在田野里捕食田鼠。|近义词：kite, hawk, raptor|反义词:prey|词性:n.
