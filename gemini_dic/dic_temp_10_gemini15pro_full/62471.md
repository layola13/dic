endoskeletal:/ˌɛndoʊˈskɛlɪtl/
adjective
内骨骼的
Having a skeleton on the inside of the body.
例句：Humans have an endoskeleton.  人类有内骨骼。

例句：An endoskeleton provides structural support and protection for internal organs. 内骨骼为内部器官提供结构支撑和保护。

近义词：internal skeleton
反义词：exoskeleton
词性：adjective


endoskeleton:/ˌɛndoʊˈskɛlɪtl/
noun
内骨骼
An internal skeleton.
例句：The endoskeleton of a vertebrate is made of bone or cartilage. 脊椎动物的内骨骼由骨骼或软骨构成。

例句：The fish's endoskeleton allowed for flexible movement.  鱼的内骨骼使其能够灵活运动。

近义词：internal skeleton
反义词：exoskeleton
词性：noun


Etymology:
From endo- (inside) + skeletal (relating to the skeleton).  From Greek "endon" (within) + "skeleton" (dried body).
