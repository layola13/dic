overspecialized:/ˌoʊvərˈspɛʃəlaɪzd/
adjective
过度专业化的
Having a very narrow range of skills or knowledge, often to the point of being disadvantageous.  Focusing too much on a single area of expertise.
The company's overspecialized workforce struggled to adapt to the changing market.  这家公司过度专业化的员工难以适应变化的市场。
The overspecialized curriculum left students ill-prepared for the broader job market. 过于专业的课程让学生对更广泛的就业市场缺乏准备。
近义词: narrow, limited, restricted, inflexible
反义词: versatile, adaptable, broad, generalized
词性: adjective

词源:
over- (prefix) + specialize (verb) + -ed (suffix)

"over-" 表示 "过度", "过分"
"specialize" 源于拉丁语 "specialis"，意思是 "特殊的", "特有的"
"-ed"  将动词转换为形容词
