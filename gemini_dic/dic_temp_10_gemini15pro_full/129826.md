non-hedonistic:/ˌnɒn-hɪdəˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非享乐主义的 | Not pertaining to or characteristic of hedonism; not devoted to the pursuit of pleasure as a way of life. |  She adopted a non-hedonistic lifestyle, focusing on service to others. 她采取了一种非享乐主义的生活方式，专注于服务他人。 | Example: The monk lived a non-hedonistic life, rejecting material possessions and worldly pleasures.  这位僧侣过着非享乐主义的生活，拒绝物质财富和世俗享乐。 |  ascetic, austere, spartan | hedonistic, pleasure-seeking, self-indulgent | adjective
