cocultivation:/ˌkəʊˌkʌltɪˈveɪʃən/
n.
共同栽培，共培养，共同发展
The cocultivation of different cell types can lead to new discoveries. 不同细胞类型的共培养可以带来新的发现。
Cocultivation of bacteria and fungi can produce novel antibiotics.  细菌和真菌的共培养可以产生新的抗生素。
joint cultivation, co-culture
monoculture
noun
