garishly:/ˈɡɛərɪʃli/ | adv. | 花哨地，俗丽地 | in a way that is too bright and showy, and therefore unpleasant |  The dancers were garishly dressed in neon pink and green. 舞蹈演员穿着霓虹粉和绿的衣服，显得俗不可耐。 | Examples: The building was garishly painted in bright orange and purple.  这栋楼被涂成了鲜艳的橙色和紫色，十分俗气。  She wore a garishly patterned dress to the party. 她穿着一件图案花哨的连衣裙去参加派对。 | Synonyms: gaudily, flashily, ostentatiously, showily | Antonyms: subtly, tastefully, understatedly | 词性: 副词
