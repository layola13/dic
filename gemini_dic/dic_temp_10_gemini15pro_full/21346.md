bloomless:/ˈbluːmləs/| adj.|无花的，不开花的|Not producing flowers; lacking blossoms.  The bloomless plant looked barren and stark against the vibrant backdrop of the garden.|例句：The bloomless rose bush was a disappointment this year. 今年这棵不开花的玫瑰丛真令人失望。

例句：The harsh winter resulted in a bloomless spring for many fruit trees.  严酷的冬季导致许多果树在春天不开花。|近义词：flowerless, barren, sterile|反义词：blooming, flowering, blossoming|词性:形容词
bloom:/bluːm/| n. vi.|花，开花，青春焕发，繁荣昌盛|The state of having flowers; the period of greatest beauty, freshness, or vigor.|词源：From Middle English blome, from Old Norse blóm (“blossom, flower”), from Proto-Germanic *blōmô (“flower, blossom”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰleh₃- (“to blow, bloom”). Cognate with German Blume (“flower”).|词性:名词,动词
-less:/ləs/| suffix.|无...的，没有...的|A suffix forming adjectives meaning "without" or "lacking".|词源：From Old English -lēas, from Proto-Germanic *-lausaz (“-less”), from Proto-Indo-European *-los (“without”).|词性:后缀
