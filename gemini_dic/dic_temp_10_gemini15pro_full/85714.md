havocs:/ˈhævəks/| n. |浩劫，破坏，混乱|widespread destruction or disorder; great confusion and disorder|The storm wreaked havoc on the coastal towns. 这场风暴对沿海城镇造成了严重破坏。|The unexpected resignation of the CEO caused havoc in the company. 首席执行官意外辞职给公司带来了混乱。|近义词：devastation, ruin, chaos, disarray|反义词:order, peace, tranquility|词性:名词
词源:From Anglo-French *havok, alteration of Old French havot "pillaging, looting," of Germanic origin.
