bibliopolic:/ˌbɪbliəˈpɒlɪk/ | adj. |  书籍买卖的；书商的；书店的 | Relating to bookselling; of or pertaining to a bookseller. |  He had a bibliopolic interest in first editions. 他对初版书有书商的兴趣。 | 近义词: bookselling, bookish | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

bibliopole:/ˈbɪbliəˌpoʊl/ | n. | 书商；书贩 | A seller of books. | The bibliopole had a vast collection of rare manuscripts.  这位书商收藏了大量珍稀手稿。| 近义词: bookseller, book dealer | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Greek βιβλιοπώλης (bibliopōlēs, “bookseller”), from  βιβλίον (biblion, “book”) + πωλέω (pōleō, “I sell”).
