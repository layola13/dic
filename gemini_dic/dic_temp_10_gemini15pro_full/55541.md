distributivenesses:/dɪˌstrɪbjʊˈtɪvnəsɪz/
|n.|分配性，分布性|The quality or state of being distributive; the fact or property of being distributed or spread over a wide area or among many people.  The distributivenesses of resources among the population were uneven.|例句：The study examined the distributivenesses of wealth across different social groups.  这项研究调查了不同社会群体之间的财富分配情况。

例句：The government aimed to improve the distributivenesses of educational opportunities. 政府旨在改善教育机会的分配。|近义词：distribution, dispersion, dissemination|反义词：concentration, centralization|词性：名词
