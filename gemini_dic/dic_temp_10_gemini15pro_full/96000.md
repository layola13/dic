incurred:/ɪnˈkɜːrd/ | v. (past participle)|招致，引发，遭受|To become subject to something unpleasant as a result of one's own actions or behavior.  It often refers to costs or expenses.|例句：The company incurred heavy losses due to the economic downturn.  公司由于经济衰退而遭受了严重的损失。|例句：She incurred the wrath of her parents by staying out late. 她因为很晚才回家而招致了父母的愤怒。|近义词：suffered, experienced, sustained, contracted|反义词：avoided, prevented|词性：动词(过去分词)
