coenobite:/ˈsiːnəˌbaɪt/| n. |修道士；僧侣；过集体生活的人|A person who lives in a religious community, especially a monk or nun.|Example 1: The coenobites lived a simple life of prayer and work.  修道士们过着祈祷和劳作的简朴生活。

Example 2:  The ancient monastery housed hundreds of coenobites. 这座古老的修道院容纳了数百名修道士。|Synonym: monk, nun, friar, ascetic|Antonym: hermit, recluse|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *coenobita*, from Greek *koinobitēs* "one living in community," from *koinos* "common" + *bios* "life."
