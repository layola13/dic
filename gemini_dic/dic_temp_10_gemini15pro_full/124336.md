mothiest:/ˈmɔːθiɪst/
adjective
满是虫蛀的；陈腐的；过时的
smelling or tasting stale and musty, like clothes that have been stored away for too long.  Infested with moths, especially clothes moths. Figuratively, outdated or antiquated.
The old house was dark and mothiest, filled with the scent of decay. 这座老房子阴暗潮湿，满是虫蛀的味道，充满了腐朽的气味。
Her ideas about fashion were decidedly mothiest. 她对时尚的看法显然已经过时了。
近义词:musty, stale, fusty, antiquated, outdated, old-fashioned
反义词:fresh, modern, contemporary, up-to-date
词性:adjective
