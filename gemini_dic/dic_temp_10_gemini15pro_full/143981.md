outran:/ˌaʊtˈræn/
verb
超过；跑得比…快
to run faster than someone or something else
例句：He outran the police and escaped. 他跑得比警察快，逃走了。

例句：The horse easily outran its competitors. 这匹马轻松地超过了它的竞争对手。

近义词：outstripped, surpassed, exceeded, beat
反义词：lost to, was beaten by
词性：verb
