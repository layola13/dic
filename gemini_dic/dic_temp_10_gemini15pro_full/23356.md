brabble:/ˈbræbəl/ (US), /ˈbræbəl/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|争吵，吵闹；口角|A noisy quarrel about something trivial.|例句：They were brabbling over who got the last cookie. 他们在为谁拿到最后一块饼干而争吵。

例句：The children brabbled incessantly in the backseat of the car. 孩子们在汽车后座上不停地吵闹。|近义词：squabble, quarrel, argue, bicker|反义词：agree, concur, harmonize|词性: 名词, 动词
