gimmick:/ˈɡɪmɪk/| n.| 花招，噱头，小玩意儿 | A trick or device intended to attract attention, publicity, or business. |  The free toy in the cereal box is just a marketing gimmick.  谷物盒里的免费玩具只是一个营销噱头。| synonyms: trick, device, stunt, scheme, ploy | antonyms: genuineness, authenticity | noun


gimmick:/ˈɡɪmɪk/| vt.|  给…装上小玩意儿；用小玩意儿吸引 | To equip or enhance with a gimmick. |  They gimmicked the car with all sorts of gadgets. 他们给汽车装上了各种各样的小玩意儿。 | synonyms: rig, equip, trick out | antonyms: simplify, strip down | verb
