courtlike:/ˈkɔːrtlaɪk/
adj.
像法庭的；庄重的；正式的
Resembling or characteristic of a court of law; formal and dignified.
The meeting had a courtlike atmosphere, with everyone speaking formally and respectfully.  会议气氛庄重，像法庭一样，每个人都正式而恭敬地发言。
The arbitration proceedings were surprisingly courtlike, with witnesses being sworn in and evidence presented. 仲裁程序出人意料地正式，证人宣誓，并出示了证据。
近义词:formal, official, ceremonial, solemn
反义词:informal, casual, relaxed
