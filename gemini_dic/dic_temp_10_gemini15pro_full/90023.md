hosed:/hoʊzd/
adjective
完蛋的，搞砸的，累垮的
In a state of disrepair or exhaustion, typically as a result of excessive use or abuse.  Often used to describe being overwhelmed or in a difficult situation.
例句：I'm absolutely hosed after that marathon. 我跑完马拉松后累垮了。

例句：We're hosed if we don't finish this project by Friday. 如果我们周五前完不成这个项目，我们就完蛋了。
近义词：ruined, screwed, exhausted, overwhelmed
反义词：fine, okay, prepared


hosed:/hoʊzd/
verb
用软管冲洗
To wash or spray something with a hose.
例句：He hosed down the patio furniture. 他用水管冲洗了庭院家具。

例句：The firefighters hosed the burning building. 消防员用水管浇灭了燃烧的建筑物。
近义词：spray, wash, drench
反义词：dry
