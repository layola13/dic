hydroguret:/ˈhaɪdrəɡjʊəˌrɛt/ | n. | 氢化物 | A compound of hydrogen with a more electropositive element or group.  A binary compound of hydrogen with a metal. | Example: Lithium hydroguret (LiH) is a powerful reducing agent. 氢化锂是一种强还原剂。

Example: Sodium hydroguret (NaH) reacts violently with water. 氢化钠与水剧烈反应。 | 近义词: hydride | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
