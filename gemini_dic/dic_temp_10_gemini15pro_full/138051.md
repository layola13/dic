noria:/ˈnɔːriə/ | n. |戽水车，水车 |A water wheel with buckets attached to its rim, used for raising water, especially for irrigation. |The noria creaked as it slowly lifted water from the river. 水车吱吱作响，慢慢地把水从河里提上来。| synonym: water wheel, Persian wheel | antonym: pump | noun

noria:/ˈnɔːriə/ | n. |摩天轮 |A Ferris wheel or similar structure. (Less common usage) |The carnival noria offered stunning views of the city.  嘉年华的摩天轮提供了令人惊叹的城市景观。| synonym: Ferris wheel, big wheel | antonym: N/A | noun
