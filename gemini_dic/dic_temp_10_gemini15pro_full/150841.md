paragneiss:/ˈpærəˌnaɪs/| n.|副片麻岩|A metamorphic rock, usually formed by the metamorphism of sedimentary rock, that has a banded structure characterized by alternating layers of light and dark minerals.|The paragneiss exposed in the cliff face showed intricate folding patterns.  悬崖面上露出的副片麻岩呈现出复杂的褶皱图案。|The geologist identified the sample as a biotite-rich paragneiss.  地质学家将该样本鉴定为富含黑云母的副片麻岩。|Synonyms: gneiss (more general), schist (similar texture but finer grained)|Antonyms: None (It's a specific rock type, not a concept with an opposite.)|Noun
