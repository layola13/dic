condominial:/ˌkɒndəˈmɪniəl/ /ˌkɑːndəˈmɪniəl/| adj.|公寓的；共同管理的；共有的|Relating to or characteristic of a condominium; jointly owned or managed.|例句：Condominial living offers many advantages. 公寓生活有很多优势。|例句：The condominial fees cover maintenance and repairs. 公寓费包括维护和修理。|近义词：joint, shared, common|反义词：private, individual|词性:形容词
condominium:/ˌkɒndəˈmɪniəm/ /ˌkɑːndəˈmɪniəm/| n.|公寓；共同统治；共管|An apartment building in which each apartment is individually owned; joint sovereignty or control.|例句：They bought a condominium in Florida. 他们在佛罗里达买了一套公寓。|例句：The island was a condominium of three countries.  这个岛屿由三个国家共同管辖。|近义词：apartment, flat|反义词：house|词性:名词
