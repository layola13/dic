housebound:/ˈhaʊsbaʊnd/| adj.|无法出门的，居家不出的|Unable to leave one's house, typically due to illness, disability, or old age.|She has been housebound for months due to a broken leg. 她因为腿断了，几个月都无法出门。|近义词：homebound, confined, restricted|反义词:mobile, active|词性:形容词
Etymology: "house" (Old English "hūs") + "bound" (past participle of "bind," Old English "bindan")
