overtipping: /ˌoʊvərˈtɪpɪŋ/ | n. | 过度付小费 | The act of giving a tip that is excessively large. |  She felt guilty about overtipping the waiter. 她对于给服务员太多小费感到内疚。

| Example: He has a habit of overtipping. 他有一个过度付小费的习惯。| Synonym: overgenerosity, excessive tipping | Antonym: undertipping, stiffing | Noun

Etymology:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "tipping" (from "tip," meaning a small sum of money given for service).
