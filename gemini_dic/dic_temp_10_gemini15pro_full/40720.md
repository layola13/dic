converts:/kənˈvɜːrts/ | n. |皈依者；改变信仰者|A person who has changed their religious or political beliefs.|Example: Many converts to Buddhism find peace and tranquility. 许多皈依佛教的人都找到了平静和安宁。|近义词:proselyte, neophyte|反义词:apostate|词性:noun

converts:/kənˈvɜːrts/ | v. |（使）转变；（使）转换；（使）改变信仰|To change the form, function, or character of something; to cause someone to adopt a different religion or belief.|Example: The factory converts raw materials into finished products. 这家工厂将原材料转化为成品。|近义词:transform, change, alter, modify|反义词:preserve, maintain|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *convertir*, from Latin *convertere* "to turn around, to change," from *com-* "together, completely" + *vertere* "to turn."
