induct:/ɪnˈdʌkt/ (US), /ɪnˈdʌkt/ (UK)| vt.|使正式就任；使了解；征召入伍|To formally introduce someone into a position or organization, especially with a ceremony; to introduce someone to a new skill, subject, or activity; to enlist someone into military service.|The new president will be inducted into office next month.  下个月新总统将正式就职。

He was inducted into the mysteries of computer programming. 他被引入了计算机编程的奥秘。

My brother was inducted into the army last year.  我弟弟去年被征召入伍。|近义词：install, initiate, introduce, enlist|反义词：discharge, dismiss|verb
