clutchingly:/ˈklʌtʃɪŋli/
adverb|紧紧地，抓牢地|In a way that involves holding onto something tightly, often out of fear or anxiety.|She held onto his arm clutchingly as they crossed the busy street. 她紧紧地抓住他的胳膊穿过繁忙的街道。|tightly, firmly, desperately|loosely, gently|词性: 副词

clutchingly:/ˈklʌtʃɪŋli/
adverb|痉挛性地|In a way that resembles or suggests a spasm or convulsion.|His hand clutchingly grasped the railing as the pain intensified. 随着疼痛加剧，他的手痉挛地抓住栏杆。|convulsively, spasmodically, tensely|relaxedly, calmly|词性: 副词
