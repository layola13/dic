charismas: /kəˈrɪzməz/ | n. | 魅力，感召力 | Compelling attractiveness or charm that can inspire devotion in others. |  He has such charisma that everyone wants to follow him. 他拥有如此强大的魅力，每个人都想追随他。 |  
charismas: /kəˈrɪzməz/ | n. | 魅力，感召力 | A divinely conferred power or talent. | The preacher's charismas led to a huge increase in church membership.  这位传教士的感召力使教会成员大幅增加。|
charismas: /kəˈrɪzməz/ | n. | 魅力，感召力 | (plural of charisma) |  The various charismas of the leaders created a dynamic team.  领导者们各自的魅力创造了一个充满活力的团队。 | synonyms: allure, magnetism, appeal, charm, presence | antonyms: dullness, ordinariness, mediocrity | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek χάρισμα (khárisma, “gift of grace”), from χαρίζομαι (kharízomai, “I show favour, I grant”), from χάρις (kháris, “grace, favour”).
