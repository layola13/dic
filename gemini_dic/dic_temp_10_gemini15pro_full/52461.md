dies:/daɪz/| v.|死亡，去世；消失；停止运转|The engine sputtered and died. 引擎发出噼啪声然后熄火了。|近义词：deceases, expires, perishes|反义词：lives, survives|verb

Etymology: From Old English *dēġan "to die," from Proto-Germanic *dagijaną, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰegʷʰ- "to perish."
