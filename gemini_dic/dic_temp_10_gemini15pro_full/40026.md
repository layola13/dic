consumerists:/kənˈsjuːmərɪsts/| n. |消费者主义者；消费主义支持者|People who believe in consumerism, a social and economic order that encourages the acquisition of goods and services in ever-increasing amounts.|Consumerists often advocate for stronger consumer protection laws. 消费者主义者经常倡导更强有力的消费者保护法。|synonyms: shoppers, buyers, customers|antonyms: producers, manufacturers|noun

consumerists:/kənˈsjuːmərɪsts/| n. |消费主义者；消费主义支持者|A person who supports or participates in consumerism, the theory that an increasing consumption of goods is economically desirable.|Many consumerists believe that buying products is the best way to support the economy. 许多消费主义者认为购买产品是支持经济的最佳方式。|synonyms: materialists, spenders|antonyms: savers, conservationists|noun
