efficaciousnesses:/ˌefɪˈkeɪʃəsnəsɪz/
|n.| 功效，有效性（复数）|The plural form of "efficaciousness," referring to multiple instances of effectiveness or potency.  It describes the ability of something to produce a desired or intended result multiple times or in multiple ways. |The scientist compared the efficaciousnesses of different treatments. 科学家比较了不同疗法的功效。|近义词：effectivenesses, potencies|反义词：ineffectivenesses, inefficiencies|词性:名词
