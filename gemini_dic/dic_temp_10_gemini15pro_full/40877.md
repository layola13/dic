cookies:/ˈkʊkiz/| n. |饼干|Small, flat, and sweet baked goods, often made with flour, sugar, and butter or shortening.|例句：I baked chocolate chip cookies for the party. 我为聚会烤了巧克力曲奇饼干。

例句：She loves eating cookies with milk. 她喜欢用牛奶配饼干吃。


近义词：biscuits, crackers|反义词:|词性:noun


词源：From the Dutch word *koekje*, meaning "small cake."
