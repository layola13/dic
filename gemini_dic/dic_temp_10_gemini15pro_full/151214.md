pardoned:/ˈpɑːrdənd/ | v. | 原谅，赦免 |To officially forgive someone for a crime or wrongdoing and release them from punishment. | The president pardoned the convicted criminal.  总统赦免了被定罪的罪犯。 |  The governor pardoned him for his crime. 州长赦免了他的罪行。 | synonyms: forgiven, excused, absolved, reprieved | antonyms: condemned, punished, convicted, sentenced | verb

pardoned:/ˈpɑːrdənd/ | adj. | 被原谅的，被赦免的 |Having been officially forgiven for a crime or wrongdoing. | The pardoned man returned to his family. 被赦免的人回到了他的家人身边。| The pardoned prisoners were released. 被赦免的囚犯被释放了。| synonyms: forgiven, excused, absolved, reprieved | antonyms: condemned, punished, convicted, sentenced | adjective
