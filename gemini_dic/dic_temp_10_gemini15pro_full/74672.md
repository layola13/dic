freebooter:/ˈfriːbuːtər/| n.|海盗，掠夺者|A pirate or plunderer, especially one who preys on ships at sea.|The freebooters seized the merchant ship and plundered its cargo. 海盗们夺取了商船并掠夺了货物。|Synonyms: pirate, buccaneer, corsair, privateer, raider, marauder|Antonyms: lawman, protector|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Dutch *vrijbuiter*, from *vrij* ("free") + *buit* ("booty," "plunder," "loot").  Equivalent to free + boot (in the obsolete sense of "profit" or "plunder").
