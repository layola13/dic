comprise:/kəmˈpraɪz/| vt.|包含，构成，由…组成|This anthology comprises a selection of poems from the 20th century. 这本选集包含了20世纪的一些诗歌。|近义词：consist of, be composed of, include, encompass|反义词:exclude|verb

comprise:/kəmˈpraɪz/| vt.|组成，构成|Ten chapters comprise this book. 这本书由十章组成。|近义词：make up, constitute, form|反义词:decompose|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *compris, past participle of comprendre (“to understand, include”), from Latin comprehendere (“to grasp, seize, comprehend, include”), from com- (“together”) + prehendere (“to grasp, seize”).

PHRASE->
be comprised of = 由…组成
comprise of (incorrect usage, often considered redundant) = 由…组成 (不正确的用法，通常被认为是多余的)
is comprised of (passive voice, sometimes used but less common than active voice) = 由…组成（被动语态，有时使用但不如主动语态常见）

注意事项:
Comprise is often confused with "compose."  "Comprise" means "to include" or "to consist of," while "compose" means "to make up" or "to constitute."  Avoid using "comprise of."
