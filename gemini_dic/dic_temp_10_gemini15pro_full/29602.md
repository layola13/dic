ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *categoricus, from Ancient Greek κατηγορικός (katēgorikós, “predicative, affirmative”), from κατηγορέω (katēgoreō, “I accuse, assert, predicate”), from κατά (katá, “down, against”) + ἀγορεύω (agoreúō, “I speak in the assembly, harangue”).
