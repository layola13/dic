forefatherly:/ˈfɔːrˌfɑːðərli/
adjective
像祖先的；祖传的
Characteristic of or befitting a forefather; patriarchal.
例句：He had a forefatherly concern for the younger generation. 他对年轻一代怀有像祖先一样的关切。

例句：She spoke with a forefatherly tone, full of wisdom and experience. 她用一种像祖先一样的语气说话，充满了智慧和经验。

近义词：ancestral, patriarchal, avuncular
反义词：modern, contemporary
词性：adjective
