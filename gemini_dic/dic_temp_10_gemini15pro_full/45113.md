cuckoos:/ˈkuːkuːz/ (UK), /ˈkʊkuːz/ (US)| n.|布谷鸟; 杜鹃; 傻瓜; 疯子|A medium-sized, slender bird known for its distinctive two-note call and the habit of some species laying their eggs in the nests of other birds.|
The cuckoos are singing in the woods.  布谷鸟在树林里歌唱。

The escaped convict was considered a cuckoo by the police.  逃犯被警方认为是个疯子。

|近义词：fool, madman, lunatic (for the informal meaning),  koel (for the bird) |反义词:genius, sane person|词性:noun|
