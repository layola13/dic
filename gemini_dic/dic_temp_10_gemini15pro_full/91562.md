hyper-romantic:/ˌhaɪpər roʊˈmæntɪk/ | adj. |过度浪漫的，极度浪漫的 | Excessively or unrealistically romantic; characterized by an idealized or exaggerated view of love and relationships. |  She has a hyper-romantic view of love, expecting grand gestures and constant affection. 她对爱情有着过度浪漫的看法，期待着宏大的姿态和不断的爱慕。

| Example: He wrote her hyper-romantic poems filled with flowery language and idealized imagery. 他给她写了充满华丽辞藻和理想化意象的过度浪漫的诗。 | Synonyms: excessively romantic, overly sentimental, unrealistic, starry-eyed | Antonyms: pragmatic, realistic, cynical, down-to-earth | Adjective
