emblazoned:/ɪmˈbleɪzənd/ | adj. | 装饰华丽的，纹章鲜明的 | conspicuously or prominently displayed, often in bright colors or with symbols |  The knight's shield was emblazoned with a golden lion. 骑士的盾牌上饰有金色的狮子。 |  The banner was emblazoned with the school's crest.  横幅上印有学校的校徽。| synonyms: adorned, decorated, ornamented | antonyms: plain, bare, unadorned | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "emblazoner," meaning "to adorn with heraldic arms," from "en-" (in) + "blason" (coat of arms).  Ultimately from Germanic origin.  来自古法语“emblazoner”，意思是“用纹章装饰”，由“en-”（在...里面）+“blason”（盾形纹章）组成。最终源于日耳曼语。
