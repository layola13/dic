enjoyer:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪər/ | n. | 享受者，爱好者 | Someone who enjoys something. | She is an enjoyer of fine wines. 她是一个品酒爱好者。|  He's an enjoyer of life's simple pleasures. 他是一个享受生活简单乐趣的人。 |  enthusiast, aficionado, fan, devotee  |  hater, detractor | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From enjoy + -er.  "Enjoy" comes from Old French "enjoir"  (to give joy to, delight), from "en-" (in) + "joir" (to rejoice), ultimately from Latin "gaudia" (joy). "-er" is a suffix forming nouns denoting persons who perform an action or are characterized by a certain quality.
