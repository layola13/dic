palliators:/ˈpæliˌeɪtərz/
n.
缓和者；减轻痛苦的人或物
People or things that relieve or lessen the severity of pain or suffering without dealing with the underlying cause.
The use of opiates as palliators can ease the suffering of the terminally ill.  使用鸦片类药物作为缓和剂可以减轻绝症患者的痛苦。
Synonyms:  mitigators, alleviators, relievers
Antonyms:  aggravators, intensifiers
noun


palliative:/ˈpæliətɪv/
n.
缓和物；治标剂
A remedy that alleviates pain or reduces the severity of symptoms without curing the underlying cause.
The doctor prescribed a palliative for his cough. 医生给他开了治标不治本的咳嗽药。
Synonyms:  remedy, relief, solace
Antonyms:  cure, treatment
noun

palliate:/ˈpælieɪt/
v.
缓和；减轻
To make (a disease or its symptoms) less severe or unpleasant without removing the cause.
The drugs palliated the pain, but they didn't cure the disease. 这些药物缓解了疼痛，但并没有治愈疾病。
Synonyms:  alleviate, mitigate, ease, relieve
Antonyms:  aggravate, worsen, exacerbate
verb
