hanging-in-there:/ˈhæŋɪŋ ɪn ðɛr/ |idiomatic phrase|坚持下去；挺住；撑住|To persevere through a difficult situation; to continue trying despite challenges.| Example: "It's been a tough week, but I'm hanging in there."  这周很艰难，但我还在坚持。| 近义词: persevering, coping, holding on, not giving up | 反义词: giving up, quitting |phrase

hanging-in-there:/ˈhæŋɪŋ ɪn ðɛr/ |idiomatic phrase|还活着；还健在|To remain alive or healthy, often used in a humorous or understated way when someone has been through a difficult experience. |Example: "How are you doing after your surgery?" "I'm hanging in there."  “你手术后怎么样？”“我还活着。”|近义词: surviving, managing, getting by |反义词: deteriorating, worsening |phrase
