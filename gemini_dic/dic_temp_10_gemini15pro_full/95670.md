inconceivable:/ˌɪnkənˈsiːvəbl/ | adj. | 不可思议的，难以置信的 | Not capable of being imagined or grasped mentally; unbelievable. |  It's inconceivable that he would do such a thing.  他竟然会做这种事，真是不可思议。| Synonyms: unimaginable, unthinkable, incredible, unbelievable | Antonyms: conceivable, imaginable, believable, credible | adjective

inconceivable:/ˌɪnkənˈsiːvəbl/ | adj. | 难以理解的 | Too strange or unusual to be thought real or possible. | The complexity of the universe is almost inconceivable. 宇宙的复杂性几乎难以理解。| Synonyms: incomprehensible, unfathomable | Antonyms: comprehensible, understandable | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French *inconcevable or medieval Latin inconceivabilis, from in- ‘not’ + conceivabilis (from concipere ‘conceive’).
