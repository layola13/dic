antihygienically:/ˌæntiˌhaɪdʒɪˈenɪkəli/ | adv. |不卫生地，不健康地 | In a manner that is not hygienic; in a way that promotes or supports unsanitary conditions. |  He prepared the food antihygienically, leading to food poisoning. 他准备食物的方式很不卫生，导致食物中毒。 | The raw meat was stored antihygienically next to the vegetables. 生肉不卫生地区储存在蔬菜旁边。 | 近义词:insanitarily, unhealthily | 反义词: hygienically, sanitarily | 词性:副词

antihygienic:/ˌæntiˌhaɪdʒɪˈenɪk/ | adj. |不卫生的，有害健康的 | Not hygienic; likely to cause or promote disease due to unsanitary conditions. |  The antihygienic conditions in the kitchen attracted cockroaches. 厨房不卫生的环境引来了蟑螂。|  His antihygienic habits made him prone to illness.  他不卫生的习惯使他容易生病。| 近义词:insanitary, unhealthy | 反义词: hygienic, sanitary | 词性:形容词


hygiene:/ˈhaɪdʒiːn/ | n. |卫生 | Conditions or practices conducive to maintaining health and preventing disease, especially through cleanliness. |  Good hygiene is essential for preventing the spread of infection. 良好的卫生习惯对于防止感染的传播至关重要。| Personal hygiene is important for overall health. 个人卫生对整体健康很重要。 | 近义词: cleanliness, sanitation | 反义词: filth, dirt | 词性:名词


词源:

anti- (prefix): 来自希腊语 anti-，意为“反对，相反”。
hygienic (adjective): 源自希腊语 hygieinos，意为“有益健康的”，与 Hygieia（健康女神）有关。
-ally (suffix): 副词后缀，表示“以...方式”。
