hereto:/ˈhɪərtʊ/ | adv. | 至此，在此 | To this place or point; to this document or subject. |  例句：The parties hereto agree to the following terms.  双方在此同意以下条款。 | 近义词：herewith, herein | 反义词: (No direct antonym, consider using "therefrom" or similar directional adverbs) | 词性: 副词

hereto:/ˈhɪərtʊ/ | adj. | （法律或正式）上述的，此处的 | Attached to this document or mentioned previously. | 例句：Please refer to the hereto attached document. 请参考随附的文档。 | 近义词：aforesaid, aforementioned | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: 形容词
