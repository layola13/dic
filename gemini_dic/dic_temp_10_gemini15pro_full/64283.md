equitably:/ˈɛkwɪtəbli/
adverb
公平地，公正地
in a fair and just way, treating everyone equally.
The inheritance was divided equitably among the siblings. 遗产被公平地分配给了兄弟姐妹们。
The resources were distributed equitably among the different departments. 资源被公平地分配给了不同的部门。
fairly, justly, impartially
unfairly, unjustly, biasedly
adverb
