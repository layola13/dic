nonsubmissiveness:/ˌnɒnsʌbˈmɪsɪvnəs/ | n. |不服从，不顺从|The state or quality of not being submissive; resistance to authority or control. |Her nonsubmissiveness made her a difficult employee to manage. 她的不服从使她成为一个难以管理的员工。| The protesters showed their nonsubmissiveness by refusing to leave the square. 抗议者们拒绝离开广场，以示他们不服从。 |Near synonyms: defiance, rebelliousness, insubordination, disobedience |Antonyms: submissiveness, obedience, compliance, docility |noun
