chadars:/ˈtʃɑːdɑːrz/
|n.|裹尸布，大披巾|A large cloth, often worn as a shawl or head covering, especially in South Asia.  It can also refer to a shroud.|She wore a beautiful silk chadar.|近义词：shawl, wrap, blanket, shroud|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "undergarment" in a very loose sense)|Noun
