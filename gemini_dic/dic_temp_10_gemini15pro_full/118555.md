mesophyll:/ˈmɛsəfɪl/ | n. |叶肉|The tissue between the epidermal layers of a leaf, consisting of parenchyma cells containing chloroplasts.  It is the primary site of photosynthesis.|The mesophyll of the leaves was thick and spongy.|Synonyms: leaf parenchyma|Antonyms: epidermis|noun
