aggravator:/ˈæɡrəveɪtər/| n. |加重者；加剧者；恶化因素|Something that makes a problem or bad situation worse.|The noise was a constant aggravator of her headache. 噪音不断加剧她的头痛。|近义词: irritant, exacerbator, intensifier|反义词: alleviator, mitigator|noun
