carbine:/ˈkɑːrbaɪn/ /ˈkɑːrbɪn/| n. |卡宾枪|A lightweight rifle with a shorter barrel than a standard rifle, often used by cavalry or special forces.|The soldier carried a carbine slung over his shoulder. 士兵肩上扛着一支卡宾枪。|Synonyms: short-barreled rifle, light rifle|Antonyms: long rifle|noun
