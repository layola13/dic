over-immunized: /ˌoʊvərˈɪmjʊnaɪzd/
adjective
过度免疫的
Having received more immunizations than necessary or recommended, potentially leading to adverse effects.
The pediatrician expressed concern that the child might be over-immunized.  儿科医生表示担心孩子可能过度免疫了。
Some parents worry about over-immunizing their children.  一些父母担心给孩子过度免疫。
Near Synonyms:  over-vaccinated
Antonyms:  under-immunized, unvaccinated
词性: adjective


Etymology:
"over-" (prefix): from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond, excessively"
"immunize": from "immune" + "-ize." "Immune" comes from Latin "immunis," meaning "exempt, free, not liable."  "-ize" is a suffix used to form verbs meaning "to make or become."
