goter:/ˈɡoʊtər/ (US) /ˈɡəʊtər/ (UK)| n.| 污水沟，排水沟；(引申义)装满液体的容器；(引申义)大量，很多|A channel at the edge of a road or roof for carrying away rainwater.  Figuratively, it can refer to a container filled with liquid or a large quantity of something.|The street goter was overflowing after the heavy rain. 街上的排水沟在大雨过后溢出来了。|近义词：gutter, drain, channel, conduit|反义词: N/A|词性:名词


词源:
From Old French *goutere, from Old North French *gotere, from Vulgar Latin *guttura ("throat, gullet"), from Latin guttur ("throat").  Doublet of gutter.
