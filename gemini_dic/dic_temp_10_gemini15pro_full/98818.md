intellectuality:/ˌɪntəˌlɛktʃuˈælɪti/ | n. | 智力，知性，理智 | The state or quality of being intellectual; devotion to intellectual pursuits. |  Her writings reflect her deep intellectuality. 她的作品反映了她深厚的知识底蕴。 | 近义词：intelligence, intellect, brainpower, erudition | 反义词： ignorance, stupidity, simplemindedness | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *intellectualitas*, from intellectualis (“intellectual”) + -itas.  Intellectualis from intellectus (“understanding, perception, comprehension”) + -alis. Intellectus  from intelligere (“to understand”), from inter- (“between, among”) + legere (“to choose, gather, read”).
