noneffusive:/ˌnɒnɪˈfjuːsɪv/
adjective
不热情洋溢的；含蓄的；内敛的
Not effusive; restrained or reserved in emotional expression.
Her reaction to the news was noneffusive, a simple nod and a quiet "That's good." 她对这个消息的反应并不热情，只是简单地点了点头，平静地说了一句“那很好”。
The British are stereotypically known for their noneffusive manner. 英国人通常以其内敛的举止而闻名。
Synonyms: reserved, restrained, subdued, undemonstrative, quiet, low-key
Antonyms: effusive, demonstrative, emotional, exuberant, passionate
词性: 形容词
