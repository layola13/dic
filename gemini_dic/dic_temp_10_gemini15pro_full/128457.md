newsflash: /ˈnjuːzflæʃ/ | n. | 新闻快讯；突发新闻 | A brief news report interrupting other programs. |  We interrupt this program for a newsflash. 我们中断此节目插播一条新闻快讯。

| Example: A newsflash just reported a major earthquake.  一条新闻快讯刚刚报道了一场大地震。| Synonym: bulletin, alert, update | Antonym: N/A | Noun
