bracteole: /ˈbræktiˌoʊl/ | n. | 小苞片 | A small leaf-like structure at the base of a flower stalk or pedicel, especially in grasses, sedges, and other plants with inconspicuous flowers. |  The tiny bracteoles are barely visible to the naked eye. 这些小苞片几乎肉眼看不见。| synonyms: bractlet, prophyllum | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical structure) | noun
