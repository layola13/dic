indigenous:/ɪnˈdɪdʒənəs/| adj.|土著的，本地的，固有的|originating or occurring naturally in a particular place; native.|例句：The kangaroo is indigenous to Australia.  袋鼠是澳大利亚的土著动物。

例句：Many indigenous plants are used in traditional medicine. 许多本土植物用于传统医学。|近义词：native, aboriginal, endemic|反义词：foreign, alien, exotic|词性:adjective
