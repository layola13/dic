customhouse:/ˈkʌstəmhaʊs/| n.|海关, 海关大楼|A government building where customs duties are paid and goods are inspected.|The goods were held at the customhouse until the duty was paid. 货物被扣在海关，直到关税付清。|Synonyms: customs, port of entry|Antonyms: None|noun

Etymology->
From custom + house.  "Custom" in this sense refers to taxes or duties levied on imported or exported goods.
