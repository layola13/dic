goalie: /ˈɡoʊli/ (US), /ˈɡəʊli/ (UK)| n.|守门员|A player whose special role is to prevent the opposing team from scoring by protecting the goal.  A goalie is typically used in sports like hockey, soccer (football), lacrosse, and water polo.|The goalie made a spectacular save. 守门员做出了精彩的扑救。|The team's goalie was injured during the game.  这个队的守门员在比赛中受伤了。|近义词：goalkeeper, netminder, goaltender|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps attacker/forward as an opposing role)|noun
