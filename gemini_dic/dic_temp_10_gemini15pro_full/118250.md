merchandise:/ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪs/ (US) /ˈmɜːtʃəndaɪz/ (UK)| vt. | 推销；销售 | To promote the sale of (goods). | The company merchandised its new product line through television advertising.  该公司通过电视广告推销其新的产品线。| market, sell, promote |  buy | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "marchandise" meaning "trade, commerce, goods," derived from "marchant" (merchant). Ultimately from Late Latin "mercatare" meaning "to trade," from "mercatus" (market).
