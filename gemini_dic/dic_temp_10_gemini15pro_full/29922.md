cavaliering:/ˌkævəˈlɪərɪŋ/
verb
轻慢对待；傲慢地对待
treating in a haughty or disdainful manner; acting in a superior or condescending way, especially regarding someone's feelings or concerns.
He was cavaliering his responsibilities, ignoring deadlines and skipping meetings. 他对自己的职责漫不经心，无视截止日期，还经常缺席会议。
She cavaliered her way through the interview, acting as if the job was already hers.  她在面试中表现得非常傲慢，好像这份工作已经是她的囊中之物了。
synonyms:  disregarding, dismissing, slighting, scorning, flouting
antonyms: respecting, honoring, appreciating, valuing, heeding
