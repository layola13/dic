benefitters:/ˈbɛnɪfɪtərz/
n.
受益人，受惠者
Those who receive an advantage or profit from something.
The new tax policy will greatly benefit the benefitters of the trust fund.  新的税收政策将使信托基金的受益人大大受益。

The benefitters of the charity program were extremely grateful.  慈善项目的受益者非常感激。

近义词:recipients, beneficiaries
反义词:losers, sufferers
词性:noun
