banderillero:/ˌbændərɪˈjɛroʊ/| n.|斗牛士助手，插花镖手|A bullfighter's assistant who plants banderillas (barbed darts) in the bull's shoulders.|The banderillero skillfully placed the banderillas in the bull's shoulders. 斗牛士助手熟练地将花镖插在了公牛的肩上。|Synonyms:  matador's assistant, bullfighter's aide|Antonyms: bull, matador (although the matador is the lead, not an opposite)|noun
