oscillogram:/əˈsɪləɡræm/ /ɑːˈsɪləɡræm/| n.|波形图，示波图|A visual representation of the variation of an electrical quantity, such as voltage or current, as a function of time, typically displayed on the screen of an oscilloscope.|The oscillogram showed a regular sine wave. 示波图显示了一个规则的正弦波。|Synonyms: waveform, trace|Antonyms: N/A|noun

oscillogram:/əˈsɪləɡræm/ /ɑːˈsɪləɡræm/| n.|振动图，震动图|A visual representation of the oscillations or vibrations of a physical system, such as a pendulum or a vibrating string, typically recorded as a graph or a series of images.|The oscillogram of the pendulum's motion showed a gradual decrease in amplitude. 振动图显示了钟摆运动的振幅逐渐减小。|Synonyms: vibration graph, oscillation graph|Antonyms: N/A|noun
