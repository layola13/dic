dingily:/ˈdɪŋɪli/| adv.|昏暗地，阴沉地|In a dingy or gloomy manner; dimly.|The room was dingily lit, creating a somber atmosphere. 房间昏暗的灯光营造出一种忧郁的氛围。|The bar was dingily lit and smelled of stale beer. 酒吧灯光昏暗，弥漫着变质啤酒的味道。|近义词：dimly, gloomily, darkly|反义词：brightly, cheerfully|词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "dingy" + "-ly".  "Dingy" likely originated from "dung," referring to its dirty or discolored appearance.
