destructionist: /dɪˈstrʌkʃənɪst/ | n. | 破坏主义者；毁灭主义者 | A person who advocates or engages in destruction, especially of property or established institutions. |  The destructionist plotted to bomb the government building.  这个破坏主义者密谋炸毁政府大楼。| synonyms: saboteur, vandal, anarchist, iconoclast | antonyms: builder, creator, constructor, preservationist | noun

destructionist: /dɪˈstrʌkʃənɪst/ | adj. | 破坏性的；毁灭性的 | Relating to or characteristic of destruction or destructionists. | His destructionist tendencies led him to tear down everything he built. 他具有破坏性的倾向，导致他拆毁了他建造的一切。| synonyms: destructive, ruinous, devastating, catastrophic | antonyms: constructive, beneficial, helpful, productive | adjective
