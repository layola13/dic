motets:/moʊˈtɛts/ | n. |经文歌|A short piece of sacred choral music, typically polyphonic and unaccompanied, with words from a Latin text other than the ordinary of the Mass.|The choir sang several beautiful motets during the service.  教堂礼拜仪式上唱诗班唱了几首优美的经文歌。|Synonyms: anthem, canticle, hymn |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps secular song) | Noun
