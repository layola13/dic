cymatium:/saɪˈmeɪʃiəm/| n.|  (建筑) 线脚，波状线饰，最上层线脚 |A molding which crowns a cornice or other architectural member; the upper member of a classical cornice.| The cymatium of the temple was intricately carved.  寺庙的线脚雕刻精美。| synonyms: cornice, molding, fillet | antonyms: base, foundation | noun

cymatium:/saɪˈmeɪʃiəm/| n.| (植物) 浪花状边缘 | A wavy edge, as of a leaf or petal. | The cymatium of the orchid's petal was a vibrant purple. 兰花花瓣的波浪状边缘是鲜艳的紫色。| synonyms: margin, edge, border | antonyms: center, middle | noun
