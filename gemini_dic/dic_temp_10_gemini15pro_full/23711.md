brasshatted:/ˈbræsˌhætɪd/
adjective
高级军官的；高级将领的；官僚的
Relating to or characteristic of high-ranking military officers, often used to imply a disconnect from the realities faced by lower-ranking personnel.  Also sometimes used more generally to describe any high-ranking official or bureaucrat perceived as out of touch.
The brasshatted generals were making decisions without any understanding of the conditions on the ground.  高级将领们在不了解地面情况的情况下做出决定。

The brasshatted bureaucrats in Washington seem to have forgotten about the needs of ordinary people. 华盛顿的官僚们似乎已经忘记了普通人的需求。
Synonym: top-brass, high-ranking, senior
Antonym: low-ranking, junior, enlisted
词性: 形容词
