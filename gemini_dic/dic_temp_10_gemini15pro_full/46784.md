danglement:/ˈdæŋɡlmənt/| n.|悬垂，悬挂物；未决状态|A state of uncertainty or suspense, as in a dangling modifier or an unresolved plot point.  The danglement of the rope from the ceiling was a safety hazard.  天花板上的绳子悬着是一种安全隐患。|近义词：suspension, overhang, protrusion|反义词:attachment, security, resolution|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French dangler "to hang," from Latin *danglare, frequentative of demorari "to delay, linger," from de- "down" + morari "to delay."
