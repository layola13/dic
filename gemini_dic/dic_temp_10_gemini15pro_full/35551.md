coalface:/ˈkoʊlfeɪs/ (US), /ˈkəʊlfeɪs/ (UK)| n. |煤炭工作面；最前线；（斗争的）第一线|The part of a coal mine where coal is cut from the seam.  Figuratively, it refers to any place where the most difficult or challenging work is done. |The miners at the coalface work in dangerous conditions. 煤矿工人工作条件危险。|近义词：front line, sharp end, frontline, trenches|反义词: headquarters, office, base|词性:名词

词源:  Compound of 'coal' and 'face'. The 'face' refers to the exposed surface of the coal seam being worked on.
