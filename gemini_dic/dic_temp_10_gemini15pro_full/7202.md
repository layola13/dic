angle-of-dip:/ˈæŋɡəl əv dɪp/ | n. | 倾角 | The angle between the horizontal and a line in the direction of the greatest slope of an inclined plane, especially of a stratum of rock or a vein of ore.  It is the angle that a magnetic needle makes with the horizontal. | The geologist measured the angle-of-dip of the rock strata.  地质学家测量了岩层的倾角。|  inclination, slope, gradient | ascent | noun
