feigned:/feɪnd/
adjective
假装的，虚假的
pretended to be affected by (a feeling, state, or injury).
She feigned sleep to avoid having to answer him. 她假装睡觉以避免不得不回答他。
近义词:pretended, simulated, sham, affected, counterfeit
反义词:genuine, real, sincere, authentic
词性:adjective

词源:
From Middle English *feynen, *faynen, from Old French feindre, from Latin fingō (“I form, shape, invent, feign”), related to figūra (“shape, figure”).
