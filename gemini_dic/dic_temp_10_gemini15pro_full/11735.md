aromatherapists:/əˌrəʊməˈθerəpɪsts/ | n. |芳香治疗师|A person who practices aromatherapy, the use of essential oils for therapeutic purposes.|例句：The aromatherapists blended oils to create a calming atmosphere. 芳香治疗师混合精油以营造平静的氛围。

例句：Many aromatherapists believe essential oils can improve overall well-being. 许多芳香治疗师认为精油可以改善整体健康。|近义词：massage therapist, holistic practitioner|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) conventional doctor, physician |词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From aroma + therapy + -ist.  "Aroma" comes from Greek "arōma" meaning "spice, fragrant herb."  "Therapy" comes from Greek "therapeia" meaning "healing, medical treatment."  "-ist" is a suffix denoting a person who practices or is skilled in something.
