overweathered:/ˌoʊvərˈwɛðərd/ | adj. |过度风化的|  Excessively weathered; exposed to the elements for too long, resulting in deterioration.  This term is primarily used in geological contexts to describe rocks or landforms.| The overweathered cliff face was prone to landslides.  过度风化的悬崖面容易发生山体滑坡。|Synonyms: weathered, eroded, decayed, disintegrated| Antonyms: fresh, new, unweathered | adjective
