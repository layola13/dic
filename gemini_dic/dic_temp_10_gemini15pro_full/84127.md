half-marked:/ˌhɑːfˈmɑːrkt/| adj.| 半标记的，部分标记的 | Partially marked; marked in some way but not completely. | The exam papers were half-marked when the fire alarm went off.  考试卷子批改到一半时火警响了。 |  partly marked, incompletely marked | fully marked, completely marked | adjective


half-marked:/ˌhɑːfˈmɑːrkt/| adj.| 半成品的，未完成的 |  Unfinished, incomplete. | The project was half-marked, with many tasks still outstanding.  这个项目只完成了一半，还有很多任务未完成。| partially completed, unfinished | finished, complete | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"half-" + "marked"
"half-" comes from Old English "healf," meaning "one of two equal parts."
"marked" comes from Old English "mearcian," meaning "to mark, put a mark on."
