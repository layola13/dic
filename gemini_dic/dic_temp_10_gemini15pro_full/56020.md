do-time:/ˈduː taɪm/
verb phrase
服刑；坐牢
To serve a prison sentence.
He did time for armed robbery. 他因持械抢劫而服刑。
synonyms: serve time, be in prison, be incarcerated
antonyms: be free, be released
verb phrase

do-time:/ˈduː taɪm/
verb phrase
消磨时间
To spend time, often in a leisurely or unproductive way.
We did some time at the beach waiting for the tide to go out. 我们在海滩上消磨了一些时间，等待退潮。
synonyms: kill time, pass time, spend time, while away the time
antonyms: work, be productive
