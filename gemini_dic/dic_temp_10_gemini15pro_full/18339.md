belligerency:/bəˈlɪdʒərənsi/
n.|好战性，交战状态|The state of being at war or being actively hostile.|例句：The country's belligerency led to a declaration of war.  这个国家的好战性导致了战争的宣告。

例句：His belligerency made him unpopular. 他的好战性使他不受欢迎。|近义词：hostility, aggressiveness, militancy|反义词：peacefulness, pacifism|词性:名词
Etymology: From Latin bellum "war" + -ger "carrying, bearing" + -ency (suffix forming nouns of state or quality).
