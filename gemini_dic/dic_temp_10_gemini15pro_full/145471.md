overclassify:/ˌoʊvərˈklæsɪfaɪ/| v. |过度分类|To classify something too specifically or into too many categories.|例句：Researchers sometimes overclassify data, leading to unnecessarily complex models. 研究人员有时会过度分类数据，导致模型不必要地复杂。

例句：The library's overclassification system made it difficult to find books. 图书馆的过度分类系统使得查找书籍变得困难。|近义词：overcategorize, oversubdivide|反义词: underclassify, simplify|词性:动词
