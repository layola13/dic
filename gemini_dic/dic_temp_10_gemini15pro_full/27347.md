cambium:/ˈkæmbiəm/| n. |形成层|A layer of cells in a plant that produces new phloem and xylem cells.|The cambium is essential for the growth of woody plants.|Synonyms: growth layer, meristem|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "dead tissue" in a broad sense)|Noun
