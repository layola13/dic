meet-cute:/ˈmiːtˌkjuːt/
n.
相遇相爱的桥段;偶遇;
A situation, typically in a film or television programme, in which two people destined to be lovers meet in an amusing or charming way.
例句：Their meet-cute was at a coffee shop where they both reached for the same book. 他们在一家咖啡店相遇相爱的桥段是两人同时伸手去拿同一本书。

例句：The movie's meet-cute involved a spilled latte and a shared umbrella. 这部电影的相遇相爱桥段包括一杯洒出来的拿铁和一把共用的雨伞。

近义词：chance encounter, accidental meeting
反义词:planned meeting
