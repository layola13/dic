notionist:/ˈnoʊʃənɪst/| n.|空想家，理论家|A person who holds or promotes notions, especially impractical or speculative ones.  A theorist, often derogatory.|He was dismissed as a mere notionist, with no practical experience.|近义词：theorist, idealist, visionary, dreamer, utopian|反义词：pragmatist, realist|词性:noun
