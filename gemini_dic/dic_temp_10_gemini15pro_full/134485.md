nonlaminating:/ˌnɒnˈlæmɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非层压的
Not undergoing or designed for lamination; not forming or arranged in layers.
The nonlaminating material was chosen for its breathability.  这种非层压材料因其透气性而被选中。
These nonlaminating fabrics are easier to sew. 这些非层压织物更容易缝制。
近义词: nonlayered, nonstratified
反义词: laminating, layered, stratified
词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "laminating" (present participle of "laminate," from Latin "lamina" meaning "thin plate").  由表示“不”的前缀“non-”和“laminate”的现在分词构成，“laminate”源自拉丁语“lamina”，意为“薄板”。
