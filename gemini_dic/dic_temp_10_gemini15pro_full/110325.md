lighter:/ˈlaɪtər/ (US), /ˈlaɪtə/ (UK)| n., adj., v.|打火机；较轻的；更明亮的；点火者；点燃物|A device used to create a flame, typically fueled by butane or other flammable liquid.  Can also be an adjective meaning less heavy, or a verb meaning to make something lighter in color or weight.|例句：He flicked his lighter and lit a cigarette. 他啪地一声打开打火机，点燃了一支香烟。|近义词：match, kindling (n.), brighter, paler (adj.), illuminate (v.)|反义词：heavier, darker (adj.), extinguisher (n.)|词性:名词, 形容词, 动词

lighter:/ˈlaɪtər/ (US), /ˈlaɪtə/ (UK)| adj.|较轻的；更明亮的|Less heavy than something else; having a paler shade of color.|例句：The feather is lighter than the rock. 羽毛比石头轻。|近义词：brighter, paler|反义词：heavier, darker|词性:形容词

lighter:/ˈlaɪtər/ (US), /ˈlaɪtə/ (UK)| v.|点燃；使更明亮|To set something on fire; to make something lighter in color or weight.|例句：She used a match to lighter the candle. 她用火柴点燃了蜡烛。|近义词：ignite, illuminate|反义词：extinguish, darken|词性:动词

词源：From Middle English *lightere, from Old English *lēohtora, comparative of lēoht (“light”).
