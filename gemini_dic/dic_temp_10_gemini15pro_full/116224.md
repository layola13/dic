masoola:/məˈsuːlə/ | n. |彩礼，聘金 (尤指在南亚文化中) | A payment of money or goods by the bridegroom to the bride's family, especially in South Asian cultures. | Example: The masoola was a substantial amount, reflecting the bride's status. 彩礼数额巨大，反映了新娘的地位。

Example:  Negotiations over the masoola continued for several weeks before the wedding. 婚礼前，关于彩礼的谈判持续了好几个星期。 | 近义词: dowry, bride price | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun


词源:  From Urdu and Hindi मसूला (masūlā), from Arabic مَسْئُول (masʾūl, “responsible, liable”).  Originally referred to a tax or levy, later evolving to specifically denote the bride price.
