inlandish:/ɪnˈlændɪʃ/
adjective
内陆的；偏僻的；粗野的；土气的
characteristic of or appropriate to the interior of a country or region; unsophisticated; rustic
例句：They thought his inlandish customs were strange. 他们觉得他那内陆的习俗很奇怪。

例句：Her inlandish manners embarrassed her sophisticated friends. 她土气的举止让她那些见多识广的朋友感到尴尬。

近义词：rural, rustic, provincial, backwoods, unsophisticated, unpolished
反义词：cosmopolitan, sophisticated, urbane, polished, refined
词性：adjective
