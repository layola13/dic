caesura:/siˈzjʊərə/ /sɪˈʒʊrə/ | n. | 休止；停顿 | A pause or break in a line of verse, or in a musical composition.  |  Example 1: The caesura in the middle of the line emphasizes the contrast between the two images.  这行诗中间的休止强调了两种意象之间的对比。

Example 2: The composer used a caesura to create a sense of drama.  作曲家运用休止来营造戏剧性的感觉。 | Synonym: pause, break, interruption, rest | Antonym: continuation, flow  | noun
