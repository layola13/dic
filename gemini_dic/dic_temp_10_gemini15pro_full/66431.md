exegetics:/ˌɛksɪˈdʒɛtɪks/| n. |释经学，圣经注释|The critical explanation or interpretation of a text, especially of scripture.|Her work focuses on feminist exegetics of the Bible.|近义词：interpretation, exposition, commentary|反义词：misinterpretation, distortion|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Latin exegetica, from Greek exēgētikos "leading out, explanatory," from exēgeisthai "to explain," from ex- "out" + hēgeisthai "to lead."
