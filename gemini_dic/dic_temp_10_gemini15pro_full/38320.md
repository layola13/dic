conceded:/kənˈsiːdɪd/
verb
承认，让步
admitted (something) unwillingly, especially that one is wrong, or that another person is right; granted (a privilege or right)
例句：He conceded defeat. 他承认失败。
例句：The government has conceded the right to peaceful protest. 政府已经承认和平抗议的权利。
近义词：admit, acknowledge, grant, allow
反义词：deny, refuse, dispute
词性：v.


conceded:/kənˈsiːdɪd/
adjective
被承认的，被让步的
granted or yielded
例句：a conceded goal  一个被承认的进球
例句：The conceded point was minor but important.  被承认的观点虽小但很重要.
近义词：acknowledged, admitted, granted
反义词：denied, disputed, contested
词性：adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from Old French conceder, from Latin concedere ‘yield, grant’, from con- ‘together’ + cedere ‘go, yield’.
