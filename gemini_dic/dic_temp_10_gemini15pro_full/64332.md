eradicate:/ɪˈrædɪkeɪt/
verb
根除，消灭
To completely destroy or get rid of something, especially something bad.
The government is trying to eradicate corruption. 政府正在努力根除腐败。
Malaria has been eradicated in many countries. 疟疾在许多国家已被根除。
synonyms: eliminate, abolish, exterminate, wipe out
antonyms: cultivate, foster, promote
