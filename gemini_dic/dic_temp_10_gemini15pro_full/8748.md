antidilution:/ˌænti daɪˈluːʃən/ |adj. n.|反稀释|A provision in an agreement or security that protects an investor from a decrease in their ownership percentage of a company.| The company's charter includes strong antidilution provisions to protect early investors. 该公司的章程包括强有力的反稀释条款，以保护早期投资者。 |Synonyms: preemptive right, subscription right| Antonyms: dilution| adjective, noun

antidilution:/ˌænti daɪˈluːʃən/ |n.|反稀释|The act or process of preventing dilution.|The company adopted an antidilution policy to maintain the value of existing shares. 公司采取了反稀释政策，以维持现有股份的价值。|Synonyms: protection against dilution| Antonyms: dilution| noun
