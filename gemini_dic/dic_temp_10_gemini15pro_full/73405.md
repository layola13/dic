foreintend:/fɔːrɪnˈtɛnd/
verb
预先打算，预谋
to intend beforehand; to have in mind as a purpose or plan
He foreintended to build a large house. 他预先打算建一座大房子。
foresee, anticipate, plan
intend afterward
