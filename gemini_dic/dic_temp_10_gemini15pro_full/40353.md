contractility:/ˌkɒntrækˈtɪləti/ /ˌkɑːntrækˈtɪləti/| n. |收缩性；收缩力|The ability of muscle fibers to shorten and generate force.|The heart muscle’s contractility is essential for pumping blood throughout the body. 心肌的收缩性对于将血液泵送到全身至关重要。

The drug increased the contractility of the blood vessels. 这种药物增加了血管的收缩性。


|近义词：contractile force, contractile power|反义词: dilatancy, expansibility|词性:noun
