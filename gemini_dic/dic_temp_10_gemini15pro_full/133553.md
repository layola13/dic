nonfiniteness:/nɒnˈfaɪnaɪtnəs/ |n.| 非限定性; 非限定形式 |The nonfiniteness of the verb allows it to function as a noun. 动词的非限定性使其能够充当名词。| Non-finite clauses, like the one in "Running is good exercise", are often used in more formal writing.  像“跑步是很好的锻炼”中的非限定性从句，经常用于更正式的写作中。|近义词: non-finite form, verbal noun|反义词: finiteness|词性: 名词
