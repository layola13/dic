fluorescent:/flɔːˈres(ə)nt/ /flʊˈres(ə)nt/
adjective
荧光的；发荧光的
Emitting light when struck by radiation, typically ultraviolet light.  A fluorescent object absorbs light of one wavelength and emits light of a longer wavelength.
The fluorescent lights buzzed overhead. 荧光灯在头顶嗡嗡作响。
The fish emitted a fluorescent green glow.  这条鱼发出荧光绿色的光。
synonyms: luminous, glowing, bright, radiant, shining
antonyms: dull, dark, dim
adjective
