feastables:/ˈfiːstəblz/
n.
可供盛宴享用的食物; 美味佳肴
Foods fit for a feast; delicacies.
We prepared a table laden with feastables for the holiday. 我们为假期准备了一桌丰盛的佳肴。
Synonyms: delicacies, treats, goodies, viands
Antonyms: scraps, leftovers, slops
词性: 名词
