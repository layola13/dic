bellona:/bəˈloʊnə/ | n. |贝罗娜, 战神|The Roman goddess of war, often identified with the Greek goddess Enyo.  |Bellona was worshipped as a goddess of war. 贝罗娜被奉为战神。|例句：The temple of Bellona was located outside the pomerium. 贝罗娜神庙位于波梅里乌姆城外。|近义词：Enyo, goddess of war|反义词: goddess of peace|词性:noun

bellona:/bəˈloʊnə/ | n. |战争, 冲突|A situation of war or conflict. (Figurative usage) |The bellona of political infighting tore the country apart.  政治内斗的战争撕裂了这个国家。|例句：The nation was consumed by the bellona of civil war. 这个国家被内战的战火吞噬了。|近义词：war, conflict, strife|反义词:peace, harmony|词性:noun
