anti-malthusian:/ˌæntiˈmælθjuːziən/
anti-malthusian: adj. | 反马尔萨斯主义的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a theory that opposes Malthus's predictions, especially his prediction that population growth will outstrip food supply. |  The economist presented an anti-Malthusian view of population growth. 这位经济学家提出了一个反马尔萨斯主义的人口增长观点。 |  neo-Malthusian | Malthusian | adjective


anti-malthusian:/ˌæntiˈmælθjuːziən/
anti-malthusian: n. | 反马尔萨斯主义者 | A person who opposes Malthus's predictions, especially his prediction that population growth will outstrip food supply. | He was a staunch anti-Malthusian, believing that technology would solve food scarcity. 他是一位坚定的反马尔萨斯主义者，相信科技能够解决食物短缺问题。| neo-Malthusian | Malthusian | noun
