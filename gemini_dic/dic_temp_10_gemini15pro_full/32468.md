chinampas:/tʃɪˈnɑːmpəs/
|n.|奇南帕斯（人工浮岛）|A system of raised agricultural beds, constructed in shallow lake beds in the Valley of Mexico.|The Aztecs cultivated chinampas to produce food for their large population.|Synonyms: floating gardens, artificial islands|Antonyms: mainland, terra firma|noun
