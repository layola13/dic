disinhabit:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhæbɪt/
verb
使无人居住，使荒芜
To cause (a place) to become empty of inhabitants; depopulate.
The war disinhabited the village. 战争使这个村庄荒无人烟。
近义词:depopulate, evacuate
反义词:populate, inhabit
词性:v.
