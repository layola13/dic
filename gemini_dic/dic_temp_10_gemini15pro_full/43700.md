crediting:/ˈkrɛdɪtɪŋ/
v.
记入贷方；归功于；相信
Assigning a value or recognition to something.  Attributing something to someone or something. Believing something to be true.
The company is crediting my account with the refund.  公司正在将退款记入我的账户。
He credited his success to hard work and dedication. 他将他的成功归功于努力工作和奉献精神。
I find it hard crediting that story. 我很难相信那个故事。
synonyms:ascribing, attributing, imputing, recognizing, acknowledging
antonyms:debiting, discounting, disbelieving
verb
