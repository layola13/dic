hatchetmen:/ˈhætʃɪtmɛn/| n.|打手；暴徒；杀手|A person hired to carry out violent acts, especially murder, on behalf of another. |The dictator employed hatchetmen to silence his political opponents. 独裁者雇佣打手来压制他的政敌。|Example: The mob boss sent his hatchetmen to deal with the informant. 黑帮老大派他的打手去处理告密者。|Synonyms: assassin, killer, thug, goon, enforcer|Antonyms: protector, guardian, defender|noun
