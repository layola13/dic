cypraea:/saɪˈpriːə/ | n. |宝螺；宝贝|A tropical marine gastropod mollusk (family Cypraeidae) having a brightly colored, glossy shell with a long, narrow opening.  It's also known as a cowrie or cowry. |The cypraea shell was once used as currency in some parts of the world. 宝螺壳曾经在世界某些地方用作货币。|She found a beautiful cypraea shell on the beach. 她在海滩上发现了一个美丽的宝螺壳。|Synonyms: cowrie, cowry|Antonyms: None|Noun

cypraea:/saɪˈpriːə/ | n. |(总称)宝螺科动物|Referring to the family of mollusks that includes cowries.|Cypraea are found in warm, shallow waters around the world. 宝螺科动物生活在世界各地温暖的浅水中。|The diversity of cypraea shells is remarkable. 宝螺壳的多样性非常引人注目。|Synonyms: cowries|Antonyms: None|Noun
