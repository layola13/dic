lithontriptor:/ˈlɪθəntrɪptər/
noun
碎石器
A medical instrument used to crush stones in the body, especially kidney or bladder stones.
The lithontriptor was used to break up the kidney stones without invasive surgery.  碎石器被用于无创手术粉碎肾结石。
lithotripter, stone crusher
