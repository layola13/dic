nonadopter:/ˌnɒnəˈdɒptər/ | /ˌnɑːnəˈdɑːptər/ | n. | 非采用者，未采用者 | Someone who does not adopt something, especially a new technology or product. | Example 1:  The company is trying to understand why some consumers remain nonadopters of their new software.  这家公司正试图弄明白为什么一些消费者仍然没有采用他们的新软件。

Example 2: Nonadopters of electric vehicles often cite concerns about range and charging infrastructure.  不采用电动汽车的人经常提到对续航里程和充电基础设施的担忧。 | Synonyms:  laggard, holdout, resister | Antonyms: adopter, user, consumer | noun
