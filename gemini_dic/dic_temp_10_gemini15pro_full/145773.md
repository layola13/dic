overdaintily:/ˌoʊvərˈdeɪntɪli/ | adv. | 过分讲究地，过分精致地 | Excessively dainty or delicate in manner or action; too refined or fastidious.  Characterized by an exaggerated or affected delicacy. | She ate her cake overdaintily, picking at it with her fork. 她吃蛋糕的样子过分讲究，用叉子一点一点地挑着吃。|  The dancer moved overdaintily across the stage, as if afraid to touch the floor. 舞蹈演员在舞台上过分讲究地移动，仿佛害怕碰到地板。 | 近义词: primly, fastidiously, affectedly, mincingly | 反义词: roughly, clumsily, coarsely | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (excessively) + "daintily" (in a dainty manner). "Dainty" comes from Old French "daintie" (delight, pleasure), ultimately from Latin "dignitas" (worthiness, dignity).
