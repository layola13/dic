magnetically:/mæɡˈnɛtɪkli/
adverb
磁力地；磁性地
in a way that involves magnetism or magnetic force.
The iron filings were magnetically attracted to the magnet. 铁屑被磁铁磁性地吸引。
The door closed magnetically. 门磁力地关上了。


近义词:
by magnetism
through magnetism

反义词:
non-magnetically

词性:
adverb
