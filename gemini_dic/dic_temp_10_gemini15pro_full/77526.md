/hwɒt/ |pron. | 什么 | Used to ask for information about something. | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？ |which, who | --- | pronoun

/hwɒt/ |det. | 什么；多么 | Used to ask about the identity or nature of something, or to emphasize its amount or degree. | What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！|which, this, that | --- | determiner

/hwɒt/ |int. | 什么？| Used to express surprise, disbelief, or anger. | What? You didn't tell me! 什么？你没告诉我！| --- | --- | interjection

Etymology: From Old English _hwæt_, neuter singular of _hwā_ ("who"). Related to who, which, and how.
