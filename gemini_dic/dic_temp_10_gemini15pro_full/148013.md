overripely: /ˌoʊvərˈraɪpli/ | adj. | 过熟地 | Excessively ripe; too ripe.  Beyond the stage of perfect ripeness. | The overripely bananas were turning brown.  过熟的香蕉正在变成褐色。 | The overripely mangoes were mushy and sweet. 过熟的芒果很软很甜. | 近义词: overripe, rotten | 反义词: unripe, green | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above") + "ripe" (Old English rīpe "ripe, mature," from Proto-Germanic *rīpijaz) + "-ly" (Old English -līce, adverbial suffix, from Proto-Germanic *-līkō)
