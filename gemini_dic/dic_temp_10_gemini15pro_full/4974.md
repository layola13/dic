alkalinity:/ˌæl.kəˈlɪn.ɪ.ti/ | n. |碱性，碱度 |The alkalinity of the soil is important for plant growth. 土壤的碱性对植物生长很重要。| The alkalinity of the water was tested.  水的碱度被测试了。 | 近义词：basicity | 反义词：acidity | 词性: noun
Etymology: From alkali + -ity.  Alkali ultimately comes from Arabic *al-qalīy* "ashes of saltwort," a plant whose ashes were used in soapmaking.
