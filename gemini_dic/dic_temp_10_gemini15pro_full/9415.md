antiradiation:/ˌæntiˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən/
adjective
防辐射的
Designed to protect against or reduce the harmful effects of radiation.
例句：
The workers wore antiradiation suits.  工人们穿着防辐射服。

The bunker is antiradiation.  这个掩体是防辐射的。
