bodhisattva:/ˌboʊdiːˈsʌtvə/ | n. | 菩萨 | A person who is able to reach nirvana but delays doing so out of compassion in order to save suffering beings. |  The Dalai Lama is considered a bodhisattva. 达赖喇嘛被认为是一位菩萨。| synonym: enlightened being, compassionate one | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "unenlightened being") | noun
