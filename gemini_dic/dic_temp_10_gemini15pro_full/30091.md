ceaseless:/ˈsiːsləs/| adj.|不停的，不断的|Never stopping or pausing; incessant.|The ceaseless noise of the traffic kept me awake.  不停的交通噪音让我无法入睡。|近义词：incessant, constant, continuous, unending, perpetual|反义词：intermittent, sporadic, occasional|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Middle English *ceseles*, from *cesen* ("to cease") + *-les*. Ultimately from Latin *cessāre*.
