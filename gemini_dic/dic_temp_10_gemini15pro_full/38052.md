composedly:/kəmˈpoʊzɪdli/
adverb
镇定地；沉着地
In a self-possessed and calm way, without showing any worry or excitement.
She accepted the bad news composedly. 她镇定地接受了这个坏消息。
近义词: calmly, serenely, coolly, placidly
反义词: nervously, anxiously, agitatedly
词性: 副词
