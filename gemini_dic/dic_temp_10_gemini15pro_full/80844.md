ghoul: /ɡuːl/ | n. |食尸鬼；盗墓者；贪婪残忍的人|An evil spirit or phantom, especially one supposed to rob graves and feed on corpses; a person morbidly interested in death or disaster; a person who is excessively greedy or cruel.|  The old legends told tales of ghouls who haunted graveyards.  古老的传说讲述了食尸鬼出没于墓地的故事。| Synonym: fiend, demon, monster, vampire | Antonym: angel, saint | noun
