communalism: /kəˈmjuːnəlɪzəm/ | n. | 共产主义，地方自治主义，社群主义 |  A principle of political organization based on federated communes.  A theory or system of social organization based on small self-governing communities. An allegiance to one's own ethnic group rather than to the wider society. | India has suffered from communalism between Hindus and Muslims. 印度一直遭受着印度教徒和穆斯林之间的社群冲突。| Synonyms: collectivism, communism, socialism | Antonyms: individualism, capitalism | noun

communalism: /kəˈmjuːnəlɪzəm/ | n. | (印度)宗教社群主义 |  Specifically in India, refers to adherence to a particular religious community, often with strong implications of political and social division and sometimes violence. |  Communalism has been a persistent problem in India since independence. 自独立以来，宗教社群主义一直是印度的一个持续存在的问题。| Synonyms: sectarianism, bigotry, prejudice | Antonyms: tolerance, secularism | noun
