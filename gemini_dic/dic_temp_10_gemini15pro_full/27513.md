camphor:/ˈkæmfər/ (US) /ˈkæmfə(r)/ (UK)| n.|樟脑|A white, crystalline, volatile substance with a strong, aromatic odor, obtained from the wood and bark of the camphor tree and used in medicine and industry.|Camphor is used in some mothballs. 樟脑用在一些樟脑丸里。|Synonyms:  camphor tree, camphora|Antonyms: None|noun

camphor:/ˈkæmfər/ (US) /ˈkæmfə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|樟脑的；似樟脑的|Having the smell or properties of camphor.|The ointment had a distinct camphor scent.  这药膏有明显的樟脑味。|Synonyms: camphoraceous|Antonyms: None|adjective
