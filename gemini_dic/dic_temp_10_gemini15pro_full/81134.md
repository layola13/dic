gramophonically:/ˌɡræməˈfɒnɪkli/ | adv. |  按照留声机的方式；与留声机相关地 |  In the early days of recording, music was reproduced gramophonically.  在录音的早期，音乐是通过留声机复制的。 |  例句：The music was gramophonically reproduced, resulting in a slightly distorted sound.  这音乐是通过留声机复制的，导致声音略有失真。

例句：He described the sound gramophonically, focusing on the technical aspects of the recording. 他从留声机的角度描述了声音，重点讲述了录音的技术方面。 | 近义词: phonographically | 反义词: digitally | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "gramophone" + "-ically". "Gramophone" comes from the Greek "gramma" (letter, writing) + "phone" (sound).
