overpolishes:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːlɪʃɪz/
|v.|过度抛光，过度润色|To polish something excessively, often to the point of detracting from its appearance or quality.|He overpolished the car, and now it looks like plastic. 他把车擦得太亮了，现在看起来像塑料的。|overdoes, overworks|underpolishes|verb


overpolishes:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːlɪʃɪz/
|n.|过度抛光，过度润色 (的行为或结果)|The act of polishing something excessively, or the state of being overpolished.|The overpolish on the furniture made it look cheap. 家具过度抛光使它看起来很廉价。|excess polishing, overdoing|underpolishing|noun
