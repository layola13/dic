fibreless:/ˈfaɪbərlɪs/
adjective
无纤维的；无筋的
Lacking fibers; without fiber.
The cake had a strangely fibreless texture.  这蛋糕的质地异常地没有纤维感。
The fruit has a smooth, fibreless flesh.  这种水果的果肉光滑，没有纤维。
synonym: smooth, fine-textured, pulpy
antonym: fibrous, stringy, coarse
adjective
