illeviable:/ɪˈliːviəbl/
adjective
不可征税的; 不能征收的
not capable of being levied (as a tax).  Something that cannot be legally or practically taxed.
The judge ruled that the family heirloom was illeviable due to its historical significance. 法官裁定，由于其历史意义，该传家宝是不可征税的。
Certain religious donations are considered illeviable.某些宗教捐款被认为是不可征税的.
Synonym: unexempt, nontaxable, untaxable
Antonym: leviable, taxable
词性: adjective
