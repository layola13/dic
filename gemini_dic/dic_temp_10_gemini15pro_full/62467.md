endoscopist:/ɛnˈdɒskəpɪst/ /ɛnˈdɑːskəpɪst/
n.
内窥镜医师；内窥镜检查者
A doctor who specializes in using an endoscope to examine the internal organs of the body.
The endoscopist performed a colonoscopy to check for polyps. 内窥镜医师进行了结肠镜检查以检查息肉。
synonyms:  endoscopy specialist, gastroenterologist (sometimes)
antonyms: None (This is a profession, not a concept with an opposite.)
noun
