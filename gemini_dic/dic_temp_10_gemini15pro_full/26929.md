calamus:/ˈkæləməs/| n.|菖蒲；芦苇；（古希腊罗马的）芦苇笔|A tall perennial marsh plant with sword-shaped leaves and aromatic roots.|例句：The ancient Egyptians used calamus for medicinal purposes. 古埃及人用菖蒲作药用。

例句：The calamus swayed gently in the breeze. 菖蒲在微风中轻轻摇曳。|近义词：reed, rattan, sweet flag|反义词:|noun
