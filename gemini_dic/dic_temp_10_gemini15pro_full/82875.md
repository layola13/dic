gullwing:/ˈɡʌlˌwɪŋ/ | n. adj.|鸥翼; 鸥翼式的|A gull wing is a type of door that opens upwards and outwards, resembling the wings of a gull in flight. It is often used on sports cars.  As an adjective, it describes something that has this type of door.| The Mercedes-Benz 300SL is famous for its gullwing doors. 梅赛德斯-奔驰300SL以其鸥翼门而闻名。| The DeLorean DMC-12 is a classic example of a gullwing car.  德罗宁DMC-12是鸥翼式汽车的经典例子。| synonyms: butterfly doors, scissor doors | antonyms: conventional doors, suicide doors | noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From gull + wing.  The term arose from the resemblance of the open doors to the outstretched wings of a gull.
