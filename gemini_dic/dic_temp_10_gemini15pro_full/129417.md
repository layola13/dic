nominating:/ˈnɒmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 提名的；任命的 |  Present participle of "nominate," meaning to propose or formally enter as a candidate for election or for an honor or award. | The nominating committee will meet next week. 提名委员会将于下周举行会议。 | Synonyms: appointing, proposing, recommending | Antonyms: rejecting, dismissing | Verb (present participle)

nominate:/ˈnɒmɪneɪt/ | vt. | 提名；任命；指定 | To propose or formally enter as a candidate for election or for an honor or award. |  I nominate John for president. 我提名约翰为主席。| Synonyms: appoint, propose, designate | Antonyms: reject, dismiss | Verb
