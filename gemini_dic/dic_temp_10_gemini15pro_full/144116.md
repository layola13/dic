outscore:/ˌaʊtˈskɔːr/
verb
以较多得分胜过；比…得分高
To defeat (an opponent) by scoring more points.
The Warriors outscored the Cavaliers in the final quarter.  勇士队在最后一节比骑士队得分高。
Our team outscored the opponents by 10 points.  我们队比对手多得了10分。
近义词: beat, defeat, triumph over, surpass
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb
