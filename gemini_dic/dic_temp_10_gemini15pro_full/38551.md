conclusional:/kənˈkluːʒənəl/ | adj. |结论性的，总结性的 | Relating to or based on a conclusion. |  The report's conclusional statement summarized the key findings. 报告的结论性陈述总结了主要发现。 | Example: His conclusional remarks were brief and to the point. 他的总结性发言简短扼要。 | Synonyms: conclusive, final, summary, concluding | Antonyms: introductory, preliminary, initial | etymology: From Late Latin *conclusionalis*, from Latin conclusio (“conclusion”), from concludere (“to conclude”). | word type: adjective
