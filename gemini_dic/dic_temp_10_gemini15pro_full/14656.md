avowed:/əˈvaʊd/| adj.|公开承认的；公然的|openly acknowledged or declared; unashamed|He is an avowed supporter of the president. 他是总统的公开支持者。|近义词：admitted, acknowledged, professed, declared|反义词：unacknowledged, undisclosed, secret, hidden|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle French *avouer* (to avow), from Latin *advocare* (to call to witness, summon), from *ad-* (to) + *vocare* (to call).
