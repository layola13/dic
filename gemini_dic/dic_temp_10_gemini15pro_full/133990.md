nonhistrionically:/ˌnɒnhɪstrɪˈɒnɪkli/
adverb
不夸张地；平静地；不做作地
in a manner that is not dramatic or overly emotional.
She accepted the award nonhistrionically, simply thanking her team and family. 她平静地接受了奖项，只是简单地感谢了她的团队和家人。
He reported the incident nonhistrionically, sticking to the facts. 他平静地报告了这起事件，只陈述了事实。
Synonyms: calmly, quietly, soberly, understatedly, without fanfare
Antonyms: dramatically, theatrically, hysterically, melodramatically
词性: 副词
