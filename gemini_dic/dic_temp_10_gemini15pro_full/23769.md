bravure:/brəˈvjʊər/| n.|勇气；虚张声势；炫耀|A flamboyant confidence of style or manner.  A display of daring; an act of bravery.|He played the difficult sonata with bravure. 他以炫技的方式演奏了这首艰难的奏鸣曲。|近义词：daring, audacity, swagger, flamboyance, panache|反义词：timidity, cowardice, apprehension|词性:noun
词源:From French bravoure, from brave 'brave'.
