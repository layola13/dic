egalitarian: /ɪˌɡælɪˈtɛəriən/ | adj. n. | 平等主义的；平等主义者 | Believing in or based on the principle that all people are equal and deserve equal rights and opportunities. |  例句：She is a strong advocate for egalitarian policies. 她是一位平等主义政策的坚定拥护者。

例句：The company strives to create a more egalitarian workplace. 这家公司致力于创造一个更加平等的工作场所。| 近义词：democratic, equitable, fair | 反义词：elitist, hierarchical, unequal | 词性：adjective, noun

egalitarians: /ɪˌɡælɪˈtɛəriənz/ | n. | 平等主义者 | People who believe in or support egalitarianism. | 例句：Egalitarians advocate for social justice and equality for all. 平等主义者倡导社会公正和人人平等。


例句：Many egalitarians support progressive taxation systems. 许多平等主义者支持累进税制。 | 近义词：equalists, levelers | 反义词：elitists, snobs | 词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *égalitaire*, from égal (“equal”) (from Latin aequālis) + -itaire (from Latin -ārius).
