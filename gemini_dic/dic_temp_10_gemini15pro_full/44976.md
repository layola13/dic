crystallinity:/ˌkrɪstəˈlɪnɪti/ | n. |结晶度，晶体度|The degree to which a substance is crystalline; the state or condition of being crystalline.  A measure of the extent to which a polymer has an ordered, crystalline structure as opposed to an amorphous structure.| The crystallinity of the polymer affects its mechanical properties.  这种聚合物的结晶度会影响其机械性能。|Synonyms: crystallisation, crystallization |Antonyms: amorphousness| noun
