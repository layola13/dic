orientated:/ˈɔːriənˌteɪtɪd/ /ˈɔːrɪˌɛnˌteɪtɪd/
|adj.|以…为方向的；面向…的|adjusted or aligned in a specific direction or position; having a particular focus or outlook|例句：The company is orientated towards the export market. 这家公司面向出口市场。|例句：He is very career-orientated. 他非常注重事业。|近义词：oriented, directed, focused|反义词：disoriented, lost|词性：形容词

orientated:/ˈɔːriənˌteɪtɪd/ /ˈɔːrɪˌɛnˌteɪtɪd/
|v.|使适应；确定方向|to familiarize someone with new surroundings or circumstances; to determine one's position or direction|例句：The program is designed to orientate new employees.  这个项目旨在帮助新员工适应环境。|例句：We need to orientate ourselves before we start the hike. 在开始徒步旅行之前，我们需要确定方向。|近义词：orient, acclimate, adjust|反义词：disorient, confuse|词性：动词 (过去分词)
