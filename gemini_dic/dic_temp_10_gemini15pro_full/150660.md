paprika: /ˈpæprɪkə/ (US) /ˈpəˈpriːkə/ (UK)| n.|甜椒|A large, sweet pepper of various colours, such as red, yellow, or green.|We stuffed the paprika with rice and meat. 我们用米饭和肉把甜椒填满。|
近义词:bell pepper, sweet pepper|反义词:chili pepper, hot pepper|词性:noun

词源:
From Hungarian *paprika*, from Serbian *papar*, meaning "pepper," ultimately from Latin *piper*.
