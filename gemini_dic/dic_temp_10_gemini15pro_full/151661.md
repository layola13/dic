parting:/ˈpɑːrtɪŋ (UK), ˈpɑːr.t̬ɪŋ (US)/| n. adj.| 分别，分离，告别; 分开的，告别的|A parting is the act of leaving someone or something, or the occasion when you leave someone or something.  As an adjective, it describes something that happens or is given when people separate.|例句：We said our sad partings and went our separate ways. 我们伤心地告别，各奔东西。|近义词：farewell, separation, departure, leave-taking|反义词：meeting, reunion, arrival|词性: noun, adjective

parting:/ˈpɑːrtɪŋ (UK), ˈpɑːr.t̬ɪŋ (US)/| n. | 分缝，分界线 |A parting is a line or division that separates things, particularly in hair.|例句：Her hair had a parting down the middle. 她的头发在中间分缝。|近义词：division, separation, line|反义词：joining, union|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *partinge, from Old English *partung, from the verb *partan (“to part, divide, share, separate”), from Proto-Germanic *partōną (“to divide, distribute”), from Proto-Indo-European *pert- (“to distribute, assign”). Cognate with Scots pairtin (“parting”), West Frisian parting (“parting, partition”), Dutch parting (“parting”), German Partung (“parting”).
