guardlike:/ˈɡɑːrdlaɪk/ | adj. | 像卫兵的；警惕的；警觉的 | Resembling or characteristic of a guard; watchful and vigilant. |  The dog stood guardlike at the door. 那条狗像卫兵一样守在门口。|  The cat sat with a guardlike expression, watching the birds. 猫咪带着警惕的表情坐着，注视着鸟儿。 | 近义词：watchful, vigilant, alert, attentive | 反义词：inattentive, careless, negligent | 词性: 形容词

词源: guard (守卫) + like (像)  -  A straightforward combination of "guard" and the suffix "-like," meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
