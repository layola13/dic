outshined:/ˌaʊtˈʃaɪnd/
verb
胜过，使黯然失色
surpassed in achievement or excellence; eclipsed.
例句：Her performance outshined all the others. 她的表演胜过所有其他人。
例句：The new building completely outshined the old one. 新建筑完全使旧建筑黯然失色。
近义词：eclipse, overshadow, surpass, outclass, outstrip
反义词：pale in comparison, be inferior to
词性：动词(past tense and past participle of outshine)
