nonretentively:/ˌnɑːn rɪˈtɛntɪvli/
adverb|非保留地，不持久地|In a way that does not retain or store information or memories.|The information was processed nonretentively, leaving no trace in the system.|近义词: transiently, fleetingly, temporarily|反义词: retentively, permanently|词性: 副词
词源: From non- + retentive + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "retentive" comes from the Latin "retent-" meaning "held back," and "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
