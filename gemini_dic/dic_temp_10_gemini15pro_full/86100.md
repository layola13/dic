heart-stoppingly: /ˈhɑːrtˌstɒpɪŋli/ (UK) /ˈhɑːrtˌstɑːpɪŋli/ (US)
|词源| From "heart-stopping" + "-ly". "Heart-stopping" is a compound adjective formed from "heart" (referring to the organ) and "stopping" (from the verb "stop"), meaning causing the heart to cease beating, either literally or figuratively. The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective into an adverb. |
|例句|  The acrobat performed a heart-stoppingly dangerous stunt.  杂技演员表演了一个惊险万分的危险特技。|
|例句|  She delivered the news heart-stoppingly slowly.  她以令人心跳停止的缓慢速度宣布了这个消息。|
|近义词| breathtakingly, thrillingly, astonishingly, amazingly |
|反义词| calmly, quietly, boringly, tediously |
|词性| 副词(adverb)
