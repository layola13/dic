chlorine:/ˈklɔːriːn/ (US), /ˈklɔːriːn/ (UK)|n.|氯|A common chemical element, a greenish-yellow gas, used as a disinfectant in water.|Chlorine is commonly used to purify swimming pools.|Synonyms: Cl (chemical symbol), halogen|Antonyms: None (Chlorine is a unique element)|noun

chlorine:/ˈklɔːriːn/ (US), /ˈklɔːriːn/ (UK)|adj.|氯的;含氯的|Containing or relating to chlorine.|Chlorine bleach is a powerful cleaning agent.|Synonyms: chlorinated|Antonyms:  None (This usage primarily describes presence/absence, not opposites)|adjective
