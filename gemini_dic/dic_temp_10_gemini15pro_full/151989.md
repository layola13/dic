PHRASE->
put out to pasture:  退休；退役；不再使用
The old car was put out to pasture.  那辆旧车被淘汰了。
rich pasture:  肥沃的牧场
The cattle thrived on the rich pasture.  牛在肥沃的牧场上茁壮成长。
pasture land:  牧场；草地
The farmer owns a large area of pasture land.  这位农民拥有大片的牧场。
find pasture:  找到放牧的地方；找到生计
The nomadic tribes moved their herds to find pasture.  游牧部落迁移他们的牲畜以寻找牧场。
green pastures:  肥沃的牧场；美好的前景
They moved to the city in search of greener pastures.  他们搬到城市去寻找更好的发展机会。
