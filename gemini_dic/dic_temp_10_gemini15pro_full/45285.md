culture:/ˈkʌltʃər/ (US), /ˈkʌltʃə(r)/ (UK)| n.|文化，教养，培植，培养（细菌）|The cultivation of bacteria. 细菌的培养|例句：The company has a strong culture of innovation.  这家公司有很强的创新文化。|近义词：civilization, society, customs|反义词：barbarism, ignorance|noun

culture:/ˈkʌltʃər/ (US), /ˈkʌltʃə(r)/ (UK)| vt.|培养，培植|To cultivate bacteria in a petri dish. 在培养皿中培养细菌|例句：He cultured the cells in the laboratory. 他在实验室里培养细胞。|近义词：cultivate, grow, develop|反义词：neglect, destroy|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cultura* "tilling, cultivation, worship," from *cultus* "care, cultivation, worship," past participle of *colere* "to till, cultivate, cherish, worship."
