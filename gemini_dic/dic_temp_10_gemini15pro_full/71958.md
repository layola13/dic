flightily:/ˈflaɪtɪli/ | adv. |轻浮地，轻率地，反复无常地 | In a frivolous or capricious manner; without seriousness or stability. | She behaved flightily, changing her mind every few minutes. 她表现得很轻浮，每隔几分钟就改变主意。| She dressed rather flightily for a funeral. 她穿得轻佻，不适合参加葬礼。| 近义词：capriciously, frivolously, giddily, erratically | 反义词：seriously, thoughtfully, steadily, consistently | 词性：副词

flightiness:/ˈflaɪtinəs/ | n. |轻浮，轻率，反复无常 | The state or quality of being frivolous, capricious, or unstable. | Her flightiness made it difficult to rely on her. 她的反复无常让人难以信赖她。| His flightiness was charming at first, but soon became irritating. 他起初的轻浮很有魅力，但很快就变得令人恼火了。| 近义词：capriciousness, frivolity, giddiness, erraticism | 反义词：seriousness, thoughtfulness, steadiness, consistency | 词性：名词

词源：From flight (referring to the erratic movement of a bird) + -y/-ness (suffixes indicating quality or state). 源自 flight（指鸟类飘忽不定的动作）+ -y/-ness（表示性质或状态的后缀）。
