disannex:/ˌdɪsəˈnɛks/
verb
取消兼并；脱离；分离
To separate (a territory) from a country or other political entity.
The city council voted to disannex the recently acquired land. 市议会投票决定将最近获得的土地分离出去。
After the war, the territory was disannexed. 战后，这片领土被分离出去了。
synonyms: detach, separate, disconnect, sever
antonyms: annex, attach, join, unite
词性: 动词
