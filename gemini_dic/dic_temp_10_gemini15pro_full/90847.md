hung-over: /ˌhʌŋˈoʊvər/ (US) /ˌhʌŋˈəʊvə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|宿醉的，感到难受的|Feeling unwell as a result of excessive drinking of alcohol on the previous day.|例句：I woke up feeling completely hung-over after last night's party. 昨晚派对后，我醒来时感到宿醉得厉害。

例句：He was so hung-over he couldn't go to work. 他宿醉得厉害，无法去上班。|近义词：ill, sick, unwell, crappy, rough|反义词:sober, well|词性:adjective
