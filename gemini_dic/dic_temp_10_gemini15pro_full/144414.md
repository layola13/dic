outstunk:/ˌaʊtˈstʌŋk/
verb
发出比...更难闻的气味; 恶臭胜过
surpassed in emitting a foul odor; smelled worse than.
例句：The durian outstunk the garbage.  榴莲比垃圾还臭。

例句：His gym socks outstunk the locker room. 他的健身房袜子比更衣室还臭。

近义词：out-smelled, overpowered
反义词：fragrant, perfumed
词性：verb
