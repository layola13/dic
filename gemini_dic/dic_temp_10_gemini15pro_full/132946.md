nonemergent:/nɒnɪˈmɜːdʒənt/ | adj. | 非紧急的，非急诊的 | Not requiring immediate or urgent medical attention. |  The doctor determined that the patient's condition was nonemergent. 医生确定病人的情况并非紧急。 |  The clinic handles both emergent and nonemergent cases.  这家诊所处理急诊和非急诊病例。| Synonyms: routine, elective, non-urgent | Antonyms: emergent, urgent, critical | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + emergent (arising unexpectedly). "Emergent" comes from the Latin "emergere" (to emerge, rise up).
