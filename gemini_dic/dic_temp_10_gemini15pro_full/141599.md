oppugner:/əˈpʌɡnər/| n. |质疑者，反对者，挑战者|Someone who opposes or challenges something, especially a belief, policy, or decision.|The politician faced a barrage of questions from oppugners during the debate.  这位政治家在辩论中面临来自质疑者的连番提问。|例句：His fiercest oppugner was his former mentor. 他最激烈的反对者是他以前的人生导师。

例句：The oppugner questioned the validity of the scientific study.  质疑者对这项科学研究的有效性提出了质疑。|近义词：critic, adversary, opponent, challenger, antagonist|反义词：supporter, proponent, advocate, ally|词性: noun
