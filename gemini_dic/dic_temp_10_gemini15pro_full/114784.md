maltose:/ˈmɔːltoʊs/ (US) /ˈmɔːltəʊs/ (UK)| n.|麦芽糖|A disaccharide formed by the hydrolysis of starch. It is composed of two glucose molecules linked together.|Maltose is produced during the germination of barley, which is used in brewing.|近义词:sugar, disaccharide|反义词:None|noun
