nonconsideration:/ˌnɑːnkənˌsɪdəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |不考虑，忽视，漠视|Lack of consideration; failure to consider something.|例句：His nonconsideration of her feelings hurt her deeply. 他对她的感受漠不关心，深深地伤害了她。

例句：The nonconsideration of the environmental impact led to protests.  对环境影响的忽视导致了抗议。|近义词：disregard, neglect, indifference, inattention|反义词：consideration, attention, regard, heedfulness|词性:名词
