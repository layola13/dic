convexities: /kɒnˈvɛksɪtiz/ /kɑːnˈvɛksɪtiz/ | n. |凸面，凸度| The plural form of convexity, referring to multiple outward curves or bulges. | The lens has several slight convexities. 这个透镜有几个轻微的凸面。 | synonyms: bulges, protuberances, prominences | antonyms: concavities, depressions, hollows | noun

convexities: /kɒnˈvɛksɪtiz/ /kɑːnˈvɛksɪtiz/ | n. |(金融) 凸性| In finance, it refers to the measure of the curvature in the relationship between bond prices and bond yields.  |  Investors should be aware of the convexities of their bond portfolios. 投资者应该意识到其债券投资组合的凸性。 | synonyms: curvature, second derivative | antonyms: concavity | noun
