agrarian:/əˈɡrɛəriən/ | adj. |土地的，农业的，耕地的|Relating to cultivated land or the cultivation of land.|例句：Agrarian reform is necessary to improve the lives of farmers. 土地改革对于改善农民的生活是必要的。

例句：The country's economy is primarily agrarian.  这个国家的经济以农业为主。|近义词：agricultural, rural, farming |反义词:industrial, urban|词性:adjective

agrarian:/əˈɡrɛəriən/ | n. |土地改革者，平均地权论者|A person who advocates a social movement for political reform or to improve the economic status of the farmer.|例句：The agrarians fought for land redistribution. 平均地权论者为土地重新分配而斗争。

例句：He was a staunch agrarian, believing in the importance of land ownership for all. 他是一位坚定的土地改革者，相信土地所有权对所有人的重要性。|近义词：farmer, peasant, agriculturalist |反义词:landlord, aristocrat|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin agrarius (“of land, of fields”), from Latin ager (“field”).
