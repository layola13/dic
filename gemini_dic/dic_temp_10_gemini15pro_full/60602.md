elcaja: /elˈkaxa/ (Spanish pronunciation)| n. |盒子，箱子| "El caja" is Spanish for "the box." It's a combination of the definite article "el" (the) and the noun "caja" (box).| Example:  El caja es grande. The box is big.

Example:  Pon los libros en el caja. Put the books in the box.| 近义词: box, container, carton (English equivalents) | 反义词: None (A direct antonym in either language is difficult.  One might consider concepts like "open space" or "emptiness" as contrasting ideas.) |词性: noun
