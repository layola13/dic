drippiness:/ˈdrɪpinəs/| n.|滴落；湿淋淋|The drippiness of the ice cream cone made it messy to eat. 冰淇淋蛋筒湿哒哒的，吃起来很脏。|近义词：wetness, dampness|反义词：dryness|词性:名词
Etymology: From "drip" (verb) + "-iness" (suffix forming nouns of quality or state).  "Drip" comes from Old Norse "dripa".|-|-|-
