aglet: /ˈæɡlət/ | n. | 鞋带头；金属箍 |A small sheath, often of plastic or metal, used to protect the end of a shoelace, cord, or drawstring. | The aglet on my shoelace broke, making it difficult to tie my shoe. 我的鞋带头断了，很难系鞋带。|  The aglet prevents the lace from fraying.  这个鞋带头防止鞋带磨损。| synonym: tip, tag | antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun
