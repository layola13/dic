impracticability:/ɪmˌpræktɪkəˈbɪləti/
|n.|不可行性，不切实际|The impracticability of the plan was evident from the start.  这个计划从一开始就不切实际。|
|英文解析：The state or quality of being impracticable; not capable of being put into practice or of being done or accomplished.|
|例句1：The impracticability of the proposal led to its rejection.  该提案因不可行而被否决。|
|例句2：Due to the impracticability of crossing the flooded river, they had to turn back. 由于洪水泛滥的河流无法通行，他们不得不折返。|
|近义词：infeasibility, impossibility, unworkability|
|反义词：feasibility, practicality, possibility|
|词性：noun|
|词源：From impracticable + -ity.  Impracticable from in- "not" + practicable "able to be put into practice".|
