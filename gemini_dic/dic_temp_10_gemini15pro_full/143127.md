outcaroled: /ˌaʊtˈkærəld/
verb
胜过歌声; 以更响亮的歌声压倒
to sing carols more loudly or more successfully than someone else.
例句：The choir outcaroled the other groups at the festival.  唱诗班在节日上以歌声压倒了其他团体。

例句：She outcaroled all the other carolers in the neighborhood. 她在社区的圣诞颂歌比赛中胜过了所有其他人。

近义词：outsing, surpass in singing
反义词：be outsung

词性: verb

词源:  "out-" (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + "carol" (verb, to sing carols)
