buzzphrase:/ˈbʌzfreɪz/ | n. | 流行语；时髦词语 | A word or phrase, often an item of jargon, that is fashionable for a period of time. |  Example 1: "Synergy" has become a common buzzphrase in the business world.  中文： “协同效应”已成为商界的常用流行语。

Example 2:  The consultant peppered his presentation with buzzphrases like "thinking outside the box" and "blue-sky thinking." 中文：这位顾问在他的演讲中充斥着诸如“跳出思维定势”和“大胆创新的想法”之类的流行语。 | 近义词：catchphrase, slogan, cliché, jargon | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "plain language," "simple terms") | 词性: noun
