deathlessly:/ˈdeθləsli/
adverb
不朽地，永恒地
In a manner that suggests immortality; eternally.
His name will live deathlessly in the annals of history. 他的名字将永远铭刻在历史的史册上。
The poet wrote deathlessly of love and beauty. 这位诗人永恒地歌颂着爱情和美丽。
Synonym: eternally, immortally, forever, perpetually
Antonym: mortally, transiently, ephemerally
词性: 副词
