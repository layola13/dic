atlantes:/ætˈlæntiːz/
noun
阿特兰特雕像，男像柱
Male figures used as supporting columns, especially in classical architecture.
The atlantes supporting the roof of the temple were intricately carved.  寺庙屋顶的男像柱雕刻精美。
近义词: telamones, supports
反义词: caryatids (女像柱)
词性: noun (plural)

Etymology: From Latin Atlantes, plural of Atlas, the Titan in Greek mythology condemned to hold up the sky. 源自拉丁语 Atlantes，是希腊神话中被判举天的泰坦神 Atlas 的复数形式。
