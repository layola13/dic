concorde:/ˈkɒŋkɔːd/ (US: /kənˈkɔːrd/) | n. |协和式飞机；和谐，一致 | A supersonic passenger jet jointly developed and manufactured by Sud Aviation (later Aérospatiale) and the British Aircraft Corporation (BAC). It had a maximum speed over twice the speed of sound. Also refers to agreement or harmony. |例句：The Concorde was a marvel of engineering. 协和式飞机是工程学的奇迹。|近义词：harmony, agreement, unity, accord |反义词：discord, disagreement, conflict |词性:noun

concorde:/kənˈkɔːrd/ /ˈkɒŋkɔːd/ | n. |和谐，一致 | Agreement or harmony between people or groups. |例句：There was complete concorde between the two nations.  两国之间完全和谐一致。|近义词：harmony, agreement, unity, accord |反义词：discord, disagreement, conflict |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French concorde, from Latin concordia ("agreement, union, harmony"), from con- ("together") + cord- ("heart").
