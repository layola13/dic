baited:/ˈbeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 引诱的，装了饵的；激动的，紧张的 | Having bait attached or inserted; feeling excited or tense in anticipation of something. | The fisherman cast his baited hook into the river. 渔夫把装了饵的鱼钩抛入河中。 |  The audience sat baited breath, waiting for the announcement.  观众屏息以待，等待着宣布结果。| synonyms: lured, tempted, primed, eager, anxious | antonyms: unbaited, calm, relaxed | adjective
