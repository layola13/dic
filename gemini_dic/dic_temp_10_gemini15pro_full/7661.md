anode:/ˈænəʊd/ (UK) /ˈænˌoʊd/ (US)| n.|阳极，正极|The positive electrode in an electrolytic cell, towards which the negative ions (anions) migrate.|The anode attracts negatively charged ions.|例句：Electrons flow from the anode to the cathode. 电子从阳极流向阴极。

例句：The anode in this battery is made of zinc.  这个电池的阳极是由锌制成的。


近义词：positive electrode, positive pole|反义词: cathode, negative electrode|词性:noun
