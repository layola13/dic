fuliginosity:/ˌfjuːlɪdʒɪˈnɒsɪti/ (US) /ˌfuːlɪdʒɪˈnɒsɪti/ (UK)
n.
煤烟状，乌黑
The state or quality of being sooty or smoky; sootiness.
The fuliginosity of the old chimney was evident in the thick layer of soot.  老烟囱里的煤烟在厚厚的煤灰层中显而易见。
Near synonyms: sootiness, smokiness, blackness, darkness
Opposite: brightness, whiteness
