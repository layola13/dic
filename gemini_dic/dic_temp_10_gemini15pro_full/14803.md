ax:/æks/| n. |斧头|A tool with a bladed head fixed perpendicular to a handle, used for chopping wood, etc.|He used an ax to chop down the tree. 他用斧头砍倒了树。|近义词：hatchet, tomahawk|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps saw as a contrasting tool)|noun

ax:/æks/| v. |解雇，削减|To dismiss or discharge from employment; to reduce or eliminate drastically.|The company axed 100 jobs. 公司裁员100人。|近义词：fire, dismiss, cut|反义词: hire, employ|verb
