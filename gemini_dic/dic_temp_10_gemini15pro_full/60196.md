effigiate:/ˈɛfɪdʒɪeɪt/| v. |描绘；塑造；刻画|To represent by or as if by an image; portray.|The artist effigiated the queen in marble. 艺术家用大理石雕刻了女王的雕像。|Synonyms: portray, depict, represent, delineate|Antonyms: distort, misrepresent|verb

effigy:/ˈɛfɪdʒi/| n. |肖像；雕像；塑像|A representation or image, especially sculptured, of a person.|They burned an effigy of the president. 他们焚烧了总统的肖像。|Synonyms: statue, figure, likeness, image|Antonyms: N/A|noun
