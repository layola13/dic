ETYMOLOGY->
From hydro- +‎ fluor- +‎ -ic.  "Hydro-" comes from Greek "hydōr" (water). "Fluor-" comes from Latin "fluere" (to flow), referring to the mineral fluorspar (calcium fluoride) which was used as a flux in metalworking. "-ic" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "of or pertaining to".
