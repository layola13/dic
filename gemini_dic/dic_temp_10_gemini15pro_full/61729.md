emotively:/ɪˈmoʊtɪvli/| adv.| 感情上地，情绪激动地|In a way that shows emotion.  She spoke emotively about her experiences.  她动情地讲述了她的经历。|近义词：emotionally, feelingly, passionately|反义词：apathetically, indifferently|词性:副词
Etymology:  From the adjective "emotive" + "-ly".  "Emotive" comes from "emotion" (from Old French) + "-ive". Ultimately from Latin "emovere" meaning "to move out, remove, agitate".
