hypersensibility:/ˌhaɪpərˌsɛnsɪˈbɪlɪti/ /ˌhaɪpərˌsɛnsəˈbɪləti/| n. |过敏症，过度敏感|Excessive sensitivity of the body to a particular substance or condition.|She has a hypersensitivity to certain foods. 她对某些食物过敏。|Near synonyms: allergy, intolerance, sensitivity|Near antonyms:hyposensitivity, insensitivity|noun

hypersensitive:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnsɪtɪv/ /ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnsətɪv/| adj. |过度敏感的|Excessively sensitive; easily offended or emotionally upset.|He's hypersensitive to criticism. 他对批评过度敏感。|Near synonyms: oversensitive, touchy, thin-skinned|Near antonyms:insensitive, thick-skinned|adjective
