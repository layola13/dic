iceman:/ˈaɪsmæn/| n.|冰人；冷酷无情的人；卖冰的人|A person who sells ice or works with ice. Also refers to a person who is unemotional or callous. |The iceman delivered a large block of ice to the restaurant.  冰人给餐馆送了一大块冰。

The CEO is known as an iceman because he never shows any emotion.  这位CEO被称为冰人，因为他从不显露任何情感。|近义词：glacier man, cold fish, unfeeling person|反义词: warmhearted person, sentimentalist|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From ice + man.  The term gained wider recognition after the discovery of Ötzi the Iceman, a well-preserved mummy from the Copper Age.
