misworshipping:/ˌmɪswɜːrʃɪpɪŋ/
verb
错误崇拜；不当地崇拜
The act of worshipping something incorrectly or inappropriately.
They were accused of misworshipping pagan gods. 他们被指控错误地崇拜异教神。
He felt he was misworshipping by praying to idols. 他觉得向偶像祈祷是错误的崇拜。
misguidedly worshipping, improperly worshipping
rightly worshipping, correctly worshipping
词性: 动词
