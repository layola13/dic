nonemployees:/ˌnɒnɪmˈplɔɪiːz/ (US) /ˌnɑːnɪmˈplɔɪiːz/
n.
非雇员；非员工
People who are not employed by a particular company or organization.
The company offered discounts to its employees and nonemployees alike.  这家公司为其员工和非员工都提供折扣。
Non-employees are not allowed in the staff-only area. 非员工不得进入仅限员工的区域。
synonyms: outsiders, non-staff, non-workers
antonyms: employees, staff, personnel, workforce
noun
