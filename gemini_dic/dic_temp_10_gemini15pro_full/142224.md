ornithoscelida:/ˌɔːrnɪθəˈsɛlɪdə/ | n. |鸟臀目| A group of dinosaurs characterized by a pelvic structure similar to that of birds, although birds themselves are now thought to have evolved from the saurischian dinosaurs.  This group includes diverse herbivorous dinosaurs such as Ornithopoda (duck-billed dinosaurs and Iguanodon), Thyreophora (stegosaurs and ankylosaurs), and Marginocephalia (ceratopsians and pachycephalosaurs).| Ornithoscelida was a proposed clade of dinosaurs. 鸟臀目是一个被提出的恐龙进化枝。|Synonym:  None (as the validity of the group is debated) | Antonym:  Saurischia (traditionally considered the other main dinosaur group)| noun
