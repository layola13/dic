arquebuse:/ˈɑːrkɪbʌs/ | n. | 火绳枪 | An early type of portable firearm, a forerunner of the musket. |  The arquebuse was a common weapon in the 16th century.  十六世纪火绳枪是常见的武器。| synonyms: firearm, gun, musket | antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific weapon.  Could consider "peace" or "diplomacy" as thematic opposites.) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *arquebuse*, from Dutch *haakbus*, literally "hook gun," from *haak* "hook" + *bus* "gun, cannon."  The "hook" refers to a hook used to support the weapon when firing.
