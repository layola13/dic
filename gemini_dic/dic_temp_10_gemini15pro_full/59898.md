eddy:/ˈɛdi/| n. v.|漩涡，涡流；(v.)起漩涡，旋转|A circular movement of water, counter to a main current, causing a small whirlpool.|The water eddied around the rocks. 水在岩石周围打旋。|近义词:whirlpool, swirl, vortex|反义词:current, stream|n.
eddy:/ˈɛdi/| v.|起漩涡，旋转|To move in an eddy; whirl.|The leaves eddied in the wind. 树叶在风中旋转。|近义词:swirl, whirl, spin|反义词:flow, straighten|v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *iða ("whirlpool"). Cognate with Icelandic iða ("whirlpool").
