dauphins:/ˈdɔːfɪnz/ /ˈdɑːfɪnz/| n.|海豚（复数）|Dolphins are highly intelligent marine mammals known for their playful behavior.  海豚是高度智能的海洋哺乳动物，以其爱玩耍的行为而闻名。|例句：The dauphins leaped and played in the waves. 海豚在海浪中跳跃玩耍。|近义词：porpoises, dolphins(单数)|反义词:|noun

词源:
From Old French *dalfin, from Medieval Latin *dalphinus, alteration of Latin delphinus, from Ancient Greek δελφίς (delphís, “dolphin”).
