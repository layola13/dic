endangered: /ɪnˈdeɪndʒərd/ | adj. | 濒危的 |  At risk of extinction.  Meaning a species is seriously at risk of extinction in the near future. |  The giant panda is an endangered species. 大熊猫是濒危物种。

The Amur leopard is one of the most endangered big cats in the world.  远东豹是世界上最濒危的大型猫科动物之一。| 近义词: threatened, imperiled, vulnerable | 反义词: safe, thriving, flourishing | 词性: 形容词

词源: From endanger + -ed.  Endanger comes from the Old French word "dangier" meaning "peril, power to harm."
