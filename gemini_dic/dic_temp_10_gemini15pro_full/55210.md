disservice:/dɪˈsɜːrvɪs/
n.|帮倒忙，损害，伤害|An act that harms or damages someone or something, especially when it was intended to help.|Claiming that we don't need more research into climate change is a real disservice to future generations. 声称我们不需要对气候变化进行更多研究，这对子孙后代来说是真正的损害。|近义词：harm, damage, injury, detriment, prejudice |反义词:service, help, benefit, advantage|词性:名词

disservice:|/dɪˈsɜːrvɪs/|
vt.|损害，伤害|To do something that harms or damages someone or something, especially when it was intended to help.|By not telling her the truth, you're actually doing her a disservice. 你不告诉她真相，实际上是在害她。|近义词：harm, damage, injure|反义词:serve, help, benefit|词性:动词
