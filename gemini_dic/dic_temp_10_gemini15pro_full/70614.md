filmlike:/ˈfɪlmˌlaɪk/| adj.|像电影的；电影般的|Resembling or characteristic of a film, especially in being dramatic or visually striking.|The sunset created a filmlike quality to the landscape.  日落赋予了风景一种电影般的质感。|The slow-motion fight scene had a very filmlike feel.  慢动作的打斗场面很有电影感。|cinematic, picturesque, dramatic, vivid|nondescript, dull, mundane|adjective
