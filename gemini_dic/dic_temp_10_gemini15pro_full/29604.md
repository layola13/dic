categoricalness:/ˌkætəˈɡɔːrɪkəlnəs/ | n. |绝对性，明确性|The quality of being absolute, explicit, and unambiguous.  It implies leaving no room for doubt or alternative interpretations.|The categoricalness of his denial left no room for doubt. 他的断然否认不容置疑。|近义词:certainty, definitiveness, explicitness, unambiguity |反义词:ambiguity, vagueness, uncertainty|noun
