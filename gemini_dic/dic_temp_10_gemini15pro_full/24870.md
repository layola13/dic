browser: /ˈbraʊzər/ (US) /ˈbraʊzə(r)/ (UK)| n. |浏览器；吃嫩叶的动物|A software application for retrieving, presenting, and traversing information resources on the World Wide Web.|I use Google Chrome as my browser. 我使用谷歌浏览器。|近义词：web browser, navigator|反义词: None| noun

browser: /ˈbraʊzər/ (US) /ˈbraʊzə(r)/ (UK)| n. |吃嫩叶的动物|An animal that feeds on leaves, soft shoots, or other high-growing vegetation.|The deer is a browser, feeding on the leaves of trees and shrubs. 鹿是一种吃嫩叶的动物，以树木和灌木的叶子为食。|近义词：herbivore, grazer|反义词: carnivore| noun
