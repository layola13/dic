graniform:/ˈɡrænɪfɔːrm/
adjective
颗粒状的，粒状的
Resembling or consisting of grains or granules; granular.
The rock has a graniform texture. 这块岩石具有颗粒状的结构。
近义词：granular, grainy
反义词：smooth, homogeneous
词性: adjective

graniform:/ˈɡrænɪfɔːrm/
词源：From Latin granum (“grain”) + -form.
中文翻译：颗粒状的，粒状的
英文解析：Having the form of grains or granules; granular.
例句：The substance exhibited a graniform appearance under the microscope.  这种物质在显微镜下呈现颗粒状外观。
The graniform texture of the soil improved drainage.  土壤的颗粒状结构改善了排水。

近义词：granular, grainy, particulate
反义词：smooth, homogeneous, uniform
词性: adjective
