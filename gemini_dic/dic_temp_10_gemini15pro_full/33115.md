christianized:/ˈkrɪstʃənaɪzd/ |v. |使基督教化；皈依基督教|To convert (someone or something) to Christianity.  To cause (a place or culture) to adopt Christianity.|The Roman Empire was christianized in the 4th century.  罗马帝国在4世纪被基督教化了。|The missionaries christianized many indigenous tribes.  传教士使许多土著部落皈依了基督教。|synonyms: evangelize, convert, proselytize|antonyms: paganize, secularize|verb

christianized:/ˈkrɪstʃənaɪzd/ |adj. |基督教化的|Having been converted to Christianity. Influenced by Christianity.|The christianized Roman Empire adopted new laws and customs. 基督教化的罗马帝国采用了新的法律和习俗。|The music had a distinctly christianized theme.  这首音乐有一个明显的基督教化主题。|synonyms: Christian|antonyms: pagan, secular|adjective
