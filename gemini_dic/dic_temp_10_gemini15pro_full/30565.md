cerargyrite:/səˈrɑːrdʒɪˌraɪt/
n.
角银矿
A mineral, silver chloride (AgCl), that is an important ore of silver. It is usually grayish-green, and darkens on exposure to light.
Cerargyrite is a valuable silver ore found in arid regions. 角银矿是一种在干旱地区发现的珍贵的银矿石。
Cerargyrite specimens can be damaged by prolonged exposure to bright light. 角银矿标本若长时间暴露在强光下会受损。
Synonyms: horn silver, chlorargyrite
Antonyms: None (because it's a specific mineral, it doesn't have a direct opposite)
