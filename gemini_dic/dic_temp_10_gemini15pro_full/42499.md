counterinflation:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈfleɪʃən/ | n. | 反通货膨胀；对抗通货膨胀的措施 |  Policies or actions implemented to reduce or counteract inflation. | The government introduced a series of counterinflation measures to stabilize the economy. 政府采取了一系列反通货膨胀措施以稳定经济。 | The central bank raised interest rates as a counterinflationary measure. 中央银行提高利率作为一项反通货膨胀的措施。 | synonyms: deflationary, anti-inflationary | antonyms: inflationary | noun

counterinflationary:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈfleɪʃənəri/ | adj. | 反通货膨胀的；抑制通货膨胀的 | Designed to counteract inflation. |  The government implemented counterinflationary policies to combat rising prices. 政府实施了反通货膨胀政策以对抗物价上涨。 | Counterinflationary measures often involve raising interest rates and reducing government spending. 反通货膨胀措施通常包括提高利率和减少政府开支。 | synonyms: deflationary, anti-inflationary | antonyms: inflationary | adjective

Etymology:
"counter-" (prefix of Latin origin meaning "against, opposite") + "inflation" (from Latin "inflare" - to blow into, inflate)
