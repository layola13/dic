departeds: /dɪˈpɑːrtɪdz/ | n. | 死亡者，逝者 |  This word is a non-standard plural form of "departed," which itself is used as a noun meaning "a dead person." While "the departed" is commonly used to refer to a single deceased person or collectively to deceased people, "departeds" is generally considered grammatically incorrect.  |  While grammatically questionable, one might encounter a sentence like: "We remember the departeds fondly." meaning "We remember those who have passed away fondly." However, using "the departed" or "those who have passed away" is preferred. | 近义词: the deceased, the dead | 反义词: the living | 词性: 名词
