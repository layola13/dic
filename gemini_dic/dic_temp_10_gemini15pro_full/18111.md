behemoth:/bɪˈhiːməθ/ /biˈhiːmɑːθ/
n.
巨兽，庞然大物
Something enormous, especially a large animal or powerful organization.
例句：The factory is a real behemoth, employing thousands of workers.  这家工厂是一个真正的庞然大物，雇佣了数千名工人。

例句：The new aircraft carrier is a behemoth of the seas. 这艘新的航空母舰是海上巨兽。

近义词：monster, giant, leviathan, colossus
反义词：dwarf, pygmy
词性：名词
