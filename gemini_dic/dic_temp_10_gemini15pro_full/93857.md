immobilizer:/ɪˈmoʊbəlaɪzər/ | n. | 防盗器，固定器 |A device that prevents something from moving or operating, often used in vehicles as an anti-theft measure. | The car's immobilizer prevented the thief from starting the engine. 汽车的防盗器阻止了小偷发动引擎。 | synonyms: lock, brake, restraint | antonyms: activator, starter, enabler | noun

immobilizers:/ɪˈmoʊbəlaɪzərz/ | n. | 防盗器（复数）| Plural form of "immobilizer," referring to multiple devices that prevent movement or operation. |  Modern cars are equipped with electronic immobilizers. 现代汽车配备了电子防盗器。 | synonyms: locks, brakes, restraints | antonyms: activators, starters, enablers | noun
