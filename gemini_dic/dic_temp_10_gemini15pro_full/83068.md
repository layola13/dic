gurner:/ˈɡɜːrnər/| n.|扮鬼脸的人；做怪相的人|A person who pulls faces, often to make others laugh or be repulsed.|He's such a gurner, always making silly faces. 他真是个爱扮鬼脸的人，总是做些滑稽的表情。|近义词：grimace maker, face-puller|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'stoic' in terms of facial expression) |noun
