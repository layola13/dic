impracticableness:/ɪmˌpræktɪkəbəlˈnɛs/| n.|不可行性，不切实际|The impracticableness of the plan was evident from the start.  这个计划从一开始就显而易见是不可行的。|The impracticableness of reaching the summit in such bad weather forced them to turn back.  由于天气恶劣，到达山顶是不切实际的，他们被迫返回。|近义词：infeasibility, impossibility, unworkability|反义词：feasibility, practicality, possibility|词性: noun

impracticable:/ɪmˈpræktɪkəbl/| adj.|不可行的，不切实际的|It is impracticable to finish the project within a week.  在一周内完成这个项目是不切实际的。|Building a bridge across the ocean is impracticable with current technology.  用现在的技术建造一座跨海大桥是不可行的。|近义词：infeasible, impossible, unworkable|反义词：feasible, practical, possible|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from  in- ‘not’ + practicable + -ness.
