nonebulliently:/nɒnɪˈbʌliəntli/ | adv. | 不热情地，不兴高采烈地 |  In a way that lacks enthusiasm or exuberance.  Derived from "non-" (not) + "ebullient" (overflowing with enthusiasm) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). | She accepted the award nonebulliently, almost as if she didn't care. 她不热情地接受了奖项，几乎就像她不在乎一样。|  apathetically, listlessly, unenthusiastically | enthusiastically, excitedly, ebulliently | adverb
