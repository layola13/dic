melanorrhoea: /ˌmɛlənəˈriːə/ | n. |黑漆树属;黑漆树脂|A genus of trees of the cashew family (Anacardiaceae) native to Southeast Asia and Australia, producing a black resin used as a varnish.|The melanorrhoea tree is native to Southeast Asia. 黑漆树原产于东南亚。| The sap of the melanorrhoea tree yields a valuable black varnish. 黑漆树的树液产生一种珍贵的黑色清漆。|Synonyms: Gluta, Swintonia|Antonyms: N/A| Noun
