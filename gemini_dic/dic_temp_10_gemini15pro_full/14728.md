awayness:/əˈweɪnəs/| n.|远离; 离开; 不在场; 心不在焉|The state of being away or distant, either physically or mentally.  A sense of detachment or disconnection from one's surroundings or thoughts.|He felt a strange awayness from the situation, as if it were happening to someone else. 他对这种情况感到一种奇怪的疏离感，就好像发生在别人身上一样。|近义词：absence, detachment, remoteness|反义词：presence, closeness, attention|词性:名词
