literalist: /ˈlɪtərəlɪst/ | n. | 字面主义者；拘泥字义的人 | Someone who adheres strictly to the literal meaning of words or texts, often without considering context or metaphorical interpretations. |  The literalist interpretation of the poem missed its deeper meaning.  字面意义上解释这首诗，错过了它更深层的含义。

The lawyer, a strict literalist, pointed out the ambiguity in the contract.  这位律师是一位严格的字面主义者，他指出了合同中的歧义。 |  fundamentalist, literal-minded |  figurative, metaphorical | noun
