mythe:/miːθ/| n.|神话，虚构的故事|A traditional story, especially one concerning the early history of a people or explaining some natural or social phenomenon, and typically involving supernatural beings or events.|例句：The myth of Icarus is a cautionary tale about the dangers of hubris. 伊卡洛斯的神话是一个关于傲慢危险的警示故事。

例句：Many myths feature gods and goddesses with incredible powers. 许多神话都以拥有不可思议力量的神和女神为主角。|近义词：legend, fable, story, tale|反义词：reality, truth, fact|词性:noun
