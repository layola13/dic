haywire:/ˈheɪwaɪər/| adj.|混乱的，失控的|Out of control; in a state of disorder or malfunction.|The computer system went haywire and deleted all the files. 电脑系统失控，删除了所有文件。|The plan went haywire when the car broke down. 当汽车抛锚时，计划就乱了套。|Synonyms: chaotic, disordered, erratic, malfunctioning, amuck, awry|Antonyms: orderly, controlled, functioning, organized|adjective

Etymology->
From haywire, n., "wire used for binding bales of hay," from hay + wire.  The sense of "out of control" likely arose from the tendency of this type of wire to become tangled and difficult to manage.
