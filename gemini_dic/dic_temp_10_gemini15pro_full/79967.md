go-back-on:/ɡoʊ bæk ɑːn/ | phrasal verb | 背弃，违背，食言 | To fail to keep a promise or agreement; to renege on. | He went back on his promise to help me move.  他违背了帮我搬家的承诺。| 近义词：renege on, break, violate | 反义词：keep, honor, fulfill | 词性: 动词短语

词源:  "Go back" signifies returning or retreating, and "on" in this context implies a commitment or agreement. The combination suggests retreating from a commitment.
