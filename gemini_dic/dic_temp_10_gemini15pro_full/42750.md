countries: /ˈkʌntriz/ | n. | 国家（复数） | Nations; the lands of different nations. |  Many countries participated in the Olympic Games. 许多国家参加了奥运会。| nations, states, territories | - | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *cuntrée* (Modern French *contrée*), from Vulgar Latin *(terra) contrata* (“(land) lying opposite; region, district”), from Latin *contra* (“against, opposite”) + *terra* (“land, earth”).
