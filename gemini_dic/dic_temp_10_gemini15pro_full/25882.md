burlesquely:/bərˈlɛskli/
adverb
滑稽地；荒诞地
in a way that makes something serious seem silly
He burlesquely imitated the prime minister's speech. 他滑稽地模仿了首相的演讲。
近义词：comically, ludicrously, farcically
反义词：seriously, solemnly, respectfully
词性：副词

词源：
From burlesque + -ly.  Burlesque comes from Italian burlesco "comic, ludicrous," from burla "joke, mockery."
