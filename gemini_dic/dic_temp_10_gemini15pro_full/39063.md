conformed:/kənˈfɔːrmd/ | adj. | 符合的，一致的，遵从的 | Adapted to prevailing standards or norms; behaving in accordance with socially accepted conventions or rules. |  He conformed to the dress code of the office. 他遵守了办公室的着装规定。| Synonyms: compliant, obedient, acquiescent | Antonyms: rebellious, nonconformist, individualistic | adjective

conformed:/kənˈfɔːrmd/ | v. | 遵从，顺应，符合 |  Acted in accordance with or complied with rules, standards, or laws.  | She conformed to the wishes of her parents. 她遵从了父母的意愿。 | Synonyms: complied, obeyed, adhered | Antonyms: rebelled, defied, disobeyed | verb
