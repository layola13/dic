deistic:/diːˈɪstɪk/
adj.
自然神论的
Pertaining to or characteristic of deism.
Thomas Paine's writings are considered deistic. 托马斯·潘恩的著作被认为是自然神论的。

He held a deistic view of the universe. 他持有宇宙的自然神论观点。

近义词:theistic, religious
反义词:atheistic, agnostic
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From French *déiste* (now *déistique*), from Latin *deus* ("god") + *-isticus* (from Greek *-istikos*).
