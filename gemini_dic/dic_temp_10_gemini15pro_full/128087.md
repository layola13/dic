nervure:/ˈnɜːrvjʊər/| n.|叶脉；翅脉；肋 |A vein or rib, especially of a leaf or insect wing.|The delicate nervures of a butterfly's wing shimmered in the sunlight. 蝴蝶翅膀上纤细的翅脉在阳光下闪闪发光。|Synonym: vein, rib|Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider "membrane" in certain contexts)|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French nervure, from Latin nervus ("sinew, tendon, nerve").
