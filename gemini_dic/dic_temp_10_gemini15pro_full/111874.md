It often involves excessive use of long and obscure words.|His speech was filled with logodaedaly, obscuring his actual message. 他的演讲充满了冗长空洞的言辞，掩盖了他真正想表达的信息。|Synonyms:verbosity, prolixity, circumlocution, periphrasis|Antonyms:conciseness, brevity, succinctness|noun
