interminability:/ɪnˌtɜːrmɪnəˈbɪləti/ |n.| 无休止，无限期 |The interminability of the lecture made me drowsy. 这场没完没了的讲座让我昏昏欲睡。|The seemingly interminability of the road trip frustrated the children.  看似永无止境的公路旅行让孩子们沮丧不已。|近义词：endlessness, perpetuity, infinity|反义词：brevity, finiteness, transience|词性: noun

interminable:/ɪnˈtɜːrmɪnəbl/ |adj.| 无休止的，无限期的 |The movie felt interminable.  这部电影感觉没完没了。|Her interminable complaints wore everyone down. 她无休止的抱怨让每个人都筋疲力尽。|近义词：endless, incessant, ceaseless|反义词：brief, short, finite|词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *interminabilis, from in- "not" + terminabilis "that can be limited," from terminare "to limit," from terminus "boundary, limit."
