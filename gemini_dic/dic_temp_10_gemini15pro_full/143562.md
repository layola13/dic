outhiring:/ˈaʊtˌhaɪərɪŋ/
n.
外包; 外聘
The practice of hiring external staff or companies to perform tasks, handle operations, or provide services within a company.
Many companies are turning to outhiring to reduce costs. 许多公司正转向外包以降低成本。
The company decided on outhiring its IT operations. 该公司决定外包其IT运营。
