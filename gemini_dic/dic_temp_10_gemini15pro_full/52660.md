digitised:/ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪzd/| adj.|数字化的|Converted from analog to digital form.  Representing data in a digital form, especially for use by a computer.|例句：The library has digitised all its old manuscripts.  图书馆已将其所有旧手稿数字化。

例句：Many companies are now digitising their records. 许多公司现在正在将其记录数字化。|近义词：digital, computerized, electronic|反义词：analog, non-digital|词性:adjective
digitize:/ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪz/| vt.|数字化|Convert (information or an image) into digital form.|例句：The company digitizes old films and photographs. 这家公司将旧电影和照片数字化。

例句：They are digitizing all the land registry information. 他们正在将所有土地登记信息数字化。|近义词：convert, encode, scan|反义词：analogize|词性:verb
