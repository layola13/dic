froward:/ˈfroʊərd/ (US), /ˈfrəʊwəd/ (UK)| adj.|任性的；固执的；难驾驭的|Inclined to disobey; stubbornly willful; difficult to manage or control.  Characterized by a perverse or capricious obstinacy.|The froward child refused to eat his vegetables.  这个任性的孩子拒绝吃蔬菜。|Synonyms: wayward, perverse, obstinate, refractory, headstrong, unruly, disobedient|Antonyms: obedient, compliant, docile, amenable, tractable|Adjective
