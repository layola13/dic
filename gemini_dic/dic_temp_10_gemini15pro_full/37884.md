complained:/kəmˈpleɪnd/ | v. | 抱怨，诉苦，投诉 | express dissatisfaction or annoyance about something. |  I complained to the manager about the poor service. 我向经理投诉了糟糕的服务。

She complained of a headache. 她抱怨头疼。| 近义词：grumble, moan, protest, object | 反义词：praise, commend | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French complaindre, from Latin plangere "to lament."
