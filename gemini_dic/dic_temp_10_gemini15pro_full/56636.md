donkeys:/ˈdɒŋkiz/ (UK), /ˈdɑːŋkiz/ (US)| n.|驴子|Plural form of "donkey," referring to more than one donkey.  A donkey is a domesticated member of the horse family, characterized by long ears, a short mane, and a reputation for stubbornness.|The farmer used two donkeys to pull the cart.|近义词：asses, mules, burros|反义词: None (no direct opposite for an animal)|noun
