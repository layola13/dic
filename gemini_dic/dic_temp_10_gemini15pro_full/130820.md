nonappealability:/ˌnɒnəˌpiːləˈbɪləti/
n.
不可上诉性
The quality or state of not being subject to appeal.
The nonappealability of the judge's decision led to widespread frustration. 法官的裁决不可上诉，这导致了普遍的沮丧。
The contract stipulated the nonappealability of any arbitration decisions.  合同规定任何仲裁决定都不可上诉。
