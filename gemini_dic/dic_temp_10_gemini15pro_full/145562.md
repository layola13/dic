overcomplacence:/ˌoʊvər.kəmˈpleɪ.səns/ | n. | 过于自满，沾沾自喜 | A state of smug satisfaction with oneself or one's achievements, often to the point of failing to recognize danger or room for improvement. |  The team's overcomplacence after their initial victory led to their downfall in the final.  该队在最初的胜利后过于自满，导致他们在决赛中失败。|  smugness, self-satisfaction, complacency |  anxiety, vigilance, concern | noun


overcomplacent:/ˌoʊvər.kəmˈpleɪ.sənt/ | adj. | 过于自满的，沾沾自喜的 | Excessively self-satisfied; complacent to an excessive degree. |  He became overcomplacent about his skills and stopped practicing. 他对自己的技能变得过于自满，停止了练习。|  smug, self-satisfied, complacent |  anxious, vigilant, concerned | adjective
