inconcluding:/ˌɪnkənˈkluːdɪŋ/ | adj. | 未得出结论的，无结果的 | Not leading to a definite conclusion or result; inconclusive. | The evidence was inconclusive, so the jury couldn't reach a verdict. 证据不足以定论，所以陪审团无法作出裁决。|  The meeting ended inconclusively, with no decisions made. 会议无果而终，没有做出任何决定。 | synonyms: indecisive, indeterminate, open-ended, unresolved | antonyms: conclusive, decisive, definite, definitive | word type: adjective
