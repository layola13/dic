inter-ethnic:/ˌɪntərˈɛθnɪk/ | adj. | 种族间的，跨种族的 | Existing or occurring between different ethnic groups. |  例句：Inter-ethnic marriages are becoming more common in many countries.  许多国家的跨种族婚姻变得越来越普遍。

例句：The inter-ethnic tensions in the region have led to violence. 该地区的种族间紧张关系导致了暴力。

近义词：cross-cultural, multiethnic, interracial | 反义词：intra-ethnic | 词性：形容词
