fowling:/ˈfaʊlɪŋ/
n.
捕鸟; 猎鸟;
The act of catching or killing wild birds for food or sport.
He enjoyed fowling and fishing in his spare time.  他空闲时喜欢捕鸟和钓鱼。
近义词:birding, hunting
反义词:conservation


fowlings:/ˈfaʊlɪŋz/ (This word is likely a plural form of "fowling")
n.
捕鸟活动; 猎鸟活动;（多次捕鸟行为）
Instances or occasions of catching birds.
His fowlings often yielded a good supply of meat for the family. 他的几次捕鸟活动为家人提供了充足的肉食。
近义词: hunts, expeditions
反义词:protections
