conciliable:/kənˈsɪliəbl/
adjective
可调和的;可安抚的
capable of being conciliated; placable.
The dispute proved to be conciliable, and both parties reached an agreement.  这场纠纷最终得以调和，双方达成了协议。
Her conciliable nature made her a successful mediator. 她和蔼可亲的性格使她成为一名成功的调解员。
近义词:appeasable, pacifiable, reconcilable, mediatable
反义词:intransigent, unyielding, implacable, irreconcilable
词性:adjective
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin conciliabilis, from conciliare (see conciliate).
