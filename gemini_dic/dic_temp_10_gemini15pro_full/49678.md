demisters:/diˈmɪstərz/
n.
除雾器，去雾器
A device or substance that removes mist or fog from the air or other gases.
The demisters in the car windscreen helped improve visibility in the foggy weather. 汽车挡风玻璃上的除雾器帮助改善了雾天的能见度。
Synonyms: dehumidifiers, defoggers
Antonyms: humidifiers
