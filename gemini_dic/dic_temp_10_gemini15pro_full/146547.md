overgloomy:/ˌoʊvərˈɡluːmi/
adjective
过于忧郁的，极度沮丧的
Excessively gloomy; extremely depressed.
The overgloomy weather reflected his mood.  这过于阴沉的天气反映了他的心情。
He had an overgloomy outlook on life. 他对人生抱有极其悲观的态度。
近义词:depressed, despondent, melancholy, pessimistic
反义词:cheerful, optimistic, happy, joyful
词性:adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "gloomy" (adjective meaning "dark or poorly lit, especially so as to appear depressing or frightening; dismal.")  "over-"（前缀，意思是“过度地”）+ "gloomy"（形容词，意思是“黑暗的或光线不足的，尤指显得压抑或可怕的；阴沉的。”）
