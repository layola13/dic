dowdiest:/ˈdaʊdiɪst/| adj.|最寒酸的，最土气的|Superlative adjective of dowdy: most dowdy.  It describes someone or something that is unfashionable, shabby, and unattractive in appearance.|例句：She wore the dowdiest dress to the party. 她穿着最土气的裙子去参加了派对。|近义词：frumpy, shabby, drab, unfashionable|反义词：stylish, fashionable, chic, elegant|词性:形容词
Etymology: From dowdy + -est (superlative suffix).  Dowdy itself likely originated in the late 19th century, possibly as a derogatory term for a poorly dressed woman, perhaps related to "dowd," an older term for a dull, awkward woman.
