alkaloid:/ˈælkəlɔɪd/| n. |生物碱|Any of a class of naturally occurring organic nitrogen-containing bases. Many alkaloids have pharmacological effects and are used as medications or recreational drugs.|Morphine and codeine are alkaloids found in the opium poppy. 吗啡和可待因是在罂粟中发现的生物碱。|Synonyms: nitrogenous base, organic base|Antonyms: acid|Noun
