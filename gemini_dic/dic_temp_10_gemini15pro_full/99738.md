intermarrying:/ˌɪntərˈmærɪɪŋ/ | v. |通婚，异族结婚 | The practice of members of different social or ethnic groups marrying each other. |  Example 1: Intermarrying between different religious groups was common in that region. 该地区不同宗教群体之间的通婚很常见。

Example 2: The royal families of Europe often used intermarrying to forge political alliances. 欧洲王室经常利用通婚来建立政治联盟。 |  Synonyms: intermarriage, cross-cultural marriage | Antonyms: endogamy | verb (gerund or present participle)
