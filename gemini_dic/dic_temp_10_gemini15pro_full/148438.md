overstimulation:/ˌoʊvərˌstɪmjʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. |过度刺激|The act of stimulating something excessively, resulting in a negative effect such as agitation, exhaustion, or sensory overload.| The constant noise and bright lights led to overstimulation and a meltdown. 不断的噪音和强光导致过度刺激和崩溃。|Near synonyms: bombardment, inundation, saturation |Antonyms: understimulation, deprivation |noun


overstimulate:/ˌoʊvərˈstɪmjʊleɪt/ | v. |过度刺激|To stimulate something excessively, leading to a negative outcome.|  The flashing lights and loud music overstimulated the baby. 闪烁的灯光和嘈杂的音乐过度刺激了婴儿。|Near synonyms: overwhelm, overload, bombard |Antonyms: understimulate, deprive |verb
