cromorna:/krəˈmɔːrnə/ | n. | 克鲁姆号，一种文艺复兴时期的木管乐器 | A Renaissance woodwind instrument with a capped double reed, producing a loud, somewhat raucous tone.  It belongs to the shawm family. | The cromorna's distinctive timbre can be heard in some early music ensembles. 克鲁姆号独特的音色可以在一些早期音乐合奏中听到。| The musician skillfully played a haunting melody on the cromorna.  这位音乐家熟练地在克鲁姆号上演奏了一曲令人难忘的旋律。| 近义词: shawm, dulcian, rackett | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps a contrasting instrument like a flute or recorder could be considered) | 词性: noun
