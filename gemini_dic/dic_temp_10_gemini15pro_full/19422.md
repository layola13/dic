bickerers:/ˈbɪkərərz/
noun
爭吵者，愛拌嘴的人
People who argue or quarrel about petty and trivial matters.
The bickerers next door kept me awake all night. 隔壁那些愛爭吵的人讓我整夜沒睡。
近义词:arguers, disputants, squabblers
反义词: mediators, peacemakers
词性:noun (plural)

bicker:/ˈbɪkər/
verb
爭吵，拌嘴
To argue or quarrel about petty and trivial matters.
They bickered constantly over who should do the dishes. 他們經常為了誰該洗碗而爭吵。
近义词:argue, quarrel, squabble
反义词: agree, concur, harmonize
词性:verb
