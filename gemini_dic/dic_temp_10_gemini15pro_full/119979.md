millerite:/ˈmɪləˌraɪt/
n.
镍黄铁矿
a pale brass-yellow mineral consisting of nickel sulphide (NiS). It is a source of nickel.
Millerite is a common nickel sulfide mineral.  镍黄铁矿是一种常见的硫化镍矿物。
Crystals of millerite are typically long, thin needles.  镍黄铁矿晶体通常是细长的针状。
近义词:nickel sulfide
反义词: None (No direct antonym for a mineral name)
词性:noun
