moulders:/ˈmoʊldərz/ (US), /ˈməʊldərz/ (UK)| n.| 模具工，铸工；塑造者|A moulder is a person who makes moulds, especially for casting metal or other materials, or someone who shapes or influences something.|The moulders carefully poured the molten metal into the sand moulds. 铸工小心地将熔化的金属倒入砂模中。|近义词: shapers, formers, casters|反义词: destroyers, deformers|noun


moulder:/ˈmoʊldər/ (US), /ˈməʊldər/ (UK)| v.|  腐烂，霉烂；崩塌，瓦解；塑造，影响|To decay or crumble; to shape or influence something.|The old books were left to moulder in the damp basement.  那些旧书被放在潮湿的地下室里腐烂。
The artist moulded the clay into a beautiful sculpture. 艺术家用黏土塑造了一尊美丽的雕塑。|近义词: decay, rot, crumble, shape, influence|反义词: grow, flourish, strengthen, preserve|verb
