mpegs:/ˈempeɡz/
noun
MPEGs (Moving Picture Experts Group files)
Digital video files that use MPEG compression standards.  These are commonly used for storing movies and other video content.
Example: I downloaded some MPEGs of my favorite TV shows. 我下载了一些我最喜欢的电视节目的MPEG文件。
Synonym: video files, movie files
Antonym: (No direct antonym for a file format)
词性: noun
