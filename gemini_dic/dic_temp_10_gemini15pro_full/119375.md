microcoulomb:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌkuːlɒm/ |n.| 微库仑 |A unit of electric charge equal to one millionth of a coulomb.  It is a measure of the quantity of electricity.| The capacitor stores a charge of 10 microcoulombs. 电容器储存了10微库仑的电荷。 |Synonyms: μC (symbol) | Antonyms: N/A |noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From micro- (small) + coulomb (unit of electric charge named after Charles-Augustin de Coulomb).
