nonpresentability:/ˌnɒnˌprɛzənˈtəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可展示性;  无法呈现性
The state or quality of being unsuitable or impossible to present, display, or exhibit.
The nonpresentability of the evidence led to the case being dismissed.  证据无法出示导致案件被驳回。

The artist struggled with the nonpresentability of abstract emotions.  这位艺术家难以表现抽象的情感。

近义词: undisplayability, unexhibitability, unshowability
反义词: presentability, displayability

词性: noun
