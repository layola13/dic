leverage: /ˈliːvərɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈliːvərɪdʒ/ (UK)| vt.| (用杠杆)撬动；充分利用 |We can leverage our expertise to expand into new markets. 我们可以利用我们的专业知识拓展新市场。| synonyms: utilize, exploit, capitalize on | antonyms: waste, neglect | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *levierage, from lever "to raise," from Latin levāre "to lift, lighten."
