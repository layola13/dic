acculturate: /əˈkʌltʃəreɪt/
verb
使...适应文化，使...同化
To become familiar with and adapt to a different culture.
Many immigrants struggle to acculturate to their new surroundings. 许多移民努力适应新的环境。

International students often experience a period of acculturation when they move to a new country. 国际学生搬到新国家后通常会经历一段文化适应期。


近义词: assimilate, adapt, adjust, integrate
反义词: isolate, alienate
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *acculturatus, past participle of acculturare ("to cultivate, refine"), from Latin ad- ("to, toward") + cultura ("culture, cultivation").  Thus, literally "to cultivate towards".
