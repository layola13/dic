antifeministic:/ˌæntiˈfɛmɪnɪstɪk/
adj.
反对女权主义的
Opposed to feminism; discriminatory against women based on the belief that men are superior.
She made some antifeministic remarks that offended many people at the party. 她在聚会上发表了一些反对女权主义的言论，冒犯了很多人。
His antifeministic views are outdated and harmful. 他的反女权主义观点已经过时且有害。
近义词：misogynistic, sexist
反义词：feminist, pro-feminist
词性：adjective

antifeminist:/ˌæntiˈfɛmɪnɪst/
n.
反对女权主义者
A person who opposes feminism.
He's a known antifeminist and often speaks out against women's rights. 他是一个著名的反女权主义者，经常公开反对妇女权利。
The antifeminists protested the women's march. 反女权主义者抗议妇女游行。
近义词：misogynist, sexist
反义词：feminist, pro-feminist
词性：noun

词源: anti- (against) + feminist.  "Anti-" is a Greek prefix meaning "against," "opposite of," "instead of," or "equivalent to." "Feminist" comes from the French word "féministe," ultimately derived from the Latin "femina" meaning "woman."
