brunt:/brʌnt/
n. |主要冲击； brunt of sth. 承受某事的主要冲击|The infantry bore the brunt of the attack. 步兵承受了攻击的主要冲击。|
例句：
The small towns bore the brunt of the storm.
这些小镇首当其冲地遭受了暴风雨的袭击。

The soldiers bore the brunt of the enemy attack.
士兵们承受了敌军攻击的 brunt。

近义词：impact, force, shock, burden, strain
反义词：aftermath, repercussion (These are not direct opposites, but relate to the consequences rather than the initial impact)
词性：名词

词源：From the Old French "bront," meaning "shock, blow, collision."  Ultimately from a Germanic source, related to the Old English "brinnan" meaning "to burn," suggesting the image of a searing impact.
