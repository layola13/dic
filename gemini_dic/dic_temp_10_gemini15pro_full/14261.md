autofocusing:/ˌɔːtəʊˈfəʊkəsɪŋ/ | adj. | 自动对焦的 | Automatically adjusting the focus of a lens to provide a sharp image. |  This camera has autofocusing capabilities, making it easy to take clear pictures. 这款相机具有自动对焦功能，可以轻松拍摄清晰的照片。|Synonyms: self-focusing, automatic focusing | Antonyms: manual focusing | adjective

autofocus:/ˌɔːtəʊˈfəʊkəs/ | n. v. | 自动对焦 | (n.) The function of a lens that automatically adjusts the focus. (v.) To automatically adjust the focus of a lens. | My camera's autofocus is very fast. 我的相机的自动对焦速度非常快。 The camera will autofocus on the subject's face. 相机会自动对焦在主体的脸上。| Synonyms: self-focus, automatic focus | Antonyms: manual focus | noun, verb
