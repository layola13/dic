leggier:/ˈleɡiər/| adj.|更苗条的，更修长的|Having longer or more slender legs than average or than before; more graceful or agile.|Her new exercise routine made her legs leggier.|近义词：slimmer, thinner, more slender|反义词：stockier, thicker, heavier|词性:形容词

词源: From leg + -ier (comparative suffix).  The suffix "-ier"  is used to form the comparative degree of adjectives, indicating a greater degree of the quality expressed by the adjective's base form.  In this case, "leggier" signifies "more leg-like",  implying longer, thinner, and more shapely legs.
