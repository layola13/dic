arcadians:/ɑːrˈkeɪdiənz/| n.|阿卡迪亚人；田园诗人；单纯的人|People who live a simple life in the countryside, often associated with idyllic happiness and innocence, sometimes seen as unsophisticated.|The poet portrayed the arcadians as living in perfect harmony with nature.|近义词：rustic, pastoral, idyllic, bucolic|反义词:urban, sophisticated, worldly|noun

arcadian:/ɑːrˈkeɪdiən/| adj.|阿卡迪亚的；田园诗的；单纯的|Relating to Arcadia, a region in Greece, traditionally associated with pastoral simplicity and happiness; idyllically rustic; simple and unsophisticated.|The painting depicted an arcadian scene of shepherds and nymphs.|近义词：rustic, pastoral, idyllic, bucolic|反义词:urban, sophisticated, worldly|adjective
