needier:/ˈniːdiər/| adj.|更贫困的，更需要的|More needy; requiring more attention, support, or reassurance.|例句：The charity focuses on helping the needier members of society.  这个慈善机构致力于帮助社会上更贫困的成员。|例句：She became needier after her husband passed away. 她丈夫去世后，她变得更加需要人照顾。|近义词：poorer, deprived, dependent, clingy|反义词:wealthier, richer, independent, self-sufficient|词性:形容词

词源: From "needy" + "-er" (comparative suffix). "Needy" comes from Old English *nēodig, from *nēod "need."
