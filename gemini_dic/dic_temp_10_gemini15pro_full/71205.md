fistulous:/ˈfɪstʃʊləs/ | adj. | 瘘管的；似瘘管的 | characterized by, containing, or resembling a fistula (an abnormal passage or tube-like opening between two body cavities or a cavity and the surface). | The patient presented with a fistulous tract connecting the bowel to the skin.  病人表现出肠道与皮肤相连的瘘管。| The surgeon identified a fistulous connection between the bladder and the vagina.  外科医生发现了膀胱和阴道之间的一个瘘管连接。 | synonymous:  fistular | antonymous:  normal, healthy | adjective
