outbribing: /aʊtˈbraɪbɪŋ/
verb
贿赂胜过；行贿超过
The act of bribing someone more than another person or entity.
He won the contract by outbribing his competitors. 他通过比竞争对手行贿更多而赢得了合同。
The company was accused of outbribing other firms to secure lucrative government contracts.  该公司被指控为了获得利润丰厚的政府合同而行贿其他公司。
近义词: outmaneuvering, outbidding
反义词:  undercutting
