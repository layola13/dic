chipping-in:/ˈtʃɪpɪŋ ɪn/ | n. |凑钱;分担费用;插嘴;贡献 |The act of contributing a small amount of money or effort to a shared cost or task.  | Example: Everyone chipped in for the birthday cake.  大家一起凑钱买了生日蛋糕。

Example:  They're chipping in to buy their teacher a present. 他们凑钱给老师买礼物。| Synonym: contribute, donate, pitch in, share the cost | Antonym: withhold, refuse |noun

chipping-in:/ˈtʃɪpɪŋ ɪn/ | v. |凑钱;分担费用;插嘴;贡献| The act of contributing a small amount of money or effort to a shared cost or task. | Example:  We all chipped in and bought her a gift. 我们都凑了点钱给她买了个礼物。

Example: He chipped in with a suggestion. 他插嘴提了个建议。 | Synonym: contribute, donate, pitch in, interject | Antonym: withhold, refuse |verb
