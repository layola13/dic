例句：The heliolater built an altar on the highest hill to be closer to the sun.  这位太阳崇拜者在最高的山上建了一座祭坛，以便更接近太阳。|近义词：sun worshipper|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly someone who fears or avoids the sun)|词性:noun
