caproate:/ˈkæproʊeɪt/| n.|己酸盐；羊酸盐；己酸酯|A salt or ester of caproic acid (also known as hexanoic acid).|Caproate esters are often used in the food industry for their fruity aromas. 己酸酯因其水果香味常用于食品工业。|
The medication is administered as a sodium caproate injection. 这种药物以己酸钠注射剂的形式给药。|synonyms: hexanoate|antonyms: N/A|noun
