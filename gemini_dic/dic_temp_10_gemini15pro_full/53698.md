disclaimer:/dɪsˈkleɪmər/ |n.| 免责声明；放弃权利声明|A statement that denies something, especially responsibility.|The website included a disclaimer about the accuracy of the information presented.  该网站包含一个关于所提供信息准确性的免责声明。|Synonym: waiver, denial, repudiation|Antonym:  acceptance, acknowledgment, affirmation |noun

disclaim:/dɪsˈkleɪm/ |v.| 否认；放弃权利；拒绝承认|To deny any responsibility for or connection with.|He disclaimed any knowledge of the missing funds. 他否认对丢失的资金有任何了解。|Synonym: renounce, reject, deny|Antonym: claim, accept, admit|verb
