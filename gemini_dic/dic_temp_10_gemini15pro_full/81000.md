grades:/ɡreɪdz/| n.| 成绩，等级，级别| a letter or number that shows the quality of a student's work, or the level of something |  She got good grades in all her subjects. 她所有科目都取得了优异的成绩。| synonyms: marks, scores, ratings | antonyms: failures | noun

grades:/ɡreɪdz/| v. (grades, grading, graded)| 评分，分级，评估 | to give a grade to a student's work, or to separate things into different levels of quality | The teacher graded the papers over the weekend. 老师周末给论文评分。| synonyms: assess, evaluate, rank | antonyms:  -- | verb
