coupling:/ˈkʌplɪŋ/| n.|耦合，联轴器，连接，结合|The act of joining or connecting two or more things together. Also refers to the connection itself.|The coupling between the two train cars broke, causing a delay.|synonyms: connection, link, union, joint, junction|antonyms: separation, disconnection, decoupling|noun

coupling:/ˈkʌplɪŋ/| n.|耦合，配对|The pairing of two things or people.|The coupling of the two chemicals created a dangerous reaction.|synonyms: pairing, matching, linking|antonyms: separation, division|noun
