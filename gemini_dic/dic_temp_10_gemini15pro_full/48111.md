declinometer:/ˌdiːklaɪˈnɒmɪtər/ /ˌdɛkləˈnɑːmətər/ | n. | 倾斜仪，磁偏角仪 | An instrument used for measuring the angle of a slope, or the magnetic declination. | Geologists use a declinometer to measure the dip of rock strata. 地质学家使用倾斜仪来测量岩层的倾角。

The surveyor used a declinometer to determine the angle of the slope. 测量员使用倾斜仪来确定斜坡的角度。
