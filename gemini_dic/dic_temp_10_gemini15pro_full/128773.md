nighs:/naɪz/
| n. |夜晚，夜间|Nights are generally cooler than days. 夜晚通常比白天凉爽。|The long winter nights can be depressing. 漫长的冬夜可能令人沮丧。|近义词：evenings, nighttime|反义词: days, daytime|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *nightes*, plural of *night*, from Old English *niht*, from Proto-Germanic *nahts* (cf. West Frisian *nacht*, Dutch *nacht*, German *Nacht*, Danish *nat*, Swedish *natt*, Norwegian *natt*, Icelandic *nótt*, Gothic *nahts*), from Proto-Indo-European *nokʷts-*.  Related to Latin *nox*.
