okays:/oʊˈkeɪz/| n.|可以，好的，同意|A nonstandard plural form of "okay," used informally to express agreement or acceptance.| "Okays, I'll do it."  可以，我会做的。|近义词：all right, fine, agreed|反义词: no, nope|词性:名词 (Noun)

词源:  "Okay" originated in the 19th century as a humorous misspelling of "all correct" (oll korrect). "Okays" is a more recent, informal pluralization.

注意事项:  "Okays" is considered informal and nonstandard. It's generally best to use "okay" in most situations.  The pluralization as a verb is also uncommon.  Native speakers generally repeat "okay" or rephrase for clarity.
