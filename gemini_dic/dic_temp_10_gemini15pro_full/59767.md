economize:/iˈkɑːnəmaɪz/ /ɪˈkɒnəmaɪz/| v. |节省，节约，有效利用|To reduce expenses; to be frugal.|例句：We need to economize on electricity. 我们需要节约用电。|近义词：save, conserve, cut back, retrench|反义词:waste, squander|词性:动词
Etymology: From French économiser, from économie (“economy”), from Ancient Greek οἰκονομία (oikonomía, “household management”), from οἶκος (oîkos, “house”) + νόμος (nómos, “law, custom”).
