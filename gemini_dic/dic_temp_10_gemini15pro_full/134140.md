nonimplications:/ˌnɒnɪmplɪˈkeɪʃənz/
|n.| 无蕴涵，非蕴涵 |The lack of logical consequences or inferences that can be drawn from a statement or set of statements.  It refers to situations where one thing doesn't necessarily lead to or imply another. |The lawyer argued that the witness's testimony had several nonimplications regarding the defendant's guilt. 律师辩称证人的证词对被告的罪行没有几个蕴涵意义。|Synonyms: lack of consequence, irrelevance|Antonyms: implications, consequences|noun
