noncompressible:/ˌnɒnkəmˈprɛsəbl/ (US) /ˌnɒnkəmˈprɛsɪbl/ (UK)| adj.|不可压缩的|Not able to be compressed; resisting compression.|Water is virtually noncompressible. 水实际上是不可压缩的。|The noncompressible fluid flows through the pipe. 不可压缩的流体流过管道。|近义词：incompressible, unyielding, inflexible|反义词：compressible, yielding, flexible|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- + compressible.  "Compressible" comes from Late Latin "compressibilis," from Latin "compress-," past participle stem of "comprimere" (to compress), from "com-" (together) + "premere" (to press).
