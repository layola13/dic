desolders:/diːˈsɑːldərz/ /diːˈsɒldərz/
verb
拆焊
removes solder from (something), typically in order to disconnect electronic components.
The technician desolders the faulty capacitor from the circuit board.  技术员从电路板上拆焊了故障电容器。
The engineer carefully desolders the chip. 工程师小心地拆焊芯片。

近义词: unsolders
反义词: solders
词性: verb
