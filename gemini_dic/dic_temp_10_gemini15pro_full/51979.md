diademing:/ˌdaɪəˈdɛmɪŋ/ | v. | 加冕，戴冠；使光彩夺目 | Crowning, adorning with a diadem; making glorious or resplendent. | The queen was diademing the young princess. 女王正在为年轻的公主加冕。|  The rising sun was diademing the mountain peaks. 冉冉升起的太阳使山峰光彩夺目。 | 近义词: crowning, adorning, decorating, embellishing | 反义词: stripping, divesting | 词性: 动词

词源:  From the noun "diadem" + "-ing" suffix. "Diadem" comes from the Greek "diádēma" meaning "band" or "fillet."
