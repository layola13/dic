noteboard:/ˈnoʊtbɔːrd/ | n. | 笔记板；留言板；布告栏 | A board for displaying notes, messages, or announcements. |  The teacher pinned the student's artwork to the noteboard. 老师把学生的艺术作品钉在留言板上。 |  Example:  I left a message for you on the noteboard. 我在留言板上给你留了条信息。 | Synonyms: noticeboard, bulletin board, message board | Antonyms: N/A | noun
