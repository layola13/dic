nonentrants:/ˌnɒnˈɛntrənts/ |n.| 非参赛者；未进入者；未报名者 | People who do not enter a competition, market, or other activity are nonentrants. |  Many nonentrants chose to watch the race from the sidelines. 许多未参赛者选择在场外观看比赛。| The survey included both market participants and nonentrants.  这项调查包括了市场参与者和非参与者。| synonyms: outsiders, nonparticipants, abstainers | antonyms: entrants, participants, competitors | noun
