matsus:/ˈmɑːtsuːs/ | n. | 松树（复数形式，通常不使用）|  "Matsus" is the plural form of "matsu," which is the Japanese word for "pine tree." It's rarely used in English, and even in Japanese, the singular "matsu" is generally preferred even when referring to multiple trees. | Pine trees cover the mountainside. 松树覆盖了山坡。|  近义词: pines, pine trees | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a type of tree) | 词性: noun
