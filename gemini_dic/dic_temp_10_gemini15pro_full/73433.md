foreman:/ˈfɔːrmən/ (US) /ˈfɔːmən/ (UK)| n.|工头；领班|A worker who supervises and directs other workers.|The foreman told the crew to start unloading the truck. 工头告诉工人开始卸货。|
近义词：supervisor, overseer, leader, boss, manager|反义词：subordinate, worker, employee|词性: noun


foreman:/ˈfɔːrmən/ (US) /ˈfɔːmən/ (UK)| n.|陪审团主席|The presiding member of a jury.|The foreman read the verdict aloud. 陪审团主席大声宣读了裁决。|
近义词：jury leader, presiding juror|反义词：juror|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English _foreman_, equivalent to _fore_ (before) + _man_.  Originally meaning "leader," it evolved to specify a leader of a work crew or a jury.
