heedlessly:/ˈhiːdlɪsli/ /ˈhidləsli/| adv.|不注意地，轻率地，鲁莽地|Without paying attention; carelessly.|He heedlessly crossed the road without looking. 他没有看就横穿马路。|例句：She heedlessly threw away the valuable antique. 她不经意地把珍贵的古董扔掉了。|近义词：carelessly, recklessly, thoughtlessly, rashly|反义词：carefully, cautiously, thoughtfully, prudently|词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From heedless + -ly.  Heedless comes from Old English *hēdeles, from hēd (heed) + -less.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hauzijaną (to hear, perceive).
