no-fault:/noʊ ˈfɔːlt/ | adj. | 无过错的，免责的 |  Relating to a system of insurance or law in which blame or fault is not assigned, especially in the case of automobile accidents.  Each person's insurance company pays for their own damages, regardless of who was at fault. |  This state has a no-fault insurance system for car accidents.  这个州对车祸实行无过错保险制度。| synonyms: no-blame, liability-free | antonyms: at-fault, blameworthy | adjective

no-faults:/noʊ ˈfɔːlts/ | n. | 无过错保险制度 | A system, especially for car insurance, where blame is not assigned and each party's insurer pays for their own damages. | Many people prefer no-faults because it simplifies the claims process. 许多人更喜欢无过错保险，因为它简化了索赔流程。 | synonyms: no-fault insurance, no-blame system | antonyms: at-fault system, traditional insurance | noun
