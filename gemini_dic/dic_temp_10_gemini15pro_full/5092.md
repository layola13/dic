allay:/əˈleɪ/
verb
减轻，缓和
to diminish or put at rest (fear, suspicion, or worry).
The report attempted to allay public fears.  这份报告试图减轻公众的恐惧。
近义词: alleviate, mitigate, soothe, calm, ease, relieve, subdue, lessen
反义词: aggravate, exacerbate, intensify, increase, heighten
词性: verb

allay:/əˈleɪ/
词源: From Middle English *aleien, *aleggen, from Old English ālecgan (“to put down, lay down, abolish, annul, suppress, destroy, remove, take away, put aside, renounce, deny, dissuade”), from ā- + lecgan (“to lay, put, place”). Cognate with Old Frisian ālegga (“to allay, deny”), Old Saxon āleggia (“to stop, prevent”), Middle Dutch alegghen (“to lay down, explain, translate”), Old High German irleccan, arleggen (“to lay down, put down, explain, abolish, confute”). Related to lay.
词源: 源自中古英语 *aleien, *aleggen，来自古英语 ālecgan（“放下，铺设，废除，取消，压制，摧毁，移除，带走，搁置，放弃，否认，劝阻”），来自 ā- + lecgan（“放置，安放”）。 与古弗里斯兰语 ālegga（“减轻，否认”），古撒克逊语 āleggia（“停止，阻止”），中古荷兰语 alegghen（“放下，解释，翻译”），古高地德语 irleccan, arleggen（“放下，放下，解释，废除，驳斥”）同源。 与 lay 相关。
