misdeemed:/ˌmɪsˈdiːmd/
verb
误判，错认
judged incorrectly or wrongly.
He misdeemed her silence as consent. 他把她的沉默误认为是同意。
近义词:misjudged, misinterpreted, mistaken
反义词:correctly judged, accurately assessed
