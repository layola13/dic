nonentity:/nɒnˈɛntɪti/ /nɑːnˈɛntɪti/| n. |无名小卒，不重要的人/物|A person or thing of no importance or significance.|例句：He was a nonentity in the world of finance. 在金融界，他是个无名小卒。|例句：The company was a nonentity before the new CEO took over.  在新CEO接手之前，这家公司默默无闻。|近义词：nobody, cipher, insignificant person|反义词:celebrity, VIP, luminary|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + entity.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "entity" refers to something that exists.  Thus, a "nonentity" is literally something that does not truly exist in a meaningful or significant way.
