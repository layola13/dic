counteraccusations:/ˌkaʊntərˌækjuˈzeɪʃənz/| n.|反控，反指控|An accusation made in response to a previous accusation.|The trial became bogged down in a series of counteraccusations.  审判陷入了一系列相互指控的泥潭。|Synonyms: countercharges, recriminations|Antonyms: admissions, confessions|Noun

counteraccusation:/ˌkaʊntərˌækjuˈzeɪʃən/| n.|反控，反指控|An accusation made in response to a previous accusation.|He responded to the allegations with a counteraccusation of his own. 他以自己的反控回应了这些指控。|Synonyms: countercharge, recrimination|Antonyms: admission, confession|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From counter- (prefix meaning "against, opposite") + accusation (from Latin accusatio, from accusare "to accuse").
