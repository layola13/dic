Habsburgs:/ˈhæpsbɜːrɡz/
noun
哈布斯堡家族
A royal German family that provided rulers for several European states from the 13th to the 20th century.
The Habsburgs ruled over Austria for centuries. 哈布斯堡家族统治奥地利几个世纪。
近义词:  House of Habsburg
反义词: N/A
词性: n.

Etymology:
From German *Habsburg*, from the name of the family's original castle, *Habichtsburg* ("Hawk's Castle"), in Aargau, Switzerland.
