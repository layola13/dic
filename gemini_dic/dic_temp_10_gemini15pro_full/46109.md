cylindraceous:/ˌsɪlɪnˈdreɪʃəs/
adjective
圆柱状的; 圆筒形的
Having the shape of a cylinder; approximately cylindrical.
The bacteria are cylindraceous, and occur singly or in short chains. 这些细菌呈圆柱状，单独存在或形成短链。
The fruit is cylindraceous, about 2 cm long. 果实呈圆柱形，长约2厘米。
近义词:cylindrical, columnar
反义词:spherical, globular
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin cylindrus (“cylinder”) + -aceous.
