Fulahs:/ˈfuːləz/ | n. | 富拉尼人; 富拉人 | An ethnic group of West Africa, widely dispersed across the Sahel and West Sudan.  They are traditionally nomadic pastoralists, herding cattle. |  The Fulahs have a rich culture and history. 富拉尼人有着丰富的文化和历史。 |  Many Fulahs are now settled agriculturalists. 许多富拉尼人现在是定居的农业家。| synonyms: Fulani, Fulbe | antonyms: N/A | noun

Fulah:/ˈfuːlə/ | adj. |  富拉尼人的; 富拉人的 | Relating to the Fulah people, their language, or their culture. | The Fulah language is spoken across a wide area.  富拉语在一个广阔的地区使用。 |  Fulah traditions are passed down through generations. 富拉尼人的传统代代相传。| synonyms: Fulani, Fulbe | antonyms: N/A | adjective
