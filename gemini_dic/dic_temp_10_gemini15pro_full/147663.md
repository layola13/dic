overpopulously:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːpjələsli/| adv.|人口过剩地，人口过多地|In a manner characterized by excessive population density.  Describes a place inhabited by an excessively large number of people.|The city was overpopulously congested, making it difficult to navigate the streets. 这个城市人口过密，交通拥堵，难以通行。|Example:  Resources were stretched thin in the overpopulously inhabited region.  在这个人口过多的地区，资源十分匮乏。|近义词：densely, crowdedly|反义词: sparsely, thinly|词性:副词

词源:
over- (prefix) + populous (adjective) + -ly (adverb suffix)

"over-" 表示 "过度", "过分"
"populous" 源自拉丁语 "populus" (人民)
"-ly" 将形容词转换为副词
