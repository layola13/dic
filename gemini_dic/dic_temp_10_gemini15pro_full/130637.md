nonadmissibly:/ˌnɒnədˈmɪsɪbli/
adverb
不可接受地，不予采纳地
Not in a way that is admissible; in an inadmissible manner.
The evidence was nonadmissibly obtained and therefore couldn't be used in court.  这些证据是通过不可接受的方式获取的，因此不能在法庭上使用。
The lawyer argued that the confession was nonadmissibly obtained because the defendant had not been read his rights. 律师辩称，供词是不可接受的，因为被告没有被告知他的权利。
Synonym: improperly, invalidly, unacceptably
Antonym: admissibly, properly, validly
词性: 副词
