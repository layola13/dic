light-footednesses:/ˌlaɪtˈfʊtɪdnɪsɪz/| n. |轻盈，敏捷|The quality or state of being light-footed; nimbleness and agility.|Her light-footednesses made her a natural dancer. 她的轻盈使她成为一个天生的舞者。|近义词：agility, nimbleness, dexterity, gracefulness|反义词:clumsiness, awkwardness, heaviness|词性:noun

light-footed:/ˌlaɪtˈfʊtɪd/| adj. |轻盈的，敏捷的|Moving lightly and quickly on one's feet; nimble.|The light-footed deer bounded through the forest. 轻盈的鹿跳跃着穿过森林。|近义词：agile, nimble, swift, graceful|反义词:clumsy, awkward, heavy|词性:adjective
