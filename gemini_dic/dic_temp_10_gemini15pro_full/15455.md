badasses:/ˈbædˌæsɪz/| n.|坏蛋，硬汉，狠角色|A badass is a tough, uncompromising, and intimidating person.  Often used admiringly to describe someone who is skilled and confident, especially in a difficult or dangerous situation.|Those badasses handled the crisis like pros. 那些硬汉像专业人士一样处理了危机。|近义词：tough guys, hard nuts, renegades|反义词:wimps, cowards|Noun

badass:/ˈbædæs/| n. adj.|坏蛋，硬汉；强悍的|As a noun, it refers to a tough, uncompromising person.
