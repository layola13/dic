pancultural:/ˌpænˈkʌltʃərəl/
adjective
泛文化的
Relating to or common to all cultures; transcending cultural boundaries.
Pancultural themes like love and loss resonate with people everywhere.  泛文化主题，如爱和失落，能引起世界各地人们的共鸣。
Pancultural understanding is crucial in today's interconnected world. 在当今互联互通的世界中，跨文化理解至关重要。
近义词: universal, cross-cultural, global
反义词: culture-specific, ethnocentric
