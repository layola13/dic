Uranus: /ˈjʊərənəs/ (US), /ˈjʊərənəs/ (UK)| n. |天王星|The seventh planet from the Sun, named after the Greek god of the sky.|例句：Uranus is tilted on its axis. 天王星的轴线是倾斜的。

Uranus has a faint ring system. 天王星有一个微弱的环状系统。|近义词: (As a planet, it has no true synonyms, but regarding its vastness, perhaps 'colossal', 'immense')|反义词: (No antonyms for a planet)|词性:noun
