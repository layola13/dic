nonconvivial:/ˌnɒnkənˈvɪviəl/ | adj. | 不友好的，不欢快的，不适于举行的 | Not convivial; unfriendly, unsociable, or not conducive to conviviality.  | The atmosphere at the meeting was decidedly nonconvivial, with several heated arguments breaking out. 会议上的气氛明显不友好，爆发了几次激烈的争论。

The nonconvivial host made his guests feel unwelcome.  不友好的主人让他的客人们感到不受欢迎。 | 近义词：unfriendly, unsociable, inhospitable, uncongenial | 反义词：convivial, friendly, sociable, welcoming | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From non- + convivial.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "convivial" comes from the Latin "convivialis," meaning "festive" or "relating to a feast."
