medjidieh: /məˈdʒɪdiə/ | n. | 麦吉迪勋章（奥斯曼帝国的一种勋章）; 麦吉迪耶（奥斯曼帝国的一种金币） | A Turkish order instituted in 1852 by the sultan Abd-ul-Medjid. |  He was awarded the Medjidieh for his bravery. 他因其勇敢而被授予麦吉迪勋章。 | synonym: order, decoration, medal | antonym: N/A | noun

medjidie: /məˈdʒɪdiə/ | n. | 麦吉迪耶（奥斯曼帝国的一种金币） | A former Turkish gold coin. | The medjidie was worth about 4 shillings and 6 pence.  麦吉迪耶价值约4先令和6便士。 | synonym: coin, currency | antonym: N/A | noun
