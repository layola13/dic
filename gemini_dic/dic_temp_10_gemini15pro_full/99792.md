intermesh:/ˌɪntərˈmɛʃ/
verb
啮合，紧密相扣
to fit together closely, like the teeth of gears
The gears intermesh smoothly. 齿轮啮合顺畅。
近义词: mesh, engage, interlock, dovetail
反义词: disengage, separate, disconnect
词性: v.

intermesh:/ˌɪntərˈmɛʃ/
noun
啮合
the act of fitting together closely
The intermesh of the gears ensured smooth operation. 齿轮的啮合确保了平稳运行。
近义词: meshing, engagement, interlocking
反义词: disengagement, separation
词性: n.
