obtainability:/əbˌteɪnəˈbɪləti/
noun
可获得性，可得到性
The obtainability of fresh produce varies depending on the season. 新鲜农产品的可获得性取决于季节。
例句：The obtainability of affordable housing is a major issue in many cities.  在许多城市，经济适用房的可获得性是一个主要问题。

例句：The internet has greatly increased the obtainability of information. 互联网极大地提高了信息的获取性。

近义词：availability, accessibility, attainability
反义词：unavailability, inaccessibility, rarity
词性:n.


obtainable:/əbˈteɪnəbl/
adjective
可获得的，可得到的
Tickets are obtainable from the box office.  门票可以从售票处获得。
例句：The information is readily obtainable online.  这些信息很容易在网上获得。

例句：The necessary permits are obtainable from the local council.  必要的许可证可以从当地议会获得。

近义词：available, accessible, attainable
反义词：unavailable, inaccessible, rare
词性:adj.


词源:
From obtain + -ability.  来自 obtain + -ability.
Obtain comes from Old French obtenir, from Latin obtinēre "to hold, possess, maintain, acquire, gain, keep," from ob- "toward" + tenēre "to hold."
