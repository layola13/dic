faraday:/ˈfærədeɪ/ | n. | 法拉第（电容单位），法拉第常数 | A unit of electrical charge equal to the charge on one mole of electrons (approximately 96,485 coulombs).  Also, a scientist, Michael Faraday, known for his work on electromagnetism. | The capacitance of the capacitor is measured in farads.  这个电容器的电容是用法拉测量。|  coulomb, unit of charge | N/A | noun
