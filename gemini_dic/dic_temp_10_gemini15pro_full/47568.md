debark:/dɪˈbɑːrk/
verb
下船；下飞机；下火车；卸货
To leave a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle.
The passengers began to debark the plane. 乘客开始下飞机。
Passengers are requested to debark the train. 请乘客下火车。
disembark, get off
embark, board
