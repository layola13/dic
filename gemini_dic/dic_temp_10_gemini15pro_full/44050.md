cringer:/ˈkrɪndʒər/| n.|畏缩的人，尴尬的人，令人尴尬的人或事|Someone who feels very embarrassed and uncomfortable, especially when thinking about something they have said or done, or someone who makes other people feel this way.  It can also refer to something that causes this feeling.|例句：Watching him try to dance was a total cringer.  看他跳舞真是让人尴尬得不行。

例句：He's such a cringer - he always says the wrong thing. 他真是个令人尴尬的人 - 他总是说错话。|近义词：embarrassment, wimp, coward|反义词：extrovert, showman|词性：noun
