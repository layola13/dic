erythrite:/ˈɛrɪθraɪt/| n.|红砷镍矿, 钴华|A reddish or pink mineral consisting of a hydrated cobalt arsenate, occurring in the oxidized parts of cobalt and arsenic-bearing veins.|The erythrite specimen displayed a vibrant pink hue. 这块红砷镍矿标本呈现出鲜艳的粉红色。|近义词：cobalt bloom|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
etymology: From French *érythrine*, from Ancient Greek ἐρυθρός (*eruthrós*) meaning 'red'.|词源：来自法语 *érythrine*，源自古希腊语 ἐρυθρός (*eruthrós*)，意为“红色”。
