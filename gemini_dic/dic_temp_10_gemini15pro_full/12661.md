assembled:/əˈsembld/
adjective|组装的，集合的|brought together into a group or whole; put together|The assembled crowd cheered for the winning team.  聚集在一起的人群为获胜的队伍欢呼。|synonyms: gathered, collected, congregated|antonyms: dispersed, scattered|词性:形容词

assembled:/əˈsembld/
verb (past tense)|组装，集合|fit together the separate component parts of (a machine or other object)|I assembled the furniture myself. 我自己组装了家具。|synonyms:  construct, build, put together|antonyms: dismantle, disassemble, take apart|词性:动词
