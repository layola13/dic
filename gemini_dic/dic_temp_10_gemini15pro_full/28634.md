cargoes:/ˈkɑːrɡoʊz/| n. |货物（cargo的复数形式）|Goods carried by a ship, aircraft, or other large vehicle.|The ship was carrying a cargo of bananas. 这艘船装载着一批香蕉。|近义词：freight, shipment, load|反义词: ballast (压舱物, 在没有货物时用来保持船的稳定)|noun
