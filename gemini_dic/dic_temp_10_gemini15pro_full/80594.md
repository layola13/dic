googlies: /ˈɡuːɡliz/ (UK), /ˈɡuːɡliz/ (US) | n. | 曲球（板球）；难题；迷惑 | Unpredictably spinning deliveries in cricket, designed to deceive the batsman. Also used figuratively to describe something unexpected or confusing. | The bowler's googlies completely bamboozled the batsman. 投球手的曲球完全迷惑了击球手。 |  He threw me a googly when he suddenly changed the subject.  他突然改变话题，让我措手不及。 | 近义词:curveball, spinner (in cricket),  problem, puzzle | 反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps predictability) | 词性: noun


Etymology:  Likely derived from "googly eyes," referring to the wide-eyed, surprised look of a batsman facing an unexpected delivery.
