negotiable:/nɪˈɡoʊʃiəbl/ /nɪˈɡəʊʃəbl/| adj. n.|可协商的，可转让的；(n.) 可转让证券|Something that is negotiable can be discussed or changed in order to reach an agreement.  (adj.) A negotiable document, such as a check or bond, can be legally transferred to another person. (n.)|例句：The price is negotiable. 价格是可以商量的。|近义词：discussable, transferable, bargainable|反义词：non-negotiable, fixed, firm|词性: adjective, noun

negotiables:/nɪˈɡoʊʃiəblz/ /nɪˈɡəʊʃəblz/| n.|可转让证券；可协商的条件|Plural form of "negotiable" referring specifically to financial instruments like checks, bonds, etc., or matters open for discussion or alteration.|例句：The negotiables include price, delivery date, and payment terms.  可协商的条件包括价格、交货日期和付款方式。|近义词：securities, assets,  bargaining chips|反义词：non-negotiables, fixed terms|词性: noun
