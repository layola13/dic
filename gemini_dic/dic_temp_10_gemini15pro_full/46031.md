cyberthreat:/ˈsaɪbərθrɛt/| n. |网络威胁|A malicious act or attempt to damage or disrupt a computer network or system through cyberspace.|Example 1:  The company faced a serious cyberthreat from a group of hackers.  这家公司面临着一群黑客的严重网络威胁。

Example 2: Protecting against cyberthreats is crucial in today's digital world.  在当今的数字世界中，防范网络威胁至关重要。|近义词：cyberattack, cyber risk, online threat|反义词:cybersecurity, cyber defense|词性:名词


词源:
"Cyber-" from "cybernetics," relating to computer networks and systems. "-threat" a thing that is likely to cause damage or danger.
