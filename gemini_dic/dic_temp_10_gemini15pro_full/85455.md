hasht:/hæʃt/ |n.| 标签; 井号; |A tag used on social media platforms to categorize content, represented by the symbol #. | Example:  She used the #travel hasht to share her vacation photos. 她用#travel标签分享了她的度假照片。

Example:  The hasht symbol is also known as the pound sign. 井号也被称为磅符号。 | 近义词:tag, hashtag | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词

词源: "hasht" is a shortened form of "hashtag," which is a blend of "hash" (referring to the # symbol) and "tag" (meaning a label). The term gained popularity with the rise of social media platforms like Twitter.
