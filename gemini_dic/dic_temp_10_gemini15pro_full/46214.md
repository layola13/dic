cysted:/ˈsɪstɪd/
adjective
囊状的；包于囊内的
Enclosed in a cyst or cysts.
The doctor found a cysted tumor during the ultrasound. 医生在超声波检查中发现了一个囊状肿瘤。
Synonyms: encysted, cystic
Antonyms: noncystic
