corrupters:/kəˈrʌptərz/
noun
腐败者；堕落者；使腐败的人或物
Those who corrupt others, causing them to become dishonest or immoral.
The corrupters were brought to justice. 这些腐败分子被绳之以法。
Synonyms: debauchers, perverters, degraders
Antonyms: reformers, purifiers, improvers
词性: 名词


corrupt:/kəˈrʌpt/
verb
腐蚀，使腐败；贿赂；（使文件或数据）损坏
adjective
腐败的，堕落的；贪污的；（文件或数据）已损坏的
To cause someone or something to become dishonest or immoral. / Dishonest or immoral.
The officials were corrupted by the offer of bribes. 官员们被贿赂腐蚀了。/ The corrupt officials were arrested. 腐败的官员被逮捕了。
Synonyms: (v) spoil, taint, contaminate; (adj) dishonest, immoral, crooked
Antonyms: (v) purify, cleanse; (adj) honest, moral, upright
词性: 动词, 形容词
