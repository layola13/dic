limberness:/ˈlɪmbərnəs/| n. |柔软性，灵活性|The state of being limber; flexibility.|Her limberness is impressive for her age. 她的柔韧性对于她的年龄来说令人印象深刻。|The gymnast displayed incredible limberness during her floor routine.  这个体操运动员在地板操中展现了惊人的柔韧性。|近义词：flexibility, suppleness, pliancy|反义词：stiffness, rigidity|词性:名词

词源: From limber + -ness.  Limber itself comes from Middle English *limber*, from Old English *limplic* (“pliant, flexible”), related to *lempheald* (“flabby”).
