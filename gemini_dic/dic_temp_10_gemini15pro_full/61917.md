empyrion:/ɛmˈpɪriən/| n. | 最高天，九天，苍天; 炽天，火天;|The empyrean is often used in literature to describe the highest and most pure heaven.|They soared through the empyrean.|近义词: heaven, firmament, celestial sphere|反义词: hell, underworld|词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *empyrius, from Ancient Greek ἔμπυρος (émpuros, “fiery, in or on the fire”), from ἐν (en, “in, on”) + πῦρ (pûr, “fire”).
