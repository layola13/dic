aril:/ˈærɪl/| n.|假种皮|An outer covering of a seed, formed from the outgrowth of the ovule stalk, typically brightly coloured and fleshy, e.g. that of a yew.|The yew aril is a fleshy cup that surrounds the poisonous seed.|近义词:seed covering, testa|反义词:|名词
