overconsumed:/ˌoʊvər kənˈsuːmd/
|v.|过度消费；过量消耗|To consume something in excess, beyond what is necessary or healthy.|He overconsumed sugary drinks and gained weight. 他过度饮用含糖饮料，体重增加了。|Synonyms: overindulged, overate, overspent|Antonyms: conserved, saved, underconsumed|verb (past participle)
overconsumed:/ˌoʊvər kənˈsuːmɪŋ/
|adj.|过度消费的；过量消耗的|Consuming something in excess, beyond what is necessary or healthy.|The overconsuming habits of some people contribute to environmental problems.  一些人的过度消费习惯导致了环境问题。|Synonyms: excessive, immoderate, prodigal|Antonyms: moderate, thrifty, economical|adjective
