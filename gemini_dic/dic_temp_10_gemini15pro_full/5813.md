amassers:/əˈmæsərz/| n. |积累者，收集者|People who gather or accumulate something, especially wealth or possessions.  Those who amass things.|例句：The amassers of antique cars often spend fortunes on their collections. 古董车收藏家常常花费巨资收藏。

例句：The amassers of rare stamps were excited about the new find. 稀有邮票的收藏家对新发现感到兴奋。|近义词：collectors, accumulators, hoarders, gatherers|反义词：spenders, distributors, donors|词性:Noun (plural)
