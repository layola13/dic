malic:/ˈmælɪk/| adj.|苹果酸的；由苹果得到的|Relating to or derived from apples or malic acid.  Specifically, it refers to the organic compound malic acid, a dicarboxylic acid that is made by all living organisms and contributes to the pleasantly sour taste of fruits.|Malic acid is added to some foods to give them a tart flavor. 苹果酸被添加到一些食品中以赋予它们酸味。|Synonyms: tart, sour|Antonyms: sweet, bland|Adjective

malate:/ˈmeɪleɪt/ |n.|苹果酸盐|A salt or ester of malic acid.|Malate is an intermediate in the citric acid cycle. 苹果酸盐是柠檬酸循环的中间体。|Synonyms:  (No direct single-word synonym, but can be described based on its chemical properties)|Antonyms: (No direct single-word antonym)|Noun
