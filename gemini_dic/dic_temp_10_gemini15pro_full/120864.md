misbeliever:/ˌmɪsbɪˈliːvər/ | n. |不信教者，异教徒|A person who does not believe in a particular religion, or who holds different religious beliefs.|例句：The missionaries tried to convert the misbelievers.  传教士试图改变那些不信教的人。|例句：He was considered a misbeliever by the orthodox community. 他被正统社区视为异教徒。|近义词：heretic, infidel, nonbeliever, unbeliever|反义词：believer, faithful|词性:名词
