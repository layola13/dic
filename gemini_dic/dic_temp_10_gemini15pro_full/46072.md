cyclone:/ˈsaɪkloʊn/ (US) /ˈsaɪkləʊn/ (UK)| n. |旋风，气旋，飓风|A system of winds rotating inwards to an area of low atmospheric pressure, with a counterclockwise rotation in the northern hemisphere and clockwise in the southern hemisphere.|The cyclone ripped through the coastal towns, causing widespread devastation.  飓风席卷了沿海城镇，造成了大范围的破坏。|近义词：hurricane, typhoon, tornado|反义词:calm, stillness|名词
