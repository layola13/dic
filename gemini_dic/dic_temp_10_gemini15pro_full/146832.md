overimpressionably:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈprɛʃənəbli/
| adv. | 过于易受影响地 | In a manner that is excessively susceptible to impressions; too easily influenced by external stimuli. | He listened overimpressionably to the speaker, accepting every word as truth. 他过于容易被演讲者影响，把每一句话都当作真理。 | She overimpressionably adopted the latest fashion trends, changing her style every week. 她过于容易被最新的时尚潮流影响，每周都改变自己的风格。|近义词：naively, gullibly, unsuspectingly | 反义词：skeptically, critically, discerningly | 词性: 副词


overimpressionable:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈprɛʃənəbəl/
| adj. | 过于易受影响的 | Excessively susceptible to impressions; easily influenced. |  A young and overimpressionable mind can be easily swayed by peer pressure.  年轻且易受影响的心灵很容易被同龄人的压力所左右。 |  He was overimpressionable as a child, believing every story he was told. 他小时候很容易受影响，相信他听到的每一个故事。| 近义词：impressionable, susceptible, sensitive | 反义词：impervious, resistant, skeptical | 词性: 形容词


词源:

over- (prefix):  meaning "excessively," "too much"
impressionable (adjective): from "impression" (the act of impressing or influencing) + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being")
-ly (suffix):  forms adverbs from adjectives
