outjumped:/ˌaʊtˈdʒʌmpt/
verb
跳得比…远/高; 超越
To jump further or higher than.
He outjumped everyone in the competition. 他在比赛中跳得比所有人都远/高。
He easily outjumped his rivals. 他轻松地跳得比他的对手们远/高。
surpassed, exceeded, outleaped
--- (no clear single antonym; consider "was outjumped by")
