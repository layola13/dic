exsect:/ɪkˈsɛkt/ | v. | 切除；割除 | To cut out; to remove by cutting. | The surgeon exsected the tumor. 外科医生切除了肿瘤。|  The biologist exsected a tissue sample for analysis. 生物学家切除了一块组织样本用于分析。| 近义词: excise, remove, extract | 反义词: implant, insert | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Latin *exsectus*, past participle of *exsecāre*  "to cut out, cut off," from *ex* "out" + *secāre* "to cut."
