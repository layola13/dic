leasor:/ˈliːsɔːr/| n. |出租人；租赁人|A person who leases or lets property to another; a landlord.|The leasor retains ownership of the property throughout the lease term. 出租人在整个租赁期内保留财产的所有权。|Example 2: The leasor and the lessee signed a five-year lease agreement. 出租人和承租人签署了一份五年租赁协议。|Synonym: landlord, lessor|Antonym: lessee, tenant|noun
