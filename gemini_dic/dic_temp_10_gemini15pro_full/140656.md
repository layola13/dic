ombrometer:/ɒmˈbrɒmɪtər/ | n. |雨量计；测雨计|An instrument for measuring the amount of rainfall. |The ombrometer showed that we had received two inches of rain. 雨量计显示我们这里降了两英寸的雨。|Synonyms: rain gauge, pluviometer, udometer |Antonyms: None | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *ombromètre*, from Ancient Greek ὄμβρος (*ómbros*, “rain”) + -mètre (“meter”).
