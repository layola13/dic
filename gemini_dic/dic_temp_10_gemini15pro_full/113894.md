madreporic: /ˌmædrəˈpɔːrɪk/ | adj. | 筛板的，多孔的 | Relating to or resembling a madreporite, a calcareous plate with radiating pores, part of the water vascular system in echinoderms. | The starfish's madreporic plate filters water into its vascular system.  海星的筛板将水过滤到其血管系统中。| Near synonyms: porous, perforated | Antonym: impermeable | adjective

madreporite: /ˌmædrəˈpɔːraɪt/ | n. | 筛板 | A perforated plate by which the entry of seawater into the vascular system of an echinoderm is controlled. | The madreporite is a crucial part of the starfish's anatomy. 筛板是海星解剖结构的关键部分。| Near synonyms: sieve plate | Antonym: None | noun
