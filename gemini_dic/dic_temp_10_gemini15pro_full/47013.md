dashy:/ˈdæʃi/| adj.|时髦的；浮华的；艳丽的|Stylish and showy, often in a slightly vulgar way.  Characterized by bold strokes or flourishes.|She wore a dashy red dress to the party. 她穿着一件艳丽的红色连衣裙去参加了派对。|近义词：flashy, showy, ostentatious, flamboyant|反义词：understated, subtle, modest|词性:形容词
Etymology: From dash (n.) + -y (suffix forming adjectives).  "Dash" in this context originally referred to a stroke of the pen or brush, suggesting something bold and striking.
