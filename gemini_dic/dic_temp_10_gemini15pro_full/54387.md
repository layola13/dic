disillusionist:/ˌdɪsɪˈluːʒənɪst/
n.
醒悟者；使人醒悟的人；幻灭者
a person who destroys someone's belief in a particular ideal or belief system
The disillusionist revealed the fraudulent practices of the so-called miracle worker.  这位使人醒悟的人揭露了所谓的奇迹创造者的欺诈行为。

He felt like a disillusionist, shattering the romantic ideals of his younger colleagues. 他觉得自己像个幻灭者，粉碎了年轻同事们的浪漫理想。


近义词:cynic, skeptic, pessimist, realist
反义词:idealist, optimist, dreamer, believer
词性:noun


disillusion:/ˌdɪsɪˈluːʒən/
vt.
使幻想破灭；使不再抱幻想；使清醒
to free from illusion or fantasy
The harsh realities of war disillusioned many young soldiers. 战争的残酷现实使许多年轻士兵的幻想破灭。

The experience disillusioned her about the nature of politics. 这次经历让她对政治的本质不再抱有幻想。


近义词:disenchant, disappoint, shatter, undeceive
反义词:enchant, charm, delight, fascinate
词性:verb
