multicar:/ˈmʌltiˌkɑːr/ | n. | 多节车厢；多用途车辆 | A vehicle, typically a railroad car, made up of multiple connected units or sections, often designed for transporting various types of freight or passengers. | The multicar carried a variety of goods, from lumber to automobiles. 这辆多节车厢装载了各种货物，从木材到汽车。|  Example: The railway company invested in new multicars to increase freight capacity. 这家铁路公司投资了新的多节车厢以提高货运能力。 | Synonyms: modular train, articulated car, multi-unit train | Antonyms: single-unit vehicle, single car | noun
