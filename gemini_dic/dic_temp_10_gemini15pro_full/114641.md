malexecution:/ˌmælɛksɪˈkjuːʃən/
noun
错误执行；执行不当
The project's failure was due to malexecution of the plan.  这个项目的失败是由于计划执行不当造成的。
The malexecution of the surgical procedure led to complications.  外科手术的执行不当导致了并发症。
mismanagement, mishandling, misapplication
proper execution, successful implementation
