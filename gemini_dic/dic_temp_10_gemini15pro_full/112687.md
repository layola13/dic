lowlands:/ˈləʊləndz/
n.|低地，低洼地区|A lowland area is an area of land that is lower than the surrounding land. |The Netherlands is known for its lowlands. 荷兰以其低地而闻名。|Low-lying areas are subject to flooding. 低洼地区易受洪水侵袭。|近义词：plains, valleys, flatlands|反义词: highlands, uplands|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lowelondes*, from Old English *lāglond*, from *lāg* (“low”) + *land* (“land”).  The plural form became standard in Modern English.
