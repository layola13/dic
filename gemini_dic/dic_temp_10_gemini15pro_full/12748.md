assiduous:/əˈsɪdʒuəs/| adj.|勤勉的，刻苦的|Showing great care and perseverance.|She is an assiduous student, always studying late into the night. 她是一个勤奋的学生，总是学习到深夜。|近义词：diligent, hardworking, industrious, persistent|反义词：lazy, indolent, negligent|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *assiduus*, from *assidere* "to sit beside," from *ad-* "to" + *sedere* "to sit."  Originally meant "constant in attendance," later evolving to mean "diligent" and "hardworking."
