imparipinnate:/ɪmˌpærɪˈpɪneɪt/
adjective
奇数羽状的
(of a compound leaf) having an odd number of leaflets, with a single terminal leaflet.
The leaves of the ash tree are imparipinnate. 白蜡树的叶子是奇数羽状的。

The rose bush in my garden has imparipinnate leaves. 我花园里的玫瑰丛有奇数羽状复叶。
synonym: odd-pinnate
antonym: paripinnate
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *impari-pinnatus*, from *impar* (“unequal, odd”) + *pinnatus* (“feathered, winged”).  *pinnatus* is related to *pinna* (“feather, wing, fin”).
