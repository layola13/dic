modernizations:/ˌmɑːdərnəˈzeɪʃənz/ | /ˌmɒdənaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 现代化（复数） | The process of making something more modern; updates or improvements that bring something up to date. |  The modernizations of the factory increased efficiency. 工厂的现代化提高了效率。

The recent modernizations to the city's infrastructure have greatly improved traffic flow.  这座城市基础设施最近的现代化改造极大地改善了交通流量。


| 近义词: improvements, upgrades, developments, advancements | 反义词: regressions, declines | 词性: noun (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From modernization + -s (plural suffix).
Modernization from modernize + -ation (suffix forming nouns of action or process).
Modernize from modern + -ize (suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become modern").
Modern from Middle French moderne, from Late Latin modernus, from Latin modo "just now".
