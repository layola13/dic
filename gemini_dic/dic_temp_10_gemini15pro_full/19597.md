bight:/baɪt/| n.| 海湾，绳圈，弯曲处|A bight is a curve or recess in a coastline, river, or other geographical feature, forming a bay. It can also refer to a loop of rope. |The ship anchored in the bight of the bay. 船停泊在海湾的弯曲处。|近义词:bay, cove, loop, bend|反义词:straight|词性:noun

bight:/baɪt/| n.|绳圈，弯曲| A bight is a loop of rope, as distinct from the ends. |He made a bight in the rope to secure the boat. 他在绳子上打了一个圈来固定船。 | 近义词:loop, bend, curve|反义词:straight|词性:noun
