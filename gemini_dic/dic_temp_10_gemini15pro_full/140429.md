oked:/oʊkt/
adjective
hooked
上瘾的；着迷的
Engaged or addicted, especially to a drug.
例句：He got hooked on heroin. 他对海洛因上瘾了。

例句：She's completely hooked on that new TV show. 她完全迷上了那部新的电视剧。

近义词：addicted, dependent, obsessed
反义词：indifferent, uninterested
词性：adjective
