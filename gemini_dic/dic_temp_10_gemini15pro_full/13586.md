attentiveness:/əˈtɛntɪvnəs/ |n.| 专注，用心，注意力 |The student's attentiveness in class was evident. 这个学生在课堂上的专注是显而易见的。| attentiveness is the quality of being attentive; paying close attention.  Attentiveness is important for learning and understanding. |例句：Her attentiveness to detail is remarkable. 她对细节的关注令人瞩目。|近义词：concentration, focus, heed, mindfulness|反义词：inattentiveness, distraction, negligence|词性:名词

Etymology: From attentive + -ness.  "Attentive" comes from the Late Latin *attentīvus, from attentus ("attentive"), past participle of attendere ("to attend to"), from ad- ("to") + tendere ("to stretch").
