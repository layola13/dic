discursive:/dɪˈskɜːsɪv/| adj.|散漫的，离题的，推论的|Passing from one topic to another; digressive; rambling.  Reasoning by argument rather than intuition.|例句：The professor's lectures were notoriously discursive, often veering off on tangents. 教授的讲座是出了名的散漫，经常偏离主题。

例句：The book takes a discursive approach to the history of the region. 这本书对该地区的历史采取了一种推论性的方法。|近义词：rambling, digressive, meandering, diffuse, wandering|反义词：concise, succinct, focused, direct, to the point|词性: adjective
