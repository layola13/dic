malkin:/ˈmɔːlkɪn/ /ˈmɑːlkɪn/| n.|女仆；邋遢的女人；猫；拖把|A girl or woman, especially a servant girl; a slut or prostitute; a cat, especially a female cat; a mop or rag for cleaning.|例句：The old malkin swept the floor. 老女仆扫了地。

例句：She called her a dirty malkin. 她骂她是个邋遢的女人。|近义词：wench, slattern, cat, mop|反义词:lady, gentleman|词性:noun

malkin:/ˈmɔːlkɪn/ /ˈmɑːlkɪn/| n.|稻草人；稻草（或破布等制成的）人像|A scarecrow; an effigy made of straw or rags.|例句：The farmer erected a malkin in the field to scare away the birds.  农民在田里竖起了一个稻草人来吓跑鸟儿。

例句：The protesters burned a malkin of the president. 抗议者焚烧了总统的稻草人像。|近义词：scarecrow, effigy|反义词:None|词性:noun
