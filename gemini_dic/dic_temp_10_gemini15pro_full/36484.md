colemanite:/ˈkoʊlməˌnaɪt/| n.|硬硼钙石|A hydrous calcium borate mineral, Ca₂B₆O₁₁·5H₂O, occurring as white or colorless monoclinic crystals, used as a source of borax and boric acid.|Colemanite is an important source of boron. 硬硼钙石是硼的重要来源。|Synonyms: borate, mineral|Antonyms: None|Noun
