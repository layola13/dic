hypertrophic:/haɪpərˈtrɒfɪk/ (US) /haɪpəˈtrɒfɪk/ (UK)| adj.|肥大的，过度生长的|Enlarged or overgrown due to an increase in the size of its constituent cells.|Example: The patient presented with hypertrophic cardiomyopathy.  病人表现出肥厚型心肌病。|Example:  Regular exercise can lead to hypertrophic muscles.  规律的运动会导致肌肉肥大。|Synonyms: enlarged, overgrown, expanded, dilated|Antonyms: atrophied, shrunken, diminished|adjective
