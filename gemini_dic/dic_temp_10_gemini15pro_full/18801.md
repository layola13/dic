bers: /bɜːz/ | n. (plural) | 狂暴状态；(复数) 狂怒的人们 | A state of frenzied excitement or rage; people in a state of frenzy or rage. |  The bers went on a rampage through the village. 狂暴的人们在村子里横冲直撞。 | 近义词: frenzy, fury, rage | 反义词: calm, tranquility | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old Norse *berserkr, meaning "bear-shirt" or "bear-skin," referring to warriors who fought in a state of frenzied rage.
