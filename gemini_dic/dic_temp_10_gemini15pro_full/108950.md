leerer:/ˈlɪərər/ (US), /ˈlɪərə/ (UK)| n.| 空的人；空的东西；空虚的人；欺骗者 |Someone or something that is empty or that deceives.  A person who is emotionally empty or unfulfilled can also be described as a leerer. It can also refer to someone who obtains money dishonestly.|The gold mine turned out to be a leerer, yielding nothing of value.  这个金矿结果是空的，什么值钱的东西也没出。|Synonyms: cheat, swindler, fraudster, trickster, deceiver, con artist, empty vessel, void |Antonyms: honest person, philanthropist, benefactor| Noun
