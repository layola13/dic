distill:/dɪˈstɪl/ | v. n. | 蒸馏；提取；提炼 | to purify a liquid by heating it until it becomes a gas and then cooling it back into a liquid; to extract the essential elements of something | They distill whisky from malted barley. 他们用麦芽大麦酿造威士忌。 | synonyms: purify, refine, extract, concentrate | antonyms: contaminate, pollute, adulterate | verb

distill:/dɪˈstɪl/ | n. | 蒸馏液 | the product of distillation | The distill was clear and potent. 蒸馏液清澈而浓烈。 | synonyms: essence, concentrate, extract | antonyms: mixture, blend | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *distiller, from Latin distillare "to drip down," from de- "down" + stillare "to drip," from stilla "a drop".
