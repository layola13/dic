out-work:/ˌaʊtˈwɜːrk/
verb
胜过工作；工作更努力
to work harder or more effectively than someone else
Example: He out-worked everyone else on the team. 他比队里其他任何人都更努力。

Example: She consistently out-works her colleagues, achieving impressive results. 她总是比同事工作更努力，取得了令人瞩目的成果。

近义词: outdo, surpass, outperform
反义词: underperform, lag behind
词性: verb
