concordat: /ˈkɒŋkɔːdæt/ (UK) /kənˈkɔːrdæt/ (US)| n. |和解，一致|A state of agreement or harmony.|The two sides finally reached a concordat after months of negotiations.|Synonyms: harmony, agreement, accord, unity|Antonyms: discord, disagreement, conflict|noun
