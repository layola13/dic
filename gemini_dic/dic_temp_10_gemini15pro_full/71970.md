flimflammer:/ˈflɪmˌflæmər/ | n. |骗子；江湖郎中|A person who swindles or cheats others, often using elaborate or deceptive schemes. | He was a known flimflammer, always coming up with get-rich-quick schemes. 他是个臭名昭著的骗子，总是想出各种快速致富的诡计。|  Synonyms: con artist, swindler, cheat, fraudster | Antonyms: honest person, trustworthy individual | noun

flimflam:/ˈflɪmˌflæm/ | n. |欺骗；胡言乱语 |Nonsense; deceitful or misleading talk. |  That's just a bunch of flimflam!  那只是一堆胡言乱语！| Synonyms:  nonsense, baloney, hogwash, claptrap | Antonyms: truth, honesty, fact | noun

flimflam:/ˈflɪmˌflæm/ | v. |欺骗；哄骗 |To swindle or cheat someone. | He flimflammed her out of her life savings. 他骗走了她所有的积蓄。| Synonyms: swindle, cheat, defraud, hoodwink | Antonyms: be honest, deal fairly | verb
