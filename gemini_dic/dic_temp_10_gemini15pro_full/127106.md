nationalizations:/ˌnæʃənələˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |国有化|The process of bringing privately owned businesses or industries under state control.|The post-war era saw widespread nationalizations of key industries.|Synonym: state ownership, collectivization|Antonym: privatization|Noun

nationalization:/ˌnæʃənələˈzeɪʃən/| n. |国有化|The act of a government taking control of a privately owned business or industry.|The nationalization of the railways was a controversial move.|Synonym: state takeover|Antonym: privatization|Noun
