hyperoxide:/ˌhaɪpərˈɒksaɪd/ /ˌhaɪpərˈɑːksaɪd/| n. |超氧化物|A compound containing the anion O₂⁻, formed by reaction of oxygen with alkali metals.  A highly reactive oxygen species.|Potassium hyperoxide is used in rebreathers. 超氧化钾用于呼吸器中。|近义词:superoxide|反义词:None|词性:noun
Etymology:  From *hyper-* (over, beyond) + *oxide*.
