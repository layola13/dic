generalizable:/ˈdʒɛn(ə)rəˌlaɪzəb(ə)l/ /ˈdʒɛnərəˌlaɪzəbəl/| adj.|可概括的，可推广的|Able to be made more general; applicable to a wider range of situations or circumstances.|The results of the study are generalizable to the wider population. 这项研究的结果可以推广到更广泛的人群。|Synonyms: applicable, universal, transferable, extrapolatable|Antonyms: specific, particular, unique, limited|adjective
