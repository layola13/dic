epigraphics:/ˌepɪˈɡræfɪks/ | n. | 碑文研究；金石学 | The study of inscriptions, especially those on stone or metal, as a means of understanding past cultures and events. |  The museum's new exhibit on epigraphics showcased ancient Greek inscriptions on pottery and stone.  博物馆关于碑文研究的新展览展示了古代希腊陶器和石头上的铭文。| 近义词: inscription studies, paleography | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps modern textual analysis) | 词性: noun
