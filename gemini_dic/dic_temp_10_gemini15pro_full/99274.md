interconnecting:/ˌɪntərˈkəˈnɛktɪŋ/
adjective
相互连接的，互相联系的
joining together different parts of something, especially a system or network
The rooms are interconnected by a series of passageways. 这些房间由一系列通道相互连接。
近义词: linked, joined, connected, networked
反义词: separate, disconnected, isolated
词性: adjective

interconnect:/ˌɪntərˈkəˈnɛkt/
verb
(使)互相连接，(使)互相联系
to connect similar things together
The two theories are closely interconnected. 这两种理论紧密相连。
近义词: link, join, connect, network
反义词: separate, disconnect, isolate
词性: verb


词源:
From inter- + connect.  "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between" or "among." "Connect" comes from the Latin "connectere," meaning "to bind together."
