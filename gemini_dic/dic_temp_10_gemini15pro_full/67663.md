extrudabilities:/ɪkˌstruːdəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可挤出性，挤出性能|The ability of a material to be extruded; how easily a material can be pushed through a die or opening to form a specific shape.  It often relates to viscosity and plasticity. |The extrudabilities of different polymer blends were compared in the study.  这项研究比较了不同聚合物共混物的可挤出性。|近义词：extrusion properties, flowability, moldability|反义词: non-extrudability (not a standard word), intractability|noun
