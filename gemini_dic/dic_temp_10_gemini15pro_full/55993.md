dns:/ˌdiː ɛnˈɛs/| n. |域名系统 (Domain Name System)|A hierarchical distributed naming system for computers, services, or other resources connected to the Internet or a private network. It associates various information with domain names assigned to each of the participating entities. Most prominently, it translates more readily memorized domain names to the numerical IP addresses needed for locating and identifying computer services and devices worldwide.|Example:  The DNS server wasn't responding, so I couldn't access the website.  域名服务器没有响应，所以我无法访问该网站。

Example: Configuring your DNS settings correctly is crucial for internet access. 正确配置 DNS 设置对于互联网访问至关重要。|近义词: domain name service, name server|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
