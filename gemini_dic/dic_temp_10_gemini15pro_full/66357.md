excusableness:/ɪkˈskjuːzəbəlnəs/
n.
可原谅性，可辩解性
The quality of being excusable; the fact of having a good excuse.
His excusableness for being late was accepted by the teacher. 他的迟到理由被老师接受了。
近义词：forgivability, justifiability, pardonableness
反义词：unforgivability, inexcusability
