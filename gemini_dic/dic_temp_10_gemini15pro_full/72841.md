foliosity:/foʊliˈeɪʃən/ /ˌfəʊliˈɒsəti/ | n. |叶理，[地质]面理；薄片状组织|The thin layering within metamorphic rock is called foliation. 变质岩中薄薄的层理被称为叶理。| The foliosity of the schist was clearly visible. 片岩的叶理清晰可见。 |近义词：cleavage, lamination, schistosity |反义词: massiveness |词性: noun

Etymology->
From Late Latin *foliōsitās, from Latin foliōsus (“leafy, full of leaves”), from folium (“leaf”).
