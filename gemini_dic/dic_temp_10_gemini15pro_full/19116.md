betulin:/ˈbɛtʃʊlɪn/ | n. |桦木醇|A crystalline triterpenoid alcohol, C30H50O2, obtained from the outer bark of birch trees, used in certain pharmaceuticals and in cosmetics.|Betulin has shown promise in some studies for its anti-inflammatory properties. 桦木醇在一些研究中显示出其抗炎特性。|Synonyms: betulinol, lup-20(29)-ene-3β,28-diol|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
