Indochina: /ˌɪndoʊˈtʃaɪnə/ | n. | 中南半岛 | A peninsula of southeastern Asia that includes Myanmar, Thailand, Laos, Cambodia, Vietnam, and parts of Malaysia. |  French Indochina was a federation of protectorates in Southeast Asia. 法属印度支那曾是东南亚的一个保护国联盟。

The term Indochina reflects the cultural influence of both India and China.  “印度支那”一词反映了印度和中国文化的影响。 |Synonym: Southeast Asia (broadly) | Antonym:  N/A (as it is a geographical term) | Noun
