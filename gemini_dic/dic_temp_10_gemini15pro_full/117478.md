medlar: /ˈmɛdlər/ (US), /ˈmɛdlə/ (UK)| n.|欧楂果，欧楂树|A small deciduous tree (Mespilus germanica) of the rose family, native to southwest Asia and southeastern Europe and widely naturalized elsewhere, having white flowers and a brownish-green fruit resembling a small apple.|The medlar fruits are hard and acidic when first picked but become edible after being softened, or "bletted," by frost or storage.|近义词:loquat (枇杷, though not botanically close)|反义词: N/A|noun

medlar: /ˈmɛdlər/ (US), /ˈmɛdlə/ (UK)| adj.|欧楂果的；欧楂树的|Relating to or made from the medlar fruit or tree.|Medlar jelly is a traditional preserve.|近义词: N/A|反义词: N/A|adjective
