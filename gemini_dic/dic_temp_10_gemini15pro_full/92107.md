hyperromantic:/ˌhaɪpərroʊˈmænɪk/ | adj. |过度浪漫的，极度浪漫主义的|Excessively or unrealistically romantic; characterized by an idealized or obsessive view of love.|She has a hyperromantic view of relationships, expecting them to be like something out of a fairytale.|近义词:excessively romantic, overly sentimental, starry-eyed, unrealistic|反义词:realistic, pragmatic, cynical|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"hyper-" (Greek): over, beyond, excessive
"romantic" (from Old French "romaunce," meaning a verse narrative): relating to romance, characterized by idealism and emotion
