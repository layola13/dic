liked: /laɪkt/
|v.| 喜欢；喜爱；喜欢做…；想；愿意 |  past tense of "like" - expressing enjoyment or approval of something or someone. | I liked the movie. 我喜欢这部电影。|Synonyms: enjoyed, appreciated, favored| Antonyms: disliked, hated, despised |verb


liked: /laɪkt/
|adj.| 受欢迎的；被喜欢的|Used as a past participle functioning as an adjective. Describes something or someone that was enjoyed or approved of. |The movie was well-liked by critics. 这部电影很受评论家们的喜爱。|Synonyms: popular, favored, well-received| Antonyms: unpopular, disliked, unfavored|adjective
