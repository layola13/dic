overvail: /ˌoʊvərˈveɪl/
verb
覆盖，遮蔽；笼罩，弥漫
to cover or conceal something completely
例句：A sense of gloom overvailed the city.  一种忧郁的气氛笼罩着整个城市。

例句：Mist overvailed the mountains.  薄雾笼罩着群山。

近义词：cover, envelop, shroud, obscure
反义词：reveal, uncover, expose
词性：v.
