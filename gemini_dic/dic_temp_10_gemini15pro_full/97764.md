initiatory:/ɪˈnɪʃiətɔːri/ /ɪˈnɪʃiətəri/| adj.|初步的，起始的，入会的|introducing or beginning something; relating to or characteristic of an initiation|例句：The initiatory phase of the project is complete. 项目的初始阶段已经完成。|例句：The initiatory rites of the ancient society were shrouded in secrecy.  古代社团的入会仪式笼罩在神秘之中。|近义词：introductory, preliminary, initial, beginning, inceptive|反义词：concluding, final, ending|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *initiātōrius, from Latin initiātus, past participle of initiāre (“to initiate”), from initium (“beginning”).
