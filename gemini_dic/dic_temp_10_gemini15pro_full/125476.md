multipolar:/ˌmʌltɪˈpoʊlər/ /ˌmʌltiˈpəʊlə(r)/| adj.|多极的，多极化的|Having multiple poles or centers of power or influence.|例句：The world is becoming increasingly multipolar. 世界正变得越来越多极化。

例句：The multipolar system of international relations is more complex than the bipolar system. 多极化的国际关系体系比两极体系更复杂。

近义词：polycentric, decentralized|反义词：unipolar, bipolar, centralized|词性: adjective
