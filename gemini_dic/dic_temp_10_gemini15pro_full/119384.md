microdensitometric:/ˌmaɪkroʊˌdɛnsɪtəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
显微光密度测定的
Relating to or using microdensitometry, a technique used to measure the optical density of very small areas, typically on photographic film or plates.
Microdensitometric analysis of the X-ray revealed subtle variations in bone density.  X射线的显微光密度分析揭示了骨密度的细微变化。

Microdensitometric measurements are crucial in quantifying DNA bands on electrophoresis gels. 显微光密度测量对于量化电泳凝胶上的 DNA 条带至关重要。

近义词: densitometric
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "qualitative" in some contexts)
词性: adjective
