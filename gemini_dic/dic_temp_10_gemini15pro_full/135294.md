nonpassionately:/ˌnɒnˈpæʃənətli/ /ˌnɑːnˈpæʃənətli/
adverb
冷漠地；不动感情地
Without passion or emotional involvement.
She nonpassionately delivered the news of the company's closure. 她冷漠地宣布了公司倒闭的消息。
He nonpassionately signed the divorce papers.  他毫无感情地签署了离婚文件。
Synonyms: apathetically, indifferently, dispassionately, coldly
Antonyms: passionately, fervently, enthusiastically, warmly
词性: 副词
