modulates:/ˈmɒdʒʊleɪts/ (UK), /ˈmɑːdʒəleɪts/ (US)| v. | 调节，调整；调制；变调 | to change the sound of your voice in order to create a particular effect, or to change something such as a radio wave or a signal in a similar way |  The singer modulated her voice to express a range of emotions. 歌手调节她的声音来表达各种情绪。 | synonyms: adjust, regulate, modify, vary, alter | antonyms: fix, maintain, stabilize | verb
