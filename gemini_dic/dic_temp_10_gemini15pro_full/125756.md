murder will out:/ˈmɜːrdər wɪl aʊt/
|proverb|谋杀终将败露;真相终将大白| This proverb means that murder, or any serious crime, will eventually be discovered, no matter how well it is concealed.  It expresses the belief that the truth cannot be hidden forever.|The police couldn't find any evidence, but they believed murder will out and the killer would eventually be caught. 警方找不到任何证据，但他们相信真相终将大白，凶手最终会被抓获。|近义词:truth will out, justice will prevail|反义词: (No direct antonym for a proverb, but concepts like "cover-up succeeds" or "injustice prevails" could be considered opposite ideas)|proverb
