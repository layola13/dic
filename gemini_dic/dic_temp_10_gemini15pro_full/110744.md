lingeringly:/ˈlɪŋɡərɪŋli/
adverb
缓慢地；迟延地；逗留地
In a slow, lingering manner; continuing to stay, especially when you should leave.
The scent of her perfume lingered lingeringly in the air. 她的香水味在空气中久久不散。
近义词: slowly, protractedly, hesitantly
反义词: quickly, abruptly, rapidly
词性: 副词

词源: From linger (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Linger" comes from Middle English *lingren, of uncertain origin, perhaps from Old Norse lengja "to lengthen."
