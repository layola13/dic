euhemeristic:/ˌjuːˈhɛmərɪstɪk/ | adj. |  释义论的，将神话解释为历史的 | Interpreting myths as traditional accounts of historical persons and events. |  Many scholars consider the story of Romulus and Remus to be euhemeristic, believing it to be based on real people whose lives became embellished over time. 许多学者认为罗慕路斯和雷穆斯的故事是释义论的，相信它是基于真实人物的生活，随着时间的推移而被美化。 |  mythological, allegorical | literal, historical | adjective

euhemerism:/ˌjuːˈhɛmərɪzəm/ | n. | 释义论 |  The interpretation of myths as traditional accounts of historical persons and events. |  Euhemerism offers a way to understand ancient myths in a historical context. 释义论提供了一种在历史背景下理解古代神话的方法。 | mythology, allegory | literalism, historicism | noun
