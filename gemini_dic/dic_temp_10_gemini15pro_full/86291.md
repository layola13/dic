heavying:/ˈhɛviɪŋ/| v. |加重，使沉重；使负担加重；使(局势、气氛等)变得沉重；|Making something heavier, either literally or figuratively.  The rain was heavying the branches of the trees. 雨水使树枝变得沉重。|例句：The snow was heavying the roof. 雪使屋顶的负担加重了。  The news of the accident heavied the atmosphere in the room. 事故的消息使房间里的气氛变得沉重。|近义词：burdening, weighting, weighing down|反义词：lightening, easing|词性:动词

词源: From the adjective "heavy" + the suffix "-ing" forming a present participle, which can function as a verb.
