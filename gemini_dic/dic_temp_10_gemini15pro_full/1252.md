accord:/əˈkɔːrd/ (US) /əˈkɔːd/ (UK)| n. v.|一致，符合；给予，授予；协议|An agreement or harmony between people or things.  To give or grant something to someone.|
例句：The two sides reached an accord on the trade agreement. 双方就贸易协定达成了一致。

例句：The committee accorded him the highest honor. 委员会授予了他最高荣誉。

近义词：agreement, harmony, consensus, grant, bestow|
反义词：disagreement, conflict, discord, withhold|
词性：noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "acorder" (to agree, be in harmony), from Latin "accordāre"  (to be of one heart, to agree), from "ad-" (to) + "cor" (heart).  The noun sense developed from the verb.


USAGE->

过去完成时:

(Noun - Agreement/Harmony)

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
