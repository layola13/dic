disseizure:/dɪˈsiːʒər/ /dɪˈsiːʒʊr/| n. |非法剥夺；夺取；侵占|The act of wrongfully depriving someone of something, especially real property.|The disseizure of his lands left him penniless.  非法剥夺他的土地使他身无分文。|Synonyms: expropriation, confiscation, usurpation, eviction|Antonyms: restitution, restoration|noun
