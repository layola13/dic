overfondle: /ˌoʊvərˈfɑːndəl/ | v. | 过分爱抚，过分抚摸 | To fondle excessively or inappropriately. | He was accused of overfondling the child. 他被指控过分抚摸那个孩子。| The puppy was overfondled by the children. 小狗被孩子们过分抚摸了。 | 近义词: caress excessively, paw, maul | 反义词: neglect, ignore | 词性: 动词

词源: "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "fondle" (verb meaning "to caress lovingly").  From "over-" (Old English *ofer) + "fondle" (17th century, probably from "fond," meaning "foolishly affectionate").
