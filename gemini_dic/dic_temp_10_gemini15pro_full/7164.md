angelus:/ˈændʒələs/| n.|三钟经; 安吉鲁斯钟声|A Catholic devotion commemorating the Incarnation by thrice daily reciting the Hail Mary.|The church bells rang the Angelus.|近义词:prayer, devotion|反义词:blasphemy|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *angelus, from Ecclesiastical Latin angelus (“angel, messenger”), from Ancient Greek ἄγγελος (ággelos, “messenger”). The devotion is named after the opening words of the prayer, Angelus Domini nuntiavit Mariæ ("The Angel of the Lord declared unto Mary").
