manically:/məˈnɪkəli/| adv.|狂躁地；疯狂地；着魔地|In a frenzied, wild, or obsessive manner, characteristic of mania.|She scrubbed the floor manically. 她疯狂地擦洗地板。|Synonyms: frantically, hysterically, obsessively, wildly|Antonyms: calmly, rationally, sanely|词性: 副词

词源:
From the adjective "manic" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Manic" comes from the Greek "manikos," meaning "mad, insane," related to "mania" (madness).
