overcount:/ˌoʊvərˈkaʊnt/| v. n.|多算，重复计算，过高估计|To count something more times than necessary, resulting in a total that is too high.  The cashier overcounted the items and I was overcharged. 收银员多算了商品，我被多收了钱。|近义词：overestimate, miscalculate, exaggerate|反义词: undercount, underestimate|verb
overcount:/ˌoʊvərˈkaʊnt/| n.|多算，重复计算，过高估计|The act of counting something more times than necessary, leading to an inflated total. There was an overcount in the inventory, and we thought we had more stock than we actually did. 库存盘点有多算，我们以为比实际库存要多。|近义词：overestimation, miscalculation, exaggeration|反义词: undercount, underestimation|noun
