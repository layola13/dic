overtalking: /ˌoʊvərˈtɔːkɪŋ/ | v. | 打断别人说话，抢话 | The habit of interrupting others while they are speaking. |  He has a habit of overtalking, which makes it difficult to have a conversation with him. 他有一个抢话的习惯，这使得很难和他进行对话。| Synonyms: interrupting, cutting off, talking over | Antonyms: listening, being quiet | Verb
