bacchius: /ˈbækiəs/ | n. | 长短格; 扬抑格 | A metrical foot consisting of a short syllable followed by two long syllables, or an unstressed syllable followed by two stressed syllables. |  The poem is written in bacchius. 这首诗是用扬抑格写的。 |  amphibrach, antibacchius |  -- | noun
