lovebird:/ˈlʌvbɜːrd/ (US), /ˈlʌvbɜːd/ (UK)| n. |爱情鸟，鹦鹉的一种；（尤指）情侣|A small parrot of a kind that shows great affection for its mate, often used to refer to two people who are obviously very much in love. |例句：The two lovebirds were inseparable. 这对情侣形影不离。|例句：They kept lovebirds as pets. 他们养爱情鸟作为宠物。|近义词：sweetheart, darling, couple|反义词:enemy, rival|noun
