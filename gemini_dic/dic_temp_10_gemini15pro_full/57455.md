draffish:/ˈdræfɪʃ/
adjective
粗劣的；难吃的；渣滓的；无味的
Characterized by dregs or refuse; worthless; stale and tasteless.
The beer tasted draffish, like it had been sitting out too long. 这啤酒尝起来很差，像是放了太久。
The draffish coffee made him regret skipping breakfast. 这难喝的咖啡让他后悔没吃早餐。
近义词: stale, insipid, vapid, tasteless, flat, worthless
反义词: delicious, flavorful, savory, tasty, palatable
词性: adjective

draff:/dræf/
noun
糟粕；渣滓；酒糟
The refuse, dregs, or waste from brewing or distilling; hogwash.
The pigs eagerly ate the draff from the brewery. 猪们贪婪地吃着啤酒厂的酒糟。
He considered the politician's speech to be mere draff. 他认为这位政客的演讲纯粹是废话。
近义词: dregs, lees, refuse, waste, hogwash
反义词: essence, cream
词性: noun
