outgrew:/ˌaʊtˈɡruː/
verb
长大而不适于；超过…的范围；发展得比…快
to grow too large for (something, such as clothing); to grow taller or bigger than
She outgrew her clothes so quickly. 她长得太快，衣服很快就穿不上了。
He soon outgrew his childish fears. 他很快就摆脱了童年时的恐惧。
The company outgrew its office space. 公司发展壮大，原有的办公空间不够用了。
surpass, exceed, outstrip
lag behind, fall behind
词性：动词
