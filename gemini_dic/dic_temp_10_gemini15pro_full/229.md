abderite:/ˈæbdəraɪt/| n.|愚人，傻瓜|A foolish or stupid person.  Derived from Abdera, an ancient Greek city whose inhabitants were stereotyped as simple-minded despite producing several prominent philosophers and poets.|He acted like a complete abderite, believing everything he read online.|近义词：fool, idiot, simpleton, dunce|反义词:genius, sage|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Abdera (Ἄβδηρα), an ancient Greek city in Thrace.  The inhabitants of Abdera were stereotyped as simple-minded, despite the city being the birthplace of several prominent philosophers, including Democritus and Protagoras. This stereotype likely arose from comedic portrayals in Athenian plays.
