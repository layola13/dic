disapprover:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːvər/ | n. |不赞成者，反对者|Someone who disapproves of something or someone. |例句：The disapprovers of the new policy voiced their concerns. 新政策的反对者表达了他们的担忧。

例句：He was a vocal disapprover of the war. 他是战争的直言不讳的反对者。


近义词：critic, objector, opponent |反义词: supporter, advocate, proponent |词性:noun
