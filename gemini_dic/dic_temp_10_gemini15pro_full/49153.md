dejectly: /dɪˈdʒɛktli/
adverb
沮丧地，垂头丧气地
in a dejected manner; sadly; showing low spirits.
She walked dejectly out of the room after hearing the bad news. 她听到坏消息后垂头丧气地走出了房间。
dejectedly
近义词: sadly, gloomily, despondently, dispiritedly
反义词: cheerfully, happily, joyfully
词性: adverb

deject: /dɪˈdʒɛkt/
verb
使沮丧，使灰心
to make someone feel sad and lose hope.
The news of the defeat dejected the entire team.  失败的消息使整个团队都垂头丧气。
近义词: discourage, dishearten, depress
反义词: encourage, hearten, cheer
词性: verb

词源:  From Latin dēiectus (“cast down”), past participle of dēicere (“to throw down”), from dē (“down, away from”) + iacere (“to throw”).
