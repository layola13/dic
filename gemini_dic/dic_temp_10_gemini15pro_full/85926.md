headlined:/ˈhedˌlaɪnd/ | v. | 作标题；担任主角；| to feature as the main performer or event | The festival was headlined by a famous rock band.  这个节日由一个著名的摇滚乐队领衔主演。| starred, featured, topped | supported | verb
headlined:/ˈhedˌlaɪnd/ | adj. | 头条新闻的；被作为头条新闻报道的 | featured as a headline | The headlined story was about the royal wedding.  头条新闻是关于皇室婚礼的。 | leading, main, top | minor, insignificant | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From head + line.  Originally referred to the top line of a page or article, which carried the main news.
