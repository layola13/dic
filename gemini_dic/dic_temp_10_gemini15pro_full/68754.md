farmyards:/ˈfɑːmjɑːdz/ | n. | 农场院子；农家庭院 | An area surrounded by farm buildings.  A farmyard is an outdoor area surrounded by or next to farm buildings, often containing various features such as barns, stables, silos, and storage sheds. It typically serves as a central hub for farm operations, where animals may be kept, machinery is stored, and crops are processed. | The children loved visiting their grandparents' farmyard and seeing all the animals. 孩子们喜欢去祖父母的农家庭院，看所有的动物。

The farmyard was bustling with activity as the farmers prepared for the harvest.  随着农民们准备收割，农家庭院里一片繁忙景象。|  Synonyms: barnyard, farmyard, farm, homestead | Antonyms: city, metropolis | noun
