overrates:/ˌoʊvərˈreɪts/ | v. | 高估；过高评价 | To estimate the value or importance of something or someone too highly. | He overrates his own abilities. 他高估了自己的能力。 | Example: The critic overrates that new movie; it's not that good.  评论家高估了那部新电影；它没那么好。| Synonyms: overestimate, overvalue, exaggerate | Antonyms: underestimate, undervalue, downplay | Verb


overrate:/ˌoʊvərˈreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 被高估的 | Rated or valued too highly. | The restaurant is overrated; the food is mediocre. 这家餐馆被高估了；食物很一般。| Example: Many people think that artist is overrated. 很多人认为那位艺术家被高估了。 | Synonyms: overestimated, overvalued | Antonyms: underestimated, undervalued | Adjective
