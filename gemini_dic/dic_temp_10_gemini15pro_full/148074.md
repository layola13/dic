oversaturations:/ˌoʊvərˌsætʃəˈreɪʃənz/
noun
过度饱和；过饱和状态
The state of being oversaturated; an excessive amount of something.
The market suffered from an oversaturations of cheap products.  市场上充斥着廉价产品。
