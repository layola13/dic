amortising:/ˈæmərtaɪzɪŋ/ (US), /ˈæmətaɪzɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|摊销的，分期偿还的|The process of gradually writing off the initial cost of an asset over a period. This is done by expensing a portion of the asset's cost each year until the entire cost has been expensed.|The company is amortising the cost of the new equipment over five years.  这家公司正在五年内摊销新设备的成本。|Synonyms: depreciating, expensing, writing off|Antonyms: appreciating, increasing in value|adjective

amortize:/ˈæmərtaɪz/ (US), /ˈæmətaɪz/ (UK)| v.|摊销，分期偿还|To gradually reduce or write off the cost of an asset over a period.|The loan will be amortized over 15 years. 这笔贷款将在15年内摊销。|Synonyms: depreciate, expense, write off|Antonyms: appreciate, increase in value|verb
