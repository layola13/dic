nontropically:/nɒnˈtrɒpɪkəli/| adv.|非热带地，非回归线地|Not in a tropical manner; outside the tropics.  Referring to regions or climates that are not tropical, characterized by moderate temperatures and distinct seasons.|例句：The plants grow nontropically, thriving in cooler climates. 这些植物非热带生长，在较凉爽的气候中茁壮成长。

例句：Nontropically adapted species often struggle in the humid heat. 非热带适应的物种通常在潮湿的炎热环境中挣扎。|近义词：extratropically, temperately|反义词:tropically|词性:副词
