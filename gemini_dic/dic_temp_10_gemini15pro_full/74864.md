frescade:/frɪˈskɑːdeɪ/ | n. | 清凉饮料，冰镇饮料 | A cool, refreshing drink, often made with fruit juice, sugar, and water. |  例句：On a hot summer day, a fresca is the perfect thirst quencher. 炎炎夏日，一杯清凉饮料是解渴的最佳选择。

例句：She made a delicious pineapple fresca for the picnic. 她为野餐做了一杯美味的菠萝清凉饮料。| 近义词：cooler, beverage, refresher | 反义词: hot drink | 词性: 名词
