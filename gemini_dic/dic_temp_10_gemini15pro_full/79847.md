glyptographic:/ˌɡlɪptəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
雕刻的，雕刻文字的
Relating to or consisting of carved or engraved writing or symbols.
The archaeologist discovered glyptographic inscriptions on the ancient tomb. 考古学家在古墓中发现了雕刻的铭文。

The museum houses a collection of glyptographic seals from the Mesopotamian era.  博物馆收藏了美索不达米亚时代的雕刻印章。
synonym: engraved, inscribed, carved
antonym: plain, smooth, uncarved
词性: adjective
