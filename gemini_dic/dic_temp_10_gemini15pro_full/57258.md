downlooked: /ˌdaʊnˈlʊkt/
Verb
被轻视的，被忽视的
looked down on; treated as unimportant.
He felt downlooked by his colleagues because of his humble background.  他因为出身卑微而感到被同事轻视。
Synonyms: disregarded, slighted, snubbed, overlooked, condescended to
Antonyms: respected, esteemed, valued, appreciated
词性: 动词(过去分词形式，用作形容词)


ETYMOLOGY->
From "down" + "look," literally meaning "looked down upon."  The prefix "down-" indicates a lower position or status, and "look" refers to the act of seeing or considering.  Therefore, "downlooked" describes someone who is seen as inferior or unimportant.
