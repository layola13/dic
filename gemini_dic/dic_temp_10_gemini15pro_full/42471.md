counterfeitness:/ˈkaʊntərˌfɪtnəs/| n.|伪造；假冒；赝品性质|The counterfeitness of the documents was immediately apparent. 这些文件的伪造性质立刻显现出来。|近义词：falsity, spuriousness, forgery|反义词：genuineness, authenticity|词性:名词

Etymology:
From counterfeit + -ness.  Counterfeit itself comes from Old French contrefait, past participle of contrefaire "to imitate, copy, forge," from contre- "against" + faire "to do, make."
