claro:/ˈklɑːroʊ/ (US), /ˈklɑːrəʊ/ (UK)| n.|雪茄的一种|A type of cigar|He lit a claro and sat back in his chair. 他点燃了一支雪茄，坐回到椅子上。|Synonyms: cigar|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish claro, from Latin clārus (“clear, bright, loud”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱlew- (“to hear”).
