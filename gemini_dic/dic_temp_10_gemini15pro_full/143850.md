outplans:/ˌaʊtˈplænz/
verb
制定比...更周密的计划; 超越...的计划
to make more and better plans than someone else
例句：She completely outplanned her competitors and secured the deal. 她彻底地比竞争对手计划得更周密，从而拿下了这笔交易。

例句：He outplanned everyone in the chess tournament and won easily.  他在国际象棋比赛中比所有人都计划得更周密，轻松获胜。


近义词：outsmart, outmaneuver, surpass
反义词：be outplanned, be outsmarted
