markman:/ˈmɑːkmən/ | n. | 标志人；记分员；射手；市场商人 |A person who marks, records, or identifies something.  Historically, a markman was often a skilled archer or shooter.  In modern usage, it can refer to someone who scores or keeps track of results in a competition, or someone who identifies and targets a particular market segment.| The markman carefully aimed at the target.  射手小心地瞄准目标。| synonyms: scorer, recorder, shooter, sniper, marksman, targeter | antonyms: observer, spectator | noun
