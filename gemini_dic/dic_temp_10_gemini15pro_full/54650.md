disobedience:/ˌdɪsəˈbiːdiəns/ |n.|不服从，违抗|The child's disobedience led to punishment.  这孩子的违抗行为导致了惩罚。|Refusal to obey rules or orders; the act of being disobedient.  拒绝服从规则或命令；不服从的行为。|Her act of disobedience was a cry for help. 她的不服从行为是呼救。|近义词：defiance, insubordination, noncompliance|反义词：obedience, compliance, submission|词性：名词

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + obedience (from Old French obeissance).  来自dis-（表示否定）+ obedience（来自古法语obeissance）。
