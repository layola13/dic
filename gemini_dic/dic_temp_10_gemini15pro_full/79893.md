gnatlike:/ˈnætˌlaɪk/
adjective
像蚊蚋的；微不足道的
Resembling a gnat, especially in being small and insignificant.
The gnatlike drones buzzed incessantly around our heads.  这些像蚊蚋一样的无人机在我们头顶上不停地嗡嗡作响。
His worries seemed gnatlike compared to the larger problems facing the world. 与世界面临的更大问题相比，他的担忧显得微不足道。
synonyms: tiny, minuscule, insignificant, trifling
antonyms: huge, enormous, significant, important
词性: 形容词
