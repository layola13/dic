militaristic:/ˌmɪlɪtəˈrɪstɪk/
adjective
军国主义的; 好战的
Glorifying military power and values; advocating an aggressive military policy.
The country had a militaristic culture, valuing strength and discipline above all else.  这个国家有着军国主义文化，崇尚力量和纪律高于一切。
The general made a militaristic speech, advocating for increased military spending. 将军发表了军国主义的讲话，主张增加军费开支。
近义词:hawkish, bellicose, warmongering, jingoistic
反义词:pacifistic, dovish, peace-loving
词性:adjective
词源:From military + -istic.
