maikong:/ˈmaɪkɒŋ/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkɔːŋ/ (US)| n.| 麦孔河 (湄公河)|The Mekong, or Mekong River, is a trans-boundary river in East Asia and Southeast Asia. It is the world's twelfth longest river and the seventh longest in Asia.|The Mekong Delta in Vietnam is a fertile agricultural region. 越南的湄公河三角洲是一个肥沃的农业区。|Synonyms: Mekong River|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
