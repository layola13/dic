infringers:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒərz/
n.
侵权者
People who violate a law, agreement, or right.
The company is taking legal action against copyright infringers.  这家公司正在对侵犯版权者采取法律行动。
synonyms: violators, transgressors, offenders
antonyms: conformists, adherents, followers
