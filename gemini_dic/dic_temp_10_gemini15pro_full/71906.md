词源:  From Late Latin *flexibilis, from Latin flectere "to bend" + -ibilis "-ible".


注意事项:  While "flexibles" can technically be used as a plural noun referring to flexible things, it is much less common and often sounds awkward.  Using the singular adjective "flexible" with a plural noun is generally preferred.
