matter:/ˈmætər/ (US) /ˈmætə(r)/ (UK)| n. vi.|物质；事情；问题；麻烦；重要；要紧|Something that is composed of physical material; a subject under consideration; a difficulty or problem; be of importance; have significance.|例句：The physical properties of matter. 物质的物理属性。|近义词：substance, material, issue, affair, problem, concern|反义词：nothing, immateriality|词性:名词,动词

matter:/ˈmætər/ (US) /ˈmætə(r)/ (UK)| vi.|要紧；有关系|Be important or significant.|例句：It doesn't matter what you wear. 你穿什么都无所谓。|近义词：count, signify|反义词：insignificant|词性:动词
