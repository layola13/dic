bootlegged:/ˈbuːtˌleɡd/ /ˈbuːtˌlegd/
adjective
走私的，非法制造的，盗版的
Made, copied, or sold illegally.

例句：
He was caught selling bootlegged DVDs. 他被抓到贩卖盗版DVD。

He was arrested for selling bootlegged alcohol. 他因贩卖私酒而被捕。


近义词：
illegal, illicit, contraband, pirated, counterfeit

反义词：
legal, legitimate, authentic

词性
adjective
