lockstep: /ˈlɑːkˌstɛp/ (US) /ˈlɒkˌstɛp/ (UK) | n. adv. adj. | 锁步，步伐一致 | a way of operating or moving in which you are forced to do everything in the same way and at the same time as someone else | The two companies are in lockstep on this issue. 这两家公司在这个问题上步调一致。|近义词：synchrony, unison, harmony, concord, agreement |反义词：discord, disagreement, disharmony | 词性: 名词, 副词, 形容词

lockstep: /ˈlɑːkˌstɛp/ (US) /ˈlɒkˌstɛp/ (UK)| adv. |  步伐一致地 | in a rigid, uniform manner | The soldiers marched in lockstep. 士兵们步伐一致地前进。|近义词：uniformly, consistently, regularly |反义词：irregularly, inconsistently, erratically | 词性: 副词
