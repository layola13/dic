culturing:/ˈkʌltʃərɪŋ/
n.|培养; 栽培; 养殖|The process of growing living organisms, tissues, or cells in a controlled environment.|例句：The culturing of bacteria in the lab requires sterile conditions. 实验室细菌培养需要无菌条件。|近义词：cultivation, growing, breeding|反义词:killing, destroying|词性:名词

culturing:/ˈkʌltʃərɪŋ/
gerund or present participle of culture|培养; 栽培; 养殖|The act of developing or improving something, such as the mind, tastes, or manners.|例句：Culturing a love of reading in children is crucial. 在孩子心中培养对阅读的热爱至关重要。|近义词：developing, fostering, nurturing|反义词:neglecting, hindering|词性:动名词/现在分词
