offuscation:/ˌɒfəsˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 混淆；模糊；昏迷 | The deliberate obfuscation of the truth.  对真相的故意混淆。 | The lawyer’s explanation served only to obfuscate the matter further. 律师的解释只会使事情更加混乱。 | 近义词: confusion, obscuring, clouding | 反义词: clarification, elucidation | 词性: 名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin *obfuscatio(n-), from Latin obfuscare ‘darken’, from ob- ‘over’ + fuscare ‘make dark’, from fuscus ‘dark’.
