multimotors:/ˈmʌltiˌmoʊtərz/ (US) /ˈmʌltiˌməʊtəz/ (UK)| n.|多引擎飞机，多马达飞机|An aircraft with multiple engines.|例句：The multimotors were grounded due to the storm. 由于暴风雨，多引擎飞机停飞了。|例句：Multimotors are preferred for long-haul flights due to their increased reliability. 多引擎飞机因其更高的可靠性而成为长途飞行的首选。|近义词：multi-engine aircraft|反义词: single-engine aircraft|词性: 名词

词源:
"multi-" (prefix): from Latin "multus" meaning "many, much".
"motor": from Latin "motor" meaning "mover," agent noun from past participle stem of "movere" meaning "to move."
"-s" (suffix): plural marker.
