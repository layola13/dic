一般将来时 (Simple Future):
He'll go to the store tomorrow. 他明天会去商店。
He'll finish the project next week. 他下周会完成这个项目。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
He'll be working on the report this time tomorrow. 明天这个时候他会在写报告。
He'll be traveling to Europe next summer.  明年夏天他将去欧洲旅行。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
He'll have finished the book by the end of the month. 他会在月底前看完这本书。
He'll have moved to a new apartment by then. 到那时他将已经搬到新公寓了。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
He'll have been living in London for five years by next July. 到明年七月，他将在伦敦住了五年了。
