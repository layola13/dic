ontogenic:/ˌɒntəˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
个体发生的，个体发育的
Relating to ontogeny, the development of an individual organism from its earliest stage to maturity.
The researchers studied the ontogenic development of the nervous system in mice. 研究人员研究了小鼠神经系统的个体发育。
The ontogenic changes in the bird's plumage were documented over a period of six months.  这只鸟羽毛的个体发育变化在六个月的时间里被记录下来。
Synonyms: developmental, maturational
Antonyms: phylogenetic
