aeroponic: /ˌeɪrəˈpɒnɪk/ (US) /ˌɛːrəˈpɒnɪk/ (UK)| adj.| 气栽的，无土栽培的|A method of growing plants in an air or mist environment without soil or an aggregate medium.|The farmer experimented with aeroponic cultivation of strawberries.  这位农民尝试了草莓的气栽法。

Aeroponic systems use mist to deliver nutrients directly to the roots. 气栽系统使用喷雾将营养物质直接输送到根部。|近义词: hydroponic, soilless|反义词: geoponic, soil-based|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek ἀήρ (aḗr, “air”) + πόνος (pónos, “labor, toil”). Coined in 1942 by W. Carter.  源自希腊语 ἀήρ (aḗr, “空气”) + πόνος (pónos, “劳动，辛苦”)。1942 年由 W. Carter 创造。
