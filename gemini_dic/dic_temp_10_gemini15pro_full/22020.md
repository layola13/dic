boisterous: /ˈbɔɪstərəs/ (US) /ˈbɔɪstrəs/ (UK)
adjective
喧闹的； boisterous
Noisy, energetic, and cheerful; rowdy.
The pub was filled with a boisterous crowd. 酒馆里挤满了喧闹的人群。
The children were boisterous during the party. 孩子们在聚会上很喧闹。
近义词: loud, noisy, rowdy, rambunctious, uproarious
反义词: quiet, calm, peaceful, subdued
词性: adjective

boisterous:
词源:  From Middle English *boistous, borrowed from Anglo-Norman boistous, alteration of Old French boisteus (“rough, clumsy, proud, arrogant”).  Ultimately from a Germanic source akin to Old High German *bōst (“boasting, arrogance”).
