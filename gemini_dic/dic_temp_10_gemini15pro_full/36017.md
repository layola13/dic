codify:/ˈkəʊdɪfaɪ/ /ˈkoʊdəˌfaɪ/| vt.|编纂；将…编成法典；整理|To arrange (laws or rules) into a systematic code. To arrange something, such as a set of rules, into a system.|The government has codified the rules relating to environmental protection. 政府已将与环境保护相关的规则编纂成法典。|The company decided to codify their best practices into a manual. 公司决定将其最佳实践整理成手册。|synonyms: systematize, organize, formulate, arrange, compile|antonyms: disorganize, scatter, disorder|verb

codification:/ˌkəʊdɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ /ˌkoʊdəfəˈkeɪʃn/| n.|编纂；法律汇编|The process of arranging laws or rules into a systematic code.
