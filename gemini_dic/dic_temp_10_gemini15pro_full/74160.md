fountain:/ˈfaʊntən/ (US), /ˈfaʊntɪn/ (UK)| n.|喷泉; 泉水; 源泉; 根源|A structure that sends water up into the air, or the water itself.  A source of something.|例句：The children were playing in the fountain. 孩子们在喷泉里玩耍。

例句：Knowledge is the fountain of youth. 知识是青春的源泉。|近义词：spring, well, source, origin|反义词:sink, drain|noun
