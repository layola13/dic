enepidermic:/ɛnˌɛpɪˈdɜːrmɪk/
adjective
表皮上的；作用于表皮的
Applied or acting on the skin; not penetrating below the epidermis.
This enepidermic cream soothes sunburns quickly.  这种表皮药膏能快速舒缓晒伤。
The lotion is for enepidermic use only.  这款乳液仅供表皮使用。
近义词:cutaneous, dermal, topical
反义词:hypodermic, subcutaneous
词性:adjective
词源:From Greek *ἐπενεπιδερμικός (epenepidermikos), from ἐπί (epi, “upon”) + ἐπιδερμικός (epidermikos, “of the epidermis”).
