braun:/braʊn/ (US), /braʊn/ (UK)| adj.|棕色的，褐色的|Of a dark yellowish-brown color.|例句：She has braun hair. 她有一头棕色的头发。|例句：The braun bear is a powerful animal. 棕熊是一种强壮的动物。|近义词：brown, chestnut, auburn|反义词：blonde, light, white|词性:形容词

词源：From German *braun*, from Proto-Germanic *brūnaz* (“brown”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰerH₁- (“bright; brown”). Cognate with Dutch *bruin*, West Frisian *brún*, English *brown*, Danish *brun*, Swedish *brun*, Icelandic *brúnn*.
