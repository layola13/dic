abolish:/əˈbɒlɪʃ/ (UK) /əˈbɑːlɪʃ/ (US)| vt.|废除，取消|To formally put an end to a system, practice, or institution.|The government abolished the death penalty in 1965. 政府在1965年废除了死刑。

The company abolished its traditional dress code. 公司废除了传统的着装规定。

近义词：eliminate, eradicate, extinguish, terminate, annul, repeal, revoke, nullify|反义词:establish, institute, enact, restore|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government abolishes outdated laws. = 政府废除过时的法律。
The company abolishes unnecessary paperwork. = 公司取消不必要的文书工作。

一般过去时:
They abolished slavery in the 19th century. = 他们在19世纪废除了奴隶制。
The school abolished corporal punishment years ago. = 学校多年前就废除了体罚。

现在完成时:
Many countries have abolished the death penalty. = 许多国家已经废除了死刑。
The organization has abolished several discriminatory practices. = 该组织已经废除了几项歧视性做法。

过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, they had abolished the rule. = 在我到达之前，他们已经废除了这条规则。
The committee had abolished the policy before the complaints arose. = 在投诉出现之前，委员会已经废除了这项政策。

将来时:
The council will abolish the parking restrictions next month. = 议会下个月将取消停车限制。
They will abolish this tax next year. = 他们明年将取消这项税收。

虚拟语气:
If they abolished tuition fees, more people would attend university. = 如果他们取消学费，会有更多人上大学。
I wish they would abolish this pointless regulation. = 我希望他们能废除这项毫无意义的规定。

被动语态:
The old law was abolished last year. = 旧法律去年被废除了。
The policy is being abolished due to public pressure. = 由于公众压力，这项政策正在被废除。

疑问句:
Why did they abolish that custom? = 他们为什么废除那个习俗？
When will they abolish these restrictions? = 他们什么时候取消这些限制？

否定句:
They didn't abolish the rule completely. = 他们没有完全废除这条规则。
The government hasn't abolished the tax yet. = 政府还没有取消这项税收。
