criers:/ˈkraɪərz/ | n. | 哭泣者；叫卖者；宣告者 | People who cry; those who shout out announcements or advertisements. | The town criers announced the king's arrival. 城镇的宣告员宣布了国王的到来。|  criers announced the latest news. 宣告员宣布了最新的消息。 | synonyms: weepers, mourners, hawkers, proclaimers | antonyms: laughers, rejoicers | noun
