cottoid:/ˈkɒtɔɪd/ | adj. n. |  鲉形的；鲉形目鱼类 | Relating to or resembling a cottid fish; a fish of the Cottidae family. |  例句：The cottoid fish are found in cold waters.  鲉形目鱼类生活在寒冷的水域。

例句：This species exhibits typical cottoid characteristics.  这个物种展现出典型的鲉形目特征。| 近义词：sculpin, bullhead | 反义词:  (无明确反义词) | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:  From Cottus (the type genus of the Cottidae family) + -oid (suffix meaning "resembling" or "like").  Cottus derives from the Greek word "kottós," referring to a type of fish.
