herbarial:/hɜːrˈbeəriəl/ | adj. | 植物标本的；与植物标本有关的 | Relating to or consisting of a herbarium or plant specimens. |  The museum has a vast herbarial collection.  博物馆有一个巨大的植物标本收藏。 |  botanical, plant-related | N/A | adjective

herbaria:/hɜːrˈbeəriə/ | n. | 植物标本室；植物标本集 | A collection of dried plant specimens preserved for scientific study. |  Researchers often visit herbaria to identify unknown plant species. 研究人员经常访问植物标本室来识别未知的植物种类。 | herbarium (singular) | N/A | noun


Etymology:  From Latin *herbarium*  "of herbs,"  neuter of *herbarius* "of herbs," from *herba* "herb, grass."
