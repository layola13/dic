noncorrective:/ˌnɒnkəˈrɛktɪv/
adjective
非矫正性的，非纠正性的
Relating to or denoting treatment that does not attempt to correct or cure a condition, but rather manages its symptoms or prevents it from worsening.
The doctor recommended noncorrective surgery to alleviate the pain. 医生建议进行非矫正手术来缓解疼痛。
His eyesight requires noncorrective measures like wearing sunglasses. 他的视力需要非矫正措施，比如戴太阳镜。
近义词: palliative, supportive, non-curative
反义词: corrective, remedial, curative
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not" or "opposite of"
corrective (adjective): derived from the verb "correct," ultimately from Latin "corrigere" (to set right).
