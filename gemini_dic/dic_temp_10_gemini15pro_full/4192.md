airfields:/ˈɛərfildz/ | n. |飞机场，机场群|A level area, usually paved, used for the takeoff and landing of aircraft.  It typically includes facilities for sheltering and maintaining aircraft. |The pilot landed the plane safely on the airfield. 飞行员安全地将飞机降落在机场。|The country has a network of military airfields. 这个国家拥有一个军事机场网络。| 近义词：airport, airstrip, landing field | 反义词: None | Noun
