indignance:/ɪnˈdɪɡnəns/| n.|愤怒; 愤慨; 义愤|A feeling of anger or annoyance provoked by what is perceived as unfair treatment.|She expressed her indignation at the treatment of the refugees. 她对难民的待遇表示愤慨。|近义词：resentment, anger, fury, outrage|反义词: contentment, pleasure, satisfaction|noun
