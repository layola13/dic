overcloys:/ˌoʊvərˈklɔɪz/
verb
使过饱；使腻烦；使生腻
to make someone feel that they have had too much of something pleasant, especially rich food or sweet drinks
The rich dessert overcloyed her palate. 这道浓郁的甜点让她感到腻味。
satiate, surfeit, glut
stimulate, excite
词性:v.
