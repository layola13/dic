enfleurage:/ɑ̃ˌflœˈʁaʒ/ |n.| 花香吸附法; 脂吸法 |A process in which fragrant materials, especially flowers, are placed on layers of purified fat to absorb their scent, which is then extracted with alcohol to produce perfume.|The perfumer used the traditional method of enfleurage to capture the delicate aroma of the jasmine blossoms.| 近义词: extraction, maceration | 反义词: N/A | noun
