edileship:/ˈiːdaɪlʃɪp/
n.
市政官的职位；市政官的任期
The office or tenure of an edile.
His edileship was marked by several important public works projects. 他的市政官任期以几项重要的公共工程项目为标志。

Etymology:  From Latin *aedilis* (“aedile”) + *-ship*.
近义词: magistracy, office
反义词: (None, as it's a specific office)
词性: noun
