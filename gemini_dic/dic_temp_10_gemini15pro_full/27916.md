canter:/ˈkæntər/| vi. n.|慢跑，轻快地跑；慢跑|The horse cantered across the field.  马慢跑穿过田野。|例句：She cantered her horse along the beach. 她骑着马沿着海滩慢跑。|近义词：trot, lope|反义词:gallop, stand|词性:动词/名词

词源:  From the Middle English *canteren*, probably influenced by Canterbury pilgrims riding at a moderate pace.  源自中古英语 *canteren*，可能受到坎特伯雷朝圣者以中等速度骑行的影响。
