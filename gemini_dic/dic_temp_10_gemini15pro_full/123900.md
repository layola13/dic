mordant:/ˈmɔːrdənt/ (US), /ˈmɔːdənt/ (UK)| adj. n.| 腐蚀性的，媒染剂；辛辣的，尖刻的 |A mordant is a substance, typically an inorganic oxide, that combines with a dye or stain and thereby fixes it in a material.| The mordant wit of the comedian kept the audience in stitches. | 近义词:caustic, biting, incisive, sharp, acrid, pungent, trenchant | 反义词:bland, gentle, mild | 词性:adjective, noun

mordants:/ˈmɔːrdənts/ (US), /ˈmɔːdənts/ (UK)| n.| 媒染剂 | Mordants are substances used to fix dyes to fabrics. |  Natural mordants like alum and iron were used to dye the wool. | 近义词:fixatives | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性:noun
