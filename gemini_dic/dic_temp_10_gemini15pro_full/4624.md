aldermanly:/ˈɔːldərmənli/| adj.|市議員的；像市議員的；有市議員風範的|Behaving or appearing like an alderman; dignified, respectable, perhaps somewhat pompous. |He had an aldermanly air about him, with his portly figure and booming voice. 他有着市議員般的氣度，身材魁梧，聲音洪亮。|Synonyms: dignified, respectable, august, stately, imposing, magisterial|Antonyms: undignified, disrespectful, common, lowly, humble|adjective
