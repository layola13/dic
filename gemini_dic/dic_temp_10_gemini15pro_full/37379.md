commentary:/ˈkɒmənt(ə)ri/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmənteri/ (US)| n.| 评论，解说，评注|A series of notes explaining or interpreting something; an expression of opinions or offering of explanations about an event or situation.|The sports commentary was very insightful.  体育解说很有见地。|synonyms: explanation, interpretation, analysis, exposition, annotation, notes, gloss, elucidation, review, critique, remarks, observations, opinion, view, perspective|antonyms: silence, reticence|noun
