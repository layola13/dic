elapse:/ɪˈlæps/| v. |消逝，流逝|To pass or slip by (of time).|例句：Three years elapsed before they met again. 三年过去了，他们才再次相遇。

例句：Time elapsed quickly while we were on vacation. 我们度假时，时间过得很快。|近义词：pass, go by, slip away|反义词:stand still|词性:动词
