hypopigmentation:/ˌhaɪpoʊˌpɪɡmɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 色素减退，色素脱失 |A condition in which the skin loses its normal color due to a decrease in melanin production. |  The patient exhibited hypopigmentation on several areas of her skin. 病人的皮肤上有几处色素减退。| Synonyms: depigmentation, leukoderma | Antonyms: hyperpigmentation, melanosis | noun

hypomelanosis:/ˌhaɪpoʊˌmɛləˈnoʊsɪs/ | n. | 色素减退，黑色素减少 | A condition characterized by decreased melanin production in the skin, resulting in lighter patches. | Hypomelanosis can be caused by various factors, including genetic disorders and skin damage. 色素减退可能是由多种因素引起的，包括遗传疾病和皮肤损伤。 | Synonyms: hypopigmentation, leukoderma | Antonyms: hyperpigmentation, melanosis | noun
