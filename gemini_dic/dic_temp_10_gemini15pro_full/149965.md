palmacite:/ˈpɑːlməsaɪt/| n. |棕榈酸盐|A salt or ester of palmitic acid.  Palmitic acid is a saturated fatty acid with 16 carbon atoms.  Palmacites are common in many fats and oils.|Palm oil is a rich source of palmacites. 棕榈油富含棕榈酸盐。|The researchers analyzed the palmacites in the sample. 研究人员分析了样品中的棕榈酸盐。|synonyms: hexadecanoate (more technical), palmitate (another term, sometimes used interchangeably)|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific chemical compound.  One might contrast it with unsaturated fatty acid salts.)|noun
