noncannibalistic: /ˌnɒnkænɪbəˈlɪstɪk/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnkænɪbəˈlɪstɪk/ (US)| adj. |非食人肉的；非同类相食的 | Not practicing or characterized by cannibalism; not involving the eating of one's own species. |例句：The tribe had developed a noncannibalistic culture.  这个部落已经发展出一种非食人文化的文化。

例句：Noncannibalistic spiders are less likely to harm their offspring. 非食人蜘蛛不太可能伤害它们的后代。


近义词：non-cannibal, herbivorous (for animals that eat plants) |反义词: cannibalistic |词性: adjective
