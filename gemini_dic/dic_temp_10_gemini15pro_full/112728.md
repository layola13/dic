lozenge:/ˈlɒzɪndʒ/ (UK) /ˈlɑːzɪndʒ/ (US)| n.|菱形, 斜方形, 锭剂|A rhombus, a diamond shape, a medicated tablet or sweet, often diamond-shaped, held in the mouth to dissolve slowly.|She sucked on a cough lozenge to soothe her throat. 她含着咳嗽锭剂来舒缓喉咙痛。|Synonyms: diamond, rhombus, tablet, pastille|Antonyms: circle, square (not perfect antonyms, but contrasting shapes)|noun
