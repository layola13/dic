flabellation:/ˌflæbəˈleɪʃən/ |n.|扇动，煽动; 用扇子扇; |The flabellation of the bird's wings created a gentle breeze. 鸟儿翅膀的扇动产生了一阵微风。|例句：The constant flabellation of the peacock's tail feathers was mesmerizing.  孔雀尾羽不停的扇动着，令人着迷。

The flabellation of the leaves in the wind cooled the air.  风中树叶的扇动使空气凉爽了一些。|近义词：fanning, waving, fluttering|反义词：stillness, motionlessness|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *flabellatio, from flabellare "to fan," from flabellum "fan," diminutive of flabrum "a blast, breeze."
