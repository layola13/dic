dullest: /ˈdʌlɪst/
adjective
最迟钝的；最无聊的；最阴暗的
Most lacking in sharpness, interest, or brightness.
The dullest knife in the drawer couldn't cut butter.  抽屉里最钝的刀连黄油都切不开。
The party was the dullest event I've ever attended.  那场派对是我参加过的最无聊的活动。
