haplessly:/ˈhæpləsli/| adv.|不幸地，倒霉地|In an unlucky or unfortunate way.  Derived from "hapless" (unlucky) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  The root "hap" comes from Old Norse and means "luck" or "chance."|He haplessly tripped and spilled his coffee. 他不小心绊倒了，洒了咖啡。|Sadly, unfortunately, unluckily|Fortunately, luckily|adverb

hapless:/ˈhæpləs/| adj.|不幸的，倒霉的|Unlucky or unfortunate. From "hap" (luck, chance) + "-less" (without).|The hapless traveler lost his passport.  这个倒霉的旅行者把护照弄丢了。|Unfortunate, unlucky, ill-fated|Fortunate, lucky|adjective
