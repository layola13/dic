haze:/heɪz/| v. n.|使模糊，朦胧；戏弄，欺凌|To make or become unclear or obscure; to subject (especially freshmen) to abusive or humiliating tricks and ridicule.|The smoke hazed the room. 烟雾使房间变得朦胧。|近义词：obscure, blur, mist, fog, harass, bully|反义词:clarify, clear|词性:动词,名词
