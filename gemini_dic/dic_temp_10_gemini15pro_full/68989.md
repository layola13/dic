ventriloquist:/vɛnˈtrɪləkwɪst/
|n.|腹语术表演者|A person who can speak or utter sounds so that they seem to come from somewhere else, especially an entertainer who makes their voice appear to come from a dummy of a person or animal.|The ventriloquist made the dummy appear to be alive. 腹语表演者使木偶看起来像活的一样。|Synonyms: dummy speaker, voice thrower|Antonyms: None|Noun
