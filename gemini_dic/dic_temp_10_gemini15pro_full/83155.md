guttering:/ˈɡʌtərɪŋ/
n.|水槽；檐槽；烛泪；(感情的)流露|A channel or pipe fixed to the edge of a roof to carry away rainwater.  Also refers to the melted wax that runs down the side of a candle.|The guttering was blocked with leaves. 檐槽被树叶堵住了。|近义词：drainpipe, eavestrough|反义词:|名词

guttering:/ˈɡʌtərɪŋ/
adj.|（火）闪烁的，忽明忽暗的；(尤指感情)强烈的，奔放的|Burning unsteadily with a sputtering flame; also, used figuratively to describe strong, passionate emotions.|The guttering candlelight created an eerie atmosphere. 闪烁的烛光营造了一种怪异的气氛.  /  He felt a guttering sense of hope. 他感到一丝闪烁的希望。|近义词：flickering, wavering|反义词:steady, constant|形容词
