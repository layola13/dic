nonresponse:/ˌnɑːn.rɪˈspɑːns/ |n.| 无回应，不答复 |lack of response; failure to respond| The survey suffered from a high nonresponse rate, making the results unreliable. 这项调查的无回应率很高，使得结果不可靠。| Example: The low nonresponse rate suggests that the survey was well-designed. 低无回应率表明这项调查设计得很好.|Synonyms: no reply, lack of answer, silence |Antonyms: response, reply, answer| noun

nonresponse:/ˌnɑːn.rɪˈspɑːns/ |n.| 无反应，不响应 |lack of reaction to a stimulus| The patient showed nonresponse to the medication. 病人对药物没有反应。| Example: The nonresponse of the tumor to chemotherapy is concerning. 肿瘤对化疗无反应令人担忧。|Synonyms: no reaction, unresponsiveness, inertness |Antonyms: response, reaction, responsiveness| noun
