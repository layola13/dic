guided:/ˈɡaɪdɪd/| adj.|引导的，指导的|Supervised or directed; led or steered.|例句：The guided tour of the museum was very informative. 博物馆的导览游览非常翔实。|近义词：directed, supervised, steered, led, piloted, escorted|反义词：unguided, undirected, aimless|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the verb "guide" + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  "Guide" comes from the Old French "guider," ultimately from the Frankish "*witan" (to show the way).
