haircutters:/ˈhɛərˌkʌtərz/
n.
理发师（复数）

The haircutters were very busy on Saturday. 周六理发师都很忙。
Synonyms: barbers, stylists, hairdressers
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps one could consider "hair growers" humorously)
