colliquative:/kəˈlɪkwətɪv/ /kəˈlɪkwəˌtɪv/| adj.|液化的；溶解的；衰竭性的|Characterized by excessive discharge of fluid or by liquefaction of tissue.|The patient experienced colliquative sweating due to the high fever.  病人因为高烧出了很多汗。|Synonyms: liquefying, dissolving, deliquescent, wasting, debilitating|Antonyms: solidifying, congealing, strengthening, invigorating|Etymology: From Latin *colliquativus*, from *colliquāre* "to melt, dissolve," from *com-* "together" + *liquāre* "to liquefy," from *liquidus* "liquid."|
