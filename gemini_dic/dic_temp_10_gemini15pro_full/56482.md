domableness:/ˈdoʊməbəlnəs/
noun
驯服性，可驯服性
The quality of being easily tamed or domesticated.
The domableness of the wild horse surprised the trainers.  这匹野马的驯服性让训练员们感到惊讶。
近义词:tameness, docility, tractability
反义词:wildness, savageness, intractability
词性:n.
