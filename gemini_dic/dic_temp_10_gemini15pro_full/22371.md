booby-trapped:/ˈbuːbi træpt/ | adj. | 装有诱杀装置的；设有陷阱的 | rigged with a trap or explosive device designed to harm or kill someone who triggers it. |  The soldiers were wary of booby-trapped buildings. 士兵们对装有诱杀装置的建筑物保持警惕。

| The enemy had booby-trapped the bridge. 敌人在这座桥上设下了陷阱。 | Near synonyms: trapped, mined, rigged | Near antonyms: safe, secure | adjective


booby trap:/ˈbuːbi træp/ | n. | 诱杀装置；陷阱 | A trap or explosive device designed to harm or kill someone who triggers it. | The soldiers discovered a booby trap hidden in the bushes. 士兵们在灌木丛中发现了一个诱杀装置。

| The booby trap was triggered by a tripwire.  这个诱杀装置是由绊线触发的。 | Near synonyms: trap, mine, snare | Near antonyms: safeguard, protection | noun
