favourability:/ˌfeɪvərəˈbɪləti/ | n. |好感，有利，赞同；有利条件，有利因素 | The candidate enjoyed high favourability ratings in the polls.  这位候选人在民意调查中享有很高的支持率。| The project's favourability depends on securing funding.  这个项目的可行性取决于能否获得资金。 | 近义词: acceptance, approval, popularity | 反义词: unpopularity, disapproval, opposition | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Middle English *favorablete, from Old French favorableté, from Late Latin *favorabilitatem, from Latin favorabilis (“favorable”), from favor (“favor, goodwill”).
