corralling:/kəˈrɔːlɪŋ/
verb
围捕；聚集
the act of gathering and confining animals, especially livestock, or people
The cowboys were corralling the cattle for branding. 牛仔们正在围捕牛群进行烙印。
synonyms: gathering, rounding up, herding, assembling, collecting
antonyms: dispersing, scattering, releasing
v.
