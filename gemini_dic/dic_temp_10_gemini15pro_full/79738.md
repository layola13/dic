gluing:/ˈɡluːɪŋ/| n. |粘合，胶合|The gluing process is crucial for a strong bond. 粘合过程对于牢固的结合至关重要。|例句：The gluing of the two pieces of wood took only a few seconds.  把两块木头粘在一起只花了幾秒鐘。

例句：The gluing of the broken vase was a success.  破损花瓶的粘合很成功。


近义词：adhering, bonding, cementing, sticking|反义词: separating, detaching|词性: 名词/动名词


Etymology: From glue + -ing.  来源于 glue（胶水） + -ing（动名词后缀）。
