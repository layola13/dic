bestiary: /ˈbɛstiəri/ (US), /ˈbiːstiəri/ (UK) | n. | 动物寓言集，怪兽图鉴 | A collection of descriptions of real or imaginary animals, often with moral or religious interpretations. |  Medieval bestiaries were popular, filled with fantastical creatures. 中世纪的动物寓言集很受欢迎，里面充满了奇幻的生物。|  She was fascinated by the illustrations in the old bestiary. 她被那本古老的动物寓言集中的插图迷住了。 | synonyms: compendium, menagerie, catalog, encyclopedia | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "reality" in a metaphorical sense) | noun
