light-weight:/ˈlaɪtˌweɪt/| adj. n.|轻量级的，轻量级选手|Of less than average weight; designed to be easily portable or movable. |This laptop is light-weight and easy to carry around. 这款笔记本电脑很轻，便于携带。| The boxer was a light-weight champion. 这位拳击手是轻量级冠军。 |Synonyms: portable, compact, featherweight |Antonyms: heavy, bulky, heavyweight| adjective
light-weight:/ˈlaɪtˌweɪt/| n.|轻量级选手|A person or thing that is of less than average weight, especially a boxer or wrestler belonging to a weight division having a maximum limit between 127 and 135 pounds. |He's a light-weight in the boxing world. 他是拳击界的轻量级选手。|  She felt like a light-weight compared to the experienced climbers. 与经验丰富的登山者相比，她觉得自己像个新手。|Synonyms: bantamweight, featherweight|Antonyms: heavyweight| noun
