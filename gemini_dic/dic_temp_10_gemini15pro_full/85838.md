hcf:/eɪtʃ siː ef/ |abbr.| 最高公因数 (Highest Common Factor)| Highest Common Factor, the largest number that divides exactly into two or more numbers. |The HCF of 12 and 18 is 6. 12和18的最高公因数是6。|近义词:greatest common divisor (GCD)|反义词:least common multiple (LCM)|abbr.
