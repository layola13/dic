| Example: The multiloquent professor lectured for three hours straight.  这位多话的教授连续讲了三个小时。| Synonym: talkative, loquacious, garrulous, voluble | Antonym: reticent, taciturn, laconic | Word Type: Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *multiloquus*, from *multi-* (much, many) + *loquus* (speaking, talkative), from *loqui* (to speak).
