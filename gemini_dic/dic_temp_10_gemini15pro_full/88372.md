hipsters:/ˈhɪpstərz/| n.|时髦人士，潮人|A person who follows the latest trends and fashions, especially those regarded as being outside the cultural mainstream.|The cafe was full of hipsters with their beards and vintage clothes.  这家咖啡馆里满是留着胡子、穿着复古服装的潮人。|synonyms: trendsetters, fashionistas, nonconformists|antonyms: conformists, traditionalists|noun
