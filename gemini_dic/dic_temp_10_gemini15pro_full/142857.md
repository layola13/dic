out-groups:/ˈaʊtˌɡruːps/
noun
外群体
A social group with which an individual does not identify.
例句：People tend to view members of out-groups with suspicion. 人们往往对外群体成员持怀疑态度。

例句：He felt alienated and excluded, treated like a member of an out-group. 他感到疏远和被排斥，像外群体成员一样被对待。

近义词：out-group members, outsiders
反义词：in-groups, peer groups
词性:n.
