de-sludge:/diːˈslʌdʒ/
verb
除淤；清除污泥
To remove sludge from something.
The workers de-sludged the septic tank. 工人们清理了化粪池的污泥。
The city will de-sludge the wastewater treatment plant next month. 该市下个月将清除污水处理厂的污泥。
Synonyms: clean, clear, purge
Antonyms: contaminate, pollute
