nay:/neɪ/
词性: adverb
中文翻译: 不；否，以及（用于加强语气）
英文解析:  An adverb used to express negation, refusal, or denial, or as an emphatic negative response.  It can also be used as a noun (now archaic) meaning a refusal or denial.
例句：
"Will you go?" "Nay, I will not."  “你去吗？” “不，我不去。”

He said nay to the proposal. 他拒绝了这个提议。

近义词: no, not
反义词: yea, yes
词性: adverb


nay:/neɪ/
词性: noun
中文翻译: 拒绝，否决（古语）
英文解析: A refusal or denial (archaic).
例句：
The council gave their nay to the king's request.  议会拒绝了国王的请求。 (古语用法)

His nay was final. 他的拒绝是最终的。(古语用法)

近义词: refusal, denial
反义词: acceptance, approval
词性: noun
