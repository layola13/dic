abrasively:/əˈbreɪsɪvli/ | adv. | 粗暴地，伤人地 | In a way that shows little concern for the feelings of others; harshly. |  He spoke abrasively to the waiter. 他对服务员说话很粗鲁。

She abrasively rejected his offer of help. 她粗暴地拒绝了他的帮助。

近义词: harshly, roughly, rudely, sharply, cuttingly | 反义词: gently, kindly, politely, softly, tenderly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From abrasive + -ly. Abrasive comes from the Latin abradere, meaning "to scrape off."

USAGE->
She responded abrasively to his question. 她粗暴地回答了他的问题。

The sandpaper abrasively removed the paint from the wood. 砂纸粗暴地去除了木头上的油漆。


PHRASE->
speak abrasively  粗暴地说话
act abrasively  粗暴地行事
respond abrasively  粗暴地回应
behave abrasively  粗暴地表现
treat someone abrasively  粗暴地对待某人
