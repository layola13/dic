noncomplacence:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈpleɪsəns/| n.|不满足；不懈怠；不安于现状|The company's noncomplacence about its success has helped it stay ahead of the competition.  这家公司对成功的不安于现状帮助它在竞争中保持领先。|近义词：dissatisfaction, unease, vigilance|反义词:complacency, satisfaction, contentment|词性:名词
Etymology: From non- + complacence.  Complacence comes from Latin complacere ("to be pleasing"), from com- ("with") + placere ("to please"). Non- is a prefix meaning "not."
