iliopsoas: /ˌɪliəʊˈsoʊəs/ (UK) /ˌɪlioʊˈsoʊəs/ (US)| n. | 髂腰肌 |  A compound muscle composed of the psoas major and the iliacus, which is located in the lower lumbar region and extends down to the femur (thigh bone). It acts to flex the hip joint and plays a crucial role in walking, running, and maintaining posture. | The iliopsoas muscle is essential for core stability.  髂腰肌对核心稳定性至关重要。|  The runner stretched her iliopsoas to prevent injury.  跑步者拉伸了她的髂腰肌以防止受伤。 | Synonyms:  hip flexor | Antonyms:  hip extensor | Noun
