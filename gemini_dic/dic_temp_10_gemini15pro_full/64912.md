essoiner:/ɛˈsoɪnər/| n.|辩护人；告假人；代理人；|A person who makes excuses for the absence of another, especially in a legal context.  Someone who legally offers an excuse for the non-appearance of another in court. |The essoiner presented a doctor's note to the court explaining the defendant's absence.|Synonyms: attorney, advocate, representative, proxy, legal representative |Antonyms: defendant, accused, plaintiff, opponent|Noun
