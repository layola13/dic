overobjectifies: /ˌoʊvərəbˈdʒɛktɪfaɪz/
|v.|过度物化；过分将…视为客体|To treat someone excessively as an object, especially sexually, reducing them to their physical attributes and disregarding their personality or feelings.|The media often overobjectifies women in advertisements.|近义词: sexualize, objectify, dehumanize|反义词: humanize, personalize, respect|词性:动词

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "objectify" (from "object" + "-ify," meaning "to make into an object").
