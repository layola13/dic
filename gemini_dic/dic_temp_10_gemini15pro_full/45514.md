curers:/ˈkjʊrərz/
noun
治疗者，治愈者
People who cure others of illnesses or bad habits.
The curers used traditional herbs and remedies. 这些治疗者使用传统的草药和疗法。
近义词: healers, therapists, physicians
反义词: inflictors, damagers
词性: noun
