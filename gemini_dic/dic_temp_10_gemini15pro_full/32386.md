chilis:/ˈtʃɪliz/ | n. |辣椒; 辣椒粉; 红辣椒|Hot peppers, often used as a spice or condiment.  They can refer to the fresh peppers, dried peppers, or powdered form. | I like my chili with lots of chilis. 我喜欢我的辣椒里有很多辣椒。|The recipe calls for two dried chilis.  这个食谱需要两个干辣椒。| 近义词: peppers, chili peppers | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bland" for the flavor) | noun

chili:/ˈtʃɪli/ | n. |辣椒酱; 辣肉豆酱|A dish made with ground meat, beans, tomatoes, and spices, often served hot. Can also refer to chili powder.|I made a big pot of chili for dinner. 我做了一大锅辣椒酱作为晚餐。 | Do you want chili powder on your fries? 你想在薯条上放辣椒粉吗？| 近义词: chili con carne | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | noun
