aftermost:/ˈɑːftərməʊst/| adj.|最后面的，最靠后的| Situated furthest aft; hindmost.|The aftermost cabin on the ship offered the best views of the wake. 船上最后面的船舱可以欣赏到最好的尾迹景色。|近义词：hindmost, rearmost, last|反义词：foremost, frontmost|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "after" (meaning behind) + "-most" (superlative suffix).  "After" comes from Old English "æfter," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*after." "-Most" comes from Old English "-mest," a superlative suffix.
