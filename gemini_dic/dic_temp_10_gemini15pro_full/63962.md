episkeletal:/ˌɛpɪˈskɛlɪtəl/
adjective
骨骼表面的，附着于骨骼上的
Located on or attached to the skeleton.
The episkeletal muscles are responsible for movement of the limbs.  附着于骨骼上的肌肉负责四肢的运动。
Synonyms: periosteal, supraosseous
Antonyms: subosseous, intraosseous
