croisado:/krɔɪˈseɪdoʊ/ /krɔɪˈsɑːdoʊ/
n.
牛角面包，羊角面包
A rich, flaky, crescent-shaped roll, often eaten for breakfast.
例句：I like to have a croisado and coffee for breakfast. 我喜欢早餐吃牛角面包和咖啡。

例句：The bakery is famous for its delicious croisados. 这家面包店以其美味的牛角面包而闻名。

近义词：crescent roll, croissant
反义词: None (No direct antonym for a type of pastry)
词性: noun
