inexhaustibility:/ˌɪnɪɡˈzɔːstɪbɪləti/
n.|取之不尽，用之不竭|The quality of being impossible to exhaust or use up completely. |Her inexhaustibility amazed everyone, she worked tirelessly for days. 她不知疲倦的精神让每个人都感到惊讶，她不知疲倦地工作了好几天。|Synonyms:  boundlessness, limitlessness, endlessness|Antonyms: exhaustion, finiteness, limitation
