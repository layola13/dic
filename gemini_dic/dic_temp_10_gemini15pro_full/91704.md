hyperconsciousnesses:/ˌhaɪpərˈkɒnʃəsnəsɪz/
n.
极度清醒；过度意识；超意识
An abnormally heightened state of awareness or self-awareness.
Example:  His hyperconsciousness of his appearance made him uncomfortable. 他对自身外表的过度在意让他感到不自在。

Example: The medication induced a state of hyperconsciousness in the patient.  药物使病人处于一种极度清醒的状态。

Synonym:  heightened awareness,  self-awareness, over-awareness
Antonym: unconsciousness, unawareness
词性: 名词
