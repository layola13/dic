champignon:/ʃæmˈpɪnjən/ /ʃɑːmˈpɪnjən/| n. |蘑菇，蕈|An edible fungus, typically white, with a short stalk and domed cap.|例句：I added sliced champignons to the pasta sauce. 我在意大利面酱里加了切片的蘑菇。

例句：The champignons were sautéed in butter and garlic. 蘑菇用黄油和大蒜煎了。|近义词：mushroom, fungus|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *champignon*, from Old French *champignuel* "mushroom," from Vulgar Latin *(fungus) campanius* "(fungus) of the field," from Latin *campus* "field."
