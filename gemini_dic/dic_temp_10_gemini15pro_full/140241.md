offloader:/ˈɒfləʊdə(r)/ /ˈɔːfloʊdər/ | n. |卸载器;卸货机;卸货员|A person or device that removes a load from something, especially a vehicle or ship.  Something that relieves stress or burden.| The dockworkers used a specialized offloader to move the cargo containers from the ship. 码头工人使用专门的卸载器将货物集装箱从船上卸下。|  Journaling can be a great offloader for emotional stress. 写日记可以很好地缓解情绪压力。| synonyms: unloader, discharger, handler | antonyms: loader, packer | noun

offload:/ˈɒfləʊd/ /ˈɔːfloʊd/ | v. |卸载，卸货；转嫁（工作、问题等）| To remove a load from a vehicle, ship, etc.; to transfer work, problems, etc. to someone else. | They offloaded the timber at the port. 他们在港口卸下了木材。| She offloaded some of her work to her assistant. 她把一些工作转交给了她的助手。| synonyms: unload, discharge, transfer, dump | antonyms: load, pack | verb
