desponsate:/dɪˈspɒnseɪt/ /dɪˈspɑːnseɪt/ | vt.| 订婚，许配 | To promise (a person) in marriage; betroth. | He desponsated his daughter to a wealthy merchant. 他把女儿许配给了一个富商。

The king desponsated his daughter to the prince of a neighboring kingdom. 国王将女儿许配给了邻国的王子。


近义词: betroth, engage, affiance | 反义词: divorce, separate | 词性: verb (及物动词)

词源: From Late Latin *desponsatus*, past participle of desponsare "to betroth," from Latin spondere "to promise solemnly, pledge," from Proto-Indo-European *spend- "to make an offering, perform a rite."
