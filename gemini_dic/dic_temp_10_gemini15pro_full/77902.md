genies:/ˈdʒiːniːz/
noun
精灵，妖怪
Supernatural beings in Arabian and Muslim folklore, often depicted as having magical powers and residing in lamps or other objects.
The genie granted him three wishes. 精灵满足了他三个愿望。
synonyms: spirits, djinn, efreet
antonyms: mortals, humans
