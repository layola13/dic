formidableness:/fɔːrˈmɪdəblənəs/ | n. | 令人敬畏；难对付；强大 | The formidableness of the task ahead was daunting.  前方的任务之艰巨令人畏惧。|  The sheer formidableness of the mountain range made the climbers hesitate. 连绵起伏的山脉的雄伟气势让登山者犹豫不前。 | 近义词：power, strength, might, awesomeness, impressiveness | 反义词：weakness, insignificance, powerlessness | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From formidable + -ness.  Formidable comes from French *formidable*, from Latin formidabilis "causing fear," from formidare "to fear," from formido "fear, terror, dread."
