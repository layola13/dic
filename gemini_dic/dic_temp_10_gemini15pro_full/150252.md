pandialectal:/ˌpænˌdaɪəˈlɛktəl/ | adj. | 跨方言的 |  Relating to or encompassing features common to multiple dialects of a language. It describes linguistic features that are found across different dialects, rather than being specific to one. | This pandialectal feature is common in spoken English across the United States.  这个跨方言特征在美国各地的英语口语中很常见。|  supradialectal, common, general | dialectal, regional | adjective
