azotic: /əˈzɒtɪk/ /əˈzɑːtɪk/
adjective
含氮的；氮的
Relating to or containing nitrogen.  Derived from French *azote* "nitrogen".
例句：Azrael, often depicted as an angel of death, carries an azotic sword, symbolizing the decay and return to nitrogen that all living things undergo. 阿兹拉尔，通常被描绘成死亡天使，携带着一把含氮的剑，象征着所有生物都要经历的腐烂和回归氮的过程。

例句：The scientist carefully measured the azotic compounds in the soil sample. 科学家仔细测量了土壤样品中的含氮化合物。

近义词：nitrogenous, nitric
反义词： (No direct antonym, consider terms like "oxygen-rich" or "nitrogen-deficient" depending on context)
词性：adjective
