exorcise:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪz/ | n. | 驱魔；驱邪； exorcism的复数 | The exorcises performed by the priest were successful. 神父执行的驱魔仪式成功了。 | expulsions, purgations | invocations | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin exorcizare, from Latin exorcisus, past participle of exorciō ("I adjure, I bind by oath, I exorcise"), from ex ("out") + horreō ("I shudder, I bristle").  The original sense was thus "to bind by oath", later to drive out by adjuration.
