multifold:/ˈmʌltɪˌfoʊld/ /ˈmʌltiˌfəʊld/| adj. adv.|多重的，多方面的；许多倍|Having many parts or aspects; many times greater in number, quantity, or extent.|The benefits of exercise are multifold. 运动的益处是多方面的。|近义词：multiple, numerous, manifold, various|反义词：single, simple, few|词性:形容词/副词

multifold:/ˈmʌltɪˌfoʊld/ /ˈmʌltiˌfəʊld/| adv.|许多倍地|In a way that increases greatly in number, quantity, or extent.|The value of the company has increased multifold over the last decade.  这家公司的价值在过去十年里增长了许多倍。|近义词：greatly, considerably, significantly|反义词：slightly, minimally|词性:副词
