burdensomeness:/ˈbɜːrdənsəmnəs/
n.
沉重；繁重；累赘
The state or quality of being burdensome; oppressiveness.
The burdensomeness of his responsibilities made him age prematurely.  他责任的沉重使他过早衰老。

近义词:onerousness, heaviness, weightiness, cumbersomeness
反义词:lightness, easiness, simplicity

词性:noun
词源:From burdensome + -ness.
