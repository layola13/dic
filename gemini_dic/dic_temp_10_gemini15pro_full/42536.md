countermark:/ˈkaʊntərmɑːrk/ | n. | 副戳，反标记，对戳 | A mark added to a coin or postage stamp after its original issue, often to indicate a change in value or validity; a mark added to counteract a previous one. | The coin bears a countermark from a later reign. 这枚硬币带有后期统治时期的副戳。| 近义词: counterstamp, overmark | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: Noun


countermark:/ˈkaʊntərmɑːrk/ | v. | 加盖副戳，反标记 | To add a countermark to. |  They countermarked the coins with a new symbol. 他们在硬币上加盖了一个新的符号。| 近义词: counterstamp, overmark | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: Verb
