adfected:/əˈfektid/ | adj. | 做作的，不自然的 | Affected manners or speech are unnatural and artificial, intended to impress. |  She spoke in a rather affected accent. 她说话的口音相当做作。

He struck an affected pose. 他摆出一个做作的姿势。| 近义词：artificial, pretentious, contrived, phony, put-on | 反义词：natural, genuine, sincere, unaffected | 词性:形容词

adfected: /əˈfektid/ | adj. | 受影响的（古语）|  Influenced or touched by something. | This meaning is now largely archaic.  This meaning is now largely archaic.| 近义词：influenced, touched | 反义词：unaffected | 词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin affectus, past participle of afficere ("to influence, act on"), from ad- ("to, towards") + facere ("to do, make").

USAGE->

一般现在时:
His affected laugh annoys me. = 他那做作的笑声让我很烦。
Her affected accent makes her sound pretentious. = 她那做作的口音让她听起来很装腔作势。


一般过去时:

She was criticized for her affected way of speaking. = 她因做作的说话方式而受到批评。


现在完成时:
He has always been affected in his mannerisms. = 他的举止一直都很做作。
She has affected a British accent since moving to London. = 自从搬到伦敦后，她就一直装腔作势地操着英国口音。


过去完成时:

She had affected a high-pitched voice for so long that she had forgotten her natural voice. = 她装腔作势地用高亢的声音说了这么久，以至于忘记了自己本来的声音。


将来时:
He will likely affect a more serious demeanor in his new role. = 在他的新角色中，他可能会装出一副更严肃的样子。
If she continues to affect this persona, she will lose her friends. = 如果她继续装腔作势，她会失去朋友。


虚拟语气:

被动语态: (被动语态不适用于描述人“做作”这个性质)
