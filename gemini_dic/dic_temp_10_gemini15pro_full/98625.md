instrumentalness:/ˌɪnstrəˌmɛnˈtælnəs/ | n. | 乐器性，器乐性 |The degree to which a piece of music relies on musical instruments rather than vocals or other sounds. | The instrumentalness of the track was high, featuring a complex interplay of guitar and drums. 这首曲子的乐器性很强，吉他与鼓的相互作用很复杂。 | synonyms: instrumentation, musicality | antonyms: vocality, a capella | noun
