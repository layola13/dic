absolutory:/ˌæbsəˈlʊtəri/
adjective
赦免的，免罪的
Serving to absolve; containing or implying absolution.  A declaration of forgiveness or acquittal.
The judge issued an absolutory decree, freeing the defendant from all charges. 法官发布了赦免令，免除了被告的所有指控。

The priest offered the dying man absolutory words of comfort.  神父向垂死的人说了些安慰和赦免的话。
近义词:exculpatory, acquitting, freeing
反义词:incriminating, condemning
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *absolutorius, from Latin absolvere "to absolve" (see absolve).  The suffix -ory indicates "pertaining to" or "serving to".

USAGE->
This word is primarily used in legal or formal contexts.
