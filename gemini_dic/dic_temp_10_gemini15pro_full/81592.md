The mechanic's hands were covered in greasiness. 技工的手上沾满了油污。|近义词：oiliness, slickness, fattiness|反义词：cleanliness, dryness|词性:名词

词源: From greasy + -ness.  由 greasy（油腻的）加名词后缀 -ness 构成。
