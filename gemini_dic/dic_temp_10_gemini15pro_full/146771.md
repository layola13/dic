overhysterical:/ˌoʊvərɪˈstɛrɪkəl/
adjective
过度歇斯底里的
Excessively hysterical; overly emotional and agitated.
She became overhysterical when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息后变得过度歇斯底里。
The children were overhysterical after the rollercoaster ride.  孩子们坐完过山车后异常兴奋激动。
近义词: overwrought, overexcited, frantic
反义词: calm, composed, serene
词性: adjective
