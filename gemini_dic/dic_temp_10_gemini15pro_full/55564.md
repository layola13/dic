disturb:/dɪˈstɜːrb/ (US), /dɪˈstɜːb/ (UK)| v.|打扰，扰乱，使不安|To interrupt the quiet, peace, or order of; to make someone worry or upset.|The loud noise disturbed my sleep. 吵闹的声音打扰了我的睡眠。|Synonyms: interrupt, bother, upset, agitate|Antonyms: soothe, calm, comfort|verb

disturb:/dɪˈstɜːrb/ (US), /dɪˈstɜːb/ (UK)| n.|(罕见)骚乱，动乱|A state of agitation or unrest.|There was a great disturb in the crowd when the police arrived.  警察到达时人群中一片骚乱。(这个名词用法比较过时罕见)|Synonyms: commotion, uproar, tumult|Antonyms: peace, tranquility, calm|noun
