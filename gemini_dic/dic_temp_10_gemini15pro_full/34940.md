cloche:/klɒʃ/ (UK) /kloʊʃ/ (US)| n.|钟形罩，女式钟形帽|A bell-shaped cover, especially one used to protect plants from frost, or a woman's close-fitting, bell-shaped hat.|The gardener placed a cloche over the delicate seedlings. 园丁在娇嫩的幼苗上放了一个钟形罩。|She wore a stylish cloche hat. 她戴着一顶时尚的钟形帽。|synonyms: bell jar, dome, bonnet|antonyms: (For plant cloche) uncovered, exposed|noun

cloche:/klɒʃ/ (UK) /kloʊʃ/ (US)| adj.(rare)|钟形的|Bell-shaped.|a cloche hat. 一顶钟形女帽|This rare plant needs a cloche environment. 这种稀有植物需要钟形环境。|synonyms: bell-shaped, domed|antonyms: flat, open|adjective
