breakbone:/ˈbreɪkbəʊn/ (UK) /ˈbreɪkˌboʊn/ (US)| n. adj.|破骨热，登革热; 引起剧痛的|A severe tropical disease also called dengue fever, characterized by high fever, intense joint and muscle pain (hence "breakbone"), and rash. Also used adjectivally to describe something causing such pain.|

例句：
He contracted breakbone fever while traveling in Southeast Asia.
他在东南亚旅行时感染了破骨热。

例句：
The breakbone pain made it impossible for him to move.
这种剧痛让他动弹不得。


近义词：dengue, dengue fever, break-bone fever|
反义词: (No direct antonym for a disease, but could use "health", "wellness")|
词性: noun, adjective|
