adducing: /əˈdjuːsɪŋ/
verb
引用，举出（理由、证据等）
presenting something as evidence or as a reason in support of something.
The lawyer is adducing evidence to support his client's claim.  律师正在引证证据来支持其委托人的主张。

He adduced several reasons for his absence. 他为自己的缺席列举了几个理由。

近义词: citing, quoting, alleging, presenting, advancing
反义词: refuting, denying, contradicting
词性: Verb (现在分词)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adducere*  (to lead to, bring to), from *ad-* (to) + *ducere* (to lead).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The prosecution adduces forensic evidence. = 控方出示了法医证据。
She adduces her extensive experience in the field. = 她举出了自己在该领域的丰富经验。

一般过去时:
They adduced witness testimony to corroborate their story. = 他们援引证人证词来证实他们的说法。
The defense adduced evidence that challenged the prosecution's claims. = 辩方提出了质疑控方主张的证据。

现在完成时:
The researchers have adduced compelling evidence to support their theory. = 研究人员已经提供了令人信服的证据来支持他们的理论。
He has adduced several examples to illustrate his point. = 他列举了几个例子来说明他的观点。

过去完成时:
Before the trial concluded, the defense had adduced new evidence. = 在审判结束前，辩方已经提出了新的证据。
She had adduced all the necessary documents to prove her identity. = 她已经提供了所有必要的文件来证明她的身份。

将来时:
The expert will adduce further evidence at the next hearing. = 专家将在下次听证会上提供进一步的证据。
They will adduce scientific data to support their argument. = 他们将引用科学数据来支持他们的论点。

被动语态:
New evidence was adduced in court today. = 今天法庭上出示了新的证据。
Several arguments were adduced against the proposal. = 针对该提案提出了几个论点。
