overtime:/ˈoʊvərtaɪm/ | n. adj. adv.|加班时间；超时；加班地；超时的；加班的|Extra time worked beyond the regular hours; also, the payment for this.|例句：He earns a lot of money from overtime. 他通过加班赚了很多钱。|近义词：extra time, extended time|反义词:regular time|词性:名词

overtimes:/ˈoʊvərtaɪmz/ | adv.|多次加班|Multiple instances of working overtime.|例句：She has worked overtimes this month to finish the project.  这个月她为了完成项目加班了很多次。|近义词：frequently overtime, repeatedly overtime|反义词:N/A|词性:副词

注意事项:
"overtimes" 作为名词的复数形式，指多次加班的情况，相对少见。更常见的是用 "overtime" 作为不可数名词表示加班时间或加班费，或者用 "a lot of overtime"，"lots of overtime" 等表达多次加班。
