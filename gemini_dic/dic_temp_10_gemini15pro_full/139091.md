obcompressed:/ˌɒbkəmˈprɛst/
adjective
扁压的；倒卵形的；侧向压扁的
Flattened dorsoventrally or from back to front.  Specifically, in botany, flattened from front to back, as opposed to laterally flattened (compressed).
The obcompressed fruits of this species are a key identifying feature.  这种植物扁压的果实是其关键识别特征。
近义词: compressed, flattened
反义词: inflated, turgid
词性: adjective
