nest-like:/ˈnestˌlaɪk/| adj.| 巢状的，像鸟巢的|Resembling a nest, especially in shape or structure.|The bird built a nest-like structure in the tree. 这只鸟在树上筑了一个像鸟巢一样的构造。|近义词：nest-shaped, cozy, snug|反义词:exposed, open|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "nest" (Old English *nest) + "-like" (Old English -līc).
