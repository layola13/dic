disposers:/dɪˈspoʊzərz/
noun
（垃圾）处理机
A device, usually electrically powered, that shreds food waste so that it can be flushed down a drain.
Most modern kitchens are equipped with garbage disposers. 大多数现代厨房都配备了垃圾处理机.
