clavigerous:/kləˈvɪdʒərəs/
adjective
持钥匙的；掌管钥匙的
bearing or carrying keys, especially as a symbol of office or authority.
The clavigerous guard unlocked the gate. 持钥匙的警卫打开了大门。
Synonyms: key-bearing, key-holding
Antonyms: keyless
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *clāviger* (key-bearer), from *clāvis* (key) + *-ger* (bearer).  The *-ger* element is related to *gerō* (I carry, I bear).
