dimensionality:/ˌdɪmenʃəˈnæləti/ | n. | 维度；次元 |The quality of having a particular number of dimensions. It refers to the number of independent variables needed to describe a space or object. |例句：The dimensionality of the problem is too high for our current algorithms.  这个问题的维度对于我们目前的算法来说太高了。

例句：Understanding the dimensionality of data is crucial in machine learning. 理解数据的维度在机器学习中至关重要。|近义词：dimension, size, magnitude |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From dimension + -ality.  "Dimension" comes from the Latin "dimensio," meaning "measurement." The suffix "-ality" forms nouns of quality or state.
