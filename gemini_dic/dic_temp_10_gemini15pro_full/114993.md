mandamus:/mænˈdeɪməs/| n.|强制令|A judicial writ issued as a command to an inferior court or ordering a person to perform a public or statutory duty.|The lawyer filed for a writ of mandamus to compel the agency to release the documents. 律师申请了强制令，迫使该机构公布文件。|Synonyms: order, command, injunction, decree|Antonyms: prohibition, refusal, denial|Noun
