hexane:/ˈhɛkseɪn/| n.|己烷|A colorless, flammable liquid alkane with the chemical formula C6H14, used as a solvent and a component of gasoline.|Hexane is commonly used in laboratories as a non-polar solvent.|近义词:  n-hexane, solvent |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound) |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *hex- (six) + -ane (suffix denoting an alkane). Ultimately from Greek ἕξ (héx, “six”).
