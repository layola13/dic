depulse:/dɪˈpʌls/| v. |击退，驱逐; 拒绝，排斥|To drive back or away; to repel.  To reject with rudeness or disdain.|The defenders depulsed the enemy attack.  守军击退了敌人的进攻。  He depulsed her offer of help with a sneer. 他轻蔑地拒绝了她的帮助。|近义词:repel, repulse, reject, snub, decline, rebuff|反义词:accept, welcome, attract, embrace|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *depulsare, from Latin de- "away" + pulsare "to push, beat," frequentative of pellere "to drive."
