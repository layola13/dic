hygienics:/haɪˈdʒiːnɪks/
noun
卫生学；保健法
The science of health and its preservation.
例句：The course covers the basic principles of hygienics. 这门课程涵盖了卫生学的基本原理。

例句：Proper food hygienics are essential to prevent food poisoning.  适当的食品卫生对于预防食物中毒至关重要。

近义词：hygiene, sanitation
反义词：uncleanliness, filth
词性：noun
