longiloquence:/ˌlɒŋɡɪˈləʊkwəns/ /ˌlɑːŋɡɪˈloʊkwəns/| n.|冗长；啰嗦|the state of using too many words in speech or writing|His longiloquence made it difficult to follow his point. 他冗长的发言让人难以理解他的观点。|Synonyms: verbosity, prolixity, wordiness|Antonyms: brevity, conciseness, terseness|noun
