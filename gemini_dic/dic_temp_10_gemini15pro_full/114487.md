malacology:/məˈlɒkəli/ /məˈleɪkɑːlədʒi/| n. |软体动物学|The study of mollusks, which are a large phylum of invertebrate animals including snails, slugs, mussels, octopuses, and squid.|She's pursuing a degree in malacology. 她正在攻读软体动物学学位。|近义词：conchology (贝壳学, 部分重叠)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From French *malacologie*, from Ancient Greek *μαλακός (malakós)* meaning "soft" + *-λογία (-logía)* meaning "study of".
