nonexternalities:/ˌnɒnˌɛkstɜːˈnælɪtiz/ | n. | 非外部性 |  The absence of externalities, meaning the costs and benefits of a transaction are fully borne by the parties involved.  It refers to situations where the actions of one party do not have unintended consequences for others who are not directly involved. |  The pricing model assumes nonexternalities, meaning the cost of production is entirely internal to the company.  这个定价模型假设不存在外部性，意味着生产成本完全由公司内部承担。

The contract explicitly addressed nonexternalities to ensure all parties were responsible for their own actions. 合同明确地提到了非外部性，以确保所有各方都对自己的行为负责。 | 近义词: internal costs and benefits | 反义词: externalities | 词性: noun
