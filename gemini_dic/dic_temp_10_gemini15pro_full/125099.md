mullioned: /ˈmʌljənd/ | adj. | 有竖框的，多竖框的 | Divided by mullions (vertical bars between panes of glass in a window). | The mullioned windows of the cathedral bathed the interior in soft light.  大教堂的竖框窗户使室内沐浴在柔和的光线下。| Example 2: The old house boasts beautiful mullioned and transomed windows. 这座老房子拥有美丽的竖框和横梁窗户。 | Synonyms: multi-paned, latticed | Antonyms: single-paned, plain | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle English *mulyon*, from Old French *moillon*, of uncertain origin, possibly from Vulgar Latin *muliōnem*, accusative of *muliō* (“moulding, millstone”), from Latin *mola* (“millstone”).
