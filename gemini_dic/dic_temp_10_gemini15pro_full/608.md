abrader:/əˈbreɪdər/| n.|磨料，研磨工具，磨损物|A substance or tool used for abrading; something that abrades.|The sandpaper acted as an abrader, smoothing the rough wood. 砂纸用作磨料，使粗糙的木头变得光滑。|Synonyms: abrasive, grinder, polisher|Antonyms: smoother, lubricant|noun
