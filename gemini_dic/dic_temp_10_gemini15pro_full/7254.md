anglo-vernacular:/ˌæŋɡloʊ vərˈnækjələr/| adj.|英语本土化的，融合英语和当地语言的|Relating to or denoting a hybrid language or dialect combining elements of English and a local vernacular, typically in a former British colony.|The play was performed in an anglo-vernacular style, blending Shakespearean English with local slang. 这部戏剧以英语本土化的风格演出，融合了莎士比亚式的英语和当地的俚语。|Example 2: Anglo-vernacular schools were common during the colonial period, catering to a growing bilingual population.  殖民时期，英语本土化学校很常见，以满足日益增长的双语人口的需求。|Synonyms: hybrid, mixed, blended|Antonyms: pure, unmixed|Adjective
