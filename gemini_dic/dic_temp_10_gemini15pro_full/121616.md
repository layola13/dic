misos:/ˈmiːsɒs/ (UK), /ˈmiːsoʊz/ (US)| n. |味噌|A fermented Japanese seasoning paste produced by fermenting soybeans with salt and kōji (the fungus Aspergillus oryzae) and sometimes rice, barley, seaweed, or other ingredients.|I love the rich flavor of miso soup. 我喜欢味噌汤浓郁的味道。|Near synonyms: fermented soybean paste, soy paste|Antonym: None (no direct opposite)|Noun
