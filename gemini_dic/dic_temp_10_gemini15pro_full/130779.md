nonanesthetic:/ˌnɑːnˌænɪsˈθɛtɪk/
adjective
非麻醉的；不用麻醉的
Not producing or related to anesthesia; not involving the use of anesthetic agents.
The procedure was nonanesthetic, causing the patient no pain.  这个手术是非麻醉的，没有给病人造成任何疼痛。
The doctor opted for a nonanesthetic approach to the minor surgery. 医生选择了对这个小手术采用非麻醉方法。
synonyms: non-narcotic, analgesic (for pain relief specifically)
antonyms: anesthetic, narcotic

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + anesthetic (from Greek "anaisthesia" meaning "lack of sensation")
