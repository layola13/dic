overprovidently:/ˌoʊvərˈprɒvɪdəntlɪ/
adverb
过于谨慎地；过度准备地
Providing for the future with excessive care or foresight.
He had overprovidently packed three suitcases for a weekend trip. 他为了周末旅行过度谨慎地打包了三个行李箱。
The squirrel overprovidently buried nuts for the winter, forgetting where most of them were. 松鼠为了冬天过分谨慎地埋藏坚果，却忘记了大部分坚果在哪里。
近义词: excessively, unduly, too carefully
反义词: improvidently, carelessly, negligently
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From "over-" (excessively) + "provident" (making provision for the future) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Provident" comes from Latin "providens," present participle of "providēre" (to foresee, provide), from "pro-" (before) + "vidēre" (to see).
