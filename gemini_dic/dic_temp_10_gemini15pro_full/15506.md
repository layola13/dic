baetulus:/ˈbeɪtʊləs/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A foolish or simple person.  Derived from Latin.  Originally referred to a stammerer or stutterer, later broadened to mean any simple or foolish person.|He acted like a baetulus, tripping over his own feet.|Synonyms: fool, idiot, simpleton, dunce|Antonyms: genius, sage, intellectual|Noun
