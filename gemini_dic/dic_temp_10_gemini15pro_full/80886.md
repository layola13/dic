governed:/ˈɡʌvənd/ /ˈɡʌvərnd/| adj. |受…支配的，受…统治的；被…控制的|Controlled or ruled by something, such as laws, rules, or a particular person or group.|The country is governed by a democratically elected president. 这个国家由民选总统统治。|例句：Her decisions were governed by her desire for power. 她的决定受她对权力的渴望支配。|近义词：ruled, controlled, regulated, directed|反义词：ungoverned, uncontrolled, independent|词性:形容词

词源: From the Middle English *governer*, from Old French *governer*, from Latin *gubernare* "to steer, guide, direct, govern," related to Greek *kubernān*, from *kubernētēs* "pilot, steersman."
