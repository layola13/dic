adjutor:/əˈdʒuːtər/
n.
助手，帮手
Someone who helps or supports someone else, an assistant.
The general relied on his trusted adjutor to manage the details of the campaign.  将军依靠他信任的助手来管理战役的细节。

He acted as her adjutor in the complex negotiations. 他在复杂的谈判中担任她的助手。

近义词:assistant, helper, aide, supporter
反义词:opponent, adversary, enemy
词性:名词

词源:
From Latin *adjūtor*, from *adjūvāre* "to help," from *ad-* "to" + *juvāre* "to help."
