excavate:/ˈekskəveɪt/ | v. | 挖掘，开凿，发掘 | To dig out and remove earth, revealing something beneath. |  Archaeologists excavated the ancient city. 考古学家挖掘了这座古城。

The workers excavated a tunnel through the mountain. 工人们在山中开凿了一条隧道。 | 近义词:dig, unearth, uncover, exhume | 反义词: bury, fill, cover | 词性: 动词

词源: From Latin *excavātus*, past participle of *excavāre* "to hollow out," from *ex-* "out" + *cavāre* "to hollow."
