anaglyptography:/ˌænəˌɡlɪpˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 浮雕印刷术；立体雕刻术 | The art or process of producing representations of solid objects on a flat surface so that they appear to stand out in relief, as by engraving, embossing, or stereoscopic photography. | Anaglyptography was a popular way to create 3D images in the early 20th century.  浮雕印刷术是20世纪早期一种流行的创作3D图像的方式。|  stereoscopy, embossing | intaglio | noun
