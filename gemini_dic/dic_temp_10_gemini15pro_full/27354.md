camboge:/kəmˈboʊdʒ/ /ˈkæmbɒdʒ/ | n. |藤黄；柬埔寨（旧称）|A gum resin obtained from Southeast Asian trees of the genus *Garcinia*, used as a yellow pigment and a purgative. Also, formerly, Cambodia. |The artist used camboge to create a vibrant sunset in the painting. 艺术家用藤黄在画中创作了一个充满活力的日落景象。|The ancient Khmer empire was centered in what was once called Camboge. 古代高棉帝国的中心位于曾经被称为柬埔寨的地方。| synonyms: gamboge, yellow, gold  | antonyms:  (For the color) blue, violet,  (No direct antonym for the country) | noun


camboge:/kəmˈboʊdʒ/ /ˈkæmbɒdʒ/ | adj. |(指颜色) 深黄色的 |Of a deep yellow or yellowish-orange color. |The camboge robes of the monks were striking against the grey walls.  僧侣们深黄色的长袍与灰色的墙壁形成了鲜明的对比。|She wore a camboge dress to the summer party. 她穿着一件深黄色的连衣裙去参加夏季派对。|synonyms:  deep yellow, golden, saffron, orange-yellow |antonyms: blue, violet, dark | adjective
