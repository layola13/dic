chlorinating:/ˈklɔːrɪneɪtɪŋ/
verb
氯化，加氯于
The process of adding chlorine to something, typically water, to purify or disinfect it.
They are chlorinating the swimming pool.  他们正在给游泳池加氯。
The water is chlorinated to kill bacteria.  为了杀死细菌，水中加了氯。
近义词: disinfecting, sterilizing, purifying
反义词: dechlorinating
词性: verb (present participle)


chlorinate:/ˈklɔːrɪneɪt/
verb
氯化，加氯于
To treat or combine with chlorine.
The city chlorinates its drinking water.  这个城市对其饮用水进行氯化处理。
We chlorinate the pool weekly to keep it clean. 我们每周给泳池加氯以保持清洁。
近义词: disinfect, sterilize, purify
反义词: dechlorinate
词性: verb
