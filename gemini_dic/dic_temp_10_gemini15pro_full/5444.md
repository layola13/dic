almses: /ɑːmzɪz/ | n. |施舍物，救济金| Money or goods given to poor people. | The beggar gratefully accepted the almses. 乞丐感激地接受了施舍。| 近义词: charity, donations, offerings | 反义词: withholdings | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old English ælmesse, from Late Latin eleemosyna, from Greek eleēmosynē "pity, alms,"  from eleēmōn "merciful, compassionate."
