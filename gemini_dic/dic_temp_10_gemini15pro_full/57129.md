dovekie: /ˈdʌvki/ (US), /ˈdʌvki/ (UK)| n. | 海鸠 | A small, plump, black and white seabird in the auk family, found in the North Atlantic. It breeds in crevices and burrows on rocky coasts and winters at sea. |  Example 1: Dovekies are excellent divers and can stay underwater for a considerable amount of time. 海鸠是优秀的潜水员，可以在水下停留相当长的时间。
