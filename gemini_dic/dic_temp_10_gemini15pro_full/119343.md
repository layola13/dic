microcephalous: /ˌmaɪkrəˈsɛfələs/ | adj. | 小头的，头小畸形的 | Having an abnormally small head. |  Microcephalous babies often have developmental problems.  头小畸形的婴儿通常会有发育问题。

Microcephaly can be caused by genetic mutations or infections during pregnancy. 小头畸形可能是由怀孕期间的基因突变或感染引起的。 |  近义词: microcephalic | 反义词: macrocephalous, normal head size | 词性: 形容词
