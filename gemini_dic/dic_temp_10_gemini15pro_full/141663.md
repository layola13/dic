option:/ˈɒpʃ(ə)n/ /ˈɑːpʃən/ | n. |选择，选项，选择权；选择自由；（股票等的）买入期权|A thing that is or may be chosen.|例句：We have the option of going or staying. 我们可以选择去或留。|例句：She had no option but to quit her job. 她别无选择，只能辞职。|近义词：choice, alternative, selection|反义词：obligation, necessity|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *opcion, from Latin optiō (“choice, free will, liberty”), from optō (“I choose, wish, desire”).
