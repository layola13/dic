conveyances:/kənˈveɪənsɪz/
noun
运输工具；交通工具；运送；传递
The act of conveying; transmission; communication.  A means of conveyance, especially a vehicle; transportation. Legal transfer of property from one owner to another.
The company provides conveyances for its employees.  这家公司为其员工提供交通工具。
Synonyms: transport, transportation, vehicle, carriage, transfer
Antonyms: retention, keeping
