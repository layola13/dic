girlishly:/ˈɡɜːlɪʃli/ /ˈɡɝːlɪʃli/| adv. |女孩子地，少女般地|In a manner considered typical of a girl, often in a playful or slightly immature way.|She giggled girlishly and hid her face behind her hands. 她少女般地咯咯笑着，用手捂住了脸。|例句：She skipped girlishly down the street. 她像小女孩一样蹦蹦跳跳地沿着街道走。|近义词：femininely, childishly|反义词：boyishly, maturely|词性:副词
