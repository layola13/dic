headshots:/ˈhɛdʃɒts/ (US) /ˈhɛdʃɒts/ (UK)| n.|头像，头部特写照片|A photograph of a person's head, typically taken from the shoulders up, often used for professional profiles, acting portfolios, or other identification purposes.  |例句：The actor's headshots were impressive and landed him several auditions.  这位演员的头像照令人印象深刻，为他赢得了几次试镜机会。

|例句：She updated her LinkedIn profile with a new professional headshot. 她用一张新的专业头像照更新了她的领英个人资料。|近义词：portraits, profile pictures|反义词: full-body shots|词性:noun
