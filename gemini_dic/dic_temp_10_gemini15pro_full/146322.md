overfemininities: /ˌoʊvərˌfɛmɪˈnɪnɪtiz/
| n. | 过度女性化；过分娇柔 |  The exaggerated display of stereotypical feminine traits.  It often implies an artificial or excessive adherence to traditional gender roles associated with women. |  Some critics argued that the movie portrayed overfemininities, depicting women solely as damsels in distress.  一些评论家认为这部电影描绘了过度的女性化，将女性完全描绘成落难少女。| 近义词：effeminacy, excessive femininity | 反义词：masculinity, tomboyishness | 词性: noun


Etymology:
over- + femininity + -s
* over-:  Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi (meaning "over, above, beyond").
* femininity: from French féminité, from Latin femina (“woman”).
* -s: plural marker
