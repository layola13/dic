desubsidize:/ˌdiːˈsʌbsɪdaɪz/
verb
取消补贴，停止资助
To remove a subsidy from (something).  To stop paying part of the cost of something.
The government decided to desubsidize the farming industry. 政府决定取消对农业的补贴。
Farmers are protesting the government's plan to desubsidize agriculture. 农民抗议政府取消农业补贴的计划。
近义词:defund, withdraw funding
反义词:subsidize
词性:v.
