monoecism: /məˈnɪːsɪɪzəm/ /məˈnɑːsɪɪzəm/
n. |雌雄同株，雌雄同体|The condition of having both male and female reproductive organs in the same individual. |Monoecism is common in many plant species, such as corn. 玉米等许多植物都雌雄同株。|hermaphroditism, cosexuality |dioecism| noun
