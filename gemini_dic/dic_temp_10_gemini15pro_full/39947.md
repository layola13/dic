constructionist:/kənˈstrʌkʃənɪst/
n.
建构主义者；构成主义者
Someone who believes that knowledge and reality are created through individual experiences and social interactions, rather than being objectively true or inherent.
Constructionists argue that gender roles are socially constructed, not biologically determined. 建构主义者认为性别角色是社会建构的，而不是由生物决定的。
近义词:constructivist
反义词:essentialist, realist
词性:noun


constructionist:/kənˈstrʌkʃənɪst/
adj.
建构主义的；构成主义的
Relating to constructionism, a theory that knowledge and reality are created through individual experiences and social interactions.
A constructionist approach to education emphasizes active learning and student-centered inquiry. 建构主义的教育方法强调主动学习和以学生为中心的探究。
近义词:constructivist
反义词:essentialist, realist
词性:adjective
