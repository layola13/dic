balas:/ˈbɑːləs/| n. |菲律宾香脂树；菲律宾香脂树的树脂|A resin obtained from the Philippine balsam tree (Canarium luzonicum), used in varnishes and perfumes. |The balas resin is highly prized for its fragrant aroma.  菲律宾香脂树脂因其芬芳的香味而备受推崇。|The varnish was made with balas.  这清漆是用菲律宾香脂树脂制成的。|synonyms: Manila elemi, elemi|antonyms: N/A|noun
