miscategorized:/ˌmɪskætəɡəˈraɪzd/ | adj. | 错误分类的 | Incorrectly placed in a category. | The books were miscategorized as fiction when they were actually biographies. 这些书被错误地归类为小说，而实际上它们是传记。 | The email was miscategorized as spam. 这封电子邮件被错误地归类为垃圾邮件。 | 近义词: misclassified, wrongly categorized | 反义词: correctly categorized, accurately classified | 词性:形容词

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + "categorize" (verb meaning "to place in a category") + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-", meaning "wrong," "bad," or "amiss." "Categorize" comes from the Greek "kategoria" meaning "predicate" or "accusation."
