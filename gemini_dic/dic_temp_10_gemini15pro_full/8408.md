anti-stalinist:/ˌæntiˈstɑːlɪnɪst/
adjective
反斯大林主义的
Opposed to the policies and practices of Joseph Stalin, the dictator of the Soviet Union from 1929 to 1953.
The anti-stalinist movement gained momentum after his death.  反斯大林主义运动在他死后获得了动力。
The author's anti-stalinist views are evident throughout his work. 作者的反斯大林主义观点在他的整个作品中都很明显。
近义词:dissident, nonconformist
反义词:stalinist, pro-Stalin
词性:adjective
