oversweetnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈswiːtnəsɪz/
n.
过分的甜味；甜腻
Excessive sweetness; a state of being too sweet.
The oversweetnesses of the dessert made her feel sick. 这道甜点的甜腻让她感到恶心。
Near Synonyms: cloyingness, saccharinity, sickliness
Antonyms: tartness, sourness, bitterness
noun
