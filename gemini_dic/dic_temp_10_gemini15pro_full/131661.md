nonconcentrations: /ˌnɒnkɒnsənˈtreɪʃənz/ | n. | 非集中; 非浓缩; 分散 |  The state of being dispersed or not concentrated.  Absence of concentration or focus. |  The report criticized the company's nonconcentrations of resources, leading to inefficiency.  报告批评了公司资源的非集中化，导致效率低下。 | 近义词: dispersions, scattering, diffusion | 反义词: concentrations, consolidation | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "concentration" (from Late Latin "concentratio," from "con-" (together) + "centrum" (center)).
