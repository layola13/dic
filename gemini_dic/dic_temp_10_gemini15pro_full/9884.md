apartness:/əˈpɑːrtnəs/
n.|隔离，分离，种族隔离|The policy of racial apartness in South Africa caused great suffering. 南非的种族隔离政策造成了巨大的苦难。|
例句：The sense of apartness she felt in a foreign country was overwhelming. 在异国他乡，她感到强烈的疏离感。|
例句：His emotional apartness made it difficult to connect with him. 他情感上的疏离让人很难与他建立联系。|
近义词：separation, isolation, segregation, aloofness|
反义词：togetherness, closeness, integration, connection|词性: 名词
