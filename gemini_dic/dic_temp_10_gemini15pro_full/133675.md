nonfraternal:/ˌnɑːnfrəˈtɜːrnl/
adjective
非同胞的，异父（母）的
Not having the same father and mother; not fraternal.
Example: The twins were nonfraternal, one being a boy and the other a girl. 这对双胞胎是异卵双胞胎，一个是男孩，一个是女孩。

Example:  They shared a close bond despite being nonfraternal siblings. 尽管不是同父同母的兄弟姐妹，他们之间的关系非常密切。

Synonym:  half, step, unrelated
Antonym: fraternal, twin, identical
Etymology:  non- (not) + fraternal (brotherly)  From Latin *non- (not) + fraternus (brotherly)
