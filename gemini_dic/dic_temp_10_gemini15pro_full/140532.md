oleograph:/ˈəʊliəɡrɑːf/ /ˈoʊliəɡræf/
noun
彩色石印画
A picture printed in oil colors.
The oleograph hung above the fireplace, depicting a bucolic scene.  彩色石印画挂在壁炉上方，描绘了一幅田园风光。
synonyms: chromolithograph, print, picture
antonyms: original painting, photograph
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From oleo- (oil) + -graph (writing, drawing).  Ultimately from Greek elaion (oil) and graphein (to write).
