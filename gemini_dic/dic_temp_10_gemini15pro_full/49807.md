例句：The book detailed the horrific acts of demonry performed by the cult. 这本书详细描述了邪教进行的可怕的恶魔行为。|近义词：witchcraft, sorcery, black magic, evil, wickedness|反义词：holiness, piety, virtue|词性:名词
