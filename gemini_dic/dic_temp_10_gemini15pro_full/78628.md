gibers:/ˈdʒaɪbərz/| n.|胡言乱语，无稽之谈|Meaningless or nonsensical talk; gibberish.  Often used in the plural.  Derived from "gibber," meaning to speak rapidly and unintelligibly.|He was spouting complete gibers about alien conspiracies.|近义词：nonsense, gibberish, balderdash, drivel|反义词:sense, truth|词性:noun
