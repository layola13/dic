anti-biblically:/ˌæntiˈbɪblɪkli/
| adv. |
| 反圣经地；与圣经教义相违背地 |
| Against the teachings or principles of the Bible.  |
| His lifestyle was considered anti-biblically by the church elders. 教会长老认为他的生活方式与圣经教义相违背。|

|  His actions were seen as anti-biblically by many religious scholars. 许多宗教学者认为他的行为是反圣经的。|
|  unscriptural, irreligious, profane, impious |
|  scriptural, pious, devout, religious |
| adverb |
