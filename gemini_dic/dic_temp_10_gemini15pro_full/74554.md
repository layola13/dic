fraudfully:/ˈfrɔːdjʊləli/ (US) /ˈfrɔːdjʊləli/ (UK)
adverb|欺诈地；欺骗地|In a fraudulent manner; deceitfully.  Done with the intent to deceive or cheat.|He obtained the money fraudulently by forging his father's signature. 他通过伪造父亲的签名骗取了这笔钱。|近义词: deceitfully, dishonestly, deceptively, falsely|反义词: honestly, truthfully, legitimately|词性: 副词
词源: From Late Middle English *fraudulentli*, from Anglo-Norman *fraudulentement*, from Old French *fraudeleusement*. Equivalent to fraudulent +‎ -ly.
