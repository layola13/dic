halls:/hɔːlz/ | n. | 大厅，礼堂，走廊 |  A large room or building used for public gatherings, meetings, or entertainment. Also, a corridor in a building. | We walked down the long halls of the museum. 我们沿着博物馆的长廊走着。| synonyms: corridors, passages, lobbies, auditoriums, chambers | antonyms: rooms, closets | noun

halls:/hɔːlz/ | n. | (尤指大学的)宿舍 | A building or part of a building in which students live. |  She lived in the university halls during her first year.  她第一年住在大学宿舍。 | synonyms: dormitories, residence halls | antonyms: homes, apartments | noun
