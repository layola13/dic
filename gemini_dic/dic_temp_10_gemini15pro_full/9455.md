antireform:/ˌænti rɪˈfɔːrm/
adjective
反改革的
Opposed to or resisting reform.
The antireform movement hindered progress for decades. 反改革运动阻碍了几十年的进步。
The government faced strong antireform sentiment. 政府面临着强烈的反改革情绪。
近义词: reactionary, conservative, die-hard
反义词: reformist, progressive, liberal
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From anti- + reform.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against," "opposite of," or "hostile to." "Reform" comes from the Old French reformer and ultimately from the Latin reformare, meaning "to form again," "to reshape."
