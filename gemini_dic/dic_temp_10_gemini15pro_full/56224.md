doers:/ˈduːərz/| n. |实干家，行动者|People who take action rather than just talking about it.  那些付诸行动而不是光说不做的人。|例句：The doers in the company are the ones who get things done. 公司里那些实干家是把事情做成的人。

例句：Doers are valued more than talkers in this organization.  在这个组织里，实干家比空谈家更有价值。|近义词：actors, performers, achievers, executors|反义词:talkers, procrastinators, idlers|词性:noun

Etymology: From Middle English *doere*, from Old English *dēore*, agent noun from *dōn* (“to do”). Cognate with Dutch *doener*, German *Tuer*.  源于中古英语 *doere*，来自古英语 *dēore*，是动词 *dōn*（“做”）的施动者名词。与荷兰语 *doener*、德语 *Tuer* 同源。
