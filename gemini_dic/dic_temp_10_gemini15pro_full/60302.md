eggshells:/ˈɛɡʃɛlz/| n. | 蛋壳；如履薄冰；小心翼翼的状态 |Fragments of eggs' outer coverings.  Figuratively, a state of extreme sensitivity or nervousness. |She was so nervous she felt like she was walking on eggshells. 她非常紧张，感觉自己如履薄冰。|近义词：fragile, delicate, sensitive, tense, precarious|反义词:robust, strong, secure, confident, relaxed|noun
