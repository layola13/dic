nonsilvers:/nɒnˈsɪlvəz/
noun
非银金属; 非银质餐具
metals other than silver; tableware not made of silver
The nonsilvers include copper, nickel, and aluminum. 这些非银金属包括铜、镍和铝。
The museum's collection includes both silvers and nonsilvers.  博物馆的收藏包括银器和非银器。
Synonyms: base metals
Antonyms: silvers, silverware
