baloney:/bəˈloʊni/
|n.|胡说，废话；熏肠|Nonsense; foolishness; a type of sausage.|He's full of baloney. 他满口胡言。|近义词：nonsense, rubbish, hogwash, bunk, claptrap|反义词：truth, fact|词性:名词

baloneys:/bəˈloʊniz/
|n.|胡说八道（baloney的复数形式，不常用）|Plural form of baloney, rarely used.|All those promises were just a bunch of baloneys. 所有的承诺都只是一堆废话。|近义词：nonsense, rubbish, hogwash, bunk, claptrap|反义词：truth, fact|词性:名词
