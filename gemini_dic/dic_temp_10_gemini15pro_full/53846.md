discontentation:/dɪskənˌtɛnˈteɪʃən/| n. |不满，不满足|A state of being dissatisfied or discontented; lack of satisfaction.|The workers' discontentation led to a strike. 工人们的不满导致了罢工。|近义词：dissatisfaction, disgruntlement, unhappiness|反义词：contentment, satisfaction, happiness|词性:名词
discontent:/dɪskənˈtɛnt/| n. adj. |不满，不满足的|Lack of contentment; dissatisfaction.|There was widespread discontent about the new policy. 新政策引起了广泛的不满。|近义词：dissatisfaction, unhappiness|反义词：contentment, happiness|词性:名词/形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French descontentement, from des- (expressing negation, privation) + contentement (contentment), from contenter (to content), from Latin contentus (content), past participle of continēre (to contain, hold together).  Ultimately from Latin com- (together) + tenēre (to hold).
