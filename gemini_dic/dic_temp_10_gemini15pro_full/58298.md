dry-cleanable:/ˌdraɪˈkliːnəbl/
adjective
可干洗的
able to be cleaned by dry cleaning.
This silk dress is dry-cleanable. 这件丝绸连衣裙是可干洗的。
The coat is expensive but dry-cleanable, so it's worth the investment.  这件外套很贵，但可以干洗，所以值得投资。
近义词:washable, launderable
反义词:non-dry-cleanable, hand-wash only
词性:adjective
