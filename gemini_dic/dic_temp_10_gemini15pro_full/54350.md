dishome:/dɪsˈhoʊm/ | v. | 使无家可归；逐出家门 | To deprive someone of a home; to make someone homeless. | The war dishomed thousands of families. 战争使成千上万的家庭无家可归。|  They were dishomed by the flood. 他们因洪水而无家可归。 | 近义词: displace, evict, exile | 反义词: house, shelter | 词性: 动词

词源:  "dis-" (expressing negation or reversal) + "home" (one's dwelling place).  The prefix "dis-" changes the meaning of "home" to its opposite, creating the meaning of "to deprive of a home."
