braindamaged:/ˈbreɪnˌdæmɪdʒd/
adjective
脑损伤的
Having suffered damage to the brain, which has resulted in impaired intellectual functioning.
He suffered a severe head injury in the accident, leaving him braindamaged. 他在事故中头部受重伤，导致脑损伤。
The braindamaged patient struggled with basic tasks.  这位脑损伤的病人连基本的任务都难以完成。

近义词: brain-injured, mentally disabled, intellectually impaired
反义词: healthy, unimpaired, sound

词性: adjective
词源:  A compound word from "brain" and "damaged".  "Brain" comes from Old English *brægen. "Damaged" comes from the verb "damage," from Old French *damager.
