crowstone:/ˈkroʊˌstoʊn/| n. |界石，边界石，鸦石|A large stone, often placed at the edge of a property or parish, to mark a boundary. Historically, some were also used as mooring posts for barges. |例句：The crowstone marked the boundary between the two estates. 这块鸦石标志着两处地产的边界。

例句：The barge was moored to the ancient crowstone. 这艘驳船被系泊在古老的鸦石上。|近义词：boundary stone, landmark, border stone|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
