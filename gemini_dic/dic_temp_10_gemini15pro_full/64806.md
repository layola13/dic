esops:/ˈiːsɒps/| n.|员工持股计划 (Employee Stock Ownership Plans)|An ESOP is a qualified defined-contribution employee benefit plan designed to invest primarily in the stock of the sponsoring employer.|Many companies offer ESOPs as a way to incentivize employees and improve retention. 许多公司提供员工持股计划作为激励员工和提高留任率的一种方式。|近义词:employee stock ownership plan, stock option plan|反义词:N/A|noun
