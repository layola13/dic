nag:/næɡ/| v. n.|唠叨，抱怨；令人烦恼的人或事物|To annoy or irritate persistently with fault-finding or urging.|She nagged him constantly about his messy room. 她不断地唠叨他房间很乱。|近义词：pester, badger, harass, annoy|反义词:praise, commend|词性:动词,名词
nag:/næɡ/| n.|令人烦恼的人或事物|A person or thing that annoys or irritates persistently.|The dripping faucet was a constant nag. 滴水的水龙头一直很烦人。|近义词：nuisance, pest|反义词:pleasure, delight|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *naggen*, of uncertain origin, perhaps of Scandinavian origin.  Possibly related to Old Norse *nagga* (to gnaw, bite).
