ductilenesses:/ˌdʌktɪlɪtɪz/| n.|延展性，韧性|The quality of being ductile; capable of being drawn out into wire or thread; malleability.|The ductilenesses of copper and gold make them ideal for jewelry making. 铜和金的延展性使它们成为制作珠宝的理想材料。|近义词：malleability, plasticity, flexibility|反义词: brittleness, rigidity|noun

ductility:/ˌdʌkˈtɪləti/| n.|延展性，韧性|The quality of being ductile; capable of being drawn out into wire or thread; malleability.|The ductility of copper allows it to be easily shaped into wires. 铜的延展性使其易于被塑造成电线。|近义词：malleability, plasticity, flexibility|反义词: brittleness, rigidity|noun
