hematachometer: /ˌhiːmətəˈkɒmɪtər/ (US) /ˌhiːmətəˈkɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |血流速度计，血速计|An instrument for measuring the velocity of the blood current.|The doctor used a hematachometer to measure the blood flow in the patient's artery. 医生用血流速度计测量病人动脉的血流速度。|Synonyms:  rheometer, blood flow meter|Antonyms: None|noun
