normie:/ˈnɔːrmi/| n.|普通人，主流人士，缺乏独特个性或兴趣的人|A person who is considered to be conventional or mainstream in their tastes, interests, and lifestyle, often perceived as lacking individuality or coolness.|例句：He's such a normie, he only listens to top 40 music. 他真是个普通人，只听排行榜前40名的音乐。

例句：She felt like a normie at the comic convention, surrounded by people in elaborate costumes. 在漫画大会上，她感觉自己像个普通人，周围都是穿着精心制作服装的人。|近义词：basic, mainstream, conventional|反义词:hipster, nonconformist, alternative|词性:名词
