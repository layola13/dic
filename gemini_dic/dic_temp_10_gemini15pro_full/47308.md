de-intensify:/diːɪnˈtɛnsɪfaɪ/
verb
降低强度，缓和
To reduce the intensity or severity of something.
The government is trying to de-intensify the conflict. 政府正试图缓和冲突。
The therapist helped him de-intensify his anger.  治疗师帮助他缓和愤怒。
synonyms: lessen, reduce, diminish, mitigate, alleviate, moderate, temper, tone down, weaken, soften, subdue
antonyms: intensify, escalate, heighten, aggravate, exacerbate
词性: 动词

de-intensification:/diːɪnˌtɛnsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
noun
降低强度，缓和
The act of reducing the intensity or severity of something.
The de-intensification of the conflict was welcomed by all parties. 冲突的缓和受到了各方的欢迎。
There has been a de-intensification of the violence in recent weeks. 最近几周暴力事件有所缓和。
synonyms: lessening, reduction, diminution, mitigation, alleviation, moderation, tempering, toning down, weakening, softening, subduing
antonyms: intensification, escalation, heightening, aggravation, exacerbation
词性: 名词
