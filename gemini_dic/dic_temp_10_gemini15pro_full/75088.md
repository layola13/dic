frillier:/ˈfrɪliər/| adj.|更加褶边多的，更加花哨的|Comparative form of frilly: more frilly.|The dress she wore was even frillier than the last one. 她穿的裙子比上一条更加褶边多。|Synonyms: fancier, more ornate, more elaborate|Antonyms: plainer, simpler, less ornamented|adjective

frilly:/ˈfrɪli/| adj.|有褶边的，花哨的|Decorated with frills or ruffles; elaborate and showy.|She wore a frilly pink dress. 她穿了一件粉红色的褶边连衣裙。|Synonyms: lacy, ruffled, ornate|Antonyms: plain, simple, unadorned|adjective

Etymology:  From *frill* + *-y*.  *Frill* is of uncertain origin, possibly imitative.
