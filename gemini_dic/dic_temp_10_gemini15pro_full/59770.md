economizer:/iˈkɒnəmaɪzə(r)/ (UK) /iˈkɑːnəmaɪzər/ (US)| n.|节约器，省油器； economizers (plural)|A device, especially in a steam boiler, that preheats feed water by using the heat of the flue gases.  It improves boiler efficiency by recovering waste heat and reducing fuel consumption. |The power plant installed new economizers to reduce energy costs.|Synonyms: fuel saver, energy saver, heat recovery unit|Antonyms: waster, spendthrift|noun

economizer:/iˈkɒnəmaɪzə(r)/ (UK) /iˈkɑːnəmaɪzər/ (US)| n.|节俭的人； economizers (plural)|A person who avoids waste and practices thrift. |He's a real economizer, always looking for ways to save money.|Synonyms: penny-pincher, tightwad, frugal person|Antonyms: spendthrift, squanderer|noun
