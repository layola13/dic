calorimetry:/ˌkæləˈrɪmətri/ | n. |量热法，热量测定法 |The process of measuring the heat of chemical reactions or physical changes.  | Calorimetry is essential for determining the energy content of foods. 量热法对于测定食物的能量含量至关重要。|  synonyms: thermal analysis, heat measurement | antonyms: N/A | noun
