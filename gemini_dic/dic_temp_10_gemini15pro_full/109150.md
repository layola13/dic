legislatorship:/ˈledʒɪsleɪtərʃɪp/
n.
立法权；立法机构任期；议员身份
the state or quality of being a legislator; the term of office of a legislator
His governorship ended, but he retained his legislatorship. 他的州长任期结束了，但他保留了议员身份。
近义词：legislation, lawmaking
反义词：N/A
词性：noun

legislatorship:/ˈledʒɪsleɪtərʃɪp/
词源：From legislate + -or + -ship.  Ultimately from Latin *lēgislātor, from lēgis (“of the law”) + lātor (“proposer”), from lātus (“carried, borne”).
