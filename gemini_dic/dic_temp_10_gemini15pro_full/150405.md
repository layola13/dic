panpsychism:/pænˈsaɪkɪzəm/| n.|泛心论|The theory that mind or consciousness is a fundamental and ubiquitous feature of reality, present in some form in all things.|Panpsychism offers a potential solution to the mind-body problem. 泛心论为身心问题提供了一个可能的解决方案。|
|Synonyms:  universal consciousness, panexperientialism, cosmopsychism|Antonyms: materialism, physicalism|noun|
