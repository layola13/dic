dinoxide: /daɪˈnɒksaɪd/ | n. | 二氮氧化物 (化学) |  A compound of nitrogen and oxygen, specifically N₂O₂.  It is a dimer of nitric oxide (NO).  It exists as a transient intermediate in reactions involving nitric oxide. | Nitric oxide reacts with oxygen to form dinoxide. 一氧化氮与氧反应生成二氮氧化物。| synonym: dinitrogen dioxide | antonym: N/A | noun
