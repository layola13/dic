nontumultuous: /ˌnɒn.tʃuːˈmʌl.tʃu.əs/ | adj. | 非骚乱的；平静的；安静的 | Not tumultuous; peaceful and quiet. Characterized by the absence of tumult or unrest. | The meeting was nontumultuous, with everyone calmly expressing their opinions.  会议很平静，每个人都心平气和地表达了自己的意见。| The library offered a nontumultuous environment for studying. 图书馆提供了一个安静的学习环境。 | 近义词: peaceful, calm, tranquil, serene, quiet | 反义词: tumultuous, turbulent, chaotic, noisy, riotous | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From non- + tumultuous. "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "tumultuous" comes from the Latin word "tumultus," meaning "uproar" or "commotion."
