nonentitous:/ˌnɒnɛnˈtaɪtəs/
adjective
虚无的；不存在的；无实体的
Having no existence; not real; having no essence or substance.
His nonentitous claims were quickly dismissed by the court. 他那些虚无缥缈的主张很快就被法庭驳回了。
Near Synonyms: nonexistent, unreal, imaginary, fictitious, illusory, chimerical
Antonyms: real, existent, actual, substantial, concrete
词性: adjective
