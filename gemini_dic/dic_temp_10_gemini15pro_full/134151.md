nonimpressionableness:/ˌnɑːnɪmˈprɛʃənəbəlnəs/
n.
不易受影响；无动于衷
The state of being difficult to impress or influence.
His nonimpressionableness made him a difficult person to persuade. 他不易受影响，很难被说服。
Synonyms: impassiveness, insensitivity, apathy, indifference
Antonyms: impressionability, sensitivity, responsiveness
词性: 名词


Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + impressionable + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
