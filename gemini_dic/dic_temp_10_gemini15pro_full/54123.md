disenchantment:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtʃæntmənt/ | n. | 失望；醒悟；解除魔法 | A feeling of disappointment about someone or something that you previously respected or admired; the state of being free from illusion. |  Example 1:  There is growing disenchantment with the government.  对政府越来越失望。

Example 2: He experienced a sense of disenchantment with his job.  他对自己的工作感到失望。 | Synonyms: disillusionment, disappointment, dissatisfaction | Antonyms: enchantment, fascination, contentment | noun
