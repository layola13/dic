glyceryl:/ˈɡlɪsərəl/| n. |甘油基；丙三醇基|A radical derived from glycerol by the removal of one or more hydroxyl groups.  It forms the basis of many fats and oils.|Glyceryl trinitrate is a powerful explosive. 甘油三硝酸是一种强力炸药。|近义词:glycerin, glycerol (when referring to the whole molecule)|反义词: None (It's a chemical component, not a concept with an opposite)|noun
