machian:/ˈmeɪkiən/ | adj. | 马赫的；符合马赫原理的 | Relating to the theories of Ernst Mach, especially his principle that inertia depends on the interaction of all matter in the universe. |  The concept of machian universes is complex and requires a deep understanding of physics. 马赫宇宙的概念很复杂，需要对物理学有深入的理解。

The physicist proposed a machian explanation for the origin of inertia.  这位物理学家提出了一个关于惯性起源的马赫式解释。 | 近义词: relational (关系的), relative (相对的) | 反义词: absolute (绝对的), Newtonian (牛顿的) | 词性: adjective
