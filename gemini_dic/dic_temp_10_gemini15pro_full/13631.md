attires:/əˈtaɪərz/| n.| 服装，盛装| Clothes, especially fine or formal clothes. |The queen's attires were always magnificent. 女王的服装总是很华丽。|近义词：clothes, garments, apparel, outfits|反义词：nudes, rags|noun

attire:/əˈtaɪər/| vt.| 穿着，打扮| To dress, clothe, or array, especially in fine or festive garments. |He was attired in a black suit. 他穿着一套黑色西装。|近义词：dress, clothe, garb, array|反义词：undress, disrobe|verb
