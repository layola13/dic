outbuying:/ˌaʊtˈbaɪɪŋ/
verb
超过…的购买力; 比…买得更多
The act of buying more than someone else, especially of a particular commodity.
China is outbuying the US in many global commodity markets. 中国在许多全球大宗商品市场的购买量超过了美国。
Synonyms: outspending, surpassing
Antonyms: underspending, losing (in a bidding war)
词性: 动词
