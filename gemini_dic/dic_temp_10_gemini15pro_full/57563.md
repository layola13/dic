dramatis personae:/drəˈmætɪs pərˈsoʊniː/ |n.| 剧中人; 戏剧角色 | The dramatis personae of the play included a king, a queen, and a jester.  这个戏剧的剧中人物包括一个国王，一个王后和一个小丑。 | synonyms: characters, cast, actors, players | antonyms: audience, spectators | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dramatis personae*, literally, "persons of the drama".  "Dramatis" is the genitive plural of *drama*, and *personae* is the plural of *persona* (mask, character). 源自拉丁语 *dramatis personae*，字面意思是“戏剧中的人物”。 "Dramatis" 是 *drama* 的复数属格，*personae* 是 *persona*（面具，角色）的复数。
