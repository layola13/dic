heroku:/ˈhɛrəkuː/ | n. | Heroku | A cloud platform as a service (PaaS) supporting several programming languages.  It allows developers to build, run, and operate applications entirely in the cloud. |  I deployed my new web application to Heroku. 我把我的新网络应用程序部署到Heroku。| 近义词: Netlify, AWS Elastic Beanstalk, Google App Engine | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific brand name.  One could consider "on-premise server" as a conceptual opposite.) | 词性: noun
