lavages:/ˈlɑːvɪdʒɪz/
n.
清洗，灌洗（尤指医疗上的）
The act of washing or rinsing a body cavity, such as the stomach or bowel.
Gastric lavage was performed to remove the ingested poison.  进行了胃灌洗以清除摄入的毒物。
近义词：washings, rinses
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "contamination")
词性: noun


lavage:/ˈlɑːvɑːʒ/
n.
清洗，灌洗（尤指医疗上的）
The act of washing or rinsing a body cavity, such as the stomach or bowel.
The doctor ordered a bronchoalveolar lavage to diagnose the lung infection. 医生下令进行支气管肺泡灌洗以诊断肺部感染。
近义词：washing, rinsing
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "contamination")
词性: noun


词源: From French "lavage," from "laver" (to wash), from Latin "lavāre" (to wash).
