disappointedly:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntɪdli/ | adv. |失望地|In a manner expressing disappointment; sadly.  Showing that you are unhappy because something you hoped for did not happen.| She sighed disappointedly when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息后失望地叹了口气。|近义词：sadly, glumly, dejectedly|反义词：happily, joyfully, excitedly|词性:副词
Etymology: From disappointed + -ly. Disappointed comes from the verb disappoint, which originated from the Old French desapointer, meaning "to undo an appointment."
