franion:/ˈfrænjən/| n.|伙伴，朋友，密友|A close friend or companion; a comrade.|例句：He confided his secrets to his trusted franion. 他向他信任的密友倾诉了他的秘密。

例句：The two franions embarked on a perilous journey together.  这两个伙伴一起踏上了一段危险的旅程。|近义词：friend, companion, comrade, pal, chum|反义词：enemy, foe|词性: noun

词源: From Middle French *franion, itself from Old French fraignon, a derivative of the verb fraindre "to break". The original meaning was "a rascal, a scoundrel", later evolving to "a boon companion, a close friend".
