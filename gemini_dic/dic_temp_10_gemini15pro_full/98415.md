insouciance:/ɪnˈsuːsiəns/｜n.｜无忧无虑；漫不经心；漠不关心｜Lack of concern; indifference.  A carefree attitude or state of mind.｜She faced the future with cheerful insouciance. 她以愉快的无忧无虑面对未来。｜Synonyms: nonchalance, unconcern, indifference, carelessness, lightheartedness｜Antonyms: anxiety, worry, concern, solicitude, care｜noun
