extractively:/ɪkˈstræktɪvli/
adverb|提取地，萃取地|in a way that involves extracting something.|例句：The oil is extractively obtained from olives. 这种油是从橄榄中提取出来的。|近义词：by extraction|反义词: synthetically|词性: 副词

extractive:/ɪkˈstræktɪv/
adjective|提取的，萃取的|relating to or involving extraction.|例句：Extractive industries are important to the economy. 采掘业对经济很重要。|近义词：mining, quarrying|反义词: synthetic|词性: 形容词

extract:/ɪkˈstrækt/
verb|提取，萃取|remove or take out, especially by effort or force.|例句：They extracted the oil from olives. 他们从橄榄中提取油。|近义词：remove, obtain|反义词: insert, implant|词性: 动词
extract:/ˈekstrækt/
noun|提取物，摘录|a short passage taken from a piece of writing, music, or film.|例句：The following extract is from his latest novel.  以下摘录来自他最新的小说。|近义词：excerpt, passage|反义词: whole, complete work|词性: 名词
