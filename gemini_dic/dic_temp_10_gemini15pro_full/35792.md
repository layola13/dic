cockcrowing:/ˈkɒkˌkrəʊɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkˌkroʊɪŋ/ (US)| n.|鸡鸣，清晨|The sound of a rooster crowing, typically at dawn.  The time of day shortly before sunrise when roosters crow.|The cockcrowing woke me up. 鸡鸣声把我吵醒了。|Synonyms: dawn, daybreak, sunrise|Antonyms: sunset, dusk, nightfall|noun

cockcrowing:/ˈkɒkˌkrəʊɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkˌkroʊɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|（发生在）清晨的|Relating to or occurring at the time of cockcrowing.|He went for a cockcrowing walk. 他在清晨散步。|Synonyms: early morning, predawn|Antonyms: evening, late|adjective
