pandemics:/pænˈdɛmɪks/
|n.| 流行病，大流行病 |A pandemic is an epidemic of an infectious disease that has spread across a large region, for instance multiple continents or worldwide, affecting a substantial number of people.|The COVID-19 pandemic has impacted lives globally.  新冠疫情已经影响了全球人民的生活。| The 1918 influenza pandemic was devastating. 1918年的流感大流行是毁灭性的。| epidemic, outbreak, widespread disease |localized disease, endemic|noun
