molehilly:/ˈmoʊlˌhɪli/| adj.|多鼹鼠丘的，起伏不平的|Covered with molehills; uneven, bumpy.|The field was molehilly and difficult to walk across.  这块田地有很多鼹鼠丘，很难走过去。|The golf course was surprisingly molehilly.  高尔夫球场出奇地起伏不平。|近义词：uneven, bumpy, lumpy, undulating|反义词:flat, smooth, even|词性:形容词
Etymology: mole + hill + -y. From "mole" (the burrowing animal) + "hill" (a small mound) + the adjective-forming suffix "-y" indicating "full of" or "characterized by".
