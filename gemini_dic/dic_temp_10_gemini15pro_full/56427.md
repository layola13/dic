dollie:/ˈdɑːli/ (US), /ˈdɒli/ (UK)| n.|小玩偶，小推车，移动摄影车|A small doll, often a child's toy; a small wheeled platform or frame used for moving heavy objects; a wheeled platform for a movie or television camera.|例句：The little girl loves playing with her dollie. 小女孩喜欢玩她的洋娃娃。


例句：The movers used a dollie to transport the refrigerator. 搬运工用小推车运送冰箱。
