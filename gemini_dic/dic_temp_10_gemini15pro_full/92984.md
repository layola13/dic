idiolatry: /aɪˈdɒlətri/ (US) /aɪˈdɒlətri/ (UK) | n. | 偶像崇拜；盲目崇拜 |  Extreme admiration, love, or reverence for something or someone, especially when considered excessive or misplaced. |  His idolatry of his favorite band bordered on obsession. 他对他最喜欢的乐队的崇拜近乎痴迷。 | synonyms: worship, adoration, veneration, deification | antonyms: disdain, contempt, disregard | noun
