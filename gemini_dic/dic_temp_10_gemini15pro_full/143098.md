outbribes: /ˌaʊtˈbraɪbz/
verb
用贿赂胜过
To surpass or defeat someone by offering a larger bribe.
The corrupt official was outbribed by a rival company.  这个腐败的官员被一家竞争公司用更高的贿赂收买了。
He outbribed his competitors to secure the contract. 他用更高的贿赂胜过竞争对手，从而获得了合同。
近义词: outbid, overbribe
反义词: underbid
