half-indignant:/ˌhæf ɪnˈdɪɡnənt/
| adj. | 半愤慨的，略带愤怒的 | Feeling or expressing mild indignation; slightly offended or resentful. | She gave him a half-indignant, half-amused look. 她给了他一个半愤慨、半好笑的眼神。 | Example:  He let out a half-indignant snort. 他发出一声略带愤怒的哼声。|Synonyms:  slightly annoyed, mildly offended, somewhat resentful | Antonyms: pleased, delighted, happy | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "half" + "indignant". "Half" means partially or to some extent. "Indignant" comes from the Latin "indignari" meaning "to deem unworthy".
