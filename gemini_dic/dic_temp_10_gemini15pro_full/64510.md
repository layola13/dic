erosions:/ɪˈroʊʒənz/
n.
腐蚀，侵蚀；磨损；削弱
the process of eroding or being eroded by wind, water, or other natural agents.
The erosions of the coastline are caused by the constant pounding of the waves. 海岸线的侵蚀是由海浪的不断冲击造成的。
近义词：corrosion, abrasion, attrition, weathering, decay
反义词：accretion, deposition, buildup
词性：名词(plural)
