explications:/ˌɛksplɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |解释，说明，阐释|  A detailed explanation or interpretation of a text, theory, or idea.  The professor offered several explications of the poem's complex symbolism. | 近义词: interpretations, explanations, clarifications, elucidations | 反义词: obfuscations, misinterpretations, distortions | noun
