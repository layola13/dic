-cessor:/ˈsɛsər/| n. |接替者，继承者|Someone who takes a position or office after someone else.|The new CEO is the predecessor's successor. 新任首席执行官是前任的继任者。|近义词：heir, inheritor, follower, replacement|反义词:predecessor|词性:名词后缀

词源:
From Latin -cessor, from cedere "to yield, withdraw".  It forms nouns indicating a person who follows or takes over from another.
