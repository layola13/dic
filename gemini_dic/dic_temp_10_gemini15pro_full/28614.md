caressingly: /kəˈrɛsɪŋli/ | adv. | 爱抚地，抚摸地 | In a way that shows affection by touching or handling someone or something gently. | She stroked the cat caressingly. 她爱抚地摸了摸猫。| 近义词：tenderly, lovingly, affectionately | 反义词：roughly, harshly, coldly | 词性: 副词

词源: From caress + -ing + -ly.  "Caress" comes from the Old French word "caressier," meaning "to touch gently," ultimately from the Latin "carus," meaning "dear."
