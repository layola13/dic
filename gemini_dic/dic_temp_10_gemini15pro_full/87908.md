hieroglyphic:/ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪk/ | adj. n. | 象形文字的；象形符号 | relating to or written in hieroglyphics, a system of writing using picture-like symbols; a picture or symbol representing a word, syllable, or sound, especially in ancient Egyptian writing. |  The Rosetta Stone helped scholars decipher Egyptian hieroglyphics.  罗塞塔石碑帮助学者们破译了埃及象形文字。| synonyms: pictographic, ideographic | antonyms: alphabetic | adjective

hieroglyphic:/ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪk/ | n. | 象形文字；象形符号 | a picture or symbol representing a word, syllable, or sound, especially in ancient Egyptian writing. |  The walls of the tomb were covered in hieroglyphics. 墓穴的墙壁上覆盖着象形文字。 | synonyms: pictograph, ideogram | antonyms: letter, character | noun
