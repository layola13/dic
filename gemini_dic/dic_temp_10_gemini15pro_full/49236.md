Almost exclusively used in the phrase "Carthago delenda est".|Example:  "Cato the Elder famously ended every speech in the Roman Senate with 'Carthago delenda est.'"  老加图以在罗马元老院的每一次演讲都以“迦太基必须毁灭”结束而闻名。|Synonym: destructible, doomed|Antonym: preservable, protected|adjective
