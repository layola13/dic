inhabitancy:/ɪnˈhæbɪtənsi/
n.
居住; 居住权; 居住期间
The state or fact of inhabiting a place; residence.
The period of time during which a place is inhabited.
The legal right to inhabit a place.
The family enjoyed their inhabitancy of the old farmhouse.  这个家庭享受着他们在这座古老农舍的居住时光。
The inhabitancy of the island dates back centuries.  这个岛屿的居住历史可以追溯到几个世纪以前。
His inhabitancy of the apartment was short-lived. 他在这套公寓的居住时间很短。

近义词: residence, occupation, tenancy, habitation
反义词: absence, vacancy
