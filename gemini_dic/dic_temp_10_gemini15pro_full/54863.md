displease:/dɪsˈpliːz/
| vt.|使不愉快，使生气，得罪|To cause someone to be annoyed, unhappy, or unsatisfied.|His constant complaining displeases me. 他不断的抱怨让我很不高兴。|近义词：annoy, irritate, offend, vex|反义词：please, delight, gratify|verb

displeased:/dɪsˈpliːzd/
| adj.|不高兴的，生气的|Feeling or showing annoyance or displeasure.|She was displeased with his behavior. 她对他的行为感到不高兴。|近义词：unhappy, dissatisfied, annoyed, irritated|反义词：pleased, happy, satisfied|adjective
