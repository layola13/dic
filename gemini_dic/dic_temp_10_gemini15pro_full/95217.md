inbounds:/ˈɪnbaʊndz/| adj.|界内的，有效的，在范围内的|Within the established boundaries or limits; allowed by the rules.|The basketball player made an inbounds pass. 篮球运动员进行了一次界内传球。|Synonyms:  within bounds, inside, legal, permissible|Antonyms: out of bounds, outside, illegal, impermissible|adjective
