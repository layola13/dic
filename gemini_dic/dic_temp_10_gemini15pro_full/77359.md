gauffering:/ˈɡɔːfərɪŋ/
noun
压花，压制花纹，(尤指)用热熨斗在布料上压出褶子
The act or process of creating a textured surface, especially by pressing a heated tool onto fabric.
The gauffering on the silk dress added a touch of elegance.  这件丝绸连衣裙上的压花增添了一丝优雅。
embossing, decorating, ornamenting
smoothing, flattening
n.
