centrales:/sɑ̃ˈtʁal/ (French pronunciation)| adj. n.|中心的，中央的；总站；中心机构|Central refers to being in the middle or the most important part of something. It can also refer to a main office or control center.  It's derived from the Latin word "centralis".|The central argument of the book is convincing. 这本书的中心论点令人信服。|The centrales of the nervous system are the brain and spinal cord. 神经系统的中枢是脑和脊髓。|近义词：main, chief, principal, core|反义词:peripheral, outer, subsidiary|词性:adjective, noun (plural)
