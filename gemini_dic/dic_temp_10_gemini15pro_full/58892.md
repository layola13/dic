duress:/ˈdjʊərɛs/, /dʊˈrɛs/| n. |胁迫，强迫|Compulsion by threat; forcible confinement.|He signed the confession under duress. 他是在胁迫下签署了供词。|近义词：coercion, compulsion, force, intimidation, pressure|反义词:persuasion, freedom, liberty|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old French *durece "hardness, severity," from Latin dūritia "hardness," from dūrus "hard."
