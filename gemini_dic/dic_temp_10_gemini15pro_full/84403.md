halloed:/ˈhæləʊd/ (UK) /ˈhæloʊd/ (US)| v. |被视为神圣的；被誉为英雄的|Made holy; regarded as holy; greatly revered or admired.|例句：The soldiers who died in the war were halloed as heroes.  那些在战争中牺牲的士兵被誉为英雄。


例句：His name was halloed throughout the land. 他的名字在全国各地都受到崇敬。


近义词：revered, venerated, consecrated, extolled|反义词：desecrated, profaned, condemned|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *halowien, from Old English *hālgian, from Proto-Germanic *hailagōną, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱélyḱ (“to shout, call”).  Related to holy. The sense shifted from "to make holy" to "to honor as holy."
