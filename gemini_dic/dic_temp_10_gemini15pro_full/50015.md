denominate:/dɪˈnɒmɪneɪt/ |dɪˈnɑːmɪneɪt/| v. |命名，称呼；以…为单位|To give a name or designation to something. To express or measure something in a specified unit.|The scientist denominated the newly discovered species "Aurora Borealis." 科学家将新发现的物种命名为“北极光”。

The currency is denominated in euros.  这种货币以欧元为单位。|近义词：name, call, designate, term, entitle, label|反义词: N/A |词性: verb

denominate:/dɪˈnɒmɪneɪt/ |dɪˈnɑːmɪneɪt/| adj. |(货币) 面额为…的|Having a specified denomination (in reference to currency). |The hundred-dollar bills were denominated in US currency.  这些百元钞票的面额是美元。|近义词：specified, designated|反义词: N/A |词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin denominatus, past participle of denominare "to name," from Latin de- "completely" + nominare "to name," from nomen "name."
