absentaneous: /ˌæbsənˈteɪniəs/
adjective
缺席的；心不在焉的
Relating to or characterized by absence, especially habitual absence of mind or attention.
He had an absentaneous air about him, as if his mind were elsewhere. 他一副心不在焉的样子，好像心不在焉。

The professor noticed the student's absentaneous gaze during the lecture. 教授注意到学生在讲课时心不在焉的目光。

synonym: absent-minded, distracted, inattentive, preoccupied, abstracted
antonym: attentive, present, focused, concentrated
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin absens (absent) + -aneous (suffix forming adjectives).

USAGE->
This word is not commonly used in modern English.  It's considered somewhat archaic or literary.  "Absent-minded" or "distracted" are more frequent choices.
