ovaries:/ˈoʊvəriːz/| n. |卵巢|Female reproductive organs that produce eggs (ova) and hormones such as estrogen and progesterone. |例句：The ovaries release an egg each month during ovulation. 卵巢在排卵期每月释放一个卵子。

例句：The doctor examined her ovaries for cysts. 医生检查了她的卵巢是否有囊肿。|近义词：female gonads|反义词:testes (male gonads)|Noun
