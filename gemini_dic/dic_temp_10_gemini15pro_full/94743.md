improvident:/ɪmˈprɒvɪdənt/ (US) /ɪmˈprɑːvɪdənt/| adj.|挥霍的；浪费的；不顾将来的；无远见的|Not having or showing foresight; spendthrift or thoughtless.  Lacking thrift or prudence; neglecting to provide for future needs.|He was improvident and quickly spent all his money. 他挥霍无度，很快就花光了所有的钱。|Synonyms: wasteful, prodigal, reckless, spendthrift, extravagant|Antonyms: thrifty, frugal, provident, cautious, prudent|adjective
