hypothesizers:/haɪˈpɑːθəsaɪzərz/
n.
假设者，提出假设的人
People who formulate hypotheses; those who engage in hypothesizing.
The hypothesizers presented their theories at the conference.  假设者们在会议上提出了他们的理论。
Scientists are often hypothesizers, constantly testing new ideas. 科学家通常是假设者，不断地测试新的想法。
近义词:theorists, speculators, conjecturers
反义词:experimenter, pragmatist
词性:noun (plural)
