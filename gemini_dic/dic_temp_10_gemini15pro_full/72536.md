fluster:/ˈflʌstər/ (US), /ˈflʌstə(r)/ (UK)| v. n.|使慌乱，使不安；慌乱，不安|To make someone nervous and confused, or the state of being nervous and confused.|The unexpected question flustered her and she couldn't answer.  突如其来的问题让她慌了神，答不上来。|The tough interview questions left him in a fluster.  棘手的面试问题让他不知所措。|近义词:unnerve, agitate, disconcert, confuse|反义词:calm, compose, reassure|词性:verb, noun
