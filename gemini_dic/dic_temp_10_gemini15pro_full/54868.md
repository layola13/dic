displeases:/dɪsˈpliːziz/
| v. |使不悦，使生气，惹恼|To cause someone to be annoyed, unhappy, or dissatisfied.|His constant complaining displeases me. 他不停的抱怨让我很生气。|近义词：annoys, irritates, offends, bothers|反义词：pleases, delights, gratifies|词性：动词
Etymology: From Old French "desplaisir" (to displease), from "des-" (dis-) + "plaisir" (to please).  "Plaisir" comes from Latin "placere" (to please).
