admixture:/ˈædmɪkstʃər/ /ədˈmɪkstʃər/| n. |混合物，掺杂物|A substance added to another, typically in small quantities, to modify its properties or to create a desired effect.|The concrete used in the foundation contained several admixtures to improve its strength and durability.  地基中使用的混凝土包含几种外加剂以提高其强度和耐久性。

The cake recipe calls for an admixture of spices.  蛋糕食谱需要混合各种香料。|近义词：blend, compound, mixture, composite|反义词:  element, component|词性:名词

admixture:/ˈædmɪkstʃər/ /ədˈmɪkstʃər/| n. |混合，掺杂（的行为）|The act of mixing something with something else.|The admixture of different cultures in the city created a vibrant and diverse community.  城市中不同文化的融合创造了一个充满活力和多元化的社区。

The artist experimented with the admixture of various painting techniques. 这位艺术家尝试了各种绘画技巧的混合。|近义词：blending, combining, mixing|反义词: separation, division|词性:名词
