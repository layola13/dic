ejector:/ɪˈdʒɛktər/| n.|喷射器; 弹射器; 排出器;|A device that forces something out, typically gas, liquid, or an object.  A mechanism in a firearm that throws out the empty cartridge case.|The pilot activated the ejector seat. 飞行员启动了弹射座椅。|Synonyms: propeller, expeller, discharger|Antonyms: injector, loader|Noun
