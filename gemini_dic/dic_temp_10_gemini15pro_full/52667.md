digitizes:/ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪzɪz/
verb
数字化，将…数字化
converts analog information into digital form.
The library is digitizing its collection of old manuscripts.  图书馆正在将其收藏的旧手稿数字化。
The company digitizes paper documents for archiving. 公司将纸质文件数字化存档。
Synonyms: encode, computerize, scan
Antonyms: analogize
词性: 动词
