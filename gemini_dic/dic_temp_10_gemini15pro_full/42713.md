counteruniversities:/ˌkaʊntərˌjuːnɪˈvɜːrsɪtiz/| n. |反大学，另类大学|Institutions of higher learning established as alternatives to traditional universities, often emphasizing radical social and political perspectives and non-traditional teaching methods.|Example: The counteruniversities of the 1960s offered free education and challenged conventional academic structures.  60年代的反大学提供免费教育，并挑战传统的学术结构。|Example: Some counteruniversities focused on practical skills and community engagement, rather than abstract theory. 一些反大学侧重于实践技能和社区参与，而不是抽象的理论。|Synonym: free university, alternative university|Antonym: traditional university|noun
