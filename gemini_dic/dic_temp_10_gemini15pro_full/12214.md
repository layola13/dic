asap:/ˌeɪɛsˌeɪˈpiː/ /ˌeɪsæp/ | adv. | 尽快；尽早 |  An acronym for "as soon as possible." It indicates urgency or the need for something to be done quickly. | Please send me the report asap. 请尽快把报告发给我。|  immediately, promptly, quickly, swiftly, at once, right away, without delay | slowly, leisurely | adverb
