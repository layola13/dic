fluorescent:/flɔːˈres(ə)nt/ /flʊˈres(ə)nt/
adjective|荧光的；发荧光的|Emitting light when struck by radiation, especially ultraviolet light.|The fish tank was lit by a fluorescent light. 鱼缸被荧光灯照亮。|luminous, phosphorescent|dull, dark
noun|荧光灯；日光灯|A tubular electric lamp coated with a fluorescent material.|The office was lit by rows of fluorescents. 办公室被一排排的荧光灯照亮。|fluorescent lamp, fluorescent tube|incandescent lamp, incandescent bulb
词性


fluorescents:/flɔːˈres(ə)nts/ /flʊˈres(ə)nts/
noun|荧光灯（复数）|Plural form of fluorescent (noun), referring to multiple fluorescent lamps.|The fluorescents buzzed overhead.  头顶上的荧光灯发出嗡嗡声。|fluorescent lamps, fluorescent tubes|incandescent lamps, incandescent bulbs
