acetated:/ˈæsɪteɪtɪd/
adjective
醋酸化的；醋酸盐的
Treated with or containing acetate.  Combined with acetic acid.
The acetated compound showed increased solubility.  该醋酸化的化合物显示出增加的溶解度。
The fabric was acetated to improve its texture.  这种织物经过醋酸处理以改善其质地。
近义词: acetylated
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "deacetylated" as a process antonym)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From acetate + -ated.  Ultimately from Latin acētum (“vinegar”).
