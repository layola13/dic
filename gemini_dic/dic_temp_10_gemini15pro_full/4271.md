airstrips:/ˈɛərstrɪps/ |n. |简易机场；简易跑道|Airstrips are landing strips used for aircraft, often in remote areas. |The small plane landed on the bumpy airstrip. 小飞机降落在崎岖不平的简易跑道上。| The pilot skillfully navigated the short airstrip. 飞行员熟练地驾驶飞机通过了短跑道。 |Synonyms: landing strip, runway, airfield| Antonyms: None | Noun
