crystallin:/ˈkrɪstəlɪn/| n. adj.|晶体蛋白；水晶的，透明的|A water-soluble structural protein found in the lens and cornea of the eye, contributing to transparency and refractive index.  Also, resembling crystal; transparent. |例句：The crystallin proteins in the lens help focus light onto the retina. 晶状体中的晶体蛋白有助于将光线聚焦到视网膜上。

例句：The lake was so clear and crystallin that we could see the bottom.  湖水清澈透明，我们能看到湖底。|近义词：transparent, clear, crystalline|反义词:opaque, cloudy|词性:名词, 形容词

crystallins:/ˈkrɪstəlɪnz/| n. |晶体蛋白（复数）|The plural form of crystallin, referring to a group of water-soluble structural proteins found in the eye lens. |例句：Different types of crystallins are present in the eye lens.  眼睛晶状体中存在不同类型的晶体蛋白。

例句：Researchers are studying the role of crystallins in cataract formation. 研究人员正在研究晶体蛋白在白内障形成中的作用。|近义词：lens proteins|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
