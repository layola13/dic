disingenuous:/ˌdɪsɪnˈdʒɛnjuəs/
|adj.|不真诚的，虚伪的，不坦率的|Not candid or sincere, typically by pretending that one knows less about something than one really does.|Her disingenuous smile made me suspicious. 她那虚伪的笑容让我起了疑心。|近义词: insincere, dishonest, deceitful, untruthful, duplicitous |反义词: sincere, honest, candid, frank, genuine |词性: 形容词


词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + ingenuous (frank, innocent, naive) + -ness (forming nouns expressing a state or quality). Ultimately from Latin ingenuus (native, freeborn, noble, frank).
