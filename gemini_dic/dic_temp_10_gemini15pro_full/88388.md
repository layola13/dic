hireless: /ˈhaɪərlɪs/
adjective
无工作的；失业的
without a job; unemployed.
例句：He's been hireless for six months and is struggling to make ends meet. 他已经失业六个月了，难以维持生计。

例句：The hireless rate in the region has risen sharply due to the factory closures. 由于工厂关闭，该地区的失业率急剧上升。

近义词：unemployed, jobless, out of work
反义词：employed, working
