conspiratorial:/kənˌspɪrəˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
阴谋的；密谋的；像有阴谋的
Suggesting the existence of a secret agreement or plot, especially one to do something wrong or harmful.

例句：
He had a conspiratorial wink.
他眨了眨眼，像是在密谋什么。

She lowered her voice to a conspiratorial whisper.
她压低了声音，像是在密谋什么似的耳语。


近义词：
scheming, plotting, clandestine, secretive, furtive

反义词：
open, honest, forthright

词性
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Latin *conspiratorius, from Latin conspiratus (past participle of conspirare ‘breathe together, agree, plot’).
