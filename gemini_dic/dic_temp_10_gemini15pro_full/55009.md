disputation:/ˌdɪs.pjʊˈteɪ.ʃən/ /ˌdɪs.pjuːˈteɪ.ʃən/
n.
争论，辩论
a formal discussion of a subject in which opposing points of view are put forward, especially in an academic context.
The students engaged in a lively disputation about the merits of different economic theories.  学生们就不同经济理论的优点进行了激烈的辩论。
argument, debate, controversy
agreement, accord, harmony
noun
