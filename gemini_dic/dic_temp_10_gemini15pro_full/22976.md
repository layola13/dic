botuliform: /ˌbɒtʃʊlɪfɔːm/
adjective
腊肠形的，香肠状的
shaped like a sausage; sausage-shaped.
The botuliform cloud formation stretched across the sky.  腊肠状的云层横跨天空。
The doctor noted the botuliform swelling on the patient's arm. 医生注意到病人手臂上腊肠状的肿胀。
近义词: sausage-shaped, allantoid
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider shapes like spherical, flat, etc.)
词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Latin "botulus" (sausage) + "-form" (shaped like).
