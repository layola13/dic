obedible:/əˈbiːdiəbl/
adj.
可食用的；顺从的；服从的
Suitable for eating; readily yielding to authority or direction.
The berries are obedible, but not very tasty. 这些浆果可以食用，但不太好吃。
The dog was perfectly obedible. 这只狗非常听话。
近义词: edible, compliant, docile, submissive
反义词: inedible, disobedient, rebellious
词性: 形容词

词源: 
From Latin obēdiēns, obēdient- (present participle of obēdīre ‘obey’), from ob- ‘towards’ + audire ‘hear’.
