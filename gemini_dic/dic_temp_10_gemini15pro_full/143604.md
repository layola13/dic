outintrigue: /ˌaʊtɪnˈtriːɡ/
verb
以智取胜，比…更会耍阴谋诡计
to be more skillful at scheming and plotting than someone else
She outintrigued her rivals and won the top job. 她以智取胜，击败了竞争对手，赢得了最高职位。
Synonyms: outmaneuver, outwit, outsmart
Antonyms: be outwitted, be outsmarted
