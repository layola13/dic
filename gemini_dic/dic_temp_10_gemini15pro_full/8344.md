anti-platonisms: /ˌæntiˈpleɪtənɪzəmz/ | n. | 反柏拉图主义 |  Philosophical doctrines that oppose or reject the central tenets of Platonism, particularly the Theory of Forms.  It encompasses various schools of thought that challenge the existence of abstract, universal entities separate from the physical world. |  Modern science's emphasis on empirical observation can be seen as a form of anti-platonism. 现代科学对经验观察的强调可以被视为一种反柏拉图主义。

Anti-platonisms question the very foundation of Plato's philosophy. 反柏拉图主义质疑柏拉图哲学的根基。 | 近义词: nominalism, materialism, empiricism | 反义词: Platonism, idealism | 词性: 名词
