dhoti:/ˈdəʊti/| n.|缠腰布，男士围裙（印度传统服饰）|A dhoti is a single piece of unstitched cloth, usually around 5 yards long, wrapped around the waist and legs, traditionally worn by men in India. |He wore a crisp white dhoti. 他穿了一条洁白的缠腰布。|Synonym: lunghi, mundu|Antonym: trousers, pants|noun
