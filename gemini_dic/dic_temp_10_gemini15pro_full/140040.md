oenomania:/ˌiːnəˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 酒狂，嗜酒如命 | An insatiable craving for wine; dipsomania. | He suffered from oenomania and could not control his wine consumption. 他患有酒狂，无法控制自己饮酒。| 近义词: dipsomania, alcoholism | 反义词: temperance, sobriety | 词性: noun

词源: From Greek *oinos* (wine) + *mania* (madness).
