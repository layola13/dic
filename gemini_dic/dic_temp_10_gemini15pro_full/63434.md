entreater:/ɪnˈtriːtər/ | n. |恳求者，请求者 |A person who entreats; one who makes an earnest request. |The entreater knelt before the king, begging for mercy. 恳求者跪在国王面前，祈求怜悯。|  She was a persistent entreater, never giving up until she got what she wanted. 她是一个执着的恳求者，从不放弃，直到得到她想要的东西。| 近义词：supplicant, petitioner, beggar, pleader | 反义词：demander | 词性：名词
