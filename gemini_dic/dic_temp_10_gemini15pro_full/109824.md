what:/wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American)|pron. det. int. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something.  Also used to express surprise or disbelief.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|近义词：which, how much|反义词: N/A |pronoun, determiner, interjection, adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你觉得怎么样？
What does she want? = 她想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching? = 你在看什么？
What is he cooking? = 他在做什么菜？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought? = 她买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? = 你一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? = 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 pm? = 晚上8点你在做什么？
What was she wearing? = 她当时穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you eaten before you got sick? = 你生病前吃了什么？
What had they decided before the meeting? = 会议前他们决定了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been studying before you changed majors? = 你转专业前一直在学什么？
What had she been doing all afternoon? = 她整个下午一直在做什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing next summer? = 明年夏天你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 会议上他们会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the age of 30? = 到30岁时你会取得什么成就？
What will have changed by 2050? = 到2050年会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for a living for ten years? = 十年后你会以什么为生？
What will they have been researching by the end of the grant? = 拨款结束时他们将一直在研究什么？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to say. = 我希望我知道该说什么。

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是哪里？
What are you thinking about? = 你在想什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
There's nothing what I want. = 没有我想要的东西。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/你好吗？
What the hell? = 搞什么鬼！
What if we go to the beach? = 如果我们去海滩怎么样？
What for? = 为了什么？
What about dinner? = 晚饭怎么样？
