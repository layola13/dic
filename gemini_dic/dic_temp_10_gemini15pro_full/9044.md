antimechanistic:/ˌæntiˌmɛkəˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
反机械论的
opposed to the theory of mechanism, especially in philosophy or biology; believing that not all phenomena are explicable by mechanical principles.
His antimechanistic views on biology led him to explore vitalism. 他在生物学上的反机械论观点使他开始探索活力论。
近义词:vitalistic, nonmechanistic, teleological
反义词:mechanistic, reductionist
词性:adjective
