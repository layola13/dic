hokiest:/ˈhoʊkiɪst/| adj.|最老套的，最俗气的|Of or relating to something overly sentimental or clichéd, often in a contrived or artificial way.  It implies a lack of originality and sophistication.|That movie had the hokiest dialogue I've ever heard. 那部电影的对话是我听过的最俗气的。|synonyms: corny, cheesy, clichéd, sentimental, mawkish, trite, hackneyed|antonyms: original, fresh, innovative, sophisticated|adjective
