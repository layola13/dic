infinity:/ɪnˈfɪnəti/ | n. |无限，无穷|A number greater than any assignable quantity or countable number.  A point in space or time infinitely distant.|例句：The universe stretches into infinity. 宇宙延伸至无限。|例句：Some people believe in the infinity of space.  有些人相信空间的无限性。|近义词：boundlessness, endlessness, eternity|反义词：finitude, limit|词性:名词
