The misleadingness of the advertisement caused many customers to buy the product.

deceptiveness, deceptivity, fallaciousness
truthfulness, accuracy, honesty
noun

Etymology->
From misleading + -ness.  Misleading comes from the verb mislead, which is formed from the prefix mis- (meaning "wrong" or "bad") and the verb lead.
