butting:/ˈbʌtɪŋ/
verb
顶撞;用头撞;碰撞
The goat was butting its head against the fence. 山羊用头撞击篱笆。
The two rams were butting heads in a fight for dominance. 两只公羊为了争夺统治地位而用头撞击。
近义词:ramming, bumping, pushing, shoving
反义词:yielding, receding
词性:v.

butting:/ˈbʌtɪŋ/
noun
顶撞;用头撞
The constant butting of the sheep was damaging the fence. 羊不断的顶撞损坏了篱笆。
The butting between the two colleagues created a tense atmosphere. 两位同事之间的冲突制造了紧张的气氛。
近义词:ramming, bumping, clash
反义词:yielding, harmony
词性:n.
