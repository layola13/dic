look-up-to:/lʊk ˈʌp tuː/| phrasal verb | 尊敬；敬仰 | To admire and respect someone. | Example: He's always looked up to his older brother. 他一直很尊敬他的哥哥。

Example:  She looks up to her mother as a role model. 她把她的母亲视为榜样。| 近义词: admire, respect, idolize | 反义词: look down on, despise, disdain | 词性: 动词短语
