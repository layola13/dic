hypergenesis:/ˌhaɪpərˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.|过度生长，过度发育|Excessive growth or development, especially of tissue.|The doctor diagnosed the unusual growth as hypergenesis.|近义词：overgrowth, hyperplasia|反义词:hypoplasia, atrophy|词性:名词

Etymology: From Greek *hyper* ("over, excessive") + *genesis* ("origin, creation").
