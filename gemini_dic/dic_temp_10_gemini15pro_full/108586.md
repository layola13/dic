leachy:/ˈliːtʃi/| adj.|容易渗漏的；(土地)贫瘠的；(颜色)褪色的|Tending to lose color or substances when soaked or washed; (of soil) lacking nutrients and unable to retain moisture.|The dye in these cheap clothes is very leachy and runs in the wash. 这些廉价衣服的染料很容易渗漏，洗的时候会掉色。|近义词：porous, permeable, thin, infertile|反义词：impermeable, retentive, fertile|词性:形容词
