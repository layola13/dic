ethenoid:/ˈiːθənɔɪd/| adj.|乙烯基的，含双键的|Relating to or containing a double bond, characteristic of ethene (ethylene).|例句：The ethenoid bond in ethene is reactive. 乙烯中的乙烯基键具有反应活性。

例句：Ethenoid compounds are important in organic chemistry. 乙烯基化合物在有机化学中很重要。|近义词：olefinic, unsaturated|反义词：saturated|词性:adjective
