montanist:/ˈmɒntənɪst/ | n. | 孟他努斯主义者；孟他努斯派的信徒 | A follower of Montanus, a second-century religious leader who claimed prophetic inspiration and advocated for a stricter form of Christianity. |  例句：The Montanists believed in the imminent Second Coming of Christ. 孟他努斯主义者相信基督即将第二次降临。

例句：Early church fathers condemned Montanism as heresy. 早期教会神父谴责孟他努斯主义为异端邪说。 | 近义词：heretic, sectarian | 反义词：orthodox | 词性：名词
