fastidious:/fæˈstɪdiəs/ /fəˈstɪdiəs/| adj.|一丝不苟的，挑剔的，苛求的|Very attentive to and concerned about accuracy and detail.|例句：He is fastidious about his clothes. 他对衣着很讲究。|例句：She has a fastidious approach to her work. 她工作一丝不苟。|近义词：meticulous, scrupulous, particular, demanding, fussy|反义词：careless, sloppy, uncritical, undemanding|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *fastidiosus*, from *fastidium* (“loathing, disgust”), from *fastus* (“haughtiness, arrogance”) + *-idium* (a suffix forming abstract nouns).
