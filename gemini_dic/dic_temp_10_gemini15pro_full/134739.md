nonmedicinal:/ˌnɒn.mɪˈdɪsɪnəl/ /ˌnɑːn.mɪˈdɪsɪnəl/
adjective
非药用的；非医疗的
Not having medicinal properties; not used for medicinal purposes.
This lotion contains nonmedicinal ingredients like aloe vera and shea butter.  这款乳液含有芦荟和乳木果油等非药用成分。
The spa offers nonmedicinal treatments such as massages and aromatherapy.  这家水疗中心提供按摩和芳香疗法等非医疗护理。

Synonyms: nontherapeutic, non-medical
Antonyms: medicinal, therapeutic, pharmaceutical
词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From non- (not) + medicinal.  "Medicinal" comes from Late Latin medicinalis, from Latin medicīna (medicine).
