acerbic:/əˈsɜːrbɪk/| adj.|尖刻的，辛辣的|Sharp and forthright, especially in style of speaking or writing.  Characterized by a biting or sarcastic wit.|Her acerbic wit made her a popular guest on talk shows. 她尖刻的才智使她成为脱口秀节目的热门嘉宾。

His acerbic comments about the film alienated many viewers.  他对这部电影的尖刻评论疏远了许多观众。|近义词：bitter, caustic, sharp, biting, sardonic, sarcastic|反义词：sweet, kind, gentle, mild|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acerbus* "sour, bitter, harsh," from *acer* "sharp."
USAGE->
The critic's acerbic review of the play angered the actors. 评论家对这部戏剧的尖刻评论激怒了演员们。

Her acerbic tone made it clear she wasn't happy with the decision. 她尖刻的语气表明她对这个决定不满意。

He delivered an acerbic speech criticizing the government's policies. 他发表了一篇尖刻的演讲，批评了政府的政策。

She responded to the insult with an acerbic retort. 她用尖刻的反驳回应了侮辱。

The comedian's acerbic humor was not appreciated by everyone in the audience.  这位喜剧演员尖刻的幽默并没有受到所有观众的欣赏。
