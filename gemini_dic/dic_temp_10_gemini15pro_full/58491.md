duebill:/ˈduːbɪl/ /ˈdjuːbɪl/| n. |欠条，欠单|A non-negotiable, signed acknowledgment of a debt. It is essentially a short-term IOU. |Example 1: The farmer gave the feed store a duebill, promising to pay for the grain after the harvest. 农民给了饲料店一张欠条，承诺在收割后支付谷物费用。

Example 2:  Instead of cash, she offered a duebill for services rendered. 她没有付现金，而是提供了一张服务欠条。|Synonym: IOU, promissory note |Antonym: receipt, payment|noun
