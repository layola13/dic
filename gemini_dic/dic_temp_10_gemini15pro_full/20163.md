bipolarizes:/baɪˈpoʊləˌraɪzɪz/
verb
使...两极化，使...截然对立
Causes something to divide into two sharply contrasting groups or opinions.
The new law has bipolarized public opinion. 这项新法律使公众舆论两极分化。
polarize, divide, split
unite, join, merge
v.
