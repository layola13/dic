bowge: /boʊdʒ/
noun
budge, move slightly
A slight movement or shaking.  The table gave a slight bowge when I leaned on it.
The earthquake caused a noticeable bowge in the ground.
近义词: shift, budge, stir, wiggle
反义词: stillness, fixity
词性: noun


词源:  Likely a variant or dialectal form of "budge," which comes from Old French "bouger" (to move, stir).
