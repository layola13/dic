californication:/ˌkælɪˌfɔːrnɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 加州化；加州梦的幻灭 | A blend of "California" and "fornication," referring to the perceived materialism, hedonism, and superficiality associated with the state, particularly Hollywood. It also suggests a process of becoming like California, often in a negative sense. | The song "Californication" by the Red Hot Chili Peppers critiques the state's culture of excess. 红辣椒乐队的歌曲《加州化》批判了该州的过度文化。|  decadence, superficiality | authenticity, modesty | noun
