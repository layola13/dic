hellishly:/ˈhɛlɪʃli/| adv.|极其地，非常地，可怕地|extremely, intensely, in a hellish manner.  It describes something as being very unpleasant or difficult, as if it were like hell.|The traffic was hellishly congested. 交通拥堵得可怕。|Synonyms: infernally, diabolically, fiendishly, awfully, terribly|Antonyms: heavenly, divinely, blissfully|词性:副词
