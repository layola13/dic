hotpot:/ˈhɒtpɒt/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtpɑːt/ (US)| n.|火锅，砂锅菜；热盆菜|A metal pot with a lid, used for cooking food on top of a stove.  A dish of meat and vegetables cooked in a simmering liquid at the table.|We had a delicious hotpot for dinner last night. 昨晚我们吃了一顿美味的火锅。|Synonyms: stew, casserole, fondue|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "cold dish")|noun

hotpot:/ˈhɒtpɒt/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtpɑːt/ (US)| n.|热点；麻烦事；棘手问题|A difficult situation or issue.|The political scandal became a real hotpot. 这起政治丑闻成了一个棘手的问题。|Synonyms: predicament, dilemma, quandary|Antonyms: solution, resolution|noun
