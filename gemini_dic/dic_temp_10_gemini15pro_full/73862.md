formulate:/ˈfɔːrmjuleɪt/ | v. | 规划；明确表达；构想 | To create or develop something, especially a plan, system, or product.  | The company formulated a new marketing strategy.  这家公司制定了一个新的营销策略。| synonyms: devise, conceive, develop, frame, articulate | antonyms:  dismantle, ruin | verb
