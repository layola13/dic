leaf-through:/ˌliːf ˈθruː/
|v.|快速翻阅，浏览|To quickly turn the pages of a book or magazine, looking at them briefly but not reading everything.|I leafed through the magazine while waiting for my appointment. 我在等候预约的时候翻了翻杂志。| skim, browse, glance over|pore over, scrutinize|verb

leaf-through:/ˌliːf ˈθruː/
|n.|快速翻阅，浏览（的动作）|The act of quickly turning the pages of a book or magazine.|A quick leaf-through revealed nothing of interest. 快速翻阅一遍并没有发现什么有趣的内容。|skimming, browsing|close reading|noun
