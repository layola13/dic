nonmusicalness:/ˌnɒnˈmjuːzɪkəlnəs/ /ˌnɑːnˈmjuːzɪkəlnəs/
noun
非音乐性；缺乏音乐才能
Lack of musical talent or aptitude; the quality of not being musical.
His nonmusicalness was evident in his inability to carry a tune. 他缺乏音乐才能，表现在他唱歌不着调。
synonyms: unmusicality, lack of musicality, tone-deafness
antonyms: musicality, musical talent, aptitude for music
n.
