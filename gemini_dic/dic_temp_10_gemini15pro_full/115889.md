marmatite:/ˈmɑːrmətaɪt/ | n. |铁闪锌矿|A dark-brown to black variety of sphalerite, a zinc sulfide mineral, containing iron.  It is an important ore of zinc.|The miners discovered a vein of marmatite deep within the earth. 矿工们在地球深处发现了一条铁闪锌矿脉。|Synonyms: sphalerite, blende, blackjack|Antonyms: N/A (Minerals rarely have true antonyms)|Noun
