creditworthiness:/ˈkrɛdɪtˌwɜːrðiːnɪs/| n.|信誉；信用可靠性|The bank assessed the creditworthiness of the applicant before approving the loan. 银行在批准贷款前评估了申请人的信誉。|例句：The company's creditworthiness has been severely damaged by the recent scandal.  这家公司的信誉因最近的丑闻而严重受损。

例句：High creditworthiness is essential for securing favorable loan terms.  高信誉对于获得优惠的贷款条件至关重要。|近义词：credit standing, credit rating, solvency, reliability|反义词：insolvency, unreliability|词性:noun
