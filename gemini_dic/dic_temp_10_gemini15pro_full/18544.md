beneficiary:/ˌbenɪˈfɪʃiəri/| n.|受益人，受惠者|a person or organization that receives something good, especially money or property|She was the sole beneficiary of her uncle's will. 她是她叔叔遗嘱的唯一受益人。|synonyms: recipient, heir, legatee|antonyms: benefactor, donor|noun

beneficiary:/ˌbenɪˈfɪʃiəri/| adj.(较少使用)|受益的，有益的|receiving or entitled to receive something good, especially money or property|The new tax laws are beneficiary to small businesses. 新的税法对小企业有利。|synonyms: advantageous, beneficial, favorable|antonyms: detrimental, harmful, unfavorable|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French benefice (“benefit, kindness, privilege”) + -ary.  Ultimately from Latin bene (“well”) + facere (“to do”).
