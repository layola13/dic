avowant:/əˈvaʊənt/| adj.|公开承认的，坦率的|Openly acknowledged or confessed; frank. |He was an avowant supporter of the new policy. 他是这项新政策的公开支持者。|例句：She is an avowant critic of the government. 她是一位公开批评政府的人。|近义词：professed, admitted, declared, avowed|反义词：unacknowledged, hidden, secret, covert|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "avow" + "-ant" (suffix forming adjectives and nouns from verbs).  "Avow" comes from Old French "avouer," from Latin "advocare" (to call to, summon, claim).
