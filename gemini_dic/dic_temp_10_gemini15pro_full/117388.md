Medicaid:/ˈmedɪkeɪd/| n. |医疗补助; 穷人医疗救助|A government program in the US that provides free or low-cost medical care to people with low incomes.|
例句：Medicaid helps millions of Americans access necessary healthcare. 医疗补助帮助数百万美国人获得必要的医疗保健。

例句：She qualified for Medicaid after losing her job.  她失业后获得了医疗补助资格。

近义词：Medicare (for seniors and disabled), public health insurance|
反义词: private health insurance|
词性: noun|

Etymology:  A blend of "medical" and "aid".  It was established in 1965 as part of President Lyndon B. Johnson's "Great Society" social reform program.
