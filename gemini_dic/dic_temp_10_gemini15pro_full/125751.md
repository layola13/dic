muralists:/ˈmjʊərəlɪsts/
n.
壁画家
Artists who paint murals.
The muralists transformed the dull walls of the city into vibrant works of art.  壁画家们将城市沉闷的墙壁变成了充满活力的艺术品。
The muralists worked tirelessly to complete the project on time.  壁画家们不知疲倦地工作，以按时完成项目。
近义词: artists, painters, fresco painters
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


Etymology:
From mural + -ist.  "Mural" comes from the Late Latin "muralis," meaning "of a wall," from "murus" (wall). "-ist" is a suffix forming agent nouns.
