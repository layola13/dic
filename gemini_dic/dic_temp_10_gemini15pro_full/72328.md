flow-chart:/ˈfləʊ tʃɑːrt/ /ˈfloʊ tʃɑːrt/| n.|流程图|A flow-chart is a diagram of the sequence of operations in a computer program or an accounting system.|The flow-chart helped us understand the complex process.|近义词：diagram, chart|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "unorganized process" in a descriptive sense)|词性: 名词

词源:  "Flow" comes from Old English "flōwan," meaning "to flow." "Chart" comes from Old French "charte," from Latin "charta," meaning "paper, map."
