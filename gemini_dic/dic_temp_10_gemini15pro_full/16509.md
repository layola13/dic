barista:/bəˈriːstə/ (US), /bɑːˈriːstə/ (UK)| n.|咖啡师，咖啡调配师|A person who makes and serves coffee (especially in a coffee bar).|例句：The barista skillfully poured latte art into my cup. 咖啡师熟练地在我的杯子里倒入了拿铁艺术。

例句：The barista recommended their new Ethiopian blend. 咖啡师推荐了他们新推出的埃塞俄比亚混合咖啡。|近义词：coffee maker, bartender (for alcoholic drinks)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps customer)|词性:noun
