impassable:/ɪmˈpæsəbl/
adjective
不能通行的，无法通过的
Not able to be traveled or crossed, especially because of bad conditions.
The road became impassable after the heavy rain.  大雨过后，道路变得无法通行。
The mountain pass is impassable in winter.  这条山口在冬天无法通行。
近义词: inaccessible, impenetrable, unnavigable
反义词: passable, accessible
词性: adjective
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French, or from medieval Latin impassabilis, from in- ‘not’ + passabilis (from passare ‘to pass’).
