pancreatin:/ˌpænkriˈeɪtɪn/ /ˌpæŋkriˈeɪtɪn/ | n. | 胰酶; 胰酶制剂 | A preparation of enzymes secreted by the pancreas, used as a digestive aid. |  She was prescribed pancreatin to help with her digestion. 她被开了胰酶来帮助消化。|  The pancreatin supplements helped him absorb nutrients better. 胰酶补充剂帮助他更好地吸收营养。| synonym: pancreatic enzyme, digestive enzyme | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "enzyme inhibitor") | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From pancreas + -in, denoting a substance.  "Pancreas" itself comes from Greek pankreas, from pan- "all" + kreas "flesh," because of the gland's fleshy consistency.
