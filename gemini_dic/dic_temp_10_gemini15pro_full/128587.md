nice-as-pie:/ˌnaɪs əz ˈpaɪ/ | adj. | 非常友善的，和蔼可亲的 | Extremely pleasant and agreeable, often deceptively so.  | She was nice-as-pie to me, even though I knew she didn't like me. 她对我非常友善，尽管我知道她不喜欢我。 | He acted nice-as-pie after he broke the vase, hoping to avoid punishment. 打碎花瓶后，他表现得非常乖巧，希望能逃避惩罚。 | 近义词: sweet, pleasant, agreeable, amiable, charming | 反义词: nasty, unpleasant, disagreeable, rude, mean | 词性: 形容词
