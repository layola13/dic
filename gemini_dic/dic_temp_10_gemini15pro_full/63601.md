envy: /ˈɛnvi/ (US) /ˈɛnvi/ (UK)| n. vt.| 羡慕；嫉妒；忌妒 | A feeling of discontented or resentful longing aroused by someone else's possessions, qualities, or luck. | She felt a pang of envy when she saw her friend's new car. 她看到朋友的新车时感到一阵嫉妒。 | He envied his brother's athletic ability. 他羡慕他哥哥的运动能力。 | 近义词: jealousy, covetousness, resentment | 反义词: admiration, goodwill, generosity | 词性: noun, verb
