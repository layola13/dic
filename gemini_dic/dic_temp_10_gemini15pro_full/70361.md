fiercer:/ˈfɪərsər/ (US), /ˈfɪəsə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更凶猛的，更激烈的|Comparative form of fierce: more fierce.  Indicates a greater degree of intensity or aggression.|The competition this year is fiercer than last year. 今年的竞争比去年更激烈。|Synonyms: more aggressive, more intense, more violent, harsher, wilder|Antonyms: gentler, calmer, milder, tamer|adjective
