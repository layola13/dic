overwide:/ˌoʊvərˈwaɪd/| adj.|过宽的，超宽的|Excessively wide; wider than necessary or desirable.|The truck was overwide for the narrow bridge. 这辆卡车对于这座窄桥来说太宽了。|The road is overwide for just two lanes. 这条路对于只有两条车道来说太宽了。|近义词：too wide, excessively wide, broader than necessary|反义词：narrow, too narrow, undersized|词性:形容词
