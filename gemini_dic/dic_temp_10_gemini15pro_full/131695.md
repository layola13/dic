noncondescending:/ˌnɒnkɒndɪˈsendɪŋ/ | adj. | 没有优越感的，平易近人的 | Treating people as equals; not patronizing. |  She spoke to him in a noncondescending way, which he appreciated. 她以一种没有优越感的方式和他说话，他对此很感激。|  The teacher explained the complex concept in a noncondescending manner, making it easy for the students to understand. 老师用平易近人的方式解释了这个复杂的概念，让学生很容易理解。 | 近义词: approachable, respectful, egalitarian, unpretentious | 反义词: condescending, patronizing, arrogant, haughty | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + condescending (from condescend, from Latin condescendere "to descend, stoop, lower oneself," from com- "together" + descendere "to climb down").
