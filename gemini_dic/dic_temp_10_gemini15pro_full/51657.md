deviators:/ˈdiːvieɪtərz/
n.
偏差者；偏离者；背离者
Those who deviate from a norm or standard.
The deviators from the group's plan were ostracized.  这些偏离小组计划的人被排斥了。
The study identified the deviators in the test scores. 这项研究确定了测试分数中的偏差值。
nonconformists, mavericks, outliers
conformists, followers
noun
