occipital:/ɑːkˈsɪpɪtəl/
adjective
枕骨的；后头的
Relating to the back of the head or skull.
The occipital lobe is responsible for vision. 枕叶负责视觉。

The occipital bone forms the back of the skull. 枕骨形成了头骨的后部。

Synonym:  posterior, caudal
Antonym:  frontal, anterior
词性: adjective
