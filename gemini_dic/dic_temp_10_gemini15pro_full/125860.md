muscariform:/ˌmʌskərɪˈfɔːrm/ | adj. | 蕈状的，伞形的 | Resembling a mushroom in shape; umbrella-shaped. |  The cloud was muscariform, spreading out at the top. 这朵云呈蕈状，顶部向外展开。| The architect designed a muscariform roof for the pavilion.  建筑师为亭子设计了一个伞形的屋顶。 | 近义词：umbrella-shaped, fungiform | 反义词: columnar, elongated | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *muscariformis* "fly-shaped", from *musca* "fly" + *-formis* "-shaped".  The connection is likely due to the resemblance of some mushroom caps to fly swatters.
