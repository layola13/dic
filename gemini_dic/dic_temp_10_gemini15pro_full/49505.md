demagogism: /ˈdeməɡɑːɡɪzəm/ | n. |煽动主义，蛊惑人心 | The act of a demagogue; inflammatory appeals to the passions and prejudices of the mob. |  His demagogism riled up the crowd and led to violence. 他的煽动性言论激怒了人群，导致了暴力事件。|近义词：demagoguery, rabble-rousing |反义词: statesmanship, reason |词性: 名词

demagogue: /ˈdeməɡɑːɡ/ | n. |煽动者，蛊惑民心的政客 | A political leader who seeks support by appealing to popular desires and prejudices rather than by using rational argument. |  The demagogue skillfully manipulated the fears of the public.  这个煽动者巧妙地操纵了公众的恐惧。|近义词：agitator, firebrand |反义词: statesman, diplomat |词性: 名词
