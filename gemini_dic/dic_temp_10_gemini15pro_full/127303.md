navvy: /ˈnævi/ | n. | 掘土工人，壮工 | A manual laborer employed in excavating or constructing canals, railroads, etc. | 例句：The navvies worked tirelessly to build the railway.  那些掘土工人不知疲倦地修建铁路。

例句：Many navvies lost their lives during the construction of the Panama Canal.  许多掘土工人死于巴拿马运河的修建过程中。| 近义词：laborer, worker, construction worker | 反义词:  supervisor, manager | 词性: noun

navvied: /ˈnævid/ | v. | 做苦工，从事体力劳动 | Worked as a navvy; performed heavy manual labor. | 例句：He navvied for years to save money for his family. 他做了多年的苦工来为家人攒钱。

例句：She navvied on the construction site throughout the summer. 她整个夏天都在建筑工地做苦力。| 近义词：toiled, labored, slogged | 反义词:  rested, idled | 词性: verb
