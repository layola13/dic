fluential: /ˌfluːˈɛnʃ(ə)l/
adjective
有影响的；有势力的
Having the power to affect or change people or things, especially in an indirect way.
The influential senator swayed the vote.  这位有影响力的参议员左右了投票结果。
近义词: powerful, impactful, dominant
反义词: powerless, insignificant, weak
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *fluentialis, from fluens, fluentis, present participle of fluere "to flow".  源自拉丁语 *fluentialis，来自 fluens, fluentis，fluere “流动”的现在分词。
