overpositivenesses: /ˌoʊvərˈpɑːzɪtɪvnɪsɪz/
noun
过度积极；过分乐观
The state of being excessively positive or optimistic.
His overpositivenesses about the project's success seemed unrealistic given the challenges. 他对项目成功的过度乐观，考虑到挑战，似乎不现实。
The team's overpositivenesses blinded them to the potential risks.  团队的过度乐观使他们对潜在的风险视而不见。
Synonyms:  excessive optimism, overoptimism, Pollyannaism
Antonyms: pessimism, negativity, cynicism
