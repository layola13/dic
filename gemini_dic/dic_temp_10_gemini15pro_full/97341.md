informs:/ɪnˈfɔːrmz/
verb
通知，告知
gives knowledge or facts or news
The teacher informed the students of the test date. 老师通知了学生考试日期。
近义词: tells, notifies, apprises
反义词: withholds, conceals
词性: verb

informs:/ɪnˈfɔːrmz/
verb
告发，检举
gives evidence against someone
He informed on his colleagues to the police. 他向警察告发了同事。
近义词: reports, accuses, denounces
反义词: protects, defends
词性: verb


Etymology:
From Middle English *enformen*, from Old French *enformer*, from Late Latin *informare* "to shape, train, instruct," from Latin *in-* "into" + *formare* "to form."
