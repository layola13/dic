luvvy:/ˈlʌvi/ | n. |宝贝，亲爱的（常用作昵称，略带贬义）|A person who is excessively sentimental or gushing, often used ironically or dismissively. |例句：She's such a luvvy, always going on about her cats. 她真是个多愁善感的人，总是谈论她的猫。

例句：He called her "luvvy" in a sarcastic tone. 他用讽刺的语气叫她“宝贝”。|近义词：dear, darling, sweetheart, pet (though these can be more neutral or affectionate) |反义词: (No direct antonym, but the implication is often of insincerity, so perhaps "foe," "enemy") |词性:noun
