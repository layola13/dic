asseverate:/əˈsɛvəˌreɪt/
verb
郑重声明；断言
To declare or state positively or earnestly; affirm.
He asseverated his innocence. 他郑重声明自己是无辜的。
The witness asseverated that she had seen the defendant at the scene of the crime.  证人断言她看到被告在犯罪现场。
近义词:affirm, assert, declare, maintain, avow
反义词:deny, gainsay, contradict
词性:v.
