Non-intoxicating beer is becoming increasingly popular. 无酒精啤酒越来越受欢迎。


| 近义词:alcohol-free, sober, soft | 反义词: intoxicating, alcoholic | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + intoxicating.  "Intoxicating" comes from Late Middle English, from Latin intoxicat-, past participle stem of intoxicare "to poison," from in- "in" + toxicum "poison."
