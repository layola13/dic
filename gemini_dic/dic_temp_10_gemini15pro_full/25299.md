bugger-off:/ˈbʌɡə(r) ɒf/ (UK), /ˈbʌɡər ɔːf/ (US)| vi. int.|滚开；走开；别烦我|A rude way of telling someone to go away and leave you alone.|Bugger off, I'm busy! 滚开，我很忙！|例句：He told them to bugger off and leave him in peace. 他叫他们滚开，让他清静清静。

例句："Bugger off!" she shouted, slamming the door. “滚！”她喊道，砰地一声关上了门。

近义词：go away, get lost, piss off, clear off|反义词: come here, stay|词性: 惯用语 (主要用作动词短语)
