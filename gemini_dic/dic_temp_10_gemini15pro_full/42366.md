counterassaults:/ˈkaʊntərəˌsɔːlts/ /ˈkaʊntərəˌsɑːlts/
| n. |反击，反攻|An attack made in response to an enemy's attack.  A counteroffensive.|The general ordered a series of counterassaults to regain lost territory. 将军下令进行一系列反击以夺回失地。|近义词：counterattack, counteroffensive|反义词: assault, attack|词性:名词

counterassault:/ˈkaʊntərəˌsɔːlt/ /ˈkaʊntərəˌsɑːlts/
| v. |反击，反攻|To attack in response to an enemy's attack.|The troops counterassaulted the enemy position. 部队反攻了敌人的阵地。|近义词：counterattack|反义词: attack|词性:动词
