overabounding:/ˌoʊvərəˈbaʊndɪŋ/
adjective
极其丰富的，过多的
excessively abundant; overflowing.
The garden was overabounding with flowers. 花园里鲜花盛开。
近义词: overflowing, profuse, exuberant, copious
反义词: scarce, meager, deficient, lacking
词性: adjective

词源: From over- + abounding, present participle of abound (from Old French abonder, from Latin abundare "to overflow, abound," from ab- "from" + unda "wave").  Thus, literally "overflowing from every side."
