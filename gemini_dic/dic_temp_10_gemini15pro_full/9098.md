antimonarchists:/ˌæntiˈmɒnəkɪsts/ | n. | 反君主主义者；反对君主制的人 | People who oppose monarchy or monarchical rule.  Those who believe that a monarchy is an unjust and outdated form of government. |  The antimonarchists protested outside the palace. 反君主主义者在宫殿外抗议。| The rise of antimonarchists threatened the stability of the kingdom. 反君主主义者的兴起威胁着王国的稳定。| 近义词: republicans, revolutionaries | 反义词: monarchists, royalists | 词性: noun

Etymology:
anti- (Greek): against, opposing
monarch (Greek monarkhes): sole ruler
-ist: one who adheres to a particular doctrine or system
