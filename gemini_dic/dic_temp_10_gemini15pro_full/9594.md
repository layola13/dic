antisophistries:/ˌæntiˈsɒfɪstrɪz/
noun
诡辩的反驳，驳斥诡辩之辞
arguments or writings against sophistry (subtle, superficially plausible, but fallacious reasoning)
His book is filled with antisophistries aimed at exposing the flawed logic of his opponents.  他的书中充满了对诡辩的驳斥，旨在揭露对手的逻辑缺陷。
synonyms: refutations, counterarguments, rebuttals
antonyms: sophistries, fallacies, deceptions
