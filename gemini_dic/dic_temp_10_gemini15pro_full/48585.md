defeatment: /dɪˈfiːtmənt/
n.
失败，挫败
The state of being defeated; overthrow; rout.
His defeatment in the election was a crushing blow. 他在选举中的失败是一个沉重的打击。
近义词：defeat, loss, failure, rout
反义词：victory, triumph, success
词性: 名词

词源:  From defeat + -ment.  Ultimately from the Old French *desfaire "undo," from des- "un-, out" + faire "to do."
