nonintegrations:/ˌnɑːnˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃənz/
noun
未整合，未集成
Lack of integration or the state of not being integrated.  The failure to combine or incorporate different parts into a whole.
The report highlighted the nonintegrations between the different software systems.  报告强调了不同软件系统之间缺乏整合。
The nonintegrations of the immigrant community into the wider society is a complex issue. 移民群体未能融入更广泛的社会是一个复杂的问题。
Synonyms: disunity, separation, division, fragmentation
Antonyms: integration, unity, unification, cohesion
词性: 名词
