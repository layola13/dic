antisyndicalism:/ˈæntiˈsɪndɪkəlɪzəm/
n.
反工团主义，反工会主义
Opposition to trade unions; the belief that trade unions are harmful.
The government's antisyndicalism led to violent clashes with workers. 政府的反工会主义导致与工人的暴力冲突。
The dictator's regime was marked by strong antisyndicalism. 独裁者的政权以强烈的反工会主义为标志。
近义词:anti-unionism
反义词:pro-unionism
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (against) + syndicalism (from French syndicalisme, from syndicat "trade union")
