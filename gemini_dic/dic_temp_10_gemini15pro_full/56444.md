dolman:/ˈdɒlmən/  /ˈdoʊlmən/ | n. | 长袖宽松女衫；多尔曼袖；匈牙利轻骑兵的短上衣 | A woman's garment, such as a jacket or sweater, with dolman sleeves;  A hussar's short jacket. | She wore a stylish black dolman over her dress. 她穿着一件时髦的黑色多尔曼外套，罩在连衣裙外面。

The Hungarian hussar was easily recognizable by his brightly colored dolman.  这位匈牙利轻骑兵很容易通过他颜色鲜艳的多尔曼短上衣被认出来。 | 近义词：jacket, coat, blouse | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps tight-fitting garment) | 词性: noun
