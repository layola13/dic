divergence:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒəns/ /dɪˈvɜːrdʒəns/| n.|分歧，差异，背离|The state or process of diverging.  A difference between two or more things.|Example: The divergence between their opinions was clear. 他们的观点分歧很明显。

Example:  The divergence of the light rays created a beautiful pattern. 光线的散射创造了一个美丽的图案。|近义词：difference, disagreement, deviation, disparity, separation|反义词：convergence, agreement, similarity, unity, conformity|词性:名词
