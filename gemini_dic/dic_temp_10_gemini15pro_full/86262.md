heavens:/ˈhɛvənz/| n.|天，天空，天堂|Used to express surprise, annoyance, or other strong emotions. Also used to refer to the sky or God.|Good heavens, what a mess! 天哪，真是一团糟！|Synonyms: sky, paradise, firmament|Antonyms: hell, earth|noun

heavens:/ˈhɛvənz/| n.|天命，天意|Used to refer to fate or destiny.|The heavens have decreed that we should meet. 天意注定我们会相遇。|Synonyms: fate, destiny, providence|Antonyms: - |noun
