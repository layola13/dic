educator:/ˈɛdʒʊkeɪtər/| n.|教育家，教育工作者|A person who educates others, especially as a profession.  A teacher, instructor, or trainer. |例句：The educator inspired her students to pursue their dreams.  这位教育家激励她的学生去追求他们的梦想。

例句：He is a respected educator in the field of early childhood development. 他是一位在幼儿发展领域受人尊敬的教育工作者。|近义词：teacher, instructor, trainer, tutor, mentor|反义词:student, pupil|noun
