babygirl:/ˈbeɪbi ɡɜːrl/ /ˈbeɪbi ɡɝːl/| n. |宝贝女孩，小女孩，女朋友（昵称）|A term of endearment for a young girl or a romantic partner.  It can be used affectionately within families or between romantic partners.  However, its use outside these contexts can be perceived as patronizing or inappropriate. |例句：He called his daughter "babygirl." 他叫他的女儿“宝贝女孩”。

例句：He affectionately called his girlfriend "babygirl." 他亲昵地称他的女朋友为“宝贝女孩”。|近义词：sweetheart, darling, honey, babe|反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on context, perhaps "old woman" if contrasting age) |词性:noun
