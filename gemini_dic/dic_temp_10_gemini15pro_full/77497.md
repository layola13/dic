gazetted:/ɡəˈzɛtɪd/
adj.
公布的，刊登宪报的
Officially published or announced, especially in a government gazette.
The appointment was gazetted last week.  该任命已于上周公布。
synonyms: announced, published, proclaimed, declared
antonyms:  unannounced, unpublished, concealed, suppressed
词性: 形容词


gazetted:/ɡəˈzɛtɪd/
v.
公布，刊登宪报
To publish or announce officially, especially in a government gazette.
The government gazetted the new law. 政府公布了新法律。
synonyms: announce, publish, proclaim, declare
antonyms: suppress, conceal, withhold
词性: 动词 (过去分词)


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "gazette," ultimately from Venetian "gazeta," a small coin, the price of a newspaper, hence the newspaper itself.
