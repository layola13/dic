hemelytron: /ˈhemɪˌlaɪtrən/ (US), /ˈhɛmɪˌlaɪtrən/ (UK)| n. |半鞘翅|The hemelytron is the modified forewing of certain insects, particularly in the order Hemiptera (true bugs). It is thickened and leathery at the base, and membranous at the tip.|The stink bug's hemelytra are easily recognizable.  椿象的半鞘翅很容易辨认。|Synonym: forewing (in this context) |Antonym: hindwing|noun
