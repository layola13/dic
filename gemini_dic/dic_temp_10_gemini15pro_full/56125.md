doctrinist:/ˈdɑːktrɪnɪst/ | n. | 教条主义者；信条主义者 | A person who adheres rigidly to a set of doctrines or principles, especially in religion or politics.  |  Example: The doctrinist refused to consider any viewpoints that challenged his beliefs.  这位教条主义者拒绝考虑任何挑战他信仰的观点。 | Example:  He was known as a doctrinist, unwilling to compromise on his political ideals. 他被称为一个教条主义者，不愿在他的政治理想上妥协。 | Synonyms: dogmatist, ideologue, rigid believer | Antonyms: freethinker, pragmatist, skeptic | noun
