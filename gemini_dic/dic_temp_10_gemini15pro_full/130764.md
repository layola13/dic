nonamputations:/ˌnɒnˌæmpjuˈteɪʃənz/
|n.| 非截肢，未截肢|The study focused on the treatment of injuries with nonamputations.  这项研究的重点是用非截肢的方式治疗损伤。|
|n.| 非截肢术|The surgeon specialized in limb salvage and nonamputations.  这位外科医生擅长保肢和非截肢手术。|
|英文解析|The plural form of nonamputation, meaning the absence or avoidance of amputation (the surgical removal of a limb).|
|例句|The advancements in microsurgery have led to an increase in nonamputations. 显微外科的进步导致非截肢病例的增加。|
|例句|The doctor explored all possible treatment options before considering amputations, prioritizing nonamputations whenever feasible.  医生在考虑截肢之前探索了所有可能的治疗方案，尽可能优先考虑非截肢。|
|近义词|limb salvage, limb preservation|
|反义词|amputations|
|词性|Noun|
|词源|non- (prefix meaning "not") + amputation (from Latin amputare "to cut around, prune") + -s (plural suffix)|
