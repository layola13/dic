cusk:/kʌsk/ | n. |鱈魚的一種；綠青鱈|A food fish (Brosme brosme) of the cod family, found in northern Atlantic waters. It has a long barbel on the chin and three dorsal fins.|例句：The cusk is a deep-water fish. 綠青鱈是一種深海魚。

例句：We caught a large cusk while fishing off the coast of Norway.  我们在挪威海岸捕获了一条大綠青鱈。|近义词：cod, hake, ling |反义词: None (No direct opposite for a type of fish)|Noun
