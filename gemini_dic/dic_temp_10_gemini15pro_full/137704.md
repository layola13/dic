nonusurping:/ˌnɒnjuːˈsɜːpɪŋ/
adjective
不篡夺的，不侵占的
Not usurping; not claiming or seizing power or property unjustly.
The king promised to be a nonusurping ruler, respecting the traditional laws and customs of the land. 国王承诺做一个不篡位的统治者，尊重这片土地的传统法律和习俗。
He inherited the business legitimately, a nonusurping heir to his father's company. 他合法地继承了这家企业，是他父亲公司的合法继承人。
Synonyms: legitimate, rightful, lawful
Antonyms: usurping, seizing, appropriating
词性: 形容词
