embroiling:/ɪmˈbrɔɪlɪŋ/
verb
使卷入，使纠缠
involving (someone) deeply in an argument, conflict, or difficult situation.
The two countries were embroiled in a bitter dispute over territory.  这两个国家卷入了一场激烈的领土争端。
The CEO found himself embroiled in a scandal. 首席执行官发现自己卷入了一场丑闻。
synonyms:entangling, involving, enmeshing, mireing
antonyms:extricating, disentangling, separating
v.
