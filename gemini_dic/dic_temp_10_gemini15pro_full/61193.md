elution:/iˈluːʃən/ | n. | 洗脱; 析出 | The process of eluting. | The elution of the compound was monitored by UV spectroscopy. 通过紫外光谱监测化合物的洗脱。|近义词：extraction, separation, desorption |反义词: adsorption |词性:名词
