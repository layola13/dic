gloss:/ɡlɒs/| n. vt. |光泽，注解；注释|The gloss on the furniture made it look new. 家具上的光泽使它看起来像新的。 He glossed over the difficult parts of the text. 他掩盖了文本中难懂的部分。|例句：The gloss on the apple made it look appetizing. 苹果上的光泽使它看起来很诱人。  He added a gloss to the text to explain the difficult words. 他在文本中添加了注释来解释难懂的词语。|近义词：shine, luster, annotation, explanation|反义词：dullness, matte|词性:名词,动词

词源: From Middle English *glosen, *glossyn, from Old French gloser (“to gloss, explain; flatter”), from Late Latin glōsāre (“to explain, interpret”), from Ancient Greek γλῶσσα (glôssa, “tongue, language, foreign word needing explanation, gloss”).
