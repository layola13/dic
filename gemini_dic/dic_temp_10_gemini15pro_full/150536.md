panyard:/ˈpænˌjɑːrd/| n.|钢片琴，钢盘琴|A musical instrument consisting of a set of tuned steel pans mounted on a frame, played with sticks.  It originated in Trinidad and Tobago.|The panyard erupted in a joyous cacophony of sound. 钢片琴爆发出一阵欢快的嘈杂声。|Synonyms: steel drum, steel pan|Antonyms: None|Noun
