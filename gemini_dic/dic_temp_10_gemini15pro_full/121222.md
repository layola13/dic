misemploying:/ˌmɪsɪmˈplɔɪɪŋ/
v.
滥用；使用不当；大材小用
Using someone or something in a way that does not utilize their full potential or is not suitable for their skills.
The company is misemploying its talented staff by giving them menial tasks.  这家公司让有才能的员工做琐碎的工作，是大材小用。
She felt she was being misemployed in her current role. 她觉得她在目前的角色中没有得到充分的利用。
Synonyms: misusing, underutilizing, wasting
Antonyms: utilizing, maximizing, optimizing
