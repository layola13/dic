friaries:/ˈfraɪəriːz/
noun
男修道院（复数）
A building or buildings occupied by a community of friars.
The ruins of the old friaries still stand on the hill.  山坡上仍然矗立着古老修道院的废墟。
monasteries, convents, abbeys
--- (no antonym applicable)
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *frairie (modern French frarie), from Medieval Latin fraria "brotherhood, community of friars," from Latin frater "brother."
