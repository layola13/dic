evoker:/ɪˈvoʊkər/| n. |召唤者；唤起者|Someone or something that evokes a particular feeling, memory, or image.  A person who summons spirits or magical powers. |The scent of jasmine is an evoker of warm summer nights. 茉莉花的香味能唤起温暖夏夜的回忆。|近义词：conjurer, summoner, elicitor|反义词:suppressor|noun
