hemadrometer:/ˌhiːməˈdrɒmɪtər/ /ˌhiːməˈdrɑːmɪtər/| n. |血流速度计；血液流速计|An instrument for measuring the velocity of the blood current. |The hemadrometer provided crucial data for the study. 血流速度计为这项研究提供了关键数据。|The doctor used a hemadrometer to measure the patient's blood flow. 医生用血流速度计测量病人的血流速度。|近义词:rheometer, blood flow meter|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology:  From Greek *haima* (blood) + *dromos* (running, course) + *-meter* (measure).
