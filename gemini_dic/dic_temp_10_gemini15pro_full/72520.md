flushboard:/ˈflʌʃbɔːrd/ | n. |齐平板; 嵌板; 平板 |A board set flush with an adjoining surface. |The carpenter installed a flushboard to create a smooth, uninterrupted surface. 木匠安装了一个齐平板，以创造一个光滑、不间断的表面。| The flushboard covered the gap between the two walls seamlessly. 齐平板完美地覆盖了两堵墙之间的缝隙。 | synonym:  panel, clapboard | antonym:  protrusion, projection | noun
