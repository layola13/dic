nonremovable:/ˌnɒnrɪˈmuːvəbl/ (US) /ˌnɒnrɪˈmuːvəbl/ (UK)| adj.|不可拆卸的，不能移动的|Not able to be removed.|The battery in this phone is nonremovable. 这款手机的电池是不可拆卸的.|The display is fixed and nonremovable. 显示屏是固定的，不可拆卸的.|近义词：fixed, permanent, attached|反义词:removable, detachable|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- + removable.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "removable" comes from the verb "remove," ultimately from the Latin "removēre," meaning "to move back or away."
