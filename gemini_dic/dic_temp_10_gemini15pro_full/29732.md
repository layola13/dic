catlinite:/ˈkæt.lɪ.naɪt/| n.|红管石，烟斗泥|A type of red clay stone, typically found in southwestern Minnesota, traditionally used by Native Americans, particularly the Sioux, for making ceremonial pipes.|The Sioux consider catlinite sacred and use it to carve elaborate pipe bowls.|近义词:pipestone|反义词: (No direct antonym)|名词
