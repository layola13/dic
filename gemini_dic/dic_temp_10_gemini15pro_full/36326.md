cohosts:/ˈkoʊhoʊsts/ (US), /ˈkəʊhəʊsts/ (UK)| n.|共同主持人，联合主持人|A person who hosts something jointly with another or others.|例句：The cohosts of the podcast have great chemistry. 播客的联合主持人配合默契。

例句：The event was cohosted by two local charities.  这次活动由两个当地慈善机构联合举办。|近义词：joint hosts, presenters|反义词:guest, audience|词性:noun
