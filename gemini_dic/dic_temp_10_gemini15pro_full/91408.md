hydrozoon:/haɪˈdrəˌzoʊən/ /ˈhaɪdroʊˌzoʊən/| n. |水螅纲动物|An animal belonging to the class Hydrozoa, a group of invertebrates including hydras, jellyfish, and Portuguese man-of-war, typically having a polyp stage and a medusa stage in their life cycle. They are characterized by their simple body structure and radial symmetry.|例句：The Portuguese man-of-war is a dangerous hydrozoon.  葡萄牙战舰是一种危险的水螅纲动物。

例句：Hydrozoans are found in both freshwater and marine environments. 水螅纲动物在淡水和海洋环境中都有发现。|近义词：cnidarian|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
