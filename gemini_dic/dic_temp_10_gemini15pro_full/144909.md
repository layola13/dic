over-stoutness:/ˈoʊvərˈstaʊtnəs/
|n.|过度肥胖，过于粗壮|The state of being excessively stout or fat.  It implies an unhealthy level of body weight.|His over-stoutness made it difficult for him to climb the stairs. 他过度肥胖，爬楼梯很困难。|Synonyms: obesity, corpulence, adiposity|Antonyms: thinness, slenderness, leanness|noun

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) + "stout" (Middle English stout, from Old French estout, from Germanic) + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
