antispreader:/ˈæntiˈspredər/| n.|防扩散装置，防扩散器|A device or measure designed to prevent the spread of something, typically a disease.|例句：The restaurant installed antispreaders on the tables to protect diners.  餐厅在桌子上安装了防扩散装置以保护用餐者。

例句：Antispreaders, like masks and social distancing, are important tools in preventing the spread of COVID-19.  像口罩和保持社交距离这样的防扩散措施是预防新冠病毒传播的重要工具。|近义词：barrier, shield, protection|反义词:spreader, accelerator|词性:noun
