examinership:/ɪɡˈzæmɪnərʃɪp/
n.
审查员的职位；审查员资格；审查员身份；破产保护期
The state of being under examination, especially in a legal or financial context.  Often refers to a period of protection from creditors granted to a company in financial difficulty, allowing it to restructure its debts.
The company entered examinership to avoid liquidation.  这家公司进入破产保护期以避免清算。
synonyms: receivership, administration, bankruptcy protection
antonyms: solvency, prosperity
noun
