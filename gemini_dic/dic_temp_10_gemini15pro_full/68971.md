fathoming:/ˈfæðəmɪŋ/ | n. | 理解，探测，测深 | The act of understanding or measuring the depth of something. |  例句：The fathoming of the ocean's depths is a complex undertaking.  探测海洋的深度是一项复杂的工作。

例句：The fathoming of her motives remains a mystery.  对她动机的理解仍然是个谜。 | 近义词：understanding, comprehending, grasping, plumbing, sounding | 反义词：misunderstanding, ignoring, neglecting | 词性: noun (gerund or verbal noun)

fathom:/ˈfæðəm/ | v. | 理解，领会；测量(水的)深度 | To understand something complex or measure the depth of water. | 例句：I couldn't fathom why she would do such a thing.  我无法理解她为什么 would做这样的事。

例句：The sailors used a rope to fathom the depth of the water. 水手们用绳子来测量水的深度。| 近义词：understand, comprehend, grasp, gauge, plumb, sound | 反义词：misunderstand, misinterpret | 词性: verb
