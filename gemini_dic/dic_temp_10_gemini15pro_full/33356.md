chumpish:/ˈtʃʌmpɪʃ/
adjective
愚蠢的，傻气的
Like a chump; stupid or foolish.
He made a chumpish mistake and lost the game. 他犯了一个愚蠢的错误，输掉了比赛。
近义词：foolish, silly, stupid, doltish, dimwitted
反义词：smart, intelligent, clever, wise, astute
词性: adjective

chump:/tʃʌmp/
noun
傻瓜，笨蛋
A fool; a gullible person.
He felt like a chump for believing her lies. 他觉得自己像个傻瓜，竟然相信了她的谎言。
近义词：fool, idiot, dolt, sucker, dupe
反义词：genius, sage, wise person, mastermind
词性: noun
