digraph:/ˈdaɪɡræf/| n.|双字母，二合字母|A digraph is a combination of two letters representing one sound, such as "sh" in "ship" or "ea" in "head".|The digraph "ch" can represent different sounds depending on the word.|近义词:ligature|反义词:|noun

"th" sound = /θ/ or /ð/ 音
"ph" sound as in "phone" = /f/ 音
"wh" sound = /w/ or /h/ 音 (depending on dialect)
