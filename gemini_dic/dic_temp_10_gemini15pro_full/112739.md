lpns:/ˈɛl piː ˈɛn ɛs/ | n. | Licensed Practical Nurses |执业实习护士|  LPNs provide basic nursing care under the supervision of registered nurses (RNs) or physicians. |  LPNs work in hospitals, nursing homes, and doctor's offices.  执业实习护士在注册护士 (RN) 或医生的监督下提供基本护理。 执业实习护士在医院、疗养院和医生办公室工作。 | Near Synonyms: LVN (Licensed Vocational Nurse), nurse aide | Antonym: physician, doctor | noun
