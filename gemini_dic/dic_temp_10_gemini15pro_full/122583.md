modern-day:/ˈmɒdən deɪ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdərn deɪ/ (US)| adj.|当代的，现代的|Relating to the present time; contemporary.  Formed by combining "modern" (meaning relating to the present or recent times as opposed to the past) and "day" (referring to the current period).|Modern-day technology has transformed the way we live. 当代科技改变了我们的生活方式。|Modern-day explorers are still making discoveries.  当代探险家仍在不断地探索发现。|contemporary, present-day, current|ancient, archaic, outdated|adjective
