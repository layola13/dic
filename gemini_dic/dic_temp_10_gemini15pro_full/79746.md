glumly:/ˈɡlʌmli/| adv.|闷闷不乐地，阴郁地|In a glum or dejected manner.  Showing or expressing gloom.|He stared glumly out the window. 他闷闷不乐地盯着窗外。|Synonyms: dejectedly, morosely, sullenly, gloomily|Antonyms: cheerfully, happily, brightly|adverb

Etymology->
From glum + -ly.  Glum itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to gloom.
