cinematics:/ˌsɪnəˈmætɪks/ | n. |电影艺术；运动学|The study of motion in film, including camera movement, editing, and visual effects, or the art of making films.  It can also refer to the characteristics of a film relating to motion and visual presentation.|The cinematics in that action movie were breathtaking. 那部动作片的电影艺术令人叹为观止。|Synonym: filmmaking, cinematography |Antonym: N/A | Noun


cinematic:/ˌsɪnəˈmætɪk/ | adj. |电影的；影片的|Relating to the art or technique of motion-picture photography.|The film had a very cinematic feel. 这部电影有一种非常电影化的感觉。|Synonym: filmic |Antonym: un-cinematic | Adjective


Etymology:
From French *cinématique*, from Greek *kínēma*  "movement"  + *-ikos* "-ic".
