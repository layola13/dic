distichously: /dɪˈstɪkəsli/
adverb
二列地，成二列地
arranged in two rows or ranks on opposite sides of a stem or axis
The leaves are arranged distichously on the stem. 叶子在茎上二列排列。
近义词: alternately, bifariously
反义词: spirally, whorled
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From Greek *distichos* (two-rowed) < *di-* (two) + *stichos* (row).
