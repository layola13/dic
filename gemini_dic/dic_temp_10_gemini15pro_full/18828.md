beryllia:/bəˈrɪliə/| n.|氧化铍|A white, crystalline powder, BeO, used in ceramics, as a moderator in nuclear reactors, and in specialized electronic components.|Beryllia is used as a heat sink in some electronic devices. 氧化铍在一些电子设备中用作散热器。|Synonyms: beryllium oxide|Antonyms: N/A|noun
