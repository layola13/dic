lolloping:/ˈlɒləpɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈlɑːləpɪŋ/ (US)| adj. | 笨拙地蹦跳的；蹒跚的 | moving in a clumsy, bounding way | The dog came lolloping across the field. 狗笨拙地蹦跳着穿过田野。 | synonyms: bounding, gamboling, lumbering, rolling | antonyms: graceful, agile | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From lollop (v.) + -ing.  Lollop likely imitative of the sound and movement.
