intercrural:/ˌɪntərˈkrʊərəl/| adj.| 胯间的；大腿间的|Situated between the legs, specifically between the thighs or crura.  Relating to the space between the crura of the diaphragm, or any other pair of crura.|The intercrural fibers form a complex network supporting the diaphragm.|近义词：interfemoral, interthigh|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider terms relating to *outside* the legs)|词性: adjective
