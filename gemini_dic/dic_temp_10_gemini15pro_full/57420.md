drabbiness:/ˈdræbinəs/| n.|单调乏味，沉闷|The drabbiness of the town was depressing.  这个小镇的单调乏味令人沮丧。|例句：The drabbiness of the winter landscape made her long for spring. 冬日景色的单调乏味让她渴望春天。|近义词：dullness, monotony, bleakness, dreariness|反义词：brightness, vibrancy, colorfulness|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From drab + -ness.  Drab itself comes from the Old French *drape, meaning "cloth" or "rag," referring to undyed, natural-colored cloth that was considered dull and plain.
