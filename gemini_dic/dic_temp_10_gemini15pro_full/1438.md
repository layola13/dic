ace-up-one's-sleeve:/eɪs ʌp wʌnz sliːv/| idiom | 袖中藏有王牌; 暗藏的优势; 秘策 |  To have a secret advantage or resource that can be used when needed.  This idiom refers to cheating at cards by hiding an ace in one's sleeve, giving a hidden advantage. | He had an ace up his sleeve during the negotiations – he knew the other company was desperate to close the deal. 他在谈判中藏有王牌——他知道对方公司非常渴望完成这笔交易。

He always seems to have an ace up his sleeve when he's playing poker.  他打扑克时似乎总是有锦囊妙计。| 近义词: secret weapon, hidden advantage, trump card | 反义词: disadvantage, weakness | 词性: idiom


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She has an ace up her sleeve for the competition. = 她为比赛准备了秘密武器。
He always has an ace up his sleeve in business negotiations. = 他在商务谈判中总是留有后手。

一般过去时:
He won the game because he had an ace up his sleeve. = 他赢得了比赛，因为他藏有王牌。
She had an ace up her sleeve during the debate and surprised everyone. = 她在辩论中出其不意，让所有人大吃一惊。

现在完成时:
I've always had an ace up my sleeve in case things go wrong. = 我总是有备无患，以防万一。
He has had an ace up his sleeve throughout this entire process. = 他在整个过程中一直都留有后手。


过去完成时:
She had had an ace up her sleeve, but she didn't need to use it. = 她本来留有后手，但最终没有用到。
He hadn't realized his opponent had an ace up his sleeve until it was too late. = 他直到为时已晚才意识到对手留有后手。


将来时:
I will have an ace up my sleeve for the next meeting. = 我会在下次会议上留有后手。
She will have an ace up her sleeve if they try to pressure her. = 如果他们试图向她施压，她会有应对之策。


虚拟语气:
If I had an ace up my sleeve, I would use it now. = 如果我有什么锦囊妙计，我现在就会用。
I wish I had an ace up my sleeve to solve this problem. = 我希望我有什么妙招能解决这个问题。


被动语态:
(Not applicable - this idiom doesn't lend itself to passive voice)


疑问句:
Does she have an ace up her sleeve? = 她有什么秘密武器吗？
Did he have an ace up his sleeve during the trial? = 他在审判中有什么秘密武器吗？


否定句:
He didn't have an ace up his sleeve and lost the negotiation. = 他没有锦囊妙计，输掉了谈判。
She doesn't have an ace up her sleeve, she's just very skilled. = 她没有什么秘密武器，只是非常熟练。
