convenience: /kənˈviːniəns/ (US) /kənˈviːniəns/ (UK)| n.| 便利，方便；便利设施；方便的用具|The state of being able to proceed with something with little effort or difficulty. |The 24-hour convenience store is a lifesaver. 这家24小时便利店真是救星。|synonyms: handiness, usefulness, serviceability|antonyms: inconvenience, difficulty, hardship|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French convenance "agreement, suitability," from convenir "to agree, suit," from Latin convenire "to come together, assemble, agree," from com- "together" + venire "to come."
