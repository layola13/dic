bacterial:/bækˈtɪəriəl/| adj.|细菌的；由细菌引起的|Relating to or caused by bacteria. |Bacterial infection can be treated with antibiotics. 细菌感染可以用抗生素治疗。|近义词：microbial, germinal|反义词：viral, fungal|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *bacterialis*, from Latin bacterium + -alis.  Ultimately from Greek baktērion "small staff, stick," diminutive of baktron "staff."
