papulose:/ˈpæpjʊloʊs/| adj.|生有小突起的，长满丘疹的|Covered with small raised bumps or pimples.  Characterized by papules (small, solid, raised lesions on the skin).|The dermatologist diagnosed the rash as a papulose eruption.|近义词：pimply, papular, bumpy, pustular|反义词:smooth, clear|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *papula* (pimple, pustule) + -ose (suffix forming adjectives indicating fullness or abundance).
