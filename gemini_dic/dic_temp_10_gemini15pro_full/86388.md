hedgehogs:/ˈhedʒhɒɡz/ (UK) /ˈhedʒhɑːɡz/ (US)| n.|刺猬|Small, spiny mammals with a protective coat of quills.|Example 1: The hedgehogs curled up in a ball when they sensed danger. 刺猬在感觉到危险时蜷缩成一团。

Example 2:  We often see hedgehogs in our garden at night. 我们经常在晚上看到刺猬在花园里。|近义词: porcupine (豪猪 -  larger and with longer quills) |反义词: N/A (没有直接的反义词)|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *heggehoge*, from Old English *hyggehogg*, from *hegge* ("hedge") + *hogg* ("hog").  So named because they are found in hedges and root around like pigs.  来自中古英语 *heggehoge*，源自古英语 *hyggehogg*，由 *hegge*（“树篱”）+ *hogg*（“猪”）组成。之所以这样命名，是因为它们在树篱中被发现，并且像猪一样四处觅食。
