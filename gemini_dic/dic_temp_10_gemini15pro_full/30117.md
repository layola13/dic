cede:/siːd/
verb
割让；放弃
to give something such as power, land, or a right to someone else, especially unwillingly
Spain ceded Florida to the United States in 1819. 西班牙于1819年将佛罗里达州割让给了美国。

cede:/siːd/
verb
(正式)（尤指不情愿地）放弃，割让
to surrender possession of, especially by treaty
Hong Kong was ceded to Britain after the Opium War. 香港在鸦片战争后被割让给英国。

近义词:yield, surrender, relinquish, concede
反义词:retain, withhold, maintain
