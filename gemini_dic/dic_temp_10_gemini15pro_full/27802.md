cannonballing:/ˈkænənˌbɔːlɪŋ/
n.
跳炮弹式入水
The act of jumping into water feet first with knees bent, so that the body makes a large splash.
He did a cannonball into the pool, splashing everyone. 他跳炮弹式入水，溅了每个人一身水。
diving, jumping in
climbing out, wading
noun
