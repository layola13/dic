insatiably:/ɪnˈseɪʃəbli/
adverb
贪得无厌地，不知足地
in an insatiable manner; so as not to be satisfied.
He read insatiably, devouring books on every subject. 他如饥似渴地阅读，贪婪地阅读各种主题的书籍。
She worked insatiably, driven by a desire for success. 她不知疲倦地工作，被成功的渴望所驱使。
近义词: greedily, ravenously, voraciously
反义词: moderately, temperately
词性: 副词

insatiable:/ɪnˈseɪʃəbl/
adjective
贪得无厌的，不知足的
(of an appetite or desire) impossible to satisfy.
She had an insatiable curiosity about the world. 她对世界有着永不满足的好奇心。
His insatiable appetite for power led him to betray his friends. 他对权力贪得无厌的欲望导致他背叛了他的朋友。
近义词: unquenchable, voracious, ravenous
反义词: satisfied, content
词性: 形容词

词源:
From Late Latin insatiabilis, from in- ‘not’ + satiabilis (from satiare ‘satisfy’).
