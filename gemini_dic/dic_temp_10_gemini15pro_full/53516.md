disassociations:/dɪsˌəˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃənz/
|n.| 分离，脱离，分裂|The process of disassociation involves separating oneself from a group, belief, or situation.  分离的过程包括将自己与一个群体、信仰或情况分开。|She experienced feelings of disassociation after the traumatic event.  她在经历了创伤性事件后感到精神恍惚。|近义词：separation, detachment, alienation|反义词：association, connection, union|词性:名词
disassociation:/dɪsˌəˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən/
|n.|  解离，精神分裂（医学术语）|Disassociation is a mental process that causes a lack of connection in a person's thoughts, memory and sense of identity. 解离是一种心理过程，会导致一个人的思想、记忆和身份认同感之间缺乏联系。|He suffered from disassociation after the car accident. 他在车祸后患上了精神分裂症。|近义词：disconnection, detachment |反义词： integration, connection|词性:名词
