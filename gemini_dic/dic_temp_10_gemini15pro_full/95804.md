inconvertibly:/ˌɪnkənˈvɜːrtəbli/
adverb
不可改变地，确定地
in a way that is impossible to change or deny.
His fate was inconvertibly sealed. 他的命运已不可逆转地注定。
近义词:irrevocably, definitively, unchangeably, undeniably
反义词:changeably, reversibly, alterably
词性:副词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + convertible (able to be changed) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The root "convert" comes from Latin "convertere," meaning "to turn around."
