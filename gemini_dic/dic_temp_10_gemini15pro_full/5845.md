amazednesses: /əˈmeɪzɪdnɪsɪz/
|n.| 惊讶，惊奇（复数）|The state of being amazed; feelings of great surprise or wonder. |The amazednesses of the children at the magic show were delightful to watch. 孩子们在魔术表演中表现出的惊奇令人愉快。| 近义词: astonishments, surprises, wonders | 反义词: apathy, indifference, boredom | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From amazed + -ness + -es.  "Amazed" comes from the Old French "esmayer" meaning to dismay or bewilder.  The "-ness" suffix creates a noun from an adjective, indicating a state or quality.  The "-es" creates the plural form.
