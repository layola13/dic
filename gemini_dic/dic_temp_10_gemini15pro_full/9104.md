antimonic:/ˌæntɪˈmɒnɪk/ | adj. |锑的，五价锑的 | Relating to or containing antimony, especially in its +5 oxidation state. | Antimonic acid is a strong oxidizing agent. 锑酸是一种强氧化剂。|  synonyms: stibic | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider compounds of antimony in lower oxidation states, e.g., antimonous) | adjective


antimonic:/ˌæntɪˈmɒnɪk/ | adj. |锑的，五价锑的 |  Antimonic compounds are used in flame retardants. 锑化合物用于阻燃剂。|  synonyms: stibic | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French antimonique, from antimoine (“antimony”) + -ique (“-ic”).  Ultimately from Late Latin antimonium.
