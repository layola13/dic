overtolerance:/ˌoʊvərˈtɑːlərəns/ |n.|过度容忍；过度耐受|Excessive tolerance; acceptance of behavior or situations beyond what is considered reasonable or healthy.|The overtolerance of disruptive behavior in the classroom led to a decline in learning. 课堂上对破坏性行为的过度容忍导致学习成绩下降。| The overtolerance of pollution has severely damaged the ecosystem. 对污染的过度容忍严重破坏了生态系统。| 近义词:excessive tolerance, undue tolerance|反义词:intolerance, zero tolerance|词性:名词
