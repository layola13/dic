glorifiers:/ˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪərz/| n.|颂扬者，赞美者|People who glorify something, often excessively or without justification.  Those who praise or exalt something or someone.|例句：The glorifiers of violence in movies are contributing to a dangerous culture.  电影中暴力行为的颂扬者正在促成一种危险的文化。

例句：The king's glorifiers filled the court with their lavish praise. 国王的颂扬者们用奢华的赞美填满了宫廷。|近义词：praisers, eulogizers, exaltors, celebrants|反义词：critics, detractors, denouncers|词性:noun
