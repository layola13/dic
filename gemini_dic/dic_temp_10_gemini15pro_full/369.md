abietic:/ˌeɪbiˈɛtɪk/| adj.| 松香的；枞酸的|Relating to or derived from rosin, especially abietic acid.| Abietic acid is a major component of rosin.  枞酸是松香的主要成分。

Abietic acid can be used in the production of varnishes. 枞酸可以用于生产清漆。|近义词：resinous|反义词:|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abiēs* (“fir tree”) + *-ēticus* (suffix forming adjectives).

USAGE->
The abietic acid is crystallized from rosin.  枞酸是从松香中结晶出来的。

The varnish contains abietic resins.  这种清漆含有松香树脂。


PHRASE->
abietic acid = 枞酸
abietic resin = 松香树脂
abietic type resin = 松香型树脂
methyl abietate = 松香甲酯
abietic anhydride = 枞酸酐
