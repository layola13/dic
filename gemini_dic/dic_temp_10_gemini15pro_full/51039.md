desmognathous: /dɛzˈmɒɡnəθəs/
adjective
颌骨联合的；固颌型的
Having the maxillae immovably united with the skull, as in most vertebrates above fishes.  This is in contrast to the more ancestral condition where the maxilla could move relative to the other bones of the skull, a condition called "amphikinetic".
例句：Desmognathous skulls are a characteristic feature of most tetrapods. 固颌型颅骨是大多数四足动物的特征。

例句：The desmognathous jaw structure provides greater stability for biting. 固颌型的颌骨结构为咬合提供了更大的稳定性。

近义词：akinetic (though this applies to joints in general, not just the jaw)
反义词：amphikinetic, kinetic (for joints in general)
词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *desmos* (δέσμος) meaning "bond" or "ligament" + *gnathos* (γνάθος) meaning "jaw."
