overdecoratively:/ˌoʊvərˈdɛkərətɪvli/
| adv. | 过度装饰地 | In a way that uses too much decoration. |  She had overdecoratively adorned her small apartment with knick-knacks from her travels. 她把旅行带回来的小摆设过度装饰在她的小公寓里。|  例句：The cake was overdecoratively iced, making it almost too sweet to eat. 蛋糕上的糖霜装饰得太多了，甜得几乎吃不下。| 近义词：ornately, excessively, elaborately | 反义词：simply, plainly, minimally | 词性：副词
Etymology: "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above") + "decorative" (from Latin decorare "to adorn, embellish, decorate") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
