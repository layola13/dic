districting:/ˈdɪstrɪktɪŋ/
n.
选区划分
The process of dividing a geographical area into districts, often for electoral purposes.
The districting process was highly controversial. 选区划分过程争议很大。

近义词: redistricting, apportionment
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "unification")
词性: noun
