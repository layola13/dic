checky:/ˈtʃɛki/| adj.|厚颜无耻的，放肆的，轻佻的|Impudent or disrespectful, typically in a playful way.  Suggesting a tendency to make cheeky remarks or behave in a way that pushes the boundaries of acceptable behavior, often with a sense of humor or mischievousness.|He gave her a cheeky grin. 他厚着脸皮冲她咧嘴一笑。|近义词：impudent, impertinent, bold, saucy, forward|反义词：respectful, polite, reserved, shy|词性:形容词

词源:
From 'cheek' (meaning impudence) + '-y' (forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Old English *cēace.
