what: /wɒt/ (British) /wɑːt/ (American) | pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么 |

英文解析:  "What" is a versatile word used as a pronoun, determiner, adverb, and interjection.  As a pronoun, it asks for information about something. As a determiner, it modifies a noun to indicate which thing or things are being referred to. As an adverb, it modifies an adjective or another adverb. As an interjection, it expresses surprise or disbelief.

例句：
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？

What do you want for dinner? = 你晚饭想吃什么？

近义词：which, how

反义词:  (What doesn't have a direct antonym.)

词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection
