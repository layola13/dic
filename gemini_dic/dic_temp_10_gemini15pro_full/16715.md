barrooms:/ˈbɑːˌruːmz/ | n. |酒吧间；酒馆|A room or establishment where alcoholic drinks are served over a counter. |He frequented dimly lit barrooms, seeking solace in the bottom of a glass.| 近义词：pubs, saloons, taverns | 反义词: None | 词性:Noun

Etymology:
From bar + room.  "Bar" in this context originally referred to the counter where drinks were served.
