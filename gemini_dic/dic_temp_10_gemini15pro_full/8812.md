antienvironmentalists:/ˌæntiɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəlɪsts/
|n.|反环保主义者|People who oppose environmentalism or environmental protection measures.  Those who actively work against environmental regulations or policies.|
例句：The antienvironmentalists lobbied against the new emissions standards. 反环保主义者游说反对新的排放标准。

例句：The documentary explored the tactics used by antienvironmentalists to spread disinformation.  这部纪录片探讨了反环保主义者散布虚假信息的策略。

近义词：conservationist, ecologist, environmentalist, preservationist
反义词：environmentalists, conservationists


词性: noun (plural)
