over-emote:/ˌoʊvərɪˈmoʊt/ | v. | 过度表达；表现得过分热情 | To express emotions in an excessive or exaggerated way. | She tends to over-emote when she's telling a story, which can be a bit much sometimes. 她讲故事时容易过度表达，有时会有点过火。 | He over-emoted his sadness at the funeral. 他在葬礼上过度表达了自己的悲伤。 | 近义词：overact, exaggerate, dramatize | 反义词：understate, downplay | 词性: 动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "emote" (verb meaning "to express emotion"). "Emote" comes from the French "émouvoir," meaning "to stir up, excite."
