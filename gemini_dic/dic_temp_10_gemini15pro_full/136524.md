nonruinously:/ˌnɒnˈruːɪnəsli/
adverb
非毁灭性地，不会造成毁灭地
in a way that does not cause ruin or destruction
The company managed to downsize nonruinously, preserving most of its core workforce.  公司设法以非毁灭性的方式缩减规模，保留了大部分核心员工。
The government sought to implement reforms nonruinously, minimizing disruption to the economy. 政府试图以非毁灭性的方式实施改革，尽量减少对经济的干扰。
Synonyms: safely, harmlessly, nondestructively
Antonyms: destructively, ruinously
词性: 副词
