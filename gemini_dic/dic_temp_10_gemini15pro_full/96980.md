infantilised:/ˈɪnfəntɪlaɪzd/ | adj. | 幼稚化的，被当作婴儿对待的 | Treated as a child; made to appear or behave like a child; having characteristics of a child. |  He felt infantilised by his overprotective parents. 他觉得被过度保护的父母幼稚化了。

She found the company's culture infantilised its employees. 她发现公司的文化把员工幼稚化了。

近义词:childish, immature, babyish | 反义词: mature, adult, grown-up | 词性: adjective
