fracking:/ˈfrækɪŋ/
n.|水力压裂法|The process of injecting liquid at high pressure into subterranean rocks, boreholes, etc., so as to force open existing fissures and extract oil or gas.|Fracking has become a controversial method for extracting natural gas. 水力压裂法已成为一种颇具争议的天然气开采方法。|近义词：hydraulic fracturing|反义词:|词性:名词

frack:/fræk/
v.|水力压裂|To extract oil or gas from (a rock formation) by fracturing it with pressurized liquid.|They plan to frack for shale gas. 他们计划用水力压裂法开采页岩气。|近义词：hydraulically fracture|反义词:|词性:动词
