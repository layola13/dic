brookside: /ˈbrʊksaɪd/ | n. | 小溪边，溪岸 | The area along the side of a brook. | We picnicked by the brookside. 我们在小溪边野餐。| 近义词: riverbank, streamside, waterside | 反义词: hilltop, mountainside | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From brook + side.  "Brook" comes from Old English *brōc (meaning "marsh, bog") related to Dutch "broek" (meaning "marsh") and German "Bruch" (meaning "bog, marsh, break").  "Side" comes from Old English "sīde" (meaning "side, flank").
