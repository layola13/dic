haughtinesses:/ˈhɔːtinɪsɪz/
n.
傲慢；高傲；自大
The state of being haughty; arrogance.
Her haughtinesses alienated her from her colleagues. 她的傲慢使她疏远了同事。
Her haughtinesses made her unpopular. 她的傲慢让她不受欢迎。

近义词: arrogance, pride, disdain, superciliousness, loftiness
反义词: humility, modesty, humbleness

词性: noun (plural)
词源: From Middle English *hauteinesse*, from Old French *hauteinesse, hautesse*, from *haut* (“high”) + *-esse* (suffix forming abstract nouns).
