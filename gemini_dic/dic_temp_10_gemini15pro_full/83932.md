half-civilized: /ˌhæf ˈsɪvɪlaɪzd/ | adj. | 半开化的，未完全开化的 |  Partially civilized; not fully developed culturally or socially; barbaric.  Describes a society or people considered to be at an intermediate stage of development, often implying a negative judgment. |  The explorers encountered a half-civilized tribe in the remote jungle. 探险家在偏远的丛林中遇到一个半开化的部落。

The journalist's article described the living conditions as half-civilized. 记者的文章将生活条件描述为半开化的。 | 近义词: barbaric, uncivilized, primitive, savage | 反义词: civilized, cultured, sophisticated, refined | 词性: 形容词
