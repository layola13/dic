inclusively:/ɪnˈkluːsɪvli/
adverb
包含地，包括在内地
including everything or everyone; all-embracing.
The price is $20 inclusively. 价格是20美元，包括一切费用。
inclusively:/ɪnˈkluːsɪvli/
adverb
包容地
in a way that includes a particular person or thing.
Our aim is to create a truly inclusively environment. 我们的目标是创造一个真正的包容性环境。
inclusively:/ɪnˈkluːsɪvli/
adverb
(用于数字、日期等) 从…到…，首尾两天均包括在内
from and including a particular day, number or time.
The festival runs from July 1st to 5th inclusively. 节日从7月1日持续到5日，包括首尾两天。


近义词: comprehensively, totally, altogether, fully
反义词: exclusively
词性: adverb
