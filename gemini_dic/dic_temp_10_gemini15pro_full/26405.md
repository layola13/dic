bye's:/baɪz/
noun
告别，再见（bye 的复数形式，非正式用法）
Plural form of "bye," used informally.
"See ya later, bye's!" 再见，拜拜！
近义词：goodbyes, farewells
反义词:greetings, hellos
词性:n.

Etymology:
From "bye," a shortened form of "goodbye," which in turn comes from "God be with ye."
