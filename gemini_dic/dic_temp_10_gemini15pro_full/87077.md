heptarchy: /ˈhɛptɑːki/ | n. | 七王国时代（指公元5至9世纪盎格鲁-撒克逊人建立的七个小王国） |  A collective term for the seven Anglo-Saxon kingdoms (Northumbria, Mercia, East Anglia, Essex, Kent, Sussex, and Wessex) that existed in England from the 6th to the 9th century. | The heptarchy was a period of frequent conflict and shifting alliances.  七王国时代是一个充满冲突和联盟变化的时期。 |  The heptarchy eventually coalesced into a single kingdom under the rule of Wessex.  七王国最终在威塞克斯的统治下合并成一个王国。 | Synonyms:  none (specific historical term) | Antonyms: none | noun
