allodial:/ˌælˈoʊdiəl/
adjective
中文翻译：完全保有土地所有权的；自由保有土地的
英文解析：Of or relating to land ownership in which the owner has full and absolute rights and is not subject to any superior landlord or feudal obligations.  Essentially, it means outright ownership.
例句：He inherited an allodial title to the land, meaning he owned it completely. 他继承了这块土地的完全所有权，这意味着他完全拥有它。

例句：Allodial ownership is less common in Europe than in the United States.  完全所有权在欧洲比在美国少见。
近义词：freehold, absolute
反义词：feudal, leasehold
词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *allodialis, from Frankish *allōd, meaning "full property," of Germanic origin.
