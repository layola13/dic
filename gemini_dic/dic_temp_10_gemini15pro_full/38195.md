compulsively:/kəmˈpʌlsɪvli/| adv.|强迫地，不由自主地|In an irresistible and repetitive manner, often despite negative consequences.  Driven by an inner urge or obsession.|He compulsively checks his phone every few minutes. 他每隔几分钟就忍不住要查看一下手机。|近义词:obsessively, uncontrollably, involuntarily|反义词:willingly, voluntarily|词性:副词
Etymology: From the adjective "compulsive," derived from the verb "compel" (from Latin "compellere," meaning "to drive together, force"), with the suffix "-ive" forming the adjective and "-ly" forming the adverb.
