franticness:/ˈfræntɪknəs/| n. |疯狂；狂乱|The state of being frantic; wild or distraught with fear, anxiety, or other emotion.|Her franticness was evident in her rapid breathing and wide eyes. 她的呼吸急促，眼睛睁得大大的，显然是慌乱极了。|例句：The franticness of the situation overwhelmed him.  情况的紧急让他不知所措。

例句：She tried to hide her franticness, but her trembling hands gave her away. 她试图掩饰自己的慌乱，但她颤抖的双手暴露了她。|近义词：frenzy, hysteria, wildness, desperation|反义词：calmness, composure, tranquility|词性:noun
词源: From frantic + -ness.  Frantic comes from the Old French *frantique, from Late Latin phreneticus, from Greek phrenitikos (“suffering from delirium”), from phrenitis (“inflammation of the brain”), from phrēn (“mind”).
