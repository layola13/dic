interdetermining:/ˌɪntərdiˈtɜːrmɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 相互决定的，互相制约的 | Mutually determining or influencing each other; reciprocally causative.  The actions of one element influence and shape the actions of the others in a complex system. |  The interdetermining factors of climate and vegetation shape the landscape. 气候和植被的相互作用因素塑造了地貌。

The interdetermining relationship between supply and demand affects market prices. 供求之间的相互制约关系影响着市场价格。 | 近义词：interdependent, interacting, correlated | 反义词：independent, unrelated | 词性: adjective

词源:  From "inter-" (between, among) + "determine" (to fix, settle). "Determine" itself comes from Latin "determinare," from "de-" (completely) + "terminare" (to set bounds to).
