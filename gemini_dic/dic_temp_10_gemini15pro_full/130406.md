non-volatility:/ˌnɒn vɒləˈtɪləti/ (US) /ˌnɑːn vɑːləˈtɪləti/| n.| 非易失性，非挥发性 |The non-volatility of the data is crucial for our system.  数据的非易失性对我们的系统至关重要。|  permanence, stability, durability | volatility, instability, fluctuation | noun
non-volatile:/ˌnɒn ˈvɒlətaɪl/ (US) /ˌnɑːn ˈvɑːlətəl/| adj.| 非易失的，非挥发性的 |This memory chip is non-volatile, meaning it retains data even when the power is off.  这个内存芯片是非易失性的，这意味着即使断电也能保存数据。| permanent, stable, durable | volatile, unstable, impermanent | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "non-" (not) + "volatile" (easily evaporating or changing; from Latin "volatilis," meaning "flying, fleeting").
