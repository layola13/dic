galvanotropism: /ˌɡælvənəˈtrəʊpɪzəm/ /ˌɡælvənəˈtroʊpɪzəm/
n. |向电性| The directional growth or movement of a living organism in response to an electric current. |Example: The galvanotropism of the plant's roots caused them to grow towards the positive electrode.  植物根的向电性使它们朝着正极生长。| Example: Researchers are studying the galvanotropism of certain bacteria to understand how they navigate in their environment. 研究人员正在研究某些细菌的向电性，以了解它们如何在环境中导航。 | 近义词: electrotropism | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From galvano- (relating to electric currents, after Luigi Galvani, Italian physician and physicist) + -tropism (a turning or movement in response to a stimulus).
