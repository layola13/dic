expended:/ɪkˈspendɪd/
verb
花费的，用尽的
past participle of expend.  Meaning "used up" or "spent completely".
They had expended all their ammunition. 他们已经用尽了所有的弹药。
近义词:spent, used, consumed, exhausted
反义词:saved, conserved, hoarded
词性:Verb (past participle)


expended:/ɪkˈspendɪd/
adjective
花费的，用尽的
Modified form of expend, describing something that has been used up or spent.
The expended fuel rods were carefully stored.  用过的燃料棒被小心地储存起来。
近义词:depleted, exhausted, spent
反义词:unused, remaining, conserved
词性:Adjective

词源：
From Latin expendere "to weigh out, pay out, disburse," from ex- "out" + pendere "to weigh, hang, pay."  The original sense of weighing out money or other valuables gradually shifted to the more general meaning of using up or spending.
