mahomedan:/məˈhɒmədən/ /məˈhɑːmədən/| adj. n.|穆罕默德的信徒，伊斯兰教徒 (现已弃用，带有冒犯意味)|An outdated and offensive term for a Muslim.  It is considered derogatory because it implies worship of Muhammad, which is against Islamic doctrine.  Use "Muslim" instead.|Example: The term "Mahomedan" was commonly used in the past, but it is now considered offensive.|近义词:Muslim (preferred), Islamic|反义词:Non-Muslim|词性:adjective, noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *Mahometan*, from Old French *Mahometan*, from Medieval Latin *Mahometanus*, ultimately derived from the name of the prophet Muhammad. The suffix "-an" indicates "follower of" or "pertaining to."
