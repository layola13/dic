n.
轻盈；不笨重
lack of ponderousness; the state or quality of not being ponderous.
Her nonponderousness allowed her to dance with effortless grace. 她的轻盈使她能够毫不费力地优雅地跳舞。
Synonyms: lightness, airiness, gracefulness, buoyancy, fleetness
Antonyms: ponderousness, heaviness, clumsiness, awkwardness
