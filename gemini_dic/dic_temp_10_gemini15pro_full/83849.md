halberdier:/ˈhælpərˌdɪər/ | n. |戟兵；持戟卫士|A soldier armed with a halberd (a combined spear and battle-axe).|例句：The halberdiers stood guard at the castle gate. 戟兵守卫着城堡大门。

例句：The king's personal guard was composed of highly trained halberdiers. 国王的私人卫队由训练有素的戟兵组成。

|近义词：spearman, pikeman, guardsman|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps civilian) | 词性: noun
