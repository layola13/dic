opercula: /əˈpɜːrkjʊlə/ /oʊˈpɜːrkjʊlə/
| n. |鳃盖；（某些腹足纲软体动物的）壳盖|A bony, movable flap that covers and protects the gills of most bony fishes. Also, the covering of the opening of certain gastropod mollusks.|The fish opened and closed its opercula as it breathed.| 近义词: gill cover, operculum (singular)| 反义词: None| Noun

operculum: /əˈpɜːrkjʊləm/ /oʊˈpɜːrkjʊləm/
| n. |鳃盖；（某些腹足纲软体动物的）壳盖|A bony, movable flap that covers and protects the gills of most bony fishes. Also, the covering of the opening of certain gastropod mollusks.|The snail retreated into its shell, sealing the opening with its operculum.| 近义词: gill cover| 反义词: None| Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *operculum* "lid, cover,"  from *operire* "to cover, close, shut," from *ob-* "over" + *wer-*, Proto-Indo-European root meaning "to cover, enclose."
