chioppine:/tʃɒpiːn/ | n. | 厚底高跟鞋；木屐 | A woman's shoe with a very thick wooden or cork sole. |  She wore chioppine to make herself appear taller. 她穿着厚底高跟鞋让自己看起来更高。 |  The actress donned a pair of chioppine for her role as a Venetian noblewoman.  这位女演员为扮演威尼斯贵族女子而穿上了一双厚底高跟鞋。 |  platform shoes, high heels | flats, low heels | noun

Etymology:
From Italian *cioppino* (Venetian *zoppin*), from *zoppo* "lame," ultimately from Vulgar Latin *(ex)cippus* "block of wood."
