disinterested:/dɪsˈɪntrəstɪd/
adjective
无私的；公正的；不感兴趣的
Uninfluenced by considerations of personal advantage; impartial.  Lacking interest.
例句：A disinterested observer may be able to provide a more objective perspective.  一个公正的观察者或许能够提供更客观的视角。

例句：He seemed disinterested in the lecture, constantly checking his phone.  他似乎对讲座不感兴趣，不停地查看手机。
近义词：impartial, unbiased, neutral, indifferent, apathetic
反义词：biased, prejudiced, partial, interested, keen


词源：
From dis- + interested.  The original sense was "unbiased, impartial," but from the early 18th century, it also acquired the sense "not interested," probably through confusion with uninterested.
