nonarbitrary:/ˌnɒnˈɑːbɪtrəri/ | /ˌnɑːnˈɑːrbɪtreri/
adjective
非任意的；非专横的；必然的
Not arbitrary; not based on random choice or personal whim, but rather on some reason, system, or principle.
例句：There's a nonarbitrary reason for choosing this location. 选择这个位置有一个非任意性的原因。
例句：The rules of grammar are nonarbitrary. 语法规则并非随意制定的。
近义词：rational, reasoned, logical, systematic, principled
反义词：arbitrary, random, capricious, whimsical
词性: adjective
