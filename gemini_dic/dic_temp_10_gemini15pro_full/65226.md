etiquettical: /ˌetɪˈkɛtɪk(ə)l/
adjective
礼仪的；礼节的
Relating to or conforming to etiquette; prescribed by etiquette.
She made an etiquettical blunder by arriving late to the dinner party. 她因迟到晚宴而犯了礼仪上的错误。
His etiquettical behavior impressed everyone at the formal event. 他在正式场合的礼仪行为给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。
近义词: polite, courteous, proper, decorous
反义词: impolite, rude, improper, indecorous
词性: adjective
词源: From French *étiquette* (label, ticket) + -al.
