aircrew:/ˈeəkruː/| n.|机组人员，机组|The aircrew prepared the plane for takeoff. 机组人员准备飞机起飞。|The entire aircrew was highly trained. 整个机组人员都受过严格训练。|近义词：flight crew, cockpit crew|反义词:passengers|noun

词源: air + crew.  "crew" comes from Old French "creue" meaning "growth, increase," later referring to a group of people.
