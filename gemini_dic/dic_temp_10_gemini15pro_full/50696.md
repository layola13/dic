dermestoid: /dɜːrˈmɛstɔɪd/ | adj. |  皮蠹状的；与皮蠹科甲虫有关的 | Relating to or resembling dermestid beetles, a family of beetles whose larvae feed on animal products such as skin, fur, and feathers. |  The dermestoid beetle infestation ruined the museum's taxidermy collection. 皮蠹状甲虫的侵扰毁坏了博物馆的动物标本收藏。 | Near synonyms:  dermestid | Antonyms: None | adjective
