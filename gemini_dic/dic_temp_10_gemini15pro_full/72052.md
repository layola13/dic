flirter:/ˈflɜːrtər/ (US) /ˈflɜːtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|调情者，卖弄风情的人|A person who flirts; someone who behaves as though attracted to someone, but not seriously. |She's a terrible flirter, she charms everyone she meets. 她是个十足的调情高手，她迷住了她遇到的每一个人。|近义词：coquette, tease|反义词：prude|词性:noun

flirter:/ˈflɜːrtər/ (US) /ˈflɜːtə(r)/ (UK)| v.|调情，卖弄风情|To behave as though attracted to someone, but not seriously. |He was flirting with the waitress. 他在和女服务员调情。|近义词：toy, trifle, dally|反义词：ignore, shun|词性:verb

词源: From the verb "flirt," which likely originated as an imitative word, perhaps suggestive of a light, quick movement or utterance.
