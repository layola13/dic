词源：From hydro- +‎ theca.  Hydro- comes from Greek "hydōr" meaning "water," referring to the aquatic nature of hydroids.  Theca comes from Greek "thēkē" meaning "case" or "receptacle."  Thus, hydrotheca literally means a "water case" or "water receptacle."
