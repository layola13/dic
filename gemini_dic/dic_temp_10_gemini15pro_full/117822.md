mellay:/ˈmeleɪ/| n.|混战，格斗，混乱|A confused fight or struggle among several people.|The two armies clashed in a fierce mellay.|近义词：brawl, fray, scuffle, tussle, free-for-all, melee|反义词：peace, order, calm, tranquility|词性:名词
Etymology: Middle English *melé*, from Anglo-French *medlee*, *meslee* “mixture, fight,” from *meler* “to mix.”
