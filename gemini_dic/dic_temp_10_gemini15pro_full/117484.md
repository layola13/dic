medley:/ˈmedli/ | n. |混合物，杂烩，混杂曲|A mixture of different things, especially a musical piece made up of parts of other pieces.|The band played a medley of Beatles songs. 乐队演奏了一首披头士歌曲的串烧。|近义词：mixture, assortment, potpourri, compilation |反义词: uniformity, homogeneity |词性:名词

medley:/ˈmedli/ | n. |混杂，混合物|A varied mixture of people or things.|The market offered a medley of sights, sounds, and smells. 市场提供了各种景象、声音和气味的混合体。|近义词：jumble, hodgepodge, melange |反义词: uniformity, homogeneity |词性:名词
