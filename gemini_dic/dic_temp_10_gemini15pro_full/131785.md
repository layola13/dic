nonconnotatively:/ˌnɑːnkɑːnəˈteɪtɪvli/ /ˌnɒnkɒnəˈteɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 非引申地，字面上地 |  In a way that focuses on the literal meaning of a word and avoids any implied or associated meanings. |  He defined the term nonconnotatively, sticking strictly to the dictionary definition. 他对这个术语进行了非引申的定义，严格遵循字典释义。

The scientist described the process nonconnotatively, avoiding any emotional or figurative language.  科学家非引申地描述了这个过程，避免使用任何情感或比喻性的语言。 | 近义词：literally, objectively, denotatively | 反义词：connotatively, figuratively, subjectively | 词性：副词
