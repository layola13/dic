factorizable:/ˈfæktərɪzəbl/ | adj. |可分解的，可因式分解的 | Able to be factored; that can be resolved into factors.  |The number 12 is factorizable into 2, 2, and 3.  12可以分解成2、2和3。|  The polynomial is easily factorizable. 这个多项式很容易分解。| synonyms: decomposable, resolvable | antonyms: irreducible, prime (for numbers), indecomposable | adjective
