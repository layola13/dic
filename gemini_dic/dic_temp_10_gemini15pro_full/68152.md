failingly:/ˈfeɪlɪŋli/
adverb
adv. |总是失败地；必然失败地|
English Explanation: In a way that is destined to fail; unsuccessfully.  It describes an action performed in a manner that guarantees failure.
例句：He failingly attempted to climb the mountain without proper equipment. 他总是失败地尝试在没有合适装备的情况下攀登这座山。

例句：She failingly tried to bake a cake, forgetting the baking powder. 她总是失败地尝试烤蛋糕，却忘记了发酵粉。


近义词：unsuccessfully, inevitably, constantly failing
反义词：successfully, effectively
词性：adverb
