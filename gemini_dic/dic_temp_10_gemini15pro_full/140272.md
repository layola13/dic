offtake:/ˈɔːfteɪk/ | n. | 承购；承购量；销路 | The act of taking off or away something, especially of buying or carrying away a product or a quantity of it.  A guaranteed minimum quantity of a product or service that a buyer agrees to purchase from a supplier. | The company signed an offtake agreement for the entire production of the new wind farm. 这家公司签署了一份承购协议，购买新风力发电厂的全部产量。 | synonyms: purchase, sale, consumption, demand | antonyms: supply, production | noun

offtakes:/ˈɔːfteɪks/ | n. | 承购；承购量；销路（offtake的复数形式） | The plural form of "offtake," referring to multiple instances of purchasing or carrying away products or services. |  Several large retailers have secured offtakes for the farmer's organic produce. 几家大型零售商已经获得了该农民的有机农产品的承购合同。 | synonyms: purchases, sales, consumption, demands | antonyms: supplies, production | noun
