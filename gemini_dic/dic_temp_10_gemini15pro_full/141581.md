oppressingly: /əˈprɛsɪŋli/
adverb
压抑地；沉重地
in a way that makes you feel worried and uncomfortable
The atmosphere in the room was oppressingly silent. 房间里的气氛压抑得令人窒息。
近义词: stiflingly, heavily, overwhelmingly
反义词: lightly, airily, cheerfully
词性: adverb

词源: From oppress + -ing + -ly.  Oppress comes from Old French *oppresser, from Latin oppressare "press against, press down, overburden," from ob- "against" (see ob-) + premere "to press" (see press (v.1)).
