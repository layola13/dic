obscurement:/əbˈskjʊəmənt/ |n.| 掩盖，隐藏，模糊 |The obscurement of the truth is a serious offense.  真相被掩盖是一项严重的罪行。| The heavy fog caused the obscurement of the distant mountains. 浓雾遮蔽了远处的山脉。 |近义词：concealment, hiding, obfuscation, eclipse |反义词：clarification, revelation, exposure |词性: 名词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *obscurement, from Middle French obscurcir, from Latin obscūrāre (“to darken”), from obscūrus (“dark”).
