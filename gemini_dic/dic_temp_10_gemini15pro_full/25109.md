buccinator:/ˈbʌsɪneɪtər/| n.| 颊肌；吹号手 |A thin, flat muscle in the wall of the cheek, used in blowing, whistling, and smiling.  A person who plays the trumpet or bugle.| The buccinator muscle helps us control the shape of our mouths. 颊肌帮助我们控制嘴巴的形状。| The principal buccinator of the town band was known for his impressive high notes.  小镇乐队的首席小号手以其令人印象深刻的高音而闻名。| synonyms: trumpeter, cornetist | antonyms: listener | noun
