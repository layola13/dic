non-mercantile:/ˌnɒn mɜːˈkæntaɪl/
adjective
非商业的，非营利的
Not primarily concerned with the making of profit; not connected with trade or commerce.
The organization is a non-mercantile venture, focused on social impact.  这个组织是一个非营利性企业，专注于社会影响。
Their motives were purely non-mercantile; they simply wanted to help. 他们的动机纯粹是非商业的；他们只是想帮忙。
synonym: non-profit, non-commercial, charitable
antonym: commercial, mercantile, for-profit
词性: adjective
