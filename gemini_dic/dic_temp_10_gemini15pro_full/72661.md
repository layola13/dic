foal:/foʊl/| n.|小马驹，幼驹|A young horse, especially one less than one year old.  A young donkey or zebra can also be called a foal.|The mare nuzzled her newborn foal.|Synonyms: colt (male foal), filly (female foal), yearling (horse between one and two years old)|Antonyms: stallion (adult male horse), mare (adult female horse)|noun


foal:/foʊl/| v.|产（驹）|To give birth to a foal.|The mare foaled last night.|Synonyms:  deliver (a foal)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the verb "to foal")|verb
