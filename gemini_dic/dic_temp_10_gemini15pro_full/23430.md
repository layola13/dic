bracteal:/ˈbræktɪəl/| adj.|苞片的，苞叶的|Relating to or resembling a bract; bractlike.  A bract is a modified leaf, usually small, associated with a flower or inflorescence.|The bracteal leaves are small and inconspicuous. 苞叶很小，不显眼。|Synonyms: bracted|Antonyms: N/A|adjective

bract:/brækt/| n.|苞片|A modified leaf, usually small, associated with a flower or inflorescence.  It may be brightly colored or reduced and scale-like.|The bright red bracts of the poinsettia are often mistaken for petals. 一品红的鲜红色苞片常被误认为是花瓣。|Synonyms: bracteole (for smaller bracts)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
