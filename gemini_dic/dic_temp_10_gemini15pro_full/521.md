aboma:/əˈboʊmə/
n.
蟒蛇; 蚺蛇
A large nonvenomous snake found in tropical South America.
The aboma coiled around its prey, slowly suffocating it.  巨蟒盘绕在猎物周围，慢慢地将其窒息而死。

近义词:boa constrictor, python
反义词:(无直接反义词，可考虑venomous snake 毒蛇)
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Tupi *mboîa.

USAGE->
The aboma is a powerful constrictor. =  蟒蛇是一种强大的缢缩者。

Local tribes have long revered the aboma. = 当地部落长期以来都崇敬蟒蛇。


PHRASE->
the aboma's coils = 蟒蛇的盘绕
an aboma attack = 蟒蛇的攻击
a giant aboma = 一条巨大的蟒蛇
the aboma's habitat = 蟒蛇的栖息地
track an aboma = 追踪蟒蛇
