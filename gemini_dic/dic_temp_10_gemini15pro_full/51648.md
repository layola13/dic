deviates:/ˈdiːvieɪts/ (US), /ˈdiːviˌeɪts/ (UK)| v. |偏离，背离|To depart or swerve from a procedure, course of action, or acceptable norm.|例句：The bus deviated from its usual route because of the road closure. 因为道路封闭，公共汽车偏离了通常的路线。

例句：His behavior deviates significantly from the norm. 他的行为明显偏离了规范。|近义词：diverges, strays, departs, swerves|反义词：conforms, adheres, follows|词性：动词

deviate:/ˈdiːvieɪt/ (US), /ˈdiːviˌeɪt/ (UK)| n. |偏离者，背离者|A person who departs from an established norm or course.|例句：He was considered a deviate because of his unconventional beliefs. 由于他不合常规的信仰，他被认为是一个背离者。

例句：She was seen as a deviate from the strict social norms of her community. 她被视为背离了她社区严格的社会规范的人。|近义词：nonconformist, maverick, dissident, heretic|反义词：conformist, traditionalist|词性：名词


词源: Late Middle English: from Latin deviat- ‘gone astray’, from the verb deviare, from de- ‘away’ + via ‘way’.
