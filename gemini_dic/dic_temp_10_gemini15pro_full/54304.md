dish-antenna:/ˈdɪʃ ænˌtɛnə/ | n. |碟形天线|A concave antenna used to collect or transmit radio waves, especially for satellite communication. It is named for its resemblance to a dish.|The dish antenna on the roof provides our internet access. 屋顶上的碟形天线为我们提供互联网接入。| synonym: parabolic reflector, satellite dish | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps yagi antenna as a contrasting type) | noun
