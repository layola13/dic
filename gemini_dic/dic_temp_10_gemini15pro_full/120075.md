mil:/mɪl/| n.|密耳，毫英寸，千分之一英寸|A unit of length equal to one thousandth of an inch (0.001 inch or 0.0254 millimeter), used especially in specifying the diameter of wires or the thickness of sheets.|The wire has a diameter of 10 mils.|近义词:thou (informal)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

mil:/mɪl/| n.|毫弧度，千分之一弧度|A unit of angular measurement equal to 1/6400 of a circle or approximately 0.056 degrees. It is often used in military and navigational applications.|The artillery piece was adjusted by 5 mils.|近义词:milliradian|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
