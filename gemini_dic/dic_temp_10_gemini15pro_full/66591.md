exhumation:/ˌɛkshjuˈmeɪʃən/ |n.|挖掘；发掘；掘尸|The exhumation of the body was ordered by the court. 法院下令挖掘尸体。|例句：The exhumation of the ancient city revealed many fascinating artifacts.  对这座古城的挖掘揭示了许多迷人的文物。

例句：The family requested the exhumation of their loved one to perform further DNA testing. 家人要求挖掘他们所爱之人的尸体进行进一步的DNA检测。

近义词：disinterment, unearthing|反义词: burial, interment|词性:noun

exhume:/ɪɡˈzjuːm/ |vt.|挖掘；发掘；掘尸|The police exhumed the body to investigate the cause of death. 警方掘尸调查死因。|例句：Archaeologists plan to exhume the remains of a Viking ship. 考古学家计划挖掘一艘维京船的残骸。

例句：The truth was exhumed after years of secrecy.  真相在多年的保密之后被挖掘出来。


近义词：disinter, unearth|反义词: bury, inter|词性:verb
