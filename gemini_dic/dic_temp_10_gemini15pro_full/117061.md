mdf:/ɛm diː ɛf/ | n. | 中密度纤维板 (Medium-Density Fiberboard) | A type of engineered wood product made by breaking down hardwood or softwood residuals into wood fibers, often in a defibrator, combining it with wax and a resin binder, and forming panels by applying high temperature and pressure. | The cabinets are made of mdf.  这些橱柜是用中密度纤维板做的。| synonym: fiberboard, chipboard | antonym: solid wood
