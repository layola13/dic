huffington:/ˈhʌfɪŋtən/ | adj. n.| 哈芬顿的；哈芬顿邮报（的）|  Relating to Arianna Huffington or the Huffington Post, a news website and blog. |  She's a regular contributor to the Huffington Post. 她定期为《赫芬顿邮报》撰稿。|  Synonym:  (for Huffington Post) HuffPost | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, could be contrasted with specific other news outlets, e.g., Fox News, Breitbart) | proper adjective, noun

Etymology:  Derived from the surname of Arianna Huffington, the Greek-American author, syndicated columnist, and co-founder of The Huffington Post.  "Post" in this context refers to a news publication.
