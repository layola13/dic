mydriasis:/mɪˈdraɪəsɪs/
noun
瞳孔散大
Dilation of the pupil of the eye.
The doctor observed mydriasis in the patient's eyes after the trauma.  医生在病人遭受创伤后观察到他的瞳孔散大。
Mydriasis can be a symptom of certain medical conditions. 瞳孔散大可能是某些疾病的症状。
近义词: dilated pupils
反义词: miosis
词性: noun


mydriatic:/ˌmɪdriˈætɪk/
adjective
散瞳的
Causing dilation of the pupil of the eye.
The ophthalmologist used a mydriatic eye drop to examine the patient's retina. 眼科医生使用散瞳眼药水检查病人的视网膜。
Some medications have mydriatic side effects.  一些药物有散瞳的副作用。
近义词: pupil-dilating
反义词: miotic
词性: adjective


mydriatic:/ˌmɪdriˈætɪk/
noun
散瞳剂
A substance that causes dilation of the pupil of the eye.
The doctor administered a mydriatic before the eye exam. 医生在眼科检查前使用了散瞳剂。
Atropine is a commonly used mydriatic. 阿托品是一种常用的散瞳剂。
近义词: pupil dilator
反义词: miotic
词性: noun


Etymology: From Ancient Greek μυδρίασις (mudríasis, “excessive dilation of the pupil”), from μυδρός (mudrós, “red-hot, glowing”).
