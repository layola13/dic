carry-away:/ˈkæri əˈweɪ/ | adj. |忘乎所以的，欣喜若狂的| Overwhelmed by emotion, especially excitement or enthusiasm.|  例句：The music carried her away. 音乐让她忘乎所以。|近义词：ecstatic, elated, thrilled, overjoyed|反义词：calm, composed, serene|词性:形容词
carry away:/ˈkæri əˈweɪ/ | v. |带走，拿走；使失去自制力| 1. To remove something from a place. 2. To cause someone to lose self-control due to strong emotion.| 例句：The flood carried away their house. 洪水冲走了他们的房子。|近义词：transport, remove, sweep away|反义词：bring, return|词性:动词

词源：From the Middle English *carien awei*, meaning "to carry away," from the Old English *carian* (to carry) and *aweg* (away).
