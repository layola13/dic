hyperromanticism:/ˌhaɪpərroʊˈmæntɪsɪzəm/
n.
过度浪漫主义；极端浪漫主义
An exaggerated form of romanticism characterized by an intense focus on emotions, imagination, and individualism, often to the point of impracticality or idealism.
Her poetry is filled with hyperromanticism, dreaming of unattainable love and exotic escapes. 她写的诗充满了过度浪漫主义，梦想着无法实现的爱情和异国情调的逃离。
近义词:  excessive romanticism, extravagant romanticism
反义词: realism, cynicism
词性: noun
