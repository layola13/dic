nonreceptions:/ˌnɒn rɪˈsɛpʃənz/
|n.|未接待; 未接收; 冷遇|The nonreceptions of the refugees at the border was appalling.  边境对难民的冷遇令人震惊。|The embassy's nonreceptions of visa applications caused widespread frustration. 大使馆对签证申请的不受理引起了普遍的沮丧。|近义词: rejection, refusal, snub|反义词: reception, acceptance, welcome|词性: 名词


Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non, meaning "not"
reception (noun):  from Late Middle English: from Old French recepcion, from Latin receptio(n-), from recipere ‘take back, receive’.
