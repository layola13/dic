inconformity's:/ˌɪnkənˈfɔːrmətiz/ | n. |不整合；不一致；不符合| The state or quality of not conforming; lack of conformity.  Specifically in geology, a break in the rock record produced by erosion or non-deposition. | The unconformity's age was determined by radiometric dating.  这个不整合面的年龄是由放射性测年确定的。| Near synonyms: discordance, disparity, disharmony,  inconsistency,  variance,  variation. | Antonyms: conformity, agreement, harmony, consistency.
词性: 名词 (Noun), 所有格形式
