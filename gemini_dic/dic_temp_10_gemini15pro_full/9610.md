antispiritualism:/ˌæntiˈspɪrɪtʃuəlɪzəm/
n.
反唯心主义，反精神主义
Opposition to or rejection of spiritual or religious beliefs, values, and practices.
The rise of antispiritualism in the 20th century can be attributed to several factors, including scientific advancements and the horrors of war.  20世纪反唯心主义的兴起可归因于多种因素，包括科学进步和战争的恐怖。

His antispiritualism led him to reject all forms of organized religion. 他的反唯心主义使他拒绝所有形式的有组织宗教。

近义词:materialism, secularism, atheism
反义词:spiritualism, religiosity, piety
词性:noun
