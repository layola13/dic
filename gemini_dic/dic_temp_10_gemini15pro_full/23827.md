breach:/briːtʃ/

| n. |违反，破坏；缺口，裂口|An act of breaking a law, agreement, promise or relationship.  A gap or hole made in a wall, defence, etc.|The company sued him for breach of contract.  该公司控告他违反合同。|synonyms: violation, infringement, infraction, contravention |antonyms: compliance, observance, adherence|noun
| vt. |违反，破坏；突破，攻破|To break a law, agreement, promise or relationship. To make a gap or hole in something.|The attackers breached the castle walls. 攻击者攻破了城堡的城墙。|synonyms: violate, infringe, break, contravene |antonyms: comply with, observe, adhere to|verb
