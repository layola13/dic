downcastness:/daʊnˈkæstnɪs/| n. |沮丧，垂头丧气|A state of being downcast; low spirits; dejection.|She couldn't hide the downcastness in her eyes after hearing the bad news.  听到坏消息后，她无法掩饰眼神中的沮丧。|近义词：dejection, despondency, gloom, sadness, melancholy|反义词：cheerfulness, happiness, elation, joy|noun


downcast:/ˈdaʊnkæst/| adj. |沮丧的，垂头丧气的|Directed downwards, typically as a sign of sadness or shame.|He walked with a downcast gaze, his shoulders slumped. 他低着头，肩膀耷拉着走着。|近义词：dejected, despondent, gloomy, sad, melancholy|反义词：cheerful, happy, elated, joyful|adjective
