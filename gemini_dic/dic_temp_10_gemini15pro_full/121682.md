mispleading:/mɪsˈpliːdɪŋ/
noun
误辩；错误陈述
Presenting a case or argument incorrectly in a legal context.
The lawyer's mispleading resulted in the case being dismissed. 律师的误辩导致案件被驳回。
misstatement, false pleading
correct pleading
