alongshore: /əˈlɔːŋʃɔːr/ /əˈlɑːŋʃɔːr/
adjective
沿岸的，沿着海岸的
situated or moving along the shore.
例句：Alongshore currents can be dangerous for swimmers. 沿岸流对游泳者来说可能很危险。

例句：The alongshore winds kept the sailboat close to the coast.  沿岸的风使帆船靠近海岸。

近义词：coastal, littoral, shoreline
反义词：offshore, inland
词性：adjective

alongshore: /əˈlɔːŋʃɔːr/ /əˈlɑːŋʃɔːr/
adverb
沿着海岸
along the shore.
例句：They walked alongshore, collecting seashells. 他们沿着海岸走，收集贝壳。

例句：The lifeguard patrolled alongshore, watching for swimmers in distress. 救生员沿着海岸巡逻，注意是否有遇险的游泳者。

近义词：coastwise, shoreward
反义词：seaward, inland
词性：adverb
