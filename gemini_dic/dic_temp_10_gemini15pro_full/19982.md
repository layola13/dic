biogenist:/baɪˈəʊˈdʒenɪst/ | n. | 生物学家；生物遗传学家 | A biologist specializing in the study of the genetic basis of life.  |  例句：The biogenist studied the DNA of various species to understand their evolutionary relationships.  生物学家研究了各种物种的DNA，以了解它们的进化关系。
