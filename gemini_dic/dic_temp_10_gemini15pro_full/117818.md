melitose:/ˈmelɪtoʊs/| n.|蜜里三糖; 松三糖; 棉子糖|A trisaccharide sugar found in some plant nectars and in several leguminous plants.|Melitose is found in significant quantities in eucalyptus honey.  蜜里三糖大量存在于桉树蜂蜜中。

Melitose can be hydrolyzed to glucose and melibiose. 蜜里三糖可以水解成葡萄糖和蜜二糖。|近义词：raffinose (棉子糖, often used interchangeably, though technically slightly different)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
