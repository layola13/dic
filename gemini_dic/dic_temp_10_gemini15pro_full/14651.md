ETYMOLOGY->
From avowable + -ness.  Avowable comes from the verb "avow" (meaning to declare openly and unashamedly), which in turn comes from Old French *avouer (from Latin advocare "to call to, summon"). The suffix "-ness" forms nouns indicating a state or quality.
