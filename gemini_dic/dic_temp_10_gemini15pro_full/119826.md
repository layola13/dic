milady:/mɪˈleɪdi/ | n. | 夫人；小姐；（古）我的夫人 | A term of respectful or polite address to a woman, somewhat archaic and often used playfully or ironically. | Milady, your carriage awaits. 夫人，您的马车已备好。|  My lady, would you care for another glass of wine? 女士，您想再来一杯酒吗？ | 近义词：madam, ma'am, lady | 反义词: (No direct antonym, but could be contrasted with terms of disrespect) | noun
