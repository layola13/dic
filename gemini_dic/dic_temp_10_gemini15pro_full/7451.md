anisole:/ˈænɪsɒl/ (UK), /ˈænɪsoʊl/ (US)| n. |苯甲醚;茴香醚|A colorless liquid, CH3OC6H5, with an aromatic odor, used in perfumery and organic synthesis.|Anisole is used as a starting material in the synthesis of various pharmaceuticals.|近义词:methoxybenzene|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From French anis + -ol.  Anis from Latin anethum, from Ancient Greek ἄνηθον (ánēthon, “anise, dill”).  -ol indicates an alcohol or phenol.
