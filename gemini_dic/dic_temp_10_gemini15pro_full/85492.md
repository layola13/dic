haste:/heɪst/ |n.| 匆忙，急忙；轻率 |Excessive speed or urgency of movement or action; needlessly hurried action. |She finished the job in haste. 她匆忙地完成了工作。|近义词: hurry, rush, speed, expedition |反义词: leisure, calmness, deliberation | noun

haste:/heɪst/ |v.| 催促；赶紧 |To hurry; to move or act quickly.  (Archaic usage, more common as a noun) |He hasted to finish the project. 他赶紧完成这个项目。| 近义词: hurry, rush, speed up |反义词: delay, dawdle, procrastinate | verb
