mad-as-a-hatter:/ˌmæd əz ə ˈhætər/| adj.|疯狂的，精神错乱的|Completely insane or eccentric. This idiom likely originated from the 18th and 19th centuries when hat makers frequently suffered from mercury poisoning due to their use of mercury nitrate in felt processing, leading to neurological damage and erratic behavior.|He's been acting mad as a hatter lately, talking to himself and wearing mismatched socks.|近义词：crazy, insane, bonkers, lunatic, demented|反义词：sane, rational, lucid|词性：形容词
