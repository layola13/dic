dangerously:/ˈdeɪndʒərəsli/ | adv. | 危险地 | In a way that is likely to cause harm or injury. | He was driving dangerously fast. 他开车开得非常危险，速度很快。| The building was dangerously close to collapsing. 这栋楼非常危险，快要倒塌了。| 近义词: precariously, hazardously, riskily | 反义词: safely, securely | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *dangerosly*, from Old French *dangereusement*, from *dangereus* (dangerous) + *-ment* (adverbial suffix). Ultimately from Latin *dominus* ("master, lord").
