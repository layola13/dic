cloyingness:/ˈklɔɪɪŋnɪs/| n.|甜得发腻；腻味；|Excessive sweetness or sentimentality that becomes unpleasant or distasteful.|The cloyingness of the perfume made her head ache. 这款香水的甜腻让她头疼。|近义词：sickliness, sweetness, saccharinity|反义词:tartness, acidity, blandness|词性:noun

cloy:/klɔɪ/| v.|使生腻；使吃腻；|To make someone feel sick or disgusted by providing too much of something rich, sweet, or sentimental.|The overly sweet dessert began to cloy. 过甜的甜点开始让人感到腻味。|近义词：satiate, surfeit, glut|反义词:stimulate, refresh|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cloien*, "to clog, obstruct," from Old French *cloer*, "to nail, spike, stop up," ultimately from Latin *claudere*, "to close, shut."  The sense of "to make sick with sweetness" developed later.
