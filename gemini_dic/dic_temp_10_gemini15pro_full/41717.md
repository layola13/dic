corroboree: /kəˈrɒbəri/ (UK), /kəˈrɑːbəri/ (US) | n. | 狂欢会，欢庆仪式; (澳洲土著的) 夜间歌舞集会 | A nocturnal gathering of Australian Aboriginals, especially one marked by elaborate rites and dances. |  The tribe held a corroboree to celebrate the successful hunt. 部落举行狂欢会庆祝狩猎成功。|  The corroboree featured traditional music and storytelling.  狂欢会上展现了传统的音乐和故事讲述。 | synonyms: celebration, festival, ceremony, ritual, gathering | antonyms: wake, funeral | noun

corroboree: /kəˈrɒbəri/ (UK), /kəˈrɑːbəri/ (US) | vt. | 为…举行狂欢会；庆祝 | To hold or celebrate with a corroboree. | They corroboreed the victory with a night of dancing and singing. 他们举行歌舞庆祝胜利。 | The community corroboreed the arrival of the elders. 社区为长老们的到来举行了庆祝活动。 | synonyms: celebrate, commemorate, festivate | antonyms: mourn, grieve | verb
