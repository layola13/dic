lousier:/ˈluːziər/ (US), /ˈluːzɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更糟糕的，更差的，更低劣的|Comparative form of lousy: worse, of inferior quality.|My new apartment is even lousier than my old one. 我的新公寓比我的旧公寓更糟糕。|The weather has been getting lousier and lousier. 天气越来越糟了。|近义词：worse, poorer, inferior, shoddier|反义词：better, superior, finer|词性:形容词
词源: From lousy + -er (comparative suffix). Ultimately from louse, referring to infestation with lice.
