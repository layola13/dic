intermarriage:/ˌɪntərˈmærɪdʒ/ | n. |通婚，异族通婚 | Marriage between people of different social groups, races, or religions. | Example 1: Intermarriage between different religious groups has become more common. 不同宗教群体之间的通婚变得越来越普遍。

Example 2: The intermarriage rate between different ethnic groups is rising. 不同种族群体之间的通婚率正在上升。 | Synonyms: interracial marriage, mixed marriage | Antonyms: endogamy, homogamy | noun
