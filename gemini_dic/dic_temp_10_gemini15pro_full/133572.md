nonflagrant:/ˌnɒnˈfleɪɡrənt/
adjective
非公然的；不显著的；轻微的
Not conspicuously or obviously offensive, blatant, or outrageous.
The referee determined that the foul was nonflagrant and awarded the player two free throws. 裁判判定该犯规不明显，并判给该球员两次罚球。
The lawyer argued that the violation was nonflagrant and did not warrant a severe penalty. 律师辩称，违规行为并不明显，不应该受到严厉的惩罚。
Synonyms: minor, slight, inconspicuous, unobtrusive, subtle
Antonyms: flagrant, blatant, obvious, conspicuous, outrageous
词性: 形容词
