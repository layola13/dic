counterjumper:/ˈkaʊntəˌdʒʌmpə(r)/| n.| 店员，柜台售货员 | A person who works selling goods in a shop, especially one who has to climb over or reach across a counter to get them. |  The counterjumper was busy serving customers all afternoon.  那个店员整个下午都在忙着服务顾客。| synonyms: shop assistant, salesclerk, clerk | antonyms: customer, shopper, buyer | noun

counterjumper:/ˈkaʊntərˌdʒʌmpər/| n.| (derogatory, dated)  店员，柜台售货员（贬义，过时）| A derogatory and outdated term for a shop assistant, implying a low-status, unskilled job. | The old man referred to the young shop assistant as a counterjumper, much to her annoyance. 老人称这位年轻的店员为柜台售货员，这让她很恼火。| synonyms: shop assistant, salesclerk, clerk (though these aren't derogatory) | antonyms: customer, shopper, buyer | noun

词源:  "counter" (柜台) + "jumper" (跳跃者).  Originally referred to shop assistants who had to jump or climb over counters to retrieve goods for customers, especially in older shops. Now largely outdated and sometimes considered derogatory.
