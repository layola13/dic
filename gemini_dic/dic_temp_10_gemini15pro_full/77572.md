gears:/ɡɪərz/| n.|齿轮，传动装置，（汽车）挡位，工具，设备|A toothed wheel that works with others to alter the relation between the speed of a driving mechanism (such as the engine of a vehicle) and the speed of the driven parts (such as the wheels).  A particular setting of gears in a vehicle. The equipment or apparatus needed for a particular activity.|The car's gears were grinding. 汽车的齿轮发出嘎吱嘎吱的响声。|synonyms: cogs, cogwheels, sprockets, equipment, apparatus|antonyms: -|noun
