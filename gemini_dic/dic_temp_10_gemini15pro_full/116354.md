masthead:/ˈmæstˌhɛd/ (US), /ˈmɑːsthed/ (UK)| n.|桅顶; 报刊名称及出版人员名单; (公司或组织的)领导层|The masthead lists the publisher and editors of the newspaper. 报刊名称及出版人员名单列出了报纸的出版商和编辑。|例句：

The ship's masthead was damaged in the storm.
船的桅顶在暴风雨中受损了。


The newspaper's masthead proudly displayed its long history.
这家报纸的报头自豪地展示了其悠久的历史。

|近义词：header, banner, title, leadership, management|反义词:staff, employees|词性:noun
