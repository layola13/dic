Barakpur: /ˈbɑːrəkˌpʊr/ | n. | 巴拉克普尔 | A city and a municipality of North 24 Parganas district in the Indian state of West Bengal. |  Barakpur is known for its historical significance. 巴拉克普尔以其历史意义而闻名。|  Barakpur was a cantonment town during British rule. 巴拉克普尔在英国统治时期是一个兵营镇。 | synonyms:  city, town, municipality | antonyms: countryside, rural area | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Bengali "Barrackpore," derived from the English word "barracks" referring to the military housing established there during British rule.  The "-pore" suffix is a common Bengali place name element, often indicating a settlement or town.
