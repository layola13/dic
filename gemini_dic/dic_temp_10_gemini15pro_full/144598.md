outweave:/ˌaʊtˈwiːv/
verb
交织；编织出
to weave or interlace elaborately
The tapestry was outwoven with gold and silver threads. 这幅挂毯用金线和银线交织而成。

outweave:/ˌaʊtˈwiːv/
verb
胜过编织
to surpass in weaving
Her skill outwove that of all the other weavers. 她的技艺胜过所有其他织工。


近义词: interweave, intertwine, interlace
反义词: unravel, untwine

词性: verb (动词)
