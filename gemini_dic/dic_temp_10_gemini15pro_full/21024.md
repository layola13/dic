blether:/ˈblɛðər/ (British English), /ˈblɛðər/ (American English)| vi. n.|胡说八道，闲聊，废话|To talk foolishly or at length; meaningless or foolish talk.|例句：Stop blethering and get to the point! 别胡扯了，说重点！|近义词：babble, chatter, prattle, ramble|反义词: be quiet, be silent|词性:verb, noun


词源: From Scots *blether, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse *blaðra (“to talk nonsense”).
