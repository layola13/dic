aurigraphy:/ɔːˈrɪɡrəfi/
n.
耳诊
The diagnosis of disease by examination of the ear.
Aurigraphy is a diagnostic technique that has not been scientifically validated.  耳诊是一种尚未得到科学验证的诊断技术。
Aurigraphy is sometimes used in alternative medicine. 耳诊有时用于替代医学。
近义词:  ear examination, auriculodiagnosis
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider standard medical diagnosis)
词性: noun
