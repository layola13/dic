connective:/kəˈnɛktɪv/ | adj. n.|连接的，连接词|Serving to connect; connecting.  A word or phrase that connects clauses or sentences.|The bridge serves as a connective structure between the two cities. 这座桥是连接两个城市的纽带。|Synonyms: joining, linking, uniting|Antonyms: disconnecting, separating|adjective

connective:/kəˈnɛktɪv/ | n.|连接词|A word or phrase that connects clauses or sentences.| "And," "but," and "or" are common connectives. “和”、“但是”和“或”是常见的连接词。|Synonyms: conjunction, link, tie|Antonyms: N/A|noun
