hypersensuousnesses:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnʃuːəsnəsɪz/
|n.|超感性，极度感性|A state of heightened or excessive sensitivity to sensory stimuli, especially those considered aesthetic or spiritual.|Her poetry is filled with hypersensuousnesses, evoking a world of vibrant colors and intoxicating fragrances. 她的诗歌充满了超感性，唤起了一个充满活力色彩和醉人香味的世界。|The artist's hypersensuousnesses allowed him to perceive subtle nuances in light and shadow that others missed. 这位艺术家的超感性让他能够感知到其他人忽略的光影微妙之处。|近义词：hypersensitivity, supersensitivity|反义词：insensitivity, numbness|词性: noun
