attentat:/ˌætɑ̃ˈtɑː/ /əˈtɛntæt/ | n. | 袭击，攻击，暗杀 | An attempt to kill or injure someone, especially a prominent person, for political or ideological reasons. |  The president survived the attentat. 总统在袭击中幸存下来。


The attentat on the prime minister failed. 对总理的暗杀失败了。 | 近义词：attack, assault, assassination | 反义词：defense, protection | 词性：noun

Etymology: From French *attentat*, from *attenter* "to attempt," from Latin *attentare* "to attempt, try," from *ad-* "to" + *tentare* "to handle, touch, test, try."
