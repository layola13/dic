deposition:/ˌdɛpəˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 沉积；沉积物；免职；证词 | the process by which sediment, soil, and rocks are added to a landform or land mass | The deposition of sand on the beach created a beautiful landscape. 沙子在海滩上的沉积创造了美丽的景观。 | synonyms: sediment, deposit, accumulation, testimony, affidavit | antonyms: erosion, removal, reinstatement | noun

deposition:/ˌdɛpəˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 宣誓作证；证词 |  a formal, usually written, statement to be used as evidence |  His deposition was crucial to the case. 他的证词对这个案子至关重要。| synonyms: testimony, affidavit, sworn statement | antonyms:  repudiation, denial | noun
