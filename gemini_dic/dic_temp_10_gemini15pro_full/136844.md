nonsimplicities:/ˌnɒnsɪmˈplɪsɪtiz/ | n. | 非简单性，复杂性 |  The state or quality of not being simple; complexity. The nonsimplicities of the human brain are still being unravelled by scientists.  科学家们仍在努力解开人脑的复杂性。 | Synonyms: complexities, intricacies, complications | Antonym: simplicities | noun
