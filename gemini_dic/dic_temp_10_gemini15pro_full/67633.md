extremists:/ɪkˈstriːmɪsts/
n.
极端主义者；极端分子
A person who holds extreme political or religious views, especially one who resorts to or advocates extreme action.
Extremists from both sides of the political spectrum clashed violently.  政治光谱两端的极端分子发生了暴力冲突。

近义词:fanatics, radicals, zealots
反义词:moderates, centrists
词性:noun


extremist:/ɪkˈstriːmɪst/
adj.
极端的；极端主义的
Holding extreme political or religious views.
The group holds extremist views on immigration.  该组织对移民持有极端观点。

近义词:radical, extreme, fanatical
反义词:moderate, centrist
词性:adjective
