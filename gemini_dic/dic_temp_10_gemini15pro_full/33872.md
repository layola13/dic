circumspect:/ˈsɜːrkəmspekt/ | adj. | 谨慎的，慎重的，周到的 | Careful to consider all circumstances and possible consequences; cautious. | She was circumspect in her choice of words, knowing the issue was sensitive. 她知道这个问题很敏感，所以在措辞上很谨慎。

He was circumspect about making promises he couldn't keep. 他对做出无法兑现的承诺持谨慎态度。


| 近义词：cautious, prudent, wary, discreet, judicious | 反义词：reckless, impulsive, careless, rash, imprudent | 词性: adjective
