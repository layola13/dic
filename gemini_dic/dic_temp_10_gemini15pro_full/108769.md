leased:/liːst/| adj.|租用的，出租的|Rented out under a lease agreement; satisfied or pleased.|例句：The company leased a new office building.  这家公司租了一栋新的办公楼。|例句：She was leased with the outcome of the negotiation. 她对谈判结果感到满意。|近义词：rented, hired, satisfied, content|反义词：owned, bought, dissatisfied, discontented|词性:形容词
leased:/liːst/| v.|出租|Past participle of "lease," meaning to grant use or occupation of under a lease.|例句：The building is leased to a large corporation. 这栋楼租给了一家大公司。|例句：They leased the car for three years. 他们租了这辆车三年。|近义词：rent, hire, let|反义词：buy, purchase|词性:动词

词源:
From Old French lesser "to let, allow," from Latin laxare "to loosen, slacken, let go," from laxus "loose."  The meaning shifted from "allow" to "grant use of" over time.
