lifer:/ˈlaɪfər/ (US), /ˈlaɪfə/ (UK)| n.| 终身职业者；无期徒刑犯|A person who spends their entire career in a particular profession or organization, or a person serving a life sentence in prison.|He's a lifer in the army, having served for over 30 years. 他在军队服役超过30年，是个老兵。

He was sentenced to life, becoming a lifer at the age of 25. 他被判处无期徒刑，25岁就成了无期徒刑犯。|近义词：veteran, long-timer (for career); convict, inmate (for prison)|反义词:short-timer, newcomer|词性:名词
