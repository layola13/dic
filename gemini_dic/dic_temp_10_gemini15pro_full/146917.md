overintellectualization: /ˌoʊvərɪntəˌlɛktʃuələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度理性化，过度思考 | The act of thinking about something too much and making it more complicated than it needs to be.  It often involves analyzing a situation or problem excessively, to the point where it becomes confusing or paralyzing. |  He suffered from overintellectualization, analyzing every detail of his relationships until they fell apart. 他过度思考，分析了他恋爱关系中的每一个细节，直到它们破裂。

Overintellectualization can be a barrier to taking action. 过度理性化可能会成为行动的障碍。 | 近义词: overthinking, overanalyzing | 反义词:  simplifying, intuition | 词性: noun
