expansible:/ɪkˈspændsəbl/
adjective
可扩展的，可膨胀的
Capable of being expanded or enlarged.
The suitcase is expansible, allowing for extra packing space.  这个行李箱是可扩展的，可以提供额外的打包空间。
The company's market share is expansible with the right marketing strategy.  凭借正确的营销策略，该公司的市场份额是可以扩展的。
近义词: expandable, extensible, stretchable
反义词: non-expansible, inflexible, rigid
词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *expandsibilis, from Latin expansus (past participle of expandere "to spread out") + -ibilis (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being").
