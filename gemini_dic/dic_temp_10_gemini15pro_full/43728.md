creedlessness:/ˈkriːdləsnɪs/
n.
lack of creed; absence of religious belief
The rise of creedlessness among young people is a complex sociological phenomenon. 年轻人中缺乏宗教信仰的现象日益增多是一个复杂的社会学现象。
Creedlessness can stem from various factors, including disillusionment with organized religion.  缺乏宗教信仰可能源于多种因素，包括对有组织宗教的失望。
近义词:nonbelief, unbelief, irreligion, atheism, agnosticism
反义词:faith, belief, religiosity, piety
词性:noun

词源: creed + -less + -ness
creed (from Latin credo "I believe") denotes a system of religious belief.
-less is a suffix indicating absence or lack of something.
-ness is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives or other parts of speech, indicating a state or quality.
