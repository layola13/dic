materially:/məˈtɪriəli/
adverb|实质上地，重要地，显著地|In a way that relates to physical objects or money; significantly.|Higher prices materially affected the company's profits.  高价显著影响了公司的利润。|
materially:/məˈtɪriəli/
adverb|物质上地|Relating to worldly possessions; in a physical or monetary way.|Their living conditions have improved materially. 他们的物质生活条件得到了改善。|significantly, substantially, considerably|immaterially, insignificantly, negligibly|adverb
materially:/məˈtɪriəli/
adverb|在很大程度上|To a significant extent; considerably.|The witness's testimony materially altered the outcome of the case. 证人的证词在很大程度上改变了案件的结果。 |significantly, substantially, considerably|slightly, minimally, insignificantly|adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *materialy*, from Old French *material*, from Latin *materialis* ("of or belonging to matter"), from *materia* ("matter").
