burghbote:/ˈbɜːrɡboʊt/
n.
城镇修缮义务；城镇维护费
An archaic term referring to a contribution or tax levied on inhabitants of a borough for the maintenance or repair of its fortifications or other public works.
The burghbote was collected annually to maintain the town walls.  城镇修缮费每年都会被征收用于维护城墙。
The lord demanded a heavy burghbote after the siege damaged the town's gates.  围攻破坏了城门之后，领主要求征收大量的城镇维护费。
近义词:  town tax, borough rate, municipal levy
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps exemption from burghbote)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *burgbot, from burg "borough, fortified town" + bot "remedy, compensation, amendment."
