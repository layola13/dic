doting:/ˈdoʊtɪŋ/ /ˈdəʊtɪŋ/
adjective
溺爱的；宠爱的
showing fondness or excessive love, especially to children or pets.
She is a doting mother who loves her children very much. 她是一位慈爱的母亲，非常爱她的孩子。
He's always been a doting grandfather. 他一直是一位溺爱孙辈的祖父。
近义词:affectionate, fond, loving, adoring
反义词:neglectful, indifferent, uncaring
词性:adjective

doting:/ˈdoʊtɪŋ/ /ˈdəʊtɪŋ/
verb (present participle of 'dote')
溺爱；宠爱
To be excessively fond and indulgent.  This is less common than the adjective form.
She's always doting on her grandchildren, buying them whatever they want. 她总是溺爱她的孙子孙女，给他们买任何他们想要的东西。
He's been doting on that puppy ever since he got it. 自从他得到那只小狗以来，他就一直宠爱它。
近义词:pampering, spoiling, indulging
反义词:neglecting, ignoring
词性:verb
