near-and-far:/ˌnɪr ən(d) ˈfɑːr/ | adj. | 远近的；四面八方的；来自各地的 | Affecting or relating to places or people both near and far; from everywhere. |  The festival attracts visitors from near and far.  这个节日吸引了来自四面八方的游客。 |  The king ruled over lands near and far. 国王统治着远近的土地。 | Synonyms: widespread, extensive, far-flung, far-reaching | Antonyms: local, regional, nearby, close | adjective
