bauk: /baʊk/
n.
鬼怪，妖怪；吓唬人的东西
A mythical creature, typically a frightening apparition.
The children were scared by stories of the bauk that lived in the forest. 孩子们被森林里住着妖怪的故事吓坏了。

近义词: monster, ghost, goblin, spectre, phantom
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用reality真实，或human人类)
词性: 名词


词源:  The word "bauk" originates from Slavic folklore, particularly from South Slavic languages like Serbo-Croatian and Slovene. It refers to a mythical creature often depicted as a frightening apparition or monster.
