conjuncture:/ˈkɒndʒəŋktʃə(r)/ (UK) /kənˈdʒʌŋktʃər/ (US)| n. |结合, 同时发生, 情况, 局面|A particular combination of circumstances.  The coming together of several events or conditions.|例句：A rare conjuncture of events led to his success.  一系列罕见的事件导致了他的成功。|例句：The current political conjuncture is highly volatile. 当前的政治局势非常不稳定。|近义词：convergence, juncture, confluence,  situation, state of affairs|反义词:disjunction, separation|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English:  from Latin *conjunctura, from conjunct- ‘joined together’, from the verb conjungere.
