hederaceous:/ˌhɛdərˈeɪʃəs/ | adj. | 似常春藤的，常春藤状的 | Resembling or characteristic of ivy (Hedera).  Covered with or resembling ivy. | The hederaceous vine climbed the old stone wall.  这株常春藤状的藤蔓爬上了古老的石墙。 | Near Synonyms: ivy-like, hederiform | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider terms like "bare," "uncovered" if referring to a surface) | adjective
