disproportionate:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənɪt/ | adj. | 不成比例的，不相称的 | Not in proportion; out of proportion; too large or too small in comparison with something else. | The punishment was disproportionate to the crime.  惩罚与罪行不成比例。| 近义词:incommensurate, excessive, inordinate | 反义词: proportionate, commensurate | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From dis- + proportionate, from Latin proportionatus, past participle of proportionare "to proportion," from proportio "proportion, analogy, comparative relation."
