outkills:/ˌaʊtˈkɪlz/
|v.|超过…的杀敌数|Surpasses in the number of kills (usually in a video game or competitive context).|例句：She outkilled everyone on her team in the last match. 她在上一场比赛中比她队里的每个人都杀敌多。

例句：He consistently outkills his opponents, making him a valuable asset to the team. 他总是比对手杀敌多，这使他成为团队的宝贵资产。|近义词：outperforms, dominates, surpasses|反义词：loses to, is defeated by|词性:动词
