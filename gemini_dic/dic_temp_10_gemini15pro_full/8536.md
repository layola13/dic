anticeremonialism:/ˌæntiˌserɪˈməʊniəlɪzəm/
n.
反对礼仪主义，反仪式主义
Opposition to or avoidance of ceremony or ritual, especially in religion.
His anticeremonialism extended to refusing to wear a tie at his wedding. 他对繁文缛节的反对甚至延伸到拒绝在婚礼上打领带。
The movement embraced anticeremonialism, preferring simple gatherings to elaborate rituals.  这场运动拥护反仪式主义，喜欢简单的聚会而不是精心设计的仪式。
近义词:informality, casualness, simplicity, iconoclasm
反义词:ceremonialism, ritualism, formalism
词性:名词
