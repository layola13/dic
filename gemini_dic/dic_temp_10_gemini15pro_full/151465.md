parousia:/pəˈruːziə/ /ˌpɑːˈruːʒə/
noun
降临，出现，(尤指基督的)再临
The Second Coming of Christ, especially as a sudden, dramatic event.  The term is often used in Christian theology to refer to the return of Jesus Christ at the end of time.
The early Christians eagerly awaited the parousia. 早期基督徒热切地等待着基督的降临。
The preacher spoke of the imminent parousia. 传教士谈到了即将到来的基督再临。
Synonyms: advent, arrival, appearance, coming
Antonyms: departure, going, leaving
词性: 名词
