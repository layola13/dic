out-streams:/ˈaʊtˌstriːmz/
n.
输出流
A sequence of data elements made available over time by a source.
Data out-streams from the sensor to the central processing unit. 数据从传感器输出到中央处理器。
近义词:outputs, effluence, outflow
反义词:in-streams, inflow
词性:名词


out-streams:/ˈaʊtˌstriːmz/
v.
流出;涌出
To flow or issue outward. (Usually used in the third person singular form)
Information out-streams from the website to users around the world. 信息从网站流向世界各地的用户。
近义词:emanate, pour, discharge
反义词:inflow, receive
词性:动词
