coquilla:/koʊˈkiːjə/ (US), /kɒˈkiːjə/ (UK)| n.| 贝壳; 贝壳状物; 海贝壳做的饰品|A small, often brightly colored shell, especially one used for decoration. |She collected coquillas on the beach. 她在海滩上收集贝壳。|Seashell, shell, conch| N/A| noun
Etymology: Spanish *coquilla*, diminutive of *concha* "shell," from Latin *concha*.
