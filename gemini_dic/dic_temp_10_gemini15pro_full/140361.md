oil-bearing:/ˈɔɪlˌberɪŋ/
adjective
含油的；产油的
Containing or yielding oil.
例句：Oil-bearing rocks are a valuable resource. 含油岩石是一种宝贵的资源。

例句：The company is exploring oil-bearing formations in the region.  这家公司正在勘探该地区的含油地层。

近义词：oleiferous, petroliferous
反义词：barren, infertile
词性：adjective
