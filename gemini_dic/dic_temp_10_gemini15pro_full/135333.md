nonpenal:/nɒnˈpiːn(ə)l/
adjective
非刑罚的
Not involving or subject to punishment by law.
This is a nonpenal approach to drug addiction.  这是一种非刑罚的药物成瘾治疗方法。
The committee recommended nonpenal measures for first-time offenders. 委员会建议对初犯者采取非刑罚措施。
近义词:nonpunitive, civil, remedial
反义词:penal, punitive, criminal
词性:adjective
