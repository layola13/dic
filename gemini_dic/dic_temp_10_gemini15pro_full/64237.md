equinumerant:/ˌiːkwɪˈnjuːmərənt/ | adj. | 等数的，数量相等的 | Having the same number of elements.  | The two sets are equinumerant, each containing five elements. 这两个集合等数，每个集合包含五个元素。 |  The two groups were equinumerant, both consisting of ten members.  这两个组人数相等，都由十名成员组成。 | 近义词: equal in number, equivalent, commensurate | 反义词: unequal in number, disparate | 词性: adjective

equinumerant:/ˌiːkwɪˈnjuːmərənt/ | n. | 等数之物 | Something that has the same number of elements as something else. |  The two sets are equinumerants, each containing five elements.  这两个集合是等数之物，每个集合包含五个元素。|  Finding an equinumerant for this complex equation was a challenge. 为这个复杂的方程式找到一个等数之物是一项挑战。| 近义词: equivalent | 反义词: none | 词性: noun

词源：
From equi- + numerant.  Equi- means "equal," and -numerant relates to number.  Therefore, equinumerant means "equal in number."  来源于 equi- + numerant。Equi- 表示“相等”，-numerant 与数字有关。因此，equinumerant 的意思是“数量相等”。
