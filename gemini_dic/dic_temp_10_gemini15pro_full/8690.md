anticosmetics:/ˌæntɪkɒzˈmetɪks/ | n. | 反化妆品；抵制化妆品 | Products or practices that oppose or reject the traditional ideals of cosmetics, often focusing on natural appearance or challenging beauty standards. |  Example: She embraced the anticosmetics movement and stopped wearing makeup altogether. 她接受了反化妆品运动，完全停止了化妆。

Example: The blogger's anticosmetics stance resonated with many who felt pressured by societal beauty norms.  这位博主的反化妆品立场引起了许多感受到社会审美规范压力的人的共鸣。 | 近义词: natural look, unadorned,  | 反义词: cosmetics, makeup | 词性: 名词

Etymology: anti- (prefix meaning against) + cosmetics
