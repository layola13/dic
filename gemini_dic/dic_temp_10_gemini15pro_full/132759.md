nondomineering: /ˌnɒnˌdɒmɪˈnɪərɪŋ/ | adj. | 非支配性的，不专横的 | Not asserting one's will over another; not domineering. |  She has a nondomineering personality, which makes her easy to work with. 她性格不专横，很容易共事。|  He adopted a nondomineering approach to parenting, allowing his children to make their own choices. 他采取了一种非支配性的育儿方式，允许孩子们自己做选择。 | Synonyms:  unassuming, modest, humble, gentle, meek | Antonyms: domineering, overbearing, authoritarian, controlling, tyrannical | adjective
