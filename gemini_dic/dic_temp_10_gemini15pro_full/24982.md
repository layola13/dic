brut:/bruːt/| adj. n.|天然的，未加工的；香槟酒；暴徒|Raw, unrefined, especially of champagne; a thug or ruffian. |The restaurant has a fantastic selection of brut champagne.|近义词:raw, unrefined, dry, unsweetened, natural, coarse, harsh, savage, cruel, brutal|反义词:refined, sweet, cultured, civilized, gentle, kind|词性:adjective, noun

Etymology: From French *brut*, meaning "raw, unprocessed," originally from Latin *brutus*, meaning "heavy, dull, stupid, senseless."
