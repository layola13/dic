non-predicative:/nɒnˈprɛdɪkətɪv/| adj.|非谓语的|Not functioning as a predicate; specifically (of an adjective) attributing a quality to a noun without asserting it, as *old* in *an old friend*.|例句：The adjective "red" in "a red car" is used predicatively, while "red" in "the big red ball" is non-predicative.  红色在“一辆红色的车”中作谓语，而在“那个大的红球”中是非谓语的。

例句：In "My friend is old," "old" is predicative. In "My old friend came to visit," "old" is non-predicative. 在“我的朋友老了”中，“老”是谓语。在“我的老朋友来拜访”中，“老”是非谓语的。|近义词：attributive, modifying|反义词：predicative|词性:adjective

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The non-predicative adjective modifies the noun directly.  非谓语形容词直接修饰名词。
He is a good boy. 他是一个好男孩。

A big red ball bounced down the street. 一个大的红球沿着街道弹跳。
The little girl laughed. 小女孩笑了。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The non-predicative adjective describes the noun that is performing the action. 非谓语形容词描述正在执行动作的名词。

The blooming flowers are beautiful. 盛开的花朵很美丽。

The spilled milk is a mess. 洒出来的牛奶弄得一团糟。
