byo:/ˈbaɪoʊ/
abbreviation|自带酒水 (Bring Your Own)|BYO is an abbreviation for "Bring Your Own," typically referring to alcoholic beverages.  It indicates that an establishment, such as a restaurant, allows patrons to bring their own alcohol, often for a corkage fee.|This restaurant is BYO, so we can bring our favourite wine.|近义词:None (as it's an abbreviation)|反义词:None (as it's an abbreviation)|abbr.
