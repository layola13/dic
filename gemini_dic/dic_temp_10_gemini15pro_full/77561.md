gear-up:/ˈɡɪər ʌp/| phrasal verb |准备；装备；促进；增加|To prepare for something; to get ready for something; to increase the level or amount of something. |The company is gearing up for a major expansion. 公司正在为大规模扩张做准备。|Synonyms: prepare, ready, equip, ramp up|Antonyms: wind down, scale back|verb phrase


gear-up:/ˈɡɪər ʌp/| n. |装备, 准备工作 (较少用作名词) |The act of preparing or equipping. |The gear-up for the expedition took several months. 远征的准备工作花了几个月的时间。(较少用)|Synonyms: preparation, equipment|Antonyms: dismantling, disbanding|noun
