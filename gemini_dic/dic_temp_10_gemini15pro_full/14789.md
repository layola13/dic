awol:/ˈeɪˌwɒl/ (US) /ˈeɪˌwɔːl/ (UK)| adj. n.| 擅离职守的；逃兵；擅离职守者；逃兵 | Absent Without Official Leave (or Leave, in US English).  Being away from one's military duties without permission. Can also be used figuratively for anyone absent from their expected place. | The soldier went AWOL after learning of his mother's illness.  这名士兵在得知母亲生病后擅离职守。|Synonyms: absent, deserted, truant, missing | Antonyms: present, accounted for, on duty | adjective, noun

awols:/ˈeɪˌwɒlz/ (US) /ˈeɪˌwɔːlz/ (UK) | n. | 擅离职守者；逃兵（复数）| Plural form of AWOL, referring to multiple people absent without official leave. | The army was searching for the awols who had deserted their posts. 军队正在搜寻那些擅离职守的逃兵。| Synonyms: absentees, deserters, truants | Antonyms: attendees, presents | noun
