entombed:/ɪnˈtuːmd/ | v. |埋葬，安葬；使陷入 | To place in or as if in a tomb; bury.  To enclose or confine as if in a tomb. | The ancient king was entombed in a pyramid. 古代国王被安葬在一座金字塔里。 |  The miners were entombed alive after the cave-in. 矿工们在山洞塌方后被活埋了。| 近义词: bury, inter, inhume, immure | 反义词: exhume, unearth |词性: 动词

entombed:/ɪnˈtuːmd/ | adj. |被埋葬的，被掩埋的 | Placed in or as if in a tomb; buried. |  His hopes and dreams were entombed by the harsh realities of life. 他的希望和梦想被残酷的现实埋葬了。| 近义词: buried, interred | 反义词: unearthed, exhumed |词性: 形容词
