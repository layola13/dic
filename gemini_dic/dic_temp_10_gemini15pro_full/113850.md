madeleines:/ˌmædˈleɪnz/ | n. |玛德琳蛋糕 |Small, shell-shaped sponge cakes, often flavored with lemon zest and made with ground almonds.  They are typically baked in a special mold that gives them their distinctive shape.  These cakes are particularly associated with Marcel Proust's novel "In Search of Lost Time," where the taste of a madeleine dipped in tea evokes a flood of childhood memories. | Example:  She baked a batch of delicate madeleines for the afternoon tea. 她为下午茶烤了一批精致的玛德琳蛋糕。

Example: The madeleines, fresh from the oven, filled the kitchen with a warm, citrusy aroma.  刚出炉的玛德琳蛋糕，让厨房充满了温暖的柑橘香味。| 近义词:  petit fours, sponge cakes | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it is a specific type of cake) | 词性: noun
