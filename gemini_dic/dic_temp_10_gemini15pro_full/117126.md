meanderingly:/mɪˈændərɪŋli/
adverb
漫无目的地；曲折地；蜿蜒地
in a meandering manner; windingly.  Moving in a slow, indirect, and rambling way.
The river flowed meanderingly through the valley. 这条河蜿蜒流过山谷。
Synonyms: windingly, circuitously, tortuously, indirectly
Antonyms: directly, straightly, purposefully
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From meander (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Meander" comes from the Greek "Maiandros," the name of a winding river in Phrygia (now Turkey).
