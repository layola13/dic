clinal:/ˈklaɪnəl/| adj.|渐变的，倾斜的|Relating to or characterized by a cline, a gradual change in a character or feature across a geographic area.|The clinal variation in the plant's size is noticeable as you move from the coast to the mountains.  这种植物的大小随着你从海岸到山区的移动而呈现出渐变的变化。|Near synonyms:  gradual, graded, continuous|Near antonyms: abrupt, discontinuous, stepped|adjective

clinal:/ˈklaɪnəl/| n.|渐变群|A series of biotypes that show continuous variation, especially with regard to phenotype, along an environmental gradient.|The clinal of this species shows a gradual color change from north to south. 这个物种的渐变群显示出从北到南逐渐的颜色变化。|Near synonyms: cline, gradation|Near antonyms: - |noun
