offloaders:/ˌɒfˈləʊdəz/ | n. |卸货机；卸货员；卸载程序|People or machines that unload something, especially cargo from a ship or truck.  It can also refer to software that moves data or processing tasks from one system to another to reduce load. |The offloaders worked quickly to get the cargo off the ship before the storm. 卸货员们在暴风雨来临之前迅速地将货物从船上卸下。

The company invested in automated offloaders to increase efficiency. 公司投资了自动化卸货机以提高效率。|Synonyms: unloaders, dischargers | Antonyms: loaders | Noun
