harmonised:/ˈhɑːmənaɪzd/ | adj. | 和谐的，协调的，统一的 | Brought into agreement or made compatible; made consistent or uniform. | The regulations were harmonised across the European Union. 这些法规在整个欧盟范围内得到了统一。|  The different parts of the machine are perfectly harmonised.  这台机器的不同部件完美协调。| 近义词: coordinated, unified, standardized, consistent, aligned, integrated | 反义词: discordant, conflicting, disparate, divergent, incompatible | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *harmoniser*, from *harmonie* ("harmony"), from Late Latin *harmonia*, from Ancient Greek *ἁρμονία* (*harmonía*, "joint, agreement, concord of sounds").
