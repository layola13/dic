formulating:/ˈfɔːrmjəleɪtɪŋ/ |v.|规划；明确表达；构想|The committee is formulating a new policy. 委员会正在制定一项新政策。|Synonyms: devising, planning, developing, composing, framing|Antonyms: dismantling, disorganizing, neglecting|verb

formulate:/ˈfɔːrmjəleɪt/ |v.|规划；明确表达；构想|She formulated a plan to increase sales. 她制定了一个提高销售额的计划。|Synonyms: devise, plan, develop, compose, frame|Antonyms: dismantle, disorganize, neglect|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin formulat-, past participle stem of formulare "to reduce to a formula," from Latin formula "formula, rule, method, pattern"  + -ate.
