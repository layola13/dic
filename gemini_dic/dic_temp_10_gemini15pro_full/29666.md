catharsis:/kəˈθɑːrsɪs/
n.
情感宣泄，精神净化
the process of releasing, and thereby providing relief from, strong or repressed emotions.
She experienced a catharsis after writing about her traumatic childhood. 她写下自己童年时期遭受的创伤后，经历了一次情感宣泄。
近义词：release, purgation, cleansing
反义词：repression, suppression
词性：名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin catharsis, from Ancient Greek κάθαρσις (kátharsis, “purification, cleansing”), from καθαίρω (kathairō, “I cleanse, purge”).
