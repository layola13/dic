magnetizer:/ˈmæɡnɪˌtaɪzər/ | n. |磁化器，磁化者|A person or device that magnetizes something.  A device or substance that imparts magnetic properties. |The magnetizer created a strong magnetic field. 磁化器制造了一个强磁场。|
The technician used a magnetizer to magnetize the screwdriver.  技术员用磁化器将螺丝刀磁化。|近义词:magnetizing force,  magnet |反义词:demagnetizer |词性:noun
