adenosclerosis: /əˌdiːnoʊskləˈroʊsɪs/ | n. |腺硬化；腺体硬化| Hardening of a gland, especially a lymphatic gland. | Adenosclerosis can sometimes be a symptom of underlying infection. 腺硬化有时可能是潜在感染的症状。

The doctor discovered adenosclerosis in the patient's lymph nodes. 医生在病人的淋巴结中发现了腺体硬化。| 近义词: sclerosis, induration | 反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) softening, atrophy | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From adeno- + -sclerosis.  "Adeno-" comes from Greek *adḗn (ἀδήν), meaning "gland". "-sclerosis" comes from Greek sklērós (σκληρός), meaning "hard".
