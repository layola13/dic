coverlets: /ˈkʌvərlets/ | n. | 床罩，被单 | A lightweight, usually decorative bedspread.  |  She spread the colorful coverlets over the beds. 她把五颜六色的床罩铺在床上。 |  Synonyms: bedspread, quilt, comforter | Antonyms: sheet | noun

coverlets: /ˈkʌvərlets/ | n. | (古语) 遮盖物 | Something that covers or conceals. |  The mist served as a coverlet for the approaching army.  薄雾成了逼近的军队的遮盖物。| Synonyms: covering, blanket | Antonyms: exposure | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *covrelit (Modern French couvre-lit), from covrir "to cover" (from Latin cooperire) + lit "bed" (from Latin lectus).
