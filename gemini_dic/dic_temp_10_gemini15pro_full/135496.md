nonphotobiotic:/ˌnɒnˌfəʊtəʊbaɪˈɒtɪk/
adjective
非光合生物的;非光养的
Not obtaining energy from light.  Relating to organisms that do not obtain energy from sunlight.
Deep-sea hydrothermal vents support thriving communities of nonphotobiotic organisms. 深海热液喷口支持着繁荣的非光合生物群落。
Nonphotobiotic bacteria play a crucial role in nutrient cycling in the deep ocean. 非光养细菌在深海营养循环中起着至关重要的作用。
