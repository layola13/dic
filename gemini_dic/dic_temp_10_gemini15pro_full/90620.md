humanics:/hjuːˈmænɪks/
n.
人文学科；人文科学；以人为本的学科
The study of the humanities; subjects concerned with human culture, especially literature, philosophy, art and history.
The university offers a diverse range of courses in the humanics. 这所大学提供各种各样的人文学科课程。
Humanics emphasizes the importance of critical thinking and creativity. 人文学科强调批判性思维和创造力的重要性。
近义词: humanities, liberal arts
反义词: sciences, technology
词性: 名词
