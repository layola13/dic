gamebred:/ˈɡeɪmbred/| adj.| 斗志昂扬的，好斗的，为比赛而生的 |Bred or trained for hunting or fighting; having a spirited, combative nature.  |He was a gamebred fighter, never backing down from a challenge. 他是一个斗志昂扬的战士，从不畏惧挑战。|近义词：spirited, plucky, courageous, feisty |反义词: cowardly, timid, fearful |词性:形容词
词源: game (n.) + bred (adj.).  "Game" in this context refers to animals hunted for sport, and "bred" means raised or trained.  Thus, "gamebred" originally described animals bred for hunting.  The meaning later extended to people with a similar fighting spirit.
