hyperpromiscuousness:/ˌhaɪpərˌprɑːmɪˈskjuːɪsnəs/ | n. |过度滥交，极度乱交|The state of engaging in sexual activity with a large number of partners with little or no emotional connection.  It often implies a compulsive or excessive pattern of behavior. | His hyperpromiscuousness led to a number of health concerns. 他的过度滥交导致了许多健康问题。|  Excessive promiscuity, nymphomania (for women), satyriasis (for men)| Monogamy, fidelity, chastity| noun
