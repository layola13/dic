instigating:/ˈɪnstɪˌɡeɪtɪŋ/| adj. |煽动的，教唆的 | Encouraging or urging (someone) to do something, especially something bad. |  例句：He was accused of instigating the riot. 他被指控煽动暴乱。| 近义词：inciting, provoking, abetting | 反义詞：deterring, preventing, discouraging | 词性:形容词
instigate:/ˈɪnstɪɡeɪt/| vt. |煽动，教唆 | Bring about or initiate (an action or event). | 例句：They instigated a rebellion against the government. 他们煽动了一场反政府叛乱。| 近义词：incite, provoke, foment | 反义词：prevent, deter, suppress | 词性:动词

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin instigat- ‘urged, incited’, from the verb instigare, from in- ‘in, on’ + stigare ‘prick, goad’.
