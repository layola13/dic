nonconcentricity:/ˌnɒnkɒnsənˈtrɪsɪti/ | n. | 非同心度，偏心率 | The state or quality of not having the same center; the amount or degree by which objects deviate from having the same center. | The nonconcentricity of the two circles caused a vibration. 两个圆的非同心度导致了振动。 |  eccentricity, off-center, misalignment | concentricity, alignment | noun

nonconcentricities:/ˌnɒnkɒnsənˈtrɪsɪtiz/ | n. | 非同心度（复数）|  Plural form of nonconcentricity, referring to multiple instances of not having the same center. | The engineer measured the nonconcentricities of several components. 工程师测量了几个部件的非同心度。| eccentricities, off-centers, misalignments | concentricity, alignment | noun
