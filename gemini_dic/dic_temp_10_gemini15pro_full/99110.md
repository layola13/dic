intercartilaginous:/ˌɪntərˌkɑːrtɪˈlædʒɪnəs/
adjective
软骨间的
Located or occurring between cartilages.
The intercartilaginous ligament connects the costal cartilages.  肋间软骨韧带连接肋软骨。
The surgeon repaired the intercartilaginous tear in the patient's rib cage.  外科医生修复了病人胸腔的软骨间撕裂。
近义词:interchondral
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "intracartilaginous" if referring to within the cartilage itself)
词性: adjective
