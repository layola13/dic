overarranging: /ˌoʊvərəˈreɪndʒɪŋ/
overarranging: v. |过度安排，安排得过分|The act of arranging something excessively, often to the point of being fussy or unnatural. |She was criticized for overarranging the flowers, making the bouquet look stiff and artificial. 她因为把花安排得过分而受到批评，让花束看起来僵硬且不自然。|近义词: overorganizing, overdoing|反义词: underarranging, simplifying|词性: 动词
