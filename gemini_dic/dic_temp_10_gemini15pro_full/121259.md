misevaluate: /ˌmɪsɪˈvæljuˌeɪt/
verb
错误地评估；估错
To assess or estimate something incorrectly.
He misevaluated the cost of the project, and it went way over budget. 他错误地估计了项目的成本，结果大大超出了预算。
misjudge, underestimate, overestimate
correctly evaluate, accurately assess
v.
