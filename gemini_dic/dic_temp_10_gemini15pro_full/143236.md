outdazzle: /ˌaʊtˈdæzəl/
verb
使黯然失色；胜过；使相形见绌
to surpass in dazzling; outshine.
The fireworks display outdazzled all previous celebrations. 这场烟花表演使所有之前的庆祝活动都黯然失色。
Synonyms: outshine, eclipse, overshadow
Antonyms: pale in comparison, be overshadowed by
词性: 动词
