nonsaner:/nɒnˈseɪnə(r)/ | n. |  胡说八道的人；荒谬的人 | A person who talks nonsense or behaves absurdly. |  He's a complete nonsaner, always spouting ridiculous theories. 他完全是个胡说八道的人，总是滔滔不绝地说着荒谬的理论。 | synonyms: fool, idiot, lunatic, madman, crackpot | antonyms: sage, wise person, genius | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "nonsense" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person or thing associated with the base word). "Nonsense" comes from "non-" (not) + "sense" (meaning or understanding).
