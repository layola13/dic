bitternut:/ˈbɪtərˌnʌt/ | n. |美洲苦 hickory; 白胡桃木; 美洲山核桃树的一种|A type of hickory tree (Carya cordiformis) native to eastern North America, known for its bitter nuts and strong, durable wood.  Also refers to the nut itself.| The squirrels avoided the bitternuts because of their bitter taste. 松鼠因为苦味而避开美洲苦 hickory 果。| synonyms: swamp hickory, pignut hickory | antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific tree species) | noun
