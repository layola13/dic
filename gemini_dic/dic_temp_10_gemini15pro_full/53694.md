disciplining: /ˈdɪsəplɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 训练；惩戒 | The act of training someone to obey rules or a code of behavior, using punishment to correct disobedience. |  The parents are disciplining their child for misbehaving. 父母正在因为孩子不听话而管教他。 | Synonyms: training, correcting, punishing, chastising | Antonyms: praising, rewarding, indulging | Verb

disciplining: /ˈdɪsəplɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 惩戒性的 | Having the quality of or relating to discipline. | Disciplining actions are necessary sometimes. 有时惩戒性措施是必要的。| Synonyms: corrective, punitive | Antonyms: rewarding, encouraging | Adjective
