neurotoxin:/ˈnjʊərəʊˌtɒksɪn/ /ˈnʊroʊˌtɑːksɪn/| n. |神经毒素|A poisonous substance that acts on the nervous system.|Example 1: The neurotoxin in the venom of the spider paralyzed its prey.  蜘蛛毒液中的神经毒素使其猎物瘫痪了。

Example 2:  Exposure to certain neurotoxins can cause permanent neurological damage. 接触某些神经毒素会导致永久性神经损伤。|近义词：nerve poison, nerve agent|反义词: neurotrophic factor (promotes nerve growth, not a true antonym but represents an opposing function)|词性: noun
