antiecclesiastics:/ˌæntiɪˌkliːziˈæstɪks/

antiecclesiastical:/ˌæntiɪˌkliːziˈæstɪkəl/
| adj. | 反教会的；反对教会的 | Opposed to the power or influence of the church or clergy. |  His antiecclesiastical views made him unpopular with the religious community. 他的反教会观点使他在宗教界不受欢迎。 | Synonyms: secular, irreligious, profane | Antonyms: religious, pious, devout | Adjective
