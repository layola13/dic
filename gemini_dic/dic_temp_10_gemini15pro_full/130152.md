/nɑnˈreləvənt/ | adj. | 不相关的，不重要的 | Not relevant or pertinent; not applicable or germane to a particular subject or matter. |  The lawyer objected to the witness's testimony, arguing that it was non-relevant to the case. 律师反对证人的证词，认为这与案件无关。 |  The color of his shirt is non-relevant to his qualifications for the job.  他的衬衫颜色与他的工作资格无关。 |  applicable, pertinent, relevant, germane, material | irrelevant, immaterial, extraneous | adjective

Etymology:
From non- + relevant.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "relevant" comes from the Latin word "relevare," meaning "to lift up, relieve, lighten," later developing the sense of "to be important or significant."
