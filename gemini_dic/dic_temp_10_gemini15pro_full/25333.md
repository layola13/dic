buhl:/buːl/ | n. |镶嵌细工；用金属、龟甲、珍珠母等材料镶嵌的装饰品 |Inlaid work; ornamentation of inlaid metal, tortoiseshell, mother-of-pearl, etc. | The antique table featured intricate buhl work. 这张古董桌子以复杂的镶嵌细工为特色。|近义词: inlay, marquetry |反义词: None | Noun

词源: From André-Charles Boulle (1642–1732), French cabinetmaker. 源自法国细木工匠安德烈-查尔斯·布尔(André-Charles Boulle，1642-1732)的名字。
