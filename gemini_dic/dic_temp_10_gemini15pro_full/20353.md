bismuthinite:/ˈbɪzməθɪnaɪt/| n. |辉铋矿|A lead-gray mineral, Bi2S3, the principal ore of bismuth.  It occurs as acicular orthorhombic crystals or massive.|Bismuthinite is a common ore found in Bolivia. 辉铋矿是在玻利维亚发现的一种常见矿石。|Synonyms: bismuth glance, bismuth sulfide|Antonyms: None|Noun
