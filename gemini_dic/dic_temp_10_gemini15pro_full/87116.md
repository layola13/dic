herbaged:/ˈhɜːrbɪdʒd/
adjective
草本植物覆盖的；长满草的
covered with herbage; grassy
The hills were herbaged and dotted with wildflowers.  这些小山丘上长满了草，点缀着野花。
Near synonyms: grassy, verdant, leafy
Antonym: barren
词性: adjective
