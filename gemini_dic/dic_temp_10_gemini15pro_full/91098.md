hyacinth:/ˈhaɪəsɪnθ/ | n. |风信子；紫蓝色|A bulbous plant of the lily family, with straplike leaves and a fragrant spike of bell-shaped flowers, native to the eastern Mediterranean.| The hyacinths in the garden are blooming beautifully. 花园里的风信子开得很漂亮。| synonyms:bluebell, grape hyacinth | antonyms: N/A | noun

hyacinth:/ˈhaɪəsɪnθ/ | adj. |紫蓝色的；风信子颜色的|Of a purplish blue colour.| She wore a hyacinth dress to the party. 她穿着一件紫蓝色的连衣裙去参加聚会。| synonyms:purplish blue, lavender | antonyms: N/A | adjective
