funding:/ˈfʌndɪŋ/| n.| 资金；基金；经费；提供资金|The project received funding from the government.  这个项目得到了政府的资助。| synonyms: finance, backing, support, grant, subsidy | antonyms: defunding, divestment | noun

Etymology:  From the verb "fund," which comes from the Old French "fond" meaning "bottom, foundation."  The meaning shifted to "a stock or supply of something" and then to "money available for a specific purpose."
