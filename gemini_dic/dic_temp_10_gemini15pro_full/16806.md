baseless:/ˈbeɪslɪs/| adj.|无根据的，无基础的|Without a basis in reason or fact; unfounded.|例句：The accusations were completely baseless. 这些指控完全没有根据。|例句：Her fears proved to be baseless. 她的担心被证明是毫无根据的。|近义词：groundless, unfounded, unsubstantiated, unsupported|反义词：grounded, founded, substantiated, well-founded|词性:形容词
Etymology: From base (foundation) + -less (without).
