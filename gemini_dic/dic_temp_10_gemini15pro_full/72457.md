flummery:/ˈflʌməri/ | n. | 糊状甜食；空洞的恭维；废话 | a sweet, soft food made with flour, milk, eggs, etc.; empty compliments; meaningless talk |  She served a delicious flummery for dessert. 她餐后甜点上了一道美味的糊状甜食。| synonyms: nonsense, balderdash, hogwash,  empty talk, sweet talk | antonyms: sense, truth, facts | noun
