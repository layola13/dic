nonreserves:/ˌnɒnriˈzɜːrvz/ | n. | 非储备 | Assets or resources that are not held in reserve or are not considered part of a reserve.  Specifically, in banking, this often refers to assets not counted towards reserve requirements. | The bank's nonreserves were invested in high-yield bonds.  这家银行的非储备资产投资于高收益债券。 | The company's nonreserves included real estate and equipment.  公司的非储备资产包括房地产和设备。 | 近义词: non-reserve assets | 反义词: reserves | 词性: 名词
