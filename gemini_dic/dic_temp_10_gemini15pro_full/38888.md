conferree:/ˌkɑːnfəˈriː/
n.
与会者；参加会议的人
A person who participates in a conference or meeting, especially one who is conferred with.
The conferees discussed the terms of the agreement.  与会者讨论了协议的条款。
The conferree arrived late to the meeting.  这位与会者开会迟到了。
synonyms: participant, attendee, delegate, representative
antonyms: absentee, nonparticipant
noun
