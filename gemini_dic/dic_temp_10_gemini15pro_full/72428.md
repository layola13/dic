fluidal:/ˈfluːɪdəl/| adj.|流体的，流动的|Relating to or characteristic of a fluid; fluid-like.  Able to flow easily.|The paint had a fluidal consistency, spreading smoothly across the canvas.|近义词：liquid, flowing, runny|反义词:viscous, solid, rigid|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Latin "fluidus" (flowing) + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives).
