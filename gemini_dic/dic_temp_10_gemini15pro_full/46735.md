dandiprat:/ˈdændɪpræt/| n.|小家伙，矮子，小不点|A small, insignificant, or contemptible person, often a child.  A short person; a dwarf.  Someone considered to be unimportant or insignificant.|例句：The dandiprat tried to reach the cookie jar on the top shelf.  那个小家伙试图够到最上层架子上的饼干罐。

例句：Don't listen to that dandiprat, he doesn't know what he's talking about.  别听那个小不点胡说八道，他根本不知道自己在说什么。|近义词：shrimp, runt, midget, dwarf, pipsqueak|反义词:giant|词性:noun
