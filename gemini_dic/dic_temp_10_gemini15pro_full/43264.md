craft:/kræft/ | n. | 手艺，工艺；技巧；飞机，飞船 | Skill in making things, especially with the hands; an activity involving skill in making things by hand.  A vehicle designed for navigation in or on water or air or through outer space. |  The potter displayed her craft at the local market.  这位陶艺家在当地市场展示了她的手艺。

The aircraft landed safely despite the storm.  尽管暴风雨，飞机还是安全着陆了。| 近义词：skill, art, trade, vessel, ship, airplane | 反义词：incompetence, clumsiness | 词性: 名词

craft:/krɑːft/ | vt. | 精巧地制作 | To make or manufacture (an object) with skill and careful attention to detail. | He carefully crafted a small wooden boat. 他精心制作了一艘小木船。

She crafted a compelling argument for her proposal. 她为她的提议精心设计了一个令人信服的论点。| 近义词：make, create, fashion, construct | 反义词：destroy, ruin | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old English cræft "strength, skill, power," from Proto-Germanic *kraftiz (source also of Old Frisian kreft "strength," Middle Dutch cracht, Dutch kracht "force, power," Old High German chraft, German Kraft "strength, power").
