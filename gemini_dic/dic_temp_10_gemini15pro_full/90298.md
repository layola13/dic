housemen's:/ˈhaʊsmənz/
n.
住院医师们的；实习医生们的
Belonging to or associated with housemen (junior doctors in a hospital, typically undergoing residency training).  This word refers to something owned by or relating to them.
The housemen's quarters were cramped and basic.  住院医师们的住处狭小简陋。
近义词: interns', residents', junior doctors'
反义词: attending physicians', consultants'
词性:  possessive noun (名词所有格)
