co-op:/ˈkoʊˌɑːp/ (US), /ˈkəʊɒp/ (UK)| n.|合作社，合作商店；（大学）带薪实习项目|A business or organization owned and run jointly by its members, who share the profits.|例句：She buys all her groceries at the local co-op. 她所有的食品杂货都在当地的合作社购买。

|例句：He got a valuable co-op placement with a software company. 他在一家软件公司获得了一个宝贵的带薪实习机会。|近义词：cooperative, collective|反义词：corporation, conglomerate|词性:名词
