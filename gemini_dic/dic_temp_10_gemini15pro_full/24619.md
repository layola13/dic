broke off:/broʊk ɔːf/
verb
中断，突然停止，断绝，解除
The two countries broke off diplomatic relations. 这两个国家断绝了外交关系。
He broke off in the middle of a sentence. 他一句话说到一半突然停住了。
近义词：ceased, discontinued, ended, halted, interrupted, severed, stopped
反义词：began, commenced, continued, initiated, resumed, started
词性：verb

broke off:/broʊk ɔːf/
adjective
断裂的，破碎的
The handle of the cup is broke off. 杯子的把手断了。
A broke-off piece of the rock fell into the ravine. 一块断裂的岩石掉进了峡谷。
近义词：broken, detached, fractured, separated, severed
反义词：attached, connected, joined, whole
词性：adjective


词源：From the phrasal verb "break off," where "break" traces back to Old English "brecan" and "off" to Old English "of."
