harmonize:/ˈhɑːrmənaɪz/ /ˈhɑːrmonaɪz/| v. vi. vt.|使和谐，协调，使一致|To bring into or be in accord or agreement; make harmonious or consistent.|The colors in the painting harmonize beautifully. 这幅画中的色彩非常和谐。|The government's policy harmonizes with the needs of the people. 政府的政策与人民的需求相一致。|近义词：accord, agree, blend, coordinate, match|反义词：clash, conflict, disagree, jar|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *harmonizāre, from Ancient Greek ἁρμονίζειν (harmonízein, “to fit together”), from ἁρμονία (harmonía, “harmony”).
