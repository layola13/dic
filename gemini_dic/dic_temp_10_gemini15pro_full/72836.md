foliolate:/ˈfoʊliəˌleɪt/ | v. |  (使)分成小叶 | To divide into leaflets. | The botanist carefully foliated the dried specimen for examination.  植物学家仔细地将干燥的标本分成小叶进行检查。| synonyms: divide, separate, dissect | antonyms: combine, join | verb
