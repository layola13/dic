edingtonite:/ˈɛdɪŋtəˌnaɪt/ | n. |埃丁顿沸石 |A rare zeolite mineral, a hydrated barium aluminum silicate.  It is typically colorless to white, and forms prismatic crystals. | Edingtonite was first discovered in the Kilpatrick Hills, Scotland. 埃丁顿沸石最早发现于苏格兰的基尔帕特里克山。

Edingtonite crystals are often found in cavities within basalt. 埃丁顿沸石晶体经常在玄武岩的空腔中被发现。 | 近义词:zeolite（沸石类矿物） | 反义词: N/A | 词性:noun
