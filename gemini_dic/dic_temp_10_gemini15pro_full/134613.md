nonluminousnesses:/ˌnɒnlʊmɪˈnɒsnɪsɪz/
n.
无光性；不发光；无光泽
lack of luminosity; the quality of not emitting light.
The nonluminousnesses of the moon is due to its reflection of sunlight. 月球的无光性是由于它反射了太阳光。
Near synonyms: dullness, opacity, darkness
Antonyms: luminosity, brightness, radiance
词性: noun
