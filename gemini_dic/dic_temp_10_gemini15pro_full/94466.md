imponderabilities:/ɪmˌpɒndərəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 不可预测性；无法衡量的事物；未知因素 |  Factors that are difficult or impossible to assess or measure with certainty. These are often abstract concepts or unpredictable events. |  The success of the project depends on a number of imponderables, such as the weather and the political climate. 这个项目的成功取决于许多不可预测的因素，例如天气和政治气候。| synonyms: uncertainties, unknowns, variables | antonyms: certainties, knowns, constants | noun

imponderable:/ɪmˈpɒndərəbl/ | adj. | 不可估量的；无法衡量的 |  Impossible to estimate, assess, or measure precisely. | The imponderable effects of the new policy made it difficult to predict the outcome. 新政策无法估量的影响使得难以预测结果。 | synonyms: immeasurable, incalculable, unpredictable | antonyms: measurable, calculable, predictable | adjective
