ideologist:/ˌaɪdiˈɒlədʒɪst/ /ˌɪdiˈɑːlədʒɪst/| n. | 思想家；理论家；意识形态拥护者 | A person who strongly believes in a particular set of ideas, especially political ones.  |  He's a staunch ideologist who refuses to compromise. 他是一个坚定的理论家，拒绝妥协。

|  She's known as a feminist ideologist. 她以女权主义理论家而闻名。 | 近义词：theorist, doctrinaire, partisan | 反义词：pragmatist | 词性: noun
