loopholing:/ˈluːphəʊlɪŋ/
n.
漏洞利用，钻漏洞
The act of exploiting loopholes.
Tax loopholeing is a serious problem.  逃税漏税是一个严重的问题。
Exploitation of loopholes, finding loopholes.
Closing loopholes, strengthening regulations.
noun

loophole:/ˈluːphəʊl/
n.
漏洞，空子
An ambiguity or inadequacy in the law or a set of rules.
They found a loophole in the contract. 他们在合同中发现了一个漏洞。
Opening, gap, ambiguity.
Closure, strictness, clarity.
noun
