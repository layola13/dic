chorusing:/ˈkɔːrəsɪŋ/ | n. | 合唱；齐声；齐鸣 | The act of performing in a chorus; singing or speaking in unison. | The chorusing of frogs filled the night air. 青蛙的合唱声充满了夜空。|  近义词: unison, concert, harmony | 反义词: cacophony, discord | noun

chorusing:/ˈkɔːrəsɪŋ/ | adj. | 合唱的；齐声的 |  Relating to or resembling a chorus. | The chorusing voices of the children were beautiful. 孩子们齐声的歌声很美。| 近义词: unison, concerted, harmonious | 反义词: discordant, cacophonous | adjective
