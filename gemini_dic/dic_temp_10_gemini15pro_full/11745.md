aromatization:/əˌroʊmətaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 芳香化，芳构化 |The process of converting a non-aromatic compound into an aromatic one.  This often involves the formation of a ring structure with alternating single and double bonds.|The aromatization of cyclohexane to benzene requires a catalyst.|近义词: aromatisation, dehydrogenation|反义词: hydrogenation|词性: noun

Etymology: From aroma + -tization (suffix forming nouns of action or process).  Ultimately from Greek 'arōma' meaning 'spice'.
