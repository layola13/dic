disaccept:/ˌdɪsəkˈsɛpt/
verb
不接受，拒绝
To refuse to accept; to reject.
The committee disaccepted the proposal due to budget constraints. 委员会因预算限制拒绝了该提案。
The university disaccepted his application. 大学拒绝了他的申请。
近义词: reject, refuse, decline
反义词: accept, approve
词性: verb

词源: dis- (prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of") + accept
