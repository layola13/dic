excited:/ɪkˈsaɪtɪd/| adj.|兴奋的，激动的|Feeling or showing happiness and enthusiasm.|例句：I'm so excited about the trip! 我对这次旅行感到非常兴奋。|例句：The children were excited to open their presents. 孩子们对打开礼物感到很兴奋。|近义词：thrilled, enthusiastic, eager|反义词：calm, bored, apathetic|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin excitare "to rouse, stir up," frequentative of exciere "to call forth, summon," from ex- "out" + ciere "to set in motion."
