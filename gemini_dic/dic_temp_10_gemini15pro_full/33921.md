cirque:/sɜːrk/ (UK) /sɝːk/ (US)| n. |马戏，马戏团|A traveling company of acrobats, clowns, and other performers.|例句：The children were excited to see the cirque perform. 孩子们很兴奋能看到马戏团表演。

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cirque*, from Latin *circus* ("circle, ring"), from Greek *κίρκος* (*kirkos*) ("circle, ring").  The geological sense developed from the resemblance of the landform to a circular amphitheater.
