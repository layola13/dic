imposingly:/ɪmˈpoʊzɪŋli/
adverb
令人印象深刻地；威严地
in a way that is impressive because of large size, stately appearance, or dignity
He stood imposingly at the head of the table. 他威严地站在桌首。
Synonyms: grandly, impressively, majestically, stately
Antonyms: humbly, meekly, modestly, unimpressively
词性: 副词
