gambist:/ˈɡæmblɪst/| n. |赌徒|A person who gambles, especially as a habit or profession.  Someone who frequently engages in gambling, often excessively or compulsively.| He was a known gambler and often lost large sums of money. 他是一个知名的赌徒，经常输掉大笔钱。| The gambler placed his bet on the roulette table.  这个赌徒在轮盘赌桌上下了注。|近义词：better, punter, high-roller|反义词：None (A direct opposite doesn't exist. Perhaps "saver" or "investor" could be considered antonyms in terms of financial habits.)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From gamble + -ist.  "Gamble" itself comes from Old English *gamenian, meaning "to play, game."
