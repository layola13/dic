aftershaft:/ˈæftərʃæft/| n. |副羽，绒羽|A small, fluffy feather that grows from the underside of the base of a bird's main feather.|The aftershaft provides excellent insulation for the bird. 副羽为鸟类提供了极好的隔热效果。|synonyms:  hypopenna, plumule|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps main feather/contour feather)|noun
