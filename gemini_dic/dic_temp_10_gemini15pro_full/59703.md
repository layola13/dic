eclaircise:/eɪˈklɛrsaɪz/ | v. | 阐明；使清楚 | To make clear or lucid; to explain. |  例句：The professor eclaircised the complex theory with a simple analogy. 教授用一个简单的类比阐明了这个复杂的理论。

例句：She attempted to eclaircise the confusing instructions. 她试图阐明令人困惑的指示。| 近义词：clarify, elucidate, explicate, illuminate | 反义词：obfuscate, confuse, obscure | 词性: 动词
