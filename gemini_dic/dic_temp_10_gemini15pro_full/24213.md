bridge-building:/ˈbrɪdʒ ˌbɪldɪŋ/ | n. | 桥梁建设；搭建桥梁（比喻意义上的）；消除隔阂 | The act of constructing physical bridges or metaphorically establishing connections and relationships between different groups or individuals. |  The mayor praised the bridge-building efforts between the two communities. 市长赞扬了两个社区之间建立联系的努力。| synonyms: reconciliation, mediation, connecting, networking | antonyms: division, separation, alienation | noun

bridge-building:/ˈbrɪdʒ ˌbɪldɪŋ/ | adj. |  桥梁建设的; 弥合分歧的 | Related to the construction of bridges or the process of establishing connections and understanding. | The bridge-building project will create jobs and improve transportation. 这个桥梁建设项目将创造就业机会并改善交通。 | synonyms:  connective, unifying, reconciliatory | antonyms: divisive, separating, alienating | adjective
