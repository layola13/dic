estacade:/ˌɛstəˈkɑːd/ | n. | 木桩；栅栏；桩围；栈桥 | A row of stakes driven into the seabed to support a structure or to act as a barrier. | 例句：The estacade protected the harbor from the strong currents. 木桩保护港口免受强流的侵袭。

例句：An estacade was built to support the new pier. 建造了一个栈桥来支撑新的码头。

近义词：palisade, stockade, barrier, pier, jetty, wharf | 反义词：opening, gap | 词性：noun
