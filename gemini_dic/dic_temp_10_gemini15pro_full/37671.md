compactly:/kəmˈpæktli/ | adv. | 紧密地，简洁地 | in a compact manner; tightly packed together; concisely | The information was presented compactly in a one-page summary.  这些信息被简洁地概括在一页纸的摘要里。 | The clothes were compactly folded in the drawer. 衣服被紧密地叠放在抽屉里。|  closely, densely, tightly, concisely, briefly | loosely, diffusely, wordily


ETYMOLOGY->
From the adjective "compact" + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  "Compact" comes from the Latin "compactus," past participle of "compingere" (to fasten together), from "com-" (together) + "pangere" (to fix).
