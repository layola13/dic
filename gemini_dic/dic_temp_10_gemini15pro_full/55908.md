divulgate:/daɪˈvʌlɡeɪt/ | v. | 泄露；透露；公布 | To make known (something private or previously unknown) to the public. | The company divulged its plans for expansion. 公司公布了其扩张计划。| reveal, disclose, unveil, publish, broadcast | conceal, hide, suppress | verb

divulgate:/daɪˈvʌlɡeɪt/ | n. |  (罕见) 泄露；透露；公布 (主要用作动词) | Something that has been divulged.  |  The divulgate of the secret recipe caused a scandal. 秘方的泄露引发了一场丑闻。| revelation, disclosure | concealment, suppression | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *divulgare, from Latin divulgare "to spread among the people," from dis- "widely" + vulgare "to make common or public," from vulgus "the common people, crowd, multitude."
