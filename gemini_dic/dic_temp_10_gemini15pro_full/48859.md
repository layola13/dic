deformations:/ˌdiːfɔːrˈmeɪʃənz/
|n.|变形，畸变|alteration in the shape or dimensions of an object or material under the influence of an external force or stress.  A change from the original shape or form of something.|The deformations in the metal were caused by the intense heat.|近义词: distortions, contortions, warps, buckles|反义词:  perfections, symmetries, regularities|词性: noun, plural form of deformation

deformation:/ˌdiːfɔːrˈmeɪʃən/
|n.|变形，畸变|alteration in the shape or dimensions of an object or material under the influence of an external force or stress. ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin dēformātiōn- (stem of dēformātiō), from dēformāre (to deform), from dē- (de-) + formāre (to form).
