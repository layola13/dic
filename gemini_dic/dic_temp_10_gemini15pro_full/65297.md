eudaemonist:/ˌjuːdɪˈmɒnɪst/ | n. | 快乐主义者；幸福论者 | A person who believes in eudaemonism (the ethical theory that happiness is the chief good). |  He is a eudaemonist, always seeking joy and fulfillment. 他是一位快乐主义者，总是追求快乐和满足感。|  She considered herself a eudaemonist, prioritizing experiences that brought her happiness. 她认为自己是一个快乐主义者，优先考虑那些能给她带来快乐的经历。 | synonyms: hedonist, epicurean | antonyms: ascetic, stoic | noun

eudaemonistic:/ˌjuːdɪˈmɒnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 快乐主义的；幸福论的 | Of or relating to eudaemonism. |  He took a eudaemonistic approach to life, focusing on personal well-being. 他采取了一种快乐主义的生活方式，注重个人幸福。 | Her eudaemonistic philosophy guided her decisions, always seeking the greatest happiness. 她的快乐主义哲学指导着她的决定，总是追求最大的幸福。| synonyms: hedonistic, epicurean | antonyms: ascetic, stoic | adjective
