flyweight:/ˈflaɪˌweɪt/| n.|蝇量级选手，轻量级选手，无足轻重的人或物|A boxer in the flyweight division.  A person or thing of little importance or influence.|例句：He's a flyweight in the political arena. 他在政坛上是个无足轻重的人物。

例句：The flyweight champion defended his title successfully.  这位蝇量级冠军成功卫冕了他的头衔。|近义词：lightweight, insignificant, unimportant|反义词:heavyweight, important, significant|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From fly (n.) + weight (n.).  Originally referring to a boxer in the lowest weight class.  The metaphorical meaning developed later.
