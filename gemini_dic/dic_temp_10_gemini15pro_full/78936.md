girasol:/ˌxiɾaˈsol/ (Spanish), /ˌdʒɪərəˈsɒl/ (English approximation)| n.|向日葵|A tall plant with large yellow flowers that turn to face the sun.  It is grown for its seeds and oil.|The farmer planted a field of girasoles. 农民种了一片向日葵地。|Synonyms: sunflower, helianthus|Antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *girasol*, from *girar* ("to turn") + *sol* ("sun"). Ultimately from Latin *gyrare* ("to turn round in a circle") and *sōl* ("sun").
