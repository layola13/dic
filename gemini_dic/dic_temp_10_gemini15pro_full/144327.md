outspurted:/ˌaʊtˈspɜːtɪd/
verb
突然喷出，迸发
gushed out suddenly and forcefully.
The ketchup outspurted from the bottle when I squeezed it too hard.  当我用力挤瓶子时，番茄酱突然喷了出来。
He laughed so hard that milk outspurted from his nose. 他笑得太厉害了，牛奶从鼻子里喷了出来。
Synonyms: spurt, gush, squirt, jet
Antonyms: trickle, seep, dribble
词性: 动词
