antitragus:/ˌæntɪˈtreɪɡəs/ /ˌæntiˈtreɪɡəs/
n.
对耳屏；耳屏相对隆起
A small eminence obliquely opposite the tragus on the lower border of the auricle.
The piercing went through her antitragus.  穿孔穿过她的对耳屏。

近义词:  (没有直接的近义词，可以用描述性词语)  part of the ear, ear cartilage
反义词:  (没有反义词)
词性: noun


Etymology:
From  anti- + tragus.  Anti- meaning opposite, and tragus referring to the small pointed eminence of the external ear.
