emergencies:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒənsiz/
n.
紧急情况；突发事件
A sudden and unexpected event requiring immediate action.
例句：The hospital is well-equipped to handle emergencies.  这家医院设备齐全，可以处理紧急情况。

例句：They had an emergency plan in case of fire. 他们制定了火灾应急方案。

近义词：crises, exigencies, urgencies
反义词：normalities, routines
词性：名词（复数）


词源：
From Late Middle English: *emergencie, from Old French, or from late Latin *emergentia, from Latin emergere ‘emerge’.
