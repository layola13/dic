hussar:/hʊˈzɑːr/ | n. | 轻骑兵；骠骑兵 | A member of a class of light cavalry, originally of Hungarian origin, noted for their brilliant uniforms. |  Example 1: The hussar charged across the battlefield, his saber flashing in the sun. 轻骑兵冲过战场，他的马刀在阳光下闪闪发光。

Example 2:  The hussar regiment was known for its bravery and daring.  这个轻骑兵团以其勇敢和胆大闻名。 | synonym:  cavalryman, trooper, soldier | antonym: civilian | noun
