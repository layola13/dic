audience /ˈɔːdiəns/ (US) /ˈɔːdiəns/ (UK)| n. |观众，听众，读者；觐见，会见|A group of people who have gathered to watch or listen to something (a play, concert, somebody speaking, etc.) or are targeted by a piece of writing or other content. |The audience applauded enthusiastically at the end of the concert. 音乐会结束时，观众热情地鼓掌。|Synonyms: spectators, listeners, viewers, readership |Antonyms: performers, speakers, presenters| Noun
