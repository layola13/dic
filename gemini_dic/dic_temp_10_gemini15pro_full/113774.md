macroscopic:/ˌmækrəˈskɒpɪk/ (US) /ˌmækrəˈskɑːpɪk/| adj.|宏观的，肉眼可见的|Large enough to be visible to the naked eye; not microscopic.|例句：The macroscopic changes in the landscape were evident after the flood. 洪水过后，地貌的宏观变化显而易见。|例句：Macroscopic examination revealed a large tumor. 肉眼检查发现了一个大肿瘤。|近义词：large-scale, visible, observable|反义词：microscopic, minute, invisible|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From Ancient Greek  *μακρός (makrós, “long, large”) +‎ *-skopikos* (“-scopic”).
