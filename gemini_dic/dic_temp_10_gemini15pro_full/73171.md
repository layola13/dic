forbiddenness:/fərˈbɪdnəsnɪs/ /fɔːˈbɪdnəsnɪs/
n.
禁忌, 禁止, 被禁止的状态
The state of being forbidden; prohibition.
The forbiddenness of the subject made it all the more alluring.  这个主题的禁忌性反而使它更具诱惑力。

近义词:prohibition, ban, taboo, interdiction
反义词:permission, allowance, authorization
词性:名词
