herrings:/ˈherɪŋz/
n.
鲱鱼；鲱鱼群
A small, oily fish found in large schools in the North Atlantic and North Pacific oceans.
例句：
The fishermen caught a large haul of herrings. 渔民捕获了大量的鲱鱼。

Fishermen were mending their nets after a night of herring fishing.  渔民们在捕了一夜鲱鱼后在修补渔网。

herring:/ˈherɪŋ/
n.
鲱鱼
A small, oily fish found in large schools in the North Atlantic and North Pacific oceans.
例句：
The herring is an important food fish. 鲱鱼是一种重要的食用鱼。

Grilled herring is a popular dish in many coastal regions. 烤鲱鱼是许多沿海地区的热门菜肴。


近义词：fish, sardine, sprat
反义词: (无明显的反义词，可以考虑与之相对的陆地生物)
词性: noun (singular)


词源: From Old English *hæring, from Proto-Germanic *haringaz, likely from the root *harjaz meaning "grey," referring to the fish's color.
