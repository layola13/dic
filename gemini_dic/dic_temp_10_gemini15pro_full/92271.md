hypervirulently: /ˌhaɪpərˈvɪrələntli/
| adv. | 极度强毒地，极度剧毒地 |  In an extremely virulent manner; with extreme virulence. |  The bacteria spread hypervirulently through the population. 这种细菌在人群中极度强毒地传播。 | 近义词: extremely virulently, exceptionally virulently | 反义词: mildly virulently, weakly virulently | 词性: 副词

hypervirulent: /ˌhaɪpərˈvɪrələnt/
| adj. | 极度强毒的，极度剧毒的 | Excessively or extremely virulent. | The new strain of the virus is hypervirulent. 这种新病毒株毒性极强。| 近义词: highly virulent, extremely virulent | 反义词:  hypovirulent, avirulent | 词性: 形容词

virulent: /ˈvɪrələnt/
| adj. | (疾病)剧毒的；(病毒等)致命的；(言辞)恶毒的 |  (of a disease or poison) extremely severe or harmful in its effects. | A particularly virulent strain of flu has recently emerged. 最近出现了一种特别厉害的流感病毒。| 近义词: poisonous, toxic, noxious | 反义词: harmless, benign | 词性: 形容词


词源:
From hyper- + virulent.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, above, beyond." "Virulent" comes from Latin "virulentus," meaning "full of poison," from "virus" meaning "poison."
