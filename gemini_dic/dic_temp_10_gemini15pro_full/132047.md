noncorruptibly:/ˌnɑːnkəˈrʌptəbli/
adverb
不可腐蚀地，廉洁地
in a way that cannot be corrupted; in a way that resists being morally corrupted.
The judge ruled noncorruptibly on the case, refusing any bribes.  法官廉洁地审理了这个案子，拒绝了任何贿赂。
He served the public noncorruptibly for decades. 他廉洁地为公众服务了几十年。
Synonyms: honestly, uprightly, ethically, virtuously
Antonyms: dishonestly, corruptly, unethically, venally
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + corruptible + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Corruptible" comes from the Latin "corruptus," the past participle of "corrumpere," meaning "to break up, destroy, spoil, bribe."  "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
