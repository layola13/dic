ascidiozooid:/ˌæsɪdiəˈzoʊɔɪd/ | n. |  海鞘个体；海鞘群体中的一个个体 | An individual zooid within a colonial ascidian (sea squirt). Each ascidiozooid has its own set of organs, but they are often connected to a shared circulatory system. | The ascidiozooids in this colony are remarkably small.  这个群体中的海鞘个体非常小。 | synonyms: zooid (in the context of ascidians), individual (in the context of a colony) | antonyms: colony (as the opposite of an individual) | noun
