familistery:/fəˈmɪlɪstəri/
n.
家庭式住宅，和谐的大家庭
A large building or complex designed to house a cooperative community, often with shared living spaces and resources; a household characterized by close relationships and cooperation.
The familistery provided affordable housing and a supportive community for its residents.  这个家庭式住宅为其居民提供了负担得起的住房和支持性的社区。
The Jones family lived in a familistery-like arrangement, sharing meals and responsibilities.  琼斯一家以类似家庭式住宅的方式生活，共同用餐和分担责任。
近义词：commune, collective, cooperative
反义词：single-family home, individual household
词性：noun

Etymology: From French *familistère*, coined by Charles Fourier from *famille* (family) + *-stère* (a suffix denoting a place or location).
