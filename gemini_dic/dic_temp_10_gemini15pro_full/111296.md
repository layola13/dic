littoral: /ˈlɪtərəl/ (US), /ˈlɪtərəl/ (UK)| adj. n.| 海岸的，沿海的；海岸地区，沿岸地区|Of, relating to, or situated on the shore of a sea or lake.|The littoral zone is rich in biodiversity. 沿海地区生物多样性丰富。|例句：The hotel is located in a beautiful littoral area. 这家酒店位于美丽的沿海地区。

例句：Many species of birds inhabit the littoral zone. 许多鸟类栖息在沿海地区。|近义词：coastal, shoreline, seaside|反义词：inland, interior|词性：形容词, 名词

littorals: /ˈlɪtərəlz/ (US), /ˈlɪtərəlz/ (UK)| n.| 海岸地区，沿岸地区 (复数)|Coastal regions or areas.|The littorals of the Mediterranean are popular tourist destinations. 地中海沿岸地区是热门的旅游目的地。|例句：The littorals of many countries are facing erosion problems. 许多国家的沿海地区正面临侵蚀问题。

例句：Protecting the world's littorals is crucial for maintaining biodiversity. 保护世界各地的沿海地区对于维护生物多样性至关重要。|近义词：coasts, shores, seasides|反义词：inlands, interiors|词性：名词


词源: From Latin *litoralis*, from litus (“shore”).
