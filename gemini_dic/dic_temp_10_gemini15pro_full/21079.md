blindworm:/ˈblaɪndwɜːrm/ | n. | 蚓蜥，蛇蜥 | A legless lizard resembling a worm or snake, especially *Anguis fragilis*. |  Example 1:  The gardener unearthed a blindworm while digging in the flowerbed.  园丁在花坛里挖土时挖出了一条蚓蜥。

Example 2: Blindworms are harmless despite their resemblance to snakes. 尽管蚓蜥长得像蛇，但它们是无害的。 | Synonym: slowworm, glass lizard (for some species) | Antonym: snake (although not a true antonym, it represents a common misidentification) | noun
