mutinies:/ˈmjuːtɪniz/ | n. |叛乱，兵变|An open rebellion against constituted authority, especially by soldiers or sailors against their officers. | The captain was killed during the mutinies. 船长在叛乱中被杀。|Synonyms: rebellions, revolts, uprisings | Antonyms: obedience, submission | Noun

mutiny:/ˈmjuːtɪni/ | v. |叛乱，反抗|To engage in mutiny; revolt or rebel. | The crew mutinied against the cruel captain. 船员们反抗残暴的船长。|Synonyms: revolt, rebel, rise up | Antonyms: obey, submit | Verb
