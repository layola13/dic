lithomancy: /ˈlɪθəˌmænsi/ | n. |  占卜术（通过石头）| Divination by means of stones.  Specifically, it refers to the practice of interpreting the patterns or shapes of stones, or by casting stones to gain insight into the future or answer questions. | Example: She practiced lithomancy, seeking answers in the patterns of the river stones. 她练习占卜术，在河石的纹路中寻找答案。

| Example:  Ancient cultures often used lithomancy to predict the outcome of battles. 古代文化经常使用占卜术来预测战斗的结果。 | Synonym: divination, fortune-telling | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, could consider "scientific analysis") | noun
