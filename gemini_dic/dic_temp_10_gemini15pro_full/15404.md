backwoodsmen:/ˈbækˌwʊdzmən/| n. |拓荒者；居住在偏远森林地区的人；粗俗的人|A person who lives in or comes from a remote, wooded area, often characterized as being uneducated or unsophisticated.|The backwoodsmen lived off the land, hunting and trapping for survival. 这些拓荒者以土地为生，通过狩猎和诱捕生存。|Synonyms: frontiersman, pioneer, bushman, woodsman, hillbilly|Antonyms: city-dweller, sophisticate|Noun
