awarder:/əˈwɔːrdər/ | n. | 颁奖者；授予者 | A person or organization that gives an award. | The Academy is the awarder of the Oscars.  学院是奥斯卡奖的颁奖机构。|Synonyms: presenter, donor, grantor | Antonyms: recipient, receiver | noun

awarder:/əˈwɔːrdər/ | n. | 授予者；给予者 | Someone who bestows or confers something, especially a prize or an honor. | The awarder of the scholarship congratulated the student on her hard work. 奖学金的授予者祝贺这名学生努力学习。|Synonyms: bestower, conferrer | Antonyms:  taker | noun
