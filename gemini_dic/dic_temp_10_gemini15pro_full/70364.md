fierier:/ˈfaɪəriər (US), ˈfaɪəriə (UK)| adj.|更炽热的，更激烈的，更火爆的|Comparative form of fiery: more fiery.  Used to describe something that is more intense, passionate, or hot than something else.|The sunset was fierier tonight than last night. 今晚的日落比昨晚更火红。|The debate became fierier as the candidates clashed over key issues. 随着候选人在关键问题上发生冲突，辩论变得更加激烈。|近义词：hotter, more intense, more passionate, more ardent|反义词：cooler, calmer, milder, less intense|词性:形容词
