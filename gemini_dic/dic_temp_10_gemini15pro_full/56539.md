dominating:/ˈdɒmɪneɪtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈdɑːmɪneɪtɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|支配的，统治的，主要的，占优势的|Present participle of dominate: to have control or influence over someone or something.|Example:  The dominating figure in the industry was known for his ruthless business tactics.  该行业的主导人物以其冷酷无情的商业策略而闻名。

Example: Her dominating personality made it difficult for others to express their opinions. 她强势的性格让别人很难表达自己的意见。|Synonyms: controlling, overpowering, predominant, prevailing, ascendant|Antonyms: submissive, subordinate, secondary, minor|词性:形容词

dominate:/ˈdɒmɪneɪt/ (UK) /ˈdɑːmɪneɪt/ (US)| v.|支配，控制，统治，占优势|To have control or influence over someone or something.|Example: The company dominates the market for computer software.  这家公司在计算机软件市场占据主导地位。

Example:  Fear dominated his thoughts. 恐惧占据了他的思绪。|Synonyms: control, govern, rule, command, monopolize|Antonyms: submit, obey, yield|词性:动词


词源: From Late Latin *dominatus, past participle of dominari (“to rule, govern”), from Latin dominus (“lord, master”).
