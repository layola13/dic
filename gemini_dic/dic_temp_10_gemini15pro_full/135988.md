nonraisable:/ˌnɒnˈreɪzəbl/ | adj. | 不可提升的，无法引发的 |  Describes an exception (in programming) that cannot be handled by a `try-except` block because it occurs at a low level or in a critical section of code.  It often indicates a serious problem that might lead to program termination. | The system encountered a nonraisable exception. 系统遇到了一个不可提升的异常。 | The program crashed due to a nonraisable exception related to memory access. 该程序由于与内存访问相关的不可提升异常而崩溃。 | unhandleable, unrecoverable | manageable, recoverable | adjective
