parapatric:/ˌpærəˈpæt.rɪk/ | adj. | 异域性的，邻域同种的 |  Existing or occurring in adjacent but not overlapping areas.  Refers to species or populations that are geographically adjacent but do not overlap or interbreed extensively.  |  The two parapatric subspecies show slight differences in plumage. 这两个邻域同种的亚种在羽毛上略有不同。 |  The parapatric distribution of the two related species suggests a recent divergence. 这两个相关物种的邻域分布表明它们最近才分化。 |  adjacent, contiguous |  allopatric, sympatric | adjective
