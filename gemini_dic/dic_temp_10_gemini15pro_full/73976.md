fortitude:/ˈfɔːrtɪtjuːd/ (US) /ˈfɔːtɪtʃuːd/ (UK)| n.|勇气，坚韧，刚毅|Mental and emotional strength in facing difficulty, adversity, danger, or pain courageously.|例句：She endured the treatment with great fortitude. 她以极大的毅力忍受了治疗。|近义词：courage, bravery, resilience, resolve, strength, grit, determination|反义词：cowardice, fear, timidity, weakness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Latin *fortitūdō*, from *fortis* "strong, brave".
