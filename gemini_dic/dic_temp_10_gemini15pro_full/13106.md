astrophysicist: /ˌæstrəˈfɪzɪsɪst/ | n. | 天体物理学家 | A scientist who studies the physics of the universe, including the physical properties of celestial objects, such as stars, galaxies, and black holes. |  Example: The astrophysicist dedicated her life to understanding the origins of the universe.  这位天体物理学家毕生致力于了解宇宙的起源。

Example:  Astrophysicists use telescopes and other instruments to observe the universe. 天体物理学家使用望远镜和其他仪器来观测宇宙。 | 近义词: astronomer, cosmologist | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: astro- (from Greek "astron" meaning "star") + physics + -ist (suffix denoting a person who practices or is skilled in something)
