emprint:/ɪmˈprɪnt/| n. vt.|印记，铭刻；深刻的影响|A mark made by pressing something onto a softer substance; a lasting impression or influence.|The child's hand left an emprint in the wet cement. 孩子的手在湿水泥上留下了印记。|近义词：impression, mark, imprint, stamp|反义词: erasure, deletion|noun
emprint:/ɪmˈprɪnt/| vt.|印，压印；铭刻|To impress or fix something firmly, as on the mind or memory.|The experience was deeply emprinted on her memory. 这段经历深深地印在了她的记忆里。|近义词：impress, stamp, engrave, fix|反义词: erase, delete|verb
