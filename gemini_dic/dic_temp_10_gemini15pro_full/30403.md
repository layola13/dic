centenary: /senˈtiːnəri/ /sɛnˈtɛnəri/
n.|百年纪念; 一百周年|A centenary is the hundredth anniversary of a significant event.|例句：The town is celebrating its centenary this year.  这个城镇今年在庆祝其百年诞辰。

例句：The museum marked its centenary with a special exhibition.  博物馆举办了一个特别展览来纪念其百年诞辰。|近义词：anniversary, centennial|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific anniversary)|词性: 名词

centenary: /senˈtiːnəri/ /sɛnˈtɛnəri/
adj.|一百年的; 百年纪念的|Centenary means relating to a period of one hundred years or to a hundredth anniversary.|例句：The centenary celebrations lasted for a week.  百年庆典持续了一周。

例句：This is a centenary edition of the book.  这是这本书的百年纪念版。|近义词：centennial|反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific anniversary)|词性: 形容词
