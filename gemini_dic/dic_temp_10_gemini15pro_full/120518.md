minutiae:/mɪˈnuːʃiiː/ /maɪˈnuːʃiiː/
noun
细枝末节；琐事；细节
small or trivial details
The committee spent hours discussing the minutiae of the proposal. 委员会花了几个小时讨论提案的细枝末节。
synonyms: details, particulars, specifics, trivia
antonyms: essentials, fundamentals, gist, overview
n.
