microspectroscope:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈspɛktroʊskoʊp/| n. |显微分光镜|A small spectroscope attached to a microscope, used for observing the spectra of small objects or areas.|The microspectroscope revealed the presence of rare earth elements in the sample. 显微分光镜显示样品中存在稀土元素。|
|The gem specialist used a microspectroscope to analyze the diamond's color. 宝石专家使用显微分光镜分析钻石的颜色。|
|Synonyms: microspectrophotometer|Antonyms: N/A|noun
