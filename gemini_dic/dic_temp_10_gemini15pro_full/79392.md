gliding:/ˈɡlaɪdɪŋ/| n. adj.|滑翔，滑行；流畅，平稳|The smooth, effortless movement of something through the air or over a surface.|例句：The eagle's gliding was a majestic sight.  老鹰的滑翔姿态十分雄伟壮观。

例句：The skater's gliding across the ice was graceful.  滑冰者在冰上滑行姿态优美。|近义词：soaring, drifting, flowing, smooth|反义词:jerky, bumpy, rough|词性:名词, 形容词
gliding:/ˈɡlaɪdɪŋ/| v.|滑翔，滑行|To move smoothly and effortlessly through the air or over a surface.|例句：The hawk was gliding on the wind currents.  老鹰乘着气流滑翔。

例句：The boat glided silently across the lake.  小船静静地滑过湖面。|近义词：drifting, soaring, coasting|反义词:stalling, lumbering|词性:动词
