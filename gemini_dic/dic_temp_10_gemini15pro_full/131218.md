noncaking:/ˌnɒnˈkeɪkɪŋ/ | adj. | 防结块的，不结块的 |  Preventing or resistant to the formation of lumps or cakes.  This term often describes substances like powders or granules that are treated to prevent them from sticking together and forming a solid mass. |  This noncaking salt flows freely even in humid conditions. 这种防结块的盐即使在潮湿的环境下也能自由流动。| The powder is specially formulated to be noncaking.  这种粉末经过特殊配方，具有防结块的特性。 |  free-flowing, loose, powdery |  caking, lumpy, clumping | adjective
