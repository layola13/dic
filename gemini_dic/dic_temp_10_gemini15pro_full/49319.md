delightfulness:/dɪˈlaɪtfəlnəs/| n.|令人愉快；快乐|The delightfulness of the sunny afternoon made everyone feel relaxed.  阳光明媚的下午令人愉快，每个人都感到放松。|近义词：charm, pleasantness, loveliness|反义词：unpleasantness, disagreeableness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From delightful + -ness.  Delightful comes from the Old French "deleitable," ultimately from the Latin "delectare" (to delight).  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
