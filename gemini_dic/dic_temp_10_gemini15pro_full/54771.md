dispellable:/dɪˈspɛləbl/ | adj. | 可驱散的，可消除的 | Able to be dispelled or eliminated. | The fog was thick but dispellable by the rising sun.  浓雾很厚，但会被升起的太阳驱散。 | Synonyms: removable, eradicable, eliminable | Antonyms: ineradicable, permanent, ingrained | adjective

dispellable:/dɪˈspɛləbl/ | adj. | 可驱散的，可消除的 |  Something that can be driven away or caused to vanish. |  Her fears were dispellable with a little reassurance.  她的一点恐惧可以用一些安慰来消除。 | Synonyms:  exterminable, extinguishable | Antonyms: fixed, enduring | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From dispel + -able. Dispel comes from Old French dispeller, from Latin dispellere, from dis- "apart" + pellere "to drive".
