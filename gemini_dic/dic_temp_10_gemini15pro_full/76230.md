future-proofed:/ˈfjuːtʃə pruːft/ | adj. | 未来验证的；面向未来的；不会过时的 | Designed or built in a way that is unlikely to become obsolete or outdated in the foreseeable future.  Made resistant to changes that may occur in the future. |  Our software is future-proofed, so you won't need to upgrade for years. 我们的软件是面向未来的，所以你几年内都不需要升级。 |  This new phone is future-proofed with 5G capabilities. 这款新手机具备5G功能，面向未来。| 近义词: forward-looking, forward-thinking, modern, advanced, state-of-the-art | 反义词: outdated, obsolete, antiquated, archaic |词性:形容词
