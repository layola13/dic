moussakas:/ˌmuːˈsɑːkɑːs/| n. |烤肉茄子|A Greek dish consisting of layers of minced meat, eggplant (aubergine), and a creamy topping, usually béchamel sauce, baked in the oven. |例句：The moussakas was rich and flavorful, a true taste of Greece. 烤肉茄子味道浓郁，是真正的希腊风味。

例句：She made a delicious moussakas for dinner last night. 她昨晚做了一道美味的烤肉茄子作为晚餐。|近义词：casserole, gratin|反义词:|名词
