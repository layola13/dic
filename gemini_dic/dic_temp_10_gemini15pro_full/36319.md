cohort:/ˈkoʊhɔːrt/ (US), /ˈkəʊhɔːt/ (UK)| n.|同伙，一群人，同龄群，同期组|A group of people banded together or treated as a group.|例句：The 1999 birth cohort has faced unique economic challenges. 1999年出生的同龄群体面临着独特的经济挑战。

cohort:/ˈkoʊhɔːrt/ (US), /ˈkəʊhɔːt/ (UK)| n.|同伙，一群人，同龄群，同期组|A group of people who share a characteristic, usually age.|例句：Researchers studied a cohort of smokers to understand the long-term effects of smoking. 研究人员研究了一组吸烟者，以了解吸烟的长期影响。|近义词：group, band, company, posse|反义词:individual, loner|词性:名词
