homogeneous:/ˌhoʊməˈdʒiːniəs/ /ˌhɒməˈdʒiːnɪəs/| adj.|同质的，同类的，均匀的|Of the same kind or nature; uniform in composition or structure throughout.|The mixture was homogeneous, with no lumps or variations in color.|近义词：uniform, similar, consistent, alike|反义词：heterogeneous, diverse, varied|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin homogeneus, from Ancient Greek ὁμογενής (homogenḗs, “of the same kind, of common descent”), from ὁμός (homós, “same”) + γένος (génos, “kind, race, stock”).
