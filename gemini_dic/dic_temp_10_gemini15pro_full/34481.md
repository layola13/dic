clayey:/ˈkleɪi/| adj.|黏土的，含黏土的|Containing or resembling clay; consisting of or covered with clay.|The soil in my garden is quite clayey.  我的花园里的土壤黏性很重。|近义词：clayish, argillaceous|反义词:sandy, gravelly|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From clay + -y.  Clay comes from Old English clæg, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *klaijo-, meaning "sticky earth."
