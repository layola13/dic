aphonic:/eɪˈfɒnɪk/ /əˈfɑːnɪk/| adj.|失音的，无声的|Unable to produce vocal sounds; voiceless.|例句：After the surgery, he was temporarily aphonic.  手术后，他暂时失声了。

例句：The aphonic patient communicated through writing. 失声的病人通过书写进行交流。|近义词：voiceless, mute, silent|反义词：vocal, voiced|词性：adjective

Etymology:  From Greek *aphōnos* "voiceless," from *a-* "without" + *phōnē* "voice, sound."
