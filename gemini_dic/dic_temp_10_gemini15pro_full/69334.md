fecundify:/ˈfɛkəndaɪf/ /ˈfiːkəndaɪf/
verb
使多产；使肥沃；使富饶
to make fertile or productive; to increase the fertility of
The farmer used fertilizer to fecundify the soil.  农民用肥料使土壤肥沃。
New irrigation techniques have fecundified the once barren land. 新的灌溉技术使曾经贫瘠的土地变得肥沃。
近义词: fertilize, enrich, fructify
反义词: sterilize, impoverish
词性: verb

fecund:/ˈfɛkənd/ /ˈfiːkənd/
adjective
多产的；肥沃的；富饶的
producing or capable of producing offspring or vegetation in abundance; fertile
The fecund soil produced a bountiful harvest. 肥沃的土壤带来了丰收。
The Amazon rainforest is a fecund ecosystem teeming with life. 亚马逊雨林是一个充满生机的肥沃生态系统。
近义词: fertile, productive, prolific
反义词: barren, sterile, infertile
词性: adjective

词源：From Latin *fecundificare, from fecundus (“fruitful, fertile”) + -ificare (from facere (“to make, do”)).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeh₁- (“to put, place, do”).
