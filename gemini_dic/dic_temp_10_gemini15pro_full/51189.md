destabilizing:/diːˈsteɪbəlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
不稳定的；破坏稳定的
making something unstable
例句：The constant fighting is destabilizing the region.  不断的战斗正在破坏该地区的稳定。

例句：The new economic policy had a destabilizing effect on the stock market. 新的经济政策对股市产生了不稳定的影响。

近义词：disruptive, undermining, unsettling
反义词：stabilizing, strengthening, reinforcing
词性: adjective
