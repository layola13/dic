nongravity:/ˌnɒnˈɡrævɪti/
adjective
非重力的，无重力的
Relating to an environment in which there is no gravity, such as in outer space.
The astronauts trained in a nongravity environment to prepare for the spacewalk.  宇航员在无重力环境中训练，为太空行走做准备。
The nongravity chamber allowed scientists to conduct unique experiments. 非重力舱允许科学家进行独特的实验。
近义词:weightless, zero-gravity
反义词:gravitational
词性:adjective
