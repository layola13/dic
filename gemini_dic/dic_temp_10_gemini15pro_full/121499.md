misleads:/ˌmɪsˈliːdz/| v. |误导，欺骗|To cause someone to believe something that is not true, often intentionally. | The advertisement misleads consumers about the product's effectiveness.  这则广告误导了消费者对产品功效的认知。

The map misled us, and we ended up lost. 地图误导了我们，我们最终迷路了。|近义词：deceive, trick, fool, delude |反义词: guide, inform, enlighten| verb
