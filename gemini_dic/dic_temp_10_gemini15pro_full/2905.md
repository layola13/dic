advices:/ədˈvaɪsɪz/| n.|建议（错误用法）| "Advices" is an incorrect plural form of "advice."  The word "advice" is uncountable and does not have a plural form. Use "advice" for both singular and plural contexts. | Incorrect:  She gave me many good advices. | Correct: She gave me a lot of good advice. | 近义词：counsel, guidance, recommendations (when referring to multiple suggestions)|反义词: dissuasion | noun

USAGE->
"Advices" is grammatically incorrect and should not be used.  The following examples demonstrate the correct usage of "advice."

一般现在时:
She gives good advice. = 她给出好的建议。
He always follows his father's advice. = 他总是听从父亲的建议。

一般过去时:
I took her advice and went home. = 我听取了她的建议回家了。
They ignored the advice of their lawyer. = 他们忽略了律师的建议。

现在完成时:
He has given me some useful advice. = 他给了我一些有用的建议。
She has always valued her mother's advice. = 她一直很重视她母亲的建议。

过去完成时:
I had already taken his advice before he left. = 在他离开之前，我已经采纳了他的建议。
She had ignored all the advice she had received. = 她忽略了所有收到的建议。

将来时:
I will ask for her advice tomorrow. = 我明天会征求她的意见。
He will give you some good advice. = 他会给你一些好建议。

疑问句:
What advice would you give me? = 你会给我什么建议？
Did you take his advice? = 你采纳了他的建议吗？

否定句:
I don't need your advice. = 我不需要你的建议。
She didn't follow my advice. = 她没有听从我的建议。
