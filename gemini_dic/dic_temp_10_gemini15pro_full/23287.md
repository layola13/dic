boxhauling:/ˈbɒksˌhɔːlɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbɒksˌhɔːlɪŋ/ (UK)
n.
抢风行驶；逆风抢风行驶
A sailing maneuver used to sail upwind in light winds, by alternately heading off the wind to gain speed and then turning back toward the wind.
The small sailboat was making slow progress upwind using a boxhauling technique.  这艘小帆船正利用抢风行驶的技巧缓慢地逆风前进。
tacking, beating
running
