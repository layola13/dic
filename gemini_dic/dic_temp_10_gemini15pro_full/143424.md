outflees:/ˌaʊtˈfliːz/
verb
比...逃得更快；逃得比...远
To run away from someone or something faster than they can follow.
The gazelle outflees the cheetah.  羚羊比猎豹跑得更快。
He outflees his pursuers. 他比追赶他的人跑得更快。
近义词: outrun, outstrip, escape
反义词: be caught, surrender
