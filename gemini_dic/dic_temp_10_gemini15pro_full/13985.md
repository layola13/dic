auricled: /ˈɔːrɪk(ə)ld/
adjective
有耳廓的；有耳状附属物的
Having auricles or ear-like appendages.
The auricled tree frog has distinctive ear-like projections on its head.  这种有耳廓的树蛙头部有独特的耳状突起。
The shell is auricled at the aperture.  贝壳在开口处有耳状附属物。
近义词: eared, auriform
反义词: earless
词性: adjective
