bibliographer:/ˌbɪbliˈɒɡrəfər/ | /ˌbɪbliˈɑːɡrəfər/ | n. | 书目学家；文献目录学家 | A person who compiles bibliographies or studies bibliography.  A scholar who specializes in the history, description, and identification of books. | Example 1: The bibliographer meticulously cataloged the rare books.  这位书目学家一丝不苟地将珍本书籍编入目录。

Example 2:  The bibliographer's expertise was invaluable in authenticating the manuscript.  这位书目学家的专业知识对于鉴定手稿真伪至关重要。 | Synonym:  cataloguer, documentalist | Antonym: N/A | noun
