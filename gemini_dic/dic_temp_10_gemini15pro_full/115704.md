marginate:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪneɪt/ | v. | 使边缘化；排斥；使处于社会边缘 | to place in a position of marginal importance, influence, or power |  The new immigrants were often marginalized by society.  这些新移民经常被社会边缘化。 | synonyms: ostracize, exclude, alienate, isolate, segregate | antonyms: integrate, include, embrace, welcome | verb

marginate:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪneɪt/ | adj. | 有边缘的；边缘化的 | having a margin | The marginate leaf has a distinct edge. 这片叶子有明显的边缘。 | synonyms: bordered, edged, fringed | antonyms: center, core, middle | adjective
