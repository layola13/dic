exteriorly:/ɪkˈstɪəriərli/
adverb
外部地，外表上，外面
On or from the outside; externally.
The house was painted white exteriorly. 房子外面漆成了白色。
近义词: externally, outwardly
反义词: interiorly, inwardly
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exteriorius, comparative of exter (“outward, external”), from exter (“outside”), from ex (“out of, from”).
