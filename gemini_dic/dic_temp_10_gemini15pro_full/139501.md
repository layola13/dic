obstruction:/əbˈstrʌkʃən/ | n. | 障碍，阻碍，阻塞; 障碍物 |Something that blocks or gets in the way; an obstacle or impediment. | The fallen tree was an obstruction in the road.  倒下的树是道路上的障碍物。|
obstruction:/əbˈstrʌkʃən/ | n. | 妨碍，阻止 |The act of blocking or hindering something. |  His obstruction of justice led to his arrest. 他妨碍司法公正导致他被捕。|
obstruction:/əbˈstrʌkʃən/ | n. | (医学)阻塞 |A blockage in a bodily passage or organ. |  The doctor discovered an obstruction in his intestine. 医生在他的肠道里发现了一个阻塞物。 | 近义词: obstacle, barrier, impediment, blockage, hindrance | 反义词: clearance, aid, assistance | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *obstructionem, accusative of obstructio (“a building up before, a blocking up”), from obstructus, past participle of obstruere (“to build in front of, block up, obstruct”), from ob (“against, in front of, towards”) + struere (“to pile up, build”).
