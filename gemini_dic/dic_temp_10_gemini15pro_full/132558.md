nondialectic:/ˌnɒnˌdaɪəˈlɛktɪk/ | adj. | 非辩证的 | Not characteristic of or employing dialectic; not proceeding by the exchange of logical arguments. |  His approach to the problem was surprisingly nondialectic, focusing on a single perspective. 他处理这个问题的方式令人惊讶地是非辩证的，只关注单一视角。|  The artist's nondialectic presentation simply laid out the facts without exploring the complexities. 这位艺术家的非辩证性陈述只是简单地列出了事实，而没有探究其复杂性。| 近义词: undialectical, nonargumentative, linear | 反义词: dialectic, argumentative, discursive | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From non- + dialectic.  "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not". "Dialectic" comes from the Greek "dialektikē (tekhnē)" meaning "(art) of conversation, debate".
