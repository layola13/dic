geed:/ɡiːd/
| adj. |兴奋的，狂热的；沉迷于…的 |Excited, enthusiastic, or obsessed with something. |He was geed about the new video game. 他对这款新电子游戏非常着迷。|近义词：excited, enthusiastic, pumped, amped |反义词：bored, apathetic, unenthusiastic |词性:形容词

Etymology:
Likely a shortened form of "ged up," itself a variant of "get up," implying a state of heightened energy or excitement. This connection to "get up" suggests a sense of being roused or stimulated.
