homonymous: /həˈmɒnɪməs/ (British) /həˈmɑːnɪməs/ (American) | adj. | 同音异义的，同形的 | Having the same spelling or pronunciation as another word but a different meaning. | Example: The words "bat" (a nocturnal animal) and "bat" (used in baseball) are homonymous.  蝙蝠（一种夜行动物）和球棒（用于棒球）这两个词是同音异义词。 | Example:  "Lead," the metal, and "lead," to guide, are homonymous.  金属“铅”和动词“领导”是同音异义词。 | Synonyms: homonymic, like-sounding, like-spelled | Antonyms: heteronymous, antonymous | Word type: adjective
