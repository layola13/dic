octopus:/ˈɒktəpəs/ (US) /ˈɒktəpʊs/ (UK)| n. |章鱼|A sea creature with a soft body, eight arms, and a large head. It can change color and squirt ink.|例句：The octopus camouflaged itself against the coral. 章鱼在珊瑚中伪装自己。

例句：The octopus grabbed the crab with its tentacles.  章鱼用触手抓住了螃蟹。


近义词：cephalopod|反义词: 无|词性:noun
