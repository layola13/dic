pandarism: /ˈpændərɪzəm/ | n. |拉皮条；怂恿；迎合；煽动| The act or practice of pandering; procuring.  The catering to the lower tastes and desires of others or exploiting their weaknesses. | The film was criticized for its blatant pandarism to the teenage market. 这部电影因公然迎合青少年市场而受到批评。| 近义词: exploitation, pimping, procuring, indulgence, gratification | 反义词: restraint, abstinence, disapproval | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From pander + -ism.  Ultimately from Pandarus, a character in Chaucer's *Troilus and Criseyde* and Shakespeare's *Troilus and Cressida* who acts as a go-between for the lovers.
