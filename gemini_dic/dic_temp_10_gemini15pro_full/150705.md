parabolas: /pəˈræbələz/ | n. |抛物线 |A symmetrical open plane curve formed by the intersection of a cone with a plane parallel to its side. The path of a projectile under the influence of gravity ideally follows a curve of this shape. | The comet follows a parabolic trajectory. 这颗彗星沿着抛物线轨迹运行。| synonyms:  curves, arcs | antonyms: straight lines | noun
