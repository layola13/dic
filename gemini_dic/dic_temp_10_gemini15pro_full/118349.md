meringues:/məˈrɛŋz/ | n. | 蛋白酥，蛋白霜饼干 | A sweet food made from stiffly beaten egg whites and sugar, baked until crisp. |  She made a delicious dessert with fresh berries and meringues. 她用新鲜浆果和蛋白酥做了一道美味的甜点。

The meringues were light and airy, melting in the mouth. 这些蛋白酥轻盈蓬松，入口即化。 | 近义词:pavlova, soufflé | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something dense and chewy like fudge) | 词性: noun
