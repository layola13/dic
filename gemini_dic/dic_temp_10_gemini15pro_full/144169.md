outshowed:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊd/
verb
胜过，比...更出色
surpassed in performance or appearance; outperformed.
例句：Her performance outshowed everyone else's. 她的表演胜过了所有人。
例句：The new model outshowed the old one in fuel efficiency.  新款车型在燃油效率方面胜过旧款。
近义词：outperformed, surpassed, eclipsed, exceeded
反义词：underperformed, lagged behind
词性：动词
