meditater:/ˈmedɪteɪtər/| n. |冥想者；沉思者|A person who meditates; one who engages in contemplation or reflection. |例句：The meditater sat in silence for hours. 冥想者静静地坐了几个小时。

例句：She is a dedicated meditater and finds peace through her practice. 她是一位专注的冥想者，并通过练习找到了平静。|近义词：contemplator, reflector, thinker, ponderer|反义词:agitator, disrupter|noun
