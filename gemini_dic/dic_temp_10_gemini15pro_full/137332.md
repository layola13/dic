nontariffs:/nɒnˈtærɪfs/ /ˌnɑːnˈtɛrɪfs/ | n. |非关税壁垒 |Government regulations that restrict trade without imposing a direct tax or duty.  These can include quotas, embargoes, sanctions, and licensing requirements.| The government imposed new nontariffs on imported steel. 政府对进口钢材施加了新的非关税壁垒。 | synonyms: trade barriers, restrictions, impediments | antonyms: free trade, open market | noun

nontariff:/nɒnˈtærɪf/ /ˌnɑːnˈtɛrɪf/ | adj. |非关税的 | Relating to restrictions on trade that do not involve the imposition of tariffs or duties.| The country adopted a nontariff barrier to protect its domestic industry.  这个国家采取了非关税壁垒来保护其国内产业。 | synonyms: protectionist, restrictive | antonyms:  free trade, open market | adjective
