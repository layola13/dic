bloodulf: /ˈblʌdˌʊlf/ | n. | 血仇，血债 | A feud in which the relatives of a murdered person seek vengeance on the murderer or the murderer's kin. |  The two families were locked in a bitter bloodulf that spanned generations. 这两个家族陷入了持续数代的残酷血仇中。

The bloodulf ended only when the last surviving member of one family was killed.  这场血仇直到其中一个家族的最后一个成员被杀才结束。


近义词：vendetta, feud | 反义词: reconciliation, peace | 词性: noun
