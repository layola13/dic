nugatoriness:/ˈnjuːɡətɔːrinəs/
n.
无价值；琐碎；无聊
The quality or state of being nugatory; triflingness; futility.
His arguments were marked by their nugatoriness. 他的论点以其琐碎无用为特点。

Synonyms: triviality, futility, insignificance, worthlessness, pointlessness
Antonyms: significance, importance, value, relevance
