amusingly:/əˈmjuːzɪŋli/ | adv. | 好笑地，有趣地 | In a way that causes laughter or provides entertainment.  | He amusingly recounted his childhood adventures. 他有趣地讲述了他童年的冒险经历。 | She amusingly mimicked her teacher's mannerisms. 她滑稽地模仿了她老师的举止。 | 近义词: humorously, entertainingly, comically | 反义词: seriously, solemnly, dully | 词性: 副词

词源: From amusing + -ly.  "Amusing" comes from the Old French "amuser" (to entertain, divert), ultimately from the Latin "ad-" (to) + "musum" (muzzle, snout - referring to the idea of diverting attention).
