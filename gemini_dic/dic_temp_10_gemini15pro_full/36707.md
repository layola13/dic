collision:/kəˈlɪʒən/ | n. | 碰撞，冲突，抵触 | An instance of one object striking violently against another. |  The two cars were involved in a head-on collision. 两辆汽车迎头相撞。


There was a collision of interests between the two companies.  两家公司之间存在利益冲突。 | synonyms: crash, impact, smash, clash | antonyms: agreement, harmony | noun
