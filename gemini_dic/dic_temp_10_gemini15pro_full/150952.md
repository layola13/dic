paralyze: /ˈpærəlaɪz/ (US) /ˈpærəlʌɪz/ (UK) | vt. | 使麻痹；使瘫痪；使丧失作用；使惊呆 | to make unable to move or act; to cause to be unable to function | The accident paralyzed him from the waist down.  这场事故使他腰部以下瘫痪。| synonyms: disable, cripple, immobilize, incapacitate | antonyms: enable, empower, activate | verb
