catharist:/ˈkæθɑːrɪst/
n.
净化者，清洁者
a person who believes in catharsis, especially a member of a medieval religious sect (Cathari) that advocated purification of the church.
The catharist rejected the material world and sought spiritual purity.  净化者拒绝物质世界，追求精神纯洁。
近义词:purifier, cleanser
反义词:polluter, contaminant
词性:noun

cathartic:/kəˈθɑːrtɪk/
adj.
宣泄的，净化心灵的
providing psychological relief through the open expression of strong emotions.
Writing poetry can be a cathartic experience. 写诗可以是一种宣泄的体验。
近义词:cleansing, purifying, therapeutic
反义词:repressive, inhibiting
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *catharista, from Ancient Greek καθαριστής (katharistēs, “purifier, cleaner”), from καθαρός (katharós, “pure, clean”).
