foxtrotted:/ˈfɒkstrɒtɪd/ /ˈfɑːkstrɑːtɪd/| v.|狐步舞(过去式),跳狐步舞(过去式)|Past tense of "foxtrot," meaning to dance the foxtrot.|She foxtrotted across the dance floor with grace. 她优雅地在舞池中跳着狐步舞。|Synonym: danced (a foxtrot) | Antonym: stood still|verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From foxtrot (n.), named after Harry Fox, an American vaudeville performer.
