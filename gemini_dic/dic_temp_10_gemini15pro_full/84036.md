half-farthing:/ˌhɑːf ˈfɑːrðɪŋ/ | n. | 半法寻（英国旧时货币，相当于四分之一便士） | A former British coin worth half a farthing, or one-eighth of a penny.  | The half-farthing was so small it was easily lost.  半法寻太小了，很容易丢失。 |  The half-farthing was worth a tiny amount. 半法寻只值一点点钱。| synonym: mite (informal) | antonym: fortune,  king's ransom | noun

half:/hɑːf/ | n. adj. adv. | 一半，半个 | One of two equal or approximately equal parts of a divisible whole. | I ate half the pizza. 我吃了半个披萨。| He gave me half of his sandwich. 他给了我一半他的三明治。 | synonym: moiety, one-half | antonym: whole, double | noun, adjective, adverb

farthing:/ˈfɑːrðɪŋ/ | n. | 法寻（英国旧时货币，相当于四分之一便士） | A former British coin worth a quarter of a penny.  | Farthings were withdrawn from circulation in 1961. 法寻在1961年停止流通。| He wouldn't give a farthing for it. 他一分钱也不肯给。| synonym: quarter-penny | antonym: fortune | noun
