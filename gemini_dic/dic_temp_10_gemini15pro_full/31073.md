chamomile: /ˈkæməˌmaɪl/ (US), /ˈkæməˌmʌɪl/ (UK)| n.| 洋甘菊；甘菊茶|A plant with small white and yellow flowers, used to make tea and other products believed to have medicinal properties. It is known for its calming and relaxing effects.|  I drank a cup of chamomile tea before bed to help me sleep. 我睡前喝了一杯甘菊茶来帮助睡眠。

|近义词：camomile|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, potentially stimulants like coffee)|词性: noun


chamomile: /ˈkæməˌmaɪl/ (US), /ˈkæməˌmʌɪl/ (UK)| adj.| 洋甘菊的|Made from or containing chamomile.| She enjoys the scent of chamomile lotion. 她喜欢洋甘菊润肤露的香味。
