edental:/iːˈdɛntl/
adjective
无齿的;缺齿的
lacking teeth; toothless.
Edentate mammals, such as sloths and anteaters, have no or few teeth. 无齿哺乳动物，如树懒和食蚁兽，没有或只有很少的牙齿。
synonyms: toothless, lacking teeth
antonyms: dentate, toothed
adjective

edental:/iːˈdɛntl/
noun
无齿动物;缺齿动物
an edentate animal.
The giant anteater is an edental. 巨型食蚁兽是一种无齿动物。
synonyms: toothless animal
antonyms: toothed animal
noun
