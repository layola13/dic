mooningly:/ˈmuːnɪŋli/
adverb
痴痴地，出神地，梦游般地
In a dreamy, absent-minded manner, as if lost in thought or romantically preoccupied.
She gazed mooningly at the photograph of her beloved. 她痴痴地望着爱人的照片。
近义词: dreamily, abstractedly, wistfully
反义词: alertly, attentively, sharply
词性: 副词

mooningly:/ˈmuːnɪŋli/
副词
痴痴地，出神地，梦游般地

近义词: dreamily, abstractedly, wistfully

词源:  Derived from "moon" + "-ing" + "-ly".  "Moon" in this context refers to the romanticized idea of being lost in thought, like someone gazing at the moon.
