adzuki:/əˈdzuːki/ | n. |红豆|A small, round bean with a red skin, used in East Asian cuisine.|I love adzuki bean paste in mochi. 我喜欢麻糬里的红豆沙。|近义词:red bean|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Japanese アズキ (azuki), ultimately from Classical Japanese 小豆 (azuki), meaning "small bean".

USAGE->
I made adzuki bean soup for dinner. = 我晚饭做了红豆汤。
Adzuki beans are a common ingredient in Japanese desserts. = 红豆是日本甜点中常见的成分。


PHRASE->
adzuki bean paste = 红豆沙
adzuki bean ice cream = 红豆冰淇淋
adzuki bean mochi = 红豆麻糬
adzuki bean soup = 红豆汤
sweet adzuki bean filling = 甜红豆馅
