panned-out:/pænd aʊt/| adj.|成功的；有利可图的；（淘金）淘尽的；|developed or turned out; often used in relation to hopes or expectations that did not materialize or were not as successful as hoped.  Originally referring to panning for gold and finding little or none.|The venture panned out better than expected, yielding a substantial profit. 这项风险投资的结果比预期的要好，产生了可观的利润。|Synonyms: successful, profitable, fruitful, advantageous|Antonyms: unsuccessful, unprofitable, fruitless, disadvantageous|adjective


panned-out:/pænd aʊt/| v.|（淘金）淘尽；结果是；证明是；|To search for gold by using a pan. To develop or turn out in a specified way.|The expedition panned out to be a complete disaster. 这次探险结果是一场彻底的灾难。|Synonyms: turn out, develop, result, conclude|Antonyms: fail, collapse, fall through|verb
