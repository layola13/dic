crackled:/ˈkrækəld/ (US), /ˈkrækld/ (UK)| v.|发出噼啪声；爆裂声|To make slight, sharp, repeated sounds, like things burning in a fire.|The fire crackled merrily in the hearth. 炉火在壁炉里欢快地噼啪作响。|The radio crackled with interference. 收音机里发出噼啪的干扰声。|近义词：popped, snapped, sputtered|反义词: hissed, whooshed|词性: 动词

crackle:/ˈkrækəl/ (US), /ˈkrækəl/ (UK)| n.|噼啪声；爆裂声|A series of slight, sharp, repeated sounds.|The crackle of the fire was soothing. 火焰的噼啪声令人舒缓。|There was a crackle on the phone line. 电话线上传来噼啪声。|近义词：pop, snap, sputtering|反义词: hiss, whoosh|词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *craklen, frequentative of craken (“to crack”), of imitative origin.
