eland:/ˈiːlənd/ | n. | 麋羚 | A large African antelope with spirally twisted horns. |  The eland is the largest antelope species in Africa. 麋羚是非洲最大的羚羊种类。

The eland's coat is a sandy brown color. 麋羚的皮毛是沙棕色。| 近义词: antelope, kudu | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an animal) | 词性: noun
