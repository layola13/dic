nirvana:/nɪrˈvɑːnə/ /nɪrˈvænə/| n.|涅槃，极乐世界，解脱|A state of perfect happiness and peace; a state of freedom from suffering, desire, and the cycle of rebirth (in Buddhism and Hinduism).|He achieved a state of nirvana through meditation. 他通过冥想达到了涅槃的境界。|

例句：
Many people seek nirvana, but few find it.
很多人追求涅槃，但很少有人能找到。

Nirvana is often described as a state of enlightenment.
涅槃常被描述为一种觉悟的状态。


近义词：paradise, heaven, bliss, enlightenment|
反义词:suffering, torment, anguish|
词性:noun|

Etymology: From Sanskrit निर्वाण (nirvāṇa), meaning "extinction, disappearance" (of the flames of desire, hatred, and delusion).
