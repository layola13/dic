misbehaver:/ˌmɪsbɪˈheɪvər/| n. |不良行为者；行为不端的人|A person who behaves badly, especially a child.|例句：The teacher sent the misbehaver to the principal's office. 老师把行为不端的学生送到了校长办公室。

例句：The flight attendant warned the misbehavers about their disruptive conduct.  乘务员警告了那些行为不端的人，让他们注意自己的扰乱行为。|近义词：delinquent, troublemaker, culprit, offender |反义词:well-behaved person, good child, angel|词性:noun
