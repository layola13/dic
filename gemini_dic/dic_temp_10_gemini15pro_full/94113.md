impeccable:/ɪmˈpekəbəl/| adj.|无可挑剔的；完美的|Incapable of sin or error; faultless.|Her impeccable taste in clothes was admired by everyone. 她的无可挑剔的衣着品味受到大家的赞赏。|近义词：flawless, perfect, immaculate, unblemished|反义词：flawed, imperfect, faulty, blemished|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin impeccabilis, from in- "not" + peccare "to sin," from Latin peccare "to stumble, sin."
