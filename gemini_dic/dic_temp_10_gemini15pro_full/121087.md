miscorrelation:/ˌmɪskɒrəˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 错误关联，误相关 | A miscorrelation is a spurious or misleading correlation between two variables, often due to a confounding factor or chance. | The study found a miscorrelation between shoe size and intelligence. 这项研究发现鞋码和智力之间存在错误关联。 | Miscorrelation between ice cream sales and crime rates doesn't imply causality, as both are influenced by hot weather. 冰淇淋销量和犯罪率之间的错误关联并不意味着因果关系，因为两者都受炎热天气的影响。 | 近义词: spurious correlation, false correlation | 反义词: true correlation, genuine correlation | 词性: noun
