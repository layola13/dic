alkalescence:/ˌælkəˈlɛsəns/
n.
碱性，碱度
the quality or state of being alkalescent
The alkalescence of the soil was tested.  土壤的碱性经过了测试。

近义词: alkalinity, basicity
反义词: acidity
词性: noun
