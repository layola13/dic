out-gloom: /ˌaʊtˈɡluːm/
Verb
使黯然失色；胜过…的忧郁
to be or become darker or more gloomy than: to surpass in gloominess
The storm clouds out-gloomed the already somber landscape.  暴风云使本来就阴沉的景色更加黯淡。
The poet's later works out-gloomed his earlier, more cheerful verses. 这位诗人后期的作品比他早期更欢快的诗句更加忧郁。
Synonyms: overshadow, darken, eclipse
Antonyms: brighten, lighten, cheer
词性: 动词
