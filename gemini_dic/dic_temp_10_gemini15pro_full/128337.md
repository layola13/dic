neutral:/ˈnuːtrəl/ (US) /ˈnjuːtrəl/ (UK)| adj. n.|中立的，中性的；中立者|Not supporting or helping either side in a conflict, disagreement, etc.; neither acid nor alkaline| Switzerland remained neutral during World War II. 瑞士在二战期间保持中立。|synonyms: impartial, unbiased, objective| antonyms: biased, prejudiced, partial| adjective

neutral:/ˈnuːtrəl/ (US) /ˈnjuːtrəl/ (UK)| n.|中立者，空挡|A person or country that does not support or help either side in a conflict, disagreement, etc.; a position of disengagement, especially in a vehicle's transmission|He acted as a neutral in the dispute. 他在争端中充当了中立者。|synonyms: noncombatant, mediator, arbitrator| antonyms: combatant, belligerent| noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French *neutre (modern French neutre), based on Latin neuter ‘neither’, from ne- ‘not’ + uter ‘either’.
