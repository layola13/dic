nondecisively:/ˌnɒndɪˈsaɪsɪvli/
adverb
优柔寡断地，非决定性地
in a way that is not decisive; hesitantly.
The committee voted nondecisively on the proposal. 委员会对这项提议进行了优柔寡断的投票。
He acted nondecisively in the crisis. 他在危机中行动优柔寡断。
近义词:indecisively, hesitantly, tentatively, irresolutely
反义词:decisively, resolutely, firmly
词性:adverb

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + decisively (adverb form of decisive, from Latin decidere "to decide")
