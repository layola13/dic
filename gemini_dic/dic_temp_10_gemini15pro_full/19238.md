bhagat:/ˈbʌɡət/| n.|信徒，奉献者|A bhagat is a devout follower or devotee, particularly in Sikhism and Hinduism, often referring to a person who has achieved a high level of spiritual understanding.  It can also refer to a poet or saint who composed devotional hymns.|Guru Nanak included the hymns of several bhagats, regardless of their religious background, in the Guru Granth Sahib.  纳纳克大师将几位奉献者的赞美诗，不论其宗教背景，都收录在锡克教圣典《古鲁·格兰特·萨希布》中。|devotee, disciple, follower|critic, opponent|noun
