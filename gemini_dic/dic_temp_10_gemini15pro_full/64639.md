erythrocyte: /ɪˈrɪθrəsaɪt/ | n. |红细胞，红血球 |A red blood cell that (in humans) is typically a biconcave disc without a nucleus. Erythrocytes contain the pigment hemoglobin, which imparts the red color to blood, and transport oxygen and carbon dioxide to and from the tissues.|  The doctor checked the patient's erythrocyte count. 医生检查了病人的红细胞计数。| synonym: red blood cell | antonym: None | noun
