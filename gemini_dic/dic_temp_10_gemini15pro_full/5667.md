altostratus:/ˌæltoʊˈstrɑːtəs/ /ˌaltoʊˈstreɪtəs/| n. |高层云|A middle-altitude cloud genus belonging to the stratiform physical category characterized by a generally uniform gray to bluish sheet or layer. Altostratus often covers the entire sky and may be thick enough to obscure the sun or moon, giving it a blurred appearance.|The altostratus clouds made the day seem gloomy.|近义词:cloud cover|反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly clear sky)|词性:noun
