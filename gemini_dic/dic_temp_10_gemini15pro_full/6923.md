ancillary: /ænˈsɪləri/ | adj. n. |辅助的；附属的；辅助人员；附属设备|Something that is ancillary is less important than the main thing you are considering, but is connected with it in some way. | The company hopes to boost its sales through ancillary products. 该公司希望通过辅助产品来提高销售额。|  The ancillary staff of a hospital includes cleaners, cooks, and porters. 医院的辅助人员包括清洁工、厨师和搬运工。 | synonyms: auxiliary, supplementary, subordinate | antonyms: primary, main, principal | adjective

ancillary: /ænˈsɪləri/ | n. | 辅助人员；附属设备 | A person or thing providing necessary support to the primary activities of an organization, institution, industry, or system.|  The factory has a small staff of ancillaries.  这家工厂有一些辅助人员。 | The new aircraft features state-of-the-art ancillaries. 这架新飞机配备了最先进的辅助设备。 | synonyms: auxiliary, supplement, accessory | antonyms: core, essential, fundamental | noun
