fanned:/fænd/
v.|扇风的，煽动的，散开的|To move air with a fan or something flat.  To spread something out like a fan.|She fanned herself with a newspaper. 她用报纸给自己扇风。|近义词：spread, waved, dispersed|反义词:concentrated, gathered|词性:动词

fanned:/fænd/
adj.|扇形的，展开的|Spread out like a fan.|The peacock fanned its tail.  孔雀展开了它的尾巴。|近义词：spread, expanded, outstretched|反义词:folded, closed|词性:形容词
