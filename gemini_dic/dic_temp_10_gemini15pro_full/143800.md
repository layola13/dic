outorganize:/ˌaʊtˈɔːrɡənaɪz/
verb
胜过…的组织能力，组织得比…更好
to organize more effectively than someone or something else.
例句：They completely outorganized their opponents and won the election easily. 他们组织能力远胜对手，轻松赢得了选举。
例句：She outorganized the entire department, streamlining processes and improving efficiency. 她把整个部门的组织工作做得井井有条，简化了流程，提高了效率。
近义词：outmaneuver, surpass, outperform
反义词：disorganize, mismanage
词性：v.
