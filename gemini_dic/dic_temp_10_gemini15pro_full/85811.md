haze:/heɪz/| n. v.| 薄雾，霾; 模糊，迷糊 |A thin mist or smoke in the air that makes it difficult to see.|The morning haze obscured the mountains.|近义词：mist, fog, smog|反义词: clarity, clearness|名词
haze:/heɪz/| v.|使模糊，使迷糊;戏弄，欺侮|To become hazy or to make something hazy.|The heat hazed the desert landscape.|近义词：obscure, confuse|反义词: clarify, clear|动词
