acinetae: /æsɪˈniːtiː/
noun
固着期，不活动的纤毛虫
The acinetae are a stage in the life cycle of certain ciliates, characterized by a sessile, stalked form.  这些纤毛虫在其生命周期的某一阶段会呈现固着、带柄的形态，被称为固着期。
Under adverse conditions, the suctorian ciliate can transform into an acinetae. 在不利条件下，吸管虫纤毛虫可以转变为固着期。
The acinetae stage allows the ciliate to survive harsh conditions. 固着期使纤毛虫能够在恶劣的条件下生存。
synonyms: sessile stage, attached stage
antonyms: motile stage, free-swimming stage
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acinetes, plural of acineta (“grape-stone, berry”), from Greek akinētos (“motionless”), from a- (“not”) + kinein (“to move”).

USAGE->
The formation of acinetae is triggered by environmental stress. =  环境压力会触发固着期的形成。

The acinetae can remain dormant for extended periods. = 固着期可以保持休眠很长时间。
