departs:/dɪˈpɑːrts/| v.|离开，出发，起程| To leave, especially to start a journey.|The train departs from platform 4 at 10:30 am. 火车上午10:30从4号站台出发。|The flight departs for London at 2 pm.  航班下午2点起飞前往伦敦。|近义词：leave, go, set off, embark|反义词：arrive, return, come|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *departir* (Modern French *départir*), from Latin *dispartire* "to divide, distribute, separate, share," from *dis-* "apart, asunder" + *partire* "to divide, share, distribute," related to *pars* "part."
