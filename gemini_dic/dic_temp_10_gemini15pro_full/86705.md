helmed:/helmd/ | v. | 掌舵；领导；指挥 | To be in charge of something, like a ship or a project.  |例句：She helmed the company through a difficult period. 她带领公司度过了困难时期。

例句：The experienced captain helmed the ship through the storm.  经验丰富的船长掌舵带领船只度过了风暴。|近义词：lead, manage, direct, steer, command |反义词: follow |词性: 动词
