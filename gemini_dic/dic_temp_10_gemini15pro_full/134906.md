nonmuscular:/ˌnɒnˈmʌskjʊlə(r)/ |adj.|非肌肉的；无肌肉的|Not involving or relating to muscles.  Lacking muscular strength or development.|例句：The jellyfish's nonmuscular body allows it to drift effortlessly through the ocean. 水母无肌肉的身体使它能够毫不费力地在海洋中漂流。|例句：Nonmuscular movement, like the beating of cilia, can also transport substances within the body.  像纤毛的跳动这样的非肌肉运动也可以在体内输送物质。|近义词：muscleless, flabby, weak|反义词：muscular, strong, robust|词性:adjective

Etymology->
non-:  a prefix meaning "not," from Latin *non*.
muscular: from Late Latin *muscularis*, from Latin *musculus* "muscle" (literally "little mouse").
