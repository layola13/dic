hypotyposis:/haɪpəˈtaɪpəsɪs/ /hɪpɑːˈtɑɪpəsɪs/
n.
生动的描述，逼真的描绘，活灵活现的描写
A vivid, graphic, or detailed description of a scene or event, often using imagery and figurative language to create a sense of immediacy or presence.
The author's hypotyposis of the battle scene made it feel as though the reader were right there in the thick of things. 作者对战斗场景的逼真描写让读者感觉自己身临其境。
近义词: description, depiction, portrayal, representation
反义词:  summary, abstract, synopsis
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin hypotyposis, from Ancient Greek ὑποτύπωσις (hupotúpōsis, “sketch, outline, pattern, example”), from ὑποτυπόω (hupotupoó, “to sketch, outline”), from ὑπό (hupó, “under”) + τυπόω (tupóō, “to form, shape, impress, stamp”).
