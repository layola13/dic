discriminatively:/dɪˌskrɪmɪnətɪvli/
| adv. |区别地，有辨别地|In a way that shows an ability to recognize small differences between things or people.  Showing good judgment or taste.|She listened discriminatively to the different musical instruments. 她仔细聆听，辨别不同的乐器。|近义词：selectively, judiciously, discerningly|反义词：indiscriminately, randomly|词性: 副词

词源: From the Latin word "discriminare," meaning "to distinguish between," which comes from "discrimen," meaning "separation" or "distinction."
