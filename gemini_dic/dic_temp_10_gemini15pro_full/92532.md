例句：She's been quite hyppish lately, jumping at the slightest sound. 她最近很神经质，一点声音就吓一跳。|近义词：nervous, jittery, jumpy, skittish, excitable, restive|反义词：calm, relaxed, tranquil, composed|词性:形容词
