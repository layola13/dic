overking:/ˈoʊvərˌkɪŋ/| n.|僭位者，篡位者|A person who usurps the throne or authority of a king.|例句：The overking ruled with an iron fist, ignoring the rightful heir.  这个僭位者用铁腕统治，无视了合法继承人。

例句：The overking was eventually overthrown and exiled.  这个篡位者最终被推翻并流放了。|近义词：usurper, pretender|反义词: rightful king, legitimate ruler|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From 'over' (meaning "above, exceeding") + 'king' (meaning "ruler").  Indicates someone who has taken the position of king illegitimately, placing themselves "over" the rightful king.
