groundouts: /ˈɡraʊndˌaʊts/ | n. | 接地球出局；滚地球出局 | A play in baseball where the batter hits a ground ball and is put out before reaching first base. | The pitcher induced a weak groundout to end the inning. 投手使击球手击出一个软弱的滚地球，结束了这一局。|  The shortstop fielded the groundout and threw to first for the out. 游击手接住滚地球，然后扔给一垒手出局。 | 近义词: ground ball, grounder | 反义词: flyout, home run | 词性: 名词

词源:  Compound of "ground" and "out". "Ground" refers to the ball hitting the ground, and "out" signifies the batter being put out.
