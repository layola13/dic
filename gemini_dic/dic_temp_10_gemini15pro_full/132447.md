nondepositors:/ˌnɑːndɪˈpɑːzɪtərz/ | n. | 非存款人；未开户者 | People who do not have a deposit account at a particular bank or financial institution. |  Banks often have different fee structures for depositors and nondepositors. 银行通常对存款人和非存款人有不同的收费结构。 |  Nondepositors may have to pay a fee to cash a check. 非存款人可能需要支付费用才能兑现支票。 |  non-customers, non-account holders | depositors, customers, account holders | noun
