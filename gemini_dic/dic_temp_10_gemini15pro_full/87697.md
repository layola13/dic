hexandrian:/hɛkˈsændriən/
adjective
六雄蕊的；属于六雄蕊纲的
Having six stamens in the flower.  Relating to or denoting plants of the class Hexandria (now superseded), which have six stamens.
例句：The hexandrian flower has six stamens.  这朵六雄蕊的花有六个雄蕊。
例句：The botanist categorized the plant as hexandrian.  植物学家将这种植物归类为六雄蕊植物。
近义词：six-stamened
反义词：(No specific antonym, as it's a descriptive term.  Perhaps 'polyandrous' (having many stamens) could be considered a contrasting term, but not a true opposite.)
词性：adjective
