diphthongous:/dɪfˈθɒŋɡəs/ /daɪˈθɔːŋɡəs/
adjective
双元音的，包含双元音的
Containing a diphthong; characterized by diphthongs.
The word "loud" has a diphthongous sound.  "Loud" 这个单词有双元音。

diphthongal:/dɪfˈθɒŋɡəl/ /daɪˈθɔːŋɡəl/
adjective
双元音的
Relating to or characterized by a diphthong.
The pronunciation of "boil" is diphthongal. "Boil" 的发音是双元音的。


近义词:diphthongal
反义词:monophthongal
