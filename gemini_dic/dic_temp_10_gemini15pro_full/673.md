ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ab* (from, away from) + *solvere* (to loosen, detach, free, release). Originally referring to the act of absolving from sin or guilt.  The mathematical meaning derives from the idea of "freeing" a number from its sign.  "Abs" as short for abdominals is a back-formation.
