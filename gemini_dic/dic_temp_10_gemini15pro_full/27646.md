candet: /ˈkændɪt/
n. | 糖果; 冰糖 | A crystallized sugar, often flavored or colored. | The child clutched a handful of brightly colored candets. 孩子抓着一把颜色鲜艳的冰糖。| synonym: candy, sweet, confection | antonym: none | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *candi, from Arabic *qandi, ultimately from Sanskrit *khaṇḍa (“piece, broken sugar”).  Relates to "candy".
