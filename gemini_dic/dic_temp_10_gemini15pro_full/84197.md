half-reclining:/ˌhɑːf rɪˈklaɪnɪŋ/
adjective
半躺的，半斜倚的
partly lying down; in a position between sitting and lying
She was sitting in a half-reclining position on the chaise lounge. 她斜倚在躺椅上。
Near Antonyms:  upright, erect, standing
Near Antonyms:  prone, supine, lying
adjective
