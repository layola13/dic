committals: /kəˈmɪt(ə)lz/
noun
承诺，保证；收监，监禁；委托，托付
The act of committing someone or something to a particular place, course of action, or condition.
The judge ordered the committal of the defendant to prison. 法官下令将被告收监入狱。
The government made a firm committal to reducing carbon emissions. 政府坚定承诺减少碳排放。
synonyms: commitments, consignments, confinements
antonyms: releases, discharges, freedoms
n.
