adaptableness:/əˌdæptəˈbɪlnəs/
n.
适应性，适应能力
The quality of being able to adjust to new conditions.
Chameleons are known for their adaptableness to different environments. 变色龙以其对不同环境的适应能力而闻名。
Her adaptableness allowed her to thrive in a new country. 她的适应能力使她能够在一个新的国家茁壮成长。

近义词:adaptability, flexibility, versatility
反义词:inflexibility, rigidity, stubbornness
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From adapt + -able + -ness.  Adapt comes from the Late Latin *adaptare, from ad- 'to' + aptare 'to fit'.

USAGE->
Her remarkable adaptableness was key to her success. = 她非凡的适应能力是她成功的关键。
The adaptableness of the design allows for future modifications. = 该设计的适应性允许将来的修改。


PHRASE->
the adaptableness of something = 某物的适应性
demonstrate adaptableness = 展现适应性
improve adaptableness = 提高适应性
lack of adaptableness = 缺乏适应性
adaptableness to change = 对变化的适应性
