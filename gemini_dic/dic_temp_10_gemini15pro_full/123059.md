moneth:/ˈmʌnθ/ | n. | 月,月份 (古体或方言) | An archaic or dialectal word for "month." |  In the moneth of May, the flowers bloomed. 五月里，鲜花盛开。 | 近义词: month | 反义词: (无直接反义词) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *moneth*, from Old English *mōnaþ*, from Proto-Germanic *mēnōþs*,  ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *mēnsis* ("moon, month").  The word is cognate with German *Monat*, Dutch *maand*, and Latin *mensis*.  The connection to "moon" reflects the lunar cycle's influence on early calendars.
