overdeliberate: /ˌoʊvərdiˈlɪbəreɪt/
verb
过度深思熟虑；过分斟酌
To think about or consider something too carefully or for too long, often resulting in inaction or delay.
He overdeliberated the decision and missed the deadline. 他过度斟酌这个决定，错过了截止日期。
The committee overdeliberated the issue and failed to reach a conclusion. 委员会对这个问题过度讨论，未能达成结论。
近义词: overthink, ponder excessively, dwell on, ruminate
反义词: act impulsively, be decisive, be rash
