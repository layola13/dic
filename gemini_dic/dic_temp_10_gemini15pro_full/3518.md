afterwards:/ˈæftərwərdz/
adverb
后来，以后
at a later time; subsequently
例句：We had dinner and afterwards we went to the cinema. 我们吃了晚饭，后来去看电影了。

例句：I'll speak to you afterwards. 我稍后会和你谈谈。

近义词：later, subsequently, thereafter
反义词：beforehand, previously
词性：副词
