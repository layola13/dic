awaiter:/əˈweɪtər/| n.|等待者; 异步等待器|Someone or something that waits for something. Specifically in programming, a mechanism that allows asynchronous operations to be paused until a result is available. |例句：The awaiter patiently waited for the bus. 等待者耐心地等待着公共汽车。

例句：In C#, the `await` keyword requires an awaiter. 在 C# 中，`await` 关键字需要一个异步等待器。|近义词：waiter, anticipator|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
