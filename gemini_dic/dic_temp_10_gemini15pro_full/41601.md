corpuscle:/ˈkɔːrpəsl/ (US) /ˈkɔːpəsl/ (UK)| n. |微粒；小体；血细胞|A minute particle of matter, especially a blood cell, or a cell resembling a blood cell, such as a lymph cell.|Red and white corpuscles are important components of blood. 红细胞和白细胞是血液的重要组成部分。|The scientist examined the corpuscles under a microscope. 科学家在显微镜下检查了这些微粒。|synonyms: cell, particle, granule|antonyms: N/A|noun
