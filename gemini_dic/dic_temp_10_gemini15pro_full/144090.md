outsainting:/ˌaʊtˈseɪntɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的圣洁；在圣洁方面超过
acting more virtuously or self-righteously than someone else.
He was trying to outsaint her by visiting the sick every day. 他试图通过每天探望病人来在圣洁方面超过她。
Synonyms: outdo, surpass, excel, exceed
Antonyms:  lag behind, fall short of
词性: 动词
