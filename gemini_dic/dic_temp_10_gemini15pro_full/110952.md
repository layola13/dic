lipsynching:/ˈlɪpˌsɪŋkɪŋ/
n.|对嘴；假唱；|The performance was just lipsynching. 表演只是对嘴而已。|
|Mimicking the words of a song as if one were actually singing. 模仿歌曲的歌词，就好像真的在唱歌一样. |The singer was caught lipsynching during the concert.  这位歌手在演唱会期间被发现假唱。|
近义词:miming, lip-syncing|
反义词:singing live|
词性:noun (名词)


lipsynch:/ˈlɪpˌsɪŋk/
v. |对嘴；假唱|She lipsynched to the song on the radio. 她跟着收音机里的歌曲对嘴假唱。|
|To pretend to sing a song by moving one's lips in synchronization with a prerecorded vocal track. 通过将嘴唇与预先录制的音轨同步移动来假装唱歌。|He lipsynched the national anthem at the game. 他在比赛中对嘴假唱国歌。|
近义词:mime, lip-sync|
反义词:sing live|
词性:verb (动词)

词源: lip (嘴唇) + synchronize (同步) -> lipsynchronize (嘴唇同步) -> lipsynch (对嘴；假唱) -> lipsynching (对嘴；假唱)
