nonpromulgation:/ˌnɒnprɒməlˈɡeɪʃən/
n.
未公布；未颁布
The nonpromulgation of the new law led to confusion. 新法律的未颁布导致了混乱。
The nonpromulgation of the test results angered the students. 考试结果的未公布激怒了学生们。
non-disclosure, suppression, withholding
promulgation, publication, announcement

Etymology:
From non- + promulgation.  Promulgation comes from Late Latin prōmulgātiō, from Latin prōmulgāre (“to publish, make known”), from prō- (“forth, out”) + mulgāre (“to milk, spread abroad”)
