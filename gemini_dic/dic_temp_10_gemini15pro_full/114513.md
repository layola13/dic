maladroitly:/ˌmæləˈdrɔɪtli/ | adv. | 笨拙地；不灵巧地 | in a clumsy or awkward way; lacking skill or dexterity | He maladroitly knocked over a vase while trying to impress the guests. 他笨手笨脚地打翻了一个花瓶，本来是想给客人留下好印象的。| synonyms: clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly, ungainly | antonyms: skillfully, deftly, adroitly, gracefully | adverb

maladroit:/ˌmæləˈdrɔɪt/ | adj. | 笨拙的；不灵巧的 | lacking skill or dexterity; clumsy or awkward | His maladroit attempts at dancing amused the onlookers.  他笨拙的舞姿逗笑了旁观者。| synonyms: clumsy, awkward, inept, ungainly | antonyms: skillful, deft, adroit, graceful | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *maladroit*, from *mal* (badly) + *adroit* (skillful).  The word *adroit* ultimately comes from Latin *directus* (straight).
