devaluation's:/ˌdiːvæljuˈeɪʃənz/| n.|贬值（devaluation的名词所有格）|The devaluation's impact on the economy was significant. 这次贬值对经济的影响很大。|例句：The government's response to the devaluation's effects was swift. 政府对贬值的影响反应迅速。 The devaluation's consequences were felt most acutely by importers. 贬值的后果对进口商的影响最为严重。|近义词：depreciation's, decline's, reduction's|反义词: appreciation's, increase's|词性:名词所有格

词源：From de- + value + -ation + -'s.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "down" or "away from."  "Value" refers to worth. "-ation" is a suffix forming nouns of action or process. "-'s" indicates possession.  Therefore, "devaluation's" means "belonging to the act of reducing value."
