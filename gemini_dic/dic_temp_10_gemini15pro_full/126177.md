mutation:/mjuːˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 突变，变异 | A change in the DNA sequence of an organism. |  The mutation caused the plant to grow taller than normal.  这种突变导致植物比正常情况下长得更高。 | synonym: alteration, variation, modification | antonym: stability, constancy | noun

mutation:/mjuːˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 变异，变化（状态或形式）|  A significant change in the nature, form, or quality of something. | There was a sudden mutation in his voice. 他的声音突然变了。 | synonym: transformation, metamorphosis | antonym: stagnation | noun
