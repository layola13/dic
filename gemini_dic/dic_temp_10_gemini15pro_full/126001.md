muslin: /ˈmʌzlɪn/ (US), /ˈmʌzlɪn/ (UK)| n. |平纹细布，棉布|A finely woven cotton fabric, often used for clothing, curtains, and upholstery.|She wore a light muslin dress. 她穿着一件轻薄的棉布连衣裙。|The curtains were made of a delicate muslin. 窗帘是用精致的平纹细布做的。|近义词: calico, gauze, voile |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps heavy cloth, canvas)|词性: noun
