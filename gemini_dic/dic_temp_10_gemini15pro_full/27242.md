caloricity:/ˌkæləˈrɪsɪti/ | n. | 热值，发热量，热容量 | The caloricity of coal is a measure of the energy it contains.  煤的热值是其能量含量的度量。| Example: The caloricity of this fuel is very high, making it efficient for power generation. 这种燃料的热值很高，使其能够有效地发电。| Synonym:  heating value, thermal value, calorific value, fuel value | Antonym:  N/A | noun
