compacter:/kəmˈpæktər/ (US), /kəmˈpæktə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 压实机，夯土机|A machine that compresses something into a smaller volume, especially refuse or soil.|The compacter reduced the volume of garbage significantly.  这台压实机显著减少了垃圾的体积。|synonyms: compressor, press, rammer|antonyms: expander, inflater|noun

compacter:/kəmˈpæktər/ (US), /kəmˈpæktə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 紧凑型|Something that is designed to be smaller or more compact than usual, especially a car or a piece of electronic equipment.|She drives a fuel-efficient compacter. 她开一辆省油的小型车。|synonyms: subcompact, mini, small car|antonyms: large car, SUV, truck|noun
