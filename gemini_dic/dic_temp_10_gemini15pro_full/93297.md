ill:/ɪl/ | adj. adv.| 生病的，不舒服的；坏的，邪恶的 |  Not in good health; harmful, wicked. | She felt ill after eating the spoiled food. 她吃了变质的食物后感到不舒服。 | 近义词: sick, unwell, poorly, bad, evil | 反义词: well, healthy, good | 词性: 形容词, 副词

ill:/ɪl/ | adv. | 坏地，恶劣地；不利地 | Badly or poorly; in an unfavorable way. | The trip was ill-timed. 这次旅行的时间安排很糟糕。 | 近义词: badly, poorly, unfavorably | 反义词: well, favorably | 词性: 副词


词源: From Old English *ill, *yfel (adjective), of Germanic origin; related to Dutch euvel and German übel.
