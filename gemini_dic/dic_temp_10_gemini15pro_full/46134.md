cyme: /saɪm/ | n. | 聚伞花序 | A flower cluster in which the main stem ends in a flower, and any side stems also end in flowers, the central flower typically opening first. | The elder tree has a flat-topped cyme. 接骨木有一个平顶的聚伞花序。 |  synonym: inflorescence | antonym: N/A | noun

cyme: /saɪm/ | n. | 聚伞花序 | A flat-topped or domed inflorescence in which the main axis terminates in a flower and subsequent flowers develop from lateral buds progressively lower down the axis. | The gardener pointed out the cyme on the hydrangea bush. 园丁指出了绣球花丛上的聚伞花序。 | synonym: flower cluster | antonym: raceme | noun
