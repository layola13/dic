impacted:/ɪmˈpæktɪd/| adj.|受到影响的；压紧的；嵌入的|Affected, influenced, or struck with force; wedged or packed tightly.|

例句：The impacted wisdom tooth was causing a lot of pain. 那颗嵌入的智齿引起了剧烈的疼痛。

例句：The new regulations have impacted businesses both large and small. 新的规章制度对大小企业都产生了影响。


近义词：affected, influenced, wedged, embedded, compact

反义词：unaffected, loose

词性: adjective


impacted:/ɪmˈpæktɪd/| v.| 冲击，撞击；产生影响| To hit with force; to have a strong effect on something or someone.|

例句：The meteorite impacted the Earth, creating a huge crater. 陨石撞击了地球，形成了一个巨大的陨石坑。

例句：The economic downturn has impacted our sales figures. 经济衰退影响了我们的销售额。

近义词：hit, strike, affect, influence

反义词：miss (for hitting), help (for affecting)

词性: verb (past participle)


词源: From the Latin word "impactus," the past participle of "impingere," meaning "to drive or strike into."
