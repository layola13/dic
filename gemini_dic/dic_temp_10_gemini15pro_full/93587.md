imbalanced:/ɪmˈbælənst/ | adj. |不平衡的，失衡的 | Lacking balance or symmetry; disproportionate.  |  The ecosystem is imbalanced due to the extinction of several key species.  生态系统由于几个关键物种的灭绝而失衡。| synonyms: unequal, uneven, lopsided, asymmetrical, disproportionate | antonyms: balanced, equal, even, symmetrical, proportionate | adjective

Etymology->
From in- (not) + balanced (being in a state of equilibrium).  The word "balance" ultimately comes from the Latin "bilanx" (having two scales), from "bi-" (two) + "lanx" (plate, dish, scale).
