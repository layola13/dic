avoidances:/əˈvɔɪdənsɪz/
noun
避免；回避；逃避
the act of avoiding something or someone.
He was criticized for his avoidance of the issue. 他因回避这个问题而受到批评。
Synonyms: evasion, dodging, shunning
Antonyms: confrontation, engagement, acceptance
词性: 名词
