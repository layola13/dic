gentiopicrin:/ˌdʒɛnti.oʊˈpɪkrɪn/ | n. | 龙胆苦苷 | A bitter glycoside, C16H20O9, obtained from the gentian root and used as a tonic and stomachic.  It is the primary bitter principle of the gentian root. | Gentiopicrin is responsible for the bitter taste of gentian root extracts. 龙胆苦苷是龙胆根提取物苦味的原因。 | Gentiopicrin stimulates appetite and aids digestion. 龙胆苦苷能刺激食欲，帮助消化。 | 近义词: amarogentin (another bitter compound found in gentian) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider substances that induce sweetness or suppress bitterness) | 词性: noun
