gaieties:/ˈɡeɪətiz/| n. |欢乐，庆祝活动，娱乐|A state of joyful activity and merriment, especially festive celebrations or entertainment.|The holiday season was filled with gaieties and laughter. 假期充满了欢乐和笑声。|近义词：festivities, celebrations, merrymaking, revelries|反义词：gloom, sadness, sorrow|noun

Etymology:
From Old French "gaiete" meaning joy, mirth, from "gai" meaning joyful, merry.  Ultimately from Frankish "*gahi," related to Old High German "gāhi" (quick, sudden).
