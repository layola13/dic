chronostratigraphic: /ˌkrɒnəʊˌstrætɪˈɡræfɪk/ /ˌkroʊnoʊˌstrætəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. |  年代地层学的 | Relating to chronostratigraphy, the branch of geology concerned with the relative ages of rock strata and their correlation in time. | The chronostratigraphic chart shows the geological timescale. 年代地层图表显示了地质时间尺度。|  The geologist used chronostratigraphic methods to date the fossils. 地质学家使用年代地层学方法来确定化石的年代。| 近义词: time-stratigraphic | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From chrono- (time) + stratigraphic (relating to strata).  Chrono- comes from Greek khronos "time".  Stratigraphic comes from Latin stratum "layer" + Greek graphikos "relating to writing or drawing".
