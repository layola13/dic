frequency:/ˈfriːkwənsi/ (US) /ˈfriːkwənsi/ (UK)| n.|频率，发生率，频繁|The frequency of earthquakes in the region has increased. 该地区地震的发生频率增加了。|例句：The radio station broadcasts at a frequency of 98.5 MHz.  这家广播电台以98.5兆赫的频率播送。

例句：The frequency of his visits decreased over time. 他来访的频率随着时间的推移而减少了。|近义词：rate, occurrence, incidence, regularity|反义词:infrequency, rarity|词性:名词
