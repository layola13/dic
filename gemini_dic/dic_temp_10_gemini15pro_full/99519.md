interfuse:/ˌɪntərˈfjuːz/| v. |混合，融合，弥漫|To blend or mix together; to permeate.|例句：The golden light interfused the landscape. 金色的光芒弥漫着整个风景。|例句：Her joy interfused with a touch of sadness. 她的喜悦中夹杂着一丝悲伤。|近义词：blend, merge, mingle, permeate, suffuse|反义词：separate, divide|词性：动词

Etymology:
From Latin *interfundere*, from inter- (“between, among, amid”) + fundere (“to pour, melt, fuse”).
