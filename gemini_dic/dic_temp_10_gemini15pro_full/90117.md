hot-swappable:/ˌhɒt ˈswɒpəbəl/ | adj. | 热插拔的 | Able to be removed and replaced while the system is running without interrupting operation. | Example: The server features hot-swappable hard drives for maximum uptime.  服务器配备了可热插拔的硬盘，以最大限度地延长正常运行时间。| Example:  Hot-swappable power supplies are essential for critical systems. 对于关键系统来说，可热插拔的电源至关重要。| 近义词:plug-and-play, replaceable | 反义词:fixed, non-removable |词性:形容词

hot-swap:/ˌhɒt ˈswɒp/ | n. v. | 热插拔 | The act of removing and replacing a component while the system is running. | Example: The technician performed a hot-swap of the failed hard drive. 技术人员对出现故障的硬盘进行了热插拔。| Example: We can hot-swap these components without powering down the server.我们可以对这些组件进行热插拔，而无需关闭服务器电源。 | 近义词:plug and play, replace | 反义词:cold swap | 词性:名词,动词
