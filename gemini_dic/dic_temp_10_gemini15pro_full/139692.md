Oceania:/ˌoʊʃiˈɑːniə/ /ˌəʊʃɪˈeɪnɪə/ | n. |大洋洲|A geographic region comprising Melanesia, Micronesia, Polynesia, and sometimes Australasia, and conventionally, but not always, including the continent of Australia.  It is surrounded by the Pacific and Indian Oceans.|例句：Oceania is a vast and diverse region. 大洋洲是一个广阔而多元化的地区。|例句：Many unique cultures thrive in Oceania. 许多独特的文化在大洋洲繁荣发展。|近义词：Australasia, the Pacific Islands|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology:
From French *Océanie*, from Latin *Oceanus*, from Ancient Greek *Ὠκεανός (Ōkeanós)*.
