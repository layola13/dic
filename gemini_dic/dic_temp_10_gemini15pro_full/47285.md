de-deputized:/diːˈdɛpjʊtaɪzd/
|adj.|被解除副职的，被取消代理权的|Removed from the position of deputy or acting in a deputy capacity.  The prefix "de-" indicates removal or reversal of a status or action. "Deputized" means appointed as a deputy. Thus, "de-deputized" means having that appointment revoked.|The sheriff de-deputized him after the incident.  该事件发生后，警长取消了他的副警长职务。|stripped of authority, dismissed, discharged|authorized, appointed|adjective
