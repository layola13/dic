deploring:/dɪˈplɔːrɪŋ/
verb
表示悲痛；强烈反对
expressing strong disapproval of something
The report deplored the lack of progress in tackling poverty. 该报告对扶贫工作缺乏进展表示遗憾。
The president deplored the violence and called for calm. 总统对暴力行为表示谴责，并呼吁保持冷静。
synonyms: lamenting, bemoaning, criticizing, condemning
antonyms: praising, commending, approving, supporting
