anticlassicalist:/ˌæntiˈklæsɪkəlɪst/| n. adj.|反古典主义者，反古典主义的|Someone who opposes or rejects classicism, especially in art, literature, or music.  A person who believes that the classical traditions or styles are outdated or no longer relevant.| The anticlassicalist movement gained traction in the early 20th century.  反古典主义运动在20世纪初获得了关注。  His anticlassicalist stance made him unpopular with traditional academics. 他的反古典主义立场使他不受传统学者的欢迎。|近义词：modernist, iconoclast, avant-garde|反义词:classicist, traditionalist|词性:noun, adjective
