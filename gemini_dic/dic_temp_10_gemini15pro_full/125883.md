muscogees:/məsˈkəʊɡiːz/
|n.|马斯科吉人；克里克族人|A member of a North American Indian people formerly inhabiting much of Georgia, Alabama, and Mississippi.  They now live mainly in Oklahoma.|The Muscogees were known for their complex social structure.|近义词:Creek, Muskogee|反义词:None|noun
