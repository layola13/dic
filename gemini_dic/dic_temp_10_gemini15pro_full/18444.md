bemaul: /bɪˈmɔːl/
verb
弄脏，玷污；粗暴地对待
to handle roughly or clumsily; to paw or maul

He bemauled the delicate silk fabric with his greasy hands.
他用油腻的双手玷污了精致的丝绸面料。

The rowdy fans bemauled the visiting team's bus after the game.
比赛结束后，吵闹的球迷粗暴地对待了客队的巴士。

近义词: manhandle, maul, paw, soil, dirty
反义词: clean, respect, cherish

词性: verb
