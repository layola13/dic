eroteme: /ɪˈrɒtɪmiː/ /ɪˈroʊtɪmiː/ | n. |疑问句; 特别指修辞问句 |A question, especially one used for rhetorical effect. |"Is this a dagger which I see before me?" is a famous eroteme from Macbeth. |例句：The speaker used several erotes to emphasize his point. 演讲者用了几个修辞问句来强调他的观点。

例句：Her speech was punctuated with erotes, making it highly engaging. 她的演讲中穿插着修辞问句，使其引人入胜。|近义词：rhetorical question, interrogation, query |反义词: answer, reply, statement |词性: noun
