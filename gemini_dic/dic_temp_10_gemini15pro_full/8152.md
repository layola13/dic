anti-constitutional:/ˌæntiˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənəl/
adjective
违宪的，反宪法的
Opposing or violating the principles of a constitution.
The court ruled that the law was anti-constitutional. 法院裁定该法律违宪。
The group staged an anti-constitutional protest.  该团体组织了一场违宪抗议。
Synonyms:  unconstitutional, illegal, unlawful, illegitimate
Antonyms:  constitutional, legal, lawful, legitimate
Word origin: anti- (against) + constitutional (relating to a constitution)
