cols:/kɒlz/ | n. | 列; 栏; (报纸的) 专栏 | Columns are vertical sections in a table or document. | The newspaper has several cols dedicated to sports. 这家报纸有几个专门报道体育的专栏。 | synonyms: columns, sections, divisions | antonyms: rows | noun
