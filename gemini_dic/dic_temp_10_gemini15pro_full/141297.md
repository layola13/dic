open-jaw:/ˈoʊpənˌdʒɔː/| adj. n.|开口的，张开的；开口旅程|An open-jaw ticket, also known as a multi-destination ticket, is an airline ticket where the destination and/or the origin are not the same as the return journey.|I booked an open-jaw ticket, flying into Paris and out of Rome. 我订了一张开口机票，飞到巴黎，从罗马离开。|近义词：multi-destination, circle trip|反义词：round-trip|词性:形容词, 名词

open-jaw:/ˈoʊpənˌdʒɔː/| n.|开口旅程|A journey where the traveler arrives at one location and departs from another.|We planned an open-jaw trip, starting in London and ending in Amsterdam. 我们计划了一个开口旅程，从伦敦开始，在阿姆斯特丹结束。|近义词：multi-destination trip, circle trip|反义词：round-trip|词性:名词
