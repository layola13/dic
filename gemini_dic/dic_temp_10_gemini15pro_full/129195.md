nobilities:/noʊˈbɪlətiz/| n. |贵族, 高贵品质, 高尚行为|The qualities of high moral character, such as courage, generosity, and honor, often associated with the aristocracy.  It can also refer to the aristocratic class itself.|  He showed great nobilities in refusing the bribe. 他拒绝贿赂，表现出高贵的品质。|Synonyms: aristocracy, nobility, grandees, peerage, gentry, elite, upper class, high society, blue bloods, gentility, nobleness, greatness, dignity, magnificence, grandeur, splendor, majesty, loftiness, distinction, eminence, elevation, worthiness, merit, virtue, integrity, honor, uprightness, righteousness, morality, ethicalness, principle, rectitude, goodness, decency, respectability|Antonyms: baseness, ignobility, meanness, vulgarity, commonness, lowness|Noun
