nontherapeutic:/ˌnɒnθɛrəˈpjuːtɪk/
adjective
非治疗性的
Not designed or intended for therapeutic purposes; not contributing to recovery from illness or injury.
The doctor recommended discontinuing the nontherapeutic medication. 医生建议停止服用这种非治疗性药物。

The patient was advised to avoid nontherapeutic activities. 建议患者避免非治疗性的活动。

Synonym: non-curative, non-remedial, ineffective
Antonym: therapeutic, curative, remedial
word class: adjective
