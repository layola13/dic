handhold:/ˈhændˌhəʊld/| n.|支撑物，把手；帮助，支持|Something that can be grasped for support, especially when climbing; assistance or support given to someone.|例句：The climber reached for a handhold on the rock face. 登山者伸手去抓岩石表面上的一个支撑点。

例句：She needed a handhold during the difficult transition. 在艰难的过渡时期，她需要帮助。|近义词：support, assistance, grip, foothold|反义词:hindrance, obstacle|n.
