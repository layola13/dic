ice-bound:/ˈaɪsˌbaʊnd/ | adj. | 冰封的，被冰困住的 | Covered or blocked by ice; unable to move because of ice. | The ship was ice-bound in the Arctic for months. 这艘船在北极被冰封了数月。 |  The ice-bound harbor prevented ships from entering or leaving. 冰封的港口阻止了船只进出。 | 近义词: frozen, iced-in, ice-locked | 反义词: ice-free, thawed | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From ice + bound (past participle of bind), meaning "fastened by ice."
