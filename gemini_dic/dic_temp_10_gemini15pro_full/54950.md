dispraiser:/dɪˈspreɪzər/| n.|诽谤者，贬低者|Someone who dispraises; one who censures or blames.  A person who expresses disapproval of someone or something.|He was known as a dispraiser of modern art, finding little to admire in the current trends. 他以贬低现代艺术而闻名，觉得当前的潮流没有什么值得欣赏的。|近义词：critic, detractor, censurer|反义词: admirer, praiser, eulogist|词性:noun
词源: From dispraise + -er.  Dispraise itself comes from the Old French despreisier, from des- (expressing negation) + preisier "to prize."
