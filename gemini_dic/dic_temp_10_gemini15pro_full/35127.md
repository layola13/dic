cloudland:/ˈklaʊdlænd/| n. |云端，云海，空中楼阁，幻想的境界|A beautiful or imaginary place, especially one that is peaceful and pleasant, often associated with the sky or clouds.  It can also refer to a state of dreamy abstraction or unreality.|例句：The climbers emerged above the cloudland. 登山者出现在云端之上。|例句：He lived in a cloudland of wishful thinking. 他生活在充满幻想的空中楼阁里。|近义词：sky, heavens, paradise, dreamland|反义词:reality, earth|词性:noun
