multiplied:/ˈmʌltɪplaɪd/ |v. |乘以；增加；繁殖|Increased in number or quantity, especially greatly; the result of multiplying. |例句：The bacteria multiplied rapidly in the warm, moist environment. 细菌在温暖潮湿的环境中迅速繁殖。

例句：His anxieties were multiplied by the news. 这消息使他更加焦虑。|近义词：increased, amplified, augmented, expanded, multiplied, propagated |反义词：divided, decreased, reduced, diminished|词性：动词(Verb)


multiplied:/ˈmʌltɪplaɪd/ |adj. |增加的；乘过的|Describes something that has been increased in number or quantity, especially greatly, or that is the result of multiplying. |例句：The problems were multiplied by the lack of communication.  由于缺乏沟通，问题成倍增加。

例句：The company's profits were multiplied tenfold. 公司的利润增加了十倍。|近义词：increased, numerous|反义词：reduced, few|词性：形容词 (Adjective)



ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *multipled, *multiplide, from Latin multiplicātus, past participle of multiplicāre (“to multiply”), from multiplex (“manifold, many-folded”), from multus (“much, many”) + -plex (“-fold”).
