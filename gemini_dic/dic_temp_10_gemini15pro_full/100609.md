intimaters:/ˈɪntɪˌmeɪtərz/
n.
暗示者，透露者
People who communicate or suggest something subtly or indirectly.
The intimaters hinted at a possible merger, but nothing was officially announced.  暗示者暗示了可能进行的合并，但没有正式宣布。
近义词:  suggesters, hinters, alluders
反义词:  concealers, hiders
