ambidextrously:/ˌæmbɪˈdɛkstrəsli/| adv.| 双手灵巧地，左右开弓地 | Using both hands with equal skill.  Able to use the right and left hands equally well. |  He ambidextrously signed autographs with both hands. 他双手灵巧地签名，左右开弓。 | skillfully, adeptly, proficiently | clumsily, awkwardly | adverb
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ambidexter* (both right-handed), from *ambi-* (both) + *dexter* (right, skillful).
