equating:/ɪˈkweɪtɪŋ/
|v.|使相等；等同|The act of considering one thing to be the same as or equivalent to another thing.|例句：Equating wealth with happiness is a mistake.  把财富等同于幸福是错误的。|近义词：comparing, likening, paralleling, associating|反义词:differentiating, distinguishing|verb

equate:/ɪˈkweɪt/
|v.|使相等；等同|To consider one thing to be the same as or equivalent to another thing.|例句：He equates success with hard work. 他认为成功与努力工作是等同的。|近义词：compare, liken, parallel, associate|反义词:differentiate, distinguish|verb

equation:/ɪˈkweɪʒən/
|n.|方程式；等式；相等；同等|A mathematical statement that two expressions are equal, or the process of equating one thing with another.|例句：The equation 2x + 1 = 7 has the solution x = 3.  方程式2x + 1 = 7的解是x = 3。 |近义词：formula, equality, equivalence|反义词:inequality, difference|noun
