frownless:/ˈfraʊnləs/
adjective
没有皱眉的；无忧无虑的
Without a frown; cheerful and carefree.
Her frownless face radiated joy and contentment. 她那张没有皱眉的脸洋溢着喜悦和满足。
近义词: cheerful, happy, carefree, untroubled, serene
反义词: frowning, gloomy, sad, worried, anxious
词性: adjective

词源:
From frown + -less.  "Frown" comes from Old French *frogner "to wrinkle," of uncertain origin.  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without."
