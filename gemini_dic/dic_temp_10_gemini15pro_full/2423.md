adjournment: /əˈdʒɜːnmənt/ (US) /əˈdʒɜːnmənt/ (UK)| n. |休会，延期，暂停|A temporary postponement of a meeting or other gathering to a later date or time. |The judge called for an adjournment until the following morning.  法官宣布休庭至次日上午。

The meeting was adjourned due to lack of quorum. 由于人数不足，会议休会。|近义词：postponement, recess, suspension, break|反义词:continuance, resumption|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ajournement*, from ajourner "to summon to court, to put off to a certain day," from a- "to" + journ "day."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The adjournment gives us time to prepare. = 休会给了我们准备的时间。
The chairman calls for an adjournment of the meeting. = 主席要求休会。

一般过去时:
The adjournment lasted for several hours. = 休会持续了几个小时。
The judge announced the adjournment of the court. = 法官宣布休庭。

现在完成时:
The meeting has been adjourned until next week. = 会议已休会到下周。
The committee has voted for an adjournment. = 委员会已投票决定休会。

过去完成时:
The trial had been adjourned before the witness could testify. = 在证人可以作证之前，审判已经休庭。
The meeting had already been adjourned when I arrived. = 当我到达时，会议已经休会了。

将来时:
The meeting will be adjourned until further notice. = 会议将休会，直至另行通知。
The case will be adjourned if the defendant is not present. = 如果被告不出席，案件将延期审理。

虚拟语气:
If the meeting were adjourned, we could discuss the matter privately. = 如果会议休会，我们可以私下讨论这个问题。
I wish the debate were adjourned so we could have a break. = 我希望辩论休会，这样我们可以休息一下。

被动语态:
The meeting was adjourned by the chairman. = 会议被主席休会。
The trial has been adjourned until next month. = 审判已被延期到下个月。

疑问句:
When will the adjournment be? = 什么时候休会？
Why was the meeting adjourned? = 为什么会议休会？

否定句:
The meeting was not adjourned until late in the evening. = 会议直到深夜才休会。
The judge did not grant an adjournment. = 法官没有准予休庭。



PHRASE->
call for an adjournment = 要求休会
move for an adjournment = 提议休会
grant an adjournment = 批准休会
adjournment of the meeting = 会议休会
adjournment of the court = 休庭
