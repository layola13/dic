fitter:/ˈfɪtər/ (UK), /ˈfɪtər/ (US)| n.|装配工，钳工|A person who assembles or installs machinery or equipment, especially one who shapes or adjusts metal parts.|The fitter is installing the new air conditioning unit.  装配工正在安装新的空调设备。|The fitter adjusted the metal frame to fit perfectly. 钳工调整了金属框架以使其完美贴合。|近义词：mechanic, assembler, installer|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

fitter:/ˈfɪtər/ (UK), /ˈfɪtər/ (US)| adj. (comparative fitter, superlative fittest)|更健康的，更强壮的|In better physical condition; more healthy and strong.|After months of training, she was fitter than ever. 经过几个月的训练，她比以往任何时候都更健康。|He's become much fitter since he started cycling. 自从他开始骑自行车以来，他变得更健康了。|近义词：healthier, stronger, more robust|反义词:weaker, unhealthier, less fit|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fitt (“suitable, proper”) (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *fitiz (“fitting, appropriate”). Cognate with West Frisian fit (“appropriate”), Dutch fit (“clever, smart, quick”), German fit (“fit”), Icelandic fit (“fit”).  The noun sense of "one who fits" is from the verb.
