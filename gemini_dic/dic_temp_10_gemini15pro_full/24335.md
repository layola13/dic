brimfulness:/ˈbrɪmˈfʊlnəs/| n.|充满；盈满|Her eyes shone with brimfulness of joy. 她的眼睛里充满了喜悦的光芒。|近义词：fullness, abundance, overflowing|反义词：emptiness, lack, scarcity|Noun


Etymology:
From "brimful" + "-ness."  "Brimful" comes from "brim" (edge, rim) + "full" (completely filled).
