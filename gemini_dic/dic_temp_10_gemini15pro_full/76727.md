gamboled:/ˈɡæmbəld/ | v. | 嬉戏玩耍，雀跃 | To run or jump about playfully; to frolic. | The lambs gamboled in the meadow. 羊羔在草地上嬉戏玩耍。 | The children gamboled on the beach. 孩子们在沙滩上嬉戏玩耍。 | 近义词：frolic, romp, frisk, cavort, prance | 反义词：trudge, plod | 词性：动词

Etymology:
From the Old French *gamboler* "to leap," perhaps of Germanic origin.
