allude:/əˈlud/| v. |暗示，间接提到|To suggest or call attention to indirectly; hint at.|He alluded to problems with the new management but didn't say anything directly. 他暗示了新管理层的问题，但没有直接说什么。|The article alludes to the possibility of a merger. 这篇文章暗示了合并的可能性。|近义词：suggest, imply, hint, intimate, refer|反义词：state, declare, specify, explain|词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *alludere*, from *ad-* ("to, toward") + *ludere* ("to play").  Originally meant "to play with," "to joke," or "to mock." The sense of "to hint at" developed later.
