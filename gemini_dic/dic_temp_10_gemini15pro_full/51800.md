devoutly:/dɪˈvaʊtli/| adv.|虔诚地，衷心地|In a deeply religious or sincere manner.  Showing deep commitment and reverence.|She devoutly prayed for her son's safe return. 她虔诚地祈祷儿子平安归来。|例句：He devoutly believes in the power of prayer. 他虔诚地相信祈祷的力量。|近义词：religiously, piously, earnestly, sincerely|反义词：irreverently, impiously, insincerely|词性:副词
词源: From Old French *devot, from Latin devotus "given up by vow, devoted," past participle of devovere "dedicate by vow."
