incorruptibleness:/ˌɪnkəˈrʌptɪbəlnəs/
n.
 incorruptibility; inability to decay or be corrupted.
不可腐败性；廉洁；不朽
The incorruptibleness of his spirit impressed everyone. 他的精神廉洁给每个人都留下了深刻印象。
近义词: integrity, honesty, probity, uprightness, righteousness
反义词: corruptibility, dishonesty, venality


incorruptible:/ˌɪnkəˈrʌptɪbl/
adj.
 not susceptible to corruption or decay; honest or morally upright.
不可腐蚀的；不朽的；廉洁的；正直的
He was known for his incorruptible character. 他以廉洁的品格而闻名。
近义词: honest, upright, righteous, principled
反义词: corrupt, dishonest, venal
