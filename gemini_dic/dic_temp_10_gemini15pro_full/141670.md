optioned:/ˈɑːpʃənd/ | v. | 选择，拥有选择权 | to secure or grant an option on something, especially a film or book | The studio has optioned the rights to her novel.  这家制片厂已经获得了她小说的改编权。| 近义词: chosen, selected, secured, acquired | 反义词: released, relinquished | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From option + -ed.  Ultimately from Latin optio ("choice").
