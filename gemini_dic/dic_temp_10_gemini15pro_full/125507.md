multirooted:/ˌmʌltiˈruːtɪd/ | adj. | 多根的；多根化的 | Having multiple roots or root systems. |  The multirooted tree was able to withstand the strong winds. 这棵多根的树能够抵御强风。 | The mangrove's multirooted structure helps it thrive in the harsh coastal environment.  红树林的多根结构帮助它在恶劣的海岸环境中茁壮成长。 |  deep-rooted, fibrous-rooted | shallow-rooted | adjective
