eversive:/ɪˈvɜːsɪv/
adjective
颠覆性的，破坏性的
tending or seeking to overthrow, subvert, or destroy an established order, institution, or authority.
The government accused the group of engaging in eversive activities. 政府指控该组织从事颠覆活动。
The book was banned for its eversive content. 这本书因其颠覆性的内容而被禁。
Synonyms: subversive, revolutionary, rebellious, seditious, insurrectionary
Antonyms: supportive, loyal, constructive, stabilizing
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin eversus (past participle of evertere, "to overthrow"), from ex- ("out, from") + vertere ("to turn").
