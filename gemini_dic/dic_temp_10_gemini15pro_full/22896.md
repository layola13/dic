botero: /boʊˈtɛroʊ/
Noun
波特罗（指Fernando Botero，哥伦比亚艺术家）
Referring to Fernando Botero, a Colombian figurative artist known for his depictions of plump figures and subjects.
Botero's paintings are easily recognizable. 波特罗的画作很容易辨认。
Synonym:  Fernando Botero (when referring to the artist himself)
Antonym: N/A
词性: Noun


botero: /boʊˈtɛroʊ/
Adjective
波特罗风格的
Characteristic of the artistic style of Fernando Botero, typically featuring rounded, voluminous figures.
The sculptures had a distinctly Boteroesque quality. 这些雕塑具有明显的波特罗风格。
Synonym:  plump, voluminous, rounded (when describing the artistic style)
Antonym: slender, thin, elongated (when describing the artistic style)
词性: Adjective

词源：
The word "Botero" is a surname derived from the Spanish word "botero," which means "boatman" or "ferryman."  However, when used in the context of art, it refers specifically to the Colombian artist Fernando Botero and his distinct artistic style.
