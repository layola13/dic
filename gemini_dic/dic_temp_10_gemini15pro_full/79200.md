glances:/ɡlænsɪz/ | n. | 瞥见，一瞥 | A quick look. |  She cast a few furtive glances at the stranger. 她偷偷地瞥了那个陌生人几眼。 | synonyms: glimpse, peek, glance(singular) | antonyms: stare, gaze | noun

glances:/ɡlɑːnsɪz/ | v. | 瞥一眼，扫视 | To look quickly or briefly. | He glanced at his watch and realized he was late. 他看了一眼手表，意识到自己迟到了。 | synonyms: glimpse, peek, glance(singular) | antonyms: stare, gaze | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *glancer "to slip, slide," probably of Germanic origin.
