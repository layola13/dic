disport| n. |娱乐，玩耍|Amusement, play, or sport.|The children's disport in the park was a joy to watch. 孩子们在公园里玩耍的景象令人赏心悦目。|近义词：amusement, entertainment, pastime, recreation|反义词：work, labor|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English disporten, from Old French desporter, desportoir (“to divert, amuse, relax”), from des- (“away from”) + porter (“to carry”), from Latin portare (“to carry”).  The sense of "to display ostentatiously" arose later.
