lob:/lɒb/ (UK), /lɑːb/ (US)| n. v.|高抛球，吊高球；耳垂；笨蛋；大懒虫|A gentle throw or hit of a ball, especially in tennis or cricket, that makes it go high in the air.  Can also refer to an earlobe or, informally, a foolish or lazy person.|He lobbed the ball over the net. 他把球高高地抛过网。|Near synonyms: toss, throw, pitch (for the ball action); earlobe (for the body part); fool, idiot, lout (informal, for the person).|Antonyms: smash, volley (for ball action); genius, bright spark (informal, for the person).|Noun/Verb
