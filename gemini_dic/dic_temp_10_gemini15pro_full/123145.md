monitors:/ˈmɒnɪtəz/ (UK), /ˈmɑːnɪtərz/ (US)| n. v.|显示器；监视器；监控；班长；监听|A screen that displays information from a computer; a person or device that watches or checks something; a student appointed to help a teacher.|
例句：The hospital monitors the patient's vital signs. 医院监控着病人的生命体征。
例句：She works as a hall monitor at the school. 她在学校担任走廊班长。

近义词：screens, displays (for the device); observes, watches, supervises (for the verb); overseers, inspectors (for the person)|
反义词:ignore, neglect (for the verb)|
词性:noun, verb
