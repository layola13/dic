ornithologist:/ˌɔːrnɪˈθɑːlədʒɪst/ | n. | 鸟类学家 | A scientist who studies birds. |  例句：The ornithologist spotted a rare species of woodpecker.  鸟类学家发现了一种稀有的啄木鸟种类。

例句：The ornithologist banded the birds to track their migration patterns. 鸟类学家给鸟儿系上环志以追踪它们的迁徙模式。

近义词：birdwatcher, birder | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ornis*, *ornithos* (bird) + *-logos* (one who studies, discourse)
