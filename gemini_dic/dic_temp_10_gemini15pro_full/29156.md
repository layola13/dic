casework:/ˈkeɪswɜːrk/
noun
个案工作；社会工作；个案研究
The investigation of individual cases, especially in social work or welfare.  It involves providing support and guidance to individuals or families facing various social or personal problems.
The social worker's casework involved helping the family find stable housing.  这个社会工作者的个案工作包括帮助这个家庭找到稳定的住所。
synonyms: social work, welfare work, counseling, support, guidance
antonyms: neglect, indifference
