nut-like:/ˈnʌtˌlaɪk/
adjective
坚果状的；像坚果的
Resembling a nut in shape, texture, or flavor.
The cookies have a nut-like flavor.  这些饼干有坚果的味道。
The seeds are small and nut-like. 这些种子又小又像坚果。
近义词: nutty, nut-shaped
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider describing the opposite texture or flavor, e.g., smooth, fruity)
词性: adjective
