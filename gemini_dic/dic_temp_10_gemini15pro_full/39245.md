congratulatory:/kənˈɡrætʃʊlətɔːri/| adj.|祝贺的，庆贺的|Expressing or conveying congratulations.|例句：She sent a congratulatory message to the winning team. 她向获胜的队伍发送了祝贺信息。

例句：He gave her a congratulatory pat on the back. 他拍了拍她的背以示祝贺。|近义词：celebratory, complimentary, commending|反义词：critical, disapproving|词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin congratulatorius, from congratulari (see congratulate).
