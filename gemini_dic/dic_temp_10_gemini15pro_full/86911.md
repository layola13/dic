hemmed:/hemd/ | v. | 包边，缝边，包围，限制 | to sew a hem; to surround and restrict the space or movement of someone or something | She hemmed the dress. 她给连衣裙缝了边。 The troops were hemmed in on all sides. 部队被四面包围了。 | synonyms: enclose, encircle, surround, confine, restrict | antonyms: free, liberate, release | verb

hemmed in:/hemd ˈɪn/ | adj. | 被包围的，受限制的 | surrounded and unable to move or escape easily | I felt hemmed in by the tall buildings. 我感觉被高楼大厦包围了。| synonyms: trapped, confined, restricted | antonyms: free, liberated, unrestricted | adjective
