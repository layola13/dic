parsonages:/ˈpɑːrsənɪdʒɪz/
n.
牧师住宅；教区长的住宅
A parsonage is a house provided for a parson, especially in a rural parish.
The old parsonage stood next to the church.  老牧师住宅就在教堂旁边。
The parsonage was a charming, though slightly dilapidated, building. 这座牧师住宅很迷人，尽管有点破旧。
synonyms: rectory, vicarage, manse
antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "secular residence")
noun
