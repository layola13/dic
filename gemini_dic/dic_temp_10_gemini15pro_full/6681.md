anal-retentive:/ˌeɪnəl rɪˈtɛntɪv/
adj.
肛门滞留的；执拗的；一丝不苟的
Characterized by excessive neatness, orderliness, and attention to detail, often to the point of being overly controlling or rigid.  This term originates from Freudian psychoanalysis, referring to a fixation in the anal stage of development.
He is so anal-retentive about his car; it has to be spotless. 他对他的车太过于一丝不苟了；它必须一尘不染。
近义词:obsessive, compulsive, meticulous, fastidious, rigid
反义词:sloppy, disorganized, careless, flexible
词性:adjective

anal-retentive:/ˌeɪnəl rɪˈtɛntɪv/
n.
执拗的人；一丝不苟的人
A person who is excessively neat, orderly, and attentive to detail.
My boss is such an anal-retentive; he checks every single document multiple times. 我的老板真是个一丝不苟的人；他会多次检查每一份文件。
近义词:perfectionist, stickler
反义词:slob
词性:noun
