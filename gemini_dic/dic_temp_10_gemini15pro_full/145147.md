overassertiveness:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrtɪvnəs/
n.
过分自信; 过于 assertive; 自作主张
The state or quality of being overassertive; excessive assertiveness.
His overassertiveness alienated his colleagues. 他的过分自信疏远了他的同事。
近义词：aggressiveness, pushiness, bossiness
反义词：timidity, shyness, submissiveness
词性: 名词
