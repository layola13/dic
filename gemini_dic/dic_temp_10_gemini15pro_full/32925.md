She dressed choosingly for the important interview. 她为这次重要的面试精心挑选了服装。
近义词: carefully, selectively, deliberately, discriminately
反义词: indiscriminately, randomly, carelessly
词性: 副词

Etymology: From "choosing" (present participle of "choose"), derived from Old English "cēosan", meaning "to select, seek out, test, taste, approve," + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
