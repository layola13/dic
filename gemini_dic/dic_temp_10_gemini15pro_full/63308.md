enticed:/ɪnˈtaɪst/ | v. | 诱惑，吸引 | To attract someone to a particular activity or place by offering something pleasant or advantageous. |  The smell of freshly baked bread enticed me into the bakery.  新鲜出炉的面包的香味把我吸引进了面包店。|Synonyms: tempt, lure, allure, attract, charm | Antonyms: repel, deter, discourage | verb

enticed:/ɪnˈtaɪst/ | v. | 引诱，怂恿 | To persuade someone to do something, especially something wrong or foolish, by promising them something pleasant. |  He was enticed into the gambling den by the promise of easy money. 他被轻易赚钱的承诺引诱进了赌场。 | Synonyms: tempt, lure, seduce, coax, wheedle | Antonyms: dissuade, discourage, deter | verb
