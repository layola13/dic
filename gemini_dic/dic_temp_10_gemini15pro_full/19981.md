biogenetic:/ˌbaɪoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. | 生物发生的，生物起源的 | Relating to biogenesis, the production of living organisms from other living organisms.  |  Biogenetic research suggests that mitochondria were once independent organisms. 生物起源研究表明，线粒体曾经是独立的生物体。 |  Biogenic, biological | Abiogenetic | adjective

biogenetic:/ˌbaɪoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. | 生物遗传的 | Relating to the biogenetic law, a discredited theory that the development of an individual organism repeats the evolutionary history of its species. | The biogenetic law, though flawed, influenced early evolutionary thought. 生物遗传定律虽然有缺陷，但影响了早期的进化思想。 | Phylogenetic, ontogenetic |  | adjective
