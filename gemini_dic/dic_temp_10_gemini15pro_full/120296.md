minibusses:/ˈmɪniˌbʌsɪz/
noun
小型公共汽车；中巴
A small bus designed to carry about a dozen passengers.
The hotel provides minibuses to take guests to the beach.  酒店提供小型公共汽车送客人去海滩。
synonyms:minivan, shuttle bus
antonyms:(No direct antonym, but consider "coach" or "large bus" for contrast)
词性:n.
