meditatively:/ˈmɛdɪteɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 沉思地，冥想地 | in a meditative manner; thoughtfully and reflectively | She sat by the window, gazing meditatively at the falling leaves. 她坐在窗边，沉思地望着落叶。| He stroked his chin meditatively. 他若有所思地抚摸着下巴。 |  thoughtfully, pensively, reflectively | rashly, impulsively | adverb

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Old French meditatif, -ive, from late Latin meditativus, from meditari ‘meditate’.
