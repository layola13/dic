commendingly:/kəˈmɛndɪŋli/
adverb
赞赏地，称赞地
In a manner that expresses approval or praise.
He spoke commendingly of her efforts. 他赞赏地谈到了她的努力。
She smiled commendingly at the child's drawing. 她对孩子的画赞赏地笑了笑。
近义词: approvingly, laudably, praiseworthily
反义词: disapprovingly, critically
词性: adverb

词源:
From commend + -ing + -ly.  "Commend" comes from the Latin "commendare," meaning "to entrust, recommend."
