goose:/ɡuːs/ /ɡus/| n.|鹅|A large waterbird with a long neck, short legs, webbed feet, and a short broad bill.  A female goose is called a goose, a male is called a gander, and a baby goose is called a gosling.|The farmer kept a flock of geese.  农民养了一群鹅。|近义词:waterfowl, bird|反义词:(none specific, possibly duck in some contexts)|词性:noun

goose:/ɡuːs/ /ɡus/| vt.|嘘；催促；使恼怒|To hiss at; to prod; to irritate.|He goosed the actor just before he went on stage. 他在演员上台前催促了他一下。|近义词:prod, nudge, hurry|反义词:delay, hinder|词性:verb
