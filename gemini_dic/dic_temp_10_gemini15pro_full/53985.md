discretionary:/dɪˈskreʃənəri/
adjective
自由决定的；酌情决定的
Available at the discretion of the court. 由法庭酌情决定。
Judges have wide discretion in sentencing. 法官在量刑方面有很大的自由裁量权。
synonym: optional, elective, non-obligatory
antonym: compulsory, mandatory, obligatory
词性: 形容词

discretion:/dɪˈskreʃən/
noun
谨慎；慎重；自行决定的自由；自行决定的权利
She handled the matter with discretion. 她谨慎地处理了这件事。
The awards are made at the manager's discretion. 奖项由经理自行决定。
synonym: prudence, caution, judgment
antonym: recklessness, carelessness, imprudence
词性: 名词
