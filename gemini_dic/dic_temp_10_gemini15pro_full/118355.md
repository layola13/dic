merit:/ˈmerɪt/| n. vt.|优点，价值，功绩；值得，应得|A quality deserving praise or reward. |Her artistic merits were soon recognized. 她的艺术价值很快得到了认可。|Synonyms: value, worth, virtue, excellence|Antonyms: defect, flaw, fault|noun

merit:/ˈmerɪt/| vt.|值得，应得|Deserve or be worthy of (something, especially reward, punishment, or attention).|The case merits careful consideration.  这个案子值得仔细考虑。|Synonyms: deserve, earn, warrant, justify|Antonyms: forfeit|verb
