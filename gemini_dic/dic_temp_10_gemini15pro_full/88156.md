hiller:/ˈhɪlər/| n.| 堆土机；培土机；筑堤机；|A machine used for making hills or ridges of earth, especially in agriculture. |例句：The farmer used a hiller to create furrows for planting potatoes.  农民用培土机开沟种植土豆。

例句：The hiller efficiently built up the soil around the base of the young trees.  培土机有效地在幼树根部堆积土壤。|近义词：ridger, earthmover |反义词: leveler|noun
