finisher:/ˈfɪnɪʃər/| n.|完成者; 终结者; 整理工; (尤指赛跑)最后阶段跑得最快的人|A finisher is someone who completes a task, race, or project, often with a strong final effort. |The marathon finisher crossed the line with a triumphant smile. 马拉松完成者带着胜利的微笑冲过了终点线。

|The finisher in a construction crew ensures all the final details are perfect. 建筑队里的整理工确保所有最后的细节都完美无缺。|近义词：completer, concluder|反义词: beginner, starter|noun
