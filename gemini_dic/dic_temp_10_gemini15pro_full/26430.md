byon:/ˈbaɪɒn/| acronym |自带设备|Bring Your Own Network.  This refers to the practice of customers bringing their own networking equipment, such as routers and switches, to a colocation facility or cloud provider.| Many companies are adopting a BYON policy to reduce costs and increase control over their network infrastructure. 许多公司正在采用自带设备网络策略以降低成本并增强对其网络基础设施的控制。|近义词：BYOD (Bring Your Own Device)|反义词: N/A|n.
