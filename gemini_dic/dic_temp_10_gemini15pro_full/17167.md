battleship:/ˈbætəlʃɪp/ | n. | 战列舰；主力舰 | A large warship with heavy armor and guns. |  例句：The battleship was sunk by enemy fire.  战列舰被敌人的炮火击沉了。

例句：The battleship opened fire on the enemy fleet.  战列舰向敌方舰队开火。

近义词：warship, dreadnought, man-of-war | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a physical object like a battleship.  Perhaps "peace ship" could be considered a thematic antonym.) | 词性: noun
