crumbcloth: /ˈkrʌmklɒθ/ (UK) /ˈkrʌmklɔːθ/ (US)| n. |碎屑布，桌布|A cloth spread under a table to catch crumbs.  It can also refer to a tablecloth used for everyday meals.|The crumbcloth caught the spilled gravy. 碎屑布接住了洒出的肉汁。|Synonyms: tablecloth, dropcloth, undercloth|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but one could consider "placemat" as a contrasting item)|Noun
