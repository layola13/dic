intercitizenship: /ˌɪntərˈsɪtɪzənʃɪp/
n.
跨国公民身份，跨国公民意识
The concept of intercitizenship emphasizes the shared responsibilities and connections between people across national borders.  跨国公民身份的概念强调跨越国界的人们之间的共同责任和联系。
Intercitizenship promotes global cooperation and understanding. 跨国公民意识促进全球合作和理解。
Etymology: inter- (between) + citizenship
近义词: global citizenship, transnational citizenship
反义词: nationalism
词性: noun
