interfunction:/ˌɪntərˈfʌŋkʃən/| adj.|函数间的，模块间的|Occurring between functions or modules.  Relating to the interaction or communication between different functions or modules within a program or system.|The interfunction communication was flawed, causing the system to crash.|近义词:intermodular, between functions|反义词:intrafunction, within a function|词性:adjective


Etymology:
inter- (Latin) meaning "between" + function
