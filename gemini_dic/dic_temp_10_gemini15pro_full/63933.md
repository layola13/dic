epipodium:/ˌɛpɪˈpəʊdiəm/
n.|外足，上足|A structure arising from the dorsal surface of the foot in some mollusks, such as gastropods.  It forms part of the mantle and may be involved in respiration, locomotion, or sensory perception.|The epipodium of the sea slug is brightly colored.|近义词:parapodium|反义词:|noun
