image:/ˈɪmɪdʒ/ | n. | 图像，形象，印象 | A representation of the external form of a person or thing in art. |  She saw her own image reflected in the water. 她看到自己在水中的倒影。| synonym: picture, likeness, representation | antonym: reality, original | noun

image:/ɪˈmædʒɪn/ | v. | 想像，设想 | Form a mental image or concept of. | I can't imagine life without you. 我无法想象没有你的生活。| synonym: envision, visualize, conceive | antonym:  disbelieve, doubt | verb
