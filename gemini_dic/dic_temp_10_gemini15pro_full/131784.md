nonconnotative:/ˌnɒnkɒnˈəʊtətɪv/ | adj. | 非内涵的；不隐含意义的 |  Lacking connotation; purely denotative; referring only to the literal meaning.  | The word "table" is nonconnotative; it simply refers to a piece of furniture.  “桌子”这个词是非内涵的；它仅仅指一件家具。 | Synonyms: literal, denotative, objective | Antonyms: connotative, suggestive, evocative | adjective
