nims:/nɪmz/ | n. | 国家事故管理系统（National Incident Management System）| A systematic, proactive approach to guide departments and agencies at all levels of government, nongovernmental organizations, and the private sector to work seamlessly together to prevent, protect against, mitigate, respond to, and recover from the effects of incidents, regardless of cause, size, location, or complexity, in order to reduce the loss of life and property and harm to the environment. | The fire department utilized NIMS principles during the wildfire response. 消防部门在应对野火期间利用了国家事故管理系统原则。|  The NIMS structure facilitated effective communication between different agencies during the hurricane. 国家事故管理系统结构促进了飓风期间不同机构之间的有效沟通。 | 近义词: incident command system (ICS), emergency management system | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
