例句：The company was penalized for infracting safety regulations.  该公司因违反安全条例而受到处罚。

|近义词：violate, breach, break, transgress|反义词:obey, comply, observe|词性:动词

From Latin *in-*, "not, against" + *frangere*, "to break."  Meaning "to break (a law, etc.)"
