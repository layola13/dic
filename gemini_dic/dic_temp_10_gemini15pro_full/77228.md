gastrocnemius: /ˌɡæstrɒkˈniːmiəs/ (UK), /ˌɡæstrəˈniːmiəs/ (US) | n. | 腓肠肌 |  The largest and most superficial muscle of the calf of the leg, which flexes the knee and foot. It is connected to the heel bone by the Achilles tendon. |  The gastrocnemius muscle is essential for walking, running, and jumping. 腓肠肌对于行走、跑步和跳跃至关重要。| The athlete injured his gastrocnemius during the marathon.  这位运动员在马拉松比赛中伤了腓肠肌。 | synonyms: calf muscle | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific muscle.  Antagonistic muscle would be tibialis anterior.) | noun
