disproved:/dɪsˈpruːvd/
verb
反驳；证明…是虚假的
to prove that something is not true
The scientist disproved the old theory with new evidence.  这位科学家用新的证据反驳了旧理论。
近义词:refuted, debunked, invalidated
反义词:proved, confirmed, validated
词性:Verb (Past Participle)
