insomnias:/ɪnˈsɒmniəz/ | n. | 失眠（复数） | Habitual sleeplessness; inability to sleep.  It's the plural form of "insomnia," though often used interchangeably. | She has suffered from insomnias for years. 她多年来一直遭受失眠的困扰。|  I've been having terrible insomnias lately.  我最近失眠很严重。 | 近义词：sleeplessness, wakefulness | 反义词：sleep, rest | 词性: noun

词源: From Latin *insomnia*, from *in-* (not) + *somnus* (sleep).
