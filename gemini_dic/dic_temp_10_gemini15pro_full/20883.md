blazingly:/ˈbleɪzɪŋli/| adv.|炽烈地，灼热地，极度地|very brightly and intensely; extremely|The sun was blazingly hot. 太阳炽热无比。|The car was blazingly fast. 这辆车速度极快。|近义词：intensely, brightly, fiercely, extremely|反义词：mildly, dimly, faintly|词性:副词
词源: From blaze + -ing + -ly.  "Blaze" comes from Old English *blæse, from Proto-Germanic *blasaną "to blow, burn."
