abradant: /əˈbreɪdənt/
n.|磨料|A substance used for grinding or polishing.|Sandpaper is coated with an abradant. 砂纸涂有磨料。|
近义词: abrasive, corrosive, grinding|
反义词: lubricant, polish|
词性:名词

abradant: /əˈbreɪdənt/
adj.|磨蚀的，有研磨作用的|Causing abrasion; tending to abrade.|The abradant action of the waves smoothed the rocks. 海浪的磨蚀作用使岩石变得光滑。|
近义词: abrasive, erosive|
反义词: smooth, polishing|
词性:形容词


词源:
From Latin abradere, from ab- + radere ‘to scrape’.

USAGE->
名词用法:

This type of abradant is ideal for cleaning metal. = 这种磨料非常适合清洁金属。

Diamond is a very effective abradant. = 钻石是一种非常有效的磨料。


形容词用法:

The abradant surface of the sandpaper smoothed the wood. = 砂纸的磨蚀表面使木头变得光滑。

The wind carried abradant sand particles that wore away the surface of the rock. = 风携带着具有磨蚀作用的沙粒，磨损了岩石的表面。




PHRASE->
an effective abradant = 一种有效的磨料
a powerful abradant = 一种强力磨料
the abradant action of = ...的磨蚀作用
abradant materials = 磨料
abradant tools = 研磨工具
