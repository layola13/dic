aspish:/ˈæspɪʃ/
adjective
像毒蛇的；狠毒的；恶意的
Resembling or suggestive of an asp, especially in being venomous, malignant, or treacherous.
Her aspish glare made me shiver. 她那恶狠狠的瞪眼让我不寒而栗。
近义词: venomous, poisonous, viperous, malevolent, malicious, spiteful, venomous
反义词: benevolent, kind, gentle
词性: adjective

aspish:/ˈæspɪʃ/
adjective
充满怨恨的; 怀恨在心的
Full of resentment; bitter.
He cast an aspish look at his rival. 他怨恨地瞥了一眼他的对手。
近义词: resentful, bitter, acrimonious
反义词: forgiving, magnanimous
词性: adjective
