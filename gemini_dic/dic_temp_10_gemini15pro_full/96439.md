indistinguishablenesses:/ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbəlˌnɛsɪz/ | n. | 不可区分性；无法分辨 |The state or quality of being indistinguishable; inability to be distinguished. | The indistinguishablenesses of the twins made it difficult to tell them apart. 这对双胞胎的无法区分使得很难区分他们。|Synonyms: similarity, sameness, likeness, resemblance, uniformity | Antonyms: difference, dissimilarity, distinction, divergence, variance | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From indistinguishable + -ness.  Indistinguishable is formed from in- (not) + distinguishable. Distinguishable comes from distinguish (to recognize or treat (someone or something) as different) + -able (capable of being).  The verb distinguish comes from Old French distinguer, from Latin distinguere (to separate, distinguish), from dis- (apart) + -stinguere (to prick, mark).
