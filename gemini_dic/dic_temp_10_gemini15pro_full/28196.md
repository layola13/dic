capricioso:/kəˌpriˈtʃoʊsoʊ/ /kəˌprɪˈtʃoʊsoʊ/
adjective
反复无常的；任性的；随心所欲的
Given to sudden and unaccountable changes of mood or behavior.
例句：The capricious weather made it difficult to plan a picnic. 反复无常的天气使得很难计划野餐。

例句：He was a capricious boss, firing people on a whim. 他是一个反复无常的老板，一时兴起就解雇员工。

近义词：fickle, whimsical, erratic, mercurial, impulsive
反义词：steady, consistent, predictable, stable
词性: adjective
