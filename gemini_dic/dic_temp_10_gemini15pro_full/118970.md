metestrous:/ˌmiːˈtɛstrəs/| adjective |发情间期的，性周期中期的|Relating to the period of the estrous cycle following ovulation, when the corpus luteum is formed in the ovary and progesterone secretion is high.  It is characterized by a thickening of the uterine lining in preparation for possible implantation of a fertilized egg.|The veterinarian explained that the dog was metestrous and not ready to breed yet. 兽医解释说这只狗正处于发情间期，还没有准备好繁殖。

The researchers observed the metestrous changes in the uterine lining of the mice. 研究人员观察了小鼠子宫内膜的发情间期变化。|近义词:diestrous|反义词:proestrous, estrous|词性:adjective
