couchette:/kuːˈʃet/ | n. |卧铺; 躺椅 |A narrow bed or bunk on a train or ship, typically one of two or more arranged one above the other. | I booked a couchette on the overnight train to Rome. 我在开往罗马的夜车上订了一个卧铺。| synonym: bunk, berth | antonym: cabin, suite | noun

couchette:/kuːˈʃet/ | n. |躺椅 |A type of simple, often folding, chaise lounge or reclining chair. |She relaxed on the couchette by the pool. 她躺在泳池边的躺椅上放松。| synonym: chaise lounge, recliner | antonym: upright chair, armchair | noun
