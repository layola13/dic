Absence of flatulence; the state of not experiencing excessive gas in the digestive system.
My improved diet has resulted in a welcome period of nonflatulencies.  我的改善饮食带来了令人愉快的无胀气时期。

近义词: absence of flatulence, freedom from gas
反义词: flatulence, gas, bloating
