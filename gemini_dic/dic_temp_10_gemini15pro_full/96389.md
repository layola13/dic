indiscriminately: /ˌɪndɪˈskrɪmɪnətli/
adverb
不加选择地，随意地
without careful judgement or distinction; randomly.
The soldiers fired indiscriminately into the crowd. 士兵们不分青红皂白地向人群开火。
indiscriminately: /ˌɪndɪˈskrɪmɪnətli/
adverb
胡乱地，任意地
in a random manner; without careful selection.
She indiscriminately threw away all the old letters. 她胡乱地把所有的旧信都扔掉了。
indiscriminately: /ˌɪndɪˈskrɪmɪnətli/
adverb
滥加区别地
without making careful distinctions.
He indiscriminately praised everyone's work, even those who clearly hadn't put in much effort. 他不加区别地赞扬了每个人的工作，即使是那些显然没有付出太多努力的人。


近义词: randomly, haphazardly, arbitrarily, aimlessly
反义词: selectively, carefully, discriminately
词性: adverb

词源:  From Late Latin indiscriminatus, from in- "not" + discriminatus "distinguished, separated," past participle of discriminare "to distinguish," from discrimen "that which separates," from discernere "to separate."
