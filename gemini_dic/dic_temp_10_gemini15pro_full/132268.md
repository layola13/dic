nondeductive:/ˌnɒndɪˈdʌktɪv/
adjective
非演绎的
Not proceeding from the general to the particular; not deductive.  Reasoning from particular facts or cases to a general principle.
This is a nondeductive argument, as the conclusion doesn't necessarily follow from the premises.  这是一个非演绎论证，因为结论并不一定来自前提。
The scientist used nondeductive reasoning to form a hypothesis based on her observations.  这位科学家使用非演绎推理，根据她的观察结果形成了一个假设。
synonyms: inductive, empirical, a posteriori
antonyms: deductive, a priori
词性: adjective
