diabley:/ˌdiːˈɑːbleɪ/ | n. |  小恶魔; 淘气鬼; 恶作剧 | A mischievous or playful imp; a little devil. | He's a little diabley, always getting into trouble. 他是个小淘气鬼，总是惹麻烦。 | 近义词: imp, rascal, scamp, devil | 反义词: angel | 词性: 名词

词源: From French *diable*, diminutive of *diable* (“devil”), from Old French *diable*, from Late Latin *diabolus*, from Ancient Greek *διάβολος (diábolos, “slanderer, accuser”)*.
