afforested:/əˈfɔːrɪstɪd/
v.|造林的，已植树的|Covered with trees; planted with trees.|The previously barren land was afforested with a variety of native species. 这片以前贫瘠的土地种植了各种本地树种。|例句：The government has afforested large areas of wasteland. 政府已在荒地上大面积造林。|近义词：planted, wooded, forested|反义词：deforested, cleared|词性: v.

afforested:/əˈfɔːrɪstɪd/
adj.|造林的，已植树的|Covered with trees; planted with trees.|The afforested hills provided a habitat for diverse wildlife.  这些植树造林的山丘为各种野生动物提供了栖息地。|例句：The afforested areas helped to control soil erosion.  造林地区有助于控制水土流失。|近义词：planted, wooded, forested|反义词：deforested, cleared|词性: adj.
