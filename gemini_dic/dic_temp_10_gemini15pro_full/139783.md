octanoic:/ɒkˈtænoʊɪk/ | adj. | 辛酸的；辛酸的 |  Relating to or derived from octanoic acid (also known as caprylic acid), a saturated fatty acid with eight carbon atoms. | Octanoic acid is found in the milk of various mammals and is a common ingredient in perfumes. 辛酸存在于各种哺乳动物的乳汁中，是香水中常见的成分。| Synonyms: caprylic | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a chemical term, but one might consider unsaturated fatty acids as a contrasting group) | adjective

octanoate:/ɒkˈtænoʊeɪt/ | n. | 辛酸盐；辛酸酯 | A salt or ester of octanoic acid. | Sodium octanoate is used as a food preservative. 辛酸钠用作食品防腐剂。| Synonyms: caprylate | Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a chemical term) | noun
