chloralamide:/ˌklɔːrəlˈæmaɪd/ | n. |氯醛酰胺;氯醛氨|A sedative and hypnotic drug, a combination of chloral hydrate and formamide.  It is used to induce sleep and relieve anxiety.  It is less irritating to the stomach than chloral hydrate.| Chloralamide was prescribed to help her sleep.  氯醛酰胺被用来帮助她睡眠。|Synonyms: chloral formamide,  hypnotic, sedative|Antonyms: stimulant|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From chloral + amide.  Chloral refers to trichloroacetaldehyde, a chemical compound. Amide refers to a derivative of ammonia.
