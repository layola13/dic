hyperactive:/ˌhaɪpərˈæktɪv/| adj.|过度活跃的，多动的|Characterized by excessive activity and restlessness.|例句：The hyperactive child had difficulty sitting still in class.  这个多动的孩子上课很难坐得住。

例句：He was diagnosed with hyperactive disorder. 他被诊断出患有多动症。|近义词：restless, energetic, overactive, fidgety|反义词：calm, quiet, placid, tranquil|词性: adjective


Etymology:
From hyper- + active.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, above, beyond," from Greek *hyper (over, beyond). "Active" comes from Latin *activus (busy, lively), from *agere (to do, drive, act).
