non-ambiguous:/ˌnɒn æmˈbɪɡjuəs/ /ˌnɑːn æmˈbɪɡjuəs/ | adj. | 不含糊的，明确的 |  Not open to more than one interpretation; having only one meaning. | The instructions were clear and non-ambiguous. 指示很清楚，没有歧义。| clear, precise, unequivocal | ambiguous, vague, unclear | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + ambiguous.  "Ambiguous" comes from the Latin "ambiguus," meaning "doubtful, uncertain," from "ambigere" ("to wander about," "to waver").  The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning.
