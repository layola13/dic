hippie's:/ˈhɪpiz/| n.|嬉皮士的（所有格）|Something that belongs to a hippie.  A hippie is a person, especially of the 1960s, associated with a subculture involving a rejection of conventional values and the taking of hallucinogenic drugs.|Example: That's the hippie's van parked down by the river. 那是那个嬉皮士停在河边的货车。|近义词:flower child's, bohemian's|反义词:conformist's, traditionalist's|词性:名词所有格
