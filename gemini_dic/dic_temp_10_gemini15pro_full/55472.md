distortion:/dɪˈstɔːrʃn/ | n. | 扭曲，变形；失真；歪曲 | a change in the shape, sound, or appearance of something so that it is strange or not clear | The loud music caused a distortion of the sound. 喧闹的音乐导致声音失真。 | synonyms: deformation, contortion, warping, perversion, misrepresentation | antonyms: accuracy, clarity, fidelity, truth | noun

distortions:/dɪˈstɔːrʃnz/ | n. | 扭曲，变形；失真；歪曲（复数） |  plural form of distortion |  Optical distortions can make objects appear differently than they actually are. 光学扭曲会使物体看起来与实际不同。 | synonyms: deformations, contortions, warpings, perversions, misrepresentations | antonyms: accuracies, clarities, fidelities, truths | noun
