词源:  "filet" comes from the French word "filet," meaning "thread" or "string," referring to the thin, boneless strip of meat or fish.  "Fileted" is simply the past participle form of the verb "to filet."  “filet”来自法语单词“filet”，意思是“线”或“细绳”，指的是肉或鱼的薄而无骨的条状物。“fileted”只是动词“to filet”的过去分词形式。
