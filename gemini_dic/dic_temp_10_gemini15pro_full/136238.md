nonrenewals:/ˌnɒnrɪˈnjuːəlz/ (US) /ˌnɒnrɪˈnjuːəlz/ (UK)
n.
不续约，不续签; 不可再生资源（通常用复数）
instances of not renewing something, such as a contract or subscription; things that cannot be renewed or replenished naturally.
The nonrenewals of several key players' contracts caused concern among fans.  几名关键球员的合同没有续签，引起了球迷们的担忧。

Many countries are now investing heavily in renewable energy sources due to the finite nature of nonrenewals like fossil fuels. 许多国家正大力投资可再生能源，因为像化石燃料这样的不可再生资源是有限的。
近义词: expirations, terminations, discontinuations (for contracts); non-renewable resources (for resources)
反义词: renewals, extensions (for contracts); renewable resources (for resources)
词性: noun (plural)


nonrenewal:/ˌnɒnrɪˈnjuːəl/ (US) /ˌnɒnrɪˈnjuːəl/ (UK)
n.
不续约，不续签; 不可再生
the act of not renewing something, or something that cannot be renewed.
The nonrenewal of his lease forced him to find a new apartment.  他的租约没有续签，迫使他另找公寓。

Fossil fuels are a nonrenewal resource. 化石燃料是一种不可再生资源。

近义词: expiration, termination, discontinuation (for contracts); non-renewable resource (for resources)
反义词: renewal, extension (for contracts); renewable resource (for resources)
词性: noun


词源： "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "renewal" (from "renew" + "-al")
