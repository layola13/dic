faddish:/ˈfædɪʃ/| adj.|流行的；时尚的；一时流行的|Popular for a short time; trendy.|Her clothes were always faddish and expensive. 她的衣服总是赶时髦而且很贵。|Synonyms: trendy, fashionable, stylish, chic, voguish|Antonyms: classic, timeless, enduring, traditional|adjective

faddish:/ˈfædɪʃ/| adj.|一时流行的；风靡一时的|Characterized by a fleeting or superficial enthusiasm for something new or novel.|He dismissed the new technology as a faddish trend. 他认为这项新技术只是一时的潮流而予以否定。|Synonyms: trendy, fashionable, stylish, chic, voguish|Antonyms: classic, timeless, enduring, traditional|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From fad + -ish.  "Fad" originates from the late 19th century, likely a shortening of "fadaise," a French word meaning "frivolity" or "nonsense."
