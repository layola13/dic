admirabilities: /ˌædmərəˈbɪlətiz/
noun
令人钦佩的品质；优点
qualities deserving admiration.
Her numerous admirabilities include her courage and her kindness. 她众多的优点包括她的勇气和善良。
The admirabilities of the new system are clear to see. 新系统的优点显而易见。
近义词: merits, virtues, strengths, excellences
反义词: flaws, shortcomings, weaknesses, defects
词性: noun (plural)

ETYMOLOGY->
From admirable + -ity (suffix forming nouns of quality or condition) + -ies (plural suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *admirari* "to wonder at."
