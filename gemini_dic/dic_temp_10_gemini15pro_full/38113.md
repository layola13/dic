comprecatio:/kɒmprɪˈkeɪʃioʊ/ /kɑːmprɪˈkeɪʃioʊ/
|n.|恳求，祈祷，祈福|An earnest entreaty or prayer.|He offered a comprecatio for the safety of his family.|近义词：supplication, entreaty, plea, prayer|反义词：curse, malediction|词性:名词
Etymology: From Latin *comprecatio*, from *comprecor* "I pray earnestly," from *com-* "together" + *precor* "I pray."
