nonlethally:/nɒnˈliːθəli/ | adv. | 非致命地 | In a manner that is not designed to kill. |  The police used nonlethal methods to subdue the suspect. 警方使用非致命性方法制服了嫌疑人。| Synonyms: harmlessly, innocuously | Antonyms: lethally, fatally | adverb

Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "lethal" (from Latin "letalis," meaning "deadly") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
