nonappreciativenesses:/nɒnəˌpriːʃəˈtɪvnɪsɪz/
|n.|缺乏感激, 不领情|The state of being unappreciative; a lack of gratitude or recognition of value.|His nonappreciativenesses of her efforts hurt her deeply. 他对她的努力不领情，深深地伤害了她。|近义词：ungratefulness, ingratitude, thanklessness|反义词: gratitude, appreciation, thankfulness|词性: noun


Etymology:
non- (prefix): not
appreciate (verb): from Latin appretiare "to set a price to, appraise," from ad- "to" + pretium "price, worth"
-ive (suffix): forming adjectives
-ness (suffix): forming nouns from adjectives
-es (suffix): plural form
