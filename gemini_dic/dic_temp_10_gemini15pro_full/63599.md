envoys:/ˈɛnvɔɪz/| n. |使节，外交使节|A person delegated by a government to represent it in a foreign country.|The president dispatched envoys to several African nations. 总统派遣使节前往几个非洲国家。|ambassadors, representatives, delegates, emissaries|opponents, adversaries|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French envoie (“a sending, message”), from envoyer (“to send”), from Late Latin inviare (“to send on the way”).
