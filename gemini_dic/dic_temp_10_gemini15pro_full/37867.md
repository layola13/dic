compilement:/ˈkɒmpaɪlmənt/| n.|汇编；编辑；合集|The compilement of the dictionary took many years. 这部词典的编纂花了许多年。|近义词：compilation, collection, assemblage|反义词: decomposition, separation|词性:noun

词源：From the verb "compile" + "-ment" (suffix forming nouns denoting the result or product of an action). Ultimately from Latin "compilare" (to plunder, pillage, later to collect, compile).
