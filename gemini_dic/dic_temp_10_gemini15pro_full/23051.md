PHRASE->
bounce back:  恢复；反弹  He bounced back quickly after his illness. 他病后恢复得很快。
bounce off:  从…弹回；征求意见  I bounced the idea off a few colleagues. 我征求了几位同事的意见。
bounce around:  讨论；到处走动  We bounced some ideas around in the meeting. 我们在会议上讨论了一些想法。
bounce something off someone:  征求意见  Can I bounce something off you? 我能征求一下你的意见吗？
bounce a check:  开空头支票  He bounced a check at the grocery store. 他在杂货店开了一张空头支票。
