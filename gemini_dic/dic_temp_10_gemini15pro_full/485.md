abnormality: /ˌæbnɔːrˈmælɪti/ (US) /ˌæbnɔːˈmælɪti/ (UK)| n.|异常，反常，变态|A feature, characteristic, or occurrence that is not usual, typical, or expected.|Doctors are investigating the abnormality in her blood count. 医生们正在调查她血细胞计数的异常情况。

The scans showed an abnormality in the baby's brain. 扫描显示婴儿的大脑有异常。

|近义词：anomaly, irregularity, deviation, aberration, oddity|反义词：normality, regularity, typicality|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin abnormalitas, from abnormalis (“deviant from the rule”), from ab- (“away from”) + normalis (“normal”).
