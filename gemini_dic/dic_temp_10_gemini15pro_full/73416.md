forelands:/ˈfɔːrləndz/
foreland:/ˈfɔːrlænd/ | n. | 前沿地带，岬角 | A point of land projecting into the sea or other body of water; a headland or promontory. | The ship rounded the foreland and entered the bay. 船绕过岬角进入海湾。| 近义词：headland, promontory, cape | 反义词: bay, inlet | 名词

forelands:/ˈfɔːrləndz/ | n. | 前沿领域，最前沿 | The leading or most advanced position in a particular field or area of activity. |  Scientists are exploring the forelands of nanotechnology. 科学家们正在探索纳米技术的最前沿。 | 近义词: vanguard, cutting edge, frontier | 反义词: rear, backwater | 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *foreland (unattested), from fore- "before, in front" + land "land."  Cognate with Dutch voorland, German Vorland.  The sense of "advanced position" developed figuratively.
