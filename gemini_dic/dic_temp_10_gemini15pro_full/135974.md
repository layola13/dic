nonradiance:/ˌnɒnˈreɪdiəns/ | n. |无辐射，不发光|The state or quality of not emitting radiation, especially light or heat.|The stealth technology relies on the nonradiance of the aircraft to avoid detection.  这种隐形技术依靠飞机的无辐射来避免被探测到。|Example: The nonradiance of the object made it difficult to detect in the dark. 这个物体的不发光特性使它在黑暗中难以被发现。|Synonyms: darkness, dullness, opacity|Antonyms: radiance, luminescence, brightness|noun
