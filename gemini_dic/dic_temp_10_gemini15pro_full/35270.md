clusters:/ˈklʌstərz/ (US), /ˈklʌstəz/ (UK)| n.|簇，群，串; 聚集; (计算机)集群|A group of similar things or people positioned or occurring closely together.|例句：There was a cluster of grapes on the vine. 藤蔓上有一串葡萄.|例句：A cluster of fans waited outside the hotel. 一群粉丝在酒店外等候.|近义词：group, bunch, collection, agglomeration|反义词:scattering, dispersal|词性:noun
