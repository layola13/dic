misgrade:/ˌmɪsˈɡreɪd/
verb
错误地评分；评定等级不当
to give a student the wrong grade, or to judge the quality of something incorrectly.
The teacher misgraded my exam and gave me a C instead of a B. 老师把我的考试成绩评错了，给了我C而不是B。
My essay was misgraded; it deserved a better mark. 我的论文被错误地评分了；它应该得到更高的分数。
misjudge, misevaluate
grade correctly, evaluate accurately
verb
