anaphors:/ˈænəfɔːrz/| n.|回指, 指代|A word or expression that refers back to another word or expression in the text, called the antecedent.  An anaphor avoids repetition and maintains clarity.|The dog chased its tail. *Its* is an anaphor referring back to *the dog*.|近义词：reference, pronoun, deictic|反义词:cataphor|noun
