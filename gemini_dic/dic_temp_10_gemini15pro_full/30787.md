cetaceans:/sɪˈteɪʃənz/ /sɪˈteɪʃənz/| n.|鲸类动物|Aquatic mammals constituting the order Cetacea, including whales, dolphins, and porpoises.|例句：Cetaceans are known for their intelligence. 鲸类动物以它们的智力而闻名。

例句：Many cetaceans use echolocation to navigate and find food. 许多鲸类动物使用回声定位来导航和寻找食物。|近义词：whales, dolphins, porpoises|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑terrestrial mammals 陆生哺乳动物)|词性:noun
