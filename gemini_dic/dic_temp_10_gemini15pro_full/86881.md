hemipter:/ˈhemɪptər/ (US), /ˈhɛmɪptər/ (UK)| n.|半翅目昆虫|An insect of the order Hemiptera, which includes bugs, aphids, and cicadas. These insects typically have sucking mouthparts.|例句：The stink bug is a common hemipter found in gardens.  椿象是花园里常见的半翅目昆虫。

例句：Many hemipters are agricultural pests, feeding on plant sap. 许多半翅目昆虫是农业害虫，以植物汁液为食。|近义词：true bug|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


hemipteran:/ˌhemɪpˈtɛrən/ (US), /ˌhɛmɪpˈtɛrən/ (UK)| adj. n.|半翅目的；半翅目昆虫|Relating to or belonging to the order Hemiptera; a hemipterous insect.|例句：Hemipteran insects have piercing-sucking mouthparts. 半翅目昆虫具有刺吸式口器。

例句：Aphids are a type of hemipteran that can cause significant damage to crops. 蚜虫是一种半翅目昆虫，会对农作物造成重大损害。|近义词：hemipterous|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun


hemipterous:/ˌhemɪpˈtɛrəs/ (US), /ˌhɛmɪpˈtɛrəs/ (UK)| adj.|半翅目的|Belonging to or relating to the Hemiptera.|例句：The shield bug is a hemipterous insect. 盾蝽是半翅目昆虫。

例句：Hemipterous insects are characterized by their specialized mouthparts. 半翅目昆虫的特征在于其特化的口器。|近义词：hemipteran|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective



ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἡμι- (hēmi-, “half”) + πτερόν (pterón, “wing”), referring to the partially hardened forewings of some hemipterans.
