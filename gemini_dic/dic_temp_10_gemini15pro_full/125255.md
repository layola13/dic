multidivisional:/ˌmʌltiˌdɪˈvɪʒənəl/
adjective|多分部的，多部门的|Relating to a company that is organized into several semi-autonomous divisions based on product lines, geographic areas, or other criteria.|Many large corporations adopt a multidivisional structure to improve management efficiency.|近义词:decentralized, departmentalized|反义词:centralized|词性:adjective

Etymology:
multi- (prefix): from Latin *multus* "much, many"
divisional (adjective): from division + -al (suffix forming adjectives)
division (noun): from Late Latin *divisio*, from Latin *dividere* "to divide"
