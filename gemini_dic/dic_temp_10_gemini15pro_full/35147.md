clove: /kloʊv/ (US) /kləʊv/ (UK)| n. |丁香；瓣；（尤指大蒜的）瓣|A small, aromatic flower bud from the clove tree, used as a spice.  Also, a segment of a bulb, such as garlic.|例句：The recipe calls for a clove of garlic.  食谱需要一瓣大蒜。

例句：Ground cloves are often used in baking.  丁香粉常用于烘焙。|近义词：segment, section (for garlic)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

clove: /kloʊv/ (US) /kləʊv/ (UK)| n. |丁香树|The tropical tree that produces cloves.|例句：Cloves are harvested from the clove tree. 丁香是从丁香树上收获的。

例句：The clove tree is native to Indonesia. 丁香树原产于印度尼西亚。|近义词：Syzygium aromaticum (scientific name)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
