flintily:/ˈflɪntɪli/ | adv. | 坚定地；无情地；冷酷地 | In a manner resembling flint; firmly, unyieldingly, and often coldly or sternly. |  He flintily refused to compromise. 他坚决拒绝妥协。 | 近义词: resolutely, sternly, implacably, unyieldingly | 反义词: compassionately, sympathetically, mercifully, yieldingly | 词性: 副词

词源: From flint + -ly.  "Flint" refers to a hard stone, thus "flintily" implies a hardness or inflexibility of character.
