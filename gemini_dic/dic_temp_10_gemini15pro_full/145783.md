overdazzle: /ˌoʊvərˈdæzəl/ | vt. | 使目眩,使眼花缭乱 | To dazzle excessively; overwhelm with brilliance. | The fireworks overdazzled the children. 烟花使孩子们眼花缭乱。 | 近义词: overwhelm, blind, bedazzle | 反义词: subdue, underwhelm | 词性: 动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "dazzle" (from Middle English *dasen, of uncertain origin).
