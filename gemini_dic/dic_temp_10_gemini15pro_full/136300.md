nonrepressive:/ˌnɑːn.rɪˈprɛs.ɪv/
adjective
非压抑性的；非镇压性的
Not repressive; encouraging or allowing free expression of feelings or ideas.
例句：A nonrepressive environment fosters creativity.  一个非压抑性的环境能够促进创造力。

例句：The school had a nonrepressive atmosphere where students felt comfortable sharing their opinions.  这所学校的氛围是非压抑的，学生们可以自在地分享他们的意见。

近义词：liberal, permissive, tolerant, open-minded
反义词：repressive, authoritarian, restrictive

词性: adjective

词源:  The word "nonrepressive" is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the adjective "repressive".  "Repressive" comes from the verb "repress," which derives from the Latin word "reprimere" (meaning "to press back, check, restrain").
