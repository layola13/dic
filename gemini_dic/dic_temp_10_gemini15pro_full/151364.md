parkway:/ˈpɑːrkweɪ/ (US) /ˈpɑːkweɪ/ (UK)| n.|公园道路，林荫大道|A broad landscaped highway, often divided with a median strip and intended for scenic driving, typically restricted to non-commercial vehicles.|We drove along the scenic parkway, enjoying the beautiful fall foliage. 我们沿着风景优美的公园道路行驶，欣赏美丽的秋叶。|近义词：boulevard, expressway, freeway|反义词: alley, side street|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From park + way.  First attested in the late 19th century, reflecting the development of landscaped roads designed for pleasure driving.
