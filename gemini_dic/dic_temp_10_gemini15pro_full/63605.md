envyned:/ˈɛnvid/ | v. (past participle) | 羡慕，嫉妒 | Past participle of "envy," meaning to feel a discontented longing for someone else's advantages, possessions, or qualities. | Example: She felt envyned by her colleagues for her promotion. 她因为升职而被同事们羡慕。| Synonym: envied, coveted, begrudged | Antonym: admired, praised | Verb

Etymology:
From Old French "envier" (to look askance at, to envy), from Latin "invidere" (to look at (with malice), envy), from "in-" (in, at) + "videre" (to see).
