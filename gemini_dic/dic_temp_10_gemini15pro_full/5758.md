amahs:/ˈɑːmɑːz/
n.
女佣；保姆；（尤指东方国家的）阿妈
A woman employed as a domestic servant, especially in East Asia.
例句：The amahs cleaned the house thoroughly every day.  女佣们每天都把房子打扫得干干净净。

例句：She employed two amahs to look after her children. 她雇了两个阿妈来照顾她的孩子。

近义词：maids, servants, housekeepers, nannies
反义词：employer, master
词性：noun
