nonirritancy:/ˌnɒnˈɪrɪtənsi/
n.
无刺激性；不刺激性
The nonirritancy of the product was confirmed by clinical trials.  该产品的无刺激性已通过临床试验得到证实。
Synonyms: mildness, gentleness
Antonyms: irritancy, irritation
noun

nonirritating:/ˌnɒnˈɪrɪteɪtɪŋ/
adj.
无刺激性的；不 раздражающий
This cream is nonirritating and suitable for sensitive skin.  这款面霜性质温和，适合敏感肌肤。
Synonyms: soothing, calming
Antonyms: irritating, harsh
adjective


Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + irritant + -ancy (suffix forming nouns denoting quality or state)
