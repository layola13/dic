nonphonemic:/ˌnɑːnfoʊˈniːmɪk/ | adj. | 非音位的，非音素的 |  Not relating to or based on the phonemes of a language; not distinctive in pronunciation and therefore not capable of differentiating meaning.  A nonphonemic difference in pronunciation does not change the meaning of a word. |  The difference between the aspirated 'p' in 'pin' and the unaspirated 'p' in 'spin' is nonphonemic in English.  'Pin' and 'spin' are different words, but the different pronunciations of 'p' don't create that difference. |  non-distinctive, insignificant (in pronunciation) | phonemic, distinctive | adjective
