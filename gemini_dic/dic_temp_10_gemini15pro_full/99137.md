interceptor:/ˌɪntərˈseptər/ | n. |拦截器；截击机；截击导弹|Something that intercepts, especially a fast aircraft or missile designed to intercept enemy aircraft or missiles. | The fighter jet acted as an interceptor, preventing the enemy bomber from reaching its target. 这架战斗机充当了拦截机，阻止了敌方轰炸机到达目标。| The police used an interceptor to stop the speeding car. 警察用拦截器拦住了超速行驶的汽车。 | synonyms: blocker, obstructor, preventer | antonyms: facilitator, enabler | noun
