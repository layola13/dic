overmerriness:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛrɪnəs/
noun
过度欢乐，过分高兴
Excessive merriment; boisterous gaiety.
He exhibited an almost unsettling overmerriness after winning the lottery.  他中了彩票后表现出一种几乎令人不安的过度兴奋。
The overmerriness of the party started to become tiresome.  派对上过度的欢乐开始变得令人厌烦。
Synonyms: exuberance, boisterousness, exhilaration, jollity
Antonyms: gloom, sadness, dejection, melancholy
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + merriness.  Over- is a prefix meaning "excessive," "too much." Merriness is derived from merry (happy, cheerful) + -ness (a suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality).
