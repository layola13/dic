methene:/ˈmɛθiːn/| n.|甲烯;亚甲基|A simple organic compound containing one carbon atom and two hydrogen atoms, represented by the formula CH2. It is a highly reactive molecule and usually exists only as a short-lived intermediate in chemical reactions.|Methene is formed as a reactive intermediate in many chemical reactions.|近义词: methylene, carbene|反义词: N/A|noun
