copied:/ˈkɑːpid/ | adj. | 复制的；抄袭的 | Made as an exact copy of something else. |例句：This is a copied document.  这是一份复印件。

例句：The student was accused of having copied his essay from the internet.  这个学生被指控从网上抄袭了他的论文。|近义词：duplicated, replicated, imitated |反义词：original, unique |词性:形容词

词源：From the verb "copy," which comes from the Old French "copie," ultimately from Latin "copia" meaning "abundance, plenty." The "-ed" suffix transforms the verb into an adjective indicating a state of having been copied.
