earthlinesses:/ˈɜːθlɪnɪsɪz/
n.
世俗性，尘世性
The quality or state of being earthly; worldliness.
His paintings celebrate the earthlinesses of peasant life. 他的画作歌颂了农民生活的世俗性。
earthiness, worldliness, secularity, temporality
spirituality, divinity, heavenliness
