overhostilely:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːstaɪlli/| adv.| 过于敌对地，极端敌意地|  in an excessively hostile manner.  Demonstrating hostility beyond what is considered reasonable or appropriate.| He reacted overhostilely to the criticism, shouting and storming out of the room. 他对批评反应过度敌对，大喊大叫并冲出了房间。|  overaggressively, excessively hostilely, unduly antagonistically | amicably, kindly,  peacefully | adverb
Etymology: "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) meaning "above, beyond, excessively" + "hostile" (from Latin hostilis "of an enemy") + "-ly" (suffix forming adverbs).
