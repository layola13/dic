antiskepticisms:/ˌæntiˈskɛptɪsɪzəmz/
|n.|反怀疑论|The doctrine or principles of an antiskeptic.  A rejection of skepticism.|He studied the history of philosophy, focusing on various antiskepticisms throughout the ages. 他研究哲学史，重点研究了各个时代的各种反怀疑论。|
|近义词:certainties, convictions|
|反义词:skepticisms, doubts|
|词性: Noun|


Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek)
skeptic: from Greek skeptikos "inquiring, thoughtful,"
-ism (suffix): denoting a doctrine, belief, or practice.
