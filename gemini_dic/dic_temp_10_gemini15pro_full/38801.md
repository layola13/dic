conductory: /kənˈdʌktəri/ | adj. | 传导性的; 导电的; 导热的 | Relating to or having the power to conduct heat, electricity, or sound. |  Copper is a highly conductory material. 铜是一种高导电性材料。|The conductory properties of this metal make it ideal for wiring. 这种金属的导电性使其成为理想的布线材料。 |近义词: conductive, transmitting | 反义词: insulating, nonconductive | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *conductōrius*, from condūcō (“I bring together, collect, contribute, serve, hire, contract, conduct”) + -ōrius.
