oversauce:/ˌōvərˈsɔːs/ | v. | 过度调味；加太多酱汁 | To add too much sauce or flavoring to something, often to the point of ruining it. |  The chef oversauced the pasta, making it far too salty.  厨师给意大利面加了太多的酱汁，让它太咸了。| overseason, overflavor | underseason, underspice | verb

oversauce:/ˌōvərˈsɔːs/ | n. | 过多的酱汁 | An excessive amount of sauce. | There was so much oversauce on the plate it was swimming in it.  盘子里有过多的酱汁，食物都泡在里面了。| excess sauce | lack of sauce | noun
