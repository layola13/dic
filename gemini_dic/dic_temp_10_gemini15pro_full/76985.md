garden: /ˈɡɑːrdn/ (UK), /ˈɡɑːrdn/ (US) | n. | 花园；菜园 | A piece of ground, often near a house, used for growing flowers, fruit, or vegetables. |  My grandmother spends hours tending her garden. 我的祖母花几个小时照料她的花园。

The community garden provides fresh produce for local residents. 社区花园为当地居民提供新鲜农产品。

| 近义词: yard, lawn, patch | 反义词: wilderness | 词性: noun
