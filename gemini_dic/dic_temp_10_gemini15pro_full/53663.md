disciform:/ˈdɪskɪfɔːrm/| adj.|盘状的，圆盘状的|Having the shape of a disc or disk. |例句：The lichen had a disciform thallus.  这种地衣有一个盘状的菌体。

例句：The doctor noted the disciform shape of the lesion. 医生注意到病灶呈圆盘状。|近义词：discoid, circular, round|反义词：irregular, amorphous|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *discus* (disc) + *-formis* (having the form of).
