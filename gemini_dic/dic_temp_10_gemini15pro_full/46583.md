damager:/ˈdæmədʒər/| n.|损害者，破坏者|Someone or something that causes damage.|The hailstorm was the main damager of the crops.|近义词：wrecker, destroyer, saboteur|反义词：repairer, restorer|词性:noun

Etymology: From the verb "damage" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Damage" ultimately comes from the Old French "domage," from Latin "damnum" (loss, hurt).
