frescoer:/ˈfrɛskoʊər/
n.
壁画画家
A person who paints frescoes.
The frescoer spent years perfecting his technique.  这位壁画画家花了数年时间完善他的技艺。
Michelangelo was a renowned frescoer. 米开朗基罗是一位著名的壁画画家。
近义词:muralist, fresco painter
