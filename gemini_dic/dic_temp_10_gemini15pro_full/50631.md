deride:/dɪˈraɪd/ | v. | 嘲笑，讥讽 | To laugh at or make fun of someone or something in a way that shows contempt or disrespect. |  He derided her attempts at singing. 他嘲笑她唱歌的尝试。

The critics derided his new novel as sentimental and predictable.  评论家嘲讽他的新小说感伤且老套。


近义词: mock, ridicule, scorn, taunt, jeer | 反义词: praise, admire, commend, respect | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dērīdēre*, from *dē-* (down, away) + *rīdēre* (to laugh).
