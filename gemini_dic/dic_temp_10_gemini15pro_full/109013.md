leftists:/ˈlɛftɪsts/| n.|左派，左翼人士|People who support leftist political ideals, typically involving social equality and government intervention in the economy.  A person with left-wing political views.|例句：The leftists in the party advocated for stronger social programs.  党内的左派人士主张加强社会福利项目。

例句：The meeting attracted a diverse group of leftists, from social democrats to communists. 这次会议吸引了形形色色的左翼人士，从社会民主党人到共产主义者都有。|近义词：liberals, progressives, radicals|反义词:rightists, conservatives|词性:noun
