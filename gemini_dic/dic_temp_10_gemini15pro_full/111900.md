logwood:/ˈlɔːɡwʊd/ | n. | 苏木；苏木染料 |A dark heartwood used in dyeing and to make inks.  It comes from a tropical American tree (Haematoxylum campechianum).| The artist used logwood to create a rich, dark purple in his painting. 艺术家使用苏木在他的画作中创造出浓郁的深紫色。 | Synonym:campeachy wood, bloodwood  | Antonym: N/A (color/dye producing material, no direct opposite) | noun
