lionlike: /ˈlaɪənˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像狮子的; 勇敢的, 庄严的 | Resembling a lion in appearance, character, or behavior; courageous, majestic. |  The leader had a lionlike presence, commanding respect from everyone. 这位领导者有一种像狮子般的存在感，赢得了所有人的尊重。


The old warrior, with his flowing mane of white hair, had a lionlike roar.  这位老战士，留着飘逸的白色长发，发出像狮子一样的吼声。


Synonyms: leonine, courageous, brave, majestic, regal, noble, stately, imposing


Antonyms: cowardly, timid, meek, humble


Etymology: From lion + -like. "Lion" comes from Old French "lion," ultimately from Latin "leo." "-like" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling," "characteristic of," from Old English "-lic."
