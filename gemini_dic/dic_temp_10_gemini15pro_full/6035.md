amenableness:/əˈmiːnəbəlnəs/
n.
易控制, 顺从; 愿意接受建议
the quality of being open to suggestion or persuasion; willingness to accept something
Her amenableness to the proposal surprised everyone. 她对这个提议的接受态度让每个人都感到惊讶。

近义词:  receptiveness, compliance, docility, tractability
反义词:  unwillingness, resistance, intractability, stubbornness
词性: noun
