imbosom: /ɪmˈbʊzəm/ /ɪmˈbʌzəm/ | vt. | 拥抱；怀抱；隐藏；珍藏 | To enclose in the bosom; to hold dear; to cherish; to embrace; also, to admit to confidence; to tell in secret. |  She imbosomed the child in her arms. 她把孩子抱在怀里。| 近义词： embrace, cherish, harbor, conceal | 反义词： expose, reveal | 词性：动词

imbosom: /ɪmˈbʊzəm/ /ɪmˈbʌzəm/ | vt. | 嵌入；使沉浸在 | To enclose or embed something, as if within one's bosom; to place in a secure or comfortable position. | The village is imbosomed in the valley. 这个村庄坐落在山谷中。| 近义词： nestle, embed, enclose | 反义词： expose | 词性：动词
