castle: /ˈkɑːsl/ (US) /ˈkɑːsəl/ (UK)| n. |城堡，堡垒|A large fortified building, typically of the medieval period, occupied by a ruler or important noble.|例句：The king lived in a grand castle overlooking the valley. 国王住在一座俯瞰山谷的宏伟城堡里。

例句：The castle walls were thick and strong, designed to withstand attacks. 城堡的墙壁又厚又坚固，旨在抵御攻击。

近义词：fortress, stronghold, citadel|反义词:hut, shack|词性:noun
