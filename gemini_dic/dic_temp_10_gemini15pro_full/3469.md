after-taste:/ˈæftərteɪst/
n.
余味；回味；后味
A lingering taste or other sensation experienced after the main flavor or feeling has passed.
The coffee had a bitter after-taste. 这咖啡有苦涩的回味。

The medicine left an unpleasant after-taste in my mouth.  这种药在我的嘴里留下了一种令人不快的余味。


近义词: aftertaste (alternative spelling), lingering taste, residual flavor
反义词: foretaste, initial taste
