denominated:/dɪˈnɒmɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
以…计价的；被称为…的
expressed or measured in a particular unit; called a particular name or kind
例句：The loan was denominated in US dollars. 这笔贷款以美元计价。

例句：The bond is denominated in euros.  该债券以欧元计价。

近义词：expressed, measured, called, termed
反义词：unnamed
