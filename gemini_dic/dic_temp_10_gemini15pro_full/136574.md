nonsalutarily:/nɒnˌsæljuˈtɛrɪli/
|adv.|无益地，不利地|In a way that is not beneficial to health or well-being.  It describes actions or situations that have a negative or harmful effect.|His lifestyle choices affected him nonsalutarily. 他的生活方式选择对他产生了不利的影响。|
|The excessive heat affected the crops nonsalutarily. 过度的 Hitze 对农作物产生了不利影响。|
|Synonyms: harmfully, detrimentally, unhealthily, adversely|
|Antonyms: beneficially, healthily, advantageously, positively|
|词性: adverb|


词源:

non- (prefix):  Meaning "not" or "opposite of." From Latin *non*.
salutary (adjective): Meaning "beneficial," especially to health. From Latin *salūtāris*, from *salūs* "health," related to *salvus* "safe."
-ly (suffix):  Forms adverbs from adjectives.
