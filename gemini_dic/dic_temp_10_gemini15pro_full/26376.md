by leaps and bounds:/baɪ liːps ænd baʊndz/
|adv.|突飞猛进地，飞速地|Making rapid progress; advancing very quickly.  This idiom alludes to the long jumps and high bounds of a running animal.|Her English has improved by leaps and bounds this year. 她的英语水平今年突飞猛进。|Synonyms: rapidly, quickly, swiftly, dramatically, considerably, significantly|Antonyms: slowly, gradually, slightly|adverb
