cullers:/ˈkʌlərz/
noun
拣选者，剔除者
People who select or remove items from a larger group, often based on quality or other criteria.
The cullers sorted the apples, discarding those that were bruised or damaged. 拣选者将苹果分类，丢弃了那些碰伤或损坏的。
selectors, pickers, sorters
rejecters, keepers (if considering the opposite action of keeping)
n.
