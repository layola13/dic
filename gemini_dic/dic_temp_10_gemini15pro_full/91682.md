hypercoagulable:/ˌhaɪpərkoʊˌæɡjʊləbəl/
adjective
高凝的，易凝血的
Characterized by an increased tendency to form blood clots.
Example:  The patient's hypercoagulable state put him at risk for a stroke.  这位病人高凝状态使他面临中风的风险。

Example:  Certain genetic mutations can make someone hypercoagulable.  某些基因突变会导致易凝血。
Synonym: thrombophilic
Antonym: anticoagulant, hypocoagulable
词性: adjective
