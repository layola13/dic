artboard:/ˈɑːrtbɔːrd/
noun
画板，绘图板
A defined area on a screen in a graphics program such as Illustrator or Photoshop, representing the printable area of a document.
The designer arranged all the elements on the artboard. 设计师在画板上排列了所有元素。

The logo was perfectly centered on the artboard.  标志完美地居中于画板上。

近义词:canvas, pasteboard
反义词:(无直接反义词)
词性:n.
