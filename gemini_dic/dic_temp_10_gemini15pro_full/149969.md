palmate:/ˈpælˌmeɪt/ /ˈpɑːlmeɪt/| adj.|掌状的，蹼状的|Resembling a hand with the fingers spread; having lobes radiating from a common point.|例句：The maple leaf is a classic example of a palmate leaf.  枫叶是掌状叶子的典型例子。

例句：Ducks have palmate feet, which help them swim. 鸭子有蹼状的脚，这有助于它们游泳。|近义词：webbed, lobed|反义词:pinnate|词性:形容词
