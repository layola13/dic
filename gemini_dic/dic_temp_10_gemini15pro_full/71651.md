flattering:/ˈflætərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈflætərɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|奉承的；讨人喜欢的；显得更漂亮的|Making someone seem more attractive or impressive than they really are.  It can also refer to something (like clothing) that makes someone look good.|That dress is very flattering on you.  那件衣服你穿起来很漂亮。|近义词：complimentary, approving, enhancing|反义词：unflattering, critical, detracting|词性: adjective

flatterable:/ˈflætərəbl/ (US), /ˈflætərəbl/ (UK)| adj.|容易奉承的；可讨好的；可使显得更漂亮的|Susceptible to flattery, or capable of being made to appear more attractive or impressive.|She's very flatterable and enjoys being complimented. 她很容易被奉承，很喜欢被赞美。  /  That shade of blue is very flatterable to most skin tones.  那种蓝色对大多数肤色都很衬托。|近义词：impressionable, susceptible, enhanceable|反义词：unimpressed, resistant, unyielding|词性: adjective
