dipteran:/ˈdɪptərən/| n. adj.|双翅目昆虫；双翅目的|A dipteran is an insect of the order Diptera, such as a fly or mosquito, having a single pair of wings and a pair of halteres.|例句：The housefly is a common dipteran. 家蝇是一种常见的双翅目昆虫。

例句：Dipteran larvae are often found in decaying organic matter. 双翅目昆虫的幼虫常在腐烂的有机物中发现。|近义词：fly, mosquito (when referring to specific types)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, adjective

dipterous:/ˈdɪptərəs/| adj.|双翅目的|Dipterous means having two wings, characteristic of the insect order Diptera.|例句：Mosquitoes are dipterous insects known to transmit diseases. 蚊子是传播疾病的双翅目昆虫。

例句：The dipterous nature of the fly allows for agile flight. 苍蝇的双翅特性使其能够灵活飞行。|近义词：two-winged|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective
