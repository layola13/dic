matronage:/ˈmeɪtrənɪdʒ/ | n. | 赞助；惠顾；光顾；（尤指对医院、艺术家等的）资助；管理；指导 | patronage; support; guidance, especially financial support; the power to appoint someone to a job | The hospital benefited from the matronage of several wealthy families. 这家医院受益于几个富裕家庭的赞助。 | 近义词: patronage, sponsorship, support, backing, aegis | 反义词: neglect, opposition | 词性: noun

词源:
From Late Middle English: from Old French matronage, from matrone ‘matron’ (see matron).
