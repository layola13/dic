creeled:/kriːld/
verb (past tense of creel)
把（鱼）放入鱼篓
The fisherman creeled his catch. 渔夫把捕到的鱼放进了鱼篓。
past participle of creel
装入鱼篓的
The creeled fish were still alive. 装在鱼篓里的鱼还活着。

词性: verb (past tense/past participle)
中文翻译: 放入鱼篓；装入鱼篓的
英文解析: put (fish) in a creel; contained in a creel.  It acts as both the past tense and past participle of the verb "creel."
例句:
He creeled a dozen trout. 他把一打鳟鱼放进了鱼篓。
The creeled trout were taken back to the camp.  被装进鱼篓里的鳟鱼被带回了营地。

近义词: basket, catch
反义词: release
