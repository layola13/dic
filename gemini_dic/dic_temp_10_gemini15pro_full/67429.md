extinctions:/ɪkˈstɪŋkʃənz/ | n. |灭绝，绝种|The process of a species or group of organisms ceasing to exist. |The Permian-Triassic extinction event was the most severe known extinction in Earth's history. 二叠纪-三叠纪灭绝事件是地球历史上已知最严重的灭绝事件。

The dodo's extinction is a classic example of human impact on the environment. 渡渡鸟的灭绝是人类影响环境的典型例子。

|近义词：annihilations, disappearances, eradication |反义词: survival, preservation |词性: noun
