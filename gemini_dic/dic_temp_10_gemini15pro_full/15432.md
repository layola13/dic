bacteroidal:/bækˈtɪrɔɪdəl/
adjective
类菌体的；拟细菌的
Resembling bacteria in form or function.  Relating to or resembling bacteroids.
Example: The bacteroidal form of the nitrogen-fixing bacteria is found in root nodules.  这些固氮细菌的类菌体形态存在于根瘤中。

Example: The scientists observed the bacteroidal cells under the microscope. 科学家们在显微镜下观察了这些类菌体细胞。


synonym:  bacterial, bacteroid-like
antonym:  (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "non-bacteroidal" or describing the dissimilar shape/function)
Etymology:  From bacteroid + -al.  Bacteroid from bacteria + -oid.
