non-reductive:/ˌnɒn rɪˈdʌktɪv/ | adj. | 非还原性的，非简化的 | Not reducing or simplifying something to a more basic or essential form; preserving complexity and nuance. |  This non-reductive approach considers all aspects of the situation.  这种非还原性方法考虑了情况的各个方面。

The artist's non-reductive style embraces the chaos and beauty of the natural world.  这位艺术家的非还原性风格拥抱了自然世界的混乱和美。 | 近义词：holistic, comprehensive, nuanced | 反义词：reductive, simplistic, oversimplified | 词性：形容词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "reductive" (from "reduce," ultimately from Latin "reducere" - to lead back, bring back).
