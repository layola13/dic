one-upsmanship: /ˌwʌnˈʌpmənˌʃɪp/
n.
胜人一筹；压倒别人；技高一筹
The act or practice of gaining a feeling of superiority over another person, especially by succeeding in something that person has attempted and failed at.
He’s always trying to gain one-upsmanship over his colleagues. 他总是试图在同事面前技高一筹。
近义词: competitiveness, rivalry, outdoing
反义词: cooperation, collaboration, modesty
词性: noun

one-upsmanship: /ˌwʌnˈʌpmənˌʃɪp/
noun
胜人一筹；压倒别人；技高一筹
The practice of trying to seem better than someone else, especially at a particular activity.
There’s always an element of one-upmanship between them. 他们之间总有一些想要胜过对方的意思。
近义词: one-upmanship
反义词: cooperation
词性: noun
