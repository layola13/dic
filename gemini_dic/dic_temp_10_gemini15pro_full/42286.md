counter-faller:/ˈkaʊntərˌfɔːlər/
n.|反落者，反驳者|Someone who opposes or contradicts another, especially in argument. |He's a real counter-faller, always arguing the opposite point of view. 他真是个反驳者，总是反驳别人的观点。| The experienced debater was a skilled counter-faller, adept at dismantling his opponents' arguments.  这位经验丰富的辩论家是一位熟练的反驳者，擅长瓦解对手的论点。 |opponent, adversary|supporter, ally|noun
