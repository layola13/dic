coltish: /ˈkoʊltɪʃ/ (US), /ˈkəʊltɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.| 小马似的；活泼的；不受拘束的；不老练的|Resembling or characteristic of a young horse; playful, energetic, but also awkward and lacking in grace or restraint.|  The coltish young actress bounced onto the stage.  这位活泼的年轻女演员蹦蹦跳跳地上了舞台。|  His coltish enthusiasm was infectious. 他那奔放的热情很有感染力。 |Synonyms: frisky, playful, spirited, exuberant, lively, skittish, frolicsome| Antonyms: sedate, composed, restrained, mature, experienced| adjective
