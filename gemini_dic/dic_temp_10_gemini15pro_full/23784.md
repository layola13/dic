brawner:/ˈbrɔːnər/| n.|争吵者，好斗的人|A person who is aggressive and inclined to fight; a troublemaker. |He's a real brawner, always starting fights in the pub.|近义词：fighter, scrapper, ruffian, bully|反义词:pacifist, peacemaker|noun

词源:
From the verb "brawl" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Brawl" comes from Middle English "braulen," of uncertain origin, possibly from Old French "brailler" (to shout).
