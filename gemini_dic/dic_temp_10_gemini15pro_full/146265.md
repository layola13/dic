overfacility: /ˌoʊvər fəˈsɪləti/
noun
过度简化；过度熟练；过分熟练
Excessive ease or fluency, often leading to inaccuracy or superficiality.
His overfacility with the language led to grammatical errors. 他对语言的过度熟练导致了语法错误。
Overfacility in playing the piano can sometimes mask a lack of musicality.  过分熟练地演奏钢琴有时会掩盖音乐性的不足。
Synonyms: glibness, slickness, superficiality
Antonyms:  thoroughness, depth,  meticulousness
词性: 名词
