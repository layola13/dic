excitatory:/ɪkˈsaɪtətɔːri/
adjective
兴奋的；刺激的
Increasing the likelihood of a neuron firing an action potential.  Stimulating or tending to stimulate.
The excitatory effects of caffeine can make it difficult to sleep. 咖啡因的刺激作用会使人难以入睡。
synonyms: stimulating, arousing, invigorating, energizing
antonyms: inhibitory, depressing, suppressing
词性: adjective
