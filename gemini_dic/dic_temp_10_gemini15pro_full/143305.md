outdrink:/ˌaʊtˈdrɪŋk/
verb
喝赢，比…更能喝
to drink more alcoholic beverages than (someone else)
He can outdrink anyone in the bar. 他比酒吧里的任何人都能喝。
近义词: outmatch, surpass
反义词: be outdrunk by
词性: verb
