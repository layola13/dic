backtracker:/ˈbækˌtrækər/| n.|回溯算法；回溯者；倒退者|A person or thing that backtracks, especially a computer program that explores paths from a starting point, returning to earlier points when a path proves unsuccessful.|The backtracker algorithm is used in many search problems. 回溯算法在许多搜索问题中都有应用。|The programmer implemented a backtracker to solve the maze. 程序员实现了一个回溯算法来解决迷宫问题。|Synonyms: retracer, regressor|Antonyms: advancer, progressor|Noun
