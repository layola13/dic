marvelous:/ˈmɑːrvələs/ (US), /ˈmɑːvələs/ (UK)| adj.|不可思议的；惊人的；非凡的；了不起的；|causing wonder; extraordinary.|例句：We had a marvelous time on vacation. 我们假期玩得很开心。|例句：The view from the mountaintop was marvelous. 从山顶望下去的景色美极了。|近义词：wonderful, amazing, fantastic, superb, excellent, outstanding|反义词：terrible, awful, dreadful, horrible|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from Old French merveillos, based on Latin mirabilis ‘wonderful’, from mirari ‘to wonder’.
