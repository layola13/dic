bombard:/bɒmˈbɑːd/ /bɑːmˈbɑːrd/| v.|轰炸，炮击；大量提问；向…大量发送信息|To attack a place with continuous heavy fire from large guns or bombs; to direct a large number of questions or requests at someone. |The city was bombarded for days. 这个城市被轰炸了数日。|近义词：shell, attack, barrage|反义词:defend, protect|动词

bombardment:/bɒmˈbɑːdmənt/ /bɑːmˈbɑːrdmənt/| n.|轰炸，炮击；连番提问；大量发送信息|A continuous attack with bombs, shells, or other missiles; a large number of questions, criticisms, or requests directed at someone. |The bombardment of the city caused widespread destruction. 对城市的轰炸造成了广泛的破坏。|近义词：barrage, attack, shelling|反义词:defense, protection|名词
