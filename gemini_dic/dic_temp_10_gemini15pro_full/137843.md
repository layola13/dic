nonvictory:/ˌnɒnˈvɪktəri/ | n. | 非胜利，未取得胜利 |  Lack of victory; the absence of winning.  It implies not losing, but also not winning, a sort of draw or stalemate. | The game ended in nonvictory for both teams. 这场比赛以两队都没有取得胜利而告终。|  draw, stalemate, tie | victory, win, triumph | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + victory.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Victory" comes from Old French "victorie," ultimately from Latin "victoria" meaning "victory, conquest."
