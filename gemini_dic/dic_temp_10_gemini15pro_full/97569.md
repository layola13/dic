ingrowth:/ˈɪnɡroʊθ/
n.
向内生长，内生，嵌入
The ingrowth of scar tissue can cause problems.  瘢痕组织的向内生长会引起问题。
The ingrowth of roots into the pipe caused a blockage.  树根长入管道造成了堵塞。
近义词:  infiltration, penetration, intrusion
反义词:  outgrowth, extrusion
词性:  noun

Etymology:  From in- + growth.  "In-" indicates direction or location, and "growth" refers to the process of increasing in size.
