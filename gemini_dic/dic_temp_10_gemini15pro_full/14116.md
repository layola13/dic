authenticities:/ˌɔːθɛnˈtɪsɪtiz/ | n. | 真实性；确实性 | The painting’s authenticities has been confirmed by experts.  这幅画的真实性已被专家证实。|  The museum questioned the authenticities of the artifact.  博物馆对文物的真实性提出了质疑。 | 近义词：genuineness, validity, veracity | 反义词：falseness, artificiality | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *authenticitas, from authenticus (“authentic”) + -itas. Ultimately from Ancient Greek αὐθεντικός (authentikós).
