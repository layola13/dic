abscission: /æbˈsɪʒən/
n.
脱落，切除
The natural separation of flowers, fruit, or leaves from a plant.
The surgeon performed an abscission of the diseased tissue.  外科医生切除了病变组织。

The abscission of leaves in the autumn is a normal process. 秋天树叶的脱落是一个正常的过程。

近义词: separation, detachment, shedding
反义词: attachment, adhesion
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abscissio (“a cutting off”), from abscissus, past participle of abscindere (“to cut off, tear away”), from ab- (“off, away from”) + scindere (“to cut, split, cleave”).
