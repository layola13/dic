child:/tʃaɪld/ | n. |孩子|A young human being below the age of puberty or below the legal age of majority.| Example: The child was playing with a toy car.  孩子正在玩一辆玩具汽车。 Example: Every child deserves a good education. 每个孩子都应该接受良好的教育。| 近义词: kid, youngster, offspring | 反义词: adult, grown-up | 词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cild, from Proto-Germanic *kiltham.  Cognate with Dutch kind, German Kind, and Gothic kilþei.
