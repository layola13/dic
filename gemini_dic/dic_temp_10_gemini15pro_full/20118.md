bipalmate:/baɪˈpælmɪt/
adjective
双掌状的；二回掌状的
Having two palmate structures or divisions.  Specifically referring to a leaf or a foot where the structure resembles two open hands joined at the base.
The bipalmate leaves of the Schefflera are quite distinctive.  鹅掌柴的双掌状叶子非常独特。
近义词: palmate, digitate
反义词: pinnate
词性: adjective
