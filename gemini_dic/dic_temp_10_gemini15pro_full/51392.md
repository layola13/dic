determinative:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnətɪv/ |adj. n.|限定的，限定词|A determinative is a word (such as *a*, *the*, *some*, *any*, *my*, *your*, *his*) or a group of words that introduces a noun and limits or clarifies its meaning.|The determinative "the" specifies a particular book.|近义词:limiting, defining, restrictive|反义词:indefinite, nonrestrictive|词性:adjective, noun

determinative:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnətɪv/ | n.|限定词|A determinative is a word (such as *a*, *the*, *some*, *any*, *my*, *your*, *his*) or a group of words that introduces a noun and limits or clarifies its meaning.|The word "the" is a determinative.|近义词:article, quantifier, demonstrative|反义词:None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *determinatyf*, from Late Latin *dēterminātīvus*, from Latin *dētermināre* (to determine).
