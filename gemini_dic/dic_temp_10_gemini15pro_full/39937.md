constructable:/kənˈstrʌktəbl/
adjective
可建造的，可构筑的
capable of being constructed or built.
The bridge is constructable within the proposed budget. 这座桥可以在拟议的预算内建造。
The architect confirmed that the design was constructable. 建筑师确认该设计是可建造的。
近义词:buildable, erectable
反义词:unbuildable, non-constructible
词性:adjective
