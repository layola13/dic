foothills:/ˈfʊthɪlz/| n.|山麓，丘陵地带|A low series of hills at the base of a mountain or mountain range.|We drove through the foothills of the Himalayas. 我们开车穿过喜马拉雅山麓。|Synonyms: lower slopes, foothills, piedmont|Antonyms: summit, peak|noun
