churl:/tʃɜːrl/
|n.|乡下人，粗人，吝啬鬼|A rude, bad-tempered, or stingy person.|The churl refused to give the beggar a penny.|Synonyms: boor, lout, miser, skinflint|Antonyms: gentleman, philanthropist, generous person|noun

|n.|乡下人，粗人，吝啬鬼（churl的复数形式）|Plural form of churl, referring to multiple rude, bad-tempered, or stingy people.|The churls at the tavern mocked the traveler's clothes.|Synonyms: boors, louts, misers, skinflints|Antonyms: gentlemen, philanthropists, generous people|noun
