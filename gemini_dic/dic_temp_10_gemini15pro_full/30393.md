centiloquy: /ˈsɛntɪˌloʊkwi/ | n. | 百言篇; 百句格言集 | A work or collection of one hundred sayings or sentences. |  He compiled a centiloquy of wise sayings from various philosophers. 他从各哲学家那里汇编了一百句格言。 | synonyms: collection, compilation, anthology | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a collection of sayings) | noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *centiloquium, from Latin centum (“hundred”) + loquium (“speech, discourse”).
