disagreer:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːər/
n.|不同意者，反对者|A person who disagrees or holds a contrary opinion.|He's a known disagreer and always challenges the status quo. 他是一个著名的反对者，总是挑战现状。|近义词：dissenter, objector, opponent|反义词：agreer, supporter, proponent|词性:名词
Etymology: dis- (expressing negation) + agree + -er (agent suffix) From the verb "disagree," meaning to have or express a different opinion.  Ultimately from Latin dis- (not) + aggregare (to attach to).
