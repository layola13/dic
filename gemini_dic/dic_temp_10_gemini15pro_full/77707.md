gemlike:/ˈdʒɛmˌlaɪk/
adjective
宝石般的；像宝石的
Resembling a gem, especially in brilliance or color.  Having the qualities of a gem, such as beauty, rarity, or preciousness.
Her eyes were gemlike, sparkling with an inner light. 她的眼睛像宝石一样，闪烁着内在的光芒。
The dew drops on the spider web looked gemlike in the morning sun.  晨曦中的露珠在蜘蛛网上看起来像宝石一样。
synonyms: brilliant, sparkling, glittering, lustrous, radiant, dazzling, jeweled, precious
antonyms: dull, lackluster, opaque
词性: 形容词
