nonequestrians:/ˌnɒnɪˈkwɛstriənz/
noun
非骑马者；非马术爱好者
People who do not ride horses or participate in equestrian activities.
Many nonequestrians enjoy watching horse races. 许多非马术爱好者喜欢观看赛马。
synonyms: nonriders
antonyms: equestrians, horsemen, horsewomen
n.
