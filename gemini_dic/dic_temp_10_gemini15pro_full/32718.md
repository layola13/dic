chlorines:/ˈklɔːriːnz/
noun
氯（气）的复数形式 (更常见用 chlorine 表示)
Plural form of chlorine, referring to multiple instances or types of chlorine (though often simply "chlorine" is used).
The chlorines in the swimming pool kept the water clean.  游泳池里的氯使水保持清洁。
Near synonyms: halogens (more general)
Antonyms: None (chlorine is a specific element)
n.


chlorine:/ˈklɔːriːn/
noun
氯（气）
A greenish-yellow, poisonous gas with a strong smell, used to purify water and to make cleaning products.
Chlorine is commonly used to disinfect swimming pools. 氯通常用于消毒游泳池。
Near synonyms: Cl (chemical symbol) , halogen
Antonyms: None (chlorine is a specific element)
n.
