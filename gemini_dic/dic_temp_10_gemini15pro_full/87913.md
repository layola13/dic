hieroglyphs:/ˈhaɪərəˌɡlɪfs/ | n. | 象形文字 |  A system of writing using picture-like symbols, particularly used in ancient Egypt. Each symbol represents a word, sound, or concept. |  The Rosetta Stone helped scholars decipher Egyptian hieroglyphs.  罗塞塔石碑帮助学者们破译了埃及象形文字。

|  Ancient Egyptians used hieroglyphs to record their history and religious beliefs. 古埃及人使用象形文字记录他们的历史和宗教信仰。 | synonyms:  symbols, pictographs, ideograms | antonyms: alphabet, letters | noun
