melanoscope:/ˈmɛləˌnəˌskoʊp/| n. |黑视素镜，黑素检测器|An instrument used to measure or detect melanin levels.  It can refer to devices used in dermatology to assess skin pigmentation or in other fields to analyze melanin content in biological samples.|The dermatologist used a melanoscope to measure the patient's skin pigmentation.|近义词:  pigmentometer, melanin meter|反义词:  N/A|词性: noun
