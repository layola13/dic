dower:/ˈdaʊər (US), ˈdaʊə (UK)| n. vt.|嫁妆，天赋，给予嫁妆|A dower is the portion of a deceased husband's real property allowed to his widow for her lifetime. As a verb, it means to provide with a dower or a natural gift or talent.|例句：She brought a substantial dower to the marriage. 她为这桩婚姻带来了丰厚的嫁妆。|近义词：dowry, endowment, gift|反义词: debt|noun

dower:/ˈdaʊər (US), ˈdaʊə (UK)| vt.|给予嫁妆，赋予|To dower someone means to provide them with a dower or a natural gift or talent.|例句：Nature had dowered her with both beauty and intelligence.  大自然赋予她美貌和智慧。|近义词：endow, bestow, furnish|反义词: deprive|verb
