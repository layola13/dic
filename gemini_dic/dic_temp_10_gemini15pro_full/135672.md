nonpremium:/ˌnɒnˈpriːmiəm/ | adj. | 非高级的，非优质的，普通的，不加价的 | Not of premium quality; standard or ordinary; not involving a premium payment. |  This nonpremium gasoline is cheaper but might not be as efficient. 这种非高级汽油更便宜，但效率可能不如高级汽油。| The airline offers nonpremium seating at a lower price. 该航空公司以较低的价格提供非高级座位。 | 近义词: standard, regular, ordinary, basic | 反义词: premium, high-end, luxury, top-quality | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (not) + "premium" (of superior quality or value). The prefix "non-" negates the meaning of "premium."
