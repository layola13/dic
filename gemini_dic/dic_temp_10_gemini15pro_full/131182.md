nonbrowsing:/nɒnˈbraʊzɪŋ/
adjective
非浏览的；不浏览的
Not browsing; not engaging in casual looking or searching, especially on the internet.
The library offers nonbrowsing access to specific databases.  图书馆提供对特定数据库的非浏览访问。
Students were given nonbrowsing access to the exam materials. 学生被给予非浏览访问考试资料的权限。
Near Synonyms: restricted, limited, direct, focused
Antonyms: browsing, searching, exploring
词性: adjective
