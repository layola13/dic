mushin:/muːˈʃɪn/| n. | 無心;  空靈;  純粹之心 | A state of mind of no-mindness, free from thoughts and distractions, allowing one to act and react without hesitation or self-consciousness. It is often associated with Zen Buddhism and martial arts.|  He achieved mushin during the kendo match, allowing him to react instinctively. 他在劍道比賽中達到了“無心”的境界，使他能夠本能地做出反應。 |Synonyms:  tranquility, composure, serenity, equanimity |Antonyms: distraction, anxiety, agitation |noun

mushin:/muːˈʃɪn/| n. | 无心; 空灵; 纯粹之心 | The state of mind of no-mindness, being free of thoughts and distractions and thus open to all possibilities. This concept is often associated with Zen Buddhism and martial arts, where it is seen as a desirable state for optimal performance. |  The archer achieved a state of mushin before releasing the arrow.  弓箭手在放箭之前達到了一種無心的狀態。 |Synonyms: calmness, focus, presence |Antonyms:  distraction, anxiety,  preoccupation |noun
