iridescent:/ˌɪrɪˈdɛsənt/| adj.|彩虹色的，闪光的|Showing luminous colors that seem to change when seen from different angles.|The butterfly's wings were iridescent, shimmering in the sunlight. 蝴蝶的翅膀闪着虹彩，在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：opalescent, shimmering, pearly, glistening|反义词：dull, matte|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *īridiscent- (stem of īridiscens), present participle of īridescere ‘to become rainbow-hued’, from īris (genitive īridis) ‘rainbow’ + -escere (inceptive suffix).
