deracinated:/dɪˈræsɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
中文翻译： uprooted; displaced; lacking a sense of belonging; alienated
英文解析：torn up by the roots; having been removed from one's native environment or culture; deprived of one's roots or accustomed environment, especially so as to cause a sense of alienation and loss of identity.
例句：The deracinated refugees struggled to adapt to their new country.  背井离乡的难民努力适应他们的新国家。

例句：He felt deracinated after leaving his childhood home.  离开童年故居后，他感到无所适从。
近义词： uprooted, displaced, exiled, alienated, dispossessed, estranged
反义词： rooted, settled, established, native, indigenous
词性：adjective
