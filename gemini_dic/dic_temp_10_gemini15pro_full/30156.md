celebrated: /ˈsɛləˌbreɪtɪd/ (US), /ˈsɛlɪbreɪtɪd/ (UK)| adj.|著名的，有名望的|Widely known and praised for some special achievement or quality.|例句：He is a celebrated author. 他是一位著名的作家。|近义词：famous, renowned, acclaimed, distinguished|反义词：unknown, obscure, infamous|词性:形容词

celebrated: /ˈsɛləˌbreɪtɪd/ (US), /ˈsɛlɪbreɪtɪd/ (UK)| v. (past participle)|庆祝|To mark (a special occasion, such as a birthday or anniversary) with festivities or other appropriate ceremonies.|例句：They celebrated their anniversary with a lavish party. 他们举办了一个奢华的派对来庆祝他们的结婚纪念日。|近义词：commemorate, observe|反义词：mourn|词性:动词(过去分词)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin celebratus, past participle of celebrare (“to frequent, honor, praise”), from celeber (“frequented, populous, famous”).
