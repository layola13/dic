nonanticipations:/nɒnænˌtɪsɪˈpeɪʃənz/
n.
non-anticipations, lack of anticipation
The absence of expectations or predictions about future events.  This word emphasizes a lack of forethought or planning, often in a financial or legal context.
The contract included a clause regarding nonanticipations of future profits. 合同中包含一项关于不预期未来利润的条款。
Synonyms:  non-expectations, lack of foresight
Antonyms:  expectations, predictions, foresight
noun
