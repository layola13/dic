decoding:/diːˈkəʊdɪŋ/ | n. | 解码，译码 | The process of converting a coded message into intelligible language. |  The decoding of the enemy's messages provided valuable intelligence.  译码敌人的信息提供了宝贵的军事情报。| synonyms: deciphering, interpreting, translating | antonyms: encoding, encrypting | noun


decoding:/diːˈkəʊdɪŋ/ | adj. | 解码的 | Relating to or involved in decoding. |  The decoding software was essential for understanding the data. 解码软件对于理解数据至关重要。 | synonyms: decipherment, interpretive | antonyms: encoding | adjective
