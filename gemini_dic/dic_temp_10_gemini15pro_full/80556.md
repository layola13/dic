goodwill:/ˌɡʊdˈwɪl/| n.|友好, 善意, 商誉|The company's reputation for fair dealing has created a lot of goodwill. 该公司公平交易的声誉创造了大量的商誉。|例句：He bears no ill will toward his ex-wife.  他对前妻没有恶意。

例句：The merger failed due to a lack of goodwill between the two companies.  由于两家公司缺乏善意，合并失败了。|近义词：benevolence, kindness, amity|反义词：hostility, malice, ill will|词性:名词
