disbarring:/dɪsˈbɑːrɪŋ/
verb
剥夺律师资格; 禁止(某人)从事(某职业)
The act of officially preventing someone from continuing to work as a lawyer.
He was disbarred for misconduct. 他因行为不端被取消了律师资格。
Synonyms:  expelling,  debarring,  excluding
Antonyms: admitting, reinstating
verb
