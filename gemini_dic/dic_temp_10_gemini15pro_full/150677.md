papyrus: /pəˈpaɪrəs/ | n. | 纸草，莎草纸 | A material similar to thick paper that was used in ancient times for writing or painting on, made from the pith of the papyrus plant. |  Example 1:  The ancient Egyptians used papyrus to create scrolls. 古埃及人用纸莎草制作卷轴。

Example 2:  The museum displayed a collection of papyrus manuscripts. 博物馆展出了一批莎草纸手稿。 | 近义词: parchment, vellum | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps modern paper) | 词性: noun


papyrus: /pəˈpaɪrəs/ | n. | 纸草植物 | The tall aquatic plant, Cyperus papyrus, native to North Africa, from which papyrus was made. | Example 1:  Papyrus grows along the banks of the Nile River. 纸莎草生长在尼罗河畔。

Example 2:  The papyrus plant can grow up to 16 feet tall. 纸莎草植物可以长到16英尺高。 | 近义词: bulrush, reed | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
