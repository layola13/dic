caryatides:/ˌkærɪˈætɪdiːz/
noun
女像柱；女像柱式；雕刻成妇女形象的支柱
Sculptured figures of women, used as supporting columns.
The Erechtheion temple on the Acropolis is famous for its Porch of the Caryatides.  雅典卫城的厄瑞克忒翁神庙以其女像柱门廊而闻名。
The museum displayed several Roman copies of Greek caryatides.  博物馆展出了几件罗马复制的希腊女像柱。
synonyms:atlantes (male figures used as columns), supporting figures, architectural sculptures
antonyms:column, pillar (generic terms, not specifically figurative)
n.
