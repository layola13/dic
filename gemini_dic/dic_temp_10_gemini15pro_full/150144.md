pan-caking:/ˈpænˌkeɪkɪŋ/
verb
煎饼化; 扁平化
The process of a structure collapsing or flattening, resembling a stack of pancakes.
The building pan-caked during the earthquake.  大楼在地震中像煎饼一样坍塌了。
The soil pan-caked under the weight of the heavy machinery.  重型机械的重量使土壤变得扁平。
近义词: collapsing, flattening, crumbling
反义词: rising, expanding, strengthening
词性: verb
