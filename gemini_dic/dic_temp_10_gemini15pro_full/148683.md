overtinseled: /ˌoʊvərˈtɪnsəld/ | adj. | 过分装饰的，过于华丽的 | Decorated with too much tinsel or other showy ornaments; excessively ornate or gaudy.  Having too many decorations, especially shiny ones, in a way that looks cheap or excessive. | The overtinseled Christmas tree looked gaudy and cheap.  这棵圣诞树装饰得过于华丽，看起来很俗气。| The celebrity's overtinseled mansion was featured in a magazine spread.  这位名人的豪宅装饰得过于华丽，在杂志上刊登了。 | 近义词: gaudy, garish, ostentatious, flashy | 反义词: simple, understated, plain | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "tinseled" (past participle of "tinsel," meaning "to decorate with tinsel"). The word "tinsel" itself comes from the Old French "estincelle," meaning "spark."
