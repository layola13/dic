outriggerless:/ˌaʊtˈrɪɡərlɪs/
adjective
无支腿的，无舷外支架的
Lacking outriggers; without stabilizing floats.
The outriggerless canoe was less stable than the one with outriggers.  这艘没有支腿的独木舟比有支腿的独木舟稳定性差。
The outriggerless boat was difficult to maneuver in rough seas.  这艘没有舷外支架的船在波涛汹涌的海面上很难操控。
Synonyms: unsupported, unstabilized
Antonyms: outriggered, stabilized
词性: adjective
