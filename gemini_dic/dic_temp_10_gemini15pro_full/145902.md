overdiffuses:/ˌoʊvərdiˈfjuːzɪz/
verb
过度扩散
To spread or disperse something excessively or beyond what is necessary or desirable.
The paint overdiffused on the canvas, creating a blurry effect.  油漆在画布上过度扩散，造成模糊的效果。
The speaker overdiffused his message, losing the audience's attention. 演讲者过度传播他的信息，失去了观众的注意力。
近义词: overspread, oversaturate, overextend
反义词: concentrate, focus, condense
词性: verb

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "diffuse" (from Latin "diffundere" meaning "to pour out, spread")
