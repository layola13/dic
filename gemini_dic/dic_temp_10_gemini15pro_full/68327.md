fall-flat:/fɔːl ˈflæt/ | phrasal verb | 彻底失败；完全不起作用 | To fail completely to achieve the desired effect or outcome; to be unsuccessful. |  The comedian's jokes fell flat and the audience remained silent.  喜剧演员的笑话完全失败了，观众席上一片寂静。

The advertising campaign fell flat and sales actually declined. 广告宣传活动彻底失败了，销售额实际上反而下降了。 | 近义词: fail miserably, flop, bomb, tank | 反义词: succeed, triumph, be a hit | 词性: 动词短语
