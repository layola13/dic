bemingle:/bɪˈmɪŋɡəl/
verb
混合，交往
To mix or mingle together; to cause to be mixed or mingled.
The scents of various spices bemingled in the air.  各种香料的气味在空气中混合。
At the party, guests from different backgrounds bemingled easily.  在派对上，来自不同背景的客人们轻松地交往。
近义词:mix, blend, intermingle, socialize, fraternize
反义词:separate, segregate, isolate
词性:v.

Etymology:
From be- + mingle, from Old English mengan "to mix."
