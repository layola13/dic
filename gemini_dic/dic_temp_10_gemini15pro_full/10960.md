arbitrages:/ˈɑːrbɪtrɑːʒɪz/
n.
套利；套汇；仲裁
The simultaneous purchase and sale of an asset to profit from a difference in the price. It exploits price discrepancies for the same or similar financial instruments traded on different markets or in different forms.
He made a fortune from currency arbitrages. 他通过货币套利发了财。
Synonyms: speculation, profiteering, exploitation
Antonyms: investment, lending, borrowing
noun
