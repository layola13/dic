cession:/ˈsɛʃən/| n.|割让，转让；放弃|The cession of Hong Kong to China took place in 1997. 香港于1997年割让给中国。|Example: The treaty included the cession of several territories. 该条约包括了几个领土的割让。|Synonyms: surrender, relinquishment, transfer|Antonyms: acquisition, retention|noun

cession:/ˈsɛʃən/| n.|（财产、权利等的）转让，让与；（尤指债权人对债务人财产的）让与|The bank demanded the cession of his assets to cover the debt. 银行要求他转让资产以偿还债务。|Example: The cession of the property to his son was completed last week.  房产上周已转让给他儿子。|Synonyms: conveyance, assignment|Antonyms: acquisition, retention|noun
