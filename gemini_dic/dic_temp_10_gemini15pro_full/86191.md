heatednesses:/ˈhiːtɪdnɪsɪz/
noun
激烈；热烈；激动
the state of being heated or excited
例句：The heatednesses of the political debate surprised many. 政治辩论的激烈程度让许多人感到惊讶。

例句：The heatednesses of their argument showed how much they cared. 他们争论的激烈程度表明他们多么在乎。

近义词：intensity, fervor, passion, vehemence
反义词：calmness, coolness, composure, tranquility
词性：n.
