localistic:/ˌloʊkəlˈɪstɪk/
adjective
地方主义的；地方性的
Relating to or characterized by localism; limited to a particular locality.
The museum's collection has a strong localistic focus, showcasing the history and art of the region.  这个博物馆的收藏具有强烈的地方主义色彩，展示了该地区的历史和艺术。
The politician's localistic approach alienated voters in other parts of the country.  这位政治家的地方主义做法疏远了该国其他地区的选民。
近义词:regional, provincial, parochial
反义词:cosmopolitan, global, universal
