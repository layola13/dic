hyads:/ˈhaɪədz/| n.|毕星团; 雨星|A star cluster in the constellation Taurus, containing hundreds of stars, including the bright stars Aldebaran, and marking the Bull's face.  It is associated with rain in ancient mythology.|The Hyades appear low in the winter sky.|近义词：star cluster, open cluster|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific star cluster)|noun
