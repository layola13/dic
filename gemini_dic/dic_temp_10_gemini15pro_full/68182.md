faintishness:/ˈfeɪntɪʃnəs/| n. |昏厥；眩晕；虚弱|A state of feeling faint, lightheaded, or weak.|She experienced a sudden faintishness and had to sit down. 她突然感到一阵眩晕，不得不坐下来。|例句：The heat and the long journey brought on a feeling of faintishness. 炎热和长途旅行使她感到一阵虚弱。|近义词：dizziness, lightheadedness, weakness, giddiness|反义词:strength, robustness|词性:noun
