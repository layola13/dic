homebody:/ˈhoʊmˌbɑːdi/| n.|宅男，宅女；喜欢待在家里的人|A person who prefers to stay at home and seldom goes out for social events.|例句：She's a real homebody and prefers a quiet night in to a wild party. 她是个真正的宅女，喜欢安静的夜晚而不是狂野的派对。

例句：He's always been a bit of a homebody, content with his books and his garden. 他一直有点宅，满足于他的书籍和花园。|近义词：stay-at-home, introvert, recluse|反义词：social butterfly, extrovert, party animal|词性: noun
