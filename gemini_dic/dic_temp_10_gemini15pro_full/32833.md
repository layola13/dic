cholagogue:/ˈkoʊləɡɔːɡ/| n.|利胆剂；促胆汁分泌剂|A cholagogue is a substance that promotes the discharge of bile from the liver and gallbladder.|The doctor prescribed a cholagogue to help with the patient's gallstones.|近义词:choleretic|反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly bile suppressant)|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Greek *kholē* (bile) + *agōgos* (leading, conveying).
