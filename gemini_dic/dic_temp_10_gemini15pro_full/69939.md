fess's:/ˈfɛsɪz/| n.| 费用，供认，坦白 |  Archaic or dialect form of "fees," meaning charges or payments for services.  It is the possessive form, indicating ownership or belonging to a fess.  In some contexts, "fess" itself can also mean a confession. |  The fess's were exorbitant for the services rendered.  这些服务的费用过高。 | Synonym: fees, charges, costs, confession, admission | Antonym:  income, earnings, denial | noun
