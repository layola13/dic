hedger:/ˈhɛdʒər/| n.| 套期保值者；规避风险者 |A person or company that tries to reduce the risk of losing money on an investment by buying or selling contracts that protect them against future price changes. |Example:  The hedger bought put options to protect against a fall in the stock price. 套期保值者购买了看跌期权，以防止股价下跌。

Example:  Large agricultural companies often act as hedgers in the commodities markets. 大型农业公司通常在大宗商品市场上充当套期保值者。| 近义词: speculator (投机者 - 反义), investor (投资者) | 反义词: speculator (投机者)| noun
