The boxer trained like a grinder, pushing himself to the limit every day.  这位拳击手像个苦工一样训练，每天都把自己逼到极限。|近义词：mill, pulverizer, crush (for machine); toiler, drudge (for person)|反义词: (For person) loafer, idler|词性:noun
