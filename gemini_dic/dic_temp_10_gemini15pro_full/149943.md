pallidly:/ˈpæɪlɪdli/| adv.|苍白地；暗淡地|in a pale manner; wanly.  The moon shone pallidly through the clouds.|例句：He smiled pallidly, clearly unwell. 他脸色苍白地笑了笑，显然不舒服。

例句：The streetlights shone pallidly in the fog. 街灯在雾中发出苍白的光。|近义词：wanly, palely, faintly|反义词：brightly, vividly, healthily|词性：副词
