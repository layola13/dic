deary:/ˈdɪəri/ /ˈdɪri/| n. adj.|亲爱的，宝贝；可爱的|A term of endearment, often used in a slightly patronizing or humorous way.|例句：Oh deary me, what a mess! 哦，天哪，真是一团糟！

例句：Come along, deary, let's get you home. 来吧，亲爱的，我们送你回家。|近义词：dear, darling, sweetheart, honey|反义词: (No direct antonym for a term of endearment)|词性:名词，形容词
