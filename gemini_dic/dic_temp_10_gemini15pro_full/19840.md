binbashi:/bɪnˈbɑːʃi/| n. |营长；千夫长|A military rank in some Middle Eastern and North African armies, roughly equivalent to major.  It was used in Egypt and the Ottoman Empire. | Example 1: The binbashi led his troops into battle.  营长率领他的部队投入战斗。

Example 2: He was promoted to the rank of binbashi. 他被提升为营长。|Synonym: major, commander|Antonym: private, soldier|Noun
