enoptomancy:/ɪˌnɒptəˈmænsi/ | n. |镜卜；镜占|Divination using a mirror, often by observing reflections or hallucinatory images. It involves using a mirror as a tool for scrying.  |Example: The ancient Greeks practiced enoptomancy to foresee the future. 古希腊人通过镜卜来预测未来。

Example: She attempted enoptomancy, hoping to catch a glimpse of her future husband. 她尝试镜卜，希望能瞥见她未来的丈夫。| 近义词: scrying, crystal gazing | 反义词:  None (lack of a direct antonym for this specific divination method)|词性:noun
