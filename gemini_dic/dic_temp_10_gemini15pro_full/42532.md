countermanifesto:/ˌkaʊntərˈmænɪˈfɛstoʊ/
n.|反宣言，反对声明|A public declaration, usually in writing, that opposes or challenges the principles or claims of a previous manifesto.|The artist issued a countermanifesto rejecting the prevailing artistic trends of the time. 这位艺术家发表了一份反宣言，反对当时的流行艺术趋势。|rebuttal, denunciation, refutation|proclamation, affirmation, manifesto
词性:名词
