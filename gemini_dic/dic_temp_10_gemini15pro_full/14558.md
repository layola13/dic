averting:/əˈvɜːrtɪŋ/
verb
避免，防止
preventing something bad from happening
例句：
The company took steps to avert a financial crisis.
公司采取措施避免了金融危机。

He swerved to avert a collision.
他突然转向避免了碰撞。


近义词：preventing, avoiding, precluding, obviating
反义词：causing, provoking, inciting
词性：v.
