partially:/ˈpɑːrʃəli/ | adv. | 部分地，不完全地 | Partly; not completely. | The house was only partially built. 这房子只建了一部分。| The road is partially blocked by a fallen tree. 道路被一棵倒下的树部分阻塞了。| 近义词: partly, incompletely, in part | 反义词: completely, fully, totally | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *partialis, from Latin pars (genitive partis) "part, piece, share, portion, fraction; party, faction; role, character (in a play); side, direction".
