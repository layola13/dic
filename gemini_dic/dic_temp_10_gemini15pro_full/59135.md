dynamites:/ˈdaɪnəˌmaɪts/| n. vt.|炸药，炸毁|A high explosive consisting of nitroglycerin mixed with an absorbent material and typically molded into sticks.  It can also be used as a verb meaning to blow up with dynamite.|例句：The miners used dynamites to blast through the rock. 矿工们用炸药炸穿岩石。

例句：They dynamited the old bridge. 他们炸毁了那座旧桥。|近义词：explosives, TNT, bombs|反义词: N/A (no direct antonym for a physical object)|词性: noun, verb
