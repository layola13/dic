chalazal:/kəˈleɪzəl/| adj.|合点的，基生的|Relating to or situated at or near the chalaza, the region where the yolk is attached to the egg white.  It refers specifically to the part of a plant ovule or seed where the integuments and nucellus are joined.|The chalazal end of the ovule is opposite the micropyle.|近义词:basal, antipodal|反义词:micropylar|词性:形容词
