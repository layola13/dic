blackfly:/ˈblækflaɪ/| n. |黑蝇|A small, biting fly of the family Simuliidae, found near fast-flowing streams.  The females suck blood and can transmit diseases.|Blackflies are a real nuisance when camping near rivers.  在河边露营时，黑蝇真是一大 nuisance。|近义词：gnat, midge|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific insect)|noun
