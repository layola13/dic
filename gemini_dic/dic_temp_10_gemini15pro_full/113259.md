lustrical:/ˈlʌstrɪkəl/
adjective
净化的；用于净化的
relating to or used in purification
例句：Lustrical water was used in the ancient ceremony. 古代仪式中使用了净化水。

例句：The priest performed the lustrical rites.  牧师进行了净化仪式。
近义词：purifying, cleansing, cathartic
反义词：contaminating, polluting, defiling
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lustricus, from lustrare (“to purify”), from lustrum (“purification”).
