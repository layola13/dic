overcleans:/ˌoʊvərˈkliːnz/
verb
过度清洁
To clean something excessively, often to the point of damaging or removing beneficial elements.
She overcleans her skin, which strips away its natural oils. 她过度清洁皮肤，导致皮肤天然油脂流失。
My mother tends to overclean the house, using harsh chemicals that are probably not necessary. 我妈妈总是把房子打扫得过度干净，使用一些可能并不必要的刺激性化学品。
近义词: overwash, scrub excessively
反义词: underclean
词性: verb
