huntable:/ˈhʌntəbl/| adj.|可猎捕的，适于猎取的|Suitable or permitted to be hunted.  This refers to animals or, metaphorically, to things that can be pursued or sought after.|The deer is huntable during certain seasons.  鹿在某些季节是可以猎捕的。|The elusive hacker proved huntable after all.  那个难以捉摸的黑客最终还是被抓住了。|近义词：catchable, quarry, game, prey|反义词：protected, endangered, off-limits|词性:形容词
