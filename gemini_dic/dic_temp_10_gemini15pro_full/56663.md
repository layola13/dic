donyo:/ˈdɔnjoʊ/ | n. | 山峰，小山 | A hill or small mountain, especially in East Africa. | The donyo overlooks the vast plains. 这座小山俯瞰着广阔的平原。 | 近义词：hill, knoll, mound | 反义词: valley, plain | 词性: noun

词源:  From Swahili *donyo, from Proto-Bantu *-donyo.
