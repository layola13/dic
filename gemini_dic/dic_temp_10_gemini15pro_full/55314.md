dissolve:/dɪˈzɒlv/ (British), /dɪˈzɑːlv/ (American)| v. n.|溶解，解散，消失|To become incorporated into a liquid so as to form a solution; to cause a solid to pass into solution; to bring to an end; to terminate.|The sugar dissolved quickly in the hot water. 糖在热水中很快溶解了。|近义词：melt, liquefy, disintegrate, disperse|反义词: solidify, congeal, freeze|verb
dissolve:/dɪˈzɒlv/ (British), /dɪˈzɑːlv/ (American)| n.|溶解，溶化|The process of dissolving; the state of being dissolved.|The dissolve between the two scenes was seamless.  两个场景之间的溶解过渡天衣无缝。|近义词：solution, disintegration|反义词: solidification, congelation|noun
