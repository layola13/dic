hummock:/ˈhʌmək/ (US), /ˈhʌmək/ (UK)| n. | 小丘；土墩；冰丘 | A small, rounded hill or mound of earth or ice. | 例句：The explorers crossed a landscape dotted with hummocks of frozen earth. 探险者们穿越了点缀着冻土丘的景色。

例句：The ice floes were broken into a chaotic jumble of hummocks. 浮冰被打破成一堆混乱的冰丘。

近义词：knoll, mound, hillock, dune | 反义词: hollow, depression | 词性: noun
