impenetrableness:/ɪmˌpɛnɪtrəbəlnəs/| n.| 不可渗透性；难以理解|The impenetrableness of the jungle made it difficult to explore.  这片丛林的不可渗透性使它难以被探索。| The impenetrableness of his silence frustrated her.  他沉默的难以理解让她感到沮丧。| 近义词：impermeability, opacity, incomprehensibility | 反义词：permeability, transparency, clarity | 词性: noun

impenetrable:/ɪmˈpɛnɪtrəbəl/| adj.| 不可渗透的；难以理解的| The fortress walls were impenetrable.  堡垒的城墙坚不可摧。| His motives remained impenetrable. 他的动机仍然难以理解。| 近义词：impervious, opaque, incomprehensible | 反义词：permeable, transparent, clear | 词性: adjective

penetrate:/ˈpɛnɪtreɪt/| v.| 穿透；渗透；洞察| The bullet penetrated the wall. 子弹穿透了墙壁。| The rain penetrated his coat. 雨水渗透了他的外套。| 近义词：pierce, permeate, fathom | 反义词： - | 词性: verb

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from late Latin impenetrabilis, from in- ‘not’ + penetrabilis (from penetrare ‘to penetrate’).
