multichromes:/ˈmʌltiˌkrəʊmz/
|n.|多色，变色颜料|A substance, typically used in cosmetics or paints, that appears to shift between multiple colors depending on the angle of view or lighting.  This effect is caused by thin-film interference.|The makeup artist used multichromes to create a dazzling eye look. 化妆师用多色颜料打造了一个令人眼花缭乱的眼妆。|近义词:iridescent, chameleon, duo-chrome, pearlescent|反义词:monochrome, single-color|词性:名词

Etymology:
"multi-" (from Latin "multus" meaning "many") + "-chrome" (from Greek "chrōma" meaning "color")
