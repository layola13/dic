ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *abel*, from Old French *able*, from Latin *albellus* ("whitish"), diminutive of *albus* ("white").

USAGE->
The abele tree is known for its silvery leaves. = 白杨树以其银色的叶子而闻名。

The carpenter used abele wood to make the furniture. = 木匠用白杨木制作家具。
