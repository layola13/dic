manganese-dioxide:/ˌmæŋɡəˈniːz daɪˈɒksaɪd/ | n. | 二氧化锰 | A black or brown mineral occurring naturally as pyrolusite and used as a depolarizer in dry-cell batteries and as an oxidizing agent. | Manganese dioxide is used in the production of batteries. 二氧化锰用于电池生产。| Manganese dioxide is commonly used in dry cell batteries to prevent the buildup of hydrogen gas.  二氧化锰常用于干电池中以防止氢气积聚。| Synonyms: pyrolusite | Antonyms: N/A | noun
