liberalisms:/ˈlɪbərəlɪzəmz/
n.
自由主义（的各种形式）
Various doctrines of liberalism.
Liberalism has taken many forms throughout history.  历史上，自由主义采取了多种形式。
synonyms: ideologies, doctrines, philosophies, principles, tenets, schools of thought
antonyms: conservatisms, authoritarianisms
