arseniate:/ˈɑːrsəneɪt/ | n. |砷酸盐|A salt or ester of arsenic acid.  Arsenic acid is H3AsO4, so an arsenate contains the arsenate anion, AsO43-.|Sodium arsenate is a common herbicide. 砷酸钠是一种常见的除草剂。|Synonyms: arsenical (more general, refers to arsenic compounds)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific chemical compound)|noun
