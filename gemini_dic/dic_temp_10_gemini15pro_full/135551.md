nonpoisonousness:/ˌnɒnˈpɔɪzənəsnəs/
noun
无毒；无害
The nonpoisonousness of the berries was confirmed by the botanist.  这些浆果的无毒性得到了植物学家的证实。
例句：The mushroom's nonpoisonousness made it safe to eat. 这种蘑菇的无毒性使得它可以安全食用。
例句：He assured us of the plant's nonpoisonousness. 他向我们保证这种植物是无毒的。
近义词：innocuousness, harmlessness, safety
反义词：toxicity, poisonousness, venomousness
词性:n.


Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + poisonous + -ness (suffix forming nouns of quality or state)
From non- + poisonous + -ness.  Non- comes from Latin, meaning "not." Poisonous comes from Old French *poisonos, ultimately from Latin potionem "a drink, draught," especially a poisonous drink.  The suffix -ness is used to form nouns from adjectives.
