boomeranger's:/ˈbuːməræŋərz/ |n. |回旋镖拥有者（的）| belonging to a person who uses or throws boomerangs.  This is the possessive form of "boomeranger". |  The boomeranger's skill was impressive.  回旋镖拥有者的技术令人印象深刻。| The boomeranger's pouch held several different boomerangs.  回旋镖拥有者的小袋里装着几个不同的回旋镖。 | owner, possessor |  --- | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "boomerang" + "-er" (agent suffix) + "-'s" (possessive suffix). "Boomerang" itself is from Dharug (an Aboriginal Australian language) *bumariny*.
