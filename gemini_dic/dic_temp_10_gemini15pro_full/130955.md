nonassisters:/ˌnɑːnəˈsɪstərz/ | n. | 非助攻者；未助攻者 |  People who do not provide an assist (in sports, especially basketball).  It refers to players who are on the court during a scoring play but don't directly contribute to the basket being made. | The nonassisters on the team need to work on their passing and court awareness.  该队未助攻的球员需要改进他们的传球和场上意识。|  non-contributors, bystanders | contributors, assisters | noun
