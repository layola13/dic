Carpathians: /kɑːrˈpeɪθiənz/ | n. |  喀尔巴阡山脉 | A mountain range in Central and Eastern Europe. |  The Carpathians stretch across several countries, including Romania and Ukraine. 喀尔巴阡山脉横跨几个国家，包括罗马尼亚和乌克兰。| synonyms: mountain chain, range | antonyms: plain, flatland | noun

Etymology: From German *Karbaten, from Latin Carpates, ultimately from a pre-Roman name, probably of Dacian or Thracian origin. Possibly related to the Albanian word *karpë "rock".
