communicators:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtərz/
|n.|传播者；沟通者|People who communicate information, ideas, or feelings.|The conference attracted communicators from all over the world.  这次会议吸引了来自世界各地的传播者。|synonyms: messengers, speakers, broadcasters, journalists, reporters, correspondents|antonyms: receivers, audience, listeners|noun


communicators:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtərz/
|n.|通讯设备|Devices used for communication.|Modern communicators, such as smartphones, have revolutionized the way we interact. 现代通讯设备，例如智能手机，彻底改变了我们互动的方式。|synonyms: devices, gadgets, apparatus, instruments|antonyms:  N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "communicate" + "-or" (agent suffix).  "Communicate" comes from the Latin "communicatus," past participle of "communicare," meaning "to share, divide out; communicate, impart, inform," from "communis" meaning "common."
