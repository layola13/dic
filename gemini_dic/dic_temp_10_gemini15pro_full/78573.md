giallolino:/ˌdʒɑːloʊˈliːnoʊ/| adj.|淡黄色的，浅黄色的|A pale or light yellow color.  It comes from the Italian word "giallo" meaning yellow, with the suffix "-ino" indicating smallness or lightness.|The walls were painted a soft giallolino.|近义词：pale yellow, light yellow, straw-colored, primrose|反义词: dark yellow, golden yellow|词性:adjective
