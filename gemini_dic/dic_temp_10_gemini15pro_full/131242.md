noncarbohydrates:/ˌnɒnˌkɑːrboʊˈhaɪdreɪts/ | n. |非碳水化合物|Substances that are not carbohydrates, including fats, proteins, and fiber.  These do not break down into simple sugars.|  Athletes often consume noncarbohydrates before endurance events to avoid sugar crashes.  运动员在耐力比赛前经常食用非碳水化合物以避免血糖骤降。 | synonyms: non-saccharides | antonyms: carbohydrates | noun
