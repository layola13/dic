intelligencio: /ɪnˌtɛlɪˈdʒɛntsioʊ/
n.
知识分子；知识阶层
A group of intelligent and well-educated people who guide or try to guide the political and cultural life of their society.
例句：The intelligencio of the country were critical of the government's policies.  这个国家的知识分子批评了政府的政策。

例句：The artist found a receptive audience among the intelligencio.  这位艺术家在知识分子中找到了接受他的观众。

近义词：intellectuals, intelligentsia
反义词：masses, commoners
词性：名词
