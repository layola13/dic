nondiligently:/ˌnɒnˈdɪlɪdʒəntli/ | adv. | 不勤奋地，不努力地 | Not diligently; in a lazy or careless manner. | He completed the project nondiligently, resulting in numerous errors. 他很不认真地完成了这个项目，导致了许多错误。 |  She approached her studies nondiligently, preferring to socialize. 她学习不努力，更喜欢社交。 | 近义词: carelessly, lazily, idly | 反义词: diligently, assiduously, carefully | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + diligently (in a hard-working manner).  "Diligent" comes from the Latin *diligens*, present participle of diligere "to value highly, esteem, love; to select carefully," from dis- "apart" + legere "to choose, gather, read."
