unlimited:/ʌnˈlɪmɪtɪd/
adj.
无限的，无限制的
without any limits or restrictions.
例句：They offer unlimited data plans. 他们提供无限流量套餐。
例句：The possibilities are unlimited.  可能性是无限的。
近义词：boundless, infinite, unrestricted
反义词：limited, restricted, finite
词性: 形容词

词源:
From un- (not) + limited (restricted).  The word "limit" comes from the Latin "limes" meaning "boundary."
