dualism:/ˈduːəlɪzəm/ | n. |二元论; 双重性|The view that two fundamental concepts or forces exist, such as good and evil, mind and matter, or body and soul.|
Descartes' philosophy is often described as a form of dualism.  笛卡尔的哲学常被描述为一种二元论的形式。

The novel explores the dualism of good and evil in human nature. 这部小说探讨了人性中善与恶的二元论。|
近义词：dichotomy, polarity |反义词：monism, unity | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
