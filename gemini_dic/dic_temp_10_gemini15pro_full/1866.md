acropodium: /əˈkrɒpəʊdiəm/
noun
肢尖
The terminal part of a limb, such as a foot, hand, or tentacle.
The acropodium of the primate hand allows for intricate manipulation. 灵长类动物手的肢尖可以进行复杂的操控。
The gecko's acropodium has specialized adhesive pads for climbing. 壁虎的肢尖有专门的粘性垫，用于攀爬。

近义词: extremity, apex, tip
反义词: base, proximal end
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek akros (highest, outermost) + pous, pod- (foot).  Thus, literally "tip of the foot".
