eyeleting: /ˈaɪlɪtɪŋ/
n.
穿孔，打孔眼；孔眼，金属圈
The act of putting eyelets in something, or the eyelets themselves.
Example: The eyeleting on the shoes is decorative and functional. 鞋子上的孔眼既装饰又实用。

Example: She spent the afternoon eyeleting the curtains. 她一下午都在给窗帘打孔眼。

近义词: grommeting, punching
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "sealing" as an opposite action)


词性: noun


词源: From eyelet + -ing.  "Eyelet" comes from the Old French "oeillet" meaning "little eye."
