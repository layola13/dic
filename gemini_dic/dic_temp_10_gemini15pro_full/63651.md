eozoon:/ˌiːəˈzoʊɒn/
n.
Eozoon canadense (obsolete)
加拿大始生虫（已废弃）
A layered mineral structure, once thought to be the fossilized remains of a foraminiferan, but now considered to be of inorganic origin.
一种层状矿物结构，曾被认为是有孔虫的化石残骸，但现在被认为是无机成因的。
The discovery of eozoon sparked a heated debate in the scientific community.  加拿大始生虫的发现引发了科学界的激烈争论。
Eozoon was once presented as evidence of early life on Earth.  始生虫曾被作为地球早期生命存在的证据。
