节俭；俭省
The state or quality of being frugal; thriftiness.
Her frugalnesses allowed her to save a significant portion of her income. 她的节俭使她能够节省很大一部分收入。
My grandmother's frugalnesses was legendary; she could stretch a dollar like nobody else.  我祖母的节俭是传奇性的；她比任何人都更能精打细算。
synonyms: thrift, economy, frugality, parsimony
antonyms: extravagance, lavishness, prodigality, wastefulness


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *frugalnesse*, from Old French frugalité, from Latin frugalitas (“frugality, temperance”), from frugālis (“fruitful, useful, economical, temperate”), from frux (“fruit, produce, profit”).
