airier:/ˈeəriər/ | adj. | 更通风的，更轻快的 | More open to the passage or circulation of air; lighter or more delicate. | The new office is much airier than the old one. 新办公室比旧办公室通风多了。|Synonyms: breezier, lighter, more spacious | Antonyms: stuffier, heavier, more cramped | adjective

airier:/ˈeəriər/ | adj. | 更轻快的，更无忧无虑的 | Having a light-hearted or carefree quality. | She felt airier after a good night's sleep.  睡了一夜好觉后，她感觉轻松多了。| Synonyms: carefree, lighthearted, cheerful | Antonyms: serious, heavy-hearted, gloomy | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From air + -ier (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *aerius, from aer "air."
