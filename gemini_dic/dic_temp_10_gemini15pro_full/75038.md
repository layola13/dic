friezy:/ˈfriːzi/ | adj. | 毛茸茸的；有毛边的；起绒的 | Covered with or resembling a frieze; having a nap or pile. | The old teddy bear was friezy and worn.  这只旧泰迪熊毛茸茸的，而且破旧了。| The fabric has a friezy texture. 这块布料有一种毛茸茸的质感。 |  近义词: fuzzy, nappy, shaggy, fluffy | 反义词: smooth, sleek | 词性: adjective

frieze:/friːz/ | n. | (建筑)中楣；横饰带；粗呢 | A broad horizontal band of sculpted or painted decoration, especially on a wall near the ceiling; a coarse woolen cloth. | The frieze of the temple was decorated with intricate carvings.  寺庙的中楣装饰着复杂的雕刻。 |  He wore a frieze coat to keep warm in the winter. 他穿了一件粗呢大衣在冬天保暖。| 近义词: border, band, trim, decoration | 反义词: (For cloth) fine fabric | 词性: noun

词源:  "Frieze" (the cloth) comes from Middle French "frise," referring to Friesland, a region known for its wool. "Frieze" (the architectural term) comes from Italian "fregio," ultimately from Latin "phrygium" (embroidered work), referring to Phrygia, an ancient region in Asia Minor.
