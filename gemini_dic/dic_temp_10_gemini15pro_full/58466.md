ductless:/ˈdʌktləs/
adj.
无管的，没有导管的
Not having ducts; lacking tubes or channels for conveying fluids.
The ductless glands secrete hormones directly into the bloodstream. 无管腺体直接将激素分泌到血液中。

The house has a ductless air conditioning system. 这房子有一个无管空调系统。
