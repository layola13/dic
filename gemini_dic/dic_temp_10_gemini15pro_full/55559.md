distrustful:/dɪsˈtrʌstfəl/ | adj. |不信任的，怀疑的 | Lacking trust; inclined to doubt or suspect. | She was distrustful of strangers. 她不信任陌生人。|  He gave me a distrustful look. 他给了我一个怀疑的眼神。 | suspicious, wary, skeptical, dubious | trusting, credulous, gullible | adjective

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation) + trustful (full of trust).  The word "trust" ultimately comes from Old Norse "traust," meaning "confidence, support."
