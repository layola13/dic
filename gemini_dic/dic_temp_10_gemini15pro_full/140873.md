on-the-side:/ˌɑːn ðə ˈsaɪd/ | adj. adv.| 额外的，兼职的；秘密地，暗地里 | Something done "on the side" is done in addition to one's regular job or activity, often secretly or discreetly. | He has a regular job, but he also does some web design work on the side. 他有一份正式工作，但也兼职做一些网页设计工作。 | synonyms: extra, additional, supplementary, secretly, discreetly | antonyms: primary, main, openly, publicly | adverb adjective
