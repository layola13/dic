hyperlethal:/ˌhaɪpərˈliːθəl/
adjective
极度致命的，超级致命的
Excessively lethal; extremely deadly.  Characterized by a very high mortality rate.
The hyperlethal virus quickly spread through the population.  这种极度致命的病毒迅速在人群中传播。
The researchers were studying a hyperlethal strain of the bacteria. 研究人员正在研究一种极度致命性的细菌菌株。
Synonym: deadly, fatal, mortal, virulent
Antonym:  harmless, benign, innocuous
