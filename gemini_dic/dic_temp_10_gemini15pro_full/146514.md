overgenerous:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒenərəs/| adj.|过分慷慨的，过于大方的|Excessively generous; giving more than is necessary or expected.|例句：He was overgenerous with his praise. 他过分地赞扬了别人。|近义词：lavish, extravagant, prodigal|反义词：stingy, miserly, parsimonious|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "over-" (excessively) + "generous" (giving freely). "Generous" ultimately derives from Latin "generosus" (of noble birth), implying nobility of spirit and liberality.
