beltane:/ˈbɛl.teɪn/| n. |五朔节|A Pagan May Day festival, celebrating the beginning of summer.|例句：The villagers gathered for the Beltane celebrations. 村民们聚集在一起庆祝五朔节。

例句：Beltane is a time of rebirth and renewal. 五朔节是重生和 renewal 的时刻。|近义词：May Day, Walpurgis Night|反义词:Samhain (Halloween)|词性:noun
