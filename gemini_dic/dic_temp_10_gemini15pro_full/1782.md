acral: /ˈækrəl/
adjective
肢端的，末梢的
Of or relating to the extremities (hands and feet).
Acral lentiginous melanoma typically arises on the palms of the hands or the soles of the feet. 肢端雀斑样黑色素瘤通常出现在手掌或脚底。

Acral erythema can be a side effect of certain chemotherapy drugs.  肢端红斑可能是某些化疗药物的副作用。

近义词: peripheral, distal
反义词: proximal, central
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *akron* (ἄκρον) meaning "extremity, tip, summit".

USAGE->
The acral areas are often affected by frostbite. = 肢端区域经常受到冻伤的影响。
The patient presented with acral cyanosis. = 病人出现肢端发绀。


PHRASE->
acral lentiginous melanoma = 肢端雀斑样黑色素瘤
acral erythema = 肢端红斑
acral keratosis = 肢端角化病
acral parts of the body = 身体的肢端部位
acral distribution = 肢端分布
