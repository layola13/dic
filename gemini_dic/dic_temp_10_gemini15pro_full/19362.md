bibliopolist: /ˌbɪbliˈɒpəlɪst/ (UK), /ˌbɪbliˈɑːpəlɪst/ (US)
|n.| 书商；爱书人；藏书家|A person who sells or deals in books, especially rare or old books; a lover of books.|The bibliopolist carefully examined the first edition Shakespeare. 这位书商仔细地检查了这本莎士比亚的第一版书籍。|Synonyms: bookseller, book dealer, book lover, bibliophile|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who dislikes or destroys books - biblioclast)|Noun
