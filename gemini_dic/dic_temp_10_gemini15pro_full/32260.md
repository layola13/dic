chico:/ˈtʃiːkoʊ/ (US), /ˈtʃɪkəʊ/ (UK)| n.|男孩，小伙子；（尤指拉丁美洲国家的）小伙子，家伙|A young man or boy, especially in Spanish-speaking countries.|He's a nice chico. 他是个不错的小伙子。|Synonyms: boy, lad, fellow, guy|Antonyms: girl, woman|noun

chico:/ˈtʃiːkoʊ/ (US), /ˈtʃɪkəʊ/ (UK)| adj. (informal)|小的，小型的|Small, little. (Used primarily in the American Southwest)|It's a chico town with only one store. 这是个只有一家商店的小镇。|Synonyms: small, little, tiny|Antonyms: large, big, huge|adjective
