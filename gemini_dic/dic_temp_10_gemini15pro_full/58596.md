dull-witted:/ˌdʌlˈwɪtɪd/| adj.|愚笨的|Not having or showing much intelligence|She made a few dull-witted comments. 她发表了一些愚蠢的评论。|Synonyms: unintelligent, simpleminded, foolish, brainless|Antonyms: intelligent, bright, smart, clever|adjective
