outsideness:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪdnəs/| n.|外在性，外部性，局外性|The state or quality of being outside or external; exteriority.  The sense of being an outsider or alienated.|Her writing explored the outsideness of the immigrant experience. 她的作品探讨了移民经历的局外性。|近义词：exteriority, externality, otherness, alienation|反义词：insideness, belonging, inclusion|词性:noun
