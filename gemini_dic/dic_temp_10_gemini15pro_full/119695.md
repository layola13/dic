midlife: /ˈmɪdlaɪf/ | n. adj. | 中年|The period of life from approximately 45 to 65 years old.  As an adjective, relating to or characteristic of middle age. | He's reached midlife and is reflecting on his career choices.  他到了中年，正在反思自己的职业选择。|Near synonyms: middle age |Antonyms: youth, old age| noun, adjective
