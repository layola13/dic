overtrip:/ˈəʊvərtɹɪp/| n.|过度旅行；过度劳累的旅行|A journey that is too long or tiring, or involves too much travelling in a short period.|After a hectic overtrip through five countries in two weeks, I was exhausted.  在两周内疯狂地游历了五个国家后，我筋疲力尽了。|近义词：excessive travel, whirlwind tour|反义词:short trip, relaxing vacation|词性:名词

overtrip:/ˌoʊvərˈtrɪp/| v.|过度旅行；因旅行过度而疲惫|To travel excessively, resulting in exhaustion.|He overtripped himself trying to fit in too many destinations on his vacation. 他在假期里试图去太多的地方，结果过度旅行，把自己累垮了。|近义词：overexert oneself travelling, travel excessively|反义词:relax, staycation|词性:动词
