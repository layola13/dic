bedeviling:/bɪˈdevlɪŋ/
verb
中文翻译：  折磨，困扰
英文解析：causing continual trouble or difficulty.  Continuously bothering or pestering someone or something.
例句：Technical problems have been bedeviling the project from the start.  技术问题从一开始就一直困扰着这个项目。

例句：He was bedeviled by insecurities throughout his life.  他一生都被不安全感所困扰。

近义词：plaguing, tormenting, harassing, troubling, afflicting
反义词：aiding, assisting, helping
词性：v.


bedeviled:/bɪˈdevld/
adjective
中文翻译：受折磨的，受困扰的
英文解析：greatly troubled by.  Tormented or harassed by something.
例句：The bedeviled team finally managed to finish the project despite numerous setbacks.  饱受困扰的团队终于克服重重挫折完成了项目。

例句：She had a bedeviled expression on her face as she struggled with the complex puzzle. 当她努力解开复杂的谜题时，脸上露出了苦恼的表情。


近义词：troubled, plagued, tormented, harassed, afflicted
反义词：untroubled, unworried, peaceful
词性：adj.


词源:
From "bedevil," formed from "be-" (making a verb causative) + "devil" (n.).  Meaning to torment or plague as if by a devil.
