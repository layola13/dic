guaranteed:/ˌɡærənˈtiːd/ | adj. |有保证的，一定的，担保的| Certain to happen or be the case; assured; protected by a guarantee. |  The loan is guaranteed by the government. 这笔贷款由政府担保。| Synonyms: assured, certain, secured, pledged | Antonyms: uncertain, doubtful, risky, unsecured | adjective

guaranteed:/ˌɡærənˈtiːd/ | v. |保证，担保 | To promise or make certain. |  I guarantee you'll love this movie. 我保证你会喜欢这部电影。| Synonyms: assure, promise, pledge, warrant | Antonyms: deny, refuse, reject, renounce | verb
