overtire: /ˌoʊvərˈtaɪər/ | v. | 使过度疲劳；使疲惫不堪 | To exhaust with too much work or exertion. |  He overtired himself with too much exercise. 他运动过度，把自己累坏了。

|近义词：exhaust, fatigue, wear out | 反义词：refresh, energize, rejuvenate |词性: 动词


overtire: /ˌoʊvərˈtaɪər/ | adj. | 过度疲劳的；疲惫不堪的 | Excessively tired; exhausted. | The overtired children fell asleep quickly.  过度疲劳的孩子们很快就睡着了。

|近义词：exhausted, fatigued, worn out | 反义词：refreshed, energized, rejuvenated |词性: 形容词


词源: "over-" (过度) + "tire" (使疲倦)
