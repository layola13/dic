overabstemiousness:/ˌoʊvərˌæbstˈiːmiəsnəs/| n.|过度节制；极度禁欲|Excessive abstinence, especially in the use of food and drink.  It implies an unhealthy or extreme level of self-denial.|His overabstemiousness led to malnutrition.  他的过度节制导致营养不良。|abstemiousness, austerity, self-denial|gluttony, indulgence, intemperance|noun
