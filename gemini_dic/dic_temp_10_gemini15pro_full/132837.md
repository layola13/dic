noneducated:/ˌnɒnˈɛdʒʊkeɪtɪd/
adjective
未受教育的，缺乏教育的
Lacking formal education; not schooled.
The noneducated population often faces greater challenges in finding employment.  未受教育的人口在找工作方面经常面临更大的挑战。
Many noneducated workers struggle with basic literacy skills. 许多未受教育的工人在基本识字技能方面很吃力。
synonym: uneducated, illiterate, unschooled, ignorant
antonym: educated, literate, learned, schooled
word type: adjective
