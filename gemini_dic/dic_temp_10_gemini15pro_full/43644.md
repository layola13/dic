creatives:/ˈkriːeɪtɪvz/
n.|创意人员；广告素材|People who are creative, especially in the advertising or artistic world. |The agency’s creatives came up with a brilliant new campaign. 这家广告公司的创意人员想出了一个精彩的新广告活动。|
近义词:artists, designers, innovators|
反义词:unimaginative people|
词性:名词


creatives:/ˈkriːeɪtɪvz/
n.|创意作品；广告作品|Creative works, typically advertisements or artistic productions.|The new creatives for the fashion brand were stunning. 这个时尚品牌的新广告作品令人惊艳。|
近义词:artworks, designs, productions|
反义词:copies, imitations|
词性:名词


词源:From the adjective "creative," ultimately from Latin "creare" (to create).
