cooccuring:/ˌkoʊəˈkɜːrɪŋ/ | adj. | 共同发生的，同时出现的 | happening at the same time or in the same place; occurring together. | Example 1: Cooccurring symptoms can help doctors diagnose the illness. 共同出现的症状可以帮助医生诊断疾病。

Example 2: The cooccurring events led to a significant change in policy. 同时发生的事件导致了政策的重大改变。 | Synonym:  concurrent, simultaneous, concomitant | Antonym: separate, independent, distinct | word type: adjective

Etymology: co- (together) + occurring (present participle of occur, from Latin occurrere "to run towards, meet, present itself," from ob- "towards" + currere "to run").
