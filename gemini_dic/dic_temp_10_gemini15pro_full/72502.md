fluorine:/ˈflʊəriːn/, /ˈflɔːriːn/ | n. | 氟 |A pale yellow, highly corrosive, poisonous, gaseous halogen element, the most electronegative and reactive of all elements. It is used in the production of uranium and other chemicals, and its compounds are used in refrigerants and other industrial applications. |Fluorine is the most reactive element. 氟是最活泼的元素。| 近义词: halogen (as a general category) | 反义词: None (as it's a specific element) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *fluorine*,  from *fluor* (“flowing”) (ultimately from Latin *fluō* (“flow”)) + *-ine* (suffix used to form names of chemical elements).  Relates to *fluorescent*.
