drupelet:/ˈdruːplɪt/| n. |小核果|A small drupe, one of many that make up an aggregate fruit like a raspberry or blackberry. |The raspberry is made up of numerous drupelets clustered together.  覆盆子是由许多小核果聚集在一起组成的。|Synonym: segment, section|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "whole fruit" in the context of aggregate fruits)|Noun
