breaststroke: /ˈbrɛststroʊk/ (US) /ˈbrɛstrəʊk/ (UK) | n. | 蛙泳 | A swimming stroke performed on the chest, with both arms moving symmetrically and the legs kicking outward in a frog-like motion. |  He won the race using the breaststroke. 他用蛙泳赢得了比赛。 | Michael Phelps is known for his butterfly stroke, not his breaststroke. 迈克尔·菲尔普斯以蝶泳闻名，而不是蛙泳。 | 近义词: frog kick | 反义词: backstroke, crawl, butterfly | 词性: noun
