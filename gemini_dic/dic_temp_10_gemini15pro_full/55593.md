disunited:/ˌdɪsjuːˈnaɪtɪd/
adjective
分裂的，不团结的
not united; divided
The disunited party was unable to agree on a strategy.  分裂的政党无法就战略达成一致。
The team, disunited by internal conflicts, performed poorly.  由于内部冲突，团队不团结，表现很差。
近义词: divided, separated, fragmented, disjointed
反义词: united, unified, cohesive, integrated
词性: adjective

词源:
From dis- + united, from unite, from Latin unitus, past participle of unire "to unite."
