botnet:/ˈbɒtnɛt/ (UK) /ˈbɑːtnɛt/ (US)| n. |僵尸网络|A network of private computers infected with malicious software and controlled as a group without the owners' knowledge, e.g., to send spam or launch attacks.|例句：The botnet was used to distribute malware to thousands of computers. 这个僵尸网络被用来向成千上万的电脑散播恶意软件。

例句：Cybercriminals often use botnets to carry out DDoS attacks. 网络犯罪分子经常使用僵尸网络进行DDoS攻击。|近义词：zombie army, robot network|反义词: None|词性:noun
