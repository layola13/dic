intermobilities:/ˌɪntərmoʊˈbɪlətiz/
noun
相互流动性，跨领域流动性
The increasing intermobilities of students and researchers between universities foster academic exchange.  大学之间学生和研究人员日益增长的相互流动性促进了学术交流。
The project explores the intermobilities of ideas, people, and goods in a globalized world. 该项目探讨了全球化世界中思想、人员和商品的相互流动性。
Synonyms: movements, exchanges, interactions, flows
Antonyms: stagnation, immobility, fixity
词性: 名词
