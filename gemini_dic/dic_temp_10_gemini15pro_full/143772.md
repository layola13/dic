outmerchant:/ˌaʊtˈmɜːrtʃənt/ | v. | 以更低的价格出售，胜过…的商业手段 | To sell at a lower price than; to surpass in business acumen or trading. |  Example 1: The new store outmerchanted all its competitors by offering deep discounts. 这家新店通过提供大幅折扣胜过了所有竞争对手。

Example 2: They were able to outmerchant their rivals and gain a larger market share. 他们能够以更低的价格出售商品，胜过竞争对手，并获得更大的市场份额。 | Synonyms: outsell, undersell, outcompete | Antonyms: be outcompeted, be undersold | verb
