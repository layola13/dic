fervidness:/ˈfɜːrvɪdnəs/| n. |热情，热烈，炽热|The fervidness of his love for her was evident in every word he spoke.  他的每一句话都流露出他对她的炽热的爱。|例句：The fervidness of her belief inspired others. 她热烈的信仰激励了其他人。

The artist painted with a fervidness that reflected his passion.  这位艺术家以一种反映他激情的热情绘画。|近义词：passion, ardor, zeal, fervor, intensity|反义词：apathy, indifference, coldness|词性:noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *ferviditas, from Latin fervidus (“glowing, burning, vehement”), from fervere (“to boil, glow”).
