cretonne:/krɪˈtɒn/ /krɪˈtɑːn/| n. |印花棉布，克里东呢|A strong, unglazed cotton fabric, typically with a floral pattern, used for curtains and upholstery.|The curtains were made of a cheerful yellow cretonne.|近义词:chintz, calico|反义词:None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cretonne*, named after Creton, a village in Normandy where it was originally manufactured.
