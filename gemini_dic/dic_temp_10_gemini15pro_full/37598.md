communicate:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/ (US) /kəmˈjuːnɪkeɪt/ (UK)
verb
交流；沟通；传达；传送
to share or exchange information, news, or ideas
例句：We communicate with each other through language. 我们通过语言相互交流。

例句：The teacher communicated the assignment to the students. 老师把作业布置给了学生。

近义词：convey, impart, transmit, share
反义词：withhold, suppress, conceal
词性：v.


communicate:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/ (US) /kəmˈjuːnɪkeɪt/ (UK)
adjective
相通的
able to be shared or exchanged
例句：These two rooms are communicating. 这两个房间是相通的。
例句：Communicating ideas effectively is crucial in business. 有效地沟通想法在商业中至关重要。
近义词：connected, adjoining, interlinked
反义词：separate, isolated, detached
词性：adj.
