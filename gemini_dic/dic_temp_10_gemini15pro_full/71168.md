fissiparous:/fɪˈsɪpərəs/
adjective
分裂的，裂变的，易分裂的
Inclined to cause or undergo division into separate parts or groups.
例句：The fissiparous tendencies of the political party led to its eventual downfall.  这个政党的分裂倾向导致了它最终的垮台。

例句：The fissiparous nature of the single-celled organism allowed it to reproduce quickly.  这种单细胞生物易分裂的特性使它能够快速繁殖。

近义词：divisive, schismatic, factious
反义词：uniting, cohesive, unifying
词性: adjective
