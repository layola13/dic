harlem: /ˈhɑːrləm/ (US) /ˈhɑːləm/ (UK)| n. |哈莱姆|A large neighborhood in the northern part of the Manhattan borough of New York City. Historically known as a center of African American culture.|例句：He grew up in Harlem. 他在哈莱姆长大。|例句：Harlem is famous for its jazz clubs. 哈莱姆以其爵士乐俱乐部而闻名。|近义词：neighborhood, district, community|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

词源:  From the Dutch Nieuw Haarlem, named after the city of Haarlem in the Netherlands.
