magicked:/ˈmædʒɪkt/ |adj. |被施了魔法的；神奇的|Having been affected by magic; magical. |The magicked sword glowed in the darkness.  被施了魔法的剑在黑暗中闪闪发光。|Synonyms: enchanted, charmed, magical |Antonyms: ordinary, mundane|adjective
