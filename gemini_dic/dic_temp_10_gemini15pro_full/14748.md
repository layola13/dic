awesomest:/ˈɔːsəməst/| adj.|最棒的，最令人惊叹的|Superlative form of awesome; most impressive, inspiring awe.|例句：That was the awesomest concert I've ever been to! 那是我去过的最棒的音乐会！|近义词：greatest, best, most excellent, most impressive|反义词：worst, terrible, awful|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "awesome" + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Awesome" comes from "awe" (a feeling of reverential respect mixed with fear or wonder) + "-some" (causing or characterized by).
