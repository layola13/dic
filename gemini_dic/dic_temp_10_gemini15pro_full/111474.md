load-bearing:/ˈloʊdˌberɪŋ/| adj.|承重的，负重的|Supporting a load or capable of supporting a load.  Able to carry weight or stress.|The load-bearing walls of the house are made of brick. 这栋房子的承重墙是用砖砌成的。|Synonyms: supporting, weight-bearing, structural|Antonyms: non-load-bearing, decorative|adjective
