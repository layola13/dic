malnourished: /ˌmælˈnɜːrɪʃt/ /ˌmælˈnʌrɪʃt/ | adj. | 营养不良的 |  deficient in or lacking adequate nourishment |  The malnourished children were given vitamin supplements. 营养不良的儿童得到了维生素补充剂。|  undernourished, underfed |  well-nourished, healthy | adjective

malnutrition: /ˌmælnuˈtrɪʃən/ /ˌmælnuˈtrɪʃn/ | n. | 营养不良 |  lack of proper nutrition; inadequate or unbalanced nutrition | Malnutrition is a serious problem in many developing countries. 营养不良在许多发展中国家是一个严重的问题。 | undernourishment | nourishment, health | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mal- (bad, ill) + nourished (provided with food necessary for growth and health).  The root "nourish" comes from Old French "norrir," ultimately from Latin "nutrire" (to nourish).
