caruncula:/kəˈrʌŋkjʊlə/| n. | 肉阜，小肉瘤 | A small, fleshy eminence, often resembling a comb or wattle.  It typically refers to a small, fleshy outgrowth on an animal or plant. In humans, it can refer to specific anatomical structures like the caruncula lacrimalis (the small, reddish elevation at the inner corner of the eye).| The veterinarian identified the caruncula on the chicken's head. 兽医辨认出了鸡头上的肉阜。|  eminence, papilla, nodule | - | noun
