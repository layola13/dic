amending:/əˈmɛndɪŋ/
Verb
修改，修正
The process of making changes to a law, document, or agreement to improve or correct it.
The committee is amending the constitution. 委员会正在修改章程。
Synonyms: altering, modifying, revising, correcting, improving
Antonyms: preserving, maintaining, keeping, upholding
词性: 动词 (Verb)


amending:/əˈmɛndɪŋ/
Adjective
修正的，改正的
Serving to amend or correct something.
They made several amending proposals to the bill. 他们对法案提出了几项修正提案。
Synonyms: corrective, revisory, improving, rectifying
Antonyms: damaging, worsening, deteriorating
词性: 形容词 (Adjective)
