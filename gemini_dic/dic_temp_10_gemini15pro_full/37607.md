communicative:/kəˈmjuːnɪkətɪv/| adj.|交际的，健谈的，沟通的|Willing to talk and share information.|She's a very communicative person and makes friends easily. 她是一个非常健谈的人，很容易交朋友。|例句：He's not very communicative, is he? 他不是很健谈，是吗？|近义词：talkative, chatty, sociable, outgoing|反义词：reserved, withdrawn, uncommunicative|词性:形容词

communicative:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtɪv/| adj.|传达的，表达的|(of a piece of writing, a look, etc.) able to express something clearly.|Her smile was communicative of her good mood. 她的笑容表达了她的好心情。|例句：The photograph was strangely communicative. 这张照片的表达力很强。|近义词：expressive, eloquent|反义词：inexpressive|词性:形容词
