nonremuneration:/ˌnɒnˌrɪˌmjuːnəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 无报酬，无偿 | Absence of payment or reward for work or services. |  Volunteering is often done on a nonremuneration basis.  志愿工作通常是无偿进行的。

The artist offered his services on a nonremuneration basis for the local charity.  这位艺术家为当地慈善机构无偿提供服务。 | 近义词：unpaid, voluntary, gratuitous | 反义词: paid, remunerated, compensated | 词性: noun

nonremunerative: /ˌnɒnˌrɪˈmjuːnərətɪv/ | adj. | 无报酬的，不赚钱的 | Not providing any payment or reward. | Working for the charity was nonremunerative but fulfilling. 为慈善机构工作没有报酬，但令人感到满足。

Many internships are nonremunerative, offering experience instead of pay. 许多实习是没有报酬的，提供的是经验而不是薪水。 | 近义词：unpaid, unprofitable, unrewarding | 反义词: paid, profitable, lucrative | 词性: adjective
