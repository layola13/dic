cretaceous:/krɪˈteɪʃəs/| adj. n.|白垩纪的，白垩纪|From Latin *cretaceus* "chalky," from *creta* "chalk."  Relating to or denoting the last period of the Mesozoic era, between the Jurassic and Tertiary periods, lasting from 145 to 66 million years ago.  Also, chalky.|例句：The Cretaceous period saw the extinction of the dinosaurs. 白垩纪时期见证了恐龙的灭绝。

|例句：Cretaceous rocks are often rich in fossils. 白垩纪的岩石通常富含化石。|近义词：chalky|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun
