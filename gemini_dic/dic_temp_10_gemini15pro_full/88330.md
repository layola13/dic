hippocampus:/ˌhɪpəˈkæmpəs/ /ˌhɪpəˈkæmpəs/| n. |海马体|A complex neural structure (shaped like a sea horse) consisting of gray matter and located on the floor of each lateral ventricle of the brain. It is primarily associated with memory, particularly long-term memory, and spatial navigation. |The hippocampus plays a crucial role in forming new memories. 海马体在新记忆的形成中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonym: memory center|Antonym: N/A|Noun

hippocampus:/ˌhɪpəˈkæmpəs/ /ˌhɪpəˈkæmpəs/| n. |海马|A small marine fish with a head and neck suggestive of a horse.  |The hippocampus is an unusual and fascinating creature. 海马是一种奇特而迷人的生物。|Synonym: seahorse|Antonym: N/A|Noun
