auditor:/ˈɔːdɪtər/ (US), /ˈɔːdɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |审计员，查账员|A person who officially examines the financial records of a company. | The auditors discovered some irregularities in the company's accounts. 审计员在公司的账目中发现了一些违规行为。

The external auditor will review our financial statements next month.  外部审计员下个月将审查我们的财务报表。

|Synonyms: examiner, inspector, controller, accountant|Antonyms:  None (The opposite concept might be a "fraudster" or someone trying to *hide* financial information, but there isn't a direct antonym.)| Noun
