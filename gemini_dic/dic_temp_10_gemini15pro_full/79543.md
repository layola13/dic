glockenspiels:/ˈɡlɒkənʃpiːlz/ /ˈɡlɑːkənˌspiːl/| n. |钟琴（复数）|A percussion instrument consisting of a set of tuned metal bars arranged in the fashion of a keyboard, played with small mallets.|The orchestra's glockenspiels shimmered in the high register. 管弦乐队的钟琴在高音区闪耀。|The glockenspiels added a bright, delicate touch to the piece. 钟琴为这首曲子增添了明亮细腻的触感。|Synonyms: metallophone, bell lyre|Antonyms: None|noun
