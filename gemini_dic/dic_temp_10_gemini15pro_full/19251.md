bhaskara: /bɑːˈskɑːrə/ /bəˈskɑːrə/
n.
Bhaskara (also Bhaskaracharya)
印度数学家婆什迦罗（也称为婆什迦罗第二）
Bhaskara was a prominent Indian mathematician and astronomer. 婆什迦罗是一位杰出的印度数学家和天文学家。
Bhaskaracharya made significant contributions to algebra and trigonometry. 婆什迦罗对代数和三角学做出了重大贡献。
Synonym: mathematician, astronomer
Antonym: N/A
词性: noun
