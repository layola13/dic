oxyhaemocyanin:/ˌɒksiˈhiːməʊˈsaɪənɪn/ |n.|氧合血蓝蛋白|A form of haemocyanin that is saturated with oxygen, and is typically bright blue in colour.  It is the oxygenated form of haemocyanin, a respiratory pigment found in the blood of some mollusks and arthropods.|The oxyhaemocyanin in the octopus's blood gave its gills a vibrant blue hue.| 近义词: oxygenated haemocyanin |反义词: deoxyhaemocyanin| 词性: noun
