defeasible:/dɪˈfiːzɪbl/
adj.
可撤销的；可废止的；可击败的
capable of being annulled or made void; open in principle to revision, valid unless or until overridden by something more compelling
例句：The contract contains a defeasible clause.  合同包含一个可撤销条款。

例句：The presumption of innocence is defeasible by evidence to the contrary.  无罪推定可以被相反的证据推翻。
近义词：revocable, voidable, annullable, overridable, rebuttable
反义词：indefeasible, absolute, irrevocable
词性：adjective
词源：Late Middle English: from Old French *desaiblisable ‘that can be undone’, based on Latin dis- ‘un-’ + facere ‘to do’.
