home-in-on:/hoʊm ˈɪn ɑːn/ /həʊm ˈɪn ɒn/| phrasal verb | 瞄准；对准；集中注意力于；越来越接近(目标) | To direct or focus attention, effort, or activity toward something specific; to move closer to a target or goal. | The missile homed in on the target. 导弹瞄准了目标。

The police are homing in on the suspect. 警方正越来越接近嫌疑人。| 近义词: zero in on, focus on, concentrate on, target | 反义词: lose sight of, ignore, neglect | 词性: 动词短语
