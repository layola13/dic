garbed:/ɡɑːrbd/
|v. (past participle of garb)|穿着…服装的；打扮的|dressed in a particular way, especially in a striking or elaborate manner|The king was garbed in royal purple. 国王身着皇家紫袍。|
|近义词：dressed, clothed, attired, arrayed|反义词：undressed, naked|词性：动词(过去分词，用作形容词)


garb:/ɡɑːrb/
|n.|服装；装束|clothing, especially of a distinctive or particular kind|He wore the garb of a monk. 他穿着僧侣的服装。|
|vt.|给…穿衣；装扮|to clothe or dress, especially in distinctive or impressive attire|The dancers were garbed in colorful costumes. 舞者们穿着五彩缤纷的服装。|
|近义词：clothing, attire, apparel, costume|反义词：nudity|词性：名词，动词


词源:
From Old North French *garber, of Germanic origin; related to Old High German garawen ‘make ready’.
