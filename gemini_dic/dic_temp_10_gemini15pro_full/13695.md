attributions:/ˌætrɪˈbjuːʃənz/| n.| 属性，归因，归属| the act of regarding something as being caused by a person or thing; the act of attributing a work of art, etc. to a particular artist, writer, etc. |The painting's attributions to Rembrandt are now being questioned. 这幅画归属于伦勃朗的说法现在受到了质疑。|synonyms: ascription, credit, assignment|antonyms: denial, disclaimer|noun

attributions:/ˌætrɪˈbjuːʃənz/| n.|贡献| something that is positive about a situation or event, or the act of mentioning something positive |She listed the attributions of organic food. 她列举了有机食品的优点。|synonyms: qualities, characteristics, traits|antonyms: drawbacks, disadvantages|noun
