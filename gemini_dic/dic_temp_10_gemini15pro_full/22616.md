boozers:/ˈbuːzərz/| n.|酒鬼，酒徒，常去酒吧的人|People who drink alcohol frequently, especially to excess, and often spend time in bars.  They are habitual drinkers, especially those who drink to excess.|The boozers at the corner pub were already loud and boisterous by midday.  中午时分，街角酒吧里的酒鬼们已经吵吵嚷嚷，喧闹不已了。|The old boozers were reminiscing about their younger days. 那些老酒鬼正在追忆他们的年轻时光。|近义词：alcoholics, drinkers, drunks, tipplers|反义词:teetotalers, abstainers|词性:名词
