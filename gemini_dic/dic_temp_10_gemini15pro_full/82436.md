gruffs: /ɡrʌfs/| n. |粗暴的态度或言语 (通常用复数形式)|Rough or surly manners or remarks (usually plural).|His gruffs put everyone off. 他的粗暴态度让每个人都反感。|Synonyms: rudeness, brusqueness, curtness|Antonyms: politeness, courtesy, civility|noun
