noetic:/noʊˈɛtɪk/ /nəʊˈetɪk/
adjective
理智的；知识的；精神的；Noetic
Of or relating to the mind; intellectual.  Relating to or based on pure reason, not involving the senses or emotions.
例句：She described a noetic experience that transformed her understanding of reality. 她描述了一次改变了她对现实理解的理智体验。

例句：Noetic science explores the nature of consciousness.  理智科学探索意识的本质。

近义词：intellectual, mental, cognitive, rational, spiritual
反义词：physical, emotional, sensory, irrational

词性: adjective

词源:  From Greek *noētikos*, from *noētos* “intelligible,” from *noeîn* “to think, perceive,” related to *nous* “mind, intellect.”
