intercollegiate:/ˌɪntərˈkəˈliːdʒɪət/ | adj. | 大学间的，学院间的 | Existing or occurring between different colleges. | Example: The intercollegiate debate competition drew students from across the country.  大学间的辩论比赛吸引了来自全国各地的学生。| Example:  She participated in intercollegiate athletics, representing her university in swimming. 她参加了校际运动会，代表她的大学参加游泳比赛。 | Synonym:  interscholastic, interuniversity | Antonym: intracollegiate, intramural | word type: adjective
