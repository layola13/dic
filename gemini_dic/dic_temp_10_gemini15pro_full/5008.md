all-around:/ˌɔːl əˈraʊnd/ | adj. | 全能的，全面的，多才多艺的 | Able to do many things well; versatile.  |  He's an all-around athlete, excelling in swimming, running, and cycling. 他是一位全能运动员，擅长游泳、跑步和骑自行车。 | versatile, competent, adaptable | specialist, limited | adjective

all-around:/ˌɔːl əˈraʊnd/ | n. | 全能选手，多面手 | A person who is proficient in many things. |  She's the all-around in our team, capable of playing any position. 她是我们队的全能选手，可以打任何位置。 |  versatile person, jack-of-all-trades | specialist | noun
