mercurialization:/ˌmɜːrkjʊəriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 汞化；汞中毒；性情反复无常 | The process of combining or treating with mercury. mercurialized:/ˌmɜːrkjʊəriəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 汞化的；受汞影响的 | Treated with mercury or affected by mercury poisoning. |  The mercurialized thermometer broke, spilling its toxic contents. 受汞污染的温度计破裂，泄漏了有毒物质。| Synonyms: unstable, volatile, erratic | Antonyms: stable, constant | adjective
