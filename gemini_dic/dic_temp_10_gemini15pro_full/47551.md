deauration:/diːɔːˈreɪʃn/
n.
去镀金，脱金
The process of removing gold or gilding from a surface.
The deauration of the antique mirror revealed the silver underneath.  古董镜子的去镀金过程显露出了下面的银。
removal of gold, gold removal
gilding, gold plating
noun
