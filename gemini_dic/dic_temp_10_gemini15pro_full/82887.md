gulpiest:/ˈɡʌlpiɪst/
adjective
最容易上当受骗的，最轻信的
Most gullible; most easily tricked or fooled.
例句：He was the gulpiest person I'd ever met, believing every tall tale and outlandish claim. 他是我见过最容易上当受骗的人，每一个荒诞的故事和古怪的说法都信以为真。

例句：The gulpiest investors are often the first to lose their money in a scam. 最容易上当受骗的投资者往往是在骗局中最先赔钱的。

近义词：naive, credulous, unsuspecting
反义词：skeptical, cynical, discerning
词性：adjective
