hemiplegia:/ˌhemiˈpliːdʒə/ (US) /ˌhemiˈpliːdʒɪə/ (UK)| n. |偏瘫，半身不遂|Paralysis of one side of the body.|After the stroke, he suffered from hemiplegia. 中风后，他患上了偏瘫。|
The hemiplegia affected her left side, making it difficult to walk. 偏瘫影响了她的左侧身体，使她行走困难。|近义词：paralysis, palsy|反义词:mobility, movement|词性:noun
