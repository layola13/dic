nyctitropic:/nɪkˈtɪtrɒpɪk/ | adj. | 夜合性的，夜间活动的 | Relating to or exhibiting nyctitropism, the tendency of leaves or petals of certain plants to fold up or droop at night and open during the day. |  The nyctitropic movements of the prayer plant's leaves are fascinating to watch.  祈祷植物叶子的夜合性运动非常迷人。| synonyms:  nyctinastic | antonyms: diurnal | adjective
