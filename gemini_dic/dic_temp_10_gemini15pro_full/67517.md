extractive:/ɪkˈstræktɪv/| adj. |提取的，萃取的|Relating to or involving extraction, especially the extraction of raw materials from the earth.|例句：Extractive industries, such as mining and logging, can have a significant impact on the environment. 采掘业，例如采矿和伐木，会对环境产生重大影响。

例句：The company uses an extractive process to obtain valuable minerals from the ore. 这家公司使用提取工艺从矿石中提取有价值的矿物。|近义词：extracted, mining, quarrying|反义词:synthetic, manufactured|词性:adjective
