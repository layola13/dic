insensibilities:/ɪnˌsɛnsɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
n.
麻木；无知觉；冷淡；漠不关心
lack of awareness or feeling; lack of sensitivity or responsiveness
He showed a chilling insensibility to the suffering of others. 他对别人的苦难表现出令人心寒的冷漠。

The drug induced a temporary state of insensibility. 这种药物会引起暂时的麻木状态。

His insensibilities to her plight made her cry. 他对她困境的漠不关心让她哭了。

近义词: apathy, indifference, numbness, unfeelingness, callousness
反义词: sensitivity, responsiveness, compassion, empathy, awareness
