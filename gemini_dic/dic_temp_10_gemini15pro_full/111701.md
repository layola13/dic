lockouts:/ˈlɒkaʊts/ (UK) /ˈlɑːkaʊts/ (US)| n.|停工，闭厂； lockout |A lockout is a work stoppage or denial of employment initiated by the management of a company during a labor dispute.  It is the exclusion of employees by their employer from their place of work until certain terms are agreed to.|The company initiated lockouts after the union refused to accept the proposed pay cuts. 公司在工会拒绝接受提议的减薪后，启动了停工。|The NFL lockouts in 2011 disrupted the entire football season. 2011年NFL的停工扰乱了整个足球赛季。|Synonyms: shutdown, closure, work stoppage|Antonyms:  reopening, admittance, work resumption|noun
