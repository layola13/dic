impalpable:/ɪmˈpæl.pə.bəl/| adj.|难以捉摸的，无法感知的，无形的|Unable to be felt by touch; intangible. Also, difficult to understand or grasp.|例句：The tension in the room was almost impalpable. 房间里的紧张气氛几乎难以察觉。

例句：His influence on the team, though impalpable, was immense. 他对团队的影响虽然难以捉摸，但却非常巨大。|近义词：intangible, imperceptible, elusive, subtle|反义词：tangible, palpable, perceptible, concrete|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin impalpabilis, from in- "not" + palpabilis "tangible," from palpare "to touch, stroke."
