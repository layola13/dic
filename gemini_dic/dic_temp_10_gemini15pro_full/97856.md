inlet:/ˈɪnlɛt/ (US), /ˈɪnlɪt/ (UK)| n.|入口，进口; 小湾，水湾|A place where water flows into a larger body of water, or a small arm of a sea or lake.|例句：The boat sailed into the sheltered inlet.  船驶入了 sheltered 的小湾。

例句：The town has a small inlet that's perfect for kayaking.  这个小镇有一个非常适合皮划艇的小海湾。|近义词：bay, cove, harbor, entrance|反义词:outlet, exit|词性:名词

inlet:/ˈɪnlɛt/ (US), /ˈɪnlɪt/ (UK)| n.|（液体、气体、光线等的）入口，进口|A passage or opening by which a fluid or other substance is admitted.|例句：The inlet valve on the engine was faulty. 发动机的进气阀坏了。

例句：The fresh air inlet was blocked. 新鲜空气入口被堵住了。|近义词：intake, opening, aperture|反义词:outlet, exhaust|词性:名词
