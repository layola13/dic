hat-trick:/ˈhæt trɪk/| n.|帽子戏法; 三连胜;  (板球)三个连续的投球得分|A hat-trick is the achievement of three successes one after the other, especially in sport.|He scored a hat-trick in the final game. 他在决赛中上演了帽子戏法。|Synonyms: triple, treble|Antonyms: failure, loss|noun
