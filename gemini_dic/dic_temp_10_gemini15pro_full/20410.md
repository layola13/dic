biter:/ˈbaɪtər/| n.|咬人者；爱咬东西的人（尤指动物）；骗子|A person or animal that bites, especially habitually. Also, someone who cheats or deceives others.|The little biter nipped my finger. 小家伙咬了我的手指。|Synonyms: nipper, chewer, gnawer, swindler, con artist|Antonyms: prey, victim|noun
