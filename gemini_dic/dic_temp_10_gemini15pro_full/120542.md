mirabilite:/mɪˈræbɪlaɪt/| n. |芒硝； glaubers salt|A hydrous sodium sulfate mineral, Na2SO4·10H2O, occurring in white or colorless monoclinic crystals, often as an efflorescence on alkaline soils in arid regions.|Mirabilite is often found in dry lake beds. 芒硝常在干涸的湖床中发现。|Synonyms: Glauber's salt, sodium sulfate decahydrate|Antonyms: N/A|noun

mirabilite:/mɪˈræbɪlaɪt/| n. |芒硝； glaubers salt|A naturally occurring mineral form of hydrous sodium sulfate.|Large deposits of mirabilite are found in some salt lakes. 一些盐湖中发现了大量的芒硝。|Synonyms: Glauber's salt|Antonyms: N/A|noun
