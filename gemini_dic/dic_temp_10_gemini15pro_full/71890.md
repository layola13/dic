flew:/fluː/
verb
飞, 飞行（fly的过去式）
The bird flew across the sky.  鸟儿飞过天空。
The plane flew from London to Paris.  飞机从伦敦飞往巴黎。
近义词:soared, glided, jetted
反义词:landed, descended
词性:动词

词源: Old English *flēogan (not attested), from Proto-Germanic *fleuganą (whence also Old Norse fljúga, Old Frisian fliūga, Old Saxon fliogan, Old High German fliogan, German fliegen, Dutch vliegen, Gothic  flygjan), from Proto-Indo-European *pleuk- “to fly.”
