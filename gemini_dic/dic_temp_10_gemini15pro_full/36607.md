collectable:/kəˈlɛktəbl/ | adj. n. | 可收集的，收藏品 | Able to be collected, or worth collecting; an item worth collecting. |  This antique vase is highly collectable.  这个古董花瓶很有收藏价值。| Synonyms: collectible, desirable, sought-after | Antonyms: worthless, undesirable | adjective

collectable:/kəˈlɛktəbl/ | n. | 收藏品 | An item worth collecting. |  She has a large collection of collectables, including stamps and coins. 她收藏了很多收藏品，包括邮票和硬币。| Synonyms: collectible, curio, artifact | Antonyms: junk, trash | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "collect" + suffix "-able," meaning "capable of being." "Collect" comes from the Latin "colligere," meaning "to gather together."
