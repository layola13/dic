centralest: /ˈsɛntrəlɪst/
adjective
最中心的
most central; situated at the very center.  While grammatically possible, it's considered non-standard and rarely used.  "Most central" is generally preferred.
例句：He claimed to have the centralest apartment in the city. 他声称拥有这个城市最中心的公寓。
例句：She wanted the centralest table in the restaurant. 她想要餐厅里最中心的那张桌子。
近义词：most central, innermost, middlemost
反义词：outermost, peripheral, furthest
词性: adjective
