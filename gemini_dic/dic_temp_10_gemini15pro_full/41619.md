corrals:/kəˈrɑːlz/
n.|畜栏，围栏|An enclosure for confining or capturing livestock.|The cowboys herded the cattle into the corrals.|近义词：pens, enclosures, stockyards|反义词: open field
v.|把…关进畜栏，围住|To enclose or confine in a corral.|The rancher corralled the horses for the night.|近义词：pen, enclose, confine|反义词: release, free
