hue:/hjuː/
n.|色调，色彩，颜色|A particular shade of a given color.|The artist captured the subtle hues of the sunset. 艺术家捕捉到了日落微妙的色彩。
近义词:color, shade, tint, tone
反义词:(没有明确的反义词，可以考虑achromatic 无色的)
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hīw (attested only in compounds), from Proto-Germanic *hīwiz (source also of Old Saxon hīwi "form, appearance," Old Norse hý "appearance," Old High German hiwi "color, appearance"), from Proto-Indo-European *kʲey- "to see, appear."
