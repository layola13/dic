comfreys: /ˈkʌmfriz/| n. |紫草科植物；聚合草|A perennial plant of the borage family, native to Europe and Asia, with hairy leaves and small bell-shaped flowers, traditionally used in herbal medicine.|Comfreys are known for their healing properties. 聚合草以其愈合特性而闻名。|近义词：boneset, knitbone|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


Etymology: From Old French *confortie, from Late Latin *confirma, from Latin confirmare "to strengthen," from con- "together" + firmare "to make firm."  The plant was believed to promote the knitting of broken bones.
