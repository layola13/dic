Etymology: From Late Middle English *contemptuosnes*, from Anglo-Norman *contemptuosnes*, from Late Latin *contemptuōsus* (contemptuous) + *-ness* (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality). Ultimately from Latin *contemnere* (to despise).
