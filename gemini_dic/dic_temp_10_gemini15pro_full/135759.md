nonprofession:/ˌnɒn.prəˈfeʃ.ən/ | n. | 非专业；非职业；|Something that is not a profession, typically referring to an activity undertaken for enjoyment rather than as a paid occupation. | Example:  Photography started as a nonprofession for her, but now it's her career. 她最初只是把摄影当成一项业余爱好，但现在成了她的职业。

Example: He treated his "job" as a nonprofession, rarely taking it seriously. 他把自己的“工作”当作儿戏，很少认真对待。 | Synonym: avocation, hobby, pastime | Antonym: profession, vocation, occupation | noun
