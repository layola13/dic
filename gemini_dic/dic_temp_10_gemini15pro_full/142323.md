orthodoxy: /ˈɔːrθədɒksi/ (UK) /ˈɔːrθədɑːksi/ (US)| n.|正统；正统观念；正统做法|Authorized or generally accepted theory, doctrine, or practice.|例句：The challenge to religious orthodoxy. 对宗教正统观念的挑战。|例句：He challenged the political orthodoxy of his time. 他挑战了他那个时代的政治正统观念。|近义词：doctrine, dogma, tenet, belief, creed|反义词：heterodoxy, heresy, unorthodoxy|词性:名词

orthodoxically: /ˌɔːrθəˈdɒksɪkli/ (UK) /ˌɔːrθəˈdɑːksɪkli/ (US) |adv.|正统地；正统式地|In an orthodox manner; conventionally.|例句：She dresses orthodoxically. 她穿着很正统。|例句：He behaved orthodoxically in every way. 他的行为举止都很正统。|近义词：conventionally, traditionally|反义词：unconventionally, heterodoxly|词性:副词
