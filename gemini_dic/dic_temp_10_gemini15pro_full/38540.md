concluded:/kənˈkluːdɪd/
verb
结束，推断出，断定
brought to an end; reached a decision
The meeting concluded at 5 p.m. 会议在下午5点结束。
After reviewing the evidence, the detective concluded that the suspect was guilty.  审查证据后，侦探断定嫌疑人有罪。
synonyms: finished, ended, determined, deduced
antonyms: began, started, commenced, initiated
词性:动词


conclude:/kənˈkluːd/
verb
结束，推断出，断定
bring to an end; reach a decision
We concluded the meeting with a vote of thanks. 我们以感谢投票结束了会议。
From the evidence, we can conclude that he is guilty. 从证据来看，我们可以断定他有罪。
synonyms: finish, end, determine, deduce
antonyms: begin, start, commence, initiate
词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *concludere, from Latin con- + claudere "to shut, close".  The original sense was "to shut up completely, enclose," later "to bring to an end, finish."  The sense of "to infer, deduce" arose from the idea of bringing an argument to a close.
