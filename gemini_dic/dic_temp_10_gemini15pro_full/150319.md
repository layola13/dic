pangolins:/ˈpæŋɡəlɪnz/| n.|穿山甲|Any of several Asian and African mammals of the order Pholidota, having a body covered with overlapping horny scales and a long snout specialized for feeding on ants and termites.|Pangolins are endangered because of poaching.|Synonyms: scaly anteater|Antonyms: None.|noun
