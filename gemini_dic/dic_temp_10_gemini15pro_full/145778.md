overdate:/ˌoʊvərˈdeɪt/| vt. n.|过时；过期；填写比实际日期晚的日期；过时的文件|To assign a date later than the actual date.|例句：I overdated the check by mistake. 我不小心把支票日期填晚了。|近义词：postdate, antedate|反义词: predate|verb
overdate:/ˌoʊvərˈdeɪt/| n.|过时的文件；过时的日期|A document or date assigned a date later than the actual date.|例句：The overdate on the contract caused some confusion. 合同上的过时日期引起了一些混乱。|近义词：postdate, antedate|反义词: predate|noun
