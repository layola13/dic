inaurate:/ɪˈnɔːɡjəreɪt/ /ɪˈnɑːɡjəreɪt/
verb
开启，开创；为…举行就职典礼；引进，开创
To begin or introduce (something new, such as a program, policy, or period of time). To formally install (someone) in office.
The mayor inaugurated the new park with a ribbon-cutting ceremony. 市长为新公园举行了剪彩仪式。
The president was inaugurated in January. 总统在一月份宣誓就职。
近义词:begin, commence, launch, initiate, install
反义词:terminate, conclude, end
词性:v.

Etymology:
From Latin inaugurare "to take omens from the flight of birds; to consecrate, inaugurate," from in- "in, on" + augurare "to predict, foretell," from augur "soothsayer, diviner."
