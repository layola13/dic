daughterly:/ˈdɔːtəli/ | adj. | 女儿般的；孝顺的 |  Of or relating to a daughter; like a daughter; dutiful, affectionate, and obedient as a daughter should be.  | She displayed a daughterly concern for her aging father. 她对年迈的父亲表现出女儿般的关怀。 | Example: She took care of her sick mother with daughterly devotion. 她以女儿般的奉献精神照顾生病的母亲。 | Near synonyms: filial, dutiful, respectful, devoted | Antonyms: unfilial, disrespectful, rebellious | Adjective
