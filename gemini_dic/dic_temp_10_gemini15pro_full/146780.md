overidentifications:/ˌoʊvər aɪˌdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度认同，过度认同感 | The process where an individual, often unconsciously, takes on the characteristics, beliefs, or emotions of another person or group, often as a defense mechanism or way of coping. This can occur in therapy, particularly psychoanalysis, where a patient may overidentify with the therapist. It can also manifest in other relationships or social contexts. |  The child's overidentifications with his favorite superhero led him to believe he could fly. 这个孩子过度认同了他最喜欢的超级英雄，以至于他相信自己能飞。

His overidentifications with the company made it difficult for him to accept criticism of its practices. 他对公司的过度认同让他难以接受对其做法的批评。 | synonyms:  over-identification, excessive identification | antonyms: disidentification, detachment | noun
