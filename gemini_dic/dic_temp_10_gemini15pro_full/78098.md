geographers:/dʒiˈɒɡrəfərz/
n.
地理学家们
Experts in geography, the study of the Earth's physical features, climate, and human populations.
Geographers study a wide range of topics, including climate change and urbanization.  地理学家研究范围广泛的主题，包括气候变化和城市化。
The geographers presented their findings at the conference. 地理学家们在会议上展示了他们的发现。
近义词:cartographers, earth scientists
反义词:None
词性:noun (plural)

geographer:/dʒiˈɒɡrəfər/
n.
地理学家
A person who studies geography, the study of the Earth's physical features, climate, and human populations.
The geographer specialized in the study of rivers. 这位地理学家专门研究河流。
A geographer uses maps and other tools to understand the Earth. 地理学家使用地图和其他工具来了解地球。
近义词:cartographer, earth scientist
反义词:None
词性:noun (singular)
