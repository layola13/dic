hypertorrid:/ˌhaɪpərˈtɔːrɪd/ | adj. | 极热的，酷热的 | Excessively hot and arid; intensely torrid.  Characterized by extreme heat and dryness. | The hypertorrid climate of the desert made it difficult to survive.  沙漠极度酷热的气候使得生存变得困难。|  The hypertorrid summers in Death Valley are notorious.  死亡谷酷热的夏天是出了名的。 | 近义词: scorching, sweltering, blistering, torrid | 反义词: frigid, freezing, arctic, glacial | 词性: adjective
词源: From hyper- "excessive" + torrid "burning, parched."
