insectivores:/ɪnˈsɛktɪvɔːrz/ | n. | 食虫动物 | Animals that feed primarily on insects. |例句：Bats are nocturnal insectivores.  蝙蝠是夜行性食虫动物。

例句：Shrews are small insectivores with long snouts.  鼩鼱是长着长鼻子的小型食虫动物。| 近义词：insectivorous animals | 反义词: herbivores, carnivores | 词性: Noun


Etymology: From Latin *īnsectīvorus*, from īnsectum (“insect”) + vorāre (“to devour”).
