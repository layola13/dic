embedded:/ɪmˈbedɪd/ /ɛmˈbɛdɪd/ | adj. |嵌入的，植入的，内含的|Fixed firmly and deeply in a surrounding mass; implanted.|
The thorn was embedded in her finger. 刺扎进了她的手指。|
近义词：inserted, implanted, rooted, ingrained|
反义词：superficial, removable, detachable|
词性：形容词


embedded:/ɪmˈbedɪd/ /ɛmˈbɛdɪd/ | n. |嵌入式系统|A computer system designed to perform a dedicated function, often embedded within a larger mechanical or electrical system.|
This new car uses an embedded system to control the engine. 这辆新车使用嵌入式系统来控制引擎。|
近义词：integrated system, built-in system|
反义词：standalone system|
词性：名词

词源: From the verb embed, which comes from the Middle English word embeden, from em- (in) + bed (bed).
