geminiflorous:/ˌdʒɛmɪnɪˈflɔːrəs/
adjective
成对开花的
Bearing flowers in pairs.
The geminiflorous plant produced two blossoms from each stem.  这种成对开花的植物每个茎上都开两朵花。

The botanist was fascinated by the geminiflorous nature of the rare orchid.  这位植物学家对这种稀有兰花的成对开花的特性着迷。
近义词: paired, twinned
反义词: solitary, single
词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *geminiflorus, from gemini (“twins”) + -florus (“-flowered”).
