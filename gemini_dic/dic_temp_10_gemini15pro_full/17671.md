becomely:/bɪˈkʌmli/
adjective
合适的，得体的，漂亮的
suitable, proper, and pleasing in appearance; attractive
She wore a becomely dress to the wedding. 她穿了一件得体的连衣裙去参加婚礼。
Synonyms: attractive, graceful, charming, elegant, pleasing, pretty, handsome
Antonyms: unattractive, ungainly, unseemly, inappropriate, unbecoming
词性: 形容词
