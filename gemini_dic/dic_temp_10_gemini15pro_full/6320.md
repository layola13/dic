amounted:/əˈmaʊntɪd/
|v.|总计，达到|equivalent to a particular amount or total.| The total cost amounted to $500.  总费用达500美元。|totaled, reached, added up to|decreased, lessened|verb


amounted:/əˈmaʊntɪd/
|adj.|（用在名词前）相当于…的；等同于…的|being the equivalent of something, or having the same effect as something|His silence amounted to an admission of guilt. 他的沉默等于承认有罪。|equivalent to, tantamount to|different from|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French amonter "to ascend, climb," from a- "to" + monter "to mount, ascend" (from Latin montem, accusative of mons "mountain").  The financial sense developed from the idea of "adding up to."
