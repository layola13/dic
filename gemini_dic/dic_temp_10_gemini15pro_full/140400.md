ointment:/ˈɔɪntmənt/| n. |药膏；软膏|A smooth oily substance that is put on the skin to heal it. |例句：The doctor prescribed an ointment for my rash. 医生给我开了治疗皮疹的药膏。

例句：Apply the ointment to the affected area twice a day.  每天两次将药膏涂抹在患处。


|近义词：cream, balm, salve|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps irritant)|词性:noun
