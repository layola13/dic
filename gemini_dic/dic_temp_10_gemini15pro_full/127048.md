nasturtium: /nəˈstɜːʃəm/ (UK), /nəˈstɜːrʃəm/ (US) | n. |旱金莲; 金莲花 | A kind of garden plant with brightly colored, usually orange or yellow, flowers. The leaves and flowers can be eaten.  | Example: The nasturtiums in her garden were a vibrant shade of orange. 她花园里的旱金莲是鲜艳的橙色。

Example: We added nasturtium leaves to the salad for a peppery kick. 我们在沙拉里加了旱金莲叶子来增加辣味。| 近义词: Indian cress | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
