outwrestled: /ˌaʊtˈrɛsəld/
verb
胜过，击败（通过摔跤）
to defeat someone by wrestling; to take something away from someone with difficulty
He outwrestled his opponent in the final round. 他在最后一轮摔跤比赛中击败了对手。
synonyms: overcome, defeat, conquer, overpower, best, triumph over
antonyms: lose to, be defeated by, succumb to
词性: 动词
