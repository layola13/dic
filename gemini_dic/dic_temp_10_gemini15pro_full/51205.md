destinist:/ˈdɛstɪnɪst/
n.
宿命论者；命运决定论者
A person who believes in the philosophical doctrine of determinism, the idea that all events, including human actions, are ultimately determined by causes external to the will.
例句：The destinist believed that his future was predetermined. 宿命论者相信他的未来是注定的。

例句：She argued against the destinist's view, claiming free will plays a significant role. 她反驳了宿命论者的观点，声称自由意志起着重要作用。


近义词：fatalist, determinist
反义词：indeterminist, libertarian (in philosophy)
词性：noun
