menacer:/ˈmenəsər/ (US), /ˈmɛnəsə(r)/ (UK)| n.|威胁者，恐吓者|A person or thing that represents a threat or danger.  A menacer is someone who poses a threat, either through direct action or through creating a sense of danger.|The dictator was a menacer to world peace.  这个独裁者对世界和平构成威胁。

The rising floodwaters were a menacer to the nearby village.  不断上涨的洪水对附近的村庄构成了威胁。|近义词:threat, danger, peril, hazard, risk|反义词:protector, guardian, defender|Noun
