complementariness:/ˌkɒmplɪmɛnˈtærɪnəs/ |n.| 互补性，补充性|The complementariness of their skills made them a great team. 他们的技能互补，使他们成为一个很棒的团队。| complementariness relates to the way two things complement or complete each other, often by supplying what is lacking in the other. It signifies a relationship where separate elements enhance or emphasize each other's qualities, creating a more complete or effective whole.  For instance, the complementariness of DNA base pairs allows for accurate replication and storage of genetic information. | synonym: compatibility, synergy, interdependency| antonym: incompatibility, conflict, disparity| noun
