noways:/ˈnoʊˌweɪz/| adv. |绝不，没门| An emphatic expression of disagreement or refusal.| "Noways am I going to that party!"  绝不，我不会去那个派对！| 近义词: no way, absolutely not, by no means | 反义词: definitely, certainly, by all means | 词性: 副词

词源:  "Noways" is a variant of "no way," which itself is a shortening of "in no way." The "noways" form is older and now considered more informal or dialectal, particularly associated with some dialects of English, like Scottish English.
