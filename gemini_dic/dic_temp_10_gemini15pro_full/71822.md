flees:/fliːz/
verb
逃跑，逃走
Third-person singular present tense form of flee.  It describes the action of running away from danger or an unpleasant situation.
The thief flees the scene after robbing the bank.  小偷抢劫银行后逃离现场。
The family flees the burning building. 这家人逃离燃烧的建筑物。
近义词: escapes, runs away, absconds, bolts
反义词: faces, confronts, remains, stays
词性: verb
