cybersquatter:/ˈsaɪbərˌskwɑːtər/| n. |域名抢注者，网络蟑螂|Someone who registers an Internet domain name (especially one that includes the name of a well-known company or person) in the hope of selling it at a profit.|Example 1: The cybersquatter was asking for a huge sum of money for the domain name. 这个域名抢注者对这个域名要价很高。

Example 2:  Many famous brands have been victims of cybersquatting. 许多知名品牌都成为域名抢注的受害者。|近义词：domain name hijacker|反义词: legitimate domain owner|词性:noun
