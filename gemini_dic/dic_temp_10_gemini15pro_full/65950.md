exactitudes:/ɪɡˈzæktɪtjuːdz/| n.|精确性；一丝不苟；（尤指艺术上的）刻意追求|The exactitudes of the performance impressed the audience.  表演的精确性给观众留下了深刻的印象。|例句：He insisted on exactitudes in every detail. 他坚持每个细节都要一丝不苟。|近义词：precision, accuracy, meticulousness|反义词:inexactitude, imprecision, carelessness|词性:名词

词源:  From French *exactitude*, from Latin *exactitūdō*, from *exactus*, past participle of *exigō* (“to demand, require, ascertain”).
