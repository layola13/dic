incubator:/ˈɪŋkjuˌbeɪtər/| n.|孵化器；培养箱；早产儿保育箱|An apparatus used to hatch eggs or grow microorganisms under controlled conditions.  A place, especially one offering support, guidance, and resources, where new businesses are helped to develop.|The university has a business incubator to support student startups.|近义词:couveuse, hatchery, nurturer|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用destructor 或 inhibitor)|名词
