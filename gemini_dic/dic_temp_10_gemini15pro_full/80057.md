goaling:/ˈɡoʊlɪŋ/
n.|目标设定，目标管理|The process of setting and achieving goals.  The act of establishing objectives.|
例句：Goaling is a crucial part of project management. 目标设定是项目管理的关键部分。

例句：Effective goaling requires clear, measurable objectives. 有效的目标设定需要清晰、可衡量的目标。
近义词：goal setting, target setting, objective setting
反义词：aimlessness, drifting, lack of direction
词性: 名词 (noun)

词源:  Derived from the noun "goal" with the suffix "-ing" forming a gerund or present participle.  "Goal" itself originates from the Old English "gāl" meaning "obstacle, barrier."  Over time, the meaning shifted from something to be avoided to something to be strived for.
