metabasis:/mɛˈtæbəsɪs/
n.
过渡，转移; (修辞学) 转折
A transition from one subject to another in writing or speaking.
In the next chapter, we will discuss the implications of this theory. This metabasis marks a shift in our focus from theoretical foundations to practical applications.  下一章，我们将讨论这个理论的含义。这种过渡标志着我们的重点从理论基础转向实际应用。

Metabasis is used to signal a change in topic or argument.  过渡用于表示主题或论点的变化。

近义词:transition, shift, change
反义词:continuation, sameness
词性:名词
