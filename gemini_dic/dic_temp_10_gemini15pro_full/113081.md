lunarian:/luːˈnɛəriən/| n. |月球居民；月球研究者|A person who studies the moon or a hypothetical inhabitant of the moon.|例句：The lunarian observed the lunar eclipse through his telescope.  这位月球研究者用望远镜观察了月食。

例句：Science fiction often depicts lunarians as advanced beings. 科幻小说经常将月球居民描绘成高级生物。|近义词：selenite, moon dweller|反义词:earthling|词性:noun
