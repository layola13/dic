misprovoke:/ˌmɪsprəˈvoʊk/
verb
错误地激怒，无故挑衅
To provoke wrongly or without due cause.
He misprovoked the guards with his careless words. 他用粗心的话语无故激怒了警卫。
The comedian misprovoked the audience with a controversial joke.  这位喜剧演员用一个有争议的笑话错误地激怒了观众。

近义词:  provoke, irritate, annoy, aggravate
反义词: placate, appease, soothe, calm
词性: verb
