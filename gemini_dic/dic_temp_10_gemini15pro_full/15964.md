balsamine:/ˈbɔːlsəmɪn/| n.|凤仙花；凤仙花属植物|A flowering plant of the genus Impatiens, typically with brightly colored flowers.  The name derives from its supposed medicinal properties, particularly for healing wounds.|The garden was full of balsamines in vibrant shades of pink and orange. 花园里开满了鲜艳的粉色和橙色的凤仙花。|Synonyms: impatiens, touch-me-not|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

balsam:/ˈbɔːlsəm/| n.|香脂；香膏；镇痛药膏|A fragrant, resinous substance obtained from certain trees, used in perfumes and medicines. Also refers to a soothing ointment.|She applied balsam to the cut to soothe the pain. 她把香膏涂在伤口上以减轻疼痛。|Synonyms: balm, ointment, salve|Antonyms: irritant|Noun
