matelasse:/ˌmæt.ləˈseɪ/ /ˌmɑːt.ləˈseɪ/
noun
凸纹织物，提花织物
A woven fabric with a raised or quilted design.
例句：She bought a beautiful matelasse bedspread. 她买了一条漂亮的凸纹床罩。

例句：The matelasse fabric added a touch of elegance to the room.  这种凸纹织物给房间增添了一丝优雅。

ETYMOLOGY->
From French matelasse, from matelas ("mattress"). Ultimately from Arabic مطرح (maṭraḥ, “place where something is thrown”).
