partridge:/ˈpɑːrtrɪdʒ/ (US), /ˈpɑːtrɪdʒ/ (UK)| n.|鹧鸪|A medium-sized game bird with a short, curved bill and strong legs, often found in open fields and farmland.|例句：The hunter flushed a covey of partridges from the bushes. 猎人从灌木丛中惊起一群鹧鸪。

例句：Partridges are known for their distinctive drumming sound. 鹧鸪以其独特的鼓声而闻名。


近义词：grouse, quail|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


partridge:/ˈpɑːrtrɪdʒ/ (US), /ˈpɑːtrɪdʒ/ (UK)| n.|鹧鸪肉|The meat of a partridge, considered a delicacy.|例句：We had roast partridge for dinner last night. 我们昨晚吃了烤鹧鸪肉作为晚餐。


例句：Partridge meat is quite lean and gamey. 鹧鸪肉很瘦，而且有野味。


近义词：game (as in game meat)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
