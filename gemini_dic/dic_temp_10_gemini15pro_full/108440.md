laxative:/ˈlæksətɪv/
adjective|通便的，泻药的|Having the power to relieve constipation.|Prunes are known for their laxative effect. 西梅以其通便作用而闻名。|Synonyms: purgative, cathartic|Antonyms: astringent, constipating
word type: adjective

laxative:/ˈlæksətɪv/
noun|泻药|A medicine or agent for relieving constipation.|The doctor prescribed a mild laxative. 医生开了些温和的泻药。|Synonyms: purgative, cathartic, aperient|Antonyms: antidiarrheal, constipating agent
word type: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *laxativus, from Latin laxare (“to loosen”), from laxus (“loose”).
