homeopathy/ˌhoʊmiˈɑːpəθi/| n. |顺势疗法|A system of treating diseases by using very small amounts of a substance that would in larger amounts produce the same symptoms of the disease in healthy people.|She's trying homeopathy to treat her allergies.|Near synonyms: alternative medicine, holistic medicine|Antonyms: conventional medicine|noun

homoeopathy/ˌhɒmɪˈɒpəθi/| n. |顺势疗法|(British spelling of homeopathy) A system of treating diseases by using very small amounts of a substance that would in larger amounts produce the same symptoms of the disease in healthy people.|He opted for homoeopathy after conventional medicine failed to alleviate his symptoms.|Near synonyms: alternative medicine, holistic medicine|Antonyms: conventional medicine|noun
