immersively:/ɪˈmɜːrsɪvli/
adverb|沉浸式地，身临其境地|in a way that involves someone completely|例句：The game is immersively designed to make players feel like they are really there.  这款游戏采用沉浸式设计，让玩家感觉身临其境。

例句：The virtual reality experience was incredibly immersive. 虚拟现实体验令人难以置信地身临其境。


近义词：deeply, intensely, completely, engagingly
反义词：superficially, distantly, shallowly
词性: adverb
词源: From immerse + -ive + -ly.  Immerse comes from Late Latin immersus, past participle of immergere "to plunge in," from in- "in" + mergere "to dip, plunge."
