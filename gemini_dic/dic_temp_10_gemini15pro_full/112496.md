louring: /ˈlaʊərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈlaʊərɪŋ/ (UK) | adj. | 阴沉的，怒容满面的 | appearing dark and threatening, especially of the sky; frowning or scowling |  The louring sky threatened rain. 阴沉的天空预示着要下雨。

The captain's louring face showed his displeasure. 船长阴沉的脸色表明了他的不悦。 | 近义词：gloomy, dark, menacing, scowling, sullen | 反义词：bright, sunny, cheerful, smiling | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *louren, *luren (“to frown, scowl, look sullen”), of uncertain origin. Possibly from or related to Middle Dutch *luren (“to peer, lurk, lie hidden”), or from a Scandinavian source akin to Icelandic laumr (“deceit, treachery”).
