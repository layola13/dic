effusive:/ɪˈfjuːsɪv/
|adj.|热情洋溢的；感情奔放的|Expressing feelings of gratitude, pleasure, or approval in an unrestrained or heartfelt manner.|She greeted him with effusive praise. 她热情洋溢地赞扬了他。|Synonyms: enthusiastic, gushing, exuberant, demonstrative|Antonyms: reserved, restrained, undemonstrative, reticent|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin effūsus, past participle of effundere (“to pour out”), from ex- (“out”) + fundere (“to pour”).
