hypernutrition:/ˌhaɪpərnuˈtrɪʃən/
n.
过度营养，营养过剩
A condition of excessive nutrition, often characterized by overconsumption of calories and specific nutrients.
Excessive intake of nutrients, leading to an imbalance and potential health problems.
例句：Hypernutrition can lead to obesity and other health issues. 过度营养会导致肥胖和其他健康问题。

例句：Childhood hypernutrition is a growing concern in developed countries. 儿童过度营养在发达国家是一个日益严重的问题。

近义词：overnutrition, overfeeding
反义词：malnutrition, undernutrition
词性：名词

词源：hyper- (Greek: over, excessive) + nutrition (Latin: nutritio, nourishment)
