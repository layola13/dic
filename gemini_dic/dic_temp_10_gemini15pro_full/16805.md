baselard:/ˈbæsəlɑːrd/ (US), /ˈbæsləd/ (UK)| n.|短剑，匕首|A type of dagger with an I-shaped cross-section blade popular in the 14th and 15th centuries. It was primarily used as a thrusting weapon.|He drew his baselard and prepared to defend himself. 他拔出匕首准备自卫。|The baselard was often worn as a civilian weapon. 这把匕首经常被作为平民武器佩戴。|近义词：dagger, knife, poniard|反义词: (No direct antonym for a weapon.  Perhaps "shield" in a conceptual sense)|词性: noun
