inequitableness:/ɪnˈɛkwɪtəbəlnəs/
n.
不公平, 不公正, 不平等
The state or quality of being inequitable; unfairness; injustice.
The inequitableness of the legal system was apparent to all.  法律制度的不公正对所有人来说都是显而易见的。
Synonyms: unfairness, injustice, partiality, bias, discrimination
Antonyms: fairness, justice, equity, impartiality, evenhandedness
