erasing:/ɪˈreɪsɪŋ/
v.
擦除；清除
The act of removing something written, drawn, recorded, or stored.
He was erasing the mistake from the whiteboard. 他正在擦除白板上的错误。
近义词: deleting, removing, obliterating, wiping
反义词: writing, drawing, recording, storing
词性: 动词 (Verb)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "esraser" (to scratch out, erase), from "ex-" (out) + "rader" (to scrape).  The Latin root is "radere" (to scrape, shave).
