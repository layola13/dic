farm:/fɑːrm/ (UK) /fɑːrm/ (US)| n. v.|农场；农田；养殖场；经营农场；耕种|A piece of land used for growing crops or raising animals.|例句：My grandfather owns a large farm. 我祖父拥有一个大农场。|近义词：ranch, plantation, homestead|反义词:city, factory|词性:名词, 动词

farm:/fɑːrm/ (UK) /fɑːrm/ (US)| v.|耕种；养殖|To cultivate land or raise animals for food or other products.|例句：They farm organically. 他们从事有机农业。|近义词：cultivate, grow, raise|反义词:neglect|词性:动词
