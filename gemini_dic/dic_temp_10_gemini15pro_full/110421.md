likeability:/ˌlaɪkəˈbɪləti/| n.|好感度，受欢迎程度|The quality of being pleasant and easy to like.  It refers to how agreeable, pleasant, and appealing someone is perceived to be by others.|Her likeability made her a popular choice for class president. 她很受欢迎，因此被选为班长。|近义词：affability, charm, charisma, pleasantness|反义词：unpopularity, obnoxiousness|noun
