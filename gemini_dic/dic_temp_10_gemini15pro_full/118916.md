metazoon:/ˌmɛtəˈzoʊən/
n.
后生动物，多细胞动物
A multicellular animal; an animal whose body is composed of many cells differentiated into tissues and organs.
Sponges, jellyfish, insects, and humans are all examples of metazoans. 海绵、水母、昆虫和人类都是后生动物的例子。
metazoa (plural)
multicellular animal, animal
protozoon (单细胞动物)
noun
