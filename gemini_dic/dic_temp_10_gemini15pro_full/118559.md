mesopodiale:/ˌmɛsəˈpoʊdiˌeɪl/ | adj. | 中间足的，中足的 | Relating to or situated in the middle region of the foot.  Specifically refers to the central ray of the foot, consisting of the second, third, and fourth metatarsals and their corresponding phalanges. | The *mesopodiale* element of the foot is crucial for weight-bearing. 中足部分对承重至关重要。|  Mesopodial reduction is observed in some ungulates. 在一些有蹄类动物中观察到中足的退化。 | medial, central | lateral, peripheral | adjective
