go-to-it:/ˈɡəʊ tuː ɪt/ | adj. | 积极肯干的；有进取心的 |  Someone who is go-to-it is very energetic and determined. | She's a real go-to-it person; she'll get the job done. 她是一个真正积极肯干的人；她会完成这项工作的。|  energetic, driven, ambitious, motivated | lazy, apathetic, lethargic | adjective

go-getter:/ˈɡəʊˌɡetər/ | n. |  积极进取的人；能干的人 | A go-getter is someone who is very energetic, determined to be successful, and able to deal with new or difficult situations easily. | He's a real go-getter and I'm sure he'll do well in the new job. 他是一个真正的实干家，我相信他会在这份新工作中做得很好。 |  achiever, high-flyer, dynamo | slacker, underachiever | noun
