overloving:/ˌōvərˈlʌvɪŋ/
adjective
过分溺爱的，过分关爱的
Showing excessive love or affection, often to a point of being detrimental.
She was overloving to her children, smothering them with attention. 她对孩子们过分溺爱，让他们感到窒息。
近义词：doting, smothering, overprotective
反义词：neglectful, indifferent, detached
词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "loving" (present participle of "love")
