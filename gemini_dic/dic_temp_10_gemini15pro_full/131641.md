noncompromising:/nɒnˈkɒmprəmaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
不妥协的，不让步的
Unwilling to make concessions or to yield from a principle or position.
例句：He has a reputation for being a tough and noncompromising negotiator. 他以强硬且不妥协的谈判者而闻名。

例句：Her noncompromising stance on ethical issues earned her the respect of her colleagues.  她在道德问题上毫不妥协的立场赢得了同事们的尊重。

近义词：inflexible, unyielding, resolute, firm, steadfast
反义词：compromising, flexible, yielding, accommodating
词性: adjective
