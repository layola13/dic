conceptualize:/kənˈsɛptʃuəlaɪz/ | v. |使形成概念；构思；将…概念化|To form a concept or idea of something; to think of something in terms of its abstract characteristics. |例句：Scientists are trying to conceptualize how the universe began. 科学家们正试图构想宇宙是如何起源的。

|例句：It's difficult to conceptualize the vastness of space. 很难想象太空的浩瀚。|近义词：conceive, envision, imagine, formulate, ideate|反义词: misconceive, misunderstand|词性:动词
