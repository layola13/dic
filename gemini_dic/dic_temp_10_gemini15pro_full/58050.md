drollnesses:/ˈdroʊlnəsəz/
noun
滑稽古怪；古怪有趣之处
The quality or state of being droll; whimsical, quaint, or odd humor.
His book is full of drollnesses that make you chuckle unexpectedly. 他的书里充满了让你意想不到地咯咯笑出声的古怪幽默。
synonyms: eccentricities, oddities, quirks, whimsicalities, peculiarities
antonyms: normalities, commonalities, regularities
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

drollness:/ˈdroʊlnəs/
noun

The quality or state of being droll; whimsical, quaint, or odd humor.

droll:/droʊl/
adjective
滑稽古怪的；离奇可笑的
Curious or unusual in a way that provokes dry amusement.
He has a droll sense of humor. 他有一种古怪的幽默感。
synonyms: amusing, comical, funny, humorous, witty, whimsical, quirky
antonyms: serious, solemn, grave, humorless, dull
词性: 形容词
