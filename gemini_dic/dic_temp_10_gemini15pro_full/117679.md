meiotically:/maɪˈɒtɪkli/ |adv.| 减数分裂地 | In a manner relating to meiosis, a type of cell division that results in four daughter cells each with half the number of chromosomes of the parent cell. | The chromosomes separate meiotically. 染色体进行减数分裂。|  Synonym: reductionally | Antonym: mitotically | Word Type: Adverb

meiosis:/maɪˈoʊsɪs/ |n.| 减数分裂 | A type of cell division that results in four daughter cells each with half the number of chromosomes of the parent cell. | Meiosis is essential for sexual reproduction. 减数分裂对于有性繁殖至关重要。| Synonym: reduction division | Antonym: mitosis | Word Type: Noun
