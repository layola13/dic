beneficed:/ˈbɛnɪfɪst/| adj.|有俸禄的，享有圣俸的|Holding a church office with an endowed income.  This adjective describes a member of the clergy who holds a benefice.|The beneficed clergyman lived in a large house provided by the church.  这位享有圣俸的神职人员住在教堂提供的一所大房子里。|Synonyms: endowed,  stipendiary|Antonyms: unbeneficed, lay|adjective
