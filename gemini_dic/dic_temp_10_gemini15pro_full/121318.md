misfortuned:/ˌmɪsˈfɔːrtʃənd/ /ˌmɪsˈfɔːrtʃʊnd/ | adj. |不幸的，倒霉的 |  Unlucky; having bad fortune.  Derived from "misfortune" with the suffix "-ed."  Implies a state of being afflicted by bad luck.  Less common than "unfortunate." | He was a misfortuned soul, plagued by ill health and bad luck. 他是一个不幸的人，饱受病痛和厄运的折磨。| synonym: unfortunate, unlucky, ill-fated, hapless | antonym: fortunate, lucky, blessed | adjective
