litchis:/ˈliːtʃiːz/
n.
荔枝
Tropical fruit with a rough reddish-brown rind and sweet white flesh.
I love the sweet taste of fresh litchis. 我喜欢新鲜荔枝的甜味。
The market was filled with piles of ripe litchis. 市场里堆满了成熟的荔枝。
近义词:lychees
反义词:(无明确反义词)
词性:名词
