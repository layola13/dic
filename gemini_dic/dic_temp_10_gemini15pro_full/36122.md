coerce:/koʊˈɜːrs/| vt.|胁迫，强迫|To compel by force, intimidation, or authority, especially without regard for individual will.|The government coerced the rebels into surrendering their weapons. 政府胁迫叛军交出武器。|近义词：force, compel, pressure, intimidate|反义词:persuade, encourage|动词

coerces:/koʊˈɜːrsɪz/| vt.|胁迫，强迫（第三人称单数形式）|The third-person singular simple present indicative form of coerce.|He coerces his employees to work overtime without pay. 他强迫他的员工无薪加班。|近义词：forces, compels, pressures, intimidates|反义词:persuades, encourages|动词

词源:
From Latin *coercēre, from com- "together" + arcēre "to enclose, confine, keep off."
