deghosts:/diːˈɡoʊsts/
verb
去除重影，消除鬼影
Removes ghosting artifacts, typically in images or videos.
The new software deghosts the security camera footage, making it much clearer.  这个新软件去除了监控录像中的重影，使画面更加清晰。
The editing software can deghosts the image, removing the blurry double image.  这个编辑软件可以去除图像中的重影，消除模糊的双重影像。
近义词:clarifies, sharpens, cleans, enhances
反义词:blurs, distorts
词性:v.
