dunnies:/ˈdʌniz/ | n. | 厕所，盥洗室（主要用于澳大利亚和新西兰）| a toilet, lavatory (chiefly Australia and New Zealand) | Example:  I need to use the dunnies. 我需要用一下厕所。

Example:  Where are the dunnies? 厕所在哪里？

近义词: toilet, lavatory, bathroom, restroom, loo, WC | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词


词源:  Possibly from the British dialect word *dunnyken*, meaning a dunghill or privy. This in turn may be related to *dung*.
