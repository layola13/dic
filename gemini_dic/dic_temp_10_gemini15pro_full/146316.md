overfeeling:/ˌōvərˈfiːlɪŋ/
adjective
过度感伤的，过于敏感的
Excessively emotional or sentimental; too readily affected by emotions.
She's a bit overfeeling and takes everything to heart. 她有点过度感伤，什么事都往心里去。
He's overfeeling about the criticism he received. 他对收到的批评过于敏感。
近义词:oversensitive, sentimental, emotional
反义词:insensitive, unemotional, apathetic
词性:adjective

overfeeling: /ˌōvərˈfiːlɪŋ/
noun
过度感伤，过度敏感
The state of being excessively emotional or sentimental.
Her overfeeling made it difficult for her to cope with stress. 她的过度敏感使她难以应对压力。
His overfeeling was evident in his reaction to the news.  从他对这消息的反应可以明显看出他过于感伤。
近义词:oversensitivity, sentimentality
反义词:insensitivity, apathy
词性:noun
