monolith:/ˈmɒnəlɪθ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəlɪθ/ (US)| n. |巨石，独石；庞然大物；大型单体结构|A large single upright block of stone, especially one shaped into or serving as a pillar or monument.  A large, powerful, and indivisible entity, especially a business or political organization.|例句：The company is a monolith in the computer industry.  这家公司是计算机行业的巨头。|例句：We visited the ancient monolith on the Salisbury Plain. 我们参观了索尔兹伯里平原上的古代巨石。|近义词：pillar, obelisk, megalith, behemoth, giant|反义词:fragment, piece|词性:noun
