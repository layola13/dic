open-minded:/ˌoʊpən ˈmaɪndɪd/ | adj. | 思想开明的，虚心的 | Willing to consider new ideas; unprejudiced. | She has a very open-minded attitude towards new experiences. 她对新体验持非常开放的态度。|  He's open-minded about trying different kinds of food.  他愿意尝试不同种类的食物。 | 近义词: receptive, tolerant, broad-minded | 反义词: narrow-minded, prejudiced, dogmatic | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "open" + "minded."  "Open" traces back to Old English "open," meaning "not closed, accessible." "Minded" comes from Old English "gemyndig," meaning "mindful." The combination emphasizes a mind that is "open" to new ideas and perspectives.
