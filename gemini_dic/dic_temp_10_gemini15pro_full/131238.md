capricious (adjective): given to sudden and unaccountable changes of mood or behavior. From Latin "capriciosus," meaning "whimsical," derived from "caper," meaning "goat" (referring to a goat's frisky and unpredictable nature).
-ness (suffix): forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
