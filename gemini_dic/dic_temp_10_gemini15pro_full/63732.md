epiblastic:/ˌepɪˈblæs.tɪk/
adjective
外胚层的，上胚层的
Relating to or derived from the epiblast.  The epiblast is the outermost layer of cells in the early embryo, which gives rise to the ectoderm and part of the mesoderm.
The epiblastic cells differentiate into various tissues.  外胚层细胞分化成各种组织。
The study focused on the epiblastic origin of the nervous system. 这项研究的重点是神经系统的外胚层起源。
Synonyms: ectodermal, ectoblastic
Antonyms: hypoblastic
词性: adjective
