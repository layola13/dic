contrariant:/kənˈtreəriənt/ /kɑːnˈtreəriənt/
adjective|乖戾的，反对的|Inclined to oppose or contradict; perverse.|He was being difficult and contrariant, refusing to agree with anything we said. 他很固执，处处唱反调，拒绝同意我们说的任何话.|近义词:perverse, awkward, contrary, opposing, recalcitrant, refractory, uncooperative|反义词:agreeable, compliant, cooperative, obedient, willing|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Late Latin contrarius "opposite, contrary" + -ant.
