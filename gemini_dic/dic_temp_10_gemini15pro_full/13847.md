auger:/ˈɔːɡər/ (US) /ˈɔːɡə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.| 螺钻，钻孔，钻孔机|A tool with a helical blade for boring holes, especially in wood or ice.  It can also refer to the act of boring with such a tool.|例句：He used an auger to drill a hole for the fence post. 他用螺钻钻了一个洞来立栅栏柱子。

例句：They had to auger through the thick ice to reach the water. 他们不得不钻透厚厚的冰层才能取到水。|近义词：drill, borer, gimlet|反义词: fill, plug|词性:名词，动词
