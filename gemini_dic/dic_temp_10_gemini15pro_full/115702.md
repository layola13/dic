marginally:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnəli/ | adv. | 少量地，稍微地，边缘地 | In a way that is small and not very important; slightly. | The results were marginally better than last year. 结果比去年略好一些。|  The drug improved the patients' condition only marginally.  这种药只略微改善了病人的病情。 | 近义词: slightly, minimally, insignificantly, barely | 反义词: significantly, substantially, considerably | 词性: 副词

margin:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪn/ | n. | 边缘，空白，利润，余地 | The edge or border of something. The amount by which one thing is different from another.  |  The book has wide margins. 这本书的页边距很宽。 | The company operates on very narrow profit margins. 这家公司的利润率很低。 | 近义词: edge, border, rim, periphery, profit, leeway | 反义词: center, middle, core | 词性: 名词
