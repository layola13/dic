packrats:/ˈpækræts/| n.|鼠类; 囤积狂; |A person who compulsively saves things, often of little or no value.  指强迫性地储存东西的人，通常这些东西没什么价值或毫无价值。|My grandmother was a packrat; her attic was filled with old newspapers and broken appliances.  我祖母是个囤积狂；她的阁楼里堆满了旧报纸和破损的家电。|synonyms: hoarder, collector, accumulator|antonyms: minimalist, purger|noun

packrat:/ˈpækræt/| n.|鼠类; 囤积狂; |A rodent of the genus *Neotoma*, known for collecting and hoarding various objects. 指*Neotoma*属的一种啮齿动物，以收集和囤积各种物品而闻名。|Packrats often build large nests filled with sticks, leaves, and shiny objects.  林鼠经常用树枝、树叶和闪亮的物体建造大型巢穴。|synonyms: woodrat, trade rat|antonyms: N/A|noun
