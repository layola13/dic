alarmingly:/əˈlɑːmɪŋli/ | adv. | 惊人地，令人担忧地 | In a way that causes worry or fear. |  The pollution levels are alarmingly high. 污染水平高得惊人。| Synonyms: worryingly, disturbingly, concerningly | Antonyms: reassuringly, comfortingly | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From alarm + -ing + -ly.  "Alarm" comes from the Italian "all'arme" meaning "to arms," a cry to warn of danger.
