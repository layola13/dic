chain-store:/ˈtʃeɪn stɔːr/ (US) /ˈtʃeɪn stɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.|连锁店|A group of similar shops/stores owned by the same company and located in different places.|Many chain-stores offer discounts and promotions.|synonyms: multiple store, retail chain|antonyms: independent store, local business|noun

chain-store:/ˈtʃeɪn stɔːr/ (US) /ˈtʃeɪn stɔː(r)/ (UK)| adj.|连锁店的|Relating to or characteristic of a chain store.|The chain-store mentality often prioritizes profit over customer service.|synonyms: corporate, mass-market|antonyms: independent, local|adjective
