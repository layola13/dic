overstorey:/ˈəʊvəˌstɔːri/ (UK), /ˈoʊvərˌstɔːri/ (US)| n. |上层树冠，林冠层|The overstorey of a rainforest absorbs most of the sunlight.|雨林的林冠层吸收了大部分阳光。|例句：The monkeys lived in the overstorey of the jungle. 猴子生活在丛林的林冠层。

例句：Researchers are studying the impact of climate change on the forest overstorey. 研究人员正在研究气候变化对森林冠层的影响。

近义词：canopy, crown|反义词:understorey|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over + storey.  "Storey" refers to a level of a building.  "Overstorey" extends this concept to the uppermost layer of a forest, analogous to the top storey of a building.
