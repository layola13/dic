desperateness:/ˈdɛspərətnəs/| n. |绝望，拼命，铤而走险 |The state of being desperate; hopelessness or extreme recklessness.|Driven to desperateness by hunger, he stole a loaf of bread. |近义词：desperation, hopelessness, despair, recklessness|反义词：hope, calmness, composure|词性:名词
词源: From desperate + -ness.  "Desperate" comes from the Late Latin "desperatus," past participle of "desperare"  meaning "to give up hope," from "de-" (down, away) + "sperare" (to hope).
