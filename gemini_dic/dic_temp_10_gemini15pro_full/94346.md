impinges:/ɪmˈpɪndʒɪz/| v.| 撞击，冲击，影响 |The noise of the traffic impinges on my concentration. 交通噪音影响我的注意力。|例句：The light impinges on the retina. 光线撞击视网膜。|例句：Her problems impinge on all of us. 她的问题影响到我们所有人。|近义词：affect, influence, impact, encroach|反义词：avoid, miss|词性：动词

impinges:/ɪmˈpɪndʒɪz/| v.| 侵犯，侵害|The new law impinges on our civil liberties. 新法律侵犯了我们的公民自由。|例句：His actions impinged upon her privacy. 他的行为侵犯了她的隐私。|例句：The building impinges on the neighbor's property. 这座建筑侵占了邻居的财产。|近义词：encroach, infringe, intrude|反义词：respect, uphold|词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impingere, present active infinitive of impingō (“I push/drive into, strike against”), from in- (“in, into, on, upon, against”) + pangō (“I fix, fasten, insert”).
