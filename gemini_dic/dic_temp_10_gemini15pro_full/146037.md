overdrily: /ˌoʊvərˈdraɪli/ | adv. | 过于干燥地，过于冷淡地 |  In an excessively dry or unemotional manner.  It describes something done or said in a way that lacks warmth or feeling, often to an excessive degree. | He responded overdrily to her enthusiastic greeting, making her feel unwelcome. 他对她的热情问候反应过于冷淡，让她感到不受欢迎。|  The comedian delivered his jokes overdrily, which surprisingly made them even funnier.  这位喜剧演员非常冷淡地讲笑话，令人惊讶的是，这反而让笑话更有趣了。 |  apathetically, coldly, indifferently, flatly | warmly, enthusiastically, emotionally | adverb
