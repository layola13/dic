immeasurablenesses:/ɪˈmɛʒərəblənɪsɪz/
|n.|不可估量性；无限性；无边无际|The immeasurablenesses of the universe continue to fascinate scientists. 宇宙的浩瀚无垠继续吸引着科学家们。|The immeasurablenesses of his love for her were evident in his every action. 他对她无限的爱体现在他的一举一动中。|近义词: vastness, immensity, boundlessness, infinity|反义词: finiteness, smallness, limitedness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From immeasurableness + -es (plural suffix).  Immeasurableness is derived from immeasurable + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality). Immeasurable is formed from in- (prefix meaning "not") + measurable. Measurable comes from measure + -able (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being"). Ultimately from Latin *metiri "to measure."
