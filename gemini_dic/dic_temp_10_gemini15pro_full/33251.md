chronoscope: /ˈkrɒnəskəʊp/ (British) /ˈkrɑːnəskoʊp/ (American) | n. | 计时器；精密计时器 | An instrument for measuring very short intervals of time, often down to milliseconds or even smaller units. It's used in scientific experiments, psychology, and other fields where precise time measurement is crucial. |  The psychologist used a chronoscope to measure the subject's reaction time. 心理学家使用计时器来测量受试者的反应时间。

The chronoscope recorded the exact duration of the electrical impulse. 计时器记录了电脉冲的确切持续时间。


|Synonym: timer, stopwatch, chronograph |Antonym: N/A | noun
