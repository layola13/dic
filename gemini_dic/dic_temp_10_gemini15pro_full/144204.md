outsing:/ˌaʊtˈsɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的歌唱，比…唱得好
To sing louder or better than someone else.
He outsing all the other contestants in the competition. 他在比赛中胜过所有其他选手。
She can outsing anyone in the choir.  她能在唱诗班里胜过任何人。
synonyms: surpass, outdo, excel, outperform
antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb
