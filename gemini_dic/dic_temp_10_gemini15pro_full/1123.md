ETYMOLOGY->
From French *accessoiriser*, from *accessoire* "accessory," ultimately from Latin *accessorius* "additional, supplementary."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
She accessorises her outfits with scarves. = 她用围巾搭配她的服装。
He always accessorises with a hat. = 他总是戴着帽子。

一般过去时:
She accessorised her dress with a pearl necklace. = 她用珍珠项链搭配了她的裙子。
He accessorised his suit with a pocket square. = 他用口袋巾搭配了他的西装。


现在完成时:
She has accessorised her outfit perfectly. = 她完美地搭配了她的服装。
He has never accessorised before. = 他以前从未搭配过服装。


过去完成时:

He had already accessorised his suit when I arrived. = 当我到达时，他已经搭配好了他的西装。


将来时:
