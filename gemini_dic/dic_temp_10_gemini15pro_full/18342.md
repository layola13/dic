belligerent: /bəˈlɪdʒərənt/ | adj. n. |好战的；交战的；交战国，交战者 | Inclined to or exhibiting assertiveness, hostility, or combativeness.  A nation or person engaged in war or conflict. |  例句：His belligerent attitude made it difficult to negotiate. 他的好战态度使谈判变得困难。| 近义词：aggressive, hostile, warlike, combative | 反义词：peaceful, conciliatory, amicable | 词性: adjective

belligerent: /bəˈlɪdʒərənt/ | n. | 交战国，交战者 | A nation or person engaged in war or conflict. | 例句：The belligerents finally agreed to a ceasefire. 交战双方最终同意停火。| 近义词：combatant, adversary, opponent | 反义词：ally, friend | 词性: noun
