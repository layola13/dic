cancels: /ˈkænsəlz/ (US), /ˈkænslz/ (UK)| n.|取消，撤销（罕用作名词，通常用cancellation）|The act of officially ending or stopping something.|There were many cancels due to the bad weather. 由于恶劣天气，有很多取消。（这种用法不如 cancellations 常见）|synonyms: cancellations, revocations, terminations|antonyms: approvals, confirmations, continuations|noun
