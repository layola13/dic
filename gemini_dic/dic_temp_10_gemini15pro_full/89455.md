honestest:/ˈɑːnɪstɪst/ | adj. | 最诚实的 |  Although grammatically correct as a superlative form of "honest," "honestest" is rarely used in modern English. "Most honest" is generally preferred.  It emphasizes the highest degree of honesty. |  He was the honestest man I ever knew. 他是我认识的最诚实的人。| Synonym: most honest | Antonym: most dishonest | Word type: Adjective
