blancher:/ˈblɑːntʃər/ (US), /ˈblɑːnʃə/ (UK)| n.| 烫漂机，热烫机 |A device used to blanch food, typically vegetables, by briefly immersing them in boiling water or steam.|The chef used a blancher to prepare the green beans.|Synonyms: blanching machine|Antonyms: N/A|noun
blanch:/blɑːntʃ/ (US), /blɑːnʃ/ (UK)| v.| 焯水，烫漂;  (使)变白，(使)失色|To briefly immerse food, usually vegetables, in boiling water or steam, typically to inactivate enzymes, enhance color, or remove skins; to become pale or lose color.|She blanched the almonds before slicing them. 她把杏仁焯水后再切片。| He blanched at the sight of the blood. 看到血，他脸色变得苍白。 |Synonyms: parboil, scald, pale, whiten|Antonyms: darken, flush, redden|verb
