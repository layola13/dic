词源: From Middle English *carpen, from Old North French carp(er) (“to speak, chat, talk, prattle”), from Frankish *karpōn, from Proto-Germanic *karpōną (“to boast, lament”), from Proto-Indo-European *kerp- (“to cry out, lament”).

注意事项:  Carping often has a negative connotation and suggests excessive or unreasonable complaining.  It implies focusing on minor flaws while ignoring the overall good.
