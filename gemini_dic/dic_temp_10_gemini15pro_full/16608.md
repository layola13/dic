barometries:/ˌbærəˈmɛtriz/
n.
气压测定法；气压测定学
The study of barometric pressure and its variations, especially as an indicator of weather changes.
例句：The barometries of the region show a significant drop in pressure, indicating an approaching storm. 该地区的气压测定显示气压显着下降，表明暴风雨即将来临。

例句：His research in barometries led to a better understanding of weather patterns. 他在气压测定学方面的研究使人们对天气模式有了更好的理解。
