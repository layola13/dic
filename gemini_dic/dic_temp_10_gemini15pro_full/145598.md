overconfidence: /ˌoʊvərˈkɒnfɪdəns/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈkɒnfɪdəns/ (UK)| n.|过度自信；自负|Excessive confidence in oneself or one's abilities.|His overconfidence led him to make some risky investments. 他的过度自信导致他进行了一些冒险的投资。|近义词：arrogance, conceit, hubris|反义词:humility, modesty|noun

overconfident: /ˌoʊvərˈkɒnfɪdənt/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈkɒnfɪdənt/ (UK)| adj.|过度自信的；自负的|Having or showing too much confidence in oneself or one's abilities.|He was overconfident about his chances of winning the election. 他对赢得选举的机会过于自信。|近义词：arrogant, conceited, presumptuous|反义词:humble, modest, diffident|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + confidence.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Confidence" comes from the Latin "confidentia," meaning "trust, reliance, assurance."
