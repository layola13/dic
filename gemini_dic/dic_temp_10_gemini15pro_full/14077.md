australopithecus:/ˌɔːstreɪloʊˈpɪθɪkəs/ | n. |南方古猿|An extinct hominin genus that existed in Africa between four and two million years ago, considered an ancestor of humans. |  Australopithecus afarensis is one of the best-known species of early hominin.  南方古猿阿法种是最著名的早期人类物种之一。

Australopithecus played a crucial role in human evolution. 南方古猿在人类进化中扮演了至关重要的角色。

|近义词:hominin, hominid |反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a specific genus) |词性:noun
