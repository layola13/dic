mediatorial:/ˌmiːdiəˈtɔːriəl/
mediatorial|adj.|调解的，斡旋的|Relating to or characteristic of a mediator; acting as or involving mediation.|The UN played a mediatorial role in the conflict.|近义词: mediating, arbitrating, conciliatory, intermediary, intervening|反义词: antagonistic, non-interfering|词性:形容词
mediatorial:|/ˌmiːdiəˈtɔːriəl/|adj.|调解的，斡旋的|Involving or relating to the process of mediation, especially between disputing parties.|The ambassador's mediatorial efforts helped to prevent a war.|近义词: conciliatory, peacemaking, diplomatic|反义词: aggressive, confrontational|词性:形容词
