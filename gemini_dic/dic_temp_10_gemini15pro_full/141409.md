ophiomancy:/ˈɒfiəmænsi/ | n. | 蛇占卜；以蛇的行为预测吉凶 | Divination by observing snakes, especially the way they move. |  She practiced ophiomancy, believing she could predict the future by watching serpents. 她练习蛇占卜，相信通过观察蛇可以预测未来。 | Example: The ancient Greeks sometimes used ophiomancy to guide their decisions. 古希腊人有时用蛇占卜来指导他们的决策。 | Synonym: divination, augury | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "scientific prediction") | noun
