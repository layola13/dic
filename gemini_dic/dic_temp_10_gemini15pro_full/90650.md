humanly:/ˈhjuːmənli/| adv.|人性地，可能地，在人类能力范围内|Within the limits of human capability; in a way that is characteristic of humans. |例句：It's not humanly possible to work 24 hours a day.  一天工作24小时是不可能的。|近义词：naturally, possibly, conceivably|反义词:superhumanly, impossibly|词性:adverb
humanly:/ˈhjuːmənli/| adv.|从人的角度；同情地|In a human or sympathetic way. |例句：She treated the prisoners humanely. 她人道地对待囚犯。|近义词：compassionately, kindly, sympathetically|反义词:cruelly, inhumanely|词性:adverb
