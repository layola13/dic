credentialing:/krɪˈdɛnʃəlɪŋ/
n.
资格认证；资格审查
The process of verifying and evaluating the qualifications of a person or organization.
The hospital's credentialing process is very rigorous.  这家医院的资格认证程序非常严格。
certification, accreditation, licensing
n/a
noun
