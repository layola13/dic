calin:/kɑːˈlɪn/ | n. |拥抱，爱抚| An affectionate embrace or caress. | She gave her child a warm calin. 她给了她的孩子一个温暖的拥抱。| He received a comforting calin from his mother. 他得到了母亲安慰的拥抱。 | 近义词：hug, cuddle, embrace | 反义词: rejection, spurn | 词性: 名词


词源:  Borrowed from Romanian *călin ("dear, beloved; gentle, sweet"), ultimately from Latin *călīdus, from căleō ("to be warm").
