hatpin:/ˈhætˌpɪn/| n.|帽针；插在帽子上的装饰别针|A long pin used to secure a hat to the hair.|She used a hatpin to keep her hat from blowing away in the wind. 她用帽针防止帽子被风吹走。|近义词：brooch, pin|反义词: None|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hat + pin.  First recorded in the late 19th century.
