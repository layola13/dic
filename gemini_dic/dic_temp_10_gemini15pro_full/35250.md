clunk:/klʌŋk/| n. vi. |发出沉闷的撞击声；沉闷的撞击声|A dull, heavy sound, such as that made by a heavy object hitting something hard. |The car made a loud clunk as it went over the pothole. 汽车驶过坑洼时发出一声沉闷的撞击声。| synonyms: thud, bang, clank | antonyms: tinkle, chime | noun
clunk:/klʌŋk/| vi. |发出沉闷的撞击声|To make a dull, heavy sound. |The engine clunked and then stopped. 引擎发出一声沉闷的撞击声，然后停止了。| synonyms: thud, bang, clank | antonyms: tinkle, chime | verb
