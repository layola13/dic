board's:/bɔːrdz/
noun
董事会的；木板的；委员会的
Possessive form of "board," indicating ownership or association.  It refers to something that belongs to or is related to a board (of directors, of education, etc.) or a physical board.
The board's decision was final. 董事会的决定是最终的。
The ship's board's creaked under the weight. 船的甲板在重压下嘎吱作响。
synonyms: council's, committee's, panel's (for board of directors); plank's, timber's (for physical board)
antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a possessive form.  One could consider antonyms for the noun "board" itself, such as "individual," "member" in some contexts.)
