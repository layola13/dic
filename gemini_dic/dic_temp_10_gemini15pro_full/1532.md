acharon:/ˈɑːxɑːrɒn/| n.|末世，来世|Acharon refers to the afterlife or the world to come, particularly in Jewish mysticism. It is a concept of a future time of universal peace and spiritual perfection.|The righteous will experience the bliss of acharon after death.  义人死后将在来世体验极乐。

The Kabbalists describe acharon as a realm beyond human comprehension. 卡巴拉学者将来世描述为一个超越人类理解的境界。|近义词:afterlife, next world, world to come|反义词:this world, present life|词性:noun

From Hebrew  אחרון (’acharon), meaning "last," "final," or "end." This reflects the idea of acharon as the final stage of existence.
