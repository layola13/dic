armoire:/ɑːrˈmwɑːr (US), ɑːˈmwɑː (UK)| n.|衣橱，衣柜|A large wardrobe or movable cupboard, typically one that is ornate or antique.|例句：The antique armoire held her grandmother's wedding dress.  这个古董衣橱里放着她祖母的结婚礼服。

例句：They stored their linens in a beautiful carved armoire.  他们把他们的亚麻布存放在一个雕刻精美的衣橱里。|近义词：wardrobe, cupboard, closet|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *armoire*, from Old French *armaire*, from Latin *armarium* ("chest, closet for storing arms"), neuter of *armarius* ("of or belonging to arms"), from *arma* ("arms, weapons"). The meaning shifted from a place to store arms to a place to store clothes.
