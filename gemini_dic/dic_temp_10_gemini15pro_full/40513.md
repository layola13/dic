control /kənˈtroʊl/ (US) /kənˈtrəʊl/ (UK) | vt.| 控制；管理；抑制；克制 |  To exercise restraint or direction over; dominate or command. | She struggled to control her anger. 她努力控制自己的愤怒。|  manage, regulate, govern | lose, release | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "controle" (a duplicate register used to verify accounts), from Medieval Latin "contra-rotulus" ("counter-roll").
