dills:/dɪlz/| n.|莳萝; 莳萝泡菜; (傻瓜)|Aromatic annual herb of the parsley family, with feathery leaves and yellow flowers, the leaves and seeds of which are used in pickles and to flavour food. Also refers to a type of pickle, especially cucumber, flavored with dill.|She added some fresh dills to the salad. 她往沙拉里加了一些新鲜莳萝。|近义词：fennel(茴香), herbs(香草)|反义词:|noun


dills:/dɪlz/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|(Informal) A foolish or inept person.|He's such a dills, he locked his keys in the car again. 他真是个笨蛋，又把钥匙锁在车里了。|近义词：fool, idiot, dunce|反义词:genius, sage|noun
