convenance:/kənˈviːnəns/ /kənˈvenjəns/| n.|礼仪；习俗；社交礼节；（婚姻）便利；权宜婚姻|conformity to accepted standards of behaviour or morals, especially in social conduct; a marriage made for reasons other than love, such as money or social status|例句：She married him for convenience, not love. 她嫁给他只是为了便利，而不是因为爱情。

例句：The marriage was a matter of convenience rather than love. 这桩婚姻是出于权宜之计，而非爱情。


近义词：propriety, decorum, etiquette, suitability, expediency|反义词: impropriety, indecorum, inconvenience|词性:noun
