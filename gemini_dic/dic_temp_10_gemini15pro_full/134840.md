nonmobile:/ˌnɒnˈməʊbaɪl/
adjective
非移动的，固定的，不动的
Not able to move or be moved freely or easily.
The sculpture is a nonmobile work of art. 这座雕塑是一件不可移动的艺术品。
The patient is currently nonmobile due to a broken leg. 由于腿断了，病人目前无法移动。
近义词:immobile, fixed, stationary, static
反义词:mobile, movable, portable
