overflowable:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊəbl/
adjective
可溢出的，可泛滥的
able to overflow or be overflowed; too large or too abundant to be contained
The river was overflowable after the heavy rain.  这条河在大雨过后很容易泛滥。
Data was overflowable from the small drive.  数据从小驱动器中溢出。
近义词: overflowing, inundable, floodable
反义词: contained, limited, restricted
词性: adjective

overflow:/ˌoʊvərˈfloʊ/
verb
溢出；泛滥
(of a liquid) flow over the brim of a container.
The river overflowed its banks. 河水漫过了河岸。
The bathtub overflowed.  浴缸里的水溢出来了。
近义词: flood, inundate, brim over
反义词: drain, empty
词性: verb
词源: From over- + flow.

overflow:/ˈoʊvərˌfloʊ/
noun
溢出；容纳不下的人（或物）
an excess or superabundance.
An overflow of emotion.  情感的泛滥。
The overflow from the concert went to a nearby park.  音乐会容纳不下的人去了附近的公园。
近义词: excess, surplus, overabundance
反义词: shortage, deficit, lack
词性: noun
词源: From over- + flow.
