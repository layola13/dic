mongrelize: /ˈmɒŋɡrɪlaɪz/ (British) /ˈmɑːŋɡrɪlaɪz/ (American) | v. | 使杂交；使混杂；降低…的纯洁性 | To cause something to become mixed or impure, especially by interbreeding different breeds or mixing different elements. |  The breeder refused to mongrelize his prize-winning poodles.  这个饲养员拒绝让他的获奖贵宾犬杂交。

| Example: The influx of foreign words has mongrelized the language. 外来词的涌入使得这门语言变得混杂。 | Synonym:  hybridize, crossbreed, intermix, adulterate, contaminate, debase | Antonym: purify, refine, homogenize | Verb
