amethyst:/ˈæmɪθɪst/| n.|紫水晶；紫晶色|A transparent purple or violet variety of quartz, used as a gemstone.|She wore an amethyst pendant. 她戴着一个紫水晶吊坠。|近义词:violet, purple, lavender|反义词:(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑颜色上的对比色，例如yellow, orange)|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ametiste (Modern French améthyste), from Latin amethystus, from Ancient Greek ἀμέθυστος (améthustos, “not drunken”), from ἀ- (a-, “not”) + μεθύσκω (methúskō, “to intoxicate”).  The stone was believed to prevent intoxication.
