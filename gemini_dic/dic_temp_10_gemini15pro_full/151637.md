particularises:/pərˈtɪkjələrʌɪzɪz/
v.
详细说明；列举；具体说明
To mention or describe something in detail, giving specific examples or instances.
She particularised the steps involved in the complex procedure. 她详细说明了这个复杂程序的步骤。
The report particularised every expense incurred during the project.  该报告详细列出了项目期间发生的每一笔费用。

近义词:detail, specify, itemize, enumerate
反义词:generalize, summarize, abstract

词性:verb

词源: From the adjective "particular" + "-ise" (suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or render"). "Particular" comes from Late Latin particularis "of or belonging to a part, private, special," from pars "part."
