non-heinous:/nɒnˈheɪnəs/ | adj. | 非十恶不赦的，非极凶的 | Not heinous; not grossly wicked or reprehensible. |  The judge determined that the crime, while serious, was non-heinous and did not warrant the death penalty. 法官判定该罪行虽然严重，但并非十恶不赦，不应判处死刑。

| Example: Although he committed a crime, it was a non-heinous one and he was given a lenient sentence. 虽然他犯了罪，但罪行并非十恶不赦，因此他得到了轻判。| Near Synonyms: minor, petty, venial, unimportant, insignificant | Antonyms: heinous, atrocious, monstrous, egregious, abhorrent | Adjective
