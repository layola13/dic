outcourts:/ˈaʊtkɔːrts/
n.
外场；室外球场
Tennis outcourts are usually less prestigious than the main show courts.  网球外场通常不如主要的表演场地有声望。
The outcourts were packed with spectators watching the qualifying matches.  外场挤满了观看资格赛的观众。
synonyms: outer courts, outside courts
antonyms: center court, main court
