benevolence:/bəˈnevələns/| n.|仁慈，善意，善行|The king was known for his benevolence and generosity. 国王以仁慈和慷慨闻名。|例句：He treated his employees with benevolence and understanding. 他对员工仁慈而富有理解。

His benevolence extended to all creatures, great and small. 他的仁慈惠及所有生物，无论大小。|近义词：kindness, generosity, charity, compassion, goodwill|反义词：malevolence, cruelty, hostility, unkindness|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin benevolentia, from bene "well" + volens "willing".
