lophophore:/ˈlɒfəfɔːr/ (UK) /ˈloʊfəfɔːr/ (US)| n. | 触手冠 |A ring of ciliated tentacles encircling the mouth of certain small marine invertebrates, used for filtering food from the water.|Bryozoans possess a lophophore, a crown of tentacles used for feeding.  苔藓虫拥有一个触手冠，用于摄食。|cilia, tentacles|None|noun
