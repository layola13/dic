engarboil:/ɪnˈɡɑːrbɔɪl/
v.
to throw into confusion or disorder; to entangle; to muddle; to jumble
To involve in trouble or disorder; to complicate.
He engarboiled the plot of the story with unnecessary subplots. 他用不必要的次要情节把故事的情节弄得一团糟。
Synonyms: confuse, muddle, jumble, entangle, complicate, embroil
Antonyms: clarify, simplify, organize, disentangle, resolve
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From en- + garboil, from Old French garbouil "confusion, tumult."
