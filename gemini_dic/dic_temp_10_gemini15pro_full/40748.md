conveys:/kənˈveɪz/| v. |传达，表达，传递|communicates or makes known; transports or carries something from one place to another|Her smile conveyed her happiness. 她的笑容表达了她的快乐。 The train conveys passengers from London to Paris. 火车把乘客从伦敦送到巴黎。|近义词：communicates, transmits, transports|反义词：withholds, obscures, receives|词性:动词

词源: From Old French conveier, from Latin conviare "to accompany," from com- "together" + via "way, road."
