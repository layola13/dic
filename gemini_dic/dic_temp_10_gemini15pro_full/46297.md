d-day:/ˈdiː deɪ/
noun
决战日；关键时刻
The day of a planned military operation, especially the day on which Allied forces landed in Normandy in World War II.
The D-Day invasion was a turning point in World War II.  诺曼底登陆是二战的转折点。
