chiral:/ˈkaɪrəl/| adj.| 手性的，不对称的|A chiral molecule is a type of molecule that has a non-superimposable mirror image, like your left and right hands.|Many biological molecules, such as amino acids, are chiral.|近义词:asymmetric, dissymmetric|反义词:achiral|词性:形容词

chiral:/ˈkaɪrəl/| n.| 手性分子，手性物质| A chiral substance is one that exists as two or more stereoisomers that are mirror images of each other but cannot be superimposed.|Chiral molecules are important in pharmaceuticals.|近义词:enantiomer, stereoisomer|反义词: racemate|词性:名词 (较少使用)


ETYMOLOGY->
