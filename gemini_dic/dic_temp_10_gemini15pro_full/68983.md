fatigued:/fəˈtiːɡd/| adj.|疲倦的，疲劳的| feeling tired, weary, or exhausted, especially due to physical or mental exertion.|  I was fatigued after the long journey. 我长途旅行后感到疲倦。|  She felt fatigued after working for hours without a break. 她连续工作几个小时没有休息，感到很疲惫。| 近义词: tired, weary, exhausted, drained, worn out | 反义词: energetic, refreshed, invigorated |词性:形容词

词源:
From French *fatigué*, past participle of *fatiguer* "to tire," from Late Latin *fatigare* "to weary," from Latin *fatigatus*, past participle of *fatigare*, of unknown origin.
