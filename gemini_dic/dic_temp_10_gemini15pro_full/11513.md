arietta:/ˌærɪˈɛtə/ | n. |小咏叹调，短抒情调|A short song, typically one that is light and graceful, similar to an aria but usually shorter and less elaborate. | She sang a charming arietta by Mozart. 她演唱了一首莫扎特迷人的小咏叹调。| The composer included several a charming ariettas in his opera.  这位作曲家在他的歌剧中加入了几首迷人的小咏叹调。| synonym: song, melody, air, tune | antonym: symphony, concerto | noun
