outflashes:/aʊtˈflæʃɪz/
verb
突然闪耀; 闪耀更胜过
shines more brightly than; emits a sudden flash of light surpassing another
The lightning outflashed the city lights. 闪电比城市灯光还要耀眼。
Synonyms: outshines, surpasses, eclipses
Antonyms: dims, pales, fades
词性: 动词
