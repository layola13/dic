passionary:/ˈpæʃəˌneri/ | n. | 激情澎湃的人；煽动者 | A person who speaks or writes with great fervor and excitement, especially on political or religious topics; an agitator. |  He was a true passionary, stirring the crowd with his fiery rhetoric. 他是一位真正的煽动者，用他激烈的言辞煽动着人群。 |  zealot, fanatic, firebrand, demagogue |  moderate, pacifist | noun

Etymology->
From Late Latin *passionarius, from Latin passio (“suffering, passion”).  Influenced by French passionné (“passionate”).
