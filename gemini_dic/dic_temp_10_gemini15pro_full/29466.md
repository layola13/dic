catamaran:/ˌkætəməˈræn/ | n. | 双体船，帆船 | A boat with two parallel hulls. | We sailed around the bay on a catamaran. 我们乘双体船绕着海湾航行。| The catamaran skimmed across the waves. 双体船掠过波浪。 | 近义词：twin-hulled boat, outrigger canoe | 反义词: monohull | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Tamil கட்டுமரம் (kaṭṭumaram, “tied wood, raft”), from கட்டு (kaṭṭu, “to tie, bind”) + மரம் (maram, “tree, wood”).
