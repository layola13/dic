lowbrow:/ˈloʊbraʊ/| n. adj.|低俗的人；低级趣味的|A person who has little or no intellectual interest or taste.|The movie was criticized for its lowbrow humor. 这部电影因其低俗的幽默而受到批评。|例句：The comedian's lowbrow jokes offended many in the audience.  这位喜剧演员低俗的笑话冒犯了在场的许多人。

例句：The museum curator dismissed the exhibit as lowbrow and tasteless.  博物馆馆长认为这次展览低俗无味，不屑一顾。|近义词：uncultured, philistine, vulgar, tasteless|反义词：highbrow, sophisticated, refined, cultured|词性:名词，形容词
