anticommercialistic:/ˌænti kəˈmɜːrʃəlˈɪstɪk/
adjective
反商业主义的，反对商业化的
Opposed to commercialism; against the emphasis on maximizing profit or promoting products or services, often at the expense of quality, artistic integrity, or other values.
Her art is deliberately anticommercialistic, focusing on personal expression rather than market appeal. 她刻意地进行反商业主义的艺术创作，专注于个人表达而不是市场吸引力。

The band's anticommercialistic stance meant they refused to license their music for advertising.  这支乐队的反商业主义立场意味着他们拒绝将他们的音乐授权用于广告。

近义词: noncommercial, uncommercial, antimarket
反义词: commercial, pro-business, mercantile
词性: adjective
