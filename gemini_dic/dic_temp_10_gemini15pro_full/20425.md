bitmapping:/ˈbɪtmæpɪŋ/ | n. | 位图，位图法 | The process of representing an image or display as a matrix of individual bits, each corresponding to a single pixel. | Example 1: Bitmap images are made up of pixels. 位图图像由像素组成。

Example 2: Bitmapping is essential for displaying graphics on a screen. 位图对于在屏幕上显示图形至关重要。 | synonyms: rasterization, pixelation | antonyms: vectorization | noun
