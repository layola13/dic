outlining:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪnɪŋ/
n.|概述；略述；描绘轮廓|The act of creating a general sketch or summary of something, highlighting the main points.|He began by outlining the project's goals. 他首先概述了项目的目标。|summarizing, sketching, drafting|detailing, elaborating|noun


outlining:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪnɪŋ/
v.|概述；略述；描绘轮廓|To give a summary of the main points of something; to describe something in a general way.|She outlined the company's history in her presentation. 她在演讲中概述了公司的历史。|summarizing, sketching, drafting|detailing, elaborating|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "outline," which comes from "out-" (meaning "outside" or "beyond") and "line" (referring to a boundary or contour).  The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb into a gerund or present participle.
