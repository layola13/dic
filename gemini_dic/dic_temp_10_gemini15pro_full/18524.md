benefactor:/ˈbenɪfæktər/ | n. | 恩人，捐助者 | A person who gives money or other help to a person or cause. |  Example 1: The university's new library was funded by a generous benefactor. 这所大学的新图书馆是由一位慷慨的捐助者资助的。

Example 2:  He was a great benefactor to the arts. 他是艺术的伟大赞助人。 | Synonyms: patron, donor, sponsor | Antonyms: malefactor, villain | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *benefactor*, from *bene* ("well") + *factor* ("maker, doer")
