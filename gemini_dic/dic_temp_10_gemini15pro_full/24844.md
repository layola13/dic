brownie:/ˈbraʊni/| n.|布朗尼蛋糕；女童子军；小精灵|A small square of rich, chocolate cake.|例句：I baked a batch of brownies for the party. 我为聚会烤了一批布朗尼蛋糕。|例句：She joined the Brownies when she was seven. 她七岁时加入了女童子军。|近义词：chocolate cake, brownie points (比喻用法)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


brownie:/ˈbraʊni/| n.|深棕色；棕色人种|A dark brown color.|例句：She has brownie eyes. 她有一双棕色的眼睛。|例句：He prefers brownie shoes to black ones. 他喜欢棕色的鞋子胜过黑色的。|近义词：brown, brunette|反义词:blonde, fair|词性:noun/adjective (rarely used as adjective)



ETYMOLOGY->
From brown + -ie (diminutive suffix). The cake is named after its color.  The mythical creature sense is of uncertain origin. The Girl Scout sense comes from the color of their uniforms.
