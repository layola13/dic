heliogram:/ˈhiːliəɡræm/ | n. |日光信号，日光电报；日射强度记录|A message transmitted by reflecting sunlight with a mirror, specifically a heliograph.  A record of the intensity of solar radiation. |例句：The heliogram flashed a message across the valley. 日光信号在山谷间闪烁传递信息。

例句：The scientists studied the heliogram to understand solar activity. 科学家们研究日射强度记录以了解太阳活动。|近义词：signal, message,  heliograph communication |反义词: None |词性: noun
