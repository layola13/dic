overtender:/ˌoʊvərˈtɛndər/ | adj. | 过嫩的，过于柔软的 | Cooked for too long or at too high a temperature, resulting in a soft or mushy texture.  Relates primarily to food, especially meat. | The steak was overtender and lacked texture. 牛排太嫩了，缺乏嚼劲。| The vegetables were overtender from being overcooked. 蔬菜煮过头了，太软烂了。 | 近义词: mushy, soft, overcooked | 反义词: tough, firm, undercooked | 词性: adjective | 词源: "over-" (prefix meaning excessively) + "tender" (adjective meaning soft or easy to cut)
