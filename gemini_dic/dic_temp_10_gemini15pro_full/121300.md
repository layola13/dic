misfitted:/ˌmɪsˈfɪtɪd/| adj.|不合适的，格格不入的，不适应的|Not fitting properly or failing to adjust to a situation or environment.|  The misfitted coat made him look awkward. 这件不合身的衣服让他看起来很笨拙。|Synonyms: maladjusted, unsuitable, incompatible, out of place|Antonyms: well-adjusted, suitable, compatible, fitting, appropriate|adjective

misfit:/ˈmɪsfɪt/| n.|不适应环境的人，格格不入的人|A person who is not suited to their environment or circumstances, or who is considered strange or unusual by other people.| He was a social misfit in high school. 他在高中时是一个不合群的人。|Synonyms: nonconformist, eccentric, maverick, outcast|Antonyms: conformist, insider|noun
