interdeceived:/ˌɪntərdiˈsiːvd/
verb
相互欺骗
To deceive each other; to be involved in a situation of mutual deception.
The two spies interdeceived each other, each believing they had the upper hand. 两个间谍互相欺骗，每个人都认为自己占了上风。
The warring factions interdeceived the public about their true intentions. 交战各派互相欺骗公众，掩盖他们的真实意图。
Synonyms: mislead each other, delude each other, double-cross each other
Antonyms: be honest with each other, trust each other
