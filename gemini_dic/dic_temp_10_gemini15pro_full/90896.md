hunter-gatherer:/ˈhʌntər ˈɡæðərər/ | n. |狩猎采集者| A member of a nomadic people who live chiefly by hunting, fishing, and harvesting wild food.|  Early humans were primarily hunter-gatherers. 早期人类主要是狩猎采集者。|  nomadic forager,  | farmer, settler | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
"hunter" comes from Old English hunta "hunter," agent noun from hunt "to hunt."
"gatherer" comes from Old English gædrian "to gather, assemble, collect."
