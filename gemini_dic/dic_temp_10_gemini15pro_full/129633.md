non-contributory:/ˌnɒn kənˈtrɪbjʊtəri/ (US) /ˌnɑːn kənˈtrɪbjətɔːri/| adj.|非缴费的；非供款的；无偿的|Not requiring a contribution of money or other resources from the person or entity receiving a benefit or service.  Relating to a pension or other benefit scheme where the employee does not make regular contributions.|例句：A non-contributory pension scheme is funded entirely by the employer. 非缴费型养老金计划完全由雇主出资。|例句：He receives a non-contributory disability benefit. 他领取的是非缴费型残疾补助。|近义词：free, gratuitous, unearned|反义词：contributory|词性:adjective
