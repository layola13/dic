dispersable:/dɪˈspɜːrsəbl/ /dɪˈspəːrsəbl/| adj.|可分散的，可分散的|Capable of being dispersed or scattered.  Something that can be spread out or distributed over a wide area.|The powder is easily dispersable in water. 这种粉末很容易分散在水中。|Synonyms: diffusible, scatterable, spreadable, distributable|Antonyms:  cohesive, concentrated,  clumped|adjective


disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/ /dɪˈspəːrs/| v.|分散，驱散|To scatter or distribute over a wide area.|The police dispersed the crowd. 警察驱散了人群。|Synonyms: scatter, disseminate, distribute|Antonyms: gather, collect, assemble|verb
