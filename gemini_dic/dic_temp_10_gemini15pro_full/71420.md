flak-vests:/ˈflæk vɛsts/| n. |防弹背心|A protective vest, usually made of layers of strong fabric or plates, designed to stop or deflect bullets or other projectiles. |The soldiers wore flak-vests for protection during the patrol. 士兵们在巡逻时穿着防弹背心以获得保护。|The police officers donned their flak-vests before entering the building.  警察进入大楼前穿上了防弹背心。|Synonyms: bulletproof vest, body armor|Antonyms: None|Noun
