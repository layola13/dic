leopard: /ˈlɛpərd/ (US) /ˈlɛpəd/ (UK)| n. |豹|A large, solitary cat with a yellowish-brown coat marked with black rosettes, native to Africa and Asia.|The leopard stalked its prey through the tall grass. 豹子在高高的草丛中跟踪猎物。|近义词：panther, jaguar|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑prey猎物)|noun
