gnathopodite:/ˈnæθəpəˌdaɪt/| n. |颚足|A paired appendage located near the mouth of an arthropod, often modified for feeding.  It is typically one of several pairs of appendages that make up the mouthparts.|The crayfish uses its gnathopodites to grasp and manipulate food.|Synonyms: maxilliped, mouthpart|Antonyms: None|Noun
