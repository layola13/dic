nonstudiousnesses:/ˌnɒnˌstjuːdiəsnəsɪz/
noun
不勤奋，不爱学习
lack of studiousness; the characteristic of not being studious.
His nonstudiousness resulted in failing grades. 他的不勤奋导致成绩不及格。
Synonyms: indolence, laziness, apathy
Antonyms: studiousness, diligence, assiduity
n.
