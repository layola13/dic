hydrometeorology:/ˌhaɪdrəˌmiːtiəˈrɒlədʒi/ | n. |水文气象学|The study of the atmospheric processes that affect the Earth's water resources, especially precipitation, its distribution, and its effects on the environment. |Hydrometeorology plays a crucial role in flood forecasting. 水文气象学在洪水预报中起着至关重要的作用。| Hydrometeorology is essential for understanding drought patterns.  水文气象学对于理解干旱模式至关重要。 | 近义词: hydroclimatology | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: "hydro-" (from Greek "hydōr," meaning "water") + "meteor-" (from Greek "meteōron," meaning "thing high up," referring to atmospheric phenomena) + "-ology" (from Greek "-logia," meaning "study of").
