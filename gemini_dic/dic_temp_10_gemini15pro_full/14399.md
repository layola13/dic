autosuggestion:/ˌɔːtəʊsəˈdʒɛstʃən/| n.|自我暗示|The process by which a person induces a particular mental or physical state by repeatedly suggesting to themselves that it is happening or will happen.|He used autosuggestion to overcome his fear of public speaking. 他用自我暗示来克服对公开演讲的恐惧。|近义词：self-hypnosis, autohypnosis|反义词: N/A|noun
