lucency:/ˈluːsənsi/ | n. |透明度；透光性；（X光片）透亮区|The lucency of the glass allows ample light to enter the room. 玻璃的透明度使充足的光线进入房间。|例句：The X-ray showed a lucency in the lung. X光片显示肺部有一个透亮区。

例句：The lucency of the diamond added to its brilliance.  钻石的透明度增加了它的光彩。|近义词：transparency, clarity, translucence |反义词：opacity, density |词性:名词

词源:  From Latin "lucens" (shining, bright) + "-y" (suffix forming nouns of quality or state)
