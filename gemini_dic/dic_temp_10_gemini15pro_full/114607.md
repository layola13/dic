malefaction:/ˌmælɪˈfækʃən/ | n. | 罪行，恶行，坏事 | An act of wrongdoing, especially one that is illegal or morally wrong.  | The mayor was accused of malefaction in office. 市长被指控在职期间有不法行为。 |  The malefaction of the CEO cost the company millions. 首席执行官的渎职行为使公司损失了数百万美元。| 近义词: wrongdoing, misdeed, offense, crime, sin | 反义词: benefaction, good deed | 词性: noun

malefactor:/ˈmælɪˌfæktər/ | n. | 罪犯，作恶者 | A person who commits a crime or some other wrong. | The malefactors were apprehended by the police. 罪犯被警方逮捕了。 | The police are still searching for the malefactors. 警方仍在搜捕罪犯。 | 近义词: criminal, culprit, offender, villain | 反义词: benefactor | 词性: noun
