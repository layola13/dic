disunites:/ˌdɪsjuˈnaɪts/
verb
使分裂，使不团结
to cause (people or things) to be no longer united
The issue of immigration disunited the party. 移民问题使该党分裂。
近义词: divides, separates, alienates, fragments
反义词: unites, joins, connects, integrates
词性:v.
