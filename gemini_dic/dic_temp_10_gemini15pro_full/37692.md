companionably:/kəmˈpænjənəbli/ | adv. | 友好地；亲切地；和睦地 | In a friendly and sociable manner, enjoying each other's company. | They chatted companionably for hours. 他们友好地聊了几个小时。| 近义词: amicably, sociably, agreeably, genially | 反义词: unsociably, inhospitably, hostilely | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From companionable + -ly.  Companion ultimately derives from Late Latin *compāniō, from Latin com- 'with' + pānis 'bread'.  Thus, a companion originally meant someone with whom one shares bread.
