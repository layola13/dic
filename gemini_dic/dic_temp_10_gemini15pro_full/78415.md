get lost:/ɡet lɒst/ (UK) /ɡet lɑːst/ (US)| phrasal verb | 迷路；走开；滚开 | To become lost or disoriented; to go away, often as a rude dismissal. | Example: I got lost in the woods. 我在树林里迷路了。

Example: "Get lost!" he shouted angrily. “滚开！”他生气地喊道。 | 近义词: go away, scram, beat it, clear off | 反义词: stay, come here | 词性: 动词短语
