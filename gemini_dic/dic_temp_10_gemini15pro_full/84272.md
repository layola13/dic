half-taught:/ˌhæfˈtɔːt/ | adj. | 半吊子；未受过良好教育的；粗通的 |  Lacking adequate or complete instruction or training.  Derived from "half" (meaning partially) and "taught" (past participle of "teach").  Implies a superficial understanding or skill level. | He was a half-taught mechanic and often made mistakes. 他是个半吊子机械师，经常犯错误。 |  semi-skilled, poorly trained, inexperienced | well-educated, skilled, expert | adjective
