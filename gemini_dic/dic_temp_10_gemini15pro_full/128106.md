nesting:/ˈnɛstɪŋ/| n.|筑巢，嵌套|The process of building a nest, or fitting one part inside another.|Birds are nesting in the eaves of the house. 鸟儿在屋檐下筑巢。|近义词: incubation, breeding|反义词: abandoning|noun


nesting:/ˈnɛstɪŋ/| adj.|嵌套的|Arranged in a series of successively smaller or enclosed structures.|The dolls were nesting, one inside the other. 这些娃娃是嵌套的，一个套一个。|近义词: embedded, layered|反义词: separate|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Old English word "nest," meaning "nest."  Ultimately from the Proto-Germanic *nistaz, from the Proto-Indo-European root *nizdo-, meaning "to sit down, settle."
