outloved:/ˌaʊtˈlʌvd/
verb
比...活得更长，爱得更久
To have loved longer than someone else, typically referring to surviving a loved one.
例句：She outloved her husband by ten years. 她比她丈夫多活了十年。

例句：The old oak tree had outloved generations of families who had picnicked beneath its branches.  这棵老橡树比在它枝下野餐的几代家庭都活得更久。

近义词：outlive, survive
反义词：predecease
词性：verb
