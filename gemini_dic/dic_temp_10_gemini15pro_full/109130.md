legionnaire:/ˌliːdʒəˈnɛər/| n.|军团士兵，退伍军人|A member of a legion, especially of the French Foreign Legion.|例句：He was a proud legionnaire who had served for many years. 他是一位自豪的军团士兵，服役多年。

例句：The legionnaires marched tirelessly through the desert. 军团士兵不知疲倦地在沙漠中行军。


|近义词：soldier, trooper, veteran|反义词:civilian|Noun
