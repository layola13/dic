outsteamed:/ˌaʊtˈstiːmd/
verb
以蒸汽胜过；在蒸汽方面超过；蒸汽更多
surpassed or defeated by producing more steam or using steam more effectively
The old locomotive was outsteamed by the new, more powerful engine.  旧机车被新的、更强大的发动机在蒸汽方面超过了。
The competitor's ship was outsteamed and left behind.  竞争对手的船在蒸汽方面被超越，落在了后面。
近义词:outperformed, surpassed, defeated, outmatched
反义词:outdone, beaten

词性:v.

词源:  "out-" (prefix indicating surpassing or exceeding) + "steam" (verb meaning to produce or use steam)
