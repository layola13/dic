digitule:/ˈdɪdʒɪˌtjuːl/| n. |趾突；爪垫；|A small, usually hollow, protuberance or appendage on the foot of an insect or arachnid, often secreting a sticky fluid.| Aphids often have prominent digitules. 蚜虫通常有明显的趾突。|Near synonyms: adhesive pad, arolium|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "claw" in some contexts, indicating a different type of appendage)|Noun
