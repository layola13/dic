impatient:/ɪmˈpeɪʃnt/| adj.|不耐烦的，急躁的|Not patient; easily annoyed by someone's mistakes or because you have to wait.|例句：He's very impatient with his children. 他对他的孩子们很不耐烦。|例句：I get impatient when people are slow. 当人们慢吞吞的时候，我会不耐烦。|近义词：restless, irritable, fidgety|反义词：patient, tolerant, calm|词性:形容词
impatience:/ɪmˈpeɪʃəns/| n.|不耐烦，急躁|The tendency to be impatient; lack of patience.|例句：His impatience with the slow progress was obvious. 他对进展缓慢明显很不耐烦。|例句：She tapped her foot with impatience. 她不耐烦地跺着脚。|近义词：irritability, restlessness|反义词：patience, tolerance|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + patient (suffering or enduring without complaint).  Ultimately from Latin *patientem* (suffering, enduring).
