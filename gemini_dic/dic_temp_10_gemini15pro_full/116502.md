maternalism:/məˈtɜːrnəlɪzəm/ | n. | 母性主义，母性保护 | The principle or practice of maternal protection and care, especially by the state or other institutions.  It often emphasizes the importance of women's role in society as mothers and caregivers. |  The social welfare programs of the 1960s were seen by some as an example of maternalism.  20世纪60年代的社会福利计划被一些人视为母性主义的例子。| 近义词: motherliness, motherhood | 反义词: paternalism | 词性: noun
