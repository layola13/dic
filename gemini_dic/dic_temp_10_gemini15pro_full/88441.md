hissers:/ˈhɪsɚz/
n.
发出嘶嘶声的人或物
People or things that make a hissing sound
The snake raised its head and the hissers warned us away. 那条蛇抬起头，嘶嘶声警告我们离开。
sibilants, whisperers
roarers, shouters
