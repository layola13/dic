connivance:/kəˈnaɪvəns/| n. |默许；纵容；共谋；串通|Secret or tacit consent to wrongdoing; collusion.|The crime was committed with the connivance of the guards. 这起犯罪是在警卫的纵容下犯的。|Synonyms: collusion, complicity, conspiracy, collaboration|Antonyms: opposition, resistance, obstruction|noun

connivances:/kəˈnaɪvənsɪz/| n. |默许；纵容；共谋；串通（connivance的复数形式）|Plural form of connivance, referring to multiple instances of secret or tacit consent to wrongdoing.|The series of robberies were made possible through the connivances of several bank employees. 这一系列抢劫案是通过几名银行雇员的串通才得以实现的。|Synonyms: collusions, complicacies, conspiracies, collaborations|Antonyms: oppositions, resistances, obstructions|noun
