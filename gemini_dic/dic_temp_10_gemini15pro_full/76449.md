gainsay:/ˌɡeɪnˈseɪ/ /ˌɡeɪnˈseɪ/| v. |否认；反驳；否定|To deny or contradict; to dispute or oppose.|例句：No one could gainsay the fact that she was intelligent. 没有人能否认她很聪明。|例句：He attempted to gainsay the evidence, but it was overwhelming. 他试图反驳证据，但证据确凿。|近义词：deny, dispute, contradict, controvert|反义词：confirm, acknowledge, admit, concede|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *gēansecgan (“to speak against, contradict”), from gēan (“against”) + secgan (“to say”).  Cognate with Old Norse gegnisögn ("contradiction").
