multisect:/ˈmʌltiˌsɛkt/ | v. | 多处切割，多方面划分 | To divide into many sections or parts. |  The surgeon multisected the tumor for removal.  外科医生将肿瘤多处切割以便移除。

The city council proposed to multisect the park into different zones. 市议会提议将公园划分成不同的区域。 | 近义词: subdivide, partition, dissect | 反义词: unify, combine | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "multi-" (many) + "sect" (cut, section).  "Multi-" comes from Latin "multus" (much, many). "Sect" comes from Latin "sectus," past participle of "secare" (to cut).
