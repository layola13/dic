athermous:/ˌeɪˈθɜːrməs/| adj.|不透热的，隔热的|Not transmitting heat; impervious to radiant heat.  Specifically designed to prevent the passage of heat, especially radiant heat.|The athermous fabric protected the workers from the intense heat of the furnace. 这种不透热的织物保护工人免受熔炉强烈的热量。|Near synonyms: heat-resistant, heat-insulating, non-conducting, adiabatic|Near antonyms: thermally conductive, diathermanous|adjective
