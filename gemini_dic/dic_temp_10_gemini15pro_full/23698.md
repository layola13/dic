brasilin:/brəˈzɪlɪn/
noun
巴西木素
A reddish crystalline compound, C₁₆H₁₄O₅, extracted from brazilwood and used as a dye.
The craftsman used brasilin to stain the wood a deep red. 工匠用巴西木素将木头染成深红色。
synonyms: brazilin, Natural Red 24
antonyms: None
n.
