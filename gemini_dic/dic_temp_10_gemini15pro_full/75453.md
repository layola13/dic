fruitlessness:/ˈfruːtləsnəs/
n.
徒劳，无果，无效
The fruitlessness of their efforts was discouraging. 他们的努力毫无结果，令人沮丧。
The fruitlessness of the negotiations led to war.  谈判的无果而终导致了战争。
futility, inefficacy, unproductiveness
effectiveness, productivity, success
noun
