oss:/ɒs/ | n. | 开源软件 (Open Source Software) | Open Source Software refers to software whose source code is made available for anyone to inspect, modify, and enhance. | Example: Many popular web servers run on oss. 很多流行的网络服务器都运行在开源软件上。| Example:  The oss community is very active and supportive. 开源软件社区非常活跃，而且互相支持。 | Synonym: open-source | Antonym: proprietary | Noun
