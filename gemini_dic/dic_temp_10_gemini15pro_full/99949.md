internuncio:/ˌɪntərˈnʌnsioʊ/
noun
教廷大使，教皇使节
A papal legate of lower rank than a nuncio, permanently accredited to the government of a smaller nation.
The internuncio delivered a message from the Pope. 教廷大使传达了教皇的讯息。
Synonyms: legate, envoy, emissary, ambassador, representative
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, consider words like "rebel," "dissident" in specific contexts)
