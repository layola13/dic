cobia:/ˈkoʊbiə/ (US) /ˈkəʊbiə/ (UK)| n.|军曹鱼，黑鲪|A marine fish (Rachycentron canadum) found worldwide in warm waters, valued as a food and game fish.  It is the only species in the family Rachycentridae.|例句：The cobia is a fast-growing fish that can reach a considerable size. 军曹鱼是一种生长迅速的鱼，可以长到相当大的尺寸。

例句：We caught a large cobia while fishing offshore. 我们在近海捕鱼时捕获了一条大型军曹鱼。|近义词：black kingfish, prodigal son, crabeater, black salmon (regional names)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
