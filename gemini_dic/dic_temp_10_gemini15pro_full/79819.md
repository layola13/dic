glyconic:/ɡlaɪˈkɒnɪk/ | adj. | 葡糖酸的; 葡糖酸的 | Relating to or derived from gluconic acid. | Gluconic acid is readily produced by the oxidation of glucose. 葡糖酸很容易由葡萄糖氧化制得。 |  Gluconic acid is an important ingredient in many cleaning products due to its chelating properties. 葡糖酸因其螯合性是许多清洁产品中的重要成分。 | Synonyms:  gluconic | Antonyms: N/A | adjective
