nonpercussive:/ˌnɑːnpərˈkʌsɪv/ | adj. |非打击的，非敲击的 |Not involving percussion; not percussive.  Relating to musical instruments that are not played by striking, such as wind or string instruments. |The flute is a nonpercussive instrument. 长笛是一种非打击乐器。| The orchestra featured a beautiful interplay between the percussive and nonpercussive sections.  管弦乐队以打击乐和非打击乐部分之间美丽的相互作用为特色。 |近义词：melodic, harmonious |反义词：percussive, rhythmic |词性：形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
percussive (adjective): from Latin percuss- "struck," past participle stem of percutere "to strike violently," from per- "thoroughly" + quatere "to shake."
