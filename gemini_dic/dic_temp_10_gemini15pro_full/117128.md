meander:/miˈændər/ | v. | 漫步；蜿蜒而行；迂回曲折 | To wander aimlessly or casually; to follow a winding course. | The path meanders through the woods. 小路蜿蜒穿过树林。| synonyms: wander, ramble, stroll, roam, drift | antonyms: hurry, rush | verb
