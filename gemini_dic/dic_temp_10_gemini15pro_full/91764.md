hyperemotivities:/ˌhaɪpərɪˌmoʊtɪˈvɪtiz/

A tendency to experience and express emotions with unusual intensity.|Her hyperemotivities made it difficult for her to handle even minor stressors.|Synonym: hypersensitivity, oversensitivity|Antonym: apathy, indifference|noun
