misgauge:/ˌmɪsˈɡeɪdʒ/
verb
错误估计；判断失误
to estimate or judge something wrongly
He misgauged the strength of the wind and capsized his boat. 他错误估计了风力，导致他的船翻了。
The company misgauged the public's reaction to the new product. 该公司错误估计了公众对新产品的反应。
miscalculate, misjudge, underestimate, overestimate
correctly gauge, accurately assess
verb
