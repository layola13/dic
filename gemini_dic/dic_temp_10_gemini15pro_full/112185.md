loom:/luːm/
word property:verb
中文翻译: 隐约出现；逼近
英文解析: (of something large, threatening, or impressive) appear or become visible in a way that suggests something bad might happen.
例句：
A dark shape loomed out of the fog.
一个黑色的身影从雾中隐约出现。

The threat of war loomed large.
战争的威胁逼近。

近义词：emerge, appear, rise
反义词: recede, disappear
词性: verb


loom:/luːm/
word property:noun
中文翻译: 织布机
英文解析: a machine for weaving cloth.
例句：
She sat at her loom, weaving a tapestry.
她坐在织布机旁，织着一块挂毯。

The rhythmic clatter of the loom filled the room.
织布机有节奏的咔嗒声充满了整个房间。

近义词：weaving machine
反义词: None
词性: noun
