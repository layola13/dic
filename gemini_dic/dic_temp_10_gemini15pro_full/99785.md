interment:/ɪnˈtɜːrmənt/ (US) /ɪnˈtɜːmənt/ (UK)| n. |埋葬，安葬|The act of burying a dead person, especially in a grave or tomb.  The interment will take place on Tuesday.  安葬仪式将于周二举行。|例句：

The interment of the king was a grand ceremony. 国王的葬礼是一场盛大的仪式。

His interment was attended by family and close friends.  他的葬礼由家人和密友参加。

|近义词：burial, sepulture, inhumation|反义词：exhumation|词性: 名词
