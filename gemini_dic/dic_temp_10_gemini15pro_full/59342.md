词源:
From "earliness" + possessive suffix "-'s".  "Earliness" comes from "early" + "-ness". "Early" comes from Old English *ærlice (adverb) and *ærlic (adjective), related to Old Frisian ērlik "early," Old Saxon arlik, Old High German arlih. The "-ness" suffix is used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
