homoeopaths:/ˌhəʊmiˈɒpæθs/ | n. |顺势疗法医师|A practitioner of homeopathy, a system of alternative medicine based on the principle of "like cures like". |Many homoeopaths believe that highly diluted substances can stimulate the body's healing processes. 许多顺势疗法医师认为高度稀释的物质可以刺激人体的自愈过程。|Synonyms: homeopathists|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider) conventional doctors, allopathic physicians| Noun
