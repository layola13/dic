flannel:/ˈflæn(ə)l/| n. adj.|法兰绒，法兰绒制的|A soft, slightly fuzzy woolen or cotton fabric, typically made of loosely woven yarn.  It can also refer to clothing made from this fabric.|She wore a warm flannel shirt. 她穿着一件温暖的法兰绒衬衫。|近义词：wool, fleece|反义词:silk, linen|noun

flannel:/ˈflæn(ə)l/| adj.|法兰绒制的|Made of flannel.|He had a flannel shirt on. 他穿着一件法兰绒衬衫。|近义词：woolen, fleecy|反义词:silken, linen|adjective
