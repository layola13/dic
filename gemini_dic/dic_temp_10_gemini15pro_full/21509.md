ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bleu, *blew, from Old French bleu, blo, from Frankish *blāo, from Proto-Germanic *blǣwaz (“blue, livid”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰleh₁- (“to bloom, blossom”). Cognate with Old High German blāo (“blue, black, lead-colored”), Old English blǣwen (“blue”). More at blow.
