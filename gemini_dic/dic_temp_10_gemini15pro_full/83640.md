haemolutein:/ˌhiːməˈluːtiːn/ | n. |血黄素| A carotenoid pigment found in egg yolk and some plants, contributing to their yellow color. It is a type of xanthophyll, and in the body, it's converted to lutein. |  The haemolutein in egg yolks gives them their characteristic yellow hue.  鸡蛋黄中的血黄素赋予了它们特有的黄色。| Synonyms: xanthophyll | Antonyms: N/A | noun

haemolutein:/ˌhiːməˈluːtiːn/ | n. | 血黄素 |  A type of xanthophyll and carotenoid pigment found in egg yolks, certain plants, and some algae, which contributes to their yellow coloration. It's considered a precursor to lutein in the body.  |  The study analyzed the haemolutein content of various egg yolks. 这项研究分析了各种蛋黄中血黄素的含量。 | Synonyms: xanthophyll, carotenoid (broadly) | Antonyms: N/A | noun
