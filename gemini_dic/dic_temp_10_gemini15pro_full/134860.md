nonmomentary:/ˌnɒnˈməʊməntəri/ /ˌnɑːnˈmoʊmənteri/
adjective
非瞬时的，持续的
Not momentary; lasting for an appreciable time.
The pain was nonmomentary, lasting for several hours. 疼痛持续了几个小时，并非短暂的。
The contract stipulated a nonmomentary commitment to the project. 合同规定了对该项目的持续承诺。
近义词:continuous, sustained, prolonged, enduring
反义词:momentary, fleeting, transient, brief
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + momentary.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "momentary" refers to something lasting only a moment.
