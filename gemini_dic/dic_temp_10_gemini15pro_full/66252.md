excludes:/ɪkˈskluːdz/| v.|排除，不包括|This diet excludes all dairy products.  这种饮食不包括任何乳制品。|The price excludes tax. 价格不含税。|omit, leave out|include|verb
Etymology:  From Latin "excludere" (to shut out), from "ex" (out) + "claudere" (to close).
