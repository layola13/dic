beatific:/ˌbiːəˈtɪfɪk/| adj.|幸福的；快乐的；安详的|Showing or expressing great joy and serenity, often in a way suggesting spiritual blessedness.|She had a beatific smile on her face after the birth of her child. 她孩子出生后脸上露出了幸福的笑容。|Synonyms: blissful, radiant, serene, joyful, ecstatic|Antonyms: miserable, unhappy, sorrowful, wretched|adjective

beatific:/ˌbiːəˈtɪfɪk/| adj.|幸福的；快乐的；安详的|Characterized by complete happiness or joy, often with a suggestion of spiritual enlightenment.|The beatific vision of the saints is described in religious texts.  宗教文本中描述了圣徒的幸福景象。|Synonyms: blessed, divine, holy, sacred, rapturous|Antonyms: cursed, damned, unholy, profane, tormented|adjective
