neurodevelopmental:/ˌnʊr.oʊ.dɪ.ˌvɛl.əpˈmɛn.təl/| adj.|神经发育的|Relating to the development of the nervous system.|例句：Neurodevelopmental disorders can affect learning and behavior. 神经发育障碍会影响学习和行为。|例句：Early intervention is crucial for children with neurodevelopmental difficulties. 早期干预对于有神经发育障碍的儿童至关重要。|近义词：neurological, neural|反义词：(none,  the opposite would imply a lack of development which isn't a single word)|词性:adjective

Etymology->
"neuro-" + "developmental"
"neuro-" comes from Greek "neuron" meaning "nerve, sinew, tendon."
"developmental" comes from "development" which originates from the Old French "desveloper" meaning "to unwrap, unfold."
