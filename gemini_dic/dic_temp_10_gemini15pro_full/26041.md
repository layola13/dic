bushel:/ˈbʊʃəl/| n.|蒲式耳（容量单位，用于干货，尤指谷物）|A bushel is a unit of dry volume, especially for grain, equal to 8 gallons or approximately 36.4 liters.|The farmer harvested 100 bushels of wheat this year. 今年农民收获了100蒲式耳小麦。|Synonyms: peck, dry quart|Antonyms: N/A|noun
