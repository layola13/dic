cajolingly:/kəˈdʒoʊlɪŋli/
adverb
诱哄地，劝诱地
in a way that persuades someone to do something by being friendly and pleasant, or by flattering them
She smiled cajolingly at him. 她劝诱性地对他笑了笑。
近义词: persuasively, coaxingly, wheedlingly, flatteringly
反义词: forcefully, demandingly, threateningly
词性: adverb
