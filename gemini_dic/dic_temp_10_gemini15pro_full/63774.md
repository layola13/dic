epictetian: /ˌɛpɪkˈtiːʃən/ | adj. n.|  エピクテトス的; 禁欲主义的; 斯多葛学派的 | Of or relating to Epictetus, a Greek Stoic philosopher, or his philosophy.  Characterized by or suggesting the philosophy of Epictetus, especially his emphasis on self-control and acceptance of what is inevitable. |  He maintained an Epictetian calm throughout the ordeal. 他在整个考验中都保持着禁欲主义的冷静。|  Stoic, philosophical, austere, impassive, indifferent | Hedonistic, indulgent | adjective, noun
