ctenophore:/ˈtɛnəfɔːr/ /ˈtiːnəfɔːr/| n. |栉水母|A marine invertebrate animal of the phylum Ctenophora, which comprises the comb jellies. They are characterized by eight rows of cilia, which are used for locomotion.|
例句：The bioluminescent ctenophore lit up the dark ocean depths.  发光的栉水母照亮了黑暗的海洋深处。

例句：Researchers are studying the unique nervous system of the ctenophore. 研究人员正在研究栉水母独特的神经系统。
