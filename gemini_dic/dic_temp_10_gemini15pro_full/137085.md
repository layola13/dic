nonstudies:/ˌnɑnˈstʌdiz/ | n. | 非学习内容，非学术活动 | Activities or subjects that are not related to academic studies. |  Example: He spends most of his time on nonstudies like video games. 他大部分时间都花在电子游戏之类的非学习内容上。

Example:  Her nonstudies include painting and playing the guitar. 她的非学习活动包括绘画和弹吉他。| Synonym: extracurricular activities, hobbies, leisure pursuits | Antonym: studies, academics, coursework | noun
