intercrusted:/ˌɪntərˈkrʌstɪd/
adjective
镶嵌的，覆盖硬壳的
Covered with a hard crust; having a crust formed between layers.
The ancient artifact was intercrusted with jewels and precious metals. 这件古代文物镶嵌着珠宝和贵金属。
The geological formation was intercrusted with layers of minerals.  这个地质构造镶嵌着多层矿物质。
近义词: encrusted, inlaid
反义词: bare, smooth
词性: adjective
