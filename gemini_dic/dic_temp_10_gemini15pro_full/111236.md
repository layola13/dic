lithotype:/ˈlɪθəˌtaɪp/
noun
岩型
A rock type or category defined by its physical characteristics, especially in coal petrology.
The lithotype of the coal seam was determined through microscopic analysis. 通过显微镜分析确定了煤层的岩型。
synonyms: rock type, petrographic type
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, as it's a classification term)
