bulked:/bʌlkt/
| v. |增大，使更大；显得庞大|To make something bigger or appear bigger, often by adding material.|He bulked up for his role as a boxer. 他为了扮演拳击手的角色而增重。|近义词：enlarge, expand, increase, swell, augment|反义词:shrink, reduce, decrease|Verb

bulked:/bʌlkt/
| adj. |庞大的，体积大的|Large in size or volume.|He looked bulked up in his new suit.  他穿着新西装看起来很魁梧。|近义词：large, massive, bulky, huge|反义词:small, tiny, slight|Adjective
