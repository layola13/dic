mucate: /ˈmjuːkeɪt/ | n. | 粘酸盐; 粘液酸盐 | A salt or ester of mucic acid. |  Calcium mucate is a white crystalline powder. 粘酸钙是一种白色结晶粉末。|  Mucates are formed by the oxidation of gums and sugars. 粘酸盐是由树胶和糖的氧化形成的。 | synonyms: salt, ester (in the context of chemical compounds derived from mucic acid) | antonyms: acid (in a broad chemical sense, though not a direct opposite) | noun
