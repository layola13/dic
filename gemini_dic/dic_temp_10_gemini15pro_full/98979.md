inter-sampled:/ˌɪntərˈsæmpəld/ | adj. | 间采样的，互相采样的 | Sampled at intervals or from different parts of a larger sample, especially to allow comparison or to ensure representativeness. | Example: Inter-sampled data were used to assess the variability of the soil properties. 间采样数据用于评估土壤性质的变异性。| Example: The study used an inter-sampled design to compare the effectiveness of two different treatments. 这项研究采用间采样设计来比较两种不同治疗的有效性。 | 近义词: cross-sampled, intermittently sampled | 反义词: continuously sampled | 词性: adjective
