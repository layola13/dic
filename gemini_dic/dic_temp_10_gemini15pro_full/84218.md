half-ruefully:/ˌhɑːfˈruːfəli/
| adv. | 半懊悔地，略带悔意地 | Partly rueful; showing slight remorse or regret. | He half-ruefully admitted he'd eaten all the cookies. 他半懊悔地承认他把饼干都吃光了。 | He smiled half-ruefully, acknowledging his mistake.  他半带悔意地笑了笑，承认了自己的错误。 | 近义词: apologetically, regretfully, remorsefully | 反义词: unrepentantly, defiantly | 词性: 副词

half:/hæf/
| adj., n., adv. | 一半的，一半 | One of two equal or approximately equal parts of a divisible whole. | I'll take half the pizza. 我要一半披萨。| I ate half of the cake. 我吃了蛋糕的一半。 | 近义词: moiety, part | 反义词: whole, double | 词性: 形容词，名词，副词

ruefully:/ˈruːfəli/
| adv. | 懊悔地，悔恨地 | In a way that expresses sorrow or regret, especially for something one has done. | He shook his head ruefully. 他懊悔地摇了摇头。| "I wish I'd never said that," she admitted ruefully. “我希望我从未说过那句话，”她懊悔地承认道。 | 近义词: regretfully, remorsefully, sorrowfully | 反义词: unrepentantly, gleefully | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->

"ruefully" comes from "rueful," from Middle English "rewful," from "rew(e)" (sorrow, regret) + "-ful."
