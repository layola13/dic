accommodativeness: /əˌkɒməˈdeɪtɪvnəs/
|n.| 乐于助人; 通融; 随和 |The hotel staff were known for their accommodativeness.  这家酒店的员工以乐于助人而闻名。| willingness to help; obligingness; adaptability. | The host showed great accommodativeness in rearranging the seating to fit our large group.  主人非常乐于助人，重新安排座位以容纳我们的大团队。|  helpfulness, flexibility, adaptability, cooperation, willingness | inflexibility, rigidity, stubbornness, unhelpfulness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From accommodate + -ive + -ness.  Accommodate comes from the Latin accommodare "to make fit, adapt," from ad- "to" + commodare "to make fit," from commodus "suitable, convenient."
