cluttered:/ˈklʌtərd/| adj.|杂乱的，凌乱的|Covered with or full of scattered things in a disorderly way.|例句：Her desk was cluttered with papers and books. 她的桌子堆满了文件和书籍。

例句：The room was so cluttered that it was hard to move around. 房间太乱了，难以走动。|近义词：messy, untidy, disorganized, chaotic|反义词：tidy, neat, organized, orderly|词性:形容词

clutter:/ˈklʌtər/| n. |杂乱，凌乱|A collection of things lying about in an untidy mass.|例句：There was so much clutter in the attic that I could hardly find anything. 阁楼里堆满了杂物，我几乎找不到任何东西。

例句：The artist's studio was a delightful clutter of paints, brushes, and canvases. 艺术家的工作室里堆满了颜料、画笔和画布，一片赏心悦目的凌乱。|近义词：mess, disorder, chaos|反义词：tidiness, order, neatness|词性:名词

clutter:/ˈklʌtər/| v. |使凌乱，堆满|Fill or cover with scattered things in a disorderly way.|例句：Newspapers and magazines cluttered the coffee table. 报纸和杂志堆满了咖啡桌。

例句：Too many ornaments cluttered the shelves.  太多的装饰品把架子都堆满了。|近义词：mess up, litter, disorganize|反义词：tidy, organize, declutter|词性:动词
