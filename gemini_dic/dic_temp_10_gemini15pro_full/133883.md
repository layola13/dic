The artist's nongregariousnesses allowed him to focus intensely on his work. | Synonyms: unsociability, introversion, aloofness | Antonyms: gregariousness, sociability, extroversion | noun

Etymology:
From non- (not) + gregarious (fond of company; sociable) + -ness (forming nouns expressing state or quality).  Gregarious ultimately derives from the Latin word "grex," meaning "flock" or "herd."
