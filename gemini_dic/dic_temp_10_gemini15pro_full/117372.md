mediative:/ˈmiːdiətɪv/| adj.| 冥想的，沉思的；调解的| tending to mediate or reconcile; involving or characterized by meditation |She adopted a mediative posture, closing her eyes and taking deep breaths. 她摆出一个冥想的姿势，闭上眼睛，深呼吸。| The monk led the group in a mediative chant.  僧侣带领大家吟诵冥想圣歌。| 近义词：contemplative, reflective, thoughtful, introspective, meditative, reconciliatory |反义词：unreflective, impulsive, rash |词性: adjective
