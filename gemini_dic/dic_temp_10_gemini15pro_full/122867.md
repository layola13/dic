mollifier:/ˈmɒlɪfaɪər/ /ˈmɑːlɪfaɪər/| n. |缓和剂；调解人；润肤剂|Something that softens or soothes; a mediating agent.  A substance added to a material to make it softer or more pliable.|The diplomat acted as a mollifier between the two warring nations.  外交官在两个交战国之间充当了调解人。|synonyms:  mediator, conciliator, appeaser, softener, emollient,  pacifier|antonyms:  irritant, aggravator, provocateur|noun

mollify:/ˈmɒlɪfaɪ/ /ˈmɑːlɪfaɪ/| v. |缓和；平息；抚慰|To appease the anger or anxiety of (someone). To make (a substance) softer or more pliable.|He tried to mollify his angry wife with flowers. 他试图用鲜花来平息他愤怒的妻子。 The lotion mollified her dry skin.  润肤露舒缓了她干燥的皮肤。|synonyms:  appease, placate, soothe, pacify, calm, soften|antonyms:  enrage, incense, aggravate, provoke, irritate|verb
