leagued:/liːɡd/
adjective
结盟的，同盟的
Joined together in a league or alliance.
例句：The leagued nations fought against the common enemy.  结盟的国家共同对抗敌人。

例句：They were leagued together in a conspiracy to overthrow the government. 他们结盟密谋推翻政府。

近义词：allied, united, confederated, combined
反义词：separate, divided, disunited

词性: adjective

词源：From the verb "league" + "-ed," ultimately from Latin "ligare" (to bind).
