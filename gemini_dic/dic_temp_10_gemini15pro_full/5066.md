all-the-better:/ɔːl ðə ˈbetər/
adjective
更好
even better; improved as a result of something.
The rain stopped, which made the picnic all the better. 雨停了，野餐就更好了。
近义词：even better, still better, much better
反义词：worse, all the worse
词性:adjective

词源: From Middle English *al the bettre*, from Old English *eall þæt betere*  ("all the better").  The phrase is a comparative formed with the adverb *all* intensifying the comparative adjective *better*.
