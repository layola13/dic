nonvaporousness:/ˌnɒnˈveɪpərəsnəs/
n.
无挥发性；不蒸发性
The nonvaporousness of the liquid made it suitable for the experiment.  这种液体的无挥发性使其适合用于实验。
The substance's nonvaporousness ensures its stability at high temperatures.  该物质的无挥发性确保了其在高温下的稳定性。
近义词: nonvolatility, stability
反义词: volatility, vaporousness
词性: noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix) meaning "not" + vapor + -ous (suffix forming adjectives) + -ness (suffix forming nouns)  From Latin "vapor" meaning "steam, vapor".
