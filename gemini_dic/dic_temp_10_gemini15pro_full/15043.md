baby's:/ˈbeɪbiz/| n.|婴儿的，宝宝的|Something that belongs to a baby.  It is the possessive form of "baby".|例句：This is the baby's bottle. 这是婴儿的奶瓶。|例句：The baby's crying woke me up. 婴儿的哭声把我吵醒了。|近义词：infant's, newborn's|反义词:adult's|词性:Possessive noun (名词所有格)
