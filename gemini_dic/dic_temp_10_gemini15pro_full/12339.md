asphyxiate:/əsˈfɪksiˌeɪt/ /æsˈfɪksiˌeɪt/| v. |使窒息，闷死|To cause someone to be unable to breathe, usually resulting in death; to suffocate.|The fire produced toxic fumes that asphyxiated several people.|近义词：suffocate, choke, smother|反义词:resuscitate, revive|词性:动词

asphyxiate:/əsˈfɪksiˌeɪt/ /æsˈfɪksiˌeɪt/| n. |窒息|The state of being unable to breathe.|The victim died of asphyxiation.|近义词：suffocation|反义词:respiration|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin asphyxiatus, past participle of asphyxiare, from Greek asphyxia "stopping of the pulse," from a- "without" + sphyxis "pulse."
