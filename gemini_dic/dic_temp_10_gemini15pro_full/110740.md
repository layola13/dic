lingerers:/ˈlɪŋɡərərz/| n. |逗留者；徘徊者|People who stay in a place longer than necessary or expected. |例句：The lingerers at the party were reluctant to leave.  聚会上那些逗留的人都不愿意离开。|近义词：loiterers, dawdlers, remainers|反义词:leavers, goers|词性:noun

词源: From the verb "linger" (Middle English *lingren*, from Old Norse *lengja*, meaning "to lengthen") + "-ers" (suffix forming plural nouns).
