harmattan:/ˌhɑːrməˈtæn/ | n. | 哈马丹风（西非的一种干燥多尘的贸易风） | A dry, dusty northeasterly trade wind blowing from the Sahara across western Africa. |  The harmattan winds brought a thick haze of dust. 哈马丹风带来了浓厚的尘雾。|  The crops withered under the relentless harmattan.  庄稼在持续不断的哈马丹风中枯萎了。 |  trade wind, desert wind | monsoon | noun
