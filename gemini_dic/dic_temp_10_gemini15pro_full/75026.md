friese:/friːz/ /friːsə/| n.|弗里斯兰马，弗里斯兰羊，弗里斯兰布|A breed of horse originating in Friesland, Netherlands, known for its long, flowing mane and tail. Also, a breed of sheep and a type of heavy woolen cloth.|例句：The Friesian horse is known for its elegant gait. 弗里斯兰马以其优雅的步态而闻名。

例句：The Friesian sheep is prized for its wool. 弗里斯兰羊因其羊毛而备受珍视。|近义词：horse, sheep, wool (depending on context)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From West Frisian *friis, from Proto-Germanic *fraisaz. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *perkʷ- (“spotted, speckled”).
