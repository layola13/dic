mouthily:/ˈmaʊðɪli/| adv.|夸夸其谈地，多嘴地|In a garrulous or indiscreet manner, often with excessive talk.  Characterized by speaking too much or too freely, often revealing confidential information.|He spoke mouthily about his boss's plans, not realizing the secretary was in the room. 他夸夸其谈地谈论着他老板的计划，没有意识到秘书在房间里。|近义词：garrulously, loquaciously, volubly, talkatively|反义词：taciturnly, reticently|词性:副词
