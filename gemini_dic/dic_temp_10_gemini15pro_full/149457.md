paddlecock: /ˈpædlˌkɒk/ (UK) /ˈpædlˌkɑːk/ (US)| n. |羽毛球，毽球|A small cone-shaped object with feathers, used in the game of badminton.  Also sometimes refers to the game of badminton itself (especially in older usage). |例句：The paddlecock flew over the net. 羽毛球飞过了网。

例句：He hit the paddlecock with his racket. 他用球拍击中了羽毛球。|近义词：shuttlecock, birdie|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


词源:  From earlier *battelcock, from battle (meaning "battledore," a kind of racket) + cock (a rounded top or tuft of feathers).
