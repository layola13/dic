hypomagnesaemia: /ˌhaɪpəʊˌmæɡnɪˈsiːmiə/ (UK) /ˌhaɪpoʊˌmæɡnɪˈziːmiə/ (US)
noun
低镁血症
A condition characterized by abnormally low levels of magnesium in the blood.
Hypomagnesaemia can cause a variety of symptoms, including muscle cramps and seizures. 低镁血症会导致各种症状，包括肌肉痉挛和癫痫发作。
近义词: magnesium deficiency
反义词: hypermagnesaemia
词性: noun
