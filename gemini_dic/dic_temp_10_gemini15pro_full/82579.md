guardedly:/ˈɡɑːrdɪdli/ | adv. |谨慎地，小心地|In a cautious or reserved manner, showing reluctance to speak freely or take risks.| He spoke guardedly about the sensitive issue, careful not to reveal too much. 他对这个敏感问题说得谨慎，小心翼翼地不透露太多。|近义词: cautiously, carefully, prudently, discreetly |反义词: openly, frankly, carelessly, recklessly |词性:副词

词源: From "guarded" + "-ly." "Guarded" comes from the verb "guard," which originates from Old North French "garder" (to guard, protect, keep), ultimately from Frankish "*wardōn."  The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective "guarded" into an adverb.
