arir: /əˈrɪr/ | adj. |  in arrears; overdue; behind in payment | owing money that should have been paid earlier |  His rent is three months in arirr. 他的租金拖欠了三个月。| synonyms: overdue, outstanding, unpaid, delinquent | antonyms: current, paid, up-to-date | 词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ariere "behind," from Latin ad "to" + retro "backward."  The word "arrear" came into English through Anglo-French.  The "-s" in "arrears" is not a plural marker, but part of the original French word.

注意事项:
"arir" is an older spelling, sometimes seen in legal or historical contexts. The modern spelling is "arrear," and more usually in the plural form "arrears." While technically an adjective, it is almost always used with the preposition "in" and functions much like a noun.
