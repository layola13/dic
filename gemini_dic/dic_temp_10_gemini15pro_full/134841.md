nonmobilities:/ˌnɒnmoʊˈbɪlətiz/
noun
缺乏流动性；不流动性
The state or quality of being nonmobile; lack of mobility.  This can refer to people, goods, capital, or information.
The report highlighted the nonmobilities of the workforce due to lack of affordable housing.  这份报告强调了由于缺乏经济适用房导致的劳动力缺乏流动性。
The pandemic exacerbated the nonmobilities of international trade.疫情加剧了国际贸易的不流动性。
immobility, inflexibility, fixedness
mobility, fluidity, flexibility
n.
