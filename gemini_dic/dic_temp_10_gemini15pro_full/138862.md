nursery: /ˈnɜːrsəri/ (UK), /ˈnɜːrsəri/ (US) | n. | 托儿所；苗圃；温床 | A place where young children are cared for during the day. A place where plants are grown for sale or for planting elsewhere.  |  例句：The children are at nursery. 孩子们在托儿所。

例句：She runs a successful plant nursery. 她经营着一家生意兴隆的苗圃。

近义词：creche, daycare, garden center | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词) | 词性: 名词
