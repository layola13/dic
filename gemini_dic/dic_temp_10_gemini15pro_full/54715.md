disownment:/dɪsˈoʊnmənt/
|n.|断绝关系，否认，抛弃|The act of refusing to acknowledge or accept someone or something as one's own.|His disownment of his family shocked everyone. 他与家人断绝关系震惊了所有人。|近义词：rejection, renunciation, repudiation|反义词：acceptance, acknowledgment, embrace|词性:名词

disown:/dɪsˈoʊn/
|vt.|否认，断绝关系，不承认|To refuse to acknowledge or accept as one's own; to renounce.|He disowned his son after the scandal.  丑闻曝光后，他与儿子断绝了关系。|近义词：reject, renounce, repudiate|反义词：accept, acknowledge, embrace|词性:动词
