buggy's:/ˈbʌɡiz/| n.|婴儿车；轻型马车|A small car, often without a roof, used for travelling over rough ground.  A small carriage for a baby, pushed by a person on foot. |例句：The family took a buggy ride through the park. 这家人乘坐轻型马车穿过公园。

例句：She pushed the buggy along the sidewalk. 她推着婴儿车沿着人行道走。|近义词：stroller, pram, pushchair, carriage|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "truck" if contrasting size and terrain use)|词性:名词

词源: From "buggy," a light carriage, originally from "bug," a hobgoblin or ghost (referring to the perceived lightness and swiftness of the vehicle).  The "'s" indicates possessive form.
