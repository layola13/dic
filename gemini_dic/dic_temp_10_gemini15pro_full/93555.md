imagineering:/ɪˌmædʒɪˈnɪrɪŋ/
n.|幻想工程；梦想工程|The process of designing and building new things by using your imagination and creativity, especially in entertainment and theme parks.|Disney is famous for its imagineering, creating immersive experiences for visitors.|近义词：innovation, creativity, design, invention|反义词：stagnation, imitation|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
A portmanteau of "imagination" and "engineering," coined by Walt Disney in 1957.
