demential:/dɪˈmɛnʃəl/
adjective
痴呆的；失智的
Of, relating to, or suffering from dementia.
例句：Demential changes can make it difficult for a person to remember recent events. 痴呆症的变化会使人难以记住最近发生的事情。

例句：The demential patient struggled to recognize his own family. 这位痴呆症患者难以认出自己的家人。

近义词：demented, insane, senile
反义词：lucid, sane, rational
词性: adjective
