dischargeable:/dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒəbl/ /dɪsˈtʃɑːr-dʒə-bəl/
adjective
可免除的；可卸下的；可放电的
Able to be discharged, released, or excused from an obligation or debt.  Also, capable of being unloaded or emptied.
The debt was deemed dischargeable in bankruptcy court.  这笔债务被认为在破产法庭上可以免除。
The battery is fully dischargeable and rechargeable.  这块电池可以完全放电和充电。
Synonyms: releasable, remittable, excusable, payable
Antonyms: non-dischargeable, unpayable, inescapable, unreleasable
词性: adjective
