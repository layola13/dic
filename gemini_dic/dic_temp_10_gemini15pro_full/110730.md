/lɪŋ/ | n. | 苔藓; 石南丛; 石楠荒野 | A low, evergreen plant that grows in clumps, forming a soft, dense carpet on the ground, often in damp or shady areas. Also refers to areas of land covered with this plant, especially in mountainous regions. |  The hills were covered in ling.  这些小山丘上长满了石南。 | synonyms: heather, heath, gorse | antonyms: grassland, meadow | noun


/lɪŋ/ | v. (dialectal) | 采集石南 | To gather ling, typically for use as fuel or bedding. |  They went linging on the moors. 他们去荒野上采集石南。 | synonyms: gather, collect, harvest | antonyms: scatter, disperse | verb
