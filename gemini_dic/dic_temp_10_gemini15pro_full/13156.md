asyndetic:/ˌeɪsɪnˈdɛtɪk/
adjective
无连词的；连词省略的
Characterized by asyndeton; omitting conjunctions between words, phrases, or clauses.
She spoke in short, asyndetic phrases. 她说话简短，没有连词。
Near synonyms: unconnected, unlinked, disjoined
Antonyms: polysyndetic, syndetic
词性: 形容词
