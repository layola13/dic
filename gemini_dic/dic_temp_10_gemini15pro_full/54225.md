disformity:/dɪsˈfɔːrmɪti/ /dɪsˈfɔːrməti/| n.|畸形，残疾|a difference in form from what is usual, especially when considered to be abnormal or ugly; a malformation.|例句：The child was born with a disformity of the hand.  这个孩子出生时手部畸形。|近义词：deformity, malformation, abnormality|反义词：normality, symmetry|词性:名词

disformity:/dɪsˈfɔːrmɪti/ /dɪsˈfɔːrməti/| n.|丑陋，难看|something that is disfigured or misshapen, especially in a way that is unpleasant to look at.|例句：The disformity of the building was shocking. 这座建筑的丑陋令人震惊。|近义词：ugliness, unsightliness|反义词：beauty, attractiveness|词性:名词
