estatlich:/ˈʃtaːtlɪç/ (German)| adj.|国家的；国有的；政府的|Of or relating to the state; governmental; public.  Derived from the German word "Staat" (state) with the suffix "-lich" which forms adjectives meaning "relating to" or "characteristic of".|例句：Die estatliche Schule ist kostenlos. = The state school is free.


例句：Estatliche Unterstützung ist für viele Familien wichtig. = Government support is important for many families.


近义词：öffentlich (public), staatlich geführt (state-run), governmental|反义词:privat (private)|词性:adjective
