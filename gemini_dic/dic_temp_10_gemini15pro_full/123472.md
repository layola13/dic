monostatic:/ˌmɒnəˈstætɪk/ | adj. |单基地式的，单站式的| Relating to a radar or sonar system in which the transmitter and receiver are at the same location. | The monostatic radar system is easier to deploy than a bistatic one. 单基地雷达系统比双基地雷达系统更容易部署。| The submarine used its monostatic sonar to detect nearby objects. 潜水艇使用其单基地声纳探测附近的物体。|近义词: single-station |反义词: bistatic |词性: adjective
