nuttier:/ˈnʌtɪər/| adj.|更疯狂的，更古怪的|Comparative form of nutty: more nutty.  Meaning even more crazy, strange, or eccentric.|He's gotten even nuttier since he won the lottery.  他自从中了彩票后变得更古怪了。|Synonyms: crazier, madder, loopier|Antonyms: saner, more rational|adjective

nuttier:/ˈnʌtɪər/| adj.|更傻的|Comparative form of nutty: more nutty. Meaning even more foolish or silly.|That's the nuttiest idea I've ever heard!  那是我听过的最傻的主意！|Synonyms: sillier, goofier, dafter|Antonyms: wiser, more sensible|adjective
