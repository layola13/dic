atria:/ˈeɪtriə/ (plural), /ˈeɪtriəm/ (singular)| n.|心房|The upper chambers of the heart that receive blood from the veins and pump it into the ventricles.|Blood flows from the atria to the ventricles. 血液从心房流向心室。|The left atrium receives oxygenated blood from the lungs. 左心房接收来自肺部的含氧血液。|Synonyms: auricle (anatomy, outdated)|Antonyms: ventricle|Noun

atria:/ˈɑːtriə/ (plural), /ˈɑːtriəm/ (singular)| n.|中庭，天井|A large open space, typically one that is partly enclosed, located within a building.|The hotel has a beautiful atrium filled with plants. 这家酒店有一个充满植物的美丽中庭。|The museum's atrium provides a spacious area for visitors to gather. 博物馆的中庭为游客提供了一个宽敞的聚会场所。|Synonyms: courtyard, lobby, forecourt|Antonyms: closet, cubicle|Noun
