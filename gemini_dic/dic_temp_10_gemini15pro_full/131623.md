noncomposites:/ˌnɑːn.kəmˈpɑː.zɪts/ | n. |非合数，质数| Numbers that have only two factors (1 and itself) are called noncomposites or prime numbers. |  Example: 7 is a noncomposite number because its only factors are 1 and 7.  7是一个非合数，因为它的因子只有1和7。| Example:  2, 3, and 5 are examples of noncomposites. 2，3和5是非合数的例子。 | Synonym: primes, prime numbers | Antonym: composites, composite numbers | Noun
