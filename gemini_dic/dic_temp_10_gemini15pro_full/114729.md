malls:/mɔːlz/
n.
购物中心；商场
Large enclosed shopping areas containing various stores and often restaurants and entertainment venues.
We went to the malls to do some Christmas shopping.  我们去购物中心买圣诞礼物。
The new malls has a cinema and a bowling alley.  新的购物中心有电影院和保龄球馆。
近义词:shopping centers, plazas, retail complexes
反义词:individual stores, boutiques
