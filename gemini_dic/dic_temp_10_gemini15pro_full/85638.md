haunt:/hɔːnt/ (British), /hɑːnt/ (American)| v. n.| 经常出没；鬼魂般出现；萦绕；(鬼魂)常出没于| The memory of that day still haunts me. 那一天的记忆仍然萦绕着我。|近义词:plague, torment, frequent, visit|反义词:avoid, shun|词性:动词

haunt:/hɔːnt/ (British), /hɑːnt/ (American)| n.| 常去的地方；消磨时间的地方| This café is a favorite haunt of artists.  这家咖啡馆是艺术家们最爱去的地方。|近义词:hangout, resort, refuge|反义词: N/A |词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *hanter "to frequent, practice," of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Frankish *heim "home," or from a Scandinavian source related to Old Norse heimta "to go home."
