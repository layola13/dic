gorge:/ɡɔːrdʒ/ | n. |峡谷；暴食；咽喉；大量|A deep, narrow valley with steep sides.|例句：The river carved a deep gorge through the mountains. 河流在群山中 carving 出了一条深邃的峡谷。|近义词：canyon, ravine, chasm|反义词：plain, plateau|词性:名词

gorge:/ɡɔːrdʒ/ | v. |狼吞虎咽；塞饱；贪婪地阅读|To eat a large amount greedily; to fill completely.|例句：He gorged himself on pizza. 他狼吞虎咽地吃了披萨。|近义词：stuff, cram, devour|反义词：nibble, starve|词性:动词

gorged:/ɡɔːrdʒd/ | adj. |吃饱的；充满的|Having eaten to excess; filled to capacity.|例句：I felt gorged after the Thanksgiving feast.  感恩节盛宴后，我感觉吃撑了。|近义词：stuffed, full, satiated|反义词：hungry, empty|词性:形容词
