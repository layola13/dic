maizes:/ˈmeɪzɪz/| n.|玉米（复数）|Plural form of maize, referring to multiple types or instances of maize.  Maize itself is a cereal grain first domesticated by indigenous peoples in southern Mexico about 10,000 years ago.|The farmer planted several different maizes this year, including sweet corn and popcorn. 今年农民种植了几种不同的玉米，包括甜玉米和爆米花。|Synonyms: corns (less formal, more common in British English)|Antonyms: None (lack of maize would be described as absence, scarcity, etc.)|Noun
