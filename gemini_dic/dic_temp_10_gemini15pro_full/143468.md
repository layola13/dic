outgain:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪn/
verb
胜过，超过（在数量或程度上）
to surpass or exceed in gaining something.
The visiting team outgained the home team by 100 yards. 客队比主队多进了100码。
Our company outgained all of its competitors in sales last year. 去年，我们公司的销售额超过了所有竞争对手。
synonyms: surpass, exceed, outstrip, outdo
antonyms: lag behind, fall short of
