braiders:/ˈbreɪdərz/| n.| 编辫子的人；编织者 |People who braid hair or other materials are called braiders.  编辫子的人或编织其他材料的人被称为编辫子的人。|  The braiders were busy creating intricate hairstyles.  编辫子的人正忙着创作复杂的发型。|近义词：plaiters, weavers|反义词: (None, as it describes a profession)|词性: 名词

词源:
From the verb "braid" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Braid" comes from the Old English "bregdan," meaning "to weave, plait." 源自动词 "braid" + "-er"（表示行为者的后缀）。"Braid" 来自古英语 "bregdan"，意思是“编织”。
