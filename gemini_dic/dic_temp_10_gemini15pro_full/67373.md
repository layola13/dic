extensors:/ɪkˈstɛnsərz/| n.|伸肌|Muscles that extend a limb or other body part.|例句：The extensors in her forearm were strained from playing tennis. 她前臂的伸肌因为打网球而拉伤了。

例句：The quadriceps are the extensors of the knee. 股四头肌是膝关节的伸肌。|近义词：straightening muscles|反义词:flexors|noun
