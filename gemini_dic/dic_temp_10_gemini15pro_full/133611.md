nonfluidly:/nɒnˈfluːɪdli/
adverb|不流畅地，不灵活地|Not smoothly or easily; in a stiff or jerky manner.  Lacking fluidity or grace.| The robot moved nonfluidly, its joints creaking with each step.  机器人动作不流畅，关节每走一步都嘎吱作响。|stiffly, awkwardly, jerkily|fluidly, smoothly|词性: 副词
