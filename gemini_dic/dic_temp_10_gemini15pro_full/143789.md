outmuscling: /ˌaʊtˈmʌsəlɪŋ/
verb
以强力胜过；压制；比…力气大
overpowering or defeating someone or something by using greater strength or power
The larger company outmuscled its competitors in the bidding war. 这家规模较大的公司在竞标战中以强力击败了竞争对手。

The team outmuscled their opponents to win the championship.  这支队伍凭借强大的实力战胜了对手，赢得了冠军。

近义词: overpower, dominate, subdue, crush
反义词: yield, succumb, surrender
词性: verb (present participle)

He outmuscled the other wrestlers and won the match. 他力压其他摔跤选手，赢得了比赛。

近义词: overpower, dominate, subdue, crush
反义词: yield, succumb, surrender
词性: verb


词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "muscle" (noun referring to bodily strength).  The verb form "outmuscle" arose by adding the verbal suffix "-le" to the noun "muscle," creating the meaning "to surpass in muscle power."  "Outmuscling" is the present participle form of the verb "outmuscle."
