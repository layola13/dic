cognizance:/ˈkɒɡnɪzəns/ (UK) /ˈkɑːɡnɪzəns/ (US)| n. |认知，认识；审理；权限|knowledge or awareness of something; the right to deal with particular matters, especially in a legal context|The court took cognizance of the mitigating circumstances. 法院考虑了情有可原的情况。|Synonyms: awareness, knowledge, notice, perception, consciousness|Antonyms: ignorance, unawareness|Noun


cognizant:/ˈkɒɡnɪzənt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːɡnɪzənt/ (US)| adj. |已认知的，知道的|having knowledge or awareness of something|He was cognizant of the risks involved. 他知道所涉及的风险。|Synonyms: aware, conscious, mindful, informed, apprised|Antonyms: unaware, ignorant, oblivious, unconscious|Adjective
