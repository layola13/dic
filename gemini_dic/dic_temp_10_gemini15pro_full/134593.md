nonlubriciously:/ˌnɒnlubriʃəsli/
| adv. |不滑地，缺乏润滑地|Lacking lubrication; not slippery.  Describes something moving or functioning without smoothness or ease due to a lack of lubrication.|The gears ground nonlubriciously, creating a horrible screeching sound. 齿轮不滑地摩擦，发出可怕的 screeching 的声音。|Synonyms: roughly, harshly, gratingly, stickily|Antonyms: smoothly, slickly, easily, lubricatedly
|词性:副词
