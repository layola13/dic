misinferred:/ˌmɪsɪnˈfɜːrd/ | v. | 错误地推断；曲解 | To infer incorrectly; to draw a wrong conclusion. |  She misinferred his silence as agreement. 她把他的沉默误解为同意。

The journalist misinferred the politician's statement and published a misleading article. 记者误解了政治家的声明，并发表了一篇误导性的文章。

| 近义词：misinterpreted, misconstrued, misunderstood | 反义词：understood, correctly inferred, deduced | 词性：动词
