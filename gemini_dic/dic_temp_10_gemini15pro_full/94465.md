impoliticnesses:/ɪmˌpɒlɪˈtɪknɪsɪz/
|n.|失礼;失策|The diplomat's impoliticnesses strained relations between the two countries.  这位外交官的失策使两国关系紧张。|例句：His impoliticnesses cost him his job. 他的失策让他丢了工作。|近义词：rudeness, incivility, tactlessness, indiscretion|反义词：politeness, courtesy, tact, diplomacy|词性:名词

Etymology:
From impolitic + -ness.  "Impolitic" comes from "im-" (not) + "politic" (wise or prudent in regard to the public good).  "Politic" ultimately derives from the Greek "politikos" (of citizens or the state).
