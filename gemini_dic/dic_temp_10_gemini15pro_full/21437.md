blowhard:/ˈbloʊhɑːrd/| n.|吹牛大王，自吹自擂的人|A person who boasts loudly and emptily; a braggart.|He's a blowhard who is always talking about his accomplishments, most of which are exaggerated or entirely fabricated. 他是个吹牛大王，总是谈论他的成就，其中大部分都是夸大其词或完全捏造的。

The CEO, a notorious blowhard, claimed he single-handedly saved the company.  这位臭名昭著的CEO吹嘘说，他单枪匹马拯救了公司。|近义词：braggart, boaster, windbag|反义词：humble person, modest person|词性：noun
