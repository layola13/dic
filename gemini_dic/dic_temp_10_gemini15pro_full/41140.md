copublish:/ˈkoʊˌpʌblɪʃ/| v.| 共同出版，联合出版 |To publish something together with another company or person.|Example:  The book was copublished by two university presses. 这本书由两家大学出版社联合出版。
Example: We copublished a series of travel guides with a local publisher. 我们与当地一家出版商联合出版了一系列旅游指南。|近义词：jointly publish, co-issue|反义词:  solely publish, independently publish|词性: verb
