breaststroke:/ˈbreststroʊk/ (US) /ˈbrɛstrəʊk/ (UK)| n.|蛙泳|A swimming stroke in which the arms are moved forward, outward, and backward while the legs are drawn up and then kicked outward in a frog-like motion.|He won the race using the breaststroke. 他用蛙泳赢得了比赛。|近义词: frog kick (informal)|反义词: backstroke, crawl, butterfly|noun

breaststrokes:/ˈbreststroʊks/ (US) /ˈbrɛstrəʊks/ (UK)| n.|蛙泳（复数）|Plural form of breaststroke, referring to multiple instances or types of the breaststroke swimming technique.|She practiced several different breaststrokes to improve her speed. 她练习了几种不同的蛙泳姿势来提高速度。|近义词: frog kicks (informal)|反义词: backstrokes, crawls, butterflies|noun
