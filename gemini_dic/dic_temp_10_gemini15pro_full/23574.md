braising:/ˈbreɪzɪŋ/| v. | 焖；炖 | To cook (food) by browning it in fat, then simmering it in a small quantity of liquid in a covered container. | Example 1: She braised the beef for three hours until it was fork-tender. 她把牛肉炖了三个小时，直到用叉子就能轻易分开。

Example 2:  Braising is a great way to cook tougher cuts of meat. 焖是一种烹饪较硬肉块的好方法。 | Near Synonyms: stewing, simmering, pot-roasting | Antonyms: frying, grilling, roasting | Verb


braise:/breɪz/| n. | 焖；炖 | The act or process of braising. | Example 1: The pot roast needs another hour in the braise.  这锅烤肉还需要再焖一个小时。

Example 2: The long, slow braise made the lamb incredibly tender.  长时间的慢炖使羊肉变得非常嫩。 | Near Synonyms: stew | Antonyms: fry, grill, roast | Noun
