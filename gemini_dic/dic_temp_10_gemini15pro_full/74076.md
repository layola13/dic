fossilize:/ˈfɒsəlaɪz/ (UK), /ˈfɑːsəlaɪz/ (US)| v. | 使…变成化石；使…过时；使…僵化|To convert into a fossil; to preserve or become preserved in a fossil state. To become rigidly fixed or unchanging; to make something old-fashioned or outdated.|The dinosaur bones were fossilized over millions of years.  恐龙骨骼经过数百万年变成了化石。

The company's management structure had fossilized, hindering innovation.  这家公司的管理结构已经僵化，阻碍了创新。|近义词：petrify, ossify, harden|反义词:modernize, revitalize|词性:动词
