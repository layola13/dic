epicarican: /ˌɛpɪˈkɑːrɪkən/
adjective
享乐主义的；快乐主义的
Relating to the philosophy of Epicurus, which emphasized pleasure as the highest good.  Characterized by or devoted to the pursuit of pleasure; hedonistic.
例句：He lived an epicarican lifestyle, indulging in fine food and wine. 他过着享乐主义的生活方式，沉迷于美食和美酒。

例句：Her epicarican tastes were evident in her lavishly decorated home. 她享乐主义的品味在她奢华的家中显而易见。

近义词：hedonistic, pleasure-seeking, sybaritic, voluptuary
反义词：ascetic, abstemious, austere


词性: adjective
