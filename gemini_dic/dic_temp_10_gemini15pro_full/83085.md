gus:/ɡʌs/| n.|伙计，小子 (非正式)|A fellow; a chap.  A term of address, often friendly or slightly condescending. |Example: Hey, gus, what's up? 嘿，伙计，怎么了？|Example:  That gus over there looks familiar.  那边那个小子看起来很眼熟。|近义词：fellow, chap, guy, bloke|反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on context, could be enemy, adversary)|noun
