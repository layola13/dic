chromatism:/ˈkrəʊmətɪzəm/ (US) /ˈkroʊmətɪzəm/| n. | 色差；彩色；半音阶 |A phenomenon in which light rays of different wavelengths are refracted differently, resulting in the separation of light into its constituent colors.  Also refers to the use of semitones in music.| The prism caused noticeable chromatism, creating a rainbow effect. 棱镜引起了明显的色差，产生了彩虹效应。 | synonyms: chromatic aberration, coloration, iridescence | antonyms: achromatism | noun

chromatism:/ˈkrəʊmətɪzəm/ (US) /ˈkroʊmətɪzəm/| n. | 半音阶；半音体系|The use of semitones or the chromatic scale in music.| The composer's frequent use of chromatism gave the piece a melancholic feel. 作曲家频繁使用半音阶，使这首曲子有一种忧郁的感觉。 | synonyms: chromatic scale, chromaticism | antonyms: diatonicism | noun
