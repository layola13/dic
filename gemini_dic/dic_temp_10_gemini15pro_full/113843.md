madefaction: /ˌmeɪdəˈfækʃən/ | n. | 湿润; 潮湿; 浸透 |  The act of moistening or wetting; the state of being moistened or wetted. | The madefaction of the soil from the rain helped the seeds germinate. 雨水浸润土壤有助于种子发芽。 | 近义词: wetting, moistening, dampening, humidification | 反义词: drying, desiccation | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *madefactio, from Latin madefacere "to moisten," from made- (past participle stem of madēre "to be wet") + facere "to make, do."
