over-heaps:/ˌoʊvərˈhips/| v.|过度堆积，过多给予|To give or load with too much of something; overload.|He over-heaps his plate with food at every meal. 他每顿饭都把盘子堆得满满的。|Example: The charity was over-heaped with donations after the disaster. 灾难过后，慈善机构收到了大量的捐赠。|Synonyms: overload, overburden, overwhelm|Antonyms: underwhelm, deprive|verb

over-heaps:/ˌoʊvərˈhips/| adj.|过度堆积的|Loaded with too much of something.|The table was over-heaped with gifts. 桌子上堆满了礼物。|Example: The over-heaped landfill posed an environmental hazard. 过度堆积的垃圾填埋场构成了环境危害。|Synonyms: overloaded, overburdened|Antonyms: empty, bare|adjective
