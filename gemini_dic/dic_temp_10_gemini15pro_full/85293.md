harmonizing: /ˈhɑːmənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 和谐；协调；使一致 | Making different things agree or go well together. | The choir is harmonizing beautifully tonight. 今晚合唱团的和声非常优美. | synonyms: coordinating, blending, unifying, integrating, reconciling | antonyms: clashing, conflicting, disagreeing | verb

harmonizing: /ˈhɑːmənaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 和谐的；协调的 | Used to describe something that creates or promotes harmony. | The harmonizing effect of the music was calming. 音乐的和谐效果令人平静. | synonyms: harmonious, agreeable, concordant, compatible | antonyms: discordant, inharmonious, incompatible | adjective
