box-out:/ˈbɑːks aʊt/ (US) /ˈbɒks aʊt/ (UK)
noun, verb
卡位（篮球术语）；阻挡（对方球员抢篮板球）
The act of positioning oneself between an opponent and the basket to gain better rebounding position.  A player boxes out by widening their stance and using their body to prevent the opponent from getting close to the basket.
例句：
The center did a great job of boxing out his opponent, allowing his teammate to grab the rebound.
中锋成功地卡住了他的对手，让队友抢到了篮板。

He boxed out the defender and secured the rebound.
他卡住了防守队员，确保了篮板球。

近义词：block out, screen out
反义词: None (No direct antonym, perhaps "let through")
词性: noun, verb
