analogously:/əˈnæləɡəsli/
adverb|类似地；相似地|in a similar way|例句：The brain processes information analogously to a computer. 大脑处理信息的方式类似于计算机。|例句：Just as a car needs fuel, analogously, the human body needs food. 正如汽车需要燃料一样，人体也需要食物。|近义词：similarly, likewise, correspondingly|反义词：differently, dissimilarly|词性:副词

Etymology->
From analogy + -ous + -ly. Analogy comes from Late Latin analogia, from Greek analogia "proportion," from analogos "proportionate," from ana- "according to" + logos "ratio."
