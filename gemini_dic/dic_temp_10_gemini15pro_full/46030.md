cybersquatting: /ˈsaɪbərˌskwɑːtɪŋ/
n.
域名抢注；网络域名抢注
The act of registering a domain name that is the same as, or confusingly similar to, a well-known trademark, with the intention of selling it to the trademark owner for a profit.
Cybersquatting is illegal in many countries.  域名抢注在许多国家都是非法的。

The company sued him for cybersquatting.  该公司起诉他域名抢注。

近义词: domain hijacking, domain name grabbing
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider legitimate domain registration)
词性: noun
