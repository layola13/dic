nonblunderingly:/ˌnɒnˈblʌndərɪŋli/
adverb
无差错地，完美地
without making any blunders; flawlessly.
He executed the plan nonblunderingly.  他完美地执行了计划。
Synonyms: flawlessly, impeccably, perfectly, faultlessly
Antonyms: blunderingly, clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly
词性: 副词


Etymology:
From non- + blundering + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "blundering" is an adjective meaning "making mistakes," and "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.  Therefore, "nonblunderingly" means "in a way that does not involve making mistakes."
