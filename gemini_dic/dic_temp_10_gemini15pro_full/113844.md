madefication: /ˌmeɪdəfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 湿润，潮湿; (罕用)醉酒 | The process of making something wet or moist; (rare) the state of being drunk. | The madefication of the soil after the rain was evident. 雨后土壤的湿润很明显。|  The old sailor's madefication was apparent in his slurred speech. 那位老水手的醉态从他含糊不清的言语中显露出来。 | 近义词: moistening, dampening, wetting, intoxication (for drunkenness)| 反义词: drying, desiccation, sobriety (for drunkenness) | 词性: 名词

词源：From Latin *made- (related to madere, "to be wet") + -fication (from Latin -ficatio, indicating the process of making or becoming).
