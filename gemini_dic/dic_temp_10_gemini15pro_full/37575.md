The communalistic violence flared up again.  宗派暴力再次爆发。

communalistic:/kəˌmjuːnəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
共享的;公共的;社区的
Of or relating to a commune or community; shared by members of a community.
They live in a communalistic settlement where all property is shared. 他们生活在一个共享所有财产的公社定居点。
The communalistic spirit of the village was heartwarming. 这个村庄的公共精神令人感到温暖。
近义词: collective, collaborative, cooperative
反义词: private, individual, separate
词性: adjective
