dive-bombing:/ˈdaɪvˌbɑːmɪŋ/| n. |俯冲轰炸; 猛冲; |A sudden, swift descent, especially of an aircraft in an attack.|The pilot executed a daring dive-bombing maneuver. 飞行员执行了一次大胆的俯冲轰炸动作。|近义词：swooping, plunging|反义词:ascending, climbing|名词

dive-bomb:/ˈdaɪvˌbɑːm/| v. |俯冲轰炸; 猛扑; |To attack by making a sudden, steep descent, typically from an aircraft.|The hawk dive-bombed the unsuspecting rabbit. 老鹰俯冲攻击那只毫无防备的兔子。|近义词：swoop down on, pounce on|反义词:ascend, climb|动词

词源：dive + bomb.  "dive" 指的是快速下降的动作, "bomb" 指的是炸弹.  这个词最初用于描述飞机的攻击方式，后来也用于描述鸟类和其他动物类似的快速俯冲动作.
