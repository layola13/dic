freesia:/ˈfriːʒə/ | n. | 小苍兰，香雪兰 | A fragrant South African plant with cream, yellow, pink, or purplish flowers. |  例句：The freesia's sweet scent filled the room. 香雪兰的甜香充满了整个房间。

例句：She received a bouquet of freesias for her birthday. 她生日那天收到了一束香雪兰。 | 近义词：flower, blossom | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: Named after Friedrich Heinrich Theodor Freese (died 1876), a German physician.
