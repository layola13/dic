forpine:/fɔːrˈpaɪn/| v.|渴望，苦苦思念（尤指失去的东西）|To yearn intensely, especially for something lost or unattainable.|She forpined for her lost love. 她苦苦思念着她失去的爱人。|近义词：yearn, long, pine, grieve|反义词:rejoice, celebrate|词性:动词

词源:  From "for-" (intensive prefix) + "pine" (to waste away with longing).  "For-" adds emphasis to the sense of longing and suffering involved in pining.
