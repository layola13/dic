lop's:/lɒps/ | n. |  lop的复数形式，指修剪下来的枝条; (非正式)兔子的耳朵 | Lop's refer to the branches cut off from a tree, especially those removed by pruning. It can also informally refer to a rabbit's ears. | The gardener gathered the lops and put them in the compost bin. 园丁把修剪下来的枝条收集起来放进堆肥箱里。 |The rabbit's lops drooped sadly. 兔子的耳朵悲伤地垂了下来。 | 近义词: clippings, prunings, (for rabbit ears) ears | 反义词: 无 | 词性: 名词
