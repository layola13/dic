keeldragging:/ˈkiːldræɡɪŋ/
n.
强行拖延，阻挠
The act of deliberately delaying or obstructing progress.
The senator is accused of keeldragging the bill.  这位参议员被指控阻挠该法案。
stonewalling, obstructing, delaying
expediting, facilitating
noun
