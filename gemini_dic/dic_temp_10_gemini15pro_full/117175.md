measles:/ˈmiːzəlz/| n. |麻疹|An infectious viral disease causing fever and a red rash, typically occurring in childhood.|  She caught the measles from her classmate. 她被同学传染了麻疹。|近义词: rubeola|反义词: (无直接反义词，可用health健康来表示相对概念)|词性: noun

Etymology: Middle English *maselen*, plural of *masel* 'spot, blemish', influenced by Old English *mase* 'spot' and ultimately from West Germanic *masōn*.  The plural form is now standard.
