brecciated:/ˈbrɛʃiˌeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 角砾化的，由角砾岩组成的 | Composed of angular fragments; resembling a breccia.  A rock composed of sharp-angled fragments embedded in a fine-grained matrix. | The geologist examined the brecciated rock face.  地质学家检查了角砾化的岩壁。| The museum displayed a brecciated meteorite.  博物馆展出了一块角砾状陨石。| fragmented, angular, broken | whole, solid | adjective

breccia:/ˈbrɛtʃə/ or /ˈbrɛʃə/ | n. | 角砾岩 | A rock composed of sharp-angled fragments embedded in a fine-grained matrix. | The breccia was formed by volcanic activity.  角砾岩是由火山活动形成的。| The geologist collected a sample of breccia for analysis. 地质学家收集了一块角砾岩样本进行分析。| conglomerate, rubble |  n/a (no direct opposite for a rock type) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *breccia* "broken stones, rubble," from a Germanic source related to *brechen* "to break."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *brekan*, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰreg-* "to break."
