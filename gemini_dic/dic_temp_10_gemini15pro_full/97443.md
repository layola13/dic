infuse:/ɪnˈfjuːz/ /ɪnˈfjuːs/ | vt. | 注入，灌输，浸泡 | To fill something with a particular quality or feeling; to steep something in liquid to extract its flavor or medicinal properties. |  The tea is infused with herbs.  这茶里注入了草药。

| 近义词：instill, imbue, permeate, steep, brew | 反义词：drain, extract, remove | 词性：动词

infuse:/ɪnˈfjuːz/ /ɪnˈfjuːs/ | n. | 注入，灌输 | The act of infusing or the condition of being infused. | The infuse of new technology revitalized the industry. 新技术的注入 revitalized the industry. | 近义词：instillation, imbuement, permeation | 反义词：extraction, removal | 词性：名词
