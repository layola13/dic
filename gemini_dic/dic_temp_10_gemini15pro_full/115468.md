manufactured: /ˌmænjuˈfæktʃərd/ | adj. | 制造的，人造的 | Made in a factory, by hand, or by other means; produced by processing raw materials. |  例句：The car is manufactured in Germany. 这辆车是在德国制造的。

例句：These toys are manufactured from recycled plastic. 这些玩具是用回收塑料制成的。

近义词：produced, fabricated, created, made | 反义词：natural, handmade (in some contexts) | 词性：形容词

manufacture: /ˌmænjuˈfæktʃər/ | n. | 制造，制造业 | The process of making something, especially on a large scale using machinery. | 例句：The manufacture of cars is a major industry in this country. 汽车制造业是这个国家的主要产业。

例句：The company specializes in the manufacture of high-quality furniture. 这家公司专门生产高品质家具。


manufacture: /ˌmænjuˈfæktʃər/ | v. | 制造，生产 | To make something, especially on a large scale using machinery. | 例句：They manufacture a wide range of electronic goods. 他们生产各种电子产品。

例句：The company manufactures its products in China. 这家公司在中国生产产品。

近义词：produce, fabricate, create, make | 反义词：destroy, dismantle | 词性：名词, 动词

词源：From Middle French *manufacturer*, from Latin manu factus 'made by hand', past participle of manu facere, from manus 'hand' + facere 'to make'.
