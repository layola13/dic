canceleer: /ˌkænsəˈlɪr/ | v. | 取消，撤销 | To revoke or annul something that was previously arranged or planned.  |  The concert was cancelled due to bad weather. 音乐会因为天气不好被取消了。

The meeting was cancelled because the CEO was ill.  会议因为CEO生病被取消了。 | Synonyms:  abort, annul, revoke, rescind, withdraw | Antonyms:  approve, authorize, confirm, establish, validate | Verb

词源: From Old French *canceler, from Latin cancellare "to make crossbars, cross out," from cancelli "lattice work."

注意事项:  虽然"canceleer"看起来像西班牙语动词，但它实际上不是一个标准的西班牙语单词。正确的西班牙语动词是"cancelar".
