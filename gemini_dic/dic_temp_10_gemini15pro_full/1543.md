achenium:/əˈkiːniəm/
n.
瘦果
A small, dry, one-seeded fruit that does not open to release the seed.
The dandelion's seeds are dispersed by wind, each carried on a tiny achenium. 蒲公英的种子随风飘散，每一颗都长在一个小瘦果上。

The sunflower produces achenes, commonly known as sunflower seeds. 向日葵产生瘦果，通常被称为葵花籽。
近义词: cypsela, seed
反义词: (No direct antonym for a botanical term like achenium)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From New Latin achenium, from Ancient Greek ἀ (a-, 'not') + χαίνειν (khaínein, 'to gape open').  So literally, "not gaping open."  This refers to the fruit not splitting open to release the seed.
