orthis:/ˈɔːrθɪs/| n.|正形贝；腕足类动物的一种|An extinct brachiopod of the order Orthida, common as a fossil in Palaeozoic rocks, characterized by a hinged shell with a prominent beak.|Example: Orthis fossils are frequently found in Ordovician rocks.  正形贝化石经常在奥陶纪岩石中被发现。
Example:  The orthis is a useful index fossil for dating Paleozoic strata. 正形贝是一种用于确定古生代地层年代的有用标准化石。|近义词:brachiopod|反义词:None (It's a specific genus, not a concept with an opposite)|词性:noun
