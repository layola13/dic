buzziest:/ˈbʌziɪst/
adjective
最热闹的，最受关注的
Generating the most excitement, interest, or discussion.
The buzziest new restaurant in town is always packed.  这个城里最热闹的新餐厅总是挤满了人。
The buzziest topic on social media right now is the upcoming election.  目前社交媒体上最热门的话题是即将到来的选举。
synonyms:  hottest, trendiest, most popular, in demand
antonyms:  dullest, least interesting, unpopular, obscure
词性: adjective
