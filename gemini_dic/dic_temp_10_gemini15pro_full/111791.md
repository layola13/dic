loftinesses:/ˈlɔːftɪnɪsɪz/
n.
崇高，高尚；高傲，傲慢
The state of being lofty, either literally (great height) or figuratively (noble, elevated in character or style).
He spoke of the loftinesses of human aspiration. 他谈到了人类崇高理想。
近义词：nobleness, grandeur, sublimity, hauteur, arrogance
反义词：humility, modesty, lowliness, baseness
