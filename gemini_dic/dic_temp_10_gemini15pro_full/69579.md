felsitic:/fɛlˈsɪtɪk/ | adj. | 霏细岩的; 霏细质的 | Relating to or consisting of felsite, a fine-grained igneous rock. | The geologist examined the felsitic texture of the rock sample.  地质学家检查了岩石样本的霏细结构。| The cliff face was composed of felsitic porphyry. 悬崖表面由霏细斑岩构成。 | 近义词: aphanitic, fine-grained | 反义词: phaneritic, coarse-grained | 词性: 形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From felsite + -ic.  Felsite from German Felsit, from Fels "rock" + -it. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *felisaz.
