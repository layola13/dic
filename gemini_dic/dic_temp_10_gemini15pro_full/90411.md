PHRASE->
Howzit going? =  怎么样？/ 你好吗？
Howzit bru? = 你好，兄弟？ (South African slang)
Howzit, my china? = 你好，朋友？(South African slang, "china" means friend)
Howzit ek sê? = 你好，我说？ (Afrikaans influenced greeting)
Howzit daar? =  你好吗？ (Afrikaans influenced greeting, "daar" means there)


注意事项:
"Howzit" is very informal and primarily used in South African English.  Using it in other English-speaking regions might be misunderstood or sound unusual.
