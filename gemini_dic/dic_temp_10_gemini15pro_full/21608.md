blunger:/ˈblʌndʒər/| n.|搅拌机，混合器|A machine with rotating paddles used for mixing slip (liquid clay) in pottery.|The blunger mixed the clay and water into a smooth slip. 搅拌机将黏土和水混合成光滑的泥浆。|Synonyms: mixer, agitator, blender|Antonyms: separator|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "blunge," meaning "to mix or blend," likely of imitative origin.
