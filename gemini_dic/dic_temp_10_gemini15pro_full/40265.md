continue:/kənˈtɪnjuː/ (US), /kənˈtɪnjuː/ (UK)| v.|继续，持续，延伸|To persist in an activity or process; to resume after interruption.|例句：The rain continued to fall all day. 雨持续下了一整天。|近义词：persist, proceed, resume, last, endure, carry on|反义词:stop, cease, halt, discontinue|verb

continue:/kənˈtɪnjuː/ (US), /kənˈtɪnjuː/ (UK)| adj.|继续的，持续的 (less common)|Extending or prolonged without interruption.|例句：There was a continue flow of traffic on the highway. 公路上车辆川流不息。（这个用法比较少见，通常用 continuous）|近义词：continuous, ongoing, uninterrupted|反义词:interrupted, discontinuous|adjective
