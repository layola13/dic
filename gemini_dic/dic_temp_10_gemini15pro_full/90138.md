hotchpot:/ˈhɒtʃpɒt/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtʃpɑːt/ (US)| n.|大杂烩；混合物；（尤指法律）共同财产；|A confused mixture of different things; a jumble. In law, especially the blending of properties for inheritance purposes.|The inheritance was thrown into hotchpot and divided equally among the heirs. 遗产被混在一起，然后平均分配给继承人。|近义词: mixture, medley, jumble, blend, mingle, amalgamation|反义词: separation, division|词性:名词
