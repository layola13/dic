leary:/ˈlɪəri/| adj.|谨慎的，怀疑的，不信任的|Suspicious, wary, distrustful; hesitant or reluctant to do something.|例句：I'm a little leary of investing in that company. 我对投资那家公司有点谨慎。|例句：She was leary of strangers. 她对陌生人不信任。|近义词：wary, cautious, suspicious, skeptical, distrustful|反义词：trusting, confident, gullible|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Irish Gaelic word *learaí* meaning "watchful, alert," ultimately from *lear* meaning "sea, ocean."  The original sense referred to sailors being watchful of the sea, and this sense of watchfulness evolved into cautiousness and distrust.
