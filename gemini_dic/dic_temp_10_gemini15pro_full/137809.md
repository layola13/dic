nonverminousness:/ˌnɒnvɜːˈmɪnəsnəs/
noun
无害性；非害虫性
The state or quality of not being verminous; freedom from vermin.
The nonverminousness of the newly renovated apartment was a major selling point.  这套新装修的公寓的无虫害性是一个主要的卖点。
The inspector confirmed the nonverminousness of the grain silo. 检查员确认了粮仓里没有害虫。
近义词:cleanliness, hygiene, purity
反义词:verminousness, infestation, pestilence
词性:noun

Etymology:
From non- + verminous + -ness.  "Verminous" comes from the Old French "vermine" (vermin) and ultimately from the Latin "vermis" (worm).
