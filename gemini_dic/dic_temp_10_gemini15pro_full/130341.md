non-transparency:/ˌnɒn trænsˈpærənsi/ | n. | 不透明性；缺乏透明度 |  Lack of transparency refers to the concealment or withholding of information, making it difficult for others to see or understand the truth or the processes involved. |  The non-transparency of the government's decision-making process led to public distrust. 政府决策过程的不透明导致公众不信任。

The non-transparency of the company's financial dealings raised concerns among investors. 公司财务交易的不透明性引起了投资者的担忧。| 近义词：opacity, opaqueness | 反义词：transparency, clarity | 词性：名词
