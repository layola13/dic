corrugator:/ˈkɔːrəɡeɪtər/ (US), /ˈkɒrəɡeɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |皱肌；波纹板机|A muscle that wrinkles or contracts a part of the body, especially a muscle that draws the eyebrows together. A machine used to make corrugated cardboard or other materials.|The corrugator supercilii muscle is responsible for frowning.  皱眉肌负责皱眉。|The factory uses a high-speed corrugator to produce cardboard boxes.  这家工厂使用高速波纹板机生产纸板箱。|synonym: wrinkle, furrower, contractor|antonym: relaxer, smoother|noun
