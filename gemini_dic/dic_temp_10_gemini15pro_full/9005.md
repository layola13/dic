antilogical:/ˌæntɪˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
反逻辑的；不合逻辑的
Contrary to logic; illogical.
His argument was completely antilogical and made no sense. 他的论点完全不合逻辑，毫无意义。

The politician's antilogical stance on the issue confused many voters.  这位政治家在这个问题上的反逻辑立场让许多选民感到困惑。

近义词: illogical, irrational, absurd, unreasonable
反义词: logical, rational, reasonable, sensible
词性: adjective
