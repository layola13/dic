glassier:/ˈɡlæsiər/ /ˈɡlæsiər/ | adj. | 更光滑的，更像玻璃的，更冷淡的 | More like glass; smoother; more icy or cold in manner. | The lake was glassier than usual this morning. 今早湖面比平时更平静。| Synonyms: smoother, slicker, icier | Antonyms: rougher, coarser, warmer | adjective

glassier:/ˈɡlæsiər/ /ˈɡlæsiər/ | adj. | 更冷淡的，更冷漠的 |  More cold and unfriendly in manner. | Her expression became glassier as she listened to the news. 她听到这个消息后，表情变得更加冷漠。| Synonyms: colder, more distant, more aloof | Antonyms: warmer, friendlier, more approachable | adjective
