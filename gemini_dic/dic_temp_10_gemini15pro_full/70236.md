fico:/ˈfiːkoʊ/ (US) /ˈfaɪkəʊ/ (UK)| n. | FICO信用评分 | FICO score, a credit score developed by the Fair Isaac Corporation (FICO). It is used by lenders to assess credit risk. | Example:  My FICO score dropped after I missed a credit card payment. 我的FICO评分在我错过一次信用卡付款后下降了。
