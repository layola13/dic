deprive:/dɪˈpraɪv/| vt.|剥夺，使丧失|To take something away from someone, especially something important or necessary.|The new law deprives them of their right to vote. 新法律剥夺了他们的投票权。|
synonyms: dispossess, strip, rob, bereave|antonyms: provide, grant, endow, give|verb

deprive:/dɪˈpraɪv/| vt.|剥夺，使丧失|To prevent someone from having or enjoying something.|He was deprived of sleep by the noise. 噪音使他无法入睡。|synonyms: deny, refuse, withhold|antonyms: offer, allow, permit|verb
