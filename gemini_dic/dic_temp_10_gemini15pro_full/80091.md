goatsucker:/ˈɡoʊtsʌkər/| n. |夜鹰|A nocturnal bird with a wide mouth, traditionally believed to suck the milk of goats.|例句：The goatsucker is a master of camouflage. 夜鹰是伪装大师。

例句：Goatsuckers are known for their distinctive night calls.  夜鹰以其独特的夜间鸣叫而闻名。|近义词：nighthawk, nightjar|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 diurnal birds - 昼行鸟)|词性:noun
