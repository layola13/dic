outspun:/ˌaʊtˈspʌn/
verb
纺出，拉出（线）；胜过，超过（在讲述故事或编造谎言方面）
drawn out (by spinning); surpassed in storytelling or fabrication
The spider outspun a delicate web.  蜘蛛纺出了一张精美的网。
He outspun his rivals with a tale of daring adventure. 他用一个大胆冒险的故事胜过了他的对手。

past participle of outspin
