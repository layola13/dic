nonviscous:/nɒnˈvɪskəs/
adjective
非粘性的; 不粘稠的
Having low or no viscosity; not thick or sticky.
The liquid flowed easily through the pipe because it was nonviscous.  这种液体很容易流过管道，因为它是非粘性的。
The nonviscous oil quickly lubricated the gears. 非粘性油迅速润滑了齿轮。
近义词:thin, runny, fluid
反义词:viscous, thick, sticky
词性:adjective
词源:non- (not) + viscous (sticky, thick)
