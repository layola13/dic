counter-motion:/ˈkaʊntərˈmoʊʃən/ |n.|反向运动，对抗运动|An opposing or opposite motion; a motion made in resistance to another. | The swimmer's counter-motion propelled him forward against the current. 游泳者的反向运动推动他在逆流中前进。

The lawyer filed a counter-motion to the plaintiff's request. 律师对原告的请求提出了反诉。|近义词: countermove, reaction, opposition |反义词: motion, action, initiative |词性: 名词
