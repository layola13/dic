nincompoopery:/ˌnɪŋkəmˈpuːpəri/
n.
愚蠢的行为；胡闹
foolish or silly behavior
His latest investment was an act of sheer nincompoopery. 他最近的投资纯属愚蠢行为。

近义词:foolishness, stupidity, silliness, absurdity
反义词:wisdom, intelligence, sense, rationality
词性:noun
