host:/hoʊst/ (US) /həʊst/ (UK)| n. vt. vi.|主人，主持人，主办方，大量，主办|A person who receives or entertains other people as guests.|例句：He was a gracious host at the party. 他在聚会上是一位和蔼可亲的主人。|例句：She hosted a dinner party for her friends. 她为朋友们举办了一个晚宴。|近义词：entertainer, emcee, landlord, owner|反义词:guest, visitor|noun, verb

host:/hoʊst/ (US) /həʊst/ (UK)| n. vt. vi.|寄主，宿主|An animal or plant on or in which a parasite or commensal organism lives.|例句：The mosquito is the host for the malaria parasite. 蚊子是疟疾寄生虫的宿主。|例句：The oak tree is a host to many different species of insects. 橡树是许多不同种类昆虫的宿主。|近义词：carrier|反义词:parasite|noun, verb
