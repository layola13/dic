discernment:/dɪˈsɜːrnmənt/| n. |识别力，洞察力，判断力|The ability to judge well, especially to recognize the quality of something.|She showed great discernment in her choice of friends. 她选择朋友很有眼光。|近义词：judgment, insight, perception, discrimination, acumen|反义词：ignorance, naivety, blindness|noun

discern:/dɪˈsɜːrn/| v. |辨别，识别，察觉|To recognize or understand something, especially something that is not obvious.|I could discern a faint light in the distance. 我能辨认出远处微弱的灯光。|近义词：perceive, detect, distinguish, recognize|反义词：miss, overlook|verb
