non-zionists:/ˌnɒnˈzaɪənɪsts/ | n. | 非犹太复国主义者 |  People who do not subscribe to Zionism, the national movement for the return of the Jewish people to their historical homeland and the establishment of a Jewish state.  |  Many non-Zionists are still supportive of the existence of Israel. 许多非犹太复国主义者仍然支持以色列的存在。|  Non-Zionists believe there are other solutions to the Palestinian-Israeli conflict. 非犹太复国主义者认为巴以冲突还有其他解决方案。 |  anti-Zionists, critics of Zionism | Zionists | noun
