dwarf:/dwɔːrf/ (UK), /dwɔːrf/ (US)| n. adj. v. |侏儒，矮子；矮小的|A person, animal, or plant that is much smaller than the usual size of its kind. Also used as an adjective to describe something that is smaller than the usual size. As a verb, it means to cause to appear small or insignificant by comparison.|
例句：Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs. 白雪公主和七个小矮人。

例句：The bonsai tree is a dwarfed version of a full-sized tree. 盆景树是全尺寸树木的矮化版本。

例句：The new skyscraper dwarfs all the other buildings in the city. 这座新的摩天大楼使城市里的所有其他建筑物都相形见绌。

近义词：midget, pygmy, miniature, tiny, small|
反义词：giant, enormous, huge, immense|
词性: noun, adjective, verb
