nematognath:/ˌnɛməˈtɒɡnæθ/ |n.|鲶形目|A large order of mostly freshwater ray-finned fishes, including the catfishes.|The nematognath comprises a diverse group of fishes.|近义词: catfish (common name for many members), siluriformes (synonym) |反义词: None (taxonomic term, doesn't have direct opposites)|词性: Noun

Etymology:
From Greek nema, nemat- "thread" + gnathos "jaw" referring to the barbels around the mouth of many species.
