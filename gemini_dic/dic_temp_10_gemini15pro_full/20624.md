blacklegged:/ˌblækˈleɡɪd/| adj.|黑腿的|Having black legs.  This term is often used to describe certain species of ticks and birds.|例句：The blacklegged tick is a known carrier of Lyme disease. 黑腿蜱是莱姆病的已知携带者。

例句：We saw a blacklegged kittiwake on our birdwatching trip.  我们在观鸟之旅中看到了一只黑腿三趾鸥。|近义词：dark-legged|反义词:pale-legged|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From black + legged.  "Legged" is a suffix used to form adjectives describing having a particular type or number of legs.  "Black" refers to the dark color of the legs.
