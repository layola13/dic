differentiating:/ˌdɪfəˈrenʃiˌeɪtɪŋ/| adj. v.|区分的，微分的；区别，求微分|Present participle of differentiate.  The process of identifying the differences between similar things.|例句：Differentiating between the two species can be challenging. 区分这两个物种可能具有挑战性。|近义词：distinguishing, discerning, discriminating|反义词：confusing, combining, integrating|词性: Verb (present participle), Adjective

differentiate:/ˌdɪfəˈrenʃiˌeɪt/| v.|区分，区别；求微分|To recognize or show that two things are not the same; To calculate the derivative of a function.|例句：It's important to differentiate between facts and opinions. 区分事实和观点很重要。|近义词：distinguish, discern, discriminate|反义词：confuse, combine, integrate|词性: Verb

Etymology:
From Late Latin differentiatus, past participle of differentiare "to make different," from Latin different-, differens, present participle of differre "to set apart, differ," from dis- "apart" + ferre "to carry, bear."
