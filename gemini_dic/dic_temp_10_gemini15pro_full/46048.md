cyclicly: /ˈsaɪklɪkli/ | adv. | 循环地，周期性地 | In a cyclic manner; occurring in cycles. | The seasons change cyclicly. 季节循环变化。| The machine operates cyclicly, repeating the same process over and over.  这台机器循环运转，一遍又一遍地重复同样的过程。 | 近义词: periodically, regularly, recurrently | 反义词: erratically, randomly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From cycle + -ly. "Cycle" comes from the Latin "cyclus" and the Greek "kyklos," meaning "circle" or "wheel." The suffix "-ly" transforms the noun "cycle" into an adverb.
