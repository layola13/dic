gardenia:/ɡɑːrˈdiːniə/ /ɡɑːrˈdinjə/| n. |栀子花；栀子属植物|A tropical evergreen shrub or small tree of the madder family, cultivated for its fragrant white or yellow flowers.|例句：The gardenia's sweet scent filled the air. 栀子花的甜香弥漫在空气中。

例句：She wore a gardenia in her hair. 她头发上戴着一朵栀子花。|近义词：jasmine, cape jasmine|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑与其香味或颜色相反的花卉) |词性:noun
