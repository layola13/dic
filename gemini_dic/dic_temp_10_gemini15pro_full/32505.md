chinoidine:/ˌkaɪnɔɪˈdiːn/| n. |金鸡纳碱；非结晶奎宁|A brownish amorphous residue obtained during the extraction of quinine and other alkaloids from cinchona bark, containing various quinoline derivatives.|Chinoidine was once used as a cheaper alternative to quinine in treating malaria.|近义词：quinine substitute, cinchona alkaloid residue|反义词: None|noun
