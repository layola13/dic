ibises:/ˈaɪbɪsiːz/
noun
朱鹭（ibis 的复数形式）
Plural form of ibis, a long-legged wading bird with a long down-curved bill.
例句：A flock of ibises flew overhead. 一群朱鹭从头顶飞过。

例句：The ibises were wading in the shallow water. 朱鹭在浅水中涉水。

近义词：wading birds, shorebirds
反义词：(No specific antonym for a type of bird)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin ibis, from Greek ἶβις (ibis), ultimately of Egyptian origin.
