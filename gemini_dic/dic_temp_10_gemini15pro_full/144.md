abase:/əˈbeɪs/| vt.|降低，贬低，使卑微|To lower in rank, prestige, or esteem; to humble. |He abased himself before the king, begging for forgiveness. 他在国王面前卑躬屈膝，乞求宽恕。

He abased himself by lying to his parents. 他因对父母撒谎而自贬身份。|近义词：humiliate, degrade, demean, lower|反义词：exalt, elevate, ennoble|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *abesser*, from *a-* (from Latin *ad-*) meaning "to, toward" + *basser* meaning "to lower," from Late Latin *bassare*, from *bassus* meaning "low." Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gʷadʰ-*.
