coenosarc:/ˈsiːnəʊsɑːrk/ | n. | 共肉 | The common, fleshy tissue connecting the polyps in a hydroid or coral colony. | The coenosarc allows nutrients to be shared throughout the coral colony.  珊瑚群体的共肉允许营养物质在整个群体中共享。 |  近义词: living tissue, connecting tissue | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly "individual polyp") | noun


coenosarc:/ˈsiːnəʊsɑːrk/ | n. | 共肉体 |  The continuous living substance of a colonial organism, such as a coral or hydrozoan, that connects and supports the individual polyps or zooids. | The coenosarc is essential for the integrated functioning of the coral colony. 共肉体对于珊瑚群体的整体运作至关重要。 | 近义词: protoplasm, cytoplasm | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | noun
