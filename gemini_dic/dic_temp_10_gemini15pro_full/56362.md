doing: /ˈduːɪŋ/ | v. | 做，进行，表现 | present participle of *do* |  I am doing my homework. 我正在做我的家庭作业。 | 近义词: performing, acting, executing | 反义词: abstaining, ceasing, refraining | verb

Etymology:  From Middle English *doinge*,  from Old English *dōung*, verbal noun from *dōn* (“to do”). Cognate with West Frisian *doen*, Dutch *doen*, German *tun*, Gothic *𐍄𐌰𐌿𐌾𐌰𐌽* (taujan).

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I do my homework every day.  我每天都做家庭作业。
He does his exercises in the morning. 他早上做运动。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
She is doing her best. 她正在尽力。
They are doing a science experiment. 他们正在做一个科学实验。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have done all the housework. 我已经做了所有的家务。
She has done a great job. 她做得很好。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
We have been doing this project for months.  我们几个月来一直在做这个项目。
He has been doing research on this topic. 他一直在研究这个课题。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

They had done everything they could. 他们已经尽力了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
