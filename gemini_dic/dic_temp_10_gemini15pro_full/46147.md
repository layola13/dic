cymose:/ˈsaɪmoʊs/| adj.|聚伞状的，聚伞花序的|A cymose inflorescence is one in which the main axis ends in a flower, and further growth continues by lateral branches which also end in flowers.|The plant has a cymose inflorescence.  这种植物的花序是聚伞状的。|近义词：determinate, centrifugal|反义词：racemose, indeterminate|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek *κῦμα (kûma)  "wave, swelling" + -ose.  The term refers to the wave-like or swelling appearance of a cymose inflorescence, as the central flower blooms first and then subsequent flowers bloom outward on lateral branches.
