oversway:/ˌoʊvərˈsweɪ/| v. | 支配；控制；影响；使动摇| To exert a controlling or decisive influence on; to prevail over. |  His emotions overswayed his judgment. 他的情绪左右了他的判断。

Her desire for revenge overswayed her common sense. 她复仇的欲望压倒了她的理智。| 近义词: influence, dominate, control, govern, rule | 反义词: submit, yield, obey | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + sway.  "Over-" acts as a prefix indicating "excessively" or "completely." "Sway" comes from the Old Norse "sveigja" meaning "to bend, swing."  Thus, "oversway" literally means to bend or swing completely to one side, implying dominance or control.
