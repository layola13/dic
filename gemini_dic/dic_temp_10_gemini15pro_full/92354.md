hypocarpium:/ˌhaɪpəˈkɑːrpiəm/
n.
果托，花托
A fleshy enlarged floral receptacle that becomes part of the fruit, as in the cashew.
The cashew's hypocarpium swells and becomes the cashew "apple." 腰果的果托膨胀，变成了腰果“苹果”。
The strawberry is an aggregate fruit with the fleshy part derived from the receptacle or hypocarpium. 草莓是一种聚合果，其肉质部分来源于花托或果托。
近义词: receptacle, torus
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, as it's a specific botanical term)
词性: noun
