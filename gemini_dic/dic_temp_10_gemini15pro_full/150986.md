paramnesic: /ˌpærəmˈniːzɪk/ /ˌpærəmˈniːsik/ | adj. | 记忆错误的，记忆混乱的 | Relating to or characterized by paramnesia, a distortion of memory in which fact and fantasy are confused. |  He experienced paramnesic episodes where he believed he had been to places he had only read about. 他经历了记忆混乱的插曲，他相信自己去过那些他只在书中读到过的地方。| Example: The patient's paramnesic symptoms included confusing dreams with reality.  病人的记忆错误症状包括把梦境和现实混淆。| Synonyms:  confabulatory, delusional, distorted | Antonyms: accurate, clear, factual | adjective

paramnesia: /ˌpærəmˈniːʒə/ /ˌpærəmˈniːziə/ | n. | 记忆错误，记忆混乱 | A distortion of memory in which fact and fantasy are confused. | She suffered from paramnesia and couldn't distinguish between real events and imagined ones. 她患有记忆混乱，无法区分真实事件和想象事件。 | Example: Paramnesia can be a symptom of certain neurological disorders. 记忆混乱可能是某些神经系统疾病的症状。| Synonyms:  false memory, distorted memory, confabulation | Antonyms: accurate memory, true memory | noun
