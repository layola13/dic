meagrely:/ˈmiːɡərli/| adv.|贫乏地，不足地|In a small or insufficient quantity; sparingly.  Characterized by a lack of richness, fullness, or desirable qualities.|He ate meagrely, only consuming a small piece of bread. 他吃得很少，只吃了一小块面包。|Synonyms: sparingly, scantily, sparsely, modestly, insufficiently|Antonyms: abundantly, lavishly, generously, plentifully|Adverb
