empale:/ɪmˈpeɪl/| vt.| 刺穿，穿透| To pierce with a sharp stake or point; to impale a beetle on a pin.  用尖桩或尖头刺穿；把甲虫钉在大头针上。| The defeated rebels were empaled on stakes as a warning to others.  被击败的叛军被钉在木桩上以儆效尤。|pierce, spear, transfix| - | verb
Etymology: From Old French *empailer, from en- "in" + paiel "straw," from Latin palea. Originally meant "to put on a stake," later evolved to mean "to pierce with a stake." 源自古法语 *empailer，来自 en- “在” + paiel “稻草”，来自拉丁语 palea。最初意思是“放在木桩上”，后来演变成“用木桩刺穿”的意思。
