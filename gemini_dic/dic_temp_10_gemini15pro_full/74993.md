fried:/fraɪd/ | adj. |油炸的；炒的| Cooked in hot fat or oil.|  例句：I love fried chicken. 我喜欢炸鸡。|例句：We had fried rice for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了炒饭。| 近义词：sautéed, roasted | 反义词：boiled, steamed, raw |词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *frien*, from Old English *frīġan* (“to fry”), from Proto-Germanic *frijjaną* (“to fry”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)prekʷ- (“to crackle, fry”). Cognate with West Frisian *frie* (“to fry”), Dutch *frieten*, German *fritieren* (“to deep-fry”).
