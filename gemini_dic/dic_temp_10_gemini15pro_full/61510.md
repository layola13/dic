embroidery:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdəri/
noun|刺绣；绣花；刺绣品|The art or process of decorating fabric or other materials using a needle to apply thread or yarn. It can also refer to the finished product.|Her dress featured delicate embroidery. 她的裙子上有着精致的刺绣。|近义词: needlework, needlepoint|反义词: plainness
word origin: From Middle English embrowderie, from Old French embroderie, from embroder “to embroider,” from en- “in, on” + broder “to border, edge,” from bord “border.”
