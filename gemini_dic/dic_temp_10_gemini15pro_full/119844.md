mildewy:/ˈmɪlˌdjuːi/ | adj. | 发霉的；有霉味的 | Covered with or smelling of mildew; musty. | The old books in the basement smelled mildewy. 地下室里的旧书有霉味。 | The bread had a mildewy smell and had to be thrown away. 面包有霉味，不得不扔掉。 | 近义词: musty, moldy, damp, fusty | 反义词: fresh, clean, dry | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *myldew + -y, from Old English mildeaw “honeydew, mildew” (akin to Old High German miliuto “honeydew”) + -ig.
