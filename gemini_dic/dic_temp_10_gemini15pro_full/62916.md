enlivening:/ɪnˈlaɪvənɪŋ/
adjective
使人愉快的；使生动的；使活泼的
Making something more interesting, lively, or cheerful.
The music was enlivening and made everyone want to dance.  这音乐令人振奋，让每个人都想跳舞。
The speaker told some jokes to enliven his presentation. 演讲者讲了一些笑话来活跃他的演讲。
synonyms: animating, invigorating, stimulating, exciting, cheering
antonyms: dulling, boring, depressing, tedious, monotonous
词性: 形容词
