nasalising:/ˈneɪzəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使鼻音化；从鼻腔发音
The process of producing a sound by allowing air to escape through the nose, either partially or completely.
He was nasalising his vowels, making him sound congested. 他说话时元音鼻音化，听起来像是鼻塞了。
nasalizing (American English)
Making a sound nasal.
She was nasalizing her speech because of a cold. 她因为感冒而说话带鼻音。
近义词: nasalizing, twanging
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider "oralizing" as a conceptual opposite.)
词性: verb (present participle)
