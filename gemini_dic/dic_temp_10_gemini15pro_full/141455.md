opine:/oʊˈpaɪn/| v. |表达观点；想；认为|to express an opinion|例句：He opined that the movie was overrated.  他认为这部电影被高估了。

例句：She opined on the current political situation. 她对当前的政治局势发表了看法。|近义词：comment, suggest, declare, assert, maintain|反义词：withhold opinion, remain silent|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin opinari (“to think, suppose, judge, be of opinion”), from Proto-Italic *opīnārī, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁ep- (“to work, produce in abundance”).
