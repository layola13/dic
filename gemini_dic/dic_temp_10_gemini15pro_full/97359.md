infracted:/ɪnˈfræktɪd/
verb
违反，违背
Broken, violated, transgressed.  A rule or law has been disobeyed.
He infracted the company's policy by sharing confidential information. 他因泄露机密信息而违反了公司政策。
Synonyms: violated, breached, broken, transgressed, disobeyed
Antonyms: obeyed, followed, complied with, adhered to
词性: verb
