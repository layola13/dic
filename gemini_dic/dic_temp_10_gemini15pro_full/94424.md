implies:/ɪmˈplaɪz/| v.|暗示，意味着，包含|The high price implies a good quality. 高昂的价格暗示着良好的品质。|suggest, indicate, mean|infer, deny|verb
Etymology->
From Late Latin implicare, from Latin in- (“in, into”) + plicare (“to fold”).  Originally meaning "to enfold, entangle, involve," the sense of "suggest, hint at" developed later.
