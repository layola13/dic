garum:/ˈɡɑːrəm/ | n. |鱼酱，鱼露|A fermented fish sauce used as a condiment in ancient Roman and Greek cuisine. It was made by fermenting the entrails of fish, especially mackerel, with salt and herbs. |例句：Garum was a staple condiment in ancient Rome.  鱼酱是古罗马的主食调味品。

|例句：The recipe called for a generous amount of garum.  这个食谱需要大量的鱼酱。|近义词：fish sauce, liquamen |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
