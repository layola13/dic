misconceiving:/ˌmɪskənˈsiːvɪŋ/
verb
误解，错误地理解
incorrectly interpreting or understanding something.
She was misconceiving his actions as hostile when he was just trying to help. 她误解了他的行为，以为他是敌意的，而他只是想帮忙。
misinterpreting, misunderstanding, misconstruing, misapprehending
correctly understanding, comprehending
verb
