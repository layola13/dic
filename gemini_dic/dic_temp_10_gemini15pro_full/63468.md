entrochal:/ˈɛntrəʊkəl/ | adj. | 海百合的；由海百合化石组成的 | Composed of, or resembling, the petrified stems of crinoids (sea lilies). | The cliff face was covered in entrochal limestone.  悬崖表面覆盖着海百合灰岩。| Synonym: crinoidal | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-crinoidal") | adjective

entrochal:/ˈɛntrəʊkəl/ | n. | 海百合茎化石 | A fossilized stem segment of a crinoid. |  The geologist found several entrochals scattered amongst the rocks. 地质学家在岩石中发现了散落的海百合茎化石。| Synonym: crinoid stem, columnal | Antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun
