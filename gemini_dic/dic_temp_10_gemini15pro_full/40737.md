conveyancer:/kənˈveɪənsər/ | n. | 房产转让人，过户律师 | A lawyer who specializes in the legal aspects of buying and selling property. |  例句：The conveyancer is preparing the documents for the sale. 房产转让人正在准备出售文件。

例句：We hired a conveyancer to handle the purchase of our new house.  我们聘请了一位过户律师来处理我们新房的购买事宜。| 近义词：property lawyer, real estate lawyer | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From convey + -er. Convey comes from Anglo-French conveier, from Old French convoier, from Late Latin *conviare, from Latin com- + via "way."
