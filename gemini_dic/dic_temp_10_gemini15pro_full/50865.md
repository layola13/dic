desensationalized:/diːˌsɛnsəˈʃənəlaɪzd/
adjective|去掉了耸动性的，不再引起轰动的|Deprived of sensational qualities; made less exciting or shocking.  The media presented a desensationalized account of the event.|近义词: toned down, understated, muted, unexaggerated, downplayed|反义词: sensationalized, exaggerated, hyped up|词性:adjective
Etymology: de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + sensationalize + -ed (past participle suffix)  From Latin 'sensatus' meaning 'perceived by the senses'.
