ondogram: /ˈɒndəɡræm/ | n. | 声波图，声谱图 | A visual representation of sound, showing its frequency and intensity over time. | Example: The ondogram showed the different frequencies present in the bird's song. 声波图显示了鸟鸣中存在的不同频率。 | Example: Scientists use ondograms to analyze the complex calls of marine mammals. 科学家使用声波图来分析海洋哺乳动物的复杂叫声。 | Synonym: sonogram, spectrogram | Antonym: N/A | noun
