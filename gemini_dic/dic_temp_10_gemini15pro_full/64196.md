equiangular:/ˌiːkwɪˈæŋɡjʊlər/
|adj.|等角的|Having all angles equal.|The architect designed an equiangular building, ensuring structural integrity.|近义词:isogonal|反义词:non-equiangular|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *aequiangulus*, from *aequus* (equal) + *angulus* (angle).
