idiomorphic:/ˌɪdɪəˈmɔːrfɪk/ | adj. | 自形的，真形的 | Having its own characteristic form; fully developed crystal form.  A crystal that has developed its characteristic external shape. | The garnet crystals in the rock are idiomorphic, showing well-defined faces.  这块岩石中的石榴石晶体是自形的，呈现出轮廓分明的晶面。 | Synonyms: euhedral, automorphic | Antonyms: anhedral, xenomorphic | adjective

idiomorphic:/ˌɪdɪəˈmɔːrfɪk/ | adj. | 自形的，真形的 | Having a distinct and well-defined crystal form, not influenced by the growth of adjacent crystals.  |  The quartz crystals are idiomorphic, indicating they formed early in the rock's crystallization sequence. 这些石英晶体是自形的，表明它们在岩石的结晶序列中形成较早。| Synonyms:  automorphic, euhedral| Antonyms: subhedral, anhedral | adjective
