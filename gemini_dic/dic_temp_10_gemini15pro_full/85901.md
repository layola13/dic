headhunter:/ˈhedˌhʌntər/| n.|猎头|A person who identifies and approaches suitable candidates employed elsewhere to fill business positions.|The headhunter called me about a job opportunity in Shanghai. 猎头打电话给我，介绍了上海的一个工作机会。|Synonyms: recruiter, executive searcher|Antonyms:  applicant, job seeker|Noun
