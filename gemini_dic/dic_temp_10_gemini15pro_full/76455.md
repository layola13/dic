gainsome:/ˈɡeɪnsəm/| adj.|有收益的，有利可图的，合算的|Producing or likely to produce a gain or profit; advantageous. |例句：It was a gainsome venture, yielding high returns. 这是一项有利可图的事业，收益很高。

例句：The farmer hoped for a gainsome harvest after the long drought.  这位农民在经历了长期干旱后希望能有一个丰收的收获季节。|近义词：profitable, lucrative, advantageous, rewarding|反义词:unprofitable, loss-making, disadvantageous|词性:形容词

词源: From 'gain' + '-some' (a suffix forming adjectives meaning "characterized by or tending to").  The suffix '-some' is related to the word 'same' and originally meant "like, similar."  Thus, 'gainsome' means "like gain," or "tending to gain."
