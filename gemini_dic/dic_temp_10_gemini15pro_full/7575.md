annotations:/ˌænəˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 注释，注解 | Notes added to a text or diagram, giving explanation or comment. |  The annotations in the margins of the old book provided valuable insights into the author's thinking.  旧书页边空白处的注释为了解作者的思想提供了宝贵的见解。 | notes, comments, remarks | omissions, deletions | noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *annotationem,  from Latin annotatio, from annotare "to mark, note," from ad- "to" + notare "to note."
