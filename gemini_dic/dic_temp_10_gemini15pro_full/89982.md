horsewood:/ˈhɔːrsˌwʊd/| n. |马蹄木；马蹄树|A North American tree (Cornus florida) having showy white or pink bracts resembling petals, small greenish flowers clustered in the center of the bracts, and scarlet fruits. Also called flowering dogwood.|The horsewood tree is blooming beautifully this spring.  马蹄木树今年春天开得很漂亮。| The horsewood's bracts are often mistaken for petals. 马蹄木的苞片经常被误认为是花瓣。 |Synonyms: flowering dogwood, boxwood, cornel|Antonyms: N/A|noun
