overinterest:/ˌəʊvərˈɪntrəst/ /ˌoʊvərˈɪntrəst/| n.|过度利益，过高的利息|An excessive amount of interest, especially in financial matters.  A state of being too interested or invested in something.|He accused the bank of charging overinterest on his loan. 他指责银行对他贷款收取过高的利息。|近义词：usury, profiteering, exorbitant interest|反义词: underinterest|noun
