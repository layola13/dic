gauchely:/ˈɡoʊʃli/| adv.|笨拙地，不自然地|In a socially awkward or clumsy way. 词源: From French gauche "left, awkward," ultimately from Frankish *wank "crooked, wrong."  The connection with "left" likely arose because left-handedness was traditionally associated with clumsiness or awkwardness.
