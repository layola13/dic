constitutor:/ˈkɒnstɪtjuːtə(r)/ | n. | 设立者，创立者 |  A person who establishes, appoints, or creates something, especially a trust or other legal entity.  | The constitutor of the trust ensured that the funds would be used for charitable purposes.  信托的设立者确保资金将用于慈善目的。 | synonyms: founder, establisher, creator, author, originator | antonyms: destroyer, abolisher, liquidator | noun

constitute:/ˈkɒnstɪtjuːt/ | v. | 组成，构成；设立，制定 | To make up or form something; to establish or create by law. |  Twelve months constitute a year. 十二个月构成一年。 The new law constitutes a major change in policy.  这项新法律构成了政策上的重大变化。 | synonyms: compose, comprise, form, establish, create, enact | antonyms: dissolve, dismantle, abolish | verb
