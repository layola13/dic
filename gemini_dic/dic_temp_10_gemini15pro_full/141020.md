ongoing:/ˈɒnɡəʊɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈɑːnɡoʊɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|持续的，进行中的|continuing; happening now|The police investigation is ongoing. 警方调查正在进行中。|The project is ongoing and should be completed by next year.  这个项目正在进行中，预计明年完成。|近义词：continuing, in progress, current, underway|反义词：finished, completed, concluded, stopped|词性:形容词

词源:  "on" (meaning continuing) + "going" (present participle of "go")
