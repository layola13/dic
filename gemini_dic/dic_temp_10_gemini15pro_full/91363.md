hydrospeed:/ˈhaɪdrəʊˌspiːd/
n.
激流板运动，激流板
A whitewater sport in which the participant navigates a river using a buoyant sled or board.
He went hydrospeeding down the rapids. 他乘着激流板冲下急流。
近义词：whitewater sledding, riverboarding
反义词: None
