insuring:/ɪnˈʃʊərɪŋ/
|v.|投保，确保，给…保险|The company is insuring its assets against theft.  这家公司正在为其资产投保防盗险。|
|v.|保证，担保| I can't insure that he will be there on time. 我不能保证他会准时到那里。|
|近义词：guaranteeing, ensuring, protecting|
|反义词： jeopardizing, endangering|
|词性：动词|
|词源：From Old French enseurer "make certain, guarantee," from en- "make" + seur "sure, secure."|
