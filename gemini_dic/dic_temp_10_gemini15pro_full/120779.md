misapprehensively:/ˌmɪsæprɪˈhɛnsɪvli/
adv.
误解地，错误地
in a way that shows you have understood something wrongly
He misapprehensively thought I was criticizing him. 他误以为我在批评他。
近义词:incorrectly, wrongly, mistakenly
反义词:correctly, accurately, rightly
词性:副词

misapprehension:/ˌmɪsæprɪˈhɛnʃən/
n.
误解，误会
a failure to understand something correctly
There seems to be some misapprehension about the arrangements. 对安排似乎有些误解。
近义词:misunderstanding, misconception, misinterpretation
反义词:understanding, comprehension
词性:名词

misapprehend:/ˌmɪsæprɪˈhɛnd/
v.
误解，误会
understand something wrongly
I think you have misapprehended my meaning. 我想你误解了我的意思。
近义词:misunderstand, misconceive, misinterpret
反义词:understand, comprehend
词性:动词


词源:
From mis- (wrongly) + apprehend (to understand).  Ultimately from Latin *prehendere* (to grasp, seize).
