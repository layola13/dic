epithalamy: /ˌɛpɪθəˈleɪmi/ | n. | 婚礼歌，结婚颂歌 | A song or poem written in honor of a bride and bridegroom. |  Example 1: The poet composed a beautiful epithalamy for his sister's wedding. 诗人为他妹妹的婚礼创作了一首美丽的婚礼歌。

Example 2:  The epithalamy celebrated the couple's love and wished them a long and happy life together. 婚礼歌颂扬了这对夫妇的爱，并祝愿他们白头偕老。 | Synonym:  nuptial song, wedding poem,  hymeneal | Antonym:  elegy, dirge | noun
