hibernacle: /ˈhaɪbərnækl/ | n. |冬眠处，藏身处，圣所|A place of shelter or refuge, especially for the winter; a sacred place. |例句：The bear retreated to its hibernacle for the winter. 熊回到它的冬眠处过冬。

|例句：The ancient temple served as a hibernacle for the community's most sacred relics. 这座古老的寺庙是社区最神圣的遗物的圣所。|近义词：shelter, refuge, sanctuary, den |反义词: exposure, vulnerability |词性:noun

Etymology:  From Late Latin *hibernaculum, from Latin hibernāre "to winter," from hiems "winter."
