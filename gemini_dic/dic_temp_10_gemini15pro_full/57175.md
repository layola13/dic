dowered:/ˈdaʊərd/
adjective
赋予的；有天赋的；有嫁妆的
provided or endowed with a quality, ability, or asset, often used in the context of marriage to refer to a dowry or natural gifts.
She was dowered with both beauty and intelligence. 她兼具美貌与智慧。
近义词:endowed, gifted, talented, blessed
反义词:deprived, lacking, bereft
词性:adjective


dowered:/ˈdaʊərd/
verb
给予嫁妆；赋予
to provide with a dowry or to endow with a gift, talent, or quality.
Nature has dowered her with a beautiful voice. 大自然赋予了她美妙的歌喉。
近义词:bestow, endow, grant, furnish
反义词:deprive, strip, divest
词性:verb (past participle)
