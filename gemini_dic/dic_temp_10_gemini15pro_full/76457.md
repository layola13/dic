gainstrive: /ɡeɪnˈstraɪv/ | v. | 努力争取；力求获得 | To strive earnestly to gain or achieve something. | He gainstrived for recognition in his field. 他努力争取在他的领域获得认可。 | She gainstrived to improve her skills. 她努力提高自己的技能。 | 近义词: strive, endeavor, struggle, toil | 反义词: surrender, yield, give up | 词性: 动词


词源:
"Gainstrive" is a combination of "gain" and "strive". "Gain" comes from Old North French "gaigner" (to cultivate, acquire, win), ultimately from Frankish "*waidanjan" (to pasture, hunt, acquire). "Strive" comes from Old French "estriver" (to quarrel, dispute, contend), ultimately from Frankish "*strijan" (to fight, argue).
