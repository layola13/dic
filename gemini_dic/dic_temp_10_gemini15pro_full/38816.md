condylopod:/ˈkɒndɪləpɒd/ (US) /kənˈdaɪləpɑːd/| n. |髁足动物，节肢动物|An arthropod, especially an insect or crustacean, having jointed appendages.  Specifically, an animal with walking legs in which a single proximal segment is followed by the tibia, without a separate femur. |The condylopod, a small insect, scurried under the leaf.  这只髁足小昆虫 scurried under the leaf.|近义词：arthropod, insect, crustacean|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps invertebrate if considering broader classification) |noun
