magnetiferous:/ˌmæɡnɪˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
含磁的；产磁铁矿的
Containing magnetic iron or other material having magnetic properties.
The magnetiferous rocks of the region have attracted the attention of geologists for centuries.  该地区的含磁岩石几个世纪以来一直吸引着地质学家的注意。
The expedition discovered a magnetiferous mountain range rich in iron ore.  探险队发现了一个富含铁矿石的产磁铁矿山脉。
近义词:magnetic, ferrous
反义词:non-magnetic
词性:adjective
