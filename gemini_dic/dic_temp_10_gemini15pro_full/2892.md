advertises:/ˈædvərtaɪzɪz/ | v. | 登广告，宣传，做广告 | To make something known generally or in public, especially in order to sell it. | The company advertises its products on television.  这家公司在电视上宣传它的产品。

The university advertises its courses in newspapers. 这所大学在报纸上刊登课程广告。 | synonyms: promote, publicize, market | antonyms: conceal, hide, suppress | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from Old French *advertiss-, lengthened stem of advertir ‘give notice of, warn’, based on Latin advertere ‘turn towards’, from ad- ‘to, towards’ + vertere ‘to turn’.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
She advertises her business on social media. = 她在社交媒体上宣传她的业务。
He advertises his services in the local newspaper. = 他在当地报纸上刊登他的服务广告。

一般过去时:
They advertised their new product on TV last month. = 他们上个月在电视上宣传了他们的新产品。
We advertised the job vacancy online. = 我们在网上发布了招聘广告。

现在完成时:
The company has advertised the position extensively. = 这家公司已经广泛地宣传了这个职位。
I have advertised my car for sale. = 我已经登广告卖我的车了。

过去完成时:
They had advertised the event before it was cancelled. = 他们在活动取消前就已经宣传过了。
She had advertised her services for months before getting a client. = 在找到客户之前，她已经宣传她的服务好几个月了。

将来时:
We will advertise the sale in the local paper. = 我们将在当地报纸上宣传这次促销。
The store will advertise its new collection next week. = 这家商店下周将宣传它的新系列。

虚拟语气:
If I were to advertise my services, I would use social media. = 如果我要宣传我的服务，我会使用社交媒体。
I wish I had advertised the event more widely. = 我希望我当时更广泛地宣传了这个活动。
