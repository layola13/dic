hedgehog:/ˈhedʒ.hɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈhedʒ.hɑːɡ/ (US)| n.|刺猬|A small mammal with a spiny coat.|The hedgehog curled up into a ball when it sensed danger. 刺猬感觉到危险时蜷缩成一团。|Synonyms: porcupine (less precise), echidna (less common)|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

hedgehog:/ˈhedʒ.hɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈhedʒ.hɑːɡ/ (US)| v.|采取规避风险的措施|To protect oneself from financial loss.|The company hedged its bets by investing in a variety of assets.  这家公司通过投资各种资产来规避风险。|Synonyms: safeguard, protect, cushion|Antonyms: risk, expose|Verb
