dotings:/ˈdoʊtɪŋz/
noun
溺爱；宠爱
Excessive fondness or affection.
She showered her grandchildren with dotings. 她对孙子们非常溺爱。
近义词: affection, fondness, indulgence, pampering
反义词: neglect, indifference
词性: noun (plural)

词源:  From the verb "dote," meaning "to be excessively fond of," from Middle English *doten*, perhaps of Scandinavian origin.
