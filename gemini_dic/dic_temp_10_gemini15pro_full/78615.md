The gibboseness of the camel's hump allows it to store water. 骆驼驼峰的凸状使其能够储存水分。|近义词：hunchback, hump, convexity|反义词：flatness, concavity|词性: 名词

词源: From gibbous + -ness.  Gibbous comes from Latin gibbosus "humpbacked," from gibbus "hump."
