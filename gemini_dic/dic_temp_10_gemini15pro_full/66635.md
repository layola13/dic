existential:/ˌɛɡzɪˈstɛnʃəl/ | adj. | 存在的；存在主义的 | Relating to existence, especially human existence and experience; characteristic of existentialism, a philosophical movement emphasizing individual freedom, responsibility, and the search for meaning in a meaningless world. |例句：He was having an existential crisis, questioning the meaning of life. 他正经历一场存在主义危机，质疑生命的意义。

例句：Existential philosophy emphasizes individual freedom and responsibility. 存在主义哲学强调个体自由和责任。|近义词：philosophical, ontological |反义词：tangible, concrete |词性: adjective
