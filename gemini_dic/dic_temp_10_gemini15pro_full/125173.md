multibusiness:/ˌmʌltiˈbɪznəs/ |adj.| 多业务的，涉及多种经营的 | Relating to or operating several different businesses. | Example:  A multibusiness company often faces the challenge of allocating resources effectively across its various divisions.  一家多业务公司经常面临在其各个部门有效分配资源的挑战。

Example:  The conglomerate's multibusiness strategy allowed it to weather economic downturns by diversifying its risk.  这家企业集团的多业务战略使其能够通过分散风险来抵御经济衰退。 | Synonym: diversified, conglomerate, multi-industry | Antonym: specialized, single-industry, niche | adjective
