first-footing:/ˈfɜːrstˌfʊtɪŋ/| n.|踩门槛；第一个访客|The custom of first-footing is believed to bring good luck for the new year. 踩门槛的习俗被认为会为新年带来好运。|例句：Their first-footing brought us good luck last year. 他们的踩门槛去年给我们带来了好运。

例句：It's traditional for a dark-haired man to be the first-footing.  传统上，第一个踩门槛的人应该是一位深色头发的男士。

近义词：housewarming, visiting|反义词: (No direct antonym, could be considered the opposite of leaving)|词性:noun
