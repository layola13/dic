cursive:/ˈkɜːrsɪv/ /ˈkɝːsɪv/| adj. n.|草书的，草书|flowing handwriting with the letters joined together|She writes in a beautiful cursive script. 她用漂亮的草书写字。|近义词：running, flowing, script|反义词:print|词性:adjective, noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *cursivus, from Latin cursus (“a running”), from currere (“to run”).
