miseducating:/ˌmɪsˈɛdʒʊkeɪtɪŋ/
verb
错误地教育；误导
educating someone badly or giving them wrong information
The media was accused of miseducating the public about the health risks. 媒体被指责在健康风险方面误导了公众。
misinforming, misleading
informing, educating
