mackinaw:/ˈmækɪˌnɔː/ (US), /ˈmækɪnɔː/ (UK)| n. adj.|厚重的羊毛外套；麦基诺岛（地名）|A heavy, short, double-breasted woolen coat, often plaid, worn especially in the Great Lakes region of North America; also, of or relating to Mackinac Island in Michigan.|He wore a thick mackinaw to protect himself from the cold wind. 他穿了一件厚重的麦基诺外套来抵御寒风。|近义词:coat, jacket, parka|反义词:N/A|词性:noun, adjective
