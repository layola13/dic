collateral:/kəˈlætərəl/| adj. n.| 担保的，附属的; 抵押品 |Something pledged as security for repayment of a loan, to be forfeited in the event of a default.  |  She used her house as collateral for the loan. 她用房子作抵押贷款。 | synonyms: security, guarantee, pledge, deposit | antonyms: debt, liability | noun

collateral:/kəˈlætərəl/| adj.| 附属的，次要的，旁系的| Resulting from or occurring in addition to the main action or effect. | The collateral damage of the bombing was extensive. 轰炸造成的附带损害是巨大的。| synonyms: secondary, subsidiary, incidental, ancillary | antonyms: primary, main, principal | adjective
