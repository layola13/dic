illusion:/ɪˈluːʒn/ /ɪˈluːʒən/ | n. | 幻想，错觉 | something that deceives by producing a false or misleading impression of reality. |  She had no illusions about her beauty fading. 她对自己的美貌消逝不抱幻想。

The magician created the illusion of a disappearing rabbit. 魔术师制造了兔子消失的幻觉。

近义词：delusion, fantasy, mirage, misconception
反义词：reality, truth, fact
词性：noun
