anti-management:/ˌæntiˈmænɪdʒmənt/ | adj. | 反管理层的；反对管理的；敌视管理层的 | Opposed to or hostile towards management, especially in a workplace context.  Characterized by a belief that management is inherently exploitative or oppressive. |  The union adopted an anti-management stance during the contract negotiations. 工会在合同谈判期间采取了反管理层的立场。

The anti-management sentiment among the workers was palpable. 工人们的反管理情绪很明显。| Synonyms:  anti-establishment,  insubordinate, dissident, rebellious | Antonyms: pro-management, cooperative, compliant, obedient | adjective
