noncommodiousnesses:/ˌnɒnkəˈməʊdiəsnɪsɪz/
noun
不便之处；不宽敞
lack of commodiousness; the state or quality of being not commodious; inconvenient or cramped conditions.
The noncommodiousnesses of the small apartment made it difficult to live comfortably.  这间小公寓的不便之处使得舒适地生活变得困难。
Near synonyms: inconveniences, disadvantages, shortcomings
Antonyms: advantages, conveniences, comforts
词性: 名词
