anorthite:/ˈænɔːrθaɪt/| n.|钙长石|A feldspar mineral, CaAl2Si2O8, the calcium end member of the plagioclase series.|Anorthite is a common constituent of lunar rocks.  钙长石是月球岩石的常见成分。|Anorthite crystals were found in the volcanic eruption. 在火山喷发物中发现了钙长石晶体。|Synonyms: calcium feldspar, plagioclase|Antonyms: N/A|noun
