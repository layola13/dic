garnierite:/ˈɡɑːrniəˌraɪt/ | n. |硅镍矿，水蛇纹石|A green nickel ore consisting of hydrated nickel magnesium silicate. It is found in New Caledonia and other locations. |Garnierite is an important source of nickel. 硅镍矿是镍的重要来源。| The garnierite deposits in New Caledonia are among the largest in the world. 新喀里多尼亚的硅镍矿矿床是世界上最大的矿床之一。|Synonyms: noumeite |Antonyms: None |Noun
