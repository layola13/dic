drumlinized:/ˈdrʌmlɪnaɪzd/
adjective
成鼓丘状的，被冰川磨蚀成鼓丘状的
Resembling or shaped like a drumlin; having drumlins.  Formed or streamlined by glacial action.
The landscape is drumlinized, with numerous elongated hills oriented in the direction of ice flow.  这片地貌呈鼓丘状，许多细长的山丘朝着冰川流动的方向排列。
The drumlinized terrain provided excellent grazing land for the sheep.  这片呈鼓丘状的地形为绵羊提供了极好的牧场。
近义词: glacially sculpted, streamlined
反义词:  undulating, irregular
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From drumlin + -ize + -ed.  "Drumlin" comes from the Irish Gaelic word "droimnín," meaning "little ridge,"  and ultimately from "druim," meaning "ridge" or "back."
