hyperextensibility:/ˌhaɪpərɪkˌstɛnsɪˈbɪlɪti/
|n.|过度伸展性，超伸展性|The ability of a joint to be extended beyond its normal range of motion.|Her hyperextensibility allows her to bend her fingers backward. 她的过度伸展性使她能够向后弯曲手指。|近义词：flexibility, laxity|反义词：stiffness, rigidity|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, beyond, excessive"
extend (verb): from Latin "extendere," meaning "to stretch out"
-ibility (suffix): forming nouns of quality or state, meaning "capable of being"
