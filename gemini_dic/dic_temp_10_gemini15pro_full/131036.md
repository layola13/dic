nonautonomous:/ˌnɒn ɔːˈtɒnəməs/| adj.|非自主的，非自治的|Not self-governing; lacking autonomy; subject to external forces or control.|例句：The nonautonomous region relies heavily on the central government for funding. 这个非自治地区严重依赖中央政府的资金。|近义词：dependent, subordinate, controlled|反义词: autonomous, independent, self-governing|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- (Latin prefix meaning "not") + autonomous (from Greek autonomos, meaning "self-governing")
