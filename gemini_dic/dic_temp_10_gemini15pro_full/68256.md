faithist:/ˈfeɪθɪst/ | n. | 信仰主义者; 宗教主义者 | A person who emphasizes the importance of faith, especially religious faith.  A faithist prioritizes belief over reason or evidence. |  Example: Some faithists find scientific explanations challenging to reconcile with their beliefs.  一些信仰主义者发现科学解释很难与他们的信仰协调一致。

Example: The faithist argued that proof was not necessary for true understanding. 这位信仰主义者认为，真正的理解不需要证据。| 近义词: believer, devotee, adherent | 反义词: atheist, agnostic, skeptic | 词性: noun
