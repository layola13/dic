macro-evolutionary:/ˌmækroʊˌevəˈluːʃəneri/ |adj.|宏观演化的|Relating to major evolutionary change at or above the level of species, over long periods of time, resulting in the formation of new taxonomic groups.|The emergence of mammals from reptilian ancestors is a macro-evolutionary process. 哺乳动物从爬行动物祖先的出现是一个宏观进化过程。|Example: The study focuses on the macro-evolutionary trends in the development of the horse.  这项研究的重点是马的宏观进化趋势。|Synonyms: large-scale evolutionary, transpecific evolution|Antonyms: micro-evolutionary|Word Type: Adjective
