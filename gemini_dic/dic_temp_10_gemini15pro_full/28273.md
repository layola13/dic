captivation:/kæp.tɪˈveɪ.ʃən/| n. |迷住，吸引，魅力|The captivating beauty of the sunset held me spellbound. 日落的迷人美景让我着迷。|近义词：fascination, enchantment, allure|反义词：repulsion, boredom|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin captivatio, from Latin captivatus, past participle of captivare "to take captive," from captivus "captive," from captus, past participle of capere "to take, seize."
