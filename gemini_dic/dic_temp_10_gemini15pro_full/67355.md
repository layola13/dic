extensible:/ɪkˈstɛnsəbl/ | adj. | 可扩展的，可延伸的 | Able to be extended or enlarged in scope or size. | This software is extensible, meaning you can add new features to it.  这个软件是可扩展的，意味着你可以添加新的功能。 | Example: The system is extensible and can accommodate future growth. 系统是可扩展的，可以适应未来的增长。| Synonyms: expandable, adaptable, flexible, scalable | Antonyms: inflexible, rigid, fixed, limited | word property: adjective
