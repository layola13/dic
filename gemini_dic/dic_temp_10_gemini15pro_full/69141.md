favourable:/ˈfeɪvərəbl/ (US) /ˈfeɪvərəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|有利的；良好的；赞成的|Expressing approval; giving consent.|The weather was favourable for sailing. 天气适合航行。|近义词:advantageous, beneficial, positive, auspicious, propitious|反义词:unfavourable, adverse, negative, inauspicious|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *favorable*, from Middle French *favorable*, from Latin *favorabilis* (“well-disposed, propitious”), from *favor* (“favor, goodwill”) + *-abilis* (suffix forming adjectives meaning “capable of, worthy of, tending to”).
