oppugnant: /əˈpʌɡnənt/ | adj. | 反对的，对抗的；敌对的 | Opposing or resisting; antagonistic.  Characterized by or inclined to opposition or conflict. | His oppugnant attitude made negotiation difficult. 他的对抗态度使得谈判变得困难。 |  The lawyer presented an oppugnant argument against the prosecution's claims. 律师提出了反对控方主张的论点。 | 近义词: antagonistic, hostile, opposing, contrary, conflicting, resistant, adversative | 反义词: agreeable, amenable, compliant, cooperative, supportive | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: From Latin *oppugnans*, present participle of *oppugnare* "to fight against, attack, besiege," from *ob* "against" + *pugnare* "to fight."
