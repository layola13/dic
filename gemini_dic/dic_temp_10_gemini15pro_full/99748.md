intermeasured:/ˌɪntərˈmeʒərd/ | adj. | 相互测量的，互相衡量的 | Measured against or in comparison with each other.  | The two systems are intermeasured to ensure compatibility. 这两个系统相互测量以确保兼容性。|  The success of the two projects are intermeasured. 这两个项目的成功是相互衡量的。| 近义词: correlated, compared, related | 反义词: independent, unrelated | 词性:形容词

词源:  "inter-" (prefix meaning "between, among, mutually") + "measured" (past participle of "measure").  The prefix "inter-" indicates a reciprocal relationship between the elements being measured.
