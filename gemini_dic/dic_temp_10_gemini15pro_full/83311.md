gynobasic: /ˌɡaɪnəˈbeɪsɪk/
gynobasic: adj.
gynobasic: 基生的，雌蕊基生的
gynobasic:  (of a style) arising from the base of the ovary, not its apex.
gynobasic: The mint family (Lamiaceae) often has gynobasic styles. 唇形科植物通常具有基生的花柱。
gynobasic: basal
gynobasic: apical
gynobasic: adjective
