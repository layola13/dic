flat-footed:/ˌflætˈfʊtɪd/ | adj. | 笨拙的，措手不及的，扁平足的 |  Clumsy, awkward, or unprepared; also, having flat feet. |  The sudden question left him flat-footed.  突如其来的问题让他措手不及。| synonyms: awkward, clumsy, unprepared, unready, off guard | antonyms: agile, graceful, prepared, ready, alert | adjective

flat-footed:/ˌflætˈfʊtɪd/ | adj. | 坚定不移的，直言不讳的 | Firm, resolute, and outspoken. | He gave a flat-footed denial of the accusations. 他坚决否认了这些指控。 | synonyms: resolute, firm, steadfast, unwavering, blunt, forthright | antonyms: hesitant, wavering, equivocal, ambiguous | adjective
