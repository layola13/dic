afoul:/əˈfaʊl/
adjective
陷入麻烦，与...冲突
Into conflict or difficulty with.
The ship ran afoul of the reef. 这艘船触礁了。
近义词: amiss, awry, wrong
反义词: clear, free
词性: adjective

afoul:/əˈfaʊl/
adverb
与...冲突地，陷入麻烦地
In a way that involves conflict or difficulty.
They often run afoul of the law. 他们经常与法律相冲突。
近义词: wrongly, improperly
反义词: correctly, properly
词性: adverb


词源:  From Middle English *a foul, from Old English on feolu "on many folds," from on "on" + feolu, dative plural of feola "much, many."  The meaning shifted from "in the folds of (a net or garment)" to "entangled" and then to "in trouble."
