bubblelike:/ˈbʌbəlˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 泡沫状的；像气泡的 | Resembling a bubble in form, texture, or lightness. | The soap formed bubblelike foam. 肥皂形成了泡沫状的泡沫。| The fabric had a light, bubblelike texture.  这种织物有一种轻盈的、泡沫状的质地。 |Synonyms: bubbly, frothy, foamy, airy | Antonyms: flat, solid, dense | adjective
