decorticator:/diːˈkɔːrtɪkeɪtər/| n.|脱皮机，剥壳机|A machine used to remove the outer covering (cortex or husk) from seeds, nuts, or fibers.|The decorticator efficiently removes the husk from the rice grains.|近义词：husker, sheller, huller|反义词:|noun
