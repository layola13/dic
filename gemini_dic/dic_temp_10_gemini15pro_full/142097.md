orientate:/ˈɔːriənteɪt/ (UK) /ˈɔːriəntˌeɪt/ (US)| v. |使适应；确定方向；朝向|to align or position something relative to a particular point or direction; to familiarize oneself with a situation or environment|例句：The hikers used a compass to orientate themselves.  徒步旅行者用指南针确定自己的方向。

例句：New students need time to orientate themselves to college life. 新生需要时间适应大学生活。|近义词：orient, adapt, adjust, familiarize|反义词：disorient, confuse|词性:动词
