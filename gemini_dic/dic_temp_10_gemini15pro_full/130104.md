non-provided:/ˌnɒn prəˈvaɪdɪd/ | adj. | 未提供 | Not given or supplied; withheld.  Specifically used in web analytics to refer to search keywords that are not shared by search engines. |  Example:  A large portion of our search traffic is now marked as "non-provided."  我们的很大一部分搜索流量现在被标记为“未提供”。| Synonym: unspecified, withheld, unavailable | Antonym: provided, supplied, available | adjective

non-provided:/ˌnɒn prəˈvaɪdɪd/ | adj. | 未提供的 | Not given or supplied; withheld.
