overillustrates:/ˌoʊvərˈɪləstreɪts/
verb
过度图解；过分插图说明
To provide too many illustrations, making something unnecessarily complex or cluttered.
The textbook overillustrates the basic concepts, making it difficult for students to focus on the key information. 这本教科书对基本概念的图解过多，使得学生难以集中于关键信息。

The author overillustrates their point with numerous redundant examples. 作者用大量多余的例子过度阐释了他们的观点。

Synonyms: overexplain, overdo, belabor
Antonyms: understate, simplify, summarize
词性: 动词
