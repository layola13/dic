/ˈlɪmɪt/ | n. vt.| 限制；界限；限度；限定；|A point or level beyond which something does not or may not extend or pass. | The speed limit is 60 mph.  速度限制是每小时60英里。|  There's a limit to how much I can tolerate. 我能忍受的程度是有限的。 |近义词：restriction, boundary, constraint, cap|反义词:infinity, boundlessness| noun, verb

/ˈlɪmɪt/ | vt.| 限制；限定；| To restrict or confine within limits. |  We must limit our spending. 我们必须限制我们的开支。 | We need to limit the number of guests. 我们需要限制客人的数量。 | 近义词：restrict, confine, restrain, curb|反义词:expand, increase| verb

From Old French "limite" and directly from Latin "limes" (genitive "limit-is") meaning "boundary, frontier."
