gunged:/ɡʌndʒd/
adjective
粘糊糊的，脏兮兮的
covered with thick, sticky, unpleasant matter.
The kitchen floor was gunged with grease. 厨房地板上满是油腻腻的污垢。
近义词:dirty, grimy, filthy, mucky
反义词:clean, spotless, pristine
词性:adjective

词源:
Likely from "gunge," a British slang term for thick, sticky matter, possibly imitative of the sound of something sticky.
