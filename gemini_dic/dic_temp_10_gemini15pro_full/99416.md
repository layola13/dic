The correct spelling is "introduce".  There is no word "interduce".  Here's the breakdown for "introduce":

introduce: /ˌɪntrəˈd(j)uːs/ (UK) /ˌɪntrəˈduːs/ (US)| vt.|介绍，引进|To present (someone) by name to another person, to bring (something, especially a product, measure, or bill) into use or operation for the first time.|例句：I'd like to introduce my friend, John. 我想介绍一下我的朋友，约翰。|例句：The company introduced a new line of products. 这家公司推出了一系列新产品。|近义词：present, familiarize, launch|反义词:remove, withdraw|词性:verb
