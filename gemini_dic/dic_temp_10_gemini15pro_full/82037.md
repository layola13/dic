grisled:/ˈɡrɪzəld/ /ˈɡrɪzld/| adj.|灰白的，头发斑白的；饱经沧桑的|Having gray or partly gray hair, especially as a result of age or stress, often used figuratively to describe someone who is experienced and wise, perhaps a bit cynical due to hardships. |例句：The grisled veteran recounted his war stories.  头发斑白的老兵讲述了他的战争故事。

例句：The grisled detective had seen it all.  饱经风霜的侦探什么都见过。|近义词：gray, grizzled, hoary, seasoned, veteran|反义词：youthful, inexperienced, green|词性: adjective
