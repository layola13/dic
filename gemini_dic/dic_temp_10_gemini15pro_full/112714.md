loxes:/ˈlɒksiːz/
noun
氧化酶 (复数)
oxidases (plural form).  Enzymes that catalyze oxidation reactions.
Loxes are crucial for various biological processes. 氧化酶对各种生物过程至关重要。
Loxes play a key role in collagen and elastin cross-linking. 氧化酶在胶原蛋白和弹性蛋白的交联中起关键作用。
Synonyms: enzymes, catalysts
Antonyms: inhibitors
