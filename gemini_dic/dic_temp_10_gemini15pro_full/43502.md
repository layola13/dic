cravat:/krəˈvæt/| n. |领巾，围巾，领结|A piece of cloth worn around the neck, often tied in a decorative knot.  A cravat is a wider and more loosely tied neckwear than a tie.|He wore a silk cravat with his suit. 他穿西装配了一条丝绸领巾。|近义词：necktie, scarf, neckerchief|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "open collar")|noun
