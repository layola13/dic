mislearning:/ˌmɪsˈlɜːrnɪŋ/ | n. | 错误学习，误学 | The act or process of learning something incorrectly or developing a misunderstanding. |  His mislearning of the formula led to incorrect calculations. 他对公式的错误学习导致了计算错误。 | misconception, misunderstanding, false impression | correct learning, accurate understanding | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (prefix meaning 'wrong' or 'bad') + learning (from Old English leornian, meaning 'to acquire knowledge or skill').
