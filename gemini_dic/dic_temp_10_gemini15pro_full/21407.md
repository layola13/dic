bloviation:/ˌbloʊviˈeɪʃən/| n.|空谈；吹嘘；夸夸其谈|Talk that is empty, pompous, and prolonged; bluster.|The politician's speech was full of bloviation, but offered few concrete solutions.  这位政客的演讲充满了空话，但几乎没有提供任何具体的解决方案。|Example 2:  His bloviation about his accomplishments bored everyone. 他对他成就的吹嘘让每个人都感到厌烦。|近义词：bombast, bluster, rhetoric, hot air|反义词:conciseness, brevity|词性:noun

词源: From bloviate (verb), from blow, suggesting puffed-up, inflated speech.
