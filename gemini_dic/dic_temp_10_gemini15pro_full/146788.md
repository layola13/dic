overidly: /ˌoʊvərˈaɪdli/ | adv. | 过于懒散地，过度悠闲地 | Excessively idly; too lazily or leisurely. Characterized by excessive inactivity or relaxation.  | He overidly strolled through the park, ignoring his deadlines. 他过于悠闲地在公园里漫步，无视他的截止日期。|  He overidly flipped through the channels, unable to decide what to watch. 他漫不经心地转换着频道，不知道该看什么。 | 近义词: lazily, indolently, sluggishly | 反义词: diligently, energetically, actively | 词性: 副词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "idly" (adverb meaning "in a lazy or inactive way")
