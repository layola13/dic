broke:/broʊk/ (US) /brəʊk/ (UK)| adj.| 破产的；坏掉的；一文不名的；|Without money; penniless. Also, damaged or no longer functioning.|例句：I'm broke. 我身无分文。|例句：My phone is broke. 我的手机坏了。|近义词：penniless, bankrupt, ruined, broken|反义词: rich, wealthy, affluent, functioning|形容词


broke:/broʊk/ (US) /brəʊk/ (UK)| v.| break的过去式|Past tense of "break".  To separate into pieces suddenly or violently; to interrupt; to fail to keep.|例句：He broke his leg. 他摔断了腿。|例句：She broke the silence. 她打破了沉默。|近义词：smashed, shattered, fractured, interrupted|反义词: fixed, repaired, mended, continued|动词


Etymology: From Middle English *broken*, past participle of *breken*, from Old English *brecan*, of Germanic origin.
