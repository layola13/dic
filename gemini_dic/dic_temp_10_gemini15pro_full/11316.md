arduousnesses:/ˈɑːrdʒʊəsnəsɪz/
n.
艰难, 艰辛, 费力
The quality or state of being arduous; difficulty; laboriousness.
Climbing Mount Everest is an arduousness that tests the limits of human endurance. 攀登珠穆朗玛峰是一项考验人类耐力极限的艰巨任务。
The arduousnesses of the journey wore down the travelers. 旅途的艰辛使旅行者疲惫不堪。
近义词: difficulties, hardships, toils, labors
反义词: easiness, facility, simplicity
