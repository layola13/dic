nonability:/ˌnɒnəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 无能;无力 | Lack of ability; inability. |  His nonability to make a decision cost them dearly. 他无力做出决定，让他们付出了高昂的代价。|  The team's nonability to score goals led to their defeat. 球队无力进球导致了他们的失败。 | 近义词: inability, incapacity, incompetence | 反义词: ability, capability, competence | 词性: 名词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + ability (from Old French "ableté," ultimately from Latin "habilitas," from "habilis" meaning "apt, skillful").
