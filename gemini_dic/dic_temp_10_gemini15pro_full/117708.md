melancholiae:/ˌmɛlənˈkəʊliːiː/ | n. | 忧郁症，忧郁，悲哀 | A state of deep sadness, pensiveness, and inactivity.  This is an archaic plural form of melancholia.  Now generally only used in the title of Dürer’s famous engraving *Melencolia I*. |  Dürer's *Melancholiae* is a complex and enigmatic work.  丢勒的《忧郁症》是一幅复杂而神秘的作品。 | 近义词：melancholy, sadness, depression, gloom | 反义词：joy, happiness, elation, cheerfulness | 词性: noun
