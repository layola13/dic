nails:/neɪlz/ | n. |指甲；钉子|Hardened keratin plates covering the upper surface of the tips of the fingers and toes; small, slender, typically pointed pieces of metal used to fasten things together.|She painted her nails red. 她把指甲涂成了红色。| He hammered the nails into the wood. 他把钉子钉进了木头里。|Near synonyms: claws, talons (for animals); spikes, tacks (for metal fasteners) | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the body part) screws, bolts (as alternatives to metal nails)| noun
