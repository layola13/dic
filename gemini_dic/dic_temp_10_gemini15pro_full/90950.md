hurly-burly:/ˈhɜːrli ˈbɜːrli/ /ˈhɝːli ˈbɝːli/
n.|喧闹；骚动；混乱|A state of commotion, uproar, and confusion.|The marketplace was a hurly-burly of shoppers and vendors.  市场里挤满了购物者和小贩，一片喧闹。|Synonyms: commotion, tumult, uproar, chaos, bedlam|Antonyms: peace, quiet, tranquility, calm, order
词性:名词
