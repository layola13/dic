hubbuboo:/ˈhʌbəˌbuː/ | n. |喧闹，骚动；吵闹；混乱| A loud confused noise, typically of many people talking at the same time. | There was a great hubbuboo in the marketplace. 市场上一片喧闹。 | Synonyms: commotion, din, uproar, racket, clamor | Antonyms: quiet, silence, peace, tranquility | noun

hubbuboo:/ˈhʌbəˌbuː/ | n. |喧闹，骚动；吵闹；混乱|  A state of confusion and disorder. | The sudden announcement caused a hubbuboo in the office. 突如其来的宣布在办公室里引起了一阵骚动。 | Synonyms: chaos, disorder, turmoil, pandemonium | Antonyms: order, calm, peace, tranquility | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Likely an expressive formation, imitating the sound of a noisy crowd. Possibly influenced by words like "hubbub" and "bamboozle."
