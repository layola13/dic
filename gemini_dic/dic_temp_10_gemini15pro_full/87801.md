hiccough:/ˈhɪkʌp/ (US), /ˈhɪkʌp/ (UK)| n. v.|打嗝|A sudden, involuntary contraction of the diaphragm muscle, followed by a closure of the glottis, which produces a characteristic sound.|例句：He had a bout of hiccoughs after drinking too much soda. 他喝太多汽水后打嗝了一阵。|近义词：hiccup|反义词: (None -  a physiological process doesn't have a direct opposite)|词性:noun, verb

词源:  From Middle English *hik-* + *cough*, of imitative origin.
