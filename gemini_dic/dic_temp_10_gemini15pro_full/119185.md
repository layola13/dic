mezzotint: /ˈmetsəʊtɪnt/ (US) /ˈmedzəʊtɪnt/ (UK)| n.| 铜版雕刻(一种版画技法)，中间色调|A printmaking process that creates rich, velvety textures and subtle gradations of light and shade.| The artist's mezzotints were known for their evocative atmosphere. 这位艺术家的铜版雕刻以其引人入胜的氛围而闻名。| synonyms: engraving, etching, print | antonyms: None (lacks a direct opposite)| noun

mezzotint: /ˈmetsəʊtɪnt/ (US) /ˈmedzəʊtɪnt/ (UK)| v.| 制作铜版雕刻|To create a print using the mezzotint technique.|  He mezzotinted a portrait of his mother. 他用铜版雕刻技法制作了他母亲的肖像。| synonyms: engrave, etch | antonyms: None (lacks a direct opposite)| verb
