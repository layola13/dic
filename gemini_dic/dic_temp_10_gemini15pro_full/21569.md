bluestockingism: /ˈbluːˌstɒkɪŋɪzəm/ (UK) /ˈbluːˌstɑːkɪŋɪzəm/ (US) | n. |  蓝袜学究；女学者主义 |  An affected and pedantic devotion to learning and intellectual pursuits, especially by women; the characteristics or behavior of a bluestocking.  |  Bluestockingism was seen as a challenge to traditional gender roles in the 18th century.  18世纪，蓝袜学究被视为对传统性别角色的挑战。

Bluestockingism often involved women gathering for intellectual discussions, excluding men. 蓝袜主义通常包括女性聚集在一起进行智力讨论，排除男性。 | 近义词：intellectualism, pedantry | 反义词：ignorance, philistinism | 词性: noun
