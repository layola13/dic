lavishment:/ˈlævɪʃmənt/ |n.| 挥霍；铺张；慷慨赠予 | An act of spending or giving something generously or extravagantly. | The lavishment of gifts upon the young prince was excessive.  年轻王子被过度地赠予礼物。| 近义词: extravagance, profusion, generosity | 反义词: stinginess, frugality | 词性: 名词


lavish:/ˈlævɪʃ/ | adj. | 奢华的；丰富的；慷慨的 |  (adj.) sumptuously rich, elaborate, or luxurious; (v.) bestow something in generous or extravagant quantities upon. | The lavish decorations in the ballroom were breathtaking.  舞厅里奢华的装饰令人叹为观止。| 近义词: luxurious, opulent, profuse | 反义词: meager, sparse, frugal | 词性: 形容词


词源: From Old French "lavasse" (deluge, downpour), ultimately from Latin "lavare" (to wash).  最初的含义是“大量泼洒”，后来演变为“慷慨地给予”和“奢华的”。
