例句：Don't be such a dawk!  别这么傻！|近义词：idiot, fool, dunce, nitwit, simpleton|反义词: genius, intellectual|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain, possibly related to dialectal 'daw', meaning 'sluggish' or 'lazy'.
