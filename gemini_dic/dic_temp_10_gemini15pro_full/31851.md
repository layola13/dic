cheered:/tʃɪrd/ | v. |欢呼；喝彩；感到高兴| to shout approval or praise |The crowd cheered as the team scored.  人群为球队的得分欢呼。| synonyms: applauded, acclaimed, rooted for | antonyms: booed, jeered | verb

cheered:/tʃɪrd/ | adj. |高兴的；振奋的| feeling happy and encouraged |I was cheered by the good news.  好消息让我很高兴。| synonyms: happy, joyful, elated | antonyms: sad, depressed, discouraged | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cheren, from Anglo-Norman cherer, from Late Latin *carāre (“to hold dear, to care for”), from Latin cārus (“dear”).
