hornpipes:/ˈhɔːrnpaɪps/ | n. | 水手舞；角笛舞曲；角笛 | A lively dance traditionally performed by sailors, or the music for this dance, originally played on a hornpipe. | Sailors danced hornpipes on the deck. 水手们在甲板上跳水手舞。 The band played a lively hornpipe. 乐队演奏了一首轻快的角笛舞曲。 | 近义词：jig, reel | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps slow dance or waltz) | 词性: noun
