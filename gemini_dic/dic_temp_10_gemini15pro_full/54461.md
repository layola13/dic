disintegrate:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪt/
verb
瓦解，碎裂，崩溃
to break up into small parts or pieces, or to make something do this
例句：The spacecraft disintegrated as it re-entered the Earth's atmosphere.  宇宙飞船在重返地球大气层时解体了。

例句：The old papers had disintegrated into dust.  旧文件已经碎成了灰尘。

近义词：crumble, decompose, fall apart, break down
反义词：integrate, combine, unite
词性：v.


disintegrated:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪd/
adjective
瓦解的，碎裂的，崩溃的
broken up into small parts or pieces
例句：The disintegrated remains of the building were scattered across the site.  建筑物瓦解后的残骸散落在现场各处。

例句：The disintegrated rock was easy to remove.  碎裂的岩石很容易清除。

近义词：fragmented, broken, shattered
反义词：whole, intact, integrated
词性：adj.
