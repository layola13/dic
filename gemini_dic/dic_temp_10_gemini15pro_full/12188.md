as-is:/ˌæzˈɪz/| adj. adv.|按现状；原样；照原样|In its present condition, without any changes or modifications.  Existing in its current state.| We bought the house as-is, knowing it needed renovations. 我们按原样买下了这栋房子，知道它需要翻新。|近义词：unaltered, unchanged, existing|反义词：renovated, modified, improved|词性:形容词/副词
Etymology: From the phrase "as it is," with the "it" eventually dropped in common usage.
