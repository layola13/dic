crack:/kræk/
n.|裂缝；爆裂声；俏皮话|A narrow opening or fissure, typically one that has appeared suddenly in a hard surface. |The earthquake caused cracks in the walls. 地震使墙上出现了裂缝。
crack:/kræk/
n.|尝试；机会|An attempt or opportunity to do something.|I'll have a crack at fixing the computer. 我会尝试修理一下电脑。
crack:/kræk/
v.|破裂；砸开；破解|To break or cause to break without complete separation of the parts.|The ice cracked as I stepped on it. 当我踩到冰上时，冰裂开了。
crack:/kræk/
v.|发出爆裂声；（嗓音）变嘶哑|To make a sharp, sudden sound.|The whip cracked loudly. 鞭子发出很大的爆裂声。
crack:/kræk/
adj.|优秀的；一流的|Highly skilled and proficient.|He's a crack shot. 他是个神枪手。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English craken, from Old English cracian (“to crack, crash, resound”), of imitative origin. Cognate with Middle Dutch craken (“to crack, creak”), Middle High German krachen (“to crack, crash”), Old Norse kraka (“to crow”), krak (“crash, crowing”).

近义词:split, break, fracture, snap, burst, chap, fissure, cleft, crevice, break into, decipher, decode, solve, attempt, try, shot, expert, proficient, first-rate, top-notch
反义词:mend, repair, fix, join, weld, close, amateur, unskilled
