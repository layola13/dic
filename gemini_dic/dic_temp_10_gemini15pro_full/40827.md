convulsedly:/kənˈvʌlsɪdli/
adverb
痉挛地，抽搐地
in a way that involves sudden and violent movements of the body, caused by uncontrolled muscle contractions
He convulsedly clutched his stomach. 他痉挛地捂着肚子。
The child convulsedly sobbed. 孩子抽搐地哭泣着。
Synonyms: spasmodically, jerkily, fitfully
Antonyms: smoothly, calmly, steadily
词性: 副词
