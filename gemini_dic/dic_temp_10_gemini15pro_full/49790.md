demonize:/ˈdiːmənaɪz/ /ˈdɛmənaɪz/ | vt. | 妖魔化；将…描绘成恶魔；污蔑 | To portray someone or something as wicked or threatening. | The media often demonizes politicians it disagrees with. 媒体经常妖魔化与其意见相左的政客。

The opposition party demonized the government's new policy. 反对党将政府的新政策妖魔化了。

| 近义词：vilify, slander, denigrate, disparage | 反义词：praise, extol, laud, celebrate | 词性：动词
