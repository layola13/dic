doctrinal: /ˈdɑːktrɪnəl/ | adj. | 教义的；学说的；主义的 | Relating to or involving doctrine; expressing a particular principle or set of principles. |  Example 1: The book presents a doctrinal analysis of the philosopher's work. 这本书对这位哲学家的著作进行了学说分析。

Example 2:  His doctrinal beliefs are very strict. 他的教义信仰非常严格。 | Synonyms: dogmatic, ideological, theoretical | Antonyms: undogmatic, practical, empirical | word type: adjective
