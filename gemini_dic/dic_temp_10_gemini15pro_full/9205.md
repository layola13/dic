antioriental:/ˌæntiɔːriˈentl/ | adj. | 反东方的，仇视东方的 | Opposed to or prejudiced against people or things from the Orient (East Asia). |  The politician's antioriental rhetoric fueled racial tensions.  这位政客的反东方言论加剧了种族紧张局势。 | Example 2:  The antioriental sentiment in the early 20th century led to discriminatory immigration policies. 20世纪初的反东方情绪导致了歧视性的移民政策。 | 近义词：xenophobic, racist, prejudiced, bigoted | 反义词：pro-Asian, tolerant, accepting | 词性: adjective

词源:  "anti-" (against) + "oriental" (of or relating to the East). "Oriental" itself derives from the Latin "oriens" meaning "east" or "rising sun."
