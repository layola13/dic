degraders:/dɪˈɡreɪdɚz/| n.|降解剂，降解者|Substances or organisms that break down or decompose a material, often a complex organic molecule.  They play a crucial role in natural cycles and waste management.|Proteasome degraders are a promising new class of drugs for cancer. 蛋白酶体降解剂是一种很有前途的新型抗癌药物。

The city employs bacteria as degraders in its sewage treatment plant. 该市在其污水处理厂中使用细菌作为降解剂。|Synonyms: decomposers, destructors, catalysts|Antonyms: builders, synthesizers|Noun
