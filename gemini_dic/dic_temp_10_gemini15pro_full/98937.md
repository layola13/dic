inter-allied:/ˌɪntərˈælaɪd/
adjective
相互结盟的；同盟国之间的
Existing or occurring between allied nations.
The inter-allied debts after World War I caused significant economic strain. 第一次世界大战后，盟国之间的债务造成了巨大的经济压力。
The inter-allied conference addressed the post-war reconstruction efforts. 同盟国会议讨论了战后重建工作。
近义词: allied, cooperative
反义词: enemy, opposing
词性: adjective


词源:
From inter- + allied.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "allied" means joined by treaty or agreement.
