overtaxing: /ˌoʊvərˈtæksɪŋ/ | adj. | 负担过重的，苛求的 | Making excessive demands on someone or something. | The schedule was overtaxing, leaving the employees exhausted.  这个时间表负担过重，让员工们精疲力竭。|  The overtaxing workload led to burnout. 繁重的工作导致倦怠。 |Synonyms: burdensome, demanding, excessive, strenuous, onerous | Antonyms: light, easy, manageable, undemanding | adjective

overtax: /ˌoʊvərˈtæks/ | vt. | 对…征税过重；使负担过重；| To tax excessively; to burden heavily. | The government overtaxed the citizens, leading to widespread discontent. 政府对公民征税过重，导致普遍不满。| Don't overtax yourself by taking on too many responsibilities. 不要承担太多责任，以免让自己负担过重。 | Synonyms: burden, overload, strain | Antonyms: undertax, relieve | verb


Etymology:
From over- + tax.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much."  "Tax" comes from Old French "taxer" meaning "to assess, estimate."
