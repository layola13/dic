malonate:/ˈmæləneɪt/| n.|丙二酸盐；丙二酸酯|A salt or ester of malonic acid.|Sodium malonate is commonly used in research. 丙二酸钠常用于研究。|近义词: propanedioate (更正式的化学名称)|反义词: None (没有直接的反义词，可能需要根据具体语境找到化学上的拮抗剂)|noun

malonate:/ˈmæləneɪt/| adj.|丙二酸的|Relating to or derived from malonic acid.|Malonate pathway is important in metabolism. 丙二酸途径在新陈代谢中很重要。|近义词: propanedioic (更正式的化学名称)|反义词: None (没有直接的反义词)|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From malonic acid + -ate (suffix forming nouns denoting salts and esters).  Malonic acid is named after malic acid, from which it was first prepared, ultimately from Latin mālum "apple".
