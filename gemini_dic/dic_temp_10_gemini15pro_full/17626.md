bebop:/ˈbiːbɑːp/ (US) /ˈbiːbɒp/ (UK)| n. |比波普|A style of jazz characterized by fast tempos, complex harmonies, and virtuosic improvisation.|Miles Davis was a pioneer of bebop.  迈尔斯·戴维斯是比波普的先驱。|The bebop era produced some of the most innovative jazz music. 比波普时代产生了一些最具创新性的爵士乐。|近义词：jazz, modern jazz|反义词:traditional jazz|词性:noun
