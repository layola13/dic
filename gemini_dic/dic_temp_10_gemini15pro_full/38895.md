confer:/kənˈfɜːr/ (US) /kənˈfɜː(r)/ (UK)
verb
授予，给予；商谈，协商
To grant or bestow (a title, degree, benefit, or right).  To discuss something with someone in order to exchange opinions or get advice.
The university conferred an honorary degree on the renowned scientist. 大学授予这位著名科学家荣誉学位。
We need to confer with our lawyer before making a decision. 我们需要与我们的律师商谈后再做决定。
近义词: bestow, grant, award, consult, discuss
反义词: withhold, deny, refuse
词性: verb


confer:/kənˈfɜːr/ (US) /kənˈfɜː(r)/ (UK)
noun
(正式) 会议，商讨
A meeting for discussion or exchange of views. (Often used in the plural.)
The two leaders held a series of private confers to discuss the peace treaty.  两位领导人举行了一系列秘密会议，讨论和平条约。
The results of the confers will be released next week. 会议的结果将于下周公布。
近义词: meeting, discussion, consultation
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


词源: From Latin *conferre*, from com- "together" + ferre "to bring, bear".
