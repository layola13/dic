overrunner:/ˌoʊvərˈrʌnər/
noun
超额完成者；溢出部分；超支者；泛滥成灾之物
Someone or something that overruns or exceeds a limit.  Something produced in excess of a planned amount.  A device or part that allows a rotating part to continue turning after power is cut off.

The project went over budget because of unexpected costs, making the manager an overrunner on the project.  这个项目因为意外的成本超出了预算，使得经理成为了该项目的超支者。

The factory produced a large number of overrunners, which they sold at a discounted price.  这家工厂生产了大量的过剩产品，并以折扣价出售。

The overrunner on the conveyor belt prevented the packages from piling up when the machine stopped.  传送带上的溢出装置防止了机器停止时包裹堆积。

近义词:exceed, surpass, overflow
反义词:underrunner, shortfall

词性:n.
