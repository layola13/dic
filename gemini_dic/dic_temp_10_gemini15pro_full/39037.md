conflictions:/kənˈflɪkʃənz/| n. |冲突，矛盾|A situation in which two or more ideas, beliefs, or things are incompatible or opposed.|The project was delayed due to conflicts between the team members.  项目由于团队成员之间的冲突而被推迟了。|synonyms: clashes, disagreements, disputes, frictions|antonyms: agreements, harmonies, accords|noun

Etymology:  From Late Latin *conflictus*, past participle of *confligere* "to strike together," from *com-* "together" + *fligere* "to strike."
