microseismograph:/ˈmaɪkroʊˈsaɪzməɡræf/ /ˈmaɪkrəʊˈsaɪzməɡrɑːf/| n. |微震仪|An instrument that detects and records very faint earth tremors, too small to be noticed by ordinary seismographs.|The microseismograph registered a slight tremor that went unnoticed by most people. 微震仪记录到了一次轻微的震动，大多数人都没有注意到。|Synonyms: seismograph, seismometer|Antonyms: None|Noun

microseismography:/ˌmaɪkroʊsaɪzˈmɒɡrəfi/ /ˌmaɪkroʊsaɪzˈmɑːɡrəfi/| n. |微震学|The study of microseisms (faint earth tremors).|Microseismography helps scientists understand the subtle movements of the Earth's crust. 微震学帮助科学家了解地壳的细微运动。|Synonyms: seismology|Antonyms: None|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From micro- + seismograph.  "Micro-" comes from Greek "mikros" meaning "small". "Seismograph" comes from Greek "seismos" meaning "shaking" + "-graph" meaning "instrument for recording".
