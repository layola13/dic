emplunge: /ɪmˈplʌndʒ/ | v. |  投入；使陷入；使突然进入 | To thrust or plunge forcefully into something, often a liquid or a difficult situation. |  He emplunged his hands into the icy water. 他把手猛地 plunged into the icy water. 他把手猛地插入冰冷的水中。

The country was emplunged into war. 这个国家突然 plunged into war. 这个国家突然陷入战争。 | 近义词：immerse, submerge, plunge | 反义词：extricate, withdraw | 词性: 动词

词源:  From French *emplonger, from en- + plonger (“to plunge”), from Vulgar Latin *plumbicare, from Latin plumbum (“lead”).  最终源自拉丁语 plumbum（“铅”），原意指用铅锤探测水深，后来引申为“投入”或“使陷入”。
