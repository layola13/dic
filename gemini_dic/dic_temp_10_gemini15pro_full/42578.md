counterplayer:/ˈkaʊntərˌpleɪər/
n.
对手，竞争者
A person who plays against another, especially in a game or sport.
例句：In chess, your counterplayer is the person sitting across from you. 在国际象棋中，你的对手就是坐在你对面的人。

例句：He studied his counterplayer's moves carefully. 他仔细研究了对手的 moves。

近义词：opponent, adversary, rival, competitor
反义词：ally, teammate, partner
词性：名词
