intercommunity:/ˌɪntərˈkəˈmjuːnɪti/
adjective
社区间的，跨社区的
Existing or occurring between different communities.
Example: Intercommunity trade has increased significantly in recent years. 近年来，社区间贸易显著增加。

Example: The intercommunity bus service connects several neighboring towns. 社区间的巴士服务连接了几个邻近的城镇。

近义词: inter-community, between communities
反义词: intracommunity
词性: adjective
