indicia:/ɪnˈdɪʃiə/ /ɪnˈdɪsiə/ | n. |标志，标记，迹象|Marks or signs that point to something; distinguishing characteristics. |The torn label and faded colors are indicia of the package's age.  破损的标签和褪色的颜色是包裹年代久远的标志。

The presence of certain plants is an indicia of the soil's acidity. 某些植物的存在是土壤酸性的标志。|近义词：signs, marks, indicators, tokens, evidence|反义词: Nothing specific, possibly lack of evidence/proof|词性:noun
