anisic:/əˈnɪsɪk/ | adj. |茴香的；茴香味的|Of or relating to aniseed; having the flavor or odor of anise.|The anisic scent filled the bakery.  面包店里充满了茴香味。|Synonyms: anise-flavored, aniseed, licorice-like|Antonyms: bland, tasteless|adjective

anisic:/əˈnɪsɪk/ | n. |茴香酸|An organic compound, CH3OC6H4COOH, derived from anisole and existing as colorless prisms.|Anisic acid is used in some perfumes. 茴香酸用于一些香水。|Synonyms: p-methoxybenzoic acid, draconic acid, umbellic acid methyl ether|Antonyms: N/A|noun
