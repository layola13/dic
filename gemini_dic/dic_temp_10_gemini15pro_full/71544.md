flareups:/ˈflɛərˌʌps/ (US) /ˈflɛəˌʌps/ (UK)| n. |爆发，突发，加剧|A sudden outburst or intensification of something, especially of a disease or a conflict.|  My eczema has had a few flareups recently. 我的湿疹最近几次发作。|近义词：outbreaks, bursts, aggravations, exacerbations|反义词: improvements, remissions|词性:名词

Etymology:
From the verb "flare up," which combines "flare" (meaning to burn brightly or suddenly) and "up" (an adverb indicating increased intensity).  The noun form "flareup" arises from this verb.
