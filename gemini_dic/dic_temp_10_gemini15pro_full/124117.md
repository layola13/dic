mortaring:/ˈmɔːrtərɪŋ/ | n. | 砌築，用砂漿填縫 | The act of applying mortar, a workable paste used to bind building blocks such as stones, bricks, and concrete masonry units, together, fill and seal the irregular gaps between them, point and waterproof the masonry joints, and sometimes add decorative colors or patterns to masonry walls.  |  The mortaring of the new wall was completed yesterday. 昨天新墙的砌筑工作完成了。 | synonyms: cementing, plastering, pointing | antonyms: demolition, dismantling | noun

mortaring:/ˈmɔːrtərɪŋ/ | v. | 用砂漿砌築 |  To apply mortar to something, typically in construction. |  He was mortaring the bricks together. 他正在用砂漿砌磚。 | synonyms: cementing, plastering, pointing | antonyms: demolishing, dismantling | verb
