overmodified:/ˌoʊvərˈmɑːdɪfaɪd/ | adj. |过度修饰的；过分修饰的|Excessively or inappropriately modified, often referring to language, writing, or vehicles.  Having too many decorative features or alterations.| The car was overmodified, with spoilers, neon lights, and a ridiculously loud exhaust. 这辆车改装过度，有扰流板、霓虹灯和吵得离谱的排气管。|  The sentence was overmodified with unnecessary adjectives and adverbs.  这个句子过度修饰，用了太多不必要的形容词和副词。| 近义词: overdone, embellished, excessive | 反义词: simple, understated, plain | 词性: adjective
