outbeam:/ˌaʊtˈbiːm/
verb
发出比...更强的光
To surpass in radiance or brilliance; to shine more brightly than.
The new lighthouse outbeamed the old one. 新灯塔的光比旧灯塔更强。
Synonyms: outshine, eclipse, surpass
Antonyms: dim, darken
词性: 动词
