customer-facing:/ˈkʌstəmərˌfeɪsɪŋ/
adjective
面向客户的，与客户打交道的
Relating to or involving direct interaction with customers.
Our customer-facing staff are trained to handle complaints effectively. 我们的面向客户的员工都接受过有效处理投诉的培训。
customer-oriented, client-facing
back-office, internal
