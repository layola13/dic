humiliative:/hjuːˈmɪlɪeɪtɪv/
adj.
羞辱性的，丢脸的
Tending to cause humiliation; humbling.
例句：The experience was deeply humiliative for him. 这段经历对他来说极其羞辱。

例句：She found his comments humiliative and offensive. 她觉得他的评论具有羞辱性而且冒犯了她。

近义词：degrading, mortifying, shameful, embarrassing
反义词：exalting, uplifting, honoring
