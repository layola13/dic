half-completed:/ˌhɑːf kəmˈpliːtɪd/
adjective
半完成的，未完成的
Partially finished; not entirely done.
The project is half-completed, with several key components still missing.  项目只完成了一半，还有几个关键组件缺失。
The half-completed building stood abandoned. 这栋未完工的建筑被废弃了。
Near Synonyms: partially completed, unfinished, incomplete
Antonyms: finished, completed, done
词性: 形容词
