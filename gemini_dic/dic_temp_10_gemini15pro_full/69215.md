fearfully:/ˈfɪərfʊli/| adv.|害怕地，恐惧地，非常地|In a way that is full of fear or anxiety; extremely.|She looked fearfully at the dark shadows. 她害怕地看着黑暗的阴影。|近义词：nervously, anxiously, apprehensively, terribly, awfully|反义词：bravely, courageously, confidently|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle English *ferfully, from Old English *fǣrfullīce (“fearfully, timidly”), equivalent to fearful +‎ -ly. Cognate with Dutch vaarlijk (“dangerously”).
