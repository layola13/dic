drafty:/ˈdrɑːfti/ | adj. | 通风的；有穿堂风的；(指房间、房屋等)冷飕飕的 | Exposed to drafts of air; chilly. | The old house was drafty and cold in the winter.  这栋老房子冬天冷飕飕的，到处漏风。| synonyms: airy, breezy, ventilated | antonyms: airtight, stuffy, sealed | adjective

drafty:/ˈdrɑːfti/ | adj. | (指计划、文件等)粗略的；初步的；未定稿的 | (of a plan, document, etc.) preliminary; not in final form. |  We reviewed the drafty proposal and suggested several changes. 我们审查了初步的提案，并提出了一些修改意见。| synonyms: preliminary, provisional, tentative | antonyms: final, finished, complete | adjective
