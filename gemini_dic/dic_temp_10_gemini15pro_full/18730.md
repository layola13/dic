bergh:/bɛrx/ | n. | mountain, hill | A mountain or hill, especially a prominent or imposing one.  From Middle Dutch *berch, from Proto-Germanic *bergō (“mountain”). Cognate with German Berg, Dutch berg, and English barrow. |  The hikers reached the bergh after a long climb.  登山者经过长时间的攀登到达了山顶。 | synonyms: mount, peak, summit | antonyms: valley, dale | noun
