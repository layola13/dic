brinkmanship: /ˈbrɪŋkmənʃɪp/ | n. | 边缘政策 | The art or practice of pursuing a dangerous policy to the limits of safety before stopping, especially in politics. |  The two countries were engaged in a dangerous game of brinkmanship over the disputed territory. 这两个国家在争议领土问题上进行着一场危险的边缘政策游戏。

The Cold War was a period of intense brinkmanship between the US and the Soviet Union. 冷战时期是美苏之间激烈边缘政策的时期。


近义词:  calculated risk, gamble, daredevilry | 反义词: caution, prudence, diplomacy | 词性: noun
