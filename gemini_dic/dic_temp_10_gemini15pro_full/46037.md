cyclamen: /ˈsaɪkləmən/ (US), /ˈsɪkləmən/ (UK) | n. | 仙客来，兔耳花 | A plant of the primrose family, with pink, red, or white flowers with backward-turned petals.  It is commonly cultivated as a houseplant. |  The cyclamen brightened up the windowsill. 仙客来点亮了窗台。| synonyms: sowbread | antonyms: N/A | noun
