contingent:/kənˈtɪndʒənt/| adj. n.|依情况而定的，可能的；偶然事件；代表团，小分队|Depending on something else that might or might not happen; a group of people representing a larger group|The trip is contingent on the weather. 这趟旅行取决于天气。|近义词：dependent, conditional, group, delegation|反义词:independent, unconditional|词性:形容词,名词

contingent:/kənˈtɪndʒənt/| n.|代表团，小分队|A group of people representing a larger group|The university sent a large contingent to the conference. 大学派了一个很大的代表团参加会议。|近义词：group, delegation, detachment|反义词:individual|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French contingent, from Latin contingens, present participle of contingere "to touch, befall," from com- "together" + tangere "to touch."
