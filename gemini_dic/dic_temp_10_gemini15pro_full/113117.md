lunged:/lʌndʒd/
| v. | 猛冲，突刺，|A sudden forward movement.  |He lunged at me with a knife. 他拿着刀向我猛扑过来。|
|近义词：thrust, pounce, dive, leap|
|反义词：retreat, withdraw|
|词性: 动词(past tense of lunge)|
|词源: From Old French "лонgier," meaning "to go along."|
