foreword:/ˈfɔːrwɜːrd/ (US) /ˈfɔːwəd/ (UK)| n.|前言，序言|A short introductory section of a book, typically written by someone other than the author, that provides context or an endorsement.|The foreword to the book was written by a renowned scholar.|Synonyms: preface, introduction, preamble|Antonyms: afterword, epilogue|noun
