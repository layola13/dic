gilled:/ɡɪld/| adj.|有鳃的，有菌褶的|Having gills (respiratory organs of fish) or gill-like structures, especially the radiating plates on the underside of the cap of a mushroom.|例句：The gilled mushroom was poisonous.  这种有菌褶的蘑菇有毒。|近义词：lamellate, lamelled|反义词:agill|词性:形容词

gilled:/ɡɪld/| adj.|浸透的，充满的 (less common)|Saturated, imbued. (This meaning is less common and usually poetic.)|例句：His shirt was gilled with sweat. 他的衬衫被汗水浸透了。|近义词：saturated, soaked, drenched|反义词:dry|词性:形容词
