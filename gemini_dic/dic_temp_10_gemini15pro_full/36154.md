cofavorite:/ˈkoʊˈfeɪvərɪt/
noun
共同的最受欢迎的人/物
a person or thing equally favored with another
例句：The two horses were cofavorites to win the race.  这两匹马是比赛的共同热门。

例句：Both candidates are cofavorites going into the final debate. 两位候选人在进入最终辩论时都是最受欢迎的。
近义词：joint favorite, equal favorite
反义词: underdog, long shot
词性: n.
