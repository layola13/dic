nonsacrificial:/ˌnɒnˌsækrɪˈfɪʃəl/
adjective
非牺牲的; 不需牺牲的
Not involving or requiring sacrifice.
The company adopted a nonsacrificial approach to environmental protection, finding ways to reduce emissions without impacting productivity.  这家公司采取了一种非牺牲性的环境保护方法，找到了在不影响生产力的情况下减少排放的方法。
They developed a nonsacrificial testing method that didn't harm the animals. 他们开发了一种非牺牲性的测试方法，不会伤害动物。
synonym: non-immolating, sparing
antonym: sacrificial, immolating
词性: adjective
