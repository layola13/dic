moratoriums:/ˌmɔːrəˈtɔːriəmz/
n.
暂停，中止，延期
A temporary prohibition of an activity.
The government has announced a moratorium on nuclear testing. 政府宣布暂停核试验。
suspension, postponement, halt, ban
resumption, continuation
noun
