gardenful: /ˈɡɑːrdənˌfʊl/ | n. | 一花园; 满园; 花园里的全部 | A gardenful is the amount of something that a garden can hold or produce. |  The child brought in a gardenful of flowers. 孩子带进来满满一花园的花。| synonyms: abundance, plenty | antonyms: lack, scarcity | noun

gardenful: /ˈɡɑːrdənˌfʊl/ | adj. | 充满花园的; 足够填满花园的 | Filled with or sufficient to fill a garden. | The gardenful scent of roses filled the air. 浓郁的玫瑰花香弥漫在空气中。| synonyms: abundant, copious | antonyms: sparse, meager | adjective
