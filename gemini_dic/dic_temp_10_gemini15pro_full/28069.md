caperingly:/ˈkeɪpərɪŋli/
adverb
雀跃地，欢快地
in a playful and lively way, skipping or jumping about.
She danced caperingly across the meadow. 她雀跃地穿过草地。
Synonyms: playfully, merrily, joyfully, friskily, exuberantly
Antonyms: solemnly, gloomily, sadly, dejectedly, listlessly
词性: 副词
