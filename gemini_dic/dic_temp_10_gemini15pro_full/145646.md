overcontentiousness: /ˌoʊvərˌkənˈtɛnʃəsnəs/
overcontentiousness: |n.| 过于争论的倾向；好争辩 |The state of being excessively inclined to argue or dispute. |His overcontentiousness made it difficult to have a productive conversation. 他好争辩的性格使得很难进行富有成效的对话。|Near synonyms: argumentativeness, disputatiousness, quarrelsomeness |Antonyms: agreeableness, amiability, peacefulness
overcontentiousness: |noun| 过于争论的倾向；好争辩


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + contentiousness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," and "contentiousness" is the noun form of "contentious," meaning "likely to cause an argument; controversial."  "Contentious" ultimately derives from the Latin word "contendere," meaning "to strive, contend."
