aren't:/ɑːrnt/ |v. |不是|Contraction of "are not". It's used in negative statements and questions.|例句：They aren't coming to the party. 他们不会来参加聚会了。|近义词：are not|反义词:are|verb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
They aren't happy with the results.  他们对结果不满意。
We aren't going to the cinema tonight. 我们今晚不去电影院。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
You aren't listening to me. 你没有在听我说话。
They aren't working on the project right now. 他们现在没有在做这个项目。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I aren't finished with my homework yet. 我还没有完成我的作业。（不标准用法，通常用haven't）
We aren't been to Paris before. 我们以前没去过巴黎。（不标准用法，通常用haven't）

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
They aren't been working on this all day. 他们并没有整天都在做这件事。（不标准用法，通常用haven't）
We aren't been waiting here for very long. 我们没有在这里等很久。（不标准用法，通常用haven't）

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
They weren't at the meeting yesterday. 他们昨天没有参加会议。
We weren't invited to the party. 我们没有被邀请参加聚会。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I wasn't sleeping when you called. 你打电话的时候我没有在睡觉。
They weren't playing football at that time. 那时他们没有在踢足球。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
They hadn't finished the project before the deadline. 他们在截止日期前没有完成项目。
We hadn't been to London before last year.  去年之前我们没有去过伦敦。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
They hadn't been working on the problem for very long. 他们没有处理这个问题很长时间。
We hadn't been waiting for the bus for long when it arrived. 公交车到达时，我们没有等很久。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
They won't be happy with the outcome. 他们不会对结果感到满意。（更常用）
We aren't going to be there tomorrow. 我们明天不会在那里。（aren't going to 比 will not be 更常用）


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I won't be working at this time tomorrow. 明天这个时候我不会在工作。（更常用）
They won't be attending the conference next week.  他们下周不会参加会议。（更常用）

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
They won't have finished the project by next week.  他们下周前不会完成这个项目。（更常用）
We won't have been to Japan by then. 到那时我们还不会去过日本。（更常用）

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
They won't have been working on this for very long by the time you arrive. 到你到达的时候，他们做这件事的时间不会很长。（更常用）
We won't have been living here for ten years by next June. 到明年六月，我们住在这里的时间还不到十年。（更常用）



PHRASE->
Why aren't you coming? 你为什么不来？
They aren't what they used to be. 他们已经不是以前的样子了。
Aren't you forgetting something? 你是不是忘了什么？
Aren't you glad to see me? 你难道不高兴见到我吗？
I aren't I lucky? 我不是很幸运吗？ (非正式， 语法上不完全正确，通常用 Aren't I lucky? 或 Am I not lucky?)


注意事项:

"aren't" 主要用于一般现在时和一般过去时。在其他时态中，使用完整的 "are not" 或 "were not" 更为常见，尤其是在正式场合。

"aren't I" 在疑问句中代替 "am I not"，虽然语法上不完全正确，但在口语中很常见。
