nonvaporosities:/ˌnɒnvæpəˈrɒsɪtiz/
noun
非挥发性; 不挥发性
The lack of volatility or the property of not readily evaporating.  The nonvaporosities of the substance made it suitable for long-term storage.
The nonvaporosities of the heavy oils made them difficult to ignite.  这种重油的非挥发性使它们难以点燃。
Near synonyms: nonvolatility, fixity
Antonyms: volatility, evaporativeness
