geotag:/ˈdʒiːoʊtæɡ/ | n. |地理标记，地理标签| A piece of data that associates a particular location with digital content, such as a photograph or video.  It typically contains latitude and longitude coordinates, and may also include altitude, bearing, or other information. | I always geotag my travel photos so I can remember where I took them. 我总是给我的旅行照片添加地理标签，这样我就能记住在哪里拍的。 | Example: The geotag on the photo showed that it was taken in Paris.  照片上的地理标签显示它是在巴黎拍摄的。 | Synonyms: geolocaiton, place tag, location tag | Antonyms: N/A | noun

geotag:/ˈdʒiːoʊtæɡ/ | vt. |添加地理标记，添加地理标签| To add a geotag to digital content. | I geotagged all the photos from my trip. 我给旅行的所有照片都添加了地理标签。| Example:  She geotagged the video of the concert. 她给演唱会的视频添加了地理标签。 | Synonyms: geolocate, place tag, location tag | Antonyms: N/A | verb
