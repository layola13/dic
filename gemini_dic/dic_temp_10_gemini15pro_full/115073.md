maneuver:/məˈnuːvər/ (US) /məˈnuːvə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|策略，调遣；演习；操作|A movement or series of moves requiring skill and care.|例句：The driver made a skillful maneuver to avoid the accident. 司机熟练地操纵方向盘以避免事故。|近义词：tactic, strategy, operation, manipulate|反义词:inaction, stillness|词性:noun, verb

maneuver:/məˈnuːvər/ (US) /məˈnuːvə(r)/ (UK)| v.|操纵，控制；调遣；演习|To move skillfully or carefully.|例句：The troops maneuvered into position for the attack. 部队调遣到位准备进攻。|近义词：manipulate, operate, control, navigate|反义词:fail, neglect|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *manœuvrer*, from Italian *manovrare*, "to handle," ultimately from Latin *manu operare*, "to work by hand."
