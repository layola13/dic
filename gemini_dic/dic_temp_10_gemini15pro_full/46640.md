damnedest:/ˈdæmdɪst/ | adj. | 最奇特的，最令人惊讶的；极其的，非常的 |  Used to emphasize how surprising or unusual something is.  It's a euphemism for "damned," softening its impact. | That's the damnedest thing I've ever heard!  那是我听过的最奇怪的事情！

She had the damnedest time trying to start her car this morning. 今早她发动汽车费了好大的劲。 | synonyms: strangest, oddest, weirdest, most peculiar, most unusual, most surprising, darnedest (more informal) | antonyms:  ordinary, usual, common, expected | adjective
