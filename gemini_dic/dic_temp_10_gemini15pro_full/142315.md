例句：She had an orthodoxastical approach to grammar, correcting everyone's slightest mistakes. 她对语法有一种正统的方法，纠正每个人最细微的错误。 | 近义词：dogmatic, rigid, doctrinaire | 反义词：unorthodox, heterodox, liberal | 词性：adjective
