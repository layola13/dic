binocularly:/bɪˈnɒkjʊləli/ /baɪˈnɑːkjələrli/| adv.|用双眼地，双目地|Using both eyes at the same time; with both eyes.|例句：He looked at the bird binocularly through his field glasses. 他用望远镜双眼观察那只鸟。|近义词：stereoscopically|反义词:monocularly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *binoculus* (having two eyes), from *bini* (two each, two by two) + *oculus* (eye).
