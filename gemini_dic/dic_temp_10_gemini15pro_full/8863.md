antifeminists:/ˌæntiˈfɛmɪnɪsts/ | n. | 反女权主义者 |  A person who opposes feminism or the goals of the feminist movement. |  Some antifeminists believe that feminism has gone too far.  一些反女权主义者认为女权主义已经走得太远了。|  misogynists, sexists | feminists, egalitarians | Noun

antifeminist:/ˌæntiˈfɛmɪnɪst/ | adj. | 反女权主义的 | Opposing or hostile to feminism. |  He made some antifeminist remarks. 他发表了一些反女权主义的言论。 |  misogynistic, sexist | feminist, egalitarian | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + feminist.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to." "Feminist" comes from "feminism,"  which is derived from the French word "féminisme," ultimately from Latin "femina" meaning "woman."
