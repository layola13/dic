cruellest:/ˈkruːəlɪst/ | adj. | 最残酷的，最狠毒的 |  Superlative form of cruel: most cruel.  Exhibiting or having the quality of inflicting pain and suffering, especially deliberately. |  He committed the cruellest act imaginable. 他犯下了最残酷的罪行。| Synonyms:  most brutal, most heartless, most merciless, most savage, most vicious | Antonyms: kindest, most compassionate, most merciful, most humane | adjective
