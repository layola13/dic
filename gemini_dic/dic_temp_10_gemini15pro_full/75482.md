frustration:/frʌˈstreɪʃn/ | n. | 沮丧，挫败；令人沮丧的事物 |The state of being frustrated; a feeling of annoyance and dissatisfaction because you cannot achieve what you want. |  I felt a deep sense of frustration at my inability to communicate. 我因无法沟通而感到深深的沮丧。

The continued delays were a source of frustration for everyone.  持续的延误让每个人都很沮丧。

近义词:disappointment, annoyance, exasperation | 反义词:satisfaction, contentment | 词性:noun
