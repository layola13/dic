centauromachy:/ˌsɛntɔːˈrɒməki/| n.|人马之战，半人马之战|A battle between centaurs and Lapiths, a frequent subject in Greek art and literature.  It symbolizes the struggle between civilization and barbarism, reason and instinct.|The Parthenon frieze depicts scenes of the intense centauromachy.|Synonyms: battle, struggle, conflict|Antonyms: peace, harmony, truce|noun
