berthierite:/ˈbɛrθiəˌraɪt/| n. |硫锑铁矿|A steel-gray metallic mineral, FeSb₂S₄, an important ore of antimony. |Berthierite is often found in hydrothermal veins. 硫锑铁矿常在热液矿脉中发现。|Berthierite is a source of antimony used in flame retardants. 硫锑铁矿是用于阻燃剂的锑的来源。|Synonyms: stibnite, jamesonite|Antonyms: None|Noun
