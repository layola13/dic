medleyest:/ˈmɛdliɪst/ | adj. | 最混合的;最多样化的 |  This word is a superlative form of "medleyed," meaning "consisting of a medley."  It describes something composed of a diverse range of elements or characterized by a great variety. While grammatically correct, it is exceedingly rare and archaic.
