intent:/ɪnˈtɛnt/ | n. adj.| 意图，目的；坚决的 | An aim or plan.  Determined to do something.| She went there with the intent of finding a job. 她去那里的目的是找工作。 He had an intent look on his face. 他脸上有一种坚定的神情。 |近义词：purpose, aim, goal, objective, resolute, determined |反义词: apathy, indifference, hesitant | noun, adjective
