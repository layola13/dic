anagogical:/ˌænəˈɡɒdʒɪkəl/| adj.|神秘的，寓意的，灵性的|Relating to or revealing a higher spiritual meaning, typically concerning biblical interpretation.|The professor offered an anagogical interpretation of the text, connecting the story to the journey of the soul.|近义词：spiritual, allegorical, symbolic, mystical|反义词：literal, concrete|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *anagogicus*, from Greek *anagōgikos*, from *anagōgē* "a leading upward," from *ana-* "up" + *agein* "to lead."
