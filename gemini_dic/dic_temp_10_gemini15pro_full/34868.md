clint:/klɪnt/
n.
悬崖，峭壁
A steep, high rock face, especially one that drops to the sea.
The clint rose sharply from the ocean. 峭壁从海洋中陡然升起。
近义词:cliff, precipice, bluff
反义词:plain, flat, valley
词性:名词
