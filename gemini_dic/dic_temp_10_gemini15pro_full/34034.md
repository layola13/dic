citronella:/ˌsɪtrəˈnɛlə/| n.|香茅，香茅油|A natural oil derived from lemongrass and other plants, used as an insect repellent.|例句：She burned citronella candles to keep the mosquitoes away. 她点燃香茅蜡烛来驱赶蚊子。

citronellas:/ˌsɪtrəˈnɛləz/| n.|香茅油（复数）|Plural form of citronella, referring to multiple instances or types of citronella oil.|例句：The store sold various citronellas, each with a slightly different scent.  这家商店出售各种香茅油，每种都有略微不同的香味。|近义词：lemongrass oil, insect repellent|反义词:|词性:名词

词源: From Spanish *citronela, diminutive of *citrón "citron," from Italian cedro.
