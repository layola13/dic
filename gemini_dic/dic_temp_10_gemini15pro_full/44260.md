croise:/kwɑːˈzweɪ/ /krwɑːˈzeɪ/ | adj. | 交叉的，斜纹的 |  (from French, past participle of *croiser* "to cross") Interlaced, intertwined, or arranged in a crosswise pattern; (of fabric) woven with a diagonal rib or twill. | The curtains were made of a beautiful croise fabric. 窗帘是用漂亮的斜纹布料做的。 |  The knight wore a surcoat of croise patterned silk. 骑士穿着一件交叉图案丝绸的罩袍。 | Synonyms:  twilled, diagonal, intersecting | Antonyms: straight, parallel | Adjective
