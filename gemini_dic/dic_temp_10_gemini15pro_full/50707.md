dermophyte:/ˈdɜːrməˌfaɪt/| n. |皮肤癣菌|A fungus that causes skin diseases, especially ringworm.|Example 1: The dermatologist diagnosed the rash as a dermophyte infection. 皮肤科医生诊断皮疹为皮肤癣菌感染。

Example 2:  Dermophytes thrive in warm, moist environments. 皮肤癣菌在温暖潮湿的环境中 thrive。|Synonym: tinea, ringworm fungus|Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym for a biological organism)|noun
