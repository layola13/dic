misthrow:/ˌmɪsˈθroʊ/ | v. | 投掷失误，错扔 | To throw inaccurately or to the wrong place; to throw away (a chance, opportunity, etc.). | The quarterback misthrew the ball, and the receiver couldn't catch it. 四分卫传球失误，接球手没接住。

He misthrew his best opportunity to get promoted. 他错失了晋升的最佳机会。 | 近义词: miscast, mishandle, fumble | 反义词: throw accurately, catch, seize | 词性: 动词
