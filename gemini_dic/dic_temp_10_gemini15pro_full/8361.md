anti-pragmatical:/ˌæntipræɡˈmætɪkəl/
adjective
反实用主义的；反对实用主义的
opposed to or distrustful of practical considerations.
He has a rather anti-pragmatical approach to life, preferring ideals to practicality. 他有一种相当反实用主义的生活方式，偏爱理想主义而非实用主义。
She took an anti-pragmatical stance, arguing for ethical considerations over economic ones.  她采取了反实用主义的立场，主张伦理考虑高于经济考虑。
近义词: impractical, idealistic, unpractical, unrealistic
反义词: pragmatic, practical, realistic
词性: adjective

词源:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to  (源自希腊语 anti-)
pragmatical: dealing with things sensibly and realistically in a way that is based on practical rather than theoretical considerations (源自希腊语 pragma, meaning "deed, act")
