drop-out:/ˈdrɑːpaʊt/ (US) /ˈdrɒpaʊt/ (UK)| n. |辍学者；中途退学的人|A person who leaves school, college, or university before completing a course of study.|He was a high school dropout. 他是个高中辍学生。|Synonyms: quitter, non-graduate|Antonyms: graduate, completer|noun


drop out:/ˈdrɑːp aʊt/ (US) /ˈdrɒp aʊt/ (UK)| v. |辍学；退出；脱离|To withdraw from school, college, or a course of study; to cease to participate in something.|She dropped out of college in her sophomore year. 她在大学二年级时辍学了。|Synonyms: withdraw, quit, leave|Antonyms: continue, persist, graduate|verb
