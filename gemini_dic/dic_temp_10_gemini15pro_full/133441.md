nonfastidiously:/ˌnɒnfæsˈtɪdiəsli/
adverb
不挑剔地，不苛求地
in a way that is not fastidious; not overly concerned with detail or cleanliness.
He ate the street food nonfastidiously, enjoying every bite despite the less-than-sanitary conditions. 他不挑剔地吃着街边小吃，尽管卫生条件不太好，却享受着每一口。
She packed her suitcase nonfastidiously, simply throwing in whatever clothes she could find. 她不讲究地收拾行李箱，只是把能找到的衣服都扔进去。
Synonym: indiscriminately, casually, uncritically
Antonym: fastidiously, meticulously, scrupulously
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): not
fastidious (adjective): very attentive to and concerned about accuracy and detail. From Latin fastidium "loathing, disgust"
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
