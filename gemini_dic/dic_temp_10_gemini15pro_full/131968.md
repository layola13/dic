nonconventionally: /ˌnɒnkənˈvɛnʃənəli/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈvɛnʃənəli/ | adv. | 非传统地；非惯例地 | In a way that is not usual, traditional, or accepted by most people. |  She decorated her apartment nonconventionally, using recycled materials and found objects. 她用回收材料和捡来的物品非传统地装饰了她的公寓。| Synonyms: unconventionally, unusually, atypically, idiosyncratically | Antonyms: conventionally, traditionally, typically, normally | 词性: 副词

nonconventional: /ˌnɒnkənˈvɛnʃənəl/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈvɛnʃənəl/ | adj. | 非传统的；非惯例的 | Not conforming to accepted rules or methods; different and interesting. |  He has a nonconventional approach to teaching. 他有一种非传统的教学方法。 | Synonyms: unconventional, unusual, atypical, unorthodox | Antonyms: conventional, traditional, typical, orthodox | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + conventional + -ly.  "Conventional" comes from the Late Latin "conventionālis," meaning "agreed upon."  "Non-" is a prefix indicating negation.  Therefore, "nonconventionally" means "not in an agreed-upon or usual way."
