non-advantageous:/ˌnɒnˌædvənˈteɪdʒəs/| adj.|无利的，不利条件的|Not favorable or beneficial; disadvantageous.|Investing in that company right now would be non-advantageous given the current market conditions.  鉴于目前的市场状况，现在投资那家公司是不利的。|The non-advantageous location of the store led to its closure. 这家店位置不好，导致其关闭。|近义词:disadvantageous, unfavorable, detrimental, harmful|反义词:advantageous, beneficial, favorable, helpful|词性:形容词

词源:
"non-" (prefix):  from Latin *non < *noenum < ne oinom "not one," from PIE *ne "not" + *oinos "one." 表示否定。
"advantageous": from Late Middle English, from Old French avantageus, from avantage "advantage."  表示有利的。
