gauge:/ɡeɪdʒ/| n. v. |测量仪器，规格，标准；估计，判断|An instrument for measuring or testing; a standard of measure or assessment; to estimate or judge.|例句：The fuel gauge indicated that the tank was almost empty. 油表显示油箱快空了。|近义词：measure, estimate, assess, judge|反义词:misjudge|noun, verb


gauge:/ɡeɪdʒ/| n. v. |厚度，直径（金属板、金属丝）|The thickness, diameter, or size of something, especially wire, sheet metal, or ammunition.|例句：Use a thin gauge wire for this project.  这个项目要用细规格的金属丝。|近义词：thickness, diameter, caliber|反义词: N/A|noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old North French *gaugie (unattested), from Vulgar Latin *galgia, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Frankish *galgo "rod, stick" (compare Old High German galgo "pole").
