Often used to describe jewelry, fur, or other materials that imitate the real thing.| This handbag is made of fausse crocodile skin.  这个手提包是用人造鳄鱼皮做的。|  She wore a string of fausse pearls. 她戴着一串人造珍珠。|synonyms: artificial, fake, imitation, synthetic, counterfeit, spurious|antonyms: genuine, real, authentic, natural|adjective
