nummular:/ˈnʌmjʊlər/| adj.|钱币状的，圆形的，硬币状的|Resembling a coin or coins in shape; disc-shaped.  Derived from "nummus," Latin for "coin."|The lichen formed nummular patches on the rock.  地衣在岩石上形成了硬币状的斑块。|The doctor noted the nummular eczema on the patient's arm. 医生注意到病人手臂上的硬币状湿疹。|近义词：circular, discoid, round|反义词:irregular, amorphous|词性:adjective
