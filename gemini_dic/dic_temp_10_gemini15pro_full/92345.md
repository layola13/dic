hypoblast:/ˈhaɪpəˌblæst/| n. |下胚层|The inner layer of cells in the blastocyst, which develops into the endoderm.  它是囊胚的内细胞层，发育为内胚层。|The hypoblast is a crucial structure in early embryonic development. 下胚层是早期胚胎发育中的关键结构。| The hypoblast gives rise to the yolk sac. 下胚层会形成卵黄囊。|synonym: primitive endoderm|antonym: epiblast|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hypo- (under) + *blastos (germ, sprout)
