meaningfully:/ˈmiːnɪŋfəli/| adv.|有意义地，意味深长地|In a way that has meaning and importance; significantly.|She smiled meaningfully at him. 她意味深长地对他笑了笑。|近义词：significantly, purposefully, expressively|反义词：meaninglessly, pointlessly|词性:副词

词源：meaningful (形容词) + -ly (副词后缀)
meaningful 来自 meaning (名词，意义) + -ful (形容词后缀)
meaning 来自 mean (动词，意思是)
