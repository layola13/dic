demerge:/diːˈmɜːrdʒ/
verb
分拆，拆分（公司）
To separate (a business) into two or more independent companies.
The company plans to demerge its retail division next year.  这家公司计划明年将其零售部门分拆。
The telecommunications giant will demerge its mobile and fixed-line businesses. 这家电信巨头将分拆其移动和固定电话业务。
Synonyms:  separate, split, divest, spin off, unbundle
Antonyms: merge, combine, consolidate
词性: 动词
