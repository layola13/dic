cerote:/seˈɾote/| n. |耳屎; (墨西哥俚语, 贬义) 白痴，笨蛋 |A derogatory term used in Mexican Spanish to refer to someone as an idiot or fool.  It literally refers to earwax.|Example 1: ¡No seas cerote! (Don't be an idiot!)  别犯傻了！

Example 2:  Ese cerote no sabe nada. (That idiot doesn't know anything.) 那个笨蛋什么都不知道。|Synonym: idiota, imbécil, estúpido, tonto|Antonym: genio, inteligente|Noun
