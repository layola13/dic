antievolutionisms:/ˌæntiˌevəˈluːʃənɪzəmz/
n.
反对进化论
Theories and beliefs that oppose the concept of biological evolution.
His antievolutionisms led him to believe the earth was only a few thousand years old. 他对进化论的反对使他相信地球只有几千年的历史。
近义词: creationism, intelligent design
反义词: evolutionism, Darwinism
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (against) + evolution (the process of gradual development) + -ism (a doctrine, belief, or theory)
