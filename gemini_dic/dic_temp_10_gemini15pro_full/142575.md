osteoclasis:/ˌɒstiːˈɒkləsɪs/ /ˌɑːstiːoʊˈkleɪsɪs/
noun
外科骨折; 骨折术
The surgical breaking of a bone, often done to reset a badly healed fracture.
Example: The doctor performed osteoclasis to correct the malunion of the femur.  医生进行了外科骨折术以矫正股骨的畸形愈合。

Example: Osteoclasis was necessary to straighten the child's deformed leg. 为了矫正孩子的畸形腿，需要进行外科骨折术。
Synonym: osteoclasty, fracture (noun, verb)
Antonym: bone healing, osteogenesis
n.
