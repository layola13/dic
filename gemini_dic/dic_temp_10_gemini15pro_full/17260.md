baying:/ˈbeɪɪŋ/ | n. | 吠叫声；强烈抗议 | The baying of the hounds echoed through the forest.  猎犬的吠叫声在森林中回荡。| The baying of the crowd grew louder as the referee made a controversial call.  当裁判做出有争议的判罚时，人群的抗议声越来越大。 | 近义词：howling, barking, clamor | 反义词：silence, whisper | 词性: 名词

baying:/ˈbeɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 吠叫的；强烈抗议的 |  The baying hounds chased the fox. 吠叫的猎犬追赶着狐狸。|  The baying mob demanded justice.  喧嚣的暴民要求正义。| 近义词：howling, barking, clamorous | 反义词：silent, quiet | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Middle English *bayen*, from Old French *abaier* (to bark), from Vulgar Latin *badare* (to gape, open the mouth wide). Ultimately from Latin *battuere* (to beat, strike).
