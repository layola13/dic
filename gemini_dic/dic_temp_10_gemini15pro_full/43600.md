creaminesses:/ˈkriːminɪsɪz/
n.
乳脂状，浓郁，柔滑
The quality of being creamy; a smooth, rich texture.
The creaminesses of the cheese sauce were delightful.  奶酪酱的浓郁口感令人愉悦。
近义词: richness, smoothness, velvetiness
反义词: thinness, wateriness
词性: 名词 (plural of creaminess)

creaminess:/ˈkriːminɪs/
n.
乳脂状，浓郁，柔滑
The quality of being creamy; a smooth, rich texture.
The creaminess of the ice cream was perfect.  冰淇淋的奶油味恰到好处。
近义词: richness, smoothness, velvetiness
反义词: thinness, wateriness
词性: 名词

词源: creaminess comes from the adjective "creamy," which in turn derives from "cream." "Cream" has Proto-Germanic roots and ultimately traces back to the Proto-Indo-European root *kre-, meaning "to grow, swell."  The suffix "-iness" is used to form nouns indicating a state or quality.
