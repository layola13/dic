four-star:/ˈfɔːr stɑːr (US) / /fɔː stɑː (UK)/| adj.|四星级的；一流的|Of or relating to a hotel, restaurant, etc., that has been given a rating of four stars, indicating high quality.|This is a four-star hotel with excellent service. 这是一家服务一流的四星级酒店。|Synonyms: top-rated, first-class, high-quality, luxury, deluxe|Antonyms: low-quality, poor, substandard|adjective
