cormophytes:/ˈkɔːrməˌfaɪts/| n. |维管植物；茎叶植物|Plants that possess a stem, roots, and leaves, and reproduce by seeds or spores, including ferns, conifers, and flowering plants.  These plants have a vascular system for transporting water and nutrients.|Example: Cormophytes are the dominant group of land plants. 维管植物是陆地植物的优势群体。

Example:  Ferns and flowering plants are examples of cormophytes. 蕨类植物和开花植物都是维管植物的例子。|近义词：vascular plants|反义词: Thallophytes (e.g., algae, fungi, lichens)|词性: noun
