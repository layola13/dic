PHRASE->
There isn't a specific set of phrases using "bear up" like "look up" or "give up."  It's generally used on its own. However, you can modify it with adverbs:

He bore up bravely.  他勇敢地坚持着。
She bore up remarkably well. 她表现得非常好。
He struggled to bear up under the pressure. 他努力承受压力。
She managed to bear up despite the difficulties. 尽管困难重重，她还是设法坚持了下来。
It was hard for him to bear up during that time. 在那段时间里，他很难坚持下去。


词源： “bear” (承受) + “up” (向上，维持) -  表示在困难中保持精神或情绪上的“向上”状态。
