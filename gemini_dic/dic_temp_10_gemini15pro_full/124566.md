mourn:/mɔːrn/ (UK), /mɔːrn/ (US)| v.|哀悼，忧伤|To feel or express deep sorrow, especially because of someone's death.|She mourned the loss of her beloved pet. 她哀悼她心爱宠物的离去。

He mourned for his lost youth. 他为他逝去的青春而悲伤。

近义词：grieve, lament, sorrow, bewail|反义词：rejoice, celebrate|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *murnan, from Proto-Germanic *murnaną, from Proto-Indo-European *smer- (to care, worry). Cognates include Old High German mornēn, Old Norse morna, Gothic maurnan.
