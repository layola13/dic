|n.|非消费性，不消费的状态|The nonconsumptivenesses of certain goods during a recession can be a telling economic indicator.|某些商品在经济衰退期间的非消费性可以成为一个重要的经济指标。|近义词:non-consumption, abstinence|反义词:consumption, expenditure|词性:名词

词源: non- + consumption + -tiveness + -es

non-:  prefix meaning "not"
consumption:  from Late Latin *consumptio(n-), from consumere "to consume,"
-tiveness: suffix forming nouns from adjectives, expressing state or condition
-es: plural suffix
