comitia:/kəˈmɪʃ(ɪ)ə/ /kəˈmɪtiə/ | n. |古罗马平民大会，选举会议| An assembly of the Roman people, especially for elections. |  The comitia centuriata elected the consuls. 古罗马百人团大会选举了执政官。
