capstone:/ˈkæpstəʊn/ (UK) /ˈkæpˌstoʊn/ (US)| n.|顶石，基石，毕业项目|A capstone is the final, most important, or crowning part of something, like the top stone of a building or a final project in a student's education.|The senior thesis is the capstone of the undergraduate program. 毕业论文是本科课程的顶峰。|synonyms: culmination, climax, keystone, centerpiece|antonyms: foundation, base|noun
