disciplines:/ˈdɪsəplɪnz/ (US), /ˈdɪsɪplɪnz/ (UK)| n. |学科，纪律，训练，惩罚|A branch of knowledge or learning. Training that corrects, molds, or perfects the mental faculties or moral character. Control gained by enforcing obedience or order. Punishment intended to correct or train.|
例句：The university offers disciplines in arts and sciences. 这所大学提供文科和理科的学科。
例句：He excelled in all academic disciplines. 他在所有学科都很优秀。

近义词：fields, areas, subjects, training, instruction, control, order, punishment, correction
反义词：disorder, chaos, lawlessness, freedom, liberty

词性: noun
