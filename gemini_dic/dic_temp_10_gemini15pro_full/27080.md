calibrators: /ˈkælɪˌbreɪtərz/ | n. | 校准器，校准员 | A person or device that calibrates something.  A calibrator is used to ensure the accuracy of a measuring instrument. | The technician used a calibrator to adjust the sensor. 技术人员使用校准器调整传感器。|  The lab uses certified calibrators to maintain accuracy. 实验室使用经过认证的校准器来保持精度。 | synonyms: adjuster, regulator, standardizer | antonyms:  N/A | noun
