ludig:/ˈluːdɪɡ/ | adj. | playful; mischievous; boisterous |  Characterized by noisy, energetic, and often slightly unruly fun.  Derived from the German word "lud" meaning "loud" or "noisy" and the suffix "-ig" which makes it an adjective. | The children were ludig, running around and shouting with glee. 孩子们嬉闹着，跑来跑去，高兴地喊叫着。|  The ludig puppies tumbled over each other in the garden.  嬉闹的小狗在花园里互相翻滚。| Synonyms:  playful, frolicsome, rambunctious, mischievous | Antonyms: quiet, calm, subdued, well-behaved | word type: adjective
