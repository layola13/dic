hutzpah:/ˈhʊtspə/ (US), /ˈxʊtspə/ (UK)| n.|胆大妄为；厚颜无耻；放肆；勇往直前|Supreme self-confidence or audacity. Often used to describe someone who is so bold or brazen that it's almost admirable, even if their actions are questionable.|He had the hutzpah to ask for a raise after only a week on the job. 他竟然敢在工作才一周就要求加薪。|Synonyms: nerve, audacity, chutzpa, guts, boldness, brazenness, temerity|Antonyms: timidity, shyness, meekness, diffidence, humility|Noun
