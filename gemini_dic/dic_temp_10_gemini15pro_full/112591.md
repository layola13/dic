lovey-dovey:/ˈlʌvi ˈdʌvi/| adj.|卿卿我我的，情意绵绵的|Expressing or showing a lot of love and affection, often excessively.|They were being all lovey-dovey in the corner. 他们在角落里卿卿我我。|近义词：affectionate, amorous, cuddly|反义词：cold, distant, aloof|词性:形容词
Etymology:  Reduplication of "lovey," a pet name derived from "love."  The reduplication adds a playful and sometimes slightly mocking tone.
