homotropal:/ˌhɒməˈtrəʊpəl/ /ˌhoʊməˈtroʊpəl/ | adj. | 同向的，顺生的 | Having the same direction or curvature; specifically (Botany) describing ovules that are curved or turned in the same direction as the placenta. | Example 1: The homotropal arrangement of the ovules is a characteristic feature of this plant species.  这种植物物种的胚珠同向排列是一个特征。 | Example 2:  In contrast to the more common anatropous ovules, these plants have homotropal ovules. 与更常见的倒生胚珠相比，这些植物具有顺生胚珠。 | 近义词: orthotropous | 反义词: anatropous, campylotropous | 词性: 形容词
