old-worldly:/ˌoʊld ˈwɜːrldli/ | adj. | 旧世界的；古朴的；守旧的 | Characteristic of or belonging to a former time, especially in being quaint, charming, or unsophisticated.  It often implies a resistance to modern ways. |  She had an old-worldly charm about her, always wearing vintage dresses. 她有一种旧世界的魅力，总是穿着复古的连衣裙。 | The village had an old-worldly atmosphere, with cobbled streets and thatched cottages. 这个村庄有一种旧世界的氛围，鹅卵石街道和茅草屋顶的小屋。| 近义词: traditional, quaint, nostalgic, old-fashioned, antique | 反义词: modern, contemporary, up-to-date, progressive | 词性: 形容词
