organize: /ˈɔːrɡənaɪz/ (UK) /ˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪz/ (US) | v. |组织，安排，整理| To arrange something in a structured way; to put things into order. |例句：She organized a surprise party for her friend. 她为她的朋友组织了一个惊喜派对。|例句：He needs to organize his thoughts before the presentation. 他需要在演讲前整理一下思路。|近义词：arrange, coordinate, manage, plan, structure|反义词：disorganize, mess up|词性:动词

词源: From Old French *organiser, from Late Latin organizare, from Latin organum (“instrument, tool”) (from Ancient Greek ὄργανον (órganon, “instrument, tool, organ”)).
