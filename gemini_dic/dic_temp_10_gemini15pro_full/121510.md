misliking:/mɪsˈlaɪkɪŋ/
n.
不喜欢，厌恶
a feeling of dislike or aversion.
例句：She had a deep misliking for loud music. 她非常不喜欢吵闹的音乐。

例句：His misliking of the new manager was evident. 他对新经理的厌恶显而易见。

近义词：dislike, aversion, antipathy, distaste
反义词：liking, fondness, affection
词性：名词

词源：From Middle English *mislikyng, equivalent to mis- + liking.  The verb "mislike" predates the noun form.
