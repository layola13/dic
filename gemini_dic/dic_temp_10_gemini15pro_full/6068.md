amentum:/ˈæməntəm/ /əˈmɛntəm/| n. |柔荑花序; 尾状物|A drooping spike of flowers, as in the willow or hazel.  A catkin.  Also, a thong-like appendage.|The willow tree's amentum swayed gently in the breeze. 柳树的柔荑花序在微风中轻轻摇曳。|Synonyms: catkin, spike|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
