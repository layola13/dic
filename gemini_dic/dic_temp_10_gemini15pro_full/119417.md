microfinance:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈfaɪnæns/ | n. | 小额信贷；微型金融 | The provision of small loans and other financial services to individuals and small businesses in developing countries. | Microfinance helps people in developing countries start their own businesses. 小额信贷帮助发展中国家的人们创业。 |Synonyms: microcredit, microlending | Antonyms: macrofinance | noun
