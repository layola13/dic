nitrometer:/naɪˈtrɒmɪtər/ | n. | 氮量计；氮素计 | An instrument used to measure the amount of nitrogen in a substance, especially in nitrates and nitrites. |  The chemist used a nitrometer to determine the nitrogen content of the fertilizer. 化学家用氮量计测定肥料中的氮含量。

The farmer purchased a nitrometer to analyze the soil samples. 农民购买了一台氮量计来分析土壤样本。 | 近义词: nitrogen analyzer | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源：
From nitro- + -meter.  "Nitro-" comes from Greek *nítron, meaning "natron" (a naturally occurring mixture of sodium carbonate and sodium bicarbonate). "-meter" comes from Greek *métron, meaning "measure."
