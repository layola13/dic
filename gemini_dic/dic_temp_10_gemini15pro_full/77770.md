genera:/ˈdʒɛnərə/
n.
复数 (plural)
属 (biology)
The biologist identified several new genera of insects in the rainforest.  这位生物学家在雨林中发现了几个新的昆虫属。
类别，种类 (general)
Several genera of artistic expression flourished during the Renaissance.  文艺复兴时期，几种艺术表现形式蓬勃发展。
Etymology:  From Latin *genera*, plural of *genus* "race, stock, kind, family," from PIE root *gene- "give birth, beget," with derivatives referring to procreation and familial and tribal groups.
Synonym:  classes, categories, kinds, types (in general sense)
Antonym: (No direct antonym for a taxonomic rank. Could consider "species" as a contrasting, more specific rank within biology.)
词性: 名词
