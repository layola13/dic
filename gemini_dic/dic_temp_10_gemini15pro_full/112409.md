lothario:/ləˈθɑːrioʊ/
noun
情场浪子；风流浪子
A man who habitually seduces and deceives women.
例句：He was known as a lothario, breaking hearts wherever he went. 他被称为情场浪子，走到哪里都会伤透女人的心。

例句：The play featured a charming lothario who eventually gets his comeuppance.  这出戏以一位迷人的花花公子为主角，他最终得到了报应。

近义词：womanizer, philanderer, playboy, rake, Don Juan
反义词：faithful husband, devoted partner
词性：n.
