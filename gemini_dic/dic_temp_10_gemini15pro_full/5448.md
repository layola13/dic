almsgiving:/ˈɑːmzˌɡɪvɪŋ/
n.
施舍，赈济
The act of giving money or food to poor people.
The church encouraged almsgiving to the less fortunate.  教堂鼓励向不幸的人施舍。
Synonyms: charity, donation, philanthropy
Antonyms: stinginess, miserliness, greed
