overexpends:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspɛndz/
| v. |过度支出，超支|To spend more than one can afford or more than is budgeted.|She overexpends every month and ends up in debt. 她每个月都超支，最终负债累累。|He consistently overexpends on luxury items. 他总是过度消费奢侈品。|近义词：overspend, splurge|反义词: underspend, save, budget|词性:动词

词源: over- (prefix) + expend (verb).  "Over-" signifies "in excess" or "too much." "Expend" comes from Latin "expendere," meaning "to weigh out," later evolving to mean "to pay out" or "spend."
