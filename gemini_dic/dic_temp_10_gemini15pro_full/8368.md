anti-protectionist:/ˌænti prəˈtɛkʃənɪst/
adjective
反保护主义的
opposed to protectionism (the theory or practice of shielding a country's domestic industries from foreign competition by taxing imports).
The senator is known for his anti-protectionist stance.  这位参议员以其反保护主义的立场而闻名。
The article argued for anti-protectionist policies to stimulate economic growth. 这篇文章主张采取反保护主义政策来刺激经济增长。
synonyms:  free trade,  liberal, laissez-faire
antonyms:  protectionist, mercantilist
