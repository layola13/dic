bluebeard:/ˈbluːbɪərd/| n.|蓝胡子，指杀妻的丈夫；残暴的丈夫|A man who marries and kills one wife after another.  A man who is cruel to his wife.|例句：The story of Bluebeard is a cautionary tale about the dangers of unchecked power. 蓝胡子的故事是一个警示性的故事，讲述了不受约束的权力的危险。

例句：He was known as a bluebeard figure in the community, feared by many women. 他在社区里被认为是一个蓝胡子式的人物，许多妇女都害怕他。|近义词：wife-killer, tyrant, abuser|反义词:protector, guardian|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the French "Barbe bleue", referring to a folktale character. The "blue beard" may have referred to a historical figure, Gilles de Rais, or it may symbolize dark magic or a hidden, monstrous nature.
