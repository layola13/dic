cushion-like:/ˈkʊʃənˌlaɪk/
adjective
像垫子一样的; 柔软的
Resembling a cushion, soft and yielding.
The seats were cushion-like and very comfortable.  这些座位像垫子一样，非常舒适。
The soft, cushion-like moss felt good underfoot. 柔软的、像垫子一样的苔藓踩上去感觉很好。
近义词:soft, padded, pillowy, spongy
反义词:hard, firm, rigid
词性:adjective


Etymology:
From "cushion" + "-like."  "Cushion" comes from Old French "coissin," from Latin "coxinum,"  meaning "hip, haunch, cushion." "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
