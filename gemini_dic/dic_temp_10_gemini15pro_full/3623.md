agelessnesses:/ˈeɪdʒləsnəsɪz/
n.
永恒，不老
The state of being ageless; timelessness.
Her paintings capture the agelessnesses of natural beauty. 她的画作捕捉到了自然美永恒的特性。
近义词: timelessness, eternity, immortality
反义词: mortality, transience, ephemerality
词性: 名词

ageless:/ˈeɪdʒləs/
adj.
永恒的，不老的
Not appearing to grow old or showing the effects of age.
She possesses an ageless beauty that defies time. 她拥有一种不老的美丽，无视时间的流逝。
近义词: timeless, eternal, youthful
反义词: aging, old, ancient
词性: 形容词
