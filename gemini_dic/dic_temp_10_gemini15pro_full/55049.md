disquisitionary:/ˌdɪskwɪˈzɪʃənəri/
adjective
论文的；专题讨论的；探究的
Relating to or characterized by formal systematic inquiry or investigation; resembling or suitable for a disquisition.
His disquisitionary style made the lecture rather dry.  他那论文式的风格使得讲座相当枯燥。
近义词:  analytical, investigative, scholarly, academic, formal
反义词: informal, casual, superficial
词性: adjective

disquisition:/ˌdɪskwɪˈzɪʃən/
noun
专题论文；专题演讲；学术讨论
A long or formal systematic inquiry or investigation; a treatise.
He delivered a lengthy disquisition on the history of the region. 他就该地区的历史作了长篇专题演讲。
近义词: treatise, discourse, essay, study, analysis
反义词: summary, overview, abstract
词性: noun


词源:
From Late Latin *disquisition-, from Latin disquisitio, from disquirere "to investigate," from dis- "apart, thoroughly" + quaerere "to seek, inquire."
