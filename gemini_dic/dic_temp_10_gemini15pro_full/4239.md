airman:/ˈɛəmən/ | n. |飞行员；空勤人员|A member of an air force, especially one who is not an officer. | Example 1: The airman checked the plane's instruments before takeoff.  飞行员在起飞前检查了飞机的仪表。

Example 2:  The airmen were awarded medals for their bravery. 空勤人员因其英勇而获得奖章。 | Synonym: pilot, aviator, flyer | Antonym: civilian, ground crew | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From air + man.  "Air" referring to the atmosphere and "man" denoting a person involved in a particular activity.
