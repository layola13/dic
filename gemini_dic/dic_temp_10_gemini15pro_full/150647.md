paplike: /ˈpæpˌlaɪk/
adjective
像糊状的，粥状的
Resembling pap; soft, pulpy.
The overcooked vegetables were paplike and unappetizing. 煮过头的蔬菜像糊状的，一点也不开胃。
近义词: mushy, pulpy, soft
反义词: firm, solid, hard
词性: adjective

Etymology:  From "pap" (meaning soft food) + "-like" (suffix indicating resemblance).  "Pap" ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *pap-, meaning "to swell."
