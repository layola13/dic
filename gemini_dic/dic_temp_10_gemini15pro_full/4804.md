algonquian:/ælˈɡɒŋkiən/ /ælˈɡɑːnkiən/
adjective|阿尔冈昆语的；阿尔冈昆人的|Relating to the Algonquian languages or peoples.|The Algonquian languages are spoken across a vast area of North America.|近义词:Algonquin|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-Algonquian)|词性:adjective

algonquian:/ælˈɡɒŋkiən/ /ælˈɡɑːnkiən/
noun|阿尔冈昆语；阿尔冈昆人|A member of any of the North American Indian peoples speaking Algonquian languages, or any of these languages.|The Algonquian peoples lived in a variety of environments, from forests to plains.|近义词:Algonquin|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
