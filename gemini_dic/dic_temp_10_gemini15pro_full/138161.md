northumbrian: /nɔːrˈθʌmbriən/ | n. |  诺森伯兰人；诺森伯兰方言 | A native or inhabitant of Northumbria. |  She is a proud Northumbrian. 她是一位自豪的诺森伯兰人。 | Near Synonyms: Northerner | Antonyms: Southerner | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Northumbria + -ian.  Northumbria comes from Old English Norþhymbre, meaning "the people north of the Humber" (river).
