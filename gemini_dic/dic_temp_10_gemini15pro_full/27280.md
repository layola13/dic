calumnied:/kəˈlʌmniːd/
|v.|被诽谤的|Having been falsely and maliciously accused; defamed.|He felt calumnied by the newspaper article that falsely accused him of theft.|近义词：defamed, slandered, libeled, vilified, traduced|反义词：praised, lauded, extolled|词性:Verb (past participle)
Etymology: From Late Latin calumniatus, past participle of calumniari "to slander," from Latin calumnia "false accusation," of uncertain origin.
