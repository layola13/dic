inequilobate:/ɪnˌiːkwɪˈloʊbeɪt/
adjective
不等裂的；不等基部的
Having lobes that are unequal or dissimilar, particularly referring to the basal lobes of a leaf or other plant organ.
The botanist identified the plant by its inequilobate leaves.  这位植物学家通过其不等裂的叶子识别出了这种植物。
The inequilobate shape of the petal was a key characteristic for species identification. 花瓣的不等基部形状是物种鉴定的关键特征。
近义词:asymmetrical, uneven, irregular
反义词:symmetrical, even, regular
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin *inequi- (not equal) + -lobatus (lobed).  来自拉丁语 *inequi- （不相等） + -lobatus （有裂片的）。
