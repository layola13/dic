nonportentousness:/ˌnɒnpɔːˈtɛntəsnəs/
noun
缺乏预兆性；平凡；平常
The state of not being portentous; lack of ominous or significant meaning.
His nonportentousness was reassuring in a time of widespread anxiety.  他在普遍焦虑的时期里，他那平凡无奇让人安心。
Synonyms: ordinariness, commonplaceness, unimportance, insignificance
Antonyms: significance, momentousness, portentousness
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + portentous + -ness.  Portentous comes from Late Middle English, from Latin *portentosus, from portentum 'portent, omen'.
