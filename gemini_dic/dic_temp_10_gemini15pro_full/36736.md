ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *collopen*, from Old French *coloper* "to cut, slice," possibly from Vulgar Latin *colpare*, from Latin *culpa* "knife," related to *culter* "knife, plowshare." The sense of "wrinkled" likely developed from the appearance of sliced or folded flesh.
