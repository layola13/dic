nongrains:/ˈnɒnˌɡreɪnz/ | n. | 非谷物，非粮食作物 |  Crops that are not cereal grains, such as legumes, vegetables, fruits, nuts, and seeds. |  Many farmers are diversifying their crops by planting nongrains like lentils and chickpeas. 许多农民通过种植扁豆和鹰嘴豆等非谷物作物来实现作物多样化。| Synonyms: non-cereals | Antonyms: grains, cereals | noun
