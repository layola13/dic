barrowist:/ˈbæroʊɪst/
n.
barrow pusher; one who uses a barrow
A person who pushes or uses a barrow (a handcart or wheelbarrow).
The barrowist struggled up the hill with a load of bricks.  手推车夫费力地推着一车砖上山。
近义词：wheeler, handcart operator
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps someone who uses motorized transport)
词性：noun
