existimation:/ˌɛɡzɪˈsteɪʃən/ | n. | 评价；尊重；名望 | An opinion or judgment of the nature, character, or quality of something or someone.  A high reputation gained by achievement or superiority. | He held her in high existimation. 他对她评价很高。| 近义词: esteem, regard, respect, admiration | 反义词: disdain, contempt | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Late Latin existimationem, accusative of existimation-, from Latin existimatio ("valuation, appraisal"), from existimare ("to estimate, value, appraise"), from ex- + aestimō ("to estimate, value").
