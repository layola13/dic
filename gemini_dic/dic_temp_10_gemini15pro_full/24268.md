briery:/ˈbraɪəri/| adj.|荆棘丛生的，充满荆棘的|Covered with briers; thorny.|例句：The path through the woods was briery and difficult to navigate. 穿过树林的小路荆棘丛生，难以通行。

例句：The briery bushes scratched my arms as I tried to get through. 当我试图穿过时，带刺的灌木丛划伤了我的手臂。|近义词：thorny, prickly, spiny|反义词:smooth, soft|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English brere, from Old English brǣr (“briar”), from Proto-Germanic *brēzō (“thorn bush”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰars- (“point, bristle”).
