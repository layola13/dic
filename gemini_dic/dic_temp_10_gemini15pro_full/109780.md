levitical: /ləˈvɪtɪkəl/
adjective
利未的；利未记的
relating to the Levites (a tribe of priests in ancient Israel), or to the biblical Book of Leviticus.
The levitical priests performed rituals in the Temple. 利未祭司在圣殿里举行仪式。
The book contains detailed levitical laws concerning sacrifices and purification. 这本书包含了关于祭祀和洁净的详细的利未律法。
近义词: priestly, clerical
反义词: secular, lay
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *Leviticus* (referring to the Book of Leviticus), from Greek *Λευιτικόν (Leuitikon)*, neuter of *Λευιτικός (Leuitikos)* "of a Levite," from Hebrew *לֵוִי (Levi)*, the name of the patriarch Levi.
