forefending:/ˌfɔːrˈfɛndɪŋ/ | adj. | 预防的，保护的 | Preventing or guarding against (something bad or harmful). |  Forefending the spread of the virus is our top priority. 预防病毒传播是我们的首要任务。 | Synonyms: preventative, protective, prophylactic | Antonyms: inviting, encouraging | adjective

forefend:/ˌfɔːrˈfɛnd/ | v. | 阻止，防止 | Avert or prevent (something bad or dangerous). |  May God forefend such a tragedy. 愿上帝阻止这样的悲剧发生。 | Synonyms: prevent, avert, preclude | Antonyms: cause, allow, permit | verb


词源:

From Middle English *forefenden, from Old English forfēon (“to hate, abhor, renounce, despise, refuse, decline, prevent”), from for- + fēon (“to hate”). Cognate with Dutch verven (“to colour, dye, paint”), German verfeinden (“to make into an enemy”). More at for-, foe.
