mesaconic:/ˌmɛsəˈkɒnɪk/
adjective
中缩的，中锥形的
Relating to or denoting a type of aconitic acid in which the carboxyl groups are attached to the first and third carbon atoms of the chain.
Mesaconic acid is used in the synthesis of certain polymers. 中缩丙二酸用于合成某些聚合物。
Synonyms:  mid-chain, medial (in a broader sense)
Antonyms:  terminal (in a broader sense)
词性: adjective
