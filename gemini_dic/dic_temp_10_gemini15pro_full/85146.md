hardrada:/ˈhɑːrdrɑːdə/ | n. | 哈拉尔·哈德拉达 | Harald Hardrada (c. 1015 – 25 September 1066) was a Viking king of Norway. He is also known as Harald III of Norway.  He invaded England in 1066 and was killed at the Battle of Stamford Bridge. | Harald Hardrada was one of the last great Viking kings. 哈拉尔·哈德拉达是最后一位伟大的维京国王。|  Hardrada claimed the English throne. 哈德拉达宣称拥有英国王位。 | synonyms: Harald III, King Harald III of Norway | antonyms: N/A | proper noun
