ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + supplying, present participle of supply (v.).  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much," or "beyond." "Supply" comes from Old French "supploier" and ultimately from Latin "supplere" meaning "to fill up" or "complete."
