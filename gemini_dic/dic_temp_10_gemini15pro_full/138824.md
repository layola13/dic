nuncupative: /nʌŋˈkjuːpətɪv/ | adj. | 口头的，口述的 | Spoken; oral; not written.  Specifically referring to a will or testament declared orally rather than written. | Example:  His nuncupative will, spoken on his deathbed, was challenged in court.  他临终前口述的遗嘱在法庭上受到了质疑。 | Example: Nuncupative agreements are generally not legally binding in real estate transactions. 口头协议在房地产交易中通常不没有法律约束力。| Synonym: oral, verbal, spoken | Antonym: written, documented | 词性: 形容词
