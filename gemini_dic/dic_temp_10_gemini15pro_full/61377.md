emblanch:/ɪmˈblɑːntʃ/ /ɪmˈblæntʃ/| v. |使变白；使(脸色)变苍白；漂白|To make white or pale; to blanch.  Derived from Old French *emblanchir, from en- "in" + blanc "white".|The chef emblanched the almonds before adding them to the cake.  厨师在把杏仁加入蛋糕之前先将它们漂白。

The sight of the blood made her emblanch. 看见血让她脸色发白。|近义词：whiten, bleach, pale, fade|反义词：darken, color, blush|词性：动词
