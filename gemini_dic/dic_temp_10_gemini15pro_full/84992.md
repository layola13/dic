hapuku:/ˈhæpʊkuː/| n. |石斑鱼（新西兰），真鲷（澳大利亚）|A large marine fish (Polyprion oxygeneios) found in deep waters around New Zealand, also called groper in Australia. |例句：Hapuku is a prized fish for its firm, white flesh. 石斑鱼因其紧实的白肉而备受珍视。

例句：We caught a huge hapuku on our fishing trip. 我们在钓鱼之旅中捕获了一条巨大的石斑鱼。|近义词：groper, bass, wreckfish|反义词: None (no direct opposite for a type of fish)|词性:noun
