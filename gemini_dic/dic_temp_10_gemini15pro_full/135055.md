nonnormalness:/ˌnɑːnˈnɔːrməlnəs/ | n. | 非正态性，非正常状态 |The state or quality of not being normal; abnormality.  This term refers to deviations from a normal distribution in statistics, or more generally, to any deviation from a typical or expected state. | The nonnormalness of the data made it difficult to analyze using standard statistical methods.  数据非正态性使得用标准统计方法难以分析。The nonnormalness of his behavior worried his friends. 他行为的异常让他的朋友们很担心。| 近义词: abnormality, irregularity, deviance, anomaly | 反义词: normality, normalcy, regularity | 词性: 名词
