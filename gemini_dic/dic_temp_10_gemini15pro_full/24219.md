bridging:/ˈbrɪdʒɪŋ/
adj.
桥接的；连接的
Connecting two things; acting as a bridge.
The bridging loan allowed them to buy a new house before selling their old one.  桥接贷款让他们能够在新房出售之前购买新房。
近义词: connecting, linking, joining
反义词: separating, dividing
词性: adjective

Etymology:  From "bridge" (n.) + "-ing" (suffix forming present participles and adjectives).  Ultimately from Old English "brycg."
