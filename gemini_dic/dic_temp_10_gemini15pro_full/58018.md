drizzling:/ˈdrɪzlɪŋ/| adj.  n.|下毛毛雨的，毛毛雨|A light rain falling in very fine drops.|It was drizzling outside, so I took an umbrella. 外面下着毛毛雨，所以我带了把伞。|近义词：sprinkling, misting|反义词：pouring, downpouring|词性:形容词, 名词
drizzle:/ˈdrɪzl/| v.  n.|下毛毛雨，毛毛雨|Rain lightly; to fall in fine drops.|It began to drizzle as we left the house. 我们离开家时开始下毛毛雨了。|近义词：sprinkle, mist|反义词：pour, downpour|词性:动词, 名词

词源: From Middle English *driselen*, frequentative of *drislen*, from Old English *drisle* (“a drop”). Cognate with Dutch *druilen* (“to drizzle”), German *rieseln* (“to trickle, drizzle”).
