deoxygenate:/diːˈɒksɪdʒəneɪt/
verb
脱氧，除去氧气
To remove oxygen from something.
例句：The process deoxygenates the water, making it unsuitable for fish.  这个过程除去了水中的氧气，使它不适合鱼类生存。

例句：Scientists are exploring ways to deoxygenate blood for use in organ preservation. 科学家正在探索去除血液中氧气的方法，用于器官保存。
近义词:deoxidize, reduce
反义词:oxygenate, oxidize
词性:v.


deoxygenated:/diːˈɒksɪdʒəneɪtɪd/
adjective
脱氧的，去氧的
Having had oxygen removed.
例句：Deoxygenated blood returns to the heart.  脱氧的血液返回心脏。

例句：The experiment requires a deoxygenated environment.  实验需要一个无氧的环境。
近义词:reduced, anoxic
反义词:oxygenated, oxidized
词性:adj.


词源:  de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + oxygen + -ate (suffix forming verbs)
