palliest:/ˈpæliɪst/
adjective
最苍白的，最没血色的
palest; superlative of pale
Her face was the palliest I had ever seen. 她的脸色是我见过最苍白的。
近义词:ashen, wan, pallid
反义词:ruddy, flushed, rosy
词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *palest, a variant of paleste, superlative of pal.  Ultimately from Old French palle (“pale”), from Latin pallidus.
