marquise:/mɑːrˈkiːz/ /mɑːrˈkwiz/
n.
侯爵夫人；女侯爵；侯爵的女儿；长椭圆形宝石
A woman holding the rank of a marquise. A long oval-shaped brilliant-cut gemstone.
The marquise was known for her extravagant parties. 侯爵夫人以其奢华的派对而闻名。
She wore a diamond marquise ring. 她戴着一枚长椭圆形钻石戒指。
近义词：marchioness
反义词: (无明确的反义词)
词性: 名词

marquise:/mɑːrˈkiːz/ /mɑːrˈkwiz/
n.
(顶篷，尤指剧院、旅馆等的)雨篷，遮篷
A canopy over an entrance, especially to a theater or hotel.
The hotel guests waited under the marquise during the rain. 旅馆客人在雨中在雨篷下等候。
The theater's marquise protected patrons from the elements. 剧院的遮篷保护顾客免受风雨侵袭。
近义词：canopy, awning
反义词: (无明确的反义词)
词性: 名词


词源:  From French *marquise*, feminine of *marquis* (“marquis”). Ultimately from Medieval Latin *marchensis* (“ruler of a march, border district”).
