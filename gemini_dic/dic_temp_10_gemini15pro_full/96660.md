industrializes:/ɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzɪz/ |v. |工业化|To develop industries in (a country or region) on a wide scale.|例句：The government is trying to industrialize the country. 政府正在努力使国家工业化。

例句：Many developing countries are rapidly industrializing. 许多发展中国家正在迅速工业化。|近义词：develop, modernize, advance |反义词:ruralize, deindustrialize |词性:动词

Etymology: From industrialize + -es (third-person singular present tense verb ending). Industrialize comes from industrial + -ize (verb suffix meaning "to make or become"). Industrial comes from industry + -al (adjective suffix). Industry comes from the Old French industrie "activity, diligence," ultimately from Latin industria "diligence, activity."
