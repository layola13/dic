dizziness:/ˈdɪzinəs/| n. |头晕，眩晕|A sensation of whirling and a tendency to fall.|例句：The dizziness caused her to stumble. 头晕使她踉跄。|例句：He experienced a sudden bout of dizziness. 他突然感到一阵头晕。|近义词：vertigo, lightheadedness, giddiness|反义词:stability, balance|词性:noun

dizzy:/ˈdɪzi/| adj. |头晕的，眩晕的|Having or involving a sensation of spinning around and losing one's balance.|例句：I felt dizzy after spinning around too many times.  我转太多圈后感到头晕。|例句：The heights made him dizzy.  高处使他感到头晕。|近义词：giddy, lightheaded, faint|反义词:steady, balanced|词性:adjective
