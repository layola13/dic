Etymology: Astley is a locational surname of English origin, derived from places named Astley in Lancashire and Warwickshire. The name means "east wood" or "east clearing".  Specifically referring to Rick Astley, it's simply the singer's surname which has become associated with the internet meme "rickrolling."
