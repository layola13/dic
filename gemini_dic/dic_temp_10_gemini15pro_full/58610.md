dullnesses:/ˈdʌlnəsɪz/
noun
迟钝；沉闷；单调
lack of sharpness or brightness; lack of excitement or interest
The dullnesses of the landscape made the journey tedious.  这单调的景色使得旅途乏味。
近义词: monotony, tediousness, boredom, flatness
反义词: excitement, vibrancy, brightness, sharpness
词性: 名词 (plural of dullness)
