dodger:/ˈdɑːdʒər/ (US) /ˈdɒdʒər/ (UK)| n. |躲避者；逃避者；骗子|Someone who avoids something, especially a duty or responsibility, or someone who is skillful at avoiding being caught. |He's a tax dodger. 他是个逃税者。

The artful dodger slipped away from the police.  那个狡猾的家伙从警察眼皮底下溜走了。|近义词：evader, shirker, scammer, cheat|反义词：conformist, honest person|词性:noun
