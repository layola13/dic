externalize:/ɪkˈstɜːrnəlaɪz/ /ɛkˈstɝːnəˌlaɪz/| vt.|使…外化；使…客观化；将（情感、想法）表露出来|To express (feelings, thoughts, etc.) outwardly.|He externalized his anger by yelling at his brother. 他通过对弟弟大喊大叫来发泄他的愤怒。|近义词：express, vent, articulate, manifest|反义词：internalize, repress, suppress|verb

externalize:/ɪkˈstɜːrnəlaɪz/ /ɛkˈstɝːnəˌlaɪz/| n.|外化；客观化|The act of expressing something outwardly.|The externalization of his fears helped him to overcome them.  他恐惧的表露帮助他克服了恐惧。|近义词：expression, manifestation|反义词：internalization, repression|noun
