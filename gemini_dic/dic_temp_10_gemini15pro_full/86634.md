hell-bent:/ˈhelˌbent/| adj.| 决心做…的；一心想…的；坚决的| Determined to do something, usually something considered foolish or dangerous.  Driven single-mindedly towards something.|He was hell-bent on revenge. 他一心想复仇。|近义词:determined, resolved, adamant, insistent, set on, intent on|反义词:hesitant, reluctant, ambivalent|词性:形容词
Etymology:  'Hell-bent' originates from the image of someone rushing towards hell with unwavering determination, suggesting a reckless and potentially self-destructive course of action.
