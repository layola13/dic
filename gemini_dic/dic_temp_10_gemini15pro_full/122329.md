mixability:/ˌmɪksəˈbɪləti/| n.|可混合性，混溶性|The ability of a substance to be mixed with another substance, typically a liquid, without separating or reacting adversely.|The mixability of the paint is crucial for achieving a smooth finish.  油漆的可混合性对于获得光滑的表面至关重要。|Synonyms: miscibility, blendability, compatibility|Antonyms: immiscibility, insolubility|noun
