bimaculate:/baɪˈmækjʊlət/
adjective
有两个斑点的；无瑕疵的
Having two spots or stains; spotless, perfectly clean.
The bimaculate ladybird has two red spots on its black shell.  这只有两个斑点的瓢虫，黑色的壳上有两个红点。
The priest wore a bimaculate white robe.  牧师穿着洁白无瑕的长袍。
近义词:spotted, unblemished, immaculate, pristine
反义词:stained, soiled, dirty, blemished
词性:adjective

Etymology: From Latin *bimaculatus*, from bi- (“two”) + maculatus (“spotted”), past participle of maculō (“spot, stain”).
