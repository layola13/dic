decussately:/ˌdiːˈkʌsətli/
adv.|交叉地，十字形地|in the form of an X; crosswise|The optic nerves decussate at the optic chiasma.  视神经在视交叉处交叉。|近义词：crosswise, intersectingly|反义词:parallel|词性:副词
Etymology: From Latin *decussat-, past participle stem of decussare “to divide crosswise, mark with an X,” from decussis “a Roman coin worth ten asses, originally marked with an X,” from decem “ten” + as “unit of weight or money.”
