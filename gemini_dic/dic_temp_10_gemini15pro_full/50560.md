deputize:/ˈdɛpjʊˌtaɪz/ /ˈdɛpjəˌtaɪz/| v. |代理；委任…为代表|To appoint someone to act as a substitute or agent; to act as a deputy.|The manager deputized his assistant to run the meeting. 经理委派他的助手主持会议。|Synonyms: represent, substitute, stand in for, act for|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider:  lead, preside, officiate)|verb

deputize:/ˈdɛpjʊˌtaɪz/ /ˈdɛpjəˌtaɪz/| n. |代理；代表|The act of appointing someone to act as a substitute or agent.|His deputizing for the manager was unexpected.  他代理经理的职务出乎意料。|Synonyms: substitution, representation, delegation|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym)|noun
