discus:/ˈdɪskəs/ (US), /ˈdɪskəs/ (UK)| n.| 铁饼；掷铁饼；讨论 |A heavy, circular, lens-shaped object thrown in track and field competitions. Also, a discussion or debate.|The athlete threw the discus with impressive force.  运动员以惊人的力量掷出铁饼。|synonyms: disc, plate, platter; debate, conversation, talk |antonyms: agreement, silence|noun

discus:/dɪˈskʌs/ (US), /dɪˈskʌs/ (UK)| v.| 讨论 |To talk about a subject with someone or a group of people.|We need to discus the budget for next year. 我们需要讨论下一年的预算。|synonyms: debate, deliberate, confer, talk over |antonyms: ignore, disregard|verb
