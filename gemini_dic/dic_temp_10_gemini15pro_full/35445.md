co-sovereign:/ˌkoʊˈsɒvrɪn/ | adj. n.| 共同主权的，共同君主的；共同拥有主权的国家或实体| Sharing sovereign power; relating to joint sovereignty. |  The two nations acted as co-sovereign entities in the disputed territory. 这两个国家在争议地区作为共同主权实体行事。|  The co-sovereign states agreed on a joint defense policy. 共同主权国家同意一项联合防御政策。|Synonyms: joint sovereign, shared sovereign| Antonyms: subordinate, dependent| adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "co-" (together, jointly) + "sovereign" (supreme ruler, possessing supreme power).  "Co-" comes from Latin "com-" (together, with). "Sovereign" ultimately derives from Latin "superanus" (supreme).
