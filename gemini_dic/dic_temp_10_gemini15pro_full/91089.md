huzz:/hʌz/
interjection
欢呼声，喧闹声
A loud confused noise, as of many voices.
The crowd gave a loud huzz as the team scored.  人群在球队得分时发出巨大的欢呼声。
近义词:cheer, clamor, uproar
反义词:silence, quiet
词性:感叹词

词源:
Likely imitative in origin, echoing the sound of a confused murmur or shout.  很可能源于模仿，模仿了混乱的低语或喊叫声。
