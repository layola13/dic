almost:/ˈɔːlmoʊst/ (US) /ˈɔːlməʊst/ (UK)| adv.|几乎，差不多|very nearly; not quite|例句：I almost missed the train. 我差点错过火车。|近义词：nearly, practically, virtually|反义词:completely, totally|词性:副词

Etymology: From Old English "allmest" (entirely most), a superlative of "eall" (all).
