interflows:/ˈɪntərˌfloʊz/ | n. | 相互流动，相互影响 | The reciprocal exchange or flow of things, ideas, or influences between different groups or systems. | Example 1: The interflows of capital and labor across national borders have increased significantly in recent years. 近年来，跨境资本和劳动力流动显著增加。

Example 2:  Cultural interflows between East and West have enriched both societies. 东西方文化交流丰富了这两个社会。|Synonyms: interaction, exchange, interplay, reciprocity | Antonyms: isolation, separation, division | noun
