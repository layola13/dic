centreboards: /ˈsɛntərbɔːrdz/ (US) /ˈsɛntəbɔːdz/ (UK)
| n. |中心板；活动龙骨|A retractable keel or fin that pivots up and down, used in sailboats to prevent leeway (sideways drift) and provide stability.  A centreboard is housed within a centreboard case in the hull of the boat.|The sailboat used its centreboard to sail closer to the wind. 这艘帆船用它的中心板顶风航行。|近义词：keel, daggerboard, fin|反义词: N/A|名词
