amniocentesis:/ˌæmniəʊsɛnˈtiːsɪs/ | n. | 羊膜穿刺术 | A medical procedure in which a small amount of amniotic fluid is removed from the amniotic sac surrounding a developing fetus. This fluid can then be tested to detect certain genetic disorders and chromosomal abnormalities, such as Down syndrome. |  Amniocentesis is offered to pregnant women who are at increased risk of having a baby with a birth defect. 羊膜穿刺术提供给有较高风险生育有出生缺陷婴儿的孕妇。 |  The doctor recommended amniocentesis to determine the baby's lung maturity. 医生建议进行羊膜穿刺术以确定婴儿的肺部成熟度。 | Synonyms:  amniotic fluid test | Antonyms: None | Noun
