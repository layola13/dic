interrogatively:/ˌɪntəˈrɑːɡətɪvli/ | adv. | 疑问地；审问地 | In a manner that asks a question; by means of interrogation. | She looked at him interrogatively. 她用 вопросительный взгляд 看着他。| 近义词: inquiringly, questioningly | 反义词: declaratively, assertively | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *interrogativus, from Latin interrogatus (past participle of interrogare “to question”), from inter- (“between”) + rogare (“to ask”).
