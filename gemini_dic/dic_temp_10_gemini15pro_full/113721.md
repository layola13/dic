macrocosmic: /ˌmækrəˈkɒzmɪk/ | adj. |宏观的，宇宙的| Relating to the universe or cosmos, especially as a whole; the opposite of microcosmic. |例句：His paintings offer a macrocosmic view of human existence. 他的画作提供了一个关于人类存在的宏观视角。

例句：She saw her own problems as a reflection of macrocosmic struggles. 她将自己的问题视为宇宙斗争的反映。|近义词：universal, cosmic, global, worldwide|反义词：microcosmic, limited, parochial|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *macrocosmus*, from Ancient Greek *μακρόκοσμος (makrókosmos)*, from *μακρός (makrós, “long, large”)* + *κόσμος (kósmos, “world, universe”)*.
