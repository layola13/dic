cartoony:/kɑːrˈtuːni/ | adj. |卡通的，卡通化的|Resembling or suggestive of a cartoon, especially by being simplified, exaggerated, or unrealistic.|The characters in the game were very cartoony, with oversized heads and bright colors. 游戏中的角色非常卡通化，有着超大的脑袋和鲜艳的颜色。|例句：The animation style was intentionally cartoony.  动画风格故意设计成卡通的。

例句：Her makeup was a bit too cartoony for everyday wear. 她的妆容对于日常穿着来说有点太卡通化了。|近义词：animated, comic, cartoonish|反义词：realistic, lifelike|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From cartoon + -y.  "Cartoon" originally referred to a preparatory drawing for a piece of art (like a fresco or tapestry).  The sense of a humorous or satirical drawing developed later.  The suffix "-y" is used to form adjectives indicating resemblance or characteristic of something.
