non-contradictory:/ˌnɒn kɒntrəˈdɪktəri/ /ˌnɑːn kɑːntrəˈdɪktəri/
adjective
无矛盾的；不矛盾的；一致的
Not contradictory; consistent.
His statements were clear and non-contradictory. 他的陈述清晰且无矛盾。
Synonyms: consistent, harmonious, compatible, congruent, coherent
Antonyms: contradictory, inconsistent, conflicting, incompatible, discordant
词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not".
contradictory (adjective): from Late Latin contradictorius, from Latin contradicere "to speak against," from contra "against" + dicere "to say."
