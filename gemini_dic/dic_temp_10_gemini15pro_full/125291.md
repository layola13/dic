multiformous:/ˌmʌltɪˈfɔːrməs/| adj.|多形的，多种多样的|Having many forms or appearances; exhibiting great diversity.|The multiformous manifestations of the disease made diagnosis difficult. 这种疾病的多样性表现使得诊断变得困难。|近义词：varied, diverse, multifaceted, polymorphic|反义词：uniform, homogeneous, similar|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin "multiformis" (many forms), from "multus" (many) + "forma" (form).
