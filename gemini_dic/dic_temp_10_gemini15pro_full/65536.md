euthanasiast:/ˌjuːθəˈneɪʒɪæst/ | n. | 安乐死实施者；赞成安乐死者 | A person who practices or supports euthanasia. |  The euthanasiast argued that the patient's suffering justified their actions.  安乐死实施者辩称，病人的痛苦证明他们的行为是正当的。| synonym:  proponent of euthanasia, advocate for assisted dying | antonym: opponent of euthanasia | noun

euthanasia:/ˌjuːθəˈneɪʒə/ | n. | 安乐死 | The painless killing of a patient suffering from an incurable and painful disease or in an irreversible coma. | The family considered euthanasia as an option to end the patient's suffering. 家属考虑安乐死作为结束病人痛苦的一种选择。| synonym: mercy killing, assisted suicide | antonym:  dyathanasia | noun
