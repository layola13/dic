interapplications:/ˌɪntərˌæplɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n.|应用程序间，跨应用程序|Communication or interaction between different software applications.|Example: Interapplications communication is crucial for a seamless user experience. 应用程序间的通信对于无缝的用户体验至关重要。|Example:  The system allows for smooth interapplications data transfer. 该系统允许应用程序间顺利进行数据传输。|近义词：cross-application, inter-app|反义词:intra-application|词性:名词

词源:
"inter-" (prefix): between, among (源自拉丁语)
"application":  (noun) a computer program designed to perform a group of coordinated functions, tasks, or activities for the benefit of the user (源自拉丁语 "applicare", meaning "to apply")
"-s": (suffix) plural marker
