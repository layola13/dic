cimolite:/ˈsɪməˌlaɪt/
n.
皂土；漂白土；一种含水的硅酸铝
A soft, earthy, hydrous silicate of alumina, used as an absorbent.
Cimolite was traditionally used to remove grease stains from clothing. 皂土传统上用于去除衣服上的油渍。
Cimolite can be found in various geological formations. 皂土可以在各种地质构造中找到。
Synonyms: fuller's earth, absorbent clay
