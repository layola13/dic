illabile:/ɪˈleɪbaɪl/ | adj. |不易损坏的，不会腐烂的| Not liable to decay or deterioration; imperishable. |  The illabile nature of gold makes it a valuable investment.  黄金不易损坏的特性使其成为一项有价值的投资。|  The artist used illabile paints to ensure the longevity of her masterpiece. 这位艺术家使用不易损坏的颜料来确保她的杰作的寿命。 | 近义词:  imperishable, enduring, lasting, durable | 反义词: perishable, decaying, transient, ephemeral | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *illabilis, from in- "not" + labilis "liable to slip, fall, or err," from labi "to slip, glide, fall."
