overgeneralized:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒɛnərəˌlaɪzd/| adj.|过度概括的，过分概括的|Based on too few examples or instances; characterized by or prone to overgeneralization.  Applying a rule or principle too broadly.|例句：Children sometimes make overgeneralized statements about grammar rules. 孩子们有时会对语法规则做出过度概括的陈述。|近义词：sweeping, broad-brush, indiscriminate, generalized|反义词：specific, nuanced, precise|词性:形容词

overgeneralize:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒɛnərəˌlaɪz/| v.|过度概括，过分概括|To draw a conclusion or make a statement about something based on limited evidence; to apply a rule or principle too broadly.|例句：It's easy to overgeneralize from a single bad experience. 从一次糟糕的经历中很容易过度概括。|近义词：generalize too much, oversimplify, jump to conclusions|反义词：specify, differentiate, nuance|词性:动词

overgeneralization:/ˌoʊvərˌdʒɛnərələˈzeɪʃən/| n.|过度概括，过分概括|The act or process of overgeneralizing; a conclusion or statement based on limited evidence.|例句：His argument was based on an overgeneralization about the entire group. 他的论点是基于对整个群体的过度概括。|近义词：generalization, sweeping statement|反义词：specification, nuance|词性:名词
