noun

lack of experience and wisdom, especially in young people
The callowness of the new recruits was apparent. 新兵的稚嫩显而易见。
immaturity, inexperience, naiveté
sophistication, maturity, worldliness
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *calu*, from Old English *calu* ("bald, bare"), from Proto-Germanic *kalwaz* ("bald, bare").  The semantic shift from "bald" to "immature" likely arose from the association of a young bird's lack of feathers with inexperience.
