go wrong:/ɡəʊ rɒŋ/ (UK) /ɡoʊ rɔːŋ/ (US)| phrasal verb |出错，出故障，失败|When something goes wrong, it does not happen in the way that it was planned, and problems happen.|例句：The plan went wrong when their car broke down. 计划出错了，因为他们的车抛锚了。|近义词：fail, miscarry, backfire, go south|反义词:succeed, go right|词性:动词短语

go wrong:/ɡəʊ rɒŋ/ (UK) /ɡoʊ rɔːŋ/ (US)| adjective |错误的，失常的 (较少用作形容词)| In a bad or incorrect state. |例句：Something has gone wrong with the computer. 电脑出毛病了。|近义词：faulty, defective, amiss|反义词:right, correct|词性:形容词
