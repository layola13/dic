departed:/dɪˈpɑːrtɪd/
v. |离开，出发，去世|To leave, especially on a journey; to die.|The train departed from platform 5. 火车从5号站台出发。|近义词：left, gone, deceased, passed away|反义词：arrived, came, returned|词性:动词
departed:/dɪˈpɑːrtɪd/
adj. |已故的，过去的|Dead; gone.|My departed grandmother was a kind woman. 我已故的祖母是一位善良的女人。|近义词：late, deceased, former|反义词：living, current, present|词性:形容词


词源:
From Old French *departire, from Latin dēpartīrī (“to divide, distribute, separate, depart”), from dē (“from”) + partīrī (“to divide, share”).
