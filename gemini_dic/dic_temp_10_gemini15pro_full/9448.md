antireductionist:/ˌænti rɪˈdʌkʃənɪst/
| adj. n.|反还原论的，反还原论者|Opposing reductionism; the belief that a complex system cannot be fully understood by analyzing its individual components.|His antireductionist stance emphasizes the importance of looking at the whole picture. 他的反还原论立场强调了观察全局的重要性。|The antireductionist argued that consciousness cannot be explained simply by brain chemistry.  这位反还原论者认为，意识不能简单地用大脑化学来解释。|近义词：holistic, synthetic|反义词：reductionist|词性：adjective, noun

antireductionism:/ˌænti rɪˈdʌkʃənɪzəm/
| n.|反还原论|The philosophical position that a complex system is more than the sum of its parts and cannot be fully understood by analyzing its individual components.|Antireductionism in psychology argues against explaining behavior solely in terms of biological factors. 心理学中的反还原论反对仅仅用生物因素来解释行为。|The book championed antireductionism in the study of social phenomena. 这本书倡导在社会现象研究中运用反还原论。|近义词：holism, synthesis|反义词：reductionism|词性：noun
