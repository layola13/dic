parodial:/pəˈrɒdiəl/ | adj. | 滑稽模仿的，戏仿的 | Of, relating to, or in the nature of a parody.  Imitating something in a humorous way. | The movie is a parodial take on classic horror films. 这部电影是对经典恐怖片的滑稽模仿。|例句：The show featured a parodial sketch of the President.  这个节目以总统的滑稽模仿小品为特色。|近义词：satirical, mocking, ironic, humorous |反义词：serious, respectful, reverent |词性: adjective

Etymology: From parody + -al.  Parody comes from Late Latin parodia "a burlesque poem," from Greek parōidia "a song sung beside" (another), from para- "beside, against, contrary to" + ōidē "song, ode."
