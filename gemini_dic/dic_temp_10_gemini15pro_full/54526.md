diskettes:/dɪsˈkɛts/
|n.|
|软盘（复数）|
|A thin, flexible magnetic disk enclosed in a protective plastic case, used for storing computer data.  This term is largely obsolete, replaced by newer storage technologies like USB drives and cloud storage.|
|I remember using diskettes to save my school projects. 我记得用软盘来保存我的学校项目。|

|We had boxes and boxes of diskettes, now they're just clutter. 我们以前有很多盒软盘，现在它们只是杂物了。|

|近义词：floppy disks|
|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider modern storage media like "hard drive," "USB drive," "cloud storage")|
|词性: noun (plural)|


diskette:/dɪsˈkɛt/
|n.|
|软盘（单数）|
|A thin, flexible magnetic disk enclosed in a protective plastic case, used for storing computer data.  This term is largely obsolete, replaced by newer storage technologies like USB drives and cloud storage.|
|The diskette was corrupted and I lost all my data. 软盘损坏了，我丢失了所有数据。|
|He tried to insert the diskette into the drive, but it wouldn't fit. 他试图将软盘插入驱动器，但它不合适。|
|近义词：floppy disk|
|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider modern storage media like "hard drive," "USB drive," "cloud storage")|
|词性: noun (singular)|
