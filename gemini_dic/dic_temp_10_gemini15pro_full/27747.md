cankering:/ˈkæŋkərɪŋ/
adjective
溃烂的；腐蚀性的；恶性溃疡的
Having a destructive or corrosive effect.
The cankering doubt eroded his confidence.  腐蚀性的怀疑侵蚀了他的自信。
近义词:corrosive, destructive, ulcerous, malignant
反义词:healing, beneficial, restorative
词性:adjective

cankering:/ˈkæŋkərɪŋ/
verb (present participle of canker)
溃烂；腐蚀；患恶性溃疡
To become infected with a canker; to cause to decay or rot.
Resentment was cankering their relationship. 怨恨正在腐蚀他们的关系。
近义词:腐烂, fester, decay, rot
反义词:heal, flourish, thrive
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cankren*, from Old North French *cancre*, from Latin *cancer* (“crab, ulcer, cancer”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱerk- (“circle, bend”).  The sense development is from the crab's pinching claws to a spreading sore.
