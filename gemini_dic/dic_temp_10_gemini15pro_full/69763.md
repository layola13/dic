feringhee:/fəˈriːŋɡiː/ /fəˈrɪŋɡiː/
noun
外国人，西方人 (尤指欧洲人)
A term used in South Asia, particularly during the British Raj, to refer to a European, especially a white European.
例句：The feringhees brought new technologies to India.  这些外国人给印度带来了新技术。

例句：During the Raj, many feringhees lived in grand bungalows.  在英国统治时期，许多西方人住在豪华的平房里。

近义词：foreigner, westerner, European
反义词: native, local

词性: n.
