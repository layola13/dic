doublet:/ˈdʌblət/ (US), /ˈdʌblɪt/ (UK)| n.|紧身短上衣；一件衣服；对；成对的东西；复制品|A close-fitting jacket worn by men from the 14th to the 17th century; one of a pair of similar things; a duplicate; a copy.|He wore a handsome doublet and hose. 他穿着一件漂亮的紧身短上衣和长袜。|Synonyms: duplicate, copy, replica, twin, match|Antonyms: original, single, unique|noun

doublet:/ˈdʌblət/ (US), /ˈdʌblɪt/ (UK)| n.|（语言学）同源异形词|One of two or more words derived from the same source but differing in form and meaning.|The words "shirt" and "skirt" are doublets. “shirt”和“skirt”是同源异形词。|Synonyms: cognate|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|noun
