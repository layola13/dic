entomologists:/ˌɛntəˈmɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. | 昆虫学家 | Scientists who study insects. |  Entomologists are fascinated by the diversity of insect life. 昆虫学家对昆虫的多样性着迷。|  
Entomologists play a crucial role in understanding and controlling insect populations. 昆虫学家在理解和控制昆虫种群方面发挥着至关重要的作用。|近义词: insect scientists, bug experts | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From the Greek  "éntomon" (insect) + "-logos" (study).
