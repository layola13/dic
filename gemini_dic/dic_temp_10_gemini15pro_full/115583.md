marathon: /ˈmærəθɒn/ (UK) /ˈmærəˌθɑːn/ (US) | n. | 马拉松，耐力赛，持久考验 | A long-distance running race of 26 miles 385 yards (42.195 kilometres). Also used figuratively to describe any long and arduous undertaking. |  She ran her first marathon last year. 她去年跑了她的第一个马拉松。|  The negotiations were a marathon, lasting for three days.  谈判是一场马拉松，持续了三天。 | 近义词: endurance test, ordeal | 反义词: sprint | 词性: noun
