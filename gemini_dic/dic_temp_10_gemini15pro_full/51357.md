detenu:/dɪˈteɪnjuː/ /dɪˈtɛnjuː/
n.
被拘留者；未决犯
a person held in custody, especially for political reasons.
例句：The government released several political detainees. 政府释放了几名政治犯。

例句：The detainee was held without charge for several weeks.  这名被拘留者未经指控就被关押了几个星期。


近义词：prisoner, captive, inmate
反义词：freeman, parolee
词性: 名词
