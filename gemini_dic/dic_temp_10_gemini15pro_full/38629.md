concubinarian:/kɒnˈkjuːbɪˌnɛːrɪən/ /kɑːnˈkjuːbɪˌnɛːriən/
n.|姘夫，妾侍；纳妾者|A person who lives with a concubine or keeps a concubine.|The emperor was a notorious concubinarian, with a harem of hundreds.|Synonym: polygamist, philanderer|Antonym: monogamist|Noun

concubinarian:/kɒnˈkjuːbɪˌnɛːrɪən/ /kɑːnˈkjuːbɪˌnɛːriən/
adj.|纳妾的，姘居的|Relating to or characteristic of a concubine or concubinage.|He led a concubinarian lifestyle, flaunting his many mistresses.|Synonym: polygamous|Antonym: monogamous|Adjective
