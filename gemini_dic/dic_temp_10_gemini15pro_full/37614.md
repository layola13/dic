communing:/kəˈmjuːnɪŋ/
| n. |交流；共享；亲密交谈；神秘体验|The act of sharing one's thoughts and feelings with another person or with a higher power; a mystical experience.| She found solace in communing with nature. 她在大自然中找到了慰藉。|近义词：connecting, sharing, conversing, meditating|反义词：isolating, withdrawing|词性:名词 (gerund or present participle of 'commune')

commune: /kəˈmjuːn/
| vi. |亲密交谈；交流；共享；与…融为一体|To exchange thoughts and feelings; to have a mystical experience.|He communed with nature, finding peace in the quiet forest. 他与大自然融为一体，在寂静的森林中找到了宁静。|近义词：connect, share, converse, meditate|反义词：isolate, withdraw|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Old French comuner "to share," from Latin communicare "to share, communicate," from communis "common."
