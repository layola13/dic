delineatory:/dɪˈlɪniətɔːri/ | adj. | 描绘的，轮廓分明的，描述性的 | Serving to delineate; descriptive.  Representing or outlining with precision. | The architect's delineatory sketches clearly showed the building's planned structure.  建筑师的描绘性草图清晰地展示了建筑物的规划结构。| synonyms: descriptive, outlining, defining, representative, illustrative | antonyms: vague, obscure, unclear, ambiguous | adjective

delineate:/dɪˈlɪnieɪt/ | vt. | 描绘，勾勒，描述 | To describe or portray (something) precisely. To indicate the exact position of (a border or boundary). | The report clearly delineates the responsibilities of each department.  该报告清晰地描述了每个部门的职责。 The map delineates the boundaries of the property. 地图标出了房产的边界。 | synonyms: describe, portray, depict, outline, sketch, define | antonyms: obscure, confuse, blur | verb
