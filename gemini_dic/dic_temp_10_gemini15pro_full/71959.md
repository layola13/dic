flightiness:/ˈflaɪtinəs/| n.|轻浮，轻佻，反复无常|The flightiness of the young girl was charming to some, but irritating to others. 这个年轻女孩的轻浮对某些人来说很迷人，但对其他人来说很烦人。|例句：Her flightiness made it difficult to have a serious conversation with her. 她的轻浮让人很难和她进行严肃的谈话。

例句：The flightiness of the market made it a risky investment.  市场的反复无常使得投资风险很高。

近义词：fickleness, capriciousness, volatility, levity|反义词:seriousness, stability, constancy|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From flighty + -ness.  Flighty itself comes from the Old English *flihting, from *fliht (flight).  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
