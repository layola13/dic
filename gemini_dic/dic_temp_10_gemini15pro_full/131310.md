nonce: /nɒns/ | n. | 一次性使用；随机数；临时使用的值；（尤指）滑稽诗 | a number used once only, in cryptography, to prevent attackers from reusing old communications |  The system generates a random nonce for each transaction.  该系统为每笔交易生成一个随机数。 | synonyms:  one-off, single-use, temporary value | antonyms: permanent, persistent | noun


nonce: /nɒns/ | n. | （尤指）滑稽诗；即兴诗 | a short poem, especially one written for a particular occasion, that is often humorous |  He wrote a nonce for the wedding. 他为婚礼写了一首滑稽诗。 | synonyms:  doggerel, jingle, rhyme | antonyms:  epic, serious poem | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *nanes "for the once," from *for then anes "for the one (occasion)," from Old English for þǣm ānes "for that one thing, for that one time, for the nonce," from ānes, genitive of ān "one."
