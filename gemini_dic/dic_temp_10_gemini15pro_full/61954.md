emulsify:/ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪ/ /əˈmʌlsəˌfaɪ/ | v. |乳化；使…乳化|To combine two liquids that don't normally mix, such as oil and water, into a stable mixture, usually by adding an emulsifier. | Oil and water don't mix easily, but you can emulsify them by adding egg yolk.  油和水不容易混合，但是你可以通过加入蛋黄来乳化它们。

The chef emulsified the oil and vinegar to make a salad dressing.  厨师将油和醋乳化，做成沙拉酱。|近义词：blend, mix, combine, homogenize |反义词:separate |词性:verb
