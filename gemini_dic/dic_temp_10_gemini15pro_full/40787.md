convivially:/kənˈvɪviəli/
adverb
欢乐地；欢宴地； convivial 的副词形式
In a convivial and friendly atmosphere. 在欢乐友好的气氛中。
The evening was spent convivially with friends.  晚上和朋友们愉快地度过了。
近义词：jovially, merrily, cheerfully, festively
反义词：somberly, gloomily, morosely
词性：副词

convivial:/kənˈvɪviəl/
adjective
欢乐的；欢宴的；好交际的
a convivial atmosphere 欢乐的气氛
a convivial gathering  欢乐的聚会
近义词：jovial, merry, cheerful, festive, sociable, gregarious
反义词：somber, gloomy, morose, unsociable
词性：形容词

词源：
From late Middle English: from Latin convivialis, from convivium ‘a feast’, from con- ‘together’ + vivere ‘to live’.
