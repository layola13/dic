decrescendo:/ˌdeɪkrəˈʃendoʊ/ /ˌdeɪkrəˈʃɛndoʊ/
noun
渐弱
a gradual decrease in the volume of music
The orchestra played a beautiful decrescendo at the end of the symphony.  管弦乐队在交响曲的结尾演奏了一段优美的渐弱。
diminution, tapering off
crescendo, increase
