haganah:/ˌhɑːɡəˈnɑː/| n. |哈加纳;  (尤指在以色列建国前的巴勒斯坦地区的)犹太准军事组织|A Jewish paramilitary organization in Palestine (especially during the mandate) before the establishment of the state of Israel. |The Haganah played a crucial role in the 1948 Arab–Israeli War. 哈加纳在1948年的阿以战争中发挥了至关重要的作用。|Synonyms:  defense force, militia, underground army|Antonyms: pacifist organization, peace movement|Noun
