overdifferentiations: /ˌoʊvərˌdɪfəˌrɛnʃiˈeɪʃənz/
| n. |过度区分；过度分化|The act of making too many distinctions or classifications, often unnecessarily subtle or complex. It can lead to confusion and inefficiency.  It can also refer to excessive specialization in biology, where cells or tissues become too specialized for their own good.|
|例句：Overdifferentiations in the marketing strategy led to a fragmented customer base. 市场策略的过度细分导致了客户群的分散。

|例句：The overdifferentiations of roles within the team hindered effective collaboration. 团队内部角色的过度区分阻碍了有效的合作。
|近义词：overcategorization, overclassification, overspecialization|
|反义词：simplification, generalization, standardization|
|词性：名词|
