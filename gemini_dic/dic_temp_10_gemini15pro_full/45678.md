curtest:/ˈkɜːrtɪst/| adj.|最简短的；最唐突的|Shortest, most abrupt, most rudely brief.|例句：He gave me the curtest reply I've ever heard. 他给我的回复是我听过的最唐突的。|近义词：abrupt, brusque, terse, succinct|反义词:polite, courteous, lengthy, wordy|词性:形容词
Etymology: From 'curt' (short, rudely brief) + '-est' (superlative suffix). 'Curt' comes from the Latin 'curtus' meaning "shortened, cut short."
