crayer:/ˈkreɪər/ | n. | 小船; 小驳船 | A small boat, especially one used for carrying goods. |  例句：The crayer transported goods along the canal. 小驳船沿着运河运送货物。

例句：Fishermen used crayers to bring their catch ashore. 渔民们用小船把捕获物带到岸上。| 近义词：barge, lighter, skiff, boat | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "ship" as a much larger vessel) | 词性: noun
