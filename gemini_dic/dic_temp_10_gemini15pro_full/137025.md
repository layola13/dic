nonstatement:/ˈnɒnˌsteɪtmənt/
n.
非陈述; 不表态; 模棱两可的话
An expression that avoids making a definite statement or commitment.
The politician's response to the scandal was a carefully crafted nonstatement.  这位政治家对丑闻的回应是一番精心设计的模棱两可之词。
近义词：equivocation, evasion, ambiguity
反义词：statement, assertion, declaration
词性：名词

nonstatement:/ˌnɑːnˈsteɪtmənt/
n.
非陈述; 不表态; 模棱两可的话
An expression that avoids making a definite statement or commitment.
Her nonstatement on the issue angered both sides. 她对这个问题的不表态激怒了双方。
近义词：prevarication, hedge
反义词：affirmation, confirmation
词性：名词


词源:
non- + statement.  "non-"  prefix meaning "not," and "statement" meaning a definite or clear expression of something in speech or writing.
