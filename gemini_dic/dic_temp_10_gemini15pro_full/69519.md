fellas:/ˈfɛləz/| n.|伙计们，小伙子们|An informal term of address for a group of men or boys, often used to express camaraderie or familiarity.  It's a plural form of "fella."|Hey fellas, what are you up to tonight? 嘿，伙计们，今晚你们打算做什么？|近义词：guys, dudes, mates, boys|反义词:ladies, gals (These are not true opposites but offer gender contrast)|Noun
