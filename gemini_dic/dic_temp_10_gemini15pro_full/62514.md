endpapers:/ˈɛndˌpeɪpəz/
n.
衬页，扉页
Sheets of paper glued to the inside covers of a book, often decorated or left blank for writing.
The endpapers of the antique book were marbled and beautifully preserved. 这本古董书的衬页是大理石花纹的，保存得非常好。
The author signed the endpapers of the first edition. 作者在第一版的衬页上签名。
synonyms: flyleaves, pastedowns
antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps book cover)
noun
