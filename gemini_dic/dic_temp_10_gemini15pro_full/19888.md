binocularity:/baɪˌnɒkjʊˈlærɪti/ | n. | 双眼性，双眼视觉 | The ability to see with both eyes simultaneously, allowing for depth perception. |  Binocularity is essential for judging distances accurately.  双眼视觉对于准确判断距离至关重要。| synonyms: stereopsis, two-eyed vision | antonyms: monocularity | noun

binocular:/bɪˈnɒkjʊlə(r)/ | adj. | 双眼的 | Relating to, using, or adapted for both eyes. | Binocular vision allows us to perceive depth. 双眼视觉使我们能够感知深度。| synonyms: two-eyed | antonyms: monocular | adjective
