ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + activate + -ion (suffix forming nouns).  "Activate" comes from Late Latin *activatus, past participle of activare "to make active," from Latin activus "active," from actus "a doing, a driving," past participle of agere "to do, drive, lead."
