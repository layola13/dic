doubtlessly:/ˈdaʊtləsli/| adv.|毫无疑问地，肯定地|Without any doubt; certainly.|He will doubtlessly succeed. 他肯定会成功。|近义词:undoubtedly, certainly, definitely, surely, unquestionably|反义词:doubtfully, questionably|词性:副词
Etymology: From doubt + -less + -ly.  "Doubt" ultimately comes from Latin "dubitare" (to hesitate, waver).
