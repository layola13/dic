cryptology:/krɪpˈtɒlədʒi/ /krɪpˈtɑːlədʒi/
n.
密码学
The study of codes, ciphers, and other means of concealing information, including both cryptography (making codes) and cryptanalysis (breaking codes).
Cryptography and cryptanalysis are the two main branches of cryptology. 密码学和密码分析是密码学的两个主要分支。
Modern cryptology plays a vital role in ensuring online security. 现代密码学在保障网络安全方面发挥着至关重要的作用。
近义词: cryptography, codebreaking
反义词: (无直接反义词)
词性: noun
