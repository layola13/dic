frivolously: /ˈfrɪvələsli/ | adv. | 轻浮地，轻率地 | In a frivolous manner; not serious or sensible. | She frivolously spent all her money on clothes. 她轻浮地把所有的钱都花在了衣服上。| He dismissed the issue frivolously, not realizing its importance. 他轻率地驳回了这个问题，没有意识到它的重要性。 | 近义词: carelessly, thoughtlessly, giddily, foolishly | 反义词: seriously, thoughtfully, prudently, soberly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *frivolous*, from Latin *frivolus* "silly, trifling, worthless," perhaps related to *friare* "to crumble, break into small pieces".
