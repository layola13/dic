hoarder:/ˈhɔːrdər/ (US), /ˈhɔːdə(r)/ (UK)| n. |囤积者，收藏者|A person who accumulates and keeps large quantities of items, often useless or of little value, due to a compulsion or fear of discarding them. |例句：The hoarder's house was filled with old newspapers, broken appliances, and other junk.  囤积者的房子里堆满了旧报纸、坏掉的电器和其他垃圾。

例句：He was a compulsive hoarder, unable to throw anything away. 他是一个强迫性囤积者，无法扔掉任何东西。|近义词：collector, accumulator, saver|反义词：spender, minimalist|词性:noun
