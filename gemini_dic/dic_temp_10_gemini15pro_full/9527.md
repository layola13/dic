antisegregation:/ˌæntiˌseɡrɪˈɡeɪʃən/
| adj. n.|反种族隔离的；反隔离的|Opposing or prohibiting segregation, especially racial segregation.|The antisegregation movement gained momentum in the 1960s.  20世纪60年代，反种族隔离运动势头强劲。|近义词：integrationist, desegregationist|反义词：segregationist|adjective, noun

antisegregation:/ˌæntiˌseɡrɪˈɡeɪʃən/
| n.|反种族隔离|The act or policy of opposing or prohibiting segregation.|The government's commitment to antisegregation was tested in the courts. 政府对反种族隔离的承诺在法庭上受到了考验。|近义词：integration, desegregation|反义词：segregation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + segregation.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to." "Segregation" comes from the Latin word "segregare," meaning "to separate from the flock."
