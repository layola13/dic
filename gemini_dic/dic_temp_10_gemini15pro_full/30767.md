cessment: /ˈsɛsmənt/
n. |停止，终止|The cessation of hostilities marked a turning point in the war. |敌对行动的停止标志着战争的转折点。|近义词：stop, termination, discontinuation|反义词：continuance, commencement|词性:名词

Etymology: From Old French "cessacion" and directly from Latin "cessatio,"  from "cessare" meaning "to cease."
