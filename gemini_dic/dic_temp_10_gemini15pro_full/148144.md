oversensitivities:/ˌoʊvərˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪtiz/
|n.| 过度敏感;过敏;过敏症|The state of being excessively sensitive to something, often resulting in an allergic reaction or emotional distress.|She suffers from several environmental oversensitivities, including pollen and dust mites. 她对几种环境因素过敏，包括花粉和尘螨。|近义词:allergies, sensitivities, susceptibilities|反义词: tolerances, immunities|词性:名词
