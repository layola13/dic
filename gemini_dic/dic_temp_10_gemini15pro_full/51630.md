develops:/dɪˈvɛləps/| v.|发展，开发，研制，患(病)，冲洗(照片)|Third-world countries need to develop their industries. 发展中国家需要发展工业。|近义词：evolve, grow, progress, mature, expand, advance, elaborate|反义词：decline, regress, deteriorate, contract, diminish|词性:动词

Etymology: From Old French *desveloper* (modern French *développer*), from *des-* (prefix meaning "un-," "out") + *veloper* "to wrap," of uncertain origin.
