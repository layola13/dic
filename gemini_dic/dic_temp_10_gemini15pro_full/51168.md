despumate:/diːˈspjuːmeɪt/ /dɪˈspjuːˌmeɪt/ | v. |去除泡沫，撇去浮沫|To remove froth or foam from (a liquid).|The winemaker despumated the fermenting grape juice. |酿酒师将发酵的葡萄汁撇去浮沫。|Synonyms: skim, clarify, purify |Antonyms: froth, foam|verb
despumate:/diːˈspjuːmeɪt/ /dɪˈspjuːˌmeɪt/ | n. |去沫剂|A substance used to remove froth or foam.|They added a despumate to the boiling wort. |他们在煮沸的麦芽汁中加入了去沫剂。|Synonyms: antifoaming agent, defoamer|Antonyms: foaming agent|noun
