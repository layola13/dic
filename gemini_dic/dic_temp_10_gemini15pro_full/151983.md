pastureland: /ˈpæstʃərˌlænd/ | n. | 牧场；草地 | Land used for grazing livestock. | The farmer rotated his cattle through different sections of pastureland. 农民轮流将他的牛放到牧场的不同区域吃草。|  The cows grazed peacefully in the lush pastureland. 奶牛在郁郁葱葱的牧场上平静地吃草。 | synonyms: pasture, meadow, grassland, grazing land | antonyms:  barren land, desert, wasteland | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *pasture lond*, from Old French *pasture*, from Latin *pastūra* ("pasture, grazing"), from *pāscō* ("to feed, graze").  The word *land* is from Old English *land*.
