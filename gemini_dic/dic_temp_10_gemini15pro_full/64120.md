epsomite:/ˈɛpsəˌmaɪt/ | n. |泻盐，七水硫酸镁 |A naturally occurring hydrous magnesium sulfate mineral, MgSO₄·7H₂O. It is used as a source of magnesium and sulfur, and in medicine as a laxative.| Epsom salts are commonly used to soothe sore muscles. 泻盐通常用来舒缓肌肉酸痛。|Synonyms: Epsom salt, magnesium sulfate heptahydrate | Antonyms: N/A | noun
