enfeoffment:/ɪnˈfiːfmənt/
n.
封地；授予封地；封臣身份
The act of granting land in exchange for a pledge of service and loyalty.
The king granted him enfeoffment of vast tracts of land. 国王将大片土地封给他。
investiture, endowment
dispossession, deprivation
noun
