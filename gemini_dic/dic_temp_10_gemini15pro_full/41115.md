coproduces:/ˌkoʊprəˈduːsɪz/
| v. | 共同制作，联合制作 | To produce something together with another person or other people. | The film was coproduced by companies in the US and France.  这部电影是由美国和法国的公司联合制作的。|
| | |  The documentary was coproduced with the BBC. 这部纪录片是与BBC联合制作的。|
| | | synonym: co-produce, collaborate on, jointly produce | antonym:  solely produce, independently produce | verb |

coproduce:/ˌkoʊprəˈduːs/
| v. | 共同制作，联合制作 | To produce something together with another person or other people. | The two countries coproduced the Olympic opening ceremony. 这两个国家联合举办了奥运会开幕式。|
| | | They coproduced a series of documentaries about wildlife. 他们联合制作了一系列关于野生动物的纪录片。|
| | | synonym: co-produce, collaborate on, jointly produce | antonym:  solely produce, independently produce | verb |

coproduction:/ˌkoʊprəˈdʌkʃən/
| n. | 共同制作，联合制作 | The act of producing something together with another person or other people, or a product that has been produced in this way. | The play was a coproduction between two theatre companies. 这部戏是两家剧院的联合制作。|
| | | The film is a coproduction between France and Italy. 这部电影是法国和意大利的合拍片。|
| | | synonym: joint production, collaboration | antonym: solo production, independent production | noun |
