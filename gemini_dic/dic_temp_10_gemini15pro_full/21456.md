blows:/bloʊz/ (US) /bləʊz/ (UK)| n. v.| 打击；吹；吹气；机会；打击；意外的挫折|
The boxer delivered a series of powerful blows to his opponent. | 拳击手向对手发出了一系列强有力的打击。|
A sudden gust of wind blows the leaves off the trees. | 一阵突如其来的狂风把树叶吹落。|
近义词: hits, punches, gusts, blasts, misfortunes, setbacks | 反义词: blessings, fortunes | 词性: 名词/动词

blows (n.): From Old English *blāwian, from Proto-Germanic *blēaną (“to blow”). Cognate with Old High German blājan (“to blow”).
blows (v.): Third-person singular present tense form of blow.
