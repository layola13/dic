neighborhood: /ˈneɪbərˌhʊd/ (US) /ˈneɪbəhʊd/ (UK)| n. |街区；邻近地区；附近；邻居|A district or area with distinctive characteristics, often with reference to the people who live there. neighborhood: /ˈneɪbərˌhʊd/ (US) /ˈneɪbəhʊd/ (UK)| n. |附近；邻近|The state of being near or next to something.|例句：The park is in the neighborhood of the school. 公园在学校附近。|近义词：proximity, vicinity|反义词:distance|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *nēahgebūr+hād.  *nēahgebūr from nēah "near" + gebūr "dweller."  -hād is a suffix denoting "condition, state, or quality."
