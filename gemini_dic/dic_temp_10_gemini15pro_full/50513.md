deprivation:/ˌdɛprɪˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 剥夺，丧失，贫困 |  The state of being deprived of something, especially something considered essential for well-being. |  The prolonged sleep deprivation had a negative impact on her cognitive functions. 长时间的睡眠剥夺对她认知功能产生了负面影响。| synonyms: dispossession, loss, poverty, hardship | antonyms: provision, abundance, wealth, privilege | noun

deprive:/dɪˈpraɪv/ | vt. | 剥夺，使丧失 | To take something away from someone or something; prevent from having or using. |  He was deprived of his freedom for many years. 他被剥夺了多年的自由。 | synonyms: dispossess, strip, rob, divest | antonyms: provide, grant, endow, enrich | verb
