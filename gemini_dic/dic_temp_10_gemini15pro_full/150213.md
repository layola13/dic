pandal:/ˈpændəl/ | n. | 棚架，帐篷，临时搭建的棚子 | A large tent or temporary structure, typically used for celebrations or exhibitions. |  The wedding reception was held under a beautifully decorated pandal. 婚礼招待会在一个装饰精美的棚架下举行。

The pandal provided shade from the scorching sun during the festival. 节日期间，棚架提供了遮蔽烈日的阴凉。| synonyms: marquee, canopy, tent | antonyms: building, permanent structure | noun
