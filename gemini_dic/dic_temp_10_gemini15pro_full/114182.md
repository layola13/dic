mahratta:/məˈrætə/ | n. | 马拉地人，马拉地语 | A member of a people of western and central India. Also, the language they speak, belonging to the Indic branch of the Indo-European family. |  Shivaji was a great Maratha warrior.  希瓦吉是一位伟大的马拉地武士。

The Maratha language is spoken by millions.  数百万人口讲马拉地语。 | 近义词:Marathi (referring to the language) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a people group) | 词性: Noun

Etymology: From Marathi मराठा (marāṭhā), ultimately from Sanskrit महाराष्ट्र (mahārāṣṭra), name of the region.
