enhydrous:/ɛnˈhaɪdrəs/| adj.|含水的，水合的|Containing water, especially water of crystallization.|The enhydrous form of the mineral is more stable. 这种矿物的含水形态更稳定。|近义词：hydrated, hydrous|反义词:anhydrous|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From en- + hydrous.  "En-" is a prefix meaning "in," "within," or "containing."  "Hydrous" comes from the Greek "hydōr" meaning "water."
