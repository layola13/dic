minder:/ˈmaɪndər/| n.|照顾者，保姆，提醒器|A person who looks after someone, especially a child or sick person.  A device that reminds you of something.|She hired a minder for her children after school. 她雇了一个人放学后照顾她的孩子。|My phone has a built-in minder for appointments. 我的手机有内置的约会提醒功能。|Synonyms: caregiver, babysitter, guardian, nursemaid,  reminder, alarm|Antonyms: neglecter, ward|noun
