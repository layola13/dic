contractionary:/kənˈtrækʃəneri/ |adj.|收缩的；紧缩的| tending to cause economic contraction, typically by reducing government spending or raising interest rates. | The government implemented contractionary fiscal policies to combat inflation. 政府实施紧缩的财政政策以对抗通货膨胀。| The central bank's contractionary monetary policy led to higher interest rates. 中央银行的紧缩货币政策导致利率上升。| synonyms: restrictive, deflationary, tightening | antonyms: expansionary, inflationary, loosening | adjective
