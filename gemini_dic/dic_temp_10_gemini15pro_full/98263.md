inserters:/ɪnˈsɜːrtərz/| n.|插入器，插入装置|A device or tool used for inserting something into something else.  Specifically in automation and robotics, it refers to a mechanism that picks and places components into designated locations, like a circuit board or a packaging container.|The factory uses automated inserters to place components on the circuit boards.|近义词: loaders, placers, feeders|反义词: extractors, removers|noun
