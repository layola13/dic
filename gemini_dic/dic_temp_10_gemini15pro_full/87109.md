heran:/ˈhɛrɑːn/ (British), /ˈhɪrən/ (American)| adj.| 奇怪的；古怪的；异常的；令人惊讶的| Strikingly unusual or surprising; odd; peculiar. |例句：Heran behaviour from the usually quiet child. 这个平时安静的孩子表现古怪。|近义词：strange, peculiar, odd, unusual, bizarre, weird|反义词：normal, ordinary, usual, common|词性:形容词

词源：From Middle English *heran, from Old English *hæran, from Proto-Germanic *hairaną. Cognate with Dutch haren (“to raise hair”), German hären (“to bristle”).  The meaning shifted from "to bristle" (like hair standing on end in surprise) to "strange, unusual".
