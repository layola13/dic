flanconade:/ˌflæŋkəˈneɪd/| n.|侧翼攻击，侧击|A flanking movement, especially in fencing or military tactics, against an opponent's side.|The general ordered a flanconade to break the enemy's line. 将军下令侧翼攻击以突破敌人的防线。|Synonyms: flanking maneuver, flank attack|Antonyms: frontal assault|noun
