forhall: /ˈfɔːrhɔːl/
n.
前厅，门厅
A forhall is an entrance hall or lobby, typically in a large building.
The forhall of the hotel was grand and impressive.  酒店的前厅富丽堂皇，令人印象深刻。
The guests gathered in the forhall before the ceremony.  客人们在仪式开始前聚集在前厅。
Synonyms: lobby, foyer, entrance hall, vestibule
Antonyms: back room, back office
