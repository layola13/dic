hyperoxygenations:/ˌhaɪpərˌɒksɪdʒəˈneɪʃənz/ | n. |  超氧合；过度氧合 | The process of increasing the oxygen concentration in a tissue or organ beyond normal levels. This can be achieved through various methods, such as administering supplemental oxygen or increasing the pressure of inspired oxygen. |  Hyperoxygenations before and after surgery can help reduce the risk of complications. 手术前后的超氧合有助于降低并发症的风险。|  hyperoxia, oxygen enrichment | hypoxia | noun
