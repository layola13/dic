groups:/ɡruːps/ | n. | 组，团体，群 | A number of people or things that are located close together or are considered or classed together. |  The children were divided into three groups. 孩子们被分成三组。 | synonyms: teams, bands, crowds, gatherings, assemblies | antonyms: individuals, singles | noun

groups:/ɡruːps/ | v. | 分组，归类 |  Arrange (people or things) into groups. | The teacher grouped the students by ability. 老师按能力将学生分组。 | synonyms: classify, categorize, sort, arrange | antonyms: separate, divide, disperse | verb
