compass:/ˈkʌmpəs/ (US) /ˈkʌmpəs/ (UK)| n.|指南针，罗盘；范围，界限|An instrument used for navigation and orientation that shows the direction of magnetic north.|He used a compass to find his way through the forest. 他用指南针在森林里找到了路。|Synonyms: navigator,  direction finder|Antonyms:  none|noun

compass:/ˈkʌmpəs/ (US) /ˈkʌmpəs/ (UK)| v.|包围，环绕；理解，领会|To surround or enclose; to grasp mentally.|The soldiers compassed the city. 士兵们包围了这座城市。|Synonyms: encircle, surround, encompass, grasp, understand|Antonyms: exclude, misunderstand|verb
