midpoly:/ˈmɪdˌpɒli/| adj. n.|中多边形，中等多边形|A 3D model with a moderate polygon count, offering a balance between detail and performance.  It's often used in games and real-time applications.|The artist used a midpoly model for the character's base mesh.  艺术家使用中等多边形模型作为角色的基础网格。|近义词:medium poly, mid-poly|反义词:highpoly, lowpoly|词性:adjective, noun


Etymology->
"midpoly" is a blend of "mid" (meaning middle or medium) and "poly," short for "polygon."  It reflects the model's position in the spectrum of polygon counts.
