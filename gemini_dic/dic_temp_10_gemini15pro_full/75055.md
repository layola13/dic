frightenedly:/ˈfraɪtndli/| adv.|害怕地，惊恐地|In a manner exhibiting fear or alarm.  Describes how an action is performed due to fear.|She looked around frightenedly. 她惊恐地环顾四周。|The child clung to his mother frightenedly. 孩子害怕地 clinging to his mother.|近义词：fearfully, nervously, anxiously, timidly|反义词：bravely, boldly, courageously|词性:副词
Etymology: From *frighten* (verb) + *-ed* (past participle suffix) + *-ly* (adverbial suffix). *Frighten* comes from Old English *fryhtan*, meaning "to frighten," from *fyrhto*, meaning "fear."
