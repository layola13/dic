martinmas:/ˈmɑːrtnˌməs/ | n. | 圣马丁节（11月11日） | A church festival held on 11 November in honor of St. Martin of Tours. |  例句：Martinmas is a time for feasting and celebration. 圣马丁节是宴会和庆祝的时候。

例句：The farmers' market was especially busy on Martinmas. 圣马丁节那天农贸市场特别繁忙。 | 近义词：Martinmas Day, Feast of Saint Martin | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *Martinemasse, from Old English *Mārtīnes mæsse (“mass of Martin”), equivalent to Martin +‎ mass. Cognate with Scots Martinmas, Saterland Frisian Määrtenmäs, West Frisian Martenmäs, Dutch Sint-Maarten, German Martinstag, Danish Mortensdag.
