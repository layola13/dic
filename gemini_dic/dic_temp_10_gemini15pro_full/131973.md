nonconversancy:/ˌnɒnkɒnvɜːrsənsi/ /ˌnɑːnkɑːnvɜːrsənsi/| n. |不熟悉，不精通|Lack of familiarity or expertise in a particular subject or area.|His nonconversancy with the local language made it difficult for him to communicate. 他对当地语言的不熟悉使他难以沟通。|Example: Her nonconversancy with the new software slowed down the project. 她对新软件的不熟悉拖慢了项目的进度。|Synonyms: unfamiliarity, ignorance, inexperience|Antonyms: familiarity, expertise, mastery|noun
