autobracketing:/ˌɔːtəʊˈbrækɪtɪŋ/ |n.|自动包围曝光|The process of automatically taking a series of photos at different exposure levels, typically three or more, to ensure a properly exposed image.  It's often used in HDR photography.|My camera's autobracketing feature is a lifesaver in challenging lighting conditions. 我的相机的自动包围曝光功能在光线复杂的条件下是个救命稻草。|Synonyms: exposure bracketing, AEB (Auto Exposure Bracketing)|Antonyms: single exposure|Noun
