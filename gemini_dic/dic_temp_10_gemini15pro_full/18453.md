bemoaned:/bɪˈmoʊnd/
verb
哀叹，悲叹
express sorrow or regret about something
The farmers bemoaned the loss of their crops due to the drought. 农民们哀叹由于干旱造成的庄稼损失。
lament, bewail, mourn
rejoice, celebrate
