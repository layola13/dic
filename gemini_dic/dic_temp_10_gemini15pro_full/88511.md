histozyme:/ˈhɪstoʊzaɪm/
n.
组织酶
An enzyme that acts on tissues.  Specifically, it refers to an enzyme preparation from hog stomach used as a meat tenderizer.
Histozyme can be used to tenderize tough cuts of meat. 组织酶可以用来使硬肉变嫩。
The butcher uses histozyme to prepare the steaks. 肉贩用组织酶来处理牛排。
Synonyms: enzyme, protease
Antonyms: inhibitor
