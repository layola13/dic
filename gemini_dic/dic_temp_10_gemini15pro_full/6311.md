amortized:/ˈæmərtaɪzd/ /əˈmɔːrtaɪzd/| adj.|摊销的，分期偿还的|Reduced or paid off gradually, especially by periodic payments of principal and interest.|The company amortized the cost of the new equipment over five years.  该公司将新设备的成本摊销了五年。|Synonyms: depreciated, expensed, written off|Antonyms: capitalized, accrued|adjective


amortize:/ˈæmərtaɪz/ /əˈmɔːrtaɪz/| v.|摊销，分期偿还|To gradually reduce or write off the cost or value of (an asset or debt) over a period of time.|They amortized the loan over ten years. 他们用十年时间分期偿还了贷款。|Synonyms: depreciate, expense, write off|Antonyms: capitalize, accrue|verb
