civilizable:/ˈsɪvɪlaɪzəbl/
adjective
可文明化的，可教化的
capable of being civilized; susceptible to civilization
例句：
The tribe was considered civilizable and open to adopting modern practices.
这个部落被认为是可以教化的，愿意接受现代习俗。

例句：
Many believed that the nomadic people were not civilizable.
许多人认为游牧民族是不可开化的。

近义词：educable, teachable, refinable, cultivable
反义词：uncivilized, barbaric, savage, primitive
词性: adjective
