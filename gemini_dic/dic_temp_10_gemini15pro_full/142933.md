out-travel:/ˌaʊtˈtrævl/
verb
超过…旅行的距离；比…更广地旅行
To travel further or more extensively than someone or something else.
He out-traveled all his colleagues, visiting every continent. 他比所有同事都旅行得更远，游遍了每个大洲。
She out-traveled her guidebook, discovering hidden gems off the beaten path. 她探索的范围超出了旅行指南的推荐，发现了一些人迹罕至的宝藏。
近义词: outdo, surpass, exceed
反义词: lag behind, fall short of
词性: verb
