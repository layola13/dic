militiamen:/məˈlɪʃəmən/| n. |民兵|A member of a militia, a military force that is raised from the civil population to supplement a regular army in an emergency.|The militiamen were called up to defend the town. 民兵们被召集起来保卫城镇。|近义词：soldier, reservist, guardsman|反义词:civilian|noun
