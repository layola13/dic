dismalest:/ˈdɪzmələst/| adj.|最凄凉的，最阴沉的|Superlative form of dismal: most dismal.  Expressing or causing gloom or depression.|例句：It was the dismalest day of the year. 这是年度最阴沉的一天。|近义词：gloomiest, bleakest, dreariest|反义词：brightest, cheeriest, happiest|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *dismal, from Anglo-Norman dismal, from Old French *dismal, from Late Latin diēs mali (“evil days”), from Latin diēs (“day”) + malus (“bad, evil”).
