gnathostegite:/ˈnæθəˌstɛdʒaɪt/| n.|颚足外肢；颚肢盖|A flattened, plate-like expansion of the outer branch of certain crustacean appendages, often forming a brood pouch or covering the gills.|The gnathotegites of the female crayfish form a brood chamber.  雌性小龙虾的颚足外肢形成了一个育儿室。|Synonym: branchiostegite|Antonym: N/A|noun
