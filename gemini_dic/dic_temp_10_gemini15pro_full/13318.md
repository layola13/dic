athrepsia: /əˈθrɛpsiə/ | n. | 婴幼儿衰竭，营养不良 |A wasting away, especially in infants and young children, from lack of nourishment.  A severe form of malnutrition characterized by growth retardation and wasting of tissues. | The doctor diagnosed the baby with athrepsia due to prolonged lack of proper nutrition.  医生诊断婴儿因长期缺乏适当营养而患有营养不良。 | Synonym: malnutrition, marasmus, cachexia | Antonym:  nourishment, thriving | noun
