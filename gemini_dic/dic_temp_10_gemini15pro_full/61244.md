emanation:/ˌeməˈneɪʃn/
n.
散发物，发出物，放射物
something that originates or issues from a source
例句：A foul emanation filled the air. 空气中弥漫着难闻的气味。

例句：The government is an emanation of the people. 政府是民意的体现。

近义词：emission, discharge, radiation, effluence
反义词：absorption

词性：名词
