devolve:/dɪˈvɒlv/ /dɪˈvɑːlv/| v. |转移（权力、责任等），退化，衰落|To transfer or delegate (power or responsibility) to a lower level, especially from central government to local or regional administration; to pass on or down (something, such as property or a title) to a successor or heir; (of something, such as responsibility) to be passed or fall on. Also, to gradually worsen.|The central government devolved power to local authorities. 中央政府将权力下放给地方当局。|Near synonyms: delegate, transfer, pass down, entrust, convey, transmit |Antonyms: centralize, consolidate, retain, assume, usurp|verb
