house-proud:/ˈhaʊs praʊd/
| adj. |
| 为自己的房子感到骄傲的；喜欢炫耀自己房子的 |
| Excessively concerned with keeping one's house clean and tidy and showing it off to visitors.  过分在意保持房子的干净整洁，并向访客炫耀。 |
| She's so house-proud, everything is always spotless. 她非常为自己的房子感到骄傲，一切都总是纤尘不染。 |

| My grandmother is very house-proud and always has a vase of fresh flowers on display. 我祖母非常注重房子的整洁，总是摆放着一瓶新鲜的鲜花。 |

| 近义词: housewifely, domesticated |
| 反义词: slovenly, untidy |
| 词性: adjective |
