feuilleton:/ˌfɜːɪˈtɒn/ (UK), /ˌfɜːˈtɒn/ (US) | n. |  文艺评论;  长篇连载小说;  (尤指报纸上的)文艺副刊 | A part of a newspaper or magazine devoted to fiction, criticism, or light literature, often serialized.  A short literary essay. | Example 1:  The author gained fame through his feuilleton in the local newspaper.  这位作者通过在当地报纸上发表的文艺评论获得了名声。

Example 2: Her latest novel began as a feuilleton in a popular magazine. 她最新的小说是从一本流行杂志上的连载小说开始的。 | Synonym: serial, column, feature, article | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "news report" for its factual nature as opposed to the often fictional or critical nature of a feuilleton) | noun
