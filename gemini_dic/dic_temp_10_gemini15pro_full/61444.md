embosses:/ɪmˈbɒsɪz/ /ɪmˈbɑːsɪz/
verb
装饰；凸印；浮雕
to decorate (a surface) with raised ornament
例句：The company embosses its logo on all its products.  这家公司在其所有产品上都印有凸起的商标。

例句：She embossed the stationery with her initials. 她用自己名字的首字母在信纸上做了凸印。

近义词：raise, carve, mold, stamp, imprint
反义词：flatten, smooth

词性
v.
