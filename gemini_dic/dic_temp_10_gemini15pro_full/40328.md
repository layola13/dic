contraband:/ˈkɑːntrəbænd/ | n. adj.| 违禁品，走私货；非法交易的 | Goods whose importation, exportation, or possession is forbidden by law. |  The customs officers seized the contraband. 海关官员查获了违禁品。| synonyms: illegal goods, smuggled goods, illicit goods, prohibited goods | antonyms: legal goods, permitted goods | noun

contraband:/ˈkɑːntrəbænd/ | adj.| 违禁的，非法交易的 | Prohibited by law from being imported, exported, or possessed. | The contraband cigarettes were confiscated. 违禁香烟被没收了。| synonyms: illegal, illicit, prohibited, unlawful | antonyms: legal, lawful, permitted | adjective
