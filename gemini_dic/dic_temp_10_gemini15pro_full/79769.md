gluteus: /ˈɡluːtiəs/ /ˈɡluːtiːəs/ | n. | 臀肌 |  Any of the three large muscles in each buttock that move the thigh, the gluteus maximus, gluteus medius, and gluteus minimus. |  Example: Squats are a great exercise for strengthening the gluteus maximus. 深蹲是增强臀大肌的有效运动。 | Synonym: buttock muscle, gluteal muscle | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a muscle) | noun

gluteus maximus: /ˌɡluːtiəs ˈmæksɪməs/ /ˌɡluːtiːəs ˈmæksɪməs/ | n. | 臀大肌 | The largest muscle of the buttock, responsible for extending and laterally rotating the hip joint. | Example: The gluteus maximus is the main muscle used for climbing stairs. 爬楼梯主要用到的肌肉是臀大肌。 | Synonym:  (No direct synonym, often referred to as glutes) | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a muscle) | noun
