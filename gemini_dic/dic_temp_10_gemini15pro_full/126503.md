myxophyta:/ˌmɪksˈɒfɪtə/ | n. | 粘菌门；粘菌类 | A division of lower plants, resembling both protozoa and fungi, commonly known as slime molds.  They are characterized by a vegetative phase consisting of a naked, multinucleate mass of protoplasm called a plasmodium. | Example: Myxophyta, also known as slime molds, are fascinating organisms. 粘菌，也被称为粘菌门，是迷人的生物。
