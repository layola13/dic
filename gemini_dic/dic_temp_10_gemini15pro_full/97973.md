inobservant:/ˌɪnəbˈzɜːrvənt/| adj.|不善于观察的，粗心大意的|Not good at noticing things; lacking in powers of observation.| He was so inobservant that he didn't even notice the elephant in the room. 他是如此不善于观察，以至于他甚至没有注意到房间里的大象。|近义词：unobservant, unmindful, heedless, oblivious|反义词: observant, attentive, mindful, aware|词性:形容词

inobservant:/ˌɪnəbˈzɜːrvənt/| adj.|疏忽的，漫不经心的|Failing to notice or take account of something.|She was inobservant of the growing tension in the room. 她没有注意到房间里日益紧张的气氛。|近义词：negligent, careless, remiss|反义词: careful, meticulous, thorough|词性:形容词
