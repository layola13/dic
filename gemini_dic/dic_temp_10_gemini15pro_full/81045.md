graftage: /ˈɡrɑːftɪdʒ/ | n. | 嫁接；接枝 |The act or process of grafting plants.  The resulting union of a scion with a stock. |The gardener demonstrated the art of graftage on a young apple tree. 园丁在一棵年轻的苹果树上演示了嫁接的艺术。| synonyms: grafting, implanting, inarching | antonyms: detachment, separation | noun
