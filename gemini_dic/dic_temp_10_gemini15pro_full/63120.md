enslave:/ɪnˈsleɪv/| vt.|奴役，使成为奴隶|To make someone a slave; to deprive someone of freedom of choice or action.|The colonists enslaved the native population.|近义词：subjugate, oppress, captive|反义词:liberate, free|动词

enslaved:/ɪnˈsleɪvd/| adj.|被奴役的，受奴役的|Forced to work without pay under threat of violence; treated like a slave.|The enslaved people yearned for freedom.|近义词：captive, oppressed, subjugated|反义词:free, liberated|形容词

enslavement:/ɪnˈsleɪvmənt/| n.|奴役，束缚|The act of making someone a slave; the state of being a slave.|The enslavement of Africans was a horrific chapter in human history.|近义词：subjugation, oppression, captivity|反义词:liberation, freedom|名词
