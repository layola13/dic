forwhy:/fɔːrˈwaɪ/| adv.|为什么，为了什么 (nonstandard, informal)|A nonstandard and informal way of asking "why." It combines "for" and "why" but doesn't add any particular nuance to the meaning of "why" on its own.  Considered incorrect in formal writing and speech. |Example: Forwhy you gotta be so rude? = 你为什么这么粗鲁？|Synonym: why|Antonym: (No direct antonym for "why")|Adverb

注意事项:

"Forwhy" is considered nonstandard and informal English. It's generally best to avoid using it in formal writing or speech.  It's more common in certain dialects. Using "why" alone is always preferable in standard English.
