goel:/ˈɡoʊəl/ | n. |血亲复仇者| A person who is responsible for avenging the death of a family member, especially in traditional societies. |  Example: In some cultures, the goel was obligated to pursue the killer. 在某些文化中，血亲复仇者有义务追捕凶手。| Example:  The family relied on their goel to bring them justice. 这家人依靠他们的血亲复仇者为他们伸张正义。 | 近义词: avenger, blood avenger | 反义词: protector, defender | 词性: 名词

词源: From the Hebrew גֹּאֵל (goʾel), meaning "redeemer, kinsman-redeemer."  It originally referred to a close relative who had the duty to redeem a family member from slavery or debt, or to avenge their death.
