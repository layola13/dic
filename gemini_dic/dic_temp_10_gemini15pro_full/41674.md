correspond:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɒnd/ (UK) /ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːnd/ (US)| vi. vt.| 对应；符合；通信|To be similar or analogous; to be in agreement or conformity; to communicate by exchanging letters.|例句：The two maps correspond closely. 这两张地图非常吻合。|近义词：match, agree, correlate, communicate, write|反义词:differ, disagree, clash|verb

correspond:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɒnd/ (UK) /ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːnd/ (US)| n.| 通信；信件|A close similarity, connection, or equivalence.|例句：I received a large correspond about the matter. 我收到了很多关于这件事的信件。|近义词：letter, communication, message|反义词: None|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *correspondre*, from Latin correspondēre (“to correspond”), from com- (“together”) + respondēre (“to answer, respond”).
