nonrecitation:/ˌnɑːnˌrɛsɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 不背诵，不朗诵 |  The act of not reciting something, especially in a classroom or formal setting.  Absence of recitation. | The teacher allowed nonrecitation for students who had memorized the poem perfectly.  老师允许那些已经完全记住这首诗的学生不用背诵。 |  non-recital, omission of recitation | recitation, recital | noun
