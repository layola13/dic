declaimant:/dɪˈkleɪmənt/
n.
朗诵者；演讲者；高谈阔论者
A person who declaims; a person who speaks rhetorically or bombastically.
The declaimant captivated the audience with his powerful voice and dramatic gestures.  这位演讲者用他洪亮的声音和夸张的姿势吸引了观众。
speaker, orator, rhetorician
listener, audience
