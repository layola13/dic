outtake:/ˈaʊtteɪk/| n. |(电影、电视节目、录音等的)废弃镜头，花絮，删减片段|A scene or sequence that is filmed or recorded but not included in the final edited version of a movie, television program, or recording. |例句：The director showed us some hilarious outtakes from the movie. 导演给我们看了一些电影里滑稽的删减片段。

例句：The blooper reel was full of outtakes of the actors flubbing their lines.  这个花絮片段充满了演员说错台词的镜头。|近义词：deleted scene, blooper, cutting-room floor material |反义词: final cut, included scene |词性:noun
