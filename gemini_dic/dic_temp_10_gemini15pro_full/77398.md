gauzed:/ɡɔːzd/
adjective
纱布包着的；覆有薄纱的
covered or draped with gauze.
The nurse gauzed the wound.  护士用纱布包扎了伤口。
The windows were gauzed with curtains. 窗户上覆着薄纱窗帘。
近义词: veiled, covered
反义词: uncovered, exposed
词性: adjective

词源: From gauze (n.) + -ed (suffix forming adjectives).  Gauze itself comes from Middle French gaze, probably from Gaza, a city in Palestine known for its fine fabrics.
