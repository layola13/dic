curriculum: /kəˈrɪkjələm/ (US) /kəˈrɪkjʊləm/ (UK)| n. |课程; 全部课程|The school's curriculum includes math, science, and English.  这所学校的课程包括数学、科学和英语。|The university offers a broad curriculum in the humanities. 这所大学提供广泛的人文学科课程。|近义词: syllabus, course of study, program|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *curriculum* "a running, course," from *currere* "to run."  Originally referring to the course of a chariot race, the word later evolved to mean a course of study.
