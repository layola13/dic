patache:/pəˈtætʃ/ | n. | 快速帆船；小邮船；调度船；轻型帆船 | A small fast sailing ship, especially one used as a dispatch boat or tender. | The patache carried messages between the flagship and the smaller vessels. 小邮船在旗舰和较小的船只之间传递信息。 |  The patache delivered supplies to the island fort. 调度船向岛上的堡垒运送补给。 | synonyms: tender, dispatch boat, advice boat, pinnace, cutter | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps "flagship" as it represents the opposite in terms of size and importance) | noun
