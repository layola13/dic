babysitting:/ˈbeɪbisɪtɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbeɪbisɪtɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|临时保姆工作；照顾婴儿|The act of caring for a child or children while the parents are away.|例句：Babysitting can be a fun and rewarding job.  照顾小孩可以是一份有趣且有益的工作。|近义词：childminding, childcare|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

词源:  "baby" + "sit".  A relatively modern word, first recorded in the 1930s, formed by combining "baby" with "sit."  It reflects the common practice of having someone "sit" with and watch over a baby.
