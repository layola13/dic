fibrillar:/fɪˈbrɪlər/ /ˈfaɪbrɪlər/| adj.|纤维状的，由纤维组成的|Consisting of fibrils; resembling or arranged in fibrils.|Electron microscopy revealed a fibrillar network within the cell. 电子显微镜显示细胞内存在纤维状网络。|The protein formed a fibrillar structure.  这种蛋白质形成了纤维状结构。|近义词：fibrous, filamentous|反义词:amorphous|词性:形容词

fibril:/ˈfaɪbrɪl/ /ˈfɪbrɪl/| n.|细纤维，原纤维|A small or slender fiber; a fine thread-like structure.|Collagen fibrils provide structural support to tissues. 胶原纤维为组织提供结构支撑。|Muscle fibrils are responsible for muscle contraction. 肌原纤维负责肌肉收缩。|近义词：fiber, filament|反义词:N/A|词性:名词
