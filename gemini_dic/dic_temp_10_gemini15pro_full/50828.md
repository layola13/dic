descriptivenesses:/dɪˈskrɪptɪvnəsɪz/| n.|描述性；描写性；叙述性|The quality of being descriptive; the degree to which something is descriptive.  指描述的性质；某事物描述的程度。|The descriptivenesses of his prose brought the scene to life.  他散文中的描写使场景栩栩如生。|近义词：detail, vividness, richness, evocativeness|反义词：vagueness, generality, ambiguity|词性:noun

词源: From descriptive + -ness + -es (plural).  来自 descriptive + -ness + -es（复数）。
