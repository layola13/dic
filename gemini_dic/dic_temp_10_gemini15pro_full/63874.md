epileptoid:/ˈɛpɪˌlɛptɔɪd/| adj. n.|癫痫样的；类癫痫的；癫痫样病人|Resembling epilepsy or a seizure; relating to a personality type characterized by explosiveness and irritability.  A person with an epileptoid personality.|例句：His epileptoid fits were frequent and disturbing. 他的癫痫样发作频繁且令人不安。

例句：The doctor diagnosed him as an epileptoid. 医生诊断他是癫痫样病人。|近义词：epileptic, convulsive, seizure-like|反义词:stable, calm|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology: From epi- (upon, over) + Greek *lēptikos (seizing, grasping) + -oid (resembling).
