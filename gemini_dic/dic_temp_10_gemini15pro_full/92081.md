hyperreactive:/ˌhaɪpərriˈæktɪv/| adj.|过度反应的，过敏的|Characterized by excessive or exaggerated reactivity, responding more strongly or frequently than is usual or considered normal.|People with allergies can be hyperreactive to certain substances like pollen or dust mites. 过敏的人可能对某些物质如花粉或尘螨过度反应。|例句：The child's immune system was hyperreactive, causing frequent allergic reactions.  这个孩子的免疫系统反应过度，导致频繁的过敏反应。

例句：The horse was hyperreactive to loud noises and easily startled.  这匹马对巨大的噪音反应过度，很容易受到惊吓。|近义词：oversensitive, overresponsive|反义词：hyporeactive, unresponsive|词性：adjective

词源:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, above, beyond"
reactive (adjective): from Latin *reactivus, from react- "acted back, driven back," past participle stem of reagere "to act back, act in opposition, react," from re- "back, again" + agere "to do, drive, act"
