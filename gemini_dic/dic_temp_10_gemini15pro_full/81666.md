green-manures:/ˈɡriːn məˈnjʊərz/
noun
绿肥
Plants that are grown and then plowed into the soil to improve its quality.
Farmers often use green manures like clover and alfalfa to enrich their fields.  农民经常使用绿肥，例如三叶草和苜蓿来肥沃田地。
synonyms: cover crops, green manure crops, soil-improving crops
antonyms: chemical fertilizers
