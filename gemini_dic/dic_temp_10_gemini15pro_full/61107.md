elocutionize: /ɪˌlɒkjuːˈʃənaɪz/ /ɪˌloʊkjuːˈʃənaɪz/
verb
雄辩地讲话；演讲；作朗诵表演
to speak or read something aloud in a formal and dramatic way
He elocutionized on the importance of education. 他雄辩地阐述了教育的重要性。
She elocutionized the poem with great passion. 她充满激情地朗诵了这首诗。
近义词: declaim, orate, pronounce, deliver, recite
反义词: mumble, mutter
词性: verb
