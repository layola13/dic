parcener:/ˈpɑːrsənər/ | n. |共同继承人；联权继承人|A joint heir, especially a female heir who inherits land along with others.|例句：The three sisters were parceners of their father's estate.  这三个姐妹是她们父亲遗产的共同继承人。

例句：As parceners, they had equal rights to the property. 作为共同继承人，她们对财产拥有平等的权利。|近义词：coheir, joint heir|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "sole heir") |词性: noun
