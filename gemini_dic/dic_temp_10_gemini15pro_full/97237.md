inflectionless:/ɪnˈflɛkʃənləs/| adj.|无屈折变化的，无词形变化的|Lacking inflections; not undergoing inflection.  A word that does not change form to mark grammatical features like tense, number, or gender.|The Chinese language is largely inflectionless, relying on word order rather than changes in word form to express grammatical relationships.|近义词：uninflected|反义词: inflected|词性: adjective
