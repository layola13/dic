outbantering: /ˌaʊtˈbæntərɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的玩笑; 以玩笑胜过
Surpassing someone in bantering; defeating someone in a witty exchange.
He outbantered his opponent, leaving them speechless with his quick wit. 他以机智的玩笑胜过了对手，让他们哑口无言。
She outbantered him with a clever retort. 她用巧妙的反驳胜过了他。
近义词: outwit, outsmart, surpass
反义词: be outwitted, be outsmarted
词性: verb
