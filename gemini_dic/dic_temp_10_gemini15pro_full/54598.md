dismarch:/dɪsˈmɑːrtʃ/ | v. | 使混乱，扰乱秩序 | To throw into disorder; disarrange; spoil the order or arrangement of. | The sudden storm dismarched the troops' neat formation. 突如其来的暴风雨打乱了部队整齐的队形。|  The playful kittens dismarched the carefully arranged books on the shelf.  顽皮的小猫弄乱了书架上精心摆放的书籍。| synonyms: disarrange, disorder, disorganize, disrupt, muddle, confuse | antonyms: arrange, order, organize, systematize | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- + march (to arrange in order).  The prefix "dis-" indicates negation or reversal, so "dismarch" means to undo the order of a march or arrangement.
