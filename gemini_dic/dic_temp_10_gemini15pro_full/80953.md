graceful:/ˈɡreɪsfəl/| adj.|优雅的，优美的|Moving with beauty and elegance.  Characterized by beauty of style, form, or execution.|She moved with a graceful fluidity. 她举手投足间流露出优雅的气质。|The swan is a graceful bird. 天鹅是一种优雅的鸟。|近义词：elegant, stylish, poised, refined|反义词：clumsy, awkward, ungainly|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *gracieux*, from Late Latin *gratiōsus* (“pleasing, agreeable, graceful”), from Latin *grātia* (“grace, favor, thanks”).
