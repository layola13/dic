changqing:/tʃɑːŋˈtʃɪŋ/ | n. | 常青 | Evergreen; referring to plants that retain their green leaves throughout the year.  It can also be a place name or a person's name in Chinese Pinyin. |  Changqing District is located in Jinan. 常青区位于济南。 | 近义词: evergreen, perennial | 反义词: deciduous | 词性: noun


changqing:/tʃɑːŋˈtʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 常青的 | Evergreen; constantly green. | The changqing pine trees stood tall against the winter sky.  常青的松树高高地矗立在冬日的天空下。 | 近义词: evergreen, perennial | 反义词: deciduous | 词性: adjective


词源:  "Changqing" is a Chinese word (常青) transliterated into Pinyin.  "Chang" (常) means constant, regular, or usual, and "qing" (青) means green or blue.  Therefore, the combined meaning is "evergreen."
