idolizer:/ˈaɪdəˌlaɪzər/| n.|崇拜者，偶像化者|Someone who idolizes another person; a devoted admirer.|例句：She is a fervent idolizer of that pop star. 她是一位狂热的流行歌星的崇拜者。

例句：He became an idolizer of the famous scientist after reading his biography.  读了这位著名科学家的传记后，他成了这位科学家的崇拜者。|近义词：admirer, fan, devotee, worshipper|反义词：critic, detractor|词性:noun
