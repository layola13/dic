multiengine:/ˌmʌltiˈɛndʒɪn/| adj.|多引擎的|Having more than one engine, typically used to describe aircraft.|例句：The pilot skillfully landed the multiengine aircraft.  飞行员熟练地驾驶多引擎飞机着陆。

例句：Multiengine aircraft are preferred for long-haul flights due to increased safety and power. 长途飞行首选多引擎飞机，因为它们更安全、动力更强。|近义词：multi-engined|反义词：single-engine|词性:形容词
