overvalues:/ˌoʊvərˈvæljuːz/
verb
高估
To place too high a value on; overestimate the worth of.
The company overvalues its assets.  这家公司高估了其资产。
He tends to overvalue his own contributions to the project. 他倾向于高估自己对项目的贡献。
近义词: overestimate, overrate, inflate
反义词: undervalue, underestimate, depreciate
词性: verb

词源：
"Over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar) meaning "above, across, beyond" + "value" (from Old French valoir, from Latin valēre "be worth").  Therefore, "overvalue" literally means to assign a worth that is beyond or above the true value.
