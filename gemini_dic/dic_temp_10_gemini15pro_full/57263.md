downpayment:/ˌdaʊnˈpeɪmənt/| n.|首付，预付定金|An initial payment made when something is bought on credit. The rest of the price is usually paid in installments.|例句：They made a downpayment on a new house. 他们在新房子上支付了首付款。|近义词：deposit, prepayment|反义词: balance due|词性:名词
