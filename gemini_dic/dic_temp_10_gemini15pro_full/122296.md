mitriform:/ˈmaɪtrɪfɔːrm/
adjective
僧帽形的；主教冠形的
Shaped like a miter, a tall, pointed hat worn by bishops and other high-ranking clergy.
The mitriform petals of the flower gave it a unique appearance.  这朵花僧帽形的花瓣使它看起来很独特。

近义词:miter-shaped, conical
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "rounded" or "flat" depending on context)
