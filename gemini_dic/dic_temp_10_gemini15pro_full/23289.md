boxiest:/ˈbɒksiɪst/ | adj. | 最像盒子的；最方正的 |  Superlative adjective of "boxy."  Describes something as resembling a box in shape to the greatest degree. | That's the boxiest car I've ever seen. 那是见过的最像盒子的车。| The building's boxiest design made it look imposing.  这座建筑方方正正的设计使它看起来很壮观。 | 近义词：squarest, blockiest | 反义词：roundest, curviest | 词性: 形容词
