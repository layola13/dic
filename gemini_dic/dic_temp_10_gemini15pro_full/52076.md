dialypetalous:/ˌdaɪəliˈpɛtələs/| adj.|离瓣的，分离花瓣的|Having petals that are separate or distinct from one another, not joined together.|The dialypetalous flower of the wild rose contrasted with the fused petals of the garden variety.|近义词:polypetalous|反义词:gamopetalous, sympetalous|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Greek *dia-* "through, across, between" + *lyein* "to loosen, dissolve" + *-petalos* "having petals," from *petalon* "leaf, petal."
