acceptingly:/əkˈsɛptɪŋli/
adverb
接受地；顺从地
In a manner that shows acceptance or willingness to receive something.
She listened to his explanation acceptingly, nodding her head in agreement. 她接受地听着他的解释，点头表示同意。

He acceptingly took the gift from his grandmother. 他欣然接受了祖母的礼物。

近义词:willingly, readily, agreeably, submissively
反义词:reluctantly, unwillingly, resistingly
词性:adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From accept (v.) + -ing (suffix forming present participles and gerunds) + -ly (suffix forming adverbs).  The verb "accept" ultimately comes from the Latin "accipere," meaning "to take to oneself," from "ad-" (to) + "capere" (to take).

USAGE->
She smiled acceptingly when he offered her the flowers. = 当他向她献花时，她微笑着接受了。
He acceptingly agreed to her terms. = 他顺从地同意了她的条件。


PHRASE->
(Not commonly used in set phrases.  Generally used as a standalone adverb.)
