controvertibly:/ˌkɒntrəˈvɜːʃəbli/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈvɜːrʃəbli/
adv.
可争议地，有争议地
In a way that can be argued against; disputably.
The judge's decision was controvertibly biased.  法官的判决有争议地偏颇。
The effectiveness of the new policy is controvertibly minimal. 新政策的效果可以说是微乎其微，这点很有争议。
近义词: disputably, debatably, arguably
反义词: indisputably, undeniably, irrefutably
词性: adverb (副词)
