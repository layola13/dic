brunetteness:/bruːˈnetnəs/
noun
深褐色; 褐发状态
the quality or state of being brunette
例句：She was known for her striking brunetteness. 她以惊人的深褐色头发而闻名。

例句：Her brunetteness made her stand out in the crowd. 她的深褐色头发让她在人群中脱颖而出。

近义词：darkness, brownness
反义词：blondness, fairness
词性：n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From brunette + -ness.  Brunette comes from French, ultimately from brun, meaning "brown."
