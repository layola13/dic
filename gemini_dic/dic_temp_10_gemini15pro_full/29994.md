cavitation:/ˌkævɪˈteɪʃən/| n.|空化，空穴现象|The formation of vapour cavities in a liquid, small pockets of gas, caused by a localized reduction in pressure.|The pump suffered damage from cavitation.  这个泵由于空化作用受到了损坏。

The propeller showed signs of cavitation. 螺旋桨显示出空化的迹象。|近义词:erosion, pitting|反义词:solidification, compression|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cavitatem, accusative of cavitas (“hollowness, cavity”), from cavus (“hollow”).
