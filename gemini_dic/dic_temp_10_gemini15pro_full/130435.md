nonabridgable:/ˌnɒnəˈbrɪdʒəbl/ | adj. | 未删节的，完整的 | Not shortened or condensed; complete. |  This dictionary is a nonabridgable edition, containing every word. 这本词典是未删节版，包含了每一个单词。

The nonabridgable audiobook was a massive 24 hours long. 这套未删节的有声书长达24小时。


近义词: complete, unabridged, full, entire | 反义词: abridged, condensed, shortened | 词性: adjective


词源:  Late 16th century: from non- ‘not’ + abridge + -able.
