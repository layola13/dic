fussy:/ˈfʌsi/ (US), /ˈfʌsi/ (UK)| adj.|挑剔的，过分讲究的，难取悦的|Inclined to complain or find fault; fastidious; easily upset or irritated.| The cat is a fussy eater and only likes one brand of food. 这只猫很挑食，只喜欢一个牌子的猫粮。| She's very fussy about her clothes. 她对穿着很挑剔。|近义词：picky, finicky, particular, fastidious, demanding|反义词：easygoing, undemanding, adaptable, tolerant|词性:形容词

词源:  From earlier *fuss,* probably imitative of the sound of fretting or complaining.
