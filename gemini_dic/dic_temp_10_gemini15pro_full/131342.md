nonchastisement: /ˌnɒnˈtʃæstaɪzmənt/ (British) /ˌnɑːnˈtʃæstaɪzmənt/ (American)
|n.| 未受懲罰；免於懲罰 |Absence of punishment or reprimand; freedom from punishment.|  The child's nonchastisement for his bad behavior only encouraged him to act out more.  这个孩子因其不良行为未受懲罰，反而助长了他更放肆的行为。|Near synonyms: impunity, exemption from punishment, letting off, exoneration|Antonyms: punishment, chastisement, discipline,  reprimand|Noun
