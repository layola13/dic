overcommunicates:/ˌoʊvər kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪts/
| v. |过度沟通；交流过多|To communicate excessively, often to the point of redundancy or causing confusion.  The act of sharing too much information, or repeating information unnecessarily.|She overcommunicates her anxieties, sending dozens of emails a day. 她过度沟通她的焦虑，每天发送几十封电子邮件。|Synonyms: overshare, overexplain|Antonyms: undercommunicate, withhold information|Verb
