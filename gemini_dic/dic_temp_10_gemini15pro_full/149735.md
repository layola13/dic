palatability:/ˌpælətəˈbɪləti/| n. |适口性；美味；可口性|The palatability of the food is crucial to its success.  食物的适口性对其成功至关重要。|例句：The chef is concerned about the palatability of the new dish.  厨师担心新菜的适口性。

例句：The palatability of the dog food was tested on a panel of canine judges.  狗粮的适口性由一组犬类评委进行了测试。|近义词：tastiness, flavor, deliciousness, savoriness|反义词:unpalatability, unpleasantness, distastefulness|词性:noun

palatable:/ˈpælətəbl/| adj. |美味的；可口的；合意的|The steak was perfectly cooked and very palatable. 牛排做得恰到好处，非常可口。|例句：He found her suggestion quite palatable. 他觉得她的建议很合意。

例句：The medicine had a surprisingly palatable flavor.  这药的味道出奇地好。|近义词：tasty, delicious, agreeable, acceptable|反义词:unpalatable, unpleasant, distasteful|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin palatabilis "agreeable to the palate," from Latin palatum "palate" + -abilis "-able."
