lithotomical:/ˌlɪθəˈtɒmɪkl/ | adj. |  截石的，切石术的 | Relating to lithotomy (the surgical removal of stones, especially from the bladder or kidney). | The patient was placed in the lithotomical position for the surgery. 病人被置于截石位进行手术。 | The surgeon used a lithotomical instrument.  外科医生使用了截石器械。 | 近义词: surgical, operative | 反义词:  None | 词性: adjective

词源:
From French *lithotomique*, from Ancient Greek *λιθοτομία* (lithotomía, “cutting for stone”), from *λίθος* (líthos, “stone”) + *τέμνω* (témnō, “I cut”).
