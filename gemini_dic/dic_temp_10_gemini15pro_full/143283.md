outdoorness:/ˈaʊtˌdɔːrnəs/ /ˈaʊtˌdʊrnəs/ | n. |户外性，热爱户外活动 |The quality or state of being outdoors; a love or preference for outdoor activities.| She has a deep sense of outdoorness and loves hiking and camping. 她对户外活动有着浓厚的兴趣，热爱徒步旅行和露营。 | 近义词: love of the outdoors, outdoor spirit, outdoorsmanship | 反义词: indoorness, sedentariness | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
"outdoor" + "-ness"

"outdoor" (adjective): from "out" + "door". "Out" meaning outside, beyond, or away from. "Door" refers to a movable barrier for closing an entrance.

"-ness" (suffix): forms nouns indicating a state, quality, or condition.
