macropus: /ˈmækrəpʊs/ | n. | 大袋鼠 | A large kangaroo.  It refers to a genus of marsupials, which includes kangaroos and wallaroos. |  Macropus rufus is the largest of all kangaroos.  红大袋鼠是所有袋鼠中最大的。|  Macropus giganteus is also known as the eastern grey kangaroo.  东部灰大袋鼠也被称为大灰袋鼠。 | synonym: kangaroo, wallaroo | antonym: N/A | noun
