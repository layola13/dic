enrollee:/ɪnˌroʊˈliː/ /ɪnˌrɒlˈiː/| n. |注册者，入学者，登记人|A person who enrolls in a program, course, or institution.|Example: The enrollee received a welcome packet from the university.  这位注册者收到了大学的欢迎礼包。|Example:  New enrollees must attend an orientation session. 新入学的人必须参加一个新生指导会。|Synonym: registrant, applicant, candidate, participant|Antonym: dropout, withdrawer|noun
