nurse:/nɜːrs/ (UK) /nɜːrs/ (US)| n. v.|护士, 照顾, 养育|A person trained to care for the sick or infirm, especially in a hospital.|例句：The nurse took the patient's temperature. 护士量了病人的体温。

例句：She worked as a nurse for over 30 years. 她做了30多年的护士。

近义词：caregiver, attendant, medic|反义词: patient, doctor|词性: 名词, 动词


nurse:/nɜːrs/ (UK) /nɜːrs/ (US)| v.|照顾, 养育, 培养|To care for someone who is ill or injured.|例句：She nursed her husband back to health. 她照顾她丈夫直到他康复。

例句：He carefully nursed the small plant. 他细心照料这株小植物。


近义词：tend, care for, look after|反义词: neglect, abandon|词性: 动词


词源: From Old French *norice* "wet nurse," from Latin *nutricia*, feminine of *nutricius* "nourishing," from *nutrire* "to nourish, suckle, rear, bring up, cherish," related to *nutrix* "nurse" (from PIE root *nau-* "to flow").
