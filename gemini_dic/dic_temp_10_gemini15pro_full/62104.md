enchantingly:/ɪnˈtʃæntɪŋli/| adv.|迷人地，令人陶醉地|In a way that is enchanting; charmingly; delightfully.  It describes something done in a magical, captivating, or alluring manner.|She sang enchantingly, captivating the entire audience. 她唱得令人陶醉，迷住了所有的观众。|Synonyms: charmingly, magically, delightfully, captivatingly, alluringly|Antonyms: displeasingly, unattractively, repellently|adverb
