adaptness:/əˈdæptnɪs/| n. |适应性，适应能力|The adaptness of this species to the changing environment is remarkable.  这个物种对不断变化的环境的适应性非凡。|例句：The camel's adaptness to desert conditions is well-known. 骆驼对沙漠环境的适应能力是众所周知的。

The adaptness of the company's strategy to the market demands ensured its success. 公司战略对市场需求的适应性确保了它的成功。|近义词：adaptability, flexibility, suitability|反义词：inadaptability, inflexibility, unsuitability|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From adapt + -ness.  Adapt comes from Late Latin *adaptare, from Latin ad- + aptare "to fit," from aptus "fit, suitable."

USAGE->
The high adaptness of wolves contributes to their survival in diverse habitats. = 狼的高度适应性有助于它们在不同栖息地中生存。

Her remarkable adaptness to new situations made her a valuable asset to the team. = 她对新情况的非凡适应能力使她成为团队的宝贵财富。

PHRASE->
degree of adaptness = 适应程度
high/low adaptness = 高/低适应性
evolutionary adaptness = 进化适应性
adaptness to change = 适应变化的能力
environmental adaptness = 环境适应性
