indocibility:/ˌɪnˌdɑːsəˈbɪləti/ /ˌɪnˌdəʊsəˈbɪləti/
n.
可教性；温顺；易驯服
The quality of being easily instructed or taught; teachableness; docility.
Her indocibility made her a quick study in the new programming language.  她的可教性使她很快学会了新的编程语言。
Synonyms: teachableness, docility, malleability, tractability
Antonyms: intractability, unruliness, stubbornness, obstinacy
