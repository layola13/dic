nonsuccessful:/ˌnɒnsəkˈsɛsfʊl/ (US) /ˌnɒnsəkˈsɛsf(ə)l/ (UK)| adj.|不成功的，失败的|Not achieving success; resulting in failure.  Formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the word "successful."  The root "success" comes from the Latin "successus," meaning "advance, a good result."|The nonsuccessful attempt to climb Mount Everest left the team disappointed.|Synonyms: failed, unsuccessful, fruitless, unproductive, ineffective, vain, futile|Antonyms: successful, triumphant, victorious|adjective
