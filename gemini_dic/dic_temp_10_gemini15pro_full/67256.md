expurgate: /ˈekspərɡeɪt/ /ˈɛkspərɡeɪt/
verb
删除（不当内容）；净化
to remove matter thought to be objectionable or unsuitable from (a book or account)
The editor expurgated the offensive language from the novel.  编辑从小说中删除了冒犯性的语言。
The censor expurgated all references to sex and violence from the film. 审查员从电影中删除了所有关于性和暴力的内容。
近义词: censor, bowdlerize, sanitize, purify
反义词: insert, include, add

词性: v.

词源: From Latin *expurgatus*, past participle of expurgare (“to cleanse, purify”), from ex- (“out”) + purgare (“to cleanse”).
