elma:/ˈɛlmə/| n.|苹果 (Turkish)|"Elma" is the Turkish word for "apple". It's derived from Proto-Turkic *ālma. It has no direct English equivalent besides the translation "apple".|例句：Elma çok lezzetli.  The apple is very delicious.|近义词：apple (English equivalent)|反义词: None (Fruits generally don't have antonyms).|Noun
