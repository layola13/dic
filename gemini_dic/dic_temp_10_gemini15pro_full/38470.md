conched:/kɒntʃt/ |v. |形成贝壳状;使(某物)成贝壳状;|The fabric was conched into graceful folds. 这块布料被弄成优美的贝壳状褶皱。|近义词:shaped, molded, curved|反义词:flattened, straightened|词性:动词
词源: conch (n.) + -ed (suffix forming past participles and adjectives)  From Old French conche "shell," from Latin concha "shellfish, mussel, shell," from Greek konkhē "mussel, shell," related to konkos "shell, sea snail."
