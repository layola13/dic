dispossessory:/dɪspəˈzesəri/
adjective
剥夺占有的；驱逐的
Relating to or effecting dispossession; depriving someone of land or property.
The landlord filed a dispossessory action against the tenant for failure to pay rent.  房东因租户未付房租而提起驱逐诉讼。
The dispossessory notice gave the family just three days to vacate the premises. 驱逐通知只给了这家人三天时间搬离房屋。
synonyms: evictive, expropriative, ousting
antonyms: granting, bestowing, providing
词性: adjective
