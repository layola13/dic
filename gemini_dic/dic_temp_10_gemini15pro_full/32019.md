cheque:/tʃek/ (British English), /tʃɛk/ (American English)| n.|支票|A written order directing a bank to pay money to the person named on it.|例句：I wrote a cheque for the rent. 我写了一张房租支票。|例句：He paid by cheque. 他用支票付款。|近义词：check (American English), draft, money order|反义词:cash|名词

cheques:/tʃeks/ (British English), /tʃɛks/ (American English)| n.|支票（复数）|Plural form of "cheque".|例句：I have several cheques to deposit. 我有几张支票要存。|例句：She keeps all her cheques in a file. 她把所有的支票都存放在一个文件夹里。|近义词：checks (American English), drafts, money orders|反义词:cash|名词
