amniotic:/ˌæmniˈɒtɪk/ (US) /ˌæmniˈɑːtɪk/| adj. |羊膜的|Relating to the amnion.|Amniotic fluid cushions the fetus.|Synonym:  amniotic|Antonym: None|adjective
