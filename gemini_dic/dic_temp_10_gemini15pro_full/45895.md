cuties:/ˈkjuːtiz/| n.|小可爱们，美人儿们|A term of endearment, often used for children or attractive young people or animals, sometimes considered condescending or objectifying.|例句：Look at those cuties playing in the park! 看看那些在公园里玩耍的小可爱们！

例句：She called her kittens her little cuties. 她称她的小猫为她的小可爱。|近义词：sweethearts, darlings, honeys|反义词: brats, monsters|Noun
