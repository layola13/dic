heathenness:/ˈhiːðənnəs/| n.|异教, 野蛮状态, 未开化|The state of being a heathen; barbarism; lack of culture or civilization. |The missionary lamented the heathenness of the remote tribe.  传教士对偏远部落的异教状态感到悲痛。| The museum exhibit explored the heathenness of ancient cultures.  博物馆的展览探索了古代文化的野蛮状态。 |近义词：paganism, barbarity, savagery|反义词：civilization, Christianity, enlightenment|词性:noun

词源: From heathen + -ness.  "Heathen" comes from Old English "hæðen" (meaning "inhabitant of the heath, dweller on the uncultivated land"), implying those outside of Christian influence.
