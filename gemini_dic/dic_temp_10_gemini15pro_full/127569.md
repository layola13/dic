nectared:/ˈnɛktərd/| adj.|充满花蜜的，甜蜜的|Containing or filled with nectar; sweet like nectar.|The nectared air of the garden was intoxicating. 花园里充满花蜜的空气令人陶醉。|近义词：sweet, honeyed, fragrant|反义词：bitter, sour|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From nectar + -ed.  Nectar comes from the Greek νέκταρ (néktar), the drink of the gods, often associated with immortality. The suffix -ed forms adjectives meaning "having, characterized by, or provided with."
