mess with:/mɛs wɪð/| phrasal verb |招惹；干扰；与…发生性关系；乱动，摆弄|To interfere with someone or something, often in a harmful or annoying way.  It can also mean to become romantically or sexually involved with someone, or to handle or play with something.|Don't mess with my computer.  别乱动我的电脑。|近义词：interfere with, tamper with, provoke, bother, trifle with |反义词:leave alone, ignore|词性:短语动词

mess with:/mɛs wɪð/| phrasal verb |胡闹；瞎搞|To engage in foolish or risky behavior.|Stop messing with that firecracker!  别玩那个鞭炮了！|近义词：fool around, play around|反义词:behave, act responsibly|词性:短语动词
