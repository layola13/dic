anticonvulsant:/ˌæntiˈkənˈvʌlsənt/| n. adj.|抗惊厥药，抗惊厥的|A drug used to prevent or reduce the severity of epileptic or other convulsive seizures.|The doctor prescribed an anticonvulsant to control her seizures. 医生给她开了抗惊厥药来控制她的癫痫发作。|近义词：antiepileptic, antiseizure|反义词:convulsant, proconvulsant|词性:名词，形容词

anticonvulsant:/ˌæntiˈkənˈvʌlsənt/| adj.|抗惊厥的|Preventing or reducing the severity of epileptic or other convulsive seizures.|The anticonvulsant properties of the medication helped to stabilize the patient. 这种药物的抗惊厥特性帮助稳定了病人的病情。|近义词：antiepileptic, antiseizure|反义词:convulsant, proconvulsant|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + convulsant.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to". "Convulsant" comes from the Latin "convulsus," the past participle of "convellere," meaning "to pull violently,"  from "com-" (together, with) + "vellere" (to pluck, tear).
