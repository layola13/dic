overstraitly:/ˌoʊvərˈstreɪtli/
| adv. | 过于严格地，过分拘谨地 | Too strictly or rigorously; excessively tightly. | He interpreted the rules overstraitly, causing unnecessary difficulties. 他对规则的解释过于严格，造成了不必要的困难。 |  She laced her corset overstraitly, making it difficult to breathe. 她把紧身胸衣系得太紧，呼吸困难。 | 近义词: rigidly, severely, strictly, rigorously | 反义词: leniently, loosely, flexibly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From Middle English *overstraitly, from over- + straitly (from Old French estreit, from Latin strictus).
