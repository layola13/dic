memorialised: /məˈmɔːriəlaɪzd/ /məˈmɔːriəˌlaɪzd/
verb
纪念，使永垂不朽
To preserve the memory of someone or something, especially by creating a monument or a work of art.
The war dead are memorialised by this monument.  这座纪念碑是为了纪念战争死难者。
The events of that day were memorialised in a poem. 那一天的事件在一首诗中被纪念。
Synonyms: commemorate, remember, celebrate, immortalize
Antonyms: forget, ignore, neglect
词性: 动词
