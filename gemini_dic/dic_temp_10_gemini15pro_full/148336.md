oversparing:/ˌoʊvərˈspɛərɪŋ/
adjective
过度节俭的；过分吝啬的
excessively frugal; too unwilling to spend money.
He was oversparing with praise for his employees, rarely offering any encouragement.  他对员工的赞扬过度吝啬，很少给予鼓励。
They were oversparing with the food, fearing they would run out before the week was over.  他们对食物过度节俭，担心这周结束前就会吃完。
近义词: parsimonious, stingy, miserly, frugal
反义词: generous, extravagant, lavish, prodigal
词性: adjective
