occlude:/əˈkluːd/| v.| 闭塞，阻塞，遮挡 |To stop, close, or obstruct an opening, passage, or vessel.|The artery was occluded by a blood clot. 这条动脉被血块阻塞了。|Synonyms: block, obstruct, close, clog |Antonyms: open, clear, unblock|verb

occluded:/əˈkluːdɪd/| adj.| 闭塞的，阻塞的 |Closed or blocked off.|The dentist checked for occluded arteries. 牙医检查了阻塞的动脉。|Synonyms: blocked, obstructed, closed, clogged |Antonyms: open, clear, unblocked|adjective
