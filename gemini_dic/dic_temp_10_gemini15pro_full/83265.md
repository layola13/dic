gymnopaedic: /ˌdʒɪmnəˈpiːdɪk/ | adj. n. |  裸体的；裸舞的；体操的；《裸体舞曲》（Gymnopédies）|  Relating to gymnastic exercises performed naked, or a style of slow, simple piano music by Erik Satie reminiscent of ancient Greek dances.  Derived from Greek *gumnos* (naked) + *paideia* (education, training of boys). |  Satie's Gymnopédies are hauntingly beautiful.  萨蒂的《裸体舞曲》萦绕于心，优美动听。 | The dancers performed a gymnopaedic ritual. 舞者们表演了一种裸体仪式。 |  Synonyms:  nude, bare, stark, simple (for the music),  | Antonyms: clothed, covered, ornate, complex (for the music) | adjective, noun
