attribut:/əˈtrɪbjuːt/ (US), /ˈætrɪbjuːt/ (UK)| n. |属性，特性，特征；标志，象征|A quality, characteristic, or feature that is considered to belong to or be typical of someone or something.|The golden lion is the attribut of royalty. 金狮是皇室的象征。|synonyms: characteristic, trait, quality, property, feature, aspect, mark, sign|antonyms:  lack, absence|noun

attribut:/əˈtrɪbjuːt/ (US), /ˈætrɪbjuːt/ (UK)| vt. |把…归因于，把…归属于|To regard something as being caused by someone or something.|She attributs her success to hard work and a little luck. 她把自己的成功归因于努力工作和一点运气。|synonyms: ascribe, assign, credit, impute, associate|antonyms: disassociate, deny|verb
