brocade:/brəˈkeɪd/ /broʊˈkeɪd/| n. adj.|锦缎，织锦；|A rich fabric woven with a raised pattern, typically with gold or silver thread.|The queen wore a robe of gold brocade. 女王穿着一件金锦缎长袍。|近义词：tapestry, damask, embroidery|反义词:plain cloth, muslin|noun
brocade:/brəˈkeɪd/ /broʊˈkeɪd/| adj.|织锦的，锦缎制的|Made of or resembling brocade.|She wore a brocade dress to the wedding. 她穿着一件织锦连衣裙去参加婚礼。|近义词：embroidered, figured, tapestry-like|反义词:plain, simple|adjective
