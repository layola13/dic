haily:/ˈheɪli/| adv. |冰雹般地，像下冰雹一样|Falling thickly or heavily like hail. |The rain came down haily. 雨像冰雹一样倾盆而下。|近义词:torrentially, heavily|反义词:lightly, sparsely|词性:副词

haily:/ˈheɪli/| n. |冰雹 (罕见用法)|Hailstones; a shower of hail. |There was a sudden haily and the crops were damaged.  突然下了一阵冰雹，庄稼受到了损害。|近义词:hail, hailstorm|反义词:sunshine|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
