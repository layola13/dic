come-yourself:/kʌm jɔːrˈsɛlf/ | adj.|自己动手做的，自助的|A come-yourself activity or service is one that you do yourself rather than having someone do it for you. |Example: We had a come-yourself barbecue. 我们举办了一个自助烧烤。|Example: It's a come-yourself car wash. 这是一个自助洗车场。|Synonym: self-service, DIY |Antonym: full-service|adjective
