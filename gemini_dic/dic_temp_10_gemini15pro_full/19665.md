bilaterality:/ˌbaɪˌlætəˈræləti/
n.
双边性，双侧性
The state of having two sides, or involving two parties.
例句：The bilaterality of the agreement ensured both countries benefited. 该协议的双边性确保了两国都能受益。

例句：Bilaterality is a key principle in international diplomacy. 双边性是国际外交中的一个关键原则。


近义词：two-sidedness, reciprocity, mutuality
反义词：unilaterality, one-sidedness
词性：noun
