bartering:/ˈbɑːrtərɪŋ/
n.|物物交换|The exchange of goods or services for other goods or services without using money.|Example: Bartering was common before the widespread use of money.  物物交换在货币广泛使用之前很常见。|Synonyms: trading, exchanging, swapping|Antonyms: purchasing, buying, selling|noun

barter:/ˈbɑːrtər/
v.|物物交换|To exchange goods or services for other goods or services without using money.|Example: They bartered their surplus grain for tools. 他们用剩余的谷物交换工具。|Synonyms: trade, exchange, swap|Antonyms: purchase, buy, sell|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *barater* (to cheat, haggle), of uncertain origin.
