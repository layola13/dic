electromagnetism:/ɪˌlektrəˈmæɡnəˌtɪzəm/ | n. |电磁学；电磁力|The study of the combined phenomena of electricity and magnetism, and the way they interact.  The force itself resulting from the interaction of electricity and magnetism. |Electromagnetism is a fundamental force in nature. 电磁学是自然界中的一种基本力。| The train uses electromagnetism to levitate above the track.  这列火车利用电磁力悬浮在轨道上方。 | synonyms:  electromagnetic force, Lorentz force | antonyms:  N/A | noun
