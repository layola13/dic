lono:/ˈlonoʊ/
n.
god, idol
A Polynesian god, especially of agriculture and peace.
Ancient Hawaiians prayed to Lono for bountiful harvests.  古代夏威夷人向罗诺祈祷丰收。
deity, divinity
false god
noun
