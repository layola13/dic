beltfeed:/ˈbeltfiːd/
noun
输弹带，弹链供弹;输送带供给
A mechanism that uses a continuous belt to feed ammunition into a firearm.
The machine gun's beltfeed jammed during the firefight. 机枪的输弹带在交火中卡住了。
belt-fed mechanism, ammunition belt, cartridge belt
manual feed
