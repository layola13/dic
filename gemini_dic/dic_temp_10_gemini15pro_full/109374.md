lenslet: /ˈlɛnzlɪt/ | n. | 小透镜 | A small lens, typically one of an array.  A lenslet array is often used in integral imaging or other optical applications. |  Each lenslet in the array focuses light onto a small portion of the sensor. 每个小透镜都将光线聚焦到传感器的一小部分。 |  The lenslet array helps to create a 3D image. 小透镜阵列有助于创建 3D 图像。 |  microlens | N/A | noun
