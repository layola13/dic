epee:/ˈeɪpeɪ/ | n. | 重剑 | A fencing sword with a bowl-shaped guard and a rigid blade, heavier than a foil. |  The epee is the heaviest of the three weapons used in fencing.  重剑是击剑运动中使用的三种武器中最重的。 | Example:  She lunged forward with her epee. 她用重剑向前刺去。

Example: He parried the attack with his epee. 他用重剑挡开了攻击。 | 近义词: sword (广义上的剑), foil (花剑), sabre (佩剑) | 反义词:  None (没有直接的反义词) | 词性: noun
