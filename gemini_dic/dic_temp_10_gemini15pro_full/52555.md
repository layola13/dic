diffraction:/dɪˈfrækʃən/ |n.| 衍射 |The bending of waves, such as light or sound, as they pass around an obstacle or through a narrow opening.|  The diffraction of light through a prism creates a rainbow. 光通过棱镜的衍射产生了彩虹。| synonyms: scattering, dispersion | antonyms: reflection, refraction | noun
