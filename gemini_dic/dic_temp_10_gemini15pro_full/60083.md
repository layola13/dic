eelbuck:/ˈiːlbʌk/ | n. | 麋鹿（主要指欧洲的麋鹿）| A large deer with palmate antlers, found in northern Europe and Asia. |  The eelbuck's broad antlers are impressive. 麋鹿宽阔的鹿角令人印象深刻。

The eelbuck roamed freely through the forest. 麋鹿在森林里自由漫步。 | 近义词：elk, moose (North American) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *elbuk, *elbuckes, from Old English eloboc, from elh + bucca ("male deer, buck").  The word is now archaic or dialectal and has been largely replaced by "elk."
