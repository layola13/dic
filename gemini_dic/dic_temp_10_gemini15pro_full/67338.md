extendable:/ɪkˈstendəbl/| adj.|可延长的，可扩展的|Able to be extended or made longer or wider.|This table has extendable legs, so it can accommodate more people. 这张桌子有可伸缩的桌腿，所以可以容纳更多的人。|近义词：expandable, stretchable, lengthenable|反义词：fixed, non-extendable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From extend + -able.  "extend" comes from the Latin "extendere," meaning "to stretch out." "-able" is a suffix indicating capability or possibility.
