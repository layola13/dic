crabbedness's:/ˈkræbɪdnəsɪz/
n.
坏脾气，乖戾，暴躁
The state or quality of being crabbed; characterized by ill temper, irritability, and a sour disposition.
His crabbedness's made him difficult to work with. 他的坏脾气让他很难共事。
近义词：grumpiness, irritability, surliness, cantankerousness
反义词：good humor, affability, pleasantness
词性：名词

crabbed:/ˈkræbɪd/
adj.
暴躁的，易怒的，乖戾的
(of handwriting) cramped and illegible; (of style) obscure and difficult; bad-tempered and irritable.
The old man's crabbed handwriting was difficult to decipher.  这位老人的潦草笔迹难以辨认。
近义词：grumpy, irritable, surly, cantankerous
反义词：good-humored, affable, pleasant
词性：形容词

-ness:/nəs/
suffix
构成名词，表示状态，性质，程度
Forms nouns chiefly from adjectives, meaning ‘state, quality, or degree of being (adj.)’.
Happiness is a state of well-being. 幸福是一种幸福的状态。
词性：后缀


词源：crabbedness's 源于形容词 crabbed，crabbed 源于 crab（螃蟹），最初指螃蟹横行霸道的动作，后来引申为脾气暴躁、乖戾。-ness 是一个名词后缀，表示状态或性质。's 是所有格后缀。
