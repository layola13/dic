impermanence:/ɪmˈpɜːrmənəns/| n. |无常，短暂，变幻无常|The state or fact of not lasting forever; transience.|例句：The impermanence of life is a constant reminder to cherish every moment.  人生的无常时刻提醒我们要珍惜每一刻。

例句：He accepted the impermanence of his feelings. 他接受了自己情感的无常。|近义词：transience, ephemerality, mutability, instability|反义词：permanence, eternity, stability, immutability|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *inpermanence*, from Old French, from in- (not) + permanence. Equivalent to in- +‎ permanence.
