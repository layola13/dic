bolete:/boʊˈliːt/ /ˈbɒliːt/| n. |牛肝菌；伞菌|A fleshy, edible fungus with pores rather than gills on the underside of the cap.|例句：I found a beautiful bolete while hiking in the woods. 我在树林里远足时发现了一个漂亮的牛肝菌。

例句：This type of bolete is considered a delicacy. 这种牛肝菌被认为是一种美味佳肴。|近义词：mushroom, fungus, toadstool|反义词:|noun
