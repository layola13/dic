chain-smokers:/ˈtʃeɪn ˌsmoʊkərz/| n.|烟鬼，连续抽烟的人|A person who smokes cigarettes one after another.|例句：He was a chain-smoker and his clothes always smelled of smoke. 他是个烟鬼，衣服上总是有一股烟味。

例句：The chain-smokers huddled outside the building during breaks. 那些烟鬼在休息时间都挤在楼外抽烟。|近义词：heavy smoker, inveterate smoker|反义词:non-smoker|词性:noun
