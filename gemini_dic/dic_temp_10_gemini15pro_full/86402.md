hedonism:/ˈhiːdəˌnɪzəm/ | n. |享乐主义，快乐主义 | The pursuit of pleasure; sensual self-indulgence. |  He embraced a life of hedonism, indulging in every imaginable pleasure. 他过着享乐主义的生活，沉溺于各种可以想象的快乐之中。|  例句：The hedonism of the rich and famous is often criticized.  富人和名人的享乐主义经常受到批评。| 近义词：pleasure-seeking, indulgence, sensuality, epicureanism | 反义词：asceticism, self-denial, abstinence | 词性: noun
