ductilely:/ˈdʌktaɪlli/ /ˈdʌktɪlli/ | adv. | 延展地；柔软地；易变形地 |  In a ductile manner; capable of being drawn out into wire or threads; easily molded or shaped. |  The metal was ductilely drawn into a thin wire.  这种金属被延展地拉成细丝。| 近义词：pliably, flexibly, malleably | 反义词：rigidly, inflexibly, brittlely | 词性: 副词

词源: From the Latin word "ductilis," meaning "that can be led or drawn."  源自拉丁语 "ductilis"，意思是“可以被引导或拉伸的”。
