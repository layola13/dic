allowance:/əˈlaʊəns/| n.|折扣，减价|A reduction in the price of something.|They gave us an allowance of 10% on the price. 他们在价格上给了我们10%的折扣。 |Synonyms: discount, deduction, rebate|Antonyms: increase, surcharge|noun
