electioneerer:/ɪˌlɛkʃənˈɪərər/
noun
竞选者；拉选票的人
Someone who works to get a person elected, often using questionable tactics.
He's known as a ruthless electioneerer, willing to do anything to win. 他是一个以不择手段著称的竞选者，为了获胜什么都愿意做。
Synonyms: campaigner, canvasser, political operative
Antonyms: non-partisan, impartial observer
