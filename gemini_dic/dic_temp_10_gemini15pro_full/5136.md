allegro:/əˈlɛɡroʊ/ (US), /əˈleɡrəʊ/ (UK)| adj. adv. n.|快的，轻快的，活泼的；快速地，轻快地；快板|Characterized by a brisk tempo, typically between 120 and 168 beats per minute.  Used in music to indicate a fast and lively tempo.|The symphony's final movement was an exciting allegro. 交响曲的最后乐章是一段激动人心的快板。|Synonyms: brisk, lively, rapid, swift, quick, fast|Antonyms: slow, adagio, lento|adjective, adverb, noun
