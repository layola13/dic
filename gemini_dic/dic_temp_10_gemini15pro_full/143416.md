outflash:/ˈaʊtflæʃ/| v. |突然闪耀，爆发，胜过|To shine or burst forth suddenly and intensely; to surpass or outshine.|The lightning outflashed the city lights. 闪电比城市灯光更加耀眼。|近义词：outshine, outdazzle, surpass, excel|反义词:dim, fade|词性:动词

词源:  "out-" (超过) + "flash" (闪光)
