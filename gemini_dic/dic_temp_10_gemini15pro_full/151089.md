parasail:/ˈpærəseɪl/| n. vi. |滑翔伞，帆伞|A parachute designed to be towed by a motorboat or other vehicle, providing an experience similar to hang gliding.| We went parasailing in Cancun. 我们在坎昆玩了帆伞。|近义词：parachute, kite|反义词:|n. vi.


parasail:/ˈpærəseɪl/| vi. |玩帆伞，滑翔伞运动|To engage in the activity of parasailing.|She wanted to parasail, but I was too scared. 她想去玩帆伞，但我太害怕了。|近义词:|反义词:|vi.


ETYMOLOGY->
From para- (beside, alongside) + sail.  The activity involves being lifted into the air by a parachute-like sail while being towed alongside a boat.
