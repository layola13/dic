ampliative:/ˈæmpliətɪv/
adjective
扩展的，放大的
extending; enlarging; increasing scope by inference
The ampliative nature of inductive reasoning allows us to draw conclusions about the future based on past observations. 归纳推理的扩展性质使我们能够根据过去的观察得出关于未来的结论。
近义词: expansive, extending, enlarging, augmentative
反义词: restrictive, limiting, reductive
词性: adjective
