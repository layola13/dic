fundus:/ˈfʌndəs/| n. |底，底部；基底；眼底|The part of a hollow organ (such as the uterus or the stomach) that is farthest from the opening.|The doctor examined the fundus of the eye. 医生检查了眼底。|近义词：base, bottom, floor|反义词：apex, top|noun


fundus:/ˈfʌndəs/| n. |眼底|The interior surface of the eye opposite the pupil and lens, including the retina, optic disc, macula, and fovea.|During the eye exam, the ophthalmologist carefully examined the fundus. 在眼科检查中，眼科医生仔细检查了眼底。|近义词：eyeground|反义词：None|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin fundus "bottom, foundation, piece of land," related to fundare "to found, lay the bottom."
