fruiteress:/ˈfruːtərəs/| n.|女水果商；女果农|A woman who sells fruit; a female fruit grower.|例句：The fruiteress arranged her colorful display of apples, oranges, and bananas.  女水果商摆放着她五颜六色的苹果、橙子和香蕉。

例句：The fruiteress at the market had the freshest peaches I've ever tasted.  市场上的女水果商的桃子是我吃过最新鲜的。|近义词：fruit seller, greengrocer (if selling vegetables too)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps customer)|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From fruit + -er (agent suffix) + -ess (feminine suffix).  The "-ess" suffix denotes a female person performing the role. The word has become less common in modern usage, often replaced by the gender-neutral "fruit seller."
