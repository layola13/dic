obedient:/əˈbiːdiənt/| adj.|顺从的，服从的|Willing to obey; complying with or submissive to authority.|The obedient dog sat when told to.|近义词:compliant, submissive, docile, dutiful|反义词:disobedient, rebellious, defiant|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *obediēns*, present participle of *obēdire* (“to obey, be subject to, listen to, give ear to”), from *ob-* (“to, towards, against”) + *audiō* (“I hear, listen to”).
