antireflectively:/ˌæntiɹɪˈflɛktɪvli/
| adv. |抗反射地|In a manner that reduces or eliminates reflection.|The lens was coated antireflectively to minimize glare.  镜头经过抗反射涂层处理，以最大限度地减少眩光。|近义词:nonreflectively|反义词:reflectively|词性:副词

词源:
anti- (prefix): against, opposite 反，对抗
reflect (verb): throw back light, heat, or sound  反射光、热或声音
-ive (suffix): forming adjectives  构成形容词
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs  构成副词
