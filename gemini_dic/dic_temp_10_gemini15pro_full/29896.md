|n.| 烧灼；烙术|The act or process of cauterizing.|The cauterization of the wound helped prevent infection. 烧灼伤口有助于防止感染。|
|近义词: searing, burning|反义词: healing|词性: 名词|


cauterize:/ˈkɔːtəraɪz/
|v.| 烧灼；烙|To burn (tissue) with a hot iron, electric current, or caustic substance, typically to stop bleeding or prevent infection.|The surgeon cauterized the blood vessel.  外科医生烧灼了血管。|
|近义词: sear, burn|反义词: heal|词性: 动词|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cauterizātiōn- (stem of cauterizātiō), from Latin cauterizātus (past participle of cauterizāre 'to cauterize'), from Greek kautēriāzein 'to brand with a hot iron', from kautḗrion 'branding iron', from kaiein 'to burn'.
