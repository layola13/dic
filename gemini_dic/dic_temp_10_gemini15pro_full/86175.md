heartthrob:/ˈhɑːrtθrɒb (UK), ˈhɑːrtθrɑːb (US)/
n.|万人迷，令人心动的人|A person, typically a celebrity, who is widely adored, especially by young people, for their attractiveness.|例句：He became a teenage heartthrob after starring in the hit movie. 他主演了这部热门电影后成了青少年偶像。|近义词：idol, crush, darling|反义词: nobody, unknown|词性:名词
Etymology: heart (Old English heorte) + throb (Old English þrobban, meaning "to swell, pulsate").  The word evokes the image of a heart beating faster due to romantic excitement.
