omizu: /oʊˈmiːzuː/ | n. | 御水, (Japanese) honorable water, usually used in tea ceremony |  O-mizu is essential for preparing matcha.  御水对于准备抹茶至关重要。| 近义词: water (general term) | 反义词: None (O-mizu is a specific type of water, not a quality) | Noun

Etymology:
From Japanese 御 (o, honorific prefix) + 水 (mizu, water).
