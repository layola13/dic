cheviot:/ˈʃɛviət/ /ˈtʃiːviət/| n. |舍维奥特羊，舍维奥特花呢|A breed of sheep originating in the Cheviot Hills, known for their hardiness and the quality of their wool. Also, a type of twill fabric, originally made from Cheviot wool, that is durable and slightly rough. |A herd of Cheviot sheep grazed on the hillside. 一群舍维奥特羊在山坡上吃草。|synonyms: tweed, twill |antonyms: None (lacks a direct opposite)|noun


cheviot:/ˈʃɛviət/ /ˈtʃiːviət/| adj. |舍维奥特的|Relating to or made from the wool of Cheviot sheep. |She wore a cheviot suit. 她穿着一套舍维奥特羊毛套装。|synonyms: woolen, tweed |antonyms: None (lacks a direct opposite)|adjective
