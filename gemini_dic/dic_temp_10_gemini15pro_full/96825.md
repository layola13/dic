inescutcheon:/ɪnˈɛskʌtʃən/ | n. |小纹章，内盾 |A small shield within a larger one, often bearing the arms of a wife or heiress. | The inescutcheon displayed the family crest of his mother. 小纹章展示了他母亲的家族徽章。| Synonym:  small shield, inner shield | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "main shield" or "field" in heraldry) | noun
