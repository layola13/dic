motionable:/ˈmoʊʃənəbəl/
adjective
可控告的，可起诉的
Relating to an act or omission that provides legally sufficient grounds for a lawsuit.  A claim is actionable if recognition of the claim by a court would afford relief to the claimant.
The lawyer determined that the company's actions were actionable.  律师判定该公司的行为是可以起诉的。

The offensive remarks were deemed actionable defamation. 这些攻击性的言论被认为是可起诉的诽谤。

近义词:suable, justiciable
反义词:non-actionable, unjusticiable
词性:adjective
