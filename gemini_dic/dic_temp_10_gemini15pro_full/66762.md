exospore:/ˈɛksoʊspɔːr/| n. |外生孢子|A spore formed by budding from the tip or side of another spore.  It is a type of asexual reproduction seen in certain fungi and algae. |The exospores of this fungus are easily dispersed by wind.  这种真菌的外生孢子很容易被风传播。|近义词:spore, conidium (in some cases)|反义词:endospore|词性:noun
