incompliant:/ˌɪnkəmˈplaɪənt/ | adj. | 不服从的，不顺从的；不配合的 | Not compliant; failing or refusing to comply. |  The incompliant child refused to eat his vegetables.  不服从的孩子拒绝吃蔬菜。 | synonyms: disobedient, noncompliant, resistant, defiant, uncooperative, recalcitrant | antonyms: compliant, obedient, cooperative, submissive, amenable | word type: adjective

incompliant:/ˌɪnkəmˈplaɪənt/ | adj. | 不符合的，不达标的 | Not meeting official requirements or specifications. | The incompliant software was rejected by the quality control team. 不符合标准的软件被质量控制团队拒绝了。 | synonyms: non-compliant, substandard, faulty, defective, flawed | antonyms: compliant, standard, satisfactory, acceptable | word type: adjective
