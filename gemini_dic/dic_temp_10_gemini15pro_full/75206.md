frog:/frɒɡ/ (UK), /frɑːɡ/ (US)| n.|青蛙|A small, tailless amphibian with smooth skin, long hind legs for jumping, and webbed feet, typically living near water.|The frog leaped into the pond. 青蛙跳进了池塘。|近义词：toad, amphibian|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *frogga, from Proto-Germanic *frakaz.  Related to Old Norse froskr, Dutch vrosch, German Frosch. Ultimately imitative of the frog's croaking sound.
