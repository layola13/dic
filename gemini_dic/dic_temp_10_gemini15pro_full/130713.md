nonalignment:/ˌnɒnəˈlaɪnmənt/ | n. |不结盟，不结盟运动|The state of not being formally allied with any other nation, especially with either of the major power blocs.|例句：India's policy of nonalignment during the Cold War was a delicate balancing act. 冷战期间，印度的不结盟政策是一项微妙的平衡之举。

例句：Many newly independent nations chose a path of nonalignment. 许多新独立的国家选择了不结盟的道路。|近义词：neutrality, non-involvement|反义词：alignment, alliance|词性: 名词
