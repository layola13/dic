czarish:/ˈzɑːrɪʃ/| adj.|沙皇似的；专横的；傲慢的|Resembling or characteristic of a czar; domineering; arrogant.|His czarish demeanor alienated many of his colleagues.  他专横的态度疏远了许多同事。|The CEO's czarish pronouncements made everyone uncomfortable. 首席执行官沙皇般的声明让每个人都感到不舒服。|近义词：autocratic, dictatorial, imperious, tyrannical|反义词：democratic, egalitarian|词性:形容词
词源: From czar + -ish.  Ultimately from Latin Caesar.
