genuflectors:/ˈdʒɛnjʊˌflɛktərz/
n.
跪拜者，屈膝礼者
People who genuflect; those who perform the act of bending the knee as a sign of reverence or respect.
The genuflectors bowed their heads in prayer. 跪拜者低头祈祷。
The genuflectors lined up to receive the blessing. 跪拜者排队接受祝福。
Synonyms: worshippers, supplicants
Antonyms: dissenters, rebels
noun
