endophragmal:/ˌɛndoʊˈfræɡməl/
adjective
内phragmal的，phragmal内的
Located or occurring within a phragma (a partition or dividing wall, especially in a fungal fruiting body or a snail shell).
The endophragmal tissue plays a crucial role in spore dispersal. 内phragmal组织在孢子传播中起着至关重要的作用。

Antonyms: exophragmal (less common)
