hauan:/ˈhaʊən/| n.| 豪安 (一种夏威夷传统地炉) |A traditional Hawaiian underground oven.  It is a pit dug in the ground, lined with heated rocks, and used to cook food.|The pig was cooked in the hauan for hours.  这只猪在地炉里烤了好几个小时。|近义词:imu (another type of Hawaiian underground oven), earth oven|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "modern oven")|词性:noun
