counterponderation:/ˌkaʊntərˌpɑːndəˈreɪʃn/
| n. |平衡；抵消；抗衡力 | the act of counterbalancing something; counterpoise | The counterponderation of power between the two countries maintained peace. 两国之间的力量抗衡维持了和平。 | 近义词: counterbalance, offset, compensation, neutralization | 反义词: imbalance, dominance |词性:名词

词源:
From Late Latin *counterponderatus, past participle of counterponderare, from counter- + ponderare (“to weigh”), from pondus (“weight”).
