nonharmoniousness:/ˌnɒnhɑːrˈməʊniəsnəs/ |n.|不和谐，不协调|Lack of harmony; discordance.  The state of being not harmonious. It refers to a lack of agreement, compatibility, or congruity.|The nonharmoniousness of the colors clashed and created a jarring effect.  颜色不协调，产生了刺耳的效果。|Near synonyms: discord, dissonance, disharmony, incongruity, conflict, clash, friction|Antonyms: harmony, concord, agreement, consonance, unity, compatibility|noun
