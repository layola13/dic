flexural:/ˈflekʃərəl/| adj.|弯曲的，挠曲的|Relating to bending or flexing.  Characterized by or tending to flex or bend.|The bridge's flexural strength was tested under heavy load. 这座桥的抗弯强度在重载下进行了测试。|The flexural rigidity of the beam is crucial for its stability. 梁的抗弯刚度对其稳定性至关重要。|Synonyms: bending, flexible, pliable|Antonyms: rigid, inflexible, stiff|adjective
