mellowly:/ˈmeloʊli/ | adv. | 温柔地；成熟地；甜美地 | In a mellow, soft, or gentle manner; sweetly and richly; genially. | The sun set mellowly over the hills. 夕阳温柔地落在山上。 | 近义词: softly, gently, sweetly, calmly | 反义词: harshly, stridently, roughly | 词性: 副词

词源: From mellow + -ly.  "Mellow" itself comes from Old English *mearu, meaning "soft, tender, ripe."
