pachydermal:/ˌpækɪˈdɜːrməl/| adj.| 厚皮动物的；迟钝的；感觉迟钝的| Relating to pachyderms (thick-skinned mammals, traditionally including elephants, rhinoceroses, hippopotamuses, etc.), or having thick skin; insensitive or unfeeling. | Elephants and rhinoceroses are pachydermal animals. 大象和犀牛是厚皮动物。|Synonyms: thick-skinned, insensitive, callous, impassive|Antonyms: thin-skinned, sensitive, compassionate, empathetic|adjective
pachyderm:/ˈpækɪdɜːrm/| n.| 厚皮动物| A very large mammal with thick skin, such as an elephant, rhinoceros, or hippopotamus. | The zoo's pachyderm house features elephants and rhinoceroses.  这家动物园的厚皮动物馆里有象和犀牛。|Synonyms: elephant, rhinoceros, hippopotamus|Antonyms: N/A|noun
