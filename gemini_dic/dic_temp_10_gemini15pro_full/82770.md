guillotine:/ˈɡɪlətiːn/ | n. | 断头台；切纸机；截止时间 | A machine with a heavy blade sliding vertically in grooves, used for beheading people. Also, a similar machine used for cutting paper. Can also refer to a deadline. | The guillotine was a symbol of the French Revolution. 断头台是法国大革命的象征。 | synonyms: beheader, executioner (for the execution device), cutter (for paper), deadline (figurative) | antonyms: reprieve, pardon (for the execution device) | noun


guillotine:/ˈɡɪlətiːn/ | vt. | 用断头台处死；用切纸机裁切 | To behead someone with a guillotine or to cut something with a guillotine-like cutter. | The newspapers were guillotined into neat stacks. 报纸被切纸机裁切成整齐的叠。 | synonyms: behead, decapitate, execute (for execution), trim, cut (for paper) | antonyms:  resurrect, revive (for execution) | verb
