categorized:/ˈkætɪɡəˌraɪzd/ |v. |分类的，归类的| Divided into categories or classes. |The books were categorized by genre. 这些书按类型分类。| classified, grouped, sorted |uncategorized, miscellaneous|Verb


Etymology->
From category + -ize. Ultimately from the Greek word κατηγορία (katēgoria).
