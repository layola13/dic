casks:/kæsks/
n.
木桶；酒桶
A large container, typically made of wood, for storing liquids, especially alcoholic drinks.
The casks of wine were stored in the cellar. 酒桶存放在地窖里。
barrels, tuns, vats
bottles, cans
noun
