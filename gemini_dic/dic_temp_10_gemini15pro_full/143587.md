outhunted:/ˌaʊtˈhʌntɪd/
verb
胜过狩猎;猎取更多
to hunt more successfully than someone else.
例句：He outhunted his companions, bringing back three deer. 他比同伴猎取的更多，带回了三只鹿。

例句：She outhunted all the other competitors in the annual hunting competition.  她在年度狩猎比赛中胜过所有其他参赛者。


近义词：outdo, surpass, excel
反义词：lose to, be defeated by
词性：verb
