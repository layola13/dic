fiscal:/ˈfɪskəl/| adj.|财政的，国库的，会计年度的|Relating to government revenue, especially taxes.|The government's fiscal year starts in April. 政府的财政年度从四月开始。|近义词：financial, budgetary, monetary|反义词: None|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "fiscalis" (relating to the treasury), from "fiscus" (treasury, purse).
