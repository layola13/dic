concealedly:/kənˈsiːlɪdli/
| adv. |秘密地，隐蔽地|in a concealed manner; secretly; not openly.  Hidden from sight or knowledge; kept secret.|He concealedly slipped the note into her hand. 他偷偷地把纸条塞到她手里。|近义词：secretly, furtively, covertly, stealthily|反义词：openly, publicly, visibly|词性:副词

conceal:/kənˈsiːl/
| vt. |隐藏，隐瞒|To keep from sight; hide.|He tried to conceal his disappointment. 他试图掩饰他的失望。|近义词：hide, disguise, mask|反义词：reveal, expose, show|词性:动词

词源:
From Late Middle English *concelen, from Old French conceler, from Latin concēlāre (“to hide, conceal”), from com- + cēlāre (“to hide”).
