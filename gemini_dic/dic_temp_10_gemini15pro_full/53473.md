disarrange:/ˌdɪsəˈreɪndʒ/
verb
扰乱，弄乱
To disturb the arrangement or order of; throw into disorder.
The wind disarranged her hair. 风吹乱了她的头发。
The earthquake disarranged the furniture in the house. 地震弄乱了房子里的家具。
synonyms: disorder, disorganize, disturb, mess up, muddle, confuse
antonyms: arrange, organize, order, tidy
词性: 动词
