gride:/ɡraɪd/| v.|摩擦，发出刺耳声；折磨，使痛苦|To grind harshly; to grate; to irritate.  It means to cause someone or something to experience something unpleasant or painful.|The sound of the rusty hinges grided on my nerves. 生锈的铰链发出的声音刺激着我的神经。|近义词：grate, rasp, grind, irritate, vex|反义词：soothe, comfort|verb

gride:/ɡraɪd/| n.|刺耳的声音；摩擦|A harsh, grating sound; a grating or grinding. It refers to the unpleasant sound made when two hard surfaces rub against each other. |The gride of metal on metal set my teeth on edge. 金属摩擦金属的刺耳声让我牙痒痒。|近义词：grating, rasping, grinding|反义词：silence, quiet|noun
