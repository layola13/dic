expressive:/ɪkˈspresiv/| adj.|富有表现力的；意味深长的；表达的|effectively conveying thought or feeling.|Her expressive eyes conveyed a deep sadness. 她富有表现力的眼睛流露出深深的悲伤。|近义词：articulate, eloquent, communicative|反义词：reserved, inhibited, inexpressive|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin expressivus, from Latin expressus (past participle of exprimere ‘press out’), from ex- ‘out’ + premere ‘press’.
