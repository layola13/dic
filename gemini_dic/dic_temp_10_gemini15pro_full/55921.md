divvies:/ˈdɪviz/| v.|平分，分享|To divide something between a number of people.|They divvied up the candy among the children. 他们把糖果分给了孩子们。|synonyms: share, split, distribute, apportion|antonyms: hoard, keep, monopolize|verb
