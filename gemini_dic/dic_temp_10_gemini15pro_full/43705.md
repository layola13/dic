creditorship:/ˈkrɛdɪtərʃɪp/
n.
债权人身份，债权
The status or position of being a creditor.
例句：He assumed the creditorship of the failing company.  他承担了这家倒闭公司的债权人身份。

例句：The bankruptcy court recognized her creditorship. 破产法庭承认了她的债权人身份。

近义词：creditor status, claim
反义词：debtorship, indebtedness
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From creditor + -ship.  Creditor comes from the Latin "creditor" meaning "one who believes, trusts, lends," from "credere" meaning "to believe, trust."  The suffix "-ship" denotes state, condition, or status.
