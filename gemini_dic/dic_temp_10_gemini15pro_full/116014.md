marshlands:/ˈmɑːrʃlændz/ /ˈmɑːrʃləndz/
| n. |沼泽地，湿地|A tract of soft wet land, often periodically inundated.|例句：The marshlands are home to a variety of bird species. 沼泽地是各种鸟类的家园。

例句：The developers plan to drain the marshlands and build houses. 开发商计划排干沼泽地并建造房屋。


|近义词：swamps, bogs, fens, wetlands|反义词: uplands, highlands |词性: noun|
