embroider:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdər/| v. |刺绣，修饰，渲染|To decorate cloth by sewing patterns on it with thread.  To add fictitious details to a story to make it more interesting. |She embroidered a beautiful floral design on the tablecloth.  她在桌布上绣了一个漂亮的花卉图案。He embroidered his account of the journey with tales of daring escapades. 他在讲述旅程的故事时添油加醋地加了一些大胆的冒险经历。|近义词：stitch, adorn, embellish, decorate|反义词：simplify, strip down|词性:动词
