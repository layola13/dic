bumphs:/bʌmps/
| n. |碰撞；肿块；隆起物|A bump is a raised, rounded area on a surface, or a swelling on the body.|The car hit a bump in the road. 汽车在路上撞到了一个凸起。|Synonyms: lump, swelling, bulge, knob|Antonyms: hollow, depression|noun


bumphs:/bʌmps/
| v. |碰撞；撞击；颠簸而行|To bump into something means to accidentally hit it with your body or another object.|I bumped my head on the low doorway. 我的头撞到了低矮的门框上。|Synonyms: knock, hit, collide, bang|Antonyms: avoid, miss|verb
