bunkhouses:/ˈbʌŋkhaʊzɪz/
n.
工棚；集体宿舍
A rough sleeping accommodation, typically for workers or campers.
The bunkhouses were spartan, with only basic beds and a shared bathroom. 工棚很简陋，只有基本的床位和一个共用浴室。
The ranch hands lived in bunkhouses during the cattle drive. 牧场工人在赶牛期间住在工棚里。
