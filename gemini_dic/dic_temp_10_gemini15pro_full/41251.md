corbeling:/ˈkɔːrbəlɪŋ/ | n. | 叠涩 | A construction technique where each course of masonry projects slightly beyond the one below it, creating a stepped overhang. |  The castle walls featured intricate corbeling. 城堡的墙壁上有复杂的叠涩结构.  The corbeling supported the balcony. 叠涩支撑着阳台。 | synonyms:  oversailing, projecting, cantilevering | antonyms: receding, flush | noun

corbel:/ˈkɔːrbəl/ | n. | 叠涩 | A structural piece of stone, wood, or brick projecting from a wall to support a weight above, such as a beam or arch.  | The corbel beneath the statue was intricately carved. 雕像下面的叠涩雕刻精美。 The roof beam rested on a sturdy corbel. 屋顶梁搁在一个坚固的叠涩上。 | synonyms: bracket, support, console | antonyms: none | noun
