discrediting:/dɪsˈkrɛdɪtɪŋ/
verb
使丧失信誉；败坏名声；使不可信
damaging the reputation of someone or something; causing something or someone to seem not credible or trustworthy
The lawyer presented evidence discrediting the witness's testimony.  律师出示了证据，损害了证人证词的可信度。
synonyms: disparaging, defaming, slandering, vilifying, denigrating, maligning, traducing, besmirching
antonyms: praising, commending, lauding, extolling, accrediting, validating
v.

discredit:/dɪsˈkrɛdɪt/
noun
丧失信誉；丢脸；耻辱
loss or damage to someone's reputation; disgrace
The scandal brought discredit upon the entire organization. 这桩丑闻给整个组织带来了耻辱。
synonyms: disgrace, dishonor, disrepute, ignominy, shame, opprobrium
antonyms: credit, honor, prestige, esteem, reputation
n.
