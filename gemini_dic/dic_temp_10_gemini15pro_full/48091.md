declaw:/diːˈklɔː/
verb
去爪
to remove the claws of (an animal), especially a cat.
例句：The vet declawed the kitten. 兽医给小猫做了去爪手术。

例句：Declawing cats is a controversial practice.  给猫去爪是一个有争议的做法。

近义词：onychectomy
反义词：(none - the opposite would be to *add* claws, which isn't a real procedure)
词性：verb
