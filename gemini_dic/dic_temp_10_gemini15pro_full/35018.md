clonked:/klɒŋkt/ /klɑːŋkt/| v. |发出沉闷的敲击声；（非正式）猛击；（非正式，尤指头部）被重击；（机器、车辆等）突然停止运转|To hit something hard, producing a dull sound; (informal) to hit someone hard, especially on the head; (of a machine, vehicle, etc.) to suddenly stop working.|例句：I clonked my head on the low beam. 我的头撞到了低矮的横梁上。

例句：The engine clonked out halfway up the hill. 引擎在半山腰突然熄火了。|近义词：bang, bash, bonk, hit, strike, thump, conk|反义词:repair, fix|词性：动词

clonk:/klɒŋk/ /klɑːŋkt/| n. |沉闷的敲击声|A dull, heavy sound, as of a blow.|例句：There was a loud clonk as the metal bar hit the floor. 金属棒撞到地板上发出一声巨响。

例句：She heard a clonk from the kitchen. 她听到厨房传来砰的一声。|近义词：bang, thud, thump|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑用silence安静)|词性：名词
