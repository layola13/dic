delivering:/dɪˈlɪvərɪŋ/
verb
递送;发表;履行;接生
present participle of deliver
The postman is delivering a package. 邮递员正在送包裹。
Synonyms: distributing, handing over, presenting, conveying
Antonyms: receiving, accepting, withholding, keeping
