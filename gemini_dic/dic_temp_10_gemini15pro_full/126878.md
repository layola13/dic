napster:/ˈnæpstər/| n.|纳普斯特|A pioneering peer-to-peer (P2P) file-sharing service that facilitated the exchange of MP3 music files over the internet. It was highly popular in the late 1990s and early 2000s but was eventually shut down due to copyright infringement lawsuits.|Napster was a revolution in music sharing. 纳普斯特是音乐共享领域的一场革命。|Synonyms: P2P service, file-sharing platform|Antonyms: Streaming service, record label|Noun
