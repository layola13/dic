humourlessness:/ˈhjuːmələsnəs/ | n. | 缺乏幽默感 |The state of being devoid of humor; the inability to appreciate or express humor. | Her humourlessness made it difficult to enjoy lighthearted conversations. 她缺乏幽默感，让人很难享受轻松愉快的谈话。 | seriousness, solemnity, gravity | lightheartedness, wit, humor | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From humour + -less + -ness.  "Humour" comes from Middle English and Old French, ultimately from Latin "humor" meaning "moisture, fluid."  "-less" is an Old English suffix meaning "without." "-ness" is an Old English suffix forming nouns from adjectives.
