overjudiciousness:/ˌoʊvərˌdʒuːˈdɪʃəsnəs/
n.
过度谨慎；判断过严
The state of being excessively judicious or prudent, often to the point of being overly critical or hesitant.
His overjudiciousness prevented him from taking necessary risks in business.  他过度谨慎，以至于在生意上不敢冒险。
近义词：overcautiousness, excessive prudence, hyperprudence
反义词：rashness, recklessness, imprudence
词性：名词

overjudicious:/ˌoʊvərˌdʒuːˈdɪʃəs/
adj.
过度谨慎的；判断过严的
Excessively judicious or prudent, often to the point of being overly critical or hesitant.
She was overjudicious in her assessment of the situation, failing to see the potential benefits. 她对情况的评估过于谨慎，没有看到潜在的好处。
近义词：overcautious, excessively prudent, hyperprudent
反义词：rash, reckless, imprudent
词性：形容词

词源:
From over- + judicious + -ness.  由"over-"(过度) + "judicious"(明智的) + "-ness"(名词后缀)构成。  "Judicious" 源于拉丁语 "iudicium" (判断)。
