castered:/ˈkæstərd/ | adj. | 有脚轮的，装有脚轮的 | Having casters or wheels, especially for easy movement. | The hospital bed was castered, allowing nurses to move it easily.  医院的床装有脚轮，方便护士移动。| The piano was castered so it could be moved across the stage.  钢琴装有脚轮，以便在舞台上移动。 | 近义词: wheeled, mobile, rolling | 反义词: fixed, stationary | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "caster" (a wheel mounted on a swivel) + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Caster" itself comes from the verb "cast," originally meaning "to throw" or "project," likely referring to the way the wheel projects outwards.
