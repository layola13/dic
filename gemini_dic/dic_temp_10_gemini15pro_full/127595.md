need-blind:/ˌniːdˈblaɪnd/ | adj. | 不考虑经济需要的；需盲的 |  An admissions policy that does not consider students' financial need when deciding whether to admit them. |  Many prestigious universities have a need-blind admissions policy, meaning your ability to pay won't affect your chances of getting in.  许多名牌大学都有不考虑经济需要的招生政策，这意味着你的支付能力不会影响你被录取的机会。 | synonyms: need-unaware | antonyms: need-aware | adjective
