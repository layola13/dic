mysticete:/ˈmɪstɪˌsiːt/ | n. | 须鲸 | A whale of the suborder Mysticeti, characterized by having baleen plates in the mouth for straining plankton from the water; a baleen whale. | Example 1: The humpback whale is a mysticete known for its acrobatic displays. 座头鲸是一种以其杂技表演而闻名的须鲸。

Example 2:  Mysticetes are filter feeders, unlike the toothed whales. 须鲸是滤食动物，不像齿鲸。| 近义词: baleen whale | 反义词: odontocete (齿鲸) | 词性: noun
