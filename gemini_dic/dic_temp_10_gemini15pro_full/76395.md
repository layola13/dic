gaffes:/ˈɡæfs/| n.|失态，失礼，过失|An unintentional act or remark causing embarrassment to its originator; a blunder.|例句：His career was blighted by a series of diplomatic gaffes.  他的一系列外交失态毁了他的职业生涯。

例句：The politician made a series of embarrassing gaffes during the interview.  这位政治家在采访中犯了一系列令人尴尬的失误。|近义词：blunder, mistake, error, slip, faux pas|反义词:coup, success|词性:noun
