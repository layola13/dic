microscopical:/ˌmaɪkrəˈskɒpɪkəl/ (US) /ˌmaɪkrəˈskɒpɪkl/ (UK)| adj.|微观的；极小的|Relating to or visible only with a microscope; extremely small.|例句：The bacteria are microscopical and cannot be seen with the naked eye. 细菌是微观的，肉眼无法看到。

例句：The changes were microscopical and went unnoticed. 这些变化微乎其微，没有被注意到。|近义词：minute, tiny, minuscule, infinitesimal|反义词：macroscopic, huge, enormous|词性：形容词
