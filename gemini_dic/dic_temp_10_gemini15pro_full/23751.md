bravely: /ˈbreɪvli/ | adv. | 勇敢地 | In a brave manner; courageously. |  She bravely faced her fears. 她勇敢地面对她的恐惧。|  The firefighters bravely entered the burning building. 消防员勇敢地进入了燃烧的大楼。| 近义词: courageously, valiantly, heroically, fearlessly | 反义词: cowardly, timidly, fearfully | 词性: 副词
词源: From Middle English *braveli, from Old French *bravelier, from brave (“brave”).
