mushroom:/ˈmʌʃˌrum/ (US), /ˈmʌʃruːm/ (UK)| n.|蘑菇|A fleshy spore-bearing fruiting body of a fungus, typically produced above ground on soil or on its food source.|I love mushrooms on my pizza.|Synonyms: toadstool, fungus|Antonyms: None|Noun

mushroom:/ˈmʌʃˌrum/ (US), /ˈmʌʃruːm/ (UK)| vi.|迅速增长；迅速发展|To grow or expand rapidly.|The town mushroomed after the discovery of gold.|Synonyms: proliferate, burgeon, expand|Antonyms: shrink, dwindle, contract|Verb
