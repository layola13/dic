daguerreotype: /dəˈɡɛrəˌtaɪp/ /dəˈɡɛrəˌtʌɪp/ | n. | 达盖尔银版照相；早期照相法|A photograph taken by an early photographic process employing an iodine-sensitized silvered plate and mercury vapor.|例句：The museum has a fascinating collection of daguerreotypes from the 19th century.  这家博物馆收藏了19世纪引人入胜的达盖尔银版照片。

例句：The daguerreotype captured a fleeting moment in time, preserving it for posterity. 这张达盖尔银版照片捕捉到了一个稍纵即逝的时刻，并将其保存了下来。|近义词：photograph, image, picture |反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, perhaps consider "modern photograph") |词性: noun
