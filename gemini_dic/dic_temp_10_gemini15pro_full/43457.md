crash-lands:/ˈkræʃ lændz/ | v. | 坠毁着陆；迫降 |  To land roughly or violently, usually because of damage or mechanical failure. | The pilot skillfully crash-landed the damaged plane in a field. 飞行员巧妙地将受损的飞机迫降在一块田地里。| The airplane crash-landed on a deserted island. 飞机在一个荒岛上迫降。 | Synonyms: ditch, force-land | Antonyms: land smoothly, take off | verb

crash-lands:/ˈkræʃ lændz/ | n. | 坠毁着陆；迫降 | The act of crash-landing. | The crash-lands of the plane was a miracle, everyone survived. 飞机的迫降是一个奇迹，所有人都幸存了下来。|  Several crash-lands have been reported in this area due to the bad weather.  由于恶劣天气，该地区已报告数起迫降事件。 | Synonyms: crash landing, forced landing | Antonyms: smooth landing, takeoff | noun


词源: crash + land.  "Crash" meaning a violent collision, and "land" referring to the act of coming down to the ground.
