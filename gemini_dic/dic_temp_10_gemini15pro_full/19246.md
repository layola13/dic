bhangra: /ˈbʌŋɡrɑː/ (UK), /ˈbɑːŋɡrɑː/ (US)
noun
宝莱坞歌舞，旁遮普邦的一种传统民间歌舞
A type of Punjabi folk music and dance with strong, energetic rhythms, often accompanied by dhol drums and other instruments, which has become popular in other parts of India and internationally.
