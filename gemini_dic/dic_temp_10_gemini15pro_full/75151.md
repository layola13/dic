fritillary:/ˈfrɪtɪləri/ | n. | 贝母；贝母属植物;  (尤指棋盘花) |A plant of the lily family, with usually solitary nodding flowers, often checkered or mottled in purple, red, or yellow.  Specifically, a butterfly with checkered markings. | 例句：The fritillary is a beautiful spring flower. 贝母是一种美丽的春花。

例句：The snake's head fritillary is a rare and protected species.  蛇头贝母是一种稀有且受保护的物种。 | 近义词：lily, flower | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


fritillary:/ˈfrɪtɪləri/ | n. | 贝母蝶 | Any of various brightly colored butterflies of the family Nymphalidae, having wings with a checkered pattern. | 例句：I saw a fritillary butterfly flitting among the wildflowers. 我看到一只贝母蝶在野花丛中飞舞。

例句：The underside of the fritillary's wings is often intricately patterned. 贝母蝶翅膀的底面通常图案复杂。 | 近义词：butterfly | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
