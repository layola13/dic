fairness:/ˈfeərnɪs/| n. |公平，公正|The quality of being fair and just.|例句：He treated everyone with fairness. 他待人公平。|例句：The fairness of the judge's decision was questioned.  法官判决的公正性受到了质疑。|近义词：justice, impartiality, equity, objectivity|反义词：unfairness, injustice, bias, partiality|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *fayrnes*, from *fayr* (“beautiful, pleasing, fitting, proper”) + *-nes* (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality). *Fayr* is from Old English *fæġer* (“fair, beautiful”), from Proto-Germanic *fagraz* (“fit, suitable”), from Proto-Indo-European *peh₂ǵ- (“to adorn, to fit”).
