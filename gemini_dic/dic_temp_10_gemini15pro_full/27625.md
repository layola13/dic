cancerously:/ˈkænsərəsli/
adverb|癌性地，恶性地；像癌症一样蔓延地|In a manner resembling the growth and spread of cancer.  This can refer to both physical and metaphorical contexts.|
The tumor was growing cancerously.  肿瘤恶性生长。

His negative influence spread cancerously throughout the organization. 他的负面影响像癌症一样在整个组织中蔓延开来。
|malignantly, destructively, virulently|benignly, healthily|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From "cancer" + "-ous" (forming adjectives meaning "full of" or "possessing the qualities of") + "-ly" (forming adverbs).  The word "cancer" ultimately comes from the Latin "cancer," meaning "crab," due to the appearance of some tumors.
