indoctrination:/ɪnˌdɒktrɪˈneɪʃn/ /ɪnˌdɑːktrɪˈneɪʃn/| n. |灌输，教导|The process of teaching a person or group to accept a set of beliefs uncritically.|The regime's indoctrination of the youth was thorough and relentless. 这个政权对年轻人的灌输是彻底和无情的。|Synonyms: brainwashing, propaganda, inculcation|Antonyms: education, enlightenment, liberation|noun

indoctrinate:/ɪnˈdɒktrɪneɪt/ /ɪnˈdɑːktrɪneɪt/| vt. |向…灌输|To teach (a person or group) to accept a set of beliefs uncritically.|The cult leader indoctrinated his followers with bizarre rituals.  邪教头目向他的追随者灌输怪异的仪式。|Synonyms: brainwash, propagandize, inculcate|Antonyms: educate, enlighten, liberate|verb
