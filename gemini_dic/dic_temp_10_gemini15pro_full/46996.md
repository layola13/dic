dashboard: /ˈdæʃbɔːrd/ (US) /ˈdæʃbɔːd/ (UK) | n. | 仪表盘；控制面板；信息显示板 | A panel in a vehicle or aircraft containing dials and controls.  A visual display of the most important information needed to achieve one or more objectives; consolidated and arranged on a single screen so the information can be monitored at a glance. |  The car's dashboard was lit up with warning lights. 汽车的仪表盘上亮起了警示灯。

|  The company uses a dashboard to track key performance indicators.  这家公司使用信息显示板来跟踪关键绩效指标。 | synonyms: console, control panel, instrument panel | antonyms: N/A | noun
