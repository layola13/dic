methaemoglobin:/ˌmetˈhiːməˌɡloʊbɪn/ /ˌmetˈhiːmoʊˌɡloʊbɪn/ | n. |高铁血红蛋白|A form of hemoglobin in which the iron in the heme group is in the ferric state (Fe3+) rather than the ferrous state (Fe2+), and is thus unable to bind oxygen.|Methemoglobinemia can be caused by exposure to certain drugs and chemicals. 高铁血红蛋白血症可能是由接触某些药物和化学物质引起的。|Methemoglobin is normally present in small amounts in the blood.  少量的高铁血红蛋白通常存在于血液中。|Synonym: ferrihemoglobin|Antonym: hemoglobin (in its oxygen-binding state)|noun
