cambro:/ˈkæmbroʊ/
noun
餐具，餐盘，（尤指）塑料餐盘
A brand of durable, reusable food storage containers, trays, and other tableware often used in the food service industry.
The restaurant used cambro containers to store prepped ingredients.  这家餐馆用cambro餐盒储存准备好的食材。
synonyms: food container, storage container, tray
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps disposable container)
n.
