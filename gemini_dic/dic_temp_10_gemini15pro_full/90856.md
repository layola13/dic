hungerless:/ˈhʌŋɡərləs/
adjective
无饥饿感的；不觉得饿的
Not feeling hunger; without hunger.
The medication left him feeling hungerless for hours. 这种药让他几个小时都不觉得饿。
synonyms: satiated, full, satisfied
antonyms: hungry, starving, ravenous
词性: 形容词
