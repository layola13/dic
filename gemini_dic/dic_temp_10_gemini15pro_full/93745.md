immature:/ˌɪməˈtʃʊr/ /ˌɪməˈtʊr/ | adj. |不成熟的，未成熟的|Not fully developed or grown; childish or lacking in experience. |例句：He showed immature behavior at the meeting. 他在会议上表现得很不成熟。|例句：The immature fruit is sour.  未成熟的果实是酸的。|近义词：undeveloped, childish, juvenile, green|反义词：mature, ripe, developed, grown-up|词性:形容词
immatured:/ˌɪməˈtʃʊrd/ /ˌɪməˈtʊrd/ | adj. |不成熟的，未成熟的 (This form is less common than "immature") |Not fully developed or grown; childish or lacking in experience.  While grammatically correct, "immatured" is less frequently used than "immature."|例句：The immatured plans were quickly scrapped. 不成熟的计划很快就被废弃了。|例句：The wine, still immatured, needed more time in the cellar.  这酒还不够成熟，需要在酒窖里再放一段时间。|近义词：undeveloped, childish, juvenile, green|反义词：mature, ripe, developed, grown-up|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *immaturatus, past participle of immaturare "to make not ripe," from in- "not" + maturare "to ripen," from Latin maturus "ripe."
