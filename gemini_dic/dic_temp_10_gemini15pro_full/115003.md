mandas:/ˈmændəs/| n. | 命令，指令; 授权，委托; 托管地，委任统治地 |A mandate is an official order or commission to do something.  A mandate can also refer to the authority given to a political representative by the people who elect them. It can also be a territory under the authority of another country, especially a territory taken as a result of war.|  The government received a mandate to reduce crime. 政府ได้รับ了减少犯罪的授权。| synonyms: order, command, instruction, authorization, commission, directive | antonyms: prohibition, forbiddance | noun
