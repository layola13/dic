brad:/bræd/
n.
细钉，无头钉
A small, thin wire nail with a small head or no head, used for fixing thin pieces of wood.
He used brads to attach the molding to the wall. 他用无头钉把装饰线条固定在墙上。

近义词:nail, tack, pin
反义词:(无明确反义词)
词性:noun
