cresselle: /krɛˈsɛl/
noun
新月形饰物；新月形面包
A crescent-shaped ornament or roll.
The baker made delicate cresscelles dusted with powdered sugar.  面包师做了精致的新月形面包，上面撒了糖粉。
Cresselle bread is a popular breakfast item in some European countries. 新月形面包在一些欧洲国家是受欢迎的早餐食品。
synonyms: crescent roll, croissant
antonyms: N/A
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cresselle*, diminutive of *creissant* "crescent," from Old French *creissant* "growing, increasing," present participle of *creistre* "to grow," from Latin *crescere* "to arise, grow," from PIE root *ker-* "to grow."  The word specifically refers to a crescent-shaped object, especially a bread roll.
