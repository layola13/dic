abundancy:/əˈbʌndənsi/| n.|丰富，充裕，大量|The farm boasts an abundancy of fresh produce.  这个农场拥有丰富的农产品。| The region enjoys an abundancy of natural resources. 该地区拥有丰富的自然资源。|近义词：abundance, plenty, profusion|反义词：scarcity, lack, dearth|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *abundantia, from Latin abundans (present participle of abundare, "to overflow, abound"), from ab- ("from, away from") + unda ("wave") + -ans (present participle suffix).  最终源于原始印欧语 *wed- (“水；湿的”).
