outschemed:/ˌaʊtˈskiːmd/
verb
智胜，以计谋胜过
defeated by superior tactics or planning.
He was completely outschemed by his opponent in the negotiation. 他在谈判中被对手彻底智胜了。
synonyms: outwitted, outmaneuvered, outsmarted
antonyms: outplayed, defeated, overpowered
词性: 动词
