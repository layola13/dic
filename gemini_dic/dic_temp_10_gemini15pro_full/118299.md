mercurialness:/mɜːrˈkjʊəriəlnəs/ | n. | 善变；反复无常；活泼；敏捷 | The quality of being characterized by rapid and unpredictable changeableness of mood. |  His mercurialness made it difficult to predict his reactions. 他的反复无常让人难以预测他的反应。| Synonyms: volatility, fickleness, capriciousness, instability, flightiness | Antonyms: stability, steadiness, constancy, predictability | noun
