lees:/liːz/
n.
酒糟，沉淀物
Sediment, especially that of wine, that settles at the bottom of a container.
The winemaker carefully removed the lees from the barrel.
酿酒师小心地从桶中去除了酒糟。
sediment, dregs, grounds
clear liquid
noun
