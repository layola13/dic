cia:/ˌsiː.aɪˈeɪ/ | n. | 中央情报局（Central Intelligence Agency） |  The Central Intelligence Agency (CIA) is a civilian foreign intelligence service of the federal government of the United States, tasked with gathering, processing, and analyzing national security information from around the world, primarily through the use of human intelligence (HUMINT). | The CIA is involved in covert operations around the world. 中央情报局参与世界各地的秘密行动。 |  近义词: intelligence agency, spy agency | 反义词:  None (No direct opposite for an organization) | noun
