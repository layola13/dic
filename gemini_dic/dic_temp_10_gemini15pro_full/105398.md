kiddos:/ˈkɪdoʊz/
noun
孩子们，小家伙们
An informal and affectionate term for children.
例句：See you later, kiddos!  回头见，孩子们！
例句：The kiddos are having a blast at the party. 孩子们在派对上玩得很开心。
近义词：children, kids, youngsters
反义词：adults, grown-ups
词性：n. (noun)
