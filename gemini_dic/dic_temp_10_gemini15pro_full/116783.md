maverick: /ˈmævərɪk/ (US) /ˈmævərɪk/ (UK)| n. adj.| 特立独行的人，持不同意见的人；不墨守成规的；标新立异的|An independent individual who does not go along with a group or party; unconventional, unorthodox. |例句：He was a maverick in the field of physics, challenging established theories. 他是物理学领域的独行侠，挑战既定的理论。

|例句：She's known for her maverick style of filmmaking. 她以其不落俗套的电影制作风格而闻名。|近义词：nonconformist, individualist, rebel, dissenter, iconoclast|反义词：conformist, follower, traditionalist|词性:名词, 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Samuel Augustus Maverick (1803-1870), a Texas rancher who did not brand his cattle.
