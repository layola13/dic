USAGE->
Simple Present:
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? 他靠什么谋生？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? 她晚饭在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned recently? 你最近学了什么？
What has he done to help? 他做了些什么来帮忙？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading this week? 她这周一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before I arrived? 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision? 他们在做决定之前讨论了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before you called me? 你打电话给我之前一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? 她换专业之前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们在下次会议上会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have finished by tomorrow afternoon? 到明天下午他们将完成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
