bedewer:/bɪˈdjuːər/ | n. | 露水沾湿者；使沾露水的人或物 | One who or that which bedews.  Something that moistens or covers with dew, like a gentle rain or mist. | The morning mist acted as a bedewer, leaving tiny droplets on the spiderwebs.  清晨的薄雾像露水一样，在蜘蛛网上留下了细小的水滴。 | 近义词：moistener, sprinkler | 反义词：dryer, desiccant | 词性: noun

Etymology: From the verb "bedew" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Bedew" comes from Middle English "bedewen," from "be-" (prefix meaning "thoroughly") + "dew."
