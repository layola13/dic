enteropneusta:/ˌɛntərɒpˈnjuːstə/ | n. |肠鳃纲动物|A member of the invertebrate phylum Enteropneusta, commonly known as acorn worms. These marine animals are characterized by their proboscis, collar, and trunk body regions, and are considered to be closely related to chordates.|Acorn worms, belonging to the Enteropneusta, play a crucial role in marine ecosystems. 橡实虫，属于肠鳃纲，在海洋生态系统中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonym: acorn worm|Antonym: N/A|noun
