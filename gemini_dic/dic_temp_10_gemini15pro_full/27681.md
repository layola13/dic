candlelight:/ˈkændlˌlaɪt/| n.|烛光|The soft glow of candlelight created a romantic atmosphere. 柔和的烛光营造了浪漫的氛围。|例句：The dinner was held by candlelight. 晚餐在烛光下举行。  The room was lit by candlelight. 房间里点着蜡烛。|近义词：lamplight|反义词:daylight, sunlight|名词

词源：From Middle English *candel liht*, equivalent to *candle* + *light*.
