bevellers:/ˈbevələrz/
n.
斜边器; 倒角器;  做斜边的人;
People or tools that create bevels (sloped edges) on materials like glass, wood, or metal.
The bevellers carefully shaped the edges of the stained glass.  工匠们小心翼翼地打磨着彩色玻璃的边缘。
The bevellers were essential for creating the intricate design.  这些斜边器对于创作复杂的图案至关重要。
Synonyms: chamferers, beveling tools
Antonyms: N/A
