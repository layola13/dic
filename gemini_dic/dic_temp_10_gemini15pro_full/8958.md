antihunting:/ˌæntiˈhʌntɪŋ/ | adj. | 反狩猎的，反对狩猎的 | Opposed to hunting animals. |  Many celebrities are vocal in their antihunting stance. 许多名人都公开表达了他们反对狩猎的立场。| Antihunting groups are protesting the upcoming fox hunt.  反狩猎组织正在抗议即将到来的猎狐活动。 | 近义词：against hunting | 反义词：pro-hunting | 词性:形容词

词源:  "anti-" (against) + "hunting" (the activity of hunting wild animals).  "Anti-" comes from Greek, and "hunting" comes from Old English.
