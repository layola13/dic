coanchored:/koʊˈæŋkərd/
adjective
共同锚定的，共同定位的
Two genes are said to be coanchored if they are located close together on the same chromosome and tend to be inherited together.
例句：The two genes are coanchored on chromosome 17. 这两个基因共同锚定在17号染色体上。

例句：Coanchored genes are less likely to be separated during recombination. 共同锚定的基因在重组过程中不太可能被分开。
近义词：co-located, linked
反义词：unlinked, separated
词性：adjective
