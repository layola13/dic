fernticle: /ˈfɜːrn.tɪ.kəl/ | n. |蕨类植物的小叶; 羽片 |A small leaflet or division of a fern frond. |The fernticle was delicate and bright green. 这片蕨类植物的小叶精致而翠绿。| The fern's fronds were covered in tiny fernticles. 蕨类的复叶上覆盖着细小的羽片。|Synonym: pinnule, leaflet |Antonym: frond (arguably, as the frond is the whole structure and the fernticle is a part of it) | noun
