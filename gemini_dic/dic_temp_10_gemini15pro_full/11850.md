arrhizal:/əˈraɪzəl/ | adj. | 非根生的，无根的 | Lacking roots, especially lacking mycorrhizae (a symbiotic association between a fungus and the roots of a plant). |  Many orchids are epiphytic and arrhizal, obtaining nutrients from the air and rain. 许多兰花是附生的，没有根，从空气和雨水中获取营养。| Example: The arrhizal plant derived its nutrients from the decaying leaves around it. 这种无根植物从周围腐烂的叶子中获取营养。 | Synonym: rootless | Antonym: rooted | Part of Speech: Adjective
