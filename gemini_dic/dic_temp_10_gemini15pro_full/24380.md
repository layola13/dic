brininess:/ˈbraɪninəs/| n. |咸味；咸度；海水的咸味|The brininess of the air reminded her of the coast. 空气中的咸味让她想起了海岸。|例句：The brininess of the oyster was perfect. 牡蛎的咸度恰到好处。

例句：The soup had a distinct brininess that I found unpleasant. 这汤有一种明显的咸味，我觉得很不舒服。

近义词：saltiness, salinity|反义词:freshness, sweetness|词性:名词

Etymology->
From brine + -ness.  Brine comes from Old English *bryne, from Proto-Germanic *brunjo, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰer- (to boil, bubble, effervesce). The suffix -ness forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
