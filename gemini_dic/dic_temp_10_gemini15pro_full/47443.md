deafens:/ˈdɛfənz/ (US), /ˈdɛfənz/ (UK)| v.|使聋，震耳欲聋|Makes someone temporarily or permanently unable to hear, usually due to loud noise. |The explosion deafened him for several minutes. 爆炸声让他几分钟都听不见。|近义词：stuns, overwhelms, drowns out|反义词: softens, quiets|verb
