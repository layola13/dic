orthopnea:/ˌɔːrθəpˈniːə/ /ˌɔːrθɑːpˈniːə/
noun
呼吸困难；端坐呼吸
Shortness of breath (dyspnea) that occurs when lying flat, causing the person to have to sleep propped up in bed or sitting in a chair.
The patient complained of orthopnea, making it difficult for him to sleep lying down.  病人抱怨呼吸困难，这使得他难以躺下睡觉。
She experiences orthopnea and has to sleep with several pillows. 她会出现端坐呼吸，必须用几个枕头睡觉。
dyspnea, shortness of breath
Eupnea (normal breathing)
n.
