hurkaru: /hərˈkɑːruː/ | n. |  传令兵，信使 | A messenger, especially one employed by a person of rank or authority. | Example 1: The hurkaru arrived at the palace, bearing news from the battlefield. 传令兵到达了宫殿，带来了战场上的消息。

Example 2: The Raja sent a hurkaru to deliver a message to the neighboring kingdom.  拉贾派了一名信使向邻国传递消息。| Synonym: messenger, courier, runner, envoy | Antonym: recipient, receiver | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi and Urdu  harkāra  (हरकारा, ہرکارہ), ultimately from Persian  harkāra  (هرکاره, "one who does every kind of work"), from  har  (هر, "every") +  kār  (کار, "work, deed").
