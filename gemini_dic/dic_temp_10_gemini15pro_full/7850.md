antasthmatic:/ˌæntiæsˈmætɪk/| adj. n.|抗哮喘的；抗哮喘药|Used to prevent or relieve asthma.  It describes something that works against or counteracts asthma. It can also be a noun referring to a medication used to treat asthma.|例句：She takes an antasthmatic medication daily. 她每天服用抗哮喘药物。|近义词：antiasthmatic, bronchodilator|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly pro-asthmatic, but this is rarely used) |词性: adjective, noun

antasthmatic:/ˌæntiæsˈmætɪk/| n.|抗哮喘药|A medication used to prevent or relieve asthma.|例句：The doctor prescribed a new antasthmatic for her. 医生给她开了一种新的抗哮喘药。|近义词：antiasthmatic, bronchodilator|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: noun
