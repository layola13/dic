ethnography: /ɛθˈnɒɡrəfi/ (UK) /ɛθˈnɑːɡrəfi/ (US) | n. | 人种志，民族志 | The scientific description of the customs of individual peoples and cultures. |  Example: Her ethnography of the remote tribe provided valuable insights into their social structures. 她对这个偏远部落的民族志研究为了解他们的社会结构提供了宝贵的见解。 | Example: The ethnography explored the rituals and beliefs of the community. 这部民族志探讨了该社区的仪式和信仰。 |  Synonyms:  anthropology, cultural study | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
