deed:/diːd/| n.|行为，行动；证书；契约|An action that is performed intentionally or consciously.  A legal document that is signed and delivered, especially one regarding the ownership of property or legal rights.|She performed a good deed by helping the homeless man. 她帮助了那个无家可归的人，做了一件好事。|The deed to the house is in my name. 房子的契约在我的名下。|近义词：act, action, feat, exploit, document, certificate|反义词:inaction, omission|词性:noun
