brontosaurian:/ˌbrɒntəˈsɔːriən/| n.|雷龙|A large quadrupedal herbivorous dinosaur. |例句：The brontosaurian roamed the earth millions of years ago.  数百万年前，雷龙漫游在地球上。

词源:  From Brontosaurus + -ian.  Brontosaurus comes from Greek *brontē* "thunder" and *sauros* "lizard".
