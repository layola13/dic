disarranged:/dɪsəˈreɪndʒd/
adjective
混乱的，紊乱的
Thrown into disorder; out of the usual or proper arrangement.
The room was disarranged after the party.  房间在聚会后变得凌乱不堪。
Her hair was disarranged by the wind.  她的头发被风吹乱了。
近义词: disordered, messy, chaotic, jumbled, rumpled
反义词: arranged, organized, tidy, orderly, neat
词性: adjective

disarrange:/dɪsəˈreɪndʒ/
verb
弄乱，扰乱
To disturb the arrangement or order of; to put out of order.
The wind disarranged the papers on my desk. 风吹乱了我桌上的文件。
Don't disarrange the books on the shelf. 不要弄乱书架上的书。
近义词: disorder, disorganize, mess up, disturb, muddle
反义词: arrange, organize, tidy, order, neaten
词性: verb

词源: dis- (prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of") + arrange (from Old French arengier, meaning "to put in a rank or row")
