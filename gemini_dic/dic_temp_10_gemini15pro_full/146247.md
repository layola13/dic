overextracted:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈstræktɪd/| adj.|过度萃取的|Having had too much flavor, color, or other desirable quality removed by a process of extraction, especially of coffee or tea.|The coffee was overextracted and tasted bitter. 这咖啡过度萃取，尝起来很苦。|近义词：overbrewed, bitter, harsh|反义词: underextracted, weak, mild|词性:形容词

overextracted:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈstræktɪd/| adj.|过度提取的|Having had too much of a substance removed through a process of extraction, such as in chemistry or mining.|The sample was overextracted, resulting in a lower yield than expected. 样本过度提取，导致产量低于预期。|近义词：overprocessed, depleted|反义词: underextracted|词性:形容词
