gavelkind: /ˈɡævəlkaɪnd/ | n. |均分遗产制 |A system of inheritance in which land is divided equally among all sons.|  The Kentish custom of gavelkind ensured that all male heirs received a share of the estate. 肯特郡的均分遗产制确保所有男性继承人都能获得一部分遗产。|Synonyms: equal inheritance, partible inheritance | Antonyms: primogeniture | Noun
