motorways:/ˈməʊtəweɪz/ (UK), /ˈmoʊtərˌweɪz/ (US)| n. |高速公路|A road, especially a major one, designed for fast traffic, typically with three or more lanes in each direction, limited access, and usually bordered by a median or central reservation. |We took the motorway to London. 我们走高速公路去伦敦。|例句：The motorways were congested with holiday traffic.  高速公路上挤满了假日出行的车辆。|近义词：freeway, expressway, highway, turnpike|反义词: byway, side road|词性:noun
