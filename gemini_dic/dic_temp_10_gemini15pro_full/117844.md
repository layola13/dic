mellone:/məˈloʊni/ | n. | 甜瓜，蜜瓜 | A melon; especially, a muskmelon. | We ate a delicious mellone for dessert. 我们吃了一个美味的甜瓜作为甜点。| 近义词:melon, muskmelon, cantaloupe | 反义词: None (not typically applicable) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Italian *melone*, from Latin *melonem*, accusative singular of *melo* ("apple, fruit, melon"), from Ancient Greek *μῆλον* (*mēlon*, "apple, any tree-fruit").
