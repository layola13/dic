nether:/ˈneðər/ (US), /ˈneðə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|下面的，下方的；地下的，阴间的|Situated below or under; lower.|The nether regions of the earth. 地球的底层区域。|近义词：lower, under, infernal|反义词：upper, higher, celestial|词性:形容词

nether:/ˈneðər/ (US), /ˈneðə(r)/ (UK)| n.|（常用复数）下部，底部；阴间|The lower or under part of something; the underworld.|The flames of the nether. 地狱之火。|近义词：underworld, hell, inferno|反义词：heaven, paradise|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *neothera, from Proto-Germanic *nizwō (“low”), from Proto-Indo-European *ni- (“down, below”). Cognate with Dutch neder, German nieder, Old Norse niðr.  The original sense was simply "lower," but it came to be associated with the underworld, especially in the plural form "nethers."
