braggartism:/ˈbræɡərtɪzəm/| n. | 吹牛，自夸 | The act or practice of bragging excessively; boastfulness. | He couldn't help but roll his eyes at her constant braggartism. 他忍不住对她不断的自夸翻白眼。|  His braggartism alienated his friends. 他的自夸疏远了他的朋友。 | 近义词：boasting, bragging, vaunting, swaggering | 反义词：humility, modesty | 词性: noun

词源:  From *braggart* (a boastful person) + *-ism* (forming nouns of action, practice, or condition). *Braggart* itself comes from the verb *brag*, of uncertain origin.
