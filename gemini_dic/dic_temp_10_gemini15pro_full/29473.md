catadromous:/kəˈtædrəməs/
adjective
降河产卵的; 降河洄游的
Migrating down rivers to the sea to spawn.  A catadromous fish lives in fresh water and migrates to salt water to breed.
例句：The European eel is a catadromous fish. 欧洲鳗鲡是一种降河产卵的鱼。

例句：Catadromous fish species are less common than anadromous ones. 降河洄游的鱼类比溯河洄游的鱼类少见。
近义词:oceanodromous (migrating within the sea)
反义词:anadromous (migrating up rivers from the sea to spawn)
词性:adjective
