councillorships:/ˈkaʊnsələʃɪps/
n.
市议员职位；市政委员的职位
the position of a councillor
She won the councillorship by a narrow margin. 她以微弱优势赢得了市议员职位。

近义词:council seat, council membership
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider positions outside of local government)


词源: councillor + -ship
councillor (n.) : late Middle English: from Anglo-Norman French conseillour, from conseiller ‘to counsel’.
-ship (suffix): Old English -scipe (denoting condition or state, office, skill).
