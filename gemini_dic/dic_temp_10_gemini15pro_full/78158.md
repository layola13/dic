geopolitists:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈpɒlɪtɪsɪsts/
noun
地缘政治学家
Experts in geopolitics, studying the influence of geography on international politics and relations.
Geopolitists often debate the future of global power dynamics. 地缘政治学家经常就全球力量动态的未来展开辩论。
Geopolitists predicted the conflict based on the region's resources. 地缘政治学家根据该地区的资源预测了这场冲突。

synonyms: geopolitical analysts, international relations experts, strategists
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps pacifists or diplomats in certain contexts)
