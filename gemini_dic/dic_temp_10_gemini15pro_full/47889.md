decemvirship:/ˌdɛsəmˈvɜːrʃɪp/
noun
十人执政官的职位（或任期）; 十人统治
The office or term of office of a decemvir; government by decemvirs.
The decemvirship was a period of political upheaval in ancient Rome.  十人执政时期是古罗马政治动荡的时期。
Synonym:decemvirate
Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider terms like "monarchy" or "dictatorship" which represent opposite forms of government)
