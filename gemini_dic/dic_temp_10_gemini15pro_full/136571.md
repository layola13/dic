nonsalubriously:/ˌnɒnsælˈjubriəsli/ /ˌnɑːnsælˈjubriəsli/
adverb
有害地；不健康地
in a way that is not healthy or pleasant
The prisoners were kept nonsalubriously in cramped, dark cells.  囚犯们被不健康地关在狭窄黑暗的牢房里。
The meat was stored nonsalubriously in the hot sun. 肉不健康地放在烈日下。
近义词: unhealthily, insalubriously, harmfully
反义词: healthily, healthfully, salubriously
词性: adverb

词源:
From non- + salubrious + -ly.  Non- 表示“不”，salubrious 表示“有益健康的”，-ly 是副词后缀。
