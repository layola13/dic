discongruity:/ˌdɪskənˈɡruːɪti/| n. |不一致，不协调，不相称|lack of agreement, harmony, or conformity; incongruence|The discongruity between his words and actions was apparent. 他的言行不一很明显。|近义词：incongruity, disparity, discrepancy, incompatibility, inconsistency|反义词： congruity, harmony, agreement, consistency|词性:noun

discongruous:/ˌdɪskənˈɡruːəs/| adj. |不一致的，不协调的，不相称的|lacking in harmony, conformity, consistency, or propriety|Her discongruous outfit clashed with the formal event. 她不协调的着装与正式场合格格不入。|近义词：incompatible, inconsistent, unsuitable, inappropriate, incongruous|反义词： congruous, harmonious, consistent, appropriate, suitable|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dis- "apart, asunder" + congruus "fit, suitable, agreeable," from com- "together" + -gruus "coming together," related to grex "flock, herd."
