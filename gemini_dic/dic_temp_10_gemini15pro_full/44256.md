crofts: /krɒfts/ (British English) /krɑːfts/ (American English)
| n. | 小农场，佃户耕地 | Small farms, especially those worked by tenant farmers. | The crofts on the Scottish islands are often quite isolated. 苏格兰岛屿上的小农场通常都很偏僻。|  The family worked hard to maintain their small croft. 这家人努力工作以维持他们的小农场。 | synonyms: smallholdings, farmsteads, plots | antonyms: estates, plantations | noun

crofts: /krɒfts/ (British English) /krɑːfts/ (American English)
| v. |  (古语) 建造，种植（尤指庄稼）|  (archaic) To build or plant (especially crops).  | "He crofted wheat and barley on his land."  他在他的土地上种植小麦和大麦。（古语用法，现在很少使用）|  "They crofted a small dwelling out of stone and wood." 他们在石头和木头上建造了一个小住所。（古语用法，现在很少使用）| synonyms: cultivate, plant, build | antonyms: destroy, demolish | verb (archaic)
