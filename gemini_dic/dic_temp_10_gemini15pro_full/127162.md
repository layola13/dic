naturalesque:/ˌnætʃərəˈlɛsk/
adjective
自然风格的，类似自然的
Resembling or imitating nature; having a natural appearance or quality.
The painting had a naturalesque quality, capturing the essence of the forest. 这幅画有一种自然风格，捕捉到了森林的精髓。

The architect designed the building with naturalesque elements, incorporating wood and stone.  这位建筑师设计这座建筑时融入了自然元素，使用了木材和石头。

near synonym:naturalistic, nature-inspired, nature-mimicking
antonym:artificial, unnatural, synthetic
word class:adjective
