deviating:/ˈdiːvieɪtɪŋ/
adjective
偏离的，背离的
straying from an established course, principle, or standard
The car was deviating from its lane. 汽车偏离了车道。
Deviating from the norm can be exciting.  打破常规可能会令人兴奋。
近义词: diverging, straying, swerving, veering
反义词: conforming, adhering, complying
词性: adjective

deviating:/ˈdiːvieɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle of deviate)
偏离，背离
to depart or swerve from a procedure, course of action, or acceptable standard
He is deviating from the plan. 他正在偏离计划。
The compass needle is deviating from north. 指南针的指针偏离了北方。
近义词: diverging, straying, swerving, veering
反义词: conforming, adhering, complying
词性: verb (present participle)
