libriform:/ˈlɪbrɪfɔːrm/
adjective
纤维状的，木质纤维的
Resembling a fiber; specifically, designating elongated, thick-walled cells found in the xylem of woody plants, providing mechanical support.
The libriform fibers provide structural support to the tree. 木质纤维为树木提供结构支撑。
The scientist examined the libriform cells under a microscope. 科学家在显微镜下检查了木质纤维细胞。
近义词:fibrous, woody
反义词:soft, flexible
词性:adjective
