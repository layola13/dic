noninquiringly:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈkwaɪrɪŋli/
adverb
不加询问地；不打探地
without asking questions; in a way that does not seek information.
He accepted her explanation noninquiringly. 他不加询问地接受了她的解释。
近义词: passively, uncuriously, unquestioningly
反义词: inquisitively, curiously
词性: adverb

noninquiringly:/ˌnɒnɪnˈkwaɪərɪŋli/
adverb
不加询问地；不打探地
without asking questions; in a way that does not seek information.
She nodded noninquiringly and followed him. 她不加询问地点了点头，跟着他走了。
近义词: submissively, compliantly
反义词: probingly, inquisitively
词性: adverb


词源:
From non- + inquiring + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Inquiring" comes from the verb "inquire," which means "to ask for information."  "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
