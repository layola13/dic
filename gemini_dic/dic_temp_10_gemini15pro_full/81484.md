gravelish:/ˈɡrævəlɪʃ/
adj.
多碎石的；含碎石的；像碎石的
Containing or resembling gravel; gravelly.
The path was gravelish and difficult to walk on.  这条小路有很多碎石，很难走。
近义词: gravelly, stony, pebbly
反义词: smooth, sandy, silty
词性: 形容词

词源: gravel + -ish.  From *grafin, from Proto-Germanic *grabō (“gravel”).  The suffix "-ish" forms adjectives meaning "somewhat like" or "having the quality of."
