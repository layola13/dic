antihumanities:/ˌæntihjʊˈmænɪtiz/
n.|反人文学科；反对人文学科的思潮|A sentiment or movement opposed to the humanities, often characterized by a focus on STEM fields and a perceived devaluation of subjects like literature, philosophy, and history.|There's a growing antihumanities sentiment in education, prioritizing STEM subjects over the liberal arts. 教育界越来越反人文学科，重视STEM学科胜过文科。|The recent budget cuts reflect the ongoing antihumanities trend. 最近的预算削减反映了持续的反人文学科趋势。|近义词: anti-intellectualism, philistinism|反义词: pro-humanities, humanism|词性:名词
