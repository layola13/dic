emend:/ɪˈmend/ | v. | 修改；修订；校订 | To correct or improve a text, typically a literary work. | The editor emended the manuscript before publication. 编辑在出版前修订了稿件。| Synonyms: correct, revise, amend, improve | Antonyms: worsen, corrupt, damage | verb

emended:/ɪˈmendɪd/ | adj. | 修改过的；修订过的；校订过的 | Having been corrected or improved, especially a text. | The emended version of the play was much clearer.  修改后的剧本清晰多了。| Synonyms: corrected, revised, amended, improved | Antonyms: uncorrected, original, unaltered | adjective
