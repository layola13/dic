highermost:/ˈhaɪərməʊst/ |adj.|最高的；最上面的|Situated at the highest point; uppermost.|The highermost branches of the redwood tree swayed gently in the breeze.  红杉树最高的树枝在微风中轻轻摇曳。|
Example: He climbed to the highermost point of the hill to get a better view. 他爬到山顶最高处，以便更好地欣赏风景。|Synonyms: highest, uppermost, topmost|Antonyms: lowest, bottommost|Word type: adjective
