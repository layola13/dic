cyrenian:/sɪˈriːniən/
n.|昔兰尼人；(尤指)帮助耶稣背十字架的人|A native or inhabitant of Cyrene, an ancient Greek and Roman city in North Africa.  Specifically refers to Simon of Cyrene, who was compelled to carry the cross of Jesus. |Simon the Cyrenian helped Jesus carry the cross. 西门，昔兰尼人，帮助耶稣背了十字架。|Synonym:Helper, assistant|Antonym:Opponent, enemy|Noun
