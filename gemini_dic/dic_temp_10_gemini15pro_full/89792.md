horizontalness:/ˌhɔːrɪˈzɒntəlnɪs/ |n.|水平; 水平状态|The horizontalness of the plains was striking. 平原的平坦令人印象深刻。|Example: The artist emphasized the horizontalness of the landscape in his painting.  这位艺术家在他的画作中强调了景色的水平感。

Example:  The horizontalness of the water's surface was disturbed by the sudden gust of wind. 水平的表面被突如其来的阵风扰乱了。|Synonyms: levelness, flatness, evenness|Antonyms: verticality, steepness, slope|noun


Etymology->
From horizontal + -ness.  Horizontal comes from Late Latin horizontālis, from horizontem (nominative horizōn), from Ancient Greek ὁρίζων (horízōn, “bounding, defining”), present participle of ὁρίζω (horízō, “I limit, define”).
