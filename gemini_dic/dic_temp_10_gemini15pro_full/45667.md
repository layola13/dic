curtaining:/ˈkɜːrtənɪŋ/ | n. | 窗帘；帷幔；遮蔽物 |The act of providing or hanging curtains.  The curtains themselves can also be referred to as curtaining. |  The curtaining in the hotel room was thick and luxurious. 酒店房间里的窗帘厚重而奢华。| synonyms: drapery, draping, hangings | antonyms: unveiling, exposing | noun

curtaining:/ˈkɜːrtənɪŋ/ | v. | 用窗帘遮盖；像窗帘般垂下 |  To cover or enclose with curtains; to hang like a curtain. |  She was curtaining the windows when the doorbell rang.  门铃响的时候她正在挂窗帘。 | synonyms: covering, screening, draping | antonyms: uncovering, exposing | verb
