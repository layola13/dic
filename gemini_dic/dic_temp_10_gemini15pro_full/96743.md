ineligibly:/ɪnˈɛlɪdʒɪbli/
adverb|不合格地，无资格地|in a way that is not eligible; not allowed to do or have something|His name was written ineligibly on the form. 他的名字在表格上写得难以辨认，不符合资格。|近义词: illegibly, unreadably, improperly|反义词: legibly, clearly, properly|词性: 副词

ineligible:/ɪnˈɛlɪdʒəbl/
adjective|不合格的，无资格的|not allowed to do or have something|He was ineligible for the competition because he was too young.  因为他太年轻了，所以没有资格参加比赛。|近义词: disqualified, unfit, unsuitable|反义词: eligible, qualified, suitable|词性: 形容词

词源:
From in- (not) + eligible (suitable, qualified), from Latin *eligere (to choose), from ex- (out) + legere (to choose, gather, read).
