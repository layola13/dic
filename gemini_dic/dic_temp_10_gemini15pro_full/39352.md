conjugacy:/ˈkɒndʒʊɡəsi/ (UK) /ˈkɑːndʒəɡəsi/ (US)| n. |共轭性|The state of being conjugate.  A relationship between things that are similar in some way, especially in mathematics and chemistry.  It often involves a transformation that preserves certain properties. |The conjugacy of these two subgroups is a key element of the proof. 这两个子群的共轭性是证明的关键要素。|近义词：relationship, connection, correspondence|反义词：dissimilarity, disparity|noun
