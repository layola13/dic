heartbroken:/ˈhɑːrtˌbroʊkən/| adj.|心碎的，悲痛欲绝的|Overwhelmed by grief or sorrow, especially due to romantic loss or rejection.|He was heartbroken when she left him. 她离开他时，他心碎了。|Near synonyms: devastated, grief-stricken, inconsolable|Near antonyms: joyful, happy, elated|adjective
