bezoar:/ˈbiːzɔːr/ | n. | 胃石，毛团 | A mass of undigested material, usually hair or plant fibers, that accumulates in the stomach or intestines of some animals, especially ruminants. |  A bezoar was found in the cow's stomach during the necropsy.  在尸检过程中，牛的胃里发现了一块胃石。

The museum displayed a large bezoar taken from a horse.  博物馆展出了一块从马身上取下的大胃石。 | synonym: calculus, concretion | antonym: N/A | noun
