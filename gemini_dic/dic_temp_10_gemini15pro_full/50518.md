deprostrate: /ˌdiːˈprɒstreɪt/ /ˌdiːˈprɑːstreɪt/
verb
使俯卧；使拜倒；使屈服
to cast oneself face downward in humility or adoration; to reduce to extreme weakness or helplessness
The worshippers deprostrated themselves before the altar.  信徒们在祭坛前俯伏叩拜。
The illness deprostrated him for weeks.  这场病使他虚弱了好几周。
近义词: prostrate, abase, humble
反义词: elevate, exalt
词性: verb


deprostrate: /ˌdiːˈprɒstreɪt/ /ˌdiːˈprɑːstreɪt/
adjective
俯卧的；拜倒的；屈服的
lying flat, especially face downward, in humility or adoration; completely overcome or helpless
The deprostrate figure lay before the king.  俯卧的身影躺在国王面前。
She was deprostrate with grief.  她悲痛欲绝。
近义词: prostrate, abject, submissive
反义词: upright, erect
词性: adjective


词源: From Latin *deprostratus*, past participle of *deprostrare*, from *de-* (down) + *prostrare* (to throw down, prostrate).
