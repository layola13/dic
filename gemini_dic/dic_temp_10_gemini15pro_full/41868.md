coscript:/ˈkoʊskrɪpt/| n.|共用稿，合作稿|A script written collaboratively; a joint script.|Example 1: The play was a coscript, blending the talents of two renowned writers. 这部戏剧是一个合作稿，融合了两位著名作家的才华。

Example 2:  They decided to create a coscript for their film project, sharing the writing responsibilities. 他们决定为他们的电影项目创建一个合作稿，共同承担写作责任。|近义词：joint script, collaborative script|反义词: solo script|词性:名词

Etymology:  "co-" (prefix meaning "jointly," "together") + "script" (from Latin "scriptum," meaning "something written").
