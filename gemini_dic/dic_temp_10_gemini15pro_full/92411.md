hypogeum:/ˈhaɪpəˈdʒiːəm/| n. |地下墓穴，地下室|An underground chamber, often used as a tomb or for religious purposes.  A subterranean structure, especially one used for burial in ancient times. |例句：The archaeologists discovered a hypogeum filled with ancient artifacts. 考古学家发现了一个充满古代文物的地下墓穴。

例句：The hypogeum served as a secret meeting place for the early Christians. 这个地下墓穴曾是早期基督徒的秘密聚会场所。|近义词：crypt, catacomb, vault, tomb|反义词:surface structure|词性:noun
