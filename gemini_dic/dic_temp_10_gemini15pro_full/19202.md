bewrayment:/bɪˈreɪmənt/
n.
背叛；泄露；暴露
The act of betraying someone or something, or the state of being betrayed.  A disclosure of something secret or private.
His sudden change of allegiance was seen as a bewrayment of his former comrades. 他突然改变立场被视为对以前同志的背叛。
Her flushed cheeks bewrayed her embarrassment. 她涨红的脸颊暴露了她的尴尬。
近义词: betrayal, treachery, disclosure, exposure
反义词: loyalty, fidelity, secrecy, concealment
词性: 名词
