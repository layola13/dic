municipals:/mjuːˈnɪsɪpəlz/
|n.|市政债券|Debt securities issued by state and local governments, generally to finance public projects such as schools, highways, and sewers.  These are often tax-exempt.|Investors are drawn to municipals because of their tax-exempt status.|Synonyms: municipal bonds, munis|Antonyms: corporates (corporate bonds), federal bonds|noun
