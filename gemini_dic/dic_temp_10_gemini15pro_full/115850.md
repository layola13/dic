markovian:/mɑːrˈkoʊviən/ /mɑːrˈkoʊviən/| adj.|马尔可夫的，马尔科夫的|Relating to or based on a Markov process, a stochastic model where the future state depends only on the current state and not on the sequence of events that preceded it.  This means the system has "no memory" of past events.|The stock market is often modeled as a Markovian process, assuming today's price fully reflects all past information.  股票市场通常被建模为马尔可夫过程，假设今天的价格充分反映了所有过去的信息。

The weather can be simplified as a Markovian system for short-term forecasting. 天气可以简化为马尔可夫系统用于短期预测。|近义词：memoryless, stochastic|反义词:deterministic|词性:adjective
