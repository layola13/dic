malignant:/məˈlɪɡnənt/| adj.|恶性的；有害的；怀有恶意| (of a disease) very virulent or infectious. Tending to produce death or deterioration. Showing deep-seated ill will or hatred.|例句：She was diagnosed with a malignant tumor. 她被诊断出患有恶性肿瘤。

例句：He gave her a malignant look. 他恶狠狠地瞪了她一眼。|近义词：cancerous, malevolent, harmful, destructive, pernicious|反义词：benign, benevolent, harmless|词性:形容词

malignancy:/məˈlɪɡnənsi/| n.|恶性；恶意|The state or presence of a malignant tumor; intense ill will or hatred.|例句：The malignancy of the tumor was confirmed by the biopsy. 活检证实了肿瘤的恶性。

例句：His malignancy towards his rivals was well known.  他对竞争对手的恶意是众所周知的。|近义词：malevolence, spite, venom|反义词：benevolence, kindness|词性:名词
