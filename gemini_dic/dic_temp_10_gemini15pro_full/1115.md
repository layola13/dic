accessor:/ˈæksesər/| n.|访问器，存取器|A piece of software or hardware that allows you to access something.  It often refers to a method in programming that gets or sets the value of a private field.|This class provides accessors for all its member variables.  这个类为其所有成员变量提供了访问器。|synonyms: getter, setter, interface|antonyms: N/A|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From access + -or. Ultimately from Latin accedere "to approach".

USAGE->
In programming:
The accessor methods allow you to modify the private data of the class. = 访问器方法允许您修改类的私有数据。

The `getName()` accessor retrieves the value of the name field. =  `getName()` 访问器检索 name 字段的值。

In general usage:
He used an accessor to connect his camera to the computer. = 他使用了一个连接器将他的相机连接到电脑上。

She needed a special accessor to open the locked cabinet. = 她需要一个特殊的工具来打开锁着的柜子。

PHRASE->
