mapouka:/məˈpuːkə/
noun
马普卡舞（一种起源于科特迪瓦的性感挑逗舞蹈）
A provocative dance originating from Côte d'Ivoire, often considered sexually suggestive.
Mapouka is a traditional dance from Côte d'Ivoire. 马普卡舞是科特迪瓦的一种传统舞蹈。
The mapouka dance is known for its suggestive movements. 马普卡舞以其暗示性的动作而闻名。
synonym:  Côte d'Ivoire dance, Ivorian dance
antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "formal dance" or "ballet")
