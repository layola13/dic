fry-ups:/ˈfraɪ ʌps/
noun
油煎食品; 煎炸食物
A meal of fried food, typically bacon, eggs, sausages, tomatoes, and mushrooms.
例句：We had a full English fry-up for breakfast. 我们早餐吃了全套英式煎炸食物。

例句：She loves a good fry-up on a Sunday morning. 她喜欢在星期天早上吃一顿美味的煎炸食物。

近义词：cooked breakfast, full English
反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps "healthy meal," "light meal")
词性:noun
