nonbeneficially:/ˌnɒnbɛnɪˈfɪʃəli/
adverb
无益地，不利地
in a way that does not produce any benefits or advantages.
The funds were managed nonbeneficially, resulting in significant losses. 这些资金管理不善，导致重大损失。
The policy changes affected the community nonbeneficially. 这些政策变化对社区产生了不利影响。
Synonym: detrimentally, harmfully, disadvantageously
Antonym: beneficially, advantageously, profitably
词性: 副词
