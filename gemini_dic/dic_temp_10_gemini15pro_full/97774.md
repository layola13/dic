injectors:/ɪnˈdʒɛktərz/ | n. | 注射器；喷射器 |A device used to inject something, typically a fluid, into something else. | The diesel engine's injectors were faulty, causing poor performance. 柴油发动机的喷油器出现故障，导致性能下降。

The doctor used a syringe as an injector to administer the vaccine. 医生用注射器作为注射器来注射疫苗。 | synonyms: syringe, nozzle, needle, sprayer | antonyms: extractor, remover | noun
