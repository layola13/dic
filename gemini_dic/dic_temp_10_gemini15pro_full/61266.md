emarginately:/ɪˈmɑːrdʒɪnətli/ | adj. | 边缘地，略微地 | Having a margin; slightly notched at the apex, as a leaf.  Characterized by having a shallow notch at the tip. | The leaf is emarginately shaped. 这片叶子的形状略带缺刻。 | 近义词: marginally, slightly, faintly | 反义词: centrally, deeply | 词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *emarginatus*, past participle of *emarginare* "to take away the margin," from *e-* "out" + *marginare* "to furnish with a border," from *margo* "edge, border."
