overextravagant: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈstrævəɡənt/
adjective
过度奢侈的；过分铺张的
excessively extravagant; spending more than is reasonable or necessary.
Her overextravagant lifestyle eventually led to bankruptcy. 她过度奢侈的生活方式最终导致了破产。
The party was an overextravagant affair, with mountains of food and rivers of champagne.  这个派对过于铺张，食物堆积如山，香槟酒更是源源不断。
近义词: lavish, exorbitant, immoderate, excessive
反义词: frugal, economical, modest, thrifty
词性: adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "extravagant" (from Latin "extravagans," present participle of "extravagari" - to wander outside or beyond).
