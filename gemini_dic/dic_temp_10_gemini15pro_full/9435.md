antirealistic:/ˌæntiˌriːəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
反现实的；非写实的
Opposed to or rejecting realism, especially in art or literature.
His paintings are vibrant and antirealistic, exploring dreams and fantasies. 他的画作充满活力，反现实主义，探索梦想和幻想。
近义词: unrealistic, nonrealistic, surreal, idealistic, fanciful
反义词: realistic, naturalistic, factual, pragmatic
词性: adjective
