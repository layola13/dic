despairer:/dɪˈspeɪrər/
n.
绝望者，感到绝望的人
A person who feels or shows despair.
He became a despairer after losing everything in the fire.  他在大火中失去一切后变得绝望。
The despairer sat alone, lost in thought.  绝望者独自坐着，陷入沉思。
Synonyms: pessimist, cynic
Antonyms: optimist, hoper
