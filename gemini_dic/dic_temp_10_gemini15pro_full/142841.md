out-dodging:/ˌaʊtˈdɒdʒɪŋ/
verb
巧妙地躲避，比...更善于躲避
Cleverly avoiding or surpassing someone in dodging.
The fox was out-dodging the hounds with its cunning maneuvers. 狐狸用狡猾的策略巧妙地躲避了猎犬。
Synonyms: outmaneuvering, outsmarting, evading
Antonyms: confronting, facing, surrendering
