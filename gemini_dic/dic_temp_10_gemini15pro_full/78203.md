geotropic:/ˌdʒiːəˈtrɒpɪk/ /ˌdʒiːoʊˈtrɑːpɪk/ | adj. |向地性的；趋地性的 | Influenced by gravity; growing or turning towards or away from the earth. | The geotropic roots grow downwards into the soil.  植物的向地性根向下生长到土壤里。|  The plant exhibited strong geotropic curvature.  这种植物表现出强烈的向地性弯曲。 | 近义词: gravitropic | 反义词: apogeotropic | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From geo- (earth) + -tropic (turning), from Greek gē (earth) + tropos (turn).
