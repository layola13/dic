overweeningnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈwiːnɪŋnɪsɪz/
|n.|傲慢自大；狂妄自负；|The state or quality of being overweening; excessive arrogance or presumption.|His overweeningnesses alienated his colleagues. 他的傲慢自大疏远了他的同事。|Synonyms: arrogance, haughtiness, conceit, presumptuousness|Antonyms: humility, modesty, humbleness|noun


overweening:/ˌoʊvərˈwiːnɪŋ/
|adj.|傲慢的；自负的；过于自信的|Showing excessive confidence or pride.|He had an overweening ambition to become president. 他有一个过分的野心，想成为总统。|Synonyms: arrogant, haughty, conceited, presumptuous|Antonyms: humble, modest|adjective


词源:
From Middle English *overwening, from Old English oferwenian (“to be arrogant, presume”), from ofer (“over”) + wenian (“to accustom, wean”).
