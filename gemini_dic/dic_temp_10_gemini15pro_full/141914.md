ordinal:/ˈɔːrdɪnəl/ | adj. n. | 序数的，序数 |  Relating to a position in a series, or denoting such a position; a number that tells the position of something in a list. |  She came in third, winning the bronze medal. 她获得了第三名，赢得了铜牌。|  The ordinal number for 100 is one hundredth. 100的序数词是第一百。| synonym: ranking, numbered | antonym: cardinal | adjective, noun
