nimbyism:/ˈnɪmbɪɪzəm/
n.
邻避症候群；邻避主义
NIMBY is an acronym for "not in my backyard." It describes the phenomenon where people oppose the development of projects or facilities that they believe will negatively impact their local area, even if they recognize the need for such projects elsewhere.
The proposed construction of a new prison was met with fierce nimbyism from residents.  居民们强烈反对拟建的新监狱，表现出典型的邻避主义。
Local nimbyism has delayed the construction of the much-needed waste recycling plant. 当地居民的邻避主义拖延了急需的垃圾回收厂的建设。
近义词: localism, parochialism
反义词:  None (The opposite would be supporting development even if it affects one's own area, but there isn't a single established word for this.)
词性: noun

Etymology:  Acronym from "not in my backyard" (1980s)
