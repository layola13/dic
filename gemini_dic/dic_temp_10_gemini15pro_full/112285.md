lopsided:/ˌlɒpˈsaɪdɪd/ | adj. | 不平衡的，倾向一方的 |Heavier, larger, or more developed on one side than on the other; unevenly balanced. | The painting hung lopsided on the wall.  这幅画歪歪斜斜地挂在墙上。| unbalanced, uneven, asymmetrical, askew | balanced, symmetrical, even, straight | adjective
