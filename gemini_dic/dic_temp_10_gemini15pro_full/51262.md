desubsidizing:/diːˈsʌbsɪdaɪzɪŋ/
verb
取消补贴，停止资助
The act of removing or reducing a subsidy.  The government is desubsidizing the farming industry.
The government is desubsidizing the farming industry. 政府正在取消对农业的补贴。
Many farmers are worried about the desubsidizing of their crops. 许多农民担心他们的作物补贴会被取消。
synonyms: defunding, withdrawing funding, phasing out subsidies
antonyms: subsidizing, funding, supporting, sponsoring
