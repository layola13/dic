nonjuridically:/ˌnɒnˌdʒʊrɪˈdɪkəli/
adv.
非司法地；在法律之外
Not legally; outside the scope of the law.
The matter was settled nonjuridically, through mediation. 这件事是通过调解，在法律之外解决的。
They agreed to handle the dispute nonjuridically. 他们同意在法律框架外处理这一纠纷。
近义词: extrajudicially, informally
反义词: judicially, legally
词性: 副词
