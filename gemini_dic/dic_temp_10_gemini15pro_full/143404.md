outflames:/ˌaʊtˈfleɪmz/
verb
燃烧得比...更旺；胜过
burn more brightly or intensely than; surpass
The bonfire outflamed the nearby streetlights.  篝火比附近的街灯更明亮。
Her passion for art outflamed all her other interests. 她对艺术的热情胜过所有其他兴趣。
近义词: outshine, eclipse, surpass, exceed
反义词: pale, dim, dwindle

词性:v.
