nondistracted:/ˌnɒndɪˈstræktɪd/
adjective
未分心的，专注的
Not distracted; able to concentrate fully.
She remained nondistracted despite the noise. 她尽管噪音很大，仍然保持专注。
He worked in a nondistracted manner. 他以一种专注的方式工作。
近义词: focused, attentive, concentrated
反义词: distracted, inattentive, unfocused
词性: adjective

词源:
Prefix "non-" (not) + "distracted" (past participle of "distract," from Latin *distractus*, past participle of *distrahere* "draw apart," from *dis-* "apart" + *trahere* "to draw").
