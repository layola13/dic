注意事项:
Eau is not commonly used in modern English to refer to plain water.  It's primarily used in phrases like eau de toilette, eau de parfum, etc., referring to fragrances or other liquids.  Using "eau" for plain water would sound affected or pretentious in most everyday conversations.
