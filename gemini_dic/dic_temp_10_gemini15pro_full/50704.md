dermoid:/ˈdɜːrmɔɪd/| adj. n.|皮样囊肿的，皮样囊肿|A dermoid cyst is a sac-like growth containing structures such as hair, fluid, teeth, or skin glands that are present at birth.  It forms from embryonic cells.|例句：The surgeon removed a dermoid cyst from the patient's ovary.  外科医生从病人的卵巢中取出一个皮样囊肿。

例句：Dermoid cysts are usually benign. 皮样囊肿通常是良性的。|近义词：teratoma|反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a specific medical term)|词性: adjective, noun
