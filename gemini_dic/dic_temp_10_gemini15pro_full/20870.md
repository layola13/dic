blavatsky:/bləˈvætski/
n.
布拉瓦茨基夫人
Helena Petrovna Blavatsky (1831-1891), Russian occultist, philosopher, and author who co-founded the Theosophical Society.  She is best known for her works *Isis Unveiled* and *The Secret Doctrine*.
Blavatsky is considered one of the founders of modern Theosophy.
Synonyms:  Madame Blavatsky, HPB
No real antonyms exist, as it's a proper noun.
