outhire:/ˌaʊtˈhaɪər/
verb
外包；外聘
To contract work to an external organization or individual.
Many companies outhire their IT services to save costs. 许多公司将他们的IT服务外包以节省成本。
The company decided to outhire the manufacturing of its new product.  这家公司决定将其新产品的制造外包。
