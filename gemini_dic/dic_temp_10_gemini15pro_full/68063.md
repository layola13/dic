facundity:/fəˈkʌndɪti/
n.
流利；雄辩；多产；丰富
the quality of being able to express yourself easily and well
例句：He spoke with the natural facundity of a born orator. 他天生就是一个演说家，说起话来流畅自如。

例句：The writer displayed great facundity in her descriptions of the natural world.  这位作家在描写自然世界时展现了非凡的表达能力。

近义词：eloquence, fluency, articulateness, prolificness, abundance, copiousness
反义词：inarticulateness, taciturnity, barrenness, scarcity
词性: noun
