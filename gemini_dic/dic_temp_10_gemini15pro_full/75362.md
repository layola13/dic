frothless:/ˈfrɒθlɪs/| adj.|无泡沫的，不起泡沫的|Lacking froth or foam; not producing froth or foam.|The beer was disappointingly frothless.|近义词:flat, still|反义词:frothy, foamy|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From froth + -less.  Froth comes from Old Norse *froða.  -less is a common Old English suffix meaning "without".
