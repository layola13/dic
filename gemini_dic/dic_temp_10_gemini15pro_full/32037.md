cherishingly:/ˈtʃɛrɪʃɪŋli/
adv.
珍爱地，爱惜地
Holding the fragile ornament cherishingly, she recalled the day her grandmother gave it to her.  珍爱地捧着易碎的装饰品，她回想起祖母把它送给她的那一天。
He spoke of his childhood home cherishingly, his voice full of warmth and nostalgia. 他深情地谈起他童年的家，声音充满了温暖和怀旧之情。
Etymology: From cherish (v.) + -ing (present participle suffix) + -ly (adverb suffix).  来自cherish（动词）+ -ing（现在分词后缀）+ -ly（副词后缀）。
Synonyms: tenderly, lovingly, affectionately, dearly
Antonyms: carelessly, indifferently, negligently
词性: 副词 (adverb)
