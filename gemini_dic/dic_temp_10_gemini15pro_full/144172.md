outshows:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊz/
verb
胜过，优于，比...更出色
surpass in performance or showiness; outdo
Her performance outshowed all the others. 她的表演胜过所有其他人。
近义词: surpass, excel, outdo, outshine, outperform
反义词: underperform, fall short of
词性:动词
