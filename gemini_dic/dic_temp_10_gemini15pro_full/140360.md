oil:/ɔɪl/ (US), /ɔɪl/ (UK)| n. vt.|油，石油；润滑油；油画颜料；给…加润滑油|A viscous liquid derived from petroleum, especially for use as a fuel or lubricant.  Also, a paint made with oil, or the act of applying oil.|The car needs an oil change. 这辆车需要换机油。|He oiled the hinges to stop them squeaking. 他给铰链上了油，以免它们发出吱吱声。|synonyms: petroleum, lubricant, fuel, grease|antonyms: water, dryness|noun, verb
