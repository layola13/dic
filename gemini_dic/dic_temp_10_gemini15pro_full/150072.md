palsylike:/ˈpɔːlziˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 类似麻痹的，像瘫痪的 | Resembling or suggestive of palsy; exhibiting weakness or uncontrolled tremors. | His palsylike grip on the pen made it difficult to write. 他那像瘫痪般无力的手握着笔，使他难以书写。|  His hand had a palsylike tremor. 他的手像得了麻痹一样颤抖。 | 近义词: palsied, paralytic, paretic | 反义词: strong, steady, coordinated | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From palsy +‎ -like.  Palsy itself comes from Middle English palesie, from Old French palasie, and ultimately from Medieval Latin paralysis, from Greek παράλυσις (parálusis, “paralysis”).
