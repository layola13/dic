impetuousness:/ɪmˈpɛtʃʊəsnəs/| n. |冲动，鲁莽|The quality of being impetuous; acting or done quickly and without thought or care.|His impetuousness led him to make a hasty decision. 他的冲动让他做出了草率的决定。|Synonyms: impulsiveness, rashness, recklessness, hastiness|Antonyms: caution, prudence, deliberation, forethought|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impetuositas, from Latin impetuosus (“impetuous”), from impetus (“attack, assault, onset; impulse, impetus”) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives indicating fullness or abundance).
