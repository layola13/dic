nonelopements:/ˌnɒnɪˈləʊpmənts/
n.
私奔的反义词；未私奔；未私奔事件
The act of not eloping; instances where couples choose a traditional wedding ceremony instead of running away to get married secretly.
Many couples are choosing nonelopements and opting for large, traditional weddings. 许多情侣选择不私奔，而选择大型的传统婚礼。
After careful consideration, they decided on a nonelopement and a big celebration with family and friends. 经过仔细考虑，他们决定不私奔，而与家人朋友一起举行盛大的庆祝活动。
近义词: traditional weddings, conventional weddings, formal weddings
反义词: elopements
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
