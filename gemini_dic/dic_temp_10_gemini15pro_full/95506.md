inclined:/ɪnˈklaɪnd/| adj.|倾向的，倾斜的|feeling willing or favorably disposed toward something; sloping.|例句：I'm inclined to agree with you. 我倾向于同意你的观点。

例句：The land is inclined towards the river.  这块地向河边倾斜。

近义词：disposed, prone, apt, likely, slanted, sloping|反义词：disinclined, averse, level, flat|词性：形容词
