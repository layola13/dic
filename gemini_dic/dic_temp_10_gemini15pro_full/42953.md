coverchief: /ˈkʌvərˌtʃiːf/
n.
头巾，头盖
A cloth used to cover the head.
She wore a colorful coverchief tied around her head. 她头上系着一条色彩鲜艳的头巾。
Synonyms: headscarf, head covering, kerchief
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps uncovered hair?)
noun
