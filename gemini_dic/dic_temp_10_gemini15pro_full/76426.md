gaiety:/ˈɡeɪəti/| n.|欢乐，愉快，庆祝活动|A feeling of cheerfulness and excitement.|The festival atmosphere filled the streets with gaiety. 节日的气氛让街道充满了欢乐。|近义词：merriment, mirth, cheerfulness, festivity, jollity|反义词：gloom, sadness, melancholy, dejection, sorrow|词性:名词
Etymology: From Old French "gaiete" meaning "gaiety, mirth," from "gai."  Ultimately from Frankish "*gahi," meaning "joyful, merry."
