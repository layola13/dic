noncensored:/nɒnˈsɛnsəd/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɛnsərd/ | adj.|未经审查的，未经删节的|Not having been censored; containing all original material, including potentially offensive or sensitive content.|The noncensored version of the film contains graphic violence. 这部电影的未删减版包含了大量的暴力画面。|近义词：uncut, unexpurgated, unedited, raw|反义词: censored, expurgated, bowdlerized|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From non- + censored.  "Censor" comes from Latin "censor," a Roman official who conducted the census and supervised public morals.
