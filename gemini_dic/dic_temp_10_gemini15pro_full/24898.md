bruisewort:/ˈbruːzwɜːrt/| n. |康复力，紫草科植物，疗伤草|A perennial plant (Symphytum officinale) of the borage family, having hairy leaves and small purplish or yellowish flowers, and formerly used medicinally.|The bruisewort was traditionally used to heal bruises and wounds.|近义词：comfrey, knitbone, boneset|反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑表示“毒药”或“有害植物”的词汇，例如: poison ivy)|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Old English *brūsewyrt, from brūse (“bruise”) + wyrt (“wort, plant”).  The word "wort" was commonly used in Old English as a suffix denoting a plant used for medicinal purposes.
