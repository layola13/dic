bonnet:/ˈbɒnɪt/ (UK) /ˈbɑːnɪt/ (US)| n.|帽子；引擎盖；阀盖|A type of hat, typically tied under the chin and worn by babies and young children, or by women and girls, often forming part of a historical costume.
