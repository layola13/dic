headlongnesses:/ˈhɛdˌlɔŋnɪsɪz/
n.
轻率，鲁莽，仓促
The quality or state of being headlong; rashness, impetuosity.
His headlongnesses led him into trouble. 他的轻率让他陷入了麻烦。
近义词：rashness, recklessness, impetuosity, haste
反义词：caution, prudence, deliberation
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From headlong + -ness.  Headlong itself comes from Old English *hēafodlong, from hēafod "head" + long "long."  The suffix -ness forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
