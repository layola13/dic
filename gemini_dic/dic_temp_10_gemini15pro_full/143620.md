outjet:/ˈaʊtdʒɛt/
n.
喷出;喷射;喷出物
an outflow of gas or liquid; something that is outjetted
例句：The outjet of water from the pipe was strong. 水管喷出的水很强。
例句：The volcanic outjet created a new island. 火山喷发物形成了一个新岛。
近义词：outflow, ejection, discharge, emission
反义词：inflow, intake

词性:n.
