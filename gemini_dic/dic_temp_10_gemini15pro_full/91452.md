hygroplasm:/ˈhaɪɡroʊplæzəm/
n.
细胞质液体;透明质
The liquid portion of the cytoplasm.  The fluid portion of the protoplasm surrounding the nucleus and organelles of a cell.
The metabolic reactions of the cell take place in the hygroplasm. 细胞的代谢反应发生在透明质中。

Etymology:
From hygro- +‎ -plasm, from Greek *ὑγρός (hugrós, “wet, moist, fluid”) +‎ -plasm.
