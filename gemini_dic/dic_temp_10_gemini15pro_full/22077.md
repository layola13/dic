bollard:/ˈbɒləd/ (British) /ˈbɑːlərd/ (American)| n. |系船柱；护柱；路障|A short, thick post on a wharf or ship used to secure mooring lines; a similar post set in the ground to prevent the passage of vehicles.|The ship was tied to the bollard.  这艘船被系在系船柱上。

The bollards prevented cars from entering the pedestrian zone.  护柱阻止车辆进入步行区。|近义词：post, pillar, marker|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "opening" in the context of blocking passage)|noun
