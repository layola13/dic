outwearied: /ˌaʊtˈwiːrid/ | adj. | 极其疲惫的，精疲力竭的 | Exhausted, worn out, especially from prolonged exertion or strain. |  The outwearied travelers finally reached their destination.  精疲力竭的旅行者们终于到达了目的地。 | The outwearied soldiers collapsed after the long march.  精疲力竭的士兵们在长途行军后倒下了。 |  exhausted, fatigued, worn out, spent, drained | refreshed, energetic, invigorated | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *outwerien, from Old English *ūtwerigan (“to tire out, weary”), equivalent to out- + weary.
