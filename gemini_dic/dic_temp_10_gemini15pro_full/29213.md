cask:/kæsk/| n.|木桶，桶装酒|A large container, made of wood, metal, or plastic, that is used for storing liquids, especially alcoholic drinks. |The wine was aged in oak casks.  这葡萄酒是在橡木桶中陈酿的。|Synonyms: barrel, keg, vat|Antonyms: None|noun

cask:/kæsk/| vt.|把…装入桶内|To put something into a cask. |They cask the wine after fermentation.  发酵后他们把酒装入桶中。|Synonyms: barrel|Antonyms: None|verb
