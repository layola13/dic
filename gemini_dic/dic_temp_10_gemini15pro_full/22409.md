boohoos:/ˈbuːhuːz/
|n.|哭闹，假哭|An exaggerated display of grief or distress, often feigned.  A fit of crying and complaining.|例句：The child's boohoos could be heard throughout the store.  孩子的哭闹声响彻整个商店。

例句：Stop your boohoos; it's just a small scratch.  别哭了，只是一个小划痕。|近义词：crying, sobbing, wailing, whining|反义词:laughter, cheer, joy|词性:名词

词源:Likely imitative of the sound of crying.  很可能是模仿哭泣的声音。
