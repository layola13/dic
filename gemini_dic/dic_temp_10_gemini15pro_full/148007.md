overrigidnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪdʒɪdnəsɪz/
|n.|过度僵硬；过度刻板|The overrigidnesses of the system stifled innovation. 这个系统过于僵化，扼杀了创新。|例句：The overrigidnesses of his upbringing led to difficulties in adapting to new situations. 他过于刻板的教养导致他难以适应新环境。

The overrigidnesses in the company's policies prevented employees from taking initiative. 公司政策的过度僵化阻碍了员工发挥主动性。|近义词：inflexibility, rigidity, stiffness|反义词：flexibility, adaptability, pliability|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
over- + rigid + -ness + -es
"over-"  prefix meaning "excessively"
"rigid" (adjective) from Latin "rigidus" meaning "stiff, hard, inflexible"
"-ness" suffix forming nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality
"-es" suffix forming plural nouns
