chilblain:/ˈtʃɪlbleɪn/ (US), /ˈtʃɪlbleɪn/ (UK)| n.|冻疮|A painful, itchy swelling on the fingers, toes, or ears, caused by prolonged exposure to cold, but not freezing, temperatures.|My fingers got chilblains after playing in the snow without gloves.  我的手指在没戴手套玩雪后得了冻疮。|Synonyms: pernio, frostnip|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider "warmth," "heat")|noun

chilblain:/ˈtʃɪlbleɪn/ (US), /ˈtʃɪlbleɪn/ (UK)| adj.|冻疮的，生冻疮的|(Less common usage) Affected with chilblains.|Her chilblain fingers were very painful. 她的长了冻疮的手指很疼。|Synonyms: (No common synonyms in adjectival form)|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|adjective
