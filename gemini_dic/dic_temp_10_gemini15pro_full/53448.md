disapprobatory:/dɪsəˈprɒbətəri/ | adj. | 不赞成的，表示反对的 | Expressing disapproval; condemnatory. |  She gave him a disapprobatory look. 她不赞成地看了他一眼。|  He shook his head in a disapprobatory manner. 他不赞成地摇了摇头。| 近义词: disapproving, condemnatory, censorious | 反义词: approving, commendatory, laudatory | 词性: adjective

disapproving:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːvɪŋ/ | adj. | 不赞成的，表示反对的 | Showing that you do not approve of something or someone. |  She cast a disapproving glance at her son's messy room. 她不赞许地瞥了一眼儿子凌乱的房间。 | He gave a disapproving frown. 他不赞成地皱起了眉头。 | 近义词: disapprobatory, censorious, critical | 反义词: approving, supportive, encouraging | 词性: adjective
