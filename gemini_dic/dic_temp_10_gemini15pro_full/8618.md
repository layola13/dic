anticolonialism:/ˌæntiˈkəˈloʊniəlɪzəm/| n. |反殖民主义|Opposition to the creation or maintenance of colonies, especially in opposition to the political control of one country by another.|例句：The rise of anticolonialism after World War II led to the independence of many nations. 二战后反殖民主义的兴起导致许多国家独立。

例句：Her research focuses on the literature of anticolonialism. 她的研究重点是反殖民主义文学。|近义词：decolonization, independence, self-determination|反义词:colonialism, imperialism|词性:noun
