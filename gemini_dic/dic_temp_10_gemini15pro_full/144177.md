outshrill:/ˌaʊtˈʃrɪl/
verb
盖过…的尖声；叫喊得比…响亮
to shout louder than someone or something else
The protesters' chants outshrilled the police sirens. 抗议者的呼喊声盖过了警车的警笛声。
Synonyms: outshout, drown out, overpower
Antonyms: be drowned out by, be quieter than
词性: 动词
