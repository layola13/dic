nut:/nʌt/| n.|坚果；螺母；难对付的人|A small dry fruit with a hard shell, or the hard shell itself. Also, a crazy or eccentric person. |例句：Squirrels bury nuts for the winter. 松鼠冬天埋藏坚果。

|例句：He's a tough nut to crack. 他是个难对付的家伙。|近义词：seed, kernel, nutcase (for a person)|反义词: None (for the food sense), sane (for the person sense)|词性:noun


nut:/nʌt/| v.|采坚果|To gather nuts.|例句：They went nutting in the woods. 他们在树林里采坚果。

|例句：We were nutting all afternoon. 我们采了一下午的坚果。|近义词：forage, gather|反义词: None|词性:verb
