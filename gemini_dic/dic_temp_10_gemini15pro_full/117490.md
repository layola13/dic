meds:/mɛdz/
n.
药物;药;药品(medication的非正式说法)
Medication in the form of pills, tablets, or liquids.
I forgot to take my meds this morning. 我今天早上忘了吃药了。
synonyms: medicine, drugs, pills, tablets
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for medication, perhaps consider "remedies" which could encompass non-drug treatments, or "placebo")
noun
