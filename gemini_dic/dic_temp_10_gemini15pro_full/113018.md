lumbriciform:/lʌmˈbrɪsɪfɔːrm/
adjective
蚯蚓状的；像蚯蚓的
Resembling an earthworm in shape; worm-shaped.
The lumbriciform larva burrowed through the soil.  蚯蚓状的幼虫在土壤中挖洞。
The parasite had a lumbriciform body, wriggling its way through the tissue.  这种寄生虫的身体呈蚯蚓状，在组织中蠕动。
近义词:vermiform, wormlike
反义词: (No direct antonym, could describe opposite shapes, e.g., spherical, flat)
