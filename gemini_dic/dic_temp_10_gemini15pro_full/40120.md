contemplate:/ˈkɑːntəmplˌeɪt/ /ˈkɒntəmˌpleɪt/| v. |沉思；仔细考虑；打算；凝视|To think profoundly and at length; to look at or consider thoughtfully.|She sat by the window, contemplating the falling leaves. 她坐在窗边，凝视着落叶。|He contemplated the meaning of life. 他思考着人生的意义。|近义词：ponder, consider, meditate, muse, reflect|反义词：ignore, disregard|词性:动词
