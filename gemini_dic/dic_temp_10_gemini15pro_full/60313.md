ego:/ˈiːɡoʊ/ (US), /ˈiːɡəʊ/ (UK)| n.|自我，自负，自尊心|The part of the mind that mediates between the conscious and the unconscious and is responsible for reality testing and a sense of personal identity.  It is often referred to as the "self" and is associated with self-esteem, self-importance, and a sense of individuality.|He has a huge ego. 他非常自负。|Synonyms: self-esteem, self-importance, pride, vanity|Antonyms: humility, modesty|noun
