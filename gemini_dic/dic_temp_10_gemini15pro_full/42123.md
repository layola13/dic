cotton-to:/ˈkɒtn tuː/ (US) /ˈkɒtn tuː/ (UK)| phrasal verb|喜欢，亲近，适应|To begin to like or become fond of someone or something; to become accustomed to or comfortable with something.|

例句：
She didn't cotton to the new manager right away.
她并没有马上喜欢上新经理。

It took him a while to cotton to the idea of living in a big city.
他花了一段时间才适应在大城市生活。


近义词：take to, warm to, become fond of|
反义词：dislike, abhor, detest|
词性：短语动词|
