aconite:/ˈækəˌnaɪt/ | n. |乌头，附子|A poisonous plant of the buttercup family, with purplish-blue or yellow flowers. The roots and extracts of certain species are used in herbal medicine.|The gardener warned us to be careful of the aconite, as it is highly toxic. 园丁警告我们要小心乌头，因为它有剧毒。

Aconite is a beautiful but deadly plant. 乌头是一种美丽但致命的植物。

|近义词：wolfsbane, monkshood|反义词:|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *aconitum*, from Old French *aconit*, from Latin *aconītum*, from Ancient Greek *ἀκόνιτον* (*akóniton*), of unknown origin. Possibly related to *ἀκόνη* (*akónē*, “whetstone”), referring to the plant's use in poisoning arrows.
