bruted:/ˈbruːtɪd/| v. |粗暴对待，强迫 | to treat someone or something brutally or by force | The dictator bruted his way to power.  独裁者用暴力夺取了政权。| 近义词：forced, coerced, bullied | 反义词:coaxed, persuaded | verb

bruted:/ˈbruːtɪd/| adj. |（仅用于被动语态）被粗暴对待的 | treated brutally |  He felt bruted and humiliated. 他感到自己被粗暴对待，受尽屈辱。 | 近义词：mistreated, abused | 反义词:cherished, protected | adjective
