ginhouse:/ˈdʒɪnhaʊs/| n. |杜松子酒厂；非法酿酒厂|A place where gin is made, especially illegally.|The police raided the ginhouse and confiscated all the equipment. 警察突袭了这家非法酿酒厂，并没收了所有设备。|近义词：distillery, still|反义词: None|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From gin + house.  "Gin" refers to the spirit, and "house" refers to the building where it is produced. The term gained notoriety during the Gin Craze in 18th-century England, a period of widespread gin consumption, often in illicitly produced and sold gin.
