The plural form of mustiness, referring to multiple instances or sources of a stale, damp, and often unpleasant smell.
The old books in the attic had a distinct mustinesses about them.  阁楼里的旧书有一股明显的霉味。

Synonyms: staleness, dampness, fustiness, moldiness
Antonyms: freshness, cleanness, sweetness
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From musty + -ness + -es. Musty comes from Middle English *musti, from Old French moisi "moldy," past participle of moisir "to grow moldy," from Vulgar Latin *mucire, of uncertain origin.
