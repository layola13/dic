ails:/eɪlz/| v.|使苦恼，困扰；（疾病）折磨|To cause someone to suffer physically or mentally; to trouble or afflict.|  He was ailed by a persistent cough. 他一直咳嗽，很痛苦。 |近义词: trouble, bother, afflict, plague|反义词: comfort, soothe, heal|词性:动词

Etymology: From Middle English *ailen, from Old English *eglan, from Proto-Germanic *aglijaną (“to cause pain, trouble”), from Proto-Indo-European *agʰ- (“to drive, draw, move”). Related to Old Norse egle (“to annoy”), and possibly to Old English egle (“troublesome”).
