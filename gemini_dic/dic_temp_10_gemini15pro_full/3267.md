affirmations:/ˌæfərˈmeɪʃənz/ | n. |肯定；断言；确认|Positive statements that can help you to change negative thinking patterns and develop a more optimistic outlook.  They are typically repeated regularly to reinforce positive beliefs about oneself or the world.|She started each day with positive affirmations. 她每天以积极的肯定开始新的一天。| I repeat affirmations to myself every morning to boost my confidence. 我每天早上对自己重复肯定语来增强自信。|synonyms: assertions, declarations, pronouncements|antonyms: denials, negations, contradictions|noun

affirmation:/ˌæfərˈmeɪʃən/ | n. |肯定；断言；确认|The act of affirming or the state of being affirmed; a positive assertion. |The jury's verdict was an affirmation of his innocence. 陪审团的裁决肯定了他的清白。|He nodded in affirmation. 他点头表示肯定。|synonyms: confirmation, validation, corroboration|antonyms: denial, negation, refutation|noun
