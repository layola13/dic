nun:/nʌn/
n.
修女
a woman who is a member of a religious community and lives in a convent
She decided to become a nun after a profound spiritual experience. 她在一场深刻的灵性体验后决定成为一名修女。
近义词：sister
反义词: monk

词源:
From Old English *nunne, from Vulgar Latin *nonna, from Late Latin nonna (“grandmother, nun”), a child's word.  Ultimately of imitative origin. Cognate with Old Frisian nunne, Middle Dutch nonne, German Nonne.
