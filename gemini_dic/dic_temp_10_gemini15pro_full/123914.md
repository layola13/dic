moreland:/ˈmɔːrlənd/ | n. | 荒野；未开垦地；边远地区 | A tract of open or uncultivated upland; wild, high land. |  The sheep grazed on the moreland. 羊群在荒野上吃草. | synonyms: moor, heath, wasteland, wilderness | antonyms: cultivated land, farmland | noun
