circumscribable:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈskraɪbəbl/
adjective
可限制的，可限定的
able to be circumscribed; restricted or limited
The government's power is circumscribable by law. 政府的权力受法律限制。
Her social life was circumscribable due to her illness. 由于生病，她的社交生活受到了限制。
Synonyms: limitable, restrainable, confinable, definable
Antonyms: unlimited, boundless, infinite, unrestricted
词性: adjective
