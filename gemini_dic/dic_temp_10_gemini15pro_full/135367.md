nonpercipiences:/ˌnɒnpərˈsɪpiənsɪz/
n.
无知觉;未能察觉;未能理解
lack of perception or awareness; failure to perceive or understand something.
His nonpercipience of the danger led to the accident. 他未能察觉到危险导致了事故。
近义词:unawareness, ignorance, insensitivity, obliviousness
反义词:awareness, perception, consciousness, sensitivity
词性:名词


Etymology:
From non- + percipience.  "Percipience" comes from the Latin "percipere" meaning "to seize thoroughly, grasp, comprehend," from "per-" (thoroughly) + "capere" (to seize).  The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning.
