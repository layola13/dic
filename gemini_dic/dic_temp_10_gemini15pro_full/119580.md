microwaving:/ˈmaɪkrəˌweɪvɪŋ/

| adj. |微波的; 用于微波炉的|related to or used for microwave ovens|a microwaving dish 一个微波炉适用的盘子|近义词：microwaveable|反义词: non-microwavable|adjective
