flashmob: /ˈflæʃmɒb/ (UK) /ˈflæʃmɑːb/ (US)
| n. |快闪族，快闪行动|A large public gathering at which people perform an unusual or seemingly random act and then disperse, typically organized by means of the internet or social media.|Hundreds of people took part in a flashmob dance in the city center. 数百人在市中心参加了一场快闪舞蹈。|近义词：smart mob, sudden gathering|反义词: N/A|名词

flashmob: /ˈflæʃmɒb/ (UK) /ˈflæʃmɑːb/ (US)
