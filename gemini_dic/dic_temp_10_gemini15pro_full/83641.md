haemolysis:/hiːˈmɒlɪsɪs/ /hiːˈmɑːlɪsɪs/| n.|溶血|The rupture or destruction of red blood cells.|例句：The venom caused rapid haemolysis.  这种毒液引起快速溶血。

例句：The patient exhibited signs of haemolysis after the blood transfusion.  病人在输血后出现溶血的迹象。

近义词：hemolysis (美式拼写), erythrocytolysis|反义词:haemostasis, hemostasis|词性:名词

haemolytic:/ˌhiːməˈlɪtɪk/ /ˌhiːməˈlɑːlɪtɪk/| adj.|溶血的|Relating to, or causing haemolysis.|例句：The snake venom had a haemolytic effect. 蛇毒有溶血作用。

例句：Haemolytic disease of the newborn can be a serious condition. 新生儿溶血症可能是一种严重的疾病。|近义词：erythrocytolytic|反义词: 无|词性:形容词
