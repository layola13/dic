| adj. adv.|东北方的；来自东北的；朝东北的|Coming from the northeast; situated toward or in the northeast.  Directed toward the northeast.|The wind blew northeasterly. 风从东北方向吹来。|The ship sailed in a northeasterly direction. 船朝东北方向航行。|近义词：northeastern, northeastward|反义词:southwesterly, southeastern|词性:形容词, 副词
