heterocephalous:/ˌhɛtərəˈsɛfələs/| adj.|异头的，异型头的|Having flower heads of two or more distinct forms, as in certain composite plants.|Some daisy species exhibit heterocephalous development, producing different types of flower heads for various functions.  一些雏菊物种表现出异头发育，产生不同类型的头状花序以实现各种功能。|近义词:heteromorphic|反义词:homomorphic|词性:adjective

heterocephaly:/ˌhɛtərəˈsɛfəli/| n.|异头性，异型头性|The condition of having flower heads of two or more distinct forms.|Heterocephaly is an adaptation observed in some plants to optimize pollination and seed dispersal. 异头性是在一些植物中观察到的一种适应性，以优化授粉和种子传播。|近义词:heteromorphism|反义词:homomorphism|词性:noun
