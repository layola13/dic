digestor:/daɪˈdʒɛstər/ | n. | 消化器；消化罐；文摘 | A machine or container in which something is treated with heat, enzymes, or chemicals to break it down, especially for industrial or agricultural purposes.  Also, a person or thing that digests something, or a collection of summaries or excerpts from a larger work. | The farmer uses a digester to break down organic waste. 农民用消化器分解有机废物。| synonyms: bioreactor, fermenter, autoclave, composter, condenser, extractor, processor | antonyms: synthesizer, assembler | noun

digestor:/daɪˈdʒɛstər/ | n. | 文摘；摘要 | A collection of summaries or excerpts from a larger work. | She read a digester of recent scientific articles. 她读了一份最近科学文章的摘要。 | synonyms: abstract, summary, synopsis, compendium, précis | antonyms: expansion, elaboration | noun
