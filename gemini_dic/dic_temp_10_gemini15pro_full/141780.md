oratorios:/ˌɔːrəˈtɔːriəʊz/ (British), /ˌɔːrəˈtɔːrioʊz/ (American)| n.|清唱剧（复数）|A large-scale musical work for orchestra and voices, often based on a religious theme, but without costumes, scenery, or action. It is typically performed in a concert hall or church.|Handel's "Messiah" is one of the most famous oratorios.  亨德尔的《弥赛亚》是最著名的清唱剧之一。|
