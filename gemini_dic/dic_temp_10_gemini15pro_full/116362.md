masticates:/ˈmæstɪkeɪts/| v. | 咀嚼，仔细思考 | chews (food) slowly and thoroughly.  To also consider or ponder something carefully. | The cow masticates its cud.  奶牛咀嚼着反刍的食物。

The philosopher masticated the complex problem for weeks. 哲学家几周来一直在仔细思考这个复杂的问题。|近义词：chews, grinds, ponders, contemplates, ruminates | 反义词：swallows, gulps, ignores, disregards |词性：verb
