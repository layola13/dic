look-and-say:/ˈlʊk ən seɪ/| adj. | 看图说话的；描述所见的 |A sequence or system based on describing the previous element.  It describes a sequence of numbers where each subsequent number is derived by describing the previous number. |The look-and-say sequence starts with 1, then 11 (one 1), then 21 (two 1s), then 1211 (one 2, one 1), and so on. 看图说话序列从1开始，然后是11（一个1），然后是21（两个1），然后是1211（一个2，一个1），依此类推。| descriptive, observational | N/A | adjective
