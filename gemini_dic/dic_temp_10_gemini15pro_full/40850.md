cooee:/ˈkʊiː/ (Australian English)| n. vi. vt. int.|呼喊；招呼；库伊（澳大利亚土著居民用以互相招呼的喊声）|A long, high-pitched call used to attract attention, especially in the Australian bush.|例句：He cooeed to his friends across the valley. 他在山谷对面呼唤他的朋友们。

例句：She heard a faint cooee in the distance. 她听到远处传来微弱的呼喊声。|近义词：shout, yell, call, holler|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:名词，动词，感叹词

词源:
From Dharuk *guwiy
