hoodwink:/hʊdˈwɪŋk/
verb
欺骗，蒙蔽
to deceive or trick someone
例句：The con man hoodwinked the elderly woman out of her life savings. 骗子骗走了老妇人的毕生积蓄。

例句：They were hoodwinked into believing they had won a prize. 他们被骗去相信自己中奖了。
近义词：deceive, trick, dupe, swindle, cheat, defraud
反义词：enlighten, inform, tell the truth
词性：v.
