implausibility:/ɪmˌplɔːzəˈbɪləti/
noun
难以置信；不可能；不合理
the quality of being difficult to believe
The implausibility of his story made it hard to believe. 他故事的难以置信让人难以相信。

implausible:/ɪmˈplɔːzəbl/
adjective
难以置信的；不可能的；不合理的
not seeming reasonable or probable; failing to convince
He gave an implausible excuse for his absence. 他为自己的缺席找了个难以置信的借口。
