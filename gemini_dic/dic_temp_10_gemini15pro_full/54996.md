dispunishable: /dɪsˈpʌnɪʃəbl/
adjective
可免于处罚的，不必受罚的
Not deserving of or liable to punishment.
His actions, though regrettable, were not dispunishable under the law. 虽然他的行为令人遗憾，但在法律上是可以免于处罚的。
近义词: excusable, pardonable, forgivable
反义词: punishable, culpable, censurable
词性: adjective

词源:  dis- (prefix meaning "not") + punishable (from punish + -able)
