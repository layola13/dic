entourage:/ˈɑːntʊˌrɑːʒ/ (US), /ˈɒntʊˌrɑːʒ/ (UK)| n.|随行人员，随从；环境，周围|A group of people attending or surrounding an important person.|The celebrity arrived with a large entourage.|近义词：retinue, escort, cortege, following|反义词: N/A|noun

词源:
From French *entourage*, from *entourer* "to surround," from *en-* "in" + *tour* "turn, circuit."
