lein:/liːn/ | n. | 留置权；扣押权 | A legal claim on someone's property as security for a debt. | Example 1: The bank had a lein on his house. 银行对他的房子有留置权。

Example 2:  She placed a lein on his car until he paid the debt. 她扣押了他的汽车，直到他还清债务。| Synonym: charge, security, encumbrance | Antonym: release | noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *lein, from Latin *ligamen "band, tie," from ligare "to bind."
