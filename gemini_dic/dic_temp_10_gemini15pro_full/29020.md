cartograph:/ˈkɑːrtəɡræf/ | n. |地图; 海图|A map, especially one showing geographical features. |例句：The cartograph clearly showed the mountain ranges. 这张地图清晰地展示了山脉。

例句：Ancient cartographs often included mythical creatures. 古代地图通常包含神话生物。

|近义词：map, chart, plan|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑globe(地球仪)表示不同呈现方式)|词性:noun
