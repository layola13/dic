conferrable:/kənˈfɜːrəbl/
adj.
可授予的，可赋予的
able to be conferred or bestowed.
The honorary degree is conferrable on anyone who has made a significant contribution to the field.  这个荣誉学位可以授予给任何对该领域做出重大贡献的人。
The title of professor is conferrable only after years of dedicated service. 教授的头衔只有在多年的奉献服务之后才能授予。
近义词:bestowable, grantable, awardable
反义词:non-conferrable, unbestowable
词性:adjective
