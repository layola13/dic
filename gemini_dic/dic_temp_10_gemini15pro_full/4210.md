airiest:/ˈɛəriɪst/| adj.|最通风的，最轻快的，最空灵的|Lightest and most airy; delicate or insubstantial.|The airiest of the bedrooms faced south, overlooking the gardens.  最通风的卧室朝南，可以俯瞰花园。|Synonyms: light, airy, delicate, ethereal, gossamer, flimsy, insubstantial|Antonyms: heavy, dense, solid, substantial|adjective

Etymology: From air + -y + -est.  Ultimately from Latin aer.
