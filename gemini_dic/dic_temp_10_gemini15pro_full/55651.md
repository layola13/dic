例句：The dithery old man couldn't decide which flavor of ice cream to choose.  这位犹豫不决的老人无法决定选择哪种口味的冰淇淋。|近义词：hesitant, indecisive, uncertain, wavering, vacillating|反义词： decisive, resolute, determined|词性:形容词
