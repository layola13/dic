culpable:/ˈkʌlpəbl/| adj.|有罪的；应受谴责的；难辞其咎的|Deserving blame.|例句：The jury found the defendant culpable of manslaughter. 陪审团认定被告犯有过失杀人罪。

例句：He felt culpable for not being there when she needed him. 他因为在她需要他时不在身边而感到内疚。|近义词：guilty, blameworthy, responsible|反义词：innocent, blameless, faultless|词性:形容词
词源:From Late Middle English *culpable, *coupable, from Old French coupable, culpable, from Latin culpabilis (“blameworthy”), from culpa (“fault, blame”). Doublet of culpable.
