nonagglutinative: /ˌnɒnəˈɡluːtɪnətɪv/ | adj. | 非黏着语的 | Not agglutinative; characterized by a language in which words are not formed by stringing together a series of morphemes, each of which expresses a single grammatical or semantic unit. |  English is a largely nonagglutinative language, unlike Turkish or Hungarian.  英语在很大程度上是一种非黏着语，不像土耳其语或匈牙利语。| non-inflectional, isolating | agglutinative, synthetic | adjective
