leash:/liːʃ/| n. v. |皮带，牵狗绳；约束，控制|A strap or cord for restraining and guiding an animal, especially a dog.  Also, figuratively, anything that restrains or controls.|The dog strained at its leash, eager to chase the squirrel.  那只狗用力拉扯着皮带，急于追逐松鼠。|近义词：lead, tether, restraint, control|反义词:freedom, liberty|noun
leash:/liːʃ/| v. |用皮带系住，束缚|To fasten or restrain with a leash.|She leashed the dog before taking it for a walk.  她在带狗散步之前用皮带拴住了它。|近义词：tie, fasten, restrain, control|反义词:unleash, free, release|verb
