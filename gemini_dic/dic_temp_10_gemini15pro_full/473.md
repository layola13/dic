abnegate: /ˈæbnɪɡeɪt/
verb
放弃，舍弃，克制
to deny oneself (something); renounce or reject (something desired or valuable)
She abnegated all claims to the property. 她放弃了所有财产的索取权。

He abnegated his personal desires for the good of the team. 他为了团队的利益而放弃了个人愿望。

近义词: renounce, relinquish, forgo, forsake, surrender, sacrifice
反义词: indulge, embrace, accept, claim
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abnegatus*, past participle of *abnegare* ("to deny, refuse"), from *ab* ("from, away from") + *negare* ("to deny").

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He abnegates his desires for the sake of his family. = 他为了家庭而放弃自己的欲望。
She abnegates her right to speak. = 她放弃了发言权。

一般过去时:
The king abnegated his throne. = 国王放弃了王位。
She abnegated her responsibility. = 她放弃了她的责任。

现在完成时:
He has abnegated all forms of luxury. = 他已经放弃了各种奢侈品。
She has abnegated her claim to the inheritance. = 她已经放弃了继承权。

过去完成时:
He had abnegated his position before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他已经放弃了他的职位。
She had abnegated her dreams of becoming a doctor. = 她已经放弃了成为医生的梦想。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
