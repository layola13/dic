odorating:/ˈoʊdəˌreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 加味；加香；除臭 | Imparting an odor to, especially a pleasant one; deodorizing. |  The company is odorating the natural gas so that leaks can be detected.  该公司正在给天然气加味，以便可以检测到泄漏。 | Synonyms: perfuming, scenting, aromatizing | Antonyms: deodorizing, neutralizing | verb

odorating:/ˈoʊdəˌreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 加味的；加香的；除臭的 |  Used to describe something that adds or removes odor. |  The odorating agent made the room smell fresh.  加香剂使房间闻起来很清新。| Synonyms: perfuming, scenting, aromatizing | Antonyms: deodorizing, neutralizing | adjective
