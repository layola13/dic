causers:/ˈkɔːzərz/
n.
起因，肇事者，原因
Those responsible for causing something.
The causers of the accident were never identified. 事故的肇事者从未被确认。
Synonyms: creators, instigators, agents, sources, origins
Antonyms: results, effects, consequences, outcomes
词性: 名词
