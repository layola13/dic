incriminates:/ɪnˈkrɪmɪneɪts/| vt.|控告；暗示...有罪；牵连|To make (someone) appear guilty of a crime or wrongdoing; to show evidence of involvement in a crime.|The evidence found at the scene incriminates him in the robbery.  现场发现的证据表明他参与了抢劫。|synonyms: implicate, accuse, charge, blame|antonyms: exonerate, acquit, absolve|verb
