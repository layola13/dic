dissensions:/dɪˈsɛnʃənz/| n. |不和，纷争，意见不合|Disagreement and discord among a group of people.|The political dissensions within the party threatened to tear it apart. 党内政治纷争有可能使其分裂。|近义词：disagreements, disputes, discord, strife, conflict|反义词：agreement, harmony, concord, unity|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dissensio*, from Latin dissentire (“to disagree”), from dis- (“apart, away”) + sentire (“to feel, think”).
