hemostat:/ˈhiːməˌstæt/ (US), /ˈhiːməstæt/ (UK)| n.|止血钳，止血剂|A surgical instrument used to compress blood vessels and control bleeding.  A hemostat can also refer to a substance that stops bleeding.|The surgeon used a hemostat to clamp the bleeding artery. 外科医生用止血钳夹住出血的动脉。|Synonyms: clamp, forceps, styptic|Antonyms: coagulant (although this can be context-dependent, as some coagulants are used in conjunction with hemostats)|noun
