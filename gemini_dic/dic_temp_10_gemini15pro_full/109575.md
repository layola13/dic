let someone have it:/lɛt ˈsʌmwʌn hæv ɪt/ | idiom (v.) | 严厉批评；狠狠惩罚；给某人一顿教训 | To criticize someone severely; to punish someone harshly; to attack someone physically or verbally. |  "After he missed the deadline, his boss really let him have it." 他错过了截止日期后，他的老板狠狠地训斥了他一顿。| Synonyms: scold, berate, reprimand, castigate, lambaste,  give someone a piece of one's mind, blast, chew out, dress down | Antonyms: praise, commend, compliment, laud | idiom
