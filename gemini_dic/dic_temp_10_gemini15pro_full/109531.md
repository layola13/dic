less:/les/ | adj. adv.| 更少的，较小的；更少地，较小地 |  A smaller quantity or amount of; used to form the comparative of many adjectives and adverbs, especially those of one or two syllables. |  I have less money than you. 我比你的钱少。 | 近义词: smaller, fewer, minor | 反义词: more, greater, larger | 词性: 形容词，副词

less:/les/ | n. | 较少量；较小程度 | A smaller amount or quantity. |  Less is more. 少即是多。 | 近义词: smaller amount, lesser quantity | 反义词: more | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old English *læssa (comparative of *lytel "little"), from Proto-Germanic *laisizō, from PIE *leis- "small, weak."
