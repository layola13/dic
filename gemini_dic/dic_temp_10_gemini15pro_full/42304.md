counter-offer:/ˈkaʊntər ɔːfər/ | n. |还价，反 پیشنهاد |An offer made in response to a previous offer, typically proposing different terms.|  The seller rejected our initial offer, but we made a counter-offer and they accepted. 卖家拒绝了我们最初的报价，但我们提出了还价，他们接受了。| Synonyms: counterproposal, return offer | Antonyms: acceptance, initial offer | noun

counter-offer:/ˈkaʊntər ˈɔːfər/ | v. |还价，反要约 | To make a counter-offer. | They countered our offer with a lower price.  他们对我们的报价提出了更低的价格作为还价。| Synonyms: counterpropose | Antonyms: accept | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + offer.  "Counter-" acts as a prefix meaning "against" or "in opposition to." "Offer" comes from Old French "offrir," meaning "to offer, present, or propose."
