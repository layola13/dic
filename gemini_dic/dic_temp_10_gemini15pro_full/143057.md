outboard:/ˈaʊtbɔːrd/ (US) /ˈaʊtbɔːd/ (UK)| adj. n. adv.|舷外的，船外的；外置的；舷外发动机|Situated or fitted outside a boat's hull.  A motor mounted at the stern of a boat.|

例句：
The outboard motor sputtered and died.
舷外发动机发出噼啪声，然后熄火了。

例句：
He bought a new outboard for his fishing boat.
他为他的渔船买了一个新的舷外发动机。

近义词：external, exterior|
反义词：inboard|
词性：形容词, 名词, 副词


outboard:/ˈaʊtbɔːrd/ (US) /ˈaʊtbɔːd/ (UK)| n.|舷外发动机|A detachable motor mounted at the stern of a boat.|

例句：
He lowered the outboard into the water.
他把舷外发动机放进水里。

例句：
The outboard was powerful enough to pull a water skier.
这台舷外发动机的动力足以拉动一个滑水者。

近义词：outboard motor|
反义词：inboard motor|
词性：名词


outboard:/ˈaʊtbɔːrd/ (US) /ˈaʊtbɔːd/ (UK)| adv.|向船外，向外|Towards the outside of a boat or aircraft.|

例句：
He leaned outboard to see what was causing the noise.
他向船外探出身子，想看看是什么东西发出的噪音。

例句：
The wingtips flexed outboard during the flight.
机翼尖在飞行过程中向外弯曲。

近义词：outward|
反义词：inboard|
词性：副词


词源:  From out + board.  Originally referred to a rudder positioned outside the hull of a boat.
