connoisseur:/ˌkɒnəˈsɜː(r)/ /ˌkɑːnəˈsɝː/| n. |鉴赏家；行家|A person who has expert knowledge and keen discrimination, especially in matters of art, music, or taste.|The wine connoisseur recommended a robust Cabernet Sauvignon.  这位葡萄酒鉴赏家推荐了一款浓郁的赤霞珠葡萄酒。|近义词：expert, specialist, aficionado, buff|反义词：amateur, novice, beginner|词性:noun

connoisseurs:/ˌkɒnəˈsɜː(r)z/ /ˌkɑːnəˈsɝːz/| n. |鉴赏家们；行家们|Plural form of connoisseur.|The connoisseurs gathered to admire the rare collection of Impressionist paintings.  鉴赏家们聚集在一起欣赏这批罕见的印象派绘画收藏。|近义词：experts, specialists, aficionados|反义词：amateurs, novices|词性:noun
