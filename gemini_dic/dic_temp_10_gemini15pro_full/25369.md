bukshish:/ˈbʊkʃiːʃ/| n.|小费，赏钱，贿赂|A tip or gratuity, especially one given in the Middle East or India.  It can also refer to a bribe.|例句：He gave the porter a small bukshish. 他给了搬运工一小笔小费。

例句：The official hinted that a little bukshish would expedite matters.  这位官员暗示，一点小贿赂可以加快事情的办理。|近义词：tip, gratuity, bribe, kickback|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps penalty if referring to a bribe)|词性:noun
