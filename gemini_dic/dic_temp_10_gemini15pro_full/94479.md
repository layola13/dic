imporous:/ɪmˈpɔːrəs/
adjective
无孔的，不透水的
not allowing liquid or air to pass through
The imporous rock prevented water from seeping through.  这块不透水的岩石阻止了水渗透。
The membrane is imporous to water.  这层膜不透水。
近义词: impermeable, nonporous, airtight, watertight
反义词: porous, permeable, pervious
词性: adjective
