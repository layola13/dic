botched:/bɒtʃt/ /bɑːtʃt/ | adj. | 拙劣的，搞砸的 | carried out badly or carelessly |  The surgery was botched, and the patient suffered complications.  手术搞砸了，病人出现了并发症。| 近义词: bungled, ruined, messed up, flawed, faulty | 反义词: successful, perfect, flawless, impeccable | 词性: 形容词

词源: From botch (v.) "to patch clumsily," of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Low German butsen "to strike, mend," or Dutch botsen "to bump, bruise."
