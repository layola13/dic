outjumping:/ˌaʊtˈdʒʌmpɪŋ/
verb
超越跳跃，跳得比...远/高
The act of jumping further or higher than someone or something else.
He outjumped his competitors in the high jump finals. 他在跳高决赛中跳得比他的竞争对手都高。
outleap, surpass
--- (no clear antonym, possibly "being outjumped")
