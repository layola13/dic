oculary:/ˈɒkjʊlə(ri)/ | n. |目镜，接目镜|A lens or combination of lenses in a telescope or microscope, or other optical instrument, through which the eye views the image.|She looked through the ocular of the microscope. 她透过显微镜的目镜观察。|Synonyms: eyepiece, lens|Antonyms: N/A|noun

oculary:/ˈɑːkjʊləri/ | adj. |眼睛的，视觉的|Of or relating to the eye or vision.|Ocular problems can affect your ability to drive. 眼睛问题会影响你的驾驶能力。|Synonyms: visual, ophthalmic|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
