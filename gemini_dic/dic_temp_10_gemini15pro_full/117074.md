meacock:/ˈmiːkɒk/| n.|懦夫，胆小鬼|A timid or cowardly person. | He called his opponent a meacock for refusing to debate him. 他称他的对手是懦夫，因为他拒绝和他辩论。|近义词：coward, wimp, milksop|反义词：hero, brave, courageous|词性:名词
词源: From Middle English *mekecok, equivalent to meek + cock.  Originally referring to a meek or tame cock (rooster).
