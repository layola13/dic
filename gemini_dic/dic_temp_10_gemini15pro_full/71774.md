fleasome: /ˈfliːsəm/
n.
跳蚤市场上的商品；廉价商品
Items sold at a flea market; inexpensive merchandise.
例句：She found a vintage dress at the fleasome for a steal.  她在跳蚤市场上淘到了一件复古连衣裙，价格非常便宜。

例句：The fleasome was full of interesting trinkets and antiques. 跳蚤市场上摆满了各种各样有趣的小玩意和古董。

近义词：bargains, rummage, secondhand goods
反义词：expensive items, luxury goods
词性：名词


词源：
A blend of "flea" (referring to flea markets) and "some" (suggesting a collection or assortment). "Flea market" itself originates from the French marché aux puces ("market of the fleas"), likely because used goods sold there were thought to be infested with fleas.
