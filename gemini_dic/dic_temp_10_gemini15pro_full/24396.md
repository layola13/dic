brinksmanship: /ˈbrɪŋksmənʃɪp/ | n. |边缘政策；危险边缘策略 | The art or practice of pursuing a dangerous policy to the limits of safety before stopping, typically in order to secure an advantage. |  The two countries were engaged in a dangerous game of brinksmanship. 这两个国家正在进行一场危险的边缘政策游戏。|  brinkmanship pushed the world to the edge of nuclear war. 边缘政策将世界推向了核战争的边缘。 |  gambling, high-stakes diplomacy, power politics, saber-rattling |  conciliation, compromise, diplomacy, negotiation | noun
