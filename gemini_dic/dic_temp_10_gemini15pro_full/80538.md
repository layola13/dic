goodliest:/ˈɡʊdliɪst/| adj.|最美好的，最优秀的|Superlative form of goodly: most goodly.  It implies something of exceptional beauty, excellence, or impressiveness.|例句：The king held court in his goodliest hall. 国王在他最富丽堂皇的大厅里举行朝会。

例句：She wore her goodliest gown to the ball. 她穿着她最漂亮的礼服去参加舞会。|近义词：finest, best, most excellent, most splendid|反义词：worst, poorest, ugliest|词性：形容词
词源：From Middle English *goodliest*, superlative of *goodly*, from Old English *gōdlīc* (“good, goodly, excellent, beautiful, kind, gentle, courteous, noble, splendid”), equivalent to *good* +‎ *-ly*. More at good, -ly.
