mucivore: /ˈmjuːsɪvɔːr/ | n. | 食黏液动物;  黏液食性动物 | An animal that feeds on mucus.  |  The cleaner wrasse is a mucivore that removes parasites and mucus from larger fish.  清洁濑鱼是一种食黏液动物，它会清除大鱼身上的寄生虫和黏液。 |  近义词: mucus-feeder | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun

mucivorous: /mjuːˈsɪvərəs/ | adj. | 食黏液的；黏液食性的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a mucivore; feeding on mucus. | Some species of butterfly are mucivorous in their larval stage.  有些种类的蝴蝶在幼虫阶段是食黏液的。 | 近义词: mucus-feeding | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective
