nonadjacently:/ˌnɒnəˈdʒeɪs(ə)ntli/ /ˌnɑːnəˈdʒeɪs(ə)ntli/| adv.|不相邻地，不连续地|Not adjacent; separated by something else.|The numbers were arranged nonadjacently in the sequence.|近义词：separately, discontinuously, apart|反义词: adjacently, contiguously|词性:副词

Etymology:
From non- + adjacent + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Adjacent" comes from the Latin "adjacēns," present participle of "adjacēre" meaning "to lie near."  "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
