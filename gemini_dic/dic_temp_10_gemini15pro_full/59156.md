dynasty:/ˈdaɪnəsti/ | n. | 王朝；朝代 |A sequence of rulers from the same family, stock, or group. | The Ming dynasty ruled China for over 275 years. 明朝统治中国超过275年。| synonym: house, lineage, reign | antonym: anarchy, disorder | noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *dynastia*, from Ancient Greek *δυναστεία (dunasteía)*, meaning "lordship, power, dominion," from *δυνάστης (dunástēs)* "lord, ruler."
