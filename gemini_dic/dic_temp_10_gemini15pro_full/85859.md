head-hunter:/ˈhedˌhʌntər/| n.|猎头|A person who identifies and approaches suitable candidates employed elsewhere to fill business positions.|The head-hunter called me about a job opportunity at a tech startup. 猎头打电话给我，说一家科技初创公司有一个工作机会。|Synonyms: recruiter, executive search consultant|Antonyms: applicant, job seeker|Noun
