hickory-stripe:/ˈhɪkəri straɪp/| adj. n.|山核桃条纹（的）; 山核桃条纹布|A pattern of stripes, typically narrow and closely spaced, resembling the grain of hickory wood.  It can also refer to fabric with this pattern.|He wore a hickory-striped shirt. 他穿着一件山核桃条纹衬衫。|The hickory-stripe suit is a classic summer look.  这套山核桃条纹西装是经典的夏日造型。|striped, pinstriped|solid, plain|adjective, noun
