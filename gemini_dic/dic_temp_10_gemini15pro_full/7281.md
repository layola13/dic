angrier:/ˈæŋɡriər/ | adj. | 更生气的, 更愤怒的 |Comparative form of angry: more angry.  It indicates a higher degree of anger than 'angry'. | She was angrier than I had ever seen her. 她比我以前见过的任何时候都更生气。|  He became angrier with each passing minute. 随着时间的流逝，他越来越生气。| 近义词:madder, furiouser, more enraged | 反义词: calmer, more peaceful | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *angrier, *angerer, comparative of angry, from Old Norse angri, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *angustiz.
