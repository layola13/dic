barrack: /ˈbærək/ (US) /ˈbærək/ (UK)| n. v.|兵营；营房；简陋的棚屋；（为…）提供营房|A building or group of buildings used to house soldiers.|The soldiers returned to their barracks after a long day of training. 士兵们经过一天的训练后回到了他们的营房。|They barracked the troops in tents. 他们让部队住在帐篷里。|近义词：quarters, dormitory, hut, camp|反义词:house, home|词性:名词,动词

barrack: /ˈbærək/ (US) /ˈbærək/ (UK)| v. |（通常指体育迷）嘲笑，奚落，喝倒|To shout loudly and disapprovingly at someone, usually at a sporting event.|The crowd barracked the visiting team mercilessly.  观众毫不留情地奚落客队。|近义词：jeer, heckle, taunt|反义词:cheer, applaud|词性:动词

Etymology: From French *baraque*, from Italian *baracca*, probably of Germanic origin.
