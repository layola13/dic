nonvolubly:/ˌnɒnˈvɒljʊbli/ | adv. | 不健谈地，沉默寡言地 | Not talkative; reticent.  Characterized by a lack of fluency or readiness of speech. | He answered nonvolubly, offering only short, clipped responses. 他回答得很简洁，只说了几个简短的词。| Synonyms: taciturnly, quietly, reservedly, succinctly | Antonyms: volubly, loquaciously, garrulously | adverb
