bestower:/bɪˈstoʊər/| n.| 授予者，赋予者 |A person who confers or presents something, especially a gift, honor, or right.|The king was the bestower of honors and titles. 国王是荣誉和头衔的授予者。|近义词：donor, giver, grantor|反义词：recipient, receiver|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bestowere*, from bestowen + -er.  Bestowen comes from Old English *bestandan* "to stand by, assist, support," later "to give, bestow," from *be-* "by" + *standan* "to stand."
