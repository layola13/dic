例句：They face a monumental task in rebuilding the war-torn city.  他们在重建饱受战争蹂躏的城市方面面临着艰巨的任务。

近义词：immense, massive, significant, historic, important, colossal, enormous, gigantic|反义词：insignificant, minor, trivial, small|词性：形容词
