paleobotany:/ˌpeɪlɪˈbɒtəni/ /ˌpeɪlɪˈbɑːtəni/| n. |古植物学|The study of fossil plants.  It involves identifying and classifying fossilized plant remains and using them to reconstruct past vegetation and climates, and understand the evolution of plants.| Paleobotany helps us understand how Earth's flora has changed over millions of years. 古植物学帮助我们了解地球上的植物群在数百万年间的变化。|近义词：paleophytology|反义词: - |词性: noun

paleobotany:/ˌpeɪlɪˈbɒtəni/ /ˌpeɪlɪˈbɑːtəni/| n. |古植物学|Researchers in paleobotany have discovered a new species of fossilized fern.  古植物学研究人员发现了一种新的蕨类植物化石。|近义词：paleophytology|反义词: - |词性: noun
