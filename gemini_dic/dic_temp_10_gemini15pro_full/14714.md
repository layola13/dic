awarding:/əˈwɔːrdɪŋ/
Verb
授予，颁发
The process of giving someone a prize or other reward.
The academy is awarding him a lifetime achievement award.  学院将授予他终身成就奖。
Synonyms: giving, presenting, conferring, bestowing, granting
Antonyms: revoking, withdrawing, confiscating, denying
词性: 动词
