hidrosis:/hɪˈdroʊsɪs/ /haɪˈdroʊsɪs/
n.
出汗，盗汗
Excessive sweating.
例句：The doctor diagnosed his hidrosis as a side effect of the medication. 医生诊断他的盗汗是药物的副作用。

例句：Hidrosis can be a symptom of various medical conditions.  出汗过多可能是各种疾病的症状。

近义词：perspiration, sweating, diaphoresis
反义词：anhidrosis

词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek  ἱδρώς (hidrṓs, “sweat”) +‎ -osis.
