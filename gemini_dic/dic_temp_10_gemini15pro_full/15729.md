balboa:/bælˈboʊə/| n.|巴波亚（巴拿马货币单位）; 巴波亚（一种拉丁舞）|A currency unit of Panama, equal to 100 centésimos, and also used in El Salvador.  Also, a type of Latin American dance.|例句：The price was 50 balboas. 价格是50巴波亚。

例句：They danced the balboa all night. 他们整晚都跳巴波亚舞。|近义词：(currency) dollar, peso; (dance) swing|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


balboa:/bælˈboʊə/| n.|巴尔博亚（西班牙探险家）|Vasco Núñez de Balboa (c. 1475 – c. 1519) was a Spanish explorer, governor, and conquistador. He is best known for having crossed the Isthmus of Panama to the Pacific Ocean in 1513, becoming the first European to lead an expedition to have seen or reached the Pacific from the New World.|例句：Balboa discovered the Pacific Ocean. 巴尔博亚发现了太平洋。
