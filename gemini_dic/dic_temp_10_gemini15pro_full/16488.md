bargecourse:/ˈbɑːrdʒkɔːrs/
n.
驳船航道;驳船水道
A bargecourse is a waterway, either natural or artificial, designed or used for the passage of barges.
Barges transported goods along the bargecourse.  驳船沿着驳船航道运送货物。
The new bargecourse significantly reduced travel time. 新的驳船航道大大缩短了行程时间。
近义词:canal, waterway, channel
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps overland route)
词性: noun
