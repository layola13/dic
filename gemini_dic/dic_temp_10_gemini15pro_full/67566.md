extrapolate:/ɪkˈstræpəleɪt/| v. | 推断；外推 | To infer something by assuming that present trends will continue or a current method will remain applicable. | The scientists extrapolated the results of the study to the general population. 科学家们将研究结果推断到普通人群。| infer, deduce, project | interpolate | verb
extrapolated:/ɪkˈstræpəleɪtɪd/| adj. | 外推的；推断的 | Based on inference from known facts or data. | The extrapolated figures suggest a significant increase in sales next year. 外推的数据表明明年销售额将大幅增长。| inferred, deduced, projected | interpolated | adjective
