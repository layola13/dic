moaned:/moʊnd/ (US) /məʊnd/ (UK)| v. |呻吟; 抱怨|To make a long, low sound expressing pain, unhappiness, or sexual pleasure.  A low, mournful sound.|The injured man moaned in pain. 受伤的男子痛苦地呻吟着。|近义词：groaned, complained, whimpered, grumbled|反义词：cheered, rejoiced, exulted|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->From Middle English *monen*, of uncertain origin. Perhaps related to Old English *mǣnan* "to lament, complain," or to Old Norse *mæna* "to mention, remember."
