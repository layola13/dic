dewed:/djuːd/ /duːd/
adjective
 покрытый росой (Russian);  沾满露水的 (Chinese)
Covered with dew; moistened with dew.
The dewed grass sparkled in the morning sun. 沾满露水的草地在晨曦中闪闪发光。
The spider web was dewed with tiny droplets.  蜘蛛网上沾满了细小的露珠。
近义词: dewy, moist, damp
反义词: dry, arid
词性: adjective

词源: From dew + -ed.  Dew comes from Old English *dēaw, from Proto-Germanic *dawwaz, from Proto-Indo-European *dheus- (“to flow, drip, melt”).
