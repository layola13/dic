ergot:/ˈɜːrɡət/ (US) /ˈɜːɡɒt/ (UK)| n.|麦角；麦角菌|A fungus that grows on rye and other grains, producing toxins that can cause hallucinations and other serious health problems.|The ergot contaminated the rye crop, leading to widespread illness.|Synonyms: ergot fungus, spurred rye|Antonyms: None|noun

ergotism:/ˈɜːrɡətɪzəm/ (US) /ˈɜːrɡətɪzəm/ (UK)| n.|麦角中毒|Poisoning caused by eating grains contaminated with ergot fungus.|Ergotism was a serious problem in medieval Europe.|Synonyms: Saint Anthony's fire|Antonyms: None|noun
