carve:/kɑːrv/ (US) /kɑːv/ (UK)| v. |雕刻，切，开创|To cut (a hard material) in order to produce an object, design, or inscription.|He carved a beautiful statue out of wood. 他用木头雕刻了一座美丽的雕像。

The chef carved the turkey expertly.  厨师熟练地切火鸡。


He carved out a successful career in finance. 他在金融领域开创了一番成功的事业。

|近义词：sculpt, engrave, chisel, slice, whittle|反义词：construct, build, assemble|词性:动词
