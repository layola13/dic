gummed:/ɡʌmd/
adjective
涂胶的，粘性的
covered with gum or glue; sticky.
The envelopes were gummed shut.  这些信封用胶粘住了。
近义词：sticky, adhesive, glued
反义词：ungummed, dry
词性：adjective

gummed:/ɡʌmd/
verb
用胶粘，涂胶
to apply gum or glue to something.
He gummed the label onto the package. 他把标签粘在包裹上。
近义词：glue, stick, paste
反义词：unglue, unstick, detach
词性：verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gommen, from Old English *goman "to pay attention to," of uncertain origin.  The sense shifted to "gum" (the sticky substance) and then to "gummed" (covered in gum).
