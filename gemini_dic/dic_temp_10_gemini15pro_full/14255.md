autoecious: /ɔːˈtiːʃəs/ | adj. | 自主寄生 | (of rust fungi) Completing its life cycle on one host. | Example:  The rust fungus is autoecious, completing its life cycle on the wheat plant. 锈菌是自主寄生的，在小麦植株上完成其生命周期。

Example: Unlike heteroecious rusts, autoecious rusts do not require an alternate host. 与异主寄生锈菌不同，自主寄生锈菌不需要交替寄主。 | 近义词: homoecious | 反义词: heteroecious | 词性: adjective
