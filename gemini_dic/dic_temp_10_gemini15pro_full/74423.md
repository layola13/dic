franchising:/ˈfræntʃaɪzɪŋ/
n.
特许经营；特许加盟
A system of distributing goods or services involving a franchisor, who establishes the brand's trademark or trade name and a business system, and a franchisee, who pays a royalty and often an initial fee for the right to do business under the franchisor's name and system.
McDonald's is a globally recognized example of franchising. 麦当劳是全球公认的特许经营的例子。
Many entrepreneurs choose franchising as a way to start their own business. 许多企业家选择特许经营作为创业的一种方式。
近义词：licensing, chain operation
反义词：independent business
