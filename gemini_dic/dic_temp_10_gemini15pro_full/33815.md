circumfluous:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈfluːəs/| adj.|环绕流动的；冗长的；迂回的|Flowing around; surrounding; encompassing; also, verbose, roundabout.|The circumfluous river meandered through the valley. 这条环绕流动的河流蜿蜒穿过山谷。| The speaker's circumfluous style made it difficult to follow his argument. 演讲者冗长的风格使得他的论点难以理解。 |近义词：surrounding, encompassing, verbose, prolix|反义词：concise, succinct|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin circumfluus, from circum- (around) + fluere (to flow).
