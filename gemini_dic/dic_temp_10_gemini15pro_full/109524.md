lesioning:/ˈliːʒənɪŋ/
n.|损毁，毁损; 病灶; 病变|The process of damaging or destroying a specific area of the brain or other tissue, typically for research purposes or to treat a medical condition.  This can be done through various methods, including surgery, radiation, or the injection of chemicals.|Researchers used lesioning techniques to study the function of the hippocampus. 研究人员使用损毁技术来研究海马体的功能。|Synonyms: ablation, destruction|Antonyms: repair, healing|noun

lesioning:/ˈliːʒənɪŋ/
v.|损毁，毁损|The act of damaging or destroying a specific area of the brain or other tissue. |Lesioning the amygdala can reduce aggression in some animals. 损毁杏仁核可以减少某些动物的攻击性。|Synonyms: ablating, destroying|Antonyms: repairing, healing|verb
