bristle-like:/ˈbrɪslˌlaɪk/| adj.|像鬃毛一样的；毛发状的|Resembling a bristle; stiff and short like a bristle.|The plant has bristle-like leaves.  这种植物的叶子像鬃毛一样。|The caterpillar has bristle-like hairs. 这条毛毛虫有像鬃毛一样的毛。|近义词：bristly, setaceous, spiny, prickly|反义词：smooth, soft|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From bristle +‎ -like.  "bristle" comes from Middle English *bristel, *bristle, from Old English byrstl (“bristle”), from Proto-West Germanic *burstil, from Proto-Germanic *burstiz (“bristle”).  "-like" is a common suffix meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of".
