nonornamental:/ˌnɒnˌɔːrnəˈmentl/
adjective
非装饰性的，不加装饰的
Not decorative; plain.
The building's facade was nonornamental, emphasizing function over form.  这栋建筑的正面不加装饰，强调功能而非形式。
The furniture was simple and nonornamental, reflecting the owner's minimalist style. 这家具简单朴素，体现了主人极简主义的风格。

synonym:plain, simple, unadorned, undecorated
antonym: ornamental, decorative, ornate, embellished
词性:adjective
