abhorrences: /əbˈhɒrənsɪz/ /əbˈhɔːrənsɪz/
noun
憎恶，厌恶之物
Things that are abhorred; feelings of strong dislike or disgust.
He spoke of his abhorrence of violence. 他表达了他对暴力的憎恶。
She looked at the cockroaches with abhorrence. 她厌恶地看着蟑螂。
synonyms: hatred, loathing, detestation, aversion, disgust, repugnance, revulsion
antonyms: love, admiration, liking, fondness
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *abhorrentia, from Latin abhorrens, present participle of abhorrēre (“to shrink back from, shudder at, detest”), from ab- (“away from”) + horrēre (“to bristle, shudder”).
