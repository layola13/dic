ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀκρασία (akrasía, “lack of mastery”), from ἀ- (a-, “not, without”) + κράτος (krátos, “power, strength”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱer- (“hard”).
