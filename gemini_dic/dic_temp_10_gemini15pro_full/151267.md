parepididymis:/ˌpærəˌɛpɪˈdɪdɪmɪs/
noun
附睾旁体
A small, rudimentary structure representing the cranial end of the Wolffian duct, situated on the head of the epididymis.  It is a remnant of the mesonephric duct.
The parepididymis is a vestigial structure found near the head of the epididymis. 附睾旁体是位于附睾头部附近的一个退化结构。
The doctor explained that the parepididymis has no known function in adult males. 医生解释说，附睾旁体在成年男性中没有已知的功能。
