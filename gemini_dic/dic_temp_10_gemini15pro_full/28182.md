capper:/ˈkæpər/| n.|骗子，诱饵；（尤指赌博或商业中的）圈套；完成者，结束者；（非正式）出乎意料的事件或言论|A person who swindles or defrauds, especially in gambling or business. Something that completes or finishes. An unexpected event or remark (informal).|The police arrested the capper who had been running a Ponzi scheme. 警方逮捕了那个一直在经营庞氏骗局的骗子。

The final goal was a real capper to a thrilling game.  最后一个进球为这场激动人心的比赛画上了圆满的句号。

"And then, to cap it all off, he proposed!" "然后，最令人意外的是，他求婚了！"|近义词：swindler, cheat, con artist, finisher, closer|反义词:sucker, victim|词性:noun
