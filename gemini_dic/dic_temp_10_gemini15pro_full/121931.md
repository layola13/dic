misshipping:/ˌmɪsˈʃɪpɪŋ/
n.
错误发货，运输错误
the act of sending goods to the wrong destination or recipient.
The customer complained about the misshipping of their order. 顾客投诉他们的订单被发错了地方。
misdelivery, incorrect shipment
correct shipment, accurate delivery
noun
