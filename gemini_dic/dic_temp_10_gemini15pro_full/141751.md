orangery:/ˈɒrɪndʒəri/ /ˈɔːrɪndʒəri/
n.
柑橘园，温室
a building with a glass roof and walls, attached to a house, used for growing orange trees and other plants that need warmth and light.
The Duke built a magnificent orangery on his estate. 公爵在他的庄园里建造了一座宏伟的柑橘园。

近义词: greenhouse, conservatory
反义词: garden, yard
词性: noun
