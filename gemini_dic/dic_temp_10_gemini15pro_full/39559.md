conscientiousness:/ˌkɒnʃiˈɛnʃəsnəs/ /ˌkɑːnʃiˈɛnʃəsnəs/| n. |认真；尽责；一丝不苟|The quality of wishing to do one's work or duty well and thoroughly.|She is known for her conscientiousness and attention to detail. 她以认真负责和注重细节而闻名。|近义词：diligence, meticulousness, scrupulousness|反义词：negligence, carelessness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *conscienciousnes*, from *consciencious* + *-nes*. *Consciencious* comes from Late Latin *conscientiōsus*, from Latin *conscientia* ("conscience").
