hakeems:/hɑːˈkiːmz/ | n. |哈基姆们（hakeem的复数形式）| Plural form of hakeem, referring to multiple individuals named Hakeem or multiple practitioners of traditional Islamic medicine. | The hakeems gathered for their annual conference.  哈基姆们聚集在一起参加他们的年度会议。| Hakeems from across the region shared their knowledge. 来自该地区的哈基姆们分享了他们的知识。 | 近义词: healers, physicians | 反义词: (None specific, perhaps patients) | Noun

词源:
From Arabic حَكِيم‎ (ḥakīm, “wise, judicious, learned, a sage, a physician”), active participle of حَكَمَ‎ (ḥakama, “to judge, to govern, to be wise”). Ultimately from the root ح ك م‎ (ḥ-k-m).
