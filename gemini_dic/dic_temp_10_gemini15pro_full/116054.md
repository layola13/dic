martialness:/ˈmɑːrʃəlnəs/ | n. | 武勇；好战；军事素质 | The martialness of the Spartans was legendary. 斯巴达人的武勇是传奇的。 |  A sense of martialness permeated the army camp. 军营中弥漫着一种武勇之气。 |  militancy, belligerence, aggressiveness | pacifism, gentleness, meekness | noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *martialnes*, from Old French *martialnesse*, from Late Latin *martialitas*, from Latin *martialis* (“of Mars, warlike”), from *Mars* (“Mars, the Roman god of war”).
