overdoubt:/ˌoʊvərˈdaʊt/ | v. |过度怀疑；怀疑过多|To doubt excessively; to be overly skeptical.| He overdoubted his abilities and missed a great opportunity. 他过度怀疑自己的能力，错失了一个良机。|例句：She overdoubted her ability to finish the race. 她过度怀疑自己完成比赛的能力。|近义词：distrust, suspect, question, mistrust|反义词：trust, believe, have faith in|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (Old English *ofer*, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, beyond") + "doubt" (from Old French *douter*, from Latin *dubitare*, meaning "to hesitate, waver").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "doubt," implying an excessive or unwarranted level of skepticism.
