frankly:/ˈfræŋkli/| adv.|坦白地，坦率地|In a frank, direct, and honest manner.  It means expressing something openly and truthfully, without hesitation or disguise.|Frankly, I don't think he's the right person for the job. 坦白说，我认为他不是这份工作的合适人选。|Frankly, I'm tired of hearing excuses. 坦白说，我厌倦了听借口。|近义词：honestly, candidly, truthfully, openly|反义词：dishonestly, deceptively, evasively|词性:副词
词源：From Old French *francment (Modern French franchement), from franc (“free”) + -ment, the adverbial suffix.
