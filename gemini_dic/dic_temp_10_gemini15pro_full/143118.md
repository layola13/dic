outburn:/ˌaʊtˈbɜːrn/
verb | 烧尽;烧坏;超过燃烧 | To burn longer or more intensely than something else. | The bonfire outburned the smaller fire.篝火比小火烧得更久更旺。 | outlast, survive | extinguish, quench | v.


outburn:/ˌaʊtˈbɜːrn/
noun | 烧坏;烧尽 | The result of burning longer or more intensely than something else.  | The outburn of the forest fire left only charred remains.森林大火烧尽后，只剩下烧焦的残骸。| burnout, combustion | extinguishing | n.
