norian:/ˈnɔːriən/| adj. n.|诺利期,诺利阶的|Relating to or denoting the Norian stage, the uppermost stage of the Triassic period, lasting from approximately 228 to 208.5 million years ago.|The Norian stage saw the evolution of several important dinosaur groups. 诺利期见证了几个重要恐龙群的进化。|The Norian limestone is a prominent geological feature in the region.  该地区的诺利阶石灰岩是一个显著的地质特征。|近义词：Late Triassic, Upper Triassic|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider earlier periods like Carnian or Permian)|adjective, noun
