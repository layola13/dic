brachypterous:/ˌbrækɪˈtɛrəs/| adj.|短翅的，翅发育不全的|Having short or underdeveloped wings, unable to fly.|例句：The brachypterous grasshopper couldn't fly to escape the predator. 短翅的蚱蜢无法飞起来躲避捕食者。

例句：Brachypterous insects often rely on other means of locomotion. 短翅昆虫通常依靠其他运动方式。|近义词：short-winged, flightless|反义词：macropterous, long-winged|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek βραχύς (brakhús, “short”) + πτερόν (pterón, “wing”)
