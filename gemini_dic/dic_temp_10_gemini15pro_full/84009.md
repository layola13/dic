half-disposed:/ˌhɑːf dɪˈspəʊzd/ | adj. | 倾向于…的；有点儿想…的；不太情愿的 | Partly inclined or willing; somewhat reluctant. |  He was half-disposed to agree. 他有点儿想同意。| 近义词:  inclined, willing, somewhat reluctant | 反义词:  disinclined, unwilling, eager | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "half" + "disposed". "half" 表示“一半的，部分的”，"disposed" 源自拉丁语 "disponere"，意为“安排，布置”，引申为“倾向于”。
