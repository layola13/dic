crozier:/ˈkroʊʒər/| n. |权杖，牧杖，蕨类植物新芽|A bishop's crozier is a symbol of pastoral office. 主教的权杖是牧职的象征。|The croziers of the ferns unfurled in the spring. 春天蕨类植物的新芽舒展开来。|Synonyms: staff, crook, pastoral staff|Antonyms: None|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *crocier (Modern French croisier), from Medieval Latin crociarius ("crosier bearer"), from Latin crux ("cross").
