materialism:/məˈtɪəriəlɪzəm/ | n. |物质主义；唯物主义|A philosophy that emphasizes material possessions and physical comforts as more important than spiritual values.  |例句：Materialism is rampant in modern society.  现代社会充斥着物质主义。

例句：He rejected materialism and embraced a simpler life. 他拒绝物质主义，拥抱更简单的生活。|近义词：consumerism, worldliness |反义词：spiritualism, idealism |词性: noun
