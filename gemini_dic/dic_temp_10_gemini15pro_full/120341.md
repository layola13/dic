minimalism: /ˈmɪnɪməlɪzəm/ | n. | 极简主义 | A style or technique (as in music, literature, or design) that is characterized by extreme spareness and simplicity. |  She adopted minimalism in her home décor, getting rid of anything that wasn't essential. 她在家居装饰中采用了极简主义，去除了所有不必要的东西。

Minimalism is becoming increasingly popular as people seek to declutter their lives. 随着人们寻求简化生活，极简主义越来越受欢迎。


近义词: simplicity, reductionism | 反义词: maximalism, extravagance | 词性: noun
