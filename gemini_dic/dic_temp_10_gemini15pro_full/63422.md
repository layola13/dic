entrapments: /ɪnˈtræpmənts/
noun
陷阱，圈套
The act of entrapping or the state of being entrapped.  A situation or action that traps someone.
He fell victim to one of the many entrapments set by his enemies. 他掉进了敌人设下的众多陷阱中的一个。
The police used entrapment to catch the thief. 警方使用诱捕来抓贼。
