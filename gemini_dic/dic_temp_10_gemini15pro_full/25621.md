bumptiously:/ˈbʌmpʃəsli/ | adv. |傲慢地，自夸地 | In a self-assertive and offensively conceited manner. | He bumptiously declared himself the winner before the votes were counted. 他傲慢地在计票前就宣布自己是赢家。|  He strode bumptiously into the room, interrupting the conversation. 他傲慢地大步走进房间，打断了谈话。 | 近义词：arrogantly, conceitedly, presumptuously, boastfully | 反义词：humbly, modestly, unassumingly | 词性: 副词

bumptious:/ˈbʌmpʃəs/ | adj. |傲慢的，自夸的 | Offensively self-assertive or conceited. | His bumptious behavior alienated his colleagues. 他傲慢的行为疏远了他的同事。 | The bumptious young man boasted about his accomplishments.  这个傲慢的年轻人吹嘘自己的成就。 | 近义词：arrogant, conceited, presumptuous, boastful | 反义词：humble, modest, unassuming | 词性: 形容词
