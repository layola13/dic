doozers:/ˈduːzərz/
noun
杜泽尔人，建筑工人
Small, industrious, and green creatures from the fictional world of Fraggle Rock, known for their construction projects and love of radishes.
The doozers were busy building a new archway. 杜泽尔人正忙着建造一个新的拱门。
近义词:builders, constructors, workers
反义词:destroyers, demolishers
词性:n.


doozer:/ˈduːzər/
noun
推土机
A large, powerful tractor with a blade used for moving earth and debris.
The doozer cleared the land for the new building. 推土机清理了新建筑的土地。
近义词:bulldozer, earthmover
反义词:N/A
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
The word "doozer" (referring to the Fraggle Rock creatures) is of unknown origin, likely coined by Jim Henson.  The word "doozer" (meaning bulldozer) is a shortened form of "bulldozer."
