confix:/ˈkɒnfɪks/ | n. | 缀合词，粘着词 | A confix is an affix which surrounds the root. | Example: enlighten is formed with the confix en-…-en surrounding the root 'light'.  启发这个词是由缀合词 en-…-en 包围词根 'light' 形成的。 | Example:  The confix ge-...-e surrounds the root 'lieb' in the German word 'Geliebte' (beloved). 在德语单词“Geliebte”（心爱的）中，缀合词 ge-...-e 包围着词根“lieb”。 | 近义词: affix | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin con- (together, with) + -fix (attached, fastened), from figere (to fix, fasten).
