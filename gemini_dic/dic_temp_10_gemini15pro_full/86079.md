hears:/hɪərz/ | v. | 听见，听说 | Third person singular present tense form of the verb "hear".  It indicates that someone perceives sound or receives information aurally. | She hears the music playing softly in the distance. 她听到远处轻轻地播放着音乐。 | He hears rumors of a new project at work. 他听到了公司里一个新项目的传闻。 | listens, perceives, catches | ignores, misses | verb

词源：From Middle English *heren, from Old English hēran (“to hear, listen, obey, heed, belong to”), from Proto-Germanic *hauzjaną (“to hear”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱh₂eus- (“to perceive, hear”). Cognate with West Frisian hearre (“to hear”), Dutch horen (“to hear”), German hören (“to hear”), Danish høre (“to hear”), Swedish höra (“to hear”), Icelandic heyra (“to hear”), Latin caveō (“I beware”), Ancient Greek κλύω (klúō, “I hear”).
