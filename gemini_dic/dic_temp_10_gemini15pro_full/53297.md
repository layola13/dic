dirty: /ˈdɜːrti/ (British) /ˈdɜːrti/ (American)| adj. n. v. | 脏的；下流的；卑鄙的；肮脏的(名词)；弄脏(动词)| Covered or marked with an unclean substance.  Immoral or dishonest. |  The car was dirty after the long drive. 这辆车在长途驾驶后很脏。| 近义词: filthy, soiled, unclean, foul, nasty, sordid, contaminated | 反义词: clean, pure, spotless, immaculate | 词性: adjective, noun, verb

词源: From Old English *dyriht, *dryge  "excrement," related to Old Norse drit "excrement."  The sense evolution went from "covered in excrement" to "covered in any filth" to "morally unclean."
