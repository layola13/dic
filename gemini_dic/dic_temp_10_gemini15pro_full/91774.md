hyperenthusiasm:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˈθuːziæzəm/| n.|过度热情，极度热情|Excessive enthusiasm; extreme eagerness or excitement.|He approached the project with hyperenthusiasm, which sometimes overwhelmed his colleagues. 他以极大的热情投入到这个项目中，有时让他的同事们难以招架。|His hyperenthusiasm for the new band bordered on fanaticism. 他对这支新乐队的过度热情近乎狂热。|近义词:zeal, fervor, passion, exuberance|反义词:apathy, indifference, lethargy|noun
