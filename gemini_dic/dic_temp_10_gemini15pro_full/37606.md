communications:/kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n.|通讯，通信；交流，沟通|The company's communications network is very advanced. 这家公司的通讯网络非常先进。|例句：Effective communications are essential in any business. 有效的沟通在任何行业中都至关重要。

例句：The poor communications led to misunderstandings.  沟通不良导致了误解。


|近义词：correspondence, interaction, contact, intercourse|反义词:silence, isolation|词性:名词


词源: Late Middle English: plural of communication, from Old French communicacion or late Latin communicatio(n-), from the verb communicare (see communicate).
