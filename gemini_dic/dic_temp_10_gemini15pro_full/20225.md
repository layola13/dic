birdseeds:/ˈbɜːrdˌsiːdz/
n.
鸟食
A mixture of seeds, such as sunflower, millet, and niger, used as food for birds.
The children scattered birdseeds on the ground for the pigeons. 孩子们把鸟食撒在地上喂鸽子。
近义词:bird food, birdfeed
反义词:None
词性:名词


Etymology:
From bird + seed + -s (plural marker).  "Seed" comes from Old English "sæd."  "Bird" comes from Old English "brid."
