formful:/ˈfɔːrmfʊl/ | adj. | 充满形式的；注重形式的；形式上的 | Having or characterized by pleasing or significant form; shapely.  |  Her prose is formful and elegant. 她的散文形式优美，文雅流畅。 | 近义词: shapely, well-formed, artistic | 反义词: formless, shapeless, amorphous | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From "form" + "-ful".  "Form" comes from Old French "forme," ultimately from Latin "forma" (shape, figure, beauty). "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
