imitability:/ˌɪmɪtəˈbɪləti/
n.
可模仿性，易模仿性
The imitability of a product is a key factor in its market success. 一个产品的可模仿性是其市场成功的关键因素。
The low imitability of the company's technology gave them a competitive advantage.  该公司技术的低可模仿性赋予了他们竞争优势。
Near Synonyms:  reproducibility, copyability, duplicability
Antonyms: originality, uniqueness, inimitability
noun
