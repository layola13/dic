data-processing:/ˈdeɪtə ˈprɑːsesɪŋ/ | n. | 数据处理 | The act of using computers to organize and analyze information. |  Large companies rely heavily on data-processing systems.  大型公司严重依赖数据处理系统。 |  information processing, data analysis | data mismanagement | noun

data-processing:/ˈdeɪtə ˈprɑːsesɪŋ/ | adj. | 数据处理的 | Relating to or involved in the processing of data. | The company invested in new data-processing equipment.  这家公司投资了新的数据处理设备。 | computational, analytical |  N/A | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
