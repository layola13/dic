droughtinesses:/ˈdraʊtinɪsɪz/
n.
干旱；干燥
the state or condition of being affected by drought or lacking sufficient moisture.
The prolonged droughtinesses devastated the crops, leaving the farmers with nothing to harvest. 长时间的干旱摧毁了农作物，使农民颗粒无收。
The region suffered a series of droughtinesses over the past decade. 该地区在过去十年中遭受了一系列的干旱。

反义词:wetness, humidity, dampness
词性:noun (plural)

droughtiness:/ˈdraʊtinɪs/
n.
干旱；干燥
the state or condition of being affected by drought or lacking sufficient moisture.
The droughtiness of the land made it unsuitable for farming. 土地的干旱使其不适合耕种。
The prolonged droughtiness led to water shortages in the city. 长时间的干旱导致城市缺水。
近义词:aridity, dryness, desiccation
反义词:wetness, humidity, dampness
词性:noun

droughty:/ˈdraʊti/
adj.
干旱的；干燥的
characterized by drought or lacking sufficient moisture.
The droughty conditions caused the plants to wither and die. 干旱的条件导致植物枯萎死亡。
The farmers were struggling to cope with the droughty summer. 农民们正努力应对干旱的夏季。
近义词:arid, dry, parched
反义词:wet, humid, damp
词性:adjective

词源：From drought + -y/-ness/-inesses.  "Drought" itself comes from Old English *drugoth, related to "dry."
