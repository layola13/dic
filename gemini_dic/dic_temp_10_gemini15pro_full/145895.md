overdifferentiation: /ˌoʊvərˌdɪfəˌrɛnʃiˈeɪʃən/
| n. |过度区分，过度分化|The act of making too many distinctions or subdivisions, often to an unnecessary or excessive degree.  It can refer to the creation of overly specific categories or the overemphasis on minor differences.| Sociologists have noted overdifferentiation in some cultures regarding social status. 社会学家已经注意到一些文化中关于社会地位的过度区分。|  The overdifferentiation of tasks in the company led to inefficiency. 公司内部任务的过度分化导致效率低下。| hyperdifferentiation, overcategorization | underdifferentiation, simplification | noun
