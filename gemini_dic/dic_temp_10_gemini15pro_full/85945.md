headmen:/ˈhedmən/| n. |工头；领班|A foreman or overseer, especially in a mine or factory.|The headmen directed the workers in the factory. 工头指挥工厂里的工人。|Synonyms: foreman, supervisor, overseer|Antonyms: worker, laborer|noun
