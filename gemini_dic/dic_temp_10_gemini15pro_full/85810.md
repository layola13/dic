hazards:/ˈhæzərdz/| v.|冒…的危险；使…遭到危险|To expose to danger or risk.|I wouldn't hazard a guess. 我不敢妄加猜测。|Synonyms: risk, endanger, imperil|Antonyms: protect, safeguard|verb
