neolocal:/ˈniːoʊˌloʊkəl/
adjective
新居的；从夫居的
Relating to a custom in which a married couple resides separately from both the husband's natal household and the wife's natal household.
Neolocal residence is the most common pattern in Western societies.  新居制是西方社会最普遍的模式。
The newlyweds decided on a neolocal residence, finding an apartment in a new city. 这对新婚夫妇决定在新地方安家，在一个新城市找到了一套公寓。
近义词:none (in the strict anthropological sense)
反义词:patrilocal, matrilocal, avunculocal
词性:adjective
