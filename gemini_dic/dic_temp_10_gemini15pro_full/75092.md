frillinesses: /ˈfrɪlinəsɪz/
noun
多余的装饰；花哨；繁琐
Excessive or elaborate ornamentation or decoration; superfluous details or embellishments.

Frillinesses in her dress made her look like a child playing dress-up.
她衣服上的过多装饰让她看起来像个玩变装游戏的孩子。

The frillinesses of the architecture were impressive but ultimately detracted from the building's functionality.
建筑的繁复装饰令人印象深刻，但最终却削弱了建筑的功能性。

Synonyms: embellishments, ornamentations, flourishes, decorations, adornments
Antonyms: simplicity, plainness, austerity, minimalism
词性: 名词


frilly: /ˈfrɪli/
adjective
多褶边的；装饰华丽的；繁琐的
Characterized by frills or excessive ornamentation; elaborate and fussy.

The little girl loved her frilly pink dress.
小女孩喜欢她那件粉红色的褶边连衣裙。

The curtains were frilly and elaborate.
窗帘装饰华丽，十分繁复。

Synonyms: ornate, elaborate, fussy, fancy, decorated
Antonyms: plain, simple, unadorned, austere
词性: 形容词


frill: /frɪl/
noun
褶边；装饰；额外的东西
A strip of gathered or pleated material used as a decoration or trimming; something extra or unnecessary added for embellishment.

The dress had a delicate frill around the neckline.
这件连衣裙的领口有一圈精致的褶边。

He considered the extra buttons on the jacket to be a frivolous frill.
他认为夹克上多余的纽扣是无用的装饰。

Synonyms: ruffle, flounce, furbelow, trimming, decoration
Antonyms: plainness, simplicity
词性: 名词


frill: /frɪl/
verb
给…加上褶边；装饰
To decorate with a frill or frills; to add something extra or unnecessary for embellishment.

She frilled the edges of the tablecloth.
她在桌布的边缘加上了褶边。

The company frilled up the basic model with a lot of unnecessary features.
该公司在基本型号上添加了许多不必要的功能。


Synonyms: decorate, adorn, ornament, embellish, trim
Antonyms: simplify, strip
词性: 动词

词源:
From Middle English *frillen, probably frequentative of Old French friller (“to fray, shred”), from Frankish *frizōn (“to curl”).
