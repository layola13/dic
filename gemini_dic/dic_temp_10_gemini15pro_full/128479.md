newspaperdom:/ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpərˌdəm/| n. | 报界，新闻界 |The state of the newspaper industry; the world of newspapers.  It refers to all the people involved in producing, publishing, and distributing newspapers. | The decline of newspaperdom has been a topic of much discussion. 报界衰落一直是人们热议的话题。|  The internet has significantly impacted newspaperdom. 互联网对报界产生了重大影响。| 近义词：press, journalism, the fourth estate | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


Etymology:
newspaper + -dom.  "Newspaper" dates back to the late 17th century, referring to a periodical publication containing news. "-dom" is a suffix of Germanic origin, meaning "state, condition, or domain."  Therefore, "newspaperdom" literally means the "domain of newspapers."
