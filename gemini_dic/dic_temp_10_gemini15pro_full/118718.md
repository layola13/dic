metafictionists:/ˌmɛtəˈfɪkʃənɪsts/
n.
元小说家；后设小说家
Writers of metafiction, a type of fiction that self-consciously addresses the devices of fiction.
The conference attracted prominent metafictionists from around the world.  这次会议吸引了来自世界各地的著名元小说家。
Contemporary metafictionists often blur the lines between reality and fiction. 当代元小说家经常模糊现实与虚构之间的界限。
Synonyms: postmodern novelists, experimental writers
Antonyms: traditional novelists, realist writers
noun
