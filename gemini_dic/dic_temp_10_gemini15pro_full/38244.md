computerizes:/kəmˈpjuːtəraɪzɪz/
verb
计算机化；用计算机处理
converts to a computer system or introduces computers
The factory has been fully computerized.  这家工厂已经完全计算机化了。
The company computerized its records. 公司用电脑储存记录。
automate, digitize
manual
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From computer + -ize.  Ultimately from Latin computare.
