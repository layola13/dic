elusory:/ɪˈluːsəri/ | adj. | 虚幻的，迷惑人的 | Based on or having the nature of an illusion; deceptive. |  The elusory promise of easy money led many into trouble. 容易赚钱的虚幻承诺让很多人陷入了困境。

The elusory nature of happiness makes it all the more precious. 幸福的虚幻本质让它更加珍贵。

| 近义词：deceptive, misleading, illusory, fallacious, evasive | 反义词：real, tangible, concrete, substantial, genuine | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *elūsōrius, from Latin ēlūsus (past participle of ēlūdere, “to elude, mock, deceive”), from ē- (“ex-”) + lūdere (“to play”).
