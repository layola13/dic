abada:/əˈbɑːdə/
n.
犀牛
A rhinoceros.
The abada is a mythical beast, often depicted as a rhinoceros or unicorn.  这头阿巴达是一只神话中的野兽，通常被描绘成犀牛或独角兽。
The explorer claimed to have seen an abada in the jungle.  探险家声称在丛林中看到了一头阿巴达。
近义词:rhinoceros, unicorn
词性:名词


abada:/əˈbɑːdə/
n.
一种衣料，阿巴达呢
A rich silk, usually striped.
She wore a gown of shimmering abada. 她穿着一件闪闪发光的阿巴达长袍。
The abada fabric was prized for its softness and durability.  阿巴达布料因其柔软性和耐用性而备受推崇。
近义词:silk, fabric, textile
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Portuguese *abada*, possibly from Arabic *ʾabbād* (“striped cloth”).  The rhinoceros sense may be a misunderstanding or misapplication of the term, perhaps influenced by descriptions of the Indian rhinoceros's thick, folded skin resembling armor or layered cloth.

USAGE->
The abada, a type of rhinoceros, is rarely seen.  这种犀牛，阿巴达，很少见到。
The queen's robe was made of the finest abada silk.  女王的长袍是用最好的阿巴达丝绸制成的。

He was hunting for abada in Africa. 他在非洲猎杀犀牛。
The merchant sold bolts of beautiful abada. 商人出售漂亮的阿巴达布匹。
