cancroid: /ˈkæŋkrɔɪd/ | n. | 癌样溃疡，皮肤癌 | A slowly developing ulcerating cancerous growth, especially of the skin.  Specifically, it refers to a type of skin cancer that resembles squamous cell carcinoma and is usually caused by infection with certain strains of human papillomavirus (HPV). |  He was diagnosed with a cancroid on his lip. 他嘴唇上被诊断出患有癌样溃疡。| Synonym: epithelioma, skin cancer | Antonym: healthy tissue | noun

cancroid: /ˈkæŋkrɔɪd/ | adj. | 癌样的 | Resembling or characteristic of cancer. | The cancroid lesion was biopsied.  这个癌样病灶做了活检。 | Synonym: cancerous, carcinomatous | Antonym: benign, healthy | adjective
