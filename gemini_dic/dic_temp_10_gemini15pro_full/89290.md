homoeomeric:/ˌhɒmiːəˈmɛrɪk/ |adj.|同质的，由相同部分组成的|Composed of similar parts or units; homogeneous.  Referring especially to muscles composed entirely of one type of fiber (e.g., all fast-twitch or all slow-twitch).|例句：The homoeomeric structure of the muscle allowed for efficient contraction. 这块肌肉的同质结构使其能够有效收缩。

Etymology:  From Greek *homoios* (similar) + *meros* (part).
