foreseeing:/fɔːrˈsiːɪŋ/
adjective
预见的，有先见之明的
The company was praised for its foreseeing investment in new technology.  该公司因其对新技术的预见性投资而受到赞扬。
foreseeing the potential problems, they took steps to avoid them. 预见到潜在的问题，他们采取了措施来避免它们。
近义词:prescient, anticipatory, visionary
反义词:shortsighted, myopic
词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *forseinge, from Old English *forseoende, present participle of forseon (“to despise, scorn, condemn, refuse, renounce, foresee”), from Proto-Germanic *faurasehwaną (“to foresee”), equivalent to fore- +‎ see.
