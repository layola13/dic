kettleful:/ˈkɛt(ə)lfʊl/
noun
一壶水; 满壶
The amount that a kettle can hold.
She boiled a kettleful of water for tea. 她烧了一壶水泡茶。
Synonyms: potful,  a full kettle
Antonyms:  a drop, a little bit
