arty-farty:/ˈɑːti ˈfɑːti/ | adj. | 附庸风雅的；装腔作势的；矫揉造作的 |  Pretentious or affectedly artistic.  Relating to or characteristic of art or artists, often used in a slightly derogatory way to imply pretentiousness or excessive concern with artistic matters. |  "That gallery is full of arty-farty types."  那个画廊里满是附庸风雅的人。 | Synonyms: pretentious, affected, highfalutin, pompous, ostentatious | Antonyms: down-to-earth, unpretentious, genuine, sincere | adjective
