bract:/brækt/| n. |苞片；苞|A modified leaf, usually small, growing at the base of a flower or flower cluster.|The bracts of the poinsettia are often mistaken for petals. 一品红的苞片经常被误认为是花瓣。|Synonyms: scale, spathe|Antonyms: N/A|noun

bract:/brækt/| n. |苞片；苞|A specialized leaf or leaf-like structure, typically small and associated with a flower or inflorescence, often differing in shape, color, or texture from foliage leaves and sometimes resembling petals.|The vibrant red bracts of the bougainvillea attract pollinators. 九重葛鲜艳的红色苞片吸引传粉者。|Synonyms:  floral leaf, scale leaf|Antonyms: N/A|noun
