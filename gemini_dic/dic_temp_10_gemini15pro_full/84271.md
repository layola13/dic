Expressing oneself incompletely or hesitantly.|He gave a half-syllabled reply, seemingly afraid to commit to a full answer. 他含糊地回答，似乎不敢做出完整的回答。|近义词：mumbled, muttered, indistinct, hesitant|反义词：clear, articulate, distinct|词性:形容词
