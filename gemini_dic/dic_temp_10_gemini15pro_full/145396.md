overcausticity:/ˌoʊvərˌkɔːˈstɪsɪti/| n.|过度刻薄；过度腐蚀性|Excessive causticity; excessive sharpness or bitterness of temper or speech, or excessive corrosive properties.|Her overcausticity alienated even her closest friends. 她过分的刻薄甚至疏远了她最亲密的朋友。|The overcausticity of the cleaning solution damaged the countertop.  清洁液的过度腐蚀性损坏了台面。|近义词：acrimony, bitterness, mordancy, corrosiveness|反义词：geniality, mildness, blandness|词性:名词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + causticity (noun derived from caustic, from Latin causticus, from Greek kaustikos, "burning," from kaiein, "to burn").
