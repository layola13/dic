axium:/ˈæksiəm/| n. |公理；格言；自明之理|A statement or proposition which is regarded as being established, accepted, or self-evidently true.|Mathematics is based on certain axioms. 数学是建立在某些公理之上的。| synonym: principle, theorem, postulate| antonym: paradox, absurdity |noun
