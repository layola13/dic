allineation:/əˌlaɪnˈeɪʃən/ |n.|结盟，联合，一致|The two countries formed an allineation to defend against their common enemy. 这两个国家结成联盟，共同防御敌人。|例句：Their allineation with the rebels brought them international condemnation. 他们与叛军的联盟招致了国际社会的谴责。

The allineation between the two political parties surprised many. 这两个政党之间的联合让许多人感到惊讶。|近义词：alliance, union, coalition, partnership|反义词：estrangement, division, disunity|词性:名词

Etymology: From French *alignement*, from *aligner* "to align," from Old French *à ligne* "into line," from Latin *ad lineam* "to the line."
