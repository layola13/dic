exhorters:/ɪɡˈzɔːrtərz/
n.
劝告者，激励者
People who urge or encourage others to do something, especially something good or noble.
The conference was attended by motivational speakers and exhorters from around the country.  这次会议有来自全国各地的励志演说家和激励者参加。
The president's speech was full of exhortations to the nation. 总统的讲话充满了对国家的劝勉。
近义词：motivators, encouragers, inspirers, urgers
反义词：discouragers, deterrents, dissuaders
词性: noun (plural)

exhorter:/ɪɡˈzɔːrtər/
n.
劝告者，激励者
A person who exhorts.
He was a powerful exhorter, inspiring his followers to greater efforts. 他是一位强有力的激励者，激励他的追随者更加努力。
She acted as an exhorter for the cause, tirelessly encouraging others to join. 她作为这项事业的倡导者，不懈地鼓励其他人加入。
近义词：motivator, encourager, inspirer, urger
反义词：discourager, deterrent, dissuader
词性: noun (singular)


词源: From the verb "exhort," which comes from the Latin "exhortari," meaning "to encourage strongly."  源自动词“exhort”，该词来自拉丁语“exhortari”，意思是“强烈鼓励”。
