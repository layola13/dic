flatbeds:/ˈflætˌbɛdz/| n.|平板卡车，平板拖车|A truck or trailer with a flat bed, used for carrying large or awkward loads.|The flatbed was loaded with construction materials. 平板卡车上装满了建筑材料。|synonyms:  flatbed truck, flatbed trailer, lowboy (for especially heavy loads), platform truck|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider) enclosed trailer, van, box truck|noun

flatbed:/ˈflætˌbɛd/| adj.|平板的|Having a flat bed, typically referring to a truck or trailer.|They used a flatbed truck to transport the heavy machinery. 他们用一辆平板卡车来运输重型机械。|synonyms: flat, level|antonyms:  sloped, inclined|adjective
