mow-down:/moʊ daʊn/| v. | 击倒，摧毁，杀死 | To kill or injure a large number of people indiscriminately, especially with gunfire or a vehicle. | The soldiers mowed down the enemy troops with their machine guns. 士兵们用机枪扫射敌军。

The car mowed down several pedestrians. 汽车撞倒了几名行人。 | 近义词：slaughter, massacre, decimate | 反义词：protect, save, defend | 词性: 动词

词源: From "mow" (to cut down grass) + "down" (to the ground), implying cutting down people like grass.
