hourer:/ˈaʊərər/ | n. | 计时员；按小时计酬者 | A person who works or is paid by the hour. | Example: The hourer was paid $15 per hour.  计时员的时薪是15美元。| Example: Many factory workers are hourers. 许多工厂工人是按小时计酬的。 | Synonyms: worker, employee, laborer | Antonyms: employer, manager | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "hour" + "-er" (suffix denoting a person or thing that performs an action or has a particular characteristic).  "Hour" comes from Old French "hore," ultimately from Latin "hora."
