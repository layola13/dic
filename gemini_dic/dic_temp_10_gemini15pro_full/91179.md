hydrargyrum:/haɪˈdrɑːrdʒərəm/
n.
水银；汞
A silvery-white metallic element, liquid at room temperature.  It is used in thermometers, barometers, and other scientific instruments.
Mercury is a toxic heavy metal. 汞是一种有毒的重金属。
The thermometer contained hydrargyrum. 温度计里含有水银。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin hydrargyrum, from Ancient Greek ὑδράργυρος (hudrárguros, “liquid silver, quicksilver”), from ὕδωρ (húdōr, “water”) + ἄργυρος (árguros, “silver”).
