hyperapophysis:/ˌhaɪpərəˈpɒfɪsɪs/
noun
突起，过度生长，赘生骨
A bony outgrowth or process, especially an abnormal or excessive one.
The paleontologist identified a hyperapophysis on the dinosaur's vertebra.  古生物学家在恐龙的椎骨上发现了一个赘生骨。
The hyperapophysis on the bone indicated a previous injury.  骨头上的赘生骨表明之前受过伤。
Synonyms: outgrowth, process, projection, excrescence
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, consider words like:  depression, concavity, indentation)
n.


Etymology:
From hyper- + apophysis.  Hyper- is a Greek prefix meaning “over, above, excessive.”  Apophysis is from the Greek "apophusis" meaning "a growing out, offshoot."
