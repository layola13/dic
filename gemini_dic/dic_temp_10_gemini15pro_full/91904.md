It refers to instances where logic is applied rigidly and without consideration for context, emotions, or other relevant factors. |  His arguments, while technically sound, descended into hyperlogicalities that ignored the human element.  他的论点虽然在技术上是合理的，但却陷入了忽略了人文因素的超逻辑性。 |  overthinking, excessive analysis, hairsplitting, casuistry | impracticality, irrationality | noun
