pacifico:/pəˈsɪfɪkoʊ/| adj. n.|太平洋的；平静的；太平洋啤酒(Pacifico Clara)| Relating to the Pacific Ocean; calm and peaceful; a brand of Mexican beer. |例句：The Pacifico region is known for its beautiful beaches. 太平洋地区以其美丽的海滩而闻名。

例句：He has a pacifico nature. 他性情平和。|近义词：calm, tranquil, serene, peaceful, placid|反义词：turbulent, agitated, violent, stormy, aggressive|词性:形容词, 名词

pacifico:/pəˈsɪfɪkoʊ/| n.|太平洋啤酒(Pacifico Clara)|A brand of Mexican beer. |例句：I enjoy a cold Pacifico on a hot day. 我喜欢在大热天喝一杯冰镇太平洋啤酒。

例句：He ordered a Pacifico at the bar. 他在酒吧点了一杯太平洋啤酒。|近义词：beer, lager|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific beer brand) |词性:名词

词源: From Spanish *Pacífico*, "Pacific", ultimately from Latin *pacificus*, "peace-making, peaceful".
