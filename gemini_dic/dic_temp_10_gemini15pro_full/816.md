abstemiousnesses: /ˌæbstɪˈmiːəsnɪsɪz/
|n.|节制，禁欲|The state of being abstemious; moderation, especially in eating and drinking.|His abstemiousnesses surprised his friends who were used to his lavish lifestyle. 他的节制让习惯了他奢华生活方式的朋友们感到惊讶。|
| |They admired her abstemiousnesses in a world of excess. 在一个充满过度放纵的世界里，他们钦佩她的节制。|
| |近义词:  abstinence, sobriety, temperance, moderation|
| |反义词: indulgence, excess, gluttony|
| |词性: 名词 (plural of abstemiousness)|

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *abstemiosus* + *-ness*.  *abstemiosus* is from Latin *abs* (from) + *temetum* (strong drink).  The suffix *-ness* forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
