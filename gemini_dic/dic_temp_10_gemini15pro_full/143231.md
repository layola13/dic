outdare:/ˌaʊtˈdɛər/
verb
超越；胜过；在胆量上超过
To surpass or exceed in daring; to challenge successfully by greater daring.
He outdared his rival by jumping from the higher cliff. 他跳下更高的悬崖，在胆量上胜过了他的对手。
Synonyms: outbrave, surpass, exceed
Antonyms: cower, yield, submit
词性: 动词
