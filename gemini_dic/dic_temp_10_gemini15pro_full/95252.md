incanescent:/ˌɪnkænˈdɛsənt/
adj.
白炽的；发出白热光的；辉煌的
Emitting light as a result of being heated.  Shining brilliantly.
The metal was incanescent from the heat of the furnace.  金属因炉子的高温而白炽发光。

The incanescent glow of the lava lit up the night sky. 熔岩的白炽光芒照亮了夜空。

近义词:glowing, incandescent, radiant, luminous, bright
反义词:dark, dim, dull, unlit, extinguished
词性:adjective

词源: From Latin incandescens, present participle of incandescere (“to glow, to become white-hot”), from in- (“in, on, at”) + candescere (“to become white-hot, to glow”), from candēre (“to glow, to shine”).
