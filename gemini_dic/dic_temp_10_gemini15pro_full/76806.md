gammoning:/ˈɡæmənɪŋ/
noun
欺骗；哄骗；胡说八道
The act of deceiving or hoaxing someone, often in a playful or teasing way.  It can also refer to nonsense or meaningless talk.
He accused her of gammoning him with a sob story.  他指责她用一个悲伤的故事欺骗他。
deception, hoaxing, trickery
truth, honesty
n.

gammoning:/ˈɡæmənɪŋ/
verb
欺骗；哄骗；胡说
To deceive or hoax someone, often in a playful or teasing way. It can also mean to talk nonsense or meaninglessly.
He tried to gammon the guards into letting him escape. 他试图哄骗警卫让他逃跑。
deceive, hoax, trick
be honest, tell the truth
v.
