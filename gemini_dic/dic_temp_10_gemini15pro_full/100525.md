intervisible:/ˌɪntəˈvɪzəbl/
adjective
相互可见的

例句：The watchtowers were placed at intervisible points along the border. 瞭望塔被设置在边界沿线相互可见的地点。

反义词：invisible, hidden, obscured
词性: adjective
