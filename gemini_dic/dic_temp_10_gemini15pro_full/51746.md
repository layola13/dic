devolvement:/ˌdiːˈvɒlvmənt/ |/ˌdiːˈvɑːlvmənt/|n.|下放，移交；权力下放|The devolution of power from central government to the regions. 将权力从中央政府下放到地方。|近义词：decentralization, delegation, transfer|反义词：centralization|词性:名词

devolvement:/ˌdiːˈvɒlvmənt/ |/ˌdiːˈvɑːlvmənt/|n.|衰落，退化（较少用）|The devolution of a species. 物种的退化。|近义词：degeneration, decline, deterioration|反义词：evolution, progress|词性:名词
