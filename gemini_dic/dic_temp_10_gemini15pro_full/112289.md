loquaciousness:/ˌloʊkweɪʃəsnəs/ | n. | 饶舌，健谈，多话 | The quality of being loquacious; talkativeness. |  His loquaciousness made it difficult to get a word in edgewise.  他太健谈了，别人很难插上一句话。 | Synonyms: talkativeness, garrulity, volubility | Antonyms: reticence, taciturnity | noun

loquacious:/loʊˈkweɪʃəs/ | adj. | 健谈的，多话的 | Tending to talk a great deal; talkative. | He was a loquacious and entertaining companion. 他是一个健谈且有趣的同伴。| Synonyms: talkative, garrulous, voluble | Antonyms: reticent, taciturn | adjective
