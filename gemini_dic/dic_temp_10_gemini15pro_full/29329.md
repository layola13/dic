castigatory:/ˈkæstɪɡətɔːri/
adjective|惩罚性的；惩戒的|Relating to or involving punishment or correction.|The teacher gave the student a castigatory glance. 老师严厉地瞪了学生一眼。|
近义词:punitive, disciplinary, corrective|反义词:lenient, forgiving, rewarding|词性:adjective

castigatory:/ˈkæstɪɡətɔːri/
noun|惩罚场所；惩戒所|A place of punishment or correction.|The old school building was used as a castigatory for disobedient children.  这栋旧校舍曾被用作惩戒不听话孩子的场所。|近义词:prison, jail, reformatory|反义词:haven, sanctuary, refuge|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *castigatorius, from Latin castigare (“to correct, chastise”), from castus (“pure”) + agere (“to do, drive”).
