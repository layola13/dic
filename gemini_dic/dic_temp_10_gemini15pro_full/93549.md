imaginativeness:/ɪˌmædʒɪnətɪvnəs/
n.|想象力; 创造力|The quality of being imaginative; the power to create new images or concepts.|Her imaginativeness allows her to create fantastical worlds in her writing. 她丰富的想象力使她能够在写作中创造奇幻的世界。|近义词：creativity, ingenuity, inventiveness, originality|反义词：lack of imagination, unimaginativeness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From imaginative + -ness.  "imaginative" comes from Late Middle English *imaginatif*, from Medieval Latin *imaginativus*, from Latin *imaginari* "to form an image, imagine," from *imago* "image."  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being."
