contemplative:/ˈkɒntəmplətɪv/ (US) /kənˈtemplətɪv/ (UK)| adj.|沉思的；冥想的；深思熟虑的|Given to or characterized by contemplation; thoughtful.  Engaged in contemplation or reflection.|She sat in a contemplative pose, gazing out the window. 她坐着沉思，凝视着窗外。|He spent a contemplative afternoon reading poetry by the lake. 他在湖边读诗，度过了一个沉思的下午。|近义词：reflective, meditative, thoughtful, pensive, introspective|反义词：rash, impulsive, unthinking, frivolous|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin contemplativus, from Latin contemplari ‘gaze attentively at’.
