malamethane: /ˌmæləˈmɛθeɪn/
noun
丙二酸二乙酯
diethyl malonate, also known as DEM, is the diethyl ester of malonic acid. It is a common reagent in organic synthesis used in malonic ester synthesis.
Diethyl malonate is used in the synthesis of barbiturates.
近义词: DEM, diethyl propanedioate
反义词: N/A
词性: n.
