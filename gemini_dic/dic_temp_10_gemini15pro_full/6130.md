amiability:/ˌeɪmiəˈbɪləti/| n. |友善，和蔼，亲切|The quality of being amiable; having a pleasant and friendly disposition.|Her amiability made her popular. 她的和蔼可亲使她很受欢迎。|近义词：affability, friendliness, geniality, cordiality, warmth|反义词：unfriendliness, hostility, coldness, sullenness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *amiabilité*, from amiable + -ité.  Ultimately from Latin *amābilis* ("lovable"), from *amāre* ("to love").
