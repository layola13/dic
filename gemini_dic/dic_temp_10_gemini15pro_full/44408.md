cross-leggedness:/ˌkrɒsˈlɛɡɪdnəs/ | n. | 盘腿，盘坐 |The state or posture of having one's legs crossed, typically at the knees. |He sat in cross-leggedness on the floor. 他盘腿坐在地板上。|She maintained her cross-leggedness throughout the meditation.  她在整个冥想过程中都保持着盘腿的姿势。| 近义词: cross-legged position, tailor fashion | 反义词: (No direct antonym, consider perhaps: standing, outstretched legs) | noun

cross-legged:/ˌkrɒsˈlɛɡɪd/ | adj. | 盘腿的，交叉腿的 |Having the legs crossed, typically at the knees. |He sat cross-legged on the floor. 他盘腿坐在地板上。|She sat cross-legged, reading a book. 她盘腿坐着看书。| 近义词:  cross-legged, tailor fashion | 反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider: straight-legged) | adjective
