outdrink: /ˌaʊtˈdrɪŋk/
verb
胜过…的酒量；比…喝得更多
To drink more alcohol than someone else.
He can outdrink anyone in the bar. 他比酒吧里的任何人都能喝。
Synonyms: outmatch, surpass
Antonyms: be outdrunk by
词性: 动词

outdrink: /ˌaʊtˈdrɪŋk/
verb
比…更能喝
To be able to consume more alcoholic beverages than someone else.
She can outdrink all her friends. 她比她所有的朋友都能喝。
Synonyms: drink more than
Antonyms: drink less than
词性: 动词
