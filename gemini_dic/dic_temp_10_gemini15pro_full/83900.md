half-asleep:/ˌhɑːf əˈsliːp/ /ˌhæf əˈsliːp/
adjective
半睡半醒的，昏昏欲睡的
Being in a state between sleeping and waking; drowsy.
例句：I was half-asleep when the phone rang. 我在电话响的时候处于半睡半醒的状态。

例句：He stumbled into the kitchen, half-asleep and still in his pajamas. 他跌跌撞撞地走进厨房，半睡半醒，还穿着睡衣。

近义词：drowsy, sleepy, dozy, somnolent
反义词：awake, alert, wide-awake
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"half" + "asleep"
"half" (Old English healf) meaning "one of two equal parts of a whole"
"asleep" (Old English on slæpe) meaning "in a state of sleep"
