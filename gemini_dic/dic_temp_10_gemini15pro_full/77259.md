gastropod:/ˈɡæstrəpɑːd/| n. |腹足纲动物；腹足类动物|A mollusk of the large class Gastropoda, such as a snail, slug, or whelk.  They are characterized by a single shell (often coiled) and a broad, muscular foot.|Snails and slugs are common gastropods.  蜗牛和蛞蝓是常见的腹足纲动物。|Synonyms: univalve, snail, slug|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a taxonomic class)|noun
