co-payment:/ˈkoʊˌpeɪmənt/ | n. | 共付额，共同付款 | A fixed amount that an insured person must pay for covered healthcare services, in addition to what the insurer pays. | Example 1: My co-payment for a doctor's visit is $25. 我的每次看医生的共付额是25美元。

Example 2: The co-payment for this prescription is $10.  这张处方的共付额是10美元。 | Synonym: cost-sharing, out-of-pocket expense | Antonym: full coverage | noun
