hemisystole:/ˌhɛmiˈsɪstəli/
noun
半收缩
Half of a systole; specifically, the contraction of only one ventricle of the heart.
During fetal development, hemisystole is a normal phenomenon. 在胎儿发育过程中，半收缩是一种正常现象。
Hemisystole can be a sign of serious heart conditions. 半收缩可能是严重心脏疾病的征兆。
