mayorships:/ˈmeɪərʃɪps/| n. |市长职位；市长任期|The period of time during which someone serves as mayor, or the office itself.  It refers to the position and tenure of a mayor.|She held two consecutive mayorships. 她连续担任了两届市长。|Synonyms: mayoralty, administration, incumbency|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "citizenry," "public," representing the governed rather than the governor) | noun
