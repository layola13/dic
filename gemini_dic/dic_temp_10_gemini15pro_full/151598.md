partialise:/ˈpɑːrʃəlaɪz/ /ˈpɑːrʃəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 使...偏袒；使...不公正；偏袒；偏爱 | To make someone biased or prejudiced, especially in favor of one side or person; to show favoritism. | The judge was accused of partialising towards the defendant.  法官被指控偏袒被告。| Synonyms: favor, prefer, bias, prejudice | Antonyms:  impartial, neutral, unbiased, objective | verb

partialise:/ˈpɑːrʃəlaɪz/ /ˈpɑːrʃəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 部分化；分割 | To divide something into parts or sections. |  The company decided to partialise its operations into smaller, more manageable units. 公司决定将其业务部分化成更小、更易于管理的单元。 | Synonyms: divide, separate, segment, compartmentalize | Antonyms: combine, unite, integrate, merge | verb
