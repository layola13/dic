one-off:/ˈwʌnˌɒf/ (UK) /ˈwʌnˌɔːf/ (US)| n.|一次性事物|A thing that is made or happens only once.|例句：It was a one-off payment. 这是一次性付款。|近义词：rarity, anomaly|反义词:norm, standard|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "one" + "off," originally a printing term referring to a single impression or print.  It broadened in usage in the mid-20th century to mean anything occurring only once.
