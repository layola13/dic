ohps:/oʊps/ | interj. | 哎哟；糟糕 | Used to express mild apology or surprise, especially when one has made a small mistake or caused a slight accident. | Oops, I spilled the coffee. 哎哟，我洒了咖啡。 | 近义词: whoops, uh-oh | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: interjection
词源:  Likely a shortening of "whoops," which is imitative in origin.
