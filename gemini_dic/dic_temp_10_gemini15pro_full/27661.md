candidatures:/ˈkændɪdətʃəz/
n.
候选资格；候选人身份；申请
the state of being a candidate in an election or for a job or prize; applications
Many people submitted their candidatures for the vacant position. 许多人提交了空缺职位的申请。
nominations, applications, submissions
rejections, withdrawals
noun
