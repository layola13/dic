devolutionists:/ˌdiːvəˈluːʃənɪsts/
n.
权力下放主义者；地方分权主义者
A person who advocates devolution (the transfer of power from a central government to regional or local government).
The devolutionists argued for greater regional autonomy.  权力下放主义者主张更大的区域自治。
Scotland has seen a rise in support for devolutionists in recent years. 近年来，苏格兰对权力下放主义者的支持有所上升。
近义词:decentralists, regionalists, autonomists
反义词:centralists, unitarians
词性:noun
