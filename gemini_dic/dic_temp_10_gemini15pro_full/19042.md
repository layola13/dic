bethabara:/bɛθˈæbərə/
proper noun
伯大尼
A place east of the Jordan River, mentioned in the New Testament as the location where John the Baptist baptized.
John the Baptist baptized Jesus in Bethabara. 约翰在伯大尼为耶稣施洗。

synonym: Bethany beyond the Jordan, Bethania
antonym:  (No direct antonym for a place name)
词性: proper noun
