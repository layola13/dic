duenya:/ˈdwenjə/ | n. | 女伴；陪伴老年妇女或年轻未婚妇女的年长已婚妇女；监护人；（尤指西班牙或葡萄牙的）年长已婚妇女（负责监管年轻女性） | A duenna is an older woman who is responsible for supervising young women, especially in Spanish or Portuguese cultures. |  Her aunt acted as her duenna, accompanying her everywhere. 她的姑妈充当她的女伴，陪她去任何地方。

The family hired a duenna to chaperone their daughter.  这家人雇了一位年长已婚妇女来陪伴他们的女儿。 | 近义词: chaperone, governess, companion | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "charge" in the sense of someone being supervised) | 词性: noun
