altarpiece: /ˈɔːltərpiːs/ | n. | 祭坛画，祭坛装饰 | A work of art, especially a painting, sculpture, or carved panel, placed above and behind an altar in a Christian church. | The altarpiece depicts the Crucifixion.  祭坛画描绘了耶稣受难的场景。| The altarpiece was richly adorned with gold leaf.  祭坛装饰着丰富的金箔。 | synonyms: reredos, retable | antonyms: None | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *alterpece*, equivalent to *alter* (altar) + *pece* (piece).  The word refers to a decorative piece placed above and behind an altar.
