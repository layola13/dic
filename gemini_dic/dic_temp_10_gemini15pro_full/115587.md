maraud:/məˈrɔːd/| v. n.| 抢劫，掠夺；袭击；海盗行为|To roam in search of things to steal or people to attack.  To raid for plunder.|例句：The Vikings marauded along the coast. 维京人沿着海岸掠夺。

例句：Bands of soldiers marauded through the countryside, stealing food and livestock. 士兵成群结队地在乡间掠夺，偷窃食物和牲畜。|近义词：raid, pillage, plunder, loot, foray|反义词：protect, defend|词性：动词，名词

maraud:/məˈrɔːd/| n.| 抢劫，掠夺；袭击；海盗行为|A raid for plunder.|例句：The city was subjected to nightly marauds. 这座城市每晚都遭到抢劫。

例句：The museum was the victim of a daring maraud. 博物馆遭到了一次大胆的抢劫。|近义词：raid, incursion, attack|反义词：defense, protection|词性：名词
