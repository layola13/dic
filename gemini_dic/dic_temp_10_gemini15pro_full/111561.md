lobefoot:/ˈloʊbfʊt/| n. | 裂足动物 | An animal with lobe-shaped feet, typically a member of the phylum Lobopodia, which includes extinct and extant worm-like animals with stubby legs. |  Example:  Lobefoot fossils provide insights into the evolution of arthropods. 裂足动物化石为了解节肢动物的进化提供了线索。
