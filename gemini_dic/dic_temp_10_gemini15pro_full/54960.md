disprofess:/ˌdɪsprəˈfɛs/ | v. |否认；放弃；拒绝承认|To deny or renounce a belief, claim, or association that one previously held. | He disprofessed his former religious beliefs. 他否认了他以前的宗教信仰。|  She disprofessed any knowledge of the crime. 她否认对这起犯罪有任何了解。|近义词：renounce, repudiate, deny, disavow, disclaim|反义词：profess, avow, affirm, acknowledge|词性：动词

Etymology:
From dis- + profess, from Latin *disprofiteri, from dis- "apart, away" + profiteri "to declare publicly, profess," from pro- "forth" + fateri "to confess."
