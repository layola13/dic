institutionalizing: /ˌɪnstɪˈtuːʃənəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使制度化；使机构化；使成惯例 | The process of establishing something as a normal, accepted part of a system or organization.  |  The government is institutionalizing new regulations for environmental protection. 政府正在将新的环境保护法规制度化。 |  They are institutionalizing a new policy for employee training. 他们正在将新的员工培训政策制度化。 |  formalizing, standardizing, systematizing |  abolishing, dismantling | verb
