endureth:/ɪnˈdjʊərəθ/| v.| 持续，忍耐，持久 |Archaic third-person singular present form of endure.  It means "lasts" or "continues to exist".  This form is no longer commonly used in modern English.|例句：He endureth great hardship. 他忍受着巨大的苦难。|近义词：lasts, persists, remains, abides, suffers, tolerates|反义词:perishes, succumbs, yields|词性:verb
