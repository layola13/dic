imputably:/ɪmˈpjuːtəbli/
adjective
可归咎地，可归罪地
in a way that can be imputed or attributed to someone or something.
The accident was imputably caused by the driver's negligence. 这场事故可归咎于司机的疏忽。
近义词:attributably, accountably
反义词:unaccountably, irresponsibly
词性:adjective

imputing:/ɪmˈpjuːtɪŋ/
verb
归罪；归咎；归因于
represent (something, especially something undesirable) as being done, caused, or possessed by someone; attribute.
The crimes imputed to Richard. 归咎于理查德的罪行。
近义词:attribute, ascribe, assign
反义词:exculpate, absolve
词性:verb (present participle of impute)

impute:/ɪmˈpjuːt/
verb
归罪；归咎；归因于
represent (something, especially something undesirable) as being done, caused, or possessed by someone; attribute.
People often impute his silence to unfriendliness. 人们常常把他的沉默归咎于不友好。
近义词:attribute, ascribe, assign
反义词:exculpate, absolve
词性:verb
