earthworks:/ˈɜːrθwɜːrks/| n. |土方工程；土木工事|Earthworks are engineering works created through the processing of parts of the earth's surface involving quantities of soil or unformed rock.|The Roman earthworks are still visible today. 罗马时代的土方工程今天仍然可见。

The extensive earthworks required for the new highway took several months to complete.  修建新公路所需的大量土方工程耗时数月才完成。|近义词：excavation, embankment, fortification, rampart|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "leveling")|词性:noun
