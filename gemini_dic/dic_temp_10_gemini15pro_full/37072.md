combattant:/kɒmˈbætənt/ (UK), /kəmˈbætənt/ (US)| n. | 参战者，战斗员 |A person who takes part in fighting, especially in a war.| The Geneva Conventions protect the rights of combatants and non-combatants during wartime.  日内瓦公约在战时保护战斗人员和非战斗人员的权利。|Synonyms: fighter, soldier, warrior| Antonyms: civilian, noncombatant | noun

combattant:/kɒmˈbætənt/ (UK), /kəmˈbætənt/ (US)| adj. | 战斗的，好战的 |Engaged in fighting; ready or eager to fight. |  He had a combattant spirit, always ready to defend his beliefs. 他有一种战斗精神，随时准备捍卫自己的信仰。  |Synonyms: belligerent, pugnacious, aggressive | Antonyms: peaceful, pacifistic, nonviolent | adjective
