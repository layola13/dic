dictatress:/dɪkˈteɪtrəs/ | n. | 女独裁者，女暴君 | A female dictator, a woman who rules oppressively and autocratically. |  She ruled as a dictatress, silencing all opposition. 她以女独裁者的身份统治，压制所有反对派。

The dictatress demanded complete obedience from her subjects.  这位女暴君要求臣民绝对服从。 | 近义词：autocrat, tyrant, despot | 反义词：democrat | 词性：名词

Etymology:
From Latin *dictatrix*, feminine form of *dictator*, from *dictare* "to say often, prescribe," frequentative of *dicere* "to say, speak."
