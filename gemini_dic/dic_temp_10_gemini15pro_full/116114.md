marvelling: /ˈmɑːvəlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːrvəlɪŋ/ (US)
verb (present participle)
惊叹；感到惊奇
Feeling or expressing great surprise or admiration.
She stood marvelling at the beauty of the sunset. 她站在那里，惊叹日落的美丽。
The children were marvelling at the magician's tricks. 孩子们对魔术师的戏法感到惊奇。
Synonyms: wondering, admiring, awestruck
Antonyms: unimpressed, indifferent
