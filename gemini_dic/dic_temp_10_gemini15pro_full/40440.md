contraster:/kənˈtræstər/ | n. | 对比者，对比物 |Something that contrasts or is used to contrast. | The black and white squares on the chessboard serve as effective contrasters.  棋盘上的黑白方块起到有效的对比作用。 | Synonyms: foil, antithesis | Antonyms: complement, harmonizer | noun

contrasting:/kənˈtrɑːstɪŋ/ | adj. | 对比的，形成对照的 | Showing marked difference when compared. | The contrasting colors of the flowers made a beautiful display.  鲜花对比鲜明的颜色构成了美丽的景象。 | Synonyms: different, dissimilar, divergent | Antonyms: similar, alike, uniform | adjective
