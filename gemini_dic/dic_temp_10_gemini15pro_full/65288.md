euclidean:/juːˈklɪdiən/
adjective
欧几里得的; 欧式几何的
Relating to Euclid or his geometry, especially the geometry of spaces with a notion of distance defined by the Pythagorean theorem.
Euclidean geometry is the study of plane and solid figures. 欧几里得几何学是对平面和立体图形的研究。
Euclidean space is a mathematical concept. 欧几里得空间是一个数学概念。
Near Synonyms: geometric, geometrical
Antonyms: non-Euclidean
