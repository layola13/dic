cemeterial:/ˌseməˈtɪəriəl/
adjective
墓地的；与墓地有关的
relating to a cemetery or burial ground.
The cemeterial silence was broken only by the chirping of crickets. 墓地般的寂静只有蟋蟀的鸣叫声打破。
The groundskeeper maintains the cemeterial pathways meticulously. 墓地管理员一丝不苟地维护着墓地的道路。
近义词: sepulchral, funerary
反义词: lively, vibrant
词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *coemeterialis, from Latin coemeterium (“cemetery”).
