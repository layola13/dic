hydride:/ˈhaɪdraɪd/| n. |氢化物|A binary compound of hydrogen with a more electropositive element or group.|Lithium aluminum hydride is a powerful reducing agent. 氢化铝锂是一种强还原剂。|近义词: compound |反义词: None |词性:noun

Etymology->
From hydro- + -ide, from French hydrure. Ultimately from Ancient Greek ὕδωρ (húdōr, “water”) + -ide.
