nonmasculinities:/ˌnɒnˌmæskjʊˈlɪnɪtiz/ | n. |非男性气质 | Qualities or ways of being that are not traditionally associated with masculinity.  It often refers to expressions of gender that challenge or subvert dominant norms of manhood. |  The book explores the diverse spectrum of nonmasculinities present in contemporary society. 这本书探讨了当代社会中存在的各种非男性气质。| Near synonyms:  alternative masculinities, non-hegemonic masculinities | Antonym: masculinity | Noun
