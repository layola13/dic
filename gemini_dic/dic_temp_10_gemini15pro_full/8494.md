antibiotics:/ˌæntibaɪˈɒtɪks/ | n. |抗生素|A medicine (such as penicillin or its derivatives) that inhibits the growth of or destroys microorganisms.|The doctor prescribed antibiotics for her infection. 医生给她开了抗生素治疗感染。|Synonyms: antimicrobials, antibacterials|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "probiotics" which promote bacterial growth)|noun

antibiotic:/ˌæntibaɪˈɒtɪk/ | adj. |抗生的；抗菌的|Relating to or produced by antibiotics; tending to destroy life.|The antibiotic properties of penicillin were discovered by Alexander Fleming. 青霉素的抗菌特性是由亚历山大·弗莱明发现的。|Synonyms: antibacterial, antimicrobial|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|adjective
