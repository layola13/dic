multiformity:/ˌmʌltɪˈfɔːrmɪti/| n. |多样性，多形性|The multiformity of the city's cultural landscape is breathtaking.  这座城市文化景观的多样性令人叹为观止。| The artist explored the multiformity of human emotions in his work. 这位艺术家在他的作品中探索了人类情感的多样性。|近义词：diversity, variety, multiplicity|反义词：uniformity, homogeneity|词性: noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin multiformitas, from multiformis (“multiform”), from Latin multus (“much, many”) + forma (“form”).
