imperspicuity:/ˌɪmpərspɪˈkjuːɪti/
|n.|晦涩难懂，费解，不明确|The imperspicuity of the legal jargon made it difficult for the average person to understand the contract.  法律术语的晦涩难懂使得普通人难以理解合同。|例句：The professor's lectures were known for their imperspicuity, leaving students often confused.  这位教授的讲座以晦涩难懂而闻名，经常让学生感到困惑。|近义词：obscurity, unclarity, vagueness|反义词：clarity, perspicuity, lucidity|词性:名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin imperspicuitas, from imperspicuus ‘not clear’ (see imperspicuous).
