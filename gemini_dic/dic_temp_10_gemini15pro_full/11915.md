arroyos:/əˈrɔɪoʊz/
n.
(通常干涸的)小河，溪流；沟壑
A watercourse in an arid or semiarid region, often dry except after heavy rains.
Flash floods are common in arroyos.  山洪暴发在干涸的河床上很常见。
synonyms: creek, gully, wadi, ravine
antonyms: river, ocean
noun
