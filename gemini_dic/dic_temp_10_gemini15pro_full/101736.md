irresistibleness:/ˌɪrɪˈzɪstəbəlnəs/
n.
不可抗拒性; 无法抗拒
The quality of being impossible to resist.
Her irresistibleness drew him in like a moth to a flame. 她的魅力让他像飞蛾扑火般被吸引。
allure, magnetism, charm
resistance, repulsiveness
