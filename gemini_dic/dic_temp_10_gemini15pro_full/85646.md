hauntings:/ˈhɔːntɪŋz/
n.
萦绕；困扰；常去的地方
The act of a ghost or spirit repeatedly appearing in a place or to a person.  The experience of being troubled by a repeated thought, image, or memory.  A place frequently visited.
The hauntings in the old house scared the family away. 这栋老房子里的闹鬼事件把这家人吓跑了。
近义词:apparitions, visitations, obsessions, memories
反义词:peace, tranquility
词性:名词


haunting:/ˈhɔːntɪŋ/
adj.
令人难忘的；萦绕心头的；常浮现于脑海中的
Evoking a poignant sense of sadness or regret.  Difficult to ignore or forget.
The haunting melody stayed with me for days.  这首令人难忘的旋律萦绕在我脑海中好几天了。
近义词: unforgettable, memorable, poignant, evocative
反义词:forgettable, unremarkable
词性:形容词



词源:
From Middle English *haunten, from Old North French *hanter (“to frequent, practice”), of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Frankish *heim (“home”) (whence also English home).
