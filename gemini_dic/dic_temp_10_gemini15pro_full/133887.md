nongrievously: /nɒnˈɡriːvəsli/
adverb
非严重地；不严重地
Not in a grievous or serious manner; lightly.
The patient was nongrievously injured in the accident. 病人在事故中受了轻伤。
Synonyms: lightly, mildly, slightly, superficially
Antonyms: grievously, seriously, severely
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + grievously.  "Grievously" comes from the Old French "grevos" meaning heavy or burdensome.  "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not".
