crosser:/ˈkrɒsər/ (UK), /ˈkrɔːsər/ (US)| n.| 渡河者，穿越者，杂交者|Someone or something that crosses, especially a person or animal that breeds two different species. |例句：The experienced crosser navigated the river with ease.  经验丰富的渡河者轻松地渡过了河。

例句：He is a known crosser of different breeds of dogs. 他是一位知名的不同犬种杂交者。|近义词：hybridizer, crossbreeder (for breeding), traveler, voyager (for crossing)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
