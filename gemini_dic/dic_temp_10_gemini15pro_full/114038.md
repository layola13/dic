magnesium:/mæɡˈniːziəm/| n.|镁|A light, silvery-white, metallic element, the lightest of the structural metals. It burns with a dazzling white light and is used in alloys, pyrotechnics, and flash photography.|Magnesium is essential for human health. 镁对人体健康至关重要。|Magnesium alloys are used in aerospace applications due to their lightweight and high strength. 镁合金因其轻质高强度的特性被用于航空航天应用。|Synonyms: Mg (symbol), element|Antonyms: None|noun
