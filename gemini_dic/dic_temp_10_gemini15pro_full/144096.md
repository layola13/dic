outsatisfied: /ˌaʊtˈsætɪsfaɪd/
verb
比...更满意
to satisfy more than or to a greater degree than another.
She outsatisfied her customers with exceptional service. 她以卓越的服务让顾客比预期的更满意。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel
反义词: disappoint, underperform
词性: verb


outsatisfied: /ˌaʊtˈsætɪsfaɪd/
adjective
更满意的
more satisfied than another or than expected.
The customers were outsatisfied with the new product. 顾客对新产品比预期的更满意。
近义词: more satisfied, happier, more content
反义词: dissatisfied, unhappy, discontented
词性: adjective
