flagration:/fləˈɡreɪʃn/
n.
大火灾，熊熊燃烧，爆发
a large destructive fire; a blaze
The forest fire was a flagration that destroyed thousands of acres. 这场森林大火是一场烧毁了数千英亩土地的熊熊烈火。
近义词：inferno, blaze, conflagration
反义词：deluge, flood
