counterpicket:/ˈkaʊntərˌpɪkɪt/| n. |反纠察队；反警戒线|A picket formed to oppose another picket.|The union organized a counterpicket to face the company's hired security guards. 工会组织了一支反纠察队，对抗公司雇佣的保安。|Synonyms: counter-demonstration, counter-protest|Antonyms: picket, demonstration|noun


counterpicket:/ˈkaʊntərˌpɪkɪt/| v. |进行反纠察|To picket in opposition to another picket line.|They counterpicketed the factory to protest the strikebreakers. 他们对工厂进行反纠察，抗议破坏罢工的人。|Synonyms: counter-demonstrate, counter-protest|Antonyms: picket, demonstrate|verb
