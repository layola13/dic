ichnite:/ˈɪknaɪt/| n. |足迹，化石足迹|A fossilized footprint.  A track or footprint of an extinct animal. |The paleontologist discovered an ichnite of a dinosaur in the sandstone.  古生物学家在砂岩中发现了恐龙的足迹。|Synonyms: footprint, track, trace, imprint|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical trace, but conceptually: absence, blank)|noun
