overtop:/ˌoʊvərˈtɑːp/ | v. | 超越，胜过；覆盖，淹没 | To rise above or extend over the top of; to surpass or defeat. | The floodwaters overtopped the levee. 洪水漫过了堤坝。| 近义词: surpass, exceed, overwhelm, inundate | 反义词:  be inferior to, lose to | 词性: 动词


overtop:/ˌoʊvərˈtɑːp/ | adj. |  (罕见) 上层的，最高的 | Situated above or on top.  | the overtop branches of the tree 树最上面的枝杈 | 近义词: uppermost, topmost | 反义词: bottom, lowest | 词性: 形容词



词源: From over + top.  "Over" indicates position above, and "top" refers to the highest point. The combination creates the meaning of going over the top of something.
