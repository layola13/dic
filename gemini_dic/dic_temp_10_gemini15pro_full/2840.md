adventurism:/ədˈvɛntʃərɪzəm/
n.
冒险主义；投机主义
The tendency to engage in risky or reckless behavior, especially in political or military affairs, without proper planning or consideration of the potential consequences.
His adventurism in the stock market led to significant financial losses. 他在股市的冒险主义导致了严重的经济损失。

The general's adventurism put the entire mission at risk.  将军的冒险主义使整个任务处于危险之中。

近义词: recklessness, rashness, audacity, foolhardiness
反义词: caution, prudence, circumspection
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From adventure + -ism.  "Adventure" comes from Old French "aventure" (chance, happening), ultimately from Latin "adventura" (things about to happen).  "-ism" is a suffix forming nouns of action, practice, principle, etc.
