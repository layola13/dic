coevolution:/ˌkoʊˌɛvəˈluːʃən/ | n. |共同进化|The influence of closely associated species on each other in their evolution.|

例句：The coevolution of predator and prey is a classic example of natural selection. 捕食者和猎物的共同进化是自然选择的一个典型例子。

例句：The coevolution of flowering plants and their pollinators is a fascinating area of study.  开花植物和它们的传粉者的共同进化是一个引人入胜的研究领域。


近义词：coadaptation, reciprocal adaptation|反义词:|词性:noun
