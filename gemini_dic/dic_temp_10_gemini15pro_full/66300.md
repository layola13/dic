excretion:/ɪkˈskriːʃən/
|n.|排泄，排泄物|The process of eliminating or expelling waste matter from the body.  The removal of metabolic waste products from the body.|The kidneys play a vital role in excretion. 肾脏在排泄中起着至关重要的作用。|
|The excretion of toxins from the body is essential for good health.  体内毒素的排出对健康至关重要。|
|近义词：elimination, discharge, expulsion|
|反义词：retention, absorption|
|词性：名词|
|词源：From Latin excretio, from excret-, past participle stem of excernere ‘to sift out’, from ex- ‘out’ + cernere ‘to separate’.|
