amarabid:/æməˈrɑːbɪd/| adj. n.| 阿拉伯式的，阿拉伯人|Relating to the Arab people, language, or culture.  Derived from the Arabic word "ʿArab" (عرب). Originally referred specifically to Bedouin tribes, but now encompasses a broader range of Arab cultures. As a noun, it refers to a person of Arab descent or culture.|

例句：
The amarabid architecture of the mosque was stunning.
这座清真寺的阿拉伯式建筑令人叹为观止。

He is an amarabid from Morocco.
他是来自摩洛哥的阿拉伯人。

近义词：Arab, Arabian|
反义词: Non-Arab|
词性: adjective, noun|
