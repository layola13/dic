frozenness:/ˈfroʊzənnəs/| n. |冰冻状态，冻结状态|The frozenness of the lake prevented us from skating. |例句：The frozenness of the ground made it difficult to dig.  地面冻结得很厉害，很难挖。

The extreme frozenness of the meat made it difficult to thaw. 肉冻得非常硬，很难解冻。|近义词：frigidity, iciness |反义词:thaw, warmth |词性:noun

Etymology: From "frozen" + "-ness." "Frozen" comes from the Old English "freosan," meaning "to freeze." The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
