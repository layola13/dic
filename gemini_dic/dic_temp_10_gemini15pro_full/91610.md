hyperangelic:/ˌhaɪpərænˈdʒɛlɪk/| adj.|超天使的，超越天使的|Exceeding the qualities of angels; more than angelic.  Characterized by a beauty, purity, or goodness beyond that normally attributed to angels.|Her voice was hyperangelic, so pure and ethereal it seemed to come from another realm. 她的声音如同超天使般，如此纯净空灵，仿佛来自另一个世界。|The painting depicted a hyperangelic being, radiating light and love. 这幅画描绘了一个超天使般的存在，散发着光芒和爱。|近义词：divine, celestial, ethereal, seraphic|反义词：demonic, infernal, hellish|词性:adjective
