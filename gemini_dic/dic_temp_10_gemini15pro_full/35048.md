closedness:/ˈkloʊzdnɪs/ /ˈkləʊzdnɪs/
n.
封闭性，闭合性
The closedness of the community made it difficult for outsiders to integrate.  这个社区的封闭性使外来者难以融入。
The mathematical concept of closedness refers to a set remaining unchanged after an operation.  封闭性这个数学概念指的是一个集合在运算后保持不变。
近义词:closure, insularity, exclusiveness
反义词:openness, accessibility, inclusivity
词性:名词

Etymology: From "closed" + "-ness." "Closed" comes from the Old English "clūsed," past participle of "clēosan" (to close), related to the Old High German "chlūsan."  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
