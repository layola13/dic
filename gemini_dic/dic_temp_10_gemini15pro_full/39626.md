consequence:/ˈkɒnsɪkwəns/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsɪkwɛns/ (US)| n. |结果，后果；重要性，重大|a result or effect of an action or condition|
The consequences of her actions were severe. 她的行为后果很严重。

consequence:| n. |结果，后果；重要性，重大|something that happens as a result of a particular action or set of conditions|
He broke the law, and now he has to face the consequences. 他违反了法律，现在必须承担后果。

consequence:| n. |重要性，重大|importance or significance|
His work is of little consequence. 他的工作没什么重要性。


近义词: result, outcome, effect, ramification
反义词: cause, origin, source
词性: noun

词源:
From Old French *consequence (Modern French conséquence), from Latin consequentia (“a following, a result”), from consequi (“to follow after”), from con- (“with, together”) + sequi (“to follow”).
