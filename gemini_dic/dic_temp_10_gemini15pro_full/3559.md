agamogenesis:/ˌeɪɡæmoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
| n. |无性生殖；无配子生殖|Reproduction without the fusion of gametes.|Agamogenesis is a common reproductive strategy in single-celled organisms like bacteria. 无性生殖是像细菌这样的单细胞生物的常见繁殖策略。|例句：Many plants propagate through agamogenesis, such as through budding or fragmentation. 许多植物通过无性生殖繁殖，例如通过出芽或断裂。

|例句：The study of agamogenesis is crucial for understanding the evolution of reproductive strategies. 对无性生殖的研究对于理解生殖策略的进化至关重要。|近义词：asexual reproduction, vegetative reproduction|反义词：sexual reproduction, gamogenesis|词性: noun
