ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *Cornyshe, *Cornysch, from Old English *Cornwīlisċ (attested as Cornwālisċ), equivalent to Cornwalas (genitive plural of Cornwalas, an inhabitant of Cornwall) + -isc. Cognate with West Frisian Kornwalsk, Dutch Kornouws. More at Cornwall.
