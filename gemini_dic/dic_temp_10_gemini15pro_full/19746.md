billers:/ˈbɪlərz/| n. |开账单的人，收款人|People or organizations who issue bills or invoices for goods or services.|The billers sent out invoices to all customers at the end of the month. 月底，收款人向所有客户发送了发票。|The company hired new billers to handle the increased workload. 公司雇佣了新的开票员来处理增加的工作量。|synonyms: invoicers, collectors|antonyms: payers, debtors|noun
