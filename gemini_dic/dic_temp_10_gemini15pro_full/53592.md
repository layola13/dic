disbursers:/dɪsˈbɜːrsərz/ | n. |支付者，付款人| People or organizations that pay out money, often from a fund. | The disbursers of the grant ensured the funds reached the intended recipients. 支付者确保拨款到达了预定的接受者。| Synonyms: payers, distributors, allocators | Antonyms: recipients, collectors | noun

disburser:/dɪsˈbɜːrsər/ | n. |支付者，付款人| A person or organization that pays out money, often from a fund. | The disbuser of the loan was a local bank.  这笔贷款的支付者是一家当地银行。| Synonyms: payer, distributor, allocator | Antonyms: recipient, collector | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desboursier, from des- (dis-) + bourse (purse), ultimately from Late Latin bursa (leather bag, purse).
