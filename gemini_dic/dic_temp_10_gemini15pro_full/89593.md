hoodless:/ˈhʊdləs/| adj.|无帽的，没有帽子的|Without a hood. This word describes something that lacks a hood, typically a garment.|The hoodless coat offered little protection against the biting wind. 这件无帽大衣几乎无法抵御刺骨的寒风。|Near synonyms: uncovered, unhooded|Opposite: hooded|adjective
