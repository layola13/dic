bivouac: /ˈbɪvuæk/ (US), /ˈbɪvuæk/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|露营；临时宿营|A temporary camp without tents or cover, used especially by soldiers or mountaineers.|例句：The soldiers made bivouac for the night. 士兵们扎营过夜。

例句：They bivouacked near the summit. 他们在山顶附近露营。|近义词：camp, encampment, lodge, shelter|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) permanent residence, established dwelling|词性: 名词, 动词
