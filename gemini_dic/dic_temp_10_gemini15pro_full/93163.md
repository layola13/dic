igniters:/ɪɡˈnaɪtərz/
|n.|点火器，点燃器|A device used to ignite a fuel mixture, such as in a gas appliance or internal combustion engine.|The igniters on the gas stove weren't working.  煤气炉上的点火器坏了。|Synonyms: lighter, spark plug, detonator|Antonyms: extinguisher, suppressant
|词性:名词

igniter:/ɪɡˈnaɪtər/
|n.|点火器，点燃器|A person or thing that kindles or sets something on fire (literally or figuratively).|He was the igniter of the revolution. 他是革命的点燃者。|Synonyms: instigator, catalyst, initiator|Antonyms: peacemaker, mediator
|词性:名词
