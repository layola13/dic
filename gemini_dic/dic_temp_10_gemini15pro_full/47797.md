decapod:/ˈdekəpɒd/ (UK) /ˈdekəˌpɑːd/ (US)| n. |十足目动物；十足动物|An animal, such as a crab, lobster, shrimp, or prawn, that has ten legs, specifically five pairs of appendages on the thorax.|例句：The decapod crustaceans include crabs and lobsters. 十足目甲壳类动物包括螃蟹和龙虾。

例句：The giant squid is not a decapod, as it has eight arms and two tentacles.  大王乌贼不是十足目动物，因为它有八条腕足和两条触须。


|近义词：crustacean|反义词:|词性:noun
