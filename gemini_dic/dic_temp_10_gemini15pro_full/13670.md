attractionally:/əˈtrækʃənəli/
adverb|吸引地，有吸引力地|in a way that attracts someone or something; appealingly|例句：The food was attractionally presented. 食物摆放得很有吸引力。|近义词：appealingly, enticingly, alluringly, charmingly|反义词：repulsively, unattractively|词性:副词

Etymology:
From "attraction" + "-al" (forming adjectives) + "-ly" (forming adverbs).  "Attraction" comes from Late Latin *attractionem (nominative attractio) "a drawing to," noun of action from past-participle stem of attrahere "draw to," from ad- "to" + trahere "to draw."
