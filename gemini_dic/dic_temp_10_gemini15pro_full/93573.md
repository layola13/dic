imago:/ɪˈmeɪɡoʊ/ (US), /ɪˈmɑːɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. | 成虫；意象；理想中的形象 |The final stage of an insect's metamorphosis, when it reaches its adult form.  A mental image, especially of a person, usually formed in childhood and remaining influential throughout life. |例句：The butterfly emerged from its chrysalis in its imago form. 蝴蝶从蛹中羽化成成虫。

例句：He had an imago of his father as a strong and silent man. 他心目中父亲的形象是一位强壮而沉默寡言的人。|近义词：adult, image, ideal |反义词: larva, pupa |noun
