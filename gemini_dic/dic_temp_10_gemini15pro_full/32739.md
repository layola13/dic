chloropeptic:/ˌklɔːrəˈpɛptɪk/
adjective
氯胃蛋白酶的; 与胃蛋白酶和盐酸有关的
Relating to or promoting the secretion of gastric juice, especially its hydrochloric acid and pepsin.  Characterized by excessive secretion of hydrochloric acid in the stomach.
Chloropeptic disorders can lead to ulcers. 氯胃蛋白酶紊乱会导致溃疡。
近义词:  hyperchlorhydric
反义词:  hypochlorhydric
词性: adjective
