etrurian:/ɪˈtrʊəriən/ /ɪˈtruːriən/
adjective|伊特鲁里亚的；伊特鲁里亚人的|Relating to Etruria, an ancient country in central Italy, or its people or culture.|The museum houses a fascinating collection of Etruscan artifacts.  这家博物馆收藏了大量迷人的伊特鲁里亚文物。|
noun|伊特鲁里亚人|A native or inhabitant of ancient Etruria.|The Etruscans were skilled metalworkers. 伊特鲁里亚人都是技艺精湛的金属工匠。|
近义词:Tuscan (loosely)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:adjective, noun
词源：From Latin *Ētruscus*, related to Greek *Τυρρηνός (Turrhēnós)*.
