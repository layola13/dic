exilition: /ˌɛksɪˈlɪʃən/
n.
流放，放逐;驱逐出境
The act of exiling someone, or the state of being exiled.
He was punished by exilition. 他被放逐作为惩罚。
近义词: banishment, deportation, expulsion, exile
反义词: repatriation, return
