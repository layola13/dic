parterre:/pɑːrˈtɛər/ | n. | 花坛；花圃 |A level space in a garden or park that is occupied by an ornamental arrangement of flower beds.  Also, the part of the ground floor of a theater beneath the balconies. | The parterres were meticulously designed. 花坛经过精心设计。| The audience in the parterre cheered enthusiastically. 正厅的观众热情地欢呼。 | Synonyms: flower beds, flower gardens, orchestra (in theater context) | Antonyms: wasteland, wilderness, balcony (in theater context)| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *parterre*, from *par* (on, by) + *terre* (earth, ground). Ultimately from Latin *terra* (earth).
