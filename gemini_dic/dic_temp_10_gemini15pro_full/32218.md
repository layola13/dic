chich: /tʃɪtʃ/
noun
小鸡;雏鸡
a young chicken or other bird
例句：The chich pecked at the grain. 小鸡啄食谷粒。

例句：The farmer carefully gathered the chichs. 农夫小心地把小鸡聚集起来。

近义词：chick, chicken (young), hatchling, fledgling
反义词：hen, rooster, adult bird
词性：n.
