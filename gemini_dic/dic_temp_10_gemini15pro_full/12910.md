assurednesses:/əˈʃʊərdnɪsɪz/
|n.|确信；把握；自信|The state of being assured; certainty or confidence.|His assurednesses about the future of the company inspired confidence in the investors. 他对公司未来的信心激发了投资者的信心。|
|近义词：certainties, confidences, assurances|
|反义词：doubts, uncertainties, hesitations|
|词性：名词|


ETYMOLOGY->
From assuredness + -es (plural suffix).  Assuredness comes from assured + -ness. Assured comes from the Old French *asseurer (to assure), from Latin ad- (to) + securus (secure).
