absorbency:/əbˈsɔːrbənsi/ /əbˈzɔːrbənsi/
noun
吸收性，吸水性
The ability of a material to absorb liquids or gases.  A material's capacity to soak up a substance.
This towel has excellent absorbency.  这条毛巾吸水性非常好。
The diaper's absorbency kept the baby dry overnight.  尿布的吸收性使婴儿整夜保持干燥。
近义词: absorption, porosity, permeability
反义词: impermeability, impenetrability
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From absorb + -ency.  Absorb comes from Latin absorbere "to suck in, swallow down," from ab- "away" + sorbere "to suck in."  The suffix -ency forms nouns of quality or state.

The absorbency of the soil is crucial for plant growth. 土壤的吸水性对于植物生长至关重要。

Different types of fabrics have varying degrees of absorbency. 不同类型的织物具有不同程度的吸水性。

The scientist measured the absorbency of the material using specialized equipment. 科学家使用专门的设备测量了材料的吸水性。
