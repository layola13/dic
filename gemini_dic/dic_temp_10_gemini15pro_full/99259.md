intercomplimentary:/ˌɪntərˌkɑːmplɪˈmɛntəri/ | adj. | 互相补充的，相辅相成的 | Serving to complement each other mutually. |  The two systems are intercomplimentary, each providing what the other lacks. 这两个系统相辅相成，各自提供对方所缺乏的东西。|  The intercomplimentary nature of their skills made them a powerful team.  他们技能的互补性使他们成为一个强大的团队。| 近义词: complementary, synergistic, mutually reinforcing | 反义词: conflicting, contradictory, incompatible | 词性: adjective

词源: "inter-" (between, among) + "complimentary" (expressing praise or admiration; serving to complete something). The prefix "inter-" indicates a reciprocal relationship between the elements being described.
