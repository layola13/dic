eulogium:/juːˈləʊdʒiəm/
n.
formal praise; eulogy
a speech or piece of writing that praises someone or something highly, typically someone who has just died.
The professor delivered a moving eulogium at the memorial service for his mentor.  教授在他的导师追悼会上发表了感人的颂词。
Synonyms: tribute, commendation, panegyric, encomium, accolade
Antonyms: criticism, condemnation, denunciation
noun
