hid:/hɪd/| v. (past tense of hide)|隐藏，躲藏|The thief hid the stolen jewels in a secret compartment. 小偷把偷来的珠宝藏在一个秘密隔间里。|He hid from the police in an abandoned building. 他在一栋废弃的建筑里躲避警察。|近义词：concealed, secreted, stashed|反义词：revealed, exposed|词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hȳdan, from Proto-Germanic *hūdaną, from Proto-Indo-European *keudh- (“to hide, conceal”).  Cognates include German hüten (“to guard, watch over”), Latin cūstos (“guardian”), and Sanskrit gudhá (“hidden”).
