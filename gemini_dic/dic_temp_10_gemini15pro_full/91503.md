hymnlike:/ˈhɪmnˌlaɪk/
adjective
像赞美诗的，赞美诗似的
Resembling or characteristic of a hymn; having a reverent and solemn quality.
The music was hymnlike and uplifting.  音乐如同赞美诗一般，令人振奋。
The slow, hymnlike melody filled the church. 缓慢的、如同赞美诗的旋律充满了教堂。
近义词: reverent, solemn, devotional, worshipful
反义词: irreverent, secular, profane
词性: adjective
词源: From hymn + -like.
