aridify:/əˈrɪdɪfaɪ/
verb
使干燥，使荒芜
To make or become arid; to dry out.
The ongoing drought is continuing to aridify the region.  持续的干旱正在使该地区越来越干燥。
The climate change is gradually aridifying the land, making it unsuitable for farming. 气候变化正逐渐使土地变得干旱，使其不适合耕种。
近义词:desiccate, parch, dehydrate
反义词:moisten, irrigate, humidify
词性:v.
词源:From arid + -ify.  来自arid（干燥的） + -ify（使...化）。
