parvenu:/ˈpɑːrvənuː/ | n. | 暴发户，新贵 | Someone who has recently become wealthy and who likes to show off their wealth, often in a way that is considered vulgar.  A person of obscure origin who has gained wealth, influence, or celebrity. |  The lottery winner quickly became a parvenu, flaunting his new riches.  那个彩票中奖者迅速变成了一个暴发户，炫耀着他的新财富。|Synonyms: upstart, social climber, nouveau riche | Antonyms: aristocrat, old money | Noun
