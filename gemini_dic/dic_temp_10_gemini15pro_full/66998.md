expiates:/ˈɛkspiˌeɪts/ | v. |赎罪，弥补|To make amends for; to atone for (a sin, crime, etc.).  To avert the consequences of (misconduct) by atonement or propitiation. | He expiated his crime by serving a long prison sentence. 他服了很长的刑期来赎他的罪。 | Synonyms: atone, redeem, make amends for, compensate, rectify | Antonyms: sin, offend, transgress | verb
