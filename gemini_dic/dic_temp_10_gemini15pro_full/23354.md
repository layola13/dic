n.|户外烤肉；烧烤野餐|An outdoor barbecue where meat is cooked over an open fire, a popular social event in South Africa.|We're having a braai this weekend.  我们这周末要举行户外烧烤。|barbecue, cookout|picnic (indoors), indoor gathering|noun
braai/braɪ/ (South African English)
v.|户外烤肉；烧烤|To cook meat over an open fire outdoors.|We braaied some boerewors and steaks. 我们烤了一些香肠和牛排。|barbecue, grill|bake, boil|verb
