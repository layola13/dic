etherlike:/ˈiːθərlaɪk/| adj.|像乙醚的，飘渺的，轻飘飘的|Resembling ether; especially : lacking material substance : immaterial, intangible.  类似以太；尤指：缺乏物质实体：非物质的，无形的。|Her voice was etherlike and haunting. 她的声音飘渺而萦绕。|Synonyms: ethereal, airy, gossamer, insubstantial|Antonyms: substantial, tangible, concrete|adjective

etherlike:/ˈiːθərlaɪk/| adj.|像乙醚的，飘渺的，轻飘飘的|Having a light, airy, or delicate quality, suggesting something not of this world. 具有轻盈、通透或精致的品质，暗示着超凡脱俗。 |The music had an etherlike quality that transported me to another realm. 这音乐有一种飘渺的特质，把我带到了另一个境界。|Synonyms: unearthly, otherworldly, celestial, spiritual|Antonyms: earthly, mundane, material|adjective
