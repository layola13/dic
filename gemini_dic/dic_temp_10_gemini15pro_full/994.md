acaridan: /ˌækəˈraɪdən/
n.
螨虫类的；螨类动物
Relating to, or caused by, acarids (mites and ticks).
例句：The infection is acaridan, caused by a type of mite. 这种感染是由一种螨虫引起的，属于螨类感染。

例句：Acaridan infestations can cause severe skin irritation. 螨虫感染会导致严重的皮肤刺激。


近义词：mite, tick
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a biological classification)
词性:  adjective (形容词)

ETYMOLOGY->
From New Latin *Acarina (now Acari), from Greek ἀκαρής (akarēs, “indivisible, tiny”), from ἀ- (a-, “not”) + κεῖρω (keírō, “to cut, shear”).  The term refers to the mites' small size, seemingly without segmentation.
