lythontriptic:/ˌlɪθɒnˈtrɪptɪk/| adj. n.|溶石的；碎石术|A lythontriptic agent is a medication or other substance that helps to dissolve or break down kidney stones.  Lythontriptic can also refer to the procedure of breaking down kidney stones.|例句：The doctor prescribed a lythontriptic medication to help dissolve the kidney stones. 医生开了溶石的药物来帮助溶解肾结石。

例句：Extracorporeal shock wave lithotripsy (ESWL) is a common lythontriptic procedure. 体外冲击波碎石术是一种常见的碎石术。|近义词：antilithic|反义词: N/A|词性：adjective, noun
