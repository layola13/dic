granite:/ˈɡrænɪt/| n.|花岗岩|A very hard, granular, crystalline, igneous rock consisting mainly of quartz, mica, and feldspar and often used as a building stone.|The kitchen countertops are made of polished granite. 厨房的台面是用抛光花岗岩做的。|近义词：stone, rock|反义词:|noun
