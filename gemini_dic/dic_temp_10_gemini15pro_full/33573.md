ciliata:/ˌsɪliˈɑːtə/ /ˌsɪliˈeɪtə/
noun
纤毛虫类
a large group of protozoans characterized by the presence of cilia.
Ciliata are a diverse group found in various aquatic environments.  纤毛虫是一类多样化的生物，存在于各种水生环境中。
Paramecium is a well-known genus of ciliata. 草履虫是纤毛虫中一个著名的属。
近义词: ciliophoran
反义词: (无明确的反义词，可考虑其他生物类别，例如flagellates鞭毛虫)
词性: n.


Etymology:
From Latin *ciliatus (“furnished with eyelashes”), past participle of ciliare (“to furnish with eyelashes”), from cilium (“eyelid, eyelash”).
