exauctorate: /ˌɛksˈɔːktəreɪt/
verb
discharge; dismiss
To deprive of office or authority; to dismiss from service.
The Roman Senate exauctorated the general after his defeat.  罗马元老院在将军战败后解除了他的职务。
The company exauctorated several employees due to budget cuts. 由于预算削减，公司解雇了几名员工。
近义词: dismiss, discharge, cashier, remove, depose
反义词: appoint, hire, employ, commission
词性: verb


Etymology:
From Latin *exauctoratus*, past participle of *exauctorare*, from *ex-* ("out of, from") + *auctor* ("author, creator, originator; increaser, augmentor; surety, voucher; master, leader, commander").
