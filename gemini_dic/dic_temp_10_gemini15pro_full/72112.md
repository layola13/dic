flocks:/flɒks/ (UK) /flɑːks/ (US)| n.|群;人群;大量;羊群|A large number of birds or animals, especially sheep, goats, or sheep, that are together in one place.|例句：Flocks of birds flew overhead. 大群的鸟儿从头顶飞过。

例句：Flocks of tourists descended on the town. 成群的游客涌入小镇。|近义词：groups, herds, swarms, crowds|反义词:individuals, scattering|词性:名词

flock:/flɒk/ (UK) /flɑːk/ (US)| v.|聚集;成群结队地去|To gather or move in a large group.|例句：People flocked to the beach to enjoy the sunshine. 人们成群结队地去海滩享受阳光。

例句：Shoppers flocked to the sales. 购物者涌向促销活动。|近义词：gather, congregate, assemble|反义词:disperse, scatter|词性:动词
