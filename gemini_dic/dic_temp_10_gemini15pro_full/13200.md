at one's mercy:/æt wʌnz ˈmɜːrsi/
|phrase|
|受…支配；任…摆布；在…的掌握中|
|Being at someone's mercy means being completely under their control and having no power to protect yourself from them.|
|The hostages were at the mercy of the kidnappers.|人质任由绑匪摆布。

|The small boat was at the mercy of the storm.|小船在暴风雨中颠簸。

|in someone's power, under someone's control, in someone's hands, at the mercy of someone|
|in control, safe, protected, independent|
