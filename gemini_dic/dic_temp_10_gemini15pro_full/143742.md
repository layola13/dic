outmaking:/ˌaʊtˈmeɪkɪŋ/
v.
胜过; 超过; 比...做得更好
surpassing or exceeding in performance or achievement.
例句：He outmade all his rivals in sales this quarter. 他在本季度销售额上超过了所有竞争对手。

例句：She consistently outmakes her colleagues in productivity. 她在生产力方面一直胜过她的同事。


近义词：outperform, outdo, surpass, excel, exceed
反义词：underperform, lag behind

词性: 动词 (verb)


词源：
"out-" (prefix meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "make" (verb meaning to create or produce).  The word combines the idea of exceeding in the act of making or producing something, thus surpassing others in achievement.
