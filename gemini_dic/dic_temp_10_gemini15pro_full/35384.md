co-operate:/kəʊˈɒpəreɪt/ (UK) /koʊˈɑːpəreɪt/ (US)| v. |合作，协作|To work together towards a common goal or purpose.|例句：The two companies co-operated on the project.  两家公司合作开展这个项目。

例句：We need to co-operate if we want to succeed. 如果我们想成功，就需要合作。

|近义词：collaborate, work together, team up, join forces|反义词: compete, obstruct, hinder |词性:动词

co-operation:/kəʊˌɒpəˈreɪʃn/ (UK) /koʊˌɑːpəˈreɪʃn/ (US)| n. |合作，协作|The act of working together towards a common goal or purpose.|例句：International co-operation is essential to solve global problems. 国际合作对于解决全球问题至关重要。

例句：The success of the project depended on the co-operation of everyone involved.  这个项目的成功取决于所有参与者的合作。|近义词：collaboration, teamwork, partnership|反义词: competition, conflict |词性:名词


co-operative:/kəʊˈɒpərətɪv/ (UK) /koʊˈɑːpərətɪv/ (US)| adj. |合作的，协作的|Willing to work together towards a common goal or purpose.|例句：She was very co-operative and helpful. 她非常合作，也很乐于助人。

例句：The team adopted a co-operative approach to problem-solving.  该团队采取了一种合作的方式来解决问题。|近义词：collaborative, helpful, accommodating|反义词: uncooperative, unhelpful, obstructive |词性:形容词



词源: From Latin 'co-' (together) + 'operari' (to work).
