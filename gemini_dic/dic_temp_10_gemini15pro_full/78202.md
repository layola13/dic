geotracking:/ˈdʒiːoʊˌtrækɪŋ/
noun
地理追踪；地理定位
The process of tracking the location of something or someone using GPS or other location-based technology.
We used geotracking to monitor the movement of the endangered species. 我们使用地理追踪来监测濒危物种的移动。
The company uses geotracking to monitor its fleet of delivery vehicles.  这家公司使用地理追踪来监控其送货车队。
geolocation, location tracking
