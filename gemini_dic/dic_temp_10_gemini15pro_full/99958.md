interoperable:/ˌɪntərˈɑːpərəbl/| adj.|可互操作的，可互相操作的|Capable of operating with other systems or products, especially those of different manufacturers.|例句：The new software is designed to be interoperable with existing systems.  新的软件被设计成可以与现有系统互操作。

例句：Interoperable systems are crucial for efficient data exchange. 可互操作的系统对于高效的数据交换至关重要。|近义词：compatible, interchangeable|反义词：incompatible, proprietary|词性:adjective
词源:inter- (between) + operable (able to operate)
