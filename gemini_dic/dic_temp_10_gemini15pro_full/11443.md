arguable:/ˈɑːrɡjuːəbəl/ | adj. | 可争辩的；可论证的；有争议的 | Open to argument or debate; not definitely correct. | It's arguable whether his contribution was really so significant.  他的贡献是否真的如此重要是有争议的。|
近义词: debatable, disputable, contestable, questionable | 反义词: undeniable, irrefutable, indisputable, unquestionable | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Late Middle English *arguable, from Old French arguable, from arguer "to argue" + -able.  Ultimately from Latin arguere "to make clear, prove, accuse."
