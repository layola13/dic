decompartmentalizing:/ˌdiːkəmˈpɑːrtˈmɛntəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
消除隔阂；打破界限；去部门化
The act of breaking down barriers between separate departments or areas of thought.
The company is decompartmentalizing its operations to improve communication. 公司正在打破其运营部门之间的隔阂，以改进沟通。
The new software helps decompartmentalize information, making it accessible to everyone.  这款新软件帮助打破信息壁垒，让每个人都能访问信息。
integrating, unifying, merging, consolidating
separating, isolating, dividing, segregating
