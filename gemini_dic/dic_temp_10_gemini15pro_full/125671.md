munchausenism:/mʌnˈtʃaʊzənɪzəm/| n. |孟乔森综合征|A factitious disorder in which a person repeatedly and deliberately acts as if he or she has a physical or mental illness when he or she is not really sick.|John's constant trips to the hospital with fabricated symptoms led doctors to suspect munchausenism.| 近义词: factitious disorder, fabricated illness, pathomimia |反义词: genuine illness |词性: noun

munchausen syndrome by proxy:/mʌnˈtʃaʊzən ˈsɪndroʊm baɪ ˈprɒksi/ | n. |代理型孟乔森综合征|A factitious disorder in which a person, usually a caregiver, deliberately fabricates or induces illness in another person, often a child, to gain attention or sympathy.|The mother's insistence that her child was constantly ill, despite medical tests showing otherwise, suggested munchausen syndrome by proxy. | 近义词: factitious disorder imposed on another (FDIA), medical child abuse| 反义词: genuine caregiving| 词性: noun
