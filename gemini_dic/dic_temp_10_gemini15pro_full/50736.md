derotremata:/ˌdɛrəˈtrɛmətə/| n.|后口动物亚门|A subphylum of chordates that includes the tunicates (also known as urochordates) and the lancelets (also known as cephalochordates).  These animals are characterized by having pharyngeal gill slits at some stage in their life cycle, a dorsal nerve cord, and a notochord. In derotremates, the anus forms before the mouth during embryonic development, hence the name "Deuterostomia," meaning "second mouth."|The sea squirt is a member of the derotremata. 海鞘是后口动物亚门的一员。|Lancelets and tunicates are derotremates. 文昌鱼和海鞘是后口动物。|synonyms: Deuterostomia|antonyms: Protostomia|noun
