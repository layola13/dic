conductress:/kənˈdʌktrɪs/ /kənˈdʌktərɪs/
n.
女售票员；女指挥
a woman who collects fares on a bus or train, or who conducts an orchestra or choir.
The conductress checked our tickets. 女售票员检查了我们的车票。
The orchestra was led by a renowned conductress.  这支管弦乐队由一位著名的女指挥家领导。
