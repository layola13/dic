marabout:/ˈmærəbuːt/| n.| 北非穆斯林圣人，隐士；秃鹳 |A Muslim holy man or hermit in North Africa; a large stork.|例句：The marabout was revered for his wisdom. 这位圣人因其智慧而受到尊敬。

例句：We saw a marabout soaring high above the savanna. 我们看到一只秃鹳在热带草原上空翱翔。|近义词：saint, hermit, stork|反义词: sinner|词性: noun
