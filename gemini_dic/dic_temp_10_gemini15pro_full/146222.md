overexplanation:/ˌoʊvərˌɛkspləˈneɪʃən/
n.|过度解释；解释过多|The act of explaining something too thoroughly, often to the point of confusion or boredom. |She lost me with her overexplanation of the process. 她对这个过程的过度解释让我搞糊涂了。|近义词：verbosity, prolixity|反义词:conciseness, brevity|词性:名词
overexplain:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspleɪn/
v.|过度解释；解释过多|To explain something too thoroughly, often to the point of confusion or boredom. |He overexplained the joke, and it wasn't funny anymore. 他把笑话解释得太多了，一点也不好笑了。|近义词：belabor, overelaborate|反义词:understate, summarize|词性:动词
