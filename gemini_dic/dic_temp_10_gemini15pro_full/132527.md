nondeveloping:/ˌnɒndɪˈvɛləpɪŋ/ | adj. | 非发展中的，不发展的 | Not developing; not undergoing development or growth; underdeveloped. |  The report highlighted the plight of nondeveloping nations.  这份报告强调了不发达国家的困境。|  The nondeveloping economy posed a significant challenge. 不发展的经济带来了重大挑战。| 近义词: underdeveloped, stagnant, undeveloped | 反义词: developing, growing, progressing | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + developing (present participle of "develop," from Old French *desveloper, "to unfold, unwrap," from des- "un-" + voloper "to wrap").
