gieseckite:/ˈɡiːsɪkaɪt/
noun
基性辉绿岩
A pseudomorph of biotite after nepheline.  It is a variety of pinite.
Gieseckite is often found in igneous rocks, particularly those rich in nepheline. 基性辉绿岩常发现于火成岩中，尤其是富含霞石的岩石。
Gieseckite can be identified by its characteristic greenish-gray or brownish color. 基性辉绿岩可以通过其特有的灰绿色或棕褐色来识别。
Synonyms: pinite, liebenerite
Antonyms: N/A
