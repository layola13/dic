cataphract:/ˈkætəˌfrækt/| n. adj.|重装骑兵，全身披甲的|A soldier, both horse and rider, completely covered in armor.|The cataphracts charged into the enemy lines, a wall of steel and muscle.|近义词：armored cavalry, clibanarius|反义词: light cavalry|词性: noun, adjective

cataphract:/ˈkætəˌfrækt/| adj.|全身披甲的|Completely covered in armor; relating to cataphracts (heavily armored cavalry).|The cataphract armor provided excellent protection against arrows and spears.|近义词：armored, mail-clad|反义词: unarmored, exposed|词性: adjective
