entoblast:/ˈɛntəˌblæst/| n. |内胚层细胞|An embryonic cell that differentiates into the endoderm.  It is a cell of the inner cell mass of the blastocyst that differentiates to form the epithelial lining of the gastrointestinal and respiratory tracts.|The entoblast gives rise to the lining of the digestive system.|Synonym: endoblast, hypoblast|Antonym: None|Noun
