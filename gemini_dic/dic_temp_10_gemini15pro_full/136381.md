nonrestoration:/ˌnɑːnrɛstəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 非恢复，不恢复原状 | The nonrestoration of the building to its former glory caused outrage among locals.  这座建筑没有恢复昔日的辉煌，引起了当地人的愤怒。 | The policy of nonrestoration of the wetlands has had a devastating impact on the ecosystem.  不恢复湿地的政策对生态系统造成了毁灭性的影响。 |  近义词: non-renewal, neglect, abandonment | 反义词: restoration, renewal, rehabilitation | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "restoration" (from Late Middle English, from Old French "restauracion," from Latin "restaurationem," noun of action from past-participle stem of "restaurare" "to restore").
