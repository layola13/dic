infeasible:/ɪnˈfiːzəbl/
adjective
不可行的，办不到的
not capable of being carried out or put into practice; not practicable or workable.
The project was deemed infeasible due to its high cost.  这个项目由于成本高昂被认为是不可行的。
It is infeasible to complete the task within the given timeframe. 在规定的时间内完成这项任务是不可行的。
近义词:impractical, unworkable, impossible, unattainable
反义词:feasible, practical, possible, achievable
词性:adjective

infeasible:/ɪnˈfiːzəbl/
adjective
不可行的，办不到的
Origin: Late Middle English: from in- ‘not’ + feasible.
not capable of being carried out or put into practice; not practicable or workable.
The proposed solution was technically infeasible. 提出的解决方案在技术上是不可行的。
Building a bridge across the ocean is infeasible with current technology. 以目前的科技水平建造一座跨越海洋的大桥是不可行的。
