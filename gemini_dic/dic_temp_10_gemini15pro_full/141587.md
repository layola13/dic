oppressor:/əˈprɛsər/| n.|压迫者，暴君|A person or group that oppresses someone.  A person or group that keeps someone in subservience and hardship, especially by the unjust exercise of authority.|The dictator was a cruel oppressor who denied his people basic human rights.  这个独裁者是一个残酷的压迫者，他剥夺了人民的基本人权。|Synonyms: tyrant, despot, dictator, autocrat|Antonyms: liberator, emancipator, savior, protector|noun
