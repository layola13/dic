gang: /ɡæŋ/ | n. |帮派，团伙，一群人|A group of people who work together or spend time together, often associated with criminal activity, but also used for other groups.|例句：The police arrested the entire gang. 警方逮捕了整个团伙。|例句：A gang of youths were hanging around on the street corner. 一群年轻人在街角闲逛。|近义词：crew, band, group, team, clique|反义词: individual, loner|词性: noun

gang: /ɡæŋ/ | v. |联合起来，成群结队|To form a group or unite against someone.|例句：The other kids ganged up on him and stole his lunch money. 其他孩子联合起来欺负他，偷了他的午餐钱。|例句：They ganged together to protest the new policy. 他们联合起来抗议新政策。|近义词：unite, combine, join forces|反义词: separate, divide|词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *gangr "a going, a course," related to Old English gang "a going, a journey," and German Gang "course, passage, walk, gait."  The sense of "a group of people" developed later.
