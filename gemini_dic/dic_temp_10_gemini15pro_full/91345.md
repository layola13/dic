hydroplaning:/ˈhaɪdrəˌpleɪnɪŋ/| n.|水上滑行，滑水|The car lost control due to hydroplaning on the wet road. 汽车因在湿滑路面上滑水而失控。|hydroplaning occurs when a layer of water builds between the tires of a vehicle and the road surface, leading to a loss of traction. 当一层水在车辆的轮胎和路面之间形成时，就会发生滑水，导致失去牵引力。|例句：The heavy rain caused the car to hydroplane. 大雨导致汽车滑水。

The car hydroplaned across the road and hit a tree. 汽车在路上滑水，撞到了一棵树。|近义词：aquaplaning|反义词: traction |词性:noun
