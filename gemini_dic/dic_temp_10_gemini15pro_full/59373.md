earns:/ɜːrnz/
verb
挣得；赢得；获得
receives (money) in return for work or other activity.

She earns a good salary as a software engineer.
她作为一名软件工程师，薪水很高。

He earned a scholarship to study abroad.
他获得了出国留学的奖学金。

Synonyms: makes, receives, gains, obtains, acquires
Antonyms: loses, forfeits, spends

词性: verb (动词)
