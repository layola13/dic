adstringent:/əˈstrɪndʒənt/ | adj. n. |收敛的，收涩的；收敛剂|Causing the contraction of skin cells and other body tissues; harsh; severe.|
The witch hazel is an astringent, which helps to reduce the size of pores.  金缕梅是一种收敛剂，有助于缩小毛孔。

An astringent mouthwash can temporarily relieve the discomfort of mouth ulcers.  收敛性漱口水可以暂时缓解口腔溃疡带来的不适。|
近义词:styptic, tonic, constrictive, harsh, severe |反义词:soothing, relaxing, emollient|词性:形容词, 名词
