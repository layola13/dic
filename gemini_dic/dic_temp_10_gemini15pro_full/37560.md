commemoration:/kəˌmɛməˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 纪念，庆祝 | the act of remembering officially and giving respect to a great person or event |  A ceremony was held in commemoration of the war dead.  一个仪式是为了纪念战争中的死难者而举行的。 | synonyms: remembrance, celebration, observance | antonyms: disregard, neglect | noun

commemorative:/kəˈmɛməˌreɪtɪv/ | adj. | 纪念性的 | intended to commemorate something or someone |  The commemorative stamp marked the centenary of her birth. 这枚纪念邮票是为了纪念她诞辰一百周年而发行的。 | synonyms: memorial, celebratory, remembrance | antonyms: forgetful, unmindful | adjective
