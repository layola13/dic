astrologists:/əˈstrɒlədʒɪsts/
n.
占星家
A person who studies astrology; a person who practices astrology.
Many astrologists believe that the stars influence our lives. 许多占星家认为星星会影响我们的生活。
Astrologists predicted a difficult year ahead. 占星家预测未来一年会很艰难。

synonyms:  stargazers, soothsayers, diviners, fortune tellers
antonyms: astronomers, scientists, skeptics

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English astrologien, from Old French astrologie, from Latin astrologia, from Ancient Greek ἀστρολογία (astrología), from ἄστρον (ástron, “star”) + -λογία (-logía, “-logy”).
