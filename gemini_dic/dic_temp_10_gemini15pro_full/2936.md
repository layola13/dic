advocating:/ˈædvəkeɪtɪŋ/
v.
提倡，拥护，主张
Publicly recommending or supporting.
She is advocating for stricter gun control laws. 她正在倡导更严格的枪支管制法。

He's been advocating a vegetarian diet for years. 他多年来一直倡导素食。

近义词:supporting, promoting, championing, endorsing
反义词:opposing, condemning, denouncing
词性:动词(present participle of advocate)

词源:
From Late Latin *advocatus, past participle of advocare (“to summon, call to one's aid”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + vocare (“to call”).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He advocates for human rights. = 他倡导人权。
She advocates a healthy lifestyle. = 她提倡健康的生活方式。

一般过去时:
They advocated for peace during the war. = 战争期间，他们倡导和平。
The group advocated changes to the law. = 该团体倡导修改法律。

现在完成时:
He has been advocating for this cause for many years. = 多年来，他一直倡导这项事业。
She has advocated for education reform in her community. = 她一直在她的社区倡导教育改革。

过去完成时:

She had advocated for animal rights since she was a child. = 她从小就倡导动物权利。

将来时:
