epitomising:/ɪˈpɪtəmaɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
体现；是…的典型；概括
being a perfect example of something
Her work epitomises the elegance and craftsmanship of a bygone era. 她的作品体现了过去时代优雅的工艺和精湛的技艺。
synonyms: embodying, exemplifying, representing, typifying, personifying
antonyms: misrepresenting, contradicting
verb

epitomise:/ɪˈpɪtəmaɪz/
verb
体现；是…的典型；概括
be a perfect example of a quality or type of thing
This building epitomises the spirit of the modern age. 这座建筑体现了现代精神。
synonyms: embody, exemplify, represent, typify, personify
antonyms: misrepresent, contradict
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin epitomizare, from Ancient Greek ἐπιτομίζειν (epitomizein, “to abridge, summarize”), from ἐπιτέμνειν (epitemnein, “to cut short”), from ἐπί (epi, “on, upon”) + τέμνειν (temnein, “to cut”).
