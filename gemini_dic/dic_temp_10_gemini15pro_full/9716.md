antityphoid:/ˌæntiˈtaɪfɔɪd/
adjective
抗伤寒的
Preventing or counteracting typhoid fever.
Example:  She received an antityphoid inoculation before travelling to a high-risk area. 她前往高风险地区前接种了抗伤寒疫苗。

Example:  Antityphoid measures are crucial in areas with poor sanitation.  在卫生条件差的地区，抗伤寒措施至关重要。

synonym:  antityphic
antonym:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider "typhoid-causing" or "typhoid-promoting" as conceptually opposite, though these aren't single words)
word class: adjective
