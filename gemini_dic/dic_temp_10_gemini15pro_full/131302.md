noncertainties:/nɒnˈsɜːrtntiz/
|n.|不确定性；不确定因素|The plural form of noncertainty, referring to multiple instances of lack of certainty or definite knowledge.  A state of being unsure or indefinite.|Due to the current economic noncertainties, many businesses are hesitant to expand. 由于当前的经济不确定性，许多企业不愿扩张。|近义词：uncertainties, doubts, ambiguities|反义词：certainties, assurances|词性:名词

noncertainty:/nɒnˈsɜːrtnti/
|n.|不确定性；不确定因素|A state of being unsure or indefinite; lack of certainty or definite knowledge.|There's a degree of noncertainty surrounding the election results.  选举结果存在一定程度的不确定性。|近义词：uncertainty, doubt, ambiguity|反义词：certainty, assurance|词性:名词


词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
certainty: from Middle English *certeinte, from Old French, from Late Latin *certitudo, from Latin *certus "settled, sure"
