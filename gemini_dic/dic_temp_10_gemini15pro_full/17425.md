bean-sprout:/ˈbiːn ˌspraʊt/| n.|豆芽|A sprout that is produced from the germination of a bean, typically mung beans or soybeans.  It's a common ingredient in Asian cuisine.|Example: I added bean sprouts to my stir-fry. 我把豆芽加到我的炒菜里。|Example:  Bean sprouts are a good source of vitamins. 豆芽是维生素的良好来源。|近义词:sprout, mung bean sprout, soybean sprout|反义词:(none)|noun
