cordy:/ˈkɔːrdi/ | adj. | 心形的; 绒布似的; 厚实的 | Covered with a soft, thick, raised fabric like corduroy; velvety; hearty. |  The toy bear was made of a soft, cordy material.  玩具熊是用柔软的绒布材料制成的。 | 近义词: velvety, plush, soft, thick | 反义词: thin, smooth, hard | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From corduroy, referring to the ribbed texture. Ultimately from corde du roi "cord of the king," because the fabric was originally a luxury item.
