nonappealability:/ˌnɒnəˌpiːləˈbɪləti/
noun
不可上诉性
The quality or state of being unable to be appealed.
The nonappealability of the judge's decision frustrated the defendant.  法官的判决不可上诉，这使被告感到沮丧。
The contract stipulated the nonappealability of the arbitrator's ruling. 合同规定仲裁员的裁决不可上诉。
Synonym: finality, unappealableness
Antonym: appealability
