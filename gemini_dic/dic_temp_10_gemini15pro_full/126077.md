mustiness:/ˈmʌstɪnɪs/ | n. | 霉味；陈腐；发霉 | a stale, moldy smell or taste | The old books had a distinct mustiness to them. 这些旧书有一股明显的霉味。| synonyms: fustiness, moldiness, dampness, staleness | antonyms: freshness, sweetness | noun


musty:/ˈmʌsti/ | adj. | 发霉的；陈腐的；霉臭的 | smelling or tasting stale, moldy, or damp | The basement was dark and musty. 地下室又黑又霉。| synonyms: fusty, moldy, damp, stale | antonyms: fresh, sweet | adjective
