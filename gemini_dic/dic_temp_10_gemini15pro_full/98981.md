inter-service:/ˌɪntərˈsɜːrvɪs/
adjective
跨军种的，部门间的
Existing or occurring between different branches of the armed forces or different departments.
The inter-service rivalry between the army and the navy has been going on for decades. 陆军和海军之间的跨军种竞争已经持续了几十年了。
Inter-service cooperation is essential for effective national defense.  跨军种合作对于有效的国防至关重要。
近义词: interdepartmental, joint
反义词: intraservice, within-service
词性: adjective
