crinoidea:/kraɪˈnɔɪdiə/
n.
海百合纲
marine animals that make up the class Crinoidea; characterized by a cup-shaped body and five or more feathery arms
Fossil crinoids are relatively common. 海百合化石相对常见。
近义词:feather stars, sea lilies
反义词: (No direct antonym for a taxonomic classification)
词性:noun
