groundable:/ˈɡraʊndəbl/| adj.|可禁足的，可接地的|Susceptible to being grounded, as of a person (typically a child or teenager) or an aircraft.  Able to be connected to the ground electrically. |例句：His behavior was so bad he was groundable. 他的行为太差了，应该被禁足。

The electrical system is groundable for safety. 为了安全起见，该电气系统可接地。|近义词：punishable, restrictable (for people); earthing, grounding (for electrical systems)|反义词: ungroundable |词性: adjective
