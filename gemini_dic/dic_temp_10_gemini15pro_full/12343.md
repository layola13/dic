ashame:/əˈʃeɪm/| n.|羞耻，耻辱，可惜|A feeling of guilt, regret, or sadness because you know you have done something wrong.  Something that causes you to feel ashamed. A source of disgrace.|例句：It's a shame that you missed the party. 你错过派对真是太可惜了。

例句：He felt a deep sense of shame after lying to his parents.  他对父母撒谎后感到深深的羞耻。|近义词：disgrace, dishonor, pity, regret|反义词：pride, honor|词性:noun
