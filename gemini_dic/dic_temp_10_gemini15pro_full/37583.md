commune: /kəˈmjuːn/ (n.) /kəˈmjuːn/ (v.)
noun, verb
公社; 与...融为一体; 交谈
(n.) A group of people living together and sharing possessions and responsibilities.  A small, self-governing community.
(v.)  To communicate intimately; to feel a strong sense of spiritual connection.

例句：
The commune members shared all their resources.
公社成员共享所有资源。

They formed a commune based on shared values.
他们建立了一个基于共同价值观的公社。

She communes with nature during her long walks in the forest.
她在森林里长时间散步时与大自然融为一体。

He communed with his ancestors through meditation.
他通过冥想与他的祖先交流。

近义词：(n.) community, collective, kibbutz; (v.) connect, empathize, share
反义词：(n.) individual, solitude


词性: noun, verb
