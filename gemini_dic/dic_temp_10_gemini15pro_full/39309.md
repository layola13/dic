conifers:/ˈkɑːnɪfərz/ /ˈkɒnɪfəz/| n.|针叶树|A tree that bears cones and needle-like or scale-like leaves that are typically evergreen.  Most conifers are cone-bearing seed plants, a subset of gymnosperms.|例句：The mountains were covered in conifers. 山上覆盖着针叶树。|近义词：evergreens, softwoods|反义词:deciduous trees|Noun
