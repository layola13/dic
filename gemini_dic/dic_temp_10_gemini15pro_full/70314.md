If you're interested in learning about the word "what," here's an analysis:

what: /wɒt/ (British), /wɑːt/ (American) | pron. det. adv. int. | 什么，多么 | Used to ask for information about something. |
Example: What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

Example: What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！

Synonym: which, how, why (depending on context)
Antonym: (No direct antonym for "what")
Part of Speech: Pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection
