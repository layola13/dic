aeromechanical: /ˌɛroʊmɪˈkænɪkəl/
adjective
空气机械的；航空力学的
Relating to the mechanics of air or gases, especially in relation to aircraft or other flying objects.  Pertaining to the mechanical properties of air or gases, especially in relation to their motion and forces.
The aeromechanical properties of the wing were carefully analyzed.  机翼的空气力学特性经过了仔细的分析。

The helicopter's aeromechanical system suffered a critical failure. 直升机的空气机械系统发生了严重故障。
