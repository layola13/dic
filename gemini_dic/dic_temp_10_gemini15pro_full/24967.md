brusk:/brʌsk/| adj.|唐突的，粗暴的，无礼的|Abrupt and curt in manner or speech; often seen as unfriendly or impatient.|例句：His brisk manner can be off-putting to some people. 他的粗鲁举止可能会让一些人反感。

例句：She gave a brisk reply and turned away. 她简短地回答后就转身离开了。

近义词：abrupt, curt, short, sharp, blunt, gruff, rude, impolite, discourteous|反义词：polite, courteous, friendly, warm, gentle, kind, gracious|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *brusque*, from Italian *brusco* "sour, tart," perhaps from Late Latin *bruscus* "butcher's broom" (a prickly shrub), of uncertain origin.
