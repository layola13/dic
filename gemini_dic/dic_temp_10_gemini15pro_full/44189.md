croakiest:/ˈkroʊkiɪst/| adj.|最嘶哑的|Superlative form of croaky: most croaky.  Describes a voice that is extremely rough, harsh, and low, often due to a sore throat or illness.|例句：The singer had the croakiest voice I've ever heard.  这位歌手的声音是我听过的最嘶哑的。

例句：After yelling all day at the game, my voice was the croakiest it had ever been. 在比赛中喊了一整天后，我的声音变得前所未有的嘶哑。|近义词：hoarse, rasping, gravelly|反义词:clear, smooth, melodious|词性:adjective
