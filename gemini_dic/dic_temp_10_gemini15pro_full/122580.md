moderatress:/ˈmɒdərətrɪs (UK), ˈmɑːdəreɪtrɪs (US)/
noun
女主持人，女仲裁人
A woman who presides over a meeting, discussion, or debate, ensuring that it is conducted in an orderly and fair manner.
The moderatress skillfully guided the panel discussion, ensuring all viewpoints were heard. 女主持人巧妙地引导了小组讨论，确保所有观点都被听到。
近义词：chairwoman, chair, facilitator, mediator
反义词：participant, attendee
