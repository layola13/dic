execrableness:/ˌɛksɪˈkreɪbəlnəs/

The execrableness of his behavior shocked everyone.
他行为的恶劣程度震惊了所有人。
近义词: awfulness, terribleness, vileness, depravity, wickedness
反义词: goodness, excellence, virtue, merit
词性: 名词
