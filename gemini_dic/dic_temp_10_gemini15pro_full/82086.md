groaned:/ɡroʊnd/ | v. | 呻吟，抱怨 | to make a deep, long sound expressing pain, grief, or disapproval | The injured man groaned in pain. 受伤的人痛苦地呻吟着。| synonyms: moaned, sighed, grumbled | antonyms: cheered, rejoiced | verb

groaned:/ɡroʊnd/ | v. | 呻吟，抱怨 | to complain or express disapproval | He groaned about the amount of work he had to do. 他抱怨他必须做的工作量很大。| synonyms: complained, grumbled, protested | antonyms: praised, approved | verb
