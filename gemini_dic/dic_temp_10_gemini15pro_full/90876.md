hunkerisms:/ˈhʌŋkərɪzəmz/
|n.|蹲守主义；居家主义；（尤指疫情期间）待在家里的生活方式|A set of behaviors and practices adopted during periods of prolonged staying at home, often characterized by embracing comfortable clothing, home-based activities, and a focus on domesticity.  These behaviors often reflect a shift in priorities and a heightened appreciation for the comforts of home.| During the pandemic lockdown, many people embraced hunkerisms, baking sourdough bread and taking up knitting.|Synonyms: cocooning, nesting, sheltering in place|Antonyms: socializing, going out, adventuring|noun
