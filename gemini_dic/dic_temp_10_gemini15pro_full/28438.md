carboxyl:/kɑːrˈbɒksɪl/ |adj. n.|羧基的；羧基|A carboxyl group is a functional group consisting of a carbon atom double-bonded to an oxygen atom and single-bonded to a hydroxyl group. |Carboxyl groups are found in organic acids like acetic acid. 羧基存在于像醋酸这样的有机酸中。| synonym: carboxy, carboxylic | antonym: N/A | word type: adjective/noun

carboxyl:/kɑːrˈbɒksɪl/ | n. |羧基|A univalent radical, -COOH, characteristic of organic acids. |The carboxyl group is responsible for the acidic properties of many organic molecules. 羧基是许多有机分子酸性性质的原因。| synonym: carboxy group | antonym: N/A | word type: noun
