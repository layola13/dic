nonextractable:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstræktəbl/ | adj. | 不可提取的；不能被提取的 | Not capable of being extracted. |  The report detailed the presence of nonextractable residues in the soil.  这份报告详细说明了土壤中存在不可提取的残留物。 | The artist used nonextractable pigments in his painting, ensuring its longevity.  这位艺术家在他的画作中使用了不可提取的颜料，以确保它的持久性。 | 近义词: non-removable, inseparable, fixed | 反义词: extractable, removable, separable | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + extractable (from extract, from Latin extractus, past participle of extrahere "to draw out")
