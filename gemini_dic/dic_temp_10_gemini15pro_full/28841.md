carpels:/ˈkɑːrpəlz/ | n. | 心皮 | The female reproductive organs of a flower, consisting of the stigma, style, and ovary. | The flower has five carpels.  这朵花有五个心皮。| synonyms: pistil, gynoecium | antonyms: stamen, androecium | noun


carpels:/ˈkɑːrpəlz/ | n. | 心皮 | One of the structural units of a pistil, representing a modified leaf bearing ovules along its margins.  |  Each carpel develops into a fruitlet after fertilization. 每个心皮在受精后会发育成一个小果实。| synonyms: megasporophyll | antonyms: microsporophyll | noun
