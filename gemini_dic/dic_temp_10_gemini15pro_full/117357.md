medians:/ˈmiːdiənz/| n. |中位数，中值；中线|A median is a value separating the higher half from the lower half of a data sample, a population, or a probability distribution.  In the case of a data sample, it may be thought of as the "middle" value. |The median house price in this area is $500,000. 这个地区房屋价格的中位数是50万美元。

The median income for this city is higher than the national average.  这个城市的中位数收入高于全国平均水平。|近义词：average, midpoint|反义词: extreme, outlier|词性:noun
