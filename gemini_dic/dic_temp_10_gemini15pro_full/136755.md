nonsentient:/nɒnˈsɛnʃənt/ |adj.|无知觉的，无感觉的|Not having the power of sensation or feeling; inanimate.|例句：Rocks and minerals are nonsentient objects.  岩石和矿物质是无知觉的物体。|例句：The robot, though sophisticated, remained a nonsentient machine. 尽管这个机器人很复杂，但它仍然是一台没有知觉的机器。|近义词：inanimate, insensate, unconscious|反义词：sentient, conscious, living|词性:形容词

词源: non- (not) + sentient (having the power of perception by the senses)
