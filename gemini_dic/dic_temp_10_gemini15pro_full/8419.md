anti-west:/ˌæntiˈwɛst/
adjective
反西方的
Opposed to or hostile towards the values, policies, and culture of Western countries, especially North America and Western Europe.
The government's anti-west rhetoric fueled nationalist sentiment. 政府的反西方言论激起了民族主义情绪。
Anti-western sentiment is growing in some parts of the world. 在世界某些地区，反西方情绪正在增长。
Synonyms: anti-occidental
Antonyms: pro-west, westernized
词性: 形容词
