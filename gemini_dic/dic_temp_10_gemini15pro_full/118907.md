metavolcanic:/ˌmɛtəvɒlˈkænɪk/ | adj. | 变质火山岩的；变质火山岩 | Formed from volcanic rock that has been altered by heat, pressure, and chemical activity. |  The geologist identified the rock formation as metavolcanic.  地质学家将岩层确定为变质火山岩。 | Example: The cliffs were composed primarily of metavolcanic rocks. 这些悬崖主要由变质火山岩构成。 | Synonyms: metaigneous, metamorphosed volcanic | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider sedimentary or igneous if not metamorphosed) | Adjective
