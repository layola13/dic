mummying:/ˈmʌmiɪŋ/
n.|木乃伊化; 做成木乃伊; 用绷带包扎|The process of preserving a body as a mummy.  The act of wrapping something or someone tightly, like a mummy.|例句：The mummying process involved elaborate rituals and the use of natron.  木乃伊化过程包括 elaborate 的仪式和使用泡碱。

例句：The doctor's mummying of the injured leg helped to stabilize it. 医生用绷带包扎受伤的腿以帮助其稳定。
近义词：embalming, preserving, wrapping, bandaging|
反义词：exhuming, unwrapping|
词性：名词(n.), 动名词(gerund)

mummying:/ˈmʌmiɪŋ/
v.|把…做成木乃伊; 用绷带包扎|present participle of  "mummy"|例句：They are mummying the pharaoh. 他们正在把法老做成木乃伊。

例句：She is mummying the baby in a blanket. 她正把婴儿用毯子裹起来。
近义词：embalming, preserving, wrapping, bandaging|
反义词：exhuming, unwrapping|
词性：动词(v.)


ETYMOLOGY->
From mummy + -ing.  "Mummy" ultimately comes from Persian "mūmiyā"  meaning "bitumen," referring to the blackened appearance of some mummies.
