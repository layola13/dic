moxa:/ˈmɒksə/ (UK) /ˈmɑːksə/ (US)| n.|艾，艾绒|A soft woolly mass prepared from the leaves of mugwort (Artemisia vulgaris), burned on the skin as a counterirritant in traditional Chinese and Japanese medicine.|Example 1: The therapist applied moxa to acupuncture points on the patient's back.  治疗师将艾灸应用于病人背部的穴位。

Example 2: Moxa is believed to stimulate blood flow and relieve pain. 艾被认为可以促进血液循环并缓解疼痛。|近义词:mugwort (艾草，指植物本身), acupuncture (针灸，常与艾灸合用)|反义词: (无直接反义词)|词性:noun
