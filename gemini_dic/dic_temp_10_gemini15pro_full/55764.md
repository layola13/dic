diversifications:/daɪˌvɜːrsɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |多元化，多样化|The act of diversifying; making or becoming more diverse.|Many farmers are looking at diversifications of their businesses to cope with the downturn in the agricultural industry. 许多农民正在考虑使其业务多样化，以应对农业经济衰退。|近义词：variations, expansions, broadenings|反义词：limitations, restrictions, specializations|词性:名词

词源:
From Late Latin *diversificātiōn-, from Latin diversificāre (to diversify) + -tiōn-
