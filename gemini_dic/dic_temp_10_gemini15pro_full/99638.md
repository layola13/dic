interlamination:/ɪntərˌlæmɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. | 交错层压，夹层 | The process or result of arranging in or forming thin alternating layers. | The strength of the plywood comes from the interlamination of wood and adhesive.  胶合板的强度来自于木材和粘合剂的交错层压。|  The archaeologist discovered pottery with an interlamination of different colored clays. 考古学家发现了不同颜色粘土交错层压的陶器。 |  layering, stratification | delamination | noun
