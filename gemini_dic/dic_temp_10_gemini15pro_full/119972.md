millepede:/ˈmɪlɪpiːd/ /ˈmɪləpiːd/| n. |千足虫，马陆|A myriapod invertebrate with an elongated body composed of many segments, most of which bear two pairs of legs.|例句：I saw a millepede crawling in the garden. 我看到一只千足虫在花园里爬行。

例句：Millepedes play an important role in breaking down organic matter. 千足虫在分解有机物方面起着重要作用。|近义词：millipede|反义词:None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *millepeda, from Latin mille (“thousand”) + pes (“foot”).
