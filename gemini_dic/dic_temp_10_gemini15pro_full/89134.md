PHRASE->
what if = 如果...会怎么样
What if interest rates rise before we close on the house? 如果我们在房屋成交前利率上升怎么办？

what for = 为了什么
What are you saving all that money for? 你存那么多钱是为了什么？ (暗示可能是为了买房)

no matter what = 无论如何
No matter what the market does, we're determined to buy a house this year. 无论市场如何，我们都决心今年买房。

what's more = 而且；更重要的是
The house is in a great location, and what's more, it's within our budget. 这所房子位置很好，而且，它在我们的预算之内。

what about = ...怎么样
We've looked at the kitchen; what about the bedrooms? 我们已经看过厨房了；卧室怎么样？
