Nathula:/ˈnɑːθʊlə/ | n. |乃堆拉山口| A high mountain pass in the Himalayas in East Sikkim district. It connects the Indian state of Sikkim with the Tibet Autonomous Region of China. |  We crossed Nathula Pass into Tibet. 我们穿过乃堆拉山口进入西藏。 |  Synonym: mountain pass, Himalayan pass | Antonym: valley, plain | Noun
