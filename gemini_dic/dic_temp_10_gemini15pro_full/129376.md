nomadize:/ˈnəʊmədaɪz/ (UK) /ˈnoʊməˌdaɪz/ (US)| v. |游牧；过游牧生活；使…过游牧生活|To live as a nomad; to travel from place to place without a fixed home.  To cause or force (a group of people) to adopt a nomadic lifestyle.|Many tribes in Central Asia still nomadize.  中亚的许多部落仍然过着游牧生活。|

The government's policies have nomadized many farming communities. 政府的政策使许多农业社区过上了游牧生活。|synonyms: roam, wander, migrate, travel, trek|antonyms: settle, establish, reside, stay|verb
