feminate:/ˈfɛmɪneɪt/| adj. v. |女性化的；使女性化|Having or showing qualities or characteristics traditionally associated with women; to make feminine. |例句：He had a rather feminate voice. 他的声音很女性化。

例句：Some cultures encourage parents to feminate their sons.  一些文化鼓励父母把儿子女性化。|近义词：effeminate, womanly, ladylike|反义词：masculine, manly|词性:形容词/动词

feminate:/ˈfɛmɪneɪt/| v. |使女性化|To make feminine or cause to become feminine. |例句：Social pressures can feminate men in certain cultures. 社会压力会在某些文化中使男性女性化。

例句：The director tried to feminate the character's costume. 导演试图将角色的服装女性化。|近义词：emasculate (in the sense of depriving of masculine qualities)|反义词：masculinize|词性:动词
