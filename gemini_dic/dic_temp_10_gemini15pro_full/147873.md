overreaches:/ˌoʊvərˈriːtʃɪz/
| v. | 过分扩张；不自量力；做得过火 | To reach or extend too far; to go beyond what is appropriate or achievable. | The company overreached itself by expanding too quickly and ended up in debt.  这家公司扩张太快，导致过度扩张，最终负债累累。

The government overreached its authority by imposing the new tax. 政府通过征收新税超越了其权限。 | 近义词：overextend, exceed, overdo | 反义词: underreach, restrain, moderate | 词性: 动词

overreach:/ˌoʊvərˈriːtʃ/
| n. | 过度扩张；不自量力；过分行为 | An act of overreaching; an excessive extension or exertion. | The company's overreach into new markets led to its downfall.  公司过度扩张到新市场导致了它的垮台。

His ambitious overreach ultimately cost him his job.  他雄心勃勃的过度扩张最终让他丢了工作。| 近义词：overextension, excess | 反义词: restraint, moderation | 词性: 名词


词源: From Middle English *overrechen, from Old English *oferreccan (“to overreach, deceive”), equivalent to over- + reach.  来源于中古英语 *overrechen，来自古英语 *oferreccan（“过度扩张，欺骗”），相当于 over- + reach。
