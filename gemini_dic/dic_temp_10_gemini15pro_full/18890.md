besit:/bɪˈzɪt/ | v. |  困扰，使烦恼 | To trouble or obsess. | He was besit by doubts about his ability to handle the job. 他对自己的工作能力充满了疑虑。 | synonyms: trouble, plague, haunt, torment, vex | antonyms: soothe, comfort, reassure | verb

besit:/bɪˈzɪt/ | adj. | 被...困扰的 (古语，罕用) | Troubled or obsessed (archaic and rare). |  He was besit with worries. 他被忧虑所困扰。 | synonyms: troubled, worried, anxious | antonyms: calm, peaceful, untroubled | adjective
