hoecake:/ˈhoʊˌkeɪk/| n. |玉米饼，烙饼|A type of cornbread originally cooked on a hoe or griddle over an open fire, particularly in the Southern United States.|Example 1: My grandmother used to make delicious hoecakes on a wood-burning stove. 我的祖母过去常在烧木头的炉子上做美味的玉米饼。

Example 2: Hoecakes were a staple food in the early American South. 玉米饼是早期美国南方的主食。|近义词：cornbread, johnnycake|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, potentially "wheat bread" as a contrasting food)|词性: noun
