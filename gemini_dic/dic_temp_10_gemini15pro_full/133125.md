nonest:/ˈnʌnɪst/ |n.| 最诚实的人；最正直的人 |  A person who is considered to be the most honest or upright. |  The nonest among us will always speak the truth. 我们中最诚实的人总是会说实话。| 近义词: paragon of honesty, epitome of integrity | 反义词: rogue, scoundrel |词性: 名词


词源: Formed from "none" (meaning not any) + "-est" (superlative suffix). While grammatically incorrect in Standard English, it is sometimes used informally for emphasis.  Technically, the superlative of "honest" is "most honest."
