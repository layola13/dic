daub:/dɔːb/| n. vt.|涂抹，涂料；拙劣的画|A sticky substance used for coating a surface.  To coat or smear with a sticky substance; to paint clumsily.|He daubed some mud on the wall to fill the crack. 他在墙上涂了些泥巴来填补裂缝。|近义词：smear, plaster, coat, slather|反义词:clean, wipe|词性:名词, 动词
daubs:/dɔːbz/| n.|涂抹，涂料；拙劣的画（复数）|Plural form of "daub," referring to multiple applications of a sticky substance or several clumsy paintings.|The artist's early daubs showed little promise.  这位艺术家早期的涂鸦作品没有什么前途。|近义词：smears, plasters, coatings|反义词:N/A|词性:名词

词源：From Old French *dauber 'to plaster,' of uncertain origin.


注意事项:  As "daub" can refer to both the act of smearing and the substance smeared, context is key to understanding its meaning. It often carries a connotation of clumsiness or a lack of skill, especially when referring to painting.
