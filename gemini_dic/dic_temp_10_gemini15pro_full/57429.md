drabs:/dræbz/| n.| 乏味的衣服；浅褐色；单调乏味的事物 |Dull, typically brownish, clothes; something dull or monotonous. |She wore drabs all winter, mirroring her gloomy mood. 她整个冬天都穿着单调乏味的衣服，反映了她阴郁的心情。|The endless meetings were a series of drabs.  无休止的会议是一系列单调乏味的事情。|近义词：drabness, dullness, monotony, tedium|反义词:brightness, vibrancy, excitement, color|词性:名词

drabs:/dræbz/| v. (drabs, drabbing, drabbed)| 使…显得单调乏味；穿着单调乏味的衣服 |To make something dull or monotonous; to dress in dull, typically brownish clothes.|The beige walls drabbed the entire room.  米色的墙壁使整个房间显得单调乏味。|She drabbed herself in an old brown coat. 她穿着一件旧的棕色外套，显得毫无生气。|近义词:subdue, mute, tone down|反义词:brighten, enliven, invigorate|词性:动词

drabs:/dræbz/| adj.| 乏味的；单调的；浅褐色的 |Dull; monotonous; of a light brown color.|The drab landscape stretched for miles. 单调乏味的景色绵延数英里。|He wore a drab suit to the funeral.  他穿着一套浅褐色的西装去参加葬礼。|近义词:dull, monotonous, uninteresting, colorless|反义词:bright, vibrant, exciting, colorful|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *drabbe, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse draf (“dregs, refuse”).
