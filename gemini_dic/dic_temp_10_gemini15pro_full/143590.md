词源:  "outhurl" is likely a combination of "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) and "hurl" (meaning to throw with great force).  "Hurl" itself comes from the Old French word "hurler," meaning "to howl or yell," likely reflecting the sound made when something is thrown forcefully.
