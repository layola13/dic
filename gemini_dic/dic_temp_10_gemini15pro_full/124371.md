motive:/ˈmoʊtɪv/ (US) /ˈməʊtɪv/ (UK)| n.| 动机，目的，主题 |Something that causes a person to act in a particular way; a reason for doing something. |例句：His motive for the crime remains a mystery.  他的犯罪动机仍然是个谜。|近义词：reason, purpose, incentive, drive|反义词:deterrent |词性:noun

motive:/ˈmoʊtɪv/ (US) /ˈməʊtɪv/ (UK)| adj.|  动机的，主题的（较少使用） |Relating to motion or causing motion; activating.  |例句：The motive power of the engine is impressive.  这台发动机的动力令人印象深刻。|近义词：motivational, driving|反义词:static, inert |词性:adjective
