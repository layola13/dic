nonrealism:/ˌnɒnˈriːəlɪzəm/ /ˌnɑːnˈriːəlɪzəm/
noun
非现实主义
A genre or style that does not represent things as they are in the real world, often involving fantasy, magic, or dreamlike elements.
His paintings embrace nonrealism, depicting fantastical creatures and impossible landscapes.  他画作包含非现实主义，描绘了幻想的生物和不可能的景象。
近义词: surrealism, fantasy, idealism
反义词: realism, naturalism
词性: noun

nonrealistic:/ˌnɒnriːəlˈɪstɪk/ /ˌnɑːnriːəlˈɪstɪk/
adjective
非现实的
Not representing or imitating reality.
Her goals were nonrealistic, given her limited resources and experience. 她的目标不切实际，考虑到她有限的资源和经验。
近义词: impractical, unrealistic, fanciful
反义词: realistic, practical, pragmatic
词性: adjective
