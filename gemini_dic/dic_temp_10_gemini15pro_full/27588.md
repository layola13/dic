canaster:/kəˈnæstər/ | n. | 茶叶罐；柳条筐；烟草；大篮子 | A small basket or container, typically used for storing tea, coffee, or sugar. Also, a large basket used for carrying goods.  It can also refer to a type of tobacco or marijuana. |  The canaster of tea was beautifully decorated.  茶叶罐装饰精美。

She carried a canaster full of fresh vegetables from the garden. 她提着一篮子从花园里摘来的新鲜蔬菜。| 近义词: basket, container, caddy, canister | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, depends on the specific meaning used.  For example, if used in the sense of a container, an antonym might be "open space" or "nothingness". ) | 词性: 名词
