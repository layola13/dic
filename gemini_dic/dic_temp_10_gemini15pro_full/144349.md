outstated:/ˌaʊtˈsteɪtɪd/
adjective
过分陈述的；夸大的
stated too strongly; exaggerated.
例句：His claims about the product's effectiveness were outstated.  他关于产品功效的声明过于夸大。

例句：The advertisement outstated the benefits of using the cream.  广告夸大了使用这种面霜的好处。

近义词：overstated, exaggerated, inflated
反义词：understated, downplayed

词性: adjective
