ennui:/ˈɒnwiː/ (UK), /ɑːnˈwiː/ (US)| n. |倦怠；厌烦；无聊|A feeling of listlessness and dissatisfaction arising from a lack of occupation or excitement.|例句：She suffered from a deep ennui and couldn't find anything to interest her. 她感到深深的倦怠，找不到任何让她感兴趣的事情。

例句：The endless lecture filled the students with ennui. 无休止的讲座使学生们感到厌烦。

近义词：boredom, tedium, listlessness, apathy, languor|反义词:enthusiasm, interest, excitement, energy|词性:noun
