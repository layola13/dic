fulsome:/ˈfʊlsəm/ | adj. | 丰满的；充足的 | large in size or quantity | The harvest was fulsome this year. 今年的收成很丰盛。 | synonyms: abundant, copious, plentiful | antonyms: meager, scanty, sparse | adjective
