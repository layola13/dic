loins:/lɔɪnz/| n.|腰部；(尤指)生殖器|The area of the body between the ribs and the hip bones, often regarded as the seat of strength and procreative power.  Also often used euphemistically to refer to the genitals.|He felt a sharp pain in his loins. 他感到腰部一阵剧痛。|Near synonyms: hips, flanks, lower back|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps shoulders/upper body as an opposing body region.)|noun
