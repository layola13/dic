nigglers:/ˈnɪɡlɚz/
n.
爱挑剔的人；吹毛求疵的人；琐碎地争论的人
People who find fault with minor details; those who cavil or raise petty objections.
The nigglers complained about the font size on the menu. 那些吹毛求疵的人抱怨菜单上的字体大小。
Synonyms: carpers, cavilers, critics, faultfinders, nitpickers
Antonyms: admirers, praisers, flatterers
