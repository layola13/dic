carp:/kɑːrp/ (US) /kɑːp/ (UK)| n. v.|鲤鱼；吹毛求疵|A freshwater fish with a long body, a pair of barbels on each side of the mouth, and typically a forked tail.|We caught a huge carp in the lake. 我们在湖里钓到了一条大鲤鱼。|近义词：(n.) fish, koi|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, verb

carp:/kɑːrp/ (US) /kɑːp/ (UK)| v.|吹毛求疵，挑剔|To complain or find fault continually, typically about trivial matters.|He's always carping about the food. 他总是对食物吹毛求疵。|近义词：complain, grumble, nitpick, criticize|反义词: praise, commend|词性: verb
