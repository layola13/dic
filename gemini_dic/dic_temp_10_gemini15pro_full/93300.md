ill-assorted:/ˌɪl əˈsɔːrtɪd/
adjective
不相配的，不协调的
not well matched; incongruous
The furniture was an ill-assorted collection of hand-me-downs. 这些家具是一些不相称的旧家具的集合。
The team was an ill-assorted bunch, but they somehow managed to work together.  这个团队是由一群不协调的人组成的，但他们还是设法一起工作。
近义词:mismatched, incongruous, incompatible, unsuitable
反义词:well-matched, compatible, suitable, harmonious
词性:adjective
词源:From ill + assorted, from assort (v.), from Old French asorter, from a- "to" + sorte "kind, sort".  Meaning "badly sorted" is from the early 17th century.
