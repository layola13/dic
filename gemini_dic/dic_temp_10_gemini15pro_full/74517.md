frater:/ˈfreɪtər/| n. |兄弟；修士；同僚|A male sibling; a member of a religious order, especially a friar; a colleague or member of the same profession or society.|例句：He joined the monastery and became a frater. 他加入了修道院，成了一名修士。

例句：The frater bond between them was strong.  他们之间的兄弟情谊很深。|近义词：brother, monk, colleague, peer|反义词：sister, enemy, opponent|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin frater ("brother"), from Proto-Italic *frāter, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰréh₂tēr ("brother"). Cognates include Sanskrit भ्राता (bhrātā), Greek φράτηρ (phrátēr), and Old Irish bráthair.
