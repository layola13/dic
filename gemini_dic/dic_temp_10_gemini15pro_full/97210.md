inflammatory:/ɪnˈflæməˌtɔːri/ (US) /ɪnˈflæmətri/ (UK)| adj.| 炎症性的；煽动性的|Relating to or causing inflammation of a part of the body; tending to excite anger, disorder, or tumult.|例句：The politician's inflammatory speech incited violence.  这位政客煽动性的演讲引发了暴力。|近义词：provocative, incendiary, agitating|反义词：soothing, calming, conciliatory|词性:形容词

inflammatory:/ɪnˈflæməˌtɔːri/ (US) /ɪnˈflæmətri/ (UK)| n.| 促炎药；促炎物|A substance that causes inflammation.|例句：Certain foods can act as inflammatory agents in the body.  某些食物在体内可以作为促炎剂。|近义词：irritant|反义词：anti-inflammatory|词性:名词 (罕用)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inflammatorius, from Latin inflammare “to set on fire,” from in- “in” + flamma “flame.”
