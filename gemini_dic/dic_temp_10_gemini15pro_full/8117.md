anti-aristotelian:/ˌæntiˌærɪstəˈtiːliən/
adjective
反亚里士多德的；非亚里士多德学派的
Opposed to or differing from the philosophical doctrines of Aristotle.
His anti-Aristotelian stance made him unpopular among the traditional scholars. 他的反亚里士多德立场使他在传统学者中不受欢迎。
Synonyms: non-Aristotelian, anti-peripatetic
Antonyms: Aristotelian, peripatetic
词性: adjective
