misclassified:/ˌmɪsˈklæsɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 错误分类的，分类错误的 | Incorrectly assigned to a category. | The email was misclassified as spam and ended up in the junk folder. 这封电子邮件被错误地归类为垃圾邮件，最终进入了垃圾邮件文件夹。| The algorithm misclassified several images as cats when they were actually dogs.  该算法将几张图片错误地分类为猫，而实际上它们是狗。| 近义词: wrongly categorized, incorrectly labeled, misidentified | 反义词: correctly classified, accurately categorized | 词性: adjective

词源: mis- + classified.  "mis-" is a prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly." "Classified" comes from the verb "classify," which means to arrange or organize according to class or category.
