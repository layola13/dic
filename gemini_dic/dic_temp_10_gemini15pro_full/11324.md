arrear:/əˈrɪr/| n.|欠款，拖欠，逾期|An amount of money that is owed and should have been paid earlier.  Something that is overdue or in arrears is late, especially in payment.|例句：The company is in arrears with its rent.  这家公司拖欠租金。

例句：He is several months in arrears with his mortgage payments. 他拖欠了几个月的抵押贷款。|近义词：debt, default, overdue, outstanding, unpaid|反义词：prepayment, advance, ahead|词性:名词
