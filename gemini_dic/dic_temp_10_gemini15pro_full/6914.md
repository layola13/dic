ancientest:/ˈeɪnʃənɪst/ | adj. | 最古老的 | Superlative form of "ancient," meaning of the greatest age. While grammatically correct, it's considered archaic and rarely used.  Modern usage prefers "most ancient." |  He claimed to have the ancientest manuscript in the collection. 他声称拥有收藏中最古老的手稿。 | 近义词: most ancient, oldest | 反义词: newest, youngest | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *auncienteste, derived from Old French ancien, ultimately from Latin ante (“before”). The -est suffix is the superlative ending in English.
