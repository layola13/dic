crystallographical: /ˌkrɪstələˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
晶体学的
Relating to the study of crystals, their structure, and properties.
The crystallographical analysis revealed a unique arrangement of atoms. 晶体学分析揭示了一种独特的原子排列。
The scientist used crystallographical techniques to determine the mineral's composition.  科学家使用晶体学技术来确定矿物的成分。
近义词: crystallographic
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "amorphous" in some contexts referring to non-crystalline structures)
词性: adjective
