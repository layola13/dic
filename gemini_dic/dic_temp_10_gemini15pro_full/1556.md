achievement: /əˈtʃiːvmənt/
n.
成就；成绩；完成；达到
Something done successfully; something gained by working or trying hard.
She felt a great sense of achievement after finishing the marathon. 她跑完马拉松后感到巨大的成就感。

He was proud of his son's achievements. 他为儿子的成就感到骄傲。

近义词: accomplishment, attainment, feat, triumph
反义词: failure, defeat, setback
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French achever "to finish, complete, achieve," from a- "to" (from Latin ad) + chever "to come to a head," probably from Vulgar Latin *capare "to take, seize" (source of Spanish, Italian caber "to hold, contain," Old French chevir "to happen, arrive").

USAGE->
一般现在时:
His greatest achievement is his contribution to medical research. = 他最大的成就是他对医学研究的贡献。
Hard work is essential to achievement. = 努力工作对于取得成就是至关重要的。

一般过去时:
Winning the championship was her greatest achievement last year. = 赢得冠军是她去年最大的成就。
They celebrated the achievement of their sales target. = 他们庆祝实现了销售目标。

现在完成时:
He has achieved great success in his career. = 他在事业上取得了巨大的成功。
She has achieved all her goals for this year. = 她已经实现了今年的所有目标。

过去完成时:
By the time he was 30, he had already achieved more than most people do in a lifetime. = 到30岁时，他已经取得了比大多数人一生都多的成就。
She had achieved recognition for her work before she retired. = 她在退休前就因其工作获得了认可。

将来时:
I believe I will achieve my dreams one day. = 我相信我总有一天会实现我的梦想。
He will achieve greatness if he continues to work hard. = 如果他继续努力，他将取得伟大的成就。
