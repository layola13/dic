obsessively:/əbˈsɛsɪvli/
adverb|痴迷地，着魔地|In a way that shows an obsession.|He checks his phone obsessively. 他不停地查看手机。|compulsively, fixatedly|casually, indifferently|词性: 副词

obsessively:/əbˈsɛsɪvli/
adverb|过分地，极度地|Excessively; to an extreme degree.|She cleans her house obsessively. 她过度地清洁她的房子。|excessively, immoderately|moderately, reasonably|词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From obsessive + -ly.  "Obsessive" comes from the Late Latin *obsessus, past participle of obsidēre "to besiege, blockade," from ob- "against" + sedēre "to sit."  The psychological sense developed from the idea of being besieged by thoughts. The suffix "-ly" is a common adverbial ending.
