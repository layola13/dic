misapplied:/ˌmɪsəˈplaɪd/| adj.|误用的，滥用的|incorrectly or inappropriately applied. |例句：The funds were misapplied to other projects. 资金被误用于其他项目。|近义词：misused, abused, mishandled|反义词：properly applied, correctly used|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- + applied, past participle of apply.  "mis-" is a prefix of Germanic origin meaning "wrong," "bad," or "incorrect." "Apply" comes from the Old French "aplier," ultimately from the Latin "applicare," meaning "to fold, fasten, or attach to."
