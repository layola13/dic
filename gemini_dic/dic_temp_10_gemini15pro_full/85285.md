harmonizable:/ˈhɑːrmənaɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可协调的，可调和的 |  Able to be brought into harmony or agreement; capable of being made consistent or compatible. | The different legal systems are not easily harmonizable. 不同法律体系不容易协调。|  The company's goals and the employees' desires are harmonizable. 公司的目标和员工的愿望是可以协调的。 | synonyms: compatible, consistent, reconcilable, adaptable | antonyms: incompatible, inconsistent, irreconcilable, inflexible | adjective
