coryphodont:/ˈkɔːrɪfədɒnt/ | n. | 冠齿兽 | An extinct ungulate mammal of the Eocene epoch, having large teeth and a heavy body. |  Coryphodonts were among the largest mammals of their time. 冠齿兽是它们那个时代最大的哺乳动物之一。 |  The coryphodont fossil was remarkably well-preserved. 这块冠齿兽化石保存得非常好。 | synonyms: pantodont (more general), Uintatherium (a related genus) | antonyms: N/A (extinct animal, no direct opposite) | noun
