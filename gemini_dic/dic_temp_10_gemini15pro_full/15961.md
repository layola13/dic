balsamic: /bɔːlˈsæmɪk/ | adj. | 香脂的，香醋的 |  Relating to or containing balsam, a fragrant resinous substance.  Often used to describe a type of vinegar. |  Example: The balsamic vinaigrette dressing was delicious. 香醋油醋汁非常美味。| Example:  She used a balsamic glaze on the roasted vegetables. 她用香脂釉淋在烤蔬菜上。 | Synonym: fragrant, aromatic, resinous | Antonym: bland, tasteless | adjective


balsamic: /bɔːlˈsæmɪk/ | n. | 香脂，香醋 | A fragrant resinous substance, especially balsam of Peru or Tolu. Also, a dark, intensely flavored vinegar made from the concentrated juice of the Trebbiano grape. | Example:  She added a dash of balsamic to the salad. 她在沙拉里加了一点香醋。 | Example: The balsamic gave the dish a rich, tangy flavor. 香醋给这道菜增添了浓郁的香味。| Synonym: vinegar (when referring to balsamic vinegar) | Antonym: N/A | noun
