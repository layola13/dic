funniest:/ˈfʌniɪst/| adj.|最滑稽的，最有趣的|Superlative form of funny: most funny.  The funniest joke I ever heard was about a talking dog. |例句：That's the funniest movie I've ever seen. 那是我看过的最有趣的电影。

例句：He told the funniest joke at the party. 他在派对上讲了最有趣的笑话。

近义词：most amusing, most hilarious, most comical|反义词：least funny, most serious, most boring|词性:形容词
