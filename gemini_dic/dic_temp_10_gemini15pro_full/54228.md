disfriar: /disˈfraɪər/ | v. |  to unfrock, to secularize, to return (a cleric) to lay status |  The bishop disfriared the priest after he broke his vows.  主教在神父违反誓言后将他免职。| 近义词: defrock, laicize | 反义词: ordain, consecrate | 词性: verb

Etymology:  From Spanish *disfriar*, from *dis-* (meaning "un-") + *friar* (meaning "friar, monk"). Ultimately from Latin *frater* (meaning "brother").
