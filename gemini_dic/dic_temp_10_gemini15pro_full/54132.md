disendowment:/dɪsɪnˈdaʊmənt/ | n. | 剥夺捐赠，撤销基金 | The act of depriving an institution of its endowment.  The withdrawal or termination of financial support, especially to a charitable organization or institution. |  The disendowment of the college library led to a decline in available resources.  这所大学图书馆的基金被撤销，导致可用资源减少。

The disendowment of church lands in the 16th century. 16世纪教会土地的充公。| synonyms: divestiture, deprivation, stripping | antonyms: endowment, funding, financing | noun
