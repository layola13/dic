overpublicized: /ˌoʊvərˈpʌblɪsaɪzd/
adjective
过度宣传的
Given excessive publicity, often to the point of diminishing public interest or creating a negative perception.
The overpublicized movie failed to live up to the hype. 这部被过度宣传的电影没能达到宣传的效果。

The overpublicized celebrity retreated from public life. 这位被过度宣传的名人退出了公众生活。

近义词: overhyped, overexposed
反义词: underpublicized, unknown, obscure
词性: adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "publicize" (verb meaning "to make something known to the public") + "-ed" (suffix forming the past participle and often adjectives)
