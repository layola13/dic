obturator:/ˈɒbtjʊˌreɪtər/ (US) /ˈɒbtʃʊˌreɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |闭孔器；(照射时用的)遮光器；(牙科)阻塞器|A muscle, device, or substance that closes or blocks an opening in the body. |The surgeon inserted an obturator to close the fistula.  外科医生插入了一个闭孔器来关闭瘘管。|Synonyms: plug, stopper, sealant, bung|Antonyms: opener, dilator|noun

obturator:/ˈɒbtjʊˌreɪtər/ (US) /ˈɒbtʃʊˌreɪtə(r)/ (UK)| adj. |闭合的|Relating to or functioning as an obturator.|The obturator nerve supplies the muscles of the thigh. 闭孔神经支配大腿肌肉。|Synonyms: closing, occluding, blocking|Antonyms: opening, dilating|adjective
