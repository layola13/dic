mosasaurian:/ˌmoʊzəˈsɔːriən/ | adj. n. |  沧龙类的；沧龙类动物 | Relating to or denoting a large extinct marine reptile of the late Cretaceous period, with a long body and tail and paddle-like limbs.  A mosasaur. | Example (adj.): The mosasaurian fossil was remarkably well-preserved.  (中文: 这块沧龙类的化石保存得非常完好。)

Example (n.): The museum displayed a complete skeleton of a mosasaurian. (中文: 博物馆展出了一具完整的沧龙骨架。) | 近义词: (n.) mosasaur, (adj.) none (highly specific) | 反义词: None | 词性: Adjective, Noun

Etymology:  From *Mosasaurus*, the genus name, + *-ian*. *Mosasaurus* comes from Latin *Mosa* "Meuse River" (where the first specimen was found) + Greek *sauros* "lizard."
