friendliest:/ˈfrɛndliɪst/ | adj. | 最友好的 |  Superlative form of "friendly," meaning exhibiting the greatest degree of kindness, amiability, and approachability. | She is the friendliest person I know. 她是我认识的最友好的人。|  Kindest, most amiable, most welcoming, most affable | Unfriendliest, least friendly, hostile | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *frēondlīc (unattested), from frēond (“friend”) + -līc (adjectival suffix). Cognate with Dutch vriendelijk, German freundlich, Swedish vänlig. The -est ending marks the superlative degree.
