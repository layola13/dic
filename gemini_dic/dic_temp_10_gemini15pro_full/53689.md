disciplinedly:/ˈdɪsəplɪndli/| adv.|纪律严明地，训练有素地|In a controlled and orderly way, showing a high degree of self-control and obedience to rules.|She worked disciplinedly, focusing on her tasks and avoiding distractions. 她工作很有纪律性，专注于她的任务，避免分心。|Synonyms: rigorously, methodically, systematically|Antonyms: haphazardly, erratically, carelessly|Adverb

Etymology:
From "disciplined" + "-ly."  "Disciplined" comes from the Latin word "disciplina" meaning "instruction, knowledge, learning, military discipline."
