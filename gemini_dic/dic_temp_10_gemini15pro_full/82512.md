/wɒt/ /hwɑːt/ | det. | 什么 | Used to ask about the identity or nature of a particular thing or things.| What books do you like? 你喜欢什么书？ | which, whatever | N/A | determiner

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you usually eat for breakfast? 你早餐通常吃什么？
What does she do for a living?  她是做什么工作的？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么菜？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to deserve this award? 她做了什么才值得这个奖？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading all afternoon? 他整个下午都在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last weekend? 你上周末做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? 她对这部电影说了些什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 9 o'clock last night? 昨晚九点你在做什么？
What was she thinking when she said that? 她那样说的时候在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? 你走之前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting? 他们在会议前讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  她转专业前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后打算做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们在下次会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will she have learned by the time she finishes the course? 她完成课程时将学到什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么专业多久了？
