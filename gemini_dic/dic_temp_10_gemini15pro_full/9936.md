apeshit:/ˈeɪpʃɪt/| adj. | 疯狂的，暴怒的 | Extremely angry, excited, or agitated; frenzied. | He went apeshit when he found out his car had been stolen. 他发现他的车被偷后勃然大怒。|  She was apeshit about the news. 她对这个消息欣喜若狂。 | 近义词：furious, enraged, livid, frantic, ballistic | 反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
A combination of "ape" (referring to the perceived wild behavior of apes) and "shit" (used as an intensifier).  The term likely originated in the late 20th century, gaining popularity in informal American English.
