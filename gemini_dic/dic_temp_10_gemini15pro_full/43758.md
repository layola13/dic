creepingly:/ˈkriːpɪŋli/
adverb
悄悄地，潜行地
in a creeping manner; slowly and stealthily
The fog crept creepingly into the valley.  雾悄悄地潜入山谷。
近义词：stealthily, insidiously, subtly
反义词：openly, blatantly, obviously
词性：副词

creep:/kriːp/
verb
爬行，匍匐前进；(植物)蔓延；(感觉)慢慢出现；(价格、数值)缓慢上涨；奉承，巴结
move slowly and quietly with the body close to the ground; (of plants) grow along the ground or other surfaces; (of a feeling) develop gradually and almost imperceptibly; (of prices or figures) increase slowly but steadily; flatter someone excessively
The ivy crept up the wall. 常春藤爬上了墙。
近义词：crawl, slither, spread, inch, fawn
反义词：soar, plummet
词性：动词

词源: From Middle English *crepen, from Old English *crēopan (“to creep”), from Proto-Germanic *kreupaną (“to creep”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ḱrewp- (“to creep, crawl”). Cognate with Scots creip (“to creep”), Saterland Frisian krüüpe (“to creep”), West Frisian krûpe (“to creep”), Dutch kruipen (“to creep”), Low German krüpen (“to creep”), German kriechen (“to creep, crawl”), Swedish krypa (“to creep, crawl”), Icelandic krjúpa (“to creep, crawl”).
