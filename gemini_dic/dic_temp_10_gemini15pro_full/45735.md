curvilinearly:/ˌkɜːrvɪˈlɪniərli/| adv.|曲线地，弯曲地|In a curvilinear manner; along a curved line.  Describes something that follows or is characterized by a curved path or shape, rather than a straight one.|The road wound curvilinearly through the mountains. 这条路弯弯曲曲地穿过群山。|Synonyms: curvingly, sinuously, windingly|Antonyms: straightly, linearly|adverb
