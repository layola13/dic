outplayed: /ˌaʊtˈpleɪd/
verb
以更高的技巧战胜，在比赛中胜过
defeated by playing better
例句：He was completely outplayed in the chess match. 他在国际象棋比赛中被彻底击败。

例句：She outplayed her opponent and won the tennis tournament. 她技高一筹，战胜了对手，赢得了网球锦标赛。

近义词：defeated, beaten, surpassed, outmaneuvered, outmatched
反义词：lost to, defeated by

词性：动词(past participle of outplay)

词源：out- + play
