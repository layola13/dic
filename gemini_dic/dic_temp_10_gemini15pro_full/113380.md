lycopodite:/laɪˈkɒpəˌdaɪt/ | adj. |石松的 |Relating to or resembling club mosses (Lycopodium).  | The lycopodite fossils were millions of years old. 这些石松化石有数百万年的历史。 | Synonyms: lycopodioid | Antonyms: N/A | adjective
