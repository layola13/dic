aristarchy:/ˈærɪstɑːrki/ | n. |贵族统治；最优秀者统治|Government by the best or most noble individuals; a governing body composed of the best. | The philosopher Plato envisioned an ideal aristarchy ruled by philosopher-kings.  柏拉图这位哲学家设想了一个由哲学家国王统治的理想贵族统治。

The small island nation was effectively an aristarchy, with a handful of wealthy families controlling all aspects of life.  这个小岛国实际上是一个贵族统治国家，少数富裕家庭控制着生活的方方面面。| 近义词：aristocracy, oligarchy, elite | 反义词：democracy, ochlocracy | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *aristokratia* (ἀριστοκρατία), from *aristos* (ἄριστος, "best") + *kratos* (κράτος, "rule, power, strength").
