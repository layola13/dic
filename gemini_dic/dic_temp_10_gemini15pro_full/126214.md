my-eye:/maɪ aɪ/ | interj. | 表示怀疑、不相信、荒谬 | An exclamation of disbelief or derision.  Used to express skepticism or to dismiss something as absurd. |"My eye! You expect me to believe that?" 你让我相信那个？简直胡扯！| 近义词: nonsense, rubbish, baloney, fiddlesticks | 反义词: I believe you, that's true | 词性:感叹词

词源：
The origin is uncertain, but it's likely a euphemism for "my God" or a similar expression, with "eye" substituting for the potentially blasphemous "God."  It's been in use since at least the 19th century.  (词源不确定，但很可能是“我的上帝”或类似表达的委婉说法，用“eye”代替可能亵渎神明的“God”。它至少从19世纪就开始使用了。)
