lofter:/ˈlɒftər/ (UK) /ˈlɔːftər/ (US)|n.|阁楼；顶楼；鸽棚|A lofter is a room or space located directly under the roof of a building, often used for storage or as a living space.|例句：
He converted the lofter into a cozy apartment.
他把阁楼改造成了一间舒适的公寓。

The pigeons cooed softly in their lofter.
鸽子在它们的鸽棚里轻轻地咕咕叫。

|近义词：attic, garret|反义词:basement, cellar|词性:名词
