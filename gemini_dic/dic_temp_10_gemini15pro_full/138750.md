numeracy:/ˈnjuːmərəsi/ /ˈnuːmərəsi/| n.|数学能力，计算能力|The ability to understand and work with numbers.  The possession of sufficient skills in, and understanding of, mathematics to cope with the practical demands of everyday life.|Many adults lack basic numeracy skills. 许多成年人缺乏基本的数学能力。|Example:  Improving numeracy is a key government objective. 提高国民的数学能力是政府的一个重要目标.|近义词:mathematical ability, mathematical literacy, quantitative skills|反义词:innumeracy|词性:noun
