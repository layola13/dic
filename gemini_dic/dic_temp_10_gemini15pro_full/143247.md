outdelivers:/ˌaʊt.dɪˈlɪ.vəz/
verb
超越交付；超出预期地交付
To perform or produce at a higher level than expected or promised, especially in terms of quantity or quality.
Our new product line has consistently outdelivered the competition in terms of sales. 我们的新产品线在销售方面一直优于竞争对手。
exceed, surpass, outperform
underperform, fall short of
词性: 动词
