ornithologist:/ˌɔːrnɪˈθɑːlədʒɪst/ | n. | 鸟类学家 | A scientist who studies birds. |  例句：The ornithologist spent years studying the migration patterns of eagles.  鸟类学家花了数年时间研究老鹰的迁徙模式。

例句：She became an ornithologist because of her lifelong fascination with birds. 她成为一名鸟类学家是因为她一生对鸟类的痴迷。 | 近义词：birdwatcher, birder | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek *ὄρνις* (*órnis*) meaning "bird" + *-λόγος* (*-lógos*) meaning "study", "word", "speech", "reason", "account".
