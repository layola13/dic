annulosa:/əˌnjuːˈloʊsə/
adjective
环节的;有环纹的
Having rings or segments; ringed.
The earthworm is an annulose animal. 蚯蚓是一种环节动物。
ringed, segmented, jointed
smooth, unsegmented
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *annulosus, from annulus (ring) + -osus (full of).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂enh₁- (bend, curve).
