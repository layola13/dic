hot-swap:/ˌhɒt ˈswɒp/ (UK), /ˌhɑːt ˈswɑːp/ (US)| adj. n.|热插拔，热交换| Allowing the removal and replacement of components while the system is running without interruption.  |This server supports hot-swap hard drives, so we can replace them without taking the server offline. 这台服务器支持热插拔硬盘，因此我们可以在不解机的情况下更换它们。

hot-swap:/ˌhɒt ˈswɒp/ (UK), /ˌhɑːt ˈswɑːp/ (US)| n.|热插拔，热交换|The act of removing and replacing a component while the system is running. |The hot-swap of the failed power supply saved us from a system outage. 对故障电源进行热插拔使我们免于系统中断。

hot-swap:/ˌhɒt ˈswɒp/ (UK), /ˌhɑːt ˈswɑːp/ (US)| v.|热插拔，热交换|To remove and replace a component while the system is running. |We can hot-swap the hard drives without shutting down the server. 我们可以不解机热插拔硬盘。


|近义词：plug and play, hot-pluggable|反义词:cold-swap|词性: adjective, noun, verb|词源: "hot" (referring to a live electrical circuit) + "swap" (to exchange).|
