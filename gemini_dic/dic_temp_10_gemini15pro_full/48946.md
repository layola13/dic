defusing:/diːˈfjuːziŋ/| v.| 拆除引信；缓和；平息| The expert is defusing a bomb. 专家正在拆除炸弹的引信。|Synonyms: neutralizing, deactivating, mitigating, alleviating, calming|Antonyms: escalating, aggravating, intensifying|verb

Etymology:  From de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + fuse (noun referring to a device that ignites an explosive) + -ing (present participle suffix).  The original meaning related to literally removing a fuse from a bomb.  The figurative sense of "reducing tension" developed from this.
