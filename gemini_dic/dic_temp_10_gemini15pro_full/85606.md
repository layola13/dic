hauerite:/ˈhaʊəraɪt/| n.|豪厄矿，硫锰矿|A mineral, manganese disulfide (MnS2), that crystallizes in the isometric system, is brownish black to black in color, and has a metallic luster.|Haurite is a relatively rare mineral found in low-temperature hydrothermal veins.|近义词: manganese disulfide|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
