entrepreneurship: /ˌɒntrəprəˈnɜːʃɪp/ (UK) /ˌɑːntrəprəˈnɜːrʃɪp/ (US)
n. |创业；企业家精神|The process of designing, launching and running a new business, which is often initially a small business. | Her entrepreneurship led her to start a successful online store. 她的创业精神使她成功开设了一家网店。 |  synonyms: enterprise, innovation, initiative | antonyms: inertia, stagnation | noun
