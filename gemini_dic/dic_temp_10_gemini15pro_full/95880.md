increasement: /ɪnˈkriːsmənt/ | n. |增加, 增量, 增长 |The act of increasing or the amount by which something increases. |The increasement of population puts a strain on resources. 人口增长给资源带来了压力。|近义词：increase, rise, growth, augmentation|反义词：decrease, reduction, decline|词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Middle English encresment, incresment, from Anglo-French encressement, encresement, from encreiss-, stem of encreistre  "to increase," from Latin increscere  "to grow in, grow upon," from in- "in, into" + crescere "to grow."
