abusing: /əˈbjuːzɪŋ/
v.
滥用；虐待；辱骂
Using something in a harmful or wrong way; mistreating someone physically, emotionally, or sexually; speaking insultingly to someone.
He was accused of abusing his position as mayor. = 他被指控滥用市长职权。
The customer verbally abused the staff. = 那位顾客辱骂了工作人员。

近义词: misusing, mistreating, maltreating, exploiting, insulting, reviling
反义词: respecting, cherishing, protecting, honoring
词性: 动词 (present participle of abuse)

词源: From Old French *abuser (“to deceive, misuse”), from Latin abūsus, perfect passive participle of abūtor (“to misuse, abuse”), from ab (“away, from”) + ūtor (“to use”).

USAGE->

一般现在时:
He abuses his power regularly. = 他经常滥用权力。
She abuses alcohol and drugs. = 她酗酒吸毒。

一般过去时:
He abused his privileges when he was in office. = 他在任期间滥用了他的特权。
She abused the trust her friends placed in her. = 她辜负了朋友们对她的信任。

现在完成时:
He has been abusing his position for years. = 他多年来一直滥用职权。
They have been abusing animals in the lab. = 他们一直在实验室虐待动物。

过去完成时:
He had been abusing his wife for years before she left him. = 在她离开他之前，他多年来一直虐待他的妻子。
She had abused the company's resources before she was caught. = 在她被抓之前，她滥用了公司的资源。

将来时:
If he continues this behavior, he will be abusing his authority. = 如果他继续这种行为，他将滥用职权。
She will be abusing the system if she claims benefits she's not entitled to. = 如果她申请不应得的福利，她将滥用该系统。


被动语态:
The system is being abused by corrupt officials. = 该系统被腐败官员滥用。
Animals are often abused in factory farms. = 工厂化农场里的动物经常受到虐待。

疑问句:
Is he abusing his power? = 他在滥用权力吗？
Why are they abusing the animals? = 他们为什么要虐待动物？

否定句:
He is not abusing his power. = 他没有滥用权力。
They are not abusing the animals. = 他们没有虐待动物。
