deadend:/ˈdɛd ɛnd/| n. adj.|死胡同，尽头；僵局|A street or passage closed at one end.  A situation in which no further progress can be made.|We reached a dead end in our research.  我们的研究走进了死胡同。|近义词：impasse, stalemate, blind alley|反义词:continuation, breakthrough|noun
deadend:/ˈdɛd ɛnd/| adj.|死胡同的，没有前途的|Closed at one end; leading nowhere.  Describing a situation with no progress possible.|She felt trapped in a dead-end job.  她感觉被困在了一份没有前途的工作中。|近义词：hopeless, futile, pointless|反义词:promising, hopeful|adjective
