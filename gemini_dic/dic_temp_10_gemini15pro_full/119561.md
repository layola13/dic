microswitch:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌswɪtʃ/| n. |微动开关，微型开关|A small, sensitive switch that is activated by very little pressure.  It is often used in appliances and other devices to control electrical circuits.|The microswitch activated the alarm when the door was opened.|Synonyms: miniature switch, snap-action switch|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps a large, insensitive switch)|noun
