interfluent:/ˌɪntərˈfluːənt/
noun
汇流处，合流
A stream that flows between two larger streams.
The interfluent of the two rivers created a fertile delta. 两条河流的汇流处形成了一个肥沃的三角洲。
confluence, junction
divergence, separation
n.
