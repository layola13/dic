neuromancer:/ˈnjʊərəˌmænsər/| n.|神经漫游者；赛博空间牛仔|A person who uses a direct neural interface to navigate and manipulate data in cyberspace.  A term coined by William Gibson in his novel *Neuromancer*.|Case was a skilled neuromancer, able to jack into the matrix with ease.  凯斯是一位熟练的神经漫游者，能够轻松地进入矩阵。|Synonyms: cyberspace cowboy, hacker, console cowboy|Antonyms: None|Noun
