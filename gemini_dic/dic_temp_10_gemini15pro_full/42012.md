costermongering:/ˈkɒstə(r)ˌmʌŋɡərɪŋ/ | n. | 叫卖；兜售（尤指水果蔬菜）；小贩的生意 | The practice of selling goods, especially fruit and vegetables, from a stall or cart in the street. |  The costermongering in the market square was lively and noisy. 市场广场上的叫卖声熙熙攘攘，热闹非凡。| synonyms: hawking, peddling, vending | antonyms: purchasing, buying | noun

costermonger: /ˈkɒstə(r)ˌmʌŋɡər/ | n. | 小贩；摊贩（尤指卖水果蔬菜的） | A person who sells goods, especially fruit and vegetables, from a stall or cart in the street. | The costermonger called out his wares in a booming voice.  那个小贩大声吆喝着他的货物。 | synonyms: hawker, peddler, vendor | antonyms: purchaser, buyer | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From "costard" (a type of large apple) + "monger" (dealer or trader).  Originally referred to apple sellers, but later expanded to include sellers of other fruits and vegetables.
