migrants:/ˈmaɪɡrənts/| n.|移民，迁徙者|People who move from one place to another, especially in order to find work or better living conditions.|Thousands of migrants arrived at the border seeking asylum. 数千名移民抵达边境寻求庇护。|近义词：immigrants, settlers, newcomers|反义词:natives, residents|Noun

migrant:/ˈmaɪɡrənt/| adj.|迁移的，迁徙的|Relating to or involving migration.|Migrant workers often face difficult living conditions.  流动工人经常面临艰难的生活条件。|近义词：migratory, mobile, itinerant|反义词:settled, stationary, resident|Adjective
