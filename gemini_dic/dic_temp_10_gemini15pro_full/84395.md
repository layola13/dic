hallmark:/ˈhɔːlmɑːrk/ (US) /ˈhɔːlmɑːk/ (UK)| n. | 特点，标志；品质证明；纯度检验戳记 | a distinctive feature, especially one of excellence; a mark stamped on articles of gold, silver, or platinum by the British assay offices, guaranteeing the purity of the metal |  The tower is a hallmark of the city's skyline. 这座塔是城市天际线的标志。| synonym: trademark, characteristic, emblem | antonym: flaw, defect | noun

hallmark:/ˈhɔːlmɑːrk/ (US) /ˈhɔːlmɑːk/ (UK)| vt. | 给…盖上纯度检验戳记；使具有…的特征 | to stamp with a hallmark; to be a typical characteristic of | The attention to detail hallmarks her work. 对细节的关注是她作品的特征。| synonym: characterize, distinguish | antonym: obscure | verb
