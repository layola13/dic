cantharidal:/kænˈθærɪdəl/
adjective
斑蝥的；由斑蝥制成的
Relating to or derived from cantharides (dried beetles used in medicine for their blistering properties).
Cantharidal preparations were historically used as aphrodisiacs but are now considered dangerous.  斑蝥制剂在历史上曾被用作春药，但现在被认为是危险的。
The cantharidal ointment caused severe blistering.  斑蝥软膏引起了严重的起泡。
近义词:blistering, vesicant
反义词:soothing, healing
词性:adjective

词源: From Late Latin *cantharidalis, from cantharis + -alis. Ultimately from Greek kantharis, meaning "blister beetle."
