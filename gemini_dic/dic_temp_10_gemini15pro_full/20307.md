biserial:/baɪˈsɪəriəl/
adjective
双列的；点双列的
Relating to a correlation coefficient computed between a continuous variable and an artificially dichotomized variable.
The biserial correlation coefficient is used when one variable is continuous and the other is dichotomous.
近义词:dichotomous
反义词:continuous
词性:adjective

biserial:/baɪˈsɪəriəl/
adjective
双数列的
Having two series or rows.  (Less common usage)
The botanist discovered a biserial arrangement of leaves on the rare plant.
近义词:double-rowed
反义词:single-rowed
词性:adjective
