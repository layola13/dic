enricher:/ɪnˈrɪtʃər/ | n. |丰富者，浓缩物，改良剂|Something that enriches something else; someone who makes something richer or better. |The addition of organic matter is a soil enricher. 添加有机物是一种土壤改良剂。|Synonyms: improver, enhancer, supplement |Antonyms: depleter, impoverisher | noun

enricher:/ɪnˈrɪtʃər/ | n. |丰富者，浓缩物，改良剂|A substance added to something to improve its quality, nutritional value, or effectiveness. |Vitamins and minerals act as enrichers in many food products. 维生素和矿物质在许多食品中充当浓缩物。|Synonyms: additive, supplement, fortifier |Antonyms: diluent, thinner | noun
