bandstands:/ˈbændˌstændz/ | n. | 露天演奏台；乐队演奏台 | A raised platform, often covered, for a band or orchestra to perform on, especially outdoors. | The band played a lively tune on the bandstand in the park. 乐队在公园的露天演奏台上演奏了一首轻快的曲子。| The town square boasts a beautiful Victorian bandstand.  城镇广场上有一个漂亮的维多利亚时代的露天演奏台。| synonyms:  platform, stage, podium, dais | antonyms:  pit, orchestra pit | noun
