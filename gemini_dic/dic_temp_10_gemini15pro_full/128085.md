nervousness:/ˈnɜːrvəsnəs/ /ˈnɜːrvəsnɪs/| n.|紧张，不安|A state of being anxious, worried, or uneasy.|She couldn't hide her nervousness before the exam. 她无法掩饰考试前的紧张。|近义词：anxiety, apprehension, unease|反义词：calmness, composure, tranquility|词性:名词

Etymology:  From *nervous* + *-ness*.  *Nervous* comes from Late Latin *nervōsus* "sinewy, vigorous," from Latin *nervus* "sinew, tendon, nerve." The psychological sense developed in the 18th century.
