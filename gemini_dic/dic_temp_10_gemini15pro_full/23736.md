brattiest: /ˈbrætiɪst/
adjective
最刁蛮的，最令人讨厌的
superlative adjective of bratty: behaving in a spoiled, unpleasant way
例句：She was the brattiest child in the class, always demanding attention. 她班上最刁蛮的孩子，总是要求别人注意她。

例句：He acted like the brattiest little prince, expecting everyone to cater to his whims. 他表现得像个最刁蛮的小王子，期望每个人都满足他的突发奇想。

近义词：spoiled, demanding, obnoxious, unruly
反义词：well-behaved, obedient, polite, respectful
词性：形容词

词源：brat (n.) + -y (adj. suffix) + -est (superlative suffix)

"brat" 源自古英语 "bratt"，意为“粗布，破布”，后来演变为指衣衫褴褛的孩子，进而指被宠坏的孩子。
