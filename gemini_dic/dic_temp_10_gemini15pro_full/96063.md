indecisivenesses:/ɪnˌdɪˈsaɪsɪvnəsɪz/
|n.|优柔寡断，犹豫不决|The state of being unable to make decisions quickly and effectively.  The plural form of "indecisiveness".|His indecisivenesses cost him the promotion. 他的犹豫不决让他错失了晋升的机会。|Her constant indecisivenesses frustrated her colleagues. 她不断的犹豫不决让她的同事们感到沮丧。|近义词：hesitation, vacillation, irresolution|反义词： decisiveness, resolution, determination|词性: 名词
