gigo's:/ˈɡiːɡoʊz/ | n. | 无用输入，无用输出（garbage in, garbage out 的缩写） |  Stands for "garbage in, garbage out," meaning that flawed or nonsensical input data produces flawed or nonsensical output. | If you enter incorrect data into the system, you'll get gigo's. 如果你在系统中输入错误的数据，你就会得到无用输出。 | 近义词: nonsense, rubbish, trash | 反义词: valid output, accurate results | abbreviation
