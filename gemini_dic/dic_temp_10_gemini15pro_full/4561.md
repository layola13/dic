alchemize:/ˈælkɪmaɪz/| v. |使发生根本性变化；使点石成金；变化；嬗变|To transform something, often in a seemingly magical or profound way.  To change the nature of something fundamentally.|The company hopes to alchemize data into actionable insights. 这家公司希望将数据转化为可操作的见解。|Synonyms: transmute, transform, convert, metamorphose|Antonyms: stagnate, fix, preserve|verb
