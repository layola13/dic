diasporas:/daɪˈæspərəz/
n.
散居群体，侨民
The scattering of people away from an established or ancestral homeland.
The Jewish diaspora has communities all over the world.  犹太人的散居群体遍布世界各地。

diasporas:/daɪˈæspərəz/
n.
流散
A dispersion of people from their homeland.
The African diaspora was a result of the slave trade. 非洲人的流散是奴隶贸易的结果。


近义词:dispersion, scattering, migration, exile
反义词:concentration, gathering, repatriation
词性:noun


词源: From Greek *diaspora "a scattering," from diaspeirein "to scatter," from dia- "across, through" + speirein "to sow, scatter."
