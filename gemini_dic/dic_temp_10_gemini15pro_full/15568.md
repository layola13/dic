词源:  From French baguette "wand, stick, rod," diminutive of Old French bac "stick." The bread sense is from the shape.  来源于法语单词 baguette，意为“魔杖、棍子、棒”，是古法语 bac “棍子”的指小词。面包的含义来源于其形状。
