nonpeace:/nɒnˈpiːs/ | n. | 非和平; 战争状态 | The state or condition of not being at peace; war or conflict. |  The world has seen too much nonpeace in the last century. 上个世纪世界经历了太多的战争。|  The prolonged state of nonpeace led to widespread suffering. 长期处于非和平状态导致了广泛的苦难。| 近义词: war, conflict, hostility, strife | 反义词: peace, harmony, tranquility | 词性: 名词

词源:  "Non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "peace" (Old French "pais," from Latin "pax").
