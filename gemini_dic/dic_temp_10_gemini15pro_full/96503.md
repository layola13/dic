indo-gangetic:/ˌɪndoʊ ɡænˈdʒɛtɪk/
adjective
印度-恒河的
Relating to the plains of the Indus and Ganges rivers.
The Indo-Gangetic Plain is one of the most densely populated areas in the world. 印度-恒河平原是世界上人口最稠密的地区之一。
The Indo-Gangetic region is known for its fertile soil. 印度-恒河地区以其肥沃的土壤而闻名。
Synonyms:  Indus-Ganges, Gangetic
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could use terms like "Himalayan" or "peninsular" to indicate different regions of India)
词性: adjective
