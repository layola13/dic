favours:/ˈfeɪvəz/ (US) /ˈfeɪvəz/ (UK)| n.|恩惠；帮助；偏袒；支持；好处；赞同；小礼物；纪念品|acts of kindness or help; preferential treatment; approval; small gifts or souvenirs|She did me a great favour by lending me her car. 她把车借给我，真是帮了我大忙。|Synonyms: kindnesses, courtesies, privileges, gifts, boons|Antonyms: disfavors, prejudices, hindrances|noun

favours:/ˈfeɪvəz/ (US) /ˈfeɪvəz/ (UK)| v.|偏爱；偏袒；有利于；有助于；长得像|to prefer or treat someone or something better than others; to be advantageous to; to resemble|The warm weather favours outdoor activities.  温暖的天气有利于户外活动。|Synonyms: prefers, promotes, supports, aids, resembles|Antonyms: opposes, hinders, disfavors|verb

词源:
