harassers:/ˈhærəsər| n. |骚扰者，不断烦扰的人|People who harass others, typically persistently. |The online harassers were eventually tracked down and banned from the platform.  网络骚扰者最终被追踪到并被禁止使用该平台。

The harassers were reported to the police. 骚扰者被举报给了警察。|近义词：bullies, tormentors, persecutors, intimidators|反义词: protectors, supporters|词性:noun

词源: From the verb "harass," which comes from the Old French "harasser," meaning "to set a dog on," from "harer" meaning "to set a dog on" or "to incite."
