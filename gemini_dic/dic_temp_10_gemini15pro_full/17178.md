batty:/ˈbæti/| adj.|疯狂的，古怪的，糊涂的|Mentally unstable; eccentric or slightly crazy.|例句：She's gone a bit batty since her husband died.  她自从丈夫去世后就有点疯了。

例句：He has some batty ideas about the universe. 他对宇宙有一些古怪的想法。|近义词：crazy, mad, eccentric, daft, nutty|反义词：sane, sensible, rational|词性:形容词

batty:/ˈbæti/| n.|蝙蝠 (复数)|A bat (usually in the plural).|例句：The bats were flitting around in the twilight.  蝙蝠在暮色中飞来飞去。

例句：The cave was full of batties.  山洞里全是蝙蝠。|近义词：bats|反义词:无|词性:名词
