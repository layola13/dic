feather:/ˈfeðər/ (US), /ˈfɛðə(r)/ (UK)| n. |羽毛|One of the light, soft, fluffy coverings that grow from a bird's skin.|The bird ruffled its feathers. 鸟儿抖了抖羽毛。|Synonyms: plume, plumage|Antonyms: scale, fur|noun

feather:/ˈfeðər/ (US), /ˈfɛðə(r)/ (UK)| v. |用羽毛装饰，长羽毛，(使)轻柔地移动|(Of a bird) to cover with feathers; to grow feathers; to turn (an oar blade) horizontal through the air at the end of a stroke.|The eagle feathered its nest with soft grass.  老鹰用柔软的草铺垫它的巢。|Synonyms: adorn, decorate, fledge|Antonyms: pluck, molt|verb
