endorser:/ɪnˈdɔːrsər (UK), ɪnˈdɔːrsər (US)| n. |背书人；代言人；签署者|Someone who publicly approves or supports someone or something.  A person who signs a check or other negotiable instrument to transfer ownership or to guarantee payment.|

例句：
The celebrity is an endorser for a popular brand of athletic shoes.
这位名人是一位流行运动鞋品牌的代言人。

例句：
The check requires two endorsers before it can be cashed.
这张支票需要两个背书人才能兑现。


近义词：supporter, advocate, promoter, sponsor, backer, signatory
反义词：opponent, critic, detractor
词性: noun
