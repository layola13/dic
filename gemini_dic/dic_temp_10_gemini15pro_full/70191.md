fibrinogen:/faɪˈbrɪnədʒən/ | n. | 纤维蛋白原 | A soluble protein present in blood plasma, from which fibrin is produced by the action of the enzyme thrombin.  This is essential for blood clotting. |  High levels of fibrinogen are a risk factor for heart disease. 纤维蛋白原水平高是心脏病的一个危险因素。 |  Low fibrinogen levels can lead to excessive bleeding. 纤维蛋白原水平低会导致出血过多。 | synonyms: clotting factor,  | antonyms: anticoagulant | noun
