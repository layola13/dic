overearnest:/ˌoʊvərˈɜːrnɪst/
adjective
过于认真的；过分热心的
Excessively earnest; too serious and intense.
She delivered an overearnest speech about the importance of recycling. 她就回收利用的重要性发表了一篇过于认真的演讲。
He was so overearnest in his attempts to impress her that he ended up looking foolish. 他过分热心地想要给她留下深刻印象，结果反而看起来很傻。
近义词:serious, intense, zealous, fervent
反义词:flippant, frivolous, casual, apathetic
词性:adjective
