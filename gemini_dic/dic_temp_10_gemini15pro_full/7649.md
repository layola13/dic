annulus:/ˈæn.jʊ.ləs/| n.|环状物；环形空间|A ring-shaped object, structure, or area. |The skin rash formed an annulus around the bite. 皮疹在咬痕周围形成了一个环状物。|Synonyms: ring, band, circle, loop|Antonyms: core, center, nucleus|noun

annulus:/ˈæn.jʊ.ləs/| n.|环状星云|A ring-shaped nebula. |The Ring Nebula is a well-known example of an annulus in space. 环状星云是太空中一个著名的环状物例子。|Synonyms: ring nebula|Antonyms: - |noun
