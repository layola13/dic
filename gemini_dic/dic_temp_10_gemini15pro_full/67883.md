fabulate:/ˈfæbjʊleɪt/| v.| 虚构；编造；创作故事 |To invent or create a story or narrative, often one that is imaginative or fantastic. |例句：He fabulated a wild tale about his adventures in the jungle. 他编造了一个关于他在丛林中冒险的离奇故事。

例句：The author fabulated a complex world with its own history and mythology.  作者虚构了一个拥有自己历史和神话的复杂世界。

|近义词：invent, fabricate, concoct, devise, imagine|反义词：report, recount, relate|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin fabulātus, past participle of fabulārī (“to talk, chat, tell stories, invent”), from fabula (“a tale, story, play, fable”).
