agrarianism:/əˈɡrɛəriənɪzəm/
|n.| 农业主义，重农主义|A social or political philosophy which values rural society as superior to urban society, the independent farmer as superior to the paid worker, and sees farming as a way of life that can shape ideal social values.|
Example 1: The agrarianism movement of the 19th century advocated for policies that supported small farmers. 19世纪的农业主义运动倡导支持小农的政策。

Example 2:  He embraced agrarianism, believing that a life close to the land was more virtuous. 他信奉农业主义，认为贴近土地的生活更加高尚。
|近义词：ruralism, physiocracy|反义词:industrialism, urbanism|词性:名词
