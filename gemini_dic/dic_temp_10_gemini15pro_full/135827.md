nonpropagative:/ˌnɒnˈprɒpəɡətɪv/ | adj. |非增殖性的，不繁殖的|Not capable of reproducing or spreading, especially of a disease or fire.| The virus is nonpropagative in this environment, meaning it cannot replicate and spread. 这种病毒在这种环境下是非增殖性的，意味着它无法复制和传播。| Example: The fire was declared nonpropagative after firefighters contained the blaze.  大火在消防员控制住火势后被宣布为非增殖性。|Near Synonyms: non-spreading, self-limiting, contained, controlled |Antonyms: propagative, spreading, contagious, infectious | Word Type: Adjective

Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + propagative (from propagate, from Latin propagare "to propagate," from pro- "forward" + pangere "to fix, fasten").
