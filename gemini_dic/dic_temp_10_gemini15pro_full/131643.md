noncompulsions:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpʌlʃənz/
|n.|非强迫行为；非强迫性冲动|The absence of compulsions; actions or behaviors that are not driven by an irresistible urge.|She experienced a period of noncompulsions after therapy, finding she could resist the urge to check the locks repeatedly.|Near Antonyms: compulsions, obsessions|Antonyms: - (Difficult to provide a true antonym, as it represents the absence of something)|Noun
