ecstatical: /ɪkˈstætɪkl/ /ɛkˈstætɪkl/ | adj. | 狂喜的，欣喜若狂的 | Feeling or expressing overwhelming happiness or joyful excitement. | She was ecstatic when she heard she'd won the lottery. 她听到自己中了彩票欣喜若狂。|  The team was ecstatic after their victory. 团队在胜利后欣喜若狂。 | 近义词： overjoyed, thrilled, elated, rapturous | 反义词： miserable, despondent, dejected | 词性: adjective
词源: From Greek ekstatikos "beside oneself," from ekstasis "displacement, trance."
