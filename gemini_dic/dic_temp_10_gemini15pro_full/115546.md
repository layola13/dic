mapper:/ˈmæpər/| n.|映射器，制图员|A person or thing that creates maps; a device or program that maps data from one format to another. |例句：The mapper created a detailed map of the region. 制图员绘制了该地区的详细地图。

例句：The data mapper transformed the XML into a relational database format.  数据映射器将XML转换成关系数据库格式。|近义词：cartographer (for map-makers), transformer, converter|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
