bioethicists:/ˌbaɪoʊˈɛθɪsɪsts/
n.
生物伦理学家
Specialists who study the ethical implications of biological research and its applications, especially in medicine.
Bioethicists are grappling with the complex issues raised by genetic engineering. 生物伦理学家正在努力解决基因工程提出的复杂问题。

Bioethicists debated the ethical implications of cloning. 生物伦理学家就克隆的伦理含义进行了辩论。

近义词: ethicists, moral philosophers
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps those who disregard ethics)
