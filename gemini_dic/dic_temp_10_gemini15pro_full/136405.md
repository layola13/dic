nonretainments: /ˌnɒnrɪˈteɪnmənts/ | n. | 保留金，尾款（通常指复数形式）| Amounts of money held back from payments due to a contractor until work is completed satisfactorily.  These funds serve as an incentive for the contractor to finish the project properly. |  The nonretainments were released once the final inspection was completed.  尾款在最终检查完成后支付。| Synonyms: retainage, holdback | Antonyms: payments, disbursements | noun

nonretainage: /ˌnɒnrɪˈteɪnɪdʒ/ | n. | 不保留, 不扣留 | The act of not withholding funds.  | The contract stipulated nonretainage due to the contractor's excellent track record.  由于承包商的优秀业绩记录，合同规定不扣留资金。| Synonyms: full payment, upfront payment | Antonyms: retention, withholding | noun
