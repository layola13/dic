inducted:/ɪnˈdʌktɪd/
verb
就职；使正式就任；接纳…为成员；吸收；征召入伍
formally admitted to an organization or group, or introduced to a new job or position.
She was inducted into the Hall of Fame last year. 她去年被正式引入名人堂。
Synonyms: initiated, installed, inaugurated, admitted, enrolled, enlisted, commissioned
Antonyms: discharged, dismissed, expelled, removed
词性: 动词
