adjective|鳃上的，位于鳃上的|Situated above the gills.|The epibranchial organs of some fish are involved in osmoregulation.  一些鱼类的上鳃器官参与渗透调节。|suprabranchial|infrabranchial|adjective

noun|上鳃骨|A small cartilage or bone situated above or attached to a branchial arch in fish.|The fish's epibranchial was clearly visible upon dissection. 解剖后，鱼的上鳃骨清晰可见。|suprabranchial bone|infrabranchial bone|noun
