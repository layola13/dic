hooting:/ˈhuːtɪŋ/ | n. | 呼啸声; 猫头鹰叫声; 汽车喇叭声; 嘲笑 |The sound of an owl, or a similar sound.  A sound made by a vehicle horn. Loud shouts of disapproval or laughter. |  The owl's hooting echoed through the forest. 猫头鹰的叫声在森林中回荡。 | synonyms: howling, screeching, yelling, jeering | antonyms: silence, quiet | noun

hooting:/ˈhuːtɪŋ/ | adj. | (非正式)滑稽可笑的 | (informal) very funny | The movie was absolutely hooting. 这部电影真是滑稽极了。| synonyms: hilarious, funny, amusing | antonyms: serious, boring, sad | adjective
