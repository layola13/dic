forgetter:/fərˈɡetər/| n. |健忘的人；容易忘记的人|A person who forgets things easily. |例句：He's such a forgetter; he's always losing his keys. 他真是个健忘的人，总是丢钥匙。

例句：She's a forgetter, so remind her about the meeting. 她很健忘，所以要提醒她开会的事。|近义词：scatterbrain, absent-minded person|反义词:  person with a good memory|词性:noun
