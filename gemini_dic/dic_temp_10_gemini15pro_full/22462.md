booklike: /ˈbʊklaɪk/
adj.
像书的；书本似的
Resembling a book, especially in shape or appearance.
The package was small and booklike.  这个包裹又小又像本书。
This electronic device is small and booklike, making it easy to carry around. 这个电子设备小巧像书，便于携带。
近义词: bookish, bibliophilic
反义词: None
词性: adjective
