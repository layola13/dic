hemigamous:/ˌhɛmɪˈɡæməs/| adj.|半婚的，半偶婚的|Relating to or characterized by hemigamy, a marriage system in which one sex (usually males) is allowed to have more than one spouse while the other is not.  It can also refer to a plant having only one sex apparent in the flower.|The anthropologist studied the hemigamous practices of the remote tribe.  这个人类学家研究了这个偏远部落的半偶婚习俗。|Near synonyms: polygynous (for males having multiple wives), polyandrous (for females having multiple husbands)|Antonym: monogamous|adjective
