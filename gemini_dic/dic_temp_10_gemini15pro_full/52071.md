dialogues:/ˈdaɪəˌlɒɡz/ (UK) /ˈdaɪəˌlɔːɡz/ (US)| n.|对话，对白|A conversation between two or more people, especially one featuring in a book, play, or film.|例句：The dialogues in the movie were witty and engaging.  电影中的对白机智又引人入胜。

dialogues:/ˈdaɪəˌlɒɡz/ (UK) /ˈdaɪəˌlɔːɡz/ (US)| n.|对话，对白|Formal discussions between opposing groups or countries.|例句：The peace dialogues between the two nations have stalled. 两国之间的和平对话陷入了僵局。|近义词：conversations, discussions, talks, exchanges|反义词：monologue, soliloquy|词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin dialogus, from Greek dialogos "conversation," from dialegesthai "converse," from dia- "through" + legein "speak."
