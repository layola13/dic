misnavigations:/ˌmɪsnævɪˈɡeɪʃənz/| n. |错误导航，导航错误|Instances of navigating incorrectly, often resulting in getting lost or arriving at the wrong destination. |The series of misnavigations led them deep into unfamiliar territory.  这一连串的导航错误使他们深入到了陌生的地域。|The ship's misnavigations caused it to run aground. 这艘船的导航错误导致它搁浅了。|近义词：errors in navigation, wrong turns, faulty guidance|反义词:accurate navigation, correct routing|Noun
