cumulatist:/ˈkjuːmjʊlətɪst/| n.|积云学者，积云爱好者|A person who studies or is particularly interested in cumulus clouds.|He's a keen amateur meteorologist, a real cumulatist at heart. 他是一位热心的业余气象学家，内心深处是一位真正的积云爱好者。|Near synonyms: cloud enthusiast, cloud watcher|Near antonyms: N/A|noun

Etymology:
From cumulus (Latin: 'heap, pile') + -ist (suffix forming agent nouns).
