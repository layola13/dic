login:/ˈlɒɡɪn/ (UK) /ˈlɑːɡɪn/ (US)| n. vi. vt.|登录，登陆|The process of accessing a computer system or online account by entering a username and password.  This action can also be referred to as "logging in".|Example:  I couldn't login to my email account because I forgot my password. 我无法登录我的电子邮件帐户，因为我忘记了密码。

Example: Please login to the website to access premium content. 请登录网站以访问高级内容。|近义词：sign in, access, enter|反义词：log out, sign out|词性:名词, 动词
