hero-worshiped:/ˈhɪəroʊ ˈwɜːrʃɪpt/ /ˈhɪərəʊ ˈwɜːʃɪpt/ | adj. | 被崇拜的，奉为偶像的 |  Past participle of "hero-worship," meaning treated with excessive admiration, as if the person were a hero. | The hero-worshiped athlete signed autographs for hours.  这位被奉为偶像的运动员签了几个小时的名片。| 近义词: idolized, lionized, glorified | 反义词: despised, scorned, reviled | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
"Hero" comes from the Greek "hērōs," referring to a demigod or a person of superhuman qualities.
"Worship" comes from the Old English "weorðscipe," meaning "worthiness" or "respect."
