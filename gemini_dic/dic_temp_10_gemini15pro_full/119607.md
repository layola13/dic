midanik: /ˈmɪdənɪk/ | n. | 醉鬼; 酒鬼 | A habitual drunkard.  | He was a midanik, spending all his money on booze. 他是个酒鬼，把所有的钱都花在了酒上。 | Example: The midanik stumbled down the street, muttering to himself.  那个醉汉在街上踉踉跄跄地走着，自言自语。 | Synonyms: drunkard, alcoholic, sot, tippler | Antonyms: teetotaler, abstainer | noun

Etymology: From Yiddish מדניק (mednik), from Hebrew מְדֻנָּק (mədunnaq, “pressed, squeezed”), referring to someone pressed or squeezed by alcohol.
