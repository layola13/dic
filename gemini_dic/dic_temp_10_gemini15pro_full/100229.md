interrow:/ˈɪntərroʊ/
noun
行间；垄沟
The space between rows, especially of crops.
The farmer planted carrots in every interrow.  农民在每行垄沟之间都种了胡萝卜。
Synonyms: aisle, space, gap, interval
Antonyms: row, line
n.
