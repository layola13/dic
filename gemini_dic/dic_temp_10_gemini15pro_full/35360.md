co-inhabitant:/ˌkoʊɪnˈhæbɪtənt/
n.|同居者，共同居住者|A person or animal that lives in the same place as another.|My roommate is my co-inhabitant in this apartment. 我的室友是这间公寓里与我同住的人。|
co-inhabitant:|The polar bears are co-inhabitants of the Arctic region. 北极熊是北极地区的共同居民。|
Synonym:|roommate, housemate, resident, dweller, occupant|
Antonym:|stranger, outsider|
Word type:|Noun|
