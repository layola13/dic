neoterist: /ˈniːəʊtərɪst/ | n. | 革新者；现代主义者；新事物爱好者 | A person who advocates new ideas or methods, especially in the arts or literature. |  例句：The neoterist challenged the traditional artistic styles with his bold use of color.  这位革新者用大胆的色彩运用挑战了传统的艺术风格。

例句：The gallery showcased the works of several young neoterists pushing the boundaries of contemporary sculpture.  画廊展出了几位年轻革新者的作品，他们拓展了当代雕塑的边界。 | 近义词：innovator, modernist, reformer, progressive | 反义词：traditionalist, conservative | 词性：名词

Etymology: From Greek *neos* "new" + *-ter*  (agent suffix) + *-ist* (suffix forming agent nouns).
