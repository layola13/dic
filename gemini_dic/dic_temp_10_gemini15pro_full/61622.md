emigrate: /ˈemɪɡreɪt/ (US) /ˈɛmɪɡreɪt/ (UK)| v. | 移居国外，移民 | To leave one's country to live permanently in another. | Many people emigrated from Europe to America in the 19th century. 许多人在19世纪从欧洲移民到美洲。

Many Irish emigrated to the United States during the potato famine.  爱尔兰大饥荒时期，许多爱尔兰人移民到了美国。

近义词: migrate, relocate, expatriate| 反义词: immigrate | 词性: verb
