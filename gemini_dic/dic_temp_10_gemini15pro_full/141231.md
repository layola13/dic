noun
卵膜
A thin-walled, chitinous capsule secreted by the Mehlis' gland surrounding the fertilized ovum in flatworms and forming the shell of the egg.
The ootype is an important part of the reproductive system of flatworms. 卵膜是扁虫生殖系统的重要组成部分。
The scientists studied the formation of the ootype in the parasite. 科学家们研究了寄生虫卵膜的形成过程。
Synonyms: egg shell, egg capsule
