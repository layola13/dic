baste:/beɪst/| v.|涂油；润湿；狠揍；痛斥|to moisten food with pan juices or other liquid while cooking; to sew loosely with long stitches; to criticize severely|例句：The turkey was basted with butter and herbs. 火鸡涂上了黄油和香草。

例句：She basted the edges of the fabric together. 她用疏缝把布料的边缘缝在一起。

例句：The coach basted the team for their poor performance. 教练严厉批评了球队的糟糕表现。|近义词：moisten, sew, criticize, lambaste|反义词:dry, praise|词性:动词
