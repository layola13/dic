orthopyroxene:/ˌɔːrθəʊˈpaɪrɒksiːn/ | n. |斜方辉石|A group of rock-forming inosilicate minerals crystallizing in the orthorhombic system. They contain iron and magnesium and are common in mafic and ultramafic igneous rocks and some metamorphic rocks.|Orthopyroxene is a common mineral in basalt.  玄武岩中常见的矿物是斜方辉石。|
|Synonyms: enstatite, hypersthene|Antonyms: N/A|noun
