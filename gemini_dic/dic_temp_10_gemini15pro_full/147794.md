overprotections:/ˌoʊvərprəˈtɛkʃənz/| n. |过度保护|The act of protecting someone excessively, often to the point of hindering their independence and development.  It refers to instances where the level of protection goes beyond what is considered reasonable or necessary, potentially stifling growth and resilience.|His parents' overprotections made him unprepared for the real world. 他父母的过度保护使他对现实世界毫无准备。|overprotection (singular form), excessive protection, cosseting, shielding, sheltering|underprotection, neglect|noun
