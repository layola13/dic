humiliatory:/hjuːˈmɪlɪeɪtəri/
adjective
屈辱的；丢脸的
Causing someone to feel ashamed and foolish.

He suffered a humiliating defeat in the election.
他在选举中遭受了耻辱的失败。

The experience was deeply humiliating for him.
这段经历对他来说极其丢脸。


近义词:degrading, shameful, mortifying, embarrassing
反义词:exalting, uplifting, honoring


词性:adjective
词源:From Late Latin humiliatorius, from Latin humiliat- ‘lowered’, from the verb humiliare, from humilis ‘low, humble’.
