agnostic-aphasias:/æɡˈnɒstɪk əˈfeɪʒəz/ | n. | 失认性失语 | A type of aphasia where the patient has difficulty recognizing objects, despite intact sensory function. This can manifest as difficulty naming objects, understanding their function, or categorizing them. | The patient with agnostic-aphasia could see the apple but couldn't name it. 这位失认性失语症患者能看到苹果，但叫不出名字。

The doctor performed several tests to confirm the diagnosis of agnostic-aphasia. 医生进行了多次测试以确认失认性失语症的诊断。 | 近义词: anomic aphasia, semantic aphasia | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider contrasting with fluent aphasia or expressive aphasia) | 词性: noun (plural)
