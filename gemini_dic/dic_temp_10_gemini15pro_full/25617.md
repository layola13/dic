bumpkinly:/ˈbʌmpkɪnli/
adjective
乡巴佬的；土气的；粗俗的
Having the characteristics of a bumpkin; awkward and unsophisticated.
He had a bumpkinly gait, stumbling over his own feet. 他走路的姿势很土气，总是绊到自己的脚。
近义词:rustic, unsophisticated, awkward, clumsy, countrified, boorish
反义词:sophisticated, urbane, refined, polished, elegant

bumpkin:/ˈbʌmpkɪn/
noun
乡巴佬；土包子
An unsophisticated or socially awkward person from the countryside.
He felt like a bumpkin when he visited the big city. 当他到大城市参观时，他觉得自己像个乡巴佬。
近义词:yokel, hayseed, rustic, churl
反义词:cosmopolitan, sophisticate
