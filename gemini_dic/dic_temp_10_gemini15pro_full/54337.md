disherit:/dɪsɪnˈhɛrɪt/
|vt.|剥夺继承权，使丧失继承权|To prevent someone from inheriting property or a title, especially a member of one's family.|He disinherited his son for marrying against his wishes. 他剥夺了违背他意愿结婚的儿子的继承权。|近义词:disown, cut off|反义词:bequeath, endow|verb

disheritance:/dɪsɪnˈhɛrɪtəns/
|n.|剥夺继承权，丧失继承权|The act of preventing someone from inheriting property or a title.|The disinheritance of his son caused a rift in the family.  剥夺他儿子的继承权导致了家庭的裂痕。|近义词:disowning|反义词:inheritance, bequest|noun
