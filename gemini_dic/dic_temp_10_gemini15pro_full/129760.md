non-existent:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzɪstənt/ /ˌnɑːnɪɡˈzɪstənt/
adjective
不存在的；虚构的
Not existing; not real or present.
例句：The evidence he presented was based on a non-existent report.  他提供的证据基于一份不存在的报告。

例句：The threat they feared was non-existent. 他们害怕的威胁并不存在。

近义词：missing, absent, lacking, unreal, imaginary, fictitious, mythical
反义词：existent, real, actual, present
词性：adjective
