overcharging:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑːrdʒɪŋ/
n./v.|过度收费；过量充电|The act of charging too much for goods or services, or putting too much electricity into a battery.|例句：The customer complained about overcharging.  顾客抱怨收费过高。|近义词：profiteering, gouging|反义词：undercharging, discounting|noun
overcharge:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑːrdʒ/
v.|过度收费；过量充电|To charge too much for goods or services, or to put too much electricity into a battery.|例句：The taxi driver overcharged me. 出租车司机向我多收了钱。|近义词：profiteer, gouge|反义词：undercharge, discount|verb
