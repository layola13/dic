insipidity:/ɪnsɪˈpɪdɪti/| n. |平淡无奇；乏味；清淡|Lack of flavor, zest, or interest; blandness.|The insipidity of the movie made me want to walk out. 这部电影的平淡无奇让我想要中途退场。|Synonyms: blandness, dullness, vapidity, flatness|Antonyms: excitement, zest, piquancy, flavor|noun


insipid:/ɪnˈsɪpɪd/| adj. |平淡的；乏味的；清淡的|Lacking flavor, zest, or interest; bland.|The soup was insipid and needed more seasoning. 这汤淡而无味，需要加些调料。|Synonyms: bland, dull, vapid, flat|Antonyms: exciting, zesty, piquant, flavorful|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *insipidus* "tasteless," from *in-* "not" + *sapidus* "savory, tasty," from *sapere* "to taste."
