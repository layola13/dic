moderator:/ˈmɑːdəreɪtər/ (US), /ˈmɒdəreɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|主持人，仲裁人，调解人|A person who presides over a discussion or debate, ensuring that it is conducted fairly and orderly. |The moderator kept the debate focused on the key issues. 主持人将辩论的重点放在关键问题上。|The moderator skillfully handled the questions from the audience.  主持人巧妙地处理了观众的提问。|近义词：chairperson, facilitator, mediator, arbitrator|反义词: instigator, agitator|noun
