crummiest: /ˈkrʌmiɪst/ | adj. | 最糟糕的，最差的 | Superlative form of "crummy," meaning of very poor quality, unpleasant, or disappointing. |  This is the crummiest movie I've ever seen. 这是我看过的最烂的电影。| The hotel room was crummiest, with stained carpets and a broken shower.  酒店房间糟糕透了，地毯上有污渍，淋浴也坏了。 | 近义词: worst, lousiest, poorest, awful, terrible | 反义词: best, finest, greatest | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From "crumb" (small fragment) + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives indicating a quality), suggesting something broken down or of poor quality.  Originally meaning "full of crumbs," the meaning shifted to "shabby" and then to its current sense of "very poor quality."
