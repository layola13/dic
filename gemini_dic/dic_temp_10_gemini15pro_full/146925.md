overintellectualnesses:/ˌoʊvərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəlˌnɛsɪz/ | n. | 过度理智，过度思辨 |  The state of being excessively intellectual or engaging in excessive intellectual activity.  This often implies a focus on abstract thought at the expense of practical considerations or emotional intelligence.  | His overintellectualnesses made it difficult for him to connect with others on an emotional level.  他过度的理智使他难以与他人建立情感上的联系。 |  overthinking, intellectualism, cerebration | practicality, pragmatism | noun
