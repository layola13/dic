commiserately:/kəˈmɪzəreɪtli/
adverb
同情地，怜悯地
Showing or expressing sympathy or pity.
She looked at him commiserately. 她同情地看着他。
近义词:sympathetically, compassionately, pityingly
反义词:unsympathetically, heartlessly, cruelly
词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From commiserate + -ly.  Commiserate comes from Late Latin commiseratus, past participle of commiserari "to pity," from com- "with" + miserari "to pity," from miser "wretched, unfortunate."
