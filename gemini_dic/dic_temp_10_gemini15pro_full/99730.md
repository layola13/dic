intermanorial:/ˌɪntər.məˈnɔː.ri.əl/
adjective
庄园间的；不同庄园之间的
Existing or occurring between manors.  Relating to or involving more than one manor.
The intermanorial disputes over grazing rights were common in medieval England.  中世纪英格兰，不同庄园之间关于放牧权的争端很常见。
The intermanorial road connected several estates. 这条庄园间的道路连接着几个庄园。
近义词: inter-estate
反义词: intramanorial
