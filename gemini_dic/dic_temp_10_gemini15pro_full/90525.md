huers:/ˈhʊər z/ | n. | 鱼群引导员 | People, especially in Cornwall, England, who used to stand on the cliffs and shout to direct fishing boats towards shoals of fish. | The huers would signal to the boats the location of the pilchards. 鱼群引导员会向渔船发出沙丁鱼所在位置的信号。 | 近义词: lookouts, spotters | 反义词: None | noun
