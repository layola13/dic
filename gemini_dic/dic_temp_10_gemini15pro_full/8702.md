|n.| 反创造性，扼杀创造力 |The anticreativeness of the education system stifled the students' imaginations. 教育体制的反创造性扼杀了学生的想象力。|This company's rigid hierarchy fosters anticreativeness and discourages innovation. 这家公司僵化的等级制度助长了反创造性，抑制了创新。|Synonyms: uncreativeness, lack of imagination,  inhibition, suppression of creativity |Antonyms: creativity, inventiveness, imagination, innovation|noun
