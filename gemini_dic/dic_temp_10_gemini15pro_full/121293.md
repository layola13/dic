misfiling: /ˌmɪsˈfaɪlɪŋ/
noun
错误归档，文件归档错误
The act of filing something incorrectly, usually a document.
The misfiling of the documents led to a significant delay in the legal proceedings.  文件归档错误导致法律程序严重延误。
misplacement, mismarking
correct filing
