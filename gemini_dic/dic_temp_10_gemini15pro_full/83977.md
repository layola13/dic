half-creole:/ˌhɑːf ˈkriːoʊl/
half-creole: |n.| 半克里奥尔语 |A language variety that shares some but not all of the characteristics of a fully developed creole language. It often arises from prolonged contact between two languages, but has not yet undergone the same degree of simplification and grammatical restructuring as a full creole. |The language spoken on this island is considered a half-creole, showing influences from both English and the indigenous language.|近义词: semi-creole|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly full creole or standard language)
half-creole:|adj.| 半克里奥尔语的 |Describing a language variety that is partially but not fully creole. |The half-creole dialect retains some complex grammatical features from its parent languages. |近义词: semi-creole|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly full creole or standard language)
