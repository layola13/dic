microinstruction:/ˌmaɪkroʊɪnˈstrʌkʃən/ | n. |微指令|A low-level instruction that specifies the elementary operations performed by a computer's central processing unit (CPU).  It controls the internal hardware of the CPU. |Example 1:  The microinstructions dictate the movement of data within the CPU's registers. 微指令规定了CPU寄存器内数据的移动。

Example 2:  A single machine instruction is often translated into a sequence of microinstructions. 一条机器指令通常会被翻译成一系列微指令。|近义词:microcode, micro-operation|反义词:macroinstruction|词性:noun
