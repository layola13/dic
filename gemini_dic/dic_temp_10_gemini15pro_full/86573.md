helicoptering:/ˈhɛlɪkɒptərɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈhɛlɪkɑːptərɪŋ/ (US)| n. v.| 直升机式育儿；盘旋；|The act of a parent constantly hovering over their child, excessively managing and intervening in their life.  A parent who engages in this behavior is sometimes called a "helicopter parent". It can also literally refer to the action of a helicopter flying or hovering.|例句：She was known for helicoptering over her children, constantly checking in and making sure they were safe. 她因对孩子们采取直升机式育儿而闻名，不断地查看并确保他们的安全。
