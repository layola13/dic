fractions:/ˈfrækʃənz/ |n.|分数，片段，碎片|A fraction represents a part of a whole or any number of equal parts.|One-third is a fraction. 三分之一是一个分数。|parts, segments, pieces|whole, entirety|noun


fractions:/ˈfrækʃnz/ |n.|小部分，少量|Only a fraction of the students passed the exam.|只有一小部分学生通过了考试。|small part, minority, bit|large part, majority|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *fraccion*, from Old French *fraction*, from Latin *fractiō* (“a breaking, a fragment”), from *frangō* (“to break”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*bʰreg-* (“to break”).
