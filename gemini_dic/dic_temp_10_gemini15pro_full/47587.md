debasingly:/dɪˈbeɪsɪŋli/
adverb
贬低地；堕落地
in a way that lowers the quality or esteem of something or someone; in a degrading manner.
He spoke debasingly about his former colleagues. 他贬低地谈论他的前同事。
Synonyms: degradingly, humiliatingly, ignominiously
Antonyms: respectfully, honorably, nobly
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "debase" + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Debase" comes from Old French "debaser"  (to lower in rank, value, or quality), from "de-" (down) + "bas" (low).
