imprenable:/ɪmˈprɛnəbəl/
adjective
攻不破的；坚不可摧的
Not able to be captured or broken into.
The fortress was built on a hilltop and considered impregnable.  这座堡垒建在山顶上，被认为是坚不可摧的。

The castle, with its high walls and deep moat, proved impregnable to the enemy.  这座城堡高墙深沟，被证明是敌人攻不破的。

synonym: invincible, unassailable, unconquerable
antonym: vulnerable, weak, defenseless
词性: 形容词
