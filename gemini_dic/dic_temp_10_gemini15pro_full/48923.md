deft:/dɛft/| adj.|灵巧的，熟练的，敏捷的|Neatly skillful and quick in one's movements. |She caught the falling vase with a deft movement. 她以一个灵巧的动作接住了掉落的花瓶。|近义词：skillful, adept, adroit, nimble, dexterous|反义词：clumsy, awkward, inept|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dæfte, from Proto-Germanic *daftīz ("fitting, suitable"). Cognate with Dutch deftig ("decorous, stately"), German tüchtig ("efficient, capable").  Originally meant "mild, gentle," then "fitting, proper," and finally acquired its modern meaning of "skillful, dexterous."
