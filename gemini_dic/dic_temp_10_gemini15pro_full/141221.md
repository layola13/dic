oort:/ɔːrt/ | n. | 奥尔特云 | A hypothetical spherical cloud of comets surrounding the solar system at a vast distance.  It is believed to be the source of long-period comets. |  Astronomers believe that long-period comets originate in the Oort cloud. 天文学家认为长周期彗星起源于奥尔特云。| synonyms:  Oort Cloud (proper noun, often used interchangeably) | antonyms: N/A (It's a unique astronomical concept, so it doesn't have a direct opposite.) | noun
