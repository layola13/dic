bismuths:/ˈbɪzməθs/
n.
铋（金属元素）
a brittle, crystalline, white metallic element with a pinkish tinge, used in alloys and medicine.
Bismuths are used in some medicines for upset stomachs.  铋被用在一些治疗胃部不适的药物中。
近义词: (As an element, bismuth doesn't have direct synonyms, but in its use in alloys, substitutes might be considered) tin, lead, cadmium (depending on application)
反义词: None (Elements don't have antonyms)
词性: noun
