catties:/ˈkætiz/
n.
斤 (Chinese weight unit)
A catty is a traditional Chinese unit of weight, equal to approximately 1.3 pounds or 600 grams.  It is still commonly used in some parts of East and Southeast Asia.
The fish weighed three catties. 这条鱼重三斤。
近义词: jin (斤), kati
反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)
词性: noun

catty:/ˈkæti/
adj.
刻薄的, 恶意的
Malicious; spiteful; mean-spirited.
She made a catty remark about her rival's dress. 她对竞争对手的穿着说了一句刻薄的话.
近义词: malicious, spiteful, mean, bitchy
反义词: kind, generous, charitable, complimentary
词性: adjective
