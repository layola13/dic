manam:/ˈmɑːnəm/ | n. | 玛纳, 精力, 威力; (印度教、佛教)咒语 | A spiritual power or force; often refers to a mantra or a sacred utterance in Hinduism and Buddhism.  It can also more generally signify power, prestige, or authority. | He repeated the manam to achieve inner peace. 他重复念诵咒语以求内心平静. | synonyms: mantra, power, prestige, authority | antonyms: weakness, impotence | noun

manam:/ˈmɑːnəm/ | v. | 尊敬，尊重 (主要用于南印度语言，如泰米尔语、泰卢固语等) | To respect or venerate someone. This usage is primarily found in South Indian languages like Tamil and Telugu. |  They manam their elders. 他们尊敬他们的长辈. | synonyms: respect, venerate, revere | antonyms: disrespect, disdain | verb
