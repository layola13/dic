corrigent:/ˈkɒrɪdʒənt/ /kəˈrɪdʒənt/| n. adj.|改正剂，矫正物；矫正的|Something that corrects or counteracts something else, especially a medicine or treatment.|
The doctor prescribed a corrigent for her digestive problems. 医生给她开了治疗消化问题的矫正药。|
This policy is a necessary corrigent to the excesses of the free market. 这项政策是对自由市场过度行为的必要修正。|
近义词:remedy, corrective, antidote, counteractive|
反义词:aggravant, exacerbating|
词性:名词, 形容词
