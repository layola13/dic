nippitate: /ˈnɪpɪteɪt/
verb
使...寒冷；使...冻僵；使...枯萎
to cause (a plant) to become withered, stunted, or blighted by cold or frost.
例句：The sudden frost nippitated the early buds. 突如其来的霜冻使早期的花蕾枯萎了。

例句：The cold wind nippitated the young plants. 寒风使幼苗冻僵了。

近义词：blight, wither, shrivel, nip
反义词：flourish, thrive
