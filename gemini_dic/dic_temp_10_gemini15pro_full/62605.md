enfiled:/ɪnˈfaɪld/ | v. |  装入枪膛；排成一线；穿成一串 | To thread something onto or through something long and narrow, especially to thread a bead onto a string or to load a gun. Also, to be in a position where a line of fire has an unobstructed view along a line of troops. |  The soldiers were enfiled by the enemy snipers. 敌人狙击手排成一线瞄准了士兵们。| 近义词: aligned, threaded, strung | 反义词: scattered, dispersed | 词性: 动词

词源: From en- + file (to string together), ultimately from Latin filum "thread".
