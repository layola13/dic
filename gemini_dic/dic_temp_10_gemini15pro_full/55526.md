distribute:/dɪˈstrɪbjuːt/ (US) /dɪˈstrɪbjuːt/ (UK)| v. |分发，分配，散布|To divide something among several or many people or places.|The company distributes its products nationwide.  这家公司在全国范围内分销其产品。|例句：The teacher distributed the exam papers to the students. 老师把试卷分发给学生们。|近义词：allocate, apportion, dispense, disseminate|反义词：collect, gather, hoard|词性:动词

distribute:/dɪˈstrɪbjuːt/ (US) /dɪˈstrɪbjuːt/ (UK)| v. |分布，散布|To spread something over an area.|The seeds were distributed by the wind. 种子被风吹散了。|例句：The population is unevenly distributed across the country. 人口分布在全国各地很不均衡。|近义词：scatter, spread, disperse|反义词：concentrate, centralize|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *distribuere*, from dis- "apart, asunder" + tribuere "to assign, allot, bestow".
