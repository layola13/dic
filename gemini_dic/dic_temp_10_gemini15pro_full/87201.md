hereby:/ˌhɪrˈbaɪ/| adv.|特此；以此；兹|By means of this document, statement, or action.|I hereby declare this meeting open. 我特此宣布会议开始。|例句：I hereby certify that the above information is true and correct. 我特此证明以上信息真实无误。|近义词：thus, therefore, formally|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "informally")|词性:副词
