grieveship: /ˈɡriːvʃɪp/
n.
悲伤，悲痛，忧伤
The state of grieving; sorrow.  A less common and somewhat archaic term for grief.
She bore her grieveship with quiet dignity. 她默默地承受着悲伤。
近义词: grief, sorrow, sadness, bereavement
反义词: joy, happiness, elation
词性: 名词

词源：From *grief + *-ship.
