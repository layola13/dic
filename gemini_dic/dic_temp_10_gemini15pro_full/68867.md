fast-tracked:/ˈfæst trækt/ | adj. |快速通道的，快速处理的| Accelerated, put on a faster schedule than usual.  This process is often used for projects or promotions considered urgent or important.| The project was fast-tracked due to its importance.  这个项目由于其重要性而被快速跟进。| 近义词：expedited, accelerated, rushed | 反义词：delayed, slowed, hindered |词性:形容词

fast-track:/ˈfæst træk/ | v. |快速跟进，加快…的进程|To accelerate the progress of something, often by skipping some of the usual procedures.|  They fast-tracked the development of the new drug. 他们加快了新药的开发速度。| 近义词：expedite, accelerate, rush | 反义词：delay, slow, hinder |词性:动词


词源:
fast (adv.) + track (n.)
