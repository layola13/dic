paragrele:/ˌpærəˈɡrɛl/| n.|防雹装置，避雷针|A device, often a metal rod or wire mesh, intended to protect buildings and crops from damage by hail. It is often confused with a lightning rod, which protects against lightning strikes.|The farmer installed a paragrele to protect his vineyard from hailstorms. 农民安装了防雹装置来保护他的葡萄园免受冰雹的袭击。|Synonyms: hail guard, hail protector|Antonyms: N/A|noun
