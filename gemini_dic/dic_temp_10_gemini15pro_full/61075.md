ellipsoidal:/ɪˌlɪpˈsɔɪdəl/| adj.|椭球的，椭圆体的|Having the form of an ellipsoid; shaped like an ellipse rotated about one of its axes. |The Earth is not perfectly spherical, but rather ellipsoidal.  地球不是完美的球形，而是椭球形的。| The watermelon was surprisingly ellipsoidal.  这个西瓜出奇地呈椭球形。|近义词：elliptical, oval, ovoid|反义词:spherical, round|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From ellipse + -oidal.  Ellipse comes from Greek elleipsis "a falling short," from elleipein "to fall short," from ek- "out" + leipein "to leave."
