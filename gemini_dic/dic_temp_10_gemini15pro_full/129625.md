non-contagious:/ˌnɒn kənˈteɪdʒəs/ /ˌnɑːn kənˈteɪdʒəs/| adj.|非传染性的|Not capable of being transmitted by contact. |例句：The doctor assured me that the rash was non-contagious. 医生向我保证皮疹不会传染。|例句：Unlike the flu, a non-contagious disease doesn't spread from person to person.  不像流感，非传染性疾病不会在人与人之间传播。|近义词：noncommunicable, noninfectious|反义词: contagious, infectious|词性: adjective

non-contagious:/ˌnɒn kənˈteɪdʒəs/ /ˌnɑːn kənˈteɪdʒəs/| n.|非传染病|A disease that is not contagious.  (This usage is less common but possible, functioning as a noun derived from the adjective.)|例句：The doctor diagnosed the illness as a non-contagious. 医生诊断这种疾病为非传染病。|例句：Non-contagiouses are often chronic conditions. 非传染病通常是慢性病。|近义词：noncommunicable disease, noninfectious disease|反义词: contagious disease, infectious disease|词性: noun (less common)
