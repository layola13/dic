boredly:/ˈbɔːrdlɪ/| adv.|无聊地，无趣地，沉闷地|Showing or feeling boredom; in a bored manner.  It describes the manner in which something is done, rather than the feeling itself. |She listened boredly to the lecture. 她无聊地听着讲座。|Synonym: listlessly, apathetically, dully, unenthusiastically|Antonym: enthusiastically, eagerly, excitedly|word property:adverb
