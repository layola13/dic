militarists:/ˈmɪlɪtərɪsts/
noun|军国主义者|People who believe that a country should maintain a strong military capability and be prepared to use it aggressively to defend or promote national interests.  A person who believes in the importance of military power.|The rise of militarists in the 1930s contributed to the outbreak of World War II.  20世纪30年代军国主义者的崛起促成了第二次世界大战的爆发。|warmongers, jingoists|pacifists|n.
