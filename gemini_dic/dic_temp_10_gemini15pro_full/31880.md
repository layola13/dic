cheerlessnesses: /ˈtʃɪrlɪsnəsɪz/
n.
没有快乐；阴郁；沉闷
The state of being cheerless; lack of cheer or happiness.
The cheerlessnesses of the long winter nights were starting to affect everyone's mood. 漫长冬夜的阴郁开始影响每个人的心情。

近义词: gloom, dreariness, despondency, melancholy, glumness, dejection, sadness, unhappiness
反义词: cheerfulness, happiness, joy, merriment, gaiety, lightheartedness
词性: 名词 (plural of cheerlessness)

词源:  From cheerless + -ness.  Cheerless itself comes from cheer + -less.  Cheer ultimately traces back to Old French "chiere" meaning "face, countenance."
