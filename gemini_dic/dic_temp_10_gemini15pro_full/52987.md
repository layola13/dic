dinking:/ˈdɪŋkɪŋ/
n.
轻击，轻扣；(尤指排球中的)吊球；(网球中的)放小球
The act of hitting a ball softly and strategically in sports like volleyball or tennis.
He won the point with a clever dinking shot. 他凭借一个巧妙的吊球赢得了这一分。
近义词: tapping, tipping
反义词: smashing, spiking
词性: 名词

dinking:/ˈdɪŋkɪŋ/
v.
轻击，轻扣；吊球；放小球 (dink 的现在分词)
Hitting a ball softly and strategically in sports like volleyball or tennis.
She was dinking the ball over the net. 她在网上吊球。
近义词: tapping, tipping
反义词: smashing, spiking
词性: 动词
