PHRASE->
find out: 找出，查明  Find out what time the train leaves. 查一下火车什么时候开。
find oneself: 发现自己处于某种状态  He found himself alone in the dark. 他发现自己独自一人在黑暗中。
find fault with: 挑剔，找茬  She always finds fault with my cooking. 她总是挑剔我的厨艺。
find your feet:  站稳脚跟；适应新环境 He's still finding his feet in his new job. 他还在适应他的新工作。
find the time: 腾出时间  I couldn't find the time to go to the gym today. 我今天没时间去健身房。
