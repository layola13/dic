disponer:/dɪsˈpəʊnər/ (UK), /dɪˈspoʊnər/ (US)| v. |布置，安排；处置；使愿意，使倾向于|To arrange, order, or prepare something; to have something available for use; to influence or incline someone towards something. |例句：El general dispuso sus tropas para la batalla.  The general disposed his troops for battle. 将军部署了他的部队准备战斗。

例句：Dispongo de un coche para el viaje.  I have a car available for the trip. 我有一辆车可以用于这次旅行。|近义词：arrange, prepare, order, have, incline, predispose|反义词：disorganize, disorder, dissuade|词性: verb
