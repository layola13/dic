oversharpness:/ˌoʊvərˈʃɑːrpnəs/| n.|过度锐化；过度清晰|An excessive sharpness, often in an image or sound, that can appear unnatural or harsh.  This can be caused by over-processing or incorrect settings in image or audio editing software.|The oversharpness of the photo made the edges of the buildings look jagged. 这张照片过度锐化，使建筑物的边缘看起来参差不齐。|The oversharpness of the audio made the cymbals sound shrill. 音频过度锐化使得铙钹的声音听起来很刺耳。|近义词：excessive sharpness, harshness, unnatural clarity|反义词:blurriness, softness, mutedness|词性:noun

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "sharp" (Old English scearp "sharp, cutting, pointed, severe," from Proto-Germanic *skarpaz) + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), a noun-forming suffix indicating a state, quality, or condition)
