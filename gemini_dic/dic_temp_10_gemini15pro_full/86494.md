heinously:/ˈheɪnəsli/| adv.|可憎地，极恶地|In a very wicked or evil way; outrageously. |The crime was heinously committed, shocking the entire community. 这起罪行极其恶劣，震惊了整个社区。|近义词：wickedly, evilly, atrociously, abominably|反义词：righteously, virtuously|词性:副词

词源: From heinous + -ly.  Heinous itself comes from Old French *haineus (modern French haineux), from haine "hatred," from hair "to hate," of Germanic origin.
