delirium:/dɪˈlɪəriəm/ /dəˈlɪriəm/| n.| 谵妄，精神错乱|A state of mental confusion and excitement characterized by disorientation, hallucinations, and incoherent speech.|The patient experienced delirium due to high fever. 这位病人因为高烧而出现谵妄。|Synonyms: madness, insanity, frenzy, hallucination|Antonyms: lucidity, sanity, coherence|noun


delirious:/dɪˈlɪriəs/ /dəˈlɪriəs/| adj.| 谵妄的，精神错乱的|Experiencing or characterized by delirium; ecstatic.|He was delirious with joy when he won the lottery. 他中了彩票欣喜若狂。|Synonyms: frenzied, frantic, crazed, ecstatic|Antonyms: sane, lucid, rational|adjective
