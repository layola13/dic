materialization:/məˌtɪəriəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 物质化，实现，实体化 | the act or process of becoming material; the act of appearing in bodily form | The materialization of her dreams took years of hard work. 她梦想的实现花费了多年的努力。 | synonyms: realization, manifestation, embodiment, actualization | antonyms: dematerialization, disappearance, vanishing | noun

materialize:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪz/ | v. | 实现，物质化，实体化 | become actual fact; appear in bodily form |  His hopes for a promotion never materialized. 他升职的希望从未实现。 | synonyms: appear, emerge, manifest, take shape | antonyms: disappear, vanish, dematerialize | verb
