overreporting:/ˌoʊvərɪˈpɔːrtɪŋ/ | n. | 过度汇报，瞒报(通常指多报收入或利润) | The act of reporting something as greater than it actually is.  This often occurs with income or profits. | The company was accused of overreporting its earnings to inflate its stock price.  这家公司被指控虚报收益以抬高股价。| overstatement, exaggeration, inflation | underreporting, understatement | noun


overreport:/ˌoʊvərɪˈpɔːrt/ | v. | 过度汇报，瞒报(通常指多报收入或利润) | To report something as greater than it actually is. |  Some people overreport their working hours to impress their bosses.  有些人为了给老板留下好印象而多报工作时间。 | exaggerate, inflate, overstate | underreport, understate | verb
