dour:/dʊər/ /daʊər/| adj.|严厉的，阴沉的，顽固的|Relentlessly severe, stern, or gloomy in manner or appearance. Expressing or suggesting ill humor. |He had a dour expression on his face. 他脸上带着阴沉的表情。|Synonyms: stern, grim, sullen, severe, harsh, unyielding, inflexible |Antonyms: cheerful, friendly, amiable, pleasant, genial|adjective

dour:/dʊər/ /daʊər/| adj.|严厉的，阴沉的，顽固的|Stubbornly unyielding. |The dour old man refused to change his mind.  那个顽固的老人拒绝改变主意。|Synonyms: obstinate, stubborn, intractable, unyielding |Antonyms: agreeable, compliant, yielding |adjective
