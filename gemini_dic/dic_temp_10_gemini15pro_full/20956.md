bleeps:/bliːps/ | n. |哔哔声，电子信号声|A short, high-pitched electronic sound.|The computer emitted a series of bleeps. 电脑发出了一连串的哔哔声。|synonyms: beeps, chirps, pings|antonyms: silence, quiet|noun

bleep:/bliːp/ | v. |发出哔哔声，审查并屏蔽（电视或广播中的粗话或敏感信息）|To make a short, high-pitched electronic sound; to censor (a word or phrase) from a broadcast.|The machine bleeped when the process was complete.  当程序完成时，机器发出哔哔声。 The offensive word was bleeped out. 那个冒犯性的词被屏蔽了。|synonyms: beep, chirp, censor, expurgate|antonyms: allow, permit|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
The word "bleep" is imitative, meaning it originated from the sound itself.  It likely emerged in the mid-20th century with the rise of electronic technology.
