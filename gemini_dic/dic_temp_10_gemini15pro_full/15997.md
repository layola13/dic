bamboozler:/bæmˈbuːzlər/
n.|欺骗者，骗子|Someone who deceives or tricks others.|He's a real bamboozler, always coming up with some scheme to make a quick buck. 他是个十足的骗子，总是想出一些快速赚钱的诡计。|
|The bamboozler convinced the elderly woman to give him her life savings.  这个骗子说服了这位老妇人把她的毕生积蓄都给了他。|
近义词:con artist, swindler, cheat, fraud, trickster|
反义词:honest person, dupe|
词性:noun


bamboozle:/bæmˈbuːzl/
vt.|欺骗，迷惑|To deceive or confuse someone.|The magician bamboozled the audience with his sleight of hand. 魔术师用他的手法迷惑了观众。|
|I was completely bamboozled by his complicated explanation. 我被他复杂的解释完全搞糊涂了。|
近义词:deceive, trick, confuse, bewilder, confound, hoodwink|
反义词:clarify, enlighten, explain|
词性:verb


词源:
Origin uncertain, possibly from dialectal English *bam, "to hoax," and slang *boozle, "to confuse."
