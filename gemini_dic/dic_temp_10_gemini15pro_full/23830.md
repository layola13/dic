bread:/brɛd/ | n. |面包 |A food made of flour, water, and yeast or another raising agent, mixed and baked. |例句：We had fresh bread and butter for breakfast. 我们早餐吃了新鲜面包和黄油。

例句：She makes her own bread every week. 她每周都自己做面包。|近义词：loaf, roll |反义词: None |词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *brēad (attested only in compounds), from Proto-Germanic *braudą (“piece of bread”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰrewd- (“to swell, sprout, grow”). Cognate with Old Frisian brād, Old Saxon brōd (Middle Low German brōt), Old High German brōt (German Brot), Old Norse brauð (Icelandic brauð), Gothic 𐌱𐍂𐌰𐌿𐌸 (brauþ), Lithuanian briedas (“leaven”), Old Church Slavonic брадъ (bradŭ, “leaven”). Related to brew, breadfruit, and possibly broad.
