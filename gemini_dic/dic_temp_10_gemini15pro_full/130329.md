non-therapeutic:/ˌnɒn-θɛrəˈpjuːtɪk/ /ˌnɑːn-θɛrəˈpjuːtɪk/| adj.|非治疗性的；非疗法的|Not designed or intended for therapeutic purposes; not related to the treatment of disease or disorders.|The patient was given non-therapeutic injections of saline solution as part of the control group in the study.  这个病人被注射了非治疗性的生理盐水，作为研究对照组的一部分。|Synonyms: non-medicinal, non-curative, preventative, prophylactic|Antonyms: therapeutic, curative, medicinal|adjective

non-therapeutic:/ˌnɒn-θɛrəˈpjuːtɪk/ /ˌnɑːn-θɛrəˈpjuːtɪk/| n.|非治疗性手段|A non-therapeutic measure or procedure.|The use of a placebo is a common non-therapeutic in research.  在研究中使用安慰剂是一种常见的非治疗性手段。|Synonyms: placebo, sham treatment|Antonyms: therapy, treatment, cure|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + therapeutic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "therapeutic" comes from the Greek "therapeutikos," meaning "healing" or "curative."
