lubricant:/ˈluːbrɪkənt/| n.|润滑剂；润滑油|A substance, such as oil or grease, that reduces friction when applied to moving parts.|The mechanic applied lubricant to the engine. 机械师给发动机加了润滑油。|Synonyms: oil, grease, emulsion|Antonyms: abrasive, desiccant|noun

lubricate:/ˈluːbrɪkeɪt/| v.|润滑；使顺利进行|To apply a lubricant to; to make smooth or slippery.|He lubricated the bicycle chain. 他给自行车链条上了油。|Synonyms: oil, grease, facilitate|Antonyms: obstruct, hinder|verb
