breadcrumbs:/ˈbredkrʌmz/ | n. | 面包屑，线索 | a trail of breadcrumbs; clues or pieces of information that help to lead someone to a particular place or conclusion | Hansel and Gretel left a trail of breadcrumbs so they could find their way home.  汉赛尔和格莱特留下了一条面包屑，这样他们就能找到回家的路。| clues, hints, traces | distractions, red herrings | noun

Etymology: From "bread" + "crumbs" (small fragments of bread).
