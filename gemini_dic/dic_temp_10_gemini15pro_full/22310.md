boning:/ˈboʊnɪŋ/| n. |剔骨；去骨；加骨架 |The process of removing bones from meat, fish, or poultry. Also, the process of adding supporting structures, like boning in a corset. |例句：The boning of the fish was expertly done.  鱼的剔骨做得非常专业。

例句：The boning in the bodice of the dress gave it a structured shape.  裙子上身的骨架赋予了它一个有型的形状。|近义词：deboning, framework (when referring to structural support)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


boning:/ˈboʊnɪŋ/| adj. |刺骨的；寒冷彻骨的 (Rarely used) |Extremely cold and piercing.  |例句：A boning wind whipped across the plains.  刺骨的寒风席卷平原。


例句：The boning cold made his fingers numb. 刺骨的寒冷使他的手指麻木了。|近义词：biting, freezing, piercing|反义词:warming, balmy|词性:adjective
