evocative:/ɪˈvɒkətɪv/ (US) /ɪˈvɑːkətɪv/ (UK)| adj.|唤起的，引起回忆的， gợi lên, khơi gợi (Vietnamese)|Bringing strong images, memories, or feelings to mind.|The scent of woodsmoke is evocative of winter evenings by the fire.  柴火燃烧的味道让人想起冬天夜晚坐在火炉旁的时光。|The music was evocative of my childhood. 这音乐使我想起了我的童年。|近义词：reminiscent, suggestive, redolent, expressive|反义词：unevocative, bland, uninspiring|词性: adjective
