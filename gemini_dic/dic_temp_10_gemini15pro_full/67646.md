extrinsically:/ɪkˈstrɪnzɪkli/ /ekˈstrɪnzɪkli/| adv.|外在地，外部地，非本质地|From the outside; externally. Not inherently or essentially.|Her motivation for the job was extrinsically driven by the high salary, rather than any intrinsic interest in the work. 她从事这份工作的动机是受高薪的外在驱使，而不是出于对工作的任何内在兴趣。|Synonyms: externally, outwardly, superficially|Antonyms: intrinsically, inherently, essentially|adverb
