contaminate:/kənˈtæmɪneɪt/
verb
污染，弄脏，玷污
To make something impure or unsuitable by contact with something unclean, bad, etc.
The water was contaminated with chemicals.  这水被化学物质污染了。
The food was contaminated by bacteria.  食物被细菌污染了。
pollute, taint, infect, defile
purify, cleanse, sterilize
v.
