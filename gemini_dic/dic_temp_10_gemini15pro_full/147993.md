overriding:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪdɪŋ/| adj.|首要的，最重要的；压倒一切的；凌驾的| having more importance or influence than anything else|例句：The overriding concern was for the safety of the passengers.  最重要的是乘客的安全。

例句：An overriding sense of responsibility compelled her to act. 一种压倒一切的责任感迫使她采取行动。|近义词：dominant, paramount, principal, primary, main|反义词：secondary, subordinate|词性:形容词

overriding:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪdɪŋ/| n.|推翻，否决；不顾| the act of setting aside a decision or opinion|例句：The CEO's overriding of the board's decision caused a lot of controversy. 首席执行官否决董事会的决定引起了很多争议。

例句：His overriding of his doctor's advice led to further health complications. 他不顾医生的劝告，导致健康状况进一步恶化。|近义词：overruling, rejection, dismissal|反义词：acceptance, approval|词性:名词
