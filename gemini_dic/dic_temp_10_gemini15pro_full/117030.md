mclan: /məˈklæn/
noun
家族;氏族;宗族
A group of families, especially in Scotland, who claim a common ancestor and are usually led by a chief.  A close-knit group of people; clique.
例句：The McLans have lived in these highlands for centuries. 麦克莱恩家族在这些高地生活了几个世纪。

例句：They formed a tight-knit mclan at university. 他们在大学里形成了一个紧密的小团体。

近义词：clan, tribe, family, group, clique
反义词：individual

词性: n.
