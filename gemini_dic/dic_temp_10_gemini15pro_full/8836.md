antiexpressionism:/ˌæntiɪkˈspreʃənɪzəm/
|n.|反表现主义|A movement in art reacting against Abstract Expressionism and characterized by a return to representational subject matter and objective painting techniques.|The exhibition showcased several pieces of antiexpressionism art, a stark contrast to the abstract works displayed nearby.|近义词: representational art, figurative art|反义词: Abstract Expressionism, expressionism|词性: noun

Etymology:
"anti-" (prefix meaning "against, opposed to") + "expressionism" (from German "Expressionismus")
