mayonnaised:/ˌmeɪəˈneɪzd/ | adj. | 涂了蛋黄酱的；加蛋黄酱的 | Covered or dressed with mayonnaise. |  例句：I had a mayonnaised chicken sandwich for lunch.  我午餐吃了一个涂了蛋黄酱的鸡肉三明治。| 近义词: mayonnaise-covered, dressed with mayonnaise | 反义词: plain, undressed | 词性: 形容词

mayonnaise:/ˌmeɪəˈneɪz/ | n. | 蛋黄酱 | A thick, creamy dressing made from oil, egg yolks, vinegar or lemon juice, and seasonings. | 例句：I always put mayonnaise on my burgers. 我总是把蛋黄酱放在我的汉堡上。 | 近义词: mayo | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider alternatives like ketchup, mustard) | 词性: 名词
