maxilliped:/mækˈsɪlɪped/| n. |颚足|An appendage modified for feeding, located near the mouth of crustaceans such as crabs and shrimps.|The crab used its maxillipeds to manipulate food towards its mouth.|Synonym:  gnathopod|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps body part not related to feeding like pleopod)|noun
