commutable:/kəˈmjuːtəbl/ /kəˈmjuːtəbəl/
adjective
可通勤的
Able to travel regularly between one's home and one's place of work.
My new job is commutable by train. 我的新工作可以坐火车通勤。
It's not commutable by car during rush hour. 在高峰时段开车通勤是不可能的。
synonyms: accessible, reachable, convenient
antonyms: inaccessible, unreachable, inconvenient
词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From commute (v.) + -able.  Commute ultimately derives from the Latin "commutare," meaning "to change thoroughly, exchange."
