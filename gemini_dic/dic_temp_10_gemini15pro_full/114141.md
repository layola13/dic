maharishis:/ˌmɑːhəˈrɪʃiːz/
n.
印度教的圣人，大圣，尊者
A maharishi is a great sage or spiritual leader in Hinduism.
The maharishis gathered for a spiritual retreat in the Himalayas.  印度的圣人们聚集在喜马拉雅山进行灵修。
The teachings of the maharishis are revered by many.  许多人尊崇圣人们的教诲。
Synonyms: sage, guru, saint, ascetic
Antonyms:  disciple, student
