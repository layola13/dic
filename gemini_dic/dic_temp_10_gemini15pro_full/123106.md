mongrelization:/mɒŋɡrɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌmɑːŋɡrələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 杂交，混杂；（尤指）种族混合 | The process of mixing different breeds or races, often with negative connotations. | The mongrelization of cultures can lead to both exciting new forms and the loss of distinct traditions. 文化的混杂既可能导致令人兴奋的新形式，也可能导致独特传统的丧失。 | 近义词：hybridization, intermixture, crossbreeding | 反义词：purification, segregation | 词性: 名词

词源: From mongrel + -ization.  "Mongrel" itself comes from Old French *mongrel, of uncertain origin, possibly from a blend of words suggesting mixed or obscure ancestry.
