embottle:/ɪmˈbɑːtl/ /ɛmˈbɒtl/| vt.|装瓶；抑制；隐藏|To put (a liquid) into a bottle or bottles.  To restrain or suppress (an emotion or feeling).|The workers embottled the wine after fermentation. 工人们在发酵后将葡萄酒装瓶。 He tried to embottle his anger but it eventually exploded. 他试图抑制自己的愤怒，但最终还是爆发了。|近义词：bottle, contain, suppress, repress|反义词：release, unleash|verb
