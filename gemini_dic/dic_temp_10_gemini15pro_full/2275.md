adeno:/ˈædɪnoʊ/
prefix|腺; 腺体相关的|A combining form representing “gland,” used in the formation of compound words: adenovirus.|Adeno-associated virus (AAV) is a small virus which infects humans and some other primate species.  腺相关病毒 (AAV) 是一种感染人类和其他一些灵长类动物的小病毒。

Adenocarcinoma is a type of cancer that starts in glandular tissue. 腺癌是一种起源于腺组织的癌症。|近义词: gland, glandular|反义词: N/A|词性: prefix


USAGE->
This prefix is generally used in scientific and medical contexts.  这个前缀通常用于科学和医学语境。

Examples:

Adenopathy refers to any disease of the glands. 腺病是指任何腺体疾病。

Adenoma is a benign tumor formed from glandular structures in epithelial tissue. 腺瘤是由上皮组织中的腺体结构形成的良性肿瘤。

PHRASE->

adenoma = 腺瘤
