badged:/bædʒd/
| adj. | 戴着徽章的；带有标记的 | Displaying or wearing a badge. |  The security guard was badged and uniformed.  保安戴着徽章，穿着制服。 | badged, marked, identified | unbadged, unmarked, unidentified | adjective


badged:/bædʒd/
| v. | 授予徽章；标记 | To award or mark with a badge. | The employees were badged after completing their training.  员工在完成培训后被授予徽章。 | award, mark, identify | remove, unmark | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From badge + -ed.  "Badge" comes from Middle English "bage," a variant of "bagge," meaning "bag" or "pouch," likely referring to a small bag or pouch used to carry a distinguishing mark.
