earshrift:/ˈɪərʃrɪft/
noun
忏悔;倾听
a hearing or attention, especially one given to a confession or complaint.
He gave me an earshrift about his problems at work. 他向我倾诉了他在工作中遇到的问题。
Synonyms: audience, hearing, attention
Antonyms: inattention, disregard
