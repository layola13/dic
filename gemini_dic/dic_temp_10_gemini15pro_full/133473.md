nonfeeblest:/ˌnɒnˈfiːblɪst/ | adj. | 不是最弱的；最强的 |  Formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to "feeblest" (the superlative form of "feeble," meaning "weak").  It describes something that is not among the weakest of a group, implying it's relatively strong or the strongest. |  He was by far the nonfeeblest of the contenders. 他是竞争者中最强的。| Synonyms: strongest, most robust, most powerful, most vigorous | Antonyms: feeblest, weakest, frailest, most delicate | word type: adjective
