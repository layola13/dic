inscrutability:/ˌɪnskruːtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可测性；难以理解；神秘莫测 | the quality of being impossible to understand or interpret |  His face held an air of inscrutability. 他的脸上带着一种难以捉摸的表情。 |  mystery, impenetrability, enigma | transparency, clarity, openness | noun

inscrutable:/ɪnˈskruːtəbl/ | adj. | 不可思议的；难以理解的；神秘的 | impossible to understand or interpret | Her expression was inscrutable. 她的表情令人难以捉摸。| mysterious, enigmatic, impenetrable | obvious, clear, transparent | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inscrutabilis, from in- "not" + scrutari "to search, examine," from scruta "trash, rags, lumber," of uncertain origin.
