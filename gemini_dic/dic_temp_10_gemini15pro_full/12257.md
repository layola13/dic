ascension-day: /əˈsɛnʃən deɪ/ | n. | 耶稣升天节 |The Christian feast celebrating the Ascension of Christ, held on the Thursday 40 days after Easter. |  例句：Ascension Day is a public holiday in many countries. 耶稣升天节在许多国家是公共假日。

例句：Services are held in churches on Ascension Day to commemorate Christ's ascension into heaven. 教堂在耶稣升天节举行仪式以纪念基督升天。| 近义词：Holy Thursday, Ascension Thursday | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

Etymology: From Old English *sēo ascensiun dæg  meaning "the ascension day,"  composed of ascensiun (from Latin ascensio, meaning "a rising, ascent") + dæg (meaning "day").
