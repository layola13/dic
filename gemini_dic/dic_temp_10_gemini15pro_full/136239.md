nonrenouncing:/ˌnɒnrɪˈnaʊnsɪŋ/
adjective
不放弃的，不摒弃的
Not renouncing or abandoning something, especially a belief, claim, or course of action.
The Dalai Lama remains nonrenouncing in his pursuit of a peaceful resolution. 达赖喇嘛仍然不放弃寻求和平解决的办法。
Near synonyms: steadfast, unwavering, resolute, firm, persistent
Near antonyms: renouncing, abandoning, relinquishing, forswearing, rejecting
word type: adjective
