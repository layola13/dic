liaison:/liˈeɪzɒn/ (UK) /liˈeɪzɑːn/ (US)| n.|联络；联络人；联络工作；（尤指不正当的）私通|A close working relationship between people or organizations.|例句：He acted as a liaison between the police and the community. 他充当了警察和社区之间的联络人。

例句：The White House press secretary is the president’s liaison to the media. 白宫新闻秘书是总统与媒体的联络人。

近义词：contact, connection, link, intermediary|反义词:separation, division|词性:noun
