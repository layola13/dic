disencumbrance:/ˌdɪsɪnˈkʌmbrəns/
n.
解除负担；摆脱累赘；除去妨碍物
The act of freeing from encumbrance, or the state of being disencumbered.
The sale of the property was a welcome disencumbrance for the family.  这处房产的出售对这个家庭来说是一种受欢迎的解脱。
Selling their old house was a great disencumbrance.  卖掉他们的旧房子是一个很大的解脱。
近义词: relief, liberation, freedom
反义词: burden, encumbrance, obstacle
词性: 名词

disencumber:/ˌdɪsɪnˈkʌmbər/
vt.
使摆脱累赘；使解除负担；清除…的障碍
To free from burdens, obstacles, or hindrances.
He disencumbered himself of his heavy coat. 他脱掉了沉重的外套。
She disencumbered the house of unnecessary furniture before moving.  她在搬家前把房子里不必要的家具都清理掉了。
近义词: relieve, liberate, free
反义词: burden, encumber, hinder
词性: 动词
