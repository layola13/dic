nonliability:/ˌnɒnˌlaɪəˈbɪləti/ /ˌnɑːnˌlaɪəˈbɪləti/
n.
无责任，免责
The contract included a clause of nonliability for accidental damage.  合同中包含了意外损害免责条款。
例句：The company's nonliability in this matter was clearly stated in the contract.  公司在这件事上的免责条款在合同中已明确说明。

The homeowner signed a waiver of nonliability before the construction began.  房主在施工开始前签署了一份免责声明。


近义词：immunity, exemption, exoneration
反义词：liability, responsibility, accountability
词性：名词
