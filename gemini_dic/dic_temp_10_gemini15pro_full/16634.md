baroquely:/bəˈrɒkli/ /bɑːˈroʊkli/ | adv. | 华丽地；装饰地；巴洛克式地 | In a baroque manner; extravagantly ornamented and convoluted in style.  Characterized by grotesque or fanciful ornamentation. | The hall was baroquely decorated with elaborate carvings and gilded moldings.  大厅以精美的雕刻和镀金的装饰华丽地装饰着。|  The music was baroquely complex, full of counterpoint and ornamentation. 这首音乐巴洛克式的复杂，充满了对位法和装饰。| 近义词：ornately, elaborately, extravagantly | 反义词：simply, plainly, austerely | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From French *baroquement*, from *baroque* + *-ment*.  Ultimately from Portuguese *barroco* ("irregular pearl"), with connotations of "bizarre," "flamboyant," "overdone."
