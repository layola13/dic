overstrained:/ˌoʊvərˈstreɪnd/ | adj. | 过度紧张的；过劳的；负担过重的 | subjected to excessive strain; excessively stressed or exerted |  The overstrained hospital staff worked tirelessly through the crisis. 过度劳累的医院工作人员在危机期间不知疲倦地工作。

She felt overstrained by the demands of her job and family.  她感到工作和家庭的需求让她不堪重负。 | 近义词：overworked, stressed, exhausted, overburdened | 反义词: relaxed, rested, calm | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From over- + strained, past participle of strain.  "Over-" 表示 "过度"，"strain" 表示 "拉紧，紧张"。
