The new employee has a nonconflictive personality and fits in well with the team.  这位新员工性格平和，与团队相处融洽。


| 近义词: peaceful, harmonious, amicable, cooperative | 反义词:  conflictive, antagonistic, hostile, aggressive | 词性: adjective
