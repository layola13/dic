ETYMOLOGY->
From aggregate + -ness.  "Aggregate" comes from Latin aggregatus, past participle of aggregare "to add to," from ad- "to" + gregare "to herd," from grex "flock."  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being."
