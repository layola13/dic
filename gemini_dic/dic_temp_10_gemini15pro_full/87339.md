A subfamily of birds in the family Ardeidae, commonly known as herons.  It includes typical herons and egrets.
Herons in the Herodiones subfamily are known for their long legs and necks.  苍鹭亚科的鹭以其长腿和长脖子而闻名。
The Herodiones are a diverse group of wading birds.  苍鹭亚科是一个多样化的涉禽群体。
Synonyms: herons, egrets
