manualist:/ˈmænjʊəlɪst/
n.
手工劳动者；手册编写者；手工操作者
A person who works with their hands or who specializes in manual skills; a person who writes or compiles manuals.
The manualist carefully assembled the intricate clockwork mechanism.  这个手工技艺者小心翼翼地组装了复杂的钟表机械装置。
The company hired a manualist to write a comprehensive user manual for their new software.  公司聘请了一位手册编写者为他们的新软件编写一份全面的用户手册。
近义词:artisan, craftsman, technician, writer, compiler
反义词: theorist, intellectual, machine
词性:noun
