eclectic:/ɪˈklɛktɪk/| adj.|兼收并蓄的，博采众长的|Selecting or choosing from various sources.|She has an eclectic taste in music, enjoying everything from classical to hip-hop. 她对音乐的品味兼收并蓄，从古典音乐到嘻哈音乐，什么都喜欢。|Synonyms: diverse, varied, broad, wide-ranging|Antonyms: narrow, limited, restricted|adjective

eclecticist:/ɪˈklɛktɪsɪst/| n.|折衷主义者，博采众长者|One who selects or chooses from various sources.|The designer was an eclecticist, drawing inspiration from various cultures and time periods.  这位设计师是一位折衷主义者，从不同的文化和时代汲取灵感。|Synonyms: selector, compiler, synthesizer|Antonyms: specialist, purist|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *eclecticus*, from Greek *eklektikós* (“selective, choosing”), from *eklégō* (“I pick out, choose”), from *ek* (“out”) + *légō* (“I gather, collect”).
