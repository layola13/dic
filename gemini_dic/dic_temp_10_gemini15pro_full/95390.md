inchest:/ˈɪntʃɪst/| adj.| 最长的（以英寸计）| superlative adjective of "inch"; longest in length as measured in inches. | The redwood was the tallest, but the oak was the **inchest** of the two trees. 这棵红木是最高的，但这棵橡树是两棵树中最粗的（以英寸计）。| 近义词: thickest, broadest, widest | 反义词: thinnest, narrowest, shortest | 词性: 形容词
