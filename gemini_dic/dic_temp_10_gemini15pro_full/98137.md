insanitary:/ɪnˈsænɪˌtɛri/
|adj.|不卫生的；不健康的
|Not sanitary; unhealthy or unhygienic.|
|The insanitary conditions in the slum posed a serious health risk. 贫民窟里不卫生的条件构成了严重的健康风险。|
|The restaurant was closed down due to its insanitary practices.  这家餐馆因其不卫生的操作而被关闭。|
|近义词:unhygienic, unhealthy, dirty, filthy|
|反义词:sanitary, hygienic, clean, healthy|
|词性:adjective|

词源:
From in- (not) + sanitary.  "Sanitary" comes from Latin "sanitas" meaning "health".
