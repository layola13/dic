neighed:/neɪd/
verb
嘶叫
A long, high-pitched cry made by a horse.
The horse neighed loudly when it saw its owner.  这匹马看到主人时大声嘶鸣。
近义词:whinnied, nickered
反义词:(no single antonym, consider "was silent", "remained quiet")
词性:v.

neighed:/neɪd/
词源:From Middle English *neighen, *neien, from Old English hnǣgan (“to neigh”), from Proto-Germanic *hneighaną (“to neigh”), from Proto-Indo-European *kneigh-.  Cognate with Dutch neighen (“to neigh”), German wiehern (“to neigh”), Old Norse hneggja (“to neigh”).
用法说明:Used to describe the sound a horse makes.  Can be used literally or figuratively (e.g., to describe a loud, harsh laugh).
