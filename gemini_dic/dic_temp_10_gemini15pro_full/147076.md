overliberalities: /ˌoʊvərˌlɪbərˈælɪtiz/
noun
过度慷慨；过分宽容的行为（通常复数）
Excessive generosity or tolerance, often to a fault.  It refers to acts of generosity that are considered excessive or unwise.
His overliberalities led him to financial ruin. 他的过度慷慨导致他破产。
Synonyms: excesses, extravagances, lavishness
Antonyms: stinginess, miserliness, parsimony
