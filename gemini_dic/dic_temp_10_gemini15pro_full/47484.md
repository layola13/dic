deambulatory:/diˈæmbjʊləˌtɔːri/ /diˈæmbjəˌtɔri/
adjective
可步行的；走动的
Relating to or adapted for walking; able to walk.
Example: The patient is now deambulatory after the surgery.  手术后病人现在可以走动了。
Example: The hospital wing has a deambulatory garden for patients.  医院的侧楼有一个供病人散步的花园。
Synonym: ambulant, mobile, walking
Antonym: bedridden, immobile, non-ambulatory
词性: adjective


deambulatory:/diˈæmbjʊləˌtɔːri/ /diˈæmbjəˌtɔri/
noun
散步场所；迴廊
A place for walking, especially a cloister.
Example: The monks took a meditative walk in the deambulatory.  僧侣们在迴廊里冥想散步。
Example: The museum features a beautiful deambulatory with stunning views.  博物馆有一个美丽的迴廊，可以欣赏到迷人的景色。
Synonym: cloister, ambulatory, walkway, passage
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "confined space" or "room")
词性: noun
