bazooka:/bəˈzuːkə/| n.|火箭筒；火箭炮|A portable rocket launcher used to fire armor-piercing projectiles.|The soldier fired the bazooka at the tank. 士兵用火箭筒向坦克开火。|Synonyms: rocket launcher, RPG|Antonyms: None|noun

bazooka:/bəˈzuːkə/| n.|喇叭状乐器|A musical instrument resembling a trombone, invented by Bob Burns.|Bob Burns played his homemade bazooka. 鲍勃·伯恩斯演奏了他自制的喇叭状乐器。|Synonyms: trombone-like instrument|Antonyms: None|noun
