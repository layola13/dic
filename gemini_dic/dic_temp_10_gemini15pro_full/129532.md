non-belligerency:/ˌnɒn bɛˈlɪdʒərənsi/ | n. |不交战；中立|The state of not being at war, but not necessarily being neutral.  A non-belligerent nation may provide aid to a belligerent, but does not engage in direct combat. | Example 1: Switzerland maintained a policy of non-belligerency during World War II.  瑞士在二战期间保持了不交战政策。

Example 2:  The small nation declared non-belligerency, refusing to take sides in the conflict.  这个小国宣布不交战，拒绝在冲突中选边站。 | Synonym: neutrality, non-involvement | Antonym: belligerency, hostility | noun
