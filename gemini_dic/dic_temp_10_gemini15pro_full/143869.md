outpointed:/ˌaʊtˈpɔɪntɪd/
verb
以点数击败，胜过
defeated by scoring more points in a contest, especially boxing or other sports judged by points.
The champion was outpointed by the challenger in a thrilling 12-round bout. 这位冠军在激动人心的12回合比赛中被挑战者以点数击败。
outperform, surpass, beat
lose to, be defeated by
v.
