overstretching:/ˌoʊvərˈstrɛtʃɪŋ/
|n.|过度伸展，承担过多|The act of stretching something too far, or taking on more than one can handle.|例句：Overstretching of the muscles can cause injuries.  肌肉过度伸展会导致受伤。

例句：The company's resources were overstretched due to rapid expansion.  由于快速扩张，公司的资源被过度使用了。|近义词：overexertion, overextension, straining|反义词:undertaking, relaxing|词性:名词

overstretch:/ˌoʊvərˈstrɛtʃ/
|vt.|过度伸展，承担过多|To stretch something too far, or to take on more than one can handle.|例句：He overstretched his budget by buying a new car. 他因为买了新车而超出预算。

例句：Don't overstretch yourself by trying to do too many things at once. 不要试图同时做太多事情而让自己负担过重。|近义词：overexert, overextend, strain|反义词:undertake, relax|词性:动词
