barmcloth:/ˈbɑːrmklɒθ/| n.|酵母布，发酵布|A cloth used to cover dough while it rises, or to wrap around a container of fermenting yeast.  It helps maintain a warm, humid environment conducive to yeast activity.|The baker covered the dough with a barmcloth and placed it in a warm place to rise.|近义词：proofing cloth|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
