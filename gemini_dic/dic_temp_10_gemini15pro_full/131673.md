nonconcerns:/ˌnɑːnkənˈsɜːrnz/
n.
不关心的事情，无关紧要的事情
Things that are not considered important or worrisome.
The CEO dismissed the rumors as nonconcerns. 首席执行官将这些谣言斥为无关紧要的事情。
The environmental impact was one of his nonconcerns. 环境影响是他不关心的事情之一。
synonyms: trivialities, irrelevancies, unimportant matters
antonyms: concerns, worries, important issues
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
non- + concerns
