grew-up:/ɡruː ˈʌp/ | adj. | 长大的，成年的 | Having reached maturity; raised. |  He has a grown-up son. 他有一个成年的儿子。| Example: She acts very grown-up for her age. 她年纪轻轻就表现得很成熟。 | Synonyms: mature, adult, developed | Antonyms: immature, childish, young | adjective

grew-up:/ɡruː ˈʌp/ | v. | 长大，成长 |  Past tense of "grow up," meaning to develop from childhood to adulthood. | Example: I grew up in a small town. 我在一个小镇长大。| Synonyms: mature, develop | Antonyms: regress, shrink, diminish | verb
