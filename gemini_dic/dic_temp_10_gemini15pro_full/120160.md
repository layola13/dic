mind-blowingly:/ˈmaɪndˌbloʊɪŋli/
adjective
令人极度兴奋的；令人印象深刻的；令人震惊的
Extremely impressive, exciting, or shocking.
The special effects in the movie were mind-blowingly realistic.  这部电影的特效逼真得令人震惊。
Synonyms: astonishingly, incredibly, amazingly, astoundingly
Antonyms: underwhelmingly, dully, boringly
词性: 副词 (adverb)
