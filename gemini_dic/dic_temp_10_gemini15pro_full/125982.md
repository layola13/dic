musketo:/mʌsˈkiːtoʊ/ | n. | 蚊子 | A small, flying insect that bites people and animals and sucks their blood. Some kinds of mosquitoes spread diseases like malaria. | Example:  Mosquitoes are a common pest in warm, humid climates. 蚊子在温暖潮湿的气候中是一种常见的害虫。

Example: She swatted the mosquito that landed on her arm. 她拍死了落在她胳膊上的蚊子。 | 近义词: gnat, midge | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用驱蚊剂或灭蚊器等相关词语表达相反的概念，例如：mosquito repellent, mosquito net) | 词性: noun
