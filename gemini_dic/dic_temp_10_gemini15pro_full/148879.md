overweight: /ˌoʊvərˈweɪt/ | adj. n. | 超重的，过重的；超重，过重 |  Heavier than the standard weight range for your height and build.  Being too heavy for your height. |  例句：The doctor told him he was overweight and needed to lose weight. 医生告诉他，他超重了，需要减肥。

例句：Many children are overweight due to poor diet and lack of exercise. 许多孩子由于饮食不良和缺乏运动而超重。 | 近义词：obese, heavy, stout, plump | 反义词：underweight, thin, slim | 词性: 形容词, 名词


overweights: /ˌoʊvərˈweɪts/ | n. | 超重者，超重的人（复数）| People who are overweight. | 例句：The program is designed to help overweights achieve a healthy weight.  这个项目旨在帮助超重者达到健康的体重。

例句：The study focused on the health risks faced by overweights. 这项研究的重点是超重者面临的健康风险。 | 近义词：obese people, heavy people | 反义词：underweight people, thin people | 词性: 名词


词源:  "over" (超过) + "weight" (重量)
