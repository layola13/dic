austral:/ˈɔːstrəl, ˈɒs-/| adj.|南方的，南风的|Relating to the south or the Southern Hemisphere.|例句：The austral summer is from December to February. 南半球的夏季是从十二月到二月。|例句：Austral winds blow from the south. 南风从南方吹来。|近义词：southern, southerly|反义词：northern, boreal|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin australis "southern," from auster "south wind," from Proto-Indo-European *aus-tero-, comparative of *aus- "to shine," in reference to the sunrise.
