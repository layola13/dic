anticommercial:/ˌænti kəˈmɜːrʃl/ | adj. | 反商业的，反对商业化的，非商业性的 | Opposed to or hostile towards commercial principles or practices; not motivated by profit. |  Example 1:  He has a very anticommercial attitude and refuses to buy anything advertised on television. 他有很强的反商业态度，拒绝购买任何在电视上做广告的东西。

Example 2: The artist's anticommercial stance meant they refused corporate sponsorship.  这位艺术家的反商业立场意味着他们拒绝企业赞助。 | 近义词: noncommercial, uncommercial | 反义词: commercial, pro-business | 词性: 形容词
