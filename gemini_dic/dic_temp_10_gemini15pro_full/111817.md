logbooks:/ˈlɒɡbʊks/ (UK) /ˈlɔːɡbʊks/ (US)| n. |航海日志，日志，记录簿|A book in which official records of events, especially on a ship or aircraft, are kept.|The captain meticulously filled out the logbook every day. 船长每天一丝不苟地填写航海日志。|The pilot noted the turbulence in the aircraft's logbook. 飞行员在飞机的日志中记录了湍流。|synonyms: journal, record, diary, register|antonyms: N/A|noun
