leucopyrite:/ˈluːkoʊˌpaɪ.raɪt/| n. |白砷钴矿|A naturally occurring iron arsenide mineral with the chemical formula FeAs₂.  It is typically silver-white to steel-gray in color and has a metallic luster. It's a significant source of arsenic.|The geologist identified the sample as leucopyrite due to its metallic luster and composition. 地质学家根据其金属光泽和成分将样品鉴定为白砷钴矿。|Synonym: loellingite|Antonym: N/A|noun
