cinerulent:/ˌsɪnəˈruːlənt/ | adj. | 灰色的；灰烬般的；像灰烬的 | Of the color of ashes. Resembling ashes. |  The cinerulent landscape after the wildfire was a chilling sight. 野火过后，灰烬般的景象令人不寒而栗。

The moon cast a cinerulent glow over the sleeping city. 月光给沉睡的城市蒙上了一层灰色的光辉。 | 近义词: ashen, ash-colored, gray, greyish | 反义词: colorful, vibrant, bright | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *cinerulentus*, from *cinis*, *cineris* "ashes" + *-ulentus*  a suffix forming adjectives denoting "full of" or "abounding in".
