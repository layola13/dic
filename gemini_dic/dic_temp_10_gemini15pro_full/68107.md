faff:/fæf/
faff | n. | 忙碌而无意义的事情；小题大做；瞎忙活 | Fussing about and wasting time on trivial matters. |  I spent all morning faffing about and got nothing done. 我整个上午都在瞎忙活，什么也没做。 | synonym: fuss, fiddle, dither | antonym: focus, concentrate | noun
faff | vi. | 浪费时间；小题大做；瞎忙活 | To waste time on unimportant things; to dawdle. | Stop faffing and get ready! 别磨蹭了，赶紧准备好！ | synonym: dawdle, potter, mess around | antonym: expedite, hasten | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
Possibly imitative of light, restless movement. First recorded in the early 20th century.
