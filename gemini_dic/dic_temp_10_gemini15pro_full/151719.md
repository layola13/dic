party-walled: /ˈpɑːrti wɔːld/ | adj. | 共墙的，共用隔墙的 | Having a shared wall with an adjoining building.  A party wall is a dividing wall between two properties, jointly owned and maintained by both owners. |  Our house is party-walled with our neighbour's. 我们的房子和邻居的房子共用一堵墙。 |  The noise from the party-walled apartment kept me awake.  从共用隔墙的公寓传来的噪音让我无法入睡。 | synonyms: adjoining, attached, connected | antonyms: detached, separate | adjective

party-walled: /ˈpɑːrti wɔːld/ | n. | 共墙，共用隔墙 | A shared wall between two properties. | The party-walled caused a dispute between the neighbours. 共用墙引发了邻居之间的纠纷. |  We had to get permission to make changes to the party-walled. 我们必须获得许可才能对共用墙进行更改。| synonyms: dividing wall, common wall | antonyms: freestanding wall | noun
