ideology: /ˌaɪdiˈɒlədʒi/ (US) /ˌaɪdiˈɒlədʒi/ (UK)| n. | 思想体系，意识形态 | A system of ideas and ideals, especially one that forms the basis of economic or political theory and policy. |  The party's ideology is based on socialist principles.  该党的意识形态建立在社会主义原则之上。

The ideology of the ruling class is often used to justify their power. 统治阶级的意识形态常被用来为他们的权力辩护。| 近义词: doctrine, philosophy, creed, belief system | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps contrasting ideologies like *capitalism* vs. *communism*) | 词性: noun
