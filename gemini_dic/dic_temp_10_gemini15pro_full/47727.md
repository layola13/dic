decaffeinated:/diːˈkæfɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
脱咖啡因的
Having had caffeine removed.
例句：I ordered a decaffeinated coffee because I can't sleep after regular coffee. 我点了一杯脱咖啡因的咖啡，因为喝完普通咖啡后我睡不着。

例句：Decaffeinated tea is a good option for those sensitive to caffeine.  脱咖啡因茶叶对咖啡因敏感的人来说是一个不错的选择。

近义词：caffeine-free
反义词：caffeinated
词性：adjective


decaffeinate:/diːˈkæfɪneɪt/
verb
去除咖啡因
To remove caffeine from.
例句：They decaffeinate the coffee beans using a special process. 他们使用一种特殊工艺去除咖啡豆中的咖啡因。

例句：The company decaffeinates its tea to offer a wider range of products.  这家公司去除茶叶中的咖啡因以提供更广泛的产品。

近义词：remove caffeine from
反义词：caffeinate
词性：verb
