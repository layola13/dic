hucksterish:/ˈhʌkstərɪʃ/ | adj. | 商贩气的；叫卖的；夸夸其谈的；奸商的 | Suggestive of a huckster; aggressively self-promoting or overly insistent on selling something.  Characterized by blatant, insistent, or exaggerated self-promotion, often involving dubious claims or methods. | The politician's hucksterish promises failed to convince many voters.  这位政客的叫卖式承诺没能说服很多选民。 |  The infomercial had a hucksterish tone that made me distrust the product. 这则电视购物广告有一种商贩气的腔调，让我不信任这个产品。 | synonyms: pushy, aggressive, slick, shifty, mercenary | antonyms: sincere, genuine, honest, understated | adjective
