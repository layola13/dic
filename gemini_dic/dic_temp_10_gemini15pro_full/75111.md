fripperer:/ˈfrɪpərər/
n.
旧货商；衣着过时的人；穿着破旧衣服的人
A person who deals in frippery (old clothes and cheap finery); one who dresses in showy but cheap and worn-out finery.
He looked like a fripperer in his tattered, yet ostentatious coat. 他穿着那件破烂却招摇的外套，看起来像个旧货商。
fripperer is someone who deals in old clothes, or someone who dresses in old, showy clothes. 这个词指的是旧货商，或者穿着破旧华丽衣服的人。
Synonyms: dealer in second-hand clothes, rag dealer, bargain hunter
Antonyms: fashionista, trendsetter
noun
