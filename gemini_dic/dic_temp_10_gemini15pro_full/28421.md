carbonizable:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪzəbl/
adjective
可碳化的
Capable of being carbonized; convertible into carbon.
Coal is a carbonizable substance. 煤是一种可碳化的物质。
The organic material is carbonizable under high temperatures.  这种有机材料在高温下可以碳化。
近义词:combustible, ignitable
反义词:noncombustible, incombustible
词性:adjective

carbonize:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪz/
verb
碳化
To convert or be converted into carbon or a carbon residue by partial burning or destructive distillation.
The wood was carbonized by the intense heat. 木头被高温碳化了。
The process carbonizes the organic matter, leaving behind a charcoal-like substance. 这个过程使有机物碳化，留下类似木炭的物质。
近义词:char, burn
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "purify" or "refine" in certain contexts)
词性:verb

Etymology:
From carbon + -ize + -able.  Ultimately from Latin "carbo" (coal, charcoal).
