carnic:/ˈkɑːrnɪk/ | n. | 食肉动物 | A carnivorous animal. |  The carnic, a large predator, roamed the plains.  这只大型食肉动物在平原上游荡。 | Synonyms: carnivore, predator | Antonyms: herbivore | noun


Etymology: From Latin *carnicus, from caro, carnis ("flesh").
