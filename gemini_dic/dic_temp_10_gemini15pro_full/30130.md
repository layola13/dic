cedriret:/ˈsɛdrɪrɛt/ | n. | 雪松油精 | An active principle contained in cedar oil.  A sesquiterpene alcohol found in cedarwood oil. |  Cedriret is used in microscopy as a stain. 雪松油精在显微镜检查中用作染色剂。| Cedriret gives wood its characteristic scent. 雪松油精赋予木材特有的香味。 |Synonyms: cedrol, cedrene | Antonyms: N/A | noun
