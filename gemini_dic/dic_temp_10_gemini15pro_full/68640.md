faquir:/fəˈkɪə(r)/ | n. |托钵僧；苦行僧|A Muslim or Hindu religious ascetic who lives solely on alms.|He gave his money to a wandering faquir. 他把钱给了云游的托钵僧。|Synonyms: ascetic, mendicant, dervish|Antonyms: materialist, hedonist|noun

Etymology:
From French *fakir*, from Arabic *faqīr*  "poor," "needy."
