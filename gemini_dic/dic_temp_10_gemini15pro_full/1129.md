accessorizes: /əkˈsɛsəˌraɪzɪz/
verb
搭配装饰;佩戴饰品
To add accessories to something, typically clothing or an outfit, in order to enhance its appearance.
She accessorizes her outfits with colorful scarves and jewelry. 她用色彩鲜艳的围巾和珠宝来搭配她的服装。

He always accessorizes with a stylish hat. 他总是戴着一顶时尚的帽子作为装饰。

近义词: adorn, ornament, decorate, embellish
反义词: strip, simplify
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From accessory + -ize.  "Accessory" comes from the Late Latin word *accessorius*, meaning "additional," from the Latin "accessus," meaning "approach" or "addition."  The suffix "-ize" forms verbs meaning "to make or cause to be," "to subject to the process of," or "to become like."
