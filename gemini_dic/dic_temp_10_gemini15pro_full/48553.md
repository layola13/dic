defamatory:/dɪˈfæmətɔːri/
adjective
诽谤的，中伤的
(of remarks, writing, etc.) damaging the good reputation of someone; slanderous or libelous.
The article made defamatory remarks about the company's CEO. 这篇文章对公司首席执行官发表了诽谤性言论。
synonyms: libelous, slanderous, calumnious, denigratory, vilifying, traducing, aspersive, maligning, derogatory, disparaging, insulting, offensive, abusive, scurrilous, scandalous, vituperative, opprobrious, damaging, harmful, injurious, prejudicial, detrimental
antonyms: complimentary, flattering, laudatory
