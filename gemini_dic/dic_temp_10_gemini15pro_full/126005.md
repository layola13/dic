muslins:/ˈmʌzlɪnz/| n.|平纹细布，薄棉布，穆斯林服装|A fine, plain-woven cotton fabric, often used for clothing and other textiles. Also, sometimes used to refer to clothing made from this fabric, particularly in the context of South Asian or Middle Eastern cultures. |She wore a beautiful dress made of white muslin. 她穿着一件漂亮的白色平纹细布连衣裙。|The market stalls were filled with colorful muslins. 市场摊位上摆满了五颜六色的平纹细布。| 近义词：cotton, gauze, voile| 反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps heavy cloth, or wool)|词性:noun
