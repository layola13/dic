commercialists:/kəˈmɜːrʃəlɪsts/
| n. | 商业主义者；重商主义者 | People who are primarily focused on profit and commercial success, often at the expense of other values. | The commercialists pushed for deregulation to maximize profits. 商业主义者为了最大化利润而推动放松管制。| The gallery owner, ever the commercialist, refused to exhibit the work of young, unknown artists.  画廊老板，一贯的商业主义者，拒绝展出年轻的、不知名艺术家的作品。| synonyms: profiteers, merchants, businesspeople | antonyms: philanthropists, altruists | noun
