formability:/ˌfɔːməˈbɪləti/
noun
可成形性，成形性
The quality of a material that allows it to be shaped or formed without breaking or cracking.
The formability of the metal allows it to be easily pressed into complex shapes.  这种金属的可成形性使它很容易被压制成复杂的形状。
malleability, plasticity, ductility
inflexibility, rigidity
n.
