overpromoting: /ˌoʊvərprəˈmoʊtɪŋ/ | v. | 过度晋升；过分宣传 | Promoting someone to a position beyond their capabilities or excessively promoting a product or service. |  The company overpromoted him to a managerial role, and he struggled to cope.  公司过度提拔他到管理职位，他难以胜任。 | Overpromotion can lead to employee burnout. 过度晋升会导致员工倦怠。 | 近义词: overselling, overhyping | 反义词: underpromoting | 词性: 动名词 (gerund)
