dreadest:/ˈdredɪst/ | adj. | 最可怕的，最令人恐惧的 | Superlative form of "dreadful," meaning most extremely bad or serious, inspiring great fear. |例句：It was the dreadest news I had ever heard. 这是我听过的最可怕的消息。|例句：The dreadest storm of the century ravaged the coast.  本世纪最可怕的风暴席卷了海岸。| 近义词：worst, most terrible, most frightening, most awful | 反义词：best, most wonderful, most pleasant | 词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *dredest, superlative of *dred, from Old English *drǣdan "to fear, dread."
