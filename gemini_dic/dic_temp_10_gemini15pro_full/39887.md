constitute:/ˈkɒnstɪtjuːt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnstɪtuːt/ (US)| v.|组成，构成；设立，建立|to be the parts or members that form something; to establish by law|Twelve months constitute a year. 十二个月构成一年。The committee was constituted in 1974. 该委员会成立于1974年。|近义词：form, compose, comprise, establish, create|反义词：dissolve, abolish|词性:动词

constituter:/ˈkɒnstɪtjuːtər/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnstɪtuːtər/ (US)| n.|组成者，构成者；设立者|a person or thing that constitutes something|The constituters of the new company were mainly small businesses. 新公司的组成者主要是小型企业。|近义词：founder, creator, establisher|反义词：dissolver, abolisher|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *constituten*, from Latin *cōnstitūere* (“to set up, establish, appoint, make, create, form, compose”), from *con-* (“together”) + *statuere* (“to set, place, cause to stand”), from *status* (“standing, position, condition”).
