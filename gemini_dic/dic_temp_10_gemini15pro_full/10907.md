arak: /ˈɑːræk/ | n. | 阿拉克酒，烧酒 | An alcoholic spirit distilled from the fermented sap of various palm trees, especially the coconut palm, or from rice or sugar cane, widely consumed in Southeast Asia and the Middle East. | Example: He ordered a glass of arak. 他点了一杯阿拉克酒。

Example: Arak is a popular drink in some countries.  阿拉克酒在一些国家很受欢迎。
