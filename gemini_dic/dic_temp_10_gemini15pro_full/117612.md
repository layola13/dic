megaphyllous:/ˌmɛɡəˈfɪləs/| adj.|巨叶的，大型叶的|Having large leaves with a complex system of veins, characteristic of ferns and seed plants.|Ferns and most seed plants are megaphyllous, unlike lycophytes which have microphylls.|近义词: macrophyllous|反义词: microphyllous|词性:形容词

词源:
mega- (large) + -phyll (leaf)  From Greek *megaphyllon*, from *megas* (large) + *phyllon* (leaf).
