frail:/freɪl/| adj.|脆弱的，虚弱的，易碎的|Easily broken or damaged, physically weak and delicate.|The frail old woman needed help crossing the street.  这位虚弱的老妇人需要帮助才能过马路。|Synonyms: delicate, fragile, weak, feeble|Antonyms: strong, robust, sturdy|adjective

frail:/freɪl/| n.|柳条筐；（用柳条等编织的）篮子|A basket made of rushes, twigs, or strips of wood.|She carried her belongings in a frail. 她用柳条筐装着她的 belongings。 |Synonyms: basket, hamper|Antonyms: N/A|noun
