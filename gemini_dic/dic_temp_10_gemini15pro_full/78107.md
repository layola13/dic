geologist:/dʒiˈɒlədʒɪst/ /dʒiˈɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |地质学家|A scientist who studies the earth, its structure, and its history.|例句：The geologist examined the rock samples. 地质学家检查了岩石样本。

例句：The geologist studied the earth's layers to understand its formation.  地质学家研究了地球的层级以了解其形成过程。|近义词：earth scientist, geoscientist|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

词源: From French *géologiste*, from géo- + -logiste (as in biologiste). Surface analysis: géo- +‎ -logist. From geo- +‎ -logy +‎ -ist.
