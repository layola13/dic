maidens:/ˈmeɪdnz/| n.|少女们，处女们|A maiden is a young unmarried woman, especially one who is still a virgin.  Maidens refers to multiple such women. |The maidens danced gracefully in the moonlight. 少女们在月光下优雅地跳舞。|近义词：girls, young women, virgins|反义词:matrons, crones|Noun
