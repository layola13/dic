actualises:/ˈæktʃuəlaɪzɪz/
verb
实现，实行
To make something real or to put something into action.
The company actualised its plans for expansion. = 公司实现了其扩张计划。

He finally actualised his dream of becoming a pilot. = 他终于实现了成为飞行员的梦想。

synonyms: realize, achieve, fulfill, implement
antonyms: neglect, abandon, fail, postpone
词性: 动词(verb)

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company actualises its profits annually. = 公司每年实现其利润。
He actualises his potential through hard work. = 他通过努力工作实现自己的潜力。

一般过去时:
They actualised their plan ahead of schedule. = 他们提前实现了他们的计划。
She actualised her vision of a better future. = 她实现了对美好未来的愿景。

现在完成时:
He has actualised many of his goals. = 他已经实现了他的许多目标。
The team has actualised a significant breakthrough in their research. = 该团队在研究中取得了重大突破。

过去完成时:

将来时:
The company will actualise its new marketing strategy next quarter. = 公司将在下个季度实施其新的营销策略。
They will actualise their plans for a new product launch soon. = 他们很快将实现推出新产品的计划。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
