montgolfier:/mɒnˈɡɒlfiə(r)/ /mɑːnˈɡoʊlfiər/
n.
热气球；蒙哥尔费兄弟（发明热气球）
A hot-air balloon; referring to the Montgolfier brothers, inventors of the hot-air balloon.
The Montgolfier brothers are credited with inventing the hot-air balloon.  蒙哥尔费兄弟被认为是热气球的发明者。
We took a ride in a colorful montgolfier. 我们乘坐了一个色彩鲜艳的热气球。
近义词:hot-air balloon
反义词:(None -  no direct opposite for a specific type of balloon)
词性:noun
