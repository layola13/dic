aborter:/əˈbɔːrtər/| n.|堕胎者；中止者；放弃者|A person who has an abortion or who causes an abortion; someone who abandons an undertaking.|She felt judged by society for being an aborter. 她感觉因为堕胎而被社会批判。|The aborter decided to stop the project due to unforeseen circumstances. 由于不可预见的情况，放弃者决定停止该项目。|近义词：miscarrier, terminator, abandoner|反义词: continuer, finisher|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "abort," which comes from the Latin "abortus," past participle of "aborīrī," meaning "to miscarry," from "ab-" (away, from) + "orīrī" (to arise, to be born).
