microblogging:/ˈmaɪkroʊblɒɡɪŋ/ /ˈmaɪkroʊblɑːɡɪŋ/| n. |微博客；微型博客|The act of posting short, frequent updates to a microblog, like Twitter or Tumblr.  It involves sharing small pieces of content like text, images, or links.|She's built a huge following through consistent microblogging.|近义词：social networking, tweeting (specifically for Twitter)|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "long-form blogging")|词性:noun
