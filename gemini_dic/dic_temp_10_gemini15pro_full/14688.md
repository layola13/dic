awaked: /əˈweɪkt/ | v. | 唤醒，醒来，觉醒 | past tense of awake; roused from sleep; stimulated into action or awareness |  He awaked to the sound of birds singing. 他被鸟儿的歌声吵醒了。| 近义词: awakened, roused, stirred | 反义词: asleep, dormant | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *awaken, from Old English āwæcnan (“to awaken, awake”), from Proto-Germanic *wakjaną (“to watch, wake up”), from Proto-Indo-European *wog- (“to be awake, watchful”). Cognate with Scots awauken (“to awake”), West Frisian wekkerje (“to wake”), Dutch wekken (“to wake”), German wecken (“to wake”), Danish vække (“to wake”), Swedish väcka (“to wake”).
