nondirectional:/ˌnɒndɪˈrɛkʃənəl/ | /ˌnɑːndɪˈrɛkʃənəl/
adjective
非定向的；无方向性的
Not having, exhibiting, or dependent on a direction.  Lacking direction or aim.
The study used a nondirectional hypothesis, meaning it didn't predict which group would perform better.  这项研究使用了一个非定向假设，这意味着它没有预测哪一组会表现得更好。

The plant's growth was nondirectional, spreading equally in all directions.  这种植物的生长是非定向的，向各个方向均匀地扩展。

synonym: aimless, random, undirected, diffuse
antonym: directional, directed, focused, oriented
词性: 形容词
