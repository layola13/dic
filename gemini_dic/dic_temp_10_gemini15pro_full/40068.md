contractual:/kənˈtræktʃuəl/
adj.
契约的，合同的
Relating to or contained in a contract.
例句：We have a contractual obligation to finish the project by the end of the year. 我们有合同义务在年底前完成这个项目。

例句：The contractual terms were clearly defined. 合同条款有明确的定义。

近义词：agreed, stipulated, binding
反义词：non-contractual, informal

词性: adjective
