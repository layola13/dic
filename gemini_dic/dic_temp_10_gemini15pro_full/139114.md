obeah: /ˈoʊbiə/ (US), /ˈɒbiə/ (UK)| n.|奥比亚，巫毒教|A kind of sorcery or witchcraft practiced especially in the West Indies.  It involves the use of charms, spells, and rituals, often with malevolent intent. |She believed she was the victim of obeah. 她相信她是奥比亚的受害者。|He was accused of practicing obeah. 他被指控施行奥比亚。|近义词：witchcraft, voodoo, sorcery, black magic|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "benediction", "blessing")|词性:noun
