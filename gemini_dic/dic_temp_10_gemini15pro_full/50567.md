dequantitate:/diːˈkwɒntɪteɪt/
verb
使非量化；取消量化
To remove from a quantitative form; to make non-quantitative.  This means to express something in a way that doesn't involve specific numbers or amounts.
The artist sought to dequantitate her work, focusing on the emotional impact rather than precise measurements.  这位艺术家试图将她的作品非量化，专注于情感冲击而不是精确的测量。
The report dequantitated the findings, presenting them as general trends instead of specific statistics.  报告对调查结果进行了非量化处理，将其作为一般趋势而不是具体统计数据呈现。
近义词:qualify, modify
反义词:quantify, measure
词性:v.
