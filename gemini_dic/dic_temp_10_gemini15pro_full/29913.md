cautiousness:/ˈkɔːʃəsnəs/| n.|谨慎,小心,慎重|The quality of being cautious; carefulness in avoiding potential danger or harm.|He approached the task with extreme cautiousness. 他极其谨慎地处理这项任务。|近义词:carefulness, prudence, circumspection|反义词:recklessness, carelessness, imprudence|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: cautiousnes(se), from Old French caustos, cautos ‘cautious’ (from Latin cautus, past participle of cavere ‘beware’) + -ness.
