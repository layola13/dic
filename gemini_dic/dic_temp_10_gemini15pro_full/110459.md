liliaceous:/ˌlɪliˈeɪʃəs/
adj.
百合科的；像百合花的
Of or relating to the lily family (Liliaceae), typically having flowers with parts in threes, including six colored tepals, six stamens, and a superior ovary.
Lilies, tulips, and daffodils are liliaceous plants. 百合花、郁金香和水仙花都是百合科植物。
Near synonyms: lily-like,  lily-flowered
Antonyms: non-liliaceous
