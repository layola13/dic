nematogene:/ˈnɛmətəˌdʒiːn/ | n. | 线形动物发生; 线形动物成因 | An agent or factor causing nematodiasis (infestation with nematodes). |  Exposure to contaminated soil can be a nematogene for various parasitic infections.  接触受污染的土壤可能是各种寄生虫感染的线形动物成因。 | 近义词:  helminthogene | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


nematogene:/ˈnɛmətəˌdʒiːn/ | adj. | 产生线虫的; 线虫发生的 | Producing or giving rise to nematodes. | The contaminated water supply proved to be nematogene, leading to widespread parasitic infections in livestock. 受污染的供水被证明会产生线虫，导致牲畜中广泛的寄生虫感染。 | 近义词: helminthogenic | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective
