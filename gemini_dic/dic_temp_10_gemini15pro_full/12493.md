aspergillosis:/æsˌpɜːrdʒɪˈloʊsɪs/| n.|曲霉病|A disease caused by fungi of the genus *Aspergillus*, affecting the respiratory system and other parts of the body.|Example 1: The farmer contracted aspergillosis after inhaling moldy hay.  农民吸入发霉的干草后感染了曲霉病。

Example 2:  Aspergillosis can be a serious threat to people with weakened immune systems.  曲霉病对免疫系统较弱的人来说可能是一个严重的威胁。|近义词: none (it's a specific disease)|反义词: none (diseases don't have antonyms)|词性: noun
