logic:/ˈlɒdʒɪk/ (UK) /ˈlɑːdʒɪk/ (US)| n.|逻辑, 逻辑学, 推理方法|The study of the principles of reasoning, especially of the structure of propositions as distinguished from their content and of method and validity in deductive reasoning.|He argued his case with impeccable logic. 他以无懈可击的逻辑论证了他的观点。|synonyms:reasoning, deduction, sense, rationality|antonyms:irrationality, absurdity|noun

logic:/ˈlɒdʒɪk/ (UK) /ˈlɑːdʒɪk/ (US)| adj.(rare) |逻辑的|Of or according to the rules of logic or formal argument.|a logical argument  一个合乎逻辑的论点|synonyms:rational, reasonable, coherent|antonyms:illogical, irrational|adjective
