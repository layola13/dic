fraudcast: /ˈfrɔːdkæst/
n.
欺诈性广播；诈骗广播
A fraudulent broadcast, typically one made over the internet, designed to deceive or mislead.
The fraudcast claimed the world was ending to sell survival kits.  这个欺诈广播声称世界末日即将到来，以此来兜售生存装备。
The fraudcast spread misinformation about a dangerous new virus. 这条欺诈广播散布了关于一种危险新病毒的虚假信息。
近义词: hoax, scam, deception, disinformation
反义词: truth, fact, accuracy
词性: 名词

词源:  A blend of "fraud" and "broadcast." "Fraud" comes from the Old French "fraude," meaning "deceit, trickery." "Broadcast" originates from the agricultural practice of scattering seeds widely.
