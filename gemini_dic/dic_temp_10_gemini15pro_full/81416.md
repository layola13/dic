grassward:/ˈgrɑːswərd/ /ˈɡræswərd/| adv. adj.|向草地；朝向草地|Toward or in the direction of a grassy area.  It can also function adjectivally to describe something situated or facing towards grassland.|The sheep drifted slowly grassward. 羊群缓缓地向草地移动。|近义词：toward the grass, towards the meadow|反义词: away from the grass, grassless|词性: adverb, adjective

Etymology:
From 'grass' + 'ward'. 'Grass' comes from Old English 'græs', of Germanic origin. 'Ward' comes from Old English 'weard', meaning "toward,"  related to Old High German 'wert'.
