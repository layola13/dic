drip-feeding:/ˈdrɪpˌfiːdɪŋ/
n.|点滴式喂养，少量多次地提供|The government is drip-feeding information to the public. 政府正在一点一点地向公众发布信息。|例句：The information was drip-fed to the media over several weeks.  这些信息在几周内被逐渐透露给媒体。

drip-feeding:/ˈdrɪpˌfiːdɪŋ/
v.|点滴式喂养，少量多次地提供|They drip-fed the hostages small amounts of food and water. 他们少量多次地给人质提供食物和水。|例句：The company has been drip-feeding us with promises, but nothing has changed.  公司一直在给我们一点点地承诺，但什么都没有改变。


近义词: rationing, doling out, trickling, meting out
反义词:  releasing all at once, dumping, flooding

词性:  noun, verb (present participle)

词源: From "drip" (to fall in drops) + "feed" (to supply with nourishment).  The metaphorical sense of providing something gradually developed later.
