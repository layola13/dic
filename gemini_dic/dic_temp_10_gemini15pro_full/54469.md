disintegrator:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪtər/
n.|崩解器，分解器|A machine or device that breaks something down into small pieces or destroys it completely. |The disintegrator reduced the rocks to powder. 崩解器将岩石变成了粉末。|synonyms: pulverizer, crusher, grinder|antonyms: assembler, constructor
词性: 名词


disintegrate:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪt/
v.|分解，瓦解，碎裂|To break up into small parts or pieces; to decay or decompose.|The spacecraft disintegrated upon reentry into the Earth's atmosphere.  宇宙飞船重返地球大气层时解体了。|synonyms: crumble, decompose, fall apart|antonyms: integrate, assemble, construct
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing reversal) + integrate (from Latin integratus, past participle of integrare "to make whole") + -or (agent suffix).
