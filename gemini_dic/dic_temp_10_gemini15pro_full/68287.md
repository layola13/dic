falcade:/fælˈkeɪd/ | n. | 斜坡；陡坡；（尤指山）峭壁 | A steep, smooth slope or cliff face, especially of a mountain. | The climbers scaled the treacherous falcade. 攀登者攀登了险峻的峭壁。| synonym: cliff, precipice, escarpment | antonym: incline, slope (gentle) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *falcade*, from Italian *falcata*, from Latin *falcatus* "curved, sickle-shaped," from *falx* "sickle."
