overpark:/ˈoʊvərˌpɑːrk/| v. |过度停车，停车过线|To park a vehicle incorrectly, extending beyond the designated parking space.|Example: I overparked and got a ticket. 我停车过线，收到了罚单。| Example:  Be careful not to overpark, the spaces are quite tight. 小心不要停车过线，车位很紧。 |Near synonyms: mispark, park incorrectly| Antonyms: park correctly|Verb
