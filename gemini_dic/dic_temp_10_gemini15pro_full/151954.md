pastoralization:/ˌpæstərəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 牧歌化，田园化 | The process of making something more rural or idyllic, often in a romanticized or idealized way.  It can refer to the actual movement of people from urban to rural areas, or to a cultural shift towards embracing rural values and aesthetics. |  The pastoralization of the suburbs has led to a rise in small-scale farming. 郊区的田园化导致了小规模农业的兴起。

The novel depicts the pastoralization of a formerly industrial town. 这部小说描绘了一个以前是工业城镇的田园化。| 近义词: ruralization, idealization, romanticization | 反义词: industrialization, urbanization | 词性: noun
