para-transit:/ˈpærəˌtrænzɪt/
noun
辅助客运，辅助交通
A transportation service designed to complement fixed-route public transit, typically serving people with disabilities or those in areas with limited access to regular transit.
The city offers a para-transit service for elderly residents who cannot use the regular bus system.  该市为无法使用常规公交系统的老年居民提供辅助客运服务。
Para-transit services are essential for ensuring equal access to transportation for everyone. 辅助客运服务对于确保人人享有平等的交通机会至关重要。
synonyms:  accessible transportation, specialized transportation, demand-response transit
antonyms: fixed-route transit, conventional transit
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From para- (meaning "beside" or "auxiliary") + transit (meaning "transportation").  源自 para-（意为“旁边”或“辅助”）+ transit（意为“交通”）。
