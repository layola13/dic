maselyn:/ˈmeɪslɪn/ | n. | 平纹细布，薄纱 | A fine, plain-woven cotton or linen fabric. |  She wore a dress of the finest maselyn. 她穿着一件最好的平纹细布连衣裙。|  Synonyms: muslin, gauze, cambric | Antonyms: canvas, burlap | Noun

Etymology: From Middle French *maslin, from Middle Dutch maselin, from the city of Mechelen (Malines) in Belgium, where it was originally made.
