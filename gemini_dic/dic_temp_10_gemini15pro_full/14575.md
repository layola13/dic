aviator:/ˈeɪvieɪtər/| n.|飞行员，驾驶员|A person who operates an aircraft.|例句：The aviator skillfully landed the plane during the storm. 飞行员在暴风雨中熟练地将飞机降落。

例句：He dreamed of becoming an aviator since he was a child. 他从小就梦想成为一名飞行员。|近义词：pilot, flyer, airman|反义词:passenger|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *aviateur*, from Latin *avis* (bird) + *-ateur* (agent suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂éwis* (bird).
