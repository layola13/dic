fissional:/ˈfɪʃənəl/| adj.|裂变的；分裂的|Relating to or characterized by fission, especially nuclear fission.|例句：The fissional process releases a tremendous amount of energy.  裂变过程释放出巨大的能量。

例句：Uranium-235 is a fissional material. 铀-235是一种可裂变材料。|近义词：splitting, dividing, cleavable|反义词：fusional|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From fission + -al.  Fission comes from the Latin word "fissio" meaning "a cleaving or splitting."
