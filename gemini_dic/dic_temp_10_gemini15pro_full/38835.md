confab:/ˈkɒnfæb/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnfæb/ (US)| n. |Informal meeting; discussion, especially a casual or unofficial one; chat|An informal meeting or discussion, often private or confidential. It can also refer to a fabricated or distorted memory.|例句：The two friends had a long confab over coffee. 两个朋友边喝咖啡边聊了很久。

例句：After the conference, the organizers had a quick confab to discuss the day's events.  会议结束后，组织者们进行了简短的交谈，讨论了当天的活动。

|近义词：chat, discussion, meeting, talk, conversation|反义词: silence, disagreement|词性:noun
