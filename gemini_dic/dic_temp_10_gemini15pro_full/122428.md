moao: /ˈmoʊ.aʊ/ | n. | 毛奥, 茂奥 (通常指毛里塔尼亚奥林匹克和体育委员会 Comité Olympique et Sportif Mauritanien) |  MOAO is the acronym for the Mauritanian Olympic and Sports Committee. It is responsible for organizing the participation of Mauritanian athletes in the Olympic Games and other international sporting events. | The MOAO sent a team of athletes to the Tokyo Olympics. 毛奥派出了一支运动员队伍参加东京奥运会。 |  | Comité Olympique et Sportif Mauritanien (French), National Olympic Committee (NOC) | noun
