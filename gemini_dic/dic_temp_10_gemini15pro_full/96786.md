ineradicable:/ˌɪnɪˈrædɪkəbl/
adjective
不能根除的，无法消除的
Not capable of being eradicated; not able to be removed or destroyed.
His influence on the team was ineradicable.  他对团队的影响是无法消除的。
The memory of that day is ineradicable. 那一天的记忆是无法磨灭的。
Synonym: permanent, ingrained, indelible, fixed, deep-rooted
Antonym: eradicable, removable, temporary, fleeting
词性: 形容词
