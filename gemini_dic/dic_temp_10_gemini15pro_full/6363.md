amphibian:/æmˈfɪbiən/ /æmˈfɪbiən/| n. adj.|两栖动物，水陆两用车|A cold-blooded vertebrate animal of a class that comprises the frogs, toads, newts, salamanders, and caecilians. They are distinguished by having an aquatic gill-breathing larval stage followed (typically) by a terrestrial lung-breathing adult stage.|The frog is an amphibian.|近义词：frog, toad, newt, salamander|反义词:|n., adj.

amphibious:/æmˈfɪbiəs/ /æmˈfɪbiəs/| adj.|两栖的，水陆两用的|Relating to, living in, or suited for both land and water.|The amphibious vehicle can travel on both land and water.|近义词：aquatic, terrestrial|反义词:|adj.
