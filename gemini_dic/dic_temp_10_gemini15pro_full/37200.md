comeons:/ˈkʌmɒnz/ (British), /ˈkʌmɑːnz/ (American)| n.|劝诱；引诱；花言巧语|An encouragement or enticement to do something, often implying a degree of pressure or manipulation.  It can also refer to seductive gestures or remarks.| "The salesman's comeons were so insistent that I finally left the store."  销售员的花言巧语太坚持不懈了，我最后离开了商店。|Synonyms: blandishments, inducements, enticements, cajolery, sweet talk|Antonyms: deterrents, discouragements|noun
