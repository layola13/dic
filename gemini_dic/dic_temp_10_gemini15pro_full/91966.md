hypermorally:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɔːrəli/ | adv. | 超道德地，过分道德地 | Excessively or ostentatiously moral; beyond what is considered normal or necessary in terms of morality.  |  He acted hypermorally, judging everyone around him for their minor flaws. 他表现得过分道德，因为他人的小缺点而评判他们。 | 近义词:  overly moral, excessively righteous, sanctimonious | 反义词:  amoral, immoral, unethical | 词性: 副词
