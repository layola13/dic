outwriggled:/ˌaʊtˈrɪɡəld/
verb
胜过，比...更灵活，扭动胜过
To surpass or defeat someone through greater agility or maneuvering, especially in a difficult situation or contest.
The smaller boat outwriggled the larger one in the choppy waters.  小船在波涛汹涌的水域中比大船更灵活，最终胜出。
The politician outwriggled his opponents with clever arguments.  这位政治家以巧妙的论证胜过了他的对手。
近义词:outmaneuver, surpass, defeat, outsmart
反义词:be defeated, lose to
词性:verb
