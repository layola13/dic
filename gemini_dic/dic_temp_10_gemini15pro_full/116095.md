martyrizes:/ˈmɑːrtəraɪzɪz/
verb
使...殉难，使...受折磨，夸大...的痛苦
causes someone to suffer or die for their beliefs; exaggerates one's own suffering
He martyrizes himself over every little setback. 他把每一次小小的挫折都夸大成受难。
The dictator martyrized many political prisoners. 独裁者使许多政治犯殉难。
近义词: torments, persecutes, tortures
反义词: comforts, consoles, protects
词性: verb
