burlesquers:/bərˈlɛskərz/
n.
滑稽模仿演员；讽刺作家(复数)
Performers or writers of burlesque.
The burlesquers poked fun at the pompous politicians with their witty skits. 这些滑稽模仿演员用他们机智的小品嘲讽了那些自负的政客。
Synonyms: satirists, parodists, mimics, impersonators
Antonyms: serious artists, dramatists, tragedians
noun

Etymology:
From French *burlesqueur*, from Italian *burlesco* "comical, ludicrous," from *burla* "joke, mockery." Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bil-*, meaning "to deceive, mock."
