braking: /ˈbreɪkɪŋ/ | n. | 制动，刹车 | the process of slowing down or stopping a moving vehicle, typically by applying pressure to the brakes | The car's braking system failed, causing a serious accident. 汽车的制动系统失灵，导致了严重的事故。 | synonyms: deceleration, stopping, halting | antonyms: acceleration, speeding up | noun

braking: /ˈbreɪkɪŋ/ | adj. | 制动的，刹车的 | relating to the act of braking |  The braking distance of a heavy truck is much longer than that of a car. 重型卡车的制动距离比汽车长得多。| synonyms: decelerating, stopping, halting | antonyms: accelerating, speeding up | adjective
