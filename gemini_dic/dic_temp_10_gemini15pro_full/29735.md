Catmull-Rom:/ˈkætmʌl ˈrɒm/ | adj. | 卡特穆尔-罗姆（算法） | A type of spline interpolation used in computer graphics and other fields to create smooth curves that pass through a set of given data points. It is named after Edwin Catmull and Raphael Rom. |  The Catmull-Rom spline provided a smoother transition between keyframes in the animation. 卡特穆尔-罗姆样条曲线在动画的关键帧之间提供了更平滑的过渡。

The Catmull-Rom algorithm is commonly used in image resizing.  卡特穆尔-罗姆算法常用于图像大小调整。 | 近义词: spline, interpolation | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective
