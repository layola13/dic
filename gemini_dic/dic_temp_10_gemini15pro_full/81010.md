gradiometer:/ˌɡreɪdiˈɒmɪtər/ (US) /ˌɡreɪdiˈɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |梯度仪，梯度计|An instrument used to measure the gradient of a physical quantity, such as the earth's magnetic field or gravity.|A gradiometer is used to detect subtle changes in the Earth's magnetic field.  梯度仪用于探测地球磁场的细微变化。|synonyms: gradient sensor, magnetometer (for magnetic fields), gravimeter (for gravity)|antonyms:  N/A (no direct antonym)|noun

gradiometry:/ˌɡreɪdiˈɒmɪtri/ (US) /ˌɡreɪdiˈɒmɪtri/ (UK)| n. |梯度测量法|The technique or process of measuring gradients using a gradiometer.|Gradiometry is used in archaeological surveys. 梯度测量法用于考古调查。|synonyms: gradient measurement|antonyms: N/A|noun
