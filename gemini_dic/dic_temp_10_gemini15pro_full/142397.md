orthotropous:/ɔːˈθɒtrəpəs/| adj.| 直生的，正生的| (of a plant ovule) Having the micropyle at the apex and the chalaza at the base, in a straight line with the hilum.|  The orthotropous ovule is the most primitive type of ovule. 直生胚珠是最原始的胚珠类型。| The lily has an orthotropous ovule. 百合有一个直生胚珠。 |synonyms: atropous, straight| antonyms: anatropous, campylotropous| adjective
