bewitch:/bɪˈwɪtʃ/| vt.|使着迷，使迷惑，施魔法于|To cast a spell on; to enchant and fascinate.|Her beauty bewitched all who saw her. 她的美使所有见到她的人都为之倾倒。|Synonyms: enchant, captivate, fascinate, charm|Antonyms: repel, disgust, disenchant|Verb

bewitch:/bɪˈwɪtʃ/| adj.|迷人的，令人着迷的 (less common usage)|Enchanted, fascinating. |She had a bewitching smile. 她有着迷人的微笑。|Synonyms: enchanting, captivating, fascinating, charming|Antonyms: repulsive, disgusting, disenchanting|Adjective
