multifidus:/ˌmʌltɪˈfaɪdəs/| n. |多裂肌|A series of small muscles located deep in the spine that span two to four vertebral segments and contribute to spinal stability and posture.|The multifidus muscles play a crucial role in stabilizing the spine. 多裂肌在稳定脊柱方面起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms:rotatores, interspinales, intertransversarii|Antonyms:N/A (Muscles don't typically have antonyms)|noun

multifidus:/ˌmʌltɪˈfaɪdəs/| adj. |多裂的|Having many clefts or divisions.|The multifidus muscle is so named because of its multifid structure. 多裂肌因其多裂的结构而得名。|Synonyms:multipartite, multilobed, divided|Antonyms:undivided, whole, entire|adjective
