measurably:/ˈmeʒərəbli/| adv.|可测量地，显著地|In a way that can be measured or noticed.  It signifies a degree of change that is noticeable or quantifiable.|例句：The air quality has measurably improved since the factory closed. 自从工厂关闭以来，空气质量有了显著改善。

例句：Her health has measurably declined in the past few months. 过去几个月里，她的健康状况明显恶化了。|近义词：noticeably, significantly, perceptibly, appreciably|反义词：imperceptibly, slightly, insignificantly|词性:副词
