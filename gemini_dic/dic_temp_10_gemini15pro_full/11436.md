argons:/ˈɑːrɡɒnz/ | n. | 氩气 | A colorless, odorless, inert gaseous element constituting approximately one percent of the earth's atmosphere, the most abundant of the inert gases.  | Argon is used in welding to protect the weld from oxidation. 氩气用于焊接，以保护焊缝不被氧化。 | synonyms: inert gas, noble gas | antonyms: reactive gas | noun
