palpiform:/ˈpælpɪfɔːrm/
adjective
触须状的，似触须的
Resembling a palp or palpus; palpate.  Having the form of a palp; like a feeler.
The palpiform antennae of the insect helped it navigate.  这种昆虫的触须状触角帮助它导航。
The creature had palpiform appendages near its mouth.  这个生物的嘴附近有触须状的附属物。
近义词:tentacular, antenna-like, feeler-like
