mahjong:/mɑːˈdʒɒŋ/ (UK) /mɑːˈʒɑːŋ/ (US)| n.|麻将|A Chinese game played by four people with tiles resembling dominoes, the object being to collect sets of matching tiles.|We played mahjong all afternoon. 我们下午打了一下午麻将。|Synonyms: mah-jongg, mahjongg|Antonyms: None|Noun
