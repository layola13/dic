overbeats:/ˌoʊvərˈbiːts/| n.|过强拍；重拍；（音乐）强拍过强|A rhythmic emphasis stronger than expected or desired, often occurring on a normally weak beat.|The drummer's overbeats disrupted the flow of the music. 鼓手的过强拍打乱了音乐的流畅性。|synonyms: accents, stresses|antonyms: weak beats|noun

overbeat:/ˌoʊvərˈbiːt/| v.|（心脏）过强搏动；（音乐）强拍过强；胜过；击败|To beat with excessive force; to pulsate too strongly (heart); to emphasize a beat too strongly (music); to defeat, surpass.|The conductor instructed the orchestra to avoid overbeating the downbeat.  指挥指示乐队避免强拍过强。 He overbeat his opponent in the final round. 他在最后一轮击败了对手。|synonyms:  outdo, overcome, conquer, surpass, thump (music)|antonyms: lose to, be defeated by|verb


Etymology: "over" (Old English "ofer") + "beat" (Old English "bēatan")
