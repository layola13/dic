deuterogamy:/ˌdjuːtərˈɒɡəmi/
n.
二次婚；再婚；重婚
A second marriage, specifically after the death of the first spouse or after divorce.
Deuterogamy is more common now than it was a century ago.  二次婚现在比一个世纪以前更普遍了。
