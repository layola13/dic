legitimation:/ləˌdʒɪtɪˈmeɪʃən/
n.|合法化，授予合法性|The legitimation of power through democratic elections. 通过民主选举使权力合法化。
|例句：The government sought legitimation for its actions through a public inquiry. 政府试图通过公开调查使其行动合法化。

|例句：The company's success provided legitimation for its unconventional business practices.  公司的成功使其非传统的商业行为合法化。
|近义词：validation, justification, authorization, sanction|反义词：delegitimization, invalidation|词性：名词


legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/
adj.|合法的，正当的，合理的|A legitimate business expense. 一笔合法的商业支出.
|例句：He had a legitimate reason for being late. 他迟到是有正当理由的。

|例句：The government is a legitimate representative of the people. 政府是人民的合法代表。
|近义词：lawful, legal, valid, justifiable|反义词：illegitimate, unlawful, invalid|词性：形容词


legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmeɪt/
v.|使合法化，宣布…为合法|The new law legitimized the workers' claims. 新法律使工人的诉求合法化。
|例句：The government has legitimized the use of force in this case. 政府已经批准在此案中使用武力。

|例句：  Many try to legitimize their questionable actions by claiming they were following orders. 许多人试图通过声称自己是在服从命令来使他们有问题的行为合法化。
|近义词：validate, justify, authorize, sanction|反义词：delegitimize, invalidate|词性：动词
