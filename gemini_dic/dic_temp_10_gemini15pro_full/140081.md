of-that-ilk:/əv ðæt ɪlk/ | adj. | 同一家族的；同类的；同名的 | Of the same name or place; of the same kind. Originally Scottish, referring to a person designated by the name of their estate, followed by "of that ilk". Now more generally used to mean "of that type" or "of the same sort".  |  MacIntosh of that ilk means MacIntosh of the estate of MacIntosh. 麦金托什家族的麦金托什 | synonyms: of the same kind, of the same sort, of the same type, of that type | antonyms: different, dissimilar, unlike, various | adjective
