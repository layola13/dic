overtask: /ˌəʊvərˈtɑːsk/ /ˌoʊvərˈtæsk/ | vt. |  使工作过度；使任务过重 | To give someone too many tasks or too much work. |  The project manager overtasked the team, leading to burnout. 项目经理给团队布置了太多的任务，导致他们精疲力竭。|  The company overtasks its employees, expecting them to work long hours.  这家公司给员工布置过多的任务，期望他们长时间工作。 | overburden, overload | undertask | verb

overtask: /ˌəʊvərˈtɑːsk/ /ˌoʊvərˈtæsk/ | n. | 过度的工作；过重的任务 (较少使用) | The state of being given too many tasks or too much work. | The constant overtask led to a decline in productivity.  持续的过度工作导致生产力下降。 |  He complained about the overtask, saying it was impossible to complete everything. 他抱怨工作过重，说不可能完成所有的事情。 | overburden, overload |  lack of work | noun
