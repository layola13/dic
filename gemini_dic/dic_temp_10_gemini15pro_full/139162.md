obiism:/ˈoʊbiɪzəm/| n. |对腰带的过度喜爱或痴迷; 腰带崇拜|An excessive fondness or obsession with obis (sashes worn with traditional Japanese clothing).|She had a serious case of obiism, her collection boasting over 200 antique obis.|近义词: obsession, fascination|反义词: indifference, apathy|词性:noun

Etymology: From "obi" (Japanese: 帯, sash) + "-ism" (suffix denoting a doctrine, belief, practice, or characteristic).
