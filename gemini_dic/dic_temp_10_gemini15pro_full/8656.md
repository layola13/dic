anticonservative:/ˌæntiˌkənˈsɜːrvətɪv/
adjective
反保守的，非保守的
Opposed to or rejecting traditional values, beliefs, or practices, especially in politics or religion.
The student protest was marked by anticonservative slogans and demands for change.  学生抗议的特点是反保守的口号和变革的要求。
His anticonservative views often put him at odds with his family. 他的反保守观点经常使他与家人不和。
近义词:liberal, progressive, radical, reformist, revolutionary
反义词:conservative, traditional, orthodox, reactionary
词性:adjective
