overslavish:/ˌōvərˈslāvɪʃ/
|adj.| 过分奴性的，过分顺从的|His overslavish devotion to his boss was embarrassing to watch. 他对他老板过分奴性的奉承令人尴尬。|近义词: servile, subservient, obsequious|反义词: independent, assertive|词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (excessive) + "slavish" (like a slave)  来自 "slave"（奴隶）一词，加上前缀 "over-" 表示过度或过分。
