areopagitic:/ˌæriːəˈpædʒɪtɪk/| adj.|阿瑞俄巴古的，像阿瑞俄巴古的，严肃的，说教的|Pertaining to the Areopagus, the highest judicial court of ancient Athens, known for its gravity and authority; hence, grave, dignified, judicial, or severely critical.|His areopagitic pronouncements on the state of modern art were met with a mixture of awe and resentment. 他对现代艺术状况的阿瑞俄巴古式宣言，引来人们敬畏和怨恨交织的反应。|Synonyms: grave, solemn, serious, judicial, authoritative, dignified, magisterial|Antonyms: flippant, frivolous, lighthearted, informal, casual|adjective
