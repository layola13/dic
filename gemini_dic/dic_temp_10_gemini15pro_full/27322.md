calyptriform: /ˌkælɪpˈtrɪfɔːrm/
adjective
帽状的；僧帽形的
Shaped like a calyptra, the hood-like covering of the spore case of a moss or related plant, or like an extinguisher cap.
The fungus had a distinctive calyptriform cap.  这种真菌有一个独特的帽状菌盖。
Near Synonyms:  cap-shaped, hood-shaped, mitriform
Antonyms:  uncapped, uncovered
Word Origin: From Latin *calyptra (from Greek kaluptra ‘veil, hood’) + -form.
