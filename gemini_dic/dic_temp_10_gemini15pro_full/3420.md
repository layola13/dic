afraid: /əˈfreɪd/ (UK) /əˈfreɪd/ (US)| adj.|害怕的，担心的|Feeling fear or anxiety; worried.|例句：I'm afraid of spiders. 我很怕蜘蛛。|例句：She was afraid to go out alone at night. 她晚上不敢一个人出去。|近义词：scared, frightened, fearful|反义词：brave, courageous|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *afraid, afrayed, past participle of affrayen (“to frighten, terrify”), from Anglo-Norman afrayer, Old French esfreer (“to frighten”), from Late Latin exfridare (“to break the peace”), from Latin ex (“out of, from”) + Latin frīdus (“peace”).
