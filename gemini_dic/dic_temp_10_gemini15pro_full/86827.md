hematoma:/ˌhiːməˈtoʊmə/ (US), /ˌhiːməˈtəʊmə/ (UK)| n. |血肿|A localized swelling filled with blood, caused by a break in a blood vessel.|She developed a hematoma after bumping her knee. 她撞到膝盖后形成了一个血肿。|Example: The doctor drained the hematoma on his arm. 医生排出他手臂上的血肿。|Synonyms: bruise, contusion|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a physical condition)|noun
