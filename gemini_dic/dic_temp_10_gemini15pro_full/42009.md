costermonger:/ˈkɒstəˌmʌŋɡə(r)/ (UK), /ˈkɑːstərˌmʌŋɡər/ (US)| n.| 叫卖小贩；水果贩|A person who sells fruit and vegetables, especially from a barrow in the street.|例句：The costermonger's barrow was piled high with fresh produce.  水果小贩的手推车堆满了新鲜的农产品。

例句：The costermongers loudly advertised their wares in the marketplace. 小贩们在市场上大声吆喝兜售他们的商品。|近义词：vendor, hawker, street vendor, greengrocer (for those selling fruits and vegetables)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "supermarket" or "grocery store" in a modern context) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "costard" (a type of apple) + "monger" (dealer or trader).  The word originally referred specifically to apple sellers, but later expanded to encompass sellers of other fruits and vegetables.
