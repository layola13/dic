desalination:/diːˌseɪlɪˈneɪʃn/ |n.|脱盐，淡化|The process of removing salt from seawater.  The desalination plant provides fresh water for the island. |例句：Desalination is an important process for providing fresh water in arid regions.  脱盐是干旱地区提供淡水的重要的过程。

Desalination plants are becoming increasingly common in water-scarce countries.  海水淡化厂在缺水国家越来越普遍。|近义词：desalting|反义词：salination|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "removal or reversal") + saline (salty) + -ation (suffix forming nouns of action or process). Ultimately from Latin sal (salt).
