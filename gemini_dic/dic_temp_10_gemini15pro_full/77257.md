gastrophrenic:/ˌɡæstrəˈfrɛnɪk/
adjective
胃膈的
Relating to the stomach and the diaphragm.
The gastrophrenic ligament connects the stomach to the diaphragm.  胃膈韧带连接胃和横膈膜。
The patient complained of gastrophrenic pain. 病人抱怨胃膈疼痛。
Synonyms: gastrodiaphragmatic
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one might consider terms relating to other specific organ connections,  e.g.,  cardiopulmonary - 心肺的)
Etymology:  gastro- (Greek: stomach) + phrenic (Greek: relating to the diaphragm)
