de-seeded:/diːˈsiːdɪd/
adjective
去籽的
Having had the seeds removed.
The de-seeded grapes were perfect for the fruit salad.  去籽的葡萄非常适合做水果沙拉。
近义词:seedless, pitted
反义词:seeded
词性:adjective
