circumdenudation: /ˌsɜːrkəmˌdɪnjuːˈdeɪʃən/ | n. | 剥蚀，环状剥蚀作用 | The process of wearing away of the land surface by weathering, erosion, and transportation, resulting in the exposure of underlying rocks, often in a circular or ring-like pattern. | The circumdenudation of the mountain revealed ancient rock formations. 这座山的剥蚀作用暴露出古老的岩层。 | Synonyms: denudation, erosion, weathering | Antonyms: deposition, accretion | noun
