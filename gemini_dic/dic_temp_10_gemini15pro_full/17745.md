bedevilled:/bɪˈdevəld/| adj.|被困扰的，折磨的|Tormented, plagued, or harassed by persistent problems or difficulties.  Derived from "devil," implying a persistent and troublesome affliction.|The project was bedevilled by technical problems from the start.  这个项目从一开始就受到技术问题的困扰。|Synonyms: plagued, troubled, beset, cursed, haunted, jinxed|Antonyms: blessed, fortunate, lucky|adjective
