estranged:/ɪˈstreɪndʒd/ | adj. | 疏远的，分居的，不和的 | No longer close or affectionate to someone; alienated. |  例句：He became estranged from his family after the argument.  他与家人在争吵后疏远了。

例句：She felt estranged from her former friends after moving to a new city.  搬到新城市后，她感到与以前的朋友疏远了。

近义词：alienated, separated, distant, unfriend|反义词：close, intimate, reconciled, friendly|词性:形容词
