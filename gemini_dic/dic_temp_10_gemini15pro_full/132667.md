nondisparate:/ˌnɒndɪˈspærət/ | adj. | 无差别的，平等的 | Not disparate; not markedly different. |  Example: The company strives to create a nondisparate work environment for all employees. 公司努力为所有员工创造一个平等的工作环境。| Example:  The judge ruled that the evidence presented was nondisparate and relevant to the case. 法官裁定，所提供的证据没有差异，且与案件相关。 | Synonym: equal, equivalent, uniform, similar, alike | Antonym: disparate, unequal, different, dissimilar | 词性: 形容词
