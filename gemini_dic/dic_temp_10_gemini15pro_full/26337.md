buzzed:/bʌzd/
| adj.|兴奋的，激动的；微醉的；嗡嗡作响的|Excited, thrilled; slightly drunk; making a buzzing sound|例句：The crowd was buzzed after the concert.  演唱会后人群很兴奋。|近义词：excited, thrilled, tipsy, stimulated|反义词：bored, depressed, sober|词性:形容词

buzzed:/bʌzd/
| v.|（使）发出嗡嗡声；(bee)飞来飞去；匆忙走动；打电话|To make a buzzing sound; (of bees) to fly around; to move quickly and busily; to telephone someone|例句：The bees buzzed around the flowers. 蜜蜂在花丛中嗡嗡作响。|近义词：hum, drone, whirr, rush, call|反义词：be silent, be still|词性:动词
