chorale: /kəˈræl/ (US), /kɔːˈrɑːl/ (UK)| n.|赞美诗，合唱曲|A hymn or psalm tune, specifically a simple sacred tune for congregational singing, often in a slow, stately style and in harmony.  A musical composition (or performance of it) for such singing.|The choir sang a beautiful chorale at the Easter service.  教堂唱诗班在复活节仪式上唱了一首优美的赞美诗。|Synonyms: hymn, psalm, anthem|Antonyms: none|noun

chorale: /kəˈræl/ (US), /kɔːˈrɑːl/ (UK)| adj.|合唱的|Relating to or performed by a choir.|The chorale music filled the church with a sense of reverence.  合唱音乐让教堂充满了一种崇敬感。|Synonyms: choral|Antonyms: none|adjective
