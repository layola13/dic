一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He goes to work by bus. 他乘公共汽车去上班。
She goes to the library every weekend. 她每个周末都去图书馆。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He is going to the store now. 他现在正要去商店。
She is going home after work. 她下班后要回家。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has gone to London twice. 他去过伦敦两次。
The price of gas has gone up recently. 最近汽油价格上涨了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
She has been going to yoga classes for a year. 她上瑜伽课已经一年了。
They have been going out together for a few months. 他们已经交往几个月了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He went to the park yesterday. 他昨天去了公园。
She went to a concert last night. 她昨晚去听音乐会了。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was going to the store when I saw her. 我正要去商店的时候看到了她。
He was going home when it started to rain. 他正要回家的时候开始下雨了。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had gone to the bank before he came to see me. 他来见我之前去过银行了。
She had gone to bed before the movie started. 电影开始前她已经上床睡觉了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
They had been going to that restaurant for years before it closed. 在那家餐馆关门之前，他们已经去那里吃饭很多年了。
She had been going to the gym every day for months before she got injured.  受伤前她已经每天去健身房好几个月了。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
He will go to the party tomorrow. 他明天会去参加聚会。
She will go on vacation next week. 她下周要去度假。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
This time next week, I will be going to the beach. 下周的这个时候，我将要去海滩。
He will be going to school next year. 他明年将要去上学。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, he will have gone to Europe three times. 到明年，他将去过欧洲三次。
She will have gone home by the time you arrive. 你到达的时候她已经回家了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next month, she will have been going to therapy for a year. 到下个月，她将已经接受治疗一年了。
By the time he retires, he will have been going to the same office for 40 years. 到他退休的时候，他将已经在同一个办公室工作40年了。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would go to the doctor. 如果我是你，我会去看医生。
I wish I could go to the party, but I'm sick. 我希望我能去参加聚会，但我病了。

被动语态 (一般来说，go 没有被动语态，但某些情况下可以用 "be gone" 表示消失或离开的状态):
The milk is gone. 牛奶没了。
The opportunity is gone. 机会已经错过了。

疑问句:
Where does he go every day? 他每天都去哪里？
When did she go to the store? 她什么时候去商店的？

否定句:
He doesn't go to school on weekends. 他周末不去上学。
She didn't go to the party last night. 她昨晚没去参加聚会。
