ageless:/ˈeɪdʒləs/| adj.|永恒的，不老的|Not appearing to grow old; eternal.|Her ageless beauty captivated everyone. 她永恒的美丽吸引了每一个人。|近义词：eternal, timeless, youthful, immortal|反义词：aging, old, elderly|词性:形容词
Etymology: From age + -less.  The suffix "-less" means "without" or "lacking". Therefore, "ageless" literally means "without age".
