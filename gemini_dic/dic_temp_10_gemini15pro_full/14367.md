autopoietic:/ˌɔːtəʊpɔɪˈɛtɪk/ | adj. | 自创生的，自组织的 |  Self-producing and self-maintaining, used especially of biological systems.  Relating to or denoting systems that are capable of producing and maintaining themselves by creating their own parts and components. |  Living organisms are autopoietic systems. 生物是有机体是自创生的系统。|  The theory describes social systems as autopoietic.  该理论将社会系统描述为自创生的。| 近义词: self-organizing, self-producing, self-maintaining | 反义词: allopoietic | 词性: adjective

autopoiesis:/ˌɔːtəʊpɔɪˈiːsɪs/ | n. | 自创生，自组织 | The property of a system that allows it to maintain and renew itself. The process whereby a system produces and maintains itself by creating its own parts and components.  | Autopoiesis is a key concept in systems theory. 自创生是系统理论中的一个关键概念。|  The concept of autopoiesis has been applied to various fields, including biology and sociology. 自创生的概念已被应用于各个领域，包括生物学和社会学。 | 近义词: self-organization, self-production | 反义词: allopoiesis | 词性: noun
