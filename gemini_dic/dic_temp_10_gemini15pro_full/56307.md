doggy-paddled:/ˈdɒɡi pædəld/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡi pædəld/ (US)| v. |狗刨式游泳|To swim using a style resembling a dog's paddling motion, characterized by short, quick movements of the arms and legs.|He doggy-paddled to the edge of the pool. 他狗刨式游到了泳池边。|Synonyms: dog-paddle, paddle, |Antonyms: swim (with a proper stroke), dive|verb

doggy-paddle:/ˈdɒɡi pædəl/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡi pædəl/ (US)| n. |狗刨式|A swimming stroke resembling a dog's paddling motion.|The doggy-paddle is not the most efficient way to swim, but it'll keep you afloat. 狗刨式不是最有效的游泳方式，但它能让你浮起来。|Synonyms: dog paddle|Antonyms: crawl, backstroke, butterfly, breaststroke|noun
