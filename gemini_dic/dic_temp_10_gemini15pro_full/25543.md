bully-boy:/ˈbʊli bɔɪ/| n.|恃强凌弱者，恶霸|A person who uses their strength or power to frighten or hurt weaker people. |例句：He was known as the school bully-boy. 他是学校里有名的恶霸。

例句：The bully-boys were intimidating the younger students.  这些恶霸恐吓着年纪小的学生们。|近义词：bully, thug, intimidator, ruffian|反义词：protector, defender|词性:名词
