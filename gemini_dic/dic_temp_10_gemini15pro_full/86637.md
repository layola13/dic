hella:/ˈhɛlə/| adv.|很，非常，极其|Used to emphasize the degree of something, similar to "very" or "really".  Originates from Northern California slang, possibly a shortened form of "hell of a".|例句：That concert was hella fun. 那场音乐会非常有趣。|例句：There were hella people at the party. 派对上有很多人。|近义词：very, really, extremely, totally|反义词:slightly, a little|词性:副词
