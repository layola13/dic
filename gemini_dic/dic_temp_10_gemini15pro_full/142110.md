orienters:/ˈɔːriˌɛntərz/
n.
定向者，指引者
People who help others find their way or adjust to a new situation.
The camp counselors acted as orienters for the new arrivals, showing them around and explaining the rules.  营地辅导员充当新来者的向导，带领他们四处参观并解释规则。

The orienters helped the freshmen navigate the complexities of college life.  指导员帮助新生们适应复杂的大学生活。

近义词：guides, mentors, advisors
反义词：disorienters, confounders
词性：名词
