fickle:/ˈfɪk(ə)l/
adjective
善变的，反复无常的
Liable to change one's mind or opinions suddenly and frequently; capricious.
She's so fickle - she's changed her mind about the wedding dress three times already. 她太善变了——她已经改变了三次关于婚纱的主意了。
He was fickle in his affections, falling in and out of love easily.  他的感情善变，很容易坠入爱河，也很容易变心。

synonyms:capricious, changeable, erratic, flighty, inconstant, mercurial, temperamental, variable, volatile, whimsical
antonyms:constant, dependable, faithful, loyal, reliable, resolute, stable, steadfast, steady, unwavering
词性:形容词
