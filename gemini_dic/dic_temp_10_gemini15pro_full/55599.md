disunity:/dɪsˈjuːnɪti/
n.|不团结，分裂|lack of unity or agreement; discord.|The disunity within the party led to its defeat in the election. 党内不和导致了他们在选举中的失败。|近义词：discord, division, disharmony, friction, strife|反义词：unity, harmony, concord, agreement, solidarity|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + unity.  The word "unity" comes from the Late Latin unitas, meaning "oneness," ultimately derived from the Latin unus, meaning "one."
