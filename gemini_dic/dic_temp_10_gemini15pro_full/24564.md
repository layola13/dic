brobdingnagian:/ˌbrɑːbdɪŋˈnæɡiən/
adjective
巨大的，庞大的
Of extraordinary size; gigantic.  Relating to the land of giants in Jonathan Swift's Gulliver's Travels.
Example: The company unveiled its brobdingnagian new headquarters, a skyscraper that dwarfed all other buildings in the city. 公司公布了其巨大的新总部，一座让城市中所有其他建筑都相形见绌的摩天大楼。

Example:  His appetite was truly brobdingnagian; he ate enough for three people. 他的胃口真是惊人；他吃得够三个人吃的。

近义词: gigantic, enormous, colossal, immense, huge, massive
反义词: tiny, minuscule, small, diminutive

词性: adjective
