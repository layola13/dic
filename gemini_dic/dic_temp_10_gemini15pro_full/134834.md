nonmissionaries:/ˌnɒnˈmɪʃənɛriz/ /ˌnɑːnˈmɪʃənɛriz/
noun
非传教士;非宣教者
People who are not missionaries; individuals who do not actively engage in missionary work, which involves spreading religious beliefs or performing charitable services in foreign lands.
The majority of the passengers on the ship were nonmissionaries, traveling for business or leisure. 船上大多数乘客都不是传教士，而是为了商务或休闲旅行。
Most of the residents in the village are nonmissionaries, focusing on farming and local trade. 村里的大多数居民都不是传教士，他们专注于农业和当地贸易。
Synonyms: civilians, laypeople, secular individuals
Antonyms: missionaries, evangelists, preachers
