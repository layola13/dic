denigrations:/ˌdɛnɪˈɡreɪʃənz/| n.|诽谤，诋毁，贬低|The politician's speech was filled with denigrations of his opponent. 这位政客的演讲充满了对对手的诋毁。|Synonyms: defamation, slander, disparagement, vilification|Antonyms: praise, commendation, acclaim, applause|noun

Etymology->
From Late Latin *denigrāre*, from Latin *de-* (down, away) + *nigrāre* (to blacken), from *niger* (black).
