overnoise:/ˌoʊvərˈnɔɪz/| v.|盖过噪音，用更大的噪音盖过|To drown out or overwhelm with a louder noise.|The loud music overnoised our conversation. 喧闹的音乐盖过了我们的谈话。|近义词：drown out, overwhelm, muffle|反义词: amplify, enhance|词性:动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "above, excessive") + "noise" (n.)
