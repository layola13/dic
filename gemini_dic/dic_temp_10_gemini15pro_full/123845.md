moralistically:/ˌmɔːrəˈlɪstɪkli/
adv.|道德主义地，道貌岸然地|In a way that emphasizes moral principles, often in a self-righteous or preachy manner.  Judging or behaving in a way that suggests one has superior moral standards.|He spoke moralistically about the dangers of gambling. 他道貌岸然地谈论赌博的危害。|Synonyms: self-righteously, preachily, sanctimoniously|Antonyms: pragmatically, realistically|词性:副词
