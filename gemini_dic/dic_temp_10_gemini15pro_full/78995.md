gisle:/ˈɡɪslə/ | n. | 冰柱, 冰锥 | A pendent spike of ice formed by the freezing of dripping water. |  Example: The gisle hung from the roof, glistening in the sunlight. 屋檐上挂着冰柱，在阳光下闪闪发光。

Example:  The climbers had to be careful of falling gisle. 登山者必须小心掉落的冰锥。 | 近义词: icicle | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, possibly "thaw" as a verb related to the disappearance of gisle) | 词性: noun
