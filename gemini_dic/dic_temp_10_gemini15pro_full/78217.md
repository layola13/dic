geraniaceous:/ˌdʒɛrəˈneɪʃəs/
adjective
牻牛儿苗科的;天竺葵似的
Belonging to or resembling the Geraniaceae family of plants, which includes geraniums and pelargoniums.
The geraniaceous scent filled the greenhouse. 温室里充满了天竺葵的香味。
The plant has geraniaceous leaves.  这种植物有天竺葵似的叶子。
synonyms: geranium-like
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but could use terms like "uncharacteristic of geraniums")
word type: adjective
