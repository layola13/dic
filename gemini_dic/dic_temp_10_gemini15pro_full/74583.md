frazzled:/ˈfræzəld/| adj.|疲惫不堪的，精疲力竭的|Exhausted and stressed, especially after a period of sustained activity or pressure.|例句：After a week of working overtime, I felt completely frazzled.  加班一周后，我感到筋疲力尽。

例句：The frazzled mother finally managed to get her children to bed.  疲惫不堪的母亲终于设法让她的孩子们上床睡觉了。|近义词：exhausted, worn out, stressed, burnt out, drained|反义词：relaxed, refreshed, energized|词性:形容词
