deceleration:/diːˌsɛləˈreɪʃən/
noun
减速，减速运动
a reduction in speed or rate.
The pilot initiated deceleration as the plane approached the runway. 飞行员在飞机接近跑道时开始减速。
The deceleration of the economy is a major concern. 经济减速是一个主要问题。
synonyms: slowing down, retardation, decrease in speed
antonyms: acceleration, speeding up
