dischurch:/dɪsˈtʃɜːrtʃ/
verb
逐出教会,剥夺教籍
To expel from church membership; deprive of ecclesiastical authority.
He was dischurched for his heretical views. 他因为异端观点被逐出教会。
近义词:excommunicate, defrock
反义词:ordain, consecrate
词性:v.
