oxes:/ˈɒksɪz/| n. |公牛（ox 的复数形式）|The plural form of "ox," referring to more than one ox.  Oxen are large, strong cattle, especially castrated males, used for heavy work such as plowing.|The farmer used his oxen to plow the field. 农民用他的牛耕地。|近义词：cattle, bullocks|反义词:cow (母牛), calf (小牛)|名词
