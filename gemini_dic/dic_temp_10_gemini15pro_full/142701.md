othernesses:/ˈʌðərnɪsɪz/
n.
差异性，不同之处，另类
The book explores the othernesses of various cultures. 这本书探讨了不同文化的差异性。
例句：The exhibition celebrates the othernesses of human experience.  这个展览颂扬了人类经验的多样性。

例句：She embraced the othernesses of her new community. 她欣然接受了她新社区的种种不同之处。

近义词：differences, distinctions, diversities, variations, peculiarities
反义词：similarities, sameness, uniformity, homogeneity
词性: 名词(plural noun)

词源：From "otherness" + "-es" (plural suffix). "Otherness" is derived from "other" + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  "Other" ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *antharaz, meaning "second," "different."
