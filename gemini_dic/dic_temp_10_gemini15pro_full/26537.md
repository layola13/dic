cabezon:/ˈkæbəˌzɑːn/ | n. | 大头鱼；大头人 | A fish of the family Scorpaenidae, found in the Pacific Ocean, having a large head and spiny fins.  Also, informally, a person with a large head. |  例句：The cabezon is a popular game fish along the California coast.  大头鱼是加州海岸的一种受欢迎的游钓鱼。

例句：He was nicknamed "Cabezon" because of his unusually large head.  他因为头特别大而被起了个绰号叫“大头”。 | 近义词：sculpin (for the fish) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


cabezon:/ˌkɑːbeˈsɔn/ | adj. | 大头的 | Having a large head. (Primarily used in Spanish, less common in English) | 例句：El niño cabezón miraba con curiosidad.  那个大头的男孩好奇地看着。

例句：Some breeds of dogs are known for their cabezón appearance.  有些品种的狗以其大头的模样而闻名。| 近义词：large-headed | 反义词: small-headed | 词性: adjective
