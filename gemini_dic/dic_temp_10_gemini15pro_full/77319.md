gatherings:/ˈɡæðərɪŋz/
n.
聚会，集会
assemblies or meetings of people, often for a specific purpose
例句：The family gatherings always bring joy and laughter. 家庭聚会总是充满欢乐和笑声。

例句：There were several gatherings planned for the holiday weekend. 假期周末计划了好几次聚会。

近义词：assemblies, meetings, congregations, get-togethers
反义词：dispersals, separations
词性：名词
