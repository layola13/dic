particularizing:/pərˈtɪkjələrˌaɪzɪŋ/
adjective|使具体的，详细说明的|The act of specifying or making more specific.  Making something more detailed or distinct.|She was particularizing the steps involved in baking the cake. 她正在详细说明烤蛋糕的步骤。|近义词：specifying, detailing, itemizing, elaborating|反义词：generalizing, summarizing|词性: adjective
Etymology: From the verb "particularize," which comes from the adjective "particular" (meaning "pertaining to a single or specific thing") + the suffix "-ize" (meaning "to make or cause to be"). Ultimately from the Latin "particularis" meaning "of a part, partial."
