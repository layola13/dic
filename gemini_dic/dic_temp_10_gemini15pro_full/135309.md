nonpaternally:/ˌnɒnpəˈtɜːrnəli/
adverb
非父系地，非经父系遗传地
Inherited from the mother or another ancestor not in the paternal line.  Derived genetically from the mother or other ancestor not in the male line.
The gene is inherited nonpaternally.  该基因是非父系遗传的。
近义词: maternally
反义词: paternally
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From non- + paternally.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not." Paternally means "from the father's side of the family."
