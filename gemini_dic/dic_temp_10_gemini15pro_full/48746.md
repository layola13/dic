definite:/ˈdefɪnət/ (US), /ˈdefɪnɪt/ (UK)| adj.|明确的，肯定的，一定的|Clearly defined or determined; not vague or doubtful; fixed; precise; positive.|例句：I need a definite answer by tomorrow. 我明天需要一个明确的答复。

例句：There's a definite improvement in his work. 他的工作有了明显的进步。

近义词：certain, specific, precise, explicit, unambiguous|反义词：indefinite, vague, uncertain, ambiguous|词性: adjective
