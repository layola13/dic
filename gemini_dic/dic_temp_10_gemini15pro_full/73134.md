forage:/ˈfɔːrɪdʒ/ (UK), /ˈfɔːrɪdʒ/ (US)| n. v.| 觅食，草料 |The act of searching for food or provisions. As a noun, it refers to food, especially for horses and cattle, such as hay or straw. As a verb, it means to search for food or provisions.|
例句：The rabbits forage for food in the garden. 兔子在花园里觅食。

例句：The farmer stored forage for his cattle during the winter. 农民在冬天储存饲料给他的牛。

近义词：search, hunt, rummage, fodder, feed|
反义词: starve, fast |
词性: noun, verb


forages:/ˈfɔːrɪdʒɪz/ (UK), /ˈfɔːrɪdʒɪz/ (US)| v. | 觅食（第三人称单数形式）|The third person singular present tense form of the verb "forage," meaning searches for food or provisions.|
例句：The squirrel forages for nuts in the trees. 松鼠在树上觅食坚果。

例句：He forages for wild mushrooms in the forest. 他在森林里寻找野生蘑菇。


近义词：searches, hunts, rummages |
反义词: starves, fasts|
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *forrage, from forrer "to line, stuff," from Old French feurre "straw," from Late Latin fodrum "fodder."
