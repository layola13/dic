hydrocarbon:/ˈhaɪdrəʊˌkɑːbən/ (UK) /ˈhaɪdroʊˌkɑːrbən/ (US)| n. |碳氢化合物；烃|An organic compound consisting entirely of hydrogen and carbon. |例句：Methane is the simplest hydrocarbon. 甲烷是最简单的碳氢化合物。

例句：Petroleum and natural gas are mixtures of hydrocarbons. 石油和天然气是碳氢化合物的混合物。|近义词：alkane, alkene, alkyne (for specific types)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology->
From hydro- +‎ carbon.  "Hydro-" ultimately from Ancient Greek ὕδωρ (húdōr, “water”), referring to the hydrogen component. "Carbon" from French carbone, ultimately from Latin carbō (“charcoal”).
