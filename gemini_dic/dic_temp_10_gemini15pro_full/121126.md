misdeem:/ˌmɪsˈdiːm/
verb
判断错误；误判；认为…不好
To judge wrongly; to have an erroneous opinion of.
She misdeems his silence as an acceptance. 她误以为他的沉默是接受了。
misjudge, misinterpret
judge correctly, interpret correctly
词性: 动词
