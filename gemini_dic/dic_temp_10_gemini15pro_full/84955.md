hapless:/ˈhæpləs/| adj.|不幸的；倒霉的|Unlucky and unfortunate; deserving or inciting pity.|He was a hapless victim of circumstance.|近义词：unlucky, unfortunate, ill-fated, jinxed|反义词：lucky, fortunate, blessed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From hap (meaning chance, luck, fortune) + -less (suffix indicating lack of).  Ultimately from Old Norse *happ.
