bustler:/ˈbʌslər/| n.| 忙碌的人，奔忙的人；精力充沛的人|A person who is energetic and works hard; a go-getter.|例句：She's a real bustler and gets a lot done. 她是一个真正的忙碌的人，完成了许多工作。

例句：He's a bustler, always on the go. 他是个精力充沛的人，总是在忙个不停。|近义词：workaholic, go-getter, dynamo, hustler|反义词：idler, loafer, slacker|词性:noun

bustle:/ˈbʌsl/| v. | 忙乱；喧闹；奔忙；催促|To move about energetically; to teem with activity.|例句：She bustled about the kitchen, preparing dinner. 她在厨房里忙着准备晚餐。

例句：The market bustled with shoppers. 市场里挤满了购物者。|近义词：hurry, rush, dash, scurry|反义词：dawdle, linger|词性:verb

bustle:/ˈbʌsl/| n.| 喧闹；熙熙攘攘；裙撑|Energetic activity; a commotion; a frame worn under a woman's skirt to puff it out.|例句：The hustle and bustle of city life. 城市生活的喧嚣。

例句：She wore a bustle under her skirt. 她在裙子下穿了一个裙撑。|近义词：activity, commotion, fuss, flurry|反义词：calm, quiet, stillness|词性:noun
