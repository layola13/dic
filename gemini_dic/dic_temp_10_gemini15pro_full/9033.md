antimaterialism:/ˌænti məˈtɪəriəlɪzəm/| n. |反物质主义，非物质主义|The rejection of materialism, emphasizing spiritual or intellectual values over material possessions.  It prioritizes experiences, relationships, and personal growth over acquiring wealth and consumer goods.| He embraced antimaterialism and lived a simple life. 他信奉反物质主义，过着简朴的生活。|  She expressed her antimaterialism by donating most of her income to charity. 她通过将大部分收入捐赠给慈善机构来表达她的反物质主义。|近义词：spiritualism, idealism, asceticism|反义词:materialism, consumerism|词性:noun
