fabricius:/fəˈbrɪʃəs/
n.|法布里丘斯; 法布里奇乌斯 (姓氏)|A Roman surname, notably borne by Hieronymus Fabricius (1537-1617), Italian anatomist and surgeon.|Hieronymus Fabricius was a pioneering anatomist.  希罗尼姆斯·法布里丘斯是一位先驱解剖学家。|Fabricius ab Aquapendente discovered the valves in the veins.  法布里丘斯·阿布·阿夸彭登特发现了静脉瓣膜。|Harvey, Vesalius|None|noun
