naff:/næf/| adj.|俗气的；没品味的；糟糕的|Something that is naff is unfashionable or in bad taste. |That plastic garden gnome is so naff. 那个塑料花园侏儒太俗气了。|Synonyms: tacky, cheesy, tasteless, unfashionable, dowdy|Antonyms: stylish, fashionable, chic, elegant|adjective

naff:/næf/| n.|俗气的东西；没品味的东西|(informal, chiefly British) something considered to be unfashionable or of poor taste|Wearing socks with sandals is considered a bit naff. 穿凉鞋配袜子被认为有点土。|Synonyms: kitsch, tat|Antonyms: style, elegance|noun
