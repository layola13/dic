outbrawling:/ˌaʊtˈbrɔːlɪŋ/
verb
以更强悍的姿态击败; 在争吵或打斗中胜过
defeating someone by being more forceful or aggressive in a quarrel or fight
He outbrawled his opponent and won the championship. 他以更强悍的姿态击败了他的对手，赢得了冠军。
The two gangs outbrawled each other for control of the territory.  两个帮派为了争夺地盘而大打出手。
Synonyms: outfight, overpower, subdue, vanquish
Antonyms: lose, surrender, yield
词性：动词 (verb)
