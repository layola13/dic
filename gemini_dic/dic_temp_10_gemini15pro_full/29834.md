cauma:/ˈkaʊmə/| n.|烙印，烧灼的痕迹，烧焦的木头|A mark made by burning or branding, especially on the skin or wood. It can also refer to the charred remains of burnt wood.|The cauma on the tree was a stark reminder of the lightning strike. 树上的烧焦痕迹清楚地提醒人们闪电的袭击。|synonyms: brand, scar, scorch, burn mark|antonyms: healing, repair|noun
