mortgageable:/ˈmɔːrɡɪdʒəbl/
adjective
可抵押的
Suitable or acceptable for use as security against a mortgage.
Our house is mortgageable. 我们的房子可以抵押。
Synonyms: pledgeable, securable
Antonyms: unmortgageable
