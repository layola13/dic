disgusts:/dɪsˈɡʌsts/
| v.|使作呕；使厌恶；|to cause a strong feeling of dislike or disapproval|The smell of rotten eggs disgusts me.  腐烂鸡蛋的味道让我作呕。|近义词：repels, sickens, nauseates|反义词：pleases, delights|词性:动词

disgust:/dɪsˈɡʌst/
| n.|厌恶；憎恶；|a strong feeling of dislike or disapproval|She felt a deep disgust at his behavior. 她对他的行为感到深恶痛绝。|近义词：revulsion, aversion, loathing|反义词：pleasure, delight|词性:名词
