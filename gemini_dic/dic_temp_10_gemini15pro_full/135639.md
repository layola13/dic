nonprecious:/nɑːnˈprɛʃəs/ | adj. | 非贵重的，不值钱的 | Not precious; of little value. |  The frame is made of nonprecious metal. 这个框架是由非贵重金属制成的。 |  The jewelry used nonprecious stones. 这件首饰使用的是非贵重宝石。 | 近义词: cheap, inexpensive, common, base | 反义词: precious, valuable, expensive, costly | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- + precious.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Precious" comes from the Old French "precios," ultimately from the Latin "pretiosus," meaning "valuable."
