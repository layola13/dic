chime:/tʃaɪm/| n. vi. vt.|钟声，和谐，鸣响；鸣响，和谐，插话|a set of bells, especially in a church tower, or the sound they make when they are rung; to make a clear ringing sound, or to play a tune on a set of bells; to agree with or match something else |The chimes of Big Ben rang out across London. 大本钟的钟声响彻伦敦。|近义词：ring, peal, clang, jingle, harmonize, agree|反义词：clash, disagree|词性:名词, 动词

chime:/tʃaɪm/| n. vi. vt.|和谐，一致；一致，符合|agreement or harmony; to be in agreement or harmony|Her views chimed with mine. 她的观点与我的观点一致。|近义词：accord, harmonize, correspond, agree|反义词：clash, disagree, conflict|词性:名词, 动词
