bloatware:/ˈbloʊtwɛr/| n.|预装软件；膨胀软件|Software included on a new computer or mobile device by the manufacturer that is often considered unnecessary and unwanted, and that slows down the device's performance.  It takes up valuable storage space and system resources.|This new phone is full of bloatware that I can't uninstall. 这款新手机装满了无法卸载的预装软件。|My computer is running slow because of all the bloatware. 我的电脑因为预装软件太多而运行缓慢。|synonyms: crapware, shovelware|antonyms: essential software|noun
