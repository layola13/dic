parapsychologists:/ˌpærəsaɪˈkɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. | 超心理学家 |  People who study parapsychology, a field that investigates psychic phenomena like telepathy, clairvoyance, and precognition. | Parapsychologists are often skeptical of the phenomena they study. 超心理学家通常对他们研究的现象持怀疑态度。|  Researchers conducted a survey among parapsychologists regarding their beliefs about the existence of psychic abilities. 研究人员对超心理学家进行了一项调查，询问他们对通灵能力存在的看法。 | synonyms: psychic researchers, investigators of paranormal | antonyms: skeptics, debunkers | noun
