liaising:/liˈeɪzɪŋ/
|v.|联络；联系；接洽|acting as a link between people or groups|The company is liaising with customers to resolve the issue. 公司正在与客户联系以解决问题。|Synonyms: coordinating, collaborating, communicating, networking|Antonyms: isolating, separating|verb

liaison:/liˈeɪzɒn/ (UK) /liˈeɪzɑːn/ (US)
|n.|联络；联系；接洽|communication or cooperation which facilitates a close working relationship between people or organizations.|She acts as a liaison between the school and parents. 她在学校和家长之间起联络作用。|Synonyms: intermediary, contact, connection|Antonyms: separation, division|noun
