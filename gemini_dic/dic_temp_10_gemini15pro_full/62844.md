enjambement:/ɪnˈdʒæmbmənt/ |n.|跨行连续; 行间转行|The continuation of a sentence without a pause beyond the end of a line, couplet, or stanza.|Her poem made good use of enjambement, creating a sense of flow and urgency.|Near synonyms: run-on line|Antonyms: end-stopped line|Noun
