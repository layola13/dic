exigency:/ˈɛksɪdʒənsi/ /ˈɛɡzɪdʒənsi/| n. |紧急情况；迫切需要；苛求|An exigency is an urgent need or demand.  It describes a situation that requires immediate attention and action.|例句：The exigency of the situation demanded immediate action.  情况的紧急需要立即采取行动。

例句：They were forced to sell their home due to financial exigencies. 由于经济上的紧急情况，他们被迫卖掉了房子。|近义词：emergency, urgency, necessity, demand, requirement|反义词:calm, peace, tranquility|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *exigence, from Late Latin exigentia "urgency," from Latin exigent-, present participle stem of exigere "to demand, require, exact, drive out," from ex- "out" + agere "to drive, lead, act."
