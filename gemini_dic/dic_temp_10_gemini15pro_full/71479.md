flammability:/ˌflæməˈbɪləti/| n. |易燃性，可燃性|The flammability of the material is a key concern for safety regulations.  这种材料的可燃性是安全法规的一个关键问题。|例句：The flammability of dry leaves makes them a fire hazard. 干树叶的易燃性使它们成为火灾隐患。

例句：Regulations regarding the flammability of children's pajamas are very strict.  关于儿童睡衣易燃性的规定非常严格。|近义词：combustibility, inflammability|反义词：non-flammability, fire resistance|词性:noun
词源-> From Late Latin *flammabilitas, from Latin flammabilis (“flammable”) + -itas.  Ultimately from Latin flamma (“flame”).
