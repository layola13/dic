overlush:/ˌoʊvərˈlʌʃ/| adj.|过于茂盛的，过于繁茂的|Excessively lush or luxuriant; too richly vegetated.|The overlush vegetation made it difficult to see the path.  过于茂盛的植被使道路难以辨认。|The garden was a riot of color, perhaps a little overlush for some tastes.  花园里色彩缤纷，对某些人来说可能有点过于繁茂了。|近义词：luxuriant, profuse, rampant|反义词：sparse, barren, scanty|词性:形容词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "lush" (adjective meaning "growing vigorously").  由表示“过度”的前缀 "over-" 和形容词 "lush"（意为“生长旺盛的”）组成。
