logarithm:/ˈlɒɡərɪðəm/ (US) /ˈlɔːɡərɪðəm/ (UK)| n. |对数|A quantity representing the power to which a fixed number (the base) must be raised to produce a given number.|例句：The logarithm of 100 to the base 10 is 2.  100以10为底的对数是2。

例句：We can use logarithms to simplify complex calculations. 我们可以使用对数来简化复杂的计算。|近义词：exponent, power |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
