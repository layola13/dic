non-bolshevik:/nɒn ˌbɒlʃɪˈvɪk/
| adj. n.|非布尔什维克的；非布尔什维克主义者|Someone or something that is not Bolshevik; not a member of or relating to the Bolshevik party.  Referring to individuals, groups, or ideologies that were not aligned with the Bolshevik faction of the Russian Social Democratic Labour Party, which later became the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.|The non-Bolshevik socialists were marginalized after the October Revolution. 十月革命后，非布尔什维克的社会主义者被边缘化了。|The Mensheviks were a prominent non-Bolshevik group.  孟什维克是一个著名的非布尔什维克团体。|Near synonyms:  Menshevik, anti-communist, non-communist|Antonym: Bolshevik|adjective, noun
