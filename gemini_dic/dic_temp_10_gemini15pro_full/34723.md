clicked: /klɪkt/ | v. | 点击；发出咔哒声；突然明白；一见如故 | to press a button on a computer mouse etc. |  I clicked the link to open the website. 我点击链接打开了网站。 | synonyms: pressed, tapped | antonyms: ignored, avoided | verb

clicked: /klɪkt/ | adj. |  合得来的；成功的 |  having suddenly become successful or popular | The new marketing campaign really clicked. 新的营销活动真的成功了。| synonyms: successful, hit | antonyms: failed, unsuccessful | adjective
