outcourted:/ˌaʊtˈkɔːrtɪd/
verb
胜过…的追求；在追求上超过
To surpass someone in courting; to woo more effectively than another.
He outcourted his rivals and won the hand of the princess. 他在追求中胜过对手，赢得了公主的芳心。
outdo, outstrip, surpass
be outcourted
