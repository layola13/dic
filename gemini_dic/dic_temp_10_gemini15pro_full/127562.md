necrosis:/nəˈkroʊsɪs/| n.| 坏死，组织坏死|The tissue damage was so extensive that it resulted in necrosis.|组织损伤非常严重，导致了坏死。|例句：The doctor diagnosed necrosis in the patient's toe. 医生诊断病人的脚趾坏死。

necrosis:/nəˈkroʊsɪs/| n.| 坏死，腐烂|The lack of blood flow caused necrosis of the tissue.| 血液流通不足导致组织坏死。|例句：Necrosis of the plant tissue was observed after the frost. 霜冻后观察到植物组织坏死。|近义词：gangrene, mortification, decay|反义词:growth, regeneration|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek νέκρωσις (nékrōsis, “a killing, making dead”), from νεκρός (nekrós, “dead body, corpse”) + -σις (-sis, noun-forming suffix denoting action, state, or condition).
