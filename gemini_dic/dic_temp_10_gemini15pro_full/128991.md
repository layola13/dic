nippingly:/ˈnɪpɪŋli/
adverb
刺骨地；尖刻地；凛冽地
In a way that is sharp, biting, or stinging, especially of cold or wind.
The wind blew nippingly across the frozen lake. 寒风凛冽地吹过结冰的湖面。
Near synonyms:bitingly, sharply, keenly, piercingly
Opposite:gently, mildly, warmly
词性: 副词
