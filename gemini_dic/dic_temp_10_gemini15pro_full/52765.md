dilating:/daɪˈleɪtɪŋ/
| v. | 扩张；扩大；使膨胀 | making or becoming wider or larger; enlarging |  The doctor dilated her pupils to examine her eyes. 医生扩大了她的瞳孔来检查她的眼睛。 | expand, widen, enlarge | constrict, contract | verb

dilate:/daɪˈleɪt/
| v. | 扩张；扩大；使膨胀 | to make or become wider or larger; to expand | The blood vessels in his face dilated with anger. 他脸上的血管因愤怒而扩张。 | expand, widen, enlarge | constrict, contract | verb

dilated:/daɪˈleɪtɪd/
| adj. | 扩张的；扩大的 | widened or enlarged |  Her pupils were dilated. 她的瞳孔放大了。| expanded, widened, enlarged | constricted, contracted | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dilatare, from Latin dilatare "to spread out, enlarge, extend," from di- "apart, out" + latus "wide, broad."
