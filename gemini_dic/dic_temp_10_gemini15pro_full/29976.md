caviler:/ˈkævɪlər/| n.|吹毛求疵的人；苛责者|A caviler is someone who raises petty objections or criticisms.|He's a caviler who finds fault with everything. 他是一个对所有事情都吹毛求疵的人.|近义词：critic, nitpicker, faultfinder, carper|反义词:supporter, admirer|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *cavileur*, from Old French *caviller* "to mock, quibble, carp," from Latin *cavillārī* "to jest, banter, argue scoffingly," perhaps frequentative of *callāre* "to call, proclaim."
