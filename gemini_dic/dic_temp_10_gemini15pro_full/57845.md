dressing-down:/ˈdrɛsɪŋ daʊn/
noun
斥责，训斥
A severe reprimand or scolding.
例句：He received a dressing-down from his boss for being late. 他因为迟到受到了老板的训斥。

例句：She gave her son a dressing-down for his messy room. 她因为儿子房间凌乱而训斥了他。

近义词：reprimand, scolding, rebuke, lecture
反义词：praise, commendation, compliment


dressing-down:/ˈdrɛsɪŋ daʊn/
noun
申斥; 责骂; 训斥
A severe reprimand or scolding.
英文解析: A dressing-down is a strong scolding or reprimand given to someone for something they have done wrong. It implies a serious and often formal telling-off.
例句：The CEO gave the marketing team a dressing-down after their disastrous product launch. 首席执行官在产品发布失败后严厉斥责了营销团队。

例句：He expected a dressing-down from his parents when he got home late.  他预料到回家晚了会受到父母的责骂。

近义词：rebuke, scolding, reprimand, lecture
反义词：praise, commendation, compliment
