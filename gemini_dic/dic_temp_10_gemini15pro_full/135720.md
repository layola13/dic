nonprimitive:/nɒnˈprɪmɪtɪv/ | adj. | 非原始的，非基本类型的，复合的 | Not primitive; derived or composed of simpler units. Java有很多非原始数据类型，例如String和数组。 | composite, complex, derived | primitive, basic | adjective
