blu-ray:/ˈbluːreɪ/| n.|蓝光光碟|A type of high-definition optical disc format for storing digital data, especially high-definition video.|I bought the movie on Blu-ray for the best picture quality.|近义词:Blu-ray Disc, BD|反义词:DVD, CD|词性:名词

Etymology:  "Blu" refers to the blue-violet laser used to read the disc (as opposed to the red laser used for DVDs). "Ray" refers to the optical ray.
