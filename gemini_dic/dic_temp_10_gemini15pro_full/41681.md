correspondingly:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɒndɪŋli/ /ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndɪŋli/| adv.| 相应地，对应地|In a way that relates to or matches something else.  It indicates a parallel or connection between two things. |As the demand increased, the prices rose correspondingly. 随着需求的增加，价格也相应上涨。| synonym: accordingly, consequently, therefore, thus| antonym:  inversely, contrarily| adverb
