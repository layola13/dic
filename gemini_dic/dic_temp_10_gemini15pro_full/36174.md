coffers:/ˈkɒfəz (British), ˈkɔːfərz (American)| n.|金库，资金，国库|A strongbox or chest, especially one for storing valuables.  Figuratively, the funds or financial resources of a government, organization, or individual.|The company's coffers were depleted after the costly lawsuit. 公司的资金在昂贵的诉讼之后耗尽了。|synonyms: treasury, funds, resources, reserves, wealth|antonyms: debt, liabilities|noun
