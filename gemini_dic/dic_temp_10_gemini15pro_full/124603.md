mousetrapping:/ˈmaʊsˌtræpɪŋ/
n.|捕鼠；诱捕；欺骗|The act of trapping mice, or figuratively, a deceptive practice.  This can refer to literal mouse traps or schemes designed to trick someone. |He was accused of mousetrapping unsuspecting investors. 他被指控诱骗毫无戒心的投资者。|近义词：deception, trickery, ensnarement|反义词:honesty, fairness|词性:名词

mousetrapping:/ˈmaʊsˌtræpɪŋ/
adj.|欺骗性的；诱捕的|Descriptive of something that is deceptive or acts like a mousetrap, often used figuratively.|The company used mousetrapping tactics to lure customers into signing long-term contracts. 这家公司使用欺骗性策略诱使客户签署长期合同。|近义词：deceptive, tricky, misleading|反义词:honest, straightforward|词性:形容词
