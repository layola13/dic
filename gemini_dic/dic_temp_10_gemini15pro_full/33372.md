chunji:/ˈtʃʌndʒiː/ | n. | 春季,春天 | Spring, the season after winter and before summer.  It is associated with new life, growth, and warmth.  This word is a transliteration from the Korean word 춘지 (pronounced similarly). | The chunji flowers are blooming beautifully. 春天的花开得很漂亮。| The birds returned in chunji. 鸟儿在春天回来了。| 近义词：springtime, vernal season | 反义词: autumn, fall | 词性: 名词

词源: From Korean 춘지 (chunji), meaning "spring season."  춘 (chun) meaning spring and 지 (ji) being a suffix indicating a season.
