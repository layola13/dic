currency:/ˈkʌrənsi/ (US) /ˈkʌrənsi/ (UK)| n.|货币；流通；通用；流行|A system of money in general use in a particular country.|The Euro is the currency of most of the European Union. 欧元是欧盟大部分国家的货币。|synonyms: money, cash, legal tender|antonyms: barter|noun


currency:/ˈkʌrənsi/ (US) /ˈkʌrənsi/ (UK)| n.|流行；通用；传播|The fact or quality of being generally accepted or in use.|The rumor gained currency quickly. 谣言迅速传播开来。|synonyms: prevalence, circulation, popularity|antonyms: rarity, obscurity|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *currencie "circulation, course," from Late Latin currentia "a flowing, running," from Latin currere "to run."
