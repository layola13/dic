coveting: /ˈkʌvɪtɪŋ/ | n./adj. | 垂涎，贪图，渴望 | The act of desiring something that belongs to someone else; excessively desirous. |  She couldn't help coveting her neighbor's beautiful garden. 她忍不住垂涎邻居漂亮的花园。| 近义词: desiring, yearning, craving, envying | 反义词:  indifferent, apathetic | 词性:  现在分词 (present participle) of the verb "covet" (来自古法语 *cuveitier, 源自拉丁语 cupidus “渴望的”,  cupiditas “渴望，贪婪”)
