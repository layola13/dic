dearly:/ˈdɪrli/| adv.|昂贵地；深深地；热烈地|At great cost; with deep affection.|She loved him dearly. 她深深地爱着他。|近义词：expensively, fondly, deeply, greatly|反义词：cheaply, indifferently, superficially|词性:副词

词源: From Old English *dēorlīce (attested only in dēorlīcor 'more dearly, more expensively'), from dēore 'dear' + -līce '-ly'.
