One might consider parenthood from the perspective of the parent, but it's not a true opposite.)|词性:名词

Etymology:
From aunt + -hood.  "Aunt" comes from Old French "ante," from Latin "amita," meaning "father's sister."  "-hood" is an Old English suffix denoting "state, condition, quality, character."
