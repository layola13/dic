outsophisticating:/ˌaʊtsəˈfɪstɪkeɪtɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的诡辩；在诡辩上超过
surpassing in sophistication; outdoing in worldly wisdom or refinement.
He tried outsophisticating his opponent in the debate, but failed. 他试图在辩论中用诡辩胜过对手，但失败了。
近义词: outmaneuvering, outsmarting, outwitting
反义词: being naive, being gullible
词性: verb (present participle)


outsophisticating:/ˌaʊtsəˈfɪstɪkeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
极其精明的；过于世故的
excessively sophisticated or worldly-wise.
Her outsophisticating manner made her seem aloof. 她过于世故的态度让她显得冷漠。
近义词: pretentious, affected, ostentatious
反义词: genuine, sincere, unpretentious
词性: adjective
