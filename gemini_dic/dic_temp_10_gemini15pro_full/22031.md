bokage:/ˈboʊkɑːʒ/| n.|树篱，灌木丛|A landscape of mixed woodland and pasture, with fields and farms separated by hedges and banks.  A type of countryside typical of parts of Normandy, France.|The bocage country made it difficult for tanks to advance.|Synonyms: hedgerow, thicket, shrubbery|Antonyms: open field, plain|noun
