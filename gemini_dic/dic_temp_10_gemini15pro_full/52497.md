diffarreation:/ˌdɪfəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |解除婚姻（古罗马的一种宗教仪式）| The act of dissolving a marriage by a religious ceremony in ancient Rome. It was a specific ritual for marriages contracted by confarreatio. |  The diffarreation ceremony was as complex as the initial marriage rite. 解除婚姻的仪式和最初的结婚仪式一样复杂。

The couple underwent a diffarreation to formally end their sacred union. 这对夫妇进行了解除婚姻仪式，正式结束他们的神圣结合。 | 近义词: divorce, annulment | 反义词: marriage, confarreatio | 词性: noun
