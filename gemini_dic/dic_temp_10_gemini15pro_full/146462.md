overfrugal:/ˌoʊvərˈfruːɡəl/
adjective
过度节俭的，过于吝啬的
Excessively frugal; too economical.
My grandfather was so overfrugal that he reused tea bags multiple times. 我祖父太节俭了，以至于茶包都重复使用多次。
He was overfrugal to the point of being miserly. 他节俭到吝啬的地步。
近义词: parsimonious, stingy, miserly, penny-pinching
反义词: extravagant, lavish, generous, prodigal
词性: adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "frugal" (from Latin "frugalis," meaning "economical")
