antipestilence:/ˌæntiˈpɛstɪləns/| adj.|防瘟疫的，抗瘟疫的|Preventing or curing pestilence; effective against plagues.|例句：The doctor prescribed antipestilence herbs. 医生开了防瘟疫的草药。|近义词：antiplague, prophylactic|反义词:pestilential, infectious|词性:adjective

antipestilence:/ˌæntiˈpɛstɪləns/| n.|防瘟疫措施，防疫物|Something that prevents or cures pestilence.|例句：The community adopted strict antipestilence measures. 社区采取了严格的防疫措施。|近义词：preventative, remedy|反义词:plague, contagion|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + pestilence.  "Anti-" is a prefix of Greek origin meaning "against" or "opposed to." "Pestilence" comes from the Old French "pestilence" and ultimately from the Latin "pestilentia," meaning "plague," "pestilence," or "infectious disease."
