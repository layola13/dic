missay:/mɪˈseɪ/ | n. |失言，说错话| An unintentional error in speech; a slip of the tongue. | She made a missay and accidentally insulted her boss. 她失言了，不小心侮辱了她的老板。| Example:  He apologized for his missay, explaining that he had meant to say something else entirely. 他为自己的失言道歉，解释说他本来是想说完全不同的话。| Synonym:  gaffe, slip, blunder, lapse, error | Antonym:  accuracy, precision | noun
