dripped:/drɪpt/| v.|滴下，流下|The water dripped from the faucet. 水从水龙头滴下来。|例句：The rain dripped from the leaves. 雨水从树叶上滴下来。|近义词：trickled, dribbled|反义词:gushed, poured|verb

dripped:/drɪpt/| adj.|滴落的，湿淋淋的|Her hair was dripped with rain. 她的头发被雨淋湿了。|例句：His clothes were dripped with sweat. 他的衣服被汗水浸湿了。|近义词：soaked, drenched|反义词:dry|adjective
