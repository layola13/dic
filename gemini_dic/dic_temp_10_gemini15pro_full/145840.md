overdelighted:/ˌoʊvərdiˈlaɪtɪd/| adj.|欣喜若狂的，非常高兴的|Extremely delighted; overjoyed.|She was overdelighted to receive the scholarship. 她收到奖学金欣喜若狂。|近义词：overjoyed, ecstatic, thrilled, elated|反义词：disappointed, unhappy, sad|词性:形容词

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "delighted" (adjective from the verb "delight," ultimately from Latin "delectare" meaning "to allure, charm").
