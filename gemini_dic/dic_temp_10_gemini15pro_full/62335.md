endemic:/ɛnˈdɛmɪk/ | adj. | 地方性的；特有的；流行的 | Native or confined to a particular region or people; characteristic of or prevalent in a field. | Malaria is endemic in many hot countries. 疟疾在很多炎热的国家流行。 |  The kangaroo is endemic to Australia. 袋鼠是澳大利亚特有的动物。 | 近义词：native, indigenous, restricted, prevalent | 反义词：cosmopolitan, pandemic, exotic | 词性: adjective
Etymology: From Greek *éndēmos* "native," from *en* "in" + *dēmos* "people."
