nonremembrances:/ˌnɒn rɪˈmɛmbrənsɪz/
noun
无记忆;不被铭记的事物;遗忘
The state of being forgotten or not remembered; things that are not remembered.
He spoke of a time of nonremembrances, a period erased from history.  他谈到了一段无人铭记的时期，一段被从历史上抹去的时期。

Near synonyms: oblivion, forgetfulness, amnesia
Antonyms: remembrance, memory, recollection
词性: 名词 (plural)
