experts:/ˈekspɜːrts/ (US) /ˈekspɜːts/ (UK)| n.|专家，行家|People who have a high level of knowledge or skill in a particular subject or activity.|The museum consulted experts on the authenticity of the painting.  博物馆就这幅画的真伪咨询了专家。|synonyms: specialists, professionals, authorities, connoisseurs|antonyms: amateurs, novices, beginners|noun

experts:/ˈekspɜːrts/ (US) /ˈekspɜːts/ (UK)| adj.|熟练的，内行的|Having or showing a high level of knowledge or skill.|She's an expert pianist. 她是一位技艺精湛的钢琴家。|synonyms: skilled, proficient, adept, masterful|antonyms: unskilled, inept, amateurish|adjective
