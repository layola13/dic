housewright:/ˈhaʊsˌraɪt/| n.|房屋建造者，木匠|A person who builds houses; a carpenter.|例句：The housewright carefully measured the timber before cutting it.  木匠在切割木材之前仔细地测量了它。

例句：The skilled housewright crafted a beautiful staircase.  这位熟练的木匠制作了一个漂亮的楼梯。|近义词：carpenter, builder, joiner|反义词:demolitionist, wrecker|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *houswright*, equivalent to *house* + *wright* (worker, maker).  Cognate with Dutch *huiswricht*.  The word *wright* is now archaic and mostly survives in compounds such as *shipwright* and *playwright*.
