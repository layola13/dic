deifying:/ˈdiːɪfaɪɪŋ/
verb
神化；奉若神明
The act of treating someone or something as a god.
The Romans deified their emperors. 罗马人神化他们的皇帝。
近义词: idolizing, exalting, worshipping, glorifying
反义词: vilifying, denigrating, condemning, disparaging
词性: verb (present participle)

deify:/ˈdiːɪfaɪ/
verb
神化；奉若神明
To treat someone or something as a god.
Some cultures deify animals. 一些文化神化动物。
近义词: idolize, exalt, worship, glorify
反义词: vilify, denigrate, condemn, disparage
词性: verb

词源: From Late Middle English *deifien*, from Old French *deifier*, from Latin *deificāre* (“to make a god of, deify”), from *deus* (“god”) + *-ficāre*, a combining form of *facere* (“to make, do”).
