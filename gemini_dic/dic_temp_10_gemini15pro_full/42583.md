counterploy:/ˈkaʊntərplɔɪ/| n.|对策，反计，策略|A counterploy is a strategy or action used to counter or thwart another strategy or action.|例句：His counterploy was to leak the information to the press. 他的对策是将信息泄露给媒体。

例句：The company developed a counterploy to the competitor's aggressive marketing campaign.  公司针对竞争对手咄咄逼人的营销活动制定了应对策略。|近义词：stratagem, tactic, maneuver, riposte|反义词:ploy, trick|词性:noun

counterploy:/ˈkaʊntərplɔɪ/| v.|实施反计，反击|To counterploy is to use a counterploy.|例句：He countered their move by counterploying with a surprise announcement. 他以一个意外的公告来反击他们的行动。

例句：She skillfully counterployed against her opponent's accusations. 她巧妙地反击了对手的指控。|近义词：outmaneuver, thwart, foil|反义词:yield, surrender|词性:verb
