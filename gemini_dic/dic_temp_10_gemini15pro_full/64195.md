equianalgesic: /ˌikwiˌænəlˈdʒiːzɪk/ /ˌikwiˌænəlˈdʒizɪk/
adjective
等效镇痛的
Having equal analgesic (pain-relieving) effects.  Relating to or denoting doses of different analgesic drugs that produce the same degree of pain relief.
例句：The doctor prescribed an equianalgesic dose of a different opioid. 医生开了另一种阿片类药物的等效镇痛剂量。

例句：The chart showed the equianalgesic conversions for various pain medications.  图表显示了各种止痛药的等效镇痛剂量转换。

近义词：equipotent (in terms of pain relief)
反义词:  None (The concept implies equivalence, not opposition.)
词性: adjective
词源: equi- (Latin, meaning "equal") + analgesic (from Greek an-, "without," + algos, "pain")
