demonstrance:/ˈdemənstreɪns/| n. |示威，游行；演示，展示|A public display of opinion or feeling, typically involving a march or rally; a practical exhibition and explanation of how something works or is done.|例句：The students held a demonstrance against the tuition increase. 学生们举行示威抗议学费上涨。

例句：The salesman gave a demonstrance of the new kitchen appliance. 销售员演示了新的厨房用具。|近义词：protest, rally, march, exhibition, display, presentation|反义词：concealment, suppression|词性：名词
