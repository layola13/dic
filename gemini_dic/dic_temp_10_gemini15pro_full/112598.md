lovingly:/ˈlʌvɪŋli/| adv.|爱恋地，亲切地，深情地|In a way that shows love and care.|She looked at him lovingly. 她深情地望着他。|例句：She held the baby lovingly in her arms. 她深情地把婴儿抱在怀里。|近义词：affectionately, tenderly, warmly|反义词：coldly, harshly, indifferently|词性:副词
词源: From love + -ing + -ly.  Ultimately from the Old English lufu.
