inhibition:/ˌɪnhɪˈbɪʃən/ | n. | 抑制，阻止，禁止 |  The conscious or unconscious restraint of a behavioral process, desire, or impulse. |  His shyness is a form of social inhibition. 他的害羞是一种社交抑制的表现。| synonyms: restraint, suppression, repression | antonyms: encouragement, stimulation | noun

inhibit:/ɪnˈhɪbɪt/ | v. | 抑制，阻止，禁止 | To hinder, restrain, or prevent (an action or process). |  Fear can inhibit people from expressing their opinions. 恐惧会阻止人们表达自己的观点。| synonyms: hinder, restrain, impede | antonyms: encourage, promote | verb
