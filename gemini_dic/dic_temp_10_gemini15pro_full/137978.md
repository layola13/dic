nonzealousness:/ˌnɒnˈzɛləsnəs/| n. |缺乏热情，不热心|Lack of zeal or enthusiasm; indifference.|His nonzealousness towards the project worried his team.  他对这个项目缺乏热情，让他的团队很担心。|The teacher was concerned by the student's nonzealousness in class. 老师对学生在课堂上缺乏热情感到担忧。|近义词：apathy, indifference, lack of enthusiasm, listlessness|反义词: zeal, enthusiasm, fervor, passion|词性:名词

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not" or "opposite of"  来源于拉丁语 non
zealous (adjective): characterized by passionate and intense enthusiasm  来源于希腊语 ζῆλος (zēlos) meaning "zeal, ardour"
-ness (suffix): forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality
