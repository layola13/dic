blubberers:/ˈblʌbərərz/
n.
爱哭的人，哭诉的人
People who blubber; those who weep loudly and uncontrollably.
The blubberers huddled together, their faces streaked with tears.  爱哭的人们挤在一起，脸上满是泪痕。
After the heartbreaking news, the room was filled with blubberers.  听到令人心碎的消息后，房间里充满了哭泣的人。
近义词:criers, weepers, wailers
反义词:stoics
词性:名词

blubberer:/ˈblʌbərər/
n.
爱哭的人，哭诉的人
A person who blubbers; someone who weeps loudly and uncontrollably.
The little blubberer finally calmed down after receiving a hug.  小哭包在得到拥抱后终于平静下来了。
He's such a blubberer; he cries at the drop of a hat. 他真是个爱哭鬼，动不动就哭。
近义词:crybaby, whiner
反义词:stoic
词性:名词
