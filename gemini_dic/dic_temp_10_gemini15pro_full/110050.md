licensors:/ˈlaɪsənsərz/
n.
许可人；授权人
People or organizations that grant licenses.  Those who give permission for the use of something they own or control, such as a patent, copyright, or trademark.
The licensors receive a percentage of the profits from the sale of the licensed product. 许可方从销售授权产品的利润中获得一定比例的分成。
Synonym: grantor, authorizer
Antonym: licensee
词性: 名词
