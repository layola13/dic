overmoralizingly:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrəlaɪzɪŋli/
adverb
过分道德化地，过分说教地
In a manner that emphasizes moral principles or judgments excessively or unnecessarily.
He spoke overmoralizingly about the dangers of social media, alienating his teenage audience. 他过分强调社交媒体的危害，疏远了他的青少年听众。
She overmoralizingly condemned the behavior of the younger generation. 她过分道德地谴责了年轻一代的行为。
Synonyms: sanctimoniously, preachily, moralistically
Antonyms: permissively, indulgently
词性: 副词
