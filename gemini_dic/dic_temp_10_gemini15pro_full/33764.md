circulating:/ˈsɜːrkjəleɪtɪŋ/ (US), /ˈsɜːkjʊleɪtɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|循环的，流通的|Moving continuously or freely through a closed system or area; being passed from person to person or place to place.|The circulating rumors caused a lot of anxiety. 流言四起，造成了很大的焦虑。|近义词：rotating, flowing, spreading|反义词：stagnant, static|adjective

circulate:/ˈsɜːrkjəleɪt/ (US), /ˈsɜːkjʊleɪt/ (UK)| v.|循环，流通；传播|To move around or through something, or to make something do this; to pass from person to person or place to place.|Blood circulates throughout the body. 血液在全身循环。|近义词：distribute, spread, disseminate|反义词：restrict, confine|verb
