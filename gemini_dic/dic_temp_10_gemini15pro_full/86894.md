hemispherically:/ˌhemɪˈsfɛrɪkli/
adjective
半球地，在半球范围内
Relating to or affecting a hemisphere or hemispheres.
The Earth is hemispherically divided by the equator. 地球被赤道半球状地划分。
Global warming is affecting the planet hemispherically. 全球变暖正在半球范围内影响着地球。
近义词: globally, universally
反义词: locally, regionally
词性: adjective
