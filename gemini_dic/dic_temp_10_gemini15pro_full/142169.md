orlop:/ˈɔːləp/ | n. | 最下层甲板；底舱 | The lowest deck of a ship, especially one below the waterline, often used for storage. | The ship's orlop was filled with barrels of rum and salted pork.  这艘船的底舱装满了朗姆酒桶和咸猪肉。 | synonym: hold, lower deck | antonym: upper deck | noun
