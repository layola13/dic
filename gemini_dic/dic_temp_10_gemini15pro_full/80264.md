gogglier:/ˈɡɒɡliə(r)/| adj.|更加瞪眼的，更加护目镜似的|More goggly; having or showing large, staring, and bulging eyes, often enhanced by goggles.|Her eyes got even gogglier when she saw the surprise gift. 她看到惊喜礼物时，眼睛瞪得更大了。|The fish looked gogglier than usual after bumping its head on the tank. 那条鱼撞到鱼缸上后，眼睛比平时瞪得更大了。|近义词：bulging, staring, wide-eyed|反义词：narrowed, squinted|词性:形容词
goggle:/ˈɡɒɡ(ə)l/| v. n.|瞪大眼睛看，护目镜|To stare with wide eyes; a pair of protective glasses, often worn for sports.|He goggled at the price of the car. 他看到汽车的价格，瞪大了眼睛。|She wore goggles while swimming. 她游泳时戴着护目镜。|近义词：stare, gape, gawk (v.); spectacles, glasses (n.)|反义词：squint (v.)|词性:动词,名词
