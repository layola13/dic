coolie: /ˈkuːli/ | n. | 苦力；劳工；搬运工 | A derogatory and offensive term for an unskilled laborer, typically of Asian descent, historically subjected to harsh working conditions.  This word carries a heavy historical baggage of exploitation and racism and should be avoided. |  This term is now considered offensive. | 近义词: laborer, worker (neutral terms),  navvy | 反义词: employer, boss, manager | 词性: noun
