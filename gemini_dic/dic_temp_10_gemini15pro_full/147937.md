overreported:/ˌoʊvərɪˈpɔːrtɪd/| adj.|过分报道的，夸大报道的|Reported at a higher level or frequency than is accurate or justified.  It implies an exaggeration or inflation of the true figures or facts.|The number of flu cases was overreported in the media, causing unnecessary panic. 媒体夸大了流感病例的数量，造成了不必要的恐慌。|Synonyms: exaggerated, inflated, overstated, overemphasized|Antonyms: underreported, downplayed, minimized|adjective

overreport:/ˌoʊvərɪˈpɔːrt/| v.|过分报道，夸大报道|To report something at a higher level or frequency than is accurate or justified.|Several news outlets overreported the severity of the storm. 几家新闻机构夸大了风暴的严重程度。|Synonyms: exaggerate, inflate, overstate, overemphasize|Antonyms: underreport, downplay, minimize|verb
