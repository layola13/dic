fragmentariness:/ˌfræɡmənˈtɛrɪnɪs/ | n. |碎片化，片段性；不完整性|The state of being fragmentary; incompleteness.|The fragmentariness of the available data made it difficult to draw any firm conclusions.  现有数据的碎片化使得难以得出任何确定的结论。|Synonyms: incompleteness, disjointedness, disconnectedness |Antonyms: completeness, wholeness, entirety|noun


fragmentary:/ˌfræɡmənˈtɛri/ | adj. |碎片的，片段的；不完整的|Consisting of fragments; incomplete.|The police only found fragmentary evidence at the crime scene. 警方只在犯罪现场发现了碎片化的证据。|Synonyms: incomplete, partial, piecemeal |Antonyms: complete, whole, entire|adjective
