clangourously:/ˈklæŋɡərəsli/ | adv. | 铿锵作响地，喧闹地 |Characterized by or producing a loud resonant metallic sound, like a bell or clashing metal. | The bells clanged clangourously, announcing the start of the festival. 钟声铿锵作响，宣告了节日的开始。 | The cymbals clashed clangourously during the orchestra's performance.  钹在管弦乐队的演奏中铿锵作响。 | 近义词:noisily, resonantly, loudly, clamorously | 反义词:quietly, softly, silently | 词性:副词

clangour:/ˈklæŋɡər/ | n. | 铿锵声，喧闹声 |A loud resonant metallic sound or a din of metallic sounds. |The clangour of the blacksmith's hammer filled the air. 铁匠的锤子铿锵声响彻空中。 | The clangour of the city traffic made it difficult to sleep. 城市交通的喧闹声使人难以入睡。 | 近义词: din, clamor, racket | 反义词: silence, quiet | 词性:名词


词源: From Middle English *clangor, *clangour, from Anglo-Norman clangur, Old French clangor, from Latin clangor (“a loud sound, a blare, clang”), of imitative origin.
