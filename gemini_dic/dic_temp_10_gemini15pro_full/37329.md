commemorators:/kəˈmɛməˌreɪtərz/
n.
纪念者，庆祝者
People who commemorate something or someone.
The commemorators gathered at the monument to pay their respects. 纪念者们聚集在纪念碑前表达敬意。
The commemorators laid wreaths at the tomb of the unknown soldier.  纪念者们在无名战士墓前敬献花圈。
