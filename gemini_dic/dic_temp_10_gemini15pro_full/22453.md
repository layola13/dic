bookishnesses:/ˈbʊkɪʃnɪsɪz/
noun
书呆子气，学究习气
The quality of being excessively devoted to reading and studying, often to the exclusion of practical matters.
Her bookishnesses sometimes made it difficult for her to relate to her less academically inclined peers. 她的书呆子气有时让她很难与不那么喜欢学术的同龄人相处。
studiousness, pedantry, scholarly
worldliness, practicality
