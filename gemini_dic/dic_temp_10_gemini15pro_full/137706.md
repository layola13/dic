nonutilitarian:/ˌnɒnjuːˌtɪlɪˈteəriən/
| adj. |非功利的，非实用性的|Not designed primarily for or directed toward a practical or useful purpose; not utilitarian.|She pursued nonutilitarian hobbies like painting and poetry. 她追求绘画和诗歌等非功利的爱好。|The museum displayed nonutilitarian artifacts from ancient civilizations.  博物馆展出了古代文明的非实用性文物。|近义词：nonpractical, aesthetic, decorative, ornamental|反义词：utilitarian, practical, functional|词性: 形容词

Etymology->
From non- + utilitarian.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "utilitarian" refers to something designed for practical use.
