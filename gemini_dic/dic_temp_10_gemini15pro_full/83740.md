hailing:/ˈheɪlɪŋ/ /ˈheɪlɪŋ/| v.  n.|下冰雹; 招呼; 来自;  冰雹|
例句：
They were hailing a taxi.
他们正在叫出租车。

例句：
The ship was hailing from New York.
这艘船来自纽约。

近义词：greeting, saluting, calling, originating, hailstones|
反义词: ignoring, farewelling|
词性: verb, noun|

词源: From Middle English *hailen, from Old Norse heill (“healthy, safe”), from Proto-Germanic *hailaz (“whole, sound, healthy”), from Proto-Indo-European *kailos (“whole, uninjured, of good omen”). Cognate with German Heil. The noun is from the verb.  The original sense was "to greet, salute," later extended to "call out to."
