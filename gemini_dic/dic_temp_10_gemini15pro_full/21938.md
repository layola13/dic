boggier:/ˈbɒɡɪər/ (UK) /ˈbɑːɡiər/ (US)| adj.|更泥泞的，更沼泽的|Comparative form of boggy: more boggy.  Characterized by an increasing presence of bog; wetter and softer.|The ground became boggier after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，地面变得更加泥泞了。| The trail was much boggier than we expected. 这条小路比我们预想的要泥泞得多。|近义词：marshier, swampier, wetter, soggier|反义词：drier, firmer|词性:形容词
