ETYMOLOGY->
From "mining" + "'s" (possessive suffix).  "Mining" comes from the verb "mine," which originates from the Old French word "miner," meaning "to dig a mine." This ultimately traces back to a Gaulish word (a Celtic language)  *mēna.
