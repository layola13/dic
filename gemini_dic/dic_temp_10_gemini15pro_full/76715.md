PHRASE->
gamble on:  冒险尝试；赌…会发生；指望…会发生  He gambled on the stock market and lost. 他冒险投资股市，结果亏了。
gamble away:  赌输；挥霍掉  He gambled away his entire fortune. 他把他的全部财产都赌输了。
gamble with:  拿…冒险  He was gambling with his life. 他在拿自己的生命冒险。
take a gamble:  冒险一试  He decided to take a gamble and start his own business. 他决定冒险一试，创办自己的企业。
big gamble:  大赌注  Investing in that company was a big gamble. 投资那家公司是一个很大的赌注。
