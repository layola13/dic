out-winging:/ˌaʊtˈwɪŋɪŋ/
verb
胜过，以更宽的翼展飞行
surpassing or exceeding someone or something, especially in scope or performance, often suggesting a more expansive or comprehensive approach.  Often used in the context of aircraft or birds having a wider wingspan than another.
The new aircraft is out-winging all its competitors in terms of fuel efficiency.  这架新飞机在燃油效率方面胜过所有竞争对手。
The eagle was out-winging the smaller birds, soaring effortlessly above them.  这只鹰的翼展超过了较小的鸟类，毫不费力地在它们上方翱翔。
近义词: surpassing, exceeding, outperforming, outdoing, outmaneuvering
反义词: lagging, trailing, underperforming
词性: verb
