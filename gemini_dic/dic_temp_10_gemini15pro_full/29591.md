catechistic:/ˌkætɪˈkɪstɪk/ | adj. | 教义问答式的，问答教学法的 | Relating to, or resembling the method of instruction by question and answer. |  The teacher employed a catechistic method to ensure the students understood the core doctrines. 老师采用问答式教学法来确保学生理解核心教义。

The catechistic approach can be effective in reinforcing key concepts.  问答教学法可以有效地强化关键概念。 | synonyms: didactic, instructional, Socratic | antonyms: n/a | adjective

catechism:/ˈkætɪˌkɪzəm/ | n. | 教理问答,问答集 | A summary of the principles of Christian religion in the form of questions and answers, used for the instruction of Christians. | The children studied the catechism to prepare for their confirmation. 孩子们学习教理问答为坚信礼做准备。

The priest referred to the catechism during his sermon.  牧师在布道时提到了教理问答。| synonyms: primer, manual, doctrine | antonyms: n/a | noun
