cracker:/ˈkrækər/ (US), /ˈkrækə/ (UK)| n.|薄脆饼干；鞭炮；解密高手|A thin, crisp biscuit/cookie, often eaten with cheese; a firework that makes a sharp cracking sound when exploded; a person who breaks into computer systems.|例句：I like to eat crackers with cheese. 我喜欢吃奶酪饼干。

例句：The cracker made a loud bang. 鞭炮发出一声巨响。

例句：The cracker bypassed the security system.  解密高手绕过了安全系统。

|近义词：biscuit, cookie (for food); firecracker (for firework); hacker (for person)|反义词:(No direct antonym)|词性:noun
