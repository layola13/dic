bolt-ons:/ˈboʊlt ɑːnz/ | n. | 附加组件，附加装置 | Something that is added to something else, often to improve it or make it more attractive. | The car came with several bolt-ons, including a sunroof and heated seats. 这辆车配有几个附加装置，包括天窗和加热座椅。 | Example 2: The company offers a range of bolt-ons for its software, such as extra storage and advanced security features.  这家公司为其软件提供一系列附加组件，例如额外的存储空间和高级安全功能。 | synonyms: additions, accessories, extras, supplements | antonyms: core, base, essential | noun

bolt-on:/ˈboʊlt ɑːn/ | adj. | 附加的，可附加的 |  Describing something that can be easily added to something else. | The company offers a bolt-on security system for its software. 该公司为其软件提供了一个可附加的安全系统。 | Example 2:  They decided to purchase a bolt-on GPS module for their car. 他们决定为他们的汽车购买一个附加的GPS模块。| synonyms: add-on, attachable, supplementary | antonyms: built-in, integrated, inherent | adjective
