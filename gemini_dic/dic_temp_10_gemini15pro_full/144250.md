outsmiling:/ˌaʊtˈsmaɪlɪŋ/
verb
胜过微笑, 以微笑胜过
outdoing someone in smiling; smiling more radiantly or genuinely.
She outsmiled him, her joy evident in the crinkle of her eyes. 她以微笑胜过他，她眼角的皱纹显示出她的喜悦。
近义词: outshining, surpassing
反义词: frowning, grimacing
词性: present participle


Etymology:
From 'out-' (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + 'smiling' (present participle of 'smile').
