chlorophylls:/ˈklɔːrəfɪlz/ | n. | 叶绿素 | Any of a group of green pigments found in photosynthetic organisms, such as plants and algae, which absorb light energy to convert carbon dioxide and water into carbohydrates. |  Chlorophyll gives plants their green color. 叶绿素使植物呈绿色。| pigment, chromophore | achromic | noun
