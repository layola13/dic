nonpersuasively: /ˌnɒnpəˈsweɪsɪvli/ /ˌnɑːnpərˈsweɪsɪvli/
| adv. | 没有说服力地 | In a manner that fails to convince or persuade. | He argued nonpersuasively, failing to sway the jury. 他辩论得没有说服力，未能动摇陪审团。|The presentation was delivered nonpersuasively, leading to a decline in sales.  这个演示文稿讲得没有说服力，导致销售额下降。 | unconvincingly, ineffectively | persuasively, convincingly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From non- (not) + persuasive + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Persuasive" ultimately derives from Latin "persuadere" (to persuade).
