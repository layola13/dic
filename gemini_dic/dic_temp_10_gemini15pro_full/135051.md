nonnormal:/nɑːnˈnɔːrməl/ | adj. | 非正态的，非正常的 | Not conforming to a normal distribution or pattern; deviating from the usual or expected. | The data showed a nonnormal distribution, requiring the use of nonparametric statistics. 数据显示非正态分布，需要使用非参数统计。 |  The psychologist noted the child's nonnormal behavior. 心理学家注意到了孩子异常的行为。 | 近义词: abnormal, atypical, irregular, deviant | 反义词: normal, typical, standard, regular | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "normal" (from Latin "normalis," meaning "according to a carpenter's square," hence "right," "regular").
