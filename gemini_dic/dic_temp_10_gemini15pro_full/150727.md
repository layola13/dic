parachordal:/ˌpærəˈkɔːrdl/ | adj. | 旁索的，脊索旁的 | Situated beside or near the notochord.  Relating to or denoting the cartilaginous skeletal rods that lie beside the notochord in the heads of vertebrates and contribute to the skull base. |  The parachordal cartilages fuse to form the basal plate of the chondrocranium. 旁索软骨融合形成软骨颅底板。 |  Parachordal plates develop on either side of the notochord.  脊索两侧发育出旁索板。 |  juxtachordal, perichordal | N/A | adjective
