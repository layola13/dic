lungi:/ˈlʌŋɡiː/| n.|隆基，纱笼|A garment consisting of a length of cotton or silk cloth wrapped around the waist or hips, worn especially by men in India, Sri Lanka, and other parts of South Asia.|He wore a brightly colored lungi. 他穿了一条颜色鲜艳的隆基。|Synonyms: sarong, dhoti|Antonyms: trousers, pants|noun

lungi:/ˈlʌŋɡiː/| n.|隆基布|A length of cotton or silk cloth used to make a lungi.|She bought some lungi cloth to make a new outfit. 她买了一些隆基布来做一套新衣服。|Synonyms: fabric, material|Antonyms: N/A|noun
