chylification:/ˌkaɪlɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 乳糜化 | The process of converting food into chyle in the small intestine.  The transformation of digested food into chyle by the lacteals of the small intestine. |  Chylification is an essential part of digestion. 乳糜化是消化过程中必不可少的一部分。|  The process of chylification is disrupted in certain diseases. 在某些疾病中，乳糜化的过程会被破坏。|  emulsification, digestion | absorption | noun
