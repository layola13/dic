nondecisiveness:/ˌnɒndɪˈsaɪsɪvnəs/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈsaɪsɪvnəs/| n. |优柔寡断，犹豫不决|The state or quality of being unable to make decisions.|His nondecisiveness cost him the promotion. 他的优柔寡断让他失去了晋升的机会.|近义词：indecision, hesitancy, vacillation|反义词: decisiveness, determination, resolution|词性:名词

Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + decisive + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)  Comes from the Latin word "decidere" meaning "to cut off, decide".
