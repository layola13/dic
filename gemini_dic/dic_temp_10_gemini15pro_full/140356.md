oicks: /ɔɪks/
| interj. | 哎呀;哎哟 | An exclamation of surprise, dismay, or pain.  | Oicks! I stubbed my toe! 哎哟！我的脚趾撞到了！| 近义词: ouch, oops, yikes | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an exclamation) | 词性:感叹词
Etymology:  Likely a variant of "ouch" or "oops," mimicking a sound of sudden pain or surprise.  Its origin is imitative.
