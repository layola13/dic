covenantal:/ˌkʌvəˈnæntl/
adjective
契约的；圣约的
Relating to or based on a covenant (a formal agreement or promise, especially one made between God and his people).
The covenantal relationship between God and Abraham is a cornerstone of Judaism.  上帝与亚伯拉罕之间的契约关系是犹太教的基石。
The church emphasizes the covenantal nature of marriage.  教会强调婚姻的契约性质。
近义词:contractual,  agreed,  pacted
反义词:non-binding, informal
词性:adjective
