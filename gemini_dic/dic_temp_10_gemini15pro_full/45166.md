cues:/kjuːz/| v.|提示，暗示|To cue someone is to give them a signal. |The director cued the orchestra to begin. 指挥示意乐队开始演奏。|synonyms: signal, prompt, hint, direct|antonyms:  mislead, confuse, distract|verb
