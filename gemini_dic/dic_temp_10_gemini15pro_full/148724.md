overtrample:/ˌoʊvərˈtræmpl/
verb
蹂躏，践踏；过度利用，滥用
to trample heavily or excessively; to exploit or overuse something to its detriment.
The retreating army overtrampled the crops, leaving the farmers with nothing. 撤退的军队践踏了庄稼，使农民颗粒无收。
The company's relentless pursuit of profit has overtrampled employee rights. 公司无情地追求利润，践踏了员工的权利。
近义词:trample, crush, abuse, exploit
反义词:protect, cherish, conserve
词性:v.

overtrample:/ˌoʊvərˈtræmpl/
noun
蹂躏，践踏；过度利用，滥用
The act of trampling heavily or excessively; the act of exploiting or overusing something to its detriment.
The overtrample of the refugees' rights was condemned by international organizations. 国际组织谴责了对难民权利的践踏。
The overtrample of natural resources has led to environmental degradation. 对自然资源的过度利用导致了环境恶化。
近义词:trampling, crushing, abuse, exploitation
反义词:protection, cherishing, conservation
词性:n.
