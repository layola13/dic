marray:/ˈmæri/ | v. | 结婚，使结婚 | To join in marriage; to wed. |  He will marry her next spring. 他将于明年春天和她结婚。 | She married a doctor. 她嫁给了一位医生。 | 近义词: wed, espouse | 反义词: divorce, separate | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *marien, *maryen, from Old French marier, from Late Latin marītāre (“to marry, give in marriage”), from Latin marītātus, past participle of marītō (“to marry, give in marriage”), from maritus (“husband, married man”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *mer- (“man”).
