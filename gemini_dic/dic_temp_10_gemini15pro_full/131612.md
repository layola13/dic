noncompliant:/ˌnɒnkəmˈplaɪənt/ |adj.|不服从的，不顺从的；不合规的|Failing to act in accordance with a wish or command.  Not conforming to rules or standards.|The company was fined for noncompliant waste disposal practices.  该公司因不合规的废物处理做法而被罚款。|Synonyms: disobedient, rebellious, unruly, defiant, recalcitrant, insubordinate, contumacious, refractory, intractable, perverse, wayward, contrary,  nonconforming, deviant, aberrant, irregular, unorthodox, unconventional|Antonyms: compliant, obedient, conforming, observing, following, abiding by|adjective
