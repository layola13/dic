diminishment:/dɪˈmɪnɪʃmənt/ | n. |减少，减小，降低|The act of diminishing or the state of being diminished; reduction; lessening. | The diminishment of resources threatens the survival of the species. 资源的减少威胁着该物种的生存。|Synonyms: reduction, decrease, decline, lessening, weakening, curtailment | Antonyms: increase, growth, enlargement, expansion | noun

diminishment:/dɪˈmɪnɪʃmənt/ | n. |衰退，衰弱|A lessening in power, importance, or stature. |There has been a diminishment in his influence since his retirement. 自从退休以来，他的影响力有所减弱。|Synonyms: decline, weakening, deterioration | Antonyms: strengthening, improvement, enhancement | noun
