bmis: /ˈbmiːz/ (No standard pronunciation as it's an acronym) | n. (plural) | 体重指数（复数） | Body Mass Indexes, the plural form of BMI (Body Mass Index). It's a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women. |  The doctor compared the bmis of his patients. 医生比较了他的病人的体重指数。 | 近义词:  (No direct synonym for the plural form, but for BMI:  weight status indicators) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
