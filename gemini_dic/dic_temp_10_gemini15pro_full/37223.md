comfiness:/ˈkʌmfinəs/
noun
舒适；惬意
The state of being comfortable and cozy.
The comfiness of the old armchair made it her favorite spot.  这把旧扶手椅的舒适惬意使它成为她最喜欢的地方。
comfort, coziness, snugness
discomfort, unease
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From comfy (adjective) + -ness (suffix forming nouns of state or quality).  Comfy is a colloquial shortening of comfortable.  Comfortable comes from Middle English confortable, from Old French confortable, from Late Latin comfortabilis, from Latin com- (with) + fortis (strong).
