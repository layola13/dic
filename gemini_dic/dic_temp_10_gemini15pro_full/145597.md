overcondensing:/ˌoʊvərˈkənˌdensɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度压缩，过度浓缩 | Reducing the volume or size of something excessively, particularly vapor or data, beyond what is optimal or desired. | The engineer warned against overcondensing the steam, as it could damage the pipes.  工程师警告不要过度压缩蒸汽，因为这可能会损坏管道。

The algorithm was overcondensing the data, resulting in a loss of important information.  该算法过度压缩了数据，导致重要信息丢失。 | 近义词: overcompressing, over-reducing | 反义词: expanding, diluting | 词性:形容词

Etymology: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "condensing" (present participle of "condense," from Latin "condensare," meaning "to thicken, make dense")
