overassuredly:/ˌoʊvərəˈʃʊrədli/
adverb
过于自信地，过于肯定地
in an overassured manner; too confidently
He overassuredly stated that he would win the race, despite having little training. 他过于自信地宣称他会赢得比赛，尽管他几乎没有训练。
The salesman overassuredly guaranteed the product's effectiveness.  这个销售员过于肯定地保证了产品的有效性。

近义词:confidently, assertively, cockily, presumptuously
反义词:hesitantly, tentatively, uncertainly
词性:adverb
