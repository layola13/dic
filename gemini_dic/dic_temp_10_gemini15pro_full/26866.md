cajoleries:/kəˈdʒoʊləriz/
noun
哄骗；甜言蜜语；奉承
acts of cajoling; attempts to persuade someone to do something by sustained coaxing or flattery.
He used his cajoleries to get what he wanted. 他用甜言蜜语来得到他想要的东西。
Synonyms: blandishments, flattery, coaxing, sweet-talk, persuasion
Antonyms: threats, intimidation, coercion
