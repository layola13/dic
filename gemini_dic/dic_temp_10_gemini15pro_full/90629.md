humanistically:/ˌhjuːməˈnɪstɪkli/
adv.
人文地；人道地；人本主义地
From a humanistic point of view, focusing solely on profit is detrimental. 从人道主义的角度来看，只关注利润是有害的。
He approached the problem humanistically, considering the emotional needs of everyone involved. 他以人道主义的方式处理这个问题，考虑了每个相关人员的情感需求。
近义词: humanely, compassionately, empathetically
反义词: inhumanely, cruelly, callously
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From humanist + -ic + -ally.  "Humanist" traces back through French and Italian to the Latin "humanista," meaning "humanist, scholar."  The suffix "-ic" forms adjectives, and "-ally" transforms adjectives into adverbs.
