hang-a-left:/ˌhæŋ ə ˈlɛft/ | phrasal verb | 左转，向左拐 | To turn left.  It's an informal way to say "turn left". |  Example 1: "Hang a left at the next intersection."  左转进入下一个十字路口。

Example 2: "After the bridge, hang a left and you'll see the gas station." 过桥后左转，你就会看到加油站。 | Synonyms: turn left, make a left | Antonyms: turn right, hang a right, make a right | phrase

Example 1:  I always hang a left at the corner to get to the store. 我总是在拐角处左转去商店。
Example 2: He hangs a left when he sees the big oak tree. 当他看到那棵大橡树时，他就左转。
