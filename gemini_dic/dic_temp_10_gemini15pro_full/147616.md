overplenteousness:/ˌoʊvərˈplɛntɪəsnəs/
n.
过度丰富，过剩
The state of being excessively plentiful; superabundance.
The overplenteousness of the harvest led to a drop in prices.  丰收导致价格下跌。
近义词: abundance, superfluity, plethora, profusion
反义词: scarcity, dearth, lack, deficiency
词性: 名词

词源:  From over + plenteous + -ness.  "Over" signifies "excessive," "plenteous" means "abundant," and "-ness" forms a noun denoting a state or quality.
