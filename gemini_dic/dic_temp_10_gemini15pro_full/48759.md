definitive:/dɪˈfɪnətɪv/| adj.|最终的；确定的；权威性的；决定性的|Serving as a final settlement or decision; conclusive. Authoritative and complete.|例句：The definitive guide to Italian cooking. 意大利烹饪的权威指南。|近义词：conclusive, final, ultimate, decisive, authoritative|反义词：tentative, provisional, inconclusive, indefinite|词性:形容词
definitive:/dɪˈfɪnətɪv/| n.|限定词；最终的事物|Something that is definitive or authoritative.|例句：This book is the definitive on the subject. 这本书是关于这个主题的权威著作。|近义词：authority, standard, benchmark|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
definitives:/dɪˈfɪnətɪvz/| n.|限定词（definitive的复数）|Plural form of definitive (noun).|例句：The museum houses several definitives of 19th-century art.  这家博物馆收藏了几件19世纪艺术的权威作品。|近义词：masterpieces, classics, standards|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
