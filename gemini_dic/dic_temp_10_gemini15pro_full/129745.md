non-ethical:/nɒnˈɛθɪkəl/ | adj. | 不道德的，缺乏伦理的 | Not conforming to approved standards of social or professional behavior.  Lacking moral principles. | The company's non-ethical business practices led to its downfall. 该公司不道德的商业行为导致了它的垮台。 | The lawyer was disbarred for non-ethical conduct.  这位律师因不道德的行为被取消了律师资格。 | 近义词: immoral, unethical, unscrupulous | 反义词: ethical, moral, principled | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + ethical (from Greek "ethikos," meaning "relating to character")
