equivalents:/ɪˈkwɪvələnts/| n.|等价物；对应物；相等物|Things that are equal in value, amount, function, or meaning.|The metric equivalent of one mile is 1.6 kilometers. 一英里的公制等价物是 1.6 公里。|synonyms: counterparts, parallels, substitutes|antonyms: opposites, dissimilarities|noun

equivalence:/ɪˈkwɪvələns/| n.|等价；等值；相等|The state or fact of being equivalent.|The teacher explained the equivalence between mass and energy. 老师解释了质量和能量之间的等效性。|synonyms: equality, parity, sameness|antonyms: inequality, disparity, difference|noun

equivalent:/ɪˈkwɪvələnt/| adj.|等价的；相等的；相当的|Equal in value, amount, function, or meaning.|His silence is equivalent to an admission of guilt. 他的沉默等同于承认有罪。|synonyms: equal, comparable, tantamount|antonyms: unequal, disparate, different|adjective
