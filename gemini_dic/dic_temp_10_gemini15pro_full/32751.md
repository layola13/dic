chlorotic:/kləˈrɒtɪk/ /klɔːˈrɑːtɪk/
adjective
萎黄的，褪绿的
(of a plant or plant part) pale yellow or yellow-green as a result of chlorophyll deficiency.  Suffering from chlorosis; abnormally yellowed.
例句：The chlorotic leaves of the plant indicated a nutrient deficiency.  植物萎黄的叶子表明营养缺乏。

例句：The prolonged drought left the crops chlorotic and stunted. 长时间的干旱使庄稼变得萎黄且发育不良。

近义词：pale, yellowed, etiolated, blanched
反义词：green, healthy, vibrant
词性: adjective
