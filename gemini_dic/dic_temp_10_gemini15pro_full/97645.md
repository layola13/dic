inheritors:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtərz/ | n. |继承人；后嗣|A person who inherits something, such as property, a title, or a responsibility.|例句：The inheritors of the estate will divide the property equally.  这些遗产的继承人将平分财产。

|例句：The young scientists are the inheritors of a rich scientific legacy.  这些年轻的科学家继承了丰富的科学遗产。

|近义词：heirs, successors, descendants|反义词: predecessors, ancestors |词性: noun
