gynantherous:/ˌɡaɪnænˈθərəs/| adj.|雌雄蕊合生的|Having stamens adnate to the gynoecium (pistil).  The stamens are fused to the carpels, often appearing to arise from the ovary.|Example 1:  The gynantherous flowers of some orchids are highly specialized for pollination by specific insects. 一些兰花的雌雄蕊合生的花高度特化，以便被特定的昆虫授粉。

Example 2:  The botanist identified the plant by its unique gynantherous structure. 植物学家通过其独特的雌雄蕊合生结构识别了这种植物。|Synonym: synantherous|Antonym: N/A (lack of a direct antonym, possibly "having separate stamens and pistils")|Word Class: Adjective
