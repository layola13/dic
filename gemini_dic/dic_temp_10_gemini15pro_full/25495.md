bullfrogs:/ˈbʊlˌfrɒɡz/ (US) /ˈbʊlˌfrɒɡz/ (UK)| n.|牛蛙|Large North American frogs known for their deep croaking call, which sounds like a bull's bellow.|Bullfrogs are often found near ponds and lakes. 牛蛙经常在池塘和湖泊附近被发现。|Synonyms: Rana catesbeiana (scientific name), giant frog, American bullfrog|Antonyms: tadpole (earlier stage of development)|Noun
