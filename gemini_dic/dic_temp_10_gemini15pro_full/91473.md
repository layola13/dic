hylotheism:/ˌhaɪloʊˈθiːɪzəm/
n.
万物有灵论;泛神论的一种
The belief that God is immanent in matter, or that the world is God.  A philosophical system recognizing matter as the only reality.
Hylotheism posits that the physical universe and God are one entity. 万物有灵论认为物质宇宙和上帝是同一个实体。
The philosopher argued for a form of hylotheism, believing that divinity resided within the natural world itself. 这位哲学家主张一种万物有灵论的形式，认为神性存在于自然世界本身。
近义词:pantheism, panentheism
反义词:atheism, deism
