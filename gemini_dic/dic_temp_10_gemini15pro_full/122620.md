modestness:/ˈmɒdɪstniːs/ (US) /ˈmɑːdɪstniːs/| n. |谦虚，谦逊；适度，朴素|The quality or state of being modest; unassuming or moderate in the estimation of one's abilities or achievements. |She accepted the award with modesty and grace. 她以谦逊和优雅的态度接受了奖项。|Synonyms: humility, unpretentiousness, simplicity, meekness |Antonyms: arrogance, boastfulness, conceit, vanity|noun
