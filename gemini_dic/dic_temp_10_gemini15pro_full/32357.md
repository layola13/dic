childly:/ˈtʃaɪldli/| adj.|幼稚的，孩子气的|Resembling or characteristic of a child, especially in a negative sense; immature or foolish.|He showed a childish delight in the new toy. 他对新玩具表现出孩子气的喜悦。|近义词：childish, immature, puerile, naive|反义词：mature, adult, grown-up|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *childly, equivalent to child +‎ -ly.
