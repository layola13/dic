gps:/dʒiː piː ˈes/ /dʒiːpiːˈɛs/| n. |全球定位系统|A radio navigation system that uses signals from satellites to give the exact position of something on Earth.|例句：I used the GPS to find the restaurant. 我用GPS找到了那家餐厅。

例句：The GPS in my car is very helpful. 我车上的GPS非常有用。|近义词：navigation system, positioning system|反义词:None|noun
