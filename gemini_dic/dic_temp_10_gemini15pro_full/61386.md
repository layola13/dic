emblazonry: /ɪmˈbleɪzənri/ | n. | 纹章装饰; 纹章学; 华丽的装饰 | The act or art of emblazoning, or describing a coat of arms; also, heraldic adornment or decoration in general; pompous or ostentatious display. | The knight's shield bore intricate emblazonry. 骑士的盾牌上有复杂的纹章装饰。| Synonym: heraldry, ornamentation, decoration | Antonym: plainness, simplicity | Noun

emblazon: /ɪmˈbleɪzən/ | vt. |  用纹章装饰;  宣扬;  颂扬;  使鲜明 | 1. To adorn with heraldic devices, figures, or ornaments. 2. To celebrate or extol publicly; to proclaim. 3. To make brilliant or conspicuous. |  His coat of arms was emblazoned on the carriage door. 他的盾形纹章装饰在马车门上。 The victory was emblazoned across the headlines. 胜利被大肆宣扬在报纸头条上。 | Synonym: decorate, adorn, celebrate, proclaim | Antonym: conceal, hide, obscure | Verb
