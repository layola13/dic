half-sightednesses:/ˌhɑːfˈsaɪtɪdnɪsɪz/ | n. | 近视，目光短浅 | The condition of being half-sighted; lack of foresight or understanding.  |  His political half-sightednesses led to disastrous consequences. 他的政治短视导致了灾难性的后果。 | 近义词: short-sightedness, myopia, lack of foresight | 反义词: foresight, vision, farsightedness | 词性: 名词

half-sighted:/ˌhɑːfˈsaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 近视的，目光短浅的 | Unable to see clearly; lacking foresight or intellectual insight. |  It was a half-sighted policy that failed to consider the long-term effects. 这是一项目光短浅的政策，没有考虑到长期影响。| 近义词: short-sighted, myopic, unthinking | 反义词: farsighted, visionary, prescient | 词性: 形容词
