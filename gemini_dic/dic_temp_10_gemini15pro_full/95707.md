incongruence:/ˌɪnkənˈɡruːəns/ /ˌɪnkɑːŋˈɡruːəns/| n.|不一致，不协调，不和谐|lack of congruence or harmony; incompatibility|Her flamboyant clothing was an incongruence with the somber atmosphere of the funeral. 她艳丽的服装与葬礼的肃穆气氛格格不入。|synonyms: disparity, discrepancy, incompatibility, dissonance, conflict, clash, mismatch|antonyms: congruence, harmony, agreement, consistency, compatibility|noun

incongruent:/ˌɪnkənˈɡruːənt/ /ˌɪnkɑːŋˈɡruːənt/| adj.|不一致的，不协调的，不和谐的|not congruent; not corresponding; incompatible|His actions were incongruent with his words. 他的言行不一。|synonyms: incompatible, inconsistent, discordant, dissonant, conflicting, clashing, mismatched|antonyms: congruent, harmonious, consistent, compatible, matching|adjective
