overreservednesses:/ˌoʊvərˌrɪˈzɜːrvɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
过度拘谨；过分矜持
The state of being excessively reserved or cautious in one's behavior or speech.
Her overreservednesses made it difficult for her to make new friends. 她过分的矜持让她很难结交新朋友。
近义词: reticence, shyness, modesty, inhibition
反义词: boldness, outgoingness, extroversion
词性: 名词(plural)


Etymology:
over- (prefix) + reservedness + -es (plural suffix)

"over-" signifies "excessively" or "too much."
"reserved" comes from the Old French "reserver" meaning "to keep back, retain."  "Reservedness" is the noun form of "reserved."
"-es" forms the plural of the noun.
