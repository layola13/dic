masculinized:/ˈmæskjʊləˌnaɪzd/
adjective
男性化的，阳刚化的
Having characteristics traditionally associated with men; made more masculine.
The fashion industry often promotes a masculinized image of women.  时尚界经常宣传女性男性化的形象。
She adopted a masculinized persona to succeed in the male-dominated field. 为了在男性主导的领域取得成功，她采用了男性化的形象。
synonyms:mannish, masculine, virile
antonyms:feminine, womanly
