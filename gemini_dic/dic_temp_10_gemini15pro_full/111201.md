lithomarge:/ˈlɪθəˌmɑːrdʒ/
n.
lithomarge, a soft, compact claylike material
A smooth, compact, clay-like material, often brightly colored, found in association with mineral deposits.
Lithomarge is a type of clay often found near ore deposits. 它是一种通常在矿床附近发现的粘土。
The geologist identified the sample as lithomarge. 地质学家将该样本鉴定为锂皂石。
近义词:clay, kaolin
反义词:(None -  lacks a direct opposite)
词性:noun


Etymology:
From French *lithomarge*, from Ancient Greek *λίθος* (líthos, “stone”) + *μάργος* (márgos, “white clay, marl”).
