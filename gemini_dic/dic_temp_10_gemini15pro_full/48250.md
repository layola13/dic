decorates:/ˈdekəˌreɪts/ | v. | 装饰，装点 |  Makes something more attractive by adding ornament, colour, etc. | She decorates the Christmas tree with colorful ornaments. 她用彩色的装饰品装饰圣诞树。| adorns, embellishes, ornaments | strips, denudes | verb

decorates:/ˈdekəˌreɪts/ | v. | 授予…勋章 | Confers an award or medal on someone. | The Queen decorated him for his bravery. 女王因他的英勇而授予他勋章。| awards, honors | dishonors, degrades | verb

Etymology:  From Late Middle English *decoraten, from Latin decorāre (“to adorn, embellish, beautify”), from decōrus (“becoming, proper, handsome”).
