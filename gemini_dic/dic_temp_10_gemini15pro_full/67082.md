exploitative:/ɪkˈsplɔɪtətɪv/ |adj.|剥削的；利用的|Taking unfair advantage of a person or situation for personal gain.|例句：The company's exploitative practices towards its workers led to protests.  该公司对工人的剥削行为引发了抗议。

例句：The film explores the exploitative nature of the fashion industry. 这部电影探讨了时尚行业的剥削本质。

近义词：abusive, manipulative, predatory, selfish, unscrupulous|反义词：fair, just, ethical, considerate|词性: 形容词

exploit:/ɪkˈsplɔɪt/ |n. vt.|功绩；勋绩；利用；剥削|A bold or daring feat; make full use of and derive benefit from (a resource); use (a person or situation) in an unfair or selfish way.|例句：His wartime exploits were legendary. 他的战时功勋是传奇的。

例句：The company is accused of exploiting its workers.  该公司被指控剥削其工人。

近义词：feat, accomplishment, utilize, manipulate|反义词：squander, neglect, protect, respect|词性: 名词, 动词
