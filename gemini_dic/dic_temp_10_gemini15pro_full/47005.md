dashiki: /dəˈʃiːkiː/
noun
达西基, 一种宽松的, 通常色彩鲜艳的, 有装饰图案的衬衫或外衣, 源自西非.
A loose-fitting, often brightly colored, pullover garment, usually with an ornate collar and worn especially by men, of a style originating in West Africa.
He wore a brightly colored dashiki to the festival. 他穿着鲜艳的达西基去参加节日。
The dashiki is a symbol of African pride. 达西基是非洲自豪的象征。
近义词: shirt, tunic, robe
反义词: (无明确的反义词，可以考虑与正式服装对比) suit, tuxedo
词性: n.

Etymology:
From Yoruba *dàńṣíkì, a loose-fitting pullover garment.
