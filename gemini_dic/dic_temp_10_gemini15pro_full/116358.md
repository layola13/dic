masticador:/ˈmæstɪkeɪtər/ (US) /ˈmæstɪkeɪtə/ (UK)| n. |咀嚼肌; 咀嚼器|A muscle used for chewing; a device for chewing food.|The masseter muscle is the primary masticador in humans.  咬肌是人类主要的咀嚼肌。|The dentist recommended a masticador for patients with jaw problems. 牙医推荐下颌有问题患者使用咀嚼器。|Synonym: chewing muscle, mandible, jaw|Antonym: N/A|Noun

masticatory:/ˈmæstɪkətɔːri/ (US) /ˈmæstɪkətəri/ (UK) | adj. |咀嚼的|Relating to chewing.|The masticatory process breaks down food into smaller pieces. 咀嚼过程将食物分解成更小的碎片。|Masticatory problems can lead to digestive issues. 咀嚼问题会导致消化问题。|Synonym: chewing, grinding|Antonym: N/A|Adjective
