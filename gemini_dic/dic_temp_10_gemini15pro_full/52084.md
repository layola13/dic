diamagnet:/ˈdaɪəˌmæɡnət/| n. |抗磁体|A material that is not attracted to a magnet but is slightly repelled by it.  It is characterized by a small negative magnetic susceptibility. |Bismuth is a well-known diamagnet. 铋是一种著名的抗磁体。|The frog was levitated in the strong magnetic field because it is primarily composed of diamagnetic water.  青蛙在强磁场中悬浮起来，因为它主要由抗磁性的水组成。|近义词:non-magnetic material|反义词:ferromagnet, paramagnet|noun

diamagnetic:/ˌdaɪə.mæɡˈnɛt.ɪk/| adj. |抗磁性的|Relating to a diamagnet; having the property of diamagnetism.|The diamagnetic properties of water allow it to be slightly repelled by a magnetic field. 水的抗磁性使其在磁场中被轻微排斥。|The researchers studied the diamagnetic behavior of the new material. 研究人员研究了这种新材料的抗磁性。|近义词:non-magnetic|反义词:ferromagnetic, paramagnetic|adjective
