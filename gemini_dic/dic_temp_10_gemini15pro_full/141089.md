onrush:/ˈɒnrʌʃ/ (UK), /ˈɑːnrʌʃ/ (US)| n.|猛冲，奔流，涌现|A sudden rapid advance or increase.  A powerful forward surge.|The onrush of the flood swept away everything in its path. 洪水奔涌而下，冲走了它路径上的一切。|近义词：surge, rush, influx, torrent, flood, avalanche|反义词: retreat, ebb, decline|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From on + rush.  "On" in this context intensifies the meaning of "rush," suggesting a particularly powerful or overwhelming forward movement.
