overtreats:/ˌoʊvərˈtriːts/| v.|过度治疗|To provide excessive or unnecessary medical treatment.|The doctor overtreats his patients, often prescribing medications they don't need. 医生过度治疗他的病人，经常开一些他们不需要的药物。|例句：Some doctors overtreat patients out of fear of lawsuits.  有些医生因为害怕被起诉而过度治疗病人。

例句：Overtreating a minor infection can lead to antibiotic resistance.  过度治疗轻微感染会导致抗生素耐药性。|近义词：overmedicate, overprescribe|反义词:undertreat, neglect|词性:动词
