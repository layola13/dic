misdirection:/ˌmɪsdɪˈrɛkʃən/ /ˌmɪsdəˈrɛkʃən/| n.|误导，转移注意力|the act of directing someone or something to the wrong place or in the wrong direction|The magician's skillful misdirection fooled the entire audience. 魔术师娴熟的误导技巧骗过了所有观众。|synonyms: deception, distraction, diversion, subterfuge|antonyms: guidance, direction, honesty, truth|noun

misdirection:/ˌmɪsdɪˈrɛkʃən/ /ˌmɪsdəˈrɛkʃən/| n.|错误的指示|an incorrect instruction or direction|Due to a misdirection from the GPS, we ended up miles away from our destination. 由于GPS的错误指示，我们最终离目的地差了几英里。|synonyms: misguidance, misinstruction|antonyms: correct direction, proper guidance|noun
