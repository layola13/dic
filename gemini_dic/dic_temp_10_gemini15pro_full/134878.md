nonmortally:/nɒnˈmɔːrtəli/ | adv. | 非致命地；不危及生命地 |  Not in a way that causes death; in a way that does not involve death. |  The soldier was nonmortally wounded in the battle.  这名士兵在战斗中受了非致命伤。|  The injury, thankfully, was nonmortally inflicted. 谢天谢地，伤势不致命。 | 近义词: harmlessly, safely | 反义词: fatally, mortally | 词性: 副词

词源:  Formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the adjective "mortal" (meaning "subject to death"), and then adding the adverbial suffix "-ly".  由表示“不”的前缀“non-”加到形容词“mortal”（意思是“会死的”）上，然后加上副词后缀“-ly”构成。
