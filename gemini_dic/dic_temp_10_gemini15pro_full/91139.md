hybridize:/ˈhaɪbrɪˌdaɪz/| v. |杂交，混合|To crossbreed or combine different varieties or species of plants or animals to create a hybrid; or to combine different elements or styles.|Scientists have hybridized different species of corn to create more resistant strains. 科学家们杂交了不同种类的玉米，以培育出更具抗性的品种。

The band hybridizes rock and electronic music.  这个乐队将摇滚乐和电子音乐混合在一起。|近义词：crossbreed, interbreed, blend, merge, mix, combine|反义词:separate, segregate|词性:动词

hybridization:/ˌhaɪbrɪdɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |杂交，混合|The process of hybridizing.|Hybridization is a common technique in agriculture. 杂交是农业中常用的技术。

The hybridization of different musical styles created a unique sound. 不同音乐风格的融合创造了一种独特的声音。|近义词：crossbreeding, interbreeding, blending, merging, mixing, combination|反义词:separation, segregation|词性:名词
