brainer:/ˈbreɪnər/| n.|容易的事情；不用动脑筋的事|Something that requires very little thought; an easy decision or problem.|Choosing vanilla ice cream is a no-brainer for me.  对我来说，选择香草冰淇淋是不费脑筋的事。|Synonyms: cinch, piece of cake, child's play, easy peasy|Antonyms: challenge, difficult task, puzzle, conundrum|noun
