director: /dəˈrɛktər/ (UK), /dɪˈrɛktər/ (US)| n.| 导演；主管；理事；董事|A person who is in charge of an activity, department, or organization; a person who supervises the making of a film. | The director yelled "Cut!" after the scene.  导演在场景结束后大喊“卡！”| The director of marketing has a lot of experience. 市场总监经验丰富。 | synonyms: manager, supervisor, administrator, executive, superintendent, leader, conductor, overseer, boss, head, chief | antonyms: subordinate, employee, follower | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *director, from Latin directus (past participle of dirigere ‘to direct’), from di- ‘apart’ + regere ‘to guide, rule’.
