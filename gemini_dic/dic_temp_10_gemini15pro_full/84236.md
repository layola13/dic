half-shoddy:/ˌhæfˈʃɒdi/ | adj. | 半劣质的；略显粗制滥造的 | Made of or containing materials of poor quality, but not entirely so.  Appearing partly cheap or poorly made. | The furniture was half-shoddy, with some pieces looking well-made and others clearly made with inferior materials.  这些家具半劣质的，有些看起来做工精良，有些则明显是用劣质材料制成的。 |  例句：The jacket looked half-shoddy, with loose threads and mismatched buttons.  这件夹克看起来半劣质的，线头松散，纽扣也不配套。 | 近义词：second-rate, inferior, partly substandard | 反义词：high-quality, superior, first-rate | 词性：形容词

half:/hæf/ | n. adj. adv. | 一半，半个 | One of two equal parts of something. |  例句：I'll take half of the pizza. 我要一半披萨。| 近义词： moiety, one-half | 反义词： whole, double | 词性：名词，形容词，副词

shoddy:/ˈʃɒdi/ | adj. n. | 劣质的；粗制滥造的；赝品 | Badly and cheaply made. |  例句：The workmanship on this bag is really shoddy.  这个包的做工真的很差。| 近义词：inferior, cheap, substandard | 反义词：superior, high-quality, well-made | 词性：形容词，名词
