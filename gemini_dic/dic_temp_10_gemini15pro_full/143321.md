outearning:/ˌaʊtˈɜːrnɪŋ/

outearning:/ˌaʊtˈɜːrnɪŋ/
adjective |  收入更高的| Having or producing a higher income. |  She is the outearning member of the family. 她是家庭中收入最高的成员。| synonyms: higher-earning | antonyms: lower-earning | adj.
