nonkinetic:/ˌnɒnkaɪˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
非动能的; 非运动的
Not involving or producing motion.
例句：
The statue's beauty was nonkinetic, relying on form and material rather than movement.  这尊雕像的美是非动能的，依靠的是形式和材质而不是运动。

The potential energy stored in the battery is nonkinetic until the circuit is closed. 储存在电池中的势能是非动能的，直到电路闭合。

近义词：static, motionless, still, inert
反义词：kinetic, dynamic, active, moving
词性：adjective
