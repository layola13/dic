hallowed:/ˈhæləʊd/ (UK) /ˈhæloʊd/ (US)| adj.|神圣的，受崇敬的|Made holy; consecrated; revered.|例句：The hallowed halls of the university. 大学神圣的殿堂。|例句：Hallowed be thy name. 愿人都尊你的名为圣。(主祷文)|近义词：sacred, holy, revered, blessed, consecrated|反义词：profane, unholy, desecrated|词性:形容词
词源: From Old English *hālgod, past participle of hālgian (“to hallow”), from Proto-Germanic *hailagōjaną (“to make holy”), from *hailagaz (“holy”). Cognate with Old Norse helga (“to hallow”), Old High German heilagōn (“to hallow”), Gothic 𐌷𐌰𐌹𐌻𐌰⠛𐌾𐌰𐌽 (hailagjan, “to hallow”). Related to holy.
