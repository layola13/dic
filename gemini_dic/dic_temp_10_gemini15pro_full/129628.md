non-contingent:/nɒn kənˈtɪndʒənt/| adj.|非偶然的；无条件的；必然的；不视情况而定的|Not dependent on or conditioned by something else; absolute.  Not subject to chance or unforeseen circumstances.|Her salary was non-contingent, meaning she received the same amount each month regardless of her sales figures. 她的薪水是无条件的，这意味着无论她的销售额如何，她每个月都能拿到相同的金额。

The funding for the project is non-contingent and will be available regardless of economic conditions.  该项目的资金是非偶然的，无论经济状况如何，都将可以使用。|近义词：absolute, unconditional, guaranteed, certain, independent|反义词: contingent, conditional, dependent, uncertain|词性:形容词
