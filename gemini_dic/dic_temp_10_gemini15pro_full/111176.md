lithoglyph: /ˈlɪθəɡlɪf/ | n. | 岩画，石刻 | A carving or inscription on a rock surface. |  Example 1: The archaeologists discovered ancient lithoglyphs depicting hunting scenes. 考古学家发现了描绘狩猎场景的古代岩画。

Example 2: The lithoglyphs on the cave walls told the story of the tribe's history. 洞穴墙壁上的岩画讲述了部落的历史。| 近义词: petroglyph, rock carving, inscription | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "blank rock face") | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *lithos* (stone) + *glyphein* (to carve).
