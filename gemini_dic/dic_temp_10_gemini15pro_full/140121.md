off-site:/ˌɒfˈsaɪt/ /ˌɔːfˈsaɪt/| adj. adv.|场外的，异地的|Located or taking place away from the main site or premises. |例句：The company held an off-site meeting at a nearby hotel.  公司在附近一家酒店举行了一次场外会议。|近义词：external, remote|反义词:on-site|词性:形容词/副词

off-site:/ˌɒfˈsaɪt/ /ˌɔːfˈsaɪt/| n.|场外，异地|A location away from the main site or premises. |例句：The team went to an off-site for team building activities.  团队去了一个异地进行团队建设活动。|近义词：external location, remote location|反义词:on-site location|词性:名词


词源: "off" + "site".  "Off" indicating separation or removal, and "site" referring to a place or location.
