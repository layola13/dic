boastful: /ˈboʊstfəl/ (US), /ˈbəʊstfəl/ (UK)| adj.|爱吹嘘的，自夸的|Given to boasting; bragging; prone to praising oneself excessively.  Characterized by or exhibiting boastfulness.|He was boastful about his achievements, constantly reminding everyone of his success. 他总是吹嘘自己的成就，不断提醒大家他的成功。|Synonyms: bragging, swaggering, vainglorious, arrogant, conceited|Antonyms: humble, modest, unassuming|adjective

boastingly: /ˈboʊstɪŋli/ (US), /ˈbəʊstɪŋli/ (UK)| adv.|自夸地，吹嘘地|In a boastful manner; with bragging or ostentation.|He boastingly announced his victory in the competition. 他自夸地宣布了他在比赛中的胜利。|Synonyms: swaggeringly, arrogantly, proudly (often excessively)|Antonyms: humbly, modestly, quietly|adverb

boast: /boʊst/ (US), /bəʊst/ (UK)| n. v.|n. 自夸，夸口；v. 自夸，夸耀|n.  An act of boasting; a brag. v. To speak with excessive pride and self-satisfaction about one's achievements, possessions, or abilities.|He made boasts about his wealth. 他吹嘘自己的财富。  She boasted that she could speak five languages. 她夸口说她会说五种语言。|Synonyms: brag, vaunt, crow|Antonyms: downplay, minimize, deprecate|noun, verb
