craftier:/ˈkræftiər/ /ˈkrɑːftiər/| adj.|更狡猾的，更精巧的|Comparative form of crafty: more crafty.  Showing skill and cunning, especially in deceiving others; more skilled in handicraft.|He's a craftier negotiator than I gave him credit for. 他比我想象中更是一个狡猾的谈判者。|The thief devised a craftier method to bypass the security system. 小偷设计了一种更巧妙的方法来绕过安全系统。|近义词：sly, cunning, wily, artful, ingenious|反义词：honest, guileless, naive, artless, clumsy|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *crafti, from Old English cræftig (“strong, powerful, skillful, cunning, crafty”), from Proto-Germanic *kraftigaz (“strong”), from *kraftō (“strength”). Cognate with Scots craftie (“crafty, clever”), West Frisian kreftich (“powerful, strong, vigorous”), Dutch krachtig (“powerful, strong, vigorous”), German kräftig (“strong, powerful, vigorous”).  The sense development in English proceeded from "strong" to "skillful" to "cunning".
