imitancy:/ˈɪmɪtənsi/ | n. | 模仿；效仿；类似 | The state or quality of being imitant; resemblance.  The imitancy of the artificial flower to the real one was remarkable.  人造花与真花的相似度非常高。 |  例句：The child's imitancy of his father's mannerisms was amusing. 孩子模仿父亲的举止很滑稽。

例句：The imitancy of the computer-generated image to a photograph was uncanny.  电脑生成的图像与照片的相似度不可思议。 | 近义词： imitation, resemblance, similarity, likeness | 反义词： originality, uniqueness, difference, distinctiveness | 词性：名词


词源：From imitant + -cy.  Imitant (adjective) comes from the Latin imitans, present participle of imitari "to imitate," from imit-, variant stem of imago "image, likeness."
