inflected:/ɪnˈflɛktɪd/ | adj. | 屈折的，变形的 |  A word is inflected when its form changes to show grammatical information such as tense, number, or person. | The verb "run" is inflected as "runs" in the third person singular present tense.  动词"run"在第三人称单数现在时中变形为"runs"。| inflected forms, conjugated | uninflected | adjective


inflect:/ɪnˈflɛkt/ | v. | 使(词)变形，屈折变化；改变(声音)的音调 | To change the form of a word to show grammatical information such as tense, number, or person; to modulate the tone of one's voice. | She inflected her voice to emphasize the importance of her words. 她变换声调以强调她话语的重要性。| conjugate, modify, vary | maintain, fix | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *inflectere*, from *in-* (into, towards) + *flectere* (to bend).
