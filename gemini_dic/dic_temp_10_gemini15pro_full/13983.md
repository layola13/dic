aurichalcite:/ɔːrɪˈkælsaɪt/
n.
绿铜锌矿
A pale-blue to bluish-green secondary mineral consisting of a basic carbonate of zinc and copper, occurring in oxidized zones of zinc and copper deposits.
Aurichalcite is often found as a crust on other minerals. 绿铜锌矿常以壳状产于其他矿物上。

Aurichalcite specimens can be quite beautiful and are prized by collectors.  绿铜锌矿标本非常漂亮，受到收藏家的珍视。

近义词: rosasite (化学成分和外观相似)
反义词:  无 (矿物通常没有反义词)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Aurichalcit*, from Latin *aurum* ("gold") + Greek *χαλκός* (*chalkos*) ("copper, bronze") + *-ite* (mineral suffix), referring to its golden-green color.  源于德语 *Aurichalcit*，来自拉丁语 *aurum*（“黄金”）+ 希腊语 *χαλκός* (*chalkos*)（“铜、青铜”）+ *-ite*（矿物后缀），指其金绿色。
