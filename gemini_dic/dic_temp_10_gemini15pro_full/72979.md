foolhardily:/ˈfuːlhɑːrdɪli/ | adv. | 鲁莽地；轻率地 | In a foolhardy manner; recklessly; without due consideration of the consequences. | He foolhardily climbed the mountain without any safety equipment. 他鲁莽地爬山，没有任何安全设备。 |  She foolhardily invested all her savings in a risky venture. 她轻率地把所有积蓄都投资到一个风险很大的企业。 | 近义词: recklessly, rashly, imprudently, unwisely | 反义词: cautiously, prudently, wisely, carefully | 词性: 副词

词源: From fool + hardy, with the suffix -ly added to form an adverb.  Fool 指的是“愚蠢的”，hardy 指的是“大胆的，勇敢的”。 foolhardy 最初的意思是“愚蠢地勇敢”，后来演变成“鲁莽的，轻率的”。
