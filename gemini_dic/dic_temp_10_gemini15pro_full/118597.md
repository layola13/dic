mesquite:/mɛˈskiːt/ | n. | 牧豆树；牧豆树木材 | A thorny tree or shrub of the genus *Prosopis*, native to arid regions of North and South America, having small flowers and long pods containing sweet pulp. |  The mesquite tree provides shade and food in the desert.  牧豆树在沙漠中提供阴凉和食物。| synonym:  prosopis | antonym: N/A | noun

mesquites:/mɛˈskiːts/ | n. | 牧豆树（复数）| Plural form of mesquite, referring to multiple trees or shrubs of the genus *Prosopis*. | Mesquites are common in the southwestern United States.  牧豆树在美国西南部很常见。| synonym: prosopis trees | antonym: N/A | noun
