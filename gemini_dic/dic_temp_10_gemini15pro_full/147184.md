overluxuriant: /ˌoʊvərˌlʌɡˈʒʊəriənt/ | adj. | 过于繁茂的，过于奢华的 | Characterized by excessive luxuriance or richness; excessively ornate or elaborate. | The overluxuriant vegetation of the rainforest made it difficult to navigate.  雨林中过于繁茂的植被使得通行变得困难。| The mansion's overluxuriant decor was considered by some to be gaudy. 这座豪宅过于奢华的装饰被一些人认为是俗气的。 |  近义词: rampant, profuse, exuberant | 反义词: sparse, meager, restrained | 词性: 形容词

词源:
over- (prefix) + luxuriant (adjective)
"over-" 表示 "过度"，"过于"
"luxuriant" 来自拉丁语 "luxuriare"，意思是 "繁茂生长"，"奢侈"
