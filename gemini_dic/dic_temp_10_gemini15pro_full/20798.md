blarneying:/ˈblɑːrniɪŋ/
verb
哄骗；花言巧语地劝诱
persuading someone to do something by using flattery or other clever means.
He blarneyed his way into the exclusive club. 他花言巧语地进入了那个高级俱乐部。
近义词:cajole, coax, wheedle
反义词:coerce, force
词性:动词

词源: From Irish *blárnaim ‘flatter’, from blárnaí ‘flatterer’
