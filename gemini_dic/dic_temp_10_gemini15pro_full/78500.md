ghat:/ɡɑːt/| n.|河坛，(印度教徒沐浴的)石梯,山路，山口|A flight of steps leading down to a river, especially in India, used by bathers.  A mountain pass.|We descended the ghat to the river. 我们沿着石梯下到河边。 The caravan traversed the treacherous ghat. 商队穿越了危险的山口。|Near synonyms: steps, stairway, staircase, ramp, incline, slope (for the riverbank sense); pass, mountain pass (for the mountain sense)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps ascent for the mountain sense)|noun


ghat:/ɡɑːt/| n.|山脉，尤指印度西部|A range of mountains, especially in western India.|The Western Ghats are a biodiversity hotspot. 西高止山脉是一个生物多样性热点地区。|Near synonyms: mountain range, mountains, highlands, hills|Antonyms: valley, plain, lowlands|noun
