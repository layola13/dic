malapropism: /ˈmæləˌprɒpɪzəm/ | n. |  误用词; 用词错误 | the unintentional misuse of a word by confusion with one that sounds similar |  "dance a flamingo " (instead of flamenco) is a malapropism. | 近义词: solecism, catachresis, misnomer | 反义词:  correct usage, proper diction | 词性: noun
