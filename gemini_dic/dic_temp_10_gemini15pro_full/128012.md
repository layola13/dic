nephron: /ˈnɛfrɒn/ (UK) /ˈnɛfrɑːn/ (US) | n. | 肾单位 |  Each of the functional units in the kidney, consisting of a glomerulus and its associated tubule, through which the glomerular filtrate passes before emerging as urine. |  Each kidney contains about a million nephrons. 每个肾脏包含大约一百万个肾单位。|  The nephrons filter waste products from the blood. 肾单位过滤血液中的废物。 | synonyms: renal corpuscle, renal unit | antonyms: N/A | noun
