interaccessory:/ɪntərækˈsɛsəri/| adj.|互相附属的，相互依赖的|Relating to or involving two or more accessory structures that are connected or related to each other.  It often implies a functional dependence or interaction between these structures.|The interaccessory muscles of the larynx work together to control vocal cord tension.|近义词：interrelated, interdependent, interconnected|反义词：independent, separate|词性:形容词
词源: "inter-" (between) + "accessory" (something added).
