birri:/ˈbɪri/| n.|澳洲土著的一种回旋镖|A type of boomerang used by Aboriginal Australians. It is non-returning and used for hunting.|He skillfully hunted kangaroos with his birri.|Synonym: boomerang (though not all boomerangs are birris), throwing stick|Antonym: N/A|noun
