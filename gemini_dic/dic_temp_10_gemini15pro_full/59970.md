edifies: /ˈɛdɪˌfaɪz/
Verb
教诲，启迪，陶冶
Instructs or improves (someone) morally or intellectually.
The sermon edified the congregation. 这篇布道陶冶了会众。
Synonyms: instructs, improves, uplifts, enlightens, inspires
Antonyms: corrupts, degrades, misleads, demoralizes
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English edifie, from Old French edifier, from Latin aedificāre (“to build”), from aedis (“house, temple”) + -ficāre (from facere, “to make, do”).  The original sense of "build up" later shifted to "build up morally."
