advocatrice: /ˈædvəkətrɪs/ | n. | 女拥护者，女提倡者 | A female advocate; a woman who pleads the cause of another, especially one who pleads for the rights of women. |  She was a fierce advocatrice for women's suffrage. 她是一位激烈的妇女参政权拥护者。

She was a tireless advocatrice for the poor and marginalized. 她是一位不知疲倦地为穷人和边缘人群辩护的人。 | 近义词: proponent, supporter, champion, defender | 反义词: opponent, antagonist, detractor | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *advocatrice*, feminine of *advocat*, from Latin *advocatus*, past participle of *advocare* "to summon," from *ad-* "to" + *vocare* "to call."  The feminine form is less common in modern English, with "advocate" often used for both genders.
