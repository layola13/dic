bopping:/ˈbɒpɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈbɑːpɪŋ/ (US)| adj. vi.| 跳跃的，有节奏地摆动的; 欢快地跳舞 | moving or dancing rhythmically, especially to pop music; enjoying oneself by dancing|  例句：The music was bopping, and everyone was having a good time.  音乐节奏欢快，每个人都玩得很开心。

例句：She was bopping along to the music on her headphones. 她跟着耳机里的音乐节奏摇摆。


近义词：dancing, grooving, jiving, rocking|反义词：still, motionless|词性：形容词/动词的现在分词
