dialoguers:/ˈdaɪəlɔːɡərz/
n.
对话者，交谈者
People who engage in a dialogue; those who converse with each other.
The dialoguers reached a compromise after hours of discussion.  对话者经过数小时的讨论后达成了妥协。
The dialoguers explored the complex issues with respect and openness.  对话者以尊重和开放的态度探讨了复杂的问题。
近义词:interlocutors, conversants, discussants
反义词:listeners, audience
词性:名词
