feted:/ˈfeɪtɪd/ /ˈfɛtɪd/| adj.|盛宴款待的，备受赞扬的|Honored or entertained lavishly; celebrated.|例句：The returning hero was feted at a grand banquet. 凯旋的英雄在盛大的宴会上受到了款待。

例句：The award-winning author was feted by her fans. 获奖作家受到粉丝的追捧。

近义词：celebrated, honored, acclaimed, lauded|反义词：ignored, overlooked, neglected|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *fêter*, "to celebrate," from Old French *feste*, "feast, celebration," from Vulgar Latin *festa*, from Latin *festum*, "festival, holiday."
