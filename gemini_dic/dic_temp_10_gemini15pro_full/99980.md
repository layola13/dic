interownerships:/ˌɪntərˈoʊnərʃɪps/
n.
相互持股，交叉持股
The situation where two or more companies own shares in each other.
Example: The complex interownerships between the companies made it difficult to determine who controlled the group.  这些公司之间复杂的交叉持股关系使得很难确定谁控制了该集团。
Example: Interownerships can create stability within a business network, but also reduce competition. 相互持股可以在商业网络中创造稳定性，但也减少了竞争。
Synonym: cross-holdings, mutual ownership
Antonym: sole ownership, independent ownership
