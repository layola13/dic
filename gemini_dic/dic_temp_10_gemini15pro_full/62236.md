encrust:/ɪnˈkrʌst/ | vt. | 包裹；覆盖 | To cover something with a hard outer layer. | The old ship's hull was encrusted with barnacles. 老船的船体上覆盖着藤壶。 | The jewels were encrusted in gold. 这些珠宝镶嵌在黄金中。| 近义词：coat, cover, overlay | 反义词：bare, expose, strip | verb

encrustation:/ɪnˌkrʌˈsteɪʃən/ | n. | 外壳；硬壳 | A hard outer layer or coating. | The encrustation on the kettle was difficult to remove. 水壶上的水垢很难去除。 |  The archaeologists carefully removed the encrustation from the ancient artifact. 考古学家小心翼翼地从古代文物上清除硬壳。 | 近义词：coating, crust, layer | 反义词: core, interior | noun
