episcopal:/ɪˈpɪskəpəl/
adjective
主教的；圣公会的
Relating to a bishop or bishops; of or relating to the Episcopal Church.
The episcopal church is holding a service this Sunday. 圣公会教堂本周日举行礼拜。
He received an episcopal blessing. 他接受了主教的祝福。
近义词:bishoply, hierarchical
反义词:lay, secular
词性:adjective


词源:
Late Middle English: from late Latin episcopalis, from episcopus ‘bishop’ (see bishop).
