coprincipal:/koʊˈprɪnsɪpəl/ | adj. n.| 共同负责人，共同本金人 |A person or entity that shares equal responsibility or authority with another, particularly in financial or legal matters.  A coprincipal can also refer to a loan or investment where two or more parties share the principal amount equally.| John and Mary are coprincipals on the mortgage.  约翰和玛丽是抵押贷款的共同负责人。|The two banks acted as coprincipals in the loan syndication. 这两家银行在贷款银团中担任共同主要银行。| synonym: joint principal, co-obligor | antonym: subordinate, secondary | adjective, noun
