granado:/ɡrəˈnɑːdoʊ/ /ɡræˈnɑːdəʊ/ | adj. | 石榴色的；多籽的; 充满的 | Of a deep red color, like that of a pomegranate; full of seeds or grains. | The curtains were a rich, granado hue. 窗帘是浓郁的石榴色。 | 近义词: pomegranate-colored, crimson, scarlet | 反义词: pale, colorless | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Spanish *granado*, from Latin *grānātus* "having many grains/seeds", from *grānum* "grain, seed".
