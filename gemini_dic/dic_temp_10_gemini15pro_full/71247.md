fitz:/fɪts/| n.|私生子；庶子|An illegitimate son, especially of a nobleman.|例句：In the historical novel, the duke's fitz sought to claim his rightful inheritance. 在这部历史小说中，公爵的私生子试图争取他应得的遗产。

例句：Fitzwilliam Darcy, a prominent character in Pride and Prejudice, is often referred to as "Fitz" by his close friends.  在《傲慢与偏见》中，重要的角色菲茨威廉·达西经常被他的密友称为“菲茨”。|近义词：bastard, illegitimate child|反义词:legitimate child|Noun
