noveldom:/ˈnɒvəldəm/ (US) /ˈnɒvəldəm/ (UK)| n. |小说界；小说领域|The world or realm of novels; the sphere of activity or interest associated with novel writing and reading.  It encompasses authors, publishers, critics, and readers of novels. |Her latest book has cemented her place in noveldom.  她最新的书巩固了她在小说界的地位。|He's a well-known figure in noveldom. 他在小说界是一位知名人物。|近义词：literary world, fiction world|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "non-fiction world") |词性: noun
