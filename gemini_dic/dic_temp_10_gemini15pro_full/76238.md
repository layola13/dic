futurist:/ˈfjuːtʃərɪst/| n. | 未来学家；未来主义者 | Someone who studies the future and makes predictions about it; an advocate of futurism (especially in art). |例句：The futurist predicted a world of flying cars.  这位未来学家预测了一个飞行汽车的世界。

例句：Many futurists believe that technology will solve most of our problems. 许多未来主义者认为科技将解决我们大部分的问题。 | 近义词：visionary, prophet, forecaster | 反义词：traditionalist, conservative | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *futuriste*, from Italian *futurista*, from *futuro* "future," from Latin *futurus*, future participle of *esse* "to be."
