meetcute:/ˈmiːtˌkjuːt/
n.
偶遇；巧遇；美好的邂逅
A humorous or charming first encounter between two people that leads to or is meant to lead to a romantic relationship.
例句：The movie opens with a classic meetcute in a coffee shop.  这部电影以咖啡店里一个经典的偶遇开场。

例句：Their meetcute involved a spilled latte and a shared laugh. 他们的偶遇包括一杯洒出的拿铁和一阵共同的笑声。

近义词：chance encounter, accidental meeting, cute meeting
反义词:planned meeting
