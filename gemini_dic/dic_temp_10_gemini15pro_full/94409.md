implication:/ˌɪmplɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 含义，暗示，牵连，卷入 | something that is suggested, or happens, indirectly | The implication of his statement was that he was not happy with the decision. 他的话暗示他对这个决定不满意。 | synonyms: consequence, significance, connotation, ramification | antonyms: explicit statement, direct meaning | noun

implication:/ˌɪmplɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 牵涉，牵连 | the fact of being involved in something bad | The police said there was no implication of the government in the scandal. 警方表示政府与此丑闻无关。| synonyms: involvement, incrimination | antonyms: exoneration, acquittal | noun
