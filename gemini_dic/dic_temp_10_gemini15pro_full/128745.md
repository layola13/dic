niggardous:/ˈnɪɡərdəs/| adj.|吝啬的，小气的|Not generous; stingy or miserly.  This word is now largely avoided due to its phonetic similarity to a racial slur.|He was niggardous with his praise, offering only a grudging nod.|近义词：stingy, miserly, parsimonious, frugal, penurious|反义词：generous, liberal, prodigal|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *nigard*, *nygart*, from Old Norse *hnǫggr* (“stingy”) + *-ard*. Cognate with Icelandic *hnöggur* (“stingy”), Swedish *njugg* (“stingy, close-fisted”), Danish *nøjsom* (“frugal”).  The "-ard" suffix is pejorative.
