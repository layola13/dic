denticulation:/dɛnˌtɪkjʊˈleɪʃən/| n.|小齿状，细齿，锯齿状|A small notch or tooth-like projection, especially along the edge of something.  A series of such projections.|The denticulation on the leaf's edge helped identify the plant.|近义词：serration, indentation, notching|反义词:smoothness|noun

denticulate:/dɛnˈtɪkjʊlət/| adj.|有小齿的，锯齿状的|Having small tooth-like projections or notches, serrated.|The denticulate edge of the stamp made it difficult to forge.|近义词：serrated, toothed, notched|反义词:smooth|adjective
