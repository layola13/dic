lipides:/ˈlɪpɪdz/ | n. | 脂质 | A broad group of naturally occurring molecules which include fats, waxes, sterols, fat-soluble vitamins (such as vitamins A, D, E, and K), monoglycerides, diglycerides, phospholipids, and others. | Lipids are essential for cell function. 脂质对细胞功能至关重要。|  Lipids store energy in the body. 脂质在体内储存能量。| synonyms: fats, oils | antonyms: carbohydrates, proteins | noun
