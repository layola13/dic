aoristic:/ˌeɪəˈrɪstɪk/
| adj.| 无时间的；未完成体的；不定过去的 |Referring to an action without indicating its completion or duration.|The aoristic narrative style conveys a sense of immediacy.  这种无时间叙事风格传达了一种即时感。|Synonyms: timeless, undefined, unspecified|Antonyms: specific, defined, completed|adjective
