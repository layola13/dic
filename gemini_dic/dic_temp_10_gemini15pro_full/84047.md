half-fictitiously:/ˌhæf fɪkˈtɪʃəsli/
| adv. |半虚构地|Partly fictional; based partly on fact and partly on imagination.|His memoir was half-fictitiously based on his time in the war, embellishing certain events for dramatic effect.  他的回忆录半虚构地基于他在战争中的经历，为了戏剧效果而润色了某些事件。|例句：The story is half-fictitiously based on real events.  这个故事半虚构地基于真实事件。

例句：He half-fictitiously recounted his adventures, blending truth with exaggeration. 他半虚构地讲述了他的冒险经历，将真相与夸张交织在一起。|近义词：semi-fictional, partly invented, partially imagined|反义词：factual, true, real|词性：副词
