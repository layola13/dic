hallmarking:/ˈhɔːlmɑːkɪŋ/
n.
官方鉴定；品质证明；特征
the official testing of the purity of gold, silver, and platinum  The assay office is responsible for the hallmarking of precious metals.  鉴定所负责贵金属的品质鉴定。
The hallmarking of the product guaranteed its quality.  产品的官方鉴定保证了它的质量。
authentication, certification, verification
counterfeiting, imitation

词性: 名词
