harker:/ˈhɑːrkər/ (US), /ˈhɑːkə(r)/ (UK)| n.|倾听者，听众；侦察员，密探|Someone who listens attentively; a spy or scout.|The harker strained to hear the faint sound in the distance. 倾听者努力想听到远处微弱的声音。|例句：
The old harker sat by the fire, listening to the tales of the travelers. 老听众坐在火炉旁，听着旅行者的故事。

She worked as a harker for the intelligence agency, gathering information from unsuspecting targets.  她作为情报机构的侦察员，从毫无戒心的目标那里收集信息。

|近义词：listener, eavesdropper, scout, spy|反义词:speaker, talker|词性:noun
