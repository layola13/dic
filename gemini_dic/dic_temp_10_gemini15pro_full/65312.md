eudoxian:/juːˈdɒksiən/ |adj.|欧多克索斯的，与欧多克索斯有关的；(尤指)支持或涉及他宇宙结构理论的|Relating to or characteristic of Eudoxus of Cnidus (a Greek astronomer and mathematician) especially supporting or involving his theory of the structure of the universe.|例句：The eudoxian model of the universe was influential for centuries. 欧多克索斯的宇宙模型影响了几个世纪。

例句：His eudoxian approach to geometry was groundbreaking. 他对几何的欧多克索斯式方法是开创性的。|近义词：geocentric (in the context of cosmology)|反义词:  heliocentric (in the context of cosmology)|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Eudoxus (Greek astronomer and mathematician) + -ian (suffix forming adjectives).  Eudoxus's name derives from the Greek elements "eu" (good) and "doxa" (glory, reputation).
