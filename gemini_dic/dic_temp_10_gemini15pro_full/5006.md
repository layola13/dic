all-americas:/ɔːl-əˈmɛrɪkəz/| adj. n.|全美明星阵容的；全美明星运动员；|A team or athlete selected as being the best in the United States or the Americas in a particular sport.|The coach announced the all-americas team for college football.|近义词：all-star, top-ranked|反义词: worst, bottom-ranked|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology:
"all-" (meaning "completely" or "entirely") + "Americas" (referring to the United States or sometimes the entire American continents).  The term originated in the late 19th century, primarily in the context of college sports.
