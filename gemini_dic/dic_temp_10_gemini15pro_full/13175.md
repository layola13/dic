at-cost:/æt kɔːst/
| adj. | 成本价的，按成本计算的 | Sold or offered for sale at the price it cost to produce or acquire. |  We offer at-cost pricing for non-profit organizations. 我们为非营利组织提供成本价。| Synonyms: at cost price, at prime cost | Antonyms: profitable, marked-up | adjective


at-cost:/æt kɔːst/
| adv. | 按成本地 | At the cost price. | The company decided to sell its excess inventory at-cost. 公司决定按成本价出售多余库存。| Synonyms: at cost price, at prime cost | Antonyms: profitably, at a markup | adverb


Etymology:
"at" (Old English æt) meaning "at, by, near" + "cost" (from Old French cost, ultimately from Latin constare "stand firm, stand together, cost")
