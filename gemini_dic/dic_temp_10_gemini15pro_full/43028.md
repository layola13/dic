cowered:/ˈkaʊərd/| v.|畏缩，蜷缩，退缩|To crouch down in fear.|The dog cowered under the table during the thunderstorm.  狗在雷雨中畏缩在桌子底下。|近义词：cringe, shrink, recoil|反义词:brave, face, confront|词性:动词
Etymology: From Middle English *cowren, from Old Norse *kura, meaning "to lie in wait, crouch."
