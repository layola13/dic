beneficiary:/ˌbenɪˈfɪʃiəri/| n.|受益人，受惠者|A person or organization that receives money or other benefits from something.|The beneficiaries of the will received a substantial sum of money. 遗嘱的受益人获得了一大笔钱。|synonyms: recipient, heir, legatee|antonyms: benefactor, donor|noun

beneficiary:/ˌbenɪˈfɪʃiəri/| adj.|受益的，受惠的|Receiving or entitled to receive money or other benefits.|The beneficiary countries received substantial aid. 受益国获得了大量援助。|synonyms: recipient, favored|antonyms: donating, contributing|adjective
