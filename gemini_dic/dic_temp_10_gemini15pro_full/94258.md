impermeability:/ˌɪmpɜːrmiəˈbɪləti/ /ˌɪmpɜːrmiəˈbɪlədi/| n. |不可渗透性；不透水性|The state or quality of being impermeable; not allowing fluids or other substances to pass through.|The raincoat's impermeability kept me dry in the downpour. 这件雨衣的不透水性让我在大雨中保持干燥。|近义词：impenetrableness, tightness|反义词: permeability, porosity|词性:noun

impermeable:/ɪmˈpɜːrmiəbəl/ /ɪmˈpɜːrmiəbəl/| adj. |不可渗透的；不透水的|Not allowing fluid to pass through.|The membrane is impermeable to water.  这层膜不透水。|近义词：impenetrable, waterproof, tight|反义词: permeable, porous|词性:adjective
