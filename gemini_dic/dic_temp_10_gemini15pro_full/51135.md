despoil:/dɪˈspɔɪl/| vt.|掠夺，蹂躏，破坏|To deprive of something valuable by force; rob; plunder.  To strip of possessions, ornaments, etc.; pillage. |The invaders despoiled the village of its treasures. 侵略者掠夺了村庄的财宝。|Synonyms: plunder, pillage, rob, loot, ransack|Antonyms: protect, preserve, enrich, enhance|verb


despoiled:/dɪˈspɔɪld/ | adj. |被掠夺的，被蹂躏的|Deprived of something valuable by force; robbed; plundered.|The once beautiful countryside was left despoiled and barren after the war. 曾经美丽的乡村在战争结束后变得荒芜一片，被洗劫一空。|Synonyms: plundered, pillaged, robbed, looted, ransacked|Antonyms: protected, preserved, enriched, enhanced|adjective
