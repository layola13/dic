overventuresome: /ˌoʊvərˈvɛntʃərsəm/
adjective
过于冒险的；胆子过大的
too willing to take risks or to try new and dangerous things
He made some overventuresome investments that he later regretted. 他进行了一些过于冒险的投资，后来后悔了。

She's an overventuresome child, always climbing trees and exploring the unknown. 她是一个胆子很大的孩子，总是爬树和探索未知的事物。

近义词: reckless, rash, audacious, foolhardy, daredevil
反义词: cautious, prudent, timid, circumspect

词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "venturesome" (from "venture," meaning "a risky or daring undertaking")
