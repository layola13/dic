aluminography:/əˌluːmɪˈnɒɡrəfi/
n.
铝版印刷术，铝版印刷品
A process of printing from an aluminum plate, or a print made by this process.
Aluminography offers a cheaper alternative to traditional lithography.  铝版印刷提供了一种比传统光刻更便宜的替代方案。
The museum displayed several examples of early aluminography.  博物馆展出了几件早期铝版印刷品的例子。
Synonyms:  offset printing, lithography
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly engraving, etching which are different printing processes)
