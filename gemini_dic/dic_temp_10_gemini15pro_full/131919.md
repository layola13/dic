noncontingent:/ˌnɑːnkənˈtɪndʒənt/ | adj. | 非偶然的；无条件的；必然的 | Not dependent on or conditioned by something else; absolute. |  Her salary is noncontingent, meaning she receives it regardless of her performance. 她的薪水是无条件的，这意味着无论她的表现如何，她都会收到。

Her love for her children was noncontingent. 她对孩子的爱是无条件的。


| 近义词: absolute, unconditional, independent, guaranteed, certain | 反义词: contingent, conditional, dependent, uncertain | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + contingent (dependent on something else).  "Contingent" ultimately derives from the Latin "contingere" (to touch, happen).
