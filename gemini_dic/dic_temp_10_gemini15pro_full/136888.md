nonsociably:/ˌnɒnˈsəʊʃəbli/ | adv. | 不合群地，孤僻地 | In a manner that avoids social interaction; unsociably. | He lived nonsociably, rarely leaving his apartment.  他过着孤僻的生活，很少离开他的公寓。|  The author nonsociably declined all invitations to literary events.  这位作家不合群地拒绝了所有参加文学活动的邀请。 | 近义词：unsociably, reclusively, introvertedly | 反义词：sociably, gregariously | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From non- + sociably.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not," and sociably is an adverb meaning "in a sociable manner."
