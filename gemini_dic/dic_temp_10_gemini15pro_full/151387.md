parliamentarization:/ˌpɑːrləˌmɛntərɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. |议会化，国会化|The process of making a political system more like a parliamentary system, typically by increasing the power of the legislature.|The parliamentarization of the political system led to greater accountability of the government. 政治体制的议会化导致政府更具问责性。|Synonyms: democratization, liberalization|Antonyms: authoritarianism, dictatorship|noun


parliamentarization:/ˌpɑːrləˌmɛntərɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. |议会化，国会化|The act or process of transforming a political system into one that resembles a parliamentary democracy, often by strengthening the role and authority of the legislative branch.| The country's parliamentarization involved transferring more power to the elected representatives.  这个国家的议会化进程包括将更多权力移交给民选代表。|Synonyms:  constitutionalization,  representative government|Antonyms: centralization,  autocracy|noun
