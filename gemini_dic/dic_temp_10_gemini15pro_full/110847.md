lintless:/ˈlɪntlɪs/
adjective
无绒毛的，不起毛的
Free from lint; not producing lint.
The new microfiber cloths are lintless and ideal for cleaning delicate surfaces. 这些新的超细纤维布没有绒毛，非常适合清洁精致的表面。
Near Synonyms: fluffless, smooth, slick
Antonyms: linty, fuzzy, downy
词性: 形容词
