heterozygous:/ˌhɛtərəˈzaɪɡəs/| adj.|杂合的|Having two different alleles of a particular gene or genes.|例句：The individual is heterozygous for the gene that controls eye color.  这个人控制眼睛颜色的基因是杂合的。

例句：Heterozygous individuals carry one dominant and one recessive allele. 杂合子个体携带一个显性和一个隐性等位基因。|近义词：hybrid, mixed|反义词：homozygous|词性：形容词

heterozygoted: /ˌhɛtərəˈzaɪɡoʊtɪd/ | adj. |杂合的|Made heterozygous; having different alleles at one or more corresponding loci on homologous chromosomes.  (This term is less common than "heterozygous") |例句：The plants were heterozygoted for the disease resistance gene. 这些植物的抗病基因是杂合的。

例句：The offspring were heterozygoted for several traits. 后代的几个性状是杂合的。|近义词：hybridized, mixed|反义词：homozygoted (also less common), homozygous|词性：形容词
