overscented: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛntɪd/ | adj. |香味过浓的，气味太重的 | Having too strong a scent; excessively perfumed. | The room was overscented with cheap air freshener. 房间里充满了廉价空气清新剂的浓烈气味。| The overscented lobby gave me a headache.  气味过浓的大厅让我头疼。 | 近义词: overpowering, cloying | 反义词: unscented, subtle | 词性: 形容词

词源: over- + scented

over-:  prefix meaning "excessively," "too much"  表示“过度”、“太多”

scented: past participle of scent (v.) meaning "to give off or cause to have a smell."  来自 scent (v.) 的过去分词，意思是“散发或使有气味”。
