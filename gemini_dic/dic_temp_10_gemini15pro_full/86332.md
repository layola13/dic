heck:/hɛk/
interjection
哎呀；见鬼（hell 的委婉说法）
A mild exclamation of surprise, annoyance, or frustration.
Heck, I didn't know that! 哎呀，我不知道！
近义词:darn, shoot, gosh
反义词:None (as it's a mild exclamation, it doesn't have a direct opposite)
词性:interjection

注意事项:
"heck" is generally used informally and is considered a milder form of "hell."  While it's acceptable in casual conversation, it's generally best to avoid using it in formal writing or professional settings.
