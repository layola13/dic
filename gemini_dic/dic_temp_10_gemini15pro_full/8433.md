antiagglutinative:/ˌæntiəˈɡluːtɪnətɪv/
adjective
抗凝集的，防止凝集的
Preventing or counteracting agglutination (the clumping together of particles, such as red blood cells).
Antiagglutinative drugs can help prevent blood clots.  抗凝药物可以帮助预防血栓。
Certain substances in the body have an antiagglutinative effect on blood cells.  体内某些物质对血细胞有抗凝集作用。
anti-clotting, anticoagulant
