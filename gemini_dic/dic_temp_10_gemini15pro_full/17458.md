bearable:/ˈberəbl/ (US), /ˈbeərəbl/ (UK)| adj.|可忍受的，可以承受的|Able to be endured; tolerable.|The pain was severe, but bearable. 疼痛很剧烈，但还可以忍受。|近义词：tolerable, endurable, sufferable, manageable|反义词: unbearable, intolerable, insufferable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *berable, from Old French portable "bearable," from porter "to carry, bear," from Latin portāre "to carry," frequentative of portāre "to carry."  The English word was influenced by the verb "to bear."
