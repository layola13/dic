misallotment:/ˌmɪsæˈlɒtmənt/
n.
错误分配，分配不当
an act of allotting something wrongly or improperly.
The misallotment of funds led to a delay in the project. 资金分配不当导致项目延误。
misallocation, misdistribution
proper allocation, correct distribution
noun
