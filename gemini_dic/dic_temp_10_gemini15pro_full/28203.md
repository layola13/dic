caprid:/ˈkæprɪd/| adj. n.| 山羊的；类似山羊的；属于山羊科的；（指人）好色的，性欲强的；山羊科动物|Of or belonging to the goat family, Capridae.  A member of the goat family.|The caprid was nimble and sure-footed on the steep mountainside. 这只山羊科动物在陡峭的山坡上行动敏捷而稳健。|He exhibited caprid behavior, chasing after anything in a skirt.  他表现出好色的行为，追逐任何穿裙子的女人。|近义词：goat-like, hircine|反义词: (no clear opposite, perhaps bovine - cow-like)|词性: adjective, noun
