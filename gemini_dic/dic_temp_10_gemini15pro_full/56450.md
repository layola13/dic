dolomitic:/ˌdɒləˈmɪtɪk/ /ˌdoʊləˈmɪtɪk/| adj.|白云石的；含白云石的|Containing or consisting of dolomite; resembling dolomite.|The dolomitic limestone cliffs were impressive.|近义词：magnesian|反义词:calcareous|词性:形容词

dolomite:/ˈdɒləmaɪt/ /ˈdoʊləˌmaɪt/| n.|白云石|A carbonate mineral consisting of calcium magnesium carbonate, CaMg(CO3)2, found in crystals and in masses as a sedimentary rock.|Dolomite is a common rock-forming mineral.|近义词：dolostone|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑其他类型的岩石，例如limestone石灰岩)|词性:名词
