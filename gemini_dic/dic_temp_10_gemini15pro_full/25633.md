bunchiest:/ˈbʌntʃiɪst/
adjective
最成束的；最成群的
Most bunched or clustered together.
The bunchiest grapes were at the top of the vine.  最成串的葡萄在藤蔓的顶部。
The florist created the bunchiest bouquet I've ever seen. 花艺师创作了我见过的最密集的花束。

近义词:densest, thickest, most clustered
反义词: sparsest, thinnest, most spread out
词性: adjective
