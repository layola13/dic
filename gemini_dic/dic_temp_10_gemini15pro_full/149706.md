palaeethnologist:/ˌpeɪliːɛθˈnɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 古民族学家 | A scientist who studies past peoples and cultures, particularly through the examination of their material remains. |  Palaeethnologists study ancient tools and artifacts to understand past civilizations. 古民族学家研究古代工具和文物以了解过去的文明。| Archaeologist, anthropologist |  (No direct antonym) | noun

Etymology:
"palaeo-" (from Greek "palaios" meaning "ancient") + "ethnology" (from Greek "ethnos" meaning "nation" + "-logy" meaning "study of")
