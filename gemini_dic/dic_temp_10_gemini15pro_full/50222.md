depeach:/dɪˈpiːtʃ/
verb
弹劾，控告
To charge (a public official) with misconduct in office before a competent tribunal.
The governor was depeached for corruption. 州长因腐败被弹劾。
近义词:impeach, accuse, indict
反义词:exonerate, acquit
词性:v.

depeach:/dɪˈpiːtʃ/
verb
剥夺(主教、僧侣等)的圣职;革除…的教籍
To deprive (a bishop, priest, etc.) of ecclesiastical office.
The priest was depeached for heresy.  这名牧师因异端邪说被革除教籍。
近义词:dismiss, remove, depose
反义词:reinstate, appoint
词性:v.

词源：From Old French despescher "to dispatch, expedite, hasten," also "to send away, dismiss," from des- "dis-" + pescher "to hinder, impede."  The original meaning was "to rid oneself of" or "to remove from office."  The sense of "to impeach" arose later.
