nonparishioner:/ˌnɒn pəˈrɪʃənər/ /ˌnɑːn pəˈrɪʃənər/
n.
非教区居民；非堂区居民
A person who does not belong to a particular parish.
The church welcomed nonparishioners to the Christmas Eve service. 教堂欢迎非教区居民参加平安夜的礼拜。
outsider, visitor
parishioner, member
noun
