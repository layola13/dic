execrable: /ˈɛksɪkrəb(ə)l/
adjective
极坏的；糟糕的；恶劣的
Extremely bad or unpleasant.
例句：The food at the hotel was execrable.  酒店的食物糟透了。

例句：His execrable behavior shocked everyone. 他恶劣的行为震惊了所有人。

近义词：terrible, awful, appalling, atrocious, dreadful, horrible
反义词：excellent, superb, wonderful, magnificent

词源：Late Middle English: from late Latin execrabilis, from execrari ‘curse’, from ex- ‘out, from’ + sacrare ‘devote to a deity’ (from sacer ‘sacred’).
