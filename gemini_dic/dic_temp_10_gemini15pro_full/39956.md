constructor:/kənˈstrʌktər/ | n. | 构造器，构造函数 | A constructor is a special method within a class that is automatically called when a new object of that class is created. It is used to initialize the object's attributes. |  The `Dog` class has a constructor that takes the dog's name and breed as arguments. | synonyms: initializer, builder | antonyms: destructor | noun
