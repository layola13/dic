caravaned:/ˈkærəˌvænd/
verb
旅行(尤指成群结队地)；车队旅行
travelled in a caravan or caravans; formed a caravan.
The nomads caravanned across the desert. 游牧民族穿越沙漠。
They caravanned to the coast for the summer. 他们组队去海边过夏天。
Synonyms: journeyed, trekked, migrated
Antonyms: stayed, settled
verb

caravaned:/ˈkærəˌvænd/
past participle
旅行(尤指成群结队地)；车队旅行的
having travelled in a caravan or caravans; having formed a caravan.
The recently caravanned group set up camp by the river.  最近旅行的队伍在河边扎营。
Having caravanned for weeks, they were relieved to reach their destination.  旅行了几个星期后，他们终于到达了目的地，感到如释重负。
