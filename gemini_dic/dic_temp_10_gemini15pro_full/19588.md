biggest:/ˈbɪɡɪst/| adj.|最大的|Superlative form of big.  Meaning the largest in size, amount, or extent.|That's the biggest apple I've ever seen! 那是我见过的最大的苹果！|近义词：largest, greatest, hugest|反义词:smallest, tiniest|词性:adjective

Etymology: From Middle English *biggest*, superlative of *big*.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bikaz* ("bulging, swelling").
