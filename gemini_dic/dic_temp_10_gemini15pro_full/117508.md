meekest:/ˈmiːkɪst/| adj.|最温顺的，最谦恭的|Superlative form of meek: most meek.  Having or showing a quiet and gentle nature; not wanting to fight or argue.  It describes someone who is humble, patient, and mild-mannered, often in the face of adversity.|例句：He was the meekest man I ever knew. 他是我认识的最温顺的人。

例句：She accepted the criticism with the meekest of smiles. 她以最谦卑的微笑接受了批评。|近义词：gentlest, mildest, humblest, quietest|反义词：boldest, proudest, most arrogant, most assertive|词性:形容词
