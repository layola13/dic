incapacitated:/ˌɪnkəˈpæsɪteɪtɪd/
adjective
丧失能力的，无能力的
Deprived of strength or power; debilitated.  Unable to act or respond.
例句：He was incapacitated by the flu. 他因为流感而丧失了行动能力。

例句：The injury incapacitated her for several weeks.  这伤让她几周都不能动弹。

近义词：disabled, debilitated, crippled, impaired, powerless
反义词：able, capable, competent, healthy, fit
词性: adjective

incapacitate:/ˌɪnkəˈpæsɪteɪt/
verb
使无能力，使丧失能力
To deprive of strength or ability; to disable.
例句：The accident incapacitated him from working.  事故使他无法工作。

例句：Her illness incapacitated her from attending the meeting. 她的病使她无法参加会议。

近义词：disable, debilitate, cripple, impair
反义词：enable, empower, strengthen
词性: verb
