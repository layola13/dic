ensue:/ɪnˈsuː/

随之而来；接着发生
follow as a consequence.
Heavy rains fell, and floods ensued.  暴雨倾盆，洪水随之而来。
synonyms: follow, result, succeed, arise, derive, stem, spring, flow
antonyms: precede, cause
