overvaluing:/ˌoʊvərˈvæljuɪŋ/
verb
高估
placing too high a value on something; estimating something to be worth more than it actually is
The company's stock is overvaluing its potential. 这家公司的股票高估了它的潜力。
Overvaluing your abilities can lead to disappointment. 高估自己的能力会导致失望。
synonyms: overestimating, overrating, overpricing
antonyms: undervaluing, underestimating, underrating
v.
