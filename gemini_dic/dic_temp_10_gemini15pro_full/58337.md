drywall: /ˈdraɪwɔːl/ (US) /ˈdraɪwɔːl/ (UK)
n.|石膏板，干式墙|A large, rigid sheet of plasterboard used especially in interior walls and ceilings.|例句：They used drywall to construct the interior walls. 他们用石膏板建造内墙。|近义词：plasterboard, gypsum board, sheetrock (US trademark)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps traditional lath and plaster)|词性:名词

drywalls: /ˈdraɪwɔːlz/ (US) /ˈdraɪwɔːlz/ (UK)
n.|石膏板（复数）|Multiple sheets of drywall.|例句：The drywalls were delivered to the construction site.  石膏板被运送到建筑工地。|近义词：plasterboards, gypsum boards|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:名词

词源: "Drywall" is a compound word from "dry" and "wall," referring to the method of installation without using plaster.
