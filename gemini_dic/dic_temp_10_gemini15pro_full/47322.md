de-pest:/diːˈpɛst/
verb
去除害虫; 消灭害虫
To rid a place or thing of pests.
The city is trying to de-pest the subway system.  这个城市正在努力清除地铁系统中的害虫。
They de-pested the house before moving in. 他们在搬进去之前对房子进行了除虫。
近义词: exterminate, delouse, disinfest
反义词: infest
词性: verb
