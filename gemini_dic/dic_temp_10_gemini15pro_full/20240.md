Birkenau: /ˌbɜːrkənˈaʊ/ | n. | 比克瑙 | Birkenau, or Auschwitz II-Birkenau, was a Nazi concentration and extermination camp located in German-occupied Poland during World War II.  It was the largest of the Auschwitz camps and the primary site for the systematic murder of Jews and other victims of the Holocaust. |  My grandfather was imprisoned in Birkenau during the war. 我的祖父在战争期间被囚禁在比克瑙。

Birkenau was a place of unimaginable horror. 比克瑙是一个难以想象的恐怖之地。| 近义词: Auschwitz (often used interchangeably but technically Birkenau is part of the larger Auschwitz complex) | 反义词: None (due to the unique and horrific nature of the term) | 词性: noun
