micromazia: /ˌmaɪkroʊˈmeɪʒə/ /ˌmaɪkrəˈmeɪziə/ | n. | 乳房过小，小乳症 |  A condition characterized by abnormally small breasts.  It's a medical term referring to underdevelopment of the mammary glands. |  She consulted a doctor about her micromazia. 她向医生咨询了她的乳房过小的问题。 | Micromastia can cause psychological distress for some women.  小乳症可能会给一些女性造成心理困扰。 | 近义词: hypomastia | 反义词: macromastia, gigantomastia | 词性: noun
