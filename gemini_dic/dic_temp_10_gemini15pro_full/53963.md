discreditor:/dɪsˈkrɛdɪtər/
n.
诽谤者，诋毁者
Someone who discredits or harms the reputation of another person or thing.
He was labeled a discreditor by his political opponents. 他被他的政敌贴上了诽谤者的标签。
近义词: slanderer, defamer, vilifier, traducer
反义词: supporter, advocate, champion
