diadelphian:/ˌdaɪəˈdɛlfiən/
adj.
二体雄蕊的, 成二组的
Having stamens united into two groups or bundles by their filaments, with one stamen often free.
The pea family is characterized by diadelphous stamens. 豆科植物的特点是具有二体雄蕊。
近义词:monadelphous (单体雄蕊的)
反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑polyandrous - 多雄蕊的，但并非完全相反的概念)
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Modern Latin diadelphus, from Greek di- "two" + adelphos "brother".
