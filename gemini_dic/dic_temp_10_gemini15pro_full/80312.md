ETYMOLOGY->
From the "gold brick" swindle, where a worthless brick plated with gold was sold as solid gold.  This term emerged in the late 19th century in the American West.  The sense of the word shifted to describe someone who appears valuable but is actually worthless, much like the deceptive gold brick.
