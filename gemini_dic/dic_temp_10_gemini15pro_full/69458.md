feigningly:/ˈfeɪnɪŋli/
adverb
假装地，虚伪地
in a way that is not sincere; pretendingly
She feigningly smiled at him, hiding her true feelings of anger. 她假装对他微笑，掩饰了她真正的愤怒。
He feigningly agreed to the proposal, planning to sabotage it later. 他假装同意这个提议，打算以后再破坏它。
近义词:pretendedly, insincerely, falsely, deceptively
反义词:genuinely, sincerely, truly, honestly
词性:adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From feign + -ing + -ly.  Feign comes from Old French feindre, from Latin fingere "to shape, form, invent, devise."
