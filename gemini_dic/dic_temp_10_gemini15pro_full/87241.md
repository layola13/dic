heretification:/ˌhɛrɪtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
异端化，宣布…为异端
The process of declaring something or someone heretical.
The heretification of Galileo's theories by the Church. 教会将伽利略的理论宣布为异端。
近义词: condemnation, denunciation
反义词: sanctification, canonization
词性: 名词
