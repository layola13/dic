laverock: /ˈlɑːrvək/ | n. | 云雀 | A small brown and grey bird that sings a beautiful song while flying high in the air. It is especially common in open fields. |  The laverock ascended into the sky, its melodious song filling the air. 云雀飞上天空，它悦耳的歌声充满了空气。

 The farmer listened to the laverock's song as he worked in the field.  农夫在田间劳作时聆听着云雀的歌声。 | 近义词: skylark | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源:
From Middle English *laverocke, from Old English lāwerce, from Proto-Germanic *laiwazikō, equivalent to lāwerce. Cognate with Scots laverock (“lark”), Saterland Frisian Lauwerke (“skylark”), West Frisian ljurk (“skylark”), Dutch leeuwerik (“skylark”), German Lerche (“lark”), Danish lærkehane (“skylark”), Swedish lärka (“lark”).
