bindi:/ˈbɪndi/ | n. | 额头上的装饰点, 眉间点 | A decorative dot worn on the forehead, especially by Hindu women. | She wore a red bindi on her forehead. 她额头上戴着一个红色的眉间点。 | 近义词: tilak, pottu | 反义词:  (无直接反义词) | 词性: 名词

词源: From Hindi बिंदी (bindī), from Sanskrit बिन्दु (bindu), meaning "point" or "drop".
