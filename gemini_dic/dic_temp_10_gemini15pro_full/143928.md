outpunches:/ˌaʊtˈpʌntʃɪz/
|v.|胜过；以拳击胜过|Defeats someone by punching more effectively or powerfully.  It can also be used metaphorically to describe outperforming someone in a competitive situation.|He outpunched his opponent in the final round and won the championship. 他在最后一轮击败了对手，赢得了冠军。|Synonyms: outbox, outperform, surpass, defeat, conquer|Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by|verb (third-person singular simple present outpunches, present participle outpunching, simple past outpunched, past participle outpunched)
