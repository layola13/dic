gummite:/ˈɡʌmaɪt/| n. | 橡胶矿，胶黄铀矿 |An amorphous alteration product of uraninite, usually containing lead, water, and various other metallic oxides.  It is a secondary uranium mineral. | The geologist discovered a vein of gummite in the uranium mine. 地质学家在铀矿中发现了一条胶黄铀矿矿脉。 | The old mine was a rich source of gummite.  这个老矿是一个丰富的胶黄铀矿来源。 | 近义词: uranophane, fourmarierite (as these are also secondary uranium minerals) | 反义词:  uraninite (as this is the primary mineral from which gummite is derived) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From *gummi* (German: gum, rubber) + *-ite* (a suffix used in mineralogy to denote minerals).
