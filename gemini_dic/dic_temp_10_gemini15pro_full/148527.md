oversurely:/ˌoʊvərˈʃʊrli/
adverb|过于自信地，过于肯定地|Acting or speaking with excessive confidence; too confidently.|He oversurely predicted his victory in the election. 他过于自信地预测自己会在选举中获胜。|近义词：overconfidently, arrogantly, presumptuously|反义词：hesitantly, tentatively, uncertainly|词性: 副词
词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + surely (adverb meaning "certainly")
