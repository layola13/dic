nominalization:/ˌnɑːmɪnələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |名词化；名词化作用| The process of forming a noun from a verb or adjective.  The nominalization of "fail" is "failure".|  The frequent nominalization in his writing makes it sound very formal.  他写作中频繁的名词化让它听起来很正式。 | synonyms: noun formation, verbal noun | antonyms: verbification | noun
