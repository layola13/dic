alkalify: /ˈælkəlaɪfaɪ/
verb
使碱化；碱化
to make something alkaline, or more alkaline than it was before
The company uses lime to alkalify the soil.  这家公司用石灰使土壤碱化。
Gardeners sometimes alkalify the soil to suit certain plants. 园丁有时会碱化土壤以适应某些植物。
近义词: neutralize, basify
反义词: acidify
词性: v.
