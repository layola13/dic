amphicoelous: /ˌæmfɪˈsiːləs/
adjective
双凹的
Having a concave surface at both ends, as in the centra of certain vertebrae.
The amphicoelous vertebrae of fish allow for a greater range of motion. 鱼类的双凹椎骨允许更大范围的运动。
The ancient fossil showed evidence of amphicoelous vertebrae.  这块古老的化石显示出双凹椎骨的证据。
近义词: biconcave
反义词: procoelous, opisthocoelous
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek amphi- "both, on both sides" + koilos "hollow"
