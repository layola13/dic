court-martialed: /ˌkɔːrt ˈmɑːrʃəld/ /ˌkɔːrt ˈmɑːrʃəld/
verb
军事法庭审判
to try (someone) in a military court
The soldier was court-martialed for desertion.  这个士兵因擅离职守而被军事法庭审判。
Synonyms: try, prosecute
Antonyms: acquit, pardon
verb
