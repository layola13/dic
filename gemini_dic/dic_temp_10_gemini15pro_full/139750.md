ockers:/ˈɒkəz/
n.
储物柜
Lockers are small, lockable compartments used for storing personal belongings.
Students keep their books and bags in lockers.
学生们把书和包放在储物柜里。
cupboards, cabinets
safes

Etymology:
From lock + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting agents or instruments).  Ultimately from Old English *loccian "to fasten, enclose."
