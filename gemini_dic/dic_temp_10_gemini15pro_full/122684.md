mody:/ˈmoʊdi/| adj.|喜怒无常的，情绪化的|Given to sudden changes of mood; temperamental. |例句：He's a moody teenager, one minute happy, the next sullen. 他是个喜怒无常的青少年，一会儿高兴，一会儿闷闷不乐。

例句：The moody weather reflected her own turbulent emotions.  阴晴不定的天气反映了她自己动荡不安的情绪。|近义词：temperamental, changeable, volatile, erratic, mercurial|反义词：stable, even-tempered, cheerful, steady|词性:形容词
