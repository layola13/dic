annihilating:/əˈnaɪɪleɪtɪŋ/
adjective
毁灭性的，歼灭性的
utterly destroying or defeating someone or something.
The enemy suffered an annihilating defeat. 敌人遭受了毁灭性的失败。
The boxer delivered an annihilating blow in the final round.  拳击手在最后一轮发出致命一击。
近义词:devastating, crushing, destructive, obliterating
反义词:constructive, creating, beneficial
词性:adjective

annihilate:/əˈnaɪɪleɪt/
verb
消灭，歼灭
destroy utterly; obliterate.
The atomic bomb annihilated the city. 原子弹摧毁了这座城市。
The company was annihilated by the competition.  这家公司被竞争对手彻底击败了。
近义词:destroy, obliterate, eradicate, decimate
反义词:create, build, establish
词性:verb

词源:
From Latin *annihilare, from ad- + nihil ("nothing").
