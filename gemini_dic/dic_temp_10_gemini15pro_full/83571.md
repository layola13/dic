haemastatics:/ˌhiːməˈstætɪks/ /ˌheməˈstætɪks/ | n. |止血药，止血法|Substances or methods used to stop bleeding.|The surgeon applied haemastatics to control the bleeding during the operation.  外科医生在手术过程中使用止血药来控制出血。|Synonyms: hemostatics, styptics, coagulants|Antonyms: anticoagulants|noun

haemastatic:/ˌhiːməˈstætɪk/ /ˌheməˈstætɪk/ | adj. |止血的|Having the property of stopping bleeding.|The nurse applied a haemastatic dressing to the wound. 护士在伤口上敷上了止血敷料。|Synonyms: hemostatic, styptic, coagulant|Antonyms: anticoagulant|adjective
