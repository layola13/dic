leathering: /ˈleðərɪŋ/
noun
痛打；鞭打；责骂
The act of beating someone with a leather strap or other implement.
He received a leathering for coming home late. 他因为回家晚了而挨了一顿揍。
beating, thrashing, flogging, whipping
scolding, reprimanding
