cleistogamous: /klaɪˈstɒɡəməs/ | adj. |闭花受精的| (of a plant) having flowers that do not open, and are self-pollinated. | Example:  Cleistogamous flowers ensure seed production even in unfavorable conditions. 闭花受精的花即使在不利的条件下也能确保种子生产。

Example:  Violets produce both cleistogamous and chasmogamous flowers. 紫罗兰既会产生闭花受精的花，也会产生开放受精的花。| 近义词: self-pollinating, autogamous | 反义词: chasmogamous | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek kleistos "closed" + gamos "marriage"
