calabar:/ˈkæləbɑːr/
noun
卡拉巴尔
A port city in southeastern Nigeria.
例句：Calabar is known for its rich history and diverse culture.  卡拉巴尔以其丰富的历史和多元文化而闻名。

例句：We visited the slave history museum in Calabar. 我们参观了卡拉巴尔的奴隶历史博物馆。
近义词：city, port
反义词:rural area
词性:n.


calabar bean:/ˈkæləbɑːr biːn/
noun
卡拉巴豆
The poisonous seed of a West African climbing plant, formerly used as an ordeal poison and now as a source of physostigmine.
例句：Calabar bean contains a potent neurotoxin. 卡拉巴豆含有一种强效神经毒素。

例句：Historically, Calabar bean was used in trials by ordeal.  历史上，卡拉巴豆被用于神判。
近义词：ordeal poison, physostigmine source
反义词:antidote
词性:n.
