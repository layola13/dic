emphyteutic:/ˌɛmfɪˈtjuːtɪk/ | adj. | 永佃的，长期租赁的 | Relating to or being a long-term lease of property, especially land, that allows the tenant to make improvements and to sublet.  The tenant pays an annual rent and enjoys most of the rights of ownership. |  The farmer held the land under an emphyteutic lease.  农民以永佃租约持有这块土地。 | Synonyms: leasehold, long-term lease | Antonyms: freehold | adjective

emphyteusis:/ˌɛmfɪˈtjuːsɪs/ | n. | 永佃权，长期租赁权 | A long-term lease of property, especially land, that allows the tenant to make improvements and to sublet. The tenant pays an annual rent and enjoys most of the rights of ownership. |  The emphyteusis granted the farmer almost all the rights of a landowner.  永佃权赋予农民几乎所有土地所有者的权利。| Synonyms: leasehold, long-term lease | Antonyms: freehold | noun
