clinanthium:/klaɪˈnænθiəm/
n.
花托，花序托
The expanded apex of a peduncle upon which the flowers of a head or cluster are borne.
The sunflower's clinanthium is broad and flat, providing a platform for numerous small flowers.  向日葵的花托宽而扁平，为众多的小花提供了一个平台。
synonym: receptacle
antonym: (No direct antonym exists for a specific plant structure like a clinanthium.  One might consider terms related to the base or root of the plant as conceptually opposite, but not true antonyms.)
