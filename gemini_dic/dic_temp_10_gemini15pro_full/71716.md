flavourlessness:/ˈfleɪvələsnəs/| n.|无味；平淡无奇|Lack of flavour; blandness.  The flavourlessness of the dish was disappointing.|例句：The flavourlessness of the soup made it unappetizing. 这汤寡淡无味，让人食欲不振。

例句：He complained about the flavourlessness of the mass-produced bread. 他抱怨批量生产的面包寡淡无味。|近义词：blandness, insipidity, vapidity|反义词：flavourfulness, tastiness, savouriness|词性:名词

词源：From flavourless + -ness.  Flavour from Old French flaur, from Latin flare "to blow."  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives.
