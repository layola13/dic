florulent:/ˈflɔːrələnt/ /ˈflʊrələnt/| adj.|繁花的；华丽的；辞藻华丽的|Covered with flowers; flowery; ornate in style.|例句：The florulent meadows were a sight to behold. 繁花似锦的草地是一道美丽的风景。

例句：His florulent writing style was appreciated by some but considered overly elaborate by others.  他华丽的写作风格受到一些人的赞赏，但也有人认为过于华丽。|近义词：flowery, ornate, embellished, flamboyant|反义词：plain, simple, unadorned|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *florulentus, from florēre (to flourish, blossom) + -ulentus (full of).  Ultimately from Proto-Italic *flōrēre, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰleh₃- (to bloom, blossom).
