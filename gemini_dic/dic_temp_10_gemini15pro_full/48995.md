deglorify: /ˌdiːˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 贬低，使失去荣耀，使丢脸 | To deprive of glory; to reduce the glory of; to treat or represent as lacking in glory or dignity. | The tabloid deglorified the athlete by publishing embarrassing photos of him. 小报刊登了这位运动员令人尴尬的照片，贬低了他。

The new regime sought to deglorify the previous ruler by removing his statues and renaming streets. 新政权试图通过移除前统治者的雕像和重新命名街道来贬低他。


近义词:  dishonor, disgrace, demean, humble, abase, belittle | 反义词: glorify, exalt, honor, praise | 词性: verb

词源:  de- (Latin prefix meaning "down from, away from, off, reversal") + glorify (from Latin glorificare, "to make glorious")
