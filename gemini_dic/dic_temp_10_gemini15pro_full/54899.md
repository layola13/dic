disporting:/dɪˈspɔːrtɪŋ/
verb
嬉戏；玩耍；娱乐
Engaging in amusement or recreation; playing.
The children were disporting themselves in the park. 孩子们在公园里玩耍。
近义词: playing, frolicking, amusing, entertaining, diverting, recreating
反义词: working, toiling, laboring
词性: verb (present participle)
词源: From Middle English disporten, from Old French desporter, from des- (expressing removal) + porter (to carry).
