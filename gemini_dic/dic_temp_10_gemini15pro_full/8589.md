anticlergy:/ˌæntiˈklɜːrʒi/
adjective
反教权主义的；反对神职人员的
Opposed to the clergy or their influence.
The French Revolution was a time of strong anticlergy sentiment. 法国大革命时期反教权情绪高涨。

The anticlergy laws were soon repealed. 反对神职人员的法律很快就被废除了。

近义词: anticlerical, secular
反义词: pro-clergy, clericalist
词性: adjective

词源:
anti- (against) + clergy (body of ordained ministers in a Christian church)
