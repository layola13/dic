curiously:/ˈkjʊəriəsli/| adv.|好奇地，奇怪地|In a way that shows curiosity or is unusual or unexpected.|She looked at him curiously. 她好奇地看着他。|近义词：inquisitively, strangely, oddly|反义词：indifferently, apathetically|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *curiouseli, from Old French curios, from Latin cūriōsus (“careful, inquisitive, meticulous, curious”), from cūra (“care, concern, attention”).
