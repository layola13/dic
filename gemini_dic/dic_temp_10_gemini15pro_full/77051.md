garmenture:/ˈɡɑːrməntʃər/ | n. | 服装，服饰 | Clothing, especially outer garments; attire.  | The king's garmenture was magnificent, adorned with jewels and gold thread. 国王的服饰华丽非凡，饰以珠宝和金线。 |  The knight donned his garmenture and prepared for battle. 骑士穿戴好他的战袍，准备战斗。|  clothing, attire, apparel, raiment |  nudity, bareness | noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Old French *garnementure, from garnir ‘equip’.
