bushido:/ˈbuːʃiːdoʊ/ /ˈbʊʃiːdoʊ/
n.
武士道，武士精神
The way of the warrior; a Japanese code of conduct for samurai, stressing loyalty, self-sacrifice, and martial arts mastery.
He lived his life by the code of bushido. 他遵循武士道精神生活。
近义词：chivalry, code of honor
反义词：cowardice, dishonor
词性:noun

Etymology: From Japanese 武士道 (bushidō), from 武士 (bushi, “warrior”) + 道 (dō, “way, path”).
