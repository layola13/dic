antidilutive:/ˌænti daɪˈluːtɪv/
adjective
反稀释的
Reducing or counteracting dilution.  Relating to a transaction or security that does not reduce earnings per share.
The issuance of these shares was antidilutive, boosting earnings per share. 这些股票的发行是反稀释的，提高了每股收益。
Near Synonyms: accretive
Antonyms: dilutive
