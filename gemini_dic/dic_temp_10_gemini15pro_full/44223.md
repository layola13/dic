crocket:/ˈkrɑːkɪt/ | v. | 用钩针编织 | To create something using crochet. | She crocheted a beautiful blanket for her granddaughter. 她为孙女钩针编织了一条漂亮的毯子。 | He crocheted a small amigurumi animal. 他钩针编织了一个小小的毛绒玩具动物。 | synonyms: knit, stitch, weave | antonyms: unravel, untangle | verb
