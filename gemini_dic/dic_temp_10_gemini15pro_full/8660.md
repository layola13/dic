anticonservatives:/ˌænti kənˈsɜːrvəˌtɪvz/| n. |反保守主义者；非保守派|People who oppose conservative views or policies.  Those who advocate for progressive or liberal ideas, often challenging traditional values and institutions.|The anticonservatives protested the proposed cuts to social programs. 反保守主义者抗议削减社会福利项目的提议。|Synonyms: liberals, progressives, reformers, radicals|Antonyms: conservatives, traditionalists|Noun
