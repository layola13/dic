neozoic:/ˌniːəˈzoʊɪk/ | adj. n. | 新生代的；新生代 |Relating to or denoting the current geological era, which began 66 million years ago and continues to the present.  It is characterized by the dominance of mammals, birds, and flowering plants. | The neozoic era is also known as the Age of Mammals. 新生代也被称为哺乳动物时代。| The fossils found in these rocks are clearly from the Neozoic period. 在这些岩石中发现的化石显然来自新生代。 | 近义词: Cenozoic | 反义词: Paleozoic, Mesozoic | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Greek *neos* "new" + *zōikos*  "of animals,"  from *zōion* "animal."
