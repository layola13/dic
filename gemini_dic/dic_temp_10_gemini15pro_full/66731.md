exorcised:/ˈeksɔːrsaɪzd/ /ˈɛksɔrsˌaɪzd/| v. |驱魔的，去除的|To drive out or attempt to drive out (an evil spirit) from a person or place, or to free (someone or something) from an evil spirit.|The priest exorcised the demons from the possessed man.|近义词：expel, banish, purge, cleanse|反义词: imbue, infuse, contaminate|词性:动词
