epilogued:/ˈɛpɪlɔːɡd/
v.
加结语；为...写结语；以结语结束
provided with an epilogue; concluded.
The author epilogued his novel with a poignant reflection on the characters' fates. 作者在他的小说结尾加了一个关于人物命运的辛酸反思。
近义词:concluded, finished, ended
反义词:began, started, commenced
词性:动词
