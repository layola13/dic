comfortable: /ˈkʌmftəbl/ (US) /ˈkʌmfətəbəl/ (UK)
| adj. | 舒适的；舒服的；自在的；安逸的 | Providing physical ease and relaxation; free from pain or constraint; at ease mentally or socially. |例句：The new sofa is very comfortable. 新沙发很舒服。|近义词：cozy, pleasant, relaxing, agreeable |反义词: uncomfortable, uneasy, awkward |词性: adjective
Etymology: From Late Middle English confortable, from Old French confortable, from Late Latin confortabilis (“strengthening”), from Latin con- + fortis (“strong”).  The original meaning was "strengthening, encouraging, giving comfort."
