hoverer:/ˈhʌvərər/ (US), /ˈhɒvərə(r)/ (UK)| n. |盘旋者，徘徊者|Someone or something that hovers.  This can refer to a person or thing that remains suspended in the air, or figuratively, to someone who lingers or waits around. |The hummingbird is a skilled hoverer, capable of remaining stationary in mid-air.  蜂鸟是一位熟练的盘旋者，能够在半空中保持静止。

The nervous groom-to-be was a hoverer outside the chapel, pacing back and forth.  紧张的新郎在教堂外徘徊，来回踱步。|近义词：lingerer, dawdler|反义词: speeder, rusher |noun
