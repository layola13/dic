defenseless:/dɪˈfɛnslɪs/| adj.|无防备的，无法自卫的|Unable to defend oneself; vulnerable to attack.|The defenseless child cowered in fear.  那个毫无防备的孩子害怕地蜷缩着。|The small animal was completely defenseless against the predator. 小动物完全无法抵御捕食者。|近义词：vulnerable, unprotected, helpless, weak|反义词：protected, defended, secure, strong|词性:形容词

词源:
From defense + -less.  Defense comes from Old French defense, from Latin dēfēnsa.
