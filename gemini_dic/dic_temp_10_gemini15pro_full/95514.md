incloister:/ɪnˈklɔɪstər/ | v. | 使禁闭；使隐居；用回廊环绕 | To seclude or confine, especially in a monastery or convent; to surround with a cloister. |  The monks were incloistered in the monastery.  僧侣们被禁闭在修道院里。| Synonyms: confine, seclude, isolate, sequester | Antonyms: free, liberate, release | verb

incloister:/ɪnˈklɔɪstər/ | n. | 回廊；修道院 | A covered walkway, especially in a religious institution, typically with an open colonnade on one side. | The nuns walked in the cloister.  修女们在回廊里散步。| Synonyms: monastery, convent, abbey | Antonyms: outside world | noun
