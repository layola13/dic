paradeful: /pəˈreɪdfʊl/
adjective
炫耀的，夸耀的；爱炫耀的
Given to or marked by ostentatious display; boastful.
例句：He had a paradeful manner of speaking, always emphasizing his own achievements.  他说话的方式很炫耀，总是强调自己的成就。

例句：Her paradeful display of wealth made many people uncomfortable. 她炫耀财富的举动让很多人感到不舒服。

近义词：ostentatious, showy, flamboyant, pretentious
反义词：modest, humble, unassuming
词性: adjective
