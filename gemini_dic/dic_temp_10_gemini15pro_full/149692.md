pakistanis:/ˌpækɪˈstɑːniz/ /ˌpɑːkɪˈstɑːniz/
noun
巴基斯坦人；巴基斯坦国民
People who are citizens of Pakistan or who have Pakistani ancestry.
Many Pakistanis live abroad. 许多巴基斯坦人居住在国外。
Pakistanis celebrate Independence Day on August 14th. 巴基斯坦人在8月14日庆祝独立日。
synonyms: Pakistani citizens, Pakistani people
antonyms: (No direct antonym, could use nationalities of other countries, e.g., Indians, Bangladeshis, etc., but this depends on context)
