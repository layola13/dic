exquisite:/ɪkˈskwɪzɪt/ /ɛkˈskwɪzɪt/| adj.|精致的；精美的；敏锐的；剧烈的|Characterized by intricate and beautiful detail; highly sensitive and refined; intensely felt.|例句：She has exquisite taste in clothes. 她对服装有很高的品味。|例句：The artist creates exquisite miniature sculptures. 艺术家创作了精美的微型雕塑。|近义词：refined, elegant, delicate, superb, perfect|反义词：crude, coarse, clumsy, rough|词性:形容词

exquisite:/ɪkˈskwɪzɪt/ /ɛkˈskwɪzɪt/| n.|讲究的人；过分在意外表的人|A person who is excessively fastidious about appearance or manner.|例句：He is such an exquisite, always concerned with his appearance. 他真是个讲究的人，总是很在意自己的外表。|例句：She's a true exquisite, with a wardrobe full of designer clothes. 她是个真正的讲究人，衣柜里满是名牌服装。|近义词：dandy, fop|反义词：sloven|词性:名词
