click-through:/ˈklɪk θruː/
noun
点击通过率；点击访问
the rate at which people click on an online advertisement to visit the advertiser's website.
The banner ad had a click-through rate of 2%.  这幅横幅广告的点击率为2%。
click-through rate, CTR
bounce rate
n.

click-through:/ˈklɪk θruː/
adjective
点击通路的；点击访问的
relating to or involving clicking on a link in an online advertisement to visit the advertiser's website.
We offer click-through advertising on our website. 我们在网站上提供点击式广告。
online, digital
offline
adj.
