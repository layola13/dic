fresh-water:/ˈfreʃˌwɔːtər/ /ˈfreʃˌwɑːtər/ | adj. | 淡水的 |  Living or growing in fresh water, not salt water. |  例句：Fresh-water fish cannot survive in the ocean. 淡水鱼无法在海洋中生存。

fresh-water:/ˈfreʃˌwɔːtər/ /ˈfreʃˌwɑːtər/ | n. | 淡水 | Water that is not salty, such as water from rivers and lakes.  | 例句：We need a supply of fresh-water for drinking. 我们需要淡水供应饮用。


近义词：fresh, potable (for drinking water) | 反义词：saltwater, marine, brackish | 词性：adjective, noun
