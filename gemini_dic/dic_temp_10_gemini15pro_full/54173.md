disentwine:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtwaɪn/
verb
解开，分开
To separate something that has become twisted or tangled together.
The little girl disentwined her fingers from her mother's hand.  小女孩把手指从妈妈的手中挣脱出来。

He carefully disentwined the fishing line from the branches. 他小心翼翼地把鱼线从树枝上解开。

近义词: untangle, unravel, unwind, separate, detach
反义词: entwine, tangle, intertwine
词性: verb
