bonze:/bɒnz/| n.|和尚，僧侣 (尤指佛教)|A Buddhist monk, especially one belonging to certain sects in East Asia. Often used with slightly derogatory connotations.|He saw a bonze meditating under a Bodhi tree. 他看到一个和尚在菩提树下冥想。|Synonyms: monk, friar, priest|Antonyms: layman, secular|noun
