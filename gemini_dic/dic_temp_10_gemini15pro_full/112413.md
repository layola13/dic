lothness:/ˈloʊθnəs/ | n. | 不情愿，勉强 | Unwillingness; reluctance to do something. | He showed a great lothness to leave his home. 他表现出极大的不愿离开家。|  She accepted the task with lothness. 她不情愿地接受了这项任务。 | 近义词：reluctance, unwillingness, disinclination | 反义词：eagerness, willingness, readiness | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *lothnes, equivalent to loth +‎ -ness.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *laithaz (“hateful, loathsome”).
