carditis:/kɑːrˈdaɪtɪs/
n.
心脏炎
Inflammation of the heart.
例句：The doctor diagnosed him with carditis after a series of tests. 医生经过一系列检查后诊断他患有心脏炎。

例句：Carditis can be caused by a variety of infections. 心脏炎可能由多种感染引起。

近义词：heart inflammation
反义词：(None, as it's a disease state)
词性：noun

endocarditis (心内膜炎) A type of carditis affecting the endocardium.
rheumatic carditis (风湿性心脏炎) Carditis caused by rheumatic fever.
infectious carditis (感染性心脏炎) Carditis caused by an infection.
