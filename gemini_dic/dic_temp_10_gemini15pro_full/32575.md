Example 2:  He presented the chirograph as evidence in court. 他将亲笔信作为证据提交法庭。 | Synonym:  holograph, manuscript | Antonym: typescript, printed document | noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *chirographum, from Greek *kheirographon, from kheir (hand) + graphon (something written).
