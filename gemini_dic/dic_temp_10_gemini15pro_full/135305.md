nonpatentable:/ˌnɒnˈpeɪtəntəbəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈpeɪtn̩təbl/
adjective
不可申请专利的
Not able to be patented.  Something that is nonpatentable cannot legally be protected by a patent.
The recipe, while unique, was deemed nonpatentable due to its reliance on common culinary techniques.  这道菜谱虽然独特，但由于依赖常见的烹饪技术，被认为不可申请专利。

The abstract idea, lacking a concrete application, was considered nonpatentable by the patent office.  这个抽象的想法缺乏具体的应用，被专利局认为不可申请专利。

近义词:unpatentable
反义词:patentable
