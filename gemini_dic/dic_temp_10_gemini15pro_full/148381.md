overspills:/ˌoʊvərˈspɪlz/ | v. |溢出；蔓延；外溢（通常指人口或事物）|  The water overspilled the glass and went all over the table. 水溢出了杯子，洒满了桌子。 | 近义词：overflow, spill over | 反义词：contain | 词性：动词


词源: "Overspill" is formed by combining the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessive" or "beyond") with the verb "spill" (meaning "to flow or run over the edge of a container"). The combined meaning is "to spill over excessively" or "to spread beyond its intended limits."
