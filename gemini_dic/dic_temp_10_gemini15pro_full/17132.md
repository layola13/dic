battier:/ˈbætiər/ (US), /ˈbætɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更难击中的，更难对付的|Harder to hit, penetrate, or get past; more capable of defending.  Derived from "bat" (as in hitting a ball) with the comparative suffix "-er".  Indicates a higher degree of difficulty compared to a baseline.  Originally used in sports, particularly baseball, but can be used metaphorically.|例句：He's a much battier pitcher this year than last. 今年他比去年更难对付了。

例句：The defense was battier than usual, and the team struggled to score. 防守比平时更严密，球队得分很困难。|近义词：tougher, more difficult, more challenging, more formidable, stronger|反义词:easier, weaker, less challenging|词性:adjective
