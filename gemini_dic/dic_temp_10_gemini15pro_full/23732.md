brattain:/ˈbrætən/| n.|布拉顿（人名）|Walter Houser Brattain, American physicist who shared the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1956 with John Bardeen and William Shockley for their research on semiconductors and their discovery of the transistor effect.  沃尔特·豪泽·布拉顿，美国物理学家，因对半导体的研究和发现晶体管效应，与约翰·巴丁和威廉·肖克利分享了1956年的诺贝尔物理学奖。|例句：Brattain, Bardeen, and Shockley invented the transistor. 布拉顿、巴丁和肖克利发明了晶体管。
