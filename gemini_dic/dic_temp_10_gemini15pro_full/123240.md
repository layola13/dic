monocultural:/ˌmɒnəʊˈkʌltʃərəl/ /ˌmɑːnəʊˈkʌltʃərəl/| adj.|单一文化的，单一种植的|Characterized by a single culture or crop.  Relating to a society or environment dominated by a single culture or characterized by a lack of cultural diversity.|The town was very monocultural, lacking the vibrancy of a diverse community. 这个小镇文化单一，缺乏多元化社区的活力。|The monocultural farming practices depleted the soil of essential nutrients. 单一种植的耕作方式耗尽了土壤中的必需养分。|近义词：homogeneous, uniform|反义词: multicultural, diverse|词性:形容词

mono- (one) + culture + -al (adjective suffix)
