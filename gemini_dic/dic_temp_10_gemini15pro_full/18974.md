bestial:/ˈbɛstiəl/
adj.
野兽般的；兽性的；残忍的
Of or like a beast; brutal; inhuman.
The murderer's bestial act shocked the community.  凶手的兽性行为震惊了社区。
He lived in bestial conditions, deprived of basic necessities. 他生活在野兽般的环境中，被剥夺了基本必需品。

ETYMOLOGY->
"bestiarist" comes from the Late Latin word "bestiarius," meaning "one who fights with beasts" (in the Roman arena). This evolved from "bestia," meaning "beast." Over time, the meaning shifted to encompass those who work with animals, especially livestock.

"bestial" also derives from the Latin "bestia," meaning "beast," and describes something related to or characteristic of a beast.
