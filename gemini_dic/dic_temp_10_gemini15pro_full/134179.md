nonincidentally:/ˌnɒnɪnsɪˈdɛntəli/ | adv. | 并非偶然地；顺便提一下；附带说一句 | Not by coincidence; by the way; in passing.  It implies that the information being added is significant, not just a casual aside. | The book, nonincidentally, was written by her grandfather.  这本书，顺便提一下，是她祖父写的。|  Nonincidentally, he also happens to be a renowned expert in the field. 顺便说一句，他恰好也是该领域的著名专家。| incidentally, significantly, meaningfully, purposely | casually, accidentally | adverb
