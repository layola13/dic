advancement:/ədˈvænsmənt/
noun
进步，进展；提升，晋升
the process of helping something/somebody to make progress or succeed; the progress that is made
She made steady advancement in her career. 她事业上稳步晋升。
His advancement to the position of manager was a surprise. 他升任经理的职位真是出乎意料。
synonym: progress, development, promotion, elevation
antonym: regression, decline, demotion, stagnation
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French avancement, from avancer "to advance." Ultimately from Latin abante "from before."
