hyperrhythmical:/ˌhaɪpərˈrɪðmɪkəl/ | adj. | 超节律的，节律过快的 | Characterized by excessive rhythm or an abnormally rapid rhythm.  |  Her hyperrhythmical heartbeat was a cause for concern. 她的心跳过快令人担忧。 |  The hyperrhythmical drumming was exhilarating.  过快的鼓声令人兴奋。 | Near Synonyms:  tachyrhythmic,  fast-paced, rapid, accelerated | Antonyms:  bradyrhythmic, slow, sluggish | word property: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (Greek: 'over, beyond') + rhythm (Greek: 'measured motion, time') + -ical (suffix forming adjectives).
