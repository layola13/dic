hyperconscientious:/ˌhaɪpərˌkɒnʃiˈenʃəs/| adj.|过度认真的，过度良心的|Excessively conscientious; characterized by extreme sensitivity of conscience.|She was so hyperconscientious about her work that she often stayed late to double-check everything.|近义词：scrupulous, meticulous, overscrupulous|反义词：careless, negligent|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From hyper- (meaning "excessive, over") + conscientious (from Latin conscientia "knowledge within oneself," from com- "with" + scire "to know").
