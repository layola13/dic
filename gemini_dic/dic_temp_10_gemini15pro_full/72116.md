flog:/flɒɡ/ (British), /flɑːɡ/ (American)| v.  n.|鞭打，鞭笞；苦苦推销|To beat with a whip or stick as punishment or torture; to try hard to sell something.|He was flogged for stealing. 他因偷窃而被鞭打。|The salesman was flogging the new product. 推销员在努力推销新产品。|近义词：whip, lash, beat, peddle, hawk|反义词：praise, commend|词性：动词，名词

词源:
From Old French *flaugier, from Vulgar Latin *flaugrare, from Latin flagellare "to whip, scourge"
