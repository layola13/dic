assists:/əˈsɪsts/| n. pl. |助攻，协助|An act of helping, typically in sports or other competitive activities.  Refers specifically to a pass or other action that directly leads to a teammate scoring a point (especially in basketball, hockey, and soccer).  Can also be used more generally to mean help or support.|例句：He finished the season with 20 goals and 15 assists. 他以20个进球和15次助攻结束了这个赛季。

例句：The software provides useful assists to the user. 这个软件为用户提供了有用的帮助。|近义词：helps, aids, supports|反义词：hinders, obstructs|词性:noun (plural)


assist:/əˈsɪst/| v. |协助，帮助|To give support or aid to someone.|例句：He assisted the police with their inquiries. 他协助警方进行调查。

例句：The company assisted him in finding a new job. 公司帮助他找到了一份新工作。|近义词：help, aid, support|反义词：hinder, obstruct|词性:verb


Etymology: From Old French assister "to stand by, be present at," from Latin assistere "to stand near, attend, assist," from ad- "to" + sistere "to stand, cause to stand."
