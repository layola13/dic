niggardish:/ˈnɪɡərdɪʃ/| adj.|吝啬的，小气的|Reluctant to give or spend; stingy; miserly.  This word sounds similar to a racial slur and should be avoided to prevent misunderstandings.|He was niggardish with his praise, offering only a grudging nod.|近义词：stingy, miserly, parsimonious, frugal, thrifty|反义词：generous, liberal, lavish, prodigal|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *nigardish, equivalent to nigard + -ish; akin to Old Norse *hnǫggr, niggardly.
