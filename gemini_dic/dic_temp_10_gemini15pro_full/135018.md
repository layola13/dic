nonnegotiability:/ˌnɒnɪɡəʊʃiəˈbɪləti/ /ˌnɑːnɪɡoʊʃiəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可协商性，没有商量余地
The nonnegotiability of the deadline caused stress among the team.  最后期限的不可协商性给团队带来了压力。
The contract emphasized the nonnegotiability of the terms.  合同强调了条款的不可协商性。
近义词:inflexibility, rigidity
反义词:flexibility, negotiability
词性:名词
