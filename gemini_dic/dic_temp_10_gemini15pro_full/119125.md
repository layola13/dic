metropolite:/məˈtrɒpəlaɪt/ | n. |都市人，大城市居民|A person who lives in a metropolis; a sophisticated urban person.|例句：The metropolite enjoyed the hustle and bustle of city life.  这位都市人享受着城市的喧嚣。

例句：Metropolites are often accustomed to a fast-paced lifestyle. 都市人通常习惯于快节奏的生活方式。|近义词：cosmopolitan, urbanite, city dweller|反义词:provincial, rustic|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek μητροπόλις (mētropolis, “mother city; capital city”), from μήτηρ (mḗtēr, “mother”) + πόλις (pólis, “city”).  Borrowed into English in the 14th century.
