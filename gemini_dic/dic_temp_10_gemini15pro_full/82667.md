guesses:/ˈɡesɪz/| n. |猜测，推测|An attempt to give an answer or opinion about something without having all the necessary information.|She made a few guesses about his age. 她猜测了他的年龄。|synonyms: estimations, conjectures, suppositions|antonyms: certainties, facts, knowledge|noun


guesses:/ˈɡesɪz/| v. |猜测，推测|Form an opinion or give an answer about something when you do not know much or anything about it.|I guessed her age correctly. 我正确地猜出了她的年龄。|synonyms:  surmise, conjecture, speculate|antonyms: know, calculate, ascertain|verb
