monothelism:/məˈnɒθɪlɪzəm/
n.
一志论
The doctrine that Christ had only one will, divine, not human.
Monothelism was condemned as a heresy. 一志论被谴责为异端邪说。
近义词:monothelitism
反义词:dyothelitism (二志论)
词性:noun
