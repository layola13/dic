insafety:/ɪnˈseɪfti/| adv.|安全地；在安全的地方|Being in a state or place of safety; free from danger or harm.|The children were brought insafety. 孩子们被带到了安全的地方。|近义词：securely, safely|反义词：dangerously, precariously|词性:副词

词源:
From in + safety.  "Safety" comes from Old French "sauvete"  ultimately from Latin "salvus" (safe, sound).
