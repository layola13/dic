fructiferous:/fruːkˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
多产的；富有成效的
Producing much fruit; fruitful.
例句：The fructiferous valley provided ample food for the villagers.  这个肥沃的山谷为村民提供了充足的食物。

例句：Her research proved fructiferous, yielding several groundbreaking discoveries. 她的研究成果丰硕，取得了几项突破性的发现。

近义词：productive, fertile, fruitful, prolific
反义词：barren, sterile, unproductive
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *fructifer* "fruit-bearing," from *fructus* "fruit" + *-fer* "bearing"
