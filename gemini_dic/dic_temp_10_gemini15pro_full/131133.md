nonbleeding:/ˌnɑːnˈbliːdɪŋ/
adjective
非出血性的
Not bleeding; not subject to bleeding.
The doctor confirmed it was a nonbleeding ulcer. 医生确认这是一个非出血性溃疡。
Near synonyms: bloodless, non-hemorrhagic
Antonyms: bleeding, hemorrhagic
词性: adjective
