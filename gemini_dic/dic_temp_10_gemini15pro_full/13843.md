auf:/aʊf/| prep.|on, upon, onto, in, at, to|A preposition with various meanings related to location, direction, or state.|例句：Das Buch liegt auf dem Tisch. The book is on the table.|近义词：an, in, über|反义词:unter, ab|preposition
Etymology: From Middle High German *ūf, from Old High German *ūf, from Proto-Germanic *up, from Proto-Indo-European *up- (up, over). Cognate with English up, Dutch op.
