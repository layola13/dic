metaphysic:/ˌmɛtəˈfɪzɪk/ | n. |形而上学| the branch of philosophy that deals with the first principles of things, including abstract concepts such as being, knowing, substance, cause, identity, time, and space. |  He studied metaphysics in college. 他在大学学习形而上学。| synonyms: ontology, cosmology, theology, philosophy | antonyms: physics, empiricism | noun

metaphysics:/ˌmɛtəˈfɪzɪks/ | n. |形而上学| abstract theory with no basis in reality | His argument was pure metaphysics. 他的论点纯粹是形而上学。| synonyms: abstraction, speculation, theory | antonyms: reality, practicality, empiricism | noun
