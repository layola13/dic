augitic:/ɔːˈdʒɪtɪk/ | adj. |辉石的；含辉石的| Containing or resembling augite, a common rock-forming mineral. | The volcanic rock was augitic in composition. 这块火山岩的成分中含有辉石。| The geologist identified the rock as augitic basalt. 地质学家将这块岩石鉴定为辉石玄武岩。|Synonyms: pyroxenic| Antonyms: non-augitic |adjective
