nondualistic:/ˌnɒnˈdʒuːəlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非二元论的 |  Relating to or characterized by nondualism; not involving a duality or separation.  It refers to a philosophical or spiritual perspective that denies the ultimate distinction between subject and object, self and other, or mind and matter. |  Nondualistic philosophies, such as Advaita Vedanta, emphasize the unity of all existence. 非二元论哲学，如吠檀多不二论，强调所有存在的统一性。

The book presents a nondualistic approach to understanding consciousness. 这本书提出了一种非二元论的方法来理解意识。| 近义词: monistic, holistic | 反义词: dualistic | 词性: adjective
