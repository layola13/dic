fogbound:/ˈfɒɡbaʊnd/ (UK), /ˈfɑːɡbaʊnd/ (US)| adj.|被雾困住的；浓雾弥漫的|Unable to proceed because of dense fog.|The fogbound airport was closed for several hours. 被大雾笼罩的机场关闭了几个小时。|例句：The ship was fogbound in the harbor. 这艘船被困在港口，因为浓雾无法航行。

例句：The fogbound travelers were stranded at the airport. 被雾困住的旅客滞留在机场。|近义词：fogged in, mistbound|反义词: clear|词性:形容词
