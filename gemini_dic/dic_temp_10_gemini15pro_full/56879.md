doser: /ˈdoʊsər/ (US) /ˈdəʊsə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 定量给药器；（尤指非法药物的）定量吸食器；给药者|A device that delivers a measured dose of something, especially a drug.  Also refers to someone who administers doses. | The doctor instructed the patient to use the doser to administer the correct amount of medication. 医生指示病人使用定量给药器以服用正确剂量的药物。| The police found a doser containing heroin in the suspect's pocket. 警察在嫌疑犯的口袋里发现了一个装有海洛因的定量吸食器。 | synonyms: dispenser, apportioner, measurer | antonyms: N/A | noun
