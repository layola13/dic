overlooks:/ˌoʊvərˈlʊks/ /ˌəʊvəˈlʊks/ | v. | 忽视，忽略；俯瞰，眺望 |Fails to notice or consider something; has a view of from above. | The report overlooks the significant role of teachers.  这份报告忽略了教师的重要作用。 | synonyms: misses, neglects, ignores, disregards | antonyms: notices, heeds, acknowledges, observes | verb (third-person singular simple present overlooks, present participle overlooking, simple past overlooked, past participle overlooked)

overlook:/ˌoʊvərˈlʊk/ /ˌəʊvəˈlʊks/ | n. | 俯瞰，眺望；高地 | A place from which one has a view of something from above. |  We had a picnic at a scenic overlook. 我们在一个风景优美的观景点野餐。| synonyms: viewpoint, vantage point | antonyms:  n/a | noun
