imbued:/ɪmˈbjuːd/ | adj. | 充满的，灌输的 | inspire or permeate with (a feeling or quality). |  The old house was imbued with a sense of history. 这座老房子充满了历史感。 |  例句：The speech was imbued with a sense of hope. 演讲充满了希望。 | 近义词：permeated, saturated, infused, steeped, instilled | 反义词：devoid, lacking, empty | 词性:形容词

imbued:/ɪmˈbjuːd/ | v. | 使充满，灌输 | past participle of imbue |  He was imbued with a strong sense of duty. 他被灌输了强烈的责任感。 | 例句：She was imbued with the spirit of adventure. 她充满了冒险精神。| 近义词：instill, inspire, permeate, infuse | 反义词：deprive, divest, strip | 词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *imbuen, imbuen, from Anglo-Norman embuer, from Old French embuir (“to moisten, soak, dye”), from Latin imbuere (“to wet, soak, dye, stain, imbue”), from in- (“in, into”) + buere (“to drink”).
