engineered:/ˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrd/ | adj. | 设计的，策划的，精密的 | Designed or modified, often skillfully or intricately; deliberately arranged or contrived. |  The car's engine is engineered for maximum fuel efficiency. 这辆车的发动机是为了最大限度地提高燃油效率而设计的。|  The politician gave a carefully engineered response to the scandal. 这位政客对这起丑闻做出了精心策划的回应。 |Synonyms: designed, contrived, planned, manipulated, constructed, fabricated | Antonyms: natural, unplanned, spontaneous, accidental | adjective
