oversentimental: /ˌoʊvərˌsɛntɪˈmɛntəl/ | adj. | 过于感伤的，多愁善感的 | Excessively sentimental; showing or expressing too much emotion, especially sadness or tenderness. |  She gets oversentimental at weddings. 她参加婚礼时总是过于感伤。 |  He wrote an oversentimental poem about his lost dog.  他写了一首关于他丢失的狗的过于感伤的诗。 | 近义词: mawkish, maudlin, saccharine, cloying, mushy | 反义词: unsentimental, hard-hearted, pragmatic | 词性: 形容词

词源: 
over- (prefix) + sentimental (adjective). The prefix "over-" means "excessively" or "too much." "Sentimental" comes from the French word "sentiment" meaning "feeling."
