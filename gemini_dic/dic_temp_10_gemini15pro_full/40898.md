cool:/kuːl/ | adj. n. v. | 凉爽的，冷静的，酷的 | Calm and composed. | 例句：He remained cool under pressure. 他在压力下保持冷静。 | 近义词：calm, composed, collected | 反义词：nervous, agitated, frantic | 词性:形容词

cool:/kuːl/ | n. | 凉爽，凉快 |  A cool temperature or place. | 例句：The shade offered a welcome cool.  阴凉处提供了受欢迎的凉爽。| 近义词：chill, coldness | 反义词：warmth, heat | 词性:名词

cool:/kuːl/ | v. | 冷却，变凉 | To become or make less hot. | 例句：Let the soup cool before you eat it.  让汤冷却一下再吃。| 近义词：chill, refrigerate | 反义词：heat, warm | 词性:动词
