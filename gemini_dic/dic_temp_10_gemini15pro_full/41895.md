cosinage:/ˈkʌzɪnɪdʒ/
n.
堂/表兄弟姐妹关系，血缘关系
The state or condition of being cousins; cousinship.  A system of inheritance by which cousins are preferred to other relatives.
The inheritance fell to him by right of cosinage.  这笔遗产根据血缘关系落到了他手里。
近义词:kinship, cousinship
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps estrangement or unrelatedness)
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *cosinage*, from *cosin* (cousin). Ultimately from Latin *consobrinus*.
