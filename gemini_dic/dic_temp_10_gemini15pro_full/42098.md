cotransporting:/ˌkoʊˈtrænsˌpɔːrtɪŋ/
verb
协同运输，共同转运
The process of transporting two or more different molecules or ions across a cell membrane simultaneously.  This transport can occur in the same direction (symport) or in opposite directions (antiport).
Sodium-glucose cotransporting is essential for glucose absorption in the intestine.  钠-葡萄糖协同转运对于肠道吸收葡萄糖至关重要。
The protein is responsible for cotransporting protons and sucrose into the cell.  这种蛋白质负责将质子和蔗糖共同转运到细胞内。
synonyms: coupled transport, symport, antiport
antonyms: uniport (though not a direct opposite, it represents a contrasting transport mechanism)
