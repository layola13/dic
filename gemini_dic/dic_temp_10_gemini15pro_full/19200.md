bewray:/bɪˈreɪ/
verb
揭发；泄露；暴露
to reveal or make known (something, typically something secret or discreditable)
His trembling hands bewrayed his nervousness. 他颤抖的双手暴露了他的紧张。
Synonyms: reveal, expose, disclose, betray, divulge
Antonyms: conceal, hide, disguise
词性: 动词
