misdial:/ˌmɪsˈdaɪəl/ /ˌmɪsˈdaɪl/
| v. | 拨错电话；拨错号码 | To dial a wrong telephone number. | I misdialed and got a wrong number. 我拨错了号码，接通了一个错误的电话。|近义词: wrong number | 反义词: correctly dial


词源: mis- (prefix meaning "wrongly") + dial (v. to operate a telephone dial)
