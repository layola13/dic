outmoded:/ˌaʊtˈmoʊdɪd/ | adj. | 过时的，不流行的 | No longer fashionable or useful; outdated. |  Her outmoded teaching methods weren't effective with the students. 她的过时的教学方法对学生们无效。|  The company's outmoded equipment needs to be replaced.  这家公司过时的设备需要更换。 | 近义词：old-fashioned, outdated, obsolete, archaic, antiquated | 反义词：modern, contemporary, current, up-to-date | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + mode + -ed.  "Mode" comes from Old French "mode" and ultimately from Latin "modus" meaning "manner, measure." The prefix "out-" signifies something beyond or surpassing, in this case surpassing its useful lifespan or fashionableness.
