oxonia:/ɒkˈsəʊniə/ | n. | 氧鎓离子 | A hydrated proton, represented by the formula H₃O⁺. It is formed when a proton (H⁺) bonds to a water molecule (H₂O). |  The concentration of oxonia ions determines the acidity of a solution. 氧鎓离子的浓度决定了溶液的酸度。| synonyms: hydronium ion, hydroxonium | antonyms: hydroxide ion | noun
