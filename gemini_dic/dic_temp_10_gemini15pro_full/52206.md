diastole:/daɪˈæstəli/ /daɪˈæstəʊli/| n.| 心脏舒张；舒张期|The period between two contractions of the heart, during which the heart refills with blood.| During diastole, the heart muscle relaxes and allows the chambers to fill with blood. 心脏舒张期，心肌放松，让心腔充满血液。| synonym: relaxation| antonym: systole| noun
