caird:/kɛərd/ | n. |石堆，石冢；堆石路标|A heap of stones, especially one marking a route in a desolate area or erected as a memorial or landmark. |例句：The hikers followed the cairns across the barren landscape.  徒者们沿着石堆穿过荒凉的景色。

例句：A cairn marked the summit of the mountain. 一堆石冢标志着山顶。|近义词：barrow, mound, pile, heap |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps valley or depression if referring to terrain)|词性:名词
