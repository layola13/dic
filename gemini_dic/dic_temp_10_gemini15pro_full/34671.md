clericalism:/ˈklerɪkəlɪzəm/
n.
教权主义；神职人员统治；僧侣统治
A belief that the clergy are superior and should have special privileges and power, often involving interference in secular affairs.
The report criticized the pervasive clericalism in the country's political system. 该报告批评了该国政治体系中普遍存在的教权主义。

The growing clericalism in the region has led to increased restrictions on women's rights. 该地区日益增长的教权主义导致对妇女权利的限制越来越严格。


近义词: priestcraft, hierocracy
反义词: secularism, laity
词性: noun
