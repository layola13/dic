functionalistic:/ˌfʌŋkʃənəˈlɪstɪk/
|adj.|
功能主义的；实用主义的|
Relating to or characteristic of functionalism.  Pertaining to the theory that mental states can be sufficiently defined by their function, i.e., their causal role with respect to sensory inputs, behavioral outputs, and other mental states.|
例句：The architect's functionalistic approach prioritized practicality over aesthetics.  这位建筑师的功能主义方法将实用性置于美学之上。

例句：Her functionalistic furniture was modern and comfortable, but lacked personal style. 她的功能主义家具现代而舒适，但缺乏个人风格。
|近义词：practical, pragmatic, utilitarian|
反义词：aesthetic, ornamental, decorative|
词性: adjective
