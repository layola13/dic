nonscarcities:/nɒnˈskɛəsɪtiz/
n.
非稀缺性，非匮乏
Things that are not scarce; resources or commodities that are readily available and abundant.
Clean air and water are becoming nonscarcities in developed countries thanks to technological advancements.  由于科技的进步，清洁的空气和水在发达国家正变得不再稀缺。
synonyms: abundance, surplus, plentifulness
antonyms: scarcities, shortages, deficiencies
noun
