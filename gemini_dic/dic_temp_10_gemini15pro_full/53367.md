disaffiliation:/dɪsˌəˌfɪliˈeɪʃən/
n.|脱离，断绝关系|The act of officially ending a connection or affiliation with a group or organization. |The union voted for disaffiliation from the national federation. 工会投票决定脱离国家联盟。|
disaffiliation:/dɪsˌəˌfɪliˈeɪʃən/
n.|脱离关系|The state of having ended a connection or affiliation with a group or organization.|His disaffiliation from the political party caused controversy. 他脱离政党一事引发了争议。|
近义词:separation, detachment, divorce, disassociation
反义词:affiliation, association, connection, union
