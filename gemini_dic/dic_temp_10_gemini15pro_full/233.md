abdicant: /ˈæbdɪkənt/ | adj. n. | 退位的；放弃权利的；退位者 |  Relinquishing or renouncing a throne, right, power, claim, responsibility, or the like, especially in a formal manner.  | The abdicant king retired to a quiet life in the countryside. 退位的国王在乡下过起了平静的生活。

The abdicant emperor spent his remaining years in exile. 退位的皇帝在流亡中度过了余生。 | 近义词: resigning, renouncing, relinquishing | 反义词:  assuming, usurping, seizing | 词性: adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abdicant-* (present participle of *abdicare* "to renounce, disown, reject"), from *ab-* "away, from" + *dicare* "to proclaim, dedicate, set apart, devote".
