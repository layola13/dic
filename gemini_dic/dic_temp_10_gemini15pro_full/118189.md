mentor-ships:/ˈmɛntɔrˌʃɪps/ | n. | 师徒关系；指导关系 | A relationship in which a more experienced or knowledgeable person helps guide a less experienced or knowledgeable person. | The company's mentorship program pairs senior employees with new hires.  该公司设立的师徒制，将老员工与新员工结成对子。 | Synonyms: guidance, tutelage, coaching, apprenticeship | Antonyms: neglect, abandonment | noun

mentor-ships:/ˈmɛntɔrˌʃɪps/ | n. | 导师制度 | A system or program that facilitates mentorship relationships. | The university has a strong mentorship program for graduate students. 这所大学为研究生设立了完善的导师制度。 | Synonyms: guidance program, development program, coaching program | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps) independent study, self-learning | noun
