homographic:/ˌhɒməˈɡræfɪk/ /ˌhoʊməˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 同形异义的 | Having the same spelling as another word but a different meaning and origin. |
例句：The words "lead" (the metal) and "lead" (to guide) are homographic.  铅（金属）和领导（引导）这两个词是同形异义词。

例句：The words "bow" (to bend at the waist) and "bow" (the front of a ship) are homographic. 鞠躬（弯腰）和船头（船的前部）这两个词是同形异义词。

近义词：homonymous, homonymic | 反义词：N/A | 词性：adjective

词源：
From Greek *homo-* (same) + *-graphos* (written).
