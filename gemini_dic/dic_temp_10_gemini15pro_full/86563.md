helicin:/ˈhɛlɪsɪn/| n.|螺旋蛋白;蜗牛酶|A protein found in the mucus of snails that has antibiotic properties.  It is a member of the hemocyanin superfamily.|Snails use helicin to protect themselves from bacterial infections.|近义词: mucin, glycoprotein|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
