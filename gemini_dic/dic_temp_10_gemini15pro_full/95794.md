inconveniencing:/ˌɪnkənˈviːniənsiŋ/
v.
使不便，打扰
Causing trouble or difficulty to someone.
I hope I'm not inconveniencing you by asking you to do this. 我希望我让你做这件事不会给你带来不便。
近义词: bothering, disturbing, troubling
反义词: accommodating, assisting, helping
词性: 动词

inconvenience:/ˌɪnkənˈviːniəns/
n.
不便，麻烦
Trouble or difficulty caused to someone.
The flight delay caused a great deal of inconvenience to passengers. 航班延误给乘客带来了极大的不便。
近义词: trouble, difficulty, bother
反义词: convenience, ease, comfort
词性: 名词

inconvenient:/ˌɪnkənˈviːniənt/
adj.
不方便的，麻烦的
Causing trouble or difficulty.
It's inconvenient for me to meet you tomorrow. 明天与你见面对我来说不方便。
近义词: troublesome, awkward, difficult
反义词: convenient, suitable, easy
词性: 形容词


词源:
From in- (not) + convenient.  The verb form "inconvenience" arises from the noun.
