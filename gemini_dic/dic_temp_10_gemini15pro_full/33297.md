chubbiest: /ˈtʃʌbiɪst/ | adj. | 最胖的，最圆乎乎的 | Superlative form of chubby: plump and rounded.  It describes someone (especially a baby or child) as being pleasantly fat. |  That baby is the chubbiest I've ever seen!  那个婴儿是我见过的最胖的！|  Example:  Her cheeks were chubbiest when she was a toddler. 她蹒跚学步时脸蛋最胖。 | Synonyms: plumpest, roundest, tubbiest | Antonyms: thinnest, slimmest | adjective
