clumplike:/ˈklʌmplaɪk/
adjective
丛生的，成块的
Resembling a clump; growing or occurring in clumps.
The bacteria formed clumplike colonies in the petri dish.  细菌在培养皿中形成了丛生的菌落。
The fungus grew in clumplike formations on the tree bark.  真菌在树皮上以丛生的形式生长。
近义词: clustered, clumped, grouped
反义词: scattered, dispersed
词性: adjective

词源: clump + like.  Clump (n.) comes from Middle Low German klumpe, meaning "lump, mass."
