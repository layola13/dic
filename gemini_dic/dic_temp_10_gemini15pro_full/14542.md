averruncator:/əˌvɛrʌnˈkeɪtər/| n. |修枝剪，高枝剪|A long-handled pruning tool with jaws operated by a rope and lever, used for cutting high branches.|The gardener used an averruncator to trim the top of the oak tree. 园丁用高枝剪修剪了橡树的顶部。|近义词：pruner, lopper, shears, secateurs|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源:
From Latin *averruncātor*, from *averruncāre* (“to avert, ward off”), from *ab-* (“away from”) + *erruncāre* (“to weed out”).  The tool's original purpose was likely to cut away unwanted growth, hence the name.
