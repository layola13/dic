death-warrant:/ˈdeθ ˌwɔːrənt/ | n. | 死刑执行令 | A document authorizing the execution of a condemned person. | The judge signed the death warrant. 法官签署了死刑执行令。| synonyms: execution order, death sentence | antonyms: pardon, reprieve | noun

death-warrant:/ˈdeθ ˌwɔːrənt/ | n. | 死亡判决；催命符 | Something that makes death or failure inevitable.  | The diagnosis of the disease was a death warrant for him.  这个疾病的诊断对他来说无异于死亡判决。 | synonyms: kiss of death,  nail in the coffin | antonyms: lifeline,  godsend | noun
