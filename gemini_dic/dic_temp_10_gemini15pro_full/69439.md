feet: /fiːt/ /fit/ | n. | 脚，英尺 | The plural form of foot.  A unit of length equal to 12 inches or 30.48 centimeters. Also refers to the lowest part of the leg, below the ankle. |  She stood with her bare feet in the grass. 她赤脚站在草地上。 The table is three feet high. 这桌子三英尺高。 | synonyms:  foot (singular), tootsies (informal) | antonyms:  head (opposite end of the body) | noun
