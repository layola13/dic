allogamous:/əˈlɒɡəməs/
adjective
异花授粉的
Characterized by allogamy; cross-fertilizing.
例句：Allogamous plants rely on external factors like wind or insects for pollination. 异花授粉植物依靠风或昆虫等外部因素进行授粉。

例句：The allogamous nature of this species contributes to its genetic diversity.  该物种的异花授粉特性有助于其遗传多样性。

近义词：cross-fertilizing, cross-pollinating
反义词：autogamous, self-fertilizing
词性：adjective
词源：From allo- + -gamous (from Greek gamos, marriage)
