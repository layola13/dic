frappe:/fræp/ (US), /frap/ (US informal), /fræpeɪ/ (UK)| n.|冰沙，奶昔；（加冰块的）浓缩咖啡|A blended iced drink, often made with milk, ice cream, and flavorings, or a chilled espresso drink poured over crushed ice.|例句：I ordered a caramel frappe. 我点了一杯焦糖冰沙。

例句：The coffee frappe was a refreshing treat on a hot day.  在炎热的天气里，咖啡冰沙是一种提神的享受。|近义词：smoothie, milkshake, iced coffee, frappuccino|反义词: hot coffee, hot chocolate|词性:noun

frappes:/fræps/ (US, plural), /fræpeɪz/ (UK, plural)|n.|冰沙（复数）；奶昔（复数）|(Plural form of "frappe") Blended iced drinks, often made with milk, ice cream, and flavorings, or chilled espresso drinks poured over crushed ice.|例句：We ordered two frappes, one strawberry and one chocolate. 我们点了两杯冰沙，一杯草莓的，一杯巧克力的。

例句：The frappes were the perfect way to cool down after a long walk. 这些冰沙是在长途跋涉后放松身心的完美方式。|近义词：smoothies, milkshakes, iced coffees, frappuccinos|反义词: hot coffees, hot chocolates|词性:noun
