docimacy:/ˌdɒsɪˈmeɪsi/ /ˌdɑːsɪˈmeɪsi/| n. |鉴定；化验；分析；检验|The process of testing or assaying to determine ingredients or quality, especially of ores or drugs. It also refers to the testing of newborns to determine their viability. |The docimacy of the ancient coins revealed their composition. 对古代硬币的鉴定揭示了它们的成分。|近义词：assay, analysis, test, examination|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *docimac(i)a, from Ancient Greek δοκιμασία (dokimasía, “test, trial, proof”), from δοκιμάζω (dokimázō, “I test, try, prove”), from δόκιμος (dókimos, “tested, proven, approved”).
