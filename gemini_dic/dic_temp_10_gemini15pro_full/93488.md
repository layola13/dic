illustriously: /ɪˈlʌstriəsli/
adverb
辉煌地；杰出地；著名地
in a way that is very distinguished; famously
例句：He served his country illustriously for many years. 他多年来杰出地为国家服务。

例句：She has had a long and illustrious career in medicine. 她在医学领域有着漫长而辉煌的职业生涯。

近义词：brilliantly, famously, notably, remarkably, splendidly, prominently
反义词：ingloriously, obscurely, insignificantly
词性：副词

词源:
From Late Latin illustriōsus (“illustrious”), from Latin illustris (“bright, clear, renowned”) + -ōsus.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *leukin- (“to shine, light”).
