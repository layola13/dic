altocumulus:/ˌæltəʊˈkjuːmjələs/ | n. | 高积云 | A type of middle-altitude cloud characterized by rounded masses or rolls, typically white or gray.  They often appear in sheets or layers. |  Example: The altocumulus clouds drifted lazily across the sky.  高积云在天空中懒洋洋地飘着。| Example:  We saw beautiful altocumulus formations during our flight.  我们在飞行中看到了美丽的高积云层。 | Synonym:  cloud,  | Antonym:  clear sky | noun
