election: /ɪˈlɛkʃ(ə)n/ (US) /ɪˈlɛkʃən/ (UK)| n. |选举；当选；选择|The process of choosing a person or a group of people for a position, especially a political position, by voting. |The upcoming presidential election is generating a lot of interest.  即将到来的总统选举引起了很多关注。|近义词：vote, poll, ballot |反义词: appointment, nomination |词性: noun

Etymology: From Old French *eleccion, from Latin ēlectiō (“choice, selection”), from ēligō (“choose, select”).
