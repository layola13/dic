overpreoccupying: /ˌoʊvərpriːˈɑːkjʊpaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 过分关注的，过分投入的 | Excessively preoccupied; dominating one's thoughts to an undue degree.  This adjective describes something that consumes someone's attention to an excessive extent. |  Her overpreoccupying thoughts about the deadline affected her sleep. 她对截止日期的过度关注影响了她的睡眠。 | The overpreoccupying nature of his new job left him little time for his family.  他新工作的过度投入让他很少有时间陪伴家人。| 近义词:  consuming, obsessive, all-consuming, preoccupying | 反义词:  inattentive, indifferent, unconcerned | 词性: adjective

词源:
From "over-" (meaning "excessive") + "preoccupy" (from Latin "praeoccupare" - to seize beforehand).  The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb "preoccupy" into a present participle adjective.
