overbrag: /ˌoʊvərˈbræɡ/ | v. | 过度吹嘘，夸大其词 | To boast or brag excessively, often to the point of being unbelievable or irritating. | He overbragged about his accomplishments, making everyone uncomfortable. 他过度吹嘘自己的成就，让每个人都感到不舒服。| She overbragged about her new car, going into excruciating detail about its features. 她过度吹嘘她的新车，详细地讲述了它的各种功能，让人难以忍受。 | 近义词：boast, brag, exaggerate, gloat | 反义词：downplay, understate | 词性：动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "brag" (verb meaning "to boast").  The prefix intensifies the meaning of "brag."
