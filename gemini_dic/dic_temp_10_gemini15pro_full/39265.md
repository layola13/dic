congressed:/ˈkɒŋɡrɛst/ |v. |参加会议；开会|To have held a congress or formal meeting; to have assembled in a congress. |The scientists congressed in London to discuss the latest findings. 科学家们在伦敦召开会议，讨论最新的发现。| The diplomats congressed to negotiate a peace treaty. 外交官们开会谈判和平条约。|近义词: assembled, convened, gathered, met|反义词: dispersed, adjourned, separated|词性: 动词

词源: From the noun "congress," ultimately from Latin "congressus," past participle of "congredi" (to come together), from "com-" (together) + "gradi" (to walk).
