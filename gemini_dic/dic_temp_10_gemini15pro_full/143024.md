outbleated:/ˌaʊtˈbliːtɪd/
| v. | 比...叫得更大声;  胜过(尤指用哭喊声) | To cry louder than; to surpass or overcome, especially by loud cries or complaints. | The grieving mother outbleated the mourners at the funeral. 这位悲痛的母亲在葬礼上哭喊的声音比哀悼者还要大。| 近义词: outshout, outcry, outwail | 反义词: whimper, murmur | 词性: 动词

词源: out- + bleat. "Out-" prefix indicating surpassing or exceeding. "Bleat" meaning the cry of a sheep or goat, or a similar sound.
