complaisance:/kəmˈpleɪzəns/ | n. | 殷勤；顺从；彬彬有礼 | disposition to please or comply; affability |  She showed great complaisance to her demanding boss. 她对苛刻的老板表现得十分殷勤。| synonyms: obligingness, agreeableness, courtesy, deference | antonyms:  rudeness,  discourtesy,  intransigence | noun

complaisant:/kəmˈpleɪzənt/ | adj. | 殷勤的；顺从的；彬彬有礼的 | showing a disposition to please or comply; affable | He was always complaisant to his guests. 他对客人总是彬彬有礼。| synonyms:  obliging, agreeable, courteous, deferential | antonyms:  rude,  discourteous,  intransigent | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French complaisance, from complaisant "obliging," present participle of complaire "to please," from Latin complacere "to be very pleasing," from com- "with" (see com-) + placere "to please" (see please).
