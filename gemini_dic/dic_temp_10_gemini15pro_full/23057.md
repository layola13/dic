bounciest:/ˈbaʊnsiɪst/| adj.|最有弹性的，最活泼的|Superlative form of 'bouncy,' meaning having a lot of bounce or elasticity; also used figuratively to describe someone lively and energetic.|That new basketball is the bounciest one I've ever used. 那颗新篮球是我用过弹性最好的。|The bounciest child in the class was always skipping and jumping. 班上最活泼的孩子总是蹦蹦跳跳的。|近义词：springiest, liveliest, most energetic|反义词：flattest, dullest, least energetic|词性:形容词
词源: From "bounce" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives) + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Bounce" originates from Middle English "bouncen," likely of imitative origin.
