diachronicness:/ˌdaɪəˈkrɒnɪknəs/ | n. |历时性 |The state or quality of being diachronic; consideration of something across time.  | Examining the diachronicness of language evolution reveals how words change meaning over centuries.  对语言演变历时性的考察揭示了几个世纪以来词义是如何变化的。 |synonyms: historicity, temporal development, evolution over time | antonyms: synchronicity, simultaneity| noun


diachronic:/ˌdaɪəˈkrɒnɪk/ | adj. |历时的，跨越时间的 | Relating to changes in something, especially language, over time. | A diachronic study of the word "nice" reveals its surprising origins. 对“nice”一词的历时性研究揭示了其令人惊讶的起源。| synonyms: historical, evolutionary | antonyms: synchronic, simultaneous| adjective
