basters:/ˈbeɪstərz/
n.|私生子；粗劣的替代品；粗制滥造的东西|A person born to parents who are not married to each other; something that is a poor imitation or substitute.|The film portrays the struggles of single mothers raising their baster children. 这部电影描绘了单身母亲抚养私生子的挣扎。|Synonyms: illegitimate children, bastards (vulgar), offspring (formal), children born out of wedlock|Antonyms: legitimate children|noun

basters:/ˈbeɪstərz/
n.|烤肉刷；滴油管；吸管|A kitchen utensil used for basting meat; a tube used for transferring liquids, especially in cooking.|She used a baster to keep the turkey moist while it roasted. 她用滴油管在烤火鸡时保持其湿润。|Synonyms:  dropper, pipette, syringe|Antonyms: N/A|noun
