nonfashionably:/nɒnˈfæʃənəbli/ | adv. | 不时尚地，不流行地 | Not in a fashionable way; in a way that is out of style. | She dressed nonfashionably, preferring comfort to style. 她穿着不时尚，更喜欢舒适而不是时尚。| He wore a nonfashionably long coat. 他穿着一件过时的长外套。 | 近义词: unfashionably, outmodedly, datedly | 反义词: fashionably, stylishly, trendily | 词性: 副词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + fashionably (adverb derived from "fashionable," which comes from "fashion" ultimately from Old French "façon")
