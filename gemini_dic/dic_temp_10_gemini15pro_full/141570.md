oppositionist:/ˌɒpəˈzɪʃənɪst/ | n. |反对派，反对者|A person who opposes a policy or government, especially one in a position of authority.|例句：The oppositionist voiced his concerns about the new economic policy. 反对派人士表达了对新经济政策的担忧。

例句：The government arrested several oppositionists after the protest.  抗议活动后，政府逮捕了几名反对派人士。|近义词：dissident, objector, antagonist, critic, opponent |反义词：supporter, proponent, advocate|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *oppositionniste*, from *opposition* (opposition), from Latin *oppositionem* (opposition), from *opponere* (to oppose).
