overphilosophized: /ˌōvərˌfɪləˈsɒfaɪzd/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˌfɪləˈsɒfaɪzd/ (UK) | adj. | 过度哲学化的，过度思辨的 |  Analyzed or discussed in an excessively philosophical or abstract way, often to the point of impracticality or obscurity.  It implies that something has been thought about too much, making it more complex than it needs to be. |  The author overphilosophized the simple act of walking, turning a mundane experience into a treatise on existentialism.  作者把简单的行走行为过度哲学化，将一个平凡的经历变成了存在主义的论文。


The movie's plot was so overphilosophized that it lost all sense of narrative drive.  这部电影的情节过度思辨，以至于失去了所有的叙事动力。


近义词: overanalyzed, overthought, intellectualized | 反义词: simplistic, straightforward, pragmatic | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "philosophize" (verb meaning "to reason in a philosophical manner") + "-ed" (past participle suffix)
