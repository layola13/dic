outthrowing:/ˌaʊtˈθroʊɪŋ/
v.
投掷得比…更远
Throwing further than someone or something else.
He was outthrowing all his competitors in the javelin event. 他在标枪比赛中投掷得比所有竞争对手都远。
outdistance, surpass, exceed
fall short of, lag behind
verb
