excoriate: /ɪkˈskɔːrieɪt/ /ɛkˈskɔːrieɪt/
verb
严厉批评；痛斥
to criticize severely; to denounce or berate strongly.
The newspaper excoriated the politician for his corrupt dealings. 这家报纸严厉批评了这位政客的腐败行为。
The coach excoriated the team for their lackluster performance. 教练痛斥了球队的毫无生气。

近义词: criticize, condemn, denounce, censure, lambaste, castigate
反义词: praise, commend, laud, compliment
