overferventness:/ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrvəntnəs/ | n. | 过分热情，过度热心 | The state of being excessively fervent or passionate.  | His overferventness about the new project alienated some of his colleagues. 他对新项目的过度热情疏远了一些同事。 |  Synonyms:  excessiveness, extravagance, immoderation, vehemence, zeal | Antonyms: apathy, indifference, coolness | noun

overfervent:/ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrvənt/ | adj. | 过分热情的，过度热心的 | Excessively fervent or passionate. |  His overfervent pleas for forgiveness were met with silence. 他过度热切的恳求原谅，却只得到了沉默。| Synonyms:  excessive, extravagant, immoderate, vehement, zealous | Antonyms: apathetic, indifferent, cool | adjective

Etymology: "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "fervent" (from Old French fervent, from Latin fervens, present participle of fervēre "to boil, glow").
