acquir:/əˈkwaɪər/|v.|获得，取得，学到|To come into possession or control of something; to obtain or gain something, typically through effort or experience.|She acquired a taste for fine wines while living in France. = 在法国生活期间，她培养了对优质葡萄酒的品味。

He acquired a new skill through online courses. = 他通过在线课程学习了一项新技能。|近义词：obtain, gain, learn, attain, secure|反义词：lose, forfeit, relinquish|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *aquere (Modern French acquérir), from Latin acquīrere "to get in addition, accumulate, acquire, gain," from ad- "to" + quaerere "to seek, gain."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He acquires knowledge quickly. = 他学习知识很快。
She acquires new clients through networking. = 她通过人际关系网获得新客户。

一般过去时:
They acquired the company last year. = 他们去年收购了这家公司。
He acquired a valuable painting at the auction. = 他在拍卖会上获得了一幅珍贵的画作。

现在完成时:
We have acquired several new properties this year. = 今年我们已经收购了几处新房产。
She has acquired a reputation for excellence. = 她已经赢得了卓越的声誉。

过去完成时:
By the time he was 30, he had acquired a substantial fortune. = 到他30岁时，他已经积累了大量的财富。
They had acquired the land before the development boom. = 他们在开发热潮之前就获得了这块土地。

将来时:
The company will acquire a new subsidiary next month. = 该公司下个月将收购一家新的子公司。
He will acquire the necessary skills for the job through training. = 他将通过培训获得这份工作所需的技能。

虚拟语气:
If I were to acquire a new car, I would choose an electric one. = 如果我要买一辆新车，我会选择电动汽车。
I wish I could acquire fluency in another language. = 我希望我能流利地说另一种语言。

被动语态:
The company was acquired by a larger corporation. = 这家公司被一家更大的公司收购了。
New knowledge is acquired through experience. = 新知识是通过经验获得的。

疑问句:
How did they acquire such a vast collection of art? = 他们是如何获得如此大量的艺术收藏的？
Where did she acquire her language skills? = 她在哪里学习的语言技能？

否定句:
They did not acquire the property legally. = 他们没有合法地获得该财产。
She hasn't acquired the necessary qualifications for the position. = 她还没有获得该职位所需的资格。
