overseas:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːz/ /ˌəʊvəˈsiːz/| adj. adv.|海外的，国外的；在海外，在国外|Situated or occurring beyond the sea; in a foreign country.|Many companies are now outsourcing jobs overseas. 许多公司现在把工作外包到海外。|近义词：abroad, foreign|反义词:domestic, local|词性:形容词, 副词
overseas:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːz/ /ˌəʊvəˈsiːz/| n.|海外，外国|Countries across the sea; foreign lands.|He spent a year overseas. 他在海外待了一年。|近义词：abroad, foreign lands|反义词:homeland|词性:名词
