discursory:/dɪˈskɜːrsəri/ /dɪˈskəːsəri/
adjective
粗略的，草率的，仓促的
passing over a subject quickly and superficially
He gave a discursory glance at the report. 他粗略地看了一眼报告。
近义词：superficial, hasty, perfunctory, cursory, brief, shallow
反义词：thorough, exhaustive, comprehensive, in-depth, detailed, meticulous
词性: adjective
