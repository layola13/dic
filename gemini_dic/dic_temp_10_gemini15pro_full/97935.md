innovated: /ˈɪnəveɪtɪd/
verb
创新（过去式/过去分词）
introduced something new; made changes in anything established.
例句：The company innovated a new approach to customer service.  这家公司创新了客户服务的新方法。

例句：They innovated by using recycled materials in their products.  他们通过在产品中使用回收材料进行创新。

近义词：pioneered, introduced, developed, created, modernized, revolutionized
反义词：copied, imitated, stagnated, regressed
