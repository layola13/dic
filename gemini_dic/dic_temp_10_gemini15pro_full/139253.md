obliged: /əˈblaɪdʒd/ | adj. | 感激的；不得不做的；有义务的 | Feeling or showing gratitude; compelled by circumstances or legally or morally bound to do something. |  I'm much obliged to you for your help. 我非常感谢你的帮助。 | 近义词：grateful, thankful, indebted, compelled, required | 反义词：ungrateful, unthankful, exempt | 词性: adjective

obliged: /əˈblaɪdʒd/ | v. |  施恩惠于；迫使；使负有义务 (past tense and past participle of oblige) | To do a favor or service for; to compel or require someone to do something by law, morality, or circumstances. |  He felt obliged to help her. 他觉得有义务帮助她。 | 近义词：compel, require, force, coerce | 反义词：exempt, excuse, release | 词性: verb (past tense and past participle)
