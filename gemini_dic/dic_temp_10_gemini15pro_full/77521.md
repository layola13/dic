gazunder:/ɡæˈzʌndə(r)/
n.
压低出价；临时杀价
The act of reducing an offer for a house purchase at the last minute, typically just before contracts are exchanged.
The seller was gazundered just two days before completion. 卖家在完成交易前两天被临时杀价了。
近义词: downvaluation, price reduction
反义词: gazumping
