billings:/ˈbɪlɪŋz/
n.
账单；开票；营业额；(电影等的)宣传广告
the process of sending people bills for goods or services; the total amount of money owed by customers for goods or services; the amount of business done by a company in a particular period; advertisements for a film, play etc
The company's billings increased by 20% last year.  这家公司的营业额去年增长了20%。
The monthly billings are sent out on the first of the month. 每月的账单都在1号寄出。
Synonyms: invoices, accounts, charges, fees, revenue, takings, receipts, proceeds
Antonyms: payments, credits
noun
