chicalote:/ˌtʃiːkəˈloʊteɪ/| n. |罂粟;罂粟的果实;罂粟籽; (尤指墨西哥北部和美国西南部地区生长的) 一种罂粟|A type of poppy, especially one native to northern Mexico and the southwestern US.  It refers to the plant itself, its seed pods, or the seeds. |The chicalote flowers are a beautiful orange.  这种罂粟的花是美丽的橙色。|The chicalote seeds are used in some traditional dishes. 这种罂粟籽用于一些传统菜肴中。|Synonyms: poppy, prickly poppy (specifically Argemone spp.)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
