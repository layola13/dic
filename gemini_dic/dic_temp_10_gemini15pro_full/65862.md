evoke:/ɪˈvoʊk/ (US) /ɪˈvəʊk/ (UK)| v. |唤起，引起，使人想起|to bring a feeling, a memory or an image into your mind|The music evoked memories of her childhood. 这音乐唤起了她童年的回忆。|近义词： elicit, arouse, inspire, summon, conjure |反义词: suppress, quell, stifle |verb

evoke:/ɪˈvoʊk/ (US) /ɪˈvəʊk/ (UK)| v. |引起，激起（尤指反应）|to cause a particular reaction or response in sb/sth|The announcement evoked an angry response from the public. 这项声明激起了公众的愤怒反应。|近义词：provoke, cause, generate, trigger |反义词: soothe, calm, placate |verb
