gunfighting:/ˈɡʌnˌfaɪtɪŋ/
n.
枪战，交火
The act or practice of fighting with guns.
The movie depicted intense gunfighting in the streets of the Wild West.  这部电影描绘了西部荒野街道上激烈的枪战。
shootout, firefight, battle, combat
peace, negotiation
