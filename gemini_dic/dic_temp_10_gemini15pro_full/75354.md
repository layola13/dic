frother:/ˈfrɒðər/ (UK) /ˈfrɑːðər/ (US)| n.|起泡器，奶泡器；（俚语）狂热者，兴奋过度的人|A device used to make froth, especially on milk for coffee.  Also, a person who is overly enthusiastic or excited about something.|例句：He used a milk frother to create the perfect cappuccino. 他用奶泡器做出了完美的卡布奇诺。

例句：She's a real frother about that new band. 她对那个新乐队非常狂热。|近义词：foamer, enthusiast, fanatic|反义词:critic, skeptic|noun
