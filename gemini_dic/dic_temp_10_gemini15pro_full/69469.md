feistiness:/ˈfaɪstɪnɪs/
n.
活泼; 泼辣; 好斗
The quality of being feisty; spiritedness and determination.
She showed her feistiness by challenging the decision. 她通过挑战这个决定展现了她的泼辣。
Synonyms:  spirit, spunk, pluck, grit, gumption, fire, mettle, nerve, pep, sass, vinegar
Antonyms:  timidity, meekness, submissiveness, apathy, docility
