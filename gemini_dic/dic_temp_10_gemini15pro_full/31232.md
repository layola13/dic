chanterelles:/ˌʃæntəˈrɛl/ /ˌʃɑːntəˈrɛl/| n. |鸡油菌|An edible mushroom, typically yellow or orange, with a funnel-shaped cap and a fruity aroma.|例句：We found a patch of chanterelles in the woods. 我们在树林里发现了一片鸡油菌。|例句：Chanterelles are prized for their delicate flavor. 鸡油菌因其细腻的味道而备受珍视。|近义词：golden chanterelle, girolle|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
