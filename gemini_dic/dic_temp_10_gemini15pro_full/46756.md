dandyize:/ˈdændɪˌaɪz/| vt.| 纨绔化；使...像花花公子；打扮成花花公子| To make or become a dandy; to make something more refined or elegant, sometimes excessively so. | He dandyized his apartment with expensive furniture and art. 他用昂贵的家具和艺术品把他的公寓装饰得像花花公子一样。| synonyms: refine, embellish, beautify | antonyms: simplify, coarsen, vulgarize | verb

dandyize:/ˈdændɪˌaɪz/| vt.| 纨绔化；使...像花花公子；打扮成花花公子|  To cause to resemble a dandy; to give a foppish character to.  |  He tried to dandyize his image with a new suit and haircut. 他试图通过一套新西装和新发型来使自己的形象更像个花花公子。| synonyms:  embellish, polish, refine | antonyms:  simplify, coarsen, roughen | verb
