antiarin:/ænˈtaɪərɪn/ | n. | 抗毒素，毒毛旋花子苷 | A highly toxic cardiac glycoside obtained from the upas tree (Antiaris toxicaria). It is a potent heart poison. |  The indigenous people used antiarin as arrow poison for hunting.  土著居民用抗毒素作为箭毒来狩猎。

The researchers isolated antiarin to study its effects on the heart. 研究人员分离出抗毒素来研究它对心脏的影响。| 近义词: cardiac glycoside, heart poison | 反义词: antidote | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *Antiaris (the genus name of the upas tree), from Javanese *ancar (“upas tree”), + -in (a suffix indicating a chemical substance).  Ultimately from Proto-Malayo-Polynesian *ha(n)car.
