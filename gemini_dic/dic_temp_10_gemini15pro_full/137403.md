nontenurial:/ˌnɒnˈtɛnjʊəriəl/
adjective
非保有土地的；非土地所有制的
Relating to or denoting rights or property that do not involve the ownership of land.
The company's nontenurial assets include patents and copyrights.  这家公司的非土地保有资产包括专利权和版权。
Nontenurial rights, such as grazing rights, can be valuable.  非土地所有权，例如放牧权，可能很有价值。
近义词:incorporeal, intangible
反义词:tenurial, landed
词性:adjective
