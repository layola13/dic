capitalisms:/ˈkæpɪtəlɪzəmz/
n.
资本主义（复数）
Capitalisms refers to different forms or variations of the capitalist economic system. This can include distinctions like state capitalism, laissez-faire capitalism, or welfare capitalism.
Many scholars debate the merits of different capitalisms across the globe. 全球许多学者都在争论不同资本主义的优劣。
The various capitalisms of the 20th century evolved in response to changing global conditions.  20世纪的各种资本主义是随着全球形势的变化而演变的。
近义词: capitalist systems
反义词: socialisms, communisms
词性: Noun
