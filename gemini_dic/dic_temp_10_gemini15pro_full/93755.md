immeasurability:/ɪˌmɛʒərəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可估量；无限；无边无际
The quality of being impossible to measure.
The immeasurability of the universe makes it a source of wonder and awe. 宇宙的浩瀚无垠使它成为奇迹和敬畏的源泉。
immensity, vastness, boundlessness
measurability, finitude, limitedness
noun
