blandishments:/ˈblændɪʃmənts/ | n. | 奉承；甜言蜜语；诱惑 | something that is said or done to persuade someone to do something |  He refused to be moved by their blandishments. 他不为他们的甜言蜜语所动。 | synonyms: flattery, cajolery, coaxing, persuasion, enticement | antonyms: threats, intimidation, criticism, reprimands | noun

Etymology:
From Middle French blandissement, from blandir "to flatter, caress," from Latin blandiri "to flatter, fawn upon."
