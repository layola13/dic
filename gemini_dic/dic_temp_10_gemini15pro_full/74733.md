freest:/friːɪst/| adj.|最自由的|superlative form of free; most free|The freest society is one where individuals can express themselves without fear.|近义词：most liberated, most independent|反义词: most restricted, most confined|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English freest, superlative of fre, from Old English frēo (“free, exempt, noble, joyful, generous”), from Proto-Germanic *frijaz (“free”), from Proto-Indo-European *priH-yós (“dear, beloved; free”).
