nonplays:/ˈnɒnpleɪz/ (US) /ˈnɒnpleɪz/ (UK)| n.|非比赛项目，非戏剧作品|Things that are not plays, such as novels, poems, or essays.  It typically refers to dramatic works that are not intended for stage performance, or to activities or content that are not part of a game or performance.|例句：His nonplays include several novels and collections of poetry. 他的非戏剧作品包括几部小说和诗集。

例句：The festival featured both plays and nonplays. 这个节日既有戏剧也有非戏剧作品。|近义词：nondramatic works, other writings|反义词:plays, dramas|词性:noun
