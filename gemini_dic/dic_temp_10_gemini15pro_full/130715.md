nonalkaloid:/ˌnɒnˈælkəlɔɪd/
adjective
非生物碱的
Not being or containing an alkaloid.
Plants produce a wide variety of secondary metabolites, including nonalkaloid compounds like terpenes and phenols.  植物产生各种各样的次生代谢物，包括萜烯和酚类等非生物碱化合物。
Certain medicinal herbs contain nonalkaloid active ingredients. 某些药草含有非生物碱活性成分。
Synonyms: non-alkaloidal
Antonyms: alkaloid
词性: adjective
