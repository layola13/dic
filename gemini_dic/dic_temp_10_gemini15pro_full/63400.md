entozoologist:/ˌɛntəzoʊˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 内寄生虫学家 | A scientist who studies entozoa, or internal parasites, especially parasitic worms. | Example: The entozoologist examined the animal's intestines for parasites.  内寄生虫学家检查了动物的肠道寄生虫。| Example:  The entozoologist published a paper on the life cycle of a newly discovered nematode. 内寄生虫学家发表了一篇关于新发现的线虫生命周期的论文。 | 近义词: parasitologist | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From ento- (within) + -zoa (animals) + -logist (one who studies).
