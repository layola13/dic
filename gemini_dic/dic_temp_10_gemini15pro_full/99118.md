interceder:/ˌɪntərˈsiːdər/| n.|调解人，说情者，代祷者|Someone who intervenes on behalf of another, especially by prayer.|例句：She acted as interceder between the warring factions. 她充当了交战派别之间的调解人。

例句：He prayed to God, asking him to be his interceder. 他向上帝祈祷，请求上帝做他的代祷者。

近义词：mediator, advocate, negotiator, pleader|反义词:opponent, adversary|词性:noun
