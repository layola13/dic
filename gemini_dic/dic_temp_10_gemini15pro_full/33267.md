chryselephantine:/ˌkrɪsəlˈɛfənˌtaɪn/
adjective
金象嵌的；用黄金和象牙装饰的
Made or ornamented with gold and ivory.
The chryselephantine statue of Zeus at Olympia was one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World. 奥林匹亚的宙斯金象嵌雕像是古代世界七大奇迹之一。

The museum displayed a chryselephantine throne, a relic of a forgotten kingdom.  博物馆展出了一座金象嵌的宝座，一个被遗忘王国的遗迹。

Synonym: gold-and-ivory, gilded
Antonym: plain, unadorned
词性: adjective
