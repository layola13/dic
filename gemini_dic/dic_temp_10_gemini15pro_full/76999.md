gardenly:/ˈɡɑːrdnli/| adv.|像花园般地，园艺式地|In a manner resembling or suggestive of a garden; in a way characteristic of gardening. |The house was gardenly decorated with flowers and vines. 房子用鲜花和藤蔓装饰得像花园一样。|近义词：horticulturally, botanically|反义词:barrenly, desolately|词性:副词

Etymology:
From "garden" + "-ly". "Garden" comes from Old North French "gardin", ultimately from Frankish "*gardo" meaning enclosure. "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives, from Old English "-lice".
