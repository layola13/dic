intercaste:/ˌɪntərˈkɑːst/ | adj. |种姓间的；不同种姓的| Relating to or involving members of different castes, especially in marriage or other relationships.|  Intercaste marriages are still relatively uncommon in some parts of India.  在印度某些地区，不同种姓间的婚姻仍然相对少见。|  The intercaste rivalry led to violence in the village.  不同种姓间的竞争导致了村庄里的暴力事件。|Synonyms: intermarriage (when referring to marriage), cross-caste| Antonyms: intracaste| Adjective
