armozeen:/ɑːrˈmoʊziːn/
n.
armozeen (fabric)
一种斜纹织的丝绸或丝绸和羊毛混纺织物，通常用于窗帘和室内装潢。
A heavy twilled fabric of silk or of silk and wool, often used for curtains and upholstery.
The curtains were made of a rich armozeen. 窗帘是用一种华丽的斜纹绸做的。
近义词:twill, damask, brocade
反义词:None

armozeen:/ɑːrˈmoʊziːn/
n.
armozeen (color)
一种浓艳的紫红色。
A rich purplish-crimson color.
The dress was a vibrant shade of armozeen. 这件连衣裙是鲜艳的紫红色。
近义词:crimson, magenta, fuchsia
反义词:None
