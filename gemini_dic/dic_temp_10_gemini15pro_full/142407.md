Orville: /ˈɔːrvɪl/ (US), /ˈɔːvɪl/ (UK)| proper n.|奥维尔|A male given name, derived from a French place name meaning "gold town".  It is best known as the first name of Orville Wright, one of the Wright brothers who invented and built the world's first successful airplane.|Orville and Wilbur Wright made history. 奥维尔和威尔伯·莱特创造了历史。|近义词: (As a name, no direct synonyms.  Consider other male names like Wilbur, George, etc.)|反义词: N/A|noun
