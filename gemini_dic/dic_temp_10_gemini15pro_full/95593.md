incommensurateness:/ɪnkəmɛnʃərətnəs/| n.|不可通约性，不相称|The incommensurateness of the two theories makes it difficult to compare them. 这两种理论的不可通约性使得难以比较它们。|例句：The incommensurateness of their worldviews led to constant misunderstandings.  他们的世界观不可通约导致了持续的误解。

例句：The incommensurateness of the two systems of measurement made conversion impossible. 这两种测量系统的不可通约性使得转换不可能。|近义词：incompatibility, disparity, incongruity|反义词：commensurability, compatibility, similarity|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + commensurable + -ness.  Commensurable comes from Late Latin commensurabilis, from com- (together) + mensurare (to measure) + -abilis (able).
