inferiae:/ɪnˈfɪəriːiː/
noun
祭品，献祭，(尤指)亡灵节
sacrifices or offerings to the dead, especially those made on Parentalia (Roman festival)
The family offered inferiae to appease the spirits of their ancestors.  家人献祭以安抚祖先的灵魂。
Synonyms: oblations, offerings, sacrifices
Antonyms:  N/A (No direct antonym for ritual offerings)
