nonparochially:/ˌnɑːnpəˈroʊkiəli/
adverb
非狭隘地；不局限于教区的
Not limited to a narrow or local perspective, especially not limited by the concerns of a particular religious parish.
The university aims to educate students nonparochially, encouraging them to consider global perspectives. 大学的目标是非狭隘地教育学生，鼓励他们考虑全球视角。
The museum's collection reflects a nonparochial approach to art, encompassing works from various cultures and time periods. 博物馆的收藏反映了一种不局限于地域的艺术方法，涵盖了来自不同文化和时期的作品。
Synonym: broadly, liberally, open-mindedly, ecumenically
Antonym: narrowly, provincially, parochially
word type: adverb
