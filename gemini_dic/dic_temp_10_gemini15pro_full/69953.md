fester:/ˈfestər/ (US), /ˈfɛstə(r)/ (UK)| v. n.| 溃烂，恶化， festering (adj.) 溃烂的 |To become infected and generate pus; to rot; for negative feelings or thoughts to worsen over time.|The wound festered for days before he finally went to the doctor.  伤口溃烂了好多天才去看医生。|Synonyms: rot, decay, worsen, aggravate, rankle|Antonyms: heal, improve, ameliorate|verb, noun

fester:/ˈfestər/ (US), /ˈfɛstə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 溃疡，脓疮|A festering sore or wound.|The doctor lanced the fester to drain the pus. 医生切开脓疮排出脓液。|Synonyms: sore, ulcer, abscess|Antonyms: healthy tissue|noun
