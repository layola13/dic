coachwork:/ˈkoʊtʃwɜːrk/| n. |车身，车体|The coachwork on that vintage car is exquisite. 那辆老爷车的车身工艺精美。|The coachwork was badly damaged in the accident. 车身在事故中严重受损。|近义词：bodywork, chassis, body |反义词: None |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "coach" (a horse-drawn carriage) + "work" (construction or manufacture).  Originally referred to the construction of horse-drawn carriages, and later extended to the body of motor vehicles.
