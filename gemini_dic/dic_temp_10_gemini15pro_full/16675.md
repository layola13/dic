barreling:/ˈbærəlɪŋ/
verb
快速移动
moving fast; speeding
The car was barreling down the highway. 汽车在高速公路上飞驰。
The truck came barreling around the corner. 卡车飞快地绕过拐角。
近义词：speeding, rushing, hurtling, dashing
反义词：crawling, creeping, inching
词性:v.


barreling:/ˈbærəlɪŋ/
noun
桶装
the act of putting something into barrels
The barreling of the wine took several hours.  葡萄酒的桶装花了几个小时。
The workers were busy with the barreling of apples. 工人们正忙着把苹果装桶。
近义词：packing, casing, crating
反义词：unpacking, unloading
词性:n.
