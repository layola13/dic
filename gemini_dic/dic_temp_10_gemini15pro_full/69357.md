federates:/ˈfɛdəˌreɪts/
verb | 联合，组成联邦 | To unite in a federation or league.
The thirteen colonies federated to form the United States of America. | 十三个殖民地联合起来组成了美利坚合众国。
Several organizations federated to increase their political influence. |  几个组织联合起来以增强他们的政治影响力。
近义词: unite, ally, join, combine, amalgamate
反义词: separate, divide, disunite
词性: verb

federates:/ˈfɛdəˌreɪts/
noun | 联邦成员 | The members of a federation.
The federates agreed to a common defense policy. |  联邦成员同意了一项共同的防御政策。
The conference brought together federates from all over the country. | 会议聚集了来自全国各地的联邦成员。
近义词: members, allies, partners, associates
反义词: opponents, adversaries, enemies
词性: noun
