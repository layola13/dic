decentralists:/diːˈsɛntrəˌlɪsts/| n.|去中心化主义者； 分权主义者| People who advocate for decentralization, the transfer of power from a central authority to regional or local authorities.  They believe in distributing control and decision-making. | Many decentralists argue that blockchain technology empowers individuals and reduces reliance on centralized institutions. 许多去中心化主义者认为，区块链技术赋予个人权力，并减少对中心化机构的依赖。

Decentralists often promote community-based governance. 去中心化主义者经常提倡基于社区的治理。 |近义词：autonomists, federalists|反义词：centralists, authoritarians|词性:noun (plural)

decentralist:/diːˈsɛntrəˌlɪst/| adj.|去中心化的；分权的|Relating to or supporting decentralization.|Decentralist systems are often more resilient to attacks. 去中心化系统通常更能抵御攻击。

A decentralized network can improve data security.  一个去中心化的网络可以提高数据安全性。|近义词：distributed, autonomous|反义词：centralized, unified|词性:adjective


词源:

From decentralize + -ist.  Decentralize comes from de- (meaning "away from" or "removal") + centralize (meaning "to concentrate at a center").  Centralize in turn comes from central (meaning "at or near the center") + -ize (a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make" or "cause to be").  The ultimate root is the Latin word "centrum" meaning "center."
