ephemeron:/ɪˈfɛmərɒn/ (US) /ɪˈfiːmərɒn/ (UK)| n. |蜉蝣，朝生暮死之物，短暂存在的事物|Something that lasts for a very short time.|The beauty of the cherry blossoms is an ephemeron, lasting only a couple of weeks.|近义词：transient, fleeting, short-lived|反义词:permanent, enduring, lasting|noun

ephemera:/ɪˈfɛmərə/ (US) /ɪˈfiːmərə/ (UK)| n. |蜉蝣，短命的东西，（尤指印刷品）短效资料|Things that exist or are used or enjoyed for only a short time, especially printed materials.|He collected ephemera such as playbills and concert programs.|近义词：transients, ephemerons|反义词:memorabilia, keepsakes|noun
