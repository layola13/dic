nonpoor:/ˌnɑːnˈpʊr/ | adj. | 非贫困的，不贫穷的 | Not poor; having sufficient resources to live comfortably.  |  The nonpoor population in the city has access to better healthcare. 城市中的非贫困人口能够获得更好的医疗保健。|  Example:  Government policies should aim to uplift both the poor and the nonpoor. 政府政策应该致力于提升贫困人口和非贫困人口的生活水平。| 近义词:  wealthy, affluent, rich | 反义词: poor, impoverished, destitute | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "poor" (adjective meaning "lacking sufficient money to live at a standard considered comfortable or normal in a society").
