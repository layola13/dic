cannon-fodder:/ˈkænən ˌfɒdə(r)/ (US) /ˈkænən ˌfɑːdər/| n. |炮灰；牺牲品|Soldiers regarded as expendable in war.  Those considered unimportant and easily sacrificed to achieve a goal.|例句：The infantry were used as cannon-fodder in the brutal assault.  步兵在残酷的攻击中被用作炮灰。

例句：The company treated its temporary workers as cannon-fodder, firing them at the first sign of trouble.  这家公司把临时工当作炮灰，一出现问题就解雇他们。|近义词：pawns, expendable, victims|反义词: valuable asset, protected individual|词性: noun
