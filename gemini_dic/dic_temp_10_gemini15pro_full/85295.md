harmony:/ˈhɑːrməni/ (US) /ˈhɑːməni/ (UK)| n.|和谐，协调，融洽|A pleasing arrangement of parts; agreement or concord.|The soft colors of the room created a sense of harmony. 房间柔和的色彩营造出一种和谐的氛围。|Synonyms: agreement, concord, unity, balance|Antonyms: discord, conflict, disharmony|noun

harmony:/ˈhɑːrməni/ (US) /ˈhɑːməni/ (UK)| n.|和声，和音|A combination of musical notes to form chords and a pleasing sound.|The choir sang in perfect harmony.  合唱团的歌声非常和谐。|Synonyms: consonance, accord|Antonyms: dissonance, discord|noun
