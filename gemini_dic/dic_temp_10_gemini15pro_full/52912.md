dimorphism:/daɪˈmɔːrfɪzəm/
n.|二形性，二态性|The condition of existing in two different forms.|Sexual dimorphism is common in birds. 鸟类中性二态性很常见。|
近义词:dimorphism, bimorphism|反义词:monomorphism|词性:noun

dimorphic:/daɪˈmɔːrfɪk/
adj.|二形的，二态的|Having two different forms.|The species exhibits sexual dimorphic traits.  该物种表现出性二形特征。|
近义词:bimorphic|反义词:monomorphic|词性:adjective
