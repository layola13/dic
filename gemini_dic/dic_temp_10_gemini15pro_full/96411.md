indisposition's:/ˌɪndɪspəˈzɪʃənz/
noun
轻微的疾病；不舒服；不情愿
a slight illness; a feeling of not being healthy; unwillingness
She couldn't attend the meeting due to a sudden indisposition. 她因为突然不舒服而无法参加会议。
His indisposition to help was obvious. 他不愿意帮忙是很明显的。
synonyms: ailment, illness, sickness, malaise, reluctance, unwillingness
antonyms: health, wellness, willingness, eagerness
