calypsonian:/kəˌlɪpˈsoʊniən/
noun
卡里普索歌曲演唱家；卡里普索音乐家
A singer or composer of calypso music.
Lord Kitchener was a celebrated calypsonian.  基奇纳勋爵是一位著名的卡里普索音乐家。
The calypsonian entertained the crowd with his witty lyrics.  这位卡里普索歌手用他诙谐的歌词娱乐了观众。
Synonyms: calypso singer, calypso artist
Antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词
