what:/wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US)| pronoun, determiner, adverb|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something; used to ask about the quality or character of something; used to express surprise or disbelief.|

例句：
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？

What did you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃了什么？


近义词：which, how|反义词: N/A |pronoun

What do you think about this movie? = 你觉得这部电影怎么样？
What does he do for a living? = 他以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is she working on right now? = 她现在在做什么工作？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? = 她最近在忙些什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been doing since I last saw you? = 自从我上次见到你以来，你一直在做什么？
What has he been thinking about all day? = 他一整天都在想什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

一般将来时 (Simple Future):

What will happen if we don't act now? = 如果我们现在不采取行动会发生什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
