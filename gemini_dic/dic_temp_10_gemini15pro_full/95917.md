incriminate: /ɪnˈkrɪmɪneɪt/ | vt. | 控告；牵连；暗示…有罪 | To make someone appear guilty of a crime or wrongdoing; to show evidence of involvement in a crime. | The evidence found at the scene incriminated the suspect.  现场发现的证据表明嫌疑人有罪。 |  synonyms: implicate, accuse, charge, indict | antonyms: exonerate, acquit, absolve | verb

incriminate: /ɪnˈkrɪmɪneɪt/ | vt. | 使负罪 | To cause someone to appear guilty or responsible for something bad. | His careless words incriminated him in the scandal. 他粗心的话让他卷入了丑闻。| synonyms: involve, entangle | antonyms: clear, vindicate | verb
