nonconfinement:/ˌnɒnkənˈfaɪnmənt/ | n. | 不禁闭，不限制，不约束 | The state of not being confined or restricted. |  The judge ordered the nonconfinement of the defendant due to lack of evidence. 法官因证据不足下令对被告不禁闭。

The policy promotes the nonconfinement of patients with mental health issues. 这项政策提倡不限制精神健康问题的患者。| 近义词：freedom, liberty, release | 反义词：confinement, imprisonment, restriction | 词性：名词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
confinement (noun): From confine + -ment, ultimately from Latin confinis "bordering upon."
