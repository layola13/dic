literature: /ˈlɪtərətʃər/ (US) /ˈlɪtrətʃə(r)/ (UK)| n. |文学; 文獻| written works, especially those considered of superior or lasting artistic merit.|  Example:  He has devoted his life to the study of English literature. 他一生致力于研究英国文学。

Example:  She's taken a course in comparative literature. 她选修了比较文学的课程。

近义词: writings, letters, books, publications, compositions

反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider concepts like "illiteracy" or "ignorance")

词性: noun

词源: From Old French littérature, from Latin litterātūra "learning, writing, grammar," from litterātus "marked with letters," from littera/litera "letter."
