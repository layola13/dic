gaga:/ˈɡɑːɡɑː/| adj.|痴呆的；疯狂的；热衷的；为…所倾倒的|Infatuated or obsessed with someone or something; enthusiastic about something.|例句：He's gaga over his new grandchild. 他对他的新孙子非常着迷。|例句：She's completely gaga about the latest fashion trends. 她对最新的时尚潮流非常着迷。|近义词：infatuated, obsessed, enthusiastic, crazy, mad|反义词：indifferent, apathetic|词性:形容词

gaga:/ˈɡɑːɡɑː/| n.|痴呆；糊涂|A state of senility or mental decline.|例句：My grandmother is starting to go a bit gaga. 我的祖母开始有点痴呆了。|例句：He was gaga from the fever. 他因为发烧而神志不清。|近义词：senile, confused, bewildered|反义词：lucid, clear-headed|词性:名词
