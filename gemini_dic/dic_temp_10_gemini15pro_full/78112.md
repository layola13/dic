geologies:/dʒiˈɒlədʒiz/
noun
地质学（geologies是geology的复数形式）
The study of the Earth, its composition, structure, processes, and history.  This can include multiple specialized fields within geology.
He studied the geologies of different planets to understand their formation. 他研究不同行星的地质学以了解它们的形成。
近义词: earth sciences, geosciences
反义词:  (geology doesn't have a direct antonym)
