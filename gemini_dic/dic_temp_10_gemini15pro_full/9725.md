antiutilitarian:/ˌæntijuːˌtɪlɪˈtɛəriən/
adjective
反功利主义的
opposed to or denying the tenets of utilitarianism.  Utilitarianism is a philosophical theory that states that actions are right if they are useful or for the benefit of a majority.  Anti-utilitarian, therefore, describes something that goes against this principle.
He wrote an antiutilitarian treatise arguing against maximizing happiness as the sole moral good. 他写了一篇反功利主义的论文，反对将幸福最大化作为唯一的道德善。
近义词:non-utilitarian,  anti-consequentialist
反义词:utilitarian
词性:adjective
