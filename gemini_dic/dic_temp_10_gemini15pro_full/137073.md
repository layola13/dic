nonstrikers:/ˌnɒnˈstraɪkəz/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈstraɪkərz/ (US)| n. |非击球手|A batsman who is not facing the bowler.  In cricket, at any given time, one batsman is facing the bowler and the other is at the opposite end of the pitch, not actively batting. This second batsman is the non-striker.|The nonstriker was backing up too far and got run out. 非击球手离球门太远，被出局了。|Near synonyms: batsman, fielder (in a broader sense) | Antonym: striker| noun
