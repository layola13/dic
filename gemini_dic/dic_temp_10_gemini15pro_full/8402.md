anti-sovietism:/ˌæntiˈsoʊviətɪzəm/
n.
反苏主义，反苏情绪
Opposition to the Soviet Union, its ideology, and its influence.
During the Cold War, anti-sovietism was a dominant force in American foreign policy.  冷战时期，反苏主义是美国外交政策的主导力量。
The senator's speech was filled with fiery anti-sovietism.  参议员的演讲充满了激烈的反苏情绪。
近义词：anti-communism, anti-stalinism
反义词：pro-sovietism, sovietism
词性：noun
