laxman: /ˈlʌksmən/ | n. | 拉克什曼, 羅什曼 |  In Hindu mythology, Laxman is the younger brother of Rama and his loyal companion and aide throughout his life. He is considered an avatar of Sheshanaga, the multi-headed serpent upon whom Vishnu rests. |  Laxman accompanied Rama and Sita during their exile in the forest. |  近义词: brother, companion, aide | 反义词: enemy, opponent | noun
