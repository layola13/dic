capaciously:/kəˈpeɪʃəsli/
| adv.|宽敞地；容量大地；包容地|Having a lot of space or capacity; able to hold or contain a great deal.|The bag was capaciously designed, holding everything I needed for the weekend. 这个包设计得很宽敞，能装下我周末所需的所有东西。|Synonyms: roomily, spaciously, extensively, comprehensively|Antonyms: narrowly, tightly, restrictedly|adverb

capaciously:/kəˈpeɪʃəsli/
| adj.(rarely used)|宽敞的；容量大的；包容的|Having a lot of space or capacity; able to hold or contain a great deal.|Although rarely used as an adjective, one might describe a capaciously built house. 尽管很少用作形容词，但人们可能会用它来形容一栋宽敞的房子。|Synonyms: roomy, spacious, extensive, comprehensive|Antonyms: narrow, tight, restricted|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *capaciosus, from Latin capax (“able to hold, spacious”), from capere (“to take, hold”).  The suffix -ly transforms the adjective "capacious" into the adverb "capaciously."
