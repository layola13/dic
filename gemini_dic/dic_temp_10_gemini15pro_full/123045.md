moneron:/ˈmɒnərɒn/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnərɑːn/ (US)| n. |单细胞生物，原核生物|A single-celled organism, especially a bacterium, belonging to the kingdom Monera (a classification no longer used).  It is characterized by the absence of a true nucleus and other membrane-bound organelles.|Monerans, such as bacteria, were once thought to be the simplest form of life.| 近义词: prokaryote, bacterium | 反义词: eukaryote | 词性: noun
