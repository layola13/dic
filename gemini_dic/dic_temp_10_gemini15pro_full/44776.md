crumpliest:/ˈkrʌmpliɪst/
adjective
最皱的
superlative adjective of crumpled: most crumpled.  Having the most wrinkles or creases.
Her dress was the crumpliest thing I'd ever seen, like it had been balled up in a drawer for weeks. 她穿的裙子是我见过的最皱巴巴的衣服，就像在抽屉里揉成一团好几个星期似的。
近义词: most wrinkled, most creased
反义词: smoothest, flattest
词性: adjective
词源:  From crumple + -y + -est.  "Crumple" likely originates from Middle Dutch "krompen" meaning to shrink or contract.
