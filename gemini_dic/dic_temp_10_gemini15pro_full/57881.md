dribs:/drɪbz/
noun
少量，点滴
a small quantity of something, often falling or flowing slowly
The tap was dripping, the water coming out in dribs and drabs. 水龙头在滴水，水一点一点地流出来。
synonyms: drops, trickles, bits, traces,少量, 点滴
antonyms: flood, torrent, deluge,大量, 洪流
n.
