abdicate: /ˈæbdɪkeɪt/
v.
放弃；退位；辞去
(of a monarch) renounce one's throne.  (formal) fail to fulfil or undertake (a responsibility or duty).
The king abdicated in favor of his son. 国王退位让位给他的儿子。
She abdicated her responsibilities as a parent. 她放弃了作为父母的责任。

近义词: relinquish, resign, renounce, step down, give up
反义词: retain, assume, usurp, seize
词性: verb
