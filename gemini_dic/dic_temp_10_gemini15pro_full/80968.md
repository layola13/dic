gracies:/ˈɡreɪsiːz/| n.|谢谢（gracia 的复数形式，主要用于巴西葡萄牙语）|This word is the plural form of "gracia" (meaning "grace" or "thanks" in Spanish and Portuguese) and is commonly used in Brazilian Portuguese to express gratitude.  It's less common in other varieties of Portuguese or Spanish.|Example:  "Muito obrigada, gracies." (Brazilian Portuguese) - Thank you very much.|近义词：thanks, thank you, obrigado (Portuguese), gracias (Spanish)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
