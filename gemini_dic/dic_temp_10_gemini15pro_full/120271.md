miniaturize:/ˈmɪnɪətʃəraɪz/ |v. |使小型化; 使微型化|To make something much smaller than its usual size.|They’ve managed to miniaturize the camera. 他们已成功地将照相机微型化。|Example: Scientists are working to miniaturize electronic devices. 科学家们正致力于电子设备的小型化。

Example: The company miniaturized the computer chip, making it much more powerful.  该公司将电脑芯片小型化，使其功能更加强大。|Synonyms: shrink, reduce, compress, condense|Antonyms: enlarge, expand, magnify|Verb
