n.|电话，通话，呼叫|A telephone conversation.| He received several calls this morning. 他今天早上接到了好几个电话。| ring, telephone, contact | silence | noun
calls:/kɔːlz/ (BrE) /kɑːlz/ (AmE)
v.|打电话，呼叫，称呼|To speak to someone by telephone; to summon.|He called me last night. 他昨晚打电话给我了。| phone, ring, summon | ignore | verb
Etymology: From Old Norse *kalla "to shout, cry, proclaim," from Proto-Germanic *kallanan.
