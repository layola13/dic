|近义词:deactivate, neutralize, disarm, alleviate, mitigate|
|反义词:activate, escalate, aggravate|
|词性:动词 (verb)|

词源:  From de- + fuse (n.), from Latin fusus "spindle," from fundere "to pour, melt."  Originally referring to removing the fuse from an explosive device.  The figurative sense of "reducing tension" developed later.
