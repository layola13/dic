nonhistrionic:/ˌnɒnhɪstrɪˈɒnɪk/ | adj. | 不夸张的，不矫揉造作的 | Not excessively dramatic or emotional; restrained and unemotional. |  She gave a nonhistrionic account of the accident. 她平静地讲述了事故的经过。

| Example: He delivered the news in a calm, nonhistrionic manner. 他以平静、不夸张的方式传达了这个消息。| Synonym: understated, calm, restrained, unemotional, subdued, composed, cool, collected, self-possessed, impassive, phlegmatic, quiet, low-key | Antonym: histrionic, dramatic, theatrical, exaggerated, over-the-top, melodramatic, affected, showy, ostentatious | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + histrionic (from Latin histrionicus, meaning "pertaining to an actor")
