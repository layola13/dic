moto:/ˈmoʊtoʊ/| n.|座右铭，箴言，格言|A motto is a short sentence or phrase that expresses a belief or ideal.|The company's motto is "Quality first."  这家公司的座右铭是“质量第一”。|synonyms: slogan, maxim, creed|antonyms: N/A|noun


moto:/ˈmoʊtoʊ/| adj. (informal, short for "motorized")|机动的，摩托化的|(Often used in combinations, less common as a standalone adjective)  A moto-taxi is a common form of transportation in some countries. 在一些国家，摩托出租车是一种常见的交通工具。|synonyms: motorized, powered|antonyms: unpowered, non-motorized|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *motto*, from Latin *muttum* ("a murmur, grunt, muttered word"), from a PIE root meaning "to mutter".
