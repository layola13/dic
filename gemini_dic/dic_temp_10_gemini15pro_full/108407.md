lawmaking:/ˈlɔːmeɪkɪŋ/
n.
立法；法律制定
The process of creating and enacting laws.
The complexities of lawmaking can be daunting. 立法的复杂性可能令人望而生畏。
New lawmaking is needed to address this issue. 需要新的立法来解决这个问题。
legislation, enactment
n.
词源：From law + making (present participle of make).  由law（法律）+ making（make的现在分词）构成。
