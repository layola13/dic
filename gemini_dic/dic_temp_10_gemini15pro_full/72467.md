flunkers:/ˈflʌŋkərz/
n.
不及格的学生;考试不及格的人;失败者
People who fail a test or course are called flunkers.
The flunkers had to retake the exam. 考试不及格的人必须重考。
synonyms: failures, losers
antonyms: passers, winners
noun

flunk:/flʌŋk/
v.
(使)不及格;(使)失败;放弃
To fail a test or course.
He flunked his math exam. 他数学考试不及格。
She flunked out of college. 她大学没毕业就退学了。
synonyms: fail, bomb
antonyms: pass, succeed
verb
