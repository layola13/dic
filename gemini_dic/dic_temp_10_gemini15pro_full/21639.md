blurringly:/ˈblɜːrɪŋli/
adv.
模糊地；朦胧地
in a way that makes something unclear or difficult to see
The camera focused blurringly on the distant figure.  دوربین模糊地对焦在远处的那个身影上。
The memories came blurringly back to him. 记忆模糊地回到了他的脑海里。
近义词: hazily, indistinctly, vaguely
反义词: clearly, distinctly, sharply
词性: 副词

词源: From blur (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Blur" comes from Middle English *blurre, of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old Norse *blundra "to shut one's eyes."
