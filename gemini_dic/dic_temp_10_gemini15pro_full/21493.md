blubbing: /ˈblʌbɪŋ/ | v. | 哭泣，抽噎 | The act of crying noisily and uncontrollably. | The little boy was blubbing because he lost his toy. 小男孩因为丢了玩具而哭泣。| 近义词：crying, sobbing, weeping | 反义词: laughing | 词性: 动词
Etymology:  From the imitative word *blub, likely representing the sound of crying.
