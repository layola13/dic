cross-path:/ˈkrɒsˌpɑːθ/ (US) /ˈkrɔːsˌpæθ/| adj. | 交叉路径的；相交的；冲突的 |  Describing situations where two or more things intersect or collide, often leading to conflict or interaction.  It can be used literally (paths crossing) or figuratively (ideas or plans conflicting). |  Our paths crossed unexpectedly at the conference. 我们的道路在会议上意外地相遇了。

The company's expansion plans cross-path with local environmental regulations.  公司的扩张计划与当地环境法规相冲突。 | 近义词：intersect, clash, collide, conflict | 反义词：parallel, diverge, separate | 词性: 形容词

cross-path:/ˈkrɒsˌpɑːθ/ (US) /ˈkrɔːsˌpæθ/| n. | 交叉路；交点 | The point where two paths cross. | Our cross-path was marked by a small monument. 我们的交点处有一个小纪念碑。| 近义词：intersection, junction, crossroads | 反义词：divergence, separation | 词性: 名词
