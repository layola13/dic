demilitarizes:/diːˈmɪlɪtəˌraɪzɪz/
verb
使非军事化;解除武装
To remove the military forces, weapons, or fortifications from a place or area; to make something no longer military in character.
The treaty demilitarizes the border zone between the two countries.  该条约使两国之间的边境地区非军事化。
The government demilitarized the island after the war. 战后，政府解除了该岛的武装。
synonyms: disarm, neutralize
antonyms: militarize, fortify
词性: 动词
