branchiopoda:/bræŋkiˈɒpədə/| n.|鳃足亚纲|A subclass of crustaceans characterized by having numerous, leaf-like appendages that serve for both locomotion and respiration.  These appendages are also called phyllopodia. |Fairy shrimp are branchiopoda that inhabit ephemeral pools. 仙女虾是栖息在临时水池中的鳃足亚纲动物。|近义词:crustacean, |反义词:|词性:名词

branchiopod:/bræŋkiˈɒpɒd/| adj. n.|鳃足类的；鳃足类动物|Relating to or belonging to the Branchiopoda subclass; a member of the Branchiopoda subclass. |The branchiopod thrived in the temporary pond.  这种鳃足类动物在临时池塘中繁衍生息。|近义词:crustacean, |反义词:|词性:形容词, 名词
