anti-polishes:/ˌæntiˈpɒlɪʃɪz/ | n. | 反抛光剂；去光剂；消光剂 | Substances or processes that reduce shine or gloss, often used to create a matte finish. |  The artist used anti-polishes on the sculpture to give it a rough, unfinished look. 艺术家在雕塑上使用了消光剂，使其呈现出粗糙、未完成的外观。

The car wax company unexpectedly started selling anti-polishes. 汽车蜡公司出人意料地开始销售消光剂。 | 近义词: matting agents, dulling agents, deglossers | 反义词: polishes, glosses, varnishes | 词性: 名词(plural)
