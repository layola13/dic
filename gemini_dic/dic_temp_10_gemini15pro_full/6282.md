amorite:/ˈæmɔːraɪt/| n. adj.|亚摩利人，亚摩利人的|A member of an ancient Semitic people who dominated the land of Canaan west of the Jordan River during the early second millennium BC.  Also refers to their language.|The Amorites were conquered by the Israelites. 亚摩利人被以色列人征服了。|Near synonyms: Canaanite, ancient Semitic people|Antonyms: Israelite (in a historical context)|noun
