earthling:/ˈɜːrθlɪŋ/ | n. | 地球人；凡人 | An inhabitant of the earth, especially when considered in relation to extraterrestrial beings or celestial powers. |  Example 1: The earthlings watched in awe as the spaceship landed. 地球人敬畏地看着宇宙飞船降落。

Example 2:  The message from space was intended for earthlings. 来自太空的信息是给地球人的。 | Synonym: human, mortal, terrestrial being | Antonym: extraterrestrial, alien | noun
