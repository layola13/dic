nondocumentary: /ˌnɒnˌdɒkjʊˈment(ə)ri/ /ˌnɑːnˌdɑːkjəˈmentəri/
adjective
非纪录片的
Not of or relating to documentaries; fictional.  Based on fiction, not fact.
This is a nondocumentary film about the life of a fictional queen. 这是一部关于虚构女王的非纪录片电影。
The director is known for his nondocumentary approach to storytelling. 这位导演以其非纪录片的叙事方式而闻名。
近义词: fictional, dramatic, invented, imaginative
反义词: documentary, factual, real
词性: adjective
