interbred:/ˌɪntərˈbred/| v. |杂交，混种|To breed with another species or variety.|Scientists interbred different varieties of corn to develop a disease-resistant strain. 科学家们将不同品种的玉米杂交以培育出抗病菌种。|The two species of birds occasionally interbred. 这两种鸟类偶尔会杂交。|Synonyms: crossbreed, hybridize|Antonyms: purebred|Verb
