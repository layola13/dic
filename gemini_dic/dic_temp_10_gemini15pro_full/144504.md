outtonguing: /ˌaʊtˈtʌŋɪŋ/
n.
吐舌；(管乐器)用舌吹奏
The rapid movement of the tongue used in playing wind instruments.
He demonstrated his outtonguing technique on the flute. 他展示了他在长笛上用舌吹奏的技巧。
