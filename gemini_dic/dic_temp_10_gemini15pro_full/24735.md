brooches:/ˈbroʊtʃɪz/ /ˈbrəʊtʃɪz/
noun
胸针（复数）
A small piece of jewelry with a pin at the back that is fastened to a woman's clothes.
Her dress was adorned with antique brooches. 她的连衣裙上装饰着古董胸针。

近义词: pins, badges, clips
反义词: (No direct antonym for a physical object.  Could consider "unadorned" as an antonym for the effect of wearing a brooch)
