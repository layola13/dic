finning:/ˈfɪnɪŋ/| n.|鱼翅割取; 切除鳍|The act of cutting off a shark's fins and discarding the body back into the ocean.  将鲨鱼的鳍割下并将身体丢回海洋的行为。|Shark finning is a cruel and wasteful practice.  鱼翅割取是一种残忍和浪费的行为。|近义词：shark fin removal, fin removal|反义词:conservation, protection|noun
