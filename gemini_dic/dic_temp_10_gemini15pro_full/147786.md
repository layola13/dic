overproportion:/ˌoʊvərprəˈpɔːrʃən/ |adj.| 过度比例的，不成比例的 | existing or represented in a greater proportion than is normal or desirable |  The city has an overproportion of young single people. 这个城市单身年轻人的比例过高。| 近义词: disproportionate, excessive, inordinate | 反义词: proportionate, balanced, proportional | 词性: adjective

overproportion:/ˌoʊvərprəˈpɔːrʃən/ |n.|  过度比例，不成比例 | the state of being overproportioned | The overproportion of government spending on defense is a concern. 政府国防开支过高令人担忧。 | 近义词: disproportion, excess, imbalance | 反义词: proportion, balance, proportionality | 词性: noun

overproportion:/ˌoʊvərprəˈpɔːrʃən/ |v.| 使…不成比例，过度分配 | to allocate or distribute something in an overproportionate manner |  The funds were overproportioned to administrative costs.  资金被过度分配到行政费用上。| 近义词: disproportion, misallocate, overemphasize | 反义词:  proportion, balance, distribute evenly | 词性: verb
