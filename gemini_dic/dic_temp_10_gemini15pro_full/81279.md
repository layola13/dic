grant-maintained:/ˈɡrɑːnt meɪnˈteɪnd/
adjective
由政府拨款维持的
Funded directly by the government, rather than through a local education authority (LEA).  This system applied to schools in England and Wales between 1988 and 1998.
Grant-maintained schools were allowed to manage their own budgets and set their own admissions policies.  Many opted out of local authority control. 许多学校选择脱离地方政府的控制。
Synonym: self-governing, independent, autonomous
Antonym: state-controlled, LEA-maintained
词性: 形容词
