ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *firen, from Old English fȳrian (“to supply with fire, kindle, burn, cauterize, consume by fire”), from Proto-Germanic *furijaną (“to supply with fire”), from Proto-Indo-European *péh₂wr̥ (“fire”). Cognate with West Frisian fjurje (“to fire (a gun), celebrate”), Dutch vuren (“to fire (a gun)”), German feuern (“to fire (a gun)”). Related to fire.
