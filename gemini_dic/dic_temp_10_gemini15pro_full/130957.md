nonassociation:/ˌnɒnəˌsəʊʃɪˈeɪʃən/ |n.|不结合，不联系，不关联|Absence of association or connection.  The state of not being associated or connected with something or someone.| Due to nonassociation with the group, he was not privy to their plans. 由于不与该团体联系，他不知道他们的计划。| near synonyms: disassociation, separation, detachment | antonyms: association, connection, union | noun
