food:/fuːd/| n.|食物，食品|Any substance consumed to provide nutritional support for an organism.|例句：We need food to survive. 我们需要食物才能生存。

例句：The food at the restaurant was delicious. 这家餐馆的食物很美味。|近义词：nourishment, sustenance, provisions, edibles|反义词: famine, starvation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fode, *foode, from Old English fōda (“food, nourishment”), from Proto-Germanic *fōdō (“food”), from Proto-Indo-European *pṓtis (“lord; husband; master; potentate”), whence also Latin potis (“able, powerful; possible”). Cognate with Old Frisian fode (“food”), Old Saxon fōda (“food”), Old High German fuotar, fuatar (“food, fodder”), Old Norse fóðr (“food, fodder; lining, case”), Gothic 𐍆𐍉𐌳𐌴𐌹𐌽𐍃 (fōdeins, “food”).
