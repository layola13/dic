demedicating:/diːˈmɛdɪkeɪtɪŋ/
verb
停止用药；撤药；逐渐减少用药
The process of gradually reducing and stopping a patient's medication, typically under medical supervision.
The doctor is demedicating the patient from his pain medication. 医生正在给病人逐渐停用止痛药。
Demedicating patients too quickly can be dangerous.  过快地给病人停药是危险的。
synonyms: weaning, tapering, withdrawing, discontinuing
antonyms: medicating, administering medication
