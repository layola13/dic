overapprehensive:/ˌoʊvərˌæprɪˈhɛnsɪv/
adjective
过度忧虑的，过分担心的
Excessively worried or anxious about possible dangers or problems.
She was overapprehensive about the upcoming exam, even though she had studied diligently. 她对即将到来的考试过度忧虑，即使她已经努力学习了。
He's overapprehensive about his health, constantly checking for symptoms. 他对自己的健康过度担忧，不断检查症状。
近义词: anxious, apprehensive, worried, fearful, neurotic
反义词: calm, relaxed, composed, confident, unconcerned
词性: adjective
词源: From "over-" (excessively) + "apprehensive" (anxious or fearful that something bad or unpleasant will happen). "Apprehensive" comes from Latin "apprehendere" (to seize, grasp), from "ad-" (to) + "prehendere" (to grasp).
