imponderability:/ɪmˌpɒndərəˈbɪləti/ /ɪmˌpɑːndərəˈbɪləti/
n.
无法估量；难以理解；不可称量
The state or quality of being imponderable; incapable of being weighed or measured.
The imponderability of the human mind makes it a fascinating subject of study. 人类思维的不可估量性使其成为一个引人入胜的研究课题。
近义词: intangibility, unfathomability, incomprehensibility
反义词: tangibility, measurability, comprehensibility
词性: noun

imponderable:/ɪmˈpɒndərəbl/ /ɪmˈpɑːndərəbl/
adj.
无法估量的；难以理解的；不可称量的
Impossible to estimate, assess, or foresee.
The future is full of imponderable factors. 未来充满了不可估量的因素。
近义词: unpredictable, uncertain, unforeseeable
反义词: predictable, certain, foreseeable
词性: adjective

imponderable:/ɪmˈpɒndərəbl/ /ɪmˈpɑːndərəbl/
n.
无法估量的事物；难以理解的事物
Something that is difficult or impossible to estimate, assess, or foresee.
There are too many imponderables to make a reliable prediction. 有太多的不确定因素，无法做出可靠的预测。
近义词: uncertainty, unknown, variable
反义词: certainty, known, constant
词性: noun
