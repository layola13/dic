becomingly:/bɪˈkʌmɪŋli/
adverb
适当地；合适地；得体地
In a way that suits the wearer or occasion; attractively.
She was dressed becomingly in a simple black dress. 她穿着一件简单的黑色连衣裙，十分得体。
Synonym: attractively, suitably, appropriately, gracefully
Antonym: inappropriately, unsuitably, awkwardly
词性: 副词


becomingly:/bɪˈkʌmɪŋli/
adverb
相称地；好看地
Making someone look more attractive.
The soft pink color of her dress was very becoming. 她裙子的柔和的粉红色让她看起来很漂亮。
Synonym: flattering, enhancing, attractive
Antonym: unflattering, unbecoming
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From become + -ing + -ly.  Ultimately from Old English becuman "to arrive, happen, suit, befit," from be- "by" + cuman "to come."
