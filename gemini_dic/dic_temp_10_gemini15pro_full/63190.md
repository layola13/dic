entangler:/ɪnˈtæŋɡlər/
n.|纠缠者；使卷入者|Someone or something that entangles or causes entanglement.|The vines were an entangler of small animals.|近义词: snarer, trapper|反义词: liberator, rescuer|词性:noun
entangle:/ɪnˈtæŋɡl/
v.|使纠缠；使卷入|Cause to become twisted together with or caught in.|The fishing line became entangled in the seaweed.|近义词: tangle, snarl|反义词: disentangle, untangle|词性:verb
