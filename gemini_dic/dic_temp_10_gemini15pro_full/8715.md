anticulture:/ˈæntiˌkʌltʃər/
n.|反文化|Opposition to or rejection of the prevailing social norms and values, especially in the arts.  A way of life that deliberately opposes or rejects mainstream culture.|The punk rock movement was a powerful expression of anticulture in the 1970s.  20世纪70年代的朋克摇滚运动是反文化的有力表达。|counterculture, antiestablishmentarianism|mainstream culture, established culture|noun
