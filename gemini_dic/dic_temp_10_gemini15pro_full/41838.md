corvetto:/kɔːrˈvɛtoʊ/ | n. | 小型护卫舰，轻巡洋舰 | A small, fast warship, especially one of a class built in the 16th and 17th centuries. |  The corvetto, nimble and swift, harried the larger galleons. 小型护卫舰，行动敏捷迅速，不断骚扰较大的盖伦帆船。 | Synonyms: corvette, sloop-of-war | Antonyms: galleon, man-of-war | noun
