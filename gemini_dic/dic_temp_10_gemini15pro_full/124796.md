mucamide: /ˈmjuːkəmaɪd/ | n. | 粘酰胺 | A white, crystalline, water-insoluble organic compound, C₄H₉NO₂, derived from mucic acid.  It is used in the production of certain plastics and adhesives. | Mucamide is a key ingredient in some high-performance adhesives. 粘酰胺是某些高性能粘合剂的关键成分。 |  Synonyms:  No commonly used synonyms. | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
