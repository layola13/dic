expansivity:/ɪkˌspænˈsɪvɪti/
n.
expansive quality; tendency to expand
The expansivity of his gestures suggested great enthusiasm. 他夸张的手势表明了他极大的热情。
Her natural expansivity made her a popular hostess. 她天生热情好客，使她成为受欢迎的女主人。
近义词：expansiveness, effusiveness, outgoingness
反义词：reserve, restraint, introversion
词性: noun

expansiveness:/ɪkˈspænsɪvnəs/
n.
the quality of being expansive
The expansiveness of the plains inspired a sense of freedom. 平原的广阔激发了一种自由的感觉。
He loved the expansiveness of the ocean view from his balcony. 他喜欢从阳台上看到的广阔海景。
近义词：expansivity, breadth, vastness
反义词：narrowness, confinement, restriction
词性: noun


词源:  From Late Latin *expansīvus, from Latin expansus (past participle of expandere “to spread out”), from ex- “out” + pandere “to spread”.  The -ity suffix transforms the adjective "expansive" into the abstract noun "expansivity".  Similarly, "-ness" creates "expansiveness".
