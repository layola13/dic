dormor:/ˈdɔːrmər/ (US) /ˈdɔːmə(r)/ (UK)| n.|老虎窗，屋顶窗|A window that projects vertically from a sloping roof.|The old house had several dormers that let light into the attic. 这栋老房子有几个老虎窗，可以让光线进入阁楼。|Example:  The dormers on the roof gave the cottage a quaint charm. 屋顶上的老虎窗赋予了小屋一种古雅的魅力。

Example:  They converted the attic into a bedroom and added two dormers for more light and ventilation. 他们把阁楼改成了卧室，并加了两个老虎窗以获得更多的光线和通风。|Synonyms: roof window, skylight, garret window|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
