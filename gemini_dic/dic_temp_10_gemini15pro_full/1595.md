acicula: /əˈsɪkjʊlə/ | n. | 针状物；（植物的）针叶 | A small needle-like part, especially a leaf of a conifer. | The acicula of the pine tree are sharp. = 松树的针叶很锋利。 | The acicula protect the tree from water loss. = 针叶保护树木免受水分流失。|  近义词：needle, spine, bristle | 反义词: leaf (broad leaf) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin acicula, diminutive of acus ("needle").

USAGE->
The acicula of certain conifers are adapted to harsh environments. = 某些针叶树的针叶适应了恶劣的环境。

The acicula of the cactus help it conserve water. = 仙人掌的针状物帮助它保存水分。

The botanist examined the acicula under a microscope. = 植物学家在显微镜下检查了针叶。

The density of acicula on the branch varied with the age of the tree. = 枝条上针叶的密度随着树龄的变化而变化。
