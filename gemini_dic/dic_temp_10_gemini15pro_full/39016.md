confiscatory:/kənˈfɪskətɔːri/
adjective
没收的；充公的；罚没的；过高的，过分的（指价格、税收等）
Relating to or involving confiscation, especially of property. Excessively high and unfairly disproportionate, as of taxes or fines.
The government imposed confiscatory taxes on the wealthy. 政府对富人征收了过高的税。
The new tax rates are so high as to be confiscatory. 新的税率高得简直就是没收。
近义词:expropriative, penal, punitive, exorbitant, excessive
反义词:reasonable, moderate, fair
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin confiscatorius, from Latin confiscare “to confiscate”  + -ory.
