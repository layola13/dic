aids's:/eɪdzɪz/ | n. | 艾滋病的 (所有格) |  Possessive form of AIDS (Acquired Immune Deficiency Syndrome).  It indicates ownership or belonging related to AIDS.  Because "AIDS" is an acronym, and already plural in a sense, the possessive is usually formed by adding 's. |  Example: The aids's impact on society is devastating. 艾滋病对社会的影响是毁灭性的. | Synonym: (None - Possessive forms don't have direct synonyms) | Antonym: (None - Possessive forms don't have direct antonyms) |  noun
