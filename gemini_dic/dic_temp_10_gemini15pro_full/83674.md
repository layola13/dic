hag:/hæɡ/| n.|丑陋的老妇人；女巫；巫婆|An ugly old woman; a witch.|例句：The old hag lived in a creepy cottage in the woods.  那个丑陋的老妇人住在森林里一个令人毛骨悚然的茅屋里。

例句：In fairy tales, hags are often portrayed as wicked characters. 在童话故事中，老巫婆通常被描绘成邪恶的角色。|近义词：witch, crone, harridan|反义词:beauty, young woman|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hagge*, from Old English *hæġtesse* (“witch”), from Proto-Germanic *hagatusjō*, *hagazusjō* (“witch, nightmare”), from Proto-Indo-European *kakkā* (“to cackle”). Cognate with Scots *hag* (“hag, witch”), West Frisian *hagetisse* (“witch”), Dutch *heks* (“witch”), German *Hexe* (“witch”), Icelandic *höggð* (“hag, witch”).
