draining:/ˈdreɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 排水的；使人精疲力竭的 | causing the gradual loss of something; exhausting | The marathon was a draining experience. 马拉松是一次令人精疲力竭的经历. |  近义词: exhausting, tiring, depleting | 反义词: invigorating, energizing | 词性: 形容词

draining:/ˈdreɪnɪŋ/ | n. | 排水; 流干 | the process of removing liquid from something | The draining of the swamp took several weeks.  沼泽的排水工作花了几个星期。 | 近义词: emptying, discharge | 反义词: filling | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *draynen*, from Old Norse *drena* (“to drain, dry”), from Proto-Germanic *drauzijaną* (“to make turbid, muddy”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰrewh₁-* (“to become muddy; to make muddy”). Cognate with Old English *drēahnian* (“to strain, filter”), Dutch *droog* (“dry”), German *trüben* (“to make turbid”). The noun is derived from the verb.
