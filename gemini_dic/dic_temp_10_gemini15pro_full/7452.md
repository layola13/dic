anisomeric:/ˌænɪsoʊˈmɛrɪk/ | adj. | 异构的，非对映异构的 | Relating to or denoting anisomers; not isomeric.  Specifically refers to isomers that are not anomers but differ in the configuration at one or more chiral centers other than the anomeric carbon. | The anisomeric forms of glucose differ in their optical rotation. 葡萄糖的异构体形式在其旋光性方面有所不同。|  The two compounds are anisomeric, differing in the configuration at C-2. 这两种化合物是异构的，在C-2的构型上有所不同。|  diastereomeric, non-anomeric | anomeric | adjective
