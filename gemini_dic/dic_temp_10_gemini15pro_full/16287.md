baptize:/ˈbæpˌtaɪz/ (US), /bæpˈtaɪz/ (UK)| vt. n.|施洗，(基督教)行洗礼；命名，给予名称|To perform the religious rite of baptism; to christen. To immerse or submerge in water. To initiate or dedicate as if by baptism.|例句：The priest baptized the baby.  牧师给婴儿施洗。|近义词：christen, initiate, dedicate|反义词：None|词性：verb, noun
