conventual:/kənˈvɛnʃuəl/ | adj. | 修道的；传统的；惯例的 | Relating to a convent or monastery; conforming to accepted customs and standards; traditional. |  The nuns lived a conventual life. 修女们过着修道院的生活。| synonyms: monastic, cloistered, traditional, orthodox, customary | antonyms: unconventional, unorthodox, modern, innovative | adjective

conventual:/kənˈvɛnʃuəl/ | n. | 修道士；修女 | A member of a convent or monastery. | The conventuals gathered for evening prayer. 修道士们聚集在一起做晚祷。| synonyms: monk, nun, friar | antonyms: layman, laywoman | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin conventualis, from Latin conventus ("assembly, meeting, convent"), from convenire ("to come together"), from com- ("together") + venire ("to come").
