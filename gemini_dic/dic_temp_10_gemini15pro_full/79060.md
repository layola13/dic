gives:/ɡɪvz/
v.
给予; 赠送; 提供; 捐赠; 产生; 引起; 导致; 表明; 透露; 让步; 屈服; 塌陷; 凹陷; (表示数量, 位置等)读作, 写作
Third person singular present tense form of give.  It is the form used with he, she, it, or singular nouns.
He gives her a flower every day. 他每天都送她一朵花。
The sun gives us light and warmth. 太阳给予我们光和热。
Synonyms: offers, presents, donates, grants, provides, furnishes, yields, produces
Antonyms: takes, receives, withholds, denies
词性: 动词 (verb)
