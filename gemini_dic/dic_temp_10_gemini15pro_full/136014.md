nonreactions:/ˌnɑːnriˈækʃənz/
n.
lack of reactions; absence of response
The politician's nonreactions to the scandal further fueled public anger.  这位政治家对丑闻的毫无反应进一步激起了公众的愤怒。
His nonreactions to her advances were confusing. 他对她示好的无动于衷令人困惑。
Synonyms: inaction, apathy, indifference, silence
Antonyms: reactions, responses, actions, engagement
noun
