misbrewing:/mɪsˈbruːɪŋ/
noun
酿造错误；酿坏的酒
The act of brewing (beer, etc.) incorrectly, resulting in a poor quality product.
The misbrewing resulted in a sour-tasting beer. 酿造错误导致啤酒有酸味。
mismanagement, miscalculation, blunder
correct brewing, successful brewing
n.
