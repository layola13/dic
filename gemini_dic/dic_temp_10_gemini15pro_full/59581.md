ebracteate:/iːˈbræktiət/ | adj. | 无苞片的 | Lacking bracts; (of a plant or flower) without bracts, the small leaflike structures often located at the base of a flower. | The ebracteate flowers of this species are quite distinctive.  这种植物的无苞片花朵非常独特。| The ebracteate inflorescence was easily identifiable.  无苞片的开花很容易识别。 |近义词: bractless |反义词: bracteate| 词性: 形容词
