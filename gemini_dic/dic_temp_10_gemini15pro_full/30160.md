celebrater:/ˈsɛləˌbreɪtər/ | n. | 庆祝者；庆贺者 |  A person who celebrates something.  A person who observes a special occasion or event with ceremonies of respect, festivity, or rejoicing. |例句：The celebraters gathered in the town square. 庆祝者们聚集在城镇广场上。

例句：He was a true celebrater of life. 他是一个真正热爱生活的人。| 近义词：reveler, carouser, partygoer | 反义词：mourner | 词性: 名词
