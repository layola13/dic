folier:/ˈfɔɪliər/| n.|挫败；阻挠者；箔|A person or thing that prevents someone from achieving something; something that highlights, by contrast, the qualities of another thing.|例句：The police foiled the bank robbery. 警察挫败了银行抢劫。|例句：His elaborate plans were foiled by a sudden change in the weather. 他精心制定的计划被突如其来的天气变化给破坏了。|近义词：thwarter, frustrater, obstructer|反义词:abettor, helper, facilitator|词性:名词

词源:
From Old French folier "to trample," from Vulgar Latin *folliare, from Latin folium "leaf."  The original sense was "to cover with leaves," hence "to stop up, obstruct."  The sense development likely occurred because leaves were sometimes used to stop up holes or cracks.
