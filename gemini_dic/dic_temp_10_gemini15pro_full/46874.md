dareth:/ˈdɛərɛθ/ /ˈdɛərɪθ/ | v. | 胆敢，敢于 |  Archaic third-person singular present form of *dare*.  It is an older form of "dares" and is rarely used in modern English. | He dareth not speak ill of the king. 他不敢说国王的坏话。 | 近义词: dares, ventures, risks | 反义词: fears, hesitates, avoids | 词性: 动词

词源:
From Middle English *dar*, from Old English *durr* (to dare), of Germanic origin.
