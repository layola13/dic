edulcorant:/ˈɛdʌlˌkɔːrənt/ /ˈiːdʌlˌkɔːrənt/
noun
甜味剂；增甜剂
A substance added to food or beverages to make them taste sweeter.
Example: This low-calorie dessert uses an artificial edulcorant derived from stevia.  这种低卡路里甜点使用了一种从甜叶菊中提取的人工甜味剂。
Example:  Many processed foods contain edulcorants to enhance their palatability. 许多加工食品中都含有甜味剂以增强其适口性。

近义词: sweetener, sweetening agent
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider) bittering agent
