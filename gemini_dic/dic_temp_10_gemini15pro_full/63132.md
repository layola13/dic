ensnarement:/ɪnˈsnɛərmənt/| n.|陷阱，圈套，束缚|The act of ensnaring or the state of being ensnared; a trap or entanglement.|Her beauty was his ensnarement. 她的美貌是他的陷阱。|Synonyms: trap, entanglement, snare, captivity|Antonyms: freedom, liberation, release|noun

ensnarement:/ɪnˈsnɛərmənt/| n.|诱捕，诱骗|The act of luring or tempting someone into a difficult or dangerous situation.|The ensnarement of young people into drug addiction is a serious problem.  将年轻人诱骗吸毒是一个严重的问题。|Synonyms: seduction, enticement, temptation, allurement|Antonyms: dissuasion, deterrence, discouragement|noun
