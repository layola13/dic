allopathic:/ˌæləˈpæθɪk/ | adj. |对抗疗法的；对症疗法的 | Relating to or characterized by allopathy; a system of medical practice that aims to combat disease by using remedies which produce effects different from those produced by the disease under treatment. | Example: Allopathic medicine is the dominant system of healthcare in many countries. 对抗疗法是许多国家的主流医疗体系。| Example:  She sought allopathic treatment for her persistent headaches. 她因为持续的头痛寻求对抗疗法治疗。| Synonym: conventional, orthodox, traditional | Antonym: homeopathic, alternative, complementary | Word type: adjective
