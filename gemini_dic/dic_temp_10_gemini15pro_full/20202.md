birdcatcher:/ˈbɜːrdˌkætʃər/ | n. |捕鸟人；捕鸟器|A person who catches birds, or a device used for catching birds.|The old birdcatcher used a net to trap the finches. 老捕鸟人用网捕捉雀类。|近义词：birder, fowler|反义词: bird protector, conservationist|noun


birdcatcher:/ˈbɜːrdˌkætʃər/ | n. |捕鸟器|A device used for catching birds.|He set a birdcatcher in the garden to control the pigeon population. 他在花园里设置了一个捕鸟器来控制鸽子的数量。|近义词：bird trap, snare|反义词: bird feeder|noun
