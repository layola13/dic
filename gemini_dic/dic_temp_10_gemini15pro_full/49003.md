degradable:/dɪˈɡreɪdəbl/ | adj. | 可降解的；可分解的 | Able to be broken down, especially by natural processes. |  This bag is made of degradable plastic.  这个袋子是由可降解塑料制成的。

This type of packaging is completely degradable. 这种包装是完全可降解的。


| 近义词:biodegradable, compostable, decomposable | 反义词:non-degradable, persistent | 词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From  degrade (v.) + -able.  Degrade comes from French dégrader, from dé- "down" + grader "to step, grade." Ultimately from Latin gradus "step, degree."
