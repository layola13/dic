nigrosine:/ˈnaɪɡrəˌsin, ˈnaɪɡrəˌsiːn/
noun
苯胺黑，黑素
a synthetic black dye made by heating a mixture of nitrobenzene, aniline, and hydrochloric acid with iron filings.  It is used in shoe polish, leather dyes, and biological stains.
Nigrosine is commonly used to stain nerve tissues. 苯胺黑常用于染色神经组织。
Nigrosine is a popular choice for coloring leather. 苯胺黑是皮革着色的常用选择。
Synonyms: black dye, aniline black
Antonyms: N/A (color, not easily assigned an opposite)
