attenuant:/əˈtɛnjuːənt/| adj. n.|稀释的，减弱的；稀释剂，减弱剂|Something that attenuates; a weakening or thinning agent. |The doctor prescribed an attenuant for her cough. 医生给她开了止咳化痰药。|近义词：diluting, weakening, thinning, reducing, mitigating|反义词：intensifying, strengthening, thickening, aggravating|词性:adjective, noun
attenuant:/əˈtɛnjuːənt/| n.|稀释剂，减弱剂|A substance that reduces the force, effect, or value of something. | This attenuant can help to reduce the pain.  这种减弱剂可以帮助减轻疼痛。|近义词：diluent, thinner|反义词：intensifier|词性:noun
