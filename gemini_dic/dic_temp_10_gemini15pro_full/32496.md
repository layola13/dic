chinking: /ˈtʃɪŋkɪŋ/ | adj. | (金属碰撞的)叮当响的 |Making a light ringing sound, like metal striking against metal. | We heard a chinking sound as the coins dropped into the jar. 我们听到硬币掉进罐子里叮当作响的声音。| 近义词: clinking, jingling, tinkling | 反义词:  silent, quiet | 词性: adjective
