localnesses:/ˈloʊkəlnəsɪz/
noun
地方性；本地性；局部性
The quality or state of being local or restricted to a particular place.
The localnesses of the dialects made communication difficult. 这些方言的地方性使得交流变得困难。
locality, regionality
globalness, universality
n.
