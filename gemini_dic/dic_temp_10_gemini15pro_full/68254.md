faithfuls:/ˈfeɪθfʊlz/| n.|信徒，忠实信徒|A group of people who are loyal and devoted, especially to a religion or cause.|The pastor addressed the faithfuls gathered in the church. 牧师向聚集在教堂里的信徒们讲话。|Synonyms: believers, adherents, devotees|Antonyms: disbelievers, skeptics, apostates|noun

faithfuls:/ˈfeɪθfʊlz/| adj.(rare, archaic) |忠实的，忠诚的 (古语，罕用)|Characterized by steadfast loyalty and devotion.|The faithfuls dog waited patiently for its master's return. 忠实的狗耐心地等待着主人的归来。|Synonyms: loyal, devoted, steadfast, constant|Antonyms: disloyal, unfaithful, treacherous|adjective
