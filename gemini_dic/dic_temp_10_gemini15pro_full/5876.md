ambassadress:/æmˈbæsədrəs/
n.
女大使；大使夫人
a woman who is an ambassador; the wife of an ambassador
She was appointed ambassadress to the United States. 她被任命为驻美国大使。
The ambassadress hosted a reception at the embassy. 大使夫人在使馆举行招待会。
近义词: ambassador, diplomat
反义词: (无直接反义词)
词性: noun
