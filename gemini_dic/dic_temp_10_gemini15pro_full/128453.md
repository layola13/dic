newscasts:/ˈnjuːzkæsts/ /ˈnuːzkæsts/
n.
新闻广播；新闻节目
A program that broadcasts news reports.
I watch the evening newscasts to stay informed about current events. 我观看晚间新闻节目以了解时事。
The newscasts reported on the developing storm. 新闻节目报道了正在发展的风暴。
synonyms: news bulletins, news reports, news programs
antonyms: documentaries, entertainment programs
noun
