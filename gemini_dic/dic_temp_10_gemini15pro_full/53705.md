disclaunder:/dɪsˈklɔːndər/
verb
为...洗清罪名；澄清；辩白
To deny responsibility for or connection with; to clear from alleged fault or guilt.
He disclaundered himself from the accusations of theft. 他为自己洗清了盗窃的指控。
近义词: vindicate, exonerate, acquit, absolve, clear, defend
反义词: accuse, incriminate, blame, condemn
词性: verb

disclaunder:/dɪsˈklɔːndər/
noun
辩白；澄清
A denial of responsibility or connection with something; a clearing from alleged fault or guilt.
His disclaunder was accepted by the court. 法庭接受了他的辩白。
近义词: vindication, exoneration, acquittal, absolution, defense
反义词: accusation, incrimination, blame, condemnation
词性: noun


词源:
From dis- + claunder (obsolete form of slander).  The prefix "dis-" indicates negation or reversal, suggesting the removal of slander or false accusation.
