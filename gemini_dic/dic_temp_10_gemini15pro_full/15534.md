bagel:/ˈbeɪɡəl/ (US), /ˈbeɪɡl/ (UK)| n.|贝果，百吉圈|A dense, ring-shaped roll made from yeasted dough, boiled then baked. It is often topped with seeds or seasonings.|I had a bagel with cream cheese for breakfast. 我早餐吃了一个涂奶油奶酪的贝果。|Synonyms: roll, bun|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "sliced bread" as a contrasting common bread type)|noun
