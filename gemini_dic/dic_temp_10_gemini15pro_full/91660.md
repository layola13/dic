hypercatharsis:/ˌhaɪpərˈkəθɑːrsɪs/
n.
过度宣泄，极度净化
An excessive purging of emotions, especially through artistic expression.  A release that goes beyond normal catharsis.
The play provided a hypercatharsis for the audience, leaving them emotionally drained. 这部戏剧为观众提供了一种过度宣泄的体验，让他们情绪枯竭。
Intense emotional release can sometimes lead to hypercatharsis, which can be detrimental. 强烈的情绪释放有时会导致过度宣泄，这可能是有害的。
近义词:catharsis, purgation, abreaction
反义词:repression, suppression
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (Greek: 'over, beyond') + catharsis (Greek: 'purification, cleansing')
