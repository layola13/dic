pals: /pælz/ | n. | 朋友，伙伴 | Close friends; companions. |  My pals and I went to the movies. 我和我的朋友们去看电影了。

pals: /pælz/ | n. | 朋友，伙伴 | People you have a friendly relationship with. | We've been pals since kindergarten. 我们从幼儿园起就是朋友了。


近义词: friends, buddies, mates, companions
反义词: enemies, foes
词性: noun (plural)

词源: Shortening of "palefaces," a term originally used by Native Americans to refer to white people.  Over time, it lost its original meaning and became a general term for friend.
