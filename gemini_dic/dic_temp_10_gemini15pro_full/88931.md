holderships:/ˈhoʊldərʃɪps/| n. |持有量，持股份额，所有权|The act, fact, or right of possessing and controlling something.  It often refers to the amount or proportion of shares owned in a company or other asset.  It can also refer to the period for which something is held.|例句：Foreign holderships of US Treasury bonds have declined. 外国持有的美国国债数量有所下降。

例句：The company's holderships in real estate are extensive.  该公司在房地产方面的持有量很大。|近义词：holdings, ownership, possession|反义词: relinquishment, surrender|词性:noun
