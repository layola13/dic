overstretched:/ˌoʊvərˈstrɛtʃt/
adjective
过度扩张的；过度紧张的；承担过多义务的
Having too many demands or responsibilities and not enough resources or time to deal with them effectively.
The hospital's resources are overstretched due to the pandemic.  这家医院的资源由于疫情而过度紧张。
The company's staff is overstretched and overworked.  这家公司的员工负担过重，工作过度。
近义词: overburdened, strained, overextended
反义词: relaxed, comfortable, well-resourced
词性: adjective
