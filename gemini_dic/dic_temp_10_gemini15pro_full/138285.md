notarizing: /ˈnoʊtəˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
公证
The official act of certifying a document.
Example: The lawyer is notarizing the will.  律师正在公证遗嘱。

Example:  Notarizing the document makes it legally binding. 公证文件使其具有法律约束力。

Synonym: certifying, authenticating, validating
Antonym: invalidating, rejecting

Etymology:  From notary + -ize + -ing.  Notary comes from the Latin word "notarius," meaning "shorthand writer" or "scribe."

Word type: Gerund or present participle of the verb "notarize"
