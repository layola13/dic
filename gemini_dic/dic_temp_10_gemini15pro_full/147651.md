overpollinating:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːləˌneɪtɪŋ/
verb
过度授粉
The act of pollinating a plant or crop excessively, often leading to diminished fruit or seed production.
The farmer worried about overpollinating his apple orchard.  农民担心他的苹果园过度授粉。
over-pollinate, excessive pollination
underpollinating
v.
