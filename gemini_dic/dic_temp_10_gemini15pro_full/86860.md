hemiglyph:/ˈhɛmɪɡlɪf/
noun
半象形文字
A written symbol that represents a word or syllable, combining features of ideographic and phonetic writing systems; a sign that is partly ideographic and partly phonetic.
The ancient Egyptian writing system utilized hemiglyphs, representing both concepts and sounds.  埃及古文字使用了半象形文字，既代表概念又代表声音。
semi-ideogram, semi-phonetic sign
