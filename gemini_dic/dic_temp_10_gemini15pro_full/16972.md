bastardization:/ˌbæstərdaɪˈzeɪʃn/
|n.|私生子化，歪曲，窜改|An act that debases or corrupts something, especially a language or text, by adulterating or simplifying it.  The process of changing something in such a way that it becomes less respected or of lower quality.|The bastardization of the language through the introduction of slang.|近义词：corruption, debasement, distortion, perversion, adulteration|反义词：purification, refinement|词性: noun

bastardize:/ˈbæstərdaɪz/
|v.|使…不纯，窜改，歪曲|To change something in such a way that it becomes less respected or of lower quality; to corrupt or debase.|The architect felt that the addition of the modern wing bastardized the original building.|近义词：corrupt, debase, distort, pervert, adulterate|反义词：purify, refine|词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From bastard + -ize.  Ultimately from Old French *bastard, of uncertain origin.
