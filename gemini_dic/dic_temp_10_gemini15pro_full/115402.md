mansuete:/mænˈswiːt/ /mænˈsjuːɪt/| adj.|温顺的，驯服的，温和的|Gentle and tame; not wild or violent.  Originating from the Latin word "mansuetus," meaning "tamed, gentle."|The farmer had a mansuete horse that was easy to ride.  那个农夫有一匹温顺的、很容易骑的马。| The zookeeper described the animal as surprisingly mansuete.  动物园管理员描述这动物出奇地温顺。| 近义词：tame, docile, gentle, meek, mild|反义词：wild, fierce, aggressive, unruly|词性:形容词
