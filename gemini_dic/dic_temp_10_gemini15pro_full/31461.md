charlatan:/ˈʃɑːrlətən/ | n. |江湖骗子；吹牛大王；庸医|A person falsely claiming to have a special knowledge or skill; a fraud.|例句：The charlatan tricked people into believing he could cure any disease. 这个江湖骗子骗人相信他能治好任何疾病。

例句：He was exposed as a charlatan who knew nothing about medicine. 他被揭露是一个对医学一无所知的骗子。|近义词：fraud, imposter, quack, fake, sham|反义词：expert, professional, authority|词性：名词

charlatans:/ˈʃɑːrlətənz/ | n. |江湖骗子们；吹牛大王们；庸医们|Plural form of charlatan.|例句：Beware of charlatans who promise quick riches.  小心那些承诺快速致富的骗子们。

例句：Many charlatans prey on vulnerable people. 许多骗子都把弱势群体作为目标。|近义词：frauds, imposters, quacks, fakes, shams|反义词：experts, professionals, authorities|词性：名词
