markswoman:/ˈmɑːrksˌwʊmən/ | n. | 女神枪手，女射手 | A woman who is skilled in shooting, especially with a rifle. |  例句：The markswoman took aim and fired, hitting the target dead center.  女射手瞄准开火，正中靶心。

例句：She was a renowned markswoman, known for her accuracy and composure. 她是一位著名的神枪手，以精准和沉着著称。 | 近义词：sharpshooter, sniper | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mark +‎ -s- +‎ woman.  The -s- probably by analogy with marksman.
