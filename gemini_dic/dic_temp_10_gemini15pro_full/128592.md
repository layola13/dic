niceness:/ˈnaɪsnɪs/
n.
仁慈；善良；美好；愉快
the quality of being pleasant, friendly, and kind.
Her niceness and generosity were appreciated by everyone. 她的善良和慷慨得到了大家的赞赏。
kindness, generosity, pleasantness, agreeableness
nastiness, unkindness, rudeness, unpleasantness
