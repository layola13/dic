pacific-times:/pəˈsɪfɪk taɪmz/ | n. |太平洋时间|The time zone observed in the westernmost parts of North America, including the states of California, Oregon, Washington, Nevada, and parts of Idaho and British Columbia.  It is 8 hours behind Coordinated Universal Time (UTC) during standard time and 7 hours behind UTC during daylight saving time.|例句：The meeting is scheduled for 2 PM Pacific Time.  会议安排在太平洋时间下午2点。

例句：It's 9 AM Pacific Time, which means it's noon Eastern Time. 现在是太平洋时间上午9点，也就是东部时间中午12点。|近义词：PT (abbreviation), West Coast time|反义词：Eastern Time (ET), Central Time (CT), Mountain Time (MT)|词性:noun
