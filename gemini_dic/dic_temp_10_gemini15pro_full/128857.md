nihilist: /ˈnaɪɪlɪst/ (US), /ˈnɪɪlɪst/ (UK) | n. | 虚无主义者； нигилист | A person who believes that life is meaningless and rejects all religious and moral principles. |  He's a nihilist who believes in nothing. 他是一个什么都不信的虚无主义者。 |  synonyms: cynic, pessimist, skeptic | antonyms: optimist, believer | noun

nihilistic: /ˌnaɪɪˈlɪstɪk/ (US), /ˌnɪɪˈlɪstɪk/ (UK) | adj. | 虚无主义的；нигилистический | Embracing or involving nihilism. | His nihilistic worldview left him feeling empty and hopeless. 他的虚无主义世界观让他感到空虚和绝望。 | synonyms: cynical, pessimistic, skeptical | antonyms: optimistic, hopeful | adjective
