cirsoid:/ˈsɜːrsɔɪd/
adjective
蔓状的，曲张的
Resembling a varix; varicose or dilated.  Characterized by a collection of dilated and tortuous vessels.
The patient presented with a cirsoid aneurysm.  病人出现蔓状动脉瘤。
近义词:varicose, dilated, tortuous
反义词:normal, healthy, straight
词性:adjective

cirsoid:/ˈsɜːrsɔɪd/
noun
蔓状血管瘤
A mass of dilated and tortuous vessels, usually arteries.
The surgeon successfully removed the cirsoid.  外科医生成功地切除了蔓状血管瘤。
近义词:varix, aneurysm
反义词: (No direct antonym for a physical abnormality)
词性:noun
