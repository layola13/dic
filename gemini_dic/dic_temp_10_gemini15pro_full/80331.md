goldest: /ˈɡoʊldɪst/
adjective
最金的 (non-standard)
This word is not considered standard English.  It attempts to create a superlative form of "golden," which is already somewhat superlative in meaning.  It suggests something is more golden than something else that is already golden, implying an even greater degree of gold-like qualities like color, brilliance, or value.
"He had the goldest hair I'd ever seen, even more radiant than the setting sun." (non-standard)
近义词: most golden, most gold-like (though even these are uncommon)
反义词: dullest, least golden (also uncommon)
词性: adjective
