densities:/ˈdɛnsɪtiz/
n.
密度；密集度
The mass of a substance per unit volume.
The population density of Tokyo is very high.  东京的人口密度非常高。

近义词: concentrations, thickness
反义词: sparseness, thinness
词性: 名词 (plural of density)

density:/ˈdɛnsɪti/
n.
密度；密集度
The degree of compactness of a substance.
The density of the fog made driving difficult. 浓雾使驾驶困难。

近义词: compactness, solidity
反义词: rarity, looseness
词性: 名词
