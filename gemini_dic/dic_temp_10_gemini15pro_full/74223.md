fouty:/ˈfaʊti/| adj.|发霉的，腐臭的|Having a stale, musty, or unpleasant smell, typically due to dampness or decay.  Smelling or tasting as if beginning to decay.|The bread had gone fouty and was covered in mould.  面包已经发霉了，上面布满了霉菌。|近义词：musty, stale, mildewy, fusty|反义词：fresh, fragrant|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Scots *fou*, related to foul.
