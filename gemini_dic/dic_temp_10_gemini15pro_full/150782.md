paradisiacal: /ˌpærədɪˈsaɪək(ə)l/ /ˌpærədɪˈsiːɪk(ə)l/
| adj. | 天堂般的，极乐的 | Resembling or suggesting paradise; heavenly. |例句：The island boasts paradisiacal beaches.  这个岛屿拥有天堂般的海滩。|例句：The resort offers a paradisiacal escape from the stresses of everyday life.  这个度假村提供了一个远离日常生活压力的天堂般的避难所。|近义词：heavenly, blissful, idyllic, utopian |反义词：hellish, infernal, nightmarish|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *paradisiacus, from Latin paradisus (“paradise”) + -acus.  Ultimately from Ancient Greek παράδεισος (parádeisos, “park, paradise”).
