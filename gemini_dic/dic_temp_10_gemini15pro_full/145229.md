overbias:/ˌoʊvərˈbaɪəs/| v. |过度偏置，过分偏袒|To introduce excessive bias into (a system, device, or process).|The algorithm was overbiased towards certain demographics, leading to unfair outcomes.  该算法对某些人群过度偏向，导致结果不公平。

The judge was accused of overbiasing the jury with his comments.  法官被指控用他的评论过度影响陪审团。|近义词：distort, skew, weight, prejudice|反义词:balance, equalize|词性:动词

词源: "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "bias" (noun meaning "prejudice in favor of or against one thing, person, or group compared with another, usually in a way considered to be unfair").
