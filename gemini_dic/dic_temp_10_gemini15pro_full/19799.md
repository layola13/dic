bilocular:/baɪˈlɒkjʊlə(r)/ /baɪˈlɑːkjələr/
adjective
双室的，有两室的
Having two cavities or compartments; divided into two cells or chambers.
The bilocular ovary is a distinguishing feature of this plant species.  这种植物的双室子房是其区别性特征。
The fruit is a bilocular capsule containing numerous seeds. 这种果实是一个包含许多种子的双室蒴果。

近义词: two-chambered, bicameral
反义词: unilocular

词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *bi- (two) + loculus (small place, compartment)
