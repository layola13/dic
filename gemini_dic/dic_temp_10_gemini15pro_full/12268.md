ascertainer:/ˌæsərˈteɪnər/
n.
确定者; 查明者; 弄清真相者
a person who ascertains something
例句：The ascertainer of the facts presented his findings to the court.  事实查明者向法庭提交了他的调查结果。

例句：The police acted as ascertainers in the investigation. 警方在调查中充当了事实查明者的角色。

近义词：investigator, researcher, inquirer
反义词：ignorer, neglecter
词性: noun
