insatiately:/ɪnˈseɪʃəbli/ | adv. |  贪得无厌地，不知足地 |  In a manner that cannot be satisfied; constantly wanting more. | He ate insatiately, as if he hadn't eaten in days. 他狼吞虎咽地吃着，好像好几天没吃东西一样。|  She shopped insatiately, filling her closet with clothes she didn't need. 她疯狂购物，把衣橱里塞满了她不需要的衣服。| 近义词: greedily, ravenously, voraciously | 反义词: moderately, temperately | 词性: 副词

insatiable:/ɪnˈseɪʃəbl/ | adj. | 贪得无厌的，不知足的 |  Impossible to satisfy. |  He has an insatiable appetite for knowledge. 他对知识有着永不满足的渴望。|  Her insatiable desire for power led her to make some questionable decisions. 她对权力永不满足的渴望导致她做出了一些 questionable 的决定。| 近义词: unquenchable, voracious, greedy | 反义词: satisfied, content | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Late Latin *insatiabilis, from Latin in- "not" + satiabilis "able to be satisfied," from satiare "to satisfy."
