miniconglomerates:/ˈmɪni kənˈɡlɒmərəts/ (US) /ˈmɪni kənˈɡlɒmərɪts/| n. |小型企业集团，微型企业集团|A group of small, diverse companies under common ownership, often formed through mergers or acquisitions.  These are smaller versions of larger conglomerates.| Berkshire Hathaway, despite its size, operates in a manner similar to a collection of miniconglomerates. |synonyms: small conglomerates, baby conglomerates |antonyms: large conglomerates, mega-corporations| noun
