overinsured:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈʃʊərd/| adj.|过度投保的|Having insurance coverage exceeding the actual value of the insured item or person.|例句：The house was overinsured, so they profited from the fire. 这房子过度投保了，所以他们从火灾中获利了。|例句：Being overinsured can be a waste of money. 过度投保可能会浪费钱。|近义词：overprotected|反义词：underinsured|词性:形容词

overinsured:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈʃʊərd/| adj.|保险过度的|Having too much insurance.|例句：He was overinsured on his car. 他的车保险过度了。|例句：It's pointless being overinsured, it won't cover emotional distress.  保险过度毫无意义，它不会弥补精神损失。|近义词：excessively insured|反义词：underinsured|词性:形容词
