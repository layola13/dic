hunk:/hʌŋk/ | n. | 大块；壮汉；有魅力的男子 | A large piece of something, especially food; A large, strong, and attractive man. | He took a hunk of bread and cheese. 他拿了一大块面包和奶酪。 She married a real hunk. 她嫁给了一个真正的壮汉。 | 近义词:chunk, piece, slab; beefcake, stud | 反义词:scrap, sliver | 词性:名词

词源:
Likely from Middle Dutch *hunke "lump, piece," related to hinken "to limp," from Proto-Germanic *hankōną. The semantic shift might be from "a lump of flesh" to "a large person."
