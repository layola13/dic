burningly:/ˈbɜːnɪŋli/
adverb
灼热地，热烈地
intensely, passionately, fervently
She looked at him burningly. 她灼热地望着他。
Synonyms: intensely, passionately, fervently, ardently, fiercely
Antonyms: coldly, indifferently, apathetically
词性: 副词
