hexanoic:/hɛkˈsænoʊɪk/ | adj. | 己酸的；正己酸的 | Relating to or derived from hexane, especially hexanoic acid (also called caproic acid), a carboxylic acid with the formula CH3(CH2)4COOH. | Hexanoic acid is found in animal fats and oils and is responsible for the pungent odor of goats and other barnyard animals. 己酸存在于动物脂肪和油中，是山羊和其他牲畜身上刺鼻气味的原因。| 近义词: caproic | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective
