cerebripetal:/ˌsɛrɪˈbrɪpɪtəl/
adjective
向心的；传入的；向脑的
Conveying impulses towards the brain or higher nerve centers.
Sensory nerves are cerebripetal, conveying information from the body to the brain. 感觉神经是向心的，将信息从身体传递到大脑。
The cerebripetal flow of information allows the brain to process sensory input. 向脑的信息流使大脑能够处理感觉输入。

近义词:afferent, centripetal
反义词:cerebrifugal, centrifugal
词性:adjective
