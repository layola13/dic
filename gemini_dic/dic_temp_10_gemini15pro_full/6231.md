amnicolist:/ˌæmnɪˈkɑːlɪst/ | n. |溪螺爱好者，淡水螺收集者|A person who studies or collects amnicolid snails (freshwater snails).| He's a keen amnicolist, with a vast collection of shells.| 近义词:conchologist (更广义的贝壳学家) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From  Amnicolidae (a family of freshwater snails) + -ist (a suffix denoting a person who studies or practices something).  Ultimately from Latin *amnis* "river" + Greek *oikos* "house" + Latin suffix *-cola* "inhabitant" +  Greek *-istēs*.
