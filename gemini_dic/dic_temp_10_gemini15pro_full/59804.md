ecraseur:/eɪˈkrɑːzɜːr/
noun
压榨器，勒除器
A surgical instrument consisting of a loop of chain or wire used for removing hemorrhoids or other growths by strangulation.
The surgeon used an ecraseur to remove the polyp.  外科医生用压榨器切除了息肉。
Synonyms: snare, ligature
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps scalpel for a cutting instrument as opposed to a constricting one)
词性: 名词
