demonises:/ˈdiːmənaɪzɪz/
verb
妖魔化; 将…描绘成恶魔
to portray as wicked or threatening
The media often demonises politicians it disagrees with. 媒体经常妖魔化它不同意的政治家。
synonyms: vilifies, denounces, condemns, criticizes harshly
antonyms: praises, glorifies, extols, celebrates
词性: 动词(第三人称单数)
