buzzword:/ˈbʌzwɜːrd/| n.|流行词，时髦词语，行话|A word or phrase, often originating in a particular profession or area of interest, that becomes fashionable and is used a lot, especially by the media.|The consultant peppered his presentation with buzzwords like "synergy" and "paradigm shift."  顾问在他的演讲中大量使用了“协同效应”和“范式转变”之类的流行语。|synonyms: jargon,  slang, catchphrase, vogue word|antonyms: cliché, archaism|noun
