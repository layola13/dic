infectiously:/ɪnˈfekʃəsli/ | adv. | 传染性地；有感染力地 | In a way that is likely to spread or influence others. | Her laughter was infectious, and soon everyone was joining in. 她的笑声很有感染力，很快大家都加入了。| contagiously, compellingly, irresistibly | dully, blandly | adverb
infectiously:/ɪnˈfekʃəsli/ | adv. | 传染性地；有感染力地 |  Capable of being transmitted quickly from one person or thing to another, especially by close contact or association.  | The enthusiasm of the fans spread infectiously through the crowd. 球迷的热情感染了整群观众。| virulently, communicably | uninspiringly, flatly | adverb
