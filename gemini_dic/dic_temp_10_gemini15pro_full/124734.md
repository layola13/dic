mozzies:/ˈmɒziz/ (UK), /ˈmɑːziz/ (US)| n. |蚊子|Informal plural of "mosquito."  It refers to those small, flying insects known for their biting and sometimes disease-carrying capabilities.|例句：The mozzies were terrible last night. 昨晚蚊子太多了。|近义词：mosquitoes, skeeters (US informal)|反义词: None (no direct opposite for an insect)|noun
