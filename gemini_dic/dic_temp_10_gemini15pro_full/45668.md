curtainless:/ˈkɜːrtn.ləs/| adj.|无窗帘的，没有窗帘的|Without curtains.  It describes a window or other opening that does not have curtains covering it.|The curtainless window allowed the morning sun to flood the room. 无窗帘的窗户让早晨的阳光洒满房间。|Near synonyms: uncovered, bare, exposed|Antonyms: curtained, draped, covered|adjective
