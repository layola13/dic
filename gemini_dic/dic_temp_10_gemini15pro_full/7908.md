antennal:/ænˈtɛnəl/| adj.|触角的，天线的|Of or relating to an antenna.  Relating to the feelers of an insect.|例句：The antennal lobes of the moth are highly sensitive to pheromones.  飞蛾的触角叶对信息素高度敏感。

例句：The butterfly's antennal movements helped it navigate through the dense forest. 蝴蝶的触角运动帮助它在茂密的森林中航行。|近义词：antenna-like, feeler-like|反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps consider "non-antennal" in specific contexts) |词性: adjective
