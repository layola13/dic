capercaillie:/ˌkæpərˈkeɪli/ | n. |西方松鸡| A large, black grouse, Tetrao urogallus, with a fan-shaped tail, found in coniferous forests of Europe and Asia. | The capercaillie is the largest member of the grouse family. 西方松鸡是松鸡科中体型最大的成员。| The male capercaillie performs an elaborate courtship display. 雄性西方松鸡会进行精心设计的求偶表演。 |Synonyms: wood grouse, heather cock | Antonyms: N/A | noun
