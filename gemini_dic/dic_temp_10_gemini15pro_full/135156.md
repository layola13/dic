nonopacities:/ˌnɒnəʊˈpæsɪtiz/ | n. | 非混浊区，非不透明区域 | Areas within a radiographic image or other visual representation that appear clear or transparent, indicating the absence of opacification or density. | The chest x-ray showed some small nonopacities in the lower lobes. 胸部X光片显示下叶有一些小的非混浊区。| The doctor pointed out the nonopacities on the CT scan, indicating healthy lung tissue. 医生指出了CT扫描上的非混浊区域，表明肺组织健康。 | synonyms: clear areas, lucencies, transparencies | antonyms: opacities, densities, shadows | noun
