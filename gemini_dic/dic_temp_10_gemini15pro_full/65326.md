eugeny:/ˈjuːdʒəni/ | n. |优生学；优生 |The study of how to arrange reproduction within a human population to increase the occurrence of heritable characteristics regarded as desirable. |Eugenics was a popular social movement in the early 20th century. 优生学在20世纪早期是一个很受欢迎的社会运动。| The eugenics movement aimed to improve the human race through selective breeding. 优生运动旨在通过选择性育种来改善人类种族。| synonyms:  selective breeding, genetic engineering | antonyms:  natural selection, genetic diversity | noun
