antieducational:/ˌæntiˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃənl/
adjective
反教育的，不利于教育的
Hindering or opposing education; not conducive to learning.
Some critics believe that excessive screen time is antieducational. 一些评论家认为过多的屏幕时间是不利于教育的。
The school board rejected the proposal, deeming it antieducational. 学校董事会否决了这项提议，认为它不利于教育。
近义词: counter-educational, miseducational
反义词: educational, pro-educational
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From anti- + educational.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," derived from Greek "anti." "Educational" comes from the Latin "educatus," past participle of "educare" (to bring up, to rear), from "ex-" (out) + "ducere" (to lead).
