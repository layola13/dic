nonaxiomatic:/ˌnɒnˌæksiəˈmætɪk/
adjective
非公理的
Not based on or derived from axioms; not self-evident.
This theory rests on several nonaxiomatic assumptions.  这个理论建立在几个非公理的假设之上。
The principle of causality is considered nonaxiomatic by some philosophers.  一些哲学家认为因果关系原理是非公理的。
non-axiomatic
unprovable, undemonstrable, non-self-evident
axiomatic, self-evident, demonstrable
