galatea:/ˌɡæləˈtiːə/ | n. | 盖拉蒂亚 | A statue brought to life by Pygmalion in Greek mythology; something inanimate given life or animation; a woman who is ideally beautiful but cold and unresponsive. |  Pygmalion fell in love with his own sculpture, Galatea, and the gods brought her to life. 皮格马利翁爱上了自己的雕塑盖拉蒂亚，众神赋予了她生命。 |  近义词: statue, creation, automaton | 反义词: human, mortal | 词性: 名词

galatea:/ˌɡæləˈtiːə/ | n. |  棉府绸 |  A strong, twilled cotton fabric.  | The dress was made of a crisp white galatea. 这条连衣裙是用挺括的白色棉府绸做的。 | 近义词:  cotton, fabric | 反义词: - | 词性: 名词
