misportrayal:/ˌmɪspɔːrˈtreɪəl/
noun
歪曲；误传；失真
an act of portraying someone or something in a false or misleading way
The film was criticized for its misportrayal of historical events. 这部电影因歪曲历史事件而受到批评。
Synonyms: misrepresentation, distortion, falsification, perversion
Antonyms: accurate portrayal, faithful representation
