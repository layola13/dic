discolourment:/dɪsˈkʌlərmənt/ /dɪsˈkʌlərment/| n.|变色，褪色|A change in the natural colour of something, typically to a less attractive shade.|The discolourment of the painting was due to sunlight exposure. 这幅画的褪色是由于阳光照射造成的。|Synonyms: discoloration, fading, staining, bleaching|Antonyms: brightening, enhancing|noun


discolour:/dɪsˈkʌlər/ /dɪsˈkʌlər/| v.|使变色，使褪色|To cause something to change colour, typically to a less attractive shade.|Exposure to sunlight can discolour fabrics. 暴露在阳光下会使织物褪色。|Synonyms: stain, tarnish, fade, bleach|Antonyms: brighten, enhance|verb
