munch-out:/mʌntʃ aʊt/
|phrasal verb|
|大吃特吃；狼吞虎咽|
|To eat a large amount of food enthusiastically, often snacks or junk food.|
|We munched out on pizza and wings while watching the game. 我们一边看比赛一边大吃披萨和鸡翅。|
|pig out, gorge, stuff oneself, feast|
|diet, nibble|
