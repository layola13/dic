diadromous:/daɪˈædrəməs/| adj.|洄游的|Migrating between fresh and salt water (of fish).|例句：Salmon are diadromous fish, spending part of their lives in rivers and part in the ocean. 鲑鱼是洄游鱼类，它们生命的一部分时间在河流中度过，一部分时间在海洋中度过。

例句：The diadromous eel travels thousands of miles from the Sargasso Sea to freshwater streams. 洄游鳗鱼从马尾藻海到淡水溪流要游数千英里。|近义词：migratory, oceanodromous|反义词：potadromous (纯淡水鱼), oceanodromous (纯海水鱼)|词性:形容词

词源:
From Greek *dia* "through" + *dromos* "running".
