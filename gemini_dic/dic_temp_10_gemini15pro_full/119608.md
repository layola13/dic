midas: /ˈmaɪdəs/ | n. |迈达斯|  A king of Phrygia in Greek mythology who was given the power by Dionysus to turn whatever he touched into gold, but later regretted it when his food and drink, and even his daughter, turned to gold. | He has the Midas touch - every business he starts makes money. 他拥有点石成金的能力——他创办的每个企业都赚钱。 | 近义词: Croesus, plutocrat | 反义词: pauper | noun
