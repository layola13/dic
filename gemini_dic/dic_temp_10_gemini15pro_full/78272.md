germanization:/ˌdʒɜːmənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 德国化；日耳曼化|The process of making something German in character or bringing something under German influence. |The germanization of the region led to the suppression of the local language.  该地区的德国化导致了当地语言的压制。|synonyms: assimilation, acculturation|antonyms: diversification, localization|noun

germanization:/ˌdʒɜːmənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 德国化；日耳曼化|The forced adoption of German culture and language, often associated with historical periods of German expansionism. |The germanization policies during the war were met with resistance.  战争期间的德国化政策遭到了抵制。|synonyms: imposition, cultural imperialism|antonyms: indigenization, cultural preservation|noun
