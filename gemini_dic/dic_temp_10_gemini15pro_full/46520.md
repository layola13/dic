dakoit:/dəˈkɔɪt/| n.|土匪，强盗|A member of a band of armed robbers in India or Burma (Myanmar).  Originally referring specifically to robbers in India who operated in the ravines and jungles, preying on travelers and villages.  The term later broadened to encompass similar bandits elsewhere. |The dakoits ambushed the caravan as it passed through the mountain pass. 土匪在商队经过山口时伏击了他们。|Synonyms: bandit, brigand, robber, outlaw, highwayman|Antonyms: lawman, police officer|noun
