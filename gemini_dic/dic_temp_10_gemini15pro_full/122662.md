modular:/ˈmɒdjʊlə(r) (UK), ˈmɑːdʒələr (US)| adj.|模块化的; 由组件构成的|Consisting of separate modules or units that can be combined or arranged in different ways.|The company uses a modular design for its software, allowing for easy customization.|近义词:component-based, prefabricated, sectional|反义词:integrated, monolithic|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From Late Latin *modularis, from Latin modulus (“small measure, standard”), diminutive of modus (“measure, manner”).
