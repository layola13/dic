elaborates:/ɪˈlæbəreɪts/ | v. | 阐述；详细说明；精心制作 |  develops or presents (a theory, policy, or system) in detail. | The author elaborates on the theme of social injustice in his latest novel.  作者在他的最新小说中详细阐述了社会不公正的主题。 | synonyms: expands, expounds, develops, amplifies | antonyms: summarizes, condenses, abbreviates | verb (third-person singular simple present elaborates, present participle elaborating, simple past and past participle elaborated)

elaborate:/ɪˈlæbərət/ | adj. | 复杂的；详尽的；精心制作的 | involving many carefully arranged parts or details; detailed and complicated in design and planning. |  She had prepared an elaborate meal for her guests. 她为客人准备了一顿丰盛的晚餐。 | synonyms: complex, detailed, intricate, complicated | antonyms: simple, basic, rudimentary | adjective
