impalpability:/ɪmˌpælpəˈbɪləti/
n.
难以捉摸；无形；不可触知
The quality of being difficult or impossible to feel or understand.
The impalpability of the mist made it seem almost supernatural.  这雾的飘渺无形使它看起来几乎是超自然的。
intangibility, insubstantiality, immateriality
palpability, tangibility, concreteness
noun
