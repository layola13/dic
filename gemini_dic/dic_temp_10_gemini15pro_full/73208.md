forceful:/ˈfɔːrsfʊl/ (US) /ˈfɔːsfəl/ (UK)| adj.|强有力的，有说服力的，强烈的|Having or showing great energy and determination; effective and persuasive; expressing opinions firmly and clearly.|例句：She made a forceful argument for change. 她提出了强有力的改革论点。|近义词：powerful, compelling, assertive, vigorous, dynamic|反义词：weak, ineffective, hesitant, timid|词性:形容词

词源: From force + -ful.  "Force" comes from Old French "force" (strength, power, violence), ultimately from Latin "fortis" (strong, brave).
