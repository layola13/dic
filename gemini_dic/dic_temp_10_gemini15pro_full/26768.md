caesar salad: /ˈsiːzər ˈsæləd/ | n. |凯撒沙拉| A salad of romaine lettuce and croutons dressed with parmesan cheese, lemon juice, olive oil, egg, Worcestershire sauce, and black pepper, often with grilled chicken or anchovies added. | I ordered a Caesar salad for lunch. 我午饭点了一份凯撒沙拉。| 近义词: green salad, tossed salad | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps dishes that are not salads, like steak, soup) | noun
