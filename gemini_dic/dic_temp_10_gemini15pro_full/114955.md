manatee:/ˈmænətiː/ | n. |海牛|A large aquatic mammal with a rounded tail fin, found in coastal waters and rivers of tropical and subtropical regions.  |例句：The manatee grazed peacefully on the seagrass. 海牛平静地吃着海草。|例句：We saw a mother manatee with her calf in the river. 我们在河里看到一只海牛妈妈和她的幼崽。| 近义词：sea cow | 反义词: None | Noun
