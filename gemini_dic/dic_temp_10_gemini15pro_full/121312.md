misformations:/ˌmɪsfɔːrˈmeɪʃənz/ | n. | 畸形，变形 |  A malformation or defect in shape or structure.  It refers to a structural anomaly that arises during development. | The baby was born with several misformations.  这个婴儿出生时有几种畸形。

The drug caused misformations in the developing fetuses. 这种药物导致发育中的胎儿畸形。| 近义词：deformities, malformations, abnormalities | 反义词: normalities, perfections | 词性: noun
