hyperventilate:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɛntɪleɪt/ | v. |过度换气，呼吸急促|To breathe at an abnormally rapid rate, so increasing the rate of loss of carbon dioxide. |例句：She began to hyperventilate when she heard the bad news. 她听到坏消息后开始呼吸急促。

例句：The stress of the situation caused him to hyperventilate.  这种紧张的局势让他呼吸急促。|近义词：overbreathe, pant |反义词: hypoventilate|词性: 动词
