ill-founded:/ˌɪlˈfaʊndɪd/
adjective
没有根据的；站不住脚的
based on wrong ideas or information
例句：Their accusations were completely ill-founded. 他们的指控完全没有根据。

例句：The rumors of his resignation proved to be ill-founded.  他辞职的谣言证明是毫无根据的。

近义词：baseless, groundless, unfounded, unjustified, spurious
反义词：well-founded, justified, valid, sound, legitimate
词性: adjective
