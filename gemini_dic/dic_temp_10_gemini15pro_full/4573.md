alcoholate: /ælˈkɒk.sə.leɪt/ /ælˈkɑː.lə.teɪt/
noun
醇盐; 烷氧基金属
An organic compound formed from alcohol by replacing the hydroxyl hydrogen with a metal.
Sodium alcoholate is a strong base used in organic synthesis.  醇钠是一种用于有机合成的强碱。
The reaction produced a potassium alcoholate.  该反应生成了醇钾。
