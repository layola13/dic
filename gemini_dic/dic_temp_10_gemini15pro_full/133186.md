noneviction:/ˌnɒnɪˈvɪkʃən/
n.
非驱逐，不驱逐
The absence of eviction; a situation where someone is not evicted.
The tenants celebrated their noneviction after a long legal battle.  租户们在漫长的法律斗争后庆祝他们免于被驱逐。
Near synonyms: non-expulsion, retention of tenancy
Antonyms: eviction, expulsion
