blagging:/ˈblæɡɪŋ/| n. v.|欺骗；花言巧语骗取；胡扯；|The act of persuading someone to give you something you want, or to allow you to do something you want, by using clever talk.|He managed to blag a free ticket to the concert. 他设法骗到了一张免费的音乐会门票。|近义词：wheedling, cajoling, deceiving,  conning|反义词: honesty, truthfulness|词性: noun, verb

blagging:/ˈblæɡɪŋ/| n. v.|胡扯；瞎编；|Talking about something as if you know a lot about it, when in reality you do not.|I was just blagging my way through the interview. 我只是在面试中胡扯。|近义词：bluffing, faking, improvising|反义词: being truthful, being honest|词性: noun, verb
