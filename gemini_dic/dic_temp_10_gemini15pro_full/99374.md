interdetermine:/ˌɪntərdiˈtɜːrmɪn/ | v. |互相决定，相互制约|To mutually determine or condition; to be reciprocally determining or conditioning.  The two factors interdetermine each other. 这两个因素相互决定。 |例句：The economic and political systems interdetermine each other. 经济和政治制度相互制约。

例句：Their choices interdetermined the outcome of the project. 他们的选择共同决定了项目的结果。 |近义词：interact, influence, affect, shape, determine, condition |反义词:  independent |词性: 动词
