compellable:/kəmˈpɛləbl/| adj.|可强制的，可强迫的|Able to be compelled or forced to do something, especially by law.|The witness was compellable to testify in court.  证人必须出庭作证。|Synonyms:obligatory, mandatory, required|Antonyms:optional, voluntary|adjective

compellable:/kəmˈpɛləbl/| adj.|可强制的，可强迫的|Able to be compelled or forced to do something, especially by law.|He is legally compellable to attend the hearing. 他在法律上有义务出席听证会。|Synonyms:coercible, enforceable|Antonyms: discretionary, elective|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English compellable, from Anglo-Norman compellable, from Latin compellere (to compel).
