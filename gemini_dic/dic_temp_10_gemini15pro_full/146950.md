overinvested:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɛstɪd/| adj.|过度投资的；过分投入的；过分沉迷的|Having invested too much (time, energy, or money) in something, often to the point of detriment or obsession.|例句：He was overinvested in the relationship and devastated when it ended. 他对这段感情投入过多，当它结束时他悲痛欲绝。

例句：She's become overinvested in her work, neglecting her family and friends. 她变得过分投入工作，忽略了家人和朋友。|近义词：obsessed, preoccupied, engrossed, fixated|反义词：uninvested, detached, apathetic|词性:形容词
