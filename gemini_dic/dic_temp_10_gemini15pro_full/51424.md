detersively:/dɪˈtɜːrsɪvli/
adv.|清洁地，去污地|In a manner that cleanses or purifies.|She wiped the counter detersively, removing every trace of the spill.| 近义词: cleansingly, purifyingly|反义词: dirtily, filthily|词性:副词

Etymology:
From detersive + -ly.  Detersive comes from Late Latin *dētersīvus, from Latin dētersus, past participle of dētergēre "to wipe off," from dē- "off" + tergēre "to wipe."
