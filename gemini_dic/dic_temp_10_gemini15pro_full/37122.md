combustions:/kəmˈbʌstʃənz/
|n.|燃烧，燃烧作用|the process of burning something.|The rapid combustions created intense heat.  快速燃烧产生了强烈的热量。|Synonyms: burning, ignition, incineration|Antonyms: extinguishing, quenching|Noun

combustion:/kəmˈbʌstʃən/
|n.|燃烧，燃烧作用|the process of burning something.|The combustion of fossil fuels releases harmful gases. 燃烧化石燃料会释放有害气体。|Synonyms: burning, ignition, incineration|Antonyms: extinguishing, quenching|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *combustion-, *combustio, from Latin combustus, past participle of comburere "to burn up," from com- "completely" + burere "to burn."
