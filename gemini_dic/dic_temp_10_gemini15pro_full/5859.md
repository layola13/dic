amazonite:/ˈæməzəˌnaɪt/| n. |天河石|A green or blue-green variety of microcline feldspar, a mineral often used as a gemstone.  It is named after the Amazon River, although it is not found there.|She wore a necklace of polished amazonite beads. 她戴着一条抛光的天河石珠项链。|

amazonite:/ˈæməzəˌnaɪt/| n. |天河石|A light bluish-green, opaque gemstone.  It's a variety of potassium feldspar called microcline.|The artisan crafted a beautiful ring with an inlaid amazonite stone.  这位工匠制作了一枚镶嵌天河石的漂亮戒指。|

amazonite:/ˈæməzəˌnaɪt/| n. |天河石|A mineral of the feldspar group, typically light green or blue-green in color.|Amazonite is believed by some to have calming and soothing properties. 有些人认为天河石具有镇静和舒缓的特性。|
