against the grain:/əˈɡenst ðə ɡreɪn/
|idiomatic phrase|违背本性；格格不入；别扭|Going against the grain means doing something that is difficult or unpleasant because it contradicts one's natural inclinations or beliefs.|Asking for help goes against the grain for him, he's always been so independent.  请求帮助违背了他的本性，他一直都很独立。| 近义词: against one's nature, contrary to one's inclination, unwillingly| 反义词: naturally, willingly, easily|phrase


against his grain: /əˈɡenst hɪz ɡreɪn/
|idiomatic phrase|违背他的本性；与他不合|Doing something against his grain means it contradicts his natural inclinations or beliefs, making it difficult or unpleasant for him.|Working on a team project goes against his grain; he prefers to work solo.  参与团队项目与他的本性不符；他更喜欢单独工作。| 近义词: against his nature, contrary to his inclination, unwillingly| 反义词: naturally, willingly, easily|phrase
