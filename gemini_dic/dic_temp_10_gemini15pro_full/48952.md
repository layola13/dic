degarnishment:/diːˈɡɑːrnɪʃmənt/
n.
解除扣押；取消扣押令
The legal process of releasing garnished funds or property.
例句：The degarnishment order was issued after the debt was paid in full.  债务全部付清后，解除了扣押令。

例句：He requested a degarnishment of his wages once the court case was settled.  案件解决后，他要求解除对其工资的扣押。
近义词：release, discharge
反义词：garnishment, attachment
词性: noun
