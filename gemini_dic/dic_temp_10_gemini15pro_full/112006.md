lonesome: /ˈloʊnsəm/ (US) /ˈləʊnsəm/ (UK)| adj.|孤独的，寂寞的|Experiencing or causing a feeling of sadness because one is alone; solitary.|例句：She felt lonesome after her children left home. 她孩子们离开家后，她感到很孤单。

例句：The lonesome howl of a wolf echoed through the valley. 一匹狼孤独的嚎叫声在山谷中回荡。

近义词：lonely, solitary, isolated, alone|反义词：accompanied, sociable, gregarious|词性：形容词
