diagrammatic:/ˌdaɪəɡrəˈmætɪk/
adjective
图解的；图表的；示意图的
Of the nature of a diagram; shown or represented by a diagram.
The teacher used a diagrammatic representation of the solar system. 老师用图表展示了太阳系。
The instructions were diagrammatic, making them easy to follow. 说明书是图解式的，很容易理解。
近义词:graphic, schematic, visual
反义词:textual, descriptive, written
词性:adjective
