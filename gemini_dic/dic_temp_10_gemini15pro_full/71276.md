fixatifs:/fɪksəˈtiːvz/
noun
固定剂；定影剂
Substances used to preserve or stabilize biological specimens or artistic works.
The scientist used fixatifs to preserve the tissue samples. 科学家使用固定剂来保存组织样本。
The artist sprayed fixatifs on the charcoal drawing to prevent smudging.  艺术家在木炭画上喷洒定影剂以防止弄脏。
synonyms: preservatives, stabilizers
antonyms: solvents, destabilizers
n.
