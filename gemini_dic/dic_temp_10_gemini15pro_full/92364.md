hypochondriac:/ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndriæk/ (US) /ˌhaɪpəˈkɑːndriæk/ (UK)| n. adj.|疑病症患者，疑病症的|A person who is abnormally anxious about their health.  Someone who habitually believes they are ill or about to become ill, despite medical reassurance to the contrary.|例句：He's a terrible hypochondriac; every time he gets a headache he's convinced he has a brain tumour. 他是一个严重的疑病症患者；每次头痛，他都确信自己得了脑瘤。

例句：She's such a hypochondriac that she goes to the doctor for every little ache and pain. 她疑心病很重，每次有点小病小痛都去看医生。|近义词：valetudinarian, malade imaginaire|反义词: None (No direct antonym exists for a person with a condition)|词性：noun, adjective

hypochondriacal:/ˌhaɪpəʊkɒnˈdraɪəkl/ (UK) /ˌhaɪpəkɑːnˈdraɪəkl/ (US) |adj.|疑病的，患疑病症的|Excessively worried about one's health; exhibiting symptoms of hypochondria.|例句：He was always hypochondriacal, worrying about every little sniffle. 他总是疑神疑鬼的，担心每一个小喷嚏。

例句：Her hypochondriacal tendencies led her to constantly research rare diseases. 她疑病的倾向导致她不断地研究罕见疾病。|近义词：valetudinarian, neurotic|反义词:  healthy, well|词性：adjective
