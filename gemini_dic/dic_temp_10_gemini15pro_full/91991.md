hypernormalness:/ˌhaɪpərˈnɔːrməlnəs/ | n. | 超常态 | A condition of heightened normality, where something appears superficially normal, but underneath, signifies a profound abnormality or distortion of reality.  It describes a situation where people are aware that something is wrong, but continue to act as if everything is fine, creating a sense of artificial stability. |  The citizens lived in a state of hypernormalness, pretending everything was fine despite the looming threat of war. 市民们生活在一种超常态中，尽管战争的威胁迫在眉睫，却假装一切都很好。

The extravagant displays of patriotism felt like hypernormalness, masking deep social divisions. 铺天盖地的爱国主义表现感觉像是一种超常态，掩盖了深刻的社会分裂。 | 近义词: artificiality, facade, pretense | 反义词: authenticity, genuineness, reality | 词性: noun
