asymmetrical: /ˌeɪsɪˈmetrɪkəl/ /ˌæsɪˈmetrɪkəl/
adjective
不对称的，不匀称的
Having parts that fail to correspond to one another in shape, size, or arrangement; lacking symmetry.
例句：The building has an asymmetrical design. 这栋建筑的设计是不对称的。

例句：Her face had a slightly asymmetrical smile. 她的脸上带着一丝不对称的微笑。

近义词：unsymmetrical, lopsided, uneven, irregular
反义词：symmetrical, balanced, even, regular
词性：adjective
