overshipment:/ˌoʊvərˈʃɪpmənt/| n. |过度装运，超额装运|The act of shipping more goods than ordered or required.  It can lead to storage problems and increased costs.|Due to an overshipment, we now have excess inventory in our warehouse. 由于过度装运，我们现在仓库里有多余的库存。|The overshipment of goods caused a logistical nightmare. 货物过度装运造成了物流噩梦。|近义词：excess shipment, surplus shipment|反义词:undershipment, shortage|noun
