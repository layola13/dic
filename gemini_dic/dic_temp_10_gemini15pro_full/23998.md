breathing:/ˈbriːðɪŋ/ | n. |呼吸；气息；短暂的休息|The act of inhaling and exhaling air. |She took a deep breathing to calm herself. 她深呼吸让自己平静下来。| synonyms: respiration, inhalation, exhalation | antonyms: suffocation, asphyxiation | noun

breathing:/ˈbriːðɪŋ/ | adj. |呼吸的；活的；栩栩如生的|Related to breathing or giving the appearance of life.|The painting captured the breathing likeness of the subject. 这幅画捕捉到了对象的栩栩如生的形象。|synonyms: living, alive, animate | antonyms: lifeless, dead, inanimate | adjective
