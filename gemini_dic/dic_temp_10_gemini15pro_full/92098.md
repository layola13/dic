hyperresponsively:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈspɑːnsɪvli/
adverb
过度反应地，反应过强地
Responding to stimuli in an exaggerated or excessive way.
The child reacted hyperresponsively to the loud noise, bursting into tears.  这个孩子对巨大的噪音反应过度，突然哭了起来。
The immune system sometimes acts hyperresponsively, leading to allergies. 免疫系统有时会反应过度，导致过敏。
近义词:overly sensitively, excessively reactively
反义词:unresponsively, apathetically
词性:adverb

词源:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, above, beyond"
responsive (adjective): from Latin *respondere, "to respond"
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
