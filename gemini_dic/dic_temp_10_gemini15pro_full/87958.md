high-explosive:/haɪ ɪkˈspləʊsɪv/
adjective
高爆炸药的；烈性的
highly explosive; designed to detonate with great force
The army used high-explosive shells. 军队使用了高爆炮弹。
Synonyms: powerful, potent, strong, intense
Antonyms: low-explosive, mild, weak
词性: 形容词

high-explosive:/haɪ ɪkˈspləʊsɪv/
noun
烈性炸药
a powerful explosive
High-explosives are dangerous to handle.  烈性炸药处理起来很危险。
Synonyms: dynamite, TNT, RDX, C4
Antonyms: low-explosive, mild explosive
词性: 名词
