deviant:/ˈdiːviənt/ (US), /ˈdiːvjənt/ (UK)| adj. n.|异常的，反常的；不正常的人（或事物）|Departing from usual or accepted standards, especially in social or sexual behavior.  A person who behaves in a way that most people consider to be not normal or morally correct.|例句：His deviant behavior worried his parents. 他的反常行为使他的父母担忧。|近义词：abnormal, atypical, perverse, unconventional, nonconformist|反义词：normal, conventional, typical, standard, conformist|词性:形容词, 名词
deviant:/ˈdiːviənt/ (US), /ˈdiːvjənt/ (UK)| n.|不正常的人（或事物）|A person whose behavior differs from the accepted standards of society.|例句：The deviants were ostracized by the community. 这些不正常的人被社区排斥了。|近义词：misfit, outcast, eccentric|反义词：conformist|词性:名词
