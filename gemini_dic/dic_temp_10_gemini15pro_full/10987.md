arbor:/ˈɑːrbər/ (US), /ˈɑːbə/ (UK)| n.|凉亭，乔木，藤架|A shady garden alcove with sides and a roof formed by trees or climbing plants trained over a framework.|We sat in the arbor, enjoying the shade. 我们坐在凉亭里，享受着阴凉。|The arbor was covered in fragrant honeysuckle. 这个凉亭上覆盖着芬芳的金银花。|synonyms: bower, pergola, gazebo|antonyms: N/A|noun
