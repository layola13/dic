brigades:/brɪˈɡeɪdz/| n.|旅，大队，队|A group of people organized for a particular purpose.|例句：Fire brigades battled the blaze for hours. 消防队与大火搏斗了几个小时。

例句：Volunteer brigades helped with the cleanup after the hurricane. 志愿队伍帮助飓风过后进行清理。|近义词：teams, groups, units, squads, corps|反义词:individual|Noun
