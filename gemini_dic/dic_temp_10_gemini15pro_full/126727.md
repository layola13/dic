namo:/ˈnɑːmoʊ/ | interj. | 南無，皈依，敬礼 |  A Sanskrit word expressing homage or reverence, often used in Buddhist chants and mantras. It signifies taking refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha. |  Namo Amitabha Buddha. (南無阿彌陀佛。) | Synonyms: homage, reverence, adoration | Antonyms: disrespect, irreverence | interjection
