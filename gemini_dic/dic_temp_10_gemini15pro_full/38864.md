confederation's:/kənˌfedəˈreɪʃənz/
n.
联盟的，联邦的
belonging to or associated with a confederation
例句：The confederation's army was small but determined.  联盟的军队虽小但意志坚定。

例句：The confederation's laws apply to all member states. 联盟的法律适用于所有成员国。


近义词：league's, alliance's, union's
反义词：None (Possessive forms don't typically have antonyms)
词性：Possessive noun (名词所有格)


confederation:/kənˌfedəˈreɪʃən/
n.
联盟，联邦
a league or alliance, especially of states
例句：The thirteen colonies formed a confederation. 十三个殖民地组成了一个联邦。

例句：The confederation was formed for mutual defense. 该联盟是为了共同防御而成立的。

近义词：league, alliance, union
反义词：division, separation
词性：Noun (名词)
