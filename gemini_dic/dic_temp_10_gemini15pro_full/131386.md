noncitable:/ˌnɒnˈsaɪtəbl/ | adj. | 不可引用的 | Not suitable or allowed to be cited or quoted as evidence or support.  Unable to be referenced. |  The preliminary report is noncitable and should not be distributed.  初步报告不可引用，不应分发。 | The information from the anonymous source was noncitable.  来自匿名消息来源的信息不可引用。|  uncitable, unquotable | citable, quotable | adjective
