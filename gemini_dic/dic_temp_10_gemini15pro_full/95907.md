increment:/ˈɪŋkrəmənt/ (US), /ˈɪŋkrɪmənt/ (UK)| n. v.|增加，增量；增值|An increase in a number, size, quantity, etc.|
The increment in her salary was substantial. 她的薪水增幅很大。
She incremented the variable by one. 她把变量增加了1。
|近义词：increase, rise, gain, addition|反义词：decrement, decrease, reduction, loss|词性: 名词, 动词
