batlet:/ˈbætlɪt/| n.| 棒槌，洗衣板 |A small bat for beating clothes while washing them, especially a paddle-shaped implement with a ribbed surface.| She used a batlet and washboard to scrub the clothes clean. 她用棒槌和洗衣板把衣服洗干净了。|近义词：paddle, beater|反义词: None|词性: noun
Etymology: From Old French *batellet, diminutive of batel "club, bat," from Late Latin *battellus, from battuere "to beat."
