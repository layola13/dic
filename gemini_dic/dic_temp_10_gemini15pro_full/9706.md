antitraditionalist:/ˌæntitrəˈdɪʃənəlɪst/ | n. adj.|反传统主义者，反传统主义的|Someone who opposes or rejects traditional beliefs, customs, or practices.  A person who challenges established norms and advocates for change.|例句：The antitraditionalist artist challenged the conventions of classical painting.  这位反传统主义艺术家挑战了古典绘画的传统。

例句：  He's quite an antitraditionalist when it comes to family values.  在家庭价值观方面，他是一个相当反传统的人。|近义词：iconoclast, rebel, nonconformist, radical, revolutionary|反义词：traditionalist, conservative, conformist|词性: 名词, 形容词
