passager:/ˈpæsɪndʒər/ (US), /ˈpæsɪndʒə/ (UK)| n. |乘客，旅客|A person who is travelling in a vehicle, aircraft, or vessel, but is not controlling it.|例句：The passengers boarded the plane. 乘客登上了飞机。|近义词：traveler, commuter, rider|反义词：driver, pilot, captain|词性:名词

passager:/ˈpæsɪndʒər/ (US), /ˈpæsɪndʒə/ (UK)| adj. |客运的|Carrying passengers.|例句：a passenger train 客运列车|近义词：transport, travel|反义词：freight, cargo|词性:形容词
