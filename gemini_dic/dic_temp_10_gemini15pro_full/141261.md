opalescent:/ˌoʊpəˈlɛsənt/ | adj. |乳白色的，蛋白石色的，闪光的 |Exhibiting a play of colors like that of an opal; iridescent. | The vase had an opalescent glaze.  花瓶上有一层乳白色的釉。| synonyms: iridescent, pearly, shimmering, nacreous | antonyms: dull, matte | adjective


opalescence:/ˌoʊpəˈlɛsəns/ | n. |乳白光，蛋白光 |The state or quality of being opalescent. | The opalescence of the moonstone was captivating. 月光石的乳白色光彩夺目。| synonyms: iridescence, pearliness, shimmer | antonyms: dullness, flatness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *opalescent*, from *opale* (opal) + *-escent* (a suffix forming adjectives meaning "beginning to be" or "slightly"). Ultimately from Sanskrit *upala* (precious stone, opal).
