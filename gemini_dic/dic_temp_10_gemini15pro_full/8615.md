anticoherer:/ˌæntiˈkoʊˈhɪərər/ | n. | 反相干器 | A device used in early radio receivers to detect radio waves. It consisted of a tube filled with loosely packed metal filings, whose electrical resistance decreased when a radio signal was received.  | The anticoherer was a crucial component in early wireless telegraphy. 反相干器在早期无线电报中是一个关键组成部分。 |  The anticoherer was superseded by more sensitive detectors. 反相干器被更灵敏的探测器所取代。| synonyms: detector, receiver | antonyms: transmitter | noun
