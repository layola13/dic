deliciousness:/dɪˈlɪʃəsnəs/
n.
美味;可口
The state or quality of being delicious.
The deliciousness of the chocolate cake was undeniable.  巧克力蛋糕的美味不容否认。
近义词:tastiness, savoriness, flavor, yumminess
反义词:unpleasantness, distastefulness, insipidity
词性:名词

deliciousness:/dɪˈlɪʃəsnəs/
n.
美味佳肴
Something delicious; a delicacy. (Often used in the plural.)
The chef prepared a table full of deliciousnesses.  厨师准备了一桌美味佳肴。
近义词:treats, delicacies, goodies
反义词:unappetizing food
词性:名词


词源: From delicious + -ness.  来自 delicious (美味的) + -ness (名词后缀，表示状态或性质)。
