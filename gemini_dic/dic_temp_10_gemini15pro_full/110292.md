lige:/liːʒ/
pron.|liege|臣下；（用于旧时君主或高级贵族）朕；寡人|A liege is a vassal or subject of a lord.|My liege, I await your command. 陛下，我等候您的吩咐。|Synonym: subject, vassal|Antonym: lord, sovereign|Pronoun

lige:/liːʒ/
adj.|liege|（封建时代用语）受某君主统治的；对某君主效忠的|Liege describes something or someone under the rule of or owing allegiance to a lord or monarch.|The liege man swore fealty to his lord.  这位臣下向他的君主宣誓效忠。|Synonym: subject, vassal|Antonym: independent, sovereign|Adjective
