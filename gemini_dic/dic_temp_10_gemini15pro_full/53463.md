disarm:/dɪsˈɑːrm/
verb
解除武装; 消除(疑虑、敌意等)
to take weapons away from someone, or to make someone feel less angry or suspicious
例句：The police disarmed the gunman. 警察解除了持枪歹徒的武装。
例句：Her smile disarmed him completely. 她的微笑彻底消除了他的敌意。
近义词：demilitarize, neutralize, appease, conciliate
反义词：arm, provoke, antagonize
词性:v.


disarm:/dɪsˈɑːrm/
noun
解除武装
the act of taking away weapons or making someone less hostile
例句：The treaty called for the complete disarmament of both countries.  该条约要求两国彻底解除武装。
例句：Her disarmament strategy involved active listening and empathy. 她的消除敌意策略包括积极倾听和表示同理心。
近义词：demilitarization, neutralization
反义词：armament
词性:n.
