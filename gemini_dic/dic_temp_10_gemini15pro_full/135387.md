nonperjuries:/ˌnɒnpɜːrdʒəriːz/
n.
没有伪证;真实陈述
The absence of perjury; truthful statements.
The judge commended the witness for his nonperjuries. 法官称赞证人没有作伪证。
近义词:truths, honesties
反义词:perjuries, lies
词性:名词(plural)


Etymology:
From non- + perjury.  "Perjury" comes from Old French *parjurer, from Latin perjurare "to swear falsely," from per- "wrongly" + jurare "to swear."
