cannulated:/ˈkæn.jə.leɪ.tɪd/
adjective
插管的，有插管的
Fitted with a cannula.
例句：The cannulated vein allowed for continuous administration of fluids.  插管静脉允许持续输液。

例句：The surgeon inserted a cannulated screw into the fractured bone.  外科医生将带孔螺钉插入骨折的骨骼中。

近义词：intubated, catheterized
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "uncannulated" if necessary, though less common)
词性: adjective


词源: From the noun "cannula" + "-ated" (suffix forming adjectives meaning "provided with").  "Cannula" ultimately comes from the Latin word "canna" meaning "reed" or "tube."
