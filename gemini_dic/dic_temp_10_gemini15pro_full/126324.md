myopias:/maɪˈoʊpiəz/
n.
近视
nearsightedness; a condition of the eye in which parallel rays are focused in front of the retina, so that distant objects appear blurred
Her myopias worsened as she got older. 她的近视随着年龄增长而加重。
近义词:nearsightedness, shortsightedness
反义词:hyperopia, farsightedness
词性:名词
