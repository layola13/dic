bassier:/ˈbeɪsiər/ (US), /ˈbeɪsɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更低沉的，更低音的|Comparative form of "bassy": having a low, deep, resonant sound, like a bass instrument or voice.|The new speakers made the music sound much bassier.|Near synonyms: deeper, lower, more resonant|Near antonyms: higher, shriller, tinnier|adjective
