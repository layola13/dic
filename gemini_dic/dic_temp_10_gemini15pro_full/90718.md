humidities:/hjuːˈmɪdɪtiz/
noun
湿度(复数)
The amount of water vapor in the air.  It refers to multiple measurements or instances of humidity levels.
The humidities in different parts of the greenhouse vary significantly.  温室不同部分的湿度差异很大。
Near synonyms: moisture levels, dampness levels
Antonym: aridity
n.
