midwifed:/ˈmɪdˌwaɪft/ | v. | 接生；促成；协助…的产生 | acted as a midwife at the birth of; assisted in the birth or development of something |  She midwifed the birth of her first grandchild. 她为她的第一个孙子接生。| synonyms: assisted, facilitated, aided, supported | antonyms: hindered, obstructed, impeded | verb

midwifed:/ˈmɪdˌwaɪft/ | adj. |  |  接生过的，被接生出来的 (This usage is less common and may feel archaic or poetic.) |  A midwifed baby. 一个被接生出来的婴儿. | synonyms: delivered, birthed (though these are more commonly used for the baby) | antonyms:  (No direct antonym exists for this adjectival use) | adjective
