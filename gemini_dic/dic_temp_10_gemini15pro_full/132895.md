nonelectrically:/ˌnɒnɪˈlɛktrɪkli/
adjective
非电的；不使用电的
Not involving or requiring electricity; not operated by electricity.
The pump operates nonelectrically, using a hand crank.  这个泵是非电动的，使用手摇曲柄。
The gate is opened nonelectrically, using a system of weights and pulleys.  这扇门是非电动的，使用重物和滑轮系统打开。
Synonyms: manually, mechanically
Antonyms: electrically, electronically
词性: 形容词
