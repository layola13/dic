nonvintages:/ˌnɒnˈvɪntɪdʒɪz/ |n.|非年份酒，非陈年酒| Wines that are not of a particular vintage year, typically blended from multiple harvests.  These wines are not meant for aging and are intended to be consumed young. | Example: The supermarket shelves were stocked with a variety of nonvintages. 超市货架上摆满了各种非年份酒。| Example:  Nonvintages are generally more affordable than vintage wines. 非年份酒通常比年份酒更实惠。|Synonyms: non-vintage wines, current releases| Antonyms: vintage wines, aged wines| noun
