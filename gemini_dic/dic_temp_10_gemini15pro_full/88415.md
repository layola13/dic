his:/hɪz/| possessive pronoun|他的|Used to show that something belongs to or is connected with a male person or animal previously mentioned or easily identified.|例句：That's his car. 那是他的车。|近义词：him (objective pronoun), hers (possessive pronoun of a female)|反义词: her, hers (feminine possessive pronouns), their, theirs (plural possessive pronouns)|pronoun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *his (genitive of *hē "he"), from Proto-Germanic *hizas, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱis, genitive of *ḱe "this." Cognate with Dutch zijn, German sein.
