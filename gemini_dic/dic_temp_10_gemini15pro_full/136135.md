nonreflector:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈflek.tər/ | n. | 非反射物，非反射体 | An object or surface that does not reflect light, sound, or other radiation. |  The walls of the anechoic chamber were coated with a special nonreflector material to absorb all sound waves. 消音室的墙壁涂有一种特殊的非反射材料，以吸收所有声波。| synonym: absorber | antonym: reflector | noun

nonreflective:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈflek.tɪv/ | adj. | 非反射的 | Not reflecting light, sound, or other radiation. | The dark, nonreflective surface of the stealth aircraft made it difficult to detect on radar. 隐形飞机的深色非反射表面使其难以被雷达探测到。| synonym: non-reflecting, matte, dull | antonym: reflective, glossy, shiny | adjective
