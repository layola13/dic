foiling:/ˈfɔɪlɪŋ/
n.|挫败，阻止，金属箔|The act of preventing something from being successful; thin sheets of metal.|The police were successful in their foiling of the robbery attempt. 警方成功阻止了抢劫企图。|近义词：thwarting, frustrating, obstructing|反义词:aiding, assisting, facilitating|词性:名词

foiling:/ˈfɔɪlɪŋ/
v.|用箔覆盖，衬托，挫败|Covering with foil; enhancing by contrast; preventing from succeeding.|She foiled the potatoes before baking them. 她烘烤土豆前先用箔纸包好。|近义词：wrapping, covering, thwarting|反义词:exposing, revealing, assisting|词性:动词(现在分词)
