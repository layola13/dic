ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diagnōsticus (whence French diagnostique), from Greek διαγνωστικός (diagnōstikós, “able to distinguish”), from διαγιγνώσκειν (diagignṓskein, “to distinguish”), from διά (diá, “through”) + γιγνώσκειν (gignṓskein, “to learn”).
