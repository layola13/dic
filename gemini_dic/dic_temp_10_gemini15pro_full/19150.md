bevy:/ˈbevi/
n.
一群；一组
a large group of people or things of a particular kind
例句：A bevy of beauties surrounded the rock star. 一群美女围着摇滚明星。

例句：A bevy of swans floated gracefully on the lake. 一群天鹅优雅地在湖面上游动。

近义词：group, collection, flock, company, gathering, assembly
反义词: few, handful, scattering

词性: 名词
