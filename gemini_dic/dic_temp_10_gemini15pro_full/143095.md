outbreathing:/ˌaʊtˈbriːðɪŋ/
n.
呼气;呼出;吐气
The act of exhaling air from the lungs.
She focused on her outbreathing, letting go of tension with each breath. 她专注于自己的呼气，每一次呼吸都释放着紧张。
Deep, slow outbreathing can calm the nervous system. 深而缓慢的呼气可以镇静神经系统。
exhalation, expiration
inhalation, inspiration
noun
