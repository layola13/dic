lead-off: /ˈliːdˌɔːf/ (US), /ˈliːdˌɒf/ (UK)| adj. n.| 首发的，领头的；开场的；首发击球员；首发项目 |The lead-off hitter got a single. 首发击球员击出一垒安打。|first, opening, beginning, initial, starting|last, ending, concluding|adjective, noun
Etymology->
From the verb phrase "lead off," meaning to begin or start something.  "Lead" in this sense comes from the Old English "lædan" (to guide, conduct). "Off" indicates the beginning of an action or process.
