mismetre:/mɪsˈmiːtər/ | n. | 格律错误；韵律错误；不合拍 | A line of verse having a faulty or irregular meter. |  The poem was marred by a glaring mismetre in the second stanza. 这首诗的第二节中有一个明显的格律错误。| synonyms: metrical fault, rhythmic flaw | antonyms: perfect meter, correct rhythm | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (prefix meaning 'wrong' or 'bad') + metre (meaning 'rhythm or meter in verse').  Ultimately from Greek 'metron' (measure).
