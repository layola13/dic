conjecture:/kənˈdʒɛktʃər/ | n. v. | 推测，猜想；推测，猜想 |  An opinion or conclusion formed on the basis of incomplete information.  |  He conjectured that the company would soon go bankrupt. 他推测这家公司很快就会破产。 | synonym: guess, surmise, speculate, hypothesize | antonym:  fact, certainty, proof | noun

conjecture:/kənˈdʒɛktʃər/ | v. | 推测，猜想 | Form an opinion or conclusion on the basis of incomplete information. |  I can only conjecture about his motives. 我只能推测他的动机。 | synonym: guess, surmise, speculate, hypothesize | antonym: know, prove, ascertain | verb
