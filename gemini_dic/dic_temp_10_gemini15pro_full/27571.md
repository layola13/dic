canalizes:/ˈkænəlaɪzɪz/
verb
引导；使固定化；形成管道；开凿运河
to cause something to follow a particular route or course; to become or cause something to become fixed and difficult to change.
The government is trying to canalize the flow of traffic away from the city center. 政府正试图将交通引导到远离市中心的地方。
His energies were canalized into writing. 他的精力都投入到写作中了。
channel, direct, guide
restrict, hinder
v.
