lichenographist: /laɪˈkɛnəˌɡræfɪst/ | n. | 地衣学家 | A person who studies lichens. | Example 1: The lichenographist discovered a new species of lichen in the rainforest.  这位地衣学家在雨林中发现了一种新的地衣。

Example 2:  The lichenographist carefully examined the lichen samples under the microscope.  这位地衣学家在显微镜下仔细检查了地衣样本。 | Synonym: lichenologist | Antonym: N/A | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From  *licheno-*  +  *-graphy* + *-ist*. *licheno-* from Greek *leikhēn* "lichen, tree-moss". *-graphy*  from Greek *-graphia* "writing, description". *-ist* a suffix forming agent nouns.  Therefore, a "lichenographist" is literally someone who writes about or describes lichens.
