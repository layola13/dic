clayier:/ˈkleɪɪər/
adjective
更黏土的，黏土含量更高的
More clayey; containing more clay.
The soil in the valley is clayier than the soil on the hillside. 山谷里的土壤比山坡上的土壤黏性更大。
This type of clay is clayier and more difficult to work with.  这种粘土更黏，更难加工。
近义词: heavier, denser, stickier
反义词: sandier, looser, less dense
词性: adjective

clayier:/ˈkleɪɪər/
词源:  From clay + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  The suffix "-ier" is used to form the comparative degree of adjectives, indicating a greater degree of the quality expressed by the adjective.  "Clay" comes from Old English "clæg," related to Old Norse "leir" and Dutch "klei."
词性: adjective
