hemathermal:/ˌhiːməˈθɜːrməl/ | adj. | 温血的; 等温的 |  Warm-blooded; having a relatively uniform body temperature maintained nearly independent of the surrounding environment.  This term is mainly used for animals that maintain a constant body temperature, regardless of the external temperature.  It's derived from Greek "haima" (blood) and "therme" (heat). | Bats are hemathermal animals.  蝙蝠是恒温动物。| Homeothermic, endothermic | Poikilothermic, ectothermic | adjective
