chasse:/ʃæs/ /ʃɑːseɪ/ | n. v. |追逐，追赶；滑步， chasse舞步 |A gliding step in dancing, especially in ballet, in which one foot chases the other.| The dancer performed a graceful chasse across the stage. 舞者在舞台上表演了一个优雅的滑步。|近义词：glide, slide, pursuit, chase |反义词: retreat, withdraw |词性:名词,动词
Etymology: From French *chasser* "to chase," from Late Latin *captiare* "to try to seize," frequentative of Latin *captare* "to try to seize," from *capere* "to take, seize."
