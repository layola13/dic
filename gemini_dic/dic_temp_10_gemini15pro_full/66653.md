exitial:/ˈɛksɪtʃəl/| adj.|致命的，毁灭性的|Destructive; fatal; causing death or ruin.|The exitial effects of the war left the country in ruins. 战争的毁灭性影响使这个国家满目疮痍。|近义词：fatal, lethal, deadly, pernicious, ruinous|反义词：beneficial, salutary, advantageous|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From Latin *exitialis*, from *exitium* ("destruction, ruin"), from *exire* ("to go out, perish").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₁eǵ- (“out, away”).
