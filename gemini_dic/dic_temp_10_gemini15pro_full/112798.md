lucans:/ˈluːkənz/| n. |萤火虫；Lucanus属甲虫（包括锹甲）|A genus of beetles, commonly known as stag beetles, characterized by their large mandibles.|The lucans are fascinating insects, especially the males with their antler-like jaws. 萤火虫是迷人的昆虫，尤其是雄性，它们的下颚像鹿角一样。|近义词：stag beetles|反义词:N/A|词性:名词

词源: From Latin "Lucanus," perhaps referring to Lucania, a region in southern Italy.  源自拉丁语“Lucanus”，可能指的是意大利南部的卢卡尼亚地区。
