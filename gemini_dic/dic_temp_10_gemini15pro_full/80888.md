governesses:/ˈɡʌvərnɪsɪz/
noun
女家庭教师（复数）
A woman employed to teach and train children in a private household.
例句：The governesses were responsible for the children's education and upbringing.  女家庭教师负责孩子们的教育和抚养。

例句：Many wealthy families in the 19th century employed governesses. 19世纪许多富裕家庭都雇佣女家庭教师。
近义词：tutors, instructresses
反义词: student, pupil
词性: noun
