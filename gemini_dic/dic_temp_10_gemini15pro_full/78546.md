ghostlily:/ˈɡoʊstˌlɪli/| n. |鬼百合，幽灵兰|A type of orchid (Dendrophylax lindenii) known for its white flowers and leafless appearance, giving it a ghostly appearance. It is also known as the white frog orchid.|The ghostlily is a rare and endangered species. 鬼百合是一种稀有濒危物种。|The ghostlily, with its ethereal beauty, captivated the botanist.  鬼百合以其空灵之美吸引了这位植物学家。|近义词：white frog orchid, palm polly, |反义词: N/A|noun
