chondrodite:/ˈkɒndrəˌdaɪt/ | n. | 硅镁石 |A mineral, a nesosilicate of magnesium, iron, and fluorine, typically occurring as yellow to reddish-brown grains in metamorphic rocks. | Chondrodite is often found in association with other magnesium-rich minerals. 硅镁石经常与其他富含镁的矿物伴生。

Chondrodite can exhibit a variety of colors, from yellow to brown. 硅镁石可以呈现多种颜色，从黄色到棕色。 | 近义词:humite (another magnesium silicate mineral) | 反义词: N/A | 词性:noun
