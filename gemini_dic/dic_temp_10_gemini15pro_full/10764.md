aquaculturist:/ˈækwəkʌltʃərɪst/| n. |水产养殖者，水产养殖专家|A person who practices aquaculture, the farming of aquatic organisms such as fish, shellfish, and seaweed.|例句：The aquaculturist carefully monitored the water quality in the fish ponds.  水产养殖者仔细监测鱼塘的水质。

例句：Experienced aquaculturists understand the delicate balance of the aquatic ecosystem.  经验丰富的水产养殖者了解水生生态系统的微妙平衡。|近义词：fish farmer, mariculturist (specifically for marine organisms)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aqua* (water) + *cultura* (cultivation) + *-ist* (one who practices)
