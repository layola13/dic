dioicous:/daɪˈɔɪʃəs/ |adj.|雌雄异株的；雌雄异体的| Having male and female reproductive organs in separate individuals.|The dioicous nature of the plant makes pollination more challenging. 这种植物的雌雄异株特性使得授粉更具挑战性。|Synonyms: unisexual, gonochoric|Antonyms: monoecious, hermaphroditic|adjective

From Greek *di- “two” + *oikos “house”
