acquits:/əˈkwɪts/| v.|宣告无罪，释放，履行（职责等）|The jury acquitted the defendant of all charges. 陪审团宣告被告所有罪名不成立。|近义词：absolve, exonerate, clear, discharge|反义词: convict, condemn|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *acquiter* (Modern French *acquitter*), from Latin *adquietare* “to set at rest, pacify, free from a charge,” from *ad-* "to" + *quietare* "to quiet, pacify," from *quietus* "quiet, at rest."

USAGE->

The judge acquits the defendant due to lack of evidence. = 由于缺乏证据，法官宣告被告无罪。
The company always acquits itself honorably in its dealings. = 该公司在交易中总是表现得光明磊落。
