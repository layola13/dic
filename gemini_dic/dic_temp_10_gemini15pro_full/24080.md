brethren's:/ˈbreðrənz/| n.|兄弟们的，同胞们的|Possessive form of "brethren," an archaic plural of "brother."  It refers to a group of male siblings or, more commonly, a group of people sharing a common belief, cause, or membership (often religious).|例句：The brethren's hymn echoed through the hall.  兄弟们的赞美诗在整个大厅回荡。

例句：The brethren's annual meeting was held in the spring. 兄弟们的年会在春天举行。|近义词：brothers', colleagues', comrades'|反义词:enemies', opponents'|词性:名词(possessive)
