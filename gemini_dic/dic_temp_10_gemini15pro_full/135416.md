nonperpetuance:/ˌnɒnpərˈpɛtʃuəns/
n.
非永久性；非永恒性
The nonperpetuance of the contract means it will expire.  合同的非永久性意味着它会到期。
The nonperpetuance of this position makes it less appealing to some candidates. 这个职位的非永久性使得它对一些候选人来说不太有吸引力。
synonyms: temporariness, transience, ephemerality
antonyms: permanence, perpetuity, eternity
noun
