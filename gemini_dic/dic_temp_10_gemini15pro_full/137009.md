nonspurious:/ˌnɒnˈspjʊəriəs/| adj.|真实的，非虚假的，非偶然的|Not false, counterfeit, or accidental; genuine, authentic, real.  Relating to a correlation or relationship between variables that is not due to chance or a third, confounding variable.|The study found a nonspurious correlation between exercise and improved mood. 这项研究发现了运动和情绪改善之间真实的关联。|Synonyms: genuine, authentic, real, valid, legitimate|Antonyms: spurious, false, counterfeit, fake, artificial|adjective
