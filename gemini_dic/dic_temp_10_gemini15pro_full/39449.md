connectivities:/ˌkɒnɛkˈtɪvɪtiz/ /ˌkɑːnekˈtɪvətiz/
n.
连接性；连通性
The state or quality of being connected or interconnected.
The internet has greatly improved global connectivities.  互联网极大地提高了全球的连通性。

Improved transport connectivities are essential for economic growth.  改善交通连接性对经济增长至关重要。
近义词: connections, links, networks
反义词: isolation, disconnection
词性: noun


Etymology:
From connect (v.) + -ivity + -ies (plural suffix).  Ultimately from Latin connectere "to bind together."
