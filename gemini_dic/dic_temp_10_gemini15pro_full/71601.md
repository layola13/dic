flat-pack:/ˈflætˌpæk/ (UK) /ˈflætˌpæk/ (US)| adj. n.|扁平包装的；扁平包装家具|Furniture or other items that are sold unassembled in flat boxes for easy transport and storage.  The buyer then assembles the item themselves.|I bought a flat-pack wardrobe from IKEA. 我从宜家买了一个扁平包装的衣柜。|Synonyms:  knock-down, ready-to-assemble, RTA|Antonyms: assembled, pre-built|adjective, noun

flat-pack:/ˈflætˌpæk/ (UK) /ˈflætˌpæk/ (US)| v. |扁平包装|To pack something flat for transportation or storage.|We flat-packed all the furniture before moving house.  我们搬家前把所有家具都扁平包装了。|Synonyms: disassemble, knock down|Antonyms: assemble, build|verb
