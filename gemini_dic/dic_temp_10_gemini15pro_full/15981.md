词源:
From French *balustre*, from Italian *balaustro*, from *balaustra* "wild pomegranate flower," from Late Latin *balaustium*, from Greek *balaustion* "flower of the wild pomegranate tree."  The shape of the baluster was thought to resemble the swelling form of the pomegranate flower.
