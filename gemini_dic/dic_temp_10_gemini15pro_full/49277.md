deliberations:/dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃənz/
|n.| 审议；考虑；评议；商议
|Careful consideration or discussion of a subject.|
|The jury began their deliberations after hearing all the evidence.| 陪审团在听取所有证据后开始审议。
|The results of the deliberations will be announced tomorrow.| 审议结果将于明天公布。
|synonym: discussions, consultations, considerations, debates|
|antonym: impulsiveness, recklessness|
|noun|

deliberation:/dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən/
|n.| 审议；考虑；评议；商议
|The act of carefully considering or discussing something.|
|After much deliberation, the committee reached a decision.| 经过深思熟虑，委员会做出了决定。
|He spoke with great deliberation, choosing his words carefully.| 他说话非常慎重，字斟句酌。
|synonym: thoughtfulness, care, caution, prudence|
|antonym: haste, impulsiveness|
|noun|
