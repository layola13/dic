infants:/ˈɪnfənts/| n. |婴儿，幼儿|A very young child, especially one that has not yet learned to walk or talk. |例句：The hospital has a special unit for premature infants. 这家医院有一个专门的早产儿病房。

例句：The infants were sleeping soundly in their cribs. 婴儿们在婴儿床里睡得很香。|近义词：babies, newborns, toddlers|反义词:adults, grown-ups|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French enfant ("child"), ultimately from Latin infans ("unable to speak," literally "not speaking"), from in- ("not") + fans (present participle of fari, "to speak").
