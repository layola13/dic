nonradiant:/ˌnɒnˈreɪdiənt/ | adj. | 非辐射的，不发光的 | Not emitting radiation, especially light or heat.  | The nonradiant side of the moon is incredibly cold. 月球不发光的那一面非常寒冷。| The heater used nonradiant technology, warming the room through convection.  这台加热器使用了非辐射技术，通过对流加热房间。 | 近义词: nonluminous, dull, dark | 反义词: radiant, luminous, bright | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "radiant" (from Latin "radians," present participle of "radiare" - to shine, emit rays)
