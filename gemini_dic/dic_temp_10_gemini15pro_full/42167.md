cougar: /ˈkuːɡər/ (US) /ˈkuːɡə(r)/ (UK)| n.|美洲狮; (尤指)中年女子, 猎艳老女人|A large American wild cat with a plain tawny to grayish coat.  A woman, especially a middle-aged or older one, who pursues younger men for romantic relationships.|例句：The cougar stalked its prey through the tall grass. 美洲狮在高高的草丛中跟踪猎物。

例句：She's been called a cougar for dating men half her age. 她因为和比她小一半的男人约会而被称为猎艳老女人。|近义词：puma, mountain lion, panther (for the animal);  predatory woman, maneater (for the woman)|反义词:cub (young animal)|词性:noun
