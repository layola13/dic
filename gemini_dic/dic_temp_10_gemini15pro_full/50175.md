deonerate:/diˈɒnəreɪt/
verb
免除；解除
to relieve of a duty, responsibility, or obligation.
The court deonerated him of all responsibility for the accident. 法庭免除了他在这起事故中的所有责任。

deonerate:/diˈɑːnəreɪt/
verb
免除；解除
to relieve of a duty, responsibility, or obligation.
The new evidence deonerated the suspect. 新的证据免除了嫌疑人的罪责。

近义词:exonerate, absolve, acquit, discharge, release
反义词:incriminate, charge, accuse


词性:v.

词源: From Latin *deoneratus, past participle of *deonerare, from de- + onus (“burden”).
