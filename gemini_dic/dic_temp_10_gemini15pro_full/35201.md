clubhouse:/ˈklʌbˌhaʊs/ (US), /ˈklʌbˌhaʊs/ (UK)| n.|会所；俱乐部；俱乐部会所|A building occupied by a club or used for social gatherings. |例句：The golf clubhouse offers stunning views of the course.  高尔夫俱乐部会所提供了球场的壮丽景色。

例句：They met at the yacht clubhouse for drinks after sailing.  帆船运动后，他们在游艇俱乐部会所见面喝酒。|近义词：club, center, hall, facility|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "home" in some contexts) |词性:noun
