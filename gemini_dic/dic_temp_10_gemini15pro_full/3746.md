agility:/əˈdʒɪləti/| n.|敏捷,灵活, 敏捷性|The gymnast displayed incredible agility on the balance beam.  这个体操运动员在平衡木上展现了惊人的敏捷性。|例句：The cat moved with remarkable agility through the trees. 猫咪非常灵活地在树林间穿梭。|近义词：nimbleness, dexterity, quickness, swiftness|反义词:clumsiness, awkwardness, stiffness|词性:noun
Etymology: From Middle French *agilité, from Latin agilitāt-, agilitās (“nimbleness, quickness”), from agilis (“nimble, quick, active”), from agere (“to do, drive, act”).
