exclusivity:/ˌɛkskluːˈsɪvɪti/
n.
排他性，独占性，独家经营权
The state of being exclusive; the right to have something exclusively.
The company negotiated exclusivity with the supplier, ensuring they were the only ones to receive the product.  这家公司与供应商谈判了独家经营权，确保他们是唯一能拿到该产品的公司。
exclusiveness, sole right, monopoly
inclusivity, openness, availability
noun

exclusivities:/ˌɛkskluːˈsɪvɪtiz/
n.
排他性，独占性，独家经营权（复数）
The plural form of exclusivity, referring to multiple instances of exclusive rights or arrangements.
The contract outlined several exclusivities granted to the distributor.  合同概述了授予经销商的几项独家经营权。
exclusive rights, monopolies, sole proprietorships
shared rights, open access, public domain
noun
