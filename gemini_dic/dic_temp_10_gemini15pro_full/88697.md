hobbits:/ˈhɒbɪts/ (UK) /ˈhɑːbɪts/ (US)| n.|霍比特人|A fictional race of small human-like creatures in J.R.R. Tolkien's fantasy world, characterized by their love of comfort, good food, and a peaceful life.|The hobbits lived in comfortable holes in the ground called hobbit-holes. 霍比特人住在舒适的洞穴里，叫做霍比特洞。|The Shire was home to many hobbits. 夏尔是许多霍比特人的家园。|近义词：halfling, dwarf (in some contexts)|反义词:giant|词性:noun
