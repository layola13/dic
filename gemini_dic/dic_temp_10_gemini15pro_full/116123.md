marver:/ˈmɑːrvər/ | n. | 大理石板；研磨机；研磨工具 | A smooth, flat stone or slab, traditionally made of marble, used for grinding and mixing pigments or other materials. Can also refer to a tool or machine used for grinding. |  He mixed the paint on the marver. 他在大理石板上调和颜料。 | Synonyms: grinding slab, grinding stone, muller | Antonyms: N/A (no direct opposite for a tool) | noun

marver:/ˈmɑːrvər/ | v. | 研磨；磨光 | To grind or polish (something) on a marver. | The glassblower marvered the molten glass to smooth its surface.  玻璃吹制工在研磨工具上磨光熔化的玻璃使其表面光滑。 | Synonyms: grind, polish, smooth | Antonyms: roughen | verb
