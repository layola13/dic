calcographer:/kælˈkɒɡrəfər/
n.
雕刻铜版者；铜版雕刻师
A person who engraves designs on metal plates, especially copper plates, for printing.
William Blake was a skilled calcographer as well as a poet. 威廉·布莱克是一位技艺精湛的铜版雕刻师，同时也是一位诗人。
