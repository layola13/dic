chym: /kaɪm/
n.
汁液；体液；精髓；炼金术士熔炉
An archaic term for the vital fluid or juice of an animal or plant; sometimes used by alchemists to refer to their furnace.

The alchemist heated the ingredients in his chym. 炼金术士在他的熔炉里加热原料。
