demonstrabilities:/ˌdɛmənˌstreɪbɪlɪtiz/
|n.|可论证性；可证明性；可示范性|The demonstrabilities of the new technology were impressive.  这项新技术的可论证性令人印象深刻。|
demonstrability (singular form)|indemonstrability|noun
demonstrable:/ˈdɛmənstrəbl/
|adj.|可论证的；可证明的；可示范的|The benefits of the program are demonstrable.  这个项目的好处是可论证的。|provable, verifiable, evident|unprovable, questionable|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *demonstrabilitas, from Latin demonstrabilis (demonstrable) + -itas (-ity).  Ultimately from Latin demonstrare (to demonstrate).
