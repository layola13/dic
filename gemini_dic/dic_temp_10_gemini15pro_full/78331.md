gersner:/ˈɡɜːrsnər/| n.|格斯纳；格斯纳波|A type of surface wave on water, characterized by small ripples preceding larger waves, often caused by a moving disturbance like a boat or duck.  The term is often associated with computer graphics, where it refers to algorithms used to simulate realistic water surfaces.|The boat's wake created distinct gersner waves.|近义词：wave, ripple|反义词:|noun
