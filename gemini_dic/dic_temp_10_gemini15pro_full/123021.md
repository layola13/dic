monasticon:/məˈnæstɪkən/ | n. | 修道院志，修道院目录 | A book or list describing the monasteries, churches, or religious houses of a particular country or region, often including their history, endowments, and privileges. |  Example 1: The scholar consulted the medieval monasticon to trace the history of the abbey. 学者查阅了中世纪的修道院志，以追溯修道院的历史。

Example 2: The monasticon provided detailed information about the abbey's founding and its subsequent growth.  修道院志提供了关于修道院建立及其随后发展情况的详细信息。 | 近义词:  cartulary, register, record | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *monasticōn*, neuter of *monasticus* (“monastic”), from Latin *monasticus*, from Ancient Greek μοναστικός (monastikós, “monastic”), from μοναστής (monastḗs, “monk”).
