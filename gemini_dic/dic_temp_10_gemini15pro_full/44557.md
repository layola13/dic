crotchetily:/ˈkrɒtʃɪtɪli/ | adv. | 脾气古怪地，乖戾地 | In a bad-tempered, irritable, and eccentric way, often marked by unpredictable whims and opinions. | The old man muttered crotchetily about the noise the children were making. 老头脾气暴躁地抱怨孩子们太吵了。 |  The composer crotchetily dismissed the orchestra's interpretation of his symphony. 作曲家暴躁地否定了管弦乐队对他交响曲的诠释。 | 近义词: grumpily, irascibly, cantankerously | 反义词: good-naturedly, pleasantly, amiably | 词性: 副词

crotchety:/ˈkrɒtʃɪti/ | adj. | 脾气古怪的，乖戾的 | Bad-tempered, irritable, and eccentric. |  My grandfather is getting quite crotchety in his old age. 我的祖父年纪大了，变得越来越古怪。 | The crotchety old woman yelled at the children for stepping on her lawn. 脾气暴躁的老妇人对着踩到她草坪的孩子们大吼大叫。 | 近义词: grumpy, irritable, cantankerous | 反义词: good-natured, pleasant, amiable | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the noun "crotchet," meaning a peculiar notion or whim, which comes from the French "crochet" (hook, whim).  The musical meaning of crotchet (quarter note) is also related, possibly due to the hooked shape of the note.
