nettlesome:/ˈnɛt(ə)lsəm/| adj.| 烦人的，恼人的，棘手的 |Causing annoyance or difficulty; irritating.|例句：The nettlesome problem of traffic congestion needs to be addressed.  交通拥堵这个恼人的问题需要得到解决。

例句：He found the constant interruptions nettlesome. 他觉得不断的打扰很烦人。|近义词：irritating, annoying, troublesome, vexing, bothersome |反义词：pleasant, agreeable, soothing, calming |词性:adjective
词源: From nettle + -some.  Nettle 指的是荨麻，一种带刺的植物，接触会引起刺痛感。因此，nettlesome 引申为“像荨麻一样烦人的”。
