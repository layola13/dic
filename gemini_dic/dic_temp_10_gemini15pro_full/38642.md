concupiscibleness:/kənˌkjuːpɪˈsɪblənəs/
n.
好色，贪欲，肉欲
The quality of being concupiscible; strong sexual desire or lustfulness.
His concupiscibleness led him down a dark path. 他的好色使他走上了一条不归路。
近义词: lust, lechery, lasciviousness, sensuality
反义词: chastity, purity, abstinence
词性: 名词

concupiscible:/kənˈkjuːpɪsəbl/
adj.
好色的，贪欲的，肉欲的
Characterized by or relating to concupiscence; having strong sexual desires.
He cast a concupiscible glance at the woman across the bar. 他向吧台对面的女人投去了贪婪的目光。
近义词: lustful, lecherous, lascivious, sensual
反义词: chaste, pure, abstinent
词性: 形容词


词源:
From Late Latin *concupiscibilis, from Latin concupiscere "to long for, covet," from com- "together" + cupiscere "to desire," related to cupidus "eager, passionate."
