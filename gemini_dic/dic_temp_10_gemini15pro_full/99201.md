interclavicle:/ˌɪntərˈklævɪkəl/
n.|锁间骨，叉骨|A bone situated between the clavicles, especially in monotremes and some reptiles and amphibians.|The interclavicle of the platypus is a distinctive feature.|近义词:episternum, interarticular cartilage|反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's an anatomical feature)|词性:noun
