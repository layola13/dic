away:/əˈweɪ/
adverb|离去；在远处；离开|indicating movement to a different place or position.|She walked away without saying a word. 她一句话也没说就走开了。
away:/əˈweɪ/
adverb|不断地；持续地|continuously or persistently.|He worked away at his desk for hours. 他在办公桌前连续工作了几个小时。
away:/əˈweɪ/
adverb|不在；外出|not present; absent.|He's away on business. 他出差了。
away:/əˈweɪ/
adverb|消失；减弱|no longer present; gone.|The pain slowly faded away. 疼痛慢慢消失了。
away:/əˈweɪ/
adjective|客场的；在远处的|playing or played at a place other than one's home ground.|The away team won the match. 客队赢得了比赛。


近义词:gone, absent, off, distant
反义词:here, present, near
词性:adverb, adjective

词源:From Old English *aweg (unattested), from a- "on, in, at" + weg "way". Cognate with Old Norse ávegu "away".
