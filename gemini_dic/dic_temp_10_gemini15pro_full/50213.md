departments:/dɪˈpɑːrtmənts/ | n. | 部门；系；科 | A part or division of a large organization such as a government, business, university, etc. | The company has several departments, including sales, marketing, and finance.  这家公司有几个部门，包括销售部、市场部和财务部。| synonyms: divisions, sections, branches, units | antonyms: whole, entirety | noun

departments:/dɪˈpɑːrtmənts/ | n. | (尤指大学的)系;科 | A division of a university or college specializing in a particular subject. |  The university has departments of physics, chemistry, and biology. 这所大学有物理系、化学系和生物系。 | synonyms: faculties, schools, colleges | antonyms: N/A | noun
