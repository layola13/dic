interclouding:/ˌɪntərˈklaʊdɪŋ/
n.
云间空隙，云的散开
The appearance of breaks in a cloud cover.
例句：The interclouding allowed glimpses of the sun. 云的散开让我们看到了太阳。
例句：The pilot skillfully navigated through the interclouding. 飞行员熟练地驾驶飞机穿过云间空隙。
近义词：cloud break, clearing
反义词：overcast, cloud cover
词性：名词

intercloud:/ˌɪntərˈklaʊd/
v.
在云间出现空隙
To form breaks in a cloud cover.
例句：The clouds began to intercloud, revealing patches of blue sky. 云开始散开，露出了片片蓝天。
例句：As the storm passed, the sky interclouded and the sun shone through. 暴风雨过后，天空云层散开，阳光照射进来。
近义词：clear, break up
反义词：overcast, thicken
词性：动词
