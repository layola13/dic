carpetbaggeries: /ˌkɑːrpɪtˈbæɡəriz/
| n. |南北战争后投机倒把行为；掠夺行为；不正当手段获取的利益|The exploitation of a region, situation, etc., by outsiders for personal gain, especially after a war or natural disaster.  It specifically refers to the actions of Northerners who went to the South after the American Civil War to profit from the Reconstruction.| Many Southerners resented the carpetbaggeries of Northern businessmen after the Civil War. 南北战争后，许多南方人对北方商人的投机倒把行为感到愤慨。 |Synonyms: exploitation, profiteering, opportunism|Antonyms: philanthropy, altruism|noun
