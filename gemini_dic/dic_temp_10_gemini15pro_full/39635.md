consequentiality:/ˌkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃiˈælɪti/ /ˌkɑːnsɪˈkwɛnʃiˈælɪti/| n. |重要性，重大，后果|The state or quality of being consequential; importance or significance.|The consequentiality of the decision was immediately apparent.  这个决定的重要性立刻显现出来。|Synonyms: importance, significance, weight, moment, gravity|Antonyms: insignificance, triviality|noun

consequential:/ˌkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃəl/ /ˌkɑːnsɪˈkwɛnʃəl/| adj. |重要的，重大的，随之发生的|Following as a result or effect; important, significant.|His resignation was a consequential event in the company's history. 他的辞职是公司历史上的一件大事。|Synonyms: important, significant, momentous, weighty|Antonyms: insignificant, trivial, unimportant|adjective
