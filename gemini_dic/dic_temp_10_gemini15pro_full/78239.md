gerent:/ˈdʒɪərənt/| n.|代理人；统治者；管理者|A person appointed to administer a kingdom or estate in the absence of the sovereign or the owner.  A person acting as a manager or administrator.|The king appointed a gerent to rule in his stead while he was away at war.|Synonyms: administrator, manager, governor, regent, deputy, representative, proxy|Antonyms: owner, sovereign, monarch|noun
