akrotiri:/əˈkrɒtɪəri/| n.|阿克罗蒂里|A Bronze Age settlement on the volcanic Greek island of Thera (modern Santorini), destroyed in the Theran eruption sometime in the 17th century BC.  It is often referred to as the "Minoan Pompeii".|例句：Akrotiri was a thriving port city before the volcanic eruption. 阿克罗蒂里在火山爆发前是一个繁荣的港口城市。

|例句：The frescoes discovered at Akrotiri offer valuable insights into Minoan culture. 在阿克罗蒂里发现的壁画为了解米诺斯文化提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词：settlement, excavation site|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "modern city")|词性:noun
