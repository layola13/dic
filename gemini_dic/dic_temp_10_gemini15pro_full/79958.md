go-aheads:/ˈɡoʊ əˌhɛdz/| n.|批准，许可，放行; (复数)进展，进步|permission to proceed; progress (plural)|例句：The boss gave us the go-ahead to start the project. 老板批准我们开始这个项目。

例句：The project has made significant go-aheads in the past few months.  这个项目在过去几个月里取得了重大进展。|近义词：approval, permission, green light, progress, advancements|反义词：rejection, denial, setback, delay|词性:名词

词源:  Compound noun from "go" and "ahead".  "Go ahead" as a verb phrase meaning "to proceed" dates back to the late 18th century. The noun form "go-ahead" emerged later, in the 19th century.
