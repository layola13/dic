overgesticulate: /ˌoʊvərjɛˈstɪkjʊleɪt/
verb
过度做手势
to use too many gestures or exaggerated gestures when speaking
The nervous speaker overgesticulated, waving his arms wildly.  这位紧张的演讲者过度地做着手势，疯狂地挥舞着他的手臂。
He tended to overgesticulate when he got excited. 他一激动就容易手舞足蹈。
synonym: exaggerate gestures, overact
antonym: understate, be subdued
