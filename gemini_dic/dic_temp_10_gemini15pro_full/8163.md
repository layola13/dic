anti-critical:/ˌæntiˈkrɪtɪkəl/
adjective
反批评的；反对批评的；压制批评的
Opposed to or discouraging criticism.  Not tolerant of criticism; defensive.  Suppressing criticism.
The government implemented anti-critical policies, stifling dissent and free speech. 政府实施了反批评政策，压制了异议和言论自由。
He has an anti-critical attitude, refusing to acknowledge any flaws in his work.  他有一种反批评的态度，拒绝承认自己工作中的任何缺陷。
synonyms: defensive, intolerant (of criticism), uncritical, censorious (of others), suppressive
antonyms: critical, receptive (to criticism), open-minded, self-critical, tolerant (of criticism)
词性: adjective
