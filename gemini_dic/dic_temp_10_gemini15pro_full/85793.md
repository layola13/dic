hayward:/ˈheɪwərd/| n.|围场；养鹿场;  (姓氏) 海沃德|A hayward is an enclosed area of land, typically used for keeping deer or other animals.  Hayward is also a surname.|例句：The hayward was full of deer grazing peacefully.  养鹿场里满是悠闲吃草的鹿。

例句：John Hayward is a famous author.  约翰·海沃德是一位著名的作家。|近义词：enclosure, paddock, field|反义词:open field|词性:noun
