lupinine: /ˈluːpɪniːn; ˈluːpɪnaɪn/ | n. | 羽扇豆碱; 路潘碱 | A quinolizidine alkaloid, C₁₀H₁₉NO, obtained from lupin beans and other plants, and formerly used as an oxytocic. |  Lupinine is a toxic alkaloid found in many lupin species. 羽扇豆碱是一种有毒生物碱，存在于许多羽扇豆种类中。|  Lupinine can cause poisoning if ingested in large quantities. 如果大量摄入羽扇豆碱会导致中毒。 | Synonyms: quinolizidine alkaloid | Antonyms: None | noun

lupinine: /ˈluːpɪniːn; ˈluːpɪnaɪn/ | adj. | 羽扇豆的; 羽扇豆碱的 | Relating to or derived from lupin.  |  Lupinine alkaloids are found in various parts of the lupin plant. 羽扇豆生物碱存在于羽扇豆植物的各个部位。|  Lupinine seeds contain a significant amount of the alkaloid. 羽扇豆种子含有大量的生物碱。 | Synonyms: lupin | Antonyms: None | adjective
