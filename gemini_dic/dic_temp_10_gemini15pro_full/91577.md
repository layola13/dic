hyperactions:/ˌhaɪpərˈækʃənz/ | n. |过度活跃，多动|Excessive actions or activity, typically characterized by restlessness and impulsivity. It often manifests as fidgeting, difficulty staying still, and excessive talking.|例句：The child's hyperactions disrupted the class. 这个孩子的多动症扰乱了课堂秩序。

例句：His hyperactions made it difficult for him to focus on his work. 他的过度活跃使他难以集中精力工作。|近义词：hyperactivity, restlessness, fidgeting|反义词：calmness, stillness, inactivity|词性: 名词
