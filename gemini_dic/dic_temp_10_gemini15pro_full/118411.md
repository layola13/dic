merriment:/ˈmerɪmənt/| n.|欢乐，欢笑，嬉戏|The room was filled with merriment and laughter. 房间里充满了欢声笑语。|例句：The festival was a time of great merriment.  这个节日是一个充满欢乐的时刻。

例句：Their merriment could be heard throughout the house. 他们的欢笑声响彻整个房子。

近义词：gaiety, mirth, cheerfulness, hilarity|反义词: gloom, sadness, sorrow|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *meriment, *meryment, from Anglo-Norman meriment, from Old French merriment, from merré (“merry”) + -ment (“-ment”).
