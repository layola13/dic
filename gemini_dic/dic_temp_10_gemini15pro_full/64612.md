eruptives:/ɪˈrʌptɪvz/
| n. | 喷出物；火山喷发物 | Substances ejected from a volcano, such as lava, ash, and rock fragments. | The eruptives from the volcano covered the surrounding area in ash. 火山喷出的物质覆盖了周围地区的火山灰。 | Synonyms: ejecta, volcanic debris | Antonyms: intrusions | noun


eruptive:/ɪˈrʌptɪv/
| adj. | 喷发的；爆发的 | Characterized by or tending to erupt; bursting forth. | The eruptive phase of the volcano lasted for several weeks.  火山的喷发阶段持续了数周。| Synonyms: explosive, volcanic | Antonyms: dormant, quiescent | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ēruptīvus*, from ēruptus (past participle of ērumpere ‘break out’), from ex- ‘out’ + rumpere ‘to break’.
