heat-resistant:/ˈhiːt rɪˈzɪstənt/
adj.
耐热的
Able to withstand high temperatures without being damaged or significantly altered.
This oven uses a heat-resistant glass door.  这个烤箱使用耐热玻璃门。
The spacecraft is coated with heat-resistant tiles.  航天器涂有耐热瓷砖。
近义词:fireproof, heatproof, thermostable
反义词:flammable, combustible
词性:adjective

词源:
From "heat" + "resistant". "Heat" comes from Old English *hætu. "Resistant" comes from Late Latin resistentem, present participle of resistere "to resist," from re- "back" + sistere "to take a stand".
