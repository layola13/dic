feudatory:/ˈfjuːdətɔːri/ (US), /ˈfjuːdətəri/ (UK)| n. adj.|封臣；诸侯；附属国；封地的|A person or state that holds land on condition of feudal service; owing feudal allegiance to or depending on a superior lord; of or relating to the feudal system.|The duke was a powerful feudatory of the king. 公爵是国王的强大封臣。|近义词：vassal, liegeman, tributary, subordinate|反义词：lord, overlord, sovereign|词性: noun, adjective

feudatory:/ˈfjuːdətɔːri/ (US), /ˈfjuːdətəri/ (UK)| adj.|封建的；诸侯的|Of or relating to the feudal system; held by feudal tenure.|The feudatory lords controlled vast estates. 诸侯领主控制着广阔的土地。|近义词：feudal, vassal|反义词：independent, sovereign|词性: adjective
