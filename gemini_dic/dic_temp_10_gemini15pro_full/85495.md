hastily:/ˈheɪstɪli/
adverb|匆忙地；草率地；仓促地|Done or acting with excessive speed and urgency, often without due consideration.|She hastily packed her bags and left the house. 她匆忙地收拾行李离开了房子。|quickly, hurriedly, rapidly, swiftly|slowly, deliberately, carefully|adverb

hastefully:/ˈheɪstfəli/
adverb|匆忙地；草率地；仓促地|In a hurried or rushed manner, often implying a lack of care or thoughtfulness.|He hastefully signed the contract without reading it properly. 他草率地签了合同，没有好好读。|hurriedly, rashly, precipitately, imprudently|carefully, thoughtfully, deliberately, slowly|adverb
