beblot:/ˈbiːblɒt/| n. |污点，墨渍|A stain or blot of ink. |例句：There was a beblot on the manuscript. 手稿上有一个墨渍。|例句：He tried to remove the beblot with a damp cloth. 他试图用一块湿布去除墨渍。|近义词：blot, stain, smudge|反义词: cleanness, spotlessness|词性:noun

词源: From "be-" (intensifying prefix) + "blot" (stain, mark).
