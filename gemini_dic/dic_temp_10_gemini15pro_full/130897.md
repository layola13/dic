nonarticulatenesses:/ˌnɒnɑːrˈtɪkjʊlətəsnəsɪz/
noun
表达不清，不善言辞
The state of being unable to express oneself clearly or fluently.
His nonarticulateness made it difficult for him to communicate his ideas. 他的不善言辞使他难以表达自己的想法。
inarticulacy, inarticulateness, unintelligibility
articulateness, eloquence, fluency
