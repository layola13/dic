happy-clappy:/ˈhæpi ˈklæpi/ | adj. | 过分热情活泼的；虚情假意的；装腔作势的 | Excessively and superficially enthusiastic, often implying insincerity or shallowness.  Characterized by ostentatious displays of emotion, especially in a religious context. |  例句：The happy-clappy atmosphere of the church service made him uncomfortable. 教堂礼拜仪式上那种过分热情活泼的氛围让他感到不舒服。 | 例句：She found the happy-clappy sales team rather irritating. 她觉得这个装腔作势的销售团队相当烦人。 | 近义词：effusive, enthusiastic, over-the-top, gushy | 反义词：reserved, restrained, subdued, low-key | 词性：形容词
