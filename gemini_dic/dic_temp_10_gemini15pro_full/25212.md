budgeness:/ˈbʌdʒɪnəs/
n.
萌芽状态; 刚露头的性质;
The state or quality of being like a bud; the beginning or nascent stage of something.
The budgeness of the project was apparent, with only a basic framework in place.  这个项目的萌芽状态很明显，只有一个基本的框架到位。
Nascence, incipience, germination
Maturity, completion
Noun
