luring:/ˈlʊrɪŋ/ | adj. | 诱人的，吸引人的 | Tempting and attractive; enticing. | The shop window displayed a luring array of cakes.  商店橱窗里摆放着一系列诱人的蛋糕。 |  The restaurant's luring aroma attracted many customers. 餐厅诱人的香味吸引了许多顾客。 | 近义词: enticing, tempting, alluring, attractive, seductive | 反义词: repellent, off-putting, unappealing, unattractive | 词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "lure," which comes from Old French "luerre" (a decoy for hawks), from Frankish "*lothr" (bait), from Proto-Germanic "*luthron" (to deceive).
