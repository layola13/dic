ended:/ˈɛndɪd/| v.|结束，终止|The meeting ended at 5 pm.  会议下午5点结束。|The movie ended with a cliffhanger. 这部电影以悬念结尾。|近义词：finished, concluded, terminated|反义词:began, started, commenced|verb (past tense of "end")

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *endian (attested only in unended), from Proto-Germanic *andijaną (“to end, finish”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂enti (“in front of, before”). Cognate with Old Norse enda (“to end”), Old High German antōn (“to finish”), Gothic ᚨᚾᛞﻴ (andi, “end”).
