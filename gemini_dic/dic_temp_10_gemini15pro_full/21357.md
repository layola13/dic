blore:/blɔːr/| n.|模糊不清，朦胧的一片；（尤指号角声等）喧闹声；（尤指号角声等）喧闹声|A blurred image or sound; a loud, confused noise, especially of horns or other instruments.|The city lights were a blore through the fog. 城市的灯光在雾中一片模糊。|近义词：blur, haze, din, clamor|反义词：clarity, sharpness, silence|noun

blore:/blɔːr/| v.|（尤指号角）发出响亮而刺耳的声音|To make a loud, harsh noise, especially of a horn.|The trumpets blored as the king entered the hall. 国王进入大厅时，喇叭声响起。|近义词：blast, blare|反义词：whisper, murmur|verb
