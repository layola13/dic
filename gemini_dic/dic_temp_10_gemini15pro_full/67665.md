extrudable:/ɪkˈstruːdəbl/ /ɛkˈstruːdəbl/
adjective
可挤压的，可挤出的
Capable of being extruded; able to be shaped by forcing through a die.
The clay is extrudable and can be molded into various shapes.  这种粘土是可挤压的，可以塑造成各种形状。
Molten plastic is extrudable through a nozzle to create different forms. 熔融塑料可以通过喷嘴挤压以形成不同的形状。
近义词: moldable, shapeable, ductile
反义词: rigid, inflexible
词性: adjective

词源:
From extrude + -able.  Extrude comes from Latin extrūdō, from ex- 'out' + trūdō 'thrust'.
