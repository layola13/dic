imply:/ɪmˈplaɪ/ (UK), /ɪmˈplaɪ/ (US)| v. |暗示，意味|To suggest something indirectly without stating it explicitly.|Her silence implied consent. 她的沉默暗示着同意。|His words implied a threat. 他的话暗示着威胁。|近义词：suggest, insinuate, infer|反义词：state, declare, express|词性:动词

词源:
Late Latin implicare "to enfold, entangle," from in- "in" + plicare "to fold"
