inapprehension:/ˌɪnˌæprɪˈhɛnʃən/| n.|不安，忧虑，恐惧|A feeling of anxiety or dread about something that might happen.|She waited for the results with a growing sense of inapprehension. 她怀着越来越强烈的不安等待结果。|Synonyms: anxiety, worry, dread, fear, unease, nervousness, apprehension, foreboding, misgiving, suspicion, distrust|Antonyms: confidence, assurance, calmness, composure, serenity|noun

inapprehensive:/ˌɪnˌæprɪˈhɛnsɪv/ | adj.|不担心的，不忧虑的|Not worried or fearful about something that might happen.|He seemed surprisingly inapprehensive about the risks involved.  他似乎对所涉及的风险出奇地不担心。|Synonyms: unafraid, unworried, unconcerned, untroubled, carefree, relaxed, calm, composed, confident, assured|Antonyms: apprehensive, anxious, worried, fearful, nervous|adjective
