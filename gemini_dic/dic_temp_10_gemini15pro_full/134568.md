nonlocalized:/ˌnɒnˈləʊkəlaɪzd/ /ˌnɑːnˈloʊkəlaɪzd/
adjective
非局部的，非定域的
Not confined or restricted to a particular location; spread out or diffused.
The pain was nonlocalized, making it difficult to diagnose.  这种疼痛不是局部的，因此很难诊断。
The electron's charge is nonlocalized, distributed throughout the molecule. 电子的电荷是非局域的，分布在整个分子中。
Synonyms: diffuse, spread out, dispersed, pervasive, widespread
Antonyms: localized, concentrated, focused, confined, restricted
词性: adjective
