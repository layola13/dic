codec:/ˈkoʊdɛk/ (US), /ˈkəʊdɛk/ (UK)| n.|编码解码器|A device or program that compresses data for transmission and decompresses it for playback, especially in audio and video.|例句：This video requires a specific codec to play.  这个视频需要特定的编码解码器才能播放。

例句：I had to install a new codec to watch the movie. 我不得不安装一个新的编解码器来看这部电影。|近义词：encoder/decoder, compressor/decompressor|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
