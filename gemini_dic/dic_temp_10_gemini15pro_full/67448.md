extirpations:/ˌekstərˈpeɪʃənz/

|例句：The extirpation of the gray wolf from Yellowstone National Park had unforeseen consequences for the ecosystem.  从黄石国家公园根除灰狼对生态系统产生了不可预见的后果。|
|例句：The organization is dedicated to the extirpation of poverty and hunger. 这个组织致力于消除贫困和饥饿。|
|近义词：eradication, elimination, extermination, removal, destruction|
|反义词：introduction, establishment, preservation, conservation|
