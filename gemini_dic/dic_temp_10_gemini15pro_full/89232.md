homie:/ˈhoʊmi/| n.|朋友，伙伴，老乡|A close friend or comrade, often used in a familiar or affectionate way.  Originating from "homeboy," shortened to "homie."|例句：Hey homie, what's up? 嘿，老兄，怎么了？

例句：That's my homie from way back. 那是我很久以前的老朋友了。|近义词：friend, pal, buddy, mate, dude|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:noun
