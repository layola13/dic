dispensational:/ˌdɪspənˈseɪʃənəl/
adj.
分配的；施予的；免除的；时代论的
Relating to or based on a system of theology that emphasizes the literal interpretation of biblical prophecy and divides history into distinct periods or dispensations, each characterized by a different way in which God deals with humanity.
Dispensational theology is a popular belief system among some evangelical Christians.  时代论神学在一些福音派基督徒中是一种流行的信仰体系。
The church adopted a dispensational view of scripture. 教会采用了对圣经的时代论观点。
synonyms: epochal, periodic, millennial
antonyms: non-dispensational, unified, atemporal
词性: 形容词
