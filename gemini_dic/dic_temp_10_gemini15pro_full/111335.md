liveable:/ˈlɪvəbl/ (US) /ˈlɪvəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|适宜居住的；可以住人的|Fit or suitable to live in; capable of supporting life.|例句：This city is becoming more and more liveable.  这座城市变得越来越宜居了。

例句：The house is small but perfectly liveable. 这房子虽小，但完全可以住人。

近义词：habitable, inhabitable, residential|反义词：unliveable, uninhabitable|词性:形容词
