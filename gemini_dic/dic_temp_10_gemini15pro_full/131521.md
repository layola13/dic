noncomedogenic:/ˌnɒnˌkɒmɪdoʊˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. | 非致粉刺的，不会阻塞毛孔的 |  Not likely to block pores and cause comedones (pimples or blackheads).  This term is often used to describe skincare products and cosmetics. | This moisturizer is noncomedogenic, so it won't clog your pores. 这款保湿霜是非致粉刺的，所以不会堵塞你的毛孔。 | This foundation is labeled noncomedogenic, which is great for my sensitive skin.  这款粉底被标记为非致粉刺性，非常适合我敏感的肌肤。 | 近义词: non-acnegenic | 反义词: comedogenic | 词性: adjective
