ETYMOLOGY->

From Latin -orius, -oria, -orium.  The suffix "-ory" is derived from the Latin "-orius," "-oria," or "-orium," which are adjective or noun suffixes. These suffixes originally indicated a place for something, a thing connected with something, or something that serves a particular function.
