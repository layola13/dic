outfame:/ˌaʊtˈfeɪm/
verb
超过…的名声; 使…的名声相形见绌
to surpass in fame; to eclipse the fame of
例句：The new artist quickly outfamed his mentor.  这位新艺术家很快就超过了他导师的名声。

例句：Her generosity outfamed her wealth. 她的慷慨胜过她的财富。

近义词：surpass, eclipse, outshine, outdo
反义词：be overshadowed by, be eclipsed by
词性：动词
