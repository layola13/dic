mercilessly: /mɜːsɪləsli/ (British) /ˈmɜːrsləsli/ (American)| adv. | 无情地，残忍地 |  In a merciless manner; without pity or compassion.  It describes actions done with extreme cruelty or without any regard for suffering. | The dictator mercilessly crushed the rebellion.  独裁者无情地镇压了叛乱。

The soldiers mercilessly slaughtered the villagers. 士兵们残忍地屠杀了村民们。 | 近义词: ruthlessly, cruelly, heartlessly, pitilessly | 反义词: mercifully, compassionately, kindly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From mercy + -less + -ly.  "Mercy" comes from Old French "merci" meaning "grace, pity, thanks."  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking." "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
