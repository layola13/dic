non-flammable:/ˌnɒnˈflæməbl/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈflæməbl/ (US)| adj.|不易燃的；非易燃性的|Not easily set on fire; not flammable.|This material is non-flammable and therefore safe to use near heat sources. 这种材料不易燃，因此可在热源附近安全使用。|The costumes are made of non-flammable material. 这些服装由不易燃材料制成。|近义词:fire-resistant, fireproof|反义词:flammable, inflammable|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + flammable.  "Flammable" itself comes from the Latin "flammare" meaning "to set on fire."  The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning of "flammable."
