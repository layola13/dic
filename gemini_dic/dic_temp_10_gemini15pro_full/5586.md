alter-egos:/ˈɔːltər ˈiːɡoʊz/ (US) /ˈɔːltə ˈiːɡəʊz/ (UK)| n. |分身；化身；另一个自我|A person's secondary or alternative personality.  It can be a fictional character, a stage persona, or a hidden aspect of one's self.| Clark Kent is Superman's alter-ego. 克拉克·肯特是超人的另一个身份。

He adopted an online alter-ego to protect his real identity. 他在网上使用了一个化名来保护自己的真实身份。|synonyms: persona, avatar, other self|antonyms: true self, real identity|noun
