adventuresses: /ədˈvɛntʃərɪsɪz/
n.
女冒险家;  喜欢冒险的女人
A woman who seeks adventure, especially one who travels to exotic or dangerous places.
The book tells the story of two adventuresses who travelled the world. 这本书讲述了两个周游世界的女冒险家的故事。

She was a renowned adventuress, famed for her expeditions to remote corners of the globe. 她是一位著名的女冒险家，以到地球偏远角落探险而闻名。
近义词:explorer, adventurer, thrill-seeker
反义词:homebody, stay-at-home
词性:名词(plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *aventuresse*, from Old French *aventure* (adventure) + *-esse* (feminine suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *adventura* ("a thing about to happen").
