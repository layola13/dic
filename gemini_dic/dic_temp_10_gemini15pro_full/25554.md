biltong:/ˈbɪltɒŋ/ (UK), /ˈbɪlˌtɔŋ/ (US)| n.|风干肉，牛肉干|A cured, air-dried meat, usually beef, originating from Southern African countries like South Africa, Namibia, Zimbabwe, and Botswana. It is typically made by marinating strips of meat in vinegar, spices, and salt, then hanging them to air-dry.|例句：I love snacking on biltong. 我喜欢吃牛肉干。

例句：Biltong is a popular snack in South Africa.  牛肉干在南非是一种很受欢迎的小吃。

|近义词：jerky, beef jerky|反义词:|noun
