gallingness:/ˈɡɔːlɪŋnɪs/| n. |令人恼火; 恼人之事; 侮辱; |The gallingness of his remark made her want to retort. 他的话让她很恼火，想反驳他。|例句：The gallingness of his betrayal was almost unbearable.  他的背叛令人难以忍受。

The gallingness of their constant criticism wore her down.  他们不断的批评让她身心俱疲。|近义词：annoyance, irritation, vexation, exasperation|反义词: pleasantness, agreeableness|词性:noun

词源: From galling + -ness.  Galling itself comes from the verb gall, meaning "to make sore by rubbing," ultimately from Old French galer.
