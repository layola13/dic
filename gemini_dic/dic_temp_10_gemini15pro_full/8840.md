antiexpressive:/ˌæntiɪkˈspresɪv/
adjective
反表现主义的；抑制情感表达的
Not allowing for or encouraging the expression of feelings or emotions; inhibiting or discouraging self-expression.
Her antiexpressive upbringing made it difficult for her to articulate her feelings.  她反表现主义的教养方式使她难以表达自己的感受。
The strict school rules created an antiexpressive environment for the students.  严格的校规为学生创造了一个抑制情感表达的环境。
近义词:unemotional, inhibited, restrained, reserved
反义词:expressive, demonstrative, emotional, open
词性:adjective
