overhunting:/ˌoʊvərˈhʌntɪŋ/| n.|过度捕猎|Excessive hunting of a species, often to the point of endangerment or extinction.|The overhunting of whales in the 19th century nearly drove them to extinction. 19世纪对鲸鱼的过度捕猎几乎使它们灭绝。|Overhunting led to a drastic decline in the elephant population. 过度捕猎导致大象数量急剧下降。|近义词：overkill, excessive hunting|反义词: conservation, preservation|noun
