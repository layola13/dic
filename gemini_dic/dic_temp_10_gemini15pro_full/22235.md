bondar:/ˈbɒndɑːr (UK), ˈbɑːndər (US)| n.|桶匠；制桶工人；合作社成员|A craftsman who makes and repairs barrels and other wooden containers.  A member of a cooperative, especially in Eastern Europe. |例句：The bondar carefully inspected the staves before assembling the barrel. 桶匠在组装木桶之前仔细检查了桶板。

例句：The bondars in the village collectively owned the forest and shared the profits from selling timber. 村里的合作社成员集体拥有这片森林，并分享出售木材的利润。|近义词：cooper, barrel maker|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
