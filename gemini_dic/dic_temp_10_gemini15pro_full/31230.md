chanter:/ˈtʃæntər (US), ˈtʃɑːntər (UK)| n.|吟唱者，歌唱家；领唱者|A person who chants, especially one who sings liturgical chants or leads the chanting in a church service. |The chanter led the congregation in a beautiful hymn. 领唱者带领会众唱了一首优美的赞美诗。|Synonyms: singer, vocalist, chorister, songster|Antonyms: listener, audience|noun

chanter:/ˈtʃæntər (US), ˈtʃɑːntər (UK)| n.|香菇|An edible mushroom, especially the chanterelle.|We found a patch of chanterelles in the woods. 我们在树林里发现了一片鸡油菌。|Synonyms: chanterelle, mushroom, fungus|Antonyms: N/A|noun
