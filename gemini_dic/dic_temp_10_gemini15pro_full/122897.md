molokany:/məˈlɒkəni/ | n. | 摩洛堪人；牛奶饮用者 | A member of a Russian Christian sect, similar to the Dukhobors, who reject military service and established communities in the US. They are known for their pacifism and vegetarianism.  |  The Molokany migrated to the United States in the early 20th century to escape persecution in Russia. 摩洛堪人在20世纪初迁移到美国以逃避在俄罗斯的迫害。|  Synonyms: Dukhobors, pacifists, vegetarians | Antonyms: meat-eaters, militants | noun
