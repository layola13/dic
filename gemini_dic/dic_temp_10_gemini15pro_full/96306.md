indigently:/ˈɪndɪdʒəntli/
adverb
贫困地，贫穷地
in a poor or needy manner; lacking the necessities of life.
He lived indigently, relying on charity for food and shelter. 他生活贫困，依靠慈善机构提供食物和住所。
The family was forced to live indigently after the factory closed down. 工厂倒闭后，这家人被迫过着贫困的生活。

近义词:poorly, needily, destitution
反义词:richly, wealthily, affluently
词性:adverb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *indigenter, from Latin indigens (“needy, poor”), present participle of indigēre (“to need, want, lack”).
