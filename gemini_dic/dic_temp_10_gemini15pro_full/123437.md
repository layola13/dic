monopolization:/məˌnɑːpəˌləˈzeɪʃn/ /məˌnɒpəˌlaɪˈzeɪʃn/| n. |垄断，独占|The act of monopolizing something, gaining exclusive control over a commodity or service.|The monopolization of the oil industry by a few large companies led to higher prices.|近义词：cornering, domination, control, cartelisation|反义词:competition, sharing|noun

monopolize:/məˈnɑːpəˌlaɪz/ /məˈnɒpəˌlaɪz/| vt. |垄断，独占|To obtain exclusive possession of; to dominate or control completely.|The company monopolized the market for that particular software.|近义词：dominate, control, corner, hog|反义词:share, compete|verb
