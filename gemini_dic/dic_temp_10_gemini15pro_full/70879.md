例句：These are some of the finest finos I've ever tasted. 这些是我品尝过的最好的菲诺雪利酒。|近义词：sherries, Amontillado (a type of sherry), choice goods, premium products|反义词:oloroso (a sweeter type of sherry), inferior goods|词性:名词
