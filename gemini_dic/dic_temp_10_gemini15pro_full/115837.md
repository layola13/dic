marketplace:/ˈmɑːrkɪtpleɪs/ | n. |市场；商场；交易场所|A public place where goods and services are bought and sold. |例句：The online marketplace is becoming increasingly competitive.  网上交易市场竞争日益激烈。

例句：Farmers bring their produce to the marketplace every Saturday. 农民们每个星期六都把他们的农产品带到市场上来卖。|近义词：market, bazaar, emporium |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle English *market place*, equivalent to market +‎ place.
