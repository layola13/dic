counterconspiracy:/ˈkaʊntər kənˈspɪrəsi/
noun
反阴谋，对抗阴谋的活动
An action or series of actions intended to prevent a conspiracy.
The government launched a counterconspiracy to expose the plot. 政府发起了一项反阴谋活动来揭露该阴谋。
synonyms: counterplot, countermove
antonyms: conspiracy, plot
n.
