bescorn:/bɪˈskɔːrn/
verb
轻蔑，嘲笑，奚落
To treat with scorn or contempt; mock.
He bescorned their attempts at reconciliation. 他嘲笑了他们和解的尝试。
近义词: deride, mock, ridicule, scoff, disdain, scorn
反义词: admire, respect, praise, commend
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + scorn.  The prefix "be-" intensifies the meaning of "scorn."
