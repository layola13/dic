impart:/ɪmˈpɑːrt/ | vt.|传授，告知，赋予|To communicate information, knowledge, or a quality to someone.|She imparted her wisdom to her students. 她把自己的智慧传授给学生们。|例句：

The teacher imparted his knowledge to his students. 老师把他的知识传授给学生们。

He imparted the bad news to his family. 他把坏消息告诉了他的家人。

|近义词：communicate, convey, transmit, bestow, grant|反义词：withhold, conceal|词性:动词
