mirror-like:/ˈmɪrərˌlaɪk/ |adj.|像镜子的；光滑的|Resembling a mirror; smooth and reflective.|The lake was mirror-like, reflecting the surrounding mountains.  湖面像镜子一样，倒映着周围的山峦。|The car had a mirror-like finish. 这辆车有着像镜子一样的光洁度。|近义词:reflective, glassy, polished, smooth|反义词:dull, rough, matte|词性:形容词

词源:  Compound word formed from "mirror" (from Old French *mireor, from Latin mīrārī "to wonder at, admire") + "-like" (from Old English -līc, meaning "having the form or appearance of").
