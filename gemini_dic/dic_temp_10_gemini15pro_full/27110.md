caliginous:/kəˈlɪdʒɪnəs/ /kəˈlɪdʒɪnəs/| adj. |黑暗的；昏暗的；朦胧的|Dark, dim, or obscure.|The caliginous night made it impossible to see the path ahead. 漆黑的夜晚使得前面的路根本看不见。|Synonyms: dark, dim, obscure, gloomy, murky|Antonyms: bright, clear, light, lucid|adjective
