forepossessed:/ˌfɔːrpəˈzɛst/ | adj. |被预先占有的；心事重重的；着迷的| Preoccupied; obsessed.  Haunted by a thought or feeling. |  He walked through the streets, forepossessed by a sense of impending doom. 他走在街上，心中充满了即将到来的厄运的感觉。 | 近义词: preoccupied, obsessed, absorbed, engrossed | 反义词: carefree, unworried, indifferent | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From "fore-" (before) + "possessed" (controlled by a feeling or idea).  The prefix "fore-" emphasizes the preemptive nature of the possession, suggesting the feeling or thought dominates the mind in advance of other considerations.
