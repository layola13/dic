eeriness:/ˈɪərɪnɪs/| n.|怪诞；阴森；神秘|The eeriness of the abandoned house made the children afraid to enter.  那栋废弃房屋的怪诞气氛让孩子们不敢进去。|例句：The eeriness of the fog made it difficult to see. 雾气的阴森使得很难看清东西。

The silence in the forest had an unnerving eeriness. 森林里的寂静有一种令人不安的怪异感。|近义词：strangeness, weirdness, creepiness, uncanniness|反义词:normality, ordinariness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From eerie + -ness.  Eerie itself comes from Old English *earg, meaning "timid, cowardly."
