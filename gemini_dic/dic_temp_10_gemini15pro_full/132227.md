"Nondeceits" is a relatively modern formation, likely coined by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not" or "lack of") to the noun "deceit."  "Deceit" comes from the Old French "deceite," ultimately deriving from the Latin "decipere" meaning "to ensnare, beguile, cheat."
