incorporealist:/ˌɪnkɔːrˈpɔːriəlɪst/
n.
无实体论者；唯心论者
A person who believes that material things have no real existence, only spiritual or mental entities.
The incorporealist argued that the physical world is merely an illusion.  无实体论者认为物质世界只是一种幻觉。
Synonyms: idealist, spiritualist, immaterialist
Antonyms: materialist, physicalist
词性: noun
