circumfuse:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈfjuːz/ /ˌsɝːkəmˈfjuːz/
verb
环绕；包围；弥漫
To pour around; to spread throughout; to encompass.
The warm light circumfused the room. 温暖的光线弥漫了整个房间。
The aroma of coffee circumfused the air. 咖啡的香气弥漫在空气中。
近义词: surround, envelop, encompass, permeate
反义词: expose, reveal
词性: verb
