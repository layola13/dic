gandhian:/ɡɑːnˈdiən/ | adj. n. | 甘地的；甘地主义的；非暴力不合作主义者 | Relating to Mahatma Gandhi, his beliefs, or his methods of nonviolent resistance.  |  例句：He led a Gandhian protest against the government. 他领导了一场甘地式的反政府抗议。 | 近义词：nonviolent, pacifist | 反义词：violent, aggressive | 词性: adjective

gandhian:/ɡɑːnˈdiən/ | n. | 甘地主义者；非暴力不合作主义者 | A follower of Mahatma Gandhi's beliefs and practices, especially those of nonviolent resistance. | 例句：She is a devout Gandhian. 她是一位虔诚的甘地主义者。 | 近义词：pacifist, nonviolent activist | 反义词：warmonger | 词性: noun

词源: From the proper noun "Gandhi" + "-an" (suffix forming adjectives and nouns meaning "related to," "characteristic of," or "a follower of").  Mahatma Gandhi (1869-1948) was a leader of the Indian independence movement.
