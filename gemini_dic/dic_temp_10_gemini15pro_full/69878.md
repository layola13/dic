ferryboat:/ˈferiˌboʊt/ (US) /ˈferiˌbəʊt/ (UK)| n. |渡船，渡轮|A boat used to transport passengers, vehicles, and goods across a body of water, especially a river or short stretch of sea.|We took the ferryboat across the river. 我们乘渡船过了河。|近义词：ferry, boat, ship|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'bridge' or 'tunnel' as alternatives to using a ferryboat) |noun
