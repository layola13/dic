flakiness:/ˈfleɪkinəs/| n.|薄片状，易碎性，古怪行为|The flakiness of the pastry made it difficult to handle.  这种糕点的易碎性使得它很难处理。|例句：The flakiness of his personality made it hard to rely on him. 他性格的古怪使人很难依赖他。|近义词：crumbliness, fragility, eccentricity, capriciousness|反义词：stability, consistency, reliability|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From flaky + -ness.  Flaky itself comes from flake, which has its roots in Old Norse *flaka, meaning "to burst, peel off."
