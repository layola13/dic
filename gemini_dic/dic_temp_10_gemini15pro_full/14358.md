autophoby:/ˌɔːtəˈfoʊbiə/ | n. | 孤独恐惧症；自闭症 | An abnormal and persistent fear of being alone or isolated.  A person with autophoby experiences intense anxiety and distress when left alone, even for short periods. |  She suffers from autophoby and can't even go to the grocery store by herself. 她患有孤独恐惧症，甚至不能独自去杂货店。

He developed autophoby after a traumatic experience of being locked in a closet as a child.  他在童年时期被锁在壁橱里的创伤经历之后患上了孤独恐惧症。 | 近义词: monophobia, isolophobia | 反义词:  N/A (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑社交依赖) | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From auto- (self) + -phobia (fear).  The prefix "auto-" comes from Greek "autos" meaning "self," and the suffix "-phobia" comes from Greek "phobos" meaning "fear" or "panic."
