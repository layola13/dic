cbt:/ˌsiː biː ˈtiː/| n. |认知行为疗法 (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy)|Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) is a type of psychotherapy that helps people learn how to identify and change destructive or disturbing thought patterns that have a negative influence on behavior and emotions. |She's been undergoing CBT for her anxiety. 她一直在接受焦虑症的认知行为疗法。|CBT is often used to treat depression and anxiety. 认知行为疗法常用于治疗抑郁症和焦虑症。|synonyms: psychotherapy, counseling, therapy|antonyms:  (CBT doesn't have a direct antonym, but one might consider unhelpful coping mechanisms or harmful therapies as opposites) |noun
