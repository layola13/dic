outinvents:/ˌaʊtɪnˈvɛnts/
verb
胜过发明；在发明方面超越
to invent something better than someone else
例句：Company A outinvents its competitors year after year. A公司年年在发明创造方面胜过其竞争对手。

例句：She outinvented everyone in her field with her groundbreaking new technology. 她凭借其突破性的新技术，在发明创造方面超越了该领域的所有人。

近义词：surpass, excel, outdo, outstrip
反义词：lag behind, fall behind
