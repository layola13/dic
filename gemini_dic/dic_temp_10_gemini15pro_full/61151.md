elritch: /ˈɛlrɪtʃ/ | adj. | 怪诞的，可怕的，令人毛骨悚然的 | Weird and sinister; strange and frightening, especially in a way that suggests the presence of supernatural powers. |  The elritch shriek echoed through the haunted house.  那怪诞的尖叫声回荡在鬼屋里。|  例句：The elritch glow of the moon cast eerie shadows on the graveyard.  怪诞的月光在墓地上投下怪异的阴影。| 近义词： eerie, uncanny, creepy, sinister, ghostly | 反义词： normal, ordinary, mundane | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Scottish and North English *elriche, alteration of earlier *elfrish, from elf + -rich "abounding in."  源自苏格兰语和北英格兰语 *elriche，早期 *elfrish 的变体，由 elf（精灵）+ -rich（丰富的）组成。
