Simple Present:
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do in her free time? 她空闲时间喜欢做什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she cooked for dinner? 她晚饭做了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading all afternoon? 他整个下午都在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? 她对这部电影说了些什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 开会的时候她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you eaten before you came here? 你来这里之前吃了什么？
What had she done before she left? 她走之前做了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? 我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  她换专业之前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do next weekend? 你下周末要做什么？
What will she wear to the party? 她会穿什么去参加派对？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be studying next semester? 她下学期会学什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你会完成什么？
What will she have finished by next week? 到下周她会完成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been learning by the time she graduates? 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习了什么多久了？


Subjunctive Mood:
If I knew what he wanted, I would tell you. 如果我知道他想要什么，我会告诉你的。
I wish I knew what to say. 我希望我知道该说些什么。


Passive Voice:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being built next to the library? 图书馆旁边正在建什么？


Interrogative Sentence:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the summer? 你暑假的计划是什么？

Negative Sentence:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you mean. 她不明白你的意思。

PHRASE->
What's up? 怎么了？/你好吗？
What the hell! 搞什么鬼！
What if it rains? 如果下雨怎么办？
What for? 为了什么？
No matter what happens, I'll be there for you. 无论发生什么，我都会在你身边。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English _hwæt_, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch _wat_ and German _was_.
