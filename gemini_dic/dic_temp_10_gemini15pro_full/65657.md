evaporometer:/ɪˌvæpəˈrɒmɪtər/ | n. | 蒸发计 | An instrument for measuring the rate of evaporation of water. | The evaporometer showed a high rate of evaporation due to the strong sun and wind.  蒸发计显示，由于强烈的阳光和风，蒸发率很高。|Synonyms: atmometer, evaporimeter | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for an instrument.  One might consider words related to condensation or precipitation like "condenser" or "rain gauge" as conceptually opposite processes, but not true antonyms.) | noun

evaporation:/ɪˌvæpəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 蒸发；消失 | The process of turning from liquid into vapor. | The evaporation of water from the lake cools the surrounding air. 湖水蒸发使周围的空气冷却。 | Synonyms: vaporization, dehydration, drying | Antonyms: condensation, precipitation | noun
