pan-fry: /ˈpænˌfraɪ/ | v. | 煎 | To cook food in a small amount of fat in a pan over moderately high heat. | I pan-fried the chicken breasts until they were golden brown. 我把鸡胸肉煎至金黄色。| 近义词: sauté, fry | 反义词: boil, steam | 词性: 动词
词源:  From "pan" + "fry".  "Pan" refers to the cooking vessel, and "fry" refers to the cooking method.
