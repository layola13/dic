ichthyophagous:/ˌɪkθiˈɒfəɡəs/| adj.|食鱼的，鱼食性的|Feeding on fish; fish-eating.  Derived from Greek *ikhthus* (fish) and *phagein* (to eat).|例句：Penguins are ichthyophagous birds.  企鹅是食鱼的鸟类。

例句：Many marine mammals are ichthyophagous. 许多海洋哺乳动物以鱼类为食。|近义词：piscivorous|反义词:herbivorous, carnivorous (if not specifically fish-eating)|词性:adjective
