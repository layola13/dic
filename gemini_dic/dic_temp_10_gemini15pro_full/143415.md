outflaring:/aʊtˈflɛərɪŋ/
| adj. |向外张开的，向外扩张的 | Spreading or widening outwards. | The outflaring skirt gave her a graceful silhouette. 这条向外张开的裙子给了她一个优雅的轮廓。| The outflaring petals of the hibiscus flower were vibrant red.  木槿花向外张开的花瓣是鲜艳的红色。 |近义词：expanding, spreading, widening |反义词：contracting, narrowing |词性:形容词

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "outward") + "flare" (verb meaning to spread outwards)  由表示“向外”的前缀 "out-" 和动词 "flare"（意为向外展开）组成。
