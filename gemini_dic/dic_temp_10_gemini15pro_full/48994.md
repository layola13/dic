deglorifies:/diːˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪz/
verb
使失去荣耀；贬低；使丢脸
to deprive of glory; to reduce in dignity or estimation.
The tabloids deglorified the actor with scandalous stories. 小报用丑闻故事贬低了这位演员。
Synonyms:  debase, demean, discredit, humble, disgrace
Antonyms: glorify, exalt, honor, praise
词性: 动词
