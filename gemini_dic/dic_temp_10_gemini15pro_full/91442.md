hygrine:/ˈhaɪɡriːn/
noun
毒藜碱；海克力碱
A pyrrolidine alkaloid found in coca leaves.
Hygrine is a secondary metabolite of the coca plant. 毒藜碱是古柯植物的次生代谢产物。
Hygrine is found in coca leaves along with other alkaloids like cocaine.  毒藜碱与可卡因等其他生物碱一起存在于古柯叶中。
