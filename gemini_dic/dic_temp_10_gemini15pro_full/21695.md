boaka:/ˈboʊəkə/| n.|蟒蛇（主要指蚺蛇，尤其在圭亚那）|A large constrictor snake, especially a boa, found chiefly in Guyana.|He saw a huge boaka slithering through the jungle. 他看到一条巨大的蟒蛇在丛林中滑行。|Synonyms: boa constrictor, anaconda|Antonyms: N/A|noun
Etymology:  Likely derived from a native South American language, possibly Tupi or Carib, referring to large snakes.  Further etymological research is needed for a definitive answer.|
