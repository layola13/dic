atonalism: /ˌeɪˈtoʊnəlɪzəm/ /ˌeɪˈtɒnəlɪzəm/
noun
无调性
A system of musical composition that avoids traditional harmonies and key signatures, using all 12 notes of the chromatic scale equally without establishing a tonic or key center.
Schoenberg's music is characterized by atonality.  勋伯格的音乐以无调性为特征。
The composer experimented with atonality in his later works.  这位作曲家在后期的作品中尝试了无调性。
Synonyms: atonality, dodecaphony, serialism
Antonyms: tonality
