campaigner:/kæmˈpeɪnər/ | n. | 运动者；活动家；竞选者 |  A person who works in an organized and active way towards a particular goal, typically a political or social one. |  Example 1: The campaigner worked tirelessly for environmental protection.  这位活动家不知疲倦地为环境保护工作。

Example 2:  She's a seasoned campaigner for human rights. 她是一位经验丰富的人权活动家。 | Synonym: activist, advocate, promoter, supporter | Antonym: opponent, adversary | Noun
