main-line:/ˈmeɪn laɪn/ | adj. n.| 主要的，干线的；主线，主要路线 |A principal route or system, especially of transportation (especially a railroad).  Relating to or being the principal route or system. |The main-line train arrived on time.  主线火车准时到达。|Synonyms: principal, primary, central, core, key|Antonyms: branch, subsidiary, secondary|adjective, noun

main-line:/ˈmeɪn laɪn/ | v.| (informal) 注射毒品（尤指海洛因）(inject (a drug, especially heroin) intravenously).|He mainlined heroin for years. 他注射海洛因很多年了。|Synonyms: inject, shoot up|Antonyms: abstain|verb
