nonsoporifics:/ˌnɒnsɒpəˈrɪfɪks/
noun
非安眠药，不致睡眠的药物
Substances that do not induce sleep.
Patients with insomnia may benefit from nonsoporifics to manage their anxiety without causing drowsiness. 失眠患者可能会受益于非安眠药来控制他们的焦虑，而不会引起嗜睡。
Synonyms: stimulants, invigorants
Antonyms: soporifics, hypnotics, sedatives
n.

Etymology:
From non- + soporific.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "soporific" comes from the Latin "sopor" (deep sleep) and "-fic" (making, causing).
