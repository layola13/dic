concreted:/ˈkɒŋkriːtɪd/ /ˈkɑːŋkriːtɪd/| v. |凝固，用混凝土浇筑|To make something solid or concrete, often literally by using concrete.  The pathway was concreted over.|近义词：solidified, cemented, paved|反义词: demolished, excavated|词性:动词

concreted:/ˈkɒŋkriːtɪd/ /ˈkɑːŋkriːtɪd/| adj. |用混凝土建造的，具体的|Made of concrete; or figurative meaning of definite and specific. The concreted driveway led to the garage.  /  We need a more concreted plan.|近义词：concrete, solid, definite|反义词: abstract, vague, indefinite|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the past participle of the verb "concrete," which itself comes from the Latin "concretus," meaning "grown together" or "solidified."
