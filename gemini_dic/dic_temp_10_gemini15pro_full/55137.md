dissatisfied:/dɪsˈsætɪsfaɪd/ | adj. | 不满意的，不满足的 | Not satisfied; discontented. |  She was dissatisfied with the service at the hotel. 她对酒店的服务不满意。| synonyms: discontented, disgruntled, unhappy | antonyms: satisfied, content, pleased | adjective
