corroborators:/kəˈrɑːbəˌreɪtərz/
n.
证实者，确证者
People who support a statement, theory, or finding with confirming evidence.
Several corroborators came forward to support her alibi.  几位证实者出面支持她的不在场证明。
synonyms: witnesses, verifiers, confirmers, supporters, validators
antonyms: contradictors, refuters, challengers, deniers
noun


corroborate:/kəˈrɑːbəˌreɪt/
v.
证实，确证
To confirm or give support to (a statement, theory, or finding).
The evidence corroborates his story.  证据证实了他的说法。
synonyms: confirm, verify, validate, substantiate, authenticate
antonyms: contradict, refute, challenge, deny
verb
