nonstrategic:/ˌnɒnstrəˈtiːdʒɪk/ | adj. | 非战略性的 | Not essential to a plan or strategy; not of strategic importance. |  The company decided to sell its nonstrategic assets.  公司决定出售其非战略性资产。 | Example: The decision to close the small branch was nonstrategic, based purely on local economics.  决定关闭这家小分店并非战略性的，纯粹是基于当地经济状况。 | Synonyms: non-tactical, unimportant, insignificant, peripheral | Antonyms: strategic, crucial, vital, essential | word type: adjective
