overmixt: /ˌoʊvərˈmɪkst/
adjective
过度混合的
mixed excessively; too thoroughly mixed.
The batter was overmixt and the cake was dense. 面糊搅拌过度了，所以蛋糕很硬。
The colors in the painting were overmixt, resulting in a muddy brown. 画中的颜色混合得太过头了，结果变成了一种浑浊的棕色。
近义词: overblended, overmixed
反义词: undermixed
