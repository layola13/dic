disingenuous:/ˌdɪsɪnˈdʒenjʊəs/
adjective
不真诚的，不坦率的
Not candid or sincere, typically by pretending that one knows less about something than one really does.
She gave a disingenuous answer to the question, clearly hiding something. 她对这个问题的回答很不真诚，显然隐瞒了一些事情。

The salesman's disingenuous smile made me doubt his promises.  销售员不真诚的笑容让我怀疑他的承诺。

近义词: insincere, dishonest, deceitful, mendacious, duplicitous, artful, two-faced, hypocritical
反义词: sincere, honest, candid, frank, genuine, straightforward, artless
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + ingenuous (frank, innocent, artless).  The word "ingenuous" comes from Latin "ingenuus" meaning "native, freeborn, noble, frank, open."
