amoebous:/əˈmiːbəs/| adj.|变形虫的；无定形的|Resembling an amoeba in having a changing shape or form; amorphous.|The amoebous shape of the slime mold pulsed slowly. 这团黏菌的变形虫状形态缓慢地跳动着。|Synonyms: amorphous, shapeless, formless, undefined, indeterminate|Antonyms: definite, fixed, rigid, shaped, structured|adjective
