choroid:/ˈkɔːrɔɪd/ | n. |脉络膜|The choroid is the vascular layer of the eye, containing connective tissue, and lying between the retina and the sclera. 脉络膜是眼球的血管层，包含结缔组织，位于视网膜和巩膜之间。|The choroid provides oxygen and nutrients to the outer layers of the retina. 脉络膜为视网膜的外层提供氧气和营养。|choriocapillaris|None|noun
