continuum:/kənˈtɪnjuəm/ | n. |连续体；统一体|a continuous sequence in which adjacent elements are not perceptibly different from each other, although the extremes are quite distinct|The spectrum of visible light is a continuum.|近义词：spectrum, range, scale, progression, sequence|反义词：discontinuity, break, interruption|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Latin *continuum*, neuter of *continuus* ("continuous").
