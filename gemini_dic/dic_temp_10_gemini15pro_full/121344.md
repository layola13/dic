misgovern:/ˌmɪsˈɡʌvərn/
verb
统治不善；管理不当
to govern badly or wrongly
The country was misgoverned for years by a corrupt dictator.  这个国家被一个腐败的独裁者统治不善了很多年。
The company was misgoverned and eventually went bankrupt.  这家公司管理不善，最终破产了。
mismanage, misrule
govern well, administer efficiently
verb
