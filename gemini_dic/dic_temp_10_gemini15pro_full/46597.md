damasked:/ˈdæməskt/ | adj. | 锦缎似的，有锦缎花纹的；大马士革钢的 | Woven with or resembling a pattern of damask; made of damask steel. | The tablecloth was damasked with intricate floral designs.  桌布上有复杂的锦缎花纹。|  The knight wielded a damasked sword of legendary sharpness. 那位骑士挥舞着一把锋利无比的大马士革钢剑。 | 近义词: patterned, brocaded, figured | 反义词: plain, unpatterned | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle French *damasqué*, past participle of *damasquer* (“to decorate with damask”), from Old French *damaschin*, ultimately from Damascus, Syria, where such fabrics were originally made.
