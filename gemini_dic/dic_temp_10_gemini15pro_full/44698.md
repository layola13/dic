cruder:/ˈkruːdər/ | adj. | 更粗糙的，更粗俗的，更原始的 | More raw, unrefined, or primitive than something else; less sophisticated or developed.  | The first version of the software was much cruder than the final product.  软件的第一个版本比最终产品粗糙得多。 |  The tools they used were cruder than ours. 他们使用的工具比我们的更原始。| Synonyms: coarser, rougher, simpler, less refined, less sophisticated | Antonyms: finer, smoother, more refined, more sophisticated | adjective

cruder:/ˈkruːdər/ | adj. | 更残酷的，更粗暴的 | More blunt, harsh, or offensive. | His jokes were cruder than I expected. 他的笑话比我想象的更粗俗。|  He made some cruder remarks about her appearance. 他对她的外貌进行了一些粗鲁的评论。 | Synonyms: harsher, blunter, more offensive,  ruder, vulgar | Antonyms: gentler, kinder, more polite, more refined | adjective
