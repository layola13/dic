bricked:/brɪkt/| adj.|变砖的，无法使用的，损坏的|Rendered unusable, like a brick; typically used to describe electronic devices that have become unresponsive or permanently damaged due to failed software updates or other malfunctions.| My phone is bricked after I tried to install the custom ROM. 我的手机在尝试安装自定义ROM后变砖了。| My old gaming console got bricked years ago. 我的旧游戏机几年前就变砖了。|近义词：broken, damaged, unusable, inoperable|反义词：functional, working|词性:形容词

bricked:/brɪkt/| v.|使变砖，使无法使用|To render (an electronic device) unusable, like a brick; usually due to a software or firmware issue.| I bricked my phone by installing a corrupted operating system. 我因为安装了损坏的操作系统而把手机弄坏了。| He bricked his router while trying to upgrade the firmware. 他在尝试升级固件时把路由器弄坏了。|近义词：break, damage, ruin, disable|反义词：repair, fix|词性:动词
