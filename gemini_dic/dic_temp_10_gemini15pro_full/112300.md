lord-like:/ˈlɔːrdˌlaɪk/| adj.|像领主的；贵族气派的；专横的|Resembling or characteristic of a lord, especially in manner or appearance; haughty or domineering.|He had a lord-like manner, expecting everyone to obey his commands. 他有一种领主般的举止，期望每个人都服从他的命令。|The mansion was decorated in a lord-like fashion. 这座宅邸以贵族气派的方式装饰。|近义词：aristocratic, noble, haughty, imperious, domineering|反义词：humble, meek, subservient|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From 'lord' (Old English *hlāford, meaning "loaf-keeper," from hlāf "loaf" + weard "keeper") + '-like' (Old English -līc, meaning "having the form or appearance of").
