fufu:/ˈfuːfuː/| n.|富富（西非一种淀粉类食物）|A staple food in West Africa, made from boiled starchy vegetables such as cassava, yams, or plantains, that are pounded into a dough-like consistency.|Fufu is often served with stews and soups. 富富通常与炖菜和汤一起食用。|近义词：dumpling, dough|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "liquid food")|词性:noun
