hyperbatic:/ˌhaɪpərˈbætɪk/ | adj. | 倒装的，修辞倒装的 |  Characterized by hyperbaton, a figure of speech in which the usual or expected word order is inverted, often for emphasis or poetic effect. |  "Her eyes, how they shone!" is a hyperbatic expression.  她闪耀的双眼！这是一个倒装句。 |  inverted, transposed, anomalistic | normal, regular | adjective
