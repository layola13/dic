deputy:/ˈdepjuti/ (US), /ˈdɛpjʊti/ (UK)| n. adj.| 副手，代理人；代表；议员 | a person who is appointed to undertake the duties of a superior in the superior's absence or to assist the superior.  A deputy has the power to act for another person. | The sheriff's deputy arrested the suspect.  治安官的副手逮捕了嫌疑人。 | synonyms: assistant, substitute, representative, agent, delegate | antonyms: chief, leader, principal, head | noun

deputy:/ˈdepjuti/ (US), /ˈdɛpjʊti/ (UK)| adj.| 副的，代理的| acting or serving in place of another, especially of a higher rank. | He was appointed deputy manager. 他被任命为副经理。| synonyms:  assistant, acting, substitute, second-in-command | antonyms: chief, head, principal | adjective
