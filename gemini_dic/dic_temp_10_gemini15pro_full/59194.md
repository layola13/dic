dyspepsia:/dɪsˈpɛpsiə/ /dɪsˈpepsiə/ | n. |消化不良，胃功能紊乱| Indigestion; impaired digestion.  Characterized by vague abdominal discomfort, a feeling of fullness after meals, heartburn, nausea, and other symptoms. |  He suffered from dyspepsia after eating too much rich food. 他吃了太多油腻的食物后消化不良。| synonyms: indigestion, heartburn, upset stomach, acid reflux | antonyms: eupepsia (good digestion - less common) | noun
