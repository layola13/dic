inclemently:/ɪnˈklɛməntli/| adv.| 严酷地；恶劣地|In a harsh or severe manner, typically referring to weather.|The game was postponed due to the inclemently cold weather.  由于天气严寒，比赛被推迟了。|近义词：harshly, severely, roughly, rigorously|反义词: mildly, gently, calmly|词性:副词
Etymology: From in- (not) + clement (mild, merciful) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin clemens (mild).
