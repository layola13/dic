nais: /neɪs/ /naɪs/ | adj. |美好的，愉快的，友善的 | pleasing, agreeable, kind | Example: We had a nice time at the beach. 我们在海滩玩得很愉快。

Example: She is a very nice person. 她是一个非常友善的人。

近义词: pleasant, agreeable, good, kind, friendly, lovely, enjoyable, delightful

反义词: unpleasant, disagreeable, bad, unkind, unfriendly, nasty, horrible

词性: adjective

词源: Middle English: via Old French from Latin nescius ‘ignorant’, from ne- ‘not’ + scire ‘know’. The original sense was ‘foolish, silly’, later ‘coy, shy’, then ‘fastidious, delicate’, giving rise to ‘agreeable, pleasant’.
