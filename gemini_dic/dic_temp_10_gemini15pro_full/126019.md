musrole: /ˈmʌsroʊl/ | n. | 菌根 |A symbiotic association between a fungus and the roots of a vascular plant.| The musrole helps the tree absorb nutrients from the soil. 菌根帮助树木从土壤中吸收养分。 | synonyms: mycorrhiza | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From myco- (fungus) + -rrhiza (root). Ultimately from Greek *mykēs* (fungus) + *rhiza* (root).
