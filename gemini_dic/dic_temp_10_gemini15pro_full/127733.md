negotiability:/nɪˌɡoʊʃiəˈbɪləti/

例句：Checking the negotiability of the instrument is crucial before accepting it. 检查票据的可转让性在接受之前至关重要。

近义词：transferability, assignability
反义词：non-negotiability, non-transferability
词性: noun
