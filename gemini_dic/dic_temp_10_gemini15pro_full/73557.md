forestallers:/fɔːrˈstɔːlərz/
| n. | 预防者；阻碍者 | People or things that forestall something; those who prevent something from happening by taking action ahead of time. | The forestallers of the rebellion were quickly imprisoned.  叛乱的阻碍者很快就被囚禁了。 | The company acted as forestallers, buying up all the available land to prevent their competitors from expanding. 这家公司充当了阻碍者的角色，买下了所有可用的土地，以阻止竞争对手扩张。 |  synonyms: preventers, obstructors, anticipators | antonyms: facilitators, enablers | noun
