congenialities:/kənˌdʒiːniˈælɪtiz/

Plural form of congeniality.|例句：The warm congenialities between the two families made the holiday gathering a joyful occasion. 两个家庭之间的融洽氛围让节日聚会成为一个欢乐的场合。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *congenialitas, from Latin congenialis ("of the same nature, kindred"), from com- ("with") + genialis ("of or belonging to birth, genial") + -itas (noun-forming suffix).
