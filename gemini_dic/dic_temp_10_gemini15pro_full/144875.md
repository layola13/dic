over-refreshed: /ˌoʊvər rɪˈfreʃt/ | adj. | 过度精神焕发的；宿醉未醒的 | Having had too much sleep, resulting in grogginess or disorientation. It can also humorously refer to being hungover. |  I felt over-refreshed after sleeping for 12 hours and woke up feeling groggy. 我睡了12个小时后感觉过度精神焕发，醒来时昏昏沉沉的。|  He stumbled into work, claiming to be over-refreshed after a stag night. 他踉踉跄跄地走进办公室，声称在单身派对后宿醉未醒。 | 近义词:  groggy, sluggish, hungover (in the humorous sense) | 反义词:  alert, refreshed, invigorated | 词性: adjective
