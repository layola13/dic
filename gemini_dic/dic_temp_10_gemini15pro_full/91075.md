hutlike:/ˈhʌtlaɪk/
adjective
像小屋的, 简陋的
Resembling a hut; small and simple.
The hermit lived in a hutlike dwelling deep in the woods.  隐士住在森林深处的一个简陋的住所。
The building was small and hutlike, barely offering protection from the elements. 这栋建筑物很小，像个小屋，几乎不能抵御风雨。
近义词:small, simple, basic, primitive, rudimentary
反义词:large, grand, luxurious, elaborate
词性:adjective
