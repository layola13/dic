debatements:/dɪˈbeɪtmənts/
noun
辩论；争论；讨论(较正式，常指正式的辩论)
A formal discussion of a subject in which opposing arguments are put forward.
The debatements concerning the new policy lasted for weeks.  关于新政策的辩论持续了数周。
The city council held lengthy debatements on the proposed budget cuts. 市议会就拟议的预算削减举行了冗长的辩论。
synonyms: debates, discussions, arguments, disputes
antonyms: agreements, accords, harmonies
n.
