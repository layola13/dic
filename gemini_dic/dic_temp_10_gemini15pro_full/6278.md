amorally:/eɪˈmɔːrəli/
adverb
不道德地；无道德感地
Without moral principles; in a way that shows no concern about whether something is right or wrong.
He acted amorally when he stole from the charity. 他从慈善机构偷窃，行为不道德。
近义词:immorally, unethically, wickedly
反义词:morally, ethically, righteously
词性:adverb

词源: a-(not) + moral + -ly (adverbial suffix).  From Latin *amoralis*, from a- "not" + moralis "moral".
