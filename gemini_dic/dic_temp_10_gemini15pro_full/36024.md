codirect:/ˌkoʊdaɪˈrɛkt/ | v. | 共同指导，共同执导 | To direct or manage something jointly with another or others. | Example: They codirected the film, sharing responsibilities for all creative decisions. 他们共同执导了这部电影，共同承担所有创作决策的责任。 | Example: The project was codirected by two senior managers, ensuring its successful completion. 该项目由两位高级经理共同指导，确保其顺利完成。 | 近义词: co-manage, collaborate on, jointly direct | 反义词: solely direct, independently manage | 词性: verb

codirect:/ˌkoʊdaɪˈrɛkt/ | adj. | 共同指导的，共同执导的 | Directed or managed jointly with another or others. | Example: The codirect approach to leadership fostered a sense of shared ownership among team members. 共同领导的方式在团队成员中培养了一种共同所有权的意识。 | Example:  The play was a codirect effort by two talented individuals.  这出戏是两位才华横溢的人共同执导的作品。 | 近义词: jointly directed, co-managed | 反义词: solely directed, independently managed | 词性: adjective


词源:  "co-" (prefix meaning "together" or "jointly") + "direct"
