outnoise:/ˌaʊtˈnɔɪz/
verb
盖过…的声音；发出比…更大的噪音
to make more noise than (something else) so that it cannot be heard
The band easily outnoised the crowd. 乐队的音量轻易盖过了人群的嘈杂声。
近义词: drown out, overpower
反义词: hush, silence
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + noise.  First recorded in the early 17th century.
