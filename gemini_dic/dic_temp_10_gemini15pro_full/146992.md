overkill:/ˈəʊvəˌkɪl/ (UK), /ˈoʊvərˌkɪl/ (US)| n. | 过度杀伤；过分；矫枉过正 |More than is necessary; excessive.  It derives from "over" and "kill".  Originally used in a military context to describe excessive firepower, it has broadened to mean any kind of excess.|例句：Bringing a tank to a knife fight is overkill. 带坦克去参加刀战是杀鸡用牛刀。

例句：Wearing a ball gown to the grocery store is overkill. 穿着晚礼服去杂货店太过了。|近义词：excess, surplus, superfluity, extravagance|反义词:  insufficiency, inadequacy, understatement|词性:noun

overkill:/ˈəʊvəˌkɪl/ (UK), /ˈoʊvərˌkɪl/ (US)| adj. | 过度的；过分的 |Excessively powerful or elaborate.|例句：The security measures seemed a bit overkill.  安全措施似乎有点过分了。

例句：His reaction was totally overkill. 他的反应完全是过激了。|近义词：excessive, extravagant, immoderate|反义词: insufficient, inadequate, moderate|词性:adjective
