biospheric:/ˌbaɪəˈsfɪrɪk/ | adj. | 生物圈的 | Relating to the biosphere, the regions of the surface and atmosphere of the Earth or another planet occupied by living organisms. |  The biospheric effects of pollution are far-reaching. 污染对生物圈的影响是深远的。|  Global warming has detrimental biospheric consequences. 全球变暖对生物圈有不利的影响。| 近义词: ecological, environmental | 反义词: abiotic, non-living | 词性: adjective

biosphere: /ˈbaɪoʊsfɪr/ | n. | 生物圈 | The regions of the surface and atmosphere of the Earth or another planet occupied by living organisms. | The biosphere is a complex and interconnected system. 生物圈是一个复杂且相互关联的系统。| Protecting the biosphere is crucial for the survival of all life. 保护生物圈对于所有生命的生存至关重要。| 近义词: ecosphere | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
