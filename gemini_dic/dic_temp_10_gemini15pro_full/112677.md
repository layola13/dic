low-grade:/ˌloʊˈɡreɪd/| adj.|低级的；低档的；劣质的；轻微的|Of inferior quality; of a lower grade, rank, or level. Also, (of a disease) mild or not very advanced.|The doctor diagnosed a low-grade fever.|近义词：inferior, substandard, poor, mild|反义词:high-grade, superior, excellent|词性:adjective
词源: "low" + "grade" -  "low" from Old English "lāg" (low), "grade" from Latin "gradus" (step, degree).
