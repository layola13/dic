confusable:/kənˈfjuːzəbəl/ | adj. | 易混淆的 |  Likely to be confused or mistaken for something else. | It's easy to get these two confusable words mixed up. 这两个易混淆的单词很容易被混淆。| 近义词: misleading, ambiguous, similar | 反义词: distinct, clear, unambiguous | 词性: 形容词

confuse:/kənˈfjuːz/ | v. | 使困惑，使混淆 | To make someone unable to think clearly or understand something. | The complicated instructions confused me. 复杂的说明把我弄糊涂了。| 近义词: bewilder, perplex, disorient | 反义词: clarify, explain, enlighten | 词性: 动词

confusion:/kənˈfjuːʒən/ | n. | 困惑，混乱 |  A state of being unable to think clearly or understand something; a lack of order or clarity. | There was a lot of confusion about the new regulations. 新规定引起了很多困惑。| 近义词: disorder, chaos, disarray | 反义词: order, clarity, organization | 词性: 名词
