noncultivability:/ˌnɒnkʌltɪvəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可培养性
The inability of a microorganism to grow in standard laboratory culture media.
The noncultivability of many environmental bacteria poses a challenge for researchers.  许多环境细菌的不可培养性对研究人员构成了挑战。
