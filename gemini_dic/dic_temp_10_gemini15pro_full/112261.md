loped:/loʊpt/ | v. | 慢跑，轻快地大步跑 |  To run or move with a long bounding stride. | The horse loped across the field. 马儿轻快地跑过田野。| The dog loped happily towards its owner. 狗开心地朝主人跑去。 |近义词：cantered, trotted, bounded |反义词：sprinted, raced |词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *lopen, from Old Norse *hlaupa (“to run, leap”), from Proto-Germanic *hlaupaną (“to run, leap, jump”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)lewə- (“to flow, run”). Cognate with Icelandic hlaupa (“to run”), Swedish löpa (“to run”), German laufen (“to run, walk”).
