ape's:/eɪps/ | n. | 猿类的，猿的 | Something that belongs to or is associated with an ape. | The ape's long arms helped it swing through the trees. 猿的长臂帮助它在树间荡来荡去。 | Synonyms: simian's, primate's, monkey's | Antonyms: human's | possessive pronoun


Etymology:
From Old English "apa" (ape), from Proto-Germanic "*apon" (ape), ultimately from a word related to Greek "kepos" (garden, monkey). The "-'s" is the possessive suffix.
