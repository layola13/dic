flavorous:/ˈfleɪvərəs/| adj.|美味的，有味道的|Having a distinct and pleasing flavor; savory.  Full of flavor; tasty.|例句：The flavorous stew simmered on the stove, filling the house with a delicious aroma.  美味的炖菜在炉子上煨着，满屋都是香味。|近义词：tasty, delicious, savory, palatable, flavorful|反义词：bland, tasteless, insipid|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From flavor + -ous.  "Flavor" comes from Old French *flaor, ultimately from Latin *flator, "a blowing, blast," related to flare.  The suffix "-ous" is of Latin origin and forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "possessing the qualities of."
