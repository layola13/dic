melaena:/məˈliːnə/ /mɪˈlinə/| n. |黑便|Dark, tarry stools containing partly digested blood, usually indicating bleeding in the upper part of the digestive tract.|The patient presented with melaena and abdominal pain. 病人出现黑便和腹痛。|The doctor suspected a bleeding ulcer after observing the patient's melaena. 医生观察到病人的黑便后怀疑是出血性溃疡。|近义词：black stool, tarry stool|反义词:hematochezia (bright red blood in stool)|词性:noun
