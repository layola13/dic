damselfly:/ˈdæmzəlˌflaɪ/ (US), /ˈdæmzəlflaɪ/ (UK)| n. |豆娘；蟌|A slender-bodied insect similar to a dragonfly but having wings that are held along the body when at rest. |例句：A damselfly flitted among the reeds by the pond. 一只豆娘在池塘边的芦苇丛中飞来飞去。

例句：The iridescent wings of the damselfly shimmered in the sunlight. 豆娘的彩虹色翅膀在阳光下闪闪发光。

近义词：dragonfly|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
