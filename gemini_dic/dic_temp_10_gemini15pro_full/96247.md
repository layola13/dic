indicative:/ɪnˈdɪkətɪv/ | adj. |指示的，象征的，表示...的 | Serving as a sign or indication of something; expressive or suggestive of something. | Her smile was indicative of her happiness. 她的笑容表明她很开心。|  Synonyms: suggestive, expressive, symptomatic, symbolic | Antonyms: concealing, hiding, masking | adjective

indicative:/ɪnˈdɪkətɪv/ | n. |陈述语气 | The mood of a verb that is used to make statements and ask questions. |  He speaks in the indicative mood. 他用陈述语气说话。| Synonyms: declarative | Antonyms: subjunctive, imperative | noun
