anthropometric:/ˌænθrəpəˈmetrɪk/
adjective
人体测量的
Relating to the measurement of the physical characteristics of the human body, such as height, weight, and limb length.
Anthropometric data is used in designing ergonomic furniture.  人体测量数据用于设计符合人体工程学的家具。
Anthropometric measurements are crucial for understanding human variation. 人体测量对于理解人类变异至关重要。
