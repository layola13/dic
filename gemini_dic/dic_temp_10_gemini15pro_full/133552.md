nonfinitely:/nɒnˈfaɪnaɪtli/ /nɑːnˈfaɪnaɪtli/| adv.|非限定地，无穷尽地|Not finitely; without limits; endlessly.|The possibilities are nonfinitely many. 可能性是无穷无尽的.|近义词：infinitely, endlessly, limitlessly|反义词: finitely, limitedly|词性:副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
finite (adjective): from Latin finitus "limited, bounded," past participle of finire "to limit, set bounds," from finis "boundary, end"
-ly (suffix): adverbial suffix
