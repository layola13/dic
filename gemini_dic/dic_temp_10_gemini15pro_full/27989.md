canvasser:/ˈkænvəsər/| n.|游说者；推销员；拉票员|A person who solicits votes, orders, subscriptions, opinions, or the like.|The canvasser went door to door trying to persuade people to vote for his candidate.|Synonyms: solicitor, campaigner, agent|Antonyms: opponent, objector|noun

canvass:/ˈkænvəs/| v. n.|游说；拉票；细查|To solicit votes, subscriptions, opinions, or the like; to examine carefully or discuss thoroughly.|They canvassed the neighborhood for the upcoming election. 他们在这个社区为即将举行的选举拉票。|Synonyms: solicit, poll, survey, scrutinize|Antonyms: ignore, neglect|verb, noun
