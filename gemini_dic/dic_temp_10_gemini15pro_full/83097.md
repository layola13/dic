gusset:/ˈɡʌsɪt/| n. |三角形衬布；腋下三角形插布；角撑板|A piece of material sewn into a garment to strengthen or enlarge it, such as at the underarm or crotch of trousers.|The gussets under the arms of the shirt allow for greater freedom of movement. 衬衫腋下的三角形衬布可以提供更大的活动自由。|Example 1: The tent's gussets provided extra stability in strong winds.  帐篷的角撑板在强风中提供了额外的稳定性。

Example 2: The gussets in the jeans prevent ripping at the seams. 牛仔裤上的三角形衬布防止接缝处撕裂。|Synonyms: insert, reinforcement, patch, panel|Antonyms: N/A (no direct antonym)|noun
