bipinnately:/baɪˈpɪnɪtli/
adjective
二回羽状的
Twice pinnately divided; having leaflets arranged along either side of a central axis, which leaflets are themselves pinnately divided.
The fern's leaves were bipinnately compound, creating a delicate, lacy appearance.  这种蕨类植物的叶子是二回羽状复叶，形成精致的蕾丝状外观。
The bipinnately structured foliage provided ample shade.  二回羽状结构的叶子提供了充足的阴凉。
synonyms:  bipinnate
antonyms:  simple (leaf structure)
词性: adjective
