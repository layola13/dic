de-integrates:/diːˈɪntɪɡreɪts/
|v.|分解，瓦解，崩溃|To break apart or decompose into constituent elements; to lose cohesion or unity.|The space station de-integrated upon re-entry into Earth's atmosphere.  空间站在重返地球大气层时解体了。|The alliance de-integrated after years of internal conflict.  联盟在多年的内部冲突后瓦解了。|近义词：disintegrate, decompose, break down, fall apart|反义词：integrate, unite, combine|词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + integrate (from Latin integratus, past participle of integrare "to make whole").
