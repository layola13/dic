iconolater:/aɪˈkɒnəleɪtər/ | n. |圣像崇拜者；偶像崇拜者 | A person who worships icons or images.  A person who excessively admires or reveres someone or something. |  John was accused of being an iconolater because of his extensive collection of religious artifacts.  约翰因为他收藏了大量的宗教文物而被指责为圣像崇拜者。

| 近义词: idolater, worshipper, devotee | 反义词: iconoclast | 词性: noun

iconolater:/aɪˈkɒnəleɪtər/ | n. |偶像崇拜者 | A person who excessively admires or reveres someone or something. | She's such an iconolater of that movie star; she has posters all over her room. 她真是那个电影明星的狂热崇拜者，她的房间里贴满了海报。 | 近义词:  fanatic, admirer | 反义词: critic, detractor | 词性: noun
