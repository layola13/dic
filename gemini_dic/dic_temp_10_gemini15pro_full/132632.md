nondiscernments:/ˌnɒndɪˈsɜːnmənts/| n. | عدم辨别; 无法区分 | Lack of discernment; inability to distinguish or perceive differences. |  The artist's nondiscernments in color led to a muddy, indistinct painting. 这位艺术家对颜色的辨别力不足，导致画作浑浊不清。

Their nondiscernments between right and wrong caused them to make poor choices. 他们无法区分是非，导致他们做了错误的选择。

| 近义词: lack of discrimination, inability to differentiate, blindness, obtuseness | 反义词: discernment, discrimination, perception, insight, astuteness | 词性: 名词
