notum:/ˈnəʊtəm/ | n. | 背板；背甲 | The dorsal part of an insect's thorax; the tergum of the mesothorax, or sometimes the terga of the meso- and metathorax together. | The beetle's brightly coloured notum was clearly visible.  甲虫颜色鲜艳的背板清晰可见。 | synonyms: tergum, dorsal plate | antonyms: sternum, ventral plate | noun
