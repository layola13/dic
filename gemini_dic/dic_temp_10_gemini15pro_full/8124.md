anti-austrian:/ˌæntiˈɔːstriən/ | adj. | 反紧缩的，反对紧缩政策的 | Opposed to the economic policies of austerity, typically involving reduced government spending and increased taxes, often associated with the Austrian School of economics. |  The anti-austrian protests gained momentum as unemployment rose. 反紧缩抗议随着失业率上升而势头增强。|  The article presented an anti-austrian argument against cutting public spending. 这篇文章提出了一个反对削减公共开支的反紧缩论点。 | Synonyms: anti-austerity, Keynesian (sometimes) | Antonyms: pro-austerity,  austere (in a fiscal sense) | adjective
