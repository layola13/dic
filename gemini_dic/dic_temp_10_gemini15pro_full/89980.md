horsemanship: /ˈhɔːrsmənʃɪp/ | n. | 马术；骑术 |  Skill in riding and handling horses.  The art or practice of riding and managing horses. |  She demonstrated excellent horsemanship in the competition. 她在比赛中展现了出色的马术。| synonyms: riding skill, equestrianism, equitation | antonyms: incompetence (in riding), ineptitude (in riding) | noun

Etymology: From "horseman" + "-ship". "Horseman" comes from Old English "horsmann," meaning "horseman," from "hors" (horse) + "mann" (man). "-ship" is a suffix denoting skill, art, or state of being.
