decennial:/dɪˈsɛniəl/
adjective
十年的；每十年的；十年一次的
Occurring or being done every ten years.
例句：The decennial census helps the government understand demographic changes.  十年一次的人口普查帮助政府了解人口变化。

例句：The town is preparing for its decennial celebration.  这个小镇正在准备它的十年庆典。
近义词：ten-year, decuple
反义词：annual, yearly
词性: adjective


decennial:/dɪˈsɛniəl/
noun
十年纪念；十周年纪念
A tenth anniversary or its celebration.
例句：The museum marked its decennial with a special exhibition.  博物馆以一个特别展览来纪念它的十周年。

例句：The school's decennial was a grand affair. 学校的十年庆典是一场盛大的活动。
近义词：tenth anniversary
反义词：N/A
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin decennis ("ten years old"), from decem ("ten") + annus ("year").
