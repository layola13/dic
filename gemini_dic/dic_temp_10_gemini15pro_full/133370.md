nonextractions:/ˌnoʊnɪkˈstrækʃənz/| n.|无提取物，无萃取物|A substance or product from which no components have been extracted.  It refers to a state where nothing has been removed or separated from the original source.|This product contains nonextractions, ensuring its purity.|近义词:whole, complete, unprocessed|反义词:extract, concentrate|词性:名词
