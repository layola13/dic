covetousness:/ˈkʌvɪtəsnəs/
noun
贪婪，贪欲
An excessive or inordinate desire to possess something, typically something belonging to someone else.
He was consumed by covetousness and wanted all the land for himself. 他被贪婪吞噬，想要所有土地都归自己所有。
The king's covetousness for power led to his downfall. 国王对权力的贪婪导致了他的垮台。
greed, avarice, rapacity
generosity, contentment, altruism
