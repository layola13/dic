multi-cultural:/ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəl/ | adj. | 多元文化的 | Relating to or containing several different cultures. |  This city is a multi-cultural hub, attracting people from all over the world.  这座城市是一个多元文化的中心，吸引着来自世界各地的人们。

multi-cultural:/ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəl/ | adj. | 多元文化的 |  Embracing or representative of multiple cultures. | The school promotes a multi-cultural environment where students can learn about different traditions. 这所学校提倡多元文化环境，学生可以在这里学习不同的传统。


multi-cultural:| adj. | 多元文化的 |  Characterized by the presence of multiple cultural groups within a society. |  The multi-cultural nature of the neighborhood is reflected in its diverse restaurants and shops. 该社区的多元文化性质体现在其多样化的餐馆和商店中。


multi-cultural:| adj. | 多元文化的 |  Designed to accommodate or appreciate diverse cultures. |  The museum's multi-cultural exhibit showcases art from around the globe.  博物馆的多元文化展览展示了来自世界各地的艺术品。

近义词: diverse, cosmopolitan, pluralistic
反义词: monocultural, homogeneous
词性: adjective

词源: multi- (many) + cultural (relating to culture)
