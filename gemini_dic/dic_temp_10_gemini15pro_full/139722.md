oches:/ˈoʊtʃɪz/ /ˈɔːtʃɪz/ | n. |痛处，疼痛(复数) | Aches are dull, persistent pains, often in muscles or joints.  | Example: My muscles aches after the marathon. 马拉松后我的肌肉酸痛。 | Example: She complained of aches and pains all over her body. 她抱怨全身疼痛。| Synonym: pains, soreness, twinges | Antonym: comfort, relief | noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *aches, plural of ache, from Old English acan, akin to Old High German acchen. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *akjaną, from Proto-Indo-European *agʰ- (“to drive, draw, lead”).
