contributors:/kənˈtrɪbjətərz/ | n. | 贡献者；投稿者；促成因素 | People or things that contribute something, especially money or time, to a cause or project. |  The contributors to the project were recognized for their hard work.  项目贡献者因其辛勤工作而受到表彰。| synonyms: donors, supporters, benefactors | antonyms: detractors, opponents | noun

Etymology:  Late Middle English: from contribute + -or.
