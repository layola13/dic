outdragged:/ˌaʊtˈdræɡd/
verb
超越，拖得更远
to have dragged something farther or for a longer time than someone or something else.  To have surpassed in dragging.
The team outdragged their opponents in the tug-of-war.  该队在拔河比赛中胜过了对手。
He outdragged the heavy box across the floor. 他把沉重的箱子拖过地板。
Synonyms: outpulled, outmatched, surpassed, bested
Antonyms: lost to, trailed, lagged behind
词性:动词
