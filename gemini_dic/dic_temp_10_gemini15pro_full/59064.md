dwen:/dwɛn/ | n. |  笨蛋，傻瓜 | A foolish or stupid person. | He's such a dwen, he locked his keys in the car again. 他真是个笨蛋，他又把钥匙锁在车里了。 | synonyms: fool, idiot, dolt, nitwit | antonyms: genius, mastermind | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
Likely a variant of "dwane," itself a variant of "dwain," from Old Norse *dveinn meaning "sluggish," "faint," or "pining." The term has evolved over time to primarily denote foolishness or stupidity.
