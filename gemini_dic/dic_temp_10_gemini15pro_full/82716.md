guidances:/ˈɡaɪdənsɪz/
n.
指导，引导
advice or information aimed at resolving a problem or difficulty, especially as given by someone in authority.
The student sought guidance from her professor about her research project.  这个学生向她的教授寻求关于她研究项目的指导。
counseling, advice, direction, support
misguidance, neglect
