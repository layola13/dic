morricer:/ˈmɒrɪsər/| n. |舞者，尤其指在莫里斯舞中表演的舞者|A dancer, especially one who performs a Morris dance.|例句：The morricers danced with bells on their legs. 舞者们腿上绑着铃铛跳舞。

例句：The village celebrated May Day with a traditional performance by the local morricers. 村民们用当地舞者表演的传统莫里斯舞来庆祝五朔节。|近义词：dancer, performer|反义词:audience, spectator|词性:noun
