hangdog:/ˈhæŋdɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈhæŋdɔːɡ/ (US)| adj.| 沮丧的，垂头丧气的，愧疚的|Having a dejected or guilty appearance; shamefaced.|He had a hangdog expression on his face after being caught cheating.  他作弊被抓后，脸上带着一副沮丧的表情。|Example:  The little boy had a hangdog look after breaking the vase. 小男孩打碎花瓶后，一脸垂头丧气的样子。|Synonyms: ashamed, dejected, downcast, crestfallen, sheepish|Antonyms: cheerful, happy, upbeat, proud|adjective
