criminalism: /ˈkrɪmɪnəlɪzəm/
n.
犯罪, 犯罪活动, 犯罪率
The study of crime and criminals.  The state of being criminal; criminality. The propensity to commit a crime or crimes.
The city has seen a rise in criminalism in recent years.  近年来，这个城市的犯罪率有所上升。
criminality, delinquency, lawlessness
lawfulness, obedience, legality
