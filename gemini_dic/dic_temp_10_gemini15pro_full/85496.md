hasteless:/ˈheɪstləs/| adj.|从容不迫的，不慌不忙的|Without haste; leisurely; deliberate. Characterized by the absence of rush or urgency.|He took a hasteless stroll through the park, enjoying the scenery. 他悠闲地漫步穿过公园，欣赏着风景。|近义词：leisurely, unhurried, deliberate, slow, measured|反义词：hasty, hurried, rushed, urgent, impetuous|词性:形容词
词源: From haste + -less.  Haste comes from Middle English *hast, from Old Norse *hastr, from Proto-Germanic *haifstiz.
