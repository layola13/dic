geminations:/ˌdʒemɪˈneɪʃənz/
|n.|重复；双生；成对出现|A doubling or repetition, especially of a consonant sound in a word.  The process of doubling a consonant. The state of being doubled or paired.|The geminations in the word "bookkeeper" make it a fun word to say.  这个词“bookkeeper”中的辅音重复使其成为一个有趣的词。|duplications, repetitions|singularity, uniqueness|noun
