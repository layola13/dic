have-a-short-fuse:/ˌhæv ə ʃɔːrt ˈfjuːz/| idiom | 易怒，脾气暴躁 |  To have a short fuse means to have a quick temper and to become angry easily. This idiom refers to the short length of a fuse on an explosive device, implying that a person with a "short fuse" can quickly explode in anger. | My boss has a short fuse, so be careful what you say to him. 我的老板脾气很暴躁，所以和他说话要小心点。|  short-tempered, irritable, irascible | patient, even-tempered | idiom
