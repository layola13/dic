nonperspective:/ˌnɒnpərˈspektɪv/ | adj. | 非透视的，没有透视效果的 | Lacking or not employing perspective; without the illusion of depth or distance. | The cave paintings are nonperspective, with figures appearing flat and without depth.  洞穴壁画是非透视的，人物显得扁平，没有深度。|  The artist intentionally created a nonperspective drawing, focusing on pattern and color rather than realism.  这位艺术家故意创作了一幅非透视的画，专注于图案和颜色而不是写实。| 近义词: flat, two-dimensional | 反义词: perspective, three-dimensional | 词性: 形容词
