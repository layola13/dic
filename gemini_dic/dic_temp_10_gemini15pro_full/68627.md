fantoccini:/ˌfæntəˈtʃiːni/| n. |木偶，牵线木偶；傀儡|Puppets, especially marionettes, manipulated by strings or wires; by extension, people who are controlled or manipulated by others.|The puppeteer skillfully manipulated the fantoccini, bringing the miniature characters to life.  木偶操纵者熟练地操纵着牵线木偶，使微型人物栩栩如生。|Synonyms: puppets, marionettes, dummies|Antonyms: independent entities, free agents|noun
