dosses:/ˈdɒsɪz/ (British English) /ˈdɑːsɪz/ (American English)| n.|剂量; 一服(药); (尤指大量给予的)一份; (尤指经历的)一次(坏事);|A dose is a measured amount of something such as medicine.|例句：He took a large dose of cough medicine. 他服用了大量的咳嗽药。

例句：She's had a dose of bad luck recently. 她最近运气不好。|近义词：amount, quantity, portion, serving|反义词:None (dose doesn't have a direct opposite)|noun
