forsaker:/fərˈseɪkər/| n.|抛弃者，遗弃者|Someone who abandons or deserts someone or something.|He felt like a forsaker, leaving his family behind to pursue his dreams. 他感觉自己像个抛弃者，为了追求梦想而离开了家人。|近义词：deserter, abandoner, traitor|反义词：protector, guardian, loyalist|词性：名词
forsake:/fərˈseɪk/| vt.|放弃，抛弃，摒弃|To abandon or leave someone or something, especially when they need you; to give up or renounce something.|She forsook her career to raise her children. 她为了抚养孩子而放弃了事业。 He has forsaken all his old friends. 他抛弃了所有老朋友。|近义词：abandon, desert, renounce, relinquish|反义词：keep, retain, cherish, embrace|词性：动词
