molle:/ˈmɒleɪ/ (US), /ˈmɒli/ (UK)| adj. n.|柔软的; 软皮革;|Soft, pliable, especially of leather.  Referring to a type of soft leather or the items made from it, particularly in a military context. Originally from Spanish.|(adj.) The molle straps on the backpack made it easy to attach additional pouches. (背包上的molle织带可以方便地连接额外的袋子。)|(n.)  The soldier's molle vest held several magazines and grenades. (士兵的molle背心装着几个弹匣和手榴弹。)|近义词：soft, supple, pliable|反义词:hard, rigid, stiff|词性:adjective, noun
