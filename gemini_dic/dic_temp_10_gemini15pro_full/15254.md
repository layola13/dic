backhandedly:/ˌbækˈhændɪdli/| adv.|拐弯抹角地，间接地，讽刺地|In a way that is not direct, often containing criticism disguised as praise.|She complimented him backhandedly, saying his presentation was "surprisingly good." 她拐弯抹角地恭维他，说他的演讲“出奇地好”。|例句：He backhandedly insulted her by saying her dress was "interesting." 他拐弯抹角地侮辱她，说她的裙子“很有趣”。

例句：She backhandedly praised his cooking, saying it was "much better than last time". 她拐弯抹角地称赞他的厨艺，说比上次好多了。|近义词：indirectly, subtly, sarcastically, ironically|反义词：directly, frankly, sincerely|词性:副词
