gyneceum:/ɡaɪˈniːsiəm/ | n. | 雌蕊群；雌蕊 |The female reproductive organ of a flower, consisting of the stigma, style, and ovary.  | The botanist carefully examined the gyneceum of the orchid.  植物学家仔细检查了兰花的雌蕊群。|  synonym: pistil | antonym: androecium | noun

gyneceum:/ɡaɪˈniːsiəm/ | n. | 妇女的房间；闺房 (古希腊) | In ancient Greece, the gyneceum was the part of the house reserved for women. |  The women gathered in the gyneceum to spin wool and share stories.  妇女们聚集在闺房里纺羊毛和分享故事。 | synonym: women's quarters | antonym: men's quarters | noun
