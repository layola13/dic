mercaptan:/mərˈkæptæn/ /mərˈkæptən/
n.
硫醇，巯基化合物
A sulfur-containing organic compound with a distinctive, often unpleasant odor.  These compounds are often added to natural gas to give it a detectable smell.
例句：The mercaptan added to natural gas makes it easily detectable in case of a leak.  添加在天然气中的硫醇使它在泄漏时易于被察觉。

例句：The skunk's spray contains mercaptans, which contribute to its strong odor. 臭鼬的喷雾中含有硫醇，这导致了它强烈的臭味。

近义词：thiol
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
