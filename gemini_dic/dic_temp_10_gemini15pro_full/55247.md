dissimulate:/dɪˈsɪmjʊleɪt/
verb
假装；掩饰；装作没注意到
To hide or disguise one's true thoughts, feelings, or intentions.
He dissimulated his fear with a false smile.  他强颜欢笑以掩饰内心的恐惧。
She dissimulated her anger by speaking calmly. 她平静地说话以掩饰她的愤怒。

近义词: pretend, feign, mask, conceal, disguise
反义词: reveal, expose, divulge, betray

词性: verb

词源: From Late Latin *dissimulāre, from Latin dissimilis (“unlike”) + simulāre (“to make like, copy, imitate, feign”).
