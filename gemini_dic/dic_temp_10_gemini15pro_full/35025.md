cloot:/kluːt/ /kluːt/| n.|蹄; (尤指牛、羊等的)偶蹄; 笨拙的人; 傻瓜|A cloot is a hoof, especially of cattle or sheep. It can also refer to a clumsy or foolish person.|The farmer examined the cloot of the injured cow.  农民检查了受伤奶牛的蹄子。

He's a bit of a cloot, always tripping over his own feet. 他有点笨手笨脚，总是绊倒自己。|近义词：hoof, oaf, klutz|反义词:genius, expert|词性:noun
