disallowance:/dɪs.əˈlaʊ.əns/ | n. | 不允许，拒绝，扣除，折扣 | The act of disallowing something; refusal to allow or accept something; a deduction or discount. | The company's disallowance of my travel expenses frustrated me. 公司不批准我的差旅费，让我很沮丧。 | The disallowance of his claim by the insurance company forced him to pay for the damages himself.  保险公司拒绝了他的索赔，迫使他自行承担损失。 | 近义词：rejection, refusal, denial, deduction | 反义词：allowance, acceptance, approval | 词性: noun
