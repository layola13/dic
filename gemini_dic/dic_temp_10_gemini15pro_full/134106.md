nonimmigrants:/ˌnɑːnˈɪmɪɡrənts/ |n.| 非移民 |People who are allowed to enter a country temporarily for a specific purpose, such as tourism, work, or study, without the intention of permanently settling there.  |The US issues visas to nonimmigrants for various reasons. 美国为各种原因向非移民签发签证。|  Nonimmigrant workers fill crucial roles in many industries. 非移民工人在许多行业中扮演着至关重要的角色。| synonyms: aliens, visitors, foreigners | antonyms: immigrants, citizens, residents | noun

nonimmigrant:/ˌnɑːnˈɪmɪɡrənt/ |adj.| 非移民的 |Relating to or characteristic of nonimmigrants; not intending to immigrate. | The nonimmigrant visa allows for a limited stay. 非移民签证允许有限期的停留。| The company sponsors nonimmigrant workers for specialized roles.  这家公司为专门职位担保非移民工人。| synonyms: foreign, temporary, visiting | antonyms: immigrant, permanent, domestic | adjective
