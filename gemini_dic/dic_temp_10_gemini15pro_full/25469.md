bulletin:/ˈbʊlətɪn/| n.|公告；简报；新闻快报|A short official statement or broadcast summary of news.|The company released a bulletin about the merger.|announcement, notice, report|dispatch, communiqué|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *bulletin*, diminutive of *bulle* (“official document sealed with a bulla”), from Latin *bulla* (“bubble, knob, stud, seal”).
