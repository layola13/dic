breathful:/ˈbrɛθfʊl/| adj.|气喘吁吁的；呼吸急促的；费力的|Characterized by labored breathing; panting.  Marked by or requiring deep breaths.|例句：After running up the stairs, she was breathful and had to pause.  跑上楼梯后，她气喘吁吁，不得不停下来。|例句：The breathful climb to the summit left him exhausted.  费力地爬到山顶让他筋疲力尽。|近义词：breathless, panting, gasping|反义词:calm, relaxed|词性:adjective
