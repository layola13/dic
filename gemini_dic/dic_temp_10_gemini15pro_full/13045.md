astringent:/əˈstrɪndʒənt/
n.|收敛剂；止血药|A substance that shrinks tissues and checks flow of blood.|The doctor applied an astringent to the wound.|styptic, hemostatic|relaxant|noun
astringent:/əˈstrɪndʒənt/
adj.|收敛的；止血的|Causing contraction of skin cells and other body tissues.|The witch hazel acted as an astringent on his skin.|tonic, constrictive|relaxing, soothing|adjective
