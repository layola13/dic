facsimile:/fækˈsɪmɪli/ /fækˈsɪməli/
n. |副本，传真|an exact copy or reproduction of something, such as a document, a book, a painting, etc.;  a system of transmitting and reproducing printed matter or pictures by means of signals sent over telephone lines.|She sent a facsimile of the contract.|copy, replica, duplicate|original, unique|noun
facsimile:/fækˈsɪmɪli/ /fækˈsɪməli/
v. |复制，传真|to make a facsimile of something.|The document was faxed to the head office.|copy, reproduce, duplicate|create, originate|verb
