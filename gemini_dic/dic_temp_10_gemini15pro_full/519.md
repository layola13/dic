abolitions: /ˌæbəˈlɪʃənz/ | n. | 废除，取消 | The act of abolishing something, such as a system, practice, or institution.  The abolition of slavery was a landmark achievement. 奴隶制的废除是一项里程碑式的成就。 The government is considering the abolition of several outdated laws. 政府正在考虑废除几项过时的法律。 | 近义词: eliminations, eradications, terminations | 反义词: establishments, institutions, restorations | 词性: 名词

词源: From the Late Latin abolitiōn- (stem of abolitiō ), from the verb abolīre, meaning "to abolish."
