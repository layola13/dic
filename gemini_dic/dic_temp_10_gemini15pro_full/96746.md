ineloquent:/ɪˈnɛləkwənt/
adjective
不善言辞的；表达能力差的
Lacking fluency or persuasiveness in speaking or writing.
His ineloquent speech failed to move the audience. 他笨拙的演讲没能打动观众。
近义词:inarticulate, tongue-tied, speechless, dumb
反义词:eloquent, articulate, fluent
词性:adjective
