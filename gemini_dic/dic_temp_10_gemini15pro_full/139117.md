obedienciary: /oʊˌbiːdiˈɛnʃəri/ /əˈbiːdiənʃəri/ | n. | 受益人；遗产管理人 | A person entitled to the residue of a personal estate after the payment of debts and legacies. |  The lawyer explained to the family that they were the named obedienciaries in the will. 律师向家人解释说，他们是遗嘱中指定的受益人。

The obedienciary received a substantial sum after the estate was settled. 遗产处理完毕后，受益人得到了一大笔钱。| Synonyms: beneficiary, heir, legatee | Antonyms: testator, grantor | noun
