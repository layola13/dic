dewdrop:/ˈdjuːdrɒp/ (US) /ˈdʒuːdrɒp/ (UK)| n.|露珠|A drop of dew.  A tiny drop of water that forms on cool surfaces at night, when atmospheric vapor condenses.|The dewdrop clung precariously to the blade of grass.|近义词：droplet, bead|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
