actless:/ˈæktləs/| adj.|无行动的，不活跃的，缺乏行动的|Without action; inactive; lacking action.|The play was surprisingly actless, with very little movement on stage.  这出戏出奇地缺乏动作，舞台上几乎没有走动。

The actless protest was criticized for its lack of impact. 这场缺乏行动的抗议因其缺乏影响而受到批评。|近义词：inactive, passive, inert, static, dormant|反义词：active, dynamic, energetic, vigorous|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From act + -less.  Act comes from the Latin "actus" meaning "a doing, a driving, an impulse."  The suffix "-less" is of Old English origin and indicates a lack or absence of something.

USAGE->
This word is not commonly used in everyday conversation.  It is more likely to be found in formal writing or critical analysis, particularly in the context of describing a performance or a protest.

Example Sentences:

1. The actless protest was criticized for its lack of impact. = 这场缺乏行动的抗议因其缺乏影响而受到批评。

2.  Despite the director's vision, the actless scene felt slow and tedious. = 尽管导演有远见，但缺乏动作的场景仍然感觉缓慢而乏味。


PHRASE->
While "actless" doesn't readily form common phrases, it can be used in combination with prepositions like "in" or "during":

1. actless in the face of adversity = 在逆境面前无所作为
2. actless during the crisis = 在危机期间无所作为
3. seemingly actless = 表面上无所事事
4. strangely actless = 奇怪地不活跃
5. surprisingly actless = 令人惊讶地缺乏行动
