answerable-to:/ˈɑːnsərəbl tuː/
adjective
对…负责的；应向…解释的
Responsible to someone or some organization for one's actions or decisions.
The CEO is answerable to the board of directors. 首席执行官对董事会负责。
He is answerable to his constituents for his actions. 他要对他的选民负责他的行为。
近义词: accountable, responsible, liable
反义词: unaccountable, irresponsible
词性: adjective

answerable-to:/ˈɑːnsərəbl tuː/
adjective
应服从的；有义务听从的
Obliged to obey or follow the instructions of someone.
Soldiers are answerable to their commanding officers. 士兵有义务听从指挥官的命令。
The company is answerable to the regulations set by the government. 公司必须遵守政府制定的规章制度。
近义词: subject to, obedient to
反义词: independent of
词性: adjective
