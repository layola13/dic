noninformativenesses:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfɔːrmətɪvnəsɪz/
|n.|无信息性，缺乏信息性|The noninformativenesses of the data made it difficult to draw any conclusions.  这些数据的无信息性使得难以得出任何结论。|The study highlighted the noninformativenesses of the survey questions. 这项研究强调了调查问卷中问题的无信息性。|近义词：uninformativeness, lack of information, meaninglessness|反义词：informativeness, richness of information, meaningfulness|词性:名词

词源:
non- (prefix): not, opposite of
informative (adjective): providing useful or interesting information
-ness (suffix): used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality
