cyclone: /ˈsaɪkloʊn/ (US) /ˈsaɪkləʊn/ (UK)| n.|气旋，旋风|A system of winds rotating inwards to an area of low barometric pressure, with an anticlockwise rotation in the northern hemisphere and a clockwise rotation in the southern hemisphere.|The cyclone ripped through the coastal town, causing widespread destruction.  气旋席卷了沿海城镇，造成了大范围的破坏。|hurricane, typhoon, tornado|anticyclone|noun
