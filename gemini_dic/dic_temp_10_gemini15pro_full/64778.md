eskar:/ˈɛskər/ | n. | 蛇形丘，冰河砂礫堤 | A long, winding ridge of stratified sand and gravel deposited by a meltwater stream flowing beneath a glacier. |  The hikers followed the esker for miles, marveling at its unusual formation.  徒步旅行者沿着蛇形丘走了好几英里，惊叹于它不同寻常的构造。

|  Eskers are important geological features that provide clues about past glaciation. 蛇形丘是重要的地质特征，为了解过去冰川作用提供了线索。| 近义词: ridge, mound | 反义词: valley | noun
