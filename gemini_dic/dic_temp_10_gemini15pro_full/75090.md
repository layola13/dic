frilliest:/ˈfrɪliɪst/
adjective
最饰有褶边荷叶边的；最花哨的
Having the most frills or ornamentation; most elaborate or showy.
The frilliest dress at the party belonged to the birthday girl.  生日女孩穿着派对上最花哨的连衣裙。
The curtains were the frilliest I had ever seen. 我从未见过如此多褶边的窗帘。

近义词: fanciest, most ornate, most elaborate
反义词: plainest, simplest, most austere
词性: adjective

词源:
From frill + -iest (superlative suffix).  "Frill" comes from the Middle English "frillen," likely from a Low German source related to curling or wrinkling.
