bedfellows:/ˈbedˌfeləʊz/ (UK) /ˈbedˌfelˌoʊz/ (US)| n.|同床者，伙伴，盟友|People allied or associated with each other, especially in a surprising or unexpected way.|例句：Politics makes strange bedfellows. 政治会使人结成奇怪的同盟。

例句：The liberal party found themselves bedfellows with the conservatives on this issue. 在这个问题上，自由党发现自己与保守党成了盟友。


近义词：allies, associates, partners, collaborators|反义词:enemies, opponents, adversaries|词性:名词
