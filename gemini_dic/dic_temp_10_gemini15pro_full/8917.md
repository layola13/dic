antigreek:/ˌæntiˈɡriːk/
adjective
反希腊的；敌视希腊的
opposed to or hostile to Greece, its people, or its culture.
The antigreek sentiment was palpable in the region after the war. 战后，该地区反希腊的情绪十分明显。
Synonyms:  Hellenophobic, anti-Hellenic
Antonyms:  pro-Greek, philhellene
词性: adjective
