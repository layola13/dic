anagram:/ˈænəɡræm/| n.|字谜，变形词|A word or phrase formed by rearranging the letters of another, such as *cinema*, formed from *iceman*.|例句：The word "listen" is an anagram of "silent".  单词"listen"是"silent"的变形词。

例句："dormitory" is an anagram of "dirty room". "dormitory"是"dirty room"的变形词。|近义词：transposition, permutation|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
