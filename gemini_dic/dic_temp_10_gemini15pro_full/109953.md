liberalizes:/ˈlɪbərəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使自由化，放宽限制
to make something less strict or controlled
The government has liberalized its policies on foreign investment. 政府放宽了对外资的政策。
synonyms: deregulate, loosen, relax, ease
antonyms: restrict, tighten, control
词性: 动词
