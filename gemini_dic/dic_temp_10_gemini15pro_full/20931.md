bleater:/ˈbliːtər/| n.|咩咩叫者；爱抱怨的人|A person or animal that bleats, especially a sheep or goat.  Figuratively, someone who complains a lot. |The little lamb was a bleater, constantly calling for its mother. 小羊羔一直咩咩叫着找妈妈。| The manager was a real bleater, always complaining about something. 经理真是个爱抱怨的人，总是抱怨个不停。|近义词：complainer, whiner, grumbler|反义词: optimist, enthusiast|noun


bleat:/bliːt/| n. vi. vt.|咩咩叫；抱怨|The sound a sheep or goat makes.  Also, to complain in a weak, whining way. | The sheep bleated nervously.  羊不安地咩咩叫。|He kept bleating about how unfair his life was. 他不停地抱怨生活是多么不公平。|近义词：complain, whine, moan|反义词: praise, celebrate|noun, verb, intransitive verb, transitive verb
