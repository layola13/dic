electric-blues:/ɪˈlɛktrɪk bluːz/| n. |电蓝调|A subgenre of blues music characterized by the use of electric instruments, particularly the electric guitar. It emerged in the 1940s and 1950s. |例句：I love listening to electric blues music. 我喜欢听电蓝调音乐。

例句：Electric blues evolved from earlier acoustic blues styles. 电蓝调是从早期的原声蓝调风格演变而来的。|近义词：Chicago blues, blues rock|反义词:Acoustic blues|词性:noun
