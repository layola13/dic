cresses:/ˈkrɛsɪz/
n.
水芹
A pungent-tasting salad plant of the mustard family, with small leaves, cultivated for its crisp stems and peppery flavour.
We had a salad of mixed greens, including cresses. 我们吃了一份混合蔬菜沙拉，里面有水芹。

Etymology:
From Old English *kæresse, from Proto-Germanic *krasjon, from the same root as "cress." Related to Old High German kresso, Middle Dutch kerse. Ultimately of uncertain origin.
