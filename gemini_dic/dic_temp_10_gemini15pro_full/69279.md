featherweight:/ˈfeð.ə(rˌ)weɪt/| n. adj.|羽量级选手，轻量级|A person or thing that is very light in weight, especially a boxer belonging to the lightest weight class.|例句：He's a featherweight in politics. 他在政界是个无足轻重的人物。|例句：The new laptop is a featherweight, making it easy to carry around. 这款新笔记本电脑非常轻便，便于携带。|近义词：lightweight, bantamweight|反义词：heavyweight, giant|词性：名词，形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From feather + weight.  Meaning literally "the weight of a feather," figuratively applied to anything exceptionally light.
