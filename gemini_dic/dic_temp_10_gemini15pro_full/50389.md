deposable:/dɪˈpoʊzəbl/
adjective
可处置的，可罢免的
able to be deposed or removed from office; disposable.
The CEO made it clear that all managers were deposable if their performance didn't improve. 首席执行官明确表示，如果所有经理的绩效没有提高，都可以被免职。
近义词:removable, displaceable, dismissable
反义词:irremovable, permanent
