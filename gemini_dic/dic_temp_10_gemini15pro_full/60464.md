一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are they watching on TV? 他们在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? 她用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading this week? 他这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say to him? 你对他说了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party?  她在派对上穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they eaten for lunch? 他们午饭吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the phone rang? 电话响之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  在她换专业之前，她一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting? 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你会完成什么？
What will they have finished by tomorrow? 到明天他们会完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
