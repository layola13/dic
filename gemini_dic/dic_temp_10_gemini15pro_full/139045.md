oarless:/ˈɔːrlɪs/| adj.|无桨的，没有桨的|Without oars.  Lacking oars; not propelled by oars.|The oarless boat drifted with the current. 那艘无桨的小船顺着水流漂流。|近义词：unpowered, adrift|反义词:oared, powered|词性:形容词
Etymology: From 'oar' + '-less'.  'Oar' comes from Old English 'ār', from Proto-Germanic '*ōzō'. '-less' is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking," from Old English '-lēas'.
