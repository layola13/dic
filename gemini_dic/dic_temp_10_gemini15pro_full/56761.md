ETYMOLOGY->
The origin of "doozy" is uncertain, but it's thought to be possibly derived from the Duesenberg automobile, a luxury brand popular in the 1920s and 30s, known for its power and elegance.  Another possible origin is the actress Eleanor Duse, who was popular around the same time.
