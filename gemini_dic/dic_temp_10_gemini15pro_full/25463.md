bulled:/bʊld/
| adj. | 上涨的；看涨的；价格上涨的；(动物)长角的 | Having experienced a rise in price; optimistic about rising prices.  (Of an animal) having horns or horn-like projections. | The market was bulled by the positive news.  市场因利好消息而上涨。| 近义词: bullish, rising, advancing | 反义词: bearish, falling, declining | 词性:形容词

bulled:/bʊld/
| v. | (使)价格上涨；看涨；(动物)用角抵 | To cause a rise in price; to be optimistic about rising prices. (Of an animal) to butt with horns. | The investors bulled the stock price up. 投资者推高了股价。 | 近义词: boost, inflate, elevate (price); butt, gore (with horns) | 反义词: bear, depress, lower (price) | 词性:动词
