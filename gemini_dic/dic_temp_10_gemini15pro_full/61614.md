emi:/ˌiːɛmˈaɪ/| n.|等额分期付款|Equated Monthly Installment, a fixed payment amount made by a borrower to a lender at a specified date each calendar month.  It applies to both interest and principal each month so that over a specified number of years, the loan is paid off in full.|例句：My car loan EMI is $300 per month. 我的汽车贷款每月还款额是300美元。

例句：She calculates her monthly EMIs using an online calculator. 她使用在线计算器计算每月的等额分期付款。|近义词：installment, monthly payment|反义词:lump sum|词性:noun
