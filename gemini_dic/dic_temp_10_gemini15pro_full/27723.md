canescent:/kəˈnɛsənt/| adj.|灰白的，老化的|Becoming gray or whitish; hoary.  Turning white or grayish, as with age or weathering. |例句：The old man's canescent hair framed his wrinkled face. 老人的灰白头发衬托着他布满皱纹的脸。

例句：The canescent  leaves of the plant indicated it was nearing the end of its life cycle.  植物灰白的叶子表明它已接近生命周期的终结。|近义词：hoary, grayish, whitening, silvery|反义词:dark, black, youthful|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *canescentem (nominative canescens), present participle of canescere "to become gray, grow white," from canus "white, gray."
