conduit:/ˈkɒndjuɪt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnduɪt/ (US)| n.|管道，导管，渠道|A channel for conveying water or other fluids.  A means by which something is transmitted.|The electrical conduit needs to be replaced. 电线管道需要更换。|Synonyms: channel, pipe, tube, duct, passage|Antonyms: barrier, obstruction|noun

conduit:/ˈkɒndjuɪt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnduɪt/ (US)| n.|渠道，途径|A means by which something is transmitted, especially information or ideas.|The newspaper acted as a conduit for political propaganda. 这家报纸充当了政治宣传的渠道。|Synonyms: medium, vehicle, channel, instrument|Antonyms: barrier, obstruction|noun
