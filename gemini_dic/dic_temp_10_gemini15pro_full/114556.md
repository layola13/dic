malassimilation:/ˌmæləˌsɪməˈleɪʃən/
|n.|消化不良；吸收不良|The process by which food is improperly or incompletely absorbed by the body.  It refers to a failure in the process of absorbing nutrients from food.|He suffered from malassimilation due to a chronic illness. 他因慢性病而患有消化不良。|近义词：malabsorption, indigestion|反义词: digestion, assimilation|词性: noun
Etymology: mal- (bad, ill) + assimilation (the process of absorbing nutrients) from Latin assimilis "like, similar"
