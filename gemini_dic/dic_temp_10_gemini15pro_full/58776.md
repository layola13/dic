dunness:/ˈdʌnəs/| n. |昏暗，阴暗，迟钝|A state of being dim, dark, or dull; lack of brightness or vibrancy.|The dunness of the November sky made me feel melancholic. 十一月的天空阴沉沉的，让我感到忧郁。|近义词：dimness, darkness, dullness, gloominess|反义词:brightness, brilliance, vividness|词性:名词
词源:From Middle English *dunnes, derived from dun +‎ -ness. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *dunnaz (“dark-colored, brown”).
