outswirls: /ˈaʊtswɜːrlz/
词性: verb (动词)
中文翻译: 向外旋转，向外涡旋
英文解析: Moves in a swirling motion outwards.  It combines "out" indicating direction and "swirls" which describes the spiraling movement.
例句：The smoke outswirls from the chimney. 烟从烟囱里盘旋而出。
例句：The water outswirls from the drain. 水从排水管里旋转流出。
近义词: spirals out, swirls outward, whirls out
反义词:  inswirls, spirals in, swirls inward
词源:  A combination of "out" (Old English *ūt, from Proto-Germanic *ut) and "swirl" (probably from Middle Low German swirlen, frequentative of a verb related to Old Norse sviri “whirl”).
