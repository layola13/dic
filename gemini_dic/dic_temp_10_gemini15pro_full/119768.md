mightful:/ˈmaɪtfʊl/| adj.|强大的，有力的|Possessing or showing great strength or power.  Having might. While technically a word, it's extremely rare and archaic.  Consider using "powerful," "mighty," or "potent" instead.|例句：In olden tales, the mightful warrior vanquished his foes. (在古代传说中，强大的战士战胜了他的敌人。)|近义词：powerful, mighty, potent, strong, vigorous|反义词：weak, powerless, feeble, impotent|词性:形容词
