despoliation:/dɪˌspoʊliˈeɪʃn/
n.
掠夺，破坏，洗劫
The act of despoiling; plundering; pillage; devastation; the act of stripping a place of its valuables.
The despoliation of the rainforest is a tragedy.  雨林的破坏是一场悲剧。

The invading army's despoliation of the city left it in ruins. 入侵军队对这座城市的掠夺使其成为废墟。

近义词:plunder, pillage, looting, devastation, destruction
反义词:preservation, conservation, restoration

词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *despoliatio, from Latin despoliare "to plunder, rob," from de- "off, down, away" + spoliare "to strip, plunder."
