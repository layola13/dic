immure: /ɪˈmjʊər/
verb
监禁，禁闭; 把…嵌在墙上
to enclose within walls; imprison
例句：The king immured his enemies in the dungeon. 国王把他的敌人囚禁在地牢里。

例句：The monk immured himself in the monastery for years of silent contemplation.  这个僧侣把自己关在修道院里多年，默默地沉思。

近义词：confine, imprison, incarcerate, enclose
反义词：liberate, free, release
词性：verb

immure: /ɪˈmjʊər/
noun
监禁，禁闭 (较少用作名词)
the act of enclosing, or the state of being enclosed, within walls
例句：The immure of the prisoners was cruel and unusual.  囚犯们的监禁是残酷和不寻常的。 (用法较罕见)

例句：Living in the remote cabin felt like an immure from the outside world. 住在偏僻的小屋里感觉像是与世隔绝。(用法较罕见，更常用figurative speech 比喻)


近义词：confinement, imprisonment, incarceration
反义词：freedom, liberation, release
词性：noun

词源:  From Late Latin *immurare, from Latin in- (“in, into”) + murus (“wall”).
