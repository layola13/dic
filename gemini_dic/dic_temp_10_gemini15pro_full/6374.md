amphichroic:/æmfɪˈkroʊɪk/
adjective
两色的，双色的
Exhibiting amphichroism; capable of appearing as one of two different colors depending on the angle of incident light.
Alexandrite is an amphichroic gem, appearing green in daylight and red in incandescent light.  亚历山大石是一种两色宝石，在日光下呈绿色，在白炽灯下呈红色。
dichroic, pleochroic
monochromatic
