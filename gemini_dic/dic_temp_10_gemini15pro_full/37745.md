compartner:/kəmˈpɑːrtnər/
n.|同伴，合伙人|A person who shares or takes part with another; a partner or colleague. |例句：She found a compartner in her new business venture. 她找到了一个新的商业伙伴。|近义词：partner, colleague, associate, confederate|反义词：opponent, adversary, rival|词性：名词

compartner:/kəmˈpɑːrtnər/
vt.|与…合伙，与…成为伙伴|To become a partner with someone. |例句：They decided to compartner to open a restaurant. 他们决定合伙开一家餐馆。|近义词：partner (with), collaborate (with), team up (with)|反义词：separate, divorce, part ways|词性：动词
