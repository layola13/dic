gauged:/ɡeɪdʒd/
Verb|测量，估计，判断|To have measured or estimated something, or formed a judgement about it.
The surveyor gauged the depth of the river. | 测量员测量了河的深度。
They gauged the public's reaction to the new policy. | 他们估计了公众对新政策的反应。
近义词:measured, estimated, assessed, judged
反义词:guessed, speculated
词性:Verb (Past Participle)


gauged:/ɡeɪdʒd/
Adjective|测量的，估计的，判断的|Measured, estimated, or judged.
The gauged depth of the river was 10 feet. | 测得的河水深度为10英尺。
The company's gauged profits were higher than expected. | 公司估计的利润高于预期。
近义词:measured, estimated, assessed, judged
反义词:ungauged, unmeasured
词性:Adjective


词源:
From the noun "gauge," which comes from Old North French "gaugie," meaning "measuring rod."
