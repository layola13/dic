infernalities:/ˌɪnfərˈnælɪtiz/ | n. |负外部性；外部成本；外部不经济|Costs or benefits that affect a party who did not choose to incur that cost or benefit.  It is a type of externality.  Often used in economics.|  The factory's pollution created significant infernalities for the surrounding community.  工厂的污染给周围社区造成了严重的负外部性。|Synonyms: external costs, negative externalities, external diseconomies|Antonyms: external benefits, positive externalities, external economies| noun
