disinterestedness:/dɪsˈɪntrɪstɪdnəs/
|n.| 无私; 公正; |Lack of self-interest; impartiality.|  The judge showed great disinterestedness in his handling of the case.  法官在处理此案时表现出了极大的公正。|Synonyms: impartiality, neutrality, objectivity, fairness |Antonyms: bias, prejudice, partiality, selfishness| Noun
disinterested:/dɪsˈɪntrɪstɪd/
|adj.| 公正的; 无私的;|Not influenced by considerations of personal advantage.|A disinterested observer is needed to mediate the dispute. 需要一位公正的观察员来调解这场纠纷。|Synonyms: impartial, unbiased, neutral, objective |Antonyms: biased, prejudiced, partial, selfish| Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- + interested.  Originally meant "uninterested," but the current meaning of "impartial" developed in the 17th century.
