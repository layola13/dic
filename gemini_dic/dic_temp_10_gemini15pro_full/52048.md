dialled:/ˈdaɪld/ (UK) /ˈdaɪəld/ (US)| v. |拨号；打电话|past tense and past participle of "dial" - to operate a telephone by pushing buttons or turning a dial to connect with a particular number. |例句：I dialled the number but there was no answer. 我拨了号码，但没有人接听。

例句：She dialled 999 for an ambulance. 她拨打999叫了救护车。|近义词：called, phoned, rang |反义词: hung up, disconnected |词性: Verb (past participle)

PHRASE->
There is no commonly used phrase with "dialled" specifically.  The phrases would generally use "dial".  Here are some examples using "dial":

dial up (someone/something)  打电话给（某人/某事）
dial in (to something)  拨入（到某事）
dial a number  拨一个号码
dial the wrong number  拨错号码
dial zero  拨零


词源: From Old French *dialer "to mark with lines," from Latin dialis "of a day" (from dies "day"), originally referring to sundials.  The meaning evolved to marking or measuring and eventually to operating a telephone dial.
