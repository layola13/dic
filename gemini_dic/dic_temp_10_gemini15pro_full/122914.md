molto:/ˈmoltoʊ/ (US), /ˈmɒltoʊ/ (UK)| adv.|很，非常，十分|Very, much, a lot.  It intensifies the meaning of the adjective or adverb it modifies.|例句：Questo caffè è molto buono. This coffee is very good.  这咖啡非常好。|近义词：very, extremely, highly, much|反义词：poco (little), slightly|词性:副词

词源：From Italian *molto*, from Latin *multum* ("much"), neuter singular of *multus* ("much, many").
