drain:/dreɪn/| v. n.| 排水，耗尽，流干|To cause or allow (liquid) to run out of something; to empty (something) of liquid; to flow away gradually; to become gradually depleted, exhausted, or emptied.|The sink drained slowly. 水槽排水很慢。  The swamp was drained to create farmland. 沼泽被排干以开垦农田。  The long war drained the country's resources. 长时间的战争耗尽了国家的资源。|近义词：empty, deplete, exhaust|反义词：fill, replenish|词性:动词, 名词

drain:/dreɪn/| n.| 排水沟，下水道|A pipe or channel that carries off surplus liquid, especially rainwater or sewage.|The drain was blocked with leaves. 排水沟被树叶堵塞了。|近义词：sewer, pipe, channel, outlet|反义词：inlet|词性:名词

词源: From Old English *drēahnian, from Proto-Germanic *draugijaną. Cognate with Old Norse dreygi (“to draw, drag”).
