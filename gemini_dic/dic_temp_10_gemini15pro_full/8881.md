antiformalist:/ˌæntiˈfɔːrməlɪst/
| adj. n.|反形式主义的；反形式主义者|Opposed to formalism, especially in art, literature, or philosophy.  Characterized by a rejection of traditional rules and conventions.|The antiformalist movement in art challenged the established norms. 反形式主义艺术运动挑战了既定的规范。|As an antiformalist, he believed that art should be accessible to everyone. 作为一名反形式主义者，他认为艺术应该面向所有人。|近义词:iconoclastic, unconventional, nonconformist|反义词:formalist, conventional, traditional|词性:adjective, noun
