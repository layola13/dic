demising:/dɪˈmaɪzɪŋ/
n.| 死亡，终止，转让 |The demise of the company was a shock to everyone.  公司的倒闭让每个人都感到震惊。|
v.|  死亡，终止，转让 |The king demised his kingdom to his son. 国王将他的王国传给了他的儿子。|
adj.|  死亡的，终止的，转让的|The demising clause in the lease specifies the end date. 租赁协议中的终止条款规定了结束日期。 |
近义词:ending, termination, death, transfer, conveyance |
反义词:beginning, commencement, birth, acquisition|
词性: noun, verb, adjective


词源:  From Old French *demis, past participle of metre "to place, put, send," from Latin mittere "to send, let go." The original meaning was "to lay down, give up," later specifically "to lease" and "to die."
