hasting:/ˈheɪstɪŋ/| adj. |匆忙的，急忙的|Characterized by speed and urgency; hurrying.|He sent a hasting messenger to deliver the news. 他派了一个匆忙的信使去传递消息。|近义词：hurrying, rushing, speedy, quick|反义词：slow, leisurely, deliberate|词性:形容词
hasten:/ˈheɪsən/| v. |加速，催促|To cause to happen sooner; accelerate.|The bad news hastened his departure. 坏消息促使他早日离开。|近义词：accelerate, expedite, quicken|反义词：delay, hinder, slow|词性:动词
