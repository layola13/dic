intentive:/ɪnˈtɛntɪv/ | adj. | 专心的，专注的，聚精会神的 | characterized by close and careful attention |  She was so intensive in her work that she didn't notice the time passing. 她如此专注于工作，以至于没有注意到时间的流逝。 |  The doctor gave the patient an intensive examination. 医生对病人进行了仔细的检查。 | 近义词: attentive, focused, concentrated, diligent | 反义词: inattentive, distracted, careless | 词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *intentīvus, from Latin intentus (“stretched out, strained, attentive”), past participle of intendere (“to stretch out, strain, pay attention to”)
