anti-confucian:/ˌænti-kənˈfjuːʃən/| adj.|反儒家的，非儒家的|Opposed to or rejecting the teachings or influence of Confucius or Confucianism.|The anti-confucian movement gained momentum during the May Fourth Movement. 五四运动时期，反儒家运动势头强劲。|The government implemented anti-confucian policies. 政府实施了反儒家政策。|近义词：non-Confucian, anti-traditional|反义词：Confucian, pro-Confucian|词性:adjective

anti-confucian:/ˌænti-kənˈfjuːʃən/| n.|反儒家者|A person who opposes Confucianism.|He was known as a staunch anti-confucian. 他以坚定的反儒家者而闻名。|Many anti-confucians advocated for modernization. 许多反儒家者提倡现代化。|近义词：opponent of Confucianism, critic of Confucianism|反义词：Confucianist, follower of Confucius|词性:noun
