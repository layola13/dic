mistuning:/ˌmɪsˈtjuːnɪŋ/ | n. | 失谐，失调 |The act or process of tuning incorrectly; the state of being out of tune.  A slight difference in tuning, especially between parts of a musical instrument or system, resulting in an unpleasant sound. | The mistuning of the piano made the piece sound jarring.  钢琴的失谐使这首曲子听起来很不和谐。 |  detuning, dissonance, discordance | tuning, harmony, concord | noun
