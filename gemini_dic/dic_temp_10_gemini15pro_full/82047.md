gristed:/ˈɡrɪstɪd/ | adj. | 恼火的，愤怒的 | Feeling annoyed or irritated. | He was gristed about the long delay. 他对长时间的延误感到恼火。|  She got gristed when she missed her train. 她错过火车后很生气。| 近义词: annoyed, irritated, vexed, peeved | 反义词: pleased, happy, content | 词性:形容词

Etymology: Likely a variation of "gristly," referring to the tough, chewy texture of gristle, metaphorically extended to a tough or irritable temperament.  The exact etymology is uncertain.
