courtiery: /ˈkɔːrtiəri/ (US) /ˈkɔːtɪəri/ (UK)| n. |殷勤；奉承；献媚；（总称） courtiers |Politeness and flattery typical of a royal court.  The art of pleasing people in authority. |The king was surrounded by courtiery. 国王身边围绕着阿谀奉承的人。|Synonyms: flattery, adulation, fawning, sycophancy |Antonyms:  rudeness, bluntness, frankness, candor| noun
