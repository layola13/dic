frankfurters:/ˈfræŋkfɜːrtərz/ (US), /ˈfræŋkfɜːtəz/ (UK)| n. |法兰克福香肠，热狗|A type of thin, smoked sausage, typically made of beef and pork, often served in a bun.  Originally from Frankfurt, Germany.|I like my frankfurters with mustard and relish. 我喜欢我的法兰克福香肠加芥末酱和酸黄瓜。|Synonyms: hot dogs, wieners, sausages|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider vegetarian sausage as an alternative)|Noun
