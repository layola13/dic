hypercivilization:/ˌhaɪpərˌsɪvɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|超级文明|A hypothetical civilization that has reached an extremely advanced stage of technological and societal development, far surpassing any known civilization.|Scientists speculate about the potential characteristics of hypercivilizations, including their energy consumption and communication methods.|近义词: advanced civilization, supercivilization|反义词: primitive civilization|词性: noun

Etymology:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek "hyper," meaning "over, beyond, exceeding"
civilization: from French "civilisation," from Latin "civilis" meaning "civil, relating to citizens"
