flexibility:/ˌflɛksɪˈbɪləti/
n.|灵活性，柔韧性，可变通性|The flexibility of the schedule allows for changes.  这个时间表的灵活性允许进行更改。|
例句：The yoga teacher emphasized the importance of flexibility. 瑜伽老师强调了柔韧性的重要性。
例句：We appreciate the flexibility of your offer. 我们感谢贵方提议的灵活性。
近义词：adaptability, versatility, elasticity, pliancy
反义词：rigidity, inflexibility, stiffness
词性: 名词

Etymology:  From Late Latin *flexibilitatem, from Latin flexibilis ("flexible") + -itas.  Ultimately from Latin flectere ("to bend").
