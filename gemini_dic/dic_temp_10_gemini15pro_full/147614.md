overplenteous: /ˌōvərˈplɛntiəs/ | adj. | 过于丰富的，过剩的 | Excessively plentiful; abundant to excess. Characterized by an overabundance or superfluity.  Derived from "over" (excessive) + "plenteous" (abundant). | The harvest was overplenteous this year, leaving much of the fruit to rot on the ground. 今年的收成过于丰富，许多水果都烂在了地上。| The overplenteous buffet offered a dizzying array of choices.  过于丰富的自助餐提供了令人眼花缭乱的选择。 | Synonyms: abundant, copious, profuse, exuberant, overflowing | Antonyms: scarce, meager, scanty, insufficient, deficient | adjective
