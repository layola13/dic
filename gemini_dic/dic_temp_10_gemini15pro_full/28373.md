carbamine:/ˈkɑːrbəmiːn/ /ˈkɑːrbəˌmaɪn/ | n. |氨基甲酸|An organic compound containing the group -NHCOOH.  It is the hypothetical acid of which carbamates are esters.  Unstable in the free state, decomposing to ammonia and carbon dioxide.|Carbamic acid decomposes readily into carbon dioxide and ammonia. 氨基甲酸很容易分解成二氧化碳和氨。| The formation of carbamine hemoglobin reduces the blood's ability to carry oxygen. 氨基甲酰血红蛋白的形成会降低血液的携氧能力。| synonyms:  (no common synonyms, often referred to by its derivatives like carbamates) | antonyms:  (none) | noun
