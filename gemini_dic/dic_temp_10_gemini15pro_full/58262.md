drummy:/ˈdrʌmi/| adj.|鼓声般的，空洞的，沉闷的|Resembling the sound of a drum; hollow-sounding.  Can also refer to a feeling of dullness or lethargy.|The old barrel sounded drummy when I tapped it.  那个旧桶当我敲击它时发出空洞的鼓声。|Synonyms: hollow, resonant, dull, lethargic|Antonyms: sharp, clear, energetic, vibrant|adjective

drummy:/ˈdrʌmi/| adj.|昏昏沉沉的，无精打采的|Characterized by a feeling of dullness, lethargy, or disorientation, often as a result of illness or medication.|After taking the cold medicine, I felt rather drummy and went straight to bed.  吃了感冒药后，我感到昏昏沉沉的，直接上床睡觉了。|Synonyms: drowsy, sluggish, groggy, dopey|Antonyms: alert, energetic, lively, vigorous|adjective
