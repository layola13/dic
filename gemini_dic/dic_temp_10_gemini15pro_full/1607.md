acidifiable:/əˌsɪdɪˈfaɪəbl/
adjective
可酸化的
Capable of being made acid; that can be acidified.
Certain substances are acidifiable by exposure to air.  某些物质暴露在空气中会酸化。
The solution is acidifiable by adding hydrochloric acid.  通过添加盐酸可以酸化该溶液。

近义词:acidifying
反义词:alkalizable, neutralizable
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From acidify + -able.  Acidify comes from acid + -ify.  Acid ultimately derives from Latin acidus "sour".

USAGE->
This word is less common than "acidifying" but can be used in similar contexts.

The compound is acidifiable under certain conditions. = 在特定条件下，该化合物是可酸化的。
The liquid is acidifiable, but we need to control the pH carefully. =  这种液体可以酸化，但我们需要仔细控制pH值。

PHRASE->
This word is not typically used in common phrases.  Instead, phrases related to "acidification" are more common (e.g., "the acidification of the oceans").
