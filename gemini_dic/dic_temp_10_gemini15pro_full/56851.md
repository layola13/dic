dorsal: /ˈdɔːrsəl/ (UK), /ˈdɔːrsəl/ (US) | adj. | 背部的，背侧的 | Relating to the back or posterior of a body or organ, especially of an animal. |  The dorsal fin of a shark helps it maintain balance.  鲨鱼的背鳍帮助它保持平衡。| synonyms: back, posterior, rear | antonyms: ventral, anterior, front | adjective

dorsal: /ˈdɔːrsəl/ (UK), /ˈdɔːrsəl/ (US) | n. | 背部，背侧 | The upper surface of an animal, plant, or organ.  (This usage is less common than the adjectival form) | The bird displayed the bright plumage on its dorsal. 鸟展示了它背部的鲜艳羽毛。| synonyms: back | antonyms: underside, belly | noun
