overhumanize:/ˌoʊvərˈhjuːmənaɪz/| v. |过度人性化；过分赋予人性|To attribute excessive human qualities or emotions to something non-human.|例句：We tend to overhumanize our pets, treating them like furry little humans. 我们倾向于过度人性化我们的宠物，把它们当作毛茸茸的小人类。

例句：Overhumanizing AI can lead to unrealistic expectations and disappointment. 过度人性化人工智能会导致不切实际的期望和失望。|近义词：anthropomorphize, personify|反义词:dehumanize, objectify|词性:动词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "humanize" (from "human" + "-ize," a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become").
