mastress: /ˈmæstrɪs/ (may-stris)
Noun
女主人; 女教师; 技艺精湛的女子
A woman who has control or power over someone or something; a woman who is highly skilled in a particular activity.
She was the undisputed mastress of the household. 她毫无疑问是这家的女主人。
The mastress of disguise slipped past the guards. 这位伪装大师从守卫身边溜了过去。
Synonyms: mistress, expert, adept, proficient
Antonyms: servant, novice, beginner
