begad:/bɪˈɡæd/| int.|真的，我的天哪（表示惊讶或强调）|An archaic or humorous exclamation of surprise or emphasis, literally "by God".|例句：Begad, that's a fine horse! 我的天哪，那真是一匹好马！

例句：Begad, I never thought I'd see you here!  我的天哪，我从没想过会在这里见到你！|近义词：by golly, by Jove, gosh, my goodness, heavens|反义词: (No direct antonym for an exclamation of surprise)|词性:感叹词
