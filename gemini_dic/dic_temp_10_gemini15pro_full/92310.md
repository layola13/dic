词源：
"hypno-" comes from the Greek word "hypnos," meaning sleep. "-therapy" comes from the Greek word "therapeia," meaning healing or treatment.
“hypno-”来自希腊语单词“hypnos”，意思是睡眠。“-therapy”来自希腊语单词“therapeia”，意思是治疗。
