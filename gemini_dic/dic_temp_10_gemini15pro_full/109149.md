legislators:/ˈledʒɪsleɪtərz/
n.
立法者，立法委员
A person who makes laws; a member of a legislative body.
The legislators voted on the new bill. 立法委员们对新法案进行了投票。
lawmakers, representatives, senators, congressmen/women, assemblymen/women
dictators, autocrats
noun
