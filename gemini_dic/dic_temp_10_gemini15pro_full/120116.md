mimickers:/ˈmɪmɪkərz/ | n. | 模仿者；模仿生物；拟态者 |  People or animals that imitate or copy the behavior, appearance, or sound of another. |  The comedians were excellent mimickers of famous politicians. 这些喜剧演员是著名政治家的优秀模仿者。

The viceroy butterfly is a well-known mimicker of the monarch butterfly. 副王蝴蝶是著名的模仿帝王蝶的蝴蝶。 | 近义词: imitators, copycats, impersonators | 反义词: originals, innovators | 词性: noun


mimic:/ˈmɪmɪk/ | v. | 模仿；摹拟 | To imitate or copy the behavior, appearance, or sound of another. | He mimicked his teacher's voice perfectly. 他完美地模仿了他老师的声音。

Parrots can mimic human speech. 鹦鹉可以模仿人类的语言。 | 近义词: imitate, copy, impersonate | 反义词: originate, innovate | 词性: verb


mimic:/ˈmɪmɪk/ | adj. | 模仿的；拟态的 | Relating to or characterized by mimicry. | The insect's mimic coloration protected it from predators.  这种昆虫的拟态颜色保护它免受捕食者的侵害。

He used a mimic device to attract the birds. 他用一个模仿装置来吸引鸟类。| 近义词: imitative, simulated | 反义词: original, genuine | 词性: adjective


词源: From the Greek word "mimos" meaning "mime, actor."
