nontransferences:/ˌnɒntrænsˈfɜːrənsɪz/
noun
非转移; 非转让; 未转让
The absence of transference, especially in psychoanalysis.  The state or quality of not being transferred.
The report detailed the nontransferences of property rights within the family.  该报告详细说明了家庭内部财产权的未转让情况。
Near synonyms: non-transfer, non-transmission
Opposite: transferences, transfers
