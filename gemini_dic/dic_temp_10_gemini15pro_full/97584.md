inhabitable:/ɪnˈhæbɪtəbl/
adjective
可居住的；适宜居住的
Suitable for living in; offering the basic necessities for human life.
The house is old, but it's still inhabitable. 这房子很旧了，但是仍然可以住。
habitable, livable, occupiable
uninhabitable, unlivable
adjective
