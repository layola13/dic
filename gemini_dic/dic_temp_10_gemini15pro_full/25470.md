system: /ˈsɪstəm/ | n. | 系统 | A set of things working together as parts of a mechanism or an interconnecting network; a complex whole. | Example 1: The computer system crashed. 电脑系统崩溃了。

ETYMOLOGY->
"bulletin" comes from French, ultimately from Italian "bulletino," a diminutive of "bulla," meaning "seal, official document."  "Board" comes from Old English "bord," meaning "plank, table." "System" comes from Latin "systema," from Greek "systēma," meaning "organized whole, a whole compounded of parts."
