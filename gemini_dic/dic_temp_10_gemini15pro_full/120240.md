mingledly:/ˈmɪŋɡəldli/
adverb|混合地，混杂地|In a mingled manner; mixed together.|The scents of flowers and spices mingledly filled the air. 花和香料的气味混合在一起弥漫在空气中。|近义词: mixedly, combinedly, blendedly|反义词: separately, distinctly, individually|词性: 副词
词源:  From mingle (v.) + -ly (adv. suffix).  "Mingle" comes from Old English *mengjan, from Proto-Germanic *mangjanan, related to Old Norse menga "to mix."
