coal-fired:/ˈkoʊlˌfaɪərd/
adjective
燃煤的
powered by burning coal.
Many countries are phasing out coal-fired power plants. 许多国家正在逐步淘汰燃煤发电厂。
Coal-fired power generation is a major source of pollution. 燃煤发电是主要的污染源。
Synonyms: coal-burning, coal-powered
Antonyms: gas-fired, oil-fired, nuclear-powered
词性: adjective
