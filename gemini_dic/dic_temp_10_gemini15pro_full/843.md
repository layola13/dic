abstracter's:/æbˈstræktərz/
n.
摘要员的；抄录员的
Belonging to or associated with an abstracter (a person who prepares summaries of legal documents or other writings).
The abstracter's office is down the hall. = 摘要员的办公室在走廊尽头。
The abstracter's notes were meticulously detailed. = 摘要员的笔记非常详细。
近义词:summarizer's, epitomizer's
反义词:None
词性:名词所有格


ETYMOLOGY->
From abstracter + 's (possessive suffix).  "Abstracter" comes from the verb "abstract," which has roots in the Latin word "abstractus," the past participle of "abstrahere" (to draw away from).
