fraternise:/ˈfrætərnʌɪz/ (UK), /ˈfrætərˌnaɪz/ (US)| v.| 交往；亲善；（尤指与敌方或不应交往的人）称兄道弟|The officers were warned not to fraternise with the enemy. 军官们被警告不要与敌人 fraternize。|Synonyms: associate, socialise, mingle |Antonyms: shun, avoid| Verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From French *fraterniser*, from Late Latin *fraternizare*, from Latin *fraternus* "brotherly," from *frater* "brother".
