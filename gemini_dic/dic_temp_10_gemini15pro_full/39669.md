conservatorships:/kənˈsɜːrvətərʃɪps/ | n. | 监护权，保护人身份 | The legal arrangement where a court appoints a person to manage the financial or personal affairs of another adult deemed incapable of managing their own affairs. | Britney Spears was under a conservatorship controlled by her father. 布兰妮·斯皮尔斯曾处于她父亲控制的监护权之下。| synonyms: guardianship, trusteeship | antonyms: independence, autonomy | noun


conservatorship:/kənˈsɜːrvətərʃɪp/ | n. | 监护权，保护人身份 | The act or process of conserving something.  | The museum has a department dedicated to the conservatorship of ancient artifacts.  博物馆有一个专门负责保护古代文物的部门。| synonyms: preservation, protection | antonyms: neglect, destruction | noun
