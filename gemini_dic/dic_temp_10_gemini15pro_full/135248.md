nonpalpably:/nɒnˈpælpəbli/
adverb
难以察觉地；无法感知地
Not able to be felt by touch; intangible.
The tension in the room was nonpalpably thick. 房间里的紧张气氛浓得化不开，却又难以察觉。
Her influence on the team was nonpalpably pervasive. 她对团队的影响无处不在，却又难以察觉。
Synonyms: imperceptibly, intangibly, subtly
Antonyms: palpably, noticeably, obviously
词性: 副词
