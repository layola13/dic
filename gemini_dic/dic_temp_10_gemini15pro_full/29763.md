catsuit: /ˈkætsuːt/
noun
紧身衣
a close-fitting one-piece garment, typically made of stretchy material, that covers the body from the neck to the ankles or wrists.
Michelle wore a black catsuit to the party.  米歇尔穿了一件黑色紧身衣去参加派对。

近义词: jumpsuit, bodysuit, unitard
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps loose clothing or regular clothes)
词性: n.
