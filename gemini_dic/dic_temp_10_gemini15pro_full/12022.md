arthrozoic:/ˌɑːrθrəˈzoʊɪk/
adjective
节肢动物传播的
Relating to or denoting the passive dispersal of microorganisms (such as bacteria, viruses, or protozoa) by arthropods.
例句：Arthrozoic transmission of disease can occur through the bites of infected insects. 节肢动物传播的疾病可以通过受感染昆虫的叮咬发生。

例句：The study investigated the arthrozoic spread of the virus. 这项研究调查了病毒的节肢动物传播。
近义词:vector-borne
反义词:None
词性:adjective
