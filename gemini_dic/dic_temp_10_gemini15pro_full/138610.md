nucleal:/ˈnjuːkliəl/
adjective
核的；细胞核的
Relating to or constituting a nucleus, especially of a cell.
The nucleal region of a prokaryotic cell contains the genetic material. 原核细胞的核区包含遗传物质。
The nucleal membrane protects the cell's DNA. 核膜保护细胞的DNA。
Synonyms: nuclear
Antonyms: anuclear, extranuclear
