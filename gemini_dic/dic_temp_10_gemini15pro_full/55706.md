diurnals: /daɪˈɜːrnlz/ | n. | 日间活动的动物; 日间发生的事件; 日报 | Animals that are active during the day; events that occur during the day; daily publications. | Diurnals, like butterflies, are most active during the daytime.  蝴蝶之类的昼行动物在白天最为活跃。| Synonyms: daytime animals, diurnal creatures | Antonyms: nocturnals, nocturnal creatures | noun

diurnal: /daɪˈɜːrnl/ | adj. | 白天的; 每日的; 周日的 | Of or during the day; daily; occurring every day. | The diurnal rhythm of the tides is influenced by the moon. 潮汐的昼夜节律受月亮的影响。| Synonyms: daily, daytime | Antonyms: nocturnal, nightly | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diurnalis, from Latin diurnus (“daily”), from dies (“day”).
