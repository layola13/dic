mousy:/ˈmaʊsi/ (US), /ˈmaʊzi/ (UK)| adj.|鼠灰色的，胆怯的，乏味的|Resembling a mouse, especially in being timid or quiet.  Having a quiet and shy personality; lacking confidence or assertiveness. Of a dull grayish brown color.|例句：She had mousy brown hair. 她有一头鼠灰色的棕色头发。

例句：He was a mousy little man, easily intimidated. 他是一个胆小怕事的小个子男人，很容易被吓倒。|近义词：timid, shy, meek, retiring, unassertive, dull, drab|反义词：bold, assertive, confident, vibrant, bright|词性:形容词
