membraneless:/ˈmɛmbreɪnlɪs/| adj.|无膜的，无隔膜的|Lacking a membrane; not enclosed or separated by a membrane.|Scientists are increasingly interested in membraneless organelles within cells. 科学家们对细胞内无膜细胞器越来越感兴趣。|Membraneless organelles play crucial roles in various cellular processes. 无膜细胞器在各种细胞过程中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：non-membranous|反义词：membranous|词性: 形容词

词源: membrane + -less. "membrane" comes from Latin "membrana" meaning "skin, parchment". "-less" is an Old English suffix meaning "without".
