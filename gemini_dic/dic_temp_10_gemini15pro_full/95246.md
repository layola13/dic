incan:/ˈɪŋkən/| adj. n.|印加的；印加人|Relating to the Inca civilization, a pre-Columbian empire in the Andes of South America. incan:/ˈɪŋkən/| n.|印加人|A member of the Inca people.|例句：The Incans were skilled engineers and astronomers. 印加人是技艺精湛的工程师和天文学家。|近义词：Peruvian, Andean|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
