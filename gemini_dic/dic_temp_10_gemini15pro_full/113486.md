lyrid: /ˈlaɪrɪd/ | n. | 天琴座流星雨;  天琴座流星群(成员) | A meteor shower that occurs annually around April 22, associated with the comet Thatcher.  These meteors appear to radiate from the constellation Lyra. | The Lyrid meteor shower peaks in late April. 天琴座流星雨在四月下旬达到高峰。| The Lyrids are not as prolific as the Perseids, but they can still produce a beautiful display. 天琴座流星雨不如英仙座流星雨那么多，但它们仍然可以产生美丽的景象。 | synonyms: meteor shower, shooting stars | antonyms: N/A | noun
