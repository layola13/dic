orbit:/ˈɔːrbɪt/ (US) /ˈɔːbɪt/ (UK)| n. v.|轨道; 运行轨道; 眼眶;势力范围|The path followed by a celestial object or spacecraft as it moves around another object due to gravity.  To move in an orbit around something.|例句：The satellite is now in a stable orbit. 这颗卫星现在处于稳定的轨道上。|近义词：path, trajectory, circuit, revolution|反义词:deviation, digression|noun
orbit:/ˈɔːrbɪt/ (US) /ˈɔːbɪt/ (UK)| v.|绕轨道运行;环绕…运行|To move in an orbit around something.|例句：The Earth orbits the Sun. 地球绕太阳运行。|近义词：circle, revolve, rotate, go around|反义词:descend, plummet|verb
