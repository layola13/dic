gravelike: /ˈɡreɪvlaɪk/ | adj. | 像坟墓的，阴森的 | Resembling a grave; suggestive of death or burial; gloomy and somber. | The abandoned house had a gravelike silence. 这栋废弃的房子有一种像坟墓一样的寂静。|  The room was dark and gravelike. 房间里阴暗得像坟墓一样。 | synonyms: sepulchral, tomblike, funereal, somber, gloomy | antonyms: lively, cheerful, bright, vibrant | adjective

Etymology:  "grave" (meaning serious or solemn, or relating to a burial place) + "-like" (suffix meaning resembling or similar to). The word "grave" ultimately comes from the Latin "gravis" meaning heavy or weighty.
