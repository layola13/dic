cylinderlike:/ˈsɪlɪndərˌlaɪk/
adjective
圆柱状的; 像圆柱的
Resembling a cylinder in shape.  Having the form of a cylinder.
The bacteria are cylinderlike in shape. 这些细菌呈圆柱状。
The building has a cylinderlike structure. 这座建筑有一个圆柱形的结构。
近义词:cylindrical, columnar, rod-shaped, tubular
反义词:spherical, globular
