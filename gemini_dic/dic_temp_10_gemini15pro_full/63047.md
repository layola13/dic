enrichers:/ɪnˈrɪtʃərz/
n.|丰富者；使丰富的人（或事物）|People who or things which enrich something, typically culture, experience, or quality of life.  |The program brought in many cultural enrichers from around the world.|Synonyms: improvers, enhancers, augmenters|Antonyms:diminishers, depleters|noun
