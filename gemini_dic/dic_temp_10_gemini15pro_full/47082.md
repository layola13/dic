datum:/ˈdeɪtəm/ (US), /ˈdeɪtəm/ (UK)| n. |数据，资料；基准；已知数|A single piece of information, often used in scientific or technical contexts. A fact, figure, or statistic.  A fixed starting point of a scale or series of calculations. |例句：The researchers collected numerous data for their study. 研究人员为他们的研究收集了大量数据。

|例句：This datum is essential for accurate calculations.  这个数据对于精确计算至关重要。


|近义词：fact, figure, statistic, information, piece of information|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "misinformation" or "inaccuracy") |词性:noun

注意事项:

* Datum is the singular form. Data is the plural form, but is often used as a singular mass noun, especially in everyday language.

* While "datum" is technically correct for the singular, "data point" is more commonly used in modern English when referring to a single piece of information.
