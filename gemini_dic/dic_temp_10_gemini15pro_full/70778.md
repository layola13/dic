fingal:/ˈfɪŋɡəl/| n.|芬戈尔; 一种海蚀洞|A Fingal is a sea cave, particularly one formed in basalt. Specifically, it refers to Fingal's Cave on the island of Staffa in Scotland.|例句：We took a boat trip to see Fingal's Cave, a spectacular fingal on the Scottish coast. 我们乘船去看了芬戈尔洞穴，这是苏格兰海岸一个壮观的海蚀洞。

例句：The poet was inspired by the majestic fingal, its basalt columns rising from the sea. 诗人被雄伟的芬戈尔所启发，它的玄武岩柱从海中升起。|近义词：sea cave, grotto|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps 'promontory' or 'headland' as the opposite landform)|词性:noun
