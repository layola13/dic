filleted:/ˈfɪlɪtɪd/
adj.
去骨的，切成鱼片的
Having had the bones removed, typically in preparation for cooking.
The chef prepared filleted salmon for the main course.  厨师准备了去骨鲑鱼作为主菜。
近义词:boneless, deboned
反义词:bone-in
词性:形容词
