hydrobarometer:/ˌhaɪdrəbəˈrɒmɪtər/ /ˌhaɪdroʊbəˈrɑːmɪtər/| n. |水压计，液体压力计|An instrument for measuring the pressure of liquids, especially water.  It can be used to determine water depth or to monitor changes in water levels.|The hydrobarometer indicated a significant increase in water pressure after the heavy rainfall. 大雨过后，水压计显示水压显著升高。|Synonyms: manometer, pressure gauge|Antonyms: N/A|noun
