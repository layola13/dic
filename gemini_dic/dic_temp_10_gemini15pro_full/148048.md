overrun:/ˌōvərˈrən/ /ˌoʊvərˈrʌn/ | v. n. | 超过；泛滥；蹂躏；超出限度 | To spread over or occupy excessively; to exceed a limit, especially of time or cost.  Also, an act of overrunning. | The project overran its budget by millions.  这个项目超出了预算数百万美元。| 近义词：overwhelm, infest, exceed, invade | 反义词：underestimate, contain, recede | verb

overrun:/ˌōvərˈrən/ /ˌoʊvərˈrʌn/ | n. | 超出限度；泛滥；蔓延 | The act or an instance of overrunning; an amount by which something overruns. | The overrun of weeds in the garden was a problem. 花园里杂草丛生是个问题。| 近义词：excess, surplus, overflow | 反义词：deficit, shortage | noun
