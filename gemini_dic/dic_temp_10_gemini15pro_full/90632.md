humanitarian:/hjuːˌmænɪˈtɛːriən/
noun.|人道主义者；慈善家|A person who seeks to promote human welfare; a philanthropist.|例句：The humanitarian worked tirelessly to provide aid to the refugees.  这位人道主义者不知疲倦地为难民提供援助。|近义词：philanthropist, altruist, benefactor|反义词：misanthrope, egoist|n.
humanitarian:/hjuːˌmænɪˈtɛːriən/
adjective.|人道主义的；博爱的|Concerned with or seeking to promote human welfare.|例句：The organization launched a humanitarian mission to the disaster zone. 该组织向灾区发起了一项人道主义援助任务。|近义词：charitable, benevolent, compassionate|反义词：cruel, inhumane, merciless|adj.

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *humanitaire*, from *humain* "human" (from Latin *humanus*) + *-itaire* (from Latin *-arius*).
