objecting:/əbˈdʒektɪŋ/
verb
反对；不赞成
present participle of object
例句：He was objecting to the proposal. 他反对这个提议。

objecting:/əbˈdʒektɪŋ/
adjective
表示反对的；不赞成的
modifies a noun and describes it as expressing opposition
例句：An objecting voice from the crowd interrupted the speaker.  人群中一个反对的声音打断了演讲者。


近义词: opposing, dissenting, protesting, resisting
反义词: agreeing, approving, consenting, supporting
词性: verb (present participle), adjective
