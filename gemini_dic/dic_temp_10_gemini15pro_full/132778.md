nondualisms:/ˌnɒnˈduːəlɪzəmz/ | n. | 非二元论 | The philosophical or spiritual concept that rejects the division of reality into two opposing categories, such as mind and body, self and other, or good and evil.  It emphasizes the interconnectedness and unity of all things. |  Many Eastern philosophies embrace nondualisms, seeing the universe as a single, unified whole.  许多东方哲学信奉非二元论，将宇宙视为一个单一、统一的整体。|  Nondualism emphasizes the interconnectedness of all beings. 非二元论强调所有存在的相互关联性。 | synonyms: monism, oneness, unity | antonyms: dualism, dichotomy | noun
