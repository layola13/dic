disallowableness:/ˌdɪsəˈlaʊəbəlnəs/
n.|不被允许; 不容许; 不许可|The disallowableness of the claim frustrated the applicant.  这项申请不被允许，这让申请人感到沮丧。|English Explanation: The quality or state of being unacceptable or ineligible, typically in a legal or official context.|例句：The disallowableness of certain evidence in court can significantly impact the outcome of a case.  某些证据在法庭上不被采纳会极大地影响案件的结果。|近义词：impermissibility, unacceptability, ineligibility|反义词：allowability, permissibility, acceptability|词性: noun


词源:
From disallow + -able + -ness.  Disallow comes from the Old French desalouer, meaning "to disapprove."
