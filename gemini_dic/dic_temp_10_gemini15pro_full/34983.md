clog:/klɑːɡ/ | n. | 阻塞；障碍；木屐 |  Something that blocks or impedes movement or progress; a wooden shoe. | The sink was clogged with hair. 水槽被头发堵塞了。 |  She wore clogs in the garden. 她在花园里穿木屐。 | 近义词: blockage, obstruction, impediment, wooden shoe | 反义词: clearing, opening | 词性: 名词

clog:/klɑːɡ/ | v. | 阻塞；堵塞；阻碍 | To block or impede movement or progress. |  Grease clogged the drain. 油脂堵塞了排水沟。 | The roads were clogged with traffic. 道路被交通堵塞了。 | 近义词: block, obstruct, impede, hinder | 反义词: clear, open, facilitate | 词性: 动词
