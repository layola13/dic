carbonating:/ˈkɑːrbəneɪtɪŋ/
verb
碳酸化
The process of adding carbon dioxide to a liquid, typically water, to create carbonation.
They are carbonating the water for the soda. 他们在给汽水用的水加碳酸气。
aerating, fizzing
degassing
v.
