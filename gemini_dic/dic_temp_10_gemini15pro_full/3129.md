aesthesia:/ɛsˈθiːʒə/ /ɛsˈθiːziə/| n.|感觉；知觉；感受性|The capacity for sensation or feeling.  The ability to experience physical sensations such as touch, pain, heat, or cold.|He regained aesthesia in his fingers after the surgery.  他手术后手指恢复了知觉。|近义词：sensation, feeling, perception, sensitivity|反义词:anaesthesia, numbness|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek aisthēsis (αἴσθησις, “sensation, perception, feeling”).
