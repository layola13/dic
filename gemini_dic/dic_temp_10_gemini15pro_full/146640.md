overhardened:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrdnd/ | adj. | 过度硬化的 | Made too hard, especially by heat treatment, resulting in brittleness and reduced toughness. | The overhardened steel was prone to cracking. 过度硬化的钢容易开裂。|The knife blade was overhardened and snapped under pressure. 刀刃过度硬化，在压力下断裂了。|  近义词: brittle, excessively hardened | 反义词: soft, malleable, ductile |词性:形容词


overhardened:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrdnd/ | v. |  过度硬化 | To make something too hard, especially by heat treatment. | They overhardened the metal, making it brittle. 他们把金属过度硬化，使其变脆。| Be careful not to overharden the chocolate, or it will be difficult to work with. 小心不要过度硬化巧克力，否则会很难操作。| 近义词: harden excessively | 反义词: soften, temper | 词性:动词
