divulge:/daɪˈvʌldʒ/
v.
泄露，透露
to make known (private or sensitive information)
例句：
The company divulged its plans for a new product.
公司透露了其新产品的计划。

He refused to divulge the source of his information.
他拒绝透露消息来源。

近义词：reveal, disclose, unveil, expose
反义词：conceal, hide, suppress, withhold
词性：动词
