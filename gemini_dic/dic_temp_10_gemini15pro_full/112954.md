lugworm:/ˈlʌɡwɜːrm/| n. |沙蚕，钓饵|A marine worm (Arenicola marina) that lives in burrows in sand or mud, used as bait by anglers.|例句：The fisherman dug up some lugworms for bait. 渔夫挖了一些沙蚕作诱饵。|例句：Lugworms are a popular bait for sea fishing. 沙蚕是海钓常用的诱饵。|近义词：sandworm, baitworm|反义词:None|Noun
