anodize:/ˈænədaɪz/| vt.| 阳极氧化，阳极电镀|To coat (a metal, especially aluminum) with a protective oxide layer by an electrolytic process in which the metal forms the anode.|The aluminum parts were anodized to prevent corrosion.  铝部件经过阳极氧化处理以防止腐蚀。|例句：The company specializes in anodizing aluminum parts for the aerospace industry.  这家公司专门为航空航天工业提供铝部件的阳极氧化处理。|近义词：coat, plate, oxidize|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑  strip, remove coating)|词性: verb
