abecedary:/ˌeɪbiˈsiːdəri/| n.|字母表；入门书|A book or other learning resource arranged alphabetically, especially one intended as a primer or elementary textbook.|The child learned his letters from an abecedary.|近义词:alphabet, primer, ABC book|反义词:None|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *abecedarium*, from Latin abecedarius ("alphabetical"), from the first four letters of the Latin alphabet: a, b, c, d.  Ultimately from Phoenician aleph, beth, gimel, daleth.

USAGE->
The abecedary is a valuable tool for young learners. = 这本字母表对年轻的学习者来说是一个很有价值的工具。

He used an abecedary to teach his children the alphabet. = 他用字母表教他的孩子们字母。
