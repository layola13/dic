arrowlike:/ˈæroʊlaɪk/
adjective
像箭的，箭状的
Resembling an arrow in shape or speed.
The bird had an arrowlike beak.  这只鸟有着像箭一样的喙。
The car shot forward with arrowlike speed.  这辆车以箭一般的速度向前冲去。
近义词:arrow-shaped, sagittal
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider describing the opposite shape, e.g., rounded, blunt)
词性: adjective
