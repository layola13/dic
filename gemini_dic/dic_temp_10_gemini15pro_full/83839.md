hala:/ˈhaːlə/ (US), /ˈhɑːlə/ (UK)| int.|嗨，你好 (一种非正式的问候)|An informal greeting, similar to "hi" or "hey".|Example: Hala, cuz! What's up? 嗨，表弟！怎么了？

|Example: Hala, how's it going? 你好，最近怎么样？

|近义词：hi, hey, hello|反义词: (Interjections generally don't have antonyms) |词性:感叹词

注意事项:
"Hala" is primarily used in informal settings, particularly among younger people. It's common in some dialects of American English and Filipino English.  It doesn't have a direct opposite or a wide range of grammatical uses outside of being a greeting.  It's mainly an interjection.
