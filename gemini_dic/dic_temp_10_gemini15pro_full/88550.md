hitch-hike:/ˈhɪtʃˌhaɪk/| v. n.|搭便车，免费搭车旅行|To travel by getting free rides from passing vehicles.|Example: We hitch-hiked across Europe last summer. 我们去年夏天搭便车游遍了欧洲.|Synonym: thumb a ride, bum a ride|Antonym: drive, fly|verb, noun

hitch-hike:/ˈhɪtʃˌhaɪk/| n.|搭便车旅行|A journey made by hitchhiking.|Example: He went on a hitch-hike across the country. 他搭便车环游全国.|Synonym: backpacking trip (if the hitchhiking involves backpacking)|Antonym:  road trip (in a personal vehicle), flight|noun
