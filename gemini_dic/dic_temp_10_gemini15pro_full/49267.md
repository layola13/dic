deliberation:/dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 仔细考虑，商议 | Careful consideration or discussion before decision. | The jury retired for two hours of deliberation before reaching a verdict. 陪审团退庭商议两小时后才做出裁决。 | Synonyms: consideration, reflection, contemplation, discussion | Antonyms: impulsiveness, rashness | noun


deliberate: /dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/ | adj. | 故意的，深思熟虑的 | Done consciously and intentionally. |  The attack was a deliberate attempt to destabilize the government. 这次袭击是蓄意破坏政府稳定的企图。 | Synonyms: intentional, planned, conscious, purposeful | Antonyms: accidental, unintentional, impulsive | adjective


deliberate: /dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/ | v. | 仔细考虑，商议 | Engage in long and careful consideration. | The committee deliberated for months before issuing its report. 委员会经过几个月的审议才发布了报告。 | Synonyms: consider, ponder, contemplate, discuss | Antonyms: rush, hasten | verb
