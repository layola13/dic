词源：
From Middle English *alburn, *auborne, from Old French alborne (“auburn, blond”), from Latin alburnus (“whitish”), from albus (“white”).  The sense shifted from "whitish, blonde" to "reddish-brown" by association with the color of dawn.  The current spelling is influenced by brown.
