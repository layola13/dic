what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) |pron. det. adv.| 什么，多么 |Used to ask for information specifying something.| Example: What color is your car? 你的车是什么颜色？| Synonym: which, whatever| Antonym: N/A | pronoun/determiner/adverb

USAGE->
Simple Present:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他以什么为生？

Present Continuous:
What are you cooking for dinner? = 你晚饭在做什么？
What is she working on right now? = 她现在在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to deserve this award? = 她做了什么才配得上这个奖？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been doing since I last saw you? = 自从我上次见到你以来你一直在做什么？
What has he been studying at university? = 他在大学里一直在学什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do last weekend? = 你上周末做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the main course? = 他们在主菜之前吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before the interruption? =  打断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been working on for so long? = 他们一直在做什么这么久？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen if we don't act now? = 如果我们现在不采取行动会发生什么？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed in the next five years? = 未来五年会发生什么变化？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2030? = 到2030年，你过去十年一直在做什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候，她将一直在学习什么？
