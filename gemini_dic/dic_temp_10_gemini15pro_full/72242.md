floridly:/ˈflɔːrɪdli/ | adv. |华丽地；绚丽地；花哨地|In a florid way; with excessive ornamentation.  Characterized by elaborate or excessively ornamented language or style.|He spoke floridly about his accomplishments, exaggerating every detail. 他华丽地讲述了他的成就，夸大了每一个细节。|The author's floridly descriptive prose evoked a vivid image of the scene. 作者花哨的描述性散文唤起了对场景的生动图像。|近义词：ornately, elaborately, flamboyantly, ostentatiously|反义词：simply, plainly, subtly, understatedly|词性:副词
词源: From Latin *floridus* “flowering, blooming, flourishing,” from *flor-,* stem of *flos* "flower."
