furlike:/ˈfɜːrlaɪk/
adjective
像毛皮的; 毛皮状的
Resembling fur; furry.
The cat had furlike markings on its back. 这只猫的背上有像毛皮一样的斑纹。
The fabric has a soft, furlike texture.  这种织物有一种柔软的、像毛皮一样的质感。

近义词:furry, downy, velvety
反义词:bald, smooth, hairless
词性:adjective
