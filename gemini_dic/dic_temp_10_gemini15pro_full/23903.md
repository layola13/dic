breakdown:/ˈbreɪkdaʊn/ | n. v. | 故障；崩溃；分解；分类 | a failure to function or progress; a sudden collapse in physical or mental health; the separation of something into its component parts; an analysis or explanation of something | The car suffered a breakdown on the motorway. 这辆车在高速公路上抛锚了。| synonyms: failure, malfunction, collapse, decomposition, analysis | antonyms: success, repair, recovery, synthesis | noun

breakdown:/breɪk ˈdaʊn/ | v. | 损坏；分解；（身体或精神）垮掉；（谈判等）失败 | to stop working; to separate something into smaller parts; to lose control of one’s emotions |  The peace talks broke down. 和平谈判破裂了。| synonyms:  malfunction, decompose, collapse, fail | antonyms: function, repair, recover, succeed | verb
