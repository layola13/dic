eryngo:/ɪˈræŋɡoʊ/ (US), /ɪˈræŋɡəʊ/ (UK)
n.
海冬青，刺芹
a plant of the parsley family, typically growing on salt marshes and having spiny leaves and blue or white flower heads.
The eryngo grew profusely along the coastline.  海冬青沿着海岸线茂盛地生长。
The pickled eryngo was a surprisingly tasty appetizer.  腌制的海冬青是一道出人意料的美味开胃菜。
synonyms: sea holly, eryngium
