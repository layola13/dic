decrease:/dɪˈkriːs/ /ˈdiːkriːs/| n. v.|减少，降低|A decrease is a reduction in the amount, number, or size of something. To decrease means to become smaller or less, or to make something become smaller or less.|The store saw a decrease in sales this month. 这个月的商店销售额下降了。|近义词：decline, reduction, diminish, lessen|反义词:increase, rise, grow, expand|词性:名词,动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *decreistre (modern French décroître), from Latin decrescere "to grow less," from de- "down" + crescere "to grow."
