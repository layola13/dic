cosponsorships:/koʊˈspɑːnsərʃɪps/
n.
共同赞助，联合赞助
The event benefited from several corporate cosponsorships.  这个活动受益于几家企业的共同赞助。
The project relies on cosponsorships from various organizations. 这个项目依赖于来自不同组织的联合赞助。
Synonyms: joint sponsorships, shared sponsorships
Antonyms: sole sponsorship, independent sponsorship
noun
