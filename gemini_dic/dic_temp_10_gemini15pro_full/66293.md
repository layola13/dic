excoriating:/ɪkˈskɔːrieɪtɪŋ/| adj.|严厉批评的；苛刻的；痛斥的|Severely critical; harshly censorious; strongly condemning.|The newspaper published an excoriating review of the politician's new book. 这家报纸发表了一篇严厉批评这位政治家新书的评论。

The coach delivered an excoriating speech to his team after their dismal performance. 教练在球队表现糟糕后，对他们进行了严厉的训斥。|近义词：scathing, blistering, harsh, severe, critical, condemnatory|反义词：praising, laudatory, complimentary, approving|词性:形容词
词源:From Latin excoriāre (“to flay, strip off the hide”), from ex- (“out, off”) + corium (“hide, skin”).
