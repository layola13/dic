ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dosen, *dosien, from Old Norse *dusa, from Proto-Germanic *dusōną (“to be sleepy, be dazed”). Cognate with Swedish dåsa (“to be sleepy, be in a doze”), Icelandic dúsa (“to doze, fall into a light sleep”).
