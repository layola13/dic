infallible:/ɪnˈfæləbəl/| adj.|不会犯错的；绝对可靠的；万无一失的|incapable of making mistakes or being wrong.  Not liable to fail.|例句：No human being is infallible. 没有人是不会犯错的。|例句：This method is not infallible, but it usually works.  这个方法并非万无一失，但通常有效。|近义词：unerring, faultless, impeccable, perfect|反义词：fallible, errant, flawed|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin infallibilis, from in- ‘not’ + fallibilis (see fallible).
