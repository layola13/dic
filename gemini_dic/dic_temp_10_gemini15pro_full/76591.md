galling:/ˈɡɔːlɪŋ/| adj.|令人恼火的，使人愤怒的|Causing irritation or annoyance; exasperating.|例句：It was galling to have to apologize to him.  不得不向他道歉真是令人恼火。|例句：The team's galling defeat in the final was a bitter pill to swallow. 球队在决赛中令人恼火的失利是一颗难以下咽的苦果。|近义词：annoying, irritating, vexing, exasperating, infuriating|反义词：soothing, pleasing, gratifying|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "gall" (meaning "to irritate or annoy"), which comes from Old French "galer" (to irritate, chafe), ultimately from a Germanic source.
