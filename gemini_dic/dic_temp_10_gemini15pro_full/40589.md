convalescence:/ˌkɒnvəˈlesəns/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈlesəns/| n. |康复期；恢复期|The period of gradual recovery of health and strength after illness or injury. |She spent a month in convalescence after her surgery. 她手术后花了一个月的时间康复。|近义词：recovery, recuperation, rehabilitation|反义词: relapse, deterioration|名词

convalescent:/ˌkɒnvəˈlesənt/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈlesənt/| adj. n. |康复的；恢复期的；康复中的病人|Recovering from an illness or medical treatment. |The convalescent patient was moved to a private room.  正在康复的病人被转移到了一间单人病房。|近义词：recovering, recuperating|反义词: sick, ill|形容词，名词
