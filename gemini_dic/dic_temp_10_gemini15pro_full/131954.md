noncontributory:/ˌnɒnkənˈtrɪbjʊtəri/ (US) /ˌnɑːnkənˈtrɪbjətɔːri/| adj.|非缴费的，无须供款的|Not requiring a contribution; especially not requiring a financial contribution from the employee or recipient.|例句：A noncontributory pension plan is funded entirely by the employer. 非缴费型养老金计划完全由雇主出资。|例句：She receives a noncontributory disability benefit. 她领取的是非缴费型残疾福利。|近义词：free, gratuitous, unearned|反义词：contributory|词性：形容词
