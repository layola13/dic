athermancy:/ˌeɪˈθɜːrmənsi/ /ˌæˈθɜːrmənsi/
| n. | 不透热性；隔热性 | The property of being opaque to radiant heat; the ability to prevent the transmission of radiant heat. |  The athermancy of the material made it ideal for insulating the spacecraft.  这种材料的不透热性使其成为航天器绝缘的理想选择。 |  opacity to radiant heat, impermeability to radiant heat | diathermancy, thermal conductivity | noun
