demise:/dɪˈmaɪz/| n.|死亡，终止，转让|Death, end, transfer of property, especially by lease or will.  A cessation of existence or activity. |例句：The demise of the company was a shock to everyone. 公司的倒闭让每个人都感到震惊。

例句：The king's demise led to a power struggle. 国王的去世导致了权力斗争。


|近义词：death, decease, end, termination, expiration, extinction |反义词: birth, commencement, start, beginning |词性:noun


demise:/dɪˈmaɪz/| vt.|遗赠；转让（财产）|To transfer (real property) by will or lease. To convey (something) to someone else. |例句：He demised his estate to his son. 他把他的财产遗赠给了他的儿子。

例句：The property was demised to the new owner.  该房产转让给了新的所有者。

|近义词：bequeath, transfer, convey |反义词: inherit, receive |词性:verb
