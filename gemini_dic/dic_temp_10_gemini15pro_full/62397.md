endoderm:/ˈendəˌdɜːrm/ /ˈendəˌdɝːm/ | n. | 内胚层 | The innermost of the three primary germ layers of an embryo, developing into the lining of the digestive tract and associated structures. |  The endoderm gives rise to the lining of the digestive tract. 内胚层发育为消化道的内壁。

The lungs develop from the endoderm. 肺由内胚层发育而来。 | 近义词: hypoblast (in older usage) | 反义词: ectoderm, mesoderm | 词性: noun
