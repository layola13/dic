antarchistic: /ˌæntɑːrˈkɪstɪk/ | adj. | 反对等级制度的，反对统治的 | Opposed to hierarchy and rule; anti-authoritarian. |  Example: His antarchistic views led him to reject all forms of government. 他的反等级制度的观点使他反对一切形式的政府。 | Example: The antarchistic collective operated without a leader or formal structure. 这个反等级制度的集体在没有领导人或正式结构的情况下运作。 | Synonyms: anti-authoritarian, anarchist, rebellious, nonconformist | Antonyms: authoritarian, hierarchical, conformist | Word Type: Adjective
