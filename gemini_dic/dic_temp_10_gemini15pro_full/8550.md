antichristianity:/ˌæntiˌkrɪstiˈænɪti/
n.
反基督教；反基督教精神
Opposition to or hostility toward Christianity, its doctrines, or its followers.
The rise of secularism is often seen by some as a form of antichristianity.  一些人常常将世俗主义的兴起视为反基督教的一种形式。
His writings were condemned for their perceived antichristianity. 他的著作因被认为是反基督教的而受到谴责。
近义词: irreligion, secularism, atheism
反义词: Christianity, piety
词性: 名词
