effaceable:/ɪˈfeɪsəbl/ | adj. | 可抹去的，可消除的 | Able to be effaced; erasable.  Something that can be removed or made to seem less important. | The pencil marks were effaceable. 铅笔痕迹是可以擦掉的。| synonyms: erasable, deletable, removable, obliterable | antonyms: indelible, permanent, lasting | adjective
