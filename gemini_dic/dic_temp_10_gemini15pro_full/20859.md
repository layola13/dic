blatherer:/ˈblæðərər/ (US), /ˈblæðərə(r)/ (UK)| n. |饶舌者，喋喋不休的人|A person who talks foolishly and at length.|He's such a blatherer, he talks for hours about nothing. 他真是个饶舌的人，一聊就是几个小时，却什么也没说。|Synonyms: chatterbox, windbag, prattler|Antonyms: reticent person, quiet person|noun
