bergo:/ˈbɜːrɡoʊ/
n.
a loose hooded cloak or robe
A bergo is a loose hooded cloak or robe often worn for protection from the elements or for modesty.
She wore a bergo to protect herself from the sun. 她穿着一件伯戈衫遮阳。
cloak, robe, mantle
dress, suit
noun
