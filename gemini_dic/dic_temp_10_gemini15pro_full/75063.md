frightfully:/ˈfraɪtfəli/ | adv. | 非常地，可怕地 | Extremely; in a frightening way. | The house was frightfully expensive. 这房子贵得吓人。

The movie was frightfully boring. 这部电影无聊得可怕。| 近义词: terribly, awfully, dreadfully, extremely, intensely | 反义词: slightly, mildly, moderately | 词性: 副词

词源: From fright + -ful + -ly.  "Fright" comes from the Old English *fryhto, from Proto-Germanic *frihtuz. "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by." "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
