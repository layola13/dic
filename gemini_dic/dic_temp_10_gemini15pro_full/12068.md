artificiality:/ˌɑːrtɪfɪʃiˈælɪti/ | n. | 人工性；不自然；矫揉造作 |  The quality of being made or produced by human beings rather than occurring naturally, often implying a lack of naturalness or spontaneity. | The artificiality of her smile made me uncomfortable. 她笑容的矫揉造作让我感到不舒服。 |  artificialness, unnaturalness, contrivedness, affectation | naturalness, genuineness, authenticity, spontaneity | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *artificialitas, from Latin artificialis (“of or belonging to art”), from artificium (“work of art, skill”) + -alis (suffix forming adjectives).  The English word was first used in the 15th century.
