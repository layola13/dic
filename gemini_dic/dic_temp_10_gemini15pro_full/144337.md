outstanding:/ˌaʊtˈstændɪŋ/| adj.|杰出的，优秀的；未偿付的，未完成的|Excellent, remarkable; still owed, unpaid|She has an outstanding record of achievement. 她有着杰出的成就记录。|近义词：excellent, remarkable, exceptional, noteworthy, prominent|反义词：poor, mediocre, ordinary, unexceptional|词性:形容词

outstanding:/ˌaʊtˈstændɪŋ/| n.|未偿贷款；未完成的事项|An amount of money still owed; something not yet done|The company has several outstandings on its accounts. 该公司账户上有几笔未偿贷款。|近义词：debt, arrears, due|反义词：payment, settlement|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English outstandinge, present participle of outstanden, from Old English ūtstundan (“to stand out, project, excel”), from ūt (“out”) + standan (“to stand”).
