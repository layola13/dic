crut:/krʌt/
noun
支柱，拐杖
A support, typically a wooden post.
The old man leaned heavily on his crut.  这位老人沉重地倚在他的拐杖上。
Synonyms: support, prop, stay, post, pole
Antonyms: weakness, instability
词性: 名词

crutch:/krʌtʃ/
noun
拐杖
A staff or support used by a person who is lame or otherwise disabled in walking.
She walks with a crutch because of her injured leg. 她因为腿受伤拄着拐杖走路。
Synonyms: support, prop, stay
Antonyms: none
词性: 名词
