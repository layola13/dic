directee:/ˌdɪrɛkˈtiː/ /ˌdaɪrɛkˈtiː/
noun
被指导者，受指导者
a person who is given direction or guidance by another, especially in spiritual matters.
The directee shared their struggles with the spiritual director.  这位受指导者与精神导师分享了他们的挣扎。
The spiritual director met with their directee weekly. 精神导师每周与他们的受指导者见面。
Synonyms: protégé, student, pupil, mentee
Antonyms: director, mentor, guide, advisor
n.
