intermenstrual:/ˌɪntəˈmenstruəl/ | adj. |月经间的，两次月经之间的|Occurring between menstrual periods.|She experienced some intermenstrual bleeding. 她经历了一些经期之间的出血。|近义词:metrorrhagia|反义词:N/A|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From inter- + menstrual.  "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between," and "menstrual" relates to menstruation (the monthly bleeding cycle in women).
