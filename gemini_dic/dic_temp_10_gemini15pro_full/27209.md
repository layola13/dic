callousness:/ˈkæləsnəs/| n.|冷酷无情；麻木不仁；无情；硬结|Emotional callousness can make it difficult to form meaningful connections with others. 情感上的麻木不仁会让人难以与他人建立有意义的联系。|例句：The callousness of the dictator shocked the world.  独裁者的冷酷无情震惊了世界。

例句：He showed a shocking callousness towards the suffering of others. 他对别人的痛苦表现出令人震惊的麻木不仁。

近义词：insensitivity, indifference, apathy, heartlessness|反义词: compassion, empathy, sensitivity, kindness|词性:noun
