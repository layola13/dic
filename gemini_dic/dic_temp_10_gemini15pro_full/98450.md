inspirationally:/ˌɪnspəˈreɪʃənəli/
adverb
鼓舞人心地，启发地
in a way that inspires or provides inspiration.
Her speech was inspirationally delivered, motivating the entire audience. 她的演讲非常鼓舞人心，激励了所有的听众。
The book is inspirationally written and offers practical advice. 这本书写得很有启发性，并提供了实用的建议。
近义词: inspiringly, encouragingly, upliftingly
反义词: discouragingly, depressingly
词性: adverb

词源:
From inspiration + -al + -ly.  "Inspiration" comes from Late Latin inspirationem (nominative inspiratio) "a breathing in, act of inspiring," from past-participle stem of inspirare "breathe or blow into," figuratively "inspire, inflame," from in- "in" + spirare "to breathe."  "-al" forms adjectives from nouns. "-ly" forms adverbs from adjectives.
