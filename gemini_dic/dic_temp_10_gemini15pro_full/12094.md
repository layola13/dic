artist:/ˈɑːrtɪst/ (US) /ˈɑːtɪst/ (UK)| n.|艺术家；美术家；(尤指在艺术方面)有才能的人|A person who creates paintings, sculptures, music, or other works of art; a skilled performer, especially a musician or actor.|例句：The artist painted a beautiful landscape.  这位艺术家画了一幅美丽的风景画。

例句：She is a talented artist. 她是一位才华横溢的艺术家。|近义词：painter, sculptor, musician, performer, creator|反义词:amateur, beginner|词性:noun
