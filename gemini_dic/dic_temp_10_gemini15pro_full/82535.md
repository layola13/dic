guadalupe:/ˌɡwɑːdəˈluːpeɪ/ | n. | 瓜达卢佩 | A common Spanish given name, also the name of various places, including a river in Texas and a town in Spain, as well as the name of Our Lady of Guadalupe, a Catholic title for the Virgin Mary. |  Juan Diego claimed to have seen Our Lady of Guadalupe on Tepeyac Hill. 胡安·迭戈声称在特佩亚克山看到了瓜达卢佩圣母。| synonyms: (for the person) none specific; (for the place) town, city, river; (for the religious figure) Virgin Mary, Our Lady | antonyms: none | noun

guadaloop:/ˌɡwɑːdəˈluːp/ | n. | 瓜达卢普岛 | A butterfly-shaped island in the Caribbean, part of the French overseas department of Guadeloupe. | We spent our honeymoon in Guadaloop. 我们在瓜达卢普岛度蜜月。 | synonyms: island, isle | antonyms: mainland, continent | noun
