cutup:/ˈkʌtʌp/| n. |捣蛋鬼，爱恶作剧的人；剪贴画|A person who behaves in a mischievous or boisterous way, often playing pranks; a picture or design created by cutting and pasting pieces of paper or other materials.|The class cutup was always getting into trouble. 班上的捣蛋鬼总是惹麻烦。|近义词：prankster, mischief-maker, rogue|反义词:goody-two-shoes|词性:noun

cut up:/kʌt ʌp/| v. |切碎；使…伤心；乱砍；（车辆）超车抢道|To cut into small pieces; to cause (someone) emotional pain; to behave in a wild or boisterous way; to overtake and cut in sharply in front of another vehicle.|He cut up the vegetables for the stew. 他把蔬菜切碎准备炖菜。
She was really cut up about losing her job. 她丢了工作非常伤心。
The teenagers were cutting up in the park. 青少年们在公园里胡闹。
