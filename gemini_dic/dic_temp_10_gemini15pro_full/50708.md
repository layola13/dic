dermoptera:/dɜːrˈmɒptərə/
noun
皮翼目动物
An order of mammals comprising the colugos or "flying lemurs," characterized by a patagium (membrane of skin) extending between the limbs and body, enabling gliding flight.
The Philippine flying lemur (Cynocephalus volans) is a dermoptera.  菲律宾鼯猴（斑鼯猴）是一种皮翼目动物。
近义词:colugos
反义词:None
词性:n.


Etymology:
From Ancient Greek δέρμα (dérma, “skin”) + πτερόν (pterón, “wing”).
