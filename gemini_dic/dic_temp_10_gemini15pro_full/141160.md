epiphyte:/ˈɛpɪˌfaɪt/ | n. | 附生植物 | A plant that grows on another plant but is not parasitic, such as a fern or orchid. It derives its moisture and nutrients from the air, rain, and debris around it. | Example 1: Orchids are a common type of epiphyte, often found growing on trees in tropical rainforests.  兰花是一种常见的附生植物，经常生长在热带雨林的树木上。

Example 2: The bromeliad is another example of an epiphyte, its leaves forming a cup that collects water and nutrients.  凤梨是附生植物的另一个例子，它的叶子形成一个收集水和养分的杯状结构。 | Synonyms: air plant, aerophyte | Antonyms: terrestrial plant | Noun
