burrhel:/ˈbʌrəl/ | n. |岩羊|A wild sheep (Pseudois nayaur) of high altitudes in the Himalayas and central Asia, with large, curving horns.|  Burrhel are well adapted to the harsh conditions of the Himalayas. 岩羊非常适应喜马拉雅山的恶劣环境。|  A herd of burrhel grazed precariously on the steep slope. 一群岩羊在陡峭的山坡上危险地吃草。 | 近义词: blue sheep, Himalayan blue sheep | 反义词: N/A | noun
