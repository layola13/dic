dormitory:/ˈdɔːrmɪtɔːri/ (US) /ˈdɔːmɪtri/ (UK)| n. |宿舍；寝室|A large room containing many beds, especially in a boarding school or university, or a building containing such rooms.|例句：The university provides dormitory housing for first-year students. 大学为一年级学生提供宿舍住宿。

例句：He lived in a dormitory during his first year of college. 他大学一年级时住在宿舍里。

近义词：dorm, residence hall, hostel|反义词:apartment, house|词性:noun
