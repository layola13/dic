misorienting:/ˌmɪsɔːˈriːəntɪŋ/
adjective
使迷失方向的，使失去方向感的
Causing someone to lose their sense of direction or purpose.
The dense fog was misorienting, making it difficult to find the trail. 浓雾使人迷失方向，很难找到小路。
The conflicting information was misorienting and made it hard to make a decision.  相互矛盾的信息令人迷惑，难以做出决定。
synonyms: confusing, bewildering, disorienting, perplexing
antonyms: orienting, guiding, directing
词性: adjective
