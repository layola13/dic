ake:/eɪk/| n.|痛，疼痛；酸痛|A dull persistent pain.|例句：He complained of aches and pains. 他抱怨全身疼痛。

例句：She had a dull ache in her lower back. 她腰部隐隐作痛。|近义词：pain, soreness, throb|反义词：comfort, relief|词性:名词
