outbawls:/ˌaʊtˈbɔːlz/
verb
大声哭喊; 哭得比...更厉害
to cry louder than someone else.
She outbawled her brother when they both wanted the same toy. 她和她弟弟都想要同一个玩具时，她哭得比他更大声。
outcry, outshout, wail
whisper, murmur
v.
