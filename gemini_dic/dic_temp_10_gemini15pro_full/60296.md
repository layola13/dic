eggnog:/ˈɛɡˌnɒɡ/ (US) /ˈɛɡˌnɒɡ/ (UK)| n.|蛋奶酒|A rich, sweet, creamy alcoholic beverage traditionally made with milk, cream, sugar, whipped eggs, and spices, often with rum, brandy, or whiskey added.|We always have eggnog at Christmas. 我们圣诞节总是喝蛋奶酒。|Synonym:nog|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps a non-alcoholic beverage like hot chocolate)|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From egg + nog (a strong ale).  The "nog" part likely comes from the word "noggin," meaning a small mug or cup.
