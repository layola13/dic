galactic:/ɡəˈlæktɪk/| adj.|银河的，星系的|Relating to a galaxy or galaxies; incredibly vast.|例句：The galactic empire spanned thousands of star systems. 银河帝国跨越了数千个恒星系。|例句：The astronomer studied galactic formation. 天文学家研究星系的形成。|近义词：cosmic, interstellar, astronomical|反义词：planetary, terrestrial|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From Greek *galaktikos* "milky," from *gala*, *galaktos* "milk."  Refers to the Milky Way galaxy, which appears as a milky band across the night sky.
