mapped:/mæpt/ | adj. | 绘制地图的；映射的；计划好的 |  represented on a map; having a plan or strategy established | The expedition mapped the uncharted territory. 探险队绘制了这片未知的领土的地图。 | charted, planned, delineated | unmapped, unplanned | adjective

mapped:/mæpt/ | v. | 绘制…的地图；映射；计划；(基因)定位 | past tense and past participle of "map," meaning to represent on a map; to establish a correspondence between two sets of things; to plan something in detail; (genetics) to determine the relative positions of genes on a chromosome |  Scientists have mapped the human genome. 科学家已经绘制了人类基因组图谱。 | charted, planned, delineated | uncharted, unplanned | verb
