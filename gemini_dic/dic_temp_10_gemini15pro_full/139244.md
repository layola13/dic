obligational:/ˌɒblɪˈɡeɪʃənəl/
adjective
义务的；强制性的；债务的
Relating to or arising from an obligation; binding.
例句：He felt an obligational duty to help his family.  他感到有义务帮助他的家人。

例句：The company's obligational debts were substantial.  这家公司的债务很庞大。
synonym: mandatory, compulsory, binding
antonym: optional, voluntary, discretionary
词性: adjective
