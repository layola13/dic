oversalted:/ˌoʊvərˈsɔːltɪd/
adjective
过咸的
Containing too much salt.
The soup was oversalted and I couldn't finish it. 这汤太咸了，我喝不完。
The steak was slightly oversalted, but otherwise cooked perfectly. 牛排有点咸，但除此之外做得非常完美。
synonyms: salty, too salty
antonyms: undersalted, bland
词性: adjective
