aerial:/ˈɛəriəl/ (US) /ˈeəriəl/ (UK)| adj. n.|空中的，航空的；天线|Existing, happening, or operating in the air.|The aerial view of the city was breathtaking. 城市的鸟瞰图令人叹为观止。|Synonyms: airborne, aloft, overhead|Antonyms: ground, terrestrial|adjective

aerial:/ˈɛəriəl/ (US) /ˈeəriəl/ (UK)| n.|天线|An antenna for radio or television.|We need a new aerial to improve our TV reception. 我们需要一个新的天线来改善电视接收效果。|Synonyms: antenna, receiver|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *aereālis*, from Latin āēr (“air”) (from Ancient Greek ἀήρ (aḗr)), + -ālis (suffix forming adjectives from nouns).
