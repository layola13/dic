collector:/kəˈlɛktər/ | n. | 收藏家；收集者；征收员 | A person who collects something, such as stamps, coins, or art.  Someone who collects payments, taxes, or debts. | He's a keen stamp collector. 他是一位热衷的集邮家。 The tax collector came to the door.  税务员上门了。 |  enthusiast, hobbyist, accumulator, gatherer |  disperser, spender, distributor | noun
