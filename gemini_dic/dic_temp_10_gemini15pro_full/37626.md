communistically:/ˌkɒmjʊˈnɪstɪkli/ /ˌkɑːmjʊˈnɪstɪkli/
adverb|共产主义地，以共产主义方式|In a manner characteristic of or consistent with communism. |The country was run communistically, with all resources controlled by the state.  这个国家以共产主义方式运行，所有资源都由国家控制。|Synonym: collectively, marxistly |Antonym: capitalistically, individualistically
