onep: /wʌnˈpiːs/
noun
一件式服装; 连体衣;  (尤指女式)连衣裙
a single garment combining a top and pants/trousers or skirt
She looked stunning in a red onepiece. 她穿着一件红色的连体衣，看起来很惊艳。
synonyms: jumpsuit, playsuit, swimsuit, all-in-one
antonyms: two-piece, separates
Etymology:  Compound of "one" and "piece"


/wʌnˈpiːs/
adjective
一件式的; 连体的
made in one piece, not separate parts
a onepiece bathing suit 连体泳衣
synonyms: all-in-one, single-piece
antonyms: two-piece, multi-piece
Etymology:  Compound of "one" and "piece"
