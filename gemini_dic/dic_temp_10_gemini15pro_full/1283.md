account-executives: /əˈkaʊnt ɪɡˈzɛkjətɪvz/ | n. |客户主管，客户经理|A person who manages the sales and relationships with particular customers, especially in advertising or financial services. |  The account executives work hard to maintain good relationships with their clients. 客户主管努力维护与客户的良好关系。

The company's account executives are responsible for generating new business. 该公司的客户经理负责开拓新业务。

近义词: account manager, client manager, sales representative, sales executive | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "client") | 词性: noun (plural)

ETYMOLOGY->
"account" (from Old French "acont" meaning "a reckoning") + "executive" (from Latin "exsequi" meaning "to follow out, carry out")
