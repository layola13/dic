crysalis:/ˈkrɪsəlɪs/
noun
蛹；茧；保护层
A hard case that protects an insect larva while it changes into an adult.  The pupa of a butterfly is enclosed in a chrysalis.
The butterfly emerged from its chrysalis. 蝴蝶从它的蛹中破茧而出。
Synonyms: cocoon, pupa
Antonyms: adult, imago
