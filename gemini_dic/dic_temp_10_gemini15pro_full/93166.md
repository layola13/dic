ignitibility:/ɪɡˌnaɪtəˈbɪləti/
n.
可燃性, 易燃性
The quality of being ignitable; readiness to catch fire.
The ignitability of dry leaves is high, especially in autumn. 干燥树叶的可燃性很高，尤其是在秋天。
Forest fires are a serious concern due to the ignitability of dry underbrush.  由于干燥的灌木丛易燃，森林火灾是一个严重的问题。
近义词:flammability, combustibility
反义词:non-flammability, fire resistance
词性:noun

ignite:/ɪɡˈnaɪt/
v.
点燃，燃烧
To set on fire; to cause to burn.
He ignited the campfire with a single match. 他用一根火柴点燃了篝火。
The fuel spontaneously ignited. 燃料自燃了。
近义词:kindle, inflame, light
反义词:extinguish, quench
词性:verb

ignition:/ɪɡˈnɪʃən/
n.
点火，点燃
The act or process of igniting; the state of being ignited.
He turned the key in the ignition to start the car. 他转动钥匙点火启动汽车。
The faulty wiring caused the ignition of the house.  错误的线路导致房屋起火。
近义词: kindling, firing
反义词:extinguishing
词性:noun
