modernizable:/ˈmɒdənaɪzəbl/ (US) /ˈmɑːdərnaɪzəbl/| adj.|可现代化的|Capable of being modernized; able to be adapted to modern needs or standards.|The factory's machinery is old but modernizable.  这家工厂的机器设备很旧，但是可以现代化。|The company is exploring modernizable options for its IT infrastructure.  该公司正在探索其IT基础设施的可现代化选项。|近义词：upgradable, adaptable, improvable|反义词：obsolete, outdated|词性: adjective

Etymology:  From modern + -ize + -able.  "Modern" ultimately derives from Latin "modernus," meaning "of the present mode or fashion."  "-ize" is a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become (something specified)."  "-able" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of, fit for, tending to, given to."
