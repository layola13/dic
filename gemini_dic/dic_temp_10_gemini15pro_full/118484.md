mesmerical:/mɛzˈmɛrɪk(ə)l/
adjective
迷人的，催眠的
Having a mesmerizing or hypnotic effect; captivating; fascinating.
Her mesmerizing performance captivated the audience. 她迷人的表演吸引了全场观众。
近义词:hypnotic, captivating, fascinating, enchanting, spellbinding
反义词:unimpressive, boring, tedious, mundane, repellent
词性:adjective

词源: From "mesmerize" + "-ical." "Mesmerize" is derived from Franz Anton Mesmer, an 18th-century Austrian physician who developed a theory of animal magnetism, which he used to treat various ailments.  His methods involved inducing trance-like states in his patients, hence the association with hypnosis and fascination.
