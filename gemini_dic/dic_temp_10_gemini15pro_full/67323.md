extemporiness:/ɪkˌstɛmpəˈrɛːrɪnɪs/
n.
即兴；即席；无准备
the quality of being extemporaneous; impromptu or improvised character.
His extemporiness in the debate impressed the judges.  他在辩论中的即兴发挥给评委们留下了深刻的印象。

近义词:impromptu, improvisation, off-the-cuff, ad-lib, spontaneousness, unpreparedness
反义词:preparedness, premeditation, deliberation
