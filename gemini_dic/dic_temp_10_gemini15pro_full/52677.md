diglottism:/daɪˈɡlɒtɪzəm/ (US) /ˈdaɪɡlɑːtɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |双语制，双语现象|The state of being bilingual in a community or society, where two languages are used regularly.  The coexistence of two languages within a community or society.|例句：Diglottism is common in many parts of the world. 世界上很多地方都存在双语现象。

例句：The country's diglottism has enriched its culture.  这个国家的双语制丰富了它的文化。|近义词：bilingualism, multilingualism|反义词:monolingualism|词性:noun
