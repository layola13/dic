brachiosaurus:/ˌbrækiəˈsɔːrəs/| n. |腕龙|A large herbivorous dinosaur of the Jurassic period, with very long forelegs and a long neck.|例句：The brachiosaurus was one of the largest dinosaurs ever to walk the earth. 腕龙是曾经在地球上行走过的最大的恐龙之一。|例句：Scientists believe the brachiosaurus could reach the tops of tall trees to feed. 科学家认为腕龙可以够到高树的顶部进食。|近义词：sauropod|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


brachiosauruses:/ˌbrækiəˈsɔːrəsiːz/| n. |腕龙（复数）|Plural form of brachiosaurus, referring to more than one of these dinosaurs.|例句：Several brachiosauruses roamed the prehistoric plains. 几只腕龙在史前平原上漫游。|例句：Museum exhibits often feature skeletons of brachiosauruses. 博物馆展览经常展出腕龙的骨骼。|近义词：sauropods|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


Etymology:  From Greek *brakhíōn* (arm) + *saûros* (lizard).
