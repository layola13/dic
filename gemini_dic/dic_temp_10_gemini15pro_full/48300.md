decreet:/dɪˈkriːt/
n.
法令，判决
A formal and authoritative order, especially one having the force of law.
The king issued a decreet banning all public gatherings. 国王颁布法令禁止所有公众集会。
edict, order, decree, mandate
ruling, judgment, decision
n.
