词源:  "Gobsmacking" originated in British slang. "Gob" refers to the mouth, and "smack" means to hit.  So, something so surprising it makes you want to smack your mouth in astonishment. "Gobsmacked" (the past participle used as an adjective) emerged first, and "gobsmacking" followed.
