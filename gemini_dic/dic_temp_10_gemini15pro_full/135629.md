nonpracticalnesses:/ˌnɒnˈpræktɪkəlnəsɪz/
n.
不实用性；不切实际之处
lack of practicality; the quality of not being suitable or appropriate for use or application.
His nonpracticalnesses made him a poor choice for the project manager role. 他的不切实际让他成为项目经理的不二人选。
The nonpracticalnesses of her design were immediately apparent. 她的设计的不实用性显而易见。
impracticality, unsuitability, inappropriateness
practicality, suitability, appropriateness
noun
