indisputable:/ˌɪndɪˈspjuːtəbl/ /ˌɪndɪˈspjʊtəbl/| adj.|无可争辩的，不容置疑的|Not able to be challenged or denied.|Her talent is indisputable. 她的才华无可争辩。|The evidence against him is indisputable.  针对他的证据确凿无疑。|近义词：undeniable, irrefutable, incontestable, unquestionable|反义词：disputable, debatable, questionable|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *indisputabilis, from in- "not" + disputabilis "debatable," from disputare "to discuss, debate."
