crustaceologist:/krʌˈsteɪʃəˈlɑːdʒɪst/
n.
甲壳动物学家
A scientist who studies crustaceans.
例句：The crustaceologist spent years studying the migration patterns of crabs.  这位甲壳动物学家花了数年时间研究螃蟹的迁徙模式。

例句：My uncle is a crustaceologist specializing in freshwater shrimp.  我的叔叔是一位专门研究淡水虾的甲壳动物学家。

近义词：marine biologist, zoologist
反义词：(None -  No direct opposite for a profession)
词性:noun
