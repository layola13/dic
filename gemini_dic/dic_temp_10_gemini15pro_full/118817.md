metamorphic:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfɪk/| adj.|变质的，变形的|Relating to or characterized by metamorphosis; changed in form or nature.|例句：The metamorphic rocks were formed under intense heat and pressure. 这些变质岩是在高温高压下形成的。|例句：The caterpillar undergoes a metamorphic process to become a butterfly. 毛毛虫经历一个变质过程变成蝴蝶。|近义词：transformed, altered, changed, transmuted|反义词：unchanged, unaltered, static|词性:adjective
词源: From Greek *metamorphōsis*  "transformation," from *meta* "change" + *morphē* "form."
