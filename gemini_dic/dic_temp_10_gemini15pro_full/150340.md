panickiest:/ˈpænɪkiɪst/| adj.|最恐慌的|Superlative form of "panicky," meaning exhibiting the most extreme fear or anxiety.|例句：She was the panickiest person in the group during the earthquake. 地震发生时，她是小组里最恐慌的人。

例句：He's always the panickiest flyer, even on short flights. 即使是短途飞行，他也总是最恐慌的乘客。|近义词：most alarmed, most frightened, most terrified|反义词：calmest, most composed, most serene|词性:形容词

词源: From "panic" + "-y" (forming adjectives) + "-est" (superlative suffix).  "Panic" ultimately derives from the Greek god Pan, whose sudden appearances were said to cause irrational fear.
