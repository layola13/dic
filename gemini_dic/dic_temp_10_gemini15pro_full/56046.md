dobs:/dɒbz/ | n. | 谎言，虚假陈述 |  Plural of dob, meaning a small amount of something, particularly something soft or sticky, or an act of informing on someone (chiefly British). While "dobs" referring to lies isn't standard English, it might be used informally or dialectically as a slang term.  | He told a pack of dobs about where he'd been. (Informal/dialectal usage indicating lies) | 近义词：lies, falsehoods, fibs | 反义词：truths | 词性: 名词
