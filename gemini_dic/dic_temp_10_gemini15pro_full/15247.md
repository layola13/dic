backgammon:/ˈbækɡæmən/ | n. |西洋双陆棋 |A board game for two players, each with 15 pieces, which are moved according to the throw of dice. The object is to move all one's pieces off the board before one's opponent. |例句：He beat me at backgammon. 他在西洋双陆棋比赛中赢了我。|近义词：tables (chiefly Britain) |反义词: None |词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English backgammon, possibly from bac gamen ("back game"), from bac ("back") + gamen ("game").  The "back" likely refers to the fact that pieces are sometimes forced to move backward.
