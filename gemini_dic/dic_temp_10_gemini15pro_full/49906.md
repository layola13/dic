demystifier:/ˌdiːˈmɪstɪfaɪər/
|n.|揭秘者，除谜者|A person or thing that makes something easier to understand, especially something complicated or mysterious.|She acted as a demystifier, explaining the complex legal jargon in simple terms. 她充当了揭秘者的角色，用简单的术语解释了复杂的法律术语。|近义词：explainer, clarifier, interpreter|反义词：mystifier, obfuscator|词性: 名词

词源:
de- (prefix): meaning "remove", "reverse", or "reduce"
mystify (verb): from the French "mystifier" and ultimately from the Latin "mysticus" meaning "secret, mystic"
-er (suffix): used to form nouns denoting a person or thing that performs a particular action.
