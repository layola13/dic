brimstony:/ˈbrɪmstəʊni/ (UK), /ˈbrɪmˌstoʊni/ (US)| adj.|硫磺味的；地狱般的；充满仇恨的|Smelling of or suggestive of sulfur; hellish; filled with hatred or fury.|例句：The air was thick and brimstony. 空气又浓又充满硫磺味。

例句：His brimstony glare made her shrink back. 他充满仇恨的目光让她退缩了。|近义词：sulfurous, hellish, fiery, sulfuric|反义词：heavenly, pleasant, sweet|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From brimstone + -y.  Brimstone itself comes from Old English *brynstān, from *byrne "burning" + stān "stone."
