noncongenital:/ˌnɒn.kənˈdʒen.ɪ.təl/ | adj. | 非先天性的；后天性的 | Not present at birth; acquired after birth. | Example 1: His blindness is noncongenital, resulting from an accident. 他的失明不是先天的，是由一次事故造成的。

Example 2: The doctor determined that the heart condition was noncongenital and likely caused by an infection. 医生确定这种心脏病不是先天的，可能是由感染引起的。 | Synonym: acquired, postnatal | Antonym: congenital, inborn | Word Type: Adjective
