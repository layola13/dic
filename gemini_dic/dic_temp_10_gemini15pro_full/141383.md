operculiferous:/ˌɒpəˈkjuːlɪfərəs/
adjective
有鳃盖的；有盖的
Bearing or provided with an operculum (a lid or covering, especially protecting a part of the body, such as the gill cover of a fish or the covering of a moss capsule).
Operculiferous snails are common in freshwater habitats. 有鳃盖的蜗牛在淡水栖息地很常见。
Synonyms: operculate
Antonyms: inoperculate
词性: 形容词
