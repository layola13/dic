marker:/ˈmɑːrkər/ (US) /ˈmɑːkə(r)/ (UK)| n. |标记，记号笔，标志物|A marker is an object, substance, etc. used for marking something.|例句：Please use a marker to highlight the important sentences. 请用记号笔标出重要的句子。

例句：The monument serves as a marker for the historical event. 这座纪念碑是这一历史事件的标志。|近义词：sign, indicator, symbol, pen|反义词: eraser (for marker pen)|词性:noun
