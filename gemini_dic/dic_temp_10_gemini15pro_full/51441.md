dethrone:/diˈθroʊn/| vt.| 废黜；罢免；取代|To remove a king or queen from power; to remove someone from a position of authority or dominance.|The rebels dethroned the king and established a republic.  叛军废黜了国王，建立了共和国。

The new smartphone app has dethroned the previous market leader.  这款新的智能手机应用取代了之前的市场领导者。|Synonyms:  depose, oust, unseat, displace, supersede|Antonyms:  enthrone, crown, install, appoint|verb
