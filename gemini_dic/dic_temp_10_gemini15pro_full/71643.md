flatten:/ˈflætən/ (US), /ˈflætn/ (UK)| v. |使变平; 弄平;  压平; 击败; 彻底打败;  (尤指生意不好) 变萧条;| To make something flat or flatter. To defeat someone completely. To become less successful or less active. |The workers flattened the ground with a roller. 工人们用滚筒把地面压平了。|synonyms: level, smooth, crush, conquer |antonyms: raise, elevate, build up|verb
