episcopalians:/ɪˌpɪskəˈpeɪliənz/
n.
圣公会教徒
Members of the Episcopal Church, a denomination of Christianity.
Many Episcopalians attend church regularly. 许多圣公会教徒定期去教堂。
The Episcopalians held a fundraising event for the new church building. 圣公会教徒为新教堂的建设举办了一场募款活动。
近义词:Anglicans
反义词:(No direct opposite, could list members of other denominations, e.g., Catholics, Baptists)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Episcopal + -ian. Episcopal comes from Late Latin episcopālis, from Latin episcopus "bishop," from Greek episkopos "overseer," from epi- "over" + skopos "watcher."
