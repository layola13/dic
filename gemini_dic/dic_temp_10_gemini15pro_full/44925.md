cryptocurrency:/ˈkrɪptoʊˌkɜːrənsi/| n. |加密货币|A digital or virtual currency that uses cryptography for security. It is decentralized, meaning it is not controlled by any government or financial institution.|Bitcoin is a popular cryptocurrency. 比特币是一种流行的加密货币。|Synonyms: digital currency, virtual currency, cybercurrency|Antonyms: fiat currency, physical currency|noun
