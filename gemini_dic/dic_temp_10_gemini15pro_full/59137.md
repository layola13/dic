dynamiting: /ˈdaɪnəˌmaɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 爆破；炸毁 | The dynamiting of the old building took place last week.  |  The act of using dynamite to blow something up. |  例句：The dynamiting of the bridge cut off the enemy's supply line. 炸毁桥梁切断了敌人的补给线。

例句：The dynamiting of the rock face was necessary to build the new road. 爆破岩壁对于修建新路是必要的。


近义词：blasting, exploding, demolition | 反义词: construction, building | 词性: 名词 (gerund)
