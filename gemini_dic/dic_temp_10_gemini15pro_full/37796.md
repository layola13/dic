compelled:/kəmˈpɛld/| v. |被迫的，不得不的|forced to do something; obliged to do something because it is the right thing to do| I felt compelled to help her. 我觉得我不得不帮助她。| synonyms: forced, obliged, obligated, required, driven, pressured | antonyms: unforced, voluntary, optional | verb

compelled:/kəmˈpɛld/| adj. |（少见）强迫的|Done or produced by compulsion.|His smile was compelled. 他的笑容是强迫的。 (This usage is less common)| synonyms: forced, strained, unnatural | antonyms: natural, genuine, unforced | adjective
