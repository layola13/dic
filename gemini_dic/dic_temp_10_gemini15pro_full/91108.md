hyalescence:/ˌhaɪəˈlɛsəns/ |n.|透明；半透明；玻璃状|The hyalescence of the jellyfish made it almost invisible in the water. 水母的透明性使它在水中几乎看不见。|例句：The mineral exhibited a slight hyalescence. 这块矿物呈现出轻微的透明状。

The hyalescence of the deep ocean creatures helps them camouflage. 深海生物的透明有助于它们伪装。|近义词：transparency, translucence, pellucidity|反义词：opacity|词性：名词

Etymology: From Greek *ὕαλος (hýalos, “glass”) +‎ *-escence
