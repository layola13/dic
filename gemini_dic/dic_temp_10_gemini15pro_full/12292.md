ascidium:/əˈsɪdiəm/ | n. | 海鞘；【植】囊状体；|A saclike structure, such as the pitcher of a pitcher plant or the spore case of certain fungi.  In zoology, it refers to a sessile tunicate (sea squirt). |The ascidium of the pitcher plant contains digestive enzymes.  这种猪笼草的囊状体含有消化酶。| The ascidium of this sea squirt is quite large.  这只海鞘的海鞘体相当大。| synonyms: pitcher, utricle,  zooid (in zoology)| antonyms: N/A | noun

ascidia:/əˈsɪdiə/ (plural of ascidium) | n. | 海鞘（复数）；囊状体（复数）| The plural form of ascidium, referring to multiple saclike structures. | Many ascidians were attached to the rocks. 许多海鞘附着在岩石上。 | The ascidians of these plants vary in size and shape. 这些植物的囊状体大小和形状各不相同。 | synonyms: pitchers, utricles, zooids (in zoology) | antonyms: N/A | noun
