capit:/ˈkæpɪt/| n.| 头, 头状物, 章, 节 |A capitulum or rounded extremity of a part or organ, especially a bone.  The top part or beginning of something. A chapter or section of a written work. |The capit of the femur articulates with the hip bone.  The capit of the novel introduces the main characters. |近义词：head, top, chapter, section|反义词: base, bottom, foot|noun
