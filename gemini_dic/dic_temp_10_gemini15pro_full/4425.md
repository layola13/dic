alarming:/əˈlɑːrmɪŋ/
adjective
令人担忧的；引起恐慌的
Causing worry or fear; disturbing.
The alarming increase in crime rates has led to public outcry. 犯罪率的惊人增长引起了公众的强烈抗议。
The doctor discovered an alarming lump in her breast. 医生在她乳房里发现了一个令人担忧的肿块。
synonyms: worrying, disturbing, concerning, unsettling, frightening, terrifying, scary
antonyms: reassuring, comforting, calming, soothing
词性: 形容词
