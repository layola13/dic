collodion:/kəˈloʊdiən/
n.|火棉胶；胶棉|A syrupy solution of nitrocellulose in ether and alcohol, used as a coating or adhesive.|Collodion was once used as a surgical dressing. 火棉胶曾被用作外科敷料。|synonyms: nitrocellulose, pyroxylin|antonyms: None|noun

collodions:/kəˈloʊdiənz/
n.|火棉胶（复数）|Plural form of collodion.|The collodions were stored in airtight containers. 这些火棉胶存放在密封容器中。|synonyms: nitrocelluloses, pyroxylin solutions|antonyms: None|noun
