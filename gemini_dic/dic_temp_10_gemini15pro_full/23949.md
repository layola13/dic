breastfeeds: /ˈbrɛstˌfidz/ | v. | 喂母乳；哺乳 | Feeds a baby with milk from the breast. |  The mother breastfeeds her newborn every two hours. 这位母亲每两个小时给她的新生儿喂一次母乳。

The baby breastfeeds contentedly. 这个婴儿满足地吃着母乳。 | 近义词: nurses, suckles | 反义词: bottle-feeds | 词性: 动词
