nonfervently:/nɒnˈfɜːvəntli/ | adv. | 不热烈地，不热情地 |  Lacking fervor; without enthusiasm or zeal.  It describes an action or expression done without much passion or intensity. | He nonfervently agreed to attend the meeting. 他不热情地同意参加会议。| She nonfervently applauded the performance. 她不热情地为表演鼓掌。| 近义词: apathetically, indifferently, halfheartedly, listlessly | 反义词: fervently, enthusiastically, passionately, zealously | 词性: 副词

词源:

non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin *non.
fervent (adjective): From Latin ferventem (nominative fervens) "boiling, glowing, hot," figuratively "eager, vehement," present participle of fervere "to boil, glow."
-ly (suffix):  Forms adverbs from adjectives.
