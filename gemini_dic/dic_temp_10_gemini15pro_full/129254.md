nod-out:/nɒdˈaʊt/ | vi. | 打瞌睡；昏昏欲睡(尤指吸毒后) | To fall asleep or become semiconscious, typically from drug use. | He nodded out on the couch after taking too many painkillers. 他服用了太多止痛药后在沙发上睡着了。|  The heroin addict nodded out in the alleyway.  那个海洛因成瘾者在小巷里昏睡过去。 | 近义词：doze off, drift off, conk out | 反义词：wake up, perk up | 词性: 动词

nod-out:/nɒdˈaʊt/ | n. |  (吸毒后的)昏睡；瞌睡 | The state of being asleep or semiconscious, typically from drug use. | The paramedics found him in a nod-out.  医护人员发现他处于昏睡状态。 | His frequent nod-outs were a clear sign of his addiction. 他频繁的昏睡是他上瘾的明显迹象。 | 近义词：stupor, daze | 反义词：wakefulness, alertness | 词性: 名词
