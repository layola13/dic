formalises:/ˈfɔːməlaɪzɪz/
verb
正式化；使正式
Makes something official or establishes it according to rules or ceremonies.
The agreement was formalised with a signed contract. 该协议以签署合同的方式正式化。
The government is planning to formalise the new regulations next month. 政府计划下个月正式实施新规。
Synonyms: officialise, codify, legitimize, regularize
Antonyms: informalise
词性: 动词
