nonaxiomatically:/ˌnɒnˌæksiəˈmætɪkli/
adjective
非公理地
not based on or derived from axioms; not axiomatic.  Taking a practical or heuristic approach, rather than strictly following axioms or established principles.
His approach to the problem was nonaxiomatic, relying on intuition and observation rather than formal proofs.  他解决这个问题的方法是非公理的，依靠直觉和观察而不是形式证明。
The theory was developed nonaxiomatically, starting with empirical observations and gradually building up a framework.  该理论是非公理地发展起来的，从经验观察开始，逐步建立起一个框架。
近义词:heuristic, empirical, intuitive
反义词:axiomatic, deductive, formal
词性:adjective
词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "axiomatic" (from Greek "axioma" meaning "something worthy, self-evident principle") + "-ally" (adverbial suffix)
