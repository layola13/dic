grisliest:/ˈɡrɪzliɪst/| adj.|最可怕的，最恐怖的|Superlative adjective of grisly; most grisly.  Evoking horror; gruesome.|The detective had seen his share of crime scenes, but this one was by far the grisliest.|Synonyms: gruesome, horrific, macabre, ghastly, appalling|Antonyms: pleasant, agreeable, delightful|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *grisli, from Old English grislic (“terrible, dreadful, horrible, hideous”), from grīs (“terror, horror”) +‎ -lic (“-ly”).  The word's roots relate to feelings of dread and fear.
