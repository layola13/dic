non-revealing:/ˌnɑːn rɪˈviːlɪŋ/ | adj. | 不泄露的；不透露的；不明显的 | Not revealing private information or details; discreet. |  She wore a non-revealing dress to the business meeting. 她穿了一件不暴露的裙子去参加商务会议。|  He gave a non-revealing answer to the journalist's question. 他对记者的提问给出了一个不透露任何信息的答案。 | 近义词: discreet, concealing, unobtrusive, uninformative | 反义词: revealing, transparent, explicit, obvious | 词性: adjective

词源:
"non-" (prefix) from Latin "non" meaning "not"
"revealing" (present participle of reveal) from Old French "reveler" meaning "to unveil"
