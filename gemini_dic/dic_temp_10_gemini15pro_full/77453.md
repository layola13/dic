gawping:/ˈɡɔːpɪŋ/| v.| 瞠目结舌地注视，呆呆地看 | staring with your mouth open, especially at something surprising or shocking |  He stood there gawping at the wreckage. 他站在那里呆呆地看着残骸。| synonyms: staring, gaping, ogling | antonyms: glancing, ignoring | verb

gawping:/ˈɡɔːpɪŋ/| adj.| 令人瞠目结舌的 | causing or characterized by staring with the mouth open in surprise | The gawping crowds watched the acrobat's daring feat.  瞠目结舌的人群看着杂技演员大胆的表演。| synonyms: astonishing, amazing, surprising | antonyms: boring, mundane, uninteresting | adjective
