outvoices:/ˌaʊtˈvɔɪsɪz/
| v. | 压过…的声音；胜过；盖过 | to speak louder than someone else so that they cannot be heard; to defeat someone in a debate or argument | The protestors were outvoiced by the supporters of the government. 抗议者的声音被政府的支持者盖过了。| drown out, overpower, muffle | - | verb
outvoices:/ˌaʊtˈvɔɪsɪz/
| n. | (罕见) 压倒性的声音；盖过的声音 | The act of outvoicing; a sound that overpowers another. | The outvoices of the crowd made it impossible to hear the speaker.  人群的喧闹声使得根本听不见演讲者的声音。| din, clamor, uproar | silence, quiet | noun
