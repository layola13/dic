antimedieval:/ˌæntiˌmiːdiˈiːvəl/
adjective
反中世纪的，反对中世纪的
Opposed to or hostile to the Middle Ages or its values and institutions.
The professor's antimedieval stance led him to focus on Renaissance literature.  教授的反中世纪立场使他专注于文艺复兴文学。
The museum's collection clearly reflected an antimedieval bias.  博物馆的藏品清楚地反映了反中世纪的偏见。
近义词:modern, contemporary
反义词:medieval, medievalist
词性:adjective
