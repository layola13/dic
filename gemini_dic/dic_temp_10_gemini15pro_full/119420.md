microfracture:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌfræktʃər/| n.|微骨折|A surgical technique where tiny fractures are made in a joint surface to stimulate the growth of new cartilage.|The athlete underwent microfracture surgery to repair the damaged cartilage in his knee.  这位运动员接受了微骨折手术来修复膝盖受损的软骨。|Synonyms:  cartilage regeneration, chondroplasty|Antonyms:  fusion, arthrodesis|noun


microfracture:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌfræktʃər/| n.|微骨折|The creation of tiny fractures in a joint surface to stimulate cartilage growth.|Microfracture is often used to treat articular cartilage defects. 微骨折常用于治疗关节软骨缺损。|Synonyms:  subchondral drilling, chondral regeneration|Antonyms:  joint replacement|noun
