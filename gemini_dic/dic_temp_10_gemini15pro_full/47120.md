dauntless:/ˈdɔːntləs/| adj.|无畏的，勇敢的|Showing fearlessness and determination.|例句：The dauntless firefighter rushed into the burning building. 无畏的消防员冲进了燃烧的建筑物。|例句：With dauntless courage, she faced her fears. 她以无畏的勇气面对她的恐惧。|近义词：fearless, intrepid, valiant, courageous|反义词：fearful, timid, cowardly|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *dauntles*, from daunten + -less. Ultimately from Anglo-Norman *danter*, from Vulgar Latin *domitare* "to tame".
