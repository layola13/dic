feram:/ˈfɛrəm/ | n. | 野兽, 野生动物 (尤指野生的四足动物) |  A wild animal, especially a wild four-footed mammal.  From Latin *ferus* meaning "wild, untamed". Originally referred to wild beasts as opposed to domesticated animals. | The hunter tracked the feram through the dense forest. 猎人追踪着野兽穿过茂密的森林。 | synonyms: beast, creature, animal | antonyms: domesticated animal, pet | noun
