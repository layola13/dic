例句：The forefield of artificial intelligence is rapidly evolving. 人工智能领域正在快速发展。|近义词：foreground, forefront, vanguard|反义词:background, backdrop|词性:名词

From 'fore-' (before, in front of) + 'field' (an area of open land).
