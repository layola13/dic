excretories:/ɪkˈskriːtəriːz/
n.
排泄器官；排泄物
Organs or substances involved in excretion, the process of eliminating waste products from the body.
The kidneys are important excretories.  肾脏是重要的排泄器官。
The body eliminates metabolic waste through its excretories. 人体通过其排泄器官排出代谢废物。
Synonyms:  eliminatory organs,  waste disposal system
Antonyms:  absorptive organs
noun
