localizing:/ˈloʊkəˌlaɪzɪŋ/| v.|本地化，局部化|The company is localizing its products to appeal to different markets.  这家公司正在将其产品本地化，以吸引不同的市场。|例句：The pain is localizing in my lower back.  疼痛集中在我的下背部。|近义词：adapting, adjusting, translating, restricting, confining|反义词:globalizing, spreading, expanding|词性:动词(present participle)

Etymology: From the verb "localize," which comes from "local" + "-ize." "Local" ultimately derives from the Latin word "locus," meaning "place."
