equalization:/ˌiːkwəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 平衡，均等化 | The process of making things equal or uniform. |  The government is working on the equalization of educational opportunities. 政府正在努力使教育机会均等。| synonyms: levelling, balancing, adjustment, standardization | antonyms: imbalance, disparity, inequality | noun

equalize:/ˈiːkwəlaɪz/ | v. | 使相等，使平衡 | To make things equal or uniform. | They equalized the pressure in the two containers. 他们使两个容器内的压力相等。| synonyms: level, balance, adjust, standardize | antonyms: unbalance, differentiate, vary | verb

equalized:/ˈiːkwəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 平衡的，均等的 | Having been made equal or uniform. | The scores were equalized in the final minutes of the game. 比分在比赛的最后几分钟被扳平了。| synonyms: level, balanced, adjusted, standardized | antonyms: unbalanced, unequal, uneven | adjective
