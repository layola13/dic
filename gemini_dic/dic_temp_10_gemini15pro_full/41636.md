correction:/kəˈrɛkʃən/ | n. |改正，修正，纠正|The teacher marked the corrections on my essay. 老师在我的作文上标出了修改的地方。| Example: He made a correction to the report. 他对报告做了更正。|Synonyms: amendment, emendation, rectification, revision |Antonyms: mistake, error, blunder | noun

correction:/kəˈrɛkʃən/ | n. |惩罚，处罚 | The prison provides correction for criminals.  监狱为罪犯提供惩罚。| Example:  He was sent to a juvenile correction facility. 他被送往少年管教所。|Synonyms: punishment, discipline, chastisement |Antonyms: reward, praise | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: correccion, from Old French coreccion, from Latin correctio(n-), from corrigere ‘to correct’.
