crypto-graphically: /ˌkrɪptəˈɡræfɪkli/ | adv. | 以密码方式，用密码学方法 |  In a manner relating to or employing cryptography; securely encoded. | The data is cryptographically signed to ensure its authenticity. 数据以密码方式签名以确保其真实性。 | Synonyms: securely, encodedly | Antonyms: insecurely, openly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From "cryptographic" + "-ally."
"Cryptographic" comes from "cryptography," which derives from the Greek "kryptós" (hidden) + "graphein" (to write).
