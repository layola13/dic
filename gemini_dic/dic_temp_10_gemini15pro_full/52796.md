diligent:/ˈdɪlɪdʒənt/| adj.|勤奋的，勤勉的|Characterized by steady, earnest, and energetic effort in a pursuit or undertaking. Showing care and conscientiousness in one's work or duties.|She is a diligent student, always completing her assignments on time. 她是一位勤奋的学生，总是按时完成作业。

He is diligent in his work, never cutting corners. 他工作勤奋，从不偷工减料。

|近义词：hardworking, industrious, assiduous, conscientious|反义词：lazy, indolent, negligent, slothful|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *diligent*, borrowed from Old French *diligent*, from Latin *dīligēns*, present participle of *dīligō* (“to love, esteem, single out, select; value highly, prize; attend to diligently”), from *dis-* (“apart, asunder, in different directions”) + *legō* (“gather, collect, choose, select; read”).
