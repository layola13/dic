oversocially:/ˌoʊvərˈsoʊʃəli/
| adv. | 过度社交地 | Excessively or too frequently engaging in social interaction.  Characterized by a tendency to socialize more than is considered typical or healthy. | She was oversocially involved in every club and organization, leaving little time for herself. 她过度参与了每个俱乐部和组织，几乎没有时间留给自己。 |
|例句：He was known for being oversocially active, attending multiple events every night. 他以过度活跃的社交活动而闻名，每晚都参加多个活动。|
|近义词：excessively sociable, overly gregarious|
|反义词：antisocial, withdrawn, reclusive|
|词性:副词|
