overpowerfulnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊərfəlnəsɪz/
|n.|压倒性的优势；强大的力量（复数）|The sheer overpowerfulnesses of the enemy forces led to a swift defeat. 敌军绝对压倒性的优势导致了迅速的失败。|The combined overpowerfulnesses of their arguments and evidence convinced the jury.  他们论点和证据的压倒性优势说服了陪审团。|近义词：dominance, supremacy, superiority|反义词：weakness, inferiority, powerlessness|词性：名词

overpowerful:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊərfəl/
|adj.|过于强大的；压倒性的|The overpowerful engine made the car difficult to control. 过于强大的引擎使汽车难以控制。|The overpowerful smell of the perfume gave me a headache. 香水过于浓烈的气味让我头疼。|近义词：dominant, overwhelming, overpowering|反义词：weak, feeble, powerless|词性：形容词

overpower:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊər/
|v.|压倒；制服；使无法忍受|The police overpowered the suspect and arrested him. 警察制服了嫌疑犯并逮捕了他。|The heat overpowered me, and I fainted.  高温让我难以忍受，我晕倒了。|近义词：overwhelm, subdue, conquer|反义词：yield, surrender, succumb|词性：动词


词源:  "overpowerfulnesses" comes from "overpower" + "-ful" + "-ness" + "-es".  "Overpower" is derived from "over-" (meaning "excessive" or "too much") and "power". "-ful" turns the verb into an adjective, "-ness" turns the adjective into a noun, and "-es" marks the plural form.
