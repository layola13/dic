disparaging:/dɪˈspærɪdʒɪŋ/
adjective
贬低的，轻蔑的
Expressing the opinion that something is of little worth; derogatory.
例句：He made some disparaging remarks about her appearance.  他对她外貌做了一些贬低的评论。

例句：She gave a disparaging laugh. 她轻蔑地笑了笑。

近义词：derogatory, belittling, deprecating, slighting, denigrating, insulting, offensive
反义词：complimentary, flattering, praising, laudatory
词性: adjective
