overguilty:/ˈoʊvərˈɡɪlti/
adjective
过度内疚的，过分自责的
Experiencing or expressing excessive guilt; more guilty than is appropriate or warranted.
She felt overguilty about forgetting his birthday, even though he insisted it was fine. 她因为忘记了他的生日而感到过分内疚，即使他坚持说没关系。
I was overguilty for snapping at her, considering she was just trying to help.  考虑到她只是想帮忙，我为对她厉声斥责感到过分自责。
近义词: excessively guilty, too guilty, overly guilty
反义词: unrepentant, shameless, unapologetic
词性: adjective
