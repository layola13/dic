numerators:/ˈnjuːməˌreɪtərz/| n.| 分子|The top number in a fraction. It shows how many parts of the whole are being considered.|The numerator of the fraction 3/4 is 3.  分数 3/4 的分子是 3。|
|synonyms: top number|antonyms: denominator|noun|
