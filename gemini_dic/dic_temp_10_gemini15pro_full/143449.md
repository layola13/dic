outfought:/ˌaʊtˈfɔːt/
verb
战胜，击败
defeated in a fight or contest, especially by using superior skill or strength
The smaller army outfought their larger enemy.  较小的军队战胜了规模更大的敌人。
Synonyms: defeated, conquered, vanquished, overcame
Antonyms: lost to, succumbed to
词性:动词
