emendatory:/ɪˈmɛndətɔːri/ /əˈmɛndətɔːri/
adjective
修正的；订正的
Serving to emend; corrective.  Relating to or making emendations.
The emendatory process can take several months.  修正过程可能需要几个月。
The senator proposed an emendatory bill to address the loopholes in the existing law.  参议员提出了一项修正法案，以解决现有法律中的漏洞。
近义词: corrective, amending, rectifying, improving
反义词: worsening, damaging, corrupting
词性: adjective

emendation:/ˌiːmenˈdeɪʃn/ /ˌɛmenˈdeɪʃn/
noun
修改，修订；修正，校订
A correction or improvement made to a text. The process of making such corrections.
The editor made several emendations to the manuscript. 编辑对原稿做了几处修改。
The latest edition of the book includes numerous emendations.  这本书的最新版本包含了许多修订。
近义词: correction, amendment, revision, improvement
反义词: corruption, distortion
词性: noun
