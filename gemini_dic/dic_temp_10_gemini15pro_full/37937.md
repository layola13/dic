completing:/kəmˈpliːtɪŋ/ | v. | 完成，结束 | The process of finishing something that was started. |  I am completing my homework. 我正在完成我的家庭作业。 | finish, concluding, finalizing, achieving | abandoning, ceasing, halting | verb

completing:/kəmˈpliːtɪŋ/ | adj. | 补充的，使完整 | Serving to make something whole or complete.  | This information is completing the puzzle. 这条信息使谜题完整了。 | supplementary, complementary, finishing | lacking, incomplete, partial | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *completen*, from Old French *completer*, from Latin *complēre* (“to fill up, complete”), from *com-* (intensive prefix) + *plēre* (“to fill”).
