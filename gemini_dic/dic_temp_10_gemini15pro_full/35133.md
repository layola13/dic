cloudlifter:/ˈklaʊdˌlɪftər/| n. |云提升器，话筒放大器|A device used to boost the signal from microphones, especially low-output ribbon microphones, before it reaches a preamplifier. It provides a clean gain boost, minimizing noise and improving the overall signal quality.| Example: The Cloudlifter made a huge difference in the sound of my ribbon mic. 云提升器极大地改善了我铝带话筒的声音。|Example: Using a Cloudlifter allowed me to record quieter sources without introducing unwanted noise. 使用云提升器让我能够录制更安静的音源，而不会引入不必要的噪音。| 近义词: microphone preamp, signal booster | 反义词: attenuator, noise suppressor|词性:名词
