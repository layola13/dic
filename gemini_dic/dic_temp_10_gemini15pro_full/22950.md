bottle:/ˈbɒtl/ (British), /ˈbɑːtl/ (American)| n. vt.|瓶子；一瓶（的量）；装瓶|A container, typically made of glass or plastic, with a narrow neck, used for storing liquids.  To put a liquid into a bottle.|例句：The wine comes in a green bottle.  这种酒装在绿色的瓶子里。 He bottled the homebrew beer carefully. 他小心翼翼地将家酿啤酒装瓶。|近义词：container, flask, jar, jug|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 open container 开放容器)|名词, 动词
