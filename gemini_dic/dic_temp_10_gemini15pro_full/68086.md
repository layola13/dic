faded:/ˈfeɪdɪd/ /ˈfeɪdɪd/| adj.|褪色的，已褪色的; 衰弱的，虚弱的; 枯萎的，凋谢的|having lost freshness or intensity of color or appearance; weak or faint; withered|The photograph had faded over time. 这张照片随着时间的推移已经褪色了。|Synonyms: bleached, washed-out, pale, dull, weak, faint|Antonyms: bright, vibrant, strong, intense|word property: adjective

faded:/ˈfeɪdɪd/ /ˈfeɪdɪd/| v.|（使）褪色; （使）变暗淡; （使）衰弱，（使）逐渐消失|to lose freshness or intensity of color or appearance; to become less clear or distinct; to become weaker|The sun faded the curtains.  太阳晒得窗帘褪色了。|Synonyms: bleach, blanch, dull, dim, weaken, decline|Antonyms: brighten, intensify, strengthen, revive|word property: verb
