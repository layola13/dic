cloudier:/ˈklaʊdiər/ (US), /ˈklaʊdɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更阴的，云量更大的|Comparative form of cloudy: more covered with clouds.|The sky is cloudier today than yesterday. 今天的天空比昨天更阴沉。|The forecast predicted cloudier weather for the weekend. 天气预报预测周末天气会更阴。|近义词：darker, gloomier, grayer, overcast|反义词：brighter, clearer, sunnier|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *cloudi, from Old English *clūdiġ, from clūd +‎ -iġ. Cognate with Dutch kloetig (“sticky, clammy”), German kletig (“sticky, clammy”).  The comparative form "-er" is a Germanic suffix.
