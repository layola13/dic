outshouldered:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊldərd/
verb
超过，胜过，比…肩宽
surpassed, especially in competition or status, often by having broader shoulders
He easily outshouldered his opponents in the rugby scrum. 他在橄榄球并列争球中轻松超过了他的对手。
Synonyms: surpass, outdo, outstrip, outmaneuver, eclipse
Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
词性: 动词

outshouldered:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊldərd/
adjective
肩部比…宽的
having broader shoulders than someone else
He was a tall, outshouldered man. 他是一个高个子，肩膀宽阔的男人。
Synonyms: broad-shouldered
Antonyms: narrow-shouldered
词性: 形容词
