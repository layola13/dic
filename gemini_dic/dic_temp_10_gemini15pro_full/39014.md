confiscator:/ˈkɒnfɪskeɪtər/ (US) /ˈkɑːnfɪskeɪtər/| n. |没收者，充公者|A person who confiscates property.|The government acted as confiscator, seizing the rebel's assets. 政府充当了没收者的角色，扣押了叛军的资产。|Synonyms: seizer, appropriator, expropriator|Antonyms: restorer, returner|Noun


confiscate:/ˈkɒnfɪskeɪt/ (US) /ˈkɑːnfɪskeɪt/| v. |没收，充公|To seize (something) by authority; to take (something) away from someone as a penalty.|The teacher confiscated the student's phone. 老师没收了学生的手机。|Synonyms: seize, impound, appropriate|Antonyms: return, restore|Verb
