abodement:/əˈboʊdmənt/ |n.|预兆，预感|An abodement is a feeling that something, usually something unpleasant, is going to happen. |There was an abodement of disaster in the air. 空气中弥漫着灾难的预兆。|The sudden darkness felt like an abodement of the coming storm. 突如其来的黑暗感觉像是暴风雨来临的预兆。|近义词：foreboding, presentiment, portent|反义词：assurance, certainty|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *abodement, from aboden + -ment, from Old French aboder to wait for, abide. Ultimately from Latin *abaudare.
