offhand:/ˌɔːfˈhænd/ /ˌɑːfˈhænd/| adj. adv.|随便的，即兴的；无准备地，立刻|Done without preparation or forethought; extempore.|例句：I can't quote the figures offhand. 我无法立刻引用那些数字。|近义词：extempore, impromptu, ad-lib|反义词:premeditated, prepared|adjective

offhand:/ˌɔːfˈhænd/ /ˌɑːfˈhænd/| adv.|随便地，即兴地|Without preparation or forethought.|例句：He answered offhand. 他随口回答。|近义词：extemporaneously, impromptu|反义词:deliberately, preparedly|adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the phrase "off hand," referring to having something readily available, as if held in one's hand.  Over time, it evolved to mean doing something without preparation, since having something "off hand" implies not needing to look it up or consult resources.
