pantomimicry:/ˌpæntəˈmɪmɪkri/ | n. |  哑剧表演；默剧；模仿 | The act of expressing or conveying something through gestures and body movements without speech, like in a pantomime. |  Her pantomimicry of a cat was hilarious. 她模仿猫的动作滑稽极了。 | synonyms: mime, dumb show, gesticulation | antonyms: speech, verbalization | noun


pantomimic:/ˌpæntəˈmɪmɪk/ | adj. | 哑剧的；默剧的| Relating to or resembling pantomime; performed in mime. |  He gave a pantomimic performance of the story. 他用哑剧表演了这个故事。| synonyms: mimetic, silent, unspoken | antonyms: spoken, verbal | adjective
