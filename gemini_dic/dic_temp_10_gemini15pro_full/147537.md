overpatriotisms: /ˌoʊvərˈpeɪtriətɪzəmz/ | n. | 过度的爱国主义; 极端爱国主义 | Excessive patriotism; extreme nationalism that can be detrimental or harmful. |  Their overpatriotisms led them to blindly support the war. 他们的极端爱国主义使他们盲目支持战争。|  The politician's overpatriotisms alienated many moderate voters.  这位政治家的极端爱国主义疏远了许多温和的选民。 | 近义词: ultranationalism, jingoism, chauvinism | 反义词: internationalism, cosmopolitanism | 词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "patriotism" (noun meaning "love for or devotion to one's country").
