inferobranchian:/ˌɪnfəroʊˈbræŋkiən/
adjective
下鳃的，鳃位低下的
situated below the gills, specifically referring to the gills of certain mollusks being positioned low on the body.
Inferobranchian snails are found in deep-sea hydrothermal vents.  下鳃蜗牛在深海热液喷口被发现。
The inferobranchian placement of the gills allows for efficient respiration in oxygen-poor environments.  鳃的低位放置使得在缺氧环境中能够有效呼吸。
