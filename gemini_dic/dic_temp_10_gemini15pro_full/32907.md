choo-choos:/ˈtʃuːtʃuːz/| n.|火车（儿语）|A child's word for a train.  It's imitative of the sound a train makes.|例句：The little boy loves his choo-choos.  小男孩喜欢他的火车玩具。

例句：Look at all the choo-choos! 看所有的火车！|近义词：train, locomotive, engine|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
