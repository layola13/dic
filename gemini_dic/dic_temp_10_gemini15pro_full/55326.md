dissuadable:/dɪˈsweɪdəbl/ | adj. | 可劝阻的；易被说服的 | Able to be dissuaded; easily persuaded to change one's mind. |  He was dissuadable from his rash plan. 他被他鲁莽的计划劝退了。| synonyms: persuadable, susceptible, amenable | antonyms:  intransigent, obstinate, unyielding | adjective

dissuade:/dɪˈsweɪd/ | vt. | 劝阻；阻止 | To persuade someone not to do something. | I tried to dissuade him from quitting his job. 我试图劝他不要辞职。| synonyms: deter, discourage | antonyms: encourage, urge | verb
