betweens:/bɪˈtwiːnz/
noun
中间物;夹层物;两物之间的空间或时间
Something intermediate between two other things.
The betweens of a sandwich are the fillings.  三明治的夹层物是馅料。
synonyms:  interval, interim, gap, space, interstice
antonyms: extremes, outsides
