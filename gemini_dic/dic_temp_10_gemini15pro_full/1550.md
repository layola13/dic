achiever:/əˈtʃiːvər/| n. |成就者，成功人士|Someone who achieves a lot, especially in their work. |例句：She is a high achiever in her field.  她是她所在领域的佼佼者。

He is a proven achiever with a track record of success. 他是一位久经考验的成功人士，有着良好的业绩记录。|近义词：winner, successor, high-flyer|反义词:failure, loser, underachiever|词性:名词
