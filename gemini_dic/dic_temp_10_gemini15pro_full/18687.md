bequeath:/bɪˈkwiːð/ /bɪˈkwiːθ/| vt.|遗赠；把…传给后世|To leave (personal property) to someone by means of a will.  To hand down or pass on.|He bequeathed his entire estate to his daughter. 他把全部财产遗赠给了女儿。|近义词：leave, will, bestow, impart|反义词: inherit, receive|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bi-cwēðan (“to bequeath, give by testament, declare, utter”), from be- (“by, about, upon”) + cwēðan (“to say, speak”). Cognate with Dutch becweten (“to confess”), German bequeme (“convenient”).
