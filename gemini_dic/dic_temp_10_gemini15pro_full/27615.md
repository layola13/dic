cancellous:/ˈkænsələs/| adj.|多孔的，网状的，海绵状的|Having a reticular or lattice-like structure with many small spaces or cavities.|The cancellous bone structure allows for lightweight yet strong support.|近义词：porous, spongy, trabecular|反义词：compact, dense|词性:形容词|词源: From Latin *cancellōsus*, from *cancellus* (“lattice, grating”).
