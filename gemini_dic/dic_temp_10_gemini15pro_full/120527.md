PHRASE->
"Mio caro/a" (My dear) - used as a term of endearment.
"Il mio amore" (My love) - used to express affection.
"Mio Dio!" (My God!) - used as an exclamation of surprise or dismay.
"A mio parere" (In my opinion) - used to express one's viewpoint.
"Mio malgrado" (Despite myself/Against my will) - used to indicate an action done unwillingly.
