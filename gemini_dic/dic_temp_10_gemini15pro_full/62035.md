embroidery:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdəri/| n.|刺绣|The decorative art of applying needlework to fabric. |Her dress featured intricate embroidery. 她的裙子上有精美的刺绣。|近义词: needlework, stitching, fancywork|反义词: plainness|noun

embroider:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdər/| vt.|刺绣，绣花；渲染，润色|To decorate fabric with needlework; to embellish a story or account. |She embroidered a beautiful floral design on the tablecloth.  她在桌布上绣了一个漂亮的花卉图案。 He embroidered the tale of his adventures, adding many exciting details. 他添油加醋地讲述了他的冒险故事，添加了许多令人兴奋的细节。|近义词: stitch, adorn, embellish|反义词: simplify, understate|verb
