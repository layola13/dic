hospice: /ˈhɒspɪs/ (UK) /ˈhɑːspɪs/ (US)| n.|临终关怀医院；收容所；旅客招待所|A place that provides care and support for people who are terminally ill, especially in their last few months of life. It aims to make them as comfortable as possible.|My grandfather spent his last few weeks in a hospice. 我的祖父在临终关怀医院度过了他最后几周。|
She volunteered at the local hospice. 她在当地的临终关怀医院做志愿者。
