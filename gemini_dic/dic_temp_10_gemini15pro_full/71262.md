fivers:/ˈfaɪvərz/
n.
五元纸币；五英镑纸币
Five-pound notes; banknotes with a value of five pounds.
I've got a couple of fivers in my wallet. 我的钱包里有两张五英镑的纸币。

n.
五美元纸币
Five-dollar bills
Can you give me change for a tenner? I only need fivers. 你能帮我把十元换成五元的吗？我只需要五元面值的。

n. (俚语)
五岁以下的儿童
Children under five years of age (slang)
The nursery looks after fivers and under.  这家托儿所照顾五岁及以下的儿童。


近义词:five-pound notes, five-dollar bills (depending on context), fives
反义词: (No direct antonym; could consider larger denominations like "tenners," "twenties," etc.)
词性:noun
