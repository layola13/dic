broad-minded:/ˌbrɔːdˈmaɪndɪd/| adj.|心胸开阔的，思想开放的|Willing to consider new or different ideas and opinions, especially those that are not traditionally accepted.|She's a broad-minded woman who is always willing to listen to different perspectives. 她是一位思想开阔的女性，总是愿意倾听不同的观点。|近义词：tolerant, liberal, open-minded, unprejudiced|反义词：narrow-minded, intolerant, prejudiced, bigoted|词性:形容词
词源:  "broad" (wide) + "minded" (having a particular kind of mind).  The word implies having a mind open to a wide range of ideas and perspectives.
