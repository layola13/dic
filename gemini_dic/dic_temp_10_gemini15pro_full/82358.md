growlers:/ˈɡraʊlər/ (US), /ˈɡraʊlə/ (UK)| n. |咆哮者；低吼者；(装啤酒的)大肚瓶|A person or thing that growls; a large jug or bottle, typically one holding beer. |The growlers at the zoo kept the visitors awake all night. 动物园里的咆哮者让游客整夜无法入睡。|The bar filled my growler with their best IPA. 酒吧用他们最好的印度淡色艾尔啤酒装满了我的大肚瓶。|Synonyms: grumbler, complainer (for person); jug, bottle, flagon (for container)|Antonyms: whisperer (for person)|Noun
