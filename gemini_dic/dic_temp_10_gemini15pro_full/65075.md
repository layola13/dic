/eθ/ |pron.| （关系代词）…的人；…的物；…的事| Used to refer to a person, thing, or event that has already been mentioned or is easily understood.| The house, which is very old, needs a lot of repairs. 这栋房子很老了，需要大量维修。 |Synonyms: that, who, whom (in certain contexts)| Antonyms: None |Pronoun


/eθ/ |det.| （疑问代词）什么；多少；…的| Used to ask for information about something.| What time is it? 现在几点了？| Synonyms: which, how much/many (in certain contexts) | Antonyms: None |Determiner
