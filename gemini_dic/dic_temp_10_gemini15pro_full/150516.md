pantomiming:/ˈpæntəˌmaɪmɪŋ/ | n./v. |  哑剧表演；用哑剧表达 | Expressing or conveying something through gestures and facial expressions without speech. |  He was pantomiming eating a large hamburger. 他用哑剧表演吃一个大汉堡包。 |  The children were pantomiming a story about a lost dog. 孩子们用哑剧表演一个关于丢失小狗的故事。|  miming, gesturing, acting out | speaking, talking | v./n.
