overskeptical:/ˌəʊvəˈskeptɪkəl/ | adj. | 过分怀疑的，怀疑过度的 | Excessively skeptical; inclined to doubt or disbelieve claims or statements more than is warranted. | She was overskeptical about the new product, refusing to believe any of the positive reviews. 她对新产品过度怀疑，拒绝相信任何正面评价。|  He's so overskeptical that he doesn't even trust scientific evidence. 他如此多疑，甚至不相信科学证据。|  cynical, distrustful, suspicious |  credulous, gullible, trusting | adjective

Etymology:
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive, too much") + "skeptical" (from Greek "skeptikos" meaning "inquiring, thoughtful," ultimately from "skeptesthai" meaning "to consider, examine").
