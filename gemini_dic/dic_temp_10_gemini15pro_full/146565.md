overgrades:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪdz/
v. |给…过高的等级；过高估计|To assign a higher grade than deserved; to overestimate.|The teacher overgraded the essays, giving everyone an A. 老师给作文打了过高的分数，每个人都得了A。|The company overgraded the potential of the new product. 公司高估了新产品的潜力。|overestimate, overvalue|underestimate, undervalue|动词

overgrades:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪdz/
n. |过高的等级|A grade that is higher than deserved.|The student's overgrades were eventually discovered. 学生过高的分数最终被发现了。|Receiving overgrades can create a false sense of accomplishment.  获得过高的等级会造成一种虚假的成就感。 |inflated grades|low grades|名词
