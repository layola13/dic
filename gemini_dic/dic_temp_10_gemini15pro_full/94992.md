in-the-wind:/ˌɪn ðə ˈwɪnd/ | adj. |即将发生，传播中，谣传 | Something that is 'in the wind' is being planned or discussed secretly, or is about to happen. It can also refer to rumors or information circulating. | There's a big change in the wind. (即将有大变动。) | synonyms: imminent, brewing, forthcoming, circulating, rumored | antonyms: settled, confirmed, definite | adjective
