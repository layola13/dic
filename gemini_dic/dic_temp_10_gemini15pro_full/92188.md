hyperstimulating:/ˌhaɪpərˈstɪmjuleɪtɪŋ/
adjective
过度刺激的；过分刺激的
Causing excessive stimulation.  Increasing the activity of something to an excessive degree.
The loud music and flashing lights were hyperstimulating to the baby.  喧闹的音乐和闪烁的灯光对婴儿来说过度刺激。
The constant notifications on his phone were hyperstimulating and made it difficult to focus. 他手机上不断的通知信息过度刺激了他，使他难以集中注意力。
overstimulating, overexciting
understimulating
