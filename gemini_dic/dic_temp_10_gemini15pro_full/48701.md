defibrillating:/diːˈfɪbrɪleɪtɪŋ/
|v. |除颤，用除颤器使（心脏）恢复正常跳动|The paramedics were defibrillating the patient.  医护人员正在给病人进行除颤。|
|v. |使（某物）恢复生机，使复苏|The government is trying to defibrillate the economy. 政府正试图使经济复苏。|
|近义词：resuscitating, reviving|
|反义词：killing, harming|
|词性：动词 (present participle of defibrillate)|

defibrillate:/diːˈfɪbrɪleɪt/
|v. |除颤，用除颤器使（心脏）恢复正常跳动|They defibrillated him twice before he regained consciousness.  他们在对他进行两次除颤后，他才恢复了意识。|
|v. |使（某物）恢复生机，使复苏|The new CEO hopes to defibrillate the failing company. 新任首席执行官希望能够重振这家濒临倒闭的公司。|
|近义词：resuscitate, revive|
|反义词：kill, harm|
|词性：动词|


词源:  From de- + fibrillation (irregular rapid contractions of the muscle fibers of the heart), from Latin fibra "fiber" + -ation.
