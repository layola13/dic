norbertine:/nɔːrˈbɜːrtɪn/
n. / adj.
Norbertine (canon), Norbertine (nun) / Norbertine
诺伯丁会修士（男）/ 诺伯丁会修女（女）/  诺伯丁会的
A member of a Roman Catholic religious order (the Premonstratensians) for men and women, founded by St. Norbert in 12th-century France.  The order follows the Rule of St. Augustine.
例句：The Norbertine canons are known for their white habits.  诺伯丁会修士以他们的白色会服而闻名。

例句：The Norbertine abbey is a place of peace and contemplation. 这座诺伯丁修道院是一个和平与沉思的地方。
近义词：Premonstratensian
反义词: N/A
词性: noun, adjective
