devotionalness:/dɪˈvoʊʃənl̩nəs/
n.
虔诚；挚爱；热忱
The state of being devoted, characterized by deep love, reverence, or piety.
Her devotionalness to her family was evident in everything she did. 她对家人的挚爱体现在她所做的一切中。
piety, devoutness, reverence, religiousness
irreverence, impiety, indifference, apathy
noun

devotion:/dɪˈvoʊʃən/
n.
献身；奉献；热爱；虔诚
The act of devoting oneself to something or someone; deep love, loyalty, or religious worship.
His devotion to his work is admirable. 他对工作的奉献精神令人钦佩。
commitment, dedication, loyalty, piety
disloyalty, indifference, neglect, apathy
noun

devote:/dɪˈvoʊt/
v.
致力于；奉献；把…用于
To give all or a large part of one's time or resources to something or someone.
She devoted her life to helping others. 她一生致力于帮助他人。
dedicate, commit, allocate, assign
neglect, ignore, abandon, forsake
verb
