bludging:/ˈblʌdʒɪŋ/| n. vi.|偷懒，逃避工作，蹭(吃喝玩乐等)|The act of avoiding work or responsibility, often by pretending to be busy.  It can also mean taking advantage of someone's generosity without contributing anything in return.|He was bludging on the job and got fired. 他在工作中偷懒被解雇了。|Synonyms: shirking, slacking, dodging, loafing|Antonyms: working hard, diligent, industrious|noun
