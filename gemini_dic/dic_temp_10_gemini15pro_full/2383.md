adipogenous: /ˌædɪˈpɒdʒɪnəs/
adjective
脂肪产生的；脂肪生成的
Producing or arising from fat; fatty.  Relating to the formation of fat.
Adipogenous tissue can accumulate around organs. 脂肪组织会在器官周围积聚。
Certain cells are adipogenous, meaning they are capable of differentiating into adipocytes (fat cells).  某些细胞是脂肪生成的，这意味着它们能够分化成脂肪细胞。
近义词: lipogenic, fat-producing
反义词: lipolytic (脂肪分解的)
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adipō- (stem of adeps "fat") + -genous "producing," from Greek -genes "born," from genos "birth."
