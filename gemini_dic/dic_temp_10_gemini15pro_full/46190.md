cyprinoid:/ˈsaɪprɪnɔɪd/| adj. n.|鲤科鱼的；鲤科鱼|Relating to, resembling, or belonging to the Cyprinidae, the carp family of fishes.|The goldfish is a common cyprinoid. 金鱼是一种常见的鲤科鱼。|近义词：carp-like, cyprinid|反义词:|adj. n.

cyprinoid:/ˈsaɪprɪnɔɪd/| n.|鲤科鱼|A fish of the family Cyprinidae, which includes carps, minnows, and barbs.|Many cyprinoids are popular aquarium fish. 许多鲤科鱼是受欢迎的观赏鱼。|近义词：carp, minnow, barb|反义词:|n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From  New Latin *Cyprinoidei, from Cyprinus (genus of the common carp) + -oidea (resembling).  Cyprinus comes from Greek kyprînos (carp).
