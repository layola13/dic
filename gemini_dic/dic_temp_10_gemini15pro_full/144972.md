overaccentuate: /ˌoʊvərækˈsɛntʃueɪt/ /ˌəʊvərækˈsɛntʃueɪt/
verb
过分强调；过分突出
To emphasize something to an excessive degree.
He tends to overaccentuate the negative aspects of every situation. 他倾向于过分强调每种情况的消极方面。

The media overaccentuated the violence at the protest. 媒体过分强调了抗议中的暴力行为。

近义词: overemphasize, exaggerate, overstate
反义词: downplay, understate, minimize
词性: verb
