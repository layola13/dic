ecphonesis:/ɛkfəˈniːsɪs/ | n. | 感叹; 呼喊; 突出的感情(尤指悲痛); | An outburst of emotion, especially grief. A figure of speech in which an exclamatory phrase or sentence is inserted to express sudden strong emotion. |  "O tempora! O mores!" is a famous example of ecphonesis from Cicero.  哦，时代！哦，道德！这是西塞罗一句著名的感叹语。| synonym: exclamation, outcry, interjection | antonym: understatement, repression | noun
