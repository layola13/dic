inequal:/ɪnˈiːkwəl/ | adj. | 不相等的，不均等的 | Not equal; uneven, disparate. | Example: The twins received inequal shares of the inheritance. 这对双胞胎继承的遗产份额不相等。

Example:  The distribution of resources was inequal across the region.  该地区的资源分配不均。

Synonyms: unequal, uneven, disparate, disproportionate, asymmetric, biased, discriminatory

Antonyms: equal, even, uniform, balanced, equitable, fair, just

词性: 形容词
