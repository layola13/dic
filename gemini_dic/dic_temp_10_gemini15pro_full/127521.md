necrolatry:/ˈnɛkrəˌlæt.ri/| n.|崇拜死者，恋尸癖|The practice of worshipping the dead.  It can involve rituals, prayers, or offerings made to deceased ancestors or other important figures. |Some cultures practice necrolatry as a way to honor their ancestors.  一些文化将崇拜死者作为一种尊敬祖先的方式。| Necrolatry is a complex phenomenon observed in various historical and cultural contexts. 崇拜死者是一种复杂的现象，在不同的历史和文化背景下都有观察到。|  synonym: ancestor worship,  | antonym: iconoclasm | noun
