favourites:/ˈfeɪvərɪts/ /ˈfeɪvərɪts/ | n. | 最喜爱的东西；最喜爱的人；收藏夹 | Something or someone that is preferred or loved above all others. A collection of preferred items, especially on a computer or phone. | My favourites are chocolate and ice cream. 我最喜欢的是巧克力和冰淇淋。 | synonyms: preferences, choices, beloved, dearest | antonyms: disliked, hated | noun

favourites:/ˈfeɪvərɪts/ /ˈfeɪvərɪts/ | adj. | 最喜欢的 | Preferred or loved above all others. (Less common usage as an adjective) |  This is my favourite song. 这是我最喜欢的歌。 | synonyms: preferred, beloved, dearest | antonyms: disliked, hated | adjective
