defriend:/diːˈfrɛnd/
verb
取消好友；删除好友
To remove someone from a list of friends or contacts on a social networking website.
I defriended him after he posted those offensive comments. 我在他发布那些攻击性评论后把他从好友列表中删除了。
She defriended several people she no longer spoke to. 她删除了几个她不再联系的人的好友关系。
Synonyms: unfriend, block, remove
Antonyms: befriend, add, follow
词性: 动词
