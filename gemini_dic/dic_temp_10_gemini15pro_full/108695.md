leaks:/liːks/
n.|泄漏，漏洞|An act or instance of leaking.  A place where fluid leaks in or out.|The roof has leaks. 屋顶有漏水的地方。|leakage, spills, escapes|patches, seals|noun

leaks:/liːks/
v.|泄漏，透露|To permit something to escape, especially through a hole or crack; to become known.|The pipe is leaking gas. 管道正在漏气。|disclose, divulge, reveal|conceal, hide|verb
