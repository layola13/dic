cliff: /klɪf/| n. |悬崖，峭壁|A steep, high rock face, especially one that drops almost vertically to the water or land below.|例句：The climber carefully scaled the cliff. 登山者小心翼翼地攀登悬崖。

例句：The waves crashed against the base of the cliff. 海浪拍打着悬崖的底部。

|近义词：bluff, precipice, crag|反义词:plain, flat|词性:noun
