out-greened:/ˌaʊtˈɡriːnd/
verb
胜过...的绿色; 比...更环保
To surpass someone in terms of environmental consciousness or practices.
Our company out-greened its competitors by implementing a zero-waste policy. 我们公司通过实施零浪费政策，在环保方面胜过了竞争对手。
The new electric car model out-greened all previous models with its innovative battery technology.  这款新的电动汽车凭借其创新的电池技术，比所有以前的车型都更环保。
近义词: outdo, surpass, eclipse
反义词: lag behind, fall short
