nonrepresentative:/ˌnɒnˌreprɪˈzentətɪv/ | adj. | 非代表性的，不典型的 | Not accurately representing the whole of something; atypical. |  This small group is nonrepresentative of the general population.  这个小组不代表总人口。| Synonyms: atypical, uncharacteristic, untypical | Antonyms: representative, typical, characteristic | adjective

nonrepresentatives:/ˌnɒnˌreprɪˈzentətɪvz/ | n. | 非代表性的人或物 | People or things that do not accurately represent the whole of something. | The nonrepresentatives were excluded from the survey. 这些非代表性的人被排除在调查之外。 | Synonyms: outliers, anomalies | Antonyms: representatives, exemplars | noun
