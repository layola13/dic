interlamellar:/ˌɪntərˈlæmələr/
adjective
层间的；片层间的
Situated or occurring between lamellae.
The interlamellar space in bone contains bone cells called osteocytes.  骨骼的层间隙包含称为骨细胞的骨细胞。
Interlamellar forces are crucial for the stability of clay minerals. 层间力对于粘土矿物的稳定性至关重要。
近义词: interlaminar
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could consider "intralamellar" - within a lamella)
词性: adjective
