pandour:/ˈpændʊər/| n.|潘多尔兵，劫掠者|A member of a former body of Hungarian infantry noted for fierceness and brutality. Historically, a pandour was an irregular soldier, especially one serving in the Austrian army in the 18th century, known for their ruthlessness.|The pandours swept through the village, pillaging and burning. 潘多尔兵席卷了村庄，烧杀抢掠。| The retreating army left a trail of destruction in their wake, reminiscent of the ravages of pandours.  撤退的军队留下了一片破坏的痕迹，让人想起潘多尔兵的蹂躏。| 近义词:marauder, raider, pillager, plunderer| 反义词:protector, defender|词性:noun
