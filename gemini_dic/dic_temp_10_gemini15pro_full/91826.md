hypergenetic:/ˌhaɪpərdʒəˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. |表生作用的，地表生成的| Formed or occurring on or near the surface of the earth; superficial.  Relating to geological processes that occur on or near the surface of the earth, such as weathering, erosion, and deposition. | The hypergenetic zone is where rocks are exposed to the atmosphere and hydrosphere. 表生带是岩石暴露于大气和水圈的地方。| Hypergenetic enrichment of gold occurs in some lateritic profiles.  在一些红土剖面中发生了金的表生富集。| 近义词:supergene, epigene | 反义词:hypogene | 词性:形容词
