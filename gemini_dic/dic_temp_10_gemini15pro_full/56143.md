documentation's:/ˌdɒkjʊmenˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 文件；文档（documentation 的所有格形式）|  The documentation's completeness is crucial for the audit.  该文档的完整性对审计至关重要。 |  Example: The project's documentation's organization was chaotic.  这个项目的文档组织混乱不堪. | synonyms: records, files, paperwork | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a possessive form.  The antonym for "documentation" itself could be "lack of documentation" or "misinformation") |  noun (possessive)


Etymology: From Late Latin *documentātiōn-, from documentāre  "to furnish with documents, provide proof," from documentum "proof, evidence, lesson, example" (see document). The -ion suffix forms nouns of action or condition. The -'s is the possessive marker.
