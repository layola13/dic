gagged:/ɡæɡd/ | v. | 塞住，使…缄默；| To prevent someone from speaking or expressing themselves freely, often by putting something in or over their mouth. | The kidnappers gagged the hostage to prevent him from crying out. 绑匪们堵住了人质的嘴，防止他喊叫。|  The journalist felt gagged by the government's censorship laws.  这位记者感觉政府的审查法律让他无法发声。 | synonyms: silenced, muffled, suppressed | antonyms:  unmuzzled, liberated, freed | verb (past participle)
