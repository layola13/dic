arroyo:/əˈrɔɪoʊ/ (US), /əˈrɔɪəʊ/ (UK)| n.|干谷，小河，溪流|A watercourse (such as a creek) in an arid region, often dry except after heavy rains.  A deep gully cut by an arroyo.|The hikers followed the winding arroyo, hoping to find a spring.  徒步旅行者沿着蜿蜒的干谷走，希望能找到一眼泉水。|近义词：creek, gully, wadi, wash|反义词:ridge, hill|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *arroyo*, diminutive of *arroyo*, "stream," from Latin *arrugia*.
