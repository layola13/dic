nonpredicatively:/ˌnɒnˌprɪˈdɪkətɪvli/ | adv. | 非断言地，非谓语地 |  In a way that does not function as a predicate.  It refers to a way of defining or referring to something where the definition or reference is not itself part of the thing being defined or referred to. |  The concept of 'set' is defined nonpredicatively in axiomatic set theory to avoid Russell's paradox.  集合的概念在公理集合论中被非断言地定义以避免罗素悖论。|  The property of being a 'tall person' is defined nonpredicatively, as it doesn't require the person to be part of the definition of 'tall'. 身高高的属性被非断言地定义，因为它不需要这个人成为“高”的定义的一部分。|  n/a |  predicatively | adverb
