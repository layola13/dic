overmourning:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrnɪŋ/
|n.|过度悲伤，过分哀悼|The act of mourning excessively or for too long a period.|Her overmourning for her pet hamster was a bit excessive.|Synonym: excessive grieving|Antonym: acceptance, moving on
