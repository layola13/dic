bestow:/bɪˈstoʊ/| vt.|授予，给予，赠予|to give something as a gift or honor|The queen bestowed a knighthood on him. 女王授予他爵位。|近义词：grant, confer, award, present|反义词：withhold, deprive, take away|词性:动词
Etymology: From Middle English *bestouen*, from Old English *bestōƿian* (“to place, put, arrange, stow”), equivalent to *be-* +‎ *stow*. Cognate with Dutch *bestowen*, German *bestowen*.
