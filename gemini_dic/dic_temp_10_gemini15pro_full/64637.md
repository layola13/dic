erythrochroic:/ɪˌrɪθrəˈkroʊɪk/
adjective
红铬的;  呈现红铬色的
Relating to or exhibiting erythrochroism.  Specifically, relating to the property of some minerals and substances to appear red by transmitted light and green by reflected light.
Alexandrite is a famous example of an erythrochroic gem.  亚历山大石是著名的红铬宝石的例子。
The mineral displayed erythrochroic properties, shifting from red to green depending on the light source.  这种矿物显示出红铬特性，根据光源的不同，颜色会从红色变为绿色。
Near synonyms: dichroic, pleochroic
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could use terms like "monochromatic" to imply the lack of color change)
词性: adjective
