bruising:/ˈbruːzɪŋ/| adj. n.| 令人痛苦的，挫伤；擦伤| causing physical or emotional pain; the act of inflicting bruises or the state of being bruised. |The team suffered a bruising defeat.  该队遭遇了一场惨痛的失败。|近义词：painful, hurtful, damaging, traumatic, crushing (adj); contusion, injury (n)|反义词:  healing, soothing, comforting (adj) |词性:adjective, noun

bruising:/ˈbruːzɪŋ/| n.| 挫伤；擦伤 | an injury appearing as an area of discoloured skin on the body, caused by a blow or impact rupturing underlying blood vessels.|She had a nasty bruising on her arm. 她手臂上有一块严重的瘀伤。|近义词：contusion, injury, lesion|反义词: healing |词性:noun
