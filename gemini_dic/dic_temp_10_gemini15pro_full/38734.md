conditionings:/kənˈdɪʃənɪŋz/
n.
条件反射，训练，空调系统
the process of training or influencing someone or something to behave in a particular way or to accept a particular set of values.
Pavlov's experiments on dogs are a classic example of classical conditioning.  巴甫洛夫的狗实验是经典条件反射的典型例子。

The air conditioner provides comfortable conditionings for the room.  空调为房间提供了舒适的环境。

Synonyms: training, indoctrination, habituation, acclimatization, adaptation
Antonyms: deconditioning, unlearning
