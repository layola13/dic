leones:/leˈoʊnez/| n.|狮子（复数）|The plural form of "león" (Spanish for "lion").  It refers to more than one lion.|We saw three leones on safari. 我们在狩猎旅行中看到了三只狮子。|近义词：lions|反义词: (No direct antonym for an animal noun)|noun
