nonlabeling:/ˌnɒnˈleɪbəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 非标记的，未标记的 | The process of not adding a label to something, often in the context of food or other products, or in research where a control group is not given a specific treatment. |  The study used a nonlabeling technique to observe natural behavior. 这项研究使用了一种非标记技术来观察自然行为。|  unlabeled, unmarked | labeled, marked | adjective
