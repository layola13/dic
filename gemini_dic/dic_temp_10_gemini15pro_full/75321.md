例句：The long, gray winter had left everyone feeling rather froppish. 漫长而灰暗的冬天让每个人都感到有些沮丧。|近义词：irritable, grumpy, sullen, morose, peevish, petulant, churlish|反义词：cheerful, happy, pleasant, amiable|词性:形容词
