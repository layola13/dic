outblesses:/ˌaʊtˈblɛsɪz/
verb
超越祝福；胜过…的祝福
To bless someone or something to a greater extent than another.  This word implies surpassing another in the act of bestowing blessings.
He outblessed his rival by donating even more to the charity. 他向慈善机构捐赠的钱比他的竞争对手更多，胜过了他的祝福。
The shaman outblessed the other priests with a more powerful invocation.  萨满用更强大的咒语胜过了其他祭司的祝福。
近义词:surpasses in blessing, outdoes in blessing
反义词: curses, damns
词性:v.
