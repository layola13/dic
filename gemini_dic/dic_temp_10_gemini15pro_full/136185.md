nonrelational:/ˌnɒnrɪˈleɪʃənəl/ | adj. |非关系型的|Not relational; not based on the relational model of data.  Used especially of databases that do not use the table/row/column structure of relational databases.| MongoDB is a popular nonrelational database.  MongoDB是一个流行的非关系型数据库。| Nonrelational databases are better suited for certain types of data than relational databases. 非关系型数据库比关系型数据库更适合某些类型的数据。|近义词: NoSQL |反义词: relational |词性: adjective
