hematuria:/ˌhiːməˈtʊəriə/ /ˌheməˈtʊriə/| n. |血尿|The presence of blood in the urine.  |Example 1: The doctor suspected a kidney infection because of the patient's hematuria.  医生怀疑病人患有肾脏感染，因为病人出现了血尿。

Example 2:  Trauma to the bladder can cause hematuria.  膀胱外伤会导致血尿。|Synonym:  blood in urine|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, consider normal urine)|noun
