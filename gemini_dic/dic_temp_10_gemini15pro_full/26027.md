bushboy:/ˈbʊʃbɔɪ/ | n. | 丛林男孩；居住在灌木丛中的男孩；野孩子 | A boy who lives in the bush, especially one who is considered uncivilized or uneducated.  A derogatory term often used with racist connotations. |  He was called a bushboy by the city kids because of his simple clothes and manners.  那些城里的孩子因为他衣着简朴、举止粗俗而叫他“野孩子”。| 近义词: wild child, feral child | 反义词: city boy, sophisticated child | 词性: noun
