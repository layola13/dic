omo:/ˈoʊmoʊ/ | adj. n. |omo, Nigerian, a brand of detergent |A popular brand of laundry detergent in Nigeria, sometimes used as a generic term for detergent. Can also refer to someone or something Nigerian.| Example:  I added some omo to the washing machine. 我在洗衣机里加了一些omo洗衣粉。

Example: He's a proud omo naija. 他是一个自豪的尼日利亚人。| Synonym: detergent, soap, Nigerian | Antonym: N/A | word type: Noun, Adjective

词源: Omo is a product name, originally a brand of detergent made by Unilever.  It became so popular in Nigeria that it's often used generically.  The Yoruba word "omo" means child, but this is likely coincidental.
