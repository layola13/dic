anecdotage: /ˈænɪkˌdoʊtɪdʒ/ | n. | 轶事；老年；爱讲轶事的年纪 | The period of life in which a person tends to reminisce about past experiences, often telling the same stories repeatedly.  It refers to the state of being old and prone to telling anecdotes. |  He's reached his anecdotage and bores everyone with the same old stories. 他已经到了爱讲古的年纪，总是用同样的老故事烦扰每个人。

My grandfather, now in his anecdotage, often tells stories of his youth. 我的祖父现在年纪大了，经常讲他年轻时的故事。| 近义词: reminiscence, memoirs, nostalgia | 反义词: youth, present | 词性: noun
