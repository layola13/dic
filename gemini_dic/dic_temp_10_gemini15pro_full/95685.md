inconcoct:/ˌɪnkɒnˈkɒkt/ /ˌɪnkɑːnˈkɑːkt/
verb
调制；捏造；编造
To devise, invent, or fabricate, especially by combining different ingredients or elements.
He concocted a strange story about being attacked by a giant squirrel. 他编造了一个关于被一只巨大的松鼠袭击的奇怪故事。
contrive, fabricate, devise, invent, make up
