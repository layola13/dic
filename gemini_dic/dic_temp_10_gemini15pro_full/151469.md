paroxysmally:/ˌpærəkˈsɪzməli/| adv.|阵发性地，突发性地|Occurring in paroxysms; recurrent or intensifying suddenly.|Her laughter came paroxysmally, interspersed with moments of quiet contemplation.|近义词: spasmodically, intermittently, fitfully|反义词: continuously, steadily|词性:副词
词源: From Greek paroxysmos "irritation, exasperation," from paroxunein "to sharpen, irritate, provoke," from para- "beside" + oxunein "to sharpen."
