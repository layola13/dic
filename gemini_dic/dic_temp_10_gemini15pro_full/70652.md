filtratable:/ˈfɪltrətəbl/| adj.|可过滤的|Able to be filtered; that can pass through a filter.|The solution is filtratable through a fine mesh.  这个溶液可以通过细网过滤。|例句：The water is filtratable using a simple cloth filter.  这水可以用简单的布过滤器过滤。

例句：Only filtratable substances can pass through the membrane. 只有可过滤的物质才能通过这层膜。|近义词：filterable, strainable|反义词：non-filtratable, unfilterable|词性:形容词
