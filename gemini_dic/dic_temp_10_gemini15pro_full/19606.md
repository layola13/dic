bignonia:/bɪɡˈnoʊniə/ | n. |紫葳；凌霄花 |A climbing plant with brightly colored, trumpet-shaped flowers, belonging to the Bignoniaceae family. |例句：The bignonia climbed up the trellis, its vibrant flowers a splash of color against the brick wall. 紫葳爬上了棚架，鲜艳的花朵在砖墙上形成了一片色彩。

例句：The fragrant bignonia filled the air with a sweet, heady perfume. 芬芳的紫葳使空气中充满了甜美、浓郁的香味。|近义词：trumpet vine, cross vine |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

Etymology: From the Modern Latin genus name *Bignonia*, named in honor of the Abbé Jean-Paul Bignon (1662–1743), librarian to Louis XIV of France.
