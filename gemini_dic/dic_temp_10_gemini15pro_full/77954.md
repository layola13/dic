genre:/ˈʒɑːnrə/ (US) /ˈʒɒ̃rə/ (UK)| n.|类型，体裁，流派|A category of artistic composition, as in music or literature, characterized by similarities in form, style, or subject matter.|This book belongs to the thriller genre. 这本书属于惊悚类型。|近义词：category, type, class, form, style|反义词: N/A|noun
