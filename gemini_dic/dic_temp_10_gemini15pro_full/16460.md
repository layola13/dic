barer:/ˈbeərər/ (UK) /ˈberər/ (US)| adj.|更裸露的；更空的；更贫瘠的|comparative adjective of bare: more bare |The north face of the mountain is barer than the south. 这座山的北坡比南坡更光秃。|近义词：more exposed, more desolate, more barren|反义词：more covered, more lush, more fertile|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *barer, *barerere, comparative of bar, from Old English bær (“bare, naked, uncovered, mere, open, manifest”). Cognate with West Frisian bearer (“barer”), Dutch barer (“barer”), German barer (“barer”).
