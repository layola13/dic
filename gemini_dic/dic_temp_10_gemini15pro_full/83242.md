gymnastic:/dʒɪmˈnæstɪk/ | adj. | 体操的；体操训练的 | Relating to gymnastics. |  She has great gymnastic ability. 她有很好的体操能力。

gymnastic:/dʒɪmˈnæstɪk/ | n. |  体操；体操运动 | A sport involving physical exercises and activities requiring balance, strength, flexibility, agility, coordination, and endurance. | Gymnastics is a demanding sport. 体操是一项要求很高的运动。


gymnastics:/dʒɪmˈnæstɪks/ | n. | 体操（通常用复数形式） | Exercises and activities performed in gymnastics.  | She competes in gymnastics. 她参加体操比赛。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *gymnastus, from Greek gymnastos "trainer of athletes," from gymnazein "to train naked, exercise," from gymnos "naked." The original Greek gymnasts exercised naked.
