ferrugineous:/fɛˌruːˈdʒɪniəs/
adjective
铁锈色的；含铁的
of the color of rust; containing iron.
The geologist identified the ferrugineous rock formation. 地质学家识别出了铁锈色的岩层。
The water flowing from the old mine was ferrugineous. 从老矿井流出的水是铁锈色的。
Synonyms: rusty, iron-colored, iron-rich, ochreous
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for color; for iron content) iron-deficient, iron-poor
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ferruginosus*, from *ferrugin* (“rust”), from *ferrum* (“iron”).
