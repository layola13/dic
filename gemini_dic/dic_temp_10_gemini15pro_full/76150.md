fuselike:/ˈfjuːzlaɪk/
adjective
像保险丝的；易熔的
Resembling a fuse; likely to melt or burn quickly.
The wire had a fuselike quality, thin and prone to overheating.  这根电线像保险丝一样，细而且容易过热。
The thin, fuselike filament glowed brightly. 细细的、像保险丝一样的灯丝发出明亮的光。
近义词:flammable, combustible, incendiary
反义词:fireproof, nonflammable
词性:adjective
词源:From "fuse" (引信，保险丝) + "-like" (像...的)
