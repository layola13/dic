augustans:/ɔːˈɡʌstənz/
|n.|奥古斯都时期的人；奥古斯都时代的作家、艺术家等|People or writers, artists, etc., of the Augustan age, a period marked by a flourishing of literature and art, often considered the golden age of a nation.|The Augustans valued clarity and elegance in their writing.|近义词:neoclassicists|反义词:|名词

augustan:/ɔːˈɡʌstən/
|adj.|奥古斯都时代的；（指文学艺术风格）古典主义的，优雅的|Relating to the Roman emperor Augustus or his age; characteristic of the golden age of Latin literature; or having a classical elegance and formality.|The poem had an Augustan formality and precision.|近义词:classical, neoclassical|反义词:romantic, modern|形容词
