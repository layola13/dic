overchafe:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃeɪf/
verb
擦伤；激怒；过度磨损
to make sore by rubbing; to irritate or vex
The rider overchafed the horse's flanks with his spurs. 骑手用马刺擦伤了马的侧腹。
The tight collar overchafed his neck.  紧绷的衣领擦伤了他的脖子。
His constant complaining overchafed my patience. 他不断的抱怨惹恼了我。
近义词: abrade, chafe, irritate, vex
反义词: soothe, calm
词性: verb
