councilmen:/ˈkaʊnslmən/ | n. | 市议员；政务委员 |  A member of a council, especially a city council.  This is now considered an outdated and sexist term. The preferred term is "council members" or "councilors." | The councilmen debated the proposed zoning changes for hours. 市议员就提议的区域划分变更进行了数小时的辩论。| council members, councilors | - | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *conseilman*, equivalent to *conseil* (council) + *man*.  The word "council" comes from Old French *conseil*, ultimately from Latin *consilium* "consultation, counsel, advice."
