munificently:/mjuːˈnɪfɪsəntli/
adverb
慷慨地；丰厚地
in a very generous way, giving or spending freely
He munificently donated a large sum of money to the charity. 他慷慨地向慈善机构捐赠了一大笔钱。
The king rewarded his loyal knight munificently. 国王慷慨地奖赏了他忠诚的骑士。

近义词: generously, lavishly, bountifully, liberally, profusely
反义词: stingily, meanly, sparingly, frugally, parsimoniously
词性: adverb
