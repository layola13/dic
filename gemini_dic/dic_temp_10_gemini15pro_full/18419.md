belted:/ˈbɛltɪd/| adj.|有腰带的，系腰带的|Wearing a belt; fastened with a belt.  Having a belt or a band.  Marked with bands or stripes.|She wore a belted dress. 她穿着一件系腰带的连衣裙。|近义词：cinched, girdled|反义词:loose, unbelted|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the verb "belt" (meaning to fasten with a belt) + "-ed" (suffix forming past participles and adjectives).  "Belt" itself comes from Old English "belt," related to Old Norse "belti," both meaning "belt, girdle."
