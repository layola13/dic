contrapuntally:/ˌkɒntrəˈpʌŋt(ə)li/ | adv. | 对位地，复调地 |  In a manner resembling counterpoint; with independent melodies sounding together.  | The two instruments played contrapuntally, creating a rich and complex texture.  两件乐器以对位的方式演奏，营造出丰富而复杂的质感。 |  polyphonically, harmonically |  monophonically | adverb
