churchmanship:/ˈtʃɜːtʃmənʃɪp/
n.
教会政策；教会忠诚；对教会的忠诚度
The principles and practices of a particular church or denomination, especially with regard to its organization, rituals, and doctrines.  A person's adherence to and support for a particular church or denomination.
He was known for his high churchmanship. 他以其对高教会派的忠诚而闻名。
loyalty, adherence, devotion, allegiance
disloyalty, apathy, indifference
noun
