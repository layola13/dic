granivorous:/ɡrəˈnɪvərəs/
adj.
食谷的；granivorous animals
Feeding on grain or seeds.
Granivorous birds, such as sparrows and finches, play an essential role in seed dispersal.  食谷鸟类，如麻雀和雀类，在种子传播中起着至关重要的作用。
Squirrels are granivorous and often bury nuts for later retrieval. 松鼠是食谷动物，经常埋藏坚果以便日后取回。
近义词:seed-eating, herbivorous
反义词:carnivorous
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin granum ("grain") + -vorus ("eating"), from vorare ("to devour").
