conf:/kɒnf/ (British) /kɑːnf/ (American) | verb | 承认；坦白；忏悔 | To admit or confess something, typically something wrong or embarrassing. | Example: He confed to stealing the cookies. 他承认偷了饼干。 | Synonym: admit, confess, acknowledge | Antonym: deny, conceal | verb
