morisco:/mɔːˈrɪskoʊ/| adj. n.|摩里斯科人的，摩里斯科人|A term used to describe the Muslims of Spain who were forced to convert to Christianity after the Reconquista.  It can refer to both the people and the cultural blend they created.|The expulsion of the Moriscos severely impacted Spain's economy. 摩里斯科人的驱逐严重影响了西班牙的经济。|Near synonyms: Mudéjar, crypto-Muslim|Antonym: Christian (in the context of 16th/17th century Spain)|Noun, Adjective


morisco:/mɔːˈrɪskoʊ/| n.|摩尔式建筑，摩尔式装饰|A style of architecture and decoration influenced by Moorish art and design, often characterized by intricate patterns, arches, and decorative tiles.|The Alhambra palace is a stunning example of Morisco architecture. 阿尔罕布拉宫是摩尔式建筑的杰出典范。|Near synonyms: Moorish, Islamic|Antonym: Gothic|Noun
