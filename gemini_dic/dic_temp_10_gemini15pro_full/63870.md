epileptic:/ˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪk/| adj. n.|癫痫的；癫痫患者|Relating to or affected by epilepsy, a neurological disorder marked by sudden recurrent episodes of sensory disturbance, loss of consciousness, or convulsions, associated with abnormal electrical activity in the brain.|She has epileptic seizures. 她有癫痫发作。|近义词：convulsive, seizure-prone|反义词: healthy, non-epileptic|词性:形容词

epileptic:/ˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪk/| n.|癫痫患者|A person who has epilepsy.|He's an epileptic and has to take medication daily. 他是一个癫痫患者，必须每天服药。|近义词：seizure sufferer|反义词: N/A (not applicable for a person)|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *epilepticus, from Greek ἐπιληπτικός (epilēptikos, “seized, attacked”), from ἐπιλαμβάνειν (epilambanein, “to seize upon, attack”), from ἐπί (epi, “upon”) + λαμβάνειν (lambanein, “to take”).
