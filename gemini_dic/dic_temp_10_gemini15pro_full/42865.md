courtesan: /ˈkɔːrtɪzæn/ (US), /ˈkɔːtɪzæn/ (UK)| n. |妓女（尤指高级妓女）|A prostitute, especially one with wealthy or upper-class clients.  A woman who had sexual relationships with rich and powerful men, often living a luxurious life as a result.|例句：The courtesan's beauty and charm captivated many noblemen.  这位妓女的美丽和魅力迷住了许多贵族。

例句：The king's favorite courtesan wielded considerable influence at court.  国王最喜欢的妓女在宫廷中拥有相当大的影响力。|近义词：prostitute, harlot, geisha (in some contexts)|反义词: (No direct antonym; consider chaste woman, nun)|词性:noun
