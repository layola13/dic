outpeopled:/ˌaʊtˈpiːpəld/
verb
超过…的人口数量;人口超过;比…人口多
to surpass in population; to exceed in the number of people inhabiting a place or area
The invaders outpeopled the native inhabitants and eventually took over the land. 入侵者的人口超过了当地居民，最终占领了这片土地。
The city was quickly outpeopled by the influx of refugees. 大量涌入的难民很快使这个城市的人口超过了负荷。
surpass, exceed, outnumber
be outnumbered by, be fewer than
v.
