diachronistically:/ˌdaɪəˌkrɑːnɪˈstɪkli/
adv.|历时地，跨越时间地|Considering or relating to the changes in something over time.|The evolution of language is studied diachronistically.  语言的演变是历时地研究的。|He analyzed the data diachronistically, tracing the changes over several decades. 他历时性地分析了这些数据，追溯了几十年来的变化。|Synonym: historically, over time|Antonym: synchronically|词性:副词
