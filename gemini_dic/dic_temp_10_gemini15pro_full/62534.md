enduringly:/ɪnˈdʊərɪŋli/ /ɪnˈdʒʊrɪŋli/| adv.|持久地，耐心地|In a manner that lasts for a long time; persistently.|Her name will enduringly be associated with the struggle for civil rights. 她的名字将永远与民权斗争联系在一起。|近义词:lastingly, permanently, persistently, continually|反义词:briefly, temporarily, fleetingly|词性:副词
词源: From endure (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Endure" comes from Old French endurer, from Latin indurare "to harden," from in- "in, into" + durare "to harden, last, endure."
