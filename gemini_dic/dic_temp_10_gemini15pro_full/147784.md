overpronunciation: /ˌoʊvərprəˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度发音；矫枉过正的发音 |  The act of pronouncing words too carefully or with exaggerated articulation, often to the point of sounding unnatural or affected.  |  His overpronunciation of every syllable made him sound pompous.  他每个音节都过度发音，让他听起来很浮夸。 |  overarticulation, exaggerated pronunciation | underpronunciation, mumbling | noun
