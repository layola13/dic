germanish:/dʒɜːrˈmænɪʃ/ | adj. | 德国式的；有点像德语的 | Resembling or characteristic of German, especially in language or culture; somewhat like German. |  The architecture had a germanish feel to it.  这建筑有点德国风格。 | Near synonyms: Teutonic, Germanic | Antonym: non-Germanic | adjective

germanish:/dʒɜːrˈmænɪʃ/ | n. |  德语方言；类似德语的语言 | A dialect of German or a language resembling German. | He spoke a strange germanish that I couldn't quite understand. 他说一种奇怪的类似德语的方言，我听不太懂。 | Near synonyms: German dialect | Antonym: N/A | noun
