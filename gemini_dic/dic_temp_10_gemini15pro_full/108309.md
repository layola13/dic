laurentide:/ˈlɔːrənˌtaɪd/| adj. n.|劳伦泰德的；劳伦泰德冰盖|Relating to the Laurentide Ice Sheet, a massive ice sheet that covered much of North America during the last glacial period.|The Laurentide Ice Sheet shaped the landscape of Canada. 劳伦泰德冰盖塑造了加拿大的地貌。|The laurentide region is known for its beautiful lakes. 劳伦泰德地区以其美丽的湖泊而闻名。|近义词:glacial, Pleistocene|反义词:interglacial|词性:adjective, noun

词源: From Laurentian (referring to the St. Lawrence River) + -ide (a suffix used to form adjectives and nouns).
