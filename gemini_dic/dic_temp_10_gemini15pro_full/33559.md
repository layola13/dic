cigarlike:/sɪˈɡɑːrlaɪk/| adj.|雪茄状的；像雪茄一样的|Resembling a cigar in shape; long and thin.|The aircraft had a cigarlike fuselage.  这架飞机有一个雪茄状的机身。|The spaceship was long and cigarlike. 宇宙飞船又长又像雪茄。|近义词：cigar-shaped, elongated, cylindrical|反义词:short, stubby|词性:形容词
词源: cigar + like.  "Cigar" comes from the Spanish "cigarro," ultimately from the Mayan "siyar." "Like" comes from Old English "gelic."
