normative:/ˈnɔːrmətɪv/| adj.|规范的，标准的，指示的|Relating to or deriving from a standard or norm, especially of behavior.|例句：Normative ethics is the study of ethical theories that prescribe how people ought to act. 规范伦理学是研究规定人们应该如何行动的伦理理论。|近义词：standard, prescriptive, regulative|反义词：descriptive, empirical|词性:形容词

normative:/ˈnɔːrmətɪv/| n.|规范，标准|A standard or norm, especially of behavior.|例句：Cultural norms are the normative expectations that guide behavior within a society. 文化规范是指在社会中指导行为的规范性期望。|近义词：standard, rule, precept|反义词：deviation, anomaly|词性:名词
