glyceride: /ˈɡlɪsərʌɪd/ | n. | 甘油酯；甘油三酸酯 | An ester formed from glycerol and one or more fatty acids. Glycerides are the principal constituents of natural fats and oils. | Vegetable oils are mostly composed of triglycerides.  植物油主要由甘油三酯组成。|  Triglycerides are a type of glyceride. 甘油三酯是一种甘油酯。 | lipid, fat |  | noun
