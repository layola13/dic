incomparabilities:/ɪnkəmˌpærəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |不可比性，无法比较|The incomparabilities of the two systems make a direct comparison difficult. 这两个系统的不可比性使得直接比较很困难。|例句：The incomparabilities between the two artists' styles make it hard to judge which is "better." 这两位艺术家的风格差异巨大，很难判断哪一个“更好”。

例句：The study highlighted the incomparabilities of the data sets, making it challenging to draw firm conclusions.  这项研究突出了数据集的不可比性，因此难以得出确切的结论。


|近义词：differences, disparities, dissimilarities|反义词：similarities, comparabilities|词性：名词|

incomparability:/ɪnkəmˌpærəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可比性，无法比较|The incomparability of the two situations makes it hard to draw a parallel. 这两种情况的不可比性使得很难进行类比。|例句：The sheer incomparability of her talent set her apart from her peers. 她无与伦比的才华使她与同龄人截然不同。

例句：He stressed the incomparability of the two legal systems, arguing that applying the same standards was inappropriate. 他强调了两种法律体系的不可比性，认为应用相同的标准是不合适的。|近义词：difference, disparity, dissimilarity|反义词：similarity, comparability|词性：名词|


词源：in- (not) + comparable + -ity (noun suffix)  来自拉丁语"comparabilis"，意思是"可比较的"。  "in-"前缀表示否定，"-ity"后缀构成名词，表示性质或状态。
