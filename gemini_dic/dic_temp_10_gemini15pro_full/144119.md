outscoring:/ˌaʊtˈskɔːrɪŋ/
verb
以超过...的比分战胜；得分超过
The act of scoring more points than an opponent.
The Lakers were outscoring the Warriors by 15 points in the first half.  湖人队在上半场比勇士队多得了15分。
Our team is consistently outscoring their opponents. 我们队一直比对手得分多。
synonyms: outpointing, defeating, surpassing, triumphing over
antonyms: being outscored by, losing to, trailing
