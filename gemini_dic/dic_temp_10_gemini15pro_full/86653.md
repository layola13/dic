helleborism:/ˈhɛlɪbɔːrɪzəm/
n.
嚏根草疗法；用嚏根草治疗精神错乱
The practice of treating mental disorders with hellebore.
Helleborism was a common practice in ancient Greece.  古希腊时期，嚏根草疗法是一种常见的做法。
The efficacy of helleborism in treating mental illness is highly questionable.  嚏根草疗法治疗精神疾病的疗效非常值得怀疑。
近义词: herbalism, phytotherapy
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider modern psychiatric medication)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Helleborus + -ism.  Hellebore (嚏根草) is from Greek ἑλλέβορος (helléboros).  The -ism suffix denotes a practice, doctrine, or system.
