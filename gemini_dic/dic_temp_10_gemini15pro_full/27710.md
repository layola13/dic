candylike:/ˈkændiˌlaɪk/| adj.|像糖果的，甜美的|Resembling candy in sweetness, appearance, or texture. | The cake had a candylike sweetness that was irresistible.  这蛋糕有着一种无法抗拒的像糖果一样的甜味。| The frosting was smooth and candylike. 糖霜光滑得像糖果一样。 |近义词：sweet, sugary, confectionary|反义词：sour, bitter, savory|词性:形容词

词源:  candy (n.) + -like (suffix).  "Candy" comes from the Arabic "qandi," meaning candied sugar.  "-like" is a common suffix forming adjectives meaning "similar to" or "characteristic of."
