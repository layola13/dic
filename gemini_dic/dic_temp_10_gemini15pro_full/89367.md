homonymity:/ˌhɒməˈnɪmɪti/ | n. |同音异义, 同形异义|The state or quality of being a homonym.  The fact that two or more words have the same spelling or pronunciation but different meanings.|The homonymity of "bat" (animal) and "bat" (sports equipment) can sometimes cause confusion.|近义词：homonymy|反义词：(No direct antonym, possibly polysemy - multiple meanings within the same word) |词性: noun
