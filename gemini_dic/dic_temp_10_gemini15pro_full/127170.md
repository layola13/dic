naturalistic:/ˌnætʃrəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
自然的；自然主义的；写实主义的
Representing something as it appears in nature; resembling something natural.
例句：The painting was done in a naturalistic style, capturing the beauty of the landscape. 这幅画是以自然主义的风格完成的，捕捉到了风景的美丽。

例句：His acting was so naturalistic that the audience felt they were watching a real event unfold. 他的表演非常自然，以至于观众感觉他们正在观看一个真实的事件的发生。


近义词：realistic, lifelike, true-to-life, representational
反义词：abstract, idealized, stylized
词性: adjective
