inefficaciously:/ˌɪnɪfɪˈkeɪʃəsli/
adverb
无效地；无用地
in a manner that does not produce the desired effect.
The medicine was administered inefficaciously, and the patient's condition worsened.  这药用得无效，病人的病情恶化了。
The plan was implemented inefficaciously, resulting in a loss of resources.  这个计划执行得很低效，导致了资源的浪费。
Synonyms: fruitlessly, ineffectively, uselessly, vainly, futiley
Antonyms: effectively, successfully, efficiently, productively
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + efficacious (effective) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Efficacious" comes from Latin "efficax," from "efficere" (to effect, bring about), from "ex-" (out) + "facere" (to make, do).
