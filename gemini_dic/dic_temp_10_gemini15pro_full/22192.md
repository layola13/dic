bombproofed:/ˈbɒmˌpruːft/ (UK) /ˈbɑːmˌpruːft/ (US)| adj.|防弹的，不受炸弹破坏的， 坚不可摧的|Made resistant to bombs; shielded against the effects of bombs.  Figuratively, impervious to criticism or failure.|The bunker was bombproofed to withstand a direct hit.  这个地堡被加固以抵御直接的炸弹袭击。|Synonyms: bomb-resistant, reinforced, impregnable, invulnerable, unshakeable|Antonyms: vulnerable, fragile, weak, exposed|adjective
