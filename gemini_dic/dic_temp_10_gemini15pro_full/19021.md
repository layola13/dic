bet-your-boots:/ˌbet jər ˈbuːts/
idiom
确信无疑，毫无疑问
Be absolutely certain; have no doubt.
You can bet your boots she'll be late. 你可以肯定她会迟到。
近义词:be sure, be certain, definitely, without a doubt
反义词:doubt, be uncertain
词性:idiom

phrase

近义词:be positive, be convinced, for sure
