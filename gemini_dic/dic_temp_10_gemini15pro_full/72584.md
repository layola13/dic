fluxionist:/ˈflʌkʃənɪst/| n. |流数学家；微积分学家|A mathematician who uses or follows the principles of fluxions (an early form of calculus).  A fluxionist employs the concept of infinitesimals and flowing quantities to analyze change and motion.| Newton and Leibniz were considered prominent fluxionists/pioneers of calculus. 牛顿和莱布尼茨被认为是杰出的流数学家/微积分学的先驱。|The fluxionist calculated the rate of change of the curve.  这位流数学家计算了曲线的变化率。|Synonyms:  mathematician, analyst,  calculator |Antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a profession)|Noun
