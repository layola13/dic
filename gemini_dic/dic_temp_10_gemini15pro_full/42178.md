could /kʊd/ | modal verb | 可以，能够，可能 | Used to indicate possibility, ability in the past, permission, polite requests, and suggestions. | I could swim when I was five. 我五岁时就会游泳了。 | might, may, be able to | couldn't, wasn't able to | modal verb
