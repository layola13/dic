blighter:/ˈblaɪtər/| n.|家伙，小子，讨厌鬼|A person, especially a man, who is regarded with a mixture of affection and mild disapproval.  Someone slightly annoying or troublesome.|That blighter parked right in front of my driveway!  那个家伙把车正好停在我家车道前面！|近义词：chap, fellow, rascal, rogue|反义词: (No direct opposite, depends on the nuanced meaning used) |noun
