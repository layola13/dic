multiplet:/ˈmʌltɪplɪt/| n.|多重态; 多重线; 多胞胎|A set of closely spaced lines in a spectrum, arising from transitions between energy levels that are split by a magnetic field or other interaction.|The spectrum showed a characteristic multiplet, indicating the presence of the element.|近义词：group, cluster, set, series|反义词: singlet|noun
