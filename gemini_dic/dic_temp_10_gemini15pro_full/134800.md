nonmicroscopically:/ˌnɒnˌmaɪkrəˈskɒpɪkli/ | adv. | 非显微镜地，肉眼可见地 | Not visible only with a microscope; visible to the naked eye. | The crystals were nonmicroscopically large, easily seen without magnification.  这些晶体很大，肉眼可见，不需要放大。| The damage was nonmicroscopically apparent.  损坏是肉眼可见的。 | Synonyms: macroscopically, visibly, discernibly | Antonyms: microscopically, invisibly, imperceptibly | adverb

Etymology:
non- (prefix) + microscopically

* non-: from Latin *non*, meaning "not"
* microscopically: from microscope + -ically
    * microscope: from Greek *mikros* (small) + *skopein* (to look at)
    * -ically: adverb-forming suffix
