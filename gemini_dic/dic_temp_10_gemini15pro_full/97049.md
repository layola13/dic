infeoff:/ɪnˈfiːf/ | vt. | 封地；授予…封地 | To grant a fief to; to invest with a feudal estate. |  The king infeoffed his loyal knight with a large estate. 国王将一大片地产封给了他忠诚的骑士。

The lord infeoffed his vassal with lands in exchange for military service. 领主将土地封给他的封臣，以换取军事服务。| 近义词:enfeoff, invest | 反义词: dispossess, divest | 词性: verb
