inflexibility:/ɪnˌfleksɪˈbɪləti/| n. |不灵活，僵化，顽固|The inflexibility of the company's policy made it difficult to adapt to changing market conditions. 该公司政策的不灵活性使其难以适应不断变化的市场环境。|例句：His inflexibility in negotiations caused the deal to fall apart.  他在谈判中的僵化态度导致交易破裂。

例句：The inflexibility of the rules frustrated the students. 规则的僵化让学生们感到沮丧。


|近义词：rigidity, stiffness, stubbornness|反义词: flexibility, adaptability, pliability|词性:名词
