artinesses:/ˈɑːrtinɪsɪz/
noun
做作；装腔作势
The affected or pretentious mannerisms, behavior, or style of a person or thing that is trying too hard to appear artistic or sophisticated.
Her artinesses were evident in her flamboyant clothing and overly dramatic pronouncements about art. 她穿着华丽的衣服，对艺术发表过于夸张的言论，她的做作显而易见。
affectations, pretensions, posturing
