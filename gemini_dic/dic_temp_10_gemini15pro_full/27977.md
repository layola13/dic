canulated:/ˈkænjʊleɪtɪd/
adjective
插管的，套管的
Having a cannula inserted, or furnished with a cannula.
The surgeon canulated the vein to administer fluids.  外科医生给静脉插管以输送液体。
The patient's artery was canulated for blood pressure monitoring. 病人的动脉被插管以监测血压。
近义词: intubated, catheterized
反义词:  None (Lack of cannulation is implied by its absence, not a specific antonym)
词性: adjective
