cocultivated: /ˌkoʊˈkʌltɪveɪtɪd/
| adj. | 共同培养的 | Grown or cultivated together. |  The cocultivated cells showed increased growth compared to the control group. 共同培养的细胞与对照组相比显示出更高的生长速度。|  Cocultivated bacteria produced a novel antibiotic. 共同培养的细菌产生了一种新的抗生素。 |  co-cultured, jointly cultivated | separately cultivated | adjective
