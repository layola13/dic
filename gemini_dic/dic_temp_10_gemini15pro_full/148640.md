overthrew: /ˌōvərˈθruː/
verb
推翻，颠覆
removed forcibly from power
The rebels overthrew the government. 叛军推翻了政府。

overthrew: /ˌōvərˈθruː/
past tense of overthrow
推翻的过去式
This verb indicates a past action of removing someone or something from power.
The dictator was overthrown in a coup d'état.  独裁者在政变中被推翻了。

overthrew: /ˌōvərˈθruː/
past tense of overthrow
推翻的过去式
This form is used when talking about a completed action in the past.
The army overthrew the monarchy centuries ago.  几个世纪以前，军队推翻了君主制。


近义词: toppled, deposed, unseated, overturned
反义词: established, instated, supported, upheld
词性: verb
