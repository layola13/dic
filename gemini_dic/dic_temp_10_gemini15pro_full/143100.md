outbringing:/ˈaʊtbrɪŋɪŋ/
noun
教养；出身；抚育；培养
The way in which a child is raised and educated, and the effects this has on their character and development.
例句：Her privileged upbringing gave her an advantage in life. 她的优越的教养给了她生活中的优势。

例句：He had a strict upbringing. 他有一个严格的教养。

近义词：upbringing, raising, rearing, education, background
反义词: neglect

词性: noun
