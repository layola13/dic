nooks:/nʊks/ | n. | 角落，隐蔽处 | A quiet, secluded, or hidden place. |  The cat loves to curl up in quiet nooks around the house. 猫喜欢蜷缩在房子周围安静的角落里。 | synonyms: corners, recesses, crannies, niches | antonyms: open areas, exposures | noun

nooks:/nʊks/ | n. | (尤指)偏僻的地方 | a remote or secluded place |  They explored the hidden nooks and crannies of the old city. 他们探索了这座老城的隐蔽角落。 | synonyms: backwaters, retreats | antonyms:  centers, hubs | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *nok, from Old English *nōc "corner, angle, projection," of uncertain origin. Related to Dutch noek, nook "corner, point," and possibly to Old Norse hnakki "nape of the neck."
