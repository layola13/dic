orectic:/ɔːˈrɛktɪk/
adjective
食欲的；促进食欲的
Relating to appetite, or increasing the appetite.
This medication has an orectic effect, stimulating hunger in patients undergoing chemotherapy. 这种药物有促进食欲的作用，能刺激正在接受化疗的病人产生饥饿感。
Synonyms:appetite-stimulating, orexigenic
Antonyms:anorectic, appetite-suppressing
词性:adjective
