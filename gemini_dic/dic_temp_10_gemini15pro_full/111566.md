lobelias: /loʊˈbiːliəz/ /ləˈbiːliəz/ | n. |半边莲（属植物）; 山梗菜（属植物）|A genus of flowering plants comprising annuals, perennials, and shrubs, several species of which are cultivated as ornamentals.  Many species have brightly colored, usually blue, purple, or red, two-lipped flowers. | The lobelias in her garden were a vibrant shade of blue. 她花园里的半边莲是鲜艳的蓝色。|  The nursery stocked a wide variety of lobelias.  这家苗圃培育了各种各样的半边莲。 |Synonyms: cardinal flower,  Indian tobacco | Antonyms: N/A | noun
