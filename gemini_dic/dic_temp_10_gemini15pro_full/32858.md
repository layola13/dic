choliambic:/ˌkəʊliˈæmbɪk/
adjective
跛行的，跛行的诗句的
A choliambic verse is a scazon, a metrical foot in poetry. It has a lame or limping quality due to a spondee or trochee replacing an iamb in the final foot.
The poet experimented with choliambic meter in his latest work.  这位诗人在他的最新作品中尝试了跛行诗的韵律。
choliambic:/ˌkəʊliˈæmbɪk/
noun
跛行诗
Choliambic refers to a type of verse in poetry that has a limping or irregular rhythm.
He wrote a choliambic about his difficult journey. 他写了一首跛行诗，讲述他艰难的旅程。
近义词:scazon, lame
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'regular' in terms of meter)
