hemihedrism:/ˌhɛmiˈhiːdrɪzəm/
|n.|半面像;半面晶体;偏形|The development of only half the number of faces required by the full symmetry of a crystal system.  A crystal form exhibiting hemihedrism.|The quartz crystal exhibits hemihedrism.  石英晶体呈现半面像。|Near synonyms: enantiomorphism, chirality|Antonym: holohedrism|noun
