galactophagist:/ɡəˌlæktəˈfeɪdʒɪst/
noun
食乳者；嗜乳者
A person who drinks milk.  Specifically, one who subsists on a diet of milk.
He is a self-proclaimed galactophagist, consuming vast quantities of milk daily. 他自称是嗜乳者，每天喝大量的牛奶。
galactophile, milk drinker
n/a
