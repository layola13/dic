hero-worshipped:/ˈhɪəroʊ ˈwɜːrʃɪpt/| adj.|被奉若神明的；偶像化的|Excessively admiring someone; treating someone as a hero.|例句：The young boy hero-worshipped the famous athlete.  这个小男孩把那位著名的运动员奉若神明。|近义词：idolized, lionized, glorified|反义词：vilified, scorned, denigrated|词性:形容词

词源: hero + worshipped (past participle of worship)
"hero" from Greek "hērōs" (demi-god, warrior)
"worship" from Old English "weorðscipe" (worthiness, respect)
