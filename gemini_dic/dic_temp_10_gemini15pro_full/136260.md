nonrepellent:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈpɛlənt/ | adj. | 不驱避的，不排斥的 | Not repelling or driving away, especially of insecticides that do not deter insects from contacting treated surfaces.  | The researchers used a nonrepellent insecticide to control the termite infestation. 研究人员使用了一种非驱避性杀虫剂来控制白蚁的侵扰。 |  The new mosquito control uses a nonrepellent spray. 新的蚊子控制方法使用了一种非驱避性喷雾。 | Synonyms: nondeterrent, unobtrusive | Antonyms: repellent, deterring | adjective
