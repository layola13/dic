deserver's:/dɪˈzɜːrvərz/ | n. |应得之物；功劳；奖赏|Something that is deserved; merit; reward.  This word is likely a non-standard or informal possessive form of "deserver," meaning "one who deserves something."  It's more common and grammatically correct to express this idea using "of a/the deserver" or "belonging to a/the deserver."|The deserver's reward was great. 应得者的奖励很丰厚。|Near Synonyms: due, right, reward|Antonyms:  penalty, punishment|Noun
