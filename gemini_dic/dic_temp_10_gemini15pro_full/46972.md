dartos: /ˈdɑːrtɒs/ (UK), /ˈdɑːrtoʊs/ (US)| n. | 肉膜 | A thin layer of smooth muscle found beneath the skin of the scrotum. It is responsible for wrinkling the scrotal skin. | The dartos muscle contracts in cold temperatures to reduce heat loss.  肉膜肌在寒冷的温度下收缩以减少热量损失。|  The dartos fascia is a continuation of Scarpa's fascia in the abdomen. 肉膜筋膜是腹部斯卡帕筋膜的延续。| synonyms: dartos tunic, dartos fascia | antonyms: N/A | noun
