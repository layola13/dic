ballisticses:/bəˈlɪstɪksɪz/
n.
弹道学(的多个分支或应用)
The study of the motion of projectiles, especially bullets, bombs, rockets, or the like; the science or art of designing projectiles for optimum flight characteristics.  Specifically used in the plural to refer to multiple branches or applications of ballistics.
The police used ballisticses to match the bullet to the murder weapon. 警方运用多种弹道学技术来匹配子弹和凶器。
Near Synonyms: ballistics, trajectories
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a field of study.  One might consider words related to stillness or lack of motion, but these are not true opposites.)
词性: 名词
