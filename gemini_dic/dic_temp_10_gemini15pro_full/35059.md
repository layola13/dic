closeout: /ˈkloʊzaʊt/ (US), /ˈkləʊzaʊt/ (UK) | n. |清仓甩卖；停业；结算；（尤指广播或电视节目的）结束；（尤指体育比赛的）最后阶段 | A sale of goods at reduced prices to clear the remaining stock. The act of closing something out, especially a business or project. | The store had a closeout sale before going out of business. 这家商店在停业前进行了清仓大甩卖。 |  synonyms: clearance sale, liquidation sale | antonyms: grand opening, commencement | noun

closeout: /ˈkloʊzaʊt/ (US), /ˈkləʊzaʊt/ (UK) | adj. |清仓的；停业前的 | Relating to a sale of goods at reduced prices to clear the remaining stock. | They were selling closeout merchandise at deeply discounted prices. 他们在以非常低的折扣价出售清仓商品。| synonyms: clearance, liquidation | antonyms: regular price, full price | adjective
