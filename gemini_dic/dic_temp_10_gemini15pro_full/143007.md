outbellowed:/ˌaʊtˈbɛloʊd/
verb
大声吼叫，胜过…的吼叫声
to shout louder than someone or something else
The sergeant outbellowed the recruits. 中士的声音盖过了新兵们。
Synonyms: outshout, drown out, overpower
Antonyms: whisper, murmur
词性: 动词
