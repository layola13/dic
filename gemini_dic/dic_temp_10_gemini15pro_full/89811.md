hornbill:/ˈhɔːnbɪl/ (US), /ˈhɔːnbɪl/ (UK)| n. |犀鸟|A tropical bird with a large, often brightly colored beak, typically with a casque on the upper mandible.|例句：The hornbill perched high in the rainforest canopy. 犀鸟栖息在雨林树冠的高处。

例句：We saw a great hornbill during our safari. 我们在狩猎旅行中看到了一只大犀鸟。

近义词：buceros (technical term)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
