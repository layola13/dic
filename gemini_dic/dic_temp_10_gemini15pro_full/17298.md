bcc'd:/biː siː siːd/ | v. | 密送；暗送 | past participle of "bcc," meaning someone has been sent a blind carbon copy of an email or letter.  It means they received a copy, but the other recipients are unaware. | I was bcc'd on the email to the client. 我被密送了给客户的电子邮件。| Synonym: blind copied | Antonym: cc'd (carbon copied) | verb (past participle)

bcc'd:/biː siː siːd/ | adj. | 被密送的 |  Used adjectivally to describe someone who has received a blind carbon copy. | The bcc'd recipients were kept informed of the situation. 被密送的收件人一直被告知情况。 | Synonym: blind copied | Antonym: cc'd (carbon copied) | adjective
