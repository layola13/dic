glassed:/ɡlæst/ | v. | (glass的过去式和过去分词)给…装玻璃；用玻璃覆盖；(非正式)把(饮料)一饮而尽 |  (past tense and past participle of glass) to fit with glass; to cover with glass; (informal) to drink (a beverage) quickly |  They glassed the porch to create a sunroom. 他们在门廊上装了玻璃，建成了一个日光浴室。| 近义词: glazed, enclosed, chugged, downed (for drink quickly) | 反义词: (for drink quickly) sip | 词性:动词
