disarmed:/dɪsˈɑːrmd/
adjective
解除武装的；消除了敌意的；被化解了敌意的
deprived of weapons; no longer hostile or suspicious; charmed and no longer hostile
The soldiers were disarmed and sent home. 士兵们被解除武装后送回家。
He was completely disarmed by her smile. 她的笑容彻底消除了他的敌意。

近义词: defenseless, unarmed, vulnerable, powerless, harmless, appeased, conciliated, placated
反义词: armed, hostile, aggressive, suspicious
