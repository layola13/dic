obey:/əˈbeɪ/ /oʊˈbeɪ/
verb
服从，遵守
to comply with or follow the orders, instructions, or requests of (someone or something)
The soldiers obeyed the general's orders.  士兵们服从了将军的命令。
The children obeyed their parents. 孩子们服从了他们的父母。
近义词：comply, follow, adhere to, submit to
反义词：disobey, defy, resist, violate
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *obeir, from Latin oboedīre (“to listen to, obey, be subject to”), from ob- (“to, toward”) + audīre (“to hear, listen”).
