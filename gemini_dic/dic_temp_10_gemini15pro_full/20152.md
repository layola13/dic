biplanar:/baɪˈpleɪnər/
adjective
双平面的; located in two planes
Relating to or situated in two planes.
The biplanar X-ray imaging technique provides two different views of the bone.  双平面X射线成像技术提供了骨骼的两个不同视图。
The biplanar fracture required a complex surgical procedure.  双平面骨折需要复杂的外科手术程序。
近义词:diplanar
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "single-plane")
词性: adjective
