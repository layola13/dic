eschewers:/ɪˈʃuːərz/
| n. |回避者，躲避者|People who deliberately avoid something, especially something considered wrong or harmful.| Those who eschew violence often prefer peaceful negotiation. 那些回避暴力的人通常更喜欢和平谈判。|近义词：avoiders, abstainers, shunners|反义词：embracers, seekers, indulgers| noun (plural)

eschew:/ɪˈʃuː/
| vt. | 避免，避开，回避|To deliberately avoid or abstain from something, especially something considered wrong or harmful.| He eschews all forms of gambling. 他避免任何形式的赌博。|近义词：avoid, abstain from, shun, forgo|反义词：embrace, seek, indulge| verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *eschuir* "to avoid, shun," from Frankish *skiuhan*, from Proto-Germanic *skeukhanan* "to shy away from, avoid."  Related to English "shy."
