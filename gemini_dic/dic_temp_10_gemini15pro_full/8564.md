anticipatingly:/ænˈtɪsɪpeɪtɪŋli/
adverb
期待地，预料地
In a manner that shows eager expectation or prediction of something.
She looked at the birthday cake anticipatingly. 她期待地看着生日蛋糕。
He waited anticipatingly for the announcement of the winner. 他期待地等待着获胜者的宣布。
近义词：expectantly, hopefully, eagerly
反义词：apathetically, indifferently
词性：adverb

词源:
From anticipate + -ing + -ly.  "Anticipate" comes from Latin anticipatus, the past participle of anticipare "to take before, to anticipate," from ante- "before" + capere "to take."
