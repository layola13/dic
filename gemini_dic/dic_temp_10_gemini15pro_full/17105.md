batta:/ˈbætə/ | n. | 津贴，额外补贴; (尤指印度) 每日生活津贴 | An allowance or per diem, especially in India, given to cover daily expenses. | Example: He received a generous batta for his overseas assignment. 他因海外任务获得了丰厚的津贴。

Example:  The company provides a daily batta to cover meals and transportation.  公司提供每日津贴以支付膳食和交通费用。

近义词: allowance, per diem, stipend | 反义词: deduction | 词性: 名词


词源:  From Hindi baṭṭā  meaning "discount," "commission," or "share," ultimately from Sanskrit bhaṭṭa  meaning "soldier," "warrior," from which the meaning shifted to "soldier's pay" and then to "allowance."
