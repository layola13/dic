coldplay:/ˈkoʊldpleɪ/ (US) /ˈkəʊldpleɪ/ (UK)| n.|酷玩乐队|A British rock band known for songs like "Yellow," "Clocks," and "Viva la Vida."| Coldplay's music is characterized by its anthemic melodies and introspective lyrics.|近义词: band, group, musicians |反义词: N/A| noun

Etymology: The name "Coldplay" was suggested by a friend who felt the original name "Starfish" was too depressing.  The word "coldplay" itself is thought to have originated from a collection of poems titled "Child's Reflections, Cold Play" by Philip Horky.
