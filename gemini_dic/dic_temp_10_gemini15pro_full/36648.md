colleens:/kəˈliːnz/ | n. |少女们，姑娘们 (plural) |A group of young women, especially considered as a group.  It's an informal and often affectionate or slightly patronizing term, primarily used in Irish English.| The colleens were all dressed up for the festival. 姑娘们都盛装打扮去参加节日庆典。 | 近义词: girls, lasses, maidens | 反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps) lads, boys | 词性: noun
