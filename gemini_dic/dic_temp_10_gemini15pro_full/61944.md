emulsifiabilities:/ɪˌmʌlsɪfaɪəˈbɪlətiz/| n.|乳化性，乳化能力|The emulsifiabilities of different oils vary greatly. 不同油的乳化能力差异很大。|例句：The emulsifiabilities of these surfactants were evaluated in various oil-in-water systems.  这些表面活性剂的乳化性在各种水包油体系中得到了评估。

例句：The study investigated the emulsifiabilities of several novel food additives. 这项研究调查了几种新型食品添加剂的乳化性。|近义词：emulsifying properties, emulsification capacities|反义词: immiscibility, non-emulsifying properties|词性:noun
