hammocklike:/ˈhæməkˌlaɪk/| adj.|像吊床一样的|Resembling a hammock in shape, appearance, or function.  Meaning something is suspended, swinging, or relaxed like a hammock.|The curtains draped in a hammocklike fashion across the window. 窗帘像吊床一样挂在窗户上。|The rope bridge swayed in a hammocklike manner. 绳桥像吊床一样摇摆。|近义词：suspended, swinging, hanging, relaxed|反义词: taut, rigid, stiff|词性: adjective

Etymology:
* hammock (n.): from Spanish hamaca, from Taino (Arawakan language) hamaka
* -like (suffix): from Old English -līc, meaning "having the form or appearance of"
