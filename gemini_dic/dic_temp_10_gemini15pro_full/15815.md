balkish:/ˈbɔːlkɪʃ/| adj.|略显笨拙的，略显粗陋的|Somewhat clumsy or unwieldy; slightly awkward.  Relating to or resembling a balk. |The balkish teenager tripped over his own feet.  这个略显笨拙的少年被自己的脚绊倒了。|Near synonyms: clumsy, awkward, ungainly, gawky|Near antonyms: graceful, elegant, agile, deft|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From balk (an obstacle or hindrance) + -ish (suffix forming adjectives).  The original sense related to obstacles, but the meaning shifted towards clumsiness or awkwardness.
