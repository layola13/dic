indolently:/ˈɪndələntli/| adv.|懒惰地，悠闲地|In a habitually lazy way; idly.|She stretched indolently on the sofa. 她懒洋洋地躺在沙发上。|Synonyms: lazily, idly, sluggishly, lethargically|Antonyms: energetically, actively, diligently|adverb

Etymology: From Late Latin *indolentia, from Latin in- "not" + dolēre "to feel pain," thus "freedom from pain or trouble," which led to the sense of "ease" and then "laziness."
