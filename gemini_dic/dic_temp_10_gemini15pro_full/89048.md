hologram:/ˈhɒləɡræm/ (UK), /ˈhoʊləɡræm/ (US)| n.|全息图，全息影像|A three-dimensional image formed by the interference of light beams from a laser or other coherent light source.|

例句：
The museum displayed a hologram of a dinosaur.
博物馆展出了一只恐龙的全息图。

The concert featured a hologram of the deceased singer.
这场音乐会以已故歌手的全息影像为特色。


近义词：3D image, three-dimensional image|
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "2D image" or "photograph" in a comparative context) |
词性: noun
