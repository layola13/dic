nonascendence:/ˌnɒnəˈsɛndəns/
n.|非超越性；非优越性|The state or quality of not transcending or surpassing ordinary limits or powers; lack of ascendancy or dominance.|His nonascendence in the field was evident in his lack of publications.|近义词：subordination, inferiority, mediocrity|反义词：ascendancy, dominance, superiority|词性：名词

Etymology:  "non-" (not) + "ascendence" (the act of rising or climbing up; domination or control).  "Ascendence" comes from Late Latin "ascendere" (to climb), from "ad-" (to) + "scandere" (to climb).
