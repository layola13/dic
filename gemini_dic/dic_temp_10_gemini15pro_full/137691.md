nonurgent:/nɒnˈɜːdʒənt/ (US) /nɑːnˈɜːrdʒənt/| adj.|非紧急的，不紧急的|Not urgent; not requiring immediate action or attention.|The email was marked as nonurgent, so I decided to deal with it later. 这封邮件被标记为非紧急，所以我决定晚点再处理。|The doctor assured me that the situation was nonurgent and could wait until morning. 医生向我保证情况并不紧急，可以等到早上。|近义词：non-critical, routine, low-priority|反义词:urgent, critical, pressing|词性:形容词

词源:
From non- + urgent.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Urgent" comes from the Latin word "urgēns," present participle of "urgēre" meaning "to press, urge, drive."
