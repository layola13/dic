habituated:/həˈbɪtʃueɪtɪd/
adj.
习惯的，适应的
Made used to something, especially something unpleasant.
例句：
The horses had become habituated to the sound of gunfire.
这些马已经习惯了枪声。

He was habituated to a life of luxury.
他习惯了奢华的生活。

近义词：accustomed, used to, inured
反义词：unaccustomed, unused to
词性: adjective
词源：From Latin *habituatus*, past participle of habituare (“to accustom, frequent”), from habitus (“condition, appearance, dress, character”).
