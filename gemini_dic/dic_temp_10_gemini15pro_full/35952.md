code-named:/ˌkoʊdˈneɪmd/ | adj. | 代号为…的；以密码命名的 | Given a code name, especially for secrecy. | The code-named operation was a success.  代号行动很成功。 |  The code-named project "Phoenix" is underway.  代号为“凤凰”的项目正在进行中。| synonyms: classified, secret, confidential | antonyms: public, open, revealed | adjective

Etymology:  "code" (from French, ultimately from Latin "codex") + "named" (past participle of "name," from Old English "nama").  Essentially, it means "given a name that is a code."
