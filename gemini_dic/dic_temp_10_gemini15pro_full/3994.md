ETYMOLOGY->

From Old English *hwæt (neuter singular nominative and accusative of hwā "who," "what"), from Proto-Germanic *hwat, from Proto-Indo-European *kwod. Cognate with Old Saxon hwat, Old Frisian hwet, Old Norse hvat, Dutch wat, German was, Gothic hva, Latin quod.  Relates to the question word "who."
