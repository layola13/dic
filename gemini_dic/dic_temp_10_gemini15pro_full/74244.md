foxes:/ˈfɒksɪz/ (UK) /ˈfɑːksɪz/ (US)| n. |狐狸（复数）|The plural form of "fox," referring to more than one fox.  A fox is a small to medium-sized, omnivorous mammal belonging to several genera of the family Canidae. Foxes are known for their slender bodies, bushy tails, pointed ears, and sharp muzzles.|例句：The foxes were hunting for rabbits in the field. 狐狸们正在田野里猎捕兔子。

|近义词：vulpes (formal, scientific name)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
