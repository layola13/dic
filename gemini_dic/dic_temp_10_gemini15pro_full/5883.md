ambergris: /ˈæmbərɡriːs/ (US) /ˈæmbəɡriːs/ (UK)| n.|龙涎香|A waxy substance produced in the intestines of sperm whales, used in perfumery.|The perfumer used a touch of ambergris to give the scent a warm, musky note.|近义词: musk, civet|反义词: None|Noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *ambre gris* and Anglo-Norman *ambre gris*, from Old French *ambre gris* (“grey amber”),  from Arabic *عنبر* (*ʿanbar*) + Old French *gris* (“grey”).  The Arabic term ultimately derives from Persian *عنبر* (*ambar*, literally “amber”).
