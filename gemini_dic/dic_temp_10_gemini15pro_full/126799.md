nanotechnology:/ˈnænoʊtɛkˌnɑːlədʒi/| n. |纳米技术|The branch of technology that deals with dimensions and tolerances of less than 100 nanometers, especially the manipulation of individual atoms and molecules.|Scientists are exploring the potential of nanotechnology in medicine. 科学家们正在探索纳米技术在医学领域的潜力。|近义词:nanoscience|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "macroscale technology")|词性:noun
