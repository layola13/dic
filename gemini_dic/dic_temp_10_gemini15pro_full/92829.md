iconology:/ˌaɪkəˈnɒlədʒi/ | n. |图像学，图像解释学 | The study of the meaning of visual images, especially in art, often with emphasis on their cultural and historical significance.  | Example: The iconology of the painting reveals a complex interplay of religious and political themes. 这幅画的图像学揭示了宗教和政治主题的复杂相互作用。

| Example:  Her dissertation focused on the iconology of Renaissance portraiture. 她的论文集中于文艺复兴时期肖像画的图像学。 | Synonym: iconography, symbolism, semiotics | Antonym:  N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *iconologie*, from Greek *eikon* (“image”) + *-logia* (“study of”).
