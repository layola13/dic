non-connotative:/ˌnɒn kɒnˈɒtətɪv/ | adj. | 非内涵的，不带感情色彩的 |  Lacking connotative meaning; denoting or relating to a meaning that is literal and explicit, as opposed to one that is evocative or suggestive. | The word "table" is non-connotative; it simply refers to a piece of furniture. “桌子”这个词是非内涵的；它仅仅指一件家具。|  denotative, literal | connotative, suggestive | adjective
