incorporator:/ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪtər/| n. |公司创办人；注册成立者|A person or group of people who establish a corporation.  A person or entity that applies for and receives a corporate charter from a government.|The incorporators filed the necessary paperwork with the state.  公司创办人向州政府提交了必要的文件。|Synonym: founder, organizer|Antonym: N/A|noun

incorporators:/ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪtərz/| n. |公司创办人；注册成立者（复数）|Plural form of incorporator.  Refers to multiple people or entities involved in establishing a corporation.|The incorporators met to discuss the company's bylaws. 公司创办人们开会讨论公司的规章制度。|Synonym: founders, organizers|Antonym: N/A|noun
