anglerphish:/ˈæŋɡlərˌfɪʃ/ | n. | 鮟鱇鱼 | A type of deep-sea fish characterized by bioluminescent lures used to attract prey.  These fish are known for their unusual mating habits where the smaller male fuses permanently with the larger female. |  The anglerfish uses its bioluminescent lure to attract smaller fish. 鮟鱇鱼用它发光的诱饵来吸引小鱼。 | synonyms: deep-sea angler, sea devil | antonyms: N/A | noun
