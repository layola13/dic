anti-egoism:/ˌæntiˈiːɡəʊɪzəm/ | n. | 反利己主义; 反自我主义 | The philosophy of anti-egoism prioritizes the well-being of others over one's own self-interest. 反利己主义哲学将他人福祉置于自身利益之上。| Example 1:  Her acts of charity are a clear demonstration of her anti-egoism. 她的慈善行为清楚地表明了她的反利己主义。

Example 2: True anti-egoism requires a deep understanding of interconnectedness. 真正的反利己主义需要对相互联系有深刻的理解。| 近义词: altruism, selflessness | 反义词: egoism, selfishness | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- "against" + egoism.  "Anti-" comes from Greek *anti (ἀντί) "against, opposite of, in place of, instead." "Egoism" comes from Latin ego "I"  + "-ism," a suffix forming abstract nouns of action, state, or condition.
