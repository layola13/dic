colourway:/ˈkʌləweɪ/| n.|配色方案；色彩组合；|A particular combination of colours used in the design or manufacture of something, especially clothing, textiles, or cars.|The new sofa comes in a variety of colourways.|近义词：color scheme, palette, combination of colors|反义词: (No direct antonym, but one could consider "monochrome" if referring to a single color)|noun
