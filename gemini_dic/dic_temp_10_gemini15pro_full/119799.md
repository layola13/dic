migratorial:/maɪˈɡreɪtɔːriəl/
adjective
迁徙的；洄游的
Relating to migration; habitually migrating.
例句：Migratorial birds fly south for the winter. 候鸟冬天飞往南方。

例句：Salmon are migratory fish, returning to their birthplace to spawn. 鲑鱼是洄游鱼类，它们会回到出生地产卵。

近义词：migrant, nomadic, wandering
反义词：resident, settled, stationary
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *migratorius, from Latin migrat-, past participle stem of migrare "to migrate."  The ending -ory/-orial forms adjectives meaning "relating to, characterized by."
