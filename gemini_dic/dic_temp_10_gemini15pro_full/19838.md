binational:/baɪˈnæʃənəl/
adjective
双国的；两国的；涉及两国的
Relating to or involving two nations.
例句：The binational agreement fostered trade between the two countries.  双边协议促进了两国之间的贸易。

例句：She holds binational citizenship, both American and Canadian. 她拥有双重国籍，既是美国人也是加拿大人。

近义词：international, bilateral
反义词：unilateral, domestic
词性：adjective
