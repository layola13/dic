lienculus: /liˈɛŋkjʊləs/ | n. | 小钩，小环 |  A small hook or loop.  Derived from Latin "lien" (spleen), likely referring to its shape. Originally used in anatomical contexts to describe small, hook-like structures, particularly in the spleen.  Now rarely used outside of highly specialized fields. |  The surgeon carefully identified the lienculus on the organ.  外科医生仔细地辨认了器官上的小钩。 | synonym: hooklet, loop | antonym: - | noun
