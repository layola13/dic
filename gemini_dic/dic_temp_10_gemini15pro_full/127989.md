nepenthes: /nɪˈpɛnθiːz/
n.
猪笼草;忘忧草
A genus of carnivorous plants, native to tropical regions, with pitcher-shaped leaves that trap insects.
Nepenthes attract insects with sweet nectar, trapping them in their pitcher-shaped leaves. 猪笼草用甜甜的花蜜吸引昆虫，将它们困在瓶状的叶子中。
The nepenthes is a fascinating example of adaptation in the plant kingdom. 猪笼草是植物界适应性的一个迷人例子。
