impasse:/ˈɪmpæs/ | n. | 僵局，死胡同 |A situation in which no progress is possible, especially because of disagreement; a deadlock.| The negotiations reached an impasse, with neither side willing to compromise.  谈判陷入了僵局，双方都不愿妥协。|Synonyms: deadlock, stalemate, standstill, gridlock | Antonyms: breakthrough, agreement, resolution | noun
