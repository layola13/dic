cheapener:/ˈtʃiːpənər/
noun
降低价格的人或物；使…便宜的东西
Something that reduces the price of something else.
The introduction of new technology has been a cheapener for air travel. 新技术的引入降低了航空旅行的价格。
近义词: reducer, discount, bargain
反义词: increaser, inflater
