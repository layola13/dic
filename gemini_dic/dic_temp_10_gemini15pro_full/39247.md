congreet:/kənˈɡriːt/
v.
致敬，祝贺，迎接
To express good wishes or congratulations to someone.
He congreets the winner of the race. 他祝贺比赛的获胜者。
They congreet their new neighbors with a welcoming party.  他们用一个欢迎派对来迎接他们的新邻居。
近义词:salute, greet, welcome, hail
反义词:snub, ignore
词性:动词
