depthless:/ˈdɛpθlɪs/
adjective
无底的; 深不可测的; 极深的
Lacking depth; immeasurable.  Also used figuratively to describe something vast or profound.
The boat drifted on the depthless ocean.  这艘船漂浮在无底的海洋上。
He stared into her depthless eyes. 他凝视着她深邃的眼睛。
bottomless, unfathomable, profound, immeasurable
shallow, superficial
