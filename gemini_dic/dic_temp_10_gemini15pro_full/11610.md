arm-twisting: /ˈɑːrmˌtwɪstɪŋ/
n.
强迫；施压
The act of trying to persuade someone to do something they do not want to do, by using strong arguments, threats, or other forms of pressure.
He didn't want to run for office, but after some arm-twisting by his colleagues, he agreed.  他不想竞选公职，但在同事们施压之后，他同意了。
She finally signed the contract after some intense arm-twisting by the sales team.  经过销售团队的强力施压，她最终签署了合同。
coercion, pressure, intimidation
persuasion, encouragement
noun
