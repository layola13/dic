hydrators:/ˈhaɪdˌreɪtərz/
n.
补水剂，保湿剂
Substances or products that hydrate or add moisture.
These hydrators are essential for dry skin. 这些保湿剂对于干性皮肤至关重要。

近义词:moisturizers, humectants
反义词:dehydrators, desiccants
词性:名词(Noun)

词源: From the verb "hydrate," which comes from the Latin "hydrātus," past participle of "hydrāre," meaning "to water, to moisten."  Ultimately from Greek "ὕδωρ" (hýdōr) meaning "water."
