aid's:/eɪdz/ | n. | 艾滋病；获得性免疫缺陷综合症 | Acquired Immune Deficiency Syndrome, a disease in which there is a severe loss of the body's cellular immunity, greatly lowering the resistance to infection and malignancy. |  AIDS is a serious global health issue. 艾滋病是一个严重的全球健康问题。|  HIV/AIDS, the syndrome |  health, wellness | n.
Etymology:  An acronym formed from the initial letters of Acquired Immune Deficiency Syndrome.  First recorded in 1982.
