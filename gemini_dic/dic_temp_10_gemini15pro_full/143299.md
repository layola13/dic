outdrinking:/ˌaʊtˈdrɪŋkɪŋ/
verb
比…喝得更多；胜过…的酒量
Drinking more alcohol than someone else.
He outdrank everyone at the party last night. 他昨晚在派对上比任何人都喝得多。
近义词: outdrink, best
反义词: be outdrunk by
