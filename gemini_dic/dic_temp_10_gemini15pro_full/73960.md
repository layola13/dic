fortification:/ˌfɔːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | (食品)营养强化，添加营养素  | Fortification of milk with Vitamin D is common practice.  在牛奶中添加维生素 D 是一种常见的做法。| Example: The fortification of cereals with iron helps prevent anemia.  在谷物中添加铁有助于预防贫血。 | Synonyms: enrichment, supplementation | Antonyms: depletion | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *fortificacioun*, from Old French *fortification*, from Late Latin *fortificātiō*, from Latin *fortificāre* (“to fortify”), from *fortis* (“strong”) + *-ficāre* (from *facere* (“to make”)).
