antitechnological:/ˌæntiˌtɛknəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
反对技术的；反技术的
Opposed to or hostile towards technological advancement or its effects.
The Amish community maintains an antitechnological lifestyle.  阿米什社区保持着一种反技术的生活方式。

His antitechnological stance prevented him from using a computer. 他的反技术立场使他无法使用电脑。

近义词: Luddite, technophobic, technology-averse
反义词:  pro-technology, technophile, technology-friendly
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
technological (adjective): relating to technology (from Greek tekhnologia)
