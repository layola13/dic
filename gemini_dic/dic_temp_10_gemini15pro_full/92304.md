hypnogenic:/ˌhɪpnəˈdʒɛnɪk/ /ˌhɪpnəˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
催眠的; 诱发睡眠的
tending to produce hypnosis or sleep.
例句：The hypnogenic effects of the medication made it difficult to stay awake. 这种药物的催眠作用使人难以保持清醒。

例句：The rhythmic rocking of the train had a hypnogenic effect, lulling me to sleep. 火车有节奏的摇晃具有催眠作用，让我昏昏欲睡。

近义词：soporific, somniferous, hypnotic
反义词：stimulating, invigorating
词性：adjective
