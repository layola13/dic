autoplasty:/ˈɔːtəˌplæsti/ | n. | 自体成形术；自体移植 | Surgical reconstruction of a part of the body using tissue taken from another part of the same body. |  The surgeon performed an autoplasty using bone from the patient's rib.  外科医生使用病人肋骨进行了自体成形术。| Synonyms: autograft, self-grafting | Antonyms: allograft, heterograft | noun
