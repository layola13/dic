missies:/ˈmɪsiz/| n.|小姐们（missie的复数形式）|A term of address for a young unmarried woman, often used in a condescending or patronizing way, now largely outdated.  It is the plural form of "missie." |The old man called out to the group of girls, "Where are you missies going?" 老头对那群女孩喊道：“你们这些小姐要去哪里？”|近义词：girls, young ladies, lasses (informal)|反义词: (No direct opposite; consider "matrons" or "elderly women" for contrast)|noun
