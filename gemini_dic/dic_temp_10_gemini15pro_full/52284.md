dichroic: /daɪˈkroʊɪk/ | adj. | 二色的，双色的 | Exhibiting two colors, especially of light transmitted or reflected at two different angles.  Relating to or exhibiting dichroism. | The dichroic glass pendant shimmered with blue and green.  二色玻璃吊坠闪烁着蓝色和绿色的光芒。|  iridescent, two-toned | monochromatic | adjective


dichroism: /ˈdaɪkroʊɪzəm/ | n. | 二色性 | The property of some crystals and other materials of exhibiting different colors, especially two, when viewed by transmitted light at different angles or by reflected light. | The gemstone displayed strong dichroism, appearing green when viewed from one direction and red from another.  这颗宝石呈现出强烈的二色性，从一个方向看是绿色，从另一个方向看是红色。| birefringence |  - | noun
