coeliac: /ˈsiːliæk/ (UK), /ˈsiːliæk/ (US)| adj. n.| 腹腔的；乳糜泻的；乳糜泻患者 |Relating to the abdomen, particularly the area between the diaphragm and the pelvis.  Also refers to a disease triggered by gluten.  As a noun, refers to a person with this disease.|She has coeliac disease and must follow a strict gluten-free diet. 她患有乳糜泻，必须严格遵守无麸质饮食。|Synonym: celiac (US spelling), gluten-intolerant|Antonym: (No direct antonym, possibly 'gluten-tolerant' in a descriptive context, but not a true antonym)|adjective, noun
