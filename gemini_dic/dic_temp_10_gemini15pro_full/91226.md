hydrocarburet:/ˌhaɪdrəˈkɑːrbərɛt/ |n.| 碳氢化合物 |An organic compound consisting entirely of hydrogen and carbon.  A hydrocarbon is an organic chemical compound composed exclusively of hydrogen and carbon atoms. |Crude oil is a mixture of many different hydrocarbons. 原油是许多不同碳氢化合物的混合物。 | synonym: hydrocarbon| antonym: N/A (Hydrocarbons don't have a direct opposite.) | noun
