baffler:/ˈbæflər/| n.|迷惑不解的事物，难题，挡板|Something that baffles or confuses; a puzzle or conundrum. Also, a device used to deflect air or sound.|The crossword clue was a real baffler. 这个纵横字谜的线索真是个难题。|近义词：puzzle, enigma, mystery, conundrum, riddle|反义词:solution, answer|noun

baffle:/ˈbæfl/| vt.|使困惑，难倒，阻碍|To confuse or perplex someone; to frustrate or thwart.|The magician's trick completely baffled the audience. 魔术师的戏法完全迷惑了观众。|近义词：confuse, perplex, bewilder, mystify, frustrate|反义词:clarify, explain, enlighten|verb

baffling:/ˈbæflɪŋ/| adj.|令人困惑的，难以理解的|Causing confusion or perplexity.|The baffling disappearance of the plane remains a mystery. 飞机令人费解的失踪仍然是个谜。|近义词：confusing, perplexing, bewildering, mystifying|反义词:clear, understandable, obvious|adjective
