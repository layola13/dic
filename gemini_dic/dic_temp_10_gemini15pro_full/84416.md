hallowednesses:/ˈhæləʊɪdnɪsɪz/ | n. | 神圣性，圣洁 | The state of being hallowed; holiness. | The hallowednesses of the ancient burial grounds filled her with awe. 古老墓地的神圣让她充满敬畏。| 近义词：holiness, sanctity, sacredness | 反义词：profaneness, impiety | 词性: 名词

词源: From hallowed + -ness.  "Hallowed" comes from Old English *hālgod, past participle of hālgian "to make holy," from hālig "holy."
