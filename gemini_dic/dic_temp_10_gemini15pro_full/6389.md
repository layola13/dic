amphigenous:/æmˈfɪdʒɪnəs/
adjective
双生的；两面生的
Growing on both sides of a leaf or other part.
例句：The amphigenous rust is a destructive pathogen of many plant species.  这种两面生的锈菌是许多植物物种的破坏性病原体。

例句：Amphigenous growth of the fungus was observed on the leaves.  在叶子上观察到了真菌的两面生长。

近义词：bifacial
反义词：unilateral, epiphyllous (specifically on the upper surface), hypophyllous (specifically on the lower surface)
词性: adjective
