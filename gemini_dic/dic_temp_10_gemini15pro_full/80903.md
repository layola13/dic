governorships:/ˈɡʌvərnərʃɪps/
n.
总督职位；州长职位
the office or position of a governor.
She held two governorships during her career. 她职业生涯中担任过两个州的州长。
近义词: governorship (singular form), state leadership
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider:  subordinate position)
词性: noun (plural)


governor:/ˈɡʌvərnər/
n.
总督；州长；地方长官
a person who governs a state or province.
The governor signed the new bill into law. 州长签署了新法案，使其成为法律。
近义词: chief executive, head of state, administrator
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider:  citizen, resident)
词性: noun
