coucher:/ˈkuːʃeɪ/ (US), /ˈkuːʃə/ (UK)| n.|就寝，躺下；日落，（尤指太阳）落山；（中世纪手抄本的）书页|The act of lying down, especially in bed; sunset, especially of the sun; a leaf of a medieval manuscript.|She reads a story to her children before coucher. 她会在孩子们睡觉前给他们读故事。|Synonyms: bedtime, lying down, sunset|Antonyms: rising, getting up|noun

coucher:/ˈkuːʃeɪ/ (US), /ˈkuːʃə/ (UK)| v.|躺下，安置|To lay down; to put in place.|He couched the spear, ready to throw. 他摆好长矛，准备投掷。|Synonyms: lay, place, position|Antonyms: raise, lift|verb
