lra:/ˈɛl ɑːr ˈeɪ/ | abbreviation | 长期风险评估 (Long-Run Average) | Long-Run Average, often used in financial and statistical analysis to represent the average outcome over an extended period.  It smooths out short-term fluctuations and provides a more stable measure of performance or trend. | The lra return of this investment is about 8%.  这项投资的长期平均回报率约为 8%。 | synonyms: long-term average, long-run mean | antonyms: short-term average, short-run mean | noun
