what: /wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American) |pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么 |

**中文翻译:**  什么，多么，何等

**英文解析:**  Used to ask for information specifying something.  Can also be used as a determiner and adverb.

**例句:**

What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？

What do you want for dinner? = 你晚餐想吃什么？


**近义词:** which, how

**反义词:**  (What doesn't have a direct antonym as it's a question word)

**词性:** pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection
