antinihilism:/ˌæntiˈnaɪɪlɪzəm/
|n.|反虚无主义|The philosophy that rejects nihilism, asserting that life has meaning and value.|His antinihilism is evident in his passionate advocacy for social justice. 他对社会正义的热情倡导体现了他的反虚无主义。|Synonyms:  optimism,  life-affirmation|Antonyms: nihilism, pessimism|noun
