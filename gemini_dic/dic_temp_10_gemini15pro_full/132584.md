nondiffusing:/ˌnɒndɪˈfjuːzɪŋ/
adjective
非扩散的，不扩散的
Not spreading or diffusing; remaining localized.
The researchers observed the nondiffusing nature of the chemical in the controlled experiment.  研究人员在受控实验中观察到这种化学物质的非扩散性质。
The nondiffusing dye allowed them to track the specific cells.  这种非扩散染料使他们能够追踪特定的细胞。
近义词: non-spreading, contained, localized
反义词: diffusing, spreading, pervasive
词性: adjective

词源:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "diffusing" (present participle of "diffuse," from Latin "diffundere" - to pour out, spread)
