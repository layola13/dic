autumn: /ˈɔːtəm/ (British) /ˈɑːtəm/ (American)| n.| 秋天，秋季 |The season after summer and before winter, characterized by falling leaves and cooler weather. |例句：Autumn is my favorite season. 秋天是我最喜欢的季节。

例句：The leaves change color in autumn. 秋天的树叶会变色。

近义词：fall (American English)|反义词：spring|词性：名词
