escapers:/ɪˈskeɪpərz/
noun
逃脱者; 逃跑者;  避难者
People who escape from a place or situation.
The escapers from the prison were quickly caught.  监狱的逃犯很快就被抓住了。
escapees, refugees, runaways
captors, prisoners, detainees
n.

escape:/ɪˈskeɪp/
verb, noun
逃脱，逃跑; 避免; 漏出;
To get away from a dangerous or unpleasant place or situation. /  An act of getting away from a dangerous or unpleasant place or situation.
He narrowly escaped death in the accident. 他在事故中死里逃生。 /  Their escape from the prison was carefully planned. 他们从监狱逃跑是精心策划的。
flee, evade, avoid
be captured, be trapped, be caught
v., n.
