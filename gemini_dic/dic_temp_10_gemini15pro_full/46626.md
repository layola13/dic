damn with faint praise:/ˌdæm wɪð feɪnt ˈpreɪz/
verb phrase
贬抑性地赞扬，明褒暗贬
To criticize someone or something indirectly by giving it very little praise.
He damned the movie with faint praise, saying it was "watchable." 他对这部电影明褒暗贬，说它“还可以看看”。
近义词: backhanded compliment, left-handed compliment
反义词: praise, commend, extol
词性: verb phrase
