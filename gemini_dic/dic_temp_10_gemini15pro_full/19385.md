bicameral:/baɪˈkæmərəl/ | adj. |两院制的，双院制的 | Having two branches, chambers, or houses, as a legislative body. | The United States has a bicameral legislature, consisting of the Senate and the House of Representatives. 美国实行两院制立法机构，由参议院和众议院组成。|  The UK Parliament is bicameral. 英国议会是两院制的。 |  bilateral, two-chambered | unicameral | adjective
