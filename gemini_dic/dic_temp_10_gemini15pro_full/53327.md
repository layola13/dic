disaccommodation:/dɪsəˌkɒməˈdeɪʃn/
n.|失适应，失调节|The process of adaptation to a stimulus ceasing or being reversed.  The failure of the eyes to accommodate.|The pilot experienced disaccommodation after the rapid descent.  飞行员在快速下降后经历了失适应。|近义词:maladjustment, imbalance|反义词:adaptation, adjustment
n.|不方便，不便之处|A lack of convenience or suitability.|The hotel's lack of air conditioning was a major disaccommodation.  酒店没有空调是一大不便之处。|近义词:inconvenience, discomfort|反义词:convenience, comfort
