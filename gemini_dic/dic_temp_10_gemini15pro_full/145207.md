overballasts:/ˌoʊvərˈbæləst/| v.|使装载过重，使压舱物过多|To load (a ship, aircraft, etc.) with excessive ballast.|The ship was overballasted and rode low in the water. 这艘船压舱物过多，吃水很深。|Synonyms: overload, overburden|Antonyms: underload, lighten|verb

overballasts:/ˌoʊvərˈbæləst/| n.|过多的压舱物|Excessive ballast.|The overballasts caused the ship to be unstable. 过多的压舱物导致船舶不稳定。|Synonyms: surplus ballast, excess ballast|Antonyms: insufficient ballast|noun
