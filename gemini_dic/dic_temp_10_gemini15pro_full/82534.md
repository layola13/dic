guadalete:/ˌɡwɑːdəˈlɛteɪ/ | n. | 瓜达莱特河 | A river in southwestern Spain. It flows into the Bay of Cádiz.  It is famous for the Battle of Guadalete in 711 AD, which marked the beginning of the Muslim conquest of Spain. | The Battle of Guadalete took place near the Guadalete River. 瓜达莱特战役发生在瓜达莱特河附近。|  近义词: river | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

guadalete:/ˌɡwɑːdəˈlɛteɪ/ | adj. | 瓜达莱特的 |  Relating to or associated with the Guadalete River.  This usage is less common than the noun form. | The Guadalete valley is known for its fertile land. 瓜达莱特河谷以其肥沃的土地而闻名。 | 近义词: N/A | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 形容词
