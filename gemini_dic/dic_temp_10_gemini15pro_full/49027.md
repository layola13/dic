degreased:/diːˈɡriːst/ /diːˈɡriːzd/
adjective
脱脂的;去油的
Removed grease from; having had grease removed.
The mechanic degreased the engine before repairing it.  机械师在修理发动机之前先去除了油污。
The degreased surface was ready for painting.  脱脂后的表面可以进行喷漆了。
Synonyms:  cleaned, cleansed, purified, scrubbed
Antonyms:  greased, oiled, lubricated
词性: adjective
