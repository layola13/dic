bontebok:/ˈbɒntəbɒk/ | n. | 角马 | A South African antelope (Damaliscus pygargus pygarus) with a distinctive chocolate-colored coat and white patches. |  The bontebok grazed peacefully on the African savanna. 角马在非洲大草原上平静地吃草。|  The bontebok, once endangered, is now thriving in protected areas.  曾经濒临灭绝的角马现在在保护区内繁衍生息。 | Synonyms: blesbok, antelope | Antonyms: (No direct antonym for an animal species) | noun
