gibbosity:/ɡɪˈbɒsɪti/
n.
驼背，隆起，凸起
a rounded swelling or protuberance
The gibbosity of his back was quite pronounced. 他的背驼得很厉害。

近义词:hump, bulge, protuberance, swelling
反义词:concavity, hollowness, indentation, depression
词性:noun
