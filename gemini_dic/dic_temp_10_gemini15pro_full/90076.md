hostel: /ˈhɒst(ə)l/ (British) /ˈhɑːst(ə)l/ (American)| n. | 旅舍，招待所，宿舍 |A supervised lodging place providing cheap, temporary accommodation, especially for young travelers.|  Example: We stayed in a hostel during our backpacking trip across Europe. 我们在欧洲背包旅行期间住在一家旅舍。|Synonyms: inn, lodge, bunkhouse| Antonyms: hotel, resort, motel| noun

hostel: /ˈhɒst(ə)l/ (British) /ˈhɑːst(ə)l/ (American)| n. | (尤指大学的)学生宿舍 | A building which provides accommodation and meals for students.| Example: The university hostel was full of first-year students. 大学宿舍里住满了大一年级的学生。|Synonyms: dormitory, residence hall| Antonyms: apartment, house| noun
