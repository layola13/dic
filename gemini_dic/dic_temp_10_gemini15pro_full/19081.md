betraying:/bɪˈtreɪɪŋ/| v. |背叛；出卖；泄露；辜负|Exposing or revealing something unintentionally; being disloyal or unfaithful to someone. | He felt betrayed by his best friend's lies. 他觉得被他最好的朋友的谎言背叛了。|
betraying:/bɪˈtreɪɪŋ/| adj. |背叛的；出卖的；泄露的| Showing disloyalty or revealing something unintentionally. | Her betraying eyes gave away her secret. 她背叛的眼神泄露了她的秘密。|


近义词: deceiving, misleading, revealing, exposing, double-crossing, backstabbing
反义词: loyal, faithful, true, protecting, supporting
词性:  Verb (present participle); Adjective (attributive)

词源: From Middle English *betrayen*, from Anglo-French *betraier*, from *be-* + *trair* (“to hand over, deliver”), ultimately from Latin *tradere* (“to hand over”).
