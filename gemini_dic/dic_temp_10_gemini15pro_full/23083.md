boundlessness:/ˈbaʊndləsnəs/
n.
无限；无边无际；广阔无垠
The quality of being boundless; vastness.
The boundlessness of the ocean always fills me with awe.  大海的无边无际总是让我充满敬畏。
immensity, vastness, infinity, limitlessness, endlessness
limitation, finiteness, boundedness
noun
