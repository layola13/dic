mass-product:/ˈmæs ˈprɑːdʌkt/ /ˈmæs ˈprɒdʌkt/| n. | 大量生产的产品，批量产品 |A product manufactured in large quantities, usually by machinery. |  Ford's Model T was one of the first true mass-produced automobiles. 福特的T型车是最早真正批量生产的汽车之一。

| mass-produced cars are often cheaper than hand-crafted ones. 大量生产的汽车通常比手工制作的汽车便宜。| 近义词: mass-produced item, bulk product, manufactured good | 反义词: handcrafted item, bespoke product, custom-made product | 词性: noun


mass-produce:/ˌmæs prəˈdjuːs/ /ˌmæs prəˈdʒuːs/| v. | 大量生产，批量生产 | To manufacture (a product) in large quantities, typically by using machinery. |  The company mass-produces clothing for budget retailers. 这家公司为廉价零售商大量生产服装。

| They mass-produce plastic toys in their factory. 他们在工厂里大量生产塑料玩具。 | 近义词: manufacture, produce in bulk, churn out | 反义词: handcraft, custom-make | 词性: verb
