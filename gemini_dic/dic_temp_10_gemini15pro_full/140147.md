offcut:/ˈɒfkʌt/ (UK) /ˈɔːfkʌt/ (US)| n. |边角料，零头，余料|A piece of material remaining after the main part has been cut off, typically fabric, wood, or paper. |例句：She used the offcuts of fabric to make a patchwork quilt. 她用布料的边角料做了一床拼布被。

例句：The carpenter saved the wood offcuts for kindling. 木匠把木头的边角料留作引火物。|近义词：remnant, scrap, leftover|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "whole", "original piece")|noun
