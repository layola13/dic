nosocomial: /ˌnɒsəˈkəʊmiəl/ (British) /ˌnoʊsəˈkoʊmiəl/ (American)
adjective
医院获得的；医院内感染的
Originating or taking place in a hospital, acquired in a hospital, especially in reference to an infection.
She developed a nosocomial infection after surgery.  她手术后发生了医院内感染。
The hospital has strict protocols to prevent nosocomial infections. 这家医院有严格的规章制度来防止医院内感染。
Synonyms: hospital-acquired, hospital-borne, iatrogenic
Antonyms: community-acquired

词性: adjective
