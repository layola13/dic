astigmatism: /əˈstɪɡmətɪzəm/ | n. | 散光 | A defect in the eye or in a lens caused by a deviation from spherical curvature, which results in distorted images, as light rays are prevented from meeting at a common focus. |  My astigmatism makes it hard to see things clearly at a distance. 我的散光使我看不清远处的东西。|  Nearsightedness, myopia |  Hyperopia (farsightedness) | noun

astigmias: /əˈstɪɡmətɪzəmz/ | n. | 散光（复数） |  Plural form of astigmatism, referring to multiple instances of this eye condition. | He suffers from astigmias in both eyes. 他的双眼都有散光。 | Nearsightedness, myopia (plural: myopias) |  Hyperopia (farsightedness - plural: hyperopias) | noun
