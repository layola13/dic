jumbomania: /ˌdʒʌmbəˈmeɪniə/
n.
狂热的收集癖，尤指集邮
An excessive enthusiasm for collecting things, especially stamps.
He had a jumbomania for collecting stamps from all over the world. 他对收集世界各地的邮票有一种狂热的癖好。
近义词:  collectomania
反义词: apathy

词性: noun
