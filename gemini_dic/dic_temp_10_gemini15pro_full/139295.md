obliviousness:/əˈblɪviəsnəs/
|n.|健忘; 忘却; 不知不觉|The state of being unaware or unconscious of something.|He drove on in happy obliviousness of the danger he was in. 他愉快地继续开车，丝毫没有意识到自己身处险境。|
|近义词：unawareness, insensibility, inattention, heedlessness|
|反义词：awareness, consciousness, mindfulness|
|词性：名词|

oblivious:/əˈblɪviəs/
|adj.|未察觉的; 不知道的; 忘却的|Not aware of or not concerned about what is happening around one.|She seemed completely oblivious to the noise around her. 她似乎完全没有注意到周围的噪音。|
|近义词：unaware, unconscious, insensible, unmindful, heedless|
|反义词：aware, conscious, mindful|
|词性：形容词|
