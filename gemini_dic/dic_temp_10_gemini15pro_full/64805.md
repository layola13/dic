esoteric: /ˌɛs.əˈtɛr.ɪk/ (US) /ˌiː.səˈtɛr.ɪk/ (UK)| adj.| 秘传的；深奥的；难理解的|Intended for or likely to be understood by only a small number of people with a specialized knowledge or interest.|例句：The group held esoteric discussions about the meaning of life. 这个小组就生命的意义进行了深奥的讨论。|例句：His esoteric knowledge of wine impressed everyone at the dinner party. 他对葡萄酒的深奥知识给晚宴上的每个人都留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：abstruse, arcane, obscure, recondite|反义词：exoteric, common, simple, accessible|词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *esotericus*, from Ancient Greek ἐσωτερικός (esōterikós, “inner”), from ἔσω (ésō, “within”).
