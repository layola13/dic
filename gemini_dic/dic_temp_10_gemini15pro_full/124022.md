morose:/məˈroʊs/ /mɔːˈrəʊs/| adj.|闷闷不乐的；阴郁的；脾气不好的|Having a sullen and gloomy disposition. Characterized by or expressing gloom. |He became morose and withdrawn after losing his job. 他失业后变得闷闷不乐，不愿与人交往。|Synonyms: sullen, gloomy, glum, sulky, grumpy, surly|Antonyms: cheerful, happy, joyful, optimistic|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mōrōsus (“peevish, fretful”), from mōs (“manner, custom, habit, humor”).
