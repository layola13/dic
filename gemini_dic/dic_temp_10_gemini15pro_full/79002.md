gismos:/ˈɡɪzməʊz/ (UK) /ˈɡɪzməʊz/ (US)| n.|小玩意儿；小装置；(尤指用途不详的)小机械|A gadget, especially one whose name the speaker does not know or cannot recall.  A mechanical device or contrivance. |例句：
He was surrounded by electronic gismos.
他周围都是些电子小玩意儿。

例句：
I don't know what all these gismos are for.
我不知道这些小装置都是干什么用的。
