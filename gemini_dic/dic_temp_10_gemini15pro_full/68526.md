fanciful:/ˈfæn.sɪ.fəl/| adj.|异想天开的；奇特的；想像的|Existing only in the imagination; unreal.  Characterized by or displaying imagination.|She had a fanciful notion of becoming a famous actress. 她有一个想成为著名女演员的奇特想法。|Synonyms: imaginative, whimsical, capricious, fantastic, unreal, dreamy|Antonyms: realistic, practical, down-to-earth|adjective

fanciful:/ˈfæn.sɪ.fəl/| adj.|装饰的；别致的|Overly elaborate or decorative.|The building had fanciful carvings around the windows. 这栋建筑的窗户周围有奇特的雕刻。|Synonyms: ornate, elaborate, decorative|Antonyms: plain, simple, austere|adjective
