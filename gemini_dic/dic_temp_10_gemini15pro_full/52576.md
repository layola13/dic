diffusing:/dɪˈfjuːzɪŋ/| v. |扩散，传播，散布|The scent of flowers was diffusing through the garden. 花香弥漫在整个花园里。|例句：The heat is diffusing through the metal plate. 热量正在通过金属板扩散。|近义词：spreading, disseminating, dispersing|反义词：concentrating, focusing|词性: 动词 (present participle of diffuse)

diffusing:/dɪˈfjuːzɪŋ/| adj. |扩散的，弥漫的|The photographer used a diffusing filter to soften the light. 摄影师用了一个漫射滤镜来柔化光线。|例句：A diffusing lens scatters the light. 漫射透镜使光线散射。|近义词：spreading, pervasive|反义词：concentrated, focused|词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin diffusus, past participle of diffundere "to pour out, spread out," from dis- "out" + fundere "to pour."
