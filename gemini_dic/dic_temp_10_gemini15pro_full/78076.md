geodesy:/dʒiˈɒdəsi/ (US) /dʒiˈɑːdəsi/ (UK)| n.|大地测量学|The science concerned with determining the exact shape and size of the Earth and its gravitational field.|例句：Geodesy plays a crucial role in mapping and navigation. 大地测量学在地图绘制和导航中起着至关重要的作用。

例句：Modern geodesy uses satellites to make precise measurements of the Earth. 现代大地测量学使用卫星对地球进行精确测量。|近义词：surveying, cartography|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *géodésie*, from Ancient Greek γεωδαισία (geōdaisía, “division of the earth”), from γῆ (gê, “earth”) + δαίω (daíō, “divide”).
