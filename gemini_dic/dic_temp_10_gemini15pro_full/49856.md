demoted:/dɪˈmoʊtɪd/
adjective
降级的；被降职的
Reduced to a lower grade, rank, or position.
He was demoted from manager to assistant manager. 他被从经理降职为助理经理。

demoted:/dɪˈmoʊtɪd/
verb
降级；降职
To reduce to a lower grade, rank, or position.
The army demoted him for disobeying orders. 军队因为他违抗命令而将他降级。

Synonyms:relegated, downgraded, lowered
Antonyms:promoted, elevated, advanced
词性:  adjective/verb (past participle of demote)
