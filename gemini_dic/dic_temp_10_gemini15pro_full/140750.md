omnispective:/ˌɒm.nɪˈspek.tɪv/
adjective
全视角的; 全方位的
Having the ability to see everything, or having unlimited perception.
The omnispective narrator in the novel knew the thoughts and feelings of every character.  小说中全知叙述者知道每个角色的想法和感受。

The security system provided an omnispective view of the entire facility. 该安全系统提供了整个设施的全方位视图。

synonym: all-seeing, panoptic, comprehensive, unlimited
antonym: limited, restricted, narrow, myopic
词性: adjective
