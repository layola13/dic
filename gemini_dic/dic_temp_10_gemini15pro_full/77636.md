gelatinate:/dʒɪˈlætɪneɪt/
v.
胶化，使凝胶化
to convert into gelatin or a substance resembling gelatin; to become gelatinous.
The mixture will gelatinate as it cools.  混合物冷却后会凝胶化。
近义词: congeal, solidify, thicken, jell
反义词: liquefy, melt, dissolve
词性: 动词(verb)

gelatinate:/ˈdʒɛlətɪnət/
adj.
胶状的
resembling gelatin; gelatinous.
The substance formed a gelatinate mass.  这种物质形成了胶状块。
近义词: gelatinous, jellylike, viscous
反义词: liquid, fluid, watery
词性: 形容词(adjective)

gelatinate:/ˈdʒɛlətɪˌneɪt/
n.
胶状物
a gelatinous substance.
The resulting gelatinate was then analyzed. 产生的胶状物随后被分析。
近义词: gel, jelly
反义词: liquid, solution
词性: 名词(noun)


词源:  From Late Latin *gelatinatus, past participle of *gelatinare (“to make into jelly”), from Latin gelatus (“frozen”), past participle of gelare (“to freeze”). Related to gelatine.
