cusping:/ˈkʌspɪŋ/| adj.|处于交点, 处于临界点|Being on the cusp; transitional.  Being born near the dividing line between two astrological signs.|Her birthday is on the cusp of Gemini and Cancer. 她的生日在双子座和巨蟹座的交界点。|近义词：transitional, bordering, in-between|反义词:stable, fixed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "cusp" (point or apex) + "-ing" (present participle suffix). Ultimately from Latin "cuspis" (point).
