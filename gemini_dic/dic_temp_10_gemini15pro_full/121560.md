misnamed:/ˌmɪsˈneɪmd/
adjective
命名错误的；错误命名的
incorrectly named
The misnamed "Affordable Care Act" has actually made healthcare more expensive for many. 被错误命名为“平价医疗法案”的法案实际上让许多人的医疗保健更加昂贵。
The lake, misnamed "Peaceful Waters," was known for its sudden and violent storms.  这个被错误命名为“平静之水”的湖泊以其突如其来的猛烈风暴而闻名。

近义词:incorrectly labeled, wrongly called, inappropriately named
反义词:aptly named, correctly named, properly named
词性:adjective

词源: mis- + named (past participle of name)
"mis-" is a prefix of Germanic origin meaning "wrong," "bad," or "wrongly."
"named" comes from the Old English word "nama" meaning "name."
