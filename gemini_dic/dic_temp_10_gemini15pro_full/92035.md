hyperperfection:/ˌhaɪpərˈpɜːrfɛkʃən/ | n. |超完全数，高度完美|A number n is called hyperperfect if n = 1 + k(σ(n) − n − 1) for some integer k, where σ(n) is the sum of the divisors of n.|The number 21 is a hyperperfect number because 21 = 1 + 2(32 - 21 - 1).  21等于1加上2乘以(32减21减1)。|Near synonyms: perfect number, multiperfect number|Near antonyms: deficient number, abundant number|noun
