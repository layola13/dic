nonfluentness:/ˌnɒnˈfluːəntnəs/ /ˌnɑːnˈfluːəntnəs/| n. |不流利，不流畅|The nonfluentness of his speech made it difficult to understand him.  他的讲话不流利，很难理解。|例句：His nonfluentness in English hindered his communication. 他英语不流利，阻碍了他的交流。

例句：The student's nonfluentness was attributed to a lack of practice.  这个学生的不流利是由于缺乏练习造成的。|近义词：disfluency, hesitancy, stammering|反义词: fluency, eloquence|词性: noun
