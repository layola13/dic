fugitive:/ˈfjuːdʒətɪv/| n. adj.|逃亡者，逃犯；短暂的，易逝的|a person who has escaped from a place or is in hiding, especially to avoid arrest or persecution|The police are searching for the fugitive who escaped from prison. 警方正在搜捕越狱的逃犯。|近义词：escapee, runaway, deserter, refugee|反义词:captive, prisoner|noun

fugitive:/ˈfjuːdʒətɪv/| adj.|短暂的，易逝的|lasting for only a short time; fleeting|A fugitive glance was all he gave her. 他只给了她匆匆一瞥。|近义词：fleeting, ephemeral, transient, transitory|反义词:permanent, enduring, lasting|adjective
