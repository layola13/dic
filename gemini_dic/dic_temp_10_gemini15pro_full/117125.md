meandering:/miˈændərɪŋ/
adjective
蜿蜒的；曲折的；漫步的；不着边际的
Following a winding or indirect course.  Rambling or aimless in speech or thought.
The river followed a meandering path through the valley. 这条河沿着蜿蜒的路径穿过山谷。

His speech was meandering and difficult to follow. 他的讲话不着边际，难以理解。

近义词:winding, twisting, rambling, indirect, circuitous
反义词:straight, direct, focused

meander:/miˈændər/
verb
漫步；蜿蜒而流；闲逛；迂回曲折
To wander aimlessly or casually; to follow a winding course.
We meandered through the streets of the old town. 我们漫步在老城的街道上。

The stream meanders through the meadow. 小溪蜿蜒流过草地。

近义词:wander, stroll, ramble, roam
反义词:rush, hurry, hasten

meander:/miˈændər/
noun
漫步；曲流；迂回曲折的进程
A winding curve or bend in a river or road. A leisurely walk or ramble.
The river's meanders created small islands. 河流的曲流形成了一些小岛。

We took a meander through the park. 我们在公园里漫步。

近义词:wind, curve, bend, turn, ramble, stroll
反义词:straight


词性:  adjective, verb, noun
词源: From the Greek Μαίανδρος (Maiandros), the name of a winding river in Phrygia (now Turkey), known for its many bends.
