corregidor:/kəˌrɛhɪˈdɔːr/ | n. | 地方行政官；科雷希多岛 | A magistrate or local administrative official in Spain or in areas formerly under Spanish control.  Corregidor also refers to Corregidor Island in the Philippines, a historic site. |  The corregidor held considerable power in his district.  地方行政官在他的辖区内拥有相当大的权力。

The Battle of Corregidor was a pivotal moment in World War II. 科雷希多岛战役是二战中的关键时刻。 | synonym: magistrate, administrator, official | antonym:  citizen, resident, subordinate | noun
