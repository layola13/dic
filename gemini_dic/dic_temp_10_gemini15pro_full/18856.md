beseechingly:/bɪˈsiːtʃɪŋli/
adverb
恳求地；哀求地
In a way that expresses urgent pleading.
She looked at him beseechingly. 她恳求地看着他。
近义词: pleadingly, imploringly, supplicatingly
反义词: demandingly, assertively
词性: adverb

beseech:/bɪˈsiːtʃ/
verb
恳求；哀求
Ask (someone) urgently and fervently to do something; implore; entreat.
They beseeched him to stay. 他们恳求他留下。
近义词: implore, entreat, beg
反义词: demand, command
词性: verb
