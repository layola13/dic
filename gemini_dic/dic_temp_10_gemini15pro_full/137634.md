nontyrannic:/ˌnɒntɪˈrænɪk/
adjective
非暴虐的;非专制的
Not tyrannical; not exercising tyranny.
The leader promoted a nontyrannic approach to governance, emphasizing collaboration and open communication. 这位领导人提倡一种非暴虐的治理方式，强调合作和公开沟通。
The new constitution established a nontyrannic government, ensuring the protection of individual rights. 新宪法建立了一个非暴政的政府，确保个人权利得到保护。

近义词:democratic, liberal, non-authoritarian, non-despotic
反义词:tyrannical, despotic, authoritarian, oppressive
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + tyrannic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "tyrannic" is an adjective relating to tyranny, derived from the Greek "tyrannos" meaning "absolute ruler."
