ensnarers:/ɪnˈsnɛərərz/
n.
陷阱设置者；诱捕者
Those who trap or lure others into difficulty or danger.
The poachers were ensnarers of endangered animals.  偷猎者是濒危动物的诱捕者。
The internet can be a dangerous place for children, with ensnarers lurking in chat rooms.  互联网对儿童来说可能是一个危险的地方，聊天室里潜伏着诱捕者。
近义词: trappers, deceivers, predators
反义词: protectors, rescuers
词性: 名词(plural)

ensnare:/ɪnˈsnɛər/
vt.
诱捕；使陷入圈套
To catch in a trap or entanglement; to lure into difficulty.
The spider ensnared the fly in its web. 蜘蛛用网捕捉了苍蝇。
He was ensnared by her beauty and charm. 他被她的美貌和魅力所迷惑。
近义词: trap, entangle, lure, seduce
反义词: free, liberate, release
词性: 动词

词源:
From en- (meaning "in" or "into") + snare (a trap for catching birds or animals).  源自en-（意为“在...内”或“进入”）+ snare（捕捉鸟类或动物的陷阱）。
