mota:/ˈmoʊtə/ (US), /ˈməʊtə/ (UK)| n.| 斑点，污点，瑕疵; 摩托车(slang)|A small spot or speck, especially on the skin; a motorbike (informal usage, primarily Spanish).|例句：He noticed a tiny mota on the apple. 他注意到苹果上有一个小斑点。

例句：I’ll get there on my mota. (Slang, primarily Spanish-speaking regions)  我会骑我的摩托车去那里。|近义词：speck, spot, blemish, dot, freckle (for skin), motorbike, motorcycle (for slang usage)|反义词:clearness, purity|词性:noun
