glossopharyngeal:/ˌɡlɒsəfəˈrɪndʒiəl/ /ˌɡlɑːsəfəˈrɪndʒiəl/| adj. n.|舌咽的；舌咽神经的|Relating to the tongue and pharynx.  Also, the ninth cranial nerve, which supplies motor fibers to the stylopharyngeus muscle of the pharynx and sensory fibers to the posterior tongue, the tonsil, and the pharynx.|The glossopharyngeal nerve plays a crucial role in swallowing. 舌咽神经在吞咽过程中起着至关重要的作用。|The doctor tested the patient's glossopharyngeal nerve function. 医生检查了病人的舌咽神经功能。|近义词:pharyngo-oral|反义词:None|词性:adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek γλῶσσα (glôssa, “tongue”) + φάρυγξ (phárunx, “pharynx”).
