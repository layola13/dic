outbleat:/ˌaʊtˈbliːt/
|v.|大声胜过；比...叫得更响亮|To cry louder than; to surpass in bleating.|The alpha male sheep outbleated the others, asserting his dominance.  领头公羊的叫声盖过了其他羊，以此宣示自己的统治地位。|
The younger politician tried to outbleat the seasoned senator, but his arguments were weaker.  年轻的政治家试图用更大的声音盖过经验丰富的参议员，但他的论点较弱。|近义词：outcry, outshout, drown out|反义词: be drowned out, be outshouted|词性:动词

outbleat:/ˌaʊtˈbliːt/
|n.|胜过的叫声|A louder bleat that surpasses others.|The shepherd heard the outbleat of the distressed lamb. 牧羊人听到了遇险羔羊胜过其他羊的叫声。|
The outbleat of the protesters could be heard from blocks away. 抗议者的叫喊声在几个街区外都能听到。|近义词：outcry, clamor|反义词: whisper, murmur|词性:名词
