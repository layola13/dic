forestalment:/fɔːrˈstɔːlmənt/ | n. | 预付款；分期付款；预示 | An early payment of part of a debt or larger sum; something that foreshadows something else. |  The company requested a forestalment of 20% of the total project cost.  公司要求预付项目总成本的 20%。

The dark clouds were a forestalment of the coming storm. 乌云预示着暴风雨即将来临。 | synonym: prepayment, down payment, installment, anticipation, portent | antonym: arrears, delay, postponement | noun
