ossivorous:/ɒˈsɪvərəs/
adjective
食骨的
feeding on bone; bone-eating.
Hyena are ossivorous animals.  鬣狗是食骨动物。
The fossilized remains suggest an ossivorous predator.  化石遗骸表明这是一种食骨的捕食者。
synonyms: bone-eating
antonyms: herbivorous, carnivorous (in the strict sense of flesh-eating)
词性: adjective
