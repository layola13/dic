insulates:/ˈɪnsəleɪts/ | v. | 隔离，使绝缘 | To cover or protect something with a material that prevents the passage of heat, electricity, or sound. |  The house is well insulated against the cold. 这房子防寒隔热很好。| synonyms: protect, shield, isolate | antonyms: expose, connect, join | verb

PHRASE->
insulate against: 隔绝…以防止… The walls are insulated against the cold. 墙壁隔热以防寒冷。
insulate from: 使…免受…的影响 He tried to insulate himself from the criticism. 他试图让自己免受批评的影响。
thermally insulate:  热绝缘  They thermally insulated the roof to reduce energy loss. 他们对屋顶进行了隔热处理，以减少能源损失。
sound insulation: 隔音 The house has excellent sound insulation. 这房子的隔音效果非常好。
electrical insulation: 电绝缘 The electrical insulation on the wires is worn. 电线上的绝缘层磨损了。
