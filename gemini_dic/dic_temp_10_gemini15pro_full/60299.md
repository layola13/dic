eggs:/ɛɡz/
n.|蛋，鸡蛋|Oval reproductive body of a bird, especially one of domestic poultry, encased in a shell and used as food.|I had scrambled eggs for breakfast. 我早餐吃了炒鸡蛋。|近义词:ova (technical/scientific term)|反义词: None (eggs don't have a direct opposite)|词性:Noun
Etymology: From Old English *æg*, from Proto-Germanic *ajjąm*, from Proto-Indo-European *ōwyo-/*əwyó- (“egg”). Cognate with Old Norse egg, Old Frisian ei, Dutch ei, German Ei.
