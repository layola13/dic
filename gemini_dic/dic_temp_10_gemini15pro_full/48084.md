declassification:/diːˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. |解密，撤销保密|The declassification of the documents revealed new information about the war.  这些文件的解密揭示了关于战争的新信息。|例句：The declassification of the files led to a public outcry. 文件的解密引起了公众的强烈抗议。

The government announced the declassification of several top-secret documents. 政府宣布解密几份绝密文件。|近义词：disclosure, release, unveiling|反义词：classification, secrecy|词性:noun

Etymology: From de- (prefix meaning "removal, reversal") + classification (meaning "the act of arranging in classes or categories").  Ultimately from Latin classis ("class, division") + facere ("to make, do").
