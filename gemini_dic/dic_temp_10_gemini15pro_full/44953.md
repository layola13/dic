cryptosporidia: /ˌkrɪptoʊspɔːˈrɪdiə/ | n. |隐孢子虫（复数）| Microscopic parasites that cause the diarrheal disease cryptosporidiosis.  They belong to the phylum Apicomplexa. | Cryptosporidia are a common cause of waterborne illness. 隐孢子虫是水传播疾病的常见原因。| Cryptosporidium parvum is the species most often associated with human infection. 小隐孢子虫是最常与人类感染相关的物种。| 近义词: (for the disease it causes)  giardia | 反义词: None | noun
