hypothecate:/haɪˈpɑːθɪkeɪt/ | v. | 抵押，担保 | To pledge something (usually property) as security for a loan without giving up possession of it. | The farmer hypothecated his land to secure a loan for new equipment. 农民用他的土地抵押贷款购买新设备。 |  He hypothecated his house to get a business loan. 他抵押了他的房子以获得商业贷款。 | synonyms: pledge, mortgage, collateralize | antonyms: redeem, reclaim | verb
