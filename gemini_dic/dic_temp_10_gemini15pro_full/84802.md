handspring:/ˈhændsprɪŋ/| n.|手翻，翻筋斗|An acrobatic movement in which the body turns over forward or backward by putting the hands on the ground one after the other.|She did a perfect handspring on the mat. 她在垫子上做了一个完美的手翻。|近义词：flip, somersault, cartwheel|反义词:|noun

handsprings:/ˈhændsprɪŋz/| n.|手翻，翻筋斗（复数）|Multiple acrobatic movements in which the body turns over forward or backward by putting the hands on the ground one after the other.|The gymnast performed a series of handsprings across the floor.  体操运动员在地板上做了一连串的手翻。|近义词：flips, somersaults, cartwheels|反义词:|noun
