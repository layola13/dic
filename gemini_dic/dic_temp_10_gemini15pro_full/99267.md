interconfound: /ˌɪntərˌkənˈfaʊnd/
verb
相互混淆，混为一谈
To mix up (different elements) so that they are difficult to distinguish; confuse.  The study interconfounded several variables making it difficult to draw clear conclusions. 这项研究混淆了几个变量，使得难以得出明确的结论。
例句：The researchers interconfounded the treatment and control groups, compromising the results. 研究人员将治疗组和对照组混淆了，损害了结果。

例句：It's easy to interconfound correlation with causation. 很容易将相关性和因果关系混为一谈。
近义词：confuse, confound, mix up, muddle, blend
反义词：distinguish, differentiate, separate
词性：v.
