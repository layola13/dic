matrimonious:/ˌmætrɪˈmoʊniəs/| adj.|婚姻的，结婚的|Of or relating to marriage or the state of being married.  Pertaining to the ceremonies or customs of marriage.|例句：They had a long and matrimonious life together. 他们一起度过了漫长而婚姻幸福的生活。

例句：The couple exchanged matrimonious vows. 这对夫妇交换了结婚誓言。|近义词：marital, conjugal, wedded, nuptial|反义词：unmarried, single, divorced, widowed|词性:形容词
matrimony:/ˈmætrɪmoʊni/|n.|婚姻，结婚|The state or ceremony of being married; marriage.|例句：They were joined in holy matrimony. 他们在神圣的婚姻中结合在一起。

例句：Matrimony is a sacred bond. 婚姻是神圣的纽带。|近义词：marriage, wedlock, union|反义词：divorce, separation|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *matrimonie*, from Old French *matrimoine*, from Latin *mātrimōnium* ("marriage, wedlock"), from *māter* ("mother") + *-monium* (suffix forming nouns related to action, result, state, or means). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *méh₂tēr* ("mother").
