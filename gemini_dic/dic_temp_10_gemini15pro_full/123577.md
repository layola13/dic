例句：The film depicted a monstrous creature from the deep sea.  这部电影描绘了一个来自深海的可怕生物。

近义词：huge, enormous, gigantic, colossal, immense, terrible, dreadful, horrific, appalling, shocking, outrageous|反义词：tiny, small, minute, insignificant, normal, ordinary|词性：形容词
