nonhyperbolically:/ˌnɒnˌhaɪpərˈbɒlɪkli/
adverb
非夸张地；不夸张地；不使用夸张手法地
Without exaggeration; literally.
The witness described the events nonhyperbolically, sticking to the verifiable facts.  证人没有夸大其词地描述了事件，坚持可证实的事实。
She spoke nonhyperbolically about the challenges they faced, avoiding any dramatic embellishments. 她没有夸大其词地讲述了他们面临的挑战，避免了任何戏剧性的修饰。
Synonym: literally, realistically, truthfully, factually, soberly
Antonym: hyperbolically, exaggeratedly, dramatically, extravagantly
词性: 副词
