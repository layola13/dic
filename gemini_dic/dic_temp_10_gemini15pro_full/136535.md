nonruminative:/ˌnɒnˈruːmɪnətɪv/ | adj. | 非反刍的，不沉思的 | Not ruminating; not chewing the cud (of animals); not meditating or pondering (of people). | The scientist studied the digestive system of nonruminative animals.  科学家研究了非反刍动物的消化系统。|  The child's nonruminative nature meant he quickly moved on from disappointments.  这个孩子不爱沉思的性格意味着他能很快地从失望中走出来。 |Synonyms: unreflective, unthinking, impulsive | Antonyms: ruminative, contemplative, reflective | adjective
