muntjac:/ˈmʌntdʒæk/ | n. | 赤麂，吠鹿 |A small deer of Southeast Asia, noted for its barking call.  |例句：The muntjac is a shy and elusive creature.  赤麂是一种害羞且难以捉摸的生物。

|例句：We saw a muntjac foraging in the undergrowth. 我们看到一只赤麂在灌木丛中觅食。 |近义词：barking deer, rib-faced deer |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
