agitprop:/ˈædʒɪtprɒp/ | n. | 政治宣传 |  Agitprop is a portmanteau of the Russian words "agitatsiya" (agitation) and "propaganda." It refers to politically charged art, literature, or theatre, often used to promote a particular ideology or cause. | The play was a blatant piece of agitprop designed to stir up revolutionary sentiment. 这部戏是一部公然的政治宣传作品，旨在激起革命情绪。 |  propaganda, indoctrination | censorship, objectivity | noun
