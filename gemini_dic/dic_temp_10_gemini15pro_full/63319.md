entire:/ɪnˈtaɪər/ (US), /ɪnˈtaɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|整个的，全部的，完整的|Complete in all respects; with no part left out; whole.|The entire building was evacuated. 整个建筑都被疏散了。|近义词：whole, complete, total, full|反义词：partial, incomplete, part|词性:形容词

词源：From Old French *entier, from Latin integer "untouched, whole, complete," literally "not touched," from in- "not" (see in- (1)) + *tag-, root of tangere "to touch."
