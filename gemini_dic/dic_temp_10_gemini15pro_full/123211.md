monocistronic:/ˌmɒnəʊsɪˈstrɒnɪk/ | adj. |单顺反子的|  Of or relating to a type of messenger RNA that can encode only one polypeptide per RNA molecule.  A monocistronic mRNA molecule contains only one open reading frame.| Eukaryotic mRNAs are typically monocistronic. 真核生物的mRNA通常是单顺反子的。

| E.g.,  Bacterial mRNA can be polycistronic, while eukaryotic mRNA is typically monocistronic.  细菌的mRNA可以是多顺反子的，而真核生物的mRNA通常是单顺反子的。 |Synonym:  None (This is a highly specific scientific term) | Antonym: polycistronic |词性: 形容词
