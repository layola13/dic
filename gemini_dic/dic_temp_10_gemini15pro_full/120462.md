minority:/maɪˈnɔːrəti/ | n. | 少数；少数人；少数民族 | A smaller number or part, especially a number that is less than half the whole number. | The ethnic minorities in this region have their own unique customs.  这个地区的少数民族有自己独特的习俗。|  Synonyms:  few, handful, small number | Antonyms: majority, mass, bulk | noun

minority:/maɪˈnɔːrəti/ | adj. | 少数的；少数民族的 | Relating to a minority group. |  Minority languages are often overlooked in education systems. 少数民族语言在教育体系中经常被忽视。| Synonyms: minor, lesser, subordinate | Antonyms: major, dominant, primary | adjective
