hispanicizes:/hɪˌspænɪsaɪzɪz/
verb
使西班牙化，使具有西班牙文化特色
To make Hispanic in character; to acculturate to Hispanic ways.

The menu was hispanicized to appeal to a wider audience.  菜单被西班牙化以吸引更广泛的顾客。

反义词: anglicize
