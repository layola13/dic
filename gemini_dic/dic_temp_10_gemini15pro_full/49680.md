demists:/ˈdiːmɪsts/
n.
除雾者，去雾者
People who or things that remove or disperse mist or fog.
The demists on the car's windshield helped improve visibility. 汽车挡风玻璃上的除雾器有助于提高能见度。
de-foggers, defoggers
foggers, misters
noun
