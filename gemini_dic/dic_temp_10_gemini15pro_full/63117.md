ensile:/ɪnˈsaɪl/ | v. n. | 贮藏, 青贮饲料 | To preserve (green fodder) in a silo or airtight container, typically by partial fermentation.  | The farmer ensiled the corn for winter feed. 农民将玉米青贮以备冬季喂养。 | 近义词: preserve, store, ferment | 反义词: rot, decay | 词性: 动词, 名词

ensile:/ɪnˈsaɪl/ | n. | 青贮饲料 | Fodder preserved in a silo or airtight container. | The cows were fed ensiled corn. 奶牛被喂食了青贮玉米。| 近义词: silage, fodder, feed | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps spoiled feed) | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (put in) + silo.  Ultimately from Spanish *silo, from Latin sirus "pit, trench."
