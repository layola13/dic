inasmuch:/ˌɪnəzˈmʌtʃ/ | adv. | 因为；由于；既然 | to the extent that; since | Example: Inasmuch as you are their commanding officer, you are responsible for their safety. 因为你是他们的指挥官，你要对他们的安全负责。

Example: The evidence is inadmissible inasmuch as it was obtained illegally.  由于这些证据是非法获取的，因此不能被采纳。| 近义词: since, because, as, seeing that, considering that | 反义词: although, though, even though | 词性: 副词
