nicotinism: /ˈnɪkətɪˌnɪzəm/ | n. | 尼古丁中毒；烟瘾 |  Poisoning by nicotine; habitual smoking, especially to excess.  | He suffered from nicotinism after years of smoking. 他多年吸烟后患上了尼古丁中毒。 | 近义词: nicotine addiction, tobacco dependence | 反义词: nicotine abstinence | 词性: noun

词源:  From nicotine + -ism.  Nicotine is named after Jean Nicot, a French diplomat who introduced tobacco to France in the 16th century.
