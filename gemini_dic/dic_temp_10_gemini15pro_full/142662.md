ostracoid:/ˈɒstrəkɔɪd/ | adj. n. |介形虫的；介形亚纲动物|Relating to or denoting ostracods.  A small crustacean of the subclass Ostracoda, which typically has a bivalved carapace.  |例句：Ostracoid fossils are useful in biostratigraphy. 介形虫化石在生物地层学中很有用。

|例句：The ostracoid fauna of this lake is diverse. 这个湖的介形虫动物群是多种多样的。|近义词：ostracod, ostracode |反义词: N/A |词性：adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
