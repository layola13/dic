overhaste:/ˈoʊvərheɪst/ | n. | 过于匆忙，仓促 | Excessive haste; undue speed. | His overhaste caused him to make several mistakes. 他的过于匆忙导致他犯了几个错误。|  The project's failure was due to overhaste.  这个项目的失败是由于过于仓促。| 近义词：rush, hurry, precipitation | 反义词： deliberation, caution, patience | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *overhast*, equivalent to *over-* +‎ *haste*.  由中古英语 *overhast* 构成，相当于 *over-* +‎ *haste*。
