demographer:/dɪˈmɒɡrəfər/ (US) /dɪˈmɒɡrəfə(r)/ (UK)| n. |人口统计学家|A specialist who studies the characteristics of human populations, such as size, growth, density, distribution, and vital statistics.|Demographers predict a decline in the birth rate over the next decade. 人口统计学家预测未来十年出生率将会下降。|Synonym: population scientist, population analyst |Antonym: N/A|noun
demography:/dɪˈmɒɡrəfi/ (US) /dɪˈmɒɡrəfi/ (UK)| n. |人口统计学|The study of statistics such as births, deaths, income, or the incidence of disease, which illustrate the changing structure of human populations.|The demography of the region has shifted dramatically in recent years.  近年来，该地区的人口结构发生了巨大变化。|Synonym: population studies, vital statistics |Antonym: N/A|noun
demographic:/ˌdeməˈɡræfɪk/ (US) /ˌdeməˈɡræfɪk/ (UK)| adj. n. |人口统计学的；人口统计数据|Relating to the structure of populations; a particular sector of a population.|The demographic trends suggest an aging population. 人口统计趋势表明人口老龄化。|Synonym: population, statistical |Antonym: N/A|adjective, noun
