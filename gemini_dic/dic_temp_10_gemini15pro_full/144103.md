outscents:/aʊtˈsɛnts/
verb
胜过…的香味；发出比…更浓烈的香味
surpasses in scent; emits a stronger or more pleasant smell than.
The jasmine outscents the roses in the garden. 栀子花的香味胜过花园里的玫瑰。
近义词:overpowers, overwhelms
反义词: is outscent by
