damon:/ˈdeɪmən/| n. |恶魔，邪恶的人；(Damon)男子名|A demon is an evil spirit or devil, especially one thought to possess a person or act as a tormentor in hell. Damon is also a male given name.|例句：In the TV series "The Vampire Diaries", Damon Salvatore is portrayed as a complex and charismatic vampire. 在电视剧《吸血鬼日记》中，达蒙·萨尔瓦多被描绘成一个复杂而有魅力的吸血鬼。

例句：He behaved like a damon, causing misery to everyone around him. 他的行为像个恶魔，给周围的每个人都带来了痛苦。|近义词：devil, fiend, demon, monster|反义词：angel, saint|词性:noun
