dimple: /ˈdɪmpl/ (US) /ˈdɪmpəl/ (UK)| n.|酒窝；小凹陷|A small natural indentation in the flesh, especially in the cheek or chin.|She has a dimple in her left cheek. 她左脸颊上有一个酒窝。|Near synonyms: indent, indentation, hollow|Antonyms:  bulge, bump|noun

dimple: /ˈdɪmpl/ (US) /ˈdɪmpəl/ (UK)| v.|使起涟漪；使现酒窝|To form dimples or ripples on a surface.|The wind dimpled the surface of the water. 风使水面起了涟漪。|Near synonyms: ripple, pucker|Antonyms:  smooth, flatten|verb
