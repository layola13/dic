granulated:/ˈɡræn.jə.leɪ.tɪd/ /ˈɡræn.jʊ.leɪ.tɪd/
adjective
颗粒状的；成粒的
Formed into grains or granules.
例句：The recipe calls for granulated sugar.  这个食谱需要用到砂糖。

例句：Granulated fertilizer is easier to spread evenly. 颗粒状肥料更容易均匀撒播。

近义词：grainy, granular, particulate
反义词：powdered, fine, smooth
词性：adjective
