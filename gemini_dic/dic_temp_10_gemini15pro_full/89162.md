homely:/ˈhoʊmli/ (US) /ˈhəʊmli/ (UK)| adj.|家常的；朴素的；相貌平平的；不漂亮的|Simple and unpretentious; lacking in physical attractiveness; plain.|例句：She had a homely but kind face. 她有一张相貌平平但很慈祥的脸。|近义词：plain, simple, unattractive, unpretentious|反义词：beautiful, attractive, gorgeous, sophisticated|词性:形容词

homely:/ˈhoʊmli/ (US) /ˈhəʊmli/ (UK)| adj.|舒适的；自在的 (主要用于美式英语)|Suggesting home life and comfort; cozy.|例句：The inn had a homely atmosphere. 这家客栈有一种家的温馨感觉。|近义词：cozy, comfortable, homey, welcoming|反义词：uncomfortable, unwelcoming, austere|词性:形容词
