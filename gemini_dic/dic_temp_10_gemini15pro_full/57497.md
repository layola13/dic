dragger:/ˈdræɡər/| n.|拖拽者；拖拉机；拖网|Someone or something that drags.  A person or thing that drags something else.  A fishing boat that drags a net along the seabed.|The fisherman used a dragger to catch shrimp. 渔民用拖网捕虾。|The old truck was a real dragger up the hill. 这辆旧卡车爬坡时真是个累赘。|近义词：puller, hauler, trawler|反义词: pusher|noun
