culture-shocked:/ˈkʌltʃər ʃɒkt/ (UK) /ˈkʌltʃər ʃɑːkt/ (US)| adj.|文化冲击的|Experiencing confusion and anxiety when encountering a new culture that is very different from one's own.|She felt culture-shocked when she moved from a small village to a big city.|Synonyms: disoriented, bewildered, overwhelmed|Antonyms: accustomed, adjusted, comfortable|Adjective
