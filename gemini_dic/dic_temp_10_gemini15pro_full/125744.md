muppet:/ˈmʌpɪt/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜；提线木偶|A foolish or incompetent person. Originally, a type of puppet used in The Muppet Show.|例句：He acted like a complete muppet, forgetting his passport. 他表现得像个十足的傻瓜，竟然忘了带护照。|例句：Kermit the Frog is a famous muppet. 青蛙克米特是著名的提线木偶。|近义词：idiot, fool, dummy, puppet|反义词:genius, mastermind|词性:noun
