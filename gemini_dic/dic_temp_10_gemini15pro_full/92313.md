hypnotherapy:/ˌhɪpnəˈθɛrəpi/| n. |催眠疗法|A type of therapy that uses hypnosis to create a state of deep relaxation and focused attention.  It aims to access the subconscious mind to address various psychological or physical issues.|She's considering hypnotherapy to help her quit smoking. 她正在考虑用催眠疗法来帮助她戒烟。|Synonyms: hypnosis treatment, suggestive therapy|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could consider conventional therapy, medication)|noun
