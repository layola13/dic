drumbeatings:/ˈdrʌmˌbiːtɪŋz/
n.
击鼓声；（比喻）宣传；鼓噪
The constant drumbeatings announced the arrival of the king.  持续的鼓声宣告了国王的到来。
The media began their drumbeatings about the upcoming election. 媒体开始大肆宣传即将到来的选举。
English Explanation: The sound of drums being beaten, often rhythmically and repeatedly. Figuratively, it refers to insistent and persistent promotion or publicity, often with the connotation of being overly forceful or manipulative.
近义词: drumming, pounding, hammering,宣传: promotion, publicity, hype
反义词: silence, quiet
词性: noun
