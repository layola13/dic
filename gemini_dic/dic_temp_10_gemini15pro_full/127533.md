necromantically: /ˌnɛkrəʊˈmæntɪkli/
Adverb
以招魂术的方式；通过招魂术
Relating to or performed by necromancy; in a necromantic manner.
The sorcerer necromantically summoned the spirits of the dead.  巫师以招魂术召唤死者的灵魂。
Near Synonyms: magically, supernaturally, mystically
Antonyms: naturally, scientifically
词性: 副词
