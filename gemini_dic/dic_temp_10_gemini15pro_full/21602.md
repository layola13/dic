blunderhead:/ˈblʌndərˌhɛd/| n. |笨蛋，傻瓜|A blunderhead is a stupid or foolish person.  It implies someone who makes clumsy mistakes due to lack of thought or intelligence.|He acted like such a blunderhead, leaving the keys in the car overnight.|近义词：fool, idiot, dolt, dunce, numskull|反义词:genius, mastermind|noun
