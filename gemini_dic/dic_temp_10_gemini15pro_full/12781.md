assimilated:/əˈsɪməleɪtɪd/
adj.
吸收了的，同化的
having been absorbed and integrated into a different culture or group.
The immigrants were quickly assimilated into the local community. 移民们很快融入了当地社区。
Synonyms: integrated, absorbed, incorporated, adapted
Antonyms: segregated, isolated, alienated
词性:形容词


assimilate:/əˈsɪməleɪt/
v.
吸收，同化
to absorb and integrate (people, ideas, or culture) into a wider society or culture.
The new students assimilated quickly into the school. 新学生很快融入了学校。
Synonyms: absorb, integrate, incorporate, digest, imbibe
Antonyms: segregate, isolate, reject
词性:动词


assimilation:/əˌsɪməˈleɪʃn/
n.
吸收，同化
the process of absorbing and integrating (people, ideas, or culture) into a wider society or culture.
The assimilation of immigrants into American society is a complex process.  移民融入美国社会是一个复杂的过程。
Synonyms: integration, absorption, incorporation
Antonyms: segregation, isolation, rejection
词性:名词

词源:
From Late Latin assimilatus, past participle of assimilare "to make similar," from Latin ad- "to" + similis "like."
