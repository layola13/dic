morinda:/məˈrɪndə/| n. |海巴戟天属;巴戟天|A genus of flowering plants in the coffee family, Rubiaceae, native to tropical regions of the world.  The most notable species is *Morinda citrifolia*, commonly known as noni.|The morinda plant is known for its medicinal properties. 海巴戟天属植物以其药用价值而闻名。|Morinda citrifolia is cultivated for its fruit.  巴戟天是为了它的果实而种植的。|Synonyms: Indian mulberry, noni|Antonyms: N/A|noun
