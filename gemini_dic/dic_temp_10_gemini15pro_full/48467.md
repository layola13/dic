deemster:/ˈdiːmstər/| n.|审判官，裁判员|A judge, especially formerly on the Isle of Man.|The deemster sentenced him to five years in prison. 审判官判他五年徒刑。|近义词：judge, magistrate, arbiter|反义词:defendant, accused|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *dómstari, from dómr ("judgment") + -stari ("doer"). Cognate with Icelandic dómstjóri ("judge").
