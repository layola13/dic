lost-out: /lɒst ˈaʊt/ (British) /lɔːst ˈaʊt/ (American)| phrasal verb | 输掉; 错失良机 | To fail to get something, such as a job or a prize, that you were competing for, or to fail to benefit from something advantageous. |  He lost out on the promotion to a younger colleague. 他输给了一个更年轻的同事，失去了晋升的机会。 |  She lost out on the chance to study abroad. 她错失了出国留学的机会。 | 近义词: miss out, lose, be defeated | 反义词: win, succeed | 词性: 短语动词
