oxybromic: /ˌɒksɪˈbrəʊmɪk/ | adj. | 氧溴的，含氧溴酸的 | Relating to or containing oxybromine, especially in the +5 oxidation state.  It refers specifically to compounds where bromine has an oxidation state of +5, such as in bromic acid (HBrO₃). | Potassium bromate is an oxybromic compound. 溴酸钾是一种氧溴化合物。|  近义词: bromic (though this can also refer to +3 oxidation state) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective
