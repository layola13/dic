addicted-to: /əˈdɪktɪd tuː/ | adj. | 对…上瘾的，沉迷于…的 |  Physically and mentally dependent on a particular substance, activity, or thing.  | He's addicted to video games. 他沉迷于电子游戏。

She became addicted to painkillers after her surgery. 她手术后对止痛药上瘾了。

| 近义词: hooked on, dependent on, obsessed with | 反义词: indifferent to, averse to | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *addictus* "assigned, devoted, attached," past participle of *addicere* "to assign, award, deliver, devote, sell."  Originally referred to someone legally bound to another as a debtor or slave. The modern sense of being enslaved to a habit or substance developed later.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is addicted to gambling. = 他沉迷于赌博。
She is addicted to social media. = 她沉迷于社交媒体。

一般过去时:
He was addicted to heroin for many years. = 他多年来一直吸食海洛因成瘾。
She was addicted to shopping when she was younger. = 她年轻时沉迷于购物。

现在完成时:
He has been addicted to nicotine for over a decade. = 他吸尼古丁成瘾已经超过十年了。
She has been addicted to exercise since she was a teenager. = 她从青少年时期就沉迷于运动。

过去完成时:
He had been addicted to alcohol before he sought help. = 他在寻求帮助之前一直酗酒成瘾。
She had been addicted to prescription drugs for years before she realized it. = 她多年来一直对处方药上瘾，直到她意识到这一点。


将来时:
If he doesn't stop, he will be addicted to this game. = 如果他不停止，他将会沉迷于这个游戏。
She is worried she will become addicted to these pills. = 她担心自己会对这些药丸上瘾。


虚拟语气:
If I were addicted to anything, it would be coffee. = 如果我对什么东西上瘾的话，那一定是咖啡。
I wish I weren't so addicted to my phone. = 我希望我没有这么沉迷于我的手机。


被动语态: (addicted to 不用于被动语态)


疑问句:
Is he addicted to drugs? = 他吸毒成瘾吗？
Are you addicted to your phone? = 你沉迷于你的手机吗？


否定句:
He is not addicted to cigarettes. = 他不吸烟成瘾。
She is not addicted to anything. = 她对任何东西都不上瘾。
