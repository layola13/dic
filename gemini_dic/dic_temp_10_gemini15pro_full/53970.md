discreetnesses:/dɪˈskriːtnəsɪz/
|n.|谨慎；慎重；言行得体|The quality of being discreet, especially in avoiding social embarrassment or distress; tactfulness.|Her discreetnesses in handling the sensitive situation were admirable. 她处理敏感情况的谨慎态度令人钦佩。|近义词: tact, diplomacy, prudence, circumspection, reserve|反义词: indiscretion, imprudence, carelessness, recklessness|词性:名词
词源: From Middle French *discretesse*, from Latin *discrētus* ("separated, distinct").
