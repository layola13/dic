berserk: /bərˈsɜːrk/, /bərˈzɜːrk/ | adj. n. | 狂暴的，疯狂的; 狂暴状态 |  Frenzied, destructively violent.  Derived from Old Norse *berserkr*, meaning "bear-shirt" or "wolf-shirt", referring to fierce warriors who wore animal skins.  |  He went berserk and started smashing things. 他勃然大怒，开始砸东西。| synonyms: frenzied, enraged, furious, amok, rabid | antonyms: calm, peaceful, tranquil, rational | adjective

berserk: /bərˈsɜːrk/, /bərˈzɜːrk/ | n. | 狂暴状态 | A state of violent or destructive rage. |  He flew into a berserk rage. 他陷入了一种狂暴的愤怒。| synonyms: frenzy, rage, fury | antonyms: calmness, peace, tranquility | noun
