disrobe:/dɪsˈroʊb/
verb
脱衣服，脱去…的衣服
to remove one's clothes or the clothes of another
The king disrobed in his private chamber. 国王在他的私人房间里脱去了衣服。
The nurse disrobed the patient for examination. 护士让病人脱掉衣服以便检查。
synonyms: undress, strip, uncover
antonyms: dress, clothe, robe
词性: 动词
