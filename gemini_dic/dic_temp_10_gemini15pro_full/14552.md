averted: /əˈvɜːrtɪd/ | v. adj. | 避免， averted gaze:躲避的目光|  to turn away or aside; to prevent something bad from happening |  He averted his eyes from the accident scene. 他把目光从事故现场移开。| synonyms: prevent, avoid, preclude, obviate | antonyms:  cause, create, allow, invite | verb

averted: /əˈvɜːrtɪd/ | adj. |  避免的| Turned away; prevented. |  The averted crisis saved the company.  避免的危机挽救了公司。 | synonyms: avoided, prevented | antonyms:  caused, created | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *āversus*, past participle of *āvertere* ("to turn away"), from *ā-* ("away") + *vertere* ("to turn").
