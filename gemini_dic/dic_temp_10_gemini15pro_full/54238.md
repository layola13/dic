disgavel:/dɪsˈɡævəl/
verb
使休会；宣布结束
To formally close a meeting or official gathering.
The judge disgavelled the court after the verdict was read.  法官宣读判决后宣布休庭。
Synonyms: adjourn, conclude, recess
Antonyms: convene, open, begin
词性: 动词


Etymology:  "dis-" (prefix meaning "reversal or undoing") + "gavel" (a small mallet used by a presiding officer to signal for attention or order)
