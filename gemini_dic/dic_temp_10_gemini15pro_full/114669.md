maliciously:/məˈlɪʃəsli/| adv.|恶意地，怀有敌意地|In a way that shows a desire to harm someone or cause them suffering.|He maliciously spread rumors about his rival. 他恶意散布关于他对手的谣言。|She maliciously damaged her ex-boyfriend's car. 她恶意损坏了前男友的车。|近义词: spitefully, viciously, malevolently, wickedly|反义词: kindly, benevolently, charitably|词性: 副词

malicious:/məˈlɪʃəs/| adj.|恶意的，怀有敌意的|Having or showing a desire to harm someone or cause them suffering.|He made malicious remarks about her appearance. 他对她的外貌发表了恶意的评论。|The malicious gossip spread quickly through the office. 恶意的流言蜚语在办公室迅速传播。|近义词: spiteful, vicious, malevolent, wicked|反义词: kind, benevolent, charitable|词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin *malitiosus, from Latin malitia "badness, malice," from malus "bad."
