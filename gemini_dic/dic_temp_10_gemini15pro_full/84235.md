half-shared:/ˌhɑːf ˈʃeərd/ | adj. | 半共享的，部分共享的 | Partly shared; shared between two or more parties but not completely. |  Example 1: They had a half-shared office, meaning they each had their own designated space but shared a common area. 他们有一个半共享的办公室，这意味着他们每个人都有自己的指定空间，但共享一个公共区域。

Example 2:  The twins had a half-shared bedroom, with separate beds but shared toys and a closet.  这对双胞胎有一个半共享的卧室，有单独的床，但共享玩具和衣柜。 | Near Synonyms: partially shared, jointly used, co-used | Antonyms:  privately owned, exclusively used, fully shared | 词性: 形容词
