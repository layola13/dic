heh:/hɛ/ /heɪ/
interjection
嘿嘿; 哼; (表示轻蔑、怀疑或嘲弄)
An interjection used to express amusement, disbelief, or derision.
Heh, I don't think so. 嘿嘿，我不这么认为。
近义词:huh, ha
反义词:None
词性:interjection


heh:/hɛ/ /heɪ/
noun
轻蔑的笑声; 哼声
A short, dry laugh expressing scorn or amusement.
He gave a heh of disbelief. 他轻蔑地哼了一声，表示不信。
近义词:chuckle, snicker
反义词:None
词性:noun
