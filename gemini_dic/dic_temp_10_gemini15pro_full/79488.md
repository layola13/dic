globalizations:/ˌɡloʊbələˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |全球化（复数）| The process by which businesses or other organizations develop international influence or start operating on an international scale.  The increasing interconnectedness and interdependence of countries through trade, technology, and cultural exchange.|  Example 1: The globalizations of markets have created new opportunities for businesses. 全球化的市场为企业创造了新的机会。

Example 2:  The protests highlighted concerns about the negative impacts of globalizations. 抗议活动突出了人们对全球化负面影响的担忧。| 近义词: internationalization, globalization (singular), mundialization | 反义词: localization, regionalization | 词性: noun
