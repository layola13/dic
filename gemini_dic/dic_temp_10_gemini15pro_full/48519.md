deescalate: /ˌdiːˈɛskəleɪt/ | v. | 减少，降低，缓和 | To reduce the intensity or seriousness of something, especially a conflict or difficult situation. | The police tried to deescalate the situation by talking to the protesters. 警察试图通过与抗议者对话来缓和局势。 | The government is working to deescalate the trade war. 政府正在努力缓和贸易战。 | synonyms: reduce, lessen, diminish, lower, moderate, mitigate, alleviate | antonyms: escalate, increase, intensify, aggravate | verb
