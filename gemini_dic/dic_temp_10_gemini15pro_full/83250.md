gymnite:/ˈdʒɪmnaɪt/ | n. | 水镁石，裸镁石 | A hydrous magnesium silicate mineral, typically white, light gray or yellowish, with a greasy luster. It is often found in serpentine rocks and is used as a source of magnesium. | The gymnite deposit was extensive and proved to be a valuable source of magnesium. 这个水镁石矿床很广，被证明是镁的宝贵来源。| Synonyms:  deweylite, sepiolite | Antonyms: N/A | noun
