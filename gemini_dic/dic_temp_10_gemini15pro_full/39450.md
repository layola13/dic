connectivity:/ˌkɒnɛkˈtɪvɪti/ /ˌkɑːnekˈtɪvəti/| n.|连接性，连通性|The state of being connected or interconnected.|例句：The internet connectivity in this area is poor.  这个地区的网络连接很差。

例句：The new highway improved the connectivity between the two cities. 这条新高速公路改善了两个城市之间的连通性。|近义词：connection, linkage, network, accessibility|反义词:isolation, disconnection|词性:noun

Etymology:
From connect + -ivity.  "Connect" comes from the Latin "connectere," meaning "to bind together."  "-ivity" is a suffix forming nouns of quality or state, from French "-ité" and Latin "-itas."
