demonizing: /ˈdiːmənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 妖魔化；将…描绘成恶魔 |  The media is often accused of demonizing certain groups.  媒体经常被指责妖魔化某些群体。|  The politician was demonized by his opponents. 这位政治家被他的对手妖魔化了。 | 近义词: vilifying, denigrating, slandering | 反义词: praising, glorifying, extolling | Verb (present participle)


Etymology:
From demon + -ize + -ing.  Ultimately from Late Latin daemonizare.
