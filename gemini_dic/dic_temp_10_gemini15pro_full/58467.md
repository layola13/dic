ductlike:/ˈdʌktˌlaɪk/
adjective
管状的；类似管道的
Resembling a duct; tube-shaped.
The plant's stem was ductlike, transporting water and nutrients.  这种植物的茎呈管状，输送水分和养分。
The surgeon carefully inserted the ductlike catheter.  外科医生小心地插入了管状导管。
近义词: tubular, pipelike, canal-like
反义词: solid, impermeable
