over-pollinating:/ˈoʊvərˌpɑːləˌneɪtɪŋ/
verb |过度授粉|The act of pollinating a plant excessively, often leading to diminished fruit or seed production.|The farmer worried about over-pollinating his apple orchard by introducing too many bees.  农民担心引入过多的蜜蜂会过度授粉他的苹果园。|Over-pollination can sometimes cause fruit to drop prematurely. 过度授粉有时会导致果实过早掉落。|overfertilizing, oversaturating|under-pollinating|v.
