encysted:/ɪnˈsɪstɪd/ | adj. | 包囊的，被膜包围的 | Enclosed in a cyst or capsule; surrounded by a protective wall. | The doctor found an encysted tumor in the patient's liver. 医生在病人的肝脏里发现了一个包囊性肿瘤。 | The parasite was found encysted in the muscle tissue.  这种寄生虫被发现在肌肉组织中形成了包囊。 | 近义词：encapsulated, enclosed | 反义词：exposed, free | 词性: adjective

词源: From en- + cyst + -ed.  "En-" is a prefix meaning "in" or "within," "cyst" refers to a closed sac having a distinct membrane and developing abnormally in a cavity or structure of the body, and "-ed" forms the past participle and adjective form.
