outlaughed:/ˌaʊtˈlæft/
|v.|笑得比...更大声；以笑声盖过| to laugh longer or louder than someone else|He outlaughed everyone in the room. 他笑得比房间里任何人都大声。|近义词: outdone, surpassed|反义词: subdued, quieted|词性: 动词

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "laughed" (past tense of the verb "laugh").  The prefix "out-" indicates exceeding someone in the act of laughing.
