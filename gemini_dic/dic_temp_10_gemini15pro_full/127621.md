needlefish:/ˈniːdlˌfɪʃ/| n. |颌针鱼，针嘴鱼|A slender marine fish with long jaws forming a beak.|The needlefish darted through the water, snatching a small shrimp. 颌针鱼在水中飞速穿梭，捕获了一只小虾。|近义词：gar, longtom|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific fish. Could use "round fish" as a contrasting shape)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From needle + fish.  The name refers to the fish's long, slender, needle-like jaws.
