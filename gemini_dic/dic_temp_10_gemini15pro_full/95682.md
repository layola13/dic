inconclusively:/ˌɪnkənˈkluːsɪvli/
adverb
不确定地；无结果地
without any definite result
The investigation ended inconclusively. 调查没有得出任何结论。
Synonyms: indecisively, uncertainly, vaguely
Antonyms: conclusively, decisively, definitely
词性: 副词
