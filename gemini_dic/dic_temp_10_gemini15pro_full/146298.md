overfavoring: /ˌoʊvərˈfeɪvərɪŋ/ | v. | 过分偏爱，过度偏袒 | Showing too much favoritism towards someone or something. | The coach was accused of overfavoring his star player. 教练被指责过分偏袒他的明星球员。|  The company was criticized for overfavoring male employees in promotions.  这家公司因在晋升方面过分偏袒男性员工而受到批评。 | 近义词:  偏袒 favoring, showing partiality to,  | 反义词: neglecting, ignoring, slighting | 词性: 动词

Etymology:  "Over-" (prefix indicating excess) + "favoring" (present participle of "favor," from Old French "faveur," ultimately from Latin "favor," meaning "goodwill, kindness").
