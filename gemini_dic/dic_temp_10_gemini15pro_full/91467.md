hylodes:/ˈhaɪləʊdiːz/ | n. |树蛙属,雨蛙属|A genus of frogs in the family Hylodidae. hylodes:/ˈhaɪləʊdiːz/ | adj. |树蛙的，雨蛙的 (rarely used as an adjective) |Relating to or characteristic of the Hylodes genus.| The hylodes habitat is typically humid and forested. 雨蛙的栖息地通常是潮湿和森林茂密的。（这个用法比较罕见，通常用“Hylodes frogs” 或 “frogs of the Hylodes genus”）|Synonyms: arboreal (referring to habitat),  Hylodidae (family name)|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
