Producing or capable of producing only one note at a time.|The Gregorian chant was sung monophonically by the monks.  僧侣们单音地唱着格里高利圣咏。|The old synthesizer could only play monophonically, limiting its musical possibilities.  这台老式合成器只能单音播放，限制了它的音乐可能性。|homophonically, unison|polyphonically|adverb
