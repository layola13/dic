introit:/ˈɪntrɔɪt/ | n. | 入场诗; 开场白; 序曲; 导言 | A psalm or antiphon sung or recited at the beginning of a religious service.  An introductory part, as of a discourse or play. | The introit for the Sunday service was particularly moving.  星期天礼拜的入场诗特别感人。 | synonyms: prelude, preface, introduction, prologue, opening | antonyms: conclusion, epilogue, ending | noun
