bottle:/ˈbɑːtl/ (US) /ˈbɒtl/ (UK)| n. vt.|瓶子；一瓶（的量）；装瓶|A container, typically made of glass or plastic, with a narrow neck, used for storing liquids.|I drank a whole bottle of water. 我喝了一整瓶水。|近义词：container, flask, jar|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用“cap”瓶盖, 表示相反的功能)|noun
bottle:/ˈbɑːtl/ (US) /ˈbɒtl/ (UK)| vt.|装瓶；抑制|To put a liquid into a bottle.|They bottled the wine after fermentation.  发酵后他们把葡萄酒装瓶。|近义词：contain, restrain|反义词:unbottle, release|verb
