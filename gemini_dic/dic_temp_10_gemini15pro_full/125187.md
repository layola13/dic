multicavous:/ˌmʌltɪˈkeɪvəs/
adjective
多孔的，多洞穴的
Having many cavities or hollows.
The multicavous limestone formations provided ideal habitats for bats.  多孔的石灰岩构造为蝙蝠提供了理想的栖息地。
The sponge was multicavous, allowing for maximum water absorption. 这块海绵有很多孔洞，可以最大限度地吸收水分。
porous, cavernous, honeycombed
solid, compact
