cross-chex:/ˈkrɒs tʃɛks/ | n. | 交叉检查，多重核对 | A method of verifying information from two or more sources. |  The police performed a cross-chex on the suspect's alibi. 警察对嫌疑人的不在场证明进行了交叉核对。| Synonyms: cross-check, double-check, verify, corroborate | Antonyms: overlook, disregard | noun

cross-chex:/ˈkrɔːs tʃɛks/ | n. | 交叉检查，多重核对 | A method of verifying information from two or more sources. | The hospital used a cross-chex system to prevent medication errors. 医院使用交叉检查系统来防止用药错误。 | Synonyms: cross-check, double-check, verify, corroborate | Antonyms: overlook, disregard | noun


Etymology:  A compound word formed from "cross" (meaning to intersect or check against something else) and "check" (meaning to verify or examine).  The "x" in "chex" is a stylistic variant.
