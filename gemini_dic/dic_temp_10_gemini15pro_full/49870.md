demotivating:/diːˈməʊtɪveɪtɪŋ/
adjective
令人沮丧的，使人泄气的
Reducing someone's motivation; discouraging.
The constant criticism was demotivating for the team.  不断的批评令团队士气低落。
The lack of recognition for her hard work was deeply demotivating. 对她辛勤工作的缺乏认可让她非常沮丧。
Synonyms: disheartening, discouraging, dispiriting, sapping
Antonyms: motivating, inspiring, encouraging
词性: adjective
