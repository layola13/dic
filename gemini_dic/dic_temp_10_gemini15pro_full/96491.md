indivisibly:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪzɪbli/
adverb
不可分割地
in a way that cannot be divided
例句：The two countries are indivisibly linked. 这两个国家有着不可分割的联系。

例句：Their fates are indivisibly bound together. 他们的命运紧密相连，不可分割。

近义词：inseparably, unitedly
反义词：separately, dividably
词性：副词

词源：From Late Latin *indivisibiliter, from Latin indivisibilis + -iter.  Latin indivisibilis is from in- "not" + divisibilis "divisible".
