addoom: /əˈduːm/
verb
宣判…的命运；注定；判决
To condemn to a certain fate or destiny; to sentence.
The judge addoomd the criminal to life in prison. 法官判处罪犯终身监禁。
He felt addoomd to fail. 他觉得自己注定要失败。
synonyms: condemn, sentence, doom, destine
antonyms: bless, save, redeem
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *addemen*, from Old French *ademer*, from Latin *ad- + damnare* "to condemn, damage".
