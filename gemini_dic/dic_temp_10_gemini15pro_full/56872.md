dos's: /duːz/ /duːzɪz/
|n.|注意事项; 行为准则; 规程|A set of rules or guidelines for how to behave or what to do in a particular situation.  Specifically referring to the positive actions one should take.| Follow these dos's and don'ts for a successful interview.  遵循这些面试成功的注意事项。 |近义词: guidelines, rules, recommendations|反义词: don'ts, prohibitions| Noun

dos's: /duːz/ /duːzɪz/
|n.| (computing, informal, plural of DOS) 磁盘操作系统 (Disk Operating System)| Refers to Disk Operating Systems, especially MS-DOS, a single-user operating system.|  He still uses dos's on some of his older computers. 他的一些旧电脑上仍然使用磁盘操作系统。 |近义词: operating systems (in a limited context)|反义词: N/A| Noun
