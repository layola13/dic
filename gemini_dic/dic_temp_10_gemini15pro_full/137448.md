nonthermoplastic:/ˌnɒnˌθɜːməʊˈplæstɪk/
adjective
非热塑性的
Not capable of being softened by heat and remolded; thermosetting.  A nonthermoplastic material undergoes chemical changes when heated, resulting in a permanently hard and rigid structure.
Epoxy resin is a nonthermoplastic adhesive.  环氧树脂是一种非热塑性粘合剂。
Near synonyms: thermoset, thermosetting
Antonyms: thermoplastic
词性: adjective
