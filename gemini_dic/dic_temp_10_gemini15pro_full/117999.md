memorially:/məˈmɔːriəli/
adv.
作为纪念; 纪念地
in a way that commemorates someone or something.
The day was memorially marked with a parade. 这一天以游行的方式作为纪念。
Synonyms: commemoratively, in remembrance
Antonyms: forgetfully, unmemorably
词性: 副词

memorially:/məˈmɔːriəli/
adv.
值得纪念地; 难忘地
in a way that is memorable or unforgettable.
The event was memorially significant. 这一事件意义非凡，值得纪念。
Synonyms: unforgettably, indelibly
Antonyms: forgettably, insignificantly
词性: 副词
