glossary:/ˈɡlɑːsəri/ | n. |词汇表；术语表|A list of terms in a particular domain of knowledge with their definitions. |Example: The glossary at the end of the book explains all the technical terms.  这本书末尾的词汇表解释了所有的技术术语。|Example:  This medical glossary is very helpful for understanding complex terminology.  这本医学词汇表对于理解复杂的术语非常有帮助。|Synonyms: vocabulary, lexicon, wordbook |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "index" if considered as a broader organizational tool) | Noun
