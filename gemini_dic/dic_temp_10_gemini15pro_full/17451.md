bear-in-mind:/ˌber ɪn ˈmaɪnd/ |v. phr.|牢记，记住|To remember and consider something when making a decision or thinking about a matter.|Please bear in mind that the deadline is next Friday. 请记住截止日期是下周五。|Synonyms: remember, consider, keep in mind|Antonyms: forget, disregard|Verb Phrase

bear-in-mind:/ˌber ɪn ˈmaɪnd/ |v. phr.|考虑到|To take something into account.|Bearing in mind the current economic climate, we have decided to postpone the project. 考虑到目前的经济环境，我们决定推迟这个项目。|Synonyms: consider, take into account, factor in|Antonyms: ignore, neglect|Verb Phrase
