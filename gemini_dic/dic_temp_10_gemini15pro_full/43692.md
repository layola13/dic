creditabilities:/ˌkrɛdɪˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 可信度，可信性 | The quality of being believable or trustworthy.  The sum of characteristics that lead one to believe that a person, institution, or nation is worthy of trust. |  The company's creditabilities suffered after the scandal.  这家公司在丑闻曝光后信誉受损。| synonyms: trustworthiness, reliability, credibility, believability | antonyms: unreliability, untrustworthiness, incredibility | noun
