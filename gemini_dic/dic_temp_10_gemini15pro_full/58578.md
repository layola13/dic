dulcet:/ˈdʌlsɪt/ | adj. | 甜美的，悦耳的 |  pleasing to the ear; melodious.  | Her dulcet tones calmed the crying baby.  她甜美的声音使哭闹的婴儿平静下来。 | synonyms: melodious, harmonious, sweet, soothing | antonyms: harsh, grating, strident | adjective

dulcifluous:/dʌlˈsɪfluəs/ | adj. |  甜美流畅的；悦耳的 |  sweet and flowing, especially of sounds or words. | The dulcifluous melody filled the concert hall.  甜美流畅的旋律充满了音乐厅。 | synonyms: melodious, euphonious, harmonious, sweet | antonyms: harsh, discordant, cacophonous | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dulcifluus*, from dulcis (“sweet”) + fluus, fluere (“to flow”).
