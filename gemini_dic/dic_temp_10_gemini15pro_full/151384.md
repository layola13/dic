parliamentarian:/ˌpɑːrləmənˈtɛəriən/ | n. |议员；精通议事程序的人|An expert in the rules and procedures of deliberative assemblies, such as a parliament.  A member of a parliament. |例句：The parliamentarian advised the speaker on the correct procedure. 议会专家就正确程序向议长提出了建议。

例句：She is a respected parliamentarian known for her eloquence. 她是一位受人尊敬的议员，以雄辩著称。|近义词：legislator, representative, congressman/congresswoman, senator, deputy, MP (Member of Parliament) |反义词: N/A |词性:noun

parliamentarian:/ˌpɑːrləmənˈtɛəriən/ | adj. |议会的；国会的|Relating to or characteristic of a parliament. |例句：Parliamentarian democracy is a system of government in which the legislature holds supreme power. 议会民主制是一种立法机关拥有最高权力的政府体制。

例句：The parliamentarian system in this country is based on the British model. 这个国家的议会制度是仿照英国模式建立的。|近义词：legislative, congressional |反义词: N/A |词性:adjective
