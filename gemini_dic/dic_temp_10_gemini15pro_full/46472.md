daily: /ˈdeɪli/ | adj. adv. n. | 每日的，每天，日报 | happening, done, or produced every day | She reads the daily newspaper. 她每天读日报。 | 近义词：everyday, quotidian | 反义词: weekly, monthly, yearly | 词性: 形容词, 副词, 名词 | 词源: From Middle English *dailich, from Old English dæġlīc (“daily”), from dæg (“day”) + līc (“like”). Cognate with German täglich (“daily”).
