meddler: /ˈmedlər/ (US), /ˈmedlə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 干涉者，爱管闲事的人 | A person who interferes in other people's affairs without being asked or needed. |  Example: He's such a meddler, always sticking his nose in other people's business. 他真是个爱管闲事的人，总是把鼻子伸到别人的事情里。

Example:  The neighborhood meddler was always calling the police about minor infractions.  那个爱管闲事的邻居总是因为一些小违规打电话报警。

| 近义词: busybody, interferer, intruder, interloper | 反义词:  mind their own business | 词性: noun
