fattily:/ˈfætlɪ/
adverb|肥胖地；油腻地|In a fat or fatty manner; excessively or unhealthily fat.|The steak was cooked fattily, swimming in grease.|近义词：greasily, oilily, richly|反义词：leanly, meagerly|词性：副词
词源：From *fat* (adjective) + *-ly* (adverbial suffix).  The word "fat" comes from Old English *fætt*, from Proto-Germanic *fatto-*, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *peyH-*, meaning "to swell".
