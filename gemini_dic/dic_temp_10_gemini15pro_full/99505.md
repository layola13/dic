interfoliaceous:/ˌɪntərˌfoʊliˈeɪʃəs/
adjective
叶间的，生于叶间的
Growing between leaves, especially between the leaflets of a compound leaf.
The interfoliaceous stipules were a key characteristic for identifying the plant species.  这种植物的关键特征是叶间的托叶。
Synonyms: interfoliar
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, consider terms like "terminal," "apical," describing growth at the ends of leaves or stems)
词性: adjective
