nadir:/ˈneɪdɪr/| n. |最低点；最底点；最坏的时刻|The nadir of his career came when he was fired. 他事业的最低谷是他被解雇的时候。|例句：The stock market reached its nadir in March. 股票市场在三月份跌到了最低点。|例句：Her performance reached its nadir in the second act. 她的表演在第二幕达到了最低谷。|近义词：bottom, lowest point, rock bottom|反义词：zenith, peak, apex|词性：名词

Etymology:
From Arabic نظير (naẓīr) "opposite, counterpart," specifically referring to the nadir as the opposite of the zenith.
