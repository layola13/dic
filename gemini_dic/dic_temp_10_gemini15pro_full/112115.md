longsome:/ˈlɒŋsəm/ (UK), /ˈlɔːŋsəm/ (US)| adj.|冗长的，乏味的|Characterized by tedious length or duration; seeming to take a long time.|The journey was longsome and uneventful. 旅途漫长而平淡无奇。|The longsome wait for the bus made me impatient. 漫长的等车时间让我不耐烦了。|近义词: tedious, boring, monotonous, wearisome|反义词: brief, short, quick, interesting|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *langsum, from lang "long" + -sum "-some."  The suffix "-some" forms adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "tending to."
