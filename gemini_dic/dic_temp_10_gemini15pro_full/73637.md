forfeiters:/ˈfɔːrfɪtərz/
n.
没收者；丧失者；违约者
Those who forfeit something; people who lose or give up a right, privilege, or possession, often as a penalty.
The forfeiters of their gaming licenses had to close their casinos.  那些丧失博彩执照的人不得不关闭他们的赌场。
The forfeiters of their deposit lost a significant amount of money.  那些丧失押金的人损失了一大笔钱。
synonyms: losers, defaulters
antonyms: winners, keepers
noun
