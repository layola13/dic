decarbonizations:/diːˌkɑːrbənaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 脱碳; 去碳作用 | The process of reducing or eliminating carbon dioxide emissions.  It refers to the conversion of an economy, a process, or a sector to a low-carbon or carbon-free alternative. |  Many countries are pursuing decarbonizations strategies to combat climate change. 许多国家正在推行脱碳战略以应对气候变化。|  The decarbonization of the transport sector is crucial for achieving net-zero emissions. 交通运输行业的脱碳对于实现净零排放至关重要。 | synonyms: decarbonisation, carbon reduction, emissions reduction | antonyms: carbonization, carbon increase | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From decarbonize + -ation.  Decarbonize is formed from de- (meaning "remove" or "reduce") + carbon + -ize (a verb-forming suffix).
