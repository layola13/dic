histolysis:/hɪˈstɒlɪsɪs/
noun
组织溶解；组织分解
The breakdown of tissues, typically during metamorphosis or atrophy.
The histolysis of the larval tissues makes way for the development of the adult organs. 幼虫组织的溶解为成虫器官的发育铺平了道路。
histolysis of tadpole tail 蝌蚪尾巴的溶解
synonyms: tissue breakdown, tissue degeneration,  catabolism
antonyms: histogenesis, tissue growth, tissue development
n.
