eldritch:/ˈeldrɪtʃ/| adj.|怪异的，恐怖的，超自然的|Strange and sinister, unearthly, weird, eerie, uncanny.  Related to elves and other supernatural beings.|The eldritch glow of the swamp made the travelers uneasy. 沼泽怪异的光芒让旅行者们感到不安。|近义词：weird, eerie, uncanny, otherworldly, supernatural|反义词：normal, ordinary, mundane|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *elriche, *elfriche (“of elves, elvish”), equivalent to elf +‎ -rich.  Originally meant "fairy-like" or "of the elves," but the meaning shifted over time to encompass a sense of unearthly strangeness and horror.
