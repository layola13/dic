nosebag: /ˈnəʊzbæɡ/ (UK) /ˈnoʊzbæɡ/ (US) | n. | 鼻袋；饲料袋 | A bag containing food, typically oats, hay, or other feed, hung from a horse's head so that it can eat.  A nosebag allows a horse to eat while standing or working, without having to be tied to a manger. |  The horse wore a nosebag filled with oats. 这匹马戴着一个装满燕麦的鼻袋。| synonyms: feed bag, fodder bag | antonyms: N/A | noun

nosebag: /ˈnəʊzbæɡ/ (UK) /ˈnoʊzbæɡ/ (US) | v. | 用鼻袋喂 (马) | To feed (a horse) using a nosebag. | He nosebagged his horse before the long journey.  他在长途旅行前给他的马戴上了鼻袋。 | synonyms: feed | antonyms: starve | verb
