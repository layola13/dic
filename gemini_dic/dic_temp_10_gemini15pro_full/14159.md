authorly: /ˈɔːθərli/ | adj. | 像作家的；有权威的；学者风范的 | Of or like an author; authoritative.  Characteristic of an author, especially in terms of knowledge, style, or temperament. |  He had an authorly air about him, constantly scribbling in his notebook.  他身上有一种作家的气质，经常在他的笔记本上涂涂写写。 | 近义词:  learned, scholarly, erudite, authoritative | 反义词:  unlearned, ignorant, amateurish | 词性: 形容词


词源:  Derived from "author" + "-ly" (suffix forming adjectives).  源自“author（作者）” + “-ly”（构成形容词的后缀）。
