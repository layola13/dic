antiquers:/ænˈtiːkərz/
n.
古董收藏家；搜寻古董的人
People who collect or search for antiques.
例句：The antiquers flocked to the flea market, hoping to find hidden treasures.  古董收藏家们涌向跳蚤市场，希望能找到隐藏的宝藏。

例句：Seasoned antiquers know how to spot a genuine antique from a reproduction.  经验丰富的古董收藏家知道如何区分真正的古董和复制品。

近义词：antique collectors, antique hunters
反义词: None (No direct antonym)
词性: Noun
