materiated:/məˈtɪəriəˌlaɪzd/
verb
实现，实体化
to become real or actual; to appear in bodily form
The genie materialized out of the lamp. 精灵从神灯里冒了出来。
Synonyms: appear, emerge, manifest
Antonyms: disappear, vanish, dematerialize
词性：动词
