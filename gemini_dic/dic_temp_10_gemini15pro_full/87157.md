herbless:/ˈhɜːrbləs/| adj.|无草的；无植物的；荒芜的|Lacking herbs or vegetation; barren. |The herbless landscape stretched for miles. 荒芜的景色绵延数英里。|Example: The asteroid impact left a herbless crater. 小行星撞击留下了一个寸草不生的陨石坑。|Synonyms: barren, desolate, infertile, sterile, vegetationless|Antonyms: fertile, lush, verdant|adjective
