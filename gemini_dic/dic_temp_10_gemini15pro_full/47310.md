de-license:/ˌdiːˈlaɪsəns/ |v.| 解除许可，取消执照 | To revoke or remove a license from someone or something, typically a business or activity. | The government decided to de-license the sale of certain firearms. 政府决定取消某些枪支的销售许可。 |  The city de-licensed the taxi company after numerous safety violations.  该市在多次安全违规后吊销了这家出租车公司的执照。 | synonyms: deregulate,  revoke, withdraw | antonyms: license, authorize, permit | verb
