debased:/dɪˈbeɪst/| adj.|堕落的，贬值的，降低的|Reduced in quality or value; morally degraded.  Lowered in status, esteem, quality, or character.|The debased coinage led to rampant inflation. 贬值的货币导致了猖獗的通货膨胀。|Synonyms: degraded, corrupted, lowered, cheapened, adulterated|Antonyms: elevated, exalted, improved, enhanced|adjective
