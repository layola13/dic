nonaromatic:/ˌnɒnˌærəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 非芳香族的 | Not having the special stability or other properties of aromatic compounds, especially not having a planar ring of atoms with delocalized electrons.  A cyclic compound that does not follow Hückel's rule (4n+2 pi electrons). | Cyclohexane is a nonaromatic compound. 环己烷是一种非芳香族化合物。|  This molecule is nonaromatic due to the presence of a sp3 hybridized carbon in the ring.  由于环中存在sp3杂化碳，该分子是非芳香族的。|  non-aromatic, alicyclic | aromatic | adjective
