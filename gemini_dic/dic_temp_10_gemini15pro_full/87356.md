heroics: /hɪˈroʊɪks/ (US) /hɪˈrəʊɪks/ (UK)| n. |英雄行为；英勇事迹|Great courage or daring action, especially when performed in a dramatic or spectacular way.|The firefighter's heroics saved many lives from the burning building. 消防员的英勇行为从燃烧的建筑物中救出了许多生命。|Example:  His heroics during the war earned him several medals.  他在战争中的英雄事迹为他赢得了数枚奖章。|Synonyms: bravery, valor, gallantry, heroism|Antonyms: cowardice, timidity|Noun
