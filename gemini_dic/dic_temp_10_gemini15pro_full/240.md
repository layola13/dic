His abdicative gesture of leaving his crown on the table spoke volumes. 他把王冠留在桌子上的放弃姿态意味深长。 | 近义词：renunciatory, relinquishing, resigning | 反义词：assuming, accepting, usurping | 词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *abdicativus, from abdicat- "renounced, abdicated," past participle stem of abdicare "to renounce, abdicate," from ab- "away, from" + dicare "to proclaim, dedicate," from dicere "to say."
