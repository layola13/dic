co-aration:/koʊˌɛəˈreɪʃən/
noun
共同耕作，共同准备，合作
The act of plowing or tilling land together; joint preparation or working together.
The co-aration of the land by the two families allowed for a larger harvest.  两个家庭共同耕作土地使得收成更多。
The project required the co-aration of several departments. 这个项目需要几个部门的合作。
Synonym: collaboration, cooperation, joint effort
Antonym: individual work, solo effort
