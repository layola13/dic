gallivant:/ˈɡælɪvænt/| v. |漫游，游荡，闲逛|To wander or travel around for pleasure and without a definite purpose.|She spent her gap year gallivanting around Europe. 她利用间隔年在欧洲四处游荡。|近义词：roam, wander, traipse, gad about|反义词:stay, remain|词性:动词
Etymology: From the 18th century, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to gallant.
