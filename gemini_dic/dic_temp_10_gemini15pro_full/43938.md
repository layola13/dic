crews: /kruːz/
noun
全体工作人员；全体船员；队；组
A group of people who work together, especially one that operates a ship or aircraft.
The flight crews were very professional.  机组人员非常专业。
The ship's crew consisted of twenty men. 船上的船员有二十人。
synonyms: team, group, staff, gang, band
antonyms: individual, passenger
