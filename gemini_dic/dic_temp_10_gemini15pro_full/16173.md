bankless:/ˈbæŋkləs/| adj.|无银行的，去中心化的|Operating or existing without reliance on banks or traditional financial institutions.  Decentralized finance (DeFi) aims to create a bankless financial system.|例句：The bankless movement advocates for financial independence from traditional institutions.  去中心化金融运动倡导摆脱传统机构的金融独立性。

例句：Bankless technologies like cryptocurrency and blockchain are disrupting the financial landscape. 像加密货币和区块链这样的无银行技术正在颠覆金融格局。|近义词：decentralized, peer-to-peer, distributed|反义词：centralized, traditional, conventional|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"bank" + "-less"
"bank" (n.) - from Old French "banque" and Italian "banca" (bench, money-changer's table).
"-less" (suffix) - Old English "-lēas," meaning "without," "free from."  Indicates deprivation, absence, or lack.
