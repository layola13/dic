noncontemporary:/ˌnɑːnkənˈtɛmpəˌrɛri/
adjective
非当代的；不同时代的
Not belonging to the same time period; not modern or current.
His noncontemporary views on women's rights caused controversy. 他在女性权利方面的非当代观点引起了争议。
The museum displayed both contemporary and noncontemporary art.  博物馆展出了当代和非当代的艺术。
Synonyms: anachronistic, outdated, old-fashioned, archaic
Antonyms: contemporary, modern, current
词性: 形容词
