lobber:/ˈlɒbə(r) (UK), ˈlɑːbər (US)| n. |高吊球运动员，投掷手|A person who lobs a ball or projectile.  A player who frequently hits or throws a ball in a high arc. |He's a good lobber, always able to get the ball over the net. 他是一个很好的吊球手，总是能把球打过网。|近义词：thrower, pitcher|反义词: catcher|词性: noun

lobber:/ˈlɒbə(r) (UK), ˈlɑːbər (US)| v. |高吊，投掷|To propel (something) with a slow, high arc.|He lobbed the ball over the fence. 他把球扔过了篱笆。|近义词：toss, throw, fling|反义词: catch|词性: verb
