hesitating:/ˈhezɪteɪtɪŋ/| adj.|犹豫的，迟疑的|Inclined to pause in indecision before saying or doing something.|She gave a hesitating smile. 她犹豫地笑了笑。|近义词：uncertain, tentative, reluctant, wavering|反义词：decisive, resolute, confident|词性:形容词
词源: From the Latin word "haesitare" meaning "to stick fast," which evolved to mean "to be undecided" in Late Latin.
