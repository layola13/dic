Derived from the French word "bon" meaning "good".|例句：She had a bonny baby girl. 她生了一个漂亮的宝贝女儿。|例句：The bonny lass danced with joy.  这位美丽的少女高兴地跳着舞。|近义词：pretty, beautiful, handsome, fair, attractive, healthy, robust, cheerful, merry|反义词：ugly, unattractive, sickly, unhealthy, gloomy, sad|词性:形容词
