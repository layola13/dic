alluvial's:/əˈluːviəl/ | adj. | 冲积的；由冲积物形成的 | Relating to or derived from alluvium; deposited by running water. |  The farmer's land was rich with alluvial soil, perfect for growing crops.  农民的土地富含冲积土，非常适合种植农作物。| Synonyms: sedimentary, deposited, fluvial | Antonyms: igneous, volcanic | adjective

alluvium:/əˈluːviəm/ | n. | 冲积层；冲积土 | A deposit of clay, silt, sand, and gravel left by flowing streams in a river valley or delta, typically producing fertile soil. | The river deposited a thick layer of alluvium on the floodplain. 这条河在泛滥平原上沉积了厚厚的一层冲积层。| Synonyms: sediment, deposit, silt | Antonyms: bedrock | noun
