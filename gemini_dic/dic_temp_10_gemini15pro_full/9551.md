antiseptic: /ˌæntiˈsɛptɪk/ | adj. n. | 抗菌的，防腐的；防腐剂，抗菌剂 |  Relating to or denoting substances that prevent the growth of disease-causing microorganisms.  |  The nurse cleaned the wound with an antiseptic solution.  护士用防腐溶液清洗了伤口。 | Synonyms: disinfectant, germicidal, antibacterial | Antonyms: septic, infected | adjective, noun

antiseptics: /ˌæntiˈsɛptɪks/ | n. | 防腐剂，抗菌剂 | Substances that prevent the growth of disease-causing microorganisms. | The doctor prescribed some antiseptics for the infection. 医生给感染开了些防腐剂。 | Synonyms: disinfectants, germicides, antibacterials | Antonyms: contaminants, pollutants | noun
