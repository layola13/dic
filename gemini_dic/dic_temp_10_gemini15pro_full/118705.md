metaconal:/ˌmɛtəˈkəʊnəl/| adj.|后锥体的|Situated behind the metacone (a cusp of a molar tooth).  Relating to or located in the posterior part of the metacone. |The metaconal groove of the upper molar is distinct. 上磨牙的后锥沟很明显。|Near Antonyms: paraconal|Antonyms: protoconal|adjective
