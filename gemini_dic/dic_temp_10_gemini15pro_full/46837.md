dapper:/ˈdæpər/| adj.|衣冠楚楚的；整洁的；漂亮的|Neat and trim in dress and appearance; stylish. |He looked very dapper in his new suit. 他穿着新西装，看起来非常衣冠楚楚。|近义词：stylish, smart, elegant, chic|反义词：scruffy, unkempt, sloppy|词性:形容词
dapper:/ˈdæpər/| adj.|小巧的；活跃的|Small and active; nimble.  (Less common usage) |The dapper little dog darted through the crowd.  那只小巧活泼的狗在人群中飞奔。|近义词：nimble, agile, spry|反义词：clumsy, awkward|词性:形容词
