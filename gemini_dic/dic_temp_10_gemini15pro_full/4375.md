alacritously:/əˈlækrɪtəsli/
| adv. |
| 迅速地；敏捷地；轻快地 |
| Cheerfully and eagerly; quickly and readily.  It implies a brisk and willing readiness. |
| The team worked alacritously to finish the project before the deadline.  团队迅速地工作以在截止日期前完成项目。|

| He alacritously accepted the invitation to speak at the conference.  他欣然接受了在会议上发言的邀请。|
| 近义词：briskly, eagerly, promptly, readily, swiftly |
| 反义词：slowly, reluctantly, sluggishly |
| 词性: 副词 (Adverb) |
