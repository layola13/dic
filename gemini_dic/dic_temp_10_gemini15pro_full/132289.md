nondefensibly:/ˌnɒndɪˈfɛnsɪbli/
adjective
不可辩护地
Unable to be defended, justified, or excused.
His actions were nondefensibly cruel. 他的行为残酷到无法辩护。
近义词: inexcusably, unjustifiably, unwarrantably
反义词: justifiably, defensibly, excusably
词性: adjective
词源: From "non-" (not) + "defensible" (capable of being defended), ultimately from Latin "defendere" (to ward off).
