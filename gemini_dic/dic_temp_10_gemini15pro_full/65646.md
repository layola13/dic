evapone: /ɪˈvæpoʊn/
noun
蒸发物; 蒸发器; (尤指)电子烟
a substance or device that evaporates, especially an electronic cigarette
The evapone produced a thick cloud of vapor.  蒸发器产生了一团浓厚的蒸汽。
The new evapone has a longer battery life.  这款新的电子烟电池寿命更长。

近义词: vaporizer, e-cigarette
反义词:  (无明确的反义词，可以考虑与"燃烧"相关的词语，例如：combustor)
