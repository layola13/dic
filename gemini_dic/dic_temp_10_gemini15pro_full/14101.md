auteurs:/oʊˈtɜːrz/ (US), /ɔːˈtɜːz/ (UK)| n. |作者（尤指电影导演，强调其对作品的个人风格和控制）|A filmmaker who has a distinctive style and control over all aspects of their work, expressing their personal vision. |例句：The French New Wave celebrated auteurs like François Truffaut and Jean-Luc Godard.  法国新浪潮推崇像弗朗索瓦·特吕弗和让-吕克·戈达尔这样的作者导演。

例句：Many consider Quentin Tarantino a modern auteur. 很多人认为昆汀·塔伦蒂诺是一位现代作者导演。|近义词：filmmakers, directors, artists|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "collaborator" in a contrasting sense) |词性: noun
