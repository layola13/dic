conceivers:/kənˈsiːvərz/
n.
构思者，设想者，构想者
Those who conceive of or create something, typically an idea, plan, or invention.
The conceivers of the project were commended for their ingenuity.  这个项目的构想者因其独创性而受到赞扬。
Synonyms: creators, designers, inventors, originators, architects
Antonyms: destroyers, imitators
