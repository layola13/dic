greffier:/ɡrɛfˈjeɪ/ (US), /ˈɡrɛfiə/ (UK)| n.|书记员，记录员|A clerk or registrar, especially one in a court of law.  This term is primarily used in Canadian French and less common in modern English. |The greffier read the verdict aloud in the courtroom.  书记员在法庭上大声宣读了判决。|Synonyms: clerk, registrar, recorder, scribe|Antonyms: N/A|noun
