nonproportional:/ˌnɒn prəˈpɔːʃənəl/ | adj. | 非比例的，不成比例的 | Not proportional; not increasing or decreasing at the same rate. | The relationship between the number of hours worked and the amount earned is nonproportional because of overtime pay.  工作时间和收入之间的关系是非比例的，因为有加班费。| The increase in price was nonproportional to the increase in demand. 价格的增长与需求的增长不成比例。 | 近义词:disproportionate, unequal | 反义词: proportional | 词性:形容词
