atticize:/ˈætɪsaɪz/ | v. | 模仿阁楼风格，使用阁楼方言 | To imitate the style of Attic Greek, especially in the purity and elegance of its language. |  He atticized his prose to give it a classical feel. 他使他的散文雅致化，以赋予它古典的感觉。 | The writer atticized his dialogue to make it sound more authentic.  这位作家使他的对话雅致化，使其听起来更真实。 | 近义词: refine, purify, classicize | 反义词: vulgarize, coarsen | 词性: 动词
