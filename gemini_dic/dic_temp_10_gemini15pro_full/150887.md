paralegal:/ˌpærəˈliːɡl/| n.|律师助理；律师帮办|A person trained to assist lawyers in their work, but who is not a fully qualified lawyer. |The paralegal spent hours organizing the documents for the upcoming trial. 律师助理花了数小时整理即将到来的审判的文件。|Synonyms: legal assistant, legal aide|Antonyms: lawyer, attorney|noun

paralegal:/ˌpærəˈliːɡl/| adj.|律师助理的；律师帮办的|Relating to or involving a paralegal. |She's taking a paralegal course to improve her skills. 她正在参加一个律师助理课程以提高自己的技能。|Synonyms: legal assistant's, legal aide's|Antonyms: lawyer's, attorney's|adjective
