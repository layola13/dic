PHRASE->
bring about 带来，引起
The new CEO brought about significant changes in the company. 新任首席执行官给公司带来了重大变化。

bring down 降低，减少
The government is trying to bring down the inflation rate. 政府正在努力降低通货膨胀率。

bring forward 提出
The committee brought forward several proposals for consideration. 委员会提出了几项建议供审议。

bring in 引进，赚取
The company brought in a new line of products. 公司引进了一条新的产品线。

bring up 抚养，提出
She was brought up by her grandparents. 她是由祖父母抚养长大的。
