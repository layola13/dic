monograph:/ˈmɒnəɡrɑːf/ (US) /ˈmɑːnəɡræf/| n. | 专著；专题论文 | A detailed written study of a single specialized subject or an aspect of it. |  例句：He is the author of several monographs on the history of science. 他是几本科学史专著的作者。

例句：She's writing a monograph on the life cycle of the mayfly. 她正在写一本关于蜉蝣生命周期的专著。

近义词：treatise, dissertation, study, paper | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "overview" or "summary" as a contrasting concept) | 词性: noun

monographs:/ˈmɒnəɡrɑːfs/ (US) /ˈmɑːnəɡræfs/| n. | 专著；专题论文（复数） | Detailed written studies of single specialized subjects or aspects of them. | 例句：The library holds a vast collection of monographs on medieval history.  该图书馆收藏了大量关于中世纪历史的专著。

例句：He has published several monographs in scholarly journals. 他在学术期刊上发表了几篇专题论文。


近义词：treatises, dissertations, studies, papers | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
