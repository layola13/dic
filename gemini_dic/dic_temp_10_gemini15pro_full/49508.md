demagogued: /ˈdɛməɡɒɡd/ (UK) /ˈdɛməɡɔːɡd/ (US)
|v.|被煽动的|influenced or manipulated by a demagogue|The crowd was demagogued into a frenzy.|近义词:manipulated, exploited, swayed|反义词:reasoned with, educated, enlightened|词性:动词

Etymology: From demagogue + -ed.  Demagogue comes from the Greek dēmagōgos "popular leader," from dēmos "people" + agōgos "leading, leader."
