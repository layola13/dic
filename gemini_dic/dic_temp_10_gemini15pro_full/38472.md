conch:/kɒŋk/ (UK) /kɑːŋk/ (US)| n. |海螺; 海螺壳|A tropical marine mollusk with a large spiral shell.|She held the conch to her ear and listened to the sound of the ocean. 她把海螺壳放在耳边，聆听着大海的声音。|Synonyms: seashell, shell|Antonyms: None|Noun

conches:/ˈkɒntʃɪz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntʃɪz/ (US)| n. |海螺（复数）|Plural form of conch, referring to multiple tropical marine mollusks with large spiral shells.|He collected various conches on the beach. 他在海滩上收集了各种各样的海螺。|Synonyms: seashells, shells|Antonyms: None|Noun
