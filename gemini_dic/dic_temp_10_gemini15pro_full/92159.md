hypersocial:/ˌhaɪpərˈsoʊʃəl/
adjective
过度社交的
Excessively sociable; unusually interested in and responsive to social situations and interactions.  Characterized by excessive social interaction or a strong need for social connection.
例句：She's become hypersocial since starting college, constantly surrounded by people. 她自从上大学后变得过度社交，总是周围围绕着很多人。

例句：His hypersocial behavior sometimes overwhelms his quieter friends. 他过度社交的行为有时会让比较安静的朋友感到不知所措。

近义词：gregarious, outgoing, extroverted, sociable
反义词：antisocial, withdrawn, introverted, recluse
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (prefix meaning "over, excessive") + social.  The prefix "hyper-" comes from the Greek "hyper" meaning "over, above, beyond."
