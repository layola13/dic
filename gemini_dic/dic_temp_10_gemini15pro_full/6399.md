amphioxus: /æmfiˈɒksəs/ | n. | 文昌鱼 | A small, translucent, fish-like marine invertebrate that is regarded as similar to the ancestor of the vertebrates. |  Amphioxus is a key organism for studying the evolution of vertebrates. 文昌鱼是研究脊椎动物进化的关键生物。| Amphioxus has a notochord, but no backbone.  文昌鱼有脊索，但没有脊椎。 | lancelet |  (No clear antonym) | noun
