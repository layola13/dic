misconstruct:/ˌmɪskənˈstrʌkt/
verb
误解，曲解；错误地建造，错误地构成
To interpret (something, such as a statement or action) wrongly. To construct or build incorrectly.
The politician's words were misconstrued by the media. 这位政治家的话被媒体曲解了。
The bridge was misconstructed and collapsed. 这座桥建造错误，倒塌了。
misinterpret, misconceive, misbuild, misunderstand
correctly interpret, correctly construct, understand
