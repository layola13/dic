lunation:/ˌluːˈneɪʃən/ |n.|朔望月; 太阴月|The time between one new moon and the next, about 29.5 days.  A complete cycle of the moon's phases.|例句：The rocket launch is scheduled for the next lunation. 火箭发射定于下一个朔望月。

例句：Ancient calendars were often based on lunations. 古代的日历通常是基于朔望月的。|近义词：synodic month, lunar month|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
