dog: /dɒɡ/ (UK) /dɔːɡ/ (US) | n. | 狗 | A domesticated carnivorous mammal that typically has a long snout, an acute sense of smell, and a barking, howling, or whining voice.  It is widely kept as a pet or for work or sport. |  My dog loves to play fetch. 我的狗喜欢玩取物游戏。 | synonyms: canine, hound, puppy, pooch | antonyms: cat (informal, as a contrasting pet) | noun


dog: /dɒɡ/ (UK) /dɔːɡ/ (US) | vt. |  尾随，纠缠 | To follow someone closely and persistently. |  The paparazzi dogged the celebrity relentlessly. 狗仔队无情地跟踪着这位名人。| synonyms: pursue, hound, shadow, trail | antonyms: avoid, evade, leave | verb
