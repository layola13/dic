daaj:/dɑːʒ/
noun
day judge (in Somalia)
A daaj is a respected elder who acts as a judge in Somali customary law.
The daaj listened carefully to both sides of the dispute before making his ruling.  达伊仔细听取了争议双方的意见，然后才做出裁决。
Synonyms: judge, arbitrator, mediator
Antonyms: defendant, plaintiff
词性: noun

daaj:/dɑːʒ/
verb
to judge (in Somalia)
To daaj is to act as a judge in Somali customary law.
The elders daajed the case fairly and impartially. 长老们公正无私地审理了这个案件。
Synonyms: judge, adjudicate, arbitrate
Antonyms: acquit, pardon
词性: verb
