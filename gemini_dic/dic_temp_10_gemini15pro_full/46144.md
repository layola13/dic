cymophane:/ˈsaɪməˌfeɪn/ | n. | 金绿宝石，猫眼石 | A variety of chrysoberyl that exhibits chatoyancy (a silky sheen and a narrow band of light that resembles a cat's eye). It is a precious gemstone. |  The cymophane ring sparkled under the lights. 金绿宝石戒指在灯光下闪闪发光。|  The queen's cymophane brooch was a family heirloom. 女王的猫眼石胸针是一件传家宝。 |  chrysoberyl cat's-eye  |   | noun
