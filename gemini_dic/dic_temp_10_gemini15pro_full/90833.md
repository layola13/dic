hundred:/ˈhʌndrəd/ (US) /ˈhʌndrɪd/ (UK)| num. n. adj.|百，一百，许多|A cardinal number, the product of ten and ten; 100.  Also used to mean a large but unspecified number.|There are a hundred students in the class. 班里有一百个学生。|近义词：century, one hundred|反义词: (No direct antonym for a number)|词性: Numeral, Noun, Adjective

hundred:/ˈhʌndrəd/ (US) /ˈhʌndrɪd/ (UK)| n.|百，一百|A group or set of one hundred things.|He bought hundreds of stamps. 他买了数百张邮票。|近义词：century, one hundred|反义词: (No direct antonym for a number)|词性: Noun
