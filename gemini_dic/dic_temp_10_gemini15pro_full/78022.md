gentries:/ˈdʒentriːz/
n.
贵族阶层，绅士阶层
The landed gentry of England historically held considerable power. 
英国的土地绅士阶层历史上拥有相当大的权力。
The gentries were known for their refined manners and elegant lifestyle.
贵族阶层以其优雅的举止和优雅的生活方式而闻名。
Synonyms: aristocracy, elite, nobility, upper class
Antonyms: commoners, peasantry, working class
词性: noun
