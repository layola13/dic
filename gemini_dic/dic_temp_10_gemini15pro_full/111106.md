literal:/ˈlɪtərəl/ /ˈlɪtrəl/| adj.|字面的，逐字的，不夸张的，完全的|Conforming or limited to the literal meaning of a word or text.  Actual, real, complete.|He gave a literal translation of the text. 他对文本进行了逐字翻译。|Synonyms: exact, precise, verbatim, accurate|Antonyms: figurative, metaphorical, symbolic|adjective
