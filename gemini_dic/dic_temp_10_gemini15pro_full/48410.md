dedicatory:/ˈdedɪkətɔːri/
adjective
奉献的；献给…的
Expressing or relating to dedication.
This concert is dedicatory to the memory of the victims.这场音乐会是献给遇难者们的。
The dedicatory plaque on the wall commemorates the founder. 墙上的题词匾是为了纪念创始人。
近义词:commemorative, honoring, tribute
反义词:unremarkable, ordinary
词性:adjective


dedication:/ˌdedɪˈkeɪʃən/
noun
奉献，献身；落成典礼；题词
The quality of being dedicated or committed to a task or purpose.  A ceremony in which something, such as a building, is officially opened or given a special purpose.  A name and inscription written in a book or work of art.
The teacher's dedication to her students was inspiring. 这位老师对学生的奉献精神令人鼓舞。
The dedication of the new hospital was attended by many dignitaries. 新医院的落成典礼有很多要人参加。
He wrote a touching dedication in his book to his wife. 他在书里写了一段感人的题词献给他的妻子。
近义词:commitment, devotion, inauguration, inscription
反义词:neglect, apathy
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dedicatorius, from dedicare "to dedicate," from de- "down, completely" + dicare "to proclaim, dedicate."
