hingeless:/ˈhɪndʒləs/| adj.|无铰链的，无枢纽的|Lacking a hinge or hinges; not hinged.  It describes something that doesn't have a hinge, a movable joint or mechanism that allows two parts to rotate or pivot relative to each other.|The hingeless laptop opened smoothly. 这款无铰链的笔记本电脑可以平滑地打开。|近义词：jointless, seamless, fixed|反义词: hinged, jointed|词性:形容词
