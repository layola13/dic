lemon:/ˈlɛmən/ | n. | 柠檬；柠檬黄；傻瓜 | A yellow, oval citrus fruit with thick skin and sour juice.  Something that is defective or unsatisfactory. |

例句：
I like to add lemon to my tea.
我喜欢在我的茶里加柠檬。

例句：
He bought a used car that turned out to be a lemon.
他买了一辆二手车，结果却是个次品。

近义词：citron, lime | 反义词:sweet | 词性:noun


lemon:/ˈlɛmən/ | adj. | 柠檬色的 | Having the pale yellow color of a lemon. |

例句：
She wore a lemon-colored dress.
她穿了一件柠檬黄的连衣裙。

例句：
The walls were painted a soft lemon.
墙壁被漆成了柔和的柠檬色。


近义词：yellowish | 反义词: - | 词性:adjective
