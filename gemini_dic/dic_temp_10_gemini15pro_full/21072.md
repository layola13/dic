blindnesses:/ˈblaɪndnɪsɪz/
n.
失明，盲目（复数）
The state of being unable to see, often used in the plural to refer to multiple instances or types of blindness.
He suffered from several blindnesses, including color blindness and night blindness. 他患有几种失明症，包括色盲和夜盲症。

blindnesses:/ˈblaɪndnɪsɪz/
n.
无知，愚昧（复数）
A lack of understanding or perception.
Her blindnesses to the political realities made her an ineffective leader. 她对政治现实的无知使她成为一个无能的领导者。


近义词:sightlessness, vision impairment (for physical blindness); ignorance, unawareness (for metaphorical blindness)
反义词:sight, vision (for physical blindness); awareness, knowledge (for metaphorical blindness)
词性:noun
