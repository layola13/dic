overnegligent: /ˌoʊvərˈnɛɡlɪdʒənt/
adjective
过度疏忽的；极度玩忽职守的
Excessively negligent; failing to take proper care in a way that shows extreme carelessness.
The overnegligent security guard allowed the thief to walk right out with the stolen goods.  过度疏忽的保安让小偷拿着赃物大摇大摆地走了出去。

The judge ruled that the company was overnegligent in its handling of hazardous materials.  法官裁定该公司在处理危险材料方面玩忽职守。

Synonym: grossly negligent, extremely careless, derelict, remiss
Antonym: careful, diligent, attentive, meticulous
词性: adjective
