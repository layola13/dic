beat-hollow:/biːt ˈhɒləʊ/ (UK) /biːt ˈhɑːloʊ/ (US)| adj.|击败的，彻底失败的；空洞的，无意义的|Defeated utterly; empty, meaningless.  It describes a feeling of emptiness and defeat, often after a significant loss or setback.  It combines the sense of being defeated ("beat") with a feeling of hollowness or lack of substance.|The team felt beat-hollow after their crushing defeat in the final.  球队在决赛中惨败后感到彻底被打垮了，内心空虚。| The once vibrant city felt beat-hollow after the war.  这座曾经充满活力的城市在战后显得空洞无意义。|Synonyms: defeated, crushed, empty, meaningless|Antonyms: victorious, triumphant, fulfilled, meaningful|adjective
