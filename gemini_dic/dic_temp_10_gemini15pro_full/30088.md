ceased: /sist/ | v. | 停止，终止 | to stop happening or existing; to stop doing something | The rain ceased and the sun came out. 雨停了，太阳出来了。| The company ceased operations last year.  这家公司去年停止了运营。 | synonyms: stopped, ended, finished, concluded, terminated | antonyms: started, began, commenced, initiated | verb
