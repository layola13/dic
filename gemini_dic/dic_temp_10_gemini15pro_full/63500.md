enubilous:/ɪˈnjuːbɪləs/| adj.|晴朗的，无云的|Free from clouds; clear.  Derived from Latin "e/ex" (out of) + "nubes" (cloud).  Meaning "out of the clouds".|The enubilous sky was a brilliant blue. 晴朗的天空是一片灿烂的蓝色。|Synonyms: clear, cloudless, bright, sunny|Antonyms: cloudy, overcast, gloomy|adjective
