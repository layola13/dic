gaudish:/ˈɡɔːdɪʃ/| adj.|俗丽的；华而不实的；花哨的|Excessively showy or ornamented; tastelessly bright and elaborate.|Her gaudish dress was inappropriate for the funeral. 她那身俗艳的衣服不适合参加葬礼。|The carnival was a riot of gaudish colours and costumes. 嘉年华会上到处都是花哨的色彩和服装。|近义词：garish, flashy, ostentatious, flamboyant|反义词：understated, subtle, elegant, refined|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *gaudysshe, from gaude (“a trinket, a showy ornament”) + -ish.
