constructiveness:/kənˈstrʌktɪvnəs/
n.|建设性|The quality of being helpful and intended to improve something.|Her criticism was always delivered with constructiveness, aiming to help rather than to hurt.|近义词:positivity, helpfulness, usefulness|反义词:destructiveness, negativity|词性:名词
Etymology: From constructive + -ness.  Constructive from construct + -ive. Construct from Latin *constructus, past participle of construere "to heap up, build," from com- "together" + struere "to pile, build."
