parasceve:/ˌpærəˈsiːvi/| n.|准备日，逾越节前一日|The Parasceve is the day of preparation before the Sabbath, especially the Friday before Easter. 准备日是安息日前的准备日，尤指复活节前的星期五。|例句：The women bought spices on the Parasceve to anoint Jesus' body. 妇女们在准备日买了香料来涂抹耶稣的遗体。

例句：The crucifixion of Jesus occurred on the Parasceve. 耶稣受难发生在准备日。|近义词：Eve, preparation day|反义词:None|词性:noun
