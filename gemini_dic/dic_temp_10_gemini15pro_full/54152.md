disensanity: /dɪsɪnˈsænɪti/
n.
精神错乱，疯狂
The state of being mentally unsound or deranged; insanity.
His descent into disensanity was a slow and tragic process. 他逐渐陷入疯狂是一个缓慢而悲惨的过程。
近义词：insanity, madness, lunacy
反义词：sanity, lucidity, rationality
词性: 名词

词源：  "Disensanity" is a relatively recent coinage, likely formed by blending "dis-" (a prefix indicating negation or reversal) with "insanity."  "Insanity" itself comes from the Latin "insanitas," meaning "unhealthiness," particularly of the mind.
