bye-laws:/ˈbaɪlɔːz/
noun
规章制度；细则
A set of rules made by a local authority or organization to regulate its members or the area under its control.
The club has strict bye-laws regarding membership fees.  这个俱乐部有关于会员费的严格规章制度。
regulations, rules, ordinances
constitution
n.
