hallow:/ˈhæləʊ/ (UK) /ˈhæloʊ/ (US)| vt.  adj. n.|使神圣；把…视为神圣；尊敬；神圣的；圣徒；神圣的东西|To make holy; to regard as holy; to venerate.  Holy; consecrated.
