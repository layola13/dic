geotic:/dʒiˈɒtɪk/
adjective
生命的，生物的；与生命有关的
Relating to life or living organisms; biological.
The geotic factors of the environment include the plants and animals that inhabit it.  这个环境的生物因素包括栖息在其中的动植物。
The researchers studied the geotic components of the soil. 研究人员研究了土壤的生物成分。
近义词:biotic, living, organic
反义词:abiotic, nonliving, inorganic
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *gē* (earth) + *-ōtēs*  (suffix forming nouns indicating states, qualities).  Ultimately related to "geo-" (earth) and "-otic" (pertaining to).
