bellar:/ˈbɛlər/| n.|战争贩子，好战者|A person who advocates war or enjoys fighting; a warmonger.|He was known as a bellar, always eager for a fight. 他被称为好战分子，总是渴望战斗。|近义词：warmonger, hawk, jingoist|反义词：pacifist, dove|词性:名词

词源: From Latin *bellator* ("warrior"), from *bellare* ("to wage war"), from *bellum* ("war").
