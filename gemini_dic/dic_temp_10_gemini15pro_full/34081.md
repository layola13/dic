civility:/səˈvɪləti/| n.|礼貌，客气，端庄|Politeness and courtesy in behavior or speech.  The act of being civil.|Example:  She treated everyone with civility, even those she disliked. 她对每个人都很客气，即使是她不喜欢的人也是如此。

Example: He was known for his civility and respect for others. 他以待人彬彬有礼和尊重他人而闻名。|近义词：courtesy, politeness, respect, decorum|反义词：rudeness, impoliteness, disrespect, incivility|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *civilitee*, from Old French *civilité*, from Latin *cīvīlitās* ("citizenship, courtesy"), from *cīvīlis* ("civil"), from *cīvis* ("citizen"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱey-*.
