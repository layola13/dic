hyperclassicalities:/ˌhaɪpərˌklæsɪˈkælɪtiz/
| n. | 过度古典主义，极端古典主义 |  Extreme adherence to classical principles or styles, often to an excessive or exaggerated degree.  This word emphasizes an excessive or affected adherence to classicism. |  The architect's designs, with their rigid symmetry and overabundance of Doric columns, bordered on hyperclassicalities.  这位建筑师的设计，以其严格的对称性和过多的多立克柱式，近乎于过度古典主义。

The museum's collection, while impressive, sometimes veered into hyperclassicalities, neglecting more modern and innovative works.  这家博物馆的藏品虽然令人印象深刻，但有时会走向极端古典主义，而忽略了更现代和更具创新性的作品。 | 近义词:  formalism, traditionalism, purism | 反义词: modernism, avant-gardism | 词性: noun (plural)
