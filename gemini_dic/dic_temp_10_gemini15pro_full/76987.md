gardenable:/ˈɡɑːrdənəbl/ | adj. | 可耕种的，适合做花园的 | Suitable for cultivation or use as a garden. | This soil is rich and gardenable. 这片土壤肥沃，适合耕种。| The area behind the house is gardenable.  房子后面的区域可以做成花园。|近义词: cultivable, arable |反义词: barren, infertile |词性: adjective

词源: From "garden" + "-able". "Garden" comes from Old North French "gardin" (modern French "jardin"), from Frankish "*gardo" meaning "enclosure".  "-able" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being", "fit to be", "liable to be", from Old French "-able" and directly from Latin "-abilis".
