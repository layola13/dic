outthundering:/ˌaʊtˈθʌndərɪŋ/
verb
胜过雷鸣般的声音; 以更大的声音压倒
overpowering or surpassing in sound, especially thunder
The roar of the jet engine outthunderinged the storm. 喷气式发动机的轰鸣声盖过了暴风雨的声音。
Synonyms: drowning out, overpowering, exceeding
Antonyms: muffling, softening, quieting
词性: 动词
