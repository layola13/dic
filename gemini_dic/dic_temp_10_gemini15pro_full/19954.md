biodynamical:/ˌbaɪoʊdaɪˈnæmɪkəl/
adjective
生物动力学的
Relating to or involving the application of biodynamic principles, especially in agriculture.  Characterized by or relating to the dynamic processes within living organisms.
Biodynamic agriculture views the farm as a self-regulating ecosystem. 生物动力农业将农场视为一个自我调节的生态系统。
The researchers studied the biodynamical properties of the human heart. 研究人员研究了人类心脏的生物动力学特性。
Near Synonyms:  biological, organic, ecological
Antonyms: inorganic, synthetic, artificial
