mis-cite:/ˌmɪsˈsaɪt/
verb
错误引用；误引
to quote incorrectly; to attribute a text, statement, or idea to the wrong author or source
He miscited Shakespeare in his essay. 他在他的文章中错误地引用了莎士比亚。
misquote, misrepresent
cite correctly, quote accurately
v.
