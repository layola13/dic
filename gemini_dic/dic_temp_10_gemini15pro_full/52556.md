diffractive:/dɪˈfræk.tɪv/ |adj.|衍射的；绕射的|Relating to or exhibiting diffraction (the spreading of waves, such as light or sound, when they pass through an aperture or around an obstacle).|The diffractive properties of the grating created a rainbow pattern. 光栅的衍射特性创造了彩虹图案。| The diffractive element in the lens improves image quality. 透镜中的衍射元件提高了图像质量。|近义词: scattering, dispersive|反义词: focused, concentrated|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From diffraction + -ive.  Diffraction comes from Latin diffractus, past participle of diffrangere "to break apart," from dis- "apart" + frangere "to break."
