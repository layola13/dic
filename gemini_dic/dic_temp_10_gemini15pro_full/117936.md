memberess: /ˈmɛmbərlɛs/
noun
无成员；没有成员
lacking members; having no members
The club is currently memberless due to lack of interest.  这个俱乐部目前由于缺乏兴趣而没有成员。
The organization became memberless after the scandal.  丑闻之后，该组织没有了成员。
synonyms: empty, vacant, deserted
antonyms: full, crowded, populated
词性: 名词
