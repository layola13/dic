acetabuliferous: /ˌæsɪtæbjʊˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
具髖臼的；有髖臼的
Bearing acetabula (the cup-shaped sockets of the hip joints).  Specifically used in zoology and paleontology to describe animals possessing such structures.
This dinosaur fossil displays an acetabuliferous structure, indicating it was capable of bipedal locomotion. 这恐龙化石展示了一个具髋臼的结构，表明它能够双足行走。
The acetabuliferous region of the pelvis is crucial for weight-bearing and movement.  骨盆的髋臼区域对于承重和运动至关重要。
近义词:  None (highly specific term)
反义词:  None (highly specific term)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acētabulum* ("vinegar cup," hence "hip socket") + *-fer* (carrying, bearing) + *-ous* (adjective ending).  The Latin *acētabulum* itself comes from *acetum* (vinegar) + *-bulum* (diminutive suffix, indicating a small vessel).


USAGE->
