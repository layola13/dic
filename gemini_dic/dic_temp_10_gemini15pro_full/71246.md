fitweed:/ˈfɪtˌwiːd/
n.
合欢草，夜合草，感应草
A sensitive plant; *Mimosa pudica*.  Also called shameplant.
The fitweed closed its leaves when I touched it.  当我触碰合欢草时，它合上了叶子。

近义词:sensitive plant, shameplant
反义词:(None -  as it's a specific plant name, it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性:noun
