crambo:/ˈkræmboʊ/
n.
字谜游戏，押韵游戏
A game in which one player gives a word or line of verse, to which each of the others has to find a rhyme.
例句：They played crambo to pass the time on the long journey. 他们在长途旅行中玩字谜游戏打发时间。

例句：The children enjoyed a game of crambo after dinner.  孩子们晚饭后玩了一场字谜游戏。

近义词：rhyming game, word game
反义词: (No direct antonym for a game)
词性: noun
