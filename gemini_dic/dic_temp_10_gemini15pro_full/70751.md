finedrawn:/ˈfaɪnˈdrɔːn/| adj.|精细的，细致的，微妙的|Subtle and delicate, often excessively so; overrefined.|The lawyer's finedrawn arguments left the jury confused.|近义词：subtle, delicate, nuanced, refined, sophisticated, elaborate, intricate, complex|反义词：crude, rough, simple, obvious, unsubtle, coarse|词性:形容词
词源：From "fine" (meaning excellent, pure, or thin) + "drawn" (past participle of "draw," meaning to pull or stretch).  Originally referred to finely spun thread or wire.  The metaphorical sense of "subtle" or "overrefined" developed later.
