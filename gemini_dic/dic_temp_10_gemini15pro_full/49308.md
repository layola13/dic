delightable:/dɪˈlaɪtəbl/| adj.|令人愉悦的，可爱的|Capable of giving delight; very pleasing.  Something that is likely to cause someone to feel happy or satisfied.|The puppy was so delightable with its playful antics. 这只小狗的顽皮滑稽动作真是可爱极了。|近义词：charming, delightful, pleasant, enjoyable|反义词：unpleasant, disagreeable, displeasing|词性:形容词
词源: From delight + -able.  "Delight" comes from the Old French "deleiter," meaning "to please greatly."
