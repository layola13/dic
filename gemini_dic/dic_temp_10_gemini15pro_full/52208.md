diastolic:/ˌdaɪəˈstɒlɪk/ (US) /ˌdaɪəˈstɑːlɪk/ (UK)| adj.|舒张的|Relating to the phase of the heartbeat when the heart muscle relaxes and allows the chambers to fill with blood.|The doctor measured her diastolic blood pressure. 医生测量了她的舒张压。|Near synonyms: relaxation, expansion|Near antonym: systolic|adjective

diastole:/daɪˈæstəli/ (US) /daɪˈæstəli/ (UK)| n.|舒张期|The phase of the heartbeat when the heart muscle relaxes and allows the chambers to fill with blood.|The diastole phase is important for the heart to rest and refill.  舒张期对于心脏休息和充盈至关重要。|Near synonyms: relaxation phase|Near antonym: systole|noun
