magical:/ˈmædʒɪkl/| adj.|神奇的，不可思议的，迷人的|Having the qualities of magic; enchanting.|例句：The sunset painted the sky in magical colors. 夕阳给天空染上了神奇的色彩。

例句：The children were captivated by the magician's magical performance. 孩子们被魔术师神奇的表演迷住了。


|近义词：enchanting, mystical, wondrous, miraculous, supernatural|反义词：ordinary, mundane, common, real, natural|词性:形容词

词源->

From Middle English *magical*, from Late Latin *magicālis*, from Latin *magicus* (“magic, magical”) + *-ālis*.
