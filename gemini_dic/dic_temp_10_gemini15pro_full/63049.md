enriching: /ɪnˈrɪtʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 使丰富的，充实的 |  Making something better or more rewarding, often by adding something to it. |  例句：Traveling is an enriching experience. 旅行是一种丰富的体验。| 近义词：rewarding, fulfilling, stimulating, enhancing | 反义词：impoverishing, depleting |词性:形容词
