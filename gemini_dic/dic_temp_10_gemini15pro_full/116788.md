mavis:/ˈmeɪvɪs/| n.|画眉鸟；女子名|A common songbird (Turdus philomelos) known for its beautiful song, also used as a girl's name.|例句：I heard a mavis singing in the woods this morning. 我今天早上听到树林里有一只画眉在歌唱。|例句：Mavis is a popular name in some parts of England. 梅维斯在英格兰某些地区是一个很受欢迎的名字。|近义词：songbird, thrush|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *mauvis, from the same Germanic source as Old High German *meiwiz. Related to German Meise "titmouse." The original meaning referred to a type of thrush, and the use as a given name is more recent.
