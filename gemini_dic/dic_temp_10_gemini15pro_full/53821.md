disconnecters:/ˌdɪskəˈnɛktərz/
|n.|断开器，断路器|A device that disconnects a part of a circuit, especially a high-voltage circuit.|The disconnecters were used to isolate the faulty section of the power grid.|近义词：isolators, switches, circuit breakers|反义词：connectors|词性: 名词

disconnecter:/ˌdɪskəˈnɛktər/
|n.|断开装置，断开机构|A device or mechanism that disconnects something.|The disconnecter in the fuel line prevented further leakage.|近义词：separator, disconnector, uncoupler|反义词：connector, coupler|词性: 名词
