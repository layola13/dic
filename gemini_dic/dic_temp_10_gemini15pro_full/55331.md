dissuades:/dɪˈsweɪdz/
|v.|劝退，阻止， dissuade (someone) from (something or doing something) |To persuade someone not to do something.|
My friend dissuaded me from quitting my job. 我的朋友劝我不要辞职。

The heavy rain dissuaded us from going to the park.  大雨阻止了我们去公园。
|近义词：deter, discourage, prevent |反义词：persuade, encourage, urge |词性: 动词
