addeem:/əˈdiːm/
verb
认为;视为
to consider or judge something in a particular way
The committee addeemed it prudent to postpone a decision.  委员会认为推迟决定是明智的。
He addeemed that it was time to leave. 他认为该走了。
近义词: consider, regard, judge, deem
反义词: disregard, ignore
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *ademen*, from Old French *aesmer*, from Late Latin *ad-aestimō* (“to estimate, value”), from Latin *ad* (“to”) + *aestimō* (“to estimate, value”).
