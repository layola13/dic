exuberant:/ɪɡˈzjuːbərənt/| adj.|繁茂的，丰富的；热情洋溢的；兴高采烈的|Full of energy, excitement, and cheerfulness.|例句：The children were exuberant when they opened their presents. 孩子们打开礼物时欣喜若狂。|近义词：enthusiastic, lively, energetic, vibrant, cheerful|反义词：depressed, subdued, apathetic, listless|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *exuberant- (stem of exuberāns), present participle of exuberāre 'to be abundant, overflow,' from ex- 'out' + über 'udder, teat' + -āre (frequentative suffix). The original sense was 'luxuriant in growth.'
