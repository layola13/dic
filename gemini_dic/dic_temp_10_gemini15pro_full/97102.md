infests:/ɪnˈfɛsts/| v. |侵扰，骚扰，寄生于|to be present in large numbers, typically so as to cause damage or disease|例句：The barn was infested with rats. 谷仓里老鼠成灾。

例句：Head lice infest the hair and scalp. 头虱寄生在头发和头皮上。|近义词：overrun, swarm, plague, beset|反义词：clear, cleanse, purify|词性：动词
