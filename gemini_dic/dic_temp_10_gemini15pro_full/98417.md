insouciantly: /ɪnˈsuːsiəntli/ | adv. | 漫不经心地，无忧无虑地 | In a carefree and unconcerned manner; without worry or anxiety.  | He insouciantly tossed the ball in the air. 他漫不经心地把球抛向空中。| She insouciantly strolled through the park, enjoying the sunshine. 她漫不经心地漫步穿过公园，享受着阳光。 | 近义词: nonchalantly, carelessly, lightheartedly | 反义词: anxiously, worriedly, seriously | 词性: 副词

insouciant: /ɪnˈsuːsiənt/ | adj. | 漫不经心的，无忧无虑的 | Showing a casual lack of concern; indifferent. | He had an insouciant air about him. 他带着一种漫不经心的气质。 |  She displayed an insouciant disregard for the rules. 她表现出对规则漫不经心的漠视。 | 近义词: nonchalant, carefree, unconcerned | 反义词: anxious, worried, concerned | 词性: 形容词

词源: From French *insouciant*, from *in-* (not) + *souciant* (caring), from *soucier* (to care), from Latin *sollicitare* (to disturb, agitate).
