do-goodery:/ˈduːˌɡʊdəri/
noun
做好事，慈善行为（常带贬义）
The act of doing good, often in a way that is perceived as self-righteous or ineffective.
She dismissed his charitable work as mere do-goodery. 她认为他的慈善工作不过是沽名钓誉。
近义词: philanthropy, charity, altruism
反义词: malevolence, malice, selfishness
词性: noun

do-goodery: /ˈduːˌɡʊdəri/
Etymology:  From "do-gooder" + "-y". "Do-gooder" is a compound noun formed from the verb "do" and the adjective "good".  The suffix "-ery" forms abstract nouns, often with a negative connotation.
中文翻译: 伪善的慈善行为；爱管闲事
英文解析:  Often used ironically or dismissively to describe actions perceived as well-intentioned but interfering, naive, or ultimately ineffective.  It suggests a superficial or self-serving motivation behind the apparent altruism.
例句:
His do-goodery annoyed his neighbors, who felt he was interfering in their lives. 他的爱管闲事惹恼了他的邻居们，他们觉得他干涉了他们的生活。

All her do-goodery couldn't hide the fact that she was only interested in publicity. 她所有的慈善行为都掩盖不了她只对宣传感兴趣的事实。

近义词:  meddling, interference, busybodying, paternalism
反义词: apathy, indifference, neglect
词性: noun
