inflamed:/ɪnˈfleɪmd/| adj.|发炎的，红肿的，激动的|Reddened, swollen, and painful, especially as a result of injury or infection. Also, (of a person or their feelings) roused to anger or another strong emotion.|例句：The wound became inflamed. 伤口发炎了。

例句：Her inflamed rhetoric only exacerbated the situation. 她煽动性的言辞只会使情况恶化。|近义词：swollen, reddened, irritated, aggravated, angry, furious|反义词：soothed, calmed, healthy, healed|词性:形容词
词源:From Late Middle English: past participle of *inflamen, from Old French enflamer, from Latin inflammare ‘set on fire’, from in- ‘into’ + flamma ‘flame’.
