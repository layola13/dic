centrism: /ˈsentrɪzəm/ | n. | 中主义；中间路线；温和主义 | The holding of moderate political views, advocating a position between the extremes of left and right wings.  A political outlook or specific position that involves acceptance or support of a balance of a range of social views, or refusal to align oneself with an extreme position. |  Many voters are dissatisfied with the two main parties and are looking for a more centrist option. 许多选民对两大主要政党感到不满，正在寻找更温和的选择。|  The party's platform reflects a centrism that appeals to a broad range of voters. 该党的纲领反映了一种吸引广大选民的中间路线。 | synonyms: moderatism, neutrality | antonyms: extremism, radicalism | noun
