eschew:/ɪsˈtʃuː/
verb
避开，回避，避免
deliberately avoid or keep away from something
例句：
He eschewed publicity and led a quiet life.
他避开公众注意，过着平静的生活。

Vegetarians eschew meat products.
素食者不吃肉制品。


近义词：avoid, shun, abstain from, forgo, renounce
反义词：embrace, indulge in, welcome
词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *eschiver "to shun, avoid, escape," from Frankish *skiuhan "to shy away from" (compare Old High German sciuhen "to frighten off, avoid").
