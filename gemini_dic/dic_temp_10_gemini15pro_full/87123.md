herbalize:/ˈɜːrbəlaɪz/ |v. |用草药治疗；采集草药|To treat with herbs; to gather herbs.|She herbalized the wound with a poultice of comfrey. 她用紫草膏药草药治疗伤口。|He spent the afternoon herbalizing in the meadows. 他下午在草地上采集草药。|近义词: treat with herbs, use herbal remedies, gather herbs|反义词: (For "treat with herbs") treat with conventional medicine|词性: 动词
