bursar:/ˈbɜːrsər/ | n. | Bursar; 財務主管 | A bursar is a person who manages the financial affairs of a college or university. | The bursar's office handles all student account inquiries.  財務主管辦公室處理所有學生帳戶的查詢。| The bursar presented the annual budget to the board of trustees. 財務主管向董事會提交了年度預算。 | Synonyms: treasurer, cashier | Antonyms: debtor | noun
