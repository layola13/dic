authoritarian:/əˌθɒrɪˈteəriən/ | adj. | 独裁的，专制的 | Favoring or enforcing strict obedience to authority, especially that of the government, at the expense of personal freedom. | The country is under authoritarian rule.  这个国家处于独裁统治之下。| Synonyms: dictatorial, despotic, tyrannical, autocratic | Antonyms: democratic, liberal, libertarian | adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin *authoritativus, from Latin auctoritas (“authority”).  Ultimately from Latin auctor (“author, originator, founder”).
