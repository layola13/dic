crack-up:/ˈkrækʌp/| n.|崩溃，垮台；(精神)崩溃；(车辆的)撞毁；大笑；|a sudden collapse or failure, especially of a person's mental health; a crash; a fit of laughter|The stress of the job led to her crack-up.|近义词：breakdown, collapse, crash, wreck, hysteria, burst, fit|反义词:recovery, composure|词性:名词

crack-up:/ˈkrækʌp/| v. (crack up)| vi. & vt.| (精神)崩溃；垮掉；撞毁；(使)大笑|to suffer a mental breakdown; to cause (a vehicle) to crash; to cause to laugh uncontrollably|He cracked up under the pressure.|近义词：break down, collapse, crash, wreck, go to pieces|反义词:recover, compose oneself|词性:动词
