bend an ear:/bɛnd ən ɪr/
idiom
倾听；听取
to listen attentively; to pay attention to what someone is saying
例句：Could you bend an ear for a moment? I need some advice. 你能听我说一会儿吗？我需要一些建议。

例句：She bent an ear to my problems and offered some helpful suggestions. 她倾听了我的问题，并提供了一些有用的建议。

近义词：listen, pay attention, heed
反义词：ignore, disregard
词性：idiom (习语)
