deliberator:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪtər/
n.
审议者，商讨者，反复思考者
A person who deliberates; one who carefully considers or discusses something.
The deliberators spent hours weighing the pros and cons of the proposal.  审议者花了几个小时权衡该提案的利弊。
The deliberators finally reached a consensus.  商讨者最终达成了共识。
Synonyms:  considerer, ponderer, thinker, muller, ruminator
Antonyms:  impulsive person, rash person
