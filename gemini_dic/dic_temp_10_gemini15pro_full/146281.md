fanciful: /ˈfænsɪfʊl/
| adj. | 想象的; 花哨的; 奇特的; | Existing only in the imagination; unreal.  / Decoratively elaborate or excessively ornamented. /  Unusual and whimsical in appearance. |  She had a fanciful notion of becoming a famous actress. 她有一个想成为著名演员的幻想。| 近义词：imaginative, whimsical, fantastic, unreal | 反义词：realistic, practical, down-to-earth, simple |词性:形容词


词源:
"fanciful" 来自于 "fancy,"  最终源于古法语 "fantasie,"  意为 "幻想，想象力"。  "over-"  是一个前缀，表示 "过度，过分"。 "-ness"  是一个后缀，用于将形容词转换为名词，表示一种状态或品质。
