demibrigade:/ˌdɛmi brɪˈɡeɪd/ | n. | 半旅，相当于一个营或团的军队单位 | A military unit, typically intermediate in size between a regiment and a battalion. |  The demibrigade suffered heavy losses during the assault. 半旅在进攻中损失惨重。

The general ordered the demibrigade to advance. 将军命令半旅前进。


|Synonym:| battalion, regiment, unit, detachment |
|Antonym:| (No direct antonym; consider "army," "division" as larger units) |
|Word type:| Noun |


Etymology:
From French *demi-brigade*, from *demi-* "half" + *brigade* "brigade".
