amphibian:/æmˈfɪbiən/ | n. adj. |两栖动物，水陆两栖的|An animal that lives both on land and in water.  Relating to or characteristic of amphibians.|例句：Frogs are amphibians. 青蛙是两栖动物。|例句：The salamander is an amphibian creature.  蝾螈是一种两栖动物。|近义词：frog, toad, newt, salamander|反义词：mammal, reptile, bird|词性: noun, adjective
amphibious:/æmˈfɪbiəs/ | adj. |两栖的，水陆两栖的|Suitable for both land and water.|例句：Marines are trained for amphibious warfare. 海军陆战队接受水陆两栖作战训练。|例句：The army launched an amphibious assault. 军队发动了两栖攻击。|近义词：aquatic, semiaquatic |反义词：terrestrial|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek 'amphibios' meaning "living a double life," from 'amphi' ("both kinds") + 'bios' ("life").
