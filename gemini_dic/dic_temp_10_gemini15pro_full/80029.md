go-to-pot:/ɡəʊ tə pɒt/| idiom | 堕落；衰败；完蛋 | To decline or deteriorate; to be ruined or destroyed. |  例句：His business has gone to pot since he lost his main investor.  他的生意自从失去了主要投资者后就一落千丈了。

|例句：The whole project went to pot when the funding was cut.  整个项目在资金被削减后就彻底失败了。 | 近义词：go downhill, deteriorate, fall apart, go to rack and ruin | 反义词：improve, flourish, thrive | 词性: idiom
