attributive:/əˈtrɪbjʊtɪv/
adjective
定语的，属性的
A word or phrase that modifies a noun.  It describes or gives more information about the noun.
The *attributive* adjective "red" modifies the noun "car" in the phrase "red car".
红色轿车里的定语形容词“红色”修饰名词“轿车”。

The word "running" is used *attributively* in "running water".
“running”一词在“流水”中作定语。

Synonyms: qualifying, modifying, descriptive
Antonyms: predicative

词性: adjective
