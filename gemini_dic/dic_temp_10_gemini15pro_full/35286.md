clutter:/ˈklʌtər/| n. |杂乱，混乱|A state of disorder, untidiness, or confusion.|The room was in a state of clutter after the party. 房间在派对后一片混乱。|近义词：mess, disorder, chaos|反义词：order, tidiness, neatness|词性：名词

clutter:/ˈklʌtər/| v. |使凌乱，堆满|To fill or cover with scattered or disordered things that impede movement or reduce effectiveness.|Too many ornaments cluttered the mantelpiece.  太多的装饰品堆满了壁炉架。|近义词：mess up, litter, overcrowd|反义词：tidy, organize, declutter|词性：动词

clutters:/ˈklʌtərz/| v. |（clutter的第三人称单数形式）使凌乱，堆满||He clutters his desk with papers. 他把桌子堆满了文件。|近义词：messes up, litters, overcrowds|反义词：tidies, organizes, declutters|词性：动词
