frontated:/ˈfrʌnˌteɪtɪd/
adjective
额部突出的，前额突出的
Having a prominent forehead.
The Neanderthals were characterized by their frontated skulls. 尼安德特人的特征是他们的前额突出。
近义词: prominent, protruding, bulging
反义词: receding, flat
词性: adjective
