go-to-the-wall:/ɡəʊ tə ðə wɔːl/| idiom | 濒临破产；彻底失败；拼死一搏 | To fail, especially in business, or to be forced into a desperate situation. | The company went to the wall after the economic downturn.  这家公司在经济衰退后破产了。| Near synonyms: go bankrupt, fail, collapse | Near antonyms: succeed, thrive, flourish | idiom

go-to-the-wall:/ɡəʊ tə ðə wɔːl/| idiom | 竭尽全力；全力以赴 | To make a determined effort, especially when facing difficulties. |  He went to the wall to finish the project on time. 他竭尽全力按时完成了项目。| Near synonyms: give it one's all, go all out, fight tooth and nail | Near antonyms: give up, surrender, quit | idiom
