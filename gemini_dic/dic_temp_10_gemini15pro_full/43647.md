creators:/kriˈeɪtərz/
n.
创造者，创建者
A person or thing that creates something.
The creators of the show were praised for their originality.  这部剧的创作者因其独创性而受到赞扬。

Near Synonyms:  authors, designers, founders, inventors, makers, originators, producers
Antonyms: destroyers, ruiners

Etymology:  Late Middle English: from Old French createur, -trice, from Latin creator, creatrix, from creare ‘to create’.
