headword: what /wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) | pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么，何等 |A function word used to ask questions about the identity, nature, or value of an object or matter. | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？ | synonyms: which, how | antonyms: (none - what is a question word) | pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection

USAGE->
Simple Present:
What is your favorite color? 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? 他做什么工作？

Present Continuous:
What are you cooking for dinner? 你晚饭在做什么？
What is she working on at the moment? 她现在在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has he done to deserve this award? 他做了什么值得这个奖？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been doing all afternoon? 你整个下午都在做什么？
What has she been studying lately? 她最近一直在学习什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派对上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you left? 你离开之前做了什么？
What had happened before the police arrived? 警察到达之前发生了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天的天气会怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting tomorrow? 明天的会议上他们会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have accomplished by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by the time we get back? 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业的时候她将已经学习什么多久了？
