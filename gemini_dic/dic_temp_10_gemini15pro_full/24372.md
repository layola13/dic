bring-up-the-rear:/ˌbrɪŋ ʌp ðə ˈrɪər/ | idiom | 殿后，落在最后 | To be at the back of a group of people or things moving in a line.  |  The slowest runners brought up the rear in the marathon. 在马拉松比赛中，跑得最慢的运动员落在最后。 | synonyms:  trail behind, lag, straggle | antonyms:  lead the way, be at the forefront, head | idiom

bring-up-the-rear:/ˌbrɪŋ ʌp ðə ˈrɪər/ | idiom | 垫底，排名最后 | To be in last place in a competition or ranking. | Our team brought up the rear in the league standings. 我们的队伍在联赛排名中垫底。 | synonyms:  be in last place, be at the bottom | antonyms:  be in first place, be at the top | idiom
