deporting:/dɪˈpɔːtɪŋ/
verb
驱逐出境，递解出境
the act of forcing someone to leave a country, usually because they have broken the law or their immigration papers are not in order
The government is deporting illegal immigrants. 政府正在驱逐非法移民。
synonyms: expelling, banishing, extraditing
antonyms: admitting, receiving, welcoming
verb

deport:/dɪˈpɔːrt/
verb
驱逐出境
to force someone to leave a country, usually because they have broken the law or their immigration papers are not in order
He was deported for drug trafficking. 他因贩毒被驱逐出境。
synonyms: expel, banish, extradite
antonyms: admit, receive, welcome
