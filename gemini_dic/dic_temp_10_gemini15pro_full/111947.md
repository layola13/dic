lollops:/ˈlɒləps/ (UK) /ˈlɑːləps/ (US)| v. | 蹒跚而行，笨拙地行走 | To move in a clumsy, rolling way, typically because of being large or heavy. | The fat cat lolloped across the room.  那只肥猫笨拙地走过房间。

The bear lolloped along the riverbank. 那只熊沿着河岸蹒跚而行。


近义词: lumber, waddle,  galumph | 反义词: dart, scamper | 词性: verb


lollop:/ˈlɒləp/ (UK) /ˈlɑːləp/ (US)| n. | 笨拙的步态 | A clumsy, rolling gait. | The dog's lollop was rather endearing.  这只狗笨拙的步态很可爱。

With a comical lollop, the puppy chased the ball. 小狗以滑稽的步态追赶着球。


近义词: waddle, lumber | 反义词: sprint, dash | 词性: noun
