dishorn:/diːˈhɔːrn/ /diːˈhɔːrn/
verb
去除角; 截去…的角
To remove the horns of an animal.
The farmer dishorned the young bull to prevent injuries to the other cattle. 农民为了防止其他牛受伤，去掉了小公牛的角。

The rancher dishorned all the calves. 牧场主把所有小牛的角都截掉了。

近义词: dehorn
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑"horn" 或 "grow horns")
词性: verb
