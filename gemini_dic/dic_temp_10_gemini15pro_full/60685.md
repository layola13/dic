electricity:/ɪˌlekˈtrɪsəti/ | n. |电，电能 | A form of energy resulting from the existence of charged particles (such as electrons or protons), either statically as an accumulation of charge or dynamically as a current. |  Electric cars are becoming increasingly popular. 电动汽车正变得越来越流行。

The power outage cut off the electricity to the entire neighborhood. 停电切断了整个社区的电力供应。 | 近义词: power, current | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "lack of power") | 词性: noun
