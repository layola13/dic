iMac: /ˈaɪmæk/ | n. | 苹果一体机 | A desktop computer manufactured by Apple Inc.  It is characterized by its all-in-one design, with the computer's components built into the same case as the display. | I just bought a new iMac. 我刚买了一台新的iMac。 | 近义词: desktop computer, all-in-one computer | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "tower PC" as a contrasting design) | 词性: noun
