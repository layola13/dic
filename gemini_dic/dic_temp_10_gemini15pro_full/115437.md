mantra:/ˈmæntrə/ (US), /ˈmʌntrə/ (UK)| n.|咒语，真言，口头禅|A word or sound repeated to aid concentration in meditation. A statement or slogan repeated frequently.|She repeated her mantra, "I will succeed." 她重复着她的咒语：“我会成功的。”|synonyms: chant, incantation, slogan, motto, catchphrase|antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps consider "silence", "expletive" depending on context)|noun
