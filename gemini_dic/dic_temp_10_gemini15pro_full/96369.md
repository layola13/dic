indiscerpibility:/ˌɪndɪsɜːrnɪˈbɪləti/
n.
不可分辨性；不可区分性
The quality or state of being indiscernible; inability to be distinguished.
The indiscerpibility of the twins made it difficult to tell them apart. 这对双胞胎难以区分，很难把他们分开。
近义词：indistinguishability, inseparability
反义词：distinguishability, separability
词性：名词

indiscernible:/ˌɪndɪˈsɜːrnəbl/
adj.
无法分辨的；难以区分的
Impossible to distinguish; not perceptible.
The differences between the two versions are indiscernible. 这两个版本之间的差异难以察觉。
近义词：indistinguishable, imperceptible
反义词：distinguishable, perceptible
词性：形容词
