mulligatawny:/ˌmʌlɪɡəˈtɔːni/
noun
羊肉浓汤
A curry-flavored soup made with chicken or meat stock, often containing coconut milk and various spices.
She served a delicious mulligatawny for dinner. 她晚饭做了一道美味的羊肉浓汤。
Synonyms: curry soup, spiced soup
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a specific type of soup)
n.
