braggartly: /ˈbræɡərtli/ | adv. | 吹嘘地，自夸地 | In a boastful or arrogant manner.  | He braggartly announced his victory, much to the annoyance of his opponents. 他自夸地宣布了他的胜利，这让他的对手们非常恼火。 |  He braggartly showed off his new car. 他吹嘘地炫耀他的新车。 | 近义词：boastfully, arrogantly, vaingloriously | 反义词：humbly, modestly | 词性: 副词

braggart: /ˈbræɡərt/ | n. | 吹牛大王，自夸者 | A person who boasts about achievements or possessions. | He's such a braggart, always talking about how much money he makes. 他真是个吹牛大王，总是谈论他赚了多少钱。 | The braggart annoyed everyone with his constant boasting.  这个吹牛大王不停地吹嘘，惹恼了所有人。 | 近义词：boaster, show-off | 反义词：humble person | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *braggen (“to boast”), of uncertain origin.
