indirectnesses:/ˌɪndɪˈrɛktnɪsɪz/
| n. |间接性；迂回曲折|The indirectnesses of his language made it hard to understand his true meaning. 他语言的迂回曲折使得很难理解他的真实意思。|例句：The lawyer's indirectnesses frustrated the judge. 律师的迂回曲折让法官很恼火。  The indirectnesses of the politician's speech left the audience confused.  这位政客讲话的迂回曲折让听众感到困惑。|近义词：circuitousness, roundaboutness, obliqueness|反义词:directness, straightforwardness|词性:名词

Etymology: From indirect + -ness.  "Indirect" comes from the Late Latin *indirectus*, from *in-* (not) + *directus* (straight). "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns expressing state, quality, or condition.
