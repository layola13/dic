divisibility:/dɪˌvɪzəˈbɪləti/| n. |可除性，可分性|The divisibility of a number by another number refers to whether the first number can be divided evenly by the second number without any remainder.| The divisibility of 12 by 3 is clear because 12 divided by 3 equals 4 with no remainder. 12除以3的整除性很明显，因为12除以3等于4，没有余数。|近义词：factorability|反义词: indivisibility|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *divisibilitas, from Latin divisibilis (divisible) + -itas (suffix forming abstract nouns).  The Latin divisibilis comes from dividere (to divide).
