boiling:/ˈbɔɪlɪŋ/| adj. n.|沸腾的，滚烫的；沸腾|The boiling water splashed onto my hand. 沸水溅到了我的手上。|例句：The kettle is boiling. 水壶正在沸腾。|近义词：scalding, simmering|反义词:freezing, cold|词性:形容词

boiling:/ˈbɔɪlɪŋ/| n.|沸腾|The boiling of the water took a long time. 水沸腾花了很长时间。|例句：Bring the water to a rolling boil.  把水烧开。|近义词：ebullition|反义词:freezing|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *byle (“boil, tumor”), from Proto-Germanic *buljō (“swelling”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰewl- (“to swell, to blow”).  Related to Old Norse bylja (“billow, wave”).
