compassionates:/kəmˈpæʃənɪts/
| adj. | 富有同情心的；表示怜悯的 | Feeling or showing sympathy and concern for others. | She compassionates the homeless and often volunteers at the shelter. 她同情无家可归的人，经常在收容所做志愿者。 | 近义词：sympathetic, empathetic, kind, caring | 反义词：cruel, heartless, indifferent, apathetic | 词性:形容词
compassionates:/kəmˈpæʃənɪts/
| v. | 同情；怜悯 |  To feel or show compassion for.  |  The nurse compassionates her patients' suffering.  护士同情病人的痛苦。 | 近义词： sympathize with, empathize with, pity | 反义词： disregard, ignore | 词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin *compassionatus, past participle of compassionare (“to have compassion”), from Latin compassio (“compassion”) + -atus (past participle suffix).
