docket:/ˈdɑːkɪt (US), ˈdɒkɪt (UK)| n. vt.|摘要;标签;待办事项;记入摘要|A docket is a summary of a document or a label attached to goods, often containing a list of contents. As a verb, it means to make a summary of a document or to place something on a docket.|

例句：
The judge reviewed the docket before the trial.
法官在审判前查看了案件摘要。

The package was clearly docketed with the recipient's address.
包裹上清楚地标明了收件人的地址。


近义词：agenda, list, schedule, summary, calendar, record, register
反义词: N/A
词性: noun, verb
