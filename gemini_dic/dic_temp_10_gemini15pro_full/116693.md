maturer:/məˈtʊərər/ /məˈtʃʊərər/ | adj. | 更成熟的 | More mature; further developed physically, mentally, or emotionally. | She has become much maturer since starting college. 她自从上大学以来变得成熟多了。|

The grapes are maturer this year and sweeter. 今年的葡萄更成熟也更甜。| 近义词: riper, more developed, more experienced, older | 反义词: less mature, younger, immature, childish | 词性: 形容词


词源: From Middle English *maturere*, comparative of *mature*, from Old French *matur*, from Latin *maturus* (“ripe, timely, early”).
