outgoer:/ˈaʊtˌɡoʊər/
n.|外出者；好交际的人|A person who is outgoing and sociable; someone who enjoys being with other people and going out to social events.|She's a real outgoer and loves parties. 她是个真正的交际花，喜欢参加派对。|Synonyms: extrovert, social butterfly|Antonyms: introvert, homebody|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From out + go + -er.  "Out-" indicates outward movement or tendency, "go" refers to moving or travelling, and "-er" forms a noun denoting a person who performs the action.  Thus, an "outgoer" is someone who goes out frequently, particularly for social purposes.
