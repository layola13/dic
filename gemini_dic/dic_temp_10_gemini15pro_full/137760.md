nonvaried:/ˌnɑːnˈvɛərid/
adjective
不变的，没有变化的
lacking variation; unchanging.
The nonvaried diet of the prisoners consisted of only bread and water.  囚犯们一成不变的饮食只有面包和水。
The landscape was flat and nonvaried.  风景平坦而单调。
synonym: monotonous, uniform, unchanging, unvarying, constant
antonym: varied, diverse, changing, fluctuating
词性: 形容词
