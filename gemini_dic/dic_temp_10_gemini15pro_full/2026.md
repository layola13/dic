actuators:/ˈæktʃueɪtərz/| n.|致动器，执行器|A mechanical device for moving or controlling something.|The robot arm's actuators allow for precise movement.  机器手臂的致动器可以进行精确的移动。

The actuators in the car's braking system are essential for safety. 汽车制动系统中的致动器对安全至关重要。

|近义词：motor, driver, effector|反义词:sensor|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin actuator, from actus ("act, drive, impulse"), past participle of agere ("to do, drive, act").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂eǵ- ("to drive, lead").
