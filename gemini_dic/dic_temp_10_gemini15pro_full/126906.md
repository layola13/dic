narcotics:/nɑːrˈkɑːtɪks/ | n. | 麻醉剂，毒品 |  Drugs, especially illegal ones, that affect the brain and can be addictive.  These substances can induce stupor, coma, or insensibility. |  The police seized a large shipment of narcotics. 警方缴获了一大批毒品。| synonyms: drugs, opiates, narcotics, painkillers | antonyms: stimulants,清醒剂 | noun


narcotic:/nɑːrˈkɑːtɪk/ | adj. | 麻醉的，催眠的；(指毒品)致瘾的 | Relating to or denoting narcotics; inducing drowsiness or sleep. |  The doctor prescribed a narcotic painkiller after the surgery.  医生在手术后开了一种麻醉性止痛药。 | synonyms: soporific, opiate, analgesic | antonyms: stimulating, invigorating | adjective
