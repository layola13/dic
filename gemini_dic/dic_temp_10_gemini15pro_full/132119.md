noncrusading:/ˌnɒn kruːˈseɪdɪŋ/
adjective
非改革的，不热衷改革的
Not engaging in or involving a crusade; not advocating or strongly supporting a cause.
The mayor took a noncrusading approach to the city's budget problems. 市长对该市的预算问题采取了非改革性的方法。
He has a noncrusading attitude towards social issues. 他对社会问题的态度并不热衷于改革。
近义词: moderate, temperate, nonmilitant,  unzealous
反义词: crusading, zealous, militant, reformist
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + crusading, where crusade derives from Old French *croisier "cross-bearer" from Latin crux, crucis "cross".  The term originally referred to participants in the Crusades, later broadened to mean any vigorous campaign for a cause.
