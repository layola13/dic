overreaching:/ˌoʊvərˈriːtʃɪŋ/
adjective
过分的，过度的；不自量力的；野心勃勃的
Going beyond what is appropriate or achievable; excessively ambitious or presumptuous.
He made an overreaching attempt to control the company. 他妄图控制整个公司。

近义词:overambitious, presumptuous, excessive, immoderate, extravagant
反义词:moderate, reasonable, modest, restrained
词性:adjective


overreach:/ˌoʊvərˈriːtʃ/
verb
做得过分；(伸手)够不着；欺骗；自食其果
To defeat (oneself) by seeking or trying to achieve too much. To deceive or cheat.
The company overreached itself by expanding too quickly. 公司扩张过快，自食其果。

近义词:outwit, cheat, deceive, defraud, trick
反义词:be honest, be fair
词性:verb


overreach:/ˈoʊvərriːtʃ/
noun
过分，过度
The act of overreaching or the condition of being overreached.
His financial overreach led to his downfall. 他的过度扩张导致了他的垮台。

近义词:excess, immoderation, extravagance
反义词:moderation, restraint
词性:noun
