drugged:/drʌɡd/
adjective
被麻醉的；吸毒的；昏昏沉沉的
Affected by drugs, especially to the point of stupor or unconsciousness; under the influence of narcotics.
The drugged patient was unresponsive to stimuli. 被麻醉的病人对刺激没有反应。
The club was full of drugged and drunken revelers. 俱乐部里挤满了吸毒和醉酒的狂欢者。
Synonyms: narcotized, intoxicated, stupefied, doped
Antonyms: sober, alert, conscious
词性: 形容词
