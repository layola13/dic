costermonger:/ˈkɒstəˌmʌŋɡə(r)/ /ˈkɑːstərˌmʌŋɡər/ | n. |叫卖小贩，街头摊贩（尤指卖水果蔬菜的）|A person who sells goods, especially fruit and vegetables, from a stall or cart in the street, typically by shouting. | Example 1: The costermonger pushed his cart through the crowded streets.  小贩推着他的手推车穿过拥挤的街道。

Example 2:  The costermongers' cries filled the air. 小贩们的叫卖声充满了空气。 | Near synonyms: hawker, peddler, vendor, street vendor | Antonyms: shopkeeper, retailer | Word type: noun
