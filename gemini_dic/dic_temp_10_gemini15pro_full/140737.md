omnipresence:/ˌɒm.nɪˈprez.əns/ | n. |无所不在；普遍存在 |The state of being everywhere at once, or seeming to be everywhere at once. |The omnipresence of the internet has transformed the way we live.  互联网的无所不在改变了我们的生活方式。|Synonyms: ubiquity, pervasiveness |Antonyms: absence, rarity |noun

omnipresent:/ˌɒm.nɪˈprez.ənt/ | adj. |无所不在的；普遍存在的|Existing or being everywhere at the same time; ubiquitous.|The omnipresent surveillance cameras made people feel uneasy. 无处不在的监控摄像头让人们感到不安。|Synonyms: ubiquitous, pervasive, widespread |Antonyms: absent, rare, localized |adjective
