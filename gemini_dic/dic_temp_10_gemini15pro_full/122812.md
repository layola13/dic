moldwarp:/ˈmoʊldwɔrp/| n.|鼹鼠|A mole, a small burrowing mammal.|例句：The moldwarp had tunneled under the lawn, leaving raised ridges of earth. 鼹鼠在草坪下挖了隧道，留下了隆起的土埂。

例句：Shakespeare used the word "moldwarp" in his play, Hamlet.  莎士比亚在他的戏剧《哈姆雷特》中使用了“moldwarp”这个词。|近义词：mole|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
