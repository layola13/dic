leery-of:/ˈlɪri əv/| adj.|对...持怀疑态度的，警惕的，不信任的|Suspicious or wary of; distrustful.|例句：I'm leery of investing in that company. 我对投资那家公司持谨慎态度。|例句：She was leery of his promises. 她对他的承诺表示怀疑。|近义词：suspicious, wary, distrustful, skeptical|反义词：trusting, confident, credulous|词性:形容词

词源: From the noun "leer," meaning a sidelong or oblique look, suggesting suspicion or distrust.
