colligates:/ˈkɒlɪɡeɪts/ /ˈkɑːlɪɡeɪts/ | v. |  共现，同现，搭配出现 | To occur regularly in close association with another word or group of words.  |  Example: The words "heavy" and "rain" frequently colligate.  “heavy”和“rain”这两个词经常搭配出现。

Example:  Certain adjectives colligate with particular nouns.  某些形容词会与特定的名词搭配出现。|  近义词：co-occur, combine, correlate | 反义词: diverge, separate | 词性: 动词(verb)
