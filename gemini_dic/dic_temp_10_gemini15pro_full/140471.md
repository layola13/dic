old-maids:/oʊld ˈmeɪdz/ | n. | 老处女（纸牌游戏） | A card game in which players try to match pairs of cards, with the person left holding the odd card (usually a queen) at the end being the "old maid". |  例句：We played old-maids after dinner.  我们晚饭后玩了老处女纸牌游戏。

例句：She always lost at old-maids and hated being called the "old maid". 她玩老处女纸牌游戏总是输，而且讨厌被称为“老处女”。 | 近义词：spinster (for the person),  card game | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
