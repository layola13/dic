inditer:/ɪnˈdaɪtər/| n.|起诉人；告发人；作者；诗人|A person who writes something, especially a poem or other literary work; a person who accuses or charges another with a crime. |例句：The inditer of the anonymous letter remains unknown. 这封匿名信的作者仍然未知。

例句：The grand jury acted as inditers in the case, bringing formal charges against the suspect.  大陪审团在此案中充当了起诉人的角色，对嫌疑人提出了正式指控。|近义词：author, writer, poet, composer, accuser, prosecutor|反义词:defendant|词性:noun
