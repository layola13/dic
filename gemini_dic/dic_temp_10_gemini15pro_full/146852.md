overindulged: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒd/
adjective
过度放纵的；沉溺的
Having indulged in something, typically food or drink, to excess.
He felt sluggish and overindulged after the holiday feast.  他假期过后感觉迟钝，过度放纵。
She looked overindulged and slightly unwell. 她看起来过度放纵，有点不舒服。
近义词:  pampered, spoiled, excessive, intemperate
反义词:  moderate, abstemious, temperate
词性: adjective


overindulge: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒ/
verb
过度放纵；沉溺
To allow oneself to have or do something to excess, especially something enjoyable.
Don't overindulge in sweets. 不要过度沉溺于甜食。
He overindulged in alcohol and became ill.  他酗酒成性，病倒了。
近义词:  pamper, spoil, coddle
反义词:  abstain, moderate
词性: verb


词源:
From over- + indulge.  "Over-"  prefix means "excessively" and "indulge" means "to allow oneself to enjoy the pleasure of."
