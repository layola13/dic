overstrongnesses: /ˌoʊvərˈstrɔːŋnɪsɪz/ |n. | 过度的强项；过分的优势|  An excess of strengths, which can sometimes be detrimental.  It implies having strengths that are so pronounced they become weaknesses. | Her overstrongnesses, like her extreme attention to detail, sometimes slowed down the project. | 近义词:  excessive strengths, overdeveloped abilities | 反义词: weaknesses, deficiencies | 词性: 名词


Etymology:
"Over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above") + "strong" (Old English strang, from Proto-Germanic *strangaz "strong, rigid, severe") + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), a suffix forming nouns from adjectives).  Essentially, "the state of being over strong."
