albite:/ˈælbʌɪt/| n.|钠长石|A plagioclase feldspar mineral, NaAlSi3O8, that is typically white or off-white and a common constituent of felsic igneous rocks.|Albite is a common component of granite. 钠长石是花岗岩的常见成分。|近义词：plagioclase, feldspar|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific mineral) |noun
