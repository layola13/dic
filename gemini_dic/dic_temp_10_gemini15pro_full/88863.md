hoise:/hɔɪs/| vt.| 吊起，升起; (尤指吃力地) 拉起，拽起| To lift or raise something, especially something heavy, with effort. |例句：They hoised the flag to the top of the mast. 他们把旗子升到桅杆顶端。

例句：He hoised himself onto the wall. 他费力地爬上墙头。|近义词：lift, raise, haul, heave|反义词:lower, drop|词性:动词
