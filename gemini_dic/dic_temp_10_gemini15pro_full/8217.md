anti-freudianisms: /ˌænti-frɔɪdiənɪzəmz/ | n. | 反弗洛伊德主义 |  Theories and practices that oppose or reject the psychoanalytic theories and methods of Sigmund Freud. |  His book is full of anti-freudianisms, arguing against the Oedipus complex and the importance of dream analysis.  他的书充满了反弗洛伊德主义，反对俄狄浦斯情结和梦的分析的重要性。 | synonyms:  anti-psychoanalytic viewpoints, dissenting psychoanalytic theories | antonyms: Freudianism, psychoanalytic orthodoxy | noun
