graffiti: /ɡrəˈfiːti/ (US), /ɡræˈfiːti/ (UK)| n. |涂鸦，graffiti art 涂鸦艺术|Writing or drawings scribbled, scratched, or sprayed illicitly on a wall or other surface in a public place.|The walls were covered in graffiti. 墙上满是涂鸦。|synonyms: tagging, street art, mural, defacement, vandalism|antonyms: artwork, masterpiece|noun

graffiti: /ɡrəˈfiːti/ (US), /ɡræˈfiːti/ (UK)| n. (plural graffiti or graffito)|涂鸦|A single piece or inscription of graffiti.|Someone had scrawled a graffito on the wall. 有人在墙上乱涂了一个涂鸦。|synonyms: scribbling, doodle, inscription, marking|antonyms: artwork, masterpiece|noun
