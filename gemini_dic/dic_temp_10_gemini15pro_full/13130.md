asunder:/əˈsʌndər/
adverb, adjective|分离地，散开地|into pieces; apart.|例句：The ship was torn asunder by the storm.  这艘船被暴风雨撕成了碎片。|近义词：apart, separate, divided|反义词：together, united|词性: 副词，形容词
Etymology: From Old English *onsundran (unattested), from on + sundor (“apart”). Cognate with Old High German insundar, insundron (“especially, particularly”).
