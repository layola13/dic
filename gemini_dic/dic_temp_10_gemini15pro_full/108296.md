v. |授予桂冠；授予荣誉|The university laureated its most distinguished alumni. 大学授予其最杰出的校友荣誉。|近义词: crown, honor, award|反义词: disgrace, condemn|词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *laureatus*, past participle of *laureare* "to crown with laurel," from *laurus* "laurel tree."  The laurel wreath was a symbol of victory and honor in ancient Greece and Rome.
