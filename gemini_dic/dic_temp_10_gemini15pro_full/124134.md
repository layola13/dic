mortise:/ˈmɔːrtɪs/| n. |榫眼，榫孔|A hole or recess cut into a piece of timber or other material, designed to receive a tenon or other projecting part so as to form a joint.|The carpenter carefully chiseled a mortise into the wooden beam.  木匠小心地在木梁上凿了一个榫眼。|近义词:slot, hole, recess, socket|反义词:tenon|noun

mortices:/ˈmɔːrtɪsɪz/| n. |榫眼，榫孔（mortise的复数形式）|Plural form of mortise, referring to multiple holes or recesses cut to receive tenons.|The craftsman precisely measured and cut the mortices for the chair legs.  工匠精确地测量并切割了椅腿的榫眼。|近义词:slots, holes, recesses, sockets|反义词:tenons|noun
