limonin:/ˈlɪməˌnɪn/| n.|柠檬苦素|A bitter, white, crystalline substance found in citrus fruits, especially in the seeds, and also formed by the oxidation of limonene.  It contributes to the bitterness of some citrus juices.|The limonin content of the grapefruit juice was unusually high.  葡萄柚汁中的柠檬苦素含量异常高。|近义词: nomilin (related compound)|反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps "sweet compound")|词性:noun
