arbitral: /ˈɑːrbɪtrəl/ | adj. | 仲裁的；裁决的 | Relating to arbitration or arbitrators. |  The arbitral tribunal ruled in favor of the plaintiff. 仲裁庭裁定原告胜诉。 | The arbitral award was binding on both parties. 仲裁裁决对双方都有约束力。 | 近义词: judicial, adjudicative | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French arbitral or late Latin arbitralis, from Latin arbiter ‘judge, umpire’ (see arbiter).
