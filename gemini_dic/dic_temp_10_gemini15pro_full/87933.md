hieroscopy:/ˌhaɪəˈrɒskəpi/ | n. | 圣书解读；神圣文字研究 | The study and interpretation of sacred writings, especially ancient Egyptian hieroglyphics. |  Hieroscopy played a crucial role in deciphering the Rosetta Stone.  圣书解读在破译罗塞塔石碑中起了关键作用。 |  Etymology: From Greek hiero- (sacred) + -scopy (viewing, observing).  词源：来自希腊语hiero-（神圣的）+ -scopy（观看，观察）。 | synonyms: sacred writing interpretation, hieroglyphic decipherment | antonyms: N/A | noun
