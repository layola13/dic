overextreme: /ˌōvərɪkˈstriːm/
adjective | 过于极端的，过激的 |  Going beyond reasonable or acceptable limits of extremity. | His political views are considered overextreme by many. 他的政治观点被许多人认为过于极端。| extreme, radical, immoderate, excessive, fanatical | moderate, reasonable, temperate, balanced, mild
Etymology: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "extreme" (from Latin "extremus" meaning "outermost, furthest")
