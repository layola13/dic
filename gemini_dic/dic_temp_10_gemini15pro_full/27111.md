adjective
昏暗的；朦胧的；黑暗的
Dark and misty; obscure.
The caliginous forest was filled with the sounds of unseen creatures. 昏暗的森林里充满了看不见的生物的声音。
The writer’s caliginous prose made it difficult to understand his meaning.  这位作家的朦胧的散文使人难以理解他的意思。

近义词: dark, dim, gloomy, obscure, murky
反义词: bright, clear, luminous

词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *caliginosus, from caligo (“darkness, mist”), from caligō (“I am dark”).
