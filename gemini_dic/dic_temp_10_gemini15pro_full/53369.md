disaffiliations:/dɪsˌəˌfɪliˈeɪʃənz/| n. |脱离关系，断绝联系|The act of officially ending a relationship or connection with a larger group or organization.  The disaffiliations of several local chapters weakened the national organization.|例句：The labor union voted for disaffiliation from the national federation. 工会投票决定脱离国家联盟。

例句：Recent disaffiliations of schools from the athletic conference have raised concerns about its future.  几所学校最近脱离了体育联盟，引发了人们对其未来的担忧。|近义词：separations, detachments, disconnections, withdrawals|反义词:affiliations, unions, connections|词性:noun
