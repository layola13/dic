nodal:/ˈnoʊdəl/ (US), /ˈnəʊdl/ (UK)| adj.|节点的；结的|Of or relating to a node or nodes, especially in a network, system, or graph.  A point of intersection or connection within a network. It can also refer to a point of stillness or zero amplitude in a vibrating system.|The nodal points in the network are crucial for data transfer. 网络中的节点对于数据传输至关重要。|The artist marked the nodal points on the sculpture. 艺术家在雕塑上标记了节点。|Synonyms: central, crucial, junction, intersection, connecting|Antonyms: peripheral, unimportant, edge|adjective

nodal:/ˈnoʊdəl/ (US), /ˈnəʊdl/ (UK)| n.|节点；结|A node or point of intersection.|The network has several nodal points. 这个网络有几个节点。|The nodal points on the graph represent key events. 图表上的节点代表关键事件。|Synonyms: node, junction, intersection, connection|Antonyms: periphery, edge|noun
