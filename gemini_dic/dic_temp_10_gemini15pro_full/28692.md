carlin:/ˈkɑːrlɪn/| n.|哈巴狗，狮子狗（一种短而皱脸的狗）|A small dog of a breed having a short muzzle, a pug nose, and a wrinkled face.  It is believed to have originated in China.|例句：The carlin snored softly at the foot of the bed. 哈巴狗在床脚轻轻地打鼾。

例句：Her prize-winning carlin had a distinctive wrinkled face. 她那只获奖的哈巴狗有一张独特的皱脸。|近义词：pug, Dutch pug, Mopshond|反义词:|n. 名词
