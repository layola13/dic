noncontiguously:/ˌnɒnkənˈtɪɡjuəsli/ | adv. | 不连续地，不相邻地 | Not touching; separated in space or time. | The data was stored noncontiguously across multiple servers.  数据不连续地存储在多个服务器上。 | The islands are noncontiguously located in the Pacific Ocean. 这些岛屿不连续地分布在太平洋上。 | 近义词: separately, discontinuously, disjointedly | 反义词: contiguously, adjacently | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From non- + contiguous + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Contiguous" comes from Latin "contiguus," meaning "touching," from "contingere," meaning "to touch."  "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
