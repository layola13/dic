dingle:/ˈdɪŋɡəl/ | n. |深谷，小峡谷；树林中的僻静处|A deep, small, secluded valley or dell, often wooded. |We hiked through the dingle, enjoying the shade of the trees. 我们徒步穿过峡谷，享受着树荫。| The robin built its nest in the dingle. 知更鸟在峡谷里筑巢。|近义词：dell, glen, ravine, valley|反义词:peak, summit, ridge|noun

dingle:/ˈdɪŋɡəl/ | v. |（使）发出丁零当啷的声音|To make a ringing or jingling sound.|The bells on the sleigh dingled merrily. 雪橇上的铃铛叮当作响。| The coins dingled in his pocket. 硬币在他的口袋里叮当作响。|近义词：jingle, tinkle, clang|反义词:silence, mute|verb
