ambulance-chasing: /ˈæmbjələns ˌtʃeɪsɪŋ/
| n. | 趁火打劫；追逐救护车（指律师或其他专业人士为了招揽生意，积极寻找事故受害者） | The practice of soliciting business from accident victims, typically by lawyers or other professionals. |  The lawyer was accused of ambulance-chasing after he showed up at the hospital shortly after the accident.  这个律师在事故发生后不久就出现在医院，被指控趁火打劫。| synonyms: soliciting, profiteering | antonyms: altruism, charity | noun
