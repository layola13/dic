luminescent:/ˌluːmɪˈnesənt/| adj.|发光的，发冷光的|Emitting light not caused by heat, as in fluorescence or phosphorescence.|The luminescent dial on my watch makes it easy to tell the time in the dark.  我的手表上的发光表盘使我在黑暗中很容易看时间。|近义词：glowing, radiant, shining, bright|反义词:dull, dark, dim|词性:adjective
luminescence:/ˌluːmɪˈnesəns/| n.|发光，冷光|The emission of light not caused by heat, as in fluorescence or phosphorescence.|The luminescence of the fireflies created a magical atmosphere in the forest. 萤火虫的冷光在森林中营造了一种神奇的氛围。|近义词：glow, radiance, shine, brightness|反义词:dullness, darkness, dimness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lūmin- (stem of lūmen “light”) + -escent (suffix forming adjectives meaning "beginning to be" or "slightly").
