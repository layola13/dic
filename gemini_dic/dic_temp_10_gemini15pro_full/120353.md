minimises:/ˈmɪnɪmaɪzɪz/
verb
最小化，使减到最少
Reduces something to the smallest possible amount or degree.
The company minimises its costs by using cheaper materials.  公司通过使用更便宜的材料来降低成本。
He minimised the importance of his contribution to the project. 他淡化了自己对这个项目的贡献的重要性。
近义词: reduces, decreases, lessens, diminishes
反义词: maximizes, increases, enlarges, amplifies
词性: verb
