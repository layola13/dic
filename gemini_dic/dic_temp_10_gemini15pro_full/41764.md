corruptibility:/kəˌrʌptəˈbɪləti/
n.
可腐败性；易腐败性；腐化堕落性
The susceptibility to corruption; the capacity to be corrupted morally or materially.
The investigation revealed the inherent corruptibility of the system. 这项调查揭露了该系统固有的腐败性。
Synonyms: venality, dishonesty, susceptibility, vulnerability
Antonyms: incorruptibility, integrity, honesty, probity
noun
