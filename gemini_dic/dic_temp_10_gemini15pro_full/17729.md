bedeck:/bɪˈdɛk/
verb
装饰，点缀
to decorate or cover something with something attractive
The hall was bedecked with flowers and ribbons for the wedding.  大厅为了婚礼用鲜花和彩带装饰了起来。
近义词: adorn, decorate, embellish, ornament
反义词: strip, bare
词性: verb
