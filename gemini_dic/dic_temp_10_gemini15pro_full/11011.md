arborists:/ˈɑːrbərɪsts/
n.
树艺师，树木栽培专家
A specialist in the cultivation and care of trees.
The arborists carefully pruned the branches of the old oak tree.  树艺师仔细地修剪了老橡树的枝条。

近义词：tree surgeon, tree specialist
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps someone who harms or neglects trees)
词性：Noun
