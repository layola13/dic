footfaulted: /ˈfʊtˌfɔːltɪd/ | v. | 脚误，踏线犯规 | To commit a foot fault, especially in volleyball or tennis.  A foot fault occurs when a server steps over the serving line before contacting the ball. | The volleyball player footfaulted, giving the other team the point. 排球运动员脚误，对方得分。|  The tennis player was called for a foot faulting on his second serve. 这名网球运动员在第二次发球时被判脚误。 | Synonyms: stepped over the line, committed a serving violation | Antonyms: served legally, fair serve | verb
