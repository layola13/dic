misawards:/ˌmɪsəˈwɔːrdz/
n.
错误的裁决，不当的奖励
The act of giving an award incorrectly or unfairly, or the award itself that is given incorrectly or unfairly.
The misawards of the prize caused a great deal of controversy.  这个奖项的错误颁发引起了很大的争议。
misallocation, wrongful award
correct awards, justified awards
noun
