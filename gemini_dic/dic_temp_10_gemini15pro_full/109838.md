lexicologist:/ˌlɛksɪˈkɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 词汇学家 | A scholar who studies the vocabulary of a language, including its origin, meaning, and usage. |  Lexicologists are fascinated by the etymology of words. 词汇学家对词的词源很感兴趣。

Lexicologists often work on compiling dictionaries. 词汇学家经常从事编纂词典的工作。 |  synonym:  lexicographer, linguist, philologist | antonym: N/A | noun
