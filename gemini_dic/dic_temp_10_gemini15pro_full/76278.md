fyrdung:/ˈfɪrdʊŋ/ | n. | 兵役，军役;  (古斯堪的纳维亚)四分之一; | A system of military organization in medieval Scandinavia, where the country was divided into four parts, each responsible for providing a quarter of the king's army and navy. |  The fyrdung was an essential part of Viking-age warfare.  古代斯堪的纳维亚的兵役制度是维京时代战争的重要组成部分。|  The king called upon the fyrdung to assemble the fleet. 国王号召兵役制度集结舰队。| synonyms: levy, conscription, draft | antonyms: demobilization, discharge | noun
