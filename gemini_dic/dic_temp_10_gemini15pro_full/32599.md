chiropodists:/kɪˈrɒpədɪsts/
n.
足病医生，足科医生
A chiropodist is a healthcare professional who specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of conditions affecting the feet and lower limbs.
例句：I made an appointment with a chiropodist to treat my ingrown toenail. 我预约了一位足病医生来治疗我的嵌甲。

例句：The chiropodist recommended special insoles for my flat feet.  足病医生推荐我使用特殊的鞋垫来矫正我的扁平足。

近义词：podiatrist
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
