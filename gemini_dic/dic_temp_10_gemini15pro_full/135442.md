nonperspectives:/ˌnɑːn.pərˈspɛktɪvz/ | n. |非透视画法；非透视效果；缺乏远见|A style of representation, especially in painting, that does not employ the conventions of perspective, resulting in a flat or two-dimensional effect. Also, a lack of foresight or long-term vision.| The ancient Egyptian art often features nonperspectives, where figures are presented without depth. 古埃及艺术经常采用非透视画法，人物形象没有深度感。| synonyms: flatness, two-dimensionality | antonyms: perspective, three-dimensionality| noun


nonperspectives:/ˌnɑːn.pərˈspɛktɪvz/ | n. |缺乏远见|A lack of foresight or long-term vision in considering a situation or making plans.|  His nonperspectives on the company's future led to poor investment decisions. 他对公司未来的缺乏远见导致了糟糕的投资决策。| synonyms: shortsightedness, myopia | antonyms: foresight, vision | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
