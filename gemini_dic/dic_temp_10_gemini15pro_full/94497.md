importing:/ɪmˈpɔːtɪŋ/ | gerund, n. | 进口，输入 | The act of bringing goods or services into a country from abroad for sale. |  Example 1: Importing luxury cars is a lucrative business.  进口豪车是一项有利可图的生意。

Example 2: The importing of skilled workers helps fill labor shortages. 进口技术工人有助于填补劳动力短缺。 | Synonyms: bringing in, introduction, shipment | Antonyms: exporting, shipping out | verb/noun (gerund)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *importen, from Old French importer, from Latin importāre (“to bring in, introduce, convey”), from in- (“in, into”) + portāre (“to carry”).
