implemented: /ˈɪmplɪmentɪd/
implemented: | v. | 实施的，已执行的 | put (a decision, plan, agreement, etc.) into effect. | The company implemented a new dress code. 公司实施了新的着装规范。 | implemented: | 近义词：executed, enforced, carried out, applied, enacted | 反义词：abandoned, neglected, discontinued | 词性: v.
implemented: | adj. | 已实施的 |  carried out; put into effect. | The newly implemented policy has already shown positive results. 新实施的政策已经显示出积极的效果。 | implemented: | 近义词：enacted, enforced, effective, in force | 反义词：planned, proposed, ineffective | 词性: adj.


Etymology: From Latin *implementāre*, from *in-* + *plēre* "to fill".
