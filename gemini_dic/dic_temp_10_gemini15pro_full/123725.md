moons:/muːnz/
n.
卫星；月亮
Natural satellites of planets, especially of the Earth. /  Something that resembles a moon in shape or appearance.
例句：Jupiter has many moons. 木星有很多卫星。

例句：The full moon shone brightly in the sky. 满月在天空中闪耀。
近义词：satellites
反义词:(无直接反义词)
词性:名词
