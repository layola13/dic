muscularly:/ˈmʌskjʊləli/ | adv. | 肌肉发达地，强壮地 | In a way that relates to or involves muscles; with well-developed muscles. | He was muscularly built and moved with effortless grace. 他身材健壮，举止优雅自如。 | The boxer punched muscularly.  拳击手强有力地挥拳出击。| 近义词：strongly, powerfully, robustly | 反义词：weakly, feebly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From muscle + -ar + -ly.  "Muscle" comes from Latin "musculus" (little mouse), referring to the shape of certain muscles. "-ar" forms adjectives from nouns.  "-ly" forms adverbs from adjectives.
