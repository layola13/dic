mudhole:/ˈmʌdˌhoʊl/ (US), /ˈmʌdˌhəʊl/ (UK)| n. |泥坑，泥潭|A hole filled with mud; a puddle or patch of wet, sticky mud.|The jeep got stuck in a deep mudhole.  吉普车陷在一个很深的泥坑里。|

The pigs were wallowing in the mudhole. 猪在泥坑里打滚。


|近义词：mire, quagmire, bog, swamp|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "solid ground")|词性: noun|
