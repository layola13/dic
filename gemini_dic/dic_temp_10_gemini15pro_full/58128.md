dropstones:/ˈdrɒpˌstəʊnz/ (UK) /ˈdrɑːpˌstoʊnz/ (US)| n. |坠石，落石|Fragments of rock transported by icebergs and deposited in finer-grained sediments after the ice melts.|Example: Dropstones are a key indicator of past glacial activity. 坠石是过去冰川活动的关键指标。

Example:  The geologist found dropstones in the ancient seabed, suggesting the presence of icebergs millions of years ago.  地质学家在古代海床上发现了坠石，这表明数百万年前存在冰山。|近义词：erratics, glacial dropstones|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


词源:  "drop" + "stones". "Drop" refers to the action of the stones falling from the melting ice, and "stones" refers to the rock fragments themselves.
