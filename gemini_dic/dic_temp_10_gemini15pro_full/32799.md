词源:  "Choir" comes from Old French "cuer" which in turn comes from Latin "chorus," ultimately from Greek "khoros" meaning "dancing, chorus." The "-ed" suffix transforms the noun "choir" into an adjective, indicating something performed or characterized by a choir.
