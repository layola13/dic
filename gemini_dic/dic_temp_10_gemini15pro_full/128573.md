niblick: /ˈnɪblɪk/ | n. | 轻击杆，九号铁杆 | A golf club with a lofted iron head, typically numbered 9, used for short approaches and chips. | He used his niblick to chip the ball onto the green. 他用九号铁杆将球切上果岭。 | 近义词: nine iron, wedge | 反义词: driver, wood | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Scots *niblick, probably from *nib, variant of neb (“beak, point, tip”), + -lick, a frequentative suffix.  The club head resembled a bird's beak.
