frill:/frɪl/| n. v.|褶边，装饰，点缀；装褶边|A strip of gathered or pleated material used as a decoration or trimming.|The dress had a delicate frill around the neckline. 这件连衣裙领口有一圈精致的褶边。|近义词：ruffle, flounce, furbelow, ornament, decoration|反义词:plainness, simplicity|noun
frill:/frɪl/| v.|给…加上褶边，装饰，点缀|To decorate with or as if with a frill.|She frilled the edges of the tablecloth. 她给桌布的边缘加了褶边。|近义词：decorate, adorn, embellish, ornament|反义词:strip, simplify|verb
