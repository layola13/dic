modernistically:/ˌmɒdəˈnɪstɪkli/ /ˌmɑːdərˈnɪstɪkli/| adv.|现代主义地|In a way that reflects the principles of modernism, particularly in art, architecture, or design.  Characterized by a deliberate break with traditional styles and a focus on new forms, materials, and techniques.|The building was designed modernistically, with clean lines and large windows. 这栋建筑以现代主义风格设计，线条简洁，窗户很大。|The artist painted modernistically, using bold colors and abstract shapes. 这位艺术家以现代主义风格作画，使用大胆的色彩和抽象的形状。|近义词： contemporarily, avant-gardely|反义词: traditionally, classically|词性:副词
