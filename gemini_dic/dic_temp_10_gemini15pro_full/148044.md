overrulers:/ˌoʊvərˈruːlərz/| n. |统治者；支配者|Those who rule over others, often implying excessive control or authority.|The overrulers of the empire imposed heavy taxes on the people. 帝国的统治者向人民征收重税。|Synonyms: rulers, governors, dictators, tyrants|Antonyms: subjects, citizens, the ruled|noun

overrule:/ˌoʊvərˈruːl/| vt. |否决；驳回；统治；支配|To reject or disallow a decision, argument, or plea; to have controlling power.|The judge overruled the objection. 法官驳回了异议.|The king overruled his advisors and declared war. 国王否决了他的顾问的意见，宣布了战争。|Synonyms: reject, disallow, override, overturn, dominate, control|Antonyms: accept, allow, uphold, submit|verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From "over-" (meaning "above" or "excessive") + "rule" (from Old French "riule," ultimately from Latin "regula").  The sense of excessive control is often implied.
