coagulable:/koʊˈæɡjələbəl/
adjective
可凝固的
Capable of being coagulated; able to change from a liquid to a thickened or solid state.
The doctor checked if the patient's blood was coagulable. 医生检查了病人的血液是否可以凝固。
The substance is highly coagulable.  这种物质很容易凝固。
近义词:clottable, thickenable
反义词:incoagulable, uncoagulable
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin coagulābilis, from coagulāre (to curdle, congeal) + -bilis (able). Ultimately from co- (together) + agere (to drive, do).
