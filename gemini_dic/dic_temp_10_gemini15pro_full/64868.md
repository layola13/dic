espringal:/ˈɛsprɪŋɡəl/| n.|巨型弩炮，弹射器|A large crossbow-like weapon used in medieval siege warfare for throwing large bolts or stones.|The espringal was capable of launching projectiles over impressive distances. 巨型弩炮能够发射抛射物，射程惊人。|The espringal's power made it a formidable weapon during sieges. 巨型弩炮的威力使它成为攻城战中的强大武器。|Synonyms: catapult, ballista, trebuchet|Antonyms: shield, armor|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *espringale, from Old Provençal espringala, from a Germanic source, related to springen "to jump".  The word suggests the springing or launching action of the weapon.
