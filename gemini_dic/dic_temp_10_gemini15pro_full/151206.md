pardner:/ˈpɑːrdnər/ (US) /ˈpɑːtnər/ (less common)| n. |伙伴，朋友，合伙人| An informal, often humorous or affectionate, way of saying "partner." It is especially common in American English, particularly in the western and southern dialects.|Howdy, pardner!  你好啊，伙计！|例句：Well, pardner, looks like we're in this together. 嗯，伙计，看来我们要共同面对这件事了。

例句：He greeted his old pardner with a slap on the back. 他拍了一下老朋友的背，向他打招呼。|近义词：partner, friend, pal, buddy, mate|反义词:enemy, foe|Noun
