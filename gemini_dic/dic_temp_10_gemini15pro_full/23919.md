breakneck:/ˈbreɪkˌnɛk/| adj.| 危险的；飞快的；要命的 |dangerously or extremely fast; reckless | The car was going at a breakneck speed. 汽车以惊人的速度行驶。| synonyms: dangerous, reckless, fast, hasty, rapid, swift, hurried, precipitate, headlong, rash, careless, heedless, incautious, imprudent | antonyms: slow, cautious, careful, deliberate | adjective

Etymology:
From "break" + "neck".  Literally meaning fast enough to break one's neck.
