noninterpretational:/ˌnɒnɪnˌtɜːrprɪˈteɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非解释性的，不涉及解释的 | Not involving or based on interpretation; purely descriptive. | The study provides a noninterpretational analysis of the data, simply presenting the findings without drawing conclusions. 这项研究提供了对数据的非解释性分析，只是简单地呈现了研究结果，没有得出结论。|  The scientist took a noninterpretational approach, focusing solely on observable phenomena.  这位科学家采取了一种非解释性的方法，只关注可观察到的现象。 | descriptive, objective, empirical, literal | interpretational, subjective, analytical | adjective
