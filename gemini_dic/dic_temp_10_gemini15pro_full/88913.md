hold-off:/ˈhoʊld ɔːf/ (US), /ˈhəʊld ɒf/ (UK)| n. |暂停，延迟，推迟|A temporary delay or postponement of an action. |The general ordered a hold-off on the attack until reinforcements arrived. 将军下令推迟进攻，直到援军到达。|
hold-off:/ˈhoʊld ɔːf/ (US), /ˈhəʊld ɒf/ (UK)| n. |抵抗，抵御|Resistance or defense against something. |The small force put up a valiant hold-off against the enemy advance.  这支小部队英勇地抵抗了敌人的进攻。|
hold-off:/ˈhoʊld ɔːf/ (US), /ˈhəʊld ɒf/ (UK)| n. |(电子)保持关闭状态|The state of keeping something off or deactivated. |The hold-off circuitry prevents the system from starting prematurely. 保持关闭电路防止系统过早启动。|
