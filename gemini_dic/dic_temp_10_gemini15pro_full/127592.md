nedermire:/ˈnɛdərmaɪər/| n.|泥潭；沼泽地|A low-lying area of waterlogged ground, often containing decaying vegetation.|The explorers struggled through the nedermire, their boots sinking deep into the mud. 探险者们在泥潭中艰难跋涉，靴子深深地陷进了泥里。|近义词：bog, marsh, swamp, mire|反义词: highland, upland|词性:名词

词源:  "nedermire" likely originates from a combination of two older English words: "neder" (meaning lower, nether) and "mire" (meaning a swamp or bog).  This etymology suggests a low-lying, muddy place.
