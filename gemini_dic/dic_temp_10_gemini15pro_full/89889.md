horribly:/ˈhɒrɪbli/ (UK) /ˈhɔːrɪbli/ (US)| adv.|可怕地，非常，极其|In a horrible manner; to a horrible extent; very unpleasantly.|The play was horribly written and acted.  这出戏写得和演得都糟透了。|She sang horribly off-key. 她唱歌跑调得厉害。|近义词：awfully, terribly, dreadfully, frightfully|反义词：wonderfully, beautifully, delightfully|词性:副词
词源：From Middle English *horribili, from Old French horrible + -ly, from Latin horribilis (“causing horror, dreadful, terrible”), from horrēre (“to bristle, shudder, tremble”) + -bilis.
