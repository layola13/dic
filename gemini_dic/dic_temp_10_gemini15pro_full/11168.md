archiannelida:/ˌɑːrkiænˈɛlɪdə/ | n. | 古环节动物门 | A small phylum of marine segmented worms, considered primitive and sometimes classified within the polychaetes. |  Archiannelida are a group of simple marine annelids. 古环节动物是一群简单的海洋环节蠕虫。|  Some scientists believe Archiannelida represent an early stage in annelid evolution. 一些科学家认为古环节动物代表了环节动物进化的早期阶段。| synonyms:  primitive annelids | antonyms:  advanced annelids | noun
