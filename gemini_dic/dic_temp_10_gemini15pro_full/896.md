abundance: /əˈbʌndəns/
n.
丰富，充裕
a very large quantity of something.
The garden produced an abundance of tomatoes this year. 今年花园里产出了大量的西红柿。

There is an abundance of evidence to support the theory. 有大量的证据支持这一理论。

近义词: plenty, profusion, wealth, copiousness, amplitude
反义词: scarcity, lack, dearth, paucity
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *abondance*, from Latin *abundantia*, from *abundans* (present participle of *abundare* "to overflow, abound"), from *ab* "from" + *undare* "to surge, overflow," related to *unda* "wave."

USAGE->
There is an abundance of opportunities in the tech industry. = 科技行业有很多机会。

The country has an abundance of natural resources. = 这个国家拥有丰富的自然资源。

PHRASE->
in abundance = 大量地; 丰富地
an abundance of = 大量的...
abundance of the heart =  衷心的情感;  溢于言表的感情
abundance of caution = 谨慎起见; 为了慎重起见
live in abundance =  生活富裕
