nonextrinsically:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstrɪnzɪkli/
adverb
非外在地；内在地
Not extrinsically; inherently; intrinsically.  Derived from "extrinsic" with the negative prefix "non-" and the adverbial suffix "-ly."
His motivation was nonextrinsically driven, stemming from a deep personal passion for the project. 他做这个项目的动力并非来自外部，而是源于内心深处对这个项目的热爱。
His love for music is nonextrinsic, coming from a deep appreciation of the art form. 他对音乐的热爱并非来自外部，而是源于对艺术形式的深刻欣赏。
Synonym: intrinsically, inherently
Antonym: extrinsically, externally
