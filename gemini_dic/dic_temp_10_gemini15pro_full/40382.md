contradictories:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktrɪz/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktəriz/| n. |矛盾，对立面|Things that are contradictories cannot both be true.|The two witnesses gave contradictory accounts of the accident.  两个目击者对事故的描述相互矛盾。|Synonyms: opposites, inconsistencies, discrepancies|Antonyms: consistencies, agreements|noun


contradictory:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktəri/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktɔːri/| adj. |相互矛盾的，对立的|Containing or characterized by contradiction.|His statements were contradictory to the evidence. 他的陈述与证据相矛盾。|Synonyms: conflicting, opposing, incompatible|Antonyms: consistent, compatible, harmonious|adjective
