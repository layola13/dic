overdominance: /ˌoʊvərˈdɑːmɪnəns/ | n. | 过度支配，过分控制 | The state or quality of overdominating. The overdominance of the ruling party stifled political discourse. 执政党的过度支配扼杀了政治讨论。| dominance, supremacy | - | noun

overdominant: /ˌoʊvərˈdɑːmɪnənt/ | adj. | 过度支配的，过分控制的 | Excessively or unduly dominant.  His overdominant personality made it difficult for others to contribute. 他过分支配的性格使其他人难以做出贡献。 | dominant, controlling | recessive | adjective
