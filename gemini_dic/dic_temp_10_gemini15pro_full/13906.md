augustinianism:/ɔːˌɡʌstɪˈniːənɪzəm/ | n. | 奥古斯丁主义 | The theological system of St. Augustine of Hippo, emphasizing the total depravity of humankind, the necessity of divine grace for salvation, and predestination. | Augustine's Confessions is a powerful expression of Augustinianism. 奥古斯丁的《忏悔录》是对奥古斯丁主义的有力表达。 |  Augustinian theology, Augustine's teachings | Pelagianism, semi-Pelagianism | noun
