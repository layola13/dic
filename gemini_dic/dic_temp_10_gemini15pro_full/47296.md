de-icer:/diːˈaɪsər/
noun
除冰器，除冰剂
A substance used to remove ice or prevent its formation.
The pilot sprayed de-icer on the wings before takeoff. 飞行员在起飞前在机翼上喷洒了除冰剂。
synonyms:anti-icer, defrosting agent
antonyms:ice, freezing agent
n.

de-icer:/diːˈaɪsər/
verb
去除冰，防止结冰
To remove ice or prevent its formation using a de-icing substance.
They de-iced the roads before the rush hour. 他们在高峰时间前给道路除冰。
synonyms:defrost, thaw
antonyms:freeze, ice up
v.
