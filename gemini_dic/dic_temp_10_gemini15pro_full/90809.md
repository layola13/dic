humps:/hʌmps/
n.
驼峰；隆起；驼背；忧郁
A rounded protuberance on the back of a camel or other animal. A rounded raised area on a surface. A fit of depression or bad temper.
The camel had two humps.  这只骆驼有两个驼峰。
The car went over the humps in the road. 汽车驶过路上的隆起。
He's got the humps today. 他今天心情不好。
近义词：bumps, lumps, protuberances, swellings, ridges, crests, knuckles, hunch, humpback, hunchback, depression, blues, melancholy, gloom
反义词：hollows, depressions, dips
词性：名词
