missteps:/ˌmɪsˈstɛps/
|n.|失策，错误，过失
|A misstep is a wrong or awkward step.  It can also refer to a mistake or blunder in conduct or judgement.|
|His biggest misstep was trusting the wrong person.|他最大的失误就是信任了错误的人。
|Her career was ruined by a series of missteps.|她的一系列失误毁了她的职业生涯。
|blunder, error, mistake, gaffe|
|correct steps, wise decisions|
|noun|
