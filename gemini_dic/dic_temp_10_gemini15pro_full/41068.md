coppel:/ˈkɒpəl/| n.|灰皿，烤钵，试金坩埚|A small, shallow, fire-resistant dish used in assaying, especially for separating noble metals from base metals by heat.|The assayer used a coppel to test the purity of the gold.  化验员用灰皿来测试黄金的纯度。|Synonyms: crucible, cupel|Antonyms: N/A|noun

coppel:/ˈkɒpəl/| v.|用灰皿精炼|To refine or assay (metal) in a coppel.|He coppelled the silver to remove impurities. 他用灰皿精炼了银子以去除杂质。|Synonyms: refine, assay, purify|Antonyms: contaminate, pollute|verb
