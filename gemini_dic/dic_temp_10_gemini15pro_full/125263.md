multiemployers:/ˌmʌltiɪmˈplɔɪərz/| n.|多雇主|A group of employers who jointly employ workers, often in a collective bargaining agreement or for a specific project or industry.|The multiemployers association negotiated a new healthcare plan for all participating companies. 多雇主协会为所有参与公司协商了一项新的医疗保健计划。|例句：The multiemployers group offers training programs for employees of all member companies.  多雇主集团为所有成员公司的员工提供培训计划。|近义词：joint employers, co-employers|反义词:single employer|词性:noun
