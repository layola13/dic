bitching:/ˈbɪtʃɪŋ/ | n.|抱怨；|The act of complaining persistently.|例句：He did nothing but bitching all day long. 他整天除了抱怨什么也没做。|近义词：complaining, whining, moaning, griping|反义词：praising, commending|词性：名词


词源：From the verb "bitch," which originated from Old Norse "bikkja" (female dog).  The meaning evolved over time to refer to a spiteful or unpleasant woman, and the verb form came to mean "to complain" or "to nag."  "Bitching" as a noun or adjective is derived from this verb.
