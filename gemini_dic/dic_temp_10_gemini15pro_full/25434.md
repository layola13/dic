bulky:/ˈbʌlki/| adj.|庞大的，笨重的，体积大的|Taking a bulky suitcase on the bus is inconvenient. |例句：The bulky package was difficult to carry.  这个笨重的包裹很难搬运。

例句：She wore a bulky sweater to keep warm in the cold weather. 她穿了一件厚重的毛衣御寒。|近义词：large, massive, cumbersome, unwieldy|反义词：compact, small, slight|词性：形容词
Etymology->
From Middle English *bulky*, from *bulk* + *-y*.  Ultimately from Old Norse *bulki* (“cargo, heap”).
