disclosers:/dɪˈskloʊzərz/| n.|披露者，揭露者|People who reveal or make something known, especially something that was previously secret.  Those who disclose information.|The whistleblowers were among the disclosers of the company's illegal activities. 这些告密者是揭露公司非法活动的人之一。|近义词：informants, revealers, whistleblowers|反义词：concealers, suppressors|noun
