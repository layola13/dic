inhibit:/ɪnˈhɪbɪt/ | v. |抑制，约束，禁止|To hinder, restrain, or prevent (an action or process).| Alcohol inhibits coordination. 酒精会抑制协调性。|The drug inhibits the growth of cancer cells. 这种药物抑制癌细胞的生长。|近义词: restrain, suppress, hinder, restrict, impede|反义词: encourage, promote, stimulate, facilitate|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *inhibere*, from *in-* (in, on, upon) + *habere* (to hold).  Literally, "to hold in."
