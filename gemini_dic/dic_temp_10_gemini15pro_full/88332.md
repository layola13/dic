hippocras:/ˈhɪpəkræs/ (US), /ˈhɪpɒkræs/ (UK)| n. |希波克拉底酒，香料酒|A spiced wine, often sweetened and mixed with other ingredients, popular in medieval Europe.|The lord offered his guests a goblet of hippocras after the feast.  领主在宴会后给他的客人一杯希波克拉底酒。|Synonyms: spiced wine, mulled wine|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps unspiced wine)|noun
