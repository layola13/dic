hypersensitive:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnsɪtɪv/| adj.|过敏的，过分敏感的|Excessively or abnormally sensitive; easily offended or upset.|例句：She is hypersensitive to criticism. 她对批评过分敏感。|例句：He's hypersensitive about his weight. 他对自己的体重过分敏感。|近义词：oversensitive, touchy, sensitive|反义词：insensitive, thick-skinned|词性:形容词
词源: From hyper- "over, excessive" + sensitive.  "Hyper-" comes from the Greek "hyper," meaning "over, above, beyond." "Sensitive" comes from the Latin "sentire," meaning "to feel."
