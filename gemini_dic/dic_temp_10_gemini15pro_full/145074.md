overanalyzing: /ˌoʊvərˈænəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度分析
Analyzing something excessively, often to the point of creating unnecessary complexity or anxiety.
She's overanalyzing the situation and making it worse than it is. 她正在过度分析情况，使事情变得比实际更糟。
He tends to overanalyze every little thing she says. 他倾向于过度分析她说的每一件小事。
Synonyms: overthinking, obsessing, scrutinizing
Antonyms: accepting, ignoring, simplifying
词性: 动词(现在分词)
