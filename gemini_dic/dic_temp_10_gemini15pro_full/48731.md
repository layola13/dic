defile:/dɪˈfaɪl/| v.|玷污，亵渎；弄脏；败坏|To make something impure or unclean, especially something sacred or valued; to corrupt the purity or sanctity of something.|The vandals defiled the church with graffiti. 那些破坏者在教堂上涂鸦，玷污了教堂。|近义词：contaminate, pollute, corrupt, desecrate|反义词：purify, cleanse, sanctify|词性：动词

defile:/ˈdiːfaɪl/| n.|峡谷，隘路|A narrow passage, especially one between mountains.|The army marched through the narrow defile. 军队穿过狭窄的峡谷行进。|近义词：gorge, canyon, pass|反义词：plain, open field|词性：名词
