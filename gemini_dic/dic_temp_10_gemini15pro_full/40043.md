consummating:/ˈkɒnsəmeɪtɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːnsəmeɪtɪŋ/
verb
完成；使完美；圆房
making (something) complete or perfect.
例句：They consummated their relationship. 他们圆房了。

例句：The deal was consummated with a handshake. 这笔交易以握手成交。
近义词：complete, finalize, conclude, fulfill
反义词：begin, start, initiate
词性：v.
