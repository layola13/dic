co-defendant:/ˌkoʊdɪˈfɛndənt/| n.|共同被告|A person or entity who is being sued or prosecuted along with one or more other defendants in the same legal action.|例句：The co-defendant pleaded guilty to lesser charges. 共同被告对较轻的罪名认罪。

例句：The co-defendant refused to testify against his partner. 共同被告拒绝作证指控他的同伙。|近义词：fellow defendant|反义词:plaintiff, prosecutor|noun
