mis-sell:/ˌmɪsˈsɛl/
verb
误售，不当销售
To sell (something, especially a financial product) to someone by misrepresenting its suitability or benefits.
The bank was accused of mis-selling pensions to its customers. 这家银行被指控向客户不当销售养老金。
The company mis-sold insurance policies to elderly people. 这家公司向老年人误售了保险单。
近义词: defraud, deceive, cheat
反义词: advise correctly, sell appropriately
