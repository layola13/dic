overdeepens:/ˈoʊvərˌdiːpənz/
verb
过度深化；过度加深
To make something excessively deep, often referring to glacial erosion.  The glacier overdeepened the valley floor, creating a fjord.
The glacier overdeepened the valley floor, creating a fjord. 冰川过度侵蚀了谷底，形成了峡湾。
The dredging process overdeepened the harbor entrance, requiring costly repairs.  疏浚过程使港口入口过度加深，需要昂贵的维修费用。
deepen, excavate excessively
shallow, fill
