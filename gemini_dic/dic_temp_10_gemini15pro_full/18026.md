begird:/bɪˈɡɜːrd/ /biˈɡərd/| vt.|包围，环绕|To surround; encircle, often with something that restricts or confines.|例句：Mountains begird the city. 群山环绕着这座城市。

例句：A high wall begirded the castle. 一堵高墙环绕着城堡。

近义词：surround, encircle, encompass, gird|反义词: expose, uncover|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *begirden, from be- + girden (“to gird”), equivalent to be- + gird.  Gird itself comes from Old English gyrdan, from Proto-Germanic *gurdjaną, from Proto-Indo-European *gʰwer- (“to enclose, encompass”).
