passenger:/ˈpæsɪndʒər/ (US), /ˈpæsɪndʒə/ (UK)| n.|乘客，旅客|a person who is travelling in a vehicle but is not driving it, flying it, or working on it|例句：The passenger asked the driver to stop the bus. 乘客要求司机停车。|近义词：traveler, commuter, rider|反义词:driver, pilot, crew|noun
