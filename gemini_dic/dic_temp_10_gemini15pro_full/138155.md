northmen:/ˈnɔːrθmən/| n. |北欧人，维京人|Men from the north, especially Vikings.  Historically, inhabitants of Scandinavia, particularly those who raided and settled in other parts of Europe.|The northmen sailed their longships down the rivers of Europe, raiding and pillaging.|Synonyms: Vikings, Norsemen, Scandinavians|Antonyms: Southerners|Noun
