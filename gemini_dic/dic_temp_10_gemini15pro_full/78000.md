gentlemanlike: /ˈdʒɛntlmənˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 绅士般的；像绅士的 | Having or showing the qualities associated with a well-bred and courteous man. | He behaved in a very gentlemanlike manner, opening doors for her and offering his coat. 他表现得非常绅士，为她开门，并主动提供他的外套。 |  He always has a gentlemanlike demeanor. 他总是举止绅士。 | 近义词: courteous, polite, refined, chivalrous | 反义词: rude, uncouth, boorish | 词性: 形容词

词源: gentleman + like.  "Gentleman" comes from Old French "gentilhomme," meaning "man of noble birth."  "Like" is from Old English "lic" meaning "having the same form or appearance as."
