cabaret:/ˈkæbəreɪ/ (US), /ˈkæbəreɪ/ (UK)| n.|歌舞表演，夜总会|A restaurant or nightclub providing entertainment, typically including music, song, and dance.| We went to a cabaret show in Paris. 我们去巴黎看了一场歌舞表演。|Synonyms: nightclub, nightspot, floor show|Antonyms: library, museum|noun

cabaret:/ˈkæbəreɪ/ (US), /ˈkæbəreɪ/ (UK)| n.|小酒馆，小餐馆|(historical) A shop selling wine and other alcoholic drinks.|The old cabaret was a popular meeting place for locals.  这家老小酒馆曾是当地人喜爱的聚会场所。|Synonyms: tavern, bar, pub|Antonyms: none|noun
