co-determination:/koʊ-dɪˌtɜːrmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |共同决定；劳资共同经营；合作决定|A system of joint decision-making by workers and management, typically in a company's board of directors. |Germany is well-known for its system of co-determination in large companies. 德国以其大公司中的共同决定制度而闻名。

Co-determination gives employees a voice in the strategic direction of the company. 共同决定制度让员工在公司的战略方向上有发言权。|Synonyms: joint decision-making, worker participation, industrial democracy |Antonyms: autocracy, dictatorship| noun
