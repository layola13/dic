complaining:/kəmˈpleɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 抱怨的，诉苦的 | Expressing dissatisfaction or annoyance about something. | He's always complaining about the weather. 他总是抱怨天气。|  The customers were complaining about the poor service. 顾客们在抱怨糟糕的服务。| 近义词: griping, grumbling, whining, moaning | 反义词: praising, commending | 词性:形容词

complaining:/kəmˈpleɪnɪŋ/ | n. | 抱怨，诉苦 | The act of expressing dissatisfaction or annoyance. |  There's been a lot of complaining about the noise. 关于噪音有很多抱怨。| Constant complaining can be draining.  持续的抱怨会让人精疲力尽。| 近义词: grievance, criticism, protest | 反义词: praise, compliment | 词性:名词
