discomforter:/dɪsˈkʌmfərtər/
n.
令人不舒服的人或事物；扰乱安宁的人或事物
Something or someone that causes discomfort or distress.
The loud music from the neighbor's party was a real discomforter.  邻居派对上的大声音乐真是令人不舒服。
The scratchy wool blanket was a discomforter on the hot summer night.  在炎热的夏夜，粗糙的羊毛毯令人很不舒服。
Synonyms: annoyance, nuisance, bother, irritant, pest
Antonyms: comforter, soother, comfort, solace
