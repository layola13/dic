exprobrative:/ɪkˈsproʊbrətɪv/ | adj. | 责备的；申斥的；表示谴责的 | Expressing strong disapproval or reproach. |  He delivered an exprobrative speech condemning the violence. 他发表了一篇谴责暴力的演讲。 | 近义词: reproachful, condemnatory, censorious, rebuking | 反义词: laudatory, commendatory, approving | 词性: 形容词
词源: Late 16th century: from Latin exprobrat- ‘reproached’, from the verb exprobrare, from ex- ‘out, forth’ + probrum ‘reproach’.
