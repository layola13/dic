anticommercialism:/ˌænti kəˈmɜːrʃəlɪzəm/
|n.|反商业主义，反商业化|Opposition to commercialism or excessive emphasis on profit-making, often associated with a preference for artistic, social, or environmental values over material gain.|The anticommercialism of the festival was evident in the absence of corporate sponsorships.  这个节日反对商业化的态度体现在没有企业赞助。|The artist's anticommercialism led him to refuse lucrative advertising deals.  这位艺术家反对商业化的态度使他拒绝了利润丰厚的广告交易。|近义词:anticonsumerism, antimaterialism|反义词:commercialism, consumerism|名词
