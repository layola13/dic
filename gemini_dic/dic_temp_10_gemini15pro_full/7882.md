antedate:/ˌæntiˈdeɪt/ | v. | 先于；早于；把日期填早 | to come before (something) in time; to give an earlier date to (something) than the actual date | The church antedates the town by several centuries. 这座教堂比这座城镇早几个世纪。 | synonyms: precede, predate | antonyms: postdate, follow | verb


antedate:/ˈæntiˌdeɪt/ | adj. | 早于的；先于的 | existing or happening before a particular date | an antedate check 一张日期填早的支票 | synonyms: earlier, prior | antonyms: later, posterior | adjective
