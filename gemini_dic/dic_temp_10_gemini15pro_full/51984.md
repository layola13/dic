diaeretic:/ˌdaɪəˈrɛtɪk/| adj. n.|分音符的；分音符|A diaeretic is a mark placed over a vowel to indicate that it is pronounced in a separate syllable, such as the umlaut in German or the tréma in French.|The word "naïve" uses a diaeretic.  这个单词“naïve”使用了分音符。|Synonyms: umlaut, trema, dieresis|Antonyms: N/A|adjective, noun
