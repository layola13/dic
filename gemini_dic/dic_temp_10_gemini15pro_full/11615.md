armadas:/ɑːrˈmɑːdəz/
n.
舰队；大型舰队
A fleet of warships; a large group of moving vehicles or aircraft.
The Spanish Armada sailed against England in 1588. 西班牙无敌舰队于1588年航行攻打英国。
The highway was filled with an armada of trucks. 公路上挤满了卡车。
fleet, navy, flotilla
n/a
noun
