finalise:/ˈfaɪnəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈfaɪnəˌlaɪz/ (US)| vt.|最终确定；完成；定稿|To complete the last part of a plan, agreement, etc. so that it is finished|例句：We'll finalize the details later. 我们稍后会最终确定细节。

例句：They haven't finalized the contract yet. 他们还没有最终确定合同。

近义词：complete, conclude, finish, settle|反义词：begin, start, commence|词性:动词
