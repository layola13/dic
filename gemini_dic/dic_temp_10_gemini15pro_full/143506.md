outgnawed:/ˌaʊtˈnɔːd/
verb
咬穿，啃坏; 比...更厉害地咬
having gnawed something more extensively or effectively than another.
The beaver had outgnawed its rival, felling the larger tree first.  海狸比它的对手啃得更厉害，先砍倒了那棵更大的树。
outbite, surpass
(no clear antonym)
词性: 动词
