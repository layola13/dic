chaplainries:/ˈtʃæplɪnriz/
n.
牧师职位；牧师的俸禄；牧师的管辖区域
The position or office of a chaplain, especially in a private chapel or institution.  Also refers to the revenue or endowment attached to such a position, or the district under a chaplain's spiritual jurisdiction.
The king endowed several chaplainries at the new cathedral. 国王在新教堂设立了几个牧师职位。
近义词:chaplainships
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: Noun
