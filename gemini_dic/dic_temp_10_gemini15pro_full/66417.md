executors:/ɪɡˈzɛkjətərz/| n.|执行者，遗嘱执行人|A person or institution appointed by a testator to carry out the terms of their will.|The executors of the estate managed the distribution of assets according to the will.  遗产执行人根据遗嘱管理资产的分配。|Synonyms: administrator, trustee, representative|Antonyms: heir, beneficiary|Noun
