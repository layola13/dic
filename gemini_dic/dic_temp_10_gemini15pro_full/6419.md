amphitheatrically:/ˌæmfɪθiˈætrɪk(ə)li/
adverb
像圆形剧场一样地；阶梯状地
in the manner of an amphitheater; in a way that resembles the tiered seating of an amphitheater.
The houses were arranged amphitheatrically on the hillside, offering stunning views of the valley below.  这些房屋依山而建，像圆形剧场一样排列，可以俯瞰下面山谷的壮丽景色。
The crowd was seated amphitheatrically around the stage.  观众像圆形剧场一样围坐在舞台周围。
近义词:tiered, stepped, graduated
反义词:flat, level
词性:adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek "ἀμφιθέατρον" (amphitheatron),  from "ἀμφί" (amphi, "on both sides, around") + "θέατρον" (theatron, "a place for viewing, a theater"). The suffix "-ically" transforms the noun into an adverb.
