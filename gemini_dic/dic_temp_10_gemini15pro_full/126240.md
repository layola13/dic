mycologist: /maɪˈkɒlədʒɪst/ (UK) /maɪˈkɑːlədʒɪst/ (US)| n.|真菌学家|A scientist who studies fungi.|例句：The mycologist identified the mushroom as a poisonous species.  这位真菌学家将这种蘑菇鉴定为有毒品种。

例句：She consulted a mycologist about the mold growing in her basement. 她向一位真菌学家咨询了她地下室里生长的霉菌。|近义词：fungi expert, mushroom expert|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From myco- + -logist.  "Myco-" comes from the Greek "mykēs" meaning "fungus". "-logist" denotes one who studies or is an expert in a particular subject.
