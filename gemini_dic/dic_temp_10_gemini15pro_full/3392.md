aficionado:/əˌfɪʃjəˈnɑːdoʊ/ (US) /əˌfɪʃjəˈnɑːdəʊ/ (UK)| n. |爱好者；狂热者|A person who is very knowledgeable and enthusiastic about an activity, subject, or pastime.|例句：He is a wine aficionado. 他是一位葡萄酒爱好者。

例句：She is an aficionado of classical music. 她是一位古典音乐爱好者。

近义词：enthusiast, devotee, fan, buff, connoisseur|反义词：critic, detractor|词性：noun
