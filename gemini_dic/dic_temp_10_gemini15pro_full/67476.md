extortionary:/ɪkˈstɔːrʃəneri/
adjective
敲诈的；勒索的；过高的；高得离谱的
Characterized by extortion; using extortion.  Excessively high; exorbitant.
The company was accused of charging extortionary prices for its products.  这家公司被指控对其产品收取过高的价格。
近义词:exorbitant, excessive, outrageous, unreasonable, usurious
反义词:reasonable, fair, moderate, just
词性:adjective

extortion:/ɪkˈstɔːrʃən/
noun
勒索；敲诈；强取； extortion
The act of obtaining something, especially money, through coercion or intimidation.
He was found guilty of extortion and blackmail. 他被判犯有勒索和敲诈罪。
近义词:blackmail, coercion, intimidation, shakedown
反义词:donation, gift
词性:noun
