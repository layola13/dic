erythrocytic:/ɪˌrɪθroʊˈsɪtɪk/ | adj. | 红细胞的，红血球的 | Relating to erythrocytes (red blood cells). |  Erythrocytic disorders can cause anemia. 红细胞疾病会导致贫血。 |  Synonyms: red blood cell,  hematopoietic | Antonyms: None | adjective

etymology->
From erythrocyte + -ic.  Erythrocyte comes from  Greek *erythrós* "red" + *kytos* "hollow vessel, cell".
