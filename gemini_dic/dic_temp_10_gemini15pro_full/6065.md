amentia:/əˈmɛnʃə/ /əˈmɛnʃiə/ | n. |痴呆，精神错乱|A state of mental impairment, especially involving loss of intellectual faculties such as memory and reasoning.  A severe impairment or loss of intellectual capacity and personality integration, due to loss of or damage to neurons in the brain.|  His advancing amentia made it impossible for him to live alone. 他日益严重的痴呆使他无法独自生活。 |Synonyms: dementia, insanity, madness| Antonyms: sanity, lucidity| Noun
