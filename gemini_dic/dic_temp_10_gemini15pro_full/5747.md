alzheimer:/ˈɔːltshaɪmər/ /ˈæltshaɪmər/| n. | 阿尔茨海默病; 老年痴呆症 |A progressive mental deterioration that can occur in middle or old age, due to generalized degeneration of the brain. It is the most common cause of premature senility.| My grandmother suffers from Alzheimer's disease. 我的祖母患有阿尔茨海默病。|Synonyms: dementia, senility|Antonyms:  (Alzheimer's doesn't have a direct antonym referring to a specific disease, but antonyms for its effects could include: mental acuity, cognitive function)|noun
