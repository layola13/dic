docudramatists:/ˈdɒkjuːˈdrɑːmətɪsts/
n.
纪录片剧作家；纪实剧作家
A docudramatist is a playwright who writes docudramas. Docudramas are plays based on real events or historical figures.
Example: The docudramatists meticulously researched the historical period to ensure accuracy in their play.  这些纪录片剧作家仔细研究了历史时期，以确保他们戏剧的准确性。

Example:  Many docudramatists face the challenge of balancing historical accuracy with dramatic storytelling. 许多纪录片剧作家面临着在历史准确性和戏剧性叙事之间取得平衡的挑战。

Synonym:  documentary playwright, historical dramatist
Antonym:  fiction writer, fantasist
词性: noun
