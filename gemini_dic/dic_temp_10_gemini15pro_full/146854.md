overindulge: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒ/ | v. | 过度放纵；沉溺 | To allow oneself to have or do too much of something enjoyable. |  He overindulged in rich food during the holidays. 假期期间他吃太多油腻的食物了。|  pamper, spoil, coddle | deprive, restrict | verb
