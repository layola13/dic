antisentimental:/ˌæntiˌsɛntɪˈmɛntəl/
adjective
反感伤的；反对感伤主义的
Opposed to or rejecting sentimentality, especially excessive or affected emotion.
His antisentimental approach to the subject was refreshing. 他对这个主题的反感伤主义的做法令人耳目一新。
近义词: unsentimental, hard-boiled, cynical
反义词: sentimental, mawkish, emotional
词性: adjective

antisentimental:/ˌæntiˌsɛntɪˈmɛntəl/
noun
反感伤主义者
A person who opposes or rejects sentimentality.
He was an antisentimental to the core, never showing any outward emotion. 他是一个彻头彻尾的反感伤主义者，从不表现出任何外在的情感。
近义词: cynic
反义词: sentimentalist
词性: noun
