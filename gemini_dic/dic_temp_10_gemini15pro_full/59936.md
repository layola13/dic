edgiest:/ˈɛdʒiɪst/
adjective
最前卫的；最尖端的；最具争议性的
Most unconventional, daring, or provocative.
That band plays the edgiest music I've ever heard.  那支乐队演奏的音乐是我听过的最前卫的。
Her fashion sense is edgy, always pushing boundaries. 她的时尚感很前卫，总是挑战常规。
synonyms:avant-garde, cutting-edge, daring, provocative, unconventional, radical, revolutionary, rebellious, nonconformist, iconoclastic, subversive
antonyms:conventional, traditional, mainstream, conservative, orthodox, conformist

词性: adjective
