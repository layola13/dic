circumstanced:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌstænst/ /ˈsɝːkəmˌstænst/| adj.|处于某种情况的，境况…的|Being in a particular condition or situation, especially financial.|He was very poorly circumstanced during his childhood. 他童年时期家境贫寒。|近义词：situated, conditioned, placed|反义词: unconstrained, unrestricted|词性:形容词

circumstanced:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌstænst/ /ˈsɝːkəmˌstænst/| v.|使处于某种情况（通常指财务状况）|To place in a particular condition or situation, especially financial (often used passively).|His family was unfortunately circumstanced after the business failed.  生意失败后，他的家庭经济状况不佳。|近义词：situate, place, position|反义词: liberate, free|词性:动词
