manan:/ˈmɑːnɑːn/| n.| 玛南（菲律宾语，意为“继承人”或“后代”） |A Filipino word meaning "heir" or "descendant". It refers to someone who inherits property, a title, or a responsibility.  It can also be used more generally to refer to future generations.|His grandfather named him manan of the family business. 他的祖父任命他为家族企业的继承人。|Synonyms: heir, successor, descendant|Antonyms: predecessor, ancestor|Noun
