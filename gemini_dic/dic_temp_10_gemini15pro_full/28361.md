caravanning:/ˈkærəvænɪŋ/
n.
旅居汽车旅行；房车旅行
The act of travelling and holidaying in a caravan.
We went caravanning in France last summer. 去年夏天我们开着旅居汽车在法国旅行。

近义词：camping, touring, road trip
反义词：staying home, settling down
词性:名词
