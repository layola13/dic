mellate:/ˈmel.eɪt/ | adj. n.|  蜜般的，蜂蜜味的；蜜的衍生物 |  Relating to, or derived from, honey; containing mellitic acid or a salt of mellitic acid. |  The mellate minerals are rare and usually found in coal deposits. 这些蜜的衍生物矿物很稀有，通常在煤矿床中发现。 |  The honeycomb had a distinctly mellate aroma.  蜂巢有一种明显的蜂蜜香味。 | 近义词: honeyed, sweet | 反义词: bitter, sour | 词性: 形容词，名词

词源: From Latin *mellātus, from mel (“honey”).
