nonsatire:/ˌnɑːnˈsætaɪər/ | n. | 非讽刺; 真实情况 | Something that is genuine and not intended to be satirical. | The news report was nonsatire; it accurately depicted the current political climate.  这则新闻报道并非讽刺；它准确地描绘了当前的政治气候。| 近义词: truth, reality, fact | 反义词: satire, irony, sarcasm | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "satire" (a literary work holding up human vices and follies to ridicule or scorn).  由表示否定的"non-"和"satire"(讽刺)组成.
