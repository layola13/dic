heartfulness:/ˈhɑːrtfʊlnəs/ | n. | 心灵丰盈；真诚；慈悲；用心 | The quality of being full of heart; sincerity, kindness, and compassion.  A way of life characterized by a quiet inner transformation and a commitment to living with love, joy, and peace. |  She approached the situation with heartfulness and understanding. 她以真诚和理解的态度来处理这种情况。 | synonyms: compassion, kindness, sincerity, empathy, benevolence, warmth, love,  | antonyms: cruelty, coldness, indifference, apathy, malevolence, hostility | noun
