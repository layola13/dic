boyos:/ˈbɔɪ.oʊz/| n.|伙计们，男孩们 (爱尔兰俚语)|A slang term, primarily used in Ireland, referring to a group of boys or men, similar to "lads" or "guys".|例句：How are ye, boyos?  你们好吗，伙计们？

例句：The boyos are heading to the pub. 小伙子们要去酒吧了。|近义词：lads, guys, fellas, mates|反义词:girls, women|Noun
