asymptote:/ˈæsɪmptoʊt/ (US) /ˈæsɪmptəʊt/ (UK)| n. |渐近线|A line that continually approaches a given curve but does not meet it at any finite distance.|The graph of the function approaches the x-axis as an asymptote. 函数的图像以x轴为渐近线接近它。|近义词:tangent, limit |反义词:none|noun

asymptotic: /ˌæsɪmˈtɒtɪk/ (US) /ˌæsɪmpˈtɒtɪk/ (UK) | adj. |渐近的|Of or relating to an asymptote.|The curve is asymptotic to the x-axis. 曲线是x轴的渐近线。|近义词:approaching, converging|反义词:diverging|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *asymptote*, from Ancient Greek ἀσύμπτωτος (asúmptōtos, “not falling together, not coincident”), from ἀ- (a-, “not”) + σύν (sún, “together”) + πτωτός (ptōtós, “fallen”).
