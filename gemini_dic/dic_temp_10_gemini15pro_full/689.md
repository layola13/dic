abscond:/əbˈskɒnd/ (UK) /æbˈskɑːnd/ (US)| v.|潜逃，逃走|To leave hurriedly and secretly, typically to avoid detection of or arrest for an unlawful action such as theft.|The cashier absconded with the day's receipts. 收银员带着当天的收入潜逃了。

The suspect absconded to avoid arrest. 嫌疑人潜逃以逃避逮捕。

|近义词：flee, escape, decamp, bolt, make off|反义词：remain, stay, surrender|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abscondere (to hide, conceal), from abs- (away, from) + condere (to put together, store, hide).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He absconds from his responsibilities. = 他逃避责任。
She often absconds from difficult situations. = 她经常逃避困境。

一般过去时:
The thief absconded with the jewels. = 小偷偷走了珠宝。
He absconded from the country after the scandal. = 丑闻之后，他逃离了这个国家。

现在完成时:
The suspect has absconded. = 嫌疑人已潜逃。
They have absconded with the company's funds. = 他们卷走了公司的资金。

过去完成时:
The prisoner had absconded before the guards noticed. = 囚犯在狱警发现之前已经逃走了。
She had absconded with the money before anyone could stop her. = 在任何人阻止她之前，她已经带着钱逃跑了。


将来时:
If he gets the chance, he will abscond. = 如果有机会，他会潜逃。
They will abscond as soon as they can. = 他们会尽快潜逃。

虚拟语气:
If he were to abscond, he would go to Mexico. = 如果他要潜逃，他会去墨西哥。
I wish he wouldn't abscond from his problems. = 我希望他不要逃避他的问题。

被动语态:
The money was absconded with. = 钱被卷走了。
The jewels were absconded with by the thief. = 珠宝被小偷偷走了。

疑问句:
Did he abscond with the money? = 他是不是带着钱逃跑了？
Why did she abscond? = 她为什么潜逃？

否定句:
He didn't abscond, he left openly. = 他没有潜逃，他是公开离开的。
She hasn't absconded yet. = 她还没有潜逃。


PHRASE->

abscond from (prison/the country/the scene) = 从（监狱/国家/现场）潜逃
abscond to (another country/a safe place) = 潜逃到（另一个国家/安全的地方）
attempt to abscond = 试图潜逃
successfully abscond = 成功潜逃
