disembodiment:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbɒdɪmənt/| n. |脱离肉体；脱离实体；抽象化|The disembodiment of the spirit from the body is a common theme in many religions.  灵魂与肉体的分离是许多宗教的共同主题。|The artist explored the concept of disembodiment through abstract sculptures.  这位艺术家通过抽象雕塑探索了脱离实体的概念。|近义词：abstraction, detachment, incorporeality|反义词：embodiment, incarnation, materialization|词性:名词

Etymology:  From dis- "not, opposite of" + embodiment.  Embodiment from embody + -ment. Embody from en- "in" + body.
