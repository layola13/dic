limbering:/ˈlɪmbərɪŋ/ | n. |  热身，准备活动 | The act of making something or someone flexible or supple, especially as preparation for physical activity. |  The athletes were limbering up before the race.  运动员们在赛前做热身。| synonyms: warming up, stretching, loosening up | antonyms: stiffening, tightening | noun

limbering:/ˈlɪmbərɪŋ/ | adj. |  使柔软的，使灵活的 | Making or becoming flexible or supple. | Limbering exercises are important before strenuous activity. 热身运动在剧烈运动前很重要。| synonyms: flexible, supple, pliable | antonyms: stiff, rigid, inflexible | adjective
