dome:/doʊm/| n.|穹顶，圆顶，半球形|A large rounded roof or ceiling, typically hemispherical. |The dome of the cathedral was covered in gold leaf.  大教堂的穹顶覆盖着金箔。| synonyms: cupola, vault | antonyms: base, foundation | noun

dome:/dəʊm/| v.|加圆屋顶于…之上，形成圆顶|To cover with a dome. |They domed the new sports arena. 他们给新的体育馆加了个圆顶。| synonyms: cover, cap | antonyms: uncover, expose | verb
