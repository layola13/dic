excrescence:/ɪkˈskresəns/ /ekˈskresəns/| n. |赘生物，瘤，突起，（不好的）附加物|An outgrowth or enlargement, especially an abnormal one, such as a wart or tumor. Something considered undesirable or superfluous.|The gargoyles were just silly excrescences on the otherwise beautiful building.  这些滴水嘴兽只是原本美丽的建筑上多余的装饰。|近义词：growth, protuberance, lump, outgrowth, appendage|反义词: indentation, concavity|词性:noun

excrescence:/ɪkˈskresəns/ /ekˈskresəns/| n. |（不好的）附加物，多余物|Something considered undesirable or superfluous, added on without essential purpose.|Bureaucratic excrescences make the process unnecessarily complex.  官僚主义的繁文缛节使得这个过程不必要地复杂。|近义词：addition, superfluity, redundancy|反义词: essential, necessity|词性:noun
