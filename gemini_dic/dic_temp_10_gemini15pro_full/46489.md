daintinesses:/ˈdeɪntinɪsɪz/
n.
精致，优雅，美味
The quality of being dainty; delicate beauty or charm; smallness and prettiness.
Her daintinesses of manner charmed everyone. 她优雅的举止迷住了所有人。
Synonyms: delicacy, exquisiteness, elegance, refinement, grace
Antonyms: coarseness, clumsiness, awkwardness, gaucheness
