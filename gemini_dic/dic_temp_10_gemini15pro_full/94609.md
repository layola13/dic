impregnably:/ɪmˈprɛɡnəbli/
adjective
坚不可摧地，无法攻破地
in a way that is impossible to enter, influence, or attack.
The fortress was impregnably defended.  这座堡垒防守得固若金汤。
The castle stood impregnably on the hilltop. 这座城堡牢不可破地矗立在山顶上。
近义词:invulnerably, unassailably, impenetrably
反义词:vulnerably, weakly
词性:adjective
