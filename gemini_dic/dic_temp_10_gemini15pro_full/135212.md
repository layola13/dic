nonoverhead:/ˌnɒnˈəʊvəhɛd/ | adj. | 非间接的，非管理费用的 |  Relating to costs or expenses that are directly associated with producing goods or services, rather than general business expenses like rent or administration.  |  The nonoverhead costs of the project were surprisingly low.  该项目的非间接成本出奇地低。 | direct, operational | overhead, indirect | adjective
