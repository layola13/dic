alleviations:/əˌliːviˈeɪʃənz/
n.
缓解，减轻，缓和
the act of making something less severe or serious
The new medicine provided some alleviation from the pain.  这种新药减轻了一些疼痛。

近义词:relief, mitigation, ease, assuagement
反义词:aggravation, exacerbation, intensification

词性:名词(plural)


词源:
From Late Latin *alleviationem, from alleviatus, past participle of alleviare “to lighten, relieve,” from ad- “to” + levis “light.”
