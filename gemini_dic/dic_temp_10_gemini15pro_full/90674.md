humblest:/ˈhʌmbləst/ (US), /ˈhʌmblɪst/ (UK)| adj.|最谦逊的，最卑微的|Having or showing a modest or low estimate of one's own importance.|He offered his humblest apologies for the mistake. 他对这个错误表示最诚挚的歉意。|Synonyms: modest, unassuming, unpretentious|Antonyms: arrogant, haughty, proud|adjective
