capias:/ˈkeɪpiæs/| n.|传票，逮捕令|A writ ordering the arrest of a named person.|The judge issued a capias for the defendant's arrest. 法官签发了被告的逮捕令。|Synonyms: warrant, summons|Antonyms: release, pardon|noun

capias:/ˈkeɪpiæs/| n.|逮捕，拘留|The act of arresting someone.|The capias resulted in the suspect being taken into custody.  逮捕导致嫌疑人被拘留。|Synonyms: apprehension, detention|Antonyms: liberation, freedom|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *capiās, second-person singular present active subjunctive of Latin capere (“to take, seize”).
