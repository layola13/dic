ingeminate:/ɪnˈdʒɛmɪneɪt/ | adj. | 重复的；加倍的 | Reduplicated; doubled. |  The ingeminate beat of the music created a hypnotic effect.  重复的音乐节拍产生了催眠的效果。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin ingeminatus, past participle of ingeminare (“to redouble, repeat”), from in- (“in, on”) + geminare (“to double, pair”), from geminus (“twin”).

SYNONYMS->
repeat, reiterate, iterate, reduplicate, echo, double

ANTONYMS->
omit, skip, single, singularize
