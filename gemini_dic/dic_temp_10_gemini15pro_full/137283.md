nonsynchronously:/ˌnɒnˈsɪŋkrənəsli/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɪŋkrənəsli/ | adv. | 非同步地，异步地 | Not occurring at the same time; in an unsynchronized manner.  | The data was transmitted nonsynchronously. 数据是非同步传输的。| 近义词: asynchronously, out of sync | 反义词: synchronously, in sync | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin *non.
synchronous (adjective): From Greek synkhronos "simultaneous," from syn- "together" + khronos "time."
-ly (suffix): Forms adverbs from adjectives.
