eleemosynarily:/ˌɛlɪmɒˈsɪnərɪli/ /ˌɛliːmoʊˈsɪnərɪli/
|adv.|以施舍的方式；出于慈善；|Given or done out of or as if out of charity; charitably.  Relating to or dependent on alms; charitable.|He gave eleemosynarily to the beggar. 他以施舍的方式给了乞丐钱。|近义词：charitably, philanthropically|反义词：stingily, meanly|词性:副词
词源：Late Middle English: from late Latin eleemosynarius (from Latin eleemosyna ‘alms’ + -arius) + -ly.
