acquaintable:/əˈkweɪntəbl/
adjective
易于结识的; 平易近人的
Easy to get to know; approachable.
He's a very acquaintable person, always happy to chat. 他是一个很容易结识的人，总是乐于交谈。
She struck me as an acquaintable and friendly colleague. 她给我的印象是一位平易近人、友好的同事。
近义词:approachable, affable, sociable, congenial
反义词:unapproachable, aloof, standoffish
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From acquaint + -able.  Ultimately from Latin *accognitare "to make known," from ad- "to" + cognoscere "to learn, get to know."
