euthanizes: /ˈjuːθəˌnaɪzɪz/
|v.| 对...实施安乐死；使安乐死| To painlessly put to death (a person or animal) suffering from an incurable and painful disease or in irreversible coma.| The vet euthanized the suffering dog.| 近义词: put down, put to sleep| 反义词: resuscitate, revive|词性: 动词
