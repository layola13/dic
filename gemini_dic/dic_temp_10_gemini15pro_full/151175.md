parcellate:/ˈpɑːsəleɪt/ | v. | 划分；分割；分成小块 | To divide into parcels or parts; subdivide. | The land was parcellated into small plots for housing. 这片土地被分割成小块用于建造房屋。 | The developer parcellated the large estate into individual lots. 开发商将这片大型地产分割成独立的土地。 | 近义词: divide, subdivide, partition, segment | 反义词: combine, unite, merge | 词性: 动词

parcellation:/ˌpɑːsəˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 划分；分割 | The act of parcellating or the state of being parcellated. | The parcellation of the land led to disputes among the owners. 土地的分割导致了业主之间的纠纷。| The parcellation of the company's assets was complex. 公司资产的分割很复杂。 | 近义词: division, subdivision, partitioning, segmentation | 反义词: combination, unification, merger | 词性: 名词
