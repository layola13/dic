antirationalism:/ˌæntiˈræʃənəlɪzəm/
n.
反理性主义
Opposition to or rejection of reason or rationalism as the basis of thought and action, often emphasizing intuition, feeling, or faith.
The rise of antirationalism in the 20th century led to various intellectual and artistic movements.  二十世纪反理性主义的兴起导致了各种知识和艺术运动。
His antirationalism made him susceptible to conspiracy theories. 他的反理性主义使他容易相信阴谋论。
近义词:irrationalism, obscurantism
反义词:rationalism, reason
词性:名词
