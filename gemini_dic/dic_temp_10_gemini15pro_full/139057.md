oas:/oʊˈeɪs/ | n. |绿洲;  舒适的地方; |A fertile spot in a desert where water is found. A pleasant or restful place or period in the midst of difficult or hectic surroundings.|  例句：The oasis provided much-needed relief from the scorching desert sun. 这片绿洲使得人们从灼热的沙漠阳光下得到了急需的缓解。

例句：The library was an oasis of calm in the bustling city.  在这个繁忙的城市里，图书馆是一片宁静的绿洲。|近义词：haven, refuge, sanctuary, retreat |反义词:desert, wasteland| 词性:名词
