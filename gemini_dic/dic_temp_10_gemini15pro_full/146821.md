overimposes:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈpoʊzɪz/ /ˌəʊvərɪmˈpəʊzɪz/
verb
叠加，重叠，将…强加于
To place or lay (one thing) over something else, typically so that both are still apparent.  The artist overimposed one image on another. 艺术家将一个图像叠加在另一个图像上。
The software overimposes graphics onto the video.  这个软件将图形叠加到视频上。
superimpose, overlay
