haemodialyser:/ˌhiːməʊˈdaɪəlaɪzər/ (US) /ˌhiːməʊˈdaɪəlaɪzə(r)/ (UK)| n.|血液透析机|A machine used in haemodialysis to filter waste products from the blood.|The haemodialyser is essential for patients with kidney failure.  血液透析机对于肾衰竭患者至关重要。|
haemodialyser:| n.|血液透析机|Haemodialysis requires a specialised machine, the haemodialyser, to cleanse the blood. 血液透析需要一台专门的机器，即血液透析机，来净化血液。|Synonym: dialysis machine, artificial kidney|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps healthy kidney)|noun
