mould:/moʊld/ (US) /məʊld/ (UK)| n. v.| 模具，霉菌；塑造，浇铸 |A hollow container used to give shape to molten or hot material when it cools and hardens; a furry growth of minute fungi occurring typically in moist warm conditions. |The jelly was set in a heart-shaped mould. 果冻被放在心形模具里凝固。| synonyms: cast, form, matrix, die, shape, mildew, fungus | antonyms: deform, distort | noun

mould:/moʊld/ (US) /məʊld/ (UK)| v.| 塑造，浇铸；使形成 |To shape or form (something) |He moulded the clay into a small figure. 他把黏土塑造成一个小雕像。| synonyms: shape, form, fashion, model | antonyms: deform, distort | verb
