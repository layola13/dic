nephew: /ˈnefjuː/ (US) /ˈnevjuː/ (UK) | n. |侄子;外甥|A son of one's brother or sister, or of one's brother-in-law or sister-in-law. |My nephew is starting school next year. 我的侄子明年就要上学了。| My sister's son, my nephew, is a talented musician. 我姐姐的儿子，我的侄子，是一位才华横溢的音乐家。 |近义词: kinsman, relative |反义词: N/A |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *neveu* (modern French *neveu*), from Latin *nepōs* "grandson, nephew," later "descendant."
