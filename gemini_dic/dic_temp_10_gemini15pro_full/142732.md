otolaryngology:/ˌoʊtəˌlærɪŋˈɡɑːlədʒi/ /ˌəʊtəˌlærɪŋˈɡɒlədʒi/
n.
耳鼻喉科
The study and treatment of diseases of the ear, nose, and throat.
Otolaryngology is a surgical subspecialty within medicine that deals with conditions of the ear, nose, and throat (ENT) and related structures of the head and neck.  耳鼻喉科是医学中的一个外科分支，处理耳朵、鼻子和喉咙 (ENT) 以及头部和颈部相关结构的疾病。
He specializes in otolaryngology and treats a variety of ear, nose, and throat conditions. 他专攻耳鼻喉科，治疗各种耳鼻喉疾病。
近义词:ENT
