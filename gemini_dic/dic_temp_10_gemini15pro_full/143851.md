outplay:/ˌaʊtˈpleɪ/| v. | 以更高超的技巧胜过，智胜 | To defeat or get the better of (someone) by greater skill or cleverness. |  例句：The experienced chess player outplayed his younger opponent.  经验丰富的棋手以更高超的技巧战胜了他的年轻对手。| 近义词：outmaneuver, outsmart, surpass, defeat | 反义词：be outplayed, lose to | 词性: 动词

outplay:/ˌaʊtˈpleɪ/| v. | 比...表现更好 | To perform better than. | 例句：She completely outplayed the rest of the orchestra. 她在整个管弦乐队中表现得最好。| 近义词：excel, surpass, outperform | 反义词：be outperformed, underperform | 词性: 动词
