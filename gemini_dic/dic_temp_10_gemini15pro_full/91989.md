hypernormality:/ˌhaɪpərˈnɔːrmælɪti/ | n. | 超常态，极端正常 | A state of exaggerated normality, often appearing artificial or forced.  It can refer to a situation where adherence to social norms becomes excessive, rigid, and potentially dysfunctional.  It can also describe a statistical concept where data points fall outside the expected range of a normal distribution. |  The pristine lawns and eerily quiet streets gave the neighborhood a sense of hypernormality.  那些整洁的草坪和安静得可怕的街道让这个社区有一种极端正常的感觉。

The protagonist's hypernormality masked a deep-seated anxiety. 主人公极端正常的表象掩盖了深层的焦虑。 | 近义词: overconformity, excessive normalcy, unnatural normalcy | 反义词: abnormality, deviance, eccentricity | 词性: noun
