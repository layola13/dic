meridian: /məˈrɪdiən/ /məˈrɪdiən/
n.|子午线，经线；顶点，最高点；全盛时期|A circle of constant longitude passing through a given place on the earth's surface and the terrestrial poles.|The prime meridian passes through Greenwich. 本初子午线穿过格林威治。|Synonym: zenith, apex, culmination, peak |Antonym: nadir, bottom, low point
词性: 名词

meridian: /məˈrɪdiən/ /məˈrɪdiən/
adj.|子午线的，经线的；正午的，中午的|Of or relating to a meridian or midday.|The meridian sun is very strong. 正午的阳光很强烈。|Synonym: midday, noontide |Antonym: midnight
词性: 形容词


词源:  From Late Latin *merīdiānus*, from Latin merīdiēs (“midday”), from medius (“middle”) + diēs (“day”).
