go-between:/ˈɡoʊ bɪˌtwiːn/ (US), /ˈɡəʊ bɪˌtwiːn/ (UK)| n.|中间人，媒人|A person who communicates or negotiates between two parties, especially when they are unable or unwilling to deal with each other directly.|She acted as a go-between for the two feuding families. 她充当了这两个不和家庭的中间人。|synonyms: intermediary, mediator, broker, negotiator|antonyms: principal, protagonist|noun

go-between:/ˈɡoʊ bɪˌtwiːn/ (US), /ˈɡəʊ bɪˌtwiːn/ (UK)| adj.|媒介的，中间人的|Acting as a link between two parties.|He played a go-between role in the peace talks. 他在和平谈判中扮演了中间人的角色。|synonyms: mediating, intermediary|antonyms: direct|adjective
