chalybite:/ˈkælɪbaɪt/ | n. | 菱铁矿 | An iron carbonate mineral (FeCO3), typically occurring as brown or black rhombohedral crystals. It is an important ore of iron. | Chalybite is commonly found in sedimentary rocks. 菱铁矿常见于沉积岩中。| Chalybite is a major source of iron. 菱铁矿是铁的主要来源。| synonyms: siderite, ironstone | antonyms: N/A | noun
