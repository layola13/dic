lull's:/lʌlz/| n.| 平静；间歇；暂停；(尤指暴风雨前的)平静期| a temporary period of quiet or reduced activity during something unpleasant |The lull in the fighting gave the villagers a chance to escape.  战斗中的平静给了村民们逃脱的机会。|Synonyms: pause, respite, break, intermission|Antonyms:  activity, continuation,  resumption|noun
