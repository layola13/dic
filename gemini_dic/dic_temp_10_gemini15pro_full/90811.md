humpy:/ˈhʌmpi/ | adj. | 驼背的，隆起的 | Having a hump or humps; rounded or protuberant. | The camel has a humpy back. 骆驼的背部隆起。| The road was humpy and difficult to drive on. 这条路崎岖不平，难以行驶。 |  近义词: bumpy, uneven, knobby | 反义词: smooth, flat | 词性: adjective

词源: From hump + -y.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hump-, of uncertain origin.
