bunker mentality:/ˈbʌŋkər mɛnˈtælɪti/
noun
碉堡心态; 闭关自守的心态
A defensive and isolated mindset characterized by prioritizing self-preservation and resisting change or external influence, often seen in groups under pressure or facing criticism.
The team developed a bunker mentality after losing several games in a row, refusing to listen to outside advice.  该队在连输几场比赛后形成了一种碉堡心态，拒绝听取外部意见。
The company's bunker mentality prevented it from adapting to the changing market. 这家公司的碉堡心态使其无法适应不断变化的市场。
近义词:siege mentality, defensive posture, insularity, parochialism
反义词:openness, receptiveness, adaptability
