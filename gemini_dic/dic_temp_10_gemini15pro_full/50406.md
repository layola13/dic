depositories:/dɪˈpɒzɪtɔːriz/ /dɪˈpɑːzɪtɔːriz/
n.
存放处，保管处，储存库
A place where something is deposited for safekeeping or storage.
The bank serves as a depository for the company's funds.  这家银行充当公司的资金存放处。
Government documents are held in secure depositories. 政府文件保存在安全的储存库中。
近义词: repositories, storehouses, vaults
反义词: N/A
词性: 名词


depository:/dɪˈpɒzɪtəri/ /dɪˈpɑːzɪtɔːri/
n.
存放处，保管处，储存库
A place where something is deposited for safekeeping or storage.
The museum is a depository of local history.  这家博物馆是当地历史的存放处。
The library acts as a depository for rare books and manuscripts.  这家图书馆充当珍稀书籍和手稿的保管处。
近义词: repository, storehouse, vault
反义词: N/A
词性: 名词


词源:  From Late Latin *depositorium, from Latin depositus (past participle of deponere "to lay down, deposit"), from de- "down" + ponere "to put, place".
