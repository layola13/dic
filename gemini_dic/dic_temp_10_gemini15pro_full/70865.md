The correct spelling is "final."  "Finnal" is a misspelling.

final: /ˈfaɪnl/ | adj. n. | 最后的，最终的；决赛；期末考试 |  Relating to or occurring at the end; last in a series. Also, an examination at the end of a term, course, or study program. | The final exam was difficult. 期末考试很难。 | The final decision rests with the CEO. 最终决定权在于首席执行官。 | 近义词: last, ultimate, concluding | 反义词: first, initial, beginning | 词性: adjective, noun

词源: From Old French and Latin *finalis, from finis "end, boundary, limit."
