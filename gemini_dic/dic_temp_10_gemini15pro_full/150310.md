pangaea:/pænˈdʒiːə/ | n. |盘古大陆；泛大陆|A supercontinent comprising all the continental crust of the earth, postulated to have existed in late Paleozoic and Mesozoic times before it broke into Gondwana and Laurasia.|Scientists believe that dinosaurs roamed Pangaea. 科学家认为恐龙曾在盘古大陆上漫游。|The continents were once joined together in a single landmass called Pangaea. 各大洲曾经连接在一起，形成一个叫做盘古大陆的单一陆块。|Synonym: supercontinent |Antonym: N/A | noun

pangaeas:/pænˈdʒiːəz/ | n. |盘古大陆们 (通常指假设存在的其他超级大陆)|Hypothetical supercontinents, similar to Pangaea, that may have existed in Earth's geological past.|Some geologists theorize about the existence of pangaeas before Pangaea. 一些地质学家推测在盘古大陆之前存在过其他的超级大陆。|Researchers are studying the possibility of future pangaeas forming. 研究人员正在研究未来可能形成超级大陆的可能性。|Synonym: supercontinents |Antonym: N/A | noun
