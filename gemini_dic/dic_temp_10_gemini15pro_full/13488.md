attacca:/əˈtækə/ | adv. |立即，紧接着|Immediately; without a break or pause.  Used as a direction in music. |The symphony ends with an *attacca* to the final movement. 交响乐以紧接着的最终乐章结束。|Synonyms: directly, instantly, forthwith, straightaway |Antonyms: gradually, slowly |adverb

attacca:/əˈtækə/ | n. |紧接着|An immediate transition without a pause, especially in music. |The *attacca* between the two movements created a sense of urgency.  两个乐章之间的紧接着的过渡营造了一种紧迫感。|Synonyms: segue, transition, link |Antonyms: pause, break, interruption |noun
