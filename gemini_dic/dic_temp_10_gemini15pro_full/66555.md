exhibiting:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪtɪŋ/
| v. |展出；表现；呈现|Presenting or displaying something; demonstrating a particular quality, feeling, or behavior.|The museum is exhibiting a collection of ancient artifacts.  博物馆正在展出一批古代文物。|
| |The patient is exhibiting signs of improvement. 病人正在表现出好转的迹象。|近义词：displaying, showing, demonstrating, presenting, revealing, manifesting|反义词：concealing, hiding, suppressing|词性:动词(present participle of exhibit)
exhibiting:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪtɪŋ/
| adj. |展出的；表现的|Used to describe something that is being shown or displayed, or that demonstrates a particular quality.|The exhibiting artist received critical acclaim.  参展艺术家获得了评论界的赞誉。|
| |The exhibiting behavior was concerning.  表现出的行为令人担忧。|近义词：displayed, shown, demonstrated, presented, revealed, manifested|反义词：concealed, hidden, suppressed|词性:形容词
