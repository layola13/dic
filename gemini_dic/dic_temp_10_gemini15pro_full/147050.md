overlead:/ˌoʊvərˈliːd/
verb
超载，过度负荷
to give someone too much work or too many responsibilities
例句：The team was overleaded with projects and couldn't meet deadlines.  这个团队项目超载，无法按时完成任务。

例句：Don't overlead yourself with too many tasks at once. 不要一次给自己太多的任务，以免超负荷。
近义词：overload, overburden
反义词：underload
词性：v.


overlead:/ˌoʊvərˈliːd/
noun
超载，过度负荷
an excessive load or burden
例句：The bridge collapsed under the overlead.  桥梁因超载而倒塌。

例句：The electrical system experienced an overlead and shorted out.  电路系统超载并短路了。
近义词：overload, excess
反义词：underload, shortage
词性：n.
