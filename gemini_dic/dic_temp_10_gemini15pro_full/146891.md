overinhibits:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈhɪbɪts/| v. |过度抑制|To inhibit excessively; to restrain or suppress beyond what is normal or desirable.|He overinhibits his emotions, rarely showing any outward sign of joy or sadness. 他过度抑制自己的情绪，很少表露出任何喜悦或悲伤的迹象。|The medication overinhibits nerve activity, causing drowsiness. 这种药物过度抑制神经活动，导致嗜睡。|近义词：suppress, repress, stifle|反义词: encourage, stimulate|词性:动词
