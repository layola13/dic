legalists:/ˈliːɡəlɪsts/
noun
法家;法学家;法律主义者
A person who believes in or adheres strictly to the law, especially to the letter rather than the spirit of the law.
The legalists believed that strict adherence to the law was the key to a stable society. 法家认为，严格遵守法律是社会稳定的关键。
The emperor relied heavily on the advice of his legalists.  皇帝非常依赖他的法学家的建议。

近义词:lawyer, jurist, legal scholar
反义词:anarchist, rebel, revolutionary
词性:n.
