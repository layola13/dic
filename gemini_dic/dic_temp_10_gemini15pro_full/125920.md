museumish: /mjuˈziːəmɪʃ/
adjective
博物馆似的，像博物馆展品的
Resembling or characteristic of a museum, especially in being old-fashioned, dusty, or uninteresting.  Evoking the atmosphere of a museum, often in a slightly negative or ironic way.
This room feels a bit museumish with all these antiques.  这个房间摆满了古董，感觉有点像博物馆。
The house was filled with museumish furniture. 这房子里摆满了像博物馆展品一样的家具。
近义词: antiquated, outdated, fusty, dusty
反义词: modern, contemporary, up-to-date
词性: adjective


词源:
From museum + -ish. The suffix "-ish" is used to form adjectives meaning "somewhat like" or "having the quality of."
