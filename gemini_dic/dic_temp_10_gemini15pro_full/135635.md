nonpragmatically:/ˌnɒnpræɡˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 非实用地；非语用地 | In a way that is not concerned with practical considerations or effects, especially in language use; without regard to context or real-world implications. |  He approached the problem nonpragmatically, focusing on theoretical principles rather than practical solutions. 他非实用地处理这个问题，专注于理论原则而不是实际的解决方案。 | She studied the text nonpragmatically, analyzing its grammatical structure without considering its intended meaning. 她非实用地研究文本，分析其语法结构而不考虑其预期的含义。 | 近义词: impractically, unrealistically, theoretically | 反义词: pragmatically, practically, realistically | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"
pragmatic (adjective): from Greek "pragma" meaning "deed, act, affair"
-ally (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
