communalization:/kəˌmjuːnəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
共有化，公社化
The process of making something communal; the transfer of property from private to communal ownership.
The communalization of land led to disputes among the farmers. 土地的共有化导致了农民之间的纠纷。
collectivization, socialization
privatization, individualization
noun
