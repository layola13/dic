equisonance:/ˈɛkwɪsənəns/ /ˈiːkwɪsənəns/
n.
均音，谐音，同音
Agreement or similarity of sound; the use of words having the same or similar vowel sounds.
The poet used equisonance to create a sense of flowing rhythm. 诗人用谐音来营造一种流畅的节奏感。
近义词：assonance, consonance, rhyme
反义词：dissonance
词性: noun
