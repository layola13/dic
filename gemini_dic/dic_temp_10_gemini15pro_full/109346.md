leninist:/ˈlɛnɪnɪst/| adj. n.|列宁主义的；列宁主义者|Of or relating to the political theories of Vladimir Lenin, especially the doctrines of democratic centralism and the dictatorship of the proletariat.|例句：The party followed a strict Leninist ideology.  这个党奉行严格的列宁主义意识形态。|例句：He was a dedicated Leninist, believing in the revolutionary potential of the working class. 他是一位忠诚的列宁主义者，相信工人阶级的革命潜力。|近义词：Marxist, communist, Bolshevik|反义词：capitalist, anti-communist|词性:形容词, 名词
Etymology: From the name of Vladimir Lenin (1870-1924), Russian revolutionary and political theorist + -ist.
