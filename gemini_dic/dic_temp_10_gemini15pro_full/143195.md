outcompliments:/ˌaʊtˈkɑːmplɪmənts/
|v.|胜过…的恭维；过分恭维|
|To surpass someone in giving compliments; to compliment excessively.|
|She outcomplimented him by praising not only his work but also his kindness and generosity.|她不仅赞扬了他的工作，还赞扬了他的善良和慷慨，恭维得他无以应对。|
|近义词: outflatter, overpraise|
|反义词: criticize, disparage|
|词性: verb (动词)|
