ostentatious:/ˌɒstenˈteɪʃəs/ /ˌɑːstenˈteɪʃəs/| adj.|炫耀的，卖弄的|Characterized by vulgar or pretentious display; designed to impress or attract notice.|He drove an ostentatious sports car. 他开着一辆炫耀的跑车。|Synonyms: showy, pretentious, flamboyant, flashy|Antonyms: modest, unassuming, understated|adjective

ostentation:/ˌɒstenˈteɪʃn/ /ˌɑːstenˈteɪʃn/| n.|炫耀，卖弄|Pretentious and vulgar display, especially of wealth and possessions, intended to impress or attract notice.|Her ostentation was evident in her designer clothes and expensive jewelry. 她的炫耀体现在她穿的名牌服装和昂贵的珠宝上。|Synonyms: showiness, pretentiousness, flamboyance, flashiness|Antonyms: modesty, humility, understatement|noun

ostensive:/ɒˈstensɪv/ /ɑːˈstensɪv/| adj.|表面上的，公开的; 明显的，显而易见的|Appearing or professed to be true or genuine, but not necessarily so; apparent, evident.|The ostensive reason for his visit was business, but he also wanted to see his family. 他表面上的访问原因是商务，但他也想看看他的家人。|Synonyms: apparent, seeming, outward, superficial, professed|Antonyms: real, genuine, actual, hidden, concealed|adjective
