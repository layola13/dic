oilcloths:/ˈɔɪlˌklɔːθs/, /ˈɔɪlˌklɑːðz/ | n. |油布；防水布 |A piece of cloth treated with oil or other waterproof substance, used to cover tables or other surfaces. | She wiped down the oilcloth on the kitchen table. 她擦了擦厨房桌子上的油布。| The picnic basket included a brightly patterned oilcloth.  野餐篮里有一块图案鲜艳的油布。| synonyms: tarpaulin, oilskin, groundsheet | antonyms: absorbent cloth, permeable fabric | noun
