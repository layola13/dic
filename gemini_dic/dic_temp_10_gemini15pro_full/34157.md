clamatorial:/klæməˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
喧闹的；吵闹的；爱叫的
Of, relating to, or characterized by loud cries or calls, especially of birds or animals.
例句：The clamatorial calls of the monkeys echoed through the jungle. 猴子喧闹的叫声在丛林中回荡。

例句：The bird's clamatorial song pierced the quiet morning air.  鸟儿喧闹的歌声划破了寂静的早晨空气。

近义词：noisy, loud, vociferous, boisterous
反义词：quiet, silent, hushed
词性：adjective
