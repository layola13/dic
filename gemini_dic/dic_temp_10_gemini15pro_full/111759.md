lodestone:/ˈloʊdˌstoʊn/| n.|天然磁石，吸引人的东西|A piece of magnetite or other naturally magnetized mineral, able to attract iron.  Something that strongly attracts people or things.|The lodestone of their policy is economic development. 他们的政策核心是经济发展。|Synonyms: magnet, attraction, allure, draw, pull|Antonyms: repellent, deterrent|Noun
