bunchier:/ˈbʌntʃiər/
adjective
更成串的，更密集的
more bunched together; forming more bunches.
The grapes this year are bunchier than last year. 今年的葡萄比去年长得更成串。
近义词：clustered, denser, thicker
反义词：sparser, looser, scattered
词性:adjective

bunchier:/ˈbʌntʃiər/
adjective
(口语)更矮胖的，更粗壮的 (Informal) shorter and thicker in build.
He's looking bunchier these days. 他最近看起来更矮胖了。
近义词：stockier, chunkier, stubbier
反义词：taller, thinner, slenderer
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From bunch + -ier (comparative suffix). Ultimately from a Scandinavian source akin to Norwegian *bunke "heap, pile."
