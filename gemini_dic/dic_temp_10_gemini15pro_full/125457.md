multiplicand:/ˌmʌltɪplɪˈkænd/| n.|被乘数|The number that is multiplied by another number (the multiplier).|Example 1: In the equation 5 x 2 = 10, 5 is the multiplicand.  在等式 5 x 2 = 10 中，5是被乘数。

Example 2: The multiplicand was too large to calculate in my head.  这个被乘数太大了，我无法心算。|近义词:factor, number|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
