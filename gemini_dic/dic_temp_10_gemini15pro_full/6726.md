analogy:/əˈnælədʒi/ | n. | 类比；相似；比拟 | A comparison between one thing and another, typically for the purpose of explanation or clarification. |  Life is often compared to a journey.  人生常被比作一次旅程。

The teacher drew an analogy between the human brain and a computer. 老师将人脑和电脑进行了类比。 | synonyms: comparison, similarity, likeness, parallel, resemblance | antonyms: difference, dissimilarity, contrast | noun
