crepusculine:/krɪˈpʌskjʊlaɪn/ /krɪˈpʌskjəˌlaɪn/| adj.|暮色的；朦胧的；黄昏的|Relating to twilight; dim; resembling or suggesting twilight.  Pertaining to or like twilight; occurring or active in twilight.|The crepusculine light cast long shadows across the lawn. 暮色使草坪上投下了长长的影子。|Synonyms: twilight, dusky, shadowy, dim, crepuscular|Antonyms: bright, daylight, light, sunny|Adjective

crepusculine:/krɪˈpʌskjʊlaɪn/ /krɪˈpʌskjəˌlaɪn/| n.|暮色；黄昏时分活动的动物|The time of twilight; an animal active during twilight.|The owl is a creature of the crepusculine. 猫头鹰是一种在黄昏时分活动的生物。|Synonyms: twilight, dusk, sundown|Antonyms: dawn, daybreak, sunrise|Noun
