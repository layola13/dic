achromatin:/eɪˈkroʊmətɪn/| n. |非染色质|The part of the chromatin complex of the nucleus that stains only faintly.|The researchers stained the cell's nucleus to observe the achromatin. 研究人员对细胞核进行染色以观察非染色质。

Achromatin is less condensed than heterochromatin and is believed to be actively involved in gene transcription.  非染色质比异染色质浓缩程度低，并被认为积极参与基因转录。|近义词: euchromatin|反义词: heterochromatin|词性:noun
