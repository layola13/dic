healful:/ˈhiːlfʊl/ | adj. | 有益健康的，促进健康的 | Conducive to good health; wholesome. |  A healthful diet is important for maintaining good health.  有益健康的饮食对保持健康很重要。

A healthful walk in the countryside. 在乡间进行一次有益健康的散步。 | 近义词: healthy, wholesome, salutary, beneficial | 反义词: unhealthy, unwholesome, harmful, detrimental | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Middle English *helful, from Old English *hǣlful, from hǣl (“health, wholeness, salvation”) + -ful.  Cognate with Dutch heilzaam (“healthful”), German heilsam (“healthful”).
