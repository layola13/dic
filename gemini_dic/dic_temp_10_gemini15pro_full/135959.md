nonqualifying:/ˌnɑːnˈkwɑːlɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
不合格的，没有资格的
Not meeting the requirements or criteria for something; ineligible.
例句：
Nonqualifying expenses cannot be claimed on your tax return.
不符合条件的费用不能在你的纳税申报表上申报。

The nonqualifying loan had a higher interest rate.
不合格的贷款利率更高。

近义词：ineligible, unsuitable, unacceptable
反义词：qualifying, eligible, suitable
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + qualifying (present participle of qualify).  "Qualify" ultimately comes from the Latin "qualitas" meaning "quality".  The prefix "non-" negates the meaning of "qualifying".
