overboil:/ˌoʊvərˈbɔɪl/| v. |煮沸过头，过度沸腾|To boil a liquid for too long or at too high a temperature.|The milk overboiled and made a mess on the stove. 牛奶煮沸过头了，弄得炉子上乱七八糟。|
The chef overboiled the eggs, making them rubbery. 厨师把鸡蛋煮得太久了，煮成了橡胶状。|近义词: boil over |反义词: simmer, undercook|verb|

overboil:/ˌoʊvərˈbɔɪl/| n. |过度沸腾的状态|The state of having been boiled for too long.|The overboil left a sticky residue on the pot. 煮沸过头在锅上留下粘稠的残留物。| There was an overboil and the water spilled everywhere. 水沸腾过度，溢得到处都是。|近义词: - |反义词: -|noun|
