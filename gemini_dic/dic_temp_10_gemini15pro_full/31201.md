changing:/ˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 改变的，变化的 | undergoing transformation or alteration | The changing seasons bring a variety of weather.  变化的季节带来了各种各样的天气。| synonyms: altering, shifting, varying, evolving, transforming | antonyms: constant, static, unchanging, fixed | adjective

changing:/ˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/ | n. | 改变，变化 | the act or process of becoming different | The changing of the guard happens every hour. 卫兵换岗每小时进行一次。 | synonyms: alteration, shift, variation, evolution, transformation | antonyms: constancy, stability | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *changinge, present participle of changen (“to change”), from Old French changier, from Late Latin *cambiare, from Latin cambire (“to barter, exchange”), of Celtic origin.
