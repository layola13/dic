hexahedral:/ˌhɛksəˈhiːdrəl/
adjective
六面的；立方形的
Having six faces or sides.
A cube is a hexahedral figure.  一个立方体是一个六面体图形。
The mineral pyrite often forms hexahedral crystals. 黄铁矿通常形成六面体晶体。
Synonyms: cubic, six-sided
Antonyms: non-cubic, irregular


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hexa- (six) + -hedral (having planes or sides)
