morass:/məˈræs/ /mɔːˈræs/| n. |沼泽；困境；混乱|An area of muddy or boggy ground. A complicated or confused situation.|例句：The army became bogged down in the morass. 军队陷入了沼泽地。

例句：She's stuck in a morass of paperwork. 她陷在一堆文书工作的泥潭里。|近义词：swamp, bog, marsh, quagmire, predicament, dilemma|反义词: order, clarity, solution|词性:noun
