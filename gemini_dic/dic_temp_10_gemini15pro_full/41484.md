cornrows: /ˈkɔːrnroʊz/ (US) /ˈkɔːnˌrəʊz/ (UK)| n.| 辫子发型，玉米垄发型 |A hairstyle, originating in Africa, in which the hair is braided very close to the scalp, creating a raised row that resembles a row of corn. |She wore her hair in cornrows. 她把头发扎成了玉米垄发型。|近义词: braids, plaits |反义词: (No direct antonym for a hairstyle. Could consider "loose hair" or "unbraided hair") | Noun

cornrow: /ˈkɔːrnroʊ/ (US) /ˈkɔːnˌrəʊ/ (UK) | vt.| 把头发编成玉米垄发型 |To braid hair in the cornrow style.|She cornrowed her daughter's hair for the school play. 她为女儿的学校演出编了玉米垄发型。|近义词: braid, plait |反义词: (No direct antonym for a styling action.  Could consider "unbraid") | Verb
