fleshling:/ˈflɛʃlɪŋ/| n. |凡人，肉体凡胎|A human being, often used in a derogatory or demeaning way by supernatural beings to emphasize mortality and weakness.  It highlights the fragility and limitations of human existence in comparison to immortal or more powerful entities.|The vampire sneered at the fleshlings, knowing their lives were fleeting.  吸血鬼轻蔑地看着这些凡人，他知道他们的生命转瞬即逝。

The demon lord saw fleshlings as nothing more than playthings.  恶魔领主将凡人视为玩物。|近义词：mortal, human|反义词：god, deity, immortal|词性:noun
