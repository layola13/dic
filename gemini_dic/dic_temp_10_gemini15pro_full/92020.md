hyperoxygenized:/ˌhaɪpərˈɒksɪdʒənaɪzd/
adjective
过度氧化的，富氧的
Containing or having received more oxygen than normal.
The hyperoxygenized blood helps to heal the wound. 富氧血液有助于伤口愈合。
The patient was given hyperoxygenized air. 病人被给予了富氧空气。

近义词: oxygen-rich, superoxygenated
反义词: hypoxic, anoxic
词性: adjective

词源:
hyper- (prefix): over, excessive, above normal (from Greek "hyper")
oxygen (root): a colorless, odorless gas essential for respiration (from French "oxygène")
-ize (suffix): to cause to be or become; to subject to a process (from Greek "-izein")
-ed (suffix): forming the past participle of verbs, or adjectives from nouns or verbs (from Old English "-ed," "-od," "-ad")
