deconsecrating:/ˌdiːkɒnˈsɪkreɪtɪŋ/
verb
取消祝圣，使不再神圣
The act of depriving a consecrated place or object of its religious significance.
The church was deconsecrated and turned into a community center.  教堂被取消祝圣，变成了一个社区中心。
desacralizing, secularizing
consecrating, sanctifying
verb
