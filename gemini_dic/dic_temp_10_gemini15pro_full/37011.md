columella:/ˌkɒljʊˈmɛlə/ /ˌkɑːljəˈmɛlə/| n. |小柱，轴柱，耳蜗|A small column or central axis, especially in plants or animals.  Specifically, the bony or cartilaginous central axis of the cochlea of the inner ear.|The columella of this flower is quite prominent.  这朵花的小柱非常突出。|近义词：axis, pillar, shaft|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

columella:/ˌkɒljʊˈmɛlə/ /ˌkɑːljəˈmɛlə/| n. |小柱|The columella a small bone or piece of cartilage that transmits vibrations across the middle ear in amphibians, reptiles, and birds. It is analogous to the stapes in mammals.|Birds use their columella to hear. 鸟类用它们的耳柱骨来听声音。|近义词：stapes (in mammals)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *columella*, diminutive of *columna* ("column").
