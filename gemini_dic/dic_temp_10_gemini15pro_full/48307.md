decrepitly:/dɪˈkrɛpɪtli/
adverb
衰弱地，破旧地
in a decrepit manner; feebly and shakily.
The old man walked decrepitly across the street. 老人步履蹒跚地穿过街道。
近义词:frailly, weakly, feebly
反义词:strongly, robustly, vigorously
词性:adverb

词源: From Late Latin *decrepitus, past participle of decrepare (“to crackle, creak”), from de- + crepare (“to rattle, creak, crack”).
