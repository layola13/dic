outshrilled:/ˌaʊtˈʃrɪld/
verb
胜过…的尖叫声；盖过…的喊叫声
To scream or shriek more loudly than someone or something else.
The soprano outshrilled the other singers in the chorus. 女高音的歌声盖过了合唱团里其他歌手的声音。
The excited fans outshrilled the referee's whistle.  兴奋的球迷的喊叫声盖过了裁判的哨声。
Synonyms: outscream, outyell, drown out
Antonyms: be drowned out, be quieter than
词性: 动词
