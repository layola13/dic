diversionist:/daɪˈvɜːrʒənɪst/ /dɪˈvɜːrʒənɪst/ | n. | 转移注意者；牵制者；扰乱者 | someone who uses diversionary tactics, especially to distract attention from something else | The magician employed a diversionist to distract the audience while he performed the trick. 魔术师雇了一个转移注意的人，以便在他表演魔术时分散观众的注意力。 | synonyms: saboteur, agitator, disruptor, troublemaker | antonyms: conciliator, peacemaker | noun
