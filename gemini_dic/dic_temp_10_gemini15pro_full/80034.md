go-together:/ɡoʊ təˈɡɛðər/| adj.|相配的，协调的，配套的|Things that go together are things that match or are associated with each other.  They can be complementary, harmonious, or naturally paired.  Sometimes they are items that are typically used or found together.|This shirt and tie go together perfectly. 这件衬衫和领带很相配。|近义词：match, suit, complement, harmonize, coordinate|反义词:clash, disagree, mismatch|adjective
