hodful:/ˈhɒdfʊl/ (UK) /ˈhɑːdfʊl/ (US)| n. |一铲之量；一满铲|The amount that a hod can carry. A hod is a V-shaped trough with a long handle, used for carrying bricks or mortar.|He carried a hodful of bricks up the ladder. 他扛着一满铲砖块上了梯子。|Synonyms: shovelful, load|Antonyms: none|noun
