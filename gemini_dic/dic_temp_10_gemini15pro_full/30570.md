cerated:/ˈsɪəreɪtɪd/
adjective
覆盖着蜡的；蜡状的；蜡制的
Covered with wax; waxy.
The cerated leaves of the plant help it retain moisture.  这种植物的蜡状叶子有助于保持水分。
近义词:waxy, wax-covered
反义词: unwaxed, waxless
词性: adjective

cerate:/ˈsɪəreɪt/
noun
蜡膏；软膏；蜡状物
A thick ointment; a waxy substance.
The doctor prescribed a cerate to soothe the burn. 医生开了一种软膏来舒缓烧伤。
近义词:ointment, salve, balm
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun

词源:  From Late Latin *cerata, neuter plural of ceratus (“waxed, covered with wax”), from Latin cera (“wax”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱēr- (“wax; horn”).
