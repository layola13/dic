militarizations: /ˌmɪlɪtərɪˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.|军事化（复数）
|The process of equipping and training for war, or of adopting a military structure and mindset.  The act of increasing the military presence or influence in a particular area or sphere of life.|
|The increasing militarizations of the police force have been a cause for concern among civil rights activists. 警察部队日益军事化一直是民权活动家关注的问题。|


|The militarizations of space have led to new treaties being negotiated.  太空军事化导致新的条约正在谈判。|

|militarization (singular), military buildup, armament, weaponization|
|demilitarization, pacification|
|noun|
