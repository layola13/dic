papillary:/ˈpæpɪləri/ /ˈpæpɪlɛri/| adj.|乳头的，乳头状的|Relating to or resembling a papilla or papillae; covered with papillae.  Small, nipple-like projections or protuberances.|The doctor noticed some papillary growths on the patient's skin. 医生注意到病人皮肤上有一些乳头状的生长物。|Synonyms: nipple-like, papilliform, verrucose, warty|Antonyms: smooth, flat|adjective

papilla:/pəˈpɪlə/ /pəˈpɪlʌ/| n.|乳头|A small, nipple-like projection or protuberance.|The taste buds are located on the papillae of the tongue. 味蕾位于舌头的乳头上。|Synonyms: nipple, protuberance, projection|Antonyms: depression, hollow|noun
