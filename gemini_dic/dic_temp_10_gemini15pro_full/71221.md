fithel:/ˈfɪðəl/| n.|菲德尔琴(一种中世纪的弦乐器)|A fithel is a type of medieval stringed instrument, similar to a fiddle.|He played a lively tune on his fithel. 他用他的菲德尔琴演奏了一曲轻快的曲子。|Synonym: fiddle, vielle|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps silence)|Noun
