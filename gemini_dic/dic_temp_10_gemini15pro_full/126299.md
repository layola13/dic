myoepithelial:/ˌmaɪoʊˌɛpɪˈθiːliəl/ | adj. | 肌上皮的 | Composed of contractile cells derived from epithelium, typically found surrounding glands and ducts.  These cells are capable of contraction, aiding in the expulsion of secretions. | Myoepithelial cells play a crucial role in milk ejection during breastfeeding. 肌上皮细胞在哺乳期间的乳汁排出中起着至关重要的作用。|  epithelial, glandular | mesenchymal | adjective
