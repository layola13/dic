nonconduction:/ˌnɑːnkənˈdʌkʃən/ |n.| 非传导，不传导 |The nonconduction of heat makes this material a good insulator. 这种材料不导热，使其成为良好的绝缘体。| English Explanation:  The lack of ability to conduct heat, electricity, or sound.  It refers to the property of a substance that prevents the flow of energy through it.| Example: The nonconduction of electricity in rubber makes it a safe material for electrical insulation. 橡胶不导电，使其成为安全的电绝缘材料。| Synonyms: nonconductivity, insulation | Antonyms: conduction, conductivity | Noun
