inertia:/ɪˈnɜːʃə/ | n. | 惯性;惰性 | A property of matter by which it continues in its existing state of rest or uniform motion in a straight line, unless that state is changed by an external force.  The tendency to do nothing or to remain unchanged. |  The car's inertia carried it through the intersection. 汽车的惯性使它穿过了十字路口。

The organization is hampered by bureaucratic inertia. 这个组织因官僚惰性而受到阻碍。 |  Synonyms: inactivity, inaction, idleness, apathy, passiveness | Antonyms: activity, energy, dynamism,  | noun
