monodimensional:/ˌmɒnəʊdaɪˈmɛnʃənəl/ /ˌmɑːnəʊdaɪˈmɛnʃənəl/ | adj. |一维的，单向度的|Having only one dimension or aspect; lacking depth or complexity.  Relating to a single dimension or aspect; linear. |The artist's monodimensional portrayal of the character lacked depth and nuance.  这位艺术家对人物的单一维度刻画缺乏深度和细微差别。|  The problem is more complex than their monodimensional analysis suggests. 这个问题比他们单向度的分析所暗示的要复杂得多。|unidimensional, one-dimensional, linear, simple |multidimensional, complex, multifaceted|adjective
