overbrowned: /ˌoʊvərˈbraʊnd/
adjective
烤过头的，煎过头的
cooked for too long until excessively brown
The cookies were overbrowned and tasted a bit burnt.  饼干烤过火了，尝起来有点焦。
The toast was overbrowned, but still edible. 吐司烤得有点过，但还可以吃。
近义词: burnt, overcooked, scorched
反义词: undercooked, underdone
词性: adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "browned" (past participle of "brown," meaning to cook until brown)
