eudiometric:/ˌjuːdiəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
气体测量法的；测气法的
Relating to or used in eudiometry (the measurement of the volume changes of gases during chemical reactions).
The eudiometric analysis revealed the composition of the gas mixture.  气体测量分析揭示了气体混合物的组成。
The chemist conducted an eudiometric experiment to determine the oxygen content of the air sample.  化学家进行了一项气体测量实验以确定空气样本中的氧含量。
近义词: gasometric
反义词:  None
