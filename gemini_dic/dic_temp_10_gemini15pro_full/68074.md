faddishness:/ˈfædɪʃnəs/
n.
时尚癖；赶时髦；追求流行
The state or quality of being faddish; frivolous and whimsical pursuit of novelty.
Her faddishness extends to constantly changing her hair color. 她追求时尚的癖好延伸到不断地改变她的头发颜色。
The faddishness of the latest diet trend is evident in its rapid decline in popularity.  最新饮食潮流的短暂流行在其迅速衰落的受欢迎程度中显而易见。
synonyms: trendiness, vogue, stylishness, fashionableness
antonyms: timelessness, classicism, enduringness, permanence
