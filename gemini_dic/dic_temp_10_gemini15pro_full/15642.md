baiting:/ˈbeɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 诱饵; 挑衅; 激怒; (动物)咬; | The act of deliberately annoying or provoking someone.  The practice of tormenting animals, especially by setting dogs on them. | He ignored their baiting and refused to get angry. 他无视他们的挑衅，拒绝生气。 |  The police were accused of baiting the suspect into confessing. 警察被指控诱使嫌疑人认罪。 | synonyms: teasing, taunting, provoking, goading, harassing | antonyms: placating, soothing, calming | noun


baiting:/ˈbeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | (用于构成复合词) 诱饵的;  | Used in compound words to describe something related to bait or baiting. | baiting behavior  挑衅行为 |  baiting tactics  引诱策略 | synonyms: enticing, alluring, tempting | antonyms: repellent, off-putting | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "bait" (Old Norse *beita "to cause to bite," causative of *bīta "to bite").  The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb into a noun or adjective, indicating the act or process of baiting.
