gabbers:/ˈɡæbərz/
n.
喋喋不休的人，饶舌者
People who talk incessantly, especially about trivial matters.
The gabbers in the cafe made it difficult to concentrate. 咖啡馆里喋喋不休的人让我很难集中注意力。

The gabbers continued their noisy conversation throughout the movie. 饶舌者们在整个电影放映过程中都在大声喧哗。
近义词: chatterboxes, gossips
反义词: quiet people, reticent individuals
词性: 名词(plural)

gabber:/ˈɡæbər/
n.
喋喋不休的人，饶舌者
A person who talks incessantly, especially about trivial matters.
He's such a gabber, he never stops talking. 他真是个饶舌的人，嘴巴就没停过。

She's known as a gabber in the office, always sharing the latest gossip. 她在办公室里被称为饶舌者，总是分享最新的八卦。
近义词: chatterbox, gossip
反义词: quiet person, reticent individual
词性: 名词(singular)


词源:
From the verb "gab," meaning to talk idly or incessantly. The verb "gab" likely originates from a Scandinavian source, related to Old Norse "gabba" (to mock, scoff).
