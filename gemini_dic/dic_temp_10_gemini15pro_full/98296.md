insiders:/ɪnˈsaɪdərz/ | n. | 内部人士；知情人 |  A person who has special knowledge or access to confidential information, typically because they are part of a particular group or organization. | The insiders were able to profit from the information before it became public. 知情人士能够在信息公开之前从中获利。|  insiders benefited from early access to the new product. 内部人员受益于提前接触新产品。| synonyms: confidants, intimates, associates | antonyms: outsiders, strangers | noun
