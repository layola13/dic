ammonite:/ˈæmənaɪt/| n. |菊石|An extinct marine mollusk of the class Cephalopoda, typically with a coiled, chambered shell.  These creatures lived millions of years ago.|The museum had a fascinating display of ammonites.  博物馆里有一个引人入胜的菊石展览。|fossil, cephalopod||noun

ammonite:/ˈæmənaɪt/| adj. |菊石的|Relating to or resembling an ammonite.|The ammonite fossil was perfectly preserved. 这块菊石化石保存得非常完好。||fossiliferous||adjective
