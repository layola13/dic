overdirecting:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈrɛktɪŋ/ | v. | 过度指导，过度指挥 |  Giving too much instruction or guidance, often to the point of hindering performance or creativity. | The director was overdirecting the actors, stifling their natural performances. 导演过度指导演员，扼杀了他们的自然表演。| Synonyms: micromanaging, overmanaging, interfering | Antonyms: empowering, delegating | Verb

overdirecting:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈrɛktɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度指导的 | Characterized by excessive direction or guidance.  |  His overdirecting style made it difficult for the team to work independently.  他过度指导的风格使得团队难以独立工作。| Synonyms: controlling, intrusive | Antonyms: hands-off, laissez-faire | Adjective
