childproofs:/ˈtʃaɪldpruːfs/
verb|使防儿童开启（或操作）|To make something safe for children, so that they cannot open, use, or damage it.  |例句：She childproofed the kitchen cabinets after her toddler learned how to open them. 她把厨房橱柜装上了儿童安全锁，因为她的小孩学会了怎么打开它们。|近义词：secure, protect, safeguard|反义词: endanger, expose|v.


childproof:/ˈtʃaɪldpruːf/
adjective|防儿童开启（或操作）的|Designed to prevent children from opening, using, or damaging something. |例句：These bottles have childproof caps. 这些瓶子有防儿童开启的瓶盖。|近义词：safe, tamper-proof, child-resistant|反义词: unsafe, accessible|adj.
