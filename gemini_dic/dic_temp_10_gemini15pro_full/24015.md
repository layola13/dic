breech:/briːtʃ/| n. adj.|臀部；后膛；裤裆；违反，破裂|The part of a gun behind the barrel.  A baby's position in the womb with the buttocks or feet closest to the cervix, rather than the head.|The baby was in a breech position.  婴儿是臀位。 The soldier loaded the powder into the breech of the gun. 士兵把火药装进枪的后膛。|Synonyms: buttocks, rump, rear, posterior (n.);  transgression, violation, infringement (n., in the sense of "breach")|Antonyms: muzzle (of a gun), observance, compliance|noun, adjective

breech:/briːtʃ/| vt.|给…穿裤子；使…露出后膛|To put breeches on.  To load (a gun) through the breech.|He breeched his son for the ceremony. 他为仪式给儿子穿上裤子。  The artillerymen breeched their cannons.  炮兵们给大炮装填炮弹。|Synonyms: clothe, dress, attire (vt. in the sense of "put breeches on")|Antonyms: undress, disrobe|verb
