/bɪˈɡɪn/ (US) /bɪˈɡɪn/ (UK)| v. |开始，着手| To start or commence an action, process, or state of being.| He will begin his new job next week. 他下周将开始他的新工作。| synonyms: start, commence, initiate | antonyms: end, finish, cease | verb

/bɪˈɡɪn/ (US) /bɪˈɡɪn/ (UK)| n. (rare) |开始，开端|The starting point of something; the first part or stage of something.  | This is only the begin of a long journey. 这仅仅是一段漫长旅程的开始。(较少用作名词)| synonyms: start, commencement, outset | antonyms: end, finish, conclusion | noun

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I begin my day with a cup of coffee. 我以一杯咖啡开始我的一天。
He begins work at 8 am every day. 他每天早上8点开始工作。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am beginning to understand the problem. 我开始理解这个问题了。
She is beginning to feel better. 她开始感觉好些了。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The movie has begun. 电影已经开始了。
The concert has already begun. 音乐会已经开始了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been beginning to learn Spanish. 我一直在开始学习西班牙语。(此用法不常见，通常用现在进行时)
He has been beginning to write a novel. 他一直在开始写小说。(此用法不常见，通常用现在进行时)

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I began working on the project last week. 我上周开始做这个项目。
She began to cry when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息后开始哭了。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was beginning to feel tired when he arrived. 他到达时我开始感到累了。
They were beginning to lose hope. 他们开始失去希望。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The play had begun before we arrived. 我们到达之前戏剧已经开始了。
The rain had begun before I left the house. 我离开家之前雨已经开始了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been beginning to suspect him before I found the evidence. 在我找到证据之前，我已经开始怀疑他了。(此用法不常见，通常用过去完成时)
She had been beginning to write a letter when the phone rang.  电话铃响时她正开始写信。(此用法不常见，通常用过去进行时)


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will begin my diet tomorrow. 我明天开始节食。
The meeting will begin at 9 am sharp. 会议将在早上9点准时开始。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be beginning my new job next Monday. 我下周一将开始我的新工作。
She will be beginning her studies in the fall. 她将在秋季开始学习。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next week, I will have begun the project. 到下周，我将已经开始这个项目了。
By the time you arrive, the party will have begun. 等你到的时候，派对就已经开始了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next month, I will have been beginning to work on the project for six months. 到下个月，我将已经开始做这个项目六个月了。(此用法不常见，通常用将来完成时)
By the end of the year, she will have been beginning to learn Chinese for a year. 到年底，她将已经开始学习中文一年了。(此用法不常见，通常用将来完成时)
