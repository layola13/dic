mudflaps:/ˈmʌdflæps/ (US), /ˈmʌdflæps/ (UK)| n. |挡泥板；溅泥板|A flap, usually made of rubber or flexible plastic, behind the wheel of a vehicle to minimize the splashing of mud or water.|The car's mudflaps were caked in mud after driving on the dirt road. 汽车的挡泥板在泥路上行驶后沾满了泥巴。|Synonyms: splash guards, spats|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
