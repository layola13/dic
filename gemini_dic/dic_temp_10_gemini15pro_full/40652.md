conventioner:/kənˈvɛnʃənər/ | n. |与会者，参加会议的人|A person who attends a convention or assembly.| 例句：The conventioner listened attentively to the keynote speaker.  与会者专心听取了主旨发言人的讲话。

例句：The hotel was filled with conventioners from all over the country.  这家酒店挤满了来自全国各地的与会者。

近义词：attendee, participant, delegate, representative |反义词: absentee |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From convention + -er.  Ultimately from Latin *conventiō*, from convenīre "to come together."
