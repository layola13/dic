extractibility:/ɪkˌstræktɪˈbɪləti/| n.|可提取性，可萃取性|The extractibility of the metal from the ore is high.  这种金属从矿石中的提取率很高。|The extractibility of the oil from the shale is a key factor in its economic viability. 从页岩中提取石油的可提取性是其经济可行性的关键因素。|近义词:recoverability, retrievability|反义词:non-extractability, insolubility|词性:noun

词源: From extract + -ibility.  源自 extract（提取）+ -ibility（可...性）。
