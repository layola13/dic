imperialists:/ɪmˈpɪəriəlɪsts/ | n. | 帝国主义者 | People who support or practice imperialism, the policy of extending a country's power and influence through diplomacy or military force. |  The imperialists sought to exploit the resources of the colonized nations. 帝国主义者试图剥削被殖民国家的资源。

The museum displayed artifacts looted by imperialists during the colonial era.  博物馆展出了帝国主义者在殖民时代掠夺的文物。| 近义词：colonialists, expansionists | 反义词：anti-imperialists, nationalists | 词性：名词

词源: From *imperialist*, from *imperial* + *-ist*.  *Imperial* derives from Latin *imperialis*, from *imperium* "command, power, empire".  *-ist* is a suffix forming nouns denoting a person who adheres to a particular doctrine or system.
