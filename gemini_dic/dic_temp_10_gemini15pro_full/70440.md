figural:/ˈfɪɡjərəl/| adj.|图形的；形象的；比喻的|Relating to, or represented by a figure or figures; emblematic; metaphorical.|例句：The poem is rich in figural language, using metaphors and similes to create vivid images. 这首诗充满了比喻的语言，运用隐喻和明喻来创造生动的形象。

例句：Figural art, unlike abstract art, represents recognizable forms.  具象艺术不同于抽象艺术，它表现的是可识别的形式。|近义词：figurative, symbolic, metaphorical, representative|反义词：literal, abstract, non-figurative|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *figuralis, from Latin figura (“figure, shape, form”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeyǵ- (“to form, build”).
