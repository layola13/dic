nondualistically:/ˌnɒnˌdʒuːəlˈɪstɪkli/
adv.
非二元地，非二元论地
In a way that does not recognize a separation between subject and object, or between mind and matter.  It emphasizes the interconnectedness and unity of all things.
She approached the issue nondualistically, seeing the interconnectedness of all perspectives. 她以非二元论的方式处理这个问题，看到了所有观点的相互联系。
Near Synonyms: holistically, integrally, unifiedly
Antonyms: dualistically, separately, dividedly
词性: 副词
