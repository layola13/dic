nondefiners:/ˌnɑːndɪˈfaɪnərz/
|n.| 非限定词 |Those words like "some," "any," "a few," "several," which don't specify a definite quantity or identify specific items are called nondefiners.|  Nondefiners like "several" give a general idea of quantity.  几个这样的非限定词，像“几个”，给出了数量的大致概念。 | synonyms: indefinite pronouns, quantifiers | antonyms: definers, determiners | noun
