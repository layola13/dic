odds's: /ɑdzɪz/ | n. |  某人或某事成功的可能性；机会；概率 |  The chances or likelihood of something happening or being the case.  This is a non-standard and generally considered incorrect form, arising from a double possessive. The correct form is usually just "odds." |  "What are the odds's of that happening?" (incorrect usage - should be "What are the odds of that happening?")  你认为那件事发生的几率是多少？（错误用法 - 应该用“What are the odds of that happening?”）| 近义词: chances, probability, likelihood | 反义词: improbability | 词性: 名词
