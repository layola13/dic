cowhiding:/ˈkaʊˌhaɪdɪŋ/| n.|用牛皮鞭打; 鞭打; 痛打|The act of whipping someone with a cowhide (a whip made from the hide of a cow).|He received a cowhiding for stealing the apples. 他因为偷苹果而被鞭打。|近义词：whipping, flogging, beating, thrashing|反义词: praising, rewarding|noun

cowhiding:/ˈkaʊˌhaɪdɪŋ/| v.|用牛皮鞭打; 鞭打; 痛打|To whip someone with a cowhide.|The overseer cowhided the slave. 监工用牛皮鞭抽打奴隶。|近义词：whip, flog, beat, thrash|反义词: praise, reward|verb
