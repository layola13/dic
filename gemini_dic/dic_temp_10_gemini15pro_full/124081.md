morris:/ˈmɒrɪs/ (UK) /ˈmɔːrɪs/ (US)| n.|莫里斯舞；一种起源于英国的传统民间舞蹈|A traditional English folk dance involving men with blackened faces, bells, and sticks.|例句：The village held its annual morris dancing festival. 这个村庄举行了年度莫里斯舞节。|例句：The morris dancers wore colorful costumes and jingled their bells. 莫里斯舞者穿着五颜六色的服装，摇响铃铛。|近义词：folk dance, country dance|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps modern dance)|词性:noun
