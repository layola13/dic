glamourize: /ˈɡlæməˌraɪz/
verb
美化；渲染；使有魅力
The movie glamourizes violence, portraying it as a solution to problems. 这部电影美化了暴力，将其描绘成解决问题的办法。
近义词: romanticize, idealize, glorify
反义词: vilify, denigrate, disparage
词性: verb

词源: glamour (魅力) + -ize (动词后缀，表示“使成为…”)  From French glamour, alteration of grammar, from Late Latin grammatica.
