concreteness:/kɒŋˈkriːtnəs/ /kɑːŋˈkriːtnəs/| n.|具体性，实在性|The concreteness of her examples made the concept easy to understand. 她例子很具体，让这个概念很容易理解。|近义词：specificity, solidity, tangibility|反义词：abstractness, vagueness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *concrētus (past participle of concrēscere “to grow together, harden, congeal”), from com- (intensive prefix) + crescere “to grow”.  The English noun is derived from the adjective concrete + -ness.
