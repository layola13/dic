misdight: /mɪsˈdaɪt/
noun
灾祸，不幸，厄运
A state or condition of misfortune or adversity.
例句：He blamed his misdight on bad luck. 他将自己的不幸归咎于运气不好。

例句：The family suffered great misdight after the fire. 这家人在火灾后遭受了巨大的不幸。

近义词：misfortune, adversity, mishap, calamity
反义词：fortune, luck, prosperity

词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *misdight, from mis- + dight (“prepared, ordained, destined”), from Old English dihtan (“to arrange, order, compose”). Equivalent to mis- + dight.
