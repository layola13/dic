fuseless:/ˈfjuːzləs/| adj.|无保险丝的，不需保险丝的|Not requiring a fuse; designed or operating without a fuse.  Relating to a device or system that doesn't utilize a fuse for protection against overcurrent.|This new circuit board is fuseless, relying on electronic safeguards instead.  这个新的电路板是无保险丝的，依靠电子保护措施。|fuse-protected, fused|fuseless|adjective
