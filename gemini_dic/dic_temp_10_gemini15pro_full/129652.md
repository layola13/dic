non-dairy:/ˌnɑːnˈdɛəri/
adjective
非乳制的
Made or prepared without milk or other dairy products.
She uses non-dairy creamer in her coffee. 她用非乳制奶精冲咖啡。
non-dairy milk is becoming increasingly popular. 非乳制牛奶正变得越来越受欢迎。

synonyms:dairy-free, milkless, lactose-free
antonyms:dairy, milk-based

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + dairy (related to milk products)
