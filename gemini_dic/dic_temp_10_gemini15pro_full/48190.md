decompoundable:/ˌdiːkəmˈpaʊndəbl/
adj.
可分解的，可化解的
Capable of being decomposed or broken down into simpler components or elements.
This complex chemical is decompoundable into several simpler substances.  这种复杂的化合物可以分解成几种更简单的物质。
The word "unbreakable" is decompoundable into "un," "break," and "able."  “unbreakable”这个词可以分解成“un”、“break”和“able”。
近义词: separable, divisible, analyzable
反义词: indecomposable, indivisible
词性: adjective

词源:
From de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + compound (verb meaning "to combine") + -able (suffix meaning "capable of being").  Ultimately from Latin componere "to put together."
