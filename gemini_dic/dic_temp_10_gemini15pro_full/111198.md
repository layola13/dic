lithologist:/lɪˈθɑːlədʒɪst/
n.
岩石学家；岩性学家
A scientist who studies the physical characteristics of rocks, including their origin, composition, texture, and structure.
The lithologist examined the core samples to determine the age of the rock formation. 岩石学家检查了岩心样本以确定岩层的年龄。
geologist, petrologist
