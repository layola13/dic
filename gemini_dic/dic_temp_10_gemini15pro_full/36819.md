colonializing:/ˈkɒlənaɪzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlənaɪzɪŋ/ (US)| v. |殖民化，殖民|The act of establishing political control over a territory and settling it with people from the colonizing country.  It often involves exploitation of resources and suppression of the indigenous population.| European powers were colonializing Africa and Asia in the 19th century. 欧洲列强在19世纪殖民了非洲和亚洲。

The company is colonializing new markets with its products.  这家公司正在用其产品殖民新的市场。|近义词：settling, occupying, conquering, imperializing|反义词：liberating, decolonizing|词性：动词

colonializing:/ˈkɒlənaɪzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlənaɪzɪŋ/ (US)| adj. |殖民的，殖民主义的|Relating to the act of colonizing; characteristic of a colonizer. |The colonializing powers often imposed their culture on the indigenous people. 殖民列强经常将他们的文化强加于土著人民。

They adopted a colonializing approach to business, exploiting local resources. 他们采取了一种殖民式的商业方法，剥削当地资源。|近义词：imperialist, expansionist|反义词：anti-colonial|词性：形容词 (较少使用)
