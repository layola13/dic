expatriates:/ˌɛksˈpæt.ri.əts/ |/ˌɛksˈpeɪ.tri.əts/| n. |侨民；移居国外者|A person who lives outside their native country.|Many expatriates in the UAE come from India and Pakistan. 许多在阿联酋的侨民来自印度和巴基斯坦。|近义词：emigrants, exiles|反义词:repatriates|词性:名词

expatriate:/ˌɛksˈpæt.ri.eɪt/ |/ˌɛksˈpeɪ.tri.eɪt/| vt. |移居国外；逐出国外|To banish (someone) from their native country.|He was expatriated for his political beliefs. 他因为自己的政治信仰而被驱逐出境。|近义词：banish, exile|反义词:repatriate|词性:动词
