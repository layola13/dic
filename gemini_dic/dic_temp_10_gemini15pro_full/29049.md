cartwheeled:/kɑːrtwiːld/ | v. | 翻筋斗 | Performed a cartwheel; moved by turning sideways in a circular motion with hands and feet touching the ground in turn. |  She cartwheeled across the lawn. 她翻着筋斗穿过草坪。|  He cartwheeled down the beach. 他沿着沙滩翻筋斗。 | synonyms: tumble, roll, flip | antonyms: stand still, stay put | verb
