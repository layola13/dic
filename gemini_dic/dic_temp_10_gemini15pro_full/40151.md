contemptibly:/kənˈtɛmptɪbli/| adv.|轻蔑地，卑劣地|In a manner that expresses contempt; scornfully.  Showing or feeling contempt.|He contemptibly dismissed her concerns. 他轻蔑地驳回了她的担忧。|Synonyms: scornfully, disdainfully, dismissively|Antonyms: respectfully, admiringly|Adverb
contempt:/kənˈtɛmpt/| n.|轻蔑，蔑视|The feeling that a person or a thing is beneath consideration, worthless, or deserving scorn.|She felt nothing but contempt for him. 她对他只有轻蔑。|Synonyms: scorn, disdain, disrespect|Antonyms: respect, admiration, esteem|Noun
