circumference: /səˈkʌmf(ə)rəns/ /sərˈkʌmf(ə)rəns/ | n. |圆周，周长| The distance around a circle or any roughly circular shape. | The earth's circumference is about 40,000 kilometers. 地球的周长约为四万公里。

The circumference of the lake is about five miles.  这个湖的周长大约是五英里。

近义词: perimeter, periphery | 反义词: diameter, radius | 词性: noun
