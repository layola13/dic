dowve: /dʌv/ | n. | dove (non-standard spelling) | A bird, symbolic of peace.  This spelling is considered archaic or dialectal and is not commonly used. |  The dowve flew across the field.  鸽子飞过田野。 | 近义词: dove, pigeon | 反义词: hawk (symbolically) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle English *douve, from Old English *dufe, from Proto-Germanic *dubō, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dʰubʰ-.  The modern standard spelling is "dove."
