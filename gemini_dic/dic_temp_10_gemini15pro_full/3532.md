against the grain:/əˈɡenst ðə ɡreɪn/ |adv. phrase|逆着潮流，违反意愿，格格不入|Going against the grain means doing something that is difficult or unpleasant because it contradicts your natural inclinations or the prevailing norms.|Brushing a cat's fur against the grain can make it angry. 逆着猫毛刷会让它生气。|

He found it hard to work for a large corporation; it went against the grain. 他发现很难为大公司工作；这违背了他的意愿。|synonyms: against one's nature, against one's better judgment, counterintuitive, awkward, uncomfortable|antonyms: naturally, smoothly, easily, with the grain, along with the grain|phrase
