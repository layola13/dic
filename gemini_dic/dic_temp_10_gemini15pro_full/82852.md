gull-like:/ˈɡʌlˌlaɪk/| adj.|像海鸥的，类似海鸥的|Resembling a gull in appearance or behavior. |The bird had a gull-like cry. 这只鸟的叫声像海鸥。| The bird had a gull-like wingspan.  这只鸟的翼展像海鸥。|Synonyms: gullish|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, consider using descriptions that contrast with gull characteristics, e.g., small, dark, songbird-like) |adjective
