garran:/ˈɡærən/ | n. | 小马驹，未阉割的小雄马 |A young horse, especially a male horse under the age of four. |  The farmer bought a new garran for his son. 农民给他的儿子买了一匹小马驹。| synonyms: colt, foal, pony | antonyms: mare, stallion | noun
