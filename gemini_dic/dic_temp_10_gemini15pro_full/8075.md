anthropomorphized: /ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfaɪzd/ | adj. | 拟人化的 | Given human characteristics or behavior.  Attributing human traits, emotions, or intentions to non-human entities (like animals, objects, or concepts). | The fox in the fable was anthropomorphized, speaking and acting like a human. 寓言中的狐狸被拟人化了，像人一样说话和行动。

The company anthropomorphized its brand by giving it a friendly face and a relatable personality.  这家公司通过赋予其品牌友好的面孔和相关的个性来使其拟人化。| 近义词: personified, humanized | 反义词: objectified, dehumanized | 词性: adjective

词源: From the Greek "anthrōpos" (human) + "morphē" (form) + "-ize" (to make or become).
