above-mentioned:/əˈbʌvˈmɛnʃənd/| adj.|上述的，前述的|Referring to something or someone previously mentioned.  It avoids repetition and adds formality.|The above-mentioned issues will be discussed in the next meeting. 上述问题将在下次会议上讨论。|The above-mentioned report is available online. 上述报告可在线获取。|近义词：aforementioned, aforesaid, previously mentioned|反义词:following, below-mentioned|形容词

USAGE->
As a modifier before a noun:

The above-mentioned points are crucial for our success. = 上述几点对我们的成功至关重要。

Please refer to the above-mentioned document for further details. =  有关更多详细信息，请参阅上述文件。


PHRASE->
(Generally used as a standalone phrase, not in combination with other words)
