parsoned:/ˈpɑːrsənd/ | v. | 任命为教区牧师 | provided with a parson or incumbent; inducted as parson of a parish. |  He was parsoned to the small village church. 他被任命为那个小村庄教堂的牧师。 |  appointed, inducted | dismissed, removed | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *parsonen, from Anglo-Norman *persuner, from Old French personner, from Late Latin personare (“to sound through, resound”), from Latin persona (“mask, character, role, part, person”).
