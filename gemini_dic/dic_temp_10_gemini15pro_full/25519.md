bullnosed:/ˈbʊlˌnoʊzd/| adj.|圆头的，钝头的，有圆形突出物的|Having a rounded or blunt end, often with a convex profile.|The bullnosed steps were worn smooth from years of use.  这些圆头的台阶因多年的使用而磨得光滑。|例句：The bullnosed bricks gave the wall a softer look.  圆头的砖块使墙壁看起来更柔和。|近义词：rounded, blunt, convex|反义词：pointed, sharp|词性:形容词
Etymology: bull + nose + -ed.  The term likely originated from the resemblance of the shape to the nose of a bull.
