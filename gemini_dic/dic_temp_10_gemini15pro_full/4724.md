alexandre:/ˌælɪkˈsɑːndrə/ /ˌælɪɡˈzɑːndrə/| n. |亚历山大|A male given name, a French form of Alexander. From the Greek name Ἀλέξανδρος (Alexandros), meaning "defender of mankind".  Derived from ἀλέξω (alexo) meaning "to defend, help" and ἀνήρ (aner) meaning "man" (genitive ἀνδρός).|例句：Alexandre Dumas was a famous French writer. 亚历山大·仲马是一位著名的法国作家。

例句：My friend's name is Alexandre. 我的朋友名叫亚历山大。|近义词：Alexander, Alex|反义词: N/A|词性:Noun
