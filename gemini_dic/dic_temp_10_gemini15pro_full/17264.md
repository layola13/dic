bayman:/ˈbeɪmən/| n. |海湾居民，渔民|A person who lives or works in or around a bay, especially a fisherman or someone involved in the fishing industry.|例句：The bayman cast his net into the water at dawn. 渔民在黎明时分将网撒入水中。

例句：The baymen depended on the bounty of the bay for their livelihood. 这些海湾居民依靠海湾的馈赠为生。|近义词：fisherman, fisher, angler|反义词:landsman|词性:noun
