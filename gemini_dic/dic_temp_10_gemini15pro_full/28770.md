carnose:/ˈkɑːrnoʊs/| adj.|肉质的；似肉的；肉色的|Having a fleshy consistency; fleshy.  Resembling flesh in appearance or texture.|The fungus had a thick, carnose cap. 这种真菌有一个厚厚的肉质菌盖。|近义词：fleshy, pulpy, succulent|反义词：thin, wiry, dry|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin "carnosus," from "caro," meaning "flesh."
