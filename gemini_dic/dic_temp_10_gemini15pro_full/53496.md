disassembler:/ˌdɪsəˈsɛmblə(r)/ | n. |反汇编程序|A program that translates machine language into assembly language. |The disassembler helped me understand the low-level workings of the program. 反汇编程序帮助我理解了程序的底层工作原理。|Example 2:  I used a disassembler to analyze the malware. 我用反汇编程序分析了恶意软件。|Synonyms: decompiler, reverse assembler |Antonyms: assembler, compiler |noun
