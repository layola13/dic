organdy:/ˈɔːrɡəndi/ | n. | 薄纱；透明硬纱 | A thin, stiff, transparent fabric of silk or cotton. |  She wore a dress of white organdy. 她穿着一件白色薄纱连衣裙。

The curtains were made of delicate organdy. 窗帘是用精致的薄纱制成的。 | 近义词: voile, muslin, gauze | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider) heavy cloth, thick fabric | 词性: noun
