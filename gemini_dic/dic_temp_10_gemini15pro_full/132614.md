nondirigibles:/ˌnɒndɪˈrɪdʒɪbəlz/ | n. | 非硬式飞艇，软式飞艇 |Airships that rely on internal pressure to maintain their shape, unlike rigid airships. |The museum has an exhibit on the history of nondirigibles and their role in early aviation.  博物馆展出了非硬式飞艇的历史及其在早期航空中的作用。|Synonyms: blimps, airships |Antonyms: dirigibles, rigid airships |Noun

nondirigible:/ˌnɒndɪˈrɪdʒɪbəl/ | adj. | 不可操纵的，不听指挥的 |Not capable of being steered or controlled. |His nondirigible temper often got him into trouble. 他那不可控制的脾气经常给他惹麻烦。|Synonyms: uncontrollable, unmanageable, intractable |Antonyms: controllable, manageable, dirigible |Adjective
