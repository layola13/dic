hear-things:/hɪər θɪŋz/| v.|幻听；胡思乱想|To perceive non-existent sounds, often as a symptom of a mental disorder.  Experiencing auditory hallucinations.|例句：He's been under a lot of stress lately and has started hearing things. 他最近压力很大，开始出现幻听了。

例句：She was so tired she thought she was hearing things. 她累得以为自己幻听了。|近义词：hallucinate, imagine, perceive falsely|反义词: perceive accurately, hear correctly|词性: verb phrase
