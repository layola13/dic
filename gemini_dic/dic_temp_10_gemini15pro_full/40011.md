consults:/kənˈsʌlts/| v. (third-person singular simple present consults, present participle consulting, simple past and past participle consulted)|咨询，商量，查阅|She consults with her colleagues before making a decision. 她做决定前会与同事商量。|例句：He consulted his doctor about the pain in his chest. 他就胸痛咨询了他的医生。|近义词：advises, discusses, refers to|反义词:ignores, disregards|词性:动词

consults:/ˈkɒnsʌlts/| n. (plural)|咨询（尤指专业的）|The doctor held several consults with the patient. 医生与病人进行了多次会诊。|例句：The architect's consults are expensive. 这位建筑师的咨询费用很昂贵。|近义词：consultations, meetings, discussions|反义词: N/A|词性:名词


词源: From Latin consultare, frequentative of consulere "to consult, deliberate, consider," from com- "together" (see com-) + -sulere (related to salvus "safe").
