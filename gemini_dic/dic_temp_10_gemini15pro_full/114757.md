malpractice: /mælˈpræktɪs/ | n. |渎职，玩忽职守；医疗事故| Improper or negligent professional activity or treatment, especially by a medical practitioner, lawyer, or public official. | The doctor was sued for malpractice after the patient died during a routine surgery.  这位医生在病人死于常规手术后被起诉医疗事故。|  The lawyer was accused of malpractice for failing to file the necessary paperwork.  这名律师因未能提交必要的文件而被指控玩忽职守。 |  negligence, misconduct, dereliction of duty |  due diligence, proper conduct | noun
