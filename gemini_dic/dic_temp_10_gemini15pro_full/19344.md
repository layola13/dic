bibliomaniac: /ˌbɪbliəˈmeɪniæk/ | n. | 藏书癖；书籍狂 | A person who has an excessive fondness for acquiring and possessing books, often regardless of their content or readability. |  He was a bibliomaniac, his house overflowing with stacks of books he'd never read. 他是个藏书癖，他的房子里堆满了从未读过的书。| bibliophile, bookworm | book hater | noun
