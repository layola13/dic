eblanin:/ˈɛblənɪn/
noun
笨蛋，傻瓜，白痴 (offensive, derogatory)
A highly offensive and derogatory term used in Ireland to insult or demean someone, implying they are stupid or foolish.
He called him an eblanin for making such a stupid mistake. 他骂他笨蛋，因为他犯了这么愚蠢的错误。

近义词: idiot, fool, moron, imbecile, cretin, nitwit, dope, dimwit, simpleton, halfwit
反义词: genius, intellectual, savant
词性: noun
