densen:/ˈdɛnsən/ | adj. | 密集的，稠密的 |  Characterized by high density or compactness; closely packed together. | The city has a densen population.  这个城市人口稠密。 | 近义词: dense, compact, thick, crowded | 反义词: sparse, thin, scattered | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the word "dense" with the suffix "-en" added to form an adjective meaning "to make or become dense."  "Dense" ultimately comes from the Latin "densus" meaning thick or compact.
