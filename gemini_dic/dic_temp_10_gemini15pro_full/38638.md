concupiscential:/kənˌkjuːpɪˈsɛnʃəl/
adjective
贪欲的; 色欲的
Characterized by or relating to strong sexual desire; lustful.
He gave in to his concupiscential desires. 他屈服于自己的色欲。
Near Synonyms: lustful, lecherous, lascivious, carnal, sensual, desirous
Antonyms: chaste, pure, virtuous, abstinent
word type: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *concupiscentiālis*, from Latin concupiscent- ‘desiring eagerly’, from the verb concupīscere, from con- ‘thoroughly’ + cupīscere ‘to desire’.
