inchoative:/ɪnˈkoʊətɪv/| adj.|初始的，开始的，刚开始的|Marking the beginning of an action or state; incipient.|The project is in its inchoative stages.  这个项目正处于初始阶段。|The inchoative tremors of the earthquake were barely noticeable. 地震最初的震颤几乎难以察觉。|近义词：incipient, beginning, initial, commencing, nascent|反义词：developed, mature, complete, finished|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *inchoātīvus, from Latin inchōātus (“begun”), perfect passive participle of inchōō (“begin”), related to incohō (“begin”).
