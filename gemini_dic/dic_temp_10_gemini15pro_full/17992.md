begem:/bɪˈdʒɛm/
verb
镶嵌宝石；点缀
adorn or stud thickly with gems.
The queen's crown was begemmed with diamonds and rubies. 女王的王冠上镶满了钻石和红宝石。
近义词:adorn, decorate, embellish, ornament, stud
反义词:strip, denude
词性:v.
