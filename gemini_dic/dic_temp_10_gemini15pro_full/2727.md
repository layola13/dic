aduki: /əˈduːki/ | n. | 赤豆，红豆 |  A small, dark-red bean used in East Asian cuisine, particularly in Japanese sweets and desserts.  |  Adzuki beans are often used to make a sweet red bean paste called anko.  赤豆经常用来做一种叫做anko的甜红豆沙。

She made delicious mochi filled with adzuki bean paste. 她做了美味的红豆馅麻糬。 | 近义词: red bean, azuki bean | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific type of bean) | 词性: 名词
