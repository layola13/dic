ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *ante, *amete, from Old English ǣmette (“ant”), from Proto-Germanic *amaitjō (“ant”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂emoy- (“ant, emmet”). Cognate with West Frisian amit (“ant”), Dutch emt (“ant”), German Ameise (“ant”).
