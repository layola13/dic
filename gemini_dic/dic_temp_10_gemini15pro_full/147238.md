overmeekly: /ˌoʊvərˈmiːkli/ | adv. | 过分温顺地，过于谦恭地 |  In an excessively meek or submissive manner; too humbly or timidly.  | She overmeekly accepted the criticism, even though it was unfair. 她过于温顺地接受了批评，即使这是不公平的。 | He overmeekly apologized for something that wasn't his fault. 他过于谦卑地为不是他的错的事情道歉。| 近义词: submissively, timidly, humbly | 反义词: assertively, boldly, confidently | 词性: 副词

词源:  "over-" (excessively) + "meekly" (in a meek manner). "Meek" comes from Old Norse *mjukr, meaning "soft" or "pliable."
