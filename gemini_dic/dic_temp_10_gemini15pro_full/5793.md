amaranthine:/ˌæməˈrænthɪn/| adj.|不凋谢的，永恒的，紫红色的|Of or relating to an amaranth; unfading, everlasting; of a purplish-red color.|例句：Her amaranthine beauty captivated everyone in the room. 她永恒的美丽迷住了房间里的每一个人。

例句：The poet wrote of amaranthine love, a love that would never die. 诗人写了关于永恒的爱情，一种永不消逝的爱情。|近义词：eternal, everlasting, undying, immortal, imperishable, unfading|反义词：fleeting, temporary, transient, ephemeral, mortal|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *amarantos* (unfading) + -ine.  The Greek word is composed of *a-* (not) + *marainein* (to wither, fade).
