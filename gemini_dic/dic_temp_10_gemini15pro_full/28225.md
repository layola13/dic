capsizable:/ˈkæpsɪzəbl/
adj.
易倾覆的，易翻覆的
able to be capsized (overturned in the water).
The small boat was capsizable in the strong winds. 这艘小船在大风中很容易翻覆。
近义词：unstable, tippy, top-heavy
反义词:stable, steady
词性:adjective


capsize:/ˈkæpsaɪz/
v.
（使船）翻覆，倾覆
(of a boat) overturn in the water.
The boat capsized in the storm. 船在暴风雨中翻了。
近义词：overturn, upend, keel over
反义词:right
词性:verb
