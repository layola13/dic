creamier:/ˈkriːmiər/| adj.|更乳脂状的，更柔滑的|Comparative form of creamy.  Having a smooth, rich texture like cream; containing more cream.|The cheesecake was creamier than I expected. 这块芝士蛋糕比我预想的更柔滑。|The new recipe makes the soup creamier. 新的食谱使汤更浓稠。|近义词：richer, smoother, silkier|反义词：thinner, runnier|词性:形容词
