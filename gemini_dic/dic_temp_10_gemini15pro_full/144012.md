outreckon: /ˌaʊtrɛkən/
verb
超过计算；胜过；超越
to surpass in calculating; to excel; to outdo.
He outreckoned his rivals in the competition and secured the first prize. 他在比赛中胜过对手，获得了第一名。
The benefits of this project outreckon the costs. 这个项目的好处超过了成本。
近义词: surpass, exceed, outdo, outstrip
反义词: fall short of, be inferior to
词性: verb
