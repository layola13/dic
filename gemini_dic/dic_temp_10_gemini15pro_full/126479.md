mythologizer:/ˌmɪθəˈlɒdʒaɪzər/ | n. |神话创造者；将…神化者|A person who creates or interprets myths; someone who imbues a person, place, or thing with mythical qualities. | He was a mythologizer of the American West, turning cowboys and outlaws into legendary figures. 他是美国西部的造神者，将牛仔和歹徒变成了传奇人物。 |  近义词: romanticizer, idealizer, glorifier | 反义词: debunker, realist | 词性: noun

mythologize:/ˌmɪθəˈlɒdʒaɪz/ | v. |将…神化；创造神话|To create or interpret myths; to imbue a person, place, or thing with mythical qualities. | The media tends to mythologize successful entrepreneurs, often ignoring their failures. 媒体倾向于将成功的企业家神化，常常忽略他们的失败。 | 近义词: romanticize, idealize, glorify | 反义词: debunk, demystify | 词性: verb
