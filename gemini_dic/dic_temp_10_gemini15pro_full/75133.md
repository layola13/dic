The state or quality of being frisky; playful and lively behavior.

The puppy's friskinesses made everyone laugh. 小狗的活泼让每个人都笑了。

frisky:/ˈfrɪski/
adj.
活泼的；嬉戏的
Playful and lively.
The frisky kitten chased a ball of yarn.  活泼的小猫追逐着毛线球。
The frisky colt pranced around the field.  活泼的小马驹在田野里欢快地蹦跳。
近义词:playful, lively, spirited, energetic
反义词:lethargic, sluggish, listless, inactive
词性:adjective

词源:
From Middle English *frisken, frequentative of frisken (“to leap, dance”), of uncertain origin. Possibly from Old Norse *friska (“to frisk, dance”) (whence also Danish friske (“to refresh, revive”)), or from Middle Dutch vrischen, vreschen (“to leap, jump, dance, be fresh, be brisk, invigorate”) (whence also Dutch frissen (“to freshen, refresh, invigorate”)), from Proto-Germanic *friską (“to jump, leap”).  More at fresh.
