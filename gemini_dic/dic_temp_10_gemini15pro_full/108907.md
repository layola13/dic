ledges:/ˈlɛdʒɪz/
n.
岩架；突出物；壁架
A narrow horizontal surface projecting from a wall, cliff, or other vertical structure.
The climber rested on a small ledge.
登山者在一个小壁架上休息。

近义词:shelf, projection, overhang, rim
反义词:abyss, chasm, void
词性:名词(plural)
