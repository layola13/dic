mimetism:/ˈmɪmɪtɪzəm/ | n. |拟态, 模仿 | The act or art of mimicking; imitation.  The resemblance of an animal or plant to another animal, plant, or inanimate object. | The chameleon's mimetism allows it to blend seamlessly with its surroundings. 变色龙的拟态使其能够与周围环境无缝融合。| The viceroy butterfly uses mimetism to protect itself from predators by looking like the poisonous monarch butterfly.  总督蝴蝶利用拟态，让自己看起来像有毒的帝王蝶，从而保护自己免受捕食者的侵害。| synonyms: mimicry, camouflage, imitation, simulation | antonyms: distinctness, difference, originality | noun

PHRASE->
Batesian mimetism 贝氏拟态 (a harmless species imitates a harmful one)
Müllerian mimetism 缪氏拟态 (two or more harmful species resemble each other)
aggressive mimetism 攻击性拟态 (a predator resembles a harmless model to lure prey)
protective mimetism 防御性拟态 (an organism blends with its surroundings)
vocal mimetism  声音拟态 (imitating sounds)
