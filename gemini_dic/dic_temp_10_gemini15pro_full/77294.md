gatefolds:/ˈɡeɪtfəʊldz/ (US) /ˈɡeɪtfəʊldz/ (UK)
n.
折叠式封面;  对开页;  折页
A record album cover that opens out like a gate.  A large sheet of paper, especially one forming part of a publication, folded in half or in three.
The vinyl came in a beautiful gatefold sleeve. 这张黑胶唱片装在一个漂亮的折叠式封套里。
The centerfold featured a gatefold poster of the band.  中间插页是一张乐队的对开海报。
近义词:  foldout, spread
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps single page or unfolded)
词性: 名词
