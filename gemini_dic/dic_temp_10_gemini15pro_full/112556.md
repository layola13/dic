lovelies:/ˈlʌvliːz/| n.|美人儿；可爱的东西；|A group of lovely people or things.  指一群可爱的人或物。|例句：Look at all those lovelies over there! 看看那边那些美人儿！

例句：She bought a bunch of lovelies at the market. 她在市场上买了一堆可爱的小玩意儿。|近义词：beauties, cuties, sweethearts, darlings|反义词:uglies, monsters|Noun

Etymology->
From lovely + -ies (plural suffix).  Lovely itself comes from Middle English *loveli*, from Old English *luflic*, meaning "worthy of love." 由lovely加复数后缀-ies构成。Lovely本身来自中古英语loveli，源于古英语luflic，意为“值得爱的”。
