abusage:/əˈbjuːsɪdʒ/ | n. | 滥用；误用；恶习 |  The incorrect or improper use of something, typically language or a privilege.  It can also refer to bad treatment or harmful practices. | His abusage of the company car led to his dismissal. 他滥用公司车辆导致他被解雇。

The dictionary highlighted several common abusages of the word "literally." 这本词典重点列出了“literally”一词的几个常见误用。 | 近义词: misuse, misapplication, perversion, corruption, ill-treatment | 反义词: proper use, correct application | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "abus" (abuse) + "-age" (suffix forming nouns of action or result). Ultimately from Latin "abusus" (past participle of "abuti" - to misuse).

USAGE->
作为名词:
The abusage of power is a serious problem. = 滥用权力是一个严重的问题。
The teacher pointed out the student's abusage of the word. = 老师指出了学生对这个词的误用。
