little:/ˈlɪtl/ (US), /ˈlɪtl/ (UK)| adj. adv. n.|小的，很少的；短时间的；幼小的|small in size, amount, degree, or duration; not much; young|例句：The little girl played with her doll. 小女孩玩着她的洋娃娃。|近义词：small, tiny, miniature, slight, few, a bit|反义词：big, large, huge, enormous, many, much|词性:形容词

little:/ˈlɪtl/ (US), /ˈlɪtl/ (UK)| adv.|很少地，稍微地|to a small extent; not much|例句：He slept very little last night. 他昨晚睡得很少。|近义词：slightly, a bit, hardly|反义词：much, greatly, a lot|词性:副词

little:/ˈlɪtl/ (US), /ˈlɪtl/ (UK)| n.|少量，少许|a small amount or quantity|例句：He knows little about art. 他对艺术知之甚少。|近义词：bit, drop, tad|反义词：lot, much|词性:名词


USAGE->

词源：From Old English *lytel, from Proto-Germanic *lītilaz, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ley- (“small, thin”). Cognate with Dutch luttel (“little, few”), German lützel (“little”).
