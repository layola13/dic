fauvists:/ˈfɔːvɪsts/
n.
野兽派画家（复数）
A group of early 20th-century French artists whose works emphasized painterly qualities and strong color over the representational or realistic values retained by Impressionism.
The Fauvists revolutionized the use of color in modern art. 野兽派画家彻底改变了现代艺术中色彩的运用。
synonyms:Expressionists, modernists
antonyms:traditionalists, classicists
noun
