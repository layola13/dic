interfaces:/ˈɪntəfɪsɪz/ (US) /ˈɪntəfeɪsɪz/ (UK)| n.|接口，界面|A point where two systems, subjects, organizations, etc. meet and interact.|The user interface is very intuitive. 用户界面非常直观。|近义词：connection, junction, link|反义词:separation, division|noun

interfaces:/ˈɪntəfɪsɪz/ (US) /ˈɪntəfeɪsɪz/ (UK)| v.|互相作用，连接|To connect with something or someone.|The software interfaces seamlessly with the hardware. 该软件与硬件无缝连接。|近义词：connect, interact, link|反义词:disconnect, separate|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (between) + face (surface).
