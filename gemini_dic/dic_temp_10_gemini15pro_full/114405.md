make ends meet:/meɪk ɛndz mit/ | idiom | 使收支相抵，勉强维持生计 | To earn just enough money to pay one's bills. | Example:  It's hard to make ends meet with the rising cost of living.  生活成本不断上涨，很难维持生计。 | Example: With her part-time job, she barely makes ends meet.  靠着她的兼职工作，她勉强糊口。 | 近义词: get by, scrape by, live hand to mouth | 反义词: live comfortably, be well-off | idiom
