dolichocephalic:/ˌdɒlɪkəsɪˈfælɪk/ /ˌdoʊlɪkoʊsɪˈfælɪk/| adj.|长头型的|Having a skull that is relatively long and narrow.|例句：Dolichocephalic skulls are characteristic of certain breeds of dogs, such as Greyhounds.  某些犬种，如灰狗，具有长头型的头骨。

例句：The anthropologist studied the dolichocephalic skull found during the excavation.  这位人类学家研究了在挖掘过程中发现的长头型头骨。|近义词：long-headed, long-skulled|反义词：brachycephalic|词性:形容词
