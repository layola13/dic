briber:/ˈbraɪbər/| n. |行贿者，贿赂者|A person who gives a bribe.  Someone who corrupts someone else by giving them money or a gift in exchange for a favor.|The briber offered the official a large sum of money to secure the contract. 行贿者向官员提供巨额资金以确保合同。|Synonyms: corrupter, fixer, paymaster|Antonyms: whistleblower, informant|noun

bribe:/braɪb/| n. vt.|贿赂，贿赂物； 向…行贿|Something, typically money or a favor, offered or given to someone in a position of trust in order to induce them to act dishonestly.|The company offered a bribe to the government official. 该公司向政府官员行贿。  He bribed the police officer to avoid a ticket. 他贿赂警察以避免罚单。|Synonyms: payoff, kickback, inducement|Antonyms: reward, gift (when given legitimately)|noun, verb
