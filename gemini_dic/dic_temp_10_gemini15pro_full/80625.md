gooseberry:/ˈɡuːsbəri/ (US), /ˈɡʊzbəri/ (UK)| n. |醋栗，鹅莓；（俚语）老婆；讨厌的人|A small, round, edible berry with a thin, translucent skin and a tart flavor.  It grows on a thorny bush. |例句：The gooseberries are ripe and ready to be picked. 醋栗成熟了，可以采摘了。

例句：He made a delicious gooseberry pie. 他做了一个美味的醋栗派。|近义词：berry|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑味道上的反义词，例如sweet fruit 甜水果)|词性:noun
