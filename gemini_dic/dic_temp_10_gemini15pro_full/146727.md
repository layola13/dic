overhonor:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːnər/
verb
过分尊敬，给予过多的荣誉
To honor excessively; to give too much honor or praise.
He felt overhonored by the lavish praise for his modest contribution. 他觉得对他微薄的贡献给予过多的赞扬，让他感到受宠若惊。
近义词: overpraise, lionize, exalt
反义词: dishonor, disrespect, slight
词性: verb

overhonor:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːnər/
noun
过分的尊敬，过多的荣誉
Excessive honor or praise.
The overhonor he received made him uncomfortable. 他得到的过分尊敬让他感到不舒服。
近义词: excessive praise, adulation
反义词: dishonor, disrespect
词性: noun
