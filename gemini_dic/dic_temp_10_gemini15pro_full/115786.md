marionettish:/ˌmærɪəˈnɛtɪʃ/ |adj.|傀儡般的，像木偶的|Resembling or characteristic of a marionette; jerky and unnatural in movement.|Her marionettish movements made her look like she was being controlled by strings. 她那傀儡般的动作使她看起来像是被绳子操纵着。|例句：His smile was stiff and marionettish. 他的笑容僵硬得像个木偶。|近义词：puppet-like, wooden, stiff, unnatural, jerky, mechanical|反义词：natural, fluid, graceful, lifelike|词性：形容词

marionettish:/ˌmærɪəˈnɛtɪʃ/|adj.|傀儡般的，像木偶的|Suggestive of being controlled or manipulated like a puppet.|His marionettish laughter seemed forced and insincere. 他那傀儡般的笑声显得勉强而不真诚。|例句：The politician's marionettish responses to the interviewer's questions made him seem untrustworthy. 这位政客对采访者问题的傀儡式回答让他显得不值得信任。|近义词：artificial, contrived, manipulated|反义词：genuine, authentic, spontaneous|词性：形容词
