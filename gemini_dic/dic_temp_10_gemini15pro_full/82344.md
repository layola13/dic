grow-out-of:/ɡroʊ aʊt əv/| phrasal verb | 长大不再…；戒除…；…不再适合… | To become too large for (something, typically clothing); to stop doing (something) as one grows older; to develop from something. |  She grew out of her childhood clothes quickly. 她很快就长大了，童装都不合身了。| synonyms: outgrow, surpass | antonyms: shrink into, remain attached to | verb phrase

grow-out-of:/ɡroʊ aʊt əv/| phrasal verb | 因…而产生；由…发展而来 | To develop from something, usually something smaller or less significant. | The project grew out of a casual conversation. 这个项目源于一次随意的谈话。| synonyms: originate from, stem from, develop from | antonyms: lead to, result in | verb phrase
