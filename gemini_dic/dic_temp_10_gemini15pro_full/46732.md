dandified:/ˈdændɪfaɪd/| adj.|打扮得花哨的，讲究的，纨绔子弟的|dressed in a very fashionable or stylish way, often in a way that is considered excessive or foppish.  Characterized by excessive refinement in dress and manner, often suggesting superficiality or vanity.|He looked very dandified in his new suit. 他穿着新西装，看起来非常讲究。|Synonyms: dapper, stylish, foppish, preened, polished|Antonyms: scruffy, unkempt, slovenly|adjective
