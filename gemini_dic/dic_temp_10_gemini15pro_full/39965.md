construes:/kənˈstruːz/| v. |解释，理解，分析|interprets, understands, explains.  It describes how someone interprets or understands something, often a text or situation, and assigns meaning to it.|She construes his silence as a sign of agreement. 她把他的沉默理解为同意。|interpret, understand, explain, decipher|misinterpret, misunderstand, misconstrued|verb
词源:
From Late Middle English construen, from Old French constreu-, past participle stem of contruire “to construct,” from Latin construere “to heap together, build, arrange, compile, construct.”
