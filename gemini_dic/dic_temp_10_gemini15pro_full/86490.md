hein:/haɪn/ | interj. | 嗯？什么？| An interrogative interjection expressing surprise, disbelief, or a request for repetition.  Used primarily in informal speech. | "Hein? I didn't hear you."  嗯？我没听见你说话。 |  Synonyms: huh, what, pardon | Antonyms: N/A | Interjection
