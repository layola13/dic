oops-a-daisy:/ˌuːps ə ˈdeɪzi/ |int.|哎呦；哎呀|An exclamation of mild apology or sympathy, typically used when someone has slightly stumbled, dropped something, or made a small mistake.  It's a lighthearted way to acknowledge a minor mishap.|Oops-a-daisy! I dropped my keys. 哎呦！我的钥匙掉了。|近义词:whoops, whoopsie-daisy, oops|反义词:(No direct antonym.  Consider expressions like "That's a disaster!" for serious mishaps.)|词性:interjection

词源:
The origin of "oops-a-daisy" isn't entirely clear. It's thought to be a playful extension of "oops" or "upsidaisy," which are themselves exclamations related to lifting or helping someone up.  The "daisy" part likely adds a lighthearted and childlike tone.
