loricate:/ˈlɔːrɪkeɪt/ /ˈlɔːrɪˌkeɪt/| adj.| 甲壳的，覆以鳞甲的；穿着盔甲的|Covered with, or made of, scales or plates overlapping like those of a reptile; having a lorica; protected by a shell or armor.|The loricated lizard blends seamlessly with the rocky terrain.  这只甲壳蜥蜴与岩石地形完美融合。 The knight, fully loricated, rode into battle.  全副盔甲的骑士骑马投入战斗。|近义词：armored, scaled, shell-covered, protected|反义词：unprotected, vulnerable, soft, exposed|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lōricātus*, past participle of *lōricāre* (“to clothe in mail”), from *lōrīca* (“corset, cuirass, armor”), probably from *lorum* (“thong, leather strap”).
