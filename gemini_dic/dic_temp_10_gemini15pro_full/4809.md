algorithmic:/ˌælɡəˈrɪðmɪk/ | adj. | 算法的，算法上的 | Relating to or using algorithms. |  Example: The search engine uses an algorithmic approach to rank websites.  搜索引擎使用算法方法对网站进行排名。

Example: Algorithmic trading has become increasingly popular in financial markets.  算法交易在金融市场中变得越来越流行。

| 近义词: computational, procedural | 反义词: heuristic, non-algorithmic | 词性: adjective
