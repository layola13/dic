airships:/ˈɛərˌʃɪps/ | n. | 飞艇 | A lighter-than-air aircraft that can be steered and propelled through the air using engines. It typically consists of a large gas-filled envelope, a gondola or cabin suspended beneath it, and propellers or other means of propulsion. | The company manufactures and sells airships for various purposes, including tourism and surveillance.  该公司制造和销售用于各种用途的飞艇，包括旅游和监视。| synonyms: dirigibles, blimps | antonyms: airplanes, helicopters | noun
