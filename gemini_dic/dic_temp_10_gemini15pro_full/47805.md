decarbonises:/diːˈkɑːrbənaɪzɪz/
verb
脱碳，减少碳排放
reduces or removes carbon dioxide from something.
The company is investing in new technologies to decarbonise its operations.  该公司正在投资新技术，以使其运营脱碳。
The government has set ambitious targets to decarbonise the economy. 政府设定了雄心勃勃的目标，以实现经济脱碳。
synonyms: decarbonize, neutralize, mitigate
antonyms:  pollute, contaminate
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "remove, reduce") + carbon + -ise (verb suffix).  The root "carbon" refers to the element carbon, which in the form of carbon dioxide is a major greenhouse gas.
