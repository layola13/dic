gyp:/dʒɪp/| n. v.|欺骗; 敲诈; 宰客; (非正式)抱怨，发牢骚|A swindle, cheat, or fraud.  To cheat or swindle someone. To complain or grumble (informal).|例句：I felt gyped when I saw the price they charged. 我看到他们要价时感到被敲诈了。|近义词：cheat, swindle, defraud, con, gripe, complain|反义词:be honest, be fair|词性:名词,动词
Etymology:  Origin uncertain, possibly from Romany *gipi, meaning "sly, crafty."
