originate:/əˈrɪdʒɪneɪt/ /ɔːˈrɪdʒɪneɪt/| v. |起源于，发源于，创始|To begin to exist; to be the source or cause of something. |例句：The idea originated with my grandfather.  这个想法源于我的祖父。

例句：The custom originated in the Middle Ages.  这个习俗起源于中世纪。

近义词：arise, begin, commence, derive, emanate, flow, issue, proceed, spring, stem|反义词: end, finish, terminate |词性:动词

originate:/əˈrɪdʒɪneɪt/ /ɔːˈrɪdʒɪneɪt/| v. |创作，发明|To create or invent something new. |例句：She originated the design for the new building. 她设计了这座新建筑。

例句：He originated a new style of painting. 他开创了一种新的绘画风格。

近义词：create, invent, devise, conceive |反义词: copy, imitate |词性:动词
