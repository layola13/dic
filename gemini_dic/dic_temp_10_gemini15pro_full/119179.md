mezzanine: /ˈmezənɪn/ (US) /ˈmɛzənɪːn/ (UK)| n.| 夹层，中间层，中层楼|A low story between two main stories of a building; an intermediate floor.|  The office has a mezzanine level for extra storage space. 办公室有一个夹层，用来存放额外的物品。

The theater has a mezzanine for additional seating. 剧院有一个夹层，可以提供额外的座位。

近义词: balcony, loft, gallery |反义词: basement, cellar | 词性: noun
