nonradicals:/nɒnˈrædɪkəlz/ | n. |非激进分子；温和派|People who are not extreme in their political or social views; those who favor moderate reforms rather than radical or revolutionary change.  They generally adhere to established traditions and institutions, advocating for gradual and incremental progress within the existing system.  This distinguishes them from radicals who seek fundamental societal transformation.| The nonradicals in the party preferred a slow, steady approach to reform.  该党中的非激进分子更喜欢缓慢而稳定的改革方式。|  The town council was mostly composed of nonradicals, wary of drastic changes.  镇议会主要由谨慎对待剧烈变化的非激进分子组成。|Synonyms: moderates, conservatives, traditionalists|Antonyms: radicals, extremists, revolutionaries|Noun
