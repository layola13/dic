half-criticalness:/ˌhæf krɪtɪkəlnəs/ | n. | 半批评性，略带批评意味 | A state of being moderately or partially critical; a disposition to find some fault but not be entirely negative. |  Her review of the film demonstrated a certain half-criticalness, praising the acting while finding the plot somewhat predictable. 她对这部电影的评论展现出一种略带批评意味的态度，赞扬了演技，但认为情节有些老套。 |  The teacher's half-criticalness encouraged the students to improve without discouraging them. 老师略带批评的语气鼓励学生改进，但又没有让他们灰心。 | Near synonyms: mild criticism, qualified approval | Near antonyms: wholehearted praise, outright condemnation | noun
