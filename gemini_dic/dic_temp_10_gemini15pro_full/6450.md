amplificatory:/æmˈplɪfɪkətɔːri/
adjective
放大的；补充说明的；详尽的
serving or tending to amplify; enlarging; expanding.  Making something larger or more important; adding details to clarify or expand upon something.
The lawyer provided amplificatory evidence to support his client's claim. 律师提供了补充证据来支持其委托人的主张。
synonyms:  expansive, elaborative, supplementary, augmentative,  expository
antonyms:  reductive, concise,  summary,  abbreviated,  curtailed
词性: adjective
