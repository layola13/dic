demurral:/dɪˈmɜːrəl/ (US), /dɪˈmʌrəl/ (UK)| n.|异议，反对，表示反对|An objection or the act of expressing an objection, often based on doubt or a polite refusal.|例句：The lawyer filed a demurral to the lawsuit, arguing it lacked merit. 律师对诉讼提出异议，认为其缺乏根据。

例句：Her demurral to his proposal was polite but firm. 她对他的提议的拒绝虽然委婉但很坚定。|近义词：objection, protest, dissent, challenge|反义词：acceptance, agreement, assent, approval|词性:名词
