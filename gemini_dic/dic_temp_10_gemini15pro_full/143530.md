outgrow:/ˌaʊtˈɡroʊ/| v. | 长大不再需要；长得太大不再合身；长得比…快；超过，胜过| to grow too large for (something) |例句：She outgrew her clothes so quickly. 她长得太快，衣服很快就穿不下了。

例句：The company has outgrown its current office space. 这家公司已经发展壮大，目前的办公空间不够用了。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip|反义词: shrink, diminish|词性:动词


Etymology: out- (prefix meaning "beyond, exceeding") + grow (verb meaning "to increase in size")
