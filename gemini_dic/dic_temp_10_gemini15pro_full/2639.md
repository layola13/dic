adoptions:/əˈdɑːpʃənz/
n.
领养，采用；采纳
The act of legally taking a child to be taken care of as one's own.  The act of choosing to take up, follow, or use something.
International adoptions have become increasingly common. 国际领养变得越来越普遍。
The company announced the adoption of a new marketing strategy. 公司宣布采用新的营销策略。

近义词:acceptance, taking in, fostering (for children), implementation (for ideas/strategies)
反义词:abandonment, rejection
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *adopcioun*, from Old French *adoption*,  from Latin *adoptionem* (nominative *adoptio*),  from *adoptare* "to choose for oneself, adopt," from *ad- "to" + *optare* "to choose, wish."
