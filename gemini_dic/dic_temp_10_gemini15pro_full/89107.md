homaging:/ˈhɒmɪdʒɪŋ/ | v. |致敬，表示敬意| paying tribute to someone or something, often by imitating or referencing their work | The film homages the classic movies of the 1940s. 这部电影向20世纪40年代的经典电影致敬。|  The director homaged Hitchcock by using similar camera angles. 导演通过使用类似的拍摄角度向希区柯克致敬。 | synonyms:  paying tribute to, honoring, saluting | antonyms:  disrespecting, insulting, mocking | Verb
