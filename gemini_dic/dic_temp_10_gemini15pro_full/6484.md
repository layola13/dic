amputators:/ˈæmpjʊˌteɪtərz/
n.
截肢者；截肢手术者
People who perform amputations, especially surgeons.  Those who have had a limb amputated are amputees.
The amputators worked quickly and efficiently to save the soldier's life.  截肢者快速有效地工作以拯救士兵的生命。

Synonyms: surgeons, limb removers
Antonyms:  N/A (No direct antonym for a person performing a medical procedure)
词性: Noun
