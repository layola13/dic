chromium: /ˈkroʊmiəm/ (US) /ˈkrəʊmiəm/ (UK)| n. |铬|A hard, brittle, steel-gray metallic element, resistant to corrosion and tarnishing. It is used in alloys to increase strength and corrosion resistance, and for electroplating.|Chromium is used to harden steel. 铬用于硬化钢。|Synonyms: chrome|Antonyms: None|Noun
