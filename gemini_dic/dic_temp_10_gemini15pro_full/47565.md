debacle:/deɪˈbɑːkl/ /dɪˈbɑːkl/| n.|彻底的失败；惨败；崩溃|a sudden and ignominious failure; a fiasco|The company's new product launch was a complete debacle.  这家公司的新产品发布是一场彻底的失败。|Synonyms: failure, fiasco, disaster, catastrophe, collapse, ruin, downfall|Antonyms: success, triumph, victory|noun

debacle:/deɪˈbɑːkl/ /dɪˈbɑːkl/| n.|混乱；灾难|a complete collapse or failure|The flood caused a major debacle in the city. 洪水给城市造成了巨大的灾难。|Synonyms: disaster, catastrophe, calamity, tragedy|Antonyms: order, stability, normalcy|noun
