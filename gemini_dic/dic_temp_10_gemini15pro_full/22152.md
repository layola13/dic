bombace:/ˈbɒmbəseɪs/| n.|木棉科；锦葵科|A family of tropical trees and shrubs that includes the kapok and baobab.  A former plant classification now included in the mallow family (Malvaceae).|The bombace family includes some of the largest trees in the world. 木棉科包括一些世界上最大的树木。|The baobab tree belongs to the bombace family. 猴面包树属于木棉科。|Synonym: Bombacaceae, Malvaceae (in current classifications)|Antonym: N/A|noun
