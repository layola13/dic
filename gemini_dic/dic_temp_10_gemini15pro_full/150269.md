panduriform:/pænˈdjʊərɪfɔːrm/| adj.|琵琶形的，梨形的|Shaped like a pandura, a type of lute, or resembling a pear; obovate with a rounded apex and a narrower base.|The panduriform leaf of the fig tree is easily recognizable. 无花果树琵琶形的叶子很容易辨认。|Synonyms: pear-shaped, obovate, fiddle-shaped|Antonyms: linear, lanceolate|adjective
