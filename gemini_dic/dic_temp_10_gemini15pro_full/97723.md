iniquity: /ɪˈnɪkwɪti/ | n. | 邪恶，罪恶，不公正 | Immoral or grossly unfair behavior. |例句：The iniquity of the slave trade is undeniable.  奴隶贸易的罪恶是不可否认的。

例句：He confessed his iniquities to the priest. 他向神父忏悔了他的罪孽。

近义词： wickedness, sin, evil, wrongdoing, injustice | 反义词： righteousness, virtue, justice, goodness | 词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *iniquité*, from Latin iniquitas (“unevenness, inequality, injustice”), from iniquus (“unequal, unjust”), from in- (“not”) + aequus (“equal, level, just”).
