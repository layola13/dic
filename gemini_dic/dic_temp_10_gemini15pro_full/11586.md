-arium:/eəriəm/ | n. | 地方，场所；…馆，…室 | A place or region; a building or enclosure for keeping specimens. |  The aquarium is home to many species of fish.  水族馆是许多鱼类的家园。| synonyms: institution, building, center | antonyms: void, emptiness | noun

-arium:/eəriəm/ | n. | 植物园；动物园 | A place where plants or animals are cultivated or kept. |  The herbarium contains a vast collection of dried plants.  植物标本馆收藏了大量的干植物。| synonyms: garden, conservatory, menagerie | antonyms: wilderness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin -ārium, neuter singular of -ārius, a suffix forming nouns denoting places, things, or people connected with something. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *-yos.
