mistitled:/ˌmɪsˈtaɪtld/| adj.|错误命名的，标题错误的|Incorrectly or misleadingly titled.  Having a wrong or unsuitable title.|The book was mistitled "A History of the World" when it only covered European history.|近义词：wrongly titled, inaccurately titled, improperly titled|反义词：correctly titled, aptly titled|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From mis- + titled, past participle of title.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English and Germanic origin meaning "wrong," "bad," or "wrongly."  "Title" comes from Old French "title" and ultimately from Latin "titulus" meaning "inscription" or "title."
