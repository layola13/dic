non-sabbatical:/ˌnɒn səˈbætɪkl/ | adj. | 非休假的，不休假的 | Not relating to or involving a sabbatical (a period of paid leave granted to a university teacher or other worker for study or travel, traditionally one year for every seven years worked). |  Example: He took a non-sabbatical leave of absence for personal reasons.  他因个人原因请了非学术休假。

Example:  She worked on the project during her non-sabbatical year.  她在非休假年度从事该项目。 | Near Synonyms: working, regular, ordinary | Antonyms: sabbatical | Adjective
