nationalization:/ˌnæʃənələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 国有化 |The action of bringing land, property, or another industry under the control of the nation.  The process of transforming private assets into public assets by bringing them under the public ownership of a national government or state.|  The nationalization of the railways was a controversial move.  铁路国有化是一项有争议的举措。 | nationalisation (British), state ownership, expropriation | privatization, denationalization | noun
