egregiousness:/ɪˈɡriːdʒəsnəs/
n.
极恶; 罪大恶极; 惊人; 过分
the quality of being egregious
The egregiousness of the mistake was apparent to everyone.  这个错误的严重性显而易见。
Near synonyms:  atrocity, enormity, outrageousness, flagrancy,  heinousness
Antonyms:  triviality, insignificance, minorness
noun


egregious: /ɪˈɡriːdʒəs/
adj.
极坏的；极恶劣的；令人震惊的
(of something bad) conspicuous; flagrant; outstandingly bad
It was an egregious error for a statesman to show such ignorance. 一个政治家表现出这样的无知，真是一个极大的错误。
Near synonyms: shocking, appalling, horrific, terrible, awful
Antonyms:  good, excellent, wonderful, marvelous
adjective
