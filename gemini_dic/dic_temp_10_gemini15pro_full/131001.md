nonattendances:/ˌnɒnəˈtɛndənsɪz/
n.
缺席，未出席
The high rate of nonattendances at the meeting was concerning.
会议的高缺席率令人担忧。
The school keeps a record of student nonattendances.
学校记录学生的缺勤情况。
近义词:absences, non-appearances
反义词:attendances, presences
词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + attendance.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "attendance" comes from the Old French "atendance," meaning "waiting, expectation," ultimately from the Latin "attendere," meaning "to attend to."
