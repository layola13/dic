e-ticket:/ˈiːˌtɪkɪt/
n.
电子机票
An electronic ticket, usually for air travel, that is stored electronically and not issued as a paper document.
Please show your e-ticket at the gate. 请在登机口出示您的电子机票。

e-ticket:/ˈiːˌtɪkɪt/
n.
电子票
An electronic ticket for any event or service.
I purchased my e-ticket for the concert online. 我在网上购买了音乐会的电子票。

近义词:online ticket, digital ticket
反义词:paper ticket
词性:noun
