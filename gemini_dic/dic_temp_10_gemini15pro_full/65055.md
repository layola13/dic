eternalism: /ɪˈtɜːrnəlɪzəm/ /ɪˈtɝːnəlɪzəm/ | n. |永恒主义| The philosophical view that all moments in time are equally real, as opposed to presentism or growing block theory.  It considers time to be a dimension similar to space, such that all points in time (past, present, and future) exist simultaneously. |Einstein's theory of relativity has lent some credence to the concept of eternalism. 爱因斯坦的相对论为永恒主义的概念增添了一些可信度。|  Eternalism provides a framework for understanding the nature of time. 永恒主义提供了一个理解时间本质的框架。| 近义词:block time theory, B-theory of time | 反义词:presentism, growing block theory |词性:名词
