marcer: /ˈmɑːrsər/ (US) /ˈmɑːsə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 商人，小贩; 零售商|A person who buys and sells goods, especially in small quantities, or a retailer.| He was a marcer selling silks and velvets in the market. 他是市场上一个出售丝绸和天鹅绒的商人。 | 近义词：merchant, trader, retailer, vendor, seller | 反义词: buyer, purchaser | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old French *marceor, from Late Latin *mercearius ‘merchant’, from merx, merc- ‘merchandise’.
