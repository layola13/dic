demographically:/ˌdeməˈɡræfɪkli/
adverb
人口统计地；就人口统计而言
Relating to the study of populations and the different groups within them, especially in terms of size, growth, density, distribution, and vital statistics.
The neighborhood is demographically diverse, with people from all over the world.  这个社区在人口构成上很多元，居民来自世界各地。
The city is changing demographically, with an increasing elderly population. 这座城市的人口结构正在发生变化，老年人口不断增加。
Synonym: statistically, population-wise
Antonym: (No direct antonym, consider using phrases like "ignoring demographics" or "regardless of population characteristics")
词性: 副词
