counterproject:/ˈkaʊntərˌprɑːdʒɛkt/ |n.|反方案，对立方案，对抗项目|A project designed to oppose or compete with another project.|The city council proposed a counterproject to the developer's plan for a new shopping mall. 市议会针对开发商的新购物中心计划提出了一个反方案。|The team developed a counterproject to address the competitor's new product launch.  团队制定了一个反方案，以应对竞争对手的新产品发布。|Synonyms: counterproposal, opposing project, alternative plan|Antonyms: supporting project, collaborative project|noun
