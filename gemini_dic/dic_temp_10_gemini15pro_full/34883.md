clipboards:/ˈklɪpbɔːrdz/
n.
剪贴板（复数）
A rigid board with a spring clip at the top for holding papers.
The coach held a clipboard and barked instructions at the players. 教练拿着一个剪贴板，对着球员们大声喊着指示。
