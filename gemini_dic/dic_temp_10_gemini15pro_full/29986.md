例句：She offered a few cavilous remarks about the painting, but overall she liked it. 她对这幅画提出了一些挑剔的评论，但总的来说她还是喜欢的。|近义词：carping, nitpicking, quibbling, faultfinding|反义词：approving, complimentary, uncritical|词性:adjective
