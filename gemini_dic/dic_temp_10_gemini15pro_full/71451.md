flame-proof:/ˈfleɪmˌpruːf/
flame-proof| adj.|防火的，耐火的|Treated so as to be resistant to catching fire.|Firefighters wear flame-proof clothing. 消防员穿着防火服。|近义词：fire-resistant, fireproof, non-flammable|反义词: flammable, inflammable|词性:形容词
flame-proof| n.|防火材料|A material that is resistant to catching fire.|The curtains were made of a special flame-proof material. 窗帘是用一种特殊的防火材料制成的。|近义词：fire-retardant material, fireproof material|反义词: flammable material|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "flame" + "proof". "Flame" comes from Old French "flamme", ultimately from Latin "flamma". "Proof" comes from Old French "prove", from Latin "probare" (to test, approve).
