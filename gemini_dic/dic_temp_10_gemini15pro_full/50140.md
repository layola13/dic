denuder:/dɪˈnjuːdər/ | n. | 除尘器；剥离器；脱衣者 | A device used to remove pollutants or coatings from a gas stream or surface. | The denuder effectively removed ammonia from the air sample. 除尘器有效地去除了空气样品中的氨。 |  The scientist used a denuder to strip the protective coating from the metal. 科学家使用剥离器去除金属上的保护涂层。 | synonyms: stripper, scrubber, purifier | antonyms: coater, applicator | noun
