hallooed:/həˈluːd/ |v. |大声喊叫，招呼|To shout or call out loudly, especially to attract attention.|The hunter hallooed to his dogs. 猎人大声呼唤他的猎犬。| 近义词: shouted, yelled, called | 反义词: whispered, murmured | verb
