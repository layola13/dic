decisioned:/dɪˈsɪʒənd/
| v. | 已决定的 (非标准用法，通常被认为是不规范的) |  Past participle form of "decision" used as an adjective, though generally considered incorrect. decided:/dɪˈsaɪdɪd/
| adj. | 决定了的；明确的；坚决的 | Clearly settled or determined; unmistakable. |  She has a decided advantage over her opponent. 她比她的对手有明显的优势。 |  definite, clear, resolute |  uncertain, hesitant, ambiguous | Adjective
