determinant:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnənt/| n. adj.|行列式；决定因素；决定性的|A factor that decisively affects the nature or outcome of something.|The determinant of the matrix is zero. 这个矩阵的行列式为零。|synonyms: deciding factor, crucial factor, key factor, decisive factor|antonyms: insignificant factor, minor factor|noun

determinant:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnənt/| adj.|决定性的|Serving to determine or decide.|Education is a determinant factor in future success. 教育是未来成功的决定性因素。|synonyms: crucial, decisive, pivotal, essential|antonyms: insignificant, unimportant, minor, trivial|adjective
