effigies:/ˈɛfɪdʒiz/
n.
雕像，肖像，模拟像
a sculpture or model of a person
The demonstrators burned effigies of the president. 示威者焚烧了总统的雕像。
synonyms: statue, figure, likeness, image, model, representation, dummy
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object.  Could consider "original" or "person" in some contexts)
noun
