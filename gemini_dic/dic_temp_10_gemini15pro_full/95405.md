linchpin:/ˈlɪntʃpɪn/| n.|关键人物，核心人物，关键，要点|A person or thing that is essential to the success of an organization or operation.  Something that holds the various elements of something together.|例句：She is the linchpin of the whole team.  她是整个团队的核心人物。

例句：The new software is the linchpin of the entire project.  这款新软件是整个项目的关键。|近义词：keystone, cornerstone, centerpiece, pillar, anchor |反义词:  nonessential, unimportant, peripheral| noun
