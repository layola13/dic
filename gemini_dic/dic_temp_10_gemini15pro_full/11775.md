arpeggio:/ɑːrˈpedʒiːoʊ/ (US) /ɑːˈpedʒiːəʊ/ (UK)| n. |琶音|A musical chord in which the notes are played in rapid succession, usually ascending, rather than simultaneously.|The guitarist played a beautiful arpeggio.|近义词：broken chord|反义词: chord|词性: noun

arpeggios:/ɑːrˈpedʒiːoʊz/ (US) /ɑːˈpedʒiːəʊz/ (UK)| n. |琶音(复数)|Plural form of arpeggio, referring to multiple instances of this musical technique.|The piece was full of rapid arpeggios.|近义词：broken chords|反义词: chords|词性: noun
