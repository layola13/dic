huntsman:/ˈhʌntsmən/ | n. |猎人；猎场看守|A person who hunts, especially one who hunts professionally or for sport. |The huntsman tracked the deer through the forest. 猎人追踪着鹿穿过森林。|Synonyms: hunter, sportsman, trapper|Antonyms: prey, quarry|noun
