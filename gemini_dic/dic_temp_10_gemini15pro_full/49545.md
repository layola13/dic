dematerialised:/diːməˈtɪəriəlaɪzd/
adjective
非物质化的；无实体的
Converted from physical to electronic form.  Existing in a non-physical form.
例句：Dematerialised shares are held electronically, not as paper certificates.  非物质化股票以电子方式持有，而不是纸质证书。

例句：The company encouraged dematerialised billing to reduce paper waste. 公司鼓励无纸化账单以减少纸张浪费。
近义词：intangible, electronic, digital, virtual
反义词：physical, tangible, material
词性：adjective

词源:  From de- (meaning "remove" or "reverse") + materialise (meaning "to become material or tangible") + -ed (past participle suffix).  Ultimately from Latin materia ("matter").
