metic:/ˈmɛtɪk/ | adj. |一丝不苟的，非常仔细的，小心翼翼的 | Excessively careful; painstaking; scrupulous. | He's very meticulous about his work, checking every detail carefully. 他对工作一丝不苟，仔细检查每一个细节。| Synonyms: painstaking, scrupulous, fastidious, punctilious | Antonyms: careless, sloppy, negligent | adjective


metic:/mɪˈtɪkəlɪs/ | adv. |一丝不苟地，非常仔细地，小心翼翼地 | In a meticulous manner; with extreme care and attention to detail. | The painting was meticulously restored to its original condition. 这幅画被一丝不苟地修复到原来的状态。| Synonyms: carefully, painstakingly, scrupulously, precisely | Antonyms: carelessly, sloppily, negligently | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *meticulosus, from Latin metus "fear, anxiety" + -culosus "-ous, full of."  The original sense was "fearful, timid," later evolving to "overly cautious, scrupulous."
