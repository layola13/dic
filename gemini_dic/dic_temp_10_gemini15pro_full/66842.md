expatriating: /ɪkˈspeɪtriˌeɪtɪŋ/ /ekˈspeɪtriˌeɪtɪŋ/
verb
放弃国籍; 移居国外
The act of leaving one's native country to live elsewhere.
She is expatriating to France. 她将移居法国。
He is in the process of expatriating to avoid high taxes. 他正在办理移居国外的手续以避开高额税收。

近义词:emigrating, relocating, leaving
反义词:repatriating, returning, immigrating
词性:verb (present participle)

expatriate: /ɪkˈspeɪtriət/ /ekˈspeɪtriət/
noun
移居国外者; 被流放者
A person who lives outside their native country.
He is an expatriate living in Spain. 他是一位居住在西班牙的侨民。
Many American expatriates live in Paris. 许多美国侨民居住在巴黎。

近义词:emigrant, exile
反义词:repatriate, immigrant
词性:noun


expatriate: /ɪkˈspeɪtriət/ /ekˈspeɪtriət/
verb

To leave one's native country to live elsewhere; to banish.
He expatriated to Canada. 他移居到了加拿大。
The government expatriated the dissidents. 政府流放了持不同政见者。

近义词:emigrate, banish, exile
反义词:repatriate, return, immigrate
词性:verb
