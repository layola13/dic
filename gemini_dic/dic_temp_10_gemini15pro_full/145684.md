overcopiously: /ˌoʊvərˈkoʊpiəsli/
adverb
过度丰富地，过多地
Excessively abundantly; more than necessary or desirable.
He praised her overcopiously, making her uncomfortable. 他过分地赞扬她，让她感到不舒服。
The book was overcopiously illustrated, distracting from the text.  这本书插图过多，分散了对文本的注意力。
Synonyms: excessively, profusely, immoderately, redundantly
Antonyms: sparsely, sparingly, moderately, briefly
词性: 副词
