massy:/ˈmæsi/| adj.|厚重的，笨重的，大量的|Large, heavy, and solid; bulky.  Having considerable mass.|The castle walls were massy and impenetrable. 城堡的墙壁厚重而坚不可摧。|近义词：massive, bulky, heavy, weighty|反义词：light, slight, airy|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *massif, *massi, from Old French massif (“massive, solid”), from masse (“mass”), from Latin massa (“lump, kneaded dough, mass”).
