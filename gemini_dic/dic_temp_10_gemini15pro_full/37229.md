comfortabilities:/ˈkʌmfərtəˈbɪlɪtiz/
noun
舒适设施；安逸的条件
material comforts and conveniences that make life pleasant.
The hotel offers a wide range of comfortabilities, including a spa, a pool, and a fitness center.  这家酒店提供各种舒适设施，包括水疗中心、游泳池和健身中心。
They sacrificed some of the comfortabilities of life in the city to live in the country.  为了在乡下生活，他们牺牲了城市生活中的一些舒适条件。

Synonyms:  amenities, conveniences, luxuries
Antonyms:  discomforts, inconveniences, hardships
词性: 名词 (plural)
