battling:/ˈbætəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 战斗的；拼搏的；奋斗的 | Engaged in a struggle or fight; struggling against something. |  The two teams are battling for the championship.  两支队伍正在争夺冠军。

| 近义词：fighting, struggling, contending, combating | 反义词：yielding, surrendering, submitting | 词性: 形容词

battling:/ˈbætəlɪŋ/ | n. | 战斗；搏斗 | The act of fighting or struggling. | The battling between the two armies lasted for days. 两军之间的战斗持续了数日。

| 近义词：fighting, combat, struggle, contention | 反义词：peace, truce, armistice | 词性: 名词


Etymology: From the verb "battle" (from Old French "bataille"), ultimately from Late Latin "battualia" meaning "military exercise."
