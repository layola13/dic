inconveniently:/ˌɪnkənˈviːniəntli/ | adv. | 不方便地，麻烦地 |  In a way that causes problems or difficulty; not easily or readily done. |  The meeting was inconveniently scheduled for 6 a.m.  会议的时间安排在早上6点，很不方便。|  The bus stop was inconveniently located far from my house. 公交车站位置很不方便，离我家很远。 | 近义词:awkwardly, unsuitably, unfortunately | 反义词: conveniently, easily, handily | 词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + convenient + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Convenient" comes from the Latin "conveniens," present participle of "convenire" (to come together, be suitable).
