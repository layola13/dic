ingot:/ˈɪŋɡət/| n. |锭; 块 |A mass of metal, such as gold, silver, or steel, cast in a convenient shape for storage or transportation.|The gold was melted down and poured into ingots. 黄金被熔化并浇铸成金锭。|近义词：bar, block, lump|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 ore 矿石, 或powder 粉末 来表示原材料状态) |noun

词源：
From Old English *ingot, from Old Norse ingot (“something poured in”), from inn (“in”) + gota (“to pour”).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *gutōną (“to pour”).
