concession:/kənˈseʃən/ /kənˈsɛʃən/ | n. |让步，妥协；特许权；优惠；承认；租界|Something granted or yielded, often in response to demands; a thing conceded.|The company made several concessions to the workers during the negotiations.  公司在谈判期间对工人做出了几项让步。|synonyms: compromise, allowance, privilege, admission, grant|antonyms: resistance, refusal, denial|noun

concession:/kənˈseʃən/ /kənˈsɛʃən/ | n. |（尤指体育比赛或演出场所的）售卖处，销售摊|A stand or area within a larger venue where food, drinks, or other items are sold.|The baseball stadium has several concessions selling hot dogs, popcorn, and beer. 棒球场内有几个摊位出售热狗、爆米花和啤酒。|synonyms: stand, booth, kiosk|antonyms: N/A|noun
