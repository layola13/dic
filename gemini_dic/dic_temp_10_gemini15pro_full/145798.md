overdecorate: /ˌoʊvərˈdɛkəˌreɪt/
verb
过度装饰
To decorate excessively; to adorn with too much ornamentation.
The living room was overdecorated, with every surface covered in knick-knacks. 客厅过度装饰，每个表面都摆满了小摆设。
She tends to overdecorate her house for Christmas. 她往往会在圣诞节过度装饰她的房子。

近义词: over-embellish, overdo
反义词: underdecorate, simplify
词性: verb
