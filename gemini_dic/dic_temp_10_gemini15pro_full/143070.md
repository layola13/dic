outbrag:/ˌaʊtˈbræɡ/
verb
吹嘘胜过; 比…更会吹牛
to boast more or better than someone else
He outbrag all his rivals. 他比他所有的对手都更会吹牛。
He outbragged his friends about his new car. 他向朋友们吹嘘他的新车。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel, boast, brag
反义词: be modest, downplay
词性: verb
