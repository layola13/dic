orphaned: /ˈɔːrfənd/ (US) /ˈɔːfənd/ (UK)| adj.| 孤儿的，失去父母的；无人照顾的|Deprived of parents by death or desertion, or (of a thing) neglected and abandoned.|The orphaned children were taken to a shelter. 这些孤儿被带到一个收容所。|Synonyms: parentless, fatherless, motherless, abandoned, neglected|Antonyms: protected, cared for|adjective


orphaned: /ˈɔːrfənd/ (US) /ˈɔːfənd/ (UK)| vt.| 使成为孤儿；使丧失|To cause (someone) to become an orphan.|The war orphaned many children. 战争使许多孩子成了孤儿。|Synonyms: bereave, deprive|Antonyms: adopt, foster|verb
