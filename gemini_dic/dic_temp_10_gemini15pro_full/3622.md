agelessness:/ˈeɪdʒləsnəs/
|n.|永恒; 不老; 青春永驻|The state of not appearing to grow old or showing the effects of age.|Her agelessness was the envy of all her friends. 她的青春永驻令所有朋友羡慕。|近义词:eternity, timelessness, immortality|反义词:aging, senescence|词性:名词
Etymology: From age + -less + -ness.  "age" comes from Old French "age," ultimately from Latin "aetas" (age, lifetime). "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking," from Old English "-lēas." "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being."
