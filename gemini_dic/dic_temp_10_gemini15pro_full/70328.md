fielded:/ˈfiːldɪd/ | v. | 接住，处理，派…上场 | To catch or stop (a ball) in baseball or cricket; to deal with (a question or argument); to put (a team, candidate, etc.) into a game or contest |  The shortstop fielded the grounder and threw to first.  游击手接住地滚球并传给一垒手。| Synonyms: handled, managed, answered, dealt with | Antonyms: ignored, avoided, dropped | verb

fielded:/ˈfiːldɪd/ | adj. | 派上场的，投入使用的 | Put into a game or contest; deployed | They fielded a strong team for the competition. 他们派出了一支强大的队伍参加比赛。 | Synonyms: deployed, utilized, employed | Antonyms: unused, benched, withdrawn | adjective
