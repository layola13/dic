inelegancy:/ɪnˈɛlɪɡənsi/
n.
不雅；粗俗；笨拙
lack of elegance or grace; awkwardness.
Her inelegancy of speech embarrassed her husband. 她讲话的粗俗让她丈夫很尴尬。
近义词:awkwardness, clumsiness, gaucherie, ungainliness
反义词:elegance, grace, refinement, polish
词性:名词


inelegant:/ɪnˈɛlɪɡənt/
adj.
不雅的；粗俗的；笨拙的
lacking elegance or grace; awkward.
He made an inelegant attempt to excuse himself. 他笨拙地试图为自己辩解。
近义词:awkward, clumsy, graceless, ungainly
反义词:elegant, graceful, refined, polished
词性:形容词
