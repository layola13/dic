dormant:/ˈdɔːrmənt/ (US) /ˈdɔːmənt/ (UK)| adj.|休眠的，蛰伏的，潜伏的|In a state of rest or inactivity; temporarily inactive, as if asleep.  Similar to hibernation in animals, but can apply to anything that is temporarily inactive.|The volcano has been dormant for centuries. 这座火山已经休眠了几个世纪了。|The seeds remain dormant until the spring. 种子一直休眠到春天。|近义词：inactive, quiescent, latent, asleep, resting|反义词：active, awake, lively, vigorous|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French *dormant, present participle of dormir (“to sleep”), from Latin dormīre (“to sleep”).
