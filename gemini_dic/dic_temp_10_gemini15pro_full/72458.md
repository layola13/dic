flummox: /ˈflʌməks/ | n. | 困惑，混乱 | A state of confusion. |  His sudden question threw me into a flummox. 他的突然提问让我不知所措。 | synonyms: confusion, bewilderment, perplexity | antonyms: clarity, understanding | noun
