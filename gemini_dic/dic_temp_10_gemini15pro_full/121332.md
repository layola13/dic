misgauging:/ˌmɪsˈɡeɪdʒɪŋ/
v.
错误估计；判断失误
Incorrectly estimating or judging something.
He misgauged the distance and drove off the road. 他错误估计了距离，把车开下了路。
The company misgauged the public's reaction to the new product. 公司错误估计了公众对新产品的反应。
miscalculate, misjudge, underestimate, overestimate
correctly gauge, accurately assess
verb
