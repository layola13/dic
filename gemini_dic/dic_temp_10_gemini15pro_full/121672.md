misplans:/ˌmɪsˈplænz/

misplan:/ˌmɪsˈplæn/
|n.|错误的计划；计划不周|A plan that is badly or incorrectly made.|The project's failure was due to a series of misplans. 项目的失败是由于一系列计划不周造成的。|miscalculation, blunder|good plan, sound strategy|noun
