browbeaters:/ˈbraʊˌbiːtərz/
noun
恐吓者；威吓者
People who intimidate or bully others into doing something through persistent criticism, threats, or other aggressive tactics.
The browbeaters forced him to sign the confession.  这些威吓者强迫他签署了供词。
Synonyms: bullies, intimidators, oppressors, tyrants
Antonyms: supporters, encouragers, protectors
n.

browbeater:/ˈbraʊˌbiːtər/
noun
恐吓者；威吓者
A person who intimidates or bullies others into doing something through persistent criticism, threats, or other aggressive tactics.
He was a notorious browbeater, making his employees' lives miserable. 他是一个臭名昭著的威吓者，让他的员工生活得很悲惨。
Synonyms: bully, intimidator, oppressor, tyrant
Antonyms: supporter, encourager, protector
n.

browbeat:/ˈbraʊˌbiːt/
verb
威吓；恐吓
To intimidate or bully someone into doing something through persistent criticism, threats, or other aggressive tactics.
She was browbeaten into accepting the deal. 她被恐吓着接受了这笔交易。
Synonyms: intimidate, bully, coerce, pressure
Antonyms: encourage, support, persuade
