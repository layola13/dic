distinguishable:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbl/| adj.|可区分的，可辨别的|able to be recognized as different; distinct|例句：The twins are so alike that they are barely distinguishable. 这对双胞胎长得太像了，几乎难以区分。|近义词：differentiable, discernible, recognizable|反义词：indistinguishable, identical, similar|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Middle English *distinguisshable*, from Old French distinguishable (modern French distinguishable), from distinguer + -able.  Ultimately from Latin distinguere "to separate, divide, distinguish".
