murray:/ˈmʌri/ (US), /ˈmʌri/ (UK)| n.|默里（男子名）; 穆雷河（澳大利亚最长的河流）|A male given name, also the longest river in Australia. |例句：Murray is a common Scottish surname. 默里是一个常见的苏格兰姓氏。

例句：The Murray River is vital to Australia's ecosystem. 穆雷河对澳大利亚的生态系统至关重要。|近义词：(For the river) waterway, stream |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
