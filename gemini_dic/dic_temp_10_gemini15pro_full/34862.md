clinometric:/ˌklɪnəˈmɛtrɪk/| adj.|测斜的，倾斜测量的|Relating to or using a clinometer, an instrument for measuring angles of slope or elevation, especially in geology, surveying, and forestry.|The geologist used a clinometric compass to determine the dip of the rock strata. 地质学家使用测斜罗盘来确定岩层的倾角。|Synonyms: inclinometric, gradient-measuring|Antonyms: level, horizontal|Adjective
