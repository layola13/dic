consequentially:/ˌkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃəli/ /ˌkɑːnsɪˈkwɛnʃəli/ | adv. | 因此，结果，所以 | As a result; therefore.  | The roads were flooded, and consequentially, schools were closed. 道路被淹了，所以学校停课了。 |  The company lost a major contract, and consequentially, had to lay off several employees. 公司失去了一份重要的合同，因此不得不解雇几名员工。 | 近义词: therefore, consequently, accordingly, hence, thus | 反义词: inconsequentially, unrelatedly | 词性: 副词

词源: Late Middle English: from medieval Latin consequentialiter, from consequens ‘following’.
