aliens: /ˈeɪliənz/ | n. | 外星人；外国人；外来物种 | Beings from another planet; people from another country; species not native to a particular environment. |  例句：Scientists are searching for signs of aliens. 科学家们正在寻找外星人的迹象。| 近义词：extraterrestrials, foreigners, non-natives | 反义词：natives, earthlings | 词性: 名词

词源: From Latin *alienus*, from *alius* "other".
