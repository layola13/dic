hordein:/ˈhɔːrdiːɪn/| n. |大麦醇溶蛋白|A prolamin protein found in barley.  It is a storage protein and a component of gluten.|Barley hordein can cause allergic reactions in some individuals. 大麦醇溶蛋白会引起某些人的过敏反应。|Hordein proteins contribute to the viscoelastic properties of barley dough. 大麦醇溶蛋白有助于大麦面团的粘弹性。|近义词:prolamin, glutenin|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific protein)|词性:noun
