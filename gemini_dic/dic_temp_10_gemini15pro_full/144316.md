outsprings:/ˈaʊtˌsprɪŋz/
noun
涌出；源泉；（尤指沙漠中的）泉水
A place where water springs from the earth.  The flow of water from such a place.
例句：The outsprings in the desert provided a vital source of water for the travelers. 沙漠中的泉水为旅行者提供了重要的水源。

例句：The outsprings were contaminated by the recent rains. 最近的降雨污染了泉水。

近义词：springs, sources, fountains, wells
反义词：inflows, sinks, drains
词性:n.
