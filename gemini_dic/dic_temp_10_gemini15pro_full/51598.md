devastate:/ˈdevəsteɪt/| v. |摧毁，毁灭；使极度震惊，使极为悲痛|To destroy or ruin something completely; to cause someone to feel extreme emotional pain.|The earthquake devastated the city. 地震摧毁了这座城市。|The news of her death devastated her family. 她去世的消息使她的家人悲痛欲绝。|synonyms: ruin, destroy, wreck, shatter, crush |antonyms: build, construct, create, repair, restore|verb
