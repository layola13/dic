coronule:/ˈkɒrənˌjuːl/ /ˈkɔːrəˌnuːl/
|n.|小冠，花冠，（鸟类）肉冠|A small crown or crown-like structure, especially in flowers or on the heads of birds.|The coronule of the flower is a delicate shade of pink.|近义词:crown, coronal, circlet|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider absence or lack of a coronule)
|noun|
