clonality:/kloʊˈnælɪti/| n.|克隆性，无性繁殖性|The state of being clonal; derived asexually from a single ancestor.|The clonality of the tumor cells was confirmed by genetic analysis. 肿瘤细胞的克隆性通过基因分析得到了证实。|Near synonyms: homogeneity, uniformity|Antonym: heterogeneity|noun
