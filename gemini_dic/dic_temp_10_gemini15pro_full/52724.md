diiambus:/ˌdaɪˈæmbəs/| n.|抑扬音步，短长格|A metrical foot in poetry consisting of one short or unstressed syllable followed by one long or stressed syllable.|The poem was written in diiambic pentameter. 这首诗是用抑扬格五音步写的。|近义词:iamb|反义词:trochee|noun
