cabbage-like:/ˈkæbɪdʒˌlaɪk/
adjective
像卷心菜的；类似卷心菜的
Resembling a cabbage in appearance, texture, or form.  Having tightly packed leaves or a rounded shape similar to a cabbage.
The plant had a cabbage-like head of leaves.  这种植物有一个像卷心菜一样的叶球。
The tumor was large and cabbage-like.  肿瘤很大，像卷心菜一样。
近义词:  cabbagey,  round, compact
反义词:  loose, sprawling
词性: adjective
