allowedly:/əˈlaʊɪdli/
adv.
容许地，无可否认地
admittedly, confessedly; in an allowed manner.  It implies that something is being acknowledged, often reluctantly or with a sense of concession.
Allowedly, he had made a mistake.  无可否认地，他犯了一个错误。
近义词:admittedly, confessedly, undeniably, undoubtedly
反义词:disputedly, contestably
词性:副词

词源: From "allowed" (past participle of "allow") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Allow" comes from Old French "alouer," which in turn comes from Latin "allocare" meaning "to place, assign."
