fecundity:/fɪˈkʌndɪti/
n.
多产；肥沃；丰饶
The ability to produce many offspring or much fruit; fertility.
例句：The fecundity of the rabbit is well known. 兔子的繁殖能力是众所周知的。

例句：The soil in this region is known for its fecundity.  这个地区的土壤以其肥沃而闻名。


近义词：fertility, productiveness, fruitfulness
反义词：sterility, barrenness, infertility
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *fecundite, from Old French fecundité, from Latin fēcunditās (“fruitfulness, fertility”), from fēcundus (“fruitful, fertile”), from *fe- (“to suck, suckle”) + *cundō (“I become pregnant”).  The same Indo-European root also produced the words "fetus" and "female."
