half-angrily:/ˌhæf ˈæŋɡrɪli/
adv.
半生气地，略带怒气地
Partly or slightly angrily.
He responded half-angrily, half-jokingly. 他半是生气半是开玩笑地回答。
近义词:irritatedly, crossly, peevishly
反义词:calmly, pleasantly, cheerfully
词性:副词

Etymology:
"half" (Old English healf) + "angrily" (from "angry" - Old Norse angr + "-ly" adverbial suffix)
