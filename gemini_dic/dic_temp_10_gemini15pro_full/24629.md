brokennesses:/ˈbroʊkənɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
破碎，破损；弱点，缺点
The state of being broken, either physically or emotionally.  This can refer to multiple instances of brokenness or a pervasive state of being broken.
Her life was marked by a series of brokennesses, from financial struggles to health problems.  她的生活充满了一系列的破碎，从经济困难到健康问题。
The brokennesses of the system were apparent to everyone.  这个系统的缺陷对每个人来说都是显而易见的。
frailty, fragility, weakness, vulnerability
strength, resilience, wholeness
