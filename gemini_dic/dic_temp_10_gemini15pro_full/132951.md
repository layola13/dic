nonemissions:/ˌnɒnɪˈmɪʃənz/
noun
无排放，零排放
Referring to something that does not release pollutants into the environment, especially referring to gases.
The company is investing heavily in nonemissions vehicles.  该公司正在大力投资无排放车辆。
synonyms: zero-emissions, pollution-free
antonyms: emissions, polluting
n.
