overlanguaged:/ˌoʊvərˈlæŋɡwɪdʒd/
adjective
过分修饰的，语言过度的
Excessively elaborate or embellished in language; using too many words or too complex a vocabulary.
The academic paper was dense and overlanguaged, making it difficult for the average reader to understand. 这篇学术论文语言繁复，过分修饰，使得普通读者难以理解。

She tried to impress the professor with her overlanguaged essay, but it ended up sounding pretentious. 她试图用一篇语言过分修饰的论文给教授留下深刻印象，但结果听起来很做作。

近义词:verbose, prolix, wordy, long-winded
反义词:concise, succinct, terse, brief
词性:adjective
