outskirmished:/ˌaʊtˈskɜːmɪʃt/ | v. | 击败，在小规模战斗中胜过 | defeated in a skirmish; prevailed in minor battles | The smaller, faster army outskirmished the larger, slower one.  这支规模较小、速度较快的军队在小规模战斗中战胜了规模较大、速度较慢的那支军队。| outfought, outmaneuvered |  | verb
outskirmish:/ˌaʊtˈskɜːmɪʃ/ | v. | 在小规模战斗中胜过 | to defeat or best in a skirmish |  The light cavalry outskirmished the enemy's scouts. 轻骑兵在小规模战斗中战胜了敌人的侦察兵。 | outfight, outmaneuver | surrender to, lose to | verb

Etymology:
From out- + skirmish.  "Out-" prefix signifies surpassing or exceeding in the action of the verb. Skirmish comes from the Old French "escarmouche" and ultimately from the Italian "scaramuccia."
