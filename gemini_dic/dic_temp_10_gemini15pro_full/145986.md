overdogmatic: /ˌəʊvədɒɡˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 过于教条的，过于武断的 | Excessively dogmatic; asserting opinions in a doctrinaire or arrogant manner.  Characterized by or given to excessive dogmatism. | He was criticized for being overdogmatic in his views on education. 他因为在教育方面的观点过于教条而受到批评。|  Synonyms: doctrinaire, rigid, inflexible, opinionated, authoritarian, peremptory | Antonyms: open-minded, flexible, tolerant, receptive, pragmatic | adjective
