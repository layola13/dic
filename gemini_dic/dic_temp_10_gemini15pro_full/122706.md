The moguls on this slope are particularly difficult.
这个斜坡上的雪丘特别难滑。

moguls: /ˈmoʊɡəlz/ (US) /ˈməʊɡ(ə)lz/ (UK)| n. |雪丘；|Bumps on a ski slope.|

例句：
The skier navigated the moguls with skill.
滑雪者熟练地绕过雪丘。

She fell while trying to ski down the moguls.
她在尝试滑下雪丘时摔倒了。


近义词：bumps, mounds|
反义词: smooth surface|
词性: noun


词源: From Yiddish מאָגעל‎ (mogel, “important person, big shot”), from German Mogel (“swindler, fraud”), from Middle High German mūgen (“to be able, can”).  The skiing sense comes from its resemblance to the shape of a mogul's grave mound.
