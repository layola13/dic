corpuscularian:/kɔːrˈpʌskjʊˈlɛəriən/ | adj. n. |微粒的，微粒说的；微粒论者|Relating to or based on the theory that matter is made up of minute particles or corpuscles.  A believer in the theory that physical phenomena are caused by the size, shape, arrangement, and motion of minute particles of matter. |Newton was a corpuscularian in his view of light. 牛顿的光学观点是微粒说。|近义词:atomistic, particulate|反义词:wave-like|词性:adjective, noun

corpuscularian:/kɔːrˈpʌskjʊˈlɛəriən/ | n. |微粒论者|A person who believes in the corpuscular theory of light.|Many prominent scientists of the 17th century were corpuscularians. 许多17世纪的杰出科学家都是微粒论者。|近义词:atomist|反义词:wave theorist|词性:noun
