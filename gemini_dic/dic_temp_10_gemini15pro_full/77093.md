garrote:/ɡəˈrɒt/ (UK), /ɡəˈrɑːt/ (US)| n. vt.|绞刑，勒死； 扼杀；压制；金属丝；弓弦|A wire or cord used for strangulation.  A method of execution by strangulation.|The dictator used the garrote to silence his opponents. 独裁者用绞刑来压制他的反对者。|He garroted the guard with a piece of wire. 他用一根金属丝勒死了警卫。|Synonyms: strangle, choke, throttle, asphyxiate|Antonyms: liberate, free, release|noun, verb
