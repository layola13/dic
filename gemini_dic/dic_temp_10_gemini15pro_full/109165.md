legitimates:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmeɪts/
|v.|使合法化；证明…为合法|To make something legal or lawful; to show something to be justified or valid.|The government legitimates the use of force in self-defense. 政府将自卫时使用武力合法化。|validates, justifies, authorizes|invalidates, prohibits|verb

legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/
|adj.|合法的；正当的；合理的|Conforming to the law or to rules; able to be defended with logic or justification; genuine.|He had a legitimate reason for being late. 他迟到是有正当理由的。|legal, valid, lawful|illegal, invalid, unlawful|adjective

legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/
|n.|合法的人（或事物）|Something that is considered legal or proper.|His business is a perfectly legitimate enterprise. 他的生意是一个完全合法的企业。|authorized, valid|illegal, illegitimate|noun
