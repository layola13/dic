madly:/ˈmædli/| adv.|疯狂地，狂热地，非常地|In a mad or insane manner; with extreme enthusiasm or passion.|She danced madly across the stage. 她疯狂地在舞台上跳舞。|近义词：insanely, wildly, frantically, furiously|反义词：calmly, sanely, rationally|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *madli, from Old English *madlice, from mad +‎ -ly.
