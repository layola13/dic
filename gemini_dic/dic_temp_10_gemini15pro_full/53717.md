noun

The act of making something known or the fact that something has become known.
The company's disclosure's of its financial difficulties led to a drop in its stock price. 该公司对其财务困境的披露导致其股价下跌。
Synonyms: revelation, exposé, unveiling
Antonyms: concealment, suppression, secrecy
