dryades:/ˈdraɪ.ə.diːz/
noun
树精; 森林女神
Wood nymphs or tree spirits in Greek mythology.
Dryades are often depicted as beautiful maidens who inhabit trees and protect the forests.  树精通常被描绘成居住在树木中并保护森林的美丽少女。
The ancient Greeks believed that harming a tree inhabited by a dryad would bring misfortune. 古希腊人认为，伤害一棵树精居住的树木会带来厄运。
