loungers: /ˈlaʊndʒərz/
n.
休闲椅；躺椅；无所事事的人
People who relax or spend time idly, often in a reclining position.  Those who habitually lounge.
The loungers on the beach were enjoying the sunshine.  沙滩上的休闲者们正在享受阳光。
synonyms: idlers, loafers, relaxers, recliners
antonyms: workers, hustlers, doers
noun
