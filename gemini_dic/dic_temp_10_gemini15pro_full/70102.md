feverous: /ˈfiːvərəs/ | adj. | 发烧的，狂热的 | Having or showing the symptoms of a fever; marked by intense emotion or activity. |  She had a feverous glow in her cheeks. 她两颊泛着发烧的红晕。|  The atmosphere in the city was feverous with anticipation. 城市里充满了期待的狂热气氛。 | 近义词：febrile, feverish, hectic, frenzied | 反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *fevorous, *feverose, from Anglo-Norman fevrus, from Old French fievreus (“feverish”), from Medieval Latin febris (“fever”).  The ending -ous is a common adjective-forming suffix derived from Latin -osus.
