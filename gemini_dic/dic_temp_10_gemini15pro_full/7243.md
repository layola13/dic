anglo-catholicisms:/ˌæŋɡləʊˈkæθəlɪsɪzəmz/
|n.|盎格鲁天主教主义|A movement within the Church of England that emphasizes the Catholic heritage of the Anglican Church, often incorporating Catholic liturgical practices and doctrines while remaining within the Anglican Communion.|The anglo-catholicisms of the parish were evident in the elaborate vestments and ceremonial rituals.|Synonym: High Churchmanship|Antonym: Low Churchmanship|Noun
