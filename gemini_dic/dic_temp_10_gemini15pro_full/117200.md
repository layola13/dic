measurement:/ˈmeʒərmənt/ (US), /ˈmeʒəmənt/ (UK)| n.|测量；尺寸；测量值；度量衡|The process of ascertaining the size, amount, or degree of something by using an instrument or device marked in standard units or by comparing it with an object of known size.|The tailor took my measurements for a new suit.  裁缝给我量了尺寸做新西装。|synonyms: dimension, size, extent, quantity, magnitude|antonyms: estimation, guess, approximation|noun
