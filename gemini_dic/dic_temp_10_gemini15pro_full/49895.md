demurrant:/dɪˈmɜːrənt/ | n. | 异议者，抗辩者 | A person who demurs; someone who raises objections or hesitates. | The demurrant argued against the proposed changes to the contract. 异议者反对对合同提出的修改建议。 |  Synonyms: objector, dissenter, protestor | Antonyms: supporter, proponent | noun

demur:/dɪˈmɜːr/ | v. | 表示异议，反对；犹豫 | To raise doubts or objections or show reluctance. |  She demurred at the idea of working late. 她对加班的想法表示反对。| Synonyms: object, hesitate, protest | Antonyms: agree, accept, consent | verb
