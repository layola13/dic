genuine:/ˈdʒɛnjuɪn/| adj.| 真实的，真正的；真诚的，诚恳的|Actually possessing the alleged or apparent attribute or character: Authentic.  Truly what something is said to be; authentic.|The painting was found to be genuine. 这幅画被发现是真的。|近义词：authentic, real, true, sincere|反义词：fake, false, counterfeit, insincere|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *genuinus* "innate, native, natural," from *genu-* (see genus).  The sense of "not counterfeit or adulterated" is first recorded in the mid-17th century.
