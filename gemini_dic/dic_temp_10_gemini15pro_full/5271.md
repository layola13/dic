allons:/ˈælɒ̃/ (UK), /æˈlɔ̃/ (US)| interj. |走吧，我们走吧| An exclamation used to encourage and rally others, primarily meaning "let's go" or "come on." It's borrowed from French. |例句：Allons, children!  Let's get this show on the road! 走吧，孩子们！让我们开始吧！

例句：Allons! We're going to be late! 走吧！我们要迟到了！|近义词：Come on, let's go|反义词: Stay, wait|词性: Interjection
