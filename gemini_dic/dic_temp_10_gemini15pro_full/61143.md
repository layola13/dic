eloquent: /ˈɛləkwənt/| adj.|雄辩的，有说服力的；流畅的，清晰的|Fluent and persuasive in speech or writing.|Her eloquent speech moved the audience to tears. 她雄辩的演讲感动了听众，让他们流下了眼泪。|近义词:articulate, expressive, fluent, persuasive|反义词:inarticulate, tongue-tied, halting|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *eloquentem (nominative eloquens) "speaking out, expressive," present participle of eloqui "speak out," from ex- "out" + loqui "to speak."
