bantering:/ˈbæntərɪŋ/ | adj. |  开玩笑的，打趣的 |  Characterized by good-humored teasing or playful exchange of remarks. |  They were having a bantering exchange about their favorite sports teams. 他们就各自最喜欢的球队开着玩笑。 |  playful, teasing, joking, lighthearted |  serious, somber | adjective

banter: /ˈbæntər/ | n. vi. vt. |  玩笑，戏谑；开玩笑，说笑 |  Playful teasing or exchange of witty remarks.  |  The friends enjoyed some friendly banter over coffee. 朋友们喝着咖啡，互相开着玩笑。 | badinage, raillery, repartee | seriousness, solemnity | noun, verb, intransitive verb, transitive verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *banterer*, of unknown origin. Possibly related to Old French *bandon* "proclamation," source of English *abandon*.
