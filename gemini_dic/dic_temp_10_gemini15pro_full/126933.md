narly:/ˈnɑːrli/ (US), /ˈnɑːli/ (UK)| adj.| 困难的；危险的；极好的；令人兴奋的|Difficult or dangerous, especially in an exciting or appealing way.  Originally surfer slang, meaning challenging but enjoyable waves. Later expanded to describe anything excellent, cool, or awesome.  Can also mean unpleasant or unattractive, especially in British English.|例句：That wave was totally gnarly!  那个浪太棒了！

例句：The hike up the mountain was pretty gnarly.  爬上这座山真是相当困难。

近义词：awesome, cool, excellent, wicked, challenging, difficult, hairy (informal), tricky|反义词:easy, simple, straightforward, boring|词性:形容词


narly:/ˈnɑːrli/ (US), /ˈnɑːli/ (UK)| adj.| 令人不快的；不吸引人的 (主要用于英式英语)|Unpleasant or unattractive.|例句：He had a gnarly old beard.  他留着令人讨厌的旧胡子。

例句：The wound looked gnarly and infected.  伤口看起来很难看而且感染了。

近义词：unpleasant, unattractive, ugly, gruesome|反义词:pleasant, attractive, beautiful|词性:形容词
