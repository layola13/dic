improves:/ɪmˈpruːvz/
verb
改进，改善
to make or become better
Regular exercise improves your health.  规律的运动能改善你的健康。
synonyms: enhances, betters, ameliorates, upgrades
antonyms: worsens, deteriorates, declines
词性: 动词(verb)

词源: From Middle English *improven, *improuen, from Old French emprouver, based on Latin in- + probare “to test, prove, approve”.  The meaning shifted from "to make a profit from" to "to make better" in the 16th century.
