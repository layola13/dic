nonmedullated:/ˌnɒnˈmɛdjʊleɪtɪd/ /ˌnɑːnˈmɛdʒəˌleɪtɪd/| adj.|无髓的，无髓鞘的|Not covered with or containing myelin; lacking a myelin sheath (referring to nerve fibers).|Example: Nonmedullated nerve fibers conduct impulses more slowly than myelinated ones. 无髓神经纤维比有髓神经纤维传导冲动更慢。

Example:  Pain signals are often transmitted via nonmedullated nerve fibers.  疼痛信号通常通过无髓神经纤维传递。|近义词:unmyelinated|反义词:myelinated|词性:形容词
