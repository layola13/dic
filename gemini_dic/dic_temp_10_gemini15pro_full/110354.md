lightninging: /ˈlaɪtnɪŋɪŋ/ | n. | 闪电(罕用，通常用 lightning) |  The act or process of emitting lightning. This form is rarely used; "lightning" itself often serves as a verb. |  The lightninging of the sky was intense during the storm. (天空在暴风雨期间闪电不断，非常强烈。) | 近义词：flashing, sparking, streaking | 反义词: darkness, gloom | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *lightninge, from Old English *līhting, from līhtan (“to shine, give light”), from Proto-Germanic *leuhtjaną (“to shine, give light”), from Proto-Indo-European *lewk- (“light, bright”).
