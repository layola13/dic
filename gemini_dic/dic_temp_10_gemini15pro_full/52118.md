Simple Present:
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? 他做什么工作？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? 她正在做什么晚餐？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has he done with the money? 他用这笔钱做了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading this week? 她这周一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派对上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you came here? 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had happened before the police arrived? 警察到达之前发生了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后打算做什么？
What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到今年年底你将取得什么成就？
What will have changed by then? 到那时会有什么变化？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2030? 到2030年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业的时候她将已经学习了多久什么专业？
