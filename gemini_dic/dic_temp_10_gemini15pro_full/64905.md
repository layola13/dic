essentials:/ɪˈsenʃəlz/ | n. |必需品；要点；要素|Something that is necessary or very important.|例句：Food, water, and shelter are the essentials of life. 食物、水和住所是生活的必需品。

例句：He packed only the essentials for the camping trip. 他只带了野营旅行的必需品。|近义词：necessities, requisites, fundamentals|反义词：nonessentials, luxuries|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *essencial*, from Old French *essenciel*, from Late Latin *essentialis*, from Latin *essentia* ("being, essence"), from *esse* ("to be").
