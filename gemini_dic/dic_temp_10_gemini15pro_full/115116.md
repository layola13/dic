mangetout:/ˈmændʒətuːt/ (UK), /ˌmɑːnʒˈtuː/ (US)| n.|荷兰豆，雪豆|A type of pea that is eaten whole, including the pod, while still young and flat.  It is also sometimes called a "snow pea".|例句：We had stir-fried mangetouts with our chicken. 我们鸡肉配炒雪豆。

例句：The mangetouts were crisp and sweet. 雪豆又脆又甜。|近义词：snow pea, sugar snap pea|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
