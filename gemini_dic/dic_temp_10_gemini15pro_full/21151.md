blitzkrieging: /ˈblɪtsˌkriːɡɪŋ/
verb
闪电战,闪电式攻击
engaging in a blitzkrieg; attacking suddenly and overwhelmingly.
The general was blitzkrieging through enemy territory. 将军正以闪电战的方式穿越敌人的领土。
Near synonyms: storming, overwhelming, blitzing
Antonyms: defending, retreating
词性：动词 (verb)

blitzkrieg: /ˈblɪtsˌkriːɡ/
noun
闪电战
A swift, intensive military attack, especially using tanks supported by aircraft, designed to defeat the opposition quickly.
The blitzkrieg was successful in the early stages of the war. 闪电战在战争初期取得了成功。
Near synonyms: onslaught, assault, attack
Antonyms: defense, retreat
词性：名词 (noun)
