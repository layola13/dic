dome-like:/ˈdoʊmˌlaɪk/
adjective
穹顶状的；圆顶形的
Resembling a dome in shape.
The yurt has a dome-like roof.  这个蒙古包有一个圆顶形的屋顶。
The observatory has a dome-like structure. 天文台有一个穹顶状的结构。
近义词: domed, vaulted, arched
反义词: flat, planar


ETYMOLOGY->
From dome + -like.  "Dome" comes from the Latin "domus" meaning "house."  "-like" is a suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
