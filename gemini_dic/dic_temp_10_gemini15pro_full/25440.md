bullace:/ˈbʊləs/ | n. |野李子; 西洋李的一种 |A small, wild plum, often dark blue or purple and somewhat astringent.  It is the fruit of the bullace plum (Prunus domestica subsp. insititia).| The children picked bullace from the hedgerow. 孩子们从灌木篱墙上摘野李子。|  Synonyms: wild plum, sloe | Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific fruit, perhaps cultivated plum) | Noun

bullace:/ˈbʊləs/ | adj. |野李子似的 |Resembling or characteristic of a bullace. (Less common usage) |The bullace tart had a pleasantly sharp flavor.  野李子馅饼的味道鲜美而略带酸味。|Synonyms: plum-like, sloe-like |Antonyms: (No direct antonym) | Adjective
