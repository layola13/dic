mestino:/mesˈtiːnoʊ/ | n. | 混血儿；杂种动物 | A person or animal of mixed race or breed. |  Example: The mestino puppy was a mix of Labrador and German Shepherd. 这只混血小狗是拉布拉多犬和德国牧羊犬的混血。| Example:  He identified as mestino, acknowledging his mixed heritage. 他认为自己是混血儿，承认自己的混血血统。 | Synonyms: hybrid, crossbreed, mixed-breed | Antonyms: purebred, pedigree | Noun
