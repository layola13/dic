feistiest:/ˈfʌɪstiɪst/ | adj. | 最活泼的，最精神的，最易怒的 |  Superlative form of feisty: most feisty. It describes someone as being lively, spirited, and perhaps a little easily provoked. |  She was the feistiest competitor in the whole tournament, never backing down from a challenge. 她是在整个锦标赛中最活泼的选手，从不畏惧任何挑战。

The feistiest kitten of the litter was always the first to pounce on a toy.  一窝小猫中最活泼的那只总是第一个扑向玩具的。 | 近义词: spirited, lively, spunky, fiery | 反义词: meek, timid, docile, subdued | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Likely a blend of "fist" (referring to a tendency to fight) and "fisty" (a now-obsolete word meaning lively).
