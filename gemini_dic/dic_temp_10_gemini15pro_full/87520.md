heterodromous:/ˌhɛtərˈɒdrəməs/
| adj. |异向旋转的，反向运动的 |Moving or acting in opposite directions.  Referring specifically to the spiral arrangement of leaves or other plant parts that change direction at some point along the stem. |Example: The heterodromous arrangement of the leaves was a key characteristic for identifying the plant species. 这种叶子的异向旋转排列是识别该植物种类的关键特征。

Example: The botanist noted the heterodromous phyllotaxy of the newly discovered vine.  植物学家注意到新发现的藤蔓的异向旋转叶序。|近义词：antidromous |反义词: homodromous |词性: adjective
