pamperer:/ˈpæmpərər/ | n. | 纵容者；溺爱者 | someone who pampers another person; someone who treats another person with excessive indulgence. | She is a real pamperer of her children, always buying them whatever they want. 她真是个溺爱孩子的人，总是给他们买他们想要的一切。 | He's such a pamperer, he gives his dog gourmet meals. 他真是个溺爱者，他给他的狗吃美食。| 近义词: indulger, spoiler | 反义词: disciplinarian, strict person | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "pamper," which comes from the Middle English word "pampren," meaning "to cram, to glut," of uncertain origin. Possibly related to the Dutch word "pampen," meaning "to cram."
