overthriftiness:/ˌoʊvərˈθrɪftɪnɪs/
|n.|过度节俭，吝啬|Excessive thriftiness; extreme frugality, often to the point of being miserly.|His overthriftiness made him unpopular with his friends, who felt he was always pinching pennies. 他的过度节俭让他在朋友中不受欢迎，朋友们觉得他总是斤斤计较。|Example: Her overthriftiness bordered on stinginess. 她的过度节俭近乎吝啬。|Synonyms: parsimony, stinginess, miserliness, penny-pinching|Antonyms: generosity, extravagance, lavishness|Noun
