goldbeckian:/ɡoʊldˈbɛkiən/
adjective
戈德贝克式的；戈德贝克学派的
Relating to or characteristic of the German architect Hermann Goldbeck (1878-1942), especially his system of reinforced concrete construction.
The Goldbeckian system allowed for the rapid construction of large industrial buildings.  戈德贝克系统使得快速建造大型工业建筑成为可能。
The architect employed a distinctive Goldbeckian style in his design.  这位建筑师在他的设计中采用了独特的戈德贝克风格。
synonyms: reinforced concrete (related to the construction method), Goldbeck's (possessive form)
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, could potentially use terms related to other construction methods like "steel-frame" or "brick-and-mortar" depending on the context)
词性: adjective
