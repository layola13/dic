outblustered:/ˌaʊtˈblʌstərd/
verb
以更大的声势压倒，以更猛烈的咆哮战胜
defeated or silenced by blustering more loudly or forcefully.
The nervous witness was easily outblustered by the aggressive lawyer.  那个紧张的证人很容易就被咄咄逼人的律师压倒了。
The CEO outblustered his opponents at the board meeting. 首席执行官在董事会上以更大的声势压倒了他的反对者。

synonym
overwhelm, subdue, cow, intimidate
antonym
submit, yield, concede
