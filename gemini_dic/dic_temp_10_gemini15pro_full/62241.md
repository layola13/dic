encrustment:/ɪnˈkrʌstmənt/| n.|结壳；包壳；硬壳|A hard coating or deposit on a surface.|The ancient vase was covered in an encrustment of dirt and grime. 古老的花瓶上覆盖着一层污垢和尘垢。|近义词：coating, covering, crust, deposit, layer|反义词: erosion, wearing away|noun

encrust:/ɪnˈkrʌst/| vt.|使…结壳；用…包壳|To cover or decorate with a hard crust.|Barnacles encrusted the hull of the ship. 藤壶包住了船体。|近义词：coat, cover, overlay|反义词: strip, uncover|verb
