anamorphosis:/ˌænəmɔːrˈfoʊsɪs/ | n. | 变形，失真；变形图像，变形画 |The process of transformation or distortion of an image, form, or shape, typically in art, that appears normal only when viewed from a particular angle or with a special device.  | Example: The artist created an anamorphosis of a skull on the floor, which only looked realistic when viewed from a specific point. 艺术家在地板上创作了一幅头骨的变形画，只有从特定角度观看才显得逼真。Example:  The funhouse mirror created an anamorphosis of my reflection, stretching and distorting my features.  游乐场的哈哈镜使我的影像变形，拉伸和扭曲了我的五官。 | 近义词: distortion, deformation | 反义词: conformity, regularity | 词性: noun
