haematosac: /ˌhiːmætəˈsæk/ /ˌhemætəˈsæk/ | n. |血囊，血袋|A sac containing blood.  It can refer to a blood-filled cyst or a part of an organism that stores blood.| The leech's haematosac was engorged after feeding. 水蛭的血囊在进食后膨胀了。|  The haematosac of the mosquito was filled with the host's blood. 蚊子的血囊充满了宿主的血液。| synonyms: blood sac, blood cyst | antonyms: N/A | noun
