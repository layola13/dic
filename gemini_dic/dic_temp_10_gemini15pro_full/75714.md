full-frontal:/ˌfʊlˈfrʌntəl/| adj.|全面的，正面的，毫无掩饰的|Characterized by complete exposure of the front of the body, especially the genitals; also, completely direct and explicit, not holding anything back.|The play featured full-frontal nudity, which shocked some audience members. 这部戏有正面全裸的镜头，让一些观众感到震惊。|近义词：explicit, graphic, unrestrained, barefaced|反义词：implicit, subtle, veiled, discreet|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"full" (Old English "full") + "frontal" (from Late Latin "frontalis," relating to the forehead, from Latin "frons," forehead).  The combined meaning developed in the 20th century, initially referring to literal nudity and later extending to metaphorical uses of complete openness or directness.
