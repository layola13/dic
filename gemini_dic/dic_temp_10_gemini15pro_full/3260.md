affirm:/əˈfɜːrm/ | n. | 确认，断言 | a statement asserting the existence or the truth of something |  The defendant's affirm was considered by the jury. 陪审团考虑了被告的断言。| synonyms: assertion, declaration, statement, affirmation, avowal, pronouncement, attestation, testimony | antonyms: denial, refutation, contradiction | noun
