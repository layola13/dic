lavenders:/ˈlævəndərz/| n.|薰衣草（复数）|A purple flower with a sweet smell, often used in perfumes and soaps.  The dried flowers and oil are also used in medicine.|The lavenders in the garden are in full bloom. 花园里的薰衣草盛开了。|Synonyms:  lavender plants, lavender blossoms|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

lavenders:/ˈlævəndərz/| v. (third-person singular simple present lavenders, present participle lavenderring, simple past and past participle lavendered) |用薰衣草熏香；使带有薰衣草香味 |To perfume or scent with lavender.|She lavendered the linens for a fresh, calming scent. 她用薰衣草熏香了亚麻布，带来清新、镇静的香味。|Synonyms: scent, perfume, aromatize|Antonyms: deodorize|Verb
