deploredly:/dɪˈplɔːrɪdli/
adverb
悲惨地，可悲地
in a way that shows strong disapproval and expresses regret
He shook his head deploredly. 他悲痛地摇了摇头。
近义词: lamentably, regrettably, sadly, unfortunately
反义词: fortunately, happily, luckily
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From  "deplore" + "-ed" + "-ly".  "Deplore" comes from the Old French "deplorer"  and ultimately from the Latin "dēplōrāre," meaning "to weep for," from "dē-" (down, completely) + "plōrāre" (to weep, cry out).
