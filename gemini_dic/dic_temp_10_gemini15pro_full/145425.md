overchargers:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑːrdʒərz/
|n.|过度充电器；超额收费者|A device or person that charges excessively.|The mechanic was an overcharger, adding unnecessary repairs to the bill.  这个机修工漫天要价，在账单上添加了不必要的维修费用。|Synonyms: profiteer, gouger|Antonyms: discounter|noun

overcharger:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑːrdʒər/
|n.|过充器|A device that charges a battery beyond its safe limit.|Leaving your phone plugged in overnight can sometimes lead to using an overcharger.  让你的手机整夜插着电有时会导致使用过充器。|Synonyms: none (specific to this meaning)|Antonyms: none (specific to this meaning)|noun
