digitalizing:/ˈdɪdʒɪtəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ |v. |数字化，将…数字化|The company is digitalizing all its records.  这家公司正在将其所有记录数字化。|Example: They are digitalizing old documents to preserve them. 他们正在将旧文件数字化以保存它们。 |Synonyms: digitizing, computerizing|Antonyms: analogizing|verb

digitalizing:/ˈdɪdʒɪtəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ |adj. |数字化的|The digitalizing process is almost complete. 数字化过程即将完成。|Example:  The library is undergoing a digitalizing transformation. 图书馆正在进行数字化转型。|Synonyms: digital, digitized|Antonyms: analog, non-digital|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From digital + -ize + -ing.  "Digital" ultimately derives from the Latin "digitus" meaning "finger" or "toe," referring to counting on one's fingers.  "-ize" is a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become." "-ing" is a suffix forming present participles and gerunds.
