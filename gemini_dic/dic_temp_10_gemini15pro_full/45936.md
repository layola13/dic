cuttystool:/ˈkʌtɪstuːl/
n.
矮凳；三脚凳
A low stool; a three-legged stool.
The old woman sat on her cuttystool by the fire. 老妇人坐在炉火旁的矮凳上。

近义词:stool, footstool, low stool
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps) high chair, armchair

词性:noun
