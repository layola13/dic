inspissation:/ɪnˌspɪˈseɪʃən/ | n. | 增稠，浓缩；加浓 | The inspissation of the syrup made it difficult to pour.  糖浆的浓缩使其难以倾倒。 |  thickening, condensation | dilution, thinning | noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *inspissationem, from Latin inspissare “to thicken,” from in- “in” + spissare “to thicken,” from spissus “thick.”
