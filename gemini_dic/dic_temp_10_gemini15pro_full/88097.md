hightailing:/ˈhaɪˌteɪlɪŋ/
verb
迅速离开；逃之夭夭
leaving somewhere quickly
The robbers hightailed it out of the bank as soon as the alarm went off.  银行的警报一响，劫匪就迅速逃离了。
After the prank went wrong, the kids hightailed it out of there.  恶作剧搞砸后，孩子们迅速逃离了那里。
近义词: fleeing, escaping, running away, bolting, making a hasty retreat, skedaddling
反义词: approaching, arriving, staying

词性: verb

词源:  From "high tail" (n.), referring to the raised tail of a fleeing animal, especially a deer.  来源于“high tail”（名词），指的是逃跑动物（尤其是鹿）翘起的尾巴。
