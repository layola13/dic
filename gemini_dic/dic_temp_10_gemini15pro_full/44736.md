crumbable:/ˈkrʌmbləbl/ | adj. | 易碎的，酥脆的 | Something that is crumbable is easily broken into small pieces, or likely to crumble.  |  The cake was so dry, it was incredibly crumbable.  这蛋糕太干了，非常容易碎。 | synonyms: brittle, friable, flaky | antonyms: tough, sturdy | adjective

crumbable:/ˈkrʌmbləbl/ | n. | 可碎的食物，通常指一种甜点 | A small, flat cake that is designed to be heated and then eaten warm, often served with custard.  |  I heated up a crumbable for dessert. 我加热了一个crumbable作为甜点。 | synonyms: crumble (as a noun referring to the dessert) | antonyms: N/A | noun
