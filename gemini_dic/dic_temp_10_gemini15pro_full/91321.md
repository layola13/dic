hydroperitoneum:/ˌhaɪdroʊˌpɛrɪtəˈniːəm/| n. |腹水|The accumulation of fluid in the peritoneal cavity, the space between the lining of the abdomen and the abdominal organs.|The doctor diagnosed the patient with hydroperitoneum. 医生诊断病人患有腹水。|Ascites, peritoneal effusion|  (No direct antonym exists, but a healthy state would imply the absence of fluid.)|noun
hydroperitoneum:/ˌhaɪdroʊˌpɛrɪtəˈniːəm/| n. |腹水|An abnormal accumulation of serous fluid in the peritoneal cavity.|Hydroperitoneum can be a sign of liver disease. 腹水可能是肝病的征兆。|Ascites, peritoneal fluid| Absence of peritoneal fluid|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hydro- + peritoneum.  "Hydro-" comes from Greek "hydōr" (water), and "peritoneum" comes from Greek "peritonaion" (stretched over),  from "peri-" (around) + "teinein" (to stretch).
