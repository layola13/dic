calamar:/kæləˈmɑːr/ | n. | 鱿鱼 | A squid, especially one served as food. |  例句：We had grilled calamari for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了烤鱿鱼。

例句：The calamari was tender and flavorful.  鱿鱼鲜嫩可口。

近义词：squid | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词) | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *calamar*, from Catalan *calamar*, from Late Latin *calamarium* (“ink pot, inkwell”), from Latin *calamus* (“reed, pen”).  So called from the ink sac of the squid.
