deeded:/ˈdiːdɪd/
adjective
已立契的，已转让的
legally transferred by deed; having been granted or conveyed by a deed.
The house was deeded to his son. 这房子已立契转让给了他的儿子。
近义词:granted, conveyed, transferred
反义词:undeeded, untransferred

deeded:/ˈdiːdɪd/
verb
立契转让，用契约授予（财产）
to transfer or convey (property) by deed.
They deeded the property to their children. 他们把财产立契转让给了他们的孩子。
近义词:grant, convey, transfer
反义词:retain, withhold
