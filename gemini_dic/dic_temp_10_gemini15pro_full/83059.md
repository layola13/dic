gurjun:/ˈɡɜːrdʒən/ | n. | 古芸香树，古芸香树脂 | A large East Indian tree (Dipterocarpus turbinatus) yielding a valuable timber and an oleo-resin. |  The gurjun tree is native to Southeast Asia. 古芸香树原产于东南亚。| synonym: keruing | antonym: N/A | noun

gurjun:/ˈɡɜːrdʒən/ | n. | 古芸香树脂 | An oleo-resin obtained from various dipterocarp trees, especially Dipterocarpus turbinatus, used chiefly in varnishes and paints. | Gurjun balsam is used in some traditional medicines. 古芸香树脂用于一些传统药物。| synonym: balsam | antonym: N/A | noun
