papillous: /pæˈpɪləs/ | adj. | 乳突状的；有小乳突的 | Covered with or resembling papillae (small, nipple-like projections). | The frog's skin was papillous, providing a textured surface.  青蛙的皮肤呈乳突状，表面很有质感。 | Example 2: The plant's leaves were papillous, giving them a rough feel.  这种植物的叶子有乳突，摸起来很粗糙。 | Synonyms: bumpy, textured, granular, warty | Antonyms: smooth, flat | Word type: adjective

papilla: /pəˈpɪlə/ | n. | 乳突 | A small, nipple-like projection or elevation. | The papillae on the tongue contain taste buds. 舌头上的乳突包含味蕾。 | Example 2: The flower's petals were covered in tiny papillae.  这朵花的花瓣上覆盖着微小的乳突。| Synonyms:  projection, protuberance, nipple | Antonyms: depression, hollow | Word type: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *papilla, diminutive of papa "nipple, teat".
