drumbeat:/ˈdrʌmˌbiːt/| n.|鼓声；节奏；强烈要求|A persistent, rhythmic sound, like that of a drum; a monotonous, insistent demand or theme.|The drumbeat of the protesters grew louder as they approached the capitol. 抗议者的鼓声随着他们接近国会大厦而变得越来越响亮。|Synonyms: rhythm, pulse, cadence, thump, beat|Antonyms: silence, quiet, stillness|noun

drumbeat:/ˈdrʌmˌbiːt/| n.|（比喻）不断重复出现的事物；（某种观点或要求的）持续宣传|A theme, idea, or demand that is repeated so often that it becomes insistent.|The media's constant drumbeat about the economy is creating anxiety. 媒体不断重复的经济报道正在制造焦虑。|Synonyms: repetition, refrain, mantra|Antonyms: variation, diversity|noun
