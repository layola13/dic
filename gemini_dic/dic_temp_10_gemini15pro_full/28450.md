carbunculous:/kɑːrˈbʌŋkjʊləs/
adjective
痈样的；长痈的；似红玉的；似红榴石的
Resembling or afflicted with carbuncles; inflamed and painful. Also, resembling a carbuncle (gemstone) in color or brilliance.
The old man's neck was covered in carbunculous sores. 老人的脖子上长满了疖子。
The setting sun cast a carbunculous glow over the mountains. 落日给群山披上了一层红宝石般的光芒。
synonyms: inflamed, infected, festering, fiery, ruby-like
antonyms: healthy, clear, unblemished, pale
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *carbunculus, diminutive of Latin carbunculus (“small coal, carbuncle”), from carbo (“coal”).
