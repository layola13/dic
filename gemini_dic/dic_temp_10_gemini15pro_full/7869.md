antecedently:/ˌæntɪˈsiːdəntli/| adv.|先前地，预先地|Previously; beforehand.  It refers to something happening earlier in time or logically prior to something else. |例句：He had antecedently agreed to the terms. 他先前同意了这些条款。

例句：The issues were antecedently discussed before the meeting. 这些问题在会议前已预先讨论过。|近义词：previously, beforehand, earlier, prior, formerly|反义词：subsequently, afterward, later|词性:副词

词源: From Late Latin *antecedentem (nominative antecedens), present participle of antecedere "to go before," from ante "before" + cedere "to go."
