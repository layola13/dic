brander:/ˈbrændər/| n.|烙印者，打烙印的人；污蔑者|Someone who brands something, especially cattle or a product.  Figuratively, it can also refer to someone who labels another person with a negative term.|The cattle brander marked each calf with the ranch's symbol.  牧场主用牧场的标志给每头小牛打上烙印。 The politician's opponent branded him a liar.  这位政客的对手给他贴上了骗子的标签。|近义词：marker, labeler, stigmatizer|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

brander:/ˈbrændər/| n.|烙铁|A tool used for branding, especially cattle.|The brander was heated in the fire before being applied to the animal.  烙铁在使用前在火中加热。|近义词：iron|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源: From the Old Norse brandr, meaning "firebrand" or "a piece of burning wood."  This evolved to refer to the mark made by such a brand, and eventually to the act of marking and the tools used for it.  The figurative meaning of "stigmatizing" or "labeling" developed later.
