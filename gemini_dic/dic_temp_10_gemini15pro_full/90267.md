household:/ˈhaʊshəʊld/ (UK) /ˈhaʊshoʊld/ (US)| n. adj.|家庭，户；家喻户晓的|A household is all the people who live together in a single house.  A household appliance is one that is used in the home.|例句：Most households now own a car.  现在大多数家庭都拥有一辆汽车。|近义词：family, home, domestic|反义词:non-domestic, industrial|n.

household:/ˈhaʊshəʊld/ (UK) /ˈhaʊshoʊld/ (US)| adj.|家喻户晓的，家庭的|Relating to a house and the people who live in it.|例句：Household chores can be time-consuming. 家务琐事很耗时。|近义词：domestic, family|反义词:non-domestic, industrial|adj.
