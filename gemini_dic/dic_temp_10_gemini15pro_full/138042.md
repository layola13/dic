The plural form of nordicity, referring to the multiple characteristics or expressions of being Nordic.  It encompasses various aspects like social values, cultural practices, geographical features, and political systems found in Nordic countries.
The conference explored the diverse nordicities of the Scandinavian region, highlighting both shared traditions and unique national identities.
