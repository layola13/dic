nonpedagogic:/ˌnɒnpɛdəˈɡɒdʒɪk/ | adj. | 非教学的，非教育学的 | Not related to teaching or education; not pedagogical. |  This book takes a nonpedagogic approach to explaining quantum physics, focusing on the wonders rather than the formulas. 这本书采用非教学的方式来解释量子物理学，侧重于奇观而不是公式。

The museum's nonpedagogic exhibit simply presented the artifacts without much explanation.  博物馆的非教学性展览只是简单地展示了这些文物，没有太多解释。 | 近义词：non-educational, unacademic, informal | 反义词：pedagogic, educational, didactic | 词性：形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Latin, meaning "not".
pedagogic (adjective): From Greek "paidagogikos" meaning "of a slave who escorted children to school", later "pertaining to teaching".
