aceric: /əˈsɛrɪk/
adjective
含槭树的; 槭树属的
Relating to or characteristic of maple trees.
The aceric acid found in maple syrup gives it its distinctive flavor.  枫糖浆中发现的槭树酸赋予了它独特的风味。
