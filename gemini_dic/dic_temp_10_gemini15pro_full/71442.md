flambeing:/flɑːmˈbeɪɪŋ/ | n. | 火焰烹制; 燃燒 | The act of igniting liquor poured over food as a culinary technique. | The chef finished the dish with a dramatic flambéing of brandy.  厨师用白兰地 flambéing，戏剧性地完成了这道菜。 |  Synonyms: flaming, igniting | Antonyms: extinguishing, dousing | noun

flambeing:/flɑːmˈbeɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 燃烧的 |  Burning, usually used to describe food being prepared with flaming liquor. |  The flambéing bananas were a spectacular end to the meal. 燃烧的香蕉为这顿饭画上了一个壮观的句号。 | Synonyms: blazing, flaming | Antonyms: extinguished, unlit | adjective
