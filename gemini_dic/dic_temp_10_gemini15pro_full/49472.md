deluginous:/dɛˈluːdʒɪnəs/
adjective
欺骗性的；迷惑性的；虚假的
Deceptive; misleading; giving a false impression. Appearing to be one thing when it is really another.
The deluginous advertisement promised a miracle cure, but the product was worthless.  这则欺骗性的广告承诺了一种神奇的疗法，但该产品毫无价值。
The politician's deluginous promises ultimately led to his downfall.  这位政客的虚假承诺最终导致了他的垮台。
近义词:deceptive, misleading, fallacious, spurious, fraudulent
反义词:genuine, authentic, real, true, honest
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *dēlūdō, from dē- + lūdō (“mock, play”)
