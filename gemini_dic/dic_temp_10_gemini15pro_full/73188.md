forbruise: /fɔːrˈbruːz/
verb
使出现青肿；挫伤
to injure the surface of something, especially fruit, and cause a soft brown area to appear
The apples were forbruised in the fall. 这些苹果在秋天摔伤了，出现了青肿。
近义词: bruise, contuse
反义词: heal
