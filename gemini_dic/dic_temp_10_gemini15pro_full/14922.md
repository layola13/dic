azonic:/eɪˈzɒnɪk/ | adj. | 非地带性的 | Not controlled by climate zones or altitude. Relating to factors other than climate and altitude that influence the distribution of organisms. |  Azonic soils are influenced more by parent material than by climate. 非地带性土壤受母质的影响大于气候的影响。 |  The azonic distribution of certain plants is determined by soil type. 某些植物的非地带性分布是由土壤类型决定的。 |  non-zonal | zonal | adjective
