famines:/ˈfæmɪnz/
noun
饥荒（复数）
a widespread scarcity of food, caused by several factors including war, inflation, crop failure, population imbalance, or government policies.
The region has suffered several famines in the last century.  该地区在上个世纪遭受了几次饥荒。
The Irish potato famine caused widespread suffering and emigration. 爱尔兰马铃薯饥荒造成了广泛的苦难和移民。
synonyms: starvation, hunger, scarcity, dearth
antonyms: abundance, plenty, surplus
