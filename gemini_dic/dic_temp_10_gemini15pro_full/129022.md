nissai:/nɪˈsaɪ/ | n. | 二斋市; 二斋市场 | A biannual fair traditionally held in Japan, often associated with temples or shrines. |  Example: We bought some beautiful pottery at the nissai. 我们在二斋市买了一些漂亮的陶器。| Synonym: fair, market | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "permanent store") | noun

nissai:/nɪˈsaɪ/ | n. | 二斋 |  Refers to the two Buddhist vegetarian days each month, traditionally the 8th and 23rd.  | Example:  We observe nissai by not eating meat. 我们通过不吃肉来遵守二斋。| Synonym: vegetarian days, fasting days | Antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun
