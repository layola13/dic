fallacy:/ˈfæləsi/| n. |谬误，谬论|A mistaken belief, especially one based on unsound argument. |例句：It's a common fallacy that money brings happiness.  金钱能带来幸福是一种常见的谬误。

例句：The argument is based on a logical fallacy. 这个论点是基于一个逻辑谬误。|近义词：error, misconception, delusion, myth |反义词:truth, fact, reality |noun
