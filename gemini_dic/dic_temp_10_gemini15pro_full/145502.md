overcloy:/ˌoʊvərˈklɔɪ/
verb
使过饱；使腻烦；使过度
To supply or fill to excess, causing disgust or satiety.
The sweetness of the dessert overcloyed after a few bites.  几口之后，甜点的甜味就让人腻了。
The constant praise overcloyed and began to feel insincere.  不断的赞扬让人感到厌烦，并开始觉得不真诚。
近义词: satiate, surfeit, glut
反义词: stimulate, tantalize
词性: verb


overcloy:/ˌoʊvərˈklɔɪ/
adjective
过饱的；腻烦的；过度的 (Rarely used as an adjective)
Excessively sweet, rich, or sentimental to the point of causing disgust.
The overcloy sweetness of the perfume gave her a headache.  香水过于甜腻的味道让她头疼。(This usage is less common.)
近义词: cloying, saccharine, treacly
反义词: refreshing, tart, piquant
词性: adjective (rare)
