overgenerously: /ˌoʊvərˈdʒɛnərəsli/
| adv. | 过于慷慨地；过分大方地 |  In a way that is too generous; excessively generously.  It combines "over" (meaning excessively) with "generously" (meaning in a kind and giving way). | He tipped the waiter overgenerously. 他给服务员的小费过于慷慨了。|Synonyms: excessively generously, too liberally, overly kind,  | Antonyms: stingily, meanly, parsimoniously | adverb
