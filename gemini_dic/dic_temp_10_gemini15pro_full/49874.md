demotivators:/diːˈməʊtɪveɪtəz/
|n.|打击积极性的事物；消极因素|Something that reduces motivation or enthusiasm.|The constant criticism from my boss was a real demotivator.|Synonyms: disincentive, deterrent, dampener|Antonyms: motivator, incentive, encouragement|noun


demotivator:/diːˈməʊtɪveɪtər/
|n.|打击积极性的人；泼冷水的人|A person who reduces motivation or enthusiasm.|He's such a demotivator; he always points out the negative aspects of any situation.|Synonyms: discourager, pessimist, cynic|Antonyms: motivator, optimist, enthusiast|noun
