epitomizers:/ɪˈpɪtəˌmaɪzərz/ | n. | 典型，象征；化身 |  People who embody or represent the essential characteristics of something.  Those who perfectly illustrate a quality, idea, or type. |  The new recruits were epitomizers of youthful enthusiasm. 这些新兵是青春热情的化身。 | The award winners are epitomizers of dedication and hard work. 这些获奖者是奉献精神和辛勤工作的典范。| synonyms: embodiments, personifications, exemplars, representatives | antonyms: deviations, anomalies, exceptions | noun
