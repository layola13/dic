man-eaters:/ˈmænˌiːtərz/
|n.|食人者；吃人动物（或植物）；危险的东西（或情况）|A creature or plant that eats humans; something that is dangerous or destructive.|例句：The villagers feared the man-eaters in the jungle. 村民们害怕丛林里的食人动物。|近义词：cannibal, predator, hazard, danger|反义词:prey|词性:名词
