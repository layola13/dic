muskar:/ˈmʊskɑːr/ |n.|麝香|A reddish-brown, unctuous substance with a penetrating and persistent odor, obtained from a gland in the abdomen of the male musk deer and used in perfumery.|The perfume had a strong scent of muskar. 这款香水有浓烈的麝香味。|Synonyms: musk, civet|Antonyms: None|Noun
