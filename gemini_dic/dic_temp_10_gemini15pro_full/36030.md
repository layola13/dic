codirectors:/ˌkoʊ daɪˈrɛktərz/ | n. |共同导演，联合导演| Two or more people who share the responsibility of directing a film, play, or other creative project.  They collaborate on all major artistic decisions. | The codirectors had a clear vision for the film. 这两位联合导演对这部电影有着清晰的愿景。

The codirectors disagreed on the casting of the lead role.  两位联合导演在主角的选角上意见不一致。 |Near synonyms: joint directors, co-leaders | Antonyms: sole director | Noun
