neuterers:/ˈnjuːtərərz/
| n. |绝育者，阉割者 |People who neuter animals, especially pets.  Those who perform the act of neutering. | Many responsible pet owners take their cats and dogs to neuterers to help control the pet population. 许多负责任的宠物主人会把他们的猫和狗带到绝育者那里，以帮助控制宠物数量。| Synonyms: castrators, spayers (for females) | Antonyms: breeders | Noun
