coiffure:/kwɑːˈfjʊər/ | n. |发型，头饰；理发；美发业| A style of arranging the hair, typically a woman's; the profession or business of hairdressing. |  She had an elaborate coiffure for the wedding. 她为婚礼做了一个精致的发型。| synonyms: hairstyle, hairdo, haircut | antonyms: unkempt hair, mess | noun

coiffure:/kwɑːˈfjʊər/ | v. |给…做头发；梳理| To arrange or style someone's hair.  | The stylist coiffured her hair into an elegant updo.  造型师把她的头发盘成一个优雅的发髻。 | synonyms: style, dress, arrange | antonyms: dishevel, mess up | verb
