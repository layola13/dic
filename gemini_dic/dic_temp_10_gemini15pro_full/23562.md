brainstormings:/ˈbreɪnˌstɔːrmɪŋz/
noun
头脑风暴，集思广益
The act or process of suggesting many ideas for a project, problem, etc., in a short period of time.
We had a brainstorming session to come up with new marketing ideas. 我们进行了一次头脑风暴会议，提出了新的营销方案。
The team held several brainstormings to develop a new product.  团队举行了几次头脑风暴会议来开发新产品。
synonyms: idea generation, spitballing, collaborative ideation
antonyms: analysis, evaluation, criticism
n.
