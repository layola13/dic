ambidextrous:/ˌæmbɪˈdɛkstrəs/| adj.| 双手灵巧的；怀有二心的 | Able to use both hands equally well; very skillful; deceitful, double-dealing. | She's an ambidextrous tennis player, equally comfortable hitting forehand and backhand shots. 她是一位双手都灵巧的网球运动员，正反手击球都同样自如。|  He’s known for his ambidextrous political maneuvering. 他以其左右逢源的政治手腕而闻名。 | synonyms: versatile, adaptable, two-handed, deceitful, duplicitous | antonyms: clumsy, awkward, inept, honest, straightforward | adjective

ambidexterity:/ˌæmbɪdɛkˈstɛrɪti/| n.|双手灵巧；怀有二心 | The state of being able to use both hands equally well; skillfulness; deceitfulness. |  Her ambidexterity is a great advantage in playing musical instruments. 她的双手灵巧对演奏乐器来说是一大优势。 | His political ambidexterity allowed him to maintain power for years.  他的政治手腕使他得以维持多年的权力。| synonyms: versatility, adaptability, two-handedness, deceit, duplicity | antonyms: clumsiness, awkwardness, ineptitude, honesty, straightforwardness | noun
