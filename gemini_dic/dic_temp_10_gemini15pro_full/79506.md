globefish: /ˈɡloubˌfɪʃ/ | n. | 河豚；鲀鱼 |  A fish known for its ability to inflate its body with water or air as a defense mechanism. Some species are considered a delicacy but require careful preparation due to the presence of tetrodotoxin, a potent neurotoxin. |  The chef carefully prepared the globefish, removing the toxic parts.  厨师小心翼翼地处理河豚，去除有毒的部分。 | synonyms: fugu (Japanese), tetraodontidae | antonyms: N/A | noun
