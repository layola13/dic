crap-up:/kræp ˈʌp/ | vi. |  (突然) 失败，垮掉 | To suddenly and completely fail. | The engine crapped up just as we were about to take off.  就在我们要起飞的时候，引擎突然坏了。| Synonyms: fail, collapse, break down | Antonyms: succeed, triumph | verb
