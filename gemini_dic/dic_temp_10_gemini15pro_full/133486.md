nonfeloniousnesses:/ˌnɑːnˌfɛˈloʊniəsnɪsɪz/
n.
无罪; 非重罪行为
The state of being characterized by the absence of felonies.
The lawyer argued the nonfeloniousnesses of his client's actions.  律师辩称其当事人的行为无罪。
His record showed a history of nonfeloniousnesses. 他的记录显示他一贯没有犯过重罪。
近义词: innocence, lawfulness, legality
反义词: criminality, guilt, feloniousness
词性: noun


Etymology:
From non- (not) + felonious (pertaining to a felony) + -ness (state or quality of being).  Ultimately from Latin "felonia" (treachery).
