enthusiastically:/ɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪkli/ | adv. | 热情地，热烈地 | In a manner showing intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval. | She greeted him enthusiastically. 她热情地跟他打招呼。|  The team enthusiastically accepted the challenge.  团队热情地接受了挑战。| 近义词: eagerly, ardently, passionately, fervently | 反义词: apathetically, indifferently, halfheartedly | 词性: 副词

词源: From the Late Latin *enthusiastice, from the Greek enthousiastikos, from enthousiasmos "divine inspiration," from enthousiazein "be inspired by a god," from en- "in" + theos "god."
