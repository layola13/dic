metrology:/mɪˈtrɒlədʒi/ | n. |计量学；度量衡学 | The science of measurement, embracing both experimental and theoretical determinations at any level of uncertainty in any field of science and technology. |  Metrology plays a crucial role in ensuring the accuracy of scientific experiments. 计量学在保证科学实验的准确性方面起着至关重要的作用。

The International Bureau of Weights and Measures (BIPM) is an intergovernmental organization that plays a key role in metrology. 国际计量局 (BIPM) 是一个在计量学中发挥关键作用的政府间组织。| 近义词: measurement science | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *métrologie*, from Ancient Greek μέτρον (*métron*, “measure”) + -λογία (*-logía*, “study of”).
