elocutionists: /ˌɛləˈkjuːʃənɪsts/
noun
演说家；雄辩家；朗诵家
A person who is skilled in the art of public speaking, especially one who delivers speeches with expressive delivery and clear articulation.
The elocutionists captivated the audience with their powerful speeches.  演说家们用他们强有力的演讲吸引了观众。
Experienced elocutionists know how to modulate their voices for dramatic effect.  经验丰富的演说家知道如何调节他们的声音以达到戏剧性的效果。
Synonym: orators, speakers, rhetoricians
Antonym: mumblers, stammerers
词性: noun
