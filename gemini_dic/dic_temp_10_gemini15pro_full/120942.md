miscellany:/ˌmɪsəˈleɪni/ | n. | 杂项；混合物；杂集 | A collection of different items, especially writings or literary works on various subjects. |  The book is a miscellany of poems, short stories, and essays. 这本书是诗歌、短篇小说和散文的杂集。| synonyms: assortment, mixture, collection, anthology, medley | antonyms: uniformity, homogeneity | noun

miscellany:/ˌmɪsəˈleɪni/ | adj. | 各种各样的；混杂的 | (Less common usage) Consisting of diverse things or elements. |  The museum houses a miscellany collection of artifacts.  这家博物馆收藏了各种各样的文物。 | synonyms: diverse, varied, assorted, mixed | antonyms: uniform, homogeneous | adjective
