monoculture:/ˈmɒnəʊkʌltʃə(r)/ (US) /ˈmɑːnəʊkʌltʃər/| n. |单一栽培，单一文化|The practice of growing only one crop or keeping only one type of livestock on a farm or in a region or country.  It can also refer to a lack of diversity in a society or culture.|例句：The reliance on monoculture farming can deplete soil nutrients.  依赖单一作物种植会耗尽土壤养分。

例句：The monoculture of ideas in the company stifled innovation. 公司思想的单一化扼杀了创新。|近义词：homogeneity, uniformity|反义词: polyculture, diversity|词性:noun
