corradiate:/kəˈreɪdieɪt/
verb
共同辐射，共射，从同一点发出
emitting from a common point; converging to one point.
The spokes of a bicycle wheel corradiate from the hub. 自行车轮的辐条从轮毂共同辐射出来。
The light rays corradiate from the bulb. 光线从灯泡辐射出来。
converge, meet, intersect
diverge
