chawdron:/ˈtʃɔːdrən/| n.|大锅，大釜|A large metal pot with a lid and handle, used for cooking over an open fire.  一个带盖和手柄的大金属锅，用于在明火上烹饪。|The witch stirred a bubbling potion in her chawdron. 女巫在她的大锅里搅拌着冒泡的药水。|近义词：cauldron, kettle, pot|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Middle English *chaudron*, from Old North French *chaudron*, from Late Latin *calderonem*, accusative of *caldero*, diminutive of Latin *caldarium* (“hot bath”), from *caldus* (“warm, hot”).  Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *kel-* (“warm”). 源于中古英语 *chaudron*，来自古北法语 *chaudron*，来自晚期拉丁语 *calderonem*，是拉丁语 *caldero* 的宾格，*caldero* 是拉丁语 *caldarium*（“热水澡”）的指小词，来自 *caldus*（“温暖的，热的”）。最终来自原始印欧语词根 *kel-*（“温暖的”）。
