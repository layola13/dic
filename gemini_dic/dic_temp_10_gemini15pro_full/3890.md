agreeably:/əˈɡriːəbli/ | adv. | 欣然地，愉快地，适宜地 | In a pleasant or pleasing way; in a way that is acceptable or satisfactory. | The evening passed agreeably.  晚上过得很愉快。 |  The temperature was agreeably cool. 气温凉爽宜人。| 近义词：pleasantly, nicely, amiably, genially | 反义词：disagreeably, unpleasantly, objectionably | 词性: 副词

词源:  From Middle French *agreablement, from agreeable + -ment. Ultimately from Latin agrēre (to please).
