howbeit:/ˌhaʊˈbiːɪt/| conj.|尽管，虽然；然而|Although; nevertheless.|He was a bit of a rogue, howbeit a charming one. 他有点流氓，尽管很有魅力。|近义词:although, nevertheless, notwithstanding, yet|反义词:therefore, consequently, thus|词性:连词

词源: From Middle English *hou be it*, literally “how be it”.  A combination of *how* + *be* (subjunctive form of the verb *to be*) + *it*.

注意事项:  Howbeit is an archaic word and rarely used in modern English.  Using it can sound overly formal or affected.  Sticking to synonyms like "although," "nevertheless," or "however" is generally recommended.
