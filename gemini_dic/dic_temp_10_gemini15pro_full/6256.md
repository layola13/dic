amoebicide:/əˈmiːbɪsaɪd/| n.|阿米巴杀虫剂；抗阿米巴药|A substance that kills amoebas (single-celled organisms).|The doctor prescribed an amoebicide to treat the patient's infection. 医生开了阿米巴杀虫剂来治疗病人的感染。|The amoebicide effectively eliminated the parasitic amoebas.  这种抗阿米巴药物有效地清除了寄生阿米巴。|近义词：antiamoebic|反义词:|n.
