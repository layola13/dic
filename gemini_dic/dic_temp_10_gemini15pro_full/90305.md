houseroom:/ˈhaʊsruːm/ (US), /ˈhaʊsruːm/ (UK)| n.|空间；地方；容纳能力|Space inside a house or building, or the amount of such space available.|例句：This apartment has plenty of houseroom for a family of four. 这套公寓有足够的空间容纳一个四口之家。

例句：We don't have much houseroom, so we can't take in any more furniture.  我们的房子空间不大，所以不能再放家具了。|近义词：space, room, accommodation, capacity|反义词: confinement, restriction|词性:名词
