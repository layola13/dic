dromedary:/ˈdrɒmədɛri/ (UK) /ˈdrɑːmədɛri/ (US)| n. |单峰骆驼|A large, fast-running camel of Africa and western Asia, having one hump on its back. |例句：The dromedary is well adapted to desert life. 单峰骆驼非常适应沙漠生活。

例句：The caravan of dromedaries crossed the Sahara Desert.  单峰骆驼商队穿越了撒哈拉沙漠。

近义词：camel|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps Bactrian camel (双峰骆驼) for contrast)|词性: noun
