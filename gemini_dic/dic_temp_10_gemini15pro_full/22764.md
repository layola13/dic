borrows:/ˈbɒrəʊz/ (UK), /ˈbɑːroʊz/ (US)| v.|借入，借用|Third-person singular present tense form of the verb "borrow". It means to take and use something belonging to someone else with the intention of returning it.|He borrows books from the library every week. 他每周都从图书馆借书。|She borrows money from her parents to pay for college. 她向父母借钱支付大学学费。|synonyms: takes, uses (temporarily), obtains on loan|antonyms: lends, gives, donates|verb
