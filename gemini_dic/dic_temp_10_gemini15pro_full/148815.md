overventilates:/ˌoʊvərˈvɛntɪˌleɪts/| v.|过度换气，呼吸过度|To breathe at an abnormally rapid rate, so increasing the rate of loss of carbon dioxide.  This can lead to dizziness and fainting.|She was so anxious she started overventilating.|Synonyms: hyperventilate, breathe too fast|Antonyms: hypoventilate, breathe too slowly|verb
