curlicued: /ˈkɜːrlɪˌkjuːd/ (US) /ˈkɜːlɪˌkjuːd/ (UK)| adj.|卷曲的，花体的|Having curls or scrolls; elaborately ornamented.|Her handwriting was elegant and curlicued. 她的字迹优雅而花哨。|近义词：curly, scrolled, ornate, flamboyant|反义词:straight, plain, simple|词性:形容词
Etymology: From curl + -icue + -ed.  "Curl" comes from Middle English *curle, from Old North French curle, from Vulgar Latin *curlare, of imitative origin. "-icue" is a diminutive suffix. "-ed" is a past participle ending that also forms adjectives.
