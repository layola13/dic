nonfiduciaries: /ˌnɒn.fɪˈd(j)uː.ʃi.ɛr.iz/ | n. | 非信托人；非受托人 |  Persons or entities that do not have a legal or ethical obligation to act in the best interests of another party. They are not bound by fiduciary duties such as loyalty, care, and good faith. | The investment firm acted as nonfiduciaries in this transaction, prioritizing their own profits. 这家投资公司在这笔交易中表现得像非信托人，优先考虑自己的利润。

The financial advisors, acting as nonfiduciaries, recommended investments that earned them high commissions but offered little benefit to their clients.  这些财务顾问以非信托人的身份行事，推荐了一些能让他们获得高佣金但对客户却没什么好处的投资。| Near synonyms: non-trustees | Antonyms: fiduciaries | Noun (plural)
