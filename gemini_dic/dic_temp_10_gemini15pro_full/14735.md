awe-inspiringly:/ˈɔːɪnspaɪərɪŋli/
adverb
令人敬畏地；令人惊叹地
in a way that inspires awe; in a way that fills you with respect mixed with fear and wonder.
The Grand Canyon is awe-inspiringly vast.  大峡谷广阔无垠，令人敬畏。
The aurora borealis danced awe-inspiringly across the night sky.  北极光在夜空中神奇地舞动，令人叹为观止。
近义词: magnificently, breathtakingly, stunningly
反义词: underwhelmingly, disappointingly
词性: adverb

awe-inspiring:/ˈɔːɪnspaɪərɪŋ/
adjective
令人敬畏的；令人惊叹的
causing a feeling of awe; filling you with respect mixed with fear and wonder.
The sheer size of the universe is awe-inspiring. 宇宙的绝对大小令人敬畏。
The view from the mountaintop was awe-inspiring. 从山顶看到的景色令人叹为观止。
近义词: impressive, majestic, grand
反义词: unimpressive, ordinary
词性: adjective

awe:/ɔː/
noun
敬畏；惊叹
a feeling of reverential respect mixed with fear or wonder.
She gazed at the star-filled sky with a sense of awe. 她敬畏地凝视着繁星点点的夜空。
The children were filled with awe when they met the astronaut. 孩子们见到宇航员时充满了敬畏之情。
近义词: wonder, amazement, reverence
反义词: disrespect, contempt
词性: noun

词源: From Old Norse *agi, meaning "fear, terror." The word evolved over time to include a sense of wonder and reverence.
