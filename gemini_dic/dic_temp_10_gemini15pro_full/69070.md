faultlessly:/ˈfɔːltləsli/| adv.|完美地，无瑕地|Without any faults or mistakes; perfectly.|She played the piece faultlessly. 她完美地演奏了这首曲子。|近义词：perfectly, impeccably, flawlessly|反义词：imperfectly, defectively|词性:副词
Etymology: From "faultless" + "-ly". "Faultless" comes from "fault" (from Old French "falte," meaning "lack, default, want") + "-less" (meaning "without").
