in-the-hopper:/ɪn ðə ˈhɑːpər/ |adj.|在进行中，正在处理中|Being processed or dealt with; under consideration.|The proposal is in the hopper and should be decided on soon.  这项提议正在审议中，应该很快就会有决定。|Synonyms: underway, in progress, pending|Antonyms: stalled, delayed, abandoned|adjective

in-the-hopper:/ɪn ðə ˈhɑːpər/ |n.|料斗|A funnel-shaped receptacle for grain or other material, especially one from which something is fed into a mill or other machine.|There's a lot of grain in the hopper, ready for milling. 料斗里有很多谷物，准备磨粉。|Synonyms: container, receptacle, bin|Antonyms: N/A|noun
