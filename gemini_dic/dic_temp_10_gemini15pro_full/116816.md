maxer:/ˈmæksər/| n.|最大化者；追求极限者|Someone who strives to maximize something, often in the context of gaming or fitness.  A person who tries to achieve the highest possible level or score in a game or activity, or to develop their physical abilities to the fullest extent. |He's a real maxer, always trying to get the best gear and stats in the game.|近义词: optimizer, maximizer, grinder|反义词: minimizer|noun
