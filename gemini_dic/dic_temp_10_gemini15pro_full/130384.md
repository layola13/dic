non-vascular:/ˌnɒnˈvæskjʊlə(r)/
adjective
非维管的
Lacking a vascular system; not containing vessels that conduct fluids such as blood or sap.
Mosses are non-vascular plants.  苔藓是非维管植物。
Non-vascular plants rely on diffusion for water transport. 非维管植物依靠扩散来输送水分。
synonyms: avascular
antonyms: vascular
词性: 形容词
