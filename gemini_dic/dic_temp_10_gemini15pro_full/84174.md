half-protested:/ˌhæf prəˈtɛstɪd/
| adj. | 半抗议的；勉强抗议的 | Expressing or characterized by a protest that is hesitant, weak, or incomplete.  It suggests a protest that is not fully voiced or committed, perhaps due to fear, uncertainty, or a desire to avoid conflict. | She gave a half-protested sigh as he pulled her onto the dance floor. 她被他拉到舞池时，勉强地叹了口气。 | Near synonyms: half-hearted, reluctant, hesitant, unenthusiastic | Antonyms:  enthusiastic, wholehearted, eager, willing | adjective
