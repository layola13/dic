miscue: /ˌmɪsˈkjuː/
n.
失误，错误
an unintentional mistake or error
The quarterback's miscue cost the team the game.  四分卫的失误使球队输掉了比赛。

miscue: /ˌmɪsˈkjuː/
v.
弄错，失误
to make a mistake
He miscued his shot and the ball went wide. 他击球失误，球偏出了。


近义词: error, mistake, blunder, slip, gaffe
反义词: success, triumph, perfection
词性: noun, verb


词源: From mis- + cue.  由mis-（错误）+ cue（提示）组成。
