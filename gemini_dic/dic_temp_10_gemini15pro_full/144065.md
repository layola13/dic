outrogued:/ˌaʊtˈroʊɡd/
verb
胜过，以流氓手段胜过
surpassed in roguery; outdone in cunning or trickery.
He outrogued his opponents in the business deal. 他在商业交易中以流氓手段胜过了他的对手。
Synonyms: outwitted, outmaneuvered, outsmarted
Antonyms: outsmarted, outwitted, defeated
词性: verb
