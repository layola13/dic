noblest:/ˈnoʊblɪst/| adj.|最高贵的；最优秀的；最崇高的|Having or showing the highest moral qualities or principles; aristocratic.|He made the noblest sacrifice for his country. 他为了国家做出了最崇高的牺牲。|Synonyms: grandest, worthiest, greatest, finest, most honorable|Antonyms: basest, meanest, lowest, worst|adjective

noble:/ˈnoʊbl/| adj. n.|高贵的；贵族的；惰性的；|adj. Having or showing fine personal qualities or moral principles; belonging to a hereditary class with high social or political status; (of a metal) not easily oxidized, especially gold and silver.  n. A person of noble rank or birth.|She came from a noble family. 她出身名门。|Synonyms: aristocratic, high-ranking, dignified, honorable, virtuous|Antonyms: ignoble, base, common, lowborn, dishonorable|adjective, noun
