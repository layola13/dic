lutulent:/ˈluːtjʊlənt/ | adj. |浑浊的；污秽的；含糊不清的 | Muddy; turbid; thick or clouded.  Also, figuratively, unclear or muddled. | The river, swollen with rain, was a lutulent brown. 雨水上涨的河水呈浑浊的棕色。| synonyms: murky, turbid, cloudy, muddy, opaque, unclear, muddled | antonyms: clear, transparent, limpid, pellucid | adjective
