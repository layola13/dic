answerableness:/ˈɑːnsərəbəlnəs/| n. |可答复性，责任，义务|The answerableness of a government to its people is a cornerstone of democracy. 政府对其人民的责任是民主的基石。|English Explanation: The quality or state of being answerable; accountability; responsibility.  It implies being liable to be called to account, or to answer for one's conduct. |例句：The manager stressed the answerableness of each team member for their assigned tasks.  经理强调每个团队成员对其分配任务的责任。

例句：Her sense of answerableness to her constituents was paramount. 她对选民的责任感至关重要。|近义词：accountability, responsibility, liability|反义词: irresponsibility, unaccountability|词性:noun
