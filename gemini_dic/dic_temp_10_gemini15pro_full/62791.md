engrosser:/ɪnˈɡrəʊsər/ |n.|  囤居者；垄断者； engrossed的施动者 |A person who buys large quantities of goods, especially food, in order to control the market and raise prices.  |  The engrossers were punished for hoarding grain during the famine.  囤积居奇者在饥荒期间因囤积粮食而受到惩罚。 | synonyms: hoarder, monopolist, profiteer | antonyms: distributor, supplier | noun

engrossers:/ɪnˈɡrəʊsərz/ |n.| 囤居者；垄断者；engrossed的施动者的复数形式 | The plural form of "engrosser," referring to multiple individuals who buy large quantities of goods to control the market and inflate prices. | The engrossers manipulated the market, driving up the cost of essential commodities. 这些囤积者操纵市场，抬高了必需商品的价格。| synonyms: hoarders, monopolists, profiteers | antonyms: distributors, suppliers | noun
