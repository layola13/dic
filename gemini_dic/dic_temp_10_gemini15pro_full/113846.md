adjective | 使充满气体；使胀气 |  Caused to become filled with gas; made flatulent. |  The beans made me feel madegassy.  豆子让我感觉胀气。 | 近义词: bloated, flatulent | 反义词: deflated | 词性: adjective

词源:  "Made" (past participle of "make") + "gassy" (adjective).  "Make" comes from Old English "macian," meaning "to make, form, build." "Gassy" comes from "gas," which ultimately derives from the Greek word "khaos," meaning "empty space."
