nihilist: /ˈnaɪəlist/ (US), /ˈnɪhɪlɪst/ (UK)| n. |虚无主义者|A person who believes that life is meaningless and rejects all religious and moral principles.| He considered himself a nihilist, believing that nothing had any inherent value. 他自认为是一个虚无主义者，认为任何事物都没有内在价值。|Synonyms: cynic, pessimist, skeptic|Antonyms: optimist, believer|noun

nonnihilists: /ˌnɒnˈnaɪəlɪsts/ (UK), /ˌnɑːnˈnaɪəlɪsts/ (US) | n. |非虚无主义者| People who do *not* believe that life is meaningless and generally *do* accept some religious or moral principles. |The nonnihilists in the group argued for the importance of human connection and purpose.  小组中的非虚无主义者论证了人际关系和人生目标的重要性。|Synonyms: believers, optimists (sometimes), moralists (sometimes)|Antonyms: nihilists|noun
