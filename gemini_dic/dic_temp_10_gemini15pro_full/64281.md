equitableness:/ˈɛkwɪtəbəlness/
n.|公平；公正；合理|The equitableness of the judge's decision was praised by all.  法官判决的公正性受到了所有人的赞扬。|The equitableness of the distribution of resources ensured everyone had enough. 资源分配的公平性确保了每个人都足够。| fairness, justice, impartiality|unfairness, injustice, bias|noun

etymology->
From equit+able+ness
equit: from Latin aequus "equal, even, level, fair, just, calm"
-able: suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of, fit for, tending to, given to"
-ness:  suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting quality or state
