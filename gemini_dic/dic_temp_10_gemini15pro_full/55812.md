divesting:/daɪˈvɛstɪŋ/
verb
剥夺；使解除；脱去
The act of selling off or disposing of assets, such as stocks, property, or a subsidiary company.
The company is divesting itself of some of its less profitable businesses.  这家公司正在剥离一些盈利较少的业务。
synonyms: selling off, disposing of, getting rid of, unloading
antonyms: acquiring, investing, purchasing
verb

divest:/daɪˈvɛst/
verb
剥夺；使解除；脱去
To deprive someone of power, rights, or possessions.  OR To rid oneself of something.
The king was divested of his power. 国王被剥夺了权力。
He divested himself of his coat. 他脱掉了外套。
synonyms: deprive, strip, dispossess, rid
antonyms: endow, invest, grant
verb
