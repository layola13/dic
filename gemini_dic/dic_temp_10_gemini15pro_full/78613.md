gibbose:/ˈɡɪboʊs/ (US), /ˈdʒɪbəʊs/ (UK)| adj.| 凸起的，驼背的，隆起的|Swelling or bulging outward; humped.  Referring to a surface that is rounded or convex, especially in a biological context.|The gibbose base of the cactus held a surprising amount of water. 仙人掌凸起的基部储存了大量的水分。|近义词：convex, protuberant, swollen, bulging|反义词：concave, flat|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin gibbosus (“humpbacked”), from gibbus (“hump”).
