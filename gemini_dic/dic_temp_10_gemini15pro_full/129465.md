non-affiliated:/ˌnɒn əˈfɪliˌeɪtɪd/ /ˌnɑːn əˈfɪliˌeɪtɪd/| adj.|非附属的，无关联的|Not officially attached to or connected with an organization or group.|例句：Many non-affiliated voters are still undecided. 许多无党派选民仍然犹豫不决。|例句：The non-affiliated medical clinic offers affordable healthcare. 这家非附属诊所提供价格合理的医疗保健。|近义词：independent, unaffiliated, unattached|反义词: affiliated, connected, associated|词性:形容词

词源:
non-(prefix) meaning "not" + affiliated (past participle of affiliate), from Latin affiliare "to adopt as a son," from ad- "to" + filius "son"
