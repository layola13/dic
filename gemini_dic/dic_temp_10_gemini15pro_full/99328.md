intercurls: /ˌɪntərˈkɜːrlz/
verb
相互卷曲；交织卷曲
When two or more things intercurl, they curl or twist together or into each other.
Her hair intercurls with the ribbons. 她的头发与丝带交织卷曲。
The vines intercurled around the trellis. 藤蔓相互缠绕在格子上。
Synonyms: intertwine, entwine, interweave, interlace
Antonyms: untangle, separate, unravel
词性: 动词
