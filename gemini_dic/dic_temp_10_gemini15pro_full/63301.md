enthusing:/ɪnˈθjuːzɪŋ/ | adj. | 热情的；使人兴奋的 |  Present participle of enthuse: expressing or causing enthusiasm. |  She was enthusing about her new job. 她对她的新工作充满热情。| 近义词：exciting, inspiring, stimulating | 反义词：boring, dull, tedious | 词性:形容词

enthuse:/ɪnˈθjuːz/ | v. | 使热衷；使充满热情 | To make (someone) enthusiastic. |  He was enthused by the idea of starting his own business.  创业的想法让他充满热情。 | 近义词：excite, inspire, motivate | 反义词：bore, discourage, dampen | 词性:动词
