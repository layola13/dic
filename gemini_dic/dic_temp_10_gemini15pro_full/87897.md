hierarchies: /ˈhaɪərɑːrkiːz/ | n. | 等级制度；统治集团；等级体系 | A system or organization in which people or groups are ranked one above the other according to status or authority. |  The company's rigid hierarchies stifle innovation.  这家公司僵化的等级制度扼杀了创新。| synonyms: ranking, order, pecking order, chain of command | antonyms: equality, disorder, anarchy | noun

hierarchies: /ˈhaɪərɑːrkiːz/ | n. | 层次结构；分级结构 | An arrangement or classification of things according to relative importance or inclusiveness. | The taxonomies of  biological hierarchies classify species into genus, family, order, class, etc. 生物等级分类法将物种分为属、科、目、纲等。| synonyms: structure, organization, system, framework | antonyms: disorganization, chaos, randomness | noun
