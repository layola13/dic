essentialized:/ɪˈsɛnʃəlaɪzd/
|adj.|本质化的，被简化到本质的，被认为是本质的|Reduced to its essence; viewed as essential.  The term is often used critically to describe a simplified or stereotyped representation of something.|Her research challenges the essentialized view of gender roles in society. 她的研究挑战了社会中对性别角色的本质化观点。|simplified, stereotyped, reduced, typified|nuanced, complex, multifaceted|adjective
essentialize:/ɪˈsɛnʃəlaɪz/
|v.|使本质化，简化到本质，认为是本质的|To reduce something to its essence, often ignoring its complexity and nuance. It can also mean to view something as inherently or fundamentally essential.|The media tends to essentialize complex political issues. 媒体倾向于将复杂的政治问题本质化。|simplify, stereotype, reduce, typify|complicate, nuance, expand|verb
