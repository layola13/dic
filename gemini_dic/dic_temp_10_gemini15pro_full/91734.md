hyperdeliciously:/ˌhaɪpərdiˈlɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 极度美味地，非常美味地 |  An adverb formed by adding the prefix "hyper-" (meaning "excessive" or "above") to "deliciously." It describes something as being exceptionally delicious. | The cake was hyperdeliciously moist and flavorful.  这蛋糕极其美味，湿润可口。|  Synonyms: extremely deliciously, incredibly deliciously, exceptionally deliciously | Antonyms:  disgustingly, unpleasantly, horribly | 词性: 副词
