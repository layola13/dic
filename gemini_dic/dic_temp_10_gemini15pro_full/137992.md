noodledom:/ˈnuːdəldəm/
n.
愚蠢；糊涂；傻瓜行为
a state of stupidity or silliness
He acted like he was in a state of noodledom, forgetting his own name. 他表现得像个傻瓜，连自己的名字都忘了。
近义词：foolishness, silliness, stupidity
反义词：wisdom, intelligence, sense
词性：名词

词源:
From "noodle" (meaning a fool) + "-dom" (a suffix forming nouns denoting state or condition). "Noodle" in this sense likely originated as slang, possibly influenced by the image of limp, lifeless noodles.
