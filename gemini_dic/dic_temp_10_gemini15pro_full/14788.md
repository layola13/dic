awol:/ˈeɪwɒl/ (US) /ˈeɪwɔːl/ (UK)| adj. n.| 擅离职守的；逃兵 |Absent Without Official Leave (or Leave), meaning away from one's military duties without permission. | He was declared AWOL after missing three roll calls. 他因错过三次点名而被宣布擅离职守。 | The soldier went AWOL. 这名士兵开了小差。 | 近义词：absent, deserted, truant | 反义词：present, accounted for | 词性: adjective, noun
