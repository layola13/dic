hellbound:/ˈhɛlˌbaʊnd/| adj.|注定要下地狱的；走向毁灭的|Condemned to hell; on the way to hell; doomed to failure or disaster.|The villain in the movie was portrayed as a hellbound soul.  这部电影中的反派被描绘成一个注定要下地狱的灵魂。|The gambler, hellbound on winning, lost everything.  这个赌徒一心想赢，结果却输得精光。|近义词：doomed, damned, cursed|反义词：blessed, saved|词性:形容词

Etymology: From "hell" + "bound." "Hell" comes from Old English "hel," meaning "the underworld." "Bound" comes from Old English "bunden," the past participle of "bindan," meaning "to tie, fasten."
