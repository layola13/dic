hydromica:/ˈhaɪdrəˈmaɪkə/ | n. | 水云母 |A group of clay minerals similar to illite, typically formed by the weathering of feldspar or other silicate minerals. Hydromicas contain more water than true micas and are important components of some soils and sedimentary rocks.| The soil sample contained a significant amount of hydromica. 土壤样本中含有大量的水云母。| synonym: illite, clay mineral | antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific mineral) | noun
