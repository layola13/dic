nonpunctuating: /ˌnɒnˈpʌŋktʃueɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不加标点的，不用标点符号的 | Not using or inserting punctuation marks. | The email was long, rambling, and nonpunctuating, making it difficult to understand. 这封邮件冗长、杂乱无章，而且没有标点符号，很难理解。|  The student submitted a nonpunctuating essay, which made it hard to grade.  这个学生交了一篇没有标点符号的文章，很难评分。| 近义词: unpunctuated | 反义词: punctuated | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "punctuating" (present participle of "punctuate," from Latin "punctuare" meaning "to prick, point, mark").
