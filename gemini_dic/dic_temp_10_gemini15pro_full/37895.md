complaisant:/kəmˈpleɪzənt/ /kəmˈpleɪsənt/ | adj. | 恭顺的，讨好的，殷勤的 | Showing a cheerful willingness to do favors for others; eager to please. |  He was always complaisant to his superiors, hoping to gain their favor. 他对上司总是很恭顺，希望能得到他们的青睐。

She had a complaisant nature, always putting others' needs before her own. 她性情温顺，总是把别人的需要放在自己之前。

| 近义词: obliging, accommodating, agreeable, amiable, compliant | 反义词: unaccommodating, disagreeable, assertive, intractable | 词性: adjective
