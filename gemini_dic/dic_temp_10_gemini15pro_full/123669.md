mood-altering:/ˈmuːdˌɔːltərɪŋ/
adjective
改变情绪的；影响心情的
affecting or changing one's mood.
This medication is mood-altering and may cause drowsiness.  这种药物会改变情绪，可能会引起嗜睡。
Certain foods can have a mood-altering effect.  某些食物可以改变情绪。
synonym: psychoactive, psychotropic
antonym: mood-stabilizing

Etymology:
mood (from Old English mōd "heart, mind, feeling, spirit") + altering (from alter, from Old French alterer "to change," from Latin alterare "to change, modify," from alter "other" )
