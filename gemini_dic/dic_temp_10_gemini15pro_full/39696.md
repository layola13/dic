considered:/kənˈsɪdərd/| adj.|被认为的；考虑过的；体贴的|Thought about carefully; taken into account; thoughtful of others.|例句：He is considered a genius. 他被认为是一个天才。|近义词：thoughtful, deliberate, reasoned,  regarded, deemed |反义词:inconsiderate, rash, impulsive|词性:形容词

considered:/kənˈsɪdərɪŋ/| prep. conj.|考虑到；鉴于|Taking into account; in view of.|例句：Considering the circumstances, he did well. 考虑到情况，他做得不错。|近义词：given, in view of, taking into account|反义词:disregarding, ignoring|词性:介词, 连词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English consideren, from Old French considerer, from Latin considerare (“to look at closely, observe, contemplate, consider”), from con- (“together”) + sīderārī (“literally ‘to observe the stars,’ figuratively ‘to contemplate’”), from sīdus, sīderis (“star, constellation”).
