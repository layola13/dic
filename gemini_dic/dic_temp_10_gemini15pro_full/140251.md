offscum: /ˈɒfˌskʌm/ | n. | 人渣，败类，社会渣滓 |  A derogatory term for people considered to be the worst or most worthless part of society.  Derived from "scum" meaning a layer of impurity on the surface of a liquid, implying these individuals are the refuse of society. |  "The offscum of the city gathered in the dark alley." 城市的败类聚集在黑暗的小巷里。| synonyms: dregs, riffraff, scum, vermin | antonyms: elite, cream of the crop | noun
