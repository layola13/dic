built-ins:/ˈbɪltɪnz/ | n. |内置组件，内置功能|Functions, methods, or features that are readily available within a programming language or system without requiring external libraries or modules.|Python offers a rich set of built-ins. | Python 提供丰富的内置函数。|synonyms: integrated functions, standard library, pre-installed functions|antonyms: external libraries, add-ons, plugins|noun


built-in:/ˈbɪltɪn/ | adj. |内置的，嵌入式的| Forming an integral part of a structure or device, not added later.| The house has built-in wardrobes. | 这房子有嵌入式衣柜。|synonyms: integrated, incorporated, embedded|antonyms: external, detachable, add-on|adjective
