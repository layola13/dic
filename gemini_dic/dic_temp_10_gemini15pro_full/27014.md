calculatory:/ˈkælkjʊlətɔːri/ |adj.|计算的；精于计算的；深思熟虑的|Used to describe something done by or involving calculation, or done in a calculating way, often suggesting cold planning or manipulation. |例句：He made a calculatory move to gain more power. 他采取了精于算计的行动来获得更多权力。

例句：Her calculatory silence made everyone uncomfortable. 她精于算计的沉默让每个人都感到不舒服。|近义词：calculated, scheming, manipulative, shrewd, cunning|反义词：impulsive, spontaneous, artless, ingenuous, naive|词性:形容词

词源: Late 18th century: from calculate + -ory.
