gigantism:/ˈdʒaɪɡənˌtɪzəm/
n.|巨大症；巨人症|A condition characterized by excessive growth and height significantly above average, usually caused by overproduction of growth hormone during childhood.|The boy's gigantism was evident in his towering height.  这个男孩的巨人症在他的身高上很明显。|近义词:acromegaly|反义词:dwarfism|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *gigantismus, from Latin gigas (“giant”) + -ismus.
