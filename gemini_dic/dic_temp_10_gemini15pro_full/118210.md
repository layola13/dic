mephitic:/məˈfɪtɪk/
adjective
恶臭的；有毒的；污浊的
(of a gas or vapour) foul-smelling; noxious.  A poisonous, suffocating, or foul-smelling gas or vapor.
The explorers were overcome by the mephitic fumes in the cave.  探险队员被洞穴中恶臭的烟雾熏倒了。

The air hung thick and mephitic, heavy with the stench of decay. 空气浓浊而恶臭，充满了腐烂的气味。

synonym:noxious, poisonous, toxic, foul, fetid, putrid, pestilential
antonym:fragrant, sweet-smelling, aromatic, perfumed, wholesome
Etymology:Late Middle English: from late Latin mephiticus, from mephitis ‘noxious exhalation’.
