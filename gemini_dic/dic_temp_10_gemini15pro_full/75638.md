fugued:/fjuːɡd/
adjective
神志不清的，精神错乱的；（尤指）在极度兴奋状态下的
Resembling or suggestive of a fugue, especially in being disordered or incoherent.
He wandered the streets in a fugued state. 他在神志不清的状态下在街上游荡。
近义词：confused, disoriented, bewildered, delirious
反义词：lucid, clearheaded, coherent
词性：形容词

fugued:/fjuːɡd/
verb
以赋格曲形式写；以赋格形式演奏
To compose or perform in the style of a fugue.
The composer fugued the main theme throughout the piece. 作曲家在整首曲子中都以赋格形式呈现了主旋律。
近义词：interweave, intertwine, blend
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "simplify" in a musical context)
词性：动词
