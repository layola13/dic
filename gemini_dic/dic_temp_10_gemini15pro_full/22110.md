bolshiness:/ˈbɒlʃɪnəs/| n. |粗暴; 蛮横; 盛气凌人|The state of being bolshie, characterized by a rebellious and often aggressive attitude.|His bolshiness made it difficult to work with him. 他的蛮横无理让他很难相处。|近义词：rebelliousness, defiance, aggressiveness, insubordination|反义词：obedience, compliance, submissiveness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From bolshie + -ness.  Bolshie itself is a shortened form of Bolshevik, referring to a member of the majority faction (Bolsheviks) of the Russian Social Democratic Party, which seized power in the October Revolution of 1917.  The term later became associated with revolutionary or rebellious behavior in general.
