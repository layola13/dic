nonreflection:/ˌnɒnrɪˈflɛkʃən/ | n. | 非反射；不反省；无思考 | The nonreflection of light from the surface created a void-like appearance. 表面没有反射光线，形成了一种类似虚空的外观。|  The president's nonreflection on his past mistakes led to further criticism. 总统对过去错误的不反省导致了进一步的批评。 | 近义词: non-reflectivity, unreflectiveness | 反义词: reflection, reflectivity | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "reflection" (from Late Latin *reflexio, from Latin reflectere "to bend back")
