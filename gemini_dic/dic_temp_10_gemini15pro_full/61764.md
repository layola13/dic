empathist:/ˈɛmpəθɪst/| n.|共情者，移情者|A person who has the ability to understand and share the feelings of another.|例句：She's a natural empath, always able to sense the emotions of those around her. 她是一个天生的共情者，总是能够感知周围人的情绪。

例句：Being an empath can be both a gift and a curse, as you experience the joys and sorrows of others as if they were your own. 成为一个共情者既是天赋也是诅咒，因为你会像亲身经历一样体验他人的喜怒哀乐。|近义词：sympathizer, compassionate person|反义词：narcissist, sociopath|词性: noun

Etymology:  From empathy + -ist.  Empathy from Greek empatheia, from em- 'in' + pathos 'feeling'.
