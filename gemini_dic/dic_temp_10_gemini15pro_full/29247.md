cassia: /ˈkæʃə/ /ˈkæsiə/ | n. | 桂皮；决明子；肉桂 | An evergreen tree or shrub of the genus Cinnamomum, whose bark is used as a spice.  A plant of the genus Cassia, especially one grown as an ornamental or for the medicinal properties of its leaves or pods. |  Cassia bark is often used as a substitute for cinnamon. 桂皮经常被用作肉桂的替代品。 The cassia tree in our garden is in full bloom. 我们花园里的决明子树正盛开着。 | 近义词: cinnamon, senna | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a plant) | 词性: noun

cassia: /ˈkæʃə/ /ˈkæsiə/ | adj. | 肉桂色的；桂皮的 |  Of the color of cassia bark. |  Her dress was a warm cassia brown. 她的连衣裙是暖暖的桂皮色。 | 近义词: cinnamon-colored, tawny | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a color) | 词性: adjective


词源: From Latin cassia, from Greek kasía, ultimately of Semitic origin.
