chic-est:/ʃiːkɪst/| adj.|最时尚的，最别致的|Superlative form of chic: most chic.  It describes something or someone as being the most fashionable, stylish, and elegant.|She wore the chicest outfit at the party. 她在派对上穿了最时尚的服装。|Synonyms: most stylish, trendiest, most fashionable|Antonyms: dowdy, frumpy, unfashionable|adjective
