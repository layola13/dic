acculturation: /əˌkʌltʃəˈreɪʃən/
noun
文化适应;同化
The process of adapting to a new culture.  This involves learning the language, customs, and values of the new culture while often retaining elements of one's original culture.
The acculturation of immigrants is a complex process. 移民的文化适应是一个复杂的过程。

The museum exhibits documented the acculturation of Italian immigrants in the early 20th century.  博物馆的展品记录了20世纪早期意大利移民的文化适应过程。

近义词: assimilation, adaptation, integration
反义词: alienation, isolation, segregation
词性: noun
词源: From Late Latin acculturatio, from Latin ad- + cultura (“cultivation").  Essentially, "cultivating towards" a culture.
