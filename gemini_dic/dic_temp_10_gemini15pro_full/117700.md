melampyrin:/ˌmɛlæmˈpaɪrɪn/
noun
美蓝吡啶，美蓝霉素
A blue pigment derived from fungi.  A toxic metabolite produced by some species of *Penicillium*.
Melampyrin is a toxic compound found in moldy bread. 美蓝霉素是一种在发霉面包中发现的有毒化合物。
Melampyrin can cause neurological symptoms. 美蓝霉素会导致神经系统症状。
Synonyms: none (highly specific chemical compound)
Antonyms: none
