nonremunerative:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈmjuː.nər.ə.tɪv/ /ˌnɑːn.rɪˈmjuː.nər.ə.tɪv/
adjective
无报酬的；不赚钱的
Not providing any reward, especially financially.
Volunteering is a nonremunerative activity, but it offers personal satisfaction.  志愿工作是无报酬的活动，但它能提供个人满足感。
She took on a nonremunerative internship to gain experience in the field. 她接受了一份无报酬的实习，以获得该领域的经验。
近义词:unpaid, unrewarded, voluntary, gratuitous
反义词:paid, remunerative, profitable, lucrative
词性:adjective
