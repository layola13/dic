assistants:/əˈsɪstənts/| n.|助手，助理，协助者|People who help or support someone else in their work or tasks.  They provide aid, assistance, or support in various capacities.|例句：The CEO has several assistants who manage her schedule.  首席执行官有几个助手来管理她的日程安排。

例句：The lab assistants prepared the equipment for the experiment. 实验室助理准备了实验设备。|近义词：helpers, aides, supporters, deputies|反义词：managers, supervisors, bosses|Noun
