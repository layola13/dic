co-opts:/koʊˈɑːpts/ | v. |  吸收；拉拢；指派 | To appoint to membership of a committee or other body by invitation of the existing members.  To appropriate something for one's own use, typically without the owner's permission. |  The committee co-opted several new members. 委员会吸收了几名新成员。 The rebels co-opted the villagers into their army. 叛军把村民编入他们的军队。 | Synonyms:  appropriate, assimilate, incorporate, enlist, recruit | Antonyms: exclude, reject, dismiss | verb
