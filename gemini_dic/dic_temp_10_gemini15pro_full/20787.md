USAGE->
Simple Present:
What do you do for a living?  你靠什么谋生？
What does she like to eat? 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to help? 她做了些什么来帮忙？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you say? 你说了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? 我看到她时她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you came here? 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? 在她换专业之前她一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting tomorrow? 明天的会议上他们会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have accomplished by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have finished by the time we arrive? 等我们到达时，他们将完成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？


Subjunctive Mood:
If I knew what he wanted, I would tell him. 如果我知道他想要什么，我会告诉他。
I wish I knew what to do in this situation. 我希望我知道在这种情况下该怎么办。


Passive Voice:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


Interrogative Sentence:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是哪里？
What are your plans for the weekend? 你周末有什么计划？


Negative Sentence:
I don't know what to say. 我不知道该说什么。
He didn't tell me what he wanted. 他没有告诉我他想要什么。
