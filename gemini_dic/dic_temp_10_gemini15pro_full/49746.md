demoing:/ˈdeməʊɪŋ/ (US) /ˈdɛməʊɪŋ/ (UK)| v.|演示，示范|presenting a product or service to demonstrate how it works|例句：The company is demoing its new software at the conference.  该公司正在会议上演示其新软件。

例句：They were demoing the latest video game to potential buyers.  他们正在向潜在买家演示最新的电子游戏。|近义词：demonstrating, presenting, showcasing, exhibiting|反义词:concealing, hiding|词性:动词
