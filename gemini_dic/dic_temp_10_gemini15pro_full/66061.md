exaugurate:/ɪɡˈzɔːɡjəreɪt/ |v. |夸大；夸张；放大|To enlarge beyond bounds or the truth; to overstate.|He exaugurated the difficulties of the task, making it seem impossible. 他夸大了任务的难度，使之看起来不可能完成。|例句：The media tends to exaugurate the dangers of air travel. 媒体倾向于夸大航空旅行的危险。

例句：She exaugurated her role in the project, claiming all the credit. 她夸大了自己在项目中的作用，声称所有的功劳都归她所有。|近义词： exaggerate, amplify, overstate, magnify, inflate |反义词: understate, minimize, downplay|词性:动词
