hyperenthusiastic:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪk/
adjective
过度热情的；极度狂热的
Excessively or extremely enthusiastic.
She was hyperenthusiastic about the new project, practically bouncing with excitement. 她对这个新项目极其热情，兴奋得几乎要跳起来了。
He gave a hyperenthusiastic welcome to the visitors, overwhelming them with his energy. 他过度热情地欢迎来访者，用他的活力让他们不知所措。

近义词:overenthusiastic, fanatical, zealous, fervent, passionate
反义词:apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic, halfhearted

词性:adjective
词源:  "Hyper-" (Greek: ὑπέρ, hupér, "over, beyond") + "enthusiastic" (from Greek "entheos," meaning "inspired by a god," later coming to mean "filled with divine inspiration" and eventually "having intense emotion or zeal").
