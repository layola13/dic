obvious:/ˈɒbviəs/ (UK) /ˈɑːbviəs/ (US)| adj.|明显的，显然的|easily perceived or understood; clear, self-evident, or apparent.|例句：It was obvious that he was lying.  很明显他在撒谎。

例句：The obvious solution is to ask for help.  显而易见的解决办法是寻求帮助。

近义词：clear, apparent, evident, plain, manifest|反义词：subtle, unclear, obscure, hidden, ambiguous|词性: adjective
