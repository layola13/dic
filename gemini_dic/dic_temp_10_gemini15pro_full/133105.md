nonerratics:/ˌnɒnɪˈrætɪks/
| n. |原生沉积物; 非冰碛物| Material native to its location, not transported by glacial action, as opposed to erratics (rocks transported by glaciers).| The geologist examined the nonerratics in the riverbed to understand the local bedrock composition. 地质学家检查了河床中的原生沉积物，以了解当地基岩的成分。| The team carefully sifted through the nonerratics, searching for ancient artifacts.  团队仔细筛选原生沉积物，寻找古代文物。| 近义词: indigenous deposits, native material | 反义词: erratics, glacial deposits | 词性: 名词
