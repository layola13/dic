mistily:/ˈmɪstɪli/| adv.|朦胧地，模糊地|Covered or obscured by mist; vaguely or unclearly.|The mountains were mistily veiled in the early morning light. 早晨的山峦笼罩在薄雾之中，朦胧一片。|The details of the event are mistily remembered.  我对这件事的细节记忆模糊。|近义词：hazily, vaguely, cloudily|反义词：clearly, distinctly|词性:副词
词源: From mist + -ly.  Mist comes from Old English *mist, of Germanic origin.
