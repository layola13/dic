deplantation:/diːplænˈteɪʃən/
n.
移植, 拔除（尤指器官或组织）
The surgical removal of an organ or tissue from its original site.
The deplantation of the diseased heart was successful.  病变心脏的移植手术很成功。
近义词:explantation, removal, extraction
反义词:implantation, transplantation
词性:名词

deplantation:/diːplænˈteɪʃən/
n.
拔除, 铲除（尤指植物）
The act of removing a plant from the ground.
The deplantation of the invasive species is crucial for ecosystem restoration.  铲除入侵物种对于生态系统恢复至关重要。
近义词:uprooting, removal, eradication
反义词:planting
词性:名词
