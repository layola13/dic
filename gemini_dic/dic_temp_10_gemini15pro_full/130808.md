nonapologetic:/ˌnɒnəˌpɒləˈdʒɛtɪk/ | adj. | 不道歉的，不后悔的，理直气壮的 | Not feeling or showing any regret or remorse.  Showing no shame about one's actions or opinions, even if they are considered controversial or offensive. |  She was nonapologetic about her controversial remarks. 她对自己的争议言论毫不后悔。 | Synonyms: unapologetic, unrepentant, remorseless, shameless | Antonyms: apologetic, remorseful, repentant, ashamed | adjective
