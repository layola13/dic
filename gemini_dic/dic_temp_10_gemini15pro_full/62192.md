encore:/ˈɑːŋkɔːr/ (US), /ˈɒŋkɔːr/ (UK)| n. int. |加演，再来一次|An additional performance of a piece of music or other item at the end of a concert, as called for by an audience.  A demand for such a performance.|The audience shouted "Encore!" after her final song.| 近义词：repeat,  reprise | 反义词: (No direct antonym)| noun, interjection
