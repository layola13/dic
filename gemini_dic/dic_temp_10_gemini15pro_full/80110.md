gobelin:/ˈɡɒblɪn/ /ˈɡɑːblɪn/| n.|哥白林挂毯; (尤指)手工编织的挂毯|A rich and elaborate tapestry woven by hand, typically depicting historical or mythological scenes.  Originally referring to tapestries made at the Gobelins factory in Paris.|The museum houses a magnificent collection of gobelins.|近义词：tapestry, arras|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "print" or "painting" in a comparative context)|词性:noun
