orientationally:/ˌɔːriənˈteɪʃənəli/
adv.
方向性地，取向性地
Relating to or determined by orientation.
The molecules are orientationally ordered. 这些分子在方向上是有序的。
Near synonyms: directionally, positionally
Opposite: randomly, haphazardly
词性: 副词
