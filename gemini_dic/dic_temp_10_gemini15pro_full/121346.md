misgovernances:/ˌmɪsˈɡʌvərnənsɪz/
| n. | 辜负,管理不善,治理不力 | The act or process of governing badly; mismanagement, especially by government officials. | The country's economic woes are largely due to years of misgovernance. 这个国家的经济困境主要是由于多年的治理不善造成的。|  The misgovernances led to widespread corruption. 管理不善导致了普遍的腐败。| synonyms: mismanagement, maladministration, misrule, corruption, incompetence | antonyms: good governance, sound management, effective administration | noun
