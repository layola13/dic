dolt:/doʊlt/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A stupid or foolish person. |He's such a dolt, he locked his keys in the car again.|Synonyms: idiot, fool, moron, imbecile, nitwit, simpleton|Antonyms: genius, intellectual, savant|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dolt*, *dulte*, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to dull, or from a Scandinavian source akin to Old Norse *dultr* (“dolt, simpleton”).
