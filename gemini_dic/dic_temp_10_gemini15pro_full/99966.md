interorbital:/ˌɪntərˈɔːrbɪtəl/| adj.|眼窝间的，两眼之间的|Situated between the orbits (eye sockets).|The interorbital distance is a key characteristic in bird identification.  眼间距是鸟类识别的一个关键特征。

The boxer suffered a fracture to his interorbital bone.  这名拳击手眼窝间的骨头骨折了。|近义词：interocular|反义词：(No direct antonym, possibly "peripheral" in some contexts referring to vision)|词性:adjective
