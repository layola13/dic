downsampled:/ˌdaʊnˈsæmpəld/ | adj. | 下采样的，降采样的 | Reduced in resolution or data points, typically by averaging or discarding some of the original data. | The image was downsampled to reduce its file size. 图像被下采样以减小文件大小。| The audio was downsampled from 48 kHz to 44.1 kHz. 音频从 48 kHz 下采样到 44.1 kHz。 | synonyms: subsampled, decimated | antonyms: upsampled, interpolated | adjective
