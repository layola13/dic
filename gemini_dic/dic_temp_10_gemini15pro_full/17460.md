bearably:/ˈberəbli/| adv.|可以忍受地，尚能忍受地|In a way that can be tolerated; tolerably.|The pain was bearably mild. 疼痛轻微，尚可忍受。|近义词:tolerably, acceptably, moderately|反义词:unbearably, intolerably|词性:副词

Etymology:
From bearable + -ly.  "Bearable" comes from the verb "bear" (Old English beran "to carry, endure") + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being").
