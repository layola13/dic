chiropractor:/ˈkaɪrəʊpræktər/ (UK), /ˈkaɪrəpræktər/ (US)| n.|脊椎按摩师|A healthcare professional who specializes in manipulating the spine and other joints to treat musculoskeletal problems, without the use of drugs or surgery.|例句：I visited a chiropractor for my back pain. 我去看脊椎按摩师治疗我的背痛。

例句：The chiropractor adjusted my neck and I felt much better. 脊椎按摩师调整了我的脖子，我感觉好多了。

近义词：osteopath, physical therapist|反义词: None|词性: Noun
