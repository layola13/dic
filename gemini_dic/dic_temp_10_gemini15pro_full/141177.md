ooh:/uː/ | interj. | 哦; 喔（表示惊讶、疼痛、喜悦等） | An exclamation expressing surprise, pain, pleasure, or other emotions. | Ooh, that's a beautiful dress! 哦，那件裙子真漂亮！| 近义词: ah, oh | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an interjection) | 词性:感叹词

例句：
Ooh, that's a surprise!
哦，真是个惊喜！

Ooh, that hurts!
哦，好痛！

PHRASE->
"Ooh" is typically used on its own and doesn't form many set phrases. However, it can be combined with other exclamations:

Ooh la la!  (Expressing delight or surprise)
哦啦啦！（表示高兴或惊讶）


Ooh, my goodness! (Expressing surprise or alarm)
哦，我的天哪！（表示惊讶或惊恐）

Ooh, ah (Expressing mild pleasure or excitement)
哦，啊（表示轻微的愉悦或兴奋）

Ooh, boy! (Expressing surprise, excitement, or anticipation)
哦，天哪！（表示惊讶、兴奋或期待）

Ooh, yeah! (Expressing strong agreement or enthusiasm)
哦，耶！（表示强烈同意或热情）
