ambiloquy: /ˌæmbɪˈloʊkwi/
n.
模棱两可的话，含糊不清的言辞
Ambiguity or ambivalence in speech; equivocation.  The use of ambiguous language.  Speaking unclearly or vaguely.
The politician's ambiloquy left the audience confused about his stance on the issue.  这位政客的含糊其辞让听众对他在这个问题上的立场感到困惑。

The witness's ambiloquy made it difficult for the jury to determine the truth.  证人的含糊其辞使得陪审团难以确定真相。

近义词: ambiguity, equivocation, vagueness, obscurity
反义词: clarity, precision, directness, explicitness
词性: noun
