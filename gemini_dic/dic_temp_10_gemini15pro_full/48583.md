defeatism:/dɪˈfiːtɪzəm/
n.|失败主义，悲观主义|The pervasive defeatism among the troops worried the general. 士兵中普遍存在的失败主义情绪令将军担忧。|Example:  The defeatism in the team made it difficult to win. 球队中的失败主义情绪让他们很难取胜。

defeatism:/dɪˈfiːtɪzəm/
n.|失败主义，悲观主义|His defeatism made him give up before even trying. 他的失败主义让他在尝试之前就放弃了。|Example:  She displayed a sense of defeatism after failing the exam. 考试不及格后，她表现出一种失败主义情绪。|Synonym: pessimism, negativity, discouragement|Antonym: optimism, hopefulness, positivism|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *défaitisme*, from *défait* ("defeated"), past participle of *défaire* ("to undo, defeat"), from Latin *disfacere* ("to undo, destroy").
