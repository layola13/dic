caliduct:/ˈkælɪdʌkt/| n.|热导管，导热管|A pipe or channel for conveying heat, especially hot air or water used for heating a building.|The Roman bath complex featured an extensive network of caliducts to distribute heat throughout the building.  罗马浴场建筑群拥有一个广泛的热导管网络，用于将热量分配到整个建筑物。|Synonyms: heat duct, heating pipe, hot air duct|Antonyms: cooling duct, refrigerant line|noun
