alkalimetry: /ˌælkəˈlɪmətri/ | n. | 碱量滴定法; 碱量测定法 | The determination of the amount of alkali present in a solution, or the strength of an alkali, by titration with a standard acid solution. | Alkalimetry is commonly used in industrial processes to control the quality of products. 碱量滴定法常用于工业过程中控制产品质量。|  Titration, acidimetry | acidimetry | noun
