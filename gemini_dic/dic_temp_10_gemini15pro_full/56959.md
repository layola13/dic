douay:/ˈduːeɪ/ | adj. n. | 杜埃版（圣经）; 杜埃 | Relating to or characteristic of Douai, France, especially the English Catholic college established there in 1568.  Also, a version of the Vulgate Bible translated at Douai and Rheims. | The Douay-Rheims Bible is still used by some traditional Catholics.  杜埃-兰斯圣经至今仍被一些传统天主教徒使用。 |  Douay Bible, Douay Version | Rheims-Douay | adjective, noun
Etymology: From Douai, a city in northern France.
