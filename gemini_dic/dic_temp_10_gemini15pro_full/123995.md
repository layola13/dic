morning-room:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋ ruːm/ /ˈmɔːrnɪŋ rʊm/
n.
晨室，早晨起居室
A room, often with large windows, used as a living room or sitting room, especially in the morning.
The family often gathered in the morning-room for breakfast and to read the newspaper.  家人经常在晨室里一起吃早餐和读报纸。
She spent her mornings writing letters in the sunlit morning-room.  她每天早晨都在阳光明媚的晨室里写信。
近义词：sitting room, living room, drawing room, parlor
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps bedroom if contrasting function)
