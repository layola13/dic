gemmated:/ˈdʒɛmeɪtɪd/
adjective
发芽的；萌芽的
having produced buds; covered in buds.
The gemmated branches of the tree promised a bountiful harvest. 这棵树发芽的枝条预示着丰收。
近义词:budding, sprouting
反义词:dormant, barren
词性:adjective

gemmated:/ˈdʒɛmeɪtɪd/
verb (used with object)
使发芽；使萌芽
to cause to bud.
The warm spring rains gemmated the seeds.  温暖的春雨使种子发芽了。
近义词:sprout, germinate
反义词:wither, die
词性:verb
