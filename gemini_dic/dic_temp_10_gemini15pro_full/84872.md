hanging-out:/ˈhæŋɪŋ aʊt/| n.|闲逛，出去玩|The act of spending time relaxing or socializing, typically with friends.  This often involves informal activities like chatting, listening to music, or simply being together.|例句：We spent the afternoon hanging out at the mall. 我们下午在商场闲逛。

例句：They enjoy hanging out at the coffee shop after school.  他们喜欢放学后在咖啡店里闲逛。|近义词：relaxing, chilling, socializing|反义词:working, studying|词性:名词 (Gerund)
