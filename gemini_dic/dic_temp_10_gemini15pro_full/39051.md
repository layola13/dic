conformable:/kənˈfɔːrməbl/| adj.|一致的，顺从的，相符的|Corresponding in form or character; compliant; obedient.|例句：Her behavior was perfectly conformable to the rules. 她的行为完全符合规则。|例句：The data is conformable with previous findings.  这些数据与之前的发现一致。|近义词：compliant, obedient, agreeable, consistent, compatible|反义词：nonconformist, rebellious, inconsistent, incompatible|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *conformabilis, from Latin conformāre (“to form together, shape”), from con- (“together”) + formāre (“to form”).
