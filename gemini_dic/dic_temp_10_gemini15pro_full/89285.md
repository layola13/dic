homodromous:/ˌhɒməˈdrɒməs/ | adj. | 同向旋转的，同向运动的 | Moving or acting in the same direction.  Referring specifically to the spiral arrangement of leaves on a stem where the spirals all ascend in the same direction. | The homodromous arrangement of the leaves allows for maximum sunlight exposure.  这种叶子的同向旋转排列方式可以最大限度地吸收阳光。| The vine's tendrils exhibited a homodromous growth pattern, twining consistently clockwise. 这株藤蔓的卷须呈现出同向的生长模式，始终按顺时针方向缠绕。 |  synonymous: concurrent, unidirectional | antonymous: antidromous, contradromous | adjective
