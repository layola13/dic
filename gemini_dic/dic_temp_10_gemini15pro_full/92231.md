hypertoxic:/ˌhaɪpərˈtɑːksɪk/ | adj. | 极度毒性的，剧毒的 | Extremely toxic; excessively poisonous. |  The waste from the chemical plant is hypertoxic and poses a serious threat to the environment. 化工厂的废物剧毒，对环境构成严重威胁。

The venom of certain spiders is hypertoxic, requiring immediate medical attention.  某些蜘蛛的毒液剧毒，需要立即就医。 | 近义词：supertoxic, ultra-toxic, highly toxic | 反义词：non-toxic, harmless | 词性：形容词

词源:  From hyper- "over, beyond" + toxic.  "Hyper-" comes from Greek "hyper" meaning "over, above."  "Toxic" comes from Late Latin "toxicus" meaning "poisoned," from Latin "toxicum" "poison," from Greek "toxikon (pharmakon)" "(poison) for use on arrows," from "toxon" "bow and arrow."
