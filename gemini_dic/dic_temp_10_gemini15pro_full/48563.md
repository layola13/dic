defatigable:/ˌdɛfətɪɡəbl/ /ˌdiːˈfætɪɡəbl/
adjective
不知疲倦的，不屈不挠的
Showing remarkable persistence; tireless.
例句：The defatigable campaigner for human rights finally saw her efforts rewarded.  这位不知疲倦的人权活动家终于看到了她的努力得到了回报。

例句：Despite the setbacks, the team remained defatigable in their pursuit of victory. 尽管遭遇挫折，团队仍然不屈不挠地追求胜利。

近义词：tireless, unflagging, indefatigable, persevering, persistent
反义词：tired, weary, exhausted, fatigued, easily discouraged
词性：adjective
