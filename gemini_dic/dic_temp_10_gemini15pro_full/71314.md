fizziest:/ˈfɪziɪst/| adj.|最嘶嘶作响的，最起泡的|The fizziest soda I've ever had.  我喝过最起泡的苏打水。|近义词：bubbly, sparkling, effervescent|反义词:flat, still|词性:形容词
词源: From fizz + -y + -est.  Fizz comes from imitative origin, likely mimicking the sound of escaping gas. The suffix -y makes it an adjective, and -est indicates the superlative form.
