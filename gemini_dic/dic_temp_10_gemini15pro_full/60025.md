edriophthalmous:/ˌed.ri.ɒfˈθæl.məs/| adj.| sessile-eyed; having sessile eyes (eyes that lack stalks).| Having eyes that are not on stalks, directly attached to the head.  Sessile means attached directly by the base without a stalk or peduncle.| Most crabs are edriophthalmous, with their eyes set directly into their carapace.|近义词: sessile-eyed|反义词: stalked-eyed (e.g., some crustaceans like shrimps have stalked eyes)|词性: adjective
