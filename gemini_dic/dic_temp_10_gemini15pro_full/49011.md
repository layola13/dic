degrader:/dɪˈɡreɪdər/
n.|降解物，降解剂，降解者|a substance or organism that degrades something, especially a pollutant.|The degrader broke down the harmful chemicals in the soil.  这种降解剂分解了土壤中的有害化学物质。|synonyms: decomposer, breaker-down|antonyms: builder, constructor
词性: 名词

degrade:/dɪˈɡreɪd/
v.|降低，贬低，使降解|to reduce someone or something to a lower rank or position, or to make the quality of something worse.|The scandal degraded him in the eyes of the public. 这桩丑闻降低了他在公众心目中的形象. | The oil spill degraded the water quality. 漏油降低了水质。|synonyms: demean, belittle, deteriorate|antonyms: elevate, enhance, improve
词性: 动词
