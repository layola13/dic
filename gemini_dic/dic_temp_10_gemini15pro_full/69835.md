ferri:/ˈfɛraɪ/
noun
铁的，含铁的
Relating to or containing iron, especially with a higher oxidation state.
Ferrimagnetism is a type of permanent magnetism that occurs in solids. 亚铁磁性是一种发生在固体中的永磁性。
The ferri ion is involved in many biological processes. 铁离子参与许多生物过程。
Synonym: ferric
Antonym: ferrous
词性: combining form (构词成分，用于构成复合词)
