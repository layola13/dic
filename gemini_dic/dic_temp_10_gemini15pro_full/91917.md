hypermanias:/ˌhaɪpərˈmeɪniəz/
|n.|躁狂症（复数）|A state of abnormally elevated mood and energy, often characterized by racing thoughts, pressured speech, and impulsive behavior.  It is a more severe form of mania.|The patient exhibited signs of hypermanias, including rapid speech and grandiose delusions.|Synonyms:  frenzy, mania, delirium|Antonyms: depression, lethargy|noun


hypermania:/ˌhaɪpərˈmeɪniə/
|n.|躁狂症（单数）|A state of abnormally elevated mood and energy, often characterized by racing thoughts, pressured speech, and impulsive behavior. It is a more severe form of mania.|His hypermania led him to make risky investments.|Synonyms:  frenzy, mania, delirium|Antonyms: depression, lethargy|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hyper* (over, excessive) + *mania* (madness)
