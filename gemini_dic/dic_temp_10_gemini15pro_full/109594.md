lethargize: /ˈlɛθərdʒaɪz/ | v. | 使昏睡；使懒散 | To make lethargic; to cause to become drowsy or sluggish. | The heat lethargized everyone.  高温使每个人都昏昏欲睡。

The long flight lethargized the passengers. 长途飞行使乘客们昏昏欲睡。

| 近义词: stupefy, dull, enervate, tire, exhaust | 反义词: energize, invigorate, stimulate, enliven | 词性: verb

词源: From lethargy + -ize.  Lethargy comes from the Greek "lēthargia"  from "lēthargos"  meaning "forgetful" or "sluggish,"  from "lēthē" (forgetfulness) + "argos" (idle).
