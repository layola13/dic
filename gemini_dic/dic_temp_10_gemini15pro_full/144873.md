over-reducing:/ˌoʊvər-rɪˈduːsɪŋ/| adj.|过度还原，过度简化|Reducing something to an excessive degree, often resulting in a loss of essential information or nuance.  The term is often used in chemistry, cooking, and data analysis contexts.|

例句：
Over-reducing the sauce resulted in a bitter, burnt flavor.
过度还原酱汁导致味道苦涩焦糊。

The model was over-reducing the complexity of the data, leading to inaccurate predictions.
该模型过度简化了数据的复杂性，导致预测不准确。

近义词：oversimplifying, dumbing down|
反义词：elaborating, expanding|
词性: adjective
