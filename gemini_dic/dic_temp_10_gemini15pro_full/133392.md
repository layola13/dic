nonfacility:/ˌnɒnfəˈsɪləti/
noun
非设施，非机构
A service or resource that is not part of a physical facility, especially in the context of healthcare.  It refers to services or resources not tied to a specific building or location.
The government is investing in nonfacility mental health services, like online therapy and crisis hotlines. 政府正在投资非设施心理健康服务，例如在线治疗和危机热线。
Synonyms:  community-based services, outreach programs
Antonyms: facility, institution
词性: 名词
