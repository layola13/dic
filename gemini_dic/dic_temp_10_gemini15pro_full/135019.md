nonnegotiables:/ˌnɒn.nɪˈɡəʊ.ʃi.ə.bəlz/ (US) /ˌnɑːn.nɪˈɡoʊ.ʃi.ə.bəlz/
noun
不可协商的事项;不可谈判的条件
Things that are nonnegotiable cannot be changed or discussed.
例句：
Salary expectations are nonnegotiable.
薪资期望是不可协商的。

There are certain nonnegotiables in any relationship.
在任何关系中都有一些不可协商的事情。

近义词：essentials, must-haves, requirements, fundamentals
反义词：negotiables, options, flexible terms

词性: noun (plural)
