opalesce:/ˌoʊpəˈlɛs/ | v. | 发出乳白光；闪现乳白色 | To exhibit a play of colors like that of an opal; to shimmer with iridescent colors. | The moonstone opalesced in the dim light. 月长石在昏暗的光线下发出乳白色光彩。

The liquid opalesced as the temperature changed. 随着温度的变化，液体闪现出乳白色。| 近义词：iridesce, shimmer, gleam | 反义词：dull, darken | 词性: 动词

词源: From Latin *opalēscent- (stem of opalēscēns), present participle of opalēscere "to begin to look like an opal," from opalus "opal" + -ēscere (inceptive suffix).
