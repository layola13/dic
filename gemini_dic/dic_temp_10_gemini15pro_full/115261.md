manipulator:/məˈnɪpjʊˌleɪtər/| n.|操纵者，控制者|A person who controls or influences others in a skillful, often unfair or insidious way.  Someone who uses or controls a machine or piece of equipment, especially in a skillful way. |He's a master manipulator, always pulling strings behind the scenes. 他是个操纵大师，总是在幕后操纵一切。|The robotic manipulator gently grasped the fragile object.  机器人操纵器轻轻地抓住了易碎的物品。|synonyms: operator, controller, handler, director, puppeteer|antonyms: pawn, puppet, victim|noun
