gashed:/ɡæʃt/
| adj. |被砍伤的，有很深裂口的|A deep cut or wound.  Marked by deep cuts or wounds.|The car's fender was gashed in the accident. 汽车的挡泥板在事故中被划破了。|近义词：cut, lacerated, torn, ripped, slashed|反义词:unblemished, smooth, intact
|形容词|

gashed:/ɡæʃ/
| vt. |砍伤，划破|To make a long, deep cut in something.|The falling tree limb gashed his arm. 掉落的树枝划伤了他的胳膊。|近义词：cut, lacerate, tear, rip, slash|反义词:heal, mend, repair
|动词|


词源:
From Old French *gaschier "to scarify, gash," from a Germanic source, from Proto-Germanic *gastjan "to stick, pierce, stab," related to Old Norse geirr "spear."
