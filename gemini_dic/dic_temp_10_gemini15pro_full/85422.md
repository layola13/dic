harvests:/ˈhɑːrvɪsts/ (US), /ˈhɑːvɪsts/ (UK)| n. v.| 收获；收成；收获期；收获物|The process or period of gathering in crops, or the amount of crops gathered.  The result of any activity or process.|The farmer celebrated a bountiful harvest. 农民庆祝丰收。|近义词: yields, crops, gathers, reaping|反义词: sowing, planting|词性:名词, 动词

harvests:/ˈhɑːrvɪsts/ (US), /ˈhɑːvɪsts/ (UK)| v.| 收获；收割|To gather in (a crop). To collect or obtain (something beneficial).|They harvested the wheat in late summer. 他们在夏末收割小麦。|近义词: reap, gather, collect|反义词: sow, plant|词性:动词
