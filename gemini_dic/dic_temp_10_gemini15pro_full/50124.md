dentize:/ˈdɛntaɪz/| v. |使齿化，加齿音|To make a sound like a dental consonant, or to cause something to sound like a dental consonant.|He dentized the 's' sound in the word 'sun'. 他把“sun”中的“s”发成了齿音。|近义词:dentalize|反义词: (No common antonym) |词性:verb
Etymology: From dent- + -ize.  "Dent-" comes from Latin "dens" (tooth). "-ize" is a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become like," "to subject to the action of,"  from Greek "-izein."
