old-fashioned:/ˌoʊldˈfæʃənd/| adj.|老式的，过时的|Characteristic of a past era; not modern.|She wore an old-fashioned dress. 她穿着一件老式的连衣裙。|例句：He has some rather old-fashioned ideas about women. 他对女性有一些相当过时的看法。|近义词：outdated, antiquated, vintage, retro|反义词：modern, contemporary, trendy, fashionable|词性:形容词
old-fashioneds:/ˌoʊldˈfæʃəndz/| n.|老式鸡尾酒（通常指Old Fashioned）|A cocktail traditionally made with whiskey, sugar, bitters, and an orange twist or cherry.|He ordered an old-fashioned. 他点了一杯老式鸡尾酒。|例句：Two old-fashioneds, please. 请来两杯老式鸡尾酒。|近义词：(referring to the drink itself, no direct synonym, perhaps "classic cocktail")|反义词：(no direct antonym)|词性:名词
