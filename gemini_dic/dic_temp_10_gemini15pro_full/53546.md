disbalance:/dɪsˈbæləns/ | n. |不平衡，失衡|lack of balance or symmetry; instability.|The sudden influx of refugees caused a disbalance in the local economy. 难民的突然涌入导致当地经济失衡。|近义词：imbalance, disproportion, inequality |反义词: balance, equilibrium, stability |词性:名词

disbalance:/dɪsˈbæləns/ | vt. |使失去平衡，使失调|to upset the balance or stability of something.|The trade deficit disbalanced the national budget. 贸易逆差使国家预算失衡。|近义词：unbalance, destabilize, upset |反义词: balance, stabilize |词性:动词
