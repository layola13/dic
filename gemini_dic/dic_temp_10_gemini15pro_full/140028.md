oenanthone:/iːˈnænθoʊn/
noun
壬酮
A colorless oily liquid ketone C7H14O found in oil of rue.  It is also known as heptanone-2.
Oenanthone is used in organic synthesis and as a flavoring agent.
Oenanthone has a pungent, fruity odor.  It can be irritating to the skin and eyes.
近义词: methyl pentyl ketone, 2-heptanone
反义词: N/A
词性:n.
