citronellol:/ˌsɪtrəˈnɛlɒl/ /ˌsɪtrəˈnɛlɔːl/| n. |香茅醇，香叶醇|From citronella + -ol (chemical suffix indicating an alcohol).  Ultimately from the Modern Latin *citronella, diminutive of citrus "citron tree".|Citronellol is named after the citronella plant from which it is derived. 香茅醇的名字来源于它所提取的香茅草。|The chemical formula for citronellol is C10H20O. 香茅醇的化学式是C10H20O。|近义词：rhodinol, geraniol|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
