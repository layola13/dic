fail-safe:/ˈfeɪl seɪf/| n. adj.|故障保险装置，自动防故障装置; 万无一失的|A fail-safe is a device or system designed to prevent failure or to minimize the harmful effects of failure.|例句：The plane has a fail-safe system that will allow it to land even if the engines fail. 这架飞机有一个故障保险系统，即使引擎发生故障也能让它安全着陆。

例句：The nuclear reactor has numerous fail-safes to prevent a meltdown. 核反应堆有许多故障保护装置以防止熔毁。|近义词：backup, safeguard, precaution|反义词: risky, dangerous|词性:名词, 形容词
