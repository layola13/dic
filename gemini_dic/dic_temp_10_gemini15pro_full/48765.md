deflagrable:/dɪˈfleɪɡrəbl/
adjective
可燃的，易燃的
Capable of burning rapidly or suddenly with intense heat and flame.
The deflagrable materials were stored in a special fireproof container.  可燃材料被储存在特殊的防火容器中。
Highly deflagrable substances should be handled with extreme caution.  高度易燃物质应极其小心地处理。
synonyms: flammable, combustible, incendiary
antonyms: nonflammable, fire-resistant, incombustible
adjective
