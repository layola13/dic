forefend:/fɔːrˈfɛnd/ | v. | 预防，阻止，避免 | To avert, prevent, or ward off (something evil or unpleasant). |  May God forefend such a tragedy. 愿上帝阻止这样的悲剧发生。| 近义词: prevent, avert, obviate, preclude | 反义词: cause, incite, encourage | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *forfenden*, from Old English *forfēon* (“to forbid, prohibit, renounce, despise”), from *for-* (“away, against”) + *fēon* (“to hate, detest”). Cognate with Dutch *verven*, German *verfeinden*.
