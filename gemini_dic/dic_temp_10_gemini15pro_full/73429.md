forelocking:/ˈfɔːrˌlɑːkɪŋ/ /ˈfɔːrˌlɒkɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
前锁,预先锁定,抢先占有
the act of securing something in advance, especially by seizing it before others can.
Farmers were forelocking their grain against the predicted shortages. 农民们为了应对预期的粮食短缺而提前锁定了他们的粮食。
近义词: preempting, securing, monopolizing
反义词: releasing, relinquishing
词性: verb (present participle)

forelock:/ˈfɔːrˌlɑːk/ /ˈfɔːrˌlɒk/
noun
额发,前额的头发, (马等的)额毛
a lock of hair growing over the forehead.
He tugged his forelock as a sign of respect. 他拉了拉额前的头发以示尊敬。
近义词: fringe, bang
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'fore-' (meaning 'before' or 'front') + 'lock' (of hair).  The verb "forelock" is a back-formation from the noun.
