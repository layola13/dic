deification:/ˌdiːəfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
神化；奉若神明
The act of treating or regarding someone or something as a god.
The Roman emperors demanded deification. 罗马皇帝要求神化。
近义词：apotheosis, exaltation, glorification
反义词：vilification, denigration, condemnation
词性: 名词


deify:/ˈdiːɪfaɪ/
vt.
神化；崇拜
To make a god of; to treat or regard as a deity.
Some cultures deify their ancestors.  一些文化神化他们的祖先。
近义词：idolize, worship, exalt
反义词：condemn, denounce, vilify
词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin deificātiō, from Latin deificāre (“to make a god of”), from deus (“god”) + -ficāre, from facere (“to make, to do”).
