atlantises:/ətˈlæntɪsɪz/ | n. | 亚特兰蒂斯（复数）| A plural form of Atlantis, referring to multiple hypothetical lost islands or continents, often associated with advanced civilizations and utopian societies.  Atlantis itself is a fictional island mentioned in historical works by Plato.  The plural form is rarely used because the original Atlantis is considered unique. |  He wrote a series of books about different atlantises, each with its unique culture and downfall. 他写了一系列关于不同亚特兰蒂斯的书，每个亚特兰蒂斯都有其独特的文化和衰落。 |  The explorer searched for traces of various atlantises across the globe.  这位探险家在全球各地寻找各种亚特兰蒂斯的踪迹。 | Synonyms: utopias, lost civilizations, mythical lands | Antonyms: reality, existing civilizations | noun
