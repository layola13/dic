mala:/ˈmɑːlə/ (US), /ˈmeɪlə/ (UK)| n.|花环，念珠，项链|A string of beads, typically used for prayer or meditation, common in Hinduism, Buddhism, and other traditions.|She wore a mala around her neck. 她脖子上戴着一条念珠。|Synonyms: rosary, beads, chaplet|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

mala:/ˈmɑːlə/ (US), /ˈmeɪlə/ (UK)| n.|不良，不适|Sanskrit word for "garland," but in some contexts can also mean impurity, dirt, or defilement.|The ancient texts spoke of cleansing the body of mala. 古代经文谈到净化身体的污秽。|Synonyms: impurity, defilement, dirt|Antonyms: purity, cleanliness|Noun
