mishap:/ˈmɪshæp/|n.|不幸事故，灾难，意外事件|An unlucky accident or small mistake that prevents something from working properly. |  There were a few minor mishaps during the journey, but we arrived safely. 旅途中发生了一些小意外，但我们安全到达了。

The launch of the new product was delayed due to a technical mishap.  新产品的发布由于技术故障而延迟了。|近义词：accident, misfortune, mishap, setback, incident, blunder|反义词：success, fortune, triumph|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *mishappen "to happen badly," from mis- "badly" + happen "to happen."
