furloughed:/ˈfɜːrloʊd/ (US) /ˈfɜːləʊd/ (UK)| adj. |休假的，暂时解雇的|Temporarily laid off from work, typically due to economic conditions, with the expectation of being recalled later.|例句：Many workers were furloughed during the pandemic. 许多工人在疫情期间被暂时解雇了。

例句：She was furloughed from her job for three months. 她被暂时解雇了三个月。


近义词：laid off, suspended, temporarily dismissed|反义词：employed, working, hired|词性：形容词
