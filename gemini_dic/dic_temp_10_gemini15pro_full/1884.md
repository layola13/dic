acroterium: /ˌækrəˈtɪəriəm/
noun
acroteria (plural)
顶饰，（尤指）屋顶饰物
An architectural ornament placed at the apex or outer angles of a pediment of a building.
The temple was adorned with elaborate acroteria depicting mythical creatures.  这座寺庙装饰着描绘神话生物的精致顶饰。
The acroteria on the corners of the roof were damaged in the storm. 屋顶角落的顶饰在暴风雨中受损了。
近义词: finial, ornament, decoration
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "plain" in the context of architectural style)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀκρωτήριον (akrōtḗrion, “summit, extremity, pediment”), from ἄκρον (ákron, “tip, summit, extremity”).
