outcull: /ˌaʊtˈkʌl/
Verb
剔除，淘汰
To remove or select (animals or plants) from a group because they are inferior or undesirable.
例句：The farmer outculled the weakest lambs from the flock.  农场主把最弱的小羊从羊群中剔除。

例句：The sickly trees were outculled to improve the overall health of the orchard.  病树被剔除以改善果园的整体健康状况。
近义词：cull, weed out, eliminate, remove, select out
反义词：retain, keep, include, preserve
词性：动词 (Verb)
