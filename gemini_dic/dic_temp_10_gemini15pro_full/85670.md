havanese:/ˌhɑːvəˈniːz/ /həˈvænɪz/| n. adj.|哈瓦那犬，哈瓦那的|A small breed of dog originating in Cuba, known for its long, silky coat.  Also used as an adjective to describe something related to Havana, Cuba.|例句：My Havanese loves to play fetch. 我的哈瓦那犬喜欢玩取物游戏。

例句：She has a Havanese puppy. 她有一只哈瓦那犬幼犬。|近义词：Bichon, dog, canine (as a noun), Cuban (as an adjective)|反义词:(No direct antonym for a breed of dog.  Perhaps "non-Havanese" in a specific context.)|词性:noun, adjective
