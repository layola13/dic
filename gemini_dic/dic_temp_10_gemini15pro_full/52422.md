didjeridoo:/ˌdɪdʒəriˈduː/ /ˌdɪdʒəriˈduː/
n.
迪吉里杜管
A long wooden wind instrument of the Australian Aborigines, traditionally made from a hollowed tree branch.
例句：He played a haunting melody on the didjeridoo. 他用迪吉里杜管演奏了一曲萦绕心头的旋律。

例句：The didjeridoo's deep drone resonated through the valley. 迪吉里杜管低沉的嗡嗡声在山谷中回荡。

近义词:drone pipe (嗡嗡管，泛指类似的乐器)
反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑其他乐器如flute笛子)
词性:noun
