creating:/kriˈeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 创造，创作；产生；引起 | Present participle of "create".  The act of making something new. |  She is creating a beautiful painting. 她正在创作一幅美丽的画。|  He is creating a new software program. 他正在创建一个新的软件程序。 | synonyms: making, producing, generating, originating, devising, inventing, designing, composing, forming, shaping, building, constructing, establishing, founding, instituting, inaugurating | antonyms: destroying, demolishing, ruining, eradicating, obliterating | verb
