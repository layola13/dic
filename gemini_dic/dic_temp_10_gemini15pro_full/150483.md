panting:/ˈpæn.tɪŋ/ /ˈpæn.t̬ɪŋ/
verb, adjective|喘气；气喘吁吁|breathing quickly and heavily, typically after exertion.|The dog was panting after its long run. 狗跑完长跑后气喘吁吁。|近义词：gasping, puffing, breathless|反义词:relaxed, calm|词性: verb, adjective
panting:/ˈpæn.tɪŋ/ /ˈpæn.t̬ɪŋ/
noun|喘气|the act of breathing quickly and heavily|The panting of the runner could be heard across the field.  可以听到跑步者在田野上喘气的声音。|近义词：gasps, puffs|反义词: calmness|词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle English *panten, from Old French panter "to pant," of imitative origin.
