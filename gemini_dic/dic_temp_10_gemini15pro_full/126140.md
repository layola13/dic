mutineer:/ˌmjuːtɪˈnɪər/ | n. | 叛变者，反叛者 | A person who takes part in a mutiny; a rebel. |  The mutineers took control of the ship. 叛变者控制了这艘船。

The captain ordered the arrest of the mutineers. 船长下令逮捕叛变者。

| 近义词: rebel, insurgent, revolutionary | 反义词: loyalist, conformist | 词性: noun
