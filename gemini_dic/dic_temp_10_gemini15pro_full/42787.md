couping:/ˈkuːpɪŋ/
n.
政变;颠覆;推翻
A sudden, violent, and illegal seizure of power from a government.
The military coup resulted in the overthrow of the democratically elected government. 军事政变导致民选政府被推翻。

The coup d'état was swiftly executed. 政变迅速执行。

近义词:overthrow, takeover, putsch, rebellion, revolt
反义词:restoration, reinstatement

词性:名词
