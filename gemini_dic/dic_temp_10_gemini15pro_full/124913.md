mudguard:/ˈmʌdɡɑːrd/ (US) /ˈmʌdɡɑːd/ (UK)| n. |挡泥板；泥罩|A curved piece of metal or plastic fitted above or behind a wheel of a vehicle to protect it from mud and water splashes.|例句：The car's mudguard was damaged in the accident. 汽车的挡泥板在事故中损坏了。|近义词：fender, splash guard|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
