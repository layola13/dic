nondeprecating:/ˌnɒnˈdeprɪkeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不自贬的，不谦虚的，不贬低的 | Not characterized by or expressing belittling of oneself; not self-deprecating. |  He spoke about his accomplishments in a nondeprecating manner. 他以不自贬的方式谈论了他的成就。 |  He gave a nondeprecating assessment of his own abilities. 他对自己能力的评估并不谦虚。 | 近义词: confident, assertive, self-assured, proud | 反义词: self-deprecating, modest, humble | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + deprecating (from deprecate, meaning "to express disapproval of")
