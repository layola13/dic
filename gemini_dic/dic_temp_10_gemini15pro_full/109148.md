legislatorially:/ˌlɛdʒɪsˈleɪtərəli/
adv.
立法地；就立法而言
In a legislative manner; by means of legislation.
The issue was handled legislatorially, through the passage of a new law.  这个问题是通过立法方式处理的，通过了一项新法律。
The matter was considered legislatorially.  这件事是从立法的角度来考虑的。
近义词: by law, legally, through legislation
反义词: illegally, extra-legally, judicially
词性: adverb
