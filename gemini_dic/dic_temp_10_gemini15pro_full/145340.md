overbuild:/ˌōvərˈbɪld/| v. |过度建设，建造过多|To build too much (housing, offices, etc.) in an area.|They overbuilt the area with cheap apartments. 他们在该地区过度建造了廉价公寓。|近义词：overdevelop|反义词：underdevelop|词性：动词
overbuild:/ˈōvərˌbɪld/| n. |过度建设，建筑过剩|Excessive construction, especially of housing or offices.|The overbuild in the city center led to a decline in property values. 市中心的过度建设导致房地产价值下跌。|近义词：overdevelopment|反义词：underdevelopment|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + build.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much." "Build" comes from Old English "buildan," meaning "to construct."
