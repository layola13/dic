fish-out-of-water:/ˌfɪʃ aʊt əv ˈwɔːtər/ | n. |  格格不入的人；不得其所的人 | Someone who is in a situation they are not used to and feels uncomfortable |  He felt like a fish out of water at the fancy dinner party. 他在那场奢华的晚宴上感到格格不入。|近义词：misfit, outsider |反义词: insider, native | noun

fish-out-of-water:/ˌfɪʃ aʊt əv ˈwɔːtər/ | adj. |  格格不入的；不得其所的 | Feeling uncomfortable or awkward because you are in surroundings that are not familiar | She looked fish out of water in her hiking boots and elegant dress. 她穿着登山靴和优雅的连衣裙，看起来格格不入。 | 近义词: awkward, uncomfortable, out of place | 反义词: comfortable, at ease, at home | adjective
