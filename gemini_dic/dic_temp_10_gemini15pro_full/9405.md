antiracial:/ˌæntiˈreɪʃəl/
adjective
反种族主义的
Opposing or working against racism.
例句：The organization held an antiracial demonstration.  该组织举行了一场反种族主义示威。

例句：She's known for her antiracial activism. 她以反种族主义行动主义而闻名。

近义词：anti-racist, against racism
反义词：racist, discriminatory
词性：adjective

词源：anti- (against) + racial (relating to race)
