dissertator:/ˈdɪsərteɪtər/| n.|论文撰写者，专题论文作者|A person who writes a dissertation, especially one submitted for a doctorate.|例句：The dissertator spent years researching their topic.  这位论文作者花了数年时间研究他们的主题。

例句：The dissertator defended their dissertation successfully. 论文作者成功地为自己的论文进行了答辩。|近义词：doctoral candidate, PhD student, researcher|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dissertator, from Latin dissertare “to discuss, debate,” from dis- “apart” + serere “to join, link.”  The original sense was "one who debates or discusses," later narrowing to "one who writes a formal treatise."
