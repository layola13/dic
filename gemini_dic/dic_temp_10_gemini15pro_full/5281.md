allophones:/ˈæləfoʊnz/ (US) /ˈæləfəʊnz/ (UK)| n.|音异音|Variations of a phoneme that do not distinguish meaning.  Different allophones of a phoneme occur in different phonetic environments.|The sounds /p/ in "pin" and "spin" are allophones; the first is aspirated and the second is not, but this difference does not change the meaning of the words.|近义词: variant, variation|反义词: phoneme|词性:Noun
