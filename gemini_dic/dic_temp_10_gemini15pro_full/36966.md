colporteur:/ˈkɒlpɔːtər/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlpɔːrtər/ (US)| n.|书商；挨户兜售小商品的商贩；传播流言蜚语的人|A person who travels from place to place selling books, religious tracts, or other small items, often door-to-door; also, someone who spreads rumors or gossip.|The colporteur traveled through the countryside, selling Bibles and religious pamphlets.|近义词：peddler, hawker, vendor, disseminator|反义词: buyer, purchaser|词性:noun

colport:/kəlˈpɔːrt/ (UK) /kəlˈpɔːrt/ (US)| v.|兜售；传播|To carry from place to place and offer for sale; to distribute or spread (news, rumors, etc.)|He colported his wares throughout the region.|近义词：peddle, hawk, vend, disseminate|反义词: withhold, suppress|词性:verb
