forebode:/fɔːrˈboʊd/ (US) /fɔːˈbəʊd/ (UK)| v. |预示; 预兆; 预感(尤指不祥之事)|To forebode is to act as a warning or omen of something bad or unpleasant in the future. |Dark clouds forebode a storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨。|The economic indicators forebode a recession. 这些经济指标预示着经济衰退。|synonyms: presage, portend, augur, foreshadow, foretell|antonyms: promise, assure|verb
