faffed:/fæft/
verb
浪费时间，闲混，瞎忙
spend time doing nothing important
例句：I faffed about all afternoon instead of doing my homework. 我整个下午都在闲混，而不是做作业。

例句：Stop faffing and get on with it! 别磨蹭了，赶紧开始吧！

近义词：dawdle, mess around, procrastinate, potter
反义词：hustle, work diligently
词性：v.
