distillment:/dɪˈstɪlmənt/ | n. | 蒸馏；蒸馏物；精华；提炼 | The process of distilling a liquid; something obtained by distilling. |  The distillment of whiskey requires careful temperature control. 威士忌的蒸馏需要仔细的温度控制。 | The rose oil is a fragrant distillment. 玫瑰油是一种芳香的蒸馏物。 | synonyms: distillation, essence, extract, concentrate | antonyms: dilution, admixture | noun

distillment:/dɪˈstɪlmənt/ | n. | 精华；提炼 | The most important or essential part of something. | The book contains the distillment of years of research. 这本书包含了多年研究的精华。 |  His speech was a distillment of his political philosophy. 他的演讲是他政治哲学的精髓。 | synonyms: quintessence, gist, summary, epitome | antonyms: entirety, whole, unexpurgated version | noun
