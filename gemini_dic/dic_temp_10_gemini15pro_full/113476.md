lyrical:/ˈlɪrɪk(ə)l/| adj.|抒情的；如歌的；充满诗意的|Expressing personal emotions in a way that is similar to poetry; expressing deep personal emotion or observations.|Her lyrical prose evokes a sense of wonder and nostalgia. 她抒情的散文唤起了一种惊奇和怀旧的感觉。|The dancer's lyrical movements captivated the audience.  舞者抒情的动作深深吸引了观众。|近义词：poetic, expressive, melodious, musical|反义词：prosaic, mundane, unimaginative|词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *lurikos* "pertaining to the lyre," from *lura* "lyre."  源自希腊语 *lurikos* “与里拉琴有关的”，来自 *lura* “里拉琴”。
