drive-wall:/ˈdraɪv wɔːl/ | n. |挡土墙；支护墙| A retaining wall built to support the sides of an excavation, typically for a road or railway. |  The drive-wall was essential for preventing landslides during the construction of the highway.  修建高速公路时，挡土墙对于防止山体滑坡至关重要。|Synonyms: retaining wall, breast wall, shoring wall | Antonyms: None | Noun

drive-wall:/ˈdraɪv wɔːl/ | n. |（矿井）巷道侧壁|The side wall of a mine shaft or tunnel.| Miners reinforced the drive-wall with timber supports. 矿工们用木支架加固了巷道侧壁。 | Synonyms: sidewall, tunnel wall, shaft wall | Antonyms: None | Noun
