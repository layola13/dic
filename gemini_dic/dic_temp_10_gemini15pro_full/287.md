abelmosk:/ˈæbəlˌmɒsk/ | n. |秋葵，黄葵|A tropical plant cultivated for its edible green seed pods, also called okra.  It also refers to the musk-like scent derived from the seeds.|The abelmosk in my garden is thriving. 我花园里的秋葵长势很好。|Synonym: okra, ambrette|Antonym: N/A|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Arabic 'al-misk' (musk) via Late Latin 'abelmoschus'.  The 'abel' part may be a corruption of a word meaning 'fragrant'.
