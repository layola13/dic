dittos:/ˈdɪtoʊz/
n.
同上；复制品；同前
the same thing(s) as what has just been mentioned.
"The findings of the study were dittoed in the later survey."  这项研究的结果在后来的调查中得到了重复。

"I need two of those, and three dittos of this." 我需要两个那个，还有三个这个（同上）。

synonyms: copies, duplicates, repeats, the same
antonyms: originals, differences, opposites
noun

"Can you ditto this page for me?" 你能帮我复制一下这一页吗？
