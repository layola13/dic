nonsusceptibly:/ˌnɑːnsəˈsɛptɪbli/ /ˌnɒnsəˈsɛptɪbli/
adjective
不易受影响地；无感觉地
not susceptible; incapable of being affected, influenced, or impressed.
The virus spread nonsusceptibly through the population, leaving many unaware they were infected. 病毒不易察觉地在人群中传播，许多人没有意识到自己被感染了。
The fortress was built nonsusceptibly to attack. 这座堡垒坚不可摧。

近义词: invulnerably, imperviously, unresponsively, resistantly
反义词: susceptibly, vulnerably, sensitively

词性: adjective
词源: From non- (not) + susceptible + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Susceptible comes from Late Latin susceptibilis, from Latin suscipere "to take up, undertake," from sub- "up" + capere "to take."
