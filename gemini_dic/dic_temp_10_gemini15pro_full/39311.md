coniine:/ˈkoʊniːn/ /ˈkɒniːn/| n.|毒芹碱|A poisonous alkaloid, C₈H₁₇N, extracted from poison hemlock (Conium maculatum) and causing paralysis.  It is a neurotoxin that blocks the nicotinic acetylcholine receptor.|Socrates was executed by drinking a hemlock concoction containing coniine. 苏格拉底被赐饮含有毒芹碱的毒芹汁而死。

Coniine can cause respiratory paralysis and death. 毒芹碱会导致呼吸麻痹和死亡。|近义词：neurotoxin, poison|反义词: antidote|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cōnīna, from Latin cōnium (“hemlock”) + -īna.  Ultimately from Ancient Greek κώνειον (kṓneion, “hemlock”).
