exterminate: /ɪkˈstɜːmɪneɪt/ /ɪkˈstɝːmɪneɪt/
verb
消灭，根除
to kill all the members of a group of people or animals
例句：The Nazis exterminated six million Jews. 纳粹杀害了六百万犹太人。

例句：Rats and other vermin must be exterminated from food preparation areas. 老鼠和其他害虫必须从食物准备区清除。
近义词：annihilate, eradicate, wipe out, destroy
反义词：preserve, conserve, protect
词性：v.


exterminated: /ɪkˈstɜːmɪneɪtɪd/ /ɪkˈstɝːmɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
被消灭的，被根除的
having been completely destroyed
例句：The exterminated species will never be seen again.  被灭绝的物种将再也见不到了。

例句：The building was fumigated to ensure all pests were exterminated.  这栋建筑物经过熏蒸以确保所有害虫都被消灭。
近义词：destroyed, eradicated, annihilated
反义词：preserved, conserved, protected
词性：adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin exterminatus, past participle of exterminare "to drive out, banish, destroy," from ex- "out" + terminus "boundary, limit."
