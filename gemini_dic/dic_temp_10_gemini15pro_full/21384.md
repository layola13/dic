blotted:/ˈblɒtɪd/ (UK) /ˈblɑːtɪd/ (US)| adj.|被弄脏的，吸干墨水的，（脸）涨红的，喝醉的|covered with or marked by blots; having absorbed ink or other liquid; (of a person's face) red and swollen, typically as a result of illness, heat, or strong emotion; drunk.|例句：His shirt was blotted with ink. 他的衬衫沾满了墨水。|例句：Her face was blotted and tear-stained. 她的脸涨红且满是泪痕。|近义词：stained, smeared, spotted, blotchy, inky, tipsy, drunk, intoxicated|反义词：clean, spotless, sober|词性:形容词

blot:/blɒt/ (UK) /blɑːt/ (US)| n. v.|污点，墨渍；吸墨纸；缺点，污点；吸干；弄脏|a spot or stain of ink or other liquid; a piece of blotting paper; a fault or flaw which spoils something; absorb or cause to absorb liquid; dry (ink) using blotting paper; make a blot or blots on (something).|例句：There was a large ink blot on the document. 文件上有一大块墨渍。|例句：He blotted the signature with a piece of tissue. 他用一张纸巾吸干了签名上的墨水。|近义词：stain, smear, spot, blemish, flaw, defect, blotch|反义词：cleanliness, spotlessness|词性:名词, 动词
