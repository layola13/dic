implosive:/ɪmˈploʊsɪv/ /ɪmˈpləʊsɪv/ | n. |内爆音；闭塞音|A consonant sound produced by stopping the breath and then releasing it suddenly, such as p, t, and k.|The linguist explained the difference between plosives and implosives. 语言学家解释了爆破音和内爆音的区别。|Synonyms: plosive, stop consonant|Antonyms: - |noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin implodere (to burst inwards), from in- (inwards) + plaudere (to clap, strike).
