explat:/ɪkˈsplæt/ |v. |解释; 说明; 阐明| To explain or make something clear, often in a detailed way. |  He explated the complex theory in simple terms. 他用简单的术语解释了这个复杂的理论。

She explated her reasons for leaving the company. 她解释了她离开公司的原因。

|近义词：explain, clarify, elucidate, interpret, expound |反义词：obfuscate, confuse, mystify |词性：动词
