oz's:/ˈɑːzɪz/| n.|盎司（复数的所有格）|A possessive form indicating ownership of multiple ounces.  It refers to something belonging to or associated with a quantity of ounces.|例句：The baby drank six oz's of milk.  婴儿喝了六盎司牛奶。|近义词:None (oz's is a unique possessive form)|反义词:None|词性:名词
