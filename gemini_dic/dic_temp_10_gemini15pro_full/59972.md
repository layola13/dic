edifying:/ˈɛdɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
启迪的；有教益的
Providing moral or intellectual instruction.
例句：I found the lecture to be very edifying. 我发现这个讲座很有启发性。

例句：Reading good literature can be an edifying experience. 阅读优秀的文学作品可以是一次有益的经历。

近义词：instructive, enlightening, informative, inspiring
反义词：unhelpful, unenlightening, frivolous, vapid
词性：adjective

词源：From Late Middle English: from Old French edifier, from late Latin aedificāre ‘build’, from aedes ‘building’ + -ficāre (from facere ‘to make’). The original sense was ‘to build up morally or spiritually’, later ‘to instruct, improve’.
