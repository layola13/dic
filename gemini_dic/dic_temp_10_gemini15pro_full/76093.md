furrowed:/ˈfɜːroʊd/ (US) /ˈfʌrəʊd/ (UK)| adj.| 皱纹的，起皱的|  Marked with lines or wrinkles, like a furrowed brow.  Characterized by deep wrinkles, typically as a result of age, worry, or concentration. | His furrowed brow showed his concern.  他皱起的眉头显示出他的担忧。|Synonyms: wrinkled, creased, lined, grooved | Antonyms: smooth, unlined, unwrinkled | word type: adjective
