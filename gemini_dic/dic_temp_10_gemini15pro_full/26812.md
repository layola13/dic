cagefuls:/ˈkeɪdʒfʊlz/
n.
满满几笼；几笼之量
Several cagefuls of chickens were transported to the market.  几笼鸡被运到市场。
English Explanation:  The plural form of "cageful," meaning the quantity that fills a cage or several cages.  It refers to the contents of the cage(s), typically animate objects like animals or birds.
Example: The shelter had several cagefuls of rescued kittens.  收容所里有几笼获救的小猫。
Synonyms: cages full of, loads of (informal), a lot of (informal)
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider) a few, a handful
Word Class: Noun
