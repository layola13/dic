ceroon:/səˈruːn/
n.
a large wicker basket or hamper.
A large wicker basket, especially one used for packing dried fruit (such as figs or raisins).
The farmers packed the dates into ceroons for shipping.  农民将椰枣装进柳条筐以便运输。
Synonyms: hamper, basket, crate
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a physical object.  Could consider concepts like "container," with antonym "emptiness")
