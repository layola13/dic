endosmose:/ˌɛnˈdɒzməʊs/ /ˌɛnˈdɑːzməʊs/
noun
内渗；渗透
The inward passage of a fluid through a membrane.
Water enters the roots of plants by endosmosis. 水通过内渗作用进入植物根部。
近义词: osmosis, inward diffusion
反义词: exosmosis
词性: n.

Etymology: From French *endosmose*, from Ancient Greek ἔνδον (*éndon*, “within”) + ὠσμός (*ōsmós*, “pushing, thrusting”).
