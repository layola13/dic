overzealous:/ˌoʊvərˈzeləs/| adj.|过分热心的，过度热情的|Characterized by excessive zeal; fanatical.|He was overzealous in his pursuit of the suspect, causing unnecessary damage. 他在追捕嫌疑犯时过于狂热，造成了不必要的损害。|近义词：fanatical, fervent, enthusiastic, impassioned, passionate|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic|词性: adjective

overzeal:/ˌoʊvərˈziːl/| n.|过度热情，过分热心|Excessive zeal; excessive fervor.|His overzeal led him to make several costly mistakes. 他的过度热情导致他犯了几个代价高昂的错误。|近义词：fanaticism, fervor, enthusiasm, passion|反义词：apathy, indifference, lack of enthusiasm|词性: noun
