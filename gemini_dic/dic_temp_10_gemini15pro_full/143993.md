outrates:/ˌaʊtˈreɪts/
verb
超过，胜过
To surpass in rate or degree; to exceed.
Electric cars are outrating gasoline cars in sales in some countries. 在一些国家，电动汽车的销量超过了汽油车。
The new model outrates the old one in fuel efficiency. 新模型在燃油效率方面超过了旧模型。
近义词: surpasses, exceeds, outstrips, outpaces
反义词: lags behind, trails, falls short of
词性: v.
