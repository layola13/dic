moth-eaten:/ˈmɔːθˌiːtn/ | adj. | 虫蛀的，破旧的 | Damaged or worn away, as if by moths. |  The old coat was moth-eaten and unusable. 这件旧外套被虫蛀了，不能再穿了。 |  The curtains were moth-eaten and dusty. 窗帘被虫蛀了，满是灰尘。 | 近义词:worn, dilapidated, shabby, threadbare | 反义词:pristine, new, immaculate | 词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *motheten, equivalent to moth +‎ -eaten.
