geophagous:/dʒiˈɒfəɡəs/ | adj. | 食土的，食泥的 |  Eating earth or soil; specifically, habitually eating clay or dirt.  This behavior is observed in some animals and humans, often to supplement mineral intake or to soothe digestive upset. |  Some parrots are geophagous, consuming clay to neutralize toxins in their diet. 一些鹦鹉有食土癖，它们吃粘土来中和食物中的毒素。

The pregnant woman experienced geophagia, craving and eating soil. 这位孕妇经历了异食癖，渴望并吃土。 | 近义词:clay-eating, soil-eating | 反义词:  N/A (No direct antonym) | 词性: adjective
