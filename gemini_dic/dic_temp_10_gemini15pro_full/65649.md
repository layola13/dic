evaporated:/ɪˈvæpəreɪtɪd/
verb
蒸发的，挥发的
having disappeared; vanished.  transformed into vapor.
The water evaporated in the sun.  水在阳光下蒸发了。
近义词:  disappeared, vanished, dissipated, vaporized
反义词: condensed, materialized, solidified
词性: verb (past participle)
词源: From Latin evaporatus, past participle of evaporare ("to exhale, emit as vapor"), from e- ("out, forth") + vapor ("vapor, steam").
