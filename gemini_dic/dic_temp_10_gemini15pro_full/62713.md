enging: /ˈɛndʒɪŋ/ | n. | 结尾，结局 | The ending of the movie was surprising.  电影的结局令人惊讶。 |  The novel has a happy ending. 这部小说有一个幸福的结局。| 近义词: conclusion, finish, termination | 反义词: beginning, start, commencement | 词性:名词

词源:  From Middle English *endynge*, from Old English *endung*, equivalent to *end* + *-ing*.  来自中古英语 *endynge*，源自古英语 *endung*，相当于 *end* + *-ing*。
