haversack: /ˈhævərsæk/ | n. | 背包，干粮袋 | A bag, typically made of canvas or other strong material, carried on the back or over the shoulder and used for carrying provisions or personal belongings. |  The soldier carried his rations in a haversack. 士兵把他的口粮装在背包里。|  backpack, rucksack, knapsack | suitcase, briefcase | noun
Etymology: From the French *havre-sac*, meaning "oat sack," from *havre* "oats" and *sac* "sack."
