deductively:/dɪˈdʌktɪvli/
adverb
演绎地，推论地
by reasoning from general principles to specific conclusions.
例句：
The conclusion was reached deductively.  这个结论是通过演绎推理得出的。

He approached the problem deductively. 他用演绎法处理这个问题。

近义词：logically, rationally, analytically
反义词：inductively
词性: adverb
