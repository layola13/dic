overflouring: /ˌoʊvərˈflaʊərɪŋ/
Noun
过度撒粉
The act of using too much flour, typically in baking, resulting in a dry or tough final product.
The cake was dry and crumbly due to overflouring.  蛋糕因为撒了太多面粉而变得干巴巴的，容易碎。
The biscuits were tough because of overflouring.  饼干因为面粉放太多而很硬。
