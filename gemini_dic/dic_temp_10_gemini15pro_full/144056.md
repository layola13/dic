outlive:/ˌaʊtˈlɪv/ /ˌaʊtˈlɪv/| v. | 比...活得长；比...持久；经受住；渡过 | to live or exist longer than someone or something|  例句：She outlived her husband by ten years. 她比她丈夫多活了十年。


例句：This building has outlived its usefulness. 这栋建筑已经失去了它的作用。


近义词：survive, outlast| 反义词: precede, predate |词性:动词

outrive:/ˌaʊtˈraɪv/ /ˌaʊtˈraɪv/| v. | 比…活得长; 比…更成功; 超越 | to surpass or excel; to outdo or defeat in competition or rivalry | 例句：He outrived all his rivals in business. 他在生意上比所有竞争对手都成功。


例句：The new technology will outrive the old one. 新技术将超越旧技术。


近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, excel | 反义词: fail, lose, fall behind |词性:动词
