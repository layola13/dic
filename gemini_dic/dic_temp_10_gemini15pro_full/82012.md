gripingness:/ˈɡraɪpɪŋnɪs/
n.
抱怨的倾向；爱抱怨的性质
The tendency to complain; the quality of being given to complaining.
He was known for his gripingness and constant dissatisfaction. 他因爱抱怨和持续不满而闻名。
Her gripingness about the weather became tiresome. 她对天气的抱怨变得令人厌烦。
complaining, grumbling, whining, carping, faultfinding
contentment, satisfaction
noun
