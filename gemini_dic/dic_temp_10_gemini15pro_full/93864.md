immoderately:/ɪˈmɑːdərətli/ | adv. | 过度地，无节制地 |  exceeding just or reasonable limits; excessive. | He drank immoderately and suffered the consequences. 他饮酒无度，自食其果。| excessively, unduly, exorbitantly | moderately, reasonably | adverb

immoderate:/ɪˈmɑːdərət/ | adj. | 过度的，无节制的 | not moderate; excessive. | Immoderate drinking can lead to health problems. 过度饮酒会导致健康问题。| excessive, extreme, unrestrained | moderate, reasonable, temperate | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *immoderatus, from in- "not" + moderatus "moderate," past participle of moderari "to moderate," from modus "measure."
