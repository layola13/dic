averred:/əˈvɜːrd/
verb
断言；坚称；肯定地说
state something clearly and strongly
The witness averred that he had seen the defendant at the scene of the crime. 目击者声称他在犯罪现场看到了被告。

He averred his innocence. 他坚称自己无辜。

The lawyer averred that his client was not guilty. 律师声称他的客户无罪。

近义词: asserted, declared, affirmed, maintained, stated
反义词: denied, refuted, contradicted
词性: v.
