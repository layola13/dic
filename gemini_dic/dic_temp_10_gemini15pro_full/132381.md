nondemanding:/ˌnɒndɪˈmɑːndɪŋ/ | /ˌnɑːndɪˈmændɪŋ/
adjective |  不苛求的；要求不高的 | Not requiring much effort or attention; easy to deal with. |  The job is nondemanding and allows me plenty of free time.  这份工作要求不高，让我有很多空闲时间。 |  undemanding, easy, simple, low-maintenance | demanding, challenging, difficult | adjective

nondemanding:/ˌnɒndɪˈmɑːndɪŋ/ | /ˌnɑːndɪˈmændɪŋ/
Etymology | From non- + demanding.  "Demanding" comes from the Old French word "demander," meaning "to ask, require." The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning. | 词源来自non- + demanding。“Demanding”来自古法语单词“demander”，意思是“要求”。前缀“non-”只是否定其含义。
