maiorum:/maɪˈɔːrʊm/
noun
of (one's) ancestors; ancestral
Relating to or characteristic of one's ancestors, especially with regard to customs, values, or traditions.
Cicero often spoke of the wisdom of the *maiorum*. 西塞罗经常谈到祖先的智慧。
