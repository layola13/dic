graziers:/ˈɡreɪʒərz/
n.
牧场主，放牧者
People who raise grazing livestock.
The graziers moved their herds to higher ground for the summer. 牧场主们把他们的牛群赶到更高的地方过夏天。
近义词：ranchers, stockmen, pastoralists
反义词: None (No direct opposite)
词性: Noun


grazier:/ˈɡreɪʒər/
n.
牧场主，放牧者
A person who grazes livestock.
The grazier carefully rotated his cattle through different pastures.  这位牧场主小心地让他的牛轮流在不同的牧场上吃草。
近义词：rancher, stockman, pastoralist
反义词: None (No direct opposite)
词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From graze (v.) + -ier. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *grasōną.
