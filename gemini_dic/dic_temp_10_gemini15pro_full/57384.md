dozedness:/ˈdoʊzɪdnəs/| n. |困倦，昏昏欲睡|A state of being sleepy or drowsy.|例句：The dozedness after a big meal made it hard to concentrate.  午饭后昏昏欲睡，很难集中注意力。

例句：He struggled to overcome his dozedness during the long drive.  在长途驾驶中，他努力克服困倦。|近义词：drowsiness, sleepiness, somnolence|反义词：wakefulness, alertness|词性: noun

Etymology: From doze (v.) + -ness (suffix forming nouns of state or quality).  Doze ultimately comes from the Middle Dutch *dusen.
