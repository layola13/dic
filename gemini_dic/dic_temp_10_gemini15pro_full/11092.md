archaizes:/ˈɑːrkeɪˌaɪzɪz/
verb
模仿古体; Archaize means to give something a deliberately archaic quality, often in language or style.
The author archaizes his language to create a sense of historical authenticity.  作者模仿古体来营造一种历史的真实感。
synonyms:  antiquate,  classicize,  retrogress
antonyms: modernize, update
词性: verb


archaizing:/ˈɑːrkeɪˌaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
(使)带有古风的; Archaizing describes something that imitates or evokes an older style, especially in language or art.
Her archaizing style of writing can be charming, but also a bit confusing for modern readers. 她带有古风的写作风格可能很吸引人，但也让现代读者有点困惑。
synonyms:  antique, antiquated,  old-fashioned
antonyms: modern, contemporary
词性: adjective
