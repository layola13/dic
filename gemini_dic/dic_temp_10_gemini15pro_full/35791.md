cockcrow:/ˈkɒkˌkroʊ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkˌkroʊ/ (US)| n.|鸡鸣，黎明|The sound of a rooster crowing, especially at dawn.  It often symbolizes the start of a new day.|The farmer woke at cockcrow.  农民鸡鸣时就醒了。|Synonyms: daybreak, dawn, sunrise, first light|Antonyms: sunset, dusk, nightfall|noun
