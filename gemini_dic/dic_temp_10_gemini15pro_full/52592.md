digastric: /daɪˈɡæstrɪk/ | adj. n. | 二腹肌的；二腹肌 |  Formed of two parts, with a central tendon joining the two fleshy bellies; relating to the digastric muscle.  A muscle with two fleshy parts separated by a tendon, especially a muscle attached to the hyoid bone and lower jaw that opens the mouth. | The digastric muscle is involved in swallowing and speaking.  二腹肌参与吞咽和说话。 | The surgeon carefully dissected the digastric muscle. 外科医生仔细地解剖了二腹肌。 | synonyms: biventral | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for an anatomical term like this) | adjective, noun
