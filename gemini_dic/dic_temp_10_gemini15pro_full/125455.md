multipliable:/ˈmʌltɪplaɪəbl/| adj.|可乘的；可增加的|Capable of being multiplied.  Something that can be increased in number by being multiplied.|The number 10 is multipliable by 2 and 5.|近义词:reproducible, duplicable|反义词:indivisible, irreducible|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *multiplicabilis, from Latin multiplicare (“to multiply”) + -bilis (“-able”). Ultimately from multus (“many”) + plicare (“to fold”).
