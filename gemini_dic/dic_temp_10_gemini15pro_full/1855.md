acronym:/ˈækrənɪm/ | n. |首字母缩略词；缩写词 | An acronym is a word formed from the first letter of each word in a series of words.  |  Example:  NATO is an acronym for the North Atlantic Treaty Organization. 北约是北大西洋公约组织的首字母缩写词。

Example:  LASER is an acronym for Light Amplification by Stimulated Emission of Radiation. 激光是“受激辐射光放大”的首字母缩写词。 | 近义词: abbreviation, initialism | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *akros* "tip, end" + *onyma* "name."  Borrowed into English in the early 20th century. 源于希腊语 *akros* “尖端，末端” + *onyma* “名称”。20世纪初借入英语。
