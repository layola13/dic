degradabilities:/dɪˌɡreɪdəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可降解性；生物降解性|The plural form of degradability, referring to the different rates or extents to which various materials can be broken down by natural processes.|The degradabilities of different plastics vary widely, with some breaking down in months while others persist for centuries.|近义词：biodegradabilities, decomposabilities|反义词：inertness, permanence|词性:名词
