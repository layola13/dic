modified:/ˈmɒdɪfaɪd/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdɪfaɪd/ (US)| adj.|修改的，改进的|Changed or altered, especially to make something more suitable or effective.|例句：The car has a modified engine. 这辆车的引擎改装过。|近义词：altered, changed, adapted, adjusted, amended|反义词：original, unchanged, unaltered|词性:形容词

modified:/ˈmɒdɪfaɪd/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdɪfaɪd/ (US)| v. (past tense and past participle)|修改，改进|(past tense and past participle of modify) To make partial or minor changes to something, typically so as to improve it or to make it less extreme.|例句：They modified the original plan to suit the client's needs. 他们修改了原计划以适应客户的需求。|近义词：alter, change, adapt, adjust, amend|反义词：maintain, preserve, keep|词性:动词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin modificatus ‘limited, measured’, past participle of modificare, from Latin modus ‘measure’ + facere ‘make’.
