dislodge:/dɪsˈlɒdʒ/ /dɪsˈlɑːdʒ/| vt.|驱逐；使移位；把…移动|To remove something or someone, often with difficulty, from a fixed position.|The earthquake dislodged several boulders from the cliff face. 地震使几块巨石从悬崖面上移位。|Synonyms: remove, displace, shift, oust, expel|Antonyms: fix, secure, lodge, embed|verb

dislodge:/dɪsˈlɒdʒ/ /dɪsˈlɑːdʒ/| n.|移位；脱离|The act of removing something or someone from a fixed position.|The dislodge of the king caused a political crisis. 国王的移位引发了政治危机。|Synonyms: displacement, removal, shift|Antonyms: fixture, placement, embedding|noun
