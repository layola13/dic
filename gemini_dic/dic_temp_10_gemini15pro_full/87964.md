high-flown:/haɪˈfloʊn/
adj.
夸张的，浮夸的，高调的
Excessively elaborate or refined in language or style; pretentious.  Characterized by elevated or pompous language.
She delivered a high-flown speech that impressed no one. 她发表了一篇浮夸的演讲，却 никого не впечатлила.

He used high-flown language to try and impress his date. 他用浮夸的语言试图给他的约会对象留下深刻印象。

近义词：bombastic, pretentious, inflated, grandiose, ostentatious
反义词：simple, plain, unpretentious, down-to-earth, modest
词性：adjective
