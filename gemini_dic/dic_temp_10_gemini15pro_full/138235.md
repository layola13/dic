nostalgia: /nɒˈstældʒə/ (UK) /nɑːˈstældʒə/ (US)| n. |怀旧，念旧|A sentimental longing or wistful affection for the past, typically for a period or place with happy personal associations.|例句：She felt a pang of nostalgia for her childhood. 她感到一阵对童年的怀念。

例句：The old photographs evoked a feeling of nostalgia.  这些老照片唤起了一股怀旧之情。

近义词：homesickness, longing, yearning, sentimentality|反义词: apathy, indifference |词性: noun
