dwarflike:/ˈdwɔːrflaɪk/| adj.|矮小的，侏儒似的|Resembling a dwarf in size or form; unusually small or stunted.|The bonsai tree, with its dwarflike proportions, was a miniature marvel. 这棵盆景树，以其矮小的比例，是一个微型奇迹。|The child's dwarflike stature was concerning to the doctors.  这个孩子矮小的身材让医生们很担心。|近义词：diminutive, miniature, stunted, pygmy, Lilliputian|反义词：giant, enormous, huge, gigantic, colossal|词性:形容词

词源:
From *dwarf* + *-like*. *Dwarf* comes from Old English *dweorg*, of Germanic origin, related to Old High German *twerg*. *-like* is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling," "characteristic of," from Old English *-līc*, of Germanic origin, related to Dutch *-lijk* and German *-lich*.
