imprescriptibility:/ˌɪmprɪˌskrɪptɪˈbɪlɪti/
n.
不可剥夺性；不受时效限制
The quality or condition of being imprescriptible; not subject to prescription or the loss of rights or property through lapse of time.
The imprescriptibility of human rights is a fundamental principle of international law. 人权的不可剥夺性是国际法的基本原则。
The lawyer argued for the imprescriptibility of the claim. 律师辩称该索赔不受时效限制。
Synonyms: inalienability, unassailability
Antonyms: forfeitability, revocability
noun
