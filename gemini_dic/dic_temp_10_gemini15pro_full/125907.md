musculous: /ˈmʌskjʊləs/ (US), /ˈmʌskjʊləs/ (UK)| adj.|肌肉发达的，强壮的|Having well-developed muscles; strong.|He was a tall, musculous man. 他是一个高大强壮的男人。|The boxer had a musculous physique.  这个拳击手有着强壮的体格。|近义词：brawny, muscular, sinewy, strong, powerful|反义词：weak, frail, feeble|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin musculosus, from Latin musculus ‘muscle’.
