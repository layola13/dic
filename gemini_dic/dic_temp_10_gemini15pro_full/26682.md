cadaverous:/kəˈdævərəs/| adj.|形容词|憔悴的，苍白的，像尸体的|Resembling a corpse; pale, gaunt, and thin.|He had a cadaverous appearance after his illness. 他生病后脸色苍白，形容枯槁。|synonyms: gaunt, haggard, emaciated, skeletal, ghastly, pallid, wan, ashen|antonyms: healthy, robust, ruddy,  florid|Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin cadaverosus, from cadaver ‘corpse’.
