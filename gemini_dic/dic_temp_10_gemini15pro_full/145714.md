overcovetousness: /ˌoʊvərˈkʌvɪtəsnəs/
noun
过度贪婪，贪得无厌
Excessive greed; an insatiable desire for more than one needs or deserves.
He was consumed by overcovetousness, always wanting more wealth and power. 他被过度贪婪所吞噬，总是想要更多的财富和权力。
The king's overcovetousness led to the impoverishment of his people. 国王的贪得无厌导致了他的人民的贫困。
近义词: avarice, greed, rapacity
反义词: generosity, contentment, altruism
词性: noun

词源:
From "over-" (excessive) + "covetousness" (desire for possessions). "Covetousness" comes from the verb "covet" (Old French *cuveitier), ultimately from Latin "cupiditas" (desire, passion).
