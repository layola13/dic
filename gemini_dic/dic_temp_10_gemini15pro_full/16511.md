baristi:/bɑːˈriːstiː/
n.
咖啡师(复数)
Plural of barista, referring to multiple people who are skilled in making and serving coffee.
The baristi at this cafe are all highly trained.这家咖啡馆的咖啡师都受过良好的培训。
近义词:coffee makers, coffee servers
反义词:(无直接反义词，可考虑 customer, patron)
词性:名词


barista:/bɑːˈriːstə/
n.
咖啡师

The barista made a perfect latte art.  这位咖啡师制作了完美的拿铁拉花。
近义词:coffee maker, coffee server
反义词:(无直接反义词，可考虑 customer, patron)
词性:名词
