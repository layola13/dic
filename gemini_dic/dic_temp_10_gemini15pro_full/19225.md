bezoartical:/ˌbezɔːrˈtɪkəl/
adjective

Relating to or resembling a bezoar; formed of a bezoar.
A bezoar is a solid mass found trapped in the gastrointestinal system, often composed of hair or fiber. Bezoartical refers to something resembling or composed of these masses.
