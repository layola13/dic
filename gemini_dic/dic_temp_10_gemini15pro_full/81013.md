gradual:/ˈɡrædʒuəl/| adj.|逐渐的，逐步的|Happening slowly over a long period; not sudden.|例句：The hospital reported a gradual improvement in the patient's condition. 医院报告病人的情况逐渐好转。

例句：There has been a gradual increase in the price of oil. 油价逐渐上涨。|近义词：slow, steady, progressive, incremental|反义词：sudden, abrupt, rapid, immediate|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *gradualis*, from Latin gradus "step, degree".
