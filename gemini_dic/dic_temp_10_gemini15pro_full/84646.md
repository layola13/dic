handbagged:/ˈhændˌbæɡd/| adj.|用手提包猛击; (尤指女性)言辞激烈地批评; 严厉斥责|Criticized harshly, especially a woman, often in a public setting.  Originally referring to being hit with a handbag.  Figuratively, it implies a verbal attack that is forceful and pointed.|The politician was thoroughly handbagged by the journalists at the press conference.  这位政客在新闻发布会上被记者们严厉斥责。|The actress handbagged the director for his insensitive remarks.  这位女演员严厉批评了导演的麻木不仁的言论。|Synonyms:  criticized, rebuked, lambasted, castigated, dressed down|Antonyms: praised, lauded, commended|adjective
