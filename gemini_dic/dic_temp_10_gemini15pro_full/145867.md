overdescribe:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskraɪb/| v. |过度描述；过分详述|To describe something in excessive detail, often to the point of tedium.|She overdescribed the plot of the movie, spoiling the ending for me. 她过度描述了电影的情节，剧透了结局。|近义词：overexplain, elaborate excessively|反义词: understate, summarize|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From 'over-' (prefix meaning "excessively") + 'describe' (from Latin 'describere' - to write down).
