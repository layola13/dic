identism: /aɪˈdɛntɪzəm/
n.|认同主义，身份认同政治|A political approach that focuses on the identity of groups, particularly marginalized groups, and emphasizes the importance of recognizing and addressing their specific needs and experiences. It often involves advocating for policies that promote social justice and equality for these groups.|例句：Identism is a complex and often controversial topic in contemporary politics. 认同主义是当代政治中一个复杂且经常引起争议的话题。

例句：Critics argue that identism can lead to division and resentment between different groups. 批评人士认为，认同主义可能导致不同群体之间的分裂和怨恨。|近义词：identity politics, social justice activism|反义词：universalism, colorblindness|词性: noun
