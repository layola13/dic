murenger:/ˈmjʊərɪndʒər/| n.| 城墙管理员，城墙维修工|A person responsible for maintaining the walls of a city or fortified place.  Historically, a murenger was a town official in charge of the town walls and defenses.|The murenger inspected the city walls every week.  城墙管理员每周都要巡视城墙。|近义词：wall-keeper, warden, guardian|反义词: attacker, assailant|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-Norman *murengour, from Old French *murengeor, from mure (“wall”) + -eor (“-er”). Ultimately from Latin mūrus (“wall”).
