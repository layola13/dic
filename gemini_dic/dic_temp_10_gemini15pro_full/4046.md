aided:/ˈeɪdɪd/| v. adj.|辅助的，帮助的|past participle of aid, meaning having received help or assistance.  It describes something that has been done with help.|The computer-aided design made the project much easier.  电脑辅助设计使项目容易了很多。|近义词：assisted, helped, supported|反义词:unaided, hindered|verb (past participle), adjective
