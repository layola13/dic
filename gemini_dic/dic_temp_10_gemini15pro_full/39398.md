conjuress:/ˈkʌndʒərɪs/ | n. | 女巫，女魔术师 | A woman who practices magic; a sorceress. |  She was accused of being a conjuress and using dark magic. 她被指控是一个女巫，使用黑魔法。

The conjuress brewed a potion said to grant eternal youth.  这个女巫调制了一种据说可以让人长生不老的药水。 | 近义词: sorceress, witch, enchantress | 反义词: None (lack of a direct antonym for a female practitioner of magic) | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From late Middle English *conjuresse*, from Anglo-Norman *conjuresse, conjurese*, feminine form of *conjureor, conjurour* (“conjurer”), equivalent to conjure +‎ -ess.
