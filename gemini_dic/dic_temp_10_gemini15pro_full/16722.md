barrows:/ˈbæroʊz/| n.| 古坟；坟冢；（尤指史前）墓穴|A mound of earth or stones over a burial site, especially one of prehistoric origin.  |例句：The archaeologists excavated several Bronze Age barrows. 考古学家挖掘了几个青铜时代的坟冢。

|例句：The barrows dotted the landscape, silent reminders of a forgotten past.  这些坟冢点缀着风景，默默地提醒着人们一段被遗忘的历史。|近义词：tombs, mounds, graves|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


barrow:/ˈbæroʊ/| n.| 手推车；独轮车|A cart with a single wheel at the front and two handles at the back, used for carrying loads. |例句：He wheeled the barrow full of bricks across the building site. 他推着装满砖块的手推车穿过建筑工地。

|例句：She used a barrow to transport the garden waste. 她用独轮车运送花园垃圾。|近义词：cart, wheelbarrow|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
