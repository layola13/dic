outdebate:/ˌaʊtdɪˈbeɪt/
verb
辩论胜过；在辩论中击败
To defeat someone in a debate.
She outdebated him on the issue of gun control. 她在枪支管制问题上在辩论中击败了他。
He outdebated his opponents with his well-researched arguments. 他用精心研究的论点在辩论中击败了对手。
Synonyms: outargue, defeat, best, conquer
Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
词性: 动词
