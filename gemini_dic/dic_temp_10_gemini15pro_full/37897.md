complaisantness:/kəmˈpleɪzəntnəs/| n.|殷勤；柔顺；讨好；彬彬有礼|The state or quality of being complaisant; a disposition or tendency to please or comply with others.|Her complaisantness made her a popular hostess, but some saw it as a lack of conviction.|近义词：agreeableness, obligingness, affability, courtesy, politeness|反义词：disagreeableness, rudeness, intractability, obstinacy|词性:名词

complaisant:/kəmˈpleɪzənt/| adj.|殷勤的；柔顺的；讨好的；彬彬有礼的|Showing a cheerful willingness to please; obliging.|He was complaisant towards his guests, always eager to fulfill their requests.|近义词：agreeable, obliging, affable, courteous, polite|反义词：disagreeable, rude, intractable, obstinate|词性:形容词
