lumpishness:/ˈlʌmpɪʃnəs/
n.
笨拙，粗鲁，愚钝
The state of being lumpish; awkwardness, clumsiness, or lack of grace.
His lumpishness made him an unlikely dancer. 他的笨拙使他不太可能成为一个舞者。
近义词:clumsiness, awkwardness, gaucheness, ungainliness
反义词:grace, elegance, agility, dexterity
词性:noun


lumpish:/ˈlʌmpɪʃ/
adj.
笨拙的，粗鲁的，愚钝的
Lacking grace or dexterity; clumsy; awkward.
He moved in a lumpish way. 他行动笨拙。
近义词:clumsy, awkward, gawky, ungainly
反义词:graceful, elegant, agile, dexterous
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From lump (n.) + -ish (adj. suffix) + -ness (n. suffix).  Lump ultimately from Proto-Germanic *lumpaz.
