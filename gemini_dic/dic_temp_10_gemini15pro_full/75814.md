fumidity:/fjuːˈmɪdɪti/
n.
潮湿，湿气
The state or quality of being fumid; smokiness; vaporousness.
The fumidity of the greenhouse made it difficult to breathe.  温室里的湿气使人难以呼吸。

近义词:dampness, humidity, moistness, mugginess
反义词:aridity, dryness

词性:noun

词源: From Latin *fūmiditās, from fūmidus (“smoky”) + -itās, equivalent to fumid +‎ -ity.
