countrymen:/ˈkʌntrimən/ (US), /ˈkʌntrimən/ (UK)| n. |同胞，同乡|A fellow citizen of a country, especially when speaking in a patriotic or formal context.  A man from one's own country.|例句：He called upon his countrymen to defend their nation. 他号召他的同胞保卫祖国。

例句：The president addressed his countrymen in a televised speech. 总统在电视讲话中向全国同胞发表了讲话。

近义词：compatriots, fellow citizens|反义词：foreigners, aliens|词性：noun
