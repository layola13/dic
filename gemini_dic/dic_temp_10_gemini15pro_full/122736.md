moire:/ˈmwɑːr; mɔːˈreɪ/| n.|云纹; 波纹绸|A shimmering pattern that appears when two or more patterned grids are superimposed, especially when the grids are slightly different sizes or orientations.|The dress had a subtle moire pattern. 这件衣服有淡淡的波纹图案。|Synonyms: shimmer, watermarked, chatoyancy|Antonyms: plain, flat, solid|noun

moire:/ˈmwɑːr; mɔːˈreɪ/| adj.|有波纹的; 波纹绸制的|Having a wavy or shimmering pattern.|She wore a moire silk gown. 她穿着一件波纹丝绸礼服。|Synonyms: shimmering, wavy, chatoyant|Antonyms: plain, flat, solid|adjective
