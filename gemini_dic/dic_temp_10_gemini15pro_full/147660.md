overpopulated:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːpjəleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 人口过密的，人口过多的 | Having too many people for the available resources. | The city is overpopulated and lacks affordable housing.  这个城市人口过密，缺乏经济适用房。| overcrowded, densely populated | underpopulated, sparsely populated | adjective

overpopulated:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːpjəleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 人口过密的，人口过多的 |  The island is overpopulated with tourists during the summer months.  这个岛在夏季人满为患。| overcrowded, teeming | underpopulated, sparsely populated | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- (meaning "excessively") + populate (from Latin populare "to inhabit") + -ed (past participle suffix).
