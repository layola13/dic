lethiferous:/ləˈθɪfərəs/
adjective
致命的，致死的
Causing or capable of causing death; deadly.
The lethiferous venom of the cobra quickly paralyzed its prey.  眼镜蛇致命的毒液迅速麻痹了它的猎物。
The assassin used a lethiferous dose of poison. 刺客使用了致命剂量的毒药。

近义词: deadly, fatal, mortal, lethal
反义词: life-giving, vivifying, harmless, innocuous
词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *lēthifer, from lētum (“death”) + -fer (“bearing”).
