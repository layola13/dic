nontoxically:/ˌnɑːnˈtɑːksɪkli/
adverb
无毒地
in a way that is not poisonous or harmful.
This cleaning product cleans nontoxically.  这种清洁产品以无毒的方式清洁。
The surfaces were cleaned nontoxically.  这些表面被无毒地清洁了。
近义词:safely, harmlessly
反义词:toxically, poisonously
词性:adverb

词源:
non- (prefix) + toxic (adjective) + -ally (adverb suffix)
"non-" 表示“不”或“非”，"toxic" 表示“有毒的”，"-ally" 是副词后缀，表示“以...方式”。
