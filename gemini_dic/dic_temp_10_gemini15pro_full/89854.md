hornswoggle:/hɔːrnˈswɑːɡəl/ /hɔːrnˈswɒɡəl/
verb
欺骗，哄骗
to deceive or trick someone in a playful or mischievous way
He hornswoggled me out of my last dollar. 他骗走了我最后一块钱。
The salesman hornswoggled them into buying a used car.  推销员哄骗他们买了一辆二手车。
synonyms: cheat, trick, dupe, swindle, bamboozle, hoodwink, defraud
antonyms:  be honest, be truthful

词性: verb
