garble:/ˈɡɑːrbl/ (US); /ˈɡɑːbəl/ (UK)| vt. n.|篡改，歪曲；混淆；(使)模糊不清|to reproduce (a message, text, etc.) in a confused and distorted way; to mix up or confuse (facts, etc.)|The message was garbled in transmission. 这条信息在传输过程中被篡改了。|Synonyms: distort, twist, misrepresent, confuse, muddle|Antonyms: clarify, explain, elucidate|verb

garble:/ˈɡɑːrbl/ (US); /ˈɡɑːbəl/ (UK)| n.|混乱，歪曲；含糊不清的话|a confused or distorted version of something; gibberish|He spoke in such a garble that I couldn't understand a word. 他说话含糊不清，我一个字也听不懂。|Synonyms: distortion, confusion, gibberish|Antonyms: clarity, precision|noun
