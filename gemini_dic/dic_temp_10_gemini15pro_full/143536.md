outgunned:/ˌaʊtˈɡʌnd/
adjective
火力不足的，武器不如人的
defeated or surpassed by having inferior weaponry.  Having fewer or less powerful guns than the opposition.
The small police force was completely outgunned by the heavily armed robbers. 这支小型警察部队的武器完全不如全副武装的劫匪。
outmatched, overpowered, surpassed
outgunning, superior
