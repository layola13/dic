canonical:/kəˈnɒnɪkəl/ | adj. |规范的，经典的，权威的 | Following the accepted rules or standards; belonging to the canon; established as genuine |  The canonical Gospels are Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John. | 近义词: standard, accepted, authoritative, orthodox, established, approved, recognized | 反义词: unorthodox, unconventional, apocryphal, heretical | 词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *canonicus* "according to rule," from Greek *kanonikos* "according to rule," from *kanōn* "rule, standard."
