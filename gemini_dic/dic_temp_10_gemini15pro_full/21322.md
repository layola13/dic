bloodwood:/ˈblʌdwʊd/| n. |血木|A type of hardwood tree native to Australia, known for its dark red sap that resembles blood. Also refers to the timber from this tree.|The bloodwood tree is highly valued for its durable and richly colored timber.  血木因其耐用且颜色丰富的木材而备受重视。|The craftsman used bloodwood to create a beautiful, intricate carving.  这位工匠用血木雕刻出了一件精美复杂的雕刻品。|近义词：redwood, hardwood|反义词:softwood|词性:noun
