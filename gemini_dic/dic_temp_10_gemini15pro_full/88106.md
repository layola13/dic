highways:/ˈhaɪweɪz/| n. |公路，干线|A main road, especially one connecting major towns or cities.|We took the highway to get there faster. 我们走高速公路以便更快到达。|近义词：freeways, expressways, motorways, throughways, turnpikes|反义词：byways, side roads, back roads|noun
