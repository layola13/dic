dispassionateness:/dɪsˈpæʃənɪtnəs/
n.|冷静；客观；不动感情|The state of being dispassionate; freedom from passion; calmness; coolness.  Lack of emotional involvement; impartiality.|He analyzed the situation with remarkable dispassionateness. 他以非凡的冷静分析了局势。|近义词:objectivity, impartiality, detachment, neutrality, coolness, calmness|反义词:passion, bias, prejudice, fervor, zeal|词性:名词

dispassionate:/dɪsˈpæʃənɪt/
adj.|冷静的；客观的；不动感情的|Not influenced by strong emotion, and therefore able to think clearly about something.|She gave a dispassionate account of the incident. 她对事件作了冷静的描述。|近义词:objective, impartial, detached, neutral, cool, calm|反义词:passionate, biased, prejudiced, fervent, zealous|词性:形容词
