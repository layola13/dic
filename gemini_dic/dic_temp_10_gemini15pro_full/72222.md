florets:/ˈflɔːrɪts/ | n. |小花；小枝 |A small flower, especially one of the small flowers making up a composite flower head such as a daisy or a head of cauliflower. | The cauliflower was composed of tightly packed florets.  这颗花椰菜是由紧密排列的小花组成的。|  The broccoli florets were perfectly cooked. 西兰花的小花煮得恰到好处。 | synonyms: floweret, blossom, bloom | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a small flower part.  Perhaps "whole head" in the context of cauliflower or broccoli) | noun
