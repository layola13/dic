breer:/briːər/ | n. | 刺藤;荆棘; 纠结; 难题 |A thorny bush or shrub.  A tangled mass of thorns or briers. A difficult or thorny problem or situation. |例句：The path was blocked by a thick breer. 小路被茂密的荆棘挡住了。

例句：Navigating the legal system can be a real breer.  在法律体系中摸索前进可能真是一个难题。 |近义词：thorn, bramble, thicket, tangle, snag, difficulty, problem |反义词: ease, simplicity, solution |词性:名词

词源: Middle English brere, from Old English brǣr; akin to Old High German brāmo bramble, Latin ferus wild
