disassemble:/dɪsəˈsɛmbəl/ | vt. | 拆卸，分解 | To take apart a machine or structure so that it is in separate pieces. |  The mechanic disassembled the engine to find the problem. 机械师拆卸发动机以查找问题。

The furniture can be easily disassembled for storage. 为了方便存放，这些家具很容易拆卸。

近义词: dismantle, take apart, break down | 反义词: assemble, put together, construct | 词性: verb
