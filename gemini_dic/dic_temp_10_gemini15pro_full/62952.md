enneapetalous: /ˌɛniəˈpɛtələs/
adjective
九瓣的; 有九片花瓣的
Having nine petals.
The enneapetalous flower bloomed in the spring. 九瓣的花在春天盛开了。
Near Synonyms: nine-petaled, nine-petalled
Antonyms: apetalous (无瓣的)
word origin: Greek ennea- "nine" + petalon "leaf, petal"
word class: adjective
