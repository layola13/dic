halberd:/ˈhɔːlbərd/ (US) /ˈhɔːlbəːd/ (UK)| n. |戟; 长柄斧|A combined spear and battle-ax, a weapon consisting of an ax blade balanced by a pick with an elongated spike at the back, mounted on a long shaft.|The knight wielded his halberd with deadly skill.  骑士熟练地挥舞着他的戟。|近义词：poleaxe, pike, spear|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific weapon)|noun
