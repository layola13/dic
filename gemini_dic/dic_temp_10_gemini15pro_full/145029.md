overaffirms:/ˌoʊvərəˈfɜːrmz/
verb
过度肯定；过分确认
To affirm something too strongly or excessively; to assert with undue emphasis.
The defendant overaffirmed his innocence, leading the jury to suspect he was lying.  被告过分强调自己的清白，导致陪审团怀疑他在撒谎。
He overaffirmed his commitment to the project, making his colleagues uncomfortable. 他过分强调自己对项目的承诺，让他的同事感到不舒服。
overstate, exaggerate, overemphasize
understate, downplay
v.
