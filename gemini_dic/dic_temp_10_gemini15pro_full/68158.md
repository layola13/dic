fails:/feɪlz/| v.|失败，不及格，辜负，失灵|The engine failed. 引擎失灵了。|近义词：loses, disappoints, malfunctions, breaks down|反义词:succeeds, passes, works, functions|Verb (Third-person singular simple present)

fails:/feɪlz/| n.|不及格，失败|His business was a complete fail. 他的生意彻底失败了。|近义词：failure, loss, defeat|反义词:success, victory, triumph|Noun (plural)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *failen, *failȝen, from Old French faillir (“to miss, fail, be lacking, err, make a mistake, deceive, be exhausted”), from Vulgar Latin *fallire, from Latin fallere (“to deceive, cheat, disappoint, escape the notice of”).
