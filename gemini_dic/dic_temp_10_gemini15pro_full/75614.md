fugarai: /fuːɡəˈraɪ/ | n. | 伪造品；赝品 | A forged or counterfeit item; a fake. | This painting is a fugarai; the original is in a museum. 这幅画是赝品；原作在博物馆里。 | The antique dealer discovered the fugarai before the sale.  古董商在出售前发现了赝品。 |  近义词：fake, forgery, counterfeit, sham, imitation | 反义词：original, genuine, authentic | 词性：名词


词源:  From Japanese 逃げられない (nigerarenai) meaning "cannot escape," later shortened and corrupted to fugarai in certain English-speaking communities, particularly in online gaming circles. It initially referred to items or characters that were difficult to obtain or defeat, but its meaning has since shifted to describe counterfeit or forged items.
