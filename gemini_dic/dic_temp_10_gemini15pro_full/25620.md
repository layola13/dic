bumptious:/ˈbʌmpʃəs/| adj.|傲慢的；自以为是的；瞎自夸的|Obnoxiously self-assertive; offensively conceited.|例句：His bumptious manner made him unpopular. 他傲慢的态度让他不受欢迎。|例句：The bumptious young man boasted about his achievements.  这个自以为是的年轻人吹嘘他的成就。|近义词：arrogant, conceited, pompous, self-important, overbearing|反义词：humble, modest, unassuming|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From bump (v.) + -tious, by analogy with fractious, based on obsolete bump (n.) "boast, brag" (c. 1600). Related: Bumptiousness.
