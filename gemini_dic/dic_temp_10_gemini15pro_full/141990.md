organicity:/ɔːrɡəˈnɪsɪti/
|n.|有机性，有机统一性|The organicity of the design impressed the judges.  设计的有机统一性给评委留下了深刻的印象。|English explanation: The state or quality of being organic; resembling or suggesting a living organism in organization or development.  It often refers to the interconnectedness and natural flow within a system or structure.|例句：The architect emphasized the organicity of the building's design, ensuring that each element flowed naturally into the next.  建筑师强调了建筑设计的有机性，确保每个元素都自然地融入到下一个元素中。

例句：The organicity of the community was evident in the way neighbors helped each other. 这个社区的有机统一性体现在邻居们互相帮助的方式上。|近义词：unity, coherence, wholeness, integration|反义词：disunity, fragmentation, disorganization|词性: noun
