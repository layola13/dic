consenter:/kənˈsɛntər/ | n. |同意者，赞同者 | A person who consents or gives permission. |例句：The consenters signed the agreement. 同意者在协议上签了字。

例句：All consenters were informed of the risks. 所有同意者都被告知了风险。|近义词：approver, assenter, accepter |反义词：dissenter, objector |词性: 名词
