accident:/ˈæksɪdənt/ (US), /ˈæksɪd(ə)nt/ (UK)| n.|事故，意外，偶然事件|An unfortunate incident that happens unexpectedly and unintentionally, typically resulting in damage or injury.|She was injured in a car accident. 她在一场车祸中受伤了。

The discovery of penicillin was a happy accident. 青霉素的发现是一个意外的惊喜。

近义词：incident, mishap, misfortune, casualty|反义词:intention, plan, design|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *accidens*, present participle of *accidere* "to happen," from *ad* "to" + *cadere* "to fall."  The notion is of something "falling upon" one unexpectedly.
