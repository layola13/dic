hoarier:/ˈhɔːriər (US), ˈhɔːrɪə(r) (UK)| adj.|更白的，更灰白的；更古老的，更陈旧的|Covered with more or a thicker layer of white or gray hairs; older and more antiquated|例句：His beard was hoarier than I remembered. 他的胡子比我记忆中的更白了。

例句：The hoarier aspects of the tradition are gradually being forgotten.  这个传统中更古老的方面正逐渐被遗忘。|近义词：grayer, whiter, older, more ancient|反义词：younger, newer, less ancient|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *horer, from Old English *hārior, comparative of hār "hoary, gray."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *haiza- "gray, hoary."
