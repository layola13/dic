attainability:/əˌteɪnəˈbɪləti/| n. |可达性，可实现性|The attainability of the goal depends on our efforts.  目标的可实现性取决于我们的努力。|例句：The attainability of his ambitions was questionable.  他目标的可达性值得怀疑。

The attainability of world peace remains a complex issue. 世界和平的可实现性仍然是一个复杂的问题。|近义词：achievability, feasibility, possibility|反义词：unattainability, impossibility|词性:名词

attainable:/əˈteɪnəbl/| adj. |可达到的，可实现的|With hard work, success is attainable. 通过努力，成功是可以实现的。|例句：Her goals were ambitious, but attainable. 她的目标雄心勃勃，但可以实现。

Setting attainable goals is crucial for maintaining motivation.  设定可实现的目标对于保持动力至关重要。|近义词：achievable, feasible, possible, realistic|反义词：unattainable, impossible, unrealistic|词性:形容词

attain:/əˈteɪn/| v. |达到，获得|She attained her goal of becoming a doctor. 她实现了成为医生的目标。|例句：He attained the summit of the mountain after a long climb.  经过长时间的攀登，他到达了山顶。

The company attained record profits last year.  该公司去年实现了创纪录的利润。|近义词：achieve, accomplish, reach, gain, obtain|反义词：forfeit, lose, miss|词性:动词
