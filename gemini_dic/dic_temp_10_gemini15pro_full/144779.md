over-expects:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspɛkts/| v.|期望过高，预期过高|To expect too much from someone or something; to have unrealistic expectations.|She over-expects from her employees, leading to high turnover. 她对员工期望过高，导致人员流动率很高.|近义词：overestimates, presumes|反义词:underestimates, underexpects|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "expect" (from Latin "expectare," meaning "to look out for," "await").
