fussiest:/ˈfʌsiɪst/| adj.|最挑剔的，最吹毛求疵的|Superlative form of "fussy," meaning excessively concerned with minor details or difficult to please.|例句：She's the fussiest eater I know; she won't touch anything with onions. 她是我认识的最挑剔的食客；任何带洋葱的东西她都不碰。

例句：He's the fussiest person about his clothes; everything has to be perfectly ironed. 他对衣服是最挑剔的人；每件衣服都必须熨烫得完美无瑕。|近义词：pickiest, finickiest, most particular, most demanding|反义词：easygoing, undemanding, adaptable, tolerant|词性:形容词

词源：From fussy + -est (superlative suffix).  "Fussy" comes from the now obsolete "fust" meaning "stale, musty," later developing the sense of "ill-tempered, irritable," and eventually "fastidious, over-particular."
