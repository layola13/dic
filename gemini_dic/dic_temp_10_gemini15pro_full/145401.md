overcautious: /ˌoʊvərˈkɔːʃəs/ | adj. | 过于谨慎的 | Excessively cautious or wary. |  He's an overcautious driver, always braking too early. 他是一个过于谨慎的司机，总是刹车太早。| The overcautious investor missed out on several lucrative opportunities.  过于谨慎的投资者错过了几个赚钱的机会。| 近义词: careful, cautious, wary, prudent | 反义词: reckless, careless, rashly | 词性: 形容词
