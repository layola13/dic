paranoias:/ˌpærəˈnɔɪəz/ | n. |妄想症；偏执狂|A mental condition characterized by delusions of persecution, unwarranted jealousy, or exaggerated self-importance, typically elaborated into an organized system. It may be an aspect of chronic personality disorder, or a symptom of another psychosis, such as schizophrenia.|He suffers from paranoias about being followed. 他患有被跟踪的妄想症。|近义词：delusions, suspicions, anxieties|反义词：trust, confidence|词性: 名词 (plural noun)
