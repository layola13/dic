adipolytic:/ˌædɪpəˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
脂肪分解的
Breaking down or capable of breaking down fats (lipids).  This term refers to the process of hydrolyzing fats into glycerol and fatty acids.
Certain enzymes are adipolytic, breaking down fats in the digestive system. = 某些酶具有脂肪分解作用，可在消化系统中分解脂肪。
Adipolytic hormones help regulate fat metabolism. = 脂肪分解激素有助于调节脂肪代谢。
近义词:lipolytic, lipoclastic
反义词:lipogenic
词性:adjective
