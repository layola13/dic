corpulency:/kɔːrˈpjʊlənsɪ/ /kɔːrˈpʌlənsɪ/| n. |肥胖，臃肿|The state of being fat; obesity.  Excessive fatness.|His corpulency made it difficult for him to climb the stairs. 他的肥胖使他爬楼梯很困难。|Synonyms: obesity, stoutness, portliness, plumpness, chubbiness|Antonyms: thinness, slenderness, leanness|noun


corpulent:/ˈkɔːrpjʊlənt/ /ˈkɔːrpʌlənt/| adj. |肥胖的，臃肿的|Fat; obese.|The corpulent man struggled to fit into the airplane seat.  这个肥胖的男人费力地挤进飞机座位。|Synonyms: obese, stout, portly, plump, chubby|Antonyms: thin, slender, lean|adjective
