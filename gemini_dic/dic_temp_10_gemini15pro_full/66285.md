excommunicator:/ˌɛks kəˈmjuːnɪˌkeɪtər/
n.
逐出教门者，革除教籍者
A person who excommunicates someone from a church or religious community.
Martin Luther was excommunicated by the Pope. 马丁·路德被教皇逐出教会。The excommunicator delivered the decree with a heavy heart. 革除教籍者心情沉重地宣布了这项法令。
近义词: anathematizer
反义词: None (The opposite action would be to admit someone into a religious community, but there isn't a single word for the person performing that action that directly parallels "excommunicator.")
词性: noun
