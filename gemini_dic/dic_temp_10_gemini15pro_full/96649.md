industrialised:/ɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzd/ | adj. |工业化的|Having become industrial; converted to industrialism.  Developed industries on a large scale.|例句：The UK was the first industrialised nation. 英国是第一个工业化的国家。|例句：The once rural area has become increasingly industrialised. 曾经的乡村地区已经变得越来越工业化了。|近义词：developed, advanced, modern, industrialized|反义词:undeveloped, agrarian, pre-industrial|词性:adjective

Etymology:  From industrial + -ise/-ize (suffix forming verbs meaning ‘to make or become’) + -ed (suffix forming past participles and adjectives). Ultimately from Latin industria ‘diligence’.
