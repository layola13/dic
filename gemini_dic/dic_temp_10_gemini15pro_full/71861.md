fleshiness:/ˈflɛʃɪnɪs/| n. |丰满，肥胖|The state of being fleshy; plumpness or stoutness.  The quality of having a lot of flesh; plumpness. |Her fleshiness was evident in her rounded cheeks and arms. 她的丰满在她圆润的脸颊和手臂上很明显。|The chef preferred a certain fleshiness to the tomatoes he used in his sauces.  厨师喜欢在他的酱汁中使用某种程度上肉质丰满的西红柿。|近义词：plumpness, stoutness, chubbiness, rotundity|反义词：thinness, leanness, slenderness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fleschinesse*, from Old English *flǣscnes*, equivalent to fleshy +‎ -ness.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *flaiskaną (“to become flesh”).
