lowlife:/ˈloʊlaɪf/| n.|低级的人，卑鄙小人|a person who is considered to be unpleasant and of low social status or moral character|He called the paparazzi a bunch of lowlifes. 他称狗仔队是一群卑鄙小人。|近义词：scumbag, degenerate, villain|反义词:gentleman, noble person|noun
low-life:/ˈloʊlaɪf/| adj.|低级的，卑鄙的|relating to or characteristic of lowlifes|She was involved in some low-life activities. 她参与了一些卑鄙的活动。|近义词：sleazy, sordid, base|反义词:respectable, honorable|adjective
