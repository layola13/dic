enraptured: /ɪnˈræptʃərd/ /ɛnˈræptʃərd/ | adj. | 着迷的，狂喜的 | Filled with intense delight and wonder; completely captivated. | The audience was enraptured by the performance.  观众被表演深深吸引住了。

She was utterly enraptured by the beauty of the sunset. 她完全被日落的美丽所陶醉。

近义词：ecstatic, delighted, thrilled, captivated, enthralled
反义词：bored, apathetic, indifferent, unimpressed
词性：adjective
词源：From en- + raptured, past participle of rapture (v.), from Latin rapere "to seize, carry off."  字面意思是“被带走”，引申为被喜悦或惊奇完全俘获。
