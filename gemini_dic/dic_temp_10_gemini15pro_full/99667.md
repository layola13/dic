interline:/ˌɪntərˈlaɪn/ | v.  n. |加衬里；写于行间；插入|To write or insert (something) between lines already written or printed; to provide (a garment) with an interlining.|The tailor interlined the coat with silk. 裁缝给外套加了丝绸衬里。|近义词：insert, interleave|反义词:|v.


interline:/ˌɪntərˈlaɪn/ | n. |衬里；行间|Something inserted between two other things, especially a lining between the ordinary lining and the outer fabric of a garment.|The interline of the jacket provided extra warmth. 夹克的衬里提供了额外的保暖。|近义词：lining, interlining|反义词:|n.
