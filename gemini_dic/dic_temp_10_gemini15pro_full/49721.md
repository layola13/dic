democratises:/ˈdɛməkrəˌtaɪzɪz/
|v.|使民主化；使大众化|To make something accessible to everyone, removing restrictions based on class, wealth, or privilege.  It can also mean to introduce democratic principles or practices into an organization or system.|The internet has democratised access to information. 互联网使获取信息大众化了。|Synonyms: popularize, broaden, open up, liberalize|Antonyms: restrict, limit, monopolize|verb
