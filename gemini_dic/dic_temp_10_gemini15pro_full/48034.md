decivilized:/ˌdiːˈsɪvɪlaɪzd/
| adj. |未开化的，野蛮的；堕落的|Lacking civilization; barbarous; unrefined.|The decivilized behavior of the rioters shocked the nation. 暴徒们不文明的行为震惊了全国。|The tribe was considered decivilized by the explorers. 探险家认为这个部落未开化。|近义词：barbaric, savage, uncivilized, primitive|反义词：civilized, cultured, refined|词性:形容词
词源:  de- (prefix meaning "reverse, remove") + civilized.  From Latin *decivilizare*, from de- + *civilizare* "to civilize".
