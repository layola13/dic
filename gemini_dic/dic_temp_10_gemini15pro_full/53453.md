disapproval's:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːvlz/
n.
不赞成，反对
The act of disapproving; an expression of disapproval.
Her disapproval's silence spoke volumes. 她 disapproval's 沉默说明了一切。
近义词：objection, condemnation, censure
反义词：approval, acceptance, endorsement
词性：名词 (Noun)

词源：
From disapproval + -'s (possessive suffix). Disapproval itself comes from dis- (expressing negation) + approval, which ultimately derives from Latin approbare "to approve."
