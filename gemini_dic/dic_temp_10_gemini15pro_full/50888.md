desertful:/ˈdɛzərtfʊl/
noun
一满勺（的量）;相当于一勺的量;一餐勺的量
A desertful is the amount of something that a dessert spoon (= a spoon with a large bowl) can hold.
She added a desertful of sugar to her tea. 她往茶里加了一勺糖。
Near synonyms: spoonful, dessertspoonful
Antonyms: None (Quantity word, doesn't have a direct opposite)
词性: 名词
