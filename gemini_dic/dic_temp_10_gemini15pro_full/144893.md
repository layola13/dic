over-served:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvd/
adjective
过度招待的；被劝酒过多的
Having been served too much alcohol; intoxicated.
The restaurant was liable for the accident because they had over-served the driver.  这家餐厅对事故负有责任，因为他们给司机劝酒过量。
He was clearly over-served and couldn't even stand up straight.  他显然喝多了，甚至站都站不稳。
近义词:intoxicated, drunk, tipsy
反义词:sober
词性:adjective
