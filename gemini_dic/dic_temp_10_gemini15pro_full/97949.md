inns:/ɪnz/
n.
小旅馆，客栈
A small hotel, typically one in the country.
We stayed at a charming country inn. 我们住在一家迷人的乡村旅馆。
hotels, lodges, guesthouses
motels, hostels

词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English inne, from Old English inn (“chamber, house, dwelling, lodging, residence”), from Proto-Germanic *innam (“inside, within”). Cognate with West Frisian yn (“in”), Dutch in (“in”), German in (“in”), Gothic ιν (“in, into”). Related also to English inner.  Meaning narrowed to "public house" in Middle English.
