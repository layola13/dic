denotation:/ˌdiːnəʊˈteɪʃən/ /ˌdiːnoʊˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 字面意义；指称意义 | The denotation of a word is its literal meaning, as opposed to its connotation.  一个词的指称意义是它的字面意义，而不是它的内涵意义。 | The denotation of "home" is simply "a place where one lives"; its connotation might be "warmth," "family," and "security." “家”的字面意义只是一个居住的地方；它的内涵可能是“温暖”、“家庭”和“安全”。 | synonyms: meaning, sense, definition, reference | antonyms: connotation, implication, nuance | noun

denotation:/ˌdiːnəʊˈteɪʃən/ /ˌdiːnoʊˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 表示；指示 | The denotation of a symbol is what it represents. 一个符号的指称意义是它所代表的东西。|  A red octagon is a denotation for "stop."  红色的八边形表示“停止”。 | synonyms:  signification, indication, representation | antonyms:  N/A | noun
