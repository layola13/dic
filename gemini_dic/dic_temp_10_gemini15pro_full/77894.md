genialness:/ˌdʒiːniˈæl.nəs/ | n. | 亲切；和蔼；温和 | The genialness of the host made everyone feel welcome. |  The atmosphere was one of warmth and genialness. |  amiability, affability, cordiality, warmth | coldness, unfriendliness, hostility | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From genial + -ness.  Genial comes from Latin genialis "festive, cheerful, pertaining to marriage," later "pleasant, kindly," from genius "guardian deity or spirit which watches over each person from birth; innate ability or inclination."
