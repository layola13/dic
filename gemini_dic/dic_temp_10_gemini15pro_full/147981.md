overrewarded:/ˌoʊvər rɪˈwɔːrdɪd/
adjective
过度奖励的，报酬过高的
given too much reward; excessively compensated
The employee felt overrewarded for the small amount of work he had done.  这位员工觉得自己因少量的工作而获得了过多的报酬。
He believed he was vastly overrewarded for his contributions to the project. 他认为他对这个项目的贡献得到了过高的回报。
Synonym: overcompensated, overpaid
Antonym: underpaid, undervalued
词性: adjective
