equilibrium:/ˌiːkwɪˈlɪbriəm/ | n. |平衡，均衡|A state of balance between opposing forces or influences.|The government is trying to maintain an equilibrium between inflation and unemployment. 政府正在努力维持通货膨胀和失业之间的平衡。|Synonyms: balance, stability, symmetry|Antonyms: imbalance, instability, asymmetry|noun

equilibrium:/ˌiːkwɪˈlɪbriəm/ | n. |平静，镇定|A state of mental or emotional calmness, composure, and evenness.|He struggled to regain his equilibrium after the shock.  他努力在震惊之后恢复平静。|Synonyms: composure, calmness, poise|Antonyms: agitation, anxiety, distress|noun
