by-product:/ˈbaɪˌprɑːdʌkt/ | n. | 副产品；副作用 | something produced in the making of something else; a secondary result |  The by-product of making cheese is whey.  制作奶酪的副产品是乳清。| synonyms: side effect, spin-off, consequence, result | antonyms: main product, primary product | noun


by-product:/ˈbaɪˌprɑːdʌkt/ | n. | 副产品；副作用 |  The factory's main product is steel, but it also sells by-products such as slag.  这家工厂的主要产品是钢，但也出售炉渣等副产品。 | synonyms:  offshoot, residual, aftereffect | antonyms: intended product, desired outcome | noun
