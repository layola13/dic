/ɪnˈsɜːrʃənd/ | v. | 插入，嵌入（insert的过去分词）| Past participle of "insert," meaning to put or fit something into something else. |  The image was inserted into the document. 图像被插入到文档中。 | inserted, embedded, implanted | removed, extracted | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *insertionem, accusative of *insertio, from Latin inserere "to insert, graft, implant," from in- "into" + serere "to join, link, bind."
