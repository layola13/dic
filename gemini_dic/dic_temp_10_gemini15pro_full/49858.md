demoth:/diːˈmɒθ/ /diːˈmɔːθ/ | v. |除蛾；去除蛾子|To rid of moths, especially clothes moths.  |例句：She demoth-balled her winter coat. 她把冬天的外套取出来，去掉了防蛀剂。

例句：I need to demoth the closet before storing my sweaters. 我需要在存放毛衣之前清除壁橱里的蛾子。|近义词：fumigate, mothproof|反义词: infest (with moths)|词性：动词
