doze-away:/doʊz əˈweɪ/
verb
打瞌睡度过（时间）;  在瞌睡中度过
spend (time) dozing.
例句：He dozed away the afternoon in his armchair. 他在扶手椅上打盹度过了下午。

例句：She dozed away the hours on the train. 她在火车上打瞌睡度过了几个小时。


近义词：sleep away, slumber away, nap away
反义词：stay awake, be alert, be active
词性：verb (phrasal verb)
