overimmunized:/ˌoʊvərˈɪmjənaɪzd/
adjective
过度免疫的
Having received more immunizations than are necessary or recommended, potentially leading to adverse effects.
The child was overimmunized and suffered some health complications as a result.  这个孩子过度免疫，因此遭受了一些健康并发症。
The vet warned against overimmunizing pets, as it can be detrimental to their health. 兽医警告不要过度免疫宠物，因为这可能对它们的健康有害。
synonyms: excessively immunized
antonyms: underimmunized, inadequately immunized
词性: adjective
