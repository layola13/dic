hizz:/hɪz/
interjection|兴奋或惊讶的感叹词，类似于“嘶嘶”声|An exclamation expressing excitement or surprise, similar to a hissing sound.|Example: Hizz, that's a cool car! 嘶，那辆车真酷！|Synonym:  wow, cool, awesome|Antonym: boring, dull|词性:感叹词

hizz:/hɪz/
noun|嘶嘶声|A hissing sound.|Example: The snake made a loud hizz. 那条蛇发出很大的嘶嘶声。|Synonym: hiss, sizzle|Antonym: silence, quiet|词性:名词
