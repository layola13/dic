littering: /ˈlɪtərɪŋ/
n.|乱扔垃圾|The act of throwing trash or garbage on the ground or in public places indiscriminately.|Littering is a serious problem in many cities. 乱扔垃圾在很多城市都是一个严重的问题.|synonyms: dumping, trashing|antonyms: cleaning, tidying|noun
