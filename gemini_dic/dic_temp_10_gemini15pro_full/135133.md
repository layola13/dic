nonoffensive:/ˌnɒnəˈfɛnsɪv/ /ˌnɑːnəˈfɛnsɪv/
adjective
无攻击性的；不冒犯的；温和的
Not causing offense or hostility; harmless.
This comedy act is completely nonoffensive and suitable for all ages.  这出喜剧表演完全没有冒犯性，适合所有年龄段的人观看。

The advertisement used nonoffensive language to appeal to a wider audience. 这则广告使用温和的语言以吸引更广泛的受众。

近义词:inoffensive, harmless, benign
反义词:offensive, hostile, aggressive
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + offensive.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "offensive" derives from the Latin "offendere" (to strike against, offend).
