bnf:/ˌbiː ɛnˈɛf/ |abbr.| 巴科斯范式，巴科斯-诺尔范式 |Backus-Naur Form or Backus Normal Form: A formal notation for describing the syntax of a programming language or other formal language.  It is used to define the grammar of a language in a precise and unambiguous way.| The grammar of the language is defined in BNF.  该语言的语法是用巴科斯范式定义的。|近义词：syntax notation,  metasyntax|反义词: None|abbr.
