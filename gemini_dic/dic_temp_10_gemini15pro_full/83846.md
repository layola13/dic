halation:/həˈleɪʃən/ | n. |晕影，光晕|A blurring or spreading of light around a bright image on a photographic film or plate.  It's often seen as a glow around bright objects in photographs. |The bright lights of the city created a halation effect around the moon in the photo.| 近义词:glow, aura, halo | 反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑darkness, shadow等表示光线暗淡的词) | noun
