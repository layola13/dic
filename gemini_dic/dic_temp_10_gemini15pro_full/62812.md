enhancing:/ɪnˈhɑːnsɪŋ/ /ɛnˈhænsɪŋ/
verb
增强，提高，改进
increasing or improving the quality, value, or attractiveness of something
例句：This software is enhancing the performance of our systems. 这个软件正在提升我们系统的性能。

例句：The new lighting is enhancing the beauty of the garden. 新的灯光增强了花园的美感。

近义词：improving, boosting, strengthening, augmenting
反义词：diminishing, reducing, weakening, impairing
词性：v.
