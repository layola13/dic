extolments: /ɪkˈstəʊlmənts/ /ɪkˈstoʊlmənts/
|n.|赞美；颂扬；称赞|Acts of praising highly; eulogies.|The king was showered with extolments upon his return from the war. 国王从战场凯旋归来时，受到了大量的赞美。|Synonyms: praises, accolades, commendations, laudations, tributes|Antonyms: criticisms, condemnations, denunciations|noun

PHRASE->
(Extolments is not typically used in phrases. It's more commonly used in formal settings and as a standalone verb.)  由于 extolments 本身是一个较为正式的名词，通常不会用于短语中。它更多在正式场合下作为独立的名词使用。 因此，以下列出的是包含动词 extol 的常用搭配：

extol the virtues of  = 赞扬...的优点
extol the benefits of =  赞扬...的好处
extol the importance of = 赞扬...的重要性
extol someone's courage/bravery/talent = 赞扬某人的勇气/勇敢/才能
extol the beauty of something = 赞扬某事物的美丽


词源: From Late Latin *extollmentum, from Latin extollere "to raise, lift up, elevate, praise"
