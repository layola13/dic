enchafe:/ɪnˈtʃeɪf/ |v.|激怒；使恼火；使焦躁|to irritate or annoy someone|The tight collar enchafed his neck.  紧绷的衣领擦伤了他的脖子。|The constant criticism began to enchafe her.  不断的批评开始激怒她。|近义词:irritate, annoy, vex, gall, chafe|反义词:soothe, calm, please|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French enchaufer "to make warm, heat," from en- "in" + chaufer "to heat" (from Latin calefacere "to make warm").  The meaning shifted from literal heating to causing irritation, like friction from something too tight.
