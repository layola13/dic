noncontextually:/ˌnɑːnkənˈtekstʃuəli/ | adv. | 非上下文地，脱离上下文地 | Without considering the surrounding context; in isolation from the situation or text in which something occurs. |  This word, noncontextually, means outside of a specific context.  脱离上下文，这个词的意思是在特定语境之外。

This statement, noncontextually, could be interpreted in many ways. 这句话，脱离上下文，可以有很多种解释。 | 近义词: abstractly, generally, universally | 反义词: contextually, specifically | 词性: 副词
