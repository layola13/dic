errability:/ˌɛrəˈbɪləti/
n.
易错性, 出错的可能性
The quality or state of being errable; liability to err.
The errability of human judgment is a constant factor to consider in legal proceedings. 人类判断的易错性是法律程序中必须考虑的一个 постоянный factor。
Synonyms: fallibility, imperfection, proneness to error
Antonyms: infallibility, impeccability, perfection
noun
