devotionalist:/dɪˈvoʊʃənəlɪst/
n.
虔诚者，信徒
a person who is extremely devoted to a religion or cause.
She is a devotionalist, attending church services several times a week. 她是一位虔诚的信徒，每周参加几次教堂礼拜。
devotee, believer, adherent, follower
atheist, skeptic, unbeliever
noun

Etymology:
From devotion + -ist.  Devotion comes from Late Middle English: from Old French devocioun, from late Latin devotio(n-), from devotus ‘devoted’.
