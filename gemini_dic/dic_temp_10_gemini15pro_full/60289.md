eggier:/ˈeɡ.i.ər/
adjective
更像鸡蛋的; (指味道、气味)更腥的
more like an egg; smelling or tasting more strongly of egg.
The cake was dense and eggier than I expected.  这蛋糕比我预料的要更密实，蛋腥味也更重。
近义词:eggy
反义词:None
词性:adjective

eggier:/ˈeɡ.i.ər/
adjective
(非正式)更胆怯的，更神经质的 (informal) more timid or nervous
He seemed eggier than usual about asking for a raise. 他似乎比平时更胆怯，不敢要求加薪。
近义词:timid, nervous, apprehensive
反义词:bold, confident
词性:adjective

词源：
"eggier" is a comparative adjective formed from the noun "egg" with the suffix "-y" (meaning "full of" or "characterized by") and the comparative suffix "-er."  The Old English root for "egg" is "æġ"  and "-y" comes from the Old English suffix "-ig."
