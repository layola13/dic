over-securities: /ˌoʊvər-sɪˈkjʊrətiz/ | n. | 过度担保；超额证券 |  The act of providing excessive collateral or securities for a loan or other financial obligation, often beyond what is reasonably required. This can indicate financial instability or an overly cautious approach. |  The company's over-securities suggest they are worried about defaulting on their loans.  这家公司的过度担保表明他们担心贷款违约。|  excess securities, overcollateralization | under-securities, insufficient collateral | noun
