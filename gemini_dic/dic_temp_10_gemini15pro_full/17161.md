battlement: /ˈbæt(ə)lmənt/ | n. | 城垛；雉堞 | A parapet wall on a fort, castle, or other building, with open spaces for firing through. |  The castle's battlements offered a commanding view of the valley. 城堡的城垛可以俯瞰整个山谷。 |  The soldiers peered over the battlements, watching for the enemy. 士兵们从城垛上向下张望，观察敌情。 | synonyms: parapet, rampart, crenelation | antonyms: none | noun

battlement: /ˈbæt(ə)lmənt/ | vt. |  用城垛加强；加雉堞于 | To furnish with battlements. | They battlemented the walls of the fortress. 他们在堡垒的墙上加筑了城垛。 | The newly constructed tower was battlemented for defense. 新建的塔楼加筑了城垛以防御。 | synonyms: fortify, strengthen, protect | antonyms: weaken, dismantle | verb
