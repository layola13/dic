checkerboarded:/ˈtʃɛkərˌbɔːrdɪd/| adj.|棋盘格的，方格图案的|arranged in a checkered pattern, resembling a checkerboard. |The floor was checkerboarded with black and white tiles. 地板是用黑白瓷砖铺成的棋盘格图案。|The field was checkerboarded with different crops. 田野上种着不同的庄稼，形成了棋盘格图案。|近义词：checkered, tessellated|反义词: plain, solid|词性:形容词

词源:
From "checkerboard" + "-ed."  "Checkerboard" itself comes from "checker" (a piece in the game of checkers) + "board."  Ultimately from the Old French "eschequier," meaning "chessboard."
