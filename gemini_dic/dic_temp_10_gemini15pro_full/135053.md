nonnormality:/ˌnɒn.nɔːrˈmæl.ɪ.ti/ | n. | 非正态性 | The state or quality of not being normal; deviation from a normal distribution. | The researcher tested for nonnormality in the data before applying parametric statistical tests. 研究人员在应用参数统计检验之前测试了数据的非正态性。|  The nonnormality of the distribution skewed the results. 分布的非正态性使结果出现偏差。 | synonyms: abnormality, irregularity, deviation, anomaly | antonyms: normality, regularity, conformity | noun
