assassinator:/əˈsæsɪneɪtər/ | n. | 刺客，暗杀者 | A person who assassinates someone. Derived from the Medieval Latin word *assassinus*, and ultimately from Arabic *hashshashin*, meaning "hashish-users," referring to a group of Nizari Ismailis who were reputed to murder their enemies while under the influence of hashish.  | The assassinator was quickly apprehended. 刺客很快就被逮捕了。| John Wilkes Booth was the assassinator of Abraham Lincoln.  约翰·威尔克斯·布斯是亚伯拉罕·林肯的刺客。 | 近义词：murderer, killer | 反义词: protector, guardian | 词性: noun
