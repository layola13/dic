floaty:/ˈfloʊti/ (US) /ˈfləʊti/ (UK)| n.|漂浮物|Something that floats.|There were several colorful floaties in the pool.  游泳池里有几个五颜六色的漂浮物。|近义词：float, raft|反义词：sinker|词性:名词
