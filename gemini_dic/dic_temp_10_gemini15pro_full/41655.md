correlatable:/ˈkɒrəleɪtəbl/ /ˈkɔːrəleɪtəbl/| adj.|可关联的，可相互关联的|Able to be correlated; mutually related.|The study found that income and education levels are highly correlatable.|近义词:related, connected, associated|反义词:unrelated, independent, disconnected|词性:形容词
Etymology: From correlate + -able. Correlate comes from the Latin com- meaning "together" and relatus, the past participle of referre meaning "to carry back, bring back, refer."
