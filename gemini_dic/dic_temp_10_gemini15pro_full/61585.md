emergentnesses:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒəntnɪsɪz/
|n.|浮现; 出现; 紧急情况; 突发事件|The quality or state of being emergent;  the fact of something emerging.  It often refers to unexpected or unplanned occurrences or properties that arise from complex systems or interactions.  It's a plural form, suggesting multiple instances of emergence.|
|例句：The emergentnesses of new technologies are transforming the world.  新技术的出现正在改变世界。|
|例句：The emergentnesses of complex behaviors from simple rules are fascinating. 从简单的规则中浮现出复杂的行为令人着迷。|
|近义词：emergences, appearances, manifestations|
|反义词：disappearances, vanishings|
|词性：名词|
