louchettes:/luːˈʃɛts/ | n. | 护目镜，眼镜 | Eyeglasses, spectacles, especially tinted or protective ones.  Derived from the French word "louchette," meaning "small opening" or "peephole." Originally referred to small openings in helmets or masks. |  He wore his louchettes while skiing. 他滑雪时戴着护目镜。 | Synonyms: goggles, spectacles, eyeglasses | Antonyms:  N/A | noun
