caricaturable:/ˌkærɪkəˈtʃʊrəbl/
adjective
可笑的，适合漫画化的
Suitable for being caricatured or exaggerated in a humorous way.
His large nose and prominent chin made him easily caricaturable.  他大大的鼻子和突出的下巴使他很容易被画成漫画。
His distinctive voice is highly caricaturable. 他独特的声音非常适合模仿。
近义词: mockable, satirizable, lampoonable
反义词: serious, realistic
词性: adjective
