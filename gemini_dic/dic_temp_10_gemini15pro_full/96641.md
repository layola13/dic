indusial:/ɪnˈdjuːʒiəl/ | adj. |孢膜的；囊群盖的 | Relating to or resembling an indusium, a membranous covering over a sorus (cluster of spore-producing structures) in ferns. | The fern's indusial protects the developing spores.  这种蕨类植物的孢膜保护着正在发育的孢子。| Synonyms: covering, protective | Antonyms: uncovered, exposed | adjective

indusium:/ɪnˈdjuːʒiəm/ | n. |孢膜；囊群盖 | A membranous covering over a sorus (cluster of spore-producing structures) in ferns. | The indusium of this fern is kidney-shaped. 这种蕨类植物的囊群盖呈肾形。| Synonyms: covering, protective layer | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific anatomical feature) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *indusium* "undergarment, tunic,"  from *induere* "to put on."  The botanical sense arises from the notion of the indusium as a "covering."
