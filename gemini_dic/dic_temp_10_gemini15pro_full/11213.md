architectures:/ˈɑːrkɪˌtɛktʃərz/
n.
架构，体系结构
The design and structure of a computer system, building, or other complex object.
The architecture of the new bridge is both innovative and aesthetically pleasing. 新桥梁的架构既具有创新性，又美观大方。
framework, structure, design
disorder, chaos
noun

architecture:/ˈɑːrkɪˌtɛktʃər/
n.
建筑学
The art and science of designing and constructing buildings.
He studied architecture at university. 他在大学学习建筑学。
building design, construction
destruction, demolition
noun
