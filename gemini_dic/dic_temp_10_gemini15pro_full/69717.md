fenugreek: /ˈfɛnjuːɡriːk/ (US), /ˈfɛnjuːɡriːk/ (UK)| n. |胡芦巴; 胡芦巴籽; 胡芦巴叶|An annual clover-like herb (Trigonella foenum-graecum) cultivated in India and the Mediterranean region for its aromatic seeds, used as a spice, in medicine, and as a food and fodder.|例句：Fenugreek is often used in Indian cuisine. 胡芦巴经常用于印度菜肴。
