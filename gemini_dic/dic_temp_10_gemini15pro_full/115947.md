marriageabilities:/ˌmærɪdʒəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |适婚性，婚姻可能性|The quality or state of being suitable for marriage; the likelihood of getting married.  It often refers to a group of people rather than individuals.|The marriageabilities of young women in the village were discussed. 村里年轻女性的适婚性被讨论了一番。|近义词：eligibility for marriage, prospects for marriage|反义词: unmarriageability|noun
