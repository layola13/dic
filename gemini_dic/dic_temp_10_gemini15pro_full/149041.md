一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I own a car.  我拥有一辆车。
He owns several properties. 他拥有几处房产。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He is owning up to his mistakes. 他正在承认自己的错误。  (Note: "owning up" is a phrasal verb, and continuous tense is less common with verbs of possession like "own")
They are owning their power. 他们在展现自己的力量。(Note: This usage is becoming more common, especially in contexts related to self-acceptance and empowerment.)


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have owned this house for ten years. 我拥有这栋房子十年了。
She has owned several businesses in her lifetime.  她一生中拥有过几家企业。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been owning up to my responsibilities lately. 我最近一直在承担我的责任。(Note:  While grammatically correct, this structure feels slightly unnatural.  "I have been taking responsibility lately" would be more common.)
He has been owning that vintage car for a while now.  他拥有那辆老爷车已经有一段时间了。(Again, slightly unnatural;  "He has had that vintage car for a while now" sounds more natural)

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
