over-discuss:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskʌs/ | v. |过度讨论，过多的讨论|To discuss something excessively or to a point where it becomes unproductive or tiresome. | We over-discussed the issue and ended up going in circles. 我们过度讨论了这个问题，结果一直在兜圈子。|The committee over-discussed the proposal and failed to reach a decision. 委员会对这项提议进行了过多的讨论，未能达成决定。| 近义词: belabor, overanalyze| 反义词: skim, gloss over|词性:动词
