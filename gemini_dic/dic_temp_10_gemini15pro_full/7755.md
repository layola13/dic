anorthopia:/ænɔːrˈθoʊpiə/| n. |斜视|A defect of vision in which straight lines appear curved.|The patient's anorthopia made it difficult for him to judge distances accurately. 病人的斜视使他难以准确判断距离。|近义词：strabismus, heterotropia|反义词: orthophoria| noun
