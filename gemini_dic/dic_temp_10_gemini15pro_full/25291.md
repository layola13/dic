bugaboo:/ˈbʌɡəˌbuː/ (US), /ˈbʌɡəbuː/ (UK)
|n.|妖怪；吓人的东西；令人烦恼的事物|Something that causes worry or fear; a source of annoyance.|例句：The economy is a major bugaboo for many people these days. 经济是现今很多人烦恼的主要问题。

例句：Math tests have always been my biggest bugaboo. 数学考试一直是我最害怕的事情。

|近义词：bogeyman, bugbear, fear, worry, problem, difficulty, obstacle, nuisance, pest|
|反义词：blessing, advantage, benefit, comfort, joy|
|词性：名词|
