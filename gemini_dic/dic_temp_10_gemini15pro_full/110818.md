linnet:/ˈlɪnɪt/| n.|红雀，亚麻雀|A small brown and grey finch with a reddish breast and forehead, common in Europe.|例句：A linnet perched on the branch, singing its cheerful song. 一只红雀栖息在树枝上，唱着欢快的歌。

例句：The linnet's song is a complex and varied melody. 红雀的歌声复杂而多变。|近义词：finch, sparrow|反义词: N/A|词性:noun|词源：From Middle English *lynet, from Old French linette, diminutive of lin “flax,” because it feeds on flaxseed.
