elvanite:/ˈɛlvəˌnaɪt/| n. | 埃尔文岩 |A fine-grained igneous rock, specifically a rhyolite porphyry, often found in Cornwall, England. It is typically light gray or pink in color and contains phenocrysts of quartz and feldspar.|The quarry produced a beautiful elvanite, perfect for building.|Synonyms: rhyolite porphyry, quartz porphyry|Antonyms: None|Noun
