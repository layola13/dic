effluents:/ˈɛfluənts/| n.|污水，废水|Liquid waste or sewage discharged into a river or the sea.|The factory's effluents contaminated the local river.  这家工厂的污水污染了当地的河流。|Synonyms: wastewater, discharge, sewage, outflow|Antonyms: inflow, intake|noun
