groundage:/ˈɡraʊndɪdʒ/ | n. | 地租；停泊费；场地使用费 | A charge for the right to park an aircraft at an airport, or a ship at a wharf; also, the charge for allowing something to remain on a piece of land. | Example 1: The groundage fees at this airport are quite high. 这个机场的停机费相当高。

Example 2:  We had to pay a hefty groundage for storing our boat over the winter. 我们不得不支付一大笔费用来存放我们的船过冬。 |Synonyms:  parking fee, docking fee, storage fee, land rent | Antonyms: N/A (There's not a direct opposite for a fee.) | noun
