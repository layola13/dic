outweighs:/ˌaʊtˈweɪz/
verb
胜过，比…更重要
be greater or more significant than.
The advantages of this deal outweigh the disadvantages. 这笔交易的利大于弊。
Synonyms:  exceed, surpass, override, prevail over, predominate over
Antonyms:  be outweighed by, lose to, be inferior to
词性: 动词
