housefuls:/ˈhaʊsfʊlz/
noun
满屋子的人；客满；爆满
A houseful is a situation where a house is full of people.
The theater had several housefuls during the popular play's run.  剧院在这出热门戏剧的演出期间场场爆满。
We had a houseful of guests for Thanksgiving. 感恩节那天我们家来了满满一屋子的客人。
synonyms: capacity crowd, full house, packed house
antonyms: empty house, vacant house
