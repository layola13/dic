gratefulnesses:/ˈɡreɪtfʊlnəsɪz/
n.
感激之情（复数）
The state of being grateful; thankfulness.  Expressing gratitude, especially repeatedly or towards multiple things.
She expressed her gratefulnesses to all who had helped her. 她向所有帮助过她的人表达了感激之情。
Near Synonyms: gratitudes, thanks, appreciations
Antonyms: ingratitude, ungratefulness, resentment
