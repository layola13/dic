interleave: /ˌɪntərˈliːv/ | v. | 交叉，交错，插叙 | To insert pages, leaves, or sections between the leaves or sections of (a book, for example). To arrange in or as if in alternate layers. | The printer interleaved blank sheets between the printed pages. 印刷厂在印刷好的页面之间插入了空白页。 The chapters are interleaved with stunning photographs. 章节之间穿插着精美的照片。 | interpose, insert, intersperse | separate, divide | verb

interleaf: /ˈɪntərˌliːf/ | n. |  插入页，衬页 | A leaf or a section inserted between the leaves of a book or between other leaves or sections. | The book contained several interleaves with handwritten notes. 书中包含几张写有手写笔记的插入页。 The artist used interleaves of tissue paper to protect the drawings. 艺术家用薄纸衬页来保护画作。 | insert, page, leaf |  - | noun
