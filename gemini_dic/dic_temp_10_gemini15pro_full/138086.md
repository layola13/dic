normand:/ˈnɔːrmən/| n. |诺曼人；诺曼底人|A member of a people of Viking descent who settled in northern France (Normandy) in the 10th century and later conquered England in 1066.  They also established settlements in southern Italy and Sicily.|The Normans introduced French language and culture to England.|近义词：Norman French|反义词:|n.
