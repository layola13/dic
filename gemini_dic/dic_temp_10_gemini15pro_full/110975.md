liquidates:/ˈlɪkwɪdeɪts/
verb
清算; 变卖; 清偿;了结;杀死，清除（尤指秘密地或有计划地）
to eliminate, typically by violent means; to convert assets into cash
The company was forced to liquidate its assets to pay off its debts.  这家公司被迫变卖资产以偿还债务。

The dictator liquidated his political opponents. 独裁者清除了他的政敌.


Synonym: sell off, dispose of, cash in, settle, eliminate, kill, remove
Antonym: acquire, invest, establish, build, protect

词性:v.
