hasteful: /ˈheɪstfəl/ (US), /ˈheɪstfʊl/ (UK)| adj. |匆忙的，仓促的，草率的| Characterized by undue haste; hurried and often therefore careless or rash. |She made a hasteful decision and later regretted it. 她做了一个仓促的决定，后来后悔了。|He gave a hasteful glance at his watch and rushed out. 他匆匆瞥了一眼手表，然后冲了出去。|近义词: hurried, rushed, hasty, precipitate, impetuous |反义词: deliberate, careful, thoughtful, measured, cautious|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *hastifull, equivalent to haste +‎ -ful.
