ford:/fɔːd/ |n. |浅滩，渡口； Ford（福特汽车公司）|A shallow place in a river or stream that allows one to walk or drive across.  A ford is also the name of a well-known car manufacturer. |We drove the truck across the ford. 我们开车穿过浅滩。|近义词：crossing, shallows |反义词:deep |名词

ford:/fɔːrd/ |vt. |涉水，渡过|To cross a river or stream at a shallow place. |They forded the river on horseback. 他们骑马涉水过河。|近义词：traverse, cross |反义词: |动词
