herolike: /ˈhɪəroʊˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 英雄般的，像英雄的 | Resembling or characteristic of a hero; heroic. |  He displayed herolike courage in rescuing the child from the burning building. 他在从燃烧的建筑物中救出孩子时表现出了英雄般的勇气。|  She faced the challenge with herolike determination. 她以英雄般的决心面对挑战。 | 近义词: heroic, courageous, valiant, brave, gallant | 反义词: cowardly, craven, timid, fearful | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From hero + -like.  "Hero" comes from the Greek "hērōs," meaning "demi-god" or "warrior."  "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
