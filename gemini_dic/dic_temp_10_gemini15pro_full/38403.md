conceptualizing:/kənˈsɛptʃʊəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 概念化，形成概念 | The process of forming a concept or idea about something. |  She is conceptualizing a new marketing campaign. 她正在构思一个新的营销活动。|  envisioning, imagining, formulating | neglecting, ignoring | verb

Etymology:  From Late Latin *conceptus* "a conceiving, concept," from Latin *concipere* "to conceive," from *com-* "together" + *capere* "to take."
