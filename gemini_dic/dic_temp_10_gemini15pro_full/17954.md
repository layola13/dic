befittingly:/bɪˈfɪtɪŋli/
adverb
适当地，恰当地
in a way that is appropriate or suitable; fittingly
She was befittingly dressed for the occasion. 她穿着得体，适合这个场合。
近义词: appropriately, suitably, fittingly, properly
反义词: inappropriately, unsuitably, improperly
词性: adverb

befitting:/bɪˈfɪtɪŋ/
adjective
适当的，恰当的
suitable or appropriate for a particular person, thing, or occasion
It was a befitting tribute to a great man. 这是对一位伟人的恰当的致敬。
近义词: appropriate, suitable, fitting, proper
反义词: inappropriate, unsuitable, improper
词性: adjective

词源: From befit + -ing + -ly.  "Befit" comes from Old English *befetian "to make fit," from be- "thoroughly" + fetian "to fetch, arrange."
