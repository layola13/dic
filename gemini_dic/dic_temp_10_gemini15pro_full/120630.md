mis-coordinated:/ˌmɪskəʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
不协调的，动作笨拙的
lacking coordination; clumsy.
Her movements were mis-coordinated and awkward. 她的动作不协调，很笨拙。
clumsy, awkward, ungainly, inept
coordinated, graceful, agile
adjective
