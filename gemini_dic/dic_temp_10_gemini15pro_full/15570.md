bagworm:/ˈbæɡwɜːrm/| n. |蓑蛾，袋蛾|The bagworm constructs a protective case around itself from silk and bits of leaves and twigs. 蓑蛾用丝绸、树叶和树枝碎片围绕自己构建一个保护壳。|例句：The bagworm camouflages its case to blend in with the tree. 蓑蛾伪装它的外壳以融入树木之中。

bagworm:/ˈbæɡwɜːrm/| n. |蓑蛾，袋蛾|Bagworms are a common pest in gardens and orchards. 蓑蛾是花园和果园里的常见害虫。|例句：The bagworms have infested my evergreen bushes. 蓑蛾侵扰了我的常绿灌木。|近义词：casebearer, case moth|反义词: (No direct antonym exists as it is a specific type of insect)|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bag + worm.  Referring to the silken case or bag the larva constructs and carries around.
