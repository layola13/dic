enunciatively:/ɪˈnʌnsiətɪvli/
adv.
说明地，阐明地
Clearly and distinctly expressing something; in a way that enunciates.
He enunciatively explained the complex theory to the students. 他清晰地向学生们阐明了这个复杂的理论。
Near synonyms:  explicitly, clearly, distinctly, articulately, pointedly
Antonyms:  vaguely, implicitly, obscurely, ambiguously
词性: 副词
