heliocentricism:/ˌhiːliəʊˈsɛntrɪsɪzəm/
n.
日心说
The astronomical model in which the Earth and planets revolve around the Sun at the center of the Solar System.
Copernicus's theory of heliocentricism revolutionized astronomy. 哥白尼的日心说理论彻底改变了天文学。
近义词:heliocentrism
反义词:geocentrism
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From helio- (Greek hēlios "sun") + centric + -ism.
