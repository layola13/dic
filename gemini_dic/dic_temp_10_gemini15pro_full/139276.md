obliquity: /əˈblɪkwɪti/ /oʊˈblɪkwɪti/
n.
晦涩；隐晦
the quality of being oblique or indirect in meaning
例句：The obliquity of the poet's language made it difficult to understand. 诗人语言的晦涩使其难以理解。

例句：She spoke with such obliquity that I couldn't grasp her meaning. 她说话如此隐晦，我无法理解她的意思。
