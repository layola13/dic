monism: /ˈmɒnɪzəm/ (UK), /ˈmɑːnɪzəm/ (US)| n. |一元论|A philosophical view that a variety of existing things can be explained in terms of a single reality or substance.|Einstein's theory of relativity suggests a kind of monism in the universe, where energy and matter are interchangeable. 爱因斯坦的相对论暗示了宇宙中的一种一元论，其中能量和物质可以互换。|近义词：unity, singularism|反义词：dualism, pluralism|词性: noun

monistic: /ˈmɒnɪstɪk/ (UK), /ˈmɑːnɪstɪk/ (US)| adj. |一元论的|Relating to or characterized by the doctrine of monism.|A monistic worldview sees all things as interconnected and ultimately one. 一元论的世界观认为所有事物都是相互关联的，最终归为一体。|近义词：unified, singular|反义词：dualistic, pluralistic|词性: adjective
