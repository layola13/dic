cross-saddle:/ˈkrɔːs ˈsædəl/ | adj. | 横鞍式的；骑马时两腿分开的 |  Sitting astride something, as if on a saddle, with legs on either side. It describes a position where one sits with legs spread apart, similar to how one sits on a horse's saddle.  | She sat cross-saddle on the motorcycle. 她岔开双腿骑在摩托车上。 |  astride, straddling |  - | adjective
cross-saddle:/ˈkrɔːs ˈsædəl/ | adv. | 横鞍式地；骑马时两腿分开地 |  In a cross-saddle manner.  | The child sat cross-saddle on the fence. 孩子岔开双腿坐在栅栏上。 | astride, straddling | - | adverb
