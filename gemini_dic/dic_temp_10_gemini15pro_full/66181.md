exchanger:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒər/| n.|交换器，交换者|A person or thing that exchanges something.  A device that transfers something (especially heat).|The heat exchanger transfers heat from the hot water to the cold water.  热交换器将热量从热水转移到冷水。|synonyms: trader, dealer, broker, swap, switch|antonyms: hoarder, keeper|noun
