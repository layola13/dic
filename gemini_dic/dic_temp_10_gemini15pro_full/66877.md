expectorating:/ɪkˈspektəreɪtɪŋ/
verb
咳出,吐痰
the act of coughing up and spitting out phlegm from the throat or lungs
He was expectorating thick phlegm. 他咳出了浓痰。
coughing up, spitting out
swallowing, inhaling
