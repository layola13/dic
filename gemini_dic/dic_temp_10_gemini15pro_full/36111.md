coercible:/koʊˈɜːrsəbl/ /kəˈɜːsəbl/
adjective
可强制的，可强迫的
Capable of being coerced; susceptible to coercion.  Able to be controlled, especially by force or intimidation.
The king believed all his subjects were coercible and would obey his every command. 国王相信他所有的臣民都是可以被强迫的，会服从他的每一个命令。

The coercible nature of the employees led to a high turnover rate.  员工的可胁迫性导致了高离职率。

近义词:compellable, controllable, restrainable
反义词:incorrigible, intractable, unyielding
词性:adjective
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin coercibilis, from Latin coercere ‘restrain’.
