dirged:/dɜːrdʒd/
verb (past tense)|悲歌；哀悼；以挽歌哀悼|to have sung a dirge or lament for|The poet dirged the fallen hero. 诗人以挽歌哀悼这位倒下的英雄。|近义词：mourned, lamented, grieved|反义词：celebrated, rejoiced
verb|以挽歌哀悼的；被哀悼的|(used as an adjective) having been mourned with a dirge|The dirged soldier was laid to rest. 被哀悼的士兵被安葬了。|近义词：mourned, lamented, grieved|反义词：celebrated, rejoiced
