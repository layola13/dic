erase:/ɪˈreɪs/ /ɪˈreɪz/ | vt. |擦掉，抹去，消除|To remove (something) from a surface or from existence.|He erased the pencil marks. 他擦掉了铅笔的痕迹。|近义词：delete, remove, obliterate, expunge|反义词:write, record, imprint|verb

erase:/ɪˈreɪs/ /ɪˈreɪz/ | n. |擦掉，消除|The act of erasing something.|The erase function on the whiteboard is very useful. 白板上的擦除功能非常实用。|近义词：deletion, removal|反义词:recording, writing|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *esracer, from Latin eradere (“to scrape out, erase”), from e- (“out”) + radere (“to scrape”).
