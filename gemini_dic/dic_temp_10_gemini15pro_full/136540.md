nonrural:/ˌnɒnˈrʊərəl/ | adj. | 非农村的 | Not relating to or characteristic of the countryside; urban. |  Example 1: The majority of the population lives in nonrural areas. 大部分人口居住在非农村地区。

Example 2:  Nonrural communities often face different challenges than rural ones. 非农村社区通常面临与农村社区不同的挑战。 | Synonyms: urban, metropolitan, city, suburban | Antonyms: rural, rustic, country, agrarian | adjective
