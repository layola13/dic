overempirically: /ˌoʊvərɪmˈpɪrɪkli/
| adv. | 过于经验地；过度依赖经验地 | Relying excessively on observation and experimentation, without sufficient regard for theory or deduction. |  His approach to the problem was criticized as being overempirically focused, neglecting important theoretical considerations. 他处理问题的方法被批评为过于注重经验，忽略了重要的理论考虑。 | over-experientially, excessively empirically | insufficiently theoretically, inadequately theoretically | adverb
