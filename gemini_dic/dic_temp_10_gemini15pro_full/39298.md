conicities:/kɒˈnɪsɪtiz/
noun
锥度，锥形度
The plural form of conicity, referring to multiple measurements or instances of the degree of taper of a cone-shaped object.
The conicities of the various components must be precisely matched for proper assembly.  各个部件的锥度必须精确匹配才能正确组装。
The engineer calculated the conicities of the nozzles. 工程师计算了喷嘴的锥度。
近义词:tapers, angles of taper
反义词:cylindricalities
词性:noun
