pan-orthodox:/pænˈɔːrθədɑːks/
adjective
泛东正教的
Relating to or involving all or most of the Eastern Orthodox churches.  Encompassing or representing the traditions and beliefs common to all Eastern Orthodox churches.
The patriarch attended a pan-orthodox conference.  主教出席了泛东正教会议。
The council issued a pan-orthodox statement on the environment.  理事会发布了一份关于环境的泛东正教声明。
Near synonyms:  inter-orthodox, ecumenical (within Orthodoxy)
Antonyms:  sectarian, denominational (within Orthodoxy)
adjective
