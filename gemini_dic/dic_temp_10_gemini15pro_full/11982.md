artful:/ˈɑːrtfʊl/| adj.|巧妙的，狡猾的，熟练的|Clever or skillful, especially in a crafty or cunning way.  Showing creative skill or taste.|She had an artful way of getting what she wanted. 她有一种巧妙的方法得到她想要的东西。|synonyms:  cunning, sly, wily, ingenious, shrewd, crafty, devious, slick, guileful|antonyms:  artless, guileless, ingenuous, naive, simple, straightforward, honest|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *artful*, from art + -ful.  Art in this sense means skill or craft.
