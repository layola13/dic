honorer:/ˈɑːnərər/ (US) /ˈɒnərə(r)/ (UK)| n.|给予荣誉者，尊敬者；授予荣誉者|A person who bestows honor or respect upon someone or something.  A person who fulfills an obligation or keeps an agreement.|例句：The king was the honorer of many brave knights. 国王授予了许多勇敢的骑士荣誉。

例句：He was an honorer of his commitments. 他是一个信守承诺的人。|近义词：admirer, respecter, celebrant|反义词：detractor, critic, disparager|词性:noun
