elbowchair:/ˈelbəʊˌtʃeə(r)/ (UK), /ˈelbəʊˌtʃer/ (US)| n.|扶手椅，单人沙发|A chair with supports for the elbows or arms.|She settled comfortably into the elbowchair by the fire. 她舒服地坐在壁炉旁的扶手椅上。|Example: He sat in his elbowchair, reading a book. 他坐在他的扶手椅上看书。|Synonyms: armchair, easy chair|Antonyms: stool, bench|Noun
