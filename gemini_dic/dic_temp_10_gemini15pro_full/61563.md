emendator:/ɪˈmɛnˌdeɪtər/ | n. | 校订者，修订者 | A person who corrects or improves texts, especially literary works. |  The emendator carefully reviewed the ancient manuscript, correcting errors and suggesting improvements. 校订者仔细审查了古代手稿，纠正错误并提出改进建议。

 The publisher hired an emendator to prepare the new edition of the classic novel. 出版社聘请了一位校订者来准备这部经典小说的新版本。 | Synonyms: editor, corrector, reviser, proofreader | Antonyms: author, writer, originator | noun
