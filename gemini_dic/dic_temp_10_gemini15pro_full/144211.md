outsized:/ˈaʊtsaɪzd/| adj.|特大的，超大的，过大的|Exceeding or surpassing usual or normal size; unusually large. |The company made an outsized donation to the charity.  该公司向慈善机构捐了一大笔钱。|近义词：enormous, huge, gigantic, massive, colossal|反义词：small, tiny, minuscule, diminutive|词性:形容词
词源:  "out-" (超出) + "sized" (大小的).  From "outsize," meaning to exceed in size, dating back to the late 19th century.
