moodle:/ˈmuːdl/| n.| (网络学习)平台，学习管理系统|A learning platform designed to provide educators, administrators and learners with a single robust, secure and integrated system to create personalised learning environments.| Our school uses Moodle to host all of its online course materials. 我们的学校使用Moodle来托管其所有在线课程资料。|synonyms: learning management system (LMS), virtual learning environment (VLE), online learning platform|antonyms: traditional classroom, physical classroom|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Moodle is a contraction of "Modular Object-Oriented Dynamic Learning Environment".  It also plays on the word "mood," suggesting a frame of mind conducive to learning and exploring.
