eclampsia:/ɪˈklæmpsɪə/ | n. | 子痫 | A serious condition in pregnancy characterized by convulsions and coma.  It is typically preceded by pre-eclampsia (high blood pressure and protein in the urine).|  The doctor diagnosed her with eclampsia. 医生诊断她患有子痫。|  She was rushed to the hospital with eclampsia. 她因患子痫被紧急送往医院。 |  Near synonyms:  toxemia,  convulsions (in pregnancy) | Antonyms:  health, wellness | noun
