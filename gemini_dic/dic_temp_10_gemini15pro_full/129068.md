nitrogen:/ˈnaɪtrədʒən/ | n. |氮，氮气|A colorless, odorless, tasteless, nonmetallic element constituting approximately four-fifths of the atmosphere by volume and present in combined form in animal and vegetable tissues, especially in proteins.|Plants absorb nitrogen from the soil. 植物从土壤中吸收氮。|Synonyms: N, N2|Antonyms: None|Noun
