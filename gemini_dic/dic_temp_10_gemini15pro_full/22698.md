boreas:/ˈbɔːriəs/ | n. | 北风之神；北风 | The Greek god of the North Wind.  A strong north wind. |  The boreas howled through the mountains. 北风呼啸着穿过山脉。 | 近义词: north wind,  aquilon | 反义词: auster (south wind) | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Latin *Boreās*, from Ancient Greek Βορέας (Boréas).
