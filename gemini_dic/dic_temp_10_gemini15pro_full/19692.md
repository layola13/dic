bilging:/ˈbɪldʒɪŋ/| n. v.| 船底破裂进水; (船)进水|The act of a ship's hull being ruptured and taking on water.  The process of a ship taking on water through a ruptured hull.|Example 1: The bilging of the ship caused it to sink quickly. 船底破裂进水导致船迅速下沉。

Example 2:  The crew struggled to control the bilging but couldn't save the vessel. 船员努力控制进水，但无法挽救船只。|近义词：flooding, foundering, sinking|反义词:  watertight, sealed|词性：名词, 动词

bilge:/bɪldʒ/| n. v.| 船底污水;  (船)污水;  船底|The lowest compartment of a ship's hull, where water collects. To bail water out of a bilge.|Example 1: The bilge was full of dirty water and oil. 船底污水里充满了脏水和油。

Example 2: They had to bilge the boat to keep it afloat. 他们不得不把船里的水舀出去以保持其漂浮。|近义词：bottom, sump|反义词: deck, topside|词性：名词, 动词
