nonrealizing:/ˌnɒnˈriːəlaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 未实现的，未变现的 | Not having converted (assets) into cash or another form of realized profit or loss.  |  Example:  The company held a significant amount of nonrealizing gains in its investment portfolio.  该公司在其投资组合中持有大量未实现收益。 | Example:  He had nonrealizing losses in the stock market due to the recent downturn.  由于最近的经济低迷，他在股市中遭受了未实现的损失。 | Synonyms: unrealized, latent, potential | Antonyms: realized, actual, cashed-in | adjective
