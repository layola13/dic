carnelians:/kɑːrˈniːliənz/
n.
玛瑙, 红玉髓（carnelian的复数）
A translucent reddish variety of chalcedony, used as a gemstone.  A semiprecious stone consisting of an orange or orange-red variety of chalcedony.
She wore a necklace of carnelians. 她戴着一条玛瑙项链。
Near synonyms: agates, chalcedonies
Antonyms: N/A (Gemstones don't typically have direct antonyms)
noun
