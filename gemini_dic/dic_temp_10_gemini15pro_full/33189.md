chromophore:/ˈkrəʊməfɔːr/ (UK) /ˈkroʊməfɔːr/ (US)| n.|发色团，生色团|A chemical group that absorbs light and imparts color to a molecule.|Chlorophyll is a chromophore that makes plants green. 叶绿素是一种使植物呈绿色的发色团。|The chromophore in hemoglobin is responsible for its red color. 血红蛋白中的发色团使其呈现红色。|Synonyms: pigment, coloring agent, colorant|Antonyms: N/A|noun
