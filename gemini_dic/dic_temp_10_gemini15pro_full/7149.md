angeling:/ˈeɪndʒəlɪŋ/ |n.|小天使; 天真可爱的孩子|A child who is as sweet and innocent as an angel. |She looked at her sleeping baby, a perfect little angeling. 她看着她熟睡的婴儿，一个完美的小天使。|Synonyms: cherub, darling, innocent |Antonyms: devil, brat, imp |Noun

angeling:/ˈeɪndʒəlɪŋ/ |n.|堕落的天使; 失足的人|A person, especially a child, who has lost their innocence. (This meaning is less common.)|The once bright-eyed angeling had become hardened by life on the streets.  曾经目光明亮的小天使已经被街头生活磨砺得冷酷无情。|Synonyms: fallen angel, outcast, lost soul|Antonyms: saint, paragon |Noun
