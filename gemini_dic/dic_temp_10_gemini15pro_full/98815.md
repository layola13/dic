intellectualism:/ˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəlɪzəm/ | n. | 知性主义；理智主义；才智高于一切 |  A doctrine that emphasizes the importance of the intellect or reason.  It's the belief that the best way to understand and solve problems is through careful thought and analysis, rather than emotion or intuition. | He embraced intellectualism and valued knowledge above all else. 他崇尚理智主义，视知识高于一切。|  The university was a center of intellectualism and debate. 这所大学是理智主义和辩论的中心。 |  rationalism, cerebration | emotionalism, anti-intellectualism | noun
