doable:/ˈduːəbl/ /ˈduːəbəl/| adj.|可行的，做得成的|Something that is doable is possible and can be achieved. |例句：This project is doable with the right resources.  这个项目在资源充足的情况下是可行的。

例句：Climbing the mountain in a day is doable, but challenging. 一天内爬上这座山是可行的，但很有挑战性。|近义词：achievable, feasible, possible, practicable, viable|反义词：impossible, impracticable, unachievable, infeasible|词性：形容词

词源: From "do" + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being").  源自“do” + “-able”（后缀，表示“能够”）。
