outpeopling:/ˌaʊtˈpiːplɪŋ/
|n.|人口超过；人口胜过；人口外流|The act of exceeding another group in population size, or the migration of people from a specific area, leading to a decrease in its population.|The outpeopling of rural areas is a concern for many governments. 农村地区人口外流是许多政府关心的问题。|近义词：depopulation, emigration, exodus|反义词：population growth, immigration|词性:名词

Etymology:  "out-" (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + "people" (noun referring to a group of humans) + "-ing" (suffix forming a noun denoting an action or process).
