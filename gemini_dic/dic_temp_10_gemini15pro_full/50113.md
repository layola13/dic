dentirostral:/ˌdɛntɪˈrɒstrəl/
adjective
齿吻的；具有齿状嘴的
Having a beak or snout resembling a tooth or teeth.
The platypus has a dentirostral bill, which it uses to detect prey.  鸭嘴兽有一个齿吻状的喙，用来探测猎物。
The fish exhibited a dentirostral morphology. 这条鱼呈现出齿吻的形态。
近义词: toothed, beaked
反义词: smooth-snouted
