abjudicate: /əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪt/
verb
正式判决；剥夺；宣告…丧失权利
to officially decide that someone does not own or have a right to something, or to order someone not to do something
The court abjudicated the company's claim to the land. 法院驳回了该公司对这块土地的所有权要求。

The judge abjudicated him of his parental rights. 法官剥夺了他的父母权利。


synonym: adjudge, decree, rule
antonym:  award, grant, bestow
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abjudicare (“to deprive of by judgment”), from ab- (“away from”) + judicare (“to judge”), from judex (“judge”).
