blander:/ˈblæn.dər/ /ˈblæn.dɚ/| adj.|更乏味的，更平淡的|Lacking strong flavor; mild; insipid.|The soup was blander than I expected. 这汤比我想象的要淡。|近义词：tasteless, insipid, mild|反义词： flavorful, spicy, tasty|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From bland + -er (comparative suffix).  Bland itself comes from Old French *bland, from Latin blandus "smooth-tongued, flattering, alluring, charming, pleasant, mild," from PIE root *mlan-, source also of Greek meilia "pleasant things, dainties."
