acclimatizers: /əˈklaɪməˌtaɪzərz/
noun
适应者;驯化者
People, animals, or plants that acclimatize, or become accustomed to a new climate or environment.
The acclimatizers introduced new species of plants to the region.  这些适应环境的人将新的植物种类引入了该地区。

The mountain climbers were experienced acclimatizers. 这些登山者是经验丰富的适应环境者。

近义词: adapters, adjusters
反义词: non-adapters

ETYMOLOGY->
From acclimatize + -er (agent suffix). Acclimatize comes from French acclimatiser, from à (to) + climat (climate).  Ultimately from Latin clīma (region, slope).

USAGE->
The acclimatizers quickly adjusted to the high altitude.  适应环境的人很快就适应了高海拔。

New acclimatizers to the desert should drink plenty of water.  刚到沙漠的适应环境者应该多喝水。
