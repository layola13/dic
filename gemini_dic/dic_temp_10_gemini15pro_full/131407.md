nonclassifiable:/ˌnɑːnˈklæsɪfaɪəbl/
adjective
不可分类的
Not able to be classified or categorized.
This newly discovered species is nonclassifiable due to its unique characteristics.  这种新发现的物种由于其独特的特征而无法分类。
The artifact's origin is nonclassifiable, baffling experts for decades.  这件文物的起源无法分类，几十年来一直困扰着专家们。
近义词: unclassifiable,  incategorizable
反义词: classifiable, categorizable
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + classifiable (from classify + -able)
