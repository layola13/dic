churchship:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃʃɪp/
n.
教会的船; 教会船
a ship used as a church, especially a floating church serving sailors or riverside communities.
The old steamship was converted into a churchship, offering services to the river community. 这艘旧的蒸汽船被改造成了一艘教会船，为河边的社区提供服务。

The churchship provided a place of worship for sailors far from home. 这艘教会船为远离家乡的水手们提供了一个礼拜场所。
近义词: floating church, mission boat
反义词: None
