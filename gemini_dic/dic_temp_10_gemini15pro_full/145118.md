overargue:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːrɡjuː/
verb
过度争论，过分辩论
To argue or debate a point excessively or beyond what is necessary or reasonable.
He overargued his case and alienated the jury. 他过度辩论他的案件，疏远了陪审团。
近义词: overstate, belabor
反义词: concede, yield
词性: verb
