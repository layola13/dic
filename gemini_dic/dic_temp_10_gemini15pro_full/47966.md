deciduousness:/dɪˈsɪdʒuːəsnəs/| n.| 落叶性，脱落性|The deciduousness of the trees creates a beautiful display of color in the fall. 秋天树木的落叶性造就了美丽的色彩。|The deciduousness of the lining of the uterus is a normal part of the menstrual cycle. 子宫内膜的脱落性是月经周期的正常组成部分。|Synonyms: caducity, dropping, shedding|Antonyms: evergreenness, persistence|noun


deciduous:/dɪˈsɪdʒuːəs/| adj.| 落叶的，脱落的|Deciduous trees lose their leaves in the fall. 落叶树木在秋天落叶。|Deer antlers are deciduous. 鹿角是会脱落的。|Synonyms: caducous, shedding, temporary|Antonyms: evergreen, persistent, permanent|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *deciduus* "falling off," from *decidere* "to fall off," from *de-* "down" + *cadere* "to fall."
