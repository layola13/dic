misorder:/ˌmɪsˈɔːrdər/ | vt. |扰乱，使失调|to put into disorder; to disrupt the normal functioning of|Stress can misorder a person's metabolism. 压力会扰乱人的新陈代谢。| 近义词: disorganize, disrupt, disturb | 反义词: organize, order, regulate | verb
