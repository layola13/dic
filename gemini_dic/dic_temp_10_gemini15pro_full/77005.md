garefowl:/ˈɡɛərˌfaʊl/ | n. |大海雀|A large, flightless, extinct seabird (Pinguinus impennis) formerly inhabiting the North Atlantic. It was hunted to extinction in the mid-19th century.|The last known pair of garefowl were killed in Iceland in 1844.| 近义词:great auk|反义词: N/A (extinct species have no direct antonyms)| 词性: noun
