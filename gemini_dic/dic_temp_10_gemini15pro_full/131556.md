noncommodities:/ˌnɑːnkəˈmɑːdətiz/ | n. | 非商品 | Goods or services that are not easily standardized or traded on a commodity market.  They are often characterized by differentiation, customization, and higher value-added. |  Example: Education and healthcare are considered noncommodities. 教育和医疗保健被认为是非商品. | Synonym: services, specialties | Antonym: commodities | noun
