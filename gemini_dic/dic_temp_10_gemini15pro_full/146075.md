overed: /ˈəʊvəd/ (UK) /ˈoʊvərd/ (US) | adj. | 覆盖的；遮盖的；重叠的 | Covered means something is over or on top of something else.  It can also refer to a distance traveled or an area included. | The table was covered with a white cloth. 桌子上盖着一块白布。| The journalist covered the entire political campaign. 这位记者报道了整个政治竞选活动。| synonyms: cloaked, draped, sheathed | antonyms: uncovered, exposed, bare | word type: adjective
