overflexion: /ˌoʊvərˈflɛkʃən/ | n. |过度屈曲，过度弯曲| Excessive bending or flexion of a limb or joint beyond its normal range of motion. | Example: Overflexion of the knee can cause damage to the ligaments.  过度屈曲膝盖会导致韧带损伤。 | Example:  The gymnast's overflexion during the routine was breathtaking but risky.  体操运动员在表演过程中过度弯曲身体，令人叹为观止，但也存在风险。| Near synonyms: hyperflexion, overbending | Antonyms: hyperextension | Noun
