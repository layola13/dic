insipience:/ɪnˈsɪpiəns/| n.|愚昧；无知；浅薄|Lack of wisdom or experience; foolishness.|His insipience led him to make a series of poor decisions. 他的愚昧让他做出了一系列糟糕的决定。|近义词：foolishness, naiveté, ignorance, immaturity|反义词：wisdom, experience, maturity, sagacity|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Latin *insipientia*, from insipiens (“unwise, foolish”), from in- (“not”) + sapiens (“wise”).
