bitterish:/ˈbɪtərɪʃ/| adj.|微苦的，有点苦的|Slightly bitter; somewhat bitter.|The coffee was a little bitterish this morning. 今早的咖啡有点苦。|近义词:sour, acrid, tart|反义词:sweet, sugary|词性:形容词
Etymology: bitter + -ish. From bitter (Old English biter "sharp, biting, painful, cruel," from Proto-Germanic *bitraz "sharp," from PIE root *bheid- "to split, cut, bite") + -ish (suffix forming adjectives meaning "somewhat," "rather," "somewhat resembling").
