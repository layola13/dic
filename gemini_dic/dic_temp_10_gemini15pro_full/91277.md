hydrognosy:/ˌhaɪdrɒɡˈnɒsi/ | n. | 水文地质学；地下水学 | The study of the distribution and movement of groundwater.  The science dealing with the properties, distribution, and circulation of water on and below the earth's surface. |  Hydrognosy plays a crucial role in managing water resources. 水文地质学在水资源管理中起着至关重要的作用。 The hydrognosy of the region indicated a plentiful supply of underground water. 该地区的水文地质学表明地下水供应充足。 | 近义词: hydrogeology, geohydrology | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
