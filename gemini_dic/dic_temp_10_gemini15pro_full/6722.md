analogs:/ˈænəlɒɡz/ (US) /ˈænəlɔːɡz/ (UK)| n.|类似物；相似体；模拟量|Substances that are similar in structure and function to another substance.  They often mimic the effects of the original substance.|Researchers are developing new analogs of existing drugs to improve their effectiveness and reduce side effects. 研究人员正在开发现有药物的新类似物，以提高其有效性并减少副作用。|synonyms: counterparts, parallels, equivalents|antonyms: originals, prototypes|noun

analogs:/ˈænəlɒɡz/ (US) /ˈænəlɔːɡz/ (UK)| n.|模拟信号|Signals that are continuous and represent physical quantities. They are distinct from digital signals, which are discrete.|Analogs watches use continuously moving hands to display time, unlike digital watches. 模拟手表使用连续移动的指针来显示时间，这与数字手表不同。|synonyms: continuous signals, wave forms|antonyms: digital signals|noun
