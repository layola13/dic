bottleneck:/ˈbɒtlˌnek/ (US) /ˈbɒtlnek/ (UK)| n.|瓶颈，障碍，狭窄路段|A bottleneck is a point of congestion or blockage in a system, process, or flow, causing a slowdown or hindrance to progress.|The construction work is causing bottlenecks in the city center.  施工正在造成市中心的交通瓶颈。|synonyms: obstacle, impediment, obstruction, constraint, restriction|antonyms:  expedite, smooth, facilitate|noun


bottlenecks:/ˈbɒtlˌneks/ (US) /ˈbɒtlneks/ (UK)| n.|瓶颈，障碍，狭窄路段（复数）|Plural form of "bottleneck," referring to multiple points of congestion or blockage.|Several bottlenecks in the supply chain are causing delays.  供应链中的几个瓶颈正在造成延误。|synonyms: obstacles, impediments, obstructions, constraints, restrictions|antonyms:  expedites, smooths, facilitates|noun
