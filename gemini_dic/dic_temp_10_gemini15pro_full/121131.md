misdefined:/ˌmɪsdɪˈfaɪnd/
adj.|定义错误的，定义不清的|Incorrectly defined; not clearly or accurately explained.|The term "success" is often misdefined in our society.  这个词“成功”在我们的社会里经常被错误地定义。|The study used a misdefined concept of poverty. 这项研究对贫困的概念定义有误。|
近义词:misinterpreted, misconstrued, wrongly defined, inaccurately defined
反义词:well-defined, clearly defined, accurately defined
词性:adjective
词源:mis- (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + defined (past participle of the verb "define")
