lilial: /ˈlɪliəl/ | adj. | 铃兰的，铃兰香的 | Relating to or characteristic of lilies of the valley; having the scent of lilies of the valley. | The perfume had a distinct lilial note. 这款香水带有明显的铃兰香味。 | Synonyms: lily-of-the-valley-like, muguet | Antonyms: N/A | adjective

lilial: /ˈlɪliəl/ | n. | 铃兰醛 (一种合成香料) |  A synthetic fragrance compound with a lily-of-the-valley scent, chemically known as butylphenyl methylpropional (BMHCA). | Lilial is a common ingredient in perfumes and soaps. 铃兰醛是香水和肥皂中的常见成分。 | Synonyms: BMHCA, butylphenyl methylpropional | Antonyms: N/A | noun
