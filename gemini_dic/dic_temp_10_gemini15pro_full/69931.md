fervor:/ˈfɜːrvər/ (US) /ˈfɜːvə/ (UK)| n. | 热情，热烈，炽热 | Intense and passionate feeling. | She spoke with great fervor about the need for change. 她热情洋溢地谈到变革的必要性。

The fervor of the fans filled the stadium. 球迷的热情充满了整个体育场。| 近义词：passion, zeal, ardor, enthusiasm | 反义词：apathy, indifference, coldness | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *fervour*, from Latin *fervor* "boiling heat, intense heat; glow, ardor," from *fervere* "to boil, glow."
