antistructuralist: /ˌæntiˈstrʌktʃərəlɪst/ | adj. n. | 反结构主义的；反结构主义者 |  Relating to or characteristic of antistructuralism, a philosophical movement that opposes structuralism's emphasis on fixed systems and meanings.  It often emphasizes the instability of meaning and the importance of individual interpretation. |  His antistructuralist approach to literary criticism challenged traditional interpretations. 他对文学批评的反结构主义方法挑战了传统解释。|  The antistructuralist argued that meaning is fluid and context-dependent.  这位反结构主义者认为，意义是流动的，依赖于语境。 | Synonyms: post-structuralist, deconstructionist | Antonyms: structuralist | adjective, noun
