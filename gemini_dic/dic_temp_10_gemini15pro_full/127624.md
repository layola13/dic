needlepoint:/ˈniːdlˌpɔɪnt/| n.| 刺绣, 针尖, 针绣活 |A form of embroidery worked with a needle and yarn on canvas by creating small stitches that cover the canvas completely.  It creates a textured surface with a variety of stitches and designs.| My grandmother loves to do needlepoint in her spare time. 我的祖母喜欢在空闲时间做针线活。| synonym: embroidery, tapestry, cross-stitch | antonym: - | noun
