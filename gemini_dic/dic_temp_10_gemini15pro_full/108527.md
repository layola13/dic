lazaret:/ˈlæzəˌrɛt/ /ˈlæzəˌret/| n.|隔离医院，检疫所；（船上的）隔离病房|A lazaret is a place, usually a hospital or ship, where people with infectious diseases are kept away from other people to prevent the disease from spreading. |The ship had a lazaret for passengers with cholera.  这艘船有一个隔离霍乱病人的隔离病房。

The sailors with yellow fever were quarantined in the lazaret.  患有黄热病的水手被隔离在检疫所。|Synonyms: quarantine station, isolation hospital, pesthouse|Antonyms: general hospital, clinic|noun
