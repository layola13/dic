chirographist:/kaɪˈrɒɡrəfɪst/
n.
书法家，手写专家
A person skilled in handwriting, especially calligraphy or penmanship.
The chirographist's elegant script adorned the wedding invitations.  这位书法家的优美字体装饰了婚礼请柬。
The lawyer hired a chirographist to examine the signature on the will. 律师聘请了一位笔迹专家来检查遗嘱上的签名。
Synonyms: calligrapher, penman, scribe
Antonyms: typist (arguably)
Noun
