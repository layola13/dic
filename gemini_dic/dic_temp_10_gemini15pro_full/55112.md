disruly:/dɪsˈruːlli/ | adj. |  不守规矩的，任性的，难以控制的 |  Behaving in a way that is difficult to control and does not obey rules. | The disruly children were sent out of the classroom.  这些不守规矩的孩子被赶出了教室。| synonyms: unruly, disorderly, boisterous, rambunctious, rebellious, defiant | antonyms: obedient, well-behaved, disciplined, compliant, docile | adjective

disrulily:/dɪsˈruːlili/ | adv. |  不守规矩地，任性地 | In a disruly manner; without discipline or control. | The crowd behaved disrulily after the concert.  音乐会结束后，人群表现得十分混乱。 | synonyms: unruliness, disorderliness, boisterousness, rambunctiousness, rebelliousness, defiance | antonyms: obedience, good behavior, discipline, compliance, docility | adverb

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation or reversal) + rule + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *dis- and regula (“rule”).
