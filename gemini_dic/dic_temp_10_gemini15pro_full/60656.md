electively:/ɪˈlɛktɪvli/| adv.|选择地，可选择地|Done by choice; not required or mandatory.  This means something is optional or can be chosen freely.|Students can electively choose to take advanced placement courses. 学生可以选择参加大学预修课程。|近义词：optionally, voluntarily|反义词:compulsorily, obligatorily|词性:副词

词源: From the adjective "elective" + "-ly".  "Elective" comes from Latin "electus" (chosen), past participle of "eligere" (to choose).
