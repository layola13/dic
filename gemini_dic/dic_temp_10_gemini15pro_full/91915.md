hypermagically:/ˌhaɪpərˈmædʒɪkli/ | adv. | 超魔般地，极其神奇地 |  In a way that surpasses even magic; with extraordinary and supernatural power.  It implies an exaggerated or heightened sense of magical ability or effect. | The potion hypermagically transformed the frog into a prince.  这药水以超魔般的方式将青蛙变成了王子。

The sword hypermagically cut through the enchanted armor. 这把剑以超魔般的方式砍穿了附魔盔甲。 | 近义词: magically, miraculously, supernaturally, preternaturally | 反义词: ordinarily, normally, naturally | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "hyper-" (prefix meaning "over, beyond, excessive") + "magical" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Hyper-" comes from the Greek "hyper" (ὑπέρ). "Magical" comes from the Late Latin "magicus," ultimately from the Greek "magikos" (μαγικός), relating to magi or magic.
