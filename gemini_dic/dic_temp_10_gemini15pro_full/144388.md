outstricken:/ˌaʊtˈstrɪkən/
adjective
受灾的，受折磨的
Overwhelmed by affliction or misfortune.  Severely affected by disease or trouble.
例句：The outstricken community rallied together after the hurricane. 飓风过后，受灾的社区团结在一起。

例句：The outstricken family received support from their neighbors. 受灾的家庭得到了邻居们的支持。

近义词：afflicted, stricken, devastated
反义词：healthy, thriving, prosperous
词性：adjective
