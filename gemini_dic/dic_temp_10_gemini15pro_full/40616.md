convenes:/kənˈviːnz/| v. |召集，集合|to come together for a meeting or activity; to cause a group of people to come together for a meeting|The council convenes three times a year. 理事会每年召开三次会议。|近义词：assemble, gather, meet|反义词: adjourn, disperse|词性:动词
Etymology: From Old French *convenir, from Latin convenīre (“to come together, assemble, agree”), from con- (“together”) + venīre (“to come”).
