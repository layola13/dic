nonrumination:/ˌnɒnˌruːmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 非反刍，不反复思考 | The state of not dwelling on negative thoughts or feelings.  It involves letting go of unhelpful thoughts and focusing on the present moment. |  Research shows that nonrumination is key to good mental health. 研究表明，非反刍对心理健康至关重要。

Nonrumination allows individuals to move forward after setbacks.  非反刍使个人能够在挫折后继续前进。 | 近义词：mindfulness, acceptance, letting go | 反义词：rumination, brooding, dwelling | 词性: noun
