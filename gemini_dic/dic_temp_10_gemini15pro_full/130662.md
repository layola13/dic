nonadventurous:/ˌnɒnədˈventʃərəs/ /ˌnɑːnədˈventʃərəs/| adj.|不爱冒险的，缺乏冒险精神的|Not willing to take risks or try new things; not involving excitement or danger.|例句：He lived a nonadventurous life, always sticking to the same routine. 他过着不爱冒险的生活，总是墨守成规。|近义词：unadventurous, cautious, timid, conservative|反义词:adventurous, daring, bold, reckless|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- + adventurous.  "Adventurous" comes from the Old French "aventure" (chance, happening, adventure), ultimately from the Latin "adventura" (a thing about to happen).
