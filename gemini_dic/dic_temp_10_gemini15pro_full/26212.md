buttered:/ˈbʌtərd/| adj.|涂了黄油的；奉承的|Covered with butter; flattering or insincere.|例句：He ate buttered toast for breakfast. 他早餐吃了涂黄油的吐司。|近义词：greased, oiled, flattered|反义词:dry, unbuttered|词性:形容词
buttered:/ˈbʌtərd/| v.(butter的过去式和过去分词)|涂黄油；奉承|To spread butter on something; to flatter someone excessively.|例句：She buttered the bread generously. 她慷慨地在面包上涂了黄油。|近义词：greased, oiled, flattered|反义词:dry|词性:动词
