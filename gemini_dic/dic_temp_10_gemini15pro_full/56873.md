dosa:/ˈdoʊsə/ (US), /ˈdəʊsə/ (UK)| n.|多萨饼，一种南印度薄饼|A thin, crispy pancake made from fermented rice and lentil batter, originating in South India. It is typically served with sambar (a lentil-based vegetable stew) and chutney.|Example 1: I had a delicious dosa for breakfast this morning. 我今天早上吃了一份美味的多萨饼作为早餐。

Example 2:  Dosa is a popular South Indian dish. 多萨饼是一种很受欢迎的南印度菜肴。|近义词:crepe, pancake|反义词: (No direct antonym for a food item)|词性:noun
