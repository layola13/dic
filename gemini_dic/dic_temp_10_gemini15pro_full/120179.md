mindfulness:/ˈmaɪndflnəs/ | n. | 正念，专注力 | The state of being conscious or aware of something.  A mental state achieved by focusing one's awareness on the present moment, while calmly acknowledging and accepting one's feelings, thoughts, and bodily sensations, used as a therapeutic technique. |  She practices mindfulness meditation every morning. 她每天早上练习正念冥想。 | synonyms: awareness, attention, concentration, focus, presence | antonyms: distraction, inattention, heedlessness | noun
