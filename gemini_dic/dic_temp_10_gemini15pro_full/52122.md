diandrian:/daɪˈændriən/| adj.|雌雄同体的，阴阳同体的|An organism or individual having both male and female reproductive organs.|例句：Some species of fish are diandrian, possessing both testes and ovaries. 一些鱼类是雌雄同体的，同时拥有睾丸和卵巢。

例句：The diandrian flower has both stamens and pistils. 这朵雌雄同体的花既有雄蕊也有雌蕊。|近义词：hermaphroditic, monoecious, bisexual|反义词: unisexual, dioecious|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek *di-* (two) + *andr-* (man, male) +-ian (suffix forming adjectives).  Essentially meaning "two-male," but in the biological sense of having both male and female characteristics.
