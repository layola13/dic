frau:/fraʊ/

词源: From German Frau (“woman, wife, Mrs.”).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *frōwaz (“lord, master, husband”).  Cognate with Dutch vrouw,  English *frowe (obsolete).  The original meaning referred to a woman of high social standing, a lady.
