animators:/ˈænɪmeɪtərz/| n.|动画师，动画制作人|A person who creates animated movies or cartoons.  They bring characters and stories to life through drawings, computer graphics, or other visual techniques.|The animators worked tirelessly to finish the film on schedule. 动画师们不知疲倦地工作，以按时完成电影。|Synonyms: cartoonist, designer, artist|Antonyms:  None (Difficult to define a direct opposite)|Noun
