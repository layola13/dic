commendatory:/kəˈmɛndətɔːri/
adjective
称赞的；赞赏的；推荐的
expressing or conveying commendation; praising or approving.
The teacher wrote a commendatory note on the student's paper. 老师在学生的论文上写了一份表扬信。
synonyms:laudatory, praising, complimentary, approving, favorable, flattering
antonyms:critical, derogatory, unfavorable, negative
词性:adjective


commendatory:/kəˈmɛndətɔːri/
adjective
称赞的；赞赏的；推荐的
serving to recommend something to another.
The professor wrote a commendatory letter for the student applying to graduate school.  教授为申请研究生院的学生写了一封推荐信。
synonyms:recommendatory, approbatory
antonyms:critical, derogatory, unfavorable, negative
词性:adjective
