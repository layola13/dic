closes:/kloʊzɪz/ /kləʊzɪz/ | v. | 关闭，结束，停止；靠近，接近；成交 | The store closes at 9 p.m.  这家商店晚上9点关门。| 近义词： shuts, ends, finishes, concludes | 反义词： opens, begins, starts | verb (third-person singular simple present closes, present participle closing, simple past and past participle closed)


closes:/kloʊzɪz/ /kləʊzɪz/ | n. | 结束，终止；（尤指街道尽头的）死胡同 | The closing of the factory resulted in job losses. 工厂的关闭导致了失业。| 近义词： end, finish, conclusion | 反义词： beginning, start, opening | noun (plural closes)


Etymology: From Middle English *closen, closin, from Anglo-Norman clos, close, past participle of clore, from Latin claudere (“to shut, close”).
