outshouting:/ˌaʊtˈʃaʊtɪŋ/
verb
大声喊叫，盖过…的喊叫声
the act of shouting louder than someone else
The two groups were outshouting each other, making it impossible to hear anything clearly.  两组人互相大声喊叫，让人根本听不清任何东西。
近义词: yelling, screaming, overpowering
反义词: whispering, murmuring
词性: verb (present participle of outshout)

outshout:/ˌaʊtˈʃaʊt/
verb
喊得比…响亮；盖过…的喊叫声
to shout louder than someone else
He outshouted the other protestors, his voice booming across the square. 他喊得比其他抗议者都响亮，他的声音在广场上回荡。
近义词: yell over, drown out
反义词: be drowned out by
词性: verb


词源:
"out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding," "surpassing") + "shout" (verb meaning "to utter a loud cry")
