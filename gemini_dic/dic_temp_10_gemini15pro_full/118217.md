meracious:/mɛˈreɪʃəs/
adjective
部分的；不完全的；零散的
Consisting of distinct parts or fragments; incomplete; piecemeal.
The meracious manuscript, with several pages missing, offered only a fragmented glimpse into the past.  残缺不全的手稿，缺少了好几页，只提供了对过去零碎的了解。
The historian pieced together the meracious accounts of the battle to form a coherent narrative. 历史学家将零碎的战斗记录拼凑在一起，形成了一个连贯的叙述。
近义词: fragmentary, incomplete, partial, piecemeal
反义词: complete, whole, entire
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *meraceus, from Latin merax (“pure, unmixed”), from merus (“pure, undiluted”). The semantic shift likely occurred through the notion of a substance being "pure" only in parts, hence "incomplete" or "partial."
