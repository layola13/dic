atomizer:/ˈætəˌmaɪzər/ (US), /ˈætəˌmaɪzə(r)/ (UK)| n.|雾化器; 喷雾器|A device that converts a liquid into a fine spray or mist.|例句：She used an atomizer to spray perfume. 她用喷雾器喷香水。

例句：The paint atomizer made it easy to cover a large surface. 油漆喷雾器使覆盖大面积变得容易。|近义词：sprayer, nebulizer, vaporizer|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


atomizers:/ˈætəˌmaɪzərz/ (US), /ˈætəˌmaɪzə(r)z/ (UK)| n.|雾化器; 喷雾器（复数）|Plural form of atomizer, referring to multiple devices that convert liquid into a fine spray.|例句：The store sells a variety of atomizers for different purposes. 这家商店出售各种不同用途的喷雾器。
