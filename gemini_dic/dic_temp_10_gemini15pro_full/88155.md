hilled:/hɪld/| adj.| 丘陵的，多丘陵的；被覆盖的 | Covered with hills; having many hills. | The region is characterized by its hilled terrain.  这个地区以丘陵地形为特征。| The path led through a hilled landscape. 这条小路穿过一片丘陵地带。 | 近义词: hilly, undulating, rolling | 反义词: flat, level | 词性: adjective

词源: From hill + -ed.  来自名词 "hill" (小山，丘陵) 加上形容词后缀 "-ed"，表示“具有...特征的”。
