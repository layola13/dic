grampus:/ˈɡræmpəs/| n.|逆戟鲸，虎鲸；海豚（旧称）|A grampus is a type of toothed whale, also known as an orca or killer whale.  In older usage, it could also refer to dolphins.|We saw a pod of grampuses breaching the surface. 我们看到一群逆戟鲸跃出水面。|The old sailor called it a grampus, but it was clearly a dolphin. 老水手称它为海豚，但很明显它是一只海豚。|Synonym: orca, killer whale, (archaically) dolphin|Antonym: (No direct antonym)|noun
