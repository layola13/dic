fluoresce:/flʊəˈrɛs/ /flɔːˈrɛs/| v. |发出荧光；荧光闪烁|To emit light by fluorescence.|例句：The mineral fluoresces under ultraviolet light. 这种矿物在紫外线下发出荧光。|例句：Certain jellyfish fluoresce in the dark depths of the ocean.  某些水母在黑暗的海洋深处发出荧光。|近义词：glow, shine, gleam|反义词: darken|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fluorescere, from fluor "a flowing, stream, river" (related to fluere "to flow") + -escere (inceptive suffix).  The sense evolution is from "flowing" to "flowing forth of light".  First used in English in the mid-19th century.
