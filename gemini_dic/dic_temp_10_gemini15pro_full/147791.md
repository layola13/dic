overprotected:/ˌoʊvərprəˈtɛktɪd/
adjective
过度保护的
Having been protected too much, especially by parents, and therefore lacking independence and the ability to deal with difficulties.
The overprotected child struggled to adapt to college life. 这个被过度保护的孩子难以适应大学生活。
overprotected children often have difficulty coping with challenges. 受过度保护的孩子通常难以应对挑战。

近义词:sheltered, coddled, pampered
反义词:independent, self-reliant, neglected

词性:adjective
