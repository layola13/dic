obnoxiousness:/əbˈnɑːkʃəsnəs/
n.
令人讨厌; 讨厌; 令人反感
The quality of being extremely unpleasant.
His obnoxiousness made him unpopular. 他的讨厌让他不受欢迎。
近义词: offensiveness, unpleasantness, repulsiveness
反义词: pleasantness, agreeableness, charm
词性: 名词

obnoxious:/əbˈnɑːkʃəs/
adj.
令人讨厌的; 讨厌的; 令人反感的
Extremely unpleasant.
He's an obnoxious man. 他是个令人讨厌的人。
近义词: offensive, unpleasant, repulsive
反义词: pleasant, agreeable, charming
词性: 形容词
