moodinesses:/ˈmuːdinɪsɪz/
n.
情绪波动；喜怒无常
The state of being moody; fits of moodiness.  
Her moodinesses made it difficult to plan activities. 她的情绪反复无常，让人很难计划活动。
近义词：capriciousness, volatility, temperamentalness
反义词：stability, even-temperedness
词性：名词(plural of moodiness)
词源：From moody + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  Moody originates from Old English *modig, meaning "proud, arrogant," later evolving to encompass changing moods.
