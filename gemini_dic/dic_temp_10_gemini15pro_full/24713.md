brontosaurus:/ˌbrɒntəˈsɔːrəs/ (US) /ˌbrɒntəˈsɔːrəs/ (UK)| n. |雷龙|A huge quadrupedal herbivorous dinosaur that lived in the late Jurassic period. It is now considered to be the same species as Apatosaurus.|The brontosaurus is one of the most iconic dinosaurs.  雷龙是最具标志性的恐龙之一。|The museum's centerpiece was a massive brontosaurus skeleton.  博物馆的中心展品是一个巨大的雷龙骨架。|Synonym: Apatosaurus|Antonym: N/A|noun
