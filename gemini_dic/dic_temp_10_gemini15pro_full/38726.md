conditionality:/ˌkɒndɪʃəˈnælɪti/ /ˌkɑːndɪʃəˈnælɪti/
n.
条件性；附带条件
The conditionality of the loan was strict. 这笔贷款的附带条件很严格。
The IMF imposed strict conditionalities on the loan. 国际货币基金组织对这笔贷款施加了严格的条件。
近义词：stipulation, provision, requirement, prerequisite
反义词：unconditionality (较少用), absence of conditions
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "conditional" + "-ity."  "Conditional" comes from the Late Latin "conditionalis,"  meaning "pertaining to a condition,"  from "condicio" (condition) + "-alis" (relating to).
