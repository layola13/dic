grevy:/ˈɡreɪvi/| adj. n.|格雷维的，格雷维氏斑马|Relating to or denoting a species of zebra (Equus grevyi) native to Kenya and Ethiopia, the largest living wild equid and characterized by narrow stripes, large ears, and a mule-like appearance.  Also can be used as a noun to refer to the Grevy's zebra itself.|例句：The Grevy's zebra is the largest species of zebra. 格雷维氏斑马是最大的斑马种类。

例句：The grevy’s coat is marked by distinctive narrow stripes. 格雷维氏斑马的皮毛上有独特的窄条纹。|近义词：zebra, equid|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun
