hominins:/ˈhɒmɪnɪnz/ | n. | 人族，人类 | Extinct and living members of the human lineage.  Specifically, the group consisting of modern humans, extinct human species and all our immediate ancestors (including members of the genera *Homo*, *Australopithecus*, *Paranthropus* and *Ardipithecus*). |  Many hominins used tools. 许多人族使用工具。| synonyms: hominids (broader term), humans, human ancestors | antonyms:  (no direct antonym, but could consider other primates or mammals) | noun
