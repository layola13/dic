blunder:/ˈblʌndər/| n. vi. |错误，失误；踉跄，跌跌撞撞地走|A stupid or careless mistake.  A clumsy or embarrassing mistake.|例句：He made a terrible blunder by revealing the secret. 他犯了一个可怕的错误，泄露了秘密。

例句：She blundered into the wrong room. 她踉踉跄跄地走进了错误的房间。|近义词：error, mistake, gaffe, slip-up|反义词：success, triumph|词性:名词,动词
