muchness:/ˈmʌtʃnəs/
n.
大量; 程度; 过多; (尤指不必要的)繁琐
A large amount or extent, especially (and often unnecessarily) elaborate or complicated.
例句：There wasn't much muchness to the party.  这个派对没什么意思。

例句：After all the hype, the film itself was a bit of a disappointment, lacking muchness.  经过大肆宣传之后，这部电影本身却有点令人失望，缺乏亮点。

近义词：excess, superfluity, abundance, elaborateness, ornateness
反义词：simplicity, conciseness, brevity, moderateness
词性:名词
