bossdoms:/ˈbɒsdəmz/
n.
统治地位；控制权；势力范围
The position, power, or authority of a boss or bosses.  The areas or spheres of influence controlled by a boss or bosses.
The mayor's bossdoms extended throughout the city. 市长的势力范围遍及全城。
Synonyms: domains, empires, territories, fiefdoms
Antonyms: subordination, subservience
词性: 名词
