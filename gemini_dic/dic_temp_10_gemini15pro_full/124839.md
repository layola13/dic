muckraker:/ˈmʌkreɪkər/| n.|揭发黑幕的记者; 专门揭发丑闻的作家|A journalist who investigates and exposes societal problems, especially in business or politics.|例句：The muckraker exposed the corrupt practices of the company. 这位揭发黑幕的记者揭露了这家公司的腐败行为。

例句：Upton Sinclair was a famous muckraker who wrote "The Jungle".  厄普顿·辛克莱是一位著名的揭发黑幕的作家，他写了《屠场》。


|近义词：investigative journalist, whistleblower, exposer|反义词:apologist, propagandist|词性:noun
