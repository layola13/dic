fluffinesses:/ˈflʌfinɪsɪz/
n.
柔软度；蓬松度；绒毛状
the quality of being fluffy; the state of having a lot of fluff.
The fluffinesses of the kittens' fur made them irresistible to cuddle. 小猫毛茸茸的皮毛让人忍不住想抱抱它们。
softness, downiness, airiness, lightness, cloudiness
hardness, firmness, stiffness, density, compactness
