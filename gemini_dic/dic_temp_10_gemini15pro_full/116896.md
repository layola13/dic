mayfish: /ˈmeɪfɪʃ/ | n. | 五月鱼，白鱼 | A small freshwater fish of the genus *Alosa*, related to the herring and shad, that migrates up rivers from the sea to spawn, typically in May. It is also known as the alewife, river herring, or shad. |  The mayfish swim upstream each spring to spawn.  五月鱼每年春天都会逆流而上产卵。| 近义词: alewife, river herring, shad | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific fish species.) | noun
