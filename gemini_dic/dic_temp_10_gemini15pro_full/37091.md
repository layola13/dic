combine:/kəmˈbaɪn/ (US) /ˈkɒmˌbaɪn/ (UK)| v.  n.|结合，联合；化合|To join or mix two or more things together.|例句：Hydrogen and oxygen combine to form water. 氢和氧结合形成水。|近义词：mix, blend, merge, unite, integrate|反义词:separate, divide, split|verb

combine:/ˈkɒmbɪn/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmbaɪn/ (US)| n.|联合收割机；联合企业|A machine that harvests grain crops; a group of people or businesses working together.|例句：The farmer used a combine to harvest the wheat. 农民用联合收割机收割小麦。|近义词：alliance, coalition, merger|反义词:division, separation|noun
