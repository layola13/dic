cocoonlike:/kəˈkuːnlaɪk/| adj.|茧状的，像茧一样的|Resembling a cocoon; protective and enclosing.|The child slept soundly, wrapped in a cocoonlike blanket. 孩子睡得很香，裹在像茧一样的毯子里。|The room had a cocoonlike atmosphere, warm and comforting. 房间里有一种茧状的氛围，温暖而舒适。|近义词：protective, sheltering, enveloping, enclosing|反义词：exposed, open, vulnerable|词性:形容词
词源: cocoon (n.) + -like (suffix). Cocoon comes from French cocon, from Provençal coucoun, of unknown origin. -like is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling," "characteristic of," "similar to," "appropriate to."
