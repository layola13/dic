cameralistic: /ˌkæmərəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
财政学的；国库的
Of or relating to public finance or public revenue.
The government adopted a cameralistic approach to budgeting. 政府采取了一种财政学的预算方法。
The cameralistic system of financial administration.  财政管理的国库制度。
近义词: fiscal, financial, budgetary
反义词: None
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From German *kameralistisch*, from *Kameralwissenschaft* "science of public finance", from Late Latin *camera* "chamber, treasury".
