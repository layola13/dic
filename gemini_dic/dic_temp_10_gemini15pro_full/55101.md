disrobed:/dɪsˈroʊbd/
verb
脱去…的衣服；使…裸露
past participle of disrobe  剥夺（某人）的（衣服、权力或财产）
The queen was disrobed and prepared for bed by her ladies-in-waiting. 女王由侍女们脱去衣服准备睡觉。
Synonyms: undressed, unclothed, naked, stripped, bare
Antonyms: dressed, clothed, robed, attired
word type: verb (past participle)
