constituencies:/kənˈstɪtʃuənsiz/| n. |选区；支持者；顾客；读者|A body of voters or the residents of a district represented by an elected legislator or official.  A group of supporters or patrons.|例句：The politician worked hard to represent the interests of his constituencies. 这位政治家努力代表他的选区的利益。

例句：The magazine aims to expand its constituencies to include younger readers. 这本杂志旨在扩大其读者群，以包括年轻读者。|近义词：electorates, districts, supporters, patrons, readership|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
