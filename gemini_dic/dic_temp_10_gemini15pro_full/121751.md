mispublicized:/ˌmɪspʌˈblɪsaɪzd/
adj.
被错误宣传的；宣传失当的
Given incorrect publicity; publicized badly or inaccurately.
The event was mispublicized, leading to a low turnout.  这个活动宣传失误，导致参加人数很少。
The new product launch was mispublicized, resulting in confusion among consumers.  新产品的发布宣传失当，导致消费者感到困惑。
近义词: misrepresented, distorted, garbled
反义词: accurately publicized, well-publicized
词性: adjective


词源:
mis- (prefix):  meaning "wrongly" or "badly"
publicize (verb): from public + -ize, meaning "to make public"
-ed (suffix):  forming the past participle of a verb, also used to create adjectives
