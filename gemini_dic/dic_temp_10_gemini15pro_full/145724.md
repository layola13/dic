overcredulous:/ˌoʊvərˈkrɛdʒələs/| adj.|过于轻信的，易受骗的|Too willing to believe things, even if they seem unlikely or improbable.|He was overcredulous and easily taken in by scams. 他太容易轻信，很容易被骗局所欺骗。|近义词：gullible, naive, trusting|反义词:skeptical, cynical, incredulous|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *overcredulus, from Latin over- (over, excessively) + credulus (believing, trustful), from credere (to believe).
