interosculation: /ɪntərˌɒskjʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 接合，吻合；（血管、树枝等的）连生 | The interosculation of the branches created a natural archway. 树枝的连生形成了一个天然的拱门。|  The process of interosculation allows the trees to share resources. 连生的过程使树木能够共享资源。 | 近义词: anastomosis, inosculation, coalescence | 反义词: separation, division | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From Latin *interosculatio*, from inter- "between" + osculatio "kissing, a kissing," from osculare "to kiss," frequentative of osculari "to kiss," from osculum "little mouth," diminutive of os "mouth."
