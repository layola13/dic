hypotheticals:/ˌhaɪpəˈθɛtɪkəlz/| n.| 假设，假设的情况|  Things that are based on a hypothesis; things that are imagined or supposed but not necessarily real or true. |Many scientists dismiss the dangers of AI as mere hypotheticals. 许多科学家认为人工智能的危险只是假设。| synonyms: suppositions, possibilities, contingencies | antonyms: realities, certainties | noun

hypothetical:/ˌhaɪpəˈθɛtɪkəl/| adj.| 假设的，假定的| Based on or serving as a hypothesis; supposed but not necessarily real or true. |Let's consider a hypothetical situation.  让我们考虑一个假设的情况。| synonyms: theoretical, conjectural, assumed | antonyms: real, actual, factual | adjective
