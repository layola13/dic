loucher:/ˈlaʊtʃər/ | n. | 懒汉；游手好闲的人；乞丐 | someone who is lazy and avoids work; an idler; a beggar | He was known as a bit of a loucher, always trying to avoid honest work. 他被认为有点懒，总是想逃避正当的工作。|  He's a loucher who prefers begging to working. 他是个游手好闲的人，宁愿乞讨也不愿工作。 | synonyms: idler, loafer, slacker, beggar | antonyms: hard worker, grafter | noun
