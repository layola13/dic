alumish:/əˈlʌmɪʃ/
adjective
像明矾的；略带涩味的；含明矾的
Somewhat resembling or suggestive of alum; slightly astringent.
The water tasted slightly alumish.  这水尝起来有点像明矾。
近义词: astringent, tart
反义词: sweet, bland
词性: adjective

alumish:/əˈlʌmɪʃ/
adjective
像明矾的；略带涩味的；含明矾的
Containing or treated with alum.
Alumish water can be used as a mordant in dyeing.  含明矾的水可以用作染色中的媒染剂。
近义词: aluminous
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: adjective
