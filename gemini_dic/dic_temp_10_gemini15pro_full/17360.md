bead:/biːd/| n. |珠子；水珠；滴；念珠|A small, usually round piece of glass, wood, plastic, or other material with a hole through it, often used for making jewellery or for decoration. Also, a drop of liquid.|She wore a necklace of beautiful glass beads. 她戴着一条漂亮的玻璃珠项链。|synonyms: pearl, droplet, globule|antonyms: N/A|noun

bead:/biːd/| v. |形成珠状；用珠子装饰|To form into beads or droplets; to decorate with beads.|Sweat beaded on his forehead. 他额头上渗出了汗珠。|synonyms: adorn, decorate, embellish|antonyms: N/A|verb
