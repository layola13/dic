acronyctous: /ˌækrəˈnɪktəs/
adjective
夜间活动的；夜间开花的
Active or occurring at night, especially after dusk.  Relating to or designating flowers that open at night and close during the day.
Bats are acronyctous creatures, flying and hunting in the dark.  蝙蝠是夜间活动的生物，在黑暗中飞行和捕猎。

The night-blooming jasmine is an acronyctous plant, releasing its fragrance after sunset. 夜来香是一种夜间开花的植物，在日落后释放香味。
近义词: nocturnal, night-blooming
反义词: diurnal, day-blooming
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *akrónyktos* "happening at nightfall," from *ákros* "at the tip, extreme" + *nýx* "night."
