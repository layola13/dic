colonizabilities:/ˌkɒlənaɪzəˈbɪlɪtiz/
n.|可殖民性；可定殖性|The colonizabilities of different environments vary greatly depending on factors like resource availability and climate. 不同环境的可殖民性差异很大，取决于资源可用性和气候等因素。|The study examined the colonizabilities of various islands in the archipelago. 这项研究调查了群岛中各个岛屿的可殖民性。|近义词:suitability, adaptability|反义词:unsuitability, resistance|词性:名词

colonizability:/ˌkɒlənaɪzəˈbɪlɪti/
n.|可殖民性；可定殖性|The colonizability of Mars is a topic of ongoing research and debate. 火星的可殖民性是一个持续研究和辩论的话题。|The colonizability of a new habitat depends on many factors, including the presence of water and suitable temperatures. 新栖息地的可殖民性取决于许多因素，包括水的存在和合适的温度。|近义词:settleability, habitability|反义词:uninhabitability, inhospitality|词性:名词


词源:
From colonize (殖民) + -able (可...的) + -ity (名词后缀).  Ultimately from Latin *colonus* (farmer, settler).
