malfunction:/mælˈfʌŋkʃən/| n. vi.|故障，失灵|A malfunction is a failure to function or operate properly.  It refers to a breakdown or problem in a system or machine.|The printer malfunctioned and printed gibberish. 打印机发生故障，打印出了乱码。|Synonyms: failure, breakdown, glitch, defect, error|Antonyms:  function, operate, work, perform|noun
malfunction:/mælˈfʌŋkʃən/| vi.|发生故障，失灵|To malfunction means to fail to function or operate properly.|The engine malfunctioned mid-flight. 引擎在飞行途中发生故障。|Synonyms: fail, break down,  go wrong, act up|Antonyms: function, operate, work, perform|verb

Simple Present:
The computer malfunctions frequently. 这台电脑经常出故障。
The system malfunctions when overloaded. 系统超载时会发生故障。

Present Continuous:
The printer is malfunctioning again. 打印机又出故障了。
The air conditioning is malfunctioning in this heat.  空调在这么热的天气里坏了。

Present Perfect:
The robot has malfunctioned several times this week.  这个机器人本周已经出现好几次故障了。
My phone has malfunctioned since the latest update.  我的手机自从最近一次更新后就一直出故障。

Present Perfect Continuous:
The engine has been malfunctioning intermittently for weeks.  发动机断断续续地故障了好几个星期。
The security system has been malfunctioning, causing false alarms.  安全系统一直出故障，导致误报。

Simple Past:
The car malfunctioned on the highway.  汽车在高速公路上抛锚了。
The elevator malfunctioned and we were trapped inside.  电梯发生故障，我们被困在里面了。

Past Continuous:
The machine was malfunctioning when the power went out.  停电时机器正在发生故障。
The traffic lights were malfunctioning, causing chaos.  交通灯发生故障，造成了混乱。

Past Perfect:
The brakes had malfunctioned before the accident.  事故发生前刹车就已经失灵了。
The computer system had malfunctioned, resulting in data loss.  计算机系统发生了故障，导致数据丢失。

Past Perfect Continuous:
The equipment had been malfunctioning for months before it was finally replaced.  这台设备在最终被更换之前已经故障了几个月。
The generator had been malfunctioning, causing power outages.  发电机一直发生故障，导致停电。

Simple Future:
If you don't maintain it properly, the machine will malfunction.  如果你没有正确维护它，机器就会发生故障。
The system will malfunction if it overheats.  如果系统过热，它就会发生故障。

Future Continuous:
The technicians will be malfunctioning the system for testing purposes. 技术人员将使系统故障以进行测试。（此句例句不太自然，因为malfunction通常不是人为故意造成的）
We anticipate the server will be malfunctioning during the peak hours. 我们预计服务器将在高峰时段出现故障。

Future Perfect:
By the time we reach Mars, the life support systems will have malfunctioned. 等我们到达火星时，生命维持系统将会出现故障。（此句例句假设一种不太可能发生的情况）
The satellite will have malfunctioned by the end of its mission. 到任务结束时，卫星将会出现故障。


Future Perfect Continuous:
By next year, the equipment will have been malfunctioning for over a decade. 到明年，该设备将已经故障超过十年了。
The spacecraft will have been malfunctioning for several months by the time it returns to Earth.  当宇宙飞船返回地球时，它将已经故障好几个月了。

Subjunctive Mood:
If the system were to malfunction, we would have a backup plan.  如果系统发生故障，我们将有一个备用方案。
I wish the printer wouldn't malfunction every time I need it.  我希望打印机不要每次我需要它的时候都发生故障。


Passive Voice:
The problem was caused by a malfunctioning sensor.  这个问题是由一个故障传感器引起的。
The error was determined to be caused by a malfunctioning component.  经确定，该错误是由一个故障组件引起的。


Interrogative Sentence:
Why is the machine malfunctioning?  为什么机器会发生故障？
What caused the system to malfunction?  是什么导致系统发生故障？


Negative Sentence:
The system did not malfunction during the test.  系统在测试期间没有发生故障。
The car hasn't malfunctioned since it was repaired.  汽车自从修理后就没有发生故障。
