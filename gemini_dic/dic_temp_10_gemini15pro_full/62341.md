endemisms:/ˈɛndəmɪzəmz/
n.
地方性，特有物种；特有种现象
The state of being unique to a defined geographic location.
The Galapagos Islands are known for their high level of endemisms.  加拉帕戈斯群岛以其高度的地方性而闻名。
Many endemic species are threatened by habitat loss. 许多特有物种都受到栖境丧失的威胁。
endemism, nativeness, indigenousness
cosmopolitanism
noun
