contending:/kənˈtɛndɪŋ/
| adj. | 争夺的，竞争的；主张的 | struggling in rivalry or competition; asserting something as a position in an argument |  Many teams are contending for the championship this year. 今年很多队伍都在争夺冠军。|  The lawyer is contending that his client is innocent.  律师主张他的客户是无辜的。 |  competing, vying, battling, arguing, asserting | conceding, yielding, surrendering | adjective

contend:/kənˈtɛnd/
| vi. | 竞争，争夺；主张，声称 | to strive in rivalry; to maintain or assert | Two teams contended for the victory. 两支队伍争夺胜利。 | He contended that the judge was wrong. 他声称法官错了。 | compete, vie, battle, argue, assert | concede, yield, surrender | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "contendere" (to stretch out, strive, contend), from "com-" (together) + "tendere" (to stretch).
