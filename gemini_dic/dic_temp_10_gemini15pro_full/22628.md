bopper:/ˈbɒpə(r)/ (US) /ˈbɑːpər/ (UK)| n.|摇摆舞爱好者，爵士乐爱好者|A person who enjoys or performs bop music, especially a young person in the 1940s or 1950s who followed the trends associated with bop music.  It can also more generally refer to someone who enjoys popular music and dancing.|She was a real bopper back in the day, always jiving to the latest tunes. 她过去是个真正的摇摆舞爱好者，总是随着最新的曲调摇摆。|Synonyms:  jitterbug, hepcat, fan, enthusiast|Antonyms:  square|noun
