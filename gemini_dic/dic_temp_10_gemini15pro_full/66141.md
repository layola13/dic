exceptionals:/ɪkˈsɛpʃənəlz/| n.|杰出的人（或事物）；残疾儿童|people or things that are exceptional, especially children with disabilities|The school has a special program for exceptionals. 这所学校为特殊儿童开设了专门的课程。|synonyms: prodigies, geniuses, stars, elites, gifted individuals (for people); rarities, anomalies, outliers (for things)|antonyms: average individuals, ordinary things, common items|noun
