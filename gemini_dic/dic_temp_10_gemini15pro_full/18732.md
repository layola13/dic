bergmaster:/ˈbɜːrɡˌmɑːstər/ | n. |矿山管理员，矿长| A person in charge of a mine, especially in South Africa. | The bergmaster ensured the safety regulations were followed meticulously. 矿长确保安全规章得到严格遵守。|  The bergmaster inspected the mine for potential hazards. 矿长检查了矿井中潜在的危险。 |Synonyms: mine manager, superintendent, foreman | Antonyms: miner, worker| noun
