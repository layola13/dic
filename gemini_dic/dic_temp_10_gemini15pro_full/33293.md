chub:/tʃʌb/| n.|圆胖的人（或动物）；鲤科小淡水鱼|A short, thick person or animal, especially a child; a small freshwater fish of the carp family.|The little chub was splashing in the shallows.|近义词：plump, chubby, stout, portly|反义词:skinny, thin|名词

chub:/tʃʌb/| v.|钓鱼（尤指钓鲤科小淡水鱼）|To fish for chub.|He spent the afternoon chubbing in the river.|近义词：fish, angle|反义词:None|动词
