etherate:/ˈiːθəreɪt/| n. |醚化物；醚合物|A compound formed by the combination of a substance with ether.|The Grignard reagent is prepared as an etherate. 格氏试剂是以醚合物形式制备的。|
etherate:/ˈiːθəreɪt/| v. |使醚化|To treat or combine with ether.|The magnesium is etherated to form the Grignard reagent. 镁被醚化形成格氏试剂。|
近义词:complex, adduct|反义词:|n.|
近义词:combine, react|反义词:decompose, separate|v.|
