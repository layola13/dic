at-the-least:/æt ðə liːst/| adverbial phrase | 至少，起码 |  Indicates the minimum amount or degree of something. It emphasizes that the actual amount or degree might be higher. | He earns at the least $50,000 a year. 他每年至少挣 5 万美元。|Synonyms: at a minimum, minimally, in the least, not less than | Antonyms: at the most, maximally, at most | phrase

at-the-least:/æt ðə liːst/| adjective | 最起码的，最低限度的 (Less common usage as an adjective) |  Describing something that is the minimum acceptable. |  The at-the-least requirement for this job is a bachelor's degree.  这项工作的最低要求是学士学位。 | Synonyms: minimum, minimal | Antonyms: maximum, maximal | adjective
