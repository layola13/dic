fets:/fɛts/ | n. | 场效应晶体管（Field-Effect Transistors）| Field-Effect Transistors, a type of transistor that uses an electric field to control the flow of current.  They are widely used in electronic circuits for amplification and switching.|  Modern computers use millions of FETs on a single chip. 现代计算机的单个芯片上使用了数百万个场效应晶体管。|  The amplifier circuit uses FETs for improved linearity. 该放大器电路使用场效应晶体管以提高线性度。| transistors, MOSFETs | diodes | noun
