communists: /ˈkɒmjənɪsts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmjənɪsts/ (US)| n. |共产主义者|A person who supports or believes in the principles of communism, a political and economic ideology that advocates for a classless society where the means of production are owned and controlled by the community as a whole.|The communists held a rally to protest the government's policies. 共产主义者举行集会抗议政府的政策。|Synonyms: Marxists, socialists, radicals|Antonyms: capitalists, conservatives|Noun
