nonapparently:/ˌnɒnəˈpærəntlɪ/ | adv. | 不明显地，不显然地 | Not in an apparent or obvious way; subtly or imperceptibly. | The changes were nonapparently made, almost invisible to the casual observer. 这些变化很不明显，几乎不会被随便的观察者注意到。 | The disease progressed nonapparently, with few outward symptoms.  这种疾病进展缓慢，几乎没有外在症状。| 近义词:inconspicuously, subtly, imperceptibly | 反义词: apparently, obviously, visibly | 词性:副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
apparently (adverb): from apparent (adjective), from Latin *apparēns, present participle of apparēre "to appear," from ad- "to" + parēre "to appear, be visible"
