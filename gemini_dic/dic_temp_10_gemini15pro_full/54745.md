disparity:/dɪˈspærəti/| n.|不同，差异，不等|a noticeable and often significant difference between two or more things; inequality.|The disparity between the rich and the poor is a serious social problem. 贫富差距是一个严重的社会问题。|synonyms: difference, inequality, gap, imbalance, variation|antonyms: similarity, equality, parity, uniformity|noun


disparity:/dɪˈspærəti/| n.|不一致，悬殊|lack of similarity or equality; inequality or difference, as in age, rank, or degree; difference.|There is a great disparity between his promises and his actions. 他的承诺和行动之间存在巨大差异。|synonyms: discrepancy, inconsistency, incongruity, divergence|antonyms: consistency, agreement, harmony, conformity|noun
