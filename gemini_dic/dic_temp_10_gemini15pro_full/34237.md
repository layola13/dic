clannishnesses:/ˈklænɪʃnəsɪz/
noun
排他性；抱团主义；小集团意识
The tendency to be clannish; exclusivity.
Their clannishnesses made it difficult for outsiders to integrate.  他们的排他性使得外人很难融入。
The tight-knit community was known for its clannishnesses. 这个紧密的社区以其抱团主义而闻名。
Synonyms: exclusiveness, insularity, cliquishness, parochialism
Antonyms: inclusiveness, openness, broad-mindedness
词性: 名词
