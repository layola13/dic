gote:/ɡoʊt/| n.|山羊（古语，现已弃用）|A young goat, especially one that is less than a year old. (This term is now mostly archaic.)|例句：The shepherd tended his flock of gotes. 牧羊人照料着他的羊群（古语用法）。

例句：In olden times, gotes were a valuable source of milk and meat. 在古代，小山羊是牛奶和肉类的宝贵来源（古语用法）。|近义词：kid, goatling|反义词:|词性:名词
