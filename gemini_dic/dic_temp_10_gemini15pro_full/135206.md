nonosmotic:/ˌnɒnɒzˈmɒtɪk/
adjective
非渗透的
Not pertaining to or involving osmosis; not caused by or dependent on osmosis.
The transport of these ions is nonosmotic. 这些离子的运输是非渗透性的。
The movement of water across the membrane is nonosmotic. 水跨膜的运动是非渗透性的。
Near Synonyms: non-osmotic, anosmotic
Antonyms: osmotic
词性: adjective
