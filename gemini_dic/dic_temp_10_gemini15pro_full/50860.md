deselectin:/ˌdiːsəˈlɛktɪn/ | n. |去选择素；去粘附素| A lectin that reverses or prevents cell adhesion. It specifically blocks selectin function.| The deselectin inhibited leukocyte rolling and adhesion to the endothelium. 去选择素抑制了白细胞滚动和粘附到内皮细胞。| Near synonyms: selectin inhibitor, adhesion blocker | Antonyms: selectin, adhesion molecule | noun
