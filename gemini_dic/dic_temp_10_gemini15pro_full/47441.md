deafeningly:/ˈdɛfənɪŋli/| adv.|震耳欲聋地，响亮得使人震耳欲聋|Making a loud and overwhelming noise, so loud as to cause temporary or permanent hearing impairment.  It describes the manner in which something is extremely loud.|The music at the concert was deafeningly loud. 音乐会上的音乐震耳欲聋。|Synonyms: extremely loudly, thunderously, earsplittingly|Antonyms: softly, quietly, faintly|Adverb
