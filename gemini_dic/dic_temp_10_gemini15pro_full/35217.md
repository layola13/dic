cluding:/ˈkluːdɪŋ/| prep.|包括，包含|Part of a larger word, usually following "in" and meaning "including."|例句：Many factors are involved, including cost and time. 涉及许多因素，包括成本和时间。|例句：The price is $10, including tax. 价格是10美元，含税。|近义词：including, comprising, encompassing|反义词：excluding|词性: preposition (介词)

词源: "Cluding" is not a standalone word. It's a truncated form of "including," which comes from the Middle English word "incluyding," and ultimately from the Latin "includere" (to shut in, enclose).
