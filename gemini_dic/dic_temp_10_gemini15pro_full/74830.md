freneticisms:/frɪˈnɛtɪsɪzəmz/
noun
疯狂行为；狂乱举动
A state of intense activity and excitement.  Plural form of freneticism.
The freneticisms of the stock market during the crash left many investors ruined.  股市崩盘期间的疯狂举动让许多投资者破产。
frenzy, hysteria, mania, wildness
calm, composure, peacefulness
n.
