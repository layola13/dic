dyslexic:/dɪsˈleksɪk/ | adj. n. |诵读困难的；患诵读困难的人| Having dyslexia; relating to or affected by dyslexia. |  He is dyslexic and struggles with reading. 他患有诵读困难，阅读很吃力。|  She's a dyslexic student who receives specialized support. 她是一位接受特殊帮助的诵读困难学生。|近义词:reading-disabled |反义词: n/a |词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:  From Greek *dys- "bad, difficult" + *lexis* "speech, word"  + *-ikos* "-ic".
