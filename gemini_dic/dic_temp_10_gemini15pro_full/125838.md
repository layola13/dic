murrion:/ˈmʌriən/ | n. | 头盔，钢盔；(尤指)轻便钢盔 | A piece of armour for the head, especially a light helmet.| He wore a murrion into battle. 他戴着钢盔上战场。 | 近义词: helmet, casque, helm | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 unprotected head 无保护的头部) | 词性: noun


Etymology:
From Middle French *morion*, from Old French *morion*, from Italian *morione*, perhaps from *morro* ("muzzle, snout").  The resemblance of the helmet's peak to a bird's beak or animal snout likely influenced the word's development.
