noticeably:/ˈnoʊtɪsəbli/| adv.|显著地，明显地|In a way that is easy to see or recognize; to a noticeable degree.|Her hand trembled noticeably. 她的手明显地颤抖着。|近义词：significantly, markedly, perceptibly, visibly, clearly|反义词：inconspicuously, subtly, slightly|词性:副词

词源: From noticeable + -ly. Noticeable is from notice + -able. Notice comes from Old French notice.
