befitted:/bɪˈfɪtɪd/| v.|适合，相称|suited, appropriate, proper.  It means something was suitable or appropriate for someone or something. It often implies a sense of dignity or propriety.|The simple elegance of the dress befitted the occasion. 这条裙子的简约优雅与这个场合很相称。|近义词：suited, became, behooved|反义词：mismatched, unbecoming|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + fit + -ed.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "thoroughly" or "completely." "Fit" means suitable or proper.  "-ed" is the past tense suffix.
