glaucophane:/ˈɡlɔːkəˌfeɪn/| n. |蓝闪石|A sodic amphibole mineral, Na2(Mg,Fe)3Al2Si8O22(OH)2, typically occurring as blue prismatic crystals in metamorphic rocks. It is an indicator of high-pressure, low-temperature metamorphism.|Glaucophane is a common mineral in blueschist facies rocks. 蓝闪石是蓝片岩相岩石中常见的矿物。|Glaucophane schist is a beautiful blue metamorphic rock. 蓝闪片岩是一种美丽的蓝色变质岩。|Synonyms:riebeckite, crocidolite|Antonyms:N/A|noun
