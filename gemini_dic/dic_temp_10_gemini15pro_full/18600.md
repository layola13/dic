benitier:/ˌbɛnɪˈtɪər/| n. |圣水器，圣水钵|A vessel for holy water, typically placed near the entrance of a church, used by worshippers for making the sign of the cross.|例句：She dipped her fingers in the benitier and made the sign of the cross. 她将手指浸入圣水钵中，划了个十字。

例句：The benitier was made of carved marble. 圣水钵是大理石雕刻成的。|近义词：holy water font, stoup|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Old French *benitier*, from *beneir* "to bless," from Latin *benedicere*, from *bene* "well" + *dicere* "to speak."
