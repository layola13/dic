overblindly:/ˌoʊvərˈblaɪndli/ | adv. | 过于盲目地; 过于轻信地 | Acting with excessive and uncritical trust or devotion, without proper consideration of consequences or alternatives. | He overblindly followed the advice of his financial advisor and lost a lot of money. 他过于盲目地听从了财务顾问的建议，损失了很多钱。|  She overblindly trusted her friend and was betrayed. 她过于轻信她的朋友，结果被背叛了。 | blindly, foolishly, uncritically, implicitly | cautiously, critically, thoughtfully | adverb
