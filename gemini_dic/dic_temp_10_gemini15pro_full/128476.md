newsmonger:/ˈnjuːzˌmʌŋɡər/| n.|新闻贩子；传播流言蜚语的人|A person who spreads gossip or rumors, especially about important people or events.  Someone who habitually spreads news, especially sensational or gossip. |The newsmonger was quick to share the latest scandal with everyone in the office. 这个新闻贩子很快就把最新的丑闻告诉了办公室里的每一个人。|Synonyms: gossipmonger, scandalmonger, rumormonger|Antonyms:  diplomat, peacemaker|noun
