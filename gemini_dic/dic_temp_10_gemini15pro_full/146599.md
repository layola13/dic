overgrieved:/ˌoʊvərˈɡriːvd/| adj.|过度悲伤的，悲痛欲绝的|Experiencing excessive or overwhelming grief.  Feeling more grief than is considered normal or healthy.|She was overgrieved by the loss of her child. 她因失去孩子而悲痛欲绝。|Synonyms: heartbroken, inconsolable, devastated, distraught|Antonyms: joyful, happy, elated|adjective
