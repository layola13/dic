nonteacher:/ˌnɒnˈtiːtʃər/ (British), /ˌnɑːnˈtiːtʃər/ (American)| n. |非教师人员；非教学人员|A person who is not a teacher; someone employed by a school or educational institution in a non-teaching role. |例句：The school employs several nonteachers, including administrative staff and counselors.  学校雇佣了几名非教师人员，包括行政人员和辅导员。

例句：The nonteachers went on strike for better pay and benefits. 非教师人员举行罢工，要求更好的工资和福利。|近义词：non-academic staff, support staff |反义词：teacher, instructor|词性：名词
