manubrial:/məˈnjuːbriəl/
adjective
胸骨柄的
Relating to the manubrium, the upper part of the sternum.
The manubrial notch is easily palpable.  胸骨柄切迹很容易触及。

manubrial:/məˈnjuːbriəl/
noun
胸骨柄
The upper part of the sternum.
The clavicles articulate with the manubrium. 锁骨与胸骨柄相连。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *manubrium sterni "handle of the breastbone," from Latin manubrium "handle, hilt" (from manus "hand") + sterni (genitive of sternum).

SYNONYMS->
(adjective) sternal
(noun) manubrium sterni


ANTONYMS->
None.
