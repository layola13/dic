mothballs:/ˈmɔːθˌbɔːlz/ (US), /ˈmɒθˌbɔːlz/ (UK)| n. |樟脑丸|Small balls of a strong-smelling chemical substance (typically naphthalene or paradichlorobenzene) used to deter moths and other insects from damaging stored clothes and other textiles.|She put mothballs in her sweaters for the summer. 她把樟脑丸放进了她的毛衣里过夏天。|近义词：moth crystals, moth flakes|反义词: N/A|名词


mothball:/ˈmɔːθˌbɔːl/ (US), /ˈmɒθˌbɔːl/ (UK)| vt. |封存，搁置|To put something into storage or disuse; to stop using or developing something. |The project was mothballed due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该项目被搁置了。|近义词：shelve, suspend, defer|反义词: reactivate, restart|动词
