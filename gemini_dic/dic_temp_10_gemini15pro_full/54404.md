disincarcerate:/ˌdɪsɪnˈkɑːrsəreɪt/
verb
释放，使脱离监禁
To release from imprisonment or confinement.
The organization works to disincarcerate non-violent offenders.  这个组织致力于使非暴力犯罪者脱离监禁。
The new law aims to disincarcerate thousands of prisoners. 新法律旨在释放数千名囚犯。
Synonyms: release, liberate, free, discharge
Antonyms: imprison, incarcerate, confine, detain
词性: 动词
