medallions:/məˈdæljənz/| n. |大奖章，勋章；圆形装饰；(尤指食物的)圆形小块|A small, flat, usually circular object, such as a piece of jewelry, a coin, or a stamped piece of metal, often bearing an inscription or design and used as a decoration or award; a small, round portion of food.|She wore a beautiful locket and a pair of matching medallions. 她戴着一个漂亮的吊坠盒和一对相配的圆形装饰。|Synonyms:  badges, decorations, emblems, ornaments, awards, plaques, insignias, tokens, charms, trinkets (especially for jewelry-like medallions) |Antonyms: (For award type) Disgrace, dishonor|Noun
