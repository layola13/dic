bottling-up:/ˈbɒtlɪŋ ʌp/ (UK) /ˈbɑːtlɪŋ ʌp/ (US)| n./gerund|压抑，抑制（感情）|The act of suppressing or repressing emotions or feelings, keeping them inside instead of expressing them.|He's been bottling up his anger for weeks and it's starting to affect his health. 他把愤怒压抑了好几个星期，这开始影响他的健康了。|近义词：suppressing, repressing, concealing, internalizing|反义词：expressing, venting, releasing|词性: 名词/动名词

bottling up:/ˈbɒtlɪŋ ʌp/ (UK) /ˈbɑːtlɪŋ ʌp/ (US)| adj.|压抑的，抑制的 (less common as an adjective)|Characterized by the suppression of emotions.|His bottling-up nature made it difficult for him to form close relationships. 他压抑的性格使他难以建立亲密关系。|近义词：repressed, suppressed|反义词：expressive, open|词性: 形容词


Etymology: From the metaphor of bottling liquids, preventing them from escaping.  Similar to keeping feelings "corked up."
