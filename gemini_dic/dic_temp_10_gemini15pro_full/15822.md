ball-like:/ˈbɔːlˌlaɪk/
adjective
球状的；像球的
Resembling a ball in shape.
The microorganism was ball-like under the microscope.  这个微生物在显微镜下呈球状。
The fruit is small and ball-like. 这种水果小而呈球形。
近义词: spherical, globular, round
反义词: elongated, flat
词性: adjective
