figurehead:/ˈfɪɡjərˌhed/| n. | 名义领袖；傀儡；船首像 |A person who appears to be in charge but has no real power or authority; a carved figure on the prow of a ship. | The king was merely a figurehead; the real power lay with the prime minister. 国王只是一个傀儡；真正的权力掌握在首相手中。

The ship's figurehead was a carved mermaid. 这艘船的船首像是美人鱼雕像。

|Synonyms: puppet, nominal leader, front, token, ornament | Antonyms: leader, authority, power, decision-maker | Noun
