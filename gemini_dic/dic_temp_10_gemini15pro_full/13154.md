asynchronous:/eɪˈsɪŋkrənəs/ /əˈsɪŋkrənəs/| adj.|异步的，非同步的|Not occurring at the same time.  Happening independently of the main program flow.|例句：Asynchronous communication, such as email, allows people to respond at their own pace. 异步通信，例如电子邮件，允许人们按照自己的节奏进行回复。

例句：Asynchronous learning is becoming increasingly popular due to its flexibility. 异步学习因其灵活性而越来越受欢迎。|近义词：non-concurrent, non-simultaneous, independent|反义词：synchronous, concurrent, simultaneous|词性:adjective
