nonprofitability:/ˌnɑːnˌprɑːfɪtəˈbɪləti/
n.
无利可图，不盈利
The state of not being profitable; generating losses or not yielding a financial gain.
The nonprofitability of the venture led to its closure.  这个项目的无利可图导致了它的关闭。
The company's nonprofitability forced them to lay off workers. 该公司的无利可图迫使他们解雇工人。
近义词: unprofitability, loss-making
反义词: profitability, lucrative, gainful
词性: noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + profitability (from profitable, from profit + -able)  "non-"（表示“不”的前缀）+ profitability（来自 profitable，来自 profit + -able）
