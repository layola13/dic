moblogger:/ˈmɒblɒɡər/ (UK) /ˈmoʊblɔːɡər/ (US)| n.|手机博客写手；移动博客作者|A person who creates and maintains a blog using a mobile device, such as a smartphone or tablet.|例句：The mobloggers were quick to post pictures of the breaking news event. 手机博客写手们迅速发布了突发新闻事件的照片。

例句：Mobloggers often use specialized apps to manage their content. 手机博客写手通常使用专门的应用程序来管理他们的内容。|近义词：mobile blogger, blogger|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps traditional blogger)|词性:noun
