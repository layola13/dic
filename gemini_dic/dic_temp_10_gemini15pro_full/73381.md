forehand:/ˈfɔːrhænd/ (US) /ˈfɔːhænd/ (UK)| n. adj. adv.|正手击球；正手的；用正手的|A stroke made with the palm of the hand facing the direction of movement.  In racket sports, it's a stroke played with the hand leading the racket across the body. |He hit a powerful forehand down the line. 他用正手大力击球沿着边线得分。|近义词: front hand (less common) |反义词: backhand|noun, adjective, adverb
