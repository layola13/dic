confederated:/kənˈfɛdəˌreɪtɪd/
adjective
联合的，同盟的
Joined together by an agreement or treaty.
The confederated tribes of the Iroquois Nation held a yearly council.  易洛魁民族的联合部落每年举行一次会议。
The cantons are confederated for common defense. 这些行政区联合起来共同防御。

近义词: allied, united, federated, leagued, joined
反义词: separate, independent, disunited

词性: adjective

词源: From the Latin word "confœderatus," past participle of "confœderare" (to unite by a league), from "con-" (together) + "fœderare" (to make a treaty), from "fœdus" (treaty, league).
