fracas:/ˈfreɪkəs/ (US), /ˈfrækɑː/ (UK)| n.|争吵，吵闹，喧嚷|A noisy disturbance or quarrel.|例句：A fracas broke out in the bar after the football game.  酒吧里在足球比赛结束后发生了一场争吵。

例句：The political rally ended in a fracas.  政治集会以一场争吵告终。

近义词：brawl, melee, uproar, commotion, disturbance, quarrel, fight, rumpus|反义词:peace, quiet, calm, tranquility|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *fracasso*, from *fracassare* "to break in pieces", from Vulgar Latin *fracassare*.
