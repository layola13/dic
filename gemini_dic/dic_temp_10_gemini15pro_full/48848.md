deforested:/diːˈfɔːrɪstɪd/
adjective
已砍伐的，已清除森林的
Cleared of forests; having had the trees removed.
The deforested land was now barren and susceptible to erosion. 这片被砍伐的土地现在变得荒芜，容易受到侵蚀。
The Amazon rainforest is being deforested at an alarming rate. 亚马逊雨林正以惊人的速度被砍伐。
Synonyms: cleared, logged, denuded
Antonyms: forested, wooded, afforested


ETYMOLOGY->
de- (prefix): meaning "remove," "reduce," or "reverse" - from Latin
forest (noun): a large area covered chiefly with trees and undergrowth - from Old French forest
-ed (suffix): forms the past participle of regular verbs and adjectives meaning "having been subjected to the action of the verb"
