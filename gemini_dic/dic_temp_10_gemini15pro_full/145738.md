overcriticizing: /ˌoʊvərˈkrɪtɪˌsaɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度批评，过分苛责
Criticizing someone or something too much or too harshly.
She's always overcriticizing her children's work, which discourages them. 她总是过度批评孩子们的作品，这让他们很沮丧。
He was constantly overcriticizing his employees, leading to low morale. 他不断地过分苛责他的员工，导致士气低落。
Synonyms: nitpicking, carping, faultfinding, caviling
Antonyms: praising, commending, lauding
