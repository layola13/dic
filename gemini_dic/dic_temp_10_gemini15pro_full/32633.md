chisel:/ˈtʃɪzəl/ (US) /ˈtʃɪzl/ (UK)| n. v. |凿子；雕刻；刻|A metal tool with a sharp, beveled edge used for shaping or cutting wood, stone, or metal.  To shape or cut something using a chisel.|例句：He used a chisel to carve the wood. 他用凿子雕刻木头。

The sculptor chiselled the marble into a beautiful statue.  雕塑家把大理石凿成了一座美丽的雕像。|近义词：gouge, carve, engrave, sculpt|反义词: assemble, construct|词性:名词,动词

chiselled:/ˈtʃɪzəld/ (US) /ˈtʃɪzld/ (UK)| adj. |轮廓分明的；雕刻般的|Having a clearly defined, sculpted appearance, often used to describe facial features.|例句：He had a chiseled jawline. 他有着轮廓分明的下颌线。

She had a chiseled and elegant profile. 她有着雕刻般优雅的侧脸。|近义词：sharp, defined, sculpted|反义词: rounded, soft|词性:形容词
