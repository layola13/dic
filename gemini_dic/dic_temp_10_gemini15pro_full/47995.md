decimators:/ˈdɛsɪmeɪtərz/| n. | 毁灭者，大批杀戮者 |  A person or thing that decimates something, drastically reducing its strength or numbers. | The locusts were the decimators of the crops, leaving nothing but barren fields. 蝗虫是农作物的毁灭者，只留下贫瘠的田野。| Synonyms: destroyers, eradicators, annihilators | Antonyms: creators, preservers, protectors | noun

decimate:/ˈdɛsɪmeɪt/| v. | 大批杀戮，毁灭 | To kill, destroy, or remove a large percentage of. Originally referred to killing every tenth person. | Disease decimated the population. 疾病毁灭了人口。| Synonyms: destroy, ravage, devastate | Antonyms: create, build, protect | verb
