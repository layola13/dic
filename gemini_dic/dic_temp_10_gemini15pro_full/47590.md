debate:/dɪˈbeɪt/ (US), /dɪˈbeɪt/ (UK)| n. v. |辩论，讨论；争论|A formal discussion on a particular topic in a public meeting or legislative assembly, in which opposing arguments are put forward.|The presidential candidates engaged in a heated debate. 总统候选人进行了激烈的辩论。|近义词：discussion, argument, dispute, controversy|反义词:agreement, harmony|noun

debate:/dɪˈbeɪt/ (US), /dɪˈbeɪt/ (UK)| v. |辩论，讨论；争论|Argue about (a subject), especially in a formal manner.|The committee debated the proposal for several hours. 委员会就这项提案辩论了几个小时。|近义词：discuss, argue, dispute, contend|反义词:agree, concur|verb
