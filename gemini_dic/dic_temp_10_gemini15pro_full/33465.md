chutzpah:/ˈχʊtspə/ (US), /ˈhʊtspə/ (UK)| n.| 大胆，厚颜无耻，过分的自信|Supreme self-confidence. Audacity or arrogance, typically in the face of criticism or rejection.|

例句：
She had the chutzpah to ask for a raise after only a week on the job.  她才工作一周就敢要求加薪，真是胆大包天。

He showed incredible chutzpah by challenging the CEO's decision. 他竟然挑战CEO的决定，真是胆大妄为。


近义词：audacity, nerve, boldness, temerity, gall, effrontery
反义词：timidity, shyness, humility, modesty
词性：名词 (noun)


词源:
From Yiddish חוצפּה‎ (khutspe), from Hebrew חֻצְפָּה‎ (ḥuṣpā, “insolence, cheek”). Ultimately from Aramaic חֻצְפָּא‎ (ḥuṣpā).
