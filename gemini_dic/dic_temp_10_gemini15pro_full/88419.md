hisingerite:/ˈhɪsɪŋɡəˌraɪt/ | n. | 海辛格矿，硅铁矿 | A hydrated iron silicate mineral, typically dark greenish-black to brownish-black in color, often found as an alteration product of other iron-rich minerals. | Hisingerite is often found in association with iron ore deposits. 海辛格矿经常与铁矿床伴生。

Hisingerite can be difficult to identify definitively due to its variable composition. 海辛格矿由于其成分多变，很难明确鉴定。| 近义词:nontronite, chloropal | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
