nymphean:/nɪmˈfiːən/ | adj. | 水仙的；睡莲的；居于水中的；清澈的 | Relating to a nymph; inhabiting or growing in water, especially of or resembling a water lily. |  The nymphean blossoms floated gracefully on the pond's surface. 睡莲的花朵优雅地漂浮在池塘的水面上。|  The pool had a nymphean quality, clear and inviting.  这个水池有一种水仙般的气质，清澈诱人。| 近义词: aquatic, water-lily,  | 反义词: terrestrial | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin "nymphaeus," from Greek "numphēios"  (belonging to a nymph), from "numphē" (nymph, bride).
