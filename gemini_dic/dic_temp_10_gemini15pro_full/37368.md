commensurate:/kəˈmɛnʃərɪt/ /kəˈmɛnʃərət/ | adj. | 相称的，相当的，相等的 | Corresponding in size or degree; in proportion. |  Her salary is commensurate with her experience. 她的薪水与她的经验相称。


A solution commensurate to the scale of the problem is needed. 需要一个与问题规模相称的解决方案。


近义词: proportionate, equivalent, corresponding, appropriate, suitable, adequate, comparable, matching | 反义词: disproportionate, inadequate, unequal, insufficient | 词性: adjective
