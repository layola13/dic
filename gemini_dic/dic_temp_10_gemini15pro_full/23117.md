bourdon:/ˈbʊərdən/ (US), /ˈbɔːrdən/ (UK)| n.|嗡嗡声，低沉单调的声音；雄蜂；低音管；（旧）朝圣杖|A deep, hollow, resonant sound, like that of a bee or a large bell. Also, a bass pipe organ stop that produces such a sound.  Historically, it also referred to a pilgrim's staff.|The bourdon of the bells filled the air. 钟声的嗡嗡声充满了空气。|近义词：drone, hum, buzz|反义词:silence, quiet|词性:noun

bourdon:/ˈbʊərdən/ (US), /ˈbɔːrdən/ (UK)| n.|低音大号|A large, deep-toned organ stop.|The organist pulled the bourdon stop to create a powerful bass line.  管风琴手拉动低音大号音栓，营造出强劲的低音线。|近义词：bass, profundo|反义词:treble|词性:noun


bourdon:/ˈbʊərdən/ (US), /ˈbɔːrdən/ (UK)| n.|（旧）朝圣杖|A staff carried by pilgrims.|The pilgrim leaned heavily on his bourdon as he climbed the hill. 朝圣者在爬山时沉重地倚靠着他的朝圣杖。|近义词：staff, crook|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

bourdon:/ˈbʊərdən/ (US), /ˈbɔːrdən/ (UK)| n.|雄蜂|A male bee, especially a bumblebee.|The bourdon flew clumsily around the flowers. 雄蜂笨拙地在花丛中飞来飞去。|近义词：drone (bee)|反义词:queen bee, worker bee|词性:noun
