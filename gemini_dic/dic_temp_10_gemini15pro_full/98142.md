insanity:/ɪnˈsænəti/ /ɪnˈsænɪti/| n. |疯狂；精神错乱；精神失常|The state of being seriously mentally ill; extreme foolishness or irrationality.|He was found not guilty by reason of insanity. 他因精神失常而被判无罪。|近义词：madness, lunacy, dementia|反义词：sanity, rationality|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French insanite, from Latin insanitas, from insanus ‘unsound’.
