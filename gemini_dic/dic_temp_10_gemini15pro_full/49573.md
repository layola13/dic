dementednesses:/dɪˈmɛntɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
痴呆状态；精神错乱
The state of being demented; severe mental impairment.
His dementednesses led him to believe he was Napoleon.  他的痴呆让他相信自己是拿破仑。
dementia, insanity, madness
sanity, lucidity
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From demented + -ness.  Demented comes from Late Latin dementare "to make mad," from de- "out of" + mens "mind."
