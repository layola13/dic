diota:/diˈoʊtə/
n.
大肚酒坛；大水罐
A large, two-handled earthenware jar, typically used for storing wine or oil.
The archaeologists unearthed a diota filled with ancient olive oil.  考古学家挖出一个装满古代橄榄油的大肚酒坛。
近义词：jar, amphora, flagon
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
