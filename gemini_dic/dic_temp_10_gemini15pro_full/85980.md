headship:/ˈhɛdʃɪp/| n.|领导地位，领导权|The position of being the head of a group or organization.  The headship of the family traditionally belongs to the father. |Example:  He assumed the headship of the department after the previous director retired. 他在前任主任退休后接任了部门的领导职务。

Example: The headship of the company has been contested for years.  这家公司的领导权多年来一直存在争议。|近义词：leadership, directorship, management, superintendency|反义词: subordination, subjection|词性: 名词
