cosmopolitism:/ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtɪzəm/ | n. | 世界主义；世界性；四海一家 |The ideology that all human beings belong to a single community, based on a shared morality. |  Example 1:  Her cosmopolitism was evident in her appreciation of diverse cultures. 她对不同文化的欣赏体现了她的世界主义精神。

Example 2: The city's cosmopolitism is reflected in its diverse culinary scene. 这座城市的多元烹饪场景反映了它的世界性。| 近义词: internationalism, globalism, multiculturalism | 反义词: nationalism, parochialism, isolationism | 词性: noun
