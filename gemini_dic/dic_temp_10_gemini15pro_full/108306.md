laurens:/ˈlɔːrənz/ | n. | 劳伦斯（姓氏或名字） | A surname or given name, derived from the Latin "Laurentius," meaning "from Laurentum," an ancient city near Rome.  It's also associated with the laurel tree, a symbol of victory and honor in ancient Rome. | Example:  Laurens was a prominent family in South Carolina during the American Revolution.  劳伦斯家族在美国独立战争期间是南卡罗来纳州的望族。
