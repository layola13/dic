loafed:/loʊft/
verb
闲逛，懒散度过
spent time idly or relaxed
He loafed around the house all day. 他整天在家闲逛。
They loafed on the beach, enjoying the sunshine. 他们在沙滩上闲逛，享受阳光。
Synonyms: laze, lounge, idle, relax
Antonyms: work, hustle, toil
