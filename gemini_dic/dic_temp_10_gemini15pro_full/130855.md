nonapprovals:/ˌnɒnəˈpruːvəlz/
n.
不批准，不认可
lack of approval; instances of something not being approved.
The project faced numerous nonapprovals from the board.  这个项目面临董事会的多次否决。
The nonapprovals of the budget caused delays. 预算未获批准导致了延误。
synonyms: rejections, disapprovals, vetoes
antonyms: approvals, endorsements, sanctions
noun
