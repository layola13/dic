oblong:/ˈɒblɒŋ/ (UK) /ˈɑːblɔːŋ/ (US)| adj. n.| 椭圆形的，长方形的；椭圆形，长方形|Having an elongated shape, like a rectangle but with rounded corners.  A shape that is oblong.|The painting was framed in an oblong frame. 这幅画装在一个椭圆形的画框里。| The table was oblong, seating about twelve people. 这张桌子是长方形的，可以坐十二个人左右。|近义词：oval, elliptical, rectangular|反义词：round, square, circular|词性: adjective, noun|词源: From Late Latin *oblongus, from Latin oblongus (“longer than broad”), from ob (“against, towards”) + longus (“long”).
