bello:/ˈbɛloʊ/ (US), /ˈbɛləʊ/ (UK)| adj.|美丽的，美好的；漂亮的|A term of endearment or admiration, meaning "beautiful" or "lovely".  Often used in Italian and sometimes adopted into other languages.|例句：That's a bello car you have! 你有一辆漂亮的车！|近义词：beautiful, lovely, handsome, pretty, gorgeous|反义词:ugly, hideous, unattractive|形容词
