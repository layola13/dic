curtana:/kɜːrˈtɑːnə/
n.
库尔塔纳（剑名）
The legendary sword of Ogier the Dane, sometimes identified with the sword of Roland.
传说中丹麦人奥吉尔的剑，有时被认为是罗兰之剑。
The king drew Curtana from its scabbard. 国王从剑鞘中拔出库尔塔纳。
Synonym: sword, blade
Antonym: sheath, scabbard
noun
