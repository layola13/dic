adjudicates:/əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪts/
verb
判决，裁定，裁决
To formally judge or decide a case or a competition; to act as a judge in a competition or argument.
The panel of judges adjudicates the art competition. = 评审团裁定了艺术比赛的结果。
The court adjudicates cases of property disputes. = 法院裁定财产纠纷案件。
近义词: judges, decides, determines, arbitrates, rules on
反义词: postpones, defers, delays
词性: verb
