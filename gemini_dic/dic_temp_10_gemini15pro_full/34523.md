cleanlier:/ˈkliːnlɪər/| adj.|更干净地，更清洁地|Comparative form of "cleanly," meaning in a more clean or neat manner.|She dusted the shelves cleanlier than usual. 她比平时更仔细地擦拭了架子。|近义词：more neatly, more tidily|反义词: dirtier, messier|词性:形容词
词源: From Old English *clænlice (unattested), from clǣne (“clean”) +‎ -lice (adverbial suffix).  The comparative form is formed by adding -er.
