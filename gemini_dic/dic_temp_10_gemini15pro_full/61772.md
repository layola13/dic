empathy:/ˈɛmpəθi/ /ˈɛmpəθi/ | n. | 同理心，共情 | the ability to understand and share the feelings of another |  I felt a deep empathy for the victims of the earthquake. 我对地震灾民深感同情。

| 近义词：compassion, understanding, sympathy | 反义词：apathy, indifference | 词性：noun


empathic:/ɛmˈpæθɪk/ /ɛmˈpæθɪk/ | adj. |  有同理心的，表示同情的 | showing an ability to understand and share the feelings of another | She gave him an empathic smile. 她给了他一个充满同情的微笑。

| 近义词： compassionate, understanding, sympathetic | 反义词：apathetic, indifferent | 词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek empatheia, from em- (in) + pathos (feeling)
