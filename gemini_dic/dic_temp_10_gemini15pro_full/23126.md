bourgeon:/ˈbɜːrdʒən/| vi. n.|发芽，萌芽；迅速发展|To begin to grow or increase rapidly; flourish.|例句：New industries are bourgeoning in the area.  该地区新兴产业正在蓬勃发展。

例句：The trees bourgeon in the spring. 树木在春天发芽。|近义词：sprout, flourish, bloom, thrive, prosper|反义词：wither, decline, decay|词性：不及物动词, 名词

Etymology: From Middle French *bourgeonner*, from Old French *burjon*, diminutive of *burge*, from Late Latin *burra* ("wool"), of Germanic origin. The original sense was "to put forth buds (covered with down)".
