clout:/klaʊt/| n.|一块布；补丁|A piece of cloth or leather, especially one used as a patch.|In the past, people used clouts as diapers. 过去，人们用碎布当尿布。|近义词：patch, rag|反义词：-|n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *clouten*, from Old English *clūt* (cloth, patch). The verb sense "to hit" developed later, perhaps from the image of hitting with a cloth or patch.
