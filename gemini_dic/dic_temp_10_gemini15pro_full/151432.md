parodize: /ˈpærədaɪz/ | v. | 模仿；滑稽地模仿；作…的滑稽模仿 | To imitate (a composition, author, etc.) for humorous effect by exaggerating the characteristic features of the original. | The show parodied several popular commercials.  这个节目滑稽地模仿了几个受欢迎的广告。| synonyms:  mock, satirize, lampoon, burlesque | antonyms:  emulate, imitate, flatter | verb

parody: /ˈpærədi/ | n. | 滑稽模仿作品；模仿诗文 | A humorous or satirical imitation of a serious literary work or writing style. |  His latest novel is a parody of the gothic romance genre. 他最新的小说是对哥特式浪漫体裁的滑稽模仿。| synonyms:  mockery, travesty, caricature, burlesque | antonyms:  homage, tribute,  serious work | noun
