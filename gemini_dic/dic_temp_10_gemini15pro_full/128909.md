nimit:/ˈnɪmɪt/| n.|预兆，征兆，先兆|A sign or indication of something about to happen.|The appearance of the black cat was seen as a nimit of bad luck. 黑猫的出现被视为厄运的预兆。|近义词：omen, portent, presage, augury, foretoken|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 "lack of sign" 或 "no indication")|词性:名词
