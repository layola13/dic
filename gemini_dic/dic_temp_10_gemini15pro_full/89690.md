hootenanny:/ˈhuːtənæni/ | n. | 民间音乐聚会；喧闹的聚会 | An informal gathering with folk music and sometimes dancing. | We went to a hootenanny last weekend and had a blast singing along. 我们上周末去参加了一个民间音乐聚会，一起唱歌玩得很开心。| The community center hosts a hootenanny every month. 社区中心每个月都会举办一次民间音乐聚会。 |  Synonyms: party, celebration, get-together, jam session | Antonyms: silence, quiet, stillness | noun
