nonqualitatively:/nɒnˈkwɒlɪtətɪvli/ | adv. | 非定性地，不考虑性质地 |  In a way that does not consider quality or type, but only quantity or magnitude.  | The survey nonqualitatively assessed the impact by simply counting the number of affected individuals.  该调查非定性地评估了影响，仅仅统计了受影响的个体数量。 |  quantitatively, numerically | qualitatively | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + qualitatively (in a qualitative manner).  Qualitatively comes from quality + -ative (forming adjectives) + -ly (forming adverbs).  Ultimately from Latin *qualitativus (assumed form) < qualitas (quality).
