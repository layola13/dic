bombarding:/bɒmˈbɑːdɪŋ/ /bɑːmˈbɑːrdɪŋ/
|v.|轰炸，炮击;大量提问;不断攻击|The enemy kept bombarding the city. 敌人不断轰炸这座城市。|例句：They were bombarding the city with artillery fire. 他们用炮火轰炸城市。|近义词：attacking, shelling, pelting, battering|反义词: defending, protecting|词性:动词现在分词/动名词

bombard:/bɒmˈbɑːd/ /bɑːmˈbɑːrd/
|v.|轰炸，炮击;大量提问;不断攻击|The reporters bombarded the president with questions. 记者们向总统提出大量问题。|例句：He was bombarded with emails from angry customers. 他被愤怒的顾客的电子邮件轰炸了。|近义词：attack, shell, pelt, batter|反义词: defend, protect|词性:动词

词源: From French *bombarder*, from *bombarde* "bombard (cannon)," from Italian *bombarda*, of uncertain origin, perhaps related to *bomba* "bomb."
