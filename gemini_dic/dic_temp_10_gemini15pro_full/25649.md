bundled-up:/ˈbʌndəld ˈʌp/| adj.|包裹严实的，穿得暖和的|Dressed warmly in many layers of clothing, especially to protect against cold weather.|例句：The children were all bundled up in coats and scarves. 孩子们都裹着外套和围巾。|例句：She was bundled up against the cold wind. 她裹得严严实实的以抵御寒风。|近义词：wrapped up, snug, cozy|反义词：exposed, uncovered|词性:形容词
