disedify: /ˌdɪsˈɛdɪfaɪ/
verb
使不 нази化; 败坏道德; 使失教养
To corrupt morally; to cause to lose morals or principles.
The explicit content of the movie disedified many viewers. 这部电影中露骨的内容败坏了许多观众的道德。
近义词: demoralize, corrupt, deprave, pervert
反义词: edify, uplift, inspire, improve
词性: verb
