dihedral:/daɪˈhiːdrəl/| adj. n.|二面的，二面角的；二面角|Relating to or formed by two intersecting planes or surfaces, especially two plane faces of a crystal.  A dihedral angle.|The dihedral angle between the wings of an aircraft is crucial for its stability.  飞机机翼之间的二面角对其稳定性至关重要。|The aircraft has a 30-degree dihedral. 这架飞机有30度的二面角。|近义词：two-sided, bilateral|反义词:|形容词,名词
