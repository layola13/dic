contaminous:/kənˈtæmɪnəs/
adjective
接触传染的；污染的
Spreading by contact, especially through the air; tending to contaminate.
The disease is highly contaminous, so quarantine measures are essential. 这种疾病极具传染性，因此隔离措施至关重要。
The air in the lab was contaminous with toxic fumes. 实验室的空气被有毒烟雾污染了。
近义词: contagious, infectious, polluting, tainting
反义词: sterile, clean, pure
词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French *contamineux, or from late Latin contaminosus, from contaminare (see contaminate).
