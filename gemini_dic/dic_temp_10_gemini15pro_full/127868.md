neocolonialisms:/ˌniːoʊkəˈloʊniəlɪzəmz/
n.
新殖民主义
The use of economic, political, cultural, or other pressures to control or influence other countries, especially former dependencies.
Many scholars argue that neocolonialisms perpetuate exploitation of developing nations. 许多学者认为新殖民主义使发展中国家的剥削永久化。
Neocolonialism continues to hinder the economic growth of many African nations. 新殖民主义继续阻碍许多非洲国家的经济增长。
近义词:imperialism, domination, hegemony
反义词:independence, self-determination
词性:noun

n.
新殖民主义
The use of economic, political, cultural, or other pressures to control or influence other countries, especially former dependencies.
The film explores the insidious effects of neocolonialism. 这部电影探讨了新殖民主义的隐蔽影响。
International corporations are often accused of practicing neocolonialism. 跨国公司经常被指责实行新殖民主义。
近义词:imperialism, domination, hegemony
反义词:independence, self-determination
词性:noun


词源: neo- (new) + colonial + -ism.  "Neo-" comes from Greek "neos," meaning "new." "Colonial" relates to "colony," ultimately from Latin "colonus," meaning "farmer, settler." "-ism" is a suffix denoting a doctrine, practice, or system.
