de-licensed:/diːˈlaɪsənst/
adjective
去执照的，被吊销执照的
Deprived of a license; having had a license revoked.
The de-licensed doctor could no longer practice medicine.  这位被吊销执照的医生不能再行医了。
The de-licensed taxi driver was forced to find another job.  这位被吊销执照的出租车司机被迫另找工作。
近义词: unlicensed, unauthorized, disqualified
反义词: licensed, authorized, certified
词性: adjective

词源:
Prefix "de-" (meaning "removal or reversal") + "licensed" (past participle of "license," from Old French "licence").  前缀“de-”（表示“移除或逆转”）+“licensed”（“license”的过去分词，源自古法语“licence”）。
