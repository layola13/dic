minuted:/ˈmɪnɪtɪd/
|v.|记录在案的；把…写进会议记录|Recorded in or as if in minutes; put (something) into the minutes of a meeting.|The agreement was duly minuted.  该协议已正式记录在案。|The secretary minuted the decisions. 秘书把决定记录了下来。|recorded, documented| unrecorded, undocumented|verb

minuted:/ˈmɪnɪtɪd/
|adj.|详细的, 详尽的 (指描述)|Characterized by great detail; meticulous.|He gave a minuted account of the meeting. 他对会议做了详细的记录。|She described the event in minuted detail. 她对事件进行了详细的描述。|detailed, meticulous, thorough|brief, concise, cursory|adjective
