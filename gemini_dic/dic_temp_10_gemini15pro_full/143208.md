outcrier:/ˈaʊtˌkraɪər/| n.|公开反对者；大声疾呼者|A person who publicly opposes or denounces something.|例句：The outcrier warned of the impending danger.  公开反对者警告了即将到来的危险。

例句：She became an outcrier against the injustices of the system. 她成为了反对体制不公的呐喊者。|近义词：protestor, dissident, objector, critic, opponent|反义词：supporter, advocate, proponent|词性:noun
