globulous:/ˈɡlɒbjʊləs/ (US) /ˈɡloʊbjʊləs/| adj.|球形的，球状的|Resembling a globe or globule; spherical or globular.  Having the shape of a small sphere; globe-shaped.|The artist created a globulous sculpture out of clay. 艺术家用黏土创作了一个球状的雕塑。|The dew drops hung globulous on the spider's web. 露珠球状地挂在蜘蛛网上。|近义词：spherical, globular, round, rotund|反义词：flat, linear, angular|词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *globulosus, from globulus (“small sphere, globule”) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'full of', 'prone to', 'like').  The Latin globulus is a diminutive of globus (“sphere, globe”).
