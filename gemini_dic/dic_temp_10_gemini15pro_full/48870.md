deformly:/dɪˈfɔːrmli/
adverb
畸形地；丑陋地
in a deformed or misshapen manner; uglily
His face was deformly twisted in rage. 他的脸因愤怒而扭曲变形。
近义词: grotesquely, misshapenly, contortedly
反义词: beautifully, symmetrically, perfectly
词性: adverb

词源:
From deform + -ly.  "Deform" comes from the Old French "deformer," and ultimately from the Latin "deformare,"  meaning "to disfigure," from "de-" (from, away from) + "formare" (to form).  "-ly" is a suffix used to form adverbs.
