interdictive:/ˌɪntərˈdɪktɪv/| adj.|禁止的，阻止的|Having the power to prohibit or forbid.  Relating to or characterized by interdiction.|The government issued an interdictive order against the import of certain goods. 政府发布了禁止进口某些商品的命令。|Synonyms: prohibitory, prohibitive, proscriptive, forbidding|Antonyms: permissive, allowing, authorizing|adjective
