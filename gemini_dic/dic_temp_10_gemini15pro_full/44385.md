cross-fenced:/ˌkrɒsˈfɛnst/ | adj. | 交叉围栏的，分隔围栏的 | Divided into smaller sections by fences.  This creates separate grazing areas within a larger pasture. | The farmer cross-fenced his pasture to better manage his livestock. 农民用交叉围栏划分牧场以便更好地管理牲畜。| The ranch was cross-fenced for rotational grazing. 这个牧场被交叉围栏隔开，用于轮牧。 |  partitioned, subdivided |  open, undivided | adjective
