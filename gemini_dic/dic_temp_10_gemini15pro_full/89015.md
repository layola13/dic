hollowly:/ˈhɒləʊli/ (UK) /ˈhɑːloʊli/ (US)| adv.|空洞地；虚伪地|In a way that sounds empty or insincere, suggesting a lack of real meaning or feeling.  It describes the manner in which something is said or done, not the thing itself.|He laughed hollowly, trying to hide his disappointment. 他空洞地笑了笑，试图掩饰自己的失望。|Synonyms: emptily, insincerely, falsely, artificially|Antonyms: sincerely, genuinely, truthfully|Adverb
