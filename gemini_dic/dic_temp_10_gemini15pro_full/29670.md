cathartin:/kəˈθɑːrtɪn/
n.|宣泄；净化|A cathartin is something that provides psychological relief through the open expression of strong emotions.  宣泄物是指通过公开表达强烈情绪来提供心理上的缓解的东西。|Listening to music can be a cathartin for some people.  对某些人来说，听音乐可以是一种宣泄。|近义词:purification, release, cleansing|反义词:suppression, repression|词性:noun

cathartic:/kəˈθɑːr.tɪk/
adj.|宣泄的；净化的|Cathartic experiences allow people to release pent-up emotions. 宣泄的经历让人们释放压抑的情绪。|Writing in a journal can be a cathartic experience. 在日记中写作可以是一种宣泄的体验。|近义词:purifying, cleansing, relieving|反义词:suppressing, repressing|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek κάθαρσις (kátharsis, “purification, cleansing”), from καθαίρω (kathairō, “I cleanse, purify”).
