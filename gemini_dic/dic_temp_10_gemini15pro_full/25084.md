bubblier:/ˈbʌbliər (US), ˈbʌbliə (UK)| adj.|更 bubbly 的，更起泡的，更活泼的|Comparative form of bubbly: more full of bubbles; more lively and cheerful.|The champagne was bubblier than the prosecco.  这香槟比普罗塞克起泡更多。|The hostess was even bubblier than usual. 女主人比平时更加活泼。|Synonyms: livelier, effervescent, more cheerful|Antonyms: flatter, duller, less cheerful|adjective
