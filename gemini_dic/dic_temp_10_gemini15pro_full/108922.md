leeched:/liːtʃt/| v. | 汲取，榨取；依附并榨取… |To drain or draw off (something) like a leech.  To cling to and exploit (someone) like a leech. |The company leeched resources from its subsidiaries.  这家公司从其子公司榨取资源。|近义词：exploit, drain, sap, bleed|反义词：give, contribute|词性:动词

leeched:/liːtʃt/| adj. | 耗尽的，被榨干的|Drained or exhausted, as if by a leech.|The leeched landscape offered little sustenance.  这片被榨干的土地几乎无法提供食物。|近义词：depleted, exhausted, drained|反义词：replenished, rich, fertile|词性:形容词

词源: 来自古英语 lǣce，意思是“医生”，也指“水蛭”，因为水蛭曾被用于医疗放血。后来，这个词的含义扩展到比喻意义上的“榨取”或“依附”。
