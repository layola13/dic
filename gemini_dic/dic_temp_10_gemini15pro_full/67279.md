exsanguinity:/ˌɛksæŋˈɡwɪnɪti/
n.
失血; 血尽; 苍白
The state of being exsanguinated; bloodlessness.  The extreme paleness caused by loss of blood.
The exsanguinity of the victim was apparent from his pale skin.  受害者苍白的皮肤显示出他失血过多。
He collapsed from exsanguinity after the accident. 事故发生后，他因失血过多而昏倒了。
近义词: anemia, bloodlessness, pallor
反义词: plethora, ruddy complexion
