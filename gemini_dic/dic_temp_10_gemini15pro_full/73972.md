fortissimi:/fɔːrˈtɪsɪmi/ | adj. | 极强音的，最强音的 |  An Italian musical term indicating the loudest possible volume. It is the superlative of "forte" (loud). | The orchestra played the final chords *fortissimi*, creating a powerful and dramatic conclusion.  管弦乐队以最强音演奏了最后的和弦，营造出强有力而戏剧性的结尾。| 近义词: very loud, extremely loud | 反义词: pianissimo | 词性: 形容词
