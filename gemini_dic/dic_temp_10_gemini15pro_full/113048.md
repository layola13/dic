lummox:/ˈlʌməks/| n.|笨蛋，傻大个|A clumsy, awkward person.|例句：He's such a lummox, always tripping over his own feet. 他真是个傻大个，总是绊倒自己的脚。

例句：The lummox spilled his drink all over me.  这个笨蛋把饮料全洒我身上了。|近义词：oaf, klutz, clod, blunderbuss|反义词: genius, adept, expert|词性:noun

词源:
Origin uncertain, possibly from dialectal lump (meaning "a heavy, clumsy person") + -ox (a suffix suggesting large size or awkwardness).
