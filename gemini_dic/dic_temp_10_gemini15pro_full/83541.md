hadron:/ˈhædrɒn/| n. |强子|A subatomic particle, such as a proton or neutron, that is subject to the strong force/strong interaction.  Hadrons are composed of quarks and/or antiquarks.|例句：Protons and neutrons are examples of hadrons. 质子和中子是强子的例子。

例句：The study of hadrons is crucial for understanding the nature of matter. 研究强子对于理解物质的本质至关重要。|近义词：subatomic particle, baryon (for some hadrons), meson (for some hadrons) |反义词:lepton|词性:noun
