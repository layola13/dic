反义词:None

cenatory:/ˈsiːnətɔːri/ /ˈsɛnətɔːri/
noun
餐厅
A dining room or dining hall.
The cenatory was filled with the aroma of roasting meat. 餐厅里充满了烤肉的香味。
The king held a grand feast in the castle's cenatory. 国王在城堡的餐厅里举行盛大的宴会。
近义词:dining room, dining hall, refectory
反义词:None
