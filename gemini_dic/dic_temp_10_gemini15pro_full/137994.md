noodle:/ˈnuːdl/| n. vi. vt.|面条；思考；闲弹|a type of food made from flour, water, and sometimes eggs, that is long, thin, and often curly; to improvise on a musical instrument; to think carefully about something for a long time|I had noodles for lunch. 我午餐吃了面条。|近义词：pasta, ramen|反义词：(n.)
