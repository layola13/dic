catalogize:/ˈkætəlɒɡaɪz/ (UK) /ˈkætəlɔːɡaɪz/ (US)| vt.|编目录；分类|To make a systematic list of items, often with descriptive details. |The librarian catalogized the new books. 图书管理员给新书编了目录。|Synonyms: classify, index, list, record, register|Antonyms: disorganize, disorder, randomize|Verb

catalogize:/ˈkætəlɒɡaɪz/ (UK) /ˈkætəlɔːɡaɪz/ (US)| n.|编目录；分类（名词形式较少用）|The act of making a systematic list of items. |The catalogization of the museum's artifacts took several years.  博物馆文物的编目工作花了数年时间。|Synonyms: classification, indexing, listing|Antonyms: disorganization|Noun
