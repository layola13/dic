grenading:/ɡrəˈneɪdɪŋ/
n.|投掷手榴弹，榴弹攻击|The grenading of the enemy position was successful. 对敌人阵地的榴弹攻击很成功。|English Explanation: The act of attacking with grenades.|例句：The grenading of the building caused significant damage.  对建筑物的榴弹攻击造成了严重的破坏。|近义词：bombarding, shelling|反义词: defending, protecting|词性:noun (gerund or verbal noun)
Etymology: From grenade + -ing.  Ultimately from Old French "granate" meaning pomegranate (due to the resemblance of the early grenades to the fruit).
