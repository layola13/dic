communalisms:/kəˈmjuːnəlɪzəmz/
n.
地方主义，社区主义；宗派主义
Allegiance to one's own ethnic group rather than to the wider society.  The principles of communalism.
Communalisms can be a destructive force in a diverse society.  地方主义可能对多元化社会造成破坏。
The rise of communalisms threatened the stability of the nation. 宗派主义的兴起威胁着国家的稳定。
近义词: sectarianism, parochialism, insularity
反义词: cosmopolitanism, universalism
词性: noun


Etymology:
From communal + -ism.  Ultimately from Latin communis "common".
