furciferous:/fɜːrˈsɪfərəs/
adjective
叉状的；分叉的
Resembling a fork; forked.  Having a forked or branching shape.
The furciferous tree stood tall in the forest.  这棵叉状的树高高地耸立在森林里。
The lightning bolt split into a furciferous pattern. 闪电分裂成叉状。
Synonyms: forked, branched, bifurcated, furcate
Antonyms: straight, unbranched, single
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *furcifer, from furca "fork" + -fer "bearing".
