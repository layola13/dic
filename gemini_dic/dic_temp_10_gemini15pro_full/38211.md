compunctiously:/kəmˈpʌŋkʃəsli/ | adv. | 内疚地，良心不安地 | Feeling or expressing guilt or remorse; with a troubled conscience. |  He apologized compunctiously for his mistake. 他内疚地为自己的错误道歉。|  He compunctiously returned the stolen money. 他良心不安地归还了偷来的钱。| 近义词: remorsefully, guiltily, penitently | 反义词: unrepentantly, shamelessly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *compunctiōsus, from Latin compunctus (past participle of compungere, "to prick sharply, sting, vex"), from com- ("together") + pungere ("to prick").
