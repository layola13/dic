angstier:/ˈæŋstiər/ | adj. | 更焦虑的，更担忧的 | More anxious or worried.  A comparative form of "angsty," which itself is an informal adjective.  It describes a state of heightened anxiety, typically associated with adolescence or young adulthood. |  She seemed angstier than usual about the upcoming exam. 她似乎比平时更担心即将到来的考试。|  He was angstier about his future than he let on. 他对自己的未来比他表现出来的要更加焦虑。 |  More anxious, more worried, more apprehensive | Less anxious, calmer, more relaxed | adjective
