electroplate:/ɪˈlɛktroʊpleɪt/| v. n. adj.|电镀，镀层|To coat (a metal object) by electrolytic deposition with chromium, silver, gold, or another metal.|例句：The spoons were electroplated with silver. 这些勺子镀了银。|近义词：plate, coat, cover|反义词:strip, uncover|verb
electroplate:/ɪˈlɛktroʊpleɪt/| v. n. adj.|电镀，镀层|A thin coating of metal deposited on another metal by electrolysis.|例句：The electroplate on the faucet was beginning to wear off. 水龙头上的镀层开始脱落。|近义词：coating, plating, finish|反义词:base, substrate|noun
