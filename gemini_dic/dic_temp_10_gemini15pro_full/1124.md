accessorising: /ækˈsesəraɪzɪŋ/
noun
搭配配饰；装饰
The act of adding accessories to something, typically clothing or a room, to enhance its appearance.
She spent hours accessorising her outfit for the party. 她花了几个小时为派对搭配服装配饰。

I love accessorising with scarves and jewelry. 我喜欢用围巾和珠宝来搭配。

近义词: decorating, ornamenting, embellishing, adorning
反义词: simplifying
词性: 动名词 (gerund)

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "accessorise" + "-ing" suffix.  "Accessorise" comes from "accessory" + "-ise" (verb-forming suffix). "Accessory" comes from the Late Latin "accessorius" meaning "additional" or "supplementary."
