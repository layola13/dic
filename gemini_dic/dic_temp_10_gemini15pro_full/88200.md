himring:/ˈhɪmrɪŋ/
n.
Himring
Himring (Sindarin for "Evercold") is a fictional location in J.R.R. Tolkien's legendarium, notably in The Silmarillion.
The Elves of the House of Bëor lived on the slopes of Himring. Maedhros established his fortress on the hill.
Synonym: fortress, stronghold
Etymology: From Sindarin *him ("cold") + ring ("hill, circle").
