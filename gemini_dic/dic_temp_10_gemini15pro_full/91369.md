hydrostatical:/ˌhaɪdrəˈstætɪkəl/
adjective
流体静力学的
relating to hydrostatics; the study of fluids at rest and the forces they exert.
Hydrostatical pressure increases with depth. 流体静压力随深度增加。
近义词: hydrostatic
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "hydrodynamic" which deals with fluids in motion)
词性: adjective


hydrostatic:/ˌhaɪdrəˈstætɪk/
adjective
流体静力学的
relating to hydrostatics; the study of fluids at rest and the forces they exert.
A hydrostatic test measures the integrity of a sealed system. 静水压试验测量密封系统的完整性。
近义词: hydrostatical
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "hydrodynamic")
词性: adjective


词源:
From hydro- + static + -al.  Hydro- comes from Greek "hydōr" (water). Static comes from Greek "statikos" (causing to stand).
