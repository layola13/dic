nappes:/næps/ | n. | 桌布；(地质)推覆体；(尤指酒的)香味 | A cloth spread over a table, especially during a meal. In geology, a nappe is a large sheetlike body of rock that has been moved far from its original place of formation, typically by faulting or folding. In oenology, it refers to the aromas of a wine. | The restaurant's white nappes shimmered in the candlelight. 餐厅的白色桌布在烛光下闪闪发光。 | 近义词：tablecloths, cloths (for table), sheet (geology), overthrust (geology), bouquet (wine), aroma (wine) | 反义词：(无直接反义词) | 词性: noun
