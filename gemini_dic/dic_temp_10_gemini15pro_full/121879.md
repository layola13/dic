misrules:/ˌmɪsˈruːlz/
|v. (third-person singular simple present misrules, present participle misruling, simple past and past participle misruled)| 误治；对…施暴政|To govern badly or unjustly. |The king misruled the country for decades, leading to widespread poverty and unrest. 国王对这个国家施行了数十年的暴政，导致了普遍的贫困和动荡。

The dictator misruled his people with an iron fist. 独裁者用铁腕统治他的人民。


|近义词：tyrannize, oppress, misgovern|反义词: govern well, rule justly|词性:动词


misrule:/ˌmɪsˈruːl/
|n.| 暴政；失政；混乱|A period of bad or unjust government; disorder. |The country suffered a period of misrule after the revolution. 革命后，这个国家经历了一段时间的混乱。

Years of misrule had left the economy in ruins. 多年的暴政使经济陷入了崩溃。

|近义词：tyranny, oppression, misgovernment|反义词: good governance, just rule|词性:名词
