dweeby:/ˈdwiːbi/| adj.|呆子气的，书呆子气的|Someone who is dweeby is considered awkward, unfashionable, or overly intellectual.  They might be passionate about niche hobbies or have difficulty with social interactions, often perceived as being out of touch with mainstream trends.|例句：He was a dweeby kid who loved collecting stamps. 他是一个喜欢集邮的呆子小孩。

例句：She thought his dweeby glasses were actually kind of cute. 她觉得他那副呆呆的眼镜其实挺可爱的。|近义词：nerdy, geeky, awkward, uncool|反义词：cool, popular, stylish, trendy|词性:形容词
