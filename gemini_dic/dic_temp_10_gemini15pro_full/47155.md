dawdler:/ˈdɔːdlər/ (US) /ˈdɔːdlə(r)/ (UK)| n.|闲逛者，磨蹭者|A person who wastes time or moves slowly, especially when they should be doing something.|He's such a dawdler; he'll never get ready on time. 他真是个磨蹭的人；他永远无法按时做好准备。|例句：
The dawdler missed the bus. 磨蹭的人错过了公共汽车。
She's a terrible dawdler in the mornings. 她早上总是磨磨蹭蹭的。

|近义词：loiterer, lingerer, straggler, slowpoke|反义词：hustler, go-getter|词性: noun
