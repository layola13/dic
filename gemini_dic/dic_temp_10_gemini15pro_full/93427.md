illuminance:/ɪˌluːmɪnəns/
n.
照度
the amount of light falling on a surface.
The illuminance on my desk is too low for reading comfortably. 我桌上的照度太低，无法舒适地阅读。

The photographer measured the illuminance of the scene before taking the shot. 摄影师在拍摄前测量了场景的照度。

近义词:light intensity, brightness
反义词:darkness

词性:noun
词源:From Latin illuminare "to light up," from in- "into, upon" + luminare "to light," from lumen "light."
