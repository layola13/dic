creepinesses:/ˈkriːpinɪsɪz/
n.
令人毛骨悚然;令人恐惧
The quality of being creepy; the state of causing an unpleasant feeling of fear or unease.
The abandoned house had an undeniable creepiness about it. 这栋废弃的房子有一种不可否认的令人毛骨悚然的感觉。
The creepinesses of the old castle were legendary. 这座古老城堡的恐怖故事是传奇性的。
近义词: eeriness, spookiness, uneasiness, uncanniness
反义词: comfort, pleasantness, normalcy
词性: 名词(plural)

creepy:/ˈkriːpi/
adj.
令人毛骨悚然的;令人恐惧的
Causing an unpleasant feeling of fear or unease.
He gave me a creepy look. 他给了我一个令人毛骨悚然的眼神。
That old house is really creepy. 那栋老房子真的很令人毛骨悚然。
近义词: eerie, spooky, unsettling, uncanny
反义词: comforting, pleasant, normal
词性: 形容词


词源:
From creep + -y.  Creep (v.) meaning "to move slowly and quietly,"  The sense of "causing a sensation of horror or fear" developed in the late 19th century.
