beadlet:/ˈbiːdlt/| n.|海滩管理员；海滩巡视员|A beach officer; a person employed to patrol a beach.|The beadlet kept a watchful eye on the swimmers.|Synonyms: lifeguard, beach patrol officer, coastguard|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bedel*, from Old English *bydel* "herald, messenger, crier, beadle," from Proto-Germanic *bidilą* "messenger," from Proto-Indo-European *bheidh-* "to split, persuade."  The diminutive form, beadlet, arose later.
