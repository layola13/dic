agrostologist:/əˌɡrɒˈstɒlədʒɪst/
n.
草地学家;牧草学家
A scientist who studies grasses, especially in relation to their use as pasture and fodder.
The agrostologist is studying the effects of different grazing patterns on the grassland ecosystem.  这位草地学家正在研究不同放牧模式对草原生态系统的影响。

The agrostologist recommended a new type of grass for the pasture.  这位草地学家推荐了一种新的牧草品种。

近义词:grass scientist, botanist
反义词:None

词性:noun
