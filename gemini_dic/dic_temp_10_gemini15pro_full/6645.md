anaesthetization:/ˌænɪsθətɪˈzeɪʃən/ /əˌnɛsθətɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.|麻醉|The process of inducing anaesthesia. The state of being anaesthetized.  |The anaesthetization of the patient took longer than expected. 病人的麻醉时间比预期的要长。| 近义词：anesthesia, narcosis | 反义词: awakening, resuscitation | noun

anaesthetize:/əˈniːsθətaɪz/ /əˈnɛsθəˌtaɪz/| vt.|麻醉|To administer an anesthetic to (a person or animal), especially so as to induce a loss of consciousness. |The vet anaesthetized the dog before the operation. 兽医在手术前给狗麻醉了。|近义词：numb, drug | 反义词: stimulate, revive | verb
