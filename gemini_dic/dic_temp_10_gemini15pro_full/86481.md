heighten:/ˈhaɪtn/| v. |加剧，提高，增高|to increase the amount, level, or degree of something|The tension in the room heightened as the deadline approached. 房间里的紧张气氛随着截止日期的临近而加剧。|近义词：increase, intensify, enhance, elevate, amplify|反义词:reduce, diminish, decrease, lower|词性:动词
词源:From Middle English heghten, from Old English hēahtan (“to make higher, heighten, exalt”), from Proto-Germanic *hauhtjaną (“to make higher”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kaud- (“high”).
