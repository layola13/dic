mightier:/ˈmaɪtɪər/| adj.|更强大的，更有力的|Comparative form of mighty: possessing greater power, strength, or influence.|例句：The pen is mightier than the sword. 笔锋胜于刀剑。

例句：Rome was once the mightier empire in the world. 罗马曾经是世界上最强大的帝国。|近义词：stronger, more powerful, more potent|反义词：weaker, less powerful, feebler|词性:形容词
