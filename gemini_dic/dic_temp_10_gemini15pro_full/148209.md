overshortens:/ˌoʊvərˈʃɔːrtənz/ | v. | 过度缩短，缩短得过分 |  Reduces the length of something excessively. | The tailor overshortened the sleeves of my jacket, making them look awkward.  裁缝把我的夹克袖子缩得太短了，看起来很别扭。 |  truncates, abbreviates excessively | lengthens, extends | verb
overshortened:/ˌoʊvərˈʃɔːrtənd/ | adj. | 过度缩短的 |  Having been shortened excessively. | The overshortened curtains didn't reach the windowsill.  过度缩短的窗帘没有够到窗台。| truncated, excessively abbreviated | lengthened, extended | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix indicating excess) + "shorten" (verb meaning to make shorter). "Shorten" comes from Old English "scortan," from "sceort" (short).
