miss out:/mɪsˈaʊt/|phrasal verb|错过，遗漏|To fail to take advantage of or participate in something beneficial or enjoyable.| I missed out on the promotion because I was sick. 我因为生病错过了晋升。|近义词: lose out (on), overlook, skip|反义词: take advantage of, participate in, partake in|词性:短语动词

miss out (on something):/mɪsˈaʊt/|phrasal verb|错过（某事）|To fail to take advantage of or participate in something beneficial or enjoyable.| Don't miss out on the chance to visit Paris. 不要错过参观巴黎的机会。|近义词: lose out (on something), overlook, skip|反义词: take advantage of, participate in, partake in|词性:短语动词


ETYMOLOGY->
'Miss' comes from Old English 'missan', meaning "to fail to hit, err, go astray". 'Out' intensifies the meaning of missing completely or failing to be included.
