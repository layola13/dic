ecossaise:/ˌeɪkɒˈseɪz/ /ˌɛkɒˈseɪz/| n.|埃科塞舞;苏格兰舞曲;苏格兰方格呢|A lively Scottish dance or dance tune, typically in quick duple or triple time.  A type of plaid fabric.|例句：The band played a lively ecossaise. 乐队演奏了一曲活泼的埃科塞舞曲。

例句：She wore a beautiful ecossaise skirt. 她穿了一条漂亮的苏格兰方格呢裙子。|近义词：reel, jig, Highland fling (for the dance), tartan, plaid (for the fabric)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
