chalera:/ˈkɒlərə/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlərə/ (US)| n.|霍乱|An acute intestinal infection caused by ingestion of contaminated water or food, characterized by profuse watery diarrhea, vomiting, and muscle cramps.|例句：The cholera outbreak spread rapidly through the refugee camp.  霍乱疫情在难民营迅速蔓延。

例句：Cholera is a serious threat in areas with poor sanitation.  霍乱在卫生条件差的地区是一个严重威胁。|近义词：dysentery|反义词:|词性:noun
