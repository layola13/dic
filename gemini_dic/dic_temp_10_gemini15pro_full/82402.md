grudge:/ɡrʌdʒ/ | v. n. | 吝惜，勉强给；怨恨，妒忌 |  To be unwilling to give or allow something; to feel resentful or envious towards someone. |  He grudged paying so much for a simple meal.  他很不情愿为一顿简单的饭菜付这么多钱。| synonyms: begrudge, resent, envy | antonyms: give freely, willingly, forgive | verb

grudge:/ɡrʌdʒ/ | n. | 怨恨，积怨 | A feeling of resentment or ill will. | He held a grudge against his former employer for years. 他对他的前任雇主怀恨在心多年。| synonyms: resentment, animosity, ill will | antonyms: forgiveness, goodwill | noun
