consumptive:/kənˈsʌmptɪv/ | adj. n.| 消耗性的；肺痨的；肺病患者 | Affected with or tending to consumption, especially pulmonary tuberculosis.  Characterized by or causing consumption or progressive wasting away. |  例句：His consumptive cough worried his family. 他的肺痨咳嗽让家人很担心。


例句：The factory's consumptive use of resources is unsustainable.  这家工厂对资源的消耗性使用是不可持续的。 | 近义词：tubercular, wasting, phthisical | 反义词：productive, restorative | 词性: adjective, noun

consumptive:/kənˈsʌmptɪv/ | n.| 肺病患者 | A person with pulmonary tuberculosis (dated). | 例句：In the 19th century, consumptives often sought treatment in sanatoriums. 在19世纪，肺结核患者经常在疗养院寻求治疗。


例句：He was a consumptive in the final stages of the disease. 他是一位处于疾病晚期的肺结核患者。| 近义词：tubercular patient | 反义词：healthy person | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Late Latin *consumptivus, from Latin consumere (“to consume”).
