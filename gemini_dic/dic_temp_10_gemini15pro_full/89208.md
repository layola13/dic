homestays:/ˈhoʊmsteɪz/ (US) /ˈhɒmsteɪz/ (UK)| n. |寄宿家庭; 在当地居民家住宿|A homestay is an arrangement where visitors stay with a local family in their home. |例句：I enjoyed my homestay experience in France. 我很享受在法国的寄宿家庭经历。

例句：Homestays are a great way to experience local culture. 寄宿家庭是体验当地文化的好方法。|近义词：lodgings, accommodation, board|反义词: hotel|词性:名词
