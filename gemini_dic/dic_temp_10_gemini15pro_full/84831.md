hang-glider:/ˈhæŋ ˌɡlaɪdər/ | n. |滑翔翼，悬挂式滑翔机 |A light aircraft consisting of a large kite-like wing of fabric on a frame, from which a pilot hangs suspended in a harness. | He took off from the cliff in his hang-glider. 他乘滑翔翼从悬崖上起飞。| 近义词: glider | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "airplane" as a powered aircraft) | 词性: noun

hang-glider: /ˈhæŋ ˌɡlaɪdər/ | v. | 滑翔 | To fly using a hang-glider. | She loves to hang-glide over the valley. 她喜欢在山谷上空滑翔。| 近义词: glide, soar | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "land" as the opposite action) | 词性: verb
