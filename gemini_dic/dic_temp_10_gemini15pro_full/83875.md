half-admitted:/ˌhɑːf ədˈmɪtɪd/ | adj. | 半承认的；不完全承认的 | Partially acknowledged or confessed; not fully admitted. | He gave a half-admitted smile, suggesting he knew more than he was letting on. 他勉强笑了笑，暗示他知道的不止这些。 |  She half-admitted her guilt. 她半承认了自己的罪行。

| Synonym: partially acknowledged, tacit, implied, hinted | Antonym: fully admitted, confessed, acknowledged, declared | adjective |
