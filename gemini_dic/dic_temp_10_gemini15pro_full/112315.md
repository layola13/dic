lordolatry: /ˌlɔːrdəˈlɒtri/ (US) /ˌlɔːdɒlətri/ (UK)| n. | 主崇拜，对君主的过度崇拜 | Excessive devotion to a lord or ruler; worship of a lord as a god. | The historian criticized the lordolatry prevalent in the Victorian era. 历史学家批评了维多利亚时代盛行的君主崇拜。 | 近义词: idolatry, hero-worship, sycophancy | 反义词: iconoclasm, irreverence | 词性: 名词

词源： From lord + -latry (from Greek latreia 'worship, service').
