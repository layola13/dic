feudatories:/ˈfjuːdəˌtɔːriz/
n.
封臣，诸侯
vassals or tenants who hold land from a lord in return for homage and military service.
The king granted lands to his feudatories in exchange for their loyalty and military support. 国王将土地赐予他的诸侯，以换取他们的忠诚和军事支持。
vassals, tenants, liegemen
lords, overlords
noun
