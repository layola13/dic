dayflower:/ˈdeɪˌflaʊər/| n. |鸭跖草，鸭拓草|A plant of the genus Commelina, typically having blue flowers with three petals. These flowers often bloom for only a single day.|The dayflower in my garden bloomed a vibrant blue this morning. 我花园里的鸭跖草今天早上开出了鲜艳的蓝色花朵。|Synonyms: Asiatic dayflower, common dayflower|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
