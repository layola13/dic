miscreancy: /mɪˈskriːənsi/
noun
恶行，不端行为
A wicked or villainous act; wrongdoing.
His miscreancy shocked the entire community. 他的恶行震惊了整个社区。
Synonyms: villainy, wickedness, wrongdoing, malfeasance, iniquity
Antonyms: goodness, righteousness, virtue, morality
词性: 名词
