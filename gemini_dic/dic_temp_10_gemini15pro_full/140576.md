oligochete:/ˈɑːlɪɡoʊkiːt/ | n. |寡毛类动物|A segmented worm of the class Oligochaeta, which includes earthworms and freshwater worms.  These worms are characterized by having bristles (chaetae) on their bodies, but fewer than in polychaetes. | Earthworms, a type of oligochete, are beneficial to soil health. 蚯蚓，一种寡毛类动物，对土壤健康有益。| synonym: earthworm, freshwater worm | antonym: polychaete | noun

oligochaete:/ˈɒlɪɡəʊkiːt/ | n. |寡毛类动物|A segmented worm of the class Oligochaeta, which includes earthworms and freshwater worms.  These worms are characterized by having bristles (chaetae) on their bodies, but fewer than in polychaetes. | Many oligochaetes live in aquatic environments. 许多寡毛类动物生活在水生环境中。 | synonym: earthworm, freshwater worm | antonym: polychaete | noun
