chagrin:/ʃəˈɡrɪn/ /ʃəˈɡrin/| n. vt.|懊恼，失望，恼怒；使懊恼|A feeling of annoyance or disappointment at having failed or been humiliated.|He felt deep chagrin at being rejected. 他被拒绝后感到非常懊恼。|Synonyms: annoyance, vexation, irritation, displeasure, mortification|Antonyms: delight, pleasure, satisfaction|noun
chagrin:/ʃəˈɡrɪn/ /ʃəˈɡrin/| vt.|使懊恼，使失望|To cause to feel chagrin; mortify.|It chagrined him that his efforts had been wasted.  他的努力白费了，这让他很懊恼。|Synonyms: annoy, vex, irritate, displease, mortify|Antonyms: delight, please, satisfy|verb
