denouement:/ˌdeɪnuˈmɑːŋ/ /ˌdeɪˈnuːmɒŋ/
n.
结局，结果
the final part of a play, movie, or narrative in which the strands of the plot are drawn together and matters are explained or resolved.
The denouement of the film was predictable.  这部电影的结局是可以预见的。
synonyms: outcome, conclusion, resolution, culmination, climax, finale
antonyms: beginning, start, commencement, introduction, prelude
词性: 名词
