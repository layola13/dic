empathetically:/ˌɛmpəˈθɛtɪkli/ | adv. | 同理地，设身处地地 |  In a way that shows an understanding of and identification with another person's feelings, situation, and motives. | She listened empathetically to his problems. 她设身处地地倾听了他的问题。 | 近义词：compassionately, sympathetically, understandingly | 反义词：apathetically, indifferently, callously | 词性: 副词

词源:  From empathetic + -ally.  Empathetic comes from empathy, which is derived from the German Einfühlung (feeling into), coined by German psychologist Theodor Lipps.  Einfühlung combines ein (in) and Fühlung (feeling).
