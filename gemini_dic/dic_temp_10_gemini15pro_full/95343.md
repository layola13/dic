incenter:/ˈɪnsɛntər/| n.|内心, 内切圆心|The incenter of a triangle is the point where the angle bisectors intersect. 三角形的内心是角平分线的交点。|The incenter is equidistant from the sides of the triangle. 内心到三角形各边的距离相等。|近义词: center (in a specific context)|反义词: excenter|词性:noun

Etymology->
From Modern Latin *in centrum (“into center”), from in (“in”) + centrum (“center”).
