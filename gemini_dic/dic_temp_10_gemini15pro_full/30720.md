cerumens:/səˈruːmɪnz/
noun
耳垢（复数）
Earwax; a yellowish waxy substance secreted in the ear canal of humans and other mammals.
Cerumens can sometimes build up and cause hearing problems. 耳垢有时会堆积并导致听力问题。
Synonyms: earwax, cerumen
Antonyms: N/A
n.

cerumen:/səˈruːmən/
noun
耳垢（单数）
Earwax; a yellowish waxy substance secreted in the ear canal of humans and other mammals.
Excess cerumen can block the ear canal. 过多的耳垢会堵塞耳道。
Synonyms: earwax, cerumens
Antonyms: N/A
n.
