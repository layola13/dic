consociational:/kənˌsoʊʃiˈeɪʃənl/
adjective
协商的，联合的
relating to a form of government involving power-sharing by different groups, especially in societies deeply divided along ethnic, religious, or linguistic lines.
Consociational democracy is often seen as a way to manage conflict in deeply divided societies. 协商民主制通常被视为在严重分裂的社会中管理冲突的一种方式。
The consociational approach emphasizes grand coalition governments and proportionality. 协商方法强调大联合政府和比例代表制。
Synonyms: power-sharing, cooperative, joint
Antonyms: majoritarian, exclusive, unitary
