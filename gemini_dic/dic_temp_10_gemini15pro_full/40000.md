consultation:/ˌkɒnsəlˈteɪʃn/ (UK) /ˌkɑːnsəlˈteɪʃn/ (US)| n.|咨询，磋商，会诊|A meeting with an expert or professional, such as a doctor or lawyer, to get advice or treatment.|例句：I had a consultation with a specialist about my back pain. 我就我的背痛咨询了一位专家。|近义词：meeting, discussion, conference, advice|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
etymology: Late Middle English: from Latin consultatio(n-), from consultare ‘consult’.
