localizers:/ˈloʊkəlaɪzərz/| n.|本地化人员，本地化器|People who adapt products or content for a specific locale, adjusting language, cultural references, and technical aspects.|The localizers worked hard to ensure the game was culturally appropriate for the Japanese market. 本地化人员努力确保游戏在文化上适合日本市场。|近义词：translators, adapters|反义词:|noun

localizer:/ˈloʊkəlaɪzər/| n.|定位器，局部化装置|A device or system used to determine the location of something.|The aircraft's localizer helped it land safely in the fog. 飞机的定位器帮助它在雾中安全着陆。|近义词：locator, positioner|反义词:|noun
