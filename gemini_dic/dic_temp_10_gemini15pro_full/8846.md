antifascisms:/ˌæntiˈfæʃɪzəmz/
n.
反法西斯主义
Opposition to fascism, a political ideology characterized by authoritarianism, ultranationalism, and suppression of opposition.
The rise of antifascisms in the 1930s was a response to the growing threat of fascist regimes.  20世纪30年代反法西斯主义的兴起是对法西斯政权日益增长的威胁的回应。

Many different groups united under the banner of antifascisms.  许多不同的群体团结在反法西斯主义的旗帜下。


近义词:anti-fascism
反义词:fascism
词性:noun
