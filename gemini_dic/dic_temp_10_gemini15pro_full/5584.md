altazimuth: /ˌæltˈæzɪməθ/ | n. adj. | 地平坐标, 地平经纬仪 | An altazimuth is a mounting for a telescope or other instrument that allows it to be rotated about two perpendicular axes, one vertical and one horizontal, to allow it to be pointed at any position in the visible sky. |  The amateur astronomer used an altazimuth mount for his new telescope.  那位业余天文学家为他的新望远镜使用了地平式支架。 |  The observatory's altazimuth telescope allowed for precise tracking of celestial objects.  天文台的地平经纬仪望远镜可以精确跟踪天体。| synonym: altitude-azimuth,  alt-azimuth | antonym: equatorial mount | noun, adjective

Etymology->
From altitude + azimuth.  Altitude referring to the vertical angle and azimuth referring to the horizontal angle.
