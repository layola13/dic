assumingness:/əˈsjuːmɪŋnɪs/
n.
傲慢；自以为是；自作主张
The quality of being assuming; arrogance or presumption.
His assumingness made him unpopular. 他的傲慢让他不受欢迎。
近义词：arrogance, presumption, audacity, boldness, forwardness
反义词：humility, modesty, diffidence
词性：名词

词源：From assuming + -ness.  "assuming" comes from the verb "assume," which has its roots in the Latin word "assumere" meaning "to take up, adopt, or take upon oneself."  The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns denoting a state or quality.
