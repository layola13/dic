noncorrespondence: /ˌnɒnkɒrɪˈspɒndəns/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnkɔːrəˈspɑːndəns/ (US)| n. |不一致，不符，不相符|Lack of agreement or conformity; failure to match or correspond.|例句：There is a noncorrespondence between the two maps. 这两张地图不一致。|近义词：discrepancy, disparity, difference, divergence, incongruity|反义词：correspondence, agreement, conformity|词性:名词

noncorrespondence: /ˌnɒnkɒrɪˈspɒndəns/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnkɔːrəˈspɑːndəns/ (US)| n. |不一致，不符，不相符| Etymology: From non- + correspondence.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "correspondence" comes from the Latin "correspondēre," meaning "to answer together" or "to agree."|例句：The noncorrespondence between the witness's testimony and the physical evidence raised doubts about his story.  证人证词与物证的不符，使人们对他的说法产生了怀疑。|近义词：disagreement, inconsistency, variance|反义词：accordance, harmony, consistency|词性:名词
