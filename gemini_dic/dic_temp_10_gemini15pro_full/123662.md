mooch:/muːtʃ/| v.  n.| 索取；蹭（饭）；闲逛；溜达 |To ask for or obtain (something) without paying for it; to wander or roam idly.| He's always mooching cigarettes off his friends.  他总是蹭朋友的烟抽。|Synonyms: scrounge, sponge, cadge, bum, freeload|Antonyms: give, provide, donate|verb

mooch:/muːtʃ/| n.| 索取；蹭饭；闲逛 |The act of mooching; a person who mooches.| He went for a mooch around the shops. 他去商店闲逛了一圈。|Synonyms: scrounger, sponger, cadger, bum, freeloader|Antonyms: giver, provider, donor|noun
