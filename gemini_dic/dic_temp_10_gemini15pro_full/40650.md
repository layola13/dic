conventioneering:/kənˌvɛnʃəˈnɪrɪŋ/
n.
组织会议；会议管理；会议服务
The act or profession of organizing and managing conventions.
The city thrives on conventioneering, hosting numerous large events every year. 这个城市依靠会议服务而繁荣，每年举办许多大型活动。
Conventioneering is a lucrative business, especially in popular tourist destinations. 会议服务是一个利润丰厚的行业，尤其是在热门旅游目的地。
近义词:conference management, conference planning, event planning, meeting planning
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider inactivity, leisure)
