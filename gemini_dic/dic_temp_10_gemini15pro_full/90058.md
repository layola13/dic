hospitate: /ˈhɑːspɪteɪt/ /ˈhɒspɪteɪt/
verb
招待；款待
to receive as a guest; to provide lodging for
例句：The hotel hospitates hundreds of tourists every year. 这家酒店每年招待数百名游客。

例句：They were hospitated at the ambassador's residence. 他们在大使官邸受到了款待。

近义词：accommodate, lodge, entertain, host
反义词：eject, expel

词性：v.
