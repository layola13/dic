formlessness:/ˈfɔːrmləsnəs/
|n.|无形；无定形；混沌|The state of lacking definite form or shape.  The formlessness of the fog made it difficult to see. 浓雾的无形使得很难看清。|
|近义词：amorphousness, shapelessness, indefiniteness|
|反义词：form, shape, structure, definition|
|词性: 名词|


词源:  From "formless" + "-ness". "Formless" comes from Old French "formeles," from "forme" (form) + "-les" (without).  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
