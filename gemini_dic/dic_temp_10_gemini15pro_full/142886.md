out-of-the-chute:/ˌaʊt əv ðə ˈʃuːt/
adjective
中文翻译：刚开始的；一开始就…的；迅速的
英文解析：From the very beginning; immediately; rapidly, especially performing well or badly right from the start.  Like a horse racing out of the starting gate.
例句：The new product was out-of-the-chute successful, selling out in the first week.  这个新产品一上市就取得了成功，第一周就卖光了。

例句：His career was out-of-the-chute impressive, landing major roles right after graduation. 他的职业生涯一开始就令人印象深刻，毕业后立即获得了重要的角色。
近义词：fast-starting, initial, immediate, quick off the mark
反义词：slow-starting, gradual, delayed

词性: adjective
