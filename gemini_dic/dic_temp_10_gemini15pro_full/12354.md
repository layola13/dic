ashery:/ˈæʃəri/| n.|灰烬制造厂，制碱工厂|A place where potash (an alkali) is made by burning wood or other vegetable matter to ashes.|The old ashery was abandoned after a more efficient method of potash production was discovered.  旧的制碱工厂在发现更有效的钾碱生产方法后被废弃了。|Example: The ashery provided potash for the local soap makers.  这家制碱工厂为当地的肥皂制造商提供钾碱。|Synonym: alkali works, potash works|Antonym: N/A|noun
ashery:/ˈæʃəri/| n.|骨灰存放处，骨灰堂|A place where ashes, especially the cremated remains of the dead, are kept or deposited.|The family visited the ashery to pay their respects. 家人前往骨灰堂祭奠。|Example:  The ashery held the remains of generations past. 这座骨灰堂存放着过去几代人的骨灰。|Synonym: columbarium, ossuary|Antonym: N/A|noun
