The doltishnesses of the character in the play provided much comic relief.  剧中人物的愚蠢行为提供了很多喜剧效果。

Antonyms: wisdom, intelligence, cleverness
n.


Etymology:
From doltish + -ness.  Doltish comes from dolt, meaning a stupid person, of uncertain origin, possibly related to dull.
