nonerrant:/ˌnɑːnˈɛrənt/ | adj. | 无误的，不出错的 | Free from errors; infallible. | The Bible is considered by some to be nonerrant.  一些人认为圣经是无误的。| The Pope's pronouncements on matters of faith are considered nonerrant by Catholics.  天主教徒认为教皇在信仰问题上的声明是无误的。 | Synonyms: infallible, inerrant, unerring, faultless | Antonyms: errant, fallible, flawed, imperfect | adjective
