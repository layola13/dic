odist:/ˈoʊdɪst/| n. |颂诗作者，赋诗人|A person who writes odes (lyrics that express exalted or enthusiastic emotion).|例句：The odist captured the beauty of nature in his verses. 这位颂诗作者在他的诗句中捕捉到了大自然的美丽。

例句：The odist was known for his elaborate and passionate language. 这位赋诗人以其华丽而充满激情的语言而闻名。|近义词：poet, lyricist, versifier|反义词: None (no direct opposite)|词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *odiste*, from Greek *ōidistēs*  "singer of odes," from *ōidē* "ode" + *-istēs* "one who does."
