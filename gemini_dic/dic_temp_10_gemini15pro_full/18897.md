besmear:/bɪˈsmɪr/| vt.|涂抹；弄脏；诽谤|To spread or cover with something sticky or messy; to smear something all over something else. Also, to falsely accuse someone to damage their reputation.|He besmeared his face with mud as camouflage. 他把脸上涂满泥巴作为伪装。|The wall was besmeared with graffiti. 墙上涂满了涂鸦。|近义词：smear, daub, coat, cover, soil, sully, tarnish|反义词:clean, cleanse, purify|verb
