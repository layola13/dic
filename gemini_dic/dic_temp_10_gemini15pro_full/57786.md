drecche:/ˈdrɛtʃɪ/| n. |糟粕；废物；渣滓|Refuse; dregs; trash; waste matter.  Something worthless or of low quality.|The drecche of society were often left to fend for themselves.|近义词：dregs, refuse, rubbish, trash, waste|反义词: valuables, treasures|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *drecche, from Old English *dræcce (“dregs, lees, grounds”), from Proto-Germanic *drakkaz (“dregs, grounds”). Cognate with Middle Low German drak (“dregs”), Middle Dutch drac (“dregs”), Old High German traht (“dregs”), German Treck (“migration, baggage, refuse”). Related to draff, dreg.
