misconfident:/ˌmɪskɒnfɪdənt/| adj.|过于自信的；自信过度的；|Having or showing too much confidence; overconfident.|He was young and misconfident, leading to several poor decisions. 他年轻又过于自信，导致做出了一些糟糕的决定。|例句：His misconfident attitude made him underestimate his opponents. 他过于自信的态度使他低估了对手。

例句：She spoke with a misconfident swagger that masked her insecurity. 她说话时带着一种过于自信的 swagger，掩盖了她的不安全感。|近义词：overconfident, presumptuous, arrogant|反义词：diffident, insecure, hesitant|词性:形容词


Etymology->
"mis-" (prefix) + "confident"
"mis-" means "wrongly" or "badly."
"confident" comes from the Latin "confīdere" (to trust firmly).
