hecky:/ˈhɛki/| adj. |见鬼的，讨厌的，该死的 (mildly offensive)|A mild oath, used to express surprise, annoyance, or emphasis.  Similar in meaning to "heck," but can sometimes imply a slightly stronger feeling. |"That was a hecky of a good game!"  那是一场非常精彩的比赛！|"Hecky, I forgot my keys!"  见鬼，我忘了我的钥匙！|近义词: darn, heck, gosh, flipping |反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a mild oath)|词性:adjective
