noncomparability:/ˌnɒnkəmˌpærəˈbɪləti/ |n.| 不可比性；无法比较 |The noncomparability of the two studies made it difficult to draw any firm conclusions.  这两项研究的不可比性使得难以得出任何确定的结论。|例句：The noncomparability of the data sets presented a challenge to the researchers.  数据集的不可比性给研究人员带来了挑战。

例句：Due to the noncomparability of the grading systems, it's hard to say which student performed better. 由于评分系统的不可比性，很难说哪个学生表现更好。 |近义词：incommensurability, disparity, difference, dissimilarity |反义词：comparability, similarity, likeness |词性: noun
