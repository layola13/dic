choices:/ˈtʃɔɪsɪz/| n.|选择，选择权；供选择的东西|The store offers a wide variety of choices. 这家商店提供各种各样的选择。|Example:  She had to make a difficult choice between her career and her family. 她不得不在事业和家庭之间做出艰难的选择。

Example:  He had no choice but to accept the offer.  他别无选择，只能接受这个提议。


|Synonyms: options, alternatives, selections|Antonyms: limitations, restrictions|noun
