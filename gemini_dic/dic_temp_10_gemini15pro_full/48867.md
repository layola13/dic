deforming:/dɪˈfɔːrmɪŋ/
|v.|使变形；损毁…的形状；使变丑|Changing the shape of something, usually by force; making something ugly or misshapen.|The intense heat was deforming the plastic.  高温使塑料变形。|synonyms: distorting, warping, twisting, contorting|antonyms:  reforming, reshaping, fixing, perfecting|verb

deforming:/dɪˈfɔːrmɪŋ/
|adj.|使变形的；损毁形状的|Causing deformation; making something ugly or misshapen.|The deforming pressure of the glacier carved out the valley. 冰川的变形压力雕刻出了山谷。|synonyms:  distorting, warping, disfiguring|antonyms:  beautifying, enhancing, improving|adjective
