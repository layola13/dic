dispersedness:/dɪˈspɜːrsdnəs/ |n.|分散性，分散度|The degree to which something is spread out or scattered.  指某物散布或分散的程度。|例句：The dispersedness of the seeds ensured the survival of the species. 种子的分散性确保了物种的生存。|近义词：diffusion, dissemination|反义词：localization, centralization|词性:名词
