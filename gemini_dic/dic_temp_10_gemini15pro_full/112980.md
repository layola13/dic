lullabying:/ˈlʌləˌbaɪɪŋ/
v.
哼唱摇篮曲；哄，抚慰
Singing or humming a lullaby to soothe someone, typically a baby, to sleep.  Also used figuratively to describe something that has a calming or soothing effect.
例句：She was lullabying the baby to sleep. 她正哼着摇篮曲哄婴儿睡觉。

例句：The gentle rhythm of the waves lullabyed me into a peaceful state. 海浪轻柔的节奏使我进入了一种平和的状态。
近义词：soothing, calming, comforting, quieting
反义词：agitating, disturbing, exciting, rousing
词性：动词(present participle)
