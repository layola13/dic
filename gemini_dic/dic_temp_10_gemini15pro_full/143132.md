outcarry:/ˌaʊtˈkæri/
verb
胜过，优于
to surpass or exceed in carrying; to carry further or longer
The new plane can outcarry older models by a significant margin.  这架新飞机的载货量明显超过了旧型号。
surpass, exceed, outdo, outperform
lag behind, fall short of
v.
