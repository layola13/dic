monodramatist:/ˈmɒnəʊˌdræmətɪst/ /ˈmɑːnəʊˌdræmətɪst/| n.|独角剧演员；单人剧演员|A person who writes or performs a monodrama, a dramatic piece for a single performer.|Samuel Beckett is considered a master monodramatist for his play "Krapp's Last Tape."  萨缪尔·贝克特因其戏剧《克莱普的最后录音带》而被认为是独角剧大师。|
