exotics: /ɪɡˈzɑːtɪks/ /ɛɡˈzɑːtɪks/ | adj. | 外来的；异国情调的；奇异的 |  Belonging to or coming from a distant foreign country; strikingly unusual or strange in effect or appearance.  |  Exotic birds filled the air with their calls. 异国鸟类的叫声响彻天空。 | synonyms: foreign, tropical, strange | antonyms: native, indigenous, domestic | adjective
