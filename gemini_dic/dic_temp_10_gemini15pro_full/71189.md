fisticuffs:/ˈfɪstɪˌkʌfs/| n.| 拳斗，互殴 |A fight or brawl involving the fists. |They settled their differences with a brief round of fisticuffs. 他们用一场短暂的互殴解决了彼此的分歧。|Example: The two boys engaged in fisticuffs after school.  两个男孩放学后打了一架。|Synonym: brawl, fight, scuffle, melee |Antonym: peace, truce, agreement|noun
fisticuff:/ˈfɪstɪkʌf/| n.| (单数形式，较少用) 拳斗，殴斗 |A blow with the fist. |He landed a fisticuff on his opponent's jaw. 他一拳打在了对手的下巴上。|Example: He received a painful fisticuff to the nose. 他鼻子挨了一拳，很疼。|Synonym: punch, blow, clout|Antonym: caress, embrace|noun
