escapee:/ɪˌskeɪˈpiː/ /əˈskeɪpi/
n.
逃脱者；逃跑者
A person who has escaped from confinement or captivity.
The escapee was apprehended just a few miles from the prison.  这名逃犯在离监狱几英里的地方被抓获。
The police are searching for the escapee who broke out of jail last night. 警方正在搜捕昨晚越狱的逃犯。
近义词：fugitive, runaway
反义词: captive, prisoner
词性: noun
