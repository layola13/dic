heptameter:/hɛpˈtæmɪtər/
noun
七音步诗
A line of verse consisting of seven metrical feet.
Shakespeare occasionally used heptameter in his plays.  莎士比亚偶尔在他的戏剧中使用七音步诗。
The poem was written in iambic heptameter. 这首诗是用抑扬格七音步诗写的。
