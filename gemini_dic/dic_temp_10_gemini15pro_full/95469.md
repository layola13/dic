incitements:/ɪnˈsaɪtmənts/
n.
煽动；刺激；激励
the act of encouraging or stirring up violent or unlawful behavior.
The incitements to violence led to riots in the city. 城市中的暴力煽动导致了骚乱。
synonyms:  provocations, instigations, goadings, urgings, promptings
antonyms: deterrents, dissuasions, discouragements
noun
