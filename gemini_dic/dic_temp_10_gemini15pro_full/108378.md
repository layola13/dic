law-abidingness:/lɔː əˈbaɪdɪŋnɪs/
n.
守法；遵纪守法
The quality or state of being law-abiding.  It refers to a person's tendency to obey and respect the law.
His law-abidingness was evident in his meticulous adherence to traffic regulations. 他对交通规则一丝不苟的遵守表明了他遵纪守法的品质。
The community is known for its law-abidingness and low crime rate.  这个社区以其居民守法和低犯罪率而闻名。
近义词：obedience, compliance, conformity
反义词：lawlessness, criminality, disobedience
词性: 名词

词源:  From law-abiding + -ness.  Law-abiding itself comes from law + abiding (present participle of abide, meaning "to accept or act in accordance with"). Ultimately from Old English *ābīdan, from ā- 'continuously' + bīdan 'wait, remain'.
