gusting: /ˈɡʌstɪŋ/ | adj. | 阵风的；刮大风的 | Characterized by sudden, strong gusts of wind. | The gusting wind made it difficult to walk. 阵阵强风使行走困难。| The ship battled gusting winds and high seas.  这艘船与狂风巨浪搏斗。| 近义词: blustery, windy, squally | 反义词: calm, still | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the noun "gust" + "-ing" suffix. "Gust" comes from the Old Norse word "gustr," meaning "blast of wind."
