nonimprovements:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpruːvmənts/ | n. | 无改进; 未改进之处; 没有改进的地方 | Absence of improvements; things that have not been improved. | The report highlighted the nonimprovements in the customer service department. 报告强调了客户服务部门没有改进的地方。| The nonimprovements in the education system are concerning. 教育系统的未改进之处令人担忧。| 近义词: shortcomings, deficiencies, weaknesses, flaws | 反义词: improvements, enhancements, advancements | 词性: 名词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non < ne "not"
improvement (noun): from improve (verb) + -ment (suffix).  Improve comes from Old French emprouwer, based on Latin prodesse "to be useful."
