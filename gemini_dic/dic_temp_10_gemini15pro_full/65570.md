evacuate: /ɪˈvækjueɪt/ (US) /ɪˈvækjʊeɪt/ (UK)| v. |撤离，疏散|To remove (someone) from a dangerous place.|The police evacuated the building after the bomb threat. 警方在炸弹威胁后疏散了大楼。|例句：Residents were evacuated because of the flood. 由于洪水，居民被疏散了。|近义词：clear, remove, withdraw|反义词: occupy, inhabit|词性:动词

evacuation: /ɪˌvækjuˈeɪʃn/ (US) /ɪˌvækjʊˈeɪʃn/ (UK)| n. |撤离，疏散|The act of removing someone from a dangerous place.|The evacuation of the city was completed within hours. 城市的疏散在几小时内完成。|例句：The hurricane led to a mass evacuation. 飓风导致了大规模的疏散。|近义词：removal, withdrawal|反义词: occupation, inhabitation|词性:名词
