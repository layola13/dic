paining:/ˈpeɪnɪŋ/| n.|疼痛，痛苦; (绘画作品)|A physical or mental sensation of suffering or discomfort.  A painted picture.|例句：The paining in his leg was unbearable. 他的腿疼得难以忍受。

例句：The museum has a stunning collection of 17th-century painings.  这家博物馆收藏了令人惊叹的17世纪绘画作品。|近义词：ache, suffering, agony, discomfort (for pain); picture, painting, artwork (for painting)|反义词：comfort, relief, pleasure (for pain)|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *peynyng*, verbal noun from *peynen*  "to inflict pain,"  from Anglo-French *peiner*, Old French *peiner*, from Latin *poena* "penalty, punishment, pain."
