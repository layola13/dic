metafictionist:/ˌmetəˈfɪkʃənɪst/ | n. |元小说家；后设小说作家 | A writer of metafiction, which is fiction that self-consciously addresses the devices of fiction. |  Example: The metafictionist blurred the lines between reality and fiction in her novel.  这位元小说作家在小说中模糊了现实与虚构的界限。| Example:  As a metafictionist, he often included characters who were aware they were in a story. 作为一位元小说作家，他经常加入一些意识到自己身处故事中的角色。 |Synonyms: postmodernist, experimental writer | Antonyms: traditional novelist | noun
