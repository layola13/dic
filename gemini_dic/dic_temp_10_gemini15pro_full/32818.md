chokebore:/ˈtʃoʊkbɔːr/| n.| 缩口枪管，收束器|A narrowed section at the muzzle end of a shotgun barrel, designed to concentrate the shot pattern for increased range and accuracy. |The hunter used a chokebore shotgun to improve his accuracy.|近义词：choke, constriction|反义词:cylinder bore|名词
