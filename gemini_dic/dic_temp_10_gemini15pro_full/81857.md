gridiron:/ˈɡrɪdˌaɪərn/| n. |烤架; 美式橄榄球场|A metal frame used for cooking food over an open fire; a football field.|例句：The steaks sizzled on the gridiron. 牛排在烤架上发出滋滋声。

例句：The gridiron was packed with cheering fans. 橄榄球场挤满了欢呼的球迷。|近义词：grill, barbecue (for cooking); football field (for sports)|反义词: None|词性: Noun

gridiron:/ˈɡrɪdˌaɪərn/| adj. |像烤架的|Resembling a gridiron.|例句：The city's gridiron street plan was easy to navigate. 这座城市像烤架一样的街道规划很容易导航。
