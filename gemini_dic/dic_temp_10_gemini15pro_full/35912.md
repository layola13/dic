cocreated:/ˌkoʊkriˈeɪtɪd/| adj.|共同创造的|Created jointly by two or more people or entities.|例句：The project was cocreated by students and faculty. 这个项目是由学生和教职工共同创建的。

例句：This artwork was cocreated by two artists with very different styles. 这件艺术品是由两位风格迥异的艺术家共同创作的。|近义词：jointly created, collaboratively developed, co-authored, co-produced|反义词：solely created, independently developed|词性:形容词

cocreate:/ˌkoʊkriˈeɪt/| v.|共同创造|To create something jointly with another or others.|例句：We cocreated a new marketing strategy with our clients. 我们与客户共同创建了一个新的营销策略。

例句：They cocreated a unique performance piece that blended music and dance. 他们共同创作了一部融合了音乐和舞蹈的独特的表演作品。|近义词：collaborate on, jointly develop, co-author, co-produce|反义词：create alone, develop independently|词性:动词
