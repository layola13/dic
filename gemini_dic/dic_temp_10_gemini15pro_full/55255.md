dissimulator:/dɪˈsɪmjʊleɪtər/ | n. | 伪装者，掩饰者 | A person who dissimulates; one who conceals one's true motives, feelings, or beliefs. | He was a master dissimulator, hiding his true intentions behind a facade of charm. 他是个伪装大师，用迷人的外表掩盖他真实的意图。|  近义词: hypocrite, pretender, charlatan | 反义词: honest person, candid person | 词性: noun

dissimulate:/dɪˈsɪmjʊleɪt/ | v. | 掩饰，假装 | To conceal or disguise one's true thoughts, feelings, or character. | She dissimulated her anxiety with a forced smile. 她强颜欢笑以掩饰内心的焦虑。| 近义词: feign, pretend, mask | 反义词: reveal, expose, divulge | 词性: verb
