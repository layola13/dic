palatalism:/ˈpælətəlɪzəm/| n.|腭音化；硬腭音化|The pronunciation of this word involves palatalism.  这个单词的发音涉及腭音化。|例句：The "y" sound in "cute" is a result of palatalism.  “cute”中的“y”音是腭音化的结果。

例句：Palatalism is a common feature in many languages. 腭音化是许多语言中的共同特征。

近义词：palatalization|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, potentially velarization)|词性:noun

palatalize:/ˈpælətəlaɪz/| v.|腭音化；使成硬颚音|The consonant is palatalized in this context.  在这个语境中，辅音被腭音化了。|例句：The /t/ sound is palatalized before /i/ in some dialects. 在某些方言中，/t/ 音在 /i/ 之前被腭音化了。

例句：Try to palatalize the 'n' sound in 'canyon'.  试着把“canyon”中的“n”音腭音化。


近义词：None|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially velarize)|词性:verb

palatalized:/ˈpælətəlaɪzd/| adj.|腭音化的|The palatalized consonant creates a softer sound. 腭音化的辅音产生更柔和的声音。|例句：The word contains a palatalized 'l' sound.  这个词包含一个腭音化的“l”音。


例句：The palatalized consonant distinguishes this word from its counterpart. 腭音化的辅音将这个词与其对应词区分开来。


近义词：None|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially velarized)|词性:adjective


词源: From Late Latin *palatalis, from Latin palatum (“palate”).

用法说明:  Palatalism refers to the articulation of a sound with the tongue raised towards the hard palate. It's a secondary articulation that can affect consonants, making them sound "softer" or more "y-like."  The term can be used as a noun, verb, or adjective.
