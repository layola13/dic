agitpropist:/ˈædʒɪtprɒpɪst/ | n. |宣传鼓动员|Someone who creates or disseminates agitprop, which is political propaganda, especially disseminated through literature, drama, music, or art.|The agitpropist distributed pamphlets urging workers to join the strike.  宣传鼓动员散发传单，敦促工人参加罢工。|Synonyms: propagandist, ideologue |Antonyms:  dissident, apolitical|noun
