hemimetabola:/ˌhɛmiˌmɛtəˈboʊlə/ | n. | 半变态昆虫 | Insects that undergo incomplete metamorphosis, characterized by a nymph stage that resembles the adult form. |  Mayflies are hemimetabolous insects. 五月蝇是半变态昆虫。 |近义词：Paurometabola |反义词：Holometabola |词性: 名词

hemimetabolous:/ˌhɛmiˌmɛtəˈbɒləs/ | adj. | 半变态的 |  Undergoing incomplete metamorphosis; having a life cycle that includes egg, nymph, and adult stages, but lacking a pupal stage. | Grasshoppers are hemimetabolous insects. 蚱蜢是半变态的昆虫。 |近义词：Paurometabolous |反义词：Holometabolous |词性: 形容词

From Ancient Greek ἡμι- (hēmi-, “half”) + μεταβολή (metabolḗ, “change”).
