mixed-ability:/ˌmɪkst əˈbɪləti/ | adj. | 混合能力的；能力参差不齐的 | Catering to students of mixed ability can be challenging for teachers.  |  A mixed-ability class includes students with different levels of skill or knowledge. | Example: The school implemented mixed-ability grouping in math classes. 这所学校在数学课上实施了混合能力分组。

Example:  Teaching a mixed-ability class requires differentiated instruction. 教授能力参差不齐的班级需要差异化教学。 | Synonym: heterogeneous, diverse, varied | Antonym: homogeneous, uniform | adjective
