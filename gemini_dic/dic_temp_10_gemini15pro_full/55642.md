ditheist: /ˈdaɪθiːɪst/
n.
二神论者; 相信两个神的存在的人
A person who believes in two gods, typically a good god and an evil god.
She identified as a ditheist, believing in both a benevolent creator god and a malevolent destroyer god.  她自认为是二神论者，相信仁慈的造物主神和恶毒的毁灭之神。
The ancient religion had ditheistic elements, with two powerful deities representing opposing forces.  这种古老的宗教具有二神论的元素，两个强大的神代表着对立的力量。
近义词: dualist
反义词: monotheist, polytheist
词性: noun
