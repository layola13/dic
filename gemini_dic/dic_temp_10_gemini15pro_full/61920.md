ems:/iːエムズ/ /ɛmz/ | n. |紧急医疗服务（Emergency Medical Services）|Emergency Medical Services, a system that provides out-of-hospital acute medical care and/or transport to definitive care. |Example:  The ems arrived quickly and stabilized the patient.  急救人员迅速赶到并稳定了病人的病情。

Example:  Calling ems is crucial in a medical emergency.  在医疗紧急情况下，呼叫急救人员至关重要。|近义词：paramedics, ambulance service|反义词: None |词性: noun
