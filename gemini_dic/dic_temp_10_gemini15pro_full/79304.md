glazinesses:/ˈɡleɪzinəsɪz/
n.
光泽; 光滑; 光滑的表面;  (尤指眼神的)呆滞无神
a smooth and shiny surface or appearance, often referring to a dull or unfocused look in the eyes
Her glazinesses were a sign of exhaustion. 她的眼神呆滞是疲惫的迹象。
synonyms: gloss, sheen, polish, luster, dullness, blankness
antonyms: roughness, dullness (when referring to surface), alertness, focus (when referring to eyes)
noun
