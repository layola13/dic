detonatabilities:/ˌdetəˌneɪtəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |爆炸性，起爆能力|The detonatabilities of different explosives vary significantly.  不同炸药的爆炸性差异很大。|例句：The researchers are studying the detonatabilities of various new compounds. 研究人员正在研究各种新化合物的爆炸性。

detonatabilities:/ˌdetəˌneɪtəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |爆炸性，起爆能力|Certain conditions can affect the detonatabilities of even stable explosives.  某些条件甚至会影响稳定炸药的爆炸性。|例句：The detonatabilities of the materials were tested under controlled conditions.  这些材料的爆炸性是在受控条件下进行测试的。|近义词：explosiveness, instability (in the context of explosives)|反义词:stability, inertness|词性:名词(plural)
