fruitfulness:/ˈfruːtfʊlnəs/
n.
成果丰硕; 产量丰富; 富有成效
The state or quality of being fruitful; producing good results or abundant crops.
The fruitfulness of the land provided ample food for the community.  这片土地的肥沃为社区提供了充足的食物。
Her research proved to be incredibly fruitful, leading to several breakthroughs. 她的研究成果丰硕，带来了几项突破。
近义词: productivity, fertility, abundance, richness
反义词: barrenness, sterility, unproductiveness
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fruitfulnes*, equivalent to fruitful +‎ -ness.
