erythraean:/ˌɛrɪˈθriːən/ | adj. n.| 红色的，红海的；红海居民 | Of or relating to the Red Sea.  Pertaining to the color red, or a reddish color. | The erythraean sunset painted the sky in vibrant hues.  红彤彤的日落将天空染成了鲜艳的色彩。 | The ancient Erythraean Sea encompassed a vast area. 古代的红海涵盖了广阔的区域。 |  近义词: red, reddish, crimson, ruby | 反义词: blue, green | 词性: adjective, noun

词源: From Latin *Erythræus*, from Ancient Greek Ἐρυθραῖος (*Eruthraîos*) "red," ultimately from ἐρυθρός (*eruthrós*) "red".
