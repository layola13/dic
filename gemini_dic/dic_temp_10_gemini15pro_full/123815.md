mopsey:/ˈmɒpsi/ (UK) /ˈmɑːpsi/ (US)| n.| 小女孩，小家伙（尤指可爱或调皮的）|A term of endearment for a young girl, often implying sweetness or mischievousness.  It can also be used more generally for a small, cuddly creature.|例句：She's such a little mopsey, always getting into trouble. 她真是个小淘气，总是惹麻烦。

例句：Look at that mopsey playing in the garden! 看那个小家伙在花园里玩！|近义词：kid, tot, urchin, rascal, scamp|反义词: adult, grown-up|词性:noun
