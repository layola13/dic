homeopaths:/ˌhoʊmiˈɒpəθs/ | n. |顺势疗法医生|A practitioner of homeopathy, a system of alternative medicine.|例句：Some people prefer to consult homeopaths for their ailments.  有些人喜欢咨询顺势疗法医生治疗他们的疾病。

例句：The conference attracted homeopaths from around the world.  这次会议吸引了来自世界各地的顺势疗法医生。|近义词：alternative medicine practitioners, naturopaths|反义词：conventional doctors, allopathic physicians|词性：名词


词源: From homeopathy + -path.  Homeopathy is from Greek homoios, "similar" + pathos, "suffering."
