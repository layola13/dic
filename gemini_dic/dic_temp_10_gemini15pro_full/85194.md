hariali:/hərɪˈɑːliː/ | adj. | 亮绿色的，翠绿色的；与春天相关的 | Bright green, verdant; associated with spring.  Relates to the vibrant green color of new vegetation, often associated with spring and new life. It is particularly used in South Asian contexts. | The fields were a beautiful hariali after the monsoon rains. 雨季过后，田野一片翠绿。 |  The hariali kurta looked stunning on her.  那件翠绿色的库尔塔在她身上显得格外漂亮。 | synonyms: verdant, lush, green, emerald | antonyms: brown, arid, withered | Adjective
