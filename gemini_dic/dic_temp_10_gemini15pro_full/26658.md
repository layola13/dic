cacophonous:/kəˈkɒfənəs/ | adj. | 刺耳的，不和谐的 |  Producing or characterized by harsh, discordant sounds.  | The cacophonous sound of the construction site made it impossible to concentrate. 建筑工地的刺耳声音让人无法集中注意力。 | The cacophonous music from the party next door kept me awake all night. 隔壁派对传来的嘈杂音乐让我整夜无法入睡。 | synonyms: discordant, dissonant, jarring, raucous, strident | antonyms: harmonious, melodious, euphonious | adjective

Etymology->
From Greek *kakophōnos*  "bad-sounding," from *kakos* "bad" + *phōnē* "sound, voice."
