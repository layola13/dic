examen:/ɪɡˈzæmən/ (UK), /ɪɡˈzæmən/ (US)| n.|审查, 检查|A detailed inspection or investigation.|The doctor gave the patient a thorough examen. 医生对病人进行了彻底的检查。|近义词：inspection, investigation, scrutiny|反义词: None|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *examen*, from Latin *examen* ("a weighing, examination, trial"), from *examinare* ("to weigh, examine"). Ultimately from *ex* ("out") + *agmen* ("a drove, herd, flock; a train, procession").
