evangelizes:/ɪˈvændʒəlaɪzɪz/
|v.|传福音；传播…的福音；宣传|To preach the gospel; to convert or seek to convert to Christianity; to advocate or promote something with enthusiasm.|She evangelizes to the poor in the inner city. 她向市中心的穷人传福音。|近义词：preach, proselytize, promote, advocate|反义词：suppress, discourage|词性:动词
evangelise:/ɪˈvændʒəlaɪz/
