okenite:/ˈoʊkəˌnaɪt/ | n. |okenite, 欧肯石 |A hydrated calcium silicate mineral, often found in radiating clusters of delicate, needle-like crystals.  It is typically white or colorless and has a pearly luster.| The okenite specimen was a beautiful cluster of radiating crystals.  这块欧肯石标本是一簇美丽的放射状晶体。|Synonyms: none (highly specific mineral name) |Antonyms: none |noun
