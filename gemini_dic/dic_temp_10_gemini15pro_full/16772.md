bas-relief: /ˌbɑː rɪˈliːf/ /ˌbæs rɪˈliːf/
noun
浅浮雕
A sculpture in which the figures project slightly from the background.
The Parthenon frieze is an example of bas-relief.  帕台农神庙的雕带是浅浮雕的一个例子。

synonym: low relief, basso-relievo
antonym: high relief, alto-relievo
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French bas-relief (low relief), from bas (low) + relief (relief).  Ultimately from Latin bassus (low) and relevare (to raise).
