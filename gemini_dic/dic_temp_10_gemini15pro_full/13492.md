attached: /əˈtætʃt/ | adj. | 附加的，附属的，依恋的 | joined or fixed to something; feeling love or strong affection for someone or something |  The document is attached to the email.  该文件附加在电子邮件中。| synonyms:  connected, affixed, joined, fastened, appended,  devoted, fond, loving | antonyms: detached, separate, unattached, indifferent, aloof | adjective

attached: /əˈtætʃt/ | v. | 附上，贴上，使依恋 | to fasten or join something to something else; to cause someone to feel love or loyalty towards someone or something  | Please attach the photo to your application form.  请将照片附在您的申请表上。| synonyms: affix, append, fasten, connect, join,  bond, endear | antonyms: detach, separate, remove, unfasten | verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From Old French *attacher, from a- (ad-) + *tacher "to fasten," from Latin taxare "to touch sharply, handle, censure, estimate," frequentative of tangere "to touch."
