non-methodistic:/ˌnɒnˌmɛθəˈdɪstɪk/
adjective
非循道宗的；非墨守成规的
Not following the practices or doctrines of Methodism, especially not adhering to strict rules or a formal system.
The group took a non-methodistic approach to problem-solving, brainstorming freely and considering unconventional ideas.  这个小组采取了一种非墨守成规的方法来解决问题，自由地进行头脑风暴，并考虑一些非传统的观点。
His teaching style was non-methodistic, adapting to the needs of each individual student. 他的教学风格不墨守成规，能够适应每个学生的不同需求。

synonym: unconventional, informal, flexible, adaptable, open-minded
antonym: methodical, conventional, rigid, inflexible, doctrinaire
词性: 形容词
