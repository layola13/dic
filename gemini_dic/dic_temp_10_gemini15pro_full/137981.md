nonzodiacal:/ˌnɒnzoʊˈdaɪəkəl/
adjective
非黄道的
Not of or relating to the zodiac; outside the zodiac.
The comet's orbit is nonzodiacal. 这颗彗星的轨道是非黄道的。
Near synonyms:  non-zodiacal, extrazodiacal
Antonyms:  zodiacal
词性: adjective
