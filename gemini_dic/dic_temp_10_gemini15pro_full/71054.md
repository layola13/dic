first-past-the-post:/ˌfɜːst pɑːst ðə ˈpoʊst/ /ˌfɜːst pɑːst ðə ˈpəʊst/| adj. n.|领先者当选制，相对多数制|An electoral system in which the candidate who receives more votes than any other candidate wins the election, regardless of whether they receive a majority of the votes.|The UK general election uses a first-past-the-post system. 英国大选采用领先者当选制。|Synonym: winner-take-all, plurality voting|Antonym: proportional representation|adjective, noun
