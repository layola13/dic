epinikian: /ɛpɪˈnɪkiən/
adjective
享乐主义的; 奉行享乐主义的
Of or relating to Epicurus or his philosophy.  Focused on pleasure or happiness as the chief good.
例句：He lived an epinikian lifestyle, indulging in fine food and wine. 他过着享乐主义的生活方式，沉迷于美食和美酒。

例句：Her epinikian tendencies led her to prioritize immediate gratification over long-term goals. 她享乐主义的倾向导致她优先考虑眼前的满足而不是长远的目标。

近义词：hedonistic, pleasure-seeking, sybaritic
反义词：ascetic, abstemious
词性：adjective


词源: From Ancient Greek  Ἐπίκουρος (Epikouros, “Epicurus”) + -an.  Epicurus was a Greek philosopher who founded the school of Epicureanism.
