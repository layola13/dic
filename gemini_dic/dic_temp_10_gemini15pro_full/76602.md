galliums:/ˈɡæliəmz/| n. |镓（复数）|A soft, silvery-white metallic element, liquid at room temperature but readily alloying with most metals.  It's a post-transition metal in group 13 of the periodic table. Used in semiconductors, LEDs, and solar cells.|Gallium arsenide is used in some types of solar cells.  砷化镓用于某些类型的太阳能电池。|Synonyms: None (as it's an element)|Antonyms: None (as it's an element)|noun
