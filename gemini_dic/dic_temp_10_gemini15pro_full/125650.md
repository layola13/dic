mummified:/ˈmʌmɪfaɪd/| adj.|木乃伊化的，干瘪的|Preserved by embalming and wrapping in bandages, as in ancient Egyptian burial practices; extremely dry and shrunken.|The archaeologist discovered a mummified cat in the tomb. 考古学家在墓中发现了一只木乃伊化的猫。|The intense desert heat had mummified the fruit, leaving it shrunken and leathery. 强烈的沙漠高温使水果干瘪，变得又小又硬。|Synonyms: embalmed, preserved, desiccated, withered|Antonyms: fresh, moist, living|adjective
