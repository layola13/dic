infusible:/ɪnˈfjuːzəbl/| adj.|不能注入的，不能灌输的，不能溶解的|Not capable of being infused; that cannot be instilled or imparted; incapable of being dissolved.|The substance proved infusible, resisting all attempts to melt it.  这种物质被证明是不能熔化的，抵抗了所有使其熔化的尝试。|The ancient prejudices were infusible, deeply ingrained in the culture. 这些古老的偏见根深蒂固，深深地植根于文化之中。|Synonyms: non-fusible, insoluble, unchangeable, ingrained, fixed, resistant|Antonyms: fusible, soluble, changeable, malleable, adaptable|adjective
