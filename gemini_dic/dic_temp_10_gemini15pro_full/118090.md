menial:/ˈmiːniəl/ | adj. n. | 卑微的，仆人的；佣人 |  (of work) not requiring much skill and lacking prestige.  A person who does menial work. |  She found herself stuck in a menial job. 她发现自己被困在一个卑微的工作中。 |  He disliked having to perform menial tasks around the house. 他不喜欢做家务杂事。 |  lowly, humble, servile |  exalted, noble, prestigious | adjective, noun

menial:/ˈmiːniəl/ | n. | 佣人，仆人 | A domestic servant. |  The menials were expected to obey without question.  佣人们被要求毫无疑问地服从。 |  The king addressed the assembled menials. 国王向聚集的仆人们讲话。| servant, domestic,  handmaid | master, lord, employer | noun
