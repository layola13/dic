例句：She looked down on the bellhopped guests. 她瞧不起那些需要侍者搬运行李的客人。|近义词：bellhop (n.), porter|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词


Etymology->

From bellhop + -ed.  Bellhop itself comes from bell + hop, referring to the quick movements of a bellhop.
