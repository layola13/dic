compassionate:/kəmˈpæʃənət/ | adj. | 富有同情心的，怜悯的 | Having or showing sympathy and concern for others. | She was a compassionate nurse, always taking the time to listen to her patients. 她是一位富有同情心的护士，总是花时间倾听病人的心声。|  He felt compassionate towards the homeless man. 他对那个无家可归的人感到同情。 | 近义词: sympathetic, empathetic, kind, caring, humane | 反义词: uncaring, callous, indifferent, ruthless, cruel | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French *compassionat, from compassion + -ate.
