ocellus:/oʊˈsɛləs/| n. |单眼，眼点|A simple eye or eyespot found in many invertebrates, especially insects and arachnids, often in groups of three.|Many insects have three ocelli on the top of their heads. 许多昆虫的头部顶部有三个单眼。|eye spot, simple eye||noun
ocelli:/oʊˈsɛlaɪ/| n. |单眼，眼点 (ocellus的复数形式)|The plural of ocellus, referring to multiple simple eyes or eyespots.|Dragonflies have large compound eyes and three ocelli. 蜻蜓有大复眼和三个单眼。|eye spots, simple eyes||noun
