codeveloper:/ˌkoʊdɪˈvɛləpər/| n. |共同开发者，合作开发者|A person who develops something jointly with another or others.|Example:  She worked as a codeveloper on the new software project. 她作为新软件项目的共同开发者参与了工作。

Example: The two companies were codevelopers of the groundbreaking technology. 这两家公司是这项突破性技术的共同开发者。|近义词：collaborator, partner, joint developer|反义词:sole developer, independent developer|词性:noun
