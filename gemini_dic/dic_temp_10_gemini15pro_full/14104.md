authentical: /ɔːˈθɛntɪkəl/
adjective
真实的，真正的
Genuine and not fake; actually produced by or proceeding from the original source.
This is an authentical Ming Dynasty vase.  这是一个真正的明代花瓶。
The document is authentical. 这份文件是真实的。
近义词: authentic, genuine, real, true
反义词: fake, false, counterfeit, spurious
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin authenticus, from Greek authentikos "original, genuine, authoritative," from authentēs "one who acts on his own authority," from autos "self" + -entēs agent noun suffix.
