canot:/kəˈnɒt/ | n. | 小舟，独木舟 | A small, light boat, typically pointed at both ends and propelled by paddles. |  We paddled the canot down the river. 我们划着独木舟顺流而下。| synonyms: canoe, skiff, dinghy | antonyms: ship, yacht | noun

Etymology: From French *canot*, from Canadian French *canot*, from Algonquian (probably Eastern Abenaki) *kaneht* 'canoe'.
