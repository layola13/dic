idioticon: /ˈɪdiəˌtɪkɒn/ (US) /ˈɪdiəˌtɪkən/ (UK)| n. |方言词典；特殊语言词典|A dictionary of idiom, slang, or dialect.  A book illustrating the characteristic peculiarities of a language, as the vulgarisms, provincialisms, or cant terms.|This idioticon focuses on the unique vocabulary of the Appalachian region.|近义词：glossary, lexicon, vocabulary, dictionary|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
