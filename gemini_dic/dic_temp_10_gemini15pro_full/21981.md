bohemians:/boʊˈhiːmiənz/| n.| 波西米亚人；放荡不羁的艺术家|People who live unconventional lives, often associated with artistic pursuits, and disregard conventional rules and behaviors.  They often travel and have a free-spirited attitude.| The bohemians gathered in the cafe, discussing art and philosophy.  波西米亚人聚集在咖啡馆里，讨论艺术和哲学。| synonyms: nonconformists, free spirits, unconventional people| antonyms: conformists, traditionalists| noun

bohemian:/boʊˈhiːmiən/| adj.| 波西米亚的；放荡不羁的|Describing a person or lifestyle that is unconventional, artistic, and often nomadic.| She led a bohemian lifestyle, traveling from country to country and painting portraits. 她过着波西米亚式的生活，周游列国，画肖像画。| synonyms: unconventional, nonconformist, free-spirited| antonyms: conventional, conformist, traditional| adjective
