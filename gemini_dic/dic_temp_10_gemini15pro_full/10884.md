arachnologist: /əˈræknɒlədʒɪst/ (UK) /əˈræknɑːlədʒɪst/ (US)
n. |蜘蛛学家|A scientist who studies arachnids (spiders, scorpions, mites, etc.).|She's a renowned arachnologist, specializing in the venom of tropical spiders.|近义词: spider expert|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun

Etymology:
From Greek ἀράχνη (aráchnē, “spider”) + -logist.
