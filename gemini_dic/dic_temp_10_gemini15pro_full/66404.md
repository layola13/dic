executionary:/ˌɛksɪˈkjuːʃəneri/
adjective
执行的，行刑的
Relating to or involved in the carrying out of a plan, order, or course of action, especially one involving punishment or death.
The executionary phase of the project is expected to begin next month.  该项目的执行阶段预计将于下个月开始。
The executionary order was signed by the judge. 法官签署了执行令。
近义词:  executive, administrative, implemental
反义词:  planning, preparatory
词性: adjective
