diaphanoscope:/daɪˈæfənəskoʊp/
n.
幻灯机，透视镜
An optical instrument used to project images of transparent objects, such as slides or photographic negatives, onto a screen.
The lecturer used a diaphanoscope to show detailed images of plant cells. 演讲者用幻灯机展示了植物细胞的详细图像。
slide projector, projector, magic lantern
opaque projector
noun
