misgrading:/mɪsˈɡreɪdɪŋ/
n.
错误评分；评级错误
The misgrading of the essays resulted in several students receiving lower grades than they deserved.  这些论文的错误评分导致几名学生获得了比他们应得的更低的分数。
The teacher apologized for the misgrading and promised to correct the error. 老师为评分错误道歉，并承诺纠正错误。
incorrect grading, wrong grading
accurate grading, correct grading
noun
