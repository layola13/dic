hyperurbanism:/ˌhaɪpərˈɜːrbənɪzəm/
noun
超级城市化
The condition of excessive urban growth and concentration, typically characterized by high population density, extensive infrastructure, and a complex interplay of social, economic, and environmental factors.
The unprecedented levels of hyperurbanism in some developing countries are putting a strain on resources and infrastructure.  一些发展中国家前所未有的超级城市化水平给资源和基础设施带来了压力。
Hyperurbanism led to increased social inequality and environmental degradation in the city. 超级城市化导致该市社会不平等加剧和环境恶化。
近义词:overurbanization, mega-urbanization
反义词:ruralization
词性:n.
