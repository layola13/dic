backlash:/ˈbæklæʃ/| n.|强烈反应；反冲；后坐力|A strong negative reaction by a large number of people, especially to a social or political change.|例句：The government is facing a backlash over its new tax proposals. 政府正面临着针对其新税收提案的强烈反对。

例句：There was a public backlash against the violence shown on television. 公众对电视上播放的暴力内容表示强烈反对。|近义词：reaction, resistance, opposition, recoil|反义词：support, approval|词性：名词

backlash:/ˈbæklæʃ/| v.|发生强烈反应；产生反冲|To react strongly against something.|例句：Voters backlashed against the new policy. 选民强烈反对这项新政策。

例句：The market backlashed when the company announced its plans. 当公司宣布其计划时，市场做出了强烈反应。|近义词：recoil, react, resist|反义词：support, approve|词性：动词
