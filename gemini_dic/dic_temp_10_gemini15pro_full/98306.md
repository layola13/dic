insightfully:/ɪnˈsaɪtfʊli/| adv.|深刻地；富有洞察力地|With deep understanding and perception.  Showing clear and deep understanding of a complex situation.|She insightfully analyzed the political situation. 她深刻地分析了政治局势。|近义词：perceptively, astutely, wisely|反义词：superficially, shallowly, obtusely|词性: 副词

词源:  From "insightful" + "-ly."  "Insightful" comes from "insight" (from "in-" + "sight") + "-ful."
