bargain-hunter:/ˈbɑːrɡɪn ˌhʌntər/ | n. | 廉价商品搜寻者；逢低买入者 | A person who regularly searches for and buys things at a lower price than usual. | My aunt is a real bargain-hunter; she never pays full price for anything. 我阿姨是个真正的廉价商品搜寻者，她从来不买全价的东西。 | shopper, deal-seeker | spendthrift, big spender | noun


bargain-hunter:/ˈbɑːrɡɪn ˌhʌntər/ | n. | 特价商品猎人；精打细算的购物者 | Someone who actively seeks out and purchases goods or services at significantly reduced prices. |  Bargain-hunters flocked to the store's annual clearance sale. 特价商品猎人们蜂拥而至参加这家商店的年度清仓大甩卖。 |  cheapskate, economizer |  high roller, free spender | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "bargain" (from Old French "bargaignier," meaning "to haggle") + "hunter" (from Old English "hunta," meaning "hunter").
