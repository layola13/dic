macle: /ˈmækl/ | n. | 双晶;孪晶 | A twin crystal, especially one in which two individuals are united in a symmetrical manner. | The mineralogist identified the specimen as a macle of gypsum. 矿物学家将该标本鉴定为石膏的孪晶。 |  近义词: twin | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle French *macle* "twin," from Old French *mascle* "mesh, spot, stain," from Vulgar Latin *macula* "spot, mesh," from Latin *macula* "spot, stain."
