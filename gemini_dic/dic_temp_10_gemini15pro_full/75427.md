fruitarianism:/fruːˈteəriənɪzəm/| n. |果食主义|The practice of eating only fruits, nuts, and seeds, sometimes including other plant-based foods such as vegetables depending on the definition.|She adopted fruitarianism for ethical and health reasons. 她出于伦理和健康原因采用了果食主义。|Synonyms: frugivorism|Antonyms: omnivorism, carnivorism|noun
