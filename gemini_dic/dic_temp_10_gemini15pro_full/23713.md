brassicaceous: /ˌbræsɪˈkeɪʃəs/
adjective
十字花科的
Belonging to or relating to the family Brassicaceae (also called Cruciferae), which includes plants such as cabbage, mustard, and radish.
Kale is a brassicaceous vegetable that is packed with nutrients. 羽衣甘蓝是一种富含营养的十字花科蔬菜。
Brussels sprouts are small, leafy green vegetables that belong to the brassicaceous family. 抱子甘蓝是属于十字花科的小型绿叶蔬菜。
synonyms: cruciferous
antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could use terms like "non-brassicaceous" or names of other plant families like "solanaceous" or "leguminous")
word type: adjective
