amebean:/əˈmiːbiən/| adj. n.|变形虫的；变形虫似的；像变形虫的；变形虫(尤指阿米巴变形虫) |Relating to or characteristic of an amoeba;  an amoeba, especially an amoeboid protozoan.|The amebean movement of the cell was fascinating to observe.  细胞的变形虫运动令人着迷。|The doctor identified the infection as being caused by an amebean parasite. 医生确认感染是由一种变形虫寄生虫引起的。|近义词：amoeboid, protozoan|反义词:/(none -  no direct opposite for a biological classification)/|词性:adjective, noun


词源: From Modern Latin *amoeba* + *-ean*.  Ultimately from Greek *amoibḗ*, "change," referring to the constantly changing shape of the organism.
