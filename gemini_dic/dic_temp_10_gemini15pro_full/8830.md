antiexpansion:/ˌæntiɪkˈspænʃən/
adjective
反扩张的，反对扩张的
Opposing or resisting expansion, especially territorial expansion.
The senator voiced his antiexpansion sentiments during the debate.  参议员在辩论中表达了他的反扩张情绪。
The antiexpansion policy was popular with the voters. 反扩张政策受到选民欢迎。

近义词:anti-growth, contractionary
反义词:expansionist, pro-growth
词性:adjective
