outflee: /ˌaʊtˈfliː/
verb
逃走比…更快；胜过…逃跑
to flee faster or better than someone or something else
The gazelle outfled the lion. 瞪羚比狮子跑得更快逃脱了。
近义词: outrun, escape, evade
反义词: surrender, yield
词性: verb

词源:  From Old English *ūtflēon, from ūt (out) + flēon (to flee).  Essentially, "to flee out" or "to flee away from."
