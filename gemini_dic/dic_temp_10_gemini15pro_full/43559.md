craziest:/ˈkreɪziest/| adj.|最疯狂的；最荒唐的；最热衷的|Superlative form of crazy: most crazy.  It means extremely enthusiastic or excited about something, or mentally deranged, extremely foolish, or impractical.| That was the craziest party I've ever been to! 那是我参加过的最疯狂的派对！|Synonyms: wildest, maddest, insanest|Antonyms: sanest, calmest|adjective
