mustanging:/ˈmʌstæŋɪŋ/
n.
野马捕猎;捕捉野马;驯服野马
The act of capturing, breaking, or taming wild horses, especially Mustangs.
He made his living by mustanging in the American West.  他靠在美国西部捕捉野马为生。
近义词：horse wrangling, bronco busting
反义词: releasing, freeing
词性: 名词 (noun)

词源:  Derived from "mustang" + "-ing" (present participle/gerund suffix). "Mustang" itself comes from the Spanish word "mestengo," meaning "stray" or "ownerless beast," ultimately derived from "mesteño,"  meaning "belonging to the mesta" (a stockbreeders' association).
