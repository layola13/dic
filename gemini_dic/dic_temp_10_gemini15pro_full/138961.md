What is his next move? = 他的下一步行动是什么？
What is the next step in the process? = 这个过程的下一步是什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you doing next? = 你接下来要做什么？
What is she planning next? = 她接下来计划做什么？

What have you done next? = 你接下来做了什么？

What has she been planning next? = 她接下来一直在计划什么？
