lawyer-like:/ˈlɔːjərˌlaɪk/
adj.
像律师的；具有律师特征的；精明谨慎的
Resembling or characteristic of a lawyer; shrewd and cautious.
He gave a lawyer-like explanation of the contract. 他对合同作出了像律师一样精辟的解释。

近义词:attorney-like, legalistic, precise, meticulous, careful, shrewd, astute
反义词:careless, negligent, inattentive, sloppy

词性:adjective

词源: lawyer (n.) + -like (suffix)
"lawyer" from Anglo-French laweier, from lai "law" + -ier.
"-like" Old English -lic, from Proto-Germanic *-likaz.
