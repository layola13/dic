gamogenetic:/ˌɡæmoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. |有性生殖的，配子生殖的 | Relating to or reproducing by the union of gametes; sexual. |例句：Gamogenetic reproduction is more common in animals than asexual reproduction. 有性生殖在动物中比无性生殖更常见。

例句：The gamogenetic life cycle involves the fusion of male and female gametes. 有性生殖的生命周期包括雄性和雌性配子的融合。 | 近义词: sexual | 反义词: agamogenetic, asexual | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *gamos* "marriage" + *genesis* "origin, creation"
