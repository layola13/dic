maki:/ˈmɑːkiː/| n.|寿司卷；卷寿司|A type of sushi roll, often made with seaweed, rice, and various fillings such as fish, vegetables, or egg.|例句：I ordered a California maki for lunch. 我午饭点了一份加州卷。

例句：The maki was fresh and delicious. 这寿司卷很新鲜美味。|近义词：sushi roll, norimaki|反义词: (No direct antonym)|Noun
