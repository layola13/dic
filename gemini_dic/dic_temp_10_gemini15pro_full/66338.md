exculpate: /ɪkˈskʌlpeɪt/ | v. | 宣告无罪，开脱，使免受责备 | To clear from blame or guilt; to free from a charge or accusation. | The evidence presented in court exculpated the defendant from all charges.  法庭上提供的证据使被告免于所有指控。 | Synonyms: absolve, acquit, exonerate, vindicate | Antonyms: accuse, blame, convict, incriminate | verb

exculpated: /ɪkˈskʌlpeɪtɪd/ | adj. |  被证明无罪的，被开脱的 | Cleared of blame; declared innocent. |  The exculpated man walked free after years of wrongful imprisonment.  被证明无罪的男子在被冤枉入狱多年后重获自由。| Synonyms: absolved, acquitted, exonerated, vindicated | Antonyms: accused, blamed, convicted, incriminated | adjective
