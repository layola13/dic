incubatory:/ˈɪŋkjuːbeɪtəri/ /ˈɪŋkjəbətɔːri/ |adj.| 孵化的；潜伏的；培养的 |Relating to incubation; the process of keeping something at a suitable temperature and under suitable conditions so that it can develop.  Also, referring to a period of development before symptoms appear (especially of a disease). |The virus has an incubatory period of two to three weeks.| 近义词: incubation, developmental, latent | 反义词: active, manifest | 词性: adjective
