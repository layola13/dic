co-option:/ˌkəʊˈɒpʃən/ | n. | 共同选择，增选；拉拢，吸收 | The co-option of outside experts can bring valuable insights. 外部专家的增选可以带来宝贵的见解。 |  The opposition party criticized the co-option of several prominent activists into the government. 反对党批评政府拉拢了几位著名的活动家。 | 近义词：absorption, assimilation, incorporation | 反义词：exclusion, rejection | 词性: 名词

co-options:/ˌkəʊˈɒpʃənz/ | n. | 共同选择，增选；拉拢，吸收（复数） |  The co-options of new members strengthened the organization. 新成员的增选加强了该组织。 | Several recent co-options have raised concerns about the group's direction.  最近的几次吸收引发了人们对该组织发展方向的担忧。 | 近义词：absorptions, assimilations, incorporations | 反义词：exclusions, rejections | 词性: 名词


词源：From the Latin "co-optāre", meaning "to choose together," from "co-" (together) + "optāre" (to choose, wish).
