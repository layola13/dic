miliola:/ˌmɪliˈoʊlə/
n.
粟虫,粟虫属的有孔虫
A genus of foraminifera (single-celled organisms with shells) characterized by a porcellaneous (opaque white or cream-colored) test (shell) with numerous chambers arranged in a coiled pattern.
The miliola are common in shallow marine environments. 粟虫在浅海环境中很常见。
Miliola fossils are often found in limestone.  粟虫化石经常在石灰岩中被发现。
近义词:foraminifer, foraminifera
反义词: (No direct antonym for a genus of organisms)
词性:noun
