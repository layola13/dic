cliché:/ˈkliːʃeɪ/ (US), /ˈkliːʃeɪ/ (UK)| n.| 陈词滥调；老生常谈；套话|a phrase or opinion that is overused and betrays a lack of original thought.|  The phrase "time heals all wounds" is a cliché.  “时间会治愈一切创伤”这句话是老生常谈。| stereotype, platitude, banality | original, fresh, novel | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French cliché, past participle of clicher “to click, stereotype,” from clic “click.”  Originally a printing term referring to a stereotype plate.
