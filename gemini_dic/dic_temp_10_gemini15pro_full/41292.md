cordlesses:/ˈkɔːrdləsəz/
noun
无绳设备（复数）
The plural form of "cordless," referring to multiple devices that operate without a cord.
My cordlesses include a phone, a vacuum cleaner, and headphones. 我的无绳设备包括一部电话、一台吸尘器和一副耳机。
Synonyms: wireless devices, untethered devices
Antonyms: corded devices, wired devices
词性: 名词
