palmtop:/ˈpɑːmˌtɒp/ (UK), /ˈpɑːmˌtɑːp/ (US)| n.|掌上电脑；掌上型电脑|A small portable computer that fits in the palm of your hand, now largely superseded by smartphones. |例句：He used to carry a palmtop everywhere before smartphones became popular. 他在智能手机普及之前经常随身携带一台掌上电脑。|近义词：PDA (Personal Digital Assistant), handheld, pocket computer|反义词:desktop, laptop|noun

palmtop:/ˈpɑːmˌtɒp/ (UK), /ˈpɑːmˌtɑːp/ (US)| adj.|掌上型的；掌上电脑的|Designed to fit in the palm of the hand. |例句：Palmtop computers were once very popular. 掌上电脑曾经非常流行。|近义词：handheld, pocket-sized|反义词:desktop, full-sized|adjective
