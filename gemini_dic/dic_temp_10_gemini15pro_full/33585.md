cill:/sɪl/ | n. | 窗台，门槛 |A shelf or slab, especially one forming the bottom part of a window frame. | The cat sat on the window cill, watching the birds. 猫坐在窗台上，看着鸟儿。 | 近义词: windowsill, ledge | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *syll, from Old English *syll, from Proto-Germanic *sulją (“sill, threshold”), from Proto-Indo-European *swel- (“to swell, to swing, to turn”). Cognate with Scots sill (“threshold”), West Frisian syl (“sill, threshold”), Dutch zuil (“pillar, column”), German Säule (“pillar, column”), Swedish syll (“sill, sleeper, ground plate”).  The semantic development is from "beam, timber" to "threshold" to "window sill".
