overfaithfulness: /ˌoʊvərˈfeɪθfəlnəs/ | n. | 过度忠诚，过分忠实 | Excessive faithfulness or loyalty, often to the point of being detrimental. |  His overfaithfulness to the company's outdated policies hindered innovation. 他对公司过时政策的过度忠诚阻碍了创新。|  Her overfaithfulness to her friend blinded her to his flaws. 她对她朋友的过分忠诚使她对他的缺点视而不见。| 近义词: excessive loyalty, blind devotion, undue allegiance | 反义词: disloyalty, infidelity, treachery | 词性: noun

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "faithfulness" (noun from "faithful" + "-ness")
