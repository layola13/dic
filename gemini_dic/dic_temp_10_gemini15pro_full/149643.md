painters:/ˈpeɪntərz/| n.|画家，油漆工|A person who paints pictures or applies paint to surfaces.|The painters are coming tomorrow to repaint the house. 油漆工明天要来重新粉刷房子。|近义词：artists, decorators|反义词:(无直接反义词)|名词

词源:  From Middle English *peyntour*, *peyntur*, from Anglo-Norman *peintur*, *peintour*, *pinteur*, from Old French *peintre*, *pinteur* (French *peintre*), from Latin *pinctor*, agent noun from *pingō* (“I paint”).
