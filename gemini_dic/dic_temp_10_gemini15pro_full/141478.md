opinionation:/əˌpɪnɪˈeɪʃən/ | n. |武断，固执己见，自以为是| The state of being opinionated; the expression of strong opinions. |  He's known for his strong opinionation on political matters. 他以在政治问题上固执己见而闻名。

|  Her opinionation made it difficult to have a productive discussion. 她的武断使得很难进行富有成效的讨论。

|近义词：dogmatism, inflexibility, stubbornness|反义词：open-mindedness, flexibility|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->

From Late Latin *opinionatio*, from Latin opinio (“opinion”).
