inexcitability:/ˌɪnɪkˌsaɪtəˈbɪləti/
n.
无兴奋性，不易激动，冷静
The state or quality of being unexcitable; not easily excited.
His inexcitability in the face of danger was remarkable. 他在危险面前的冷静令人瞩目。
近义词：calmness, composure, tranquility, impassivity
反义词：excitability, nervousness, agitation
