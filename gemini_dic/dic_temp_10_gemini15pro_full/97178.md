infirmness:/ɪnˈfɜːrmnəs/ /ɪnˈfɝːmnəs/| n. |虚弱，衰弱；不坚定，不果断|The state of being weak, especially physically or mentally; lack of strength or firmness.|His infirmness prevented him from walking long distances. 他的虚弱使他无法长途跋涉。|Synonyms: weakness, frailty, debility, feebleness, fragility|Antonyms: strength, robustness, vigor, firmness, resolution|noun

infirmness:/ɪnˈfɜːrmnəs/ /ɪnˈfɝːmnəs/| n. |不坚定，不果断|Lack of firmness of will or character; irresolution.|He showed infirmness of purpose when faced with the difficult decision.  面对艰难的决定时，他表现出意志的薄弱。|Synonyms: irresolution, indecision, vacillation, hesitation|Antonyms: resolution, determination, decisiveness, firmness|noun
