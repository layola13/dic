consumingnesses:/kənˈsjuːmɪŋnɪsɪz/| n. | 消耗性；消耗量（罕用，通常用单数形式 consumingness） |  The state or quality of being consuming, especially in terms of time, energy, or resources.  It refers to the extent to which something consumes or uses up these things. This word is very rarely used in modern English.  |  The consumingnesses of modern life can be overwhelming. (现代生活的消耗性可能会让人难以承受。)  (This example is grammatically correct but sounds unnatural. It's much more common to say "The consuming nature of modern life..." or "How consuming modern life is...") | Near synonyms:  consumption, drain, demand | Antonyms:  productivity, efficiency | noun
