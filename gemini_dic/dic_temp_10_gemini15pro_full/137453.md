nonthinkings:/nɒnˈθɪŋkɪŋz/ | n. |缺乏思考的人（群体）; 思想僵化的人（群体）| People who do not engage in critical thinking or reflection, often accepting information or ideas without questioning them.  Those who are unwilling or unable to think independently or creatively. |  The politician dismissed his critics as a group of nonthinkings.  这位政治家把批评他的人斥为一群不动脑筋的人。

The nonthinkings just follow the crowd; they don't have their own opinions.  那些不动脑筋的人只会随波逐流，没有自己的见解。 | 近义词：conformists, sheep, uncritical masses | 反义词: freethinkers, intellectuals, critical thinkers | 词性：名词
