parturient:/pɑːrˈtʊəriənt/ /pɑːrˈtʃʊəriənt/ | adj. n.| 分娩的；临产的；即将生产的（adj.）；产妇（n.） | Giving birth or about to give birth; a woman who is giving birth. |  例句：The parturient cow was moved to a separate stall.  临产的母牛被移到了单独的畜栏里。

例句：The hospital wing was specifically designed for parturient mothers. 这家医院的侧楼是专为产妇设计的。

近义词：childbearing, pregnant, expectant, delivering | 反义词: barren, infertile | 词性: adjective, noun
