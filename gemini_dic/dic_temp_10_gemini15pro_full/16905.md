basked:/bɑːskt/ /bæskt/
verb
晒太阳；取暖；沉浸于；沐浴于
To lie or sit enjoying warmth or sunshine.  To feel pleasure from something that makes you feel good.
The cat basked in the sun. 猫在阳光下晒太阳。
She basked in the admiration of her fans. 她沉浸在粉丝们的赞美之中。
Synonyms:  revel, wallow, luxuriate
Antonyms:  shiver, freeze
词性：动词
