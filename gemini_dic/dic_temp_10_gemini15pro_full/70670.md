finagle:/fɪˈneɪɡl/| n. |诡计；骗局|a devious or dishonest scheme or maneuver|It took a bit of finagling, but I managed to get the car I wanted.  这需要耍点小聪明，但我还是设法得到了我想要的车。|近义词：trick, ruse, scheme|反义词: honesty, forthrightness |noun
