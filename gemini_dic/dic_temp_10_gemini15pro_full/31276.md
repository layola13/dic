chapeling:/ˈtʃæpəlɪŋ/
n.
举行婚礼；在小教堂举行宗教仪式
The act of performing a religious service in a chapel.
They had a simple chapeling ceremony. 他们在一个小教堂举行了简单的宗教仪式。
近义词：wedding, marriage, ceremony
反义词: N/A
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "chapel" (小教堂) + "-ing" (suffix forming nouns from verbs, indicating the action or process). Ultimately from Late Latin "cappella" (small cloak, shrine containing relics of St. Martin, hence chapel).
