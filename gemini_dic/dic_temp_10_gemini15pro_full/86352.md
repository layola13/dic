hectocotylized:/ˌhɛktəˈkɒtɪlaɪzd/ | adj. | 交接腕化的；茎化的 |  Having a hectocotylus; specifically : having an arm specialized for delivering spermatophores. (Of a cephalopod, especially a male) having one of the arms or tentacles modified for sexual reproduction.  | The male octopus has a hectocotylized arm for transferring sperm. 雄章鱼有一条交接腕用来传递精子。 |  modified, specialized | unmodified, unspecialized | adjective

hectocotylus:/ˌhɛktəˈkɒtɪləs/ | n. | 交接腕；茎化腕 | One of the arms of a male cephalopod mollusk, modified for delivering spermatophores to the female.  A modified arm or tentacle used by male cephalopods (such as octopuses and squids) to transfer sperm to the female. |  The hectocotylus of the octopus is a fascinating example of sexual adaptation. 章鱼的交接腕是性适应的一个迷人例子。 |  spermatophore-bearing arm | standard arm | noun
