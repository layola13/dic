demigrate:/ˌdɛmiˈɡreɪt/ | v. | 降级；部分迁移；半迁移 | To migrate a part of a system, or to move to a less important or powerful position. |  Example 1: The company decided to demigrate some of its servers to a cloud-based platform. 公司决定将其部分服务器降级到基于云的平台。

Example 2:  He was demigrated to a junior role after the company restructuring. 公司重组后，他被降级到初级职位。| 近义词: downgrade, relegate, move | 反义词: upgrade, promote | 词性: 动词
