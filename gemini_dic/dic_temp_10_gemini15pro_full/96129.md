indemnifiers:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪfaɪərz/ | n. |赔偿者，保障者|  A person or entity who agrees to protect another from financial loss or damage. | The contract clearly identifies the indemniters and their responsibilities. 合同明确规定了赔偿者及其责任。|  The company acted as indemnifier for its employees' legal expenses.  公司为其员工的法律费用提供赔偿。| synonyms: guarantors, surety, underwriters | antonyms:  injured party, claimant | noun

indemnify:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪfaɪ/ | v. |赔偿，保障| To compensate for loss or damage; to protect against future loss or damage. | The insurance policy will indemnify you against any losses due to fire. 保险单将赔偿你因火灾造成的任何损失。 |  The company agreed to indemnify the customer for any defects in the product. 公司同意赔偿客户产品中的任何缺陷。 | synonyms: compensate, reimburse, protect, secure | antonyms: penalize, charge, fine | verb
