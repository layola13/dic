hypermodernist:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɒdənɪst/ (UK) /ˌhaɪpərˈmɑːdɜrnɪst/ (US)| n. adj.| 超现代主义者；超现代主义的|A person who adheres to hypermodernism, a cultural, artistic, or architectural style that emerged in the late 20th and early 21st centuries, characterized by a self-conscious embrace of technology, globalization, and consumer culture, often with an ironic or playful attitude.|
Example: The architect's hypermodernist designs incorporated sleek lines and cutting-edge materials.  这位建筑师的超现代主义设计融合了流畅的线条和尖端材料。

Example:  She's a self-proclaimed hypermodernist, always chasing the latest trends. 她自称是超现代主义者，总是追逐最新的潮流。
|近义词：postmodernist, avant-garde, futurist|反义词: traditionalist, classicist|词性: noun, adjective
