despeed:/ˌdiːˈspiːd/| v. |减速，降低速度|To reduce the speed of something, especially a vehicle or engine.|The train despeeded as it approached the station. 火车接近车站时减速了。|近义词：decelerate, slow down, reduce speed|反义词: accelerate, speed up|词性:动词

词源:  "de-" (prefix indicating removal or reversal) + "speed" (noun and verb relating to velocity).  Essentially, it means to remove or reduce speed.
