inholding:/ˈɪnhəʊldɪŋ/ (US) /ˈɪnhəʊldɪŋ/ (UK)
n.
私有地；飞地
A piece of land owned by a person or organization within an area owned by another person or organization.
The farmer had a small inholding of land surrounded by the national park. 这位农民在国家公园内拥有一小块私有土地。
近义词：enclave, exclave
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps surrounding land)
词性: Noun
