evolving:/ɪˈvɒlvɪŋ/ (UK) /ɪˈvɑːlvɪŋ/ (US)
adjective
进化中的，发展中的
developing gradually, especially from a simple to a more complex form.
例句：The company culture is constantly evolving. 公司文化在不断发展。
例句：The evolving situation requires our constant attention. 不断变化的局势需要我们持续关注。
近义词：developing, progressing, changing, advancing, growing
反义词：static, unchanging, fixed, stagnant, regressing
词性：adjective

evolve:/ɪˈvɒlv/ (UK) /ɪˈvɑːlv/ (US)
verb
进化，发展
develop gradually, especially from a simple to a more complex form.
例句：The company has evolved into a major player in the market.  这家公司已经发展成为市场上的主要参与者。
例句：Birds are thought to have evolved from dinosaurs.  鸟类被认为是从恐龙进化而来的。
近义词：develop, progress, change, advance, grow
反义词：devolve, regress, degenerate
词性：verb
