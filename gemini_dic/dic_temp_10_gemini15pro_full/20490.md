bituminous:/bɪˈtjuːmɪnəs/ /bɪˈtuːmɪnəs/
adjective
沥青的；含沥青的
Containing or treated with bitumen.
The road was paved with bituminous asphalt. 这条路是用沥青铺成的。

The bituminous coal burned with a smoky flame. 这种烟煤燃烧时冒着浓烟。

synonym: asphaltic, tarry
antonym: non-bituminous

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *bituminosus, from Latin bitumen "bitumen, asphalt" (see bitumen) + -osus "-ous."
