n.
昏沉；迟钝；麻木
A state of being drowsy, sluggish, or mentally slow.
His dopinesses made it difficult for him to concentrate in class. 他的昏沉让他难以集中注意力上课。
The medication caused some dopinesses as a side effect.  这种药物的副作用是引起一些迟钝。

反义词：alertness, wakefulness, vivacity
