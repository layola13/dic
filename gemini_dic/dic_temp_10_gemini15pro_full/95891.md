incredibility:/ˌɪnkrɛdəˈbɪləti/
n.
难以置信，不可思议
the quality of being difficult or impossible to believe.
The sheer incredibility of the story made it hard to accept.  这个故事太不可思议了，让人难以接受。
近义词：unbelievability, implausibility
反义词：credibility, believability
词性：名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *incredibilitas, from Latin incredibilis (“unbelievable”), from in- (“not”) + credibilis (“believable”).  Ultimately from the Latin credere (“to believe”).
