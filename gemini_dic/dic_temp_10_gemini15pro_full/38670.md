condemnation:/ˌkɑːndɛmˈneɪʃn/ /ˌkɒndɛmˈneɪʃn/| n. |谴责，定罪，非难|The act of condemning something or someone as wrong, guilty, or bad.  It can also refer to the state of being condemned.|
The jury's condemnation of the defendant was swift. 陪审团迅速对被告做出了判决。

His actions drew condemnation from all sides. 他的行为受到了各方的谴责。


近义词：censure, criticism, denunciation, disapproval, reprobation|
反义词：approval, praise, commendation, exoneration, acquittal|
词性: noun
