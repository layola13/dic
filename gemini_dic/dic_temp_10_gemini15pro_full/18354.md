bellowing:/ˈbeləʊɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈbɛloʊɪŋ/ (US)| v. n. adj.|吼叫，咆哮；大声叫喊；（风）呼啸；|A deep roaring shout or sound, like that of a bull.
