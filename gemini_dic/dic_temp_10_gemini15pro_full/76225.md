futons:/ˈfjuːtɒnz/ (British) /ˈfutɑːnz/ (American)| n. |日式床垫，蒲团|A Japanese quilted mattress, often used for sleeping on the floor, or as a sofa.|例句：We bought two new futons for the guest room. 我们为客房买了两个新的蒲团。

例句：The futon can be folded up and stored away during the day.  这个蒲团白天可以折叠起来存放。

|近义词：mattress, bed, sofa bed|反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps "conventional bed")|词性:noun
