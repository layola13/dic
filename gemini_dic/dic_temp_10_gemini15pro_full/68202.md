fair-traded:/ˌfeər ˈtreɪdɪd/| adj.|公平交易的|Produced or bought and sold under fair-trade principles, meaning producers in developing countries receive a fair price for their goods.|例句：She always buys fair-traded coffee. 她总是买公平交易的咖啡。|例句：The shop sells a range of fair-traded products. 这家商店出售一系列公平交易的产品。|近义词：ethically sourced, sustainably produced|反义词：exploitative, unethically produced|词性:形容词
