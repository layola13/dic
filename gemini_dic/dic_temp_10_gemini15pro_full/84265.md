half-successfully:/ˌhæf səkˈsɛsfəli/
| adv. | 部分成功地；不太成功地 | Partially successful; not completely successful. |  The project was half-successfully implemented, with some aspects working well and others failing completely.  该项目部分成功实施，一些方面运作良好，而其他方面则完全失败。|Near Synonyms: partially successfully, moderately successfully, somewhat successfully | Antonyms: completely successfully, totally successfully, utterly successfully|词性:副词

half-successfully:/ˌhæf səkˈsɛsfəli/
|  |  | Formed by combining "half" (Old English healf) meaning "one of two equal parts" and "successfully" (from "success" - Latin successus "advance, a good result") meaning "in a successful manner". The hyphen connects the two words to form a single adverb. | | | |词性:副词
