adulatress: /əˈdʌltərəs/
n.
奸妇
A woman who commits adultery.
The adulatress was ostracized by the community. =  这名奸妇被社区排斥。
She was branded an adulatress after her affair was discovered. = 她的婚外情被发现后，她被打上了奸妇的烙印。
近义词: adulterer, cheat, infidel
反义词: faithful wife, loyal wife
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *adulterece, from Latin adultera (“adulteress”), feminine of adulter (“adulterer, adulterous”).

USAGE->
The word "adulatress" is generally used in a formal or literary context.  It carries a strong negative connotation and is less common in everyday conversation.
