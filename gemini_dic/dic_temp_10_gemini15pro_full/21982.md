boho: /ˈboʊhoʊ/ (US) /ˈbəʊhəʊ/ (UK) | adj. n. | 波西米亚风格的；波西米亚人 |  Short for "bohemian," referring to a style of fashion drawing on various bohemian and hippie influences, often involving long flowing or tiered skirts, embroidery, ethnic patterns, fringe, and natural fabrics. Also used to describe a person who adopts this style. | She has a very boho chic style. 她有一种非常波西米亚的时尚风格。 |  Synonyms: bohemian, hippie, free-spirited, unconventional | Antonyms: conventional, traditional, conformist | adjective, noun


boho: /ˈboʊhoʊ/ (US) /ˈbəʊhəʊ/ (UK) | n. | 波西米亚人 | A person who adopts a bohemian lifestyle, often associated with artistic or literary pursuits, unconventional dress, and a rejection of traditional values. |  He's a bit of a boho, always travelling and writing poetry.  他有点像个波西米亚人，总是旅行和写诗。 | Synonyms: bohemian, hippie, free spirit | Antonyms: conformist, traditionalist | noun
