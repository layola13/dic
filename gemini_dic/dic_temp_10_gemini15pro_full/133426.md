nonfarcicalities:/ˌnɒnˈfɑːsɪˈkælɪtiz/ | n. | 非闹剧性的事物；严肃的事情 |  Things that are not farcical; matters of serious concern.  The plural form of "nonfarcicality." | Amidst the nonfarcicalities of the situation, a moment of levity emerged. 在局势的严肃性中，出现了一丝轻松的时刻。|  seriousnesses, realities, gravities | frivolities, absurdities | noun

Etymology:
"Non-" (prefix): From Latin "non," meaning "not."
"Farcical": From "farce," from Old French "farce" (stuffing), from Latin "farsa" (stuffed).  Implies something absurd and exaggerated.
"-ity" (suffix):  Forms nouns of quality or state.
"-ies" (suffix): Plural marker.
