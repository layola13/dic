amphiarthrodial:/ˌæmfiˌɑːrˈθroʊdiəl/
adjective
微动的；半关节的
Relating to or designating an amphiarthrosis. An amphiarthrosis is a type of joint that allows slight movement, such as the joints between the vertebrae.
The pubic symphysis is an example of an amphiarthrodial joint.  耻骨联合是微动关节的一个例子。
The cartilaginous joints between the vertebrae are amphiarthrodial, allowing limited movement. 脊椎之间的软骨连接是微动的，允许有限的运动。
synonym: slightly movable
antonym: immovable, diarthrodial
词性: adjective
