merrily:/ˈmɛrɪli/ | adv. | 愉快地，高兴地 | In a cheerful and happy manner.  | They sang merrily around the campfire. 他们围着篝火愉快地唱歌。 |  happily, cheerfully, joyfully | sadly, gloomily | adverb

词源:
From Middle English *merili, from Old English *mergelice (“merrily, pleasantly, sweetly”), equivalent to merry +‎ -ly. Cognate with Dutch meerlijk (“merrily”), German merrily.
