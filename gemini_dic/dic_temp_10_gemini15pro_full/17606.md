beauty:/ˈbjuːti/ (US) /ˈbjuːti/ (UK)| n.|美；美人；美好的东西|A combination of qualities, such as shape, color, or form, that pleases the aesthetic senses, especially the sight.|She was a woman of great beauty. 她是一位绝世美人。|synonyms: loveliness, attractiveness, elegance, charm|antonyms: ugliness, hideousness, unattractiveness|noun
