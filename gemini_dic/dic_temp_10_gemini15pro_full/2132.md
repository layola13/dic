adaptive:/əˈdæptɪv/| adj.|适应的，适应性的|Able to adjust to new conditions.|The company has developed an adaptive marketing strategy that responds to changes in consumer demand.  这家公司开发了一种适应性营销策略，以应对消费者需求的变化。

Adaptive learning software adjusts the difficulty level based on the student's performance.  自适应学习软件会根据学生的表现调整难度级别。|近义词：flexible, adjustable, responsive, versatile|反义词：inflexible, rigid, unyielding|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adaptīvus*, from Latin adaptāre (to adapt), from ad- (to) + aptāre (to fit). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ep- (to take, seize).


USAGE->

Predicative usage:
The human brain is remarkably adaptive. = 人的大脑具有非凡的适应能力。
Organisms need to be adaptive to survive in changing environments. = 生物体需要适应才能在不断变化的环境中生存。
