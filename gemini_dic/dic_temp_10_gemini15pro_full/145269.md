overbook: /ˌoʊvərˈbʊk/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈbʊk/ (UK)| v. |超额预订|To sell more tickets or reservations (for a flight, hotel, etc.) than there are places available.|The airline overbooked the flight, and some passengers had to be bumped. 航空公司超额预订了航班，一些乘客不得不被拒绝登机。|近义词：overcrowded, oversell|反义词: underbook|词性:动词
