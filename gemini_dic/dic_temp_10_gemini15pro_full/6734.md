analyzed:/ˈænəˌlaɪzd/ | v. | 分析过的，解析过的 |  Having been subjected to analysis; examined closely and critically. |  The results were analyzed and interpreted. 结果经过分析和解释。 | examined, studied, investigated, scrutinized | ignored, neglected | verb

Etymology:
From the verb "analyse" (British English) or "analyze" (American English), which comes from the French "analyser," ultimately from the Greek "analyein," meaning "to loosen up, dissolve, resolve."  The suffix "-ed" forms the past participle, indicating a completed action.
