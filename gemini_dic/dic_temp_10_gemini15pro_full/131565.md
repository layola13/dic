noncommunicativeness:/ˌnɒnkəˈmjuːnɪkətɪvnəs/ | n. | 不善交际；沉默寡言 |  The state or quality of being unwilling or unable to communicate effectively.  It refers to a reluctance or inability to share information, thoughts, or feelings with others. | His noncommunicativeness made it difficult to work with him. 他的不善交际使得很难和他共事。|  reticence, taciturnity, unresponsiveness |  communicativeness, loquacity, talkativeness | noun
