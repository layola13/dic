galvanises:/ˈɡælvənaɪzɪz/
verb
激励；刺激；镀锌
to shock or excite someone into taking action; to coat iron or steel with a layer of zinc to prevent it from rusting
The news of the attack galvanised the community into action.  这起袭击的消息激励了社区采取行动。
The steel is galvanised to prevent rust. 钢铁镀锌以防生锈。
近义词:stimulate, excite, rouse, spur, prod, inspire, jolt, electrify, energize, invigorate, coat, plate
反义词:demotivate, discourage, depress, soothe, calm
词性:v.


galvanised:/ˈɡælvənaɪzd/
adjective
镀锌的；激励的
coated with zinc; shocked or excited into taking action
The bridge is made of galvanised steel. 这座桥是用镀锌钢建造的。
He felt galvanised by the challenge. 他被挑战激励了。
近义词:zinc-coated, stimulated, excited, roused, spurred
反义词:uncoated, demotivated, discouraged, depressed
词性:adj.


词源:From French galvaniser, from galvanisme 'galvanism', named after Luigi Galvani (1737–98), Italian physiologist.
