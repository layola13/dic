dementias:/dɪˈmɛnʃəz/ /dɪˈmɛnʃiəz/
n.
痴呆症（复数）
A group of related syndromes characterized by a decline in cognitive function, affecting memory, thinking, and behavior.  This decline is severe enough to interfere with daily life.
Many forms of dementia exist, such as Alzheimer's disease. 许多类型的痴呆症存在，例如阿尔茨海默病。
Dementias are a growing concern as the population ages. 随着人口老龄化，痴呆症越来越受到关注。
近义词:  cognitive impairments,  neurocognitive disorders
反义词:  cognitive health, mental acuity
词性: noun
