barney:/ˈbɑːrni/| n.|巴尼；吵闹；争吵；欺骗；哄骗|A noisy or drunken gathering; a quarrel; a hoax or deception.|例句：They had a real barney last night. 昨晚他们大吵了一架。|近义词：quarrel, argument, fight, brawl, ruckus, deception, hoax|反义词：agreement, harmony, peace, truth|noun

barney:/ˈbɑːrni/| vt.|哄骗；欺骗|To cheat or deceive someone.|例句：He tried to barney me into buying his old car. 他想骗我买他的旧车。|近义词：cheat, deceive, trick, swindle, con|反义词：be honest, tell the truth|verb
