midtone:/ˈmɪdˌtəʊn/ (UK) /ˈmɪdˌtoʊn/ (US)| n. adj.| 中音; 中色调 |A tone, colour, or shade that is neither very light nor very dark.  In music, it refers to a tone approximately in the middle of the audible range. |The artist used a subtle midtone to create a sense of depth.  艺术家用了一种微妙的中色调来营造一种深度感。| The piano's midtones were particularly resonant. 这架钢琴的中音区特别洪亮。|近义词:mezzo-tone, middle tone, neutral tone |反义词:highlight, shadow, high tone, low tone|noun, adjective
