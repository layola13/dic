mockery:/ˈmɑːkəri/ (US), /ˈmɒkəri/ (UK)| n. |嘲弄，嘲笑；愚弄；拙劣的模仿；笑柄|The trial was a mockery of justice. 这场审判是对正义的嘲弄。|例句：His attempts at cooking were a mockery. 他做饭的尝试简直就是个笑话。|近义词：derision, ridicule, scorn, contempt, travesty|反义词：respect, admiration, tribute|词性:noun
词源:Late Middle English: from Old French moquerie, from moquer ‘to mock.’
