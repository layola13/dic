nebulas:/ˈnɛbjʊləz/ (US), /ˈnɛbjʊliː/ (UK)| n. |星云; 云状物; 模糊的形状|A nebula is a cloud of gas and dust in outer space, visible in the night sky either as an indistinct bright patch or as a dark silhouette against other luminous matter.|The Horsehead Nebula is a famous dark nebula.|Synonyms: cloud, haze, mist|Antonyms: clarity, clearness|Noun
