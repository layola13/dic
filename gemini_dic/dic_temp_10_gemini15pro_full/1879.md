ETYMOLOGY->
From French *acrostiche*, from Late Latin *acrostichis*, from Greek *akrostikhis*, from *akros* (at the end) + *stikhos* (line of verse). 最终源自希腊语 akros（末端的）+ stikhos（诗句）。
