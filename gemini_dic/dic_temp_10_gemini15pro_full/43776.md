cremains:/krɪˈmeɪnz/
n.
骨灰
Cremated remains; ashes.
The cremains were scattered at sea. 骨灰撒在了海上。
近义词:ashes
反义词:body, corpse
词性:名词


Etymology:
A portmanteau of "cremated" and "remains."  First recorded in the early 20th century.  From "cremated" (past participle of "cremate," from Latin "cremare" - to burn) + "remains" (from Old French "remaindre" - to stay, remain).
