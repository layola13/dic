mossie:/ˈmɒzi/ (UK), /ˈmɑːzi/ (US)| n.|蚊子|An informal word for mosquito, a small flying insect that bites people and animals and sucks their blood.|例句：The mossies were out in force last night and I got bitten several times. 昨晚蚊子成群出动，我被咬了好几口。|例句：I hate camping because the mossies are always so bad. 我讨厌露营，因为蚊子总是很多。|近义词：mosquito, gnat|反义词: (No direct antonym for an insect)|词性:noun
