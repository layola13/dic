aristocratism:/ˌærɪˈstɒkrətɪzəm/
n.
贵族主义，贵族统治；贵族作风，贵族气派
The belief in or support for government by an aristocracy or the upper classes.  The behavior and attitudes characteristic of aristocrats.
He exhibited an air of aristocratism that set him apart from the commoners. 他展现出一种贵族主义的气派，让他与平民百姓区分开来。
Synonyms: elitism, snobbishness, classism, blue blood
Antonyms: egalitarianism, populism
noun
