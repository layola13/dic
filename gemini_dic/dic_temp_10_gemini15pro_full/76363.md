gadgetries: /ˈɡædʒɪtriz/
noun
小玩意儿；小配件；精巧的小机械装置
A collection of small, often ingenious, mechanical or electronic devices or tools.
He was fascinated by all sorts of gadgetries, from pocket knives to miniature drones. 他对各种各样的小玩意儿都很着迷，从袖珍小刀到微型无人机。
synonyms: gadgets, devices, gizmos, contraptions
antonyms: necessities, essentials
词性: 名词
