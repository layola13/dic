adjourned: /əˈdʒɜːrnd/ (UK) /əˈdʒɜːrnd/ (US)| v. adj.|休会，延期，推迟；|To formally end a meeting, gathering, or official proceeding, often with the intention of resuming it later.  Also used as an adjective to describe something that has been postponed. |The meeting was adjourned until the following week. 会议延期到下周。

The judge adjourned the trial. 法官宣布休庭。


The adjourned meeting will take place next month.  延期的会议将于下个月举行。 |synonyms: postponed, suspended, deferred, recessed, prorogued| antonyms: convened, commenced, initiated, resumed|verb
adjourned: /əˈdʒɜːrnd/ (UK) /əˈdʒɜːrnd/ (US)| adj.|休会的，延期的|Describing something that has been postponed or suspended. |The adjourned meeting will be held tomorrow. 休会的会议将于明天举行。

The case was left adjourned.  案件被延期审理。|synonyms: postponed, suspended, deferred, recessed, prorogued| antonyms: convened, commenced, initiated, resumed|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ajourner*  "to summon to court," later "to put off, postpone," from *a* "to" + *jour* "day."  The sense shifted from "summoning to a particular day" to "postponing to another day."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee adjourns the meeting every week. = 委员会每周都会休会。
The court adjourns cases regularly. = 法院定期休庭。

一般过去时:
They adjourned the meeting due to lack of quorum. = 由于缺乏法定人数，他们休会了。
The judge adjourned the trial until new evidence was found. = 法官将审判延期，直到找到新的证据。

现在完成时:
The meeting has been adjourned until further notice. = 会议已延期，直至另行通知。
Congress has adjourned for the summer recess. = 国会已休会，开始夏季休假。

过去完成时:
The meeting had been adjourned before I arrived. = 在我到达之前，会议已经休会了。
The trial had already been adjourned several times before a verdict was reached. = 在达成判决之前，审判已经延期了好几次。

将来时:
The meeting will be adjourned if there are no further questions. = 如果没有其他问题，会议将休会。
The conference will adjourn at 5 p.m. tomorrow. = 会议将于明天下午5点休会。


被动语态:
The meeting was adjourned by the chairman. = 会议由主席宣布休会。
The case was adjourned until next month. = 案件延期到下个月审理。


PHRASE->
adjourn the meeting = 休会
adjourn the court = 休庭
adjourn the debate = 暂停辩论
adjourn to a place = 转移到某地（继续进行）
adjourn for lunch/dinner/the day = 暂停去吃午饭/晚饭/结束一天的会议
