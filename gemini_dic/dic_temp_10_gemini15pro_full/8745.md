antidevelopment:/ˌænti dɪˈvɛləpmənt/
| n. |反发展，反开发|Opposition to planned growth, especially economic growth, often due to concerns about environmental damage, social disruption, or loss of traditional values.|The local community voiced strong antidevelopment sentiments, fearing the proposed resort would destroy the natural beauty of the coastline.|近义词：opposition to development, resistance to growth, preservationism|反义词:development, pro-development, growth|noun
