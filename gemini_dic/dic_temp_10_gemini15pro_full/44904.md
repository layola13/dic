cryonicses:/kraɪˈɒnɪsiːz/
n.
人体冷冻法（的复数形式）

Some people believe cryonicses is the key to immortality.  有些人相信人体冷冻法是获得永生的关键。
Cryonicses facilities are becoming more prevalent. 人体冷冻设施正变得越来越普遍。
