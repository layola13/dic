dancy:/ˈdænsi/| adj.|优美的，适合跳舞的|Characterized by or suitable for dancing; graceful and rhythmic.|Her dancy movements captivated the audience. 她优美的舞姿吸引了观众。|近义词：rhythmic, graceful, flowing|反义词:clumsy, awkward|词性:形容词

词源: From "dance" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives).  Derived from Old French "dancier" and ultimately from Frankish *dansōn.
