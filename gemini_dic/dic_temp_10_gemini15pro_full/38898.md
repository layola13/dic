confervoid:/kənˈfɜːrvɔɪd/| adj. |丝状的，似绿藻的|Resembling or characteristic of Conferva, a genus of filamentous green algae.|The confervoid growth in the pond indicated an excess of nutrients.|近义词：filamentous, algal|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Conferva + -oid. Conferva is from Latin conferruminare "to solder together," from com- "together" + ferruminare "to solder," from ferrumen "cement, rust of iron." -oid means "resembling" or "having the form of."
