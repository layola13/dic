diachronic:/ˌdaɪəˈkrɒnɪk/ /ˌdaɪəˈkrɑːnɪk/| adj.|历时的，贯穿历史的|Relating to changes in something, especially language, that happen over time.|The diachronic study of language considers its evolution through history.  历时语言学研究考察语言在历史长河中的演变。|Example: A diachronic analysis of the word "nice" reveals its surprising journey from meaning "foolish" to "pleasant."  对“nice”一词的历时分析揭示了它从“愚蠢的”到“令人愉快的”的惊人演变过程。

|近义词：historical, evolutionary|反义词：synchronic|词性: adjective
