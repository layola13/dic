oenophile:/ˈiːnəfaɪl/ /ˈiːnəˌfaɪl/| n.|葡萄酒爱好者；品酒行家|A person who is knowledgeable about and enjoys wine.|She's a true oenophile, with a cellar full of vintage wines. 她是一位真正的葡萄酒爱好者，地窖里收藏了大量的年份葡萄酒。|He considers himself an oenophile, but he actually only drinks expensive Cabernet Sauvignon. 他自认为是葡萄酒鉴赏家，但实际上他只喝昂贵的赤霞珠。|近义词：wine lover, wine connoisseur, wine aficionado|反义词：teetotaler, abstainer|词性:noun
Etymology: From Greek *oinos* (wine) + *philos* (loving).
