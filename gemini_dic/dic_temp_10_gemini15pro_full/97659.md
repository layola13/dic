inhibitor:/ɪnˈhɪbɪtər/| n. |抑制剂，抑制物|A substance that slows down or prevents a particular chemical reaction or other process, or that reduces the activity of a particular enzyme.|The drug acted as an inhibitor, preventing the virus from replicating.|Synonyms: suppressant, retardant, blocker|Antonyms: activator, catalyst, stimulant|noun

inhibit:/ɪnˈhɪbɪt/| vt. |抑制，约束|To prevent or slow down the activity or occurrence of something.|Fear inhibited him from speaking out.|Synonyms: restrain, hinder, impede|Antonyms: encourage, promote, stimulate|verb
