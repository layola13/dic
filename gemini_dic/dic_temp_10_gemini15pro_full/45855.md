cut-it:/ˈkʌt ɪt/ | idiom | 行了，够了，停止 | Stop doing something annoying or undesirable. | "Cut it out, you two! I'm tired of your bickering." 你们两个够了！我厌倦了你们的争吵。 | Example:  He was making fun of her, but she told him to cut it out. 他在取笑她，但她让他住嘴。| Synonyms: stop it, knock it off, give it a rest | Antonyms: continue, keep going | idiom
