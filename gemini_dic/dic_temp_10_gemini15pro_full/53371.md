disaffirmance:/ˌdɪsəˈfɜːrməns/ | n. | 否认，拒绝批准，废除 | The disaffirmance of the contract by the minor rendered it void. 未成年人对合同的否认使其无效。|  A court decision resulting in the disaffirmance of the lower court's ruling. 法院的判决导致下级法院的裁决被废除。 | 近义词：repudiation, rejection, revocation | 反义词：affirmation, ratification, acceptance | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From dis- (expressing negation) + affirmance (from Old French afermer, ultimately from Latin affirmare).
