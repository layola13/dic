epicedium:/ˌɛpɪˈsiːdiəm/| n. |挽歌；悼亡诗|A song of mourning or lament for the dead, especially one intended to be sung at a funeral. |Example 1:  He wrote a touching epicedium for his late wife. 他为已故的妻子写了一首感人的挽歌。

Example 2: The epicedium was performed by a renowned choir. 这首挽歌由一个著名的合唱团演唱。|近义词：elegy, lament, dirge, requiem|反义词:paean, encomium|词性:noun
