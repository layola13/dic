mineral:/ˈmɪnərəl/ | n. adj. | 矿物，矿物质；矿泉水 | A solid inorganic substance of natural occurrence.  A substance obtained from the ground, especially one that is valuable. |  The rocks are rich in minerals. 这些岩石富含矿物质。 | synonyms: ore, rock, stone, gem, jewel | antonyms: organic | noun, adjective
