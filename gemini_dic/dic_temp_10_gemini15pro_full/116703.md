matutine:/ˈmætjʊtaɪn/| adj. n.|早晨的，清晨的；晨祷|Of or relating to the morning; occurring in the morning, especially during the period from midnight to noon.  Also, a service of morning prayer. |例句：The matutine dew glistened on the grass. 清晨的露珠在草地上闪闪发光。

例句：He attended the matutine service at the cathedral. 他参加了大教堂的晨祷。|近义词：morning, early, dawn,  matinal|反义词:vespertine, evening, nocturnal|词性:adjective, noun

Etymology: From Latin *matutinus*, from *Matuta*, goddess of the dawn.
