inspecting:/ɪnˈspɛktɪŋ/
verb
检查，视察
the act of looking at something carefully in order to learn more about it or find flaws
The health inspector was inspecting the kitchen. 卫生检查员正在检查厨房。
examining, scrutinizing, surveying
ignoring, neglecting
v.
