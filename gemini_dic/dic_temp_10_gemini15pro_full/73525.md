foreshot:/ˈfɔːrʃɒt/ (UK) /ˈfɔːrʃɑːt/ (US)| n.| 前馏分；（蒸馏出的）最初的液体；预感；预兆|The foreshot, collected first in the distillation process, contains methanol and other volatile impurities.  前馏分在蒸馏过程中首先被收集，含有甲醇和其他挥发性杂质。| The artist's early works offer a foreshot of his later masterpieces.  这位艺术家早期的作品预示了他后来的杰作。|Synonyms: precursor, harbinger, omen,  forewarning|Antonyms: afterthought, hindsight|noun
