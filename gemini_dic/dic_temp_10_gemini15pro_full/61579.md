emergence:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒəns/ (US) /ɪˈmɜːdʒəns/ (UK)| n. |出现，浮现，露出|The process of coming into existence or becoming known or apparent.|The emergence of new technologies has changed our lives. 新技术的出现改变了我们的生活。|近义词：appearance, arrival, rise, development|反义词：disappearance, vanishing, decline, submergence|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *emergentia, from Latin emergere "to rise out, come forth, bring forth," from ex "out" (see ex-) + mergere "to dip, plunge, immerse."
