moralism:/ˈmɔːrəlɪzəm/ /ˈmɔːrələzəm/| n. |道德主义，说教|The practice of moralizing, especially showing a tendency to make judgments about others' morality.|His speech was filled with moralism and condemnation of modern society. 他的演讲充满了道德主义和对现代社会的谴责。|近义词：preaching, didacticism, moralizing|反义词：immorality, amorality|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *moralisme*, from *moral* (moral) + *-isme* (-ism). Ultimately from Latin *moralis* (moral).
