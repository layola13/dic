constraining:/kənˈstreɪnɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
限制；约束；强迫
limiting or restricting something
High taxes are constraining economic growth. 高额税收正在限制经济增长。
Synonyms: restricting, limiting, inhibiting, confining, controlling
Antonyms: freeing, liberating, releasing, encouraging, promoting
词性: 动词现在分词
