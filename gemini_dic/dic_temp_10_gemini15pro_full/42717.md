countervair:/ˌkaʊntərˈvɛər/ | n. | 反面, 对立面; (纹章学的)逆向毛皮图案 | Something that is the opposite or contrary of something else, especially a contrasting argument or idea. In heraldry, it refers to a fur pattern in which the lower half of each of a series of bell shapes is of a different tincture from the upper. | The lawyer presented a strong countervair to the prosecution's case. 律师提出了一个强有力的反驳，以对抗控方的案件。 | The shield was emblazoned with countervair. 盾牌上饰有逆向毛皮图案。 | 近义词: contradiction, reversal, opposite | 反义词: agreement, conformity, similarity | 词性: 名词

词源:
From Old French *contrevair (unattested), from contre (“against”) + vair (“a type of fur”). Ultimately from Latin contra (“against”) and varius (“variegated, diverse”).
