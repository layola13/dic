madrigals:/ˈmædrɪɡəlz/
| n. |短歌; 牧歌; 合唱曲|A part-song for several voices, especially one of the Renaissance period, typically unaccompanied and arranged in elaborate counterpoint.|We sang madrigals in the church choir. 我们在教堂唱诗班唱了牧歌。|近义词：part-song, glee, chanson|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "monologue" in a performance context) |词性:noun
