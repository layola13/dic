mangiest:/ˈmeɪndʒɪɪst/| adj.|最肮脏的，最破烂的|Superlative adjective of mangy: most mangy.  Having the most severe case of mange; most shabby or run-down.|The mangiest dog I've ever seen was scavenging for food in the alley.|Synonyms:  shabbiest, seediest, rattiest, most dilapidated|Antonyms:  cleanest, tidiest, pristine, immaculate|adjective
