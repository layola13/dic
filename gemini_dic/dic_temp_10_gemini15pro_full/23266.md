bowwow:/ˈbaʊˌwaʊ/ (US), /ˈbaʊˌwaʊ/ (UK)| n. |狗叫声，狂吠声；(非正式)狗|The sound a dog makes, a bark; (informally) a dog.|The puppy's bowwow woke me up.| 近义词: bark, woof, yap | 反义词: whimper, whine| noun


bowwow:/ˈbaʊˌwaʊ/ (US), /ˈbaʊˌwaʊ/ (UK)| vi. |(狗)吠，叫|To bark (of a dog).|The dog bowwowed at the mailman.| 近义词: bark, woof, yap | 反义词: whimper, whine| verb (intransitive)


ETYMOLOGY->
From the imitative word "bow-wow," representing the sound of a dog barking.  This type of word formation is called onomatopoeia.
