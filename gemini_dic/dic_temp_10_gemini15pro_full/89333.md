homogenous:/ˌhoʊməˈdʒiːniəs/ /ˌhɒməˈdʒiːniəs/ | adj. |同质的，同类的，均匀的 | Of the same kind or nature; consisting of similar parts or elements. | The population of the village is quite homogenous, mainly farmers and their families. 这个村庄的人口构成相当同质，主要是农民和他们的家庭。|  The mixture needs to be thoroughly blended until it becomes homogenous. 这种混合物需要充分搅拌，直到它变得均匀。 |  近义词: uniform, similar, consistent, homogeneous | 反义词: heterogeneous, diverse, varied | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Ancient Greek ὁμογενής (homogenḗs), from ὁμός (homós, “same”) + γένος (génos, “kind, race, stock”).
