loamless:/ˈloʊmlɪs/
adjective
无沃土的；缺乏肥沃土壤的
lacking loam; having no loam
The loamless soil made it difficult to grow anything.  这片没有沃土的土地使得种植任何东西都很困难。
The farmer struggled with the loamless terrain.  农民在这片贫瘠的土地上艰难耕作。
Synonyms: barren, infertile, sterile, unproductive
Antonyms: fertile, rich, loamy
