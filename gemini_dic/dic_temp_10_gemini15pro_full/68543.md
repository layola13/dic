fandoms:/ˈfændəmz/ | n. | 粉丝圈；粉丝群体 | A group of fans of a particular person, team, fictional series, etc., especially one formed on the internet.  |  例句：She's active in several online fandoms.  她活跃在几个网络粉丝圈。

例句：The fandoms for this TV show are incredibly passionate. 这部电视剧的粉丝群体非常热情。| 近义词：fanbase, following, supporters | 反义词： (No direct antonym, perhaps "detractors" in a specific context) | 词性：noun
