displume:/dɪˈpluːm/
verb
剥夺（羽毛）；使失去荣耀；贬低
deprive of feathers; deprive of something that adorns or dignifies; degrade
The knight was displumed of his honors after he was found guilty of treason.  这位骑士被发现犯有叛国罪后，被剥夺了所有荣誉。
The scandal displumed the politician of his reputation.  这桩丑闻损害了这位政治家的名誉。
synonyms:  degrade, humiliate, shame, dishonor, strip, rob
antonyms:  honor, exalt, praise, elevate
