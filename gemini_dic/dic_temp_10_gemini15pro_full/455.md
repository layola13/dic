ablet:/ˈæblət/| n.|药片；小块；碑，牌|A small, flat, and usually round piece of a solid substance, especially a pill or tablet of medicine. A small, flat piece of something, typically one that is inscribed.|The doctor prescribed me some ablets for the pain. 医生给我开了一些药片来止痛。

He swallowed two ablets with water. 他用水吞服了两片药。|近义词：pill, tablet, capsule|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "liquid medicine", "injection")|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ablete, diminutive of able, meaning "board, plank," from Latin abolla, a kind of cloak. The sense shifted from a small, flat piece of wood to a small, flat piece of any material, eventually including medicine.
