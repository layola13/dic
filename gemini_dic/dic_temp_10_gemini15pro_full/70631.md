filter:/ˈfɪltər/ (US), /ˈfɪltə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|过滤器，过滤，筛选|A porous device for removing impurities or solid particles from a liquid or gas.|The coffee filter keeps the grounds out of the brewed coffee. 咖啡过滤器将咖啡渣与煮好的咖啡隔开。|近义词：sieve, strainer, screen|反义词:pollute, contaminate|noun

filter:/ˈfɪltər/ (US), /ˈfɪltə(r)/ (UK)| v.|过滤，筛选|To pass (a liquid, gas, light, or sound) through a device to remove unwanted material.|She filtered the gravy to remove the lumps. 她过滤了肉汁以去除块状物。|近义词：purify, refine, strain|反义词:pollute, contaminate|verb
