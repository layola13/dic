napus:/ˈneɪpəs/| n.|油菜；芸苔属|A genus of herbaceous plants in the mustard family (Brassicaceae), including rapeseed and rutabagas.  It is known for its oil-rich seeds and some varieties are cultivated as vegetables.|Farmers cultivate napus for its oil-rich seeds. 农民种植油菜是为了获取富含油脂的种子。|synonyms: Brassica napus (scientific name), rapeseed (for oilseed varieties), rutabaga (for certain root vegetable varieties)|antonyms: N/A|noun
