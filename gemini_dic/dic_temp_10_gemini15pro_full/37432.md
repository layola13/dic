commingler: /kəˈmɪŋɡlər/
noun
混合者；合并者
A person or thing that commingles.  Someone or something that blends or mixes things together.
The commingler combined the different investments into a single fund.  这位混合者将不同的投资合并成一个单一基金。
mixer, blender, combiner
separator, divider
n.

commingling: /kəˈmɪŋɡlɪŋ/
noun
混合；合并
The act of commingling. The act of blending or mixing things together.
The commingling of funds can lead to accounting difficulties. 资金的混合会导致会计困难。
mixing, blending, combining
separation, division
n.

commingle: /kəˈmɪŋɡl/
verb
混合；合并
To mix or blend together; to combine.
The company commingled customer funds with its own operating capital.  该公司将客户资金与其自有运营资本混合在一起。
mix, blend, combine, merge
separate, divide, sort
v.
