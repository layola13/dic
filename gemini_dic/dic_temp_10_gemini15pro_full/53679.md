disciplinarian:/ˌdɪsəplɪˈnɛəriən/ | n. | 纪律严明的人；执行纪律者 | A person who believes in or enforces strict discipline. |  My high school principal was a strict disciplinarian.  我的高中校长是一个严格的纪律执行者。

The coach was known as a tough disciplinarian, demanding punctuality and hard work from his players.  这位教练以严格的纪律著称，要求他的球员准时和努力工作。

| 近义词：martinet, stickler, authoritarian | 反义词：pushover, lenient person | 词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *disciplinaire*, from Latin *disciplina* (instruction, knowledge, discipline) + *-arius* (forming adjectives and nouns denoting connection).
