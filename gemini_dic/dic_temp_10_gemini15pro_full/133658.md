nonfortifiable:/ˌnɒnfɔːrtɪˈfaɪəbl/ | adj. | 不可设防的，不易防守的 | Not capable of being fortified or strengthened against attack. | The town's position on the flat plains made it nonfortifiable.  这个城镇位于平坦的平原上，使其不易防守。|  The coastal village, with its exposed shoreline, proved nonfortifiable against pirate raids.  这个沿海村庄，由于其暴露的海岸线，被证明无法抵御海盗的袭击。 | 近义词: indefensible, vulnerable | 反义词: fortifiable, defensible | 词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"
fortify (verb): from Old French *fortifier, from Late Latin fortificare "to strengthen," from Latin fortis "strong" + facere "to make."
-able (suffix): meaning "capable of being"
