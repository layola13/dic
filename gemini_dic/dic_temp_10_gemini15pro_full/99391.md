interdictory:/ˌɪntərˈdɪktəri/
adjective
禁止的，禁令的
Having the nature or effect of an interdict; prohibitory.
The judge issued an interdictory order preventing the company from selling the product. 法官发布了一项禁令，禁止该公司销售该产品。
interdictory injunction 禁止令
近义词:prohibitive, forbidding, proscriptive
反义词:permissive, allowing
词性:adjective

interdict:/ˈɪntərdɪkt/
noun
禁令，禁止
An authoritative prohibition; a formal or authoritative decree of prohibition.
The church placed an interdict on the king. 教会对国王施加了禁令。
近义词:prohibition, ban, embargo
反义词:permission, allowance
词性:noun

interdict:/ˌɪntərˈdɪkt/
verb
禁止，阻止
To prohibit or forbid by decree.
The government interdicted the sale of the drug. 政府禁止销售这种药物。
近义词:prohibit, forbid, ban
反义词:permit, allow
词性:verb
