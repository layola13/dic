abseiling: /ˈæbseɪlɪŋ/ /əˈbseɪlɪŋ/
noun
绳降; 绕绳下降
The act of descending a rock face or other near-vertical surface by using a doubled rope coiled around the body and fixed at a higher point.
We went abseiling down the cliff face. 我们沿着悬崖面进行绳降。

They learned abseiling and rock climbing. 他们学习了绳降和攀岩。

近义词: rappelling (主要用于美式英语)
反义词: ascending, climbing
词性: noun

abseil: /ˈæbseɪl/ /əˈbseɪl/
verb
绳降; 绕绳下降
Descend a rock face or other near-vertical surface by using a doubled rope coiled around the body and fixed at a higher point.
She abseiled down the wall. 她从墙上绳降下来。

The climbers abseiled into the canyon. 登山者们用绳索下降到峡谷中。

近义词: rappel (主要用于美式英语)
反义词: ascend, climb
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From German  *abseilen*, from *ab* (“down, off”) + *Seil* (“rope”).
