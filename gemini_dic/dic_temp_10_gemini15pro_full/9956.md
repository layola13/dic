apheliotropism:/ˌæfiːliˈɒtrəpɪzəm/| n. |背日性，负向光性|The tendency of a plant to turn away from the sun.  The movement of a plant away from light.|例句：The apheliotropism of the plant caused it to grow away from the window. 这种植物的负向光性导致它远离窗户生长。

例句：Researchers are studying the apheliotropism of certain desert plants. 研究人员正在研究某些沙漠植物的负向光性。|近义词：negative phototropism|反义词：heliotropism, phototropism|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀπό (apó, “away from”) + ἥλιος (hḗlios, “sun”) + τροπή (tropḗ, “a turning”) + -ism.
