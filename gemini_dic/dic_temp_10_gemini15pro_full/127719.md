negligee:/ˈnɛɡlɪʒeɪ/ /ˈnɛɡləˌʒeɪ/
|n.|女士睡衣；轻薄的晨衣；|A woman's light, loose dressing gown or robe, typically made of sheer or delicate fabric, worn at home.|She slipped into a silk negligee. 她穿上一件丝绸睡衣。|近义词：nightgown, robe, peignoir|反义词:daywear, outerwear|词性:名词
词源:From French négligé, past participle of négliger "to neglect," from Latin neglegere "disregard, neglect."  Originally referring to a carelessly or informally worn garment.
