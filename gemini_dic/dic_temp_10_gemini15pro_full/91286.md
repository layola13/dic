hydrokinetic:/ˌhaɪdrəʊkɪˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. |水动力的，水力学的|Relating to the energy of moving water, or the conversion of this energy into power. |  Hydrokinetic energy offers a clean and sustainable energy source. 水动能提供了一种清洁和可持续的能源。|  The company specializes in hydrokinetic turbines for tidal power generation. 该公司专门生产用于潮汐发电的水动涡轮机。|  近义词:hydropower, waterpower | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
hydro- (from Greek ὕδωρ (hýdōr) meaning "water") + kinetic (from Greek κίνησις (kínēsis) meaning "motion")
