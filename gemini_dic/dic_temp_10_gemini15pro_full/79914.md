gneissose:/ˈnaɪsˌoʊs/| adj.|片麻岩状的，似片麻岩的|Resembling gneiss; having the structure or appearance of gneiss.  The rock sample exhibited a gneissose texture, with alternating bands of light and dark minerals.|The outcrop displayed a distinctly gneissose structure.|近义词：gneissic, foliated|反义词:massive, non-foliated|词性:形容词
gneiss:/naɪs/| n.|片麻岩|A metamorphic rock with a banded or foliated structure, typically consisting of alternating layers of light and dark minerals.|The geologist identified the sample as a biotite gneiss.|近义词：schist|反义词:N/A|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From German Gneis, of unknown origin.  The adjectival form, gneissose, is derived by adding the suffix -ose, which denotes "full of" or "resembling."
