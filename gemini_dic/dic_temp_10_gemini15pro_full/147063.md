overlegislating: /ˌoʊvərˈlɛdʒɪsleɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度立法
Creating an excessive amount of laws, often to the point of being burdensome or counterproductive.
The government is overlegislating on minor issues, stifling innovation and economic growth. 政府在一些小问题上过度立法，扼杀了创新和经济增长。
Overlegislating can lead to confusion and make it difficult for businesses to operate. 过度立法会导致混乱，并使企业难以经营。
Synonyms: overregulating
Antonyms: deregulating
