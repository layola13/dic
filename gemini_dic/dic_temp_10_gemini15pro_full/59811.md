ecstatically:/ɪkˈstætɪkli/
adverb
欣喜若狂地；狂喜地
In an overwhelmingly joyful manner; with intense delight.
She danced ecstatically when she heard the good news. 她听到这个好消息后欣喜若狂地跳了起来。
The crowd cheered ecstatically as their team won the championship. 当他们的球队赢得冠军时，人群欣喜若狂地欢呼。
近义词:joyfully, rapturously, blissfully, euphorically
反义词:sorrowfully, dejectedly, miserably, apathetically
词性:adverb
词源:From the Greek ekstasis, meaning "standing outside oneself" or "trance," via Late Latin ecstaticus and French extatique.
