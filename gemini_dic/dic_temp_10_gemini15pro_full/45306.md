cumber:/ˈkʌmbər/| v. |阻碍，拖累；妨碍|To hamper or hinder; to burden or weigh down.|The heavy backpack cumbered his progress up the mountain.  沉重的背包阻碍了他上山的进度。|Synonyms: encumber, hinder, obstruct, impede|Antonyms: assist, aid, help|verb

cumbersome:/ˈkʌmbərsəm/| adj. |笨重的；累赘的；难处理的|Large or heavy and therefore difficult to carry or use; unwieldy.|The old furniture was cumbersome and difficult to move.  旧家具笨重，难以搬动。|Synonyms: awkward, unwieldy, clumsy|Antonyms: convenient, manageable, handy|adjective
