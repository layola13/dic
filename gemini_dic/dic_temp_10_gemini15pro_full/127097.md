nationalism:/ˈnæʃənəlɪzəm/| n.|民族主义；国家主义|  A political ideology that involves a strong identification of a group of individuals with a nation and a belief in its promotion above all other nations.  It often emphasizes a nation's shared culture, history, and language, and can sometimes manifest as feelings of superiority or hostility towards other nations.|  The rise of nationalism in the 19th century led to many conflicts. 19世纪民族主义的兴起导致了许多冲突。

 The government's policies fueled nationalism among the people. 政府的政策激起了人民的民族主义情绪。|近义词：patriotism, jingoism|反义词：internationalism, globalism|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *nationalisme*, from *national* (national) + *-isme* (-ism). Ultimately from Latin *natio* (nation, race, kind, species).
