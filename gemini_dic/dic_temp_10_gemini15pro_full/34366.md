classicism:/ˈklæsɪsɪzəm/| n.|古典主义|A style in literature, art, music, architecture, etc., characterized by adherence to the principles of ancient Greek and Roman art, emphasizing order, balance, and simplicity.|The museum's collection showcases the beauty and elegance of classicism.|近义词：formalism, traditionalism|反义词: romanticism, modernism|名词
