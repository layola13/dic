dematerialize:/diːməˈtɪəriəlaɪz/
verb
非物质化，消失，使消失
To cease to exist in material form; disappear.  To convert assets into cash.
The magician made the rabbit dematerialize. 魔术师使兔子消失了。
The company decided to dematerialize its share certificates. 公司决定将其股票证书非物质化。
synonyms: disappear, vanish, evaporate, dissolve
antonyms: materialize, appear, emerge
词性: 动词
