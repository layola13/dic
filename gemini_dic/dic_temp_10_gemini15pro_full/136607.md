nonsanity:/nɒnˈsænɪti/
n.
非疯狂；理性；神志清醒
The state of being sane; sanity.  A relatively recent coinage, formed by analogy with words like "nonviolence."
His nonsanity was evident in his calm and collected demeanor during the crisis.  在危机期间，他冷静沉着的举止表明他神志清醒。
近义词: sanity, lucidity, rationality
反义词: insanity, madness, lunacy
词性: 名词

词源:  Formed by prefixing "non-" (meaning "not") to "sanity." "Sanity" ultimately comes from the Latin "sanus" meaning "healthy" or "sound."
