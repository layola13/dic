absorbs:/əbˈzɔːrbz/ /əbˈsɔːrbz/
verb
吸收;理解;使全神贯注
takes in or soaks up (liquids or energy, etc.); occupies the attention of
The sponge absorbs water. 海绵吸水。
The black cloth absorbs light.  这块黑布吸收光线。

He was completely absorbed in his work. 他完全沉浸在他的工作中。
She was so absorbed in her book that she didn't hear me come in. 她太专注于她的书，以至于没听到我进来。

近义词: soak up, take in, imbibe, assimilate, engross
反义词: emit, excrete, release, reflect
词性: verb


USAGE->

A sponge absorbs water quickly. = 海绵吸收水很快。

现在完成时:

He has been absorbed in that video game for hours. = 他已经沉迷于那个电子游戏好几个小时了。

过去完成时:

She had been absorbed in her studies all afternoon. = 她整个下午都专心学习。

将来时:

被动语态:

否定句:
