co-worker:/ˈkoʊ wɜːrkər/ (US) /ˈkəʊ wɜːkə(r)/ (UK)| n.|同事|A person who works with another person, especially in the same department or office.|例句：I get along well with all my co-workers. 我和我的所有同事都相处得很好。

例句：My co-worker helped me finish the project on time. 我的同事帮助我按时完成了项目。

近义词：colleague, associate, peer|反义词:boss, supervisor, manager|词性:noun
