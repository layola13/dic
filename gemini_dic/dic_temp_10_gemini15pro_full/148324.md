oversoul: /ˈoʊvərˌsoʊl/ | n. | 超灵; 超越的灵魂 |  A spiritual entity or consciousness of high development, thought to encompass individual souls.  It represents a higher self or a universal consciousness.  This concept was popularized by Ralph Waldo Emerson. | Emerson believed that each person's soul is part of a larger Oversoul.  爱默生相信每个人的灵魂都是一个更大的超灵的一部分。 |  The artist felt connected to the oversoul, finding inspiration in nature's beauty.  这位艺术家感觉与超灵相连，从大自然的美丽中找到了灵感。 | synonyms: universal consciousness, higher self, cosmic consciousness, world soul | antonyms: individual soul, ego | noun
