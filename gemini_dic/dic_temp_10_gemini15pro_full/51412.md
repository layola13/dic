deterrence:/dɪˈtɜːrəns/| n.|威慑，制止|The country relies on nuclear deterrence to prevent attack. 这个国家依靠核威慑来阻止袭击。|Example: The presence of police officers acted as a deterrence to crime. 警察的存在对犯罪起到了威慑作用。

Example: The harsh penalties for drunk driving serve as a deterrence.  对酒后驾车的严厉处罚起到了威慑作用。

|Synonyms: dissuasion, prevention, intimidation|Antonyms: encouragement, incitement, inducement|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēterrentia, from Latin dēterreō (“I frighten off, deter”), from dē (“away from”) + terreō (“I frighten”).
