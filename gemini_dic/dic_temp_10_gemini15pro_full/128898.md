nimbostratus:/ˌnɪmboʊˈstrɑːtəs/| n. |雨层云|A dark gray cloud, often continuous, that is thick enough to block out the sun and produce steady rain or snow.|例句：The nimbostratus clouds brought days of steady rain. 雨层云带来了连日的持续降雨。

例句：The sky was covered in a thick blanket of nimbostratus, making the day feel gloomy. 天空被厚厚的雨层云覆盖，使白天感觉阴沉。|近义词：rain cloud|反义词: (No direct antonym for a cloud type, but perhaps) clear sky|词性:noun
