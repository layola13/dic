ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *futile, from Old French futile (“worthless, futile”) and directly from Latin fūtilis (“leaky, easily poured out, hence, failing to hold, untrustworthy, failing to achieve the intended result”), from fūtis, (“water-skin”).
