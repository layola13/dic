disarmament:/dɪsˈɑːməmənt/
n.
裁军，解除武装
The act of reducing, limiting, or abolishing weapons.
The United Nations called for complete nuclear disarmament. 联合国呼吁彻底核裁军。
synonyms: demilitarization, demobilization
antonyms: armament, militarization
noun
