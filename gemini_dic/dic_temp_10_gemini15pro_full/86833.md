hematosis:/ˌhiːməˈtoʊsɪs/ /ˌheɪməˈtoʊsɪs/
noun
血液氧化；气体交换；血的形成
The process of oxygenation of the blood in the lungs or gills.  The process by which venous blood is converted into arterial blood. The formation of blood.
The doctor explained that hematosis is essential for maintaining life. 医生解释说，血液氧化作用对维持生命至关重要。
oxygenation, aeration
deoxygenation
