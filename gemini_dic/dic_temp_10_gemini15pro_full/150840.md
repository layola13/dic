paragnathus:/ˈpærəɡˈnæθəs/| n. |副颚，侧颚|A small, hard, jaw-like structure found in some invertebrates, especially certain worms and mollusks, often used for feeding or grasping.|Example: The paragnathus of the polychaete worm helps it to grasp and manipulate food particles. 多毛环节动物的副颚帮助它抓住和操纵食物颗粒。|Example: Researchers studied the morphology of the paragnathus to understand its role in feeding. 研究人员研究了副颚的形态，以了解它在摄食中的作用。|近义词: mandible, jaw|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
