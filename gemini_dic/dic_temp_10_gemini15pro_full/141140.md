onyx:/ˈɑːnɪks/ | n. |玛瑙，缟玛瑙 |A variety of chalcedony, a cryptocrystalline form of quartz, that is banded in shades of black and white or black and brown.|The ring featured a large, polished onyx. 这枚戒指上有一颗很大的抛光玛瑙。|Synonyms: chalcedony, agate|Antonyms: N/A|noun
