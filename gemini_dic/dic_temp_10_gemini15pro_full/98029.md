inordination:/ˌɪnɔːrdɪˈneɪʃn/
n.|过度；无节制；紊乱|Excessive or uncontrolled behavior or expression; lack of moderation; disorder.|His inordination at the buffet was embarrassing. 他在自助餐上的无节制令人尴尬。|近义词:excess, immoderation, intemperance, unrestraint|反义词:moderation, restraint, temperance|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inordinationem*, accusative singular of *inordinatio*, from Latin *inordinatus* "disordered, irregular, immoderate," from *in-* "not" + *ordinatus* "ordered, arranged" (see ordinate).
