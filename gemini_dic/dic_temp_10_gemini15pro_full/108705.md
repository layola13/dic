lean-to:/ˈliːn tuː/ (US), /ˈliːn tuː/ (UK)| n.|棚屋，披屋；单坡小屋|A simple shelter with a sloping roof, typically built against a wall or other support.|The hikers built a lean-to for the night to protect themselves from the storm.  徒步旅行者搭建了一个披屋以便在夜间躲避暴风雨。|Synonyms: shed, hut, shack|Antonyms: mansion, palace|noun

lean-to:/ˈliːn tuː/ (US), /ˈliːn tuː/ (UK)| adj.|单坡的；披屋式的|Having a single sloping roof.|They constructed a lean-to roof against the side of the barn. 他们在谷仓的侧面搭建了一个单坡屋顶。|Synonyms: sloping, slanted|Antonyms: level, flat|adjective
