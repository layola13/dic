blessers:/ˈblesərz/
n.
包养者，干爹 (通常指男性)
A person who provides financial support or gifts to a younger person, usually in exchange for companionship or sexual favors.  This term is often used with negative connotations.
She was seen with her blesser at an expensive restaurant. 她被看到和她的包养者在一家昂贵的餐厅吃饭。
The blesser showered her with expensive gifts. 包养者送给她很多昂贵的礼物。

近义词:sugar daddy, benefactor, patron
反义词: (No direct opposite, possibly dependent)
