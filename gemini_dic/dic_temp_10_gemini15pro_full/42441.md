counterdemonstrating: /ˌkaʊntərˈdɛmənˌstreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 反示威，抗议 | Engaging in a demonstration against another demonstration. |  Example 1:  The group was counterdemonstrating against the proposed cuts to education funding.  该团体正在举行反示威，抗议提议削减教育经费。

Example 2:  They were counterdemonstrating the far-right rally. 他们在反示威极右集会。 | Synonyms: protesting against, opposing, countering | Antonyms: supporting, agreeing with | verb

counterdemonstration: /ˌkaʊntərˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃən/ | n. | 反示威，抗议 | A demonstration in opposition to another demonstration. | Example 1: The counterdemonstration drew a larger crowd than the original protest. 反示威吸引了比最初抗议更多的人群。

Example 2:  Police were present to maintain order between the demonstration and the counterdemonstration.  警察在场维持示威和反示威的秩序。 | Synonyms: protest, opposition, counter-protest | Antonyms: support, agreement | noun
