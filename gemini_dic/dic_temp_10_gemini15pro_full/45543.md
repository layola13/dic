curious: /ˈkjʊəriəs/ (US) /ˈkjʊərɪəs/ (UK) | adj. | 好奇的；古怪的 | Eager to know or learn something; strange; unusual. | She was curious about the contents of the box. 她对盒子里的东西很好奇。| The house had a curious smell. 这房子有一股奇怪的味道。 | inquisitive, nosy, inquiring | indifferent, apathetic | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "curiosus" meaning "careful, diligent, inquisitive," derived from "cura" meaning "care, concern."
