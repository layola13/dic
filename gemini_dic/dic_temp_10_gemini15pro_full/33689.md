cinnoline:/ˈsɪnəlɪn/ | n. |噌啉;1,2-二氮萘|A crystalline heterocyclic compound, C8H6N2, derived from naphthalene by replacement of two adjacent CH groups by N atoms. Used in making dyes.|Cinnoline derivatives have shown promising activity against certain cancers.|近义词:benzopyridazine (化学名称上的近义词, 指的是同一种物质)|反义词:无|词性:noun
