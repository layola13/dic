coproducers:/ˌkoʊprəˈdusərz/| n. |共同制片人；联合制片人|People or companies who produce something together, such as a film, play, or album.|Example 1: The two coproducers worked tirelessly to bring the film to fruition.  两位联合制片人不知疲倦地工作，使这部电影得以完成。

Example 2: The Broadway show had five coproducers, each responsible for different aspects of the production.  这部百老汇演出有五位联合制片人，每位负责制作的不同方面。|近义词：joint producers, collaborating producers|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "sole producer")|词性:名词
