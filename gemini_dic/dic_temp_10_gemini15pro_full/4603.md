alcubierre:/ˌælkʊˈbjɛəri/ | n. | 阿库别瑞引擎; 阿库别瑞驱动 | A theoretical concept for faster-than-light travel proposed by Mexican physicist Miguel Alcubierre, involving the warping of spacetime.  It is a solution of Einstein's field equations in general relativity. |  The Alcubierre drive is a fascinating concept, but remains highly theoretical. 阿库别瑞引擎是一个引人入胜的概念，但仍然停留在理论层面。 | Synonym: warp drive | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "sublight propulsion") | noun
