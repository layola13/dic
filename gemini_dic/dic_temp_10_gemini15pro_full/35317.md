cnemial: /ˈniːmiəl/ | adj. | 胫骨的，小腿骨的 | Relating to the tibia, the larger of the two bones in the lower leg, also known as the shinbone. | The cnemial crest is a prominent bony ridge on the anterior surface of the tibia. 胫骨嵴是胫骨前表面一个突出的骨嵴。| The cnemial nerve supplies sensation to the skin of the lower leg. 胫神经为小腿皮肤提供感觉。 | tibial, crural |  (No common antonym) | adjective
