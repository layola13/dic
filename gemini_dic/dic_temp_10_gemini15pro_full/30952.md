chaise-d'ors:/ʃɛz ˈdɔːr/ |n.|金色椅子；镀金座椅|A chair ornamented with gold. |The king sat upon his chaise-d'ors.| 近义词: golden chair, throne | 反义词: plain chair | noun

Etymology->
From French, literally "chair of gold."  "Chaise" comes from Latin "cathedra," meaning "seat" or "chair." "D'ors" is a contraction of "de or," meaning "of gold."
