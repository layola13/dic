bulimia nervosa:/ˌbuːˈlɪmiə nɜːrˈvoʊsə/ (US), /ˌbjuːˈlɪmiə nɜːˈvəʊsə/ (UK)| n. |神经性贪食症|An eating disorder characterized by episodes of binge eating followed by purging (self-induced vomiting, misuse of laxatives, diuretics, or other medications, fasting, or excessive exercise) to prevent weight gain.  It is often accompanied by a distorted body image and an intense fear of gaining weight.|She struggled with bulimia nervosa for years before seeking help. 她与神经性贪食症斗争了多年才寻求帮助。|Synonym: bulimia|Antonym: anorexia nervosa|Noun

binge eating:/ˈbɪndʒ ˌiːtɪŋ/| n. |暴饮暴食|The consumption of a large amount of food in a short period of time, usually accompanied by a feeling of loss of control.|Binge eating is a serious problem that can lead to health complications. 暴饮暴食是一个严重的问题，会导致健康并发症。|Synonym: overeating, gorging|Antonym: dieting, fasting|Noun

purge:/pɜːrdʒ/ (US), /pɜːdʒ/ (UK)| v. |清除，净化|To rid of something unwanted or undesirable.|She purged her closet of old clothes. 她清除了衣柜里的旧衣服。|Synonym: cleanse, purify|Antonym: contaminate, pollute|Verb
