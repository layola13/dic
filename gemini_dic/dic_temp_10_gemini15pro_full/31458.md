charlatanistic:/ˌʃɑːrlətəˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. |江湖骗子的；装腔作势的；招摇撞骗的|Relating to or characteristic of a charlatan; fraudulently claiming to have more knowledge or skill than one possesses. | He gave a charlatanistic performance, pretending to be a world-renowned expert. 他装腔作势地表演，假装自己是世界知名的专家。|  The charlatanistic salesman convinced the elderly woman to buy the worthless product.  这个骗子推销员说服了老妇人购买毫无价值的产品。 | 近义词: fraudulent, deceptive, phony, sham, bogus | 反义词: genuine, authentic, sincere, honest | 词性: adjective

charlatan:/ˈʃɑːrlətən/ | n. |江湖骗子；吹牛大王；庸医|A person falsely claiming to have a special knowledge or skill; a fraud. | The charlatan was eventually exposed and arrested.  这个骗子最终被揭穿并逮捕。| He promised a miracle cure, but he was just a charlatan. 他承诺能奇迹般地治愈，但他只是一个江湖骗子。| 近义词: fraud, imposter, quack, fake | 反义词: expert, professional, authority | 词性: noun
