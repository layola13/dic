noninquiring:/ˌnɒnɪnˈkwaɪərɪŋ/
adjective
不打听的，不好奇的
Not showing or expressing curiosity; not asking questions.
He had a noninquiring nature, rarely asking personal questions. 他生性不爱打听，很少问私人问题。

She gave him a noninquiring look. 她给了他一个不带疑问的眼神。


近义词: incurious, uninterested, indifferent, apathetic
反义词: inquisitive, curious, inquiring, interested


词性: adjective
词源:  From non- + inquiring, present participle of inquire.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not."  "Inquire" comes from Old French "enquerre," ultimately from Latin "inquirere" meaning "to seek into, investigate."
