opisthobranchia:/ˌɒpɪsθəˈbræŋkiə/
n.
后鳃亚纲；后鳃类
A subclass of Gastropoda (snails and slugs) in which the gills, when present, are located behind the heart.  Many species have lost their shells entirely. They are mostly marine.
Opisthobranchia are a group of marine gastropods. 后鳃类是一群海洋腹足动物。
Many opisthobranchs are brightly colored and toxic. 许多后鳃类动物颜色鲜艳且有毒。
近义词:gastropods, sea slugs
反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a taxonomic classification)
词性:noun
