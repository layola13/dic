disceptation:/ˌdɪsɛpˈteɪʃən/
n.
争论，辩论
A formal or serious discussion or debate on a particular subject.
The two scholars engaged in a learned disceptation on the merits of different philosophical schools. 两位学者就不同哲学流派的优劣进行了学术性的辩论。
discourse, debate, argument, contention, controversy
agreement, harmony, concord, unanimity
noun
