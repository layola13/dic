churlishness:/ˈtʃɜːrlɪʃnəs/| n.|粗鲁，无礼，乖戾|The waiter's churlishness ruined our dining experience. 服务员的粗鲁毁掉了我们的用餐体验。|Example:  His churlishness made it difficult for him to make friends. 他的粗鲁让他很难交到朋友。|Synonyms: rudeness, impoliteness, surliness, boorishness, incivility|Antonyms: politeness, courtesy, civility, graciousness|noun

Etymology->
From Middle English *churlischnes*, equivalent to churlish +‎ -ness.  "Churlish" itself comes from Old English *ceorlisc* "belonging to a churl," with "churl" originally meaning a freeman of low rank, later developing the sense of "rude, ill-bred person."
