nepotists:/ˈnɛpətɪsts/| n. | 裙带关系者；任人唯亲者 | A person who favors relatives or friends, especially by giving them jobs. |  The company was accused of being run by nepotists, as all the top positions were held by family members. 这家公司被指责由裙带关系者经营，因为所有高层职位都由家族成员担任。

He refused to hire his nephew, wanting to avoid any accusations of nepotism. 他拒绝雇佣他的侄子，想避免任何任人唯亲的指责。 | 近义词：cronies, favorites | 反义词:meritocrats | 词性: noun
