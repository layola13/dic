classicalisms:/ˈklæsɪsɪzəmz/
|n.|古典主义；古典风格；古典习语|A style or expression characterized by the use of traditional or classical forms or principles.  The use of traditional literary or artistic styles and forms, especially those of ancient Greece or Rome.|His writing is full of classicalisms. 他的写作充满了古典主义色彩。|近义词：classicism, traditionalism|反义词: modernism|noun
