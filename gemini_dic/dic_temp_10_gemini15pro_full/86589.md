heliograph:/ˈhiːliəɡrɑːf/ | n. | 日光反射信号器，日光信号机 | An apparatus for signaling by flashes of sunlight reflected from a mirror. | Example 1: The army used a heliograph to communicate across the valley. 军队使用日光反射信号器跨越山谷进行通信。

Example 2:  The heliograph proved invaluable for long-distance communication in clear weather. 在晴朗的天气下，日光反射信号器被证明对长距离通信非常宝贵。 | 近义词:semaphore, signal lamp | 反义词: None | noun
