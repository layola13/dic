euganoidei:/ˌjuːɡæˈnɔɪdiː/
| n. |硬鳞鱼总目|A superorder of ray-finned fishes characterized by ganoid scales.  These fishes, while diverse in the Paleozoic Era, are now represented by seven living species.|The euganoidei include the bichirs, reedfishes, sturgeons, paddlefishes, and gars.|近义词：ganoid fishes|反义词: (No direct antonym. Could list a contrasting fish group like Teleostei - 真骨鱼)|noun
