headers:/ˈhɛdɚz/ /ˈhɛdəz/| n.|标题; 页眉; 头球|A line of text serving as a title, especially at the top of a page or section.  The top margin of a page, especially when used for headings, page numbers, etc. (In soccer) the act of hitting the ball with one's head.|The newspaper headers were in bold type.  报纸的标题是用粗体字写的。  She checked the page headers for the chapter number. 她检查了页眉的章节号。  The striker scored a magnificent header.  这名前锋用头球攻入了一个精彩的进球。|titles, headings, subheadings|footers, details|noun
