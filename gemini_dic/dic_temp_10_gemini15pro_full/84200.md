half-reluctant:/ˌhæf rɪˈlʌktənt/| adj. | 半心半意的，勉强，有点不情愿的 | Showing some unwillingness or hesitation. | She gave a half-reluctant smile. 她勉强地笑了笑。 |  He offered a half-reluctant apology. 他很不情愿地表示了歉意。 |  willing, eager, enthusiastic | unwilling, hesitant, resistant | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"half-" (Old English healf) + "reluctant" (from Latin reluctantem, present participle of reluctari "to struggle against," from re- "back" + luctari "to wrestle")
