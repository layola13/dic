bestraught:/bɪˈstrɔːt/| adj.|发狂的，心烦意乱的，精神错乱的|Extremely distressed and upset; agitated with doubt or mental conflict.|She was distraught after hearing the news of her husband's death. 她听到丈夫去世的消息后心烦意乱。|
The parents were distraught when their child went missing. 孩子失踪后，父母都疯了。|
Near Synonyms: frantic, distressed, agitated, overwrought, beside oneself, inconsolable|
Near Antonyms: calm, composed, serene, tranquil|
word property:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + distraught, past participle of distract.  Ultimately from Latin *distractus, past participle of distrahere “to pull apart, divide, distract,” from dis- “apart” + trahere “to draw, pull.”
