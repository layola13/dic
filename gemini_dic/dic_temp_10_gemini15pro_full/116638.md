matted:/ˈmætɪd/| v.| （使）缠结，（使）乱蓬蓬（mat 的过去式和过去分词）|To become or cause to become tangled and clumped together.| The rain matted her hair. 雨水把她头发弄乱了。|近义词：tangle, snarl|反义词:smooth, comb, groom|词性:动词
