cartographic:/ˌkɑːrtəˈɡræfɪk/| adj.|制图的，地图绘制的|Relating to the making of maps or charts.|例句：Cartographic skills are essential for geographers.  地理学家必须具备制图技能。

例句：The cartographic representation of the terrain was very detailed.  该地形的地图绘制非常详细。|近义词：mapping, mapmaking|反义词：(No direct antonym, possibly "non-cartographic")|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cartographique*, from *carte* "map" (from Latin *charta* "paper, map") + *-graphique* "related to writing or drawing" (from Greek *-graphikos*).
