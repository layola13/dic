fungi:/ˈfʌŋɡaɪ/ (ˈfʌndʒaɪ)/
n.|真菌|Any of a group of spore-producing organisms feeding on organic matter, including molds, yeast, mushrooms, and toadstools.|Mushrooms and toadstools are types of fungi.  蘑菇和毒菌都是真菌。|Synonyms:  mold, yeast, mushroom, toadstool|Antonyms: None|Noun


fungus:/ˈfʌŋɡəs/ (ˈfʌndʒəs)/
n.|真菌|A single organism of the fungi kind, such as a mushroom or a toadstool.|The tree was covered in a strange green fungus.  这棵树上覆盖着一种奇怪的绿色真菌。|Synonyms:  mold, yeast, mushroom, toadstool|Antonyms: None|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fungus* "mushroom, fungus," of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Greek *sphongos* "sponge," from the porous nature of some fungi.
