nighthawk:/ˈnaɪtˌhɔːk (US), ˈnaɪtˌhɔːk (UK)| n.|夜鹰|A nocturnal bird with long pointed wings, a short bill, and a large gape, usually found in urban areas. It is known for its distinctive, nasal "peent" call.|The nighthawk swooped down and caught an insect in mid-air. 夜鹰俯冲下来，在半空中抓住了一只昆虫。

nighthawk:/ˈnaɪtˌhɔːk (US), ˈnaɪtˌhɔːk (UK)| n.|夜猫子 (informal)|A person who is active or works late at night.|He's a real nighthawk; he rarely goes to bed before 2 am. 他是个真正的夜猫子，很少在凌晨2点前睡觉。

近义词:nightjar, goatsucker (for the bird); night owl (for the person)
反义词:early bird
词性:noun
