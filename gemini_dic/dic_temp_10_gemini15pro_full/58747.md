dungfork:/ˈdʌŋfɔːrk/| n.|粪叉，堆肥叉|A tool with a long handle and two or more prongs used for lifting and turning over dung or compost. |He used a dungfork to spread the manure on the field. 他用粪叉把肥料撒到田里。|Synonym: manure fork, pitchfork|Antonym: N/A|noun

dungfork:/ˈdʌŋfɔːrk/| v.|用粪叉叉起|To lift or move something with a dungfork.|She dungforked the hay into the barn. 她用粪叉把干草叉进谷仓。|Synonym: pitchfork|Antonym: N/A|verb
