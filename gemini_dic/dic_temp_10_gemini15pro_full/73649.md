forficate:/ˈfɔːrfɪkeɪt/| adj.|叉状的，分叉的|Divided or forked into two branches or parts.  Resembling a fork.|The forficate antennae of the insect helped it navigate. 这种昆虫叉状的触角帮助它导航。|近义词：forked, bifurcated, branched|反义词:straight, unbranched|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *forficatus*, past participle of *forficare* "to cut with shears or scissors," from *forfex*, *forficis* "shears, scissors."
