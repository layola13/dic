artifact's:/ˈɑːrtɪfækts/
noun
人工制品(的)
An object made by a human being, typically an item of cultural or historical interest.  The possessive form indicates belonging to an artifact.
The artifact's age was determined by carbon dating.  这件文物的年代是通过碳定年法测定的。
The museum displayed the artifact's intricate carvings. 博物馆展示了这件文物复杂的雕刻。
synonyms: relic, antiquity, object, item
antonyms: natural object
