impudentnesses:/ɪmˈpjuːd(ə)ntnəsɪz/
|n.|无礼，厚颜无耻|The plural form of impudence, referring to multiple instances of disrespectful or insolent behavior.  It describes a collection of acts characterized by a lack of respect and excessive boldness.|His impudentnesses finally led to his dismissal from the company. 他的屡次无礼行为最终导致他被公司解雇。|Synonyms: insolences, audacities, impertinences|Antonyms: politenesses, courtesies, respects|noun
