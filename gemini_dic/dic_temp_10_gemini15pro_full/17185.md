baubling:/ˈbɔːblɪŋ/ | n. | 小玩意儿；廉价的饰品 | A small, showy trinket of little value. | She was fascinated by the baublings at the market stall. 她被市场摊位上的小玩意儿迷住了。|  The child clutched a handful of brightly colored baublings.  孩子抓着一把颜色鲜艳的小玩意儿。 | trinket, knick-knack, gewgaw, trifle | valuable, precious | noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *baube, probably from Old French *baube ("toy, doll"), of unknown origin.
