numbskull:/ˈnʌmskʌl/ (US), /ˈnʌmskʌl/ (UK)| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A stupid person.|例句：He's such a numbskull, he locked his keys in the car again. 他真是个笨蛋，又把钥匙锁在车里了。

例句：Don't be such a numbskull! Think before you act. 别这么傻！三思而后行。

近义词：dunce, idiot, moron, blockhead, dolt|反义词:genius, mastermind|词性:noun
