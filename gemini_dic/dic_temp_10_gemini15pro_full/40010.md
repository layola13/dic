consulto:/kənˈsʌltoʊ/ (US), /kənˈsʌltəʊ/ (UK)| v.|咨询，商量，查阅|To seek advice or information from someone, especially an expert; to refer to a book or other source for information.|I consulted my doctor about the pain in my back. 我咨询了医生关于我背痛的问题。|synonyms: confer, discuss, refer|antonyms: ignore, disregard|verb

consulto:/kənˈsʌltoʊ/ (US), /kənˈsʌltəʊ/ (UK)| n.|顾问，咨询师 (less common usage)|A person who gives professional advice or services.|He works as a consulto for a large financial firm. 他在一家大型金融公司担任顾问。|synonyms: advisor, consultant|antonyms: N/A|noun

词源： From Latin consultare, frequentative of consulere "to consult, deliberate, take counsel," from com- "together" (see com-) + -sulere, related to salvus "safe" (see salute (v.)).


注意事项:  consult 后面可以直接跟名词作宾语，也可以跟 with + 名词/代词，表示“与...商量/咨询”。
