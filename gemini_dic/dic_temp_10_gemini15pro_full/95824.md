incorporation:/ɪnˌkɔːrpəˈreɪʃn/ /ɪnˌkɔːr.pəˈreɪ.ʃən/| n.|公司，法人，合并，吸收|The process of forming a legal corporation.  The inclusion of something as part of a whole.|The incorporation of the two companies took several months. 这两家公司的合并花了几个月的时间。|近义词：integration, inclusion, amalgamation, combination|反义词:separation, division|noun

incorporate:/ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪt/ /ɪnˈkɔːr.pə.reɪt/| vt.|包含，合并，组成公司|To combine (something) into something else, so that it forms a part of the whole. To form into a legal corporation.|The new design incorporates the latest technology. 新设计融合了最新的技术。|近义词：integrate, include, combine, merge|反义词:separate, divide, exclude|verb
