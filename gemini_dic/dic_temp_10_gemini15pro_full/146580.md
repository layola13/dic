overgratitude: /ˌoʊvərˈɡrætɪˌtjuːd/ /ˌəʊvəˈɡrætɪˌtjuːd/ | n. | 过度感激，过分的感谢 | Excessive gratitude; being overly thankful. |  She showered him with gifts in a fit of overgratitude. 她在一阵过度感激中送给他大量的礼物。|  He felt a pang of overgratitude for their kindness. 他对他们的好意感到一阵过分的感激。| 近义词: excessive gratitude, profuse thanks | 反义词: ingratitude, ungratefulness | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From over- + gratitude.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Gratitude" comes from the Late Latin word "gratitudo," meaning "thankfulness."
