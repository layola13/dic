haphazard:/ˌhæpˈhæzərd/ | adj. | 偶然的，随意的，杂乱无章的 | Lacking any obvious principle of organization. |  The books were piled on the shelves in a haphazard manner.  这些书杂乱无章地堆放在书架上。| synonyms: random, chance, casual, disorganized, unsystematic | antonyms: organized, systematic, planned, methodical | adjective

haphazardly:/ˌhæpˈhæzərdli/ | adv. | 偶然地，随意地，杂乱无章地 | In a random manner. | The furniture was haphazardly arranged around the room. 家具随意地摆放在房间里。 | synonyms: randomly, carelessly, casually | antonyms: systematically, methodically, deliberately | adverb

haphazardness:/ˌhæpˈhæzərdnəs/ | n. | 偶然性，随意性，杂乱无章 | The quality of lacking any obvious principle of organization. | The haphazardness of the arrangement made it difficult to find anything.  安排的随意性使得很难找到任何东西。 | synonyms: randomness, disorder, chaos | antonyms: order, organization, system | noun
