bran:/bræn/ | n. |麸皮；麦糠 |The outer layer of the grain kernel that is separated from the endosperm during milling. | She added bran to her muffin mix for extra fiber. 她为了增加纤维在松饼混合物中加入了麸皮。| synonyms: chaff, hull | antonyms: endosperm, flour | noun

bran:/bræn/ | n. |谷类作物外壳 |The husk or outer covering of grain, especially of wheat, rye, or oats. | The bran was used as animal feed. 麸皮被用作动物饲料。| synonyms: husk, shell | antonyms: kernel, grain | noun
