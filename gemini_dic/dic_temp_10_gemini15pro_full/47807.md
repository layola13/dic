decarbonization:/diːˌkɑːrbənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /diːˌkɑːrbənɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
脱碳；去碳化
The process of reducing or eliminating carbon dioxide emissions.  This involves transitioning to cleaner energy sources, improving energy efficiency, and adopting carbon capture technologies.
Many countries are pursuing policies of decarbonization to mitigate climate change.  许多国家正在奉行脱碳政策以缓解气候变化。

近义词: decarbonisation, emission reduction, carbon neutrality
反义词: carbonization, increased emissions
