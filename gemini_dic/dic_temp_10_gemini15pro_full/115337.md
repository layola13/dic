mano:/ˈmɑːnoʊ/ (US), /ˈmænoʊ/ (UK)| n.| 手 |A hand, especially in Spanish or Italian contexts.  It's often used in a figurative sense to refer to skill, power, or control.|He showed his mano in the poker game. 他在扑克游戏中展示了他的技巧。|近义词：hand, grip, control|反义词: (No direct opposite, depends on context.
