nondamagingly:/ˌnɒnˈdæmɪdʒɪŋli/
adverb
无损地；不破坏地
in a way that does not cause damage
The product cleans nondamagingly.  这种产品清洁起来无损物体表面。
Synonyms: harmlessly, safely, innocuously
Antonyms: destructively, harmfully, damagingly
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + damaging + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not". "Damaging" comes from the verb "damage," which ultimately derives from the Old French "damager," meaning "to inflict loss or injury."  "-ly" is a suffix used to form adverbs.
