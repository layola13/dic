iodhydrin:/ˌaɪədoʊˈhaɪdrɪn/ | n. | 碘醇，碘代醇 | An organic compound containing both a hydroxyl group (-OH) and an iodine atom attached to adjacent carbon atoms.  It is formed by the addition of hypoiodous acid (HOI) to an alkene. |  The reaction yielded a mixture of iodohydrins. 这个反应产生了一种碘醇的混合物。|  halohydrin |  N/A | noun
