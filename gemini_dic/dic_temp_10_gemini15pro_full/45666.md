curtained:/ˈkɜːrtənd/
adjective
被窗帘遮蔽的，装有窗帘的
screened or hung with curtains.
The room was dark and curtained. 房间昏暗，且挂着窗帘。
The stage was curtained. 舞台被窗帘遮住了。
近义词:draped, covered
反义词:uncovered, exposed
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From curtain + -ed.  "Curtain" comes from Old North French cortine, ultimately from Latin cortina.
