counterclaim:/ˈkaʊntərkleɪm/ |ˈkaʊntərkleɪm/
n.|反诉; 反驳; 抗辩|A claim made to offset another claim, especially one made by the defendant in a legal action.|The defendant filed a counterclaim for damages.|反诉；反要求；反请求|rebuttal, rejoinder|claim, assertion|noun

counterclaims:/ˈkaʊntərkleɪmz/ |ˈkaʊntərkleɪmz/
