inflict:/ɪnˈflɪkt/| v. |使遭受，施加| to cause someone to experience something unpleasant or harmful | The typhoon inflicted severe damage on the coastal areas. 台风给沿海地区造成了严重的破坏。|  The judge inflicted a harsh penalty on the defendant.  法官对被告处以严厉的惩罚。 | synonyms: impose, cause, wreak, visit | antonyms: alleviate, soothe, relieve | verb

inflict:/ɪnˈflɪkt/| v. |使遭受，施加| to force someone to spend time with someone or something that they find unpleasant |  I don't want to inflict my problems on you. 我不想把我的问题强加给你。 | She inflicted her terrible singing on the audience. 她把糟糕的歌声强加给了观众。| synonyms: subject, expose, force | antonyms: protect, shield, spare | verb
