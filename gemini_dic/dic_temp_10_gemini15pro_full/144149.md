outshape:/ˌaʊtˈʃeɪp/
verb
胜过…的体形; 比…体形好
to be in better physical condition than
例句：He easily outshaped his opponent in the boxing match. 他在拳击比赛中轻易地胜过了对手的体形。

例句：She outshaped all the other runners in the marathon. 她在马拉松比赛中比所有其他跑步者的体形都好。
近义词：outperform, surpass, excel
反义词：underperform
词性:v.
