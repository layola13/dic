inabilities:/ˌɪnəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |无能；无力；不能|The report highlighted the company's inabilities to adapt to the changing market.  这份报告强调了该公司无力适应不断变化的市场。|例句：His inabilities to manage his finances led to bankruptcy. 他无力管理自己的财务导致破产。

例句：Her physical inabilities prevented her from participating in the marathon. 她的身体状况使她无法参加马拉松比赛。|近义词：incompetences, disabilities, shortcomings|反义词:abilities, capabilities, competencies|词性:名词(plural)

词源:
Late Middle English: from in- ‘not’ + ability + -ity.
