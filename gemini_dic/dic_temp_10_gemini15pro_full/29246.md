cassette:/kəˈsɛt/ /kæˈsɛt/| n.|盒式磁带；卡式磁带|A sealed plastic unit containing a length of magnetic tape, used for recording sound or pictures.|I found a box of old cassettes in the attic. 我在阁楼里发现了一箱旧磁带。|近义词:tape, cartridge|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps digital file)|noun


cassettes:/kəˈsɛts/ /kæˈsɛts/| n.|盒式磁带；卡式磁带（复数）|Plural form of cassette.|He has a large collection of music cassettes. 他收藏了很多音乐磁带。|近义词:tapes, cartridges|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps digital files)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cassette*, diminutive of *casse* ("box, case"), from Latin *capsa*.
