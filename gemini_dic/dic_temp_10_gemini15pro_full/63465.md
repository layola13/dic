entresol:/ˈɑːntrəˌsɔːl/ /ˈɛntrəˌsɒl/| n. |夹层，中间层，楼中楼|A low story between two main stories of a building; a mezzanine.|例句：The entresol provided additional office space. 夹层提供了额外的办公空间。

例句：The architect designed an entresol to maximize the use of vertical space.  建筑师设计了一个夹层以最大限度地利用垂直空间。|近义词：mezzanine, balcony, loft|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "main floor")|词性:noun
