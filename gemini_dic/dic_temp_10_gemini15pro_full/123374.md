monomeniscous:/ˌmɒnəməˈnɪskəs/| adj.|单子囊的|Having or consisting of a single sporangium.|This fungus is monomeniscous, producing only one sporangium per individual.|近义词:haplosporangiate|反义词:None|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *mono- ("one") + *mēninx* ("membrane") + *-ous* (adjective suffix).  The term refers to the single-walled nature of the sporangium.
