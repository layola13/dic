misreferences:/ˌmɪsˈrɛfərənsɪz/
|n.|错误引用；误称|An act of referring to something incorrectly. The book contains several misreferences.|The author made several misreferences to historical events in his latest novel.  作者在他最新的长篇小说中犯了几个史实引用错误。|miscitations, misquotes|correct references, accurate citations|noun
misreference:/ˌmɪsˈrɛfərəns/
|vt.|错误地引用；误称|To refer to something incorrectly. He misreferenced several key studies in his paper.|She misreferenced the title of the book in her bibliography. 她在参考文献中写错了书名。|miscite, misquote|cite correctly, quote accurately|verb
