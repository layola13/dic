consoled:/kənˈsoʊld/
verb
安慰; 慰藉
past tense and past participle of console.  To comfort someone at a time of grief or disappointment.
He consoled her after her father's death. 他在她父亲去世后安慰她。
近义词: comforted, soothed, reassured
反义词: distressed, upset, aggravated
词性: verb

console:/kənˈsoʊl/
verb
安慰; 慰藉
To comfort someone at a time of grief or disappointment.
She tried to console him after his team lost.  她在他球队输球后试图安慰他。
近义词: comfort, soothe, reassure
反义词: distress, upset, aggravate
词性: verb


console:/ˈkɑːn.soʊl/
noun
(游戏机、电脑等的)控制台，操纵台; (教堂内的)唱诗班席位; (管风琴的)琴座; (尤指旧式收音机、电视机的)落地式机壳
A panel or unit accommodating a set of controls for electronic or mechanical equipment.  A cabinet for a radio, television, or phonograph.
The pilot adjusted the controls on the console. 飞行员调整了控制台上的控制装置。
近义词: control panel, dashboard, instrument panel
反义词: None (This noun doesn't typically have direct antonyms)
词性: noun


词源:
From Late Middle English *consolen*, from Late Latin *consolari* (“to comfort, console”), from Latin *con-* (“together”) + *solari* (“to soothe, comfort”).
