aflutter:/əˈflʌtər/ | adj. | 激动不安的，兴奋的，紧张的 | In a state of nervous excitement or agitation.  |  She was all aflutter about her upcoming wedding. 她对即将到来的婚礼感到非常兴奋。 |  The children were aflutter with excitement on Christmas morning. 圣诞节的早晨，孩子们兴奋极了。 |  excited, nervous, agitated | calm, composed | adjective

Etymology->
From a- + flutter.  The prefix "a-" often indicates a state or process. "Flutter" itself comes from the Old Norse "flotra" meaning "to float, flutter".
