crawler:/ˈkrɔːlər/| n.|爬行者；履带式车辆；网络爬虫|A crawler is something that crawls, such as an insect, or a vehicle that moves on tracks. It can also refer to a program that systematically browses the World Wide Web. |The baby crawler explored the living room.  婴儿爬行者在客厅里探索。

The army crawler moved slowly across the terrain. 军队履带车缓慢地穿过地形。

The web crawler indexed millions of web pages. 网络爬虫索引了数百万个网页。|近义词：spider (for web crawler), creeper (for web crawler), insect, reptile|反义词:flyer, walker (depending on context)|noun
