enlargers:/ɪnˈlɑːrdʒərz/
|n.|放大机；扩大器；增大器|A device, typically a projector, used to make an enlarged photographic print from a negative.|He used an enlarger to create a poster-sized print from his 35mm negative. 他用放大机把他的35毫米底片放大成海报大小的图片。|synonyms: magnifiers, amplifiers, expanders|antonyms: reducers, miniaturizers|noun

enlarger:/ɪnˈlɑːrdʒər/
|n.|放大器；扩大者|Something that enlarges or increases something else.|Education is often seen as an enlarger of opportunities. 教育常被视为扩大机会的途径。|synonyms: amplifier, expander, increaser|antonyms: reducer, diminisher, decreaser|noun
