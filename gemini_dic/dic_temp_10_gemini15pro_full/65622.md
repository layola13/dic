evangelicism: /ɪˌvænˈdʒɛlɪzəm/ /ɪˌvænˈdʒələzəm/
n.
福音主义; 传福音; 福音传道
The spreading of the Christian gospel by public preaching or personal witness.
例句：Her evangelicalism led her to missionary work in Africa. 她的福音主义信仰使她去非洲从事传教工作。

例句：The rise of evangelicalism in the 20th century has been significant.  20世纪福音主义的兴起意义重大。

近义词：proselytism, missionizing, preaching
反义词：secularism, atheism
词性：noun
