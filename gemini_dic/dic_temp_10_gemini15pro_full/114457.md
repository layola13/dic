makeweight:/ˈmeɪkˌweɪt/| n.|补充物；凑数的人或物；|Something added to make up a required weight or number, especially something of inferior quality.  A person or thing that is added to a group or collection to make it complete or stronger, but that is not really suitable or important. |例句：He was a political makeweight in the cabinet. 他在内阁中只是一个政治上的凑数人物。|近义词：filler, substitute, stopgap, supplement|反义词:essential, crucial, key|noun
