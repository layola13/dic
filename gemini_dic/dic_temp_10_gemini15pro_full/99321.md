interculturally:/ˌɪntərˈkʌltʃərəli/
adv.
跨文化地
in a way that relates to different cultures or involves people from different cultures
例句：Students need to be able to communicate interculturally. 学生需要能够进行跨文化交流。

例句：The company operates interculturally, with offices in several countries. 该公司跨文化运营，在多个国家设有办事处。

近义词：cross-culturally
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "intra-culturally" within a single culture)
词性: 副词
