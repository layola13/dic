floral:/ˈflɔːrəl/ (US) /ˈflɒrəl/ (UK)| adj.|花的，花卉的；植物的|Relating to flowers or plants. Used to describe things made of flowers, decorated with flowers, or having a flower-like pattern.|例句：She wore a floral dress to the wedding. 她穿着一件花卉连衣裙去参加婚礼。

例句：The floral arrangements in the church were beautiful. 教堂里的插花很漂亮。

近义词：flowery, blooming, blossoming|反义词:plain, unadorned|词性:adjective
