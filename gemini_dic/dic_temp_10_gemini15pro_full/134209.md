nonindictment:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdaɪtmənt/| n.|不起诉，不予起诉|A formal decision by a grand jury not to bring charges against someone accused of a crime.|The grand jury's nonindictment came as a surprise to many. 大陪审团的不起诉决定让许多人感到意外。

The nonindictment allowed him to walk free.  不起诉让他得以自由。|近义词：acquittal, discharge, exoneration|反义词:indictment, prosecution|词性:noun
