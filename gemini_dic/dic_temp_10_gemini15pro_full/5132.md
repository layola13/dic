allegorizer: /ˈælɪɡəˌraɪzər/ | n. | 寓言家，讽喻者 |  A person who creates or interprets allegories.  An allegorizer uses symbols and metaphors to represent abstract ideas or moral qualities. |  The allegorizer used the characters of animals to represent different human vices. 寓言家用动物角色来代表不同的人类恶习。| synonym: symbolist, interpreter | antonym: literalist | noun

allegorize: /ˈælɪɡəˌraɪz/ | v. |  寓言化，用寓言表达 | To represent or interpret something as an allegory.  To express something in an allegorical way. | The author allegorizes the struggle between good and evil through the characters of a knight and a dragon. 作者通过骑士和龙的角色来寓言化善与恶之间的斗争。| synonym: symbolize, represent, personify | antonym: literalize, demythologize | verb
