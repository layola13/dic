handicap:/ˈhændikæp/ (US), /ˈhændɪkæp/ (UK)| n. vt.|障碍，不利条件；残疾；给…设置不利条件|A handicap is a circumstance that makes progress or success difficult.|His shyness was a handicap in social situations. 他的害羞在社交场合是一种障碍。|Synonyms: disadvantage, impediment, obstacle, disability|Antonyms: advantage, benefit, asset|noun

handicap:/ˈhændikæp/ (US), /ˈhændɪkæp/ (UK)| vt.|妨碍，使不利|To handicap is to place someone or something at a disadvantage.|The injury handicapped him throughout his career.  这次受伤在他的整个职业生涯中都对他造成了障碍。|Synonyms: hinder, hamper, impede, obstruct|Antonyms: help, aid, assist, facilitate|verb
