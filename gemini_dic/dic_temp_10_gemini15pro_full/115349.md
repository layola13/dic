manometer:/məˈnɒmɪtər/ (US) /məˈnɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |压力计；测压计|An instrument for measuring the pressure of gases or liquids.|The engineer used a manometer to measure the pressure in the pipeline. 工程师用压力计测量管道中的压力。|Synonyms: pressure gauge, pressure indicator|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps vacuum gauge for measuring very low pressures)|noun


manometer:/məˈnɒmɪtər/ (US) /məˈnɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |压力计；测压计|From Greek *manos* "thin, rare" + *metron* "measure". Originally referred to instruments that measured the rarity (low pressure) of air.|A manometer is essential for monitoring the pressure in the system. 压力计对于监测系统中的压力至关重要。|Synonyms: pressure gauge, pressure indicator|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps vacuum gauge for measuring very low pressures)|noun
