例句：The galaxy's central black hole is incredibly massive.  这个星系中心的的黑洞质量巨大。|近义词：galactic|反义词: (No direct antonym for a possessive form.  One could consider "extragalactic" in some contexts, referring to something outside the galaxy.)|词性:Possessive form of the noun "galaxy"
