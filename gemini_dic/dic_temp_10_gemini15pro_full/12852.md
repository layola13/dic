assoilment:/əˈsɔɪlmənt/
noun
赦免; 解除(宗教上的罪责); 净罪; 松土
the act of absolving someone of a sin or wrongdoing.  The freeing of land from a feudal obligation.  The act of turning over soil.
The priest granted the penitent assoilment after his confession.  神父在忏悔者忏悔后给予他赦免。
The assoilment of the land from its feudal obligations allowed the farmer to prosper.  这块土地从封建义务中解脱出来，使农民得以繁荣。
The farmer's assoilment of the soil prepared it for planting.  农民翻土为播种做准备。
synonym:absolution, forgiveness, pardon, release, acquittal, exoneration, discharge, liberation, freeing, manumission, emancipation; tillage, cultivation, plowing
antonym:condemnation, conviction, incrimination, accusation; neglect
