courageousnesses:/kəˈreɪdʒəsnɪsɪz/| n. |勇敢，勇气（罕用，通常用courage）|The state or quality of being courageous; bravery. This plural form is extremely rare and generally considered non-standard. It is much more common and natural to use "courage" or other related nouns like "bravery" or "valor."|例句：His courageousnesses in the face of danger was remarkable. (Non-standard usage - better to say: His courage in the face of danger was remarkable.) 他面对危险的勇气非凡。

例句：The courageousnesses of the firefighters saved many lives. (Non-standard usage - better to say: The courage of the firefighters saved many lives.) 消防员的勇气挽救了许多生命。|近义词：courage, bravery, valor, boldness, fearlessness|反义词：cowardice, timidity, fearfulness|词性:名词
