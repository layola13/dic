astronomer: /əˈstrɒnəmər/ (UK) /əˈstrɑːnəmər/ (US)| n. |天文学家|A scientist who studies the universe and celestial objects like stars, planets, and galaxies.|例句：The astronomer made a groundbreaking discovery about a distant galaxy.  这位天文学家对一个遥远的星系做出了突破性的发现。

例句：She dreamed of becoming an astronomer since she was a little girl. 她从小就梦想成为一名天文学家。|近义词：astrophysicist, cosmologist|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle French *astronome*, from Latin astronomus, from Ancient Greek ἀστρονόμος (astronomos, “astronomer”), from ἄστρον (astron, “star”) + νόμος (nomos, “law, custom; assigned portion, province”).
