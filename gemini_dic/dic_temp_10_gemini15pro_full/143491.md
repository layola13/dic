outgleaming:/ˌaʊtˈɡliːmɪŋ/
| adj. |胜过…的光辉，比…更闪亮|Surpassing in brightness; shining more brightly than something else.|The outgleaming jewels caught everyone's attention. 这些闪耀夺目的珠宝吸引了所有人的目光。|The new star outgleaming all others in the sky. 这颗新星比天空中所有其他星星都更加闪耀。|近义词：outshining, outdazzling, surpassing|反义词:dull, dim|词性:形容词

词源:
"out-" (prefix): meaning "surpassing," "exceeding," "outside," or "beyond"
"gleam" (verb/noun): from Old English "glæm" meaning "gleam, brightness, splendor"
