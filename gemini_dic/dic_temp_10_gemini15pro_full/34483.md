clayiest:/ˈkleɪiɪst/
adjective
最黏土的；含黏土最多的
having the most clay; most clayey.
The clayiest soil in the garden is difficult to work with. 花园里黏土最多的土壤很难耕作。
The pottery used the clayiest mud from the riverbank.  这家陶器厂使用了河岸边黏土含量最高的泥巴。
近义词: stickiest, heaviest
反义词: sandiest, lightest
词性: adjective

词源:
From clay + -y + -est.  "Clay" comes from Old English "clæg," meaning "sticky earth." The suffix "-y" forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by," and "-est" is the superlative suffix.
