nonscrutiny:/nɒnˈskruːtɪni/| n. |不审查；免检；不详细检查|Absence or lack of scrutiny; a state of not being scrutinized or examined closely.|The documents were released for publication after a period of nonscrutiny. 这些文件在一段时间的不审查后被公布发表。|The donations received nonscrutiny, raising concerns about potential fraud.  这些捐款未经审查，引发了人们对潜在欺诈的担忧。|近义词:nonexamination, overlooking, disregard|反义词:scrutiny, examination, inspection|词性:名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "scrutiny" (from Latin "scrutinium" meaning "a search, inquiry").
