eucopepoda:/ˌjuːkɒˈpiːpədə/ | n. |真桡足类|A subclass of copepods (small crustaceans) characterized by having a heart and a fifth pair of legs modified for jumping. |Eucopepods are an important part of the marine food web. 真桡足类是海洋食物网的重要组成部分。| Copepoda, Maxillopoda | - | noun
etymology-> From Greek *eu- "good, well" + *kope* "oar" + *pous* "foot".
