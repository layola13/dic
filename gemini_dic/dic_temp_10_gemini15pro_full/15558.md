bagpipe:/ˈbæɡpaɪp/| n.|风笛|A wind instrument consisting of a reed melody pipe and from one to five drones, all fed from a bag inflated by the player.|The sound of the bagpipes filled the air. 风笛声充满了空气。|Near synonyms: pipes, drone, chanter|Antonyms: None|Noun
