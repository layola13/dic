multispecies:/ˌmʌltiˈspiːʃiːz/
adjective
多物种的
involving or relating to more than one species
例句：Multispecies fisheries management is crucial for ecosystem health.  多物种渔业管理对生态系统健康至关重要。

例句：The study focused on the multispecies interactions within the coral reef.  这项研究的重点是珊瑚礁内的多物种相互作用。

近义词：polyspecific, interspecific
反义词：monospecific, single-species
词性：adjective
