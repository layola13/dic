botanizer:/ˈbɒtənaɪzər (UK) / ˈbɑːtəˌnaɪzər (US)| n.| 植物学家；采集植物标本的人| A person who botanizes; a student of plants.|  Example: The botanizer spent the afternoon collecting specimens in the forest.  这位植物学家下午在森林里采集标本。 | Example:  A keen botanizer, she could identify almost every wildflower.  作为一名热心的植物学家，她几乎能辨认出每一种野花。 | Synonym: botanist, plant collector, herbalist | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who destroys plants?) | noun
