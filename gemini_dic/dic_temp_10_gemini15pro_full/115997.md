marshaller:/ˈmɑːrʃələr/ | n. |编组员，调度员|A person who arranges or organizes things, often people or vehicles, in a systematic way, especially for a particular event or purpose.|The marshaller directed the planes to their assigned parking spots.  调度员指挥飞机停放到指定的停车位。|近义词：organizer, coordinator, director, arranger|反义词: disorganizer |noun

marshaller:/ˈmɑːrʃələr/ | n. | (computing)编组器，序列化器 | In computer science, a marshaller transforms the memory representation of an object into a data format suitable for storage or transmission, and vice versa. | The marshaller converts the data into a format that can be sent over the network.  编组器将数据转换为可以通过网络发送的格式。|近义词：serializer, deserializer|反义词: N/A |noun
