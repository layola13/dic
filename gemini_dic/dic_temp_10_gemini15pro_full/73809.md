formatters:/ˈfɔːrmætərz/ (US) /ˈfɔːmætəz/ (UK)| n.|格式化程序，格式化器|A formatter is a program or a piece of code that arranges data or text in a specific format, often for display or storage. |The formatter converted the raw data into a user-friendly table. 格式化程序将原始数据转换为用户友好的表格。|synonyms: styler, organizer, formatter, arranger|antonyms: disorganizer, scrambler|noun
