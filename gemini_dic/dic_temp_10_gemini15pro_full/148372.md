overspends:/ˌoʊvərˈspɛndz/ | v. | 超支，过度消费 | To spend more money than one has budgeted or can afford. |  He overspends on clothes every month. 他每个月都在衣服上超支。| 近义词：splurge, squander | 反义词：save, budget | 动词

overspend:/ˌoʊvərˈspɛnd/ | n. | 超支 | The act of spending more than one can afford. | The family's overspend on their vacation put them in debt.  家庭度假超支使他们负债累累。| 近义词：excess spending | 反义词：saving, underspending | 名词
