half-time:/ˌhɑːfˈtaɪm/ /ˌhæfˈtaɪm/| n. adj.|中场休息，半场|a period of rest between the two halves of a sports game, or the end of the first half. |The score was 2-1 at half-time.  上半场比分是2比1。|synonyms: intermission, break, recess|antonyms: full-time, game time|noun
half-time:/ˌhɑːfˈtaɪm/ /ˌhæfˈtaɪm/| adj.|半场的，中场的|Relating to or occurring during the period of rest between the two halves of a sports game. |It was a half-time substitution.  这是一个中场换人。|synonyms: midterm|antonyms: full-time|adjective
