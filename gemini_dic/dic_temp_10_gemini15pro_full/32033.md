cherisher:/ˈtʃɛrɪʃər/ |n.|珍爱者，爱护者|Someone who holds something dear and treats it with affection and care.|She is a cherisher of old photographs and memories. 她是一位珍爱旧照片和回忆的人。|近义词：protector, guardian, custodian, nurturer|反义词：neglecter, abuser|noun

cherish:/ˈtʃɛrɪʃ/ |v.|珍爱，爱护，怀有（感情等）|To hold (something) dear; feel or show affection for.|I cherish the time I spend with my family. 我珍视与家人共度的时光.|近义词：treasure, value, prize, adore, hold dear|反义词：despise, scorn, neglect|verb
