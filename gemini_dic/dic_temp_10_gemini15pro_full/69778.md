fermentable:/fərˈmɛntəbl/ | adj. | 可发酵的 | Capable of undergoing fermentation, a chemical process by which complex organic compounds are broken down into simpler substances, typically by the action of microorganisms such as yeast or bacteria. |  Grapes are fermentable and can be used to make wine. 葡萄可以发酵并用来酿酒。 |  The mixture is fermentable under the right conditions. 这种混合物在合适的条件下可以发酵。 | synonyms: zymogenic, fermentive | antonyms: non-fermentable, unfermentable | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Latin *fermentābilis, from Latin fermentāre (“to ferment”) + -ābilis.  From Latin fermentum (“leaven, yeast”).
