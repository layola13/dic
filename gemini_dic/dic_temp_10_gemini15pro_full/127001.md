nasalized:/ˈneɪzəlaɪzd/| adj.|鼻音化的，带鼻音的|A sound is nasalized when air passes through the nose as well as the mouth during its production.|The "n" in "sing" is nasalized.|近义词：nasal|反义词：oral|词性:形容词
nasalize:/ˈneɪzəlaɪz/| vt.|使鼻音化，用鼻音发音|To make a sound nasal by lowering the soft palate so that air passes through the nose.|French nasalizes certain vowels.|近义词：None|反义词：None|词性:动词
