megadeaths:/ˈmɛɡədɛθs/| n. |百万人口死亡数；大规模死亡人数|A unit of death equal to one million deaths, typically used in discussing large-scale disasters or hypothetical scenarios.|The potential megadeaths from a nuclear war are horrifying. 核战争可能造成的数百万人死亡令人恐惧。|Synonyms: mass casualties, mass fatalities|Antonyms: survival, life|noun
