minced:/mɪnst/| adj.|切碎的； minced meat 肉末|cut up or ground into very small pieces|I made spaghetti with minced beef. 我用牛肉末做了意大利面。|Synonyms: chopped, ground, pulverized|Antonyms: whole, uncut|adjective

mince:/mɪns/| v.|切碎；碎步走；装腔作势地说|to cut into very small pieces; to walk with short, dainty steps; to speak in an affected way|She minced the garlic finely. 她把大蒜切得很碎。|Synonyms: chop, grind, dice (for cutting); tiptoe, prance (for walking); euphemize, prevaricate (for speaking)|Antonyms:  (for cutting) leave whole; (for walking) stride; (for speaking) be blunt|verb
