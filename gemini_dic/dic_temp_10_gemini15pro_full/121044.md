misconduct:/ˌmɪsˈkɑːndʌkt/ /ˌmɪsˈkɒndʌkt/| n.  v.| 不端行为，失职；处理不当 |Improper or unprofessional behavior, especially by a person in a position of authority; to manage (affairs) badly.| The lawyer was accused of professional misconduct.  该律师被指控职业失当。|Synonyms: wrongdoing, misbehavior, malpractice, impropriety|Antonyms: propriety, good conduct, rectitude|noun
