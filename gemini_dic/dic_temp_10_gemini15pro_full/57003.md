double-jointed:/ˌdʌbəlˈdʒɔɪntɪd/
adjective
双关节的；关节过度活动的
having joints that allow a greater than normal range of movement
My sister is double-jointed and can bend her fingers backwards. 我的姐姐是双关节的，可以把手指向后弯曲。
He's double-jointed in his thumbs. 他的拇指是双关节的。
近义词:hypermobile
反义词:(no common single word antonym)
词性:adjective
