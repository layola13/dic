nourishing:/ˈnɜːrɪʃɪŋ/| adj.|营养的，滋养的|Providing the substances needed for growth, health, and good condition.|例句：A nourishing meal is essential for good health. 一顿营养的餐食对健康至关重要。|例句：The lotion is nourishing and leaves my skin feeling soft. 这款乳液滋养肌肤，用后感觉皮肤柔软。|近义词：nutritious, wholesome, healthy|反义词：unhealthy, detrimental, harmful|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *norisshinge*, present participle of *norisshen* (“to nourish”), from Anglo-Norman *nurir*, *norir* and Old French *norrir*, *nourir* (“to nourish, nurture, educate”), from Latin *nutrire* (“to suckle, nourish”), from *nutrix* (“nurse”).
