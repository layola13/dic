ancipitous:/ænˈsɪpɪtəs/
adjective
双刃的；危险的；不确定的
Having two edges; of doubtful outcome; uncertain
The ancipitous nature of the surgery made her anxious.  这项手术的不确定性使她感到焦虑。
The general faced an ancipitous battle, unsure if victory or defeat awaited. 将军面临着一场胜负难料的战斗。
近义词:precarious, perilous, risky, ambiguous, double-edged
反义词:safe, secure, certain, unambiguous
词性:adjective

Etymology: From Latin *ancipitis*, genitive of *anceps* "two-headed, double, uncertain," from *ambi-* "around, on both sides" + *caput* "head."
