orator:/ˈɔːrətər/ (US) /ˈɒrətə(r)/ (UK)| n. |演说家，雄辩家|A skilled and eloquent public speaker, especially one who delivers formal speeches.|例句：The orator captivated the audience with his powerful speech.  这位演说家以他强有力的演讲吸引了观众。

例句：He was a gifted orator, able to move people with his words. 他是一位天才的演说家，能够用他的语言打动人。

近义词：speaker, lecturer, rhetorician|反义词:listener, audience|词性:noun
