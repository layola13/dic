incubates:/ˈɪŋkjuːbeɪts/
v.
孵化，培养，孕育
causes (something) to develop slowly
例句：The hen incubates her eggs for three weeks. 母鸡孵蛋三周。

The company incubates new ideas and businesses. 这家公司培养新的想法和业务。

近义词：develops, fosters, nurtures, hatches
反义词：neglects, abandons, stifles
词性：动词


incubate:/ˈɪŋkjuːbeɪt/
v.
孵化，培养，孕育
to maintain (something, such as an embryo or a bacteria culture) under conditions favorable for hatching, development, or reaction
例句：The samples are incubated at 37 degrees Celsius. 样品在37摄氏度下孵育。

She continued to incubate her plan for revenge. 她继续酝酿她的复仇计划。

近义词：develop, foster, nurture, hatch
反义词：neglect, abandon, stifle
词性：动词


词源：From Late Latin incubatus, past participle of incubare "to lie on, brood over," from in- "on" + cubare "to lie down."
