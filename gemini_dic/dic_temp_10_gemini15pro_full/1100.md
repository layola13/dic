accessory: /əkˈsesəri/ | n. adj. | 配件，附件；从犯 | A thing that can be added to something else in order to make it more useful, versatile, or attractive.  A person who helps another commit a crime. |  The dress came with a matching handbag and other accessories. 这条裙子配有一个配套的手提包和其他配件。 He was charged as an accessory to murder. 他被指控为谋杀案的从犯。 | 近义词: (n.) attachment, appendage, addition, supplement, accomplice, abettor (adj.) supplemental, auxiliary, secondary | 反义词: (n.) principal, perpetrator | 词性: 名词, 形容词

USAGE->
作为名词:
She wore a simple black dress with striking accessories. = 她穿着一件简单的黑色连衣裙，搭配引人注目的配饰。
Car accessories such as alloy wheels and spoilers are popular with young drivers. =  合金轮毂和扰流板等汽车配件很受年轻司机的欢迎。

作为形容词:
The engine was fitted with accessory parts. =  发动机安装了辅助部件。
Accessory equipment for the computer included a printer and scanner. = 电脑的附属设备包括打印机和扫描仪。


PHRASE->
fashion accessories = 时尚配饰
car accessories = 汽车配件
optional accessories = 可选配件
accessory equipment = 辅助设备
accessory before/after the fact = 事前/事后从犯


词源:
From Old French *accessoire (Modern French accessoire), from Latin accessorius (“additional”), from accedere (“to approach, be added to”).
