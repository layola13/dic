hastated:/ˈheɪsteɪtɪd/| adj.|戟形的，矛形的|Shaped like a spearhead with barbs, resembling the head of a halberd. |The leaf of the plant was distinctly hastate, with its pointed lobes flaring outwards.  这种植物的叶子明显呈戟形，尖尖的裂片向外张开。|近义词：spear-shaped, lanceolate|反义词:rounded, ovate|词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *hastatus, from hasta (“spear”).
