cowardish:/ˈkaʊərdɪʃ/| adj.|懦弱的，胆小的|Lacking courage and resolution; timid.|He made a cowardly decision to abandon his friends.|近义词：timid, fearful, craven, pusillanimous|反义词：brave, courageous, valiant|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *cowardish*, from *coward* + *-ish*.  *Coward* comes from Old French *coart*, ultimately from Latin *cauda* (tail), referring to an animal with its tail between its legs, a sign of fear.
