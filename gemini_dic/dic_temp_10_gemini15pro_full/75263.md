frondescence:/frɒnˈdɛsəns/ | n. | 叶茂盛；发叶；长绿 | The process of putting forth leaves, or the time of leafing. | The frondescence of the ferns in the rainforest was breathtaking. 雨林中蕨类植物的繁茂景象令人叹为观止。 | Synonyms:  leafing, burgeoning, sprouting | Antonyms: defoliation, wilting | noun

frondescent:/frɒnˈdɛsənt/ | adj. | 叶茂盛的；长绿的 | Characterized by or having fronds; leafy; producing fronds. | The frondescent ferns created a lush, green carpet on the forest floor.  叶茂盛的蕨类植物在森林地面上形成了郁郁葱葱的绿色地毯。 | Synonyms: leafy, verdant, lush | Antonyms: barren, leafless | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *frondescens*, present participle of *frondescere* "to put forth leaves," from *frons* "leaf, foliage" + *-escere* (inceptive suffix).
