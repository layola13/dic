concomitant:/kənˈkɒmɪtənt/ /kənˈkɑːmɪtənt/| adj. n.|伴随的，共存的；伴随物|happening or existing at the same time as something else|The fever is concomitant with the rash. 发烧伴有皮疹。|Synonyms: accompanying, attendant, concurrent, coincident, related|Antonyms: unrelated, separate, independent|adjective

concomitant:/kənˈkɒmɪtənt/ /kənˈkɑːmɪtənt/| n.|伴随物，伴随现象|something that happens or exists at the same time as something else|Loss of memory is a frequent concomitant of old age.  记忆力减退是老年人的常见伴发现象。|Synonyms: accompaniment, attendant, corollary, consequence|Antonyms:  cause, origin, source|noun
