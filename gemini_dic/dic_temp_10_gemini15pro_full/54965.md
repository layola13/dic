disproperty:/dɪsˈprɒpəti/
n.
剥夺财产；丧失财产；被剥夺的财产
The act of depriving someone of property.  The state of being deprived of property. Property of which one has been deprived.
He suffered the disproperty of his family estate after the war.  战争结束后，他的家族财产被剥夺了。
Synonyms: deprivation, divestiture, forfeiture
Antonyms: acquisition, endowment, bestowal
词性: 名词
