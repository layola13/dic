outsuffer:/ˌaʊtˈsʌfər/
verb
比...更长久地受苦；比...活得更长
To suffer longer than (someone or something else).
He outsuffered his illness and lived to a ripe old age. 他战胜了病魔，活到了高龄。
Synonyms: outlive, survive
Antonyms: succumb
词性: 动词

outsuffer:/ˌaʊtˈsʌfər/
verb
忍受更大的痛苦
To suffer more intensely or for a longer duration than someone or something else.
She outsuffered her competitors and won the marathon. 她比她的竞争对手承受了更大的痛苦，赢得了马拉松比赛。
Synonyms: endure, tolerate
Antonyms: give in, surrender
词性: 动词
