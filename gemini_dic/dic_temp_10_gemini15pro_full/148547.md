oversweetens:/ˌoʊvərˈswiːtnz/
verb
加糖过多，过甜
Makes something excessively sweet.
The chef oversweetened the cake, and it was too sugary for most people.  厨师在蛋糕里加了太多的糖，对大多数人来说太甜了。
cloy, saccharify
unsweetened, bland
词性:动词
