The fosterment of new talent is crucial for the company's future.  培养新的人才是公司未来的关键。|近义词：encouragement, promotion, development, nurturing|反义词：neglect, suppression, hindrance|词性：名词

Etymology:
From  foster + -ment.  "Foster" comes from Old English *fostrian "to nourish, support, bring up," related to Old Norse fostra "to nurse, bring up," and ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fod- "food," from PIE root *pa- "to feed."  "-ment" is a suffix forming nouns of action or result.
