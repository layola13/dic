furrowless:/ˈfɜːroʊləs/
adjective
无皱纹的；光滑的
smooth and unlined, especially of a brow or face.
Her furrowless brow showed no sign of worry. 她光滑的额头没有一丝忧虑的痕迹。
近义词:smooth, unlined, unwrinkled
反义词:furrowed, wrinkled, lined
词性:adjective
