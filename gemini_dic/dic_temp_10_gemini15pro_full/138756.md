numerated:/ˈnjuːməreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 被计数的，被列举的 |  Listed or numbered individually.  | The items were carefully numerated in the inventory.  这些物品在清单中被仔细编号。 |  numbered, listed, counted, itemized | unnumbered, uncounted | adjective

numerated:/ˈnjuːməreɪtɪd/ | v. |  计算，列举  | To count or list (items) individually. |  The clerk numerated the items as he scanned them. 店员一边扫描一边计算物品数量。 |  count, list, enumerate, itemize | omit, exclude | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *numeratus*, past participle of *numerare* "to number, count," from *numerus* "number."
