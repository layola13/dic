dochmius:/ˈdɒkmiəs/| n.|抑扬格四音步, 长短短长格|A metrical foot in Greek and Latin verse consisting of a long syllable followed by two short syllables and another long syllable.  A line of verse consisting of dochmii.|The chorus in Greek tragedy often employed dochmii.|近义词: foot, meter, rhythm|反义词: (No direct antonym for a metrical foot)|词性:noun
