nonpotential:/ˌnɒnpəˈtɛnʃəl/
adjective
非势的，非保守的
Not derivable from a potential; not conservative. A nonpotential field is a vector field that is not the gradient of a scalar potential field.
The electric field produced by a changing magnetic field is nonpotential.  变化的磁场产生的电场是非势的。
Gravity is a potential force, while friction is a nonpotential force. 重力是一种势力，而摩擦力是一种非势力。
synonym: nonconservative, dissipative
antonym: potential, conservative
adjective
