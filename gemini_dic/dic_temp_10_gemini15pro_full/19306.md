bibbed:/bɪbd/
adjective
围兜的，戴着围兜的
Wearing a bib.
The baby, bibbed and ready for lunch, gurgled happily.  戴着围兜准备吃午饭的婴儿高兴地咯咯叫。
The bibbed toddler smeared mashed carrots on his face.  戴着围兜的小孩把胡萝卜泥涂抹在脸上。
近义词:  aproned
反义词:  bibless
词性: adjective
