experimentalize:/ɪkˌspɛrɪˈmɛntəlaɪz/ | v. | 实验;进行实验 | To conduct experiments; to try out new methods or ideas. |  Scientists experimentalized with different chemicals to find a cure. 科学家们用不同的化学物质进行实验以寻找治疗方法。

They experimentalized with new teaching methods. 他们尝试了新的教学方法。


| 近义词：experiment, test, try, trial | 反义词: theorize |词性: 动词
