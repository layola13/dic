alabastrine:/ˌæləˈbæstrɪn/ | adj. | 雪白的；光滑的；半透明的 |  Of or like alabaster; smooth, pale, and slightly translucent. |  Her skin was alabastrine, almost glowing in the dim light. 她的皮肤雪白，在昏暗的光线下几乎闪闪发光。| The statue was carved from alabastrine marble. 这座雕像是用光滑的大理石雕刻而成的。 | 近义词: pale, white, smooth, translucent | 反义词: dark, rough, opaque | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Alabaster (雪花石膏) + -ine (形容词后缀，表示“像…的”). Ultimately from Greek *alabastros*.
