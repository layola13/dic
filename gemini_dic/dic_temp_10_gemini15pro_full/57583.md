dramaturgies:/ˌdræməˈtɜːrdʒiz/
n.
戏剧理论; 剧作法; 戏剧性;
The theories and practices involved in playwriting and theatrical production, including the analysis of dramatic texts, the development of dramatic concepts, and the collaboration between playwrights, directors, and other theatre artists.
Example: The conference explored various contemporary dramaturgies.  这个会议探讨了各种当代戏剧理论。
Example:  Her dramaturgies often incorporate elements of absurdism and social commentary. 她的戏剧创作通常包含荒诞主义和社会评论的元素。
Synonyms: dramatic theory, playwriting theory, theatrical theory, dramaturgy
Antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词
