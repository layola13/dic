decimetric:/ˌdɛsɪˈmɛtrɪk/| adj.|分米的，十厘米的|Relating to or having a wavelength in the decimeter range (0.1 to 1 meter).  It is often used in the context of radio waves.|Decimetric radio waves are used in some radar systems. 分米波雷达系统中会使用分米波。|Synonyms: None specific, but can be described as relating to wavelengths between centimeter and meter waves.|Antonyms: None specific.|adjective

decimeter:/ˈdɛsɪˌmiːtər/| n.|分米|A metric unit of length equal to one tenth of a meter (10 centimeters).|The wavelength of this signal is measured in decimeters. 这个信号的波长以分米为单位测量。|Synonyms: None (it's a specific unit of measurement)|Antonyms: None (it's a specific unit of measurement)|noun
