disillusioner:/ˌdɪsɪˈluːʒənər/
n.
使人醒悟的人；使人幻想破灭的人
One who frees from illusion; one who causes disillusionment.
The harsh realities of war were a stark disillusioner for the young soldier. 战争的残酷现实让这位年轻的士兵彻底醒悟了。
The author's cynical portrayal of love acted as a disillusioner for many romantic readers.  作者对爱情的愤世嫉俗的描写使许多浪漫的读者幻想破灭。
synonyms:  disenchanter, undeceiver
antonyms:  romancer, idealist
noun
