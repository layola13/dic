Beelzebub: /biːˈɛlzɪbʌb/ | n. |别西卜，魔王| A name for the devil, especially as the prince of devils.  Derived from a Philistine deity, originally Baal-zebub, "lord of the flies."  It later became associated with Satan in Christian demonology. |例句：Beelzebub is often depicted as a powerful demon. 别西卜通常被描绘成一个强大的恶魔。

例句：Some scholars believe Beelzebub was originally a Philistine god. 一些学者认为别西卜最初是腓力士人的神。|近义词：Satan, Devil, Lucifer |反义词: God, Angel |词性: noun
