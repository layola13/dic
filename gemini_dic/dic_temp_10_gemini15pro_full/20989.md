blenheim:/ˈblɛnɪm/ | n. | 布伦海姆（一种金毛猎犬） | A breed of small King Charles spaniel, with a white and orange or white and liver-colored coat. |  The Blenheim spaniel is known for its gentle temperament. 布伦海姆猎犬以其温顺的性情而闻名。 | synonym: King Charles Spaniel (partially) | antonym: N/A | noun

etymology: Named after Blenheim Palace in Oxfordshire, England, the birthplace of Sir Winston Churchill, where the breed was developed.
