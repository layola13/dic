caravan: /ˈkærəvæn/ (US) /ˈkærəvæn/ (UK)| n. | 旅行队，商队；大篷车；房车|A group of people, especially traders or pilgrims, traveling together across a desert or through hostile territory. A large, covered vehicle, typically towed by a car or truck and used for living in while traveling.|  The caravan crossed the Sahara desert. 商队穿越了撒哈拉沙漠。|synonyms: convoy, procession, train, motorcade|antonyms:  individual, single| noun
