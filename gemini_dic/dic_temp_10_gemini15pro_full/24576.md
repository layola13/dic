brooch:/brəʊtʃ/ (UK) /broʊtʃ/ (US)| n.|胸针，领针|A decorative pin or clasp, typically worn at the neck or on a hat.|She wore a beautiful antique brooch on her lapel.|近义词：pin, clasp, badge|反义词: None|词性: Noun

Etymology: From Old French broche "spit, pointed tool," from Vulgar Latin *brocca, of unknown origin. The semantic development is from "pointed instrument" to "pointed ornament."
