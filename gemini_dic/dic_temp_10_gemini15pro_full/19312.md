bibelot:/ˈbɪbəloʊ/ (US), /ˈbɪbləʊ/ (UK)| n. | 小装饰品；小摆设；古玩 |A small, decorative object; a trinket; a curio. |She collects bibelots from all over the world. 她从世界各地收集小摆设。|synonyms: trinket, knick-knack, bauble, curio, ornament|antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider: necessity, essential)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bibelot*, ultimately of unknown origin.  Possibly related to *bébé* (baby) or *bibloquet* (cup and ball toy).
