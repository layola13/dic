demoniacism:/ˌdiːməˈnaɪəsɪzəm/
n.
恶魔附身；着魔；疯狂
The state of being possessed by demons or evil spirits; intense frenzy or rage.
He raved in demoniacism, claiming to be possessed by the devil. 他疯狂地咆哮，声称自己被魔鬼附身。
近义词: possession, obsession, madness, frenzy
反义词: sanity, lucidity, rationality
词性: noun


demoniacal:/ˌdiːməˈnaɪək(ə)l/
adj.
恶魔的；着魔的；疯狂的
Of, like, or characteristic of a demon; intensely frenzied or furious.
His demoniacal laughter echoed through the halls. 他恶魔般的笑声回荡在大厅里。
近义词: demonic, fiendish, hellish, satanic
反义词: angelic, divine, heavenly
词性: adjective


词源:
From Late Latin *daemoniacismus, from Ancient Greek δαιμονιακός (daimoniakos, “possessed by a demon”).
