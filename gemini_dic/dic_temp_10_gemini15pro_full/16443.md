barefacednesses:/ˌbɛəˈfeɪsɪdnɪsɪz/
noun
厚颜无耻；公然无耻的行为
The quality or state of being barefaced; impudence; shamelessness.
His barefacednesses shocked everyone present. 他厚颜无耻的行为震惊了在场的所有人。
Synonyms: audacity, effrontery, impudence, insolence, nerve, shamelessness
Antonyms: humility, modesty, shyness, timidity
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From barefaced + -ness.  Barefaced (adjective) comes from bare (meaning uncovered, exposed) + faced.  Therefore, the original sense was having an exposed face, then figuratively having nothing to hide, hence shameless or impudent.
