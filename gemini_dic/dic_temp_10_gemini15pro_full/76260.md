fuzzboxes:/ˈfʌzbɒksɪz/ (US) /ˈfʌzbɒksɪz/ (UK)| n.|失真效果器（复数）|A device, usually an electronic pedal, used to create a distorted, fuzzy sound in electric guitars or other instruments.  It modifies the audio signal to produce a warm, overdriven, and sometimes chaotic effect.|The guitarist used two fuzzboxes to get his signature sound. 吉他手使用了两个失真效果器来获得他的标志性声音。|近义词：distortion pedals, overdrive pedals|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "clean sound" as a concept)|noun
