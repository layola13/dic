aisles:/aɪlz/| n.|过道，通道|a passage between rows of seats in a building such as a church or theatre, an aircraft, or between rows of shelves in a supermarket|例句：The bride walked down the aisle.  新娘沿着过道走来。

例句：I always get lost in supermarket aisles. 我总是在超市的过道里迷路。|近义词：passageways, corridors, walkways|反义词:None|Noun
