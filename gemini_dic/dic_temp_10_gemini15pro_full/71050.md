first-footer:/ˈfɜːrstˌfʊtər/| n.| 踩门人，新年第一位访客|A first-footer is the first person to enter a home on New Year's Day, and is traditionally thought to bring good luck for the coming year.  新年第一天第一个进入家门的人，传统上认为会为来年带来好运。|例句：It is considered good luck in Scotland to have a dark-haired man as your first-footer.  在苏格兰，有一个黑发男人作为你的新年第一位访客被认为是好运。

例句：My uncle is always our first-footer, arriving just after midnight. 我叔叔总是我们的新年第一位访客，总是在午夜刚过就来了。|近义词：New Year's visitor, first guest|反义词: None|词性: Noun
