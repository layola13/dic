creativity:/ˌkriːeɪˈtɪvɪti/ | n. | 创造力，创造性 | The ability to produce or use original and unusual ideas. |  She showed great creativity in her artwork. 她在艺术作品中展现了非凡的创造力。|  imagination, innovation, ingenuity, originality |  unoriginality, imitation, conformity | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *creativitas, from Latin creare "to create".  The English word "creativity" emerged in the 17th century.
