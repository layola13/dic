overaptness:/ˈoʊvəræptnɪs/
n.|过度倾向; 过于贴切|The overaptness of the metaphor made it seem contrived. 这个比喻过于贴切，反而显得 artificial.|例句：His overaptness to agree with his boss made him seem like a yes-man. 他过分倾向于同意老板的意见，这让他看起来像个唯唯诺诺的人。|例句：The overaptness of the analogy, while initially impressive, ultimately undermined the argument.  这个类比虽然最初令人印象深刻，但最终却损害了论证。|近义词：excessiveness, inappropriateness, overzealousness|反义词:understatement,  restraint,  apathy|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + aptness.  "Over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much." "Aptness" refers to the suitability or appropriateness of something. Thus, "overaptness" describes a state of being excessively or inappropriately suitable or fitting.
