paddlelike:/ˈpædəlˌlaɪk/| adj.|桨状的，像桨一样的|Resembling a paddle, typically broad and flat.|The duck's feet are paddlelike, perfect for swimming. 鸭子的脚像桨一样，非常适合游泳。|The beaver's tail is broad and paddlelike. 海狸的尾巴又宽又像桨一样。|近义词：oarlike, spatulate|反义词:needlelike, pointed|词性:形容词

词源: paddle (n.) + -like (suffix). "Paddle" comes from Old English *pædel, from Proto-Germanic *paþlą. "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
