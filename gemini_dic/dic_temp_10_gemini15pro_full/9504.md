antiscepticism:/ˌæntiˈskeptɪsɪzəm/
|n.|反怀疑论|The rise of antiscepticism is a worrying trend. 反怀疑论的兴起是一个令人担忧的趋势。|English Definition: Opposition to skepticism; the belief that knowledge is possible.  It represents a philosophical stance against the doubts and challenges raised by skeptical arguments, asserting that genuine knowledge or justified belief can be attained.|Example: His antiscepticism led him to believe in the inherent goodness of people. 他的反怀疑主义使他相信人性的本善。|Synonym:belief, conviction, faith|Antonym:skepticism, doubt, cynicism|noun
