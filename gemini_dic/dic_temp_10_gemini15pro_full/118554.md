mesophyll:/ˈmɛsəfɪl/| n. |叶肉|The tissue of a leaf, located between the upper and lower epidermis, that contains chloroplasts and is the primary site of photosynthesis.|The mesophyll cells are packed with chloroplasts.|近义词:parenchyma|反义词:|noun
