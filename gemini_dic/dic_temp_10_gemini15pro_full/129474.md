non-aligned:/ˌnɒn əˈlaɪnd/ | adj. |不结盟的，不联盟的 | Not formally allied with any other group, especially with any of the major power blocs. |  India remained strictly non-aligned during the Cold War.  印度在冷战期间一直保持严格的不结盟立场。 | Synonyms: neutral, independent, uncommitted | Antonyms: aligned, allied, committed | adjective

non-aligned:/ˌnɒn əˈlaɪnd/ | n. |不结盟国家 | A country that is not formally allied with any other group, especially with any of the major power blocs. | Many non-aligned nations benefited from trade with both the US and the USSR. 许多不结盟国家受益于与美国和苏联的贸易。 | Synonyms: neutral state, independent state | Antonyms: allied nation, member state | noun
