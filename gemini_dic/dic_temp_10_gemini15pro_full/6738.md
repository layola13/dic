analysis:/əˈnæləsɪs/| n.|分析，解析|A detailed examination of something, typically as a basis for discussion or interpretation.|The analysis of the data revealed some surprising trends. 数据分析揭示了一些令人惊讶的趋势。|近义词：examination, study, investigation, assessment|反义词：synthesis|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Latin analysis, from Ancient Greek ἀνάλυσις (análysis, “a loosening up, a resolving”), from ἀναλύω (analýō, “I loosen up, resolve”), from ἀνά (aná, “up”) + λύω (lýō, “I loosen”).
