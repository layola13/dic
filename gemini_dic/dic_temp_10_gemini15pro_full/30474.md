centripetalisms: /ˌsɛntrɪˈpiːtəlɪzəmz/ | n. | 向心力；向心性；向心主义 | The tendency to unify or centralize, especially political power.  The forces or influences that unify a state or group.  A political or social system characterized by centralizing tendencies. | The government's policies fostered centripetalisms, drawing power away from the regions. 政府的政策促进了向心主义，将权力从地方收归中央。 |  The historian argued that the centripetalisms of the Roman Empire ultimately led to its downfall. 这位历史学家认为，罗马帝国的向心力最终导致了它的灭亡。 | synonyms: centralism, centralization, unification, integration | antonyms: centrifugalism, decentralization, fragmentation, disintegration | noun
