cold-blooded:/ˈkəʊldˈblʌdɪd/ /ˈkoʊldˈblʌdɪd/| adj.|冷血的，残酷的|Used to describe animals whose body temperature changes with the temperature of their surroundings.|例句：Snakes are cold-blooded animals. 蛇是冷血动物。

ETYMOLOGY->
From cold + blooded.  The literal meaning refers to animals whose blood temperature varies with the surrounding environment.  The figurative meaning developed from this, implying a lack of warm-blooded passion or emotion.
