antiparticles:/ˈæntiˌpɑːrtɪkəlz/
|n.|反粒子|A subatomic particle that has the same mass as another particle, but opposite electrical charge and other properties.  For example, the antiparticle of the electron is the positron.|Every particle of matter has a corresponding antiparticle. 每个物质粒子都有一个对应的反粒子。|Synonyms: counterpart, opposite number|Antonyms: particle|noun
