life-enhancing:/ˈlaɪf ɪnˈhænsɪŋ/| adj.|改善生活的，提高生活质量的|Improving the quality of life; making life better or more fulfilling.|例句：Regular exercise can be a life-enhancing activity.  规律的运动可以改善生活。|例句：The new medication proved to be life-enhancing for many patients.  这种新药被证明可以提高许多患者的生活质量。|近义词：beneficial, enriching, rewarding|反义词：detrimental, harmful|词性:形容词
词源:  "life" (Old English līf) + "enhancing" (present participle of enhance (from Old French enhaucer, meaning "to raise")).
