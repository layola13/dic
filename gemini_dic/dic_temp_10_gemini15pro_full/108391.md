lawful:/ˈlɔːfəl/ (US) /ˈlɔːfʊl/ (UK)| adj.|合法的，依法的|Allowed or recognized by law; conforming to the law. |例句：It's not lawful to drive without a license.  没有驾照开车是违法的。

例句：The police made a lawful arrest. 警方进行了合法逮捕。


近义词：legal, legitimate, licit|反义词：illegal, unlawful, illicit|词性: adjective
