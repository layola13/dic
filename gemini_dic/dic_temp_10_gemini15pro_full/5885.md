ambergris:/ˈæmbə(r)ɡriːs/, /ˈæmbərˌɡrɪs/ | n. |龙涎香|A waxy substance secreted by the sperm whale, used in perfumery as a fixative.|The perfumer prized the rare ambergris for its unique qualities.|近义词:fixative (as a perfume ingredient)|反义词:None (no direct opposite for a natural substance)|词性:Noun

Etymology: From Old French *ambre gris* "grey amber," from Arabic *ʿanbar* "ambergris" + French *gris* "grey."
