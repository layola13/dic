atic:/ˈætɪk/ | adj. | 阁楼的；顶楼的；斜屋顶的 |  Relating to or characteristic of an attic.  |  The attic room was dusty and full of old furniture. 阁楼房间里满是灰尘和旧家具。 |  The house has a large, unfinished attic space. 这房子有一个很大的，未完成的阁楼空间。 | 近义词: garret, loft | 反义词: basement, cellar | 词性: 形容词

词源: From French *atique*, from Latin *Atticus* “Athenian,” used in Latin *Attica aedes* “Attic style building," referring to a decorative story above the main cornice.
