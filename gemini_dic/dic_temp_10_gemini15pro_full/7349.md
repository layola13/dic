anidiomatic:/ˌænɪdiəˈmætɪk/
adj.
不符合语言习惯的，不合乎惯用法的
Not idiomatic; not in accordance with the particular character of a language.
His French was grammatically correct but somewhat anidiomatic. 他的法语语法正确，但有些不合乎惯用法。
近义词:unidiomatic, awkward, unnatural
反义词:idiomatic, natural, fluent
词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀν- (an-, “not, without”) + idiom + -atic.
