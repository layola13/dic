divis:/ˈdaɪvɪs/ |n.| 除数 |A divisor is a number that divides another number. | Example: In 10 / 2 = 5, 2 is the divisor. 在 10 / 2 = 5 中，2 是除数。|Example: The divisors of 12 are 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, and 12. 12 的除数是 1、2、3、4、6 和 12。|Synonyms: factor |Antonyms: multiple, dividend | Etymology: From Latin *dīvīsor*, agent noun from *dīvidere* "to divide." |
