negatively:/ˈneɡətɪvli/
adv.
消极地，负面地
in a way that expresses denial, disagreement, or refusal; in a way that is not hopeful or encouraging.
The proposal was negatively received by the board.  董事会对这项提议的反应是负面的。
She spoke negatively about her experience. 她对自己的经历评价很负面。
近义词:pessimistically, unfavorably, adversely
反义词:positively, favorably, affirmatively
词性:adverb
词源: From Latin *negātīvus*, from negāre (“to deny”).
