glasslike:/ˈɡlæsˌlaɪk/| adj.|像玻璃的；光滑的；透明的|Resembling glass in smoothness, transparency, or brittleness.|The lake was glasslike, reflecting the surrounding mountains.  湖面像玻璃一样，倒映着周围的山峦。|The frozen pond had a glasslike surface.  结冰的池塘表面光滑如镜。|近义词：glassy, vitreous, transparent, smooth, clear|反义词：opaque, rough, cloudy|词性:形容词
Etymology: glass + -like.  From "glass" (meaning the material) and the suffix "-like" (meaning resembling). 源自“glass”（表示材料）和后缀“-like”（表示类似）。
