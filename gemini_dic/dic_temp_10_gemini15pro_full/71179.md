fissureless:/ˈfɪʃər.ləs/| adj.|无裂缝的，无裂隙的|Without fissures; having no cracks or clefts.|The surgeon was pleased to find the bone fissureless after the accident.  外科医生很高兴地发现事故发生后骨头没有裂缝。|The gemstone was prized for its fissureless surface. 这颗宝石因其无裂缝的表面而备受珍视。|seamless, unbroken, intact, uncracked|fissured, cracked, cleft, split|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From fissure + -less.  "Fissure" comes from the Latin "fissura," meaning "a cleaving" or "a split."  The suffix "-less" is of Old English origin and means "without" or "lacking."
