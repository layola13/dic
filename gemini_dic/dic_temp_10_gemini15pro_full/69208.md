fear-monger: /ˈfɪrˌmʌŋɡər/ | n. | 恐怖散布者；危言耸听者 | A person who deliberately tries to create fear in others, especially by spreading frightening rumors or exaggerating dangers. | The fear-monger was spreading false information about the virus. 恐怖散布者正在散播关于病毒的虚假信息。 |  The politician was accused of being a fear-monger.  这位政治家被指控是一个危言耸听的人。 | synonyms: alarmist, scaremonger, doomsayer | antonyms: optimist, reassurer | noun

fear-mongering: /ˈfɪrˌmʌŋɡərɪŋ/ | n. | 散布恐惧；危言耸听 | The act of deliberately creating fear in others. | The media's fear-mongering about the economic crisis worsened the situation. 媒体对经济危机的危言耸听加剧了局势的恶化。 |  His fear-mongering tactics didn't work on the informed public. 他的危言耸听策略对知情的公众不起作用。 | synonyms: alarmism, scaremongering | antonyms: reassurance, optimism | noun
