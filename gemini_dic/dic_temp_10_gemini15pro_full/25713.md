bunodonts:/ˈbjuːnəˌdɑːnts/ | n. |丘齿兽；低冠齿兽|Animals, typically ungulates, possessing bunodont teeth, which are characterized by low, rounded cusps.  These teeth are adapted for crushing and grinding relatively soft vegetation. |Example: Bunodonts like pigs are omnivorous and their teeth reflect their varied diet. 丘齿兽，例如猪，是杂食动物，它们的牙齿反映了它们多样化的饮食。

Example:  Ancient bunodonts played an important role in the ecosystem. 古代丘齿兽在生态系统中扮演着重要的角色。|近义词:ungulates (a broader category) |反义词:hypsodonts |词性:noun
