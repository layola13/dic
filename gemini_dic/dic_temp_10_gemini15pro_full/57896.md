driftiest:/ˈdrɪftiɪst/
adjective
最漂流的；最随波逐流的
Most prone to drifting; most subject to being carried along by currents (of water, air, or opinion).
The driftiest boat in the harbor was eventually found washed up on the shore. 港口里最容易漂流的船最终被发现冲上了岸。
Synonyms: aimless, rootless, afloat, adrift, rambling
Antonyms: purposeful, directed, anchored, grounded
词性: 形容词

drift:/drɪft/
noun
漂流；趋势；大意
A gradual movement or change from one place or position to another; a general tendency; the general meaning of something.
The drift of the conversation was towards a compromise.  谈话的趋势是走向妥协。
Synonyms: tendency, trend, current, flow, direction
Antonyms: stability, stillness, fixity
词性: 名词

drift:/drɪft/
verb
漂流；漂泊；堆积
To be carried along by currents of water or air; to wander aimlessly; to accumulate in heaps.
Snow drifted against the walls of the house. 雪堆积在房子的墙壁上。
Synonyms: float, wander, roam, stray, accumulate
Antonyms: anchor, settle, remain, stay
词性: 动词
