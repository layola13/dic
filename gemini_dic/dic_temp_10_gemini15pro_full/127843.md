nemesis: /ˈnemɪsɪs/ (US) /ˈnemɪsɪs/ (UK)| n. |报应；天罚；劲敌；克星 |A source of harm or ruin; an unconquerable opponent or rival.  A person's nemesis is someone or something that is very difficult for them to defeat. |例句：The team finally beat their old nemesis in the championship game.  这个队终于在冠军赛中击败了他们的老对手。

例句：In the film, the superhero's nemesis is a brilliant but evil scientist.  在这部电影中，超级英雄的克星是一位才华横溢但邪恶的科学家。|近义词：rival, opponent, foe, adversary, bane|反义词：ally, friend, supporter|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek νέμεσις (némesis, “distribution, dispensation, divine retribution, indignation”), from νέμω (némō, “I distribute, allot, apportion”).
