bathorse:/ˈbæθɔːrs/| n.|浴马；浴架；洗澡用的马|A bathorse is a frame, typically made of wood or metal, placed in a bathtub to hold a baby or young child securely while bathing. It allows the caregiver to have both hands free to wash the child. |The baby loved splashing in the bathorse.  这个婴儿喜欢在浴架里玩水。| synonyms: bath seat, bath support, baby bather | antonyms: N/A | noun
