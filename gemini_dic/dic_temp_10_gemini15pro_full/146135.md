overemployments:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈplɔɪmənts/ | n. |过度就业，超额就业|A situation where more people are employed than are needed or can be supported by the available resources.  It can lead to inefficiency and reduced productivity.|The factory's overemployments led to layoffs within a year.  这家工厂的过度就业导致一年内裁员。|Overstaffing, surplus labor|Underemployment, labor shortage|noun
