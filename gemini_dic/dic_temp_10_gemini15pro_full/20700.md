bladesmith:/ˈbleɪdsmɪθ/
n.
刀匠，制刀匠
A person who makes blades, especially knives.
The bladesmith carefully hammered the steel into shape.  刀匠小心翼翼地把钢材锤打成形。
blacksmith, swordsmith, knifemaker
