A tightrope walker.
The funambulatory walked across the Niagara Falls on a tightrope.  走钢丝者在尼亚加拉瀑布上走钢丝。

词源:  From Latin *funambulus* (rope-walker), from *funis* (rope) + *ambulare* (to walk).
