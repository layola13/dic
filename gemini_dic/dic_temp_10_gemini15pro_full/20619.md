blackjacked:/ˈblækˌdʒækt/ | v. | 勒索，胁迫；以武力强迫；（在二十一点游戏中）击败庄家 | To extort or coerce by threat of force; to force into doing something against one's will. In the context of the card game blackjack, it means to beat the dealer by having a hand value of 21 or closer to 21 than the dealer without exceeding 21. |  The driver was blackjacked into driving to a remote location. 司机被胁迫开车到一个偏僻的地方。 | Synonyms: coerce, extort, force, compel, strong-arm | Antonyms: persuade, request, comply, yield | verb

blackjacked:/ˈblækˌdʒækt/ | adj. | 被勒索的，被胁迫的 |  Subjected to extortion or coercion. |  He felt blackjacked into signing the contract. 他感到被迫签署了合同。 | Synonyms: coerced, extorted, forced, compelled | Antonyms: willing, voluntary | adjective
