forbearance:/fɔːrˈbɛərəns/ /fɔːrˈberəns/| n. |忍耐，克制，宽容|Patient self-control; restraint and tolerance.|例句：He showed great forbearance in dealing with the difficult customer. 他在与难缠的顾客打交道时表现出了极大的耐心。

例句：The bank granted him forbearance on his loan repayments. 银行同意他延期偿还贷款。

近义词：patience, tolerance, restraint, leniency, indulgence|反义词：impatience, intolerance, harshness, severity|词性：名词

forbear:/fɔːrˈbɛər/ /fɔːrˈber/| v. |忍耐，克制，避免|To refrain from; to abstain.|例句：I will forbear from commenting on his behavior. 我将避免评论他的行为。

例句：She forbore to mention the incident to her parents. 她忍住没向父母提起这件事。

近义词：refrain, abstain, desist, avoid|反义词：indulge, engage in|词性：动词
