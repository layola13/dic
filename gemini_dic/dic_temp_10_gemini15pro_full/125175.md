multibuy:/ˈmʌltɪbaɪ/| n.|多件购买，批量购买|A purchase of several of the same items at a reduced price.  It encourages consumers to buy more of a product by offering a discount for buying in bulk.|例句：The supermarket had a multibuy offer on toilet paper – buy three packs for the price of two. 超市有卫生纸的多件购买优惠——买三包相当于两包的价格。

例句：She took advantage of the multibuy deal and stocked up on canned goods. 她利用多件购买的优惠，囤积了罐头食品。|近义词：bulk buy, quantity discount|反义词:single purchase|词性:noun
