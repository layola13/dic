freebootery:/ˈfriːˈbuːtəri/ (US), /ˈfriːˈbuːtəri/ (UK)| n. |掠夺行为；海盗行为；非法抢夺|The act or practice of freebooting, which is robbery or piracy, especially at sea.  It refers to the illegal seizure of goods or property.|Example 1: The freebootery of the pirates caused significant disruption to maritime trade. 海盗的掠夺行为严重扰乱了海上贸易。

Example 2:  The government vowed to crack down on the rampant freebootery in the region. 政府誓言要打击该地区猖獗的掠夺行为。|近义词：piracy, buccaneering, plundering, looting|反义词:lawfulness, legality|词性:noun

freebooter:/ˈfriːˈbuːtər/ (US), /ˈfriːˈbuːtər/ (UK)| n. |海盗；掠夺者|A person who engages in freebootery, typically a pirate or raider.|Example 1: The freebooter plundered the coastal villages, taking whatever valuables they could find.  海盗掠夺了沿海村庄，拿走了他们能找到的任何值钱的东西。

Example 2: The infamous freebooter terrorized the shipping lanes for years.  臭名昭著的海盗恐吓了航线多年。|近义词：pirate, buccaneer, raider, plunderer|反义词:lawman, police officer|词性:noun
