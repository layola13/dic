nonspectacular:/ˌnɒnspɛkˈtækjʊlə(r)/ /ˌnɑːnspɛkˈtækjələr/
adjective
不引人注目的，平凡的
Not spectacular; ordinary or unimpressive.
The scenery was pleasant but nonspectacular.  风景宜人，但并不引人注目。
The team's performance was nonspectacular. 该队的表现并不出色。
近义词:ordinary, unimpressive, mundane, average, mediocre
反义词:spectacular, impressive, remarkable, extraordinary
词性:adjective
