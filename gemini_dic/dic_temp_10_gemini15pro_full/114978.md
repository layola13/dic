mancipate: /ˈmænsɪpeɪt/ | vt. | 解放，释放（尤指从奴隶制、束缚或压迫中） | To free from slavery, bondage, or oppression. |  The slaves were mancipated after the Civil War. 内战后奴隶们被解放了。

The new law mancipated women from oppressive social customs. 新法律将妇女从压迫性的社会习俗中解放出来。 | 近义词: liberate, free, release, emancipate | 反义词: enslave, imprison, confine, restrain | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Latin *mancipātus, past participle of mancipāre (“to take possession of, transfer ownership of, sell”), from manus (“hand”) + capere (“to take”).
