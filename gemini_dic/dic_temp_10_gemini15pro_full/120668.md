misadaptations:/ˌmɪsædæpˈteɪʃənz/| n. | 适应不良；不适应性|Traits or behaviors that are not well-suited to the current environment and hinder survival or reproduction.  It's the opposite of adaptations.|The polar bear's thick fur, a useful adaptation in the Arctic, becomes a misadaptation in a warmer climate.| 近义词: maladaptations, inaptations| 反义词: adaptations | 词性: noun
