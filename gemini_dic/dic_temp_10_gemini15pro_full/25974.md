bursa:/ˈbɜːrsə/ (US) /ˈbɜːsə/ (UK)| n. |囊；滑囊；交易所|A small fluid-filled sac or saclike cavity, especially one countering friction at a joint.|The inflammation of the bursa in his shoulder made it difficult to lift his arm. 他肩膀滑囊发炎，抬胳膊都困难。|Synonyms: sac, pouch, cavity|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical structure)|noun

bursa:/ˈbɜːrsə/ (US) /ˈbɜːsə/ (UK)| n. |交易所；证券交易所|(especially in some European countries) a stock exchange.|The Frankfurt bursa is one of the largest in Europe. 法兰克福证券交易所是欧洲最大的交易所之一。|Synonyms: stock exchange, exchange, market|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for an institution)|noun
