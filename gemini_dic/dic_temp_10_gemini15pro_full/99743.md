intermazes:/ˌɪntərˈmeɪzɪz/ | v. | 使…错综复杂；使…迷惑 | To make intricate or confusing; to entangle or bewilder.  | The twisting paths intermazed the travelers, leaving them lost and disoriented.  蜿蜒的小路使游客们迷失了方向，让他们迷失了方向。| The dense jungle intermazed the soldiers, making it difficult for them to advance. 茂密的丛林使士兵们迷失了方向，使他们难以前进。| 近义词: entangle, complicate, confuse, bewilder | 反义词: clarify, simplify, disentangle | 词性: 动词

词源:  "Intermaze" is a combination of the prefix "inter-" (meaning "between, among, mutually") and the verb "maze" (meaning "to amaze, bewilder, confuse"). The word "maze" itself ultimately derives from the Old English word "amasian," meaning "to confuse, confound."
