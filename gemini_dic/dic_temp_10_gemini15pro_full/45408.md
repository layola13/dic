cupolaed:/ˈkjuːpələd/ | adj. |  有圆屋顶的；圆顶状的 | Having a cupola or dome.  Resembling a cupola or dome. | The cupolaed roof of the church was visible for miles. 教堂的圆顶屋顶数英里外都能看到。| The building was cupolaed, giving it a majestic appearance. 这座建筑有圆顶，使其显得雄伟壮观。 | synonyms: domed, vaulted, crowned | antonyms: flat, planar | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From cupola + -ed.  Cupola comes from Italian cupola, meaning "small dome," ultimately from Late Latin cupula, diminutive of cupa "tub, cask."
