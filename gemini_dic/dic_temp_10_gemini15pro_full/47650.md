debonairness:/ˌdebəˈnɛrnəs/
noun
儒雅；温文尔雅；落落大方
A sophisticated and charming manner; urbane and affable.
He exuded an air of debonairness that charmed everyone in the room. 他身上散发出的儒雅气质迷住了房间里的每个人。
Synonyms: sophistication, charm, elegance, grace, polish, suavity, urbanity
Antonyms: awkwardness, clumsiness, gaucheness, uncouthness
