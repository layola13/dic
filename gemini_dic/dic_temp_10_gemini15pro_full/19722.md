bilateralism:/ˌbaɪˈlætərəlɪzəm/
noun
双边主义
A system of policies or agreements based on the mutual agreement of two countries.
Bilateralism has become increasingly important in international relations. 双边主义在国际关系中变得越来越重要。
The two countries signed a bilateral trade agreement.  两国签署了一项双边贸易协定。
近义词：two-sidedness, duality
反义词：multilateralism, unilateralism
词性：n.
