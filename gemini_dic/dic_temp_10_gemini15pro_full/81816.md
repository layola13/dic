gretto:/ˈɡrɛtoʊ/ | adj. | 粗糙的，沙砾的 | Covered with grit or small stones; gravelly. | The path was gretto and difficult to walk on. 这条小路很粗糙，很难走。 |  The gretto surface of the sandpaper made it effective. 砂纸粗糙的表面使它很有效。 | 近义词: gravelly, gritty, rough, coarse | 反义词: smooth, fine | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Italian "gretto" (narrow, tight), from Latin "strictus" (drawn tight).  Originally used to describe narrow streets paved with rough stones, the meaning shifted to focus on the texture of the paving itself.
