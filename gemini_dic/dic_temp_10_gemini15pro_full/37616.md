communionable:/kəˈmjuːnɪkəbl/ | adj. |可传染的；可交流的；可共有的|Capable of being transmitted from one person to another, especially of diseases; also, capable of being shared or communicated.|例句：Measles is a highly communionable disease. 麻疹是一种高度传染性疾病。

例句：They shared a communionable sense of joy. 他们共享着一种共通的喜悦之情。|近义词：contagious, infectious, transmissible (for diseases); sharable (for qualities) |反义词:non-communicable, noncontagious|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *communionabilis*, from communionem (nominative communio) "sharing, fellowship, participation," from communis "common" (see common (adj.)).  The sense of "capable of being communicated" is attested by 1540s.
