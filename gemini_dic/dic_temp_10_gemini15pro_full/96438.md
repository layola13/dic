indistinguishable:/ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbl/| adj.|难以区分的，无法辨别的|Not able to be identified as different or distinct.|The twins are so alike they are virtually indistinguishable.|Synonyms: identical, alike, similar, twin, matching|Antonyms: distinguishable, different, distinct, dissimilar|adjective

indistinguishable:/ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbl/| adj.|不能识别的|Not able to be identified or recognized.|Her voice on the phone was indistinguishable from her sister's.|Synonyms: unrecognizable, unidentifiable|Antonyms: recognizable, identifiable|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + distinguishable (able to be distinguished).  Distinguishable comes from the Old French distinguer (to distinguish), ultimately from the Latin distinguere (to separate, distinguish).
