asphyxiates:/əsˈfɪksieɪts/
verb
使窒息，使憋死
deprives of oxygen, causing unconsciousness or death; suffocates.
The fire produced toxic fumes that asphyxiated several people. 大火产生了有毒烟雾，导致数人窒息而死。

近义词:suffocates, smothers, chokes
反义词:resuscitates, revives

asphyxiate:/əsˈfɪksieɪt/
noun
窒息物
something that causes asphyxia.
Carbon monoxide is a common asphyxiate. 一氧化碳是一种常见的窒息物。

近义词:choking agent, suffocant
反义词: N/A

asphyxiate:/əsˈfɪksieɪt/
adjective
窒息的
(of a condition) caused by asphyxia.
asphyxiate death. 窒息死亡。

近义词:suffocating
反义词: N/A


词性: v./n./adj.
词源: From French asphyxier, from Greek asphyxia ‘stopping of the pulse,’ from a- ‘without’ + sphyxis ‘pulse.’
