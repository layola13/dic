benumbed:/bɪˈnʌmd/| adj.|麻木的，失去知觉的|Deprived of physical sensation or the power to feel or move normally; made numb with cold, shock, etc.  Emotionally or mentally stunned or unresponsive.|例句：My fingers were benumbed with cold. 我的手指冻僵了。

例句：He was benumbed by grief after his wife's death.  妻子去世后，他悲痛欲绝。|近义词：numb, insensible, stunned, dazed, paralyzed|反义词：sensitive, alert, aware|词性:形容词

词源: From be- + numb.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "thoroughly," intensifying the meaning of "numb." "Numb" comes from Old English *numen, past participle of *niman "to take," related to German nehmen "to take." The original sense was likely "taken away" (in terms of sensation).
