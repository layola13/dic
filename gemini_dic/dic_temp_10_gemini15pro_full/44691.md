crudding:/ˈkrʌdɪŋ/
adjective|粗糙的；粗劣的；未加工的|Relating to or consisting of crude materials or matter; unrefined.|The crudding oil was thick and black.|近义词:raw, unrefined, unprocessed|反义词:refined, processed|词性:形容词

crudding:/ˈkrʌdɪŋ/
noun|凝结；凝块|The process of forming curds or clots.|The crudding of the milk was a sign it had gone bad.|近义词:coagulation, curdling|反义词:liquefaction|词性:名词

词源: From "crud" + "-ing," where "crud" is likely a back-formation from "crudy" (dirty, covered in crud), which itself is of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to "curd."
