discommodity:/ˌdɪskəˈmɑːdəti/ | n. | 不便之处；缺点；劣势；滞销商品 | something that is inconvenient or disadvantageous; a product that is difficult to sell |  The lack of public transport is a major discommoditiy of living in the countryside.  在乡村生活，缺乏公共交通是一个主要的不便之处。| 近义词：disadvantage, drawback, inconvenience, handicap, impediment, burden, nuisance, problem, trouble, difficulty | 反义词: advantage, benefit, convenience, asset | 词性: 名词

discommodity:/ˌdɪskəˈmɑːdəti/ | n. | 商品过剩；销售困难 | a surplus of goods; difficulty in selling goods | The current discommoditiy of wheat has led to a drop in prices. 目前小麦的过剩导致价格下跌。 | 近义词：glut, surplus, oversupply | 反义词: shortage, scarcity, deficit | 词性: 名词
