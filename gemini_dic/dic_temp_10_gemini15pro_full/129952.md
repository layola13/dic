non-market:/ˌnɒnˈmɑːkɪt/ | adj. | 非市场的 | Not related to or determined by the market; not bought or sold in the open market. |  例句：Non-market goods like clean air and water are essential for life.  非市场商品，如清洁的空气和水，对生命至关重要。

|例句：The government often provides non-market services such as education and healthcare. 政府通常提供非市场服务，如教育和医疗保健。

|近义词：non-commercial, public, social |反义词: market, commercial, private |词性: adjective
