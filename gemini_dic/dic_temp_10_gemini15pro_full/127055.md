nat:/næt/
n.
nit.
A unit of information or entropy.
A nat is a unit of information or entropy, based on natural logarithms and the number *e*, rather than powers of 2 which form the basis of the more familiar shannon or bit.
One nat is the amount of information gained by observing an event of probability 1/e.
近义词: bit, shannon
反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)
词性: noun
