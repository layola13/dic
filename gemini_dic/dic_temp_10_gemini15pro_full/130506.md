nonaccidentalnesses:/ˌnɒnˌæksɪˈdɛntəlnəsɪz/
n. |非偶然性；故意| The high frequency of nonaccidental injuries in young children is a serious public health concern. 幼儿非意外伤害的高发率是一个严重的公共卫生问题。|This term refers to the quality of not being accidental, often used in the context of child abuse.  这个术语指的是非偶然的性质，常用于虐待儿童的语境中。|例句：The nonaccidentalnesses of the injuries suggested foul play.  这些伤口的非偶然性表明有谋杀的嫌疑。

例句：The authorities investigated the nonaccidentalnesses of the child's death. 当局调查了孩子死亡的非偶然性。


近义词：intentionality, deliberateness, purposefulness
反义词：accidentality, randomness
词性：noun
