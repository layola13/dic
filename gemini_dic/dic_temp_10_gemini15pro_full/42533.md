countermanifesto: /ˌkaʊntərˈmænɪˈfɛstoʊ/ /ˌkaʊntərˈmænɪˈfɛstəʊ/ | n. | 反宣言，反对声明 | A public declaration, usually political, in direct opposition to a previous declaration.  It challenges the principles or policies outlined in the original manifesto. | The artist released a countermanifesto rejecting the commercialization of art.  这位艺术家发表了一份反宣言，反对艺术商业化。 | synonyms: rebuttal, denunciation, repudiation | antonyms: manifesto, platform, declaration | noun
