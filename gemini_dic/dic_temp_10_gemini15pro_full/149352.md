pachydermatous:/ˌpækɪˈdəːmətəs/| adj.| 厚皮类的；迟钝的；麻木不仁的|Having very thick skin; insensitive; unfeeling.|The pachydermatous rhinoceros barely flinched at the sting of the insect. 厚皮犀牛对昆虫的叮咬几乎没有反应。|Synonyms: thick-skinned, insensitive, impassive, callous|Antonyms: sensitive, thin-skinned, compassionate, empathetic|adjective

pachyderm:/ˈpækɪdəːm/| n.| 厚皮动物（尤指象、犀牛、河马）|A very large mammal with thick skin, especially an elephant, rhinoceros, or hippopotamus.|Elephants are pachyderms. 大象是厚皮动物。|Synonyms: elephant, rhinoceros, hippopotamus|Antonyms: N/A|noun
