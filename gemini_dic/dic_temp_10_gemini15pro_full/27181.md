calligraphist: /kəˈlɪɡrəfɪst/
n.
书法家
A person who produces beautiful handwriting using special pens or brushes.
The calligraphist spent hours perfecting each stroke of the character.  这位书法家花费数小时完善每个字的笔画。
The calligraphist's work was admired for its elegance and precision. 这位书法家的作品因其优雅和精确而备受赞赏。

近义词:scribe, penman, letterer
反义词: (无明显的反义词)
词性: noun
