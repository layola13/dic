overornamental: /ˌoʊvərˌɔːrnəˈmɛntəl/ | adj. | 过于装饰的，装饰过分的 | Excessively ornamented; decorated to an excessive degree. | The architecture was overornamental, bordering on gaudy.  这栋建筑装饰过度，近乎俗丽。 | The cake was overornamental, with too many flowers and figurines. 这个蛋糕装饰过度，有太多的花朵和小雕像。 | 近义词：ostentatious, flamboyant, excessive, elaborate | 反义词：simple, plain, understated, minimalist | 词性: 形容词

词源: over- + ornamental

over-:  prefix meaning "too much," "excessively," "above," "beyond" 表示“太多”、“过度”、“在…之上”、“超越”

ornamental:  from ornament + -al  来自 ornament（装饰品）+ -al（形容词后缀）
