hoisler:/ˈhɔɪslər/ | n. | 吊车操作员，起重机操作员 | A person who operates a hoist or crane.  A skilled worker responsible for lifting and moving heavy loads using a hoist or crane. |  The hoisler carefully lifted the steel beam into place. 吊车操作员小心翼翼地将钢梁吊到位。

The hoisler had years of experience operating heavy machinery.  这位起重机操作员有多年的重型机械操作经验。 | 近义词: crane operator, hoist operator | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
