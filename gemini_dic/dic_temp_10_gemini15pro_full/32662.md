chitterlings:/ˈtʃɪtərlɪŋz/
n.
Chitlins, Chitterlings
The small intestines of a pig, prepared as food.
Chitterlings are a traditional dish in some cultures. 猪小肠是一些文化中的传统菜肴。
My grandmother used to make chitterlings every Christmas. 我祖母过去每年圣诞节都做猪小肠。
Synonyms: intestines, guts, tripe
Antonyms: None
