agbada:/ɑːɡˈbɑːdə/
n.
长袍，大袍，阿格巴达(西非Yoruba, Igbo等民族的男性传统服装)
A flowing wide-sleeved robe worn by men in some West African cultures, particularly among the Yoruba and Igbo peoples.
例句：He looked regal in his agbada. 他穿着阿格巴达，看起来很威严。

例句：The agbada is often worn for formal occasions.  阿格巴达通常在正式场合穿着。


近义词：robe, gown
反义词：(None -  No direct opposite for a specific garment)
词性:noun
