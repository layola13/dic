overcommending:/ˌoʊvər.kəˈmɛn.dɪŋ/
| adj. | 过度称赞的; 过分推荐的 | Praising or recommending something excessively, beyond what is warranted or deserved. |  He was criticized for overcommending a mediocre product. 他因过度称赞一件平庸的产品而受到批评。| synonyms: overpraising, exaggerating, gushing | antonyms: underpraising, downplaying, criticizing | adjective


overcommending:/ˌoʊvər.kəˈmɛn.dɪŋ/
| v. | 过度称赞; 过分推荐 | The act of praising or recommending something excessively. |  Stop overcommending him; it's going to his head. 别再过度称赞他了，他会骄傲自满的。 | synonyms: overpraising, exaggerating, gushing | antonyms: underpraising, downplaying, criticizing | verb


词源:
"Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "commending" (present participle of "commend," from Latin "commendare," meaning "to entrust, praise").
