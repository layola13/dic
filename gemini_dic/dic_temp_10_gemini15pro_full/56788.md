docket:/ˈdɑːkɪt/ /ˈdɒkɪt/| n. vt.|摘要；标签；（待办事项的）清单；正式记载|A docket is a summary or list of cases for trial or people having cases pending, or a label attached to goods containing details of their destination and contents.  A docket can also be a document or label listing the contents of a package or consignment.|
例句：The judge reviewed the docket before the hearing.  法官在听证会前审阅了案件摘要。

例句：The package arrived with a docket clearly marked with the sender's address. 包裹到达时附有一个标签，上面清楚地标明了寄件人的地址。|近义词：list, agenda, schedule, summary, label, tag|反义词: N/A |词性:noun, verb


docket:/ˈdɑːkɪt/ /ˈdɒkɪt/| vt.|将…列入摘要；加标签|To docket means to enter (a case or suit) onto a docket; to make a summary of, or to mark or label (a package, etc) with a docket. |
例句：The clerk docketed the new case. 书记员将新案件列入了摘要。

例句：The warehouse worker docketed each box before shipping. 仓库工人装运前给每个箱子都贴上了标签。|近义词：list, record, register, label, tag|反义词: N/A |词性:verb
