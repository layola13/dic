anti-physical:/ˌæntiˈfɪzɪkəl/
| adj. | 反物质的; 非物质的; 超自然的; 精神上的 | Opposed to or rejecting the physical or material world; spiritual, metaphysical.  Focusing on the mind or spirit rather than the physical body. |  His beliefs are very anti-physical, prioritizing spiritual enlightenment over bodily needs. 他非常反物质，重视精神启蒙胜过身体需求。|  The guru preached an anti-physical lifestyle, encouraging his followers to fast for extended periods.  这位大师宣扬一种反物质的生活方式，鼓励他的追随者长时间禁食。 | spiritual, metaphysical, immaterial, incorporeal | physical, material, corporeal | adjective
