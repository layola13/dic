collingly: /ˈkɒlɪŋli/ (UK) /ˈkoʊlɪŋli/ (US) | adv. | 冷淡地，冷漠地 | In a manner that is cold and distant, lacking warmth or friendliness. | She looked at him collingly and turned away. 她冷漠地看了他一眼，然后转过身去。| He responded collingly to her enthusiastic greeting.  他对她的热情问候反应冷淡。 | 近义词：coldly, distantly, aloofly, frostily | 反义词：warmly, affectionately, cordially | 词性: 副词
