gratuitous:/ɡrəˈtuːɪtəs/
adjective
免费的；无理由的；无端的
given or done free of charge; uncalled for; lacking good reason; unwarranted.
She made a gratuitous remark about his appearance. 她对他的外貌作出了无端的评论。
Synonyms: unnecessary, unwarranted, needless, unprovoked
Antonyms: necessary, essential, justified, deserved
词性: 形容词

gratuitous:/ɡrəˈtuːɪtəs/
adjective
多余的
superfluous
The film was criticized for its gratuitous violence. 这部电影因其多余的暴力场面而受到批评。
Synonyms: excessive, superfluous, redundant
Antonyms: necessary, essential
词性: 形容词
