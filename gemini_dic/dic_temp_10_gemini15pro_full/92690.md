icebox:/ˈaɪsbɑːks/ (US) /ˈaɪsbɒks/ (UK)| n.|冰箱|A box or cupboard, often insulated, used to store ice for keeping food cold, especially before the invention of refrigerators.  A refrigerator.|例句：We kept milk and butter in the icebox. 我们把牛奶和黄油放在冰箱里。

例句：The icebox was filled with leftovers from Sunday dinner. 冰箱里装满了周日晚餐的剩菜。|近义词：refrigerator, fridge, cooler|反义词:oven, stove|词性:noun
