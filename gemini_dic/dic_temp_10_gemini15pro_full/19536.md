bifocals: /baɪˈfoʊkəlz/ | n. | 双光眼镜 | Eyeglasses with lenses that have two different sections for seeing at different distances. The upper part is for distance vision and the lower part is for near vision. | She needs bifocals to read and drive. 她需要双光眼镜才能阅读和开车。| My grandmother started wearing bifocals when she turned sixty. 我祖母六十岁时开始戴双光眼镜。 | 近义词: multifocal lenses | 反义词: single-vision lenses | 词性: noun

词源: bi- (two) + focal (related to the focus of the eye)
