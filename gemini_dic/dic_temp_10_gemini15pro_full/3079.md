ETYMOLOGY->
From French *aérométrie*, from Ancient Greek *ἀήρ* (aḗr, “air”) + *-μετρία* (-metría, “measurement”).

USAGE->
The principles of aerometry are crucial for understanding weather patterns.  空气测量学的原理对于理解天气模式至关重要。

Aerometry plays a significant role in the design of aircraft. 空气测量学在飞机设计中起着重要作用。
