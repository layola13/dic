montanic: /mɒnˈtænɪk/ | adj. | 蒙旦蜡的，褐煤蜡的 | Relating to or derived from montan wax, a hard wax obtained from lignite. | Montanic acid is a component of montan wax. 蒙旦酸是蒙旦蜡的一种成分。 |  Montanic deposits are found in certain types of coal. 在某些类型的煤中发现了蒙旦蜡沉积物。 | 近义词: lignite wax (褐煤蜡) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

词源: From German "Montanwachs" (montan wax), from "Montan" (mining, mountainous) + "Wachs" (wax).
