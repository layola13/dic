heathiest:/ˈhɛlθiɪst/| adj.|最健康的|Superlative form of healthy; in the best physical condition.|She chose the healthiest option on the menu. 她选择了菜单上最健康的选择。|例句：This is the healthiest meal I've had all week. 这是我一周以来吃得最健康的一餐。|近义词：fittest, well, robust|反义词：unhealthiest, sickest, weakest|词性:形容词

health:/hɛlθ/| n.|健康|The state of being free from illness or injury.|Good health is essential for a happy life. 健康的身体对于幸福的生活至关重要。|例句：He is in good health. 他身体健康。|近义词：wellness, fitness|反义词：sickness, illness|词性:名词

词源：From Old English *hǣlþ (attested only in derivatives), from Proto-Germanic *hailiþō, from Proto-Indo-European *kail- (“whole, uninjured, of good omen”). Cognate with Old Saxon hēlitho, Old Frisian hēlith (“health”), Dutch heil (“health, salvation”), German Heil (“health, salvation”), Old Norse heill (“healthy”), Gothic 𐌷𐌰𐌹𐌻𐍃 (hails, “whole, sound”).  The superlative suffix "-est" comes from Old English "-est", ultimately from Proto-Germanic *-istaz.
