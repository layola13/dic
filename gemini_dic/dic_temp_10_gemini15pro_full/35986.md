codeshare:/ˈkoʊdʃɛr/| n. |代码共享；联合经营航班|An arrangement where two or more airlines share the same flight.  A flight operated by one airline that is also marketed by another airline.|例句：I booked a codeshare flight on United, but it was actually operated by Lufthansa. 我在美联航预订了一个代码共享航班，但实际上是由汉莎航空运营的。

例句：The codeshare agreement allows passengers to earn miles on either airline. 代码共享协议允许乘客在任何一家航空公司赚取里程。|近义词：joint venture, alliance, partnership|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
