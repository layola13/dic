immensest:/ɪˈmɛnsɪst/| adj.|最大的，最广阔的|superlative adjective of immense; extremely large or great|The immensest ocean is the Pacific.  太平洋是最大的海洋。|The universe holds immensest mysteries. 宇宙蕴藏着最大的奥秘。|近义词：vastest, hugest, gigantic, enormous|反义词:smallest, tiniest, minute|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle French *immensif*, from Latin *immensus* "immeasurable," from *in-* "not" + *mensus* "measured," past participle of *metiri* "to measure."
