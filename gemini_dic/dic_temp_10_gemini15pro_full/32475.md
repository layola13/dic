chincha:/ˈtʃɪntʃə/ | n. |亲切的称呼;宝贝;亲爱的 (主要用于秘鲁)| A term of endearment, similar to "honey" or "dear," primarily used in Peru. | Ejemplo: ¡Chincha, te quiero mucho!  Honey, I love you very much! | Synonym: dear, honey, sweetie, love | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a term of endearment) | Noun
