nonintrusively:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtruːsɪvli/ /ˌnɑːnɪnˈtruːsɪvli/
adverb
非侵入式地，不引人注目地
in a way that does not attract attention to itself or cause annoyance or disruption.
The software updates nonintrusively in the background.  该软件在后台非侵入式地更新。
Researchers observed the animals nonintrusively using hidden cameras.  研究人员使用隐藏的摄像头非侵入式地观察动物。
近义词: discreetly, subtly, unobtrusively
反义词: intrusively, obtrusively, conspicuously
词性: adverb
