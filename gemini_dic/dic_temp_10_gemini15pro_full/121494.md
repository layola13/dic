misleadingly:/mɪsˈliːdɪŋli/| adv.|误导地，容易引起误解地|In a way that is likely to cause someone to have a wrong idea or impression. |The graph was misleadingly labelled, making it appear that profits had increased significantly.  图表上的标签具有误导性，使利润看起来大幅增长。|Synonym: deceptively, falsely, erroneously|Antonym: accurately, truthfully, correctly|词性:副词
