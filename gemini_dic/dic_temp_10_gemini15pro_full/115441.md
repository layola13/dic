mantra:/ˈmæntrə/ | n. | 咒语，真言；准则；口号 | A word or sound repeated to aid concentration in meditation. A statement or slogan repeated frequently. |  She repeated her mantra, "I will succeed." 她重复着她的咒语：“我会成功的。” |  She chants mantras every morning as part of her spiritual practice. 她每天早上念诵咒语作为她精神修行的一部分。 | synonym: chant, slogan, creed, motto, axiom  | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "curse" in some contexts) | noun
