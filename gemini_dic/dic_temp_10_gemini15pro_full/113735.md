macroeconomist: /ˌmækroʊɪˈkɑːnəmɪst/ | n. | 宏观经济学家 | An economist who specializes in the study of the overall aspects and workings of a national economy, such as income, output, and the interrelationships among diverse economic sectors. |  The macroeconomist predicted a recession.  宏观经济学家预测经济衰退。

| 近义词: economist | 反义词: microeconomist | 词性: noun
