beseek:/bɪˈsiːk/| vt.|恳求，乞求|To earnestly or humbly request; to implore.|例句：The refugees beseeched the government for asylum.  难民恳求政府给予庇护。

例句：She beseeched him to forgive her. 她恳求他原谅她。|近义词：implore, entreat, supplicate, beg, plead|反义词:demand, command|词性:动词
