arrangeable:/əˈreɪndʒəbl/
adj.
可安排的，可整理的，可商定的
Able to be arranged; capable of being put in order or settled.
The meeting is arrangeable next week.  会议可以安排在下周举行。
The flowers are arrangeable in a vase.  这些花可以插在花瓶里。
近义词: manageable, organizable, orderable, adaptable, flexible
反义词: unmanageable, inflexible, fixed, unchangeable
词性: adjective
词源: From arrange + -able.  来自动词arrange加上后缀-able.
