axillary: /ˈæksɪləri/ (US), /ækˈsɪləri/ (UK)| adj. n.|腋窝的，腋生的；腋窝|Relating to the armpit; of or pertaining to the axilla (armpit). A lymph node located in the armpit.|The axillary artery supplies blood to the arm. 腋动脉为手臂供血。|The doctor checked the patient's axillary lymph nodes. 医生检查了病人的腋窝淋巴结。|近义词: underarm|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider terms referring to other body parts, e.g., inguinal)|词性: adjective, noun
