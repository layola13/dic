alomancy:/ˈæləˌmænsi/ | n. | 金属操控术 | A fictional magic system in Brandon Sanderson's *Mistborn* series, where users burn ingested metals to gain supernatural abilities. | Vin burned pewter, enhancing her strength and speed.  文恩燃烧锡，增强了她的力量和速度。 |  Near synonyms: metalbending, feruchemy (within the *Mistborn* universe) | Antonyms: None | noun
