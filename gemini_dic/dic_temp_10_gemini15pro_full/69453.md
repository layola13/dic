feignedly:/ˈfeɪnɪdli/
adverb
假装地，虚伪地
in a feigned manner; pretendingly.  The action or emotion is not genuine.
She feignedly smiled at him, hiding her true feelings of anger. 她假装对他微笑，掩饰了她真正的愤怒。
He feignedly agreed to the proposal, planning to sabotage it later. 他假装同意这个提议，计划稍后进行破坏。
近义词:pretendedly, falsely, deceptively, insincerely
反义词:genuinely, sincerely, truly, honestly
词性:adverb

词源:
From feign (v.) + -ed (suffix forming past participles) + -ly (suffix forming adverbs).  Feign comes from Old French feindre, from Latin fingere "to shape, form, invent, feign."
