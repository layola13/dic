nightclubbers:/ˈnaɪtklʌbərz/
n.
常去夜总会的人；夜店咖
People who frequent nightclubs; regular patrons of nightclubs.
例句：The nightclubbers danced until dawn.  夜店咖们跳舞跳到天亮。

例句：The nightclubbers were dressed in their finest clothes. 夜店咖们穿着他们最漂亮的衣服。

近义词：clubbers, partygoers, revelers
反义词：homebodies, introverts
词性：名词
