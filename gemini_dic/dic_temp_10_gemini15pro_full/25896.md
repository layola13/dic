burly:/ˈbɜːrli/ | adj. | 魁梧的，健壮的 | Large and strong; heavily built. |  The burly lumberjack easily lifted the heavy log. 魁梧的伐木工人轻松地举起了沉重的原木。| synonyms: husky, brawny, muscular, sturdy, robust | antonyms: slight, thin, weak, frail | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *borlich, from Old English *burlic (attested only in compounds such as *ceorlburlic 'churl-like, rustic'), equivalent to *bur 'bower, dwelling' +‎ -ly. More at bower. The sense development is uncertain; compare German *bäurisch 'rustic', from Bauer 'farmer'.  The current meaning developed by the 16th century.
