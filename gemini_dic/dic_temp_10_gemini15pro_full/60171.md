effervescent:/ˌɛfərˈvɛsənt/| adj.|兴奋的，活跃的，冒泡的|She has an effervescent personality. 她性格活泼。|例句：Her effervescent spirit brightened the room. 她活泼的精神照亮了整个房间。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *effervescens, present participle of effervescere (“to boil up, boil over”), from ex- (“out of, from”) + fervescere (“to begin to boil, grow hot”), from fervere (“to boil, glow”).
