ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *lymphoma, from lymph + -oma.  "Lymph" comes from Latin *lympha, meaning "water," related to Greek *nymphe, also meaning "water, nymph." "-oma" is a Greek suffix meaning "tumor" or "swelling."
