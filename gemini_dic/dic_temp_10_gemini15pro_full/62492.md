endostyle:/ˈɛndoʊstaɪl/
n.|内柱|A glandular groove in the floor of the pharynx of lower chordates, such as tunicates and lancelets, that secretes mucus to trap food particles. It is considered homologous to the thyroid gland in vertebrates.|The endostyle of the lancelet is responsible for filtering food particles from the water.|近义词:hypobranchial groove|反义词:|词性:noun

endostyle:/ˈɛndoʊstaɪl/
noun|内柱|A longitudinal ciliated groove on the ventral wall of the pharynx which produces mucus to gather food particles.  It is a characteristic of urochordates and cephalochordates, and is homologous with the thyroid gland in vertebrates.|The endostyle plays a crucial role in the lancelet's filter feeding mechanism.|近义词:hypobranchial groove|反义词: |词性:noun
