adios:/ˌɑːdɪˈoʊs/ /ˌædiˈoʊs/| interjection |再见，告别|Used as a farewell remark.  A Spanish word used in English.|例句：Adios, amigos! 再见，朋友们！

Adios, I'm off to the beach. 再见，我要去海滩了。|近义词：goodbye, farewell, so long, see you later, bye|反义词: hello, welcome|词性:interjection

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *adiós*, from the phrase *a Dios (vos encomiendo)* "to God (I commend you)".

USAGE->
It's primarily used as a standalone interjection.

Adios! See you next week! 再见！下周见！

Adios, it was nice meeting you. 再见，很高兴见到你。


PHRASE->
While "adios" is usually standalone, it can be part of longer phrases like:

"Adios and good riddance!"  再见，一路走好！（表示不再想见到对方）

"Say adios to your worries."  和你的烦恼说再见吧。
