godot:/ˈɡɒdoʊ/ (US) /ˈɡɒdəʊ/ (UK)| n.|戈多（人名，《等待戈多》中的角色）; 戈多（象征虚无缥缈的希望或救星）|A fictional character in Samuel Beckett's play "Waiting for Godot"; often used metaphorically to represent something that is endlessly awaited but never arrives.|例句：They waited for Godot, but he never came. 他们等待戈多，但他从未出现。

|例句：The promised funding is our Godot; we keep waiting, but it never materializes.  承诺的资金就是我们的戈多；我们一直在等待，但它从未实现。|近义词：savior, hope (when used metaphorically)|反义词:certainty, reality (when used metaphorically)|词性:noun
