ambulacrum:/ˌæmbjʊˈleɪkrəm/ | n. | 步道，走廊，通道 | A place for walking, especially an aisle in a church or a covered walkway. |  The monks walked slowly through the ambulacrum, contemplating their scriptures. 修士们在走廊里慢慢地走着，思考着他们的经文。

The museum's ambulacrum offered a stunning view of the city.  博物馆的走廊提供了一个令人惊叹的城市景观。 | Synonyms: walkway, passageway, aisle, corridor | Antonyms: barrier, obstacle | noun
