airheads:/ˈɛrˌhɛdz/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A person who is not very intelligent; a scatterbrain.|例句：Those airheads forgot to bring the map! 那些笨蛋忘记带地图了！|例句：He acted like such an airhead when he locked his keys in the car. 他把钥匙锁在车里的时候表现得像个傻瓜。|近义词：ditz, scatterbrain, bimbo, space cadet|反义词：genius, intellectual, brainiac|Noun


Etymology->
"Airhead" is a slang term that emerged in the mid-20th century.  It combines "air," suggesting emptiness or lack of substance, and "head," referring to the mind or intellect.  Thus, an "airhead" is someone whose head is filled with "air" rather than brains.
