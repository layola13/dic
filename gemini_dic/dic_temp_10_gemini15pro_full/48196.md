decompression:/ˌdiːkəmˈpreʃn/ |n.|减压；解压|The act or process of reducing or relieving compression; the release of pressure.|Divers must undergo decompression after deep dives to avoid the bends. 潜水员深潜后必须进行减压以避免减压病。|synonyms:release, alleviation, reduction|antonyms:compression, pressurization|noun

decompression:/ˌdiːkəmˈpreʃn/ |n.|（计算机）解压缩|The process of expanding a compressed file to its original size.|The downloaded file needs decompression before you can use it. 下载的文件需要解压缩才能使用。|synonyms:extraction, expansion, unpacking|antonyms:compression, zipping|noun
