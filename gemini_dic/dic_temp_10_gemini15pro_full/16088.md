bandrol:/ˈbændrɒl/ (British English), /ˈbændroʊl/ (American English)| n.| 旗帜，横幅；(尤指) 标语；(商品价格的) 标签| A small flag or streamer, typically attached to a lance or mast. A strip of paper attached to something to indicate a price, brand, or other information. |The knight's bandrol fluttered in the wind. 骑士的旗帜在风中飘扬。| The bandrol on the wine bottle indicated its vintage.  酒瓶上的标签标明了它的年份。|synonyms: banner, pennant, streamer, label, tag| antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider words like "unmarked," "unlabeled")| noun
