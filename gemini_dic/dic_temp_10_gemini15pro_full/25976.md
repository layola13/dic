bursa:/ˈbɜːrsə/
n.
滑囊
A small fluid-filled sac or saclike cavity, especially one countering friction at a joint.
Inflammation of the bursa can cause pain and restrict movement. 滑囊炎会导致疼痛并限制活动。
近义词: synovial bursa, bursa synovialis
反义词: (None)
词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "bursa," meaning "purse, bag."  This connection relates to the sac-like appearance of a bursa.
