pantographical:/ˌpæntəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
缩放的，按比例放大的；由缩放仪绘制的
Relating to or produced by a pantograph; resembling the action of a pantograph.
The artist used a pantographical device to enlarge his sketch onto the canvas.  艺术家用缩放仪把他的草图放大到画布上。
The machine uses a pantographical arm to etch the design. 这台机器使用一个缩放臂来蚀刻设计。
近义词:proportional, scaled, enlarged, reduced
反义词:unscaled, disproportionate
词性:adjective


pantograph:/ˈpæntəɡrɑːf/
noun
缩放仪，比例放大器
An instrument for copying a drawing or diagram on a different scale by a combination of levers.
He used a pantograph to enlarge the map. 他使用缩放仪放大地图。
The pantograph allowed her to create a smaller version of the blueprint. 缩放仪让她得以创建蓝图的缩小版。
近义词:proportional compass, eidograph
反义词:N/A
词性:noun
