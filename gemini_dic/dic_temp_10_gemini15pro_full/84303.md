half-way:/ˌhɑːfˈweɪ/ | adj. adv.|中途的，半途的；不彻底的|Located or occurring midway between two points; not fully or properly done.|We met half-way between our two houses. 我们在我们两家之间的中途相遇了。|
half-way:/ˌhɑːfˈweɪ/ | adj. adv.|不彻底的，不完全的|Not thorough or complete; partial.|The reforms were only half-way measures. 这些改革只是些不彻底的措施。|
half-way:/ˌhɑːfˈweɪ/ | adj. adv.| |例句：The project is only half-way complete.  这个项目只完成了一半。|近义词：midway, partially, incompletely|反义词:completely, fully, totally|词性:形容词/副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From half + way.  "Half" comes from Old English "healf," meaning "side, part, portion." "Way" comes from Old English "weg," meaning "road, path, journey."
