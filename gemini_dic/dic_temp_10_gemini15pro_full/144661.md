outwriggle: /ˌaʊtˈrɪɡəl/
词性: verb
中文翻译: 扭动着逃脱；比…更会扭动
英文解析: To escape or extricate oneself by wriggling or twisting movements, or to wriggle more or better than someone or something else.  It combines the prefix "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) with the verb "wriggle" (meaning to twist and turn the body with quick movements).
例句: The fish outwriggled the fisherman's grasp and escaped back into the water. 鱼扭动着身体逃脱了渔夫的抓捕，回到了水中。

例句: The magician outwriggled his assistant's attempt to tie him up securely. 魔术师扭动身体，摆脱了助手试图将他牢牢捆绑的束缚。

近义词: wiggle free, squirm out, escape, evade
反义词: be trapped, be caught
