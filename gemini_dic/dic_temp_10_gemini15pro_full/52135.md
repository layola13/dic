diape:/ˈdaɪəpiː/
noun
幻灯片，透明正片
A transparency for projection, especially of medical or scientific subjects.
The doctor showed us a diape of a diseased lung. 医生给我们看了一张病变肺部的幻灯片。
synonym: slide, transparency
antonym:  (No direct antonym, could consider "print", "negative" in specific contexts)
词性: noun
