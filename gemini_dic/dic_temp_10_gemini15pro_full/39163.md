congas:/ˈkɒŋɡəz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋɡəz/ (US)| n. |康加鼓|A tall, narrow, single-headed drum played with the hands, originally from Cuba.  It's often used in Latin American music.|The band's congas player kept a steady beat. 乐队的康加鼓手保持着稳定的节奏。|
conga:/ˈkɒŋɡə/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋɡə/ (US)| n. |康加舞|A Cuban dance, typically performed in a long, winding line.
