dividant: /ˈdɪvɪdənd/
noun|股息；红利；回报；被除数|A sum of money paid regularly (typically annually) by a company to its shareholders out of its profits (or reserves). The number being divided. |Example: The company announced a dividend of $2 per share.  公司宣布每股派息2美元。
Example: In the equation 10 / 2 = 5, 10 is the dividant. 在等式 10 / 2 = 5 中，10是被除数。|Synonyms: payout, distribution, return,  numerator|Antonyms: divisor, denominator|n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French dividende, from Latin dividendum ‘thing to be divided’, neuter gerundive of dividere.
