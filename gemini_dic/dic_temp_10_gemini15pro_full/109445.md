lepal: /ˈsiːpəl/ | n. | 萼片 | One of the separate, usually green, leaf-like parts that surround and protect a flower bud and extend from the base of the flower after it has opened. | The sepals of the rose were a deep crimson. 玫瑰的萼片是深红色的。|  The lepal protected the delicate bud. 萼片保护着娇嫩的花蕾。 | synonym: calyx (when referring to all sepals collectively) | antonym: petal | noun
