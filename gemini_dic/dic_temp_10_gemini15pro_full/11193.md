archist:/ˈɑːrkɪst/
n.
统治者; 提倡某种政治制度的人
a person who advocates or practices a particular form of government or social organization
The anarchist argued for a society without government.  这位无政府主义者主张建立一个没有政府的社会。

archist:/ˈɑːrkɪst/
n.
首领，倡导者
a leader or advocate of a movement or idea.
He is an archist of the new educational reform. 他是新教育改革的倡导者。

近义词:ruler, leader, advocate, proponent
反义词:follower, opponent
词性:noun
