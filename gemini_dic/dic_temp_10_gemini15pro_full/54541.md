dislikeness:/dɪsˈlaɪknɪs/
n.
不喜欢，厌恶
The feeling of not liking something or someone; antipathy.
She couldn't hide her dislikeness for the new manager. 她无法掩饰对新经理的厌恶。
近义词：antipathy, aversion, distaste, hostility
反义词：liking, fondness, affection
词性：名词

dislikeness:/dɪsˈlaɪknɪs/
n.
不相似, 差异
lack of resemblance
There is a marked dislikeness between the two brothers. 这两兄弟之间差异明显。
近义词：difference, dissimilarity, unlikeness
反义词：likeness, resemblance, similarity
词性：名词


词源：
From Middle English *dislikenesse, equivalent to dislike +‎ -ness.  Dislike itself comes from dis- + like.
