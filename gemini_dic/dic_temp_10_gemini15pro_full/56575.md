dominus:/ˈdɒmɪnəs/ /ˈdɑːmɪnəs/| n. |主人，领主，阁下|A person who dominates or rules; a lord or master.  A title of respect, especially in the Roman Empire and later in medieval Europe.|The dominus of the estate controlled all the land and the people who worked on it.|近义词：lord, master, ruler, sovereign|反义词:servant, slave|noun

dominus:/ˈdɒmɪnəs/ /ˈdɑːmɪnəs/| adj. |（不常用）主要的，支配的|Dominating or ruling; principal.  (This adjectival usage is less common than the noun form.) | While not strictly accurate grammatically, the term "dominus influence" is sometimes used poetically to describe a controlling power.|近义词：dominant, controlling, ruling|反义词:subservient, subordinate|adjective
