farinaceous:/ˌfærɪˈneɪʃəs/ | adj. | 面粉的；谷类的 | Relating to or resembling flour or meal. |  The cake had a slightly farinaceous texture.  这个蛋糕的质地有点像面粉。

farinaceous:/ˌfærɪˈneɪʃəs/ | adj. | （指食物）由面粉或淀粉制成的 | (of food) made from or rich in flour or starch. |  He prefers farinaceous foods like pasta and bread. 他更喜欢意大利面和面包之类的淀粉类食物。



Etymology->

Synonyms->
starchy, mealy, floury

Antonyms->
