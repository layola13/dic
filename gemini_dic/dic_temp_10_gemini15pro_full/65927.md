exacerbate: /ɪɡˈzæsərbeɪt/ /ɪkˈsæsərbeɪt/
verb
加剧，恶化
to make something worse, especially a disease or problem
The humid weather exacerbated her asthma. 潮湿的天气加剧了她的哮喘。
近义词: aggravate, worsen, intensify, compound
反义词: alleviate, improve, mitigate, lessen
词性: verb

exacerbated: /ɪɡˈzæsərbeɪtɪd/ /ɪkˈsæsərbeɪtɪd/
adjective
加剧的，恶化的
made worse, especially of a disease or problem
Her eczema was exacerbated by the stress of her job.  她的湿疹因工作压力而加剧。
近义词: aggravated, worsened, intensified
反义词: alleviated, improved, mitigated
词性: adjective
