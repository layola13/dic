idolising:/ˈaɪdəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 偶像化，崇拜 |  The act of admiring or worshipping someone excessively, treating them as an idol. | She was idolising a pop star. 她正在崇拜一位流行歌星。 |  worshipping, adoring, venerating | criticizing, condemning | verb

idolising:/ˈaɪdəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 崇拜的，偶像化的 (less common as an adjective, usually used as a present participle) |  Characterized by excessive admiration or worship. | Her idolising gaze made him uncomfortable. 她崇拜的目光让他感到不舒服。 | worshipping, adoring, venerating | critical, condemning | adjective
