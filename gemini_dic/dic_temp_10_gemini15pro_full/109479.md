词源: From Middle English *lepros, from Old French lepros, leprose, from Late Latin leprosus (“leprous”), from Latin lepra (“leprosy”), from Ancient Greek λέπρα (lépra, “leprosy”), from λεπρός (leprós, “scaly, rough”), from λέπω (lépō, “to peel, scale”).
