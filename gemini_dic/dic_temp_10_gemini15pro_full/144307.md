outsported:/ˌaʊtˈspɔːrtɪd/
verb
在体育运动中胜过，比…更擅长运动
to defeat someone in a sporting competition

Example:  She felt outsported and humiliated after the match.  比赛结束后，她感到自己在运动方面被击败，很丢脸。

Synonym: outplayed, defeated, beaten
Antonym: defeated, lost to
词性: 动词
