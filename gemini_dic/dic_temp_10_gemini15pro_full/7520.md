annelid:/ˈænəlɪd/| n. |环节动物|A segmented worm, such as an earthworm or leech.  Belonging to the phylum Annelida, characterized by a body divided into ring-like segments.|Earthworms are beneficial annelids that improve soil quality. 蚯蚓是有益的环节动物，可以改善土壤质量。|
The leech, an annelid, has been used medicinally for centuries. 水蛭，一种环节动物，几个世纪以来一直被用于医疗。|Synonyms: segmented worm |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a biological classification)|noun
