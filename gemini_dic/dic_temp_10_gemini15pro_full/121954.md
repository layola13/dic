missionarize:/ˈmɪʃənəˌraɪz/ /ˈmɪʃəˌnaɪz/| v. |使皈依；进行传教活动；|To convert or attempt to convert someone to a particular religion, especially Christianity. To imbue with missionary zeal.|例句：They tried to missionarize the indigenous people. 他们试图使原住民皈依基督教。

例句：The organization's goal is to missionarize the entire region.  这个组织的目标是向整个地区传教。|近义词：proselytize, evangelize, convert|反义词:secularize|词性:动词
