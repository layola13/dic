epidermises:/ˌɛpɪˈdɜːrmɪsiːz/
n.
表皮（复数）
the plural form of epidermis, referring to the outer protective layers of the skin of multiple organisms.
The epidermises of reptiles are often thick and scaly. 爬行动物的表皮通常又厚又多鳞。
The researcher compared the epidermises of different plant species. 研究人员比较了不同植物物种的表皮。
近义词:skins, outer layers
反义词:dermis, hypodermis
词性:noun

epidermis:/ˌɛpɪˈdɜːrmɪs/
n.
表皮（单数）
the outer, non-vascular, protective layer of the skin.
The epidermis protects the body from the environment. 表皮保护身体免受环境的侵害。
Sunburn damages the epidermis. 晒伤会损害表皮。
近义词:skin, outer layer
反义词:dermis, hypodermis
词性:noun

词源: From Ancient Greek  *epí* (ἐπί, “upon, on”) + *dérma* (δέρμα, “skin”).
