一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她正在做什么晚餐？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has he done with the money? = 他用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been reading this week? = 她这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? = 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you eaten before you got sick? = 你生病前吃了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision was made? = 决定做出之前他们讨论了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the interruption? =  被打断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到今年年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习五年什么了？
What will they have been working on for so long? = 到那时他们将一直在做什么这么久？
