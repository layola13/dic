counterweight:/ˈkaʊntərˌweɪt/ | n. | 平衡重，平衡物；抗衡力，抵消因素 | a weight that balances another weight, especially one that moves up and down or backwards and forwards | The crane is fitted with a counterweight to prevent it from tipping over.  起重机装有平衡重以防止倾覆。 | synonyms: balance, counterpoise, offset | antonyms: imbalance, instability | noun

counterweights:/ˈkaʊntərˌweɪts/ | n. | 平衡重，平衡物；抗衡力，抵消因素（复数） | weights that balance other weights | The elevator uses counterweights to assist its movement. 电梯使用平衡重来辅助其运动。 | synonyms: balances, counterpoises, offsets | antonyms: imbalances, instabilities | noun
