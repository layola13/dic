USAGE->
Simple Present:
What do you do for a living? = 你靠什么谋生？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃了什么？
What did she say to him? = 她对他说什么了？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会时穿了什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she told him before the meeting? = 会议前她告诉他什么了？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before the phone rang? = 电话响之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? = 他们一直在争论什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this weekend? = 你这个周末会做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们将在会议上讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the age of 30? = 到30岁时你将取得什么成就？
What will they have finished by Friday? = 到星期五他们将完成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习五年什么了？
What will she have been working on for the past decade? = 过去十年她一直在做什么？



Subjunctive Mood:
If I knew what to say, I would tell you. = 如果我知道该说什么，我会告诉你。
I wish I knew what she was thinking. = 我希望我知道她在想什么。

Passive Voice:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

Interrogative Sentence:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the summer? = 你暑假的计划是什么？

Negative Sentence:
I don't know what to think. = 我不知道该怎么想。
He didn't tell me what happened. = 他没有告诉我发生了什么。
