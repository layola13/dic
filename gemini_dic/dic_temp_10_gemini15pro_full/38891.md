conferrers:/kənˈfɜːrərz/
n.
授予者; 赋予者; 商讨者; 交谈者
Those who confer, or discuss something together.
The conferrers of the award met to decide the winner. 颁奖者开会决定获奖者。
The conferrers reached an agreement after hours of discussion.  经过数小时的讨论，与会者达成了一致。
synonyms: discussants, negotiators, participants, conferees
antonyms: recipients, receivers (if referring to those who receive something conferred)
