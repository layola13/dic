condignly:/kənˈdaɪnli/| adv.|适当地；应得地；恰如其分地| deservedly; appropriately (used of punishment or retribution). It implies that the punishment or fate is fitting and deserved given the severity of the wrongdoing.|例句：He was condignly punished for his crimes. 他因罪行受到应有的惩罚。|近义词：justly, deservedly, appropriately, fittingly|反义词:unjustly, undeservedly, inappropriately|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *condign, from Old French condigne, from Latin condignus (“entirely worthy, very deserving”), from com- (“together”) + dignus (“worthy”).
