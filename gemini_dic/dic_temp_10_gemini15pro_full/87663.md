hexadic:/hɛkˈsædɪk/
adjective|十六进制的；由十六个部分组成的|Relating to or based on the number 16; composed of sixteen parts.|Hexadic notation is commonly used in computing. 十六进制计数法在计算中很常用。|
hexadic:/hɛkˈsædɪk/
noun|十六进制数|A number expressed in the hexadecimal system.|The hexadic value 'FF' represents 255 in decimal. 十六进制值“FF”表示十进制的255。|近义词: hexadecimal|反义词:|词性:adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hexa- "six" + Greek *deka "ten," combined to form "sixteen."  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *sweks "six" and *dekm "ten."
