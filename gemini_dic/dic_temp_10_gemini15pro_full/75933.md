fungiform:/ˈfʌŋdʒɪfɔːrm/| adj.|菌状的，蕈形的|Resembling a fungus in shape, especially a mushroom; specifically designating the fungiform papillae on the tongue.|The fungiform papillae are mushroom-shaped structures on the tongue that contain taste buds.|近义词：mushroom-shaped, club-shaped|反义词:|adjective
