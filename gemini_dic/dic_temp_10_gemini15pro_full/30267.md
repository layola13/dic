celtium:/ˈsɛltiəm/| n. | 鎱 | A metallic chemical element, atomic number 72, belonging to the group of transition metals. It is a lustrous, silver-gray metal, resistant to corrosion and used in alloys, nuclear reactors, and some electronic components.  Formerly also called hafnium.|Celtium is used in the control rods of nuclear reactors. 鎱用于核反应堆的控制棒。|Synonym: hafnium|Antonym: N/A|noun
