librarians: /laɪˈbreəriənz/ | n. | 图书管理员 | A person who works in a library, organizing and managing books and other resources, and assisting patrons with research and information needs. | The librarians helped the students find the books they needed for their research project. 图书管理员帮助学生们找到了他们研究项目所需的书籍。| The librarians are very knowledgeable about the library's resources. 图书管理员对图书馆的资源非常了解。 | synonyms: library staff, information specialists, archivists | antonyms: N/A | noun
