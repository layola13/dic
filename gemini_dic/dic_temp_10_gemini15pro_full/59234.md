dziggetai:/ˈdzɪɡətaɪ/ /ˈdʒɪɡətaɪ/
n.
野驴; 亚洲野驴;
A wild ass (Equus hemionus) native to central Asia, characterized by a sandy or grayish-brown coat and a dark stripe along its back. It is closely related to the onager and the kiang.
The dziggetai roamed freely across the Mongolian steppe. 野驴在蒙古草原上自由漫步。
