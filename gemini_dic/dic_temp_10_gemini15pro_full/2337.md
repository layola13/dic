adhering:/ədˈhɪərɪŋ/
Verb
粘附; 坚持; 遵守
sticking fast to a surface or substance.  Following or upholding a particular belief, principle, or way of life.
The tiles weren't adhering properly to the wall.  这些瓷砖没有很好地粘在墙上。

He adhered to his principles/beliefs. 他坚持自己的原则/信仰。

近义词: sticking, clinging, attaching, holding fast;  abiding by, complying with, conforming to, following, observing, upholding
反义词: detaching, separating;  disobeying, violating, ignoring
词性:  present participle of adhere


adhere:/ədˈhɪər/

stick fast to a surface or substance.  Believe in and follow the practices of.

She adhered to her diet plan. 她坚持她的饮食计划。
