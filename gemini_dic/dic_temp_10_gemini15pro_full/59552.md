eavesdropped:/ˈiːvzdrɒpt/ (US) /ˈiːvzdrɒpt/ (UK)| v. |偷听，窃听|To secretly listen to a conversation.|例句：I overheard them talking about me. 我无意中听到他们在谈论我。
例句：She eavesdropped on their conversation from behind the curtain. 她从窗帘后面偷听他们的谈话。|近义词：overhear, listen in, snoop|反义词:ignore, disregard|词性:动词

词源:
From *eavesdrip*, from *eaves* (屋檐) + *drip* (滴水).  Originally referred to the dripping of water from eaves, and then to the area close to the walls of a house.  The verb developed from the idea of standing under the eaves to listen to conversations inside.
