freewheeler:/ˈfriːwiːlər/| n.|自由自在的人，不受约束的人；独立自主的人|A person who acts independently and without constraint, often disregarding rules or conventions.  A freewheeler is someone who does what they want, when they want, without feeling tied down by responsibilities or expectations.|He's a real freewheeler – he quit his job and travelled the world for a year. 他真是个自由自在的人——他辞掉了工作，环游世界一年。

She's always been a freewheeler, never letting anyone tell her what to do. 她一直都是个独立自主的人，从不让任何人告诉她该做什么。|近义词：nonconformist, maverick, individualist, independent|反义词：conformist, follower|词性：名词
