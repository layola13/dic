alabaster: /ˈæləˌbæstər/ /ˈæləˌbɑːstər/
| n. adj.|雪花石膏; 雪花石膏制的;  |A translucent, finely granular variety of gypsum, often white and used for carving.  A pale, smooth, or translucent surface, especially of skin.|The statue was carved from alabaster.  这座雕像是用雪花石膏雕刻而成的。|近义词:gypsum, marble, plaster|反义词: - |noun
alabaster: /ˈæləˌbæstər/ /ˈæləˌbɑːstər/
| adj.|雪花石膏制的;  苍白的; 光滑的; 半透明的|Made of alabaster. Having a pale, smooth, translucent surface, typically of a person's skin. |Her skin was alabaster white.  她的皮肤像雪花石膏一样白。|近义词:pale, white, smooth|反义词:dark, rough|adjective
