overall:/ˌoʊvərˈɔːl/ /ˌəʊvərˈɔːl/| adj. adv.|全部的，总体的；总的来说|covering or including everything|The overall cost of the project was much higher than we expected.  这个项目的总成本比我们预期的要高得多。|Synonyms: general, total, complete|Antonyms: partial, specific|adjective

overall:/ˌoʊvərˈɔːl/ /ˌəʊvərˈɔːl/| n.|工作服，罩衣|a loose-fitting garment worn over other clothes for protection|He wore an overall to protect his clothes while painting. 他穿着一件工作服在刷油漆时保护他的衣服。|Synonyms: coveralls, jumpsuit|Antonyms: N/A|noun

overall:/ˌoʊvərˈɔːl/ /ˌəʊvərˈɔːl/| adv.|总的来说，大体上|in general, considering everything|Overall, the performance was quite good. 总的来说，演出相当不错。|Synonyms: generally, on the whole, all in all|Antonyms: specifically, particularly|adverb
