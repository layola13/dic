break-even:/breɪkˈiːvən/ | adj. n.| 盈亏平衡的；盈亏平衡点 |The point at which total cost and total revenue are equal, meaning there is no profit or loss. |The company reached break-even point after two years of operation.  公司运营两年后达到了盈亏平衡点。| synonyms: cost-recovery, no-profit-no-loss | antonyms: profitable, loss-making | adjective, noun
