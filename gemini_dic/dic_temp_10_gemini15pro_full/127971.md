neoteric:/ˌniːəˈtɛrɪk/ | adj. | 新近的；现代的；新式的 | Relating to or characteristic of recent times; modern. |  The architect's neoteric design incorporated innovative materials and sustainable technologies.  这位建筑师的新式设计融合了创新材料和可持续技术。|  The gallery showcased neoteric art, pushing the boundaries of traditional expression.  画廊展示了新潮艺术，突破了传统表达的界限。 | 近义词: modern, contemporary, recent, new, novel, innovative | 反义词: old, ancient, traditional, outdated, archaic | 词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *neotericus, from Ancient Greek νεωτερικός (neōterikós, “recent, modern”), from νεωτερίζω (neōterízō, “innovate, introduce novelties”), from νέος (néos, “new, young”).
