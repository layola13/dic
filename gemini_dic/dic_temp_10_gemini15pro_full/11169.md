archiater:/ˌɑːrkiˈeɪtər/ | n. | 首席医师，宫廷御医 | A chief physician; a physician to a sovereign or high-ranking official. |  The king's archiater attended to his every ailment. 国王的首席医师照料他的一切疾病。| synonym: chief physician, court physician | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps junior doctor or medical student) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *archiater, from Greek *ἀρχιατρός (arkhiatros), from *ἀρχι- (arkhi-) "chief, ruler" + *ἰατρός (iatros) "physician, healer."
