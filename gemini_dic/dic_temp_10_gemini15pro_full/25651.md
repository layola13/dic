bundlers:/ˈbʌndlɚz/| n.|捆绑机，捆扎机，打包机，打包程序|People who bundle things, or software that bundles files.|Webpack and Parcel are popular JavaScript bundlers. Webpack和Parcel是流行的JavaScript打包程序。|近义词：packagers, assemblers|反义词: separators, dividers|Noun

bundler:/ˈbʌndlər/| n.|捆绑机，捆扎机，打包机，打包程序|A person or thing that bundles, especially a machine for bundling things together or a software tool that bundles files.|The farmer used a bundler to tie the hay. 农民用捆扎机把干草捆起来。|近义词：packager, assembler|反义词: separator, divider|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bundle + -er.  Bundle comes from Middle Dutch "bondel," meaning "bunch" or "package."
