in the teeth of the wind:/ɪn ðə tiːθ əv ðə wɪnd/
prepositional phrase
迎着风；顶着风；逆风
Facing strong opposition or difficulty, especially from the elements. Literally, facing directly into a strong wind.
The ship sailed on, in the teeth of the wind and waves. 这艘船迎着风浪继续航行。
The climbers struggled to reach the summit, in the teeth of the wind and snow. 登山者顶着风雪艰难地向顶峰攀登。
近义词:against the wind, into the wind, against all odds, facing adversity
反义词:with the wind, downwind, with the flow
