bedfellow:/ˈbedˌfeləʊ/ (UK), /ˈbedˌfeloʊ/ (US)| n. |同床人，伙伴，盟友|A person who shares a bed with another; a person or group allied or associated with another in a particular activity or endeavor. |例句：Strange bedfellows politics makes. 政治会促成奇怪的联盟。

例句：He found himself bedfellows with his former enemy. 他发现自己与以前的敌人成了盟友。

近义词：ally, associate, partner, companion, comrade|反义词：enemy, opponent, adversary|词性：名词
