dissipated: /ˈdɪsɪpeɪtɪd/
adjective
消散的，浪费的，放荡的
overindulging in sensual pleasures; dissolute.  Scattered and dispersed. Wasted or squandered.
He led a dissipated life, frequenting nightclubs and gambling heavily. 他过着放荡的生活，经常出入夜总会和豪赌。
The fog dissipated quickly in the morning sun.  晨曦中的雾气迅速消散。
synonyms: dissolute, profligate, debauched, intemperate, prodigal, squandered, dispersed, scattered
antonyms: temperate, moderate, ascetic, collected, gathered, conserved
词性: 形容词


dissipate: /ˈdɪsɪpeɪt/
verb
消散，驱散，浪费
to (cause to) gradually disappear or waste.
The smoke dissipated into the air. 烟雾消散在空中。
He dissipated his fortune on gambling. 他把财产都浪费在赌博上了。
synonyms: disperse, scatter, squander, waste, vanish, evaporate
antonyms: gather, collect, accumulate, conserve, save
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dissipatus, past participle of dissipare ("to scatter, disperse"), from dis- ("apart, asunder") + sipare ("to throw, cast").
