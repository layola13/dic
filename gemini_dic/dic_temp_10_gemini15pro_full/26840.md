caimans:/ˈkeɪmənz/ | n. |凯门鳄|A semiaquatic reptile similar to an alligator, but with a broader, shorter snout. Caimans are native to Central and South America. |Caimans are found in the swamps of Brazil.  凯门鳄常见于巴西沼泽。|
Caimans inhabit slow-moving rivers. 凯门鳄栖息在流速缓慢的河流中。|Synonyms: alligator, crocodile, crocodilian |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for an animal) | Noun
