grecianizing:/ˌɡriːʃənaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使希腊化，希腊化
Making something Greek in nature or style, adopting Greek customs or culture.
The Roman Empire, despite grecianizing many of its institutions, maintained its distinct Roman identity.  罗马帝国虽然希腊化了许多机构，但仍保持着其独特的罗马身份。
He was accused of grecianizing Roman traditions. 他被指责将罗马传统希腊化。
近义词: Hellenizing, classicizing
反义词: Romanizing, Latinizing
词性: v.
