extirpator:/ˈekstərpeɪtər/ | n. | 根除者，消灭者 |  A person or thing that extirpates (roots out, destroys completely).  | The extirpator cleared the land of invasive weeds.  根除者清除了土地上的入侵杂草。 | synonyms: destroyer, eradicator, exterminator | antonyms: preserver, protector, cultivator | noun

extirpate:/ˈekstərpeɪt/ | v. | 根除，消灭，彻底摧毁 | To root out, destroy completely. |  They tried to extirpate the disease from the population. 他们试图从人群中根除这种疾病。 | synonyms: eradicate, exterminate, abolish | antonyms: cultivate, preserve, foster | verb
