corrugent:/kəˈrʌdʒənt/
adjective
腐蚀性的；使起皱纹的
causing wrinkles or corrugations; having the power to corrode or eat away
The acid was so corrugent that it burned a hole in the fabric. 这种酸腐蚀性很强，把织物烧了个洞。
近义词：corrosive, caustic, erosive
反义词：protective, preservative
词性：adjective

corrugent:/kəˈrʌdʒənt/
adjective
起皱的；有皱纹的
wrinkled or corrugated
Her face was corrugent with worry. 她满脸皱纹，显得忧心忡忡。
近义词：wrinkled, furrowed, creased
反义词：smooth, unwrinkled
词性：adjective

词源:  From Latin *corrugant- (stem of corrugans), present participle of corrugare "to wrinkle," from com- "together" + rugare "to wrinkle," from ruga "wrinkle."
