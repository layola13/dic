outrival:/ˌaʊtˈraɪvl/
verb
胜过，超越
to surpass or defeat a rival or competitor
例句：
He outrivaled his competitors in the race and won the gold medal.
他在比赛中胜过对手，赢得了金牌。

She outrivaled all the other applicants for the job.
她胜过所有其他求职者，获得了这份工作。

近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, outstrip, beat, defeat
反义词：lose to, be defeated by, be inferior to
词性：v.
