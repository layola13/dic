caas:/kɑːz/ /kæz/| n.| 通信即服务; 通讯即服务 | Communications as a Service. A cloud computing offering that enables businesses to access and manage communications services, such as voice, video, messaging, and fax, over the internet.| Our company is considering switching to a CaaS provider to streamline our communications.| 近义词: UCaaS (Unified Communications as a Service)| 反义词: On-premises communications| 词性: noun
