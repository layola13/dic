cyborg: /ˈsaɪbɔːrɡ/ | n. | 半机械人；电子人 | A being with both organic and biomechatronic body parts. A person whose physical abilities are extended beyond normal human limitations by mechanical elements built into the body. | The cyborg in the movie had superhuman strength.  电影里的电子人拥有超人的力量。|  The scientist envisioned a future populated by cyborgs.  这位科学家展望了一个由电子人组成的未来。 | synonyms: bionic human, android, robot | antonyms: human, organic being | noun
