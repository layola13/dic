dramming:/ˈdræmɪŋ/| n. |击鼓，敲击声|The constant drumming of the rain on the roof kept me awake. 屋顶上持续不断的雨声让我无法入睡。|近义词：tapping, beating, rapping|反义词:silence|词性:名词
dramming:/ˈdræmɪŋ/| v. |击鼓，敲击|He was drumming his fingers nervously on the table. 他紧张地用手指敲着桌子。|近义词：tapping, beating, rapping|反义词:stillness|词性:动词


词源: From the verb "drum" + "-ing" suffix, indicating the act or process of drumming. 源自动词 "drum" 加上 "-ing" 后缀，表示击鼓的行为或过程。
