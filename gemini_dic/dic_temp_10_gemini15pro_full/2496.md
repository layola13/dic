admin:/ˈædmɪn/| n. |管理员；行政人员|Short for administration, referring to a person or a system responsible for managing a computer network or system, or an organization.|The admin locked my account. 管理员锁定了我的帐户。|近义词：administrator, manager, superintendent|反义词:user, client|noun

admin:/ədˈmɪn/| adj. |行政的；管理的|Relating to administration or management.|He has an admin role in the company. 他在公司担任行政职务。|近义词：administrative, managerial, executive|反义词:non-administrative, operational|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From abbreviation of *administration*, ultimately from Latin *administratio*, "management, direction."
