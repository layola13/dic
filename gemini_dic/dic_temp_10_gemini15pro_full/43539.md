crawly:/ˈkrɔːli/| adj.|令人毛骨悚然的；爬行的|Causing a sensation of insects crawling on the skin; creepy.  Relating to or resembling crawling.|例句：The dark, damp basement gave me a crawly feeling.  黑暗潮湿的地下室让我感到毛骨悚然。|近义词：creepy, itchy, squirmy, unpleasant|反义词：pleasant, comfortable|词性:形容词
crawly:/ˈkrɔːli/| n.|爬行动物|A crawling creature, especially an insect.|例句：The kids were fascinated by the crawlies in the garden. 孩子们对花园里的爬行动物很着迷。|近义词：insect, bug, creepy-crawly|反义词：None|词性:名词
