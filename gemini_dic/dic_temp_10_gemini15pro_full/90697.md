humdrums:/ˈhʌmdrʌmz/| n.|单调乏味，无聊，沉闷|A state of boredom or monotony; a dull routine.|例句：I'm tired of the humdrums of everyday life. 我厌倦了每天单调乏味的生活。|例句：She longed to escape the humdrums of her small town. 她渴望逃离小镇的沉闷生活。|近义词：monotony, boredom, tedium, dullness, routine|反义词：excitement, thrill, adventure, novelty, stimulation|词性:名词

humdrum:/ˈhʌmdrʌm/| adj.|单调乏味的，无聊的，沉闷的|Lacking excitement or variety; dull; monotonous.|例句：He led a humdrum existence. 他过着单调乏味的生活。|例句：The work was humdrum and repetitive.  这项工作单调乏味，重复性很高。|近义词：monotonous, boring, tedious, dull, routine|反义词：exciting, thrilling, adventurous, novel, stimulating|词性:形容词
