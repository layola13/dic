craniometer:/ˌkreɪniˈɒmɪtər/ (US) /ˌkreɪniˈɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |颅骨测量器，颅测量计|An instrument for measuring the dimensions of the skull, used especially in anthropometry. |The craniometer was used to determine the skull's size and shape.  颅骨测量器被用来确定颅骨的大小和形状。

The museum's collection includes a 19th-century craniometer.  博物馆的藏品中包括一个19世纪的颅骨测量器。


|近义词：cephalometer|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From cranio- (combining form meaning "skull") + -meter (suffix meaning "instrument for measuring").  Ultimately from Greek "kranion" (skull) and "metron" (measure).
