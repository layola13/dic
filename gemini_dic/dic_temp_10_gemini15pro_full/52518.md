differentiator:/ˌdɪfəˈrenʃieɪtər/ | n. | 区分者；区别因素；微分器 |Something that distinguishes one thing from another; a distinguishing characteristic or feature. |  The key differentiator between the two products is the battery life. 这两种产品的主要区别在于电池续航时间。| synonyms: distinguishing feature, distinguishing characteristic, hallmark, mark, marker, badge, indicator, criterion, point of difference, USP (unique selling point/proposition) | antonyms: similarity, commonality, resemblance | noun

differentiate:/ˌdɪfəˈrenʃieɪt/ | v. | 区分，辨别；区别对待 | 1. Recognize or ascertain what makes (someone or something) different. 2. Make or become different in the process of growth or development. 3. Make (someone or something) appear different or distinct. 4. Find the derivative of (a function or equation). | He is unable to differentiate between fantasy and reality. 他无法区分幻想和现实。 | synonyms: distinguish, tell apart, discriminate, discern | antonyms: confuse,  conflate | verb
