parafovea:/ˌpærəˈfoʊviə/| n. |旁中心凹|The area of the retina surrounding the fovea, responsible for less acute vision but important for peripheral awareness.  |The parafovea plays a crucial role in reading, as it allows us to anticipate upcoming words. 旁中心凹在阅读中起着至关重要的作用，因为它使我们能够预测即将出现的单词。|Near synonyms: perifovea, parafoveal region|Antonym: fovea|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From para- (Greek, "beside," "alongside") + fovea (Latin, "small pit").
