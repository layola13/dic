overridden:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪdn/ | v. (past participle) |  覆写，覆盖，推翻 |  Past participle of "override." It means to set aside or disregard a previous decision, rule, or setting.  It's often used in the context of computer programming, where a method in a subclass can override a method in its superclass. | The safety protocols were overridden by the manager.  经理推翻了安全协议。|  The function's default behavior was overridden by a custom script.  该函数的默认行为被自定义脚本覆盖了。| superseded, replaced, overturned | disregarded, upheld | verb
