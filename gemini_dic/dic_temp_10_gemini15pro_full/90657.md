humate:/ˈhjuːmeɪt/ | n. |腐植酸盐；腐殖酸盐|A salt of humic acid, found in soil and formed from decaying plant matter.  It is often used as a soil conditioner. |The farmer used humate to improve the soil structure.  农民用腐植酸盐来改良土壤结构。|Humate granules were added to the potting mix. 腐植酸盐颗粒被添加到盆栽混合物中。|Synonyms: humic acid salt,  soil conditioner|Antonyms: N/A|noun
