parthenogen:/ˌpɑːrθɪˈnəʊdʒɛn/| n.|单性生殖，孤雌生殖|Asexual reproduction in which an egg develops without being fertilized.|Parthenogenesis is a common reproductive strategy in some insect species. 一些昆虫物种中，单性生殖是一种常见的繁殖策略。|The Komodo dragon is capable of parthenogenesis. 科莫多巨蜥能够进行单性生殖。|Synonyms:  apomixis, asexual reproduction|Antonyms: sexual reproduction|noun
