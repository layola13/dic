osier:/ˈōʒər/ | n. | 柳条，柳树枝 | A willow tree, especially one of a variety used for basketwork, or a branch or twig of such a willow. |  The basket was woven from osier. 这个篮子是用柳条编织的。 | synonyms: wicker, withe, withy | antonyms: N/A | noun


osier:/ˈoʊʒər/ | adj. |  柳条制的; 由柳条构成的 | Made of or resembling osier. |  She wore an osier hat. 她戴着一顶柳条帽。 | synonyms: wicker, withe | antonyms: N/A | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *osier, from Frankish *wisja, from Proto-Germanic *wizją (“willow, osier”). Cognate with Old English wiþig (“willow”), Dutch wilg (“willow”), German Weide (“willow”).
