fov:/fɒv/ (UK) /fɑːv/ (US)| n. |视野，视场|The extent of the observable world that is seen at any given moment.  It is usually measured angularly.|The game allows you to adjust the FOV.  这款游戏允许你调整视野。|Synonyms: field of view, field of vision, visual field, angle of view|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but a narrow FOV could be considered the opposite of a wide FOV)|noun
