moribundities:/ˌmɔːrɪˈbʌndɪtiz/ | n. | 垂死状态；临终状态（复数）|The state of being moribund; the condition of being near death. It often refers to multiple instances or aspects of this state.| The moribundities of the old industries were evident in the declining employment figures.  这些老工业的垂死状态在不断下降的就业数字中显而易见。|Near-death states, dying conditions, final stages| Rejuvenation, vitality, flourishing| Noun
