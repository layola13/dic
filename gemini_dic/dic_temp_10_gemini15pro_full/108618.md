leadered:/ˈliːdərd/| v. |领导，担任领导|Led or guided, especially in a formal capacity.  This word is not standard English and considered archaic or nonstandard. Its use is discouraged.  |Example: He leadered the expedition into the unknown. 他领导了这次远征，深入未知领域。 |Synonym: led, headed, commanded, guided|Antonym: followed, obeyed|Verb
