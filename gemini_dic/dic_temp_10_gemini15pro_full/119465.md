micrometeorites: /ˌmaɪkroʊˈmiːtiəˌraɪts/ | n. | 微陨石 |  Extremely small meteorites, typically less than a millimeter in size, that are constantly bombarding Earth.  These tiny particles of space dust originate from asteroids, comets, and other celestial bodies. |  Scientists collect micrometeorites from Antarctica because they are easier to find on the pristine ice. 科学家们从南极洲收集微陨石，因为它们在原始冰层上更容易被发现。

Micrometeorites are a valuable source of information about the early solar system. 微陨石是关于早期太阳系的有价值的信息来源。 | 近义词: space dust, cosmic dust | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: Noun
