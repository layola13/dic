impasture:/ɪmˈpæstʃər/ | vt. | 放牧; 吃(草); 使沉浸于 | To place (livestock) in a pasture for grazing; to feed on pasture. To infuse; saturate. | The farmer impastured his cattle in the lush green field. 农民把他的牛群放牧在郁郁葱葱的绿野上。

The scholar was impastured in his studies. 这位学者沉浸在他的研究中。 | 近义词: graze, pasture, immerse, saturate | 反义词: confine, restrict | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impasturare, from Latin in- + pastura (“pasture”). Equivalent to im- +‎ pasture.
