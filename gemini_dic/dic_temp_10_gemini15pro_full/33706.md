ciphering:/ˈsaɪfərɪŋ/| n. |加密，计算，算术|The act of performing calculations or encryption. |Example: The ciphering of the message ensured its secrecy.  信息加密确保了其机密性。|Example: He spent hours ciphering the complex equation. 他花了几个小时计算这个复杂的方程式。|Synonyms: encryption, calculating, computing|Antonyms: decryption, decoding|noun

ciphering:/ˈsaɪfərɪŋ/| v. |计算，加密|To perform calculations or encryption. |Example:  She ciphered the code to unlock the door. 她计算出密码打开了门。|Example: They were ciphering sales figures for the quarter. 他们正在计算本季度的销售数据。|Synonyms: calculate, compute, encrypt|Antonyms: decrypt, decode|verb
