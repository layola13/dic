cerographical: /ˌsɛrəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
蜡刻的；关于蜡刻的
Relating to cerography, the art or process of writing or drawing with wax, especially as used in ancient times for making tablets.
Cerographical tablets were used in ancient Rome. 古罗马时期使用蜡板。
近义词: wax-based, waxen
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "digital" in a modern context)
词性: adjective
