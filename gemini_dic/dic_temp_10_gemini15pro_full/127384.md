neap:/niːp/| adj. n.|小潮的，低潮的；小潮；低潮|Relating to or denoting a tide that occurs at the first or third quarters of the moon, when there is the least difference between high and low water.|The neap tides this month occur on the 10th and 24th. 这个月的小潮发生在10号和24号。|近义词：low tide|反义词：spring tide|词性:形容词, 名词
Etymology: Old English *nēp-flōd ‘tide at which the water does not rise high’, of unknown origin; compare with Dutch nip- and German Nipp-.
