longed-for:/ˈlɒŋd fɔːr/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋd fɔːr/ (US)| adj.|渴望的，梦寐以求的|Something that is longed-for is something that someone has wanted very much for a long time.|She finally bought her longed-for sports car. 她终于买到了她梦寐以求的跑车。|近义词：desired, wished-for, coveted, yearned-for|反义词：undesired, unwanted|词性:形容词

longed-for:| | |The word "longed-for" is formed from the verb "long" (to have a strong desire or craving for something) and the past participle of "for," indicating the object of the longing.  The "-ed" ending transforms "long" into an adjective describing something that is the subject of longing.| | | | |
