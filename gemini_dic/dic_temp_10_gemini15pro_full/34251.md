clapbread:/ˈklæpbrɛd/| n. |拍拍麵包，薄脆麵包|A thin, crisp, unleavened bread, often made with rye flour and traditionally baked by clapping the dough between the hands.|She offered us some delicious homemade clapbread with cheese. 她用奶酪招待我们一些美味的自制拍拍麵包。|Synonym: flatbread, cracker, crispbread|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "leavened bread")|noun
