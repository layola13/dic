conundrum:/kəˈnʌndrəm/ | n. | 难题，谜题 |A confusing and difficult problem or question. |  She faced the conundrum of whether to tell the truth or lie. 她面临着是说实话还是撒谎的两难境地。|  The detective was stumped by the conundrum of the locked room mystery.  侦探被这间密室之谜难住了。| synonym: puzzle, riddle, enigma, problem, dilemma | antonym: solution, answer, explanation | noun
