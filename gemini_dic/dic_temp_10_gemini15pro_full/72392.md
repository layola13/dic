fluctuation:/ˌflʌktʃuˈeɪʃən/| n. |波动，起伏，变动|An irregular rising and falling in number or amount; a variation.|The fluctuation in the stock market made investors nervous. 股票市场的波动让投资者感到紧张。|近义词：variation, oscillation, swing, instability|反义词：stability, constancy, steadiness|词性: 名词

fluctuate:/ˈflʌktʃueɪt/| v. |波动，起伏，变动|To rise and fall irregularly in number or amount.|The price of oil fluctuates depending on global demand. 石油价格随着全球需求的变化而波动。|近义词：vary, oscillate, swing, change|反义词：stabilize, remain constant|词性: 动词
