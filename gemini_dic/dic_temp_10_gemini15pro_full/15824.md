ballad:/ˈbæləd/| n.|民谣，歌谣；叙事诗|A ballad is a poem or song narrating a story in short stanzas.  民谣是一种用短节叙述故事的诗歌或歌曲。|例句：The minstrel sang a beautiful ballad about a lost love.  吟游诗人唱了一首关于失恋的优美民谣。

例句：She wrote a haunting ballad about the war. 她写了一首萦绕心头的关于战争的民谣。|近义词：song, poem, ditty, rhyme|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑prose 散文)|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "balade" (dancing song), from Late Latin "ballare" (to dance).  Ultimately from Greek "ballizein" (to dance or jump). 源自古法语 "balade"（舞曲），来自晚期拉丁语 "ballare"（跳舞）。最终源自希腊语 "ballizein"（跳舞或跳跃）。
