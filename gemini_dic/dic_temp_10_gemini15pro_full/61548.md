embushment:/ɪmˈbʊʃmənt/| n. |埋伏，伏击|An act of lying in wait to attack by surprise.  The act of ambushing someone or something.| The soldiers lay in ambush, waiting for the enemy patrol. 士兵们埋伏起来，等待着敌人的巡逻队。|Near synonyms: ambush, ambuscade|Antonyms:  None (The opposite of an ambush would be an open or direct confrontation.)|Noun
