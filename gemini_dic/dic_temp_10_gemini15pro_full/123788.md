moots:/muːts/
n.|争论点；讨论事项|Points of dispute or discussion; matters for debate|The two sides met to discuss the moots of the contract.|synonyms: arguments, issues, points, questions, subjects, topics|antonyms: agreements, conclusions, resolutions
词性:名词

moots:/muːts/
v. (third-person singular simple present moots, present participle mooting, simple past and past participle mooted)|提出…供讨论；辩论|To raise (a question or topic) for discussion; to debate|The idea of building a new library was mooted at the council meeting.|synonyms: bring up, discuss, introduce, propose, raise, suggest|antonyms: dismiss, ignore, reject
词性:动词
