followup:/ˈfɒləʊʌp/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊʌp/ (US)| n. adj.|后续行动，跟进；后续的，补充的|A followup is something that is done to continue or add to something that has already been started or done.|例句：I sent a followup email to confirm the details. 我发了一封后续邮件确认细节。|近义词：continuation, sequel, follow-through|反义词:prelude, beginning|词性:名词

followup:/ˈfɒləʊʌp/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊʌp/ (US)| v.|跟进，后续|To followup is to continue or add to something that has already been started or done.|例句：I need to followup on that lead. 我需要跟进那条线索。|近义词：pursue, continue, follow through|反义词:initiate, begin|词性:动词

follow-up (hyphenated form is also common, especially as an adjective):


ETYMOLOGY->
From "follow" and "up." "Follow" traces back to Old English "folgian," meaning "to follow, pursue." "Up" originates from Old English "up," meaning "up, upward." The combined meaning emerged in the late 19th century.
