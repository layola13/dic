antihunter:/ˈæntiˌhʌntər/| n.|反狩猎者；反对打猎的人|A person who opposes hunting.|例句：The antihunters protested outside the hunting lodge. 反狩猎者在狩猎小屋外抗议。

例句：She became an antihunter after witnessing the cruelty of the sport.  在目睹了这项运动的残酷性之后，她成了一名反狩猎者。|近义词：animal rights activist, conservationist, opponent of hunting|反义词：hunter, huntsman|词性:名词
