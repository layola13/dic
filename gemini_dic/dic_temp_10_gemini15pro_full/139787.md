octaroon:/ˌɒktəˈruːn/ /ˌɑːktəˈruːn/| n.|奥克通人，一种糕点|A person of one-eighth black ancestry (having one black great-grandparent); a small cake or cookie made with coconut.  This term is now considered offensive due to its historical association with slavery and racial classification.|例句：The bakery sold octaroons and macaroons. 这家面包店出售奥克通人和马卡龙。

例句：Historical novels sometimes mentioned octaroons as part of the social hierarchy of the antebellum South.  历史小说有时会提到奥克通人，作为战前南方社会等级制度的一部分。
