annex:/əˈneks/
verb
吞并；附加
To attach, append, or add, especially to something larger or more important.
Russia annexed Crimea in 2014.  俄罗斯于2014年吞并了克里米亚。
Synonyms: seize, appropriate, acquire, incorporate
Antonyms: relinquish, cede, surrender
词性: 动词


annex:/ˈæneks/
noun
附属建筑物；附件
A building joined to or associated with a main building, often used for a specific purpose.  An addition to a document.
The library has a new annex for rare books.  图书馆有一个新的附属建筑用于存放珍本书。
Synonyms: addition, extension, wing, appendage
Antonyms: main building, original structure
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *annexus*, past participle of *annectere* "to tie or bind to," from *ad-* "to" + *nectere* "to bind."
