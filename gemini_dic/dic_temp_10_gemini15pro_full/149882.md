palindromists:/ˌpælɪnˈdroʊmɪsts/ | n. | 回文爱好者；创作回文的人 | A person who creates or is interested in palindromes (words, phrases, or numbers that read the same backward as forward). |  Example 1: The palindromists gathered at the annual conference to share their latest creations. 回文爱好者们聚集在年度会议上分享他们最新的创作。

Example 2:  He is a known palindromists, capable of crafting intricate palindromic sentences. 他是一位著名的回文爱好者，能够创作复杂的回文句子。 |  Synonyms: wordplay enthusiasts, logophiles | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider those averse to wordplay) | Noun
