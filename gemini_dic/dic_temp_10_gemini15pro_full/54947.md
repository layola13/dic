The disposure of the property is in the hands of the executor. 该财产的处置权掌握在执行人手中。
He showed remarkable disposure in the face of danger. 面对危险，他表现出非凡的镇静。
Etymology: From late Middle English *disposoure*, from Anglo-Norman *disposure*, from Old French *disposure*, from Latin *dispositūra*, from *dispositus*, past participle of *disponere* "to arrange".
Synonyms: arrangement, disposal, inclination, tendency, composure, calmness
Antonyms: agitation, excitement, disarray, disorder
