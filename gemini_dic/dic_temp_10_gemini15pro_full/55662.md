ditransitives:/daɪˈtrænsɪtɪvz/ | adj. | 双及物动词的 |  Relating to or denoting a verb that takes two objects, a direct object and an indirect object. |  She gave him the book. (book is the direct object, him is the indirect object) 她把书给了他。|  |  | adjective
