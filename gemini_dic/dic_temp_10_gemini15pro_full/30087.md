cease-fire: /ˈsiːsfaɪər/ | v. |停火|to stop firing weapons|例句：The rebels ceased fire after the negotiations.  谈判后叛军停止了射击。

近义词：stop firing, lay down arms|反义词:open fire, attack |词性:verb
