cadaverously:/kəˈdævərəsli/
adjective
形容词
惨白地；像尸体的；憔悴地
Resembling a corpse; pale, gaunt, and thin.  Having the qualities of a corpse; ghastly.
He smiled cadaverously. 他惨白地笑了笑。
synonyms: gauntly, pallidly, wanly, ghastly, deathly
近义词：憔悴地，苍白地，惨白地，可怕地，致命地
antonyms: healthily, robustly, vibrantly
反义词：健康地，强壮地，充满活力地
词性: adjective
