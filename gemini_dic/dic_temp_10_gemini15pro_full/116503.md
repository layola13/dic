maternalisms:/məˈtɜːrnəlɪzəmz/
|n.|母性主义，母性政策|Policies and practices, often government-sponsored, based on the idea of women's special role as mothers and caregivers, often aimed at protecting mothers and children, but sometimes also used to control women's behavior and reinforce traditional gender roles.|The government's new childcare policies are seen by some as a form of maternalisms, promoting stay-at-home motherhood. 政府新的儿童保育政策被一些人视为一种母性主义，提倡在家做母亲。|The historian analyzed the maternalisms of the early 20th century, focusing on how they affected working-class women.  这位历史学家分析了20世纪早期的母性主义，重点关注它们是如何影响工人阶级妇女的。|近义词：maternalism (singular form), social welfare programs, family policies|反义词：paternalism, individualism|词性:Noun
