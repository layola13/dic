ballproof:/ˈbɔːlˌpruːf/
adjective
防弹的，刀枪不入的
Impervious to the impact of bullets or other projectiles, especially those fired from small arms.
The car was ballproof, protecting the passengers from the hail of gunfire.  这辆车是防弹的，保护乘客免受枪林弹雨的袭击。
The new vest is said to be ballproof.  据说这件新背心是防弹的。
近义词: bulletproof, bullet-resistant
反义词: vulnerable, unprotected
词性: adjective
