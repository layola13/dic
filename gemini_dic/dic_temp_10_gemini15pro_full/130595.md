nonadaptational:/nɒnˌædæpˈteɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非适应性的 | Not related to adaptation; not contributing to or enhancing fitness in a given environment. | The peacock's bright plumage might be a nonadaptational consequence of other evolutionary pressures. 孔雀鲜艳的羽毛可能是非适应性的，是其他进化压力造成的。| The human appendix is often cited as a nonadaptational organ. 人类的阑尾经常被认为是一个非适应性器官。| 近义词:maladaptive, nonfunctional | 反义词: adaptive, functional | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + adaptational (adjective derived from adaptation, from Latin adaptare "to fit")
