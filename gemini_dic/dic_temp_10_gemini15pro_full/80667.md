gordius:/ˈɡɔːrdiəs/| adj.|戈尔迪乌斯的，极其复杂的，难解的|Relating to Gordius, a legendary Phrygian king who tied an intricate knot (the Gordian knot) which according to prophecy could only be untied by the person who was to rule Asia. Alexander the Great cut the knot with his sword.|The gordius knot of international politics is difficult to unravel. 国际政治的戈尔迪乌斯之结很难解开。|近义词：intricate, complex, involved, tangled|反义词：simple, straightforward|词性:形容词
