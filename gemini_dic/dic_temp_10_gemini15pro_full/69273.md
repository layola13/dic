featherlet:/ˈfeðərˌlɛt/| n.|小羽毛，绒羽|A small, delicate feather, often downy.  A tiny, soft feather, typically one of the down feathers.|The baby bird was covered in soft featherlets.|近义词:down, plume, plumule|反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "quill" for a mature, stiff feather)|noun
