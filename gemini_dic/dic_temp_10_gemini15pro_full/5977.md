ambulatorial:/ˌæmbjʊləˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
可走动的，适宜于步行的
Relating to or adapted for walking; able to walk.
The patient was ambulatorial after the surgery.  这个病人手术后可以走动了。
The hospital has an ambulatorial care unit. 这家医院有一个步行护理单元。
synonyms:  walking, mobile, peripatetic
antonyms:  bedridden, immobile, sedentary
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From Late Latin *ambulatorius, from Latin ambulātōrius (“of walking”), from ambulō (“I walk”).
