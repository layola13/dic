maneuverability:/məˌnuːvərəˈbɪləti/| n. | 机动性，操控性 |The quality of being easy to maneuver.  The ease with which a vehicle, aircraft, etc., can be maneuvered. |The fighter jet's high maneuverability gave it an advantage in dogfights. 这架战斗机的出色机动性使其在空战中占据优势。| synonyms: agility, flexibility, mobility | antonyms: clumsiness, rigidity, inflexibility | noun

maneuverabilities:/məˌnuːvərəˈbɪlətiz/ | n.
