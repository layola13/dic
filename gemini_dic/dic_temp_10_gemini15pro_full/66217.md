excitedly:/ɪkˈsaɪtɪdli/
adverb|兴奋地，激动地|In a manner showing strong feeling of happy enthusiasm and eagerness.|She jumped up and down excitedly. 她兴奋地跳上跳下。|eagerly, enthusiastically|calmly, apathetically
Etymology: From *excited* (past participle of *excite*), from Latin *excitare* "to rouse, call out, summon forth, awaken, incite, stimulate."  The suffix *-ly* transforms the adjective into an adverb.
