aggest:/əˈdʒɛst/| v. |堆积，积累，聚集|to gather or collect into a mass or whole|例句：The snow aggested against the wall. 雪堆积在墙边。

例句：Debris aggested at the mouth of the river. 碎屑堆积在河口。|近义词：accumulate, amass, gather, collect|反义词:disperse, scatter|词性:动词

词源: From Latin aggestus, past participle of aggerere “to heap up,” from ad- “to” + gerere “to carry.”
