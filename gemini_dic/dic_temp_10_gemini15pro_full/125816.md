murmuringly:/ˈmɜːrmərɪŋli/ | adv. | 低声地，喃喃地 | In a murmuring, low, and indistinct voice. | She murmured the answer murmuringly. 她低声喃喃地说了答案。|近义词：softly, quietly, under one's breath, in a low voice |反义词：loudly, clearly |词性:副词

murmuringly:/ˈmɜːrmərɪŋli/ | adv. | 咕哝地，小声抱怨地 | In a way that expresses dissatisfaction or complaint in a low voice.  | He complained murmuringly about the long wait. 他小声地抱怨等待时间太长。|近义词：grumblingly, complainingly |反义词： approvingly, enthusiastically |词性:副词
