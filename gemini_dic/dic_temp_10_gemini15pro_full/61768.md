empathizer:/ˈɛmpəθaɪzər/| n.|同情者，感同身受的人|A person who has the ability to understand and share the feelings of another.|She's a natural empathizer, always able to put herself in other people's shoes.|近义词：sympathizer, compassionate person|反义词:apathetic person, uncaring person|词性:noun

Etymology:
From empathize + -er.  Empathize comes from empathy + -ize. Empathy from German Einfühlung (literally, "feeling into").  Einfühlung is from ein ("in") + Fühlung ("feeling").
