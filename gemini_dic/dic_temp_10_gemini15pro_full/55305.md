dissolvability:/dɪˌzɒlvəˈbɪləti/ /dɪˌsɑːlvəˈbɪləti/
n.|可溶性，溶解性|The quality of being able to be dissolved. The dissolvability of a substance depends on its chemical structure and the solvent used.|The dissolvability of sugar in water is high. 糖在水中的溶解度很高。|solubility, dissolubility|insolubility, indissolubility|noun
