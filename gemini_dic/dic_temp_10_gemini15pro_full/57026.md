doublers:/ˈdʌblərz/
n.|加倍者; 使…加倍的人(或物)|People or things that double something.  Those who make something twice as large, twice as many, or twice as strong. |The doublers in the competition increased their scores significantly. 比赛中那些得分翻倍的人得分显著提高。|synonyms: multipliers, increasers|antonyms: halvers, reducers|noun

double:/ˈdʌb(ə)l/
v.|加倍，翻倍|To make twice as large, twice as many, or twice as strong.|He doubled his investment in just one year. 他在短短一年内就让他的投资翻了一番。|synonyms: increase twofold, multiply by two|antonyms: halve, reduce by half|verb

double:/ˈdʌb(ə)l/
adj.|双的，两倍的|Twice as large, twice as many, or twice as strong.|She ordered a double espresso. 她点了一杯双份浓缩咖啡。|synonyms: twofold, twice|antonyms: half, single|adjective
