ham-fistedly:/ˌhæmˈfɪstɪdli/
adverb
笨手笨脚地；粗鲁地
in a clumsy, awkward, or insensitive way.
He ham-fistedly tried to fix the broken vase, making it worse. 他笨手笨脚地试图修理破碎的花瓶，结果反而弄得更糟了。
clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly, gracelessly
deftly, skillfully, adroitly
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From ham-fisted + -ly.  Ham-fisted is from ham + fisted, referring to large, clumsy hands like a ham.  "Ham" in this context refers to the back of the thigh, traditionally a large cut of meat.
