long-windedly:/ˌlɔːŋˈwɪndɪdli/ | adv. |冗长地，啰嗦地|In a tediously lengthy manner; using more words than necessary.|He spoke long-windedly about his travels, boring everyone in the room. 他冗长地讲述他的旅行，让房间里的每个人都感到无聊。|近义词：tediously, verbosely, prolixly|反义词：concisely, succinctly, briefly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From long-winded + -ly. Long-winded itself is formed from long + wind (breath), implying speaking for a long time without pausing for breath.
