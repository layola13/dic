hypernaturalnesses:/ˌhaɪpərˈnætʃrəlnəsɪz/
|n.| 超自然性（复数）
|The state or quality of being beyond or exceeding what is considered natural or normal, often with a spiritual or mystical connotation.  This plural form suggests multiple instances or aspects of this quality.|
|The hypernaturalnesses of her visions left the scientists baffled. 她的异象的超自然性使科学家们感到困惑。|
|The ancient texts spoke of the hypernaturalnesses of the gods. 古代文献讲述了众神的超自然性。|
|Near synonyms: supernaturalities, otherworldlinesses, transcendencies|
|Near antonyms: normalities, naturalnesses, mundanities|
