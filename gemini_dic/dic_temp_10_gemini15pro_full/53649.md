dischargee:/ˌdɪsˌtʃɑːrˈdʒiː/
noun
被解雇者；被释放者；退伍军人
a person who has been discharged from a hospital, the military, a job, or prison
The dischargee was given a small severance package.  这个被解雇的员工得到了一小笔遣散费。
The hospital dischargee went straight home to rest.  出院的病人直接回家休息了。
synonym: ex-employee, veteran, released prisoner, former patient
antonym: employee, recruit, inmate, patient
n.
