nontraditionalist:/ˌnɑːn.trəˈdɪʃ.ən.əl.ɪst/ | n. | 非传统主义者；不墨守成规的人 | A person who rejects traditional beliefs, customs, or practices. |  Many nontraditionalists are choosing to live together before marriage. 许多非传统主义者选择婚前同居。 | synonyms: unconventionalist, innovator, reformist, maverick, iconoclast | antonyms: traditionalist, conventionalist, conformist | noun

nontraditionalist:/ˌnɒn.trəˈdɪʃ.ən.əl.ɪst/ | adj. | 非传统的；不墨守成规的 | Not adhering to or in accordance with tradition. |  They took a nontraditional approach to education. 他们采取了一种非传统的教育方式。 | synonyms: unconventional, innovative, modern, progressive, avant-garde | antonyms: traditional, conventional, orthodox, conservative | adjective
