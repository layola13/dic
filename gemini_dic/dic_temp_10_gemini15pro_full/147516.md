overparking:/ˈoʊvərˌpɑːrkɪŋ/| n.|过度停车，停车过久|The act of parking a vehicle for a longer period than permitted or necessary.  This can refer to exceeding the time limit on a parking meter, staying in a parking space designated for a shorter duration, or occupying a space that causes inconvenience or obstruction to others.|He received a ticket for overparking. 他因为停车过久而收到罚单。|近义词: illegal parking, excessive parking|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
