moonshiny: /ˈmuːnˌʃaɪni/ | adj. | 月光照耀的; 私酿的(尤指威士忌酒); 充满幻想的; 不切实际的 | Illuminated by moonlight; (especially of whisky) illicitly distilled; dreamy and impractical; visionary | The moonshiny night was perfect for a romantic stroll. 月光照耀的夜晚非常适合浪漫的散步。 | near synonyms: moonlit, moon-bright; (for illicit alcohol) bootleg, contraband; (for dreamy) fanciful, whimsical | antonyms: dark, sunlit; (for illicit alcohol) legal; (for dreamy) practical, realistic | 词性: 形容词
