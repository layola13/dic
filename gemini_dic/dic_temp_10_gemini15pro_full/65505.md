eurosceptic:/ˌjʊərəˈskɛptɪk/ /ˌjʊroʊˈskɛptɪk/
noun, adjective|欧洲怀疑论者，对欧洲一体化持怀疑态度的|A person who is opposed to increasing the powers of the European Union.  Also used as an adjective to describe such a person or their views.|例句：He is a well-known eurosceptic. 他是一位著名的欧洲怀疑论者。|近义词：anti-EU, Europhobe|反义词：Europhile, pro-European|词性: n., adj.


Etymology:
Euro- (from Europe) + -sceptic (from Greek skeptikos, "inquiring, reflective," from skeptesthai "to look, consider").
