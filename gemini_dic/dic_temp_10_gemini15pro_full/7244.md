anglo-catholics:/ˈæŋɡləʊ ˈkæθəlɪks/| n.|英国国教高派教会信徒|Members of the High Church tradition within the Church of England who emphasize the Catholic aspects of its heritage, such as liturgical rituals and apostolic succession.|例句：The Anglo-Catholics often hold traditional views on church governance. 英国国教高派教会信徒通常对教会管理持传统观点。

例句：Some Anglo-Catholics prefer to be called "Anglo-Papalists." 一些英国国教高派教会信徒更喜欢被称为“盎格鲁-教皇主义者”。|近义词：High Churchmen, Anglo-Papalists|反义词：Low Churchmen, Evangelicals|词性: Noun


anglo-catholic:/ˈæŋɡləʊ ˈkæθəlɪk/| adj.|英国国教高派教会的|Relating to the High Church tradition within the Church of England, emphasizing Catholic aspects of its heritage.|例句：Anglo-Catholic beliefs are sometimes perceived as closer to Roman Catholicism than to Protestantism. 英国国教高派教会的信仰有时被认为比新教更接近罗马天主教。
