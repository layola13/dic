fissions:/ˈfɪʃənz/
n.
核裂变，分裂
the act or process of splitting the nucleus of an atom, causing a large release of energy
例句：Nuclear fissions release tremendous amounts of energy. 核裂变释放出巨大的能量。

例句：The fissions of uranium atoms are the basis of nuclear power. 铀原子的裂变是核能的基础。

近义词：splitting, division, cleavage
反义词：fusion, merging

词性：名词 (Noun)

词源：From Latin *fissio, from findere "to split."
