materializers:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪzərz/
n.|具体化的人（物）; 实现者|People or things that cause something to become real or concrete.|The materializers of this project worked tirelessly to bring their vision to life. 这个项目的实现者们不知疲倦地工作，使他们的愿景成为现实。|realizers, actualizers, implementers| - |noun
materialize:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪz/
v.|实现，具体化; 突然出现|To become real or concrete; to appear suddenly.|Her dreams of traveling the world finally materialized. 她环游世界的梦想终于实现了。|appear, emerge, manifest|disappear, vanish|verb
