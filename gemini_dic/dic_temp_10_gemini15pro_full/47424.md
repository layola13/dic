deadnesses:/ˈdɛdnɪsɪz/
n.
麻木；迟钝；沉闷；无生气
lack of vitality or responsiveness; dullness.
The deadnesses of her spirit made her indifferent to the beauty around her. 她精神上的麻木让她对周围的美景无动于衷。
apathy, dullness, numbness, insensibility
liveliness, vibrancy, sensitivity, responsiveness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From deadness + -es (plural suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *daudaz.
