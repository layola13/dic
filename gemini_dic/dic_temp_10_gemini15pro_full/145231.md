overbide:/ˌoʊvərˈbaɪd/| v.|出价过高；过分抑制|To bid more than is justified; to offer too high a price.  To repress or restrain excessively.|He overbid on the antique vase and ended up paying far too much. 他在古董花瓶上出价过高，最终付了太多钱。 She tried to overbide her feelings of grief, but they eventually overwhelmed her. 她试图抑制自己的悲伤，但最终还是被淹没了。|近义词：outbid, overestimate|反义词：underbid, underestimate|词性:动词
