alluviation:/əˌluːviˈeɪʃən/
n.
冲积；冲积层；冲积作用
the process of depositing alluvium (sediment such as sand, silt, or clay, deposited by flowing water) or the alluvium itself.
The fertile plains of the Nile are a result of centuries of alluviation. 尼罗河肥沃的平原是几个世纪冲积作用的结果。
The alluviation of the river delta created new farmland. 河流三角洲的冲积作用创造了新的农田。
synonyms: deposition, sedimentation, accretion
antonyms: erosion, denudation
noun
