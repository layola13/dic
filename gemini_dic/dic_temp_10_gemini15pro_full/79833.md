glyoxalic: /ɡlaɪɒkˈsælɪk/ /ɡliɑːkˈsælɪk/
noun|乙醛酸，草酸醛|An aldehyde and a carboxylic acid, occurring as a colorless crystalline solid or liquid.  It is an intermediate in the metabolism of glycine and other amino acids.|Glyoxalic acid is used in the production of vanillin.|近义词: oxoacetic acid|反义词: N/A|词性:n.

glyoxylate: /ɡlaɪˈɒksɪleɪt/  /ɡliˈɑːksɪleɪt/
noun|乙醛酸盐，乙醛酸酯|A salt or ester of glyoxalic acid.|Glyoxylate is an important intermediate in the glyoxylate cycle.|近义词: oxoacetate (for salts)|反义词: N/A|词性:n.
