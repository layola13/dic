mallam:/ˈmæləm/| n. |先生，老师，学者 (尤指西非穆斯林)|A title of respect for a learned man or teacher, especially in Muslim communities of West Africa. |例句：The mallam taught the children the Quran.  这位先生教孩子们古兰经。

例句：We sought advice from the local mallam. 我们向当地的学者寻求建议。

近义词：teacher, scholar, sir, mister|反义词:|n.(名词)
