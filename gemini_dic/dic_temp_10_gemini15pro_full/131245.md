noncarbonate:/nɒnˈkɑːrbənət/ | adj. | 非碳酸盐的 | Not containing carbonates; specifically (Geol) designating a rock or deposit containing no or only a negligible proportion of calcium or magnesium carbonate. |  Limestone is a carbonate rock, while shale is a noncarbonate rock. 石灰岩是碳酸盐岩，而页岩是非碳酸盐岩。| synonyms: non-calcareous | antonyms: carbonate | adjective
