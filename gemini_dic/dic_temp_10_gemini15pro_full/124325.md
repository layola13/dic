motherly:/ˈmʌðərli/| adj.|母性的；慈爱的|Characteristic of a mother; kind, loving, and protective.|She gave me a motherly hug. 她给了我一个母性的拥抱。|近义词：maternal, nurturing, affectionate|反义词：cold, uncaring|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English moderlich, from Old English mōdorlīc (“motherly”), from mōdor (“mother”) + -līc (a suffix forming adjectives).  Cognate with Dutch moederlijk (“motherly”), German mütterlich (“motherly”).
