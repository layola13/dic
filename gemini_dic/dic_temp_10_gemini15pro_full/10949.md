arbalest: /ˈɑːrbəlɪst/ | n. | 弩，十字弓 | A powerful crossbow used in medieval times.  A type of medieval crossbow, typically a large and powerful one, requiring a windlass or other mechanical device to draw. |  The arbalest was a fearsome weapon on the medieval battlefield.  中世纪战场上的弩是一种可怕的武器。 | Example: The knight raised his arbalest and aimed at the approaching enemy. 骑士举起弩，瞄准逼近的敌人。

Example:  Arbalests required considerable strength to operate.  操作弩需要相当大的力量。 | Synonyms: crossbow, ballista | Antonyms: bow, sling | noun
