disagreeing:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːɪŋ/
adjective
不同意的；有分歧的
present participle of disagree
They were disagreeing about the best way to proceed. 他们在就最佳的进行方式产生分歧。
近义词: differing, dissenting, objecting, opposing
反义词: agreeing, concurring, assenting
词性: adjective (形容词)
