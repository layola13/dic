nonthinkers:/ˌnɒnˈθɪŋkəz/ | n. |不思考者，不动脑筋的人|People who do not engage in critical thinking or thoughtful consideration.  Those who accept information passively without questioning or analyzing it. |The dictator surrounded himself with nonthinkers who would not challenge his authority. 独裁者周围都是些不动脑筋、不会挑战他权威的人。

The advertisement was clearly targeted towards nonthinkers who impulse buy. 这则广告显然是针对那些冲动购物、不动脑筋的人。|近义词：conformists, sheep, followers|反义词：thinkers, intellectuals, analysts|词性: 名词
