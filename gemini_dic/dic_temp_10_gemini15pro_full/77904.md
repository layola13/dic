genio:/ˈdʒiːnioʊ/ (US), /ˈdʒiːniəʊ/ (UK)| n.|天才，天赋，守护神|A person of exceptional intellectual or creative power or other natural ability.  A tutelary spirit or guiding genius.|例句：He was a musical genio. 他是一位音乐天才。

例句：She has a genio for languages. 她有语言天赋。

近义词：talent, gift, aptitude, genius, intellect, mastermind, prodigy, whiz, wizard|反义词:incompetence, inability, stupidity|词性:noun
