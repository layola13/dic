gurus:/ˈɡʊruːz/| n. |大师，专家，权威|A guru is a recognized leader or expert in a particular field. |例句：He is considered a guru in the tech industry. 他被认为是科技行业的权威。

例句：Many people seek advice from financial gurus. 很多人向金融专家寻求建议。


|近义词：expert, specialist, master, authority|反义词:amateur, novice, beginner|词性:noun
