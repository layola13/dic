franklinite: /ˈfræŋklɪˌnaɪt/
noun
锌铁尖晶石，弗兰克林矿
A black isometric mineral consisting of oxides of iron, zinc, and manganese. It is an ore of zinc.
Franklinite is found in New Jersey.  在新泽西州发现了锌铁尖晶石。
Synonyms:  zincite, gahnite
Antonyms:  None (It's a specific mineral, so it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性: noun
