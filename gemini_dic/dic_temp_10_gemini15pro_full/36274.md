cohabit:/koʊˈhæbɪt/ | v. |同居，共栖|To live together in the same dwelling place, especially as an unmarried couple.|例句：They decided to cohabit before getting married. 他们决定在结婚前同居。|例句：Many species of birds cohabit peacefully in the same forest. 许多种类的鸟在同一片森林里和平共处。|近义词：live together, cohabitate, shack up|反义词: live apart, separate|词性:动词

cohabiter:/koʊˈhæbɪtər/ | n. |同居者，共栖者|A person or animal that lives with another.|例句：The cohabiters decided to split the rent equally. 同居者决定平分房租。|例句：The two cohabiters were seen arguing outside their apartment. 这两个同居者被看到在他们的公寓外争吵。|近义词：housemate, roommate, partner, cohabitant|反义词: stranger, individual|词性:名词
