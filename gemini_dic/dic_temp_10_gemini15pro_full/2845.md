adventurously:/ədˈventʃərəsli/
adverb
冒险地；大胆地
In a way that is full of adventure; boldly.
She lived adventurously, traveling the world and trying new things. 她过着冒险的生活，周游世界，尝试新事物。

He adventurously climbed the highest mountain. 他大胆地攀登了最高的山峰。

近义词:boldly, daringly, bravely, recklessly
反义词:cautiously, timidly, carefully
词性:adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From adventure + -ous + -ly.  Ultimately from the Latin *adventura* "a thing about to happen".

USAGE->
She adventurously decided to quit her job and travel the world. = 她冒险地决定辞掉工作去环游世界。
He adventurously explored the unknown cave. = 他冒险地探索了那个未知的洞穴。


PHRASE->
travel adventurously = 冒险旅行
live adventurously = 过冒险的生活
explore adventurously = 冒险探索
act adventurously = 行事大胆
dress adventurously = 穿着大胆
