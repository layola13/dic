curcuma:/ˈkɜːrkjʊmə/ | n. | 姜黄，郁金 | A rhizomatous herbaceous plant of South Asia, the powdered root of which is widely used as a spice, especially in curries. |  Example: Curcuma is a key ingredient in many Asian dishes. 姜黄是许多亚洲菜肴的关键成分。

Example:  The curcuma plant has bright yellow flowers. 姜黄植物开着鲜艳的黄花。 | Synonym: turmeric | Antonym: N/A | Noun
