mischief-making:/ˈmɪstʃɪfˌmeɪkɪŋ/
noun
恶作剧，捣乱
the act of causing trouble or annoyance in a playful or mischievous way
The children were punished for their mischief-making in the classroom. 孩子们因为在教室里捣乱而受到惩罚。
Synonyms: troublemaking, mischief, naughtiness, prankishness
Antonyms: obedience, helpfulness, cooperation
n.

mischief:/ˈmɪstʃɪf/
noun
恶作剧，淘气，损害
playful misbehaviour or troublemaking, especially in children
The children were full of mischief. 孩子们很淘气。
Synonyms: trouble, prank, naughtiness
Antonyms: obedience, goodness

make:/meɪk/
verb
做，制造，使成为
to create or construct something
She made a cake for his birthday. 她为他做了一个生日蛋糕。
Synonyms: create, produce, construct
Antonyms: destroy, break, demolish
v.
