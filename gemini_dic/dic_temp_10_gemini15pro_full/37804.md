compendiarious:/kəmˌpɛn.diˈɛə.ri.əs/
adj.
简明的，摘要的，概括的
Containing or presenting the essential facts of something in a comprehensive but concise way.
This compendiarious history of the world is surprisingly detailed.  这本简明的世界史出奇地详尽。
近义词: concise, succinct, summary, abridged, condensed
反义词: prolix, verbose, lengthy, detailed, comprehensive
词性: adjective
