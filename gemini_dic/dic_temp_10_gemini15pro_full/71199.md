fistularioid:/ˌfɪstjʊˈlɛəriɔɪd/
adjective
管状的，似管的
resembling a fistula; tubular.
The fistularioid growth on the coral was concerning to the marine biologists.  珊瑚上管状的生长物让海洋生物学家感到担忧。
近义词:tubular, pipe-like
反义词:solid, compact
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *fistulārius (“of or pertaining to a pipe”), from fistula (“pipe, tube”) + -oid
