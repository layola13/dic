enacting:/ɪˈnæktɪŋ/
verb
制定；扮演；颁布
Putting a law or regulation into effect; performing a role or part.
The legislature is enacting a new law to protect consumers. 议会正在制定一项保护消费者权益的新法律。
She is enacting the role of Juliet in the play. 她在戏剧中扮演朱丽叶的角色。
synonyms: performing, passing, legislating, decreeing, ordaining, portraying, representing
antonyms: repealing, abolishing, revoking, ignoring
