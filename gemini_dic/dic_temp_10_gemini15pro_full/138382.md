notorhizal: /ˌnoʊtɔːˈraɪzəl/ | adj. | 非根瘤的 | Not forming root nodules, especially not forming root nodules containing nitrogen-fixing bacteria.  | Example:  Notorhizal plants must obtain nitrogen from other sources. 非根瘤植物必须从其他来源获取氮。

Example:  Researchers are studying the differences between notorhizal and nodulating plant species. 研究人员正在研究非根瘤植物和结瘤植物物种之间的差异。 | 近义词: non-nodulating | 反义词: nodulating | 词性: adjective

词源:  "not-" (meaning "not") + "rhizo-" (Greek root meaning "root") + "-al" (adjective suffix)
