athletic: /æθˈlɛtɪk/ | adj. | 运动的，强壮的 | Physically strong, active, and good at sports. |  She has a very athletic build. 她身材很健美。| synonyms: sporty, muscular, fit, strong, vigorous | antonyms: weak, frail, unfit, sedentary, inactive | adjective

athletic: /æθˈlɛtɪk/ | n. | 运动员（通常用复数形式）| A person who is proficient in sports and other forms of physical exercise. | The school's athletic program is very competitive. 这所学校的运动项目竞争非常激烈。 (referring to the athletes collectively) | synonyms: sportsperson, athlete, player | antonyms: N/A (no direct antonym for a noun) | noun (often plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin athleta, from Greek athletes "contestant in games," from athlos "contest," from athlein "to contend for a prize," related to athlon "prize."
