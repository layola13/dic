gemmuliferous:/ˌdʒɛmjʊˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
带有芽体的；产生芽体的
Bearing or producing gemmules.
Bryozoans are gemmuliferous, meaning they produce gemmules for asexual reproduction.  苔藓虫是带有芽体的，这意味着它们会产生用于无性繁殖的芽体。
Sponges and some freshwater bryozoans are gemmuliferous. 海绵和一些淡水苔藓虫都带有芽体。
Synonym: gemmiparous
Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider terms related to sexual reproduction)
word type: adjective
