atmospherics:/ˌætməsˈfɪrɪks/ | n. | 气氛，环境，（尤指商店、餐馆等场所的）氛围，声景 | The atmospherics of the restaurant were very romantic.  这家餐厅的氛围非常浪漫。|  The music and lighting are part of the atmospherics.  音乐和灯光是氛围的一部分。| ambiance, atmosphere, aura, vibe | sterility, dullness | noun


atmospheric:/ˌætməsˈfɛrɪk/ | adj. | 大气的，空气的，大气层的，有情调的，富有气氛的 | The atmospheric pressure is dropping. 大气压力正在下降。| The film had a very atmospheric soundtrack. 这部电影的配乐很有气氛。| aerial, airy, gaseous | unaesthetic, unartistic | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *atmosphérique*, from Ancient Greek *ἀτμός* (atmós, “vapor, steam”) + *σφαῖρα* (sphaîra, “sphere”).
