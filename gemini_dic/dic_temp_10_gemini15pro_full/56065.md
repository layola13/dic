docility:/dɑːˈsɪləti/ | n. | 温顺；驯服；可教性 | The docility of the dog made it easy to train. |  The horse's docility made it a pleasure to ride. 这匹马很温顺，骑起来很舒服。| 近义词: obedience, compliance, submissiveness, meekness | 反义词: stubbornness, defiance, rebelliousness, intractability | 词性: noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *docilite*, from Latin docilitās ("teachableness," "aptness to learn"), from docilis ("teachable," "apt to learn"), from docēre ("to teach"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *deḱ- ("to take," "accept," "learn").
