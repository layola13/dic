paler:/ˈpeɪlər/ (US), /ˈpeɪlə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更苍白的，更浅的|Comparative form of pale: lighter in color than usual, especially because of illness or fear.|Her face was paler than usual. 她的脸色比平时更苍白。|synonyms: lighter, whiter, ashen, wan |antonyms: darker, brighter, flushed, ruddy|adjective

paler:/ˈpeɪlər/ (US), /ˈpeɪlə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更淡的，更浅的|(of a color) less intense or bright than usual.|The paler shade of blue is more calming.  更淡的蓝色更令人平静。|synonyms: lighter, fainter, washed-out |antonyms: darker, brighter, deeper, richer|adjective
