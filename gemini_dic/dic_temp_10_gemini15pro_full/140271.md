offtake:/ˈɔːfteɪk/ /ˈɑːfteɪk/| n.|  销售量，承购量，消耗量，提取，支流 | The offtake of computers has slowed down recently. | 近义词:  sales, consumption, demand, uptake | 反义词: supply, production | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From "off" + "take," literally meaning "taken off" or "taken away."  The original meaning referred to a channel taking water off or away from a larger body of water.  This evolved to encompass the taking away of goods by purchase or consumption.
