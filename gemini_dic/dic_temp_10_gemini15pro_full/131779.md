nonconnectivity:/ˌnɒnkənɛkˈtɪvɪti/ | n. | 非连接性，不连通性 | Lack of connection or communication between things, especially parts of a network or system. | The network experienced periods of nonconnectivity due to server issues. 由于服务器问题，网络经历了断断续续的连接中断。|  The remote village suffered from nonconnectivity, hindering its economic development.  这个偏远村庄因缺乏连接而受阻，阻碍了其经济发展。| synonyms: disconnectedness, isolation, separation | antonyms: connectivity, connection, linkage | noun
