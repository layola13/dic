parachutists:/ˈpærəʃuːtɪsts/ | n. | 跳伞者；伞兵 | A person who jumps from an aircraft using a parachute. |  The parachutists landed safely in the designated drop zone. 跳伞员安全地降落在指定的空投区。

The parachutists jumped out of the plane one by one. 跳伞员一个接一个地跳出飞机。 | 近义词：skydiver | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From parachute + -ist.  "parachute" comes from the French words "para-" (meaning "against" or "to protect against") and "chute" (meaning "fall").
