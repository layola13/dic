brattier:/ˈbrætiər/ (US), /ˈbrætɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更讨厌的，更任性的|Comparative form of "bratty," meaning more spoiled, ill-mannered, and demanding.|例句：He's even brattier than his younger sister. 他比他妹妹更讨厌。

例句：The child became brattier after receiving too many toys.  这个孩子在收到太多玩具后变得更加任性了。|近义词：naughtier, more mischievous, more spoiled, more unruly|反义词：better-behaved, more obedient, more polite|词性:形容词
