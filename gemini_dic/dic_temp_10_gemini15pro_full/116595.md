matriculating:/məˈtrɪkjʊleɪtɪŋ/
verb
入学，注册
The act of enrolling or registering as a student at a college or university.
She is matriculating at Harvard University this fall. 她今年秋季将在哈佛大学入学。
enroll, register, enter
drop out, withdraw
v.
