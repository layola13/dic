muggur:/ˈmʌɡər/| n.| 傻瓜，笨蛋，容易受骗的人 |A muggur is a gullible or easily fooled person, often seen as naive or foolish.  It can also refer to someone considered stupid or unintelligent. | He was such a muggur, he believed everything the salesman told him. 他真是个傻瓜，竟然相信了销售员说的每一句话。|近义词：fool, idiot, dupe, sucker, chump |反义词: genius, sage, wise person | noun
