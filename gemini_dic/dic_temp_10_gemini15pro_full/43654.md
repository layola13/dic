creature-comfort:/ˈkriːtʃər ˈkʌmfərt/| n.|生活舒适；物质享受|Something that contributes to physical ease and well-being.|例句：After a long day of hiking, a hot shower and a soft bed were welcome creature comforts.  徒步旅行了一整天后，热水淋浴和柔软的床铺是受欢迎的舒适享受。

例句：He's not interested in creature comforts; he prefers a simple life. 他对物质享受不感兴趣；他更喜欢简单的生活。|近义词：luxury, amenity, convenience|反义词：hardship, deprivation|词性:noun
