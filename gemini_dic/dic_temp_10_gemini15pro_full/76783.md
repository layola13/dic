gamification:/ˌɡeɪmɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. |游戏化|The application of game-design elements and game principles in non-game contexts.  It can be used to engage users, motivate behavior, promote learning, and solve problems.|例句：Gamification can make learning more fun and engaging. 游戏化可以让学习更有趣、更吸引人。

例句：Many companies use gamification to increase customer loyalty. 许多公司使用游戏化来提高客户忠诚度。|近义词：ludification|反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "game" + "-ify" + "-cation". "Game" comes from Old English "gamen" meaning amusement, sport, or fun. "-ify" is a suffix meaning "to make or cause to be," derived from French "-ifier" and ultimately Latin "-ficare." "-cation" is a suffix forming nouns of action or process.
