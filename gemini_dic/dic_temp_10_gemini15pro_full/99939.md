internode:/ˈɪntərˌnoʊd/ (US), /ˈɪntəˌnəʊd/ (UK)| n. |节间；植物茎上两节之间的部分|The part of a plant stem between two successive nodes.|The botanist measured the internode length of the bamboo.  植物学家测量了竹子的节间长度。|Synonyms: segment, section|Antonyms: node|noun

internode:/ˈɪntərˌnoʊd/ (US), /ˈɪntəˌnəʊd/ (UK)| n. |（神经网络中的）节点间|In a neural network, the connection between two nodes.|The signal travels through the internode to reach the next processing unit. 信号通过节点间到达下一个处理单元。|Synonyms: connection, link|Antonyms: node|noun
