failingnesses:/ˈfeɪlɪŋnɪsɪz/
noun
缺点，不足之处
The state or quality of being prone to failure; a weakness or shortcoming.
His failingnesses as a leader ultimately led to the team's downfall. 他作为领导者的缺点最终导致了团队的垮台。
shortcomings, weaknesses, deficiencies, flaws
strengths, virtues, merits
n.
