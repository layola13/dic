词源:  From Middle English *doggen, from Old French dogier (“to move deviously, dodge, slink”), of uncertain origin. Possibly from or related to a Vulgar Latin *dŏticāre, from Latin dŏtěx, dŏticis (“vat, wine-jar”), hence to move around large obstacles.
