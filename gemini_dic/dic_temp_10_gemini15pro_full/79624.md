/ɡlɒˈseəriəl/ | /ɡlɑːˈseriəl/
adj.|词汇表的；术语表的|Relating to a glossary; containing or consisting of a glossary. |This glossarial section provides definitions for technical terms.  这个词汇表部分提供了技术术语的定义。|synonym: lexical, vocabulary | antonym: textual, non-lexical | adjective

/ɡlɒˈseəriəl/ | /ɡlɑːˈseriəl/
n.|词汇表；术语表|A glossary; a collection of glosses or explanations of words or terms. |The book includes a helpful glossarial at the end. 这本书末尾包含一个有用的词汇表。|synonym: glossary, lexicon, vocabulary | antonym:  N/A (A concrete noun like "glossary" doesn't have a direct antonym.) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *glossarialis*, from glossarium (“glossary”) + -alis (adjective suffix).  The noun form is a back-formation from the adjective.
