downsize:/ˈdaʊnsaɪz/| v. |缩小规模，裁员|To reduce the size and operating costs of a company, especially by reducing the number of employees.|The company downsized its workforce by 10%.  这家公司裁员10%。|Synonyms: reduce, decrease, shrink, cut back|Antonyms: expand, enlarge, increase, grow|verb

downsize:/ˈdaʊnsaɪz/| n. |缩小规模，裁员|The act of reducing the size and operating costs of a company, especially by reducing the number of employees.|The company announced a downsize in operations. 公司宣布缩减运营规模。|Synonyms: reduction, decrease, shrinking|Antonyms: expansion, enlargement, increase, growth|noun
