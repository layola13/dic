corpuscularity:/kɔːrˈpʌskjʊˈlærɪti/
n.
微粒性，粒子性
The corpuscularity of light was a major topic of debate in 17th-century physics.  17世纪物理学界对光的微粒性展开了激烈的辩论。
The theory emphasizes the corpuscularity of matter. 该理论强调物质的粒子性。
Synonym:  granularity, particularity
Antonym:  continuity, wave nature
noun
