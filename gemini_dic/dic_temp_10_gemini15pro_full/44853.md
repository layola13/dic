crusting:/ˈkrʌstɪŋ/| n. |结壳，硬皮|The formation of a hard outer layer on something.|The crusting on the wound indicated it was healing. 伤口上的结痂表明它正在愈合。|近义词：scab, coating, covering|反义词:softening, dissolving|noun

crusting:/ˈkrʌstɪŋ/| adj. |结壳的，形成硬皮的|Having or forming a hard outer layer.|The crusting bread was left out too long.  结块的面包放的时间太长了。|近义词：hardened, caked, solidified|反义词:soft, moist|adjective
