parading:/pəˈreɪdɪŋ/ | n. |游行，炫耀 |The act of walking or marching in a public procession, especially as a demonstration or celebration. Also, ostentatious display. |The soldiers were parading through the town after their victory. 士兵们在胜利后在城镇中游行。| Synonyms: marching, procession, flaunting, showing off | Antonyms: hiding, concealing | Verb (present participle)

parading:/pəˈreɪdɪŋ/ | adj. |炫耀的，游行的|Ostentatiously displaying something; walking or marching in a public procession. |She was parading her new handbag around the office.  她在办公室里炫耀她的新包。|Synonyms: ostentatious, showy, flamboyant |Antonyms: modest, unassuming| Verb (present participle)
