词源：entrepreneur + -ing.  "Entrepreneur" comes from the French word "entreprendre," meaning "to undertake."


注意事项:  While "entrepreneuring" is grammatically correct, it is less commonly used than "entrepreneurship" (the noun form) or  phrases like "being entrepreneurial," "engaging in entrepreneurship," or simply "starting a business."  Using these alternatives often sounds more natural.
