outgazes:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪzɪz/
verb
凝视胜过; 比...更能凝视
looks at someone or something for a longer time than they can
He outgazed his opponent in a staring contest. 他在瞪眼比赛中胜过对手。
Synonyms: outstare, stare down
Antonyms: glance, look away
词性: 动词(verb)
