gendering:/ˈdʒɛndərɪŋ/ |v. |赋予性别，性别化 |The process of ascribing gender to someone or something.  The act of constructing or representing something as masculine or feminine. |例句：The media's gendering of athletes often reinforces traditional stereotypes. 媒体对运动员的性别刻画通常会强化传统的刻板印象。

例句：The author discusses the gendering of language in her latest book. 作者在她最新的书中讨论了语言的性别化。|近义词：categorizing, labeling, classifying |反义词: degendering, neutralizing |词性: 动词


词源:
From the noun "gender" + "-ing" suffix, indicating the process or action of.  "Gender" itself derives from Old French "gendre" (kind, sort), ultimately from Latin "genus" (origin, type, race).
