harkness:/ˈhɑːrknəs/| n.|哈克尼斯教学法；研讨会；讨论会|A teaching method involving students sitting in a circle and discussing a topic, often with a teacher acting as a facilitator rather than a lecturer.  It emphasizes student-centered learning and active participation.  Also refers to the discussion itself.|The class thrived in the Harkness method, delving deep into complex texts.  这个班级在哈克尼斯教学法中蓬勃发展，深入研究复杂的文本。

The Harkness discussion today focused on the implications of artificial intelligence. 今天的哈克尼斯讨论集中讨论了人工智能的意义。|近义词：discussion, seminar, colloquium, roundtable|反义词:lecture, monologue|词性:名词
