charisma: /kəˈrɪzmə/ | n. | 魅力，感召力 | A special personal quality or power of attraction that enables a person to influence others. |  She has a natural charisma that draws people to her. 她有一种天生的魅力，吸引着人们靠近她。

| 近义词: charm, allure, magnetism, appeal | 反义词: repulsiveness, unattractiveness | 词性: noun


charisma: /kəˈrɪzmə/ | n. | 神授的力量或才能 |  A divinely conferred power or talent. | He believes his charisma for healing comes from God. 他相信他治愈的神授能力来自上帝。


词源: From Greek kharisma "divine gift, favor, grace," from kharizesthai "to show favor, bestow freely," from kharis "grace, favor, kindness."
