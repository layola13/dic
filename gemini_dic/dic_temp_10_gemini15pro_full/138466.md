novelism: /ˈnɒvəlɪzəm/ (US) /ˈnɒvəlɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |新奇事物；小说风格；小说笔法；小说化|The quality of being novel or unusual;  the act of novelizing or writing in a novelistic style.|His paintings are characterized by a certain novelism of technique. 他的绘画的特点是某种新颖的技巧。|近义词：innovation, novelty, originality|反义词：banality, cliché, staleness|词性：名词

Etymology:
From novel + -ism.  "Novel" ultimately derives from the Latin "novus" meaning "new".  The suffix "-ism" denotes a doctrine, practice, or characteristic.
