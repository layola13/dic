nonconformist:/ˌnɑːnkənˈfɔːrmɪst/ /ˌnɒnkənˈfɔːmɪst/
n. adj.
不墨守成规的人；不遵奉传统的人
a person who does not conform to prevailing ideas or established practice in behavior or thought.
例句：He was a nonconformist in his youth, rejecting the traditional values of his family.  他年轻时是个不墨守成规的人，拒绝接受家庭的传统价值观。

例句：She dressed as a nonconformist, eschewing popular fashion trends.  她的穿着打扮不落俗套，避开了流行的时尚趋势。

近义词：dissenter, maverick, individualist, rebel, iconoclast
反义词：conformist, traditionalist
词性：名词，形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + conformist.  "Conformist" comes from the Late Latin "conformis" (similar in form), from "com-" (with, together) + "formis" (form, shape).
