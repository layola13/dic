granule:/ˈɡrænjuːl/ (US), /ˈɡrænjuːl/ (UK)| n. |颗粒，小粒，微粒|A small, compact particle of a substance.|The dry fertilizer comes in granules.  这种干肥料是颗粒状的。|Example:  The sand on the beach was made up of tiny granules.  沙滩上的沙子是由细小的颗粒组成的。

|Synonyms: grain, particle, speck, bit, fragment|Antonyms: lump, mass, bulk|noun
