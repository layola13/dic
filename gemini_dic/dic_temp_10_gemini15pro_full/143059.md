outboards:/ˈaʊtbɔːrdz/
noun
舷外发动机；舷外马达
a detachable motor mounted at the stern of a small boat.
The fisherman started his outboard and headed out to sea. 渔夫启动了他的舷外发动机，驶向大海。

outboard:/ˈaʊtbɔːrd/
adjective
船外的；舷外的
situated on or towards the outside of a boat or aircraft.
The outboard motor sputtered and died. 船外发动机发出噼啪声，然后熄火了。
近义词: external, outer
反义词: inboard, internal
