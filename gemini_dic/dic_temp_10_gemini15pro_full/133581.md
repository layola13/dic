nonflammable:/ˌnɑːnˈflæməbəl/| adj.|不易燃的，不燃烧的|Not easily set on fire; not flammable.|例句：The curtains are made of nonflammable material.  窗帘是用不燃材料制成的。|例句：Always store flammable liquids away from nonflammable materials.  始终将易燃液体存放在远离不燃材料的地方。|近义词：fireproof, fire-resistant, incombustible|反义词：flammable, combustible|词性:形容词
Etymology: non- + flammable.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Flammable" comes from the Latin "flammare" meaning "to set on fire."
