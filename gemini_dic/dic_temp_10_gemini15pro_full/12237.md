ascariasis:/ˌæskəˈraɪəsɪs/| n.|蛔虫病|A disease caused by parasitic roundworms (Ascaris lumbricoides) in the small intestine.|例句：Ascariasis is a common intestinal infection in tropical and subtropical regions. 蛔虫病是热带和亚热带地区常见的肠道感染。

例句：The doctor diagnosed the child with ascariasis. 医生诊断这个孩子患有蛔虫病。|近义词：roundworm infection, helminthiasis|反义词: health|词性:noun

词源: From Modern Latin Ascaris + -iasis.  Ascaris comes from the Greek *askarís* meaning "intestinal worm."  The suffix *-iasis* denotes a diseased condition.
