anneal:/əˈniːl/| vt.|退火，使（金属、玻璃等）缓慢冷却；使变坚韧；使变软|To heat (metal or glass) and allow it to cool slowly, in order to remove internal stresses and toughen it.  To make (something, such as feelings) less strong or intense.|The glass is annealed to make it stronger.|近义词：temper, toughen, strengthen, soften, mitigate|反义词: harden, embrittle|verb
