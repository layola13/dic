defenders:/dɪˈfɛndərz/| n.|防御者，守卫者，拥护者|People or things that defend something or someone.  Those who protect or support something.|例句：The defenders of the castle fought bravely. 城堡的守卫者英勇作战。

例句：She is a staunch defender of animal rights. 她是一位坚定的动物权利拥护者。|近义词：protector, guardian, advocate, supporter|反义词：attacker, aggressor, opponent|noun
