hemophilia:/ˌhiːməˈfɪliə/ /ˌheməˈfɪliə/| n. |血友病|A medical condition, mostly affecting males, in which the blood does not clot normally, causing excessive bleeding from even minor injuries.|例句：Hemophilia is a genetic disorder. 血友病是一种遗传性疾病。

例句：He suffers from hemophilia. 他患有血友病。|近义词：bleeding disorder|反义词: (No direct antonym for a disease, but one could consider "normal blood clotting" as an opposite concept) |词性: noun
