disworkmanship: /dɪsˈwɜːkmənʃɪp/
n.
拙劣的工艺；粗制滥造
poor workmanship; faulty construction or craft
The disworkmanship of the cabinet was evident in the uneven drawers and poorly fitted doors.  这橱柜的做工很差，体现在抽屉不平整和门安装不当。
The crumbling facade of the building was a testament to its disworkmanship. 这栋建筑摇摇欲坠的正面证明了其糟糕的工艺。
近义词: bad workmanship, poor craft, shoddy work, faulty construction
反义词: good workmanship, excellent craft, fine work, quality construction
词性: 名词

Etymology:  "dis-" (prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of") + "workmanship" (skill in making things)
