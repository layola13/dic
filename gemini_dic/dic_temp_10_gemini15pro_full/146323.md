overfemininity: /ˌoʊvərˌfɛmɪˈnɪnɪti/
noun
过度女性化
An exaggerated display of stereotypically feminine behaviors and traits.
She felt pressured into overfemininity to fit in with the popular girls. 她感到压力，为了融入受欢迎的女孩群体而过度女性化。
His overfemininity made him the target of bullies. 他的过度女性化使他成为欺凌者的目标。
Near Synonyms: excessive femininity, exaggerated femininity
Antonyms: masculinity, androgyny
