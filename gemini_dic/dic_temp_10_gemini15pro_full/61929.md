emulatively:/ˈɛmjʊlətɪvli/
adverb|模拟地，竞争地|in a way that involves emulation; in an emulative manner|He studied emulatively, hoping to surpass his classmates. 他以竞争的心态学习，希望能超过他的同学们。|Example: The young artist painted emulatively, trying to capture the style of the masters.  这位年轻的艺术家模仿大师的风格作画。|Synonyms: imitatively, mimicking, competitively|Antonyms: originally, innovatively|词性: 副词

emulate:/ˈɛmjʊleɪt/
verb|模仿，效仿，同…竞争，仿真|match or surpass (a person or achievement), typically by imitation. To imitate with the intent of equaling or surpassing the model.|例句1：He tried to emulate the success of his older brother. 他试图效仿他哥哥的成功。|例句2：The software emulates the hardware of the older system.  这个软件模拟了旧系统的硬件。|近义词：imitate, copy, mimic, mirror, follow|反义词：originate, innovate|词性: 动词
