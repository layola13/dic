exterminates:/ɪkˈstɜːrmɪneɪts/
verb
消灭，根除
completely destroys or gets rid of something, especially something harmful
The pest control company exterminated the termites in the house.  害虫防治公司消灭了房子里的白蚁。
近义词: annihilates, eradicates, destroys, eliminates
反义词: creates, builds, cultivates, preserves
词性: verb
