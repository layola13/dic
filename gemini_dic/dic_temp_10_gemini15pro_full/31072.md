chamois:/ˈʃæmi/ /ʃæmˈwɑː/| n.|羚羊，麂皮，擦拭布|A type of mountain antelope or the soft leather made from its skin, also used to describe a highly absorbent cleaning cloth.|例句：The chamois gracefully leaped across the rocks. 羚羊优雅地跳过岩石。

例句：He used a chamois to dry the car. 他用麂皮擦干了汽车。|近义词：antelope, leather, cloth|反义词:None|词性:noun


chamois:/ˈʃæmi/ /ʃæmˈwɑː/| adj.|麂皮制的|Made from chamois leather.|例句：She wore chamois gloves for driving. 她戴着麂皮手套开车。

例句：The chamois cloth absorbed the spill quickly. 麂皮布迅速吸收了溢出物。|近义词：suede, leather|反义词:None|词性:adjective
