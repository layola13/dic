covariant:/koʊˈværiənt/| adj. n.|协变的，共变的；协变量|Varying in a way that corresponds with variations in something else.  (mathematics) Changing in the same manner as another variable.|例句：The length and the width of the rectangle are covariant. 长方形的长和宽是协变的。

例句：Covariance is an important concept in statistics. 协方差是统计学中的一个重要概念。|近义词：correlated, related, dependent|反义词:contravariant, invariant|词性:adjective, noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From co- (together) + variant (varying). Ultimately from Latin variare (to vary).
