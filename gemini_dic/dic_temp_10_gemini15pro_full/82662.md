guess:/ɡes/| v. n.| 猜测，推测；猜想；估计；认为；料想 |To form an opinion or give an answer about something when you do not know much or anything about it.| I guessed her age to be about 30. 我估计她大概30岁。|近义词:conjecture, surmise, estimate, predict, assume|反义词:know, calculate, ascertain|词性:动词, 名词

guess:/ɡes/| n.| 猜测，推测 |An attempt to give an answer or form an opinion when you are not sure whether you will be correct.| My guess is that she's gone to the library. 我猜她是去图书馆了。|近义词:conjecture, supposition, hypothesis, estimate|反义词:certainty, knowledge|词性:名词
