importune:/ɪmˈpɔːrtjuːn/ /ɪmˈpɔːrtuːn/
verb
恳求；不断要求；纠缠
to ask someone repeatedly and urgently to do something
例句：He importuned her to help him. 他恳求她帮助他。
例句：The children importuned their father for a new video game. 孩子们缠着爸爸要买新的电子游戏。
近义词：beg, beseech, implore, pester, badger
反义词：leave alone, ignore
词性：verb
