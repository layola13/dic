nonrepentance:/ˌnɒnrɪˈpɛntəns/
noun
不悔改，不忏悔
Lack of remorse or regret for wrongdoing; impenitence.
His nonrepentance for his crimes shocked the judge. 他对罪行毫无悔意，这让法官感到震惊。

The murderer showed no nonrepentance for his actions.  凶手对自己的行为没有表现出任何悔意。

近义词: impenitence, remorselessness, obduracy
反义词: repentance, remorse, contrition
词性: noun
