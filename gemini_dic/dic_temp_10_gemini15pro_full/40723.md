convexedly:/kənˈvɛksɪdli/ | adv. | 凸地，凸起地 | In a convex manner; outwards.  Describing something that curves or bulges outwards. | The lens was convexedly shaped, magnifying the image. 镜头呈凸形，放大了图像。| The surface bulged convexedly. 表面凸起。 | 近义词: outwardly, bulgingly | 反义词: concavely, inwardly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From convex + -ed + -ly.  "Convex" comes from Latin "convexus,"  past participle of "convehere" (to bring together, carry together), from "com-" (together) + "vehere" (to carry).
