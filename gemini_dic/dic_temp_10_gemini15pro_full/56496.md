domestic:/dəˈmestɪk/ (US) /dɒˈmestɪk/ (UK)| adj. n.|国内的，家庭的；家养的，驯养的|Relating to the home, household, or family affairs; existing or occurring inside a particular country; not foreign or international; (of an animal) tame and kept by humans.|例句：She enjoys domestic life. 她喜欢家庭生活。|近义词：home, household, family, local, national, native|反义词：foreign, international, wild|词性:形容词

domestic:/dəˈmestɪk/ (US) /dɒˈmestɪk/ (UK)| n.|佣人，家仆|A servant who works in a person's home.|例句：They employed a domestic to help with the housework.  他们雇了一个佣人来帮忙做家务。|近义词：servant, maid, housekeeper|反义词：employer, master|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin domesticus "belonging to the house," from domus "house."
