hypermilitant:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɪlɪtənt/| adj.|极端好战的，极端激进的|Excessively or aggressively militant; extremely devoted to a cause and willing to use violence to achieve it.|The hypermilitant group claimed responsibility for the bombing. 极端好战的组织声称对爆炸事件负责。|The hypermilitant faction within the party pushed for immediate war. 该党内极端激进的派系推动立即开战。|Synonyms: extremist, radical, fanatic, zealot|Antonyms: moderate, pacifist|Adjective
