backfield: /ˈbækˌfiːld/ | n. | 后场，后卫组 |The area of a football field behind the line of scrimmage.  The group of offensive players who line up behind the line of scrimmage, typically including the quarterback, running backs, and fullback. | The quarterback dropped back into the backfield. 四分卫后退到后场。| The coach was impressed with the team's strong backfield. 教练对球队强大的后卫组印象深刻。 | synonyms: offensive backfield, back line | antonyms: frontline, forward line | noun

backfield: /ˈbækˌfiːld/ | adj. | 后场的 |Of or relating to the backfield. |  The backfield players practiced their passing drills. 后场球员练习传球。 |  The coach made a backfield adjustment during the timeout.  教练在暂停期间调整了后场。| synonyms: rear, posterior | antonyms: front, anterior | adjective
