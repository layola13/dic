dammed:/dæmd/ | adj. | 被诅咒的；该死的；筑坝的 |  Built or provided with a dam. dammed:/dæmd/ | v. | 筑坝；诅咒 (damn的过去式和过去分词) | Past participle of "damn."  Can refer to the act of building a dam or cursing something/someone.  | The engineers dammed the river to create a reservoir. 工程师们在河上筑坝，建造了一个水库。 | He dammed the government for its inaction. 他咒骂政府不作为。| synonyms: cursed, damned | antonyms: blessed | verb
