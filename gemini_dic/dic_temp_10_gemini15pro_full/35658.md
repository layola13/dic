cobalance:/ˈkoʊˌbæləns/ /kəʊˈbæləns/| n. |钴平衡，钴含量|The amount or proportion of cobalt present in something, especially in an alloy.|The cobalance of the steel was carefully controlled.|近义词：cobalt content|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

cobalance:/ˈkoʊˌbæləns/ /kəʊˈbæləns/| vt. |使钴含量平衡|To adjust or maintain the cobalt content of something.|They cobalanced the alloy to improve its magnetic properties.|近义词：adjust cobalt content|反义词: N/A|词性:动词
