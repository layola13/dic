Etymology:
From Late Latin *chaoticalis, from Latin chaos (“gaping void, vast chasm, immense abyss, primordial emptiness, formless state of matter before creation of distinct forms”) + -alis, equivalent to chaos +‎ -ical.  Ultimately from Ancient Greek χάος (kháos, “yawning void, chasm, abyss”).
