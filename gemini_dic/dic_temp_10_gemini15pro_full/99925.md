internee:/ˌɪn.tɜːrˈniː/ /ˌɪn.tərˈniː/
n.
实习生；扣留者；被拘禁者
A person who is confined or restricted in their movements, especially during wartime or for political reasons; a person who is receiving training by working for a short period in a particular job.
例句：The Japanese internees were held in camps during World War II.  二战期间，被拘禁的日本人被关在集中营里。

例句：The summer intern learned a lot about marketing during her internship.  暑期实习生在实习期间学到了很多关于市场营销的知识。

近义词：detainee, prisoner, captive, trainee, apprentice
反义词：employee, worker, citizen

词性: noun

词源：From French *interné*, past participle of *interner* (“to confine”), from Latin internus (“internal”). The sense of "trainee" arose in the U.S. c. 1935.
