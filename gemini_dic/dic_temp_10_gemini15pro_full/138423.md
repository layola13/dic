nounless:/ˈnaʊnlɪs/| adj.|无名词的；没有名词的|Lacking a noun or nouns; without nouns.  Describes a phrase, clause, or sentence that doesn't contain a noun functioning as a subject or object. |例句：The sentence "Run!" is nounless.  句子“跑！”是无名词的。|近义词：nominal, substantive (these are opposites in terms of *having* a noun, but can be used in similar grammatical contexts)|反义词: nominal, substantive |词性: adjective
