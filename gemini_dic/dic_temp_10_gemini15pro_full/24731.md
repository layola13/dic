bronzist: /ˈbrɒnzɪst/ (US) /ˈbrɒnzɪst/ (UK)| n.|青铜器工匠；青铜器收藏家|A person who works with bronze or collects bronze objects. |例句：The bronziers were hard at work, creating intricate sculptures. 青铜器工匠们正在努力工作，创作复杂的雕塑。

例句：He was a renowned bronzier, with a vast collection of ancient artifacts. 他是一位著名的青铜器收藏家，收藏了大量的古代文物。|近义词：metalworker, sculptor, collector|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

词源:  From French *bronze*, from Italian *bronzo*. Ultimately from Late Latin *Brundisium*, referring to the city of Brindisi, Italy, a center of bronze trade.  The suffix "-ist" denotes a person who practices or is involved with something.
