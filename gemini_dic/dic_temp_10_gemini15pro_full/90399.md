howler:/ˈhaʊlər/ (US), /ˈhaʊlə/ (UK)| n.|嚎叫; 咆哮; 滑稽可笑的错误;  |A loud howl, as of a dog, wolf, or other animal. A ridiculously funny mistake or blunder.|The comedian's jokes were such howlers that the audience roared with laughter. 这位喜剧演员的笑话滑稽得让观众哄堂大笑。|Synonyms: blunder, gaffe, error, mistake, howler, scream, yell, shriek, roar |Antonyms: whisper, murmur, silence|noun
