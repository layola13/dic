indexes:/ˈɪndɛksiːz/| n.|索引; 指数;指标|A list of words or phrases ('headings') and associated pointers ('locators') to where useful material relating to that heading can be found in a document or collection of documents.  A number or ratio (a ‘statistic’) that measures change.|The library has an index of all its books. 图书馆有所有藏书的索引。|synonyms:  indices, directory, register, catalog, table of contents|antonyms:  disorder, disorganization|noun
