coexisting:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪstɪŋ/
adjective
共存的
Existing together or at the same time.
Different species of animals coexisting in the same habitat.  不同种类的动物在同一栖息地共存。
The two cultures coexisted peacefully for centuries. 这两种文化和平共处了几个世纪。
近义词: cohabiting, concurrent, simultaneous
反义词: conflicting, incompatible, separate
词性: adjective

词源:
From co- + existing, present participle of exist. Co- means "together," and exist means "to have being."
