corallaceous:/ˌkɒrəˈleɪʃəs/ | adj. | 珊瑚色的，珊瑚红的 | Of a colour resembling that of coral; reddish pink. | The dress she wore was a beautiful corallaceous hue, perfect for the summer wedding. 她穿的裙子是美丽的珊瑚色，非常适合夏季婚礼。 | The sunset painted the sky in corallaceous tones.  日落将天空染成了珊瑚色。 | 近义词: coral, pinkish, salmon | 反义词: turquoise, azure | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *corallinus*, from *corallium* "coral".
