anti-philosophical:/ˌæntiˌfɪləˈsɒfɪkəl/ | adj. | 反哲学的，反对哲学的 | Opposed to or distrustful of philosophy; rejecting the relevance or value of philosophical inquiry. |  His anti-philosophical stance surprised many of his colleagues who had considered him a deep thinker.  他反哲学的立场让很多曾经认为他思想深刻的同事感到惊讶。 | Example 2: The artist's work expresses an anti-philosophical sentiment, favoring emotional experience over intellectual analysis. 这位艺术家的作品表达了一种反哲学的情绪，倾向于情感体验而非理性分析。 | synonyms:  unphilosophical,  non-philosophical | antonyms: philosophical, pro-philosophical | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"anti-" (prefix): from Greek "anti" meaning "against, opposite of"
"philosophical": from Late Latin "philosophicus" and Greek "philosophos" meaning "lover of wisdom"
