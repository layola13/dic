nonextenuating:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstenjueɪtɪŋ/
| adj. | 非减轻罪行的，没有情有可原的 | Not lessening the seriousness of an offense or mistake; without mitigating circumstances. |  The judge found no nonextenuating circumstances and imposed the maximum sentence. 法官没有发现任何可以减轻罪行的理由，并判处了最高刑罚。|  The jury believed there were nonextenuating factors in the case. 陪审团认为案件中没有可以减轻罪行的因素。| 近义词: incriminating, aggravating | 反义词: extenuating, mitigating | 词性: adjective


词源:
"Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "extenuating" (from Latin "extenuare," meaning "to make thin, lessen").
