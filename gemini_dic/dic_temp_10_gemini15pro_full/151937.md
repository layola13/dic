pastilles:/ˈpɑːstɪlz/ /pɑːˈstiːlz/ | n. |含片；糖果；香丸|Small, flavored tablets, often medicated, that dissolve slowly in the mouth.  They can be for soothing sore throats, freshening breath, or delivering a dose of vitamins or other supplements.  Also refers to small, colored disks of opaque glass used in mosaic work. |例句：
The doctor recommended cough pastilles to soothe her throat. 医生建议她服用止咳含片来舒缓喉咙。

She bought some peppermint pastilles to freshen her breath. 她买了一些薄荷糖来清新口气。


|近义词：lozenges, tablets, candies, sweets (for throat/breath), tesserae (for mosaic) |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
