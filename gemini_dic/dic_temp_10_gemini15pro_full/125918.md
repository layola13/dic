musette:/mjuːˈzɛt/ | n. | 小风笛，轻快的乐曲，（士兵的）小背包 | A small bagpipe with a bellows, played with a chanter and drones; a piece of music suited to such an instrument; a small bag, especially one carried by a soldier. | He played a lively tune on his musette. 他用他的小风笛演奏了一曲轻快的乐章。 | Synonyms: bagpipe, small bag, knapsack | Antonyms: N/A | noun

musette:/mjuːˈzɛt/ | n. |  轻快的小步舞曲 |  A musical piece, originally for the musette (bagpipe), with a drone bass and usually in 3/4 time, later any composition in this style. |  The musette was a popular dance in the Baroque period.  这种轻快的小步舞曲在巴洛克时期很流行。| Synonyms:  gavotte, minuet | Antonyms: N/A | noun
