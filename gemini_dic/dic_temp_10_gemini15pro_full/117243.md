meccan's:/məˈkænz/ | n. | 技工的（东西）; 机械师的（东西） |  Belonging to or associated with a mechanic.  This is a possessive form of "meccan," a shortened, informal term for "mechanic." While "mechanic's" is the standard and preferred possessive, "meccan's" might be used in very informal spoken language. | Example: That's the meccan's toolbox over there. 那是那边那个机械师的工具箱。| Synonym: mechanic's | Antonym: N/A | Possessive Noun
