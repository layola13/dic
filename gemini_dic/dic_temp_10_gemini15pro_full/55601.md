disusage:/dɪsˈjuːsɪdʒ/ | n. | 不使用，废弃 | Lack of use; discontinuance of use or practice. | The disusage of certain words can make texts from the past difficult to understand.  某些词语的废弃使得过去的文本难以理解。 |  The disusage of old traditions is a common consequence of modernization.  旧传统的废弃是现代化的常见结果。 |  近义词：disuse, obsolescence | 反义词：usage, use | 词性：名词

disusage:/dɪsˈjuːsɪdʒ/ | n. | 不使用，废弃 |  From dis- "not, opposite of" + usage.  | The disusage of this technology has led to a decline in its production.  这项技术的不使用导致其产量下降。| Archaic words fall into disusage over time. 古老的词语随着时间的推移而废弃。| 近义词：disuse, obsolescence, neglect | 反义词：usage, use, practice | 词性：名词
