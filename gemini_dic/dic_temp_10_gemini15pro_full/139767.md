octagynous:/ɒkˈtæɡɪnəs/
adjective
八雌蕊的；有八个雌蕊的
Having eight pistils (the female reproductive part of a flower).
例句：The botanist identified the rare octagynous flower.  植物学家鉴定出了这种稀有的八雌蕊花。

例句：Octagynous plants are less common than those with fewer pistils.  八雌蕊的植物比雌蕊较少的植物少见。
