knitster:/ˈnɪt.stər/
noun
编织者；针织工
A person who knits, especially one who does so professionally or habitually.
She's a skilled knitster, creating intricate designs with ease. 她是一位技艺精湛的编织者，轻松地创造出复杂的图案。
knitter, crocheter
weaver
