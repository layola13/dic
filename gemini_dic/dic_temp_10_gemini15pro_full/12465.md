aspalathus:/ˌæspəˈleɪθəs/
n.
红灌木；南非茶；芦笋豆
A South African shrub (genus Aspalathus) of the pea family.  Several species, especially A. linearis, are used to make rooibos tea.
Rooibos tea is made from the aspalathus plant.  红灌木茶是由芦笋豆植物制成的。
synonym: rooibos, red bush
词性: noun
