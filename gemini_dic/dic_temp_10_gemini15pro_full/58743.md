dungarees:/ˌdʌŋɡəˈriːz/
n.
工作裤，背带裤
Loose-fitting trousers, usually of denim, with a bib and shoulder straps.
He wore a pair of faded dungarees. 他穿了一条褪色的背带裤。

近义词:overalls, bib overalls
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "formal wear")
词性:noun
