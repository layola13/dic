hyria:/ˈhaɪriə/| n.|海瑞亚（一种淡水双瓣贝）|A hyria is a genus of freshwater mussels, aquatic bivalve mollusks in the family Unionidae, the river mussels.|The hyria is a common sight in the rivers of South America.|近义词：freshwater mussel, bivalve, unionid|反义词:无|词性:名词

词源: From Ancient Greek ὑρίον (hūríon, “small leather flask, small pot”).  The shape of the shell resembles these objects.
