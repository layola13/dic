camouflages:/ˈkæməˌflɑːʒɪz/ | v. | 伪装，掩饰 |  To disguise or conceal by making something appear to be part of its natural surroundings.  | The soldiers camouflaged themselves with leaves and branches. 士兵们用树叶和树枝伪装自己。 |  conceals, disguises, masks | reveals, exposes | verb

camouflage:/ˈkæməˌflɑːʒ/ | n. | 伪装，掩饰 | The act, means, or result of camouflaging something. | The chameleon's camouflage allows it to blend in with its surroundings. 变色龙的伪装使它能够与周围环境融为一体。 |  disguise, mask, concealment | exposure, revelation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *camoufler*, "to disguise," from Italian *camuffare*,  "to blindfold, to disguise," of uncertain origin, perhaps from *ca-*, an intensive prefix + *muffare*, "to muffle."
