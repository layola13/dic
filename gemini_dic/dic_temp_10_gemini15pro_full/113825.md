maddening:/ˈmædənɪŋ/ (US), /ˈmædnɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|令人发狂的，使人恼火的|Extremely annoying or frustrating.|例句：The maddening traffic made me late for work. 令人抓狂的交通让我上班迟到了。|例句：The constant dripping of the faucet was maddening. 水龙头不停地滴水真是烦死人了。|近义词：infuriating, exasperating, frustrating, annoying|反义词：soothing, calming, relaxing|词性:形容词
Etymology: From mad + -en (to make or become) + -ing (present participle suffix).  Ultimately from Old English *gemædan "to make mad".
