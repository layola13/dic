fulgurite:/ˈfʊlɡəˌraɪt/| n. |闪电熔岩；雷击石|A glassy tube formed in sand or rock by the discharge of lightning. |例句：The hikers discovered a fulgurite on the mountaintop, a testament to a powerful lightning strike.  登山者在山顶发现了一块闪电熔岩，证明这里曾遭受过强烈的雷击。

例句：Fulgurites are often sought after by collectors for their unique and natural beauty.  闪电熔岩因其独特而自然的美丽而经常受到收藏家的追捧。|近义词：petrified lightning|反义词: None|词性:noun
