paralysingly:/ˈpærəlaɪzɪŋli/
|adv.|令人麻痹地，使人瘫痪地|Causing a person or part of the body to become partly or wholly incapable of movement.  It can also describe a situation that makes someone feel powerless to act.|The fear was paralysingly intense.  这种恐惧强烈得令人麻痹。|近义词：cripplingly, debilitatingly, incapacitatingly|反义词: invigoratingly, energizingly|词性:副词
Etymology: From paralyse + -ing + -ly.  "Paralyse" comes from French "paralyser," ultimately from Greek "paralyein" (to disable), from "para-" (beside) + " lyein" (to loosen).
