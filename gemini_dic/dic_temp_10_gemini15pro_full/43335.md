crampest:/ˈkræmpɪst/| adj.|最狭窄的，最拥挤的，最受限制的|Superlative form of "cramped," meaning extremely confined and uncomfortable.|The crampest room I've ever seen was barely big enough to stand in. 我见过的最狭窄的房间小到几乎站不下。|The attic room was the crampest space in the whole house. 阁楼房间是整栋房子里最狭窄的空间。|近义词: tightest, smallest, most confined|反义词: roomiest, largest, most spacious|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "cramp" + "-est" (superlative suffix).  "Cramp" originates from Old North French "cramper," meaning to bend, hook, or fasten.
