nonphotographically:/ˌnɒnˌfəʊtəˈɡræfɪkli/
adv.
非摄影地；不用照相地
in a way that is not photographic; without using photography
The scene was described nonphotographically, focusing on the emotions it evoked.  这个场景是用非摄影的方式描述的，重点在于它唤起的情感。
non-photographically (variant spelling)
photographically (antonym)
adverb
