blastophore:/ˈblæstəˌfɔːr/| n. |胚孔|The opening of the archenteron in the gastrula that develops into the mouth in protostomes and the anus in deuterostomes.|The blastophore is a crucial structure in embryonic development. 胚孔是胚胎发育中的关键结构。|The position of the blastophore determines whether the animal is a protostome or a deuterostome. 胚孔的位置决定了该动物是原口动物还是后口动物。|Synonym: archenteron pore|Antonym: N/A|noun
