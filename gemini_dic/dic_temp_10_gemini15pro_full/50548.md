deputations:/ˌdɛpjuˈteɪʃənz/
n.
代表团；派遣；委托
A group of people appointed to represent others on a mission or at a conference.
The city council sent a deputation to the state capital to lobby for more funding.  市议会派了一个代表团到州首府去游说以获得更多资金。

The trade union sent a deputation to meet with the management. 工会派了一个代表团与管理层会面。
