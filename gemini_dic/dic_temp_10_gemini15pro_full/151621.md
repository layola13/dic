participative:/pɑːrˈtɪsɪpətɪv/ | adj. | 参与式的，共同参与的 | Encouraging or allowing everyone to take part or become involved in something. |  例句：The company has a participative management style.  公司采用参与式管理风格。| 近义词：collaborative, democratic, joint | 反义词：autocratic, dictatorial | 词性: 形容词

participative:/pɑːrˈtɪsɪpətɪv/ | adj. | 参与式的，共同参与的 | Characterized by or involving participation. | 例句：The workshop adopted a participative approach to learning.  该研讨会采用了一种参与式的学习方法。 | 近义词：interactive, shared | 反义词：authoritarian, non-participatory | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From participate + -ive.  "Participate" comes from the Latin "participatus," the past participle of "participare," meaning "to share in," from "pars" (part) + "capere" (to take).
