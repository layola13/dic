detasseling:/diːˈtæs(ə)lɪŋ/
n.| 除须; 去雄|The process of removing the tassel (the pollen-producing part) from the top of corn plants, typically to control pollination and produce hybrid seed. |Detasseling corn is a common summer job for teenagers in the Midwest. 在美国中西部，玉米除须是青少年常见的暑期工作。|Synonyms:  emasculation (in plants), tassel removal|Antonyms: pollination|noun
