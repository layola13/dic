conformability:/kənˌfɔːrməˈbɪləti/ | n. | 一致性; 符合性; 适应性 | The property of being able to conform or be conformed; the quality of being in agreement or harmony.  | The conformability of the new software to existing systems is a key factor in its success.  新软件与现有系统的一致性是其成功的关键因素。|synonyms: compatibility, conformity, compliance, adaptability | antonyms: incompatibility, nonconformity, noncompliance, rigidity | noun

conformability:/kənˌfɔːrməˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可塑性; 可模塑性 |  The ability of a material to be molded or shaped. | The conformability of clay makes it ideal for pottery. 粘土的可塑性使它成为制作陶器的理想材料。| synonyms: malleability, plasticity, ductility | antonyms: rigidity, stiffness, inflexibility | noun
