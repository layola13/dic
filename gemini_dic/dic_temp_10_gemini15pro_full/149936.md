palliatively:/ˈpæliətɪvli/ /ˈpælɪətɪvli/| adv.|姑息地，缓解地，治标不治本地|Relieving pain or alleviating a problem without dealing with the underlying cause.|The patient was given morphine palliatively to ease his suffering.  病人被给予了吗啡以姑息治疗，减轻痛苦。|Synonym:  remedially, soothingly, symptomatically|Antonym: curatively, radically|Part of Speech: adverb

palliate:/ˈpælieɪt/ /ˈpælɪeɪt/| vt.|减轻，缓和|To make a disease or its symptoms less severe or unpleasant without removing the cause.|The doctor prescribed medication to palliate the patient's pain.  医生开了药来缓解病人的疼痛。|Synonym: alleviate, ease, mitigate, relieve, soothe|Antonym: aggravate, exacerbate, worsen|Part of Speech: verb
