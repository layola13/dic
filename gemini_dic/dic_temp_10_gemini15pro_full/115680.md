margarous: /mɑːrˈɡɛrəs/ | adj. | 似珍珠的，珍珠似的 | Resembling a pearl; pearly. | The dress was adorned with margarous beads. 这件衣服上装饰着珍珠般的小珠子。 | The inside of the shell had a margarous sheen. 贝壳的内部有着珍珠般的光泽。 | 近义词: pearly, nacreous, iridescent | 反义词: dull, matte | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *margarōsus, from margarita (“pearl”). Ultimately from Ancient Greek μαργαρίτης (margarítēs, “pearl”).
