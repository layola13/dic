antidemocratical: /ˌæntiˌdɛməˈkrætɪkəl/ | adj. | 反民主的；非民主的 | Opposed to or violating the principles of democracy. |  The king's actions were seen as antidemocratical and led to widespread protests. 国王的举动被视为反民主的，并引发了广泛的抗议。

The antidemocratical regime suppressed all forms of dissent.  这个反民主的政权压制了所有形式的异议。 | 近义词: undemocratic, authoritarian, totalitarian, despotic | 反义词: democratic, egalitarian, liberal | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->

From anti- + democratical.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to." "Democratical" is derived from "democracy," which ultimately comes from the Greek words "demos" (people) and "kratos" (power).
