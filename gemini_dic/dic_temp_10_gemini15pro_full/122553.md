models:/ˈmɑːdəlz/ (US) /ˈmɒdlz/ (UK)| n.|模型，模特儿|A simplified representation of something, often used for testing or explaining. Also, a person who poses for artists or displays clothes.|

例句：
The architect built a model of the proposed building.  建筑师建造了拟建建筑的模型。

They used animal models to test the new drug. 他们用动物模型来测试新药。


近义词：representations, examples, mannequins, prototypes|
反义词: reality, original|
词性: noun


models:/ˈmɑːdəlz/ (US) /ˈmɒdlz/ (UK)| v.|模仿，塑造|To create a representation of something, or to shape something.|

例句：
He modeled the clay into a small figure. 他用粘土塑造了一个小雕像。

She models for a living. 她以当模特为生。


近义词：shape, form, imitate, emulate|
反义词: destroy, deform|
词性: verb
