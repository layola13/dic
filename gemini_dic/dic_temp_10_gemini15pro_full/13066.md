astrolabical:/ˌæstrəˈleɪbɪk(ə)l/
adjective
星盘的；与星盘有关的
Relating to or resembling an astrolabe, an ancient astronomical instrument used for determining the altitude of celestial bodies.
The museum displayed several astrolabical instruments from the 17th century.  博物馆展出了几件17世纪的星盘仪器。
The intricate design of the clock face was reminiscent of astrolabical markings.  钟面上复杂的图案让人联想起星盘上的刻度。
Synonyms: astronomical, celestial
Antonyms: terrestrial, earthly
词性: adjective
