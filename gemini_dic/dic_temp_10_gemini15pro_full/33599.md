cimiss:/ˈsiːmɪs/| n. |中国气象信息综合分析处理系统 (China Meteorological Information Comprehensive Analysis and Process System)|A comprehensive meteorological information system developed and operated by the China Meteorological Administration (CMA).  It integrates various data sources and provides tools for meteorological analysis and forecasting.|The CIMISS system plays a crucial role in China's weather forecasting and disaster prevention efforts. 中国气象信息综合分析处理系统在中国的天气预报和防灾减灾工作中发挥着至关重要的作用。|近义词:  meteorological system, weather information system|反义词: None|词性: noun
