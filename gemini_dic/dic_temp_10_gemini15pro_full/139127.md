obelion:/oʊˈbiːliən/| n. | 顶孔点 |The point on the sagittal suture of the skull midway between the parietal foramina.  颅骨矢状缝上顶骨孔之间中点。|The obelion is an important landmark in craniometry. 顶孔点是颅骨测量学中的一个重要标志。|Synonym:  lambda (in some contexts, though lambda refers to the junction of the sagittal and lambdoid sutures)  |Antonym: None (anatomical term with no direct opposite)|noun
