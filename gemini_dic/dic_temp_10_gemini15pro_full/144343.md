outstare:/ˌaʊtˈsteər/| vt.| 凝视；盯视；胜过(用眼神)|To stare at someone longer or more intensely than they stare at you, until they look away.  To defeat or subdue by staring.|He outstared his opponent across the table. 他凝视着桌子对面的对手，直到对方移开视线。|近义词：gaze, glare, stare down|反义词: glance, avert one's eyes|verb
