europeans:/jʊərəˈpiːənz/ | n. |欧洲人|People who are from Europe or have European ancestry. | Many Europeans travel within the continent during the summer. 许多欧洲人在夏天会在欧洲大陆旅行。| Europeans enjoy a variety of cuisines. 欧洲人喜欢各种各样的美食。|近义词：Continentals (more general, includes all of Europe and its islands) |反义词: Non-Europeans| noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Europe + -an (suffix forming adjectives and nouns from nouns, meaning "belonging to, relating to, or resembling"). "Europe" itself comes from the Greek name "Eurṓpē," of uncertain ultimate origin.
