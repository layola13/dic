domination:/ˌdɑːmɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. | 支配，统治，控制 | The domination of the market by a few large companies.  几家大公司对市场的支配。 | Example: The United States achieved military domination after World War II. 美国在二战后取得了军事上的支配地位。

Example: Her domination of the conversation made it difficult for others to speak. 她对谈话的掌控使得其他人难以插嘴。 | Synonyms:  supremacy, hegemony, mastery, ascendancy | Antonyms: subordination, subjection, dependence | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dominatio, from Latin dominari (“to rule, dominate”), from dominus (“lord, master”).
