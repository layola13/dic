high-graded:/haɪˈɡreɪdɪd/
verb (past participle)|高品位开采；精选；优选；|To extract the best or most valuable part of something, often leaving the remainder less valuable.|The mine was high-graded during the gold rush, leaving only low-grade ore.|近义词:cherry-pick, cream, skim|反义词:low-grade|词性:动词(过去分词)

high-grade:/ˈhaɪˌɡreɪd/
adjective|高品位的；高级的；|Of superior quality or value.|The company only uses high-grade materials in its products.|近义词:superior, premium, top-quality|反义词:low-grade, inferior|词性:形容词
