overencourage: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈkɜːrɪdʒ/ | v. | 过度鼓励；过分鼓励 | To encourage someone excessively, often to the point where it becomes detrimental. | He overencouraged his son, leading the boy to believe he was naturally gifted at everything. 他过度鼓励他的儿子，让这男孩认为自己天生就擅长一切。|  She overencouraged her employees with praise, making them complacent. 她过分地表扬她的员工，使他们变得自满。 | 近义词: coddle, pamper, spoil | 反义词: discourage, underencourage, neglect | 词性: 动词


词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "encourage" (verb meaning "to give support, confidence, or hope to someone")
