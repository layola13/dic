galangal:/ˈɡæləŋɡæl/ | n. |高良姜，良姜|Aromatic rhizome of plants of the ginger family, used as a spice, especially in Southeast Asian cooking. |  Galangal is a key ingredient in many Thai curries. 高良姜是许多泰国咖喱的关键成分。

Galangal adds a unique citrusy flavor to dishes.  高良姜为菜肴增添独特的柑橘味。 | 近义词:ginger, turmeric | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


词源: From Old French *galingale, from Medieval Latin galanga, from Arabic khalanjan, ultimately from Chinese 高良姜 (gāoliángjiāng).
