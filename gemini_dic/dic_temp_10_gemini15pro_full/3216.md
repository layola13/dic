affectionate:/əˈfekʃənət/| adj.|深情的，充满爱的，亲切的|Feeling or showing fondness or tenderness.|She gave her son an affectionate hug. 她深情地拥抱了她的儿子。|近义词：loving, tender, fond, warm, caring|反义词：cold, uncaring, indifferent, aloof|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin *affectionatus, from Latin affectio(n-) ‘disposition, affection’ (from afficere ‘affect’).
