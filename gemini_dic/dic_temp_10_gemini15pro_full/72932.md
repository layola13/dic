fondlingly:/ˈfɑːndliŋli/ | adv. | 爱抚地，温柔地 | In a way that shows affection and tenderness, often by touching or stroking gently. | She stroked the puppy's head fondlingly. 她爱抚地摸了摸小狗的头。|  The mother held her newborn baby fondlingly. 母亲温柔地抱着她刚出生的婴儿。 | 近义词：affectionately, tenderly, lovingly, gently | 反义词：roughly, harshly, coldly | 词性: 副词

词源: From the verb "fondle" + "-ing" + "-ly". "Fondle" comes from the Middle English "fondlen", meaning "to caress, treat tenderly," of uncertain origin.
