degu:/ˈdeɪɡuː/
n.
degu
A small, social rodent native to Chile, known for its active and playful nature.
Degus are popular as pets due to their intelligence and sociability.
My degu loves to run on its wheel. 我的八齿鼠喜欢在它的轮子上奔跑。

Degus are native to the Chilean Andes. 八齿鼠原产于智利安第斯山脉。
