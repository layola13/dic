half-pleasing:/ˌhæfˈpliːzɪŋ/
adjective
半令人愉悦的；不太令人满意的
Partially pleasing; not entirely satisfactory.  It suggests something that has some pleasing qualities but also some that are not.
The movie was half-pleasing, with a good story but weak acting.  这部电影差强人意，故事不错，但演技很差。
The food was half-pleasing; the appetizers were delicious, but the main course was bland.  这顿饭不太令人满意；开胃菜很好吃，但主菜很淡。
近义词:partially satisfying, moderately enjoyable, bittersweet, so-so
反义词:displeasing, unsatisfying, thoroughly enjoyable
词性:adjective
