panthalassa:/pænθəˈlæsə/
n.
泛大洋，盘古大洋
A vast, universal ocean that surrounded the supercontinent Pangaea during the late Paleozoic and early Mesozoic eras.  It encompassed all the ancient oceans combined.
The giant reptiles of the Mesozoic swam the vast Panthalassa ocean.  中生代的巨型爬行动物在广阔的泛大洋中游弋。

近义词:global ocean, world ocean
反义词: (No direct antonym exists.  One could potentially consider isolated seas or landmasses as opposites in concept.)
词性: noun
