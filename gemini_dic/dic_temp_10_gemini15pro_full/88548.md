hit-up:/ˈhɪt ʌp/ (US) /ˈhɪt ʌp/ (UK)| phrasal verb|联系；请求；索要；短暂拜访|To contact someone, often informally, typically to ask for something or to visit briefly.|例句：I hit her up for some money. 我向她要了些钱。

例句：I'm going to hit up the mall after work. 我下班后要去逛一下商场。|近义词：contact, ask, visit, drop by|反义词:avoid, ignore|词性:动词短语

hit-up:/ˈhɪt ʌp/ (US) /ˈhɪt ʌp/ (UK)| noun|联系；请求|An informal request or demand.|例句：He gave me a hit-up for money. 他找我要钱。

例句：She received several hit-ups for help with the project. 她收到了好几个请求帮忙完成项目的请求。|近义词：request, demand, appeal|反义词:offer, provision|词性:名词
