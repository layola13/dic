flannelette:/ˌflænəˈlɛt/ | n. | 法兰绒，绒布 | A soft, warm cotton fabric with a napped surface, used for making clothing and sheets. |  My favorite pyjamas are made of soft flannelette. 我最喜欢的睡衣是用柔软的法兰绒做的。
