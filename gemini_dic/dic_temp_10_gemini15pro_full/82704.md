gui's:/ˈɡiːz/| n.|家伙们; 朋友们 (Informal, often plural)|This is a contraction of "guy's," the possessive form of "guy," meaning belonging to or associated with a guy.  When made plural, it often refers to a group of people, similar to "guys."  It can also be a misspelling of "guise," meaning an external form, appearance, or manner of presentation, typically concealing the true nature of something.|Example 1:  Going to the game with the gui's tonight. 今晚和伙计们去看比赛。

Example 2: That's just John's gui's way of saying hello. 那只是约翰那帮人打招呼的方式。|近义词：guys, friends, pals, buddies|反义词:enemies, foes|词性:名词 (Technically a contraction acting as a possessive noun, often pluralized)
