demonization:/ˌdiːmənaɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|妖魔化；污名化|The act of portraying someone or something as evil or wicked.|The media's demonization of the candidate ultimately cost him the election. 媒体对这位候选人的妖魔化最终使他竞选失败。|近义词:vilification, denigration, defamation|反义词:idealization, glorification|词性:名词

demonize:/ˈdiːmənaɪz/
|vt.|妖魔化；污名化|To portray someone or something as evil or wicked.|The propaganda campaign sought to demonize the enemy.  宣传活动试图妖魔化敌人。|近义词:vilify, denigrate, defame|反义词:idealize, glorify|词性:动词
