parfit:/ˈpɑːfɪt/| adj.|完美的，完满的；最合适的| morally perfect; lacking nothing|  A parfit world is one where everyone is happy and healthy. 一个完美的世界是每个人都健康快乐的世界。|  She found the parfit dress for the wedding. 她找到了最适合婚礼的礼服。|近义词：perfect, ideal, optimal|反义词：imperfect, flawed, unsuitable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *parfit, from Old French parfit, from Latin perfectus, past participle of perficere (“to complete, accomplish”), from per- (“thoroughly”) + facere (“to do, make”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeh₁- (“to set, put”).
