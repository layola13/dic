diked:/daɪkt/
adjective
筑堤的；有堤坝的
protected or enclosed by a dike.
The farmers lived in a diked area to protect their land from flooding. 农民们住在有堤坝的地区，以保护他们的土地免受洪水侵袭。
近义词: embanked, leveed
反义词: undiked

词性: adjective

词源: From the noun "dike" + "-ed" suffix, indicating the presence of a dike. "Dike" comes from Middle Dutch "dīc," related to Old English "dīc" and Old Frisian "dīk," meaning "ditch, dike."
