gravitated:/ˈɡrævɪteɪtɪd/
verb
被吸引；受吸引；倾向于
moved toward something; was attracted to something
例句：
Young people gravitated toward the cities in search of work.
年轻人为了找工作而涌向城市。

Newcomers gravitated to the warmth of the community.
新来的人被社区的温暖所吸引。

近义词：
be drawn to, be attracted to, incline towards, tend towards, head for, drift towards
反义词：
be repelled by, avoid, shun

词性
v.

词源
From Latin *gravitatus*, past participle of *gravitare* (“to make heavy, weigh down”), from *gravis* (“heavy”).
