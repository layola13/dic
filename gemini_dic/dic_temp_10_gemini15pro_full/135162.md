nonopinionated:/ˌnɒnəˈpɪnjəneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 没有偏见的；不固执己见的 | Not having strong opinions or preferences; open to different ideas and perspectives. |  This framework is nonopinionated, allowing developers to choose their preferred tools and libraries.  这个框架没有预设观点，允许开发者选择他们喜欢的工具和库。

This software is nonopinionated and can be adapted to various workflows.  这款软件没有预设观点，可以适应各种工作流程。 | 近义词：flexible, adaptable, open-minded, impartial, neutral | 反义词：opinionated, dogmatic, inflexible, biased, prejudiced | 词性: adjective

词源:  The word "nonopinionated" is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the adjective "opinionated." "Opinionated" itself comes from the noun "opinion," which has roots in Old French and ultimately Latin ("opinio").
