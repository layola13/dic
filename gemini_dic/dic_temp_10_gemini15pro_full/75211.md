frogging:/ˈfrɒɡɪŋ/ (US) /ˈfrɒɡɪŋ/ (UK)| n.| 钩针编织; 寻找青蛙 |The act of crocheting, specifically a type of raised stitch that resembles frog skin. Also, the activity of searching for and observing frogs in their natural habitat.|She spent the afternoon frogging her latest project because she made a mistake in the pattern. 她一下午都在拆她最新的钩针作品，因为她在图样中犯了个错误。

He enjoys frogging in the local wetlands. 他喜欢在当地的湿地寻找青蛙。|近义词：crocheting (for the craft), herping (for the activity)|反义词: unravelling (for crocheting) |词性: noun
