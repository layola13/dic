moratory:/ˈmɔːrəˌtɔːri/ (US) /ˈmɒrəˌtɔːri/ (UK)| n.|暂停，中止，延期|A temporary suspension of an activity, especially by official agreement.|例句：The government has declared a moratorium on nuclear testing. 政府已宣布暂停核试验。|例句：There is a five-year moratorium on commercial whaling. 商业捕鲸有五年的暂停期。|近义词：suspension, postponement, halt, standstill, respite|反义词：continuation, resumption, progression|词性:名词

moratorium:/ˈmɔːrəˌtɔːri/ (US) /ˈmɒrəˌtɔːri/ (UK)| adj.|暂停的，中止的|Relating to a moratorium; imposing a moratorium.|例句：A moratorium period has been agreed.  已经商定了一个暂停期。|例句：The moratorium law prevents any further development. 这项暂停法阻止了任何进一步的开发。|近义词：suspensive, postponing|反义词：continuing, resuming|词性:形容词
