belabour:/bɪˈleɪbər/| v. |反复讨论；过度强调；抨击；痛打|To argue or discuss (a subject) in excessive detail.|He kept belabouring the point long after everyone had agreed.|近义词：overemphasize, harp on, dwell on, labor, overwork|反义词：understate, downplay|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *belabouren*, from be- + labour.  Equivalent to be- + labour.  Compare Dutch *belabberen*.
