disendow:/ˌdɪsɪnˈdaʊ/
verb
剥夺；取消…的捐款；终止…的资金
To deprive of endowment; to take away property or privileges previously granted to (someone or something, especially a church, college, or hospital).
The college was disendowed during the war.  战争期间，这所大学的捐款被取消了。
The government disendowed the church of its lands. 政府剥夺了教会的土地。
synonyms: divest, deprive, strip
antonyms: endow, fund, support
词性: 动词
