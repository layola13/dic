obtrude:/əbˈtruːd/ /ɑːbˈtruːd/
verb
闯入；打扰；强加于
to force oneself or one's ideas on someone, especially when unwanted
He obtruded his opinions upon everyone he met.  他把自己的意见强加于他遇到的每一个人。
intrude, impose, force, inflict
refrain, withdraw, abstain, desist
v.
