hoolock:/ˈhuːlɒk/ (UK), /ˈhuːlɑːk/ (US)| n.|黑长臂猿，呼猿|A gibbon of the genus *Hoolock*, found in eastern India, Bangladesh, Myanmar, and southwestern China.  These gibbons are known for their distinctive calls and are primarily arboreal.|例句：The hoolock gibbon's call echoed through the forest. 黑长臂猿的叫声在森林中回荡。

例句：Researchers are studying the social behavior of hoolocks. 研究人员正在研究黑长臂猿的社会行为。|近义词：gibbon, ape|反义词: None (No direct opposite for an animal name)|词性:noun
