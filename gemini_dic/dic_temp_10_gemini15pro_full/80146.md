god-given:/ˌɡɒdˈɡɪvn/ /ˌɡɑːdˈɡɪvn/| adj.|天赋的，天赐的|A natural talent or quality that is thought to be given to someone by God.|She has a god-given talent for music. 她有天生的音乐天赋。|近义词：innate, natural, inherent, inborn|反义词：acquired, learned|词性:形容词

god-given:|/ˌɡɒdˈɡɪvn/ /ˌɡɑːdˈɡɪvn/|  adj.|神授的，天赐的|Something bestowed or ordained by God.|He considered his leadership a god-given right. 他认为他的领导权是神授的。|近义词：divine, providential|反义词：earned, man-made|词性:形容词
