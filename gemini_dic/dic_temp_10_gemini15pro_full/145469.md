overclassified:/ˌoʊvərˈklæsɪfaɪd/| adj.|过度保密的，过度分类的|Classified at too high a security level, resulting in restricted access beyond what is necessary.  The information has been unnecessarily classified, making it difficult for those who need it to access it.|例句：The documents were overclassified, hindering the investigation.  这些文件被过度保密，阻碍了调查。

例句：The report was so overclassified that even senior officials couldn't access it. 这份报告被过度保密，甚至连高级官员都无法查阅。|近义词：overprotected, excessively restricted|反义词:underclassified, declassified|词性:adjective
