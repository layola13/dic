ale's:/eɪlz/ | n. | 麦芽酒; （尤指）淡色麦芽酒 |  Belonging to or associated with ale; a contraction of "ale is" or "ale has".  It is most commonly used as a possessive form, indicating ownership or association with ale.  Historically, "ale" referred to a type of beer brewed without hops. | The ale's flavor was surprisingly fruity. 这款麦芽酒的味道出奇的果味。| synonyms: beer's, brew's | antonyms: (No direct antonym for a possessive.  One could consider opposites in terms of beverage, such as "wine's" or "milk's") | Possessive Pronoun
