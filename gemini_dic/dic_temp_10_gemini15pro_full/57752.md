dreamily:/ˈdriːmɪli/| adv.|梦一般地，梦幻般地，恍惚地|In a dreamy manner; as if in a dream.|She smiled dreamily, lost in thought. 她梦幻般地笑着，陷入了沉思。|He gazed dreamily out the window. 他 мечтательно смотрел в окно (meng huan ban di kan zhe chuang wai) 他梦幻般地望着窗外。|近义词：absentmindedly, wistfully, vaguely|反义词：alertly, realistically, practically|词性:副词

词源: From dream + -ly.  "dream" comes from Old English *drēam, of Germanic origin.
