dette: /dɛt/ (Danish, Norwegian)
noun
debt; this (neuter form)
This word means "debt" in Danish and Norwegian, and "this" (neuter form) in Norwegian.  It is not an English word.
Danish Example: Han har en stor dette. (He has a large debt.)
Norwegian Example: Dette er min bil. (This is my car.)
Synonyms (for debt): liability, obligation, arrears
Antonyms (for debt): asset, credit
n. (noun)


dette: /ˈdɛtə/ (Norwegian - Nynorsk)
pronoun, determiner
this
This word means "this" in Norwegian Nynorsk. It is not an English word.
Example: Dette huset er gammelt. (This house is old.)
Synonyms: this one
Antonyms: that one
pron. (pronoun), det. (determiner)
