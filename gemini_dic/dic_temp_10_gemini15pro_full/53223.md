directionless:/dɪˈrɛkʃənlɪs/
adjective
没有方向的；无目标的；漫无目的的
Lacking a clear direction or aim; without purpose.
He felt directionless and lost after losing his job.  他失业后感到茫然若失。
The movie's plot was directionless and confusing. 这部电影的情节漫无目的，令人困惑。
aimless, adrift, lost, rudderless, purposeless
focused, purposeful, driven, motivated
词性:形容词
