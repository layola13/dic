bondes:/ˈbɒndəz/ | n. | 农民，农夫 (plural) |  Archaic plural of "bonde," meaning peasant or farmer. This word is no longer in common use. |  In medieval texts, one might read of the bondes working the land. 在中世纪的文献中，人们可能会读到农民耕种土地的记载。 | 近义词: peasants, farmers, serfs | 反义词: landlords, aristocracy | 词性: noun


bondes:/ˈbɒndəz/ | n. | 债券 (plural, uncommon usage) | Can sometimes be seen as a non-standard plural of "bond" referring to financial instruments. This usage is rare and might be considered incorrect. |  While uncommon, some older documents might refer to government bondes. 虽然不常见，但一些较旧的文件可能会提到政府债券。 | 近义词: bonds, securities, debentures | 反义词: (No direct antonym for bonds as a financial instrument) | 词性: noun


词源: From Old Norse *bóndi, meaning "householder, dweller," related to "buandi," present participle of "búa," meaning "to dwell, live, inhabit." This term evolved to refer specifically to farmers who owned or occupied land.
