nepheline:/ˈnefəˌlɪn,ˈnefəˌlin,ˈnefiˌlin,ˈnefiˌlīn/| n.|霞石|A silica-deficient feldspathoid mineral, Na3KAl4Si4O16, that occurs as colorless, white, yellowish, grayish, or reddish crystals, typically in igneous rocks. It is used in the manufacture of glass and ceramics.|Nepheline syenite is a valuable source of aluminum.|近义词:feldspathoid|反义词:quartz|词性:noun
