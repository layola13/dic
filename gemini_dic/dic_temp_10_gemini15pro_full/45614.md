currycomb:/ˈkʌriˌkoʊm/ /ˈkʌriˌkəʊm/| n. vt.|马梳，梳理|A comb with short metal teeth or serrated ridges, used for grooming horses.|He used a currycomb to remove the mud from the horse's coat. 他用马梳清理马匹皮毛上的泥巴。|The stable boy currycombed the horse until its coat shone. 马夫用马梳梳理马匹直到它的皮毛闪闪发亮。|近义词：grooming comb, horse comb|反义词:|noun, verb
