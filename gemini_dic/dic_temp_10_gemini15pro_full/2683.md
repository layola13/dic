adrenalin: /əˈdrɛnəlɪn/ (US) /əˈdrɛnəlɪn/ (UK)| n.|肾上腺素|A hormone secreted by the adrenal glands, especially in conditions of stress, increasing rates of blood circulation, breathing, and carbohydrate metabolism and preparing muscles for exertion.|When the bear appeared, I felt a rush of adrenalin. 当熊出现时，我感到一阵肾上腺素飙升。

The athlete's adrenalin was pumping as he waited for the race to begin.  这名运动员在等待比赛开始时肾上腺素激增。|近义词: epinephrine|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider terms related to relaxation, e.g., tranquilizer)|词性: noun
