one-shot:/ˈwʌnˌʃɒt/ (UK) /ˈwʌnˌʃɑːt/ (US)| adj. n.|一次性的，单发的；单发装置；一次性剂量的|Something that is done or happens only once; a single dose of something.|

例句：
This is a one-shot opportunity, don't miss it. 这是唯一的机会，不要错过。
He took a one-shot deal and regretted it later. 他接受了一次性的交易，后来后悔了。

近义词：single, once-only, one-time, one-off|
反义词:repeated, multiple, recurring|
词性:adjective, noun|
