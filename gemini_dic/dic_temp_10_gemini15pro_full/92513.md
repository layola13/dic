hypothetical:/ˌhaɪpəˈθɛtɪk(ə)l/
adjective
假设的，假定的
Based on or involving an assumption or supposition.
It's a hypothetical question, so don't take it too seriously.
这是一个假设性的问题，所以不要太认真。
The scientist proposed a hypothetical situation to test the theory.
科学家提出了一个假设的情况来检验这个理论。
synonyms: supposed, assumed, theoretical, speculative, conjectural
antonyms: real, actual, factual, concrete, proven
词性: 形容词
