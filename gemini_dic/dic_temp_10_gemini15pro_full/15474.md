badgeringly:/ˈbædʒərɪŋli/
adv.
持续不断地纠缠地
in a persistently annoying or harassing way
He badgeringly pursued her for a date, even after she said no.  他不断地纠缠她要和她约会，即使在她拒绝之后也是如此。
Synonyms: persistently, relentlessly, harassingly, importunately
Antonyms: gently, politely, respectfully
词性: 副词
