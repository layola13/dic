antidotical:/ˌæntiˈdɒtɪkl/ /ˌæntiˈdɑːtɪkl/ | adj. | 解毒的；抵消的；对抗的 | Acting against something else; counteracting. |  The medicine had an antidotical effect on the poison.  这种药对毒物有解毒作用。 | synonyms: counteractive, neutralizing, remedial, corrective | antonyms:  poisonous, toxic, venomous | adjective


antidote:/ˈæntidoʊt/ /ˈæntidəʊt/ | n. | 解毒剂；矫正方法 | A medicine taken or given to counteract a particular poison. |  This herb is a powerful antidote to snake venom. 这种草药是蛇毒的有效解毒剂。 | synonyms: remedy, cure, corrective | antonyms: poison, toxin, venom | noun
