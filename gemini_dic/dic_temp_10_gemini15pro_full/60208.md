efflorescency:/ˌefləˈresənsi/
n.
风化；(盐类)析出,  (某些物质)形成的覆盖层；(尤指文艺等的)繁荣,兴盛
The action or process of efflorescing.  The state of efflorescing.  A covering of crystals or powder formed by efflorescence. A time of flowering or flourishing, especially of artistic or intellectual activity.
The efflorescency of salt on the rocks was a sign of the dry climate. 岩石上盐的析出是气候干燥的标志。
The Renaissance was a period of great artistic efflorescency. 文艺复兴时期是艺术繁荣的伟大时期。
Synonyms: blooming, blossoming, flowering, flourishing, burgeoning, thriving
Antonyms: decline, decay, deterioration, withering
