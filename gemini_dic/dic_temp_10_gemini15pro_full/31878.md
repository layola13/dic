cheerlessly:/ˈtʃɪrləsli/| adv.|无精打采地，阴郁地|Without cheer; in a cheerless manner; gloomily; dejectedly. |He walked cheerlessly through the empty streets. 他无精打采地走在空荡荡的街道上。|近义词：gloomily, dejectedly, morosely, sadly|反义词：cheerfully, happily, brightly|词性:副词

词源:

From cheerless + -ly.  "Cheerless" comes from "cheer" (from Old French "chiere" meaning "face, countenance") + "-less" (meaning "without").  So, "cheerlessly" literally means "without a cheerful countenance" or "in a manner lacking cheer".
