cirriped:/ˈsɪrɪped/| adj. |蔓足类的，藤壶的|Of or relating to the cirripeds.|Cirriped larvae are free-swimming. 藤壶幼虫是自由游动的。|Synonym: barnacle-like|Antonym: N/A|adjective
