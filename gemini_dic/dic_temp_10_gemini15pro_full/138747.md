numby:/ˈnʌmbi/| adj.|麻木的；失去感觉的|Numb or lacking sensation, especially in a limb.  |例句：My fingers were numby with cold. 我的手指冻僵了。|近义词：insensible, numb, dead, unfeeling|反义词:sensitive, feeling|词性:adjective

numby:/ˈnʌmbi/|  v. |使麻木；使失去感觉|To make numb or deprive of sensation.|例句：The extreme cold numbed my toes.  极度的寒冷使我的脚趾麻木了。|近义词：deadened, benumbed|反义词:sensitize|词性:verb
