colossi:/kəˈlɒsaɪ/| n.|巨像，巨人| statues of gigantic size, often representing gods or heroes; people of immense stature or importance|例句：The ancient colossi of Memnon still stand in Egypt.  埃及的古老门农巨像仍然屹立不倒。

例句：The business tycoons were considered colossi in their industry.  这些商业巨头被认为是他们行业的巨擘。|近义词：giants, behemoths, titans|反义词:dwarfs, midgets|词性:noun
