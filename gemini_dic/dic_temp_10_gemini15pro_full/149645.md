paintiness:/ˈpeɪntinəs/
n.
绘画性；油画感
The quality of looking like a painting, often characterized by visible brushstrokes, rich textures, and vibrant colors.
Her paintings have a certain paintiness that I find appealing. 她的画作有一种我喜欢的绘画感。
pictorialism, picturesqueness
