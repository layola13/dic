nonsaturated:/ˌnɒnˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/ /ˌnɑːnˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/
adjective
未饱和的
Not saturated; not holding or containing as much as can be absorbed or dissolved.
This oil is nonsaturated and healthier than saturated fats.  这种油是不饱和的，比饱和脂肪更健康。
The market for this product is nonsaturated, meaning there is still room for growth.  这个产品的市场还未饱和，意味着仍有增长空间。
