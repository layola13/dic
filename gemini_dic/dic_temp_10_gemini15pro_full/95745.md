inconsistently:/ˌɪnkənˈsɪstəntli/| adv.|不一致地，不协调地，反复无常地| lacking consistency or regularity; not staying the same throughout|He played inconsistently throughout the season, sometimes brilliant, sometimes terrible. 他整个赛季表现不稳定，有时很出色，有时很糟糕。|Synonyms: erratically, irregularly, unevenly, fitfully, capriciously|Antonyms: consistently, steadily, uniformly, regularly|adverb
