derecho:/dəˈreɪtʃoʊ/ (US), /dəˈrɛtʃoʊ/ (UK)| n.|直线风，德雷乔|A widespread, long-lived, straight-line wind storm that is associated with a fast-moving group of severe thunderstorms.|The derecho raced across the Midwest, leaving a trail of destruction. 直线风席卷了中西部，留下了一片狼藉。|Synonyms: windstorm, squall, gale|Antonyms: calm, breeze|noun

derecho:/dəˈreɪtʃoʊ/ (US), /dəˈrɛtʃoʊ/ (UK)| adj.|直的，笔直的 (less common usage)|Straight, direct.|Its original Spanish meaning pertains to something straight or direct.  它最初的西班牙语含义是指直的或直接的东西。|Synonyms: straight, direct, linear|Antonyms: curved, crooked, indirect|adjective
