dormer:/ˈdɔːrmər (UK), ˈdɔːrmər (US)| n. |老虎窗，屋顶窗|A vertical window projecting from a sloping roof, usually with its own small roof.|The dormer window provided a lovely view of the garden. 老虎窗提供了花园的美丽景色。|Example:  The old house had several dormers added to increase the attic space.  这栋老房子加建了几个老虎窗，以增加阁楼空间。

Example:  Sunlight streamed through the dormer, illuminating the dusty attic room. 阳光透过老虎窗，照亮了满是灰尘的阁楼房间。

|Synonym: skylight, roof window|Antonym: N/A|noun
