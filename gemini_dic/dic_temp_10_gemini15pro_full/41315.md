coreference:/ˌkɔːˈrɛfərəns/| n.|共指，指代相同|The repetition of a particular expression, or the use of a substitute expression, to refer to the same thing or person; the relation between such expressions.  A cohesive device whereby one expression refers back to another.  It is a crucial phenomenon for understanding how different parts of a text relate to each other and how meaning is created across sentences.|Example: "The president delivered a speech. He emphasized the importance of unity."  "He" is a coreference to "the president". 总统发表了讲话。他强调了团结的重要性。“他”指的是“总统”。

Example: "My dog loves to chase squirrels. The furry rodent is his favorite prey." "The furry rodent" is a coreference to "squirrels". 我的狗喜欢追逐松鼠。这种毛茸茸的啮齿动物是它最喜欢的猎物。“毛茸茸的啮齿动物”指的是“松鼠”。|近义词：anaphor, antecedent|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
