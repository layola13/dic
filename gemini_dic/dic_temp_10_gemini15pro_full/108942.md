leemost:/ˈliːməʊst/ | adj. | 最 sheltered 的；最背风的 | Most sheltered; most protected from the wind. | The leemost side of the island was the best place to anchor. 岛屿最背风的一侧是最佳的抛锚地点。 | 近义词: sheltered, protected | 反义词: exposed, windward | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Middle English *leemest, superlative of lee, from Old English *hlēo, meaning "shelter," "protection."  Related to Old Norse hlē.
