newtonist:/ˈnjuːtənɪst/ | n. | 牛顿主义者；牛顿学说信奉者 | A follower or advocate of the theories of Sir Isaac Newton, especially in physics. |  Example 1: The newtonist argued that the apple fell due to gravity.  牛顿主义者认为苹果落地是因为重力。

Example 2:  As a staunch newtonist, she believed in the laws of motion.  作为一个坚定的牛顿主义者，她相信运动定律。 | 近义词: Newtonian | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a proponent of a different theory, e.g., Einsteinian) | 词性: Noun
