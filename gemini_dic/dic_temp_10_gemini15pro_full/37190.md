comedown:/ˈkʌmdaʊn/| n.|下降，衰落，失望; (吸毒后的)消沉，反应|A sudden descent from a high or elevated position, either literally or figuratively.  A decline in status, fortune, or mood. An unpleasant reaction experienced after taking a drug.|After winning the lottery, the comedown was harsh when he realized he'd lost the ticket.  中了彩票后，当他意识到自己把彩票弄丢了时，失望之情油然而生。|近义词：decline, descent, fall, anticlimax, letdown|反义词:upswing, rise, high|词性:名词

comedown:/ˈkʌmdaʊn/| n.| (吸毒后的)消沉，反应|An unpleasant reaction experienced after taking a drug, especially an amphetamine or other stimulant.|He experienced a terrible comedown after the party, feeling depressed and anxious.  派对结束后，他感到非常沮丧和焦虑，像是毒品后的不良反应。|近义词：crash, low|反义词:high|词性:名词
