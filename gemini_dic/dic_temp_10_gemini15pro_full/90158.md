hothead:/ˈhɑːthed/| n.| 暴躁的人，性急的人|A person who gets angry easily.|He's such a hothead; he always flies off the handle over the smallest things. 他真是个急性子，总是为了一点小事就大发雷霆。|Synonyms: firebrand, spitfire, loose cannon|Antonyms: calm person, patient person|noun


hotheaded:/ˌhɑːtˈhedɪd/| adj.|  性急的，易怒的|Easily angered; quick-tempered.|Her hotheaded reaction surprised everyone. 她冲动的反应让每个人都感到惊讶。|Synonyms: impetuous, rash, fiery|Antonyms: calm, cool-headed, level-headed|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "hot" (meaning high temperature or intense emotion) + "head" (referring to the mind or temperament).  The combination suggests someone whose head is figuratively "hot" with anger or impulsiveness.
