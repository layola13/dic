geodesic:/ˌdʒiːəˈdɛsɪk/ /ˌdʒiːoʊˈdɛsɪk/| adj. n.|测地线的，测地线|Relating to or denoting the shortest line between two points on a curved surface.|例句：The geodesic dome is a remarkable feat of engineering. 测地穹顶是一项了不起的工程壮举。

geodesic:/ˌdʒiːəˈdɛsɪk/ /ˌdʒiːoʊˈdɛsɪk/| adj. n.|测地线的，测地线|The spaceship followed a geodesic path around the planet. 宇宙飞船沿着行星周围的测地线路径飞行。|近义词：shortest, direct|反义词: indirect, roundabout|词性: adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
