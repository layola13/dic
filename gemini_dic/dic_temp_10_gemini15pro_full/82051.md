gristle:/ˈɡrɪsəl/| n.|软骨，脆骨|Tough, inedible tissue, especially in meat.  It's the cartilaginous part of meat that is difficult to chew.|The steak was full of gristle and difficult to eat. 这牛排全是软骨，很难嚼。|Synonyms: cartilage, sinew|Antonyms: flesh, muscle|noun
