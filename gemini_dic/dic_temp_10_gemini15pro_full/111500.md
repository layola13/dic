词源：
From Middle English *lom*, from Old English *lām* (“loam, clay”), from Proto-Germanic *lēimaz (“loam, clay”). Cognate with West Frisian *leem* (“clay”), Dutch *leem* (“loam, clay”), German *Lehm* (“loam, clay”), Danish *ler* (“clay”), Swedish *lera* (“clay”), Icelandic *leir* (“clay”).
