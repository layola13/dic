crawlers:/ˈkrɔːlərz/| n.|爬行者；爬虫；履带式车辆|A program or automated script that browses the World Wide Web in a methodical, automated manner.  网络爬虫，一种以有条理、自动化方式浏览万维网的程序或自动化脚本。|Search engine crawlers index websites. 搜索引擎爬虫索引网站。|The baby crawlers were exploring the living room.  婴儿们在客厅里爬来爬去。|近义词：spiders, bots, robots|反义词:|名词
