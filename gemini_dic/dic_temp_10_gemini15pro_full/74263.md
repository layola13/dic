foxtrot:/ˈfɑːkstrɑːt/ (US) /ˈfɒkstrɒt/ (UK)| n. vi.|狐步舞|A ballroom dance for couples, characterized by long, continuous flowing movements across the dance floor.|例句：They danced a graceful foxtrot. 他们跳了一支优雅的狐步舞。|例句：The band played a foxtrot. 乐队演奏了一曲狐步舞。|近义词：ballroom dance, waltz, quickstep|反义词:|noun, verb
