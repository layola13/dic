masts:/mɑːsts/ /mæsts/| n.|桅杆；天线杆；旗杆|A tall, vertical spar or pole used to support sails, flags, or antennas on a ship or boat, or on land for similar purposes. |The ship's masts were silhouetted against the setting sun. 船的桅杆在落日映衬下显出轮廓。|The radio masts were visible from miles away. 无线电天线杆在几英里外都能看到。|synonyms: poles, spars, pillars|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "base," "foundation," or "ground" in a metaphorical sense)|noun
