outperforming:/ˌaʊtpərˈfɔːrmɪŋ/
verb|胜过，优于|performing better than something or someone else|Our new product is outperforming all the competition. 我们的新产品胜过所有竞争对手。|
outperform|underperform|v.
The company has consistently outperformed the market.  这家公司一直以来都跑赢市场。

outperforming:/ˌaʊtpərˈfɔːrmɪŋ/
adjective|表现优异的，胜过的|performing better than something or someone else|The company's outperforming stock has attracted many investors.  这家公司表现优异的股票吸引了许多投资者。|
excellent, superior|inferior, underperforming|adj.
