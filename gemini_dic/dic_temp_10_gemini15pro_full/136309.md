nonrepublican:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈpʌblɪkən/
adjective
非共和党的；非共和政体的
Not of or relating to a republic or its principles; not republican.
Many nonrepublican forms of government exist around the world. 世界上存在许多非共和政体的政府形式。
European monarchies are nonrepublican. 欧洲的君主制是非共和政体的。
近义词: non-GOP, anti-republican
反义词: republican
词性: adjective
