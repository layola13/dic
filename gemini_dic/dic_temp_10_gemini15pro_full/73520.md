foreshore:/ˈfɔːrʃɔːr/ (US) /ˈfɔːʃɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. |前滩；潮间带|The part of a shore between high- and low-water marks, or between the water and cultivated or developed land.|Children were playing on the foreshore, searching for shells. 孩子们在前滩上玩耍，寻找贝壳。|Synonyms: beachfront, seaside, shoreline, coast, beach|Antonyms: inland, offshore|noun

foreshores:/ˈfɔːrʃɔːrz/ (US) /ˈfɔːʃɔː(r)z/ (UK)| n. |前滩（复数）|Plural of foreshore.|The foreshores of the lake were littered with debris. 湖的前滩上到处都是垃圾。|Synonyms: beachfronts, seasides, shorelines, coasts, beaches|Antonyms: inlands, offshores|noun
