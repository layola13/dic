overstoutness:/ˈoʊvərˈstaʊtnəs/| n. |过度肥胖，过于粗壮|The condition of being excessively stout or fat.  It implies an unhealthy level of body fat. |His overstoutness made it difficult for him to climb stairs. 他的过度肥胖使他爬楼梯很困难。|Synonyms: obesity, corpulence, stoutness, portliness|Antonyms: thinness, slenderness, leanness|noun


Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over") + "stout" (Middle English stout, from Old French estout, meaning "bold, brave, strong") + "-ness" (Old English -nes, a suffix forming nouns of quality or state)
