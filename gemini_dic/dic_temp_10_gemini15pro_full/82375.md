growths:/ɡrəʊθs/ (UK), /ɡroʊθs/ (US)| n. |增长；生长；生长物；肿瘤|The increase in size or amount of something.  Developments or tumors in a body.|
例句：The growths on the tree were unusual. 树上的生长物很不寻常。
例句：The company experienced significant growths in sales this year.  这家公司今年的销售额显著增长。
近义词：increases, expansions, developments, tumors, proliferations|反义词:declines, reductions, shrinkages|词性:名词
