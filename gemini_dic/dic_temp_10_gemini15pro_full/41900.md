cosmetical:/kɒzˈmetɪkl/ /kɑːzˈmetɪkl/
adjective
化妆用的；美容的
Relating to cosmetics; used or done to improve a person's appearance.
She underwent a cosmetical procedure to enhance her lips. 她接受了丰唇美容手术。
The changes to the car were purely cosmetical. 这车的改变纯粹是装饰性的。
synonyms: cosmetic, beautifying, decorative
antonyms: natural, unadorned
词性: 形容词
