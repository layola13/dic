gabbling:/ˈɡæblɪŋ/ | n. | 含糊不清的说话，急促而不清楚的说话 | the act of speaking rapidly and unclearly |  He was gabbling so fast I couldn't understand a word. 他说得太快了，我一个字也听不懂。| 近义词：babbling, chattering, prattling | 反义词: articulating, enunciating | 词性: 名词

gabbling:/ˈɡæblɪŋ/ | adj. | 含糊不清地说着，急促而不清楚地说着 | speaking rapidly and unclearly | The gabbling salesman tried to convince me to buy the product.  那个含糊不清的推销员试图说服我买这个产品。 | 近义词：babbling, chattering, prattling | 反义词: articulate, enunciate | 词性: 形容词
