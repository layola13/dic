haggishly:/ˈhæɡɪʃli/| adv.|像老巫婆一样地；丑陋地；令人讨厌地|In a way that resembles a hag, especially in being ugly or unpleasant.|She stared haggishly at him across the table. 她隔着桌子凶狠地瞪着他。|近义词：uglily, spitefully, shrewishly|反义词：beautifully, kindly, pleasantly|词性：副词
Etymology: From hag + -ish + -ly.  Hag ultimately comes from Old English hægtesse "witch," while -ish and -ly are suffixes denoting resemblance and manner, respectively.
