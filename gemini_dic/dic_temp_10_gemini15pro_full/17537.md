beater:/ˈbiːtər/| n.|搅拌器; 打蛋器; (旧汽车或机器)破车，老爷车; 猎场看守的助手;  |A person or thing that beats something, especially an implement for beating carpets, eggs, etc. or an old car. |例句：She used an electric beater to whip the cream. 她用电动打蛋器搅拌奶油。

例句：His old car is a real beater, but it still runs. 他的旧车真是辆破车，但还能开。|近义词：whisk, mixer, clunker, jalopy (for old car)|反义词: new car (for old car) |词性: noun


beater:/ˈbiːtər/| n.|猎场看守的助手|A person who helps beat game towards hunters in a hunt.|例句：The beaters flushed the pheasants out of the bushes. 助手们把野鸡从灌木丛中赶了出来。

例句：The beater walked through the fields, making noise to scare the birds. 助手走过田野，制造噪音吓唬鸟类。|近义词：hunter's assistant|反义词: game|词性: noun
