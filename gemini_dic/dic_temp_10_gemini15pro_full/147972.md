overrestrict:/ˌoʊvərɪˈstrɪkt/| v. |过度限制|To restrict something too much.|The government's policy overrestricts trade and hinders economic growth. 政府的政策过度限制贸易，阻碍了经济增长。|The school overrestricts students' access to the internet. 学校过度限制学生上网。|近义词：overregulate, constrain, stifle|反义词:liberate, free, encourage|词性:动词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "restrict" (from Latin "restrictus," past participle of "restringere" - to bind back, restrain).
