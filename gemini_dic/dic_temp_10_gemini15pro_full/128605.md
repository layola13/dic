nichemanship: /ˈniːʃmənʃɪp/ | n. |利基市场策略；缝隙市场策略|The art or practice of concentrating on a specialized but profitable segment of the market.  The skillful exploitation of a niche market. |Example 1: The company's nichemanship in high-end organic baby food has been very successful. 这家公司在高端有机婴儿食品领域的利基市场策略非常成功。

Example 2:  Their nichemanship in handcrafted furniture allows them to charge premium prices. 他们手工制作家具的利基市场策略使他们能够收取高价。|Synonym: specialization, niche marketing, targeting, market segmentation |Antonym: mass marketing, broad appeal, diversification |noun
