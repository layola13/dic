cyrtostyle:/ˈsɜːrtəʊstaɪl/ | n. | 圆柱式门廊，曲廊 | A circular or semicircular colonnade or portico.  A building or part of a building having such a colonnade. |  The villa featured a magnificent cyrtostyle overlooking the Mediterranean Sea.  这座别墅的特点是有一个俯瞰地中海的宏伟的圆柱式门廊。| synonym:  colonnade, portico, peristyle | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "open courtyard" could be considered a contrasting architectural feature) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *kyrtos* (curved) + *stylos* (column)
