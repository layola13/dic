bootlegger:/ˈbuːtˌlɛɡər/| n.|走私贩；非法酿酒者；盗版商|A person who makes, sells, or transports something, especially alcoholic liquor, illegally.|During Prohibition, bootleggers made a fortune selling illegal alcohol. 在禁酒时期，走私贩靠出售非法酒类发了大财。|The bootlegger was caught selling fake designer handbags.  这个盗版商因销售假冒名牌手袋而被捕。|Synonyms: smuggler, trafficker, runner|Antonyms: law enforcement officer, police|Noun
