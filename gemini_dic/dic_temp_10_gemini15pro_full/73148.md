forayed:/ˈfɒreɪd/ (UK), /ˈfɔːreɪd/ (US)| v. |突袭；涉猎；初次尝试|Made a quick raid or incursion, especially into enemy territory; ventured into a new field of activity|
The army forayed into enemy territory. 军队突袭了敌人的领土。
She forayed into the world of fashion design. 她初次尝试了时装设计领域。
|近义词：raid, incursion, venture, excursion|反义词：retreat, withdrawal|词性: 动词

foray:/ˈfɒreɪ/ (UK), /ˈfɔːreɪ/ (US)| n. |突袭；涉猎；初次尝试|A sudden attack or incursion into enemy territory, especially to obtain something; a brief venture or attempt especially outside one's usual sphere of activity|
The company's foray into the mobile phone market was unsuccessful.  这家公司进军手机市场的尝试失败了。
His first foray into politics ended badly. 他初次涉足政坛的经历以失败告终。
