beastish:/ˈbiːstɪʃ/| adj.|野兽般的；残忍的；粗野的|Resembling or characteristic of a beast; brutal; savage.|He displayed beastish behavior, snarling and snapping at anyone who came near. 他表现出野兽般的行为，对任何靠近的人都咆哮着、撕咬着。|The beastish cruelty of the dictator shocked the world.  独裁者的残暴行径震惊了世界。|近义词：animalistic, brutal, savage, ferocious, cruel|反义词：gentle, kind, humane, civilized|词性:形容词
词源:  From beast + -ish.  源自 beast（野兽） + -ish（形容词后缀，表示“像…的”）。
