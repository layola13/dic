insults:/ˈɪnsʌlts/ (US), /ˈɪnsʌlts/ (UK)| n.  v. |侮辱，辱骂；冒犯|An insult is a rude expression intended to offend or hurt someone.  As a verb, it means to speak or treat someone with disrespect. |He hurled insults at the referee. 他对裁判破口大骂。|近义词：offense, affront, slight, slur|反义词: compliment, praise|noun
insults:/ɪnˈsʌlts/ (US), /ɪnˈsʌlts/ (UK)|v.|侮辱，辱骂| To insult someone is to offend them with rude or disrespectful words or actions. |His remarks insulted my intelligence. 他的话侮辱了我的智商。|近义词：offend, affront|反义词: compliment, flatter|verb
