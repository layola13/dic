outwrenches:/ˌaʊtˈrɛntʃɪz/
verb
胜过；超过；以扳手胜过
surpasses in wrenching; outdoes
He outwrenched his opponent in the arm-wrestling match. 他在扳手腕比赛中胜过了对手。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel, outstrip
反义词: lose to, be inferior to
词性: verb


outwrenches:/ˌaʊtˈrɛntʃɪz/
verb
用扳手拧开；拧下
to unscrew or remove with a wrench
He outwrenched the bolt with a powerful wrench. 他用一把强力扳手拧下了螺栓。
近义词: unscrew, loosen, remove
反义词: tighten, screw in
词性: verb
