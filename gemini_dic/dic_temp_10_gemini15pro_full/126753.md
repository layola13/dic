nanin:/ˈnænɪn/| n.|纳米|A type of bacterial protein, notably produced by *Staphylococcus aureus*, that forms pores in cell membranes, especially in white blood cells, leading to their death.  It is a type of pore-forming toxin.|The researchers studied the effects of nanin on human neutrophils.|近义词:  pore-forming toxin, leukocidin, cytotoxin|反义词:  antitoxin|词性: noun
