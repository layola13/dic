de-objectification:/diːˌɒbdʒɛktɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
|n.|去物化，非客体化|The process of ceasing to treat someone or something as an object, recognizing their inherent worth and agency.|Feminist theory emphasizes the de-objectification of women in media and society.  女权主义理论强调媒体和社会中对女性的去物化。|dehumanization, depersonalization|objectification|noun

de-objectify:/diːˌɒbdʒɛktɪfaɪ/
|v.|去物化，非客体化|To cease treating someone or something as an object, recognizing their inherent worth and agency.|The campaign aims to de-objectify women in advertising.  这场运动旨在去除广告中对女性的物化。|humanize, personalize|objectify|verb
