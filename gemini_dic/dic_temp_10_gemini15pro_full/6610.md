anaconda:/ˌænəˈkɑːndə/ | n. |水蟒;蟒蛇 | A large, nonvenomous South American snake that kills its prey by constriction. |  例句：The anaconda slithered through the swamp. 水蟒在沼泽中滑行。

例句：The anaconda coiled around its prey. 水蟒缠绕着它的猎物。 | 近义词：boa constrictor, python | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
