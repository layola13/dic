cleek:/klik/ /klɪk/
|n.|球棒；铁钩；抓住；勾住|A long iron golf club, typically a number 1, 2 or 3 iron, also known as a driving iron. Also, a hook or crook.|He used his cleek to get the ball out of the rough. 他用他的球棒把球从长草区打出来。|近义词:club, iron, hook, crook|反义词:|noun

cleek:/klik/ /klɪk/
|vt.|用钩钩住；抓住|To catch or seize with a hook or crook.|He cleeked the fish out of the water. 他用钩子把鱼从水里钩了出来。|近义词:hook, catch, grab, seize|反义词:release|verb
