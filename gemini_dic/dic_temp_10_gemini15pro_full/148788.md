overvaliantly: /ˌoʊvərˈvæliəntli/
| adv. | 过于勇敢地，过分英勇地 | In an excessively valiant manner; with too much bravery or valor.  | He charged overvaliantly into battle, ignoring the dangers. 他过于勇敢地冲进战场，忽略了危险。 | The knight overvaliantly challenged the dragon, ultimately meeting his demise. 骑士过分英勇地挑战巨龙，最终走向死亡。 | 近义词: too bravely, excessively bravely, rashly, recklessly | 反义词: cautiously, timidly, cowardly | 词性: 副词

overvaliantly: /ˌoʊvərˈvæliəntli/
|  | 词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + valiantly (adverb form of valiant, from Old French "vaillant," ultimately from Latin "valere" meaning "to be strong") |  |  |  |  |  |
