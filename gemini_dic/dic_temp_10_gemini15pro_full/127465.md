necessarianism:/nəˌsɛsəˈrɪərɪənɪzəm/
n.
必然论，决定论
The philosophical doctrine that all events are determined by antecedent causes and are therefore inevitable.
Necessarianism implies that free will is an illusion. 必然论意味着自由意志是一种幻觉。
Determinism holds that every event, including human choices and actions, is causally determined by prior events. 决定论认为，每一个事件，包括人类的选择和行动，都是由先前事件因果决定的。
Synonyms: determinism, fatalism
Antonyms: libertarianism, indeterminism
