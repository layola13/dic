clearage:/ˈklɪərəns/| n.|清除，清理，净空；清除物，清理物|The clearage of the old building made way for the new park.  旧楼的清除为新公园腾出了空间。|English Explanation: The act of clearing something, or the amount of space cleared. |例句：The clearage of the forest for agriculture is a controversial practice.  为了农业而清理森林的做法备受争议。

例句：The city council organized a clearage of the debris after the storm. 市议会在暴风雨后组织清理残骸。|近义词：clearance, clearing, removal|反义词: accumulation, hoarding|词性:noun
