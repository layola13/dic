mitochondrion:/ˌmaɪtəˈkɒndriən/ (UK) /ˌmaɪtoʊˈkɑːndriən/ (US)| n. |线粒体|A spherical or elongated organelle in the cytoplasm of nearly all eukaryotic cells, containing genetic material and many enzymes important for cell metabolism, including the Krebs cycle and oxidative phosphorylation.|Mitochondria are the powerhouses of the cell. 线粒体是细胞的能量工厂。|Synonym: None (Specific organelle)|Antonym: None (Specific organelle)|Noun
mitochondria:/ˌmaɪtəˈkɒndriə/ (UK) /ˌmaɪtoʊˈkɑːndriə/ (US)| n. |线粒体 (复数)|Plural form of mitochondrion.|The cell contains many mitochondria.  这个细胞包含许多线粒体。|Synonym: None (Specific organelle)|Antonym: None (Specific organelle)|Noun
