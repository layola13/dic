lignitiferous:/ˌlɪɡnɪˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
含褐煤的；产褐煤的
containing or yielding lignite; producing lignite.
The lignitiferous strata are a valuable source of fuel. 这些褐煤地层是宝贵的燃料来源。
The expedition discovered a lignitiferous formation in the valley. 探险队在山谷中发现了一个褐煤地层。
synonyms: lignite-bearing, coal-bearing
antonyms: barren, unproductive
adjective
