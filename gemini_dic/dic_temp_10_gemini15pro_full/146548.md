overglut:/ˌoʊvərˈɡlʌt/ | n. vt. | 过剩，过饱和 | An excessive supply; a glut.  To supply to excess. | The market is overglutted with cheap electronics.  市场上充斥着廉价电子产品。 The manufacturer overglutted the market with its new product. 制造商过度供应市场新产品。 | 近义词：surfeit, glut, excess, surplus | 反义词: shortage, scarcity, deficit, lack | 词性: 名词, 动词

Etymology:
From over- + glut.  "Glut" comes from Old French "gloter" meaning "to swallow greedily."  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning, indicating an excessive amount.
