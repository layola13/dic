old-womanish:/ˌoʊldˈwʊmənɪʃ/ | adj. | 老气横秋的，婆婆妈妈的 | Resembling or characteristic of an old woman, especially in being fussy, timid, or overly cautious.  |  She gave me an old-womanish look of disapproval. 她用一种老气横秋的不赞成的眼神看着我。 | Example: He thought her dress was rather old-womanish. 他觉得她的裙子有点老气。| Synonyms: fussy, prissy, timid, fusty, dowdy | Antonyms: youthful, spirited, bold, modern, chic | Adjective
