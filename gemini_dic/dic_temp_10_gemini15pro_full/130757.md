nonamortizable:/ˌnɒnˌæmərˈtaɪzəbl/
adjective
不可摊销的
Not able to be amortized; (of an asset) not subject to being written off over a period of time.
The company invested in nonamortizable intangible assets.  这家公司投资了不可摊销的无形资产。
Start-up costs are often considered nonamortizable. 启动成本通常被认为是不可摊销的。
non-depreciable, non-amortisable
