nonindulgences:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdʌldʒənsɪz/
|n.| 克制；节制；禁欲；不放纵|The practice of refraining from indulging in something, typically something considered pleasurable or excessive.|Her Lent was marked by nonindulgences in sweets and alcohol.  她通过戒除糖果和酒精来度过四旬期。|Near synonyms: abstinence, self-denial, self-restraint, asceticism, austerity.|Antonyms: indulgence, excess, hedonism|Noun
