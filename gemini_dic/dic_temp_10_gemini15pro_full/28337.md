caramelizes:/ˈkærəməlaɪzɪz/
verb
焦糖化
(of a food containing sugar) be converted into caramel by heating.  Turns brown and gives off a sweet, nutty aroma.
The onions caramelized beautifully in the pan. 洋葱在锅里焦糖化得很漂亮。
brown, glaze, sweeten
bleach, whiten
词性: 动词
