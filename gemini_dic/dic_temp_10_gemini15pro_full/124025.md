Etymology: From Late Latin *morōsus* "peevish, fastidious," from Latin *mōrēs* "customs, manners"  + *-ōsus*, suffix forming adjectives from nouns.  The sense evolution is from "habitually fault-finding" to "sullen, gloomy."
