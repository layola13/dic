crowdsource:/ˈkraʊdsɔːrs/ | v. n. | 众包； crowdsourcing(n.) 众包 | To obtain (information, funding, or input into a particular task or project) by enlisting the services of a large number of people, typically via the Internet. 通过征求大量人员（通常通过互联网）的服务来获取（信息、资金或对特定任务或项目的投入）。|  The company crowdsourced the translation of its website. 这家公司将网站的翻译工作众包出去。| crowdsource information from the public 从公众那里众包信息 | commission, outsource | insource | verb, noun
