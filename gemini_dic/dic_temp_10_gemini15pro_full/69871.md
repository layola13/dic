ferruled:/ˈfɜːrʊld/ /ˈfɛrʊld/| adj.| 装有金属箍的；受金属箍保护的；受严格控制的 | Having a ferrule or ferrules; strengthened or protected with a ferrule; strictly controlled. | The artist used a ferruled brush for fine details.  艺术家使用装有金属箍的画笔来绘制精细的细节。
The workers used ferruled tools to prevent damage. 工人们使用带金属箍的工具以防止损坏。| 近义词： reinforced, strengthened, protected, bound, controlled, restricted | 反义词： loose, unprotected, uncontrolled, unrestricted | 词性: 形容词

ferrule:/ˈfɜːrʊl/ /ˈfɛrʊl/| n.| 金属箍；金属环；包头；戒尺 | A ring or cap, typically metal, that strengthens the end of a handle, stick, or tube; a cane or rod used for punishing children. | The ferrule on the end of my walking stick came loose. 我拐杖末端的金属箍松了.  The teacher used a ferrule to discipline unruly students (archaic usage). 老师用戒尺来管教不守规矩的学生（古旧用法）。| 近义词： cap, ring, band, hoop (for object); cane, rod (for punishment) | 反义词：  (No direct antonym for the object; for punishment perhaps "reward") | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old French *virol (recorded in Anglo-Norman as virol, wirel), from Latin viriola, diminutive of viriae, plural of viria "armlet, bracelet."
