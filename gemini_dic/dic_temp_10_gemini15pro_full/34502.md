clean-house:/ˌkliːn ˈhaʊs/ | v. | 打扫房屋; 整顿;清除;解雇 | To thoroughly clean a house or to get rid of unwanted people or things in an organization. |  例句：She spent the entire weekend cleaning-house. 她花了整个周末打扫房子。

clean-house:/ˌkliːn ˈhaʊs/ | n. | 大扫除; 整顿; 清除 | A thorough cleaning of a house or a purging of unwanted elements in an organization. | 例句：The company underwent a major clean-house after the scandal.  丑闻之后，公司进行了大规模的整顿。
