palavering:/pəˈlævərɪŋ/| n.|闲谈，空谈，会议|Prolonged and often idle discussion; conference.|例句：After much palavering, they finally reached a decision. 经过长时间的闲聊，他们终于做出了决定。

例句：The tribal elders held a palavering to discuss the upcoming harvest. 部落长老们举行会议讨论即将到来的收成。|近义词：chatting, gossiping, conferring, discussing|反义词:silence, action|词性:noun

palaver:/pəˈlævər/| v. |闲聊，空谈|To talk idly and at length.|例句：They spent hours palavering about nothing important. 他们花了几个小时闲聊一些无关紧要的事情。

例句：He's always palavering with his colleagues instead of working. 他总是和同事闲聊而不是工作。|近义词：chat, gossip, confer, discuss|反义词:act, work|词性:verb


词源: From Portuguese *palavra* (word, speech) < Latin parabola (comparison, parable).
