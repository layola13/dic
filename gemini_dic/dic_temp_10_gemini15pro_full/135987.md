nonrailroads:/ˌnɑːnˈreɪlroʊdz/
noun
非铁路行业；非铁路运输
Industries and businesses that are not related to railroads.  This term encompasses all economic activities outside the railroad sector.
The government invested in nonrailroads to diversify the economy. 政府投资于非铁路行业以实现经济多元化。
Nonrailroad industries are booming in the region. 该地区的非铁路行业正在蓬勃发展。
Synonyms: non-railway industries, other industries
Antonyms: railroads, railways
