heteroclitical:/ˌhɛtərəˈklɪtɪkəl/
adjective
异格的；词形变化不规则的
A heteroclitical word is one that undergoes an irregular inflection or declension, deviating from the typical pattern of its grammatical class.
The word "ox" is heteroclitical, with its plural form "oxen."  公牛这个词是异格的，它的复数形式是oxen。
The ancient Greek word for "hand" is considered heteroclitical due to its unusual plural form. 古希腊语中“手”这个词由于其不寻常的复数形式而被认为是异格的。
近义词: anomalous, irregular, abnormal
反义词: regular, normal, typical
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *heterocliticus, from Ancient Greek ἑτερόκλιτος (heteróklitos, “irregularly inflected”), from ἕτερος (héteros, “other”) + κλίνω (klínō, “I bend, incline, inflect”).
