non-coercive:/ˌnɒn kəʊˈɜːsɪv/| adj.|非强制性的，非胁迫性的|Not involving or using coercion; not forcing someone to do something against their will.|例句：The government adopted a non-coercive approach to encourage energy conservation. 政府采取了非强制性措施来鼓励节能。|例句：The negotiation was non-coercive and resulted in a mutually beneficial agreement.  这次谈判是非强制性的，并达成了互利的协议。|近义词：voluntary, persuasive, optional|反义词:coercive, compulsory, mandatory|词性:adjective

Etymology:
non-: prefix meaning "not" (from Latin non)
coercive: from Latin coercere "to restrain, confine, enclose," from com- "together" (see com-) + arcere "to ward off, enclose"
