decisionmaking: /dɪˈsɪʒənˌmeɪkɪŋ/
n.|决策， تصمیم گیری|The process of making choices, especially important ones.  This involves identifying and evaluating options, considering potential outcomes, and selecting a course of action.|The company's decisionmaking process is very slow.  这家公司的决策过程非常缓慢。| synonyms: decision-making,  deliberation, judgment, choice| antonyms: indecision, hesitation|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the combination of "decision" and "making".  "Decision" comes from the Old French "decision" and ultimately from the Latin "decidere" meaning "to cut off, decide". "Making" comes from the Old English "macian" meaning "to make."
