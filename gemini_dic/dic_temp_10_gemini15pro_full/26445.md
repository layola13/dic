byron:/ˈbaɪrən/| n.|拜伦；阴郁、孤傲、放荡不羁的浪漫主义诗人；拜伦式英雄|A famous English Romantic poet known for his melancholic and rebellious works. Refers also to a Byronic hero, a character type marked by brooding, rebelliousness, and often a troubled past.|例句：Lord Byron is considered one of the greatest English poets. 拜伦勋爵被认为是英国最伟大的诗人之一。

例句：He is a Byronic hero, full of passion and defiance. 他是一个拜伦式英雄，充满激情和反抗精神。|近义词：Romantic, poet|反义词:classicist|词性:noun
