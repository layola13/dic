gaekwar:/ˈɡaɪkwɑːr/ | n. | 盖克瓦尔（印度土邦君主的称号）| A Gaekwar was a Maratha ruler of the former princely state of Baroda in western India.  The title is derived from the Marathi word "gāyakavāra" meaning "cowherd".|  The Gaekwar of Baroda was known for his lavish lifestyle. 巴罗达的盖克瓦尔以其奢华的生活方式而闻名。 | synonym:  maharaja, ruler, prince | antonym: subject, commoner | noun

gaekwar:/ˈɡaɪkwɑːr/ | n. | 盖克瓦尔王朝 | The Gaekwar dynasty ruled Baroda for over two centuries.  盖克瓦尔王朝统治巴罗达超过两个世纪。 | synonym: dynasty, house, lineage | antonym: N/A | noun
