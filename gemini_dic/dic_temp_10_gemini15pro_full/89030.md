hollywoodites:/ˈhɑːliwʊdaɪts/
noun
好莱坞人；好莱坞人士
People who live or work in Hollywood, especially those involved in the film industry.  Often used with a slightly negative or ironic connotation, suggesting superficiality or obsession with fame.
The hollywoodites are always dressed to impress. 好莱坞的人总是穿着光鲜亮丽。
The party was full of hollywoodites. 派对上挤满了来自好莱坞的人。
synonyms: movie stars, film people, celebrities, actors, actresses
antonyms: civilians, ordinary people
