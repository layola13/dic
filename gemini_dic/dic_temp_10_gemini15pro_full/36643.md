collectors:/kəˈlɛktərz/ | n. | 收藏家；收集者 | People who collect something as a hobby or for investment. |  Many collectors specialize in stamps or coins. 许多收藏家专门收藏邮票或硬币。| synonyms: accumulator, gatherer | antonyms: disperser, distributor | noun

collectors:/kəˈlɛktərz/ | n. | 收集器；采集器 | A device or person that gathers or collects something. | The museum uses special collectors to gather rainwater. 博物馆使用特殊的收集器来收集雨水。| synonyms: accumulator, receiver | antonyms: dispenser, distributor | noun
