champertor:/ˈtʃæmpərtər/ | n. | 唆讼者，包揽诉讼者 | A person who supports or helps finance a lawsuit in return for a share of any damages awarded.  This practice is usually illegal. |  The lawyer was accused of being a champertor, funding lawsuits for personal gain.  这个律师被指控为唆讼者，为个人利益资助诉讼。 | Synonyms:  litigation funder,  lawsuit financier | Antonyms:  defendant, plaintiff (These aren't perfect antonyms, but represent opposing sides in a lawsuit.) | noun


champerty:/ˈtʃæmpərti/ | n. | 包揽诉讼 | The illegal practice of financing or supporting a lawsuit in return for a share of any damages awarded. | Champerty is considered an abuse of the legal system. 包揽诉讼被认为是滥用法律制度的行为。 | Synonyms: maintenance (legal term) | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym exists, as it's a legal term describing an illegal act) | noun
