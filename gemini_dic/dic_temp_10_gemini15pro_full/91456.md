hygrostatics:/ˌhaɪɡroʊˈstætɪks/ | n. |  静力水分学；液体静力学 | The study of the equilibrium of liquids, especially water, at rest.  It deals with the pressure and other properties of liquids at rest. | Hygrostatics plays a crucial role in understanding the water balance in soil.  静力水分学在理解土壤水分平衡中起着至关重要的作用。 | Near synonyms: fluid statics, hydrostatics | Antonyms: hydrokinetics, hydrodynamics | noun

Etymology:  From Greek *hygros* (moist) + *statikos* (causing to stand), from *histanai* (to cause to stand).
