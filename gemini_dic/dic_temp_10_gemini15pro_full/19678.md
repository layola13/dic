bilboquet:/ˌbɪlbəˈkeɪ/ /ˈbɪlbəˌke/ | n. | 空竹，杯子和球游戏 | A traditional toy consisting of a wooden cup or spike attached to a handle by a string, with a ball on the other end of the string. The object is to swing the ball upward and catch it in the cup. |  He spent hours mastering the bilboquet.  他花了几个小时掌握空竹。|  Synonyms: cup-and-ball, ball-in-a-cup | Antonyms: N/A | noun

bilboquet:/ˌbɪlbəˈkeɪ/ /ˈbɪlbəˌke/ | n. | (比喻)棘手的问题，难处理的事情 | A tricky or delicate problem or situation. | This diplomatic issue is a real bilboquet.  这个外交问题真是个棘手的问题。 | Synonyms: dilemma, predicament, puzzle | Antonyms:  solution, cinch | noun
