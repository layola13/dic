instipulate:/ɪnˈstɪpjʊleɪt/
verb
规定；约定；明确要求
To specify or arrange a condition or requirement as part of an agreement or contract.
The contract instipulates that the work must be completed within six months.  合同规定，这项工作必须在六个月内完成。
The lease instipulates no pets. 租赁协议规定不允许养宠物。
近义词: specify, stipulate, prescribe, dictate
反义词: imply, suggest
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "instipulatus," past participle of "instipulari" (to bargain, agree upon), from "in-" (in, into) + "stipula" (stalk, straw; agreement).  Originally referring to making an agreement symbolized by breaking a straw.
