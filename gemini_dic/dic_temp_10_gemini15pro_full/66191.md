excipient:/ɛkˈsɪpiənt/
n.
赋形剂，辅料
An inactive substance that serves as the vehicle or medium for a drug or other active agent.
The excipient in this tablet helps it dissolve quickly.  这种药片中的赋形剂有助于它快速溶解。
synonyms:  base, medium, vehicle, additive, filler
antonyms:  active ingredient, drug, medicine
noun
