alumnae:/əˈlʌmniː/
n.
女毕业生；女校友

The alumnae of the college were invited back for a reunion.  这所大学的女毕业生被邀请回来参加聚会。

近义词:graduates, alumni (if referring to a mixed-gender group)
反义词:student
词性:noun
