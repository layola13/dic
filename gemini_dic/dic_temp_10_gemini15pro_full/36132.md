coeternity:/ˌkoʊɪˈtɜːrnɪti/ /ˌkəʊɪˈtɜːnɪti/| n. |永恒共存，同永恒|The doctrine of the coeternity of the Son with the Father. 圣子和圣父永恒共存的教义。|例句：The coeternity of God and Christ is a central tenet of Christian theology. 上帝和基督的永恒共存是基督教神学的核心原则。

例句：Some philosophers argue for the coeternity of the universe with a divine being. 一些哲学家认为宇宙与神灵永恒共存。|近义词：eternity, perpetuity, sempiternity|反义词:temporality, transience|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *coæternitas*, from co- + æternitas (“eternity”).  The English word is attested from the late 14c.
