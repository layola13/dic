nondramatic:/ˌnɒndrəˈmætɪk/
adjective
非戏剧性的；平淡的；不夸张的
lacking drama or strong emotion; ordinary or uneventful.
The meeting was nondramatic, with everyone agreeing on the next steps.  会议平淡无奇，每个人都同意接下来的步骤。
He gave a nondramatic account of the accident. 他平淡地讲述了事故的经过。

近义词:undemonstrative, unexciting, low-key, matter-of-fact, plain, simple
反义词:dramatic, theatrical, exciting, sensational
词性:adjective
