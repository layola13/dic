audiologist:/ˌɔːdiˈɑːlədʒɪst/ | n. | 听力学家；听觉学家 | A healthcare professional specializing in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of hearing and balance disorders. |  Example: The audiologist performed a hearing test. 听力学家进行了听力测试。

Example:  My grandmother saw an audiologist about her tinnitus. 我的祖母去看听力学家治疗耳鸣。 | 近义词: hearing specialist | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin *audītōrius* ("relating to hearing") + -logist ("one who studies").  *Audītōrius* derives from *audīre* ("to hear").
