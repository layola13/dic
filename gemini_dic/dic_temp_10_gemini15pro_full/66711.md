exophthalmos:/ˌɛksɒfˈθælmɒs/| n. |眼球突出|Abnormal protrusion of the eyeball or eyeballs.|例句：The doctor noticed the patient's exophthalmos and suspected a thyroid problem. 医生注意到病人的眼球突出，怀疑是甲状腺问题。

例句：Graves' disease is a common cause of exophthalmos.  格雷夫斯病是眼球突出的常见原因。|近义词：proptosis, exorbitism|反义词:enophthalmos|词性:noun
