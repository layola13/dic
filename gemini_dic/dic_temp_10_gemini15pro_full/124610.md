mousiness:/ˈmaʊsinəs/| n.|羞怯，胆怯，不起眼|The quality of being timid, shy, and retiring, like a mouse.  It often implies a lack of assertiveness and a tendency to avoid attention.|Her mousiness made it difficult for her to speak up in class. 她的羞怯让她很难在课堂上发言。|Synonyms: timidity, shyness, meekness, diffidence, unassertiveness|Antonyms: boldness, confidence, assertiveness, aggressiveness|noun
