gaggle:/ˈɡæɡəl/| n. |一群（鹅），咯咯声|A flock of geese, especially when on the water.  The sound of geese. |例句：A gaggle of geese waddled across the road. 一群鹅摇摇摆摆地走过马路。

例句：The gaggle of tourists followed the guide closely.  一群游客紧跟着导游。|近义词：flock, group, crowd, throng|反义词：(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 individual, single)|词性：名词
