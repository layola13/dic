hippiest:/ˈhɪpiɪst/| adj.|最嬉皮的，最嬉皮士的|Most like a hippie; most characteristic of hippies.|She had the hippiest clothes, all tie-dye and fringe. 她穿着最嬉皮的衣服，都是扎染和流苏的。|Example: He was the hippiest guy at the festival, wearing flowers in his hair and spreading peace and love. 他是节日里最嬉皮的人，头发上戴着花，传播和平与爱。|Synonyms: most unconventional, most bohemian, most counter-cultural|Antonyms: most conventional, most conservative, most mainstream|Word type: adjective
