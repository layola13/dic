dispirited:/dɪˈspɪrɪtɪd/
adjective
沮丧的，灰心的
Lacking in spirit or enthusiasm; discouraged.
The team was dispirited after losing the championship game.  球队在输掉冠军赛后士气低落。
He returned home, dispirited by the news. 他回到家，被这个消息弄得很沮丧。
近义词: discouraged, disheartened, demoralized, dejected, downcast
反义词: encouraged, spirited, enthusiastic, optimistic, cheerful
词性: adjective

词源:  From dis- (expressing negation) + spirited.  由表示否定的前缀 dis- 加上 spirited（有精神的）构成。
