incivility:/ˌɪnsɪˈvɪləti/
noun
粗鲁；无礼
rudeness; lack of courtesy
The customer service representative's incivility shocked me.  客服代表的粗鲁让我震惊。
The politician was criticized for his incivility towards his opponents.  这位政治家因对对手的无礼而受到批评。
近义词:rudeness, disrespect, impoliteness, discourtesy
反义词:courtesy, politeness, respect, civility
