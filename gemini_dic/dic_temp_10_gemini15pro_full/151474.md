parqueted:/pɑːrˈkeɪtɪd/
|adj.|镶木地板的；用镶木地板装饰的|Covered with parquet flooring; decorated with parquetry.|The living room was parqueted with dark oak. 客厅铺着深色橡木拼花地板。|近义词:wood-floored, parquetry|反义词:uncarpeted, bare|词性:形容词
词源: From French *parqueter*, from *parquet* "small enclosure," diminutive of *parc* "park." Ultimately from Frankish *parruk*, from Proto-Germanic *parrukaz*.
