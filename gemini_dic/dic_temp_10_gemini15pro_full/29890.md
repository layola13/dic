cauterant:/ˈkɔːtərənt/| n. adj.| 烧灼剂，腐蚀剂；烧灼的，腐蚀性的 | A substance that burns or sears tissue, typically to stop bleeding or prevent infection.  It can also be used as an adjective to describe something that has this effect.| The doctor used a cauterant to stop the bleeding from the wound. 医生用烧灼剂来止住伤口流血。 |Synonyms: caustic, escharotic | Antonyms: demulcent, emollient| noun, adjective

cauterize:/ˈkɔːtəraɪz/| vt.| 烧灼，腐蚀| To burn or sear tissue, typically to stop bleeding or prevent infection.| The surgeon cauterized the wound to prevent infection. 外科医生烧灼伤口以防止感染。| Synonyms: sear, burn | Antonyms: heal, soothe | verb
