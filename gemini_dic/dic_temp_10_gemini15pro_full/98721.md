|n.|保险人；承保人|People are advised to shop around for the best deal from insurers. 建议人们货比三家，从保险公司那里找到最划算的交易。|Insurance companies provide financial protection to individuals and businesses against various risks.|例句：The insurers are refusing to pay out.  保险公司拒绝赔付。

例句：Many insurers offer discounts for safe driving. 许多保险公司对安全驾驶提供折扣。

insurer:/ɪnˈʃʊrər/
|n.|保险人；承保人|A person or company that underwrites an insurance risk; the party in an insurance contract undertaking to pay compensation.|例句：The insurer will cover the cost of repairs. 保险公司将承担维修费用。

例句：Contact your insurer immediately if you have an accident. 如果你发生事故，请立即联系你的保险公司。

近义词：underwriter, insurance company, insurance provider|反义词:insured, policyholder|词性:noun
