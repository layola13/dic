lugubrious:/luːˈɡuːbriəs/ | adj. | 悲哀的；阴郁的；忧愁的 |  Looking or sounding sad and dismal.  |  The funeral was a lugubrious affair, with everyone dressed in black. 葬礼是一个悲伤的场合，每个人都穿着黑色衣服。

|  He spoke in a lugubrious tone about the state of the economy. 他用忧郁的语气谈论经济状况。 |  mournful, gloomy, melancholic, somber | cheerful, joyful, happy | adjective
