doubting:/ˈdaʊtɪŋ/| adj.|怀疑的，疑惑的|Feeling or expressing uncertainty about something.|例句：He had a doubting look on his face. 他脸上带着疑惑的表情。|例句：She was doubting her ability to succeed. 她怀疑自己能否成功。|近义词：skeptical, unsure, uncertain, hesitant|反义词：certain, sure, confident, trusting|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *doutende*, present participle of *douten*, from Old French *douter*, from Latin *dubitāre* "to hesitate, waver, doubt," literally "to be in two minds."
