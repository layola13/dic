actinoid:/ˈæktɪnɔɪd/| n. |锕系元素|any of a series of fifteen radioactive metallic elements with atomic numbers from 89 (actinium) to 103 (lawrencium)|Uranium and plutonium are both actinoids. 铀和钚都是锕系元素。|synonyms: actinide|antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From actinium + -oid.
