pandits:/ˈpʌndɪts/| n.|专家，学者，权威人士；（尤指印度教的）学者，圣人|A pandit is a Hindu scholar or teacher, especially one versed in Sanskrit and Hindu philosophy and religion.  A pandit can also refer to an expert in a particular field.|The pandits debated the meaning of the sacred texts. 这些学者们辩论着神圣经文的含义。|Synonyms: scholar, expert, authority, sage, guru|Antonyms: ignoramus, amateur|noun
