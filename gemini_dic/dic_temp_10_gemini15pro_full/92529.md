hypoxia:/haɪˈpɒksiə/ (US) /haɪˈpɒksɪə/ (UK)| n. |缺氧，低氧|A deficiency of oxygen reaching the tissues of the body.|例句：The climbers suffered from hypoxia at high altitude. 登山者在高海拔地区遭受缺氧。

|例句：The report linked the fish kill to hypoxia in the lake. 报告将鱼类死亡与湖泊缺氧联系起来。

|近义词：anoxia, asphyxia|反义词：hyperoxia|词性: noun
