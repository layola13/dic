misconnection:/ˌmɪskəˈnɛkʃən/
n.
错过联系，失联，接错
a failure to connect with someone or something as planned.
例句：I experienced a misconnection in Chicago and missed my connecting flight. 我在芝加哥错过了转机。

例句：There was a misconnection with the luggage, and it arrived a day late. 行李接错了，晚了一天才到。
近义词：missed connection, failure to connect
反义词：connection, successful connection
词性：名词
