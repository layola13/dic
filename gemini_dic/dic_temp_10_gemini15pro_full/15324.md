backslapping:/ˈbækˌslæpɪŋ/
backslapping: n.
backslapping: 中文翻译：互相拍背；过分热情的恭维；虚情假意的称赞
backslapping: 英文解析：The act of slapping someone's back, often as a gesture of congratulation or camaraderie, sometimes perceived as excessive or insincere.
backslapping: 例句：The politicians engaged in a lot of backslapping after the election.  选举结束后，政客们互相拍背庆贺。

backslapping: 例句：There was much backslapping and cheering when the team won the championship.  当球队赢得冠军时，人们互相拍背欢呼。

backslapping: 近义词：flattery, praise,  congratulation, camaraderie
backslapping: 反义词：criticism, condemnation, disapproval
backslapping: 词性: 名词
