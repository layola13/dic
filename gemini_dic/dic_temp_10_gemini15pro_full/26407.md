bye-election:/ˌbaɪ ɪˈlɛkʃən/| n.|补选|A special election held to fill a political office that has become vacant between general elections.|A bye-election was called after the death of the sitting MP.  补选是在现任议员去世后举行的。

The government is expected to lose the upcoming bye-election.  预计政府将在即将举行的补选中失利。|近义词：special election|反义词:general election|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "by" (meaning secondary) + "election".  "By" in this context is an archaic form of "by-" meaning "secondary" or "incidental".
