hyperirritable:/ˌhaɪpərˈɪrɪtəbl/| adj.|过度易怒的，极易激动的|Excessively or abnormally irritable; easily provoked to anger or other extreme reactions.| The patient was hyperirritable after the surgery, reacting strongly to even slight noises.  手术后病人变得极度易怒，即使是轻微的噪音也会有强烈反应。|例句：The baby was hyperirritable due to teething. 由于出牙，婴儿非常烦躁。|近义词：irascible, touchy, choleric, irritable|反义词：calm, placid, tranquil|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (meaning "over, excessive") + irritable (meaning "easily annoyed").  The root of "irritable" comes from the Latin word "irritare," meaning "to excite, provoke."
