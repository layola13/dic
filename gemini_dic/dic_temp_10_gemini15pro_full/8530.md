anticensorship:/ˌæntiˈsɛnsərʃɪp/
adjective
反审查制度的
Opposed to censorship; advocating the freedom of expression and information.
The anticensorship group protested the government's new media regulations.  反审查制度组织抗议政府新的媒体法规。

The library has an anticensorship policy, allowing access to a wide range of materials. 图书馆有反审查制度的政策，允许读者接触各种各样的资料。

近义词:  pro-free speech, anti-censorship
反义词:  pro-censorship, censorship-supporting
词性: adjective
