nomad:/ˈnoʊmæd/ (US), /ˈnɒmæd/ (UK)| n. |游牧民；流浪者|A member of a people having no permanent abode, and who travel from place to place to find fresh pasture for their livestock.|The nomads traveled across the desert seeking water for their herds.|近义词：wanderer, migrant, rover, itinerant|反义词:settler, resident|noun
