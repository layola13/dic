lentamente:/lentaˈmente/ | adv. | slowly; at a slow pace | slowly, in a slow manner.  It describes the manner in which an action is performed. |  She walked lentamente across the room. 她慢慢地走过房间。| slowly, gradually, leisurely | quickly, rapidly, hastily | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *lentamente*, derived from *lento* (slow), ultimately from Latin *lentus* (slow, sluggish).
