enraged:/ɪnˈreɪdʒd/| adj.|被激怒的，暴怒的|Filled with rage; furious.|He was enraged by the injustice of the situation. 他对这种情况的不公正感到愤怒。|近义词：furious, infuriated, livid, outraged|反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (cause to be) + rage (violent anger).  Ultimately from Latin *rabies* (madness, rage).
