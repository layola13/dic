entrance:/ˈɛntrəns/ (US), /ˈɛntrəns/ (UK)| n. |入口，进入；入场，入学；进入权|An entrance is a place where you can enter a building or area.  It can also refer to the act of entering or the right to enter. |例句：The main entrance to the building is on Main Street.  大楼的正门在主街上.|近义词：entry, doorway, access, admission|反义词:exit, departure|noun

entrance:/ɪnˈtræns/ (US), /ɪnˈtrɑːns/ (UK)| vt. |使入迷，使狂喜|To entrance someone is to fill them with wonder and delight, often to the point of captivating their attention completely.|例句：The magician's performance entranced the children. 魔术师的表演使孩子们着迷。|近义词：captivate, fascinate, mesmerize, enchant|反义词:bore, repel|verb
