dodd:/dɑd/ | n. | 道德法案(Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act的简称)| The Dodd-Frank Act aimed to reform the financial system. | 道德-弗兰克法案旨在改革金融体系。 | synonym: Dodd-Frank Act, financial reform legislation | antonym: deregulation | noun


dodd:/dɑd/ | adj. |  与道德法案相关的 |  Dodd-Frank regulations have impacted the banking industry. | 道德-弗兰克法案的监管条例已经影响了银行业。 | synonym: Dodd-Frank related | antonym: pre-Dodd-Frank | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"Dodd" in "Dodd-Frank" refers to Christopher J. Dodd, a former U.S. Senator who co-sponsored the bill.  "Frank" refers to Barney Frank, a former U.S. Representative who also co-sponsored the bill. The act is named after them.
