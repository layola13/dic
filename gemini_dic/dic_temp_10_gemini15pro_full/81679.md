greencarder:/ˈɡriːnˌkɑːrdər/| n. |绿卡持有者；（尤指）通过不正当手段获得绿卡的人|Someone who has a green card, often with implications that it was obtained through fraudulent or questionable means.  This term sometimes carries a negative connotation. |He was accused of being a greencarder who married for convenience. 他被指控是一个为了方便而结婚的绿卡持有者。|Synonym: permanent resident, (negative) immigration fraudster |Antonym:  illegal immigrant, undocumented immigrant |Noun
