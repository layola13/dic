longirostral:/ˌlɒŋɪˈrɒstrəl/ | adj. | 长吻的，长喙的 | Having a long rostrum or beak.  | The longirostral gharial is easily recognizable by its slender snout.  长吻鳄很容易通过它细长的鼻子辨认出来。 |  long-beaked, long-snouted | short-beaked, short-snouted | adjective
Etymology->
From Latin *longirostris*, from longus (“long”) + rostrum (“beak, snout”).
