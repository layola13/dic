countercharges:/ˈkaʊntərˌtʃɑːrdʒɪz/ /ˈkaʊntərˌtʃɑːrdʒəz/
noun
反控诉，反指控
an accusation made in response to an earlier accusation
例句：He filed countercharges against his former employer.  他对他之前的雇主提出了反诉。

例句：The defendant's countercharges complicated the case. 被告的反控使案件变得复杂。

近义词：counteraccusations, counterclaims
反义词：charges, accusations
词性: noun
