outproduced:/ˌaʊtprəˈdjuːst/
verb
产量超过，生产过剩
to produce more than someone or something else.
China outproduced the US in steel last year.  中国去年钢铁产量超过美国。
The factory outproduced its quota this month. 这个工厂本月的产量超过了定额。
synonyms: surpass, exceed, outstrip
antonyms: underproduce, lag behind
词性: 动词
