axiomatically:/ˌæksiəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 公理地；不言而喻地 |  In a way that relates to or is based on axioms; self-evidently.  It is something assumed to be true as a basis for argument or inference. |  Axiomatically, a straight line can be drawn between any two points.  不言而喻，任意两点之间可以画一条直线。

Axiomatically,  free markets are considered more efficient.  公理上，自由市场被认为是更有效的。 | 近义词: self-evidently, obviously, undoubtedly, indisputably | 反义词: questionably, debatably | 词性: 副词
