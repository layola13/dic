concur:/kənˈkɜːr/ | v. | 同意，一致，同时发生 | To agree; to be of the same opinion; to happen at the same time. |  The committee concurred with the recommendations. 委员会同意这些建议。

We concurred on the need for immediate action. 我们一致认为需要立即采取行动。 | 近义词: agree, coincide, correspond | 反义词: disagree, differ, clash | verb

concur:/kənˈkɜːr/ | n. |  同意，一致（较罕见） | Agreement; concurrence. |  There was general concur among the board members. 董事会成员普遍同意。 (这个用法比较正式和少见) | 近义词: agreement, consensus | 反义词: disagreement, dissent | noun
