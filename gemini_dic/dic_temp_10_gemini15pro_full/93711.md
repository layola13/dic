immaculate:/ɪˈmækjələt/| adj.|完美的，洁净的，无瑕疵的|Perfectly clean, neat, or tidy; free from flaws or mistakes.|Her immaculate white dress flowed as she walked. 她洁白的连衣裙随着她的步伐飘动。|近义词：spotless, pristine, flawless, impeccable|反义词：dirty, flawed, imperfect|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *immaculatus*, from *in-* (not) + *maculatus* (spotted, stained).
