crystallizer:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪzər/| n. |结晶器，结晶装置|A tank, vessel, or other apparatus in which crystals are formed.|The solution was poured into the crystallizer to allow the sugar to crystallize.|Synonyms: crystallizing tank, crystallization apparatus, crystallizing pan|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "dissolver")|noun
