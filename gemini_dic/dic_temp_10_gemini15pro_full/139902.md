odd:/ɑːd/
adj.
奇怪的；古怪的；奇数的；剩余的；临时的；零散的
It's odd that she hasn't called.
她没打电话很奇怪。
He has some very odd habits.
他有一些非常奇怪的习惯。
Synonyms: strange, peculiar, unusual, bizarre, weird, quirky
Antonyms: normal, regular, usual, even, common
Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *odde, from Old Norse oddi (“third, triangular point; odd number”), from Proto-Germanic *uzdaz (“pointed, sharp”), from Proto-Indo-European *wes- (“to pierce, prick”).
