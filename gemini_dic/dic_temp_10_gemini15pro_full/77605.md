geelongs:/ˈdʒiːlɒŋz/
n.
吉朗(澳大利亚城市), 吉朗猫(澳大利亚橄榄球队)
Geelong, a city in Victoria, Australia, located on Corio Bay and the Barwon River, southwest of Melbourne. Also, the Geelong Cats, an Australian rules football club based in Geelong.
The Geelong Cats won the AFL Grand Final. 吉朗猫队赢得了澳式足球联赛总决赛。
Geelong is a beautiful coastal city. 吉朗是一座美丽的沿海城市。
synonyms: (for the city) Geelong city, (for the team) the Cats
