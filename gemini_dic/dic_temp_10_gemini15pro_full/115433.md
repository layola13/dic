manto:/ˈmæntoʊ/ (US), /ˈmæntəʊ/ (UK)| n. |斗篷；披风；覆盖物；（昆虫的）鞘翅；（墨西哥女作家）阿连德|A cloak or mantle, especially a loose sleeveless garment worn over other clothes. Also refers to the protective forewings of an insect, or the Mexican writer Isabel Allende. |She wore a beautiful, embroidered manto. 她穿着一件漂亮的绣花斗篷。|近义词：cloak, cape, mantle, shawl|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "undergarment" in a very loose sense)|noun
