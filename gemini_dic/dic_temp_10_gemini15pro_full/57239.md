downier:/ˈdaʊ.ni.ər/| adj.|更绒毛的，更柔软的，更沮丧的|Covered with or containing more down; softer. More depressed or dejected.|The baby bird looked downier than its siblings. 这只雏鸟看起来比它的兄弟姐妹更绒毛。|近义词：softer, fluffier, more depressed, more dejected|反义词：coarser, rougher, happier, more cheerful|词性:形容词
词源: From down + -ier (comparative suffix). Ultimately from Old Norse *dūnn.
