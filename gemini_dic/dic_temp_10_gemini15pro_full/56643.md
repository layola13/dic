donnish:/ˈdɒnɪʃ/ (UK) /ˈdɑːnɪʃ/ (US)| adj.|书呆子气的；学究式的；教授风范的|Resembling or characteristic of a university don; pedantic; bookish.|He had a donnish air, with his tweed jacket and pipe. 他穿着粗花呢夹克，叼着烟斗，一副学究气派。|近义词：academic, scholarly, learned, intellectual, bookish, pedantic|反义词：unintellectual, unscholarly, philistine|词性:形容词

词源: From don + -ish.  "Don" originally referred to a fellow of a college at Oxford or Cambridge.
