morning-glories:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋ ɡlɔːriz/
n.
牵牛花（复数）
A twining plant with funnel-shaped flowers that open in the morning and close during the day.
The morning-glories climbed up the trellis. 牵牛花爬上了棚架。

morning-glory:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋ ɡlɔːri/
n.
牵牛花（单数）
A twining plant with funnel-shaped flowers that open in the morning and close during the day.
The morning-glory is a beautiful flower. 牵牛花是一种美丽的花。


近义词:bindweed, convolvulus
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与不开花的植物或夜间开花的植物对比)
词性:noun
