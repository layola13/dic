heptaphyllous:/ˌhɛptəˈfɪləs/| adj.|七叶的，有七片叶子的|Having seven leaves or leaflets. |Example: The heptaphyllous plant caught my eye in the botanical garden.  我在植物园里注意到那株七叶的植物。

Example:  Heptaphyllous arrangements of leaves are rare in nature.  七叶排列的叶子在自然界中很少见。|近义词:seven-leaved|反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps few-leaved)|词性:adjective
