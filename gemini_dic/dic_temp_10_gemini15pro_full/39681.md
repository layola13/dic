considerable:/kənˈsɪdərəbl/| adj.|相当大的，重要的，值得考虑的|Notable or large in size, amount, or extent.  Worthy of attention or consideration.|例句：A considerable amount of time was spent on the project.  这个项目花费了相当多的时间。|近义词：significant, substantial, large, ample|反义词：insignificant, negligible, small, minor|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From Late Latin *considerabilis*, from Latin *considerare* "to consider," from *com-* "together" + *sidus* "star" (originally "constellation"). The original meaning was "worthy of contemplation, observable in the stars," hence "worthy of consideration."
