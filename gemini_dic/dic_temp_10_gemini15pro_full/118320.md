merest:/ˈmɪərɪst/| adj.|最纯粹的，仅仅的，只不过|smallest or slightest|The merest hint of spice was enough to ruin the delicate flavor. 一丁点的香料就足以破坏这微妙的味道。|近义词：slightest, barest, minimum|反义词:greatest, largest|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *merest, superlative of mere (“pure, unmixed; sheer, utter; only, sole”), from Old French mier, mer (“pure, unadulterated, simple”), from Latin merus (“pure, unmixed, undiluted”).
