clutchier:/ˈklʌtʃiər/ | adj. | 更抓牢的，更紧握的，更依赖的 | More inclined to clutch or cling; more possessive or dependent. |  She became clutchier as the storm intensified, holding tightly onto the railing.  她随着暴风雨的加剧变得更加抓紧，紧紧地抓住栏杆。

| Example: He's much clutchier with his money these days. 他最近对他的钱抓得更紧了。 | Near Synonyms: clingier, possessier, needier | Antonyms:  independent, detached, less possessive | Word type: Adjective

clutchier:/ˈklʌtʃiər/ | adj. | (Informal) 更关键的，更决定性的 | (Informal) Performing better in crucial situations.  | He's a clutchier player than his stats suggest. 他是一个比他的数据显示更关键的球员。

| Example:  She always makes the clutchier shots in the final minutes.  她在最后几分钟总是能投进关键球。 | Near Synonyms:  decisive, critical, crucial | Antonyms:  unimportant, insignificant, irrelevant | Word type: Adjective
