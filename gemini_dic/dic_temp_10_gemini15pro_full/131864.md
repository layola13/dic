nonconsuming:/ˌnɑːnkənˈsuːmɪŋ/
adj.
非消费的，不消耗的
Not engaging in consumption; not using up resources.
例句：
The library offers nonconsuming activities like reading and studying.
图书馆提供阅读和学习等非消费性活动。

They developed a nonconsuming process for data analysis.
他们开发了一种不消耗资源的数据分析过程。

近义词：
non-expendable, preservative, conserving
反义词：
consuming, wasteful, depleting
词性
形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + consuming.  "Consuming" comes from the Latin "consumere" meaning "to use up completely".
