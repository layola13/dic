alcoholic: /ˌælkəˈhɔːlɪk/ | adj. n. | 酒精的，酗酒的；酒鬼，酗酒者 |  Relating to or containing alcohol; a person who is addicted to alcohol. |  例句：He is an alcoholic and needs help. 他是个酗酒者，需要帮助。| 近义词：drunkard, inebriate | 反义词：teetotaler, abstainer | 词性: adjective, noun

alcoholic: /ˌælkəˈhɔːlɪk/ | n. | 酒鬼，酗酒者 | A person who is addicted to alcohol. | 例句：The alcoholic struggled to stay sober.  这个酗酒者努力保持清醒。| 近义词：drunkard, inebriate | 反义词：teetotaler, abstainer | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *alcoholicus, from Medieval Latin alcohol "powdered antimony," from Arabic al-kuḥl "kohl," referring to the fine powder produced by sublimation. The sense shifted to "any sublimated substance," then "pure spirit of wine," and finally to "intoxicating ingredient in fermented liquor."
