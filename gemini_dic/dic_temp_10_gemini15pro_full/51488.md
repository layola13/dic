detractor:/dɪˈtræktər/ | n. | 诽谤者，诋毁者 | Someone who criticizes something or someone, often unfairly. |  The project has its detractors, but it also has many supporters.  这个项目有它的批评者，但也有很多支持者。 | Synonyms: critic, attacker, slanderer, disparager | Antonyms: supporter, admirer, advocate | noun

detractor:/dɪˈtræktər/ | n. | 损害…价值的人（或事物）| Something that diminishes the worth or value of something. | The constant negative press was a major detractor from his campaign.  持续的负面报道是他竞选活动的一大损害。| Synonyms: drawback, disadvantage, detriment | Antonyms: advantage, benefit, asset | noun
