impugnability: /ɪmˌpʌɡnəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可质疑性；可责难性 | The impugnability of the evidence led to the case being dismissed.  证据的可质疑性导致案件被驳回。| Synonyms: disputability, challengeability, contestability | Antonyms: incontestability, irrefutability, unchallengeability | noun


impugn: /ɪmˈpjuːn/ | vt. | 指责；质疑；抨击 | The politician's integrity was impugned by the media. 这位政治家的正直品格受到了媒体的抨击。| Synonyms: challenge, question, dispute, attack, criticize | Antonyms: defend, support, uphold, endorse | verb


Etymology:
From Late Middle English *impugnen, from Old French empugner, from Latin impugnāre (“to fight against, attack”), from in- (“against”) + pugnāre (“to fight”).
