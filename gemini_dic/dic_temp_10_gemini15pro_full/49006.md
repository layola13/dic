degradational:/ˌdɛɡrəˈdeɪʃənəl/
adjective
降解的; 退化的; 腐蚀的
relating to or involving degradation.
The report detailed the degradational processes affecting the coastline. 报告详细描述了影响海岸线的退化过程。
The degradational effects of acid rain on ancient monuments are a serious concern. 酸雨对古代遗迹的腐蚀作用是一个严重的问题。
近义词:  degenerative, decaying, deteriorating
反义词:  regenerative, constructive, improving
词性: adjective

词源: From degradation + -al. Degradation comes from Late Latin degradationem, from Latin degradatus, past participle of degradare "to reduce in rank," from de- "down" + gradus "step, rank."
