disposer:/dɪˈspoʊzər/| n.|处理者，处置者，配置者；[指物]处理机，处置机，配置器；（尤指厨房的）食物残渣处理器|A person or thing that disposes of something, especially a garbage disposal unit in a kitchen sink. |She put the potato peelings in the disposer. 她把土豆皮放进了食物残渣处理器。|近义词:remover, clearer, disposer of|反义词:keeper, hoarder|noun

disposer:/dɪˈspoʊzər/| n.|（财产等的）支配者；赋予者|One who has control over or who bestows something, especially property or authority. |He is the disposer of his father's fortune. 他是他父亲财产的支配者。|近义词:administrator, executor, controller|反义词:recipient, beneficiary|noun
