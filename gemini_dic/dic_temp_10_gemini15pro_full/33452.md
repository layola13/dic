churr:/tʃɜːr/ (NZ) /tʃɝːr/ (US)| n. int.|朋友，伙计；加油，鼓励|A friendly greeting, often used in New Zealand. Also used as a general term of encouragement.|Example: Churr, bro, how's it going? 伙计，你好吗？

Example: Churr, team, let's get this done! 加油，团队，让我们完成这项工作！|近义词：mate, friend, buddy, thanks, cheers|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:名词，感叹词
