painful:/ˈpeɪnfʊl/| adj.|疼痛的；痛苦的；令人不快的|Causing physical or emotional pain; distressing.|例句：The injection was painful. 注射很疼。

例句：Losing her job was a painful experience. 失去工作对她来说是一次痛苦的经历。|近义词：aching, agonizing, distressing, harrowing, sore, unpleasant|反义词：painless, pleasant, comfortable|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from pain + -ful.
