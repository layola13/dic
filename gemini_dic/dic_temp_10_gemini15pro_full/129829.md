non-hepatic:/ˌnɒn hɪˈpætɪk/ /ˌnɑːn hɪˈpætɪk/| adj.|非肝脏的|Not related to or affecting the liver.  It describes something that is not of the liver, or doesn't involve the liver.|The doctor ruled out non-hepatic causes for the patient's jaundice.|近义词：extrahepatic|反义词: hepatic|词性: adjective

Etymology:

* **non-**:  a prefix meaning "not," from Latin *non*.
* **hepatic**: relating to the liver, from Greek *hēpatikos*, from *hēpar* "liver."
