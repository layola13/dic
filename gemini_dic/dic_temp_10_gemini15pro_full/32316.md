chihuahua: /tʃɪˈwɑːwə/ (US), /tʃɪˈwɑːwə/ (UK)| n. |吉娃娃|A very small breed of dog with large erect ears and short smooth coat, originating in Mexico.  It is named after the Mexican state of Chihuahua.|例句：My chihuahua, Pepe, is very protective of me. 我的吉娃娃，佩佩，非常保护我。

例句：Chihuahuas are known for their big personalities despite their small size. 吉娃娃体型虽小，但以个性鲜明著称。

近义词：dog, pup, toy dog|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific breed.  Could use "large dog" for a contrasting concept)|词性:noun
