eyebaths:/ˈaɪbɑːθs/
noun
洗眼液；洗眼杯
A small basin or cup used for bathing the eyes, typically with a sterile saline solution.
She used eyebaths to soothe her irritated eyes after working on the computer all day. 她用洗眼液舒缓了整天对着电脑工作后感到不适的眼睛。
After getting some dust in my eyes, I rinsed them with eyebaths.  我的眼睛里进了些灰尘，我用洗眼杯冲洗了一下。

反义词:None
