outtalks: /ˌaʊtˈtɔːks/
verb
胜过…的口才；说得比…多
to talk longer or more persuasively than someone else
She outtalked her opponent in the debate. 她在那场辩论中胜过对手的口才。
近义词: outargue, outspeak
反义词: be outtalked
