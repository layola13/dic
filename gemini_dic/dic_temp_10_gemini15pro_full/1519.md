acetous: /əˈsiːtəs/
adjective
酸的；醋的；像醋的
Having a sour or vinegary taste or smell; producing vinegar.
The wine had gone off and tasted acetous.  这酒变质了，尝起来有醋味。

The acetous smell filled the room.  房间里充满了醋味。


近义词: sour, vinegary, acidic
反义词: sweet, alkaline
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *acetosus, from Latin acetum (“vinegar”) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives indicating fullness or abundance).

USAGE->
The acetous liquid was used in the experiment. = 这种酸性液体被用于实验中。
The chef added an acetous dressing to the salad. = 厨师在沙拉中加入了醋汁调味料。

The smell of acetous fermentation filled the air. = 空气中弥漫着醋酸发酵的气味。
He noticed an acetous taste in the wine, indicating it was turning to vinegar. = 他注意到酒里有醋味，说明它正在变成醋。

The acetous solution reacted strongly with the base. = 酸性溶液与碱发生强烈反应。
The acetous fumes caused him to cough. = 酸性烟雾使他咳嗽。


PHRASE->
acetous fermentation = 醋酸发酵
acetous acid = 醋酸 (although the more common and scientifically preferred term is acetic acid)
acetous smell = 醋味
acetous taste = 酸味，醋味
acetous solution = 酸性溶液
