outstartling:/ˌaʊtˈstɑːrtlɪŋ/
adjective
令人吃惊的，惊人的
surpassing all others in causing surprise or astonishment.
Her beauty was outstartling. 她的美貌令人惊叹。
近义词:astonishing, amazing, surprising, shocking, startling
反义词:unremarkable, unimpressive, ordinary
