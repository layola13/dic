dispathy:/dɪˈspæθi/
n.
冷漠，缺乏热情，无动于衷
Lack of empathy or interest; indifference.
He showed a dispathy towards his studies. 他对学习表现出冷漠。
近义词：indifference, apathy, unconcern
反义词：empathy, sympathy, interest
词性: 名词

Etymology:  From dis- (expressing negation, lack) + -pathy (feeling, suffering).  Ultimately from Greek *dyspátheia*, from *dys-*, "bad, ill, difficult" + *páthos*, "suffering, feeling, experience, emotion".
