cove:/kəʊv/ (UK) /koʊv/ (US)|n.|小海湾，山凹，洞穴|A small sheltered bay or inlet, often enclosed by cliffs or steep slopes; a sheltered recess in a hillside or mountainside.|We anchored the boat in a secluded cove. 我们把船停泊在一个僻静的小海湾里。|近义词:bay, inlet, harbor, creek|反义词:cape, headland|名词

cove:/kəʊv/ (UK) /koʊv/ (US)|v.|（尤指用拱顶）覆盖，遮蔽|To arch or cover, especially with a vault.|The ceiling was beautifully coved. 天花板被拱顶覆盖得很漂亮。|近义词:arch, vault, cover|反义词:expose, uncover|动词
