antidemocrats:/ˈæntiˈdɛməˌkræts/
|n.|反民主主义者；反对民主的人|People who oppose democracy or democratic principles.  They might believe in authoritarian rule or other forms of government.|The rise of antidemocrats in the country is a concerning trend.  这个国家反民主主义者的兴起是一个令人担忧的趋势。|Synonyms: authoritarians, totalitarians, despots, dictators|Antonyms: democrats, liberals, egalitarians|Noun
