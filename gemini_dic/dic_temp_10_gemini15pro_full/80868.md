goutiness:/ˈɡaʊtɪnɪs/| n. |痛风, 患痛风的倾向|A state of being affected with gout or gouty symptoms.  The condition of having gout. |He suffered from goutiness in his later years. 他晚年饱受痛风之苦。|Synonyms: gout, arthralgia,  hyperuricemia|Antonyms: health, wellness|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From gout + -iness.  Gout ultimately comes from the Latin gutta (drop), reflecting the ancient belief that the disease was caused by a "drop" of morbid humor in the joints.
