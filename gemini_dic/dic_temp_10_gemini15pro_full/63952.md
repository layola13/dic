episcopant:/ɪˈpɪskəpənt/| n.|主教,监督者|A bishop; an overseer.  Derived from the Late Latin *episcopans*, present participle of *episcopāre*, "to act as a bishop," from the Greek *episkopein*, "to oversee."|The episcopant addressed the congregation with a powerful sermon. 主教以一篇强有力的布道向会众讲话。|近义词：bishop, prelate, overseer|反义词:laity, parishioner|词性:noun
