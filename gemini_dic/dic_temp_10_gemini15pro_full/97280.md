influential: /ˌɪnfluˈɛnʃl/ | adj. | 有影响力的；有权势的 | having the power to cause changes; having influence |  Her work has been incredibly influential in the field of psychology.  她的工作在心理学领域有着极大的影响力。

Nelson Mandela was an influential leader.  纳尔逊·曼德拉是一位有影响力的领导人。

Synonyms: powerful, impactful, significant, potent, dominant

Antonyms: powerless, insignificant, unimportant, ineffective, weak

Etymology:  Late Middle English: from late Latin *influentialis, from influentia ‘influence’ (see influence).
