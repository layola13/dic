deathlier: /ˈdɛdliər/ (US), /ˈdɛdliə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更致命的，更危险的|Comparative form of "deadly."  Indicates a greater capacity to cause death or serious harm.|The virus mutated into a deathlier strain. 病毒变异成了更致命的毒株。| The new weapons made war deathlier than ever before. 新式武器使战争比以往任何时候都更加致命。 |近义词：more dangerous, more lethal, more harmful, more pernicious|反义词:safer, less harmful, less dangerous|词性:形容词
词源: From Old English *dēadlic, from dēad "dead."  The "-lier" suffix is a comparative ending.
