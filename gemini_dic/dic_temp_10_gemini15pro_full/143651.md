outkings:/ˌaʊtˈkɪŋz/
verb
胜过；在…中称王
To surpass or excel someone in a kingly or authoritative manner.
He outkinged all the other monarchs in wisdom and diplomacy. 他在智慧和外交方面胜过所有其他君主。
She outkinged her rivals in the business world, becoming the undisputed leader. 她在商界胜过她的竞争对手，成为无可争议的领导者。
近义词:surpass, excel, outdo, outstrip, transcend
反义词:fail, lose, fall short
词性:v.


词源:
"Out-" (prefix meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "king" (noun)
