nontherapeutical: /ˌnɒnθɛrəˈpjuːtɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːnθɛrəˈpjuːtɪkəl/
adjective
非治疗性的
Not related to or used in the treatment of disease or illness.  Not serving a therapeutic purpose.
This cream has nontherapeutical benefits, like moisturizing the skin.  这款面霜具有非治疗性的益处，例如滋润皮肤。
The study focused on the nontherapeutical uses of certain herbs. 这项研究的重点是某些草药的非治疗用途。
近义词: non-therapeutic, non-medicinal
反义词: therapeutic, medicinal
词性: adjective
