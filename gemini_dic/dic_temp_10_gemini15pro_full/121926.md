ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- + shapen + -ness.  "Mis-" is a prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly". "Shapen" is the past participle of the verb "shape", meaning "to give a particular form or contour to." "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
