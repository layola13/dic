attenuator:/əˈtɛnjuːeɪtər/| n. |衰减器，减弱器|A device that reduces the strength of a signal, power, or other form of energy.|The engineer used an attenuator to lower the volume of the audio signal.|Synonyms: damper, reducer, suppressor|Antonyms: amplifier, booster|noun

attenuate:/əˈtɛnjuːeɪt/| v. |衰减，减弱|To reduce the force, effect, or value of something.|The thick walls attenuated the noise from the street.|Synonyms: diminish, lessen, weaken|Antonyms: amplify, increase, strengthen|verb
