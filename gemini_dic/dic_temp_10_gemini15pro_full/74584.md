frazzle:/ˈfræzl/| v. | 使疲惫不堪; 磨损; |to completely exhaust or wear out someone; to fray the edges or ends of something. |例句：The long journey completely frazzled me. 漫长的旅程让我筋疲力尽。

例句：The rough ground frazzled the bottom of my jeans. 粗糙的地面磨损了我的牛仔裤底部。|近义词：exhaust, tire, wear out, fray|反义词：refresh, energize, repair|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
Originates from the late 19th century, possibly from dialectal English *frazz*, meaning "to fray."  The noun form likely developed from the verb.
