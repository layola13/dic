gown:/ɡaʊn/| n. |长袍，礼服，睡袍|A long, flowing garment, typically worn on formal occasions or for specific purposes like academic ceremonies or by judges, clergy, or hospital patients.|例句：She wore a beautiful gown to the gala. 她穿着一件漂亮的礼服参加了晚会。

例句：The surgeon donned his surgical gown. 外科医生穿上了他的手术服。

|近义词：dress, robe, frock|反义词：(No direct opposite, perhaps casual clothes)|词性：名词

gown:/ɡaʊn/| v. |(formal) 给…穿长袍|To dress someone in a gown.|例句：The queen was gowned in a magnificent velvet robe. 女王穿着华丽的天鹅绒长袍。

例句：The graduates were gowned in their academic regalia. 毕业生们穿着他们的学位服。


|近义词：dress, robe|反义词：undress|词性：动词
