overhastened:/ˌoʊvərˈheɪsənd/| adj.| 过于仓促的，操之过急的| Done too quickly and therefore not done well. | Example: The overhastened decision led to disastrous consequences.  过于仓促的决定导致了灾难性的后果。| Example:  His overhastened departure left many questions unanswered. 他过于仓促的离开留下了许多未解答的问题。|Synonyms: rushed, hurried, precipitous, hasty | Antonyms: deliberate, careful, measured, cautious | Adjective
