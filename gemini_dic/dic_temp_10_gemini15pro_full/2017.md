actuaries:/ˈækʃʊɛriz/| n.|精算师|A person who compiles and analyzes statistics and uses them to calculate insurance risks and premiums.|例句：Actuaries play a vital role in the insurance industry.  精算师在保险业中扮演着至关重要的角色。

Actuaries use complex mathematical models to assess risk. 精算师使用复杂的数学模型来评估风险。|近义词：statisticians, analysts|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "risk takers" in a loose sense)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *actuarius (“registrar, clerk, stenographer”), from Latin actus (“act, action, performance, drive, impulse, a driving”), from agō (“I do, drive, act”).  最终源自拉丁语 agō (“我做，驱动，行动”)
