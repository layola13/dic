leucophyllous:/ˌluːkəˈfɪləs/| adj.|白叶的；淡色的|Having white or pale-colored leaves.|Example: Some desert plants are leucophyllous, reflecting sunlight to avoid overheating.  一些沙漠植物是白叶的，反射阳光以避免过热。

Example: The leucophyllous nature of the plant helps it survive in harsh conditions.  这种植物的白叶特性有助于它在恶劣条件下生存。|近义词：pale, light-colored, achromatic|反义词:chlorophyllous, green|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *λευκόφυλλος (leukóphullos)  meaning "white-leaved",  from  *λευκός (leukós, "white") + *φύλλον (phúllon, "leaf").
