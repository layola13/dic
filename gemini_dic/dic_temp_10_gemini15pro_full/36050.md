codriver:/ˈkoʊˌdraɪvər/ (US) /ˈkəʊˌdraɪvər/ (UK)| n.|副驾驶，领航员|A person who accompanies and assists the driver of a vehicle, especially in a rally or race.|例句：The codriver reads the pace notes to the driver. 领航员向驾驶员读路书。

例句：My brother acted as my codriver on the long road trip. 在长途公路旅行中，我哥哥担任我的副驾驶。|近义词：navigator, co-pilot|反义词:driver, pilot|词性:noun
