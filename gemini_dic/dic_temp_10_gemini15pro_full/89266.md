hommock:/ˈhɑːmək/ (US), /ˈhɒmək/ (UK)| n. |吊床；小丘；草堆|A hanging bed or couch, typically made of fabric or netting and suspended between two points, used especially in tropical regions. Also, a low mound or ridge of earth; a hummock.|

例句：
The sailor slept soundly in his hommock.
水手在吊床里睡得很香。

例句：
The explorers climbed a small hommock to survey the surrounding area.
探险家爬上一个小山丘来勘察周围的地区。


近义词：hammock (variant spelling), swing, cradle|
反义词:bed, ground|
词性:noun|

词源：
From Spanish *hamaca*, from Taino *hamaka*
