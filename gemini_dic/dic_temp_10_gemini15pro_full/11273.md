arcs:/ɑːrks/ | n. | 弧，弧形，电弧 | A curved line or part of a circle; a luminous discharge of electricity between two electrodes. | The rainbow formed a beautiful arc across the sky. 彩虹在天空中形成了一道美丽的弧线。 The welder created an arc with his torch. 焊工用焊枪制造了一个电弧。 | synonyms: curve, arch, bend | antonyms: line, straight | noun

arcs:/ɑːrks/ | v. | 形成弧形，呈弧形运动 | To move in a curved path. | The ball arced through the air. 球在空中划出一道弧线。 The car arced around the corner. 汽车绕过拐角。| synonyms: curve, bend, arch | antonyms: straighten | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *arcus (plural), from Latin arcus "bow, arch, vault," related to arca "chest, box."
