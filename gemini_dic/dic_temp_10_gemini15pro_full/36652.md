collegial:/kəˈliːdʒiəl/
adjective
大学的;学院的; 共同掌权的;合作的
Relating to or involving shared responsibility, as among colleagues. Characterized by or exhibiting colleagueship.
例句：The department maintains a collegial atmosphere.  该系保持着一种合作的氛围。

例句：We work in a collegial environment. 我们在一个合作的环境中工作。

近义词：cooperative, collaborative, joint, mutual, shared, combined, united, collective, communal, conjoint
反义词：uncooperative, individualistic, autocratic, dictatorial
词性: adjective
