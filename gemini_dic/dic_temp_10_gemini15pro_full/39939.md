constructor: /kənˈstrʌktər/ | n. | 构造器，构造函数 |  A special method within a class used to create and initialize objects of that class.  It is automatically called when a new object of the class is created. |  The constructor initialized the object's name and age. 构造函数初始化了对象的名称和年龄。|  initializer, builder | destructor | noun
