oks:/ɒks/ | interj. | OK; okay | An informal way of expressing agreement, acceptance, or acknowledgment. | Example 1: "Oks, I'll see you tomorrow."  好的，明天见。 | Example 2: "Oks, that sounds good." 好的，听起来不错。| Synonym: OK, okay, alright, all right | Antonym: no, nope | interjection


ETYMOLOGY->
Possibly from a humorous misspelling of "all correct" as "oll korrect," which was then abbreviated to "OK."  Other theories exist, but this is the most widely accepted.
