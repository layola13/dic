bolivians:/bəˈlɪviənz/
n.
玻利维亚人
People from Bolivia, a country in South America.
Many Bolivians speak Spanish and Aymara. 许多玻利维亚人说西班牙语和艾马拉语。
Bolivians are known for their rich cultural heritage. 玻利维亚人以其丰富的文化遗产而闻名。
Synonyms: Bolivian people, citizens of Bolivia
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a nationality)
