chauffeured:/ˈʃəʊfəd/ (US) /ˈʃəʊfəd/ (UK)| adj.| 专车接送的，配有司机的|Driven by a chauffeur; provided with a chauffeur.  Having a chauffeur.|例句：They arrived in a chauffeured limousine. 他们乘坐一辆配有司机的豪华轿车抵达。|例句：She enjoys the convenience of a chauffeured car. 她喜欢配有司机汽车的便利性。|近义词：driver-driven, chauffeur-driven|反义词:self-driven, driverless|词性:形容词

词源:
From the French word "chauffer," meaning "to heat," originally referring to the stoker of a steam engine.  Later, the term evolved to describe the driver of a motor vehicle.
