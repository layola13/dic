nonderogatorilyness:/ˌnɒndɪˈrɒɡətərɪlinəs/ | n. |  非贬低性；无损害性 | The state or quality of being nonderogatory; not intended to disparage or belittle. |  He spoke of his former employer with a surprising nonderogatorilyness, considering their acrimonious parting. 他谈到他以前的雇主时，语气出奇地没有贬低之意，考虑到他们不愉快的分手。 | 近义词: neutrality, impartiality, objectivity | 反义词: derogatoriness, disparagement | 词性: noun

词源:  From "nonderogatory" + "-ness."  "Nonderogatory" is formed from "non-" (not) + "derogatory" (disparaging). "Derogatory" comes from the Late Latin "derogatorius," from "derogare" (to detract from).
