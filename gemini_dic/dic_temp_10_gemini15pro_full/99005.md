interacting:/ˌɪntərˈæktɪŋ/ | adj. | 互动，互相作用，互相影响 | engaging or communicating with someone or something | The children were interacting well with each other. 孩子们彼此相处融洽。 | Synonyms: communicating, connecting, engaging, reacting | Antonyms: ignoring, isolating, neglecting | word type: adjective

interacting:/ˌɪntərˈæktɪŋ/ | v. | 互动，互相作用，互相影响 | the action of engaging or communicating with someone or something |  They are interacting with customers online. 他们在网上与顾客互动。 | Synonyms: communicating, connecting, engaging, reacting | Antonyms: ignoring, isolating, neglecting | word type: verb (present participle)

Etymology: From interact + -ing. Interact comes from Latin inter- (between) + agere (to do, to drive, to act).
