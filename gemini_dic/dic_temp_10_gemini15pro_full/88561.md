hitches:/ˈhɪtʃɪz/ | n. |故障，障碍，小问题；(绳索的)结，套 |A hitch is a temporary problem or difficulty that delays or interrupts something.  It can also refer to a knot or loop of rope used for fastening. |The project encountered several hitches along the way.  这个项目进行过程中遇到了几个小问题。| Synonyms: snag, glitch, obstacle, problem, knot, loop | Antonyms:  smoothness, ease, solution | noun

hitches:/ˈhɪtʃɪz/ | v. |(使)被钩住，(使)系住；搭便车 | To hitch something means to fasten or attach it to something else with a rope, hook, etc.  As a verb, it can also mean to hitchhike. |He hitched the trailer to his car. 他把拖车挂在汽车上。| Synonyms: fasten, attach, secure, tie, hitchhike | Antonyms: detach, unfasten, untie | verb
