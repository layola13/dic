ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *allerta* "on the watch," from *all'erta* "on the lookout," literally "to the height," from *alla* "to the" (feminine of *al*, from Latin *ad* "to") + *erta* "height, steep place," from Latin *erecta*, feminine past participle of *erigere* "to erect."
