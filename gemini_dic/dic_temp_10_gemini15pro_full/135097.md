nonobservant:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɜːrvənt/ /ˌnɑːnəbˈzɜːrvənt/| adj.|不注意的，不遵守的，不虔诚的|Not observant; failing to observe or comply with something, especially a law, custom, or religious practice.|He's fairly nonobservant of social conventions. 他不太遵守社会习俗。|Synonyms: inattentive, unmindful, heedless, lax, neglectful|Antonyms: observant, attentive, mindful, heedful, scrupulous|adjective
