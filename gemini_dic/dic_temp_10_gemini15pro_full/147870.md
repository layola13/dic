overreach:/ˌoʊvərˈriːtʃ/ | v. | 过度伸展；超越；欺骗；不自量力 | To defeat (someone or oneself) by going too far or trying too hard.  To reach or extend over or beyond. |  He overreached himself and fell. 他不自量力，摔倒了。 The company overreached itself with ambitious expansion plans. 公司因扩张计划过于宏大而力有不逮。 | 近义词: outsmart, cheat, deceive, defeat, surpass | 反义词: underestimate, fail | 词性: 动词

overreached:/ˌoʊvərˈriːtʃt/ | v. | 过度伸展的；超越的；欺骗了的；不自量力的 (overreach的过去式和过去分词) | Past tense and past participle of overreach. | He had overreached himself and lost everything. 他不自量力，结果失去了一切。 The boxer overreached with a wild punch and left himself open to a counterattack. 拳击手出拳过猛，露出了破绽，遭到反击。 | 近义词: outsmarted, cheated, deceived, defeated, surpassed | 反义词: underestimated, failed | 词性: 动词


词源:  From Middle English *overrechen, from Old English ofer- + rǣcan (“to reach”).
