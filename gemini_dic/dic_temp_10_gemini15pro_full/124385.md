motleyest:/ˈmɒt.li.ɪst/ | adj. | 最杂色的，最多样化的 |  Superlative form of "motley": exhibiting the greatest variety of elements, often incongruous and brightly colored. | The motleyest crew of pirates ever assembled gathered at Tortuga.  最杂色的海盗队伍聚集在托尔图加岛。| The jester wore the motleyest costume in the entire kingdom.  这个小丑穿着全王国最杂色的服装。 | Synonyms:  most diverse, most varied, most heterogeneous, most miscellaneous | Antonyms:  most uniform, most homogeneous, most consistent | adjective
