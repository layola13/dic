nonmodally:/nɒnˈməʊdəli/ | adv. | 非模态地 |  In a way that does not interrupt or block the main flow of interaction or execution (especially in computer interfaces).  It allows the user to continue interacting with other parts of the interface while the nonmodal window or process is active. | The dialog box appeared nonmodally, allowing me to continue working in the main application.  这个对话框以非模态方式出现，允许我继续在主应用程序中工作。

The preferences panel opened nonmodally, so I could adjust the settings without interrupting my workflow.  偏好设置面板以非模态方式打开，因此我可以调整设置而不会中断我的工作流程。 | 近义词: asynchronously, concurrently | 反义词: modally | 词性: 副词
