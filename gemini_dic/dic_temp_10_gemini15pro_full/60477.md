ejected:/ɪˈdʒektɪd/
|v.|被喷射出的，被驱逐的|forced out or expelled; removed from a position or place.|The unruly fan was ejected from the stadium.  那个不守规矩的球迷被赶出了体育场。|Synonyms: expelled, ousted, removed, discharged|Antonyms: admitted, accepted, welcomed|Verb (past participle)

eject:/ɪˈdʒekt/
|v.|喷射，驱逐|to force or throw out, especially suddenly or violently; to dismiss or expel someone from a place or position.|The pilot ejected from the failing aircraft. 飞行员从失控的飞机中弹射出来。|Synonyms: expel, oust, remove, discharge|Antonyms: admit, accept, welcome|Verb
