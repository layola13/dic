horticulturists:/ˌhɔːrtɪˈkʌltʃərɪsts/
n.
园艺学家；园艺工作者
A person who practices or studies horticulture; a gardener.
The horticulturists at the botanical garden are working on a new hybrid rose.  植物园的园艺学家正在研究一种新的杂交玫瑰。
近义词：gardeners, growers, cultivators
反义词: N/A
词性: Noun


Etymology:
From horticulture + -ist.  Horticulture from Latin hortus “garden” + cultura “cultivation”.
