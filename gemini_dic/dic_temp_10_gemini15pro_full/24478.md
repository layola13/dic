brittle:/ˈbrɪtl/| adj.|易碎的；脆弱的；尖刻的；冷淡的|Hard but liable to break or shatter easily.  Also, describes a person or relationship that is easily damaged emotionally, or a sound that is short and sharp.|The antique vase was so brittle that I was afraid to touch it.  古董花瓶太脆了，我都不敢碰它。|Synonyms: fragile, frail, delicate, crisp, short (sound)|Antonyms: resilient, flexible, tough, pliable|adjective
