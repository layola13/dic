contextualised:/kənˈtekstʃuəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 置于语境中的，根据上下文的 | Placed or studied in context; considered within its relevant context. | Example: The meaning of the word is contextualised by the surrounding sentences.  这个词的意思是由周围的句子语境化的。 | Example:  Contextualised learning helps students understand the practical applications of knowledge.  语境化学习帮助学生理解知识的实际应用。 | Synonyms: situated, placed, embedded, grounded | Antonyms: decontextualized, abstract, isolated | Adjective

Etymology:

From context + -ual + -ize + -ed.  "Context" comes from Latin "contextus" meaning "a joining together."
