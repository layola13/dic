hyperbolists:/haɪˈpɜːbəlɪsts/ | n. |夸张者，吹牛大王|A person who uses hyperbole; someone who exaggerates.|The politician was accused of being a hyperbolist, always inflating the truth for dramatic effect. 这位政治家被指责为夸张者，总是为了戏剧效果而夸大事实。|Synonyms: exaggerator, fabricator, storyteller |Antonyms: understater, minimizer |Noun


hyperbole:/haɪˈpɜːbəli/ | n. |夸张，夸饰|Exaggerated statements or claims not meant to be taken literally.|He used hyperbole to describe his fishing trip, claiming he caught a fish the size of a small car. 他用夸张的语气描述了他的钓鱼之旅，声称他钓到了一条小汽车大小的鱼。|Synonyms: exaggeration, overstatement, embellishment |Antonyms: understatement, litotes |Noun
