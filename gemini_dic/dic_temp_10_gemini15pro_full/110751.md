lingo:/ˈlɪŋɡoʊ/ (US) /ˈlɪŋɡəʊ/ (UK)| n.|行话，术语，方言|A language or dialect, especially one spoken by a particular group of people.|Example: Medical lingo can be difficult for patients to understand. 医学术语对病人来说可能很难理解。|Example:  He quickly picked up the lingo of the surfers. 他很快学会了冲浪者的行话。|Synonyms: jargon, slang, vernacular, dialect, idiom|Antonyms: standard language, formal language|noun
