overspeeded: /ˌoʊvərˈspiːdɪd/ | adj. | 超速的，速度过快的 | Exceeding a prescribed or reasonable speed. |  The overspeeded car caused a serious accident. 超速行驶的汽车造成了一起严重事故。 | The overspeeded train derailed. 超速行驶的火车出轨了。 |  近义词：excessive speed, too fast | 反义词：slow, underspeeded | 词性：形容词

overspeed: /ˌoʊvərˈspiːd/ | v. | 超速行驶，速度过快 | To drive or operate (a vehicle or machine) at a speed greater than is allowed or prudent. | He oversped and got a ticket. 他超速行驶，吃了罚单。 | The engine oversped and broke down. 引擎超速运转，然后抛锚了。 | 近义词：exceed the speed limit, drive too fast | 反义词：drive slowly, under speed | 词性：动词
