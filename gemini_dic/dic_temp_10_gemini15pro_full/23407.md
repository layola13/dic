brachyurous:/brækɪˈjʊərəs/
adjective
短尾的
Having a short tail; belonging to the Brachyura, an infraorder of decapod crustaceans characterized by a short, broad abdomen folded under the thorax.
The brachyurous crab scuttled sideways across the sand.  短尾蟹横着穿过沙滩。
近义词:short-tailed
反义词:long-tailed
词性:adjective

brachyurous:/brækɪˈjʊərəs/
noun
短尾类动物
A crustacean of the infraorder Brachyura, characterized by a short, broad abdomen folded under the thorax; a crab.
Many brachyurous species are prized as seafood. 许多短尾类物种被视为珍贵的食物。
近义词:crab
反义词: (No specific antonym, perhaps a long-tailed crustacean like a lobster or shrimp)
词性:noun
