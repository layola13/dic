hockeies:/ˈhɒkiːz/ | n. | 曲棍球比赛（复数，非标准用法） |  A non-standard plural form of "hockey," referring to multiple games or instances of playing hockey.  This form is generally considered incorrect. Should be: "We watched three hockey games last weekend.") | 近义词: hockey games (correct plural) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: Noun


Etymology: From "hockey," which ultimately derives from the Middle French "hoquet," meaning "shepherd's crook," likely referring to the shape of the stick.
