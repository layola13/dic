connutritious:/ˌkɒnjuːˈtrɪʃəs/ | adj. | 营养丰富的，共同滋养的 | Providing nourishment together; mutually nourishing. |  Example: The connutritious properties of the soil and the plants growing in it are fascinating. 土壤和生长在其中的植物相互滋养的特性令人着迷。|  Example:  A connutritious relationship exists between certain fungi and tree roots. 某些真菌和树根之间存在着一种共同滋养的关系。| Synonyms: nourishing, nutritive, beneficial, symbiotic | Antonyms: detrimental, harmful, toxic | adjective
