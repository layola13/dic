clear-from:/klɪrˈfrʌm/ | phrasal verb | 免除；清除；离开 | To remove something or someone from a place or situation, often officially or formally.  | The police cleared the protesters from the street. 警察将抗议者从街上驱散。|  Synonyms: remove from, rid of, free from, evacuate from | Antonyms:  place in, put on, introduce to | verb phrase

clear-from:/klɪrˈfrʌm/ | phrasal verb | 声明...无罪；证明...清白 | To officially declare someone innocent or free from blame or suspicion. | The investigation cleared him from any wrongdoing. 调查证明他没有做错任何事。 | Synonyms: exonerate, absolve, vindicate, acquit | Antonyms:  incriminate, accuse, convict | verb phrase
