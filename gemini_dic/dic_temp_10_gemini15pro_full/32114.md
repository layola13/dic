chesty:/ˈtʃɛsti/| adj.|胸部丰满的；胸音重的；爱逞强的|Having a large or well-developed chest; having a deep or resonant voice;  prone to boasting or showing off.|She had a chesty cough. 她咳嗽得很厉害，胸腔呼噜呼噜的。|近义词：busty, deep-voiced, boastful|反义词：flat-chested, high-voiced, modest|词性:adjective
词源: From chest + -y.
