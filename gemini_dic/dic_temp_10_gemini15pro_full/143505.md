outgnaw:/ˌaʊtˈnɔː/
verb
咬断，啃穿，超过
gnaw something out from something; gnaw through something. To bite or chew something repeatedly until a hole is made in it or it breaks off. To erode or wear away something gradually.
The dog outgnawed the rope and escaped. 狗咬断了绳子逃跑了。
Acid rain has outgnawed the inscription on the tombstone. 酸雨腐蚀了墓碑上的铭文。
Synonyms: erode, corrode, gnaw through
Antonyms: build, construct, repair
词性: 动词
