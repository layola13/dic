heartlessly:/ˈhɑːrtləsli/| adv.|无情地，冷酷地|Without heart; cruelly; unfeelingly. Showing no pity or consideration for others' feelings.|She heartlessly abandoned her family. 她无情地抛弃了她的家人。|Synonym: cruelly, callously, ruthlessly, pitilessly|Antonym: kindly, compassionately, mercifully|Part of Speech: Adverb

heartless:/ˈhɑːrtlɪs/| adj.|无情的，冷酷的|Lacking compassion or feeling; cruel.|It was a heartless act to abandon the kittens. 抛弃小猫是一种无情的行为。|Synonym: cruel, callous, unfeeling, merciless|Antonym: kind, compassionate, caring, sympathetic|Part of Speech: Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From heart + -less.  The suffix "-less" means "without" or "lacking." Therefore, "heartless" literally means "without heart," referring not to the physical organ but to compassion and empathy.
