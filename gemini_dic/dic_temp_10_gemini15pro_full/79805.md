glycocholic:/ˌɡlaɪkoʊˈkɑːlɪk/ | adj. |  糖胆酸的；含有糖胆酸的 |  Relating to or containing glycocholic acid, a bile acid. | Glycocholic acid is a major bile acid in humans. 糖胆酸是人体主要的胆汁酸。| synonym: bile, cholalic | antonym: None | adjective

glycocholic:/ˌɡlaɪkoʊˈkɑːlɪk/ | n. | 糖胆酸 |  A bile acid, C₂₆H₄₃NO₆, occurring as the sodium salt in the bile of most vertebrates. |  Glycocholic acid helps in the digestion of fats. 糖胆酸有助于脂肪的消化。| synonym: bile salt | antonym: None | noun
