pansophy:/pænˈsɒfi/ | n. | 泛智论；全知；博学 |  A system of philosophy that attempts to embrace all branches of knowledge.  A belief in the potential ability of the human mind to know everything. |  He was a proponent of pansophy, believing that all knowledge was interconnected. 他是泛智论的支持者，相信所有知识都是相互关联的。 |  His library reflected his pansophy, containing books on every conceivable subject.  他的图书馆反映了他的博学，包含了所有能想到的主题的书籍。 | 近义词: omniscience, encyclopedism | 反义词: ignorance, nescience | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek πᾶν (pân, “all”) + σοφία (sophía, “wisdom”).
