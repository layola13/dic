nonphotographical:/ˌnɒnˌfəʊtəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective

The museum has a large collection of nonphotographical materials, including sketches and paintings.  博物馆收藏了大量的非摄影资料，包括素描和绘画。

synonyms: non-photographic, nonpictorial
antonyms: photographic, pictorial
