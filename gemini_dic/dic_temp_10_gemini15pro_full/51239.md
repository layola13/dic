destructibles:/dɪˈstrʌktɪblz/
n.
可破坏物；可摧毁物
Objects or things that can be destroyed.
The game features many destructibles, including barrels, crates, and walls.  这个游戏有很多可破坏物，包括桶、板条箱和墙。
The army targeted enemy destructibles like bridges and communication towers.  军队瞄准了敌人的可破坏物，如桥梁和通讯塔。
synonyms: breakables, damageables
antonyms: indestructibles, unbreakable
