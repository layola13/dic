branch-out:/bræntʃ aʊt/ | phrasal verb | 分支出来；扩展业务；拓展（兴趣等）| To expand one's interests, activities, or business into new areas. |  The company decided to branch out into the clothing market.  这家公司决定进军服装市场。

The restaurant is branching out and offering catering services. 这家餐厅正在拓展业务，提供餐饮服务。| 近义词：expand, diversify, broaden, explore | 反义词：focus, specialize, narrow | 词性：动词短语
