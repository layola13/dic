bluishness:/ˈbluːɪʃnəs/
noun
淡蓝色; 发蓝; 浅蓝色
The quality or state of being bluish.
The sky had a slight bluishness.  天空略带蓝色。
blueness, azure,  cyan
yellowness, redness
n.
