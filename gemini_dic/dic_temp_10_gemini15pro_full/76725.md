gambogic: /ɡæmˈboʊdʒɪk/
adjective
藤黄的；柬埔寨的
Relating to or derived from gamboge, a gum resin obtained from various Southeast Asian trees, typically yellow-orange in color; also, relating to Cambodia.
The artist used a vibrant gambogic pigment in her landscape painting.  这位艺术家在她的风景画中使用了鲜艳的藤黄颜料。
The museum exhibited a collection of gambogic sculptures.  博物馆展出了一批柬埔寨雕塑。
近义词: yellow-orange, Cambodian
反义词: (No direct antonym.  Consider antonyms for "yellow" such as "blue" or "purple" if referring to color, or antonyms for "Cambodian" depending on the specific context, e.g., "Thai," "Vietnamese" if discussing nationality.)
