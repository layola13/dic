coxswain:/ˈkɒksən/ (UK), /ˈkɑːkswɪn/ (US)| n. |舵手；艇长|A coxswain is the person in charge of a boat, especially a racing boat, who steers and directs the rowers.|The coxswain shouted instructions to the crew.  艇长向队员们大声喊着指令。|Synonyms: helmsman, steersman|Antonyms: crew, rower|noun

coxswains:/ˈkɒksənz/ (UK), /ˈkɑːkswɪnz/ (US)| n. |舵手们；艇长们|Plural form of coxswain.|The coxswains met to discuss race strategy.  艇长们开会讨论比赛策略。|Synonyms: helmsmen, steersmen|Antonyms: crews, rowers|noun
