disclaimed:/dɪsˈkleɪmd/
v.|否认，放弃，拒绝承认|
The company disclaimed all responsibility for the accident.  该公司否认对这起事故负有任何责任。
He disclaimed any knowledge of the stolen goods. 他否认对被盗物品有任何了解。
近义词:denied, renounced, rejected
反义词:claimed, admitted, accepted
词性:动词

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + claim (to assert ownership of).  来自dis-（表示否定）+ claim（声称拥有）。
