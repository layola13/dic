heli:/ˈhiːli/ /ˈheliː/ | n. | 直升机; 直升飞机 | A helicopter. Short for helicopter. | Example: The heli landed on the hospital roof. 直升机降落在医院屋顶上。

Example:  They took a heli tour of the Grand Canyon. 他们乘坐直升机游览了大峡谷。


近义词: helicopter, chopper, whirlybird | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a vehicle) | 词性: noun
