diegesis:/ˌdaɪəˈdʒiːsɪs/| n.|叙事世界，故事世界|The diegesis of the film includes the events that are shown and implied to have taken place in the story world.|The background music was diegetic, meaning it was part of the narrative world and the characters could hear it.|近义词：narrative, story, plot|反义词：non-diegetic|词性：名词

From Ancient Greek διήγησις (diḗgēsis, “narration, explanation”)
