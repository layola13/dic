nonabsolutist:/ˌnɒnˈæbsəluːtɪst/
| adj. |非绝对主义的|Not adhering to or believing in absolutism (in any of its forms, e.g., moral, philosophical, political).  Characterized by a rejection of absolute principles or standards. |The professor presented a nonabsolutist view of ethics, arguing that moral principles can vary depending on the context.| 近义词:relativistic, contextualist | 反义词:absolutist |词性:形容词

nonabsolutist:/ˌnɑːnˈæbsəluːtɪst/
| n. |非绝对主义者|A person who does not adhere to absolutist principles.|The group of nonabsolutists advocated for a more flexible interpretation of the law.| 近义词:relativist, contextualist | 反义词:absolutist |词性:名词
