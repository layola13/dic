carb's:/kɑːrbz/
noun
碳水化合物（carbohydrate的缩写和非正式所有格）
Carbohydrates, often shortened to "carbs," are one of the three macronutrients and a primary source of energy for the body. The 's is a less formal way to show possession, implying "something related to carbs".
Example: I'm trying to cut down on my carb's intake.  我正在努力减少碳水化合物的摄入。
Synonym: carbohydrates, sugars, starches
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but one might consider proteins or fats in a dietary context)
词性: 名词
