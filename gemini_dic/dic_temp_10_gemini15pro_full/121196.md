misdrawn:/ˌmɪsˈdrɔːn/
adjective
支取错误的；透支的；画错的
incorrectly drawn, typically referring to a check or other financial document; also, drawn incorrectly in a more general sense.
The bank returned the check marked "misdrawn." 银行退回了这张标有“支取错误”的支票。
The architect realized the plans were misdrawn and had to start over.  建筑师意识到图纸画错了，不得不重新开始。
Synonyms: incorrect, wrong, faulty, inaccurate, erroneous
Antonyms: correct, accurate, right, proper
词性: 形容词
