expensing:/ɪkˈspɛnsɪŋ/| n.|费用支出，报销|The expensing of business trips is subject to company policy.  商务旅行的费用支出需遵循公司政策。|例句：The company's expensing policy is quite strict. 公司的报销政策相当严格。|近义词：expenditure, outlay, spending|反义词:saving, income|noun

expensing:/ɪkˈspɛnsɪŋ/| v.|把...作为开支处理；列为费用|He expensed the cost of the new computer. 他把新电脑的费用列为开支。|例句：She expensed her travel costs to the company. 她向公司报销了她的旅行费用。|近义词：charge, debit|反义词:credit|verb
