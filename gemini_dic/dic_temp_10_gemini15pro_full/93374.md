illesive: /ɪˈleɪsɪv/
adjective
内向的; 不外露的; (尤指感情)隐藏的
Tending to remain within, especially of emotions; not outwardly expressed.
Her illesive nature made it difficult to know what she was truly feeling. 她内向的性格让人很难知道她真正的感受。
He gave an illesive smile, his true feelings masked. 他露出了一个隐藏内心的微笑，掩盖了他真实的情感。
Synonyms: reserved, withdrawn, introverted, reticent, undemonstrative
Antonyms: expressive, extroverted, outgoing, demonstrative
词性: adjective
