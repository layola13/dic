bridging:/ˈbrɪdʒɪŋ/
adjective
桥接的;连接的
Connecting two things or groups.
The program is aimed at bridging the gap between school and work.  这个项目旨在弥合学校和工作之间的差距。

近义词:connecting, linking, joining
反义词:separating, dividing
词性:adjective


bridging:/ˈbrɪdʒɪŋ/
noun
桥接;连接
The act of connecting two things or groups.
The bridging of the cultural divide is essential for peaceful coexistence.  弥合文化差异对于和平共处至关重要。

近义词:connection, link, junction
反义词:separation, division
词性:noun



词源: From the verb "bridge" which comes from Old English "brycg," meaning "bridge."
