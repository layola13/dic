floridnesses:/ˈflɔːrɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
华丽；绚丽；辞藻华丽
excessively ornate or elaborate language or style.
His writing is characterized by floridnesses of style. 他的写作特点是风格华丽。

The architect was known for the floridnesses of his designs.  这位建筑师以其设计的华丽而闻名。

Synonyms:  ornateness, elaborateness, flamboyance, extravagance, embellishment, ostentation
Antonyms:  simplicity, plainness, austerity, understatement, conciseness
noun
