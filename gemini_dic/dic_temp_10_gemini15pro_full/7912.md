antennule:/ænˈtɛnjuːl/ /ænˈtɛn.juːl/
n.
小触角，触角
A small antenna, especially either of the first pair of antennae in a crustacean.
The lobster uses its antennules to smell.  龙虾用它的小触角来闻气味。

antenna, feeler
(无常用反义词)
