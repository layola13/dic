leptons:/ˈlɛptɒnz/ (US) /ˈlɛptɒnz/ (UK)| n. |轻子|A fundamental particle that is not affected by the strong nuclear force, such as an electron, muon, or neutrino.  Leptons are elementary particles, meaning they are not made up of smaller components.|Electrons and muons are examples of leptons. 电子和μ子是轻子的例子。|
Leptons are crucial for understanding the universe at its most fundamental level. 轻子对于理解宇宙的最基本层面至关重要。|
Synonyms: elementary particles, fundamental particles|Antonyms: baryons, hadrons|Noun
