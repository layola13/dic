nonsubstitutions:/ˌnɑːnsʌbstɪˈtuːʃənz/ | n. | 非替代品；不可替代物 |  The act or policy of not substituting something; the state of being nonsubstitutable. It implies that something is unique and cannot be replaced by anything else. |  The museum insisted on the nonsubstitution of its original artworks for replicas.  博物馆坚持不以复制品代替其原作。| synonyms: irreplaceability, uniqueness | antonyms: replaceability, substitutability | noun

nonsubstitutions:/ˌnɑːnsʌbstɪˈtuːʃənz/ | n. | 非替代 | The state or quality of not being substitutable or replaceable.  | The nonsubstitutions of certain essential nutrients can lead to health problems. 某些必需营养素的非替代性会导致健康问题。| synonyms: indispensability, essentiality | antonyms: dispensability, substitutability | noun
