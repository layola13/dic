mumble:/ˈmʌmbəl/ (US), /ˈmʌmbl/ (UK)| v. n.|含糊地说，咕哝；咕哝，含糊的话|To speak in a low, indistinct manner, almost to oneself; a low, indistinct utterance.|He mumbled something about being late for a meeting. 他含糊地说了一句开会迟到了。|synonyms: mutter, murmur, whisper|antonyms: articulate, enunciate, shout|verb

mumble:/ˈmʌmbəl/ (US), /ˈmʌmbl/ (UK)| n.|咕哝，含糊的话|A low, indistinct utterance.|His reply was a barely audible mumble. 他的回答几乎听不清，只是含糊地咕哝了几句。|synonyms: muttering, murmur|antonyms: articulation, enunciation, shout|noun
