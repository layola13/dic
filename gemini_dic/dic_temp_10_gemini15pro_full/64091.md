eponym:/ˈɛpənɪm/ | n. | 名字的来源；同名的人或物 | A person, place, or thing after whom or after which something is named. |  The Earl of Sandwich is the eponym of the sandwich.  三明治伯爵是三明治的名字来源。| synonyms: namesake, namegiver | antonyms: None | noun

eponym:/ˈɛpənɪm/ | adj. | 同名的 | Being the eponym of something. |  The eponymous hero of the novel is a young woman searching for her identity.  这部小说的同名主人公是一位寻找自我认同的年轻女性。| synonyms: namesake, titular | antonyms: None | adjective
