floutingly:/ˈflaʊtɪŋli/| adv.|轻蔑地，藐视地，公然违抗地|In a manner that shows contempt or disregard for rules or conventions. |She floutingly ignored the dress code and wore jeans to the formal event. 她公然无视着装规定，穿着牛仔裤出席了正式活动。|He floutingly dismissed the judge's orders. 他轻蔑地驳回了法官的命令。|Synonyms: contemptuously, disdainfully, scornfully, defiantly|Antonyms: respectfully, obediently, submissively|词性:副词
