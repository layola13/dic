callower:/ˈkæləʊər/ (UK) /ˈkæləʊɚ/ (US)| n.|  茧子；老茧；硬皮|A thickened and hardened part of the skin or an analogous outer covering, especially in an area that has been subjected to repeated friction.|The guitarist had callouses on his fingertips.  吉他手的指尖上长满了老茧。|Synonyms: callus, induration|Antonyms: softness, smoothness|noun
