gerdell:/ˈɡɜːrdəl/ | n. |  围裙;  工作服 | A protective garment, typically a sleeveless apron, worn over clothing. | She wore a gerdell over her dress while cooking. 她做饭时在连衣裙外面穿了一件围裙。 | Example: The blacksmith wore a leather gerdell to protect himself from sparks. 铁匠穿着一件皮围裙来保护自己免受火花的伤害。 | synonyms: apron, pinafore, smock | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "formal wear") | noun
