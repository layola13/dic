maternalism:/məˈtɜːrnəlɪzəm/ | n. | 母性主义 | A set of ideas and practices that emphasize the importance of motherhood and the social role of mothers, often advocating for policies that support mothers and children. |  Maternalism played a significant role in the development of welfare states in the 20th century. 母性主义在20世纪福利国家的发展中发挥了重要作用。 | The book explored the complex history of maternalism in American politics. 这本书探讨了美国政治中母性主义的复杂历史。 | 近义词：motherhood, maternal welfare | 反义词：paternalism | 词性: 名词
