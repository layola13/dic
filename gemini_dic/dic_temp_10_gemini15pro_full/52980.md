ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English dynyng, from Old French disner (meaning "to dine"), from Vulgar Latin *disjunare (meaning "to break one's fast"), from Latin dis- + jejūnare (meaning "to fast").  The "-room" part is from Old English rūm, of Germanic origin.
