intercedes:/ˌɪntərˈsiːdz/| v. |调解，斡旋，代为说情|To act or plead on behalf of someone, especially in a difficult situation; to mediate.|例句：The lawyer interceded with the judge to get a lighter sentence for his client. 律师向法官求情，希望他的当事人能得到轻判。

例句：She interceded on behalf of the refugees. 她为难民说情。|近义词：mediate, intervene, plead, negotiate|反义词:ignore, disregard|词性:动词
