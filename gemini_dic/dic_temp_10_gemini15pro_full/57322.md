downtrend:/ˈdaʊntrend/| n.|下降趋势；低迷的趋势|A downward trend in sales/prices/the stock market.  销售额/价格/股票市场的下降趋势。|The housing market has been in a downtrend for several months. 房地产市场已经持续数月的低迷。|近义词：decline, decrease, slump, downturn|反义词：uptrend, increase, rise|词性:名词

Etymology:  "down-" + "trend". "Down-" indicates direction, and "trend" refers to a general direction in which something is developing or changing.
