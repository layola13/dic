dichroite:/ˈdaɪkroʊˌaɪt/| n. |二色石，堇青石|A mineral that exhibits dichroism, meaning it appears to be different colors when viewed from different directions.  It's a variety of cordierite. |The dichroite gemstone changed from blue to violet as I turned it.  这颗二色石宝石在我转动它时从蓝色变成了紫色。|Synonym: cordierite, iolite|Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym for a mineral name)|noun

dichroism:/ˈdaɪkroʊˌɪzəm/| n. |二色性|The property of exhibiting different colors when viewed from different directions, often due to the polarization of light.|The dichroism of the tourmaline crystal was striking.  碧玺晶体的二色性很引人注目。|Synonym: pleochroism (more general term)|Antonym: N/A|noun
