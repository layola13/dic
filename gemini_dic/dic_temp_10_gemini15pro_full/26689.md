caddices:/ˈkædɪsɪz/ | n. |石蚕，石蛾幼虫|The larvae of caddisflies, aquatic insects that build protective cases of stones, twigs, or other materials.  They are often used as bait in fishing. |Caddisflies build intricate cases from materials found in their environment. 石蛾用在它们的环境中发现的材料建造复杂的壳。

Caddis larvae are an important food source for fish. 石蚕幼虫是鱼类的重要食物来源。|近义词: caddis larvae, caddis worms|反义词: None|词性: Noun
