constituent:/kənˈstɪtʃuənt/ | n. | 成分; 选民; 要素 | a person represented by an elected official; a component part of something | The congressman met with his constituents to discuss their concerns. 这位国会议员会见了他的选民，讨论他们的担忧。 | synonyms: voter, elector, component, element, ingredient | antonyms: representative, whole | noun


constituents:/kənˈstɪtʃuənts/ | n. | 成分; 选民; 要素(constituent的复数形式) | the people who live and vote in an area | The constituents were pleased with their representative's work in Congress. 选民们对他们的代表在国会的工作感到满意。 | synonyms: voters, electors, components, elements, ingredients | antonyms: representatives, wholes | noun



constituent:/kənˈstɪtʃuənt/ | adj. | 构成的; 组成的; 有选举权的 | being a voting member of a community or organization; having power to elect or appoint | The constituent assembly drafted a new constitution.  立宪会议起草了一部新宪法。 | synonyms: component, essential, integral, voting | antonyms: non-constituent, inessential | adjective


constitutors:/ˈkɒnstɪˌtjuːtəz/ /ˈkɑːnstɪˌtuːtərz/ | n. |  组成者; 设立者; 制定者 (较不常用) | those who constitute or establish something, especially a law or organization | The constitutors of the new charity were a group of philanthropists.  这个新慈善机构的设立者是一群慈善家。 | synonyms: founders, establishers, creators | antonyms: destroyers, dismantlers | noun
