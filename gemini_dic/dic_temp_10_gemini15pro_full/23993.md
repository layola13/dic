breathier:/ˈbriːðɪər/| adj.|更透气的，呼吸更顺畅的|More able to breathe easily; allowing more air to pass through.|The new fabric is much breathier than the old one.  这种新面料比旧的透气多了。|Synonyms: airy, porous, permeable, ventilated|Antonyms: stuffy, airless, stifling|adjective

breathier:/ˈbriːðiər/| adj.|呼吸更急促的|Breathing more heavily or rapidly.|After running up the stairs, he was much breathier. 跑上楼梯后，他的呼吸更加急促了。|Synonyms: gasping, panting, puffing|Antonyms: calm, relaxed|adjective
