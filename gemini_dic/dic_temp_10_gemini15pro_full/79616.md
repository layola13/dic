gloryingly:/ˈɡlɔːriɪŋli/
adverb
光荣地；辉煌地
In a glorious manner; with great splendor and triumph.
The sun set gloryingly over the mountains. 太阳光荣地落山了，映照着群山。
近义词:triumphantly, splendidly, magnificently
反义词:ignominiously, shamefully, disgracefully
词性:副词

glory:/ˈɡlɔːri/
noun
光荣；荣耀；辉煌
Magnificence, splendor, or great beauty.
The glory of the Roman Empire. 罗马帝国的辉煌。
近义词:splendor, magnificence, grandeur
反义词:shame, disgrace, ignominy
词性:名词

-ing:/ɪŋ/
suffix
构成现在分词或动名词
Used to form the present participle of verbs or gerunds.
Walking is good exercise.  走路是很好的运动。
近义词:N/A
反义词:N/A
词性:后缀

词源: From "glory" + "-ing" (present participle) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin "gloria".
