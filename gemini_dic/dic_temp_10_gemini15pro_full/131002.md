nonattender:/ˌnɒnəˈtɛndər/ | n. |未出席者，缺席者|A person who does not attend something, such as a meeting, class, or event.| Many nonattenders cited illness as the reason for their absence. 许多未出席者以生病为由缺席。| Example: The nonattenders will receive a summary of the meeting minutes. 缺席者将收到会议纪要的摘要。| Synonyms: absentee, truant (especially for school) | Antonyms: attendee, participant | noun
