inoperability:/ɪnˌɒpərəˈbɪləti/
n.|不可操作性；无法运行；失效|The inoperability of the system caused significant delays. 系统无法运行导致了严重的延误。|例句：The inoperability of the lifeboat made the situation even more dangerous. 救生艇无法使用使情况更加危险。|近义词：dysfunction, malfunction, failure, unworkability|反义词：operability, functionality, workability
词性：名词

inoperative:/ɪnˈɒpərətɪv/
adj.|无效的；不工作的；不运行的|The old machinery is now inoperative. 旧机器现在不能运转了。|例句：The button was inoperative, so I couldn't call for help.  按钮坏了，所以我无法求救。|近义词：invalid, ineffective, useless, dysfunctional, broken, defunct|反义词：operative, functional, working, effective
词性：形容词
