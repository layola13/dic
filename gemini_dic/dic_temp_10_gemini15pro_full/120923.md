词源:

"mis-" (prefix):  From Old English "mis-" meaning "wrongly," "badly," or "astray."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*missa-" meaning "wrong."

"catalogue" (verb/noun): From French "catalogue," from Latin "catalogus," from Greek "katalogos" meaning "list," from "kata-" (down, completely) + "legein" (to gather, choose).
