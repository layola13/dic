matatu:/məˈtɑːtuː/
n.
小型巴士，合乘公共汽车
A privately owned minibus, typically brightly coloured and decorated, used as public transport in Kenya and other East African countries.
I took a matatu to get to the market. 我乘坐小型巴士去市场。
The matatu was crammed full of people. 这辆小型巴士挤满了人。
synonyms: minibus, van, shuttle
antonyms: private car, taxi
noun
