mawa:/ˈmɑːwɑː/| n.|玛瓦，一种东非的甜点|Mawa is a dairy product made by slowly simmering milk until it thickens and reduces in volume. It is used in a variety of Indian sweets and desserts.|The mawa cake was rich and flavorful.|近义词:khoya|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something non-sweet like "bitter gourd")|词性:noun
