orograph:/ˈɔːrəɡræf/ |n.|地形图；山岳图|A diagram or model representing the relief features of a landscape, especially mountains.|The orograph showed the steepness of the mountain range. 这张地形图显示了山脉的陡峭程度。|Synonym: relief map, topographic map|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "bathymetric chart" for ocean floor) |noun

Etymology->
From Greek *oros* (mountain) + *graphein* (to write, draw).
