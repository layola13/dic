incorrect: /ˌɪnkəˈrekt/ | adj. | 不正确的，错误的 | Not correct; wrong or inaccurate. |  The answer you gave is incorrect. 你给的答案是不正确的。

Your calculations are incorrect. 你的计算是错误的。

近义词: wrong, erroneous, inaccurate, false, faulty

反义词: correct, right, accurate, true

词性: adjective

词源:  Late Middle English: from Latin incorrectus, from in- ‘not’ + correctus (see correct).
