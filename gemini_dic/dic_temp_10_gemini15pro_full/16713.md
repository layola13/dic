barron:/ˈbærən/| n.|男爵；大亨；巨头|A person holding the title of baron, a rank of nobility.  A powerful, wealthy, and influential businessman or industrialist. |例句：The baron lived in a grand castle. 男爵住在一座宏伟的城堡里。

例句：He was a media baron, owning several newspapers and television stations. 他是一位媒体大亨，拥有几家报纸和电视台。|近义词：magnate, tycoon, lord, nobleman|反义词：commoner, peasant|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *baron*, itself of uncertain origin. Possibly from a Frankish word *baro* meaning "free man" or "warrior," or from Late Latin *baro* meaning "man, male."  Ultimately, the origins remain debated.
