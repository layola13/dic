coheirs:/ˌkōˈeərz/ | n. |共同继承人|One of two or more people who inherit property together.  |例句：The two sisters were coheirs to their father's estate.  这两姐妹是她们父亲遗产的共同继承人。

例句：John and Mary were named as coheirs in the will.  约翰和玛丽在遗嘱中被指定为共同继承人。|近义词：joint heirs, fellow heirs|反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *coheir (modern French cohéritier), from Latin cohērēs, from co- 'together' + hērēs 'heir'.
