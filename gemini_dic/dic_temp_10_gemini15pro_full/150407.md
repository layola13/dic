panpsychists:/pænˈsaɪkɪsts/ | n. | 泛心论者 | People who believe in panpsychism, the view that consciousness, mind, or soul (psyche) is a universal and primordial feature of all things. |  Panpsychists argue that consciousness is not limited to humans or animals but extends to all matter, even inanimate objects. | 近义词:  (no direct synonyms, but related terms include) idealists,  | 反义词: materialists, physicalists | noun
