believer:/bɪˈliːvər/| n.|信徒；相信者|Someone who believes in a particular religion, system of ideas, or theory.  A person who has faith in the truth of something.|例句：She's a firm believer in alternative medicine. 她坚信替代医学。|例句：He's a true believer in the power of positive thinking. 他是积极思考力量的真正信徒。|近义词：adherent, devotee, disciple, follower|反义词：skeptic, doubter, cynic, nonbeliever|词性:noun
