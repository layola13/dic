mariculture: /ˈmærɪˌkʌltʃər/ | n. | 海水养殖；海产养殖 | The cultivation of marine organisms in their natural habitats, usually for commercial purposes.  | Mariculture is becoming increasingly important as a source of seafood. 海水养殖作为海产品的一个来源正变得越来越重要。 |  Mariculture farms can provide a sustainable source of protein. 海水养殖场可以提供可持续的蛋白质来源。 | 近义词: aquaculture, fish farming | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps wild fishing or hunting) | 词性: noun


Etymology:  From Latin "mare" (sea) + "cultura" (cultivation).
