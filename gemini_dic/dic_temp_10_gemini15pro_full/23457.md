braggadocian: /ˌbræɡəˈdoʊʃən/ | n. | 吹牛大王，自夸者 | A person who boasts about achievements or qualities, especially when these are exaggerated or untrue. |  He was a braggadocian, always telling tales of his daring exploits. 他是个吹牛大王，总是吹嘘自己大胆的功绩。

The braggadocian boasted of his wealth and connections. 这个自夸者吹嘘他的财富和人脉。


| 近义词: boaster, bragger, show-off | 反义词: humble person, modest person | 词性: noun

braggadocian: /ˌbræɡəˈdoʊʃən/ | adj. | 吹牛的，自夸的 |  Boastful or arrogant. | His braggadocian manner annoyed many people. 他自夸的举止惹恼了很多人。

She delivered a braggadocian speech about her accomplishments. 她发表了一篇自夸其成就的演讲。

| 近义词: boastful, arrogant, swaggering | 反义词: humble, modest, unassuming | 词性: adjective
