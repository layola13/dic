delegatory:/ˈdɛlɪɡətɔːri/ /ˈdɛlɪɡətəri/
delegatory| adj.|委托的；代表的|Relating to or involving delegation of power or authority.|The CEO made a delegatory decision, empowering the vice president to handle the negotiations.|近义词：representative, authorized, commissioned|反义词:retained, personal, direct|词性:形容词
delegatory| n.|受托者；代表|A person to whom authority is delegated.|He acted as a delegatory for the shareholders.|近义词：delegate, representative, agent|反义词:principal|词性:名词
