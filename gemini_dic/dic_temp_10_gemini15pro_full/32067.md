cherubim: /ˈtʃɛrəbɪm/ | n. | 智天使; 基路伯 | Angelic beings of high rank, often depicted as winged babies or childlike figures. They are associated with God's presence and glory. | The cherubim guarded the entrance to the Garden of Eden. 智天使守卫着伊甸园的入口。|  The artist painted cherubim on the ceiling of the chapel. 艺术家在教堂的天花板上画了智天使。 | Near synonyms: angel, seraph | Antonyms: demon, devil | Noun
