cystine:/ˈsɪstaɪn/ /ˈsɪstiːn/| n. |胱氨酸|A crystalline amino acid C6H12N2O4S2 formed by the oxidation of cysteine and found in keratin and other proteins.  It is a dimer of cysteine, formed by the oxidation of two cysteine molecules that covalently link via a disulfide bond.|Cystine stones are a relatively rare type of kidney stone. 胱氨酸结石是一种相对罕见的肾结石。|Cystine is important for the formation of strong hair and nails. 胱氨酸对强韧的头发和指甲的形成很重要。|近义词: (As a component of protein, no direct synonym.  Functionally related: keratin)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
