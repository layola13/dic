establishing: /ɪˈstæblɪʃɪŋ/ | v. | 建立，设立；确定；证实 |  The company is establishing a new branch in Tokyo.  该公司正在东京设立新的分公司。| 近义词：founding, creating, setting up, proving | 反义词：abolishing, destroying, dismantling | 词性: 动名词 (present participle)

词源: From Middle English *establissen, from Anglo-Norman establir, establiss-, present stem of establir, from Old French establir, from Latin stabilire (“to make stable”), from stabilis (“stable”)

用法说明:
"Establishing" is the present participle of the verb "establish." It can be used:

1. As part of a continuous verb tense:
    They are establishing a new research center. 他们正在建立一个新的研究中心。
    He was establishing his business when the pandemic hit. 当疫情爆发时，他正在建立自己的事业。

2. As an adjective (attributive):
    The establishing members of the group were honored at the ceremony.  该组织的创始成员在仪式上受到了表彰。

3. As a gerund (noun):
    Establishing a good reputation takes time and effort.  建立良好的声誉需要时间和精力。
