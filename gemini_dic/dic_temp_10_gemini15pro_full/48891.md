defraud:/dɪˈfrɔːd/
verb
欺骗，诈骗
To illegally obtain money from someone by deception.
The company defrauded investors of millions of dollars.  这家公司诈骗了投资者数百万美元。
He was found guilty of defrauding the government. 他被判犯有诈骗政府罪。
synonyms: cheat, swindle, deceive, trick
antonyms: compensate, repay, reimburse
词性: 动词
