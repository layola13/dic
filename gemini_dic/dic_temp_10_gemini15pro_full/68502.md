famulist: /ˈfæmjuːlɪst/
noun
家谱学家，家谱编纂者
A person who studies or compiles genealogies.
John is a famulist, he has traced his family back to the 17th century.  约翰是一位家谱学家，他追溯了他的家族史到17世纪。
Synonyms: genealogist
Antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词
