inconsonant:/ɪnˈkɑːnsənənt/ | adj. |不协调的，不一致的，不和谐的 | Not in agreement or harmony; discordant.  Characterized by a lack of harmony or compatibility. | Her lifestyle is inconsonant with her professed values. 她的生活方式与她所宣称的价值观不一致。| Synonyms: discordant, incompatible, incongruous, conflicting, jarring, clashing | Antonyms: consonant, harmonious, consistent, compatible, agreeable | adjective

inconsonant:/ɪnˈkɑːnsənənt/ | n. |不和谐音，不一致的事物 | Something that is not in agreement or harmony. A discordant element.  |  His sudden outburst was an inconsonant in the otherwise peaceful meeting. 他突然的爆发是原本和平会议中的一个不和谐音。| Synonyms: discordance, incongruity, inconsistency | Antonyms: consonance, harmony, consistency | noun
