conirostres:/kɒˈnɪrɒstriːz/ /kɑˈnɪrɑˌstrɪz/| n. |鸟类：锥嘴雀 |A suborder of passerine birds characterized by a cone-shaped bill, including finches, sparrows, and grosbeaks.|The conirostres are a diverse group of birds. 锥嘴雀是一个种类繁多的鸟类群体。|Conirostres such as the crossbill have specialized beaks for eating seeds.  像交嘴雀这样的锥嘴雀有专门用来吃种子的喙。|近义词：passerines, songbirds|反义词: (None - it's a taxonomic classification)|词性:Noun
