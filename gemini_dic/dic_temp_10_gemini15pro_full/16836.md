bashful:/ˈbæʃfʊl/ (US), /ˈbæʃf(ə)l/ (UK)| adj.|害羞的，腼腆的|Reluctant to draw attention to oneself; shy.  Often implies a sweet or endearing shyness.|例句：The bashful child hid behind her mother's skirt.  害羞的孩子躲在她妈妈的裙子后面。|近义词：shy, timid, coy, reserved, diffident|反义词：bold, confident, outgoing, extroverted|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *baschful*, from *baschen* (“to be ashamed, abashed”) + *-ful*.  *Baschen* is from Old French *esbahir* (“to amaze, astound”), from *es-* (out) + *bahir* (“to gape, open wide”).  Ultimately from a Germanic source.
