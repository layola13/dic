nonphilological:/ˌnɒnfɪləˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
非语文学的
Not pertaining to philology; not connected with the study of language and literature.
His approach to the text was surprisingly nonphilological, focusing more on the historical context than the linguistic nuances.  他的研究文本的方法令人惊讶地是非语文学的，更多地关注历史背景而不是语言上的细微差别。
The museum's exhibit took a nonphilological approach to ancient writings, exploring their materiality and use in daily life.  博物馆的展览采用了一种非语文学的方法来研究古代著作，探索它们的物质性和在日常生活中的用途。
近义词:nonlinguistic
反义词:philological
词性:adjective
