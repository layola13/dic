incapacitating:/ˌɪnkəˈpæsɪteɪtɪŋ/
adjective
使无能力的；使丧失能力的
Making someone unable to work or do things normally.
The injury was incapacitating, preventing him from walking for months.  这伤让他几个月都不能走路，使他丧失了行动能力。
He suffered an incapacitating back injury. 他背部受伤，丧失了行动能力。

近义词: disabling, crippling, debilitating
反义词: enabling, empowering
词性: adjective
