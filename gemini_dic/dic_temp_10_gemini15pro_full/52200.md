diastasic:/ˌdaɪəˈsteɪsɪk/ | adj. |  淀粉酶的；促淀粉酶的 | Relating to or produced by diastase, an enzyme that catalyzes the hydrolysis of starch into maltose. | Diastasic activity is crucial in the brewing process. 淀粉酶活性在酿造过程中至关重要。 | 近义词: amylolytic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider "inhibiting starch hydrolysis") | 词性: adjective
