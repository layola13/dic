bumblingly:/ˈbʌmblɪŋli/
adverb
笨手笨脚地；踉踉跄跄地
in a clumsy, awkward manner.
He bumblingly tried to explain the complex situation. 他笨手笨脚地试图解释这复杂的局面。
clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly
gracefully, deftly, skillfully
词性: 副词

bumbling:/ˈbʌmblɪŋ/
adjective
笨手笨脚的；装腔作势的
clumsy and awkward in movement or behavior.
The bumbling waiter spilled the wine.  笨手笨脚的服务员把酒洒了。
clumsy, awkward, inept
graceful, deftly, skillful
词性: 形容词

bumble:/ˈbʌmbəl/
verb
踉跄；笨拙地行动；结结巴巴地说
move or speak in a clumsy, awkward way.
He bumbled around in the dark, looking for the light switch. 他在黑暗中摸索着，寻找电灯开关。
stumble, blunder, falter
glide, sail, flow
词性: 动词

词源:  From Middle English *bomblem, frequentative of bomben (“to boom, hum, buzz”).  Ultimately of imitative origin.
