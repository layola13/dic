deprecated:/ˈdɛprəkeɪtɪd/
adjective
已弃用的，不赞成的
Discouraged from further use, typically because it has been superseded.  Something deprecated is still functional but is considered outdated and likely to be removed in the future.
This function is deprecated and will be removed in the next version.  这个函数已被弃用，并将在下一版本中移除。
近义词:obsolete, outdated, superseded
反义词:current, supported, recommended
词性:adjective

deprecated:/ˈdɛprəkeɪtɪd/
verb
不赞成，反对
Express disapproval of.
The committee deprecated the use of violence. 委员会不赞成使用暴力。
近义词:disapprove of, deplore, condemn
反义词:approve of, endorse, support
词性:verb
