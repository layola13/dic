auricularly:/ɔːˈrɪkjʊləli/
adv.
用耳朵地，耳语地，私下地
In a way that relates to the ear or hearing; by means of the ear; privately, secretly.
The doctor examined the patient auricularly. 医生用耳朵检查了病人。
The secret was passed auricularly from one person to the next.  这个秘密私下里从一个人传到另一个人。
近义词:privately, secretly, in a whisper, in confidence
反义词:publicly, openly
词性:副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *auriculariter, from Latin auricula ("ear, earlap") + -iter (adverbial suffix).
