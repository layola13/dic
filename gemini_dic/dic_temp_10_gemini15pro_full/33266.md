chrysaurin:/kraɪˈsɔːrɪn/| n. |金黄色素，橙黄色素，金莲橙|A golden-yellow or orange-yellow pigment derived from certain plants, notably the marigold (Calendula officinalis).|The chrysaurin extracted from marigolds was used to dye the fabric a vibrant orange. 从万寿菊中提取的金黄色素被用来给织物染上鲜艳的橙色。|Chrysaurin gave the artist's paintings a warm, sunny glow. 金莲橙赋予了艺术家的画作一种温暖、阳光的光芒。|近义词：xanthophyll, carotenoid|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
