glyconin:/ˈɡlaɪkənɪn/| n. |大豆球蛋白|The major storage protein found in soybeans, composed of various subunits. It plays a significant role in the nutritional value of soybeans and soy-derived products.|Soy milk contains glyconin, which contributes to its creamy texture. 豆浆中含有大豆球蛋白，这有助于其乳脂状的质地。|Synonyms: soy protein isolate, soy protein concentrate|Antonyms: None|noun
