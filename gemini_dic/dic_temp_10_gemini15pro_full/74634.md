free-and-easy:/ˌfriː ən ˈiːzi/ | adj. | 自由自在的，轻松愉快的，不拘礼节的 | Relaxed and informal; unconcerned about rules or conventions. |  He had a free-and-easy manner that made him popular. 他自由自在的态度使他很受欢迎。|  She strolled into the meeting with a free-and-easy air. 她带着轻松愉快的姿态走进会议室。 | 近义词: relaxed, informal, casual, laid-back, easygoing | 反义词: formal, stiff, uptight, tense | 词性: 形容词

词源:  This compound adjective is formed from the combination of "free" (meaning unrestrained) and "easy" (meaning relaxed and comfortable).
