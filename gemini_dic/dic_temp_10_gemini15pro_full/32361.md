childproof:/ˈtʃaɪldˌpruːf/| adj.|防儿童的，防止儿童开启的|Designed to prevent children from opening or using something potentially dangerous.  Designed so that young children cannot open or use it easily.|例句：The medicine bottle has a childproof cap.  药瓶有一个防儿童开启的盖子。

例句：We installed childproof latches on all the cupboards. 我们在所有的橱柜上都安装了防儿童锁。|近义词：child-resistant, tamper-proof, safety|反义词: accessible, easy to open|词性: 形容词
