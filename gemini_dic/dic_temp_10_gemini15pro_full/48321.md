decretorial:/ˌdɛkrɪˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
法令的，教令的
Relating to, or having the nature of, a decree or decretal.
The decretorial  nature of the papal bull caused much controversy.  教皇诏书的法令性质引起了很多争议。

The king issued a decretorial statement outlining new tax laws. 国王发布了一项法令声明，概述了新的税法。
synonym: authoritative, canonical, ordained
antonym: advisory, suggested, optional
word type: adjective
