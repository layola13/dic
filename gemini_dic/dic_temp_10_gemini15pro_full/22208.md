bonaci:/boʊˈnɑːtʃi/ | n. | 斐波那契数列的一种变体 | A generalization of the Fibonacci sequence where each term is the sum of the preceding n terms. | Example: The Tribonacci sequence (a type of Bonacci sequence) is formed by adding the previous three numbers: 1, 1, 2, 4, 7, 13, ... | 近义词: sequence, series | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a mathematical sequence) | 词性: noun
