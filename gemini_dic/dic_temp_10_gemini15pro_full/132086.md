noncredibly:/nɒnˈkrɛdəbli/
adverb
不可信地
in a way that is not credible; unconvincingly.
His claims were noncredibly presented, lacking evidence and logic. 他提出的说法缺乏证据和逻辑，不可信。
The witness noncredibly recounted the events of that night, contradicting herself multiple times.  证人对那天晚上的事件的描述前后矛盾，令人难以置信。
Synonyms: unconvincingly, implausibly, doubtfully
Antonyms: credibly, convincingly, believably
词性: 副词
