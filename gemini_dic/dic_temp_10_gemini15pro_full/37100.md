combining:/kəmˈbaɪnɪŋ/
combining |adj.| 结合的，组合的 |The combining of different elements created a unique alloy.| 不同元素的结合创造了一种独特的合金。|
combining |n.| 结合，组合 |The combining of the two companies resulted in a larger market share.| 两家公司的合并带来了更大的市场份额。|
combining |v.| 结合，组合 (present participle of combine) |They are combining their resources to complete the project.| 他们正在整合资源以完成这个项目。|
近义词: merging, uniting, integrating, blending, fusing
反义词: separating, dividing, splitting, disconnecting
词性: adjective, noun, verb (present participle)
词源: From the verb "combine," which comes from the Old French "combiner," ultimately from the Latin "combinare," meaning "to unite two by two."
