passed-on:/ˌpæst ˈɒn/ | adj. | 传下去的，过时的 | Something that is passed-on has been given or taught to someone by someone older.  It can also mean outdated or no longer in fashion. |  My grandmother passed on her love of baking to me. 我的祖母把她对烘焙的热爱传给了我。 |  Her passed-on clothes were a bit too old-fashioned for me. 她传给我的衣服对我来说有点过时了。| inherited, hand-me-down | modern, contemporary | adjective

passed-on:/ˌpæst ˈɒn/ | v. |  去世，传递 | To die; to transfer something to someone else. |  Sadly, my grandfather passed on last year.  很遗憾，我的祖父去年去世了。 | die, expire, perish, decease | survive, live | verb
