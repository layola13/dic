lithest:/ˈlaɪðɪst/| adj.|最轻盈的，最柔软的，最敏捷的|Most supple; most flexible and graceful.|例句：She was the lithest dancer in the troupe.  她是剧团里最轻盈的舞者。|例句：The gymnast's lithest movements were mesmerizing. 体操运动员轻盈的动作令人着迷。|近义词：supple, flexible, agile, nimble, graceful|反义词：stiff, rigid, clumsy, awkward|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *lith, from Old English līðe (“gentle, pleasant, mild, soft, agreeable”), from Proto-Germanic *linþją (“soft, yielding”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)lendʰ- (“flexible, thin”).  The superlative form -est was added later.
