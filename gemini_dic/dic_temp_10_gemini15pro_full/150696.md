paraa: /ˈpɑːrɑː/ (IPA pronunciation may vary depending on dialect; this is an approximation) | n. | paragraph | A distinct section of a piece of writing, usually dealing with a single theme and indicated by a new line, indentation, or numbering. | Example: The first paraa of the essay introduces the main topic.  这篇文章的第一段介绍了主题。

Example: Please read the next paraa aloud. 请大声朗读下一段。
