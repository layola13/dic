explicator:/ˈɛksplɪˌkeɪtər/| n. | 解释者，阐释者 | Someone who explains something, especially a text or work of art, in detail. | The literary critic acted as an explicator of the novel's complex themes.  这位文学评论家对这部小说复杂的主题进行了详细的阐释。| synonym: interpreter, analyst, commentator | antonym: obscurer, confuser | noun

explicate:/ˈɛksplɪˌkeɪt/| v. | 解释，阐明 | To explain something, especially a text or work of art, in detail. | The professor explicated the poem line by line. 教授逐行解释了这首诗。| synonym: explain, clarify, interpret | antonym: confuse, obscure | verb
