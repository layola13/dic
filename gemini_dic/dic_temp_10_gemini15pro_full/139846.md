octoroon:/ˌɒktəˈruːn/ | n. | 八分之一黑人血统的人（现已弃用，带有冒犯意味） | A person who has one Black grandparent and seven white grandparents. This term is now considered offensive due to its historical association with slavery and racial classification. |  This term appeared in novels and plays of the 19th century, referring to individuals of mixed race.  这个词出现在19世纪的小说和戏剧中，指的是混血人种。| 近义词:mulatto (also offensive) | 反义词: N/A | noun
