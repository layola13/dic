concretize:/ˈkɒŋkrɪtaɪz/ /ˈkɑːŋkrɪtaɪz/| v. |使具体化；使凝固|To make something concrete or definite; to give form to an idea or plan.|例句：The architect concretized his vision by creating a detailed model.  建筑师通过创建一个详细的模型将他的愿景具体化。

例句：We need to concretize our marketing strategy with specific actions. 我们需要用具体的行动来使我们的营销策略具体化。|近义词：solidify, materialize, realize, specify, define|反义词：abstract, generalize|词性：动词
