hydraulically:/haɪˈdrɔːlɪkli/
adv.
液压地，用水力地
Operated or affected by the action of water or other liquid under pressure.
The gate is hydraulically operated.  闸门是液压操作的。
Synonyms:  by hydraulics, with hydraulic power
Antonyms: manually, mechanically
词性: 副词
