ephemeralnesses: /ɪˈfɛmərəlnəsɪz/
n.
短暂性，转瞬即逝
The quality or state of being ephemeral; transience.
The ephemeralnesses of youth are often romanticized.  青春的短暂常常被浪漫化。
Near Synonyms: transience, brevity, impermanence, fleetingness
Antonyms: permanence, eternity, durability
Noun


ephemeral: /ɪˈfɛmərəl/
adj.
短暂的，转瞬即逝的
Lasting for a very short time.
Mayflies are ephemeral creatures, living only for a day.  蜉蝣是短暂的生物，只活一天。
Near Synonyms: transient, fleeting, short-lived, temporary
Antonyms: permanent, enduring, lasting, perpetual
Adjective
