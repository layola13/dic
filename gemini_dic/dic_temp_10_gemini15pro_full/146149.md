overengineered:/ˌoʊvərˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrd/
adjective
过度设计的
Designed with more complex features or a higher level of performance than is necessary or practical.
This new phone is totally overengineered – it has features I’ll never use. 这款新手机完全是过度设计的——它有很多我永远不会用到的功能。
The bridge was so overengineered that it could withstand an earthquake twice as strong as any recorded in the area. 这座桥的过度设计使其能够承受比该地区任何记录都强两倍的地震。
Synonyms: overdesigned, overbuilt, over-elaborate
Antonyms: simple, basic, minimalist
词性: 形容词
