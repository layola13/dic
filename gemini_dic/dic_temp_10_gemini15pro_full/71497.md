flange:/flændʒ/| n.|凸缘；法兰；轮缘|A projecting flat rim, collar, or rib on an object, serving to strengthen or attach it to something else.|The pipe has a flange at each end.  管道两端都有法兰。|synonyms: rim, edge, lip, collar|antonyms: center, core|noun

flange:/flændʒ/| v.|给…装凸缘；给…镶边|To provide with a flange.|The pipes were flanged together. 管道用法兰连接在一起。|synonyms: rim, edge, border|antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the verb form.  Consider "smooth" or "unrimmed" for the resulting state)|verb
