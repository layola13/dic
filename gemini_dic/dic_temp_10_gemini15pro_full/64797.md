esophageal:/ɪˌsɒfəˈdʒiːəl/ /ɪˌsɑːfəˈdʒiːəl/| adj.|食道的；食管的|Relating to the esophagus, the tube that connects the throat to the stomach.|The patient complained of esophageal pain. 病人抱怨食道疼痛。|Synonyms: oesophageal|Antonyms: None|adjective

esophagus:/ɪˈsɒfəɡəs/ /ɪˈsɑːfəɡəs/| n.|食道；食管|The muscular tube that connects the throat to the stomach.|Food passes from the mouth, down the esophagus, and into the stomach. 食物从口腔通过食道进入胃。|Synonyms: oesophagus, gullet|Antonyms: None|noun
