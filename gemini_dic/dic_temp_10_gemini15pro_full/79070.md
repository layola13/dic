gizzard:/ˈɡɪzərd/| n.| 砂囊，胗|A thick-walled part of a bird's stomach that grinds food, often with the aid of ingested stones.|Birds use their gizzards to grind up seeds and other hard foods. 鸟类用它们的砂囊磨碎种子和其他坚硬的食物。|Synonyms: crop, maw|Antonyms: None|Noun

gizzard:/ˈɡɪzərd/| n.| (非正式)勇气，决心|Informal term for courage or determination.|He doesn't have the gizzard to stand up to the boss. 他没有勇气反抗老板。|Synonyms: guts, nerve, grit|Antonyms: cowardice, timidity|Noun
