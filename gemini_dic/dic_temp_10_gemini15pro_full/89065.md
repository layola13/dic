holophrastic:/ˌhɒləˈfræstɪk/| adj.|单词句的，单音节词的|Expressing a complex of ideas in a single word; pertaining to the use of single words to express complex ideas.|The child's utterance of "milk" could be holophrastic, meaning they want milk, are asking for milk, or are commenting on the presence of milk.|近义词：one-word, single-word|反义词:multi-word|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek *hólos* (whole) + *phrastikós* (phrasal)
