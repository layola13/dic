humility:/hjuːˈmɪləti/ /hjuːˈmɪlɪti/| n.|谦逊，谦虚|The quality of having a modest or low view of one's importance.|He spoke with great humility about his role in the project. 他非常谦虚地谈论了他在项目中的作用。|近义词：modesty, humbleness|反义词：arrogance, pride|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *humilite*, from Old French *humilité*, from Latin *humilitās* (“lowness, littleness, insignificance, meanness, baseness”), from *humilis* (“low, lowly, humble, insignificant, base”).
