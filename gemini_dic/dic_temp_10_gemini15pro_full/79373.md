glib:/ɡlɪb/| adj.|油嘴滑舌的，流畅的，但不真诚的|marked by ease and fluency in speaking or writing often to the point of being insincere or superficial|He's a glib salesman, so be careful not to be taken in by his smooth talk. 他是个油嘴滑舌的推销员，所以小心别被他的花言巧语骗了。|Synonyms: articulate, fluent, smooth-talking, voluble|Antonyms: inarticulate, hesitant, sincere, earnest|Adjective
