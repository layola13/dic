newsflashes:/ˈnjuːzˌflæʃɪz/ | n. | 新闻快讯 | Short reports of important events that interrupt other programs, especially on television or radio. | Example: We interrupt this program for a newsflash. 我们中断此次节目播报一条新闻快讯。 | Example: The newsflashes about the earthquake were coming in thick and fast. 关于地震的新闻快讯接连不断地涌入。 | Synonym: bulletins, updates, alerts | Antonym: analysis, commentary | noun
