changer:/ˈtʃeɪndʒər/| n.| 变换器; 换钱机; 交换器; 更换者|A device or person that changes something. |The money changer gave me a good rate.  换钱机给了我一个很好的汇率。|Synonyms: converter, transformer, modifier, exchanger |Antonyms: stabilizer, maintainer|noun

Etymology: From Old French *changier (Modern French changer), from Late Latin *cambiare, from Latin cambiare "to barter, exchange," related to cambire "to bend, turn."
