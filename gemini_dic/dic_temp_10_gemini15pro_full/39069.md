conformist:/kənˈfɔːrmɪst/| n. adj.|墨守成规的人，循规蹈矩者；顺从的，墨守成规的|A person who conforms to accepted behavior or established practices, especially a person who uncritically accepts the prevailing social customs, attitudes, etc.|例句：He was a conformist, always following the rules. 他是一个墨守成规的人，总是遵守规则。|近义词：follower, traditionalist, conventionalist|反义词：rebel, nonconformist, maverick|词性:名词, 形容词
conformist:/kənˈfɔːrmɪst/| adj.|顺从的，墨守成规的|Inclined to conform to accepted behavior or established practices.|例句：She had a conformist attitude, never challenging authority. 她有一种墨守成规的态度，从不挑战权威。|近义词：obedient, compliant, docile|反义词：rebellious, defiant, individualistic|词性:形容词
