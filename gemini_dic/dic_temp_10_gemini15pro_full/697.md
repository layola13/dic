abseiled: /əbˈseɪld/ /æbˈseɪld/
verb
绳降，绕绳下降
descended by using a doubled rope coiled around the body and fixed at a higher point.
The climbers abseiled down the cliff face. = 登山者从悬崖面上绕绳下降。

The rescuers abseiled into the ravine to reach the injured hiker. =  救援人员绕绳下降到峡谷中去救助受伤的徒步旅行者。

近义词: rappelled, descended, lowered
反义词: ascended, climbed
词性: verb
