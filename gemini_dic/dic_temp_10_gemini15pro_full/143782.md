outmouths:/ˌaʊtˈmaʊðz/
|v.|胜过…的口才；说得比…好|To speak more effectively or persuasively than someone else; to outdo someone in speaking.|He outmouthed the other debaters and won the competition. 他在辩论中胜过其他辩手，赢得了比赛。|近义词: outspeak, outargue|反义词: be outdone, be silenced|词性: 动词

outmouths:/ˌaʊtˈmaʊðz/
|n.|比…口才好的人|A person who speaks more effectively or persuasively than another.|He's a real outmouths; no one can beat him in an argument. 他真是个口才好的人；没人能在辩论中打败他。|近义词: orator, rhetorician|反义词: poor speaker|词性: 名词
