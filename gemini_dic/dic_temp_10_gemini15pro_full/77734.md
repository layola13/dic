gemstones:/ˈdʒɛmˌstoʊnz/
n.
宝石；珍贵宝石
A precious or semiprecious stone, especially one cut, polished, and used in a piece of jewelry.
She inherited a collection of rare gemstones from her grandmother. 她从祖母那里继承了一批稀有的宝石。
synonyms: jewels, gems, precious stones
antonyms: rocks, pebbles
noun
