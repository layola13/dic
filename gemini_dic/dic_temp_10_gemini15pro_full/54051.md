disdainful:/dɪsˈdeɪnfʊl/ | adj. | 轻蔑的，鄙夷的 | Showing contempt or lack of respect. | She gave him a disdainful look. 她轻蔑地看了他一眼。|  He spoke in a disdainful tone about his rivals. 他用轻蔑的语气谈论他的对手们。| 近义词: contemptuous, scornful, haughty, arrogant | 反义词: respectful, admiring, appreciative | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Old French desdeignos, present participle of desdeignier "to disdain," from des- "away" + deignier "to deign."
