nonintuitive:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtjuːɪtɪv/ /ˌnɑːnɪnˈtuːɪtɪv/| adj.|不直观的，非直觉的|Not easily understood or operated by intuition; not readily apparent.|The software's interface is nonintuitive and difficult to use.  这个软件的界面不直观，难以使用。|The solution to the puzzle was nonintuitive, requiring several steps of logical deduction. 这个谜题的答案并不直观，需要几个步骤的逻辑推理。|近义词：unintuitive, counterintuitive, illogical, unclear, complex|反义词：intuitive, obvious, clear, simple, easy to understand|词性:adjective
