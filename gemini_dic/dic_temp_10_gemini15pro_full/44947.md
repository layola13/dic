cryptomat:/ˈkrɪptoʊmæt/ | n. | 加密货币ATM，加密货币自动柜员机 | A machine that allows people to buy or sell cryptocurrencies, such as Bitcoin, for cash.  It functions similarly to a traditional ATM but deals with digital currencies instead of fiat money. |  I used a cryptomat to buy some Bitcoin. 我用加密货币ATM买了一些比特币。

|  The cryptomat charged a high fee for the transaction. 这台加密货币ATM交易手续费很高。 | Synonyms: Bitcoin ATM, cryptocurrency kiosk | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps traditional ATM or bank teller) | noun
