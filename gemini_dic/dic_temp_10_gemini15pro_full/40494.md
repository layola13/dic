/kənˈtraɪtɪst/ | adj. | 悔悟的，痛悔的 (Rare usage - usually applied to a person, not a debate) | Showing or feeling remorse; penitent.  | He offered a contrite apology for his hurtful words. 他对自己的伤人言论表示了悔恨的歉意。| Synonyms: remorseful, penitent, repentant, apologetic | Antonyms: unrepentant, impenitent | adjective
