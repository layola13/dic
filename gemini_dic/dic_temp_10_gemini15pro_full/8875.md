antifoaming:/ˌæntiˈfoʊmɪŋ/ | adj. n.| 抗泡沫的，消泡的；抗泡剂 |Preventing or reducing the formation of foam. Also, a substance used for this purpose. |The chef added an antifoaming agent to the simmering sauce.  厨师在慢炖的酱汁中加入了消泡剂。|Synonyms: defoaming, antifoam| Antonyms: foaming, frothing|adjective, noun

antifoam:/ˈæntiˌfoʊm/ | n.| 抗泡剂 |A substance used to prevent or reduce the formation of foam. |This antifoam is effective in a wide range of industrial applications. 这种抗泡剂在各种工业应用中都很有效。|Synonyms: defoamer, antifoaming agent| Antonyms: foaming agent, frothing agent|noun
