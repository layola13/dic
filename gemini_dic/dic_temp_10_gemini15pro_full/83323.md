gypseian:/dʒɪpˈsiːən/ | adj. n.|吉普赛的；吉普赛人|Of or relating to Gypsies or their way of life; a Gypsy.  A nomadic or free-spirited person.|例句：She had a gypseian spirit, always yearning to travel. 她有一种吉普赛人的精神，总是渴望旅行。

例句：He lived a gypseian lifestyle, moving from place to place. 他过着吉普赛式的生活，四处漂泊。|近义词：nomadic, Romani, Romany, bohemian|反义词:settled, sedentary|词性:adjective, noun


Etymology:
From Gypsy + -an.  "Gypsy" itself is a corruption of "Egyptian," reflecting a mistaken belief about the Romany people's origins.
