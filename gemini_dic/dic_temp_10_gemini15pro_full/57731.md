dreadnought:/ˈdrɛdnɔːt/| n.|无畏舰，巨舰；大胆的人|A type of battleship introduced in the early 20th century, larger and more heavily armed than any previously built. Also, a person who fears nothing.|The HMS Dreadnought revolutionized naval warfare.  无畏号战舰彻底改变了海战。|Synonym: battleship, warship, intrepid person|Antonym: coward, weakling|noun
dreadnought:/ˈdrɛdnɔːt/| adj.|无所畏惧的，大胆的|Fearless; intrepid.|He had a dreadnought spirit and faced every challenge head-on.  他有一种无畏的精神，迎难而上。|Synonym: fearless, intrepid, undaunted|Antonym: fearful, timid, apprehensive|adjective
