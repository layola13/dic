anagram:/ˈænəɡræm/| n.|字谜，变形词|A word or phrase formed by rearranging the letters of another word or phrase.|例句：The word "listen" is an anagram of "silent".  单词“listen”是“silent”的变形词。

例句："cinema" is an anagram of "iceman". "cinema"是"iceman"的变形词。|近义词：transposition, permutation|反义词:|名词
