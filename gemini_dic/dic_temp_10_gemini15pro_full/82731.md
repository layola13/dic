guides:/ɡaɪdz/| n. v.|指导，指南，向导|A person who leads or directs other people on a journey, or in a particular activity.  A book, pamphlet, or other publication that gives information on a subject or explains how to do something. To lead or direct someone or something.|例句：The guide led us through the forest. 向导带领我们穿过森林。 The museum offers guided tours. 博物馆提供导游服务。|近义词：leaders, instructors, mentors, directs, steers, pilots|反义词：followers, misleads|词性:名词,动词

词源: From Old French "guider" (to guide, conduct, lead, direct), from Frankish *witan (to show the way), from Proto-Germanic *witanan (to know).
