follow suit:/ˈfɒləʊ suːt/ (UK) /ˈfɑːloʊ suːt/ (US)| v.| 跟牌; 仿效; 跟着做|To do the same thing as someone else has just done.| When one of the companies lowered its prices, the others quickly followed suit. 当其中一家公司降价时，其他公司也迅速跟进。|Example: He took a large bite of his steak and I followed suit. 他大口地吃着牛排，我也跟着照做。

Example: When one airline reduces its ticket prices, the rest usually follow suit. 当一家航空公司降低票价时，其他航空公司通常也会跟进。|Synonyms: emulate, imitate, copy, follow, mimic|Antonyms: lead, innovate, pioneer|verb
