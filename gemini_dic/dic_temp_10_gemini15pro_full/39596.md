consecratory:/ˈkɒnsɪkreɪtəri/ /ˈkɑːnsɪkreɪtɔːri/| adj.|奉献的；祝圣的；使神圣的|Making or declaring something sacred; dedicating to a religious purpose.|The bishop led the consecratory service for the new church. 主教主持了新教堂的祝圣仪式。|近义词：sacred, holy, dedicatory, sanctifying|反义词：profane, secular|词性: 形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *consecratorius, from Latin consecrat-, past participle stem of consecrare "to dedicate, hallow," from com- "together" + sacrare "to make sacred," from sacer "sacred."
