animatronic:/ˌænɪməˈtrɒnɪk/ (US) /ˌænɪməˈtrɒnɪk/ (UK)| adj. n.|电子动画的，机械模型的；电子动画装置|A device or puppet, often resembling a human or animal, that is made to move by electronic or mechanical means, typically for use in films or other entertainment.|The animatronic dinosaurs in the museum were incredibly realistic. 博物馆里的电子动画恐龙非常逼真。|近义词：robotic, automated|反义词：inanimate, still|词性: adjective, noun
