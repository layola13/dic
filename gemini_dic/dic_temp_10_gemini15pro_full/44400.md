cross-handed:/ˌkrɒsˈhændɪd/ /ˌkrɔːsˈhændɪd/| adj.| 交叉手的，反手的|With hands crossed or clasped; with the left hand on the right side and the right hand on the left side.| He played the piano cross-handed. 他反手弹钢琴。|近义词：awkward, clumsy|反义词：dexterous, skillful|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From cross + handed.  "Cross" referring to the crossing of the hands, and "handed" relating to the use of hands.
