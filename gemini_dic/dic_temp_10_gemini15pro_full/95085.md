inalienableness:/ɪnˈeɪliənəbəlnəs/
n.
不可剥夺性，不可分割性
The quality or state of being inalienable; inability to be taken away or given away.
The Declaration of Independence speaks of the inalienableness of human rights.  《独立宣言》阐述了人权的不可剥夺性。
Synonyms:  inviolable, absolute, inherent, unassailable
Antonyms:  transferable, alienable, negotiable
词性: noun
