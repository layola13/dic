orchis:/ˈɔːrkɪs/| n. |兰花，睾丸|A terrestrial orchid of a genus (Orchis) that comprises many species of temperate Eurasia having flowers with the sepals and petals often converging to form a hood over the column and a lip that is often spurred.|The botanist discovered a rare orchis in the forest.  植物学家在森林里发现了一种稀有的兰花。|Synonyms: orchid |Antonyms: N/A|noun

orchis:/ˈɔːrkɪs/| n. |睾丸|A testicle.|In ancient Greece, the orchis root was associated with virility. 在古希腊，兰花根与男性生育能力有关。|Synonyms: testicle, ball, gonad |Antonyms: N/A|noun
