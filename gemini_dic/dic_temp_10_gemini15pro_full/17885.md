beelzebub:/biːˈɛlzɪbʌb/| n.|别西卜，魔王|Beelzebub is a name derived from a Philistine god, often used as another name for Satan or a high-ranking demon in Christian demonology.  It originates from the Hebrew בעל זבוב (Ba'al Zəvûv), meaning "Lord of the Flies."|He invoked Beelzebub to curse his enemies. 他祈求别西卜诅咒他的敌人。|The priest claimed the man was possessed by Beelzebub.  牧师声称该男子被别西卜附身了。|近义词：Satan, Devil, Lucifer|反义词:God, Angel|词性:名词
