bunkums: /ˈbʌŋkəmz/
noun
胡说八道，废话
Nonsense; empty talk.
That's a load of bunkums!  那是一派胡言！
synonyms: nonsense, rubbish, baloney, hogwash, claptrap, drivel
antonyms: truth, fact, reality

Etymology:
Originates from "Buncombe County," North Carolina, US, where exaggerated stories were perceived to originate, popularized by a satirical speech in Congress in 1820.
