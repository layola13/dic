heathier:/ˈhelθiər/ (US), /ˈheθiər/ (UK)| adj.|更健康的|Comparative form of "healthy".  Indicates a greater degree of health or well-being.|She's adopted a healthier lifestyle since quitting smoking. 她戒烟后过上了更健康的生活方式。| He has a healthier diet than his brother.  他的饮食比他兄弟更健康。|近义词：fitter, stronger, well|反义词:unhealthier, sicker|词性:adjective

词源:
From Old English *hæleþīgra, comparative of *hæleþīg ("healthy"), from Proto-Germanic *hailiþīgaz.  Related to "heal" and "whole."
