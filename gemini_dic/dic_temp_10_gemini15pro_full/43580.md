creakingly:/ˈkriːkɪŋli/| adv.|嘎吱作响地，发出咯吱声地|In a way that produces a creaking sound. |The door opened creakingly. 门嘎吱一声开了。|近义词：gratingly, squeakily, shrilly|反义词:smoothly, silently|词性:副词
Etymology: From the verb "creak" + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Creak" is of imitative origin.
