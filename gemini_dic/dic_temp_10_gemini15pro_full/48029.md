decisory:/dɪˈsaɪsəri/ | adj. | 决定性的；裁决性的 | Having the power or quality of deciding; conclusive; final.  | The chairman's vote was decisory. 主席的投票是决定性的。|  The judge's ruling was decisory. 法官的裁决是最终的。 | 近义词:conclusive, definitive, crucial, pivotal | 反义词:indecisive, inconsequential, unimportant | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *dēcisōrius, from Latin dēcīsus (past participle of dēcīdere, “to decide, determine, settle”), from de- + caedere (“to cut, kill”).
