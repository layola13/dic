nonplane:/ˌnɒnˈpleɪn/
adjective
非平面的
Not lying or able to be contained within a single plane.
A nonplanar graph cannot be drawn on a plane without crossings.  非平面图不能在平面上绘制而没有交叉。
The molecule has a nonplanar structure. 这个分子具有非平面结构。
近义词: three-dimensional, non-flat
反义词: planar, flat
词性: adjective
