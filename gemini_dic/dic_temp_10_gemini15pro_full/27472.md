campanero:/ˌkæmpəˈnɛroʊ/ | n. |钟鸣鸟，敲钟人|A bird known for its bell-like song, especially the Three-wattled Bellbird (Procnias tricarunculata). Also, a person who rings a bell, especially in a church. |例句：The campanero's song echoed through the forest. 钟鸣鸟的歌声在森林中回荡。

例句：The campanero rang the bell to signal the start of the service. 敲钟人敲响了钟，标志着服务的开始。|近义词：bellbird, bell-ringer|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
