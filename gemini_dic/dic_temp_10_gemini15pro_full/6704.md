allantois:/əˈlæntɔɪs/ | n. | 尿囊 |The fetal membrane lying below the chorion in many vertebrates, formed as an outgrowth of the hindgut. In mammals, it forms part of the placenta. | The allantois helps with gas exchange and waste removal in the developing embryo. 尿囊帮助发育中的胚胎进行气体交换和废物清除。|  The allantois is connected to the embryo's bladder. 尿囊与胚胎的膀胱相连。| Synonyms: allantoidea | Antonyms: N/A | noun
