equipment:/ɪˈkwɪpmənt/ | n. |设备，器材|The laboratory has state-of-the-art equipment.  这个实验室拥有最先进的设备。| Example:  The climbers checked their equipment before starting the ascent.  登山者在开始攀登之前检查了他们的装备。|Synonyms: gear, apparatus, paraphernalia| Antonyms:  lack, deficiency| noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *equippement (from équiper "to equip, fit out," from Old Norse skipa "to arrange, fit out a ship") and directly from Middle French équipement.
