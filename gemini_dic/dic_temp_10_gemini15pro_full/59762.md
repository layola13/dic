economises:/ɪˈkɒnəmaɪzɪz/
verb
节省，节约
Reduces expenses; uses resources carefully.
The company economises by using energy-efficient light bulbs.  公司通过使用节能灯泡来节省开支。
She economises on household expenses by buying in bulk. 她通过批量购买来节省家庭开支。
saves, conserves, cuts back
spends, squanders, wastes
v.
