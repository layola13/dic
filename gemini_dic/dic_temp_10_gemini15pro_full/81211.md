ETYMOLOGY->
From grandmother + -ly.  "Grandmother" comes from the Middle English "graundmoder," from Old English "grāndmōdor," composed of "grānd" (grand, great) and "mōdor" (mother). The suffix "-ly" forms adverbs and adjectives, here indicating resemblance or characteristic.
