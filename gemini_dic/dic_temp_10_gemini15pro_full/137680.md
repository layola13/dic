nonuniversals:/ˌnɑːnˌjuːnɪˈvɜːrsəlz/
n.|非普遍性；非共性|Characteristics or features that are not shared by all cultures or languages.|Many linguists study nonuniversals to understand the diversity of human language. 许多语言学家研究非共性，以理解人类语言的多样性。|non-shared features, particularities|universals|noun
