limberest:/ˈlɪmbərɪst/| adj.| 最柔软的，最灵活的 |  Superlative form of limber: most limber.  Meaning the most flexible or supple, especially in the body.|  The gymnast was the limberest of all the competitors. 体操运动员是所有参赛者中最灵活的。 |近义词: most flexible, most supple, most agile |反义词: stiffest, least flexible |词性: adjective

词源:  From *limber* + *-est*.  *Limber* comes from Middle English *limber*, *lember*, from Old English *limpor* (“belonging to limbs”).  The *-est* suffix is a common superlative ending in English.
