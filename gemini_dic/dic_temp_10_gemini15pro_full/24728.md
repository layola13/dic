bronzine: /ˈbrɒnzin/ (UK), /ˈbrɑːnzin/ (US) | n. | 铜锌合金；青铜色的 | An alloy primarily of copper and zinc, similar to brass but with a higher proportion of zinc, or sometimes tin, lead, or other metals.  It can also refer to something resembling bronze in color. |  The clock was made of bronzine.  这个钟是用铜锌合金做的。

The sculpture had a beautiful bronzine patina. 这座雕塑有着美丽的青铜色光泽。 | 近义词: brass, gunmetal | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could be specific materials like steel or iron depending on context) | 词性: noun
