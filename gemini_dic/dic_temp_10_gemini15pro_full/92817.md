iconographer:/aɪˌkɒˈnɒɡrəfər/ | n. |圣像画家；图像学家；肖像研究者 |A person who makes or studies icons, or an expert in iconography. |例句：The iconographer carefully painted the details of the saint's halo. 圣像画家仔细地描绘了圣人光环的细节。

例句：The art historian specialized as an iconographer, focusing on the symbolism in medieval paintings. 这位艺术史学家专攻图像学，专注于中世纪绘画中的象征意义。

|近义词：artist, painter, scholar, expert |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
