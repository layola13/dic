misogynistic:/mɪˌsɒdʒɪˈnɪstɪk/ /mɪˌsɑːdʒɪˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
厌恶女人的；歧视女人的
Showing or characterized by hatred of women.
His misogynistic comments sparked outrage. 他的厌女言论引发了众怒。
近义词: sexist, anti-female, woman-hating
反义词: philogynistic
词性: adjective

misogynist:/mɪˈsɒdʒɪnɪst/ /mɪˈsɑːdʒɪnɪst/
noun
厌恶女人的人；歧视女人的人
A person who dislikes, despises, or prejudiced against women.
He was known as a misogynist who refused to hire women. 他是一个臭名昭著的厌女症患者，拒绝雇佣女性。
近义词: sexist
反义词: philogynist
词性: noun
