delphinine:/ˈdɛlfɪnaɪn/ | n. |飞燕草碱，翠雀碱 |A toxic alkaloid, C33H45NO9, extracted from the seeds of Delphinium staphisagria (stavesacre) and other species of larkspur.| Delphinine is a potent poison. 飞燕草碱是一种强效毒药。| Delphinine can cause respiratory paralysis. 飞燕草碱会导致呼吸麻痹。|Synonyms: staphisagrine | Antonyms: None | Noun
