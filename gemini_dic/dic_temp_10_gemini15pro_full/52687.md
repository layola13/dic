dignifier:/ˈdɪɡnɪfaɪər/
noun
赋予尊严的人或物
Something that gives dignity or elevates status.
The award was a dignifier for the recipient, boosting her reputation in the field.  这个奖项对获奖者来说是一种尊严的象征，提升了她在该领域的声誉。
Synonyms: ennobler, exalter
Antonyms: degrader, debaser
