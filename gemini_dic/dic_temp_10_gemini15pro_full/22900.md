botfly:/ˈbɑːtflaɪ/ /ˈbɒtflaɪ/| n. |马蝇；肤蝇|A type of fly whose larvae live as parasites in the bodies of mammals, including humans.|The botfly lays its eggs on a mosquito or other flying insect, which then carries the eggs to a host animal. 马蝇将卵产在蚊子或其他飞行昆虫身上，然后由它们将卵带到宿主动物身上。

The veterinarian removed a botfly larva from the horse's back. 兽医从马背上取出了一只马蝇幼虫。


|近义词：gadfly, warble fly|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
