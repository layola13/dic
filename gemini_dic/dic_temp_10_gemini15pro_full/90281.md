houseless:/ˈhaʊsləs/| adj.|无家可归的|Without a house; homeless.|例句：The houseless man slept under a bridge.  那个无家可归的人睡在桥下。

例句：Rising rents have contributed to a growing houseless population.  不断上涨的租金导致无家可归的人口不断增长。|近义词：homeless, roofless, dispossessed|反义词：housed, sheltered|词性:形容词


Etymology:
From house + -less.  Essentially, "without a house."
