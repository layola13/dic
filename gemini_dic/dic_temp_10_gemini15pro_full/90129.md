hot-wiring:/ˌhɒtˈwaɪərɪŋ/ (US) /ˌhɒtˈwaɪərɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |热启动，跨接启动|The act of starting a car engine without using the ignition key, typically by connecting wires to bypass the ignition system.|He got arrested for hot-wiring a car. 他因热启动汽车而被捕。|近义词：bypass starting, jumpstarting|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "legitimate starting")|noun
hot-wire:/ˌhɒtˈwaɪər/ (US) /ˌhɒtˈwaɪər/ (UK)| v. |热启动，跨接启动|To start a car engine without using the ignition key, typically by connecting wires to bypass the ignition system.|He hot-wired the car and sped off. 他热启动了汽车，然后飞驰而去。|近义词：bypass start, jumpstart|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "start legitimately")|verb
