intreated:/ɪnˈtriːtɪd/
verb
恳求；乞求
asked earnestly; begged
例句：She entreated him to stay. 她恳求他留下。

例句：He entreated the judge for mercy. 他乞求法官开恩。

近义词：begged, pleaded, implored, besought
反义词：demanded, commanded
词性：v.
