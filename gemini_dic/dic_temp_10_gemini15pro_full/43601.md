creaming:/ˈkriːmɪŋ/ | n. | 奶油状物，提取精华，(俚语)轻易击败 |The act of skimming the richest or best part of something, like cream from milk. Also, the process of beating ingredients (like butter and sugar) until light and fluffy. In slang, it can mean to defeat soundly or easily. |The baker was creaming the butter and sugar for the cake.  面包师正在为蛋糕把黄油和糖打成糊状。| Synonyms: skimming, best, elite, trouncing, thrashing | Antonyms: dregs, worst, losing | noun


creaming:/ˈkriːmɪŋ/ | adj. | 奶油色的，像奶油的|Having the color or texture of cream. | She wore a creaming white dress to the party. 她穿着一件奶油色的白裙子去参加聚会。| Synonyms: creamy, milky, off-white | Antonyms: dark, colorful | adjective
