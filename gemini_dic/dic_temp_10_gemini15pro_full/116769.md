mauther:/ˈmɔːðər/ | n. | 饶舌者，喋喋不休的人 | A person who talks incessantly; a chatterbox. | She's such a mauther, she never stops talking! 她真是个饶舌妇，嘴巴就没停过！|  He's a terrible mauther and can talk for hours about nothing. 他真是个话痨，可以连续几个小时说些无关紧要的事情。 | 近义词: chatterbox, gossipmonger, windbag | 反义词: reticent person, quiet person | 词性: 名词

词源:
From the verb "maunder" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Maunder" meaning "to talk in a rambling, incoherent way" comes from a dialectal word meaning "to mumble, mutter."
