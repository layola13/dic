fragmentary:/ˈfræɡmənˌtɛri/ | adj. | 片段的，零碎的，不完整的 | Consisting of fragments; incomplete; disconnected. |  The archaeologist pieced together the fragmentary remains of the pottery. 考古学家将碎片状的陶器残片拼凑在一起。

The evidence was fragmentary and inconclusive.  证据是零碎的，没有定论。

| 近义词: incomplete, partial, broken, fragmented, piecemeal | 反义词: complete, whole, entire, intact | 词性: adjective
