parastichy: /ˈpærəˌstɪki/| n. |螺旋排列;叶序;斜列线|A spiral arrangement of leaves or other plant parts, such as the scales of a pine cone, where the number of spirals to the left and to the right may be represented by a ratio of consecutive numbers in the Fibonacci sequence. |The parastichy of this sunflower is 34/55.  这种向日葵的叶序是34/55。|The botanist studied the parastichy of the fern.  这位植物学家研究了蕨类植物的叶序。|近义词: phyllotaxis, spiral arrangement|反义词: whorled arrangement|词性: noun
