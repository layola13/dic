avows:/əˈvaʊz/| v. |公开声明；承认；坦白|To declare or affirm openly and formally. |She avowed her love for him. 她坦白了她对他的爱。

He avowed his intention to run for president. 他公开宣布他将竞选总统。|近义词：declares, admits, confesses, asserts, affirms|反义词：denies, refutes, contradicts|词性:动词

词源: From Old French *avouer* "to admit, acknowledge, confess, avow," from Latin *advocare* "to call to (oneself), summon, claim," from *ad* "to" + *vocare* "to call."
