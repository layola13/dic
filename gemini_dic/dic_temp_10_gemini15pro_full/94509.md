importune:/ɪmˈpɔːrtjuːn/ /ɪmˈpɔːrtun/
verb
恳求；纠缠；不断要求
To ask someone repeatedly for something, especially in a way that is annoying.
He importuned the king for favors. 他不断恳求国王给予恩惠。
She importuned her parents to let her go to the party. 她缠着父母让她去参加聚会。
近义词:beg, beseech, pester, badger, harass
反义词:leave alone, ignore
词性:v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French *importuner, based on Latin importunus ‘unseasonable, inconvenient’, from in- ‘not’ + portus ‘harbour, haven’.
