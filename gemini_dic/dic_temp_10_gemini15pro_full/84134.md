half-misunderstood:/ˌhæfˌmɪsʌndərˈstʊd/
| adj. | 半被误解的 | Partly or incompletely misunderstood; not fully or correctly understood. |  His intentions were half-misunderstood by his colleagues, leading to unnecessary conflict. 他的意图被同事们半误解了，导致了不必要的冲突。| The artist felt half-misunderstood; some critics praised his work, while others dismissed it entirely. 这位艺术家感觉自己被半误解了；一些评论家称赞他的作品，而另一些则完全不予理会。 | partially misunderstood, imperfectly understood | fully understood, correctly understood | adjective
