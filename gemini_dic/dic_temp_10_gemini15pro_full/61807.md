emphysema: /ˌɛmfɪˈsiːmə/ (US), /ˌɛmfɪˈsiːmə/ (UK)| n. |肺气肿|A condition in which the air sacs of the lungs are damaged and enlarged, causing breathlessness.|

例句：
His emphysema made it difficult for him to climb stairs.
他的肺气肿使他爬楼梯很困难。

Emphysema is a serious lung disease.
肺气肿是一种严重的肺部疾病。

近义词：COPD (chronic obstructive pulmonary disease)|
反义词: healthy lungs|
词性: noun
