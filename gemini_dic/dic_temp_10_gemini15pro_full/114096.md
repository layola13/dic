magnifier:/ˈmæɡnɪfaɪər/| n. |扩大者，增强者|Something that increases or intensifies something else.|The media acted as a magnifier of the scandal. 媒体放大了这起丑闻。|Synonyms: amplifier, intensifier, enhancer|Antonyms: diminisher, reducer|noun
