fricassee:/ˈfrɪkəˌsiː/| n. vt.|炖肉块; 将…切块炖;|A fricassee is a dish of meat, especially chicken or rabbit, cut into pieces and stewed in a white sauce. |例句：She prepared a delicious chicken fricassee. 她做了一道美味的鸡肉炖菜。

例句：The chef fricasseed the rabbit with mushrooms and herbs. 厨师用蘑菇和香草炖了兔子肉。|近义词：stew, braise|反义词: roast, grill|词性:名词，动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *fricasée*, from earlier *friquasser* "to fry," of uncertain origin, perhaps ultimately from Vulgar Latin *frigicare*, from Latin *frigere* "to roast, fry."
