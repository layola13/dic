dowelling:/ˈdaʊəlɪŋ/| n.|榫接; 销钉; 暗销; 销子|A cylindrical rod, usually of wood, plastic, or metal, used for joining two pieces of wood, typically by fitting into holes drilled in both pieces.|He used dowelling to join the two pieces of wood. 他用销钉把两块木头连接起来。|近义词:peg, pin, rod|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun


dowel:/ˈdaʊəl/| n. vt.|销钉，用销钉固定|A pin or peg of wood, metal, or plastic used to reinforce a joint, or to attach something.  As a verb, it means to fasten with dowels.|The cabinetmaker dowelled the shelves to the frame.  细木工用销钉把架子固定到框架上。|近义词:peg, pin, rod, spigot|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun, verb
