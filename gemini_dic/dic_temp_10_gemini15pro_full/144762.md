over-defensive: /ˈəʊvər dɪˈfɛnsɪv/ | adj. | 过于防御的，过分自卫的 | Excessively or inappropriately defensive; reacting too strongly or too quickly to perceived threats or criticism. Characterized by a tendency to anticipate and protect oneself from criticism or attack, often to an excessive or inappropriate degree. | He became over-defensive when asked about his past.  他被问到过去时变得过分防御。

She's so over-defensive that she takes everything as a personal attack. 她太敏感了，把一切都当成是针对她个人的攻击。| Synonyms: sensitive, insecure, touchy, thin-skinned | Antonyms: confident, secure, thick-skinned | adjective
