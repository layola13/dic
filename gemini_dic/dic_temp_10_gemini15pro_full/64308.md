equivocate:/ɪˈkwɪvəkeɪt/| v. |含糊其辞，模棱两可，说谎|To use ambiguous language so as to conceal the truth or avoid committing oneself.|The politician equivocated when asked about his stance on the controversial issue.  这位政客在被问及他对争议问题的立场时含糊其辞。|例句：He equivocated about his whereabouts on the night of the crime. 他对犯罪当晚的行踪含糊其辞。|近义词：prevaricate, evade, hedge, dodge|反义词:be forthright, be direct, be honest|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin aequivocatus, past participle of aequivocare ("to call by the same name, use words ambiguously"), from Latin aequus ("equal") + vocare ("to call").
