elaeolite:/ɪˈliːəˌlaɪt/ /ɛˈliːəˌlaɪt/| n.|霞石|A glassy, translucent variety of nepheline, a feldspathoid mineral, commonly bluish gray or greenish and sometimes reddish. It occurs in igneous rocks deficient in silica.|Elaeolite is a key component of some syenites. 霞石是某些正长岩的关键组成部分。|Elaeolite can exhibit a shimmering effect when polished.  霞石抛光后会呈现出闪光效果。|Synonyms: nepheline, nephelite|Antonyms: N/A (Minerals rarely have direct antonyms)|noun
