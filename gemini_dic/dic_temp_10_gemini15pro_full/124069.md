morphonomy:/mɔːrˈfɒnəmi/ | n. | 形态命名学; 形态学和分类学 | The study of the laws governing the relations of form and function.  The study that combines morphology and taxonomy. |  The book delves into the morphonomy of various plant species. 这本书深入研究了各种植物的形态命名学。|  Morphonomy plays a crucial role in understanding evolutionary relationships. 形态命名学在理解进化关系中起着至关重要的作用。 |  morphology, taxonomy | - | noun
