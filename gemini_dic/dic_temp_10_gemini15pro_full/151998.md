patagium:/pəˈteɪdʒiəm/| n. |翼膜，飞膜|A membrane or fold of skin extending between the forelimbs and hind limbs of some vertebrates, such as bats, flying squirrels, and pterosaurs, enabling gliding or flight.|The bat's patagium allows it to fly with remarkable agility.  蝙蝠的翼膜使它能够以惊人的敏捷性飞行。|Synonyms: wing membrane, gliding membrane|Antonyms: None|Noun


patagia:/pəˈteɪdʒiə/| n. |翼膜，飞膜（patagium的复数形式）|The plural form of patagium, referring to the membranes used for gliding or flight.|The patagia of flying squirrels are covered in fur.  飞鼠的翼膜上覆盖着毛皮。|Synonyms: wing membranes, gliding membranes|Antonyms: None|Noun
