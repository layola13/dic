antiquities:/ænˈtɪkwɪtiz/
n.
古物；古迹；古代文物
objects, buildings, or works of art from the ancient past
The museum has a fascinating collection of Roman antiquities.  这家博物馆收藏了引人入胜的罗马文物。
近义词:relics, artifacts, ancient objects
反义词:modern artifacts, contemporary objects
词性:名词(plural noun)

Etymology:
From Late Middle English:  antiquitees, from Old French antiquitez,  from Latin antiquitas, from antiquus ‘ancient’.
