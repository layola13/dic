inadmissibilities:/ˌɪnədˌmɪsəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 不可受理性；不许可入境；不予接纳 | The state of being inadmissible; things or qualities which render a person or thing inadmissible. |  His inadmissibilities to the country stemmed from his past criminal record.  他因过去的犯罪记录而无法入境该国。 | 近义词: unacceptability, ineligibility | 反义词: admissibility, eligibility | 词性: noun

inadmissible:/ˌɪnədˈmɪsəbl/ | adj. | 不可接受的；不许可入境的；不予接纳的 | Not admissible; not allowable. | The evidence was deemed inadmissible in court.  该证据被认为在法庭上不可接受。 | 近义词: unacceptable, ineligible | 反义词: admissible, eligible | 词性: adjective

词源:  From in- (not) + admissible.  "Admissible" comes from the Latin "admittere" (to admit).
