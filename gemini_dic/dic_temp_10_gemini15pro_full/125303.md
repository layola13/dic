multigenic:/ˌmʌltiˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
多基因的
Relating to or determined by more than one gene.
Example: Multigenic inheritance is responsible for many common diseases. 多基因遗传是许多常见疾病的原因。
Example: Height is a multigenic trait, influenced by several different genes. 身高是一个多基因性状，受几个不同基因的影响。

Synonym: polygenic
Antonym: monogenic
词性: 形容词
