parapsychology:/ˌpærəsaɪˈkɒlədʒi/ /ˌpærəsaɪˈkɑːlədʒi/| n. | 超心理学 |The study of mental phenomena that are excluded from or inexplicable by orthodox scientific psychology (such as hypnosis, telepathy, etc.).  |  She's always been interested in parapsychology and the possibility of psychic abilities. 她一直对超心理学和通灵能力的可能性很感兴趣。 |  Example:  Researchers in parapsychology investigate claims of extrasensory perception. 超心理学的研究人员调查超感官知觉的主张。 | Synonyms: psychical research, metapsychology | Antonyms: conventional psychology | noun
