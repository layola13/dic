boroglyceride:/ˌbɔːroʊˈɡlɪsəraɪd/
n.
硼酸甘油酯
A compound formed by heating boric acid with glycerol.  It is used as an antiseptic and preservative.
Boroglyceride is commonly used in pharmaceutical preparations. 硼酸甘油酯常用于药物制剂中。
The pharmacist mixed boroglyceride with the other ingredients. 药剂师将硼酸甘油酯与其他成分混合。
Synonym: Glyceryl borate
