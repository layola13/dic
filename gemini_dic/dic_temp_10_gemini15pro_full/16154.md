banjo:/ˈbændʒoʊ/ (US) /ˈbændʒəʊ/ (UK)| n. |班卓琴；五弦琴|A musical instrument like a small guitar with a long neck and a round body covered with skin, played by plucking or strumming the strings.|He played a lively tune on his banjo. 他用班卓琴演奏了一曲欢快的曲子。|Near synonyms:  guitar, lute, ukulele|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
