docible:/ˈdɒsaɪbl/ (US) /ˈdoʊsəbl/| adj.|温顺的，易教的，可塑的|Easily taught or controlled; readily accepting instruction or guidance.  Referring to a person or animal that is easy to train or influence.|例句：The dog was highly docible and learned tricks quickly. 这只狗非常温顺，学 tricks 学得很快。

例句：The docible students eagerly participated in the class discussions. 这些温顺的学生积极参与课堂讨论。|近义词：tractable, compliant, malleable, obedient, teachable|反义词：intractable, stubborn, unyielding, obstinate, rebellious|词性:adjective
