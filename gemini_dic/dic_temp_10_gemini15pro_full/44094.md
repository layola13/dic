cripplingly:/ˈkrɪplɪŋli/| adv.|严重地，造成严重后果地|So severely as to cause a person or thing to be unable to move or operate properly.|The country's economy was cripplingly dependent on oil exports. 这个国家的经济严重依赖石油出口。|The lack of funding was cripplingly restrictive. 缺乏资金严重地限制了发展。|Synonyms: severely, devastatingly, drastically, paralyzingly|Antonyms: mildly, slightly, insignificantly|Adverb
