episternum:/ɪˈpɪstɜːrnəm/ | n. | 胸骨前板，上胸骨 | A plate of cartilage or bone in front of the sternum of many vertebrates, especially reptiles and amphibians. It is also called the interclavicle or episternal bone. |  The episternum of the frog provides attachment for the clavicles and coracoids.  青蛙的胸骨前板为锁骨和喙骨提供了附着点。|  synonym: interclavicle, episternal bone | antonym: (No direct antonym, as it is an anatomical feature) | noun
