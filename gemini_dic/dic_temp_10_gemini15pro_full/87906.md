hieroglyphed:/ˈhaɪərəˌɡlɪft/| adj.| 象形文字的；用象形文字写的|covered or decorated with hieroglyphs; written in hieroglyphs| The tomb was hieroglyphed with ancient symbols. 墓穴上刻满了古代符号。|  The priest deciphered the hieroglyphed message. 祭司破译了象形文字信息。| inscribed, carved, written | plain, undecorated| adjective

Etymology->
From hieroglyph + -ed.  "Hieroglyph" itself comes from the Greek hieros (sacred) + glyphein (to carve).
