contorted:/kənˈtɔːrtɪd/ | adj. | 扭曲的，歪曲的 | twisted or strained out of shape;  used both literally and figuratively to describe something that has been deformed or misrepresented. |  Her face was contorted in agony. 她的脸因痛苦而扭曲。| synonyms: twisted, deformed, warped, misshapen | antonyms: straight, unbent, natural, undistorted | adjective

contort:/kənˈtɔːrt/ | v. | 扭曲，歪曲 | to twist or bend out of its normal shape; can be used literally (to describe a physical action) or figuratively (to describe a distortion of facts, ideas, etc.) |  The intense heat contorted the metal bars.  高温使金属条变形。 | synonyms: twist, deform, warp, distort | antonyms: straighten, unbend | verb
