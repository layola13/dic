dismarry:/dɪsˈmæri/
verb
解除婚姻关系; 使离婚
To end a marriage, especially by divorce.
The judge dismarried the couple. 法官宣布这对夫妇离婚。
近义词: divorce, separate, annul
反义词: marry, wed
词性: verb
