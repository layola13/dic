honey-tongued:/ˈhʌniˌtʌŋd/ | adj. |甜言蜜语的；花言巧语的 | Persuasive and flattering, often insincerely so. |  He's a honey-tongued salesman; don't believe a word he says. 他是个油嘴滑舌的推销员，别相信他说的任何话。

| Example: The honey-tongued politician charmed the voters.  这位花言巧语的政客迷住了选民。| Synonym:  smooth-talking, silver-tongued, glib, flattering | Antonym:  blunt, frank, honest, straightforward | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From honey + tongued.  Honey, referring to the sweetness of speech, and tongued, meaning having a particular kind of tongue or way of speaking.  The combination implies speech that is sweet and persuasive, often to a deceptive degree.
