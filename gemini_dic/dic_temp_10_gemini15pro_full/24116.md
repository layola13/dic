brewery:/ˈbruːəri/ /ˈbrʊri/| n.|啤酒厂；酿啤酒公司|A place where beer is made commercially.|例句：The town's oldest brewery has been in operation for over 200 years.  这个镇上最古老的啤酒厂已经运营了200多年了。
