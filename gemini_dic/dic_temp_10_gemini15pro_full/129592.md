non-committally:/ˌnɑːn kəˈmɪtəli/ | adv. | 不置可否地，含糊地 |  In a way that does not express a clear opinion or decision. |  He answered non-committally, not wanting to reveal his plans. 他含糊地回答，不想透露他的计划。| Synonyms: evasively, vaguely, ambiguously | Antonyms: decisively, definitely, firmly | Adverb
non-committal:/ˌnɑːn kəˈmɪtəl/ | adj. | 不表态的，不承担义务的 | Not expressing a clear opinion or decision.  |  She gave a non-committal shrug, neither agreeing nor disagreeing. 她不置可否地耸了耸肩，既不同意也不反对。 | Synonyms: evasive, vague, ambiguous | Antonyms: decisive, definite, firm | Adjective
