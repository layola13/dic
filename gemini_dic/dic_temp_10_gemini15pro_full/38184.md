comptroller:/kənˈtroʊlər/ | n. |主计长，审计员，财务总监 | A person in charge of the financial affairs of a company or government. | The comptroller signed off on the budget. 主计长签署了预算。| The comptroller released the company's financial statements. 财务总监发布了公司的财务报表。 |Synonyms: controller, auditor, accountant | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "spender" in a loose sense) | noun
