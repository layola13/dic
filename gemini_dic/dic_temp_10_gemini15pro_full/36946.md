colourer: /ˈkʌlərə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkʌlərər/ (US)
noun
着色者；涂色者；给…着色的人
A person who colours something, especially pictures.
例句：The children were given a picture of a lion and asked to be colourers for the afternoon.  孩子们得到了一张狮子的图片，并被要求在下午做着色者。

例句：The colourer carefully filled in the intricate details of the drawing.  着色者仔细地填充了图画中复杂的细节。

近义词：artist, painter, decorator
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
