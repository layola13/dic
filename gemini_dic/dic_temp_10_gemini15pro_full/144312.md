outspreading:/ˌaʊtˈsprɛdɪŋ/
adjective
展开的，伸展的
spreading out or extending over a wide area.
The eagle soared with outspreading wings.  老鹰展开翅膀翱翔。
The outspreading branches of the oak tree provided ample shade.  橡树伸展的枝条提供了充足的阴凉。
近义词:extended, expanded, stretched, sprawling
反义词:contracted, constricted, folded

词性:adjective

词源:From out + spreading, present participle of spread.  来自out + spread的现在分词，spread意为展开。
