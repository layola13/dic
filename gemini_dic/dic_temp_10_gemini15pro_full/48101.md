declination:/ˌdɛklɪˈneɪʃən/| n.|衰退，下降；倾斜；偏差；婉拒|A downward movement or trend; a sloping or bending downward; the angular distance of a celestial body north or south of the celestial equator; a polite refusal.|The declination of the Roman Empire. 罗马帝国的衰落。|近义词：decline, descent, dip, slope, refusal, rejection|反义词：ascent, incline, acceptance|noun

declination:/ˌdɛklɪˈneɪʃən/| n.|磁偏角|The angle between true north and magnetic north.|The declination of the compass needle is 10 degrees east. 指南针的磁偏角是东偏10度。|近义词：magnetic variation, magnetic declination|反义词：-|noun
