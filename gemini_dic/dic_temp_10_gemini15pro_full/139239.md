obligate:/ˈɑːblɪɡeɪt/ (US), /ˈɒblɪɡeɪt/ (UK)| vt. adj.|强制，责成；使负义务|to bind legally or morally; to compel|例句：The law obligates parents to send their children to school. 法律规定父母必须送孩子上学。

例句：I felt obligated to help her after she helped me. 她帮了我之后，我觉得有义务帮助她。|近义词：compel, require, bind, oblige|反义词：exempt, excuse|词性：动词

obligate:/ˈɑːblɪɡɪt/ (US), /ˈɒblɪɡɪt/ (UK)| adj.|有义务的，必须的|bound by law or duty; mandatory|例句：He is obligated to pay the debt. 他有义务偿还债务。

例句：We are obligated to provide a safe working environment. 我们有义务提供一个安全的工作环境。|近义词：required, compulsory, mandatory|反义词：optional, voluntary|词性：形容词

词源：From Latin obligatus, past participle of obligare (“to bind, tie up, fasten, attach”), from ob- (“towards”) + ligare (“to bind”).
