hypertrophying:/haɪˈpɜːrtrəfiɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
肥大，过度生长
Enlarging or increasing in size or bulk, especially of a body part or organ, due to an increase in the size of its constituent cells.
The bodybuilder's muscles were hypertrophying at an alarming rate. 健美运动员的肌肉以惊人的速度肥大。
His heart muscle is hypertrophying due to the increased workload. 由于工作负荷增加，他的心肌肥大了。
Near Synonyms: enlarging, expanding, growing, developing, swelling
Near Antonyms: atrophying, shrinking, withering, diminishing, contracting
