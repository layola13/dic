outbargain:/ˌaʊtˈbɑːrɡɪn/
verb
讨价还价胜过；比…会讲价
To surpass or defeat someone in bargaining; to get a better price than someone else through negotiation.
He outbargained the street vendor and got the rug for half the asking price. 他与街头小贩讨价还价，最终以要价的一半买到了地毯。
近义词: outtrade, outsmart, outmaneuver
反义词: be outbargained, be outsmarted
词性: verb
