dishful:/ˈdɪʃfʊl/| n.|一盘(的量), 一满盘|A dishful is the amount of food that a dish will hold.|She ate a dishful of pasta. 她吃了一盘意大利面。|The waiter brought out a dishful of steaming vegetables. 服务员端出一盘热气腾腾的蔬菜。|近义词:plateful, helping, serving|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑少量 a little, a bit)|名词
