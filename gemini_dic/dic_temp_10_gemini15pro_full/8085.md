anthropopathic:/ˌænθrəpəˈpæθɪk/
adjective
拟人的，赋予人性的
ascribing human emotions or characteristics to nonhuman things, especially to a god.
例句：The anthropopathic view of God presents Him as having human-like emotions and reactions.  拟人化的上帝观将上帝描绘成具有类似人类的情感和反应。

例句：Ancient myths often feature anthropopathic deities who love, hate, and get jealous just like humans.  古代神话中经常出现有人格化的神，他们像人类一样爱恨交加。
synonym:anthropomorphic, personified
antonym:impersonal, objective
词性:adjective
