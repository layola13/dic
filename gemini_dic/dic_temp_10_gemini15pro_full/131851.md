nonconstricted:/ˌnɒnkənˈstrɪktɪd/
adjective
未收缩的，未约束的
Not constricted; not narrowed, tightened, or compressed.
The blood vessels were nonconstricted, allowing for good blood flow. 血管没有收缩，血液流动良好。
The nonconstricted airways allowed her to breathe easily. 没有收缩的气道让她呼吸顺畅。
Synonyms: dilated, open, expanded, unconfined
Antonyms: constricted, narrowed, tight, compressed
词性: 形容词
