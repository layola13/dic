lobelin: /ˈloʊbɪlɪn/ | n. | 洛贝林; 山梗菜碱 | An alkaloid derived from *Lobelia inflata*, used as a respiratory stimulant. | Lobeline stimulates breathing and can be used to treat respiratory depression. 洛贝林刺激呼吸，可用于治疗呼吸抑制。| Lobeline sulfate was administered to reverse respiratory depression.  硫酸洛贝林被用来逆转呼吸抑制。 |  Synonyms: Lobelanidine, Lobelamine | Antonyms: Respiratory depressant | noun
