decontaminate:/ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪt/
verb
净化，清除污染，消毒
To remove or neutralize contaminants, making something safe to handle or use.
The workers decontaminated the area after the chemical spill.  工人们在化学品泄漏后对该区域进行了净化。
The equipment must be decontaminated before it can be reused.  设备必须先净化才能再次使用。
Synonyms: sanitize, sterilize, purify, cleanse
Antonyms: contaminate, pollute, infect
词性: 动词


decontaminated:/ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
已净化的，已清除污染的，已消毒的
Having been cleansed of contaminants; made safe.
The decontaminated water is now safe to drink.  净化后的水现在可以安全饮用了。
They entered the decontaminated zone wearing protective suits.  他们穿着防护服进入了净化区。
Synonyms: sanitized, sterilized, purified, cleansed
Antonyms: contaminated, polluted, infected
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From de- (meaning "remove, reverse") + contaminate (from Latin contaminatus, past participle of contaminare, "to pollute").
