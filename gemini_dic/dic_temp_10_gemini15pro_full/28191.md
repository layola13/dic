capric:/ˈkæprɪk/| adj.|羊的；山羊的；有山羊气味的；反复无常的|Relating to or resembling a goat, especially in smell; characteristic of a goat.  Also, relating to capric acid, a fatty acid found in goat's milk and other animal fats.|The cheese had a distinct capric aroma.  这种奶酪有一种明显的山羊气味。|Synonyms: goatlike, hircine|Antonyms: bland, odorless|adjective


capric:/ˈkæprɪk/| adj.|（尤指情绪）反复无常的，变化多端的|Characterized by sudden, unpredictable changes in mood or behavior.|Her capric nature made it difficult to predict her reactions. 她反复无常的性格使得难以预测她的反应。|Synonyms: fickle, capricious, mercurial, erratic|Antonyms: stable, steady, consistent, predictable|adjective
