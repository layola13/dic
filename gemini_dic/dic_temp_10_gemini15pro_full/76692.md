galvanotonus: /ˌɡælˌvænoʊˈtoʊnəs/ | n. |电流紧张; 电紧张 | A state of increased muscular tension caused by the passage of an electric current. |  The galvanotonus caused by the TENS unit made his muscles twitch. 电疗仪引起的电流紧张使他的肌肉抽搐。 |  Synonym: electrotonus | Antonym: relaxation | noun
