nondisbursable:/ˌnɒndɪsˈbɜːsəbl/
adjective
不可支付的，不予支付的
Not able to be disbursed or paid out.
The grant included a nondisbursable portion for administrative costs.  这笔拨款中包含一部分不可支付的行政费用。
This type of loan is nondisbursable until all conditions are met.  这种贷款在所有条件都满足之前是不能支付的。
nonpayable, nonreimbursable
