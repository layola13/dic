outsail:/ˌaʊtˈseɪl/
verb
超过…的航速；比…航行得快
to sail faster than another ship or fleet
The smaller, lighter yacht easily outsail the older vessels in the race.  这艘更小更轻的游艇在比赛中轻松地超过了那些老船。
近义词: outstrip, surpass, beat, overcome
反义词: lag behind, trail
