dictation:/dɪkˈteɪʃn/ | n. |  dictation, 听写; 口述; 命令; |  the act of saying words for someone to write down or the act of writing down words as someone says them | The students listened to the teacher's dictation and wrote down the words in their notebooks. 学生们听着老师的口述，在笔记本上记下单词。| synonyms: transcription, recital | antonyms: listening, hearing | noun

dictations:/dɪkˈteɪʃnz/ | n. | dictations, 听写（复数）; 口述（复数）; 命令（复数）; | plural form of "dictation" | The teacher gave us three dictations this week.  这周老师给我们做了三次听写。| synonyms: transcriptions, recitals | antonyms: listenings, hearings | noun
