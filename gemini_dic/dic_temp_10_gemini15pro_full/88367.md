hippyish:/ˈhɪpiɪʃ/| adj.|嬉皮士似的，嬉皮风格的|Resembling or characteristic of a hippy, especially in dress, lifestyle, or attitudes.|She had a hippyish look with her long flowing skirt and flowers in her hair. 她穿着飘逸的长裙，头发上插着花，看起来很有嬉皮士风格。|近义词：bohemian, unconventional, alternative|反义词：conventional, traditional, conservative|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From hippy + -ish. Hippy itself is of uncertain origin, possibly from hip (meaning "aware," "in the know") + -y, or a shortening of hipster.
