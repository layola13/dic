myocomma:/ˈmaɪəˌkɒmə/| n. |肌隔|A sheet of connective tissue which separates adjacent myomeres (blocks of skeletal muscle) in vertebrates, especially fish.|The myocomma allows for more efficient muscle contraction. 肌隔使得肌肉收缩更加有效。|近义词:myoseptum|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
