daisy: /ˈdeɪzi/ | n. |雏菊；菊科植物；一流的，极好的|A small European wild flower with a yellow centre and white petals.  A type of flower with a yellow centre and white petals.|She picked a daisy from the field. 她从田野里摘了一朵雏菊。| The performance was simply daisy. 演出简直棒极了。 | 近义词: marguerite, oxeye daisy, bellis | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Old English dægeseage, meaning "day's eye," because the flower opens in the morning and closes at night.
