lividities:/lɪˈvɪdɪtiz/
noun
尸斑，瘀斑
The post-mortem lividities were consistent with the time of death.  尸斑与死亡时间一致。
The pathologist noted the lividities on the victim's back. 病理学家注意到受害者背部的尸斑。
Synonym: hypostasis, postmortem staining
Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a physical process like lividity)
词性: noun


lividity:/lɪˈvɪdɪti/
noun
青紫色， lividity通常指死后皮肤颜色变化
The lividity of the bruise indicated a severe impact.  瘀伤的青紫色表明受到了严重的撞击。
His face was marked by a sickly lividity. 他的脸上呈现出一种病态的青紫色。
Synonym: discoloration, bruise, contusion
Antonym: pallor, healthy color
词性: noun
