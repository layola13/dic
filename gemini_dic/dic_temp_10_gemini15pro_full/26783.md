caf:/kæf/ | n. | 咖啡馆，小餐馆 | A small, informal restaurant serving simple meals and drinks, typically coffee. |  I met my friend at the caf for lunch. 我和我的朋友在咖啡馆吃午饭。 | Synonyms: bistro, coffee shop, cafe | Antonyms: restaurant (more formal) | noun


caf:/kæf/ | abbreviation |  计算机辅助教学(Computer Assisted Instruction),  |  The school is using CAF to help students learn math. 学校正在使用计算机辅助教学帮助学生学习数学。| Synonyms: CAI (Computer-Aided Instruction) | Antonyms:  traditional teaching | abbreviation


Etymology:

The word "caf" (referring to a restaurant) comes from the French word "café," which itself comes from the Turkish word "kahve," and ultimately from the Arabic word "qahwah" for coffee.  The abbreviation "CAF" for Computer Assisted Instruction is simply a shortening of the full term.
