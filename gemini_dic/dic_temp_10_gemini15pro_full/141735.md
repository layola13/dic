orana:/ɔːˈrɑːnə/| int.|你好 (用于库克群岛毛利语)|A greeting in Cook Islands Māori, meaning "hello".|例句：Ōrana, e hoa! 你好，朋友！|近义词：kia orana, hello, hi|反义词:|interjection

PHRASE->
Kia orana (a more formal greeting) = 你好 (更正式的问候)
Ōrana e hoa (hello friend) = 你好，朋友
Aere ra, ōrana (goodbye, hello [to those remaining]) = 再见，你好（对留下的人说）
Ōrana koe (hello to one person) = 你好（对一个人说）
Ōrana kōrua (hello to two people) = 你好（对两个人说）
