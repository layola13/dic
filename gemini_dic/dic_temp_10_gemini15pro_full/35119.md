cloudest:/ˈklaʊdɪst/ | adj. | 最多云的 |  Superlative form of cloudy: most cloudy.  Describes something having the greatest amount of cloud cover. | The cloudest day of the year was also the coldest.  一年中最阴天的那天也是最冷的一天。|  The hike was difficult due to the cloudest conditions we'd ever encountered. 这次徒步旅行很艰难，因为我们遇到了有史以来最多云的天气。| Synonyms: overcast, gloomy, murky | Antonyms: sunniest, clearest, brightest | adjective
