dissimulated:/dɪˈsɪmjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 掩饰的，假装的 |  Hidden or disguised; not genuine. |  Her dissimulated grief made me suspicious. 她掩饰的悲伤让我起了疑心。 | synonyms: feigned, pretended, masked, disguised | antonyms: genuine, sincere, honest | adjective
