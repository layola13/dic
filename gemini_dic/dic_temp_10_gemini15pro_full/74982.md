frictionlessly:/ˈfrɪkʃən.ləs.li/
adv.
无摩擦地，流畅地
without friction; smoothly and effortlessly.
The puck slid frictionlessly across the ice. 冰球在冰面上无摩擦地滑行。
The gears turned frictionlessly. 齿轮无摩擦地转动。
smoothly, effortlessly
roughly, with friction
adverb
