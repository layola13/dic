paginated:/ˈpædʒɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
分页的；标注页码的
divided into pages; having pages added, especially as page numbers
The document was paginated and ready to be printed.  这份文件已分页并准备打印。
The paginated report was easier to follow than the long, continuous scroll. 这份分页的报告比长长的连续滚动更容易阅读。
Near Synonyms: numbered, foliated
Antonyms: unpaginated, unnumbered
Word Origin: From the Latin word "pagina" meaning "page".
