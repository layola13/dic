double-bass:/ˈdʌbəl beɪs/ (US), /ˈdʌbəl beɪs/ (UK)| n.|低音提琴；倍大提琴|A large stringed instrument, the largest and lowest-pitched of the violin family, typically played upright with a bow.|The double bass provides the foundation of the orchestra's sound. 低音提琴构成了管弦乐队声音的基础。

The double bass player plucked the strings forcefully. 低音提琴手用力拨动琴弦。

|近义词：bass, contrabass, string bass|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps piccolo bass as a smaller related instrument)|noun
