antireflective:/ˌæntiːrɪˈflɛktɪv/
adjective
防反射的，抗反射的
Reducing or preventing reflection, especially of light or other radiation.
The camera lens has an antireflective coating.  相机镜头有防反射涂层。
Near synonyms:  nonreflective, nonreflecting
Opposite: reflective
词性: adjective
