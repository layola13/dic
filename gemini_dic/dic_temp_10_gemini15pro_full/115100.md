manganesous:/ˌmæŋɡəˈniːsəs/ | adj. |亚锰的，二价锰的 | Containing or relating to manganese, especially with a valence of 2.  It indicates that manganese is in its +2 oxidation state. |  Manganesous oxide is a light green powder. 亚锰氧化物是一种浅绿色粉末。|  Manganesous sulfate is used as a fertilizer. 硫酸亚锰用作肥料。 | 近义词: manganous (more common modern term) | 反义词: manganic (referring to manganese with a valence of 3 or 4) | 词性: adjective

词源:
From French *manganeux, derived from manganèse (manganese) + -eux (suffix denoting a lower oxidation state). Ultimately from Latin magnes (magnet, lodestone) because of the magnetic properties of pyrolusite (a manganese dioxide mineral).
