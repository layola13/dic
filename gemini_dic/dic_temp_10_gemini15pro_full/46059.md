cycloganoid: /ˌsaɪkləˈɡeɪnɔɪd/ | n. |环类化合物|A natural product characterized by a cyclic structure, often derived from isoprenoids and containing oxygen. These compounds exhibit a wide range of biological activities.|Cycloganoids are a large class of natural products with diverse biological activities. 环类化合物是一大类具有多种生物活性的天然产物。| Example:  Researchers are investigating the potential of cycloganoids as novel drug candidates. 研究人员正在研究环类化合物作为新型候选药物的潜力。| Example: This particular cycloganoid exhibits anti-inflammatory properties.  这种特殊的环类化合物具有抗炎特性。|Synonym: cyclic terpenoid, isoprenoid|Antonym:  N/A|noun
