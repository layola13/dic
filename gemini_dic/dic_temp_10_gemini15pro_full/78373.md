gesticulatory:/dʒɛsˈtɪkjʊlətɔːri/
adjective
做手势的；用手势表达的
Characterized by or given to gestures, especially dramatic ones.
She gave a gesticulatory performance, conveying her excitement through her animated hand movements. 她做了一个充满手势的表演，通过她生动的手部动作表达了她的兴奋。
The speaker's gesticulatory style kept the audience engaged. 演讲者充满手势的风格让观众保持了参与感。
synonyms:  demonstrative, expressive, animated, emphatic, theatrical
antonyms:  reserved, restrained, subdued, unemotional, impassive
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *gesticulatorius, from Latin gesticulator (“one who makes gestures”), from gesticulor (“to gesticulate”), from gesticulus (“a little gesture”), diminutive of gestus (“gesture, posture, carriage”), from gerō (“bear, carry, wear, behave, manage, wage”).
