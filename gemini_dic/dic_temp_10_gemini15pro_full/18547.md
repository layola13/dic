beneficing: /ˈbɛnɪfɪsɪŋ/
verb
授予圣俸，任命…为有俸圣职；使受益
present participle of benefice.  The act of providing someone with a benefice (a church appointment that provides income).  More generally, it can mean to provide someone with a benefit or advantage.
The king was beneficing his loyal supporters by granting them land. 国王通过授予土地来使他的忠实支持者受益。
He was beneficing the community by building a new school. 他通过建造一所新学校来造福社区。
近义词: endowing, enriching, benefiting
反义词: harming, depriving
词性: verb (present participle)
