hobbs:/hɒbz/ (UK) /hɑːbz/ (US)| n. |嗜好，消遣；（尤指）手工，手艺|A hobbie is an activity that someone does for pleasure when they are not working.|例句：His hobbies include reading and playing chess. 他的爱好包括阅读和下棋。

例句：She spends most of her free time pursuing her various hobbies. 她把大部分空闲时间都花在了各种各样的爱好上。

近义词：pastimes, interests, pursuits|反义词：work, job, profession|词性：名词
