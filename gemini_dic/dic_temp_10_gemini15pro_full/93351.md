illegality:/ˌɪliˈɡæləti/
n.
非法;违法;不法
The state of being illegal or contrary to law.
The illegality of downloading copyrighted material is often overlooked.  下载受版权保护材料的非法性经常被忽视。
Synonyms: unlawfulness, illicitness, criminality
Antonyms: legality, lawfulness, legitimacy
