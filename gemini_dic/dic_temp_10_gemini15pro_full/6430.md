amphoteric:/ˌæmfəˈtɛrɪk/ | adj. |两性的，兼性的| Able to react both as a base and as an acid. |  Aluminum hydroxide is amphoteric. 氢氧化铝是两性的。| synonyms: amphoprotic, amphiprotic | antonyms: monoprotic | adjective
amphoteric:/ˌæmfəˈtɛrɪk/ | adj. |两性的，兼性的|  Capable of acting as either an acid or a base. | Water can be amphoteric, donating or accepting a proton. 水可以是两性的，既可以给出质子，也可以接受质子。| synonyms: amphoprotic, amphiprotic | antonyms: monoprotic | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek amphoteros "both, of both kinds," from amphi- "both" + -teros "either of two."
