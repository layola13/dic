anti-whaling:/ˌæntiˈweɪlɪŋ/
adjective
反捕鲸的
Opposed to the hunting and killing of whales.
Japan's anti-whaling stance has gained international support.  日本的反对捕鲸立场获得了国际支持。
近义词:conservationist, environmentalist
反义词:pro-whaling
词性:adjective


anti-whaling:/ˌæntiˈweɪlɪŋ/
noun
反捕鲸运动
The movement opposed to the hunting and killing of whales.
The anti-whaling movement has been gaining momentum for decades. 反捕鲸运动几十年来一直在 gaining momentum.
近义词:whale conservation
反义词:whaling industry
词性:noun
