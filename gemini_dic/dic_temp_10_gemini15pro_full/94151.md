impels:/ɪmˈpɛlz/| v. |推动，驱使，激励|to drive, force, or urge (someone) to do something|His conscience impelled him to confess. 他的良心驱使他坦白。|近义词：drive, compel, force, urge, motivate, prompt|反义词：restrain, deter, discourage|词性：动词
Etymology: From Late Latin *impellere, from Latin impellō ("drive on, push against, strike against"), from in- ("in, on, against") + pellō ("drive, push").
