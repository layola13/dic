lordship:/ˈlɔːdʃɪp/| n.|阁下，领主身份，统治权|A title of respect used to address or refer to a lord.  The authority and power of a lord.|例句：Your Lordship, may I present Mr. Smith? 阁下，请允许我介绍史密斯先生。|例句：The king held lordship over the entire realm. 国王统治着整个王国。|近义词：dominion, sovereignty, authority|反义词：subservience, subjection|noun
