anchylosis:/ˌæŋkɪˈloʊsɪs/ | n. | 关节强直，僵硬 | Stiffness or fixation of a joint. |  The patient experienced anchylosis of the knee after the injury.  病人受伤后膝盖僵硬。|  Synonyms:  stiffness, rigidity, immobility | Antonyms: flexibility, mobility | noun

anchylose: /ˌæŋkɪˈloʊz/ | v. |  (使)关节强直，(使)僵硬| To become stiff or fixed at a joint. | The arthritis caused his fingers to anchylose. 关节炎导致他的手指僵硬。| Synonyms:  stiffen, rigidify | Antonyms: loosen, mobilize | verb
