additional:/əˈdɪʃ(ə)nəl/| adj.|附加的，额外的，补充的|Coming in addition to what already exists or happens; supplementary.|例句：There will be an additional charge for this service. 这项服务将收取额外费用。|近义词：extra, supplementary, further, added|反义词:basic, essential, fundamental|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *additionālis, from Latin additiō (“addition”), from addō (“add”).

USAGE->
Attributive Usage (before a noun):

The museum offers additional exhibits on the weekends. = 博物馆在周末提供额外的展览。
They provided additional funding for the project. = 他们为该项目提供了额外的资金。


Predicative Usage (after a verb like "is," "are," "was," "were"):

The information he gave was additional to what we already knew. = 他提供的信息是我们已知信息的补充。
The costs are additional and will be billed separately. = 这些费用是额外的，将单独计费。


PHRASE->
additional cost = 额外费用
additional information = 附加信息
additional resources = 额外资源
additional support = 额外支持
in addition to = 除此之外
