hypermagical:/ˌhaɪpərˈmædʒɪkəl/| adj.|超级神奇的，极度魔幻的|Exceedingly or extraordinarily magical; beyond the ordinary or usual magic.|例句：The hypermagical world of the novel captivated readers. 这部小说中超级神奇的世界深深吸引了读者。|例句：Her hypermagical abilities made her a formidable opponent. 她超级神奇的能力使她成为一个可怕的对手。|近义词：enchanted, magical, mystical, supernatural, otherworldly|反义词：mundane, ordinary, commonplace|词性:形容词

词源:  "Hyper-" (Greek origin) meaning "over, beyond, excessive" + "magical" (from "magic").  The prefix "hyper-" intensifies the meaning of "magical."
