iatrogenic:/ˌaɪətroʊˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. |医源性的，因医疗引起的|Relating to illness caused by medical examination or treatment.|The patient experienced iatrogenic complications after the surgery. 病人在手术后出现了医源性并发症。|Synonyms: doctor-induced, physician-induced, treatment-induced|Antonyms: natural, spontaneous|adjective

iatrogenesis:/ˌaɪətroʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs/ | n. |医源病，医源性疾病|The production of an abnormal state by medical or surgical treatment.|The study investigated the causes of iatrogenesis in hospitalized patients. 这项研究调查了住院病人医源病的成因。|Synonyms: medical complication, adverse effect|Antonyms: natural healing, spontaneous recovery|noun
