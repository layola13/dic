odelet:/ˈoʊdəlɛt/ | n. | 删除；删节 | An odelet is a short ode, a lyric poem typically of elaborate or irregular metrical form and expressive of exalted or enthusiastic emotion.  It can also refer to something that has been deleted or omitted. |  The editor removed the controversial odelet from the anthology. 编辑从选集中删除了有争议的短颂。 | synonym: deletion, omission, excision | antonym: addition, insertion, inclusion | noun
