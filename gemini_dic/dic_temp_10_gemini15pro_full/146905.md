overinstruction:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈstrʌkʃən/| n.|过度指导，过度指令|The act of providing excessive instruction, often to the point of hindering learning or performance.  It can lead to confusion, dependence, and a lack of independent thinking.|The overinstruction from the coach confused the players and hindered their performance. 教练的过度指导让队员们感到困惑，阻碍了他们的发挥。|Synonyms: overteaching, micromanagement|Antonyms: underinstruction, neglect|noun
