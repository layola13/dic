claire:/klɛər/ | n. | 克莱尔（女子名） | A female given name, derived from the French word meaning "clear" or "bright". | Claire is a popular name in many countries. 克莱尔在很多国家都是一个很受欢迎的名字。 | My friend Claire is a talented musician. 我的朋友克莱尔是一位才华横溢的音乐家。 | 近义词: Clara, Clare | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源:
From Old French *clairiere (“clear, bright”), from Latin clarus (“clear, bright, famous, illustrious”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kle-ro-s (“clear”). Doublet of clear.
