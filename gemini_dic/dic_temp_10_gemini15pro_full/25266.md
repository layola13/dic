buffet:/ˈbʌfɪt/ (US), /ˈbʌfɪt/ (UK)| n. vt.|自助餐；打击；搏斗；餐具柜|A meal where guests serve themselves from a variety of dishes placed on a table or sideboard.  (As a verb) To strike repeatedly and violently; batter.|例句：The hotel offers a breakfast buffet.  这家酒店提供自助早餐。|近义词：smorgasbord, spread (n.); batter, pound (vt.)|反义词: (n.) à la carte, set menu|词性:noun, verb

词源: From Old French *bufet, "a blow, a puff of wind," from bufer "to puff, blow, strike."  The meaning shifted to a sideboard where drinks were served, and later to the type of meal served from such a piece of furniture.
