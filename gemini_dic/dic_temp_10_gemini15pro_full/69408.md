feel-no-pain:/fiːl-noʊ-peɪn/ | adj. |  无痛的；麻木的；感觉不到疼痛的 |  Insensible to pain, typically as a result of medication or injury.  Literally, "does not feel pain." | The dentist gave him a feel-no-pain injection before pulling the tooth. 牙医在拔牙前给他打了一针麻醉剂。|  The boxer, pumped full of adrenaline, seemed feel-no-pain throughout the fight.  这位拳击手充满了肾上腺素，在整场比赛中似乎感觉不到疼痛。 | synonyms: numb, anesthetized, insensible | antonyms: sensitive, painful | adjective
