apatosauruses:/əˌpæt.əˈsɔːr.əs.ɪz/| n. |迷惑龙（复数）|A very large herbivorous dinosaur of the Late Jurassic period, with a long neck and tail.  It was formerly called Brontosaurus.|例句：Apatosauruses were among the largest animals to ever walk the Earth. 迷惑龙是曾经在地球上行走的最大的动物之一。

例句：Scientists now know that apatosauruses held their necks horizontally, not vertically.  科学家现在知道迷惑龙的脖子是水平的，而不是垂直的。|近义词：brontosaurus, sauropod|反义词: N/A (no opposite for a specific type of dinosaur)|词性:noun
