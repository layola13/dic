call-back:/ˈkɔːlbæk/ (US) /ˈkɔːlˌbæk/ (UK)| n.|回电；召回；复试；回调函数|A request that someone return a telephone call; a return telephone call;  a request that someone return to a place, such as a doctor's office, for further examination or discussion; in computer programming, a function that is passed as an argument to another function and is executed later.| I missed his call, so I'll give him a call-back. 我错过了他的电话，所以我会给他回个电话。|近义词：return call, recall, follow-up|反义词:|noun

call-back:/ˈkɔːlbæk/ (US) /ˈkɔːlˌbæk/ (UK)| adj.|召回的；应召回的|Relating to or involving a recall or return.|The company issued a call-back for the faulty products. 公司对有缺陷的产品发布了召回令。|近义词：recalled|反义词:|adjective
