ellipsoid:/ɪˈlɪpsɔɪd/ | n. |椭球体，椭圆体 |A three-dimensional shape resembling a stretched or compressed sphere, all of whose plane sections are ellipses or circles. |The Earth is not a perfect sphere, but rather an ellipsoid. 地球不是一个完美的球体，而是一个椭球体。| Earth is an oblate ellipsoid, bulging slightly at the equator. 地球是一个扁椭球体，在赤道略微凸起。 | synonyms: spheroid, oval  | antonyms:  | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *ellipsoïde*, from *ellipse* + *-oïde* (from Greek *eîdos* “form, shape”).
