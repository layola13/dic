halcyon:/ˈhælsiən/| adj.|平静的；宁静的；幸福的|Characterized by happiness, great success, and prosperity; peaceful and tranquil.  Referring to a mythical bird, the kingfisher, believed to calm the seas while nesting on them.|例句：The halcyon days of summer.  夏日宁静的日子。|例句：She recalled the halcyon days of her youth. 她回忆起她年轻时平静幸福的日子。|近义词：peaceful, tranquil, serene, golden, blissful|反义词：turbulent, chaotic, troubled, tumultuous|词性:adjective

halcyon:/ˈhælsiən/| n.|翡翠鸟；太平鸟|A mythical bird, identified with the kingfisher, that was said to breed in a nest floating on the sea and to have the power of calming the winds and waves while it was brooding.|例句：The halcyon is a legendary bird. 翡翠鸟是一种传说中的鸟。|例句：Sailors believed in the power of the halcyon to calm the seas.  水手们相信翡翠鸟有让大海平静下来的力量。|近义词：kingfisher|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
