diasporic:/ˌdaɪəˈspɒrɪk/ /ˌdaɪəˈspɔːrɪk/| adj.|散居的；离散的|Relating to or characteristic of a diaspora; dispersed from one's homeland.|例句：The festival celebrates diasporic communities and their cultures. 这个节日庆祝散居社群及其文化。

例句：Diasporic literature often explores themes of displacement and belonging.  离散文学经常探讨流离失所和归属的主题。|近义词：dispersed, scattered, exiled, migrant|反义词：indigenous, native, local|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *diaspora, from Greek *diaspora "a scattering, dispersion," from diaspeirein "to scatter," from dia- "across, through" + speirein "to sow, scatter."
