over-feeling:/ˌoʊvərˈfiːlɪŋ/
adjective
过度感性的，过于情绪化的
Excessively emotional or sentimental; prone to strong emotional reactions.
She's a bit over-feeling and cries at everything. 她有点过度感性，什么事都能让她哭。
He gave an over-feeling performance, which some critics found unconvincing.  他表演得过于情绪化，一些评论家觉得不太可信。
近义词:oversensitive, sentimental, emotional
反义词:unemotional, stoic, apathetic
词性:adjective

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "feeling" (noun).  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "feeling," suggesting an excess of emotion.
