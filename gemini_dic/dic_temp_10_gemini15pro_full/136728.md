nonsensicality:/ˌnɒnsɛnsɪˈkælɪti/ /ˌnɑːnsɛnsɪˈkælɪti/
n.
胡说八道，荒谬
the quality of being nonsensical; absurdity.
His speech was filled with utter nonsensicality. 他的演讲充满了胡说八道。
近义词：absurdity, foolishness, illogicality, irrationality
反义词：sense, logic, rationality
词性: noun

nonsensical:/nɒnˈsɛnsɪk(ə)l/ /nɑːnˈsɛnsɪk(ə)l/
adj.
荒谬的，无意义的
not making sense; absurd.
The movie's plot was nonsensical. 这部电影的情节荒谬至极。
近义词：absurd, illogical, irrational, meaningless
反义词：sensible, logical, rational, meaningful
词性: adjective


词源:
From nonsensical + -ity.  Ultimately from Latin *non-sensicus "not pertaining to sense,"  from non- "not" + sensus "sense, feeling, thought."
