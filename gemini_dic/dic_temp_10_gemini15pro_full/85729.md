hawk:/hɔːk/ (US) /hɔːk/ (UK)| n. vt.|鹰, 贩卖, 清嗓|A bird of prey with broad rounded wings and a long tail, typically taking other birds and small mammals as prey.  To sell or offer goods aggressively, especially by going from place to place. To clear the throat noisily.|例句：The hawk soared high above the valley. 老鹰在山谷上空翱翔。|近义词：falcon, eagle, peddle, vend|反义词:dove (symbolically), buy|词性:noun, verb
