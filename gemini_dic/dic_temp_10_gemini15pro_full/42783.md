couped:/kuːpt/ /kuːpt/| adj.|被切断的，截断的；(纹章) 底部平齐切断的|A couped cross is one with its arms cut short. 截断十字架是臂部被截短的十字架。|The lion rampant couped is a heraldic charge depicting a lion standing erect with its lower half cut off horizontally.  截断直立狮是纹章上的一种图案，描绘了一只直立的狮子，下半身被水平切断。|近义词：truncated, cut off, severed|反义词:complete, whole|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old French *coupé*, past participle of *couper* "to cut," from Latin *copāre* “to strike, chop, prune.”
