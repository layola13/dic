oncotomy: /ɒŋˈkɒtəmi/ /ɑːŋˈkɑːtəmi/
noun
肿瘤切开术
The surgical incision of a tumor or swelling.
Example: The surgeon performed an oncotomy to biopsy the suspicious mass.  外科医生进行了肿瘤切开术以活检可疑肿块。
Example: Oncotomy was necessary to determine the nature of the growth.  需要进行肿瘤切开术来确定肿瘤的性质。
Synonyms: incision, biopsy
Antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek  ὄγκος (ónkos, “mass, bulk, tumour”) + -τομία (-tomía, “cutting”), from τέμνω (témnō, “I cut”).
