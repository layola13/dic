ETYMOLOGY->
From graphic + -ness.  "Graphic" originates from the Greek word "graphikos," meaning "of or for writing, drawn, painted," related to "graphein," meaning "to write, draw, paint."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns indicating a state, quality, or condition.
