firths:/fɜːrθs/
n.
狭窄的海湾，河口
A firth is a long, narrow inlet of the sea, especially in Scotland.
The boats sailed into the firth. 船只驶入了海湾。
近义词：inlet, estuary, bay
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 open sea)
词性: 名词


firths:/fɜːrθs/
n.
(古语)树林，森林 (主要指冷杉林)
An area of wooded land, especially fir trees (archaic usage).
The deer roamed through the ancient firths. 鹿在古老的森林中漫步。(古语用法，现在很少使用)
近义词：forest, woods, woodland
反义词: (没有直接的反义词)
词性: 名词
