nonforeclosure:/ˌnɑːnfɔːrˈkloʊʒər/ | n. | 非丧失抵押品赎回权 | The state of not having a property seized by a lender due to failure to make loan payments.  It means the borrower still owns the property and has not lost it to foreclosure. |  The homeowner was relieved to be in a state of nonforeclosure after successfully modifying their loan.  房主在成功修改贷款后，对没有丧失抵押品赎回权感到宽慰。

The program aims to maintain homeowners in nonforeclosure by providing financial assistance. 该计划旨在通过提供财政援助来维持房主不丧失抵押品赎回权。| 近义词: loan modification, debt restructuring, forbearance | 反义词: foreclosure | 词性: noun
