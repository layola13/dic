lesion:/ˈliːʒən/| n. |损伤；病变；损害|An area of abnormal tissue; a region in an organ or tissue that has suffered damage through injury or disease. |The doctor discovered a lesion on her lung. 医生在她的肺部发现了一处病变。|  The MRI revealed a lesion in his brain. 核磁共振成像显示他的大脑中有一个病灶。| synonym: wound, injury, sore, ulcer, abscess | antonym: healing, recovery | noun
