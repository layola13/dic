outpowered:/ˌaʊtˈpaʊərd/
Verb
击败，胜过，以力量胜过
defeated by having more power; overwhelmed.
Our team was completely outpowered by the opponents. 我们的队伍被对手彻底击败了。
The small nation was quickly outpowered by its larger neighbor.  这个小国很快就被它更大的邻国击败了。
overpowered, defeated, conquered, overwhelmed
surpassed, outmatched
verb
