bolsterer:/ˈboʊlstərər/| n.|支持者，增强者|Someone or something that bolsters or strengthens something else. |The government's financial aid package acted as a bolsterer for the struggling economy. 政府的财政援助计划对陷入困境的经济起到了支撑作用。| The new advertising campaign was a bolsterer for sales. 新的广告宣传活动促进了销售。|近义词：supporter, strengthener, reinforcer|反义词：weakener, deterrent|词性:noun

bolster:/ˈboʊlstər/| v. n.| 支撑；增强；长枕| (v.) To support or strengthen; (n.) a long, thick pillow that is placed under other pillows for support. | (v.)  Extra funding will bolster the research project. 额外的资金将支持该研究项目。 (n.) She propped herself up with a bolster while reading in bed. 她读书时用长枕支撑着自己。 |近义词：(v.) support, reinforce, strengthen; (n.) cushion, pillow|反义词：(v.) weaken, undermine|词性:verb, noun
