delightous: /dɪˈlaɪtəs/ | adj. | 令人愉悦的，美味的 | Delightful; highly pleasing.  This word is considered nonstandard or informal, though some dictionaries list it.  It likely arose from a blend of "delicious" and "delightful". | The chef prepared a delightous meal. 厨师准备了一顿美味的佳肴。 | 近义词: delicious, delectable, enjoyable, pleasant | 反义词: unpleasant, disagreeable, unappetizing, disgusting | 词性: 形容词
