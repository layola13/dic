contingency:/kənˈtɪndʒənsi/ | n. | 偶然性，意外事故，可能性 | a future event or circumstance which is possible but cannot be predicted with certainty |  The company has a contingency plan in case of a fire. 公司制定了火灾应急预案。| synonyms: possibility, eventuality, chance, accident, incident | antonyms: certainty, inevitability | noun

contingent:/kənˈtɪndʒənt/ | adj. |  偶然的，依情况而定的；可能的 | occurring or existing only if (certain other circumstances) are the case; dependent on | Payment is contingent upon delivery of the goods. 付款取决于货物的交付。| synonyms: conditional, dependent, subject to, provisional | antonyms: certain, unconditional, independent | adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *contingentia, from Latin contingere "to touch, happen," from com- "together" + tangere "to touch."
