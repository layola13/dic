calumba:/kəˈlʌmbə/
n.
卡伦巴根，金鸡纳霜
A bitter tonic made from the root of the calumba plant.
Calumba is traditionally used to treat digestive problems. 卡伦巴传统上用于治疗消化问题。
Calumba root is known for its bitter taste and is used in herbal medicine. 卡伦巴根以其苦味而闻名，并用于草药。
近义词:colombo root,  bitter tonic

词性:noun
