overjealousness:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒɛləsnəs/| n. |过度嫉妒，过度的妒忌|The state of being excessively jealous.  It implies an unhealthy and often irrational level of suspicion and possessiveness.|Her overjealousness was putting a strain on their relationship. 她的过度嫉妒使他们的关系紧张。|近义词：possessiveness, envy, suspicion, distrust|反义词:trust, security, confidence|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + jealousness. "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much." "Jealousness" comes from the Old French "jalos," meaning "zealous," or "passionate," later evolving to mean "envious" and "suspicious."
