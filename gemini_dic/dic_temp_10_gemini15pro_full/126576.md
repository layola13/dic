naffest:/ˈnæfɪst/
adjective
最俗气的，最没品味的
Superlative form of "naff":  of poor taste or quality; unfashionable.  It expresses the highest degree of naffness.
That's the naffest wallpaper I've ever seen. 那是我见过的最俗气的壁纸。
Synonyms: tackiest, cheesiest, gaudiest, most tasteless
Antonyms: most stylish, most fashionable, most elegant, classiest
词性: adjective
