friending:/ˈfrɛndɪŋ/
n.
交友，加为好友
The act of making someone a friend, especially on a social networking website.
She’s been busy friending people on Facebook. 她一直忙着在脸书上加好友。
近义词：befriending, connecting
反义词：unfriending, blocking
词性：名词

Etymology:  From friend + -ing (present participle suffix).  Relatively recent, popularized with the rise of social media.
