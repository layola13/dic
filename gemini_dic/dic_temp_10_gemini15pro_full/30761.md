cessavit:/sɛˈseɪvɪt/
verb
停止；中断；终止(尤指封建时代因未缴纳租金而丧失土地)
(Law, historical) A writ that lay where the tenant of a particular estate ceased or neglected to perform the services due from him to the lord, and had not upon his lands or tenements distrainable whereby the lord might have recompense of those services.
The lord issued a writ of cessavit against the tenant. 领主对佃户发出了停止令。
近义词: cease, discontinue, stop, halt
反义词: continue, resume, start, begin
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cessavit*, third-person singular perfect active indicative of *cessare* ("to cease, stop, remain idle, be unoccupied, linger, loiter, tarry").
