clingingly:/ˈklɪŋɪŋli/| adv.|粘着地，依附地，紧贴地|In a way that clings or holds on tightly, often in an emotional or dependent manner.  It implies excessive closeness or attachment, sometimes to the point of being suffocating or unwelcome.|She held onto his arm clingingly. 她紧紧地抓住他的胳膊。|近义词：tenaciously, persistently, desperately|反义词：loosely, independently, detachedly|词性:副词

clingingly:/ˈklɪŋɪŋli/| adv.|执着地，坚持地|Persistently and stubbornly; refusing to let go, either physically or emotionally.|He clingingly held onto the belief that he would be successful. 他执着地坚信他会成功。|近义词：doggedly, stubbornly, resolutely|反义词：easily, readily, willingly|词性:副词
