derring-doer:/ˈdɛrɪŋˈduːər/
n.
大胆冒险的人；勇士
A person who performs daring or adventurous deeds; a brave person.
He was renowned as a derring-doer, always ready for a challenge. 他以大胆冒险而闻名，总是准备好迎接挑战。
Synonyms: adventurer, daredevil, swashbuckler, hero
Antonyms: coward, timid person
noun

Etymology:
From derring-do + -er.  Derring-do itself comes from Middle English *daring do*, literally "daring to do."
