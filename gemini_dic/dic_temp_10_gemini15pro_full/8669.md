anticonsumers:/ˌæntiˈkənˈsuːmərz/
|n.|反消费者主义者；抵制消费主义的人|People who actively resist or reject consumerism, often by reducing their consumption, boycotting certain products or companies, or advocating for sustainable and ethical consumption practices.|Anticonsumers believe that rampant consumerism is harmful to the environment and society. 反消费主义者认为猖獗的消费主义对环境和社会有害。| The anticonsumers protested the opening of the new mega-mall. 反消费主义者抗议新的大型购物中心的开业。 |Near synonyms:  anti-consumerists, conscious consumers, ethical consumers, minimalist, downshifter|Antonyms: consumerists, materialists|Noun
