missionary: /ˈmɪʃənəri/ (US) /ˈmɪʃənəri/ (UK)| n. adj.|传教士，传教的|A person sent on a religious mission, especially one sent to promote Christianity in a foreign country. Also, relating to or characteristic of missionaries or their work.|例句：He spent twenty years as a missionary in Africa. 他在非洲做了二十年的传教士。

例句：She comes from a missionary family. 她来自一个传教士家庭。|近义词：apostle, evangelist, preacher|反义词: nonbeliever, heathen|词性: noun, adjective


Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin *missionarius, from missio ‘sending’ (see mission).
