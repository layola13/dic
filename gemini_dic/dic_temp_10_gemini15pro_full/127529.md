necromancy: /ˈnɛkrəˌmænsi/ | n. | 死灵法术；巫术 |  The practice of communicating with the dead, especially in order to predict the future or to influence the course of events.  It often involves rituals and spells. |  She was accused of practicing necromancy. 她被指控 practicing necromancy. | 近义词: sorcery, witchcraft, magic, black magic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly "piety" or "holiness" as contrasting concepts) | 词性: noun

necromancies: /ˈnɛkrəˌmænsiz/ | n. | 死灵法术；巫术（necromancy的复数形式）|  Plural form of necromancy, referring to multiple instances or types of communicating with the dead for magical purposes. |  Ancient texts detailed various necromancies practiced throughout history. 古代文献详细记载了历史上各种各样的死灵法术。 | 近义词: sorceries, witchcrafts, magics (though these plurals are less common) | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
