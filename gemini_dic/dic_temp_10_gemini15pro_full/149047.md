owner:/ˈoʊnər (American English) / ˈəʊnə(r) (British English)| n.|所有者，物主|A person or entity that possesses or holds legal title to something.|例句：The owner of the car is my neighbor. 这辆车的主人是我的邻居。

例句：The dog wagged its tail when it saw its owner. 狗看到主人后摇着尾巴。


近义词：proprietor, possessor, holder|反义词: renter, tenant, lessee |词性: noun
