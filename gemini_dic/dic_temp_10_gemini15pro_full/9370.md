It is a derivative of pyrazolone.| Antipyrine was commonly used to treat fevers in the past. 安替比林过去常用于治疗发烧。| Antipyrine was prescribed to reduce the patient's high temperature.  医生开了安替比林来降低病人的高烧。 |Synonyms: phenazone, analgesic, antipyretic| Antonyms: pyrogen| noun
