ablactation: /ˌæblækˈteɪʃən/
noun
断奶；戒奶
The process of weaning a young mammal from its mother's milk.
The ablactation of the calf was completed successfully.  这头小牛的断奶过程顺利完成。
synonyms: weaning, cessation of breastfeeding
antonyms: lactation, breastfeeding
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ablactatio*, from ab- + lactatio (“suckling, nursing”), from lacto (“to suckle, to nurse”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵlákts (“milk”).
