hemistichal:/ˌhemɪˈstɪkəl/
adjective
半行的，半诗行的
Relating to or consisting of a hemistich (half a line of verse).
The poem is written in hemistichal form, with each line broken in the middle. 这首诗是以半诗行的形式写成的，每一行都在中间断开。
近义词: half-line
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "full-line" or "complete line")
词性: adjective
