bobtailed:/ˈbɒbˌteɪld/ (US) /ˈbɒbˌteɪld/ (UK)| adj.|短尾的，截尾的|Having a tail that has been docked or naturally short.|The farmer had a bobtailed sheepdog. 那農夫有一隻短尾牧羊犬。|Synonyms: docked, short-tailed, stub-tailed|Antonyms: long-tailed|Adjective

bobtailed:/ˈbɒbˌteɪld/ (US) /ˈbɒbˌteɪld/ (UK)| n.|短尾动物|An animal with a docked or naturally short tail.|The bobtailed cat was very playful.  那隻短尾貓很愛玩耍。|Synonyms:  stubby|Antonyms: long-tailed|Noun
