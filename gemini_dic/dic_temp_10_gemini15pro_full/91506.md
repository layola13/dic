hymnography:/hɪmˈnɒɡrəfi/
n.
赞美诗学；赞美诗集
The study or writing of hymns.  A collection of hymns.
The professor specialized in hymnography, exploring the rich history of religious song.  教授专门研究赞美诗学，探索宗教歌曲的丰富历史。
The new church published its own hymnography containing both traditional and modern hymns. 这座新教堂出版了自己的赞美诗集，其中包含传统和现代的赞美诗。
Synonyms: hymnology
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "secular music literature")
