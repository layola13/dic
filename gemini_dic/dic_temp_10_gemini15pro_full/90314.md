housewifeliness:/ˈhaʊswɪflinəs/
n.|家庭主妇气质，贤妻良母风范|The quality of being a good housewife, encompassing skills like cooking, cleaning, and managing a household.  It also often implies a traditional and domestic focus.|She exuded housewifeliness, always ensuring a tidy home and delicious meals. 她浑身散发着贤妻良母的气质，总是把家里打扫得干干净净，饭菜也做得可口。|近义词：domesticity, homemaking|反义词：careerism, worldliness|词性: noun

Etymology:
housewife + -li- + -ness

housewife (n.):  From Middle English *huswif, from Old English hūsƿīf (“housewife”), equivalent to house +‎ wife.
-li- (suffix):  Forms adjectives meaning "like, resembling," etc.
-ness (suffix): Forms nouns from adjectives, expressing state, quality, or condition.
