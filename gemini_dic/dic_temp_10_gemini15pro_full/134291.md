noninherited:/ˌnɒnɪnˈherɪtɪd/
adjective
非遗传的，非继承的
Not inherited from a parent or ancestor; acquired.
例句：The disease is noninherited and caused by environmental factors.  这种疾病不是遗传的，是由环境因素引起的。

例句：Noninherited traits, such as learned behaviors, are not passed down through genes.  非遗传性状，例如学到的行为，不会通过基因遗传。

近义词：acquired, non-hereditary
反义词：inherited, hereditary, genetic
词性：adjective
