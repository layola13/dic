hypertense: /ˌhaɪpərˈtens/ | adj. | 高血压的，紧张的 |  Having abnormally high blood pressure.  Characterized by or tending to cause hypertension.  Also, excessively tense; strained. |  Example: The doctor diagnosed him as hypertense. 医生诊断他患有高血压。

Example:  The hypertense atmosphere in the meeting room was palpable. 会议室里紧张的气氛是显而易见的。 | 近义词: hypertensive, high-strung, stressed | 反义词: hypotensive, relaxed, calm | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *hyperēnsus, from Ancient Greek ὑπέρ (hupér, “over, above”) + Latin tēnsus (past participle of tendere, "to stretch, extend").
