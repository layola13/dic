mold's:/moʊldz/
noun
霉菌的; 模具的
belonging to or associated with mold (a furry growth of minute fungal hyphae occurring typically in moist warm conditions, especially on food or other organic matter).
This cheese has a distinct mold's flavor. 这奶酪有一种独特的霉菌味道。
近义词: fungus's, mildew's
反义词: (No direct antonym for a possessive form)
词性: possessive noun
