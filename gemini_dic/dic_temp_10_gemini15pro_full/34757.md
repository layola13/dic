cliffhangers: /ˈklɪfhæŋərz/
|n.|悬念，扣人心弦的结局|A plot device that leaves the audience in suspense, usually at the end of an episode or chapter, wanting to know what happens next.|The season finale ended on a real cliffhanger, leaving viewers wondering if the main character would survive.|Synonyms: suspense, cliffhanger ending, unresolved ending|Antonyms: resolution, closure|Noun
