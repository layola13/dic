free-falls:/ˈfriː fɔːlz/
verb (intransitive)|自由落体|To fall freely under the influence of gravity, typically without a parachute.|The skydiver free-falls for a few seconds before opening his parachute. 跳伞员在打开降落伞前自由落体了几秒钟。|plummets, descends rapidly|ascends, rises|v.
free-falls:/ˈfriː fɔːlz/
noun (plural)|自由落体|The act or process of free-falling.|The free-falls of the bungee jumpers were thrilling to watch. 观看蹦极者的自由落体令人激动。|plummets, descents|ascents, rises|n.
