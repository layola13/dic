gimmer:/ˈɡɪmər/ | n. | 母羊; 小母羊 | A young female sheep, especially one that has not yet lambed. |例句：The farmer proudly showed off his prize-winning gimmer. 农民自豪地展示了他获奖的小母羊。

例句：The gimmer bleated loudly when separated from its mother.  小母羊与母羊分开时大声咩咩叫。

近义词：ewe lamb, female lamb | 反义词: ram | 词性: noun
