假设你想了解单词 "what" 的用法，以下提供其解析：

what: /wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American) |pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么 |Used to ask for information specifying something. | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？ | What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！ | similar: which, who | opposite: N/A | pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你觉得怎么样？
What does he want for his birthday? = 他生日想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is she doing this weekend? = 她这周末要做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has he been up to lately? = 他最近在忙什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? = 你一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading lately? = 她最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had happened before the police came? = 警察来之前发生了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the accident? = 事故发生前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? = 他们一直在争论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
What will the weather be like next week? = 下周天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting? = 会议上他们会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by next year? = 到明年你会完成什么？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? = 到2033年你将已经做这件事十年了？
What will she have been studying by graduation? = 到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？
