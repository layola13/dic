cycloscope: /ˈsaɪkləskoʊp/ | n. | 环视镜；电影放映机；（早期）电影放映机；电影放映机 | An early form of motion-picture projector.  A device that allows viewing of images in a cyclical sequence. | Example: The cycloscope projected flickering images onto the wall, captivating the audience. 环视镜将闪烁的图像投射到墙上，吸引了观众。 Example: The inventor demonstrated his new cycloscope to a crowd of eager onlookers.  发明家向一群热切的旁观者展示了他的新环视镜。 | 近义词: kinetoscope, projector, cinematograph | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "still image viewer") | 词性: noun
