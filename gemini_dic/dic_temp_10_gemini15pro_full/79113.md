gladding:/ˈɡlædɪŋ/| adj.| 令人高兴的；使人愉快的 | Something that is gladdening brings joy or pleasure. | The news of her recovery was gladdening.  她的康复消息令人高兴。| synonyms: cheering, heartening, joyous, delightful | antonyms: saddening, depressing, disheartening | adjective

gladden:/ˈɡlædn/| vt.| 使高兴；使愉快 | To gladden someone is to make them feel happy or joyful. | The sunshine gladdened my heart. 阳光使我心情愉悦。| synonyms: cheer, hearten, delight, please | antonyms: sadden, depress, discourage | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *glæddian, from glæd "glad."
