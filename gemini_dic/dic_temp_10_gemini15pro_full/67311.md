ecstasy: /ˈɛkstəsi/ (US) /ˈɛkstəsi/ (UK)| n.| 狂喜；兴高采烈；入迷；忘形|An overwhelming feeling of great happiness or joyful excitement.|She was in an ecstasy of delight when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息欣喜若狂。|Synonyms: bliss, rapture, euphoria, elation|Antonyms: despair, misery, depression|noun

ecstasy: /ˈɛkstəsi/ (US) /ˈɛkstəsi/ (UK)| n.|  (尤指宗教的) 神魂颠倒，忘我状态|A state of being completely absorbed and lost in something, especially a religious experience.|The mystic experienced a state of ecstasy during meditation.  这位神秘主义者在冥想中体验到了一种忘我的状态。|Synonyms: trance, rapture|Antonyms: awareness, consciousness|noun
