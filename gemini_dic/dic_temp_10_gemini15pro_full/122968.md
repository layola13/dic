monadal:/məˈnæd(ə)l/ | adj. | 单子的， монадический (Russian) | Of or relating to a monad.  Relating to a single unit or entity.  In computer science, pertaining to monads, a structure that represents computations defined as sequences of steps. |  Monadal transformers are powerful tools in functional programming. 单子变换器是函数式编程中的强大工具。  The monadal nature of the system allows for isolated components. 系统的单子特性允许隔离的组件。 | 近义词: singular, individual, unitary | 反义词: dyadic, plural | 词性: adjective

词源: From monad + -al.  Ultimately from Greek μονάς monas (“unit”).
