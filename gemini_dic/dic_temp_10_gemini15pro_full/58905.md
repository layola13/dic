durit:/ˈdjʊərɪt/ | n. |硬质合金|A very hard alloy of tungsten carbide and cobalt, used for cutting tools.|The drill bit was made of durit for extra strength.  钻头由硬质合金制成，以增加强度。|Synonyms: cemented carbide, tungsten carbide|Antonyms: soft metal|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Durit*, a trade name. Ultimately from Latin *dūrus* ("hard").
