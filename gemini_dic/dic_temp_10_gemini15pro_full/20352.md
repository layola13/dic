bismuthine:/ˈbɪzməθiːn/
noun
铋化氢
A colorless, flammable, toxic gas (BiH3) analogous to arsine and stibine.
Bismuthine decomposes readily to bismuth and hydrogen. 铋化氢很容易分解成铋和氢。
Synonym: bismuth hydride
Antonym: N/A
词性: noun

bismuthine:/ˈbɪzməθiːn/
noun
辉铋矿
A rare mineral consisting of bismuth sulfide (Bi2S3).
Bismuthine is a source of bismuth. 辉铋矿是铋的来源。
Synonym: bismuthinite, bismuth glance
Antonym: N/A
词性: noun
