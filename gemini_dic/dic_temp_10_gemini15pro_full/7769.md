ansated:/ˈænseɪtɪd/
adjective
有柄的，有把手的
Having a handle or handles.
The ansated cup was easy to hold.  这个有柄的杯子很容易拿。
近义词: handled, ear-handled
反义词: handleless
词性: adjective

ansated:/ˈænseɪtɪd/
adjective
(尤指十字架)十字形的，形如安萨塔十字的(古埃及生命象征符号,形如T字顶端加一环)
(especially of a cross) having a handle or loop at the top, like an ankh.
The archaeologist discovered an ansated cross in the tomb. 考古学家在墓中发现了一个安萨塔十字。
近义词: ankhed, looped
反义词: (No direct antonym, could use terms like "unlooped," "simple cross")
词性: adjective
