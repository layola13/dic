epithetically:/ˌepɪˈθetɪkli/
adv.
用绰号地，加绰号地
in a way that uses or involves an epithet
He was epithetically referred to as "The Great Emancipator." 他被加绰号地称为“伟大的解放者”。
Synonyms: descriptively, attributively
Antonyms: plainly, simply
词性: 副词


epithet:/ˈepɪθet/
n.
绰号，称号
an adjective or descriptive phrase expressing a quality characteristic of the person or thing mentioned.
"Richard the Lionheart" is an epithet. “狮心王理查”是一个称号。
Synonyms: nickname, sobriquet, moniker
Antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词
