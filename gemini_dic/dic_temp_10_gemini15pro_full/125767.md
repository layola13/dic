murderous:/ˈmɜːrdərəs/| adj.|凶残的，杀人的，蓄意谋杀的|Characterized by or involving murder; having the characteristics of a murderer; extremely dangerous or destructive.|The dictator's murderous regime was responsible for countless deaths.  这个独裁者的残暴政权造成了无数人的死亡。

The storm brought murderous winds that ripped trees from the ground.  暴风雨带来了狂风，把树木连根拔起。|近义词： homicidal, deadly, lethal, savage, brutal|反义词： peaceful, gentle, harmless|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *murdrouse*, from Anglo-Norman *murdrus*, from Old French *murdre* (murder) + *-ous*. Ultimately from Latin *mort-*, past participle stem of *morī* (to die).
