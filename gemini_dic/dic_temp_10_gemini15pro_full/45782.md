cusps:/kʌsps/
n.
尖端，尖头；峰值；交点
a pointed end where two curves meet.  The cusp of a wave.  The cusps of a tooth.
The moon reached its cusp last night. 昨晚月亮到达了它的顶峰。
The two lines meet at a cusp.  这两条线相交于一个尖点。
point, tip, apex, peak
nadir, bottom, base
noun

cusp:/kʌsp/
n.
分界线，临界点
a point of transition between two different states. On the cusp of adulthood. On the cusp of a new era.
He's on the cusp of a major breakthrough in his research. 他的研究正处于重大突破的边缘。
She stood on the cusp of a new life. 她站在新生活的起点。
verge, brink, threshold, edge
center, middle, heart
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin cuspis (point, spear).
