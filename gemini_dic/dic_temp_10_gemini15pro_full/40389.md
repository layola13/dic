contradistinct:/ˌkɒntrədɪˈstɪŋkt/ | adj. | 截然不同的，对比鲜明的 | Clearly distinguished or contrasted. | The two approaches are contradistinct. 这两种方法截然不同。| 近义词: different, distinct, separate, diverse | 反义词: similar, alike, identical | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *contradistinctus*, past participle of *contradistinguere*, from *contra-* (against) + *distinguere* (to distinguish).
