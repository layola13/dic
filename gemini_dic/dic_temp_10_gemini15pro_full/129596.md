non-compensation:/ˌnɒnˌkɒmpenˈseɪʃn/ | n. | 无偿；不赔偿；无补偿 | Absence of payment or reimbursement for goods, services, or losses. |  Example: The interns were offered non-compensation internships, gaining experience but no salary.  实习生获得了无薪实习机会，获得了经验但没有工资。

Example:  He demanded non-compensation for the emotional distress caused by the incident.  他要求对事件造成的情感困扰不予赔偿。 | Synonyms: non-payment, no remuneration,  gratuitous service | Antonyms: compensation, remuneration, payment | noun
