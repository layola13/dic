dithecal: /daɪˈθiːkəl/
adjective
双药囊的，有两个药囊的
Having two thecae or sacs, especially referring to an anther that has two pollen sacs.
The dithecal anther is common among flowering plants.  双药囊花药在开花植物中很常见。
Stamens with dithecal anthers are characteristic of this species.  具有双药囊花药的雄蕊是该物种的特征。
synonym: two-celled (referring to anthers)
antonym: monothecal
