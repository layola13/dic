dust-up:/ˈdʌstʌp/| n.|争吵，小冲突，打架|A brief fight or argument; a commotion.|例句：There was a bit of a dust-up between the two teams after the game.  比赛结束后两队之间发生了一些小冲突。

例句：They had a dust-up over who would get the last slice of pizza. 他们为了谁吃最后一片披萨而吵了一架。|近义词：quarrel, squabble, brawl, altercation, spat, tiff|反义词：agreement, harmony, peace|词性:名词

词源：Likely originated in the American West, referring to the cloud of dust raised by a fight or a sudden commotion.  可能起源于美国西部，指的是打架或突然骚动扬起的尘土。
