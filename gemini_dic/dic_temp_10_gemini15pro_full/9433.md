antirealisms:/ˌæntiˈriːəlɪzəmz/
n.
反现实主义
The philosophical doctrine that rejects realism, especially in relation to the external world or morality.  It asserts that reality is dependent on the mind or language.
Philosophers debate various forms of antirealisms. 哲学家们争论各种形式的反现实主义。
Many artists embraced antirealisms in their work. 许多艺术家在他们的作品中拥护反现实主义。
近义词:irrealism, idealism
反义词:realism
词性:名词
