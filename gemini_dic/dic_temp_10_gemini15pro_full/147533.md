overpatient:/ˈoʊvərˌpeɪʃənt/| adj.| 过于耐心的，过分容忍的 | Excessively patient; more patient than is usual or necessary.  Showing too much patience or tolerance. | He was overpatient with his unruly children. 他对他不守规矩的孩子们过于耐心了。|  The teacher was overpatient with the disruptive student. 老师对那个捣乱的学生过于宽容了。| 近义词: forbearing, tolerant, indulgent | 反义词: impatient, intolerant | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "over-" (excessively) + "patient" (bearing pains or trials without complaint).
