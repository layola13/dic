bismar:/ˈbɪzmɑːr/ | n. |俾斯麦，俾斯麦号|Bismarck is the surname of Otto von Bismarck, the first Chancellor of the German Empire. It is also the name of a German battleship, the Bismarck. | Otto von Bismarck was the first Chancellor of Germany. 奥托·冯·俾斯麦是德国的第一任总理。

The Bismarck was a formidable battleship. 俾斯麦号是一艘强大的战列舰。 | 近义词: (For the person) statesman, chancellor; (For the ship) battleship, warship | 反义词: N/A | 词性: Noun

Etymology: From the German surname Bismarck, derived from the place name Biscop(es)mark (Bishop's boundary marker).
