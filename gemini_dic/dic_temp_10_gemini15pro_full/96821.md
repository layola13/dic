inescapablenesses:/ˌɪnɪˈskeɪpəbəlnəsɪz/
noun
不可避免性；无法逃避性
The quality or state of being inescapable.  The inescapableness of fate/destiny.
The inescapablenesses of aging are something we all must face. 我们都必须面对衰老的不可避免性。
Synonyms: inevitability, unavoidability, certainty
Antonyms: escapability, avoidability, uncertainty
词性: noun
