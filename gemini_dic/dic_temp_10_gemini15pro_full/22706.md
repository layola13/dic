boreholes:/ˈbɔːrhəʊlz/ (UK) /ˈbɔːrhoʊlz/ (US)| n. |钻孔，井孔|A hole drilled into the ground, especially to extract water, oil, or gas, or for geological investigation. |例句：Several boreholes were drilled to assess the geothermal potential of the area.  几个钻孔被钻探以评估该地区的 geothermal 潜力。
