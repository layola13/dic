ectostosis: /ˌɛktɒˈstəʊsɪs/ (UK) /ˌɛktɑːˈstoʊsɪs/ (US)
n.
骨疣；外生骨疣
An abnormal outgrowth of bone on the surface of a bone.  It's a benign bony growth projecting outward from the bone surface.
Example: The veterinarian discovered an ectostosis on the dog's leg.  兽医在狗的腿上发现了一个骨疣。

Example:  The X-ray revealed a small ectostosis on his rib.  X光显示他的肋骨上有一个小的骨疣。
Synonym: exostosis, osteoma
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, as it describes a presence, not an absence.  One could consider bone resorption or osteolysis as opposite processes.)
词性: noun
