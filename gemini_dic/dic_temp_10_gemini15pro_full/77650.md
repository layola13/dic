gelatins:/ˈdʒɛlətɪnz/ | n. | 明胶，凝胶 | A protein obtained by boiling skin, tendons, ligaments, and/or bones with water. It is used as a setting agent in desserts and other foods, in photographic films and plates, and in glues.  |  The dessert was made with gelatins.  这个甜点是用明胶做的。 | Synonyms: jelly, collagen | Antonyms: liquid | noun
