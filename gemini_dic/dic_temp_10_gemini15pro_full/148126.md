overseeding:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːdɪŋ/
n.
播种; 补播;
The act of sowing seed over an existing lawn or crop, typically to improve its density, quality, or resistance to disease.
We'll need to do some overseeding this fall to thicken up the lawn.  今年秋天我们需要补播一些种子来使草坪更厚实。
reseeding, topsowing
undersowing
noun
