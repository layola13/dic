capuchin:/ˈkæpʊtʃɪn/ /kæpjuːˈʃiːn/| n. |卡布奇诺（咖啡）； капуцин猴|A type of coffee made with espresso and steamed milk, often topped with foamed milk. Also, a type of monkey with a hood-like tuft of hair.|例句：I ordered a capuchin with extra foam. 我点了一杯加奶泡的卡布奇诺咖啡。

例句：The capuchin monkey deftly peeled the banana.   капуцин猴灵巧地剥开了香蕉。|近义词：(for coffee) latte, macchiato; (for monkey) cebid, primate|反义词: (no direct antonym)|词性:noun

capuchin:/ˈkæpʊtʃɪn/ /kæpjuːˈʃiːn/| adj. |卡布奇诺色的； капуцин猴的|Of or relating to Capuchin monks or their order.  Sometimes used to describe the color of their habits, a light to medium brown.|例句：The painting featured a capuchin monk in his traditional robes. 这幅画描绘了一位穿着传统长袍的 капуцин修士。

例句：She wore a capuchin-colored scarf. 她戴着一条卡布奇诺色的围巾。|近义词：(for color) brown, tan|反义词: (no direct antonym)|词性:adjective
