first-degree:/ˌfɜːrst dɪˈɡriː/ /ˌfɝːst dɪˈɡriː/
adj.|一级，第一级，轻微的|of the most basic or simple level; relating to a university degree awarded after a first course of study, usually lasting three or four years|He suffered first-degree burns on his hands. 他的手受到了一度烧伤。|Synonyms: initial, primary, basic|Antonyms: advanced, severe, high-degree|adjective
