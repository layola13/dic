mino:/ˈmiːnoʊ/| n.|米诺鱼；米诺酸；|A small, silvery fish used as bait, especially for bass.  Also, an amino acid.|He baited his hook with a live mino. 他用一条活米诺鱼作饵。|近义词：baitfish, shiner|反义词: None|词性: Noun


mino:/ˈmiːnoʊ/| n.|氨基酸|An organic molecule containing both an amino group and a carboxylic acid group, and forming the basic building blocks of proteins.|Proteins are made up of chains of amino acids, or minos. 蛋白质由氨基酸链或米诺酸构成。|近义词：amino acid|反义词: None|词性: Noun
