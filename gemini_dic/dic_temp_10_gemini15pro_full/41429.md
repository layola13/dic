cornerstones:/ˈkɔːrnərˌstoʊnz/ (US) /ˈkɔːnəˌstəʊnz/ (UK)| n. |基石，奠基石，基础|Something of great importance that everything else depends on.  It refers to the essential elements or principles on which something is built or established.|

例句：
Education and healthcare are the cornerstones of a thriving society.
教育和医疗保健是繁荣社会的基石。

例句：
Trust and communication are the cornerstones of any successful relationship.
信任和沟通是任何成功关系的基石。

近义词：foundation, basis, bedrock, underpinning|
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) periphery, nonessential element|
词性: noun
