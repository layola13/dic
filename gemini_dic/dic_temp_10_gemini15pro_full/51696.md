devine:/dɪˈvaɪn/| adj.|神圣的，天赐的；极好的，美好的|Of, from, or like God or a god.  Exceptionally good or pleasing.|例句：The music was simply devine.  这音乐简直太棒了。|近义词：holy, sacred, heavenly, wonderful, excellent|反义词：unholy, earthly, awful, terrible|词性:形容词
divine:/dɪˈvaɪn/| v.|预言，预测；（用占卜等）发现|Discover (something) by guesswork or intuition.|例句：She divined his true intentions. 她猜出了他的真实意图。|近义词：predict, foresee, foretell, intuit|反义词：misinterpret|词性:动词
