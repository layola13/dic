floramour:/ˈflɔːrəˌmʊr/| n. |花之恋，花的魅力|A coined word suggesting the enchantment or allure of flowers. It evokes a sense of romance and beauty associated with flowers. |The florist captured the floramour of the wedding with her exquisite arrangements. 花艺师用她精美的布置捕捉到了婚礼的花之恋。|The garden was filled with floramour, attracting butterflies and bees. 花园里充满了花的魅力，吸引了蝴蝶和蜜蜂。|近义词：floral charm, flower enchantment|反义词: ugliness, barrenness|词性:noun
