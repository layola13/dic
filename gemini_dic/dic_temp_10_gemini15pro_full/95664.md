incompressibility:/ˌɪnkəmˌprɛsəˈbɪləti/| n.|不可压缩性|The quality or state of being incompressible; resistance to compression.|The incompressibility of water makes it a useful hydraulic fluid.  水的不可压缩性使其成为一种有用的液压流体。|近义词: impenetrability, noncompressibility|反义词: compressibility, elasticity|词性: noun

Etymology:
From in- (not) + compressible (able to be compressed) + -ity (forming nouns of quality or state).  Ultimately from Latin *in- (not) + com- (together) + premere (to press) + -ibilis (able to be) + -ity.
