bulldozing:/ˈbʊldəʊzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbʊldoʊzɪŋ/ (US)| n. v.|推土；威逼；恐吓|The act of clearing land or demolishing structures with a bulldozer;  forcing one's way through or achieving something by using forceful or aggressive tactics. |The construction company began bulldozing the old building to make way for a new shopping mall. 建筑公司开始推倒旧楼，为新的购物中心腾出空间。| 近义词：demolishing, clearing, forcing, intimidating|反义词：constructing, building, yielding|词性：名词, 动词
