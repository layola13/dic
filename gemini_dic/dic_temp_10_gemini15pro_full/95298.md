incarnating:/ɪnˈkɑːrneɪtɪŋ/
verb
体现；化身；使…具体化
representing or embodying a quality or idea, especially a divine being in human form.
She felt she was incarnating the spirit of rebellion. 她觉得自己是反抗精神的化身。
近义词: embodying, personifying, representing, typifying
反义词: disembodying, abstracting
