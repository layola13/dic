figman:/ˈfɪɡmən/| n.|无花果商人; (旧时)贩卖无花果的流动小贩|A person who sells figs, especially a street vendor in the past. | He was a figman, pushing his cart through the bustling streets of London. 他是一个卖无花果的小贩，推着他的手推车穿过伦敦繁忙的街道。|近义词: fruit seller, vendor, peddler|反义词: buyer, customer|词性: 名词

词源:  From "fig" + "man".  "Fig" comes from Middle English *fige*, from Old French *figue*, from Latin *ficus*. "Man" comes from Old English *mann*.
