kohlrabi:/ˈkoʊlˌrɑːbi/ | n. |苤蓝，大头菜|A biennial plant, Brassica oleracea Gongylodes Group, of the mustard family, whose stem swells above ground into an edible bulb.|例句：We had kohlrabi salad for lunch. 我们午餐吃了苤蓝沙拉。

例句：Kohlrabi is a versatile vegetable. 苤蓝是一种用途广泛的蔬菜。

近义词：turnip cabbage|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German Kohlrabi, from Kohl (“cabbage”) + Rübe (“turnip”).  Ultimately from Latin caulis (“stem, stalk”) and Latin rapa (“turnip”).
