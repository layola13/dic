mollus:/ˈmɒləsk/ (US) /ˈmɑːləsk/| n. |软体动物|Any of a large phylum (Mollusca) of invertebrate animals (such as snails, clams, or squids) with a soft unsegmented body usually enclosed in a calcareous shell broadly : shellfish.|The octopus is a mollusk.|近义词：shellfish, invertebrate|反义词:vertebrate|noun
