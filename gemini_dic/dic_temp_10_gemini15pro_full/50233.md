dependably:/dɪˈpɛndəbli/ | adv. | 可靠地，确实地 | In a dependable manner; in a way that can be relied upon. | The train runs dependably every day. 火车每天都可靠地运行。

The postal service delivered the package dependably. 邮政服务可靠地递送了包裹。

| 近义词: reliably, consistently, steadily, invariably | 反义词: unreliably, erratically, inconsistently | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From depend + -able + -ly.  "Depend" comes from the Old French "dependre" meaning "to hang down," later evolving to mean "to rely on."  "-able" is a suffix meaning "capable of being," and "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
