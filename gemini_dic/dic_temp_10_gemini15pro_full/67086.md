exploiters:/ɪkˈsplɔɪtərz/| n. |剥削者，利用者|People or entities that take unfair advantage of someone or something for their own gain.  Those who exploit others for profit or power.|The exploiters of child labor were brought to justice.  童工剥削者被绳之以法。| The diamond mine owners were exploiters of the local population. 钻石矿主剥削当地居民。| synonyms: abusers, profiteers, leeches, opportunists | antonyms: benefactors, philanthropists | noun
