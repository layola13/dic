octospermous:/ˌɒktoʊˈspɜːrməs/
adjective
八籽的；有八颗种子的
Having eight seeds.
The octospermous capsule contained eight tiny seeds.  这个八籽的蒴果包含八颗小种子。
The botanist identified the plant as an octospermous species due to its eight-seeded fruit.  植物学家根据其八粒种子的果实将该植物鉴定为八籽物种。
Near Synonyms: eight-seeded
Antonyms: monospermous, dispermous, trispermous, tetraspermous, pentaspermous, hexaspermous, heptaspermous


ETYMOLOGY->
From octo- (eight) + sperm (seed) + -ous (adjective suffix)
