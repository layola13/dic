drawbridge:/ˈdrɔːbrɪdʒ/| n.|吊桥|A bridge, especially one over a castle's moat, that is hinged at one end so that it can be raised to prevent people crossing or to allow vessels to pass.|The knights quickly raised the drawbridge to protect the castle from invaders. 骑士们迅速升起吊桥，以保护城堡免受入侵者的袭击。|近义词:bascule bridge, swing bridge|反义词:fixed bridge|noun
