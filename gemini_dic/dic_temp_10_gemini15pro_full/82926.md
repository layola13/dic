The gummosity of certain tree resins makes them ideal for varnishes. 某些树脂的胶粘性使它们成为清漆的理想选择。| 近义词：stickiness, adhesiveness, tackiness | 反义词：slipperiness, smoothness | 词性：名词

Etymology:  From "gummy" (adjective) + "-osity" (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality). "Gummy" ultimately derives from "gum" (referring to a viscous substance), which has roots in Proto-Germanic and possibly even Proto-Indo-European.
