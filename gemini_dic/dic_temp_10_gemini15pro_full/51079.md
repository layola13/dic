despatch:/dɪˈspætʃ/ /dɪˈspæʧ/ | vt. n.|派遣，发送；急件|The general despatched a messenger to headquarters. 将军派遣一名信使去总部。|近义词：send, dispatch, forward, transmit|反义词：receive, hold, retain|词性：动词

despatch:/dɪˈspætʃ/ /dɪˈspæʧ/ | n.|派遣，发送；急件|The despatch arrived late last night. 急件昨晚很晚才到。|近义词：message, communication, report, shipment|反义词：delay, hold, retention|词性：名词

词源: From Old French *despacher, from des- (expressing removal) + pescher (“to fish out,” hence “to extricate,” “to hasten”). Doublet of dispatch.
