dowdyish:/ˈdaʊdiɪʃ/ | adj. | 稍显邋遢的，有点过时的 | Somewhat dowdy; shabby and unfashionable. |  She wore a dowdyish brown dress. 她穿着一件有点过时的棕色连衣裙。| The furniture was rather dowdyish. 家具显得有些陈旧。 | 近义词: frumpy, drab, shabby, unfashionable | 反义词: stylish, fashionable, chic, elegant | 词性: adjective

词源: From dowdy + -ish.  Dowdy itself has uncertain origins, possibly related to "dowd," a dull, spiritless woman.
