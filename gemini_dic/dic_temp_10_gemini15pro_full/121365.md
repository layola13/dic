misguess:/ˌmɪsˈɡɛs/ | v. | 猜错，估计错误 | To guess incorrectly; to make a wrong estimate or prediction. |  I misguessed the amount of time it would take to finish the project. 我错误地估计了完成这个项目所需的时间。

| I completely misguessed her reaction. 我完全猜错了她的反应。


| 近义词: miscalculate, misjudge, underestimate, overestimate | 反义词: guess correctly, predict accurately | 词性: 动词
