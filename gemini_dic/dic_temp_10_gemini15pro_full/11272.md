arcos:/ˈɑːrkoʊz/ (US) /ˈɑːkɒs/ (UK)| n. | （复数）拱门，弧形结构 | A structure, typically made of stone or brick, that spans an opening and is curved at the top.  A series of arches. | The Roman aqueduct featured a series of impressive arcos.  罗马渡槽有一系列令人印象深刻的拱门。| synonyms: arches, arc, vaults | antonyms:  straight lines, beams | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *arco* (arch), from Latin *arcus* (bow, arch).
