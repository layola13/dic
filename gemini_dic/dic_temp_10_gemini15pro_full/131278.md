noncaustically:/nɒnˈkɔːstɪkli/ | adv. | 非腐蚀性地，不尖刻地 |  In a way that is not corrosive or sarcastic; mildly; gently. | He noncaustically pointed out the flaws in her argument. 他温和地指出了她论证中的缺陷。| 近义词: mildly, gently, inoffensively | 反义词: caustically, sarcastically, acrimoniously | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "non-" (not) + "caustic" (burning or corrosive) + "-ally" (adverbial suffix). "Caustic" ultimately derives from the Greek "kaustikos" (capable of burning).
