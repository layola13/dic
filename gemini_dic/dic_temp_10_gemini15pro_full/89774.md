hordes:/hɔrdz/ | n. | 群，大群，游牧部落 | A large group or crowd, especially one on the move. |  Example 1: Hordes of tourists descended on the small town. 大批游客涌入小镇。

Example 2:  The invading hordes swept across the plains. 入侵的游牧部落席卷了平原。 | Synonyms: crowds, swarms, throngs, masses | Antonyms: few, handful, scattering | noun
