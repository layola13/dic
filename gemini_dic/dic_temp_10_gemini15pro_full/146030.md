overdrawn: /ˌoʊvərˈdrɔːn/ | adj. | 透支的；过度疲劳的 | Having a negative balance in a bank account; exhausted or strained. |  My account is overdrawn by $50. 我的账户透支了50美元。| synonyms: overspent, bankrupt, depleted, exhausted, fatigued | antonyms: solvent, in credit, affluent, energetic, refreshed | adjective

overdraw: /ˌoʊvərˈdrɔː/ | verb | 透支；夸大 | To draw money from a bank account in excess of the balance; to exaggerate. |  I overdrew my account last month. 我上个月透支了我的账户。 | synonyms: overspend, deplete, exaggerate, embellish | antonyms: deposit, understate | verb
