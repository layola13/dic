marbrinus:/mɑːrˈbriːnəs/
adj.|大理石的; 似大理石的|Resembling marble; made of marble, or having the qualities of marble. |The marbrinus columns supported the temple roof.  大理石柱支撑着寺庙的屋顶。|Synonyms: marble, marmoreal |Antonyms: wooden, earthen|adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *marbrinus*, from *marmor* (marble).
