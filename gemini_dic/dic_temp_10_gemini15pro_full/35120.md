cloudflare:/ˈklaʊdflɛr/| n. |云耀斑|A U.S.-based company that provides web infrastructure and website security services, acting as a reverse proxy between a website's visitor and the hosting provider's server. It offers services like CDN, DDoS mitigation, internet security, and distributed domain name server services.| Cloudflare protects websites from DDoS attacks. 云耀斑保护网站免受 DDoS 攻击。| 近义词:Akamai, Fastly| 反义词: N/A | noun
