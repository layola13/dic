matrilineality:/ˌmætɹɪˈlɪniˌælɪti/ | n. |母系制，母系社会|The tracing of descent through the female line.  A system of social organization in which descent and inheritance are traced through the female line.| The anthropologist studied the complexities of matrilineality in the Minangkabau culture.  这位人类学家研究了米南佳保文化中母系社会的复杂性。 |Synonyms: matriliny, matriarchal system| Antonyms: patrilineality, patriarchy| noun
