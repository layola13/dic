grandiosely:/ˌɡrændiˈoʊsli/ | adv. | 夸大地，宏伟地，堂皇地 | in a grandiose manner; in a way that is intended to seem impressive or magnificent but is often excessive or empty | He spoke grandiosely of his plans to build a vast empire. 他夸夸其谈地讲述了他建立庞大帝国的计划。|  近义词：pompously, ostentatiously, pretentiously | 反义词：humbly, modestly, simply | 词性: 副词

grandiose:/ˈɡrændiˌoʊs/ | adj. | 宏伟的，堂皇的，夸大的 | impressive and imposing in appearance or style, especially excessively so |  They had grandiose plans for a new opera house. 他们对建造一座新歌剧院有着宏伟的计划。| 近义词：magnificent, imposing, impressive | 反义词：humble, modest, simple |词性: 形容词

词源: From French grandiose, from Italian grandioso, from grande 'large,' from Latin grandis.
