compromise: /ˈkɒmprəmaɪz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmprəmaɪz/ (US)
|n. |妥协；折衷；妥协方案|An agreement or a settlement of a dispute that is reached by each side making concessions.|The two sides reached a compromise after hours of negotiation. 双方经过数小时的谈判达成了妥协。|agreement, settlement, concession|deadlock, impasse|noun
compromise: /ˈkɒmprəmaɪz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmprəmaɪz/ (US)
|vt. |损害；危及；妥协；让步|To expose or make vulnerable to danger, suspicion, scandal, etc.;  To settle a dispute by mutual concession.|Don't compromise your principles. 不要放弃你的原则。|endanger, jeopardize, weaken|protect, safeguard, uphold|verb
