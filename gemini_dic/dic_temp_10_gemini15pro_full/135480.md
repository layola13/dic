nonphilologic:/ˌnɒnˌfɪləˈlɒdʒɪk/
adjective
非文献学的，不属于文献学的
Not pertaining to philology; not based on the study of language and literature.
His approach to the text was nonphilologic, focusing more on the historical context than on the linguistic nuances.  他的文本研究方法是非文献学的，更多地关注历史背景而不是语言细微之处。
The museum's exhibit took a nonphilologic approach to ancient texts, emphasizing their visual and material aspects. 博物馆的展览对古代文献采取了非文献学的方法，强调它们的视觉和物质方面。
近义词:nonlinguistic
反义词:philological
词性:adjective
