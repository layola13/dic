dramatising:/ˈdræməˌtaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈdrɑːməˌtaɪzɪŋ/
v.
戏剧化；使…戏剧化；夸张；(尤指)把(小说等)改编成戏剧
present participle of dramatise
The author dramatises the conflict between good and evil. 作者将善恶之间的冲突戏剧化了。
近义词: exaggerating, overstating, theatricalizing
反义词: downplaying, understating
词性: 动词 (present participle)
