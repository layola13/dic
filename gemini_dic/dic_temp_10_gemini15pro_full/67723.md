exuviable:/ɛkˈsjuːviəbl/
adjective
可蜕皮的; 可脱落的
able to be shed or cast off, especially referring to an outer covering such as skin, shell, or cuticle.
The exuviable outer layer of the insect's body allows for growth. 昆虫可蜕皮的外层身体允许其生长。
Synonyms: shedable, moltable
Antonyms: permanent, fixed
词性: 形容词
