mar:/mɑːr/ /mær/| v. |损毁，破坏，玷污|to damage or spoil the appearance or quality of something|例句：The new building was marred by graffiti.  这栋新楼被涂鸦破坏了。|例句：A scratch marred the surface of the table.  一道划痕破坏了桌子的表面。|近义词：damage, spoil, ruin, impair, blemish|反义词：improve, enhance, beautify|词性:动词

词源:
From Old English *merran, from Proto-Germanic *marzjaną (“to hinder, confuse, stop, disturb”), from Proto-Indo-European *mers- (“to rub away, harm”). Cognate with Old Norse merja (“to bruise, crush”), Old High German merren (“to hinder, disturb, damage”), Dutch merren (“to exhaust, tire”), German marren (“to tire, exhaust”).
