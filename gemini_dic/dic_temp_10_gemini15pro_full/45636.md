cursivenesses:/ˈkɜːrsɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
草书风格；草书（的）流畅性
the flowing quality of cursive writing.
Her cursivenesses varied depending on the pen she used.  她写的草书风格会根据她使用的笔而变化。
His cursivenesses are quite elegant. 他的草书非常优雅。
近义词: calligraphy, handwriting, script
反义词: print
词性: 名词
