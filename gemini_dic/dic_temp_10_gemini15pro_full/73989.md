fortresses:/ˈfɔːtrəsɪz/
noun
堡垒；要塞
A strongly fortified place, especially a large military stronghold.
The city was ringed with imposing fortresses. 这座城市周围环绕着雄伟的堡垒。
synonyms: citadel, stronghold, bastion, fortification, castle
antonyms: weakness, vulnerability
