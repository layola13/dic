词源:
"blockhead" comes from "block," meaning a solid piece of wood, and "head," suggesting a head as thick or unresponsive as a block of wood.  The suffix "-ism" turns the noun "blockhead" into a noun representing the doctrines, beliefs, or practices of a blockhead.
