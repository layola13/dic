mansa:/ˈmænsə/| n.|曼萨（西非历史上一些帝国的统治者的称号）|A title for rulers of some empires in West African history, especially in the Mali Empire.  It denotes sovereignty, rulership, or emperor.|Mansa Musa was the most famous mansa of the Mali Empire. 曼萨·穆萨是马里帝国最著名的曼萨。|Synonym: emperor, king, ruler, sovereign|Antonym: subject, commoner|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Mandinka (Mande language) *mansa meaning "king," "emperor," or "ruler."
