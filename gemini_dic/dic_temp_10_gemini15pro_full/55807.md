divert:/daɪˈvɜːrt/ (US), /daɪˈvɜːt/ (UK)| v. | 转移；使分心；娱乐 | to turn aside from a course or direction; to distract; to amuse or entertain | The loud noise diverted my attention.  巨大的噪音转移了我的注意力。| synonyms: redirect, reroute, distract, entertain | antonyms: focus, concentrate | verb

divert:/daɪˈvɜːrt/ (US), /daɪˈvɜːt/ (UK)| n. | 转移；消遣 | something that diverts attention or provides amusement |  Reading is a pleasant divert. 阅读是一种愉快的消遣。 | synonyms: distraction, entertainment, pastime | antonyms:  focus, concentration | noun
