elytron:/ˈɛlɪtrɒn/ (US) /ˈɛlɪtrən/ (UK)| n. |鞘翅；翅鞘|The hard, protective forewing of a beetle or other insect.|例句：The beetle's elytra shielded its delicate hindwings. 甲虫的鞘翅保护着它脆弱的后翅。

例句：The brightly colored elytra of some beetles serve as camouflage. 一些甲虫颜色鲜艳的鞘翅起到了伪装的作用。|近义词：forewing, wing case|反义词:hindwing|词性:noun
