ill-tempered:/ˌɪlˈtɛmpərd/
adj.
脾气暴躁的；易怒的
Having a bad temper; easily angered.
He's an ill-tempered old man who yells at everyone. 他是一个脾气暴躁的老人，对每个人都大喊大叫。
The ill-tempered customer complained loudly about the service.  那个脾气不好的顾客大声抱怨服务。
近义词:bad-tempered, grumpy, irritable, cantankerous
反义词:good-tempered, affable, amiable, pleasant
词性:形容词

词源:
From ill (bad) + tempered (having a particular temperament).  The word "temper" originally referred to the mixture of elements in metalworking, and later came to mean a person's state of mind or disposition.  "Ill-tempered" thus literally means having a "bad mixture" in one's disposition.
