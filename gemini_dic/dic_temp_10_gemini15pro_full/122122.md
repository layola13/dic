mistranscribed: /ˌmɪstrænˈskraɪbd/ | adj. | 抄写错误的，誊写错误的 | Incorrectly transcribed; written down wrongly. | The document was mistranscribed, leading to several errors in the final report. 这份文件抄写错误，导致最终报告中出现多个错误。| The minutes of the meeting were mistranscribed. 会议记录被抄写错了。 | Synonyms: misspelled, miscopied, miswritten | Antonyms: correctly transcribed, accurately copied | adjective
