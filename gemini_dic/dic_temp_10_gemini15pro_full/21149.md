blitzkrieg:/ˈblɪtskriːɡ/| n.|闪电战|An intense military campaign intended to bring about a swift victory.|例句：The German blitzkrieg across Poland in 1939 was devastatingly effective. 1939年德国对波兰的闪电战非常有效。

例句：The blitzkrieg tactics employed by the army quickly overwhelmed the enemy defenses.  军队采用的闪电战战术迅速压倒了敌人的防御。|近义词：onslaught, assault, blitz|反义词:stalemate, defense|词性:noun

词源:From German, literally 'lightning war,' from Blitz 'lightning' + Krieg 'war.'
