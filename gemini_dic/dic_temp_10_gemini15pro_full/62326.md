endecagon:/ɛnˈdɛkəɡɒn/ /ɛnˈdɛkəɡɑːn/
noun
十一边形，十一角形
A plane figure with eleven sides and eleven angles.
An endecagon has eleven sides.  一个十一边形有十一边。
The interior angles of a regular endecagon add up to 1620 degrees.  正十一边形的内角和为1620度。
近义词:hendecagon
反义词: (No direct antonym for a geometric shape)
词性:n.
