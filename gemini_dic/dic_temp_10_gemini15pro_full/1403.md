accuses:/əˈkjuːzɪz/| v.|指责，控告|to say that someone has done something wrong or illegal|He accused her of stealing the money. 他指责她偷了钱。
The report accuses the government of incompetence. 报告指责政府无能。|近义词：blame, charge, indict|反义词: defend, excuse, exonerate|词性:动词(第三人称单数)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *acuser*, from Latin accusare "to call to account," from ad- "to" + causa "lawsuit, case."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often accuses me of being lazy. = 他经常指责我懒惰。
She accuses him of not listening to her. = 她指责他不听她的话。

一般过去时:
They accused him of murder. = 他们指控他谋杀。
The police accused her of speeding. = 警察指控她超速驾驶。

现在完成时:
The company has been accused of fraud. = 这家公司被指控欺诈。
He has accused his neighbor of trespassing. = 他指控他的邻居非法入侵。

过去完成时:
She had accused him of lying before the truth came out. = 真相大白之前，她一直指责他说谎。
The witness had accused the wrong person of the crime. = 目击者指控了错误的人犯罪。

将来时:
If you don't finish the work, I will accuse you of negligence. = 如果你不完成工作，我将指责你玩忽职守。
They will accuse him of stealing the car if they find any evidence. = 如果他们找到任何证据，他们会指控他偷车。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't accuse him without proof. = 如果我是你，我没有证据就不会指责他。
I wish he wouldn't accuse me of things I didn't do. = 我希望他不要指责我没有做过的事情。

被动语态:
He was accused of theft. = 他被指控盗窃。
She is being accused of cheating on the exam. = 她被指控考试作弊。

疑问句:
Who accused him of the crime? = 谁指控他犯罪？
Why did she accuse you of lying? = 为什么她指责你说谎？

否定句:
He didn't accuse anyone of wrongdoing. = 他没有指责任何人有不当行为。
She never accuses him of anything. = 她从不指责他任何事情。



PHRASE->
accuse someone of something = 指控某人某事
be accused of something = 被指控某事
wrongfully accuse = 错误地指控
falsely accuse = 诬告
stand accused of = 被指控
