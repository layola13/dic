hipparion:/ˌhɪˈpɛəriən/ | n. |三趾马|An extinct three-toed horse of the Miocene and Pliocene epochs, widely distributed in the Northern Hemisphere. |例句：Hipparion fossils are found across Eurasia and North America.  三趾马化石在欧亚大陆和北美都有发现。

|例句：The hipparion was smaller than the modern horse. 三趾马比现代马小。|近义词：Merychippus,  Parahippus (related genera)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

hippotherium:/ˌhɪpəˈθɪəriəm/ | n. |河马兽|An extinct genus of large mammals related to horses, that lived in Africa during the Miocene and Pliocene epochs.  |例句：Hippotherium fossils are primarily found in Africa. 河马兽化石主要在非洲发现。|例句：The hippotherium is also known as the "hippo horse". 河马兽也被称为“河马”。|近义词：Hipparion (related genus)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
