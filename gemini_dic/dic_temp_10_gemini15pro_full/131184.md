nonbrutal:/nɒnˈbruːtl/ | adj. | 非残酷的，仁慈的 | Not brutal; gentle, humane, kind. | The police employed nonbrutal methods to subdue the protester. 警察使用了非残酷手段制服了抗议者。|  The government pursued a nonbrutal policy of resettlement. 政府奉行非残酷的移民安置政策。| 近义词: gentle, humane, kind, compassionate, merciful | 反义词: brutal, cruel, savage, violent, vicious | 词性: adjective
词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "brutal" (from Late Latin "brutalis," meaning "of beasts," from Latin "brutus," meaning "dull, senseless, irrational").
