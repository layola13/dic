bedless:/ˈbedləs/
adjective
无床的，没有床铺的
Without a bed.
Many homeless people are bedless. 许多无家可归的人没有床铺。
The earthquake left thousands bedless. 地震使数千人无家可归。
近义词:homeless, without a bed
反义词:bedded

词性:adjective
