blurb:/blɜːrb/ | n. | 简介；推荐广告；印在书籍封套上的内容简介 | A short description of a book, film, or other product written for promotional purposes. |  The blurb on the back of the book made it sound very exciting. 这本书背面的简介让它听起来很刺激。

The publisher wrote a blurb for the new novel. 出版商为这部新小说写了一段简介。| synonym: synopsis, summary, abstract, puff | antonym: critique, review | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Coined by American humorist Gelett Burgess (1866–1952), apparently as the name of a character, Miss Belinda Blurb, featured on the dust jacket of his 1907 book *Are You a Bromide? or, The Sulphitic Theory*.  The character's image was accompanied by exaggerated praise for the book.
