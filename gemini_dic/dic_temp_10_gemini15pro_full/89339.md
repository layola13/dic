homoioptoton:/ˌhɒmɔɪˈɒptətɒn/ |n.|同音词尾格,同尾格|A figure of speech consisting of the use of words with similar endings in close proximity.  It is a type of paronomasia, a rhetorical device that plays on the sounds of words.|The phrase "easy peasy lemon squeezy" is an example of homoioptoton.| 近义词:paronomasia, assonance, rhyme| 反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider its opposite in effect: cacophony) |词性:noun
