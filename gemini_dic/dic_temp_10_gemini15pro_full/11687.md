armour-plating:/ˈɑːməˌpleɪtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈɑːrmərˌpleɪtɪŋ/ (US)| n.|装甲，装甲板|The process of covering something with armour, or the armour itself.  A protective layer of metal.|The warship was fitted with heavy armour-plating.|Synonyms: armor plating, armor plate, protective plating|Antonyms: vulnerability, weakness|noun

armour-plate:/ˈɑːməˌpleɪt/ (UK) /ˈɑːrmərˌpleɪt/ (US)| vt.| 装甲|To cover something with armour.|They armour-plated the vehicle to protect it from attack.|Synonyms: armor, protect, shield|Antonyms: expose, weaken|verb
