flogged:/flɒɡd/ /flɑːɡd/| v. | 鞭打，鞭笞；严厉批评；（拼命地）宣传，推销 | to beat someone with a whip or stick as punishment or to make them work harder; to criticize someone or something severely; to try very hard to sell something or make people support it| The slave was cruelly flogged for disobeying his master.  奴隶因不服从主人而被残酷鞭打。

The film was flogged relentlessly in the media. 这部电影被媒体大肆宣传。|Synonyms: whip, lash, beat, thrash, cane, castigate, criticize, lambaste, promote, push, plug |Antonyms: praise, commend, endorse|verb
