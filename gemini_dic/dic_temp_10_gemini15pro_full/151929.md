pasticcio:/pəˈstɪtʃoʊ/ /pɑːˈstiːtʃoʊ/| n. |大杂烩；混成曲；模仿作品|A musical, literary, or artistic work made up of selections from different works or in imitation of the style of another artist or period.|The opera was a pastiche of 18th-century styles. 这部歌剧是18世纪风格的大杂烩。|近义词：medley, hodgepodge, potpourri, patchwork|反义词:original|词性:名词


pastiche:/pɑːˈstiːʃ/ /pəˈstiːʃ/| n. |模仿作品；拼凑物|An artistic work in a style that imitates that of another work, artist, or period.|The film is a pastiche of several different genres. 这部电影是几种不同类型的拼凑物。|近义词：pasticcio, parody, imitation|反义词:original|词性:名词
