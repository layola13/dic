arbiter:/ˈɑːrbɪtər/ (US) /ˈɑːbɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|仲裁者，裁决人|A person who settles a dispute or disagreement between two parties.|The union and management agreed to bring in an arbiter to resolve the contract dispute. 工会和管理层同意请一位仲裁员来解决合同纠纷。|Synonyms: judge, mediator, adjudicator, umpire|Antonyms: disputant, litigant|Noun
