liefest:/ˈliːfɪst/
adjective
最爱的；最亲密的
Dearest, most beloved.
She sent the letter to her liefest friend. 她把信寄给了她最亲爱的朋友。
近义词: dearest, beloved, most cherished
反义词: least favorite, most hated
词性: adjective

词源:
From Middle English *lefest, from Old English lēofost, superlative of lēof (“dear, beloved”). Cognate with Dutch liefst, German liebst, Old Norse ljūfastr.
