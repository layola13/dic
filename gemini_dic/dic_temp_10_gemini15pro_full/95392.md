核心人物;关键人物;重要部件;关键部分;支柱;栋梁;要点;要害
A person or thing that is essential to the success of an organization or operation.  A small piece of wood, metal, or other material used to secure or strengthen a joint or framework, often wedged into place.

例句：The inchipin that held the two beams together broke, causing the structure to collapse.  连接两根梁的关键部件断裂，导致结构倒塌。

反义词：nonessential, unimportant, insignificant, peripheral
