legislated: /ˈlɛdʒɪsleɪtɪd/
verb
立法；制定法律
Made into law by a legislative body.
The new tax was legislated last year. 去年新税收被立法通过。
enacted, ordained, decreed
nullified, repealed
v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *legislatus*, past participle of *legislare* "to propose a law," from *lex* "law" + *-latus*, suffix forming past participles.
