endogeny:/ɛnˈdɒdʒɪni/ | n. |内生，内源性|The development of something generated within an organism or system.|例句：The endogeny of this disease is still unknown. 这种疾病的内生性仍然未知。

例句：The economist studied the endogeny of economic growth.  这位经济学家研究了经济增长的内生性。|近义词：internal development, internal growth|反义词：exogeny|词性:noun
