dominions:/dəˈmɪnjənz/
noun
领土，统治，支配
territories under a government's control; rule or power over something.
The British Empire once held dominions across the globe.  大英帝国曾经在全球拥有许多领土。
The king's dominions stretched far and wide. 国王的领地广阔无垠。
synonyms: territories, realms, possessions,  empire, sway, control
antonyms: subordination, subjection, dependence
