metempsychosis:/ˌmɛtəmˈsaɪkoʊsɪs/ /ˌmɛtɛmpˈsaɪkəʊsɪs/| n. |轮回；灵魂转世|The belief in metempsychosis suggests that the soul is reborn in a new body after death.  轮回的信仰表明灵魂在死后会在新的身体里重生。|例句：The ancient Egyptians believed in metempsychosis, carefully preserving bodies for the afterlife. 古埃及人相信轮回转世，小心翼翼地保存尸体以备来世。

例句：Some religions teach that metempsychosis is a cycle of death and rebirth, determined by karma. 一些宗教教导说，轮回是一个由业力决定的生死循环。|近义词：reincarnation, transmigration, rebirth|反义词: death, finality|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *metempsychosis, from Ancient Greek μετεμψύχωσις (metempsúkhōsis, “transmigration of souls”), from μετεμψυχόω (metempsukhóō, “to make the soul pass into another body”), from μετά (metá, “change”) + ἐμψυχόω (empsukhóō, “to put life into”), from ἐν (en, “in”) + ψυχή (psukhḗ, “soul, life, breath”).
