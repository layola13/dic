foamy:/ˈfoʊmi/ (US) /ˈfəʊmi/ (UK)| adj.|泡沫的；起泡沫的|Covered in or resembling foam; frothy.|The beer had a thick, foamy head. 这啤酒有一层厚厚的泡沫。|近义词：frothy, bubbly, effervescent, spumy|反义词:flat, still|词性:形容词
词源:From foam + -y.  Ultimately from the Proto-Germanic *faimaz.
