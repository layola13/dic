half-demented:/ˌhɑːf dɪˈmɛntɪd/
half-demented: adj.
half-demented: 半疯的；精神错乱的；有点疯狂的
half-demented:  Partially demented; not completely insane but exhibiting signs of mental instability or irrationality.  "Half-" acts as a mitigating prefix, suggesting a lesser degree of the condition.  "Demented" comes from Latin "demens" (out of one's mind).
half-demented:  He was acting half-demented, muttering to himself and pacing back and forth. 他表现得有点疯疯癫癫的，自言自语，来回踱步。

half-demented: The stress of the situation made her half-demented with worry. 情况的压力让她因担忧而几近疯狂。
half-demented: crazy, insane, mad, unhinged, distraught
half-demented: sane, rational, lucid, composed
