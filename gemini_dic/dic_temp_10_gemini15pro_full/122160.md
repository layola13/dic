mistrustfulness:/ˌmɪsˈtrʌstfʊlnəs/| n.|不信任，怀疑，猜疑|The state of being mistrustful; a tendency to distrust others.|Her mistrustfulness made it difficult to form close relationships. 她的怀疑使得她难以建立亲密关系。|Example:  His mistrustfulness of strangers is understandable given his past experiences.  他由于过去的经历而不信任陌生人，这是可以理解的。

Example: The atmosphere of mistrustfulness in the office made it a difficult place to work. 办公室里充满不信任的氛围，让人很难工作。|Synonyms: suspicion, distrust, wariness, skepticism, cynicism|Antonyms: trust, confidence, faith, belief|Noun
