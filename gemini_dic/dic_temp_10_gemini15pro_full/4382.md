alai: /əˈlaɪ/ | n. | 阿拉伊（夏威夷语，指领导者，皇室成员）| A Hawaiian term referring to royalty, especially a chief or monarch.  It can also be used more generally to refer to someone of high status or a leader. |  The alai traveled with a large retinue.  阿拉伊带着大批随从出行。| synonym: chief, monarch, sovereign, ruler, leader | antonym: commoner, subject, follower | noun
