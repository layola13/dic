impressionless:/ɪmˈprɛʃənləs/
adjective
无表情的，冷漠的
Showing no feeling or emotion; apathetic.
He stared at me with an impressionless gaze. 他毫无表情地盯着我。

Her impressionless face gave nothing away. 她那毫无表情的脸庞什么也看不出来。

近义词:unemotional, apathetic, impassive, stoic, indifferent, unmoved, blank, expressionless, poker-faced, deadpan
反义词:emotional, expressive, passionate
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From im- (not) + impression + -less (without).  Essentially meaning "without impression," implying a lack of emotional response.
