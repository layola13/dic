eeke: /iːk/ | n. | 渴望，热切的期盼 | An archaic or dialectal word meaning a yearning or strong desire.  |  He felt an eeke for his homeland. 他对故土充满了渴望。 | 近义词: yearning, longing, craving | 反义词: apathy, indifference | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *eken, from Old Norse eik, meaning 'increase, addition'. Related to eke (v.) meaning 'to supplement or add to'.
