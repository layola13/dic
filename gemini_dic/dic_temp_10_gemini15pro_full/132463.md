nondepressively:/ˌnɒndɪˈprɛsɪvli/
adv. | 非抑郁地；不沮丧地 | In a way that is not depressive; without causing or experiencing depression. |  She spoke nondepressively about her future, despite the challenges. 她谈到未来时并没有表现出沮丧，尽管面临挑战。|  She faced her illness nondepressively, inspiring others. 她以积极的态度面对疾病，激励了其他人。| 近义词: cheerfully, optimistically, positively | 反义词: depressively, gloomily, sadly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix) + depressively (adverb)
"non-" comes from Latin meaning "not"
"depressively" comes from "depress" (verb) + "-ive" (adjective suffix) + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
"depress" ultimately derives from Latin "deprimere" meaning "to press down."
