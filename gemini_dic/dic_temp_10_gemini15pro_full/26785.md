cafe's:/ˈkæfeɪz/| n. |咖啡馆的，咖啡馆的东西|Something that belongs to or is associated with a cafe. |例句：The cafe's atmosphere is cozy and inviting. 咖啡馆的氛围舒适宜人。

例句：I love the cafe's coffee. 我喜欢这家咖啡馆的咖啡。|近义词：coffee shop's, café's |反义词: N/A |词性:名词所有格

Etymology: From French "café," ultimately from Arabic "qahwa" (coffee).  The -'s is the English possessive suffix.
