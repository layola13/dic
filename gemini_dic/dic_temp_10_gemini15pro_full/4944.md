alisphenoidal:/æˌlɪsfɪˈnɔɪdəl/
adj.
蝶骨翼状的；与蝶骨大翼相关的
Relating to or located near the greater wing of the sphenoid bone.
The alisphenoidal ligament connects the mandible to the greater wing of the sphenoid bone. 蝶骨翼状韧带连接下颌骨和蝶骨大翼。
Near the alisphenoidal surface of the skull. 在靠近颅骨蝶骨翼状表面的位置。
近义词:sphenoidal
反义词: (None - anatomical term, lacks a direct opposite)
词性:adjective


Etymology:
ali- (Latin, meaning "wing") + sphenoid (Greek, sphēn, meaning "wedge" + eidos, meaning "form") + -al (adjective suffix)
