hard-code:/ˌhɑːrd ˈkoʊd/ (US), /ˌhɑːd ˈkəʊd/ (UK)| v. adj.|硬编码，硬代码|To embed data directly into the source code of a program or other executable object, rather than obtaining the data from external sources or generating it at runtime.|Example:  The username is hard-coded into the application.|近义词: embed, fixed, built-in|反义词: configurable, dynamic, parameterized|词性：动词，形容词

hard-coded:/ˌhɑːrd ˈkoʊdɪd/ (US), /ˌhɑːrd ˈkəʊdɪd/ (UK)| adj.|硬编码的|Embedded directly in the source code, rather than being obtained from external sources or generated at runtime.|Example:  The application uses a hard-coded password.|近义词: embedded, fixed, built-in|反义词: configurable, dynamic, parameterized|词性：形容词
