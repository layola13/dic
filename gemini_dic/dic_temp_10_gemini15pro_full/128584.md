niccolite:/ˈnɪkəˌlaɪt/| n. |红砷镍矿|A mineral, nickel arsenide (NiAs), that is a source of nickel and arsenic. It is reddish in color and occurs in igneous rocks. |Example 1:  The miners discovered a rich vein of niccolite. 矿工们发现了一条富含红砷镍矿的矿脉。

Example 2: Niccolite is an important ore of nickel. 红砷镍矿是镍的重要矿石。|近义词:kupfernickel (an old term, less precise)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
