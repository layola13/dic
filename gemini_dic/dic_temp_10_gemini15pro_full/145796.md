overdecadent:/ˌoʊvərˈdɛkədənt/ | adj. | 过于颓废的，极度奢靡的 | Excessively decadent; characterized by excessive luxury, self-indulgence, and moral decay.  Marked by or reflecting a state of moral or cultural decline. |  The overdecadent lifestyle of the Roman emperors contributed to the empire's decline. 罗马皇帝过度颓废的生活方式导致了帝国的衰落。 |  The party was an overdecadent display of wealth.  这个派对是对财富的过度炫耀。 | Synonyms: extravagant, excessive, luxurious, immoderate, dissipated | Antonyms: austere, moderate, restrained, simple, ascetic | adjective
