overreservedly:/ˌoʊvərˌrɪˈzɜːrvɪdli/
| adv. | 过分拘谨地，过分矜持地 |  In a manner that is excessively reserved or cautious.  | She spoke overreservedly, barely making eye contact. 她说话过分拘谨，几乎不敢直视对方。 | He overreservedly accepted the invitation, unsure if he truly wanted to go. 他过分矜持地接受了邀请，不确定自己是否真的想去。 | 近义词：cautiously, timidly, hesitantly | 反义词：boldly, confidently, openly | 词性: 副词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "reserved" (adjective meaning "slow to reveal emotion or opinions") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
