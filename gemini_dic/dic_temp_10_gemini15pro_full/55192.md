dissentingly:/dɪˈsɛntɪŋli/
adverb
不同意地，持异议地
in a dissenting manner; expressing disagreement
例句：She dissentingly shook her head. 她摇了摇头表示不同意。

例句：He dissentingly voted against the proposal. 他投票反对这项提议，表达了不同意见。

近义词：disagreeably, opposingly, objectionably
反义词：agreeably, approvingly, concordantly
词性：adverb

Etymology:
From dissent + -ing + -ly.  "Dissent" comes from the Old French "dissentir"  (to disagree in sentiment, be of a different opinion),  and ultimately from the Latin "dissentire" (to feel differently, disagree), from "dis-" (apart, away) + "sentire" (to feel, perceive).
