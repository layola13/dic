genuflected:/ˈdʒɛnjʊˌflɛktɪd/
verb
屈膝，行屈膝礼
Lowered one's body briefly by bending one knee to the ground, typically in worship or as a sign of respect.
She genuflected before the altar. 她对着祭坛屈膝行礼。
The knight genuflected before the queen. 骑士在女王面前屈膝行礼。
近义词: knelt, bowed
反义词: stood erect, stood up straight
词性: verb
