compressor:/kəmˈpresər/ (US) /kəmˈpresə(r)/ (UK)| n.|压缩机，压缩器|A machine that compresses air or other gases.  A device that reduces the dynamic range of an audio signal.|例句：The air compressor is used to inflate tires.  空气压缩机用来给轮胎充气。

例句：He used a compressor to reduce the dynamic range of the recording. 他用压缩器来降低录音的动态范围。|近义词：condenser, pump|反义词：expander|noun
