monesin:/ˈmoʊnɪsɪn/ | n. |莫能菌素|A polyether antibiotic isolated from *Streptomyces cinnamonensis*. It is used as a coccidiostat and growth promotant in livestock. |Monesin is commonly used in poultry feed. 莫能菌素常用于家禽饲料中。| Monesin can also be used in research to study ion transport. 莫能菌素也可以用于研究离子转运。 |synonyms: rumensin, coccidiostat| antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps consider antibiotics with different mechanisms of action)| noun
