inflationary:/ɪnˈfleɪʃəneri/
adj.
通货膨胀的; 引起通货膨胀的
Tending to cause inflation or affected by inflation.
例句：
The government adopted inflationary policies. 政府采取了通货膨胀政策。

The inflationary spiral seems to be slowing down. 通货膨胀的螺旋式上升似乎正在放缓。


近义词：inflationistic
反义词：deflationary
词性：形容词
