overshooting:/ˌoʊvərˈʃuːtɪŋ/ | n. | 超调，过冲 |  Going beyond a target or limit, especially unintentionally.  | The car overshot the junction and ended up in a field. 汽车冲过了路口，最后停在了田野里。| overrunning, exceeding, surpassing | undershooting, falling short | noun

overshoot:/ˌoʊvərˈʃuːt/ | v. | 超过，越过，过冲 | To go beyond a target or limit, especially unintentionally. |  The plane overshot the runway. 飞机超过了跑道。 | exceed, surpass, overrun | undershoot, fall short | verb

overshot:/ˌoʊvərˈʃɒt/ | adj. | 超过的，过冲的 | Having gone beyond a target or limit. |  The overshot budget caused delays in the project. 超出预算导致项目延期。| excessive, surplus, redundant | insufficient, lacking | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
