coculture:/ˈkoʊkʌltʃər/| n.|共培养，共同培养|The coculture of different cell types can mimic the in vivo environment.|不同细胞类型的共培养可以模拟体内环境。|例句：The researchers used a coculture system to study the interactions between cancer cells and immune cells. 研究人员使用共培养系统来研究癌细胞和免疫细胞之间的相互作用。

例句：Coculture of bacteria and fungi can be used to produce novel antibiotics.  细菌和真菌的共培养可以用来生产新的抗生素。

近义词：mixed culture, joint culture|反义词: monoculture|词性:noun


Etymology: co- (together) + culture (cultivation)
