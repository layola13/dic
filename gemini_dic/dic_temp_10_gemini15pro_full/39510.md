conoid:/ˈkoʊnɔɪd/ | n. | 圆锥体；类圆锥体 | A solid or surface generated by a straight line moving so that it always intersects a fixed curve and passes through a fixed point not in the plane of the curve. |  A shell has a conoid shape.  贝壳呈圆锥形。| Synonyms: cone, conic | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "sphere" if contrasting shapes) | noun

conoid:/ˈkəʊnɔɪd/ | adj. | 圆锥形的；近似圆锥形的 | Resembling a cone in shape. | The artist created a conoid sculpture. 艺术家创作了一个圆锥形的雕塑。| Synonyms: conical, cone-shaped | Antonyms: (No direct antonym) | adjective
