marmosets:/ˈmɑːrməˌzɛts/ | n. | 狨猴 | Small, New World monkeys with claws instead of nails, except for their big toes.  They are known for their social behavior and complex vocalizations. | Marmosets are popular pets in some countries. 狨猴在一些国家是受欢迎的宠物。 | Marmosets live in the rainforests of South America. 狨猴生活在南美洲的雨林中。 | synonyms: tamarins, monkeys | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific animal) | noun
