decrier:/dɪˈkraɪər/
n.|诽谤者，诋毁者|a person who publicly denounces or criticizes someone or something|The politician was a frequent decrier of the new economic policies. 这位政治家经常公开谴责新的经济政策。|critic, attacker, denouncer|supporter, advocate|noun

decry:/dɪˈkraɪ/
vt.|谴责，诋毁，贬低|to publicly denounce or criticize someone or something|The media decried the government's handling of the crisis. 媒体谴责政府对危机的处理。|denounce, condemn, criticize|praise, commend|verb
