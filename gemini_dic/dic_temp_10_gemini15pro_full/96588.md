inductees:/ɪnˌdʌkˈtiːz/
noun
入选者，新成员
People formally introduced into an organization or position of honor.
The Hall of Fame inductees were honored at a special ceremony.  名人堂的入选者们在一个特殊的仪式上受到了表彰。
The new inductees were given a tour of the facility. 新成员们参观了这个场所。
synonyms: initiates, entrants, newcomers
antonyms: veterans, old-timers
词性: 名词
