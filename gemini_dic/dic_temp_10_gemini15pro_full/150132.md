pamphletic:/pæmflɛtɪk/
adjective
小册子似的；煽动性的
like a pamphlet, especially in being short and strongly expressing a particular opinion
His writing style is quite pamphletic. 他的写作风格很像小册子。
synonyms: polemical, propagandistic, diatribe, tirade
antonyms: measured, temperate, balanced, impartial
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From pamphlet + -ic.  Pamphlet comes from the Middle English Pamphilus, de Amore, a popular 12th-century Latin love poem that was widely circulated.  The term later broadened to encompass any short treatise, especially one on a controversial topic.
