inchangeability:/ɪnˌtʃeɪndʒəˈbɪləti/
n.
不变性，不可改变性
The inchangeability of the laws of physics is a fundamental principle of science.
物理定律的不变性是科学的基本原则。
The document emphasized the inchangeability of the agreement.
该文件强调了协议的不可更改性。
近义词: immutability, constancy, permanence
反义词: mutability, variability, changeability
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + changeable + -ity (forming abstract nouns).  Ultimately from Latin *cambiare* (to exchange).
