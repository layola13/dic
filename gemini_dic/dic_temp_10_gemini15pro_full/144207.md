outsit:/ˌaʊtˈsɪt/
verb
比…坐得久；熬过；在户外待得比…久
to stay outdoors or in a particular place longer than someone or something else

例句：We managed to outsit the storm in the old barn. 我们在旧谷仓里熬过了暴风雨。
