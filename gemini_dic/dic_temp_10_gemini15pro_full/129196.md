nobility:/noʊˈbɪləti/| n.|贵族；高贵；高尚|The quality of being noble in character, mind, or rank.  The group of people belonging to the noble class.|He showed true nobility in refusing the reward. 他拒绝接受奖赏，表现出真正的高尚情操。|Synonyms: aristocracy, peerage, gentry, elite, upper class|Antonyms: commonalty, peasantry, proletariat, rabble|noun
