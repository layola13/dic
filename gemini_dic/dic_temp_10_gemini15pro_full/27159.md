call-the-tune:/ˌkɔːl ðə ˈtjuːn/ | idiom | 掌权；发号施令；做主 | To be in a position of authority or control; to dictate how things are done. |  The CEO calls the tune in this company.  这家公司的首席执行官说了算。

|近义词: be in charge, be in control, run the show, pull the strings, dominate | 反义词: be subservient, be subordinate, follow orders | 词性: idiom

ETYMOLOGY->
This idiom originates from the idea that the person who pays for the musician (the piper) gets to choose the music (the tune). This concept dates back to at least the 16th century.
