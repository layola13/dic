askant:/əˈskænt/ | adj. | 斜视的，怀疑的，不赞成的 | Looking sideways or obliquely; with disapproval or distrust. |  He looked askance at her suggestion. 他对她的建议表示怀疑。 | 近义词: sideways, obliquely, distrustfully, suspiciously | 反义词: directly, straight, trustfully, approvingly | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From a- + skant (obsolete form of slant).  Originally meant "aslant," later acquiring the connotations of suspicion and disapproval.
