nontravelling:/nɒnˈtrævəlɪŋ/
adjective
非旅行的，不旅行的
Not travelling; staying in one place.
Example: The nontravelling spouse often feels lonely.  非旅行的配偶经常感到孤独。

Example:  Nontravelling employees can work remotely. 不出差的员工可以远程工作。


近义词:stationary, resident, immobile, fixed
反义词:travelling, mobile, nomadic, itinerant
词性:adjective
