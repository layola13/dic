outstorming:/ˈaʊtˌstɔːrmɪŋ/
n.
头脑风暴，集体讨论
A process of brainstorming in a group to generate ideas or solve problems.
例句：The team engaged in a vigorous outstorming session to develop a new marketing campaign.  团队进行了一场激烈的集体讨论，以制定新的营销活动。

例句：The outstorming session produced a wealth of innovative ideas for the new product.  集体讨论会议为新产品产生了许多创新想法。


近义词：brainstorming, ideation, group discussion
反义词：(No clear antonym - perhaps individual work, solitary reflection)
词性: noun
