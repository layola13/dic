disregarder:/ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrdər/| n.|漠视者，忽视者|A person who disregards something; one who treats something as unimportant.|He was a disregarder of rules and conventions. 他是一个无视规则和习俗的人。|近义词：ignorer, neglector|反义词: observer, respecter|词性:名词

disregard:/ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrd/| vt.| 漠视，忽视，不理会|To ignore or pay no attention to something.|He disregarded the doctor's advice. 他无视医生的建议。|近义词：ignore, neglect, overlook|反义词: heed, observe, respect|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + regard (to consider or think of in a specified way). Ultimately from Latin re- (back, again) + gardare (to guard, watch).
