conferrer:/kənˈfɜːrər/
n.|授予者，赋予者|Someone who confers something, such as an honor, degree, or gift.|The university president was the conferrer of the honorary degrees.|近义词：giver, donor, grantor, bestower|反义词：receiver, recipient|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *conferren*, from Anglo-Norman *conferrer*, from Latin *conferre* ("to bring together, compare, consult, bestow"), from *com-* ("together") + *ferre* ("to bear, carry").
