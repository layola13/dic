ninia:/ˈniːnjə/ | n. | 盲蛇;细盲蛇 | A small, harmless, burrowing snake of the family Typhlopidae, found in tropical regions. |  These ninias are often mistaken for earthworms. 这些细盲蛇经常被误认为是蚯蚓。|  近义词: blind snake, threadsnake | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: 名词


词源: From Spanish *niña* (“girl, doll, pupil (of the eye)”), from Latin *ninia* (“girl, doll”), from Ancient Greek *νήννος* (*nḗnnos*, “uncle”).  The name likely refers to the snake's small size.
