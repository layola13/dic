osteologist:/ˌɒstiˈɒlədʒɪst/ /ˌɑːstiˈɑːlədʒɪst/
n.
骨学家
A scientist who studies bones, especially ancient bones.
The osteologist carefully examined the skeletal remains found at the archaeological dig.  骨学家仔细检查了在考古挖掘中发现的骨骼遗骸。
The museum's osteologist was able to determine the age and sex of the skeleton.  博物馆的骨学家能够确定骨骼的年龄和性别。
Synonym: bone specialist, skeletal biologist
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps a general scientist who doesn't specialize in bones)
noun
