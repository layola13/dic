biographers:/baɪˈɒɡrəfərz/
n.
传记作者，传记作家
A biographer is a person who writes an account of someone's life.
例句：The biographers spent years researching the president's life.  传记作者花了数年时间研究总统的生平。

例句：Several biographers have written about the famous artist's life.  几位传记作家都写过这位著名艺术家的生平。


近义词：writers, authors, historians
反义词: None (No direct antonym for a profession)
词性: Noun
