bluebottle:/ˈbluːˌbɒtl/ (UK) /ˈbluːˌbɑːtl/ (US)| n.|蓝瓶子；蓝丽蝇；僧帽水母|A bluebottle can refer to several things, including a fly with an iridescent blue abdomen, a plant with blue bell-shaped flowers, or a venomous jellyfish. |例句：
The bluebottle buzzed annoyingly around the picnic table.  蓝丽蝇烦人地在野餐桌周围嗡嗡叫。

We saw a Portuguese man-of-war, a type of bluebottle, washed up on the beach. 我们看到一只僧帽水母（一种蓝瓶水母）被冲到沙滩上。

|近义词：(for fly) blowfly, (for jellyfish) Portuguese man-of-war, (for plant) harebell|反义词: None (meaning-dependent)|词性:noun
