bust-up:/ˈbʌst ʌp/
noun
争吵，打架
a serious argument or fight.
Example: They had a terrible bust-up over money. 他们因为钱的问题大吵了一架。

Example:  The bust-up between the two families lasted for years.  两个家族之间的争吵持续了数年。
Synonym: quarrel, argument, fight, altercation, disagreement, falling-out
Antonym: agreement, reconciliation, harmony, accord
词性: 名词 (n.)

bust-up:/ˈbʌst ʌp/
adjective
破裂的，失败的
broken or failed, especially a relationship.
Example: Their marriage ended in a bust-up. 他们的婚姻以破裂告终。

Example: The business partnership became bust-up after a series of disagreements.  在经历了一系列分歧之后，商业伙伴关系破裂了。

Synonym: broken, failed, ruined, collapsed
Antonym: successful, intact, thriving, flourishing
词性: 形容词 (adj.)
