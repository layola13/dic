lesseeship: /ˌlesiːʃɪp/
n.
租赁权；承租人的身份或地位
The state or condition of being a lessee; the period of time for which one is a lessee.
The lesseeship of the property lasts for 50 years.  这处房产的租期为50年。
The lesseeship passed to his son upon his death. 他去世后，租约转给了他的儿子。

近义词: tenancy, leasehold
反义词: ownership, freehold
词性: noun

词源:  From lessee + -ship. Lessee comes from Anglo-French lesser, from lesser "to let, release," from Latin laxare "to loosen, open, extend, let go."
