dodecatemory:/ˌdoʊdəˈkætəˌmɔri/ /ˌdəʊdəˈkætəˌmɔːri/ | n. |黄道十二宫|The division of the zodiac into twelve equal parts, each associated with a particular sign.  Each of these twelve sections is called a dodecatemory.|The astrologer consulted the dodecatemory to interpret the client's birth chart.| 近义词: zodiac sign, astrological sign | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *dōdekatemōrium*, from Ancient Greek δωδεκατημόριον (dōdekatēmórion), from δωδέκατος (dōdékatos, “twelfth”) + μοῖρα (moîra, “part, portion, fate”).
