hurricane:/ˈhʌrɪkən/ (US), /ˈhʌrɪkeɪn/ (UK)| n. |飓风，暴风|A violent storm with very strong winds, especially in the western Atlantic Ocean.|例句：The hurricane caused widespread damage. 飓风造成了大范围的破坏。

例句：They boarded up their windows in preparation for the hurricane. 他们用木板封住窗户，为飓风做准备。

近义词：cyclone, typhoon, storm|反义词:calm, breeze|词性:名词
