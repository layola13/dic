nonterminals: /nɒnˈtɜːrmɪnəlz/ | n. | 非终结符 |A nonterminal is a symbol that can be replaced by other symbols (terminals or nonterminals) according to the rules of a formal grammar.  Nonterminals are used in context-free grammars and other formal grammar systems to represent syntactic structures. They are often represented by uppercase letters. | In the grammar rule S -> NP VP, S, NP, and VP are nonterminals. 在语法规则 S -> NP VP 中，S、NP 和 VP 都是非终结符。|  symbols, variables | terminals | Noun
