decidua:/dɪˈsɪdʒuə/| n. |蜕膜|The mucous membrane lining the uterus that thickens during pregnancy and is shed after childbirth.  子宫内膜在怀孕期间增厚并在分娩后脱落。|The decidua provides nourishment for the developing embryo. 蜕膜为发育中的胚胎提供营养。|近义词: endometrium (in pregnancy) |反义词: None| Noun
