existences:/ɪɡˈzɪstənsɪz/
n.
存在；生存；生活；存在物
The state of being or existing. The manner of one's life. Something that exists.
Their struggle for existence was hard. 他们为了生存而奋斗，生活艰难。
Synonyms: being, life, presence, actuality, reality
Antonyms: nonexistence, nothingness
