aard:/ɑːrd/| n.|土狼；土豚|Aardvark, a nocturnal, burrowing, ant-eating mammal of southern Africa.|The aardvark uses its powerful claws to dig for termites.|近义词：aardvark, antbear|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Afrikaans *aardvark*, from *aard* ("earth") + *vark* ("pig"). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *erþō* and *ferhaz*.

USAGE->
The aardvark is a nocturnal animal. = 土豚是一种夜行动物。

The aardvark uses its long snout to sniff out ants and termites. = 土豚用它长长的鼻子嗅出蚂蚁和白蚁。
