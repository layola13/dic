coaita:/kɔɪˈɑːtə/ | n. | 蜘蛛猴，卷尾猴 | A type of spider monkey found in South America, known for its long, prehensile tail. |  Coaitas are highly social animals, living in large groups. 蜘蛛猴是高度社会化的动物，生活在大群体中。

Coaitas are skilled climbers and can easily navigate the rainforest canopy. 蜘蛛猴是熟练的攀爬者，可以轻松地在雨林树冠中穿梭。 | 近义词：spider monkey, monkey | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源: From Portuguese *coaita*, from Tupi *kuá'ita*.
