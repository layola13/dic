a-frame:/ˈeɪ freɪm/| n. |A型框架; A字架; 人字形框架|A structure shaped like the letter A, typically used for supporting a roof or other weight.|We stayed in a cute little A-frame cabin in the mountains. 我们在山上一个可爱的小A型小屋里住了一晚。|Synonyms: A-shaped frame, triangular frame|Antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the shape resembling the capital letter "A".  A + frame (from Old North French *framer, “to construct”)

USAGE->
As a noun:

The A-frame house is very popular in mountainous areas. = A型房屋在山区很受欢迎。

They built an a-frame to support the swing. = 他们建了一个A型框架来支撑秋千。


PHRASE->

a-frame cabin = A型小屋

a-frame sign = A型标志
a-frame swing set = A型秋千架
