eurythmy: /ˈjuːrɪθmi/ | n. |  优律思美，律动 |  A system of harmonized body movement and gesture developed by Rudolf Steiner, often accompanied by music or spoken text, intended as an art form and as a means of artistic interpretation or expression. | She studied eurythmy in Switzerland. 她在瑞士学习优律思美。| Synonym: rhythmic movement, expressive movement | Antonym: stillness, immobility | noun

eurythmy: /ˈjʊrɪθmi/ | n. |  和谐，协调 |  Harmony or grace in movement or form.  | The eurythmy of the dancers was mesmerizing. 舞蹈演员们的和谐舞姿令人着迷。| Synonym: harmony, grace, rhythm | Antonym: discord, disharmony, awkwardness | noun
