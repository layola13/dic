cooking:/ˈkʊkɪŋ/ | adj. | 烹调用的，适合烹饪的 | Suitable or designed for cooking. |  We bought some cooking apples. 我们买了一些烹饪用的苹果。 | 近义词: culinary | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cokyng*, verbal noun from *coken* (“to cook”), from Old English *cocian* (“to cook”), from Proto-Germanic *kōkaną* (“to cook”), from Proto-Indo-European *peh₂ḱ- (“to cook, ripen”). Cognate with West Frisian *koekje* (“to cook”), Dutch *koken* (“to cook”), German *kochen* (“to cook”), Swedish *koka* (“to cook”).
