canker:/ˈkæŋkər/ | n. | 溃疡; 腐烂; 弊病; 害虫 | A destructive fungal disease of trees and other plants, characterized by the formation of cankers.  A canker sore. Something that corrodes, corrupts, or destroys. | The apple tree was infected with canker. 这棵苹果树感染了溃疡病。 | synonyms: ulcer, sore, blight, disease, corruption | antonyms: health, well-being | noun

canker:/ˈkæŋkər/ | v. | 溃烂; 腐蚀; 败坏; 患溃疡 | To become infected with canker. To corrode, corrupt, or destroy. |  Resentment cankered her heart. 怨恨腐蚀了她的心。 | synonyms: corrode, erode, fester, decay | antonyms: heal, flourish | verb
