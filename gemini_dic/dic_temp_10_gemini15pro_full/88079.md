highlives:/ˈhaɪˌlaɪvz/| n. |奢侈的生活；放荡的生活|a life of pleasure and luxury, often involving reckless behaviour|They lived high lives, spending money on extravagant parties and trips. 他们过着奢侈的生活，把钱花在奢华的派对和旅行上。|Synonyms: luxurious lifestyle, extravagant living, fast living, riotous living|Antonyms: frugal living, simple life, modest lifestyle|noun
