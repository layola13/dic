bunion:/ˈbʌnjən/| n. |拇囊炎，拇趾外翻|A bunion is a painful swelling on the first joint of the big toe.|My grandmother has a bunion on her foot and it makes it difficult for her to walk. 我的祖母脚上长了拇囊炎，这让她走路很困难。|Example:  The bunion on her foot made it painful to wear shoes. 她脚上的拇囊炎让她穿鞋很痛。|Synonyms: hallux valgus|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a medical condition)|Noun
