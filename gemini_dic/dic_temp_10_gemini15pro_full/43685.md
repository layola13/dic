credible:/ˈkrɛdəb(ə)l/| adj.|可信的，可靠的|Able to be believed; convincing.|Her explanation seemed credible. 她的解释似乎可信。|The witness gave a credible account of the events.  这位证人对事件的描述可信。|近义词：believable, plausible, trustworthy|反义词：unbelievable, implausible, incredible|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin _credibilis_, from _credere_ "to believe."
