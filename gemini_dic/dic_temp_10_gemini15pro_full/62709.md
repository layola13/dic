engineman:/ˈɛndʒɪnmən/ | n. | 火车司机；机工；引擎操作员 | A person who operates or maintains an engine, especially a railroad locomotive. | The engineman skillfully guided the train through the mountains. 火车司机熟练地驾驶火车穿过山区。|  The engineman carefully checked the engine before starting it. 机工在启动引擎前仔细检查了一下。| synonym: engineer, mechanic, operator | antonym: passenger, rider | noun
