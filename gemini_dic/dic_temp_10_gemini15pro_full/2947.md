adultery: /əˈdʌltəri/ | n. | 通奸，婚外情 | Voluntary sexual intercourse between a married person and a person who is not their spouse. |  He was accused of adultery. 他被指控通奸。

She admitted to committing adultery. 她承认犯了通奸罪。

近义词: infidelity, affair, unfaithfulness | 反义词: fidelity, faithfulness | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *avouterie*, from *avouter* (“to commit adultery”), from Vulgar Latin *adulterare*, from Latin *adulterare* (“to corrupt, pollute, falsify, commit adultery”), from *ad* (“to”) + *alter* (“other”).
