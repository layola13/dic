atramentaceous:/ˌætrəmɛnˈteɪʃəs/
adjective
墨黑色的；似墨的
of the color of ink; inky black
例句：The atramentaceous clouds threatened rain. 墨黑色的云预示着即将下雨。

例句：The artist used an atramentaceous paint to create a dramatic effect.  这位艺术家使用墨黑色的颜料来营造戏剧性的效果。

近义词：inky, black, dark, ebony
反义词：light, white, pale
词性：adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *atramentāceus*, from atramentum (“ink”), from ater (“black, dark”).
