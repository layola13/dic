demerit:/diːˈmɛrɪt/
n.|缺点，短处；过失；处分|A mark awarded against someone for a fault or misdeed. |She received a demerit for being late. 她因为迟到而受到处分。|Synonym: fault, flaw, defect, shortcoming|Antonym: merit, advantage, strength
词性: 名词


demerit:/diːˈmɛrɪt/
v.|记过，处分|To give a demerit to. |The teacher demerited him for talking in class. 老师因为他上课讲话而处分他。|Synonym: penalize, punish, censure|Antonym: reward, commend, praise
词性: 动词
