complimentary:/ˌkɒmplɪˈmentəri/ (US) /ˌkɑːmplɪˈmentəri/| adj.|免费的；赞美的；恭维的|Expressing praise or admiration; given or supplied free of charge.|例句：The hotel offered complimentary breakfast.  酒店提供免费早餐。

例句：He made a complimentary remark about her dress. 他对她的裙子赞美了一番。|近义词：free, flattering, laudatory, praising|反义词：critical, derogatory, paid|词性: adjective

complimentary:/ˌkɒmplɪˈmentəri/ (US) /ˌkɑːmplɪˈmentəri/| n.|赠品；免费赠送的物品 (通常用复数形式)|Something given free as a courtesy or favor, often used in the plural form.|例句：The toiletries were complimentary.  这些洗漱用品是免费赠送的。

例句：The hotel provided complimentary tickets to the show. 酒店提供免费的演出门票。|近义词：freebie, giveaway|反义词：purchase|词性: noun (often used in plural)
