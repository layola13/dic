monogamist:/məˈnɑːɡəmɪst/ /məˈnɒɡəmɪst/
n.
一夫一妻制者；单配偶制者
A person who believes in or practices monogamy, the practice of having only one spouse or sexual partner at a time.
例句：He is a committed monogamist and believes in the sanctity of marriage. 他是一个忠诚的一夫一妻制者，并相信婚姻的神圣性。

例句：She identified as a serial monogamist, moving from one long-term relationship to the next. 她认为自己是一个连续一夫一妻制者，从一段长期关系到下一段。

近义词：none (precise synonym, but terms like "faithful spouse" or "committed partner" might be used in certain contexts)
反义词：polygamist, bigamist
词性：noun
