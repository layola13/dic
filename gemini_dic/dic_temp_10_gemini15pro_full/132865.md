nonegregious:/ˌnɒnɪˈɡriːdʒəs/
adjective
无伤大雅的；不严重的；不恶劣的
Not flagrant or egregious; not conspicuously bad or offensive.
His behavior, while not ideal, was nonegregious. 他的行为虽然不理想，但还算不上恶劣。
Synonyms:  unobjectionable, inoffensive, harmless, blameless, venial
Antonyms: egregious, flagrant, outrageous, shocking, appalling
词性: 形容词
