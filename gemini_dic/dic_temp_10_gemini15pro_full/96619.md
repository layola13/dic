indulgential: /ɪnˌdʌlˈdʒɛnʃəl/
adjective
放纵的；纵容的；宽容的
Characterized by or showing indulgence; lenient.  Inclined to indulge.
He gave the children an indulgential smile. 他对孩子们露出了纵容的微笑。
Synonyms: lenient, forgiving, permissive, tolerant, indulgent
Antonyms: strict, severe, harsh, unforgiving, disciplinarian
词性: adjective
