liederkranz: /ˈliːdərkrɑːnts/ | n. | 利德克朗芝干酪（一种味道浓郁的软奶酪） | A type of strong-smelling soft cheese. |  I tried Liederkranz cheese for the first time, and the aroma was overwhelming. 我第一次尝试利德克朗芝干酪，它的香味扑鼻而来。

|  The Liederkranz was ripe and almost runny.  这块利德克朗芝干酪熟透了，几乎要流出来了。| 近义词: Limburger, brick cheese | 反义词:  (No direct opposite, perhaps mild cheeses like mozzarella) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German Liederkranz, literally "wreath of songs," named after a New York City men's singing club.
