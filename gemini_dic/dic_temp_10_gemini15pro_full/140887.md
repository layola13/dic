on-watch:/ɒnˈwɒtʃ/ | adj. | 值班的，警戒中的 | Being on duty and keeping guard or lookout. | The lifeguard was on-watch at the beach. 救生员在海滩上值班。|  The soldiers are on-watch tonight.  今晚士兵们在值班。 | 近义词: on duty, on guard, vigilant, alert | 反义词: off duty, off guard, inattentive | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "on" (preposition indicating location or state) + "watch" (noun, act of observing or guarding).  This compound word combines the meaning of being "in a state of watching" or "actively engaged in watching."
