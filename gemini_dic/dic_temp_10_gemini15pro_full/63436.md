entreaties:/ɪnˈtriːtiz/
noun
恳求；请求； entreaty的复数形式
An earnest or humble request.  The king ignored the entreaties of his advisors. 国王无视了他的顾问们的恳求。
She made repeated entreaties to her father for money. 她多次恳求父亲给她钱。
pleas, appeals, requests, supplications
demands, commands, refusals
n.
