coy:/kɔɪ/ (US) /kɔɪ/ (UK)
adjective
害羞的，腼腆的；忸怩作态的；故作神秘的
(especially with reference to a woman) making a pretense of shyness or modesty that is intended to be alluring.  Reluctant to give details about something.
She gave me a coy smile. 她对我腼腆一笑。
He's being very coy about his new girlfriend.  他对他的新女友故作神秘。
近义词: shy, bashful, demure, reticent, evasive
反义词: bold, brazen, forward, immodest
词性: adjective

词源:
From Middle English *coye, from Old French coi “quiet, still,” from Latin quietus.
