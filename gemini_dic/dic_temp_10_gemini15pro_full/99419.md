interred:/ɪnˈtɜːrd/
verb
埋葬，安葬
past tense and past participle of inter
He was interred with military honors. 他被以军礼安葬。
近义词: buried, entombed
反义词: exhumed
词性: verb

Etymology: from Latin *interrare*, from *in- + terra* "earth."
