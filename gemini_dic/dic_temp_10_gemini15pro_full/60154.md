effed:/ɛft/ | adj. | 搞砸的，完蛋的 |  A euphemism for "fucked," meaning ruined, damaged, or in a bad state.  It's used to express frustration or anger about a situation. |  "The whole project is effed now that the funding fell through."  整个项目完蛋了，因为资金没到位。 | 近义词: screwed, ruined, messed up | 反义词: perfect, fine, intact | 词性: 形容词

词源: effed is a euphemism derived from the past participle of the verb "fuck."  It replaces the vowel sound with a similar one to avoid using the more offensive word.
