PHRASE->

(Note: "had rather" doesn't form many fixed phrases.  It's mostly used independently.)

I had rather not say. 我宁愿不说.
I had rather you didn't do that. 我宁愿你不要那样做.
I had rather stay than go. 我宁愿留下也不愿走。
I had rather die than betray my country. 我宁愿死也不愿背叛我的国家。
He had rather go hungry than steal. 他宁愿挨饿也不愿偷窃。


注意事项:

"had rather"  is becoming less common in modern English, and "would rather" is generally preferred.  It retains a slightly more formal or archaic tone.
