gardon:/ˈɡɑːrdən/ | n. |花园; 菜园; 果园|A piece of ground, often near a house, used for growing flowers, fruit, or vegetables.|例句：The children were playing in the garden. 孩子们在花园里玩耍。

例句：She grows vegetables in her garden. 她在她的花园里种蔬菜。|近义词：yard, lawn, patch|反义词：wilderness|词性：名词
