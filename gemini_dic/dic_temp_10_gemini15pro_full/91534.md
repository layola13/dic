hypanthium:/haɪˈpænθiəm/
n.| 花托筒，托杯|The hypanthium is a floral structure where basal portions of the calyx, corolla, and stamens unite to form a cup-shaped tube. |The rose has a well-developed hypanthium.  玫瑰有一个发达的花托筒。|synonym: floral cup, calyx tube|antonym: N/A
词性: 名词
