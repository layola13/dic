oversoar:/ˌoʊvərˈsɔːr/
verb
高飞；超越；胜过
to fly over; to exceed or surpass
The eagle oversoared the mountain peaks.  这只鹰飞过了山峰。
His ambition oversoared his abilities. 他的野心超过了他的能力。
synonym: surpass, exceed, transcend, outstrip
antonym: fall short of, fail, underperform
词性: 动词
