granters:/ˈɡræn.tərz/ | n. | 授予者；给予者；批准者 | People or organizations that grant something, such as money, permission, or a right. | The granters of the scholarship reviewed hundreds of applications. 奖学金的授予者审查了数百份申请。|  The granters of the license stipulated strict environmental regulations. 许可证的授予者规定了严格的环境法规。 | synonyms: donors, benefactors, contributors, funders | antonyms: recipients, beneficiaries | noun
