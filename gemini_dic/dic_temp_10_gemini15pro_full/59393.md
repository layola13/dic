earsore:/ˈɪəˌsɔːr/
n.
令人厌烦的事物；刺耳的声音
Something unpleasant or annoying to hear; a grating sound.
That song is a real earsore. I've heard it way too many times. 那首歌真是听得耳朵都起茧子了，我已经听过太多遍了。
近义词:annoyance, nuisance, irritant, bother
反义词:pleasure, delight, joy
词性:名词
