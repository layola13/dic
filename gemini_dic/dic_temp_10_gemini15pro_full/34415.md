clatter:/ˈklætər/ /ˈklæɾɚ/ | n. v. | 咔哒声，碰撞声；喧闹声 | a rattling sound as of hard objects striking together; loud noise | The dishes clattered as they were stacked in the sink.  盘子堆放在水槽里发出咔哒声。 | synonyms: rattle, clang, bang, crash | antonyms: silence, quiet | noun

clatter:/ˈklætər/ /ˈklæɾɚ/ | v. | 发出咔哒声；喧闹地快速移动 | to make a rattling sound; to move quickly and noisily | The train clattered across the tracks. 火车咔哒咔哒地穿过铁轨。 | synonyms: rattle, clang, bang, crash | antonyms: tiptoe, creep | verb
