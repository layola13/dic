Many manual jobs are now obsolescent due to automation. 由于自动化，许多手工工作现在正逐渐过时。


近义词: outdated, outmoded, old-fashioned, antiquated, archaic, declining | 反义词: modern, current, contemporary, state-of-the-art, cutting-edge | 词性: adjective
