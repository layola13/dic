de-democratizations:/diːˌdɪmɒkrətɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非民主化；民主倒退 | The process of weakening or eliminating democratic institutions, practices, or principles.  It represents a decline in democratic qualities within a political system. |  The researcher documented the de-democratizations occurring in several countries after the coups.  研究人员记录了政变后几个国家发生的民主倒退。

The recent de-democratizations across the globe are a cause for concern.  最近全球范围内的民主倒退令人担忧。 | 近义词: backsliding, regression, decline, erosion | 反义词: democratization, liberalization, reform | 词性: 名词 (plural)

Etymology:

* de-:  prefix meaning "reverse," "remove," "reduce," from Latin.
* democratization: from democracy + -ization.  Democracy comes from Greek dēmokratia, from dēmos "people" + -kratia "power, rule."
