The old computer system was slow and cumbrous.  这套旧的电脑系统又慢又笨重。 | 近义词：awkward, bulky, clumsy, unwieldy | 反义词：compact, convenient, manageable, portable | 词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *comberous*, from Old French *combrer* (to encumber, obstruct), from Late Latin *cumbrus* (obstacle, barrier), of uncertain origin.
