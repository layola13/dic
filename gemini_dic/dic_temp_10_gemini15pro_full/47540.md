deathlinesses:/ˈdɛθlɪnɪsɪz/
n.
致命性，死亡率
The state or quality of being deadly or causing death; the rate of deaths.
The deathlinesses of the various strains of the virus were compared in the study. 该研究比较了各种病毒株的致命性。
近义词: fatality, mortality, lethality
反义词: harmlessness, safety, innocuousness
词性: noun (plural)


Etymology:
From Middle English *dedlinesse, from Old English *dēaðlīnes, equivalent to deadly +‎ -ness. Cognate with Middle Dutch dōdelīcheit (“mortality”).
