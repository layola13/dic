backshift:/ˈbæˌʃɪft/| n.|回移，倒移; (时态) 回溯|A change from a present, future, or perfect tense to a corresponding past tense in reported speech.|He told me that he went (backshift from 'goes') to the store yesterday. 他告诉我他昨天去了商店。|近义词：sequence of tenses, tense agreement|反义词: 无|词性:名词
