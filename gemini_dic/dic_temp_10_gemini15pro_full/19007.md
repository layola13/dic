bestriding:/bɪˈstraɪdɪŋ/
verb
跨坐，跨立，支配，控制
sitting or standing with one leg on either side of something; extending across or dominating
The Colossus of Rhodes bestrode the harbor entrance.  罗德岛巨像跨立在港口入口。
He bestrides the world of finance. 他控制着金融界。
straddling, spanning, dominating
