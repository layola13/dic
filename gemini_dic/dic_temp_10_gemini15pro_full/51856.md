dextrin:/ˈdɛkstrɪn/ /ˈdɛkstrən/| n. |糊精|A water-soluble gummy substance obtained by hydrolysis of starch, used as an adhesive, as a thickening agent, and in dietary supplements.|The adhesive on the back of the stamp is made of dextrin.|近义词：starch, gum, adhesive|反义词:|noun
