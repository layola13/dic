everyones:/ˈɛvriwʌnz/ |pron.|每个人的；大家的|A nonstandard form of everyone's, meaning "belonging to everyone."  While grammatically incorrect, it's sometimes used informally.| Everyones books were scattered on the floor. (Nonstandard) 大家的书都散落在地板上。|近义词: everyone's|反义词: no one's|词性: pronoun (nonstandard)

ETYMOLOGY->
From every + one + 's (possessive).  The nonstandard "everyones" arises from a misunderstanding of the possessive form applied to the indefinite pronoun "everyone."
