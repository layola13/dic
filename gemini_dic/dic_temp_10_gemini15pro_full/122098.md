mistimes:/mɪsˈtaɪmz/
verb
错误地安排…的时间；使不合时宜
To time or schedule something incorrectly or inappropriately.
He mistimed his jump and fell. 他跳的时机不对，摔倒了。
mistime one's entry into the market 错误地估计进入市场的时间
Synonyms: miscalculate, bungle, fluff
Antonyms: perfect, synchronize, coordinate
词性: 动词
