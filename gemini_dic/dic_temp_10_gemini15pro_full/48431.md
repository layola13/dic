deductibilities:/dɪˌdʌktɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
noun
可扣除额; 扣除项目
Amounts that can be deducted from gross income, especially for tax purposes.
Taxpayers can reduce their taxable income by claiming various deductibilities, such as charitable donations and business expenses.  纳税人可以通过申报各种可扣除额来减少应税收入，例如慈善捐款和商业支出。
The new tax laws have expanded the range of allowable deductibilities for small businesses. 新的税法扩大了小企业的可扣除范围。
synonyms: deductions, allowances, exemptions
antonyms: additions, inclusions
