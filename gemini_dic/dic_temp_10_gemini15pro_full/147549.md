overpeer:/ˌoʊvərˈpɪr/| v. | 俯瞰，超越，胜过 | To look down upon from a higher position; to surpass or excel. |  He overpeered the surrounding hills. 他俯瞰着周围的山丘。

She overpeered her classmates in mathematics. 她在数学方面超过了她的同学们。

| 近义词：surpass, excel, outdo, overlook, dominate | 反义词： lag behind, fall short, be inferior to | 词性: 动词
