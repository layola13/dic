homoclime: /ˈhɒməʊklaɪm/ | n. | 同气候区 | A region with a similar climate to another region. |  The Mediterranean region and parts of California are considered homoclimes due to their similar climates. 地中海地区和加州的部分地区因气候相似而被认为是同气候区。|  analogous climate, similar climate | dissimilar climate | noun
