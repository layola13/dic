lutherans:/ˈluːθərənz/| n.|路德教徒，信义宗信徒|Followers of Martin Luther and his teachings, forming a major branch of Protestant Christianity.|Many Lutherans attend church regularly. 许多路德教徒定期去教堂。|The church hosted a gathering of Lutherans from across the country. 教堂举办了来自全国各地的路德教徒聚会。|近义词：Protestants, Reformists|反义词：Catholics|词性:noun

词源: From Martin Luther (1483-1546), German theologian and leader of the Protestant Reformation + -an (suffix forming nouns denoting adherents of a religion, doctrine, etc.)
