boating: /ˈboʊtɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbəʊtɪŋ/ (UK)| n.| 划船，泛舟 |The act of traveling in a boat, especially for pleasure. | We went boating on the lake last weekend.  我们上周末去湖上划船了。|Synonyms: sailing, yachting, canoeing, kayaking | Antonyms: hiking, driving| noun

Etymology: From the verb "boat" + "-ing" suffix, ultimately from Old English "bāt"
