monophonic:/ˌmɒnəˈfɒnɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəˈfɑːnɪk/| adj.|单音的，单声道的|Producing or consisting of a single melodic line, without harmony or counterpoint.  Characterized by a single channel of sound.|The old record player could only play monophonic recordings.|近义词:single-channel, monaural|反义词:polyphonic, stereophonic|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek *monos* "single, alone" + *phōnē* "sound, voice".
