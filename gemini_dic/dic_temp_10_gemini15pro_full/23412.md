bracingnesses:/ˈbreɪsɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
支撑；使人振奋的特性；鼓舞
The quality of being bracing; invigorating or stimulating qualities.
The bracingnesses of the mountain air restored her health. 山上空气的清新使她恢复了健康。
Synonyms:  invigoratingness, stimulatingness, refreshingness, fortifyingness
Antonyms:  enervatingness, weakeningness, drainingness
词性: noun, plural
