bad-mouthed:/ˌbædˈmaʊðd/
adjective
说坏话的；恶意诽谤的
Criticizing someone spitefully and unfairly, especially by making untrue statements.
He was bad-mouthed by his former colleagues after he left the company. 他离开公司后，以前的同事对他说了很多坏话。
近义词: slanderous, defamatory, malicious
反义词: complimentary, flattering
词性: adjective


bad-mouthed:/ˌbædˈmaʊðd/
verb
恶意诽谤；说坏话
To criticize someone spitefully and unfairly, especially by making untrue statements.
She bad-mouthed her ex-boyfriend to all her friends. 她向她所有的朋友都说了她前男友的坏话。
近义词: slander, defame, malign
反义词: praise, commend
词性: verb


词源:
"bad" + "mouthed"  The word combines "bad" with the past participle of the verb "mouth," meaning to utter or speak.  Thus, "bad-mouthed" literally means speaking bad things about someone.
