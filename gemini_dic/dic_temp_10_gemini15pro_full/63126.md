enslavers:/ɪnˈsleɪvərz/ | n. |奴役者，使人成为奴隶的人|People who enslave others, depriving them of their freedom and forcing them to work against their will.  Those who practice or support the system of slavery.|The enslavers profited greatly from the forced labor of the enslaved people.  奴役者从被奴役人民的强迫劳动中获利颇丰。

The history books often fail to fully acknowledge the cruelty of the enslavers.  历史书常常未能充分认识到奴役者的残酷性。|Synonyms: slaveholders, slave owners, oppressors|Antonyms: liberators, abolitionists|Noun
