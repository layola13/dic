fabricant:/fæb.rɪ.kɑːnt/ | n. | 制造商，制造者 | A person or company that makes something, especially on a large scale. | The fabricant of this car is known for its high quality. 这辆车的制造商以其高质量而闻名。|  The French fabricant has been producing luxury goods for over a century. 这家法国制造商生产奢侈品已有一个多世纪了。 |Synonyms: manufacturer, producer, maker | Antonyms: consumer, buyer | Noun
