concrew: /kənˈkruː/
verb
召集，集合（船员）
to gather together a crew, especially for a ship or aircraft.
例句：The captain concrewed his men on deck. 船长把他的船员召集到甲板上。

例句：They were unable to concrew enough experienced sailors for the voyage. 他们无法为这次航行召集到足够经验丰富的水手。
近义词：assemble, gather, muster, recruit
反义词：disband, disperse
词性：v.

词源:  From con- (together) + crew.  First recorded in the late 16th century.
