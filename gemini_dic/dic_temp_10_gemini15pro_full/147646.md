overpolitical:/ˌoʊvərˈpəlɪtɪkl/ | adj. | 过于政治化的，过分政治化的 | Excessively or inappropriately focused on politics; too much influenced by political considerations. |  The discussion became overpolitical, losing sight of the real issues. 讨论变得过于政治化，忽略了真正的问题。|  The atmosphere in the office has become overpolitical lately.  办公室的气氛最近变得过于政治化了。 | 近义词: hyperpolitical, excessively political | 反义词: apolitical, nonpolitical | 词性: adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "political" (adjective relating to politics).  The prefix "over-" comes from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, over, beyond." "Political" ultimately derives from the Greek word "politikos," meaning "of citizens or the state."
