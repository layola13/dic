four-square:/ˈfɔːrˈskwɛər/| adj. adv.|正直的，公平的；正方形的；坚固的|Marked by boldness and forthrightness; straightforward.  Also, shaped like a square.|He took a four-square stance against the corruption. 他对腐败采取了直截了当的立场。|近义词：honest, upright, forthright, square|反义词:dishonest, crooked, devious|词性:形容词/副词

four-square:/ˈfɔːrˈskwɛər/| n.|一种儿童游戏（四人方块）|A children's game played on a square court divided into four smaller squares, with one player in each square.|The children were playing four-square in the playground. 孩子们在操场上玩四人方块。|近义词：(无直接近义词，可以考虑 hopscotch, jump rope 等其他儿童游戏)|反义词:(无)|词性:名词

词源:  From "four" + "square," literally meaning having four sides and four right angles.  The figurative meaning of "honest" and "straightforward" likely developed from the idea of stability and solidity associated with a square shape.
