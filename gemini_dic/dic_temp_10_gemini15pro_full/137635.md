nontyrannical:/ˌnɒntɪˈrænɪkəl/
adjective
非暴政的；非专制的
Not tyrannical; not exercising power in a cruel, unreasonable, or arbitrary way.
The leader fostered a nontyrannical environment where everyone felt safe to share their opinions.  这位领导营造了一个非专制的环境，每个人都觉得可以安全地分享他们的意见。
The new government promised a nontyrannical rule, respecting the rights of all citizens. 新政府承诺实行非专制统治，尊重所有公民的权利。
Synonyms: democratic, liberal, tolerant, lenient, permissive
Antonyms: tyrannical, despotic, autocratic, dictatorial, oppressive
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + tyrannical.  "Tyrannical" comes from the Late Latin tyrannicus, from Greek tyrannikos, from tyrannos "tyrant."
