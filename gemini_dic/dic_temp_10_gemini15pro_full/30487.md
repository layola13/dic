centrolecithal: /ˌsɛntroʊˈlɛsɪθəl/ | adj. | 中黄的 | Having the yolk concentrated in the center of the egg, as in insects. |  The centrolecithal eggs of Drosophila are a model system for studying early development. 果蝇的中黄卵是研究早期发育的模型系统。|  syn:  None (highly specific term) | ant:  telolecithal | adjective
Etymology -> From centro- + lecithal (from Greek lekithos "yolk").
