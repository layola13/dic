overbaits:/ˌoʊvərˈbeɪts/
verb
过度放饵；用饵过量
To use too much bait when fishing or trapping.
The fisherman overbaited his hook and scared away the fish. 渔夫在鱼钩上放了太多饵，把鱼吓跑了。
overfish, overfeed
underbait
