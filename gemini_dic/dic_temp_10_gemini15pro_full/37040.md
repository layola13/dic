comraderie:/ˌkɑːməˈrɑːdəri/ /ˌkɒməˈrɑːdəri/| n.|友情，同志情谊，同袍情谊|a feeling of friendship and trust among people who work or spend time together|The soldiers developed a strong sense of camaraderie during the war.  士兵们在战争期间形成了强烈的同志情谊。|Example:  There was a wonderful sense of camaraderie among the team members. 团队成员之间有一种很棒的同志情谊。|Synonyms: fellowship, companionship, esprit de corps, solidarity, fraternity, sorority|Antonyms: animosity, hostility, antagonism, enmity|noun
