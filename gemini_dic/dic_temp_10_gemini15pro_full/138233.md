nosopoetic:/ˌnəʊsəʊpəʊˈɛtɪk/ |adj.|疾病诗学的；疾病生成论的|Relating to or denoting the branch of medical humanities that deals with the representation of illness in literature and the arts, or the ways in which illness narratives shape our understanding of disease.|例句：Her novel takes a nosopoetic approach, exploring the psychological impact of chronic pain. 她的小说采用了一种疾病诗学的进路，探讨了慢性疼痛的心理影响。

例句：The conference focused on nosopoetic themes in contemporary cinema, analyzing how films portray mental illness.  这次会议的重点是当代电影中的疾病诗学主题，分析了电影如何描绘精神疾病。|近义词：pathographic,  illness-related|反义词：N/A|词性：adjective

Etymology: From Greek *nosos* (disease) + *poietikos* (productive of).
