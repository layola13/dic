arduous:/ˈɑːrdʒuəs/ | adj. | 艰难的，辛勤的 |  Characterized by effort and difficulty; laborious. | The journey through the desert was arduous. 穿越沙漠的旅程是艰辛的。| synonyms: difficult, challenging, tough | antonyms: easy, simple, effortless | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *arduus* "steep, high, difficult," from Proto-Italic *arðwos* "high, pointed,"  from Proto-Indo-European *h₂erdʰ- "high, to grow."
