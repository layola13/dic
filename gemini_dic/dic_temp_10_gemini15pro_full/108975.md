leeway:/ˈliːweɪ/ /ˈliːˌweɪ/| n.| 余地；自由；余裕；风压差|The ship had 500 yards of leeway in which to maneuver. 这艘船有500码的回旋余地。|近义词：latitude, margin, flexibility, room, space, play|反义词：restriction, limitation|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *līwai, from lī "lee" + wai "way".  Originally a nautical term referring to the sideways drift of a ship to leeward.
