obligedness:/əˈblaɪdʒɪdnəs/| n. |义务，责任，感激|The state of being obliged or indebted to someone or something; a feeling of gratitude or duty.|I felt a deep sense of obligedness to him for his help. 我对他提供的帮助深感感激。|近义词：obligation, duty, gratitude, indebtedness|反义词：ingratitude, ungratefulness|词性:名词

词源: From oblige + -edness.  "Oblige" comes from Old French obliger, from Latin obligare "to bind, tie fast," from ob- "towards" + ligare "to bind."
