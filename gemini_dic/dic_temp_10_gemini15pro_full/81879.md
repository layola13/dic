grieved:/ɡriːvd/
adjective
悲痛的，伤心的
Feeling or expressing great sadness, especially due to loss.
She was deeply grieved by the death of her mother. 她对母亲的去世深感悲痛。
近义词:sorrowful, heartbroken, distressed, devastated
反义词:joyful, happy, elated, delighted
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *greven*, from Old French *grever* "to burden, injure, annoy, harm," from Latin *gravare* "to burden," from *gravis* "heavy."
