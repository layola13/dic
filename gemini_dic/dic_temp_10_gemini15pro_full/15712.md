balanceable:/ˈbælənsəbl/| adj.|可平衡的，可抵消的|Something that is balanceable can be brought into a state of equilibrium or can be offset.|The gymnast demonstrated remarkable balanceable skills on the beam.  体操运动员在平衡木上展现了非凡的平衡技巧。|The opposing forces are balanceable, creating a stable system.  相反的力是可平衡的，创造了一个稳定的系统。|近义词：equilibriable, counterbalancable, offsettable|反义词：unbalanceable, unstable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "balance" (from Old French "balance" and ultimately from Late Latin "bilancia," meaning "two-pan scales") + "-able" (a suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being," "worthy of being," or "tending to").
