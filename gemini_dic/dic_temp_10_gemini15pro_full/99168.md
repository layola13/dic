interchokes:/ˌɪntərˈtʃoʊks/ | n. |相互扼制;相互制约|A state of mutual restraint or interference, where two or more entities limit or hinder each other's actions or development.|The two superpowers were locked in a nuclear interchoke, each afraid to make the first move. 两个超级大国陷入了核相互扼制状态，双方都不敢首先采取行动。|Near synonyms: deadlock, stalemate, impasse, standstill| Near antonyms: cooperation, collaboration, synergy|noun
