fledged:/fleʤd/ | adj. | 羽翼丰满的，成熟的 | Having developed wing feathers large enough for flight; able to fly.  Figuratively, fully developed, experienced, or qualified. | The fledged bird left the nest. 这只羽翼丰满的小鸟离开了巢穴。| 近义词: full-fledged, mature, developed, experienced | 反义词: immature, undeveloped, inexperienced | 词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *fleggen, from Old English *flycge, past participle of *fligan (“to fly”). Cognate with Old Norse fljúga (“to fly”), German fliegen (“to fly”). The -ed suffix forms an adjective from the past participle.
