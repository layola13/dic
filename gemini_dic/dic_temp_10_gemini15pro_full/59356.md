earmuffs: /ˈɪrmʌfs/ | n. | 耳罩；护耳 | A pair of coverings connected by a band, worn over the ears to protect them from cold or noise. |  I wore earmuffs to keep my ears warm in the freezing wind. 我戴着耳罩在寒风中保暖。

She put on earmuffs to block out the noise of the construction. 她戴上耳罩以阻挡施工噪音。 | 近义词: ear protectors, ear warmers | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑: headphones - 耳机- 功能相反) | 词性: 名词

earmuff: /ˈɪrmʌf/ | n. |  耳罩（单只）| A single covering for an ear, part of a pair of earmuffs.  | One of his earmuffs fell off.  他的一只耳罩掉了。 | 近义词: ear protector, ear warmer (单数) | 反义词: (无直接反义词) | 词性: 名词
