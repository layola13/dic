nonsubstitutional:/ˌnɒnsʌbstɪˈtjuːʃənəl/
adjective
非取代的，非替代的
Not capable of being substituted; irreplaceable.  Relating to or denoting a chemical reaction in which one atom or group is not replaced by another.
The nonsubstitutional nature of the enzyme makes it crucial for the specific metabolic pathway.  这种酶的非取代性使其对特定的代谢途径至关重要。
