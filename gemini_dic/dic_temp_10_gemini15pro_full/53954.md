discredence:/dɪˈskredəns/
n.
不信；怀疑；不相信
Lack of belief; disbelief.
例句：His claims were met with widespread discredence. 他的说法遭到了普遍的怀疑。

例句：She expressed discredence at his explanation. 她对他的解释表示怀疑。

近义词：doubt, skepticism, mistrust, disbelief
反义词：belief, faith, trust, credence
词性：名词
