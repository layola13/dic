insisters:/ɪnˈsɪstərz/
n.
坚持者；坚决主张者
People who insist on something; those who persistently advocate for a particular point of view.
The insisters finally persuaded the council to change its policy. 那些坚持者最终说服了委员会改变其政策。
Near Synonyms: advocates, proponents, champions
Antonyms: dissenters, opponents, objectors
词性: 名词
