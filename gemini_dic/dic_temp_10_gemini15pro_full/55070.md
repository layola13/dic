disregardfully:/ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrdfəli/
adverb
轻蔑地，漠视地
showing a lack of respect
He disregardfully tossed the invitation aside. 他轻蔑地把邀请函扔到一边。
She disregardfully ignored her mother's advice. 她漠视了她母亲的建议。
Synonyms: contemptuously, disdainfully, scornfully
Antonyms: respectfully, politely, considerately
词性: 副词
