consolato:/kɒnsoˈlɑːtoʊ/ (US), /kɒnsəˈlɑːtəʊ/ (UK)| n. |领事馆; 安慰; 慰藉|A consulate is an office representing a country in a foreign city, providing services to its citizens abroad. It can also refer to the comfort received by a person after a loss or disappointment. |例句：The Italian consulate in New York handles visa applications. 意大利驻纽约领事馆处理签证申请。

例句：He found some consolato in music after his wife's death.  妻子去世后，他从音乐中找到了一些慰藉。|近义词：comfort, solace, embassy (for building), consolation |反义词: distress, grief, discomfort|词性:noun
