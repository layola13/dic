nonbusily:/ˌnɒnˈbɪzɪli/
adverb
不忙碌地，悠闲地
in a nonbusy manner; leisurely.
She strolled nonbusily through the park, enjoying the sunshine. 她悠闲地在公园里漫步，享受着阳光。
The cat nonbusily cleaned its paws.  猫悠闲地清理着它的爪子。
Synonyms: leisurely, calmly, quietly, peacefully, relaxedly
Antonyms: busily, hurriedly, frantically, actively, energetically
词性: 副词

Derived from "busy" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix) with the prefix "non-" indicating negation.
