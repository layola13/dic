detoxifying:/diːˈtɒksɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
解毒的，排毒的
removing poisonous substances from the body or from something.
例句：She went on a detoxifying diet to cleanse her system. 她进行了一次排毒饮食来清洁她的身体系统。

例句：The detoxifying properties of the herbs are well known. 这些草药的解毒特性是众所周知的。
近义词：cleansing, purifying, purging
反义词：contaminating, polluting
词性：adjective


detoxify:/diːˈtɒksɪfaɪ/
verb
解毒，排毒
to remove poison or toxins from something.
例句：The liver detoxifies the blood. 肝脏可以解毒血液。

例句：This program is designed to detoxify your body and improve your overall health.  这个项目旨在为你身体排毒并改善你的整体健康。
近义词：cleanse, purify, purge
反义词：contaminate, pollute
词性：verb

Etymology:  From de- (meaning "remove" or "reverse") + toxic (meaning "poisonous") + -ify (a suffix that forms verbs meaning "to make or cause to be").  Ultimately derived from the Greek word toxikon (pharmakon), meaning "(poisonous) arrow (drug)".
