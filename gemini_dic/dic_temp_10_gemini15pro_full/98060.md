inquest's:/ˈɪŋkwɛsts/| n.|调查，审讯（尤指死因调查）|A judicial inquiry to ascertain the facts relating to an incident, especially a death.|The inquest's verdict was accidental death. 调查的结论是意外死亡。|synonyms: inquiry, investigation, examination, probe|antonyms: cover-up, concealment|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "enqueste" (inquiry, investigation), from Latin "inquesta" (a seeking, search), past participle of "inquirere" (to seek, inquire).
