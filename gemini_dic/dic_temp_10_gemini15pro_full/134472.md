nonjusticiable:/ˌnɒndʒʌˈstɪʃəbl/ /ˌnɑːndʒuˈstɪʃəbl/
adjective
不可司法审查的；无法诉讼的
Not capable of being settled by a court of law.  Relating to a matter that is not appropriately settled by a court of law.
The issue of whether a war should be declared is considered nonjusticiable by many.  许多人认为是否宣战的问题是不可司法审查的。
The boundary dispute between the two countries became a nonjusticiable matter due to political sensitivities.  由于政治敏感性，两国之间的边界争端变成了一个无法诉讼的问题。
近义词: non-actionable, nonreviewable
反义词: justiciable, actionable
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From non- (not) + justiciable (capable of being settled by a court of law), from justice + -iable (capable of).  Ultimately from Latin *justitia (justice).
