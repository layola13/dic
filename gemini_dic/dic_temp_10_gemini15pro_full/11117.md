archdeaconship:/ˌɑːrtʃˈdiːkənʃɪp/
n.
副主教的职位或辖区
the office or jurisdiction of an archdeacon
He was elevated to the archdeaconship of York. 他被提升为约克的副主教。

近义词: archdeaconry
反义词: (No direct antonym exists.  One could consider perhaps "laity" or a lower ecclesiastical office, but these are not true opposites.)
词性: Noun
