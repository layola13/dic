Exxon: /ˈeksɒn/ (UK) /ˈeksɑːn/ (US)| n. |埃克森|A major American oil and gas corporation, formerly known as Standard Oil of New Jersey.|Exxon was one of the largest companies in the world. 埃克森曾是世界上最大的公司之一。|ExxonMobil merged with Mobil in 1999. 埃克森公司于1999年与美孚公司合并。|Synonyms: oil company, petroleum company|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Shortened from "Exxon Corporation", which itself was a name created in 1972. The "Exx" part is believed to be derived from the "Ess" in Standard Oil's former name, "Esso", while the "on" was added for a strong and memorable sound.  The name "Esso" itself was a phonetic pronunciation of the initials "S.O." of Standard Oil.
