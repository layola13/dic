metallically:/məˈtælɪkli/ | adv. | 金属般地；像金属一样地 | In a way that resembles metal, especially in terms of sound, appearance, or texture. | The robot moved metallically across the floor.  机器人在地板上发出金属般的声音移动。|Synonyms: resonantly, clangorously, ringingly | Antonyms: dully, softly, mutedly | 词性: 副词

metallically:/məˈtælɪkli/ | adv. | 金属般地；像金属一样地 |  Having the qualities or appearance of metal. |  The paint dried to a metallically smooth finish. 油漆干后呈现出金属般光滑的表面。|Synonyms: gleamingly, lustrously, shinily | Antonyms: dully, mattely, roughly | 词性: 副词

词源: From metallic + -ly.  Metallic comes from the Late Latin metallicus, from Latin metallum ("metal"), from Greek μέταλλον (metallon).
