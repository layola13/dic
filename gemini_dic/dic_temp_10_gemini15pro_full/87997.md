high-resolution:/ˌhaɪ ˌrɛzəˈluːʃən/ | adj. | 高分辨率的 |  Having or producing a sharp, clear image, sound, or other output, due to a large number of pixels or other data points used to represent it. |  This camera takes high-resolution photographs. 这款相机可以拍摄高分辨率的照片。| synonyms: high-definition, sharp, clear, detailed | antonyms: low-resolution, blurry, fuzzy, pixelated | adjective

high-resolution:/ˌhaɪ ˌrɛzəˈluːʃən/ | n. | 高分辨率 | The quality of being high-resolution. (Less common as a noun) | The high-resolution of the image allowed us to zoom in on the details.  图像的高分辨率使我们能够放大细节。| synonyms: high-definition, clarity, sharpness | antonyms: low-resolution, blurriness, fuzziness | noun
