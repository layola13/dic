forgetfulnesses:/fərˈɡɛtfʊlnəsɪz/
n.
健忘；遗忘（forgetfulness的复数）
The state of being forgetful; tending to forget.
Her forgetfulnesses are becoming more frequent. 她的健忘越来越频繁了。
近义词:oblivions, amnesias
反义词:remembrances, mindfulness
词性:名词

forgetfulness:/fərˈɡɛtfʊlnəs/
n.
健忘；遗忘
The state of being forgetful; tending to forget.
Her forgetfulness is becoming a problem. 她的健忘正在成为一个问题。
近义词:oblivion, amnesia
反义词:remembrance, mindfulness
词性:名词

词源：From forgetful + -ness.  Ultimately from Old English *forġietan "to lose memory of," composed of for- "away" + -gietan "to get."
