drudged:/drʌdʒd/
verb
辛苦工作；做苦工
worked hard and monotonously; toiled
She drudged away at her homework all weekend. 她整个周末都在辛苦地做作业。
近义词: toiled, slaved, labored
反义词: relaxed, idled, rested
词性: verb (past tense of "drudge")

drudge:/drʌdʒ/
noun
做苦工的人；单调乏味的工作
a person who does tedious, menial, or unpleasant work
Cleaning the stables was a real drudge.  打扫马厩真是件苦差事。
近义词: laborer, worker, toiler
反义词: idler, loafer
词性: noun

drudge:/drʌdʒ/
verb
做苦工；辛苦工作
to work hard and monotonously; to toil
He drudges at his desk job every day. 他每天都埋头苦干他的办公室工作。
近义词: toil, slave, labor
反义词: relax, idle, rest
词性: verb


词源: From Middle English *druggen, from Old Norse *drugga (“to do heavy work, to trudge”), from Proto-Germanic *druggōną (“to pull, drag”). Related to drag.
