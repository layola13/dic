overbill:/ˌoʊvərˈbɪl/
verb
过度收费，超额收费
To charge someone more than the proper amount.
The contractor overbilled us by $500.  承包商多收了我们500美元。
The restaurant overbilled us for our meal. 餐厅对我们的餐费多收了钱。
近义词: overcharge
反义词: underbill, undercharge
词性: verb

overbill:/ˌoʊvərˈbɪl/
noun
超额账单
A bill that charges more than the proper amount.
The customer complained about the overbill. 客户投诉账单超额收费。
We received an overbill from the phone company. 我们收到了电话公司寄来的超额账单。
近义词: overcharge
反义词: underbill, undercharge
词性: noun
