PHRASE->
avocat commis d'office (French) = court-appointed lawyer
avocat conseil (French) = legal advisor/counsel
avocat général (French) = public prosecutor (in some jurisdictions)
avocat stagiaire (French) = trainee lawyer/pupil barrister
avocat spécialisé (French) = specialist lawyer


词源：From Old French avocat, from Latin advocatus (“one called to [one’s aid], advocate, pleader, intercessor”), past participle of advocare (“to call to [one’s aid], summon, call”), from ad- + vocare (“to call”).  Doublet of advocate.
