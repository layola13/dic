milkinesses:/ˈmɪlkɪnɪsɪz/
n.
乳状，乳白色；牛奶状；像牛奶的东西
the quality or state of being milky.
The milkinesses of the water made it difficult to see.  水的乳白色使人难以看清。
The various milkinesses of the different gemstones intrigued the jeweler.  不同宝石的各种乳白色引起了珠宝商的兴趣。
opacity, cloudiness
clarity, transparency
noun
