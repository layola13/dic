antestomach:/ˈæntiˌstʌmək/| n. |前胃|The part of the digestive system in some animals (such as ruminants and birds) that precedes the true stomach. It serves as a storage or preliminary digestive chamber.| Cows have a four-compartment antestomach.  奶牛有四室的前胃。|The bird regurgitated food from its antestomach to feed its young. 鸟儿从前胃里反刍食物来喂养幼鸟。|Synonym: proventriculus, crop (in birds)|Antonym: stomach (in a strict sense, though functionally related)|noun
