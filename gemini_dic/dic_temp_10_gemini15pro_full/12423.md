ask-for-it: /ˈæsk fɔːr ɪt/ | idiom | 自找的；活该；自作自受 |  Used to say that someone deserves something unpleasant that happens to them because of something they have done. | He kept teasing the dog, so he asked for it when he got bitten. 他一直逗那只狗，所以被咬也是活该。 | 近义词: deserve it, bring it on oneself | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "undeserved") | idiom


ask-for-it: /ˈæsk fɔːr ɪt/ | idiom |  要求得到（通常指不合理或过分的要求） | Used to describe a situation where someone makes a request that is considered unreasonable or excessive.  |  He asked for a raise after only a week on the job - he was asking for it! 他才工作一个星期就要求加薪——真是自不量力！ | 近义词:  push one's luck, overreach | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | idiom
