backgrounding:/ˈbækˌɡraʊndɪŋ/ | n. | 背景介绍；幕后活动；降低（牲畜饲料）的蛋白质含量 | The article provides some backgrounding on the history of the conflict. 这篇文章提供了一些关于冲突历史的背景资料. | Example: The documentary provided thorough backgrounding on the political climate of the time.  这部纪录片对当时的政治气候进行了全面的背景介绍。

Example:  The CEO spent the week backgrounding reporters on the upcoming merger. 首席执行官花了一周时间向记者介绍即将进行的合并。 | Synonyms: briefing, preparation, groundwork, context | Antonyms: foregrounding, highlighting | noun

backgrounding:/ˈbækˌɡraʊndɪŋ/ | v. |  提供背景资料；将…置于次要地位; 降低（牲畜饲料）的蛋白质含量 | The author backgrounded the main character's tragic past to explain his present behavior. 作者介绍了主人公悲惨的过去，以解释他现在的行为。 | Example: He backgrounded the information about the company's financial troubles. 他将公司财务困境的信息置于次要地位。

Example: The farmer is backgrounding his cattle before moving them to the feedlot. 这位农场主在把牛送到饲养场之前，正在降低它们的饲料蛋白质含量。 | Synonyms: contextualize, explain, downplay, de-emphasize | Antonyms: foreground, emphasize, highlight | verb
