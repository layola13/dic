debasers:/dɪˈbeɪsərz/
n.
堕落者，贬低者
Those who lower the quality, value, or character of something or someone.
The debasers of the currency were severely punished.  这些货币贬值者受到了严厉的惩罚。
synonyms: corrupters, degraders, perverters
antonyms: elevators, improvers, enhancers
noun

debase:/dɪˈbeɪs/
v.
降低，贬低
To reduce the quality, value, or character of something.
Do not debase yourself by lying. 不要用谎言贬低自己。
synonyms: degrade, cheapen, corrupt
antonyms: elevate, enhance, improve
verb
