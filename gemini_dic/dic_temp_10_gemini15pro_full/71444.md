flamboyance:/flæmˈbɔɪəns/
n.|华丽, 炫耀, 艳丽|The quality of being flamboyant; extravagant elaborateness.|Her flamboyant personality made her the center of attention. 她 flamboyant 的个性使她成为关注的焦点。|
近义词:ostentation, showiness, extravagance, flashiness
反义词:modesty, restraint, simplicity, understatement
词性:noun

flamboyant:/flæmˈbɔɪənt/
adj.|华丽的, 炫耀的, 艳丽的|Marked by or given to strikingly elaborate or colorful display or behavior.|He wore a flamboyant pink suit to the party. 他穿着一套华丽的粉红色西装去参加派对。|
近义词:ostentatious, showy, extravagant, flashy
反义词:modest, restrained, simple, understated
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *flamboyant* "flaming," present participle of *flamboyer* "to flame," from *flambe* "flame," from Old French *flamme*, from Latin *flamma*.  The architectural sense arose from the flame-like tracery of windows in this style.
