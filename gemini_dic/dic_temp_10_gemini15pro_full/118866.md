metapophysis:/ˌmɛtəˈpɒfɪsɪs/
|n.|
|后关节突|
|A bony process extending dorsally from the superior articular processes of a vertebra, especially in the lumbar region of mammals.|
|The metapophysis of the lumbar vertebrae provides attachment for muscles supporting the spine.|腰椎的后关节突为支撑脊柱的肌肉提供了附着点。|
|近义词: articular process, zygapophysis|
|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps body of vertebra as a contrasting structure)|
|词性: noun|
|词源:  From Greek *metá* ("after, behind, beyond") + *apophysis* ("offshoot, outgrowth").|
