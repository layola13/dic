eupatorium:/juːpəˈtɔːriəm/ | n. |  泽兰属植物；泽兰 | A genus of plants of the daisy family, typically having heads of small purplish flowers. |  Eupatorium perfoliatum is commonly known as boneset. 穿叶泽兰通常被称为骨节草。 | Eupatorium cannabinum grows in damp areas.  大麻叶泽兰生长在潮湿地区。| synonyms: boneset, thoroughwort, ageratum | antonyms: N/A | noun
