aphasic: /əˈfeɪzɪk/ /əˈfeɪsɪk/ | adj. n. | 失语的；失语症患者 |  Relating to or affected by aphasia; a person suffering from aphasia. | The stroke left him aphasic. 中风使他失语了。|  The aphasic patient struggled to communicate.  这位失语症患者难以沟通。 | synonyms: speechless, tongue-tied, inarticulate | antonyms: articulate, eloquent | adjective, noun

aphasia: /əˈfeɪʒə/ /əˈfeɪziə/ | n. | 失语症 | Loss of ability to understand or express speech, caused by brain damage. |  After the accident, she suffered from aphasia. 事故发生后，她患上了失语症。| Aphasia can make communication very difficult. 失语症会使沟通变得非常困难。 | synonyms: loss of speech, dysphasia | antonyms: fluency | noun
