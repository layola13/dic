freewill:/ˌfriːˈwɪl/ | n. | 自由意志；自主性 | The ability to choose between different possible courses of action unimpeded. |  He believes that humans have freewill and are responsible for their own choices.  他相信人类拥有自由意志，并对自己的选择负责。 | autonomy, volition, self-determination | determinism, fate, predestination | noun

freewill:/ˌfriːˈwɪl/ | adj. | 自由意志的；自主的 | Done of one's own free will; voluntary. |  It was a freewill offering, not something he was forced to give.  这是一个自愿的奉献，而不是他被迫给予的。 | voluntary, uncoerced, willing | involuntary, compelled, forced | adjective
