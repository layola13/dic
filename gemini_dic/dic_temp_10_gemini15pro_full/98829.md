intelligencia:/ɪnˌtɛlɪˈdʒɛnsɪə/| n. |知识分子，知识界|The intelligentsia of the country were critical of the government's policies. 这个国家的知识分子批评政府的政策。|例句：The intelligentsia often gathers in cafes to discuss current events. 知识分子经常聚集在咖啡馆讨论时事。

The government's policies were unpopular with the intelligentsia. 政府的政策不受知识分子欢迎。|近义词：intellectuals, educated class, literati|反义词:masses, common people|词性:noun
Etymology: From Russian интеллигенция (intelligencija), from Polish inteligencja, from Latin intelligentia ("understanding, knowledge"). Ultimately from intelligere ("to understand").
