consign:/kənˈsaɪn/
verb
交付；委托；托运；打发；使…处于（不好的境地）
to deliver (something) to a person's custody, typically in order for it to be sold
例句：
The goods were consigned to the care of the auctioneer.
这些货物被委托给了拍卖师保管。

She consigned the letter to the flames.
她把信付之一炬。

近义词：commit, entrust, deliver, remit, assign
反义词：retain, keep, withhold
词性：verb


consigned:/kənˈsaɪnd/
adjective
被打发的；被托运的
delivered to another's custody
例句：
The consigned goods arrived at the warehouse.
托运的货物到达了仓库。

The painting was a consigned work at the gallery.
这幅画是画廊寄售的作品。

近义词：entrusted, delivered, committed
反义词：retained, kept, withheld
词性：adjective (rarely used)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French consigner, from Latin consignare  "to mark with a seal, sign, stamp," from com- "together" (see com-) + signare "to mark," from signum "identifying mark, token, indication"
