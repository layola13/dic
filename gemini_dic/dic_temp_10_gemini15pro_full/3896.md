agreement:/əˈɡriːmənt/ | n. | 协议；同意；一致 | A negotiated and typically legally binding arrangement between parties as to a course of action. |  We finally reached an agreement on the price. 我们最终就价格达成了协议。

The two countries signed a peace agreement. 这两个国家签署了一项和平协议。 | synonym: accord, contract, deal | antonym: disagreement, dispute, conflict | noun
