papillulate: /ˌpæpɪˈljuːleɪt/ | adj. | 乳头状的，有小乳突的 | Covered with or resembling small papillae (nipple-like projections). | The mushroom's cap was papillulate, covered in tiny bumps. 蘑菇的菌盖呈乳头状，上面覆盖着微小的突起。 | The leaf surface is papillulate. 叶表面有小乳突。 | 近义词: papillose, papillary | 反义词: smooth | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *papillula, diminutive of papilla (“nipple”).
