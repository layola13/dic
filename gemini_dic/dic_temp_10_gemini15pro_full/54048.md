disdainable:/dɪsˈdeɪnəbl/
adjective
可鄙的，轻蔑的
Worthy of or deserving disdain; contemptible.
He gave a disdainable snort at the suggestion. 他对这个建议不屑一顾地哼了一声。
近义词：contemptible, despicable, scornful
反义词：admirable, respectable, laudable
词性：adjective
词源：From disdain + -able.  Disdain comes from Old French desdeigner, from des- (expressing negation) + deigner "to deign," from Latin dignari "to deem worthy."
