camel:/ˈkæməl/| n.|骆驼|A large, long-necked ungulate mammal of arid regions of Africa and Asia, adapted to desert conditions and able to go without water for considerable periods.|The camel carried heavy loads across the desert. 这只骆驼载着沉重的货物穿越沙漠。|近义词：dromedary (单峰驼)|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用"other desert animals" 或 "non-desert animals")|名词
