bound-with: /ˌbaʊndˈwɪð/ |adj.|合订的，装订在一起的|Refers to books or pamphlets that are physically bound together, often because they are related in subject matter or were originally issued together. |例句: The first edition was bound-with a supplement containing maps. 第一版与包含地图的附录合订在一起。
