cyperaceous:/ˌsaɪpəˈreɪʃəs/
adjective
莎草科的；像莎草的
Relating to or resembling plants of the sedge family (Cyperaceae), which includes sedges, papyrus, and cotton-grass.  Characterized by triangular stems, grass-like leaves, and inconspicuous flowers.
The cyperaceous plants thrived in the marshy environment.  这些莎草科植物在沼泽环境中茁壮成长。
Synonyms: sedge-like, grassy
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but could use terms like "woody," "tree-like" to indicate contrasting plant structures)
word class: adjective
