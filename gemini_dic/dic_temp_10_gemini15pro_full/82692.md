guey:/ɡweɪ/ | n. | 伙计，家伙，老兄 (Mexican Spanish slang) | A term of address for a man, similar to "dude," "guy," or "mate." Primarily used in Mexican Spanish slang. Can be used affectionately or dismissively depending on context and tone. | Example 1: ¡Qué onda, guey! (What's up, dude!) | Example 2: Ese guey es bien chido. (That guy is really cool.) | 近义词: dude, guy, mate, fellow, chap | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a term of address. The opposite sentiment might be expressed through insults.) | 词性: noun
