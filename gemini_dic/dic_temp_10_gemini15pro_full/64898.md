essenti:/ɪˈsɛnʃi/ | adj. | 必不可少的，本质的 |  Not a complete word in itself.  Likely intended as the root of "essential." Derived from Late Latin *essentialis*, from essentia "being, essence," from esse "to be."  Means absolutely necessary; extremely important. | Example:  (Using "essential") Essential oils are used in aromatherapy.  精油用于芳香疗法。 | Synonym: crucial, vital, indispensable | Antonym:  nonessential, unimportant, trivial | Word type: Root/Stem (becomes an adjective with the suffix "-al")
