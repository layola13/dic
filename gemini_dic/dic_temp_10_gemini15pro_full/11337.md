areligious:/ˌeɪrɪˈlɪdʒəs/
adjective
无宗教信仰的；不信教的
Not religious; having no religious beliefs.
She's always been areligious, even as a child. 她从小就没有任何宗教信仰。
He grew up in an areligious household. 他在一个没有宗教信仰的家庭中长大。
近义词:nonreligious, secular, unbelieving, irreligious
反义词:religious, devout, pious
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (not) + religious. The prefix "a-" signifies negation or absence.  "Religious" comes from the Latin "religiosus," meaning "careful, conscientious, scrupulous," and later, "observant of religious duties."
