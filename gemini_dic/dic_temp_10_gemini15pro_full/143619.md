outjests:/ˌaʊtˈdʒɛst/
verb
胜过…的玩笑；比…更会开玩笑
to surpass in jesting; to outdo or surpass someone in making jokes or witty remarks.
He outjested all the other comedians at the festival. 他在节日上比其他所有喜剧演员的玩笑都更胜一筹。
Synonyms: outwit, surpass, excel, outdo
Antonyms:  be outdone, lose to
词性:动词
