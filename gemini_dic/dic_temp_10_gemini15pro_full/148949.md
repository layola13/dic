overzealous:/ˌoʊvərˈzɛləs/| adj.|过分热心的；过分积极的|Characterized by excessive zeal; overly enthusiastic or eager.|He was an overzealous salesman, pushing products that customers didn't need. 他是一个过分热心的推销员，推销顾客不需要的产品。

The security guard was overzealous in his duties, checking everyone's bag multiple times.  保安在履行职责时过于热情，反复检查每个人的包。|近义词：fanatical, fervent, impassioned, excessive, extreme|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, lackadaisical, moderate|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + zealous.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Zealous" comes from the Greek word "zelos," meaning "zeal" or "fervor."
