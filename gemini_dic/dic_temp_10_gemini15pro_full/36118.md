coercive:/kəʊˈɜːsɪv/| adj.|强制的，胁迫的|relating to or using force or threats.|His coercive tactics didn't work on her.  他的高压策略对她不起作用。|近义词：oppressive, tyrannical|反义词：gentle, lenient|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *coercitivus, from Latin coercere "to restrain, confine," from co- "together" + arcere "to enclose, confine."
