orthopedic:/ˌɔːrθəˈpiːdɪk/ /ˌɔːrθəˈpidɪk/| adj. n.|骨科的；矫形的；矫形外科医生|Relating to the branch of medicine dealing with the correction of deformities of bones or muscles.|例句：She went to see an orthopedic surgeon about her knee. 她去看了一位骨科医生治疗她的膝盖。|例句：He needs orthopedic shoes to correct his flat feet. 他需要矫形鞋来矫正他的扁平足。|近义词：orthopaedic, musculoskeletal|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词/名词


orthopedist:/ˌɔːrθəˈpiːdɪst/ /ˌɔːrθəˈpidɪst/| n.|骨科医生|A physician who specializes in orthopedics.|例句：The orthopedist recommended surgery for her broken hip. 骨科医生建议她做髋关节骨折手术。|例句：My orthopedist prescribed physical therapy for my back pain. 我的骨科医生为我的背痛开了物理治疗处方。|近义词：orthopaedic surgeon, bone doctor|反义词: N/A|词性:名词



ETYMOLOGY->
From French *orthopédie*, from Greek *orthos* (straight, correct) + *pais* (child). Originally focused on correcting deformities in children.
