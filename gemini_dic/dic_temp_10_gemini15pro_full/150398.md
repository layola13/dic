panoramically:/ˌpænərˈæmɪkli/ | adv. | 全景地；全貌地 | In a way that shows a wide view of an area, like a panorama. |  We viewed the city panoramically from the top of the hill. 我们从山顶上饱览了这座城市的景色。| synonym: comprehensively, broadly, widely | antonym: narrowly, restrictedly | adverb
