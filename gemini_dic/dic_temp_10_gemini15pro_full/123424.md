monopody: /məˈnɒpədi/ (UK) /məˈnɑːpədi/ (US)| n.|独脚架，单脚支撑，单轴生长|A support for a camera or other instrument, having a single leg or pole. Also, a type of growth in plants where the main stem continues to grow from a single apical bud.|例句：The photographer used a monopod to steady his camera for the long exposure. 摄影师用独脚架稳定相机进行长时间曝光。

例句：The palm tree exhibited monopodial growth, with a single trunk reaching towards the sky.  棕榈树呈现出单轴生长，一根树干伸向天空。|近义词：unipod, single leg support|反义词: tripod, bipod|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek mónos (μόνος, “single, alone”) + poús (πούς, “foot”).
