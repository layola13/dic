nonapparitional:/ˌnɒnˌæpəˈrɪʃənəl/
adjective
非幽灵的；非幻影的；非鬼魂的
Not of or pertaining to an apparition; not involving or caused by a ghost or spirit.
The explanation for the strange noises was entirely nonapparitional, simply the wind rattling the window panes.  奇怪的声音完全是非幽灵造成的，只是风吹动窗玻璃发出的声音。
The scientist offered a nonapparitional explanation for the mysterious lights. 科学家对这些神秘的光芒提供了一个非超自然的解释。
Near Synonyms:  mundane, earthly, material, physical, tangible, real, concrete
Antonyms: spectral, ghostly, phantom, apparitional, supernatural, otherworldly
