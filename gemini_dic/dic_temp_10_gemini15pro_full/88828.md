hogget:/ˈhɒɡɪt/| n.|一岁绵羊，两岁绵羊，有时也指一岁牛|A hogget is a sheep in its second year, or sometimes a yearling calf.|The farmer sold his hoggets at the market. 农民在市场上卖掉了他的两岁羊。| The prize-winning hogget was admired by all.  这头获奖的两岁羊受到了所有人的赞赏。|Synonyms: yearling, shearling (for sheep), yearling (for cattle)|Antonyms: lamb (younger sheep), calf (younger cow)|noun
