obeisance:/oʊˈbeɪsəns/ /əˈbiːsəns/| n. | 敬礼，鞠躬，尊崇 |  An act showing respect and submission; a gesture expressing deference, such as a bow or curtsy. |  The knight made a deep obeisance to the queen.  骑士向女王深深鞠了一躬。 | synonyms: bow, curtsy, deference, homage, reverence, salute | antonyms: disrespect, irreverence, defiance | noun
