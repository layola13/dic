binoculate:/baɪˈnɒkjʊlət/| adj.|双目的，具有两只眼睛的|Having two eyes; two-eyed. |例句：Binocular vision allows for depth perception. 双眼视觉使我们能够感知深度。

例句：Most primates are binoculate. 大多数灵长类动物都有两只眼睛。|近义词：two-eyed|反义词:monocular, one-eyed|词性:adjective

词源: From Latin bini ("two by two, two each") + oculus ("eye").
