macrobiotics:/ˌmækroʊbaɪˈɒtɪks/| n.|长寿饮食法；长寿学|A system of principles for a way of life based on the belief that health and happiness depend on a natural diet of unprocessed foods, mainly grains, vegetables, and beans, and on an understanding of the forces of yin and yang.|She's very interested in macrobiotics and healthy eating. 她对长寿饮食法和健康饮食很感兴趣。|Synonyms: healthy diet, natural diet|Antonyms: unhealthy diet, processed food diet|noun


macrobiotic:/ˌmækroʊbaɪˈɒtɪk/| adj.|长寿饮食法的；长寿学的|Relating to macrobiotics.|She follows a macrobiotic diet. 她遵循长寿饮食法。|Synonyms: health-conscious, holistic|Antonyms: unhealthy, processed|adjective
