noncoagulation:/ˌnɒnkoʊˌæɡjuˈleɪʃən/
n.
非凝固；不凝结
The noncoagulation of the blood is a serious medical concern. 血液不凝固是一个严重的医学问题。
The experiment demonstrated the noncoagulation of the sample.  实验显示了样本的非凝固性。
non-clotting, incoagulability
coagulation, clotting

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + coagulation (from Latin coagulātiō, from coagulāre, "to curdle").
