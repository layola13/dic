narrowest:/ˈnæroʊɪst/| adj.|最狭窄的|Superlative form of narrow: most narrow.  Having the least width.|The narrowest part of the road is just ahead.  道路最窄的部分就在前面。|近义词：thinnest, slimmest, tightest|反义词:widest, broadest|词性:形容词
词源:From Old English *nearu "near, close, confined," from Proto-Germanic *narwaz "confined, pressed," related to Old Norse nær "near," German nahe "near." The superlative suffix -est is also from Old English.
