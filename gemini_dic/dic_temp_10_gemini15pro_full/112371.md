lose-out:/luːz ˈaʊt/ | phrasal verb | 输掉; 错失良机; 处于劣势 | To be unsuccessful in a competition or to fail to gain an advantage. |  He lost out on the promotion to a younger colleague. 他输给了一位更年轻的同事，失去了晋升的机会。

|  She lost out on the chance to study abroad. 她错失了出国留学的机会。 | 近义词: lose, miss out, be deprived of, fail | 反义词: win, succeed, triumph, gain | 词性: 动词短语
