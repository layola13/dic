hoarily:/ˈhɔːrɪli/| adv.|灰白地，苍白地；古老地|Covered with or as if with hoar; gray or white with age.  Showing characteristics of age, especially great age.|The ancient stones stood hoarily against the stormy sky. 古老的石头灰白地矗立在暴风雨的天空下。|近义词：grayly, whitely, anciently|反义词:youthfully, newly|词性:副词
词源: From hoar + -ly.  Hoar comes from Old English hār, meaning "gray" or "white," related to Old High German hēr, and ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hairaz.
