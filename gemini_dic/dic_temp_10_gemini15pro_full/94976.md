in-the-cards:/ɪn ðə kɑːrdz/ | adj. | 注定的；可能的；很可能的 | Likely to happen; predictable. | Example: It's in the cards that they'll get married eventually.  他们最终结婚是注定的。 | Example:  A promotion is definitely in the cards for her.  她升职是板上钉钉的事。 | Synonyms: likely, probable, foreseeable, destined, inevitable | Antonyms: unlikely, improbable, doubtful | Adjective
