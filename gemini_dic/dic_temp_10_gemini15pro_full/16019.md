banana:/bəˈnænə/ | n. | 香蕉 | A long curved fruit with a yellow skin and soft sweet flesh. | Example 1: I eat a banana every morning for breakfast. 我每天早上早餐吃一根香蕉。

Example 2: Monkeys love to eat bananas. 猴子喜欢吃香蕉。 | 近义词: plantain (cooking banana) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a fruit) | 词性: noun
