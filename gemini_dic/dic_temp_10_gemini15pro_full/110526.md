limbous:/ˈlɪmbəs/| adj.|边缘的，模糊的，昏暗的|Being in a state of uncertainty or in a transitional stage; bordering on something.  Existing in a poorly defined state, neither here nor there.|The photograph captured the limbous region between dream and reality. 这张照片捕捉到了梦境与现实之间模糊的区域。|近义词：obscure, vague, indistinct, shadowy, twilight, nebulous|反义词：clear, distinct, defined, bright|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin limbus (“border, fringe, hem”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)lemb- (“to turn, bend, limp”).
