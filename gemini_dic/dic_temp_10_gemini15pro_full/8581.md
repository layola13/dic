anticlassically: /ˌæntiˈklæsɪkli/
| adv. | 反经典地，非经典地 | In a way that opposes or departs from classical principles or traditions. | The composer structured the piece anticlassically, abandoning traditional sonata form.  作曲家以反古典的方式构建了这首曲子，放弃了传统的奏鸣曲形式。| Example 2: The physicist's anticlassically derived theory challenged established notions of space and time.  这位物理学家反经典地推导出的理论挑战了既定的时空概念。 | Near Synonyms: unconventionally, non-traditionally, heterodoxly | Antonyms: classically, traditionally, conventionally | word type: adverb
