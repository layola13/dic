bagginesses:/ˈbæɡɪnɪsɪz/
noun
松弛；袋状；宽松状态
The state of being baggy or loose.
Her dress displayed unsightly bagginesses around the elbows. 她的衣服在肘部周围显得难看地松弛。
synonyms: looseness, slackness, baggyness, droopiness
antonyms: tightness, firmness, tautness
词性: 名词

baggy:/ˈbæɡi/
adjective
袋状的；宽松的
Loose and hanging in folds.
He wore a baggy sweater. 他穿着一件宽松的毛衣。
synonyms: loose, slack, droopy
antonyms: tight, fitted, snug
词性: 形容词


bag:/bæɡ/
noun
袋子；包
A flexible container with an opening at the top, used for carrying things.
I put my groceries in a bag. 我把我的杂货放进一个袋子里。
verb
把…装进袋子；猎获；捕获
To put something into a bag.
They bagged five pheasants. 他们猎获了五只野鸡。
synonyms: sack, pouch, container (noun); capture, seize, net (verb)
antonyms: unpack, empty (verb)
词性: 名词；动词


词源:
From Middle English bagge, from Old Norse baggi (“bag, bundle, pack”), from Proto-Germanic *baggaz, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰasko- (“basket, vessel”).
