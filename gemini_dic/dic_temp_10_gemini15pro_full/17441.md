beano:/ˈbiːnəʊ/| n. |热闹的聚会，盛宴；(儿童的)庆祝会，联欢会|A beano is a party or celebration, especially one for children.|We're having a beano at the weekend to celebrate my birthday. 我们周末要举行一个聚会来庆祝我的生日。|Synonyms: party, celebration, festivity, bash|Antonyms: wake, funeral|noun

beano:/ˈbiːnəʊ/| n. |(英国非正式)宾戈游戏|A beano can also refer to a game of bingo in British informal language.|We went to the beano last night and I almost won! 我们昨晚去玩宾戈了，我差点就赢了！|Synonyms: bingo|Antonyms: N/A|noun
