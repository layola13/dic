bandleader:/ˈbændˌliːdər/| n.|乐队指挥，乐队领队|A person who leads a band, especially a musical band, often also acting as the conductor or main performer.|The bandleader tapped his baton and the music began. 乐队指挥轻敲指挥棒，音乐开始了。|synonyms: conductor, director, maestro|antonyms: musician, player (These are not strict antonyms, but represent different roles within a band)|noun
