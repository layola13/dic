parsonically:/pɑːrˈsɒnɪkli/ | adv. |像牧师一样地；以牧师的身份；以牧师的口吻|In a manner befitting or characteristic of a parson; like a parson. | He spoke parsonically, admonishing the congregation about their sins.  他以牧师的口吻说话，告诫会众他们的罪过。|例句：She delivered the news parsonically, with a mixture of solemnity and compassion. 她以牧师般的口吻宣布了这个消息，既庄严又富有同情心。|近义词：priestly, ministerially, ecclesiastically|反义词: secularly, worldly|词性:副词

词源:
From parson + -ical + -ly.  来自parson（牧师）+ -ical（形容词后缀）+ -ly（副词后缀）。  Parson 源于古法语 persone，最终源于拉丁语 persona（面具，角色，人物）。
