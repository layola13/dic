one-off:/ˈwʌnˌɒf/ (US) /ˈwʌnˌɒf/ (UK)| adj.|一次性的，独特的|Happening or done only once.|例句：They gave him a one-off payment. 他们给了他一笔一次性付款。|近义词：unique, single, exceptional|反义词:regular, common, frequent|词性:形容词

词源:  Compound formed from "one" and "off."  Meaning something done only once.  First recorded in the mid-20th century.
