nonmutative:/ˌnɒnˈmjuːtətɪv/ | adj. | 非交换的 | Not commutative; not capable of being changed without affecting the result.  A nonmutative operation is one where changing the order of the operands changes the result. |  Matrix multiplication is nonmutative. 矩阵乘法是不可交换的。|  The cross product of two vectors is a nonmutative operation. 两个向量的叉积是一个非交换运算。 | noncommutative, non-commutative | commutative | adjective
