cannonades:/ˌkænəˈneɪdz/ | n. | 炮击，连番轰炸 | A period of continuous heavy gunfire; a barrage. | The cannonade lasted for hours, shaking the very foundations of the city.  炮击持续了几个小时，震动了整个城市的地基。

The press launched a cannonade of questions at the embattled politician.  媒体向这位陷入困境的政治家发起了连珠炮般的提问。 | 近义词：barrage, bombardment, shelling | 反义词: peace, quiet | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From French *canonnade*, from *canonner* "to cannonade," from *canon* "cannon." Ultimately from Late Latin *canna* "reed, tube."
