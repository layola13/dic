nonregulatory:/ˌnɑːnˈreɡjələtɔːri/
adjective
非监管的，非管制的
Not subject to regulation or control by governmental agencies.
The company operates in a nonregulatory environment.  这家公司在一个非监管的环境中运营。
The nonregulatory nature of the cryptocurrency market presents both opportunities and risks.  加密货币市场的非监管性质既带来了机遇，也带来了风险。
synonyms: unregulated, uncontrolled, free, open, laissez-faire
antonyms: regulated, controlled, governed, overseen
adjective
