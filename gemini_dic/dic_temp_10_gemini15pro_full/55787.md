diverter:/daɪˈvɜːrtər/ /dɪˈvɜːrtər/| n.|转移器，分流器，导流器| something that diverts or turns something aside |The dam has a diverter that channels excess water away from the city.  大坝有一个分流器，可以将多余的水从城市引开。|synonyms: deflector, shunt, bypass|antonyms: concentrator, collector|noun

divert:/daɪˈvɜːrt/ /dɪˈvɜːrt/| v.|转移，使转向，使分心|to cause something or someone to change direction|The road was closed, so traffic had to be diverted. 道路封闭，所以交通不得不改道。|synonyms: reroute, redirect, distract|antonyms: focus, concentrate|verb
