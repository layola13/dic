inputer:/ˈɪnpʊtər/ | n. | 输入者，输入器 |  A person or device that enters data or information into a computer or other system. | Example: The inputer typed the data into the spreadsheet.  输入员将数据输入电子表格。|  Example: The barcode scanner acts as an inputer for the inventory system. 条形码扫描器充当库存系统的输入器。| Synonyms: operator, keyer, encoder | Antonyms: outputer | noun
