cosmolatry: /ˌkɒzmɒlətri/ /ˌkɑːzmɑːlətri/
n.
宇宙崇拜，世界崇拜
The worship of the cosmos; the reverence for the universe as a divine entity.
Cosmolatry was a common practice in many ancient cultures.  宇宙崇拜在许多古代文化中很普遍。
The philosopher's teachings bordered on cosmolatry, ascribing divine attributes to the universe itself. 这位哲学家的教义近乎宇宙崇拜，将神的属性归于宇宙本身。
近义词: nature worship, pantheism
反义词: atheism
词性: noun
