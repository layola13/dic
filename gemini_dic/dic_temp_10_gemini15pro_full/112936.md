lugged:/lʌɡd/| adj.|带把手(的)；笨重的；吃力的|Carrying, or having handles for carrying.  Difficult and tiring.|例句：She lugged a heavy suitcase up the stairs. 她提着一个沉重的行李箱上楼。|例句：It was a long and lugged journey. 这是一段漫长而艰辛的旅程。|近义词：heavy, cumbersome, burdensome|反义词:light, easy|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "lug" (to carry or pull with effort), which comes from Scandinavian sources.
