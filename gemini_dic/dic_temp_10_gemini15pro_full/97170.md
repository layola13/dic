infirmatory:/ɪnˈfɜːrmətɔːri/ | adj. | 证实的，确定的 | Serving to confirm; corroborative. |  The new evidence is infirmatory of the initial theory. 新的证据证实了最初的理论。 | 近义词: confirmatory, corroborative, validating, substantiating, supportive | 反义词:  disproving, refuting, contradictory, negating | 词性: adjective

infirmatory:/ɪnˈfɜːrmətɔːri/ | n. | 医务室，诊疗所 (古语，现已少用) | A place for the infirm or sick; an infirmary. (archaic) | The infirmatory was small but well-equipped.  这间医务室虽小，但设备齐全。| 近义词: infirmary, sickroom, hospital, clinic | 反义词:  (As a place) N/A | 词性: noun (archaic)

词源：Late Middle English: from late Latin *infirmatorius, from Latin infirmat- ‘weakened’ (past participle stem of infirmare, from in- ‘not’ + firmus ‘strong’).  The original sense was ‘weakening’, hence ‘invalidating’, giving rise to the current sense ‘confirming’ by semantic shift.
