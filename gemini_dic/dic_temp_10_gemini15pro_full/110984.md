liquidities:/lɪˈkwɪdɪtiz/
n.
流动性，流动资产
The availability of liquid assets to a market or company.
The company has enough liquidities to cover its short-term debts. 该公司有足够的流动资金来偿还短期债务。
Synonyms: cash, funds, assets, capital
Antonyms: illiquidity, liabilities
