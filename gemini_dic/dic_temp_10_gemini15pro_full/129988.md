non-mutual:/ˌnɒnˈmjuːtʃuəl/ | adj. | 非相互的，单方面的 | Not shared or felt by both sides; one-sided. |  Example: They had a non-mutual understanding of the agreement. 他们对协议的理解并非相互的。

Example:  The breakup was due to non-mutual feelings. 分手是因为单方面的感情。| Near Synonyms: one-sided, unilateral, unreciprocated | Antonyms: mutual, reciprocal, shared | Adjective
