panicle:/ˈpænɪkəl/| n. |圆锥花序，散穗花序|A branched, indeterminate inflorescence with flowers maturing from the bottom upwards.|The oat plant has a large, spreading panicle. 燕麦植株有一个大的、散开的圆锥花序。|Synonyms: inflorescence, raceme|Antonyms: N/A|noun

panicle:/ˈpænɪkəl/| v. |形成圆锥花序|To form or bear panicles.|The rice plants began to panicle. 水稻开始抽穗了。|Synonyms: blossom, flower|Antonyms: N/A|verb
