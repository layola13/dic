lothly:/ˈloʊðli/| adj.|令人厌恶的；丑陋的；可憎的|Extremely unpleasant; repulsive, especially in appearance.  Having a loathsome appearance; hideous. Inspiring or deserving of loathing; hateful.|The dragon was a lothly beast, with scales like rusty knives and breath of fire. 这条龙是一头令人作呕的野兽，鳞片像生锈的刀子，呼吸着火焰。|Synonyms: repulsive, hideous, abominable, detestable, abhorrent, disgusting|Antonyms: beautiful, attractive, lovely, pleasant, appealing|adjective
