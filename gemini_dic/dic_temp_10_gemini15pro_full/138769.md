numerologically:/ˌnuːməroʊˈlɑːdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 命理学地；根据命理学 |  Relating to or based on numerology, the study of the mystical significance of numbers. |  She interpreted the dream numerologically. 她用命理学解释了这个梦。 |  According to numerology, this year is lucky for you. 根据命理学，今年对你来说是幸运的。 | 近义词: mystically, esoterically | 反义词: scientifically, rationally | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "numerology" + "-ically."  "Numerology" comes from  French "numérologie," from "numéro" (number) + "-logie" (study of).  Ultimately from Latin "numerus" (number) + Greek "-logia" (study).
