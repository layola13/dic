outmaneuver:/ˌaʊtməˈnuːvər/| v. |以策略胜过；智胜|To cleverly outdo someone in a competition or conflict, especially by anticipating their actions and planning accordingly.|The company outmaneuvered its competitors by releasing a similar product at a lower price. 这家公司通过以更低的价格发布类似产品来智胜竞争对手。

The experienced chess player easily outmaneuvered his opponent.  这位经验丰富的棋手轻松地智胜了他的对手。


|近义词：outsmart, outwit, surpass, circumvent|反义词：be outmaneuvered, be outsmarted|词性：动词
