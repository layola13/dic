alkaloidal:/ˌæl.kəˈlɔɪ.dəl/
adjective
生物碱的；类生物碱的
Relating to or having the properties of an alkaloid.
The plant contains various alkaloidal compounds.  这种植物含有多种生物碱化合物。
The scientists isolated the alkaloidal substance from the bark. 科学家们从树皮中分离出生物碱物质。
Near synonyms: alkaloid, alkaline, basic
Near antonyms: acidic, neutral
