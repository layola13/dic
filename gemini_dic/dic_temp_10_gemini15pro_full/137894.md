nonvital:/ˌnɒnˈvaɪtl/ | adj. | 非重要的；非必需的 |  Removing a nonvital organ poses fewer risks than removing a vital one. 切除非重要器官的风险比切除重要器官的风险要小。 | Example: The report categorized the expenses as nonvital and recommended their reduction. 报告将这些开支归类为非必要的，并建议减少开支。| Synonyms:  inessential, unimportant, dispensable | Antonyms: vital, essential, crucial | adjective
