nonacquisitive:/ˌnɒn əˈkwɪzɪtɪv/
adjective
非贪婪的，不求获取的
Not excessively interested in acquiring money or material possessions.
She led a remarkably nonacquisitive life, content with simple pleasures. 她过着非常不贪婪的生活，满足于简单的快乐。
He's a nonacquisitive person, more interested in experiences than possessions.  他是一个不贪婪的人，对体验比对拥有更感兴趣。
Synonym:  unacquisitive, unselfish, altruistic, ascetic
Antonym: acquisitive, greedy, materialistic, avaricious
词性: adjective
