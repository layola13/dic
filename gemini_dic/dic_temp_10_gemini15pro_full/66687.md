exogenisms:/ˈɛksoʊdʒənɪzəmz/
n.
外来语；外来词
Words or phrases borrowed from another language and incorporated into the recipient language.
Many exogenisms in English come from French, like "ballet" and "rendezvous".  许多英语中的外来词源自法语，例如“ballet”和“rendezvous”。
Loanwords, borrowings
