overmelodiousnesses:/ˌoʊvərˌmɛloʊdiəsnɪsɪz/
n.
过度的旋律优美; 过分的悦耳
Excessive melodiousness; an overabundance of melody.
The overmelodiousnesses of the music made it cloying after a while.  这音乐过于优美，听了一会儿就觉得腻了。
The composer was criticized for the overmelodiousnesses of his latest symphony.  这位作曲家因其最新交响曲旋律过于甜美而受到批评。
近义词: sweetness, euphony
反义词: dissonance, cacophony
词性: 名词
