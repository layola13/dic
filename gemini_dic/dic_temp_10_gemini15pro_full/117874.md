melodists:/ˈmɛlədɪsts/ | n. |作曲家；旋律创作者|A person who composes melodies. |  Franz Schubert was one of the greatest melodists of the Romantic era. 舒伯特是浪漫主义时期最伟大的旋律创作者之一。|  The melodists of the Baroque period often wrote highly ornamented melodies. 巴洛克时期的作曲家经常创作装饰华丽的旋律。|Synonyms: composers, songwriters, musicians | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "arrangers" if focusing on creating new melodies vs. adapting existing ones) | noun
