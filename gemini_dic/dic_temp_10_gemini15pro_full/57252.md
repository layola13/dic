download:/ˈdaʊnˌloʊd/ (US) /ˌdaʊnˈləʊd/ (UK)| v. |下载，下装|  I downloaded the latest software update. 我下载了最新的软件更新。|近义词：transfer, copy, retrieve|反义词:upload|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "down" + "load".  "Down" indicating transfer from a larger system or central repository. "Load" refers to the act of transferring data.  First used in computing in the late 1970s.
