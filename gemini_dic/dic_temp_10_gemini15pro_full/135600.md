nonpositivistic:/ˌnɒnpɒzɪtɪˈvɪstɪk/
adjective
非实证主义的
Not based on or believing in positivism (a philosophical system that holds that every rationally justifiable assertion can be scientifically verified or is capable of logical or mathematical proof, and that therefore rejects metaphysics and theism).
Nonpositivistic research methodologies often incorporate qualitative data and subjective interpretations. 非实证主义的研究方法通常包含定性数据和主观解释。
Near Synonyms: interpretive, constructivist, qualitative, hermeneutic
Antonyms: positivistic, empirical, quantitative
词性: adjective
