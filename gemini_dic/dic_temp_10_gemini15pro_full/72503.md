fluorines:/ˈflʊəˌriːnz/
noun
氟（元素氟的复数形式）
Plural form of fluorine, a highly reactive, poisonous, pale yellow, gaseous halogen element.
Fluorines are highly reactive. 氟非常活泼。
Fluorines are used in toothpaste to prevent cavities.  氟被用于牙膏中以防止蛀牙。
Synonyms: halogens (more general)
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a chemical element)
