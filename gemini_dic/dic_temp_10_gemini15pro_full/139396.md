observation:/ˌɑːbzərˈveɪʃən/ | n. |观察；观察结果；评论；言论|The act of noticing or watching something or someone carefully.  A statement based on something one has seen, heard, or noticed.|例句：The doctor made a careful observation of the patient's symptoms. 医生仔细观察了病人的症状。

例句：Her observation about the change in his behavior was insightful. 她关于他行为变化的评论很有见地。|近义词：monitoring, surveillance, scrutiny, remark, comment|反义词：neglect, ignorance|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from late Latin observatio(n-), from Latin observare ‘observe’.
