muniments:/ˈmjuːnɪmənts/
n.
防御工事；契据；文件；（尤指）所有权文件
legal documents, especially title deeds, that are kept to show that someone owns a property or has a right
The lawyer examined the ancient muniments to confirm the family's ownership of the land. 律师检查了古代文件，以确认该家族对这块土地的所有权。
synonyms: documents, records, archives, deeds, titles
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms exist for a word referring to evidentiary documents.  One could consider concepts like "fabrications" or "forgeries" as opposing ideas.)
