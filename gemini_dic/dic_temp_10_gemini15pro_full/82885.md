gulph:/ɡʌlf/| n.|深渊，漩涡，海湾|A deep chasm or vortex; a large area of sea partially enclosed by land.|The ship was lost in the murky gulph. 这艘船消失在黑暗的深渊中。|近义词：abyss, chasm, vortex, bay, gulf|反义词:summit, peak|名词

gulph:/ɡʌlf/| vt.|吞没，吞噬|To swallow up or engulf completely.|The rising tide gulped the beach.  上涨的潮水吞没了海滩。|近义词：engulf, swallow, consume, devour|反义词:disgorge, emit|动词
