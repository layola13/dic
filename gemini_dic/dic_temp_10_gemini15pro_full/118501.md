mesmerizing:/ˈmezməˌraɪzɪŋ/| adj.|迷人的，令人着迷的|Capturing one's complete attention as if by magic; fascinating.|例句：The mesmerizing performance left the audience speechless.  令人着迷的表演让观众哑口无言。|近义词：captivating, enchanting, fascinating, hypnotic|反义词：boring, dull, tedious, uninteresting|词性:形容词
词源: From mesmerize + -ing.  From Franz Mesmer, an Austrian physician who popularized hypnotism.
