crouching:/ˈkraʊtʃɪŋ/| adj. v.|蹲伏的，蜷缩的；蹲伏，蜷缩|present participle of crouch|The cat was crouching in the grass, ready to pounce. 猫蜷缩在草丛里，准备突袭。|近义词：squatting, stooping, hunkering|反义词：standing, straightening|词性: adjective, verb
Etymology: From Middle English *cruchen, from Old Norse *krjúpa (“to creep, crawl”), from Proto-Germanic *kreupaną (“to creep”). Cognate with Dutch kruipen (“to creep, crawl”), German kriechen (“to creep, crawl”), Gothic ክᚱი𐌿ꯄ𐌰𐌽 (kriupan, “to creep”).
