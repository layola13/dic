misreward:/ˌmɪsrɪˈwɔːrd/
verb
错误地奖励；给予不当的奖励
To reward inappropriately or give a reward that is not deserved.
The company misrewarded its employees by giving bonuses to those who cut corners instead of those who followed proper procedures.  这家公司错误地奖励了员工，给那些偷工减料的人发了奖金，而不是给那些遵守程序的人。

The government's policy misrewarded speculation in the housing market. 政府的政策错误地奖励了房地产市场的投机行为。

misallocate, misprize
reward correctly, reward appropriately
v.
