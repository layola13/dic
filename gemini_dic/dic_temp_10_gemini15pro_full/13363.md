atmologist: /ˌætˈmɒlədʒɪst/
n.
大气学家; 气象学家
A scientist who studies the atmosphere.
例句：The atmologist predicted a significant increase in rainfall this year.  这位大气学家预测今年的降雨量会显著增加。

例句：Atmologists are studying the effects of pollution on the atmosphere.  大气学家正在研究污染对大气的影响。

近义词：meteorologist, climatologist

词性: noun
