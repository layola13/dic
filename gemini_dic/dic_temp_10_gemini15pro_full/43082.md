cowriter:/ˈkoʊˌraɪtər/| n.|合著者，共同撰稿人|A person who writes something jointly with another person or people.|例句：She and her brother were cowriters on the play. 她和她哥哥是这出戏的合著者。

例句：The song had four cowriters. 这首歌有四位共同作者。

|近义词：collaborator, joint author|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 sole author)|词性: noun
