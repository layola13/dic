antivirals:/ˌæntiˈvaɪrəlz/ | n. | 抗病毒药 |  Medicines that are used to treat viral infections.  These drugs work by inhibiting the development of a virus. | Doctors prescribed antivirals to combat the flu. 医生开了抗病毒药来对抗流感。 | Antiviral medication helped him recover quickly. 抗病毒药物帮助他迅速康复。 | synonyms: antiviral drugs, antiviral agents | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a medication, but conceptually: proviral) | noun


antiviral:/ˌæntiˈvaɪrəl/ | adj. | 抗病毒的 |  Having the ability to destroy or suppress the growth or reproduction of viruses. |  Antiviral treatments are crucial in managing certain infections. 抗病毒治疗对于控制某些感染至关重要。 |  The antiviral properties of this herb are well documented. 这种草药的抗病毒特性已被充分证明。 | synonyms: virus-fighting, virucidal  | antonyms: proviral | adjective


Etymology: anti- (prefix meaning "against") + viral (relating to a virus)
