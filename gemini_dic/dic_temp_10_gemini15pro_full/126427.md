mystical: /ˈmɪstɪk(ə)l/ | adj. | 神秘的；奥妙的；玄妙的 | Relating to mystics or mysticism; inspiring a sense of mystery and wonder. |  The mystical experience filled her with a sense of awe.  这种神秘的经历让她充满了敬畏之情。

The ancient ruins had a mystical quality.  这些古老的废墟有一种神秘的特质。 | 近义词: mysterious, esoteric, occult, spiritual, supernatural | 反义词: mundane, ordinary, commonplace, earthly | 词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin mysticus (see mystic).
