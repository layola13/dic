crystallometry:/ˌkrɪstəˈlɒmɪtri/
n.|晶体测量学|The science of measuring crystals, especially their angles.|Crystallometry is used to determine the precise structure of a crystal.|近义词: crystallography|反义词: None|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From "crystallo-" (from "crystal," ultimately from Greek "krystallos," meaning "ice") + "-metry" (from Greek "-metria," meaning "measuring").
