enforces:/ɪnˈfɔːrsɪz/ | v. |执行，实施，强制|compels observance of or compliance with (a law, rule, or obligation). |The police enforce the law. 警察执行法律。| The school enforces a strict dress code. 学校强制执行严格的着装规定。 |近义词：implements, applies, executes, imposes |反义词：neglects, ignores, disregards |词性:动词

词源：Late Middle English: from Old French enforcier, based on Latin in- ‘into, upon’ + force.
