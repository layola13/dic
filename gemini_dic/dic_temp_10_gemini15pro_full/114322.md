maioid:/ˈmeɪɔɪd/| adj.|蟹形类的，像蟹的|Relating to or resembling crabs; crablike.  Derived from " Maia," a genus of crabs, + "-oid," a suffix meaning "resembling" or "having the form of."|The maioid crustacean scuttled across the ocean floor.  这只蟹形类的甲壳动物匆匆穿过海底。|近义词：crablike, cancriform|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词
