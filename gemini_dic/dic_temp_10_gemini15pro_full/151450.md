paronym:/ˈpærənɪm/| n.|同形异义词|A word that is similar in form to another word but has a different meaning.  Paronyms often cause confusion because of their resemblance in spelling and/or pronunciation.|The words "affect" and "effect" are common paronyms that are often confused.|近义词:cognate, near-homonym|反义词:antonym, heteronym|词性:noun
