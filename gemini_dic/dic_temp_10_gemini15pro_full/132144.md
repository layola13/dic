nonculturally:/nɒnˈkʌltʃərəli/
adjective
非文化地；与文化无关地
Not related to or influenced by culture; in a way that does not consider or involve the culture of a particular society.
例句：The study analyzed data nonculturally, focusing solely on biological factors.  这项研究以非文化的方式分析数据，只关注生物因素。

例句：The disease spreads nonculturally, affecting people of all backgrounds equally. 这种疾病的传播与文化无关，对所有背景的人都有同等的影响。

近义词：universally, generally, a culturally
反义词：culturally

词性
adjective
