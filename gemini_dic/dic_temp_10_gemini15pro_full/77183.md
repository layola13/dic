gasometrical:/ˌɡæsəˈmetrɪkəl/
adjective
气体测量的
Relating to the measurement of gases.
This gasometrical apparatus is used to determine the composition of the gas mixture.  这套气体测量装置用于测定气体混合物的组成。
The scientist conducted a gasometrical analysis of the air sample.  科学家对空气样本进行了气体测量分析。
近义词: gas-measuring
反义词: None

ETYMOLOGY->
gas + -metrical.  From "gas" (referring to any aeriform fluid) + "-metrical" (relating to measurement).
