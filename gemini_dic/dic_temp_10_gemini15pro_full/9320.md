antipolitically:/ˌæntiˈpəˈlɪtɪkli/
| adv. | 反政治地；与政治无关地；非政治性地 |  In a way that avoids or opposes political involvement or considerations.  | He lived his life antipolitically, preferring to focus on his art. 他过着与政治无关的生活，更喜欢专注于他的艺术。|  He made an antipolitically motivated decision. 他做了一个非政治性动机的决定。 |  apolitically, nonpolitically | politically | adverb
