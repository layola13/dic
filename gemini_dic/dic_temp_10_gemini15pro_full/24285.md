brigandish:/ˈbrɪɡəndɪʃ/| adj.|土匪似的，强盗般的，凶恶的|Resembling or characteristic of a brigand; having the appearance or manner of a robber; villainous.  |He had a brigandish look about him with his scruffy beard and intense eyes. 他蓬乱的胡须和犀利的眼神让他看起来像个土匪。

The old house had a brigandish air, hidden deep in the woods.  那栋隐藏在密林深处的旧房子有一种强盗窝的气氛。|近义词：villainous, piratical, thuggish, robber-like|反义词：gentle, kind, law-abiding, respectable|词性: adjective

词源:
From brigand + -ish.  Ultimately from Italian brigante "brigand".
