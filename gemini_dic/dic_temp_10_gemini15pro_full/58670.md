dummied:/ˈdʌmid/| v. |  用假人代替；用填充物塞满；制作…的模型；假装；虚构；| To fill or stuff with a dummy; to create a mock-up or simplified version of something, often for testing or demonstration; to feign or simulate. |  They dummied up a new car design for the wind tunnel tests. 他们为风洞测试制作了一个新车设计的模型。 The boxer dummied a left hook and then delivered a right cross. 拳击手佯装左勾拳，然后打出了一个右交叉拳。| 近义词: simulated, feigned, mocked up, padded, stuffed | 反义词: actual, real, genuine | 词性: 动词

词源: From "dummy" (n.), meaning a mannequin or imitation, from German "Dummkopf" (stupid head).
