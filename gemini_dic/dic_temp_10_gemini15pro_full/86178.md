heartwood:/ˈhɑːrtˌwʊd/ | n. |心材|The dense inner part of a tree trunk, yielding the hardest timber.|The craftsman preferred heartwood for his intricate carvings because of its durability.  工匠喜欢用心材进行复杂的雕刻，因为它很耐用。|Synonyms: duramen |Antonyms: sapwood| noun
