libertinism:/ˈlɪbərtɪnɪzəm/
n.
放荡；淫荡；玩乐主义；自由思想
The state of being a libertine; a disregard of morality, especially in sexual matters; licentiousness.
He lived a life of libertinism, indulging in every pleasure imaginable. 他过着放荡不羁的生活，沉溺于各种可以想象的享乐。
licentiousness, debauchery, promiscuity, dissipation, profligacy
asceticism, chastity, morality, restraint, abstinence
noun

libertine:/ˈlɪbərtɪn/
n.
放荡不羁的人；浪荡子；自由思想者
A person, especially a man, who behaves without moral principles or a sense of responsibility, especially in sexual matters.
The aging libertine was known for his extravagant parties and scandalous affairs.  这位上了年纪的浪荡子以其奢华的派对和丑闻般的风流韵事而闻名。
profligate, rake, playboy, debauchee, roue
puritan, ascetic, moralist
noun
