overplenitude:/ˌoʊvərˈplɛnɪtjuːd/
n.
过剩，过多，过量
an excessive abundance; superfluity
The overplenitude of food at the buffet was overwhelming.  自助餐上过多的食物让人不知所措。
The farmer's market displayed an overplenitude of fresh produce.  农贸市场展示了过多的新鲜农产品。
synonyms:  abundance, surplus, excess, superfluity, plethora
antonyms:  scarcity, lack, deficiency, dearth
noun
