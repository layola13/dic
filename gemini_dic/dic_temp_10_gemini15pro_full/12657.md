assemblage:/əˈsɛmblɪdʒ/ | n. | 集合，组合，装配，（艺术）集合艺术 |A collection or gathering of things or people. An artistic form or medium usually created on a defined substrate that consists of three-dimensional elements projecting out of or from the substrate. | The museum boasts a fascinating assemblage of antique toys.  博物馆收藏了引人入胜的古董玩具。| synonyms: collection, assembly, gathering, aggregation, compilation, conglomeration | antonyms: dispersal, scattering, separation | noun

assemblage:/əˈsɛmblɪdʒ/ | n. | 集合，组合，装配，（艺术）集合艺术 |The act of assembling or the state of being assembled. An artistic form or medium usually created on a defined substrate that consists of three-dimensional elements projecting out of or from the substrate. | The assemblage of the new furniture took several hours. 新家具的组装花了几个小时。| synonyms: construction, fabrication, erection | antonyms: dismantling, disassembly, deconstruction | noun
