ETYMOLOGY->
From dastardliness + -es (plural suffix).  Dastardliness comes from dastardly (adjective) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing quality or state).  Dastardly originates from the Middle English word *dastard*, meaning "coward," of uncertain origin.
