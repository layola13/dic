outjesting:/ˌaʊtˈdʒɛstɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的玩笑; 比…更会开玩笑
Surpassing someone in jesting or witty remarks.
He outjested the king's fool, earning a place in the court. 他在玩笑上胜过了国王的小丑，赢得了在宫廷中的一席之地。

近义词: outwitting, surpassing, excelling
反义词: being outdone, losing


词性: verb (动词)


词源:  "out-" (超越) + "jest" (开玩笑)  "jest" 源自古法语 "geste"，意思是 "deed, exploit".
