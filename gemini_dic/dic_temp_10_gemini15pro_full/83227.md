gye: /ɡaɪ/ | n. | 欺骗; 诡计; 骗局 | A trick or deception. |  He tried to pull a gye on me, but I saw through it. 他想骗我，但我看穿了他的诡计。 | synonym: trick, deception, hoax, fraud, cheat, swindle | antonym: honesty, truthfulness | noun


Etymology:
Likely derived from Romani *gye or *ge, meaning "trick" or "deception". This is related to the Greek word γύη (gyē), meaning "land" or "earth," suggesting a connection to fortune-telling or trickery involving hidden objects.  Further research is needed to confirm the exact etymology.
