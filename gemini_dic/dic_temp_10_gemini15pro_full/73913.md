forswat:/fɔːrˈswɒt/ | v. | 放弃，抛弃 | To renounce or reject (something, especially a belief, cause, or allegiance). | He forswat his former beliefs.  他放弃了他以前的信仰。| 近义词： renounce, abjure, forsake, abandon | 反义词： embrace, adopt, uphold | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Old English *forswerian "to swear falsely, perjure oneself," from for- "against, away" + swerian "to swear." The meaning shifted from "swear falsely" to "renounce under oath" and finally to simply "renounce."
