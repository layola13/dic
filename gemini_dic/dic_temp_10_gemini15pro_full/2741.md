adulterant: /əˈdʌltərənt/
noun
掺杂物，伪造物
A substance that is added to another substance, making it impure or weaker.
The milk was found to contain an adulterant.  牛奶被发现含有掺杂物。

Melamine is a toxic adulterant sometimes added to food products. 三聚氰胺是一种有毒的掺杂物，有时会被添加到食品中。

近义词: additive, contaminant, impurity
反义词:  pure substance
词性: noun
