mounded:/ˈmaʊndɪd/
adjective
堆起的，隆起的
formed into a rounded heap or protuberance.
The archaeologist carefully excavated the mounded earth. 考古学家小心翼翼地挖掘了隆起的土堆。
The baker skillfully created a mounded cake.  面包师熟练地制作了一个隆起的蛋糕。
Synonyms: heaped, piled, stacked
Antonyms: flat, level
adjective
