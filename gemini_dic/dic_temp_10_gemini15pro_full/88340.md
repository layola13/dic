hippodamus:/ˌhɪpəˈdeɪməs/| n. |河马；（古希腊）希波达摩斯（城市规划师）| A hippodamus is a semiaquatic mammal, or it can refer to Hippodamus of Miletus, an ancient Greek architect and urban planner. | The hippodamus spent most of its time in the water. 河马大部分时间都待在水里。|Hippodamus of Miletus is considered the "father of European urban planning". 米利都的希波达摩斯被认为是“欧洲城市规划之父”。 | 近义词：(for the animal) hippo, river horse | 反义词:  (no direct antonym) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἱπποδάμος (hippodámos, “horse-tamer”), from ἵππος (híppos, “horse”) + δαμάζω (damázō, “I tame”).
