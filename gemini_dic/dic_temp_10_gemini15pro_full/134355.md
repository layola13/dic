noninterchangeability:/ˌnɒnɪntətʃeɪndʒəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可互换性 | The quality or state of not being interchangeable; inability to be exchanged or substituted.| The noninterchangeability of car parts can be frustrating when trying to find a replacement. 汽车零件的不可互换性在寻找替代品时可能会令人沮丧。| non-substitutability, uniqueness | interchangeability, substitutability | noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix): not
inter- (prefix): between, among
change (verb): to make or become different
-able (suffix): capable of, suitable for
-ity (suffix): state or quality of
