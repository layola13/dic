overcontracts:/ˌoʊvərˈkɒntrækt/ /ˌoʊvərˈkɑːntrækt/
verb
过度签约；过分承包
To commit oneself to too many contracts or obligations.
The construction company overcontracted and couldn't complete all the projects on time.  这家建筑公司过度承包，无法按时完成所有项目。
The freelancer overcontracted and ended up working 16-hour days. 这名自由职业者过度签约，最终每天工作16个小时。
近义词: overcommit, overextend
反义词: undercommit, underbook
词性: verb
