effervesce:/ˌɛfərˈvɛs/| v. |冒泡，兴奋，活跃|To give off bubbles of gas; to show liveliness or exhilaration.|The champagne effervesced in the glass. 香槟在玻璃杯中冒着泡泡。|The children effervesced with excitement on Christmas morning.  圣诞节早晨，孩子们兴奋极了。|近义词：bubble, fizz, sparkle, exhilarate|反义词：flatten, dull|词性:动词
Etymology: From Latin *ēx* (out) + *fervere* (to boil, be hot).
