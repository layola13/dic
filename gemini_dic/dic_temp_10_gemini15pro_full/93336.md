illacerable:/ˌɪləˈsɛrəbl/
adjective
不能撕裂的; 无法摧毁的
incapable of being torn or rent; incapable of being destroyed
例句：His spirit proved illacerable, despite the hardships he faced. 尽管他面临着艰辛，但他的精神证明是不可摧毁的。

例句：The illacerable bond between the two friends lasted a lifetime.  这两个朋友之间牢不可破的联系持续了一生。

近义词：indestructible, unbreakable, inviolable
反义词：fragile, breakable, vulnerable
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *illacerabilis, from in- (“not”) + lacerabilis (“tearable”), from lacerare (“to tear, rend”), from lacer (“torn, mangled”).
