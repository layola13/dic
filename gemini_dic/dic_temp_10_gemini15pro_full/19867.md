binged:/bɪndʒd/ | v. |  狂欢，放纵，无节制地做(尤指吃喝、看电视等) | past tense and past participle of "binge" which means to indulge in an activity, especially eating, drinking, or watching television, to excess. |  I binged on ice cream last night. 我昨晚狂吃冰淇淋。| Synonyms: gorged, indulged, overindulged | Antonyms: abstained, moderated | verb
