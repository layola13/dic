griffinhoods:/ˈɡrɪfɪnhʊdz/| n.|狮鹫兜帽（复数）|A hood or cowl resembling the head and beak of a griffin, often worn as part of a costume or ceremonial garb.  It can also refer to a metaphorical covering or disguise.|The knights wore elaborate griffinhoods during the procession.|近义词：cowl, hood, mask, disguise|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "unveiling," "exposure")|noun
