counterhypothesis:/ˌkaʊntərhaɪˈpɒθəsɪs/ /ˌkaʊntərhaɪˈpɑːθəsɪs/
noun
反假设，对立假设
A counterhypothesis is a hypothesis that contradicts another hypothesis.  It proposes a different explanation for the same observations.
The prosecution presented a counterhypothesis to explain the defendant's alibi.  控方提出了一个反假设来解释被告的不在场证明。
The researchers developed a counterhypothesis to challenge the prevailing theory. 研究人员提出了一个反假设来挑战盛行的理论。
synonyms: alternative hypothesis, competing hypothesis, rival hypothesis
antonyms:  original hypothesis, primary hypothesis
n.
