borracho:/boˈratʃo/| adj.|醉的；喝醉的|A person who is drunk.  From Spanish, literally meaning "drunk."|He stumbled into the bar, clearly borracho. 他跌跌撞撞地走进酒吧，显然是喝醉了。|
Example:  After several margaritas, she was feeling quite borracha.  几杯玛格丽塔之后，她感觉很醉。

Example: The borracho man was singing loudly in the street.  那个醉汉在大街上大声唱歌。|
Synonyms: drunk, intoxicated, inebriated, tipsy|Antonyms: sober, temperate|adjective
