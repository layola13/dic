ormolu:/ˈɔːrməluː/
noun
镀金青铜; 仿金铜制品
An alloy of copper, zinc, and tin, gilded or made to resemble gilding.
The ormolu clock glittered on the mantelpiece. 镀金青铜钟在壁炉台上闪闪发光。
synonyms: gilt bronze, gilded bronze
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific material)
