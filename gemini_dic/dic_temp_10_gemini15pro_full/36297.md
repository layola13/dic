coherently:/koʊˈhɪrəntli/ /kəʊˈhɪərəntli/
| adv. | 连贯地；条理清楚地 | in a logical and consistent way | She spoke coherently and persuasively. 她讲话连贯且有说服力。 | clearly, logically, consistently | incoherently, unclearly | adverb


Etymology:
From Latin cohaerēns, present participle of cohaerēre (“to stick together, cling”), from co- (“together”) + haerēre (“to stick”).
