disciplined:/ˈdɪsəplɪnd/| adj.|训练有素的，遵守纪律的|Showing a controlled form of behaviour or way of working.  |例句：He is a disciplined worker. 他是一个训练有素的员工。|近义词：orderly, controlled, rigorous, strict|反义词:undisciplined, unruly, chaotic|adjective

disciplined:/ˈdɪsəplɪnd/| v.|训练，惩罚|to punish someone|例句：The child was disciplined for misbehaving.  这个孩子因为行为不端受到了惩罚。|近义词：punish, chastise, correct|反义词:praise, reward|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French discipliner, from Latin disciplina ‘instruction, knowledge’, from discipulus ‘pupil’ (see disciple).
