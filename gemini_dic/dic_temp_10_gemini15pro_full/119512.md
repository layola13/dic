microportal:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌpɔːrtl/ | n. | 微门户，小型门户 | A specialized web portal designed to provide access to a limited scope of information, often related to a specific department, project, or group within a larger organization.  It serves as a single point of access for relevant resources and tools. | Example 1: The company launched a microportal for its human resources department, where employees can access benefits information, payroll details, and company policies. 公司为其人力资源部门启动了一个微门户，员工可以在其中访问福利信息、工资单详细信息和公司政策。

Example 2: The project team uses a microportal to share documents, track progress, and communicate with each other. 项目团队使用微门户共享文档、跟踪进度并相互沟通。 | Near synonyms: subportal, mini-portal, niche portal | Antonym: megaportal, enterprise portal | noun
