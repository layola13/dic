gallimaufry:/ˌɡælɪˈmɔːfri/ | n. | 大杂烩；混合物；混杂 | A confused mixture or jumble of things. |  The room was a gallimaufry of furniture, books, and clothes. 房间里摆满了家具、书籍和衣服，乱七八糟的。

The market was a gallimaufry of sights, sounds, and smells. 市场里景象、声音和气味混杂在一起。 | 近义词: hodgepodge, medley, jumble, mishmash, potpourri | 反义词: order, uniformity, homogeneity | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *gallimafrée* (a hashed meat dish), of uncertain origin. Possibly from *galimafrer*, meaning "to slash, hack," implying a dish of chopped-up meat.
