ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mitte*, from Old English *mitte* (glove), from Proto-Germanic *mitti*, of uncertain origin.  Cognate with Old Norse *mittr*.  The abbreviation "MIT" for Massachusetts Institute of Technology is simply a shortening of the full name.
