caries's:/ˈkɛəriːzɪz/ | n. | 龋齿，蛀牙 |  The possessive form of "caries," indicating belonging to or associated with dental decay.  "Caries" itself is a noun and doesn't typically take a possessive form.  It's more common to say "the effects of caries" or "caries progression" rather than "caries's effects." | This form is grammatically incorrect.  Use "caries" directly or rephrase. | 近义词: tooth decay, cavities | 反义词: dental health | 词性: (Incorrectly used) possessive noun
