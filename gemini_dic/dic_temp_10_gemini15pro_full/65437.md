euplastic:/juːˈplæstɪk/
adjective
易成型的; 适于塑造的; 可塑的
readily molded or shaped; plastic
例句：The artist found the clay particularly euplastic, allowing her to create intricate details.  这位艺术家发现这种粘土特别容易塑形，可以让她创作出复杂的细节。

例句：Euplastic materials are essential for sculpting and pottery.  可塑性材料对于雕塑和陶艺至关重要。
近义词：malleable, pliable, ductile, plastic
反义词：rigid, inflexible, stiff, unyielding
词性: adjective

euplastic:/juːˈplæstɪk/
noun
可塑物质
a substance that is readily molded or shaped
例句：The sculptor searched for a euplastic that would hold its shape well.  雕塑家寻找一种能够很好地保持形状的可塑物质。

例句：Many different types of clay can be considered euplastics. 许多不同类型的粘土都可以被认为是可塑物质。
近义词：plastic
反义词：None
词性: noun
