lavishnesses:/ˈlævɪʃnəsɪz/
noun
奢侈；慷慨赠予；丰富
The state or quality of being lavish; extravagance.  Instances of being lavish.
The lavishnesses of the feast were overwhelming.  这场盛宴的奢华令人叹为观止。
The king was known for his lavishnesses on his courtiers. 国王以对朝臣的慷慨大方而闻名。
Synonyms:  extravagances, luxuries, profusion, abundance, excesses
Antonyms:  frugality, thrift, economy, sparseness, austerity
词性: 名词(plural)
