dof:/dɒf/ /dɑːf/ | n. | 景深 | Depth of Field, the distance between the nearest and furthest objects in a scene that appear acceptably sharp in an image. |  The photographer used a shallow dof to blur the background and highlight the subject. 摄影师使用了浅景深来模糊背景并突出主体。 | Example: A wide dof ensures everything from the foreground to the background is in focus.  大景深确保从前景到背景的一切都清晰对焦。 | Synonym: focus range, focal range | Antonym: - | noun
