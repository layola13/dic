allodialist:/ˌæləˈdaɪəlɪst/
n.
自由保有土地者，完全保有土地者
One who holds land by allodial tenure; one who has full ownership of real property.
The allodialist enjoyed complete control over his land, free from any feudal obligations.  这位自由保有土地者完全掌控自己的土地，没有任何封建义务。
Mr. Smith, an allodialist, could sell or bequeath his land as he saw fit. 史密斯先生是一位完全保有土地者，可以随意出售或遗赠他的土地。
近义词:freeholder, landholder, landowner
反义词:tenant, lessee
词性:noun
