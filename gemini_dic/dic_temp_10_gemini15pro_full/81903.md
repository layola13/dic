griffus: /ˈɡrɪfəs/ | n. |狮鹫兽|A mythical creature with the body, tail, and back legs of a lion; the head and wings of an eagle; and an eagle's talons as its front feet. |例句：The griffus guarded the treasure fiercely. 狮鹫兽凶猛地守护着宝藏。

例句：Legends say that a griffus is incredibly strong and loyal. 传说狮鹫兽非常强壮和忠诚。|近义词：griffin, hippogriff |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a mythical creature) |词性:noun
