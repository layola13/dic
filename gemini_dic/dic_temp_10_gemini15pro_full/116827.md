maxilla:/mækˈsɪlə/ (US), /məkˈsɪlə/ (UK)| n. |上颌骨|The upper jawbone, specifically either of the two bones forming the upper jaw in vertebrates.|The maxilla forms a crucial part of the facial skeleton.  上颌骨构成了面部骨骼的重要组成部分。|The surgeon repaired the patient's fractured maxilla.  外科医生修复了病人断裂的上颌骨。|Synonyms: jawbone, upper jaw |Antonyms: mandible (lower jaw)|noun

maxillas:/mækˈsɪliː/ (US), /məkˈsɪliː/ (UK)| n. |上颌骨（复数）|The plural form of maxilla, referring to both the left and right upper jawbones.|The maxillas are fused in the midline to form the upper jaw. 上颌骨在中线融合形成上颌。|The blow to the face fractured both maxillas.  面部受到的打击使两块上颌骨都骨折了。|Synonyms: jawbones, upper jaws|Antonyms: mandibles (lower jaws)|noun
