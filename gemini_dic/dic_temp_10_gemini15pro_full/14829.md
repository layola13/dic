axiom:/ˈæksiəm/| n.|公理；格言；自明之理|a statement or proposition which is regarded as being established, accepted, or self-evidently true.|The axiom that supply equals demand is the cornerstone of classical economics. 供求相等的公理是古典经济学的基石。|synonyms: principle, theorem, postulate, law, rule|antonyms: absurdity, paradox|noun
