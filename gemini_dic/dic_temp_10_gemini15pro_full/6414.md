amphitheatral:/ˌæmfɪθiˈætrəl/ | adj. | 圆形剧场的，像圆形剧场的 | Resembling or characteristic of an amphitheater; having a semicircular or oval form, often tiered. | The valley had an amphitheatral shape, with the hills rising around it. 山谷呈圆形剧场状，周围群山环绕。| The seating arrangement was amphitheatral, providing excellent views for everyone. 座位安排是圆形剧场的，每个人都能看到很好的景色。 | 近义词: semicircular, oval, tiered | 反义词: flat, linear | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From amphi- (Greek: "around," "on both sides") + theater (from Greek theatron "a place for viewing").  The word "amphitheater" itself refers to an open-air venue used for entertainment, performances, and public gatherings, especially in ancient Rome. The suffix "-al" transforms the noun "amphitheater" into an adjective, describing something that resembles or has the characteristics of an amphitheater.
