hauntedness:/ˈhɔːntɪdnəs/ | n. |  闹鬼，阴森森 | The state of being haunted; the quality of being frequented by ghosts or other supernatural beings. |  The hauntedness of the old house made it difficult to sell. 这栋老房子的阴森森的氛围使它很难出售。 | synonyms: ghostliness, spookiness | antonyms: peacefulness, serenity | noun
