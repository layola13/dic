nonruinable:/nɒnˈruːɪnəbl/
adjective
不可摧毁的；不会破产的
Not able to be ruined or destroyed; impossible to bankrupt.
The company's investments are considered nonruinable. 该公司的投资被认为是不可摧毁的。
He believes in building a nonruinable financial future. 他相信建立一个不会破产的金融未来。
Synonyms: indestructible, impervious, invulnerable, unshakeable, stable
Antonyms: ruinable, destructible, vulnerable, precarious
Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + ruinable (from ruin + -able)
