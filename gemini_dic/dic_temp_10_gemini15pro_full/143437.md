outflourishing: /ˌaʊtˈflɜːrɪʃɪŋ/
Verb
繁荣胜过，比...更繁荣
Surpassing in flourishing; flourishing more than.
The roses in my garden are outflourishing my neighbor's. 我花园里的玫瑰比邻居家的更茂盛。

The economy of country A is outflourishing that of country B. A国的经济比B国更加繁荣。
Synonym: outdoing, excelling, surpassing, exceeding
Antonym: declining, deteriorating, failing, lagging
词性: v.
