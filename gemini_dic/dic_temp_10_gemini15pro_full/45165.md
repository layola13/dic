cuerpo: /ˈkwerpo/ (Spanish)| n.|身体，躯体，尸体|A body; physique.  Refers to the physical structure of a person or animal, or sometimes an inanimate object.  It can also mean a corpse.|

例句：
El cuerpo humano es una máquina compleja.
The human body is a complex machine.

Cuidar el cuerpo es importante para la salud.
Taking care of your body is important for your health.

近义词：físico, organismo, cadáver (corpse)
反义词: alma (soul), espíritu (spirit)
词性: noun
