outgreening:/ˌaʊtˈɡriːnɪŋ/ | v. | 超越环保，在环保方面胜过 | To surpass someone in environmentally friendly practices or policies. |  Example 1:  The company is outgreening its competitors by investing heavily in renewable energy.  该公司通过大力投资可再生能源，在环保方面胜过其竞争对手。

Example 2:  The city is outgreening other urban areas with its comprehensive recycling program. 这座城市凭借其全面的回收计划，在环保方面胜过其他城市地区。 | 近义词: outperforming (in environmental initiatives), surpassing (in green practices) | 反义词: lagging behind (in environmental efforts), polluting | 词性: 动词
