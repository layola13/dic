fazing:/ˈfeɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人不安的，使人慌乱的 |  Causing someone to feel confused or disconcerted. | The sudden loud noise was fazing. 突然的巨响让人不安。| 近义词: disconcerting, unsettling, unnerving | 反义词: calming, reassuring, comforting | 词性:形容词

词源:  From the verb "faze," which originated in the late 19th century, possibly as a variant of "feeze," meaning "to frighten" or "drive away."
