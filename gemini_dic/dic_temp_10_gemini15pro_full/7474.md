anker:/ˈæŋkər/| n. v.|锚；靠山；新闻节目主持人；|Something heavy that is dropped into the water to prevent a boat or ship from moving.  A person or thing that provides stability or confidence in a difficult situation. A person who presents and coordinates a live television or radio program involving other contributors.|The ship dropped anchor in the bay. 这艘船在海湾抛锚了。|近义词：anchor, mainstay, support, host, presenter|反义词：drift, instability|词性: 名词, 动词
