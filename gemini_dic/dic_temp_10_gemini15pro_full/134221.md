nonindulgently: /ˌnɒnɪnˈdʌldʒəntli/
| adv. | 不放纵地，不纵容地 |  In a way that does not indulge or yield to desires or whims; in a restrained or austere manner. |  She ate nonindulgently, choosing a small salad over the rich pasta dish. 她克制地吃着，选择了一份小沙拉而不是浓郁的意大利面。| synonyms: abstemiously, austerely, sparingly, moderately | antonyms: indulgently, lavishly, excessively, immoderately | 词性: 副词
