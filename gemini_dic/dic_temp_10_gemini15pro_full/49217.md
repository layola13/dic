delecto:/dɪˈlɛktoʊ/ /dəˈlɛktəʊ/
verb
使高兴；使愉快；取悦
To delight or please greatly.
She was delighted by the beautiful music. 她被美妙的音乐所陶醉。
The magician's tricks delighted the children. 魔术师的戏法使孩子们高兴。
近义词: please, charm, enchant, captivate, entrance
反义词: displease, bore, annoy, irritate
词性: verb


delecto:/dɪˈlɛktoʊ/ /dəˈlɛktəʊ/
noun (rare)
乐趣；快乐
Pleasure or delight. (This usage is less common)
He found great delectation in reading. 他从阅读中找到了很大的乐趣。(较少用)
近义词: pleasure, delight, enjoyment
反义词: displeasure, boredom, annoyance
词性: noun
