bundler:/ˈbʌndlər/| n.|捆绑机，打包机，捆扎器；(尤指)捆绑销售者|A bundler is a person or machine that bundles things together.  A bundler can also refer to a person who collects campaign contributions from multiple donors and then gives them to a political candidate.|The farmer used a bundler to tie the hay into bales. 农民用捆扎机把干草扎成捆。|The politician relied on bundlers to raise large sums of money.  这位政治家依靠募捐人筹集大笔资金。|synonyms: assembler, packer, combiner|antonyms: separator, divider|noun
