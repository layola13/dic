deactivate:/diːˈæktɪveɪt/
verb
使无效，使不活动，停用
to make something inactive or no longer working
The security system was deactivated.  安全系统已停用。

近义词:disable, neutralize, shut down
反义词:activate, enable, turn on
词性:v.
