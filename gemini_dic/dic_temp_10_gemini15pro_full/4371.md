alacriously:/əˈlækriəsli/| adv.|欣然地，欢快地|Cheerfully and eagerly; with enthusiastic willingness.|She accepted the invitation alacriously. 她欣然接受了邀请。|例句：He alacriously volunteered to lead the project. 他欣然自告奋勇领导这个项目。|近义词：eagerly, readily, cheerfully, enthusiastically|反义词：reluctantly, unwillingly, apathetically|词性:副词
Etymology: From Latin *alacriter* (cheerfully, briskly), from *alacer* (eager, brisk).
