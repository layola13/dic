bolster:/ˈboʊlstər/ (US), /ˈbəʊlstə(r)/ (UK)| v. n.| 支撑，加固；鼓励，增强；长枕|To support or strengthen; to prop up.  As a noun, it refers to a long pillow or cushion.|

例句：
The government's efforts to bolster the economy have been successful.
政府提振经济的努力取得了成功。

The coach's speech bolstered the team's morale.
教练的讲话鼓舞了球队的士气。

近义词：support, strengthen, reinforce, encourage, boost|
反义词：weaken, undermine, discourage|
词性：verb, noun|

bolster:/ˈboʊlstər/ (US), /ˈbəʊlstə(r)/ (UK)| n.|长枕|A long, thick pillow that is placed under other pillows for support.|

例句：
She propped herself up in bed with a bolster.
她用一个长枕把自己支撑在床上。

The bolster provided extra support for her injured back.
长枕为她受伤的背部提供了额外的支撑。

近义词：pillow, cushion|
反义词：N/A|
词性：noun|
