miosis:/mīˈōsəs/ /maɪˈoʊsɪs/| n.|瞳孔缩小，缩瞳| Constriction of the pupil of the eye, resulting in a decreased pupil size. |  The doctor observed miosis in the patient's eyes after administering the medication. 医生给病人用药后观察到病人瞳孔缩小。|  The bright light caused miosis.  强光导致瞳孔缩小。|synonyms: constriction of the pupil, pupillary constriction| antonyms: mydriasis, dilation of the pupil |noun
