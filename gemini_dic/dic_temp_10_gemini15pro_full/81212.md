grandmothers:/ˈɡrændˌmʌðərz/ | n. |祖母们；外祖母们|The plural form of "grandmother," referring to more than one grandmother.  It signifies the maternal or paternal female parent of one's parent.|My grandmothers both loved to bake. 我的祖母们都喜欢烘焙。|Near Synonyms: grannies, nanas |Antonyms: grandsons, granddaughters |Noun
