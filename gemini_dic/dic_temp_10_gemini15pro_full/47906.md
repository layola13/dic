decentralized: /diːˈsɛntrəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 分散的，去中心化的 |  Transferred or delegated authority from a central to local branches, groups, or individuals. |  The company adopted a decentralized management structure to empower regional offices.  该公司采用分散的管理结构来赋能区域办事处。 | synonyms: distributed, dispersed, localized | antonyms: centralized, concentrated | adjective


decentralise: /diːˈsɛntrəlaɪz/ | v. | 分散，去中心化 |  To transfer or delegate (authority) from a central to local branches, groups, or individuals. | They decided to decentralise the company's operations. 他们决定将公司的运营分散化。 | synonyms: distribute, disperse, devolve | antonyms: centralize, concentrate | verb


decentralization: /diːˌsɛntrəlɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 分散化，去中心化 | The transfer of control of an activity or organization to several local offices or authorities rather than one single one. | The decentralization of power has led to greater regional autonomy. 权力的下放导致了更大的区域自治。| synonyms: distribution, dispersal, localization | antonyms: centralization, concentration | noun
