mills:/mɪlz/| n.|磨坊；工厂；制造厂|Factories or plants, especially ones that process materials like grain or lumber.  A building equipped with machinery for grinding grain into flour.|The old textile mills have been converted into loft apartments. 这些老纺织厂已经被改建成阁楼公寓。|Synonyms: factory, plant, works|Antonyms: None|Noun

mills:/mɪlz/| n.|研磨机；磨碎机|A machine for grinding or crushing something.|The pepper mill grinds the peppercorns into a fine powder. 胡椒研磨机将胡椒粒磨成细粉。|Synonyms: grinder, crusher|Antonyms: None|Noun
