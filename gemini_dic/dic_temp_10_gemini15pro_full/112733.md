lozengy:/ˈlɒzɪndʒi/ (US) /ˈlɒzəndʒi/ (UK)| adj.|菱形的，斜方形的|Shaped like a lozenge; rhombic.  A surface divided by diagonal lines into diamond shapes.|The stained-glass window was composed of lozengy panes.|近义词：rhombic, diamond-shaped|反义词: square, rectangular|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Old French *losenge, from losange "lozenge," from Vulgar Latin *lausa "flat stone," from Latin laus "praise, commendation." The sense development likely occurred through the use of lozenge-shaped stones in mosaics and other decorative work.
