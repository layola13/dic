curblike:/ˈkɜːrblaɪk/
adjective
像路缘的，类似路缘石的
Resembling a curb; similar in form or function to a curb.
The low curblike wall separated the flowerbed from the lawn.  低矮的类似路缘的墙将花坛与草坪隔开了。
The edge of the raised platform was curblike in appearance.  凸起平台的边缘看起来像路缘石。
近义词: curb-shaped, edge-like
反义词:  (No direct opposite; consider antonyms for "curb" itself, such as "unlimited," "unrestricted")
