go-native:/ɡoʊ ˈneɪtɪv/| v. |入乡随俗，同化；回归自然|To adopt the lifestyle or customs of the local people, especially when living in a foreign country, often abandoning one's own culture.  Also, to revert to a wild or natural state.|He went native after living in the Amazon for a year, even learning the local dialect and hunting with a blowgun. 他在亚马逊生活了一年后入乡随俗，甚至学习了当地方言，并用吹箭打猎。

After years of city life, she decided to go native and live off the land. 在多年的城市生活之后，她决定回归自然，靠土地为生。|近义词：acculturate, assimilate, integrate|反义词:alienate, isolate|词性:动词
