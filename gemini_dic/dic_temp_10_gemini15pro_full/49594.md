demersed:/dɪˈmɜːrst/
adjective
浸没的，沉入的
Growing completely submerged in water.  Completely immersed in something.
The demersed plant thrived in the aquatic environment.  这种沉水植物在水生环境中茁壮成长。
The scholar was demersed in his research, oblivious to the world around him.  这位学者沉浸在他的研究中，对外界的一切浑然不觉。
synonym: submerged, immersed
antonym: emerged, surfaced
词性: adjective
