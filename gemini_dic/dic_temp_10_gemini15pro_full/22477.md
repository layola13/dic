bookplate: /ˈbʊkpleɪt/ | n. | 藏书票；书笺 | A label pasted in a book, often bearing the owner's name or crest, to indicate ownership. |  Example: He had a beautiful, personalized bookplate in each of his first editions.  他每一本初版书里都有一张漂亮的个人藏书票。

Example:  She designed her own bookplate featuring a whimsical drawing of a cat reading a book. 她自己设计了一张藏书票，上面是一只猫读书的奇特图画。 | Synonym: ex libris, book label | Antonym: N/A | noun
