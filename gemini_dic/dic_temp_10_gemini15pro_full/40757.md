convictional:/kənˈvɪkʃənəl/
adjective
信念的；确信的；有坚定信仰的
Characterized by or based on strong conviction; deeply held beliefs.
He has a convictional belief in the importance of education. 他对教育的重要性有着坚定的信念。
近义词: firm, steadfast, unwavering, resolute, staunch
反义词: uncertain, doubtful, hesitant, wavering, ambivalent
词性: adjective

convictional:/kənˈvɪkʃənəl/
noun
信念；确信；深信
A firmly held belief or opinion.  (Less common usage as a noun.)
His convictionals about social justice drove his activism. 他对社会正义的坚定信念驱使他积极参与社会活动。
近义词: conviction, belief, persuasion, faith
反义词: doubt, uncertainty, skepticism
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *convictionālis, from Latin convictio (“conviction, refutation”), from convincō (“I conquer, convict”), from com- (“together, with”) + vincō (“I conquer”).
