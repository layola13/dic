drollness:/ˈdroʊlnəs/| n.|滑稽；古怪；幽默|The drollness of his remarks made everyone laugh. 他的话滑稽可笑，逗得大家都笑了。|例句：The drollness of the situation was not lost on us.  我们都注意到了情况的滑稽。

例句：Her drollness and wit made her a popular guest at parties.  她的幽默和机智使她成为聚会上受欢迎的客人。|近义词：humor, wit, comicality, whimsy|反义词：seriousness, solemnity|词性:noun

词源: From Middle French *drolle (“jester, buffoon”), from Middle Dutch drol (“devil, imp, goblin, mischievous child”), of uncertain origin.
