corymb: /ˈkɔːrɪm/ (UK), /ˈkɔːrɪmb/ (US)| n. |伞房花序|A flat-topped or convex indeterminate inflorescence in which the outer flowers open first.|The elder tree has a beautiful corymb of white flowers.|Synonyms:  umbel, raceme, panicle|Antonyms:  single flower, spike|Noun


corymbose: /kəˈrɪmboʊs/ (US), /kəˈrɪmbəʊs/ (UK)| adj. |伞房状的|Arranged in or resembling a corymb.|The corymbose flowers of the yarrow plant are a favorite of butterflies.|Synonyms:  umbellate, racemose|Antonyms:  spicate, solitary|Adjective
