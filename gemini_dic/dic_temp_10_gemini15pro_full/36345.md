coiled:/kɔɪld/| adj.|盘绕的，卷曲的|Wound or arranged in a coil or coils; spirally twisted.|The snake lay coiled in the grass. 蛇盘绕在草丛中。|近义词：twisted, curled, spiralled, wound|反义词:straight, uncoiled|词性:形容词
Etymology: From coil + -ed, past participle suffix.  The verb "coil" comes from Old French "coillir" (to gather, collect), ultimately from Latin "colligere" (to gather together).
