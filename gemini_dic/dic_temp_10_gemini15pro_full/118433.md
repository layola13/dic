mesa's:/ˈmeɪsəz/
noun
台地，平顶山；（尤指美国西南部地区的）方山
a broad, flat-topped elevation with one or more clifflike sides, common in the southwestern US.
The mesa's steep sides made it difficult to climb. 这座台地的陡峭山坡使攀登变得困难。
plateaus, tablelands
hills, valleys
n.
