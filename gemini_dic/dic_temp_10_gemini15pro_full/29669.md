cathartically:/kəˈθɑːrtɪkli/
adverb|
宣泄地，净化地|
In a way that provides catharsis; purgatively.  Having the effect of catharsis; relieving strong or repressed emotions.|
She wept cathartically after hearing the news of her friend's recovery. 她听到朋友康复的消息后痛哭了一场，得到了宣泄。|
Near synonyms: purgatively, cleansingly, purifyingly
Antonyms: repressively, restrainingly
词性: adverb
