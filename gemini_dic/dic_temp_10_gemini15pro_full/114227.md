mail-bombed:/ˈmeɪl bɒmd/ |v. |邮件炸弹攻击|The act of sending a massive amount of email to a specific address, often with the intention of overwhelming the recipient's server or inbox.  It can also refer to sending physical mail containing harmful substances.|The activist group mail-bombed the company's server in protest.|Synonyms: spam, flood, bombard, inundate|Antonyms: protect, filter|verb

mail-bombing:/ˈmeɪl bɒmɪŋ/ |n. |邮件炸弹攻击|The act of sending a massive amount of email to a specific address, often with the intention of overwhelming the recipient's server or inbox. It can also refer to sending physical mail containing harmful substances.|The mail-bombing campaign crashed the company's email server.|Synonyms: spamming, flooding, bombarding, inundating|Antonyms: protection, filtering|noun
