intermezzo:/ˌɪntərˈmetsoʊ/ (US), /ˌɪntəˈmetsəʊ/ (UK)| n. |间奏曲，插曲，幕间剧|A short piece of music played between the acts of a play or opera, or a short period of time between two events.|The orchestra played a lively intermezzo between the first and second acts.  管弦乐队在第一幕和第二幕之间演奏了一段轻快的间奏曲。|The coffee break was a welcome intermezzo in the long meeting.  在冗长的会议中，休息时间是一段受欢迎的插曲。|Synonyms: interlude, interval, break, respite|Antonyms: continuation, prolongation|Noun
