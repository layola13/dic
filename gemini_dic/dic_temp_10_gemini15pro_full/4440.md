alaunt:/əˈlɔːnt/ | adj. | 警惕的，惊恐的，不安的 | On the alert, watchful, apprehensive. | The deer stood alaunt, ears twitching, sensing danger. 鹿警惕地站着，耳朵抽搐着，感觉到了危险。 | 近义词: wary, vigilant, apprehensive | 反义词: oblivious, unaware, unconcerned | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *alaunte, from Old French *alaunt, present participle of *alauntir "to urge on, incite," from *a- "to" + *launtir "to leap," of Germanic origin.  The sense shifted from "eager, ready to spring" to "watchful, on the alert."
