ametabolic:/ˌeɪmɛtəˈbɒlɪk/| adj.|非代谢的，无代谢的|Relating to or characterized by ametabolism; not involving metabolism.  Specifically, designating an insect pupa in which there is little or no internal reorganization during development into the adult.|Some insects have an ametabolic pupal stage.|近义词:non-metabolic|反义词: metabolic|词性:形容词
