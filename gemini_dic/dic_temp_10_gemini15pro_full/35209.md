clucked:/klʌkt/
verb
发出咯咯声；（母鸡）咯咯叫
to make the short, low sound of a hen, especially after laying an egg
The hen clucked happily after laying an egg. 母鸡下蛋后高兴地咯咯叫着。
近义词：cackle, chuckle
反义词: (无明显的反义词)
词性: verb

clucked:/klʌkt/
verb
(表示轻蔑或不耐烦)发出啧啧声
to make a sound with your tongue to show disapproval or impatience
She clucked disapprovingly at his messy room. 她对他凌乱的房间发出不赞成的啧啧声。
近义词：tut, tsk
反义词: (无明显的反义词)
词性: verb
