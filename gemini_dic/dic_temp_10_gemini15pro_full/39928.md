constriction:/kənˈstrɪkʃən/
n.|收缩，紧缩；压抑感|The constriction of blood vessels can lead to high blood pressure. 血管收缩会导致高血压。|Synonyms: compression, contraction, tightening|Antonyms: expansion, dilation, enlargement|noun
constrict:/kənˈstrɪkt/
v.|收缩，缩小；压缩，限制|Stress can constrict blood vessels. 压力会使血管收缩。|Synonyms: compress, contract, tighten|Antonyms: expand, dilate, enlarge|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin constrictio, from Latin constringere "to bind together, draw tight," from com- "together" + stringere "to draw tight."
