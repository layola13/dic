didelphous:/daɪˈdɛlfəs/
adjective
双雄蕊的；二体雄蕊的
Having two groups or bundles of stamens, typically joined by their filaments.
The didelphous stamens are a key characteristic of this plant family.  这种植物家族的关键特征是二体雄蕊。
Pea flowers exhibit a didelphous arrangement of stamens. 豌豆花呈现出二体雄蕊的排列。
近义词: diadelphous (variant spelling),  biserial (referring to arrangement in two rows)
反义词:  monadelphous (stamens united in a single bundle), polyadelphous (stamens united into more than two bundles)
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *di- "two" + *delphys "womb" (referring to the bundles of stamens)
