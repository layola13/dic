liquate:/ˈlɪkweɪt/| v. |液化，熔化; 清算，变卖|to become liquid or cause something to become liquid; to sell assets or investments in order to raise cash|The intense heat liquated the metal.  高温使金属液化了。

The company was forced to liquate its assets to pay off its debts. 公司被迫变卖资产以偿还债务。|近义词：melt, dissolve, liquefy|反义词：solidify, freeze|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *liquatus*, past participle of *liquāre*  "to make liquid, melt," from *liquidus* "liquid, fluid."
