incising:/ɪnˈsaɪzɪŋ/
verb
切割，雕刻
The act of cutting or carving into a surface.
The artist was incising a design into the wood. 艺术家正在木头上雕刻图案。
近义词: carving, engraving, etching
反义词: smoothing, filling
词性: verb (动名词)
