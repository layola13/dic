grannam:/ˈɡrænəm/ | n. |奶奶；外婆 |A colloquial or dialect term for "grandmother."  It's a more informal and affectionate way to refer to one's grandmother.  Primarily used in British English, particularly in certain dialects. | Example: My grannam used to bake the best apple pie. 我的奶奶过去烤的苹果派最好吃。
Example:  He went to visit his grannam in the countryside. 他去乡下看望他的奶奶。 | Near synonyms: grandma, grandmother, granny, nan |Antonyms: N/A | Noun
