airfoil:/ˈɛərfɔɪl/| n.|机翼,翼型|A surface, such as a wing, propeller blade, or rudder, whose shape and orientation control lift, thrust, or directional stability.|The airplane's airfoil was designed for maximum lift at low speeds.|近义词：wing, blade, aileron|反义词:|noun
