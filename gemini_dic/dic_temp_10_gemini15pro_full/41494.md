cornucopia:/ˌkɔːrnjəˈkoʊpiə/ /ˌkɔːnjuˈkəʊpiə/
|n.|丰饶之角；大量；丰富|A horn overflowing with fruit, flowers, and grain, symbolizing abundance; a plentiful supply of good things.|The Thanksgiving table was a cornucopia of delicious food. 感恩节的餐桌上摆满了丰盛的美食。|synonyms: abundance, profusion, bounty, plethora|antonyms: scarcity, dearth, lack, paucity|noun
