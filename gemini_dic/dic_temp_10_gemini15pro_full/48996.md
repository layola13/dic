deglorifying: /diːˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪɪŋ/
verb
使失去荣耀；贬低；使丢脸
reducing or removing the glory of something or someone.
The article deglorified the war hero by focusing on his personal failings. 这篇文章通过关注战争英雄的个人缺点而贬低了他。
近义词: disgracing, dishonoring, debasing, humiliating
反义词: glorifying, exalting, honoring, praising
词性: verb

deglorified: /diːˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪd/
adjective
被贬低的；被玷污的；失去荣耀的
having been stripped of glory or honor.
The deglorified king was forced to live in exile. 被贬低的国王被迫流亡。
近义词: disgraced, dishonored, debased, humiliated
反义词: glorified, exalted, honored, praised
词性: adjective
