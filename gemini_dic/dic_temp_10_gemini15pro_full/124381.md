motivity:/moʊˈtɪvɪti/
n.
动力; 积极性;  
The inner drive or impetus that prompts a person to act in a certain way.
His motivity for studying so hard is to get into a good university. 他如此努力学习的动力是想进入一所好大学。

近义词: motivation, drive, impetus, incentive
反义词: apathy, indifference, lethargy
词性: noun


Etymology:  From Late Latin *motivitas, from Latin motus (“motion”), past participle of movēre (“to move”). Equivalent to motive +‎ -ity.
