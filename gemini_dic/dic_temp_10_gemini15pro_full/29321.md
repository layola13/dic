castigated:/ˈkæstɪɡeɪtɪd/
verb
严厉批评；责骂；惩罚
reprimand (someone) severely.
The teacher castigated the student for cheating on the exam. 老师严厉批评了考试作弊的学生。
He was castigated by his colleagues for his unethical behavior. 他因为不道德的行为受到了同事们的谴责。
rebuke, reprimand, censure, criticize
praise, commend, laud
