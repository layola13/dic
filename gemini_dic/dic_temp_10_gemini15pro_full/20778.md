blanketless:/ˈblæŋkɪtləs/
adjective
无毛毯的; 没有毛毯的
Without a blanket.
The blanketless child shivered in the cold.  没有毛毯的孩子在寒冷中瑟瑟发抖。
Near Synonyms: uncovered, exposed
Antonyms: blanketed, covered
词性: 形容词
