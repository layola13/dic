half-regained:/ˌhɑːf riˈɡeɪnd/
adj.
部分恢复的，一半收回的
partially recovered or regained
He felt a sense of half-regained confidence after the setback.  挫折之后，他感到信心部分恢复了。
近义词: partially recovered, partly restored
反义词: completely lost, fully deprived
词性: adjective
词源: "half-" (Old English healf) + "regained" (past participle of "regain," from re- "back" + gain "obtain")
