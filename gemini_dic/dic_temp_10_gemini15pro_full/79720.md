glued:/ɡlud/ | adj. | 粘住的，固定的，全神贯注的 | firmly attached with glue or as if with glue; absorbed in something |  The pages of the old book were glued together. 这本旧书的书页粘在一起了。| synonyms: stuck, adhered, fixed, attached, engrossed, rapt | antonyms: loose, detached, unstuck, separated | adjective

glued:/ɡlud/ | v. | 粘合，紧附 | past tense and past participle of "glue" | He glued the broken pieces back together. 他把碎片粘回去了。| synonyms: stick, adhere, fix, attach | antonyms: detach, separate, unstuck | verb
