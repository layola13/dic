inferior:/ɪnˈfɪəriər/ /ɪnˈfɪriər/ | n.|下级，次品|A person lower than another in rank, status, or ability.| He felt inferior to his older brother. 他觉得自己不如哥哥。|近义词: subordinate, underling | 反义词: superior | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *īnferior* (comparative of *īnferus* "low"), from *īnfrā* "below".
