marshmallow:/ˈmɑːrʃˌmæloʊ/ (US), /ˌmɑːʃˈmæləʊ/ (UK)| n.|棉花糖；药蜀葵|A soft, spongy confection made from sugar, gelatin, and often corn syrup, typically molded into small cylindrical shapes.|I roasted marshmallows over the campfire. 我在篝火上烤棉花糖。|Synonyms: confection, sweet, treat|Antonyms: None|Noun

marshmallow:/ˈmɑːrʃˌmæloʊ/ (US), /ˌmɑːʃˈmæləʊ/ (UK)| adj.|柔软的；娇气的|Soft and yielding; effeminate or overly sentimental.|He has a marshmallow personality. 他性格软弱。|Synonyms: soft, squishy, yielding, tender, delicate, mushy, effeminate, sentimental|Antonyms: hard, firm, tough, strong, resilient, masculine|Adjective
