offloading:/ˌɒfˈləʊdɪŋ/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊdɪŋ/
n.|卸载，卸货，减负|The act of transferring something from one place to another, typically to reduce burden or capacity.|The ship was offloading cargo at the dock.  这艘船正在码头卸货。|synonyms: unloading, discharging, transferring|antonyms: loading, uploading
词性:名词


offload:/ˌɒfˈləʊd/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊd/
vt.|卸载，卸货，减负，转嫁(任务等)|To transfer something from one place to another, typically to reduce burden or capacity.|The company offloaded some of its work to freelancers.  这家公司将一些工作转嫁给了自由职业者。|synonyms: unload, discharge, transfer, dump|antonyms: load, upload
词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From off + load + -ing.  "Load" comes from Old English *lād, meaning "way, course, journey."  The prefix "off" indicates removal or separation.
