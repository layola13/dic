fishings:/ˈfɪʃɪŋz/
noun
捕鱼活动；渔场；捕鱼量
The act, practice, or occupation of catching fish.  The place where fish are caught. The amount of fish caught.
The weekend was spent at the lake, enjoying some peaceful fishings.  周末在湖边度过，享受宁静的垂钓。
