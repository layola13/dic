fishy:/ˈfɪʃi/| adj.|可疑的，引起怀疑的，不诚实的，有鱼腥味的|Appearing suspicious; suggesting that something is wrong or dishonest; smelling of fish. |例句1：His story sounds a bit fishy. 他的故事听起来有点可疑。|例句2：The deal seemed fishy, so I backed out. 这笔交易看起来很可疑，所以我退出了。|近义词：suspicious, dubious, questionable, shady|反义词：trustworthy, believable, honest|词性:形容词

词源：From fish + -y.  The literal sense "smelling of fish" dates back to the 15th century. The figurative meaning "dubious, questionable" arose in the 19th century.
