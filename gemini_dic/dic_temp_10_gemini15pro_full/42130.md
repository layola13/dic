cottonmouths:/ˈkɑːtnˌmaʊðz/ /ˈkɒtnˌmaʊθz/
n.
棉口蛇（蝮蛇的一种）; (因害怕而)口干舌燥的人
A venomous semiaquatic pit viper (Agkistrodon piscivorus) of the southeastern U.S., having a dark olive or brownish body and whitish mouth. Also called water moccasin.  A person whose mouth becomes dry from fear.
例句：Cottonmouths are known for their aggressive behavior. 棉口蛇因其攻击性行为而闻名。

例句：He got cottonmouths before his presentation. 他在演讲前感到口干舌燥。
近义词：water moccasin, viper, copperhead (for the snake);  tongue-tied, speechless (for the person)
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "calm, composed" for the person)
词性: 名词 (plural)
