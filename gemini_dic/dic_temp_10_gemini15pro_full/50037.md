denotation:/ˌdɪːnəʊˈteɪʃən/ |n.| 表示；指称；意义 |A denotation is the literal or primary meaning of a word, in contrast to the feelings or ideas that the word suggests.|The denotation of "home" is a place where one lives, but its connotations may include warmth, security, and family. |近义词:meaning, signification, sense, reference|反义词:connotation|词性:noun
