ahistorically:/ˌeɪhɪˈstɔːrɪkli/
adverb
非历史地；脱离历史背景地
Without reference to history; in a way that ignores or disregards historical context.
Her ahistorically naive interpretation of the poem ignored its cultural context. 她对这首诗非历史地天真的解释忽略了其文化背景。
The events were presented ahistorically, as if they occurred in a vacuum. 这些事件被非历史地呈现出来，就好像它们发生在真空中一样。
Synonyms: non-historically, unhistorically
Antonyms: historically, contextually
词性: 副词
