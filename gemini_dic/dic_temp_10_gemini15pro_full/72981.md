foolhardinesses:/ˌfuːlˈhɑːrdɪnəsɪz/
noun
鲁莽行为；蛮干；轻率
The tendency to be rash and reckless, disregarding potential dangers.
His youthful foolhardinesses led him into trouble. 他年轻时的鲁莽行为给他带来了麻烦。
Synonyms: recklessness, rashness, imprudence, temerity, audacity
Antonyms: caution, prudence, circumspection
n.
