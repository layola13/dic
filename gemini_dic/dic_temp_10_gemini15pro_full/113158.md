lupinin:/ˈluːpɪnɪn/| n. |羽扇豆碱|A bitter, crystalline alkaloid, C10H19ON, obtained from various lupine plants, especially *Lupinus luteus*. It is poisonous, causing convulsions, paralysis, and asphyxia.  |Lupinin is a toxic compound found in lupine seeds. 羽扇豆碱是一种存在于羽扇豆种子中的有毒化合物。

Lupin seeds must be processed to remove lupinin before they can be safely consumed. 羽扇豆种子在安全食用前必须经过加工去除羽扇豆碱。|近义词: (As a toxic alkaloid, no direct synonyms. Could consider other lupine alkaloids like sparteine or lupanine based on context.)  没有直接近义词，根据上下文可以考虑其他羽扇豆生物碱，如金雀花碱或羽扇豆碱。|反义词: (No antonyms for a chemical compound.)  化学化合物没有反义词。|词性:noun
