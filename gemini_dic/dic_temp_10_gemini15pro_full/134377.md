noninterpretable:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtɜːprɪtəbl/ | adj. | 不可解释的，无法诠释的 | Not able to be interpreted or understood; inexplicable. | The results of the experiment were noninterpretable due to a flaw in the methodology. 实验结果由于方法上的缺陷而无法解释。| 近义词: inexplicable, uninterpretable, incomprehensible | 反义词: interpretable, understandable, clear | 词性: adjective
词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + interpretable (from interpret, from Latin interpretari "to explain, expound, understand," from interpres "agent, negotiator, translator")
