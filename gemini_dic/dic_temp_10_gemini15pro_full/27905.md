canteen:/kænˈtiːn/| n.|食堂，小卖部，水壶|A canteen is a restaurant in a factory, office, school, or military base where meals are served to employees or students. It can also refer to a small container used for carrying water or other drinks.|The school canteen serves hot lunches every day. 学校食堂每天供应热午餐。|synonyms: cafeteria, mess hall, snack bar, water bottle|antonyms: restaurant (in a general sense)|noun

canteen:/kænˈtiːn/| n.|水壶|A canteen is a small container used for carrying water or other drinks, often used by soldiers or hikers.|He filled his canteen with fresh water before the hike. 他在徒步旅行前装满了新鲜的水。|synonyms: water bottle, flask|antonyms:  (no direct antonym)|noun
