duffle:/ˈdʌf(ə)l/| n.|行李袋；粗呢大衣|A large bag, typically made of canvas or other strong fabric, cylindrical in shape and closed by a drawstring, used for carrying clothes or other belongings.|He packed his clothes in a duffle bag for the weekend trip. 他把周末旅行的衣服装进了一个行李袋里。|近义词:bag, holdall, kitbag|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 suitcase (行李箱) 表示更正式的行李)|名词

duffel:/ˈdʌf(ə)l/| n.|粗呢；粗呢大衣|A coarse woolen cloth, typically used for overcoats, or a coat made of this cloth.|The soldier wore a heavy duffel coat to protect himself from the cold. 士兵穿着一件厚厚的粗呢大衣来御寒。|近义词:cloth, fabric, material|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|名词
