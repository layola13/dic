oxbows:/ˈɒksbəʊz/ (UK) /ˈɑːksboʊz/ (US)| n. |牛轭湖|A U-shaped bend in a river or stream, or a lake formed when such a bend is cut off from the main channel.|The Mississippi River is known for its numerous oxbows.|近义词：meander, loop, bend|反义词:straight (section of river)|noun
