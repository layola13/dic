daydreamers:/ˈdeɪˌdriːmərz/| n. |白日梦者，幻想家|A person who spends time daydreaming; a fanciful or impractical person.  Those lost in thought, detached from reality, often seen with a wistful gaze.|He was one of those daydreamers who could get lost in his own thoughts for hours. 他是那种一幻想起来就能几个小时都沉浸在自己世界里的人。|Synonyms: fantasists, visionaries, idealists, romantics|Antonyms: realists, pragmatists|Noun
