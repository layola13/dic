barest:/ˈbɛərɪst/| adj.|最少的，最小的，仅仅的，光秃秃的|Superlative adjective of bare: most bare.  Having the least covering or adornment; reduced to the simplest or most essential form.|The room was barest, with only a bed and a chair.  房间里空荡荡的，只有一张床和一把椅子。|Synonyms:  minimal, simplest, stark, mere, basic|Antonyms:  most, fullest, maximal, lavish, ornate|adjective
