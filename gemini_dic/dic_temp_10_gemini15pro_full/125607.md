multiversant:/ˌmʌltɪˈvɜːrsənt/| adj.| 多元的，多方面的，通晓多种语言的 | Proficient in or knowledgeable about multiple diverse fields, subjects, or cultures; having wide-ranging skills or experience. |  She is a multiversant artist, skilled in painting, sculpture, and photography. 她是一位多才多艺的艺术家，擅长绘画、雕塑和摄影。

|  The company is looking for a multiversant engineer with experience in both software and hardware development.  这家公司正在寻找一位精通软件和硬件开发的多方面工程师。| 近义词: versatile, multifaceted, polymathic | 反义词: specialized, narrow, limited | 词性: adjective

词源: "multi-" (many) + "versant" (familiar with). "Versant" comes from French "versant," present participle of "verser" (to turn, to incline), ultimately from Latin "vertere" (to turn).
