excusatory:/ɪkˈskjuːzətɔːri/
adj.
辩解的，道歉的，托辞的
Intended to excuse or justify; apologetic.
He offered an excusatory smile for his lateness.  他为迟到歉意地笑了笑。
近义词：apologetic, justifying, defensive, explanatory
反义词：accusatory, blaming, incriminating
词性: adjective
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin excusatorius from excusare ‘to excuse’.
