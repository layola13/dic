good-for:/ˌɡʊd ˈfɔːr/| adj.|有益的，有用的|Beneficial or advantageous for someone or something.  Suitable or appropriate for a particular purpose.|例句：Eating vegetables is good for your health. 吃蔬菜对你的健康有益。

例句：This soil is good for growing tomatoes.  这种土壤适合种植西红柿。|近义词：beneficial, healthy, wholesome, advantageous, suitable, appropriate|反义词：bad for, harmful, detrimental|词性:形容词
