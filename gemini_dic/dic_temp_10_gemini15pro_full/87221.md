heremitical:/ˌhɛrɪˈmɪtɪkəl/
adjective
隐居的；遁世的；像隐士的
Characterized by or suggesting the life of a hermit; solitary and secluded.
The old cabin had a heremitical feel, miles away from civilization.  这间古老的小屋有一种隐居的感觉，远离尘世。
He led a heremitical existence in the mountains. 他在山中过着隐居的生活。

近义词:reclusive, solitary, secluded, ascetic
反义词:gregarious, sociable, outgoing

词性:adjective
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin heremiticus, from Greek erēmitikos, from erēmitēs ‘hermit’ (see hermit).
