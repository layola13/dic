foreguess:/fɔːrˈɡɛs/ | v. n. |预言，预测；预想 |To predict or foresee (something); a prediction. |例句：She foreguessed the outcome of the election. 她预言了选举的结果。

|例句：His foreguess proved to be accurate. 他的预测证明是准确的。

|近义词：predict, foresee, anticipate, forecast |反义词：hindsight |词性：动词/名词


foreguess:/fɔːrˈɡɛs/ | n. |预言，预测 |A prediction or forecast. |例句：The foreguess of a recession proved correct. 对经济衰退的预测证明是正确的。

|例句：Her foreguess about the stock market was remarkably accurate. 她对股市的预测非常准确。

|近义词：prediction, forecast, prophecy |反义词：N/A |词性：名词

词源: From Middle English *forgesse, from Old English *foregiesan ("to foresee"), from fore- + -giesan, from Proto-Germanic *ga-saihwanan ("to see"), equivalent to fore- + guess.
