homecomings: /ˈhoʊmkʌmɪŋz/
n.
返校节；回家；回归
A homecoming is an occasion when someone returns home after being away for a long time, especially when celebrated by other people, or the return itself.  It can also refer to the annual celebration held at a high school or college for alumni.

Homecomings are a time for reminiscing and reconnecting with old friends.
返校节是回忆往事、与老朋友重新联系的时刻。


The town held a grand homecoming celebration for the returning soldiers.
该镇为归来的士兵举行了盛大的欢迎仪式。


近义词: return, arrival, reunion
反义词: departure, leaving


词性: 名词 (Noun)
