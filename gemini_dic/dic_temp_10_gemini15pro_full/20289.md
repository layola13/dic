biscuit-like:/ˈbɪskɪtˌlaɪk/| adj.|像饼干的，饼干状的|Resembling a biscuit in texture, shape, or taste.|The cake had a dry, biscuit-like texture.  这蛋糕口感很干，像饼干一样。|近义词：crispy, crunchy, dry, brittle|反义词:soft, chewy, moist|词性:形容词

词源:
biscuit (from Middle French bescuit, meaning "twice-cooked") + like (Old English gelic, meaning "similar")
