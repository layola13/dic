nonquantitativeness:/nɒnˈkwɒntɪtətɪvnəs/ | n. | 非量化性；非定量性 |  The quality or state of not being quantifiable or expressed in numerical terms.  It refers to aspects of information or data that are descriptive, subjective, or otherwise not easily measured numerically. | The nonquantitativeness of the study's findings made it difficult to draw firm conclusions.  这项研究结果的非量化性使得难以得出确切的结论。| Qualitative, descriptive | Quantitative | noun
