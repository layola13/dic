mola:/ˈmoʊlə/ (US), /ˈməʊlə/ (UK)| n.|磨石；臼齿；太阳鱼|A hard, round stone used for grinding grain. Also a type of large ocean sunfish.  From Latin *mola* "millstone."|The ancient Romans used a mola to grind wheat into flour.|Synonyms: grindstone, quern (for the stone); ocean sunfish, mola mola (for the fish)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
