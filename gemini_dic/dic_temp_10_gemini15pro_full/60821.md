electroplating:/ɪˈlɛktrəʊpleɪtɪŋ/ /ɪˈlɛktroʊpleɪtɪŋ/
n.|电镀|The process of coating a metal object with a thin layer of another metal using electrolysis.|The spoons were electroplated with silver. 这些勺子镀了银。|galvanization, plating|stripping|noun
electroplate:/ɪˈlɛktrəʊpleɪt/ /ɪˈlɛktroʊpleɪt/
v.|电镀|To coat (a metal object) by electrolytic deposition with another metal.|They electroplated the bumpers with chrome. 他们给保险杠镀了铬。|plate, coat|strip|verb
