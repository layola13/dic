darbys:/ˈdɑːrbiz/| n. |达比鞋, 一种系带皮鞋|A type of laced leather shoe, typically with a low heel and a plain toe.| He wore a pair of well-polished darbys to the office.  他穿着一双擦得锃亮的达比鞋去上班。|近义词：oxfords, bluchers |反义词: (No clear antonym, perhaps sandals or slippers as a contrast in formality) |词性:noun


Etymology:  Named after the 18th-century British shoemaker, Darby, who popularized the style.  源于18世纪英国鞋匠达比的名字，他推广了这种鞋的款式。
