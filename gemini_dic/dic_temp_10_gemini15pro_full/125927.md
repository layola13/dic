词源: From French *mousser, ultimately from Vulgar Latin *muttiare, frequentative of Latin mutare "to change."  The sense of "sentimental drivel" is from 1893. The command "Mush!" to sled dogs is of uncertain origin, perhaps from French marcher "to walk, march."
