palatals:/ˈpælətəlz/| n. |腭音；腭音字母|Consonant sounds articulated with the tongue against the hard palate.|The sounds /j/ and /ʃ/ are palatals.|近义词: palatal consonants|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider contrasting with other places of articulation like velars or labials)|词性: noun

palatal:/ˈpælətəl/| adj. |腭音的|Relating to or produced with the palate.|The letter 'y' often represents a palatal sound.|近义词: palatine|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider contrasting with other places of articulation)|词性: adjective
