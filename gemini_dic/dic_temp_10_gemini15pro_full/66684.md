exogen:/ˈeksədʒən/ | n. |外生变量；外生因素|Something that comes from outside a system; an external factor. |例句：The drought was an exogen that severely impacted agricultural output.  干旱是一个严重影响农业产量的外部因素。

例句：Government spending is often treated as an exogen in economic models. 在经济模型中，政府支出通常被视为外生变量。|近义词：external factor, outside influence, independent variable|反义词：endogen, internal factor|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *exo* (outside) + *gen* (producing).
