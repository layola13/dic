avadavat:/ˌævədəˈvɑːt/ | n. | 红梅花雀，梅花雀 | A small, brightly colored finch native to India and Southeast Asia, popular as a cage bird. |  The avadavat's vibrant red plumage makes it a favorite among birdwatchers. 红梅花雀鲜艳的红色羽毛使它成为观鸟者中的最爱。
