consecrate:/ˈkɒnsɪkreɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsɪkreɪt/ (US)| v. | 使神圣；奉献；祝圣；正式任命（主教等）|To make or declare something sacred; to dedicate formally to a religious or divine purpose; to devote something to a special purpose|The church was consecrated in 1854. 这座教堂于1854年祝圣。|Synonyms: sanctify, hallow, bless, dedicate|Antonyms: desecrate, profane, defile|verb

consecrate:/ˈkɒnsɪkreɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsɪkreɪt/ (US)| adj. | 神圣的；被献给神的|Dedicated to a sacred purpose|The consecrated ground was treated with reverence. 这片神圣的土地受到了崇敬的对待。|Synonyms: sacred, holy, hallowed|Antonyms: profane, unholy, secular|adjective
