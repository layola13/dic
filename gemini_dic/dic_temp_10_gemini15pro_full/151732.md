parvenu:/ˈpɑːrvəˌnuː/ /ˈpɑːrvəˌnjuː/
noun
暴发户，新贵
Someone who has recently become rich or powerful but is considered to lack social skills, good taste, or refinement.
He behaved like a parvenu, flaunting his wealth and status. 他表现得像个暴发户，炫耀着他的财富和地位。
Synonyms: upstart, social climber, nouveau riche
Antonyms: aristocrat, established member, old money
