outmanning:/ˌaʊtˈmænɪŋ/
v. |以人数取胜，在人数上超过|Defeating or surpassing someone by having a greater number of people.
The home team outmanned their opponents, fielding eleven players against their eight. 主队以十一人对八人的人数优势战胜了对手。
近义词: outnumbering, overpowering
反义词: being outnumbered, being outmatched
词性: 动词 (present participle of outman)
