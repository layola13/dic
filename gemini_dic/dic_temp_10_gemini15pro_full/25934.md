burnout:/ˈbɜːrnaʊt/ (US) /ˈbɜːnaʊt/ (UK)| n.|精疲力竭，倦怠|A state of emotional, physical, and mental exhaustion caused by prolonged or excessive stress.|She's suffering from burnout after working too many hours. 她因工作时间过长而感到精疲力竭。|近义词：exhaustion, fatigue, breakdown|反义词: energy, enthusiasm|名词
