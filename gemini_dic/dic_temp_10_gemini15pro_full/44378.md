cross-date:/ˈkrɔːs deɪt/ | adj. v. |交叉日期，跨日期；将日期填写错误 |To assign a date that spans across a period; to mistakenly write the wrong date. |例句：I accidentally cross-dated the check, putting January instead of February. 我不小心把支票的日期写错了，写了1月而不是2月。|例句：The software allows you to cross-date transactions for accurate reporting.  这个软件允许你交叉日期交易以获得准确的报告。|近义词：misdate, backdate, postdate |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "correctly date") |词性: adjective, verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From "cross-" (meaning across or intersecting) + "date" (referring to a specific point in time).  The verb usage likely developed from the adjectival form, describing the action of assigning a date that spans or intersects two periods.
