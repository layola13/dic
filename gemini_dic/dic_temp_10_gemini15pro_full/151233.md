parenchyma: /pəˈrɛŋkɪmə/ /pəˈrenkɪmə/ | n. | 薄壁组织 | The fundamental tissue of plants, composed of thin-walled living cells that function in photosynthesis, storage, and secretion.  |  The parenchyma of the stem stores water. 茎的薄壁组织储存水分。 | synonyms: ground tissue, essential tissue | antonyms: sclerenchyma, collenchyma | noun

parenchymatous: /pəˈrɛŋkɪˈmeɪtəs/ /pəˈrenkɪˈmeɪtəs/ | adj. | 薄壁组织的 |  Relating to, or consisting of parenchyma. | The apple has a parenchymatous core. 苹果有一个薄壁组织的核心。| synonyms: soft, spongy | antonyms: hard, woody | adjective
