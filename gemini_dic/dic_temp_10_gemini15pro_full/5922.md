ambitionless:/æmˈbɪʃən.ləs/
adj.
没有雄心的；缺乏抱负的
Lacking ambition; not ambitious.
He was an ambitionless young man, content with a simple life. 他是一个没有雄心的年轻人，满足于简单的生活。
Near Synonyms: unambitious, unaspiring, apathetic, listless, indolent, shiftless, lackadaisical
Antonyms: ambitious, aspiring, driven, motivated, zealous, go-getting
词性: 形容词
