inexpansible:/ˌɪnɪkˈspænsəbl/
adjective
不可膨胀的，不能扩张的
not able to be expanded or increased in size or amount
The container was made of an inexpansiible material.  这个容器是由一种不可膨胀的材料制成的。
The scientist studied the inexpansiible properties of the new alloy.  科学家研究了这种新合金的不可膨胀特性。
近义词:nonexpansive, inflexible, rigid
反义词:expansible, flexible, elastic
词性:adjective

词源:
From in- (not) + expansible (capable of being expanded), from Latin expandere (to spread out).
