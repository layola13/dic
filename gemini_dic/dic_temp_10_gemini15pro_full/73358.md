foregathered:/fɔːrˈɡæðərd/
verb
聚集，集合
To come together; assemble, especially for a social purpose.
The family foregathered for Christmas dinner.  家人聚在一起吃圣诞晚餐。
We foregathered at the local pub. 我们在当地酒吧聚会。
近义词: assemble, gather, congregate, convene
反义词: disperse, scatter
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From fore- + gather.  "Fore-" is an Old English prefix meaning "before," "in front," or "beforehand."  "Gather" comes from Old English "gadrian," meaning "to collect." Thus, "foregather" originally implied gathering beforehand or in advance.
