nondepressions:/ˌnɒndɪˈprɛʃənz/ | n. | 非抑郁状态；无抑郁症 |  A state of not having depression; the absence of depressive symptoms. This term is not commonly used in clinical settings, and "remission" or "recovery" are generally preferred. |  The study followed a group of individuals experiencing nondepressions to identify protective factors. 这项研究跟踪了一组没有经历抑郁症的个体，以确定保护性因素。|  The researchers observed a significant increase in nondepressions after implementing the wellness program. 研究人员观察到，在实施健康计划后，非抑郁症患者的数量显著增加。| 近义词: remission, recovery, wellness, mental health | 反义词: depression, depressive disorder, mental illness | 词性: noun

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "depression" (noun). The word is formed by simply negating "depression."
