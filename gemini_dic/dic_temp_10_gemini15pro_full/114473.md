makuti:/məˈkuːti/| n. |马库蒂，一种用干燥的棕榈叶制成的屋顶材料|Dried palm leaves used as roofing material, especially in East Africa.  It's a traditional building material. |The roof of the hut was made of makuti.  小屋的屋顶是用马库蒂做的。| The makuti thatching provided excellent insulation from the heat. 马库蒂茅草提供了极好的隔热效果。 | 近义词: thatch, palm leaves | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps modern roofing materials like tiles or metal) |词性:noun
