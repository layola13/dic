melt-away:/ˌmɛlt əˈweɪ/
verb phrase
逐渐消失，消散
to gradually disappear or fade away
The snow melted away as the temperature rose. 随着气温上升，雪逐渐融化了。
The crowd melted away after the concert. 音乐会结束后，人群逐渐散去。
近义词:disappear, vanish, fade away
反义词:appear, materialize
词性:动词短语
