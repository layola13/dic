disciplinal: /ˌdɪsəˈplɪnəl/
adjective
学科的；纪律的
Relating to a particular field of study (academic discipline) or to discipline in general.
Disciplinal procedures were initiated against the student. 对该学生启动了纪律处分程序。
Disciplinal knowledge in this area is constantly evolving.  这个领域的学科知识在不断发展。
近义词: academic, disciplinary
反义词: nondisciplinary, interdisciplinary
词性: adjective

词源: From discipline + -al.  "Discipline" comes from Latin *disciplīna*, meaning "teaching, learning."
