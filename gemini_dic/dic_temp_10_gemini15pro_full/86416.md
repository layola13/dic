heedful:/ˈhiːdfʊl/| adj.|留心的，注意的，深思熟虑的|Paying careful attention to something; mindful.|例句：Heedful of the warnings, they took extra precautions.  他们注意到了警告，采取了额外的预防措施。|近义词：attentive, mindful, observant, cautious, wary|反义词：heedless, careless, inattentive, negligent, reckless|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *hedful*, equivalent to heed +‎ -ful.
