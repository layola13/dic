civilizing:/ˈsɪvɪˌlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
使文明的；教化的；开化的
bringing people to a more advanced or refined state of social development; refining or improving the behaviour, manners, or morals of
例句：The civilizing influence of education.  教育的教化作用。

例句：They considered their mission to be civilizing the natives. 他们认为自己的使命是教化当地人。

近义词：refining, cultivating, enlightening, edifying
反义词：barbarizing, brutalizing
词性：adjective
