overtrain:/ˌoʊvərˈtreɪn/| v. |过度训练|To train too hard or too much, resulting in decreased performance, fatigue, and increased risk of injury.| He overtrained for the marathon and ended up injured. 他为马拉松过度训练，最终受伤了。|The athlete overtrained and suffered a stress fracture.  这位运动员过度训练并遭受了应力性骨折。|近义词：exhaust, overwork, overexert|反义词:undertrain|词性:动词
