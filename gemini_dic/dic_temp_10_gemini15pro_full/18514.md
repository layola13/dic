benediction:/ˌbenɪˈdɪkʃən/ | n. |祝福，祈祷|An utterance of good wishes, especially a blessing.  |The priest offered a benediction at the end of the service. 牧师在礼拜结束时做了祝福祈祷。|
Synonyms: blessing, invocation, grace |Antonyms: curse, malediction |noun


benediction:/ˌbenɪˈdɪkʃən/ | n. |恩赐，幸事|Something that promotes goodness or well-being.|Her smile was a benediction. 她的微笑是一种恩赐。|Synonyms: boon, blessing, gift|Antonyms: curse, misfortune|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *benedictio*, from Latin benedicere "to speak well of, bless," from bene "well" + dicere "to speak."
