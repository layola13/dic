myopia:/maɪˈoʊpiə/
n.
近视
nearsightedness; lack of foresight
例句：His myopia prevented him from seeing the distant mountains. 他的近视使他看不到远处的山脉。

例句：The government's myopia in not investing in education will have long-term consequences. 政府在教育方面缺乏远见的投资将产生长期后果。

近义词：nearsightedness, shortsightedness
反义词：hyperopia, farsightedness
词性：名词


myopic:/maɪˈɑːpɪk/
adj.
近视的；缺乏远见的
nearsighted; lacking foresight
例句：Her myopic view of the situation led to poor decision-making. 她对形势的短视导致了糟糕的决策。

例句：The company's myopic focus on short-term profits ultimately led to its downfall.  该公司近视般地专注于短期利润，最终导致了它的垮台。

近义词：shortsighted, narrow-minded
反义词：farsighted, prescient
词性：形容词
