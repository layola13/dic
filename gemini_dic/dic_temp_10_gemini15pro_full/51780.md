devotionist:/dɪˈvoʊʃənɪst/
n.
虔诚者；献身者
A person who is extremely devoted to a cause, religion, or person.
She was a true devotionist, dedicating her life to the service of others. 她是一位真正的虔诚者，一生致力于服务他人。
The devotionist spent hours each day in prayer and meditation.  这位虔诚的信徒每天花几个小时祈祷和冥想。

近义词: devotee, adherent, believer, follower
反义词: skeptic, critic, dissenter
词性: noun
