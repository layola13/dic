exscriptural:/ˌɛksˈskrɪptʃərəl/
adjective
非圣经的，圣经以外的
Not found in or derived from the scriptures.
His exscriptural beliefs caused controversy within the church. 他非圣经的信仰在教会内部引起了争议。
The professor cited exscriptural texts to support his argument. 教授引用了圣经以外的文本来支持他的论点。
近义词:apocryphal, uncanonical
反义词:scriptural, canonical
词性:adjective

词源:ex- (out of, from) + scriptural (relating to scripture)
