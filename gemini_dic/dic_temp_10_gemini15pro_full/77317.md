gathering:/ˈɡæðərɪŋ/
n.
聚会，集会
an assembly or meeting, especially one held for a specific purpose.
例句：The family gathering was a heartwarming occasion.  家庭聚会是一个温馨的场合。

例句：There was a large gathering of protesters outside the government building.  政府大楼外聚集了大批抗议者。

近义词：assembly, meeting, congregation, convention
反义词：dispersal, scattering

词性: 名词


Etymology: From Middle English *gaderynge, from Old English *gadrian, from Proto-Germanic *gadurōną (“to gather, unite”), from Proto-Indo-European *ghedh- (“to join, unite”).
