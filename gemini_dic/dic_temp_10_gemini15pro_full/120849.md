misbefitting:/ˌmɪsbɪˈfɪtɪŋ/
adjective
不合身的，不合适的
not suitable or appropriate; unbecoming
例句：He wore a misbefitting suit to the wedding. 他穿了一套不合身的西装去参加婚礼。

例句：The crown looked misbefitting on the young boy pretending to be a king.  这顶王冠戴在这个假扮国王的小男孩头上显得很不合适。

近义词：inappropriate, unsuitable, unbecoming, improper, incongruous
反义词：appropriate, suitable, fitting, proper, becoming
词性: adjective
