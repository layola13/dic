giantism:/ˈdʒaɪənˌtɪzəm/
n.
巨大症，巨人症
A condition characterized by excessive growth and height significantly above average, usually caused by overproduction of growth hormone by the pituitary gland.
The boy's giantism made him tower over his classmates.  这个男孩的巨人症使他比同学们高出一大截。
Near synonyms:  acromegaly, gigantism
Antonyms: dwarfism
noun
