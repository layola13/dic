financiering:/fɪnænˈsɪərɪŋ/
n.
融资；筹措资金；财务
The act of providing or raising funds for a purpose, especially a business venture.  It encompasses various methods and strategies to acquire capital.
The company is looking for new ways of financiering its expansion.  该公司正在寻找新的融资方式来扩展业务。
financing, funding, capitalization
defunding, divestment
noun
