impairing:/ɪmˈpɛərɪŋ/| adj. |损害的，削弱的|Weakening or damaging something, especially a faculty or function.|例句：Alcohol is impairing your judgement.  酒精正在损害你的判断力。

例句：The illness was impairing his ability to work. 这种疾病正在削弱他的工作能力。

近义词：damaging, harming, weakening, hindering, deteriorating, undermining|反义词：improving, enhancing, strengthening, benefiting|词性:形容词
