backwasher:/ˈbækˌwɒʃər/ (US), /ˈbækˌwɒʃə(r)/ (UK)| n.|反冲洗器，反冲洗装置|A device used to clean a filter by reversing the flow of water or other fluid through it.  It removes accumulated debris and cleans the filter media.|The pool's backwasher wasn't working, so the filter became clogged. 游泳池的反冲洗装置坏了，所以过滤器堵塞了。|Synonyms: filter cleaner, backflush device|Antonyms: N/A|noun

backwasher:/ˈbækˌwɒʃər/ (US), /ˈbækˌwɒʃə(r)/ (UK)| n.|反冲洗操作人员|A person who operates a backwashing system.|The backwasher cleans the filter every week. 反冲洗操作人员每周清洁过滤器。|Synonyms: filter operator, cleaner|Antonyms: N/A|noun
