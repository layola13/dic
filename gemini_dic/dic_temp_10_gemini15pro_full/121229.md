misencoding:/ˌmɪsɪnˈkoʊdɪŋ/
|n.|错误编码，编码错误|The misencoding of the data caused the program to crash. 数据的错误编码导致程序崩溃。|
|英文解析：The act or instance of encoding something incorrectly, especially digital information.|
|例句：The misencoding of the file resulted in data loss.  文件的编码错误导致数据丢失。|

|例句：Misencoding of characters can lead to display issues on different platforms. 字符编码错误会导致在不同平台上显示问题。|

|近义词：encoding error, incorrect encoding, faulty encoding|
|反义词：correct encoding, proper encoding|
|词性：noun|
