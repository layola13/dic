distressednesses:/dɪˈstrɛsɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
极度痛苦；极度悲伤
The state of being extremely distressed; multiple instances of distress.
Her recent distressednesses have been caused by financial troubles. 她最近的极度痛苦是由经济问题引起的。
近义词: agonies, miseries, sufferings
反义词: joys, comforts, happinesses
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From distressed + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality). Distressed itself comes from distress + -ed (suffix forming adjectives). Distress ultimately derives from the Old French destresse, meaning "constraint, oppression, hardship."
