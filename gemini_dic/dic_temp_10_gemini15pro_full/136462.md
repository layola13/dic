nonreviewability:/ˌnɒnriˌvjuːəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可审查性，不可复审性
The nonreviewability of the agency's decision frustrated the plaintiffs.  该机构决定的不可审查性使原告感到沮丧。
The nonreviewability of certain executive decisions is a cornerstone of the separation of powers.  某些行政决定的不可审查性是权力分立的基石。
近义词: unappealability, finality
反义词: reviewability, appealability
词性: noun

nonreviewable:/ˌnɒnriˈvjuːəbl/
adj.
不可审查的，不可复审的
The judge ruled that the decision was nonreviewable. 法官裁定该决定不可审查。
Some government actions are considered nonreviewable by the courts.  一些政府行为被法院认为是不可审查的。
近义词: unappealable, final
反义词: reviewable, appealable
词性: adjective


Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + review + -able (suffix meaning "capable of being") + -ity (suffix forming abstract nouns)
