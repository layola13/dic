legibility:/ˌledʒəˈbɪləti/| n.|清晰度，易读性|The quality of being clear enough to read. |The legibility of her handwriting is poor. 她的字迹很难辨认。| readability, clarity, decipherability |illegibility, unreadibility| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from late Latin legibilitas, from Latin legibilis ‘able to be read’ (see legible).
