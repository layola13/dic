hoorayed:/hʊˈreɪd/| v.|欢呼，喝彩|To shout 'hooray' to express excitement, joy, or approval. |The crowd hoorayed when the team scored.  人群在球队得分时欢呼雀跃。|
The children hoorayed as the parade went by. 孩子们在游行队伍经过时欢呼雀跃。|
近义词：cheered, applauded, acclaimed|反义词: booed, hissed|词性:动词
