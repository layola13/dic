matchsticks:/ˈmætʃˌstɪks/ | n. |火柴棍，火柴棒 | Small sticks of wood or cardboard tipped with a flammable substance that ignites when struck against a rough surface. |  He lit the candle with a matchstick. 他用一根火柴点燃了蜡烛。

matchsticks:/ˈmætʃˌstɪks/ | n. |火柴 | (plural of matchstick)  | She used two matchsticks to start the fire. 她用了两根火柴生火。


Etymology->
From match + stick.  "Match" originally referred to a slow-burning cord or fuse, and "stick" refers to the small piece of wood.

Synonyms->
matches (when referring to multiple matchsticks)
splints (thin, easily broken pieces of wood)
kindling (small pieces of dry wood used for starting a fire)

Antonyms->
lighter
fire starter
