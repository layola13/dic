devolve:/dɪˈvɒlv/ /dɪˈvɑːlv/| v.|转移（权力、责任等）；衰落|To transfer or delegate (power or responsibility) to a lower level, especially from a central government to local or regional administration; to degenerate or deteriorate.|The federal government devolved some of its powers to the states. |联邦政府将部分权力下放给各州。|The situation devolved into chaos. |情况恶化成了混乱。|synonyms: delegate, transfer, pass down, deteriorate, decline|antonyms: centralize, consolidate, improve, progress|verb

devolve:/dɪˈvɒlv/ /dɪˈvɑːlv/| n.|移交；衰落|The act or process of devolving.|The devolution of power to local authorities. |权力下放给地方当局。|The slow devolution of the empire. |帝国的缓慢衰落。|synonyms: delegation, transfer, decline, deterioration|antonyms: centralization, consolidation, improvement, progress|noun
