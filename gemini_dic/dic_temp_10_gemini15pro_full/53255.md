diremption:/daɪˈrɛmpʃən/
n.
分裂，分离，破坏
the act of tearing apart or separating; a breach or violent disruption
The diremption of the country after the civil war was devastating. 内战后国家的四分五裂是毁灭性的。

近义词:division, separation, severance, split, rupture, breach, rift, schism
反义词:unity, union, joining, connection, reconciliation

词性:noun

词源: From Latin *diremptio*, from *dirimere* "to separate," from *dis-* "apart" + *emere* "to take."
