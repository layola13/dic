nonprofits:/ˌnɑːnˈprɑːfɪts/ | n. | 非营利组织 | Organizations that do not operate for profit, but for religious, charitable, scientific, literary, or educational purposes.  They are often exempt from certain taxes. | Many nonprofits rely on donations and grants to fund their operations. 许多非营利组织依靠捐款和赠款来资助他们的运营。| synonyms: charities, not-for-profit organizations, voluntary organizations | antonyms: for-profit organizations, businesses | noun
