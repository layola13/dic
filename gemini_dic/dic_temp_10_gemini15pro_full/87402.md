herringboning:/ˈhɛrɪŋˌboʊnɪŋ/
n.
人字形，人字形图案；人字形砌砖法；人字形滑雪法
a pattern, arrangement, or stitching resembling the bones of a herring.
The brick pathway was laid in a herringbone pattern. 这条砖砌小路铺成了人字形图案。
近义词：zigzag, chevron
反义词：(No direct antonym, could be considered "straight" in some contexts)
词性: 名词
