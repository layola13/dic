materialisms: /məˈtɪəriəlɪzəmz/
| n. |物质主义，唯物主义|The belief that only physical matter exists and that there is no spiritual world or afterlife.  The excessive focus on acquiring possessions and material wealth. |例句：Materialisms can lead to a shallow and unsatisfying life. 物质主义会导致肤浅且不令人满意的生活。

例句：He rejected materialisms and embraced a more spiritual path. 他拒绝物质主义，选择了一条更精神的道路。|近义词：consumerism, materialism, worldliness|反义词：spiritualism, idealism|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "materialism" + "-s" (plural suffix).  "Materialism" comes from Late Latin "materialis" (material) + "-ism" (doctrine, belief). Ultimately from Latin "materia" (matter).
