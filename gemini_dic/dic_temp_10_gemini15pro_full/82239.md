ground:/ɡraʊnd/| n. v. adj.|地面，土地，立场，根据，磨碎，搁浅|The book fell to the ground. 书掉到了地上。|近义词：earth, floor, basis, reason|反义词：sky, air|词性:名词,动词,形容词
词源：From Middle English *ground, *grund, from Old English grund (“ground, foundation, bottom, abyss, plain”), from Proto-Germanic *grunduz (“ground, bottom”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰr̥n̥dʰís (“bottom, lower part”). Cognate with West Frisian grûn (“ground”), Dutch grond (“ground”), German Grund (“ground, reason”), Danish grund (“ground, reason”), Swedish grund (“ground, shallow”), Icelandic grunnur (“ground, shallow”).
