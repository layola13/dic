overreactive:/ˌoʊvərriˈæktɪv/| adj.|反应过度的，过度敏感的|Showing an excessive reaction to something; responding more strongly than is necessary or appropriate.|例句：He's a bit overreactive to criticism. 他对批评有点反应过度。

例句：She was diagnosed with an overreactive immune system. 她被诊断出免疫系统过度活跃。

近义词：hypersensitive, oversensitive, touchy|反义词:calm, placid, impassive|词性:adjective

overreact:/ˌoʊvərriˈækt/| vi.|反应过度，过分反应|To react or respond more strongly than is necessary or appropriate.|例句：I think you're overreacting to the situation. 我觉得你对这种情况反应过度了。

例句：He tends to overreact to even minor setbacks.  他即使遇到小挫折也容易反应过度。

近义词：overdo, exaggerate, go overboard|反义词:underreact|词性:verb

词源: over- + reactive.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much". "Reactive" comes from "react," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "reagere" meaning "to act back" or "to respond."
