decursive:/dɪˈkɜːrsɪv/| adj.|递推的，推论的|Proceeding by reasoning from premises to conclusions.  Relating to or involving recursion or deduction.|The decursive nature of mathematical proofs allows for complex theorems to be built upon simpler axioms. 数学证明的递推性质允许在更简单的公理上建立复杂的定理。|deductive, inferential|inductive|adjective
