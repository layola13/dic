gripman:/ˈɡrɪpmən/| n.|电车司机；缆车司机|A person who operates a grip car, especially on a cable car.|The gripman skillfully navigated the steep hills of San Francisco.  缆车司机熟练地驾驶着缆车穿过旧金山陡峭的山坡。|Example: The gripman rang the bell to warn pedestrians. 电车司机按铃警告行人。|Synonyms: operator, driver, motorman|Antonyms: passenger|noun
