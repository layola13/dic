dillard:/ˈdɪlərd/| n.|迪拉德 (姓氏，地名)|A surname, also a place name in Oregon, USA.  It may also refer to Annie Dillard, a Pulitzer Prize-winning American author.|例句：She's a Dillard from Oregon. 她是一位来自俄勒冈州的迪拉德。|近义词: (As a surname, no direct synonyms)  If referring to the author, perhaps "writer," "author."|反义词: (None)|词性:noun


dillard:/ˈdɪlərd/| adj.|迪拉德式的 (通常指安妮·迪拉德的写作风格)|Characteristic of or reminiscent of the writing style of Annie Dillard, often characterized by detailed nature observations and philosophical reflections.|例句：Her prose is distinctly Dillardesque, full of vivid imagery. 她的散文明显具有迪拉德式的风格，充满了生动的意象。|近义词: lyrical, descriptive, introspective, philosophical|反义词: plain, unadorned, superficial|词性:adjective (informal, derived usage)
