gratifications: /ˌɡrætɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
满足感，喜悦，令人高兴的事
The act of gratifying or the state of being gratified; satisfaction; pleasure.
She found great gratification in helping others. 她从帮助他人中获得了极大的满足感。
近义词: satisfaction, pleasure, contentment, fulfillment, enjoyment
反义词: dissatisfaction, displeasure, discontent, frustration
词性: 名词 (plural)


Etymology:
From Late Latin gratificationem, accusative of gratificatio (“gratifying, obliging”), from Latin gratificari (“to gratify”), from gratus (“pleasing, agreeable, thankful”) + -ficari, combinatory form of facere (“to do, to make”).
