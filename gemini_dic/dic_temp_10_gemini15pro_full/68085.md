fade-out:/ˈfeɪd aʊt/| n.|淡出，渐隐|A gradual decrease in the visibility or audibility of something, especially a scene in a film or a piece of music.|The movie ended with a slow fade-out to black.  这部电影以缓慢的淡出到黑色结束。|synonyms: dissolve, fade away|antonyms: fade-in, appear|noun

fade-outs:/ˈfeɪd aʊts/| n.|淡出，渐隐（复数）|Plural form of "fade-out," referring to multiple instances of gradual decreases in visibility or audibility.|The DJ used several fade-outs to transition smoothly between songs.  DJ用了几次淡出，在歌曲之间平滑过渡。|synonyms: dissolves, fade aways|antonyms: fade-ins, appearances|noun
