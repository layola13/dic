guesstimation:/ˌɡɛstɪˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 估计；估算 | An estimate based on a mixture of guesswork and calculation. |  Example 1: By my guesstimation, there were about 200 people at the concert.  我估计音乐会大约有200人。

Example 2:  The project's budget was just a guesstimation, and we ended up needing much more funding.  这个项目的预算是粗略估计的，我们最终需要更多的资金。 | 近义词: approximation, estimate, rough calculation | 反义词: precise calculation, exact measurement | 词性: noun
