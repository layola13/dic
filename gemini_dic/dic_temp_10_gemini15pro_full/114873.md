mammonite:/ˈmæməˌnaɪt/| n.|拜金主义者；财迷|A person who is devoted to the pursuit of wealth; a materialist. | He's such a mammonite, always chasing the next big deal. 他真是个财迷，总是追逐下一笔大生意。|Synonyms: materialist, money-grubber, mercenary |Antonyms: philanthropist, altruist| Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Mammon, the Aramaic word for riches, personified in the New Testament as a false god.
