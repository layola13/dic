legitimizer:/ləˈdʒɪtɪˌmaɪzər/ | n. | 合法化者；使…合法化的人（或事物）| someone or something that makes something legitimate | The new law served as a legitimizer for the government's actions.  新的法律使政府的行动合法化。| synonyms: validator, ratifier, authorizer | antonyms: invalidator, nullifier | noun

legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/ | adj. | 合法的；正当的；合理的| conforming to the law or to rules |  Is his business legitimate? 他的生意合法吗？ | synonyms: legal, lawful, valid | antonyms: illegal, unlawful, invalid | adjective
