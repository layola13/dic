lithiophilite:/ˌlɪθiˈɒfɪlaɪt/
n.
锂磷锰矿
A rare phosphate mineral containing lithium, manganese, and iron.
Lithiophilite is found in pegmatites. 锂磷锰矿发现于伟晶岩中。
Synonym: triphylite (when iron-rich)
Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific mineral)
