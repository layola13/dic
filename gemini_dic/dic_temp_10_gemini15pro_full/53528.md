disaugment:/dɪsɔːɡˈment/ | v. | 减少，缩小，削弱 | To lessen or decrease; to diminish or reduce in size, extent, or importance. | The new policy disaugmented the company's profits. 新政策减少了公司的利润。

The constant criticism disaugmented her confidence.  不断的批评削弱了她的自信心。| 近义词: decrease, diminish, reduce, lessen, weaken | 反义词: augment, increase, enhance, strengthen | 词性: 动词

词源:  "dis-" (not, opposite of) + "augment" (to increase).  The prefix "dis-" negates the meaning of "augment," which comes from the Latin "augmentare," meaning "to increase."
