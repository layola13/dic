chowders:/ˈtʃaʊdərz/| n.|浓汤，杂烩汤，|A thick soup or stew made with seafood or vegetables, often containing milk or cream.|例句：We had a delicious clam chowder for lunch. 我们午餐吃了美味的蛤蜊浓汤。

例句：She made a hearty corn chowder for the chilly evening. 她为寒冷的夜晚做了一份丰盛的玉米浓汤。|近义词：soup, stew, bisque|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑thin broth 清汤)|词性:noun

词源:  From earlier *jowter, probably from French chaudière (“cauldron”), from Latin caldaria (“cooking pot”), from calidus (“hot”).  The word's change in pronunciation is likely due to the influence of the word chew.
