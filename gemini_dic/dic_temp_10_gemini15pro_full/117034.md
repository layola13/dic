mclennanesque: /məˈklɛnənˌɛsk/
adjective
麦克莱南式的；模仿约翰·麦克莱南风格的
Characteristic of or resembling the fictional character John McClane, typically involving displays of resilience, resourcefulness, and a cynical sense of humor in the face of adversity, often while dealing with heavily armed adversaries.
His mclennanesque approach to the hostage situation involved more quips than careful planning. 他对人质事件的处理方式很像麦克莱南，妙语连珠多过周密的计划。
Synonyms: gritty, resilient, resourceful, defiant, wisecracking
Antonyms: timid, cowardly, cautious, helpless, humorless
词性: adjective
