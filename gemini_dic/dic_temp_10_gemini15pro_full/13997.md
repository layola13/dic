auriga:/ɔːˈraɪɡə/ | n. | 御夫座; (尤指古罗马的) 战车驭手 |  A constellation in the northern hemisphere near Perseus and Gemini, containing the bright star Capella.  It represents a charioteer. |  Auriga is easily visible in the winter sky. 御夫座在冬季夜空中很容易看到。|  The ancient Romans revered the auriga as a skilled and important figure. 古罗马人尊崇战车驭手，认为他们是技术娴熟且重要的人物。 | synonyms: charioteer, driver | antonyms: passenger | noun
