manichord:/ˈmænɪkɔːrd/ | n. | 手摇弦琴 | A medieval stringed keyboard instrument, the strings of which are plucked by plectra activated from a keyboard.  It is a precursor to the harpsichord. | The museum displayed a beautifully preserved manichord from the 16th century.  博物馆展出了一架保存完好的16世纪手摇弦琴。|  Synonyms: clavichord, virginal | Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific instrument, perhaps: modern piano, electronic keyboard) | noun
