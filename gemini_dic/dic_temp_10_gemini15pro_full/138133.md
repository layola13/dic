northeaster:/ˌnɔːrθˈiːstər/ |adj.|来自东北的；朝向东北的|Coming from the northeast; situated in the northeast.|The northeasterly wind chilled us to the bone.|近义词:northeastern|反义词:southwestern|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From north + east + -er.  "North" and "east" have Germanic origins. "-er" is a suffix denoting direction or origin.
