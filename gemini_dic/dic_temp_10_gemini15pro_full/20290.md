biscuitlike: /ˈbɪskɪtlaɪk/
adjective
像饼干的；饼干状的
Resembling a biscuit in texture, shape, or taste.
The cake had a dry, biscuitlike texture.  这蛋糕口感很干，像饼干一样。
The clouds were small and white, biscuitlike in appearance. 云朵又小又白，形状像饼干。
近义词:  crispy, crumbly, dry, hard
反义词:  soft, chewy, moist
词性: adjective
