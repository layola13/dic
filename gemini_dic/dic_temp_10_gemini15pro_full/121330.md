misgauged:/ˌmɪsˈɡeɪdʒd/
|v.|错误估计；判断失误|To estimate or judge something incorrectly.|He misgauged the distance and drove off the road. 他错误估计了距离，把车开下了路。|The company misgauged the public's interest in the product. 公司错误估计了公众对该产品的兴趣。|miscalculated, misjudged, underestimated, overestimated| correctly judged, accurately assessed|verb
