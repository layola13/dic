monopolist:/məˈnɒpəlɪst/ (UK) /məˈnɑːpəlɪst/ (US)| n.|垄断者，独占者|A person or company that has a monopoly.|The monopolist controlled the entire market for that product.  这个垄断者控制了该产品的整个市场。|近义词:monopolizer, trust, cartel|反义词:competitor, rival|noun

monopolize:/məˈnɒpəlaɪz/ (UK) /məˈnɑːpəlaɪz/ (US)| vt.|垄断，独占|To have or take control of the largest part of something so that other people are prevented from sharing it.|The company monopolized the telecommunications industry. 这家公司垄断了电信业。|近义词:dominate, control, corner|反义词:share, compete|verb

monopolistic:/məˌnɒpəˈlɪstɪk/ (UK) /məˌnɑːpəˈlɪstɪk/ (US)| adj.|垄断的，独占的|Relating to or characteristic of a monopoly.|The monopolistic practices of the company were investigated by the government.  该公司的垄断行为受到了政府的调查。|近义词:exclusive, restrictive|反义词:competitive, open|adjective
