hypersecretion:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈkriːʃən/ | n. |分泌过多；过度分泌| Excessive secretion of a substance, such as a hormone, by a gland. |  The hypersecretion of growth hormone can lead to gigantism. 生长激素分泌过多会导致巨人症。

The patient's symptoms were attributed to the hypersecretion of cortisol. 病人的症状被归因于皮质醇分泌过多。| 近义词: overproduction, oversecretion | 反义词: hyposecretion | 词性: noun
