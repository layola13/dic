battleships:/ˈbætəlʃɪps/ | n. | 战舰 | A warship of the largest size, heavily armed and armored. | The battleships exchanged fire across the bay. 战舰在海湾上互相开火。 | Example 2:  During World War II, battleships played a crucial role in naval warfare. 在第二次世界大战期间，战舰在海战中扮演着至关重要的角色。|  synonyms: warship, dreadnought, man-of-war | antonyms: dinghy, raft | noun
