acetamide:/ˈæsɪtəmʌɪd/ (US), /əˈsiːtəmaɪd/ (UK)| n.|乙酰胺|A crystalline amide CH3CONH2 that is the simplest amide derived from acetic acid. It is used as a solvent, plasticizer, and in the synthesis of other organic compounds.|Acetamide is commonly used as a solvent in organic chemistry. 乙酰胺是有机化学中常用的溶剂。

Acetamide is a precursor to thioacetamide. 乙酰胺是硫代乙酰胺的前体。
