monodactylous:/ˌmɒnəˈdæktɪləs/
adjective
单指（趾）的；一指（趾）的
Having a single finger or toe.
The aye-aye is a monodactylous primate, known for its elongated middle finger.  指猴是一种单指灵长类动物，以其细长的中指而闻名。
The fictional creature was depicted as monodactylous, with a single powerful claw on each hand.  这个虚构的生物被描绘成单指的，每只手上都有一个强大的爪子。
synonym: single-fingered, single-toed
antonym: polydactylous, multi-fingered, multi-toed
word type: adjective
