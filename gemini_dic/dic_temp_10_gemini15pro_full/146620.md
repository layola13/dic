overhandicapped:/ˌoʊvərˈhændɪkæpt/ | adj. | 过度照顾残疾人的，给予残疾人过多特殊待遇的 | Giving a person with a disability more advantages or assistance than they need, often with negative consequences. |  He felt that his son was being overhandicapped at school, and it wasn't helping him develop independence. 他觉得他的儿子在学校被过度照顾了，这不利于他培养独立性。

The overhandicapped athlete was given an unfair advantage in the competition.  那位被过度照顾的运动员在比赛中获得了不公平的优势。| 近义词: overprotected, overindulged, coddled | 反义词: neglected, underserved | 词性: 形容词
