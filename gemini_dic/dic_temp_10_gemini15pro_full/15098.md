ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bacchantique*, from Latin *bacchanticus*, from *Bacchantēs* (“Bacchantes”, plural of *Bacchans*), a priest or priestess of Bacchus, the Roman god of wine and revelry. Ultimately from Ancient Greek *Βάκχος* (*Bákkhos*, “Bacchus”).
