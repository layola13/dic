ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mistik*, from Old French *mistique*, from Late Latin *mysticus*, from Ancient Greek *μυστικός (mustikós)*, from *μύστης (mústēs)* (“one initiated into mysteries”), from *μύω (múō)* (“I close the eyes or mouth”). The original reference is to the secret rites of pagan religions, which required initiates to remain silent about them.
