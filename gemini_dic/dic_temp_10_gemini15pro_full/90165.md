hotkey:/ˈhɑːtkiː/ (US), /ˈhɒtkiː/ (UK)| n.|热键，快捷键|A key or combination of keys that performs a specific function when pressed. |Example:  I use a hotkey to quickly save my work. 我使用热键快速保存我的工作。

Example:  This program allows you to customize your hotkeys.  这个程序允许你自定义热键。|近义词：shortcut, keyboard shortcut|反义词: 无|名词
