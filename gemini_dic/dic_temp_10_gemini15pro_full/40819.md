convoy:/ˈkɒnvɔɪ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnvɔɪ/ (US)| n. vt.|护航，车队，舰队|A group of vehicles or ships traveling together, typically one accompanied by armed troops, warships, or other vehicles for protection.|The army convoy moved slowly through the mountains. 军队车队缓慢地穿过山区。|The ships sailed in convoy. 船只列队航行。|近义词：escort, fleet, procession, caravan|反义词: None|noun, verb


convoy:/ˈkɒnvɔɪ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnvɔɪ/ (US)| vt.|护送，护航|To accompany and protect (a group of vehicles or ships) while traveling.|Two destroyers convoyed the merchant ships. 两艘驱逐舰护送着商船。|Police convoyed the protesters through the city. 警察护送抗议者穿过城市。|近义词：escort, guard, protect|反义词: None|verb
