furbisher:/ˈfɜːrbɪʃər/ | n. | 磨光工，翻新者，修补匠 | A person who furbishes something, especially metal. |  The furbisher polished the silver until it gleamed.  磨光工把银器擦得闪闪发亮。| synonyms: polisher, restorer, renovator | antonyms: vandal, destroyer | noun

furbish:/ˈfɜːrbɪʃ/ | v. | 磨光，翻新，刷新 | To brighten or freshen something up; to renovate or restore. | He decided to furbish his old bicycle. 他决定把他的旧自行车翻新一下。| synonyms: polish, renovate, restore, revamp | antonyms: tarnish, dull, neglect | verb
