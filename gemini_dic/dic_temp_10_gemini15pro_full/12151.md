aruspice: /əˈrʌspaɪs/ /ɑːˈrʌspaɪs/
| n. | 占卜官；预言家 | A Roman priest who practiced divination by inspecting the entrails of sacrificial animals. | The aruspice examined the liver of the sacrificed ox to predict the outcome of the battle. 占卜官检查了祭祀用的牛的肝脏来预测战斗的结果。| synonym: diviner, soothsayer, prophet, seer | antonym: skeptic | noun
