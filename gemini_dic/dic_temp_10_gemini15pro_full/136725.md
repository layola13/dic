nonsensibly:/nɒnˈsɛnsɪbli/ /nɑːnˈsɛnsəbli/| adv.|荒谬地，愚蠢地|In a way that lacks sense or reason; foolishly.|He acted nonsensibly by investing all his money in a single, risky venture. 他把所有的钱都投资到一项单一、高风险的企业中，这是不明智的。|例句：She dressed nonsensibly for the cold weather. 她穿得太单薄，不适合寒冷的天气。|近义词：foolishly, absurdly, irrationally|反义词: sensibly, wisely, rationally|词性:副词

词源:
From non- + sensible + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "sensible" means "having or showing good sense or judgment," and "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.  Therefore, "nonsensibly" means "in a way that is not sensible."
