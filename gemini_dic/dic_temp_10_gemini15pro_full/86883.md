hemipteral:/ˌhɛmɪˈptɛrəl/| adj.|半翅目的，半鞘翅的|Having or resembling a hemelytron; relating to or characteristic of the Hemiptera (true bugs).  The forewing is hardened at the base and membranous at the tip, as in true bugs.|Example 1: Many hemipteral insects are agricultural pests. 许多半翅目昆虫是农业害虫。

Example 2: The shield bug is a common hemipteral insect found in gardens. 盾蝽是在花园中常见的半翅目昆虫。|近义词:hemelytrous|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps holometabolous for a different type of wing development)|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hēmi- (half) + pteron (wing), referring to the partly hardened forewings of these insects.
