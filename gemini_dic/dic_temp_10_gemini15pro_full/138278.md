notary:/ˈnoʊtəri/ (US) /ˈnəʊtəri/ (UK)| n.|公证人；公证员|A public official authorized to perform certain legal formalities, especially to certify documents or attest to the genuineness of signatures.|Example: I had to get my documents notarized before sending them overseas. 我必须在将文件寄往海外之前进行公证。|Synonym: notary public, legal officer, certifier|Antonym: N/A|noun

notaries:/ˈnoʊtəriz/ (US) /ˈnəʊtəriz/ (UK)| n.|公证人（复数）|Plural form of "notary," referring to multiple public officials authorized to perform certain legal formalities.|Example: The notaries verified the signatures on the contract. 公证人核实了合同上的签名。|Synonym: notary publics, legal officers, certifiers|Antonym: N/A|noun
