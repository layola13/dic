outtongue:/ˌaʊtˈtʌŋ/
|v.|伸舌头，吐舌头|To stick out one's tongue, often as a gesture of defiance, disgust, or playfulness.|The child outtongued her brother after he took her toy.  小孩在她哥哥拿走她的玩具后对他吐了舌头。|近义词: stick out one's tongue|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 retract one's tongue)|词性: verb
