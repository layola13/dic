dazedness:/ˈdeɪzɪdnəs/ /ˈdeɪzɪdnɪs/| n. |茫然，恍惚，神志不清|A state of being dazed; a feeling of being confused and disoriented.|例句：He felt a sense of dazedness after the car accident. 车祸后，他感到一阵茫然。

例句：The medication left her in a state of dazedness.  药物让她处于一种恍惚的状态。


|近义词：confusion, disorientation, bewilderment, lightheadedness|反义词: alertness, awareness, lucidity|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From daze + -ness.  Daze comes from Old Norse *dasa, meaning "to become weary."
