bowable:/ˈboʊəbl/| adj.|可鞠躬的，可弯曲的|Suitable for bowing (as a stringed instrument).|The cello is a bowable instrument.  大提琴是一种可拉奏的乐器。|The violin's strings are bowable. 小提琴的琴弦可以用弓拉。|近义词:bendable, flexible|反义词:rigid, inflexible|词性:形容词
Etymology: bow (verb, meaning "to bend") + -able (suffix meaning "capable of being")|词源：bow（动词，意为“弯曲”）+ -able（后缀，意为“能够”）
