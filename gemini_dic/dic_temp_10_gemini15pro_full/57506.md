dragnets:/ˈdræɡnɛts/
n.
拉网，拖网，地毯式搜查
A dragnet is a net drawn along the bottom of a body of water for catching fish. It can also refer to a system of coordinated measures for apprehending criminals or suspects.
The police threw out a dragnet to find the escaped convict. 警察撒下天罗地网来抓捕逃犯。
Synonyms: sweep, search, manhunt, round-up
Antonyms: release, liberation
