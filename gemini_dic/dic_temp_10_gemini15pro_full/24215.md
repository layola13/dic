bridgeboard:/ˈbrɪdʒbɔːrd/| n.|桥板，跳板|A board or platform used as a bridge, especially a temporary or makeshift one. A plank forming part of a scaffolding platform.|The workers used a bridgeboard to cross the ditch. 工人们用一块跳板跨过沟渠。|Synonyms: plank, gangplank, scaffold board|Antonyms: N/A|noun

bridgeboard:/ˈbrɪdʒbɔːrd/| n.|桥面|The flooring or deck of a bridge.|The bridgeboard was made of thick wooden planks. 桥面由厚厚的木板制成。|Synonyms: deck, flooring, roadway|Antonyms: N/A|noun
