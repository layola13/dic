annoyingly:/əˈnɔɪɪŋli/ | adv. | 恼人地，烦人地 | in a way that causes slight anger or irritation. |  The tap was dripping annoyingly. 水龙头滴水滴答答的，真烦人。 | Synonyms: irritatingly, vexingly, bothersomely | Antonyms: pleasantly, agreeably | Adverb

Etymology:
From annoying + -ly.  "Annoying" comes from the verb "annoy," which traces back to Old French "enoier" meaning "to injure, harm, vex."  Ultimately, it derives from Latin "in odio," meaning "in hatred."
