endorsee:/ɪnˌdɔːrˈsiː/
n.
被背书人；受让人
The person to whom a check, bill of exchange, or other negotiable instrument is endorsed.
The endorsee is responsible for presenting the check to the bank.  被背书人有责任向银行提示支票。
assignee, recipient, transferee
endorser
