equipped:/ɪˈkwɪpt/ | adj. | 配备的，装备的 |  Supplied with the necessary items for a particular purpose. |  The kitchen is fully equipped with modern appliances. 厨房配备齐全的现代电器。 | 近义词: furnished, prepared, geared, outfitted | 反义词: unequipped, unprepared, lacking | 词性:形容词

词源: From the Old French word *equiper*, from the prefix *e-* (meaning "out, forth") and the noun *esquip*, meaning "ship" or "equipment of a ship."  Originally meant "to fit out a ship."
