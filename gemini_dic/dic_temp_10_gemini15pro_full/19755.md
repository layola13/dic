billfold: /ˈbɪlfoʊld/ (US) /ˈbɪlfəʊld/ (UK)| n. |钱包；皮夹|A folding wallet for paper money, especially one without compartments for cards or coins.|He pulled a thick wad of cash from his billfold.|近义词：wallet, purse|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:  From bill + fold.  "Bill" in this context refers to paper money (a bill of exchange, banknote, etc.) and "fold" refers to the way it's folded.
