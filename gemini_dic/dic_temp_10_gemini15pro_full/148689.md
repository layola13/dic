overtinted:/ˌoʊvərˈtɪntɪd/
adjective
过度着色的，染色过深的
having too much color added, especially of a photograph or a hair coloring
The photograph was overtinted, making everyone look unnaturally orange.  这张照片颜色过深，让每个人看起来都像不自然地变成了橙色。
Her hair was overtinted and looked brassy. 她的头发染得太深了，看起来像黄铜一样。
近义词:overdyed, overcolored, excessively tinted
反义词:undertinted, washed-out, faded
词性:adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "tinted" (past participle of "tint," from Old French "teindre" meaning "to dye")
