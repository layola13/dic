deny-oneself:/dɪˈnaɪ wʌnˈsɛlf/| v.|克制自己，舍弃；放弃|To refuse oneself something; to abstain from something considered a pleasure or luxury.|She denied herself any treats while on her diet.  她在节食期间戒掉了所有零食。|He denied himself the pleasure of a cigarette. 他克制自己不去抽烟。|近义词：abstain, forgo, sacrifice|反义词：indulge, pamper|动词
