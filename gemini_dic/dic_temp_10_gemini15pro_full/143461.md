outfumbled:/ˌaʊtˈfʌmbəld/
verb
失球；笨拙地处理；由于笨拙而丢失
To fumble something worse than someone else, especially in a sports context, resulting in a loss of possession.  The opposing team outfumbled us, leading to their victory.  对方球队比我们失球更多，导致了他们的胜利。
He outfumbled the receiver, dropping the potential game-winning pass. 他比接球手更笨拙地处理球，丢掉了可能赢得比赛的传球。
past tense of outfumble
笨拙地处理
近义词: mishandled, botched, bungled
反义词: skillfully handled, expertly managed
