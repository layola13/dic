half-ingeniously:/ˌhɑːf ɪnˈdʒiːniəsli/
adverb|半巧妙地，略带几分巧妙地|Combining "half" and "ingeniously," it describes an action or creation that demonstrates some cleverness or originality, but not fully or completely.  It implies that something is clever in a limited or partial way. |He half-ingeniously disguised the box as a stack of books. 他略带技巧地把盒子伪装成一堆书。|Synonyms: partly cleverly, somewhat inventively, slightly resourcefully|Antonyms: clumsily, awkwardly, artlessly|word property
