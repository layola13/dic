ebionitism:/ˌiːbiˈənaɪtɪzəm/| n. |伊便尼派|An early Christian sect that believed Jesus was a human prophet but not divine, emphasizing Jewish law and rejecting Paul's teachings.|Example: Ebionitism was considered a heresy by the early Church fathers. 伊便尼派被早期教会神父视为异端。|Example:  Scholars continue to debate the precise beliefs and practices of Ebionitism. 学者们仍在继续辩论伊便尼派的确切信仰和实践。|近义词:Adoptionism (similar but not identical)|反义词:Trinitarianism|词性:noun
