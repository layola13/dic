newborn:/ˈnuːbɔːrn/ (US) /ˈnjuːbɔːn/ (UK)| adj. n.|新生儿；新生的|A baby that has just been born.  Relating to a baby that has just been born.|例句：The newborn baby slept soundly.  新生儿睡得很香。|近义词：infant, baby, neonate|反义词:adult, grown-up|词性:形容词,名词
Etymology: From Old English *niwegeboren (unattested), from niwe "new" + geboren "born".  Cognate with German neugeboren.
