homodermy:/ˌhɒməˈdɜːmi/ /ˌhoʊməˈdɜːrmi/
|n.|恒温性|The condition of maintaining a stable internal body temperature regardless of the external environment.  Maintaining a constant body temperature, characteristic of mammals and birds.|Birds and mammals exhibit homodermy. 鸟类和哺乳动物表现出恒温性。|近义词:homeothermy, endothermy|反义词:poikilothermy, ectothermy|noun
