homogamy:/həˈmɑːɡəmi/ /hɒˈmɒɡəmi/
n.
同婚，同族通婚
Marriage between people who are similar to each other, especially in terms of socioeconomic status, ethnicity, or religion.
例句：Homogamy is more common in traditional societies. 同族通婚在传统社会中更为普遍。

例句：Studies suggest that homogamy contributes to social stability. 研究表明，同族通婚有助于社会稳定。
近义词：assortative mating, endogamy
反义词：heterogamy, exogamy
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ὁμός (homós, “same”) + γάμος (gámos, “marriage”)
