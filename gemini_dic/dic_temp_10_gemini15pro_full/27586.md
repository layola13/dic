canasta:/kəˈnæstə/ | n. | 卡纳斯塔纸牌游戏；一篮子（尤指水果或蔬菜） | A card game of the rummy family, using two decks of cards. Also, a basket, especially one used for holding fruit or vegetables. |  例句：We played canasta all afternoon. 我们玩了一下午的卡纳斯塔。| 近义词：basket (for the second meaning), rummy (for the first meaning) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

canasta:/kəˈnæstə/ | n. | 一篮子 | A basketful, especially of fruit. | 例句：She brought a canasta of oranges from Florida. 她从佛罗里达带了一篮子橘子。 | 近义词：basketful | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
