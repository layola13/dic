gally:/ˈɡæli/ | n. | 厨房, 狭窄的通道, 船的厨房 | A galley is a kitchen on a ship or aircraft.  It can also refer to a long, narrow kitchen in a house or a long, narrow passage. | The ship's galley was small and cramped. 船上的厨房又小又挤。 | synonyms: kitchen, cookhouse, passageway, corridor | antonyms:  dining room, cabin, stateroom | noun

gally:/ˈɡæli/ | adj. (obsolete, dialectal) | 欢乐的，狂欢的  |  Feeling or expressing great happiness and excitement. |  "They were all in a gally mood after winning the match."  (This usage is outdated and rarely seen.) 他们赢了比赛后都兴高采烈。 | synonyms:  jolly, merry, festive | antonyms:  sad, gloomy, somber | adjective
