dialysis:/daɪˈælɪsɪs/| n.|透析|The process of separating substances from a liquid by selective diffusion through a semipermeable membrane.  This is often used to remove waste products from the blood of patients whose kidneys are not functioning properly.|He is on dialysis three times a week. 他每周透析三次。|Synonyms: hemodialysis, ultrafiltration|Antonyms: N/A|noun

dialyzation:/ˌdaɪəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/| n.|透析|The process of separating substances from a liquid by selective diffusion through a semipermeable membrane.  It is another term for dialysis and is often used interchangeably.|The patient underwent dialyzation after his kidneys failed. 病人的肾衰竭后接受了透析。|Synonyms: dialysis, hemodialysis|Antonyms: N/A|noun
