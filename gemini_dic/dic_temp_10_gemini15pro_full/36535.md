PHRASE->
collaboration with: 与...合作 (Collaboration with local communities is essential for the project's success. 与当地社区的合作对项目的成功至关重要。)
in collaboration with:  与...合作 (The research was conducted in collaboration with several universities. 这项研究是与几所大学合作进行的。)
collaboration between:  ...之间的合作 (The collaboration between the two companies has been very fruitful. 两家公司之间的合作非常富有成效。)
collaborate on:  合作进行 (We are collaborating on a new marketing campaign. 我们正在合作开展一项新的营销活动。)
collaborate to: 合作以... (They collaborated to achieve a common goal. 他们合作实现了共同的目标。)

词源：From French collaboration, from collaborer 'to work together,' from Latin collaborare, from com- 'together' + laborare 'to work.'
