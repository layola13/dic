entrepreneurial:/ˌɑːntrəprəˈnɜːriəl/
adjective
创业的；企业家的
Characterized by or having the spirit of enterprise.
例句：She has a very entrepreneurial spirit. 她很有创业精神。

例句：The company has an entrepreneurial culture.  这家公司有创业文化。

近义词：enterprising, business-minded, innovative, commercial, speculative
反义词：risk-averse, cautious, conservative
词性: adjective
