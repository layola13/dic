noncohesion:/ˌnɒnkəʊˈhiːʒən/ |n.|不凝聚; 不结合; 不粘结; 散漫|Lack of unity or connection between parts; lack of cohesion.  The essay suffered from noncohesion, jumping from topic to topic without clear transitions.|The essay exhibited a marked noncohesion, making it difficult to follow the author's argument.|Synonyms: disunity, incoherence, disconnection, fragmentation|Antonyms: cohesion, unity, coherence, connection|noun
