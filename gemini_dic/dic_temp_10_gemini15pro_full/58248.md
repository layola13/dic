drumes: /druːmz/
n.
drums
鼓，鼓乐器
Drums are percussion instruments that are played by striking them with sticks, mallets, or hands.
鼓是打击乐器，通过用棍子、槌子或手敲击来演奏。
The drummer kept a steady beat throughout the song. 鼓手在整首歌中保持着稳定的节奏。
近义词：percussion instruments
反义词：（无明显的反义词）
词性：名词
