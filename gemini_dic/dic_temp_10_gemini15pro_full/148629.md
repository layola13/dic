overthink:/ˌoʊvərˈθɪŋk/| v. |过度思考；想太多|To think about something too much or for too long, often in a way that is not helpful.|例句：I tend to overthink things and make them worse than they are. 我往往会想太多，把事情弄得比实际情况更糟。

例句：Don't overthink it, just go with your gut. 别想太多，跟着你的直觉走。|近义词：analyze excessively, ruminate, dwell on|反义词：underthink, simplify|词性：动词
