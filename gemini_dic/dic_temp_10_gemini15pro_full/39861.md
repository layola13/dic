constantinople:/ˌkɒnstæntɪˈnəʊpəl/ (US) /ˌkɑːnstæntɪˈnoʊpəl/| n. |君士坦丁堡（现伊斯坦布尔）|A historic city in Turkey, now called Istanbul.  It was the capital of the Byzantine Empire.|例句：Constantinople was once the center of the Roman Empire. 君士坦丁堡曾是罗马帝国的中心。|近义词：Istanbul, Byzantium|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源: From Latin Constantinopolis,  named after Roman Emperor Constantine I.  Ultimately from Greek Konstantinos (Constantine) + polis (city).
