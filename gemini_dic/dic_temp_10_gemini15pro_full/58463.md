ducting:/ˈdʌktɪŋ/ | n. | 管道系统；管道；通风装置 |A system of ducts or pipes used for conveying air, fluids, cables, etc. | The air conditioning system's ducting needs to be cleaned.  空调系统的管道需要清洁。| The building's electrical ducting is complex.  这栋建筑的电线管道很复杂。| synonyms: conduit, channel, pipework | antonyms: blockage, obstruction | noun


ducting:/ˈdʌktɪŋ/ | n. | （雷达波的）大气波导 |An atmospheric layer that refracts electromagnetic waves, allowing them to travel further than normal. | Ducting can affect radar performance. 大气波导会影响雷达性能。 |  The radar signals were enhanced by ducting. 雷达信号因大气波导而增强。 | synonyms: waveguide, atmospheric duct | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "atmospheric interference") | noun
