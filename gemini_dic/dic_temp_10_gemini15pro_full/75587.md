fudgy:/ˈfʌdʒi/| adj.|软糖似的；(像软糖一样)湿软的；浓郁的|Resembling fudge in texture, moist and dense, or having a rich, intense flavor.|The cake was deliciously fudgy and moist. 这蛋糕美味极了，又软又湿。|近义词：chewy, gooey, dense, rich, decadent|反义词：dry, crumbly, cakey|词性：形容词

Etymology:
From *fudge* (n.) + *-y* (suffix forming adjectives).  Fudge itself has uncertain origins, possibly related to earlier words meaning "to cheat" or "to bungle," perhaps referencing the sometimes unpredictable nature of fudge-making.
