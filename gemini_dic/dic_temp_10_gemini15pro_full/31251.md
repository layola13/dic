chanukah: /ˈhɑːnəkə/ (US), /ˈxɑːnəkə/ (UK)| n. |光明节|A Jewish festival, lasting eight days and nights, that commemorates the rededication of the Second Temple in Jerusalem by the Maccabees after its desecration by the Syrians.  It is marked by the successive kindling of eight lights in a special candelabrum (the menorah or hanukkiah).|We celebrate Chanukah by lighting the menorah. 我们通过点燃烛台来庆祝光明节。|My family exchanges gifts during Chanukah. 我的家人在光明节期间交换礼物。|近义词：Festival of Lights, Feast of Dedication|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
