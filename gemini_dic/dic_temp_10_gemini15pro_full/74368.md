frailty:/ˈfreɪlti/| n.|脆弱，虚弱|the condition of being weak and delicate.|例句：Old age is often associated with frailty.  老年往往与体弱联系在一起。

例句：The frailty of the human body is a constant reminder of our mortality. 人体的脆弱不断提醒着我们的死亡。|近义词：weakness, fragility, debility, infirmity|反义词：strength, robustness, resilience|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *freyeltee*, from Old French *frelete* (“frailty, weakness”), from *frele* (“fragile, frail”), from Latin *fragilis* (“fragile”).
