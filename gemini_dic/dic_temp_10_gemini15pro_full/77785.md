generalissima:/ˌdʒɛnərəˈlɪsɪmə/ | n. | 女统帅，女将军 | A woman holding supreme command of a military force, especially historically.  This term is the feminine form of "generalissimo." |  Example:  Queen Elizabeth I was sometimes referred to as the generalissima of England's forces during her reign.  伊丽莎白一世女王在她统治时期有时被称为英国军队的最高统帅。

Example:  The fictional character, Lady Commander, acted as the generalissima in the battle against the alien invasion.  虚构人物女指挥官在外星人入侵的战斗中担任了最高统帅。 | 近义词: commander-in-chief, supreme commander | 反义词: subordinate, private | 词性: noun
