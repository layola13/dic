macroseism:/ˈmækrəʊˌsaɪzəm/ |n.|大地震，远震|A large earthquake, especially one felt over a wide area. |The macroseism caused widespread damage across the region. 大地震对整个地区造成了广泛的破坏。|Synonym: earthquake, tremor |Antonym: microseism| noun

Etymology: From Greek *makros* (large) + *seismos* (shaking).
