gumshoeing:/ˈɡʌmˌʃuːɪŋ/
n.|侦探工作，暗中调查|The act of investigating something discreetly or secretly, like a detective.|He did a bit of gumshoeing to find out who was spreading the rumors. 他做了一些秘密调查，以找出是谁在散布谣言。|sleuthing, investigating, detective work| overt investigation|noun
gumshoe:/ˈɡʌmˌʃuː/
n.|侦探，私人侦探|A detective, especially a private investigator.|The gumshoe followed the suspect for days.  那个侦探跟踪了嫌疑人好几天。|detective, private investigator, sleuth| police officer|noun
