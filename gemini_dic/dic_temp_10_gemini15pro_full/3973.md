aground:/əˈɡraʊnd/
adjective
搁浅的；触礁的
Touching or stuck on the bottom in shallow water.
The ship ran aground on a reef. 这艘船在礁石上搁浅了。

近义词:beached, stranded, grounded
反义词:afloat, floating
词性:adjective
