odyssey: /ˈɒdəsi/ (UK) /ˈɑːdəsi/ (US)| n.|奥德赛，漫长而充满冒险的旅程|A long and eventful or adventurous journey or experience.|例句：His life has been a real odyssey. 他的人生就是一次真正的漫长冒险之旅。

例句：Her career as a dancer was an odyssey of self-discovery. 她作为一名舞蹈演员的职业生涯是一次自我发现的漫长旅程。

近义词：journey, voyage, adventure, quest|反义词:stay, idleness, inactivity|词性:noun

词源: From Late Latin Odyssea, from Greek Odysseia, the title of Homer's epic poem about Odysseus's journey home after the Trojan War.
