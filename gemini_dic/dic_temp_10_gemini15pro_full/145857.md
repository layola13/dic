overdepressiveness:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈprɛsɪvnɪs/
noun
过度抑郁，过度的沮丧
Excessive sadness or depression.
Her overdepressiveness made it difficult for her to function normally. 她过度的抑郁让她难以正常生活。
近义词: excessive sadness, extreme depression, melancholy, despondency
反义词: cheerfulness, happiness, elation, joy
词性: noun

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "depressive" (adjective meaning "sad or gloomy") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin "de-" (down) + "premere" (to press).
