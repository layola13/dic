blessingly:/ˈblesɪŋli/| adv.|幸运地，幸福地，值得庆幸地|Something that happens blessedly is fortunate and makes you feel grateful.  It often expresses relief or gratitude for a positive outcome.|例句：Blessedly, the rain stopped just before the picnic.  幸运的是，雨在野餐前停了。|近义词：fortunately, luckily, thankfully|反义词：unfortunately, unluckily, sadly|词性:副词
blessing:/ˈblesɪŋ/| n.|祝福，恩赐，幸事|Something that is good or helpful; something that you are grateful for.|例句：It was a blessing that we had travel insurance. 我们有旅行保险真是太好了。|近义词：boon, gift, advantage|反义词：curse, misfortune, affliction|词性:名词
