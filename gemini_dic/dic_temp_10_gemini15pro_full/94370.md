implanted:/ɪmˈplæntɪd/
Verb|植入的，嵌入的|Inserted or fixed firmly, especially by surgery.|The device was implanted just below the skin.|近义词:embedded, inserted, ingrained|反义词:removed, extracted|v.

implanted:/ɪmˈplæntɪd/
Adjective|植入的，嵌入的|Inserted or fixed firmly, especially by surgery.|The implanted device monitors heart rate.|近义词:embedded, inserted, ingrained|反义词:removed, extracted|adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "implantare" (to plant in), from "in-" (in) + "plantare" (to plant).
