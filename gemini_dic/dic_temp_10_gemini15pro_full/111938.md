lollers:/ˈlɒləz/ (UK) /ˈlɑːlərz/ (US)| n. |懒汉，游手好闲的人；罗拉德派教徒|A person who lolls about or idles; a follower of Lollardism (a religious movement in 14th-century England).|The lollers spent their days lounging in the park. 这些懒汉整天在公园里闲逛。|近义词：idlers, loafers, loungers|反义词: workers, strivers|Noun
