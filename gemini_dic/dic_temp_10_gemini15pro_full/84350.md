halfstocking:/ˈhæfˌstɑːkɪŋ/ | n. | 半截袜，短袜 | A stocking that reaches only to the calf or just below the knee.  A sock that reaches halfway up the calf. | She wore halfstockings and a skirt. 她穿着半截袜和一条裙子。 | The school uniform required halfstockings.  学校制服要求穿半截袜。 | 近义词: sock, ankle sock, knee sock | 反义词: stocking, thigh-highs | 词性: 名词
