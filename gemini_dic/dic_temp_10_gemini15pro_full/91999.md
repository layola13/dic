hyperoartia:/ˌhaɪpəroʊˈɑːrtiə/ | n. |七鳃鳗纲|A class of jawless fish, also known as lampreys, characterized by a toothed, funnel-like sucking mouth.| Lampreys, which belong to the class Hyperoartia, are considered primitive vertebrates. 七鳃鳗属于七鳃鳗纲，被认为是原始脊椎动物。|近义词: Petromyzontida (sometimes used interchangeably)|反义词: None (as it is a taxonomic classification)|词性: Noun
