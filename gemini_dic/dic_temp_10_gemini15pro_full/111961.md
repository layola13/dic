lombardeer:/ˌlɒmbɑːˈdɪə(r)/| n.| 贷方；典当商；放高利贷者|A person who lends money at high rates of interest, especially one who accepts personal goods as security.|He took his watch to the lombard. 他把手表拿去当了。|Synonyms: pawnbroker, moneylender, usurer|Antonyms: borrower, debtor|noun
