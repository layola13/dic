dartoid:/ˈdɑːrtɔɪd/
noun
镖形，筝形，菱形
A convex quadrilateral having two pairs of adjacent equal sides.  A kite shape.
The shape of the playground was a dartoid.  操场的形状是菱形的。
synonyms: kite, rhomb, diamond
antonyms: N/A
