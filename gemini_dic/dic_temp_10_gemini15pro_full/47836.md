decayable:/dɪˈkeɪəbl/
adjective
可腐烂的，易腐败的
Susceptible to decay; capable of rotting or decomposing.
Fruits and vegetables are decayable goods. 水果和蔬菜是易腐烂的商品。
The fallen leaves are decayable and will eventually become compost. 落叶是可腐烂的，最终会变成肥料。
近义词:perishable, decomposable, biodegradable, putrescible
反义词:imperishable, non-degradable, indestructible
词性:adjective
词源:From decay + -able.  "decay" comes from Old French "decair" meaning "to fall, decline, fail, rot".
