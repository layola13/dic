clears:/klɪərz/| v. | 清除，放晴，跳过，结算，变清晰 | Clears the table after dinner. 晚饭后清理桌子。| 近义词：cleans, removes, clarifies, brightens | 反义词：obscures, clutters, muddies | verb (third-person singular present)

clears:/klɪərz/| n. | 空旷地，净地 |  The deer emerged into the clears. 鹿出现在空地上。 | 近义词：glades, openings, spaces | 反义词：forests, thickets, woods | noun (plural)

词源: From Middle English *cleren, from Old French cler (Modern French clair), from Latin clārus (“clear, bright, distinct”).
