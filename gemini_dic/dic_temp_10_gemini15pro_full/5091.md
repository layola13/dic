allay:/əˈleɪ/
verb
减轻，缓和
to reduce the intensity of something unpleasant, such as fear, pain, or worry
例句：The government has taken measures to allay public fears about the spread of the disease. 政府已采取措施来减轻公众对疾病传播的恐惧。

例句：A hot bath can allay muscle soreness.  热水澡可以缓解肌肉酸痛。

近义词：reduce, diminish, alleviate, ease, soothe, mitigate, assuage
反义词：aggravate, exacerbate, intensify, worsen
词性：v.
