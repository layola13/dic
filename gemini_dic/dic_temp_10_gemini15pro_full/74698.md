freeholders:/ˈfriːhoʊldərz/
n.
自由地持有人；永久业主
A freeholder is someone who owns a freehold property.
例句：The freeholders of the building voted to renovate the lobby.  大楼的永久业主投票决定翻新大厅。

例句：Freeholders have certain rights and responsibilities. 永久业主有一定的权利和责任。
近义词：landowner, property owner
反义词：tenant, lessee
词性：noun
