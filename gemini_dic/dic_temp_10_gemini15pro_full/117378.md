mediators:/ˈmiːdieɪtərz/| n.|调解员，调停人，斡旋者|A person who attempts to make people involved in a conflict come to an agreement; a go-between.|The mediators worked tirelessly to bring the two sides together. 调解员不知疲倦地工作，以使双方走到一起。|The UN sent mediators to the war-torn region. 联合国向饱受战争蹂躏的地区派遣了调解员。|synonyms: arbitrators, negotiators, conciliators, intermediaries|antonyms: agitators, antagonists|noun
