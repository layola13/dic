chastise:/ˈtʃæstaɪz/| v. |责罚，惩罚，严厉批评|to rebuke or reprimand severely|例句：The teacher chastised the student for being late. 老师责备学生迟到。|近义词：reprimand, scold, rebuke, punish, discipline|反义词:praise, commend, reward|词性:动词
Etymology: From Old French chastier, from Latin castigāre "to correct, punish," from castus "pure" + agere "to drive, lead, do."
