multifariousness:/ˌmʌltɪˈfɛəriəsnəs/ | n. | 多样性；多方面性 | The state of having many varied parts or aspects. | The multifariousness of the city's cultural offerings is truly impressive.  这座城市文化产品的多样性确实令人印象深刻。 | synonyms: diversity, variety, multiplicity, heterogeneity | antonyms: uniformity, homogeneity, sameness, monotony | noun

multifarious:/ˌmʌltɪˈfɛəriəs/ | adj. | 多样的；多方面的 | Having many varied parts or aspects. | The museum houses a multifarious collection of artifacts from around the world.  这家博物馆收藏了来自世界各地的各种各样的文物。 | synonyms: diverse, varied, multiple, heterogeneous | antonyms: uniform, homogeneous, same, monotonous | adjective
