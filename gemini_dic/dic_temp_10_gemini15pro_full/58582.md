dulcimers:/ˈdʌlsɪmərz/
n.
扬琴（复数）
A stringed instrument, trapezoidal in shape, played with hammers held in both hands.
The dulcimers were beautifully crafted and produced a rich, resonant sound. 扬琴制作精美，音色丰富而洪亮。
