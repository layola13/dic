overdoubted:/ˌoʊvərˈdaʊtɪd/ | adj. | 过于怀疑的，被过度怀疑的 | Excessively or unduly doubted; doubted more than is warranted or deserved.  It describes something or someone that has been subjected to an excessive amount of doubt or skepticism. |  His abilities were overdoubted by his colleagues, who later regretted their lack of faith. 他的同事们过度怀疑他的能力，后来他们后悔自己缺乏信心。|  The authenticity of the painting was overdoubted by experts, but it turned out to be genuine.  这幅画的真伪受到了专家们的过度质疑，但事实证明它是真迹。 | 近义词:  distrusted, questioned, suspected,  | 反义词: trusted, believed, accepted | 词性: 形容词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively, too much") + "doubted" (past participle of the verb "doubt")
