ignominiously:/ˌɪɡnəˈmɪniəsli/
adverb
耻辱地，不光彩地
in a way that causes a great loss of respect; shamefully or disgracefully.
He was ignominiously defeated in the first round. 他在第一轮比赛中惨败。
ignominiously:/ˌɪɡnəˈmɪniəsli/
adverb
耻辱地
marked with or characterized by disgrace or shame.
She was ignominiously fired after the scandal.  丑闻曝光后，她被耻辱地解雇了。

ignominiously:/ˌɪɡnəˈmɪniəsli/
adverb
不光彩地
Deserving or causing public disgrace or shame.
The team lost ignominiously, giving up a ten-point lead in the final minutes. 该队以不光彩的方式输掉了比赛，在最后几分钟放弃了10分的领先优势。
近义词:shamefully, disgracefully, humiliatingly, dishonorably
反义词:honorably, gloriously, triumphantly
