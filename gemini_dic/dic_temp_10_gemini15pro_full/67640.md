extricating:/ˈɛkstrɪkeɪtɪŋ/
verb
解救，使摆脱困境
Freeing something (or someone) from a constraint or difficult situation.
The firefighters worked tirelessly extricating the survivors from the wreckage. 消防队员不知疲倦地工作，将幸存者从残骸中解救出来。

The hiker extricated himself from the thorny bush.  徒步旅行者将自己从荆棘丛中解救出来。

近义词: freeing, rescuing, liberating, disentangling, removing
反义词: entangling, trapping, snaring, involving


词性: v.
词源: From Latin extricare "to disentangle," from ex- "out" + tricae "perplexities, hindrances."
