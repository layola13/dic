deactivates:/diːˈæktɪveɪts/
verb
使失效，使不活动，停用
Makes something inactive or no longer in use.
The administrator deactivates user accounts after a period of inactivity.  管理员在一段时间不活动后停用用户帐户。
The company deactivates old credit cards after they expire. 公司在旧信用卡过期后将其停用。
neutralizes, disables, shuts down
activates, enables, starts
