cobbling:/ˈkɒblɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːblɪŋ/| n. v.|修补, 粗劣的制作, 拼凑|The act of mending shoes or other items, often in a rough or makeshift way.  Also, the creation of something hastily or clumsily.|例句：The cobbling of the old shoes extended their life by a few months.  修补旧鞋使它们的寿命延长了几个月。

例句：His essay was a cobbling together of various sources, lacking originality.  他的文章是拼凑各种资料而成的，缺乏 originality。|近义词：mending, repairing, patching, improvising, botching|反义词: crafting, constructing, building|词性:noun, verb (present participle)


cobble:/ˈkɒbl/ /ˈkɑːbl/| v. n.|修补, 鹅卵石|To mend or repair (something, especially shoes). Also, a rounded stone used for paving.|例句：He cobbled his shoes together with some strong thread. 他用一些结实的线把他的鞋子缝在一起。

例句：The streets were paved with cobblestones. 街道是用鹅卵石铺成的。|近义词：mend, repair, patch, fix|反义词: break, damage, destroy|词性:verb, noun
