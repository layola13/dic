nonfisherman: /ˌnɒnˈfɪʃəmən/ | n. | 非钓鱼者；不钓鱼的人 | A person who does not fish. |  He's a nonfisherman, so he didn't understand our excitement about the big catch. 他不是钓鱼的人，所以他不理解我们对这条大鱼的兴奋。| Near Synonyms: non-angler | Antonyms: fisherman, angler | Noun
