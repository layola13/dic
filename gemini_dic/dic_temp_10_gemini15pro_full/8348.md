anti-poetical:/ˌænti-pəˈetɪkl/
adjective
反诗意的；缺乏诗意的
Lacking poetic beauty or qualities; prosaic.
His anti-poetical language made the love poem sound awkward. 他那缺乏诗意的语言使得这首爱情诗听起来很别扭。

The report, filled with technical jargon, was decidedly anti-poetical. 这份报告充满了技术术语，显然毫无诗意。

近义词:prosaic, unpoetic, mundane, unimaginative
反义词:poetic, lyrical, imaginative, romantic
词性:adjective
