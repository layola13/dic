integrator:/ˈɪntɪɡreɪtər/| n.|积分器；集成者；整合者|A device or person that integrates.  Something that combines parts into a whole.|例句：The integrator calculated the area under the curve. 积分器计算了曲线下的面积。|例句：He acted as an integrator, bringing together different departments to work on the project. 他充当了整合者的角色，将不同的部门聚集在一起进行项目合作。|近义词：combiner, unifier, synthesizer|反义词:separator, divider|noun
