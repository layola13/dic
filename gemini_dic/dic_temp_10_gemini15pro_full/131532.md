noncommemorations: /ˌnɒnkəˌmɛməˈreɪʃənz/
noun
非纪念；不纪念
The act of not commemorating something; the absence of commemorations.
The museum's exhibition on noncommemorations explored the events and figures history often overlooks.  这家博物馆关于非纪念的展览探索了历史上经常被忽视的事件和人物。
Their policy of noncommemoration of certain historical figures has drawn criticism. 他们不纪念某些历史人物的政策引来了批评。
Near Antonyms: commemorations, celebrations, remembrances
Antonyms: commemorations
