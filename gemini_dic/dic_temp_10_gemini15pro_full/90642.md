humanized:/ˈhjuːmənaɪzd/| adj.|人性化的；人文化的|Made more humane or human; given a human character or form.  Rendering something more appealing or accessible to humans.|The company developed a humanized version of the software, making it easier for non-technical users to navigate. 公司开发了一个人性化的软件版本，让非技术用户更容易操作。|Synonyms: humane, compassionate, sympathetic, empathetic, understanding|Antonyms: inhumane, cruel, unsympathetic, impersonal, cold|adjective
