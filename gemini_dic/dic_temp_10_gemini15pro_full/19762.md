billiards:/ˈbɪljərdz/ | n. | 台球；撞球 | A game played on a rectangular table covered with green cloth, in which two people use cues to hit balls against each other and into pockets around the edge of the table. |  We played a game of billiards after dinner. 我们晚饭后打了一场台球。

Billiards is a challenging game requiring skill and strategy. 台球是一项需要技巧和策略的挑战性游戏。


| 近义词: pool, snooker | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a game) | 词性: noun
