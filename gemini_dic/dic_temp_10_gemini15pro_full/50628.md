derestricts:/ˌdiːrɪˈstrɪkts/| v. |解除限制，取消限制|To remove restrictions or limitations imposed on something or someone.| The government derestricted the sale of certain medications. 政府解除了某些药物的销售限制。| The new regulations derestricted building heights in the city center. 新规定解除了市中心建筑高度的限制。|  Synonyms: liberalize, deregulate, free, unfetter | Antonyms: restrict, limit, constrain, regulate | verb
