oblongness:/ˌɒbˈlɒŋnəs/ /ˌɑːbˈlɔːŋnəs/
n.
 oblong的性质或状态；椭圆形
The state or quality of being oblong; an oblong shape.
The oblongness of the table made it difficult to fit in the small room. 桌子的椭圆形状使得它难以放进小房间。
elongation, ovalness, lengthened shape
roundness, squareness
noun
