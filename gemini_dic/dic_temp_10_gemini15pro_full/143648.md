outking:/ˈaʊtkɪŋ/
verb
胜过；超越（在国王或统治者方面）
To surpass or defeat someone in a kingly or ruler-like manner.
King A outkinged King B in terms of wisdom and diplomacy.  A国王在智慧和外交方面胜过B国王。
The young prince outkinged his rivals in the tournament.  年轻的王子在锦标赛中胜过了他的对手。
近义词:outdo, surpass, excel, defeat, triumph over
反义词:lose to, be defeated by
词性:verb

outking:/ˈaʊtkɪŋ/
noun
胜过者;超越者
One who surpasses or defeats another, especially in a position of authority or leadership.
He proved himself the true outking of the competition. 他证明了自己是这场比赛真正的胜者。
She emerged as the outking in the political arena.  她在政治舞台上脱颖而出，成为胜者。
近义词:victor, champion, conqueror
反义词:loser, underdog
词性:noun
