bisulphite:/baɪˈsʌlfaɪt/| n. |亚硫酸氢盐|A salt containing the HSO3− anion.  It is formed by the reaction of sulfur dioxide with a base in water.|Sodium bisulphite is used as a food preservative. 亚硫酸氢钠用作食品防腐剂。|synonyms: hydrogen sulfite|antonyms: None|noun


bisulfite:/baɪˈsʌlfaɪt/| n. |亚硫酸氢盐|A salt containing the HSO3− anion.  It is formed by the reaction of sulfur dioxide with a base in water.|Potassium bisulfite is often used in winemaking. 亚硫酸氢钾常用于葡萄酒酿造。|synonyms: hydrogen sulfite|antonyms: None|noun
