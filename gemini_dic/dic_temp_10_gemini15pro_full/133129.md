nonesterified:/nɒnˈɛstərɪˌfaɪd/ | adj. | 非酯化的 | Not converted into an ester.  Specifically referring to fatty acids that are not bound to glycerol, thus circulating freely in the blood. |  High levels of nonesterified fatty acids are associated with insulin resistance.  高水平的非酯化脂肪酸与胰岛素抵抗有关。 | unbound, free, unconjugated | esterified, bound, conjugated | adjective
