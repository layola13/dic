otaheite:/ˌoʊtəˈhiːtiː/ /ˌɔːtəˈhiːtiː/
noun
大溪地岛 (旧称)
A former name for Tahiti, the largest island in French Polynesia.
Captain Cook called the island Otaheite in his journals.  库克船长在他的航海日志中称这个岛为奥塔黑特岛。
The sailors brought back exotic tales from Otaheite. 水手们从大溪地带回了奇异的故事。
Synonym: Tahiti
Antonym: N/A
词性: 名词
