crevice: /ˈkrɛvɪs/ (US), /ˈkrɛvɪs/ (UK) | n. | 裂缝，缝隙 | A narrow opening or fissure, especially in a rock or wall. |  The climbers found a handhold in a crevice in the rock face. 攀岩者在岩壁的裂缝中找到了一个可以抓住的地方。

The cold wind whistled through the crevices in the old building.  冷风呼啸着穿过老建筑的缝隙。

近义词: crack, fissure, gap, chasm, cleft
反义词: closure, seal
词性: noun
