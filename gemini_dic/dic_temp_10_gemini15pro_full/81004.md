gradient:/ˈɡreɪdiənt/| n.|梯度，坡度，倾斜度|a rate of inclination; a slope.|The cyclist struggled up the steep gradient. 骑自行车的人艰难地爬上陡峭的斜坡。|Synonyms: slope, incline, grade, slant|Antonyms: level, flat|noun

gradient:/ˈɡreɪdiənts/| n.|梯度（复数）|slopes, inclines, grades|The hikers encountered varying gradients on their climb.  徒步旅行者在攀登过程中遇到了不同的坡度。|Synonyms: slopes, inclines, grades|Antonyms: levels, flats|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Latin "gradiens," present participle of "gradi" (to walk, step), related to "gradus" (a step, degree).
