haematocrystallin:/ˌhiːmətəˈkrɪstəlɪn/ | n. | 血红蛋白 |  A crystalline protein containing iron, found in red blood cells and responsible for transporting oxygen in the blood. It's essentially the same as hemoglobin. | Hemoglobin, or haematocrystallin, is essential for life. 血红蛋白，或者说血红晶体，对生命至关重要。| synonym: hemoglobin | antonym: N/A | noun


haemocrystalline:/ˌhiːmətəˈkrɪstəlɪn/ | adj. | 血红蛋白的; 血红素的 |  Of or relating to haematocrystallin/hemoglobin. | The haemocrystalline structure is complex. 血红蛋白的结构很复杂。 | synonym: hemoglobinic | antonym: N/A | adjective
