crocketing: /ˈkrɒkɪtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkrɑːkɪtɪŋ/ (US)| n.|  垂饰，（哥特式建筑中）尖拱或角形装饰 |A series of small, projecting ornaments, usually carved in the shape of curled leaves or buds, placed along the sloping edge of a gable, spire, or other architectural member, especially in Gothic architecture.  | The elaborate crocketing on the cathedral's spires was breathtaking.  大教堂尖顶上精美的垂饰令人叹为观止。| Synonyms: ornamentation, decoration, embellishment | Antonyms: plainness, simplicity | Noun
