behemoth:/bɪˈhiːməθ/ /biˈhiːmɒθ/| n. |巨兽，庞然大物|Something enormous in size or power.  A huge creature.|例句：The company is a behemoth in the tech industry.  这家公司是科技行业的巨头。|例句：The new aircraft carrier is a true behemoth of the seas. 这艘新的航空母舰是真正的海上巨兽。|近义词：giant, monster, leviathan|反义词:dwarf, midget|词性:noun

Etymology: From late Middle English: from biblical Hebrew *bəhēmōṯ, perhaps a plural intensive form of bəhēmāh ‘beast’.
