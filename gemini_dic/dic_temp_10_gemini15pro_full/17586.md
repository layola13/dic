beautied:/ˈbjuːtɪfaɪd/| v. |美化；使变美|To make something beautiful or more beautiful. It's the past tense and past participle of the verb "beautify".|例句：They beautified the garden with flowers and lights. 他们用鲜花和彩灯美化了花园。

例句：The artist beautified the city with murals.  这位艺术家用壁画美化了城市。

近义词：adorned, embellished, decorated, enhanced|反义词:uglified, marred, spoiled|词性:动词
