lms:/ɛl ɛm ɛs/ |abbr.|学习管理系统 (Learning Management System)|A software application for the administration, documentation, tracking, reporting, automation and delivery of educational courses, training programs, or learning and development programs. |Example: Our university uses a new LMS to deliver online courses.  我们大学使用一个新的学习管理系统来提供在线课程。|Synonym:  e-learning platform, virtual learning environment (VLE) |Antonym:  traditional classroom|abbreviation
