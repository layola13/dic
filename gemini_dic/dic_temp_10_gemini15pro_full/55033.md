disquietedness:/dɪsˈkwaɪɪtɪdnəs/
n.
不安；焦虑；担忧
A state of unease or anxiety.
The disquietedness she felt about the upcoming exam was evident in her fidgeting. 她对即将到来的考试感到不安，这在她坐立不安的样子中很明显。
The news of the impending storm filled everyone with a sense of disquietedness. 即将到来的暴风雨的消息使每个人都感到不安。
anxiety, uneasiness, worry, apprehension, perturbation
calmness, composure, tranquility, serenity, peace
noun
