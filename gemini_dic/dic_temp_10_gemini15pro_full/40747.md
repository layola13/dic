conveyor:/kənˈveɪər/| n. |输送机，传送带|A mechanical apparatus that transports materials from one place to another.|The conveyor belt moved the luggage from the plane to the baggage claim. 输送带将行李从飞机运送到行李提取处。|synonyms: transporter, carrier, belt conveyor|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "manual handling" or "static storage")|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *conveiour, *conveyour, from Anglo-Norman conveiour, conveour, from conveier, based on Latin convēhere (“to carry or bring together”), from con- (“together, with”) + vēhere (“to carry”).
