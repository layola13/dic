bug-eyed:/ˈbʌɡ.aɪd/| adj.|瞪大眼睛的，吃惊的|Having large, prominent, and staring eyes, often suggesting surprise, fear, or excitement.|She gave him a bug-eyed look of disbelief. 她瞪大了眼睛，露出难以置信的表情。|近义词：wide-eyed, goggle-eyed, astonished, surprised|反义词:calm, relaxed|词性:形容词

bug-eyed:/ˈbʌɡ.aɪd/| adj.|凸眼的|Having eyes that bulge or protrude.|The bug-eyed frog stared at the fly. 凸眼的青蛙盯着苍蝇。|近义词：protruding, bulging|反义词:deep-set|词性:形容词
