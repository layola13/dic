lemony:/ˈlɛməni/| adj.|柠檬味的，柠檬色的|Resembling a lemon in taste or smell; tart.  Having the pale yellow color of a lemon.|The cake had a refreshingly lemony flavor.  蛋糕有一种清爽的柠檬味。|近义词：citrusy, tart, tangy|反义词:sweet, bland|词性:形容词
Etymology: From lemon + -y.  "Lemon" comes from the Old French "limon," ultimately from the Arabic "laymūn."
