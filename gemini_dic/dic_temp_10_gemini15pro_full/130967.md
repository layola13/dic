nonassumption:/ˌnɒnəˈsʌmpʃən/ | n. | 非假设，不臆断 | The principle of nonassumption is crucial in scientific research.  |  Example 1:  Her approach to the problem was based on nonassumption, meticulously gathering data before forming any conclusions. 她处理这个问题的方法基于不臆断，在得出任何结论之前仔细地收集数据。

Example 2: The detective operated on the nonassumption that everyone was a suspect until proven otherwise.  侦探的行动基于不臆断的原则，即在证明无罪之前，每个人都是嫌疑人。 |  Near Synonyms:  objectivity, neutrality, open-mindedness | Antonyms:  prejudice, bias, preconception | noun
