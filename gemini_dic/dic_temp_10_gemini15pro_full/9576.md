antismuggling:/ˌæntiˈsmʌɡlɪŋ/
adjective
反走私的
Preventing or opposing the illegal transportation of goods into or out of a country.
The government has implemented new antismuggling measures. 政府已经实施了新的反走私措施。
synonyms:  anticontraband
antonyms: smuggling, contraband

Etymology:
anti- (prefix) "against, opposed to" + smuggling (noun) "the illegal transportation of goods"
