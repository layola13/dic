hubbed:/hʌbd/
adjective|有轮毂的，中心式的|Having a hub or hubs; designed around a central hub.|The airline uses a hubbed network, routing most flights through a central airport.|近义词:central, centralized, focal|反义词:peripheral, decentralized|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From hub + -ed.  "Hub" originally referred to the central part of a wheel, and later extended to mean a center of activity. The "-ed" suffix transforms the noun "hub" into an adjective, describing something that possesses or is characterized by a hub.
