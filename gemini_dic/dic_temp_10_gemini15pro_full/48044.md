deckhand:/ˈdɛkˌhænd/| n. |甲板水手；水手|A sailor who performs manual labor on the deck of a ship. |The deckhands were busy securing the ropes before the storm. 甲板水手们在暴风雨来临前忙着固定绳索。|Synonyms: sailor, seaman, crewman, mariner|Antonyms: captain, officer|noun

deckhand:/ˈdɛkˌhænd/| n. |船员；船工|A member of a ship's crew who works on the deck. |The deckhands hoisted the sails. 船员们升起了帆。|Synonyms: crew member, shipmate|Antonyms: passenger, landlubber|noun
