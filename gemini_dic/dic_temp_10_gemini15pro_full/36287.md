coheirship:/ˌkəʊˈɛəʃɪp/ | n. |共同继承权，共同继承人的身份 |The state or right of being a coheir; joint inheritance.  The legal status of those who inherit property together. |  Example: The siblings established coheirship of their parents' estate. 兄弟姐妹们确立了他们对父母遗产的共同继承权。

Example:  The coheirship agreement prevented future disputes over the property. 共同继承协议防止了未来对财产的争议。|Synonym: joint inheritance, co-inheritance | Antonym: sole inheritance, individual inheritance | noun
