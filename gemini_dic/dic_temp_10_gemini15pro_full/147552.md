overpenalize: /ˌoʊvərˈpiːnəlaɪz/ | vt. | 过度惩罚，处罚过重 | To penalize someone too harshly or excessively. | The referee was accused of overpenalizing the visiting team.  裁判被指控对客队处罚过重。 |  The judge overpenalized the defendant for a minor offense. 法官对被告轻微的违法行为处罚过重。 | 近义词: overpunish, penalize excessively | 反义词: underpunish | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above, beyond") + "penalize" (from Late Latin penalis "relating to punishment," from Latin poena "punishment, penalty")
