impactites:/ˈɪmpæktaɪts/
noun
冲击岩
Rocks formed by the impact of a meteorite or comet. These rocks can be formed from the impactor, the target rocks, or a mixture of both.
The researcher discovered impactites near the crater.  研究人员在陨石坑附近发现了冲击岩。
Synonyms: impact-generated rocks, shock-metamorphosed rocks
Antonyms: sedimentary rocks, igneous rocks (not formed by impact)
