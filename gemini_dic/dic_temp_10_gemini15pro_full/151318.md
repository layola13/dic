parish-pump:/ˈpærɪʃ pʌmp/ | adj. | 狭隘的；地方性的；只关心琐碎小事的 | Relating to local, small-scale politics and issues, often considered unimportant or petty. |  The mayor's speech was full of parish-pump politics, focusing on potholes and parking permits rather than larger issues. 市长的演讲充满了狭隘的地方政治，只关注道路坑洼和停车许可，而不是更大的问题。

The newspaper is full of parish-pump gossip. 这份报纸上充斥着地方琐碎的八卦。| 近义词: parochial, local, small-town, petty | 反义词: national, international, global, broad-minded | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  The term originates from the literal parish pump, a communal water source in a parish (a small administrative district, typically associated with a church).  Discussions around the pump often revolved around local, minor concerns.
