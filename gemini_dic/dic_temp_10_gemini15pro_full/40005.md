consulted:/kənˈsʌltɪd/ | v. | 咨询，商量，查阅 |  seek information or advice from (someone with expertise in a particular area). | I consulted my doctor about the pain in my back. 我咨询了我的医生关于我背痛的问题。 |  She consulted a dictionary to check the spelling. 她查阅了字典以确认拼写。 | synonyms: conferred, deliberated, referred, checked | antonyms: ignored, disregarded | verb
