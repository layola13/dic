inharmonious:/ˌɪnhɑːrˈmoʊniəs/ /ˌɪnhɑːˈməʊniəs/| adj. |不和谐的，不协调的|Lacking harmony; discordant.|The inharmonious combination of colors clashed and created a jarring effect.  不和谐的色彩组合冲突并产生了刺耳的效果。|The band sounded inharmonious during rehearsal, as if they hadn't practiced together.  乐队在排练时听起来不和谐，好像他们没有一起练习过。|近义词:discordant, dissonant, jarring, clashing, conflicting, incompatible, incongruous|反义词:harmonious, consonant, agreeable, concordant, compatible, congruous|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + harmonious (harmonious), from Latin harmonia (harmony), from Greek harmonia (harmony).
