barques:/bɑːrks/| n. |三桅帆船，大型帆船 |A sailing ship with three or more masts, all of which are square-rigged except the after mast, which is fore-and-aft rigged.  |The barque sailed gracefully across the bay. 三桅帆船优雅地航行穿过海湾。|
barques:/bɑːrks/| n. |(诗歌用语) 船 |(poetic) A boat or ship. |The lonely barque drifted on the open sea. 孤独的帆船漂流在茫茫大海上。|近义词:ship, vessel, sailing ship|反义词:|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *barque* (attested only in the diminutive *barquette*), from Italian *barca*, from Late Latin *barca*, of uncertain origin, perhaps from Vulgar Latin *barica*, from Greek *βάρις* (*baris*) "Egyptian boat".
