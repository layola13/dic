coopered:/ˈkuːpərd/
adjective
修补过的；箍桶的
repaired or made tight (as a cask) by a cooper; resembling the work of a cooper
The coopered barrel held the wine perfectly. 修补过的酒桶完美地盛放了葡萄酒。
近义词: mended, repaired, fixed
反义词: broken, damaged, leaky
词性: adjective

coopered:/ˈkuːpərd/
verb (past tense)
箍桶
To make or repair barrels or casks.
The old barrels were coopered and used again.  旧桶被箍好后又重新使用了。
近义词: barrel-make, cask-make
反义词: break, dismantle
词性: verb
