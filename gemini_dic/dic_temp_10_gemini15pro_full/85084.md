hardback:/ˈhɑːrdbæk/ (US) /ˈhɑːdbæk/ (UK)| n. adj.|精装书，精装的|A book bound with stiff covers.|例句：I prefer to buy hardback books because they last longer. 我更喜欢买精装书，因为它们更耐用。

例句：The hardback edition is more expensive than the paperback. 精装版比平装版贵。|近义词：hardcover, case-bound|反义词：paperback, softcover, softback|词性: 名词, 形容词

词源:  "hard" + "back" -  The term is a straightforward compound of "hard" referring to the rigid covers and "back" referring to the spine and covering of a book.
