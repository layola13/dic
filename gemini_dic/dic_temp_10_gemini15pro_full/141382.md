operculated: /ˈɒpəkjʊleɪtɪd/ /ˈɑːpərkyəleɪtɪd/
adjective
有盖的；有厣的
Having an operculum, a lid or covering like a trapdoor, especially in biology.  This refers to a structure that closes or covers an opening, such as the gill cover of a fish or the lid of a moss capsule.
The operculated snail retreated into its shell.  这只带盖的蜗牛缩回了壳里。
The operculated eggs of the fish were safe from predators.  这种鱼的有盖卵可以免受捕食者的侵害。
Synonyms: covered, lidded, capped
Antonyms: open, uncovered, exposed
词性: adjective
