nuciform:/ˈnjuːsɪfɔːrm/| adj.|核形的，似核的|Resembling a nut in shape.  Having the shape of a nucleus.|The bacteria formed small, nuciform colonies. 这些细菌形成了小的，核形的菌落。|Near synonyms:  nut-shaped, nucleus-shaped|Antonym: amorphous|adjective
