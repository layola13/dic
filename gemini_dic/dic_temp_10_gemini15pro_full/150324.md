pangu: /ˈpænɡuː/ | n. | 盘古 | In Chinese mythology, Pangu is the first living being and the creator of the universe. | Example: Pangu separated the sky from the earth. 盘古开天辟地。| Synonym: creator, originator | Antonym: destroyer | Noun

pangu: /ˈpænɡuː/ | adj. | 盘古的; 巨大的，开天辟地的 | Relating to or characteristic of Pangu, the creator of the universe in Chinese mythology. Can also be used figuratively to describe something vast or groundbreaking.  | Example: The pangu achievement of building the dam benefited generations. 建造大坝的盘古成就造福了几代人。| Synonym: immense, colossal, groundbreaking | Antonym: small, insignificant | Adjective
