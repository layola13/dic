half-sighted:/ˌhɑːf ˈsaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 半盲的；视力不好的 | Having poor eyesight; not able to see clearly.  |  He's half-sighted and has to wear thick glasses. 他半盲，必须戴厚眼镜。 |  She uses a magnifying glass because she's half-sighted. 她因为视力不好而使用放大镜。 | nearsighted, visually impaired, partially sighted | farsighted, keen-sighted, sharp-sighted | adjective
