differers:/ˈdɪfərərz/
| n. |相异者；持不同意见者|People who differ, or hold different opinions or beliefs, are differers.  Those who disagree or vary are also considered differers.|例句：The differers in the group presented compelling arguments for their perspectives.  小组中持不同意见的人就他们的观点提出了令人信服的论据。|近义词：dissidents, dissenters, nonconformists|反义词：conformists, adherents|词性:名词

Etymology:
From the verb "differ" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Differ" comes from Old French "diferer"  and ultimately from Latin "differre" (to carry apart, to postpone, to differ).
