interdependabilities:/ˌɪntərˌdɪpɛndəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |相互依赖性；互相依存关系|The complex interdependabilities within the ecosystem are fascinating. 生态系统内部复杂的相互依存关系令人着迷。| The global economy is marked by the increasing interdependabilities of nations. 全球经济的特点是各国之间日益增长的相互依赖性。|近义词：interdependencies, interconnections, correlations|反义词：independencies, autonomies|词性:名词

词源:  From "interdependent" + "-ability" + "-ies".  "Inter-" (Latin) means "between" or "among". "Dependent" comes from the Latin "dependere" meaning "to hang down from" or "to rely on". "-ability" forms nouns meaning "capable of". "-ies" is the plural suffix.
