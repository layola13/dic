baby-talk:/ˈbeɪbi tɔːk/| n.|婴儿语；儿语|A simplified manner of speech used with young children, often characterized by reduplication, simplified grammar, and a higher pitch.|She often uses baby talk with her toddler. 她经常用儿语和她的小孩说话。|近义词：child-directed speech, motherese, parentese|反义词:formal language|noun
