allure:/əˈlʊr/ | n. | 魅力；诱惑力 | The power to attract or fascinate; charm. | The allure of the big city drew her in.  大城市的魅力吸引了她。| synonyms: attraction, charm, fascination, magnetism, temptation | antonyms: repulsion, aversion, distaste | noun

allure:/əˈlʊr/ | v. | 引诱；诱惑 | To attract or tempt by offering something desirable. | The promise of adventure allured him to join the expedition.  冒险的承诺诱惑他加入了探险队。| synonyms: entice, tempt, attract, charm, seduce | antonyms: repel, deter, discourage | verb
