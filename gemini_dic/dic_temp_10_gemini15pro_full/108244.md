laughterless:/ˈlɑːftərləs/| adj.|无笑的，没有笑声的|Without laughter; not laughing.  Characterized by the absence of laughter.|Her face was laughterless, a mask of grief. 她的脸上没有笑容，像戴着悲伤的面具。|近义词：unsmiling, mirthless, somber, serious, grave|反义词：laughing, mirthful, cheerful, jovial|词性:形容词

词源: laughter (n.) + -less (suffix).  "Laughter" comes from Old English *hleahter, from Proto-Germanic *hlahtraz.  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking."
