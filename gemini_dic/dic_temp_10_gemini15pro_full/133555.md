nonfinites:/nɒnˈfaɪnaɪts/ | adj. | 非限定动词 | Nonfinite verbs are verb forms that do not show tense. They cannot be the main verb of an independent clause.  Nonfinites include infinitives, participles, and gerunds. |  Reading is my hobby. 阅读是我的爱好。 |  infinitive verbs, participial verbs, gerundial verbs | finite verbs | adjective

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
She enjoys reading books. 她喜欢读书。(reading: present participle used as gerund)
He wants to eat pizza. 他想吃披萨。(to eat: infinitive)

He is reading a book now. 他现在正在读书。(reading: present participle)

He has decided to quit his job. 他已决定辞职。(to quit: infinitive)
