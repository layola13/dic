breams:/briːmz/
n.
(鱼) 欧洲鲫鱼；(光) 光束；(船) 横梁
The river teems with breams. 这条河里有很多欧洲鲫鱼。
A beam of sunlight shone through the window. 一束阳光透过窗户照射进来。
The ship's beams were made of sturdy oak. 这艘船的横梁是用结实的橡木制成的。
plural of bream
The angler caught several breams that day. 那天钓鱼者钓到了几条鲷鱼。
近义词:fish, ray, gleam
反义词:darkness
词性:名词
