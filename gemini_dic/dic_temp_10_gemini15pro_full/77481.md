gazeboes:/ɡəˈziːboʊz/
n.
凉亭，观景亭
A gazebo is a freestanding, roofed, open-sided structure, often hexagonal or octagonal in shape, commonly found in gardens, parks, and other outdoor spaces. It provides shade and a place to relax and enjoy the surroundings.
The wedding ceremony was held in a beautiful gazebo overlooking the lake. 婚礼仪式在一个美丽的可以俯瞰湖面的凉亭里举行。

近义词:pavilion, summerhouse, pergola
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly enclosed structure)
词性:noun
