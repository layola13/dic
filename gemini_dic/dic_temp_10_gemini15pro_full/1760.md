acquisition:/ˌækwɪˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 收购，获得，习得 |The acquisition of language is a complex process. 语言的习得是一个复杂的过程。|例句：The company's latest acquisition is a small software firm.  这家公司最新的收购是一家小型软件公司。

The museum announced the acquisition of a rare painting.  博物馆宣布获得了一幅珍稀的画作。|近义词：purchase, attainment, obtaining |反义词:loss, disposal |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin acquisitionem (nominative acquisitio), noun of action from past-participle stem of acquirere "to acquire," from ad- "to" + quaerere "to seek, gain."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The acquisition of new skills is essential for career advancement. = 获取新技能对职业发展至关重要。
His acquisition of the company took several months. = 他收购这家公司花了几个月的时间。


一般过去时:
The acquisition of the rival company strengthened their market position. = 对竞争对手公司的收购巩固了他们的市场地位。
Her recent acquisition of a valuable antique made headlines. = 她最近获得一件珍贵的古董成了头条新闻。


现在完成时:
They have completed the acquisition of the property. = 他们已经完成了这处房产的收购。
She has focused on the acquisition of knowledge throughout her life. = 她一生都专注于知识的获取。


过去完成时:
By the time the deal was finalized, the acquisition had already been announced. = 在交易完成的时候，收购的消息已经被宣布了。
He had planned the acquisition for years before it finally happened. = 在最终实现之前，他已经计划收购多年了。



将来时:
The acquisition will be finalized next month. = 收购将于下个月完成。
The planned acquisition will significantly expand their operations. = 计划中的收购将大大扩展他们的业务。


虚拟语气:
If the acquisition were successful, it would transform the company. = 如果收购成功，它将改变这家公司。

被动语态:
