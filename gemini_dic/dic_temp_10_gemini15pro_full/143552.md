outhear:/ˌaʊtˈhɪr/| v.|偷听到，无意中听到|To hear something unintentionally, especially something private or secret. |例句：I overheard them talking about the surprise party. 我无意中听到他们在谈论惊喜派对。|例句：She overheard a conversation that made her suspicious. 她无意中听到一段对话，这让她起了疑心。|近义词：eavesdrop, overhear |反义词: N/A |词性:动词

词源:
From Middle English *outhiren, from out- + heren (“to hear”).  Equivalent to out + hear.  First attested around 1300.
