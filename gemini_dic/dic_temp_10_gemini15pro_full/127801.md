neighing:/ˈneɪɪŋ/ | n. | 马嘶声，马嘶|The sound a horse makes.  A loud, prolonged cry that a horse makes.| The neighing of the horses echoed through the valley. 马的嘶鸣声在山谷中回荡。 | 近义词: whinnying, snorting | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑silence寂静) | 词性: 名词 (gerund)

neigh:/neɪ/ | v. | (马)嘶鸣 | To make the characteristic loud cry of a horse. | The horse neighed loudly when it saw its owner.  马看到主人时大声嘶鸣。 | 近义词: whinny | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词) | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Old English *hnægan, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch neighen and German wiehern.
