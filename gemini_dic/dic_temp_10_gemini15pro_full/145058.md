overambitious:/ˌoʊvəræmˈbɪʃəs/| adj.|野心过大的，过分雄心勃勃的|Having or showing excessively ambitious aims or desires.|He had overambitious plans for the company's expansion. 他对公司的扩张计划过于雄心勃勃。|近义词：ambitious, aspiring, aggressive|反义词：unambitious, modest, humble|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + ambitious. "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Ambitious" comes from the Latin "ambitio," meaning "a going around" (originally referring to candidates canvassing for votes), which later developed the sense of "eager pursuit of something."
