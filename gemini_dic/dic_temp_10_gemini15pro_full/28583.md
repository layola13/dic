careerwoman: /kəˈrɪərˌwʊmən/
n.
事业女性, 职业女性
A woman who is actively pursuing a career, especially in a demanding or traditionally male-dominated field.
例句：She's a successful careerwoman in the finance industry. 她是一位金融行业成功的职业女性。

例句：Being a careerwoman and a mother can be challenging. 成为一名职业女性和母亲可能具有挑战性。

近义词：professional woman, businesswoman, working woman
反义词: housewife, stay-at-home mom
词性: 名词
