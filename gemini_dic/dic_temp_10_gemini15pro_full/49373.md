delirament:/dɪˈlɪrəmənt/
|n.|
|谵妄，胡言乱语|
|A state of mental confusion and excitement characterized by incoherent speech and hallucinations.|
|The patient's delirament was caused by a high fever.|病人因高烧而出现谵妄。

|He was suffering from delirament due to the severe infection.|他因严重感染而饱受谵妄之苦。

|近义词：delirium, raving, incoherence|
|反义词：lucidity, coherence, sanity|
|词性: 名词|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēlīrāmentum, from Latin dēlīrāre (“to be insane, rave”), from dē- + līra (“furrow, track”), meaning literally "to go off the track".
