outquestioned:/ˌaʊtˈkwɛstʃənd/
v. |胜过提问，盘问得使哑口无言| To question someone more thoroughly or persistently than another, effectively silencing or surpassing them in questioning. | The lawyer outquestioned the witness, leaving him flustered and unable to respond. 律师盘问证人，使他慌乱得无法回答。|Synonyms: outargue, defeat, surpass|Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by|verb

outquestioned:/ˌaʊtˈkwɛstʃənd/
v. |比...问得更多|To ask more questions than someone else. |She outquestioned all the other reporters at the press conference. 她在记者招待会上比其他记者问的问题都多。|Synonyms: surpass in questioning|Antonyms: be outquestioned by|verb
