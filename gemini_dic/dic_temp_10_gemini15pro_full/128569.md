nibelungenlied:/ˈniːbəlʊŋənˌliːt/| n.|尼伯龙根之歌|A Middle High German epic poem written about 1200, telling the story of Siegfried at the court of the Burgundians and of his wife Kriemhild's revenge for his murder.|Example: The Nibelungenlied is a cornerstone of German literature. 尼伯龙根之歌是德国文学的基石。|Example: Wagner's operas were inspired by the Nibelungenlied. 瓦格纳的歌剧灵感来自尼伯龙根之歌。|Synonym: epic, saga|Antonym: N/A|noun
