glowlamp: /ˈɡləʊˌlæmp/ (UK) /ˈɡloʊˌlæmp/ (US) | n. | 辉光灯，白炽灯 | An electric lamp that produces light by the glowing of a filament heated by an electric current. | The old glowlamp cast a warm, yellowish light.  旧的白炽灯发出温暖的淡黄色光线。|  The glowlamp flickered and died. 白炽灯闪烁了一下就灭了。 | synonyms: incandescent lamp, incandescent bulb | antonyms: LED lamp, fluorescent lamp | noun
