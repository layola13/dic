gry:/ɡriː/
adjective
灰色的，灰白的
Of a color intermediate between black and white, as of ashes or lead.
例句：The sky was a dull gry. 天空是暗灰色的。
例句：His hair had turned gry with age. 他的头发因年老而变灰白了。
近义词：gray, grey, ashen, grizzled
反义词：bright, colorful, vibrant
词性: adjective
