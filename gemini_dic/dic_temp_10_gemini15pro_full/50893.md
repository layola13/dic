desertify:/dɪˈzɜːrtɪfaɪ/ /dɪˈzɜːrtəˌfaɪ/ | vt. | 使沙漠化，使荒漠化 | To turn (land) into desert. |  Overgrazing can desertify grassland. 过度放牧会使草原沙漠化。

The ongoing drought is rapidly desertifying the region.  持续的干旱正在使该地区迅速沙漠化。| 近义词：aridify, make barren | 反义词:reforest, reclaim | verb
