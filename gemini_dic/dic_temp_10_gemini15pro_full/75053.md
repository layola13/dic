frightened:/ˈfraɪtnd/ | adj. | 害怕的，恐惧的 | Feeling fear or alarm. |  She was frightened by the loud noise. 她被巨大的噪音吓到了。

近义词: scared, terrified, afraid, alarmed | 反义词: brave, courageous, unafraid, bold | 词性: 形容词

frighten:/ˈfraɪtn/ | vt. | 使惊恐，吓唬 | To make someone feel fear or alarm. |  The sudden movement frightened the birds. 突如其来的动作吓到了鸟儿。

近义词: scare, terrify, alarm | 反义词: comfort, reassure, soothe | 词性: 动词
