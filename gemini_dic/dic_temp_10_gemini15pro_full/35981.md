coder:/ˈkoʊdər/ (US) /ˈkəʊdə(r)/ (UK)| n. |程序员，编码员|A person who writes code for computer programs.|She's a talented coder who can program in multiple languages. 她是一位才华横溢的程序员，可以用多种语言编程。|Example: The coder worked late into the night to finish the project.  程序员工作到深夜才完成这个项目。|Synonyms: programmer, developer, software engineer|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "user," "client")|Noun
