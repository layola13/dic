hongi:/ˈhɒŋi/ (NZ) /ˈhɔːŋi/ (US)| n.|碰鼻礼|A traditional Māori greeting in which people press their noses and foreheads together.|Example 1: The visitors were welcomed with a hongi. 游客们受到了碰鼻礼的欢迎。

Example 2:  The hongi is a sign of respect and connection in Māori culture.  碰鼻礼在毛利文化中是尊重和联系的象征。|近义词:greeting, salute|反义词:farewell|词性:noun
