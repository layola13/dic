gulfs:/ɡʌlfs/
n.
海湾；深渊；分歧；隔阂
a deep inlet of the sea almost surrounded by land, typically with a narrow mouth.  A very large difference or division between two people or groups, or between two things such as opinions or situations.
The yacht sailed into the gulf.  游艇驶入了海湾。
There is a widening gulf between the rich and the poor. 贫富差距越来越大。
近义词：bay, abyss, chasm, divide
反义词：closure, connection, similarity

gulfs:/ɡʌlfs/
v.
吞没；席卷；吞噬（第三人称单数）
(of water) flow over or cover (something).
The waves gulfed the small boat. 海浪吞没了小船。
近义词：engulf, overwhelm, swallow
反义词：disgorge, release
