overrepresentation:/ˌoʊvərˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 过多代表；过高比例 |  The act of representing something to a greater degree or extent than is justified or accurate.  It often refers to a situation where a particular group is present in larger numbers than would be expected based on their proportion in the general population. | There is an overrepresentation of boys in special education classes. 特殊教育班的男生比例过高。| Overrepresentation of certain ethnic groups in crime statistics can perpetuate harmful stereotypes. 某些种族在犯罪统计数据中的过多代表可能会使有害的刻板印象永久化。|  overabundance, disproportion, preponderance | underrepresentation, scarcity | noun
