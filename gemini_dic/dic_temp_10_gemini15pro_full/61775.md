empearl:/ɪmˈpɜːrl/ /ɛmˈpɝl/| vt.|使成珍珠；用珍珠装饰；使华丽；使完美|To adorn or cover with pearls; to make something look like pearls have been added; to make something more beautiful or perfect.|She embellished her story with fanciful details, almost empearling a simple anecdote into an epic saga. 她用奇特的细节润色了她的故事，几乎把一个简单的轶事变成了史诗般的传奇。|The morning dew empearled the spiderwebs in the garden.  晨露使花园里的蜘蛛网像珍珠一样闪闪发光。|近义词：adorn, decorate, embellish, ornament, beautify|反义词:mar, spoil, blemish, disfigure|词性:动词

词源: From em- + pearl.  "Em-" is a prefix meaning "to cause to be," "to put into," or "to cover with."  Thus, "empearl" literally means to cover with or make into pearls.
