intermountain:/ˌɪntərˈmaʊntən/| adj.|山间的，群山之间的|Situated between mountains or mountain ranges.  Located within a region of mountains. |例句：The intermountain basin is known for its diverse flora and fauna.  这个山间盆地以其多样化的动植物群而闻名。

例句：Intermountain communities often experience unique weather patterns. 山区内的社区经常经历独特的天气模式。|近义词：mountainous, upland|反义词：lowland, coastal|词性:形容词

词源: inter- (prefix) + mountain (noun)
"inter-" comes from Latin, meaning "between, among"
"mountain" comes from Old French "montaigne", ultimately from Latin "montem" (accusative of "mons"), meaning "mountain".
