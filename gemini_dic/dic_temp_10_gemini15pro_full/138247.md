nostrum's:/ˈnɒstrəmz/
n.
(usually plural) 秘方，灵丹妙药，(尤指)江湖药，万灵药;  (尤指)个人或群体提出的解决社会或政治问题的方案(尤指不切实际的)
A medicine prepared by an unqualified person, especially one that is not effective.  Often used figuratively to refer to a proposed solution for a problem, especially one that is seen as simplistic or inadequate.
The politician offered a few familiar nostrums about the economy, but no real solutions.  这位政客提出了一些关于经济的熟知的“灵丹妙药”，但没有真正的解决方案。
近义词:remedy, panacea, cure-all, elixir
反义词:poison, toxin
