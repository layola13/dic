discomfitingly:/dɪskəmˈfɪtɪŋli/
adv.
令人不安地，令人尴尬地
in a way that makes someone feel uneasy, embarrassed, or disconcerted.
Her silence was discomfitingly long. 她长时间的沉默令人不安。
近义词: disturbingly, unsettlingly, awkwardly
反义词: comfortingly, reassuringly
词性: 副词

discomfit:/dɪskəmˈfɪt/
v.
使不安，使尴尬
make (someone) feel uneasy or embarrassed.
He was discomfited by her silence. 她的沉默让他感到不安。
近义词: embarrass, disconcert, perturb
反义词: comfort, reassure
词性: 动词

-ing:/ɪŋ/
suffix.
形成现在分词或动名词
forming present participles or gerunds.
Walking is good exercise.  走路是很好的运动。（动名词）
He is walking to work. 他正步行去上班。（现在分词）
词性: 后缀

-ly:/li/
suffix.
形成副词
forming adverbs.
He spoke quickly. 他说得很快。
词性: 后缀


词源:
From Old French desconfit, past participle of desconfire  "to defeat, rout," from des- "opposite of" + con- "with" + faire "to do" (from Latin facere). The original sense was "to defeat utterly," but it weakened to "to frustrate, thwart," then "to disconcert."
