cabins:/ˈkæbɪnz/| n. |小木屋，隔间|A small, simple dwelling, usually made of wood.  Also, a private compartment on a ship or aircraft. |We rented a cozy cabin in the mountains for the weekend. 我们周末在山上租了一个舒适的小木屋。|The ship's cabins were surprisingly spacious.  这艘船的船舱出奇地宽敞。|synonyms: huts, sheds, compartments, berths|antonyms: mansions, palaces|noun
