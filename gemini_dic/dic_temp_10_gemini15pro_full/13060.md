astrography: /əˈstrɒɡrəfi/ /əˈstrɑːɡrəfi/
n. |星象学；天体摄影术| The study of the positions of the stars and planets and the supposed influence they have on events and human character; the photographic mapping of stars and other celestial bodies. | Astrography played a significant role in ancient cultures.  星象学在古代文化中扮演着重要的角色。|  He dedicated his life to astrography, capturing breathtaking images of distant galaxies.  他毕生致力于天体摄影术，捕捉遥远星系的惊人图像。 |Synonyms: astrology, uranography, astrophotography | Antonyms:  None | Noun
