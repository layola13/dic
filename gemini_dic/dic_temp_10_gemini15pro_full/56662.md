donya:/ˈdʊnjə/ (US), /ˈdɒnjə/ (UK)| n.| 世界，尘世; 人类，众人 |A Persian word meaning "world," "universe," or "realm." It often refers to the material world or secular affairs, as opposed to the spiritual realm. In some contexts, it can also mean "people" or "humanity." |The dervish renounced the donya and all its temptations. 这位苦行僧放弃了尘世及其所有诱惑。|Synonyms: world, universe, realm, cosmos, humanity, people|Antonyms: afterlife, heaven, hereafter|Noun
