china-blue:/ˈtʃaɪnə blˈuː/| adj.|中国蓝，青蓝色|A vivid blue pigment, originally made in China, or the colour of this pigment.  A bright greenish-blue. |The vase was a beautiful shade of china-blue.  那个花瓶是美丽的青蓝色。|近义词：azure, cerulean, sapphire, turquoise|反义词： (No single direct antonym; consider colours like orange, red, brown)|词性:形容词
