communism:/ˈkɒmjʊnɪzəm/ (US) /ˈkɑːmjənɪzəm/| n. |共产主义|A political theory derived from Marxism, advocating class war and leading to a society in which all property is publicly owned and each person works and is paid according to their abilities and needs.|例句：Communism was a powerful ideology in the 20th century.  二十世纪共产主义是一个强大的意识形态。

例句：The country transitioned from communism to a market economy.  这个国家从共产主义过渡到市场经济。|近义词：Marxism, socialism|反义词:capitalism, free market|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *communisme*, from *commun* (“common”) + *-isme* (“-ism”).
