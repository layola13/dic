一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing outside? = 他在外面做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? = 你最近学了什么？
What has she been working on? = 她一直在忙什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been reading lately? = 你最近一直在读什么？
What has he been doing all afternoon? = 他整个下午都在做什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 聚会上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she talking about? = 她在说什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你走之前做了什么？
What had happened before the police arrived? = 警察到达之前发生了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will happen if it rains? = 如果下雨会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? = 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by Friday? = 你到周五会完成什么？
What will have changed by next year? = 到明年会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
