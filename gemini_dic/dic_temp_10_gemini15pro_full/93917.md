immund:/ɪˈmjuːnd/ | adj. | 免疫的，不受影响的 | Protected from a disease or the like, especially by inoculation or as a result of having the disease.  |  Example 1:  He is immune to chickenpox because he had it as a child. 他对水痘免疫，因为他小时候得过水痘。

Example 2: The vaccine makes you immune to the virus.  疫苗能让你对这种病毒免疫。 | Synonyms: resistant, invulnerable, exempt | Antonyms: susceptible, vulnerable, unprotected | word property: adjective
