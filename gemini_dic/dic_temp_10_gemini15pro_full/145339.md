overbudget:/ˌoʊvərˈbʌdʒɪt/
adj.|超出预算的|Exceeding the planned or allocated budget.|The project ran overbudget by 20%.  这个项目超出了预算20%。|近义词：overspent, excessive, exorbitant|反义词:underbudget, within budget|词性:形容词
overbudget:/ˌoʊvərˈbʌdʒɪt/
adv.|超出预算地|In a manner that exceeds the planned or allocated budget.|The project finished overbudget. 这个项目完成时超出了预算。|近义词：excessively, extravagantly|反义词:frugally, economically|词性:副词
