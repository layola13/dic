encroaching:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 侵入的，侵占的，蚕食的 | Gradually advancing beyond proper or usual limits; trespassing. |  The encroaching desert is threatening the farmland.  encroaching沙漠正在威胁农田。| synonyms: intruding, invading, trespassing, infringing | antonyms: retreating, withdrawing, receding | adjective

encroach:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃ/ | v. | 侵入，侵占，蚕食 | Intrude on (a person's territory or a thing considered to be a right). | The sea is encroaching on the land. 海水正在侵蚀陆地。| synonyms: intrude, invade, trespass, infringe | antonyms: retreat, withdraw, recede | verb
