moldiness:/ˈmoʊldɪnəs/
noun
霉味；发霉；霉菌
the state of being moldy
例句：The moldiness in the basement was overwhelming. 地下室的霉味很重。

例句：The old books had a distinct moldiness to them.  这些旧书有一股明显的霉味。
近义词：mildew, mustiness, dampness
反义词：freshness, cleanness
词性:n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From moldy + -ness. Moldy comes from Middle English *moldi, from Old Norse mygla (“mold”).  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
