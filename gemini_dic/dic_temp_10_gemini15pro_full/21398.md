blousiest: /ˈblaʊziɪst/
adjective
最宽松的；最肥大的
most loose and baggy; most billowing
Her blousiest dress billowed in the wind. 她最宽松的连衣裙在风中飘动。
近义词:baggiest, loosest, billowing
反义词: tightest, fitted, snug
词性: adjective
