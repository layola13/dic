cowbell:/ˈkaʊˌbɛl/| n.|牛铃；牛颈铃|A bell, typically one of a pair, hung around the neck of a cow so that the cow can be located by its sound.  It is also used as a percussion instrument, especially in certain genres of music.|The cows wore cowbells so the farmer could find them.  奶牛戴着牛铃，这样农民就能找到它们。|
The band used a cowbell to create a rhythmic effect.  乐队用牛铃来制造节奏效果。|Synonyms: bell, chime, clang, jingle|Antonyms: silence|noun
