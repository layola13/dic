conductive:/kənˈdʌktɪv/
adjective
传导的；导电的；有传导力的
Having the property of transmitting heat, electricity, or other forms of energy.
Copper is highly conductive of electricity. 铜具有很高的导电性。
synonyms: transmitting, conducting, communicative
antonyms: nonconductive, insulating, resistant
词性: 形容词

conductive:/kənˈdʌktɪv/
adjective
导致…的；有助于…的
Tending to cause or bring about.
The damp conditions were conducive to the growth of mold. 潮湿的环境有助于霉菌生长。
synonyms: contributive, favorable, helpful
antonyms: unfavorable, hindering, detrimental
词性: 形容词
