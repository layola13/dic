monopolised:/məˈnɒpəlaɪzd/ /məˈnɑːpəlaɪzd/
verb
垄断，独占
To obtain exclusive possession or control of (a trade, commodity, or service).
The company monopolised the market for computer chips.  这家公司垄断了计算机芯片市场。
The dictator monopolised the country's resources.  独裁者垄断了国家的资源。

近义词: dominated, controlled, cornered
反义词: shared, distributed, opened

词性: verb (past tense and past participle of monopolise)
