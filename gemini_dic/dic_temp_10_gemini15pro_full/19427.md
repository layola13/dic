bicker: /ˈbɪkər/ | vi. | 争吵，斗嘴 | To argue about petty and trivial matters. |  The children were bickering over who got the last cookie. 孩子们在为谁吃到最后一块饼干而争吵。

They bickered constantly about whose turn it was to do the dishes.  他们经常为了轮到谁洗碗而争吵。 | 近义词: quarrel, squabble, argue, wrangle | 反义词: agree, concur, harmonize | 词性: 动词

bickers: /ˈbɪkərz/ | n. | 争吵，口角 (通常用复数形式) | Petty and trivial arguments. | Their constant bickers were disrupting the peace of the neighborhood. 他们不断的口角扰乱了社区的安宁。

The family's meal was interrupted by the children's bickers.  家人的用餐被孩子们的争吵打断了。 | 近义词: quarrels, squabbles, arguments, wrangles | 反义词: agreements, harmonies | 词性: 名词

bicker with someone: 与某人争吵  He's always bickering with his neighbors. 他总是和他的邻居争吵。
bicker over something: 为某事争吵 They were bickering over the last piece of pizza. 他们在为最后一块披萨争吵。
bicker about something: 为某事争吵 They bicker about everything. 他们什么都吵。

petty bickering: 无聊的争吵  It's just petty bickering; don't take it seriously.  这只是无聊的争吵，别当真。
