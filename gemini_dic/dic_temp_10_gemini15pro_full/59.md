aai:/ɑːiː/
noun
assistant artificial intelligence
助理人工智能
AAI refers to a type of artificial intelligence designed to act as a personal assistant. It can perform tasks such as scheduling appointments, sending emails, and providing information.
AAI can schedule your meetings and manage your emails efficiently.  助理人工智能可以有效地安排你的会议和管理你的电子邮件。

AAI can provide real-time traffic updates and suggest alternative routes. 助理人工智能可以提供实时交通更新并建议替代路线。
Synonym:virtual assistant, AI assistant
Antonym:human assistant
n.
