dialectically:/ˌdaɪəˈlɛktɪkli/
adv.|辩证地|In a way that relates to the logical discussion of ideas and opinions.  Engaging in or relating to the art of debate; employing the method of dialectic.|Marx analyzed the capitalist mode of production dialectically. 马克思辩证地分析了资本主义生产方式。|logically, argumentatively|dogmatically, simplistically
词性: 副词
