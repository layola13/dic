deride:/dɪˈraɪd/ | v. | 嘲笑，奚落 | To treat or speak of with contempt or ridicule. | The critics derided his latest novel as sentimental and predictable.  评论家嘲笑他的最新小说感伤又老套。| synonyms: mock, ridicule, scorn, taunt, jeer | antonyms: praise, commend, laud, admire | verb

deride:/dɪˈraɪd/ | v. | 嘲笑，奚落 | To make fun of; to mock. |  They derided his efforts to learn the piano. 他们嘲笑他学习钢琴的努力。 | synonyms:  scoff at, lampoon | antonyms: respect, compliment | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dērīdēre*, from *dē-* (down, away) + *rīdēre* (to laugh).
