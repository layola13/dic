flavol:/ˈfleɪvɒl/ | n. | 黄酮醇; 黄酮类化合物 | A type of flavonoid, a group of plant pigments that are responsible for the colors of many fruits and vegetables.  They are also known for their antioxidant properties. |  Flavols are found in high concentrations in foods like onions and kale.  黄酮醇在洋葱和羽衣甘蓝等食物中含量很高。|  Recent research has focused on the health benefits of dietary flavol intake. 最近的研究集中在膳食黄酮醇摄入对健康的好处。| synonyms: flavonoid (more general), quercetin (a specific type of flavol) | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound) | noun
