cheddary:/ˈtʃɛdəri/| adj.|奶酪味的，像切达干酪的|Having a flavor or texture resembling that of cheddar cheese.|The biscuits had a distinctly cheddary aroma.  这些饼干有一种明显的奶酪香味。|近义词： cheesy|反义词: bland|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "Cheddar" (a village in Somerset, England, known for its cheese) + "-y" (a suffix forming adjectives indicating resemblance or characteristic).
