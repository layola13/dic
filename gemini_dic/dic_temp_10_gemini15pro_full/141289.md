ETYMOLOGY->
From "open-air" + "-ness". "Open-air" signifies "taking place or located in the open air," from "open" (meaning not enclosed or covered) + "air" (the invisible gaseous substance surrounding the earth).  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state, quality, or condition.
