barflies:/ˈbɑːrflaɪz/
n.
酒吧常客；酒鬼
People who spend a lot of time in bars, often drinking heavily.
The barflies at O'Malley's were already there at noon.
奥马利酒吧的那些酒鬼中午就已经在那里了。
habitual drinkers, regulars, bar regulars
teetotalers, abstainers
noun
