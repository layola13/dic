PHRASE->
operates on (something)  依靠（某种东西）运行  The machine operates on electricity. 这台机器依靠电力运行。
operates within (something) 在...范围内运作 The organization operates within strict guidelines. 该组织在严格的指导方针内运作。
operates under (something) 在...之下运作  The company operates under a new management team.  这家公司在一个新的管理团队下运作。
operates out of (somewhere) 从...地方运作 The business operates out of a small office.  这家企业在一个小办公室里运作。
operates independently 独立运作  The two branches operate independently of each other. 这两个分支机构独立运作。
