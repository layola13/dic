palliating:/ˈpæliˌeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 减轻的，缓和的 | making (a problem, pain, etc.) less severe without removing the cause |  The doctor prescribed medication for palliating the patient's pain. 医生开了药来减轻病人的疼痛。 | mitigating, alleviating, easing, relieving | exacerbating, aggravating, worsening | adjective

palliate:/ˈpæliˌeɪt/ | verb | 减轻，缓和 | to make (a problem, pain, etc.) less severe without removing the cause | His apology did little to palliate her anger. 他的道歉并没有减轻她的愤怒。 | mitigate, alleviate, ease, relieve | exacerbate, aggravate, worsen | verb
