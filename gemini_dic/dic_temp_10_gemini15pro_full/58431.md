duck:/dʌk/ | n. v. | 鸭子；俯身；躲避 | A waterbird with short legs, a broad flat beak, and webbed feet.  To bend down quickly or suddenly. To avoid something unpleasant. |  The duck quacked loudly.  鸭子大声地嘎嘎叫。 He ducked his head to avoid being hit by the branch. 他低下头以避免被树枝击中。 | 近义词：(n.) waterfowl, drake (male duck), hen (female duck); (v.) dodge, stoop, crouch | 反义词: (v.) confront, face | 词性: 名词；动词

duck:/dʌk/ | n. | 鸭肉 | The flesh of a duck as food. | We had roast duck for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了烤鸭。 | 近义词: poultry | 反义词: - | 词性: 名词
