antiempirical:/ˌæntiɪmˈpɪrɪkl/ | adj. | 反经验主义的，非经验主义的 | Opposed to, or rejecting, empiricism (the view that knowledge comes primarily from sensory experience). | His antiempirical stance led him to distrust scientific findings. 他反经验主义的立场使他不信任科学发现。|  Many religious beliefs are considered antiempirical. 许多宗教信仰被认为是反经验主义的。 | 近义词: nonempirical, a priori, theoretical, speculative | 反义词: empirical, experiential, observed | 词性: adjective
