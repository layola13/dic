breasthook:/ˈbrɛstˌhʊk/| n.|胸钩，胸梁，船首斜撑|A curved timber or metal brace connecting the stem to the forward ribs of a ship or boat. It strengthens the bow area and provides support for the mast.|The breasthook on the old sailboat was badly corroded. 那艘旧帆船的胸钩严重腐蚀了。|The breasthook helps distribute the strain from the mast. 胸钩有助于分散桅杆的压力。|Synonyms: breast beam, stemson, apron|Antonyms: N/A|noun
