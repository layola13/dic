armchairs:/ˈɑːrmtʃɛərz/ | n. | 扶手椅 | A comfortable chair with supports for the arms. |  We relaxed in the armchairs by the fire. 我们坐在壁炉旁的扶手椅上放松身心。|  lounge chairs, recliners | stools, benches | noun

armchair:/ˈɑːrmtʃɛər/ | adj. |  理论上的；空想的 | Not based on practical experience; theoretical. | He's an armchair traveler, reading about exotic places but never going there. 他是一个空想旅行家，只阅读异国情调的地方，却从不去那里。 | theoretical, speculative | practical, hands-on | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From arm + chair.  The word "arm" traces back to Proto-Germanic *armaz, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂er- "to fit together." "Chair" comes from Old French chaiere, from Latin cathedra.
