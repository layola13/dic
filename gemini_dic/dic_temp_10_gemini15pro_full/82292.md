groupage:/ˈɡruːpɪdʒ/| n.|拼装货, 集运货物|The cost of groupage is usually lower than sending a full container. 集运货物的成本通常低于发送整箱货物。|Groupage services are beneficial for smaller shipments. 集运服务对较小的货物运输有益。|近义词：consolidation, combined shipment|反义词: full container load (FCL)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *groupage*, from *grouper* "to group," from *groupe* "group." Ultimately from Germanic origin.
