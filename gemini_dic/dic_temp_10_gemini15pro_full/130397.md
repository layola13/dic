non-violently:/nɒnˈvaɪələntli/ | adv. | 非暴力地 | In a way that does not involve violence or force. |  The protesters dispersed non-violently. 抗议者非暴力地散开了。 | peacefully, passively | violently, aggressively | adverb

non-violently:/nɒnˈvaɪələntli/ | adv. | 非暴力地 |  Without resorting to violence; peacefully. | They resolved the conflict non-violently through dialogue. 他们通过对话非暴力地解决了冲突。 | amicably, peacefully | forcefully, aggressively | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + violently.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "without," and "violently" is derived from the Latin "violentus," meaning "vehement" or "characterized by force."
