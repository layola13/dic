discontented:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛntɪd/ | adj. | 不满的，不高兴的 | Feeling or showing dissatisfaction or unhappiness. | He seemed discontented with his job. 他似乎对自己的工作不满。| dissatisfied, disgruntled, malcontent | contented, satisfied | adjective

discontent:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛnt/ | n. | 不满，不满足 | Lack of contentment; dissatisfaction. | There is widespread discontent among the workers. 工人们普遍存在不满情绪。 | dissatisfaction, unhappiness, unrest | contentment, satisfaction | noun

Etymology:
From late Middle English: from dis- (expressing negation) + content, contented.
