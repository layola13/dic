papescent:/peɪˈpɛsənt/
adjective
像纸的；纸状的；薄而干燥的
Becoming like paper; papery; thin and dry.
The papescent leaves crumbled in my hand.  这些像纸一样的叶子在我手里碎了。
The old document was papescent and brittle. 这份古老的文件薄而易碎，像纸一样。
近义词: papery, chartaceous, flimsy
反义词: sturdy, robust, thick
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin "papyrus" (paper) + "-escent" (becoming).
