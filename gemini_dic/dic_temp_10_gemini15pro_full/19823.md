bimmetry: /baɪˈmɛtri/ | n. | 双侧测量，两侧对称 | The study of bilateral symmetry in organisms.  The measurement of two sides, often to assess symmetry. |  The bimmetry of the human face is often used in facial recognition technology. 人脸的双侧测量常用于面部识别技术。  The study examined the bimmetry of leaves from different species of trees. 这项研究调查了不同树种叶子的双侧测量。 | near synonyms: symmetry, bilateralism | antonym: asymmetry | noun
