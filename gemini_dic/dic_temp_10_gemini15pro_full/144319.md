outsprints:/ˌaʊtˈsprɪnts/
verb
冲刺超越，比…跑得快
He outsprinted the other runners to win the race. 他在冲刺阶段超过其他选手赢得了比赛。
outsprints
v.
to run faster than someone in a sprint
The young athlete outsprinted his rivals in the final stretch.  这位年轻的运动员在最后一段冲刺中超过了他的对手。
近义词: outruns, overtakes
反义词: lags behind
