bookbinding: /ˈbʊkˌbaɪndɪŋ/ | n. | 装订；书籍装帧 | The art or process of binding books. |  The bookbinding on this old edition is exquisite. 这本旧版书的装订精美。| synonyms: binding, bookcraft | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, potentially "unbinding," but not commonly used in this context) | noun

Etymology:  From "book" + "binding,"  "bind" from Old English "bindan," meaning "to tie, fasten."
