hang-back:/ˈhæŋˌbæk/| n.|犹豫，退缩；落后者|A person who hesitates to participate or is reluctant to move forward.|Example: He was a hang-back when it came to asking for a raise.  在要求加薪方面，他总是犹豫不决。|Example:  She's a bit of a hang-back in social situations.  在社交场合，她有点畏缩不前。|近义词：hesitater, laggard, shirker|反义词: go-getter, enthusiast|词性:noun

hang-back:/ˈhæŋˌbæk/| adj.|迟疑的，踌躇不前的|Reluctant to participate or move forward; hesitant.|Example:  His hang-back attitude prevented him from seizing the opportunity. 他迟疑的态度使他错失了良机。|Example: The hang-back investors missed out on the booming market.  踌躇不前的投资者错过了蓬勃发展的市场。|近义词：hesitant, reluctant, timid|反义词: eager, proactive|词性:adjective
