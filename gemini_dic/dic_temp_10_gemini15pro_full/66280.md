excommunicate: /ˌɛks kəˈmjuːnɪˌkeɪt/ | vt. | 开除教籍，逐出教会 | To officially exclude someone from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church. | The priest excommunicated him for his heretical beliefs.  神父因为他异端的信仰而将他逐出教会。 | Synonyms: banish, ostracize, exclude | Antonyms: admit, include, welcome | Verb

excommunicated: /ˌɛks kəˈmjuːnɪˌkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 被逐出教会的，被开除教籍的 |  Having been officially excluded from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church. | He was excommunicated and shunned by the community. 他被逐出教会，并被社区排斥。| Synonyms: banished, ostracized, excluded | Antonyms: admitted, included, welcomed | Adjective
