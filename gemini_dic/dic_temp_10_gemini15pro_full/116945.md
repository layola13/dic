mazelike:/ˈmeɪzlaɪk/
adjective
迷宫似的，像迷宫的
Resembling a maze; intricate and confusing.
The old city's streets are mazelike and easy to get lost in.  老城区的街道像迷宫一样，很容易迷路。
The corridors of the hospital were mazelike and confusing. 医院的走廊像迷宫一样，令人困惑。
Synonyms: labyrinthine, intricate, winding, complex, confusing
Antonyms: straightforward, simple, clear, direct
词性: 形容词
