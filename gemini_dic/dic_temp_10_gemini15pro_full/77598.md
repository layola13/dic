geekiest:/ˈɡiːkiɪst/| adj.|最怪咖的，最书呆子气的|Superlative form of geeky: most geeky.  It describes someone who is extremely enthusiastic about and knowledgeable about a particular, often technical, subject, often to the point of being considered socially awkward.|He had the geekiest collection of Star Wars memorabilia I'd ever seen. 他收藏的星球大战纪念品是我见过的最怪的。|Near Synonyms: nerdiest, dorkiest|Antonyms: coolest, most popular|Adjective
