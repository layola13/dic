befallen:/bɪˈfɔːlən/
verb
降临，发生（通常指不幸的事情）
(especially of something bad) happen to someone.
A great misfortune has befallen the family.  这个家庭遭遇了巨大的不幸。
近义词：happen, occur, take place
反义词：avoided
