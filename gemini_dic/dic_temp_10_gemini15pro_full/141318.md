open-air:/ˌoʊpən ˈɛər/ | adj. |露天的，户外的|Taking place or located outdoors, not enclosed by a roof or walls. |例句：We enjoyed an open-air concert in the park. 我们在公园里欣赏了一场露天音乐会。|例句：The open-air market was bustling with activity. 露天市场熙熙攘攘，非常热闹。|近义词：outdoor, alfresco|反义词：indoor, enclosed|词性：形容词

Etymology:
From "open" + "air".  "Open" traces back to Old English "open", from Proto-Germanic "*upanaz". "Air" comes from Old French "air", ultimately from Latin "aer".
