nonsecretory:/ˌnɒnˈsiːkrɪtəri/
adjective
非分泌性的
Not producing or releasing a secretion.
The nonsecretory cells of the stomach lining protect it from acid. 胃壁的非分泌性细胞保护它免受酸的侵蚀。
近义词: nonproducing, inactive
反义词: secretory, producing
词性: adjective
