broad:/brɔːd/ (UK), /brɑːd/ (US)| adj.|宽阔的，广阔的；广泛的；明显的；大概的|Wide, extensive; covering a large number of subjects or areas; obvious; general or approximate.|The river is very broad at this point. 这条河在这个地方很宽。|近义词：wide, extensive, vast, comprehensive|反义词：narrow, limited, restricted|词性:形容词

broad:/brɔːd/ (UK), /brɑːd/ (US)| n.|女人,尤指粗俗或放荡的女人(非正式且冒犯)|A woman, especially one considered vulgar or promiscuous (informal and offensive).|That broad over there looks trouble.  那边那个女人看起来很麻烦。(此用法被认为是冒犯性的，应避免使用)|近义词：(This sense has many derogatory slang synonyms which are not appropriate to list)|反义词：(No direct antonym)|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *brād, from Proto-Germanic *braidaz, from Proto-Indo-European *mr̥h₂-tós.  Cognates include Old Saxon brēd, Old Frisian bred, Old High German breit, German breit, Old Norse breiðr, Gothic 𐌱𐍂𐌴𐌹𐌸𐍃 (breiþs), Dutch breed.
