antenuptial:/ˌæntiˈnʌpʃəl/
adjective
婚前的
Relating to the period before marriage.
The couple signed an antenuptial agreement. 这对夫妇签署了婚前协议。
The antenuptial celebrations were lavish. 婚前的庆祝活动很奢华。
Synonyms: premarital, prenuptial
Antonyms: postnuptial, postmarital
词性: adjective
