on-a-cloud:/ɑːn ə klaʊd/
adjective
飘飘然的，欣喜若狂的
Extremely happy and elated, as if floating in the air.
例句：She was on a cloud after winning the lottery. 她中了彩票后欣喜若狂。

例句：He's been on a cloud ever since he got engaged. 自从订婚以来，他一直飘飘然。

近义词：elated, overjoyed, thrilled, ecstatic
反义词：depressed, downcast, miserable
词性: adjective
