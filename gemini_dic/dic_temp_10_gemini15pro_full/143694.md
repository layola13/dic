outlearn:/ˌaʊtˈlɜːrn/
verb
超越；在学习上胜过
to surpass someone in learning; to learn faster or more effectively than someone else
例句：
She outlearned all her classmates in mathematics. 她在数学方面超过了所有同学。

He quickly outlearned his mentor in computer programming. 他在计算机编程方面很快就超过了他的导师。

近义词：surpass, excel, outstrip
反义词：lag behind, fall behind
词性：v.
