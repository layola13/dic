nutbreaker:/ˈnʌtˌbreɪkər/| n.|胡桃夹子; 棘手的问题; 难对付的人|A tool used to crack nuts.  Something that is difficult to solve or overcome. A person who is difficult to deal with.|例句：This nutcracker is perfect for cracking walnuts.  这个胡桃夹子非常适合用来夹核桃。

例句：The budget deficit is proving to be a real nutbreaker for the government.  预算赤字对政府来说是一个真正棘手的问题。|近义词：nutcracker (for the tool), problem, challenge, difficulty, tough nut to crack (for the problem), adversary, opponent (for the person)|反义词:easy task, pushover (for the problem), ally, supporter (for the person)|词性:noun
