orthokinesis:/ˌɔːrθəʊkɪˈniːsɪs/ /ˌɔːrθoʊkɪˈniːsɪs/
noun
趋光性；正趋光性
The movement of an organism in response to a stimulus, whereby the speed of movement depends on the intensity of the stimulus.
Woodlice show orthokinesis in response to humidity.  潮虫对湿度表现出趋光性。

近义词: kinesis
反义词: None
词性: n.
