ditched:/dɪtʃt/
verb
抛弃，丢弃，摆脱，逃学
To get rid of something or someone that is no longer wanted.  To abandon or leave someone or something. To stay away from school without permission.
He ditched his old car for a new one. 他把他的旧车换了一辆新的。
She ditched her boyfriend last week. 她上周甩了她的男朋友。
The kids ditched school and went to the beach. 孩子们逃学去了海滩。
近义词:abandoned, deserted, dumped,  skipped (school)
反义词:kept, retained, attended (school)
词性:verb

ditched:/dɪtʃt/
adjective
被抛弃的，被丢弃的
Abandoned or left behind.
The ditched car was later towed away. 被丢弃的汽车后来被拖走了。
近义词:abandoned, deserted
反义词:kept, cherished
词性:adjective
