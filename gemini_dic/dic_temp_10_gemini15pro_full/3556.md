agamic:/əˈɡæmɪk/
adjective
无性的；非配子生殖的
Reproducing asexually; not involving the union of gametes.
Agamic reproduction is a common strategy for many invertebrates. 无性繁殖是许多无脊椎动物的常见策略。
Parthenogenesis is a form of agamic reproduction.  孤雌生殖是一种无性繁殖的形式。
Synonyms: asexual, vegetative
Antonyms: sexual
词性: adjective
