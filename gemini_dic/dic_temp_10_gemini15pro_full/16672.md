None, the concept doesn't have a direct opposite. |noun, verb


barrel:/ˈbærəl/ (US) /ˈbærəl/ (UK)| v. |快速移动；把…装桶|To move very fast; to put something into barrels.|He barreled down the hallway. 他飞奔过走廊。|近义词：speed, rush, hurtle|反义词: crawl, dawdle |verb
