crabbed:/ˈkræbɪd/ | adj. | 暴躁的；易怒的；（字迹）潦草的；难懂的 | Bad-tempered and irritable; (of handwriting) illegible; difficult to understand. |  The old man was known for his crabbed personality.  这个老人以他暴躁的性格而闻名。

The crabbed handwriting made the letter difficult to decipher.  潦草的字迹使这封信难以辨认。 | 近义词：bad-tempered, grumpy, irritable, illegible, obscure | 反义词：good-natured, cheerful, legible, clear | 词性：形容词

Etymology: From crab (n.) + -ed.  The original sense referred to something that moved sideways like a crab, then to something perverse or awkward, and finally to bad temper.
