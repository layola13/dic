ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin importunatus "troublesome, pressing," from Latin importunus "unfit, unsuitable, troublesome," from in- "not" (from PIE root *en "in") + portus "harbor," properly "fit condition, opportunity."  The notion is of something "out of place, untimely."
