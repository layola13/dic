clamp-down:/ˈklæmpˌdaʊn/ (US), /ˈklæmpˌdaʊn/ (UK)| n.|压制，取缔，打击|A sudden, forceful attempt to stop an activity, especially one that is considered undesirable or illegal.|The government announced a clamp-down on illegal immigration. 政府宣布对非法移民进行打击。|近义词：crackdown, suppression, repression|反义词:liberalisation, deregulation|noun
