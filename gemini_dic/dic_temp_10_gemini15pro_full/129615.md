non-conformity:/ˌnɒn kənˈfɔːmɪti/ (US) /ˌnɑːn kənˈfɔːrməti/| n. |不墨守成规，不一致，不合常规|Failure or refusal to conform to a prevailing rule or practice.|Her non-conformity to traditional gender roles made her a controversial figure. 她不遵循传统的性别角色，这使她成为一个有争议的人物。|Synonyms:  dissidence,  rebellion,  unorthodoxy,  individuality,  independence|Antonyms: conformity, obedience, compliance|noun
