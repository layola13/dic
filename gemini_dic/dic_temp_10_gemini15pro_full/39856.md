constancy:/ˈkɒnstənsi/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnstənsi/ (US)| n.| 恒久不变；忠诚；坚定；持久性|the quality of being faithful and unchanging|She showed great constancy in her devotion to her work. 她对工作的奉献表现出极大的坚定不移。|近义词：fidelity, loyalty, steadfastness, perseverance, persistence|反义词：fickleness, infidelity, disloyalty, variability, inconstancy|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Old French constance, from Latin constantia, from constans ‘standing firm’.
