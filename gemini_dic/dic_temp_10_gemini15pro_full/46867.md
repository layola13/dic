dareful:/ˈdɛərfʊl/
adj.
大胆的，鲁莽的
Full of daring; adventurous; bold.
He made a dareful attempt to climb the mountain. 他大胆地尝试攀登那座山。
近义词:bold, audacious, adventurous, reckless, daring
反义词:timid, cautious, cowardly, careful
词性:形容词

词源:
From dare + -ful.  "Dare" comes from Old English *durran, from Proto-Germanic *durzaną. "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of," from Old English -ful.
