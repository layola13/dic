coilable:/ˈkɔɪləbl/| adj.|可盘绕的，可卷曲的|Capable of being coiled or wound, especially into a spiral or other compact shape.  Something that is flexible and can be easily wound or rolled up.|The garden hose is coilable for easy storage.  花园水管可以盘绕起来方便存放。|近义词：rollable, windable, flexible, pliable|反义词:rigid, inflexible, stiff|adjective
