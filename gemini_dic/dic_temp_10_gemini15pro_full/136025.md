nonreality:/ˌnɑːnriˈælɪti/
n.
非现实，虚幻，不真实
The book explored themes of nonreality and fantasy. 这本书探讨了非现实和幻想的主题。
例句：
He escaped into a world of nonreality. 他逃到了一个虚幻的世界。
Her artwork often depicts scenes of dreamlike nonreality. 她的艺术作品经常描绘梦境般的非现实场景。

近义词：unreality, fantasy, illusion, fiction
反义词：reality, actuality, truth
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + reality.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of". "Reality" comes from the Late Latin "realitas", meaning "thing", derived from the Latin "res".
