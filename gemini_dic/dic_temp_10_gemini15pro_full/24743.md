broodiness:/ˈbruːdinəs/| n.|孵蛋期; 沉思; 闷闷不乐|The state of being broody, inclined to sit on eggs (in birds) or exhibiting maternal instincts (in humans or animals); also, a state of preoccupied thoughtfulness, often melancholic.|例句：The hen's broodiness lasted for three weeks.  母鸡的孵蛋期持续了三个星期。

例句：Her broodiness made her seem distant and unapproachable. 她闷闷不乐的样子让她显得疏远且难以接近。|近义词：thoughtfulness, pensiveness, contemplativeness,  maternal instinct|反义词：cheerfulness, lightheartedness|词性：名词
