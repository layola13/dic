infomercial: /ˌɪnfoʊˈmɜːrʃəl/ (US) /ˌɪnfəˈmɜːʃəl/ (UK)| n. | 商业信息片，专题广告片 | A television program that promotes a product in an informative and persuasive way, often resembling a documentary or talk show. | I saw an infomercial for a new type of blender last night. 我昨晚看到一个新型搅拌机的专题广告片。|  The infomercial demonstrated the amazing cleaning power of the new vacuum.  这个专题广告片演示了新型真空吸尘器的惊人清洁能力。 | synonyms: paid programming, teleshopping, direct response television | antonyms: commercial, advertisement (although these are related, infomercials are generally longer and more detailed) | noun
