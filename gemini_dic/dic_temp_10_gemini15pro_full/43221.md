crackdowns: /ˈkrækdaʊnz/
| n. | 镇压；打击；取缔；严厉的惩罚 | a series of severe measures to restrict or suppress undesirable or illegal people or behavior.  | The government launched a series of crackdowns on illegal drug trafficking. 政府对非法毒品交易展开了一系列打击行动。| synonyms: suppression, repression, clampdown | antonyms: leniency, tolerance | noun

crackdown: /ˈkrækdaʊn/
| n. | 镇压；打击；取缔；严厉的惩罚 | a sudden increase in official activity to enforce a law or rule. |  There has been a crackdown on speeding in the city. 城市里对超速行驶进行了严厉打击。| synonyms: offensive, blitz, campaign | antonyms:  laxity, permissiveness | noun
