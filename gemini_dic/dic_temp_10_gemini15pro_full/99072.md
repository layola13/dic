interauricular:/ˌɪntərɔːˈrɪkjʊlər/
adjective
心房间的，位于心房之间的
Located between the auricles of the heart.
The interauricular septum separates the two atria of the heart.  心房间隔将心脏的两个心房隔开。
Near synonyms: intra-atrial, interatrial
Antonyms: None (anatomical term, lacks a direct opposite)
词性: adjective
