lichened:/ˈlaɪkənd/ /ˈlɪtʃənd/ | adj. | 覆盖着地衣的；似地衣的 | Covered with lichens; resembling a lichen. | The lichened rocks provided a perfect camouflage for the small lizard.  覆盖着地衣的岩石为小蜥蜴提供了完美的伪装。| The old stone wall was lichened and crumbling.  古老的石墙覆盖着地衣，正在崩塌。 | 近义词: mossy, overgrown | 反义词: bare, clean | 词性: 形容词

词源: From lichen + -ed.  来自名词 lichen（地衣）加上形容词后缀 -ed。
