emblaze:/ɪmˈbleɪz/ | vt. | 使燃烧；使闪耀；激起；颂扬 | To cause to burn brightly or intensely; to kindle. To decorate or illuminate with bright colors or lights. To inspire with strong emotion. To celebrate or proclaim enthusiastically. | The setting sun emblazed the sky with vibrant hues. 夕阳用鲜艳的色彩照亮了天空。 | 近义词: ignite, kindle, inflame, illuminate, extol | 反义词: extinguish, quench, dim | 词性:动词

emblaze:/ɪmˈbleɪz/ | n. |  (罕见)火焰; 光辉 |  A bright fire or blaze. A state of being ablaze. | The emblaze of glory surrounded the hero.  荣耀的光辉环绕着英雄。（这个名词用法比较罕见）| 近义词: blaze, flame, fire | 反义词: darkness | 词性:名词
