guacharo:/ɡwɑːˈtʃɑːroʊ/ | n. |瓜查罗鸟（油鸱的一种）|A nocturnal bird (Stealornis caripensis) found in caves in northern South America, whose young are excessively fat and were formerly boiled down for their oil. |例句：The guacharo is a nocturnal bird that lives in caves. 瓜查罗鸟是一种生活在洞穴里的夜行动物。

例句：Guacharos navigate using echolocation, similar to bats. 瓜查罗鸟像蝙蝠一样利用回声定位导航。|近义词：oilbird |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
