geranium:/dʒəˈreɪniəm/ | n. | 天竺葵 |A flowering plant with colorful, usually red, pink, or white flowers, often grown in gardens or pots.  It belongs to the genus *Geranium*. | I have a beautiful red geranium blooming on my balcony.  我的阳台上有一株盛开的美丽的红色天竺葵。

My grandmother always had geraniums in her window boxes. 我祖母的窗台上总是摆着天竺葵。| 近义词：cranesbill (for certain types), pelargonium (for common garden varieties) | 反义词: N/A | 词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *geranium*, from Ancient Greek *γεράνιον (geránion)*,  meaning "crane", because the fruit resembles a crane's bill.  Ultimately from *γέρανος (géranos)* meaning "crane".
