councilor:/ˈkaʊnsələr (US), ˈkaʊnsələ (UK)| n.| 议员，顾问|A member of a council, especially a city council.  A person who gives advice.|例句：The city councilor voted against the new development project. 市议员投票反对新的开发项目。

例句：She sought the advice of a financial councilor. 她寻求了一位财务顾问的意见。|近义词：councilman, councilwoman, advisor, consultant|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
