craber:/ˈkreɪbər/ | n. |捕蟹人；爱抱怨的人|A person who catches crabs, or someone who complains a lot. | He's a craber by trade, spending his days out on the Chesapeake. 他以捕蟹为生，每天都在切萨皮克湾上度过。

He's such a craber, always finding something to complain about. 他真是个爱抱怨的人，总是能找到抱怨的事情。 | 近义词: complainer, whiner, fisherman (for the crab-catching sense) | 反义词: optimist (for the complaining sense) | 词性: noun
