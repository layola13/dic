cymry:/ˈkəmri/ | n. |威尔士人；威尔士语| The Cymry are the Welsh people. 威尔士人是凯尔特人的一支。|  The Eisteddfod is an important festival for the Cymry.  威尔士诗歌音乐节对威尔士人来说是一个重要的节日。 | 近义词: Welsh people, Welsh | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源:
From Welsh *kymry*, plural of *cymro* (“fellow countryman”), from Proto-Brythonic *kombrogi*, from Proto-Celtic *kom-brog-is*, meaning "compatriots".
