low-cost:/ˌloʊˈkɔːst/| adj.|低成本的，廉价的|Relatively inexpensive; charging low prices.|例句：We flew to Europe on a low-cost airline. 我们乘坐廉价航空公司飞往欧洲。|例句：Low-cost housing is desperately needed in this city.  这个城市急需廉价住房。|近义词：cheap, budget, inexpensive, affordable|反义词：expensive, costly, premium, high-end|词性:形容词

词源:  "low" (Old English "lāg," meaning "not high, short") + "cost" (from Old French "cost" and Latin "constare," meaning "to stand firm, cost")
