bends:/bɛndz/
n.|潜水夫病；减压病；肌肉疼痛|a condition that affects divers if they come to the surface too quickly, caused by nitrogen bubbles forming in the blood|The diver suffered from the bends after a rapid ascent. 潜水员快速上浮后患上了减压病。|近义词：decompression sickness, caisson disease|反义词:|名词

bend:/bɛnd/
v.|弯曲；屈服；专心于|to force something straight into a curved or angular shape; to become curved or angular|She bent down to tie her shoelace. 她弯下腰系鞋带。|近义词：curve, flex, bow|反义词：straighten|动词

bend:/bɛnd/
n.|弯曲；弯道|a curve or turn, especially in a road or river|There's a sharp bend in the road ahead. 前方道路有一个急弯。|近义词：curve, turn|反义词：straight|名词
