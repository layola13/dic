misshipping: /ˌmɪsˈʃɪpɪŋ/
misshipping |n.|  货物错运，误运 |The misshipping of the goods caused significant delays and extra costs.  货物错运导致了严重的延误和额外成本。|  The company apologized for the misshipping and offered a refund.  公司为误运道歉并提供了退款。 | incorrect shipping, wrong delivery | correct shipping, accurate delivery | noun

Etymology:
"mis-" (prefix of English and Germanic origin) meaning "wrongly," "badly," or "incorrectly" + "shipping" (noun derived from the verb "ship," meaning to transport goods)
