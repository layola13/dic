circumjacent:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈdʒeɪs(ə)nt/
adjective
周围的，邻近的
Surrounding; lying round about.
例句：The circumjacent fields were flooded. 周围的田地都被淹没了。

例句：The circumjacent area is known for its biodiversity.  周围地区以其生物多样性而闻名。

近义词：surrounding, neighboring, adjacent, bordering
反义词：distant, remote, faraway
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin circumjacentem, present participle of circumjacēre (to lie around), from circum (around) + jacēre (to lie).
