hyperemphasizing:/ˌhaɪpərˈɛmfəˌsaɪzɪŋ/
| v. |过分强调；过度强调|  Giving excessive emphasis to something; overstressing. | He was hyperemphasizing the importance of the meeting, making everyone nervous. 他过分强调了会议的重要性，让每个人都很紧张。| The media is hyperemphasizing the negative aspects of the story. 媒体过分强调了故事的负面。 | overemphasizing, overstressing, exaggerating | understating, downplaying | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (meaning "over, excessive") + emphasizing (present participle of emphasize, from French emphaser, from Greek emphasis "significance").
