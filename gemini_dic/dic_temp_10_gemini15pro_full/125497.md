multiradical:/ˌmʌltiˈrædɪkəl/
adjective|多基的，多自由基的|Containing or involving multiple radicals or free radicals.|Multiradical reactions are important in polymer chemistry. 多基反应在高分子化学中很重要。|polyradical|monoradical|adjective
Etymology->
"multi-" + "radical"
"multi-" : from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many"
"radical" : from Late Latin "radicalis" meaning "of or belonging to the root," from Latin "radix" meaning "root"
