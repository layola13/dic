ghostology:/ˌɡoʊstˈɒlədʒi/ | n. | 鬼魂学；幽灵学 | The study of ghosts, hauntings, and other paranormal phenomena.  | He was fascinated by ghostology and spent hours reading about haunted houses. 他对鬼魂学很着迷，花了几个小时阅读关于鬼屋的书籍。 |  Example 2:  She took a course in ghostology, hoping to learn more about the spirit world. 她参加了鬼魂学课程，希望能更多地了解精神世界。 | 近义词: parapsychology, psychical research | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "materialism" or "skepticism") | 词性: noun
