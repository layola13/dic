outdispatching:/ˌaʊtdɪˈspætʃɪŋ/
verb
超越调度; 调度胜过
The act of dispatching something or someone more efficiently or effectively than another.  This word implies a comparison or competition in the speed or skill of dispatching.
Our logistics team is focused on outdispatching the competition by implementing a new automated system. 我们的物流团队专注于通过实施新的自动化系统来超越竞争对手的调度效率。
The company prides itself on outdispatching its rivals in terms of delivery speed. 该公司以其在发货速度方面胜过竞争对手而自豪。
近义词: outperforming, surpassing, exceeding
反义词: lagging, falling behind
词性: verb (动词)
