disembosom:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbʊzəm/ | v. | 吐露，倾诉，公开| To reveal or disclose (thoughts or feelings).|  She finally disembosomed herself of her worries to her best friend. 她终于向她最好的朋友倾诉了她的烦恼。

She disembosomed her secret to her diary. 她把她的秘密倾诉给了她的日记。| 近义词：reveal, disclose, divulge, confess, unburden | 反义词：conceal, hide, suppress | 词性: 动词
