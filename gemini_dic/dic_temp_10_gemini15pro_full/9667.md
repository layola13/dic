antitarnish:/ˌæntiˈtɑːnɪʃ/
adjective
防锈的，防污的
Designed to prevent or inhibit tarnishing (discoloration or loss of luster) of metal surfaces, especially silver.
This antitarnish cloth helps keep your silver shining. 这块防锈布有助于保持你的银器光亮。
近义词:tarnish-resistant, non-tarnishing
反义词:tarnishable
词性:adjective

词源:
"anti-" (prefix meaning "against, opposing") + "tarnish" (verb meaning "to lose luster, discolor")
