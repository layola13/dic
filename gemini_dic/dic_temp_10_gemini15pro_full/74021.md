forwarder:/ˈfɔːrwərdər/ (US) /ˈfɔːwədə(r)/ (UK)| n.|货运代理人，转运公司|A person or company that arranges the transportation of goods for other companies.|We hired a forwarder to handle the shipping of our products overseas. 我们聘请了一家货运代理来处理我们产品的海外运输。|近义词：shipper, agent, carrier, transporter|反义词: consignee, receiver|noun

forwarder:/ˈfɔːrwərdər/ (US) /ˈfɔːwədə(r)/ (UK)| n.|转发器|A device that receives data on one communication channel and retransmits it on another.|The network uses a forwarder to send data between different segments. 该网络使用转发器在不同网段之间发送数据。|近义词：relay, repeater|反义词: N/A|noun
