decarbonizes:/diːˈkɑːrbənaɪzɪz/
verb
减少碳排放
to reduce or remove carbon, especially carbon dioxide, from something
Many countries are trying to decarbonize their economies. 许多国家正在努力减少其经济体的碳排放。
The company has pledged to decarbonize its operations by 2050. 该公司承诺到 2050 年实现运营脱碳。
Synonyms: reduce emissions, decrease carbon footprint, go green
Antonyms: increase emissions, pollute
