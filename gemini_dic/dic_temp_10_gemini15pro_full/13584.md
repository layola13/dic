attentive:/əˈtɛntɪv/| adj.|专心的，注意的，周到的|Paying close attention to something or someone; watchful.|例句：He was an attentive listener. 他是一位专注的倾听者。|例句：She was attentive to her guests' needs. 她对客人的需求很周到。|近义词：observant, alert, mindful, considerate|反义词：inattentive, distracted, negligent|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin *attent- ‘stretched out, intent’, from the verb attendere (see attend).
