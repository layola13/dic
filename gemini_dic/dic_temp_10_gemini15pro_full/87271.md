hermaphrodism:/hɜːrˈmæfrədɪzəm/ (US) /hɜːˈmæfrədɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |雌雄同体；两性畸形|The condition of having both male and female reproductive organs.|Hermaphrodism is rare in mammals. 哺乳动物中雌雄同体是罕见的。|Synonyms: intersex, bisexuality (loosely), androgyny (loosely)|Antonyms: unisexuality, gonochorism|noun
hermaphrodite:/hɜːrˈmæfrəˌdaɪt/ (US) /hɜːˈmæfrəˌdaɪt/ (UK)| n. adj. |雌雄同体者；两性体|An organism that has both male and female reproductive organs.|The earthworm is a hermaphrodite. 蚯蚓是雌雄同体的。|Synonyms: intersex individual, bisexual (loosely)|Antonyms: unisexual organism, gonochoric organism|noun, adjective
