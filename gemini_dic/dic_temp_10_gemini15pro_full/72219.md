fluorescent:/flʊəˈresənt/ /flɔːˈresnt/
adjective
荧光的；发荧光的
Emitting light during exposure to radiation, especially visible light.
The fish emitted a fluorescent green glow.  这条鱼发出荧光绿色的光芒。
The shop window was bathed in fluorescent light.  商店橱窗沐浴在荧光灯下。
synonyms: luminous, glowing, radiant, bright, shining, brilliant
antonyms: dull, dark, dim
word type: adjective
