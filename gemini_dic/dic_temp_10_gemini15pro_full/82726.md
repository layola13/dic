guidelines:/ˈɡaɪdlaɪnz/| n.|指导方针；准则；行动纲领|A set of rules or instructions that show which way something should be done or what something should be like.|The government has issued new guidelines on healthy eating. 政府发布了新的健康饮食指南。|synonyms: rules, regulations, principles, directions, instructions|antonyms:  absence of rules, lawlessness, deregulation|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "guide" + "lines". "Guide" comes from the Old French "guider" (to guide, lead, conduct), which ultimately comes from the Frankish "*witan" (to know). "Lines" comes from Old English "līne" (rope, string, line), which is related to Latin "līnea" (linen thread, line).
