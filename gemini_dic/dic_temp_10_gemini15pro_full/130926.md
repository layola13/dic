nonascetically:/ˌnɒnəˈsɛtɪkli/
adverb
非禁欲地；不苦行地
Not practicing asceticism; indulging in worldly pleasures.
He lived nonascetically, enjoying good food and wine. 他生活得并不禁欲，享受美食和美酒。
Synonyms: indulgently, luxuriously, hedonistically
Antonyms: ascetically, austerely
词性: 副词
