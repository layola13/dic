organogen:/ɔːrˈɡænədʒən/ | n. | 器官原; 器官形成素 | A substance that promotes the formation of organs in plants or animals. |  Plant hormones act as organogens, stimulating the development of roots, stems, and leaves. 植物激素作为器官形成素，刺激根、茎和叶的发育。|  Organogens play a crucial role in embryonic development. 器官形成素在胚胎发育中起着至关重要的作用。 | synonyms: growth factor, morphogen | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider inhibitors or teratogens which hinder development) | noun
