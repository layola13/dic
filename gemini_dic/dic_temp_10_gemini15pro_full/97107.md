infidelism:/ˌɪnfɪˈdɛlɪzəm/ | n. |不信神；不信教；无信仰；背教 | The state of being an infidel; disbelief in a particular religion, especially Christianity. | His infidelism caused him to be ostracized from the community. 他的不信教导致他被社区排斥。| The rise of infidelism in the 18th century. 18世纪不信教的兴起。 | 近义词: atheism, nonbelief, unbelief, skepticism, heresy | 反义词: faith, belief, piety, orthodoxy | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin infidelitas, from infidelis "unfaithful," from in- "not" + fidelis "faithful," from fides "faith."
