Ardennes:/ɑːrˈdɛn/ | n. | 阿登 | A heavily wooded and hilly region of northeast France, southeast Belgium, and Luxembourg, scene of several important battles. | The Battle of the Bulge was fought in the Ardennes forest during World War II.  二战期间，突出部战役发生在阿登森林。| Synonyms: forest, highlands, woods | Antonyms: plains, lowlands | noun

Ardennes:/ɑːrˈdɛn/ | n. | 阿登高地 | A plateau extending from northeastern France through southeast Belgium into Luxembourg.  阿登高地从法国东北部延伸，穿过比利时东南部，进入卢森堡。| Synonyms:  plateau, uplands | Antonyms: valleys, lowlands | noun
