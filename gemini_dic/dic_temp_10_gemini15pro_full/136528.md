nonrulings:/nɒnˈruːlɪŋz/| n.| 非裁决；非判决；未作裁决的事项 |Matters that have not been ruled or decided upon, often referring to legal or administrative decisions. |The committee released a summary of its nonrulings on various proposals.| 近义词:undecided matters, pending decisions | 反义词:rulings, decisions | 词性:名词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "ruling" (noun derived from the verb "rule," meaning to make an authoritative decision).
