campaigned:/kæmˈpeɪnd/
| v. | 参加竞选，开展运动 |  engaged in a campaign; worked in an organized and active way toward a particular goal. |  She campaigned tirelessly for equal rights. 她为争取平等权利不懈地努力。| synonyms: advocated, canvassed, lobbied, pushed, promoted | antonyms: opposed, resisted | verb

campaigned:/kæmˈpeɪnd/
| adj. (rare, attributive) | 用于竞选的，运动式的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a campaign. |  The campaigned slogans were plastered all over town.  竞选口号贴满了全城。 | synonyms: promotional, electioneering | antonyms:  non-political, routine | adjective
