albino:/ælˈbaɪnoʊ/ /ælˈbiːnəʊ/| n. adj.|白化病患者，白化体；白化的|A person or animal with albinism, a condition characterized by a lack of pigment in the skin, hair, and eyes.|例句：The albino rabbit stood out against the dark grass. 白兔在深色的草丛中格外显眼。

例句：Albino animals are rare in the wild. 白化动物在野外很罕见。|近义词：amelanistic|反义词:pigmented|词性:名词，形容词
