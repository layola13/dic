gymnosperm:/ˈdʒɪmnəˌspɜːrm/| n. |裸子植物|A plant that has seeds unprotected by an ovary or fruit. Gymnosperms include the conifers, cycads, and ginkgoes.|例句：Conifers, such as pines and spruces, are typical gymnosperms. 松树和云杉等针叶树是典型的裸子植物。

例句：The seeds of gymnosperms develop on the surface of scales or leaves, often modified to form cones. 裸子植物的种子生长在鳞片或叶子的表面，这些鳞片或叶子通常特化为球果。|近义词：acrogynous plant|反义词：angiosperm (被子植物)|词性：noun
