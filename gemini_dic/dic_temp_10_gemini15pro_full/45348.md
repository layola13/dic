cumulonimbus:/ˌkjuːmjʊləˈnɪmbəs/ /ˌkjuːmjʊloʊˈnɪmbəs/| n. |积雨云|A dense, towering vertical cloud characterized by its dark base and often a flat, anvil-shaped top. It is associated with thunderstorms and other severe weather.|例句：The cumulonimbus loomed large and dark, promising a heavy downpour.  积雨云显得又大又黑，预示着将有一场倾盆大雨。
