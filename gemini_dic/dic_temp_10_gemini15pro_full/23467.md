bragger:/ˈbræɡər/| n.|吹牛者，自夸者|A person who boasts about their achievements or possessions.|He's such a bragger, always going on about how much money he makes. 他真是个爱吹牛的人，总是炫耀自己赚了多少钱。|近义词：boaster, show-off|反义词：humble person, modest person|noun


brag:/bræɡ/| v. n.|吹牛，自夸|To talk boastfully about oneself or one's achievements.|He bragged about his new car. 他吹嘘他的新车。|近义词：boast, show off|反义词：be modest, downplay|verb, noun
