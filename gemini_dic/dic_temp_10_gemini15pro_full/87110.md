herapathite:/ˈhɛrəpəθaɪt/
noun
硫酸碘奎宁
a crystalline compound of quinine, iodine, and sulfuric acid, exhibiting strong polarizing properties.
Herapathite crystals were used in the first polaroid filters.  硫酸碘奎宁晶体被用于第一批偏光滤镜中。
Synonyms: iodoquinine sulfate
Etymology: Named after William Bird Herapath (1820-1868), a Bristol surgeon and chemist who discovered it.
