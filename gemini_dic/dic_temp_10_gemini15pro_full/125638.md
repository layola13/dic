mumbo-jumbo:/ˈmʌmboʊ ˈdʒʌmboʊ/| n.| 莫名其妙的话; 繁琐而无意义的仪式; 咒语; 胡言乱语|Meaningless or confusing speech or ritual; nonsense.|例句：The politician's speech was just a lot of mumbo-jumbo. 这位政客的演讲只是一堆胡言乱语。|例句：The witch doctor performed some mumbo-jumbo over the sick child.  巫医对生病的孩子念了一些咒语。|近义词：nonsense, gibberish, claptrap, balderdash|反义词:sense, meaning|词性:名词
