commissioner:/kəˈmɪʃənər/| n.|委员；专员；行政长官；地方长官|A commissioner is a person who is appointed to a position of authority in an organization or government, often to oversee a specific area of activity or to represent a particular group.|The police commissioner announced a new crime prevention program. 警察局长宣布了一项新的犯罪预防计划。|synonyms: administrator, official, executive, representative, agent, delegate, deputy, envoy, superintendent|antonyms: subordinate, employee|noun

commissioner:/kəˈmɪʃənər/| n.|（体育运动的）理事；委员|In sports, a commissioner is the highest-ranking executive officer of a league or other sports organization.|The baseball commissioner suspended the player for using steroids. 棒球理事暂停了该球员使用类固醇的资格。|synonyms: administrator, director, governor, president|antonyms: player, athlete|noun
