hyperparathyroidism:/ˌhaɪpərˌpærəˈθaɪrɔɪdɪzəm/ | n. | 甲状旁腺功能亢进症 | A condition in which the parathyroid glands produce too much parathyroid hormone, leading to high levels of calcium in the blood. |  She was diagnosed with hyperparathyroidism after experiencing persistent fatigue and muscle weakness. 她因持续疲劳和肌肉无力被诊断为甲状旁腺功能亢进症。 | hypercalcemia | hypoparathyroidism | noun

hyperparathyroid:/ˌhaɪpərˌpærəˈθaɪrɔɪd/ | adj. | 甲状旁腺功能亢进的 | Relating to or affected by hyperparathyroidism. |  Hyperparathyroid patients often have high blood calcium levels. 甲状旁腺功能亢进的患者通常血液中钙含量高。 | overactive parathyroid | underactive parathyroid | adjective
