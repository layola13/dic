geography:/dʒiˈɒɡrəfi/ /dʒiˈɑːɡrəfi/| n.|地理，地理学|The study of the earth and its features, and of the distribution of life on the earth, including human life and the effects of human activity.|例句：The geography of the region is varied, with mountains, forests, and rivers. 这个地区地理多样，有山脉、森林和河流。|例句：She studied geography in college. 她在大学学习地理。|近义词： topography, terrain|反义词:|名词

etymology->
From Old French geographie, from Latin geographia, from Ancient Greek γεωγραφία (geōgraphía, “geography”), from γῆ (gê, “earth”) + γράφω (gráphō, “I write”).
