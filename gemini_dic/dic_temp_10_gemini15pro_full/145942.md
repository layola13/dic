overdiscouraging:/ˌoʊvər dɪsˈkɜːrɪdʒɪŋ/
adjective
极其令人沮丧的
Excessively discouraging; so discouraging as to be overwhelming or debilitating.
The constant rejection was overdiscouraging, and he almost gave up on his dream.  不断的拒绝让他极其沮丧，他几乎放弃了自己的梦想。
The overdiscouraging news led her to tears.  这极其令人沮丧的消息让她流下了眼泪。
近义词: disheartening, demoralizing, dispiriting
反义词: encouraging, inspiring, uplifting
词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "discouraging" (from "discourage" - from Old French "descoragier" - from "des-" (expressing negation) + "corage" (courage)).
