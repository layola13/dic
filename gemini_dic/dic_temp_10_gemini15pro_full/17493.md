bearishnesses:/ˈbɛərɪʃnɪsɪz/| n. |看跌；悲观情绪|The state of being bearish; pessimistic outlook, especially in financial markets. |The recent bearishnesses in the market have led many investors to sell their stocks. 最近市场上的悲观情绪导致许多投资者抛售股票。| The general bearishnesses surrounding the company's future made it difficult to attract new investors.  围绕公司未来的普遍悲观情绪使其难以吸引新的投资者。| 近义词：pessimism, negativity, gloom, bearishness | 反义词：optimism, bullishness, positivity | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From bearish + -ness.  Bearish itself comes from the bear market analogy, referring to the way a bear swipes downwards.
