marsipobranch:/ˌmɑːrsɪˈpoʊbræŋk/ | n. | 袋形动物;无颌类;圆口纲动物 |A member of the superclass Agnatha, which includes the lampreys and hagfishes. These aquatic vertebrates lack jaws and paired fins, and have a cartilaginous skeleton. |  Lampreys and hagfishes are marsipobranchs. 七鳃鳗和盲鳗是圆口纲动物。 | synonyms: agnathan, cyclostome | antonyms: gnathostome | noun
