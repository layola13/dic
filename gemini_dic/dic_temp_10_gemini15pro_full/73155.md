forbear:/fɔːrˈber/ (US) /fɔːˈbeə(r)/ (UK)| v.|克制，忍耐；避免|To refrain from; to abstain.|He forbore from criticizing her work, even though he thought it was poorly done. 即使他认为她的工作做得不好，他也克制住不去批评。|Synonyms: refrain, abstain, desist, withhold|Antonyms: indulge, yield, succumb|verb

forbear:/fɔːrˈber/ (US) /fɔːˈbeə(r)/ (UK)| n.|祖先，先人|An ancestor.|His forbears came to America in the 17th century. 他的祖先在17世纪来到美国。|Synonyms: ancestor, forefather, progenitor|Antonyms: descendant, offspring|noun
