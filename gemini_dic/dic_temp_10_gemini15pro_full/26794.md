caffeinated:/ˈkæfɪneɪtɪd/| adj.|含咖啡因的，加了咖啡因的|Containing caffeine; stimulated by caffeine.|例句：I need a caffeinated beverage to wake me up. 我需要一杯含咖啡因的饮料来唤醒我。|近义词：stimulated, energized|反义词：decaffeinated, caffeine-free|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From caffeine + -ated.  Caffeine ultimately derives from Arabic *qahwa*, meaning "coffee."
