extravagance: /ɪkˈstrævəɡəns/ | n. | 奢侈，挥霍；过度；放肆的言行 |  Lack of restraint in spending money or using resources.  Going beyond what is reasonable or necessary. |  Her extravagance in buying designer clothes left her with little money for rent. 她挥霍无度地购买名牌服装，导致房租所剩无几。 | synonyms: lavishness, prodigality, profligacy, immoderation | antonyms: frugality, thrift, economy, moderation | noun

extravagant: /ɪkˈstrævəɡənt/ | adj. | 奢侈的，挥霍的；过度的；放肆的 |  Spending much more than is necessary or wise; exceeding reasonable limits. |  They lived an extravagant lifestyle, with multiple homes and luxury cars. 他们过着奢侈的生活，拥有多处房产和豪车。 | synonyms: lavish, profuse, exorbitant, immoderate | antonyms: frugal, thrifty, economical, moderate | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *extravagantem (nominative extravagans) "wandering outside or beyond," present participle of extravagari "wander beyond," from Latin extra "outside" + vagari "to wander".
