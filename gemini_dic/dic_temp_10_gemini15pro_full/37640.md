commutatively:/kəˈmjuːtətɪvli/| adv.|交换地，可交换地|In a commutative operation, the order of the operands does not affect the result.|The numbers can be added commutatively. 这些数字可以交换相加。|近义词: interchangeably, reciprocally|反义词: noncommutatively|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From commute (v.) + -ative + -ly.  The verb "commute" comes from Latin "commutare"  meaning "to change, exchange, interchange".
