burrer:/ˈbɜːrər/ (US), /ˈbʌrər/ (UK)| n. |去除毛刺的人/机器；去毛刺机|A person or machine that removes burrs (rough edges or ridges) from metal or other materials.|The burrer smoothed the edges of the newly cut metal. 去毛刺机磨平了新切割金属的边缘。|近义词：deburrer, finisher|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

词源:  Derived from the verb "burr" (meaning "to remove burrs"), which in turn comes from the noun "burr" (referring to a rough edge or ridge). Ultimately from Middle English *burre*, of uncertain origin.
