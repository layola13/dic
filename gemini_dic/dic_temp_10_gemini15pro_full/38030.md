componental:/kəmˈpoʊnəntəl/| adj.|成分的，组成的|Relating to or consisting of components; constituent.|The componental structure of the machine is complex.  这台机器的组件结构很复杂。|近义词：constituent, component, elemental|反义词: holistic, complete|词性: adjective

Etymology:  From "component" + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Component" comes from Late Latin "compōnēns", present participle of "compōnere" (to put together), from "com-" (together) + "pōnere" (to put, place).
