antiexpansionist:/ˌæntiɪkˈspænʃənɪst/
adjective
反扩张主义的；反对扩张的
Opposed to expansion, especially territorial expansion.
The senator was a staunch antiexpansionist, arguing against any further military interventions abroad.  参议员是一位坚定的反扩张主义者，反对任何进一步的军事干预。
The antiexpansionist movement gained traction as the costs of war became clear.  随着战争代价的显现，反扩张主义运动获得了支持。
Synonyms: noninterventionist, isolationist
Antonyms: expansionist, imperialist
