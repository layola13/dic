outbartering:/ˌaʊtˈbɑːrtərɪŋ/
|v.|胜过(某人)的交易; 通过比…更精明的交易而胜过|To surpass someone in bartering or trading; to outdo or get the better of someone in an exchange of goods or services.|He outbartered the rug merchant and got a much better price.|近义词：outtrade, outsmart, outmaneuver|反义词: be outmaneuvered, be outsmarted|Verb
