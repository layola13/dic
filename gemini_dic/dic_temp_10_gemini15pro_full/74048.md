fosse:/fɒs/ (UK) /fɑːs/ (US)| n.|壕沟，舞池|A long, narrow trench or ditch dug as a fortification or drain, especially in a military context. Also, a depressed area in a stage floor for musicians or dancers. |The dancers moved gracefully in the fosse. 舞者在舞池中优雅地移动。|近义词：ditch, trench, moat, pit|反义词:ridge, mound|noun

fosse:/fɒs/ (UK) /fɑːs/ (US)| n.|窝，穴|A den or lair of a wild animal.|The fox retreated to its fosse. 狐狸退回到它的窝里。|近义词：den, lair, burrow|反义词:exposure, open air|noun
