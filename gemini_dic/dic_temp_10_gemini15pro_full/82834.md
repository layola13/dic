gulchin:/ˈɡʊltʃɪn/ | n. | 沟壑，峡谷; 屠宰场; 杀戮; 毁灭 | A deep ravine or gully, often formed by water erosion. Can also metaphorically refer to a place of slaughter or destruction. | The hikers carefully descended into the gulchin.  徒步旅行者小心翼翼地走下峡谷。| The war turned the city into a gulchin of human life. 战争把这座城市变成了人间地狱。 | synonyms: ravine, gorge, canyon, abyss, chasm, slaughterhouse, massacre | antonyms: peak, summit, mountain, creation, life | noun
