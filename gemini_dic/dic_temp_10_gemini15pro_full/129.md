abandoner: /əˈbændənər/
n.
遗弃者，放弃者
A person who abandons something or someone.
The child felt like an abandoner when he left his dog at the shelter.  这个孩子把他的狗留在收容所时，感觉自己像个遗弃者。

He was known as an abandoner of lost causes. 他以放弃无望事业而闻名。

近义词: deserter, forsaker
反义词: rescuer, savior
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From abandon + -er.  来自动词abandon 加上名词后缀 -er.  Abandon comes from Old French abandoner, from a- (from Latin ad-) + bandon "control, jurisdiction, power," from Frankish *bandon "power, proclamation."
