doveling:/ˈdʌvəlɪŋ/| n.|雏鸽，幼鸽；鸽子般柔弱的人|a young dove; a gentle, innocent person|例句：The doveling huddled in the nest.  雏鸽蜷缩在巢里。

例句：She protected her child like a doveling. 她像保护雏鸽一样保护她的孩子。|近义词：squab, fledgling, chick|反义词: adult dove|词性:noun
