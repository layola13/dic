horrifically:/hɒˈrɪfɪkli/ /hɔːˈrɪfɪkli/
adverb
可怕地；恐怖地
in a horrific manner; extremely badly.
The play was horrifically bad.  这出戏糟透了。
The animal was horrifically injured. 这只动物受了重伤。
近义词:terribly, awfully, dreadfully, frightfully, appallingly
反义词:wonderfully, beautifully, delightfully
词性:adverb

词源:
From horrific + -ly.  Horrific comes from Late Latin horrificus "causing horror," from Latin horrere "to bristle, shudder with fear"  + -ficus.
