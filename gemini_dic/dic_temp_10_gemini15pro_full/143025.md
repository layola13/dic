verb

The sound of the lambs outbleating the older sheep filled the air. 羊羔的叫声盖过了老羊的声音，充满了空气。
Outbleating his rivals, the young politician secured the nomination.  这位年轻的政治家以其雄辩的口才击败了对手，获得了提名。
近义词: outshouting, overpowering, drowning out
反义词: being drowned out, being overpowered
词性: verb (present participle)

outbleat: /ˌaʊtˈbliːt/
verb
(羊叫声)盖过，大声胜过
The shepherd's voice was outbleated by the frantic bleating of the sheep. 牧羊人的声音被羊群惊恐的叫声盖过了。
She outbleated the other protesters, making her message heard. 她用更大的声音盖过了其他抗议者，让自己的信息被听到。
近义词: outshout, overpower, drown out
反义词: be drowned out, be overpowered
词性: verb

Etymology: "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "bleat" (verb meaning "to make the cry of a sheep or goat").  由表示“超过，胜过”的前缀 "out-" 和动词 "bleat"（意为“发出羊或山羊的叫声”）组成。
