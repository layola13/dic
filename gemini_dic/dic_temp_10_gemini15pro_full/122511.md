mocking:/ˈmɑːkɪŋ/ (US) /ˈmɒkɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|嘲笑的，嘲弄的；模仿的|Making fun of someone or something, often by imitating them in a cruel or disrespectful way; imitating. Also can describe something that resembles something else, often in a superficial or misleading way.|He gave her a mocking smile. 他给了她一个嘲弄的微笑。

The mockingbird imitated the other birds' songs perfectly.  这只模仿鸟完美地模仿了其他鸟类的歌声。

Her mocking laughter echoed through the hall. 她嘲弄的笑声回荡在大厅里。

The fake painting was a mocking imitation of the original masterpiece.  这幅赝品是对原作拙劣的模仿。|近义词：sarcastic, derisive, scornful, imitating, mimicking|反义词：respectful, admiring, genuine, sincere|词性: adjective
