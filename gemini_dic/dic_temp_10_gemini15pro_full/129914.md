non-judicial:/ˌnɒn dʒuːˈdɪʃəl/ | adj. | 非司法的，非裁判性的 | Not involving or relating to a court of law or the administration of justice. |  Example: The dispute was settled through non-judicial means.  这起纠纷通过非司法途径得到解决。 | Example:  A non-judicial foreclosure avoids the court system. 非司法止赎避免了法院系统。 | Synonyms: extrajudicial, nonlegal, informal, unofficial | Antonyms: judicial, legal, official | adjective
