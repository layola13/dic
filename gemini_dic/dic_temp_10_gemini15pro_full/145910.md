overdignifies: /ˌoʊvərˈdɪɡnɪˌfaɪz/
verb
过分抬举，过分尊崇
To treat (someone or something) with more dignity or respect than is appropriate or deserved.
He overdignifies the role of the critic in shaping public taste. 他过分抬举了评论家在塑造公众品味方面的作用。
近义词: exalt, elevate, aggrandize
反义词: belittle, demean, disparage
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + dignify.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much."  "Dignify" comes from the Latin word "dignitas" meaning "worth," "dignity."
