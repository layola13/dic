galah:/ˈɡælə/ (AU), /ɡəˈlɑː/ (US)| n.|凤头鹦鹉；傻瓜，蠢人|A noisy and conspicuous Australian cockatoo, often used figuratively to refer to a foolish or eccentric person.|例句：The galah squawked loudly from the treetop. 凤头鹦鹉在树梢大声鸣叫。

例句：He acted like a right galah, forgetting his own name. 他表现得像个十足的傻瓜，竟然忘了自己的名字。|近义词：fool, idiot, clown, nitwit (informal)|反义词：genius, intellectual|词性：名词
