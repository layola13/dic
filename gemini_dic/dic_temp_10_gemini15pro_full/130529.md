nonaccrued:/ˌnɒnəˈkruːd/
adjective
未累计的，未应计的
Not having accrued; not yet due or payable.
The nonaccrued interest will be paid at maturity. 未累计利息将在到期时支付。
Dividends declared but not yet payable are classified as nonaccrued. 已宣布但尚未支付的股息被归类为未累计。
Synonyms: outstanding, unpaid, due, pending
Antonyms: accrued, paid, received
词性: 形容词
