anticlogging:/ˌæntiˈklɒɡɪŋ/ /ˌæntiˈklɑːɡɪŋ/
adjective
防堵塞的，防阻塞的
Designed or made to prevent clogging.
This shampoo is anticlogging and helps to keep your drains clear.  这款洗发水具有防堵塞功能，有助于保持排水管畅通。
The new drain design is anticlogging and prevents hair from building up.  新的排水管设计具有防堵塞功能，可防止头发堆积。
non-clogging, clog-resistant
clogging, blocking
