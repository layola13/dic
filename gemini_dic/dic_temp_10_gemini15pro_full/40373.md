contradicter:/ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktər/ | n. | 反驳者，驳斥者 |  A person who contradicts or denies something. | He's a known contradicter and enjoys arguing for the sake of it. 他是一个有名的抬杠者，喜欢为了争论而争论。 | Synonyms: opponent, gainsayer, challenger, disputant | Antonyms: supporter, advocate, upholder | noun

contradict:/ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪkt/ | v. | 反驳，驳斥；与…矛盾，与…抵触 | To deny the truth of (something); to assert the opposite of (something); to be in conflict with (something). |  The witness's testimony contradicted the defendant's alibi. 证人的证词与被告的不在场证明相矛盾。| Synonyms: deny, dispute, challenge, gainsay, refute | Antonyms: confirm, support, corroborate, verify | verb
