inheritor:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtər/ | n. |继承人|A person who inherits property, a title, or a quality from someone else.|例句：He is the sole inheritor of his father's fortune. 他是他父亲财产的唯一继承人。

例句：She is the inheritor of a great musical tradition. 她继承了伟大的音乐传统。

近义词：heir, successor, beneficiary|反义词:predecessor, testator|词性:noun
