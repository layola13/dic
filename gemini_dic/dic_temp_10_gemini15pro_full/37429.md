commingle:/kəˈmɪŋɡəl/
verb
混合，掺杂
To mix together; to blend.
The company commingled customer funds with its own operating capital.  这家公司将客户资金与其自有运营资本混合在一起。
Investors should avoid commingling personal funds with business accounts. 投资者应避免将个人资金与企业账户混合。
mix, blend, combine, merge
separate, divide, sort
v.
