continuousness:/kənˈtɪnjuəsnəs/ | n. | 连续性，持续性 | The state or quality of being continuous; uninterrupted extension in time, space, or sequence. | The continuousness of the noise made it difficult to concentrate.  噪音的持续性使人难以集中注意力。 | 近义词: continuity, persistence, duration | 反义词: discontinuity, interruption, cessation | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From continuous + -ness. Continuous comes from Late Latin continuus, from Latin continere "to hang together," from com- "together" + tenere "to hold."
