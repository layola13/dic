coulee:/ˈkuːliː/
n.
深谷，峡谷；熔岩流；干涸的河床
A deep ravine or gulch, often dry in summer.  A stream of lava. The dry bed of a stream.
The Grand Coulee is a spectacular example of a glacial coulee.  大古力是一个壮观的冰川峡谷的例子。

The coulee was filled with solidified lava. 这条峡谷充满了凝固的熔岩。
近义词:canyon, ravine, gulch, channel
反义词:ridge, peak, summit
词性:名词
