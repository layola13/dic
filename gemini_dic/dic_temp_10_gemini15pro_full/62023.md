enarmed:/ɪˈnɑːrmd/
adjective
着迷的，迷恋的
Filled with love and delight; captivated.
She was completely enamored with the handsome stranger. 她完全被这位英俊的陌生人迷住了。

enamored of/with/by
He became enamored of the idea of starting his own business. 他迷上了自己创业的想法。
She was enamored with the beauty of the mountains. 她被群山的美丽所吸引。
The audience was enamored by the singer's powerful voice. 观众被歌手充满力量的嗓音所迷住。

近义词: captivated, charmed, enchanted, fascinated, infatuated
反义词: disenchanted, disillusioned, repulsed, indifferent
词性: adjective
词源: From enamour + -ed.  Ultimately from the Old French word *enamorer*, meaning "to inflame with love."
