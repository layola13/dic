panegyrical: /ˌpænɪˈdʒɪrɪkəl/ | adj. | 颂扬的，赞美的 | Expressing or containing high praise; eulogistic. |  The CEO delivered a panegyrical speech about the company's founder. 首席执行官发表了歌颂公司创始人的演讲。

The book was a panegyrical account of the president's life. 这本书是对总统生平的赞美之词。

| 近义词：laudatory, eulogistic, complimentary, acclamatory | 反义词：critical, derogatory, defamatory | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *panegyricus*, from Greek panēgyrikos "of or for a festival assembly," from pan- "all" + agyris "assembly," from ageirein "to gather."
