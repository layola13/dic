gypsums:/ˈdʒɪpsəmz/| n. |石膏（复数）|A soft white or gray mineral consisting of hydrated calcium sulfate.  It is used to make plaster of Paris and fertilizers, and occurs in various forms, including alabaster.|The gypsums were mined from a quarry. 这些石膏是从采石场开采出来的。|Synonyms: plaster, plaster of Paris, sulfate of lime|Antonyms: N/A|noun
