alcalde:/ælˈkɑːldeɪ/
n.
mayor; magistrate
A Spanish or Portuguese mayor or magistrate.
The alcalde addressed the townspeople.  市长向市民发表了讲话。
近义词：mayor, magistrate
反义词:citizen, resident
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *alcalde*, from Arabic *al-qāḍī* (“the judge”).
