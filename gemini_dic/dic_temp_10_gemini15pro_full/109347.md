leninists:/ˈlɛnɪnɪsts/
noun
列宁主义者
Followers of the political and economic theories of Vladimir Lenin, which include the establishment of a dictatorship of the proletariat as a political prelude to the establishment of communism.
Leninists believe in a vanguard party to lead the revolution.
例句：The Leninists seized power in Russia in 1917. 列宁主义者在1917年夺取了俄国的政权。

例句：Some historians argue that the Leninists betrayed the true ideals of the revolution. 一些历史学家认为，列宁主义者背叛了革命的真正理想。

近义词：communists, Marxists, Bolsheviks
反义词：capitalists, anti-communists
