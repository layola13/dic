glenoid:/ˈɡliːnɔɪd/| adj. n.|盂形的，关节窝的；关节窝|Relating to or resembling a socket, particularly the socket of the shoulder joint; a shallow cavity, especially the glenoid cavity of the scapula.|The glenoid cavity articulates with the head of the humerus to form the shoulder joint.|近义词：articular, socket-like|反义词:convex |词性:形容词，名词

glenoid:/ˈɡliːnɔɪd/| n. |关节窝|A shallow cavity, especially the glenoid cavity of the scapula.|The humerus fits into the glenoid fossa of the scapula.|近义词：cavity, fossa|反义词:process, condyle|词性:名词
