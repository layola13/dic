out-of-the-loop:/ˌaʊt əv ðə ˈluːp/ | adj. |  不知情的；不了解情况的；被排除在外的 | Not informed about something; unaware of information known to others; excluded from a group or process. |  "I've been out-of-the-loop on this project for a while now." 我有一段时间没参与这个项目了，所以不太了解情况。 |  Synonyms: uninformed, unaware, out of touch, excluded, in the dark | Antonyms: informed, aware, in the loop, included, up to date | adjective
