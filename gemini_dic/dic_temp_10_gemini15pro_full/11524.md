aril:/ˈærɪl/| n. |假种皮|A fleshy growth, often brightly colored, that partly or wholly envelops the seed of some plants, such as the yew and nutmeg.|The mace we use in cooking is the aril of the nutmeg seed. 肉豆蔻的假种皮就是我们烹饪中使用的肉豆蔻衣。|近义词:seed covering, pulp|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "seed coat" as it is a distinct structure)|词性:noun
