misclaimed:/mɪsˈkleɪmd/
verb
错误地认领；不实认领
incorrectly claimed; claimed falsely or wrongly
The lost luggage was misclaimed by another passenger.  另一名乘客错误地认领了丢失的行李。
The property was misclaimed by the squatters.  这块地产被擅自占用者不实认领。
Synonyms: misappropriate, usurp, wrongly claimed
Antonyms: rightfully claimed, correctly identified
part of speech: verb
