epididymitis:/ˌepɪdɪdɪˈmaɪtɪs/
n.|附睾炎|Inflammation of the epididymis. The epididymis is a coiled tube located at the back of the testicle that stores and carries sperm. Epididymitis is commonly caused by bacterial infections, including sexually transmitted infections (STIs) like chlamydia and gonorrhea. In older men, it can be caused by urinary tract infections. Symptoms may include scrotal pain, swelling, redness, and tenderness.|He experienced discomfort in his scrotum and was later diagnosed with epididymitis. 他感到阴囊不适，后来被诊断出患有附睾炎。|
近义词:orchitis (inflammation of the testicle, often occurs with epididymitis)|
反义词: (No direct antonym for a disease)|
词性:noun
