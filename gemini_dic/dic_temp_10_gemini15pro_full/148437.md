overstimulation:/ˌoʊvərˌstɪmjəˈleɪʃn/| n. |过度刺激|Excessive stimulation; bombardment with too many stimuli.|The constant noise and bright lights led to overstimulation and a feeling of anxiety.  持续不断的噪音和明亮的灯光导致过度刺激和焦虑感。|She found the city's constant activity and noise overwhelming, a clear case of sensory overstimulation. 她发现城市的持续活动和噪音令人难以忍受，这是一个明显的感官过度刺激的例子。|近义词：hyperstimulation, bombardment|反义词:understimulation, deprivation|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + stimulation.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much". "Stimulation" comes from the Latin word "stimulare," meaning "to prick, incite, or urge."
