pait:/peɪt/ | n. | 腹痛，腹部 |  A pain felt in the abdomen. |  He felt a sharp pait after eating too much. 他吃太多后感到一阵剧烈的腹痛。 | 近义词: stomach ache, abdominal pain | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 well-being, comfort) | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely a variant spelling of "pate," which in Middle English referred to the top of the head, but could also refer to the belly or stomach.  This connection likely stems from the perceived vulnerability of both areas.  The spelling "pait" is now considered archaic or dialectal.
