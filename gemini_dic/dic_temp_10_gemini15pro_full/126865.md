napped:/næpt/| v. |小睡，打盹|To sleep lightly or briefly, especially during the day.|例句：The baby napped for an hour after lunch.  婴儿午饭后小睡了一个小时。

例句：I napped on the train. 我在火车上打了个盹。

近义词：dozed, slept, slumbered|反义词:stayed awake, worked|词性:动词
