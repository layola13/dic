glandular:/ˈɡlændʒələr/| adj.|腺的；腺状的|Relating to or involving glands, or resembling a gland in appearance or function.|The glandular tissue secretes hormones. 腺组织分泌激素。|Synonyms: glandular, secretory|Antonyms: non-glandular|adjective

glandulous:/ˈɡlændʒʊləs/| adj.|多腺的；腺发达的|Having many or prominent glands; glandular.|Some plants have glandulous hairs that secrete sticky substances.  一些植物有多腺的毛，会分泌粘性物质。|Synonyms: glandular, secretory|Antonyms: non-glandulous|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *glandulosus, from Latin glandula (“little acorn, gland”) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives of abundance).  Ultimately from glans (“acorn”).
