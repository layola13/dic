flawn:/flɔːn/
noun
瑕疵，缺点，缺陷
a mark, fault, or other imperfection that mars a substance or object.
The only flawn in his character is his impatience. 他性格中唯一的缺点就是不耐烦。
近义词:defect, blemish, fault, imperfection, shortcoming
反义词:perfection, strength, virtue
词性:n.


flawed:/flɔːd/
adjective
有缺陷的，有瑕疵的
having or characterized by a flaw.
The reasoning behind this decision is seriously flawed.  这个决定的理由存在严重缺陷。
近义词:defective, imperfect, faulty, blemished
反义词:perfect, flawless, impeccable
词性:adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *flaga (“slab of stone, flake”), from Proto-Germanic *flagō (“piece, fragment, turf”). Cognate with Old English flōh (“fragment, splinter”), Old High German flah (“flat”), German Flachs (“flax”).
