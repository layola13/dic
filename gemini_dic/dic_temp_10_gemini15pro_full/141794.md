orbation: /ɔːrˈbeɪʃən/
n.
剥夺权利，剥夺公民权;褫夺圣职
Deprivation of rights or privileges, especially the right to vote or hold office; deprivation of priestly functions.
He was sentenced to five years of orbation. 他被判处剥夺公民权五年。
近义词: disenfranchisement, deprivation, forfeiture
反义词: enfranchisement, restitution
词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin *orbationem, from Latin orbare "to bereave, deprive," from orbus "bereft, orphaned."
