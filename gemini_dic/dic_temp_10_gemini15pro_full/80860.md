gourmand:/ˈɡʊərmɑːnd/ (US), /ˈɡɔːmənd/ (UK)| n.| 美食家；贪吃的人 |A person who enjoys eating and often eats too much.| He's a gourmand who loves to try new restaurants. 他是一位喜欢尝试新餐馆的美食家。|Synonyms: epicure, gourmet, glutton| Antonyms: ascetic, abstainer| Noun

gourmands:/ˈɡʊərmɑːndz/ (US), /ˈɡɔːməndz/ (UK)| n.| 美食家们；贪吃的人们 |Plural form of gourmand.| The gourmands sampled every dish on the menu. 这些美食家品尝了菜单上的每一道菜。|Synonyms: epicures, gourmets, gluttons| Antonyms: ascetics, abstainers| Noun
