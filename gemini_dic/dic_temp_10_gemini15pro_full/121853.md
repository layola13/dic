misrepresentation:/ˌmɪsrɛprɪzɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 歪曲；误传；虚假陈述 |  The act of giving a false or misleading account of the nature of something. |  His comments were a gross misrepresentation of the facts. 他的评论是对事实的严重歪曲。| synonyms: distortion, falsification, misstatement | antonyms: accuracy, truth, correctness | noun

misrepresent: /ˌmɪsrɛprɪˈzɛnt/ | vt. | 歪曲；误传；虚报 |  Give a false or misleading account of the nature of something. |  The advertisement misrepresents the actual size of the product.  这则广告歪曲了产品的实际尺寸。| synonyms: distort, falsify, misstate | antonyms: portray accurately, represent truthfully | verb
