incurable:/ɪnˈkjʊərəbl/| adj.|无法治愈的，不可救药的|Not able to be cured or healed.  This describes a condition, usually medical, that cannot be remedied.  It can also be used figuratively.|The disease is incurable at this stage.  这种疾病在这个阶段是无法治愈的。|Synonyms: irremediable, terminal, hopeless|Antonyms: curable, remediable|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *incurable, from Old French incurable, from Latin incūrābilis, from in- (“not”) + cūrābilis (“curable”).
