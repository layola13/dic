nonnegligible:/ˌnɒnˈnɛɡlɪdʒəb(ə)l/
adjective
不可忽略的；重要的
Not negligible; significant enough to be worth considering; too important to be ignored.
The impact of the new policy was nonnegligible. 新政策的影响是不可忽略的。
The cost of the repairs was nonnegligible, so we had to budget carefully.  维修费用不容忽视，所以我们不得不仔细预算。
近义词:significant, substantial, considerable, appreciable, material
反义词:negligible, insignificant, trivial, minor
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + negligible (from Latin negligibilis, from negligere "to neglect," from nec "not" + legere "to gather, choose")
