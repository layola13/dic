easeless:/ˈiːslɪs/| adj.|不停的，不懈的，持续不断的|Characterized by incessant or relentless activity; never ceasing.|The ceaseless rain caused the river to overflow.  不停的雨导致了河水泛滥。|近义词：incessant, constant, continuous, perpetual, unrelenting|反义词：intermittent, sporadic, occasional|词性:形容词
词源:  From Old English *ēaðlēas "without ease, difficult," from ēað "easy" + -lēas "without."
