dismask:/dɪsˈmɑːsk/
verb
揭露，暴露；摘掉面具
to remove a mask from; to expose; to reveal the true nature of
例句：
The journalist dismasked the corrupt politician.  记者揭露了腐败政客的真面目。

He dismasked his intentions. 他露出了他的意图。

近义词：unmask, expose, reveal, uncover
反义词：mask, conceal, hide, disguise
词性：v.

dismask:/dɪsˈmɑːsk/
noun
揭露，暴露；摘掉面具的行为
the act of removing a mask; the act of exposing or revealing
例句：
The dismask of the conspiracy shocked the nation.  阴谋的揭露震惊了全国。

His dismask was met with applause. 他的揭露赢得了掌声。

近义词：exposure, revelation
反义词：concealment, hiding
词性：n.
