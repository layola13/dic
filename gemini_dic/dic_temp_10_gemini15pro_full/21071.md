blindness:/ˈblaɪndnɪs/| n.|失明，盲目|The condition of being unable to see.  It can be partial or total, temporary or permanent. |He suffered from blindness after the accident. 他在事故后失明了。|Near synonyms: sightlessness, vision impairment|Near antonyms: sight, vision|noun

blindness:/ˈblaɪndnɪs/| n.|盲目，无知|A lack of understanding or perception. |His political blindness led him to make poor decisions. 他的政治盲目导致他做出了糟糕的决定。|Near synonyms: ignorance, unawareness, naivety|Near antonyms: awareness, knowledge, insight|noun
