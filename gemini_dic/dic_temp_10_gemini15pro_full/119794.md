migration:/maɪˈɡreɪʃn/ | n. | 迁移，移居；移动 | The seasonal migration of birds is a fascinating phenomenon. 鸟类的季节性迁徙是一个迷人的现象。| The software company began its migration to cloud computing. 该软件公司开始向云计算迁移。|近义词：movement, relocation, emigration, immigration | 反义词: settlement, sedentariness | noun

etymology: From Latin *migratio*, *migrationis*, from *migrare* "to change one's abode, depart," related to *migrare* "to move from one place to another."
