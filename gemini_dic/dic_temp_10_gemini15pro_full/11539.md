arises:/əˈraɪzɪz/
verb
出现，发生，产生
comes into being; appears; originates
The problem arises from a lack of communication.  这个问题源于缺乏沟通。
New opportunities arise every day. 新的机会每天都会出现。
synonyms: emerge, occur, spring up, develop, ensue, result
antonyms: disappear, vanish, cease, end, subside
