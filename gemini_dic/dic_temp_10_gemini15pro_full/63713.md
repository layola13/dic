ephemerist:/ɪˈfɛmərɪst/
noun
蜉蝣研究者; 短暂事物爱好者
A person who studies ephemera or things that are short-lived.
He was an ephemerist, fascinated by old postcards and playbills. 他是一位蜉蝣研究者，对古老的明信片和海报着迷。
Synonyms: collector, antiquarian
Antonyms:  perennialist, futurist
词性: 名词
