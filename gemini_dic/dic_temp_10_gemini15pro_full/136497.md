nonromanticism:/ˌnɒnroʊˈmæntɪsɪzəm/
n.
非浪漫主义
The absence or rejection of romanticism, especially in art, literature, or music.  A movement or style characterized by a focus on realism, objectivity, and restraint.
His nonromanticism was evident in his preference for documentaries over romantic comedies. 他对纪录片的偏爱胜过浪漫喜剧，这表明了他非浪漫主义的倾向。
近义词: realism, classicism, objectivity
反义词: romanticism, sentimentalism
词性: noun

nonromantic:/ˌnɒnroʊˈmæntɪk/
adj.
非浪漫的
Not romantic; practical or unsentimental.
Their relationship was very nonromantic, focused more on shared interests and mutual respect. 他们的关系很不浪漫，更多地关注共同的兴趣和相互尊重。
近义词: practical, unsentimental, pragmatic
反义词: romantic, sentimental, passionate
词性: adjective
