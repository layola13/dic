guildships:/ˈɡɪldʃɪps/
n.
行会船，同业公会船只
Ships owned or operated by a guild, a medieval association of craftspeople or merchants.
The guildships dominated the trade routes of the Baltic Sea.  行会船只控制着波罗的海的贸易路线。
The guildships brought prosperity to the Hanseatic League cities. 行会船只给汉萨同盟城市带来了繁荣。
synonyms: merchant ships, trading vessels
antonyms: warships, pirate ships
noun
