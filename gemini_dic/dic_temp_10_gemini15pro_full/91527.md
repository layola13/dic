hyosternal:/ˌhaɪoʊˈstɜːrnəl/| adj.|胸骨舌骨的|Relating to the hyoid bone and the sternum.|The hyosternal muscle connects the hyoid bone to the sternum. 胸骨舌骨肌连接舌骨和胸骨。|近义词:infrahyoid (some contexts)|反义词: None|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Greek *hyoeides* (U-shaped, like the hyoid bone) + Latin *sternum* (breastbone).  The term combines elements referring to both the hyoid bone and the sternum.
