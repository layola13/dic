durity:/ˈdjʊrɪti/ | n. | 硬度，坚固性 | Hardness, firmness, solidity. The quality of being hard or firm. | The durity of the diamond makes it ideal for cutting glass. 钻石的硬度使其成为切割玻璃的理想选择。| 近义词: hardness, firmness, solidity, toughness | 反义词: softness, flexibility, malleability | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely a back-formation from "durable," ultimately from Latin "dūrāre" (to harden, last, endure). Though not commonly used, it can be found in some older texts and technical contexts.
