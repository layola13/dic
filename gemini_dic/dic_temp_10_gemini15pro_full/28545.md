cardiographic:/ˌkɑːrdiəˈɡræfɪk/| adj.|心动描记的，心脏记录的|Relating to or produced by cardiography (the process of recording the electrical activity of the heart).|The cardiographic readings showed an irregular heartbeat. 心动图读数显示心跳不规律。|近义词:electrocardiographic|反义词:None|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From cardio- (heart) + -graphic (relating to writing or recording).  Ultimately from Greek *kardía* (heart) and *gráphein* (to write).
