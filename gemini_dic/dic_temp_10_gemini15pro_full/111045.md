lisps:/lɪsps/| n. v.| 咬着舌头发音; 口齿不清 |A speech defect that involves pronouncing "s" and "z" sounds like "th".  The act of speaking with a lisp. | Example: He speaks with a slight lisp. 他说话有点口齿不清。| Synonym:  slur, mispronounce | Antonym: articulate, enunciate | noun
lisps:/lɪsps/| v. |咬着舌头发音; 口齿不清地说 | To speak with a lisp.  | Example: She lisps when she says "sister".  她说“sister”的时候口齿不清。| Synonym: slur, mumble | Antonym: articulate, enunciate | verb
