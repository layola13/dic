obsessional:/əbˈseʃənl/
adjective
强迫性的；着迷的
Relating to or characterized by obsession; compulsive.
He suffers from obsessional jealousy. 他患有强迫性嫉妒。
近义词:compulsive, obsessive
反义词:casual, indifferent
词性:adjective

obsessional:/əbˈseʃənl/
noun
强迫症患者
A person who suffers from an obsession.
He was an obsessional, constantly checking and rechecking his work. 他是一个强迫症患者，不断地检查和复查他的工作。
近义词:compulsive
反义词:None
词性:noun


词源:From the noun "obsession" + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Obsession" comes from the Late Latin "obsessio," meaning "siege," from the Latin "obsidere" ("to besiege"), from "ob" ("against") + "sedere" ("to sit").
