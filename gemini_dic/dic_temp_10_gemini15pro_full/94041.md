imparter:/ɪmˈpɑːrtər/| n.|传授者，赋予者|A person or thing that imparts something, especially knowledge, a quality, or a feeling.|The teacher was a skilled imparter of knowledge. 这位老师是一位知识渊博的传授者。|近义词：communicator, teacher, instructor, giver, conveyor|反义词：receiver, withholder|noun
impart:/ɪmˈpɑːrt/| vt.|传授，赋予，告知|To communicate information, knowledge, wisdom, etc.; to bestow (a quality).|She imparted her wisdom to her students. 她把自己的智慧传授给学生们。|近义词：communicate, convey, transmit, bestow, grant|反义词：withhold, conceal|verb
