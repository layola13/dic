crestfallen: /ˈkrɛstˌfɔːlən/ (US), /ˈkrɛstfɔːlən/ (UK) | adj. | 沮丧的，垂头丧气的 | Dejected; dispirited; discouraged, especially after a loss or disappointment.  Characterized by a drooping head or hanging crest (as in a bird). |  He looked crestfallen after his team lost the championship game. 他在球队输掉冠军赛后看起来很沮丧。 | Synonyms: dejected, despondent, disheartened, downcast | Antonyms: elated, cheerful, jubilant | word type: adjective
