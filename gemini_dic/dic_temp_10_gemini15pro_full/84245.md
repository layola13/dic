half-silk:/ˌhɑːf ˈsɪlk/ | adj. | 半丝绸的；仿丝的 | Made of a mixture of silk and another fiber, typically rayon or cotton.  A fabric resembling silk but containing other fibers. |  She wore a beautiful half-silk dress to the party. 她穿了一件漂亮的半丝绸连衣裙去参加聚会。 | The curtains were made of a shimmering half-silk material. 窗帘是用一种闪闪发光的仿丝绸材料制成的。 | Near synonyms: semi-silk, part-silk | Antonym: pure silk | adjective
