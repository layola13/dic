emergent:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒənt/| n.|出现的事物，新兴事物|something that emerges|例句：Artificial intelligence is an emergent technology. 人工智能是一项新兴技术。|近义词：development, phenomenon|反义词：establishment|词性:名词
