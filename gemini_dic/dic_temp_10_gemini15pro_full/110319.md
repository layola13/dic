lightened:/ˈlaɪtənd/| v. adj.|使变亮，使变轻，减轻，轻松|Past participle of "lighten."  Can mean made brighter, less heavy, or less serious. |The sun lightened the room. 太阳照亮了房间。|The company lightened its debt burden. 公司减轻了债务负担。|近义词：brightened, eased, relieved|反义词：darkened, burdened, aggravated|词性:动词(过去分词), 形容词
