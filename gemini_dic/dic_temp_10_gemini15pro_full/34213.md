clanged:/klæŋd/| v. |发出铿锵声；发出当啷声|to make a loud, ringing metallic sound, like that of metal hitting metal|The gate clanged shut behind him.  大门在他身后砰地关上了。| The trolley clanged as it rumbled down the street. 手推车哐当哐当的响着，沿着街道隆隆驶去。|近义词：banged, clashed, resounded|反义词: whispered, murmured |词性:动词

clanging:/ˈklæŋɪŋ/| n. |铿锵声；当啷声|a loud, ringing metallic sound|The clanging of the bells woke me up. 钟声的铿锵声把我吵醒了。|The constant clanging from the construction site was driving everyone crazy. 建筑工地不断传来的叮当声快把大家都逼疯了。|近义词：banging, clashing, ringing|反义词: silence, quiet |词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *clangen, from Old Norse *klanga (“to clang, resound”), from Proto-Germanic *klanganą (“to clang, resound”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ḱleng- (“to ring, resound”). Cognate with Danish klange (“to clang, resound”), Swedish klang (“clang, sound, resonance”), German klingen (“to sound, ring”).
