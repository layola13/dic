mechanographic:/ˌmɛkəˈnəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
机印的，机械制图的
Relating to or denoting a method of recording data by mechanical means, especially using punched cards.
Mechanographic data processing was used extensively before computers became widespread.  机印数据处理在计算机普及之前被广泛使用。
The company used mechanographic equipment to process large volumes of data. 这家公司使用机印设备来处理大量数据。
近义词:automated, computerized
反义词:manual
词性:adjective
