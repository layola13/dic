apheliotropic:/ˌæfiːliəˈtrɒpɪk/ /ˌæfiːliəˈtroʊpɪk/
adjective
背光的；避日的
Turning or growing away from light.
The apheliotropic plant turned its leaves away from the intense sunlight.  背光的植物将叶子避开了强烈的阳光。

The roots of some plants are apheliotropic, growing away from the light source.  一些植物的根是背光的，会远离光源生长。
近义词:negative phototropic, light-avoiding
反义词:heliotropic, phototropic
词性:adjective
