gurglingly:/ˈɡɜːrɡlɪŋli/ | adv. |汩汩地，咕噜咕噜地 |Characterized by a gurgling sound, like water flowing unevenly or air bubbling through liquid.  This word describes the manner in which something happens.| The brook flowed gurglingly over the rocks. 小溪汩汩地流过岩石。| 近义词：bubblingly, murmuringly | 反义词: silently, quietly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From "gurgle" + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  "Gurgle" likely originates from imitative roots, reflecting the sound it describes.  It's related to Dutch "gorgelen" and German "gurgeln."
