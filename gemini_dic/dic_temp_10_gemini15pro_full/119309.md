microballoon:/ˈmaɪkroʊbəˌluːn/| n. |微型气球，空心微球|A tiny, hollow sphere, often made of glass, ceramic, or plastic, used in various applications such as fillers, lightweight composites, and buoyancy aids.|The microballoons in the syntactic foam increased its buoyancy.|Synonyms: microsphere, bubble (informal)|Antonyms: macrosphere (rarely used), solid sphere|noun
