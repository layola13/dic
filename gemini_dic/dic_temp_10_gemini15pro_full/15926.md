ballyhoo:/ˌbæliˈhuː/
ballyhoos:/ˌbæliˈhuːz/
n.|喧嚣，大肆宣传|A noisy, attention-getting demonstration or talk.
v.|大肆宣传，吹捧|To advertise or publicize something vigorously.
|The circus came to town with a great ballyhoo.|马戏团带着盛大的宣传来到镇上。
|The company ballyhooed its new product.|公司大肆宣传它的新产品。
|hype, promotion, publicity|
|understatement, downplay|
noun
verb
