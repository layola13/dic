blabber:/ˈblæbər/ (US), /ˈblæbə(r)/ (UK)| v. n.|喋喋不休，胡言乱语|To talk foolishly or excessively.|He blabbered on about his new car for hours. 他喋喋不休地谈论他的新车好几个小时。|近义词：chatter, prattle, babble|反义词:be quiet, shut up|verb, noun

blabber:/ˈblæbər/ (US), /ˈblæbə(r)/ (UK)| n.|泄密者，饶舌者|A person who talks foolishly or reveals secrets.|He's such a blabber; he can't keep a secret. 他真是个大嘴巴，守不住秘密。|近义词：gossip, telltale|反义词:discreet person|noun
