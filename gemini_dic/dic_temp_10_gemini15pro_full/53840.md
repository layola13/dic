disconsolation:/dɪsˌkɑːnsəˈleɪʃn/
n.
沮丧，悲伤，忧郁
A state of deep sadness or dejection; comfortlessness.
She was overcome with disconsolation after the death of her husband. 她丈夫去世后，她悲痛欲绝。
Near synonyms: grief, sorrow, sadness, dejection, desolation
Antonyms: consolation, comfort, cheer, joy
noun

disconsolate:/dɪsˈkɑːnsələt/
adj.
沮丧的，悲伤的，忧郁的
Without consolation or comfort; unhappy.
He wandered disconsolately through the streets after losing his job.  他失业后在街上徘徊，神情沮丧。
Near synonyms: heartbroken, inconsolable, dejected, depressed, miserable
Antonyms: cheerful, happy, joyful, elated
adjective
