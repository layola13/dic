cornetcy: /ˈkɔːrnɪtsi/ | n. | 军旗职位;骑兵旗职位 | The position of a cornet or standard-bearer in a troop of cavalry.  A cornetcy was a commissioned rank purchased by the holder, much like other officer ranks in the British army at the time.| He purchased a cornetcy in the regiment. 他在该团买了一个骑兵旗职位。 | 近义词: commission, rank | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps demotion or dismissal) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From cornet (the rank) + -cy (suffix forming nouns of state or quality).  Ultimately from Old French "cornet" (small horn), referring to the horn-shaped pennant carried by the cornet.
