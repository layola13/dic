caftan:/ˈkæftæn/ /kɑːfˈtɑːn/| n. |长袍，罩袍|A long, loose robe or tunic, typically with long sleeves, worn in some Eastern countries.|She wore a flowing caftan to the beach party. 她穿着一件飘逸的长袍去参加海滩派对。|Synonyms: robe, gown, tunic|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: suit, jeans, shorts)|noun
