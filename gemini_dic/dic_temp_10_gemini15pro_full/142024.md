organizes:/ˈɔːrɡənaɪzɪz/
verb
组织，安排
arranges systematically; puts into working order.
She organizes her closet every week. 她每周都整理她的衣橱。
The company organizes team-building activities every quarter.  公司每季度都会组织团队建设活动。

synonyms: arranges, coordinates, structures, systematizes
antonyms: disorganizes, scatters, disrupts, muddles
词性: 动词
