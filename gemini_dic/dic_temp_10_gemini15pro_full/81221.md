grandpapa:/ˈɡræˌnˌpɑːpɑː/ /ˈɡrændˌpɑːpɑː/
noun
爷爷；外公
A grandfather.  A somewhat old-fashioned and affectionate term.
My grandpapa used to tell me stories before bedtime. 我的爷爷过去常常在睡前给我讲故事。
