out-laboring:/ˌaʊtˈleɪbərɪŋ/
verb
胜过工作；比...更努力工作
working harder or more effectively than someone else.
He out-labored his competitors and won the contract. 他比他的竞争对手工作更努力，赢得了合同。
The team out-labored their opponents and secured a victory.  这个团队比他们的对手工作更努力，取得了胜利。
近义词: outworking, surpassing
反义词: underperforming, slacking
词性: verb (动词)
