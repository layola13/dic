fortifyingly:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪɪŋli/
adverb
令人鼓舞地；增强体力地
in a way that makes you feel stronger and more determined
The fresh air acted fortifyingly on her spirits.  新鲜的空气让她精神振奋。
Synonyms: encouragingly, hearteningly, inspiringly
Antonyms: dishearteningly, discouragingly, depressingly
詞性: 副詞


ETYMOLOGY->
From fortify + -ing + -ly.  "Fortify" comes from Old French "fortifier," from Late Latin "fortificare," from Latin "fortis" (strong) + "-ficare" (to make).
