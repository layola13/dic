lithontriptist: /ˈlɪθəntrɪptɪst/
noun
碎石专家，碎石术医师
A medical specialist who performs lithotripsy (the medical procedure of breaking up stones in the kidneys, bladder, or ureters using shock waves).
The lithontriptist used shock waves to break up the kidney stones. 碎石专家用冲击波击碎了肾结石。

近义词: urologist (泌尿科医生)
反义词:  None (没有直接的反义词)
词性: noun


lithotripsy: /ˈlɪθəʊtrɪpsi/
noun
碎石术
The medical procedure of breaking up stones in the kidneys, bladder, or ureters using shock waves.
Lithotripsy is a non-invasive procedure to treat kidney stones. 碎石术是一种治疗肾结石的非侵入性手术。

近义词:  None (没有更具体的近义词，可以用更宽泛的词如"medical procedure"或"treatment")
反义词: None (没有直接的反义词)
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Greek *litho-* (stone) + *-tripsis* (rubbing, crushing).  The "-tript" part comes from the Greek *tribein*, meaning "to rub" or "to wear down."
