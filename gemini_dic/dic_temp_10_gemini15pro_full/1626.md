acidulate:/əˈsɪdʒʊleɪt/
verb
酸化；使变酸
to make something slightly acidic
The chef acidulated the sauce with lemon juice.  厨师用柠檬汁使酱汁变酸。
The soil in this area has been acidulated by industrial pollution.  这个地区的土壤已经被工业污染酸化了。
近义词:sour, acidify
反义词:sweeten, neutralize
词性:verb

acidulate:/əˈsɪdʒʊlət/
adjective
微酸的
slightly acidic or sour
The wine had an acidulate taste.  这酒有淡淡的酸味。
The berries have a pleasantly acidulate flavor.  这些浆果有一种令人愉悦的微酸味道。
近义词:tart, sourish
反义词:sweet, bland
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *acidulatus, past participle of acidulare (“to make sour”), from Latin acidus (“sour, sharp, tart”).
