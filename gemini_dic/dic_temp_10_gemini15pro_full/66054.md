exasperates:/ɪɡˈzæspəreɪts/| v. |激怒，使恼怒|To irritate intensely; annoy extremely.|Her constant complaining exasperates me. 她不断的抱怨让我很恼火。| The children's endless fighting exasperated their parents. 孩子们无休止的争吵激怒了他们的父母。| 近义词：irritates, annoys, infuriates, aggravates|反义词：soothes, calms, pleases|词性:动词

exasperate:/ɪɡˈzæspəreɪt/| v. |激怒，使恼怒|To irritate intensely; annoy extremely.|His lack of consideration exasperates me. 他缺乏体贴让我很恼火。|Trying to reason with him only exasperated her further.  试图和他讲道理只会让她更加恼火。| 近义词：irritate, annoy, infuriate, aggravate|反义词：soothe, calm, please|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin exasperatus, past participle of exasperare "to roughen, irritate," from Latin ex- "out" + asperare "to make rough," from asper "rough."
