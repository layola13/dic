dancefloor:/ˈdænsflɔːr/ (US), /ˈdɑːnsflɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. |舞池|A designated area for dancing, typically in a nightclub, bar, or at a party.|The dancefloor was packed with people moving to the music. 舞池里挤满了随着音乐起舞的人们。|例句：The band started playing and everyone rushed to the dancefloor. 乐队开始演奏，大家都涌向舞池。|近义词：dance area, dancing floor|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps seating area)|词性:noun
