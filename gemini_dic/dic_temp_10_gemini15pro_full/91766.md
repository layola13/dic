hyperemphasize:/ˌhaɪpərˈɛmfəsaɪz/ | v. | 过分强调；过分重视 | To emphasize something excessively or unduly. |  He tends to hyperemphasize the importance of his own contributions to the project. 他倾向于过分强调自己对项目的贡献。

| The media hyperemphasized the negative aspects of the story. 媒体过分强调了故事的负面。 | 近义词: overemphasize, overstress, exaggerate | 反义词: downplay, understate, minimize | 词性: verb
