disruly:/dɪsˈruːli/ | adj. | 难管教的，不守规矩的 | Showing a lack of discipline and obedience; difficult to control. | The disruly children were sent out of the classroom. 这些不守规矩的孩子被赶出了教室。| The disruly crowd became violent and started throwing rocks. 骚乱的人群变得暴力，开始扔石头。 | 近义词:unruly, disorderly, uncontrollable, rebellious, defiant | 反义词: obedient, well-behaved, disciplined, compliant | 词性:形容词

词源: From dis- (expressing negation) + rule + -ly (forming adverbs and adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin *disregula*, from dis- "not" + regula "rule".
