/ˌmʌltiˈlætərəlɪzəm/ | n. | 多边主义 | The principle of participation by three or more parties, especially by the governments of different countries. |  The UN encourages multilateralism in international affairs.  联合国鼓励国际事务中的多边主义。| synonym:  plurilateralism, international cooperation | antonym: unilateralism, isolationism | noun

/ˌmʌltiˈlætərəlɪzəm/ | n. | 多边主义 |  The practice of coordinating national policies in groups of three or more states. |  Multilateralism offers a way to address global challenges like climate change. 多边主义提供了一种应对气候变化等全球挑战的途径。 | synonym: collective action, joint effort | antonym: unilateral action, going it alone | noun
