gim:/ɡɪm/ | n. | 小玩意儿；花招；诡计；骗局 | A trick or device intended to attract notice, especially by being unusual or novel. | Example 1:  That new phone is just a gim. 那款新手机不过是个小玩意儿。

Example 2:  It was all a marketing gim to get people to buy their product.  这完全是一个营销噱头，目的是让人们购买他们的产品。 | 近义词: gimmick, trick, device, gadget, contrivance | 反义词: honesty, sincerity | 词性: 名词
