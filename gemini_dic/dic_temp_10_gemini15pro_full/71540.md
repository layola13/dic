flare-ups:/ˈfleərˌʌps/ | n. |爆发；突然加剧；突发|A sudden, temporary outburst of anger or other strong emotion. | There was a flare-up of violence after the announcement.  公告发布后，暴力事件突然爆发。 | 近义词: outbursts, eruptions, explosions | 反义词: calms, lulls | noun
