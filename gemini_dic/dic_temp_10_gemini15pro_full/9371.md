antipyretic:/ˌæntipaɪˈrɛtɪk/| adj. n.|退热的，解热药|Reduces fever; a medicine that reduces fever.|The doctor prescribed an antipyretic for her high fever. 医生给她开了退烧药来治疗高烧。|Synonyms: antifebrile, febrifuge|Antonyms: pyrogenic, fever-inducing|adjective
antipyretic:/ˌæntipaɪˈrɛtɪk/| n.|退烧药|A medicine that reduces fever.|She took an antipyretic to bring down her temperature. 她服用了退烧药来降低体温。|Synonyms: antifebrile, febrifuge|Antonyms: pyrogenic|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀντιπυρετικός (antipyretikós, “against fever”), from ἀντί (antí, “against”) + πυρετικός (pyretikós, “feverish”).
