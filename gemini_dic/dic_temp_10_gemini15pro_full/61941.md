emulsibility:/ɪˌmʌlsəˈbɪləti/| n. |乳化性；乳化能力|The ability of a liquid to mix with another liquid that it normally does not mix with, such as oil and water, and form an emulsion.|The emulsibility of this oil is poor, it separates quickly from the water.  这种油的乳化性很差，它很快就会与水分离。|Synonyms: miscibility, blendability|Antonyms: immiscibility, non-blendability|noun
