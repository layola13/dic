metathetic:/ˌmɛtəˈθɛtɪk/
| adj. |换位的，置换的|Relating to or involving metathesis, a sound change involving the transposition of sounds within a word.|The word "bird" (from Old English "brid") is an example of metathesis.|近义词：transposed, inverted|反义词:unchanged, static|词性:形容词
