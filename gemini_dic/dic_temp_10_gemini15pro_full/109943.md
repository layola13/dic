liberalistic:/ˌlɪbərəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. |自由主义的，自由化的 |  Favoring or promoting individual rights, freedoms, and limited government intervention, often associated with progressive social views.  Characterized by a belief in the principles of liberalism, which emphasize individual rights and freedoms. |  His policies were seen as too liberalistic for the conservative members of the party. 他的政策被认为对该党的保守成员来说过于自由主义。| The new mayor has a liberalistic approach to city planning. 新市长对城市规划采取了自由主义的做法。|Synonyms: liberal, progressive, left-leaning | Antonyms: conservative, reactionary, right-wing | adjective
