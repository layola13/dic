fessed:/fest/ | adj. | 供认的，坦白的 | Confessed; admitted (usually in heraldry).  | The knight fessed his crimes to the king. 骑士向国王坦白了他的罪行。 | 近义词: admitted, confessed, acknowledged | 反义词: denied, concealed | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Old French *fesser "to confess," from Latin *fessus, past participle of fateri "to confess."  The heraldic meaning developed from the appearance of a horizontal band across a shield, resembling a confession band worn by a priest.
