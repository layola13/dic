derailment:/diːˈreɪlmənt/
noun
脱轨；出轨；偏离正轨
the act of a train coming off its rails; a situation in which something fails to develop as planned
The train derailment caused significant delays. 火车脱轨造成了严重的延误。
The project suffered a derailment due to budget cuts.  由于预算削减，该项目偏离了正轨。
synonyms: deviation, diversion, deflection, miscarriage
antonyms: adherence, compliance, conformity
n.
