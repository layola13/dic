incurve:/ɪnˈkɜːrv/ | v.  n. | 向内弯曲，内曲；内弯，弯曲 | To bend or curve inwards.  The act of curving inwards. | The petals of the flower incurve to protect the pollen.  这朵花的花瓣向内弯曲以保护花粉。 |  近义词：bend, curve, curl | 反义词：outcurve | 词性: 动词, 名词


incurved:/ɪnˈkɜːrvd/ | adj. | 向内弯曲的 | Curved inwards. | The plant had long, incurved leaves.  这种植物有长长的、向内弯曲的叶子。 | 近义词：bent, curved, curled | 反义词：outcurved | 词性: 形容词


词源: From Latin *incurvare, from in- + curvare "to bend, curve," from curvus "bent, curved."
