anthropopsychism:/ˌænθrəpōˈsaɪkɪzəm/
n.
人类中心主义；拟人论
The attribution of human-like emotions, motivations, and characteristics to animals, gods, or inanimate objects.
The poem displayed a strong sense of anthropopsychism, portraying the river as a weeping widow. 这首诗展现了强烈的人类中心主义，将河流描绘成一个哭泣的寡妇。
anthropomorphism, personification
