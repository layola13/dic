circinate:/ˈsɜːsɪneɪt/ | adj. | 环状的，卷曲的 | Rolled inwards from the tip, like a fern frond. | The young fern fronds were tightly circinate.  幼嫩的蕨类植物叶子紧紧地卷曲着。| circinate vernation is a characteristic of ferns. 蕨类植物的幼叶呈环状卷曲是其特征之一。 | coiled, curled, involute | straight, uncurled | adjective
