footbridges:/ˈfʊtˌbrɪdʒɪz/
n.
人行天桥，人行桥
A bridge designed for pedestrians.
The park has several footbridges over the stream. 公园里有几座跨越小溪的人行天桥。
overpasses, walkways, pedestrian bridges
