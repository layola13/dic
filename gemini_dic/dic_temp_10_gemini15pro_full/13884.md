augurist:/ˈɔːɡərɪst/| n. |占卜者，预言家|A person who practices augury; a soothsayer.|The augurist predicted a prosperous future for the kingdom. 占卜者预言王国将繁荣昌盛。|Synonyms: soothsayer, diviner, prophet, seer|Antonyms: skeptic, doubter|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *augur*, meaning "priest who interprets omens," from *avis* "bird" + *garrire* "to chatter."  The suffix *-ist* denotes a person who practices or is skilled in something.
