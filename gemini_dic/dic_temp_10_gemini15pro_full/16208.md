bannerless:/ˈbænərlɪs/
adjective
无旗帜的；无横幅的
Without banners.  This refers to something lacking a banner, which is a long piece of cloth or similar material, typically with a slogan or design, hung in a public place or carried in a demonstration or procession.
The protesters held a bannerless march.  抗议者举行了无旗帜游行。
The website had a bannerless design, making it look clean and modern. 该网站采用无横幅设计，使其看起来简洁现代。
Near synonyms: unflagged, unmarked, undecorated
Antonyms: bannered, flagged, decorated
词性: adjective
