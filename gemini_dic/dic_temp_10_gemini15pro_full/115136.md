mangonize: /ˈmæŋɡənaɪz/
verb
芒果化；使具有芒果的味道或特征
To impart the flavor or characteristics of mango to something.
The chef mangonized the chicken by marinating it in mango puree and spices.  厨师将鸡肉浸泡在芒果泥和香料中，使其具有芒果的味道。
The drink was mangonized with a hint of mango extract.  这种饮料中加入了一点芒果提取物，使其具有芒果的味道。
Near Synonyms:  infuse (with mango), flavor (with mango)
Antonyms:  N/A (lacks a direct opposite)
词性: verb
