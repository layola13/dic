condensation:/ˌkɑːndenˈseɪʃn/ /ˌkɒndenˈseɪʃn/ | n. | 冷凝，凝结，浓缩，缩写 |The process of a gas changing to a liquid. The product of this process. A shortened version of something, especially a piece of writing.|The condensation on the window made it difficult to see outside. 窗户上的冷凝物使外面很难看清。|synonyms: liquefaction, precipitation, abridgement, summary | antonyms: evaporation, vaporization | noun
