debenture:/dɪˈbɛntʃər/
n.
债券；信用债券；无担保债券
A debenture is a type of bond or other debt instrument that is unsecured by collateral.  It is backed only by the creditworthiness and reputation of the issuer.
Example: The company issued debentures to raise capital for expansion.  该公司发行债券为扩张筹集资金。

Example: Investors were wary of purchasing the debentures due to the company's poor financial performance. 由于公司财务状况不佳，投资者对购买债券持谨慎态度。
synonym: bond, loan, obligation
antonym: asset, equity, stock
noun
