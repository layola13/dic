bowdlerizers:/ˈboʊdləˌraɪzərz/
n.
审查者，删节者
People who remove material from books, plays, films, etc., that they consider indecent or offensive.
The bowdlerizers removed all references to sex and violence from the novel.  审查者将小说中所有关于性和暴力的内容都删除了。
censors, expurgators
