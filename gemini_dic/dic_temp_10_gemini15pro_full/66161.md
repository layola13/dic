excerption:/ɪkˈsɜːrpʃən/ /ekˈsɜːrpʃən/| n.|摘录，节选|A passage or segment taken from a longer work, such as a book, film, or piece of music.|例句：The excerpt from the novel was particularly moving. 这部小说的摘录特别感人。

例句：She read an excerpt from her new poem. 她朗读了她的新诗的节选。

近义词：extract, passage, selection, quotation, citation|反义词: whole, entirety|词性:noun
