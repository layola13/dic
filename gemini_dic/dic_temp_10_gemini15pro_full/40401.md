contraindicate:/ˌkɒntrəˈɪndɪkeɪt/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈɪndɪkeɪt/ | vt. |禁忌；显示…的禁忌症|To make (a treatment or procedure) inadvisable.|The patient's heart condition contraindicates strenuous exercise. 病人的心脏状况意味着剧烈运动是被禁止的。|近义词: proscribe, forbid, preclude|反义词: indicate, suggest, recommend|verb

contraindication:/ˌkɒntrəˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːntrəˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. |禁忌症|A factor that makes a particular treatment or procedure inadvisable.|High blood pressure is a contraindication for this medication. 高血压是服用这种药物的禁忌症。|近义词: caution, warning, prohibition|反义词: indication, recommendation|noun
