cheapo:/ˈtʃiːpoʊ/| adj. n.|便宜的，廉价的；便宜货|Something that is cheapo is cheap and of poor quality.  It can be used as both an adjective and a noun.|例句：I bought a cheapo phone and it broke after a week. 我买了个便宜的手机，一周后就坏了。|近义词：cheap, inexpensive, bargain, budget|反义词:expensive, pricey, costly, high-end|词性:adjective, noun
cheapo:/ˈtʃiːpoʊ/| n.|便宜货|A person or thing that is cheap or inexpensive, often of poor quality.|例句：He's such a cheapo, he always tries to get things for free. 他真是个小气鬼，总是想免费得到东西。|近义词：cheapskate, tightwad|反义词:spendthrift, big spender|词性:noun
