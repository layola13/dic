agoraphobic:/ˌæɡərəˈfəʊbɪk/| adj.| 广场恐惧症的，患广场恐惧症的|A person who is agoraphobic has a fear of being in public places where there are a lot of people.|She became agoraphobic after the attack and rarely left her house.  她遭受袭击后患上了广场恐惧症，很少出门。|Synonyms: anxious, fearful, panicked|Antonyms: calm, composed, fearless|adjective


agoraphobe:/ˈæɡərəˌfəʊb/| n.| 广场恐惧症患者|A person who suffers from agoraphobia.|My aunt is an agoraphobe and hasn't left her house in years.  我的姑姑是一位广场恐惧症患者，已经好几年没出过门了。|Synonyms: phobic|Antonyms: None|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *agora* (marketplace) + *phobos* (fear).
