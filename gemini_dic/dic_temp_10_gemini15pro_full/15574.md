Baha'ism: /bɑːˈhɑːɪɪzəm/ /bəˈhɑɪɪzəm/
noun
巴哈伊信仰
A monotheistic religion emphasizing the spiritual unity of all humankind.
Baha'i teachings focus on the oneness of God, the oneness of humanity, and the oneness of religion.  Baha'is believe in progressive revelation, meaning that God reveals his will to humanity over time through a series of divine messengers, including Abraham, Moses, Jesus, Muhammad, and most recently, the Bab and Baha'u'llah.
Example: She converted to Baha'ism after studying its principles of unity and peace. 她学习了巴哈伊信仰的团结与和平原则后皈依了该教。
Example: Baha'ism promotes interfaith dialogue and cooperation. 巴哈伊信仰促进不同信仰间的对话与合作。
Synonym: Baha'i Faith
