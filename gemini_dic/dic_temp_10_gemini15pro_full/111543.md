lobate:/ˈloʊbeɪt/| adj.| 分叶的，叶状的| Having lobes, resembling a lobe.  Characterized by rounded projections or divisions.|The oak leaf has a lobate margin. 橡树叶有分叶的边缘。|近义词：lobed, segmented|反义词: entire, smooth|词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *lobatus*, from lobus (“lobe”).
