begirdle: /bɪˈɡɜːrdl/ /bɪˈɡɜːrdəl/
verb
环绕，围绕
to surround; to encircle
The mountains begirdle the city. 群山环绕着这座城市。
The fog begirdled the castle, making it look mysterious.  浓雾环绕着城堡，使它看起来神秘莫测。
Synonyms: encircle, surround, encompass, enclose
Antonyms: expose, uncover, reveal
词性:v.
