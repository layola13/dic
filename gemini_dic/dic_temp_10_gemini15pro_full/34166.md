clamjamphrie:/ˈklæmdʒæmfri/
noun
喧闹的聚会，吵闹的群体
a noisy, boisterous gathering or a noisy, disorderly crowd of people
The annual clamjamphrie at the beach attracted thousands of students.  每年海滩上的喧闹聚会都吸引了成千上万的学生。
The street was filled with a clamjamphrie of revelers after the concert.  音乐会结束后，街上挤满了狂欢作乐的人群。
Synonyms:  ruckus, commotion, hubbub, free-for-all
Antonyms:  silence, peace, quiet, tranquility
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain, possibly a blend of "clam" (suggesting a crowded, tightly packed environment) and "jamphrie," perhaps a playful alteration of "jamboree."  词源不确定，可能是“clam”（暗示拥挤、紧密的环境）和“jamphrie”的混合，后者可能是“jamboree”（欢庆活动）的俏皮改写。
