lonelily:/ˈloʊnlɪli/| adv.|孤单地，寂寞地|In a lonely manner; all alone.  Characterized by or causing a feeling of loneliness.|She sat lonelily by the window, watching the rain. 她孤零零地坐在窗边，看着雨。|Near Synonyms: solitarily, desolately, forlornly|Near Antonyms: sociably, companionably|Word Type: Adverb

Etymology:
From lone + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Lone" ultimately derives from Old English "ān" (one).
