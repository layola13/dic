lecherous: /ˈlɛtʃərəs/ | adj. | 好色的；淫荡的 | Having or showing excessive or offensive sexual desire. |  He gave her a lecherous look. 他用好色的眼神看着她。| synonyms: lustful, lascivious, lewd, licentious | antonyms: chaste, pure, virtuous | adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *lecherous, from Old French lecheros, from lecheor (“lecher”), from lechier (“to lick”), from Latin lingere, from Proto-Indo-European *leigh- (“to lick”).
