leggiest: /ˈlɛɡiɪst/ | adj. | 腿最长的 | Having the longest or most noticeable legs.  |  She was the leggiest dancer in the troupe.  她在那群舞蹈演员中腿最长。 |  long-legged, leggy | short-legged | adjective

Etymology->
From leg + -y + -est. The suffix -y forms adjectives meaning "having, full of, characterized by," and -est is the superlative suffix.
