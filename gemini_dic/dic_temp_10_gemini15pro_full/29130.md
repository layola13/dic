casebook:/ˈkeɪsbʊk/| n.|案例汇编；判例集|A book containing accounts of cases, especially medical or legal ones, used for teaching or reference.|Law students often refer to casebooks to study past legal precedents. 法律专业的学生经常参考案例汇编来学习过去的法律先例。|The doctor consulted a casebook to learn more about the rare disease. 医生查阅了案例汇编，以了解更多关于这种罕见疾病的信息。|synonyms: case study, dossier, record, file|antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "fiction" in a literary context)|noun
