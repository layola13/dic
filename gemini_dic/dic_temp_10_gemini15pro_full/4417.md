alarmed:/əˈlɑːrmd/| adj.|惊恐的，担忧的|Feeling or showing sudden fear or anxiety.|例句：She was alarmed by the news of the accident. 她被事故的消息吓坏了。|近义词：frightened, scared, worried, anxious, apprehensive|反义词：calm, relaxed, composed, unworried|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "alarm," which comes from the Italian "all'arme" (to arms!), indicating a call to prepare for danger.
