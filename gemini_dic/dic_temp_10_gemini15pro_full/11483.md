arhythmic:/əˈrɪðmɪk/
adjective
无节奏的，心律不齐的
Lacking rhythm or regularity, especially of the heartbeat.
His arhythmic heartbeat worried the doctors. 他的心律不齐让医生很担心。
irregular, erratic
rhythmic, regular
