librate:/ˈlaɪbreɪt/ (US), /laɪˈbreɪt/ (UK)| v.| 平衡；摇摆；摆动|To move back and forth or from side to side, as a balance does; to waver.| The pendulum librated slowly. 钟摆慢慢地摆动。|近义词：oscillate, swing, sway|反义词:fix, stabilize|verb

librate:/ˈlaɪbreɪt/ (US), /laɪˈbreɪt/ (UK)| n.| 天秤座；天平|Libra (constellation)| The sun is in librate during the fall. 秋天太阳在天平座。|近义词：Libra, Balance|反义词:None|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin lībrāre (“to weigh, balance”), from lībra (“balance, scales”).
