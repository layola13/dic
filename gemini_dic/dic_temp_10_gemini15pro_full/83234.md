gymkhana:/dʒɪmˈkɑːnə/ | n. | 体育比赛，运动会，汽车技巧赛 | A public meeting or competition featuring sports and games, especially equestrian events or motorsport involving skill in handling motor vehicles. |  The local gymkhana is held every summer and is a popular event. 当地的体育比赛每年夏天举行，是一个很受欢迎的活动。

The gymkhana included a challenging obstacle course for the drivers.  这次汽车技巧赛包括一个对车手来说极具挑战性的障碍赛。| 近义词：competition, contest, meet, tournament | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词
