euphemistically:/ˌjuːfəˈmɪstɪkli/| adv.|委婉地|In a way that uses mild or indirect words to express something unpleasant or embarrassing.  It avoids saying something directly, especially if it is unpleasant.|He euphemistically referred to his resignation as "moving on to new challenges." 他委婉地将他的辞职称为“迎接新的挑战”。|politely, indirectly, delicately|bluntly, harshly, directly|adverb
