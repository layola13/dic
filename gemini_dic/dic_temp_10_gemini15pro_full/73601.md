foretold:/fɔːrˈtəʊld/
verb
预言；预示
predicted a future event.
The fortune teller foretold a great journey in my future.  算命先生预言我未来会有一次伟大的旅行。
The ancient prophecies foretold the coming of a savior. 古代预言预示了救世主的到来。

synonyms: predicted, prophesied, forecast, prognosticated
antonyms: unpredicted, unforeseen

词性: verb (动词过去式和过去分词)

词源:  From Middle English *fortolden*, from Old English *foretealde*, past tense of *foretellan* (“to foretell”), from *fore-* (“before”) + *tellan* (“to tell, count, reckon”).
