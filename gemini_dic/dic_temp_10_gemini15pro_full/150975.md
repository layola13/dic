parameters:/pəˈræmɪtərz/ (US) /pəˈræmɪtəz/ (UK)| n. |参数；变量|A set of facts or a fixed limit that establishes or limits how something can or must be done.  A measurable factor which helps to define a particular system, object, etc.|The parameters of the experiment were carefully controlled.  实验的参数得到了仔细控制。|synonyms: factors, variables, criteria, guidelines|antonyms: constants, invariables|noun
