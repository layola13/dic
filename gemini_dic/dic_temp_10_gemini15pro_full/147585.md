overphilosophizes:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪləˌsɒfaɪzɪz/
verb
过度哲学化；想得太多；过度思辨
Thinks too much about the meaning of things, often to the point of inaction or confusion.
He overphilosophizes everything, and it paralyzes him from making decisions. 他对每件事都过度思辨，这使他无法做出决定。
overthink, intellectualize, ruminate, ponder excessively
underthink, simplify, act impulsively
词性: 动词
