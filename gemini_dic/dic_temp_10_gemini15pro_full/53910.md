discourage:/dɪˈskʌrɪdʒ/ /dɪˈskɜːrɪdʒ/
verb
使气馁，使沮丧，劝阻
To cause (someone) to lose confidence or enthusiasm; to try to prevent something
例句：
The rain discouraged us from going out.
下雨让我们打消了外出的念头。

The coach encouraged the team, telling them not to be discouraged by the loss.
教练鼓励队员们，告诉他们不要因为失败而气馁。

近义词：deter, dissuade, demoralize, dishearten
反义词：encourage, hearten, inspire, motivate
词性：v.
