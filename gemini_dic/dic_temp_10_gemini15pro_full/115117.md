mangier:/ˈmeɪndʒɪər/ | adj. | 更贪婪的，更渴望的 | More greedy or desirous, especially of food. | The mangier dog snarled at the others, guarding its bone.  更贪婪的狗对着其他狗咆哮，守护着它的骨头。| 近义词: greedier, more ravenous, more voracious | 反义词: less greedy, more generous | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *mangere*, from Old French *mangier*, present participle of *mangier* "to eat," from Latin *mandūcāre* "to chew."
