maize: /meɪz/ | n. |玉米，玉蜀黍|A tall annual cereal grass (Zea mays) that is cultivated for its large grains, or kernels, which are used as food for humans and livestock.|The farmers planted maize in the spring. 农民们在春天种了玉米。|Synonym: corn|Antonym: (No direct antonym for a plant, could consider other grains as alternatives, such as wheat, rice)|noun
