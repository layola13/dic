mobbers:/ˈmɑːbərz/ (US) /ˈmɒbəz/ (UK)| n.|暴徒，乌合之众，围攻者|A group of people who attack or harass someone, typically as part of a crowd.|The mobbers surrounded the car and began shouting abuse. 暴徒们包围了汽车，开始大声辱骂。|
mobbers:| n.| |暴民，围攻者|People who participate in mob action, harassing or attacking someone.|Online mobbers targeted her with hateful comments. 网上暴民用仇恨言论攻击她。|
mobbers:| n.| |参与围攻的人|Individuals involved in mobbing behavior, often in a workplace context.|The mobbers made her life at work miserable. 围攻她的人让她在工作中痛苦不堪。|


近义词: bullies, harassers, attackers, assailants, perpetrators
反义词: protectors, defenders, rescuers
词性: noun (plural)

词源: From "mob" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting persons who perform an action or are associated with something). "Mob" originates from the Latin "mobile vulgus" meaning "fickle crowd."
