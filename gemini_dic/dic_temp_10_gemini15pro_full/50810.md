describably:/dɪˈskraɪbəbli/| adv.|可描述地，可以形容地|In a manner that can be described; capable of being described.|The scenery was describably beautiful.  这景色美得难以言表。|近义词：expressible, depictable, portrayable|反义词：indescribable, ineffable|词性:副词
词源: From "describable" + "-ly."  "Describable" comes from "describe" (from Latin "describere," meaning "to write down") + "-able."
