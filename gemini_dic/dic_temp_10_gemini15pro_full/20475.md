bitty:/ˈbɪti/| adj.|微小的，细小的|Extremely small; tiny. |例句：She held the bitty baby bird in her hand. 她手里捧着那只小小的雏鸟。|例句：The artist specialized in bitty, detailed miniatures. 这位艺术家擅长创作细致入微的微型作品。|近义词：tiny, small, minute, miniature, wee|反义词：huge, enormous, large, massive|词性:形容词
词源:  A diminutive of bit, meaning "small piece".  Ultimately from Old English *bita, meaning "piece bitten off."
