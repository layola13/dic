bicrenate: /baɪˈkrɛneɪt/
adjective
双齿状的，重锯齿状的
Having a margin with two sets of rounded teeth, one set larger than the other.
The bicrenate leaf margins were a key characteristic for identifying the plant. 这种植物的关键特征是具有双齿状的叶缘。
The fossil leaf had a distinctly bicrenate edge. 这片化石叶子的边缘明显呈双齿状。
近义词: doubly serrate, biserrate
反义词: entire (全缘的), smooth (光滑的)
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *bi- (two) + crenatus (notched), past participle of crenare (to notch).
