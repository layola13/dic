ideography:/ˌaɪdiˈɑːɡrəfi/ /ˌaɪdiˈɒɡrəfi/| n. |表意文字; 表意符号; 表意法|A system of writing that uses ideograms, where each symbol represents an idea or concept.|The ancient Egyptians used ideography to communicate their ideas. 古埃及人使用表意文字来传达他们的想法。|Synonyms: ideogram, symbol, logogram|Antonyms: phonography, alphabet|Noun
