caloyer:/kəˈlɔɪər/ | n. | 修士，僧侣（尤指希腊东正教的） | A caloyer is a monk, especially of the Greek Orthodox Church. |  The caloyer devoted his life to prayer and contemplation.  这位修士毕生致力于祈祷和冥想。 |  The aged caloyer lived a simple life in his mountain cell.  这位年迈的修士在他的山间小屋里过着简朴的生活。 | Synonyms: monk, friar | Antonyms: layman | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *caloyer*, from Medieval Latin *caloierus*, from Byzantine Greek *καλόγερος (kalóyeros)*, meaning "good old man," from *καλός (kalós)* "good, beautiful" + *γέρων (géron)* "old man."
