bronzer: /ˈbrɑːnzər/ (US) /ˈbrɒnzə(r)/ (UK)| n.|古铜粉，晒黑粉|A cosmetic powder, cream, or lotion used to give skin a tanned appearance.|She applied bronzer to her cheekbones for a sun-kissed look. 她往颧骨上涂了古铜粉，让自己看起来像是被阳光亲吻过一样。|例句：I use bronzer to contour my face. 我用古铜粉修饰脸型。|近义词：tanner, self-tanner|反义词:skin whitener|词性:noun
