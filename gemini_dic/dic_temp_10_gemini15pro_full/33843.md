circumnavigates:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈnævɪɡeɪts/ | v. | 环航，绕...航行 | to sail or travel all the way around something, especially the world | Magellan circumnavigated the globe in 1522. | 麦哲伦在1522年环球航行。| 近义词: sail around, go around, circle | 反义词: - | verb
