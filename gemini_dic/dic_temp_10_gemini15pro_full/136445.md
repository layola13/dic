nonreverentially:/ˌnɑːnˌrɛvəˈrɛnʃəli/
| adv. |不敬地，非虔诚地|Showing a lack of reverence; disrespectfully.|He spoke nonreverentially about the sacred traditions. 他不敬地谈论神圣的传统。|例句：She tossed the holy book onto the table nonreverentially. 她不敬地把圣书扔到桌子上。|近义词：disrespectfully, irreverently, impiously|反义词：reverently, respectfully, piously|词性:副词
词源:  From non- + reverentially.  Reverentially comes from reverence (from Latin reverentia) + -ly.
