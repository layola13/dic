extracapsular:/ˌekstrəˈkæpsjʊlər/
adjective
囊外的；关节囊外的
Located or occurring outside a capsule, especially the lens capsule of the eye or the joint capsule.
The surgeon performed an extracapsular cataract extraction.  外科医生进行了囊外白内障摘除术。
Extracapsular fractures are more common in elderly patients. 老年患者更容易发生关节囊外骨折。
Synonyms: extracapsulary
Antonyms: intracapsular
