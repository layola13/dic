exilable:/ˈɛksaɪləbəl/| adj.|可放逐的，可流放的|Able to be exiled; liable or subject to exile.|The dissident writer was declared exilable by the government. 这位持不同政见的作家被政府宣布可以被流放。|The former dictator is exilable for his crimes against humanity.  这位前独裁者因其反人类罪行可被流放。|近义词：banishable, deportable|反义词: admissible, welcome|词性:形容词
词源: From exile + -able.  Exile comes from Old French exil, from Latin exilium, from exul "exile," from ex- "out" + solum "soil, ground."
