chai:/tʃaɪ/| n.|印度奶茶，香料奶茶|A spiced tea beverage originating from India. It typically contains black tea infused with aromatic spices like cardamom, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves, often brewed with milk and sweetened.|I love a warm cup of chai on a cold day. 我喜欢在寒冷的日子里喝一杯热腾腾的印度奶茶。|近义词:masala chai, spiced tea|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps coffee or herbal tea could be considered a contrasting beverage)|noun

chais:/tʃaɪz/ |n.|印度奶茶（chai的复数）|Plural form of chai, referring to multiple servings or types of the spiced tea beverage.|We ordered two chais, one with extra ginger and one regular. 我们点了两杯印度奶茶，一杯加姜，一杯普通的。|近义词: masala chais, spiced teas|反义词:  (No direct antonym)|noun
