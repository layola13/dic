canzonet:/ˌkænzəˈnɛt/ | n. |小曲，短歌|A short song, especially a light, lyrical one.|She sang a delightful canzonet by Mozart. 她唱了一首莫扎特令人愉快的短歌。|近义词：song, ditty, aria, melody|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "cacophony" if contrasting with the pleasant nature of a canzonet) |noun

Etymology:
From Italian *canzonetta*, diminutive of *canzone* ("song"), from Provençal *canso*, ultimately from Latin *cantio* ("song").
