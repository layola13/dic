nonlimiting:/nɒnˈlɪmɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非限制性的，非限定性的
Serving as an example or illustration that does not exhaust the possibilities.  Not intended to be exhaustive or exclusive.
The list of examples is nonlimiting, meaning there are other possible options.  这些例子并非详尽无遗，意味着还有其他可能的选择。
synonyms: inclusive, illustrative, exemplary, non-exhaustive
antonyms: limiting, restrictive, exclusive, exhaustive
词性: 形容词
