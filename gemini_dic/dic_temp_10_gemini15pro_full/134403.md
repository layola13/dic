nonintuitively:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtjuːɪtɪvli/ /ˌnɑːnɪnˈtuːɪtɪvli/
|adv.|非直觉地；反直觉地|Not in a way that is obvious or easy to understand without careful thought.|The software, nonintuitively, requires you to save your work before opening a new file.  这个软件反直觉地要求你在打开新文件之前保存你的工作。|counterintuitively, unexpectedly, surprisingly|intuitively, obviously, clearly|adverb
