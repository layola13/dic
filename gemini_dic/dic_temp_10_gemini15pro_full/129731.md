loanword: /ˈloʊnˌwɜːrd/ | n. | 外来词 | A word adopted from one language and incorporated into another language without translation. |  Many English words are loanwords from French. 许多英语单词是从法语借来的。| synonyms: borrowing, adopted word | antonyms: native word, inherited word | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From loan + word.  The term reflects the idea that the adopting language "borrows" the word from the source language.
