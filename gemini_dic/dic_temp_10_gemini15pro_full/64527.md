herpetologist:/ˌhɜːrpəˈtɒlədʒɪst/ /ˌhɜːrpəˈtɑːlədʒɪst/| n.|爬虫学家|A scientist who studies reptiles and amphibians.|Example: The herpetologist carefully documented the behavior of the venomous snake.  这位爬虫学家仔细记录了毒蛇的行为。|Example:  The herpetologist discovered a new species of frog in the rainforest. 这位爬虫学家在雨林中发现了一种新的青蛙。|近义词:reptile and amphibian specialist|反义词:|词性:noun
