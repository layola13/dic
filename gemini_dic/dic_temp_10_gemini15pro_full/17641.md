bechamel:/ˌbeɪʃəˈmɛl/ | n. | 白汁; 法式奶油沙司 | A rich white sauce made with butter, flour, and milk, often flavored with nutmeg, used as a base for other sauces. |  The lasagna was layered with a rich bechamel. 千层面是用浓郁的白汁分层的。| synonyms: white sauce, cream sauce | antonyms: N/A | noun
