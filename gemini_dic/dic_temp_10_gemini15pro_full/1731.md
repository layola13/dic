acquaintednesses: /əˈkweɪntɪdnəsɪz/
n.
熟人; 相识的人;
The state of being acquainted; familiarity.  A person whom one knows slightly.
She has a wide circle of acquaintances. 她有很多熟人。
I'm pleased to make your acquaintance. 很高兴认识你。

近义词: acquaintances, connections, contacts
反义词: strangers

词性: noun (plural)

词源: Late Middle English: from acquaint + -ance, modelled on Old French acointance.
