knar:/nɑːr/
noun
节; 节疤; (尤指树木的)瘤, 粗大的节
A knot or protuberance, especially in wood.
The old oak tree was covered in knars.  这棵老橡树上布满了节疤。
近义词:knot, burl, node
反义词: smoothness

词性: noun
