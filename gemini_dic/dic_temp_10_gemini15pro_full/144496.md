outtinkle: /aʊtˈtɪŋkəl/
verb
胜过(铃声)；以更清脆的铃声盖过
To surpass or outdo in tinkling; to ring or chime more clearly or musically than.
The wind chimes outtinkled the church bells. 风铃声胜过了教堂的钟声。
近义词: outsound, outdo, surpass
反义词: be drowned out by
词性: verb
