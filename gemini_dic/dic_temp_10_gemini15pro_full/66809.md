expansibility:/ɪkˌspændsəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 膨胀性，扩展性 |The expansibility of a gas allows it to fill any container. | 例句：The expansibility of the metal allows it to be shaped into various forms.  这种金属的延展性使它可以被塑造成各种形状。

例句：The expansibility of his lungs was impressive. 他的肺活量令人印象深刻。

近义词：flexibility, elasticity, stretchability | 反义词：inflexibility, rigidity, stiffness | 词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From expand + -ibility.  Expand comes from Latin expandere, meaning "to spread out".  The suffix -ibility denotes the capacity or quality of being expandable.
