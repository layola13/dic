countermand:/ˌkaʊntərˈmænd/ /ˌkaʊntərˈmɑːnd/ | v. | 撤销，取消（命令）；收回（成命） |  To revoke or cancel (a command or order); to recall or rescind by a contrary order. | The general countermanded the attack. 将军取消了进攻。 |  revoke, rescind, cancel, annul, override, overrule |  authorize, permit, allow, approve, order | verb

countermand:/ˌkaʊntərˈmænd/ /ˌkaʊntərˈmɑːnd/ | n. | 取消，撤销（命令） | An order revoking a previous command. | The countermand arrived just in time to prevent the disaster. 取消命令及时到达，阻止了灾难的发生。 | revocation, cancellation, repeal | authorization, permission, approval | noun
