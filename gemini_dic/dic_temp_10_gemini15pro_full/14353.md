autop:/ˈɔːtɒp/ (UK) /ˈɑːtɑːp/ (US)| n.|验尸，尸体解剖|A post-mortem examination to discover the cause of death. |例句：The autop revealed the cause of death to be a heart attack. 验尸结果显示死因是心脏病发作。

例句：An autop was performed to determine the exact cause of death. 进行了一次尸检以确定确切的死因。


近义词：post-mortem, necropsy|反义词:|词性:noun
