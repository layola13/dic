contorted:/kənˈtɔːrtɪd/ /kənˈtɔːrdɪd/| adj.|扭曲的，歪曲的|twisted or strained out of shape, often violently.  This can refer to physical objects or abstract concepts like truth or facts.|Her face was contorted with pain. 她的脸因痛苦而扭曲。|近义词：twisted, deformed, warped, distorted|反义词:straight, natural, undistorted|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin contortus, past participle of contorquēre "to twist violently," from com- "together" + torquēre "to twist."
