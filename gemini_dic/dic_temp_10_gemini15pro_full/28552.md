cardiometry:/ˌkɑːrdioʊˈmɛtri/| n. |心率测量；心功能测定|The study and measurement of the heart's activity, especially its force and rate.  It involves techniques and instruments to assess various aspects of cardiac function, including heart rate, blood pressure, and cardiac output.|Cardiometry revealed a significant decrease in the patient's cardiac output after surgery.  手术后心率测量显示病人的心输出量显著下降。|Near synonyms:  cardiac monitoring, cardiac assessment|Antonym: N/A|noun
