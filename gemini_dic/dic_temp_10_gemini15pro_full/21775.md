bobac:/ˈboʊbæk/| n. |土拨鼠，旱獭|A large, stout-bodied ground squirrel of mountainous areas of Asia and eastern Europe.|例句：The bobac whistled a warning to its colony. 土拨鼠向它的群体吹响了警报。

例句：Bobacs are known for their digging abilities. 土拨鼠以其挖掘能力而闻名。|近义词：marmot, groundhog, woodchuck|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


词源: From Russian *бобак* (bobak), from Turkic, perhaps ultimately from Mongolian *баба* (baba, “grandfather”).
