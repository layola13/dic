flameproofers:/ˈfleɪmpruːfərz/
noun
防火员，阻燃剂施加者
People who apply flameproofing substances or make something flameproof.
The flameproofers treated the curtains with a special chemical. 防火员用一种特殊的化学物质处理了窗帘。
Fire retardant applicators ensured the safety of the building materials. 阻燃剂施加者确保了建筑材料的安全性。
fireproofing technicians, fire retardant specialists
flammable material handlers
