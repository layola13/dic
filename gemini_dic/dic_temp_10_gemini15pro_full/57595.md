drapabilities:/ˌdreɪpəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. |悬垂性，垂坠感| The quality of a fabric that allows it to hang in graceful folds.  It refers to how well a fabric falls or drapes, particularly when used in clothing or curtains. | The dress's drapabilities were stunning, flowing elegantly around her. 这件衣服的垂坠感令人惊艳，优雅地在她周围飘动。| The heavy velvet curtains had excellent drapabilities, creating a luxurious feel in the room. 厚重的丝绒窗帘垂坠感极佳，为房间营造出奢华的感觉。 | synonyms: drape, flow, hang, fall | antonyms: stiffness, rigidity | noun
