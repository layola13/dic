aleutian:/əˈluːʃən/ | adj. n.| 阿留申的；阿留申群岛的；阿留申群岛居民| Relating to the Aleutian Islands, their inhabitants, or their language. aleut:/ˈæliuːt/ | n. adj.| 阿留申人；阿留申语的| A native or inhabitant of the Aleutian Islands, or relating to their language. | Example: The Aleut people have a rich cultural heritage. 阿留申人有着丰富的文化遗产。| Synonym: Islander| Antonym: (No direct antonym)| Noun/Adjective


Etymology: Both "Aleutian" and "Aleut" are derived from the Russian word "алеут" (aleut), the origin of which is uncertain.  Some theories suggest it's from a Chukchi word, possibly "aliat," meaning "island."
