bostonians:/bɒˈstəʊniənz/ (US) /bɔːˈstəʊniənz/ (UK)
n.
波士顿人；波士顿人的
People who live in or originate from Boston, Massachusetts.
The Bostonians are known for their love of sports. 波士顿人以热爱体育而闻名。
The Bostonians welcomed the new museum. 波士顿人欢迎新的博物馆。
近义词: residents of Boston, Boston natives
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps people from other cities, e.g., New Yorkers)
词性: Noun
