churnability:/ˌtʃɜːrnəˈbɪləti/
n.
可搅乳性，易制成黄油的性质
The ease with which milk or cream can be churned into butter.
The churnability of the cream was affected by the temperature.  这种奶油的可搅乳性受温度影响。
近义词: butterfat content, richness
反义词: resistance to churning
