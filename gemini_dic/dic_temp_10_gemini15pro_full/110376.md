ligniperdous:/lɪɡˈnɪpərdəs/
adjective
毁灭木材的；破坏木头的
destroying wood; wood-destroying.  This word combines "ligni-" (relating to wood) and "perdous" (destructive, causing loss).
The ligniperdous fungus quickly consumed the old oak tree. 这种毁灭木材的真菌迅速吞噬了古老的橡树。
The termites, known for their ligniperdous habits, caused significant damage to the wooden structure.  以其破坏木头的习性而闻名的白蚁对木结构造成了重大损害。
近义词:xylophagous, wood-destroying
反义词:wood-preserving
词性:adjective
