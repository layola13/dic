crazedly:/ˈkreɪzɪdli/| adv.|疯狂地，狂暴地|In a crazed or frenzied manner; insanely.|He laughed crazedly, his eyes wide with fear. 他疯狂地大笑，眼里充满了恐惧。|近义词：madly, frantically, wildly, insanely|反义词：calmly, sanely, rationally|词性:副词
Etymology: From craze + -ed + -ly.  Craze comes from Old Norse *kras, meaning "to shatter," related to crash.
