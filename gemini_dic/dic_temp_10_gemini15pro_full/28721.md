carminic:/kɑːrˈmɪnɪk/| adj.|胭脂红的，洋红色的|A vivid crimson or scarlet red color derived from carminic acid, a natural dye extracted from cochineal insects.|The artist used a carminic pigment to paint the vibrant sunset.|近义词：crimson, scarlet, vermilion|反义词:pale, dull|词性:adjective
carminic acid:/kɑːrˈmɪnɪk ˈæsɪd/| n.|胭脂红酸|A complex organic acid, C₂₂H₂₀O₁₃, that is the principal constituent of cochineal.|Carminic acid is used as a natural food coloring and in cosmetics.|近义词：cochineal extract|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
