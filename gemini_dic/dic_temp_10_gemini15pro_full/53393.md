disalliege:/ˌdɪsəˈliːdʒ/
verb
放弃效忠；解除臣属关系
To renounce allegiance to a lord or sovereign; to detach or separate from allegiance.
The knight disallieged himself from the king after the unjust decree.  这位骑士在国王颁布不公平的法令后宣布不再效忠于他。
The rebels disallieged themselves from the government. 叛乱分子宣布不再效忠政府。
近义词: renounce, abandon, forsake, repudiate, reject
反义词: pledge allegiance, support, uphold
词性:v.
