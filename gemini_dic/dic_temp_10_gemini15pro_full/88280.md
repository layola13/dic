hinterland:/ˈhɪntərlænd/ | n. | 内地，腹地；偏远地区 | a remote and undeveloped area inland from a coast or the banks of a major river; an area lying beyond what is visible or known |  The explorers ventured into the uncharted hinterland. 探险家们冒险进入了未知的内陆地区。 | synonyms: backcountry, outback, bush, wilderness | antonyms:  foreland, coast, metropolis | noun

hinterland:/ˈhɪntərlænd/ | n. | （某个地方的）影响范围，势力范围 | an area or sphere of influence | The company expanded its hinterland into new markets. 该公司将其影响范围扩展到新的市场。| synonyms: sphere of influence, domain, territory,  reach | antonyms:  center, core, heartland | noun
