communicated:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtɪd/
verb
交流，沟通，传达
made (an idea, impression, or feeling) known or understandable to someone.
He communicated his findings to the team. 他把他的发现告诉了团队。
Synonyms: conveyed, imparted, relayed, transmitted
Antonyms: withheld, suppressed
verb

communicated:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtɪd/
past participle
已沟通的，已传达的
having shared or exchanged information, ideas, or feelings.
The message was clearly communicated.  信息被清楚地传达了。
Synonyms: shared, exchanged, conveyed
Antonyms: unshared, withheld
