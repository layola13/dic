henchmanship:/ˈhɛntʃmənˌʃɪp/
n.
帮凶行为；爪牙行径
The act of being a henchman; the actions or practices of a henchman.
His rise to power was marked by violence and henchmanship. 他的掌权过程充满了暴力和爪牙行径。
近义词: complicity, collusion, aiding and abetting
反义词:  resistance, opposition, defiance
词性: 名词
