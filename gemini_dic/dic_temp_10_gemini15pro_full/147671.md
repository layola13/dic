overpotencies:/ˌoʊvərˈpoʊtənsiz/
| n. | 过度效力；超效力；过强药力 |  The state of having excessive potency, especially of a homeopathic remedy diluted beyond the point where any original substance remains, believed by some to increase effectiveness.  |  Some homeopaths believe in the concept of overpotencies, claiming that higher dilutions have stronger effects. 一些顺势疗法医生相信超效力的概念，声称更高的稀释度有更强的效果。 |  hyperpotency,  superpotency |  underpotency, weakness | noun
