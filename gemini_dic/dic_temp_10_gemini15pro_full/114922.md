managee:/ˌmænəˈdʒiː/
noun
被管理者；受托人
a person who is managed or overseen by someone else.
The managee was unhappy with the new policies implemented by the manager.  被管理者对经理实施的新政策感到不满。
The trustee acted as a managee for the beneficiary's estate. 受托人作为受益人遗产的管理人。
近义词: subordinate, employee, charge
反义词: manager, supervisor, boss
词性:n.
