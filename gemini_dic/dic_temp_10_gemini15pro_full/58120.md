droppingly:/ˈdrɒpɪŋli/| adv.| 垂头丧气地，沮丧地|In a manner that suggests sadness or dejection; with drooping spirits.|She walked droppingly from the room after hearing the bad news. 她听到坏消息后垂头丧气地走出了房间。|例句：He sat droppingly in the chair, staring at the floor. 他沮丧地坐在椅子上，盯着地板。|近义词：dejectedly, sadly, despondently|反义词：cheerfully, happily|词性: 副词

词源:  From the verb "droop" (to hang or bend down limply) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Old Norse *drupa.
