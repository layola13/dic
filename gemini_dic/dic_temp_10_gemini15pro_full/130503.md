nonaccidental:/ˌnɒnˌæksɪˈdɛnt(ə)l/
adjective
非偶然的；故意的；(尤指儿童受伤)人为造成的
Not occurring by chance; deliberate, especially of injuries inflicted on children.
例句：
The doctor suspected nonaccidental injuries.
医生怀疑是人为造成的伤害。

The police are investigating the child's nonaccidental death.
警方正在调查这名儿童的非正常死亡。

近义词：deliberate, intentional, purposeful
反义词：accidental, unintentional
词性：adjective
