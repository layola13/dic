minnow:/ˈmɪnoʊ/ (US), /ˈmɪnəʊ/ (UK)| n.|米诺鱼，小鱼，无足轻重的人|A small freshwater fish, typically one of the carp family. Also used figuratively to refer to an insignificant or unimportant person.|例句：The minnows darted through the shallows. 小鱼在浅滩中飞快地游动。

例句：He felt like a minnow swimming amongst sharks in the corporate world. 在企业界，他感觉自己像一条在鲨鱼群中游动的小鱼。|近义词：tidler, small fry, guppy|反义词：whale, giant|词性：名词
