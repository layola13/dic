awny:/ˈɔːni/| adj.|  芒状的，似芒的；有芒的 |Covered with awns or similar bristle-like structures.  Resembling an awn. |The awny wheat swayed in the breeze. 麦芒在微风中摇曳。|近义词：bristly, barbed, hairy|反义词: smooth, glabrous|词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *awen, from Old English *ægen, from Proto-Germanic *ahwō. Cognate with Dutch awn, German Ahne. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ekʷ- (“sharp, pointed”).
