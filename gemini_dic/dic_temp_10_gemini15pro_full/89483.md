honeyful:/ˈhʌnifʊl/| adj.|充满甜蜜的，甜蜜的|Resembling or characteristic of honey; sweet.  A honeyful smile.  A honeyful voice.|Her honeyful laughter filled the room. 她的甜蜜笑声充满了整个房间。|近义词：sweet, sugary, mellifluous|反义词:bitter, sour|词性:形容词
词源: "Honey" + "-ful" (suffix indicating fullness or abundance).  Relatively modern coinage, not found in older dictionaries.
