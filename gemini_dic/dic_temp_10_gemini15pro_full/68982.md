fatigue:/fəˈtiːɡ/ /fəˈtig/| n. v. |疲劳，疲乏；劳累|Extreme tiredness resulting from mental or physical exertion or illness.|She was suffering from fatigue.|近义词：tiredness, exhaustion, weariness|反义词:energy, vigor|n.

fatigue:/fəˈtiːɡ/ /fəˈtig/|v. |使疲劳；使疲乏|To cause someone to feel tired or exhausted.|The long journey fatigued the children.|近义词：tire, exhaust, weary|反义词:energize, invigorate|v.
