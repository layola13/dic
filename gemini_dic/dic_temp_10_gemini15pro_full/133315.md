nonexploratory:/ˌnɒnɪkˈsplɔːrətɔːri/ /ˌnɑːnɪkˈsplɔːrətɔːri/
adjective
非探索性的
Not involving or based on exploration or investigation.  Characterized by a lack of exploration or investigation.
The study used a nonexploratory, confirmatory approach to test the hypothesis.  这项研究使用了一种非探索性的、验证性的方法来检验假设。
The team took a nonexploratory approach, focusing on known solutions.  该团队采取了非探索性的方法，专注于已知的解决方案。
近义词: confirmatory, descriptive, noninvestigative
反义词: exploratory, investigative, inquiring
词性: adjective
