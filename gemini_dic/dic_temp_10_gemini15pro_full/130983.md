nonathletically:/ˌnɑːnˌæθˈletɪkli/
adverb
非运动地；不擅长运动地
in a way that does not involve athletic skill or physical prowess.
He moved nonathletically, stumbling over his own feet. 他笨手笨脚地走着，差点被自己的脚绊倒。
nonathletic, clumsily, awkwardly
athletically, skillfully, gracefully
词性: 副词
