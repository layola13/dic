inconvincible:/ˌɪnkənˈvɪnsəbl/
adjective
不可信的；无法说服的；不能使人信服的
not able to be convinced; too stubborn to change one’s mind
例句：His arguments were weak and inconvincible. 他的论点很薄弱，没有说服力。

例句：The evidence presented was inconvincible and the jury acquitted the defendant. 提出的证据不足以令人信服，陪审团宣判被告无罪。

近义词：unpersuasive, unconvincing, implausible, doubtful
反义词：convincing, persuasive, credible, believable
词性：adjective
