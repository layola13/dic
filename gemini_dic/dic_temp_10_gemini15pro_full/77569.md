gearing:/ˈɡɪərɪŋ/ | n. | 传动装置；齿轮传动；(公司)资本与负债的比率 | the system of gears used to transmit power in a machine, especially in a motor vehicle; the ratio of a company's debt to its equity capital | The car's gearing was too high for the steep hill. 这辆车的传动装置对于陡峭的山坡来说太高了。| synonyms: gear train, transmission, leverage | antonyms: N/A | noun
