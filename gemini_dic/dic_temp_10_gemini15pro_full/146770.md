overhype:/ˌoʊvərˈhaɪp/| n. v.|过度炒作，过分宣传|Excessive promotion or publicity, often creating unrealistic expectations. (n.) To promote or publicize excessively. (v.)|There was so much overhype surrounding the movie that it was bound to disappoint. 这部电影被过度炒作，注定会令人失望。  The marketing team overhyped the product, leading to customer dissatisfaction. 营销团队过度宣传了该产品，导致客户不满意。|近义词：exaggeration, hype|反义词:understatement|n. v.

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "hype" (meaning "deceptive or exaggerated publicity")
