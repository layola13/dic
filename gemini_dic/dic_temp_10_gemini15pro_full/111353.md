livener:/ˈlaɪvənər/| n.|兴奋剂，使活跃的东西|Something that enlivens; something that makes things more lively or interesting.|The band played a lively livener to get the crowd going. 乐队演奏了一首活泼的兴奋剂来带动人群。|近义词：stimulant, tonic, energizer|反义词：depressant, sedative|noun

Etymology->
From the verb "liven" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person or thing that performs the action of the verb). "Liven" comes from the adjective "live" (meaning "alive, active") + "-en" (suffix meaning "to make or become").  The adjective "live" ultimately derives from Proto-Germanic *libjaz.
