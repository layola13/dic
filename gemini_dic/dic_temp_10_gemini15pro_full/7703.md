anomalous:/əˈnɒmələs/ (US) /əˈnɑːmələs/ (UK)| adj.|异常的，反常的|Deviating from what is standard, normal, or expected.|The anomalous data point didn't fit with the rest of the results. 这个异常的数据点与其余的结果不符。|The doctor noticed an anomalous growth on the patient's arm. 医生注意到病人手臂上有一个异常的生长物。|近义词：aberrant, atypical, irregular, unusual, exceptional|反义词：normal, typical, regular, usual, expected|词性:形容词

anomalousness:/əˈnɒmələsnəs/ (US) /əˈnɑːmələsnəs/ (UK)| n.|异常，反常|The quality or state of being anomalous.|The anomalousness of the situation made everyone uneasy.  这种情况的异常性使每个人都感到不安。|The researchers were intrigued by the anomalousness of the findings. 研究人员对这些发现的异常性很感兴趣。|近义词：abnormality, irregularity, atypicality|反义词：normality, regularity, typicality|词性:名词


词源:  Late 16th century: from late Latin anomalus, from Greek anōmalos, from an- ‘not’ + homalos ‘even’.
