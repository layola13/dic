filter:/ˈfɪltər/ /ˈfɪltər/ | n. vt. vi.|过滤器，滤镜；过滤；渗透|A porous device for removing impurities or solid particles from a liquid or gas.|The coffee filter keeps the grounds out of the brewed coffee. 咖啡滤纸将咖啡渣与煮好的咖啡分离开来。|近义词：sieve, strainer, screen|反义词:pollute, contaminate|noun
filter:/ˈfɪltər/ /ˈfɪltər/ | vt. vi.|过滤；渗透|To pass (a liquid, gas, light, or sound) through a device to remove unwanted material.|She filtered the gravy to remove the lumps. 她过滤了肉汁以去除块状物。|近义词：purify, refine, strain|反义词:pollute, contaminate|verb
filter:/ˈfɪltər/ /ˈfɪltər/ | vi.|渗透，透过|To move gradually in a specified direction.|Sunlight filtered through the trees. 阳光透过树木洒下来。|近义词：percolate, seep, trickle|反义词:block, obstruct|verb
