nonsociableness:/ˌnɑːnˌsōʃəˈbɪlnəs/
n.
不善交际;孤僻
The state or quality of being unsociable; unsociability.
His nonsociableness made it difficult for him to make friends. 他的不善交际使他难以交朋友。
Synonyms: unsociability, introversion, reclusiveness, aloofness
Antonyms: sociability, gregariousness, extroversion
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + sociable + -ness.
Non-:  prefix meaning "not"
Sociable: from Late Latin sociabilis "companionable,"  from Latin sociare "to associate," from socius "companion, ally"
-ness: suffix forming nouns expressing state or condition
