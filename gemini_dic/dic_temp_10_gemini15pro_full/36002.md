codger:/ˈkɒdʒə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːdʒər/ (US)| n. |古怪老头；老家伙|An often mildly eccentric elderly man.|例句：He's a bit of a codger, but he's harmless. 他有点古怪，但没有恶意。

例句：The old codger sat on the park bench feeding the pigeons.  这位老家伙坐在公园的长椅上喂鸽子。


近义词：old man, geezer, fellow|反义词:youngster, youth|词性:noun
