paramount:/ˈpærəmaʊnt/| adj.|最重要的，主要的，至高无上的|Of chief importance; supreme.|例句：Safety is paramount. 安全至上。|例句：Education is of paramount importance. 教育至关重要。|近义词：supreme, chief, principal, dominant, overriding|反义词：minor, secondary, insignificant|词性：形容词

paramount:/ˈpærəmaʊnt/| n.|最高统治者，元首 (古用法，现已少用)|A supreme ruler.|例句：The paramount ruled the land with an iron fist. (古用法) 最高统治者以铁腕统治这片土地。|例句：He acted as paramount in the small kingdom. (古用法) 他在这个小王国里充当最高统治者。|近义词：ruler, sovereign, monarch|反义词：subject, subordinate|词性：名词

词源:
From Old French *paramont ‘above’, based on Latin paramontanus ‘rising above’, from para- ‘beside, beyond’ + montanus ‘of a mountain’ (from mons, mont- ‘mountain’). The original sense was ‘at the top, supreme in rank’.
