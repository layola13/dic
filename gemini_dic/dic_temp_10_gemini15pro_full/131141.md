nonblooming:/ˌnɒnˈbluːmɪŋ/
adjective
不开花的，不盛开的
not blooming; not producing flowers.
The nonblooming orchid was a disappointment.  这株不开花的兰花真令人失望。
The nursery had a section for nonblooming plants.  苗圃里有一块区域专门放不开花的植物。
Near synonyms: flowerless, barren, sterile
Antonyms: blooming, flowering, blossoming
word origin: non- + blooming
