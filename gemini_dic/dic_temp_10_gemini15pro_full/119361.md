microcolony: /ˈmaɪkroʊˌkɒləni/ (US) /ˈmaɪkrəʊˌkɒləni/ (UK)| n. |微菌落; 微型殖民地|A small colony of bacteria, cells, or other microorganisms, typically grown on a solid medium and visible only with a microscope. |The researchers observed the formation of microcolonies on the agar plate. 研究人员观察到琼脂平板上微菌落的形成。| The bacteria formed microcolonies within 24 hours. 细菌在 24 小时内形成了微型菌落。 | synonyms: micro-colony, mini-colony | antonyms: macrocolony | noun
