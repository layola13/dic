catholicize: /kəˈθɑːləˌsaɪz/ /kəˈθɒlɪsaɪz/
verb
使皈依天主教;使普遍化
To convert to Catholicism; to make universal or more general.
例句：The missionaries tried to catholicize the indigenous people.  传教士试图使土著居民皈依天主教。

例句：The new CEO wants to catholicize the company's appeal to attract a broader customer base. 新任首席执行官希望将公司的吸引力普遍化，以吸引更广泛的客户群。

近义词：convert, proselytize, universalize
反义词：secularize
词性：v.
