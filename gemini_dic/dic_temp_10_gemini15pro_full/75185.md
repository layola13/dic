frizzier:/ˈfrɪzɪər/| adj.|更卷曲的，更毛躁的|Comparative form of frizzy: more frizzy.  Meaning having more small, tight curls or being more rough and tangled (especially of hair).|Her hair was frizzier than usual because of the humidity. 她的头发因为潮湿比平时更卷曲毛躁了。|The dog's fur was frizzier after playing in the rain.  这只狗的毛在雨中玩耍后更毛躁了。|近义词：curlier, kinkier, more unruly|反义词：straighter, smoother, sleeker|词性:形容词

frizzy:/ˈfrɪzi/| adj.|卷曲的，毛躁的|Having small, tight curls or being rough and tangled (especially of hair).|My hair gets frizzy in humid weather. 我的头发在潮湿的天气里会变得毛躁。|The sheep's wool was thick and frizzy.  绵羊的羊毛又厚又卷曲。|近义词：curly, kinky, unruly|反义词：straight, smooth, sleek|词性:形容词
