pagoda:/pəˈɡoʊdə/| n. |宝塔；塔|A tiered tower with multiple eaves, common in East and Southeast Asia, typically associated with Buddhism.|例句：We visited a beautiful pagoda overlooking the valley. 我们参观了一座俯瞰山谷的美丽的宝塔。

例句：The ancient pagoda was intricately decorated with carvings. 这座古老的宝塔装饰着复杂的雕刻。|近义词：tower, temple, shrine|反义词: (No direct antonym exists.  Consider "modern building" for a contrasting concept)|词性:noun
