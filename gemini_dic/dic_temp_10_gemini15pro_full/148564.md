例句：The government's overt attempts to control the media were met with widespread criticism. 政府公开控制媒体的企图遭到了广泛的批评。|近义词：apparent, evident, explicit, obvious, public, unconcealed|反义词：covert, hidden, secret, clandestine, disguised|词性:形容词
