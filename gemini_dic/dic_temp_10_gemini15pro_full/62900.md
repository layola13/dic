enlimn:/ɪnˈlɪm/ | vt. | 描绘，描画；用鲜明色彩描绘 | To depict or describe vividly, especially in words; to illuminate or highlight. |  The author enlimned the beauty of the sunset in his latest novel.  作者在他最新的小说中描绘了日落的美丽。

The artist enlimned the scene with delicate brushstrokes.  艺术家用精细的笔触描绘了这个场景。 | 近义词：depict, portray, illustrate, describe, delineate | 反义词：obscure, blur, confuse | 词性: verb
词源: From Middle English *enlymnen, from Anglo-Norman enluminer, enluminier (“to illuminate, adorn”), from Old French enluminer, from en- + luminer (“to illuminate”), from Latin illuminare, from in- + lumen (“light”).
