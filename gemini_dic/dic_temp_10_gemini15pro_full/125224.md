multicourses:/ˌmʌltiˈkɔːrsɪz/ | n. | 多道菜 |  A meal consisting of several courses served one after the other.  |  The restaurant offered a delicious multicourses meal for the special occasion. 这家餐厅为这个特殊场合提供了一顿美味的多道菜。| synonyms: multi-course meal, tasting menu | antonyms: single course, à la carte | noun
