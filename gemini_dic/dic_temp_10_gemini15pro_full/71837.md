fleetnesses:/ˈfliːtnɪsɪz/
noun
迅速，敏捷
The quality of being fleet; swiftness.
The fleetnesses of a deer allowed it to escape the predator.  鹿的敏捷使它能够逃脱捕食者。
swiftness, rapidity, speed, velocity, celerity
slowness, sluggishness, tardiness
