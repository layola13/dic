multistep:/ˈmʌltiˌstɛp/ | adj. | 多步骤的，多阶段的 | involving multiple steps or stages | The synthesis of this compound is a multistep process.  这种化合物的合成是一个多步骤的过程。| The project required a multistep approach. 这个项目需要一个多步骤的方法。|  近义词: multistage, multiphase | 反义词: single-step, one-step | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
"multi-" (from Latin "multus" meaning "many, much") + "step" (from Old English "stæpe" meaning "a step, pace, stride")
