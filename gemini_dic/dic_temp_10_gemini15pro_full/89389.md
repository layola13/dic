homoplasty:/ˈhɒməˌplæsti/ /ˈhoʊməˌplæsti/| n.|同塑性，趋同|The wings of bats and birds are an example of homoplasty, as they evolved independently for flight.|Bats and birds both have wings, but this is a case of homoplasty, not homology, since their common ancestor did not have wings.|近义词：convergence, analogy|反义词：homology|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὁμός (homós, “same”) + πλάσσειν (plássein, “to mold”).
