lipogrammatist:/ˌlɪpəˈɡræmətɪst/ | n. | 字谜家；限字作家 | A writer who deliberately avoids using a particular letter of the alphabet. |  Example 1: The lipogrammatist skillfully crafted a novel without using the letter 'e'.  这个字谜家巧妙地创作了一部没有使用字母“e”的小说。

Example 2:  He challenged himself to become a lipogrammatist, writing a short story without the letter 'a'. 他挑战自己成为一名字谜家，写一个没有字母“a”的短篇小说。 | synonym: wordplay artist, constrained writer | antonym:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps) unrestricted writer | noun
