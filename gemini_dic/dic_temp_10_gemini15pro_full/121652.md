mispersuasion:/ˌmɪspərˈsweɪʒn/
n.
误导，错误的劝说
The act of persuading someone to believe something that is not true or to do something that is not wise.
The advertisement's mispersuasion led many customers to buy a faulty product.  这则广告的误导导致许多顾客购买了有缺陷的产品。
misleading, misguidance, deception
persuasion, guidance, enlightenment
noun
