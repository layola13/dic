insulters:/ɪnˈsʌltərz/
n.
侮辱者；辱骂者
People who insult others; those who use abusive or offensive language.
The online forum was overrun with insulters who attacked anyone with a differing opinion.  网上论坛上充斥着侮辱者，他们攻击任何持不同意见的人。
The insulters were banned from the platform for their hateful comments.  由于发表仇恨言论，这些辱骂者被禁止进入该平台。
Synonyms: abusers, offenders, bullies, detractors
Antonyms: praisers, commenders, flatterers
