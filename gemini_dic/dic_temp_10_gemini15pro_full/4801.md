algometer:/ælˈɡɒmɪtər/ | n. | 痛觉计，测痛仪 | An instrument for measuring the sensitivity to pain, especially by determining the amount of pressure required to produce a sensation of pain. | The doctor used an algometer to assess the patient's pain threshold. 医生用测痛仪评估病人的痛阈。 |  Example 2: The algometer registered a significant increase in pressure tolerance after the patient received pain medication. 服用止痛药后，测痛仪记录到患者的耐压程度显著提高。|Synonyms: dolorimeter | Antonyms: None | Noun
