droumy: /ˈdruːmi/ | adj. | 昏昏沉沉的，无精打采的 | Feeling sluggish or drowsy; lacking energy.  |  Example:  I felt droumy after that heavy meal.  我吃完那顿大餐后感到昏昏沉沉的。

Example: The hot weather made me feel droumy all afternoon. 炎热的天气让我整个下午都感到无精打采。 | 近义词: drowsy, sleepy, lethargic, sluggish | 反义词: energetic, lively, alert, awake | 词性: adjective

词源: Likely a variant or dialectal form of "drowsy," which comes from Old English "drusian" meaning "to become sluggish" or "to sink."
