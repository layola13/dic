crystallizability:/ˌkrɪstələˈzaɪzəbɪləti/ | n. | 结晶性，结晶能力 | The ability of a substance to form crystals.  The crystallizability of this compound is high under these conditions. | 例句：The slow cooling process increased the crystallizability of the solution.  缓慢的冷却过程增加了溶液的结晶性。

例句：The crystallizability of honey is affected by its sugar content. 蜂蜜的结晶性受其含糖量的影响。 | 近义词：crystallization tendency, propensity for crystallization | 反义词：amorphousness, non-crystallizability | 词性：noun
