panegyris:/ˌpænɪˈdʒaɪrɪs/| n.|颂词，赞美，盛大的集会|A formal or elaborate eulogy or encomium; a public speech or writing in praise of someone or something. Also, a festival or celebration, especially one held in ancient Greece.|The poet delivered a moving panegyris at the statesman's funeral. 诗人在政治家的葬礼上发表了感人的颂词。|近义词：eulogy, encomium, tribute, accolade, laudation|反义词：criticism, condemnation, denunciation|词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *panegyris*, from Greek *panēgyris* "a general or national assembly," from *pan* "all" + *agyr* "assembly".
