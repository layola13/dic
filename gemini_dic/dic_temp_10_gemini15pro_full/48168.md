decompartmentalized:/ˌdiːkəmˌpɑːrtˈmɛntəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 去隔间的，去部门化的，跨部门的 |  Removed from compartments or divided sections; having barriers between parts removed or lessened.  It also refers to a more open and collaborative approach, breaking down traditional silos. | The company benefited from a decompartmentalized approach to project management, allowing teams to share information and resources more easily. 公司受益于项目管理的去部门化方法，使团队能够更轻松地共享信息和资源。 | The university is trying to decompartmentalize its curriculum and encourage interdisciplinary studies. 大学正在尝试打破其课程的隔阂，鼓励跨学科研究。 | integrated, holistic, unified, collaborative | compartmentalized, segmented, isolated | adjective
