corniform:/ˈkɔːrnɪfɔːrm/
adjective
角形的；角状的
shaped like a horn; resembling a horn.
The antelope has corniform horns.  这只羚羊有角状的角。
The demon had a corniform headdress. 恶魔戴着角状的头饰。
近义词:horn-shaped, hornlike
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider: smooth, rounded)
词性:adjective
