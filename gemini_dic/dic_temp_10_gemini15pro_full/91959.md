hypermodest:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɑːdɪst/ /ˌhaɪpərˈmɒdɪst/ | adj. | 过分谦虚的，极其谦逊的 | Excessively modest; unduly self-deprecating.  Characterized by an exaggerated or excessive display of humility. | He gave a hypermodest account of his achievements, downplaying his significant contributions to the project. 他对自己的成就做了过分谦虚的描述，淡化了他对项目的重大贡献。

He was hypermodest about his role in the rescue, claiming he'd just been in the right place at the right time. 他对自己在救援中的作用过分谦虚，声称自己只是碰巧在正确的时间出现在正确的地点。 | 近义词: excessively modest, unduly humble, self-deprecating, unassuming | 反义词: boastful, arrogant, conceited, immodest | 词性: adjective


词源:

"hyper-" (prefix): from Greek "hyper" (over, above, beyond)
"modest": from Latin "modestus" (keeping due measure, moderate, modest)
