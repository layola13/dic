nonliabilities:/ˌnɒnlaɪəˈbɪlətiz/
|n.| 非负债，非债务 |Amounts owed to creditors are liabilities, while assets like cash and buildings are nonliabilities.| The company's nonliabilities included their valuable patents and trademarks. 公司的非负债包括他们宝贵的专利和商标。| 近义词:assets, possessions| 反义词:liabilities, debts| 词性:名词

nonliability:/ˌnɒnlaɪəˈbɪləti/
|n.| 免责，无责任 | The contract included a clause of nonliability for accidental damage. 合同中包含了对意外损害免责的条款。|He claimed nonliability for the damage, arguing he wasn't involved. 他声称对损害不负责任，辩称他没有参与。 |近义词:immunity, exemption| 反义词:liability, responsibility| 词性:名词
