hexose:/ˈhɛksoʊs/ | n. |己糖|A simple sugar (monosaccharide) containing six carbon atoms.|Glucose is a hexose and is the main source of energy for the body. 葡萄糖是一种己糖，是人体的主要能量来源。|Synonyms: monosaccharide (when referring to a single hexose unit) |Antonyms: None|Noun
