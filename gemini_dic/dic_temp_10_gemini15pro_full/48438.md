deductive:/dɪˈdʌktɪv/
adjective
演绎的; 推理的
Characterized by or based on the inference of particular instances from a general law.
例句：
Sherlock Holmes was famous for his deductive reasoning.
夏洛克·福尔摩斯以其演绎推理而闻名。

例句：
The deductive method is often used in scientific research.
演绎法常用于科学研究。

近义词：reasoned, logical, inferential
反义词：inductive
词性：adjective
