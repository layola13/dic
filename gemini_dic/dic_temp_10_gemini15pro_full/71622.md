flatiron:/ˈflætˌaɪərn/| n.|熨斗；三角形的建筑物|A flatiron is a metal tool with a handle and a flat, heated surface, used for smoothing clothes or fabrics.  A flatiron can also refer to a triangular building, usually situated on a triangular block formed by the intersection of three streets.|例句：She used a flatiron to press her dress. 她用熨斗熨平了她的连衣裙。

例句：The Flatiron Building in New York City is a famous landmark. 纽约市的熨斗大厦是一座著名的地标。|近义词：(for tool) iron, clothes iron; (for building) wedge-shaped building|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


flatiron:/ˈflætˌaɪərn/| adj.|三角形的|Shaped like a flatiron, triangular.|例句：The flatiron-shaped building stood out against the skyline.  这座三角形建筑在天际线上格外醒目。

例句：The flatiron plot of land was difficult to develop. 这块三角形的地块很难开发。 |近义词：triangular, wedge-shaped|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
