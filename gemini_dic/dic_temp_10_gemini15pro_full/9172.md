antineutrally:/ˌæntiˈnjuːtrəli/
| adv. |反中性地；反对中立|In a manner opposed to neutrality; showing bias or taking sides.  It describes actions or stances that actively oppose or reject the principle of neutrality. |The judge acted antineutrally, clearly favoring the prosecution. 法官的行为有失中立，明显偏袒控方。|The media reported antineutrally on the conflict, siding with one particular group. 媒体对这场冲突的报道有失偏颇，支持了某一特定群体。|biasedly, partially, unfairly|neutrally, impartially, objectively|adverb
