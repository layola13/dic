nonvibrator:/ˌnɒnˈvaɪbreɪtər/ | n. | 非振动器，无振动装置 | Something that does not vibrate.  A device or object that is designed not to produce vibrations or oscillations. | This particular model is a nonvibrator, making it ideal for noise-sensitive environments.  这个型号是非振动型的，非常适合对噪音敏感的环境。| Near synonyms: non-oscillator, stable device | Antonyms: vibrator, oscillator | noun
