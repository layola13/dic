antihistoricalness:/ˌæntiˌhɪsˈtɔːrɪkəlnəs/ | n. | 反历史性；非历史性 | The quality of being opposed to or ignorant of history, or of disregarding historical context. |  His antihistoricalness led him to believe that democracy was a recent invention. 他的反历史性使他相信民主是近代的发明。|  The textbook's antihistoricalness was criticized for presenting a biased narrative. 这本教科书的反历史性因其叙述带有偏见而受到批评。 |  ahistoricity, unhistoricalness | historicalness | noun
