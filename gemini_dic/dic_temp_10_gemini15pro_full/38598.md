concourse:/ˈkɒŋkɔːs/ (UK), /ˈkɑːŋkɔːrs/ (US)| n. |大厅，广场，汇合|A large open space or hall (especially in a public building such as an airport or train station) where crowds gather.|The concourse of Grand Central Terminal is always bustling with activity.  中央车站的大厅总是熙熙攘攘。|Synonyms: hall, foyer, atrium, court, plaza, square|Antonyms:  closet, cubicle|noun

concourse:/ˈkɒŋkɔːs/ (UK), /ˈkɑːŋkɔːrs/ (US)| n. |(人群的)聚集，汇合|A coming together of a large number of people.|There was a concourse of fans outside the stadium waiting for the band to arrive. 大批歌迷聚集在体育场外等候乐队到来。|Synonyms: gathering, assembly, meeting, confluence|Antonyms: dispersal, scattering|noun
