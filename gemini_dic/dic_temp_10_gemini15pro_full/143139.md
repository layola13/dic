outcaught: /ˌaʊtˈkɔːt/
verb
超过，胜过（在抓捕或获取方面）
to surpass or excel in catching or acquiring something
例句：
The experienced detective outcaught the rookie in solving the complex case.
经验丰富的侦探在解决复杂案件方面胜过新手。

The fisherman outcaught his friends, bringing home a boatload of fish.
这位渔夫比他的朋友们捕到的鱼多，带回家满满一船鱼。

近义词：outdo, surpass, excel, outstrip
反义词：lag behind, fall short

词性: v.
