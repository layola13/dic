bondsman:/ˈbɑːndzmən/ /ˈbɒndzmən/| n.| 保证人；债券持有人；奴隶 |A person who acts as surety for another, especially one who pledges money or property as bail.| The bondsman posted bail for the defendant. 保证人为被告支付了保释金。| synonyms: surety, guarantor, bail bondsman | antonyms: defendant, accused | noun

bondsman:/ˈbɑːndzmən/ /ˈbɒndzmən/| n.| 奴隶 |A slave or serf.|  In feudal times, many people were bondsmen tied to the land. 在封建时代，许多人是依附于土地的农奴。| synonyms: slave, serf, bondservant | antonyms: freeman, master | noun
