incontinent:/ɪnˈkɒntɪnənt/ (US) /ɪnˈkɑːntɪnənt/| adj. n.|失禁的，不能自制的；失禁者|Unable to control the discharge of urine or feces; lacking self-restraint.|例句：She became incontinent after the surgery.  她手术后失禁了。

|例句：His incontinent rage was frightening. 他无法控制的愤怒令人恐惧。|近义词：uncontrolled, unrestrained, immoderate|反义词：continent, restrained, controlled|词性:形容词
