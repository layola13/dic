diaphragmatic:/ˌdaɪəfræɡˈmætɪk/
| adj.|膈的，横膈膜的，呼吸的|Relating to or affecting the diaphragm.|She uses diaphragmatic breathing techniques for relaxation. 她用横膈膜呼吸技巧来放松。|近义词:abdominal, respiratory|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider terms like "costal" referring to ribs or "thoracic" referring to the chest)|词性: adjective

diaphragm:/ˈdaɪəfræm/
| n.|隔膜，横膈膜，光圈|A dome-shaped, muscular partition separating the thorax from the abdomen in mammals. It plays a major role in breathing.|The diaphragm contracts and relaxes to control breathing. 横膈膜收缩和放松来控制呼吸。|近义词:midriff|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: noun
