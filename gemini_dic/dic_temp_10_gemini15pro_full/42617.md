counterreform:/ˌkaʊntər rɪˈfɔːrm/ | n. | 反改革，反宗教改革 | A movement to counter the effects of the Reformation, especially the Protestant Reformation. | The Counter-Reformation led to the establishment of new religious orders. 反宗教改革导致了新的宗教秩序的建立。

The Council of Trent was a key part of the Counter-Reformation. 特倫特會議是反宗教改革的關鍵部分。| 近义词: Counter-Reformation (often used interchangeably), Catholic Reformation | 反义词: Reformation | 词性: noun
