nereid:/ˈnɪəriɪd/ /ˈnɪriɪd/ | n. | 海仙女；海中仙女 | A sea nymph; one of the fifty daughters of Nereus. |  例句：The nereid emerged from the waves, her hair dripping with seawater. 海仙女从波浪中浮现，头发滴着海水。

例句：Legends say that nereids would often help sailors lost at sea. 传说海仙女经常帮助在海上迷路的船员。 | 近义词：sea nymph, mermaid, oceanid | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑land nymph - 陆上仙女) | 词性: noun
