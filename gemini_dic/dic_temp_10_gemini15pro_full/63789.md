epicycloid:/ˌɛpɪˈsaɪklɔɪd/ | n. | 外摆线 | A curve traced by a point on the circumference of a circle that rolls without slipping around the outside of a fixed circle. |  A spirograph can be used to draw epicycloids.  一个万花尺可以用来画外摆线。 |  cardioid (when the rolling circle and fixed circle have the same radius) |  hypocycloid | noun
etymology: epi- (Greek: upon, on, over) + cyclo- (Greek: circle) + -oid (Greek: resembling)
