nonrecalcitrance:/ˌnɒnˌrɪˈkæl.sɪ.trən.s/ | n. |  不倔强，顺从，温顺 | The nonrecalcitrance of the students made it easy for the teacher to manage the class. |  Nonrecalcitrance implies a willingness to cooperate and follow instructions.  It's the opposite of being recalcitrant, meaning stubbornly resistant to authority or guidance. | Example:  The dog's nonrecalcitrance made training easy.  狗的温顺使训练变得容易。

Example:  Her nonrecalcitrance was a welcome change after dealing with so many difficult clients.  在她与这么多难缠的客户打交道之后，她的顺从是一个受欢迎的变化。 | 近义词: compliance, obedience, docility, acquiescence, tractability | 反义词: recalcitrance, defiance, resistance, obstinacy, stubbornness | 词性: noun
