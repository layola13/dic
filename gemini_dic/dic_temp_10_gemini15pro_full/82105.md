groener:/ˈɣrunər/ (Dutch pronunciation)| adj.|更绿的; 更环保的|Comparative form of "groen" (green) meaning more green in color or more environmentally friendly.|The trees are groener this year than last. 今年的树比去年更绿了。|近义词:greener, more eco-friendly|反义词:browner, less eco-friendly|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Middle Dutch *groene, from Old Dutch *grōni, from Proto-Germanic *grōniz. Related to English "green".
