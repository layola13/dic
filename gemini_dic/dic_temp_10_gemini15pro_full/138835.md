nunnish:/ˈnʌnɪʃ/
adjective
修道院女似的；古板的；一本正经的
Resembling or characteristic of a nun, especially in being modest, retiring, or prim.
She has a rather nunnish demeanor, always quiet and reserved. 她举止相当像修女，总是安静而矜持。
He gave her a nunnish scolding for her immodest dress. 他因她衣着不端庄而像修女一样责骂她。
Synonyms: prim, proper, demure, modest, reserved, straitlaced, prudish
Antonyms: immodest, flamboyant, outgoing, extroverted, forward
