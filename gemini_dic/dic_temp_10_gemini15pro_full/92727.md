ichnolite:/ˈɪknəlaɪt/| n. | 鱼石；遗迹化石 |A fossil consisting of the fossilized excrement of a fish.  It provides clues about the diet and environment of ancient fish. |  Example 1: The ichnolite discovered in the shale revealed the fish had a diet of small crustaceans.  这块页岩中发现的鱼石表明这种鱼以小型甲壳类动物为食。

Example 2:  Researchers studied the ichnolites to understand the ecosystem of the ancient lake. 研究人员研究了这些遗迹化石，以了解古代湖泊的生态系统。|近义词：coprolite (更通用的粪化石), fossil|反义词: (无直接反义词) |词性:noun
