biacid:/baɪˈæsɪd/| adj. n.|二酸的，二酸|A biacid is a chemical compound containing two acidic hydrogen atoms replaceable by metal atoms.  It can also refer to a substance that can react with two molecules of a monobasic acid.|Citric acid is a tribasic acid, while sulfuric acid is a biacid.|近义词：diacid|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps monoacid for contrast)|词性:adjective, noun
