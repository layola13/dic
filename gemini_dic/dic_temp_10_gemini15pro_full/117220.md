meatiest:/ˈmiːtiɪst/| adj.|最多肉的；内容最丰富的；最有实质性的|Superlative form of meaty: having a lot of meat; (figuratively) substantial or powerful.|This is the meatiest steak I've ever had. 这是我吃过的肉最多的牛排。

The professor's lecture was the meatiest of the semester, full of insightful analysis.  教授的讲座是本学期内容最丰富的，充满了深刻的分析。|近义词：fleshiest, most substantial, richest|反义词：leanest, thinnest, slightest|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *mety*, from Old English *mettig* ("containing meat"), equivalent to meat +‎ -y.  The superlative ending "-est" is a standard English suffix.
