conversant:/ˈkɒnvəsənt/ /kənˈvɜːrsənt/| adj.|熟悉的，精通的，熟练的|Having knowledge or experience of something.|She is conversant with the latest developments in medical technology. 她熟悉医疗技术的最新发展。|例句：He is conversant in several languages. 他精通几种语言。|近义词：familiar, acquainted, proficient, versed, knowledgeable|反义词:unfamiliar, ignorant, unacquainted|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French conversant, present participle of converser ‘converse’, from Latin conversari ‘live with, associate with’.
