compliancy:/kəmˈplaɪənsi/| n.|顺从，服从，合规|The company stressed its compliancy with all regulations.  这家公司强调其遵守所有规章制度。|例句：The government demands strict compliancy with the new law. 政府要求严格遵守新法律。

The level of compliancy among the workers was surprising. 工人们的顺从程度令人惊讶。|近义词：obedience, compliance, conformity, acquiescence|反义词：resistance, defiance, disobedience, noncompliance|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French complaisance, from complaisant ‘obliging’ (see complaisant). The early sense was ‘disposition to please or comply with others’ wishes’.
