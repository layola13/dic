contrast:/ˈkɑːntræst/ (US) /ˈkɒntrɑːst/ (UK)| n. v. |对比;对照;差异;差别|a clear difference between two or more things| The contrast between their lifestyles couldn't be greater. 他们的生活方式天差地别。 |synonyms: difference, distinction, disparity| antonyms: similarity, resemblance| noun

contrast:/ˈkɑːntræst/ (US) /ˈkɒntrɑːst/ (UK)| v. |对比;对照;(使)形成对比;形成对照|to compare two things in order to show the differences between them|  Contrast the two poems and highlight the differences in style. 对照这两首诗，并指出它们风格上的差异。|synonyms: compare, juxtapose, differentiate| antonyms: equate, assimilate| verb
