cwt:/ˌsiːˈdʌbəlˈjuːˈtiː/ | n. | hundredweight (一百重量) | A unit of mass equal to 112 pounds avoirdupois (approximately 50.8 kg) in the UK and some other countries, and 100 pounds avoirdupois (approximately 45.4 kg) in the US and Canada.  |  The shipment weighed 5 cwt. 这批货重5英担。 | Synonym: cental | Antonym: N/A | noun
