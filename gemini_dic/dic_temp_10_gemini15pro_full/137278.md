nonsymptomatic: /ˌnɒnsɪmptəˈmætɪk/ (US) /ˌnɒnsɪmptəˈmætɪk/ (UK)| adj.|无症状的|Not showing any symptoms of a disease, even if infected.|Many people with COVID-19 are nonsymptomatic, making it difficult to track the spread of the virus. 许多新冠肺炎患者是无症状的，这使得追踪病毒的传播变得困难。|Example: The study found a significant number of nonsymptomatic carriers of the disease. 这项研究发现了大量的无症状疾病携带者。

|Example:  Even though she was nonsymptomatic, she still needed to isolate to prevent potential transmission. 即使她没有症状，她仍然需要隔离以防止潜在的传播。
|近义词: asymptomatic, subclinical|反义词: symptomatic|词性: adjective
