painkilling:/ˈpeɪnkɪlɪŋ/| adj. n.|止痛的，止痛药|Relating to or denoting something that relieves pain; a drug that relieves pain.|  She took a painkilling tablet for her headache. 她吃了一片止痛药来治疗头痛。| The painkilling properties of the drug are well documented. 这种药物的止痛特性已被充分证明。|近义词：analgesic, anodyne|反义词: pain-inducing, pain-causing|词性:形容词, 名词
Etymology: pain + killing (from kill - to deprive of life, feeling, or activity)
