hoodlumish:/ˈhuːdləmɪʃ/| adj.|流氓似的，像流氓的，痞里痞气的|Resembling or characteristic of a hoodlum; thuggish.  Having the characteristics of a violent criminal, especially one who is part of a gang. |He had a hoodlumish air about him with his swagger and sneer.  他趾高气扬、一脸冷笑，一副流氓样子。|近义词：thuggish, gangsterish, loutish, hooliganish|反义词:gentlemanly, refined, respectable|词性:形容词
词源:From hoodlum + -ish.  Hoodlum itself has uncertain origins, possibly from a German dialect word meaning "ruffian."
