omohyoid: /ˌoʊmoʊˈhaɪɔɪd/ | n. | 肩胛舌骨肌 | A muscle that depresses the hyoid bone. It originates from the upper border of the scapula and inserts into the lower border of the hyoid bone. |  The omohyoid muscle plays a crucial role in swallowing and speech.  肩胛舌骨肌在吞咽和说话中起着至关重要的作用。

The omohyoid is divided into two bellies connected by an intermediate tendon. 肩胛舌骨肌由中间的肌腱连接的两个肌腹组成。 | 近义词:  (No direct synonyms, as it's a specific muscle)  | 反义词: (No antonyms for an anatomical structure) | 词性: noun
