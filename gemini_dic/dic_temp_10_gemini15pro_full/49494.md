dem:/dɛm/ | pronoun | 他们，她们，它们（they的宾格） |  Objective case of "they," used to refer to a group of people or things previously mentioned. | Example: I saw them at the store. 我在商店看到了他们。 | Example:  Give dem the book. 把书给他们。 | Near Synonyms:  them | Antonyms: None | Pronoun
