masora:/məˈsɔːrə/ /məˈsoʊrə/ | n. |马索拉文本，马索拉注释|The traditional system of vowel points, accents, and other annotations used in Hebrew biblical manuscripts to indicate pronunciation, cantillation, and other textual features.  It also refers to the body of traditions transmitted alongside the text itself.| The Masora ensures the accurate transmission of the Hebrew Bible. 马索拉文本确保了希伯来圣经的准确传承。|  Masoretic Text, Masoretic Notes | N/A | noun
