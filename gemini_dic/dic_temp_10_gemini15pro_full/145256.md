overboard:/ˈoʊvərˌbɔːrd/ (US) /ˈəʊvəbɔːd/ (UK)| adv. adj.| 落水， overboard;  过分， overboard;  在船外， overboard;  从船上掉下， overboard;| Going overboard can be dangerous, especially in rough seas.  落水很危险，尤其是在波涛汹涌的海上。|Synonyms:  over the side, into the water,  excessively,  extravagantly|Antonyms:  on board,  moderately,  reasonably|adverb

overboard:/ˈoʊvərˌbɔːrd/ (US) /ˈəʊvəbɔːd/ (UK)| adj.| 过分的， overboard;|  His reaction was a bit overboard. 他的反应有点过分。|Synonyms:  excessive,  extreme,  immoderate|Antonyms:  moderate,  reasonable,  tempered|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
