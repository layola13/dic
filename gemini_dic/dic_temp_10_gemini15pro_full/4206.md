airheaded:/ˈerˌhɛdɪd/ | adj. | 愚蠢的，轻浮的，傻气的 | Lacking seriousness, good judgment, or concentration; silly or scatterbrained.  | Example:  She made an airheaded comment about the seriousness of the situation. 她对情况的严重性发表了愚蠢的评论。

Example:  His airheaded approach to his studies resulted in failing grades.  他轻率的学习态度导致成绩不及格。 | 近义词: silly, foolish, frivolous, ditzy, scatterbrained, flighty | 反义词: intelligent, sensible, serious, thoughtful, wise | 词性: adjective

词源: airhead (n.) + -ed (suffix).  Airhead originally referred to a person's head being filled with air instead of brains, thus implying stupidity.
