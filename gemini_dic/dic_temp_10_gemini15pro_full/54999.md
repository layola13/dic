dispurvey:/dɪs.pərˈveɪ/
verb
取消供应,停止供应
To deprive of provisions; to dismantle or discontinue the provisioning of.
The army was dispurveyed after the war ended, leaving many soldiers without food or shelter.  战争结束后，军队停止了供应，许多士兵没有食物和住所。
The king dispurveyed the castle, leaving it vulnerable to attack. 国王取消了城堡的供应，使它容易受到攻击。
近义词:deprive, divest, strip
反义词:supply, provide, provision
词性:v.
