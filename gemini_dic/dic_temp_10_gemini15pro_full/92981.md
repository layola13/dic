idiograph:/ˈɪdiəɡræf/ | n. | 表意文字，独特记号 | A character or symbol representing a thing or idea without expressing the sounds that form its name, as in Chinese. |  Example:  Egyptian hieroglyphs are considered idiographs. 埃及象形文字被认为是表意文字。

Example:  Unlike an alphabet, an idiograph doesn't represent a sound but a whole word or concept. 与字母表不同，表意文字不代表声音，而是代表整个单词或概念。| 近义词: ideogram | 反义词: phonogram | 词性: noun
