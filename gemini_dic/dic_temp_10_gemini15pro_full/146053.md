overdub:/ˌoʊvərˈdʌb/| v. n.| 叠录，配音；叠录的音轨| To record additional sound over a previously recorded track, often to enhance or correct it; the resulting recording. | The singer overdubbed the backing vocals after the main track was laid down. 歌手在主音轨录制完成后叠录了背景和声。 | The guitar solo was an overdub, recorded separately and added later. 吉他独奏是叠录的，单独录制后再添加进去。| synonyms: rerecord, dub, overlay | antonyms: erase, delete | v. n.
