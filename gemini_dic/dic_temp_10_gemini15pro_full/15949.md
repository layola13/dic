balog:/ˈbɑːlɒɡ/ | n. | 博客;网络日志 | A weblog, a website containing a writer's or group of writers' own experiences, observations, opinions, etc., and often having images and links to other websites. |  Example 1: She updates her balog daily with photos of her travels. 她每天更新她的博客，上传旅行照片。

Example 2:  He gained a large following through his insightful balog posts. 他通过富有洞察力的博客文章获得了大量粉丝。 | Synonym: blog, weblog | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "offline journal") | Noun
