dispenser:/dɪˈspɛnsər/| n.|自动售货机，药剂师，分配器|A machine or container that dispenses something, typically a liquid or tablets, in measured amounts.  A person who dispenses something, especially medicine.|例句：The soap dispenser was empty.  肥皂机空了。

例句：The pharmacist acts as a dispenser of medication. 药剂师充当药物配发员。|近义词：distributor, vendor, machine, provider|反义词:collector, accumulator|词性:noun
