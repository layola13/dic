coleopteran:/ˌkɒliˈɒptərən/| n. |鞘翅目昆虫；甲虫|An insect of the order Coleoptera, which includes beetles and weevils.  A coleopteran has two pairs of wings, the front pair being hard and thickened to form a protective covering for the hind pair when at rest.|Coleopterans are the largest order of insects. 鞘翅目昆虫是最大的昆虫目。|The museum has a vast collection of coleopterans.  这家博物馆收藏了大量的鞘翅目昆虫。|Synonyms: beetle, weevil|Antonyms: None|noun
