aromaticities:/ˌærəməˈtɪsətiz/| n. |芳香性（复数）|The quality or state of being aromatic; pleasant smell or fragrance.  It often refers to the chemical property of cyclic, planar molecules with delocalized pi electrons, following Hückel's rule (4n+2 pi electrons).|The chemist studied the aromaticities of various benzene derivatives.  化学家研究了各种苯衍生物的芳香性。|Near synonyms: aromas, fragrances, scents|Antonym: odorlessness|noun
