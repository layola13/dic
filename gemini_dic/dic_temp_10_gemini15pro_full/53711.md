disclosed:/dɪˈskloʊzd/
verb|透露，公开，揭露|Made known; revealed.|例句：The company disclosed its financial results. 公司公布了财务业绩。|近义词：revealed, divulged, unveiled, exposed|反义词：concealed, hidden, suppressed
adjective|已公开的，已透露的|Having been made known; revealed.|例句：The disclosed information caused a scandal.  公开的信息引发了丑闻。|近义词：revealed, divulged, unveiled, exposed|反义词：concealed, hidden, suppressed

词源：From Late Latin *disclausus, past participle of disclaudere "to open, reveal," from dis- "apart, away" + claudere "to close."  The sense evolved from "unclose" to "reveal."
