bluenesses:/ˈbluːnɪsɪz/
n.
蓝色的状态；蓝色区域；悲伤
The state or quality of being blue; areas of blue color; instances of sadness or depression.
The bluenesses of the sky were breathtaking. 天空的蓝色令人叹为观止。
shades, tints, hues
grayness, darkness
noun
