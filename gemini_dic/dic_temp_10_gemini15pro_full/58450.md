ducky's:/ˈdʌkiz/| n.|小鸭子的（东西）|Something that belongs to a ducky, usually referring to a toy rubber duck.  It is formed by adding the possessive apostrophe and "s" to "ducky."|例句：The child loves playing with his ducky's squeaker.  孩子喜欢玩他的小鸭子的吱吱叫的玩具。

例句：Look at all the ducky's toys scattered around the bathtub! 看看浴缸周围散落着所有小鸭子的玩具！|近义词：duckling's|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "adult duck's")|词性:noun (possessive)
