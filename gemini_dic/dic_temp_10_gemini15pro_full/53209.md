dire:/ˈdaɪər/| adj.|可怕的，悲惨的，极端的|Causing or involving great fear or suffering; dreadful; terrible; extremely serious or urgent.|例句：The company is in dire financial straits. 这家公司陷入了可怕的财政困境。|例句：They were in dire need of food and water. 他们急需食物和水。|近义词：terrible, awful, dreadful, appalling, catastrophic|反义词：good, excellent, fortunate, favorable|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *dire*, from Old French *dire* (modern French *dire*), from Latin *dīrus* ("terrible, awful, frightful, ill-omened, dire"), perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *\*dwey-/* ("to fear").
