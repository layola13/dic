nonchampions:/ˌnɒnˈtʃæmpiənz/ | n. |非冠军，非优胜者|People or teams who did not win a championship.  Those who haven't achieved the highest rank in a competition.|The nonchampions received medals for participation.|Synonyms: runners-up, losers (can be offensive), also-rans|Antonyms: champions, winners|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"non-" (prefix): from Latin "non" meaning "not"
"champion": from Old French "champion," ultimately from Late Latin "campio," meaning "fighter in single combat," from Latin "campus," meaning "field, plain."
