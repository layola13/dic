obstructive:/əbˈstrʌktɪv/
adj.
阻碍的，妨碍的，阻塞的
Causing or tending to cause an obstruction.  Hindering or preventing progress or achievement.
The obstructive behavior of the protesters caused traffic jams. 抗议者的阻挠行为造成了交通堵塞。
His obstructive attitude made it difficult to reach a compromise.  他阻碍的态度使得难以达成妥协。
近义词:hindering, impeding, blocking, inhibiting
反义词:helpful, cooperative, facilitating, assisting
词性:形容词

obstruct:/əbˈstrʌkt/
v.
阻碍，妨碍，阻塞
To block or close up (a passage, channel, road, etc.); to be or get in the way of. To hinder or prevent the progress or achievement of.
Fallen trees obstructed the road. 倒下的树木阻塞了道路。
They were trying to obstruct the police investigation. 他们试图阻碍警方调查。
近义词:block, hinder, impede, prevent
反义词:aid, assist, help, facilitate
词性:动词
