allomerous:/əˈlɒmərəs/
adjective
异构的，异质的
Having a different number of parts in successive whorls, especially of floral organs.
Allomerous flowers have unequal numbers of sepals, petals, stamens, and carpels. 异构的花具有数量不等的萼片、花瓣、雄蕊和心皮。
The allomerous nature of the flower made it difficult to classify.  这朵花的异构性使其难以分类。
近义词: heteromerous
反义词: isomerous
词性: adjective
