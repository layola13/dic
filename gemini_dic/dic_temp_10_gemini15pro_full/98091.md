inquisitive:/ɪnˈkwɪzətɪv/| adj.|好奇的，爱打听的|Given to asking questions; eager for knowledge; intellectually curious.|例句：Children are naturally inquisitive. 孩子们天生好奇。

例句：She has an inquisitive mind and is always asking questions. 她有着一颗好奇的心，总是问问题。|近义词：curious, inquiring, questioning|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, uninterested|词性: adjective

inquisitives:/ɪnˈkwɪzətɪvz/| n.|好奇的人，爱打听的人|People who are inquisitive.|例句：The museum attracts inquisitives of all ages. 博物馆吸引了各个年龄段的好奇的人。

例句：Inquisitives are often drawn to careers in journalism or research. 好奇心强的人往往会被新闻或研究领域的工作所吸引。|近义词：seekers, investigators, explorers|反义词：apathetics, indifferents|词性: noun
