bobbing-up:/ˈbɒbɪŋ ʌp/ | adj. | 浮现的，突然出现的 |  The act of repeatedly appearing and disappearing, often on the surface of water or from behind something.  Describes something that keeps popping up or reappearing. |  The ducks were bobbing-up and down on the waves. 鸭子在波浪上上下起伏。 | Synonyms: surfacing, popping up, emerging, reappearing | Antonyms: sinking, disappearing, submerging | Verb phrase (present participle)


bobbing-up:/ˈbɒbɪŋ ʌp/ | n. | 浮现，上下摆动 | The instance of something bobbing up. | The constant bobbing-up of the cork indicated a fish was nibbling.  软木塞不停地上下跳动，表明有鱼在咬钩。| Synonyms: emergence, surfacing | Antonyms: submergence, disappearance | Noun phrase (gerund)
