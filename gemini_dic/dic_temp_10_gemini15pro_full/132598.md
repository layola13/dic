nondilatable:/ˌnɒndaɪˈleɪtəbl/
adjective
不可扩张的
Not able to be dilated or expanded.
The pupil of the eye was nondilatable due to the medication. 由于药物的作用，瞳孔无法扩张。
Near synonyms: nonexpansive, inflexible, unyielding
Antonyms: dilatable, expandable, flexible
词性: 形容词
