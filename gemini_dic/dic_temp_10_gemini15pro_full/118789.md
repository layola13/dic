metallurgists:/ˈmɛtəlɜːrdʒɪsts/
n.
冶金学家；冶金工程师
Experts in metallurgy, metallurgists play a crucial role in developing and improving metals and alloys.
例句：The metallurgists analyzed the composition of the alloy. 冶金学家分析了合金的成分。

例句：Metallurgists are in high demand in the mining industry.  采矿业对冶金学家有很高的需求。

近义词：metalworkers, metallurgic engineers
反义词: N/A
词性: noun (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From metallurgy + -ist. Metallurgy comes from the Greek metallourgos, meaning "worker in metal,"  from metallon "metal" + ergon "work."
