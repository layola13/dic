elder:/ˈeldər/ (US), /ˈɛldər/ (UK)| adj. n.|年长的，资格老的；长辈，前辈|Of greater age, senior.  A person of greater age, especially one with recognized authority or respect within a group or family.|My elder sister is a doctor. 我的姐姐是一位医生.| The village elders met to discuss the issue. 村里的长者们开会讨论这个问题.|近义词：older, senior, superior|反义词:younger, junior|adjective/noun
