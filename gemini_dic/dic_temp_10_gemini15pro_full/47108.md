daughters:/ˈdɔːtərz/ (US) /ˈdɔːtəz/ (UK)| n.|女儿们|A female child or offspring in relation to her parents.|例句：My parents have two daughters. 我的父母有两个女儿。

例句：His daughters are both doctors. 他的女儿们都是医生。


近义词：female offspring, girl children|反义词:sons|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *doughter*, from Old English *dohtor*, from Proto-Germanic *duhtēr*, from Proto-Indo-European *dhugh₂tēr*. Cognate with Dutch *dochter*, German *Tochter*, Gothic *dauhtar*.
