neurotomy:/nʊˈrɒtəmi/ | n. | 神经切断术 | The surgical division or dissection of a nerve. | Example: The patient underwent a neurotomy to relieve chronic pain. 病人接受了神经切断术以缓解慢性疼痛。 | Example: The neurotomy was successful in reducing the muscle spasms. 神经切断术成功地减少了肌肉痉挛。| Synonym: nerve section | Antonym: neurorrhaphy | noun
