distributive:/dɪˈstrɪbjətɪv/ | adj. n. | 分配的，分发的；分配律；分配词 | relating to or denoting the distribution of resources or goods.  A word, especially an adjective, that refers to each of the members of a group individually. |  Each worker received a distributive share of the profits. 每位工人都得到了利润的分配份额。| synonyms: apportioned, allocated, assigned | antonyms: collective, combined, shared | adjective


distributive:/dɪˈstrɪbjətɪv/ | n. | 分配律；分配词 | A rule or law in mathematics, especially one concerning multiplication and addition. A word or phrase that distributes a following noun phrase over conjoined elements. | The distributive property is a key concept in algebra. 分配律是代数中的一个关键概念。| synonyms: distributive law, distributive property | antonyms: N/A | noun
