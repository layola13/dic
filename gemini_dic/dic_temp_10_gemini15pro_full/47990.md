decimate:/ˈdesɪmeɪt/ | v. | 大量毁灭；大大削弱；使元气大伤 | to kill, destroy, or remove a large percentage or part of | The Black Death decimated the population of Europe. 黑死病使欧洲人口大量死亡。 | synonyms: destroy, devastate, ravage, ruin | antonyms: create, build, construct | verb

decimate:/ˈdesɪmeɪt/ | v. | (古罗马)每十人杀一人(作为惩罚) | to kill one in every ten of (a group of soldiers or others) as a punishment for mutiny or some other serious offense. |  The Roman general decimated his legions after their defeat.  罗马将军在军团战败后对他们进行了十一抽杀令。 | synonyms: punish, execute | antonyms: pardon, forgive | verb
