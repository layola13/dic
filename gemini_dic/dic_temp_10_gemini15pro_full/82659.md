guerrilla:/ɡəˈrɪlə/ /ɡəˈrɪljə/ | n. adj.|游击队员，游击战的|A member of a small independent group taking part in irregular fighting, typically against larger regular forces.  Also used as an adjective to describe tactics, warfare, etc., that are unconventional and irregular.|The guerrillas ambushed the army patrol. 游击队员伏击了巡逻队。| 近义词: partisan, freedom fighter, insurgent, rebel | 反义词: soldier, regular (forces) | 词性: noun, adjective

Etymology: From Spanish *guerrilla*, diminutive of *guerra* "war", from Late Latin *werra*, of Germanic origin.
