canonizes:/ˈkænənaɪzɪz/
verb
正式宣布（死者）为圣徒；推崇；奉为经典；使…成为准则
to declare (a dead person) to be a saint
例句：The Catholic Church canonizes saints. 天主教会册封圣徒。

例句：His works have been canonized by generations of scholars. 他的著作被一代代学者奉为经典。
synonyms: sanctifies, glorifies, exalts, deifies
antonyms: condemns, denounces, vilifies
