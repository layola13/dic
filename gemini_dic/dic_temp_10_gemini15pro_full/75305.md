frontiniac: /ˌfrʌntɪˈnjæk/
n.
Frontenac grape; Frontenac wine
A red wine grape variety.  A hybrid grape developed at the University of Minnesota. Known for its cold hardiness and used to make fruit-forward red wines.
This Frontiniac has notes of cherry and black currant.  这款弗朗蒂尼亚克葡萄酒带有樱桃和黑醋栗的味道。
Synonyms:  (For the grape itself)  cold-hardy grape, hybrid grape. (For the wine) red wine, fruit-forward wine.
Antonyms:  (Generally, for the wine) dry wine (if the Frontiniac is sweet), oaked wine (if the Frontiniac is unoaked).
词性: noun
