osteotomy: /ˌɒstiˈɒtəmi/ (US) /ˌɒstiˈɒtəmi/ (UK)| n. |骨切开术|The surgical cutting of a bone.  A surgical operation whereby a bone is cut to shorten or lengthen it or to change its alignment.|The patient underwent an osteotomy to correct a bone deformity.| 近义词: bone surgery, bone resection| 反义词: None (no direct opposite for a surgical procedure)|词性: Noun
