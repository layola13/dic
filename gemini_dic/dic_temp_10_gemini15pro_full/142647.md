ostracizing: /ˈɒstrəˌsaɪzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈɑːstrəˌsaɪzɪŋ/ (US)
verb (present participle)
排斥；孤立
excluding from a society or group
He was ostracized by his colleagues for his controversial views.  他因为有争议的观点而被同事排斥。
The community ostracized her for breaking their strict social rules.  因为违反了他们严格的社会规则，她被社区孤立了。
excluding, shunning, banishing, blackballing, boycotting
including, welcoming, embracing, accepting, integrating
verb
