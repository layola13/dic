newtons:/ˈnjuːtənz/| n.|牛顿（力的单位）|The SI unit of force. One newton is equal to the force that would give a mass of one kilogram an acceleration of one meter per second per second.|A small apple weighs approximately one newton. 一个小苹果的重量大约是一牛顿。|Synonyms: N (abbreviation)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|noun
