non-preference: /ˌnɒnˈprɛfərəns/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈprɛfərəns/ (US)
non-preference: n.
non-preference: 无偏好，不优先，不加选择
non-preference:  Lack of preference or favoritism for one thing over another.  It indicates neutrality or indifference in making a choice.
non-preference:  The company's hiring policy emphasizes non-preference based on race or gender.  该公司招聘政策强调不基于种族或性别的无偏好。
non-preference:  She expressed non-preference for either candidate. 她对两位候选人均未表示偏好。
non-preference: neutrality, impartiality, indifference
non-preference: preference, favoritism, bias
non-preference: noun
