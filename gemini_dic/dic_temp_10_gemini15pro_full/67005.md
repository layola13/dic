expiatory:/ɪkˈspiːətɔːri/
adjective
赎罪的，补偿的
serving to expiate or atone for guilt or sin; making amends
例句：He performed expiatory rites to appease the gods. 他举行了赎罪仪式来安抚众神。

例句：She made an expiatory offering to the temple. 她向寺庙献上了赎罪祭品。

近义词：atonement, propitiatory, cleansing, purifying
反义词：vindictive, retaliatory, punitive
