fumes:/fjuːmz/| n.| 烟雾，气体；一阵愤怒 | strong, unpleasant, and sometimes dangerous gas or smoke | The fumes from the car exhaust made me cough. 汽车尾气让我咳嗽。| synonyms: smoke, gas, vapor, exhaust | antonyms: fresh air | noun

fume:/fjuːm/| v.| 冒烟；发怒 | to produce or give off fumes; to be very angry | The engine fumed black smoke. 发动机冒出黑烟。  She was fuming about the delay. 她对延误感到愤怒。| synonyms: smoke, smolder; rage, seethe | antonyms: calm down | verb
