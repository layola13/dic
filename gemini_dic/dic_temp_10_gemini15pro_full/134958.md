nonnationalisms:/ˌnɒnˈnæʃənəlɪzəmz/
|n.|非民族主义；超越民族主义|The rise of nonnationalisms poses a challenge to traditional nation-states. 非民族主义的兴起对传统的民族国家构成了挑战。|
|nonnationalisms are ideologies and political movements that transcend or reject traditional nationalism.|非民族主义是指超越或拒绝传统民族主义的意识形态和政治运动。|
|The conference explored various forms of nonnationalisms, including cosmopolitanism and global citizenship.  这次会议探讨了各种形式的非民族主义，包括世界主义和全球公民意识。|
|近义词:cosmopolitanism, internationalism, globalism|
|反义词:nationalism, patriotism|
|词性:noun (plural)|


Etymology:
non- + nationalism + -s (plural)
"non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of".
"nationalism" comes from "nation" (from Old French nacion, from Latin natio "birth, origin, race, nation") + "-ism" (a suffix forming nouns meaning a doctrine, belief, or practice).
