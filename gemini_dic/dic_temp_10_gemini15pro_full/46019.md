cybersecurity:/ˌsaɪbər sɪˈkjʊərɪti/| n.|网络安全|The protection of computer systems and networks from information disclosure, theft of or damage to their hardware, software, or electronic data, as well as from the disruption or misdirection of the services they provide.|Example 1: Cybersecurity is a growing concern for businesses of all sizes. 网络安全是各种规模企业日益关注的问题。

Example 2:  The company invested heavily in cybersecurity measures to protect its data.  这家公司在网络安全措施上投入巨资以保护其数据。|近义词：information security, computer security, IT security|反义词:cybercrime, cyberattack|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
cyber- + security
cyber-:  from Greek *kúbernos 'pilot, governor', which is related to kubernetes
security: from Latin sēcūrus 'free from care'
