clamant:/ˈklæmənt/| adj.|喧嚷的；紧急的；迫切的|Loud and insistent in making a demand or complaint.  Characterized by forceful outcry, especially in protest or demand.|The clamant need for reform was evident to everyone.  改革的迫切需要对每个人来说都是显而易见的。|Synonyms: insistent, vociferous, urgent, pressing, imperative|Antonyms: quiet, silent, subdued, muted, unassertive|adjective

clamor:/ˈklæmər/| n. vi.|喧闹；大声要求|A loud and confused noise, especially that of people shouting vehemently.  To shout loudly and insistently.|The clamor of the crowd grew louder as the team scored.  随着球队得分，人群的欢呼声越来越大。|Synonyms: uproar, din, hubbub, outcry, commotion|Antonyms: silence, quiet, stillness, peace, tranquility|noun, verb
