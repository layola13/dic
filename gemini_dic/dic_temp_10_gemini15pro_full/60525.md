elaeis: /ɪˈliːɪs/ | n. |油棕属；油棕| A genus of palms, including the oil palm (Elaeis guineensis), native to Africa and South America and cultivated for their oil-rich fruit. | The elaeis is a valuable source of palm oil. 油棕是棕榈油的重要来源。| Elaeis guineensis is the most commonly cultivated species of elaeis. 油棕(几内亚油棕)是最常种植的油棕品种。| synonym: oil palm | antonym: N/A | noun
