hakkas:/ˈhɑːkəs/ | n. |客家人；客家话|A Han Chinese subgroup originating in mountainous regions of southeastern China and migrating to other parts of China and Southeast Asia. Also refers to their language. | The Hakkas are known for their distinctive architecture and cuisine. 客家人以其独特的建筑和美食而闻名。| Hakka people maintain strong traditions wherever they migrate.  无论迁徙到哪里，客家人都保持着浓厚的传统。 | synonyms:  Hakka Chinese | antonyms: N/A | noun
