double-jointedness:/ˌdʌbəlˈdʒɔɪntɪdnəs/ | n. |双关节;关节过度伸展|The ability to bend joints beyond their normal range of motion, often due to greater than normal laxity of ligaments.|She showed off her double-jointedness by bending her thumb backwards to touch her wrist. 她通过向后弯曲拇指使其触碰到手腕来展示她的双关节。|近义词：hypermobility, flexibility |反义词: stiffness, rigidity |词性:名词
