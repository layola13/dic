nonfraternization:/ˌnɑːnˌfrætərnɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
不亲密交往；禁止 fraternization（联谊）的规定；避免 fraternization（联谊）
The policy of nonfraternization between officers and enlisted personnel is strictly enforced. 军官和士兵之间禁止 fraternization（联谊）的政策得到了严格执行。
The company has a strict policy of nonfraternization to prevent conflicts of interest.  为了避免利益冲突，公司有严格的禁止 fraternization（联谊）政策。
Synonyms: aloofness, detachment, distance
Antonyms: fraternization, socializing, association
