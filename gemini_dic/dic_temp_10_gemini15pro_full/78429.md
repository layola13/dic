get-there:/ˈɡet ðɛər/| adj. informal|成功的；能到达目的地的；有效的|Achieving or likely to achieve a desired aim or result; effective.|例句：She's a get-there person; she always finds a way to succeed. 她是一个很有能力的人，总能找到成功的方法。

例句：His marketing strategy is get-there; it always brings results. 他的营销策略很有效，总能带来成果。|近义词：successful, effective, efficient, resourceful|反义词：ineffective, unsuccessful, inefficient|词性:形容词


词源:  "Get-there" is a compound adjective formed from the verb "get" and the adverb "there."  "Get" has Old Norse origins (geta, meaning "to get, obtain"), and "there" comes from Old English "thær."  The combined meaning implies reaching a destination or achieving a goal.
