internuncioship:/ˌɪntərˈnʌnʃiˌəʃɪp/
n.
教廷大使的职位；教廷大使的任期
The position or term of office of a papal nuncio.
The internuncioship was a crucial diplomatic role during the Renaissance.  文艺复兴时期，教廷大使的职位是一个至关重要的外交角色。
His internuncioship lasted for five years. 他的教廷大使任期持续了五年。
Synonyms: nunciature, legateship
Antonyms: N/A
