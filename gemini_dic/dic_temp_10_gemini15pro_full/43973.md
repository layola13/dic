cried:/kraɪd/| v.|哭泣，叫喊，大声说|The child cried for his mother. 孩子哭着要妈妈。|例句：She cried when she heard the bad news. 她听到坏消息时哭了。|近义词：wept, sobbed, shouted, yelled|反义词:laughed, rejoiced|verb

cried:/kraɪd/| n.|哭泣，叫喊|The baby's cries woke me up. 婴儿的哭声把我吵醒了。|例句：There was a loud cry from the street. 街上传来一声大叫。|近义词：sob, yell, shout|反义词:laughter|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *criden*, from Old English *crīdan* (“to cry, weep, call, proclaim”), from Proto-Germanic *krītaną* (“to cry, weep”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱrey- (“to cry, weep”). Cognate with Dutch *krijten* (“to cry, weep”), German *kreischen* (“to shriek”), Old Norse *hrjóða* (“to cry, weep”).
