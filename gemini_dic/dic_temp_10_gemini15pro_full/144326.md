outspurt: /ˈaʊtspɜːrt/ (US) /ˈaʊtspɜːt/ (UK)| n.| 冲刺，突然的加速，迸发| A sudden burst of speed or energy, typically in a race or competition.|  The runner made a final outspurt to win the race.  这位赛跑者在最后阶段突然冲刺，赢得了比赛。|
近义词: burst, sprint, surge| 反义词: slowdown, lag| 词性: 名词

outspurt: /ˈaʊtspɜːrt/ (US) /ˈaʊtspɜːt/ (UK)| v.| 突然加速，冲刺| To make a sudden burst of speed or energy.| He outspurted the other runners in the final lap. 他在最后一圈超过了其他选手。| 近义词: outpace, outrun, surpass| 反义词: lag behind, fall behind| 词性: 动词

词源:  "Out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "spurt" (a sudden gush or jet).  由"out-"（表示超过或胜过）和"spurt"（突然涌出或喷射）组成。
