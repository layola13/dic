nard:/nɑːrd/ | n. | 甘松, 甘松香, 香水 | An aromatic ointment or oil derived from an East Indian plant, Nardostachys jatamansi.  It was highly valued in ancient times. |  Mary anointed Jesus' feet with costly nard.  玛利亚用昂贵的甘松油膏抹耶稣的脚。| synonyms: spikenard, ointment, perfume | antonyms: stench, odor | noun

nard:/nɑːrd/ | n. | (北美非正式) 笨蛋，傻瓜 | A foolish or contemptible person (North American informal usage). | He's such a nard, he tripped over his own feet. 他真是个笨蛋，竟然被自己的脚绊倒了。 | synonyms: idiot, fool, dolt | antonyms: genius, sage | noun
