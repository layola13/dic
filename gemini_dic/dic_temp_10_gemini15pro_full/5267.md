allomorphism:/əˈlɒmərfɪzəm/ /əˈlɔːmərfɪzəm/
n.
多态性，同质异象
The variation in the form of a morpheme without change in meaning or grammatical function.
Example: The plural morpheme in English has several allomorphs, such as /-s/, /-z/, and /-ɪz/.  英语中的复数词素有几个同质异象，例如 /-s/、/-z/ 和 /-ɪz/。

Example: The past tense morpheme in English has allomorphs like /-d/, /-t/, and /-ɪd/. 英语中的过去时词素有 /-d/、/-t/ 和 /-ɪd/ 等同质异象。

近义词:variation, alternation
反义词:invariance, uniformity
词性:noun
