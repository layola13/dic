noninflammabilities:/ˌnɒnɪnˌflæməˈbɪlətiz/
noun
不可燃性；非易燃性
The quality or state of being noninflammable; resistance to catching fire.
The noninflammabilities of these materials make them suitable for use in high-risk environments. 这些材料的不可燃性使其适用于高风险环境。
Near Synonyms: fire resistance, incombustibility, nonflammability
Antonyms: flammability, combustibility, inflammability
词性: noun (plural)
