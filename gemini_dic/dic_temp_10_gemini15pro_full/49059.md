dehumanising:/ˌdiːhjuːmənaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
使丧失人性的；非人化的
depriving someone of human qualities, personality, or spirit; making someone seem less human and more like a machine or thing.
The refugees were subjected to dehumanising conditions in the camp.  难民在营地里遭受了非人道的待遇。
The prisoners were subjected to dehumanising treatment. 囚犯们遭受了非人道的待遇。

近义词: degrading, brutalising, inhumane, objectifying
反义词: humanising, personalising, individualising
词性: adjective
