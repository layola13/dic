literalization:/ˌlɪtərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |字面化；字面解释|The act or process of making something literal; adherence to the exact letter or meaning of a word or text.|The literalization of religious metaphors can lead to misunderstandings.  将宗教隐喻字面化会导致误解。|近义词：literalism, literal interpretation|反义词: figurative interpretation, metaphorical interpretation|词性:noun
