aluvial:/əˈluːviəl/
adjective
冲积的; 由流水搬运沉积的
Relating to or derived from alluvium; deposited by flowing water.
The alluvial soil in the delta is very fertile. 三角洲的冲积土壤非常肥沃。
Synonyms: sedimentary, deposited, fluvial
Antonyms: igneous, metamorphic
adjective


alluvium:/əˈluːviəm/
noun
冲积层; 冲积土
A deposit of clay, silt, sand, and gravel left by flowing streams in a river valley or delta, typically producing fertile soil.
The river deposited a thick layer of alluvium on the floodplain. 河流在漫滩上沉积了厚厚的一层冲积层。
Synonyms: sediment, deposit, silt
Antonyms: bedrock
noun
