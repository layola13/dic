micromanaging:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmænɪdʒɪŋ/
|n.|微观管理；事无巨细地管理|The act of controlling every small detail of employees' work, often to an excessive degree, which can stifle creativity and motivation.| Her micromanaging was driving the team crazy. 她的事无巨细的管理快把团队逼疯了。|近义词: overmanaging, overcontrolling|反义词: delegating, empowering|词性: 动名词 (gerund) derived from the verb "micromanage"

micromanage:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmænɪdʒ/
|v.|微观管理；事无巨细地管理|To control every small detail of employees' work, often to an excessive degree.|The new boss micromanages everything, which frustrates the employees. 新老板事无巨细地管理一切，这让员工们很沮丧。|近义词: overmanage, overcontrol|反义词: delegate, empower|词性: 动词 (verb)

micromanagement:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmænɪdʒmənt/
|n.|微观管理；事无巨细的管理|The practice of controlling every small detail of employees' work.|The company suffered from poor micromanagement. 该公司因糟糕的微观管理而受损。|近义词: overmanagement, overcontrol|反义词: delegation, empowerment|词性: 名词 (noun)

词源: "micro-" (small) + "management"  The term emerged in the 1970s.
