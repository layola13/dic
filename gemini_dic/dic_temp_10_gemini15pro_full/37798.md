compellers:/kəmˈpɛlərz/ | n. |强迫者，迫使者| People or things that compel someone to do something. | The economic compellers forced many people to leave their homes. 经济压力迫使许多人离开家园。| synonyms: forcers, coercers, constrainers | antonyms: persuaders, encouragers | noun

compel:/kəmˈpɛl/ | v. |强迫，迫使| To force someone to do something. | The law compels all drivers to wear seatbelts. 法律强制所有司机系安全带。| synonyms: force, coerce, oblige, constrain | antonyms: persuade, encourage | verb
