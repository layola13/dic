driftless:/ˈdrɪftləs/| adj.|无漂流物的；不受冰川作用影响的|Free from glacial drift; (of a region) not having been covered by the glaciers during the last ice age.|The Driftless Area is a unique region in the Midwest. 无漂流区是中西部一个独特的区域。|近义词：unglaciated|反义词：glaciated|词性:形容词
Etymology: drift (n.) + -less (suffix)
drift: from Old Norse *drifa "to drive"
-less: from Old English -lēas "without"
