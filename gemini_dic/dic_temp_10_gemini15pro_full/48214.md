decongregates:/diːˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪts/
verb
分散，疏散，解除隔离
To break up a gathering or concentration of people or things, especially for reasons of public health or safety.
The school decongregated the students after the fire alarm went off. 学校在火警响起后疏散了学生。

assemble, gather, congregate
