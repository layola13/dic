borneol:/ˈbɔːrniɔːl/ | n. |龙脑，冰片|A white, translucent crystalline organic compound with a pungent odor and taste, obtained from the wood of the Borneo camphor tree (Dryobalanops aromatica) and other plants. It is used in perfumes, incense, and traditional medicine. | Borneol is a common ingredient in many traditional Chinese medicines. 龙脑是许多中药的常见成分。| Borneol has a refreshing, camphoraceous aroma. 龙脑有一种清新的樟脑香味。 | synonyms: camphor, bornyl alcohol | antonyms: N/A | noun
