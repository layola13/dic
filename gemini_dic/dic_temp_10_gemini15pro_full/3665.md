agglomerator: /əˈɡlɒməreɪtər/ (US) /əˈɡlɒmərˌeɪtər/ (UK)| n. |凝聚器; 烧结机; 聚结器|A machine or device that causes small particles to adhere and form into larger masses or clumps.|The agglomerator combines small particles of iron ore into larger pellets for easier processing.|Synonyms: aggregator, collector, compiler, accumulator|Antonyms: separator, disperser|Noun

agglomerators: /əˈɡlɒməreɪtərz/ (US) /əˈɡlɒmərˌeɪtərz/ (UK)| n. |凝聚器; 烧结机; 聚结器(复数)|Plural form of agglomerator, referring to multiple machines or devices that cause small particles to adhere and form into larger masses or clumps.|The factory uses several agglomerators to process different types of ore.|Synonyms: aggregators, collectors, compilers, accumulators|Antonyms: separators, dispersers|Noun
