belying: /bɪˈlaɪɪŋ/
verb
掩饰；与…不符；使…落空
misrepresent; contradict
Her calm demeanor belied her inner turmoil. 她平静的举止掩饰了她内心的混乱。
近义词: contradicting, masking, disguising
反义词: revealing, showing, exposing
词性: verb (动词)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *belien*, from Old English *belēogan* ("to deceive, betray, belie, calumniate"), from *be-* (prefix meaning "thoroughly, completely, all around") + *lēogan* ("to lie, tell lies"). Cognate with Dutch *beliegen* ("to belie"), German *belügen* ("to tell lies to").
