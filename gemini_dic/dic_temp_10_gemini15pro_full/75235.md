frolicking:/ˈfrɒlɪkɪŋ/ (US) /ˈfrɑːlɪkɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. v.|嬉戏的，玩耍的|Playful and lively; gamboling.|The lambs were frolicking in the meadow. 羊羔在草地上嬉戏。|近义词：playing, gamboling, romping, cavorting|反义词：sullen, morose, gloomy|词性: adjective, present participle of frolic

frolic:/ˈfrɒlɪk/ (US) /ˈfrɑːlɪk/ (UK)| n. v.|嬉戏，玩耍|A playful and lively activity; to play and move about cheerfully; to gambol.|The children's frolic in the park was a joy to watch. 孩子们在公园里嬉戏玩耍，真是令人高兴。|近义词：play, romp, gambol, caper|反义词：mope, sulk|词性: noun, verb

词源: From Middle English *froklik, from Middle Dutch vrolyc (“joyful,” “merry”), from Proto-Germanic *frōlijaz (“happy,” “joyful”).
