ignorances:/ˈɪɡnərənsɪz/
noun
无知，愚昧；不知，不懂
lack of knowledge or information.
His ignorance of the law is no excuse. 他不懂法不能成为借口。
近义词:unawareness, inexperience, unfamiliarity, naivete
反义词:knowledge, awareness, wisdom, expertise
词性:n.
