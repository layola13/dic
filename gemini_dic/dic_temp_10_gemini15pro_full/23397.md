brachylogy:/brəˈkɪlədʒi/| n.|简洁, 简略|Conciseness of expression; brevity of diction.  The omission of words from a phrase or sentence without obscuring the meaning. |Example: "The omission of 'and' in 'bread and butter' is an example of brachylogy."  “在'bread and butter'中省略'and'是brachylogy的一个例子。”|Example:  Her brachylogy, while efficient, sometimes made her seem rude. 她的简洁虽然有效率，但有时让她显得粗鲁。|Synonym:  conciseness, brevity, terseness, succinctness|Antonym:  verbosity, prolixity, wordiness|noun
