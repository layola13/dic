exceeding:/ɪkˈsiːdɪŋ/
adjective
超越的，非常的
going beyond a limit; unusually great
例句：The demand for the product is exceeding the supply.  该产品的需求超过了供应。

例句：She possesses exceeding beauty and grace. 她拥有非凡的美貌和优雅。

近义词：surpassing, exceptional, extraordinary, remarkable
反义词：inferior, mediocre, ordinary, inadequate
词性：adjective


exceed:/ɪkˈsiːd/
verb
超过，超出
to be greater than a number or amount, or to go past an allowed limit
例句：The cost of the project exceeded our budget.  该项目的成本超出了我们的预算。

例句：He was fined for exceeding the speed limit. 他因超速行驶而被罚款。

近义词：surpass, outstrip, transcend, eclipse
反义词：fall short of, lag behind, be inferior to
词性：verb
