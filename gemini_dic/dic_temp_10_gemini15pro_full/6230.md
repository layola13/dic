amnestying: /æmˈnɪstiɪŋ/
verb
赦免;大赦
The act of granting an amnesty.  Officially pardoning or giving amnesty to.
The government is amnestying all political prisoners. 政府正在赦免所有政治犯。
The king amnestied the rebels. 国王赦免了叛军。
Synonyms: pardoning, forgiving, absolving, exonerating, reprieving
Antonyms: condemning, punishing, prosecuting
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French amnistier, from Late Latin amnestiare, from Latin amnestia, from Greek amnēstiā (“forgetfulness, amnesty”), from amnēstos (“forgotten”), from a- (“not”) + mnasthai (“to remember”).
