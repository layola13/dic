asseveration:/əˌsɛvəˈreɪʃən/
n.
断言，郑重声明
a positive statement or declaration, often without proof
The defendant's asseveration of innocence was not enough to convince the jury.  被告的无罪声明不足以说服陪审团。

近义词:affirmation, assertion, declaration, avowal
反义词:denial, refutation, contradiction
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *asseveratio, from Latin asseverare "to affirm, assert earnestly," from ad- "to" + severus "serious, earnest."
