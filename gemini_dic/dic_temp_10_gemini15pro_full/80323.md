ETYMOLOGY->
From German "goldener," the comparative form of "golden" (golden), from Proto-Germanic "*gulþīnaz" (golden), from "*gulþą" (gold).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*ǵʰelh₃-" (yellow, green, bright).
