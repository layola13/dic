mazunda:/məˈzʊndə/ | n. | 马尊达（一种非洲班图语）| A Bantu language spoken primarily in Mozambique. | Example: Mazunda is closely related to the languages of Malawi. 马尊达语与马拉维的语言密切相关。

Example:  Many people in central Mozambique speak Mazunda. 许多莫桑比克中部的人说马尊达语。 | 近义词: Sena (a related language) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


Etymology: From the name of the Mazunda people, an ethnic group in Mozambique. 源于莫桑比克的一个民族马尊达人的名字。
