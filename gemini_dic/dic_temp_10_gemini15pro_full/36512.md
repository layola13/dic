colicky: /ˈkɑːlɪki/ (US) /ˈkɒlɪki/ (UK)| adj.| 绞痛的；患绞痛的| Relating to or resembling colic; suffering from or caused by colic.  Characterized by sharp, intermittent abdominal pain.| The baby was colicky and cried for hours.  这个婴儿肚子绞痛，哭了几个小时。|Synonyms: griping, spasmodic, crampy| Antonyms: comfortable, painless| adjective

colic: /ˈkɑːlɪk/ (US) /ˈkɒlɪk/ (UK)| n.| 绞痛| Severe, often fluctuating pain in the abdomen caused by intestinal gas or obstruction in the intestines and suffered especially by babies.| The baby suffered from colic. 这个婴儿患有绞痛。| Synonyms: gripes, cramps, spasms| Antonyms: comfort, ease| noun
