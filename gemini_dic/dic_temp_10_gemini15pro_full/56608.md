doncella:/donˈθeʎa/ (Spanish)| n.| 少女，闺女；侍女|A young unmarried woman; a maiden. A female attendant, especially in a noble household.|例句：La doncella ayudó a la reina a vestirse.  The maiden helped the queen to dress.

例句：Las doncellas del castillo bordaban tapices. The maidens of the castle embroidered tapestries.|近义词：maiden, damsel, girl, lass|反义词：matron, crone|词性: noun
