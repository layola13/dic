herbivore:/ˈhɜːrbɪvɔːr/ (US), /ˈhɜːbɪvɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.|食草动物|An animal that feeds on plants.|Cows are herbivores. 牛是食草动物。|Synonyms: vegetarian, vegan (in a broader sense), plant-eater|Antonyms: carnivore, omnivore|noun

herbivorous:/hərˈbɪvərəs/ (US), /hɜːˈbɪvərəs/ (UK)| adj.|食草的|Feeding on plants.|Deer are herbivorous mammals. 鹿是食草哺乳动物。|Synonyms: plant-eating, phytophagous|Antonyms: carnivorous, omnivorous|adjective
