eased:/iːzd/
verb
减轻，缓和
made or became less severe, difficult, or painful
The medicine eased the pain.  这药减轻了疼痛。

近义词:alleviated, mitigated, relieved, lessened, reduced
反义词:increased, worsened, aggravated, intensified
词性:verb (past tense and past participle of "ease")


eased:/iːzd/
adjective
轻松的，舒适的
free from worry, pain, or difficulty
He looked eased after the exam. 他考完试后看起来很轻松。

近义词:relaxed, comfortable, relieved, calm
反义词:tense, anxious, stressed, worried
词性:adjective (past participle used as an adjective)
