cysts:/sɪsts/| n. |囊肿；包囊|A cyst is a closed sac, having a distinct membrane and developing abnormally in a cavity or structure of the body. |The doctor found a cyst on her ovary. 医生在她的卵巢上发现了一个囊肿。|She had a cyst removed from her wrist. 她手腕上的一个囊肿被切除了。|近义词：lump, growth, tumor|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps healthy tissue)|词性: noun
