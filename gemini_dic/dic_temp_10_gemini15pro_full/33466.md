chutzpahs:/ˈχʊtsˌpəz/| n. |厚颜无耻；胆大妄为| Supreme self-confidence or arrogance.  It's often used to describe audacity, even to the point of rudeness or disrespect. | He had the chutzpah to ask for a raise after only a week on the job. 他竟然才工作一周就敢要求加薪。| Synonyms: audacity, nerve, boldness, temerity, gall | Antonyms: timidity, shyness, humility, modesty | Noun
