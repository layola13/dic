interchangeabilities: /ˌɪntərˌtʃeɪndʒəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 可互换性；互换性 | The interchangeability of parts in these machines simplifies repairs.  这些机器零件的可互换性简化了维修工作。|  These two ingredients have a high degree of interchangeability in this recipe. 这两种成分在这个食谱中具有高度的可互换性。 | 近义词: exchangeability, substitutability, replaceability | 反义词: incompatibility, non-interchangeability | 词性: 名词

词源:  From interchangeable + -ity.  Interchangeable comes from inter- (between) + change (to make or become different) + -able (capable of).
