authorships:/ˈɔːθərʃɪps/
n.
著作权，作者身份
The authorship of the poem is unknown.  这首诗的作者身份不明。
The book is a joint authorship. 这本书是合著的。
The authorship of the painting is disputed.  这幅画的作者身份存在争议。
Authorship of the discovery is credited to a team of scientists.  这项发现的著作权归于一个科学家团队。
synonyms: credit, attribution, origin
antonyms: plagiarism, appropriation
noun
