clinium: /ˈklɪniəm/ | n. |斜面，斜坡; (尤指)床，榻|A couch or bed, especially one used in ancient Greece and Rome. |The clinium was draped with fine fabrics and piled high with cushions.  斜面榻上铺着精美的织物，堆满了垫子。|  The wealthy Romans would recline on a clinium while dining. 富有的罗马人会在用餐时斜躺在斜面榻上。| synonyms: couch, bed, chaise lounge, divan | antonyms: chair, seat | noun
