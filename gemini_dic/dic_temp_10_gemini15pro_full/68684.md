farcicalnesses:/fɑːrˈsɪkəlnəsɪz/
noun
滑稽可笑；荒诞不经
the quality or state of being farcical
The farcicalnesses of the situation was apparent to everyone.  这个情况的荒谬性对每个人来说都很明显。
近义词: absurdity, ridiculousness, ludicrousness
反义词: seriousness, solemnity

词源:
From farcical + -ness.  Ultimately from the Late Latin *farcire, "to stuff."
