capitate:/ˈkæpɪteɪt/
adjective
头状的；有头状花序的
Having a knob-like head or a head shaped like the head of a pin, or forming a dense cluster of flowers or fruits resembling a head.
The dandelion has a capitate flower head. 蒲公英有头状花序。
