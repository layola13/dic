legion:/ˈliːdʒən/| n. |军团，众多，大量|A large group of soldiers, especially in the Roman army. A vast number or multitude of people or things.|例句：The Roman legions conquered much of Europe. 罗马军团征服了欧洲的大部分地区。

例句：There were legions of fans waiting outside the stadium.  体育场外有大量的粉丝在等待。|近义词：horde, multitude, throng, army, mass, host|反义词:few, handful, minority|词性:noun

legion:/ˈliːdʒən/| adj. |大量的，众多的|Numerous, very great in number.|例句：His fans are legion. 他的粉丝众多。

例句：The problems facing the new government are legion. 新政府面临的问题很多。|近义词：numerous, countless, myriad, many, abundant|反义词:few, scarce, limited|词性:adjective
