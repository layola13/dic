egling: /ˈɛɡlɪŋ/
n.|小蛋; 蛋状物|A small egg or egg-shaped object. |The robin laid a tiny egling in the nest.| 近义词: egglet, small egg|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps large egg)|词性: 名词


词源:  Likely a diminutive of "egg," using the suffix "-ling" which denotes smallness or youth.  Not a standard English word, but a colloquial or dialectal term.
