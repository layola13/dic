overbriefing:/ˌoʊvərˈbriːfɪŋ/
n.
过度讲解；过度指示
The act of providing too much information or instruction, often to the point of confusion or inefficiency.
The overbriefing before the mission led to confusion among the team members.  任务前的过度讲解导致团队成员之间的困惑。
The lawyer's overbriefing of the jury resulted in their losing interest. 律师对陪审团的过度讲解导致他们失去了兴趣。
overinformation, excessive briefing
underbriefing, insufficient briefing
noun
