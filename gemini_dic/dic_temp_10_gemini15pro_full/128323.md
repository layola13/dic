neurotransmitters:/ˌnjʊərəʊtrænzˈmɪtərz/ /ˌnʊr-/| n.|神经递质|Chemicals that transmit signals across a synapse from one neuron (nerve cell) to another "target" neuron, muscle cell, or gland cell.|Dopamine and serotonin are examples of neurotransmitters. 多巴胺和血清素是神经递质的例子。|近义词：neurochemicals|反义词:None|noun
