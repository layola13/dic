anti-regulatory:/ˌæntiˈreɡjələtɔːri/
anti-regulatory|adj.|反监管的，反对监管的|Opposing or intended to reduce or eliminate government regulation, especially in business or industry.|The politician adopted an anti-regulatory stance, arguing that excessive rules stifle economic growth. 这位政治家采取了反监管的立场，认为过多的规则会扼杀经济增长。|Example:  Many businesses support anti-regulatory policies. 许多企业支持反监管政策。|Synonyms:deregulatory, laissez-faire|Antonyms:regulatory, pro-regulation|adjective
