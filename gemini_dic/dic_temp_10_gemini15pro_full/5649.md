altitude:/ˈæl.tɪ.tʃuːd/
n.
海拔高度；高度；高处
The plane was flying at an altitude of 30,000 feet.  飞机正在3万英尺的高度飞行。

Elevation above a given level, especially sea level.
The city of Denver is situated at a high altitude. 丹佛市位于高海拔地区。

近义词：elevation, height
反义词：depth

词性：noun

词源：From Late Latin *altitudo, from Latin altus (“high, deep”) + -itudo.
