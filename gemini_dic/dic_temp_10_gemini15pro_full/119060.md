metonymic:/ˌmetəˈnɪmɪk/ | adj. | 转喻的 | Using a word to refer to something else that it is closely associated with, rather than literally. |  The White House announced new policies. (White House refers to the US government) 白宫宣布了新的政策。（白宫指代美国政府）

The pen is mightier than the sword. (Pen refers to written words, sword refers to military force) 笔比剑更强大。（笔指代文字，剑指代武力） |  figurative, symbolic | literal | adjective
