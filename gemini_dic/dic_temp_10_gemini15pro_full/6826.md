anaseismic:/ˌeɪnˌeɪˈsaɪzmɪk/| adj.|抗震的，耐震的，不受地震影响的|Relating to or denoting a structure built to withstand earthquakes, or a region where earthquakes are rare or nonexistent.  It's formed from the prefix "an-" (meaning "without" or "not") + "seismic" (relating to earthquakes).|例句：The aneseismic design of the building protected it from damage during the earthquake. 这座建筑的抗震设计在地震中保护了它免受损害。

例句：The island is located in an anaseismic zone.  这个岛屿位于一个无震区。|近义词：earthquake-resistant, aseismic|反义词: seismic|词性:形容词
