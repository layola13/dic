nonequably:/nɒnˈiːkwəbli/
adv. |不均匀地，不平衡地|In a manner that is not equal or uniform; unevenly. |The resources were distributed nonequably across the region, leading to disparities in development. 资源分配不均，导致区域发展不平衡。|例句：The paint was applied nonequably, resulting in a patchy finish. 油漆涂得不均匀，导致表面斑驳。|近义词：unevenly, disproportionately, irregularly|反义词:equably, evenly, uniformly
词性: 副词
