nondeterminists:/ˌnɒndɪˈtɜːmɪnɪsts/
n.
非确定性主义者; 使用非确定性算法的人
People who believe in or utilize nondeterministic algorithms or models, particularly in computer science or philosophy.  These individuals might work with systems where the outcome is not solely determined by the inputs, involving randomness or choice.
Nondeterminists often challenge the traditional view of cause and effect. 非确定性主义者经常挑战传统的因果关系观点。
Researchers studying quantum mechanics often consider themselves nondeterminists. 研究量子力学的研究人员通常认为自己是非确定性主义者。
近义词:indeterminists
反义词:determinists
词性: Noun (Plural)

nondeterministic:/ˌnɒndɪˈtɜːmɪnɪstɪk/
adj.
非确定性的
Not deterministic; having multiple possible outcomes for a given input, often due to randomness or choice.  Commonly used in computer science to describe algorithms or automata.
Nondeterministic algorithms are often used in artificial intelligence. 非确定性算法常用于人工智能。
Quantum mechanics introduces nondeterministic behavior into physics. 量子力学将非确定性行为引入物理学。
近义词:indeterministic, probabilistic
反义词:deterministic
词性: Adjective
