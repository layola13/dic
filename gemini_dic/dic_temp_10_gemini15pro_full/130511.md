nonaccommodatingly:/ˌnɒnəkəˈmɒdeɪtɪŋli/
adverb
不迁就地，不妥协地，不乐于助人地
in a way that is not willing to help people or consider their needs
He nonaccommodatingly refused to change his plans. 他不妥协地拒绝改变他的计划。
近义词:uncooperatively, inflexibly, stubbornly
反义词:cooperatively, flexibly, accommodatingly
词性:adverb

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + accommodating (adjective meaning "willing to help and fit in with what others want") + -ly (adverb suffix)
