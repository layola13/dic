ossifier:/ˈɒsɪfaɪə(r)/ /ˈɑːsəˌfaɪər/ | n. |骨化器；钙化者|A person or thing that causes or tends to cause something to become rigid, inflexible, or fixed, especially bone.|The broken bone needs an ossifier to heal properly.  这根断骨需要骨化器才能正常愈合。| 近义词: calcifier | 反义词: softener | noun

ossify:/ˈɒsɪfaɪ/ /ˈɑːsəˌfaɪ/ | v. |骨化；硬化；僵化|1. Turn into bone; become bony. 2. Make or become rigid or inflexible.|1. The cartilage will ossify over time.  软骨会随着时间推移而骨化。 2. His thinking has ossified over the years.  他的思想多年来已经僵化了。 | 近义词: harden, solidify, petrify | 反义词: soften, liquefy | verb

ossified:/ˈɒsɪfaɪd/ /ˈɑːsəˌfaɪd/ | adj. |骨化的；硬化的；僵化的|Having become rigid, inflexible, or fixed.|He holds a number of ossified beliefs about education.  他抱着一系列僵化的教育观念。| 近义词: rigid, inflexible, hardened | 反义词: flexible, adaptable, soft | adjective
