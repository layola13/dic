boughs:/baʊz/
n.
树枝；大树枝
A main branch of a tree.
The boughs of the trees were laden with snow. 树枝上积满了雪。

The monkey swung from the boughs. 猴子在树枝间荡来荡去。

limbs, branches
trunk, roots
noun
