controversy: /ˈkɒntrəvɜːsi/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntrəvɜːrsi/ (US)
controversy: n.
controversy: 争论，争议
controversy:  A prolonged public disagreement or heated discussion.
controversy: The new law sparked a great deal of controversy. 新法律引发了巨大的争议。

controversy: The controversy surrounding the election results continues. 围绕选举结果的争议仍在继续。


controversy: 近义词: dispute, debate, argument, contention
controversy: 反义词: agreement, harmony, consensus
controversy: 词性: 名词
