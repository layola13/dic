hypermysticalness:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɪstɪkəlnəs/
noun
极度神秘; 超级神秘
The state or quality of being extremely mystical.
The writer evoked a sense of hypermysticalness in his description of the ancient ritual.  作者在描述古代仪式时唤起了一种极度神秘的感觉。
Her poetry is filled with a hypermysticalness that transcends ordinary understanding. 她的诗歌充满了超越普通理解的极度神秘感。
Synonyms: extreme mysticality, profound mystery, otherworldliness
Antonyms: mundanity, ordinariness, banality
