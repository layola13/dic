fidge:/fɪdʒ/| v. n.|坐立不安，烦躁；摆弄|To make small restless movements, especially of the hands and feet; fidget.|She fidgeted nervously with her pen during the interview.  她在面试时紧张地摆弄着笔。|近义词：fidget, twitch, wriggle|反义词：relax, be still|词性:动词/名词

fidge:/fɪdʒ/| n.|坐立不安的人；烦躁不安|A person who fidgets a lot.|He's such a fidge, he can never sit still. 他真是个坐立不安的人，从来都坐不住。|近义词：restless person, fidgeter|反义词：calm person, relaxed person|词性:名词

Etymology:  From Middle English *figgen* (“to move restlessly, fidget”), perhaps of imitative origin.
