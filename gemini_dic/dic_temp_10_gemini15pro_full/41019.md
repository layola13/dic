copartnery:/koʊˈpɑːrtnəri/
n.
合伙；合伙企业；合伙关系
A copartnery is a partnership, especially in business.
The family established a copartnery in the textile industry.  这个家族在纺织业建立了合伙企业。
The copartnery dissolved after a year of disagreements.  由于一年来的分歧，合伙关系解散了。
synonym: partnership, alliance, association, collaboration
antonym: sole proprietorship, independence
noun
