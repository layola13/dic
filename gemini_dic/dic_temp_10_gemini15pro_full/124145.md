mortification: /ˌmɔːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ (UK) /ˌmɔːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ (US)
|n.|屈辱；羞愧；（尤指宗教原因的）克己；禁欲；坏疽|A feeling of extreme embarrassment or shame.|To his mortification, he realized he had called his boss by the wrong name.  他意识到自己叫错了老板的名字，感到非常羞愧。|synonyms: humiliation, shame, embarrassment, chagrin|antonyms: pride, honor, glory|noun
