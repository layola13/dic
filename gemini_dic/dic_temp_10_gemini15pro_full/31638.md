chatoyant:/ʃəˈtɔɪənt/ /ʃæˈtɔɪənt/
adjective
猫眼石的，具有变彩效应的；闪光的，闪耀的
Having a changeable luster or color with an iridescent effect, like that of a cat's eye.
The opal had a chatoyant quality, shifting from blue to green as I turned it in the light. 这块蛋白石具有变彩效应，当我把它对着光线转动时，颜色从蓝色变为绿色。

The fabric shimmered with a chatoyant sheen. 这块布料闪耀着变幻的光泽。

iridescent, shimmering, glittering, glistening, opalescent
dull, matte, flat

词性: adjective
