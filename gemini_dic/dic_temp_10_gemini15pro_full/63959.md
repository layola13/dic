episepalous:/ˌepɪˈsepələs/
adjective
附萼的，萼片上的
Growing on the sepals.  The stamens are episepalous, meaning they are attached to the sepals.
例句：The flower has episepalous stamens. 这朵花有附萼的雄蕊。

例句：Episepalous petals are a characteristic of this plant species. 附萼的花瓣是这种植物的特征。

近义词:  (No direct single-word synonym, but phrases like "attached to the sepals" or "borne on the sepals" can be used)
反义词: (No direct antonym exists)
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From epi- (Greek, 'upon', 'on') + sepalous (from 'sepal', ultimately from New Latin 'sepalum').
