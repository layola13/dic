eyesplice:/ˈaɪsplaɪs/| n. |眼接绳；绳端编接；|A knot joining the ends of two ropes by intertwining their strands. 将两根绳子的绳股相互交织连接起来的结。|The sailor made a secure eyesplice to join the two ropes. 水手用牢固的眼接绳将两根绳子连接起来。|近义词：splice, short splice|反义词: untie, separate|名词

词源: From "eye" (referring to the loop formed) + "splice" (joining ropes). 由“eye”（指形成的环）和“splice”（连接绳索）组成。
