hyperkeratotic:/ˌhaɪpərˌkɛrəˈtɑːtɪk/
adjective
角化过度的；过度角化的
Characterized by or exhibiting hyperkeratosis, an excessive growth of keratin on the skin.  This results in thickened, hardened skin.
The dermatologist diagnosed the rough patch of skin as a hyperkeratotic lesion. 皮肤科医生诊断这块粗糙的皮肤是角化过度的病灶。
The patient presented with hyperkeratotic plaques on their elbows and knees. 病人的肘部和膝盖上出现了角化过度的斑块。
Synonyms:  calloused, thickened, keratotic
Antonyms:  smooth, soft, thin
