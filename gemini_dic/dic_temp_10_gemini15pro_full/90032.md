hosiery:/ˈhoʊʒəri/ (US), /ˈhəʊʒəri/ (UK)| n.|袜类；针织品|Articles of clothing worn on the feet and legs, such as socks, stockings, tights, and pantyhose.  These items are typically made of knitted or woven materials.|She bought new hosiery for the winter. 她为冬天买了新的袜类。|近义词：socks, stockings, tights, pantyhose|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bare legs/feet")|noun
