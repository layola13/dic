nonadjustably:/ˌnɒnəˈdʒʌstəbli/
adverb
不可调节地
in a way that cannot be adjusted or changed.
The handlebars are fixed nonadjustably.  车把是固定不可调节的。
The interest rate is nonadjustably fixed at 5%. 利率不可调整地固定在5%。
近义词: fixedly, permanently, unalterably
反义词: adjustably, variably
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + adjustable + -ly (adverb suffix)
"non-"这个前缀表示“不”，“adjustable”表示“可调节的”，“-ly”是副词后缀。
