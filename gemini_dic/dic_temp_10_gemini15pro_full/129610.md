non-conductor:/ˌnɒn kənˈdʌktə(r)/ (US) /ˌnɑːn kənˈdʌktər/| n. |非导体；绝缘体|A substance that does not readily transmit heat, sound, or electricity.|Rubber is a good non-conductor of electricity. 橡胶是良好的绝缘体。|Synonyms: insulator, dielectric|Antonyms: conductor|noun

non-conducting:/ˌnɒn kənˈdʌktɪŋ/ (US) /ˌnɑːn kənˈdʌktɪŋ/| adj. |不导电的；绝缘的|Not able to conduct heat, sound, or electricity.|Wood is a non-conducting material. 木头是不导电的材料。|Synonyms: insulating|Antonyms: conducting|adjective
