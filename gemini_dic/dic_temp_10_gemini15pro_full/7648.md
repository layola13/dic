annuls:/əˈnʌlz/| v. |废除，取消，宣告无效|To declare officially that something is no longer valid; to make something null and void.|The marriage was annulled after only a week. 这段婚姻一周后就被宣告无效了。|Synonyms: invalidate, nullify, abolish, revoke, repeal|Antonyms: validate, ratify, confirm|verb

annuls:/əˈnʌlz/| v. |使无效|To deprive of force or efficacy; to make ineffective or inoperative.|The contract was annulled due to a breach of agreement.  由于违反协议，合同被宣告无效。|Synonyms: negate, cancel, countermand|Antonyms: enforce, uphold|verb
