all-best:/ɔːlˈbest/ | adj. |  最好的，一切顺利的 | Expressing good wishes; used to conclude a letter or message.  | Example: All best for the future! 祝你未来一切顺利！ | Synonym: best wishes, kind regards | Antonym: worst, ill-fated | Adjective

all-best:/ɔːlˈbest/ | n. | 最好的祝愿 | A valediction expressing good wishes. | Example: Give her my all-best.  代我向她致以最良好的祝愿。| Synonym: best wishes, regards | Antonym: curse, malediction | Noun
