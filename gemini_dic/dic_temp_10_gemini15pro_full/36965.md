colportage:/ˈkɒlpɔːtɪdʒ/ /kɑːlˈpɔːrtɪdʒ/| n.|兜售；传播；兜售的印刷品|The colportage of religious tracts was common in the 19th century. 19世纪兜售宗教小册子很常见。|例句：The organization engaged in the colportage of Bibles and other religious literature.  这个组织从事圣经和其他宗教文献的兜售。

例句：The colportage of cheap novels contributed to the rise of literacy. 廉价小说的兜售促进了识字率的提高。|近义词：distribution, dissemination, peddling|反义词: suppression, withholding|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *colportage*, from *colporter* "to carry around one's neck," from *col* "neck" (from Latin *collum*) + *porter* "to carry" (from Latin *portare*).
