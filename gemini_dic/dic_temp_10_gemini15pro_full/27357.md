cambrel:/ˈkæmbrəl/| n.| 坎布雷尔（一种斜屋顶）; 坎布雷尔（一种斜屋顶的房屋）|A cambrel is a roof, or a building with such a roof, having a double slope on each side. |The old barn had a distinctive cambrel roof.  那座旧谷仓有一个独特的斜屋顶。|The cambrel-roofed house stood on a hill overlooking the valley. 斜屋顶的房子坐落在俯瞰山谷的山坡上。 |Synonyms: gambrel, gambrel roof, mansard roof| Antonyms: flat roof, shed roof| noun
