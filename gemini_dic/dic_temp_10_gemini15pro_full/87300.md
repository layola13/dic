hermitish:/hɜːˈmɪtɪʃ/ | adj. | 隐士般的，遁世的 | Like a hermit; solitary and withdrawn. |  He led a hermitish existence in a remote cabin. 他在偏远的木屋里过着隐居般的生活。

He has become quite hermitish since his wife died. 自从妻子去世后，他就变得相当孤僻。| 近义词：reclusive, solitary, withdrawn, secluded | 反义词：gregarious, sociable, outgoing | 词性: adjective

词源: From hermit + -ish.  "Hermit" comes from the Late Latin "eremita," from the Greek "erēmitēs," meaning "desert dweller," from "erēmos," meaning "desert." The suffix "-ish" forms adjectives meaning "somewhat like" or "having the quality of."
