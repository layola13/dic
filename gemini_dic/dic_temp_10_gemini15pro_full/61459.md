embrace's:/ɪmˈbreɪsɪz/| n.|拥抱, 搂抱|The act of holding someone closely in one's arms, typically as a sign of affection, comfort, or love.  It can also refer to the acceptance or adoption of something (an idea, a belief, a change).|She greeted him with a warm embrace. 她热情地拥抱了他。|近义词：hug, cuddle, accept, adopt|反义词:reject, spurn, repel|词性:名词

embrace's:/ɪmˈbreɪsɪz/| v.|拥抱;欣然接受|Third-person singular present tense form of "embrace".  It signifies the action of holding someone close in one's arms as a sign of affection or comfort. It can also mean to willingly accept or support something (a belief, a change, a challenge).|He embraces his daughter before she leaves for school. 他在女儿上学前拥抱了她。|近义词：hugs, cuddles, accepts, adopts|反义词:rejects, spurns, repels|词性:动词
