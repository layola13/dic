cadger:/ˈkædʒər/| n.|乞丐；骗子；(尤指)要饭的；揩油的人|A person who tries to get something from someone else without paying for it or by persuading them that they need it; someone who begs or sponges off others.|He's such a cadger, always borrowing money and never paying it back. 他真是个爱占便宜的人，总是借钱不还。|Synonyms: beggar, sponger, freeloader, scrounger|Antonyms: giver, donor, philanthropist|noun

cadger:/ˈkædʒər/| v.|乞讨；骗取；揩油|To beg or sponge off others; to try to get something from someone without paying for it.|He's always cadging cigarettes from his friends. 他总是从朋友那里蹭烟。|Synonyms: beg, sponge, scrounge, freeload|Antonyms: give, donate, provide|verb
