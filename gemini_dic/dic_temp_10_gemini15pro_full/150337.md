panically:/ˈpænɪkli/| adv.|惊慌地，恐慌地|In a panicked way; with panic.  Showing or feeling sudden uncontrollable fear or anxiety.|The passengers ran panically from the burning bus.  乘客们惊慌地逃离了燃烧的公共汽车。|近义词：frantically, hysterically, desperately|反义词：calmly, serenely|词性:副词
词源: From panic + -ally.  Ultimately from the Greek god Pan.
