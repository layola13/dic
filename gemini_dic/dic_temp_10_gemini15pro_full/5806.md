amas:/ˈɑːmɑːz/ | v. | 堆积；积聚 | To heap or mass; to accumulate. (This verb form is less common) | The squirrel amassed a large supply of nuts for the winter. 松鼠为冬天积攒了大量的坚果。| Near synonyms: gather, collect, accumulate, hoard | Near antonyms: disperse, scatter, distribute | verb
