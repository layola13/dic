barcon:/ˈbɑːrkən/| n.|驳船；大型平底船|A barcon is a flat-bottomed boat, often used for carrying goods on rivers and canals.|The barcon was loaded with timber and slowly made its way down the river. 这艘驳船装满了木材，缓缓地沿着河向下游驶去。|近义词：barge, lighter|反义词: (no clear opposite)|词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *bargeoun, from Old French barge, barche ("small boat, barge"), from Vulgar Latin *barca, from Late Latin barcārius ("of or pertaining to boats"), from Latin barca ("small boat"), possibly borrowed from Gaulish *barcā ("boat").
