capable:/ˈkeɪpəbl/| adj.|有能力的，能干的|Having the skill, power, or other qualities necessary to do something.|She is a capable lawyer. 她是一位能干的律师.|近义词：able, competent, skilled, proficient|反义词：incapable, incompetent, unable, inept|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *capabilis*, from Latin *capere* "to take, seize, grasp, hold, understand".  The sense evolution is from "able to grasp or hold (mentally)" to "having capacity or ability."
