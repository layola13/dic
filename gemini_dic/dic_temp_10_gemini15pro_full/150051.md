palpitating:/ˈpælpɪˌteɪtɪŋ/| adj.|悸动的，砰砰跳的，（指心脏）急速跳动的|Beating rapidly; throbbing.|My heart was palpitating as I waited for the results. 我的心怦怦直跳，等待着结果。|近义词：throbbing, pounding, racing|反义词:calm, steady|词性:形容词
词源：From Latin palpitare "to throb, flutter," frequentative of palpare "to stroke, touch gently."
