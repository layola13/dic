nonretailers:/ˌnɑːnˈriːteɪlərz/
noun
非零售商
Businesses or individuals that do not sell goods directly to consumers.
Many nonretailers, such as manufacturers and wholesalers, operate behind the scenes. 许多非零售商，例如制造商和批发商，都在幕后运作。
Nonretailers often sell in bulk to retailers. 非零售商经常批量销售给零售商。
synonyms: wholesalers, distributors, manufacturers
antonyms: retailers, shops, stores
n.
