acrolith: /ˈækrəlɪθ/
noun
木芯雕像，尖顶雕像
A statue having the head, hands, and feet of marble and the rest of wood.
The museum displayed a rare acrolith from ancient Greece.  博物馆展出了一尊来自古希腊的珍贵的木芯雕像。

The acrolith was damaged during the earthquake.  地震中，木芯雕像遭到了损坏。
synonym: composite statue, part-marble statue
antonym: monolithic statue (整块石雕)
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *akrólithos*, from *ákros* (highest, outermost) + *líthos* (stone).  源自希腊语 *akrólithos*，由 *ákros*（最高的，最外面的）+ *líthos*（石头）组成。
