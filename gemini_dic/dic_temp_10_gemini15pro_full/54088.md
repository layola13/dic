disembarrass:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbærəs/ | v. | 解除...的负担；使摆脱尴尬 | To free from embarrassment or difficulty; to relieve from something burdensome or encumbering. |  He disembarrassed himself of his coat and sat down. 他脱下外套，坐了下来。

He was disembarrassed by the unexpected praise.  突如其来的赞扬使他感到不安。| 近义词: relieve, rid, free, extricate | 反义词: encumber, burden | 词性: 动词
