architectress: /ˈɑːrkɪˌtɛktrɪs/
n.
女建筑师
A female architect.  This term is now largely outdated and considered sexist.  "Architect" is the preferred term, regardless of gender.
例句：Zaha Hadid was a renowned architect.  扎哈·哈迪德是一位著名的建筑师。

例句：The architectress designed a beautiful and functional building.  这位女建筑师设计了一座美观实用的建筑。
近义词：architect, designer, builder
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps client)
词性：noun
