masters:/ˈmɑːstərz/ (US), /ˈmɑːstəz/ (UK)| n. |大师们；硕士学位；主人翁；精通；掌握|A person who has complete skill or knowledge of something; a second degree awarded by universities or colleges|例句：He is a master of disguise. 他是伪装大师。|近义词：expert, proficient, adept, skilled, specialist|反义词：novice, beginner, amateur|noun

masters:/ˈmɑːstərz/ (US), /ˈmɑːstəz/ (UK)| v. |掌握；精通；控制；征服|To acquire complete skill or knowledge of something; to gain control of; to overcome|例句：She finally mastered her fear of public speaking. 她终于克服了对公开演讲的恐惧。|近义词：conquer, overcome, control, manage, subdue|反义词：fail, surrender, submit|verb
