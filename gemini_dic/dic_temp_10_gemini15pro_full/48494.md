deepfake: /ˈdiːpfeɪk/ | n. adj. | 深度伪造，深度换脸 | A technique for human image synthesis based on artificial intelligence. It is used to combine and superimpose existing images and videos onto source images or videos using a machine learning technique known as a generative adversarial network (GAN). |  The video was quickly debunked as a deepfake. 这段视频很快就被揭穿是深度伪造的。 | synonyms:  AI-generated media, synthetic media | antonyms: authentic media, real footage | noun, adjective

deepfake: /ˈdiːpfeɪk/ | adj. | 深度伪造的 | Made or altered using deepfake technology. |  The deepfake video caused a scandal.  这个深度伪造的视频引发了一场丑闻。 | synonyms: fabricated, manipulated | antonyms: genuine, real | adjective
