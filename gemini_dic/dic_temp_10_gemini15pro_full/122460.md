mobility:/moʊˈbɪləti/ | n. | 流动性，移动性，机动性 | The ability to move or be moved freely and easily.  |  Electric cars are increasing mobility in urban areas. 电动汽车正在提高城市地区的交通流动性。

Improved mobility is key for elderly individuals to maintain independence.  行动能力的提高对于老年人保持独立至关重要。| 近义词：flexibility, maneuverability, portability | 反义词：immobility, rigidity, inflexibility | 词性: noun
