overconsciously: /ˌoʊvərˈkɑːnʃəsli/ | adv. | 过于在意地，过分自觉地 | Excessively aware of oneself and one's actions or appearance, often to the point of awkwardness or anxiety.  Derived from "over-" (excessively) + "conscious" (aware) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). | He overconsciously adjusted his tie before the interview.  他在面试前过分在意地调整了领带。 |  self-consciously, awkwardly | naturally, unselfconsciously | adverb
