overextrapolations: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈstræpəleɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度推断；过分外推 | Inferring something beyond the range of known data, often leading to inaccurate conclusions. |  The company's overextrapolations about future market share led to disastrous investment decisions.  这家公司对未来市场份额的过度推断导致了灾难性的投资决策。 | synonyms: overgeneralizations, overestimates, projections, assumptions | antonyms: underestimations, cautious predictions | noun
