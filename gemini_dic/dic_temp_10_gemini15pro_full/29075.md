carver:/ˈkɑːrvər/ (US), /ˈkɑːvə(r)/ (UK)| n.|雕刻家；雕工；切肉刀|A person who carves wood, stone, or other materials into artistic forms.  A person who carves meat.|He was a skilled carver of wooden figures. 他是一位技艺精湛的木雕人物雕刻家。|
The restaurant hired a professional carver for the Thanksgiving turkey.  这家餐厅聘请了一位专业的切肉师傅来切感恩节火鸡。|synonyms: sculptor, engraver, artist, whittler, slicer|antonyms: n/a|noun
