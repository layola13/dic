outplotted:/ˌaʊtˈplɒtɪd/
verb
智胜，以计谋胜过
defeated by superior planning or strategy.
She outplotted her rivals and won the contract. 她智胜了对手，赢得了合同。
Synonyms: outsmarted, outmaneuvered, outwitted
Antonyms: outdone, defeated
