comum:/koˈmũ/ (Brazilian Portuguese), /kuˈmũ/ (European Portuguese)| adj.|普通的，常见的，平常的|Common, usual, ordinary, frequent.  Referring to something that happens or exists often; something that is widely available or shared.|例句：É comum chover em São Paulo no verão.  It's common to rain in São Paulo in the summer.|近义词：usual, frequent, ordinary, habitual, normal, typical|反义词：rare, unusual, uncommon, infrequent, atypical|词性:形容词

comum:/koˈmũ/ (Brazilian Portuguese), /kuˈmũ/ (European Portuguese)| n.|共用，共享，公地|Something shared or used by all members of a group or community; a common area or resource.|例句：O jardim é um comum para todos os moradores do prédio. The garden is a common area for all the building's residents.|近义词：shared, joint, collective, public|反义词：private, individual, personal|词性:名词
