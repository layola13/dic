insofar:/ˌɪnsəˈfɑːr/ /ˌɪnsoʊˈfɑːr/ | adv. | 到这种程度；在…的范围内 | To the extent that; to the degree that. |  Example 1: Insofar as I am able, I will help you.  尽我所能，我会帮助你。

Example 2: The new law is good insofar as it simplifies the tax system, but it has some flaws.  新法律在简化税制方面是好的，但它也有一些缺陷。 | 近义词: to the extent that, as far as, so far as | 反义词: completely, entirely | 词性: 副词
