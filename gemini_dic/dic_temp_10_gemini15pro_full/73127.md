for-their-part:/fɔːr ðeər pɑːrt/ | phrase | 就他们而言；至于他们；对他们来说 |  This phrase is used to introduce what a particular person or group did or thought, especially when other people or groups were involved in the same situation.  It highlights the actions or opinions of a specific party. | For their part, the company agreed to the terms of the contract. 就他们而言，公司同意了合同条款。

For their part, the students were excited to start the new school year. 就学生而言，他们对新学年的开始感到兴奋。 | Synonyms:  as for them, on their behalf,  concerning them | Antonyms:  N/A | phrase
