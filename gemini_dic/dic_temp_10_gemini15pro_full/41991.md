costage: /ˈkɒsteɪdʒ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːsteɪdʒ/ (US)| n.|共同负担的费用；分摊的费用；联合制作的费用|The costage of the production was shared between the two theatre companies.  这个作品的制作费用由两家剧院公司共同承担。|例句：The costage for the new venture was split three ways.  新企业的启动资金由三方分摊。

例句：The artists agreed on a costage arrangement for their joint exhibition. 艺术家们就联合展览的费用分摊达成了一致。

近义词：shared cost, joint expense, split cost|反义词: sole expense, individual cost |词性: 名词
