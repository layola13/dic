half-exposed:/ˌhæf ɪkˈspoʊzd/
adjective
半露的，部分暴露的
partially exposed or uncovered; not completely hidden or protected.
The half-exposed wires posed a safety hazard.  半露的电线造成了安全隐患。

The half-exposed shipwreck lay on the reef. 半露出的沉船躺在礁石上。

近义词:partially exposed, partly visible, partly uncovered
反义词:fully covered, completely hidden, concealed
词性:adjective


词源:
"half-" (Old English healf) + "exposed" (past participle of "expose," from Latin "expausare," to lay open)
