ascendantly:/əˈsɛndəntli/
adverb
上升地，优势地
In a manner of ascending; upward.  Characterized by an increase in power or influence.
Her career progressed ascendantly, quickly reaching the top of her field. 她的职业生涯蒸蒸日上，迅速达到了她所在领域的顶峰。
The sun ascendantly moved across the sky. 太阳逐渐升上天空。
近义词: upwardly, increasingly, progressively, risingly
反义词: descendingly, decreasingly, declining
词性: adverb

词源:  From ascend (上升) + -ant (形容词后缀，表示“...的”) + -ly (副词后缀).  Ultimately from Latin *ascendere, from ad- ("to, towards") + scandere ("to climb").
