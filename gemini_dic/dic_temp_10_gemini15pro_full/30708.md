certitude:/ˈsɜːrtɪtʃuːd/ /ˈsɝtɪtuːd/| n. |确定性；确信|The state of being or feeling certain; complete assurance; freedom from doubt.|She spoke with absolute certitude about the future of the company. 她对公司的前途充满信心。|近义词：certainty, conviction, assurance, confidence|反义词：doubt, uncertainty, skepticism, dubiety|词性:名词
Etymology: From Late Middle English *certitude, from Old French certitude, from Late Latin certitūdō (“certainty”), from Latin certus (“certain”).
