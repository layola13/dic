noctilucin:/ˌnɒktɪˈluːsɪn/| n.|夜光素|A luciferin found in dinoflagellates and other bioluminescent organisms that emits light when oxidized.|The glow of the sea at night is caused by noctilucin in the microorganisms.|Synonyms: luciferin, photoprotein|Antonyms: None|noun

noctiluca:/ˌnɒktɪˈluːkə/| n.|夜光虫|A genus of free-living, marine dinoflagellates that exhibit bioluminescence.|The noctiluca created a sparkling display in the bay.|Synonyms: sea sparkle, firefly of the sea|Antonyms: None|noun
