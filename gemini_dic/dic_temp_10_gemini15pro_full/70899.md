fire-eater:/ˈfaɪərˌiːtər/| n.|吞火者；敢作敢为的人；爱争吵的人|A performer who pretends to swallow fire.|The fire-eater amazed the crowd with his daring act. 吞火者以他大胆的表演使观众惊叹。|Synonyms: daredevil, stuntman, showman|Antonyms: coward, timid person|noun

fire-eater:/ˈfaɪərˌiːtər/| n.|好争吵的人|A person who is aggressive and quarrelsome.|He's a real fire-eater, always looking for an argument. 他真是个好争吵的人，总是找茬儿。|Synonyms:  rabble-rouser,  agitator,  provocateur|Antonyms: peacemaker, mediator|noun
