feasibleness:/ˈfiːzəbəlnəs/ |n.|可行性，可能性|The feasibility of the project is still under discussion.  该项目的可行性仍在讨论中。|例句：The feasibility of the project depends on funding.  该项目的可行性取决于资金。|近义词：practicability, viability, possibility|反义词：infeasibility, impracticality, impossibility|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From feasible + -ness. Feasible comes from Old French faisable "doable," from faire "to do," from Latin facere "to make, do."
