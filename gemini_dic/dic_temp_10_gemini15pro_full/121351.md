misgovernor:/ˌmɪsˈɡʌvərnər/
n.|管理不善者，治理不当者|A person who governs badly or improperly. | The misgovernor was impeached for corruption.  这个管理不善的官员因腐败被弹劾。| 近义词: bad governor, incompetent administrator, tyrant | 反义词: good governor, competent administrator |词性: 名词

misgovernor:/ˌmɪsˈɡʌvərnər/
n.|暴君|A ruler who governs oppressively and unjustly. |The misgovernor imposed harsh taxes on the people.  这个暴君向人民征收苛捐杂税。| 近义词: tyrant, despot, dictator | 反义词: benevolent ruler |词性: 名词
