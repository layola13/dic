floodlight:/ˈflʌdˌlaɪt/ | n. |泛光灯，泛光照明|A large, powerful lamp that produces a wide beam of light, typically used to illuminate outdoor areas at night. |The football field was bathed in the bright glare of the floodlights. 足球场沐浴在明亮的泛光灯下。

The security floodlights switched on as someone approached the building.  当有人靠近大楼时，安全泛光灯亮了。|近义词：spotlight, searchlight|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "dim light" or "darkness") | noun
