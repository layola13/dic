belayers: /bɪˈleɪərz/
n.
保护者，确保者
A person who belays another climber.
The belayers were attentive and kept the climbers safe.  保护者们很专注，确保了攀登者的安全。


近义词:  secures, safeguards, protectors
反义词:  endangerers


词性: 名词 (plural of belayer)


词源:  From the verb "belay," which comes from the Middle Dutch "belagen" meaning "to besiege" or "to lie in wait for."  In climbing, it evolved to mean securing a rope to protect a climber.
