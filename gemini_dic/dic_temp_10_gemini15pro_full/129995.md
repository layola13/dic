non-negotiable:/ˌnɒn nɪˈɡəʊʃiəbl/ (US) /ˌnɑːn nɪˈɡoʊʃiəbl/| adj.|不可协商的，没有商量余地的|Not open to discussion or modification; fixed and unchangeable.|The price is non-negotiable.  价格不可商量。|Synonyms: inflexible, unalterable, fixed, firm, final|Antonyms: negotiable, flexible, adaptable|adjective


non-negotiable:/ˌnɒn nɪˈɡəʊʃiəbl/ (US) /ˌnɑːn nɪˈɡoʊʃiəbl/| n.|不可协商的事项|Something that is not open to discussion or modification.|Safety regulations are non-negotiables in this company.  在这家公司，安全规章是不可协商的。|Synonyms: requirement, stipulation, condition|Antonyms: option, choice|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + negotiable.  Negotiable comes from the Latin word "negotiari" meaning "to do business."
