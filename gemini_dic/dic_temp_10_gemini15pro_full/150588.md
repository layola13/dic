paper-thin:/ˈpeɪpər θɪn/| adj.|薄如纸的，极薄的|Extremely thin; as thin as a sheet of paper.|The walls of the tent were paper-thin, offering little protection from the cold.  帐篷的墙壁薄如纸，几乎无法抵御寒冷。

The steak was cooked paper-thin and crispy.  牛排做得薄如纸，很脆。|近义词：wafer-thin, flimsy, delicate, fine, thin|反义词：thick, dense, substantial|词性:形容词
