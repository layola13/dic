clan:/klæn/
n.|家族，氏族，宗族|A group of close-knit and interrelated families, especially characteristic of traditional societies.|The MacGregor clan has a long and storied history. 麦格雷戈家族有着悠久而传奇的历史。|family, tribe, group|individual|noun
clan:/klæn/
n.|（志趣相投的）群体，帮派|A group of people with a strong common interest.|A clan of motorcycle enthusiasts gathered at the cafe. 一群摩托车爱好者聚集在咖啡馆。|group, clique, circle|outsider|noun
