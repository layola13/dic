bellyflopping: /ˈbeliˌflɑːpɪŋ/ | n. | 肚皮拍水；肚子先着水 | The act of landing flat on one's stomach in water. | He made a loud bellyflopping into the pool. 他肚子着水，重重地摔进了游泳池。 | synonyms: belly flop,  splashing, plummeting (less precise) | antonyms: diving,  pencil dive | noun

bellyflop: /ˈbeliˌflɑːp/ | n. v. | 肚皮拍水；肚子先着水 |  A dive in which the abdomen hits the water first, or the act of performing such a dive. | He did a painful bellyflop into the shallow end. 他在浅水区来了个痛苦的肚皮拍水。| synonyms: belly flopping, splashing | antonyms: diving | noun, verb
