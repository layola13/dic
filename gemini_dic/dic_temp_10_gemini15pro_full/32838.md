choledology:/ˌkɒlɪˈdɒlədʒi/ | n. | 胆道学 | The study of the bile ducts and their diseases. | Choledology is a subspecialty of gastroenterology. 胆道学是胃肠病学的一个分支学科。|  Choledochoscopy is a common procedure in choledology. 胆道镜检查是胆道学中的常见手术。 |Synonyms: biliary surgery, biliary tract surgery | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps could consider areas outside the biliary system like hepatology (liver study) as a contrasting field.) | noun
