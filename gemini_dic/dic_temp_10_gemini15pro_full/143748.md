outmanipulated:/ˌaʊtməˈnɪpjuleɪtɪd/
verb
被巧妙地操纵，被高明地控制
skillfully controlled or influenced someone to gain an advantage, typically in a dishonest or devious way.
He felt outmanipulated in the negotiation, realizing too late he'd agreed to unfavorable terms. 他感到在谈判中被巧妙地操纵了，意识到太晚了，他已经同意了不利的条款。
The politician outmanipulated his opponents and won the election.  这位政客以高超的手腕胜过对手，赢得了选举。
Synonyms: outmaneuvered, outwitted, outsmarted
Antonyms: outplayed, defeated, manipulated
