hypothec:/haɪˈpɒθɪk/ (US) /haɪˈpɒθɛk/ (UK)| n.| 抵押，担保，(尤指苏格兰法律)不动产抵押|A hypothec is a legal right over someone's property that you have until they pay back money they owe you.  It's especially used in Scottish law for mortgages.|The bank had a hypothec on the farmer's land.|Synonym: mortgage, security, lien, charge|Antonym: release|noun
