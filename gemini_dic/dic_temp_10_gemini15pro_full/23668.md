branglement: /bræŋˈɡlmənt/
n.
纠缠，混乱，争吵

The branglement between the two families lasted for years.  这两个家族之间的争吵持续了多年。
The Christmas tree was a branglement of lights and tinsel. 圣诞树上灯光和金箔缠成一团。
synonyms: tangle, snarl, brawl, wrangle, quarrel, altercation, fracas
antonyms: order, harmony, peace, agreement, calm
