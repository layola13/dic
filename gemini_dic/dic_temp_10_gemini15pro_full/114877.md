mammons:/ˈmæmənz/
n.
财富，钱财 (尤指被认为是邪恶或不道德的)
Mammon is a term for wealth or material possessions, especially as an object of worship or devotion.  It is often used to describe the corrupting influence of riches.
They worshipped the false god of mammons. 他们崇拜金钱这个邪神。
近义词: riches, wealth, money, capital
反义词: poverty, destitution

词性:n.
