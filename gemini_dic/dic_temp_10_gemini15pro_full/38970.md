confined:/kənˈfaɪnd/ | adj. | 受限的，狭窄的，幽禁的 | Restricted in area or volume; limited; cramped. Also, restricted to a certain place, as by illness. | The patient was confined to bed after the surgery. 病人手术后只能卧床休息。|  The animals were kept in confined spaces. 动物们被关在狭小的空间里。 |近义词：restricted, limited, cramped, imprisoned, captive |反义词：free, open, unrestricted, spacious, liberated| 词性: 形容词
