corrugate:/ˈkɔːrəɡeɪt/ (US), /ˈkɒrəɡeɪt/ (UK)| v. adj.|使起皱纹，起皱纹的|to contract or cause to contract into wrinkles or folds; wrinkled or furrowed.|The metal sheet was corrugated to increase its strength.  金属板被弄皱以增加其强度。|Synonyms: wrinkle, furrow, crease, crinkle|Antonyms: smooth, flatten|verb

corrugate:/ˈkɔːrəɡeɪt/ (US), /ˈkɒrəɡeɪt/ (UK)| adj.|起皱纹的，波纹的|shaped into alternating ridges and grooves.|Corrugated cardboard is commonly used for packaging. 瓦楞纸板通常用于包装。|Synonyms: wrinkled, furrowed, creased, crinkled|Antonyms: smooth, flat|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *corrugatus*, past participle of corrugare (“to wrinkle”), from com- (“together”) + rugare (“to wrinkle”), from ruga (“wrinkle”).
