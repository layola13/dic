curling:/ˈkɜːlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɝːlɪŋ/ (US)| n. |冰壶运动；卷发；卷曲|A sport in which two teams of four players slide heavy stones across a sheet of ice towards a target area.|例句：Curling is a popular winter sport. 冰壶是一项很受欢迎的冬季运动。

例句：She has beautiful curling hair. 她有一头美丽的卷发。|近义词：coiling, twisting, winding|反义词: straightening|词性: 名词


curling:/ˈkɜːlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkɝːlɪŋ/ (US)| adj. |卷曲的|Having a tendency to curl.|例句：The curling smoke rose from the chimney. 卷曲的烟雾从烟囱里升起。

例句：Her hair is naturally curling. 她的头发天生卷曲。|近义词：curly, wavy, coiling|反义词: straight|词性: 形容词
