oversilent:/ˈoʊvərˈsaɪlənt/| adj.|过分沉默的，过于安静的|Excessively silent; more silent than is usual or desirable.  Characterized by an unusual or excessive lack of sound or speech.|He was oversilent during the meeting, which made everyone else uncomfortable. 他在会议期间过于沉默，这让其他人都感到不舒服。|The library was oversilent, even for a library.  即使对图书馆来说，这里也过于安静了。|近义词：too quiet, excessively silent, unnaturally quiet|反义词：talkative, loquacious, noisy|词性:形容词
