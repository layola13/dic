oliganthous: /ˌɒlɪˈɡænθəs/ | adj. | 少花的，花少的 | Having few flowers; bearing few blossoms. | The oliganthous plant produced only a couple of blooms this year.  今年这株少花的植物只开了几朵花。| 近义词:  pauciflorous, sparsely flowered | 反义词: floriferous, multiflorous | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: From Greek *oligos* (few) + *anthos* (flower).
