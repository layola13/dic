buckleless:/ˈbʌkəlləs/
adjective
无扣的；没有扣环的
Without a buckle.
Her buckleless belt was constantly slipping. 她那条没有扣的腰带不停地滑落。
buckleless shoes 无扣鞋
unbuckled, strapless
buckled, fastened
