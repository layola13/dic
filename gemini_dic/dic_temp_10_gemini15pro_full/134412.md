noninvidiousness:/ˌnɒnɪnˈvɪdiəsnəs/ | n. | 无歧视；公正；不偏不倚 | The state or quality of being free from envy, resentment, or discrimination.  It implies fairness and impartiality. | The committee emphasized the noninvidiousness of their selection process, ensuring all applicants were treated equally. 委员会强调了他们甄选过程的公正性，确保所有申请人都得到平等对待。|  fairness, impartiality, objectivity, neutrality, equitableness | invidiousness, discrimination, bias, prejudice | noun
