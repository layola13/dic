gaming's:/ˈɡeɪmɪŋz/
|n.|游戏的；游戏产业的|Gaming's popularity has exploded in recent years.  游戏产业的受欢迎程度近年来呈爆炸式增长。|The future of gaming's development is bright. 游戏发展的未来是光明的。|近义词:game industry, video game sector|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑 traditional entertainment)|词性:名词所有格

Etymology:
From "gaming," a noun formed from the verb "game" (meaning to play games), plus the possessive suffix -'s.  The word "game" itself has a long history, originating from Old English "gamen" and ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*gamanan."
