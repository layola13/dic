oogonium: /ˌoʊ.əˈɡoʊ.ni.əm/ | n. | 卵原细胞 | An immature female reproductive cell that gives rise to primary oocytes by mitosis. |  The oogonium undergoes several rounds of mitotic divisions before entering meiosis. 卵原细胞在进入减数分裂之前 undergoes several rounds of mitotic divisions。| The oogonia develop within the ovaries of the fetus. 卵原细胞在胎儿的卵巢内发育。 | synonyms:  primitive germ cell | antonyms:  spermatogonium | noun
