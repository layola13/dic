eschaunge:/ɛksˈtʃeɪndʒ/ | n. | 交换；交易；交易所 | An act of giving one thing and receiving another (especially of the same type or value) in return. |  He made an eschaunge of prisoners with the enemy. 他与敌人交换了俘虏。 | synonyms: exchange, barter, trade, swap | antonyms: retain, keep, hoard | noun

eschaunge:/ɛksˈtʃeɪndʒ/ | v. | 交换；交易 | Give something and receive something of the same kind in return. | They eschaunged gifts at Christmas. 他们在圣诞节交换了礼物。 | synonyms: exchange, barter, trade, swap | antonyms: retain, keep, hoard | verb
