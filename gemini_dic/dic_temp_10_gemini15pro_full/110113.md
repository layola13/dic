lickspittle:/ˈlɪkspɪtl/| n.|拍马屁的人，谄媚者|A person who behaves obsequiously to those in power, a fawning flatterer.|He was a lickspittle to the boss, always agreeing with everything he said. 他对老板是个十足的马屁精，总是对老板言听计从。|近义词:sycophant, flatterer, toady, yes-man|反义词:rebel, nonconformist|词性:noun

lickspittle:/ˈlɪkspɪtl/| adj.|谄媚的，奉承的|Excessively obedient or submissive; fawning.|He gave a lickspittle response, praising the king's every decision. 他给出了一个谄媚的回应，赞扬国王的每一个决定。|近义词:sycophantic, fawning, servile, obsequious|反义词:independent, defiant|词性:adjective
