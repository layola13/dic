nonbulbous:/ˌnɒnˈbʌlbəs/
adjective
非球茎的
Not having a bulb; lacking a bulb-like swelling at the base.
Many wildflowers are nonbulbous perennials. 许多野花是非球茎多年生植物。
non-bulbous, acaulescent
