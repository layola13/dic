creditworthinesses:/ˈkrɛdɪtˈwɜːrðiːnɪsɪz/
n.
信用价值；信誉度；信用可靠性
The state of being considered a good credit risk; the extent to which a person or company is considered suitable to receive credit, especially because of their ability to repay.
The bank carefully assesses the creditworthinesses of all loan applicants. 银行仔细评估所有贷款申请人的信用价值。
Several factors affect a business's creditworthinesses, including its payment history and debt levels.  几个因素会影响企业的信用价值，包括其付款历史和债务水平。
Synonyms: credit standing, credit rating, solvency, reliability
Antonyms: unreliability, insolvency, riskiness
Noun
