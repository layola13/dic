dolesome:/ˈdoʊlsəm/ | adj. | 忧郁的；凄凉的；阴沉的 | Expressing or causing gloom or dejection; dreary. | The dolesome weather matched her mood. 天气阴沉，与她的心情相符。| The dolesome sound of the wind howling through the trees sent shivers down my spine. 风穿过树林发出凄凉的声音，让我脊背发凉。| 近义词: gloomy, melancholic, dreary, dismal | 反义词: cheerful, bright, happy | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Middle English *dolsom, from Old English *dolsum (“causing grief, sorrowful”), equivalent to dole +‎ -some. Cognate with Scots dolesome (“doleful, sorrowful”).  Related to dull.
