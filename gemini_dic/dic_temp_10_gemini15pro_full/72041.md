flipside:/ˈflɪpsaɪd/| n.|反面，另一方面，负面|the opposite side of something; a different aspect of something, especially one that is less welcome|例句：Every coin has two sides;  we need to consider the flipside of this decision. 每个硬币都有两面；我们需要考虑这个决定的另一面。

例句：The flipside of his genius was his inability to handle everyday life. 他天才的另一面是他无法处理日常生活。|近义词：downside, reverse, other side, drawback|反义词: upside, advantage|词性:名词
