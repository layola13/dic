libament:/ˈlaɪbəmənt/| n.| 祭品，少量，一小部分 |A small portion offered as a libation or sacrifice.  A tiny amount or sample of something. |  The priest offered a libament of wine to the gods.  祭司向神献上少量葡萄酒作为祭品。  She gave us a libament of her new novel to read. 她给了我们她新小说的一小部分让我们阅读。|synonyms: offering, taste, sample, sip, morsel, snippet|antonyms: whole, entirety, abundance|noun
