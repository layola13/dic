good-tempered:/ˌɡʊdˈtempərd/ | adj. | 好脾气的，温和的 | Having or showing a pleasant, calm disposition; not easily annoyed or angered.  | She's a good-tempered child, always smiling and happy. 她是个脾气很好的孩子，总是笑呵呵的。 |  He remained good-tempered despite the long delays. 尽管长时间的延误，他仍然保持着好脾气。 |  近义词: amiable, affable, easygoing, placid | 反义词: bad-tempered, irritable, grumpy | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "good" + "tempered". "Temper" in this context refers to one's disposition or state of mind.
