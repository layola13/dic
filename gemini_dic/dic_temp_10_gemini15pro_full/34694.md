cleromancy: /ˈklɪərəʊˌmænsi/ (US) /ˈklɛrəʊˌmænsi/ (UK)| n. |占卜，神签| Divination by casting lots, or by observing the behavior of sacred objects.  It involves seeking divine guidance by interpreting seemingly random events. |  They practiced cleromancy to determine the best time to plant their crops. 他们通过神签来决定最佳播种时间。|  The priest used cleromancy to determine the will of the gods.  祭司用占卜来决定神明的旨意。 |Synonyms: divination, sortilege, augury | Antonyms:  N/A | noun
