one-size-fits-all:/ˌwʌn saɪz ˈfɪts ɔːl/
adjective
一体适用的；通用的
Designed to be suitable for everyone or every purpose.
This one-size-fits-all approach to education isn't working. 这种一刀切的教育方法行不通。
Synonyms: universal, generic, standard
Antonyms: customized, bespoke, tailored
词性: 形容词


one-size-fits-all:/ˌwʌn saɪz ˈfɪts ɔːl/
noun
一体适用的东西；通用的方法
Something designed to be suitable for everyone or every purpose.
They're trying to develop a one-size-fits-all solution to the problem. 他们正试图找到一个通用的解决方案。
Synonyms: panacea, cure-all
Antonyms: specialized solution, tailored approach
词性: 名词
