nonfederated:/nɒnˈfɛdəreɪtɪd/
adjective
非联邦的；未加入联邦的
Not united under a federal government; not belonging to a federation.
例句：The nonfederated territories maintained their own distinct laws.  这些未加入联邦的领土保持着各自独特的法律。

例句：Several nonfederated states joined together to form a new alliance.  几个非联邦的州联合起来组成了一个新的联盟。

近义词：independent, autonomous, separate, sovereign
反义词：federated, unified, united, joined
词性：adjective
