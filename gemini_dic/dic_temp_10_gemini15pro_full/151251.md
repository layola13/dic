parentheses:/pəˈrɛnθəˌsiz/ /pəˈrɛnθɪˌsiz/| n.|括号，圆括号|A pair of curved marks ( ) used to enclose words or figures that clarify or are regarded as parenthetical.|The dates are shown in parentheses. 日期显示在括号中。|近义词：brackets, round brackets|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

parenthesis:/pəˈrɛnθəsɪs/ /pəˈrɛnθɪsɪs/| n.|插入语，插入成分|A word, phrase, or sentence inserted as an aside or explanation within a passage or sentence.|The parenthesis in the sentence is unnecessary. 句子中的插入语是不必要的。|近义词：interpolation, aside|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
