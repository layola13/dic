dotardly:/ˈdoʊtərdli/| adj.|老朽的；衰老的；昏聩的|Exhibiting or characteristic of the mental decline associated with old age; senile.|His dotardly actions caused much embarrassment for his family. 他的老糊涂行为让家人很尴尬。|近义词：senile, decrepit, feeble, infirm|反义词：youthful, vigorous, robust|词性:形容词
词源: From dotard + -ly. Dotard from Middle English dotard, from dote + -ard.
