arrival: /əˈraɪv(ə)l/ (US) /əˈraɪvl/ (UK)| n.|到达，抵达；到来；到达者，到达物|The arrival of the train was delayed. 火车的到达延误了。|例句：

The arrival of spring brought warmer weather. 春天的到来带来了温暖的天气。

We eagerly awaited the arrival of our guests. 我们热切地等待着客人们的到来。|近义词：advent, coming, appearance|反义词：departure, leaving|词性：名词

词源：From Old French arriver "to come to shore, arrive,"  from Vulgar Latin *arripare "to come to shore," from Latin ad "to" + ripa "shore, bank."
