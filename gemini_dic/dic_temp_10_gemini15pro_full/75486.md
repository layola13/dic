frustule: /ˈfrʌsˌtjuːl/ | n. |硅藻壳; 硅藻的细胞壁 |The intricate patterns on diatom frustules are beautiful under a microscope. 在显微镜下，硅藻壳上复杂的图案很漂亮。| The frustule of a diatom is made of silica. 硅藻的细胞壁是由二氧化硅构成的。 | 近义词:  shell, test | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源: From Latin *frustulum, diminutive of frustum (“piece, bit, fragment”).
