hyperdicrotic:/ˌhaɪpərdaɪˈkrɒtɪk/ | adj. | 过度重搏的，过度双峰脉的 |  Characterized by a double pulse beat, with the second beat being more pronounced than the first, often indicative of low blood pressure or reduced peripheral resistance.  | The patient exhibited a hyperdicrotic pulse, suggesting a significant drop in blood pressure. 病人的脉搏呈现过度重搏，表明血压显著下降。 | The physician noted a hyperdicrotic pulse in the patient's chart. 医生在病人的病历中记录了过度重搏的脉搏。 | 近义词: dicrotic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider terms like monocrotic or normal pulse) | 词性: adjective
