imbibing:/ɪmˈbaɪbɪŋ/
verb
吸收，吸取；喝；接受（信息、想法等）
absorbing or drinking (liquids); receiving and absorbing (information or ideas)
He was imbibing the culture of the new city. 他正在汲取这座新城市的文化。
近义词: absorbing, drinking, taking in, assimilating
反义词: abstaining, rejecting, exuding
词性: verb (present participle)
