paraffinized:/ˈpærəfɪnaɪzd/| adj.|石蜡化的，浸过石蜡的|Treated or impregnated with paraffin wax.  This process makes something waterproof or less permeable.|The paraffinized paper protected the documents from moisture. 石蜡纸保护文件免受潮湿。|waxed, waterproofed, sealed|permeable, absorbent|adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From paraffin + -ize (suffix forming verbs meaning "to treat with," "to cause to be," "to become," "to make like") + -ed (suffix forming past participles of regular verbs). Paraffin ultimately comes from Latin *parum affinis ("having little affinity") because it is chemically inert.
