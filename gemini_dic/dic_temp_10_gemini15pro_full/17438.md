beanies:/ˈbiːniz/| n.|无檐小便帽，针织帽|A small, close-fitting hat, typically knitted from wool.|例句：She wore a bright red beanie to keep her ears warm. 她戴着一顶鲜红色的针织帽来保暖。|近义词：cap, hat, toboggan|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the slang term "bean," meaning "head," which originated in the late 19th century.  The suffix "-ie" is a diminutive, suggesting smallness.
