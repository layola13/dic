nonnutrients:/nɒnˈnjuːtriənts/ | n. |非营养物质|Substances in food that do not provide nourishment but may have other effects on the body, such as fiber or antioxidants.| Dietary fiber is a nonnutrient that aids digestion.  膳食纤维是一种有助于消化的非营养物质。| synonyms: non-nutritive substances | antonyms: nutrients | noun

nonnutrient:/nɒnˈnjuːtriənt/ | adj. | 非营养的|Not providing nourishment.|  Some nonnutrient components of food can still be beneficial to health.  一些食物的非营养成分仍然对健康有益。| synonyms: non-nutritive | antonyms: nutritive, nutritious | adjective
