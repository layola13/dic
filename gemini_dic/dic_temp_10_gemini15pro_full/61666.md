emitting:/ɪˈmɪtɪŋ/
| v. |发射，散发|Producing and discharging something, such as light, heat, or a smell.|The volcano is emitting smoke and ash. 火山正在喷发出烟雾和火山灰。|synonyms: radiating, discharging, releasing, giving off, exuding|antonyms: absorbing, concealing, withholding|Verb
