haematic:/hɪˈmætɪk/ /hiːˈmætɪk/
adjective
血的；血液的；关于血液的
relating to blood or blood cells.
Example: Haematic disorders can be serious. 血液疾病可能很严重。

Example: The doctor ordered a haematic test. 医生要求做血液检查。

synonym: bloody, sanguineous, sanguinary
antonym: bloodless, anemic

Etymology: From Greek haima (blood) + -ic.
