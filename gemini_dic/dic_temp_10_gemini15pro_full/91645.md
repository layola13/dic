hyperbolize:/haɪˈpɜːrbəlaɪz/ | v. | 夸大；夸张 | To represent something as much larger, better, worse, etc. than it really is. | He hyperbolized the dangers of the journey to deter his friends from going. 他夸大了旅途的危险，以阻止他的朋友们去。

She tends to hyperbolize when talking about her accomplishments. 她谈论自己的成就时往往会夸大其词。

| 近义词: exaggerate, overstate, amplify, embellish | 反义词: understate, downplay, minimize | 词性: verb
