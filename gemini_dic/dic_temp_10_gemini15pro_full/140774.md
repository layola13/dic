omosternal:/ˌoʊmoʊˈstɜːrnəl/| adj.|胸骨附近的，与胸骨有关的|Relating to the omosternum, a bone or bony process connecting the coracoid with the sternum in some vertebrates, particularly amphibians and reptiles.  In humans, it refers to the area near the sternum and clavicle. |The omosternal bone is a key feature in the skeletal anatomy of certain amphibians.  某些两栖动物的骨骼解剖结构中，胸骨骨是一项关键特征。

The doctor palpated the omosternal area for any abnormalities. 医生触诊了胸骨附近区域，检查是否有异常。|Near-synonyms:  sternal, clavicular,  pectoral |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider terms referring to opposite body areas like dorsal or abdominal) |Word type: adjective
