multiseptate:/ˌmʌltiˈsɛpteɪt/
|adj.|
|多隔膜的，多隔的|
|Having multiple septa or partitions.  A multiseptate structure is divided into multiple compartments or chambers by thin walls or membranes called septa.|
|The fungus produces multiseptate spores.  这种真菌会产生多隔孢子。|
|The multiseptate uterus is a rare congenital anomaly. 多隔子宫是一种罕见的先天性异常。|
|septate, partitioned, compartmentalized|
|nonseptate, aseptate|
|adjective|
