nonalliance:/ˌnɑːnəˈlaɪəns/
n.
不结盟，不联盟
The state of not being in an alliance with any other country, group, or organization.
Switzerland maintained a policy of nonalliance throughout World War II.  瑞士在整个二战期间都保持着不结盟的政策。
non-alignment, neutrality
alliance, coalition
noun
