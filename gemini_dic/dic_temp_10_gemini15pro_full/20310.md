bisetose:/baɪˈsiːtoʊs/
adjective
具二刚毛的；有两根刚毛的
Covered with two bristles or stiff hairs.
The bisetose antennae of the insect helped it navigate.  这种昆虫的两根刚毛触角帮助它导航。

The plant's leaves are bisetose, with a pair of bristles at the tip.  这种植物的叶子是双刚毛的，顶端有一对刚毛。
近义词:bristly, setaceous
反义词:glabrous, smooth
词性:adjective
