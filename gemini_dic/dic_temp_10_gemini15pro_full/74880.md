freshed:/freʃt/ | adj. | 清新; 新鲜的 |  Made or become fresh. Feeling or appearing refreshed and revived; not tired. |  After a good night's sleep, I felt refreshed.  睡了一夜好觉后，我感到精神焕发。 | 近义词: revived, invigorated, renewed | 反义词: tired, exhausted, drained | 词性:形容词

词源: "freshed" is the past participle of the verb "freshen," which comes from the Old English word "fersc" meaning "fresh."
