baru: /ˈbaruː/
adjective|新的，最近的|new, recent| This is a baru car. 这是一辆新车。|例句：The hotel is baru and modern.  这家酒店很新也很现代。|近义词：new, recent, fresh, modern|反义词：old, ancient, antique|词性:形容词
词源: From Malay *baru*, ultimately from Proto-Malayo-Polynesian *baʀu*. Cognates include Tagalog *bago*, Fijian *vou*, and Hawaiian *hou*.
