gloriosa:/ɡlɔːriˈoʊsə/ /ɡlɔːrɪˈəʊsə/
n.|嘉兰百合，火焰百合，攀援百合|A genus of flowering plants in the family Colchicaceae, notable for their showy, often red or orange, flowers.|The gloriosa lily is a striking addition to any garden. 嘉兰百合是任何花园的一个引人注目的补充。|近义词：climbing lily, flame lily|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

Etymology->
From Latin *gloriōsus*, meaning "full of glory," from *glōria* "glory."
