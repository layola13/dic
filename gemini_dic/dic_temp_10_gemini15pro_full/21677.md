bmi:/ˌbiː ɛmˈaɪ/| n. |身体质量指数|Body Mass Index, a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.|Example:  A BMI over 30 is considered obese.  身体质量指数超过30被认为是肥胖。|Example:  Doctors use BMI to screen for weight categories that may lead to health problems. 医生使用BMI来筛查可能导致健康问题的体重类别。|近义词:Quetelet index|反义词:None|noun
