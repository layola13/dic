club:/klʌb/| n. v. |俱乐部，社团；棍棒，球杆；(纸牌中的)梅花|A group of people who meet regularly for a shared interest or activity; a heavy stick used as a weapon; the suit of playing cards marked with a black trefoil.|He joined the photography club. 他加入了摄影俱乐部。|He defended himself with a club. 他用棍棒自卫。|She played the queen of clubs. 她出了一张梅花Q。|近义词：society, association, group, stick, bat|反义词：(n.) none; (v.) separate, divide|词性:名词, 动词

club:/klʌb/| v. |用棍棒打；凑钱；联合|To hit someone with a club; to contribute money collectively; to combine or unite.|He clubbed the seal. 他用棍棒打了海豹。|They clubbed together to buy a present. 他们凑钱买了一份礼物。|Several small businesses clubbed together to share office space. 几家小企业联合起来共享办公空间。|近义词：hit, strike, beat, pool, combine|反义词：separate, divide|词性:动词
