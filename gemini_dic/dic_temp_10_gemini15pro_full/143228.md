outdanced: /ˌaʊtˈdænst/ /ˌaʊtˈdɑːnst/
词性: verb
中文翻译: 跳舞跳得比…好
英文解析: To dance better than someone else.
例句: He outdanced everyone at the party. 他在派对上跳舞跳得比所有人都好。

例句:  She outdanced her competitors and won the competition. 她比她的竞争对手跳得更好，赢得了比赛。

近义词: surpassed, beat, defeated, triumphed over
反义词: be outdanced by, lose to
