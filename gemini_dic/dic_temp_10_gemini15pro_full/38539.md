conclude:/kənˈkluːd/ /kənˈklud/
verb
结束；推断出；达成；议定
To bring to an end; finish. To arrive at a judgment or opinion by reasoning.
The meeting concluded at 5 pm. 会议在下午5点结束。
After careful consideration, we concluded that the project was not feasible. 经过仔细考虑，我们得出结论，该项目不可行。
近义词: finish, end, terminate, deduce, infer
反义词: begin, start, commence
词性: verb

conclude:/kənˈkluːd/ /kənˈklud/
noun
结论 (rarely used as a noun)
The concluding part of something.
There was no satisfactory conclude to the story. 故事没有令人满意的结局。(这种用法比较少见)
近义词: end, conclusion, finish
反义词: beginning, start, commencement
词性: noun
