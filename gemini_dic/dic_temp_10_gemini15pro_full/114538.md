malapropism: /ˌmæləˈprɒpɪzəm/ (British) /ˌmæləˈprɑːpɪzəm/ (American)| n. |  误用词，用词错误 | the unintentional misuse of a word by confusion with one that sounds similar |  She's a real dog lover - she calls her pet a "canine catastrophe" instead of a "canine companion," a classic malapropism. 她真是个爱狗人士——她把她的宠物叫做“犬类灾难”而不是“犬类伙伴”，这是一个典型的误用词。 |  misusage, solecism, catachresis |  correct usage | noun
