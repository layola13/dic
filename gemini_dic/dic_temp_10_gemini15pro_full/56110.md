doctress: /ˈdɒktrɪs/ (UK) /ˈdɑːktrɪs/ (US) | n. | 女医生；女博士 | A female doctor or physician.  Now largely considered archaic and replaced by "doctor". |  Example:  In older novels, you might encounter the term "doctress." 在旧小说中，你可能会遇到“doctress”这个词。

Example: The doctress tended to the wounded soldiers.  这位女医生照料受伤的士兵。| 近义词: doctor, physician | 反义词: patient | 词性: noun
