hyperimmunizing:/ˌhaɪpərˈɪmjʊˌnaɪzɪŋ/
verb
高度免疫; 超量免疫
The process of inducing an abnormally high level of immunity in an organism, typically through repeated injections of an antigen.
Researchers are hyperimmunizing horses to produce antivenom. 研究人员正在对马进行超量免疫以生产抗蛇毒血清。
The lab is hyperimmunizing rabbits to create antibodies.  实验室正在对兔子进行超量免疫以产生抗体。
synonyms: immunizing heavily, superimmunizing
antonyms:  weakening immunity, immunosuppressing
词性: 动词
