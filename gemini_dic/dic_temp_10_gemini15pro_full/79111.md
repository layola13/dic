gladder:/ˈɡlædər/ (US), /ˈɡlædə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更高兴的，更乐意的|Comparative form of glad: more glad.  Feeling pleasure or happiness to a greater degree. |She was glad to see him, but even gladder to hear his good news.|Synonyms: happier, more cheerful, more joyful, more delighted|Antonyms: sadder, more sorrowful, more depressed|Adjective
