paroled:/pəˈroʊld/| adj.|假释的，获得假释的|Released from prison before the end of a sentence, on the condition of good behavior.|He was paroled after serving three years of a five-year sentence. 他服刑三年后获得假释，原判五年。|近义词：released, freed, liberated|反义词：imprisoned, incarcerated|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the French "parole," meaning "word" or "speech," referring to a prisoner's promise to behave well.
