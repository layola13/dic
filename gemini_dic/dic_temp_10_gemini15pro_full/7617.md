annuals:/ˈænjuəlz/ | n. |一年生植物|Plants that complete their life cycle within one year.|She planted colorful annuals in her garden every spring. 她每年春天都在她的花园里种植色彩鲜艳的一年生植物。|The annuals bloomed brightly, adding a splash of color to the landscape.  一年生植物开得鲜艳，给景色增添了一抹色彩。|Synonyms: yearly plants |Antonyms: perennials|Noun

annuals:/ˈænjuəlz/ | n. |年刊;年鉴|Publications that are released once a year.|The library subscribes to several literary annuals. 图书馆订阅了几份文学年刊。|He researched the historical event using old newspaper annuals. 他用旧报纸年鉴研究了这一历史事件。|Synonyms: yearbooks, almanacs |Antonyms: periodicals|Noun
