implore:/ɪmˈplɔːr/ (US) /ɪmˈplɔː(r)/ (UK)| v. |恳求；哀求；|To beg someone earnestly or desperately to do something.|例句：She implored him to stay. 她恳求他留下来。

例句：He implored the judge for mercy. 他恳求法官开恩。

近义词：beg, beseech, entreat, plead, supplicate|反义词：demand, command, order|词性:动词
