overglamorized: /ˌoʊvərˈɡlæməraɪzd/ | adj. | 过分美化的，过分渲染的 |  Having been portrayed as more glamorous than is true or appropriate; romanticized excessively.  | The movie overglamorized the life of a rock star, ignoring the struggles and hardships. 这部电影过分美化了摇滚明星的生活，忽略了其中的奋斗和艰辛。 | Example: The advertisement overglamorized the effects of the diet pills.  这则广告过分夸大了减肥药的效果。| Synonyms: romanticized, idealized, glorified, exaggerated | Antonyms: deglamorized, realistic, understated | Word type: Adjective
