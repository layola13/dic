outhammering:/ˌaʊtˈhæmərɪŋ/
verb
胜过，以锤击胜过
Surpassing or defeating someone or something by hammering more forcefully or effectively.
The blacksmith outhammered his apprentice in the competition.  这位铁匠在比赛中以锤击胜过了他的学徒。
He outhammered his rivals and won the forging contest. 他以锤击胜过对手，赢得了锻造比赛。
Synonyms: outdoing, surpassing, excelling, outperforming, outstripping
Antonyms: losing to, being surpassed by, being outdone by
词性:动词
