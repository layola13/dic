exacerbation:/ɪɡˌzæsərˈbeɪʃən/ | n. | 加重，恶化 | An increase in the severity of a disease or condition. |  The smoky air exacerbated her asthma. 浓烟加重了她的哮喘。| 近义词: aggravation, worsening, intensification | 反义词: improvement, amelioration, alleviation | 词性: 名词

exacerbation:/ɪɡˌzæsərˈbeɪʃən/ | n. | 激怒 | An act of making something worse. | His insensitive remarks exacerbated the situation. 他不敏感的言论使情况恶化。| 近义词: aggravation, worsening, intensification | 反义词: improvement, amelioration, alleviation | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->

From Late Latin *exacerbationem,  accusative of exacerbatio (“irritation, exasperation”), from Latin exacerbo (“I exasperate”), from ex (“out of, from”) + acerbo (“I make bitter, worsen”), from acerbus (“bitter, harsh, severe”).
