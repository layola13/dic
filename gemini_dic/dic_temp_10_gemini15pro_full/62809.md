enhancer:/ɪnˈhænsər/ | n. |增强剂；强化剂|A substance or factor that enhances or increases the effect of something else. | The flavor enhancer made the soup taste much richer.  这味增强剂使汤的味道更加浓郁。| synonyms: intensifier, amplifier, booster | antonyms: inhibitor, suppressor | noun

enhancer:/ɪnˈhænsər/ | n. | (genetics) 增强子 | A short region of DNA that can bind proteins to increase transcription of a gene.|  Researchers discovered a new enhancer that regulates muscle growth. 研究人员发现了一种调节肌肉生长的新增强子。| synonyms: regulator, promoter | antonyms: repressor, silencer | noun
