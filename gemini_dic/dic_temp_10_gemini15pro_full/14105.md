authentically:/ɔːˈθɛntɪkli/ | adv. | 真正地，地道地 | in a genuine and true way; in a way that accurately reflects the original or a tradition | She spoke authentically about her experiences growing up in rural China. 她真实地讲述了她在中国农村长大的经历。 |  She authentically recreated the style of the 1920s. 她真实地再现了20世纪20年代的风格。 | genuinely, truly, really, sincerely | falsely, artificially, fake | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *authenticus (source of Old French autentique, source of English authentic) + -ly.  The Latin word is derived from Greek authentikos "acting with one's own authority, genuine," from authentes "one acting on one's own authority," from autos "self" + hentes.
