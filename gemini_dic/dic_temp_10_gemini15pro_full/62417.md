endolymphatic:/ˌɛndoʊlɪmˈfætɪk/| adj.|内淋巴的|Relating to the endolymph, the fluid contained within the membranous labyrinth of the inner ear.|The endolymphatic sac plays a crucial role in regulating inner ear pressure. 内淋巴囊在调节内耳压力方面起着至关重要的作用。|The doctor diagnosed an endolymphatic hydrops. 医生诊断为内淋巴积水。|近义词:intra-labyrinthine|反义词:None|词性:形容词

词源:
From endo- (within) + lymph (clear fluid) + -atic (adjectival suffix).
