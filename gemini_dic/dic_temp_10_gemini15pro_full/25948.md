burqa:/ˈbɜːrkə/ | n. | 面纱，罩袍 | A loose enveloping outer garment worn by some Muslim women, covering the body and face and having a mesh screen to see through. |  She wore a burqa that concealed her entire body. 她穿着一件遮住全身的罩袍。

She lifted the mesh screen of her burqa to speak. 她撩起罩袍的网纱说话。| 近义词：niqab, chador, veil | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly Western clothing) | 词性: noun
