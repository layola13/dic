antiprotons:/ˈæntiˌproʊtɒnz/ | n. |反质子|A subatomic particle with the same mass as a proton but opposite charge (negative).  It is the antiparticle of the proton.|Scientists at CERN have created antiprotons for their research.  欧洲核子研究中心的科学家们已经为了研究制造出了反质子。|Synonyms: antiparticle (more general)|Antonyms: proton|Noun
