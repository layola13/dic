grillers:/ˈɡrɪlərz/
n.
烧烤者，烧烤用具
People who grill food, or tools used for grilling.
The grillers were busy preparing the barbecue. 烧烤者正忙着准备烧烤。
New grillers make cooking outdoors easier. 新的烧烤用具使户外烹饪更容易。
grill chefs, barbecue cooks
oven bakers, stovetop cooks
