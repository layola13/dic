macro-engineering:/ˌmækroʊˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrɪŋ/
n.
巨型工程；宏观工程
The design, construction, and operation of extremely large-scale engineering projects.
例句：Macro-engineering projects like the Three Gorges Dam can have significant environmental impacts. 巨型工程，例如三峡大坝，会对环境产生重大影响。

例句：Space colonization is a long-term goal of macro-engineering.  太空殖民是宏观工程的一个长期目标。

近义词：mega-engineering, large-scale engineering
反义词: micro-engineering
词性: noun
