concinnity:/kənˈsɪnɪti/
n.
优雅;和谐;简洁;精炼
the skillful and harmonious arrangement or fitting together of the different parts of something.  A harmonious arrangement or fitting together of parts; neatness of literary style; elegance of design.
Her essay was a model of concinnity, blending complex ideas with elegant prose. 她的文章是简洁的典范，将复杂的想法与优雅的散文融为一体。
The architect achieved a pleasing concinnity by incorporating both modern and classical elements.  建筑师通过融合现代和古典元素，实现了令人愉悦的和谐。
elegance, harmony, grace, neatness, precision
disorder, chaos, clumsiness, awkwardness, inelegance
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *concinnitās (“skillful joining together, harmony”), from concinnus (“skillfully joined together”), from com- (“together”) + cinnus (“a medley”).
