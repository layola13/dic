literately:/ˈlɪtərətli/| adv.|照字面地，逐字地；确实地，真正地|In a literal manner or sense; exactly.  She was literally speechless. 她真的说不出话来。|literally translated 照字面翻译|figuratively, metaphorically|adverb|
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *litteraliter, from Latin litteralis (“of or belonging to letters”), from littera (“letter, character of the alphabet”).
