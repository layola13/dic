captivated:/ˈkæptɪveɪtɪd/| adj.|着迷的，被吸引的|Deeply interested or attracted; fascinated.  Holding the attention of someone by being extremely interesting, exciting, pleasant, or attractive.|例句：I was captivated by her beauty. 我被她的美貌迷住了。|例句：The audience was captivated by his performance. 观众被他的表演迷住了。|近义词：enthralled, fascinated, charmed, mesmerized, spellbound|反义词：bored, uninterested, indifferent|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *captivatus*, past participle of *captivare* "to take or hold captive," from *captivus* "captive."
