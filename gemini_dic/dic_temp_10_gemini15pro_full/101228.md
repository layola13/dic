invite:/ɪnˈvaɪt/ (US) /ɪnˈvaɪt/ (UK)| vt. n.|邀请；招待；引诱|To request the presence of someone, typically for a social occasion or meeting.  To encourage or attract (something undesirable).|例句：I invited him to dinner. 我邀请他共进晚餐。|近义词：summon, request, ask, bid|反义词:disinvite, exclude|verb

invite:/ˈɪnvaɪt/ (US) /ˈɪnvaɪt/ (UK)| n.|邀请；请柬|An invitation.|例句：I received an invite to the party. 我收到了派对的邀请。|近义词：invitation, summons|反义词:None|noun
