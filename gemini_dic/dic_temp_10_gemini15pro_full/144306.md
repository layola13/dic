outsport:/ˌaʊtˈspɔːrt/
verb
胜过；在体育运动上超过
To surpass or defeat in a sport or athletic competition.
He outsported his rival in the final match. 他在决赛中胜过了他的对手。
verb
比…更擅长运动
To be more athletic or skilled in sports than.
She outsports everyone in her class. 她比班上任何人都更擅长运动。

近义词: outdo, surpass, excel, beat, defeat, triumph over, conquer
反义词: lose to, be defeated by

词性: verb (动词)

词源:  "out-" (超过) + "sport" (运动)
