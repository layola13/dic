inky:/ˈɪŋki/| adj.|墨水的；墨黑的；沾满墨水的|Covered or stained with ink; resembling ink in color.|例句：His fingers were inky from writing. 他的手指因为写字沾满了墨水。|例句：The night sky was inky black. 夜空漆黑一片。|近义词：black, dark, inky-black|反义词:pale, light, colorless|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *enki*, from ink + -y.  Ultimately from Latin *encaustum* "burnt in," used to describe a purple-red ink.
