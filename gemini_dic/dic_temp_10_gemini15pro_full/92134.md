hypersecretions:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈkriːʃənz/
|n.|过度分泌；分泌过多|The condition is characterized by hypersecretions of mucus. 这种疾病的特征是粘液分泌过多。| Hypersecretions of gastric acid can lead to ulcers. 胃酸分泌过多会导致溃疡。|近义词: overproduction, oversecretion|反义词: hyposecretion|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From hyper- + secretion.  "Hyper-" is a prefix of Greek origin meaning "over, above, beyond,"  and "secretion" comes from the Latin "secretus," past participle of "secernere," meaning "to separate."
