detat:/ˈdeɪˌtɑː/ | n. |分遣队，独立小分队|A detachment is a group of soldiers, vehicles, ships, etc., sent away from the main force on a special mission.| The army sent a small detachment to scout the enemy's position. 军队派出一小支分队去侦察敌人的位置。|Synonyms: unit, squad, troop, contingent, party|Antonyms: main force, whole army|noun
