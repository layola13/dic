heemraad:/ˈheɪmˌrɑːt/| n.|  地方议员；地方议会 (尤指南非)| A heemraad is a local council, especially in South Africa, or a member of such a council. |The heemraad met to discuss the town's budget. 地方议会开会讨论城镇预算。|The heemraad voted to approve the new development. 地方议员投票批准了新的开发项目。|近义词：council, councilor|反义词: (无明确反义词)|noun
