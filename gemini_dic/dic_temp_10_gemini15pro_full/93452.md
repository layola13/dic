illuministic:/ɪˌluːmɪˈnɪstɪk/| adj.|启蒙主义的，光明会的|Relating to or characteristic of the Enlightenment, a philosophical movement of the 18th century that emphasized reason and individualism rather than tradition.  Also, relating to or characteristic of the Illuminati, a secret society founded in Bavaria in 1776. |The book explored the illuministic ideals of the 18th century. 这本书探讨了18世纪的启蒙思想。|Near Synonyms: enlightened, rational, liberal|Antonyms:  obscurantist, reactionary, conservative|词性:形容词
