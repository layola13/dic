embrown:/ɪmˈbraʊn/| v. |使成褐色，加深颜色，染成褐色|To make brown or darker in color.  To stain or deepen the color of something, often implying a rich, dark brown hue.|The setting sun embrowned the western sky.|近义词：darken, brown, tan|反义词:lighten, bleach|词性:动词
Etymology: From em- (meaning "to cause to be") + brown.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *brunaz.
