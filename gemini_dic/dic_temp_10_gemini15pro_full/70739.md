fine:/faɪn/
adjective
好的，健康的
satisfactory or acceptable
The food was fine. 食物还不错。
近义词：good, excellent, great, wonderful
反义词：bad, poor, awful, terrible
词性: adjective

fine:/faɪn/
adverb
很好地，精细地
in a delicate or precise manner
She sings fine. 她唱得很好。
近义词：well, excellently, superbly
反义词：badly, poorly, terribly
词性: adverb

fine:/faɪn/
noun
罚款
a sum of money exacted as a penalty for an offense
He had to pay a fine for speeding. 他因超速行驶不得不支付罚款。
近义词：penalty, charge
反义词：reward, prize
词性: noun

fine:/faɪn/
verb
处以罚金
punish by a fine
The judge fined him $500. 法官对他处以500美元的罚款。
近义词：penalize, charge
反义词：reward, compensate
词性: verb

词源:  From Middle English *fin, from Old French fin (“end, limit, conclusion, settlement, payment”), from Latin fīnis (“end, boundary, limit, conclusion”).
