beggable:/ˈbeɡəbl/| adj.|可乞求的，值得乞求的|Capable of being begged; worth begging for.  Something that is so desirable that one might beg for it.|The tickets to the concert were so beggable, fans were camping out for days.|近义词：desirable, coveted, sought-after|反义词：undesirable, worthless|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From "beg" (from Old Norse "beggia" - to ask, request) + "-able" (suffix denoting capability or worthiness).
