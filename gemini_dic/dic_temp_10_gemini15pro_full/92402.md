hypodigm:/ˈhaɪpəˌdaɪm/ | n. |范例，范式，模范 | A hypodigm is a typical example or pattern of something. It can also refer to a subset of all possible examples of a concept or phenomenon. |  This painting serves as a hypodigm of Impressionist art. 这幅画是印象派艺术的范例。| synonyms: archetype, exemplar, model, paradigm | antonyms: anomaly, exception | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hypodeigma* "example, pattern, copy," from *hypodeiknynai* "to exhibit, show," from *hypo-* "under" + *deiknynai* "to show."
