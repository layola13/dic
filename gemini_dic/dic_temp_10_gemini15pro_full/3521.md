aftmost:/ˈæftməʊst/| adj.|最尾部的，最后面的| Situated furthest aft; nearest the stern of a ship or aircraft.| The aftmost cabin had the best view of the wake.  最尾部的船舱可以看到最好的尾流景色。|例句：The aftmost section of the plane was damaged in the landing. 飞机最尾部的部分在着陆时受损了。|近义词：sternmost, rearmost|反义词：foremost, forwardmost|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From *aft* (meaning "toward the stern of a ship") + *-most* (a superlative suffix).  *Aft* ultimately comes from Old English *æftan*, meaning "behind."
