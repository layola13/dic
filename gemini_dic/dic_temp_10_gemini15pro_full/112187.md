loom:/luːm/| v. n.| 隐约出现；逼近；织布机| (of something large or unclear) appear in a threatening way. A machine for weaving.|  The crisis loomed large. 危机迫在眉睫。| 近义词：emerge, appear, threaten, | 反义词: recede, disappear | verb
loom:/luːm/| n.| 织布机| A machine for weaving.|  The loom is an ancient invention. 织布机是一项古老的发明。| 近义词： weaving machine | 反义词: - | noun

Etymology:
From Old English *geloma "tool, implement," of Germanic origin; akin to Old High German gluomo "tool." The sense narrowed to "weaving machine" by late Middle English.
