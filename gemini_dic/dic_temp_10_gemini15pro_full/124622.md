mousse:/muːs/ | n. |慕斯，泡沫，轻质甜点|A light, airy dessert made with whipped cream, egg whites, and often flavored with chocolate or fruit. Also, a foam used in styling hair.|The chocolate mousse was rich and decadent.  巧克力慕斯浓郁而美味。|She used mousse to give her hair more volume. 她用慕斯让头发更蓬松。|Synonyms: froth, foam, dessert|Antonyms: solid, dense|noun

mousse:/muːs/ | n. |(车辆等陷住时铺在轮胎下的)泥浆，草皮，落叶|A thick, soft substance, such as mud, sod, or leaves, placed under the wheels of a vehicle to help it move when stuck.|They used branches and leaves to create a mousse for the stuck jeep. 他们用树枝和树叶为陷住的吉普车做了一个泥浆垫。|Synonyms:  mud, mire, bog|Antonyms: pavement, road|noun
