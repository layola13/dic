oviparousnesses:/ˌoʊvɪˈpærəsnəsɪz/
|n.| 卵生 |The state or quality of being oviparous; the condition of reproducing by laying eggs that hatch outside the mother's body.|Many reptiles exhibit oviparousnesses, laying their eggs in warm, sandy areas. 许多爬行动物表现出卵生性，将卵产在温暖的沙地。|近义词: oviparity|反义词: viviparity|词性: 名词

oviparous:/oʊˈvɪpərəs/
|adj.| 卵生的 |Producing eggs that hatch outside the mother's body.|Birds, most reptiles, and fish are oviparous animals. 鸟类、大多数爬行动物和鱼类都是卵生动物。|近义词: egg-laying|反义词: viviparous|词性: 形容词
