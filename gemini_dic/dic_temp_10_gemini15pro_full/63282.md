enthrone:/ɪnˈθroʊn/ | vt. | 登基；加冕；使…登上王位；推崇 | To place (someone) on a throne, especially as a monarch; to invest with sovereign power. |  The archbishop enthroned the new king.  大主教为新国王加冕。|Synonyms: crown, invest, install | Antonyms: dethrone, depose | verb

enthrone:/ɪnˈθroʊn/ | vt. | 使…居于最高地位；奉…为权威 | To exalt to a position of power or authority. | The critics enthroned her as the greatest actress of our time.  评论家们把她奉为我们这个时代最伟大的女演员。|Synonyms: exalt, elevate, glorify | Antonyms:  debase, humble | verb
