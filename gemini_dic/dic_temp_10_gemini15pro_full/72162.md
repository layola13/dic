floorcloths:/ˈflɔːrˌklɔːθs/, /ˈflɔːrˌklɑːðz/ | n. |油布; 地布;  |A heavy, durable piece of fabric used to cover and protect a floor. Traditionally, floorcloths were painted or decorated and used as a decorative and practical floor covering.  |  They used colorful floorcloths to brighten up the wooden floors. 他们用色彩鲜艳的油布来装饰木地板。

The antique floorcloth was a valuable addition to the room. 这块古董油布是房间里一件珍贵的装饰品。 | synonyms: linoleum, floor covering, rug, mat | antonyms: carpet, bare floor | noun
