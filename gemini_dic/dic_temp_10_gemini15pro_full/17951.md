befits:/bɪˈfɪts/| v.|适合，相称，与…相符|It befits you to be humble.  谦逊适合你。|近义词：suits, becomes, is appropriate for|反义词：misfits, ill-becomes|verb

Etymology:
From Middle English *befitten, from Old English *befetian (“to make fit, adapt”), from be- + fetian (“to fetch, arrange, adorn”).
