aeroplane:/ˈɛərəpleɪn/ (UK) /ˈɛrpleɪn/ (US)| n.|飞机|A powered heavier-than-air aircraft with fixed wings.|The aeroplane landed safely. 飞机安全着陆。|近义词：airplane, plane, aircraft|反义词: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *aéroplane*, from *aéro-* (from Greek *aḗr* "air") + *plane* (from Latin *planus* "flat, level").  The American spelling "airplane" is a simplified version.
