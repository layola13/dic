fasti:/ˈfɑːstiː/ (plural, often treated as singular)
noun
斋戒；斋戒期；禁食期
A period of abstinence from some or all food, drink, or both, especially as a religious observance.
例句：
The Ramadan fasti is a time of spiritual reflection and prayer.
斋月斋戒期是精神反思和祈祷的时期。

During Lent, many Christians observe a fasti.
在大斋节期间，许多基督徒都会进行斋戒。

近义词：fast, abstinence, lent
反义词：feast, indulgence
词性：noun
