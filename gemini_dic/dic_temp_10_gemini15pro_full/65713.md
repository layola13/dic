eventilate:/ɪˈvɛntɪleɪt/ | v. |使通风；公开讨论；表达（感情或想法）|To cause fresh air to enter and circulate freely in (a room, building, etc.); to discuss or examine (an idea, issue, etc.) openly or publicly; to express (a feeling, thought, etc.) openly |例句：We need to eventilate the room. 我们需要给房间通风。|近义词：air, aerate, ventilate, discuss, express, voice|反义词：stifle, suppress|词性:动词
词源: Late Middle English: from Latin eventilat- ‘fanned, winnowed’, from the verb eventillare, from eventus ‘outcome’, from evenire ‘come out, happen’. The original sense was ‘to expose to wind, winnow’; the current senses date from the mid 17th century.
