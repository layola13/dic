etherealism:/ɪˈθɪəriəlɪzəm/
n.|空灵主义，超尘脱俗|The quality of being ethereal; delicate and otherworldly beauty.|Her paintings capture a sense of etherealism, with their soft colors and dreamlike figures. 她的绘画捕捉到了一种空灵的意境，柔和的色彩和梦幻般的人物。|近义词：spiritualism, otherworldliness, mysticism|反义词：materialism, realism|词性:名词
Etymology: From ethereal + -ism. Ethereal comes from Late Latin aethereus, from Greek aithérios, from aithēr ‘upper air’.
