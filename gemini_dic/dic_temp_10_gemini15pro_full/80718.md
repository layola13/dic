gormander:/ˈɡɔːrmændər/ | n. | 饕餮之徒，贪吃的人 | A person who eats greedily or in excessive amounts. | He's such a gormander; he ate the entire cake by himself. 他真是个饕餮之徒，他一个人吃掉了整个蛋糕。 | glutton, gourmand, overeater | ascetic, abstainer | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the French *gourmand*, meaning "greedy eater," ultimately from Old French *gourmer*, "to taste, to savor."
