distancy:/ˈdɪstənsi/ | n. | 疏远，距离，冷淡 |  A state of being distant or aloof, either physically or emotionally. |  There was a certain distancy in her voice that made him uneasy. 她语气中某种冷淡让他感到不安。 | 近义词：remoteness, aloofness, coldness, reserve | 反义词：closeness, intimacy, warmth | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From the adjective "distant" + "-cy" (suffix forming abstract nouns).  "Distant" comes from Old French "distant" and ultimately from Latin "distantem" (present participle of "distare" - "to stand apart").
