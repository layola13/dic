fomenting:/ˈfoʊmentɪŋ/ (US), /ˈfəʊmentɪŋ/ (UK)| v.|煽动，助长，激起|to encourage or stir up (negative feelings or actions)|The politician was accused of fomenting unrest among the people.  这位政治家被指控煽动民众的不安。|The rebels were fomenting revolution. 叛乱分子正在煽动革命。|近义词：inciting, instigating, provoking, agitating|反义词：quelling, suppressing, calming, allaying|词性: 动词
