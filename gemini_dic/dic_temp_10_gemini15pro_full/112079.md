longingly:/ˈlɒŋɪŋli/ (US) /ˈlɑːŋɪŋli/| adv.|渴望地，热切地|In a way that shows you have a strong desire for something or someone.|She looked longingly at the chocolate cake. 她渴望地看着巧克力蛋糕。|近义词：yearningly, wistfully, desirously|反义词：indifferently, apathetically|词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "longing" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Longing" comes from the Old English "langung," meaning "yearning" or "desire."
