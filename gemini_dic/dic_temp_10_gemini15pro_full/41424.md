cornerless:/ˈkɔːrnərlɪs/| adj.|无角的，没有角落的|Lacking corners or angles; rounded.  Having no corners; without corners. |The artist created a cornerless sculpture, smooth and flowing. 艺术家创作了一个没有棱角的雕塑，光滑流畅。|The new building has a cornerless design, making it appear softer and more organic.  这座新建筑采用无角设计，使其外观更柔和，更有机感。|近义词：rounded, curved, smooth|反义词: angular, sharp, pointed|词性:形容词
Etymology: corner + -less.  "Corner" comes from Old French "cornier," from Latin "cornu" meaning "horn."  The suffix "-less" means "without."|"Corner" 源自古法语 "cornier"，来自拉丁语 "cornu"，意思是“角”。 后缀“-less”表示“没有”。
