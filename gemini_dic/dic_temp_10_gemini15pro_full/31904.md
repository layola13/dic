cheesiest:/ˈtʃiːziɪst/
adjective
最俗气的，最庸俗的
superlative adjective of cheesy: most cheesy. Excessively sentimental, overly emotional, or obviously contrived; hackneyed.
That movie had the cheesiest dialogue I've ever heard. 那部电影的对话是我听过的最俗气的。
Her pick-up line was so cheesy, I couldn't help but laugh. 她的搭讪台词太老套了，我不禁笑了出来。
synonyms: corny, tacky, hackneyed, clichéd, sentimental, maudlin, mawkish
antonyms: sophisticated, tasteful, refined, elegant, subtle
词性: 形容词
