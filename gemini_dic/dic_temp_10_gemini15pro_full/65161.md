ethnics: /ˈeθnɪks/
noun
伦理，道德规范;（尤指）民族的道德规范
A system of moral principles, especially one of a particular group.
例句：The ethics of journalism are much debated. 新闻业的职业道德备受争议。
例句：He began to question the ethics of his position. 他开始质疑自己立场的伦理问题。
近义词：morals, values, principles
反义词： immorality, dishonesty
