misconstrued:/ˌmɪskənˈstruːd/
|v. (past participle)|曲解，误解|To interpret (something, such as a statement or action) wrongly.|His silence was misconstrued as arrogance. 他的沉默被误解为傲慢。|Synonym: misinterpreted, misunderstood, misapprehended|Antonym: understood, comprehended, grasped|verb
