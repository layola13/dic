ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin aphorismus, from Greek aphorismos "definition, concise sentence," from aphorizein "to define, mark off, divide," from apo- "from" + horizein "to bound, limit".  来自晚期拉丁语 aphorismus，源自希腊语 aphorismos“定义，简洁的句子”，来自 aphorizein“定义，划分，分开”，来自 apo-“来自”+ horizein“界限，限制”。
