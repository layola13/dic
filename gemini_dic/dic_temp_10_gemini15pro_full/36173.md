cofferlike:/ˈkɒfəlaɪk/| adj.|像保险箱的；像盒子的|Resembling a coffer; chest-like.  A coffer is a strongbox or chest, often for storing valuables. So, something cofferlike suggests a similar shape, sturdiness, or perhaps a sense of containing something precious.|The room had a cofferlike ceiling with recessed panels.|近义词：chest-like, boxlike, vault-like|反义词:open, exposed|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From "coffer" + "-like". "Coffer" comes from Old French "cofre", ultimately from Latin "cophinus" meaning "basket".
