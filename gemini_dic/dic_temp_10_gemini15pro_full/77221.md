gastriloquy:/ɡæˈstrɪləkwi/ | n. | 腹语术 | The art of speaking in such a way that the voice seems to come from somewhere other than the speaker, typically from a dummy. |  He amazed the audience with his gastriloquy, making the dummy seem to talk. 他用腹语术使观众惊叹，让木偶看起来像在说话。 | ventriloquism | - | noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *gastriloquium, from Greek gastēr (belly) + Latin loqui (to speak).
