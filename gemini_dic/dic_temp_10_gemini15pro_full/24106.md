brevity:/ˈbrevɪti/| n.|简洁，简短|Conciseness of expression; shortness of duration.  The quality of being brief or terse.|His essays are models of clarity and brevity. 他的文章是清晰简洁的典范。|近义词：conciseness, terseness, succinctness|反义词：prolixity, verbosity, lengthiness|noun
