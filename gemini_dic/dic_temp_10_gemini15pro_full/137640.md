nonubiquitous:/ˌnɒnjuːˈbɪkwɪtəs/
adjective
非无所不在的；非普遍存在的
Not present everywhere; not ubiquitous.
Specialized sensors are nonubiquitous and expensive.  专门的传感器并不普遍，而且价格昂贵。
The internet, while increasingly common, is still nonubiquitous in many parts of the world. 虽然互联网越来越普及，但在世界许多地方仍然并非无处不在。
近义词: rare, scarce, infrequent, localized, limited
反义词: ubiquitous, omnipresent, widespread, pervasive, universal
词性: adjective
