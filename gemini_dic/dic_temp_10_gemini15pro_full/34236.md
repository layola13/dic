clannishness:/ˈklænɪʃnɪs/
n.
排他性，抱团性，小团体意识
The tendency to be close-knit and exclusive, often associating only with members of one's own group and showing distrust or dislike of outsiders.
Their clannishness made it difficult for newcomers to integrate into the community.  他们的排他性使得新来者很难融入社区。
The tight-knit community was known for its clannishness, rarely welcoming outsiders. 这个紧密的社区以其抱团性而闻名，很少欢迎外来者。

近义词:exclusiveness, insularity, cliquishness
反义词:inclusiveness, openness, gregariousness
词性:noun
