miscut:/mɪsˈkʌt/
noun
错误切割，切错
a cut made in the wrong place or in the wrong way
The miscut on the fabric ruined the entire garment.  这块布料的错误切割毁了整件衣服。
mis-cut
verb
错误地切割
to cut something incorrectly
I miscut the wood and now it's too short. 我把木头切错了，现在它太短了。
mis-cut
adjective
切错的
cut incorrectly
The miscut pieces of paper were useless. 这些切错的纸片没用了。

近义词:  mishandle, mismanage
反义词:  cut correctly
