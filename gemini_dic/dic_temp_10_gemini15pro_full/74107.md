foucault:/fuːˈkoʊ/ (US), /fuːˈkɔː/ (UK)| n.|福柯 (Michel Foucault, 法国哲学家, 社会学家)|A French philosopher, historian of ideas, social theorist, and literary critic. He is best known for his theories on power, knowledge, and discourse.|例句：Foucault's work has had a profound impact on social theory. 福柯的著作对社会理论产生了深远的影响。

例句：Many scholars have analyzed Foucault's concept of the panopticon. 许多学者分析了福柯的全景敞视概念。|近义词：thinker, philosopher, theorist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the French surname Foucault, ultimately from a Germanic personal name, Folchard (folk + hard = "brave/hardy people").
