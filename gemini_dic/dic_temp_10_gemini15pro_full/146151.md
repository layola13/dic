overengineer:/ˌoʊvərˌɛndʒɪˈnɪr/ | v. | 过度工程化；过度设计 | To design something to be more complex or robust than necessary. |  They overengineered the product, making it too complicated for the average user. 他们过度设计了产品，使其对普通用户来说过于复杂。| overdesign, overcomplicate | underengineer, simplify | verb
overengineers:/ˌoʊvərˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrz/ | n. | 过度工程化的人；过度设计的人 | People who overengineer things. | The overengineers on the team added features that nobody needed. 团队中的过度工程化人员添加了无人需要的功能。 | overdesigners, overcomplicators | simplifiers | noun
