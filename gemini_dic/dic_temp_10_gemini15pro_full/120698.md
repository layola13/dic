misaffect:/ˌmɪsəˈfɛkt/
verb
使不满；使不忠；使疏远
To alienate the affection or loyalty of; to fill with discontent.
The king's harsh policies misaffeted his subjects, leading to widespread unrest. 国王的严苛政策使他的臣民不满，导致了广泛的动乱。

The constant criticism misaffeted the team, causing them to lose motivation.  不断的批评使团队成员产生了不满，导致他们失去了动力。

近义词: alienate, estrange, disaffect
反义词: reconcile, conciliate
词性: verb


词源:
From mis- (expressing wrongness, badness, failure) + affect (to act on; produce an effect or change in).  由表示错误、坏、失败的mis-和表示作用、产生效果或改变的affect组成。
