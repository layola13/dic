creche: /kreʃ/ (UK) /kreɪʃ/ (US)
| n. |托儿所，日托中心|A nursery where babies and young children are cared for during the day.|例句：The company provides a creche for employees' children. 公司为员工的孩子提供托儿所。|例句：She dropped her son off at the creche before going to work. 她上班前把儿子送到托儿所。|近义词：nursery, daycare, day nursery|反义词: None|词性: Noun
Etymology: From French *crèche*, ultimately from Old French *creche* "crib, manger," from Frankish *krippja*, from Proto-Germanic *kribjon* "basket, crib."
