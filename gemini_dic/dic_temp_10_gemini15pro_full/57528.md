drags:/dræɡz/
| n. | 拖，拉；累赘；乏味的东西 |Something that is dragged, pulled, or drawn along or over a surface; something tedious or boring.  |The meeting was such a drag.  会议真是无聊透顶。 |近义词：burden, bore, nuisance |反义词：pleasure, delight |词性:名词(plural)

drags:/dræɡz/
| v. | 拖，拉；缓慢地移动；搜寻 |To pull something along the ground or other surface; to move slowly and laboriously; to search (a body of water) with a drag or dredge. |She dragged the heavy suitcase across the floor. 她在地板上拖着沉重的行李箱。|近义词：haul, pull, tow |反义词：push, shove |词性:动词(third-person singular present)
