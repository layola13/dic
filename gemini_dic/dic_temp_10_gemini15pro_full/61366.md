embezzling:/ɪmˈbɛzəlɪŋ/
v.| 盗用，挪用 |The act of dishonestly appropriating or secreting property entrusted to one's care.| He was caught embezzling funds from the company. 他被抓到挪用公司资金。|近义词：misappropriating, pilfering, defalcating|反义词:repaying, returning|verb

embezzle:/ɪmˈbɛzəl/
v.| 盗用，挪用 |To appropriate fraudulently to one's own use (money or property entrusted to one's care).| She embezzled thousands of dollars from her clients. 她挪用了客户的数千美元。|近义词：misappropriate, pilfer, defalcate|反义词:repay, return|verb

embezzler:/ɪmˈbɛzlər/
n.| 盗用者，挪用者 |A person who embezzles.| The embezzler was sentenced to five years in prison. 挪用公款者被判处五年监禁。|近义词：thief, pilferer, defalcator|反义词:donor, benefactor|noun
