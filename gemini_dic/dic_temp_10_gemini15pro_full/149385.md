pacifying:/ˈpæsɪfaɪɪŋ/| adj. | 安抚的，抚慰的，平息的 | Reducing or intended to reduce someone's anger or agitation. |  The mother spoke in a pacifying tone to her crying child. 母亲用安慰的语气对哭泣的孩子说话。

The government implemented pacifying measures to quell the unrest. 政府实施了安抚措施以平息骚乱。

近义词: calming, soothing, appeasing, mollifying, conciliatory | 反义词: agitating, provoking, inflaming, inciting | 词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *pacificare, from Latin pacificus ("peace-making"), from pax ("peace") + facere ("to make").
