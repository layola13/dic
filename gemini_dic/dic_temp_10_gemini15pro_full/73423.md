forelie:/fɔːrˈlaɪ/| v. |预示，预兆，比...先发生|to exist or occur before something else; to be a warning or indication of something that will happen in the future|The events that forelie the war are complex and numerous.  预示战争发生的事件复杂且数量众多。|Events forelie disaster. 这些事件预示着灾难。|近义词：presage, portend, foreshadow|反义词：follow, succeed|词性：动词

词源: From Middle English *forelien, from Old English *forelīeġan (“to lie before”), from fore- (“before”) + līeġan (“to lie”).
