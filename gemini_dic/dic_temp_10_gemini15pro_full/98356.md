insistence:/ɪnˈsɪstəns/ /ɪnˈsɪstəns/| n.|坚持，坚决主张|The teacher's insistence on punctuality made the students arrive on time. 老师对守时的坚持让学生们都准时到达。|Her insistence that we stay for dinner was flattering. 她坚持让我们留下吃饭，这让我们很受宠若惊。|近义词: persistence, perseverance, tenacity|反义词: acquiescence, compliance, submissiveness|词性:名词

Etymology->
From Late Middle English: insistens, from Late Latin: insistentia, from Latin insistere ‘persist’, from in- ‘on’ + sistere ‘stand’.
