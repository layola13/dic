ganocephala:/ˌɡænoʊˈsɛfələ/ | n. | 革头龙类 |  A group of extinct, large, heavily built, herbivorous pareiasaurs characterized by their bony skull ornamentation. | The ganocephala were a dominant group during the Permian period. 革头龙类在二叠纪时期是一个优势群体。|  The museum displayed a fossil of a ganocephalan. 博物馆展出了一块革头龙类的化石。|  pareiasaur | N/A | noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek γάνος (gános, “brightness, sheen”) + κεφαλή (kephalḗ, “head”)
