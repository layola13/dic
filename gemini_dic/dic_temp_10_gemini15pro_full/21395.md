blouselike: /ˈblaʊslʌɪk/
adjective
类似女衬衫的
Resembling a blouse; like a blouse.
The top she wore was blouselike, loose and flowing. 她穿的上衣很像女衬衫，宽松飘逸。
Her dress had a blouselike top. 她的连衣裙上半身像衬衫。
近义词: shirtlike, tuniclike
反义词: (No clear antonym, perhaps something like "form-fitting," "tight," or a specific garment type that is unlike a blouse, e.g., "sweaterlike")
词性: adjective
