chagrinning:/ʃəˈɡrɪnɪŋ/| adj.| 懊恼的， chagrinned 的现在分词形式|Feeling or showing displeasure or annoyance at having failed or been humiliated.|He gave a chagrinning smile when he realized his mistake. 他意识到自己的错误时，露出了懊恼的笑容。|近义词：annoyed, disappointed, embarrassed, humiliated, mortified|反义词：pleased, delighted, happy|词性: adjective

词源:
From French *chagrin*, from Old North French *chagrein*, possibly from *chagrinier* "to grieve," of unknown origin.
