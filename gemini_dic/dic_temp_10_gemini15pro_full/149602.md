painful: /ˈpeɪnfʊl/ | adj. | 疼痛的，痛苦的 | Experiencing or causing pain; distressing. | The injury was extremely painful. 伤势非常疼痛。 | Losing her dog was a painful experience. 失去她的狗是一段痛苦的经历。 | 近义词: aching, agonizing, distressing | 反义词: painless, comfortable, pleasant | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *paynful, from Old French painful, from pain + -ful.  Ultimately from Latin poena (“punishment, penalty”).
