bord:/bɔːrd/ | n. | board; edge; rim; ship's side |A flat piece of wood or other material, often rectangular, used for various purposes. Also, the side of a ship, or an edge or rim.| The pirate walked the bord of his ship. 海盗在他的船边走着。 | 近义词: side, edge, rim, plank, board | 反义词: center, middle | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Old English *bord (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *burdam. Cognate with Old Norse borð (“board, table, ship's side”), Dutch boord (“edge, rim, border”), German Bord (“board, ship's side”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰerdʰ- (“board, plank”).
