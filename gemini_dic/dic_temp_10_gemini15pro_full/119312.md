microbe:/ˈmaɪkroʊb/| n. |微生物，细菌|A microorganism, especially a bacterium causing disease or fermentation. |例句：The scientist studied the microbe under a microscope. 科学家在显微镜下研究微生物。

例句：Certain microbes are beneficial to the human gut.  某些微生物对人体肠道有益。|近义词：microorganism, germ, bacterium|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider macroorganism for contrast) |词性: noun

Etymology->
From French *microbe*, from *micro-* (small) + Greek *bios* (life).
