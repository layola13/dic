hypochondrias: /ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndrɪəs/ (US) /ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndriəs/ (UK)| n. |疑病症，臆想病|A psychological disorder characterized by excessive preoccupation with one's health and the fear of having a serious disease, despite medical reassurance to the contrary. | Example:  His hypochondrias led him to constantly seek medical attention. 他的疑病症导致他不断寻求医疗帮助。

Example: She suffered from hypochondrias and was convinced she had a rare illness. 她患有疑病症，确信自己患有罕见的疾病。

|近义词：hypochondria, health anxiety|反义词:  robust health, well-being |名词
