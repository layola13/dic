cap-in-hand:/kæp ɪn hænd/
| adj./adv.| 卑躬屈膝地；低声下气地；恳求地 | humbly and respectfully, often when asking for something | He went cap-in-hand to the bank manager, begging for a loan.  他低声下气地去找银行经理，恳求得到一笔贷款。 |例句：She went cap in hand to her boss to ask for a raise. 她低声下气地去求老板加薪。|近义词：humbly, submissively, pleadingly |反义词：demandingly, arrogantly | 词性: 形容词/副词

cap-in-hand:/kæp ɪn hænd/
| n.| 恳求；乞求 | the act of asking for something humbly and respectfully | His cap-in-hand approach didn't win him any favors. 他低声下气的请求并没有为他赢得任何好处。|例句：Going cap in hand to the competitors was a last resort.  低声下气地向竞争对手求助是最后的手段。|近义词：supplication, entreaty |反义词：demand | 词性: 名词
