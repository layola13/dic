overprovides:/ˌoʊvərprəˈvaɪdz/| v. |过度提供，供应过剩|to provide more of something than is needed or wanted| The government overprovided subsidized housing, leading to a surplus of empty apartments. 政府过度提供了补贴住房，导致大量公寓空置。| The charity overprovided food, and much of it went to waste.  慈善机构过度提供了食物，很多都浪费了。|近义词：oversupply, glut|反义词: underprovide, undersupply|词性:动词

词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "provide" (verb)

"over-"  means "excessively" or "too much"
"provide" comes from the Latin "providēre," meaning "to foresee, take care of, provide."
