authenticator:/ɔːˈθɛntɪkeɪtər/ | n. | 身份验证器，认证器 | A device or program that establishes the validity of a user or device. | Example:  Two-factor authentication often requires a physical authenticator like a security token.  双因素身份验证通常需要像安全令牌这样的物理身份验证器。

Example: The website requires an authenticator app to log in.  该网站需要身份验证器应用程序才能登录。 | 近义词: verifier, validator | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun

authenticators:/ɔːˈθɛntɪkeɪtərz/ | n. | 身份验证器，认证器（复数） | Multiple devices or programs that establish the validity of users or devices. | Example: Many online services now support hardware authenticators for increased security. 许多在线服务现在支持硬件身份验证器以提高安全性。

Example:  Users can choose from a variety of authenticators, including software tokens and biometric devices. 用户可以选择各种身份验证器，包括软件令牌和生物识别设备。 | 近义词: verifiers, validators | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
