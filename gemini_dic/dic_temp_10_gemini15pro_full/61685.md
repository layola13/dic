emmys: /ˈɛmiz/
noun
艾美奖 (复数)
Emmy Awards, awards given annually for excellence in American primetime television programming.
The Emmys are considered the most prestigious awards in television. 艾美奖被认为是电视界最 prestigious 的奖项。
She won three Emmys for her role in the series. 她因在该剧中的角色获得了三项艾美奖。
synonyms: awards, prizes, accolades
antonyms: (No direct antonym for an award itself, could be considered opposite of "winning":) losses, defeats
词性: 名词
