counter-marque:/ˈkaʊntərˌmɑːrk/ |n.|对等品牌；竞争品牌；对抗性品牌|A brand or product that is created or marketed to compete directly with another established brand or product, often by offering similar goods or services at a lower price or with different features.|Example 1: The new phone is seen as a counter-marque to the leading brand's flagship model. 这款新手机被视为对领先品牌旗舰机型的对抗性品牌。

Example 2:  Several counter-marques have emerged, challenging the dominance of the established luxury car manufacturers.  几个对等品牌已经出现，挑战了老牌豪华汽车制造商的统治地位。|Synonyms: competitor, rival, competing brand, alternative brand|Antonyms: partner, ally, collaborator|noun
