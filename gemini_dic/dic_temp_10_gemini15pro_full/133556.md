nonfireproof:/ˌnɒnˈfaɪəpruːf/ /ˌnɑːnˈfaɪərpruːf/| adj.|非防火的，不耐火的|Not resistant to fire; flammable.|例句：The old building was nonfireproof and posed a serious safety hazard. 这栋老建筑不防火，构成了严重的安全隐患。|近义词：flammable, combustible, inflammable|反义词:fireproof, fire-resistant|词性:形容词
