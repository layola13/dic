baptises:/bæpˈtaɪzɪz/
verb
施洗，给人施洗礼
administers the Christian rite of baptism to.  This is the third-person singular present tense form of the verb "baptise."
John the Baptist baptised Jesus in the River Jordan. 施洗约翰在约旦河为耶稣施洗。
The priest baptises the baby with holy water.  牧师用圣水为婴儿施洗。
Synonyms: christens, immerses, initiates
Antonyms:  There isn't a direct antonym for baptise.  Perhaps "excommunicate" could be considered in some contexts.
词性: 动词
