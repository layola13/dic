manang:/ˈmɑːnɑːŋ/ | n. | 大姐; (对年长女性的尊称)|  An elder sister or a respectful term for an older woman, particularly common in Filipino and some other Southeast Asian cultures. | Example 1:  She called her "Manang" even though they weren't related. 她称呼她为“Manang”，即使她们没有血缘关系。

Example 2:  The manang at the market sold me the best mangoes.  市场上的那位大姐卖给了我最好的芒果。| Synonym:  Ate (Filipino), Elder sister,  | Antonym:  Younger sister | Noun
