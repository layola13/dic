birders:/ˈbɜːrdərz/
noun
观鸟者
People who engage in the activity of birdwatching, observing and identifying different species of birds.
例句：The birders gathered at dawn to spot the rare migratory birds. 观鸟者们聚集在黎明时分，以发现罕见的候鸟。

例句：A group of birders were excited to see a bald eagle soaring overhead. 一群观鸟者兴奋地看到一只秃鹰在头顶翱翔。

近义词：birdwatchers, ornithologists (更偏向专业研究)
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
