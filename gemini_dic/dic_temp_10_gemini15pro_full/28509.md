carded:/ˈkɑːrdɪd/ | v. | 刷卡；记名；梳理（羊毛、棉花等） |  To have recorded someone's personal details, especially on a card, or to have checked someone's identity card;  To disentangle and arrange fibers by combing or carding | He carded his library card to borrow a book. 他刷了借书卡借了一本书。 |  The wool is carded before it is spun into yarn. 羊毛在纺成纱线之前要先梳理。 | synonyms: recorded, registered, combed, brushed | antonyms: unrecorded, unregistered, tangled, matted | verb

carded:/ˈkɑːrdɪd/ | adj. |  梳理过的 |  Combed or brushed, especially wool or cotton | The carded wool was soft and fluffy.  梳理过的羊毛柔软蓬松。 | synonyms: combed, brushed, processed | antonyms: uncarded, raw, unprocessed | adjective
