assigns:/əˈsaɪnz/ | n. |受让人；受托人 (法律)| A person to whom property or rights are transferred. | The assigns of the original owner will inherit the estate. 原所有人的受让人将继承遗产。|  The contract specifies the rights of the assigns. 合同规定了受让人的权利。|  transferee, recipient |  transferor, grantor | noun
