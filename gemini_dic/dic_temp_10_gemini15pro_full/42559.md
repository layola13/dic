counterordering:/ˌkaʊntərˈɔːrdərɪŋ/ | n. | 反命令，反指令 | The act of giving an order that contradicts a previous order. | The general's counterordering caused confusion among the troops. 将军的反指令使部队陷入混乱。 |  The CEO issued a counterordering, reversing the previous decision to downsize. 首席执行官发布了一项反指令，撤销了先前裁员的决定。 | 近义词: countermand, revocation, reversal | 反义词: order, command, instruction | noun
