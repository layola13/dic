nuance:/ˈnjuːɑːns/ (US), /ˈnjuːɒns/ (UK)| n.|细微差别；微妙之处|a subtle difference in or shade of meaning, expression, or sound.|例句：The nuances of her voice conveyed her true feelings. 她声音的细微差别传达了她真实的感受。

例句：He was aware of the cultural nuances of the situation. 他意识到了这种情况下的文化差异。|近义词：subtlety, shade, distinction, refinement|反义词：blatancy, obviousness|词性:noun
