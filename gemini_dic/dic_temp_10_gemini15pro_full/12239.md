ascence: /əˈsens/
n.
上升，升高
The ascence of the hot air balloon was a beautiful sight.  热气球的升空是一道美丽的景象。
The gradual ascence of the mountain path was tiring.  山路逐渐上升，令人疲惫。
Etymology: From Latin *ascensus*, from ascendere "to climb, mount," from ad- "to" + scandere "to climb."
近义词: ascent, rise, climb, elevation
反义词: descent, fall, decline
词性: 名词
