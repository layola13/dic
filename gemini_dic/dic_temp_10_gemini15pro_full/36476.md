coleader:/ˈkoʊˌliːdər/| n.|共同领导者，共同负责人|A person who shares the leadership of a group or organization with another or others.|Example:  The two coleaders presented a united front at the press conference. 两位共同领导人在新闻发布会上展现了团结一致的姿态。

Example:  The project has three coleaders, each with specific responsibilities.  这个项目有三位共同负责人，每人都有具体的职责。|近义词：co-leader, joint leader, shared leader|反义词:follower, subordinate|noun
