crackshot:/ˈkrækʃɒt/ (UK), /ˈkrækʃɑːt/ (US)| adj. n.|神枪手，射击高手|A person who is skilled in shooting, especially with a rifle.|He's a crackshot with a pistol. 他手枪打得很准。|近义词：marksman, sharpshooter, dead shot|反义词：poor shot, amateur|词性:形容词, 名词


Etymology:
From "crack" (meaning a sharp, sudden noise, as of a gunshot) + "shot" (meaning the act of firing a gun).  Implies someone who makes a clean, accurate shot.
