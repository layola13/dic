instituter:/ˈɪnstɪˌtjuːtər/ | n. | 设立者，创立者 | A person who institutes something; a founder. |  Example 1: He was the instituter of the new educational program. 他是新教育项目的创立者。

Example 2:  As the instituter of the company, she had a clear vision for its future. 作为公司的创立者，她对公司的未来有着清晰的愿景。| 近义词: founder, originator, establisher | 反义词: destroyer, demolisher | 词性: 名词


institute: /ˈɪnstɪˌtjuːt/ | v. | 建立；制定；开始 | To establish, organize, and set in operation; to begin. | Example 1: The government instituted a new policy on education. 政府制定了一项新的教育政策。

Example 2: They instituted legal proceedings against the company. 他们对该公司提起诉讼。 | 近义词: establish, found, create, initiate, commence | 反义词: abolish, terminate, end | 词性: 动词
