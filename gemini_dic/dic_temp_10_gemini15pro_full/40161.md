contender:/kənˈtendər/ | n. | 竞争者，角逐者 | Someone who competes with others for something, especially a championship or high position. |  The two contenders for the championship will meet in the final. 两位冠军争夺者将在决赛中相遇。|
contender:/kənˈtendər/ | n. | 竞争者，角逐者 | A person or group competing with others to achieve something. | She is a strong contender for the job.  她是这份工作的有力竞争者。|
contender:/kənˈtendər/ | n. | 竞争者，角逐者 | (boxing) a boxer, usually a good one. | He's a top contender in the heavyweight division. 他是重量级比赛中的顶级竞争者。|


近义词: competitor, rival, candidate, aspirant
