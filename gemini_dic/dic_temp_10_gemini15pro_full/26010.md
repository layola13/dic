bus:/bʌs/ | n. | 公共汽车 | A large motor vehicle carrying passengers by road, typically one serving the public on a fixed route and for a fare. |  I take the bus to work every day. 我每天乘公共汽车去上班。

The bus was late due to heavy traffic. 因为交通拥堵，公共汽车晚点了。

| 近义词: coach, minivan (for smaller buses) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Shortening of omnibus, from Latin *omnibus "for all," dative plural of omnis "all".  The omnibus was originally a horse-drawn public conveyance.
