flaps:/flæps/| n. v.|襟翼，拍打|A hinged or movable section of an aircraft wing that is used to increase lift or drag, especially during takeoff and landing; to move (wings, arms, etc.) up and down.|The pilot lowered the flaps for landing. 飞行员放下襟翼准备着陆。|近义词：ailerons, spoilers|反义词: None|n.
flaps:/flæps/| n. v.|拍打，拍打声|The motion or sound of something broad and flat, such as wings or cloth, moving up and down or back and forth.|The bird flapped its wings and flew away. 鸟儿拍打着翅膀飞走了。|近义词：flutter, beat|反义词: None|v.
