halogenous:/ˌhæləˈdʒɛnəs/ /ˌheɪləˈdʒɛnəs/
adjective
卤素的；含卤素的
Relating to or containing a halogen (any of the five electronegative elements fluorine, chlorine, bromine, iodine, and astatine, that form binary compounds with metals).
Halogens are highly reactive nonmetallic elements. 卤素是高活性非金属元素。
Many plastics contain halogenous flame retardants. 许多塑料含有卤素阻燃剂。
近义词:halide
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-halogenated")
词性:adjective
