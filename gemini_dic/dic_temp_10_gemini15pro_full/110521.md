limbless:/ˈlɪmlɪs/| adj.|无肢的，没有四肢的|Lacking limbs; having no arms or legs.|The limbless lizard slithered through the grass. 这只无肢的蜥蜴在草地上滑行。|The limbless veteran showed incredible resilience.  这位没有四肢的退伍军人展现了惊人的韧性。|近义词：apodal, without limbs|反义词：limbed, four-limbed|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From limb + -less.  "Limb" comes from Old English *lim, meaning "joint, limb".  "-less" is a suffix indicating absence or lack of something.
