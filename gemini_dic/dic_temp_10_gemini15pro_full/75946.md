funguslike:/ˈfʌŋɡəsˌlaɪk/
adj.
像真菌的；似真菌的
Resembling a fungus in form or function.
This funguslike organism thrives in damp environments.  这种类似真菌的生物在潮湿的环境中生长旺盛。
The mold growing on the bread had a funguslike appearance.  面包上长的霉菌看起来像真菌。
近义词:fungoid, fungal
