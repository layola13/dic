inertia:/ɪˈnɜːʃə/ (US) /ɪˈnɜːʃɪə/ (UK)| n. |惯性;惰性;不活动|A tendency to do nothing or to remain unchanged.|例句：The bureaucratic inertia of the system makes it difficult to implement rapid change.  该系统的官僚惰性使得难以实施快速变革。

例句：Because of inertia, the car kept rolling after it hit the wall. 由于惯性，汽车撞墙后仍然继续滚动。

近义词：inactivity, idleness, apathy, passiveness, resistance (to change), momentum|反义词：activity, energy, dynamism|词性:名词
