deseasonalized:/ˌdiːˈsiːzənəlaɪzd/
| adj. |
| 季节性调整后的，消除季节性因素的 |
|Adjusted to compensate for seasonal influences, removing the seasonal component from a time series. |
|The deseasonalized unemployment figures provide a clearer picture of the underlying trend. 季节性调整后的失业率数据更清晰地反映了潜在趋势。|

|Sales data is often deseasonalized to reveal underlying trends. 销售数据通常会进行季节性调整，以揭示潜在趋势。|
|近义词：adjusted, normalized |
|反义词：seasonal, unadjusted |
|词性：形容词|


词源:
de- (prefix): meaning "remove" or "reverse"
season (noun): referring to a period of the year
-al (suffix): forming adjectives
-ize (suffix): forming verbs meaning "to make or become"
-ed (suffix): forming past participles and adjectives
