paradisial:/ˌpærəˈdɪʒɪəl/ /ˌpærəˈdaɪʒəl/| adj.|天堂般的，极乐的|Resembling or suggesting paradise; heavenly.|例句：The island boasts paradisial beaches.  这个岛屿拥有天堂般的海滩。|例句：The hotel had a paradisial garden.  这家酒店有一个极乐的花园。|近义词：heavenly, blissful, idyllic, utopian|反义词：hellish, infernal, nightmarish|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *paradisialis, from Latin paradisus (“paradise”) + -alis (adjective suffix).  最终源自希腊语 paradeisos.
