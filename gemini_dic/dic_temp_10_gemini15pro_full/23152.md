boutiquey: /buːˈtiːki/ | adj. |精品店的，有精品店风格的|Resembling or characteristic of a boutique, especially in being fashionable, stylish, or luxurious. |  This hotel has a very boutiquey feel. 这家酒店很有精品酒店的感觉。| The dress is very boutiquey and chic. 这条裙子很有精品店的风格，很时尚。| 近义词: stylish, chic, fashionable, trendy, upscale | 反义词: mass-produced, generic, ordinary | 词性: adjective

词源:  From boutique + -y.  The suffix "-y" is added to nouns to form adjectives indicating resemblance or characteristic. Boutique itself comes from the French word "boutique," meaning "shop."
