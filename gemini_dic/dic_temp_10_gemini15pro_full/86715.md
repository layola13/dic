helming:/ˈhelmɪŋ/| v. |掌舵；领导；指挥|Being in charge of something, like a ship or a project.  It implies steering or guiding.|例句：She is helming the new marketing campaign. 她正在领导新的营销活动。

例句：He was helming the ship through the storm. 他掌舵引领船只渡过风暴。|近义词：leading, directing, managing, guiding, controlling, heading, commanding|反义词:following, obeying|词性:动词
