castellations:/ˌkæstəˈleɪʃənz/ | n. | 城垛；雉堞；城堡式装饰 |  A defensive wall with battlements and indentations. Also, decorative features resembling these. | The castle's castellations provided excellent vantage points for archers. 这座城堡的城垛为弓箭手提供了绝佳的有利位置。| synonyms: battlements, crenellations, merlons | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "smooth surface") | noun


castellation:/ˌkæstəˈleɪʃən/ | n. | (机械零件的)键槽 | A groove or recess, often in a shaft, designed to receive a key for transmitting torque.  | The castellations on the motor shaft allow for secure attachment of the pulley. 电机轴上的键槽可以牢固地连接滑轮。| synonyms: keyway, slot, groove | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "smooth shaft") | noun
