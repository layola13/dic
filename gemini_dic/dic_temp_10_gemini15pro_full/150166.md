panacean:/ˌpænəˈsiːən/ | n. | 万灵药; 灵丹妙药 |A remedy for all ills; a cure-all.|  The new drug was touted as a panacea for cancer, but it proved to be ineffective.  这种新药被吹捧为治疗癌症的灵丹妙药，但事实证明它无效。|Synonyms: cure-all, elixir, remedy, nostrum | Antonyms: placebo, toxin, poison | noun

panacean:/ˌpænəˈsiːən/ | adj. |  万能的；灵丹妙药似的| Having the properties of a panacea; able to cure all ills.| There is no panacean solution to the problem of poverty.  对于贫困问题，没有万能的解决方案。|Synonyms: universal, comprehensive, all-embracing | Antonyms: limited, specific, targeted | adjective
