mauler:/ˈmɔːlər/| n.| 殴打者；粗暴对待者 |Someone who mauls or beats another person or animal severely. |The boxer was a real mauler in the ring.  这个拳击手在拳击场上真是个狠角色。|近义词：thug, brute, assailant, attacker|反义词: protector, defender|noun

maul:/mɔːl/| v.|  1. 撕咬；伤害；2. 粗暴地对待；抨击| 1. (of an animal) wound (a person or animal) by scratching and tearing. 2. handle (someone or something) roughly. |1. The dog mauled the child's arm. 狗撕咬了孩子的手臂。

2. Critics mauled the new play. 评论家严厉批评了这部新剧。|近义词：batter, beat, thrash,  mangle (for 1); criticize, lambaste (for 2)|反义词: praise, commend (for 2); protect, defend (for 1) |verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *maulen, from Anglo-Norman mauler, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old French mehaigner (“to injure, wound”), or to Old Norse malma (“to hammer, beat”).
