houseplant:/ˈhaʊsˌplænt/| n. |室内植物，盆栽植物|A plant that is grown indoors.|例句：She has a collection of houseplants in her living room. 她在客厅里收集了很多盆栽。

例句：The houseplant thrived in the bright, sunny window. 这株室内植物在明亮的阳光充足的窗户边茁壮成长。|近义词：indoor plant, potted plant|反义词:outdoor plant|词性:noun
