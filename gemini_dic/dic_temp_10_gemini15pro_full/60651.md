electioneer:/ɪˌlekʃəˈnɪr/ | v. |  参加竞选；为…助选 | To work actively for a candidate or political party. |  The candidate electioneered tirelessly across the state.  这位候选人在全州不知疲倦地参加竞选。| campaign, canvass | abstain | verb

electioneer:/ɪˌlekʃəˈnɪr/ | n. |  竞选者；助选者 | A person who electioneers. | He's a seasoned electioneer, known for his effective strategies.  他是一位经验丰富的竞选者，以其有效的策略而闻名。 | campaigner, canvasser | non-participant | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From election + -eer (agent suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *electionem (nom. electio) "a choosing, choice, selection," from electus, past participle of eligere "to pick out, choose."
