captioning:/ˈkæpʃənɪŋ/ | n. | 字幕；加字幕；字幕制作 | the process of displaying text on a television or movie screen to provide viewers with information about the dialogue or action |  The movie had captioning in both English and Spanish. 这部电影有英语和西班牙语字幕。 |  subtitling, closed captioning |  dubbing | noun

captioning:/ˈkæpʃənɪŋ/ | v. | 加字幕 | to provide captions for a film, television program, etc. | They are captioning the live broadcast for the hearing-impaired. 他们正在为听力障碍人士提供直播字幕。| subtitle, closed caption | dub | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From caption + -ing.  "Caption" comes from the Latin "captio" meaning "a taking, seizing."  Originally, captions were headings or titles.  The meaning evolved to include descriptive text accompanying an image or video.
