enthusiastic: /ɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪk/ | adj. | 热情的，热心的 | Showing great excitement and interest. |  She was enthusiastic about the new project. 她对这个新项目充满热情。|  He gave an enthusiastic speech about the importance of education. 他热情洋溢地演讲了教育的重要性。 |  eager, ardent, keen, passionate | apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin enthusiasticus, from Greek enthousiastikos, from enthousiastes, meaning "inspired by a god," from en- "in" + theos "god."  Originally, the word implied divine inspiration or possession.
