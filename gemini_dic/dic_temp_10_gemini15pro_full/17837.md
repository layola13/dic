bedwarf:/bɪˈdwɔːrf/
verb
使相形见绌；使显得矮小
to cause to seem small or insignificant in comparison
The skyscrapers bedwarf the surrounding buildings. 这些摩天大楼使周围的建筑物相形见绌。
overshadow, eclipse, diminish
augment, amplify, enhance
v.
