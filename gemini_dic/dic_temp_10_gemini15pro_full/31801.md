checkmated:/ˈtʃɛkˌmeɪtɪd/
| adj.|将死的，被击败的|Being in a position in chess where the king is under immediate attack and cannot escape. Figuratively, it means being defeated or thwarted completely.|例句：He felt completely checkmated by his opponent's clever strategy. 他感觉被对手巧妙的策略彻底击败了。|近义词：defeated, thwarted, beaten, outmaneuvered|反义词:victorious, triumphant|词性:形容词

checkmate:/ˈtʃɛkˌmeɪt/
| n. vt.|将死，击败|In chess, a position in which a player's king is directly attacked (in check) and there is no way to remove the threat. As a verb, it means to place (an opponent's king) in checkmate.|例句：With a sly grin, she moved her knight and announced, "Checkmate!" 她狡黠地一笑，移动了她的骑士，然后宣布：“将死！”|近义词：defeat, conquer|反义词:lose|词性:名词,动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *eschec mat ("checkmate," literally "the king is dead/stuck/unable to move"), from Persian *šāh māt ("the king is helpless").
