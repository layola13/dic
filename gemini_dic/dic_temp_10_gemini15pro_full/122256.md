miterwort:/ˈmaɪtərwɜːrt/| n. | 小米草；假升麻 | A plant of the saxifrage family, typically having white or pink flowers.  It's known for its delicate, frothy appearance and is often found in damp, shady woodland areas. |  The delicate miterwort bloomed in the shade of the trees. 小米草在树荫下盛开。|  The hiker admired the patch of miterwort growing by the stream.  徒步旅行者欣赏着溪边生长的小米草。 | synonyms: bishop's cap, foamflower | antonyms: N/A | noun
