opes:/oʊps/ | int. | 哎呀；糟糕 | An exclamation of surprise or mild apology, typically used when one has made a small mistake. | Oops, I spilled my coffee. 哎呀，我洒了咖啡了。| 近义词: whoops, uh-oh | 反义词: (No direct antonym for an interjection) | 词性:感叹词

词源:  Likely a shortening of "whoops," which itself is imitative in origin.
