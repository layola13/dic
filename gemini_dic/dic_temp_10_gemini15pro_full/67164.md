ex post facto:/ˌɛks poʊst ˈfæktoʊ/ /ˌɛks pɒst ˈfæktəʊ/
adjective
事后的；溯及既往的
having retroactive effect; influencing something that has already happened.
The law is not ex post facto.  这项法律不是溯及既往的。
The ex post facto law punished actions that were legal at the time they were committed. 这项溯及既往的法律惩罚了在实施时是合法行为。
Synonyms: retroactive, retrospective, subsequent
Antonyms: prior, preceding, antecedent
词性: adjective
