emerging:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 新兴的，出现的，形成的 | Appearing, developing, or becoming known. |  The company is a leader in the emerging field of nanotechnology.  这家公司是纳米技术新兴领域的领导者。

Emerging markets are attracting a lot of investment. 新兴市场正在吸引大量投资。


近义词: developing, nascent, rising, burgeoning, evolving
反义词: declining, fading, disappearing, established, mature
词性: adjective


词源:  From Latin *emergere, present active infinitive of emergo (“I emerge, arise, come forth”), from ex (“out of, from”) + mergo (“to dip, plunge, immerse”).
