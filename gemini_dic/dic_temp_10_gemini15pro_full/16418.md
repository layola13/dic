bardish:/ˈbɑːrdɪʃ/ | adj. | 诗人的；吟游诗人的；像吟游诗人的；华丽的；夸张的 | Of or relating to bards; characteristic of a bard; poetic; flowery; bombastic.  | He delivered a bardish speech, full of grand pronouncements and elaborate metaphors. 他发表了一篇吟游诗人般的演讲，充满了豪言壮语和精美的比喻。 | 近义词：poetic, lyrical, rhapsodic, flowery, bombastic | 反义词：prosaic, plain, simple | 词性: 形容词

词源: From bard + -ish.  From Middle English *bardish*, from *bard* + *-ish*.  Ultimately from Proto-Celtic *bardos*.
