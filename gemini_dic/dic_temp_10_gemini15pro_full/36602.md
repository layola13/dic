colleagueships:/ˈkɒliːɡʃɪps/
n.
同事关系；同事情谊
The spirit of colleagueships is important in a workplace.
在工作场所，同事情谊的精神很重要。
My colleagueships with my coworkers are very strong.
我和同事们的关系非常牢固。
English Explanation: The relationships between colleagues; the feeling of friendship and loyalty that exists between colleagues.
Synonyms: camaraderie, fellowship, esprit de corps
Antonyms: animosity, hostility, antagonism
词性: Noun
