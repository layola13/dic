annoying:/əˈnɔɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 讨厌的，恼人的 | Causing slight irritation or annoyance. |  Her constant complaining is very annoying. 她不停地抱怨很烦人。

The mosquito buzzing in my ear was incredibly annoying.  蚊子在我耳边嗡嗡叫，真是烦死人了。


近义词:irritating, bothersome, vexing, troublesome, pestilential | 反义词:pleasant, agreeable, delightful, pleasing, soothing | 词性:adjective
