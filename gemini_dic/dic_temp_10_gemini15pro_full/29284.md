cast lots:/kæst lɒts/ (US) /kɑːst lɒts/ (UK)| v.|抽签，拈阄|The act of drawing lots to make a decision or determine a course of action, often used in ancient times for divination or dividing property.  It involves each person involved taking a marked object, and the one who draws the designated lot is chosen.|They cast lots to decide who would go first. 他们抽签决定谁先去。|近义词：draw lots, raffle|反义词: select, choose|词性: 动词短语
