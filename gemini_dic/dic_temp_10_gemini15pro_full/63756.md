epicentres:/ˈepɪsɛntəz/ (US) /ˈɛpɪsɛntəz/ (UK)| n. |震中；中心；核心|The point on the earth's surface vertically above the focus of an earthquake.  A focal point, especially one from which something originates or spreads.|例句：The city was at the epicentre of the political unrest.  这座城市是政治动乱的中心。|近义词：centre, focus, core, heart, hub|反义词: periphery, edge, margin|noun
