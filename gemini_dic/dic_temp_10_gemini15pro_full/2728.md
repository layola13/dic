adularia:/ˌædjʊˈleəriə/
noun
冰长石，透长石
A transparent to translucent variety of potassium feldspar, orthoclase, that exhibits a pearly or opalescent schiller, also called moonstone.
Adularia is a variety of orthoclase feldspar that exhibits a pearly or opalescent schiller.  冰长石是一种正长石长石，呈现珍珠状或乳白色光泽。

The museum's collection includes a beautiful piece of adularia.  博物馆的藏品中有一块美丽的冰长石。

The pendant was made of adularia, giving it a mystical glow. 这款吊坠由冰长石制成，赋予它神秘的光泽。
