depravednesses:/dɪˈpreɪvɪdnəsɪz/
|n.|堕落；邪恶；腐败|The state of being depraved; extreme wickedness or corruption.  A collection of depraved acts or qualities.|The depravednesses of the Roman emperors are well documented. 罗马皇帝的堕落行径都有详细的记载。|Synonyms: corruption, wickedness, immorality, perversion, degeneracy|Antonyms: virtue, goodness, morality, righteousness|noun

depravedness:/dɪˈpreɪvɪdnəs/
|n.|堕落；邪恶；腐败|The state of being depraved; extreme wickedness or corruption.|The depravedness of the city shocked her.  这座城市的堕落让她震惊。|Synonyms: corruption, wickedness, immorality, perversion, degeneracy|Antonyms: virtue, goodness, morality, righteousness|noun


Etymology:
From depraved + -ness. Depraved itself comes from the Late Latin *depravare, from Latin de- "from, away from" + pravus "crooked, perverse."
