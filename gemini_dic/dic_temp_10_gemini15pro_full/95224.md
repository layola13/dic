inbreed:/ˈɪnbriːd/| v. |近亲繁殖； 同系繁殖；|To breed from closely related people or animals, especially over many generations.|Excessive inbreeding has weakened the species. 过度的近亲繁殖削弱了这个物种。|近义词：interbreed, crossbreed|反义词: outbreed, outcross|verb

inbred:/ˈɪnbrɛd/| adj. |天生的； 生来的； 同系繁殖的；|Produced by inbreeding; innate.|an inbred sense of right and wrong 天生的正邪感|近义词：innate, inherent, congenital|反义词: acquired, learned|adjective
