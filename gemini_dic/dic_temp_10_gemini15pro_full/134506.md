nonlevel:/nɒnˈlɛvəl/ /nɑːnˈlɛvəl/| adj.|不水平的，不平坦的，不一致的|Not level; uneven; not of uniform height or depth.|The floor was nonlevel, causing the table to wobble. 地板不平，导致桌子摇晃。|The playing field was distinctly nonlevel, giving the home team an unfair advantage.  比赛场地明显不平坦，给了主队不公平的优势。|近义词：uneven, sloping, tilted, irregular, unbalanced|反义词: level, flat, even, horizontal, uniform|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- + level.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Level" comes from Old French "livel" and ultimately from Latin "libella," meaning "a level, a plumb line."
