gallstones:/ˈɡɔːlˌstoʊnz/ (US) /ˈɡɔːlˌstəʊnz/ (UK)
noun
胆结石
Small, hard deposits that form in the gallbladder.  These deposits are typically made of cholesterol or bilirubin.
My grandmother had to have her gallbladder removed because of gallstones. 我的祖母因为胆结石不得不切除胆囊。
Synonyms: biliary calculi
Antonyms: None (It's a physical object; doesn't have a direct opposite.)
