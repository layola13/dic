deflationists:/dɪˈfleɪʃənɪsts/
n.
通货紧缩论者，主张通货紧缩的人
People who believe in or advocate for deflation, a general decline in prices.
Deflationists argue that falling prices benefit consumers. 通货紧缩论者认为，物价下跌对消费者有利。
The deflationists predicted a period of falling prices.  通货紧缩论者预测会出现一段时间的物价下跌。
近义词:n. tight money advocates
反义词:n. inflationists, reflationists
词性:n.
