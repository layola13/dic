crystallized:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪzd/ (US) /ˈkrɪstəlaɪzd/ (UK)| adj. |结晶的；明确的；固定的|Having become fixed and definite in form or structure; having formed crystals.|The honey crystallized in the jar.  蜂蜜在罐子里结晶了。|近义词：solidified, concretized, definite, clear, formed|反义词: fluid, amorphous, vague, uncertain|词性:形容词

crystallized:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪzd/ (US) /ˈkrɪstəlaɪzd/ (UK)| v. |（使）结晶；（使）明确；（使）固定|To cause to form crystals or assume crystalline form; to make or become definite and clear.|The events of that day crystallized his determination to leave. 那一天的事件使他下定决心离开。|近义词：solidify, concretize, define, clarify, form|反义词: melt, dissolve, confuse|词性:动词
