excarnificate: /ɛksˌkɑːrnɪfɪˌkeɪt/
verb
肢解; 剥皮; 严厉批评; 痛斥
To deprive of flesh; to flay.  Figuratively, to criticize harshly or denounce severely.
The dictator excarnificated his political opponents in a televised speech.  独裁者在电视讲话中严厉批评了他的政治对手。

The butcher excarnificated the carcass with practiced ease.  屠夫熟练地将屠体剥皮。

The critic excarnificated the author's latest novel, calling it a pretentious and derivative mess.  评论家严厉批评了作者的最新小说，称其为矫揉造作、毫无创意的烂作。

synonym: flay, denude, excoriate, lambaste
antonym: praise, commend, laud
词性: verb (动词)
