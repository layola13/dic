mortgaged:/ˈmɔːrɡɪdʒd/| adj.| 已抵押的，被抵押的 |Having a mortgage; pledged as security for a loan.| The house is mortgaged to the bank. 这房子抵押给了银行。|近义词：pledged, secured|反义词:unencumbered, free and clear|词性:形容词

词源: From the Old French "mortage" meaning "dead pledge," referring to the fact that if the debt was not repaid, the property became lost ("dead") to the borrower.
