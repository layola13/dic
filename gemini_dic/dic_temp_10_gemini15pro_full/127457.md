nebulous:/ˈnɛbjʊləs/| adj.|模糊的，朦胧的，星云的|hazy, vague, indistinct; lacking definite form or limits.  Relating to a nebula.|The details of the plan remained nebulous.  这个计划的细节仍然模糊不清。|近义词：vague, hazy, unclear, ambiguous, indistinct, obscure|反义词：clear, distinct, precise, definite|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *nebulosus*, from nebula (“mist, fog, cloud, vapor”), from Proto-Indo-European *nébʰōl (“cloud”).
