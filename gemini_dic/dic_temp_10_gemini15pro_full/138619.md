nucleate:/ˈnjuːkliˌeɪt/ /ˈnuːkliˌeɪt/
|v. |使成核；(使)聚集核心；|To form a nucleus or nuclei; to gather around a central point or principle.|Dust particles can nucleate raindrops in clouds. |近义词：form, generate, cluster, coalesce|反义词: disperse, dissolve, scatter|verb
nucleate:/ˈnjuːkliət/ /ˈnuːkliˌɪt/
|adj. |有核的；成核的|Having a nucleus or nuclei.|The nucleate cell began to divide. |近义词：nuclear |反义词: anuclear|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *nucleatus*, past participle of *nucleare* "to form into a kernel," from *nucleus* "kernel, inner part."
