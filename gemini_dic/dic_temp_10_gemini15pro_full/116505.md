maternalistic:/məˌtɜːrnəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 母性的；母性主义的；像母亲的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a mother, especially in a caring and protective way; exhibiting excessive maternal protectiveness or control, often to the detriment of the recipient of the attention. |  Her maternalistic instincts kicked in when she saw the lost child. 她看到走失的孩子时，母性本能油然而生。|  The government's maternalistic policies are designed to protect families. 政府的母性政策旨在保护家庭。 | 近义词: motherly, protective, nurturing | 反义词: neglectful, uncaring | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "maternal" (of or like a mother) + "-istic" (forming adjectives denoting doctrines, systems, or characteristics).  "Maternal" ultimately derives from Latin "mater" (mother).
