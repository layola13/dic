bicarinate:/baɪˈkærɪneɪt/
adjective
双脊的，有两条龙骨的
Having two keels or ridges.  Specifically used in biology to describe structures with two prominent longitudinal ridges.
The bicarinate sternum of the bird is a key feature for flight.  这只鸟的双脊胸骨是飞行的关键特征。
The fossil showed a bicarinate skull, unlike any seen before.  这块化石显示出一个双脊的头骨，这是以前从未见过的。
近义词: ridged, keeled
反义词: smooth, flat
词性: adjective
