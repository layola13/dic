abdomen:/ˈæbdəmən/ (US), /æbˈdəʊmən/ (UK)| n. |腹部|The part of the body containing the stomach, intestines, and other organs; the belly.|She felt a sharp pain in her abdomen. 她感到腹部一阵剧痛。|近义词：belly, stomach, gut|反义词: back|词性:名词

abdominal:/æbˈdɑːmɪnl/ (US), /æbˈdɒmɪnl/ (UK)| adj. |腹部的|Relating to the abdomen.|He underwent abdominal surgery. 他接受了腹部手术。|近义词：gastric, ventral|反义词: dorsal|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "abdomen" (belly, paunch), of unknown origin.

USAGE->
Noun form (abdomen):

例句:
He suffered a blow to the abdomen. = 他腹部受到了打击。

例句:
The surgeon made an incision in the patient's abdomen. = 外科医生在病人的腹部切开了一个口子。



Adjective form (abdominal):

例句:
Abdominal pain can be a sign of appendicitis. = 腹痛可能是阑尾炎的征兆。

例句:
She was experiencing abdominal discomfort after eating. = 她吃完饭后感到腹部不适。
