nonneutrals:/ˌnɑːnˈnuːtrəlz/ | n. | 中立国以外的国家；非中立者；交战国 |  Countries or individuals not remaining neutral in a conflict, especially in wartime.  Refers to parties actively involved in a conflict or those who take a stance, as opposed to remaining uninvolved or impartial. |  The nonneutrals in the conflict were quick to mobilize their forces.  交战国迅速动员了他们的军队。|  The journalist was criticized for taking sides, effectively becoming one of the nonneutrals. 这名记者因偏袒一方而受到批评，实际上成为了非中立者之一。| Synonyms: belligerents, combatants | Antonyms: neutrals | Noun
