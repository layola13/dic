delicatest:/ˈdɛlɪkətɪst/| adj.|最精致的，最微妙的，最纤弱的|Superlative form of delicate: most delicate.  Showing or involving great skill and care.|The delicatest porcelain figures were displayed in a locked cabinet.  最精致的瓷像被陈列在一个锁着的柜子里。|近义词：finest, frailest, tenderest, subtlest|反义词：coarsest, strongest, toughest, crudest|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French delicat, from Latin delicatus (“delightful, charming, luxurious, dainty”), past participle of delicare (“to allure, entice”), from de- (“from, away”) + lacere (“to entice, ensnare”).
