outbreast:/ˌaʊtˈbrɛst/
verb
超过；胜过
to surpass or excel, especially in courage or daring.
He outbreasted all competitors in the race. 他在比赛中胜过所有对手。
She outbreasted the storm and sailed to safety. 她战胜了暴风雨，安全航行。
Synonyms: outdo, surpass, excel, exceed, outstrip, transcend
Antonyms: lag behind, fall short, be inferior to
词性: 动词
