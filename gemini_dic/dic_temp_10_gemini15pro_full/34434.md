claustrophobically:/ˌklɔːstrəˈfəʊbɪkli/| adv.|幽闭恐惧地；患幽闭恐惧症地|In a manner relating to or exhibiting claustrophobia, a fear of enclosed spaces.|She felt claustrophobically trapped in the elevator. 她感觉被困在电梯里，幽闭恐惧症发作了。|例句：The small, windowless room made him feel claustrophobically anxious.  这间没有窗户的小房间让他感到幽闭恐惧般的焦虑。

例句：She avoided the crowded subway, fearing she would react claustrophobically. 她避开了拥挤的地铁，担心自己会有幽闭恐惧的反应。|近义词：fearfully, anxiously, apprehensively|反义词:calmly, comfortably|词性:adverb
