carbonize:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪz/ | v. | 使碳化，炭化 | To convert or be converted into carbon or a carbon residue by or as if by partial burning. | The intense heat carbonized the wood. 高温使木头炭化。| synonyms: char, scorch, sear | antonyms: purify, cleanse | verb

carbonize:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪz/ | n. | 碳化物 | A product of carbonization. (This noun usage is less common) | The carbonize was brittle and black. 碳化物又脆又黑. | synonyms: charcoal, coke | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the noun form) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *carboniser*, from carbone (“carbon”) + -iser (“-ize”). Ultimately from Latin carbō (“coal, charcoal”).
