battered:/ˈbætərd/ (US), /ˈbætəd/ (UK)| adj.| 殴打的，破旧的，受损的| Damaged by repeated blows or hard usage; worn out.  Showing the effects of prolonged hardship. | The car was old and battered. 这辆车又旧又破。| 近义词：damaged, worn, beaten, bruised, shabby, dilapidated | 反义词: new, pristine, mint, undamaged | 词性: adjective

词源: From the verb "batter," which comes from Old North French "batre" meaning "to beat."
