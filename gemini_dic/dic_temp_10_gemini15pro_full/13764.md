audibility:/ˌɔːdəˈbɪləti/| n.|可听度，可闻度|The audibility of a sound depends on its intensity and frequency.  声音的可听度取决于其强度和频率。|The poor audibility of the speaker made it difficult to follow the lecture. 演讲者声音的可闻度很差，很难听懂讲座。|近义词：clarity, distinctness, loudness|反义词：inaudibility, faintness|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *audibilitas, from Latin audibilis ("that can be heard") + -ity.  源自晚期拉丁语 *audibilitas，来自拉丁语 audibilis（“可以听到的”）+ -ity。
