balayeuse:/balɛjøz/| n.|扫地机，清扫车|A machine, typically electrically powered, used for sweeping or cleaning floors.|The balayeuse made quick work of the dust and debris on the warehouse floor. 清扫车很快就把仓库地板上的灰尘和碎屑清理干净了.|Synonyms: sweeper, cleaning machine, floor cleaner|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, consider "mop" for wet cleaning)|noun
