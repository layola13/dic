ooephorectomy:/ˌoʊ.əfəˈrɛktəmi/ | n. |卵巢切除术|Surgical removal of one or both ovaries. |例句：The doctor recommended an ooephorectomy due to the presence of ovarian cysts. 医生由于卵巢囊肿的存在建议进行卵巢切除术。

例句：She underwent a bilateral ooephorectomy as a preventative measure against ovarian cancer.  她接受了双侧卵巢切除术作为预防卵巢癌的措施。|近义词：ovariectomy |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From  oo-  +  -phore  +  -ectomy

* **oo-**:  Greek combining form meaning "egg" or "ovum."
* **-phore**: Greek  *phoros*  "bearing," from  *pherein* "to bear, carry."
* **-ectomy**:  Greek  *-ektomē*, meaning "a cutting out", surgical removal.
