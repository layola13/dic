holistically:/həˈlɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 整体地；全面地 | Considering all aspects of something; in a holistic manner. | We need to approach this problem holistically, considering all the interconnected factors.  我们需要全面地处理这个问题，考虑所有相互关联的因素。 |  She treated her patients holistically, focusing not just on their physical symptoms but also their emotional and spiritual well-being. 她全面地对待她的病人，不仅关注他们的身体症状，还关注他们的情感和精神健康。| comprehensively, integratively, systematically | partially, fragmentedly | adverb

词源:
From holistic + -ly.  Holistic from Greek holos "whole"  + -ic.
