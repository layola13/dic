conscriptive:/kənˈskrɪptɪv/
adjective
征兵的；义务兵役制的
relating to or involving conscription
例句：The country has a conscriptive military service. 这个国家实行义务兵役制。

例句：Conscriptive armies are often less motivated than volunteer forces.  义务兵军队通常不如志愿军积极主动。

近义词：draft, compulsory
反义词：voluntary
词性：adjective
