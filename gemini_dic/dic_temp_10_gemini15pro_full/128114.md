nestlings:/ˈnestlɪŋz/
n.
雏鸟; 刚孵出的幼鸟
A young bird that has not yet left the nest.
The robin flew back and forth to the nest, feeding its hungry nestlings.  知更鸟在巢穴和远处来回飞翔，喂养它饥饿的雏鸟。

近义词: chicks, hatchlings
反义词: adult birds, mature birds
词性: noun
