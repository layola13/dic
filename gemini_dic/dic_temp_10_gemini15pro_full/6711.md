analogically:/ˌænəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adverb|类比地，用类推法|in a way that involves using an analogy; by using an analogy.  Making a comparison between things that have similar features, often to explain or clarify.|例句：He argued analogically, comparing the human brain to a computer. 他用类推法论证，将人脑比作电脑。|例句：Analogically speaking, the structure of an atom is like a miniature solar system.  打个比方，原子的结构就像一个微型太阳系。|近义词：comparatively, similarly, correspondingly|反义词: literally, directly|词性: adverb
ETYMOLOGY->
From analogy + -ically. Analogy comes from Late Latin analogia, from Greek analogia "proportion," from analogos "proportionate," from ana- "according to" + logos "ratio."
