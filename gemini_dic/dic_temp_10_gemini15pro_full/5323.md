allowed: /əˈlaʊd/ | adj. | 允许的，被许可的 |  Given permission to do something or have something. |  Smoking is not allowed in the restaurant.  餐厅内禁止吸烟。| permitted, authorized | forbidden, prohibited | adjective

allowed: /əˈlaʊd/ | v. | 允许，准许 | To give permission to someone to do something or have something. |  My parents allowed me to stay out late.  我父母允许我晚点回家。| permit, authorize | forbid, prohibit | verb

词源:  From Middle English *allowen, from Old French alouer (“to praise, commend, approve, allow”), from Latin allaudāre (“to praise”), from ad- (“to”) + laudāre (“to praise”).
