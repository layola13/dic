medium-sized:/ˈmiːdiəm saɪzd/| adj.|中等大小的；不大不小的|Of average or moderate size; neither large nor small.  Formed by combining "medium" (meaning middle or average) and "sized" (indicating dimension).  The word "medium" ultimately derives from Latin "medius."|例句：They live in a medium-sized house in the suburbs. 他们在郊区住在一栋中等大小的房子里。

例句：The company is looking for medium-sized businesses to partner with.  这家公司正在寻找中等规模的企业进行合作。|近义词：average-sized, mid-sized, moderately sized|反义词: large, small, oversized, tiny|词性: adjective
