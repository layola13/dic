micromanager:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌmænɪdʒər/| n. |微观管理者；事无巨细都要管的人|A micromanager is someone who observes and/or controls the work of their subordinates or employees in a detailed and excessive way.|例句：My boss is a micromanager; he checks my work every hour. 我的老板是个事无巨细的管理者；他每小时都检查我的工作。

例句：She quit her job because her micromanaging boss was driving her crazy. 她辞职了，因为她那事无巨细的老板快把她逼疯了。|近义词：control freak, nitpicker, overseer|反义词: delegator, macromanager |词性:noun
