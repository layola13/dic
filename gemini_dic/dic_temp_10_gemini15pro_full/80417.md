gomorrah:/ɡəˈmɔːrə/| n.| 蛾摩拉（罪恶之城）|A city mentioned in the Book of Genesis, destroyed by God along with Sodom because of its wickedness. It is often used figuratively to refer to a place of immorality and vice.|The city became a modern Gomorrah, full of vice and corruption. 这个城市变成了现代的蛾摩拉，充满了邪恶和腐败。|近义词：Sodom, den of iniquity|反义词: utopia, paradise|名词
