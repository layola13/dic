busto:/ˈbʌstoʊ/ (US), /ˈbʌstəʊ/ (UK)| n.|半身像，胸像；失败；破产|A sculpture of a person's head, shoulders, and chest.  A complete failure; a fiasco. A raid or arrest, especially in connection with illegal drugs or gambling. |例句：The museum displayed a busto of Caesar. 博物馆展出了凯撒的半身像。

例句：The business venture turned into a complete busto. 这项商业冒险完全失败了。

近义词：bust (in the sense of failure or raid), sculpture, statue (for the art form), failure, flop, fiasco (for the sense of failure), raid (for the sense of arrest)|反义词:success (for the sense of failure)|词性:noun
