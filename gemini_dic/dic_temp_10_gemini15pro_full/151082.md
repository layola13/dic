parapterum:/ˌpærəpˈtɪərəm/| n. |側鰭; 副翼; 輔鰭|A small fleshy fin-like appendage, especially on the sides of the abdomen in certain fishes.|The parapterum of the fish helps it maintain stability in the water. 这条鱼的辅鳍帮助它在水中保持稳定。|近义词:finlet, adipose fin|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider general terms like "main fin")|词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek "para" (beside) + "pteron" (wing, fin).
