nostalgia: /nɒˈstældʒə/ (UK) /nɑːˈstældʒə/ (US)| n. |怀旧，念旧 | A sentimental longing or wistful affection for the past, typically for a period or place with happy personal associations. |  A wave of nostalgia washed over her as she looked at her high school yearbook. 当她看着高中年鉴时，一阵怀旧之情涌上心头。|  He felt a pang of nostalgia for his college days. 他对大学时光感到一阵怀旧之情。|近义词: reminiscence, wistfulness, sentimentality |反义词:  - |词性: noun

Etymology: From the Greek words *nóstos* (return home) and *álgos* (pain, grief).  Originally referred to a medical condition characterized by homesickness.
