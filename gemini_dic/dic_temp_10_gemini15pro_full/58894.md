duressor:/dʊˈrɛsər/ /djʊˈrɛsər/
n.
施压者，胁迫者
Someone who forces another person to do something against their will.
He claimed he signed the confession under duress from the police. 他声称他是在警方的胁迫下签署了供词的。
Synonyms: oppressor, coercer, intimidator, bully, browbeater
Antonyms: protector, defender, supporter
Noun
