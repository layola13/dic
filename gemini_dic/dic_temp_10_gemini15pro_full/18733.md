bergmeal:/ˈbɜːrɡmiːl/ | n. | 岩粉，硅藻土 | A white powder composed chiefly of the fossilized remains of diatoms. It is used as an absorbent, filtering aid, and abrasive. |  Farmers sometimes use bergmeal to improve soil drainage.  农民有时用岩粉来改善土壤排水。 | Synonyms: diatomaceous earth, kieselguhr | Antonyms: N/A | noun
