ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cloven, from Old English clofen, past participle of clēofan (“to cleave, split”), from Proto-Germanic *kleubaną (“to cleave”), from Proto-Indo-European *gleubʰ- (“to cleave, stick”). Cognate with Dutch kloven (“to cleave”), German klieben (“to cleave”), Old Norse kljúfa (“to cleave”).
