fierasfer:/ˌfɪəˈræsfər/ | n. | 隐鱼，鮈鱼 |A small, elongated fish of the family Carapidae, typically living commensally within the body cavity of sea cucumbers or other invertebrates.| The fierasfer seeks refuge inside the sea cucumber.  隐鱼在海参体内寻求庇护。|  Synonym: pearlfish | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "predator" in a loose sense) | noun

Etymology: From Modern Latin *Fierasfer*, from Latin *fera* ("wild beast") + *asfer* ("rough, rugged")
