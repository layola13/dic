caesurae:/sɪˈzjʊəriː/ |n. |休止；停顿；间断|A pause or break in a line of poetry, typically near the middle, often marked by punctuation.  A break or interruption in the continuity of something.|例句：The caesurae in the poem creates a sense of drama.  这首诗中的休止创造了一种戏剧感。

例句：There was a long caesurae in the conversation.  谈话中有一段长时间的停顿。|近义词：pause, break, interruption, hiatus|反义词：continuation, continuity|词性: 名词

词源: From Latin caesūra (“a cutting, incision”), from caedere (“to cut”).
