glaucomas:/ɡlɔːˈkoʊməz/| n. |青光眼|A group of eye conditions that damage the optic nerve, the health of which is vital for good vision. This damage is often caused by abnormally high pressure in your eye.|Glaucomas can lead to blindness if left untreated. 青光眼如果不治疗会导致失明。|My grandmother suffers from glaucoma. 我的祖母患有青光眼。|近义词:eye disease|反义词:(None -  there isn't a direct opposite of a disease)|词性:noun
