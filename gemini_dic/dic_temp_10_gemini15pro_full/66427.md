exegesist:/ˌɛksɪˈdʒiːsɪst/
n.
圣经注释家；经文解释者
A person skilled in exegesis; an interpreter of texts, especially of the Bible.
The renowned exegesist offered a compelling interpretation of the biblical passage.  这位著名的圣经注释家对这段圣经章节提出了令人信服的解释。
近义词: interpreter, commentator, analyst, expounder
反义词: misinterpreter, distorter


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin exegesis, from Greek exēgēsis "explanation, interpretation," from exēgeisthai "to explain, interpret," from ex- "out" + hēgeisthai "to lead, guide, tell."
