expostulatory:/ɪkˈspɒstʃʊlətəri/ /ɪkˈspɑːstʃʊlətɔːri/
adjective
劝诫的；责备的；告诫的
Expressing strong disapproval or disagreement; remonstrative.
例句：Her expostulatory tone made it clear she wasn't happy with the decision. 她责备的语气表明她对这个决定很不满意。

例句：He delivered an expostulatory speech about the dangers of smoking. 他发表了一篇关于吸烟危害的劝诫性演讲。

近义词：remonstrative, reproving, admonishing, reproachful, chiding
反义词：approving, commending, laudatory, praising
词性: adjective
