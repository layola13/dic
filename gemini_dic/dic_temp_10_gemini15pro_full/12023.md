arthur:/ˈɑːrθər/ | n. |亚瑟|A traditionally masculine given name of Celtic origin, meaning "bear man" or "noble; courageous". It is associated with the legendary King Arthur of Britain.|King Arthur is a prominent figure in British folklore. 亚瑟王是英国民间传说中的杰出人物。|Arthur went to the park. 亚瑟去了公园。|近义词: (For the name itself, there are no direct synonyms, but similar-sounding names could be Archer, Arter) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
