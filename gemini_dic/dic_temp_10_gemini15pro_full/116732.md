词源:  From Middle English *maule, probably ultimately from Latin *macula (“spot, stain”), possibly influenced by Old French *mal (“bad, evil”).  (来自中古英语 *maule，可能最终来自拉丁语 *macula（“斑点，污点”），可能受到古法语 *mal（“坏，邪恶”）的影响。)
