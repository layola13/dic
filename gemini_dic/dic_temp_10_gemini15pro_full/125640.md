mumm:/mʌm/
|v.|使安静；使沉默|To make silent or quiet; to silence.|The teacher mummed the noisy students. 老师让吵闹的学生们安静下来。|Synonyms: silence, quiet, hush|Antonyms: encourage, incite|verb

mumm:/mʌm/
|interj.|（表示沉默或不情愿说话）嗯，唔|Used to express silence or unwillingness to speak.|“Are you going to the party?” “Mumm…” “你要去参加派对吗？” “嗯……”|Synonyms: hmm, uh|Antonyms: N/A|interjection
