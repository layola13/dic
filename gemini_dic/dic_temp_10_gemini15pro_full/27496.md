campered:/ˈkæmpt/ /ˈkæmpərd/| v. |露营，扎营; (尤指不舒服地) 停留，待着|To live or stay in a tent or other temporary shelter, especially in a campsite. To stay somewhere temporarily, especially in a way that is inconvenient or unwelcome.| We campered by the lake for a week. 我们在湖边露营了一个星期。


The refugees were campered in a makeshift shelter. 难民们待在一个临时搭建的避难所里。|近义词：tent, bivouac, encamp|反义词: lodge, reside |词性: 动词


campered:/ˈkæmpt/ /ˈkæmpərd/| adj. |夸张的，做作的 (主要用于英国英语)|Exaggerated and theatrical in style, typically for humorous effect (chiefly British). |His campered performance was hilarious. 他夸张的表演滑稽极了。


She gave a very campered portrayal of the Queen. 她对女王的刻画非常做作。|近义词：affected, theatrical, exaggerated|反义词: natural, understated |词性: 形容词
