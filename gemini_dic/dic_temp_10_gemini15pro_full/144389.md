outstride:/ˌaʊtˈstraɪd/| vt.|超越，步幅超过；胜过|To surpass or exceed, especially in striding; to walk with longer steps than.|She easily outstrode her competitors in the race. 她轻松地在比赛中超越了她的竞争对手。|Synonyms: outpace, outrun, surpass, excel|Antonyms: lag behind, trail, fall behind|verb

outstride:/ˌaʊtˈstraɪd/| n.|大步；超越|A long stride; the act of outstriding.|With one outstride, he reached the finish line.  他一大步就到达了终点线。|Synonyms:  stride, advance, progress|Antonyms: retreat, regression|noun
