octant:/ˈɒktənt/ (UK) /ˈɑːktənt/ (US)| n. |八分圆; 象限仪; 八分仪|An octant is one of eight equal parts into which a space is divided by three axes at right angles. In two dimensions, it is equivalent to a quadrant. In three dimensions, it is one-eighth of a sphere.|The telescope was pointed toward the given octant of the sky. 望远镜指向天空的指定八分圆。|近义词:quadrant, sector|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'whole' in certain contexts)|noun
