incomputable:/ˌɪnkəmˈpjuːtəbl/ | adj. | 不可计算的 | Not able to be computed; too complex or undefined to be calculated by a computer or algorithm.  |  The complexity of the problem makes it incomputable with current technology.  这个问题的复杂性使得用现有技术无法计算。 | synonyms: incalculable, noncomputable, unsolvable, undecidable | antonyms: calculable, computable, solvable, decidable | adjective

incomputability:/ˌɪnkəmˌpjuːtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可计算性 | The state or quality of being incomputable. | The incomputability of certain mathematical problems has been proven.  某些数学问题的不可计算性已被证明。 | synonyms: incalculability, noncomputability, unsolvability, undecidability | antonyms: calculability, computability, solvability, decidability | noun
