out-take: /ˈaʊtteɪk/ | n. |  未采用部分；删剪镜头；花絮 | A scene or sequence, especially one from a film, that is not included in the final, edited version. | The DVD includes several amusing out-takes from the movie.  DVD 包含了电影中几个有趣的删剪镜头。 | Synonyms: deleted scene, blooper, extra | Antonyms:  included scene, final cut | noun


out-take: /ˈaʊtteɪk/ | n. |  (广播或录音的)节选；摘录 | A short extract from a longer recording or broadcast. |  The radio program played an out-take from the band's latest album.  广播节目播放了乐队最新专辑的节选。 | Synonyms: excerpt, clip, snippet | Antonyms: full version, complete recording | noun
