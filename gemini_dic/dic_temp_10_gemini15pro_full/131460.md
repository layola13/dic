noncognizance: /ˌnɒnˈkɒɡnɪzəns/ /ˌnɑːnˈkɑːɡnɪzəns/
n.
不知情；不认识；不承认
Lack of knowledge or awareness; failure to recognize or acknowledge.
The defendant pleaded noncognizance of the crime.  被告声称对犯罪行为不知情。
The board's noncognizance of the issue is alarming. 董事会对这个问题的不知情令人担忧。
近义词: ignorance, unawareness, obliviousness, unfamiliarity
反义词: awareness, knowledge, recognition, acknowledgment
词性: noun

noncognizances: /ˌnɒnˈkɒɡnɪzənsɪz/ /ˌnɑːnˈkɑːɡnɪzənsɪz/
n. (plural)
不知情；不认识；不承认（复数）
Instances of lacking knowledge or awareness; failures to recognize or acknowledge.
The judge cited multiple noncognizances as reasons for dismissal. 法官列举了多项不知情作为驳回的理由。
His repeated noncognizances of basic procedures are concerning.  他屡次不了解基本程序令人担忧。
近义词: ignorances, unawarenesses (less common)
反义词: awarenesses, recognitions, acknowledgments
词性: noun (plural)
