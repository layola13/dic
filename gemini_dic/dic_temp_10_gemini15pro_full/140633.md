olympiads: /əˈlɪmpiædz/
noun
奥林匹克运动会; 竞赛
A period of four years, reckoned from one celebration of the Olympic Games to the next.  A series of contests or competitions.
The winter olympiads are held every four years. 冬季奥林匹克运动会每四年举行一次。
The student participated in math olympiads.  这个学生参加了数学竞赛。
synonyms: games, competitions, contests, tournaments
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "recreation", "leisure")
