boast:/boʊst/ (US) /bəʊst/ (UK)| v. n.|吹嘘，自夸；拥有（值得自豪的东西）|To speak with exaggeration and excessive pride about oneself or something related to oneself; to possess something to be proud of.|例句：He boasted about his new car. 他吹嘘他的新车。|近义词：brag, crow, vaunt|反义词：deprecate, downplay|词性:动词/名词

boast:/boʊst/ (US) /bəʊst/ (UK)| n.|吹嘘，自夸；值得自豪的事物|An act of boasting; something that is a source of pride.|例句：The town boasts a beautiful beach.  这个小镇拥有一片美丽的沙滩。|近义词：pride, bragging|反义词：humility, modesty|词性:名词
