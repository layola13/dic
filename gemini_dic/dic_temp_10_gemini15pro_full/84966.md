ha'porth:/ˈheɪpəθ/ (UK) /ˈheɪpwərθ/ (US)| n.|少量，少许；极小的量|A small amount; very little.  It's a contraction of "halfpennyworth," referring to the small amount you could buy for half a penny.|It won't make a ha'porth of difference. 这不会有什么区别。|Synonyms:  smidgen, trifle, bit, tad, iota|Antonyms:  lot, abundance, plenty|noun

ha'pennyworth:/ˈheɪpəniwɜːθ/ (UK) /ˈheɪpəniwərθ/ (US)| n.|值半便士的东西；少量，少许|Something worth a halfpenny; a small amount.  This is the original, uncontracted form of "ha'porth." |She bought a ha'pennyworth of sweets. 她买了一点糖果。|Synonyms:  smidgen, trifle, bit, tad, iota|Antonyms:  lot, abundance, plenty|noun
