overhaughtily: /ˌoʊvərˈhɔːtɪli/
adverb
傲慢地，盛气凌人地
in an extremely haughty manner; arrogantly and disdainfully.
She dismissed his suggestion overhaughtily, barely glancing at him. 她傲慢地驳回了他的建议，几乎没有看他一眼。
He overhaughtily declared himself the winner before the votes were counted.  他傲慢地在计票前宣布自己获胜。
近义词: arrogantly, disdainfully, superciliously, contemptuously
反义词: humbly, modestly, respectfully
词性: adverb
词源:  From "over-" (excessively) + "haughtily" (from "haughty," meaning arrogantly superior and disdainful, ultimately from Old French "halt" meaning high).
