germinating:/ˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 发芽的；萌芽的；开始发展的 | Beginning to grow and put out shoots after a period of dormancy. |  The germinating seeds need warmth and moisture. 发芽的种子需要温暖和水分。| synonyms: sprouting, budding, developing, nascent | antonyms: dormant, inert, inactive | adjective

germinate:/ˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪt/ | v. | 发芽；萌芽；开始发展 | (of a seed or spore) begin to grow and put out shoots after a period of dormancy. | The beans will germinate in a few days. 这些豆子几天后就会发芽。| synonyms: sprout, bud, develop, spring up | antonyms: wither, die, decay | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin germinatus, past participle of germinare (“to sprout, bud”), from Latin germen (“bud, sprout”).
