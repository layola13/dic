outpraise:/ˌaʊtˈpreɪz/
verb
超越…的赞美；胜过…的赞美
to praise someone or something more than another person or thing
He outpraised all the other speakers at the conference. 他在会议上的发言比其他所有发言者都更受赞扬。
近义词：outdo, surpass, excel
反义词：underpraise, belittle
词性：动词
