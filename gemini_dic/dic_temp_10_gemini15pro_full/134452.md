nonitemizer:/ˌnɒnˈaɪtəmaɪzər/
n.|未逐项列出者；不逐项列举的人|a person who does not itemize deductions on their tax return.  Someone who chooses the standard deduction instead of listing individual deductible expenses.|  The nonitemizer will likely benefit more from the increased standard deduction. 未逐项列举的人可能会从增加的标准扣除额中受益更多。|  She was a nonitemizer, so the changes to the tax code didn't affect her much. 她没有逐项列举扣除额，所以税法的修改对她影响不大。|non-lister, standard deduction filer|itemizer|noun
