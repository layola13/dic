fimbria:/ˈfɪmbriə/ (plural fimbriae /ˈfɪmbriˌiː/)
|n.| 菌毛；繖毛；缘饰|A fringe of hairs or other projections, especially around the opening of the fallopian tube or the edge of a membrane.|The fimbriae of the fallopian tubes sweep the released egg into the tube.|Synonyms: fringe, cilia, appendage|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
fimbria:/ˈfɪmbriə/ (plural fimbriae /ˈfɪmbriˌiː/)
|n.| (大脑)边缘|A fringe-like projection, especially in the brain.|Damage to the fimbria can disrupt memory formation.|Synonyms: margin, edge, border|Antonyms: center, core|Noun
