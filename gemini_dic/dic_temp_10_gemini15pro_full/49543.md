demasculinizes:/diːˈmæskjʊlənaɪzɪz/
|v.|
|使失去男子气概; 剥夺男性气质|
|To deprive of masculine characteristics or qualities; make less masculine; make effeminate.|
|The media often demasculinizes men in advertisements, portraying them as sensitive and emotional.|媒体经常在广告中剥夺男性的男子气概，把他们描绘成敏感和情绪化的。

|The new school uniform policy demasculinizes boys by requiring them to wear pastel colors.|新的校服政策要求男孩穿柔和的颜色，从而剥夺了他们的男子气概。

|近义词：feminize, emasculate, soften|
|反义词：masculinize, strengthen|
|词性：verb (动词)|

Etymology:
de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + masculine + -ize (suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become")
