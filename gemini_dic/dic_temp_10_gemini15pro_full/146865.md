overinflate: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪt/ | v. | 过度充气；过分膨胀 | To inflate something beyond a safe or recommended pressure or amount. | The tires were overinflated, making the ride bumpy. 轮胎充气过度，导致行驶颠簸。|  They overinflated the importance of the issue. 他们过分夸大了这个问题的重要性。 | synonyms: overfill, pump up, inflate excessively | antonyms: deflate, underinflate | verb
