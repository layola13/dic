detractors:/dɪˈtræktərz/
n.
批评者，诋毁者
People who criticize someone or something, often unfairly.
The mayor's detractors claimed he was misusing city funds. 市长的批评者声称他滥用城市资金。

The film has its detractors, but it's still a box office hit. 这部电影有它的批评者，但它仍然是票房热门。

critics, opponents, attackers, slanderers
supporters, admirers, advocates, defenders
noun
