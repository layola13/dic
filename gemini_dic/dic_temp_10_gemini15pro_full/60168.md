efferent:/ˈɛfərənt/| adj.|传出的，输出的|Carrying away from a central organ or part, especially a nerve fiber that carries impulses away from the central nervous system.|The efferent nerve pathways carry signals from the brain to the muscles.|Synonyms: outgoing, exiting, outward|Antonyms: afferent, incoming, inward|adjective
