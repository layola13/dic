misinstructing: /ˌmɪsɪnˈstrʌktɪŋ/
verb
错误指导，误导
Giving incorrect or misleading information or instructions.
He was accused of misinstructing the jury. 他被指控误导陪审团。
misleading, misguiding, misdirecting
instructing, guiding, directing
