denotate:/ˈdiːnəʊteɪt/ /dɪˈnoʊteɪt/| v. |指示，指称；表示（字面意义）|To denote is to directly label or define something explicitly. It's the literal, dictionary definition of a word.|The word "house" denotes a building where people live. “house”这个词指的是人们居住的建筑物。|近义词：signify, represent, mean, indicate|反义词：connote|词性:动词

denotation:/ˌdiːnəʊˈteɪʃən/ /ˌdɪnoʊˈteɪʃən/| n. |指称；表示（字面意义）|Denotation is the literal or primary meaning of a word, in contrast to the feelings or ideas that the word suggests.|The denotation of "home" is a place where one lives, but its connotation can include feelings of warmth and family. “home”的字面意思是人居住的地方，但它的内涵可以包括温暖和家的感觉。|近义词：meaning, signification, definition|反义词：connotation|词性:名词
