aftest:/ˈæftɪst/| adj.|在船尾之后的；靠船尾最近的|Located closest to or behind the stern of a ship or aircraft.|The aftest cabin had the best views of the wake. 船尾最靠后的舱室可以看到最好的尾流景色。|例句：The aftest engine was the first to fail.  最靠后的发动机最先失效。

例句：The aftest section of the plane suffered the most damage. 飞机最尾部的部分损坏最严重。


近义词：rearmost, hindmost|反义词：foremost, forwardmost|词性:adjective
