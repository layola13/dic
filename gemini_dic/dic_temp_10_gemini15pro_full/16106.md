bandy:/ˈbændi/| adj. |弯曲的|Having a curve or bend, especially outwards.| He had bandy legs. 他有罗圈腿。|Synonyms: bowed, crooked, curved |Antonyms: straight | adjective

PHRASE->
bandy about (something) =  散布（通常指谣言或想法）
bandy words with (someone) =  与（某人）争吵
not bandy words = 直言不讳
bandy a word with = 和...说句话
bandy compliments = 交换恭维


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "bander" meaning "to strike a ball back and forth," ultimately from Frankish "bandon," a type of club or stick. The meaning shifted to "exchange words heatedly" by analogy to hitting a ball back and forth.
