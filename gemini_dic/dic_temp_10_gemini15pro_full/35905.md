cocounseling:/ˌkoʊˈkaʊnsəlɪŋ/
n.|共同咨询，合作咨询|A form of peer counseling where two people take turns listening to and supporting each other. It emphasizes emotional discharge and personal growth.|例句：Cocounseling helped me process my feelings about the divorce. 共同咨询帮助我处理了离婚后的感受。

例句：They meet weekly for cocounseling sessions. 他们每周都会见面进行共同咨询。|近义词：peer counseling, mutual counseling|反义词:|词性:noun
