anelectrotonus:/ˌænɪˈlɛktroʊtənəs/
noun
阳极电紧张
A localized decrease in excitability of a nerve or muscle near the anode during the passage of an electric current.
Anelectrotonus occurs near the anode during electrotherapy.  阳极电紧张在电疗过程中发生在阳极附近。
Anelectrotonus reduces the likelihood of muscle contraction. 阳极电紧张降低了肌肉收缩的可能性。
Near Antonyms: catelectrotonus
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From an- (not, without) + electro- (electricity) + -tonus (tension).
