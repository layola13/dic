congealment:/kənˈdʒiːlmənt/
n.
凝固，冻结
the process of becoming thick or solid, especially by cooling
The congealment of the blood around the wound helped stop the bleeding. 伤口周围血液的凝结有助于止血。
solidification, coagulation, thickening
melting, liquefaction
noun
