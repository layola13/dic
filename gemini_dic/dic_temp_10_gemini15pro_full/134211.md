nonindigenous:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈdɪdʒənəs/
adjective
非本地的，非土著的
Not native or original to a particular place; introduced from elsewhere.
The nonindigenous plants are crowding out the native species.  这些非本地植物正在排挤本地物种。
The island's ecosystem is threatened by nonindigenous species.  该岛的生态系统受到非本地物种的威胁。
Synonyms: foreign, alien, exotic, introduced, non-native
Antonyms: indigenous, native, endemic
