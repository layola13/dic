astylar:/əˈstaɪlər/| adj.|无柱式的，无柱的|Lacking columns or pillars, especially in classical architecture.  Characterized by the absence of a distinct style or ornamentation.|The building has an astylar facade. 这座建筑有一个无柱式的正面。|Near Synonyms: plain, unadorned, simple, austere|Antonyms: columned, pillared, ornate, decorated|adjective
