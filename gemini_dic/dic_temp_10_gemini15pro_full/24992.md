brutalisms: /ˈbruːtəlɪzəmz/
noun
野兽主义（建筑风格）;  粗野主义（艺术风格）
A style of architecture and the arts characterized by a deliberate plainness, crudity, or violence of imagery.  Often associated with concrete construction.
The National Theatre in London is a famous example of brutalisms.  伦敦国家剧院是野兽主义的著名例子。
近义词:  starkness, harshness, austerity
反义词:  ornateness, elegance, refinement
词性:  noun
