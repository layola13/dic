major-general:/ˈmeɪdʒər ˈdʒɛn(ə)rəl/| n.|少将|A military rank above brigadier general and below lieutenant general.  It is a senior field officer rank, usually responsible for a division or similar formation.|例句：The major-general addressed the troops before the battle. 少将战前对部队发表讲话。

例句：She was promoted to major-general after years of distinguished service.  经过多年的杰出服役，她晋升为少将。|近义词：two-star general|反义词:private|词性:noun
