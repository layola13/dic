my-bad:/maɪ bæd/ | interjection | 我的错；不好意思 | An informal way of acknowledging a mistake or minor wrongdoing.  It's a shortened version of "my bad," meaning "my mistake." | "Oops, I spilled the coffee. My bad!"  我不小心把咖啡洒了。我的错！ | Synonyms:  oops, sorry, my mistake, I apologize | Antonyms: N/A | Interjection

my-bad:/maɪ bæd/ | noun | 错误；失误 |  While less common, "my bad" can sometimes be used as a noun to refer to the mistake itself.  | "That was my bad, I should have been more careful." 那是我的错，我应该更小心一些。 | Synonyms: mistake, error, blunder | Antonyms: success, achievement | Noun
