interface:/ˈɪntərfeɪs/ | n. | 界面，接口，交界面 | A point where two systems, subjects, organizations, etc. meet and interact. | The user interface is very intuitive. 用户界面非常直观。 | synonym: connection, junction, link | antonym: separation, division | noun

interface:/ˈɪntərfeɪs/ | v. |  交互，连接，(使)互相作用 | To interact or communicate with or to connect. | The program interfaces with the operating system seamlessly.  这个程序与操作系统无缝连接。 | synonym: interact, connect, communicate | antonym: disconnect, separate | verb
