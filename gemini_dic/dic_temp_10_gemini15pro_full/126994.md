nasalities:/neɪˈzælɪtiz/| n.|鼻音；鼻音化|The nasalities in her speech made her sound like she had a cold. 她的讲话中带有的鼻音让她听起来像感冒了一样。|Nasality is the quality of sound produced by using the nasal passages as a resonating chamber, as in the pronunciation of the consonants /m/, /n/, and /ŋ/.|The singer's nasalities were particularly noticeable.  这位歌手的鼻音尤为明显。|近义词：nasal sounds, nasal tones|反义词:oral sounds|词性:noun
