igar:/iˈɡɑːr/ | n. |雪茄; 叶卷烟| A cigar is a roll of dried tobacco leaves that people smoke. | He lit his cigar and leaned back in his chair. 他点燃雪茄，靠在椅子上。|  例句：He enjoys a good cigar after dinner.  他喜欢在晚餐后享用一支好雪茄。| 近义词：cigarillo, stogie | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词


词源: From Spanish *cigarro*, from Mayan *siyar*.
