misintending:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɛndɪŋ/
verb
误解，曲解(某人的意图)
misinterpreting someone's intentions.
He misintending her silence as agreement. 他误解了她的沉默，以为她同意了。
近义词: misinterpreting, misunderstanding, misconstruing
反义词: understanding, comprehending, grasping
词性: verb (动词)

misintending:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɛndɪŋ/
Etymology:  From mis- (expressing wrongness or error) + intend (to have in mind as a purpose) + -ing (present participle suffix).
中文翻译: 误解，曲解(某人的意图)
英文解析:  To understand or interpret (someone's intentions) incorrectly.  It implies ascribing a wrong meaning or purpose to someone's actions or words.
例句: She misintending his offer of help as an attempt to control her. 她误解了他提供的帮助，认为他想控制她。
例句:  He apologized for misintending her comment and causing offense. 他为误解她的评论并造成冒犯而道歉。
近义词: misinterpreting, misunderstanding, misconstruing, misreading
反义词: understanding, comprehending, grasping, interpreting correctly
词性: verb (动词)
