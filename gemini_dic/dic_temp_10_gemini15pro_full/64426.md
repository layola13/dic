ergotin:/ˈɜːrɡətɪn/ | n. |麦角碱|A drug derived from ergot, a fungus that grows on rye and other grains. It is used to treat migraines and other conditions. | Ergotin was historically used to induce uterine contractions. 麦角碱过去曾用于引产。| Ergotin can cause vasoconstriction and should be used with caution. 麦角碱会导致血管收缩，应谨慎使用。|Synonyms: ergotamine, ergonovine | Antonyms: None | Noun
