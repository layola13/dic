enticingly:/ɪnˈtaɪsɪŋli/ | adv. | 诱惑地，吸引人地 | In a way that attracts or tempts someone; alluringly. | The aroma of freshly baked bread wafted enticingly from the bakery.  新鲜出炉的面包的香味从面包店里飘出来，令人垂涎欲滴。|  The travel brochure described the island enticingly. 旅游宣传册以诱人的方式描述了这个岛屿。 | 近义词: alluringly, temptingly, seductively, attractively | 反义词: repellently, unattractively, off-puttingly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From entice + -ing + -ly.  Entice comes from Old French enticier "to set on fire, incite," from Vulgar Latin *intitiare "set aflame," from Latin in- "in" + titio "firebrand."
