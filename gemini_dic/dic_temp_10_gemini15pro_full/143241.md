outdebated:/ˌaʊtdɪˈbeɪtɪd/
verb
在辩论中胜过
defeated in debate; having lost an argument.
He was completely outdebated by his opponent. 他在辩论中被对手彻底击败了。
Synonyms: outargued, bested, defeated, conquered
Antonyms: lost, defeated
词性: 动词
