acoustics: /əˈkuːstɪks/
n.|（场所的）音响效果|The qualities of a room or building that determine how well sound can be heard in it.|The acoustics in the theater are terrible. 剧院的音响效果很差。|synonyms: sound quality, resonance|antonyms: N/A|noun
