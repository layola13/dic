nonperceptibly:/ˌnɒnpərˈsɛptɪbli/ /ˌnɑːnpərˈsɛptəbli/| adv.|不易察觉地；极其细微地|In such small doses, the drug is nonperceptibly absorbed into the bloodstream.  以如此小的剂量，这种药物会不知不觉地被吸收到血液中。|例句：The days grew shorter nonperceptibly. 白天不知不觉地变短了。|近义词：imperceptibly, subtly, gradually|反义词：perceptibly, noticeably, obviously|词性:副词

Etymology:
From non- + perceptible + -ly.  Perceptible comes from Late Middle English, from late Latin perceptibilis, from Latin percept- ‘perceived’, from the verb percipere, from per- ‘thoroughly’ + capere ‘take’.
