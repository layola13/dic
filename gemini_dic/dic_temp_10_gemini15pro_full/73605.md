近义词: always, eternally, perpetually

词源:  "forevers" is a non-standard pluralization of "forever."  "Forever" itself comes from the Old English "forefre," composed of "fore" (before, for) and "fre" (ever).  The addition of "-s" to create "forevers" is a colloquial intensification, emphasizing the extended duration.
