creaser:/ˈkriːsər/| n.|折痕，压痕；起皱器|A line or mark made by folding or crushing something.|The iron left a creaser down the front of the shirt. 熨斗在衬衫前面留下了一道折痕。|近义词：wrinkle, fold, crease, line|反义词:smoothness|词性:noun

creaser:/ˈkriːsər/| n.| (美式棒球) 变化球投手|A pitcher who throws curveballs.|He was a renowned creaser, baffling batters with his unpredictable pitches. 他是一个著名的变化球投手，用他不可预测的投球迷惑着击球手。|近义词：pitcher|反义词:batter|词性:noun

词源: From the verb "crease," which comes from the Old North French "cresser" meaning "to break, crush."
