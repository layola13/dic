floss-silks:/ˈflɒs sɪlks/ | n. |  floss silk; silk floss |  A fine, lustrous silk thread composed of several filaments spun together, often used for embroidery or weaving.  It's characterized by its softness, smoothness, and sheen. | The intricate embroidery on the antique gown was crafted with floss-silks. 这件古董礼服上复杂的刺绣是用丝线绣成的。| The artisan carefully selected the floss-silks for her next project.  这位工匠仔细地为她的下一个项目挑选丝线。|Synonyms: embroidery silk, silk thread, filament silk | Antonyms:  coarse thread, rough yarn | Noun
