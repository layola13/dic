bumph:/bʌmp/| n. v.|碰撞声；撞击；宣传；推广|A dull, heavy sound, as of a blow or collision. To promote or publicize aggressively.|The car hit a pothole with a loud bumph. 汽车撞到坑洼处发出一声巨响。 The publisher bumped the new book heavily. 出版商大力宣传这本新书。|近义词：thump, bang, promote, advertise|反义词:silence, suppress|词性:名词/动词

ETYMOLOGY->
Likely imitative of the sound.  The meaning "to promote" arose in the early 20th century, perhaps from the idea of boosting something up.
