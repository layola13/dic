hydrotherapeutics:/ˌhaɪdrəʊˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪks/
noun
水疗法
The treatment of disease by the internal or external use of water.
Hydrotherapeutics involves the use of water for pain relief and treatment. 水疗法包括使用水来缓解疼痛和治疗。
The doctor prescribed hydrotherapeutics for her arthritis. 医生给她开了水疗法治疗关节炎。
synonyms:  water therapy, balneotherapy, hydropathy
antonyms:  allopathy (in the context of alternative medicine)
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From hydro- (water) + therapy (treatment)  Ultimately from Greek "hydōr" (water) and "therapeia" (healing).
