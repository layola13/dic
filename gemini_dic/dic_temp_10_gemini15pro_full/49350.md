delineation:/dɪˌlɪnɪˈeɪʃn/
n.|勾画，描绘，轮廓|The artist's delineation of the human form was masterful.  这位艺术家对人体的描绘非常精湛。|例句：The report provides a clear delineation of the company's financial position. 这份报告清晰地描绘了公司的财务状况。|近义词：description, portrayal, sketch, outline|反义词：obscuration, blurring|词性：名词

delineate:/dɪˈlɪnɪeɪt/
v.|勾画，描绘，轮廓|The lawyer delineated the terms of the contract. 律师阐明了合同条款。|例句：The map clearly delineates the boundaries of the property.  地图清晰地标出了房产的边界。|近义词：describe, portray, sketch, outline|反义词：obscure, blur|词性：动词

delineature:/dɪˈlɪnɪətʃər/
n.|勾画, 描绘, 略图, 草图|The architect's delineature of the proposed building was impressive.  建筑师对拟建建筑的草图令人印象深刻。|例句：The delineature of the landscape captured its serene beauty.  这幅风景画描绘了宁静的美丽。|近义词：delineation, sketch, outline, drawing|反义词：blurring, obscuration|词性：名词

词源: From Latin dēlineātus, past participle of dēlineāre (“to sketch, trace out”), from dē- (“down, completely”) + lineāre (“to make a line”), from līnea (“line”).
