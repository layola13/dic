overpoweringly:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊərɪŋli/| adv.|压倒性地；不可抗拒地；极其强烈地|In such a way as to completely overcome or overwhelm someone or something.|The heat was overpoweringly strong.  高温令人难以忍受。|The smell of the flowers was overpoweringly sweet. 花香浓郁得令人窒息。|近义词: overwhelmingly, irresistibly, compellingly|反义词: weakly, mildly, faintly|词性:副词|词源: From "overpowering" + "-ly". "Overpowering" comes from "overpower" (from "over-" + "power") + "-ing".
