non-dexterous: /ˌnɒnˈdɛkstrəs/ (British) /ˌnɑːnˈdɛkstrəs/ (American)| adj.|笨拙的，不灵巧的|Lacking dexterity; clumsy or awkward in movement or handling.|He was non-dexterous with tools and often dropped things. 他使用工具很笨拙，经常掉东西。|The surgery requires a dexterous hand. 这项手术需要灵巧的手。|Synonyms: clumsy, awkward, inept, maladroit, unskillful|Antonyms: dexterous, skillful, adroit, adept|adjective
