dentalium:/dɛnˈteɪliəm/
noun
象牙壳，齿贝
A tubular shell of a tusk shell, a type of marine mollusk.
Dentalium shells were once used by Native Americans as currency. 象牙壳曾被美洲原住民用作货币。
Dentalium shells are found in many oceans. 象牙壳在许多海洋中都有发现。
synonyms: tusk shell, scaphopod
antonyms: None.
