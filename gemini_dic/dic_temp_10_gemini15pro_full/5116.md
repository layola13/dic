alleghanian:/ˌæləˈɡeɪniən/ | adj. | 阿勒格尼的；阿巴拉契亚山脉的 | Relating to the Allegheny Mountains or the Alleghanian orogeny.  The Alleghanian orogeny was a major mountain-building event that formed the central and southern Appalachian Mountains. | The Alleghanian orogeny significantly impacted the geology of eastern North America. 阿勒格尼造山运动对北美东部的地理产生了重大影响。

The Alleghanian forests are known for their diverse flora and fauna. 阿勒格尼森林以其多样化的动植物群而闻名。 | 近义词: Appalachian | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective
