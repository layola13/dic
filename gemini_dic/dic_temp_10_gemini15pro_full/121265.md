ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + execute (carry out).  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-" meaning "wrongly, badly, astray."  "Execute" comes from the Latin "executus," past participle of "exequi" meaning "to follow out, carry out."
