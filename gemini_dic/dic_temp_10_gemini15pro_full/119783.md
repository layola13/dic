mignonette:/ˌmɪnjəˈnɛt/ | n. |香 mignonette; 木犀草; 辣根酱; 切碎的欧芹、刺山柑、煮鸡蛋等混合成的调味汁 |A small, fragrant, greenish-white flower. A sauce made of chopped parsley, capers, boiled eggs, etc. | The delicate mignonette scented the air.  空气中弥漫着木犀草的芬芳。She served oysters with mignonette sauce. 她上了牡蛎配辣根酱。| 近义词:sweet alyssum (for the flower), relish (for the sauce) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

mignonette:/ˌmɪnjəˈnɛt/ | adj. |娇小的; 可爱的 |Small and dainty; delicate. |She wore a mignonette ring. 她戴着一枚精致的小戒指。 | 近义词:petite, dainty, delicate | 反义词:large, coarse | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mignonette*, diminutive of *mignon* ("darling, cute, small"), from Old French. Ultimately from Germanic.
