facetiae:/fəˈsiːʃiiː/ /fəˈsiːʃiˌeɪ/
noun
滑稽的言辞；妙语；俏皮话
Witty or humorous remarks or writings, especially of a somewhat indecent or coarse kind.
He recounted the facetiae of his youth with great relish. 他津津乐道地讲述了他年轻时的趣闻轶事。
synonyms: witticisms, jests, jokes, quips, banter, badinage, repartee, drollery, humor, pleasantry
antonyms: seriousness, solemnity, gravity
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *facetiae*, plural of *facetia* "wittiness, humor," from *facetus* "witty, elegant, fine, courteous."
