nonconversantly:/ˌnɒnkɒnvəˈseɪʃənəli/ /ˌnɑːnkɑːnvərˈseɪʃənəli/
adverb
非会话地；不健谈地
Not in a conversational manner; silently or taciturnly.
She worked nonconversantly, focused intently on her task. 她默默地工作，专注于她的任务。
He nodded nonconversantly, acknowledging her presence but not engaging in conversation. 他默默地点了点头，表示注意到她的存在，但没有参与交谈。
近义词:silently, taciturnly, quietly
反义词:talkatively, loquaciously, chattily
词性:adverb
词源: non- (not) + conversant (familiar with, having frequent interchange) + -ly (adverbial suffix)
