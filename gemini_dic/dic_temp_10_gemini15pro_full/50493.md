depressurization:/diːˌprɛʃəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 减压，卸压 |The depressurization of the cabin caused the oxygen masks to drop. 机舱减压导致氧气面罩掉落。|Examples: 1. The rapid depressurization of the aircraft was terrifying. 飞机迅速减压的过程很可怕。 2.  The controlled depressurization of the chamber is part of the experiment. 对实验舱的受控减压是实验的一部分。|Synonyms: pressure reduction, decompression|Antonyms: pressurization, compression|noun

depressurize:/diːˈprɛʃəraɪz/ |v.| 使减压，卸压|To depressurize is to reduce the pressure in a sealed container, such as an aircraft cabin or a pressure cooker.  使减压是指降低密封容器（例如飞机座舱或压力锅）中的压力。|Examples: 1. They had to depressurize the cabin before opening the door. 他们在打开门之前必须给机舱减压。 2.  Don't depressurize the pressure cooker until it has cooled down. 在压力锅冷却之前不要对其减压。|Synonyms: decompress, release pressure|Antonyms: pressurize, increase pressure|verb
