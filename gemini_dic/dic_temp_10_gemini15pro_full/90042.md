hospitality:/ˌhɒspɪˈtælɪti(BrE)/ /ˌhɑːspɪˈtælɪti(AmE)/
noun
好客，殷勤，款待
the friendly and generous reception and entertainment of guests, visitors, or strangers.
例句：We were overwhelmed by the hospitality of the local people. 我们被当地人的热情好客所淹没。

例句：The hotel is renowned for its excellent hospitality. 这家酒店以其优质的服务而闻名。

近义词：welcoming, friendliness, generosity, warmth
反义词：inhospitality, hostility, coldness
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *hospitalier, from Latin hospitālis (“of a guest, hospitable”), from hospes (“guest, host”).  The -er suffix denotes an agent or one who performs an action.
