eukaryotic: /ˌjuːkæriˈɒtɪk/ /ˌjuːkɛriˈɑːtɪk/ | adj. | 真核的 | Relating to or characteristic of a eukaryote, an organism whose cells contain a nucleus and other organelles enclosed within membranes. |  Eukaryotic cells are more complex than prokaryotic cells. 真核细胞比原核细胞更复杂。 |  Most plants and animals are eukaryotic organisms.  大多数植物和动物都是真核生物。 |  Synonym: nucleated | Antonym: prokaryotic | Adjective
