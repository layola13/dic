existentialism: /ˌɛɡzɪˈstɛnʃəlɪzəm/ | n. | 存在主义 | A philosophical theory or approach that emphasizes the existence of the individual person as a free and responsible agent determining their own development through acts of the will. |  Many 20th-century artists and thinkers were influenced by existentialism.  许多20世纪的艺术家和思想家都受到存在主义的影响。| synonyms:  existential philosophy | antonyms:  essentialism, determinism | noun

existential: /ˌɛɡzɪˈstɛnʃəl/ | adj. | 存在的，存在主义的 | Relating to existence. |  He had to make some difficult existential choices. 他不得不做出一些艰难的存在选择。 | synonyms:  ontological | antonyms:  nonexistent | adjective

exist: /ɪɡˈzɪst/ | v. | 存在，生存 | To have objective reality or being. |  Dinosaurs existed millions of years ago.  恐龙存在于数百万年前。 | synonyms:  live, be, subsist | antonyms:  perish, cease | verb
