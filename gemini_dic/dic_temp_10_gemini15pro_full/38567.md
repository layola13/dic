concoct:/kənˈkɒkt/ (UK) /kənˈkɑːkt/ (US)| n. vt.|调制；捏造；编造|To create or devise something, often in a complex or elaborate way, especially a dish, drink, or story.|She concocted a delicious soup from the leftover vegetables. 她用剩下的蔬菜调制了一道美味的汤。|Synonyms: devise, contrive, formulate, invent, fabricate|Antonyms: discover, find, uncover|verb

concoct:/kənˈkɒkt/ (UK) /kənˈkɑːkt/ (US)| n. vt.|调制；捏造；编造|To make something by mixing different ingredients together, especially food or drink; To invent a story or plan, especially dishonestly.|He concocted a plausible excuse for his absence. 他为自己的缺席编造了一个貌似有理的借口。|Synonyms: prepare, mix, blend, create, invent|Antonyms: destroy, ruin, dismantle|noun
