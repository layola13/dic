entomostraca:/ɛntoʊˈmɒstrəkə/ | n. |  甲壳纲小动物;  水蚤类 | A large subclass of Crustacea, mostly of small size, including the barnacles, water fleas, and copepods. |  Entomostraca are important components of the aquatic food chain. 甲壳纲小动物是水生食物链的重要组成部分。| synonym: microcrustacean | antonym: malacostraca | noun
