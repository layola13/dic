finestiller:/ˈfaɪnˌstɪlər/
n.
精馏器；精密蒸馏器
An apparatus used for distilling liquids to a high degree of purity.
The finestiller produced a very pure alcohol.  精馏器生产出了非常纯净的酒精。
近义词: still, purifier, refining apparatus
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
