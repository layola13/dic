assassinating: /əˈsæsɪneɪtɪŋ/
Verb
暗杀;行刺
The act of murdering someone, typically a prominent person, for political or religious reasons.
He was accused of assassinating the president. 他被指控暗杀了总统。

assassinating: /əˈsæsɪneɪtɪŋ/
Verb
中伤;诋毁
To damage or destroy (someone's reputation or character).
The article assassinated his character. 这篇文章诋毁了他的人格。


Synonym: murdering, killing, slaughtering, executing, eliminating, liquidating, dispatching, slaying
Antonym: saving, rescuing, protecting, defending
