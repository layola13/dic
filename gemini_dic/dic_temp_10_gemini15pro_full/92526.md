hypovitaminosis:/ˌhaɪpoʊˌvaɪtəmɪˈnoʊsɪs/
n.
维生素缺乏症
a deficiency of one or more essential vitamins.
The doctor diagnosed her with hypovitaminosis after noticing several key symptoms. 医生注意到几个关键症状后，诊断她患有维生素缺乏症。
Severe hypovitaminosis can lead to serious health problems. 严重的维生素缺乏症会导致严重的健康问题。
近义词:avitaminosis, vitamin deficiency
反义词:hypervitaminosis
词性:名词
