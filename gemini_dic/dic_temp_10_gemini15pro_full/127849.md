nemoral:/ˈnɛmərəl/| adj.|温带落叶林的；(尤指)欧洲中部温带落叶林的|Relating to or characteristic of a temperate broadleaf forest, especially one in central Europe.|The nemoral zone is characterized by mild winters and warm summers.|近义词：temperate, deciduous|反义词:tropical, boreal|词性:形容词
词源:From Latin *nemoralis*, from nemus (“grove, wood”) +‎ -ālis.
