paracorolla:/ˌpærəˈkərɒlə/ | n. | 副花冠 | A crown of appendages or projections between the corolla and the stamens, often found in flowers like daffodils and narcissus. | The daffodil's paracorolla is a prominent feature, giving it its distinctive trumpet shape.  水仙的副花冠是一个显著的特征，赋予了它独特的喇叭形状。| Synonym: corona | Antonym: N/A | noun
