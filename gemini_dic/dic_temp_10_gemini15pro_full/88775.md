hodge-podge:/ˈhɑːdʒpɑːdʒ/ /ˈhɒdʒpɒdʒ/| n.|大杂烩；混合物；混杂的一堆|A confused mixture or collection of different things.|The room was a hodge-podge of furniture from different eras. 房间里摆放着不同年代的家具，显得杂乱无章。|Synonyms: jumble, medley, mixture, miscellany|Antonyms: order, uniformity|noun
