nonmercurial:/ˌnɒn.mɜːˈkjʊə.ri.əl/
adjective
非水银的；稳重的；冷静的
Not mercurial; not readily or capriciously changing; stable.
Her nonmercurial temperament made her a reliable and steady friend. 她沉稳的性情使她成为一个可靠而忠实的朋友。
Synonyms: stable, steady, calm, even-tempered, phlegmatic, imperturbable, unflappable
Antonyms: mercurial, volatile, erratic, fickle, capricious, temperamental
词性: adjective
