bigfooted:/ˌbɪɡˈfʊtɪd/| adj.|大脚的；大足的|Having large feet.  Relating to or characteristic of Bigfoot (a large humanlike creature believed to have been sighted in wooded areas of the US and Canada).|例句：He was a bigfooted man, leaving enormous tracks in the snow. 他是个大脚的人，在雪地上留下了巨大的脚印。

例句：The bigfooted creature disappeared into the dense forest.  那个大脚怪物消失在茂密的森林里。|近义词：large-footed|反义词：small-footed|词性：形容词
