miliolitic: /ˌmɪlioʊˈlɪtɪk/ | adj. | 粟米状的；由粟米虫化石组成的 | Composed of, or resembling, the shells of miliolids, a type of foraminifera.  | The beach sand was noticeably miliolitic, indicating a nearby reef.  这片沙滩明显是粟米状的，表明附近有珊瑚礁。 |  The geologist identified the rock as miliolitic limestone. 地质学家将这块岩石鉴定为粟米状灰岩。 | 近义词: foraminiferal | 反义词: None | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "Miliola," a genus of foraminifera, + "-itic," a suffix meaning "pertaining to" or "composed of."  源于有孔虫的一个属“粟米虫”+后缀“-itic”，意思是“与…有关”或“由…组成”。
