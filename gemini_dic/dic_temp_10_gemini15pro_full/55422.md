distilleries:/dɪˈstɪləriz/ | n. | 酒厂；酿酒厂 | A place where alcoholic beverages, especially whisky or other spirits, are manufactured.  A distillery typically uses a process of distillation to separate and purify the alcohol. | Scotland is famous for its whisky distilleries. 苏格兰以其威士忌酒厂而闻名。

The distillery produces a variety of single malt whiskies.  这家酒厂生产各种单一麦芽威士忌。 | 近义词：breweries, wineries | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

Etymology: From the Late Latin *distillātōria, from distillātus (“distilled”) and -tōria (“place for”). Ultimately from Latin stillāre (“to drip, trickle”).
