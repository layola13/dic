paradigmatically:/ˌpærədɪɡˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 范式地，作为范例地 |  In a way that relates to or is based on a paradigm; as a model or example.  |  The word "cat" is paradigmatically related to "dog," "bird," and "fish" as they are all members of the animal kingdom.  “猫”这个词与“狗”、“鸟”和“鱼”在范式上相关，因为它们都是动物王国的成员。 | synonym: typically, exemplarily, representatively | antonym: atypically, exceptionally | word type: adverb

paradigm:/ˈpærədaɪm/ | n. | 范式，范例 | A typical example or pattern of something; a model. | The discovery of penicillin was a paradigm shift in medicine. 青霉素的发现是医学上的一个范式转变。| synonym: model, example, pattern, archetype | antonym: anomaly, exception | word type: noun
