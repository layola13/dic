by-hook-or-by-crook:/baɪ hʊk ɔːr baɪ krʊk/
idiom
千方百计; 不择手段
By any means necessary, whether legitimate or illegitimate.
He was determined to win the competition, by hook or by crook. 他决心要不择手段赢得比赛。
近义词: by any means necessary, by fair means or foul
反义词: honestly, ethically
词性 idiom
