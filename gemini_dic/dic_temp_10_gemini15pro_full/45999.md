cyberattacks:/ˈsaɪbərətæks/
n.|网络攻击|An attempt to damage or disrupt computer systems or electronic data.|The company suffered a series of cyberattacks that crippled its operations. 该公司遭受了一系列网络攻击，使其运营瘫痪。|近义词：cyber intrusions, online attacks, digital attacks, hacking|反义词:cybersecurity, cyber defense|词性:名词

词源:
"cyber-" + "attacks"
"cyber-"  prefix meaning relating to computers or computer networks. From Greek *kúbernos* (pilot, governor)
"attacks" noun, plural of attack.  From Old French *atachier* (to fasten, attach).  Originally meant a physical assault, later extended to include other forms of aggression.
