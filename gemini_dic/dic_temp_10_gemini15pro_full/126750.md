nanhan:/nɑːnˈhɑːn/ |n.| 南汉 |Nanhan was a kingdom located in southern China during the Five Dynasties and Ten Kingdoms period (907-970).  It was founded by Liu Yan and later conquered by the Song Dynasty. | The Nanhan kingdom was known for its exotic customs. 南汉王国以其奇特的习俗而闻名。| Southern Han, Five Dynasties and Ten Kingdoms period |  Song Dynasty | noun
