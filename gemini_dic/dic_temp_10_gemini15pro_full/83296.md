gynandromorphous:/ˌɡaɪnænˈdrɒməfəs/ /ˌɡaɪnænˈdrɑːmərfəs/| adj.|雌雄同体的，雌雄嵌合的|Having both male and female characteristics, especially with reference to external morphology in animals that normally show sexual dimorphism.|例句：The gynandromorphous butterfly had one wing with male coloration and the other with female coloration.  这只雌雄嵌合的蝴蝶一边翅膀呈现雄性颜色，另一边翅膀呈现雌性颜色。

例句：Scientists studied the gynandromorphous bird to understand sex determination in avian species. 科学家们研究了这只雌雄同体的鸟，以了解鸟类的性别决定机制。|近义词：hermaphroditic, intersex|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective


Etymology->
From Greek *gynē* (γυνή) "woman" + *andr-* (ἀνδρ-) "man" + *-morphos* (-μορφος) "having the form of."
