hypothetical:/ˌhaɪpəˈθɛtɪk(ə)l/
adjective
假设的；假定的
Based on an assumption or guess; used as a provisional or tentative idea to guide or direct investigation.
例句：Let's consider a hypothetical situation where we win the lottery. 让我们假设一下我们中了彩票的情况。
例句：A hypothetical question can stimulate critical thinking.  一个假设性的问题可以激发批判性思维。
近义词：theoretical, supposed, assumed, conjectural, speculative
反义词：actual, real, factual, concrete, proven
词性: adjective
