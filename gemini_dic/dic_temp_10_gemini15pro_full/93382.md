illiberally: /ɪˈlɪbərəli/
adverb
狭隘地；不宽容地；吝啬地
In a manner that is not liberal; in a narrow-minded, intolerant, or stingy way.
He acted illiberally by refusing to consider other viewpoints. 他拒绝考虑其他观点，表现得很狭隘。
The funds were distributed illiberally, leaving many in need. 资金分配得很吝啬，导致许多人生活困难。
近义词: narrowly, intolerantly, bigotedly, stingily, meanly
反义词: liberally, generously, tolerantly, open-mindedly
词性: adverb

词源:
From il- (not) + liberal + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *liberalis* (of freedom, pertaining to a free man).
