oblanceolate: /ˌɒblænˈsɪələt/ /ˌɑːblænˈsiːələt/ | adj. | 倒披针形的，倒披针状的 | Shaped like a lance reversed, broadest above the middle and tapering to the base. |  The oblanceolate leaves of the plant were a distinctive characteristic.  这种植物的倒披针形叶子是一个显著特征。| Example 2:  The botanist identified the species by its oblanceolate foliage.  植物学家通过其倒披针形的叶子识别了该物种。 |  spatulate, obovate | lanceolate | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *oblanceolatus, from ob- (against, towards) + lanceolatus (lance-shaped), from lanceola (small lance).
