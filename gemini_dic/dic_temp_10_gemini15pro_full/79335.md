glede:/ɡliːd/| n.|高兴，喜悦；（尤指猎鹰的）贪婪， voracity|A feeling of great happiness and satisfaction, especially the voracity of a hawk.|The hunter felt a glede at the sight of the magnificent stag. 猎人看到雄伟的雄鹿感到欣喜若狂。|近义词：joy, delight, elation, rapture, bliss, voracity|反义词：sorrow, grief, despair, misery|Noun


glede:/ɡliːd/| v. (obsolete, archaic) |滑翔，翱翔；使高兴，使喜悦|To glide or soar smoothly through the air; to make someone happy or joyful.|The eagle gleded effortlessly through the sky.  老鹰毫不费力地在空中翱翔。
The news of their victory gleded their hearts. 胜利的消息让他们心花怒放。|近义词：soar, glide, rejoice, delight|反义词：plummet, fall, grieve, sadden|Verb
