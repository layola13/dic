offsider:/ˈɒfˌsaɪdə(r)/ /ˈɔːfˌsaɪdər/
noun
助手，帮手；搭档；亲信
A person who assists another, especially in a subordinate capacity; a partner or close associate.
He's my offsider in this project.  他是这个项目我的助手。
synonyms: assistant, helper, partner, associate, colleague, sidekick
antonyms: opponent, rival, adversary
