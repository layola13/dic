ail:/eɪl/
verb
(使)生病，(使)不适
to cause sickness, pain, or trouble
例句：
What ails you? 你怎么了？

He looked pale and seemed to be ailing. 他脸色苍白，似乎身体不适。

近义词：trouble, bother, afflict, affect
反义词：heal, cure
词性: verb
