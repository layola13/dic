nimbostratus:/ˌnɪmboʊˈstrætəs/ /ˌnɪmbəʊˈstreɪtəs/| n. |雨层云|A dark, gray cloud layer, often continuous, that is thick enough to block out the sun and produces precipitation (usually rain or snow).  It is a principal cloud type associated with steady precipitation.|例句：The nimbostratus clouds brought a steady rain all day. 雨层云带来了一整天的持续降雨。

例句：The sky was completely covered by a thick layer of nimbostratus. 天空完全被厚厚的雨层云覆盖。|近义词：stratus, rain cloud|反义词: (No direct antonym for a cloud type.  One might consider "clear sky" as a contrasting concept.)|词性:noun


Etymology:
From Latin nimbus (“rain cloud”) + stratus (“spread out”).
