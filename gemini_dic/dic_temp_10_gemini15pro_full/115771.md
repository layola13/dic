marined:/məˈriːnd/ | v. | 腌制（在卤汁中）; 用海水浸泡 | To preserve or soak something in a marinade, typically one containing vinegar or salt, or to soak in seawater.  | The chef marined the chicken in a mixture of herbs and spices.  厨师将鸡肉浸泡在香草和香料的混合物中。

The driftwood was marined by the salty ocean waves.  这块浮木被咸咸的海浪浸泡着。 | 近义词：pickle, soak, brine | 反义词: dry | 词性: 动词
