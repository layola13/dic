hang-out:/ˈhæŋaʊt/| n.|闲逛；常去的地方|A place where someone frequently spends time; a favorite haunt. |例句：The mall is their favorite hang-out.  购物中心是他们最喜欢的去处。

例句：They spend most of their time at their hang-out near the beach. 他们大部分时间都在海滩附近的常去的地方度过。|近义词：haunt, hangout, joint, rendezvous, resort|反义词: N/A |词性:名词

hang out:/ˌhæŋ ˈaʊt/| v.|闲逛；厮混|To spend time relaxing or socializing informally. |例句：They like to hang out at the coffee shop after school.  他们喜欢放学后在咖啡店闲逛。

例句：We're just hanging out, watching movies. 我们只是在闲逛，看看电影。|近义词：chill, relax, socialize, loiter|反义词:work, study|词性:动词
