brasque:/bræsk/| n.| 炉壁内衬，捣固料，夯实| A lining of finely ground charcoal, clay, and other materials, used in a forge or furnace.| The crucible was lined with a brasque of charcoal and clay.  坩埚内衬是用木炭和粘土制成的捣固料。|Synonym: lining, refractory|Antonym: N/A| noun

brasque:/bræsk/| v.| 夯实，捣固| To line or coat (a furnace or crucible) with brasque.| They brasqued the furnace before smelting the ore.  他们在熔炼矿石之前夯实了熔炉。|Synonym: line, coat, pack|Antonym: N/A| verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *brasque*, from Old French *brasquer* "to burn, roast," of uncertain origin.
