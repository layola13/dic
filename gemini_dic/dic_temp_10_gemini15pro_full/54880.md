displeasuring:/dɪsˈpleʒərɪŋ/
adjective
令人不快的，使人不高兴的
causing displeasure; offensive.
His displeasuring behavior led to his dismissal. 他的令人不快的行为导致他被解雇。
近义词:unpleasant, annoying, irritating, offensive
反义词:pleasing, delightful, agreeable
词性:adjective

词源:
From "displeasure" + "-ing" suffix.  "Displeasure" comes from Old French "desplaisir," from "des-" (expressing negation) + "plaisir" (pleasure).  Ultimately from Latin "placere" (to please).
