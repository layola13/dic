complaisantly:/kəmˈpleɪzəntli/ | adv. | 殷勤地，讨好地，顺从地 | In a way that shows a willingness to please others or to accept what they want, often without protest. | She complaisantly agreed to his request. 她顺从地同意了他的请求。|  He complaisantly held the door open for her. 他殷勤地为她扶着门。| 近义词: obligingly, agreeably, graciously, submissively | 反义词: stubbornly, defiantly, reluctantly | 词性: 副词

complaisant:/kəmˈpleɪzənt/ | adj. | 殷勤的，讨好的，顺从的 | Showing a willingness to please others or to accept what they want, often without protest. | He was always complaisant to his superiors. 他对上司总是很殷勤。| She had a complaisant nature, always willing to help. 她性情温顺，总是乐于助人。| 近义词: obliging, agreeable, gracious, submissive | 反义词: stubborn, defiant, reluctant | 词性: 形容词

词源: From French *complaisant*, present participle of *complaire* "to please," from Latin *complacere* "to be very pleasing," from *com-* "with, together" + *placere* "to please."
