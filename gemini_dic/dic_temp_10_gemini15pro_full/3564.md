agapae:/əˈɡɑːpeɪ/
n.
爱，(尤指基督教中强调的)神圣之爱, 慈善, 博爱
A Greek word referring to unconditional love, particularly the divine love of God for humankind, or the benevolent love of humankind for God and others.
Agape is often contrasted with eros (romantic love) and philia (brotherly love).

Agape is distinct from other forms of love in its emphasis on selflessness and sacrifice.

近义词:charity, benevolence, philanthropy, unconditional love
反义词:hate, malice, animosity
