mononomial: /ˌmɑːnəˈnoʊmiəl/ | n. |单项式| A single term in algebra, consisting of a product of numbers and variables raised to non-negative integer powers. | Example: 5x²y is a monomial.  5x²y是一个单项式。| Example: 7 is a monomial (it can be thought of as 7x⁰). 7是一个单项式（它可以被认为是7x⁰）。 | Synonym: term | Antonym: polynomial | noun
