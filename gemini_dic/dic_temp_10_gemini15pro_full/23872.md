break-back:/ˈbreɪkˌbæk/| adj.| 极其费力的; 劳累至极的;  (工作) 繁重的; (问题) 棘手的;|  Extremely arduous and strenuous; demanding or requiring a great deal of effort, often physically.  Derived from the idea of work so hard it could break one's back. |The miners had a break-back job digging for coal. 矿工们挖煤的工作非常辛苦。|  back-breaking, arduous, strenuous, demanding, grueling, laborious, exhausting | easy, simple, light | adjective
break-back:/ˈbreɪkˌbæk/| n.| 极其费力的工作; 劳累至极的事情;|  An extremely arduous and strenuous task or job. |Digging ditches all day is a real break-back.  整天挖沟是一项非常累人的工作。| back-breaker, arduous task, strenuous work | easy task, simple job | noun
