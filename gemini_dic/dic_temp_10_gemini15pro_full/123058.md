monetary:/ˈmʌnɪteri/ (US), /ˈmʌnɪtri/ (UK)| adj.|货币的；金融的|Relating to money or currency.  Concerned with the supply of money. |例句：The government implemented new monetary policies to control inflation. 政府实施了新的货币政策来控制通货膨胀。

例句：The monetary value of the painting is estimated to be over a million dollars. 这幅画的货币价值估计超过一百万美元。

近义词：financial, economic, fiscal|反义词: non-monetary, barter|词性:adjective
