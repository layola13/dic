deinoceras:/ˌdaɪnoʊˈsɪərəs/
noun
恐角兽（一种已灭绝的哺乳动物）
An extinct mammal belonging to the order Dinocerata, characterized by paired horns and saber-like upper canines.
Deinoceras was a large herbivore that roamed North America during the Eocene epoch. 恐角兽是一种大型食草动物，在始新世时期漫游于北美洲。
Deinoceras fossils have been found in Wyoming and Utah. 在怀俄明州和犹他州发现了恐角兽化石。
Synonym: Uintatherium
Antonym: N/A (extinct animal, no direct opposite)
