beardless:/ˈbɪədləs/| adj.|无胡须的，没有胡子的|Without a beard.  Lacking a beard; having no beard. |He was a beardless youth when he joined the army. 他参军时还是个没有胡子的年轻人。|The beardless boy tried to look older.  这个没有胡子的男孩试图让自己看起来更老。|近义词：clean-shaven, smooth-faced|反义词:bearded, hairy|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Old English *bēardlēas (unattested), from bēard "beard" + -lēas "-less". Cognate with Dutch baardloos, German bartlos.
