ligature:/ˈlɪɡətʃər/| n.|连字；捆绑；结扎（术）；|A ligature is something that is used to tie or bind something tightly.  |The surgeon used a ligature to tie off the bleeding artery. 外科医生用一根结扎线扎住了出血的动脉。|Synonyms: bond, tie, binding,  fastener|Antonyms: untie, loosen, release|noun

ligature:/ˈlɪɡətʃər/| n.|（印刷或手写体的）连字|In typography, a ligature is a character consisting of two or more joined letters.  |The letters 'a' and 'e' are often combined into a ligature, 'æ'. 字母'a'和'e'经常组合成一个连字'æ'.|Synonyms: glyph, character, symbol|Antonyms: N/A|noun

ligature:/ˈlɪɡətʃər/| n.|（音乐）连音线|In music, a ligature is a curved line connecting two or more notes of the same pitch, indicating that they are to be played as a single sustained note. |The ligature joined the two eighth notes together. 连音线将两个八分音符连接在一起。|Synonyms: slur, tie|Antonyms: N/A|noun
