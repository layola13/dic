blossomed:/ˈblɑsəmd/ /ˈblɒsəmd/| v. |开花；兴旺发达；发展成为|To have produced flowers; to have developed or matured successfully.|The cherry trees blossomed early this year. 今年樱花开得早。|近义词：flourished, thrived, bloomed|反义词：withered, declined, faded|词性:动词

blossom:/ˈblɑsəm/ /ˈblɒsəm/| n. |花；花期；青春；茂盛时期|A flower, especially one of a fruit tree; the state or time of flowering.|The apple blossom is beautiful in spring. 春天苹果花很美。|近义词：flower, bloom|反义词：decay, decline|词性:名词


词源: From Middle English *blosme, from Old English *blōstm, *blōstmian (“blossom”), from Proto-Germanic *blōstmą (“blossom, flower”). Cognate with West Frisian bloesem (“blossom”), Dutch bloesem (“blossom”), German Blüte (“blossom, bloom”).
