antipharmic: /ˌæntiˈfɑːrmɪk/
adjective
抗药的; 解毒的
counteracting the effects of poison; antidotal.  Relating to antipharmacy, opposing the use of drugs.
Example:  The antipharmic properties of the herb were well known in traditional medicine.  这种草药的解毒特性在传统医学中是众所周知的。

Example: His antipharmic stance led him to believe that most illnesses could be cured through natural remedies. 他反对用药的立场使他相信大多数疾病可以通过自然疗法治愈。

近义词: antidotal, counterpoison, alexipharmic
反义词:  toxic, poisonous
词性: adjective
