dockage:/ˈdɑːkɪdʒ/| n.|码头费，停泊费，扣重|A charge for the use of a dock. The amount deducted from the gross weight of a shipment, as for tare. |The ship owner had to pay a hefty dockage fee.|Synonyms: wharfage, moorage, berthage|Antonyms: N/A|noun


dockage:/ˈdɑːkɪdʒ/| n.|扣除,减量|The act of docking or the amount docked.  A deduction from gross weight or from wages.|There was a dockage of 10% from his salary due to late attendance.|Synonyms: deduction, reduction, abatement|Antonyms: increase, addition|noun
