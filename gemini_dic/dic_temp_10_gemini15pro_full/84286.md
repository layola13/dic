half-training:/ˌhɑːf ˈtreɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 半培训的，未完成培训的 |  Describes something or someone that has only received part of the intended or necessary training.  It implies incompleteness in the training process. | The half-trained recruits were no match for the experienced soldiers.  这些只受过一半训练的新兵不是经验丰富的士兵的对手。

The company hired half-trained interns to save money, but it ended up costing them more in the long run.  该公司雇佣了未完成培训的实习生以节省资金，但从长远来看，这最终花费了他们更多。 | Near synonyms: partially trained, incompletely trained, undertrained | Near antonyms: fully trained, well-trained, expertly trained | adjective
