inarticulate:/ˌɪnɑːrˈtɪkjələt/ | adj. |表达不清的，不善言辞的 | Unable to express oneself clearly or fluently.  | He was so shocked that he became inarticulate. 他震惊得说不出话来。 | The inarticulate young man struggled to express his feelings.  这个不善言辞的年轻人努力表达自己的感受。 | Synonyms: speechless, tongue-tied, dumb, mute | Antonyms: articulate, eloquent, fluent | Adjective
