nisey:/ˈnaɪsi/ /ˈniːsi/| n.|二世；日裔美国人（尤指出生在美国的日本人）|A person born in the US or Canada of Japanese immigrant parents, especially one born before World War II.|例句：Many nisei served in the US military during World War II. 许多二世在二战期间在美国军队服役。

例句：Her grandparents were Japanese immigrants, and her parents were nisei. 她的祖父母是日本移民，她的父母是二世。|近义词：second-generation Japanese American, American-born Japanese|反义词: Issei (first-generation Japanese immigrant)|词性:noun
