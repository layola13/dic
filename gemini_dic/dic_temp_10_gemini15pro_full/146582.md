overgraze: /ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪz/ | v. | 过度放牧 | To graze (land) so heavily that the vegetation is damaged and the ground becomes liable to erosion. | The farmer overgrazed the land, causing significant soil erosion. 农民过度放牧，导致严重的水土流失。 | The rancher was careful not to overgraze the pasture. 牧场主小心翼翼地不让牧场过度放牧。 | 近义词: overstock | 反义词: undergraze | 词性: 动词

overgraze: /ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 过度放牧 | The practice of grazing livestock on an area of land for too long, or with too many animals, which leads to damage to the environment. | Overgrazing can lead to desertification. 过度放牧会导致荒漠化。|  The effects of overgrazing are evident in the barren landscape. 过度放牧的影响在贫瘠的土地上显而易见。| 近义词: overstocking | 反义词: sustainable grazing | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + graze.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much."  "Graze" comes from Old English *græsian, from græs "grass."
