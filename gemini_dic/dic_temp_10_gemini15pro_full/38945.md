confidentialnesses:/ˌkɑːnfɪˌdɛnʃiˈælɪsɪz/
n.
机密性，保密性
the state of being confidential; the quality of being intended to be kept secret.
例句：The confidentialnesses of the documents were paramount. 这些文件的机密性至关重要。

例句：He stressed the confidentialnesses of the negotiations. 他强调了谈判的保密性。

近义词：secrecy, confidentiality, privateness
反义词：openness, publicity

词性: 名词


词源: From confidential + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin confidere "to trust"
