bruisings:/ˈbruːzɪŋz/| n.|瘀伤，擦伤|An injury appearing as an area of discolored skin on the body, caused by a blow or impact rupturing underlying blood vessels.|She had several bruisings on her arms and legs after the fall. 她摔倒后，胳膊和腿上有几处瘀伤。|近义词：contusions, injuries, marks, blemishes|反义词：healing, recovery|词性:noun

词源: From bruise + -ing.  Ultimately from the Old French *bruisier, of Germanic origin.
