nonsententious:/ˌnɒnsenˈtenʃəs/ | adj. | 简洁的，言简意赅的 |  Not given to pompous or excessive moralizing; concise.  Expressing much in few words; pithy. |  His nonsententious style was a refreshing change from the usual academic prose.  他简洁的风格与通常的学术散文相比，令人耳目一新。 |  concise, succinct, pithy, terse, brief  |  verbose, prolix, long-winded, rambling, wordy | adjective
