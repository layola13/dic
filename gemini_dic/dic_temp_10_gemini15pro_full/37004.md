columbite:/kəˈlʌmbaɪt/ | n. | 铌铁矿 | A black mineral that is the main ore of niobium. It is a niobate of iron and manganese and occurs in granite pegmatites. | Columbite is a valuable mineral because of its niobium content. 铌铁矿因其铌含量而是一种有价值的矿物。 | Columbite is often found in association with tantalite. 铌铁矿经常与钽铁矿伴生。 | synonyms: niobite, ferrocolumbite | antonyms: None | noun
