microcrith: /ˈmaɪkroʊkrɪθ/ | n. |微克立斯（原子质量单位）| A unit of mass equal to one millionth of a crith, approximately equal to the mass of a hydrogen atom. | Example: The atomic weight of hydrogen is approximately one microcrith.  氢的原子量大约是一个微克立斯。| Example:  Chemists used to use microcriths to express atomic masses. 化学家过去常常使用微克立斯来表示原子质量。 | Synonym: atomic mass unit (amu), dalton | Antonym: N/A (unit of mass, doesn't have a direct opposite) | noun
