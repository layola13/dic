cremate:/krɪˈmeɪt/
verb
火化
To reduce (a dead body) to ashes by fire.
Her body was cremated after the funeral. 她的遗体在葬礼后被火化了。
His last wish was to be cremated. 他的遗愿是火化。
synonyms: incinerate, burn
antonyms: bury, inter

cremated:/krɪˈmeɪtɪd/
adjective
火化的
Reduced to ashes by fire.
His cremated remains were scattered at sea.  他火化后的骨灰撒在了海上。
The cremated body was placed in an urn. 火化后的遗体被放置在一个骨灰盒里。
synonyms: incinerated, burned
antonyms: buried, interred

词性: 
verb (cremate)
adjective (cremated)


词源:
From Latin "crematus," past participle of "cremare" (to burn).
