electroplaque:/ɪˈlektrəˌpleɪk/| n. |电板|A flat plate-like structure found in electric organs of some fish, such as electric eels and rays, that generates electricity.|Electric eels have thousands of electroplaques that work together to produce a powerful electric shock.|Synonym: electrocyte|Antonym: (No direct antonym)|Noun
