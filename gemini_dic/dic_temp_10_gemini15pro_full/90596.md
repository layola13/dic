hulloed:/həˈloʊd/ | v. | 呼叫，喊叫 | To shout or call out loudly, often to attract attention. |  The captain hulloed to the sailors on deck. 船长向甲板上的水手们大声喊叫。| Synonyms: shouted, yelled, called | Antonyms: whispered, murmured | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From "hullo" + "-ed." "Hullo" is an alteration of "holla," from Old French "hola," an interjection used to call attention.
