nonwarranted:/ˌnɒnˈwɒrəntɪd/ /ˌnɑːnˈwɔːrəntid/| adj.|无根据的，不必要的，未经授权的|Not justified or authorized; groundless.|例句：The criticism was completely nonwarranted.  这批评完全是无根据的。|例句：Taking such drastic action seems nonwarranted. 采取如此激烈的行动似乎没有必要。|近义词：unjustified, groundless, baseless, unauthorized|反义词:justified, warranted, necessary, authorized|词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"  来自拉丁语 non.
warrant (verb/noun): meaning "to justify" or "a justification" 来自盎格鲁法语 warant, guarant.
