nigeriens:/ˌnɪdʒəˈriːənz/ | n. |尼日尔人|People from Niger, a country in West Africa. |Many Nigeriens speak French, as it is the official language of the country. 尼日尔许多人说法语，因为它是该国的官方语言。|Nigerien citizens, people of Niger | None |Noun
Etymology: From Niger + -ien (a suffix used to form adjectives and nouns relating to places or people). Ultimately from the Tuareg name *egerew n-igerewen* meaning "black people".
