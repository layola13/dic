nonconsumption:/ˌnɒnkənˈsʌmpʃən/
n.|非消费，不消费|The act of not consuming goods or services.|The trend towards nonconsumption is growing as people become more aware of the environmental impact of their purchases.  非消费的趋势正在增长，因为人们越来越意识到他们购买行为对环境的影响。|例句：Nonconsumption of certain goods can be a form of protest.  不消费某些商品可以是一种抗议形式。|近义词：abstinence, avoidance|反义词：consumption, use|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + consumption.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "consumption" refers to the using up of a resource.
