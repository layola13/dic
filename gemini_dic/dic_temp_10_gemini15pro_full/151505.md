parroter:/ˈpærətər/ | n. | 模仿者；鹦鹉学舌者 | Someone who repeats or imitates the words or actions of another without understanding or originality. | He's just a parroter, he doesn't have any original ideas of his own. 他只是一个模仿者，没有任何自己的原创想法。 |  She parroted her mother's opinions without thinking for herself. 她不假思索地重复着她母亲的观点。 | imitator, copier, mimic | originator, innovator | noun
