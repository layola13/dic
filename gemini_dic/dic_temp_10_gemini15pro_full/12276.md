ascetic:/əˈsɛtɪk/| adj. n.|禁欲的；苦行的；禁欲主义者；苦行者|Characterized by or suggesting the practice of severe self-discipline and abstention from all forms of indulgence, typically for religious reasons.|He lived an ascetic life, devoid of all luxuries. 他过着苦行僧般的生活，没有任何奢侈品。|近义词：austere, abstemious, spartan, self-denying|反义词：hedonistic, indulgent, luxurious|adjective, noun
