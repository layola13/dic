in the wake of:/ɪn ðə weɪk əv/ | prepositional phrase | 在...之后，随着...而来 | Following something, especially as a consequence or result. |例句：In the wake of the storm, the city was in chaos.  暴风雨过后，这座城市一片混乱。

例句：Many social changes occurred in the wake of the war.  战争之后发生了许多社会变革。


近义词：following, after, subsequent to, as a result of, in the aftermath of | 反义词: preceding, before, prior to | 词性: prepositional phrase
