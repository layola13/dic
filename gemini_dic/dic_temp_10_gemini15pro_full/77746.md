gender-bending:/ˈdʒɛndərˌbɛndɪŋ/
adj.
性别颠覆的，挑战性别刻板印象的
Relating to or involving a blurring of traditional gender distinctions, often by challenging conventional notions of masculinity and femininity through clothing, behavior, or other means.
David Bowie's gender-bending style in the 1970s was groundbreaking. 大卫·鲍伊在20世纪70年代挑战性别刻板印象的风格是开创性的。
Gender-bending fashion is becoming increasingly mainstream.  挑战性别刻板印象的时尚正变得越来越主流。
近义词:androgynous, non-binary, transgender, gender-fluid, gender-nonconforming
反义词:conventional, traditional, gender-normative


词性: adjective


词源:  A compound of "gender" and "bending," where "bending" refers to the act of manipulating or distorting something. The term gained popularity in the late 20th century with the rise of gender studies and the increasing visibility of transgender and gender-nonconforming individuals.
