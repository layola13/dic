one-armed bandit:/ˈwʌn ɑːrmd ˈbændɪt/ |n.|独臂强盗（老虎机）|A slot machine; so called because it has a lever on one side (resembling an arm) and robs players of their money.|He lost all his money to the one-armed bandits in Vegas. 他在拉斯维加斯把所有的钱都输给了老虎机。|Synonyms: slot machine, fruit machine, poker machine|Antonyms: N/A|noun

one-armed bandit:/ˈwʌn ɑːrmd ˈbændɪt/ |n.|单手匪|Refers to a person who robs with only one usable arm (rare usage). This meaning is less common than the "slot machine" meaning.|The old newspaper article described the robber as a "one-armed bandit."  旧报纸的文章将强盗描述为“独臂强盗”。|Synonyms: robber, thief|Antonyms: N/A|noun
