entanglements:/ɪnˈtæŋɡlmənts/| n. |纠缠，牵连，缠绕|A complicated or compromising relationship or situation.|例句：The entanglements of the plot kept the audience guessing. 剧情的纠缠让观众不断猜测。

例句：He tried to avoid any romantic entanglements. 他试图避免任何浪漫的纠葛。|近义词：complications, involvements, intricacies|反义词:simplicity, clarity|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *entanglament, from entangler (to entangle), from en- (in) + tangler (to tangle), from Vulgar Latin *tangulare, from Latin tangere (to touch).
