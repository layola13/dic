hypaxial:/haɪˈpæksiəl/| adj.|近轴的，轴下的|Situated below the vertebral column, specifically referring to muscles or other structures.  Relating to the ventral part of the body, below the main body axis.|The hypaxial muscles are responsible for flexion of the trunk. 近轴肌负责躯干的弯曲。|Synonyms: subvertebral, ventrolateral|Antonyms: epaxial|adjective

Etymology->
From Greek *hypo* (under) + *axōn* (axis).  Meaning "below the axis".
