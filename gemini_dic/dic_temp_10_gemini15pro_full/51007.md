desist:/dɪˈzɪst/ /dɪˈsɪst/
verb
停止，终止
cease to proceed; stop.  To abstain from further action.  It implies a voluntary cessation of some activity, often in response to a request or command.
The judge ordered him to desist from contacting the witness. 法官命令他停止联系证人。
The company was ordered to desist from using the trademarked name.  这家公司被勒令停止使用这个商标名称。

synonym: cease, stop, discontinue, abstain, refrain, forbear
antonym: continue, persist, persevere, proceed
词性: 动词
