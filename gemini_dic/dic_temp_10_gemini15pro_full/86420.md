heedily:/ˈhiːdəli/| adv.|轻率地，仓促地|Acting or done with excessive speed or urgency; rashly.|He heedily jumped to a conclusion without considering all the facts. 他不假思索地得出了结论，没有考虑所有的事实。|Synonyms: rashly, impulsively, hastily, recklessly|Antonyms: cautiously, carefully, deliberately|词性: 副词
