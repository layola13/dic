aisle:/aɪl/
n.
通道，走廊
a passage between rows of seats in a building such as a church or theatre, an airplane, or between rows of shelves in a supermarket
例句：Please keep the aisle clear. 请保持通道畅通。
例句：The bride walked down the aisle.  新娘沿着通道走过来。
近义词：passage, walkway, corridor
反义词：wall, barrier
词性：名词

词源：From Old French *aile (Modern French aile "wing"), from Latin ala "wing."  Originally referred to the wings or side areas of a church.  The meaning broadened to include any passageway.
