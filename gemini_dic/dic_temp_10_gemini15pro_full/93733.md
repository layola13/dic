例句：The robbers immasked their faces with scarves. 抢劫犯用围巾遮住了脸。


近义词：mask, disguise, conceal, hide, cover | 反义词：reveal, uncover, expose | 词性：动词

Etymology:  From im- (meaning "in" or "on") + mask.  Relatively modern coinage, likely formed by analogy with words like "empower" or "imbed."
