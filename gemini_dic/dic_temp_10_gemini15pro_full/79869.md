gnarlier:/ˈnɑːrliər/ | adj. | 更粗糙的，更扭曲的，更困难的 | More gnarled, twisted, or difficult.  | The old tree's branches were gnarlier than I remembered. 这棵老树的树枝比我记忆中的更扭曲。|  The trail became gnarlier as we climbed higher. 随着我们爬得越高，小径变得越崎岖难行。 | Synonyms: knottier, rougher, tougher, more challenging | Antonyms: smoother, straighter, easier | adjective

gnarlier:/ˈnɑːrliər/ | adj. | 更棒的，更酷的 (俚语) | More awesome, cooler (slang).  |  That skateboard trick was gnarlier than anything I've ever seen! 那个滑板技巧比我见过的任何东西都更酷！ | The waves were gnarlier today, perfect for surfing. 今天的海浪更棒，非常适合冲浪。 | Synonyms: awesome, cool, rad, wicked | Antonyms: lame, boring, dull | adjective
