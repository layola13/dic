cachectic:/kəˈkɛktɪk/ | adj. | 恶病质的；极度消瘦的 |  Relating to or suffering from cachexia, a state of extreme weight loss and muscle wasting, often associated with chronic disease. | The cancer patient appeared cachectic, with sunken eyes and protruding bones.  癌症病人看起来很虚弱，眼窝深陷，骨瘦如柴。 | The cachectic child was clearly malnourished. 这个恶病质的孩子明显营养不良。 |  emaciated, wasted, gaunt, skeletal | healthy, robust, well-nourished | adjective
