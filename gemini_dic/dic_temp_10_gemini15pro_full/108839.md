leavy:/ˈliːvi/| adj.|多叶的；叶茂盛的；覆盖着树叶的|covered with leaves; leafy.  The path led through a cool, leavy glade. 小路穿过一片凉爽、枝繁叶茂的林间空地。|近义词：leafy, verdant, lush|反义词:bare, leafless|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *levī, from Old English *lēafī, from lēaf (“leaf”) +‎ -ī (“-y”). Cognate with Dutch lovig.
