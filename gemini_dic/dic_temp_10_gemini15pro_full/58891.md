durene:/ˈdjʊəriːn/ | n. |杜烯; 1,2,4,5-四甲基苯|A hydrocarbon, C6H2(CH3)4, isomeric with cymene, obtained by the action of metallic sodium upon a mixture of methyl iodide and brompseudocumene. It crystallizes in prisms melting at 79-80°C.|Durene is used in the production of certain plastics. |杜烯用于生产某些塑料。|Synonyms: 1,2,4,5-tetramethylbenzene|Antonyms: None|Noun
