monosaccharide:/ˌmɒnəˈsækəraɪd/ /ˌmɑːnəˈsækəˌraɪd/| n. |单糖|A simple sugar, such as glucose or fructose, that cannot be broken down into smaller sugar units by hydrolysis.|Glucose is a monosaccharide and an important source of energy. 葡萄糖是一种单糖，是重要的能量来源。|Synonyms: simple sugar, single sugar|Antonyms: polysaccharide|noun
