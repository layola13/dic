fire-walls:/ˈfaɪərˌwɔːlz/ (US), /ˈfaɪəwɔːlz/ (UK)| n.|防火墙|A system designed to prevent unauthorized access to or from a private network.|The company has installed new fire-walls to protect its data. 公司已经安装了新的防火墙来保护其数据。|Synonyms: barrier, safeguard, shield|Antonyms: gateway, opening|Noun
