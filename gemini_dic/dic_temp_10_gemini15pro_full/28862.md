carpetbaggery:/ˈkɑːrpɪtˌbæɡəri/ /ˈkɑːrpɪtˌbæɡəri/
n.
Carpetbagging（空降的）, 投机取巧, 外来者, 牟取暴利
Exploitation by outsiders of a region, especially one seen as politically or economically vulnerable.  The term originally referred to Northerners who went to the South after the American Civil War to profit from the Reconstruction.
例句：The reconstruction era saw a lot of carpetbaggery by opportunistic Northerners.  重建时期，许多机会主义的北方人趁机牟取暴利。

例句： Accusations of carpetbaggery flew when the new company bought up local businesses. 当新公司收购当地企业时，关于投机取巧的指责声不绝于耳。
近义词：opportunism, exploitation, profiteering
反义词：altruism, philanthropy
词性：noun
