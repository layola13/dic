fundi:/ˈfʊndiː/ (UK), /ˈfʌndiː/ (US)| n.|专家，行家|A person who is very knowledgeable and skilled in a particular area.|例句：He's a real fundi when it comes to computers. 他是真正的电脑专家。|例句：She's a fundi of 19th-century literature.  她是19世纪文学的专家。|近义词：expert, specialist, connoisseur, aficionado|反义词：amateur, novice|词性:名词
