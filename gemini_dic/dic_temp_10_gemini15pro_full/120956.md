mischannelled:/ˌmɪsˈtʃænəld/ | adj. | 误导的，错误引导的 | directed or guided wrongly, into a wrong channel |  His youthful enthusiasm was mischannelled into criminal activity.  他的年轻热情被误导到犯罪活动中。

The government's funding was mischannelled due to corruption. 政府的资金由于腐败而被错误引导。| 近义词：misdirected, misused, misapplied | 反义词：well-directed, properly used, appropriately applied | 词性：形容词
