noisiest:/ˈnɔɪziɪst/ | adj. | 最吵闹的 | Superlative form of noisy: most noisy.  It describes something that makes the most sound compared to other things in a group. | The noisiest classroom in the school was always Mrs. Smith's. 学校里最吵的教室总是史密斯老师的。

The noisiest child at the party was having a tantrum. 派对上最吵闹的孩子正在发脾气。|  近义词：loudest, rowdiest | 反义词：quietest, calmest | 词性: 形容词
