beamingly:/ˈbiːmɪŋli/| adv.|容光焕发地，喜气洋洋地|In a way that expresses great happiness or satisfaction, typically with a broad smile. |She greeted him beamingly. 她笑容满面地迎接他。|近义词：radiantly, joyfully, happily, brightly|反义词：sullenly, gloomily, morosely|词性:副词
Etymology: From beam (n.) + -ing + -ly.  Beam originally referred to a ray of light, and then extended to the happy expression on one's face.
