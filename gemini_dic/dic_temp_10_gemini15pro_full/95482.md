incivism:/ɪnˈsɪvɪzəm/
|n.|不文明行为，不守公德的行为|Lack of respect for laws, social norms, and civic duty.|The mayor condemned the acts of incivism that had taken place during the protest. 市长谴责了抗议期间发生的不文明行为。|近义词：rudeness, disrespect, discourtesy, boorishness|反义词：civility, courtesy, politeness, respect|词性:名词

Etymology:
From French *incivisme*, from *in-* (not) + *civisme* (civic spirit), ultimately from Latin *civis* (citizen).
