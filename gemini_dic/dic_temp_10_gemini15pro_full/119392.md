microeconomics: /ˌmaɪkroʊˌiːkəˈnɑːmɪks/ /ˌmaɪkroʊˌɛkəˈnɒmɪks/ | n. | 微观经济学 | The study of how households, firms, and individuals make decisions to allocate limited resources, given the prices of goods and services. | Example: Microeconomics helps us understand how pricing works in different markets.  微观经济学帮助我们理解不同市场的定价机制。

Example: He studied microeconomics in college. 他在大学学习了微观经济学。| 近义词: None (as a specific field of study) | 反义词: macroeconomics | 词性: noun
