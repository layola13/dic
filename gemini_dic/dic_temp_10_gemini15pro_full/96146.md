indenters:/ɪnˈdɛntərz/| n. |缩进器，压头|A tool or device used to make indentations, especially in metal or paper.  It can also refer to something that causes an indentation.|The mechanic used an indenter to mark the metal sheet.  机械师用压头在金属板上做标记。|synonyms:  die, stamp, punch, marker|antonyms: smoother, leveler|noun
