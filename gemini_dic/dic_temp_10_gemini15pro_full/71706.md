flavorless:/ˈfleɪvərləs/| adj.|无味的；平淡的|Lacking flavor; bland or insipid.|The soup was disappointingly flavorless. 这汤淡而无味，令人失望。|近义词：bland, insipid, tasteless, unflavored|反义词：flavorful, tasty, savory, delicious|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From flavor + -less.  "Flavor" comes from Old French "flaveur," ultimately from Latin "flare" (to blow).  "-less" is a suffix indicating lack of something.
