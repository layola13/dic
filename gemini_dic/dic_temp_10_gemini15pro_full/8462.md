antiaristocratical: /ˌæntiˌærɪstəˈkrætɪkəl/
adjective
反贵族主义的; 反对贵族政治的
Opposed to or hostile towards aristocracy or its principles; not aristocratic.
The French Revolution was a deeply antiaristocratical movement.  法国大革命是一场深刻的反贵族运动。
His antiaristocratical views made him unpopular with the upper class. 他的反贵族观点让他在上流社会不受欢迎。

近义词: anti-aristocratic, egalitarian, democratic, plebeian
反义词: aristocratic, elitist, oligarchic, noble
词性: adjective
