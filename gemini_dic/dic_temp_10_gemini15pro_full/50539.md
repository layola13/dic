depurator:/ˈdepjʊˌreɪtər/ | n. |净化器；净化者；清除剂|A device or substance that cleanses or purifies something, especially a liquid or gas.  A person or thing that removes impurities or undesirable elements.|The city installed a new depurator to improve the quality of its drinking water.  这个城市安装了一个新的净化器来改善饮用水质量。|Synonyms: purifier, cleanser, filter, cleaner |Antonyms: contaminant, pollutant |noun
