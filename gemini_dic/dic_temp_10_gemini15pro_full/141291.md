open-cast: /ˈəʊpənˌkɑːst/ (UK) /ˈoʊpənˌkæst/ (US)| adj. n.|露天开采的，露天矿|A method of mining in which the mineral or ore is extracted from the surface by removing the overlying earth and rock, rather than by tunneling underground.|The coal is extracted from a huge open-cast mine.|近义词: open-pit, strip mining|反义词: underground mining|词性: adjective, noun
opencast: /ˈəʊpənˌkɑːst/ (UK) /ˈoʊpənˌkæst/ (US)| adj. n.|露天开采的，露天矿|A method of mining in which the mineral or ore is extracted from the surface by removing the overlying earth and rock, rather than by tunneling underground.|Many environmentalists oppose opencast mining due to its impact on the landscape.|近义词: open-pit, strip mining|反义词: underground mining|词性: adjective, noun
