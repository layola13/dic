commodiously:/kəˈmoʊdiəsli/
adjective
宽敞地，舒适地
in a spacious and comfortable manner
例句：The guests were commodiously lodged in the large house. 客人们被安置在这所大房子里，十分宽敞舒适。

例句：The car was commodiously designed, with plenty of legroom for all passengers. 这辆车设计得很宽敞，所有乘客都有充足的腿部空间。

近义词：comfortably, spaciously, amply, conveniently
反义词：crampedly, tightly, inconveniently
词性: adverb

commodious:/kəˈmoʊdiəs/
adjective
宽敞的，舒适的
spacious and comfortable; roomy
例句：They live in a commodious house. 他们住在一所宽敞的房子里。

例句：The hotel rooms were commodious and well-appointed. 酒店的房间宽敞且设施齐全。

近义词：roomy, spacious, ample, comfortable
反义词：cramped, small, confined
词性: adjective


词源:
From Latin commodus ("suitable, convenient, proper, agreeable") + -ous.
