endless:/ˈɛndləs/ /ˈɛndlɪs/| adj.|无止境的，无限的，无休止的|Having or seeming to have no end; continuing for a very long time.|The endless cycle of birth and death. 生与死的无休止的循环。|Synonyms:  infinite, interminable, boundless, limitless|Antonyms: finite, limited, bounded, brief|adjective

例句：
The possibilities seemed endless.
可能性似乎无穷无尽。

They had endless arguments about money.
他们没完没了地争论金钱问题。
