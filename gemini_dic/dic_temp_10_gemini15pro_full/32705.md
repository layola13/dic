chloridic:/klɔːˈrɪdɪk/
adjective
氯化的；含氯的
Containing or derived from chlorine, especially in the form of a chloride.
The chloridic solution reacted violently.  氯化溶液剧烈反应。
chlorinated, chlorine-containing
non-chloridic
adjective
