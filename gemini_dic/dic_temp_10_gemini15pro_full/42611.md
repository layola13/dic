counterraid:/ˌkaʊntərˈreɪd/
verb
反突袭，反击
To raid in retaliation for a raid. The commandos counterraided the enemy base.  突击队员对敌军基地进行了反突袭。
They planned to counterraid the enemy camp at dawn. 他们计划在黎明时分反突袭敌营。
Synonyms: counterattack, retaliate
Antonyms: yield, surrender
词性: verb (动词)


counterraid:/ˈkaʊntərreɪd/
noun
反突袭，反击
A raid made in retaliation for another raid. The counterraid was swift and decisive.  反突袭迅速而果断。
The enemy launched a counterraid in response to our attack. 敌人发动了一次反突袭来回应我们的进攻。
Synonyms: counterattack, retaliation
Antonyms: surrender, retreat
词性: noun (名词)
