healing:/ˈhiːlɪŋ/ | n. adj.|治愈，康复; 治愈的，有疗效的|The process of becoming healthy again, especially after an injury or illness; able to heal.|
例句：The healing process can take several weeks.  康复过程可能需要几周时间。

例句：This herb has healing properties. 这种草药具有疗效。

近义词：recovery, recuperation, curative, therapeutic|反义词：injuring, harming, damaging|词性:名词, 形容词
