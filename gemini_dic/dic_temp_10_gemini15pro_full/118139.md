menshevik: /ˈmɛnʃəvɪk/ | n. |孟什维克派（俄国社会民主工党中较温和的一派）| A member of the moderate, non-revolutionary wing of the Russian Social Democratic Workers' Party, opposed to the Bolsheviks. |  The Mensheviks believed in a gradual approach to socialism. 孟什维克派相信逐步走向社会主义。

|  The split between the Bolsheviks and Mensheviks deepened over time.  布尔什维克和孟什维克之间的分裂随着时间的推移而加深。 | 近义词: moderate socialist 温和社会主义者 | 反义词: Bolshevik 布尔什维克 | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Russian меньшевик (menʹševik), literally "member of the minority", from меньше (menʹše, “less, smaller”) + -вик (-vik).  Coined after the 1903 Second Congress of the Russian Social Democratic Labour Party, where the Bolsheviks briefly held a slim majority.
