convexo:/kɔnˈvɛksoʊ/ /kənˈvɛksoʊ/ | adj. | 凸的，凸面的 | Curving outward; bulging.  Having a surface that curves outward, like the exterior of a sphere or circle. | The lens had a convexo surface, magnifying the image.  这个镜片有一个凸面，放大了图像。| synonyms: bulging, rounded, protuberant, gibbous | antonyms: concave, hollow, indented | adjective

convexo:/kɔnˈvɛksoʊ/ /kənˈvɛksoʊ/ | n. | 凸面，凸状物 | A convex shape or surface.  Something that is curved or rounded outward.  (Less common usage as a noun) | The convexo of the shield deflected the blow.  盾牌的凸面挡开了攻击。 | synonyms: bulge, prominence, protuberance | antonyms: concavity, hollow, indentation | noun
