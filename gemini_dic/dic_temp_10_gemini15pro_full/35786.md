cockatrice:/ˈkɒkətraɪs/ (UK) /ˈkɑːkətraɪs/ (US)| n. |鸡蛇兽；毒蛇|A mythical creature, often depicted as a two-legged dragon or serpent-like creature with a rooster's head, said to kill with a glance or breath.|例句：Legends say that a cockatrice can turn a person to stone with its gaze. 传说鸡蛇兽可以用它的目光把人变成石头。

例句：The cockatrice was a feared beast in medieval bestiaries.  鸡蛇兽是中世纪动物寓言集中令人恐惧的野兽。|近义词：basilisk, wyvern|反义词: N/A (mythical creature)|词性: noun
