non-committal:/ˌnɒn kəˈmɪt(ə)l/ /ˌnɑːn kəˈmɪtl/| adj.|不表态的，不承诺的，含糊其辞的|Not expressing or revealing commitment to a particular opinion or course of action.|Her response to my proposal was non-committal. 她对我的提议没有表态。|Synonyms: evasive, ambiguous, vague, indifferent, uninformative|Antonyms: committed, decisive, definite, clear, forthright|adjective

non-committal:/ˌnɒn kəˈmɪt(ə)l/ /ˌnɑːn kəˈmɪtl/| n.|不表态，不承诺|The state of not expressing or revealing commitment to a particular opinion or course of action. (Less common usage)|His non-committal made it difficult to plan ahead. 他的不表态使得难以提前计划。|Synonyms: evasiveness, ambiguity, vagueness|Antonyms: commitment, decisiveness, definiteness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + committal.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "committal" refers to the act of committing or pledging oneself to a particular course of action or opinion.  Therefore, "non-committal" literally means "not committing."
