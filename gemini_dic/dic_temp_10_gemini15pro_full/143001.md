outbeg:/ˌaʊtˈbɛɡ/ | v. |  乞求超过; 比...更会乞求 | To beg more intensely or persistently than someone else; to surpass in begging. | He outbegged the other panhandlers on the street corner. 他在街角比其他乞丐乞讨得更厉害。| The charity was outbegged by the increasing needs of the community.  这个慈善机构被日益增长的社区需求所压倒，乞求的钱不够用了。 | 近义词: outdo, surpass, exceed | 反义词:  be outdone, lose to | 词性: 动词
