hatchability:/ˌhætʃəˈbɪləti/| n.|孵化率，出雏率|The ability of eggs to hatch into live offspring.  It is usually expressed as a percentage of fertile eggs that hatch.|The hatchability of the chicken eggs was 95%.  这些鸡蛋的孵化率是95%。|Synonyms: hatching rate, incubability|Antonyms: infertility, sterility|noun
