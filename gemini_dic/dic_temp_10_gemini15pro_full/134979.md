nonnaturalistic:/ˌnɒnˌnætʃərəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
非自然主义的
Not based on or derived from nature; artificial or contrived.  Relating to a philosophical viewpoint that rejects the idea that everything can be explained in terms of natural processes.
例句：
The play's nonnaturalistic sets created a dreamlike atmosphere.
该剧非自然主义的布景营造了一种梦幻般的氛围。

The philosopher argued for a nonnaturalistic account of moral values.
这位哲学家主张对道德价值观进行非自然主义的解释。

近义词：artificial, synthetic, contrived, unreal, non-realistic
反义词：natural, naturalistic, realistic, authentic

词性
adjective
