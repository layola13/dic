ferricyanide:/ˌfɛriˈsaɪənaɪd/ | n. |铁氰化物；六氰合铁(III)酸盐 |A chemical compound containing the [Fe(CN)6]3− anion. It is a deep red salt, often used as an oxidizing agent in organic chemistry and as a component in blueprint paper. | Potassium ferricyanide is used in photography. | 近义词: hexacyanoferrate(III) | 反义词: ferrocyanide | noun
