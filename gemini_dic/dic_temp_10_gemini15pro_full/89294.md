homoeomorphism:/ˌhəʊmiːəˈmɔːfɪzəm/ /ˌhoʊmiːəˈmɔːrfɪzəm/| n. |同胚|A continuous transformation between two topological spaces that has a continuous inverse function.  A one-to-one correspondence between the points of two figures that is continuous in both directions. |The coffee cup and the doughnut are homoeomorphic. 咖啡杯和甜甜圈是同胚的。| synonym:isomorphism, topological equivalence| antonym: non-homoeomorphic |noun


homoeomorphism:/ˌhəʊmiːəˈmɔːfɪzəm/ /ˌhoʊmiːəˈmɔːrfɪzəm/| n. |同胚|A one-to-one correspondence between the points of two geometric figures such that the transformation is continuous in both directions. |The sphere and the cube are homoeomorphic. 球体和立方体是同胚的。| synonym:isomorphism, topological equivalence| antonym: non-homoeomorphic |noun
