deterrence:/dɪˈtɜːrəns/| n.|威慑；制止；挽留|The act of deterring; something that deters.  The policy of developing a military capability so strong that it will discourage attack.|The country’s nuclear deterrence was enough to prevent an invasion.| 近义词：dissuasion, prevention, intimidation|反义词:encouragement, inducement|noun
