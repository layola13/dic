bardiglio:/bɑːrˈdiːljoʊ/
n.
巴迪里奥灰大理石，巴迪里奥云灰石
A type of fine-grained, bluish-grey limestone, often with dark grey or purplish veining, quarried in Italy and used in sculpture and architecture.
This elegant bathroom features countertops made of bardiglio marble.  这个优雅的浴室配有巴迪里奥大理石台面。
Synonyms: marble, limestone
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a specific type of stone)
