limitations:/ˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃənz/
n.|限制；局限性；缺点；缺陷|Something that restricts range, scope, or capacity.|The limitations of this study are the small sample size and the short duration. 这项研究的局限在于样本量小和持续时间短。|restrictions, constraints, shortcomings, weaknesses|advantages, strengths
词性:名词(plural)

limitation:/ˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃən/
n.|限制；局限性；缺点；缺陷|Something that restricts range, scope, or capacity.|One limitation of the study was the small sample size. 这项研究的一个局限是样本量小。|restriction, constraint, shortcoming, weakness|advantage, strength
词性:名词(singular)

limit:/ˈlɪmɪt/
n.|限制；限度；界限|A point or level beyond which something does not or may not extend or pass.|There is a limit to what we can do. 我们能做的事情是有限的。|restriction, boundary, constraint|infinity, boundlessness
词性:名词

limit:/ˈlɪmɪt/
vt.|限制；限定|To restrict the extent or amount of something.|We must limit our spending. 我们必须限制我们的开支。|restrict, confine, restrain|expand, increase
词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *limitacioun*, from Old French *limitacion*, and directly from Latin *līmitātiō*, from *līmitātus*, past participle of *līmitāre* (“to limit”), from *līmes*, *līmitis* (“boundary, limit”).
