hand-me-up:/ˈhænd mi ʌp/| n.|旧衣服；施舍物；救济品|Something given to someone (usually of lower economic status) after the giver no longer needs or wants it.  It often implies the item is used or secondhand.|My younger siblings always got my hand-me-ups. 我的弟弟妹妹总是穿我的旧衣服。|近义词：hand-me-down, castoff|反义词:new clothes, brand-new items|词性:noun
