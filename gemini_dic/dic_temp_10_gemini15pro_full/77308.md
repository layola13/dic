gates:/ɡeɪts/| n.|大门，出入口；(复数)比喻关口，关卡|A large structure that closes an opening in a wall, fence, etc., and that usually consists of two parts that swing on hinges or slide.|The gates of the park were locked. 公园的大门锁上了。|近义词：entrance, doorway, exit|反义词:opening|词性:名词

gatess: This is not a standard English word.  It's likely a misspelling of "gates," the plural of "gate."
