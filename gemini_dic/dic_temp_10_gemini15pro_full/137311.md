nontactics:/nɒnˈtæktɪks/
noun
非战术，非策略
Absence of tactics; actions or methods that are not strategic or planned.
Their nontactics involved simply throwing as much money at the problem as possible. 他们根本没有策略，只是尽可能地把钱砸在这个问题上。
Near synonyms:  non-strategy, lack of planning, aimlessness, disorganization
Antonyms:  strategy, tactics, planning, organization
