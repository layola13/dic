ahistoricity:/ˌeɪhɪstəˈrɪsɪti/
n.
非历史性；脱离历史；无历史根据
Lack of historical perspective or context.  A disregard for history or historical accuracy.
The film's ahistoricity, while jarring for some, allowed for a more creative interpretation of the events.  这部电影的非历史性，虽然让一些人感到不和谐，但却允许对事件进行更具创造性的诠释。
Near synonyms: anachronism, inaccuracy, fabrication
Antonyms: historicity, accuracy, authenticity
noun
