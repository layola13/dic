missupposed:/ˌmɪsəˈpoʊzd/ | adj. | 被误认为的，被错误假设的 | Incorrectly supposed; based on a false supposition. | He missupposed her intentions, thinking she was being rude when she was actually just nervous. 他误解了她的意图，以为她很粗鲁，而实际上她只是紧张。 |  She missupposed his silence as agreement, when in fact he was deeply opposed to the plan. 她把他的沉默误认为是同意，而事实上他强烈反对这个计划。 | 近义词：misunderstood, misconstrued, misapprehended | 反义词：understood, correctly perceived | 词性: 形容词
