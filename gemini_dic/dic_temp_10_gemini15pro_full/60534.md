elaiometer:/ˌiːlaɪˈɒmɪtər/ /ˌiːleɪˈɑːmɪtər/ | n. |油脂计，油度计|An instrument for measuring the specific gravity of oils.|例句：The elaiometer is used to determine the quality of olive oil.  油脂计用于测定橄榄油的质量。

例句：The chemist used an elaiometer to analyze the oil sample. 化学家用油脂计分析油样。|近义词：oleometer, hydrometer (for oils)|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *ἔλαιον* (élaion, “olive oil”) + *-meter* (suffix forming nouns denoting measuring instruments).
