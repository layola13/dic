crinolines:/ˈkrɪnəlɪnz/| n. |衬裙；裙撑|A stiffened or hooped petticoat or underskirt worn to make a skirt stand out.|The women in the old photographs wore large crinolines under their dresses.  照片里的女人们穿着很大的衬裙。|Synonyms: petticoat, underskirt|Antonyms: - |noun

crinoline:/ˈkrɪnəlɪn/| n. |衬裙；裙撑；马尾衬布|A stiff fabric made of horsehair and cotton or linen thread, used especially for stiffening petticoats or interlinings.|Her crinoline rustled as she walked. 她走动时衬裙发出沙沙声。|Synonyms: petticoat, underskirt|Antonyms: - |noun
