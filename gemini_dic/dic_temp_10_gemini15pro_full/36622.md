collectively:/kəˈlɛktɪvli/
adv.
共同地，集体地
in a way that involves or includes a group of people or things as a whole.
The farmers collectively own the land. 农民们共同拥有这块土地。
collectively bargain 集体谈判  The workers decided to collectively bargain for better wages. 工人们决定集体谈判以争取更好的工资。

synonym: together, jointly, unitedly, combinedly, conjointly
antonym: individually, separately, singly, independently
词性: 副词
