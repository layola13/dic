authorizable:/ˈɔːθəraɪzəbl/
adj.
可授权的
Able to be authorized; that may be given formal permission.
The software requires an authorizable user account.  该软件需要一个可授权的用户帐户。
The expenditure is authorizable by the manager. 这笔支出可由经理授权。
Synonyms:  permissible, allowable, sanctionable
Antonyms:  unauthorizable, forbidden, prohibited
Word Origin:  From authorize + -able.
词性: 形容词
