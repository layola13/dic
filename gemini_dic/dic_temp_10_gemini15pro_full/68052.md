factualness:/ˈfæktʃuəlˌnɛs/
n.
事实性；真实性；符合事实
The factualness of the report is unquestionable.  这份报告的真实性毋庸置疑。
English Explanation: The quality or state of being factual; adherence to or conformity with facts; truthfulness.
例句：
The historian questioned the factualness of the account. 历史学家质疑该描述的真实性。

The factualness of his statement was verified by multiple sources. 他的陈述的真实性得到了多个来源的证实。


近义词：truthfulness, accuracy, verity, authenticity
反义词：falsity, inaccuracy, fiction, fabrication
词性: noun
