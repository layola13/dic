nonverminousnesses:/ˌnɒnvɜːrˈmɪnəsnəsɪz/
|n.| 无虫害状态；没有寄生虫的状态 |The state or quality of being free from vermin.| The meticulous cleaning ensured the nonverminousnesses of the kitchen.  细致的清洁确保了厨房没有虫害。 | 近义词: cleanliness, hygiene, sanitation | 反义词: infestation, verminousness | 词性: 名词


Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
verminous (adjective): from Old French vermine "vermin," ultimately from Latin vermis "worm"
-ness (suffix):  forms nouns expressing state or quality.
