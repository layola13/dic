antiphysicalness:/ˌæntiˈfɪzɪkəlnəs/
n.
非物质性；违反物理规律的性质
The state or quality of being contrary to the laws of physics or lacking physical properties.
His theory explored the antiphysicalness of consciousness. 他的理论探讨了意识的非物质性。
近义词：immateriality, nonphysicality, abstractness
反义词：physicality, materiality
词性：名词

Etymology:
"anti-" (prefix): against, opposite of  + "physical" (adjective): relating to the body or material things + "-ness" (suffix): forming nouns expressing state or quality.  Ultimately from Ancient Greek "anti-" (ἀντί) + "physikos" (φυσικός) + Old English "-nes."
