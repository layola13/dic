dozened:/ˈdʌzənd/
| adj. | 成打的，大量的 |  Grouped or arranged by dozens; numerous. | The baker dozened the cookies for easier packaging. 面包师把饼干按打包装，以便于包装。| 近义词：numerous, many, plentiful, abundant | 反义词：few, scarce, sparse | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From dozen + -ed.  Derived from the noun "dozen" meaning a group of twelve, with the suffix "-ed" added to form an adjective indicating that something is arranged or grouped in dozens or occurs in large quantities.
