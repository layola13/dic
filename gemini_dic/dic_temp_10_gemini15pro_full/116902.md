mayhem:/ˈmeɪhem/| n.|混乱，骚乱，浩劫|Violent or damaging disorder; chaos.|The hurricane caused mayhem in the small coastal town.  飓风在这个沿海小镇造成了浩劫。|近义词：chaos, havoc, disorder, turmoil, pandemonium|反义词：order, peace, tranquility|词性:名词

词源: From Anglo-French *mahaim, from Old French mahaignier (“to maim, wound, injure”), from a Germanic source akin to Old English -mēian, -mǣjan (“to hurt, injure, wound”).
