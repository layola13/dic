circumambient:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈæmbiənt/| adj.|周围的，环绕的|Surrounding; encompassing on all sides.  It comes from the Latin "circum" (around) and "ambire" (to go).|The circumambient air was thick with the smell of pine needles. 周围的空气中弥漫着松针的气味。|Synonyms: surrounding, encompassing, ambient, environing|Antonyms: central, interior|adjective
