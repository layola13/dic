governing:/ˈɡʌvərnɪŋ/ | adj. | 统治的，支配的，管理的 | Having control or authority over something; regulating or determining the behavior or actions of something. | The governing body of the university met to discuss the new budget. 大学管理机构开会讨论新的预算。|  The governing principles of democracy are freedom and equality. 民主政治的指导原则是自由和平等。|Synonyms: ruling, controlling, regulating, directing, managing | Antonyms:  subordinate, subservient | adjective

governing:/ˈɡʌvərniŋ/ | n. |统治，治理，管理| The act or process of governing; the exercise of authority. (This usage is less common than the adjective form.) | The governing of a country requires strong leadership.  一个国家的治理需要强有力的领导。 | The new constitution outlines the principles of governing.  新宪法概述了治理的原则。| Synonyms: rule, administration, management, control | Antonyms:  submission, obedience | noun
