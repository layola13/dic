braggart: /ˈbræɡərt/ (US), /ˈbræɡɑːt/ (UK)| n.| 自夸者，吹牛大王 | A person who boasts about achievements or possessions. | He's such a braggart, always going on about how much money he makes. 他真是个吹牛大王，总是炫耀自己赚了多少钱。|  例句：The braggart annoyed everyone with his constant boasting.  这个吹牛大王不停地吹嘘，惹恼了所有人。 | 近义词：boaster, show-off, bragger | 反义词：humble person, modest person | 词性: noun

braggart: /ˈbræɡərt/ (US), /ˈbræɡɑːt/ (UK)| adj.| 吹牛的，自夸的 | Given to boasting; boastful. | He has a braggart manner that puts people off. 他有一种吹嘘的姿态，让人反感。| 例句：She found his braggart stories tiresome. 她觉得他吹牛的故事很无聊。| 近义词：boastful, bragging | 反义词：humble, modest | 词性: adjective
