endophloeum:/ˈɛndoʊfloʊiəm/
n.
内树皮，韧皮部
The inner layer of bark in trees, lying immediately outside the cambium.
The endophloeum is responsible for transporting sugars produced during photosynthesis. 内树皮负责运输光合作用产生的糖分。
The beetle larvae burrowed through the endophloeum. 甲虫幼虫钻透了内树皮。
近义词: inner bark, bast
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider outer bark or periderm)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ἔνδον (éndon, “within”) + *φλοιός (phloiós, “bark”).
