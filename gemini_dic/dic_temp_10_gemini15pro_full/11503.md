aridity:/əˈrɪdəti/ /əˈrɪdɪti/ | n. | 干旱，干燥 |The aridity of the desert made it difficult for plants to grow. 沙漠的干旱使得植物难以生长。|例句：The prolonged aridity caused a severe drought. 长期干旱导致了严重的旱灾。

例句：The aridity of the climate made farming nearly impossible.  气候的干旱使得耕作几乎不可能。

近义词：dryness, barrenness, drought |反义词: humidity, moisture, wetness | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ariditas, from Latin aridus (“dry, parched”).
