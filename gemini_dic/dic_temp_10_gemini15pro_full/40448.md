contrastives:/kənˈtræs.tɪvz/ | n. | 对比物，对比要素 |  Elements that are used to highlight differences, especially in linguistics, by comparing and contrasting them. |  The teacher used minimal pairs as contrastives to help students distinguish between similar sounds. 老师用最小对立体来帮助学生区分相似的发音。 | synonyms: comparisons, distinctions, differentiators | antonyms: similarities, likenesses | noun

contrastive:/kənˈtræs.tɪv/ | adj. | 对比的，对照的 |  Relating to or based on contrast; highlighting differences. | Contrastive analysis can reveal the key differences between two languages. 对比分析可以揭示两种语言之间的主要差异。 | synonyms: comparative, differentiating, distinctive | antonyms: similar, alike, uniform | adjective
