nondegrees:/ˌnɒndɪˈɡriːz/
noun
非学位课程；非学位证书
Courses or certificates that are not formally recognized as academic degrees.
Many universities offer nondegrees, such as certificates in specific skills or professional development programs.  许多大学都提供非学位课程，例如特定技能证书或专业发展课程。
The community college provides a variety of nondegrees for those seeking career advancement.  这所社区大学为那些寻求职业发展的人提供了各种非学位课程。
synonyms:  non-degree programs, certifications, professional development courses
antonyms:  degrees, diplomas, academic qualifications
