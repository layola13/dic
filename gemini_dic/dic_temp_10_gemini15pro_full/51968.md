diacritic:/ˌdaɪəˈkrɪtɪk/ | adj. n.|变音符号的，变音符号|A diacritic is a mark added to a letter to indicate a different pronunciation, stress, tone, or other such modification.  |The word "résumé" uses a diacritic (the acute accent) on the first and last "e".  “résumé”这个单词在第一个和最后一个“e”上使用了变音符号（锐音符）。|Synonyms: accent, mark|Antonyms: unmarked|Noun, Adjective


diacritical:/ˌdaɪəˈkrɪtɪkl/ | adj.|变音符号的|Relating to or constituting a diacritic.  |Diacritical marks are important in many languages. 变音符号在许多语言中都很重要。|Synonyms: accentual,  tonal|Antonyms: unmarked|Adjective
