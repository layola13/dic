obstreperosity:/əbˌstrɛpəˈrɑːsəti/
n.
喧闹；吵闹；不服管教；桀骜不驯
Noisy and difficult to control.  The state of being noisy and unruly.
The obstreperosity of the children made it difficult for anyone to concentrate. 孩子们的吵闹让任何人都难以集中注意力。
Synonyms: boisterousness, clamor, rowdiness, unruliness
Antonyms: quiet, calmness, peacefulness, docility
词性: 名词


obstreperous:/əbˈstrɛpərəs/
adj.
喧闹的；吵闹的；不服管教的；桀骜不驯的
Noisy and difficult to control.
The obstreperous fans cheered wildly throughout the game. 喧闹的球迷在整场比赛中疯狂欢呼。
Synonyms: boisterous, clamorous, rowdy, unruly
Antonyms: quiet, calm, peaceful, docile
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin obstrepere ("to make a noise against, clamor against, oppose noisily"), from ob- ("against, towards") + strepere ("to make a noise").
