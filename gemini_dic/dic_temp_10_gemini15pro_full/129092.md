nitrous:/ˈnaɪtrəs/ (US) /ˈnaɪtrəs/ (UK)| adj.|氮的，含氮的；硝石的|Relating to or containing nitrogen, especially in a combined state; of or relating to nitre. |Nitrous oxide is used as an anesthetic. 一氧化二氮被用作麻醉剂。|Synonyms: nitric, nitrogenous|Antonyms: non-nitrous|adjective

nitrous oxide:/ˈnaɪtrəs ˈɒksaɪd/ (US) /ˈnaɪtrəs ˈɒksaɪd/ (UK)| n.|一氧化二氮，笑气|A colorless gas (N₂O) with a sweetish odor and taste, used as an anesthetic and analgesic and also as an aerosol propellant. |The dentist used nitrous oxide to sedate the patient. 牙医使用一氧化二氮来镇静病人。|Synonyms: laughing gas, dinitrogen monoxide|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but other anesthetic gases exist e.g., halothane)|noun
