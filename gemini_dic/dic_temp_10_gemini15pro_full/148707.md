overtone:/ˈoʊvərtoʊn/ (US) /ˈəʊvətəʊn/ (UK)| n.|泛音；暗示；弦外之音|An overtone is a musical tone that is a part of the harmonic series above a fundamental note and may be heard with it.  An overtone can also be a subtle or secondary meaning or implication.|The music had ominous overtones. 这音乐有不祥的弦外之音。|synonyms: implication, nuance, undertone, suggestion, hint, intimation, connotation|antonyms: explicit statement, direct meaning|noun

overtone:/ˈoʊvərtoʊn/ (US) /ˈəʊvətəʊn/ (UK)| adj.|泛音的|Relating to or having overtones.|The overtone singing created a rich and complex sound. 泛音演唱创造了丰富而复杂的声音。|synonyms: harmonic, resonant|antonyms: fundamental|adjective
