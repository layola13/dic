desaturate:/diːˈsætʃəreɪt/
verb
降低饱和度；使（颜色）不饱和；使（某物）失去活力
To reduce the saturation of (a color); to make (something) less vibrant.
The photographer desaturated the image to give it a vintage feel. 摄影师降低了图像的饱和度，使其具有复古感。
The long winter had desaturated the landscape of its usual vibrant greens. 漫长的冬季使景观失去了往常的鲜艳绿色。
synonyms: mute, subdue, dull, soften
antonyms: saturate, brighten, intensify
词性: 动词
