chou:/ʃuː/ | n. | 臭味；白菜；卷心菜；泡芙 | A strong unpleasant smell; a type of cabbage with a large, round head; a light, puffy pastry filled with cream or other sweet filling. |
例句：The chou from the bakery was delicious.  面包店的泡芙真好吃。

例句：There's a terrible chou in the refrigerator. 冰箱里有一股难闻的臭味。

近义词：(for smell) stink, stench, odor (for cabbage) cabbage (for pastry) cream puff, profiterole |
反义词：(for smell) fragrance, aroma |
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French "chou," ultimately from Latin "caulis" meaning "stalk" or "stem."
