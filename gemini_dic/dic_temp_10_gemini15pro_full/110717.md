linen:/ˈlɪnɪn/| n. adj.|亚麻布；亚麻制品；亚麻色|A strong cloth made from flax.|She wore a crisp white linen suit. 她穿着一套清爽的白色亚麻套装。|近义词:flax, cloth|反义词:synthetic, nylon|词性:名词, 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *līnen, from Proto-Germanic *līninam, from Proto-Indo-European *līnom (“flax”). Cognate with Old Frisian līnen, Old Saxon līni, Old High German līni, German Lein, Old Norse lín, Gothic 𐌻𐌴𐌹𐌽 (lēin).
