outtyrannize: /ˌaʊtˈtɪrənaɪz/
verb
压制；专横地超过；比...更残暴
to tyrannize more severely than another tyrant; to exceed in tyranny.
The dictator outtyrannized his predecessors, imposing even harsher restrictions on the people.  这个独裁者比他的前任更加残暴，对人民施加了更严厉的限制。
He outtyrannized all the other kings in history. 他比历史上所有其他国王都更加残暴。
近义词:  oppress, subjugate, domineer, overpower
反义词:  liberate, free, empower
词性: verb

词源: out- + tyrannize.  "Out-" prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing, combined with "tyrannize" meaning to rule oppressively.
