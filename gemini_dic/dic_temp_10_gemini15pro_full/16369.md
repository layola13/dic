barbering: /ˈbɑːrbərɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbɑːbərɪŋ/ (UK)
n.
理发；理发业
the act or trade of cutting and styling hair, especially men's hair.
He learned barbering from his father. 他从他父亲那里学了理发。
近义词: haircutting, hairdressing
反义词: None
词性: 名词
