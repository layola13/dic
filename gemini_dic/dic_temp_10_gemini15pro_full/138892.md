nutation:/njuːˈteɪʃən/ | n. |章动;点头;摆动|A periodic variation in the inclination of the axis of a rotating object.|The Earth's nutation causes a slight wobble in its axis.|近义词:wobble, oscillation, variation|反义词:stability, stillness|词性:名词

Etymology: From Latin *nūtātiō*, from *nūtāre* "to nod, sway," frequentative of *nuere* "to nod."
