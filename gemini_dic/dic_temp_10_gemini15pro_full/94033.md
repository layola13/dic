imparlance:/ɪmˈpɑːrləns/ | n. | 商议，会谈，谈判 |  A discussion between opposing sides in a dispute, especially a formal one to try to find a solution. | The warring factions began an imparlance in an attempt to reach a peaceful settlement. 交战各派开始谈判，试图达成和平解决方案。 | synonyms: negotiation, discussion, dialogue, conference, consultation | antonyms: disagreement, dispute, argument, conflict | noun

imparlance:/ɪmˈpɑːrləns/ | n. | (古)休战，停战(期) |  A truce or cessation of hostilities, especially to allow for discussion.  |  The two armies declared an imparlance to discuss terms of surrender. 两军宣布休战，讨论投降条件。 | synonyms: truce, ceasefire, armistice, respite | antonyms: war, hostility, fighting, conflict | noun
