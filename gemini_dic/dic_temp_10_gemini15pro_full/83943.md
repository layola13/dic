half-cock:/ˌhæf ˈkɑːk/
| adj. adv.| 半待发状态的，不完全的 | Partially prepared, not fully ready; premature, ill-considered. | He acted half-cock and ruined the plan. 他行事仓促，毁了整个计划。 |
half-cock:/ˌhæf ˈkɑːk/
| n.| 半待发状态 | The state of being partially prepared or not fully ready. | The gun was at half-cock, dangerous but not ready to fire. 枪处于半待发状态，危险但还没准备好射击。|
half-cock:/ˌhæf ˈkɑːk/
|  |  | Etymology: From the position of a firearm's hammer when partly raised, ready for quick firing but not yet fully cocked.  源于枪械的击锤部分抬起的状态，可以快速射击但还未完全待发。|


近义词:ill-prepared, premature, incomplete, hasty
反义词:prepared, ready, complete, thorough
词性: adjective, adverb, noun
