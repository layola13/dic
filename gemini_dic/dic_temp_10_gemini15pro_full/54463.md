disintegrating: /dɪˈsɪntəɡreɪtɪŋ/
verb, adjective
瓦解，解体；崩溃；衰变 (verb)
瓦解的；崩溃的；衰变的 (adjective)
Breaking apart into smaller pieces or components; ceasing to exist as a unified whole. (English Explanation)
The old car was slowly disintegrating from rust.  这辆旧车正在慢慢地因生锈而瓦解。
The empire began disintegrating after the death of the emperor.  皇帝死后，帝国开始瓦解。
Synonyms: crumbling, decaying, falling apart, fragmenting
Antonyms: integrating, unifying, consolidating, strengthening
verb
