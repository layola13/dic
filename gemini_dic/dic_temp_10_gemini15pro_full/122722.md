moho:/ˈmoʊhoʊ/ (US), /ˈməʊhəʊ/ (UK)| n. |莫霍面; 莫霍洛维奇不连续面|The boundary between the Earth's crust and the mantle.  It is defined by a sudden increase in the speed of seismic waves.|The earthquake's epicenter was located near the moho.  这次地震的震中位于莫霍面附近。|近义词: Mohorovičić discontinuity, M-discontinuity|反义词:  N/A|名词
