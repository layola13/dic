disfavorer:/dɪsˈfeɪvərər/
n.
不赞成者，反对者
One who disfavors or opposes something or someone.
The disfavorer of the new policy voiced his concerns at the meeting.  这位新政策的反对者在会议上表达了他的担忧。
opponent, antagonist, critic
supporter, proponent
