clicket:/ˈklɪkɪt/| n.|织布梭子上的一个小部件；棘轮；(尤指昆虫发出的)咔哒声|A small part on a weaver's shuttle or a ratchet; a clicking sound, especially one made by an insect.|The clicket on the loom was broken, halting the weaving process. 织布机上的棘轮坏了，停止了织布过程。|The cricket's clicket filled the night air. 蟋蟀的咔哒声充满了夜空。|近义词：ratchet, click, sound|反义词:silence|词性:名词

词源:  Likely a diminutive of "click,"  imitating the sound.  Possibly related to Middle Dutch *klikete.
