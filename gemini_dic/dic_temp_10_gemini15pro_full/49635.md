demijohn:/ˈdɛmiˌdʒɒn/ (US) /ˈdɛmiˌdʒɒn/ (UK)| n.|大肚窄颈玻璃瓶；大坛|A large, narrow-necked bottle of glass or earthenware, encased in wickerwork or other protective material, used for holding liquids, typically wine or other alcoholic beverages.|He carefully poured the wine into the demijohn, sealing it tightly with a cork. 他小心翼翼地把酒倒入大肚瓶中，用软木塞塞紧。

The antique shop had several demijohns on display, each with a unique shape and size.  古董店里陈列着几个大肚瓶，每个瓶子的形状和大小都独一无二。


近义词：flagon, carboy, jug|反义词: 无|词性:名词
