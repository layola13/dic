epispermic:/ˌɛpɪˈspɜːrmɪk/
adjective
种皮上的；关于种皮的
Relating to or located on the episperm (the outer seed coat).
The epispermic hairs aid in seed dispersal. 这些种皮上的毛有助于种子传播。
近义词: testa
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially words relating to the inner seed coat like "tegmic")
词性: adjective
