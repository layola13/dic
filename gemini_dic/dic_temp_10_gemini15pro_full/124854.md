mucor:/ˈmjuːkɔːr/ (US), /ˈmjuːkə(r)/ (UK)| n. |毛霉菌|A genus of fungi in the family Mucoraceae, characterized by having tall sporangiophores that terminate in a globose sporangium containing numerous sporangiospores.  They are common molds found on decaying organic matter.|Mucor is often found growing on bread and other food items left out in the open. 毛霉菌经常出现在暴露在空气中的面包和其他食物上。|Synonym: mold, fungus|Antonym: None|Noun
