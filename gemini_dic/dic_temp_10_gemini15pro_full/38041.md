comport:/kəmˈpɔːrt/ | v. |  表现；举止；相称 | To behave in a particular manner; to conduct oneself. Also, to be in agreement, harmony, or conformity with something. |  She comported herself with dignity at the funeral.  她在葬礼上举止庄重。 | synonyms: behave, conduct, bear, acquit, demean | antonyms: misbehave | verb

comport:/kəmˈpɔːrt/ | v. | 符合；一致 | To be in agreement, harmony, or conformity with something. | His actions didn't comport with his words. 他的行为与他的言辞不符。 | synonyms: agree, accord, conform, harmonize | antonyms: clash, disagree | verb
