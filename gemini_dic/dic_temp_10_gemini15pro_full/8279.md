anti-Judaisms: /ˌæntiˈdʒuːdəɪzəmz/ | n. | 反犹太主义（复数形式）|  Hostility or prejudice against Jews as a religious, ethnic, or racial group, often expressed in discrimination or persecution.  It can manifest in various forms, including religious, economic, cultural, and political. The plural form suggests different types or manifestations of anti-Jewish sentiment or action throughout history or in different contexts.|  Example:  The Holocaust was a horrific culmination of centuries of anti-Judaisms in Europe.  纳粹大屠杀是欧洲几个世纪以来反犹太主义的可怕顶点。

Example: The professor's lecture explored the various anti-Judaisms that emerged in the Middle Ages. 教授的讲座探讨了中世纪出现的各种反犹太主义。 | 近义词: anti-Semitism, Judeophobia | 反义词: Philosemitism | 词性: noun
