multisourcing:/ˌmʌltiˈsɔːrsɪŋ/ | n. | 多源采购 | The practice of using multiple suppliers for goods or services, often to reduce risk or improve flexibility. |  Many companies are adopting multisourcing strategies to avoid dependence on a single supplier. 许多公司正在采用多源采购策略，以避免依赖单一供应商。

| The company's multisourcing strategy allows them to negotiate better prices. 该公司的多源采购策略使他们能够谈判更好的价格。 | Near Synonyms: multiple sourcing, diversified sourcing | Antonym: single sourcing | noun
