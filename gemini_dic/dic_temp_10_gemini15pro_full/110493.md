limacina:/ˌlaɪməˈsaɪnə/ | n. | 海蝴蝶 | A small, free-swimming sea snail, also known as a sea butterfly, belonging to the family Limacinidae.  These snails have a thin, transparent shell and wing-like parapodia used for swimming. | Limacina helicina is a common species of sea butterfly found in Arctic waters.  海蝴蝶（limacina helicina）是一种常见于北极水域的海蝴蝶物种。 | synonyms: sea butterfly, pteropod | antonyms: N/A | noun
