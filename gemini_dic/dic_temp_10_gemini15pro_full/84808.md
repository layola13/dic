handwheel:/ˈhændwiːl/| n. |手轮，手摇轮|A wheel turned by hand, used to operate a valve, machine, etc. |例句：The handwheel controls the flow of water into the tank.  手轮控制水流入水箱。

例句：He spun the handwheel to open the valve. 他转动手轮打开了阀门。|近义词：wheel, crank, knob|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "automated control")|noun
