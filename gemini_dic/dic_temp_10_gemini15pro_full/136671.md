nonsecludednesses:/ˌnɒnsɪˈkluːdɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
非隐蔽性；不隔离性
The state or quality of not being secluded or isolated.
The park's nonsecludednesses made it a popular spot for families.  这个公园的不隔离性使它成为家庭聚会的热门地点。
The nonsecludednesses of the office environment made it difficult to concentrate. 办公室环境的开放性使人难以集中注意力。
近义词: openness, accessibility, publicness
反义词: seclusion, privacy, isolation
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + secluded (isolated, private) + -ness (forming nouns denoting a state or quality).  Secluded ultimately comes from the Latin word "secludere" meaning "to shut off, separate."
