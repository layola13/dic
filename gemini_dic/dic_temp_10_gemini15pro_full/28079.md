capillaire: /kæpɪˈlɛər/ (US), /kəˈpɪleə(r)/ (UK)| n. adj.| 毛细管；毛细血管的；糖浆 (尤指用蕨类植物制成)|A syrup, formerly made from maidenhair fern, used especially as a flavouring in beverages.  Also: a preparation for promoting hair growth.|She added a dash of capillaire to her lemonade. 她往柠檬水中加了一点糖浆。|近义词：syrup, tonic|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, adjective

capillaire: /kæpɪˈlɛər/ (US), /kəˈpɪleə(r)/ (UK)| adj.| 毛细血管的|Relating to or resembling a capillary or capillaries.|Capillary action draws water up the stem of the plant. 毛细管作用使水沿着植物的茎向上吸。|近义词：fine, hairlike|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *capillaire*, from Latin *capillāris* (“of the hair”), from *capillus* (“hair”).
