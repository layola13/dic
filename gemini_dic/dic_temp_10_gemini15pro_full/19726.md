bilk:/bɪlk/
verb
欺骗; 诈骗
To cheat or defraud someone of money or other valuable possessions.
The contractor bilked the homeowners out of thousands of dollars by overcharging for materials.  承包商通过对材料多收费，骗取了房主数千美元。
He bilked investors out of millions of dollars. 他骗取了投资者数百万美元。
近义词: cheat, defraud, swindle, con
反义词: compensate, repay


ETYMOLOGY->
From perhaps a variant of obsolete *balk "to leave a remainder," from Middle French *balquer (compare Old French balquier "to pile up in ridges").  The original sense was "to cheat at cribbage by preventing one's opponent from reaching a game of 61 points."
