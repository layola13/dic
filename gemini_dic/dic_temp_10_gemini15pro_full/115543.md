mappa:/ˈmæpə/ (US), /ˈmɑːpə/ (UK)| n.|地图；（尤指）区域图；（基因的）图谱|A visual representation of an area, often a portion of land or sea, showing physical features, cities, roads, etc.  A diagram or other visual representation that shows the relative positions of genetic markers on a chromosome or DNA molecule.|例句：He consulted the mappa mundi before embarking on his voyage. 他在开始航行前查阅了世界地图。

例句：The scientists created a mappa of the human genome. 科学家们绘制了人类基因组图谱。|近义词：map, chart, plan|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "disorder," "chaos" in a figurative sense referring to a lack of organized structure)|noun
