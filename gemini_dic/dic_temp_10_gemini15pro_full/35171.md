clowns:/klaʊnz/ | n. | 小丑；笨蛋；粗鲁的人 |  A professional entertainer who wears funny clothes, has a painted face, and makes people laugh by performing tricks and silly actions.  It can also refer to someone who behaves in a foolish or clumsy way. | The circus clowns were very funny.  马戏团的小丑们非常滑稽。

The children loved the clowns at the birthday party. 孩子们喜欢生日派对上的小丑。| synonyms: buffoon, comedian, fool, jester | antonyms: serious person, intellectual | noun
