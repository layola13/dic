hemispheric:/ˌhemɪˈsfɪrɪk/ /ˌhemɪˈsfɛrɪk/| adj.|半球的，大脑半球的|Relating to or affecting a hemisphere, especially of the brain or the earth.|例句：Hemispheric specialization is a key aspect of brain function. 大脑半球专门化是大脑功能的一个关键方面。|例句：The northern hemispheric winter is from December to February. 北半球的冬季是从十二月到二月。|近义词：global, worldwide (when referring to the Earth)|反义词:local, regional|词性:adjective

hemisphere:/ˈhemɪsfɪr/ /ˈhemɪsfɪr/| n.|半球|Half of a sphere, especially the Earth or the brain.|例句：The Earth is divided into the Northern and Southern Hemispheres. 地球被分为北半球和南半球。|例句：The left hemisphere of the brain is often associated with language and logic. 左脑通常与语言和逻辑相关。|近义词：half, side|反义词:whole|词性:noun
