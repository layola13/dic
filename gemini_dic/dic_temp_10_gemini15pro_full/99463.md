interfaculty:/ˌɪntərˈfækəlti/| adj.|学院间的，跨学院的|Involving or existing between two or more faculties of a university or college.|例句：The university held an interfaculty competition to promote collaboration. 这所大学举办了一场跨学院比赛以促进合作。|例句：Interfaculty research is becoming increasingly important. 跨学院研究正变得越来越重要。|近义词：interdepartmental, cross-faculty|反义词:intrafaculty|词性:形容词
