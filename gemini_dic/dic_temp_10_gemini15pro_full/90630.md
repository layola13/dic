humanistics:/ˌhjuːməˈnɪstɪks/ | n. | 人文主义，人文学科 |  The study of human culture, especially its languages, literature, history, and philosophy.  |  She majored in humanities in college. 她大学主修人文学科。 |  liberal arts, humanism |  science, technology | noun

humanistic:/ˌhjuːməˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 人文主义的，人本主义的 |  Relating to humanism or humanists; emphasizing human potential and values. |  He took a humanistic approach to the problem. 他用人文主义的方法处理这个问题。 |  humanitarian, compassionate |  inhuman, unfeeling | adjective
