capacity's:/kəˈpæsətiz/
noun
容量的；能力的
Possessive form of capacity, referring to something belonging to or associated with the capacity of something or someone.
The factory is operating at capacity's limit.  这家工厂正在满负荷运转。
The courtroom was filled to its capacity's maximum. 法庭座无虚席。
Synonyms: limit, potential, extent, size, volume
Antonyms: incapacity, inability, limitation, restriction
词性: 名词所有格
