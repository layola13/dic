dismember:/dɪsˈmɛmbər/
|v.|肢解，分割|To cut or tear (a body) into pieces; mutilate. |The serial killer dismembered his victims and buried them in the woods. 连环杀手肢解了他的受害者，并将他们埋在树林里。|Synonyms: mutilate, dissect, sever, butcher|Antonyms: assemble, join, connect|verb

dismember:/dɪsˈmɛmbər/
|adj.|被肢解的|Cut or torn into pieces. (Rare usage) |The dismembered body was discovered by hikers.  被肢解的尸体被徒步旅行者发现。|Synonyms: mutilated, severed|Antonyms: whole, intact|adjective
