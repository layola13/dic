brickenstein: /brɪkənˌstaɪn/
n.
砖块怪人，砖块构成的怪物
A creature or structure made of bricks, often large and resembling Frankenstein's monster.  This word is a portmanteau of "brick" and "Frankenstein."
He built a giant brickenstein in his backyard for Halloween. 他在后院里建了一个巨大的砖块怪人来过万圣节。

近义词: brick monster, brick golem
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could be something delicate like "a flower arrangement")
