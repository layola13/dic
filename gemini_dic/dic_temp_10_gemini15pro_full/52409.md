didelphia:/daɪˈdɛlfiə/ | n. | 双孔类动物 | A subclass of mammals characterized by having a pouch (marsupium) in which the young are carried and nourished. |  Didelphia are found mainly in Australia and the Americas. 双孔类动物主要分布在澳大利亚和美洲。|  The kangaroo is a well-known example of a didelphian. 袋鼠是双孔类动物的一个著名例子。 | synonyms: Marsupialia | antonyms:  Eutheria (胎盘类) | noun

didelphous:/daɪˈdɛlfəs/ | adj. | 双孔类的 | Belonging to or characteristic of the Didelphia subclass of mammals. |  Opossums are didelphous mammals found in the Americas.  负鼠是美洲发现的双孔类哺乳动物。 | The didelphous reproductive system is unique. 双孔类的繁殖系统很独特。| synonyms: marsupial | antonyms: placental | adjective
