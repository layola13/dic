magisterialnesses:/ˌmædʒɪˈstɪəriəlnɪsɪz/
|n.|权威性，威严，专横|The quality of being magisterial; authoritative dignity or overbearingness.|His pronouncements were delivered with a certain magisterialness that silenced the room. 他的声明带着某种权威性，让整个房间鸦雀无声。|Synonyms: authoritativeness, imperiousness, dominance, loftiness, pomposity|Antonyms: humility, modesty, meekness, subservience|noun
