outgloom:/aʊtˈɡluːm/
verb
使忧郁, 使黯然失色
to be more gloomy than; to surpass in gloominess.
The dark clouds outgloomed the sunny sky.  乌云使晴朗的天空黯然失色。
The funeral procession outgloomed the entire town. 送葬队伍使整个城镇都笼罩在一片愁云惨雾之中。
Synonyms: overshadow, eclipse, darken
Antonyms: brighten, cheer, lighten
词性: 动词
