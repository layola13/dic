faved:/feɪvd/ | v. | 收藏，加为最爱 (past tense and past participle of "fave") |  This song has been faved by thousands of users. 这首歌已经被成千上万的用户收藏。|  I faved that tweet because it was hilarious. 我收藏了那条推文，因为它太搞笑了。 | 近义词：favorited, liked, bookmarked | 反义词：unfaved, disliked | 动词

词源: "faved" comes from "fave," which is a shortened, informal version of "favorite."  The verb form "to fave" emerged with social media platforms where users can mark items as favorites.
