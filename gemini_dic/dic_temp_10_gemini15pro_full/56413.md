dolioform:/ˈdɒliəfɔːm/| adj.|桶状的；似桶状的|Shaped like a barrel or cask; barrel-shaped.|The dolioform larva of the insect was easily recognizable. 这种昆虫的桶状幼虫很容易辨认。|近义词：barrel-shaped, cask-shaped|反义词: elongated, slender|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *doliiformis, from dolium (“cask, jar”) + -formis (“-shaped”).
