nonhomogeneousness:/ˌnɒnˌhɒməʊdʒɪˈniːɪtnəs/ /ˌnɑːnˌhoʊmoʊdʒɪˈniːɪtnəs/
noun
非均匀性，非同质性
The state or quality of being nonhomogeneous; lack of homogeneity or uniformity.
The nonhomogeneousness of the soil caused problems for the construction crew. 土壤的非均匀性给施工人员带来了问题。
nonhomogeneity, heterogeneity, inhomogeneity, disuniformity
homogeneity, uniformity
