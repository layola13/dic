groomsmen:/ˈɡruːmzmen/| n.|伴郎（复数）|A male friend or relative who attends the bridegroom at his wedding.|The groomsmen looked dapper in their matching suits. 伴郎们穿着配套的西装，看起来很帅气。|The groomsmen helped the groom get ready for the ceremony. 伴郎们帮助新郎准备婚礼仪式。|Synonyms: best man (if the lead groomsman), ushers, attendants|Antonyms: bridesmaids|noun
