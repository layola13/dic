breeders:/ˈbriːdərz/| n.|饲养员，繁殖者|People who raise animals or plants, especially for specific characteristics.|例句：The dog breeders carefully selected the parents for the next litter.  这些狗饲养员仔细地为下一窝幼犬挑选了父母。

例句：Rose breeders are constantly developing new varieties. 玫瑰种植者不断地开发新品种。|近义词：raisers, cultivators, propagators|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

breed:/briːd/| v.|繁殖，饲养，养育，产生|To produce offspring; to raise (animals); to develop (a quality or feature).|例句：These horses are bred for speed. 这些马是为了速度而饲养的。

例句：Poverty breeds crime. 贫穷滋生犯罪。|近义词：raise, rear, produce, generate|反义词:extinguish, eradicate|词性:verb
