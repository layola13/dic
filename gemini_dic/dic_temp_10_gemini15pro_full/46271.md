czarina's:/zɑːˈriːnəz/
n.
女沙皇的；皇后；女皇的
Possessive form of czarina, meaning belonging to or associated with a czarina.
The czarina's jewels were legendary. 女沙皇的珠宝富有传奇色彩。
The czarina's reign was marked by political unrest. 女沙皇的统治以政治动荡为标志。
Synonyms: empress's, queen's
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a possessive.  One could consider "subject's" or "citizen's" as contrasting concepts.)
