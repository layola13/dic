partiest:/ˈpɑːrtiɪst/
adjective
最爱参加派对的人; 热衷派对的人
Most enthusiastic about parties; a person who loves parties the most.
She's the partiest person I know; she never misses a social gathering. 她是我认识的最爱参加派对的人；她从不错过任何社交聚会。
近义词:gregarious, sociable, outgoing
反义词:antisocial, withdrawn, reserved
词性:adjective


partiest (non-standard, informal)
/ˈpɑːrtiɪst/
adjective
派对最多的；最热闹的 (This usage is less common and considered informal.  It's formed by adding "-est" to "party" in a similar way to "funniest" or "happiest," but isn't grammatically standard.)
Having the most parties; most festive.
This is the partiest week of the year with celebrations every night.  (Informal) 这是一年中最热闹的一周，每天晚上都有庆祝活动。 (非正式)
近义词:festive, lively, celebratory
反义词:quiet, subdued, uneventful
词性:adjective



词源:  "Party" comes from Old French "partie," from Late Latin "partita" meaning "a part, share, division."  The "-est" suffix is a superlative ending used in English to form the highest degree of adjectives.
