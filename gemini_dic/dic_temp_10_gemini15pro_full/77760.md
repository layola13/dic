genealogical:/ˌdʒiːniəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ (US) /ˌdʒiːniəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
系谱的；家谱的
Relating to the study or tracing of lines of family descent.
She began researching her genealogical history. 她开始研究她的家族史。
近义词：ancestral, familial
反义词：N/A
词性：adjective


genealogical:/ˌdʒiːniəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ (US) /ˌdʒiːniəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
宗谱的
Of or relating to genealogy.
He joined the genealogical society to learn more about his ancestors. 他加入了宗谱协会，以便更多地了解他的祖先。
近义词：hereditary, inherited
反义词：N/A
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *généalogique*, from Latin *genealogicus*, from Greek *γενεαλογικός (genealogikos)*, from *γενεά (genea)* "race, generation" + *-λογικος (-logikos)*, from *λόγος (logos)* "word, reason, study".
