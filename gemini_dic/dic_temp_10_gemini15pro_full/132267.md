nondeductions:/ˌnɒndɪˈdʌkʃənz/ | n. | 不可扣除额; 非扣除项目 | Amounts that cannot be subtracted from gross income to arrive at taxable income. | Example:  Nondeductions include personal expenses, such as groceries and clothing.  不可扣除额包括个人开支，例如食品杂货和服装。

Example:  Entertainment expenses are often nondeductions unless directly related to business. 除非与业务直接相关，否则娱乐费用通常是不可扣除的。 | 近义词: non-allowable expenses, disallowed expenses | 反义词: deductions, allowable expenses | 词性: 名词

词源:
non- (prefix): not, opposite of  来自拉丁语 non "not".
deduction (noun): the action of deducting or subtracting something.  来自拉丁语 deduct-, deducere "to lead down, draw away," from de- "down, away" + ducere "to lead."
