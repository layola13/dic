microphotograph: /ˌmaɪkrəˈfoʊtəɡræf/ | n. |显微照片，显微摄影|A photograph taken through a microscope, showing a greatly magnified image of a small object. |例句：The microphotograph revealed the intricate structure of the cell.  显微照片揭示了细胞的复杂结构。

例句：The researchers published microphotographs of the newly discovered bacteria. 研究人员发表了新发现的细菌的显微照片。|近义词：photomicrograph, micrograph |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps macroscopic image) |词性: noun
