client-server: /ˈklaɪənt ˈsɜːrvər/ (US) /ˈklaɪənt ˈsɜːvə/ (UK)| adj. n.| 客户端-服务器; 客户机-服务器 |A computing model in which tasks are distributed between providers of a resource or service, called servers, and service requesters, called clients.|  The company uses a client-server architecture for its network.  该公司在其网络中使用客户端-服务器架构。| 近义词: distributed system, network architecture | 反义词: peer-to-peer | 词性: adjective, noun

client: /ˈklaɪənt/ | n. | 客户端；客户；委托人 | A computer or program that requests services from a server.| The client application connects to the server to retrieve data. 客户端应用程序连接到服务器以检索数据。|近义词: customer, consumer, user | 反义词: server, provider | 词性: noun

server: /ˈsɜːrvər/ (US) /ˈsɜːvə/ (UK) | n. | 服务器；服务员 | A computer or program that provides services to other computers or programs, called clients, over a network.| The server stores the database and responds to client requests. 服务器存储数据库并响应客户端请求。| 近义词: host, provider | 反义词: client, consumer | 词性: noun
