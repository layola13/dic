abet:/əˈbɛt/| v. |教唆，煽动，怂恿|To encourage or assist (someone) to do something wrong, in particular, to commit a crime or other offense.|He was abetted in the deception by his wife.  他妻子怂恿他进行欺骗。

The lawyer was accused of abetting the criminal.  该律师被指控教唆罪犯。|近义词：encourage, support, assist, aid, incite, instigate|反义词：deter, discourage, hinder, obstruct|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French abeter "to bait, hound on," from à "to" (from Latin ad) + beter "to bait."  The original sense was of urging dogs to attack, later broadened to inciting any kind of action, especially bad ones.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He abets his friend in skipping school. = 他怂恿他的朋友逃学。
She often abets her brother's mischievous plans. = 她经常怂恿她弟弟的恶作剧计划。

一般过去时:
He abetted the thief in the robbery. = 他教唆了小偷进行抢劫。
They abetted the protestors in their violent actions. = 他们煽动了抗议者的暴力行为。


现在完成时:
The company has abetted the illegal activities of its employees. = 该公司一直纵容其员工的非法活动。
She has abetted her son's bad behavior by constantly making excuses for him. = 她一直为儿子找借口，从而纵容了他的不良行为。


过去完成时:
He had abetted the criminals before he realized the severity of their plans. = 在意识到他们计划的严重性之前，他已经怂恿了罪犯。
They had abetted the rebellion for months before the government took action. = 在政府采取行动之前，他们已经煽动叛乱好几个月了。


将来时:
If you abet him in this crime, you will face consequences too. = 如果你怂恿他犯下这桩罪行，你也要承担后果。
The authorities believe he will abet further violence if he is not stopped. = 当局认为，如果不阻止他，他会进一步煽动暴力。


虚拟语气:
If I were to abet him, I would be just as guilty. = 如果我怂恿他，我也会同样有罪。
I wish I hadn't abetted their foolish plan. = 我希望我没有怂恿他们愚蠢的计划。


被动语态:
The crime was abetted by several individuals. = 这起犯罪是由几个人教唆的。
The riot was believed to have been abetted by outside agitators. = 人们认为这场骚乱是由外部煽动者煽动的。


疑问句:
Did he abet the crime? = 他教唆了犯罪吗？
Why did she abet such reckless behavior? = 她为什么要怂恿这种鲁莽的行为？


否定句:
He did not abet the criminals. = 他没有教唆罪犯。
She does not abet his bad habits. = 她不纵容他的坏习惯。


PHRASE->
abet in = 怂恿...做...
abet the commission of a crime = 教唆犯罪
abet someone to do something = 怂恿某人做某事
abet by silence = 默许
abet wrongdoing = 怂恿做坏事
