词源：
From Middle English *carpen, from Old North French *carper, of uncertain origin. Perhaps related to Old French harper (“to grab, seize”), from Frankish *harpōn (“hook, grapple”), from Proto-Germanic *hrapōną (“hook, grapple”). More at grapple.
