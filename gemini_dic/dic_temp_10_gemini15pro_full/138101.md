normless:/ˈnɔːmlɪs/
adjective
无规范的，无法无天的，不正常的
lacking norms or standards of behavior; unregulated or uncontrolled.
The movie portrays a normless society where violence and corruption are rampant. 这部电影描绘了一个暴力和腐败猖獗的无法无天的社会。
The collapse of the government led to a period of normless chaos. 政府的垮台导致了一段无规范的混乱时期。
Synonyms:lawless, unruly, anarchic, anomic
Antonyms:lawful, orderly, regulated, normal
词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From norm + -less.  "Norm" comes from the Latin "norma" meaning "carpenter's square, rule, pattern."  The suffix "-less" indicates a lack or absence of something.
