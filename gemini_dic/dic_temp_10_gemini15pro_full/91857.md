hyperimmunizations:/ˌhaɪpərˌɪmjənɪˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.| 超免疫|The process of inducing an abnormally high level of immunity in an organism, typically through repeated administrations of an antigen.|  Hyperimmunizations were performed on the horses to produce large quantities of antivenom. 对马进行了超免疫，以产生大量的抗蛇毒血清。|近义词：hyperimmunisation|反义词:|名词
