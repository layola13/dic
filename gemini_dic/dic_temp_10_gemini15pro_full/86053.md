heap:/hiːp/| n. v.|堆，大量，许多；堆积|A disorderly pile of something; a large amount or number of something.|The laundry lay in a heap on the floor.  衣服堆在地板上。|He has heaps of money. 他有很多钱。|synonyms: pile, mound, stack, mass, abundance|antonyms: scarcity, lack, dearth|noun
heap:/hiːp/| v.|堆积，堆放|To put or throw something into a pile.|She heaped the books on the table. 她把书堆在桌子上。|The waiter heaped mashed potatoes onto my plate. 服务员把土豆泥堆在我的盘子里。|synonyms: pile, stack, amass|antonyms: scatter, disperse, distribute|verb
