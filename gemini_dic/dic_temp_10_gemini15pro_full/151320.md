parishad:/pʌˈrɪʃæd/ | n. | 委员会，理事会，议会 | An assembly or council, especially in India, for deliberation or consultation.  It often refers to a learned assembly or a religious congregation. | Example 1: The village parishad met to discuss the new water project.  村议会开会讨论新的水利项目。

Example 2: The literary parishad attracted scholars from all over the country.  文学委员会吸引了来自全国各地的学者。 | Synonym: council, assembly, committee, board | Antonym: individual, loner | Noun
