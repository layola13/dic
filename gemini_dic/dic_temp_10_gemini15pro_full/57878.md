dribbling:/ˈdrɪbəlɪŋ/ /ˈdrɪblɪŋ/| n. |运球，滴下，流口水|The act of moving a ball by bouncing it or kicking it repeatedly.  Also, the act of saliva dripping from the mouth.|例句：His basketball dribbling skills are impressive. 他的篮球运球技巧令人印象深刻。

dribbling:/ˈdrɪbəlɪŋ/ /ˈdrɪblɪŋ/| v. |运球，滴下，流口水|To move a ball by bouncing it or kicking it repeatedly. Also, to let saliva drip from the mouth.|例句：He was dribbling the ball down the court. 他正运球沿着球场前进。

dribbling:/ˈdrɪbəlɪŋ/ /ˈdrɪblɪŋ/| v. |运球，滴下，流口水|To let saliva drip from the mouth.|例句：The dog was dribbling as it watched me eat.  狗看着我吃东西时，口水直流。


词源: From Middle English *driblen, frequentative of drippen (“to drip”).

近义词:(for ball) bouncing, controlling, maneuvering; (for saliva) drooling
反义词: (no direct opposite for ball dribbling); (for saliva)  N/A
词性: noun, verb (present participle)
