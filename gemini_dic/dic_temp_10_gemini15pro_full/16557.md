barmote:/ˈbɑːrˌmoʊt/| n.|矿山法院|A court held in the lead mining districts of Derbyshire, England, to determine disputes connected with lead mining.|The barmote court settled disputes over mining claims. 矿山法院解决了采矿权纠纷。|Synonyms: mining court, mineral court|Antonyms: N/A|noun
