buster's:/ˈbʌstərz/| n.|破坏者/家伙（的）|buster's is the possessive form of buster. It refers to something belonging to or associated with a "buster."  "Buster" can have several meanings, including a person who breaks things, a boisterous child, or a fellow (often used as a term of endearment).|That's Buster's car parked outside. 那是巴斯特的车停在外面。| Buster's always getting into trouble. 巴斯特总是惹麻烦。|近义词：fellow's, chap's, guy's, kid's, breaker's (depending on the meaning of buster)|反义词:fixer's, builder's (if buster is used in the sense of breaker)|词性:名词（所有格）
