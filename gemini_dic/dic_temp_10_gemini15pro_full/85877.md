headboard:/ˈhedbɔːrd/ (US) /ˈhedbɔːd/ (UK)| n. |床头板|A board forming the head of a bed, often upholstered or decorative. |例句：The headboard was carved with intricate designs. 床头板雕刻着复杂的图案。

例句：She leaned against the padded headboard and read a book. 她靠在软垫床头板上看书。|近义词：bedhead|反义词:footboard|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From head + board.  "Head" referring to the head of the bed, and "board" referring to a flat piece of wood.  The term emerged in the late 16th century.
