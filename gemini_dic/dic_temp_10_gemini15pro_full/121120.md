misdeclare:/ˌmɪsdɪˈkleər/
verb
错误申报；谎报
to declare something incorrectly, especially in order to avoid paying the full amount of tax or duty
The company misdeclared the value of the goods.  该公司错误申报了商品的价值。
misstate, underdeclare
declare correctly, state accurately
v.
