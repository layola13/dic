non-cognate:/ˌnɒnˈkɒɡneɪt/ | adj. | 非同源的 | Not derived from the same source; not related by descent from a common ancestor. Used especially of words in different languages. | English "water" and French "eau" are non-cognate, despite their similar meanings.  英语单词 "water" 和法语单词 "eau" 是非同源词，尽管它们的意思相似。|  unrelated, dissimilar | cognate, related | adjective
