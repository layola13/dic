cryptics:/ˈkrɪptɪks/| n. |谜题; 隐晦的东西|Something having a hidden or secret meaning; a puzzle or enigma. |The newspaper's cryptics were notoriously difficult to solve. 这家报纸的谜题出了名的难解。|近义词：puzzles, riddles, enigmas, mysteries|反义词: solutions, answers, explanations| Noun


cryptic:/ˈkrɪptɪk/| adj. |隐晦的; 秘密的; 神秘的|Having a hidden or ambiguous meaning; obscure. |Her cryptic message left me wondering what she meant. 她隐晦的信息让我一直在想她是什么意思。|近义词：obscure, ambiguous, enigmatic, mysterious, puzzling|反义词: clear, obvious, straightforward, explicit| Adjective
