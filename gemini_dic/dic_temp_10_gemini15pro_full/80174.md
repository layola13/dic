godfathering:/ˈɡɒdfɑːðərɪŋ/ | n. | 教父职责；充当教父；（对新生事物）支持，引导 | The act of serving as a godfather, or the support and guidance given to something new, like a project or a career. | He took his godfathering duties very seriously. 他非常认真地对待自己的教父职责。

The CEO's godfathering of the new project was crucial to its success. 首席执行官对新项目的支持和引导对其成功至关重要。 | 近义词：mentoring, sponsoring, championing | 反义词：neglecting, abandoning | 词性: noun
