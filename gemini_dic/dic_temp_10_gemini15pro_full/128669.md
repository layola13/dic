nicotianine:/nɪˌkoʊʃiˈænɪn/| n. | 烟碱胺 | An alkaloid found in tobacco plants, believed to be involved in nicotine metabolism and metal detoxification.  It chelates metals, which may contribute to the plant's tolerance to heavy metals in the soil.| Nicotianine plays a crucial role in the long-distance transport of nickel in plants. 烟碱胺在植物中镍的长距离运输中起着至关重要的作用。| Nicotianine concentrations increase in response to excess nickel in the growth medium. 烟碱胺的浓度会随着生长介质中镍含量的增加而增加。| synonym:  None (highly specific compound) | antonym: None (chemical compound)| noun
