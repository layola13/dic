awl:/ɔːl/ | n. | 锥子；尖钻 | A small pointed tool used for piercing holes, especially in leather or wood. |  He used an awl to make holes in the leather for the stitching. 他用锥子在皮革上钻孔以便缝合。

|近义词:bradawl, piercer, punch|反义词:None|词性:noun


awl:/ɔːl/ | n. | (古语) 裁缝 | (Archaic) A shoemaker or cobbler. | The awl mended the sole of the shoe.  鞋匠修补了鞋底。(古语用法)

|近义词:cobbler, shoemaker|反义词:None|词性:noun


Etymology:
From Old English *æle, from Proto-Germanic *aliz, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂el-. Cognate with Old High German ala, Old Norse alr.
