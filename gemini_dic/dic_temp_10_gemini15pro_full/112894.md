ludibrious:/luːˈdɪbriəs/| adj.|滑稽的，可笑的，荒谬的|Tending to cause laughter because of absurdity; ridiculous.|His ludibrious attempts at juggling ended with him covered in fruit. 他滑稽的玩杂耍的尝试以他浑身沾满水果而告终。|Synonyms: ridiculous, absurd, comical, farcical, laughable, risible|Antonyms: serious, solemn, grave|adjective
