mortifying:/ˈmɔːrtɪfaɪɪŋ/ /ˈmɔːr təˌfaɪɪŋ/| adj.|令人尴尬的，羞愧的，丢脸的|Causing feelings of shame or humiliation; extremely embarrassing.|例句：It was a mortifying experience to fall over in front of everyone. 在大家面前摔倒真是丢脸的经历。

例句：Losing my pants on stage was the most mortifying experience of my life. 在舞台上掉裤子是我一生中最尴尬的经历。

近义词：humiliating, embarrassing, shameful, awkward|反义词:exhilarating, empowering, flattering|词性:adjective
