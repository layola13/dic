impassivity:/ɪmˌpæˈsɪvɪti/
n.| 冷漠，无动于衷 |Lack of emotion or feeling; an impassive state or quality.|  He watched the tragedy unfold with complete impassivity. 他冷漠地看着悲剧发生。 | indifference, apathy, stoicism | emotionality, passion, excitability | noun


impassivities:/ɪmˌpæˈsɪvɪtiz/
n.| 冷漠，无动于衷（复数）| The plural form of impassivity, referring to multiple instances of lack of emotion or feeling.|  The impassivities of the guards were unsettling. 警卫们的冷漠令人不安。 | indifferences, apathies, stoicisms | emotionalities, passions, excitabilities | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *impassivité*, from *impassif* "impassive," from *in-* "not" + *passif* "passive," from Latin *passivus*.
