detest:/dɪˈtest/| v. |憎恶，厌恶|To feel intense dislike or hatred for.|I detest hypocrisy. 我憎恶虚伪。|She detests any form of cruelty. 她厌恶任何形式的残忍。|近义词：abhor, loathe, despise, hate|反义词：love, adore, admire|词性:动词
词源：From Latin detestari "to curse while calling down upon (someone or something) the wrath of a deity," from de- "down, away" + testari "to bear witness, call as witness."
