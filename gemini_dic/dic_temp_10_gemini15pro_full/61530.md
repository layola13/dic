embryon:/ˈɛmbriən/| n. |胚胎；胚芽；初期|An unborn or unhatched offspring in the process of development, in particular a human offspring during the period from approximately the second to the eighth week after fertilization.|The doctor monitored the development of the embryon. 医生监测了胚胎的发育。|Synonyms: fetus, germ, seed, bud|Antonyms: adult, mature|noun
