monkey-wrencher:/ˈmʌŋkiˌrɛntʃ/ | n. | 扳手；障碍；捣乱的人或事 | A tool with adjustable jaws for gripping and turning nuts, bolts, pipes, etc. Also used figuratively to describe something that disrupts or sabotages a plan or process. |  He threw a monkey wrench into our plans by quitting at the last minute. 他在最后一刻辞职，打乱了我们的计划。 | synonyms: wrench, spanner (UK), obstacle, impediment, sabotage | antonyms: aid, assistance, help, support | noun


monkey-wrench (verb - informal):/ˈmʌŋkiˌrɛntʃ/ | v. | 捣乱，阻挠 | To sabotage or disrupt something. |  The sudden budget cuts monkey-wrenched our project. 突然的预算削减阻碍了我们的项目。 | synonyms: sabotage, disrupt, thwart, obstruct | antonyms: aid, assist, help, support | verb
