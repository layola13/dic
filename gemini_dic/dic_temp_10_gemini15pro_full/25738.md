buoyantly: /ˈbɔɪəntli/ | adv. | 轻快地，充满活力地 | In a cheerful and optimistic way, as if supported by something that lifts one's spirits. |  The boat bobbed buoyantly on the waves. 船轻快地在波浪上起伏。| cheerfully, optimistically, lightheartedly | dejectedly, gloomily, despondently | adverb

词源:
From buoy + -ant + -ly. Ultimately from Ancient Greek βούη (boúē, “shout, roar”) (of the sea)
