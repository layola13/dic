iff:/ɪf ænd əʊnli ɪf/| conj.|当且仅当|A logical statement meaning that one thing is true only if another thing is also true, and vice versa.  It implies equivalence or a necessary and sufficient condition.|例句：The alarm will sound iff the door is opened. 警报会在门打开时响起，并且只有在门打开时才会响起。

例句：You can go to the party iff you finish your homework. 你可以去参加派对，当且仅当你完成作业。|近义词：if and only if, just in case, only if|反义词:unless, except|词性:连词(conjunction)
