dutied:/ˈdjuːtid/| adj.|尽职的，有责任的|Assigned a duty or responsibility; obligated. Having been assigned a task or responsibility.|The dutied officer stood guard. 尽职的警官站岗。|近义词：responsible, obligated, bound|反义词：irresponsible, unbound, exempt|词性:形容词

词源: From the noun "duty" + "-ed" suffix, indicating having or characterized by the noun.  "Duty" itself comes from Old French "deu, dueté" meaning "what is owed, due," ultimately from Latin "debitum," past participle of "debere" meaning "to owe."
