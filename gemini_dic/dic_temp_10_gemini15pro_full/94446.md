implumed:/ɪmˈpluːmd/
adjective
无羽毛的，拔了毛的
Stripped of feathers; featherless.
The implumed chicken looked pathetic huddled in the corner.  这只没有羽毛的鸡可怜巴巴地 huddled 在角落里。
The implumed bird was unable to fly. 这只没有羽毛的鸟无法飞行。
近义词: featherless, unfeathered, plucked
反义词: feathered, plumed
词性: adjective
词源: im- (not) + plume (feather) - Latin origin
