filicoid:/ˈfɪlɪˌkɔɪd/| adj.|蕨类植物的，像蕨类的|Resembling a fern; fernlike.  Having the form or appearance of a fern.|The filicoid fronds unfurled in the humid air. 蕨类植物般的叶子在潮湿的空气中展开。|The prehistoric plant had a distinctly filicoid appearance.  这种史前植物具有明显的蕨类植物外观。|近义词：fernlike, pteridophyte|反义词: (No direct antonym; perhaps "non-fernlike") |词性: adjective
