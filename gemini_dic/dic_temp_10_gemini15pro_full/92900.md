ideological: /ˌaɪdiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌɪdiəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
意识形态的
Based on or relating to a particular ideology; relating to the study or promotion of a particular set of beliefs or principles, especially one with a political or economic basis.
His writings are highly ideological. 他的著作意识形态色彩很浓。
近义词: doctrinal, doctrinaire, dogmatic, opinionated
反义词: pragmatic, practical
词性: adjective
