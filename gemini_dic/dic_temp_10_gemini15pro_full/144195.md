outsift:/ˈaʊtsɪft/| vt.| 筛选；区分；挑选 | To separate or distinguish (something desirable) from a mass or mixture; to sift out.|  The historian outsifted the relevant documents from a mountain of papers. 历史学家从一大堆文件中筛选出相关的文件。| 近义词: sift, winnow, separate, filter | 反义词: combine, mix, mingle | 词性: 动词

outsift:/ˈaʊtsɪft/| n.| 筛选；区分；挑选（的结果）| The act of sifting out or separating something desirable from a mass or mixture; the result of such sifting. | The outsift of the process yielded a small but valuable collection of artifacts.  这个过程的筛选结果是一小批但很有价值的文物。| 近义词: selection, gleanings | 反义词: mixture, combination | 词性: 名词
