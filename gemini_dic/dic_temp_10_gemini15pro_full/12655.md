assegai:/ˈæsəɡaɪ/ /ˈæsɪˌɡaɪ/
n.
细长的投矛，标枪
A slender, iron-tipped spear, used chiefly by Southern African peoples.
The Zulu warriors advanced, brandishing their assegais.  祖鲁武士挥舞着他们的标枪前进。

近义词:spear, javelin, lance
反义词:(No direct antonym for a weapon)
词性:noun
