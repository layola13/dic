existentialists:/ˌɛɡzɪˈstɛnʃəlɪsts/
|n.|存在主义者|People who adhere to existentialism, a philosophical theory emphasizing the existence of the individual person as a free and responsible agent determining their own development through acts of the will.|Many French writers in the 20th century were existentialists. 许多20世纪的法国作家都是存在主义者。|Synonyms: existential philosophers|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps determinists or essentialists)|Noun
