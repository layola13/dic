dims:/dɪmz/| v.| (光线)变暗，(视力)模糊 | to make or become less bright or distinct | The lights dimmed as the movie began.  电影开始时灯光变暗了。| synonyms: fade, darken, blur | antonyms: brighten, sharpen, clarify | verb

dims:/dɪmz/| n.|  昏暗的光线 | weak or faint light |  The cat could see well even in the dim light. 即使在昏暗的光线中，猫也能看得清楚。 | synonyms: gloom, twilight | antonyms: brightness, brilliance | noun
