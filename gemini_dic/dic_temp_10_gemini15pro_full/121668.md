misplan:/ˌmɪsˈplæn/
verb
计划错误；筹划不周
to plan something badly or incorrectly
例句：
We misplanned the trip and ended up with no hotel reservations.
我们计划这次旅行时出了差错，结果没有预订到酒店。

They had misplanned the budget, resulting in a significant shortfall.
他们预算计划不周，导致了严重的资金短缺。

近义词：bungle, miscalculate, mishandle
反义词：plan well, organize effectively
词性：v.
