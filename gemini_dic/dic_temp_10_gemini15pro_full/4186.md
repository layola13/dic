airer:/ˈeərər/ (UK) /ˈɛrər/ (US)|n.|晾衣架|A frame or stand on which clothes are hung to dry or air.|I hung the washing on the airer. 我把洗好的衣服挂在晾衣架上。|近义词: clothes horse, drying rack|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology: From air + -er (suffix forming nouns from verbs).  The verb "to air" means to expose to the air, and the suffix "-er" denotes a thing that performs the action.
