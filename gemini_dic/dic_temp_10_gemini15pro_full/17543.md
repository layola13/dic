beatificate:/biːˌætɪfɪˈkeɪt/
verb
为...行宣福礼；使享福
to declare (a deceased person) to be among the blessed, especially by official papal decree, and to authorize the person's veneration as a blessed.
Pope John Paul II beatified Mother Teresa in 2003.  教皇约翰·保罗二世于2003年为特蕾莎修女举行了宣福礼。
近义词:canonize, bless, exalt
反义词:condemn, curse
词性:verb

beatific:/ˌbiːəˈtɪfɪk/
adjective
极乐的；天使般的
showing or expressing supreme happiness or blessedness.
He had a beatific smile on his face. 他脸上带着幸福的微笑。
近义词:blissful, radiant, serene
反义词:sorrowful, unhappy, miserable
词性:adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin beatificāre, from Latin beatus (“blessed”) + -ificāre (“-ify”).
