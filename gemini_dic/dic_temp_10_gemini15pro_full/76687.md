galvanoplasty:/ɡælˈvænəˌplæsti/
n.|电镀术，电铸术|The process of depositing a thin layer of metal onto a surface using electrolysis.  The process of electroplating; the coating of a surface with metal by means of an electric current.|Electrotyping is a form of galvanoplasty.|近义词:electroplating, electrotyping|反义词:N/A|名词
