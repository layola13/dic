babbitted:/ˈbæbɪtɪd/
adjective
巴氏合金处理的，衬有巴氏合金的
lined with babbitt metal (a soft, white, nonferrous alloy of tin, lead, copper, and antimony used as a bearing surface).
The engine's babbitted bearings ensured smooth operation.  这台发动机的巴氏合金轴承确保了平稳运转。
The shaft rotates in babbitted bearings.  轴在巴氏合金轴承中旋转。
Synonyms: lined (with babbitt), bearinged
Antonyms: unlined, bare
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Babbitt metal + -ed. Babbitt metal is named after Isaac Babbitt (1799–1862), the American inventor who patented it in 1839.
