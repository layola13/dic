figary:/ˈfɪɡəri/ /ˈfɪɡjəri/| n. |奇想；异想天开；怪念头|A figary is an odd or whimsical notion or an unpredictable action.|He had a sudden figary to dye his hair green. 他突然奇想把头发染成绿色。|近义词：whim, caprice, fancy, vagary|反义词：normality, conventionality|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *figurie*, from Old French *figurie* (“shape, form; figure of speech; idea, imagination”), from Latin *figūra* (“shape, form, figure”), from Proto-Italic *feikō*, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeyǵʰ- (“to form, build”).
