nonconcurrence:/ˌnɑːnkənˈkɜːrəns/ | n. |不一致，不同意，不赞同 |Lack of agreement or harmony; failure to concur.  Disagreement, especially in legal contexts. | The judge noted his nonconcurrence with the majority opinion. 法官表达了他对多数意见的不赞同。|

The project was delayed due to the nonconcurrence of several key stakeholders.  由于几个关键利益相关者意见不一致，该项目被推迟了。| 近义词：disagreement, dissent, objection, opposition | 反义词：agreement, concurrence, consent, approval | 词性: 名词

nonconcurring:/ˌnɑːnkənˈkɜːrɪŋ/ | adj. |不同意的，不赞同的 |Not concurring; disagreeing.  Expressing dissent, especially in legal contexts. |  The nonconcurring opinion was written by Judge Smith.  反对意见是由史密斯法官撰写的。 | The nonconcurring justices felt the law was unconstitutional. 不同意的法官认为该法律违宪。| 近义词：dissenting, disagreeing, objecting, opposing | 反义词：agreeing, concurring, consenting, approving | 词性: 形容词
