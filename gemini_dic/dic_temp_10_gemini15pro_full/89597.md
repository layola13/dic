hoodlumism:/ˈhuːdlʊmɪzəm/
n.
流氓行为；无赖行为
Disorderly or destructive behavior characteristic of hoodlums.
The city has seen a recent increase in hoodlumism and vandalism. 这个城市最近的流氓行为和故意破坏行为有所增加。

近义词:vandalism, hooliganism, delinquency, lawlessness
反义词:lawfulness, order, peacefulness
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hoodlum + -ism. Hoodlum's origin is uncertain, possibly from Hudlum, a San Francisco hoodlum whose name appeared in newspapers in the 1870s.
