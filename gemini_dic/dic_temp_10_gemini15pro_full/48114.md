declivity:/dɪˈklɪvɪti/
noun
下坡；倾斜
A downward slope.
The car rolled down the declivity. 汽车顺着下坡滚了下去。
synonyms: slope, incline, descent, grade
antonyms: acclivity, ascent, rise
n.
