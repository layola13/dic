dandify:/ˈdændɪfaɪ/| v. |打扮；使...像花花公子；使...讲究衣着 |To make someone or something more fashionable or dandyish.  To dress or behave like a dandy.|He dandified himself before going out to the club. 他在去俱乐部之前把自己打扮了一番。|She dandified her apartment with new cushions and throws. 她用新的靠垫和毯子装饰了她的公寓。|近义词：spruce up, doll up, groom, embellish|反义词：unkempt, dishevel, neglect|词性:动词
