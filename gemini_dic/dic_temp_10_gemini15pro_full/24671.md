bromlife: /ˈbroʊmlaɪf/ | n. | 兄弟情; 兄弟生活;  | A close male friendship, often characterized by shared activities, camaraderie, and mutual support.  It emphasizes a strong bond and shared experiences, typically between men. | My college years were filled with bromlife adventures with my roommates. 我的大学生活充满了与室友们的兄弟情谊冒险。|  He really values his bromlife with his best friends. 他非常珍视与他最好的朋友们的兄弟情谊。 | 近义词: brotherhood, camaraderie, mateship | 反义词: estrangement, animosity | 词性: 名词

词源:  A portmanteau of "bro" (short for "brother") and "life."  Relatively recent slang, emerging in the late 20th/early 21st century with the rise of internet culture.
