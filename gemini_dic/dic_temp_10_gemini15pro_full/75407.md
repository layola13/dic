fructose:/ˈfrʌktoʊs/ (US) /ˈfrʌktəʊs/ (UK)| n.|果糖|A simple sugar found in honey and fruit. It is a monosaccharide and an isomer of glucose.|Fructose is sweeter than glucose.|例句：
Fructose is found naturally in many fruits. 果糖天然存在于许多水果中。

Fructose is often used as a sweetener in processed foods. 果糖经常被用作加工食品中的甜味剂。


|近义词：levulose (technical term)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
