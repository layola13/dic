goo:/ɡuː/
n.|黏性物质，感伤，伤感|a sticky or slimy substance; sentimental or mawkish affection or feeling|The mechanic used goo to seal the leak. 机械师用黏性物质来密封泄漏处。|近义词:slime, gunk, muck, sentimentalism|反义词:dryness, unsentimental|词性:名词

goo:/ɡuː/
vi.|变得感伤|to become sentimental or mawkish|She started to goo over the old photographs. 她开始对老照片伤感起来。|近义词:slober, gush|反义词:control oneself|词性:动词
