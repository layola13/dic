bilaterally:/ˌbaɪˈlætərəli/
adverb|双边地，双方地|involving two parties or sides; mutually.|The two countries agreed to reduce their nuclear arsenals bilaterally.  这两个国家同意双边削减核武库。|
Synonym:mutually, reciprocally, jointly|Antonym:unilaterally, independently|词性:副词

bilaterally:/ˌbaɪˈlætərəli/
adjective|双边的，双方的|Having two sides or affecting two sides.|The agreement was reached bilaterally. 该协议是双边达成的。|Synonym:two-sided, mutual|Antonym:unilateral|词性:形容词
