lecanomancy: /ˌlɛkəˈnɒmənsi/ | n. | 油盘占卜；油面观测 | Divination by examining oil dropped into water, especially by interpreting the shapes or movements it makes. |  The ancient Greeks practiced lecanomancy to predict the future. 古希腊人通过油盘占卜来预测未来。

Lecanomancy was a common form of divination in ancient Babylonia. 油盘占卜是古代巴比伦一种常见的占卜形式。 | 近义词: hydromancy, divination | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, could consider "scientific prediction") | 词性: noun
