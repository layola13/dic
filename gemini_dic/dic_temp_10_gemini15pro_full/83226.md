gybing:/ˈdʒaɪbɪŋ/
n.|变换帆位（尤指顺风航行时）|The act of changing the course of a sailboat so that the stern, rather than the bow, passes through the wind.  This maneuver involves swinging the boom across the boat while sailing downwind.|The gybing was tricky in the strong winds.  在强风中变换帆位很困难。|jibing|tacking|noun
gybe:/dʒaɪb/
v.|变换帆位（尤指顺风航行时）|To change the course of a sailboat so that the stern, rather than the bow, passes through the wind.  This maneuver involves swinging the boom across the boat while sailing downwind.|We gybed several times to avoid the shallow water. 我们多次变换帆位以避开浅水区。|jibe|tack|verb
