nonperseverance:/ˌnɒnpɜːrsɪˈvɪərəns/| n.|缺乏毅力，不能坚持|lack of perseverance; inability to persist in an activity or task|He showed nonperseverance in his studies and failed most of his classes. 他学习缺乏毅力，大部分课程都不及格。|The team's nonperseverance led to their defeat in the championship game.  球队的缺乏毅力导致他们在冠军赛中失利。|近义词：instability, inconsistency, vacillation|反义词：perseverance, persistence, tenacity|词性:名词
