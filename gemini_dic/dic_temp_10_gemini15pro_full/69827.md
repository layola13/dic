ferrest: /ˈfɛrɪst/ | adj. | 野生的；未驯服的；凶猛的 | Of or pertaining to wild animals; untamed; fierce.  Derived from Latin "ferus" meaning "wild, untamed".  While "ferrest" appears in some older texts, it's archaic and rarely used in modern English.  "Feral" is a more common synonym. | The ferrest beast lurked in the shadows.  野兽潜伏在阴影中。 | Synonym: feral, savage, wild, untamed | Antonym: tame, domesticated, civilized | Adjective
