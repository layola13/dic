canaanitish:/ˈkeɪnənˌaɪtɪʃ/| adj. n.|迦南的；迦南人的；迦南人|Relating to Canaan or its people.  Also used as a noun to refer to a Canaanite person. |The archaeologist unearthed Canaanitish pottery shards. 考古学家出土了迦南人的陶器碎片。|The Canaanitish religion was polytheistic. 迦南人的宗教是多神教的。|近义词：Phoenician (sometimes), ancient Palestinian|反义词：Israelite|词性：形容词, 名词
Etymology->
From Canaan + -ite + -ish. Canaan comes from Hebrew כְּנַעַן (kənáʕan).
