boarders:/ˈbɔːrdərz/
n.
寄宿生；寄膳者；登船者；登机者
People who live and receive meals at a school, college, or other institution, or those who board a ship or aircraft.
The school has accommodation for 500 boarders. 这所学校可容纳500名寄宿生。

The boarders were waiting to embark the ship. 寄宿生们正在等待登船。

pupils, students, residents
day students, commuters

n.
(滑雪等运动用的)板
A long, thin piece of wood, plastic, or metal used in sports like snowboarding.
He waxed his boarders before hitting the slopes. 他在去滑雪坡之前给滑雪板打了蜡。

snowboards, skis, surfboards
