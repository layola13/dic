leachability:/ˌliːtʃəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可滤取性；沥滤性 | The capability of a substance to be leached or dissolved out, especially by percolating liquid.  The ease with which a substance is leached from a material. | The leachability of the toxic waste is a major concern.  这种有毒废物的可滤取性是一个主要问题。 |  solubility, extractability | insolubility | noun
