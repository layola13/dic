infraspinatus: /ˌɪnfrəspaɪˈneɪtəs/ /ˌɪnfrəspaɪˈnɑːtəs/
infraspinatus: n.|冈下肌|A thick triangular muscle, which occupies the chief part of the infraspinatous fossa; it is covered by a dense fascia which is attached to the margins of the fossa. It rotates the arm laterally and assists in holding the head of the humerus in the glenoid cavity. |The infraspinatus muscle is one of the four rotator cuff muscles. | 近义词: rotator cuff muscle | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific muscle)
词性: Noun

infraspinate: adj.|冈下的| Situated below the spine (of the scapula).|The infraspinate fossa is located on the posterior aspect of the scapula, below the spine. |近义词: subscapular | 反义词: supraspinate
