exedra:/ˈɛksɪdrə/| n.|露天座位; 室外座位; 半圆形休息处|A semicircular, often roofed, recess, usually with raised seating, as in a classical theater or a garden. |The guests gathered in the exedra overlooking the gardens. 客人们聚集在可以俯瞰花园的露天座位上。|The exedra provided a shady spot to rest during the hot afternoon. 露天座位提供了一个在炎热的下午休息的阴凉处。|近义词：alcove, niche, recess|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) open area, exposed area|词性: noun
