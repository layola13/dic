fragile:/ˈfrædʒəl/ (US) /ˈfrædʒaɪl/ (UK)| adj.|易碎的，脆弱的|Easily broken or damaged; delicate and easily destroyed.|例句：The vase is fragile, so handle it with care.  花瓶易碎，所以要小心搬运。

例句：Her health has always been fragile.  她的健康一直很脆弱。

近义词：breakable, delicate, frail, weak|反义词：strong, robust, sturdy, resilient|词性:形容词
