outjinx:/ˌaʊtˈdʒɪŋks/
verb
以更强的魔法胜过；比...更邪恶
To surpass someone in the use of jinxes or bad luck charms; to be more effective at bringing bad luck upon someone than they are at bringing bad luck upon you.
She outjinxed her rival by wearing a more powerful amulet. 她戴着更强大的护身符，胜过了她的对手。
Synonyms: outhex, outcharm
Antonyms: bless, protect
词性: 动词
