consistory:/kənˈsɪstəri/ |n.|宗教法庭；教会会议；枢密院|A consistory is a church council or court, especially in the Roman Catholic Church, or a meeting of such a body. |The consistory met to discuss the appointment of a new bishop. 宗教法庭开会讨论任命一位新主教。|The Pope addressed the consistory on the issue of climate change. 教皇在枢密会议上就气候变化问题发表了讲话。|synonym: council, assembly, synod, court |antonym:  (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "dissolution" in the context of a meeting) |noun
