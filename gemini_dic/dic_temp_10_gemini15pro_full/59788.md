ecotourism: /ˈiːkəʊˌtʊərɪzəm/ (UK) /ˈiːkoʊˌtʊrɪzəm/ (US)
|n.|生态旅游|Tourism directed toward exotic, often threatened, natural environments, especially to support conservation efforts and observe wildlife.|例句：Ecotourism is becoming increasingly popular as people seek more sustainable travel options. 生态旅游正变得越来越受欢迎，因为人们正在寻求更可持续的旅行选择。|例句：Costa Rica is a popular destination for ecotourism, offering visitors a chance to explore rainforests and see diverse wildlife. 哥斯达黎加是生态旅游的热门目的地，为游客提供了探索雨林和观赏多样野生动物的机会。|近义词：sustainable tourism, green tourism, nature tourism|反义词：mass tourism|词性：名词

Etymology:  eco- + tourism.  "Eco-" is a combining form representing ecology or environment, from Greek oikos "house, dwelling place, habitat."  "Tourism" refers to travel for pleasure or business.
