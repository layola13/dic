femalize:/ˈfiːmeɪlaɪz/ | v. |使女性化；赋予女性特征|To make or become female; to give something female characteristics. |例句：Some writers have tried to femalize the language by using feminine pronouns more often. 一些作家试图通过更频繁地使用女性代词来使语言女性化。

例句：The company's marketing strategy was criticized for femalizing its products to appeal to a narrow demographic. 该公司的营销策略因将其产品女性化以吸引狭窄的人群而受到批评。|近义词：feminize|反义词: masculinize |词性:动词

词源:  From female + -ize.  The suffix "-ize" is used to form verbs meaning "to make or become like" or "to cause to be or conform to." Therefore, "femalize" literally means "to make female" or "to cause to become female."
