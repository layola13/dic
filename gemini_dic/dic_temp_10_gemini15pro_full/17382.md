beadswoman:/ˈbiːdzˌwʊmən/
n.
念珠妇人；受施舍并为施主祈祷的妇女
A woman who is paid to pray for others, often supported by charity.
The old beadswoman knelt in the chapel, murmuring her prayers. 老妇人在教堂里跪着，低声祈祷。
近义词:prayer, supplicant
反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly benefactor)
词性:noun
