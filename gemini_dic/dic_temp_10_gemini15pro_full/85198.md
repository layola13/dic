hari-kari:/ˌhɑːriˈkɑːri/ /ˌhærɪˈkɑːri/
n.
切腹自杀;自杀
ritual suicide by disembowelment with a sword, formerly practiced in Japan by samurai as a way of avoiding dishonor.
例句：He committed hari-kari rather than face dishonor.  他为了避免蒙羞而切腹自杀。

例句：Hari-kari was a common practice among samurai in feudal Japan.  切腹是日本封建时代武士中常见的做法。

近义词：suicide, self-immolation
反义词：(No direct antonym, consider "survival", "living")
词性：noun
