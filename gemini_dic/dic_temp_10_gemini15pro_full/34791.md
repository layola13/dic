climature: /ˈklaɪmətʃər/ | n. | 气候;气候区;气候带;气候类型 | The overall average weather conditions of a place over a long period of time, including temperature, precipitation, humidity, wind, etc.  It also refers to a region with particular climatic conditions. | The climature of this region is characterized by hot summers and mild winters. 这个地区的气候特点是夏季炎热，冬季温和。

The climature of the Amazon rainforest is hot and humid. 亚马逊雨林的气候炎热潮湿。 | 近义词: climate | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From "climate" + "-ure" (a suffix forming nouns, often abstract, from verbs or other nouns).  Ultimately from Latin "clīma, clīmatis" (slope, region, zone).
