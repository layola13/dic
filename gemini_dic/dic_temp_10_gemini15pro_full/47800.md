decapodous:/dɪˈkæpədəs/| adj.|十足的，十脚的|Having ten legs or feet, especially relating to crustaceans like crabs, lobsters, and shrimp.  Relating to the order Decapoda.|例句：The decapodous crustacean scuttled across the ocean floor.  这只十足的甲壳类动物匆匆穿过海底。

例句：Decapodous creatures are a vital part of the marine ecosystem. 十足动物是海洋生态系统的重要组成部分。|近义词：ten-legged, ten-footed|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider terms like "few-legged" in specific contexts describing other organisms)|词性: adjective
