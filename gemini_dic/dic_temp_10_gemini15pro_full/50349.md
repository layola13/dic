depolarizing:/diːˈpoʊləˌraɪzɪŋ/
adjective
去极化的，除极的
reducing polarity, especially reducing the voltage difference across the cell membrane of a nerve or muscle cell.
The depolarizing effect of the drug caused the muscle to contract. 这种药物的去极化作用导致肌肉收缩。
synonyms: stimulating, exciting
antonyms: hyperpolarizing, repolarizing
词性: adjective


depolarize:/diːˈpoʊləˌraɪz/
verb
去极化，除极
to reduce polarity, especially to reduce the voltage difference across the cell membrane of a nerve or muscle cell.
The drug depolarizes the nerve cell, allowing it to fire.  这种药物使神经细胞去极化，使其能够放电。
synonyms: stimulate, excite
antonyms: hyperpolarize, repolarize
词性: verb


depolarization:/diːˌpoʊləraɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
去极化，除极
the process of reducing polarity, especially the reduction of the voltage difference across the cell membrane of a nerve or muscle cell.
Depolarization is essential for nerve impulse transmission. 去极化对于神经冲动的传递至关重要。
synonyms: stimulation, excitation
antonyms: hyperpolarization, repolarization
词性: noun
