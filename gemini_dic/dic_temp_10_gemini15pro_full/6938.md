and:/ænd/ /ənd/
conjunction
和，并且，及
A conjunction that connects words, phrases, or clauses.
She likes apples and bananas. 她喜欢苹果和香蕉。
He went to the store and bought some milk. 他去了商店，买了些牛奶。
近义词: plus, also, as well as, too
反义词: but, yet, or
词性: 连词
