dopper:/ˈdɒpər/ (UK), /ˈdɑːpər/ (US)| n.| 多普勒，多普勒效应|A device, especially a radar device, that uses the Doppler effect.|The police officer used a dopper to track the speeding car. 警察用多普勒雷达追踪超速车辆。|Example: The meteorologists used a dopper radar to track the storm. 气象学家使用多普勒雷达跟踪风暴。|Synonyms: radar, sensor|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
