ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English astrologien, from Old French astrologie, from Latin astrologia, from Ancient Greek ἀστρολογία (astrología), from ἄστρον (ástron, “star”) + -λογία (-logía, “-logy”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂stḗr (“star”).
