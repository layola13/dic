dibbel:/ˈdɪbəl/ | n. | 穴播器，移植铲 | A small, hand-held tool used for making holes in the ground for planting seeds or seedlings. | She used a dibbel to plant the lettuce seedlings. 她用穴播器种下了莴苣苗。| synonym: dibbler, planting tool | antonym: N/A | noun

dibbel:/ˈdɪbəl/ | v. | 穴播，用穴播器播种 | To plant seeds or seedlings using a dibbel. | He dibbled the seeds into the prepared soil. 他用穴播器将种子播种到准备好的土壤中。| synonym: plant, sow | antonym: N/A | verb
