bioeconomy:/ˌbaɪoʊɪˈkɑːnəmi/ | n. |生物经济|An economy based on the sustainable production and utilization of biological resources, including crops, forests, marine resources, and microorganisms, to produce food, energy, materials, and other products.| The development of the bioeconomy is crucial for a sustainable future.  生物经济的发展对于可持续未来至关重要。

The government is investing heavily in bioeconomy research. 政府正在大力投资生物经济研究。|近义词：bio-based economy|反义词:|noun
