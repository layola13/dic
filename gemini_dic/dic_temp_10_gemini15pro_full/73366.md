forego:/fɔːrˈɡoʊ/ (US) /fɔːˈɡəʊ/ (UK)| v. |放弃，摒弃|To choose not to have or do something, especially something enjoyable or pleasant.| She decided to forego dessert in order to save room for dinner. 她决定不吃甜点，以便留着肚子吃正餐。|

He forewent the opportunity to study abroad. 他放弃了出国留学的机会。


|Synonyms: abstain from, relinquish, forgo, waive, renounce|Antonyms: indulge in, partake in, embrace, accept|verb|
