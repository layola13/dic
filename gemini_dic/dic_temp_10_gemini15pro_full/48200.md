decompressor:/diːkəmˈprɛsər/| n.|减压器，解压缩器|A device or program that reduces the pressure of a gas or expands compressed data.|The file was too large to send, so I used a decompressor.  这个文件太大无法发送，所以我使用了一个解压缩器。|synonyms: expanders, inflators|antonyms: compressors, compactors|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + compress + -or (suffix forming nouns denoting an agent or device).  Ultimately from Latin com- (together) + premere (to press).
