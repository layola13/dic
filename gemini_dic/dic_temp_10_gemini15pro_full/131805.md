nonconsensual:/ˌnɑːnkənˈsɛnʃuəl/ | adj. | 未经同意的，非自愿的 | Not agreed to or allowed by the person or people involved. |  The police are investigating a report of nonconsensual sexual activity. 警方正在调查一起未经同意的性行为报告。|  She was subjected to nonconsensual medical treatment. 她被迫接受了未经她同意的医疗。| 近义词: involuntary, forced, coerced, against one's will | 反义词: consensual, voluntary, willing | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + consensual (from consensus, meaning "agreement")
