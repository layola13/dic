commandeer:/ˌkɒmənˈdɪə(r)/ /ˌkɑːmənˈdɪr/| vt.| 征用，强征；霸占|To seize (private property) for military or official use.  To take control of something for your own use, especially without permission.|The army commandeered all the vehicles in the town.  军队征用了镇上所有的车辆。|synonyms: seize, confiscate, appropriate, requisition, expropriate|antonyms: return, relinquish, surrender|verb

commandeer:/ˌkɒmənˈdɪə(r)/ /ˌkɑːmənˈdɪr/| n.| 征用；霸占|The act of commandeering.|The commandeer of the vehicle was unexpected. 对车辆的征用出乎意料。|synonyms: seizure, confiscation, appropriation, requisition, expropriation|antonyms: return, relinquishment, surrender|noun
