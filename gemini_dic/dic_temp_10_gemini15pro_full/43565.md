crazy-quilts:/ˈkreɪzi kwɪlts/| n.| 百衲被；拼布被 |A type of quilt made from various shapes and sizes of fabric pieces sewn together randomly, often embellished with embroidery and other decorative stitching.| My grandmother made a beautiful crazy-quilt from scraps of old dresses. 我祖母用旧衣服的碎片做了一床漂亮的百衲被。| 近义词: patchwork quilt, scrap quilt | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps a plain quilt) | 词性: noun


crazy-quilt:/ˈkreɪzi kwɪlt/| adj.| 拼凑的；杂乱无章的 |Resembling a crazy quilt in being made up of diverse and often incongruous elements.| The government's crazy-quilt policies on immigration have caused confusion. 政府在移民问题上拼凑的政策引起了混乱。| 近义词:  patchwork, haphazard, jumbled | 反义词: uniform, consistent, organized | 词性: adjective
