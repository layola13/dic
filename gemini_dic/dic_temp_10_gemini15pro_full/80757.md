gossamer:/ˈɡɒsəmə(r)/ (US) /ˈɡɑːsəmər/| n. adj.| 薄纱，蛛丝；轻薄的，虚无缥缈的 |A fine, filmy substance consisting of cobwebs spun by small spiders, which is seen especially in autumn. Also, something light, delicate, or insubstantial.|  The dewy gossamer draped the fields in the early morning. 清晨，沾着露水的蛛丝覆盖着田野。|近义词：cobweb, filament, delicate, ethereal, diaphanous|反义词：thick, heavy, substantial|词性: 名词, 形容词


gossamers:/ˈɡɒsəməz/ (US) /ˈɡɑːsəmərz/| n. | 薄纱，蛛丝（复数）|Fine, filmy cobwebs spun by small spiders, often seen in autumn, or something light, delicate, or insubstantial.| The gossamers glistened in the sunlight. 蛛丝在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：cobwebs, filaments|反义词：N/A|词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *gosomer, from Old French gossampine ("cotton tree"), from Medieval Latin gossipium, from Arabic قُطْن‎ (quṭn, “cotton”).
