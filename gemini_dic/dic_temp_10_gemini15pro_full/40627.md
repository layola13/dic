convented:/kənˈvɛntɪd/
verb
召集的，集合的 (past participle of convene)
Brought together for a meeting or assembly.
The duly convented members of the committee voted on the proposal.  正式召集的委员会成员对提案进行了投票。
近义词: assembled, gathered, summoned
反义词: dispersed, scattered
词性: verb (past participle)

convene:/kənˈviːn/
verb
召集，集合
To come together or cause to come together for a meeting or activity; assemble.
The chairman convened the meeting at 10 a.m. 主席在上午10点召集了会议。
近义词: assemble, gather, summon
反义词: adjourn, dismiss, disperse
词性: verb
