parergy:/ˈpærərdʒi/| n.| 副变应性，副过敏反应|A state of altered reactivity in which an allergic reaction can be elicited by a nonspecific irritant in a subject previously sensitized by a specific allergen.  A heightened sensitivity of the skin to irritants following recovery from dermatitis, especially one of allergic origin.  |Example 1: The patient exhibited parergy after recovering from contact dermatitis.  病人从接触性皮炎中恢复后表现出副变应性。

Example 2:  Parergy can make it difficult to determine the true cause of a skin reaction. 副变应性会使确定皮肤反应的真正原因变得困难。|Near Synonyms: hypersensitivity, altered reactivity|Antonyms: hyposensitivity, tolerance|Noun
