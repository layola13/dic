anatiferous:/ˌænəˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
产雁鹅的；长藤壶的
Producing barnacles; bearing or appearing to bear barnacles.
The anatiferous rocks were covered in small shellfish. 这些产藤壶的岩石上覆盖着小型贝类。
Near synonyms: barnacle-bearing, barnacle-encrusted
No commonly used antonyms
adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ānatifer, from ānas (“duck, goose”) + -fer (“bearing”).  The word arose from the medieval belief that barnacles grew into barnacle geese.
