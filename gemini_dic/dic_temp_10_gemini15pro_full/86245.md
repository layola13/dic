heave-ho:/ˈhiːv hoʊ/ | n. | 提升；用力扔；解雇；逐出 | The act of heaving something or someone, especially with great effort; dismissal from a job or position. | The sailors gave the heavy anchor a heave-ho. 水手们一起用力拉起了沉重的锚。 | 近义词：dismissal, ejection, expulsion, heave | 反义词： hiring, employment | 词性:名词

heave-ho:/ˈhiːv hoʊ/ | vt. | 举起；扔；解雇；逐出 | To lift or throw something heavy with effort; to dismiss someone from a job or position. | They heave-hoed the old sofa out the window. 他们把旧沙发从窗户扔了出去。| 近义词：dismiss, fire, sack, eject, expel | 反义词： hire, employ | 词性:动词

词源: From the nautical phrase "heave ho," used as a rhythmic chant by sailors while hauling ropes or lifting heavy objects. The phrase later extended its meaning to include dismissal, perhaps metaphorically relating the act of throwing something overboard to getting rid of someone.
