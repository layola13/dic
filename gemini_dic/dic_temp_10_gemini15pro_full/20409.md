bited:/ˈbɪtɪd/ | v. |咬过；被咬的 (past participle of bite)|The mosquito-bited arm was swollen and itchy. 被蚊子叮咬的手臂又肿又痒。|例句：The apple was bitten. 苹果被咬了一口。|近义词：chewed, gnawed, nipped|反义词:released, untouched|词性:动词

词源：From Old English *bītan, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch bijten and German beißen.
