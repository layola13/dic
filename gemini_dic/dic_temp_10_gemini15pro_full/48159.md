decommercializing: /ˌdiːkəˈmɜːʃəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
去商业化
The process of removing commercial elements or influences from something, typically in order to make it less focused on profit and more accessible or focused on other values.
The government is decommercializing public broadcasting. 政府正在将公共广播去商业化。
They are decommercializing the holiday by focusing on its historical significance. 他们通过关注其历史意义来去除节日的商业色彩。
近义词: demonetizing, deprivatizing
反义词: commercializing, monetizing
词性: verb
