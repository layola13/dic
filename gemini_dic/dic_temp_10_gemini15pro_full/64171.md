equate:/ɪˈkweɪt/| v.|使相等；等同；认为…相等|To consider something to be the same as or equivalent to something else.|He equates wealth with happiness. 他把财富等同于幸福。|近义词：equalize, compare, liken|反义词:differentiate, distinguish|词性:动词
词源:Late Middle English: from Latin aequat- ‘made equal’, from the verb aequare, from aequus ‘equal’.
