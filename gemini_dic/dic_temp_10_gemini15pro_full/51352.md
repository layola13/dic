detent:/ˈdiːtɛnt/ /dɪˈtɛnt/| n.|制动器，棘爪，卡榫，停止|A detent is a mechanism that temporarily locks or stops the movement of a part, typically in a machine or device.  It can be a small latch, catch, or notch that holds something in place.|The detent on the gear shift prevents accidental changes in gear.  变速杆上的卡榫防止意外换挡。

The camera lens has a detent at each focal length setting. 相机镜头在每个焦距设置处都有一个卡榫。|synonyms: catch, latch, click, stop, pawl|antonyms: release, accelerator|noun
