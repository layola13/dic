fortifiable:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪəbl/
adj.
可加强的，可筑防御工事的
Capable of being fortified or strengthened; able to be made more resistant to attack.
The city's location made it easily fortifiable. 这座城市的位置使其易于设防。
The report recommended fortifiable measures to protect the network from cyberattacks. 报告建议采取可加强的措施来保护网络免受网络攻击。
近义词: defensible, securable, reinforcible
反义词: vulnerable, weak, indefensible
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Middle English *fortifiable, from Old French fortifiable, from fortifier + -able. Ultimately from Latin fortificare (“to strengthen”).
