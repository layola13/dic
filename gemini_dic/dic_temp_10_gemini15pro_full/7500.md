annalistic:/ˌænəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
编年史的，纪年的
pertaining to annals or annalists; resembling annals; chronicling events year by year
例句：The annalistic accounts of the dynasty provide a valuable historical record.  王朝的编年史记录提供了宝贵的历史资料。

例句：He adopted an annalistic approach to writing his family history. 他采用编年史的方式来撰写家族史。
近义词：chronicled, historical, chronological
反义词：ahistorical
词性: adjective
