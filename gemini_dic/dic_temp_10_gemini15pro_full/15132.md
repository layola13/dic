bacillary:/bəˈsɪləri/
adjective
杆菌的，由杆菌引起的
Relating to, caused by, or containing bacilli (rod-shaped bacteria).
Bacillary dysentery is a serious health concern in many developing countries. 杆菌性痢疾是许多发展中国家的一个严重健康问题。
The doctor suspected a bacillary infection. 医生怀疑是杆菌感染。
近义词: bacterial
反义词: (No direct antonym, but could use terms like "viral" or "fungal" if contrasting infection types)
词性: adjective
