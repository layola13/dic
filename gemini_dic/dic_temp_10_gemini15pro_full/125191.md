multicellularities:/ˌmʌltɪˌsɛljʊˈlærɪtiz/ | n. |多细胞性（复数）| The state or quality of being multicellular; the presence of multiple cells in an organism. |  Multicellularities evolved independently in several lineages.  多种细胞性在几个谱系中独立进化。 |  The study of multicellularities offers insights into the evolution of complex life. 对多细胞性的研究为了解复杂生命的进化提供了见解。 | 近义词: multicellularity | 反义词: unicellularity | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From multi- (many) + cellular (relating to cells) + -ity (state or quality) + -es (plural suffix).
