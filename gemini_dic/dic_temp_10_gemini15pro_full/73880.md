fornicatress:/ˈfɔːrnɪkeɪtrɪs/
noun
淫妇；娼妓
A woman who engages in unlawful sexual intercourse.
She was accused of being a fornicatress. 她被指控为淫妇。
The Puritans condemned the fornicatress. 清教徒谴责了那个淫妇。
Synonyms: adulteress, whore, prostitute, harlot
Antonyms: virgin, chaste woman
