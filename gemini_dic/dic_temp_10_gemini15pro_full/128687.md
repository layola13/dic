nidamental:/ˌnaɪdəˈmɛntəl/
adjective
产卵的；与产卵有关的；由卵形成的
Relating to an egg, especially the outer covering of an egg.  Pertaining to the formation and laying of eggs. Formed of or by an egg.
The nidamental gland secretes the materials that form the eggshell.  产卵腺分泌形成蛋壳的物质。
近义词: oviparous, egg-laying
反义词: viviparous
