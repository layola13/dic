ice-skaters:/ˈaɪsˌskeɪtərz/| n.|滑冰者|People who skate on ice.  A person who engages in the activity of ice skating, either recreationally or professionally.|The ice-skaters glided effortlessly across the frozen lake. 滑冰者毫不费力地在结冰的湖面上滑行。|  The ice-skaters performed a dazzling routine. 滑冰运动员表演了一套令人眼花缭乱的节目。 |synonyms: skaters, figure skaters (if figure skating), hockey players (if playing hockey)|antonyms: non-skaters|noun
