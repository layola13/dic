dialogize:/ˈdaɪəlɒdʒaɪz/ | v. | 使对话化，使对话式 | To put into the form of a dialogue; to represent in dialogue. |  They dialogized the story to make it more engaging for the readers. 他们将故事对话化，以使其更吸引读者。

| Example 1: The playwright dialogized the narrative, transforming the lengthy descriptions into lively conversations. 剧作家将叙事对话化，把冗长的描述变成了生动的对话。

| Example 2:  The teacher dialogized the lesson, encouraging students to discuss the topic amongst themselves. 老师将课程对话化，鼓励学生们互相讨论这个话题。

| 近义词: dramatize, fictionalize | 反义词: narrate, summarize | 词性: verb
