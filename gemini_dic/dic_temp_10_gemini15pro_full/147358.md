overnationalization:/ˌoʊvərˌnæʃənələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 超国家化，超国家主义 | The process of transferring power from national governments to supranational organizations.  | The overnationalization of Europe has been a gradual process. 欧洲的超国家化是一个渐进的过程。| Synonyms: supranationalism, integration | Antonyms: nationalism, isolationism | noun


overnationalize:/ˌoʊvərˌnæʃənələˈzaɪz/ | v. | 使超国家化 | To transfer power or authority from national governments to a supranational organization. | Some argue that overnationalizing certain aspects of policy can weaken national sovereignty.  有些人认为某些政策方面的超国家化会削弱国家主权。| Synonyms: federalize, integrate | Antonyms: nationalize, localize | verb
