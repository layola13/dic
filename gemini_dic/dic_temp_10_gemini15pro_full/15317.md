backsettler:/ˈbækˌsetlə(r)/ /ˈbækˌsedlər/
noun
拓荒者，偏远地区居民
A person who lives in a remote or sparsely populated area, especially one who has moved there from a more developed region.
The backsettlers faced many hardships, including isolation and lack of access to resources. 拓荒者们面临许多困难，包括与世隔绝和缺乏资源。
settler, pioneer, frontiersman, homesteader
city dweller, urbanite
