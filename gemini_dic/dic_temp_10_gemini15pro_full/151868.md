passiontide: /ˈpæʃ(ə)ntaɪd/
noun
受难周，受难节期
The period of two weeks before Easter, commemorating the Passion of Christ.
例句：Services during Passiontide are often somber and reflective.  受难周期间的礼拜仪式通常是忧郁和反思的。

例句：The church was decorated simply for Passiontide. 教堂为了受难周而简单地装饰了一下。

近义词：Holy Week, Lent (partially)
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider Eastertide)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *Passiuntide, ultimately from Latin passio (“suffering”) + tid (“time”).  Relates to the Passion of Christ.
