nonsocialistic:/ˌnɒnˈsəʊʃəlɪstɪk/ |adj.| 非社会主义的|Not adhering to or based on the principles of socialism.|例句：The country adopted a nonsocialistic economic model.  这个国家采用了非社会主义的经济模式。

例句：Many nonsocialistic countries have robust welfare systems. 许多非社会主义国家拥有健全的福利体系。|近义词：capitalist, free-market|反义词：socialist, communist|词性: adjective
词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + socialistic (derived from socialism)
