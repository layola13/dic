misjudge:/ˌmɪsˈdʒʌdʒ/ | v. | 判断错误，误判 | To form a wrong opinion or make a wrong judgment about someone or something. | She misjudged the distance and drove off the road. 她错误地判断了距离，把车开出了路。|  He completely misjudged her character. 他完全误判了她的性格。 | 近义词：miscalculate, underestimate, overestimate, misread | 反义词: judge correctly, assess accurately | 词性: 动词
