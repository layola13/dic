lowballed:/ˌloʊˈbɔːld/| v. |低估，低价报价|To offer a price or value that is significantly lower than what is considered fair or reasonable.  It originates from "low ball," a pitch in baseball thrown underhand and close to the ground.|They lowballed me on my first offer, so I countered with a higher price. 他们第一次报价太低了，所以我提出了更高的价格。|underbid, undervalue|overbid, overvalue|verb

lowball:/ˈloʊˌbɔːl/| n. |低价，低估|A low offer or estimate.|The initial lowball offer was insulting. 最初的低价报价简直是侮辱。|underbid|overbid|noun

lowball:/ˈloʊˌbɔːl/| v. |低估，低价报价|To make a low offer or estimate.|The seller felt lowballed by the buyer's offer. 卖家觉得买家的报价太低了。|underbid, undervalue|overbid, overvalue|verb
