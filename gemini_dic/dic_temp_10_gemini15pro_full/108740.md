leapin:/ˈliːpɪn/| adj.|极好的，很棒的|An informal adjective used to describe something excellent or very good.  It's a variation of "leaping," suggesting something so good it makes you want to leap for joy.|That new car is leapin'! 那辆新车棒极了！|近义词：fantastic, awesome, excellent, great, superb, wonderful|反义词：terrible, awful, dreadful, bad, poor|adjective
