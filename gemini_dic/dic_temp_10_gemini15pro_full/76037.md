furlable:/ˈfɜːləbl/| adj.|可卷起的，可收拢的|Able to be furled (rolled up or folded neatly).|The yacht had a furlable jib, making it easy to handle in strong winds. 这艘游艇有一个可卷起的三角帆，因此在大风中很容易操作。|The boat's furlable sails made it easy to stow. 这艘船可折叠的帆使它很容易收起。|近义词：retractable, foldable, collapsible|反义词: unfurlable, fixed|词性:形容词

词源: From furl + -able. "Furl" comes from the Old French "ferler" meaning "to roll up."
