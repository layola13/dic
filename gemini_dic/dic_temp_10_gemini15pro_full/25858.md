burj:/bɜːrdʒ/ | n. | 塔，高楼 | A tall tower, often part of a fortified building.  Derived from the Arabic word "burj," meaning tower. |  The Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world. 哈利法塔是世界上最高的建筑。| synonym: tower, skyscraper | antonym: hut, shack | noun

burj:/bɜːrdʒ/ | n. |  堡垒 | A fortified building.  Derived from the Arabic word "burj," meaning tower, which historically were often part of fortifications. | The old burj offered little protection against modern weaponry.  古老的堡垒几乎无法抵御现代武器。| synonym: fortress, citadel | antonym:  village, unprotected area | noun
