high-pressuring:/ˌhaɪˈprɛʃərɪŋ/
adjective
高压的，强迫的
Exerting or involving intense pressure, especially to force someone to do something.
例句：The high-pressuring sales tactics made customers uncomfortable.  高压的销售策略让顾客感到不舒服。

例句：He felt high-pressuring to perform well in the important meeting. 他感到巨大的压力要在重要的会议上表现出色。

近义词：coercive, forceful, insistent, pushy
反义词：gentle, lenient, permissive, relaxed
词性: adjective
