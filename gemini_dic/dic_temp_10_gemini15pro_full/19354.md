bibliophilisms: /ˌbɪblɪəˈfɪlɪzəmz/ | n. | 藏书癖；爱书癖；书籍收藏 | The love of books, especially collecting them, often with an emphasis on rare or beautiful editions. | He indulged his bibliophilisms by collecting first editions of his favorite authors. 他通过收藏最喜欢的作家的初版来沉迷于他的藏书癖。 | 近义词: bibliomania, book collecting | 反义词: bibliophobia | 词性: noun


Etymology: From biblio- (book) + -phil- (loving) + -ism (state or quality of).  Ultimately from Greek *biblion* (book) and *philos* (loving).
