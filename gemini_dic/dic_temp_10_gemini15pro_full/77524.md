gazundering:/ɡæzˈʌndərɪŋ/
n.
压低房价（成交前一刻）
The act of lowering an offer on a house purchase at the last minute before exchange of contracts, often causing the seller considerable inconvenience.
The seller was furious about the gazundering. 卖家对买家临时压价感到愤怒。
近义词: price chipping, last-minute price reduction
反义词: gazumping
