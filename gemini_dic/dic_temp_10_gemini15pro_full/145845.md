overdemands:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈmændz/
|n.|过度需求；超额需求|Making excessive or unreasonable demands; demanding too much.  The constant overdemands of modern life can lead to burnout.|The job's overdemands left her feeling stressed and exhausted. 这份工作过高的要求让她感到压力巨大，精疲力竭。|Synonyms: excessive demands, unreasonable demands, exorbitant demands|Antonyms: reasonable demands, moderate demands|noun

overdemand:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈmænd/
|vt.|对…提出过高要求；使负担过重|To make excessive or unreasonable demands upon someone or something. The manager overdemanded his employees, leading to high turnover. | The project overdemanded the team's resources, causing delays. 这个项目对团队资源的需求过高，导致了延误。|Synonyms: overburden, overtax, strain|Antonyms: underdemand, underutilize|verb
