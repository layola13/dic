gleamy:/ˈɡliːmi/ | adj. |闪亮的，光亮的 | Shining brightly; lustrous. | The car had a gleaming, polished finish. 这辆车有着闪亮、抛光的外观。| The lake was gleamy in the morning sun.  清晨的阳光下，湖面波光粼粼。 | 近义词: shiny, bright, sparkling, glistening, radiant | 反义词: dull, matte, dim | 词性: adjective

词源: From gleam + -y.  Gleam itself comes from Old English *glǣm, from Proto-Germanic *glaimaz.
