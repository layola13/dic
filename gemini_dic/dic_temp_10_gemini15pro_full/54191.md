disestablishment's:/dɪsɪˈstæblɪʃmənts/
noun
解除建立；取消教会特权；政教分离
The act of disestablishing something, especially a church.  The noun form of "disestablishment", referring to the state of being disestablished, and showing possession.
The disestablishment's impact on the Church of England was profound.  英国教会脱离国家的影响深远。
The disestablishment's main goal was to separate church and state. 政教分离是取消教会特权的主要目标。
Synonyms: abolition, removal, termination
Antonyms: establishment, institution, founding
