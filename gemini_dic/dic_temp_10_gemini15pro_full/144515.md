outtricked:/ˌaʊtˈtrɪkt/ | v. | 胜过，以智取胜 | to defeat or surpass someone by being more clever or cunning | He outtricked his opponent in the final round of the chess match. 他在最后一轮棋局中以智取胜了他的对手。|Synonyms: outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver | Antonyms: be outwitted, be outsmarted | verb

outtricked:/ˌaʊtˈtrɪkt/ | adj. | 被胜过的，被以智取胜的 (rarely used as an adjective) | defeated or surpassed by someone more clever or cunning |  While uncommon, one might say "He felt completely outtricked after the negotiation." 他在谈判后感到完全被智取了。 | Synonyms: outsmarted, outwitted | Antonyms: clever, cunning | adjective
