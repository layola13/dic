electrolyzer:/ɪˈlɛktrəˌlaɪzər/| n. |电解器，电解槽|A device that uses electricity to decompose a chemical compound, typically by electrolysis.  It converts electrical energy into chemical energy.|The electrolyzer produced hydrogen gas from water. 电解器用水制取氢气。|Synonyms: electrolytic cell, electrolysis apparatus|Antonyms: fuel cell|noun
