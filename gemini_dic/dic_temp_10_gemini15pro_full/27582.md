canarians:/kəˈneəriənz/
noun
加那利群岛人；加那利群岛的居民
People who live in the Canary Islands or are from there.
The Canarians are known for their warm hospitality. 加那利群岛人以热情好客而闻名。
synonyms: Canary Islanders
antonyms: N/A
n.

canaries:/kəˈneəriənz/
noun
金丝雀（canary 的复数）
Small, yellow songbirds often kept as pets.
The canaries sang beautifully in the morning. 金丝雀在早晨唱得很动听。
synonyms:  serins, finches (more general)
antonyms: N/A
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *canariae (insulae) "Canary (Islands)," from canis "dog," because of the large dogs found there, according to Pliny the Elder.  The birds later took their name from the islands.
