nonabjuratory:/ˌnɒnˌæbdʒʊˈreɪtəri/
adjective
非放弃誓言的；不放弃信仰的
Not renouncing a belief or cause, especially one considered unorthodox or heretical.
The king demanded a nonabjuratory oath from his subjects, allowing them to maintain their religious beliefs. 国王要求臣民宣誓不放弃信仰，允许他们保持自己的宗教信仰。
Near synonyms:  unrepentant, steadfast, unwavering, loyal
Antonyms:  abjuratory, recanting, apostate, renouncing
词性: adjective
