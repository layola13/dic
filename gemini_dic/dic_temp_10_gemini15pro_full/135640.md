nonpreciously:/nɒnˈprɛʃəsli/| adv.|非贵重地，不珍贵地|In a way that is not precious or valuable.  It describes actions or states that are not done with great care, expense, or reverence, as one might with something precious.|She tossed the nonpreciously made trinket into her bag.|Synonym: casually, carelessly, ordinarily|Antonym: preciously, carefully, reverently|词性:副词
