apertural:/əˈpɜːrtʃərəl/
adjective
孔的；与孔有关的
relating to an aperture (an opening, hole, or gap).
The apertural size of the camera lens affects the amount of light entering.  相机镜头的孔径大小会影响进入的光线量。
The snail retreated into its shell, sealing the apertural opening.  蜗牛缩回壳里，封住了孔口。
近义词: opening, orifice, hole, gap
反义词: closed, sealed, blocked
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *apertura* ("opening") + *-al* (suffix forming adjectives).  源自拉丁语 *apertura*（“开口”）+ *-al*（构成形容词的后缀）。
