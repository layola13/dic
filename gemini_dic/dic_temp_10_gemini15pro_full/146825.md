overimpresses:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈprɛsɪz/
verb
使留下过深的印象；过分打动
to impress someone too much or to an excessive degree.
He overimpressed the interviewers with his technical jargon, making them feel inadequate. 他用他的技术术语给面试官留下了过深的印象，让他们感到自己不足。
overwhelm, overawe
