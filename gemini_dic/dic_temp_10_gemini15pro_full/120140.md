minatorially:/ˌmɪnəˈtɔːriəli/
| adv. | 以威胁的方式；以胁迫的方式 | in a threatening or intimidating manner |  He spoke minatorially, causing the others to shrink back. 他以威胁的口吻说话，导致其他人畏缩。 | 近义词: threateningly, menacingly, intimidatingly | 反义词: soothingly, gently, calmly | 词性: 副词

词源:  "minatorially" comes from the Latin word "minari," meaning "to threaten."  The suffix "-tor" forms agent nouns, and "-ial" forms adjectives.  "-ly" transforms the adjective into an adverb.
