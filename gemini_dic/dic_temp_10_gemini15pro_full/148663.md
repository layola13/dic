overtightly:/ˌoʊvərˈtaɪtli/| adv.|过紧地，过度紧地|Too tightly; excessively tight.|She screwed the lid on overtightly and couldn't get it off again. 她把盖子拧得太紧了，再也打不开了。|The bolts were overtightly fastened, causing the metal to warp. 螺栓拧得太紧，导致金属变形。|近义词：too tightly, excessively tightly|反义词:loosely, gently|词性:副词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "tightly" (adverb form of "tight")
