blacklist:/ˈblækˌlɪst/ | n. vt.| 黑名单；将…列入黑名单 | A list of people or things that are disapproved of or are to be avoided or rejected.  |  例句：The company blacklisted him after he was caught stealing.  这家公司在他被抓到偷窃后把他列入了黑名单。 | 近义词：blocklist, denylist | 反义词：whitelist | 词性：名词/动词

blacklist:/ˈblækˌlɪst/ | vt.| 将…列入黑名单 | To put someone or something on a blacklist. | 例句： Several websites have been blacklisted by the government. 一些网站被政府列入了黑名单。 | 近义词：ban, bar, exclude | 反义词：allow, permit, include | 词性：动词
