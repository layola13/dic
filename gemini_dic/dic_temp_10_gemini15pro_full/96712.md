ineffectuality:/ˌɪnɪfɛktʃuˈælɪti/
n.
无效；无用；徒劳
lack of power to produce a desired effect.
The ineffectuality of the new policy was apparent to everyone.  这项新政策的无效性对每个人来说都是显而易见的。
The committee’s ineffectuality stemmed from its inability to reach a consensus. 委员会的无效性源于其无法达成共识。

近义词:futility, inefficacy, impotence, uselessness
反义词:effectiveness, efficacy, potency
