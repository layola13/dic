| adj. |平静的，安静的|Not tumultuous; peaceful and quiet.|It was a nontumultuous gathering, unlike the previous year's protest.  这是一个平静的集会，不像去年的抗议。|例句：The meeting was surprisingly nontumultuous. 会议出奇地平静。|近义词：peaceful, calm, quiet, serene|反义词：tumultuous, boisterous, violent|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + tumultuous (characterized by tumult; noisy and disorderly) + -ly (adverb suffix) or -ous (adjective suffix).  "Tumultuous" ultimately derives from the Latin "tumultus" meaning "uproar" or "commotion."
