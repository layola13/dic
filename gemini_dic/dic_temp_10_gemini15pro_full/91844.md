hyperhilariousness:/ˌhaɪpərhɪˈlɛəriəsnəs/
|n.|极度滑稽，极度搞笑|The hyperhilariousness of the situation had everyone in stitches.  这种情况的极度滑稽让每个人都笑得前仰后合。|Extreme hilarity, extreme funniness|The comedian's performance was marked by its sheer hyperhilariousness.  这位喜剧演员的表演以其纯粹的极度滑稽为标志。|近义词: extreme hilarity, side-splitting humor|反义词: seriousness, solemnity|词性: noun

Etymology:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, beyond, excessive"
hilarious (adjective): from Greek "hilaros," meaning "cheerful, merry"
-ness (suffix): forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality
