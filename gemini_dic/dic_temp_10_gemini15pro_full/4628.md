alderman:/ˈɔːldərmən/ | n. | 市议员 | A member of a municipal council, especially in a large city.  |  The aldermen voted to approve the new budget. 市议员投票通过了新的预算。 | councilor, councilman |  (No common direct antonym) | noun

aldermen:/ˈɔːldərmən/ | n. | 市议员（复数） | Plural form of alderman. | The aldermen are meeting tonight to discuss the proposal.  市议员们今晚开会讨论这项提议。| councilors, councilmen | (No common direct antonym) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ealdormann "chief, prince, magistrate," literally "elder man," from ealdor "elder" (see elder (adj.)) + mann "man" (see man (n.)). Cognate with Old Saxon aldurman, Old Frisian alderman, Dutch ouderman, Old High German altman.
