competitor:/kəmˈpɛtɪtər/ (US) /kəmˈpetɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |竞争者，对手|A person, company, or team that competes against another.|例句：Our main competitor is a company called "Innovate Inc.". 我们的主要竞争对手是一家名为“Innovate Inc.”的公司。

例句：She finished the race ahead of her closest competitors. 她领先于最接近的竞争对手完成了比赛。

近义词：rival, opponent, challenger|反义词：ally, partner, collaborator|词性:noun
