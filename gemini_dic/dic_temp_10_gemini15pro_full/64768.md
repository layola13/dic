escutcheon:/ɪˈskʌtʃən/
n.
饰板; 盾形纹章; 钥匙孔盖; 名誉;
A flat piece of metal for ornament or protection, typically around a keyhole, door handle, or light switch.  An escutcheon can also refer to a shield or emblem bearing a coat of arms, or figuratively, to one's reputation or honor.
The antique desk featured ornate brass escutcheons.  这古董桌子上有华丽的黄铜饰板。
His family's escutcheon boasted a proud history of military service. 他的家族纹章上记载着光荣的军旅生涯。
synonym: shield, emblem, crest, badge, name, reputation
antonym: dishonor, disgrace, shame, stigma
