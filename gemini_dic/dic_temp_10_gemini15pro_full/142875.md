out-of-luck:/ˌaʊt əv ˈlʌk/
adjective
运气不好的，倒霉的
Unfortunate; having bad luck.  Not able to have something or do something that you wanted.
I was out of luck—the last ticket had just been sold. 我运气不好——最后一张票刚刚卖出。
Synonyms: unfortunate, unlucky, jinxed
Antonyms: lucky, fortunate, charmed
词性: 形容词
