novelette:/ˌnɒvəˈlɛt/ | n. | 中篇小说 | A short novel, typically one that is longer than a short story but shorter than a novel. |  Example: She wrote a charming novelette about a young woman's adventures in Paris. 她写了一部关于年轻女子在巴黎冒险的迷人中篇小说。

Example: The novelette was serialized in the magazine over several weeks. 这部中篇小说在杂志上连载了几个星期。| 近义词: novella, short novel | 反义词: novel (in the sense of being longer) | 词性: noun
