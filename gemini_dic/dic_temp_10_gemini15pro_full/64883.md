essayers:/eɪˈseɪərz/ | n. | 尝试者；试验者 |  People who try or attempt something. | The early essayers of flight faced many challenges. 早期的飞行尝试者面临许多挑战。 | 近义词: pioneers, experimenters, innovators | 反义词: followers, imitators | 词性: Noun (plural)
