abbotship: /ˈæbətʃɪp/ | n. | 男修道院院长职位；男修道院院长任期 | The office or tenure of an abbot.  The period during which an abbot holds office. |  His abbotship lasted for two decades.  他的修道院院长职位持续了二十年。

The abbotship of St. Albans was a powerful position.  圣奥尔本斯修道院的院长职位是一个很有权力的职位。 | 近义词: abbacy | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps deposition from abbotship) | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *abbodscīpe, from abbod ("abbot") +‎ -scīpe (suffix forming nouns denoting office, condition, or state).  The word "abbot" ultimately comes from Latin abbas, from Aramaic abbā ("father").
