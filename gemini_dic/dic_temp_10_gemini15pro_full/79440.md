glitchy:/ˈɡlɪtʃi/
adjective
有小故障的；容易出故障的
Tending to suffer from glitches; malfunctioning.
The software is still a bit glitchy.  这个软件还有点小毛病。
近义词:buggy, faulty, malfunctioning
反义词:smooth, functional, working
词性:adjective
