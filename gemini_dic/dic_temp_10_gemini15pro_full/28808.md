caroteel: /ˌkærəˈtiːl/ | n. |旋转木马，轮播|A merry-go-round, typically a revolving circular platform with seats shaped like horses or other animals, on which people ride for amusement. | The children squealed with delight as they rode the caroteel. 孩子们在旋转木马上玩耍时高兴地尖叫。|The brightly lit caroteel was the centerpiece of the carnival. 灯火通明的旋转木马是嘉年华的中心.|近义词：merry-go-round, roundabout |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

词源:  From French *carrousel*, from Italian *carosello*, from *carosella* "little war," a tournament on horseback, from *carro* "car," from Latin *carrus* "car, cart."  The meaning shifted over time from a mock tournament to the rotating amusement ride.
