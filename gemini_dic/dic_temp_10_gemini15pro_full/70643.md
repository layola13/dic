filthies:/ˈfɪlθiz/
noun
肮脏的东西; 污秽之物
Plural form of filthy (as a noun).  Refers to extremely dirty or disgusting things or people, often used as a derogatory term.
The dog rolled around in the filthies. 这只狗在污秽之物里打滚。
Those filthies need to be cleaned up immediately. 那些脏东西需要马上清理干净。
Near Synonyms: dirt, grime, muck, scum
Opposite: cleanliness, purity
