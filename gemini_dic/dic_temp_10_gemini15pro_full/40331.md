contrabandista:/ˌkɒntrəbænˈdɪstə/ /ˌkɑːntrəbænˈdɪstə/| n. |走私者，走私贩|A person who smuggles goods illegally.|例句：The contrabandista was caught trying to cross the border with a truck full of illicit goods.  走私犯试图用一辆装满非法物品的卡车越过边境时被抓获。

例句：The contrabandistas operated a sophisticated network for moving stolen artifacts. 这些走私犯运作着一个复杂的网络来转移被盗文物。|近义词：smuggler, trafficker, runner|反义词: customs officer, law enforcement officer|词性: 名词
