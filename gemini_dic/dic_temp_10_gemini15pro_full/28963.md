carryable:/ˈkæriəbl/| adj.|可携带的，便携的|Able to be carried; portable.|例句：The suitcase is lightweight and carryable.  这个手提箱很轻，便于携带。

例句：Is this box carryable or do I need a trolley?  这个箱子可以搬动吗，还是需要手推车？

近义词：portable, transportable, manageable|反义词:uncarryable, cumbersome, bulky|词性:形容词
