naticoid: /ˈnætɪkɔɪd/ | adj. n. |  似玉螺的，玉螺超科的; 玉螺超科动物 | Resembling or belonging to the Naticidae, a family of predatory sea snails. |  The shell had a distinct naticoid appearance.  这贝壳有着明显的玉螺科特征。 |  Synonyms: moon snail-like (for the appearance), Naticidae (for the family) | Antonyms:  None | adjective, noun


naticoid: /ˈnætɪkɔɪd/ | n. | 玉螺超科动物 | A sea snail belonging to the superfamily Naticoidea. | Several naticoids were found in the intertidal zone.  在潮间带发现了几个玉螺超科动物。 | Synonyms: moon snail (a common name for some naticoids) | Antonyms: None | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *naticus ("buttocks-shaped," referring to the shape of the shell) + -oid ("resembling").  The Latin word *naticus is likely related to natis ("buttocks").
