gneissic: /ˈnaɪsɪk/ | adj. | 片麻岩的，片麻状的 | Resembling gneiss; having the banded or foliated structure of gneiss. |  The geologist identified the rock as gneissic. 地质学家将这块岩石鉴定为片麻岩。 | The cliff face was composed of gneissic rock.  悬崖表面由片麻岩构成。 | 近义词: foliated, banded, layered | 反义词: massive, homogeneous | 词性: adjective | 词源: From gneiss + -ic.  Gneiss comes from the German word Gneis, possibly related to the Middle High German verb gneist, meaning "to spark," referring to the glittering appearance of some types of gneiss.
