assumpsit:/əˈsʌmpsɪt/
noun
承诺; 承担义务; (尤指)简易诉讼;
A former common-law action for breach of a simple contract, either oral or written, not under seal.
The lawyer filed an assumpsit against the debtor.  律师对债务人提起了简易诉讼。
synonyms: undertaking, promise, pledge, commitment, vow, word, guarantee, assurance
antonyms: breach, refusal, denial, rejection
n.
