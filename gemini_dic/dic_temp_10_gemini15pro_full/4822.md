alhenna:/ælˈhɛnə/
noun
指甲花，散沫花，指甲花染料
A reddish-brown dye obtained from the henna plant, used for coloring hair, skin, and fingernails.
She used alhenna to create intricate patterns on her hands. 她用指甲花在她手上绘制了复杂的图案。
The bride's hands were decorated with alhenna.  新娘的手上装饰着指甲花。
synonyms: henna, henna dye
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a dye, but perhaps "bleach" in the context of hair coloring)
词性: noun
