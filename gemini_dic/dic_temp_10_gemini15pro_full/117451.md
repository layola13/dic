|例句：She became a meditatist after years of searching for inner peace.  在多年寻求内心平静之后，她成为了一名冥想者。 | 近义词：contemplator, thinker, reflector | 反义词: doer, activist |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From meditate + -ist.  "Meditate" comes from the Latin "meditari," meaning "to ponder."  "-ist" is a suffix forming agent nouns.
