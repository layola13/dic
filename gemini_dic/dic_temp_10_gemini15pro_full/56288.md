dogget:/ˈdɒɡɪt/| n.|杂物，不值钱的东西|A small worthless article; a trifle. |例句：He kept his desk cluttered with doggets. 他把他的桌子堆满了不值钱的小玩意儿。

例句：The antique shop was filled with intriguing doggets. 这家古董店里摆满了各种各样引人入胜的小玩意儿。|近义词：trinket, knick-knack, bauble, gewgaw|反义词:treasure, valuable|词性:名词


词源:

From Middle English *dogget, diminutive of dog, possibly influenced by Middle Dutch docke "doll."  The original meaning was likely a small toy or trinket, hence the current sense of a worthless article.
