anti-domestic:/ˌænti dəˈmɛstɪk/ | adj. | 反家庭的，反对家庭生活的 | Opposed to traditional domestic values or lifestyle; rejecting the idea of a traditional family structure or the roles typically associated with it. |  He has very anti-domestic views, believing that marriage and family life stifle individual freedom.  他持有非常反家庭的观点，认为婚姻和家庭生活扼杀了个人自由。

She led a rather anti-domestic existence, preferring travel and adventure to settling down. 她过着一种相当反家庭的生活，喜欢旅行和冒险，而不是安定下来。 |  non-domestic, unconventional, unorthodox  | domestic, traditional, conventional | adjective
