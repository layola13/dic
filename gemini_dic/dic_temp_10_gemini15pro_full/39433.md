connectednesses:/kəˈnɛktɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
相互关联，连通性
the state of being connected or interconnected.
The internet has increased the connectedness of the world. 互联网增强了世界的连通性。

The connectedness of the global economy makes international cooperation essential. 全球经济的相互关联使得国际合作至关重要。

近义词:interconnectedness, linkage, connection, network
反义词:isolation, separation, disconnection
词性:名词(plural noun)

词源:
From connected + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin connectere "to bind together".
