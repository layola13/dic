besprinkle:/bɪˈsprɪŋkl/
verb
撒，洒；点缀
to scatter small drops or particles of something on
The baker besprinkled the cake with powdered sugar.  面包师在蛋糕上撒了糖粉。

She besprinkled the salad with chopped nuts. 她把切碎的坚果撒在沙拉上。

近义词: sprinkle, scatter, dust, dredge
反义词: cover, coat
