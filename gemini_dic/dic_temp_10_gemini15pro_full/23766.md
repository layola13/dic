bravos:/ˈbrɑːvoʊz/ | n. |喝彩，叫好；欢呼| An exclamation of praise or approval, especially one shouted at a performance. | The audience erupted in bravos at the end of the opera.  观众在歌剧结束时爆发出喝彩声。| 近义词: applause, cheers, accolades | 反义词: boos, jeers | 词性:名词

词源: From Italian *bravo* (singular), literally meaning "brave" or "skilled".
