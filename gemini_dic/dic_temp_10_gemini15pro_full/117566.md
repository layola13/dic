megaflora:/ˈmɛɡəˌflɔːrə/
noun
巨型植物群
The large-scale flora of a region or a specific geological period, typically referring to plants of unusually large size.
The Carboniferous period is known for its megaflora, including giant ferns and club mosses.  石炭纪以其巨型植物群而闻名，包括巨大的蕨类植物和石松。
The researchers studied the megaflora of the Cretaceous period to understand the climate at that time. 研究人员研究了白垩纪的巨型植物群，以了解当时的气候。
近义词:macroflora
反义词:microflora
词性:n.
