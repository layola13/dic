oratorship: /ˈɔːrətərʃɪp/ (US) /ˈɒrətərʃɪp/ (UK)
n.
雄辩术；演说才能；演说家身份
The art or practice of public speaking; skill as an orator.
He possessed remarkable oratorship, captivating audiences with his powerful voice and persuasive arguments. 他拥有非凡的演说才能，用他洪亮的声音和有说服力的论证吸引着观众。
synonym: eloquence, rhetoric, public speaking
antonym: inarticulateness, taciturnity
noun
