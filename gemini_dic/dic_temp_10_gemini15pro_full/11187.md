arching:/ˈɑːrtʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 拱形的，弯曲的 | curved like an arch; forming an arch | The cat stretched, its back arching.  这只猫伸了个懒腰，背拱了起来。| The bridge has a beautiful arching design. 这座桥有一个美丽的拱形设计。 | curved, bowed, rounded | straight, flat | adjective

Etymology->
From Middle English *archinge, from Old French archier (“to arch”), from Latin arcāre, present active infinitive of arcuō (“to bend like a bow; to arch”), from arcus (“a bow, arch”).
