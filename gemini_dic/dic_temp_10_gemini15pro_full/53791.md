discomposingly:/ˌdɪskəmˈpoʊzɪŋli/
adverb
令人不安地，令人窘迫地
Causing unease, embarrassment, or distress.
Her laughter ended discomposingly abruptly. 她的笑声突然停止，令人不安。
Synonyms: disturbingly, unsettlingly, unnervingly
Antonyms: reassuringly, calmingly, soothingly
词性: 副词
