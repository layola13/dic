buttonholed:/ˈbʌtnˌhoʊld/ (US), /ˈbʌtnˌhəʊld/ (UK)| v. | 扣住，强留（某人）交谈 | to detain (someone) in conversation, typically against their will. |  He was buttonholed by a reporter as he left the courthouse. 他离开法院时被一名记者缠住问个不停。| synonyms: accost, detain, corner, trap | antonyms: release, free, avoid | verb

buttonholed:/ˈbʌtnˌhoʊld/ (US), /ˈbʌtnˌhəʊld/ (UK)| adj. | （缝纫）钮孔的，有钮孔的| having buttonholes. | a buttonholed cuff. 一个有钮孔的袖口。| synonyms: pierced, perforated | antonyms: solid, unperforated | adjective
