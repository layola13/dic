overcharity:/ˈoʊvərˌtʃærɪti/| n.|过度慈善，过分施舍|Excessive or misplaced charity; giving more than is necessary or wise, often enabling harmful behavior or creating dependence.|He showed overcharity to the beggar, giving him enough money to buy drugs.|近义词：excessive generosity, misplaced kindness|反义词：stinginess, miserliness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over, beyond") + "charity" (from Old French charite, from Latin caritas, meaning "dearness, high price, affection, love").
