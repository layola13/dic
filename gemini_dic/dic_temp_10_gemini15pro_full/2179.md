addictedness:/əˈdɪktɪdnəs/| n. |成瘾; 上瘾;|The state of being addicted to something.  The quality of being addictive.|His addictedness to gambling cost him everything. 他对赌博的沉迷让他失去了一切。|近义词：addiction, dependence, habit, compulsion|反义词: indifference, apathy|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From addicted + -ness.  "Addicted" comes from the Latin word "addictus," meaning "assigned, devoted, bound to."  The "-ness" suffix is used to form nouns expressing a state or quality.

USAGE->
The noun "addictedness" is less common than "addiction."  While both refer to the state of being addicted, "addiction" is the more widely used and accepted term. "Addictedness" sometimes emphasizes the quality or degree of being addicted.
