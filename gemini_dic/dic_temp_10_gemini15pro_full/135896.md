nonprudently:/nɑːnˈpruːdəntli/
| adv. | 不谨慎地，轻率地 | Not in a prudent or careful manner; unwisely. | He invested his money nonprudently and lost a great deal. 他不谨慎地投资，损失惨重。|  She nonprudently shared her personal information online.  她在网上轻率地分享了她的个人信息。 | 近义词: imprudently, unwisely, rashly, foolishly | 反义词: prudently, wisely, carefully, cautiously | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"  来自拉丁语 non
prudently (adverb): from prudent (adjective) + -ly (adverb suffix)
prudent (adjective): from Latin prudentem, present participle of prudēre "to be prudent," contraction of providēre "to foresee," from pro- "forth" + vidēre "to see."
