discolouring: /dɪsˈkʌlərɪŋ/ /ˌdɪsˈkʌlərɪŋ/
discolouring: adj. | 变色的，褪色的 | losing or causing loss of colour | The discolouring of the old photographs made them difficult to identify.  这些老照片褪色了，很难辨认。 | synonyms: fading, bleaching, yellowing | antonyms: brightening, colouring, enhancing
discolouring: n. | 褪色，变色 | the process of something losing its colour | The discolouring of the painting was due to sunlight exposure. 这幅画的褪色是由于阳光照射造成的。 | synonyms: discoloration, fading, bleaching | antonyms: brightening, colouring, enhancing
