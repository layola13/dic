nonrevenue:/ˌnɒnˈrevənjuː/ /ˌnɑːnˈrevənuː/
adjective
非营利的，非创收的
Not generating revenue; not intended to generate revenue.
The museum relies heavily on donations and nonrevenue events.  这家博物馆严重依赖捐款和非营利活动。
The hospital offers several nonrevenue services to the community.  这家医院向社区提供几项非营利性服务。
non-profit, charitable
revenue-generating, for-profit
