contemporary:/kənˈtɛmpərəri/ | adj. n. |当代的;同时代的;同时代的人|Existing, occurring, or living at the same time; a person of the same time period.|例句：Contemporary art is often challenging. 当代艺术通常具有挑战性。|近义词：modern, current, present-day|反义词：ancient, old, past, archaic|词性:形容词,名词

contemporary:/kənˈtɛmpərəri/ | n. |同时代的人|A person who lives or lived at the same time as another.|例句：Shakespeare and Marlowe were contemporaries. 莎士比亚和马洛是同时代的人。|近义词：peer|反义词：predecessor, ancestor|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *contemporarius, from Latin com- "together" + temporarius "of time," from tempus "time."
