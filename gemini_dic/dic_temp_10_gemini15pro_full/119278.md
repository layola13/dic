micr:/ˈmaɪkrə(r)/| n. |磁墨字符识别, 磁墨水字符识别|Magnetic Ink Character Recognition (A technology used to verify the legitimacy of paper documents, especially checks, by analyzing characters printed with magnetic ink).|例句：The bank uses MICR to process checks quickly. 银行使用磁墨字符识别技术快速处理支票。

例句：MICR readers are essential for banks and other financial institutions. 磁墨字符阅读器对银行和其他金融机构至关重要。|近义词：OCR (Optical Character Recognition)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
