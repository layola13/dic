helluva:/hɛˈlʌvə/ | adj. | (informal) 极好的，糟糕的，非常的 | A shortened, informal pronunciation of "hell of a." It can be used to emphasize the degree of something, either positive or negative. |  That was a helluva game! 那真是一场精彩的比赛！| synonyms: terrific, amazing, awful, terrible | antonyms: mediocre, ordinary | adjective
