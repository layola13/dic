essenism:/ˈɛsɪnɪzəm/| n.|艾赛尼主义|A Jewish religious sect or school of thought that flourished in Palestine from the 2nd century BC to the 1st century AD.  Characterized by ascetic practices, communal living, and a belief in the imminent coming of the Messiah.|Example 1: Some scholars believe John the Baptist's teachings were influenced by Essenism.  一些学者认为施洗约翰的教义受到了艾赛尼主义的影响。

Example 2: The Dead Sea Scrolls offer valuable insights into the beliefs and practices of Essenism. 死海古卷为了解艾赛尼主义的信仰和习俗提供了宝贵的见解。|近义词：asceticism, Jewish mysticism|反义词：hedonism, materialism|词性:noun
