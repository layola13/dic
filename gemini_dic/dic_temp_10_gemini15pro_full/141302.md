open-mouthed:/ˌoʊpənˈmaʊðd/| adj.|张大嘴巴的，目瞪口呆的|Being in a state of surprise or astonishment, with one's mouth wide open.|She stared open-mouthed at the magician's trick. 她张大嘴巴看着魔术师的戏法。|Synonyms: agape, aghast, astonished, amazed|Antonyms: unimpressed, unfazed|adjective

open-mouthed:/ˌoʊpənˈmaʊðd/| adv.|张着嘴地|In an open-mouthed manner.|He listened open-mouthed to the news. 他张着嘴听着新闻。|Synonyms: agape, aghast|Antonyms: closed-mouthed|adverb
