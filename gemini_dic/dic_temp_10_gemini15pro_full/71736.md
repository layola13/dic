flawy:/ˈflɔːi/ | adj. | 有缺陷的；有瑕疵的 | Having imperfections; not perfect. | The diamond was flawless, but the setting was flawed. 钻石完美无瑕，但镶嵌有缺陷。|  The argument was flawed and unconvincing.  这个论点有缺陷，没有说服力。| synonyms: imperfect, defective, faulty, blemished | antonyms: perfect, flawless, impeccable | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From flaw + -y.  "Flaw" comes from Old Norse *flaga ("flake, fragment").
