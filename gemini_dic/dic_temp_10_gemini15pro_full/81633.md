greedier:/ˈɡriːdiər/| adj.|更贪婪的，更贪心的|The comparative form of "greedy," meaning wanting more than one needs or deserves.|He became greedier as he got older. 他年纪越大越贪婪。|近义词：more avaricious, more covetous, more rapacious|反义词：more generous, more charitable|词性:形容词
词源: From Old English grǣdig "greedy," from grǣd "greed."
