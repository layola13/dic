otozoum:/ˈoʊtəˌzuːm/ | n. |奥托足迹，奥托龙足迹|A genus of fossil footprints from the Early Jurassic, attributed to a large sauropodomorph dinosaur.  These tracks are notable for their large size and are among the earliest evidence of sauropods in North America.| Example:  Otozoum tracks were discovered in the Connecticut River Valley.  奥托足迹在康涅狄格河谷被发现。| Example: The otozoum trackway provided valuable insights into the locomotion of early sauropods. 奥托足迹提供了早期蜥脚类动物运动方式的宝贵见解。| Synonym: dinosaur footprint, sauropod track | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a fossil footprint) | Noun
