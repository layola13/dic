noncommiseratively:/ˌnɒnkəmɪzəˈreɪtɪvli/
adverb
不同情地，无同情心地
without pity or sympathy; unsympathetically.
He looked at the beggar noncommiseratively and walked away. 他毫无同情地看着乞丐，然后走开了。
Synonyms: unsympathetically, heartlessly, callously, pitilessly, ruthlessly, cruelly, inhumanely
Antonyms: sympathetically, compassionately, kindly, mercifully, tenderly
词性: 副词
