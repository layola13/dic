bergmote:/ˈbɜːrməʊt/
noun
矿山法院；矿工大会
a local court held in Derbyshire, England, for regulating the lead mining industry.  Historically, it also referred to a meeting or assembly, especially of miners.
The bergmote met to discuss new regulations for mine safety.  矿山法院开会讨论新的矿山安全条例。
The annual bergmote was a significant event in the mining community. 年度矿工大会是矿业社区的一项重要活动。
synonyms: court, assembly, meeting, council, tribunal
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, consider perhaps:  dispersal, adjournment)
