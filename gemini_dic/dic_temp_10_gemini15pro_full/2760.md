adultish: /əˈdʌltɪʃ/
adjective
幼稚的成年人; behaving in a way that is suitable for an adult but not very sensible
Acting in a slightly immature way, although technically an adult.
He has a rather adultish sense of humor. 他有一种相当幼稚的幽默感。
Her adultish behavior embarrassed her parents at the party.  她在派对上幼稚的行为让她的父母很尴尬。
近义词: immature, childish, puerile
反义词: mature, grown-up, sophisticated
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From adult + -ish.  "Adult" comes from the Latin "adultus," the past participle of "adolescere," meaning "to grow up." The suffix "-ish" means "somewhat" or "having the quality of."
