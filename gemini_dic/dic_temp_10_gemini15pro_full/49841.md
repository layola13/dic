demoralises: /dɪˈmɒrəlaɪzɪz/ /dɪˈmɔːrəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使士气低落，使沮丧
To cause (someone) to lose confidence or hope; dispirit.
The team was completely demoralised by their third consecutive defeat.  该队因连续三次失利而完全丧失了士气。
近义词: dishearten, discourage, depress
反义词: encourage, hearten, inspire
词性: verb

demoralise: /dɪˈmɒrəlaɪz/ /dɪˈmɔːrəlaɪz/
verb
使士气低落，使沮丧
To cause (someone) to lose confidence or hope; dispirit.
Constant criticism can demoralise employees.  不断的批评会使员工士气低落。
近义词: dishearten, discourage, depress
反义词: encourage, hearten, inspire
词性: verb

词源: From French *démoraliser*, from dé- (expressing reversal) + moraliser (“to moralize”), from moral (“moral”).
