di:/diː/ /daɪ/
pron.|什么，那一个，那一些（疑问代词）|Used to ask for information specifying something.|What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|who, which| |pron.
di:/diː/ /daɪ/
det.|什么，那一个，那一些（疑问限定词）|Used with nouns to ask which thing or things are meant.|What color is your car? 你的车是什么颜色的？|which, whose| |det.

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you usually eat for breakfast? 你早餐通常吃什么？
What does she think about the new policy? 她对新政策有什么看法？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to deserve this award? 她做了什么值得获得这个奖项？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading this week? 他这周一直在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last weekend? 你上周末做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? 她对这部电影说了些什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking when she heard the news? 她听到这个消息时在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will she wear to the party? 她会穿什么去参加派对？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

What will she have learned by the time she finishes the course? 到她完成课程的时候，她会学到什么？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？



虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What would she say if she were here? 如果她在这里，她会说什么？



被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？




疑问句:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the summer? 你暑假的计划是什么？



否定句:

She doesn't understand what you mean. 她不明白你的意思。
