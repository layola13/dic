grooviest:/ˈɡruːviɪst/| adj.|最时髦的，最酷的，最棒的|Superlative form of groovy: most groovy.  Meaning fashionable, stylish, excellent, or appealing in a modern or unconventional way.|That's the grooviest outfit I've ever seen! 那是我见过的最时髦的服装！|The band played a grooviest new song. 乐队演奏了一首最棒的新歌。|近义词：coolest, hippest, trendiest, most excellent, best|反义词：squarest, uncoolest, lamest, worst|词性:形容词

词源:
From "groove," originally referring to the groove in a vinyl record, implying a steady, enjoyable rhythm.  Evolved in the 1960s and 70s slang to describe something fashionable and enjoyable, often associated with music and counterculture.
