cochaired:/ˌkoʊˈtʃɛrd/| v.|共同主持，联合主持|To preside over a meeting or event alongside another person, sharing the responsibilities of leadership.|例句：They cochaired the conference on climate change. 他们共同主持了气候变化会议。|例句：She and her colleague cochaired the committee. 她和她的同事共同主持了委员会。|近义词：jointly chaired, co-presided|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "solely chaired")|词性:动词

cochair:/ˈkoʊtʃɛr/| n.|共同主席，联合主席|One of two or more people who share the responsibility of presiding over a meeting or event.|例句：The cochairs of the event welcomed the attendees. 活动的联合主席欢迎与会者。|例句：She was elected cochair of the fundraising committee. 她被选为筹款委员会的联合主席。|近义词：joint chair, co-president|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "sole chair")|词性:名词
