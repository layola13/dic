do-a-job-on:/duː ə ˈdʒɒb ɒn/ (British) /duː ə ˈdʒɑːb ɑːn/ (American)
verb phrase
彻底修理；狠揍；严厉批评；对...产生重大（通常是负面）影响
to damage something badly, or to hurt someone badly; to criticize someone severely; to have a significant (often negative) effect on something.
He really did a job on that car.  他把那辆车弄得一团糟。
The critics really did a job on her latest movie.  评论家们对她最新的电影进行了猛烈抨击。
damage, ruin, wreck, destroy, thrash, beat up, criticize harshly, lambaste
repair, fix, praise, commend
