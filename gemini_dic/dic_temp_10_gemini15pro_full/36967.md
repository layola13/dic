colporteur:/ˈkɒlpɔːtə(r)/ (US: /kəˈlpɔːrtər/)| n.|书商；挨户兜售者；传播流言蜚语的人|A person who travels from place to place selling books, religious tracts, or other small items, often door-to-door.  A colporteur might also spread news or gossip.|The colporteur traveled through the countryside, selling Bibles and prayer books. 这位书商走遍乡村，挨家挨户地兜售圣经和祈祷书。|Synonyms: peddler, hawker, vendor, canvasser|Antonyms: buyer, customer|noun
