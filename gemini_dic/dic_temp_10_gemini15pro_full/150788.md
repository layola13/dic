paradisically:/ˌpærədɪˈsaɪk(ə)li/| adv.|天堂般地，极乐地|In a way that is like paradise; extremely pleasant and enjoyable.|例句：We spent a week on a paradisically beautiful island. 我们在一个天堂般美丽的岛屿上度过了一周。

例句：The hotel was paradisically peaceful. 这家酒店宁静得像天堂一样。|近义词：heavenly, blissful, divine, idyllic|反义词：hellish, infernal, nightmarish|词性:副词
词源: From paradise + -ical + -ly.  Ultimately from the Avestan word *pairidaeza-, meaning "walled enclosure," via Greek, Latin, and Old French.
