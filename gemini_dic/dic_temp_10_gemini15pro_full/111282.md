littery:/ˈlɪtəri/ /ˈlɪtəri/ | adj. | 乱糟糟的，到处是垃圾的 | Covered with or containing scattered litter.  Characterized by accumulation of litter. | The park was littered with empty cans and bottles. 公园里到处都是空罐子和瓶子。 |  The cat's litter box was quite littery. 猫砂盆很脏乱。 | messy, untidy, cluttered, dirty | clean, tidy, neat | adjective
