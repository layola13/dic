murexan:/ˈmjʊrɛksæn/ | n. | 紫红酸铵; 紫脲酸铵 |  A purplish-red crystalline powder, the ammonium salt of purpuric acid, used as a dye and as an indicator.  It is formed by the action of ammonia and air on uric acid. |  Murexan was once used as a dye for wool. 紫红酸铵曾被用作羊毛染料。| synonym: ammonium purpurate | antonym: N/A | noun

murexide:/ˈmjʊrɛksaɪd/ | n. | 紫脲酸; 紫红酸 |  Purpuric acid; an indicator that changes color with pH.  | Murexide is used in analytical chemistry as a complexometric indicator. 紫脲酸在分析化学中用作络合指示剂。 | synonym: purpuric acid | antonym: N/A | noun
