haustellum:/hɔːˈstɛləm/ | n. | 吸喙；喙管 | A tubular mouthpart, usually of an insect, used for sucking liquids. | Butterflies use their haustellum to drink nectar from flowers. 蝴蝶用它们的喙管从花中吸取花蜜。 | The mosquito uses its haustellum to pierce the skin and draw blood. 蚊子用它的喙管刺穿皮肤吸血。 |  近义词: proboscis | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Latin *haustellum, diminutive of haustor "drawer, scoop, one who draws," from haurire "to draw, drink."
