foreworn:/fɔːrˈwɔːrn/
adjective
预先警告的，事先提醒的
warned beforehand; cautioned in advance.
例句：They were forewarned of the dangers of the journey. 他们被预先警告了旅途的危险。

例句：The investors were forewarned about the risks involved. 投资者被预先警告了相关的风险。

近义词：prewarned, forewarned, alerted
反义词：unwarned, unprepared

词性: adjective
