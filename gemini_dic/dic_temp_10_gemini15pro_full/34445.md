clavellate:/kləˈveɪleɪt/| adj.|棒状的， булавовидный (Russian)|Shaped like a small club; club-shaped.  Specifically, gradually thickening towards the tip. |The fungus had a distinctive clavellate fruiting body. 这种真菌有一个独特的棒状子实体。|The clavellate antennae of the insect helped it navigate. 这种昆虫的棒状触角帮助它导航。|近义词：club-shaped, claviform|反义词: filiform, acicular |adjective
Etymology->
From Latin *clavellatus*, from *clava* ("club").
