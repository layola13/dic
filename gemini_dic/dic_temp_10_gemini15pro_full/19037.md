beteem:/bɪˈstiːm/
v.
给予，赐予；认为…合适；适宜
To consider (something) suitable or appropriate; to grant or bestow (usually used with a negative connotation).
He rarely beteems his employees with praise.  他很少给予他的员工赞扬。
The king did not beteem it appropriate to meet with the rebel leader. 国王认为与叛军领袖会面是不合适的。
近义词:grant, bestow, vouchsafe, accord
反义词:withhold, deny, refuse
词性:v. (verb, 动词)
