amah:/ˈɑːmə/ | n. | 女佣；保姆；奶妈 | A female servant, especially one who does domestic work and looks after children in East and Southeast Asia. | She employed an amah to help with the children. 她雇了一个女佣来帮忙照顾孩子。|  My grandmother worked as an amah in Hong Kong. 我的祖母在香港当过女佣。 | synonyms: maid, servant, nanny, housekeeper | antonyms: employer, master | noun
