engrossed:/ɪnˈɡroʊst/ /ɛnˈɡroʊst/| adj.|全神贯注的，专注的|Completely absorbed or occupied; deeply involved in something.|She was so engrossed in her book that she didn't hear the doorbell. 她如此专注于她的书，以至于没听到门铃声。|He was completely engrossed in the video game. 他完全沉浸在电子游戏中。|近义词：absorbed, captivated, preoccupied, immersed|反义词：distracted, bored, uninterested|词性:形容词

词源: From the Old French word "engrosser," meaning "to make thick or fat," later "to buy up the whole supply of," and finally "to occupy completely."  最初源于古法语单词 "engrosser"，意为“使变厚或变胖”，后来引申为“全部买下”，最后演变为“完全占据”。
