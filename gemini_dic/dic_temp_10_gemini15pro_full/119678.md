midge:/mɪdʒ/| n.|蠓, 小蚊|A small, two-winged fly, especially one that bites or causes annoyance.  A very small person.|例句：The midges were swarming around our heads as we walked by the lake. 我们沿着湖边走的时候，蠓虫成群地围着我们的头飞。

例句：She's a tiny little midge of a thing. 她真是个小不点。|近义词：gnat, mosquito|反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps "giant" in the figurative sense)|noun
