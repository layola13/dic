at-grade:/æt ˈɡreɪd/
adjective
与路面平齐的; 与地面齐平的; 同一水平的
At the same level as the ground or street, especially of a railroad crossing.
The train collided with a truck at an at-grade crossing. 火车在一个与路面平齐的交叉口与一辆卡车相撞。
level, flush, ground-level
elevated, overhead, below-grade
adjective
