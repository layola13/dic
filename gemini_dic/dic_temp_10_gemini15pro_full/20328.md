bishoplike: /ˈbɪʃəplaɪk/
adjective
像主教的；主教般的
Resembling or characteristic of a bishop, especially in manner or appearance.
His bishoplike demeanor commanded respect. 他那像主教般的举止令人肃然起敬。

The old professor, with his flowing white beard and dignified manner, had a bishoplike presence.  这位老教授留着飘逸的白胡子，举止庄重，很有主教风范。

近义词: episcopal, pontifical, priestly
反义词: secular, lay, irreverent
词性: adjective

词源:  bishop (主教) + -like (像...的)
