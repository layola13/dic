overheat: /ˌoʊvərˈhiːt/ | v. | 过热；使过热 | to become too hot or to make something too hot | The engine overheated and stopped working. 引擎过热并停止工作。| synonyms: burn out, scorch | antonyms: cool down, chill | verb

overheat: /ˌoʊvərˈhiːt/ | n. | 过热 | the state of being overheated | The car's overheat caused serious damage. 汽车的过热造成了严重的损坏。| synonyms: overheating | antonyms: cooling | noun

overheated: /ˌoʊvərˈhiːtɪd/ | adj. | 过热的；过分激动的 | too hot; excessively enthusiastic or excited | The overheated engine began to smoke. 过热的引擎开始冒烟。 The debate became overheated. 辩论变得过于激烈。| synonyms: overwrought, feverish | antonyms: cool, calm | adjective
