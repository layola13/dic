overweeningly:/ˌoʊvərˈwiːnɪŋli/| adv.|傲慢地，自负地，过于自信地|In an overweeningly arrogant manner; excessively confidently or presumptuously.  Characterized by excessive confidence or pride.|He spoke overweeningly about his own achievements, ignoring the contributions of his team.  他傲慢地谈论着自己的成就，忽视了团队的贡献。|Synonyms: arrogantly, haughtily, presumptuously, conceitedly|Antonyms: humbly, modestly, unpresumptuously|Adverb
