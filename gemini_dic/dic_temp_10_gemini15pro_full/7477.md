ankhs: /æŋks/ | n. | 安卡 | An ankh is an ancient Egyptian hieroglyphic symbol that represents "life" or "breath of life". It is often depicted as a cross with a loop at the top.  | The pharaoh held an ankh, symbolizing his divine right to rule. 法老手持安卡，象征着他神圣的统治权。 |  She wore a necklace with a golden ankh pendant. 她戴着一条带有金色安卡吊坠的项链。 | synonyms: crux ansata (Latin), key of life,  | antonyms: death, mortality | noun
