leafy:/ˈliːfi/| adj.|多叶的；叶茂盛的；绿树成荫的|Covered with many leaves; having abundant foliage. |The leafy trees provided a welcome shade on a hot summer day.  绿树成荫的树木在炎热的夏日提供了宜人的树荫。|近义词：foliated, lush, verdant|反义词:bare, leafless|adjective

Etymology->
From Middle English *lefi*, from Old English *lēafī*, from *lēaf* (“leaf”) + *-ī* (adjectival suffix). Cognate with Dutch *loofachtig*, German *laubig*, Swedish *lövig*.
