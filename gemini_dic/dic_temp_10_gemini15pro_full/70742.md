fine-tuned:/ˌfaɪnˈtuːnd/ | adj. | 微调的，精调的 | Carefully adjusted or optimized for maximum performance or effectiveness.  Refers to something that has been precisely calibrated or refined.  Often used in the context of machine learning models that have been trained on a specific dataset to improve their accuracy. | The model was fine-tuned on a dataset of medical images to improve its diagnostic capabilities.  该模型在医学图像数据集上进行了微调，以提高其诊断能力。

The guitarist fine-tuned his instrument before the concert.  吉他手在音乐会前对他的乐器进行了微调。 | 近义词: optimized, calibrated, refined, adjusted | 反义词: unoptimized, crude, unrefined | 词性: 形容词
