lighty:/ˈlaɪti/| adv.|轻轻地，轻微地|Lightly means in a gentle way, with little force or pressure.|She touched the baby lightly on the cheek. 她轻轻地摸了摸婴儿的脸颊。|近义词：gently, softly, delicately|反义词：heavily, firmly, strongly|词性:副词

词源:  From light (adjective) + -ly (adverbial suffix).
