conglutinate:/kənˈɡluːtɪneɪt/ | adj. | 粘合的，凝结的 | stuck together; glued | The conglutinate mass was difficult to break apart.  粘结的块状物很难分开。 | synonyms: adherent, adhesive, cohesive | antonyms: separate, detached, divided | adjective
