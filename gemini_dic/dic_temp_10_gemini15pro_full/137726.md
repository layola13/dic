nonvagrancy:/ˌnɒnˈveɪɡrənsi/
noun
非流浪状态，非游荡状态
The state of not being a vagrant; not wandering from place to place without a home or regular employment.
His nonvagrancy was proven by his steady job and permanent address. 他的稳定的工作和永久地址证明了他并非流浪汉。
The police confirmed her nonvagrancy after checking her identification. 警察检查了她的身份后，确认她不是流浪汉。
Synonyms: settledness, residence, stability
Antonyms: vagrancy, homelessness, wandering
