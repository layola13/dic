fracking:/ˈfrækɪŋ/| n.|水力压裂(法)|The process of injecting liquid at high pressure into subterranean rocks, boreholes, etc., so as to force open existing fissures and extract oil or gas.| Fracking has become a controversial method of extracting natural gas. 水力压裂已成为一种有争议的天然气开采方法。|近义词：hydraulic fracturing|反义词: None|词性: Noun


Etymology->
Shortening of "hydraulic fracturing"
