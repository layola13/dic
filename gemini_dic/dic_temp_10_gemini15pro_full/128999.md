nipplewort:/ˈnɪplˌwɜːrt/| n. |乳蓟草，小苦苣|A common European weed (Lapsana communis) of the daisy family, with small yellow flowers and milky sap. It is also known as common nipplewort.|例句：The field was covered with nipplewort and dandelions. 这片田野上长满了乳蓟草和蒲公英。

例句：Nipplewort is considered an edible weed. 乳蓟草被认为是一种可食用的杂草。|近义词：sow thistle, common nipplewort|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
