longhouse: /ˈlɒŋhaʊs/ (British) /ˈlɔːŋhaʊs/ (American) | n. | 长屋，长形房屋 | A long, narrow building, typically with a single room, used as a dwelling by certain cultures. |  The Iroquois built longhouses that could hold many families.  易洛魁人建造的长屋可以容纳许多家庭。

The tribe's longhouse served as both a dwelling and a meeting place. 这个部落的长屋既是住所又是会议场所。 | 近义词: hall, building | 反义词: hut, cabin | 词性: 名词
