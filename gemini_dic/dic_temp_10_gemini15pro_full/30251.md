cellulosities:/ˌsɛljʊˈlɒsɪtiz/
noun
纤维素含量；纤维素性质
The quality or state of being cellulosic, or containing cellulose.
The cellulosities of different plant species vary. 不同植物种类的纤维素含量各不相同。
The researcher analyzed the cellulosities of the samples. 研究人员分析了样品的纤维素性质。
近义词: cellulose content, cellulose properties
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


Etymology:
From cellulose + -ity (suffix forming nouns of quality or state) + -es (plural suffix).  Ultimately from Latin cellula (small room, cell) + -ose (suffix forming adjectives and nouns from nouns).
