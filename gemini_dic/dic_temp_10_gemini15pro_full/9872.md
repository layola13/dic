apanthropy:/ˌæpənˈθrəʊpi/
n.
厌恶人类；遁世；不愿与人交往
A dislike of humankind; reclusiveness; an aversion to human society.
He lived a life of apanthropy, far from the bustle of the city. 他过着遁世的生活，远离城市的喧嚣。
近义词：misanthropy, reclusiveness, solitude
反义词：philanthropy, sociability, gregariousness
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Greek *ἀπανθρωπία (apanthrōpia)  'avoidance of human company', from ἀπ- (ap-)  'away from' + ἄνθρωπος (anthrōpos)  'human being'.
