innovate:/ˈɪnəveɪt/| v. |创新，革新|To introduce something new; make changes in something established, especially by introducing new methods, ideas, or products.|Tesla continues to innovate in the electric car industry. 特斯拉在电动汽车行业不断创新。|近义词：create, invent, pioneer, develop, modernize|反义词：imitate, copy, stagnate|词性:动词
innovate:/ˈɪnəveɪt/| n. |创新，革新（名词形式较少使用）|The act of innovating.|We need more innovate in this field. 我们在这个领域需要更多创新。|近义词：innovation, creativity, invention|反义词：imitation, stagnation|词性:名词

词源: From Latin *innovatus*, past participle of *innovare* (“to renew, alter”), from *in-* (“in, into”) + *novus* (“new”).
