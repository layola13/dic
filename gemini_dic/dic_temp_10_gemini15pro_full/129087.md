nitrosalicylic:/ˌnaɪtroʊˌsælɪˈsɪlɪk/ | adj. |  硝基水杨酸的 | Relating to or containing nitrosalicylic acid, a compound derived from salicylic acid by introducing a nitro group. | Nitrosalicylic acid is used in a colorimetric assay to determine the amount of reducing sugars.  硝基水杨酸用于比色测定还原糖的含量。| 近义词: None (highly specific) | 反义词: None (descriptive) | 词性: adjective

nitrosalicylate:/ˌnaɪtroʊsəˈlɪsɪleɪt/ | n. | 硝基水杨酸盐 | A salt or ester of nitrosalicylic acid. |  The nitrosalicylate formed during the reaction changes color, allowing for quantification.  反应过程中形成的硝基水杨酸盐会改变颜色，从而可以进行定量。| 近义词:  (depends on specific salt/ester, e.g., sodium nitrosalicylate) | 反义词: None (descriptive) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
nitro- (from Greek *nitron, "nitre, native soda") + salicylic (from Latin *salix, "willow," as salicylic acid was originally isolated from willow bark)
