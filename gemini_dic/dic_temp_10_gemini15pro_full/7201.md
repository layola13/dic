angle-dip:/ˈæŋɡəl dɪp/| n.|倾角|The angle between a planar feature, such as a bedding plane or fault, and the horizontal plane. It is measured downwards from the horizontal.|The geologist measured the angle-dip of the rock strata. 地质学家测量了岩层的倾角。|近义词：dip, inclination, slope|反义词: ascent, rise|noun
