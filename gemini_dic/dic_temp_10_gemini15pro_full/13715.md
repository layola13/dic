atwain:/əˈtweɪn/| adv. adj.| 成两半，分离 | in two, apart; into two parts |  The rope snapped atwain under the strain. 绳子在拉力下断成了两截。 |近义词：apart, asunder, in two |反义词: together, united |词性: 副词，形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *a tweyne, from Old English on twēġen (“in two, into two parts”), from Proto-Germanic *twai (“two”). Cognate with Scots atwein (“in two”), Dutch in tweeën (“in two”). Related to two.
