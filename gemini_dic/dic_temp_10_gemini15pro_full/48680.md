deferrer:/dɪˈfɜːrər/| n.|推迟者，延期者|Someone who postpones or delays something.|The deferrer of the meeting hasn't given a new date yet.  会议的推迟者还没有给出新的日期。|近义词：procrastinator, postponer, delayer|反义词:expediter, prompter|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "defer" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Defer" comes from the Old French "differer"  and ultimately from the Latin "differre," meaning "to carry apart, postpone, differ."
