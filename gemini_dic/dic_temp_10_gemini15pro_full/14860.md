axonometric:/ˌæksənəˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 轴测的，轴测投影的 | Relating to or designating a method of projection in which a three-dimensional object is represented by a drawing having all axes drawn to scale on a single plane, but with the axes not necessarily at right angles. |  The architect presented axonometric drawings of the building.  建筑师展示了该建筑的轴测图。| Isometric, orthographic | Perspective | adjective

axonometry:/ˌæksənˈɒmɪtri/ | n. | 轴测法，轴测投影法 | The science or art of making axonometric projections. | Axonometry is a useful technique for visualizing three-dimensional objects. 轴测法是一种可视化三维物体的有用技术。 | Isometric projection, orthographic projection | Perspective projection | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *axōn* (axis) + *-metros* (measure)
