hamperedly:/ˈhæmpərdli/
adverb
受阻地，困难地
in a way that is hampered; with difficulty.
Her progress was hamperedly by the heavy rain. 她前进的步伐受到了大雨的阻碍。
近义词：awkwardly, clumsily, cumbersomely, laboriously
反义词：easily, smoothly, effortlessly
词性: adverb

词源:  From hamper (verb) + -ed (forming past participles and adjectives) + -ly (forming adverbs).  The verb "hamper" comes from the Old North French "hanapier" meaning "to seize by the ham," later meaning "to shackle, fetter," and eventually "to obstruct, hinder."
