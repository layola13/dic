dispossess:/dɪspəˈzɛs/ | vt. | 剥夺；使丧失；驱逐 | To deprive someone of land, property, or other possessions. |

例句：
The landlord dispossessed the tenants for not paying the rent.
房东因租户未付房租而将其驱逐。

The government dispossessed the farmers of their land to build a new highway.
政府征用了农民的土地来修建一条新的高速公路。

近义词：evict, oust, expel, deprive, strip |
反义词：provide, give, endow, grant, bestow |
词性：动词 |
