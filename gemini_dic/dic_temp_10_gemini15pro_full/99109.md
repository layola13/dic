intercarriers:/ˌɪntərˈkæriərz/ | n. | 互相交换；（尤指电信）互联互通的运营商；运营商之间的连接 |  A system or agreement between telecommunications carriers that allows them to connect and exchange traffic.  This enables customers of one carrier to communicate with customers of another. | The intercarriers agreement ensures seamless communication across different networks. 互联互通协议确保了跨不同网络的无缝通信。 |  Intercarrier compensation is a complex issue. 运营商间的补偿是一个复杂的问题。 | synonyms: interconnection, interoperability, network connectivity | antonyms: isolation, disconnection | noun
