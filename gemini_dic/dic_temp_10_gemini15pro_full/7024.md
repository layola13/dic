androtomous:/ænˈdrɒtəmʌs/
adjective
雄性先熟的，雄性先成熟的
Having male organs mature before female organs, as in certain hermaphroditic animals.
Certain species of mollusks are androtomous, meaning their male reproductive organs mature before their female ones.  某些软体动物是雄性先熟的，这意味着它们的雄性生殖器官比雌性生殖器官先成熟。
Androtomous development is an important adaptation for some sequential hermaphrodites. 雄性先熟的发育对于一些顺序性雌雄同体动物来说是一种重要的适应。
近义词: protandrous, proterandrous
反义词: protogynous (雌性先熟的)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek andros (man) + -tomous (cutting, dividing), relating to the maturation of reproductive organs.
