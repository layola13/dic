instill:/ɪnˈstɪl/ | vt. | 逐渐灌输；慢慢滴入 | To gradually establish (an idea or attitude, especially a desirable one) in a person's mind. | The coach instilled a sense of pride in his players. 教练逐渐灌输给他的队员们一种自豪感。

| Example 1:  She instilled in her children a love of reading. 她逐渐培养了孩子们对阅读的热爱。
| Example 2:  The teacher tried to instill confidence in his students. 老师试图 instill 给学生们信心。

| Synonym: implant, inculcate, infuse, introduce, inspire | Antonym: eradicate, remove, uproot | Verb
