commiseratively:/kəˈmɪzəreɪtɪvli/
adverb
同情地，怜悯地
showing or expressing sympathy
She looked at him commiseratively. 她同情地望着他。
近义词:sympathetically, compassionately, pityingly
反义词:unsympathetically, callously, heartlessly
词性:adverb

commiserate:/kəˈmɪzəreɪt/
verb
同情，怜悯
express or feel sympathy or pity; condole
She commiserated with the losers. 她同情失败者。
近义词:sympathize, empathize, console
反义词:ignore, disregard
词性:verb

词源: From Late Latin *commiseratus, past participle of commiserārī (“to pity, feel compassion”), from Latin com- (“with”) + miserārī (“to pity”).
