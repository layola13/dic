minniver:/ˈmɪnɪvər/| n.|白鼬皮，貂皮(尤指用作服装的饰边)|A white or light grey fur, especially the fur of the stoat in its winter coat, formerly used for lining or trimming clothes.|The queen's robe was trimmed with minniver.|Synonyms: ermine, stoat, weasel|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a type of fur, could consider materials like cotton or silk as contrasting materials)|noun
