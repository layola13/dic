neighs:/neɪz/
| v. | 马嘶，马鸣 | The horse neighs loudly. 这匹马大声嘶鸣。 |The wild horses neigh as they run across the field. 野马在田野里奔跑时嘶鸣。 |近义词：whinny |反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用silent, quiet等表示没有声音) |词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *neighen, neyen, from Old English hnǣgan (“to neigh”), from Proto-Germanic *hnaigijaną (“to neigh”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)neigh₁- (“to snort, neigh”). Cognate with West Frisian neie (“to neigh”), Dutch neighen, German wiehern (“to neigh”).
