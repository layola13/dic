houseboated:/ˈhaʊsbəʊtɪd/
verb
乘船屋旅行，在船屋上度假
spent time on a houseboat, especially as a vacation.
We houseboated on Lake Powell last summer.  我们去年夏天在鲍威尔湖上乘船屋度假。
vacationed on a houseboat, lived on a houseboat, cruised on a houseboat
stayed in a hotel, camped
