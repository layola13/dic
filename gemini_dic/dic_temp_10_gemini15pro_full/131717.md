nonconfederation: /ˌnɒnkənˌfɛdəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 非邦联，不结盟 |  The state of not being a confederation; absence of a confederate structure. This implies either independence or integration into a larger, non-confederate entity. |  The small nation chose nonconfederation, preferring to remain fully independent. 小国选择不结盟，宁愿保持完全独立。 |  The region's nonconfederation was a source of instability. 该地区的不结盟是不稳定的根源。 | Near Antonyms: confederation, alliance, union | Antonyms: federation | noun
