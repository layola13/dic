cockes:/ˈkɒkɪz/| n.|公鸡（复数，中古英语）|A Middle English plural form of "cock," meaning male chickens.  This word is archaic and no longer used in modern English. |例句：And the cockes crowe, and up springeth the sonne. 公鸡啼鸣，太阳升起。（中古英语例句）|近义词：(Modern) roosters, cocks |反义词:hens |词性:noun
