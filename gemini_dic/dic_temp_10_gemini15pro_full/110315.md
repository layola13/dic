ETYMOLOGY->
From *light* + *bulb*. *Light* comes from Old English *lēoht*, from Proto-Germanic *leuhtą*, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *leuk-*.  *Bulb* comes from Late Latin *bulbus*, meaning "onion," referring to the rounded shape.
