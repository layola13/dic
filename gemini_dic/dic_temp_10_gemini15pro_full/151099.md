paraselene:/ˌpærəsɪˈliːni/ /ˌpærəsəˈliːni/ | n. |幻月; Mock moon|A bright spot of light in the sky, appearing near the moon, caused by the refraction of moonlight by ice crystals in the atmosphere.  大气中冰晶对月光折射而产生的出现在月亮附近天空中的亮点。|例句：The astronomer observed a paraselene last night. 天文学家昨晚观察到了一颗幻月。

例句：Paraselenae are less common than sun dogs. 幻月比幻日少见。|近义词：mock moon|反义词:  (No direct antonym)|词性: noun
