bloatedness:/ˈbloʊtɪdnɪs/| n. |肿胀；腹胀；臃肿|The state of being bloated, typically referring to the abdomen feeling full and tight.|例句：She experienced bloating and bloatedness after eating a large meal.  她吃完大餐后感到腹胀。

例句：The bloatedness made it difficult for him to button his pants. 腹胀让他难以扣上裤子。|近义词：swelling, distension, inflation|反义词:flatness, slimness|词性:noun

bloated:/ˈbloʊtɪd/| adj. |肿胀的；臃肿的；傲慢的|Swollen with fluid or gas; excessively ornate; puffed up with pride.|例句：He felt bloated after the Thanksgiving feast. 感恩节盛宴后，他感觉肚子胀。

例句：The company's bloated bureaucracy hindered progress. 该公司臃肿的官僚机构阻碍了发展。|近义词：swollen, distended, inflated, pompous|反义词:deflated, slim, humble|词性:adjective


Etymology:
From bloat + -ed + -ness.  Bloat comes from Old Norse *bláþr, meaning "pale, livid."
