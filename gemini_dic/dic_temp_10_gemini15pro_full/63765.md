epicoene:/ˈɛpɪˌsiːn/ | adj. n. |两性通用的，雌雄同体的；两性通用词 |A word that can refer to either a male or a female, such as "child" or "teacher," or an animal of either sex.  A person or animal that has characteristics of both sexes. |例句：The word "student" is an epicoene noun. “学生”这个词是两性通用的名词。

例句：The mythical sphinx was often depicted as an epicoene creature.  神话中的斯芬克斯经常被描绘成雌雄同体的生物。 |近义词：unisex, common gender, dual-gender |反义词: masculine, feminine |词性: adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *epicoenus, from Ancient Greek ἐπίκοινος (epíkoinos, “shared in common, common to all”), from ἐπί (epí, “on, upon, over”) + κοινός (koinós, “common”).
