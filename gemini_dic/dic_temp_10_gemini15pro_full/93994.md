impairments:/ɪmˈpeərmənts/| n. |损伤；障碍；缺陷|The state of being weakened, diminished, or damaged, especially in relation to a physical or mental function.  It often refers to a deterioration in the quality or strength of something.|His hearing impairments made it difficult for him to follow conversations. 他的听力障碍使他难以听懂对话。|Synonyms: disabilities, handicaps, deficits, weaknesses|Antonyms: strengths, abilities, enhancements|noun
