jawans:/dʒəˈwɑːnz/
noun
士兵（尤指印度或巴基斯坦的）
Plural form of *jawan*.  Refers to soldiers, especially in India and Pakistan.
The jawans marched bravely into battle. 士兵们勇敢地投入战斗。
The jawans were commended for their bravery. 士兵们因其勇敢而受到赞扬。
Synonyms: soldiers, troops, servicemen
Antonyms: civilians
