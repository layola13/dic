congeal:/kənˈdʒiːl/| v.| (感情) 冷却，(热情) 消失|to lose warmth and become stiff and formal|Their relationship had congealed into a polite formality. 他们的关系已经冷却成了一种客套的形式。|近义词：cool, stiffen, harden|反义词:warm, soften|动词
