multibillionaire: /ˌmʌltiˈbɪljəˌnɛər/ | n. | 亿万富翁，超级富豪 | A person whose net worth is valued in the multiple billions of dollars.  |  He is a multibillionaire who owns several successful companies. 他是一位拥有多家成功公司的亿万富翁。 | Synonyms: tycoon, magnate, plutocrat | Antonyms: pauper, destitute | noun

Etymology: multi- (prefix meaning "many") + billionaire (from billion + -aire, denoting a person having a specified amount of wealth).
