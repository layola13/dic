nothing-but:/ˈnʌθɪŋ bʌt/ | prep. | 只有，仅仅 |  Used to emphasize that something is only one thing and nothing more. |  He's nothing but a liar. 他只是一个骗子。| synonyms: only, just, merely, solely | antonyms: everything, all, more than | preposition

nothing-but:/ˈnʌθɪŋ bʌt/ | adj. | 只有，仅仅 | Used to emphasize that something is only one thing and nothing more. (less common as an adjective) | It was a nothing-but-trouble kind of day. 这是麻烦不断的一天。 | synonyms: only, mere, sheer | antonyms: complex, multifaceted | adjective
