nonascertainably:/ˌnɒnæsərˈteɪnəbli/
adverb
无法确定地
Not able to be ascertained; in a way that is impossible to find out for certain.
The cause of the fire was nonascertainably linked to faulty wiring. 这场火灾的原因无法确定地与线路故障联系起来。
His exact age was nonascertainably due to lost records. 由于记录丢失，他的确切年龄无法确定。
Synonyms: indeterminably, vaguely, unclearly, doubtfully
Antonyms: definitely, certainly, clearly, evidently
词性: 副词
