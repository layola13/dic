bugwort:/ˈbʌɡwɜːrt/ | n. | 臭虫草；艾菊 | A European plant (Tanacetum vulgare) of the daisy family, having feathery leaves and buttonlike yellow flower heads. It is traditionally used to repel insects. |  The settlers planted bugwort around their cabins to deter insects.  殖民者在他们的小屋周围种植臭虫草来驱赶昆虫。| Synonym: tansy | Antonym: N/A | noun

bugwort:/ˈbʌɡwɜːrt/ | n. | 臭虫草；艾菊 |  Any of several other plants used to repel insects. | They used a mixture of herbs, including bugwort, to keep moths away from their clothes.  他们用包括臭虫草在内的几种草药混合在一起，防止飞蛾靠近他们的衣服。 | Synonym: insect repellent plant | Antonym: N/A | noun
