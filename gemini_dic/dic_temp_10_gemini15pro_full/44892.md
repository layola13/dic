cryogenics:/ˌkraɪoʊˈdʒɛnɪks/ | n. |低温学| The study of the production and behaviour of materials at very low temperatures. |  Cryogenics is essential for the development of superconducting materials. 低温学对于超导材料的发展至关重要。|  The laboratory specializes in cryogenics research.  这个实验室专门从事低温学研究。 | 近义词: low-temperature physics | 反义词: high-temperature physics | 词性: 名词

cryogenicses: This word is not standard English.  The correct plural form is "cryogenics" (always singular).
