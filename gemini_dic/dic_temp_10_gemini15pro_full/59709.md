eclat:/eɪˈklɑː/ /eɪˈklɑːt/ | n. |辉煌；光彩；显赫；喝彩|Brilliant display or effect; dazzling success. |Her performance won her considerable eclat. 她的表演为她赢得了相当大的喝彩。| 近义词: brilliance, splendor, glory, renown, acclaim | 反义词: obscurity, anonymity | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From French éclat, from éclater "to burst, break out," from Old French esclater, from Vulgar Latin *exclappitare, frequentative of Latin exclamare "to cry out."
