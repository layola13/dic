ossuary: /ˈɒʃuəri/ (US) /ˈɒsjuəri/ (UK)| n. | 骨灰存放处，藏骨堂 | A container or room in which the bones of dead people are kept. |  The catacombs of Paris are a vast ossuary. 巴黎地下墓穴是一个巨大的藏骨堂。

The monks placed the bones in the ossuary. 修士们把骨头放在藏骨堂里。

| 近义词: charnel house, bone-house | 反义词: None | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Late Latin *ossuarium*, from Latin ossuarius (“bony”), from os (“bone”) + -arius.
