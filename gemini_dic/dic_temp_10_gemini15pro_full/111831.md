loggia:/ˈlɒdʒə; US: ˈlɑːdʒiə/| n.|凉廊，走廊；敞廊|A roofed open gallery or arcade at the front of a building, especially on an upper floor.|例句：We sat in the loggia, enjoying the cool evening breeze. 我们坐在凉廊里，享受着凉爽的晚风。

例句：The loggia offered a stunning view of the valley. 凉廊可以欣赏到山谷的壮丽景色。|近义词：veranda, balcony, porch, gallery|反义词:room, interior
