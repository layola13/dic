deregulating:/diːˈreɡjʊleɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
取消管制；解除管制
The act of removing regulations or controls from something, typically a business or industry.
The government is deregulating the airline industry. 政府正在解除对航空业的管制。
The deregulating of the financial sector led to increased competition. 金融部门的放松管制导致竞争加剧。
synonyms: liberalizing, freeing, loosening
antonyms: regulating, controlling, restricting
v.
