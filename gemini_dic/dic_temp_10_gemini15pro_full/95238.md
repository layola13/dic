incalculability:/ɪnˌkælkjələˈbɪləti/
n.
不可估量性；无法计算性
The incalculability of the damage caused by the hurricane made it difficult to assess the needs of the affected population. 飓风造成的损失不可估量，使得难以评估受灾人口的需求。
The sheer incalculability of the universe makes it a source of wonder and awe. 宇宙的浩瀚无垠使其成为奇迹和敬畏的源泉。
近义词:immeasurability, unpredictability, indeterminacy
反义词:calculability, predictability, certainty
词性:noun

incalculable:/ɪnˈkælkjələbəl/
adj.
不可计算的；难以估量的
The benefits of education are incalculable. 教育的好处是不可估量的。
The risks involved in such a venture are incalculable.  这项风险投资涉及的风险是不可估量的。
近义词:immeasurable, unpredictable, inestimable
反义词:calculable, predictable, estimable
词性:adjective

词源:  From in- (not) + calculable (able to be calculated), which comes from Latin calculare (to calculate), from calculus (a small stone used in reckoning).
