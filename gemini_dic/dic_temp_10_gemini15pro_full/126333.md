myosin:/ˈmaɪəsɪn/ | n. | 肌球蛋白 | A fibrous protein that forms (together with actin) the contractile filaments of muscle cells and is also involved in motion in other types of cells. | Muscle contraction is caused by the interaction of myosin and actin.  肌肉收缩是由肌球蛋白和肌动蛋白的相互作用引起的。|  Myosin filaments slide along actin filaments, generating force. 肌球蛋白丝沿着肌动蛋白丝滑动，产生力。 | 近义词: actin (in the context of muscle contraction) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
