climbings:/ˈklaɪmɪŋz/
n.
攀登；攀缘；爬山运动
the sport or activity of climbing rocks or mountains
Example:  We went climbings in the Alps last summer. 我们去年夏天去阿尔卑斯山攀岩了。

Example:  Climbings can be a dangerous sport. 攀岩可能是一项危险的运动。

Near Synonyms:  ascents, scaling, mountaineering
Antonyms: descents, rappelling
词性: 名词 (Noun)


climb:/klaɪm/
v.
攀登，攀爬
to go up something using your feet and hands, especially something high or steep
Example: The children climbed the tree. 孩子们爬上了树。

Example:  He climbed onto the roof to fix the antenna. 他爬到屋顶上修理天线。

Near Synonyms: ascend, scale, mount
Antonyms: descend, go down
词性: 动词 (Verb)


climbing:/ˈklaɪmɪŋ/
adj.
攀登的；攀缘的
used for climbing
Example:  A climbing rose 攀缘玫瑰

Example: Climbing equipment 攀登设备

Near Synonyms:  ascending, scaling
Antonyms: descending
词性: 形容词 (Adjective)


词源 (Etymology):
From Middle English climben, from Old English climban, of Germanic origin.
