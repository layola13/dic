affluentness:/ˈæfluəntnəs/
noun
富裕；富足
The state of having a great deal of money; wealth.
Her affluentness allowed her to travel the world. 她的富裕让她可以环游世界。
近义词: wealth, prosperity, opulence, richness
反义词: poverty, destitution, indigence, poorness
词性:n.

Etymology:
From affluent + -ness.  Affluent comes from Latin *affluentem (nominative affluens), present participle of affluere "to flow to, abound," from ad- "to" + fluere "to flow".
