countersigned:/ˌkaʊntərˈsaɪnd/ | v. | 副署；连署；会签 | to sign (something, such as a document) after another person has signed it, in order to confirm or validate it | The check was countersigned by the manager.  支票由经理副签。| synonyms: cosign, endorse | antonyms:  reject, refuse | verb

countersigned:/ˌkaʊntərˈsaɪnd/ | adj. | 副署的；连署的；会签的 | bearing a countersignature |  The countersigned document was legally binding.  这份副署的文件具有法律约束力。 | synonyms: cosigned, endorsed | antonyms: unsigned, unendorsed | adjective
