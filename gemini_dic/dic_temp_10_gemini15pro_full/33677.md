cinnabarine:/ˈsɪnəˌbɑːraɪn/ |adj.|朱红色的，辰砂色的|Of a brilliant red color; vermilion.  Relating to or containing cinnabar.|The cinnabarine paint added a vibrant touch to the artwork.|近义词:vermilion, scarlet, crimson|反义词:azure, cerulean|词性:形容词
Etymology: From cinnabar + -ine.  Cinnabar comes from the Late Latin cinnabaris, from the Greek kinnabari, of uncertain origin, possibly from Persian.
