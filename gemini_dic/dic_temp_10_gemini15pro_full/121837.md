misremembering:/ˌmɪsrɪˈmɛmbərɪŋ/
|n. |记错，误记|The act of remembering something incorrectly.|I have a vague misremembering of meeting her at a party years ago. 我依稀记得几年前在一个派对上见过她。|近义词：misrecollection, false memory|反义词：remembering, recalling|词性:Noun (Gerund)
misremember:/ˌmɪsrɪˈmɛmbər/
|v. |记错，误记|To remember something incorrectly.|I misremembered the date of the meeting. 我记错了会议的日期。|近义词：misrecollect|反义词：remember, recollect|词性:Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (Old English, meaning "wrongly, badly") + remember (from Old French remembre, meaning "to remember").
