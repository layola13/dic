oestrogenicity:/ˌiːstrədʒəˈnɪsɪti/ | n. | 雌激素性，雌激素活性 | The oestrogenicity of a substance refers to its ability to mimic the effects of oestrogen in the body.  一种物质的雌激素性是指它在体内模拟雌激素作用的能力。 |  The researcher tested the oestrogenicity of the new compound. 研究人员测试了这种新化合物的雌激素性。 | The high oestrogenicity of this chemical can disrupt endocrine function. 这种化学物质的高雌激素性会扰乱内分泌功能。 | synonyms: estrogenic activity, estrogenic potency | antonyms: anti-oestrogenicity | noun
