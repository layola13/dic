choke-up:/ˈtʃoʊk ʌp/ (US) /ˈtʃəʊk ʌp/ (UK)| phrasal verb |哽咽；堵塞；（因紧张或激动而）说不出话来|To become unable to speak or continue speaking because of strong emotion, or to make someone feel this way.  It can also mean to block or fill something completely.|例句：He choked up when he talked about his mother.  他谈到母亲时哽咽了。|例句：The drain was choked up with hair.  排水管被头发堵住了。|近义词：suffocate, stifle, clog, obstruct|反义词: clear, unclog|词性:动词短语


choke up:/ˈtʃoʊk ʌp/ (US) /ˈtʃəʊk ʌp/ (UK)| adj. |（指人）哽咽的，说不出话的；（指管道等）堵塞的|Being in a state of being unable to speak due to strong emotion, or being blocked or filled completely.|例句：She was so choked up she couldn't finish her speech. 她哽咽得说不出话来，无法完成演讲。|例句：The pipe was completely choked up with leaves. 管道完全被树叶堵塞了。|近义词：blocked, clogged|反义词: clear, open|词性:形容词
