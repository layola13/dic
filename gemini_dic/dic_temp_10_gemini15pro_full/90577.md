hulk:/hʌlk/| n. v.| 笨重的人或物；庞然大物；(使)变绿巨人|A large, clumsy, or awkward person or thing.  To become extremely angry, especially in a way that causes you to become physically powerful, like the Hulk.|The abandoned ship lay rusting, a hulking presence in the harbor.  那艘废弃的船停泊在那里生锈，像个庞然大物。 He was hulking with rage. 他愤怒得像个绿巨人。|近义词：giant, behemoth, monster, brute|反义词:dwarf, midget|词性:名词，动词

hulk:/hʌlk/| v.| (使)变绿巨人|To become extremely angry, especially in a way that causes you to become physically powerful, like the Hulk.|Stress can make you hulk out. 压力会让你变成绿巨人。  He hulked up and smashed the table. 他变得像绿巨人一样愤怒，砸碎了桌子。|近义词：rage, fume, seethe|反义词:calm, relax|词性:动词
