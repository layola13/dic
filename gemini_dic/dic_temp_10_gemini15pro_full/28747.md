carnauba:/kɑːrˈnɔːbə/| n. |巴西棕榈蜡，巴西蜡|A hard wax obtained from the leaves of the carnauba palm, used in polishes, varnishes, and cosmetics.  从巴西棕榈叶中提取的一种硬蜡，用于抛光剂、清漆和化妆品中。|例句：Carnauba wax is highly valued for its durability. 巴西棕榈蜡因其耐用性而备受推崇。

|例句：The car was polished with carnauba wax. 这辆车是用巴西棕榈蜡抛光的。|近义词：wax|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "solvent" if considering its use in dissolving other substances) |词性:noun
