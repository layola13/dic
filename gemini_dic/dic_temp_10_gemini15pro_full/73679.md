forgetness:/fərˈɡɛtnəs/| n. |遗忘; 健忘|The state of being forgotten or unknown; oblivion.  A tendency to forget.|His forgetness led to him missing the important meeting. 他的健忘导致他错过了重要的会议。|近义词：oblivion, amnesia, forgetfulness|反义词:remembrance, mindfulness|noun

注意事项:

"Forgetness" while grammatically correct, is less commonly used than "forgetfulness."  While both denote a tendency to forget, "forgetfulness" is generally preferred in modern usage.  "Forgetness" might be encountered in older texts or poetic contexts.
