mythologized: /ˌmɪθəˈlɒdʒaɪzd/ | v. | 神话化；赋予神话色彩；使成为神话 | To make something or someone the subject of mythology, often by exaggerating or idealizing their qualities or deeds. |  King Arthur was mythologized as a noble and courageous leader.  亚瑟王被神话成一位高贵而勇敢的领袖。

The story of the founding of Rome has been mythologized over the centuries.  罗马建城的故事几个世纪以来一直被赋予神话色彩。 | 近义词: romanticized, idealized, glorified | 反义词: demythified, debunked | 词性: 动词

What's the point of mythologizing history?  神话历史的意义是什么？(Questioning the purpose)
