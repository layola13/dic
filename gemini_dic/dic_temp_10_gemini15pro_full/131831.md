nonconsolable:/ˌnɒnkənˈsəʊləbl/
adjective
无法安慰的， inconsolable
So filled with grief or misery as to be beyond comfort.
例句：
The parents were nonconsolable after the loss of their child.
父母在失去孩子后悲痛欲绝。

例句：
She was nonconsolable when her dog died.
她的狗死了，她伤心欲绝。

近义词：inconsolable, heartbroken, devastated, grief-stricken, distraught
反义词：comforted, soothed, relieved, cheerful, happy
词性: adjective
