borax:/ˈbɔːræks/ (US) /ˈbɔːræks/ (UK)| n.|硼砂；四硼酸钠|A white crystalline compound, hydrated sodium borate, used as a flux, in making glass, as a preservative, and as a mild antiseptic.|Borax is often used in laundry detergents. 硼砂常用于洗衣粉中。|Synonyms: sodium borate, sodium tetraborate|Antonyms: None|Noun

boraxes:/ˈbɔːræksɪz/ (US) /ˈbɔːræksɪz/ (UK)| n.|硼砂（复数）|The plural form of borax, referring to multiple instances or types of this compound.|Several different boraxes are used in industrial processes.  几种不同的硼砂用于工业生产过程。|Synonyms: sodium borates, sodium tetraborates|Antonyms: None|Noun
