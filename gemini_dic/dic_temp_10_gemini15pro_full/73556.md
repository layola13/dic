forestaller:/fɔːrˈstɔːlər/ | n. | 囤积者；阻碍者；抢先者 | Someone who forestalls something prevents it from happening by acting first. | The company acted as a forestaller, buying up all the available supplies to prevent competitors from getting them.  这家公司充当了囤积者的角色，买断了所有可用的物资，以阻止竞争对手获得它们。| Synonyms: preventer, obstructor, anticipator | Antonyms: facilitator, promoter, enabler | noun

forestall:/fɔːrˈstɔːl/ | v. | 抢先；阻止；先发制人 | To prevent something from happening by acting first. |  The government forestalled a potential crisis by taking swift action. 政府迅速采取行动，阻止了一场潜在的危机。| Synonyms: prevent, preclude, obviate, avert | Antonyms: allow, permit, facilitate | verb
