nondemocracy:/ˌnɒndɪˈmɒkrəsi/ | n. | 非民主; 非民主政体 | A system of government that is not a democracy, lacking the essential characteristics of free and fair elections, citizen participation, and protection of civil liberties. |  Many countries in the world are still struggling to transition from nondemocracy to democracy. 世界上许多国家仍在努力从非民主国家过渡到民主国家。

The rise of populism can threaten even established democracies, leading to a form of nondemocracy.  民粹主义的兴起甚至可能威胁到已建立的民主国家，导致某种形式的非民主。 | 近义词: authoritarianism, dictatorship, totalitarianism | 反义词: democracy | 词性: 名词
