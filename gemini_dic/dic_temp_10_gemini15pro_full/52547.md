diffluence:/ˈdɪfluəns/| n.|分流，汇流;  散开;  流出;|A diffluence is a flowing apart or outwards, especially of a stream or river. It can also refer to the place where this occurs.  It is often used in a more metaphorical sense to describe the spreading or scattering of something.|The diffluence of the river created a fertile delta. 这条河流的分流形成了一个肥沃的三角洲。|近义词：divergence, forking, branching, outflow|反义词：confluence, convergence, meeting|noun
