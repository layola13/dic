overflog:/ˌoʊvərˈflɒɡ/ /ˌoʊvərˈflɑːɡ/ | vt. |过度鞭打；过分责罚|To whip or flog excessively; to punish too harshly. |The slave was overflogged for a minor infraction.  这个奴隶因为一个小过错而被过度鞭打。

The captain overflogged the sailors for disobeying orders. 船长因为水手不服从命令而过度鞭打他们。|近义词：flog, whip, lash, scourge|反义词:  pamper, coddle|词性:动词

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "flog" (from Old Norse "fleygja," meaning "to strike").
