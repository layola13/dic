extrafoliaceous: /ˌɛkstrəfoʊliˈeɪʃəs/
adjective
外生的，叶外的；托叶外的
Growing on the outside of a leaf or leaves; situated outside of the sheath formed by a leaf base (as in grasses).
例句：The extrafoliaceous stipules were clearly visible on the stem.  茎上清晰可见托叶外的托叶。

例句：Extrafoliaceous buds develop outside the leaf axil.  外生芽在叶腋外发育。
synonym: exterior, external, outer, outside
antonym: interior, internal, inner, inside
词性: adjective
