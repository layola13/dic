muffle:/ˈmʌfəl/ (US), /ˈmʌfl/ (UK)| v. n. |使消声；抑制；裹住；围巾，头巾|to make a sound quieter or less clear; to wrap something up to keep it warm|例句：The heavy curtains muffled the street noise. 厚重的窗帘使街上的噪音减弱了。|近义词：deaden, mute, stifle, suppress, wrap, cover|反义词：amplify, enhance, uncover|词性:动词, 名词

muffle:/ˈmʌfəl/ (US), /ˈmʌfl/ (UK)| n. |消声器；围巾，头巾|something that makes a sound quieter, or something that you wear wrapped around your neck to keep it warm|例句：She wore a thick muffle around her neck. 她脖子上围着一条厚厚的围巾。|近义词：scarf, shawl, silencer|反义词：-|词性:名词
