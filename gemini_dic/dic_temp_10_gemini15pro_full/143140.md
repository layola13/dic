outcavil: /ˌaʊtˈkævɪl/
verb
胜过诡辩；以强词夺理胜过
to surpass or overcome in caviling; to outdo or defeat in argument by subtle or frivolous objections
He outcaviled his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中以强词夺理胜过了对手。
Synonyms: outargue, quibble, nitpick
Antonyms: concede, agree, yield
词性: 动词
