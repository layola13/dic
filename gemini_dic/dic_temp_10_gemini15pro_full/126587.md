nagarjuna:/ˌnʌɡərˈdʒuːnə/
n.|龙树菩萨; 那伽阏剌树那; 那伽阿周那|A major Buddhist philosopher of India (circa A.D. 150-250), Nagarjuna is considered the founder of the Madhyamaka (Middle Way) school, which emphasizes the doctrine of emptiness (sunyata).|Nagarjuna is a pivotal figure in Mahayana Buddhism. 龙树菩萨是大乘佛教中的关键人物。|synonyms: none (as a proper noun)|antonyms: none (as a proper noun)|noun
