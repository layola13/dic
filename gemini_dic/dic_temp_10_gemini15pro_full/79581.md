gloomy:/ˈɡluːmi/| adj.|阴郁的，沮丧的，昏暗的|Characterized by darkness, sadness, or depression.  Lacking in light or brightness.|The gloomy weather reflected my mood. 阴沉的天气反映了我的心情。|近义词：dark, dim, depressing, sad, somber, melancholy|反义词：bright, cheerful, sunny, happy, joyful|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *glomi, *glommy (“dark, shadowy, cloudy”), from Old English *glom (“twilight”), from Proto-Germanic *glōmaz (“twilight”). Cognate with Dutch glommen (“to glow”), German glommen (“to glimmer”).
