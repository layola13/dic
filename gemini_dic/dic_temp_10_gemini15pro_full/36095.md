coenenchym:/siːˈnɛŋkɪm/
n.
共肉
The common tissue of a coral colony.  The living tissue overlaying the coral skeleton and connecting the polyps.
Coral reefs are built up of coenenchyme secreted by coral polyps. 珊瑚礁是由珊瑚虫分泌的共肉堆积而成。
synonym:coenosarc
antonym:(No direct antonym, potentially "skeleton" in a contrasting sense)
noun
