disinvestiture:/dɪsɪnˈvɛstɪtʃər/
n.|撤资，剥离，出售|The act of selling assets, investments, or subsidiaries, often by a government or large corporation.|The government announced the disinvestiture of several state-owned enterprises. 政府宣布出售几家国有企业。|divestment, sale, disposal|investment, acquisition, purchase|noun
disinvestiture:/dɪsɪnˈvɛstɪtʃər/
n.|放弃，褫夺（权利，特权等）|The act of formally depriving someone of power, rights, or possessions.|The king ordered the disinvestiture of the traitor's lands and titles. 国王下令剥夺叛徒的土地和头衔。|deprivation, revocation, forfeiture|investiture, bestowal, grant|noun
