groundout:/ˈɡraʊndaʊt/| n.|滚地球出局|An act of a batter hitting a ground ball that results in an out, usually by being thrown out at first base.|The batter hit a weak groundout to the shortstop.  击球手打了一个软弱的滚地球给游击手。|近义词：ground ball out, fielder's choice (sometimes)|反义词:home run, fly out|词性:noun

groundout:/ˈɡraʊndaʊt/| v.|击出滚地球出局|To hit a ground ball that results in an out.|He grounded out to second base. 他击出一个滚地球到二垒出局。|近义词：ground out|反义词:homer, fly out|词性:verb
