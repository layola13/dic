commercialism: /kəˈmɜːʃəlɪzəm/ | n. | 商业主义，营利主义，商业精神 |  An emphasis on the maximizing of profit.  | Christmas has become driven by commercialism. 圣诞节已经变得受商业主义驱使。 | synonyms: mercantilism, materialism, moneymaking | antonyms: altruism, philanthropy | noun

commercialism: /kəˈmɜːʃəlɪzəm/ | n. | 商业化，商品化 | The process of turning something into a commodity. | The commercialism of sport is evident in the huge salaries paid to athletes.  体育的商业化在支付给运动员的巨额薪水中显而易见。 | synonyms: commodification, marketization | antonyms: non-commercialization | noun
