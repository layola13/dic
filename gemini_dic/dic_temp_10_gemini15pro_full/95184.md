inaugurate: /ɪˈnɔːɡjəreɪt/ (US) /ɪˈnɔːɡjʊreɪt/ (UK)
verb
就职；开幕；举行落成典礼
To formally begin or introduce (a system, policy, or period).  To induct into office with a formal ceremony.
The president was inaugurated in January. 总统在一月份就职。
The new theater was inaugurated with a performance of Shakespeare's Hamlet. 新剧院以演出莎士比亚的《哈姆雷特》开幕。
近义词: commence, begin, launch, initiate, install
反义词: conclude, terminate, end
词性: verb

inaugurate: /ɪˈnɔːɡjəreɪt/ (US) /ɪˈnɔːɡjʊreɪt/ (UK)
noun
就职典礼
The ceremony of inducting someone into office.
The presidential inaugurate was a grand affair.  总统就职典礼是一场盛大的活动。
近义词:  commencement, initiation, installation
反义词: conclusion, termination
词性: noun (rarely used)

词源: From Latin *inaugurare*, from *in-* "in, into" + *augurare* "to take omens," from *augur* "soothsayer, diviner."
