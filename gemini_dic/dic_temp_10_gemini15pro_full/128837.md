nightward:/ˈnaɪtwərd/| adj. adv.|夜间的，向夜间的|Moving or facing towards night; pertaining to the night. |The owl flew nightward. 猫头鹰朝夜间飞去。|He felt a nightward pull, a desire to return to the shadows. 他感到一种向夜的拉力，一种回归阴影的渴望。|近义词：nightly, nocturnal|反义词:dayward, diurnal|词性:形容词/副词
Etymology: From Old English *nihtweard, from *niht (night) + *weard (toward).  The "-ward" suffix denotes direction.
