negotiate:/nɪˈɡoʊʃieɪt/ (US) /nɪˈɡəʊʃieɪt/ (UK)| v. | 谈判，协商；商定；成功越过 |To discuss something formally in order to make an agreement; to successfully travel along or over a difficult route.| We negotiated a price for the car. 我们就汽车的价格进行了协商。| synonyms: bargain, haggle, confer, parley | antonyms: dictate, impose | verb

negotiate:/nɪˈɡoʊʃieɪt/ (US) /nɪˈɡəʊʃieɪt/ (UK)| n. | 协商，谈判 |The act of negotiating.| The negotiate was successful. 谈判成功了。| synonyms: bargaining, discussion, deliberation | antonyms: dictation, imposition | noun
