bitsy:/ˈbɪtsi/| adj.|微小的，极小的|Extremely small; tiny.|The child held a bitsy ladybug in her hand. 孩子手里握着一只小小的瓢虫。|近义词：tiny, minute, wee, diminutive|反义词：huge, enormous, gigantic|词性:形容词

Etymology:
Possibly a playful alteration of *bit*, perhaps influenced by *itsy-bitsy*.
