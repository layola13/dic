overfunds:/ˈoʊvərˌfʌndz/
n.
超额资金，过量资金
Excess funds; more funds than are needed or required.
The company had significant overfunds this quarter due to unexpectedly high sales.  这家公司本季度由于销售意外高涨而拥有大量超额资金。
The committee decided to invest the overfunds in a new project. 委员会决定将多余的资金投入到一个新项目中。
surplus, excess capital
deficit, shortfall
noun
