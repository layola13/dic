aeronautics: /ˌɛərəˈnɔːtɪks/ /ˌɛroʊˈnɔːtɪks/
noun
航空学;飞行术
The science or art involved with the study, design, and manufacturing of air flight capable machines, or the techniques of operating aircraft.
He studied aeronautics at university. 他在大学学习航空学。

The principles of aeronautics were not well understood at the time. 当时人们对航空学的原理还不太了解。

近义词: aviation, aerial navigation, airmanship
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
