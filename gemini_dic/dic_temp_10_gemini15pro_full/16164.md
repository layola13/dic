bankcard:/ˈbæŋkˌkɑːrd/ | n. | 银行卡 | A plastic card issued by a bank that allows the holder to access their bank account electronically, typically to withdraw cash or make purchases. | I used my bankcard to pay for groceries. 我用银行卡支付了杂货费用。 | My bankcard was stolen, so I had to report it lost. 我的银行卡被偷了，所以我不得不报告丢失。 | 近义词: ATM card, debit card, credit card | 反义词: cash | 词性: 名词
