nonhunting:/ˌnɒnˈhʌntɪŋ/ | adj. | 非狩猎的，不打猎的 | Not related to or involved in hunting.  |  Many birdwatchers enjoy nonhunting activities in nature reserves. 许多鸟类观察者喜欢在自然保护区进行非狩猎活动。 |  Example: The reserve is dedicated to nonhunting observation of wildlife.  这个保护区致力于对野生动物的非狩猎观察。| Synonyms: non-hunting, conservationist | Antonyms: hunting, predatory | adjective
