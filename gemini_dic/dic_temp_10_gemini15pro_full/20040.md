bionic:/baɪˈɒnɪk/ | adj. |仿生的；具有生物功能的| Having or enhanced by electronic or mechanical components that copy or improve on the functions of living organisms.|例句：The company is developing a bionic hand that can perform delicate tasks.  这家公司正在开发一只可以完成精细任务的仿生手。|例句：She received a bionic implant to improve her hearing. 她接受了仿生植入以改善听力。|近义词：artificial, prosthetic, cybernetic|反义词：natural, organic|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek *bio- 'life' + -ic. Coined in 1958.  源自希腊语 *bio- “生命” + -ic。1960年杜撰。
