antihierarchy:/ˌæntiˈhaɪərɑːrki/| n.| 反等级制度；反等级结构；反层次结构 |A system or organization that is opposed to or rejects traditional hierarchies or power structures.|  The antihierarchy movement within the company aimed to flatten the organizational structure and empower employees at all levels. 公司内部的反等级运动旨在扁平化组织结构，赋能各级员工。| synonyms: egalitarianism, non-hierarchical, horizontal organization | antonyms: hierarchy, hierarchical, stratified, graded | noun

antihierarchy:/ˌæntiˈhaɪərɑːrki/| adj.| 反等级的；反层次的 | Opposed to or rejecting traditional hierarchies or power structures.| The collective embraced an antihierarchy approach to decision-making, ensuring everyone had a voice.  这个集体采用了一种反等级的决策方式，确保每个人都有发言权。| synonyms: egalitarian, non-hierarchical, horizontal | antonyms: hierarchical, stratified, graded | adjective
