nonfiler:/ˈnɑːnˌfaɪlər/ /ˈnɒnˌfaɪlər/
noun
未申报者；不提交文件者
A person who fails to file a required document, especially a tax return.
The IRS is cracking down on nonfilers. 国税局正在严厉打击未申报者。
近义词:  tax evader, delinquent
反义词: filer, compliant taxpayer
词性: n.

nonfiler:/ˈnɑːnˌfaɪlər/ /ˈnɒnˌfaɪlər/
adjective
未申报的
Relating to or characteristic of a nonfiler.
The government is trying to locate nonfiler income. 政府正在努力寻找未申报的收入。
近义词: unreported, undeclared
反义词: filed, reported
词性: adj.
