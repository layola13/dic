choleriform:/ˈkɒlərɪfɔːm/ | adj. |  类似霍乱的，霍乱样的 | Resembling cholera; of the nature of cholera. | The patient presented with choleriform symptoms, including severe diarrhea and vomiting. 病人出现类似霍乱的症状，包括严重的腹泻和呕吐。 | Example: The outbreak exhibited choleriform characteristics, leading to widespread panic.  这次爆发呈现出类似霍乱的特征，导致 widespread恐慌。|Synonyms: choleraic | Antonyms: healthy, asymptomatic | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From cholera + -iform.  Cholera comes from Greek *choléra*, meaning "bile."  The suffix "-iform" comes from Latin "-formis," meaning "having the form of."
