outbid:/ˌaʊtˈbɪd/ | v. | 出价高于（某人）; 超过…的出价 | To offer a higher price than (someone else) for something, especially at an auction. |  I was outbid at the last minute. 我在最后一刻被超过出价了。 | Synonyms: overbid, outdo, surpass | Antonyms: underbid | verb

outbid:/ˌaʊtˈbɪd/ | v. |  胜过；超过 | To surpass someone in a particular activity. |  She outbid all her rivals for the contract.  她在竞争合同中胜过了所有对手。 | Synonyms: surpass, exceed, outstrip | Antonyms: lose to | verb
