ingenue:/ˈɪndʒəˌnjuː/ /ˈɑːnʒəˌnuː/| n.|天真无邪的少女; 天真烂漫的女子;  扮演天真少女的女演员|An ingenue is a young woman who is innocent and trusting.  |She played the ingenue in the play, a naive young woman who falls in love with a much older man. 她在这部戏里扮演了一个天真无邪的少女，爱上了一个比她大得多的男人。|Synonyms: innocent, naive, unsophisticated, artless|Antonyms: sophisticated, worldly, experienced|Noun
