coinvents:/ˌkoʊɪnˈvɛnts/
verb
共同发明
To invent something together with someone else.
Bell co-invented the telephone with Watson. 贝尔和沃森共同发明了电话。
近义词:collaborate on, develop jointly, create together
反义词:copy, plagiarize, steal
词性:v.

coinvent:/ˌkoʊɪnˈvɛnt/
noun
共同发明者
A person who invents something together with someone else.
The two scientists are recognized as co-inventors of the device.  这两位科学家被认为是该装置的共同发明者。
近义词:collaborator, joint developer, co-creator
反义词:imitator, plagiarist, thief
词性:n.
