damagingly:/ˈdæmɪdʒɪŋli/
adverb
有害地，破坏性地
In a way that causes damage or harm.
The company's reputation was damagingly affected by the scandal. 这家公司的声誉受到了丑闻的严重损害。

The oil spill damagingly impacted the local ecosystem.  石油泄漏对当地生态系统造成了破坏性影响。

Synonym: detrimentally, harmfully, destructively, injuriously
Antonym: beneficially, helpfully, constructively, positively
词性: 副词
