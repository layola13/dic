hotheadedness:/ˌhɒtˈhɛdɪdnəs/ /ˌhɑːtˈhɛdɪdnəs/| n.|性急，鲁莽，冲动|The quality of being easily angered and acting rashly.|His hotheadedness often got him into trouble. 他的冲动常常给他带来麻烦。|近义词：rashness, impulsiveness, recklessness, impetuosity|反义词：calmness, patience, levelheadedness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hotheaded + -ness.  Hotheaded (easily angered) is from hot (having a high temperature) + headed (having a head of a specified kind).  The metaphorical use of "hot" to describe anger or passion dates back to the 14th century.
