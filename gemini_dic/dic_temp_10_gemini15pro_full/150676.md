papyrography:/ˌpæpɪˈrɒɡrəfi/ | n. |  焦木画，烙画 | The art or process of producing pictures on wood, leather, or other material by burning the surface with a heated metallic point. | She took up pyrography as a hobby and now creates beautiful artwork on wood. 她把焦木画作为一种爱好，现在在木头上创作美丽的艺术品。 |  Synonyms: woodburning, pokerwork | Antonyms: N/A | noun
