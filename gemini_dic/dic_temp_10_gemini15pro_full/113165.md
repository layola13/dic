lupus:/ˈluːpəs/ | n. |狼疮;贪婪的人|A chronic autoimmune disease that can damage any part of the body (skin, joints, and/or organs inside the body).  一种慢性自身免疫性疾病，可损害身体的任何部位（皮肤、关节和/或体内器官）。|例句：She suffers from lupus. 她患有狼疮。

例句：He has a form of lupus that affects his kidneys. 他患有一种影响肾脏的狼疮。|近义词：disease, illness|反义词: health|词性:noun
