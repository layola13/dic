gyrate:/ˈdʒaɪˌreɪt/ | v. | 旋转，绕圈旋转 | To move in a circle or spiral, especially quickly. | The dancers gyrated wildly to the music. 舞者随着音乐疯狂地旋转。| synonyms: spin, whirl, twirl, rotate, revolve | antonyms: still, stationary | verb

gyrated:/ˈdʒaɪˌreɪtɪd/ | v. | 旋转过，绕圈旋转过 | Past tense of gyrate. | The top gyrated for a few seconds before falling over. 陀螺旋转了几秒钟后倒下了。| synonyms: spun, whirled, twirled, rotated, revolved | antonyms: stilled, stationaried | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "gyratus," past participle of "gyrare" meaning "to turn round in a circle," from Greek "gyros" meaning "circle" or "ring."
