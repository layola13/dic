barnacled: /ˈbɑːrnək(ə)ld/ | adj. |  被藤壶覆盖的；陈旧的，过时的 | Covered with barnacles; (figurative) old and outdated. | The barnacled hull of the ship slowed its progress.  这艘船被藤壶覆盖的船体减慢了它的前进速度。 | The museum displayed barnacled diving equipment from the early 20th century.  博物馆展出了20世纪早期被藤壶覆盖的潜水设备。 | 近义词：encrusted, overgrown, outdated | 反义词: clean, new, modern | 词性: 形容词

词源: From barnacle + -ed.  来自名词“barnacle”（藤壶）加上形容词后缀“-ed”。
