inquisitional:/ɪnˌkwɪzɪˈʃənəl/| adj.|审讯式的，酷似宗教法庭的|Relating to or resembling an inquisition, especially the Inquisition, in severity or harshness.|The atmosphere in the room was inquisitional, with everyone afraid to speak their minds. 房间里的气氛很像宗教法庭，每个人都不敢说出自己的想法。|The government's inquisitional tactics were widely condemned. 政府的审讯策略受到了广泛谴责。|近义词：inquisitive, probing, scrutinizing, examining, questioning|反义词：lenient, tolerant, merciful, forgiving|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin inquisitorius, from inquirere ‘inquire’.
