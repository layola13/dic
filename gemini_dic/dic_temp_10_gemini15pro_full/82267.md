groundlessnesses:/ˈɡraʊndləsnɪsɪz/
n.
无根据；无稽之谈；空穴来风
The state of being groundless; lack of justification or foundation.
The groundlessnesses of her accusations were clear to everyone. 她的指控毫无根据，每个人都清楚。
The report exposed the groundlessnesses of the conspiracy theory. 报告揭露了阴谋论的荒谬性。

近义词:falsity, unsubstantiatedness, baselessness, unfoundedness, spuriousness
反义词: validity, truth, soundness, legitimacy, justification
词性:名词
