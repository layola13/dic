geomagnetism:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈmæɡnɪtɪzəm/
n.|地磁，地磁学|The study of the Earth's magnetic field, its origins, properties, and variations.  地球磁场的起源，性质及其变化的研究。|The study of geomagnetism helps us understand the Earth's core.  地磁学研究帮助我们了解地球的核心。|近义词: terrestrial magnetism|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:
geo- (Greek): earth, land  地球，土地
magnetism (Latin, from magnes "magnet"):  磁力，来自拉丁语magnes，意为“磁铁”
