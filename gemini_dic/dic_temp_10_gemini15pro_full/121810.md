misreformed:/ˌmɪsrɪˈfɔːrmd/
adjective
错误改革的；改革不当的
incorrectly or incompletely reformed.
The misreformed banking sector continued to struggle.  错误改革的银行部门继续挣扎。
The report criticizes the misreformed education system.  该报告批评了改革不当的教育体系。
Synonyms:  malformed, flawed, defective, imperfect, faulty
Antonyms:  reformed, improved, corrected, amended, perfected
Word Origin:  "mis-" (badly, wrongly) + "reformed" (changed for the better)
