parostotic:/ˌpærɒˈstɒtɪk/| adj.|近骨的，骨旁的|Situated near or beside a bone; juxtaosseous.  Relating to or designating abnormal bone formation on the surface of a bone.|The parostotic growth was initially misdiagnosed as a simple bone spur.|Near-bone, juxtaosseous|Subchondral (referring to the bone beneath cartilage)|adjective
