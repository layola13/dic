internection:/ˌɪntərˈnɛkʃən/ | n. | 互相连接，互连 |The internection of roads and railways is crucial for efficient transportation. 道路和铁路的互连对于高效的交通至关重要。|例句：The internection of computer networks has revolutionized communication. 计算机网络的互连彻底改变了通信方式。 | 近义词：interconnection, network, nexus, linkage | 反义词：separation, isolation | 词性：名词

Etymology: From inter- (between) + -nection (act of connecting).  Ultimately from Latin "inter" (between) and "nectere" (to bind, tie).
