breakouts:/ˈbreɪˌkaʊts/| n.|突破；爆发；(尤指粉刺)疹子；越狱|A sudden increase in the number of something unpleasant, such as a disease or fighting.  A rash on the skin, typically small, red spots.|例句：There was a breakout of measles in the school. 学校爆发了麻疹。

例句：Stress can cause breakouts. 压力会导致长痘痘。|近义词：eruptions, outbreaks, rashes (for skin breakouts)|反义词：remission, clearing (for skin breakouts), containment (for disease or fighting)|词性:名词
