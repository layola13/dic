desensitize:/diːˈsɛnsɪtaɪz/
verb
使脱敏；使不再敏感
To make someone less sensitive to something.
Doctors try to desensitize patients to the allergens that trigger their symptoms. 医生试图使患者对引发他们症状的过敏原脱敏。

The constant violence in the media has desensitized many people to real-life suffering. 媒体上持续不断的暴力内容使许多人对现实生活中的苦难变得麻木。

近义词:numb, dull, inure
反义词:sensitize, sharpen

词性:verb
词源:de- (down, away from) + sense (feeling) + -ize (to make)
