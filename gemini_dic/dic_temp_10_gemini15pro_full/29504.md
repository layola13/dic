catarrhine:/ˈkætəˌraɪn/| adj. n.|狭鼻猴类的，狭鼻猴类动物|Of or relating to primates of the suborder Catarrhini, characterized by downward-pointing nostrils, as distinguished from the platyrrhine primates of the New World.|Old World monkeys and apes are catarrhine primates. 旧大陆的猴子和猿类是狭鼻猴类灵长动物。|The catarrhine nose is a defining feature. 狭鼻是其 определяющей характеристикой.|近义词：Old World monkey, ape|反义词：platyrrhine|词性：adjective, noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek κατά (katá, “down”) + ῥίς (rhís, “nose”).
