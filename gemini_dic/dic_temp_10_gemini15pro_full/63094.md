ensembled:/ɑːnˈsɑːmbəld/ | v. | 组合的，集成的 | brought together as a group; formed into a whole. | The orchestra was ensembled from the finest musicians in the city. 这支管弦乐队由全市最优秀的音乐家组成。|  The research team was carefully ensembled to include experts from various fields.  这个研究团队经过精心组合，包含了来自各个领域的专家。 | 近义词: assembled, gathered, collected, combined | 反义词: disassembled, separated, dispersed | 词性: 动词(过去分词形式，用作形容词)


词源:
From French *ensembler*, from *en-* (in) + *sembler* (to seem, to assemble), from Latin *simul* (together).  The original sense was "to gather together," and the modern usage retains this meaning, particularly in the context of groups of performers.
