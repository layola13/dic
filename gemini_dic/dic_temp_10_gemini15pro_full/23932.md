breakwater: /ˈbreɪkˌwɔːtər/ (US) /ˈbreɪkˌwɔːtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |防波堤；防浪堤|A structure built out into the sea to protect a coast or harbor from the force of waves.|The breakwater protected the harbor from the storm. 防波堤保护了港口免受风暴的袭击。|Synonyms: jetty, mole, seawall, groyne|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "open sea")|noun
