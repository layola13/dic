baffin:/ˈbæfɪn/ | n. adj.| 巴芬岛；巴芬湾；巴芬岛的|Baffin Island, a large island in northeastern Canada, or Baffin Bay, the sea between Baffin Island and Greenland. Also used as an adjective, relating to Baffin Island.|例句：They sailed across Baffin Bay. 他们横渡了巴芬湾。

Baffin Island is the largest island in Canada. 巴芬岛是加拿大最大的岛屿。|近义词：Arctic, northern (as adjectives) |反义词: (No direct antonym for a place name) |词性:noun, adjective


词源: Named after William Baffin, a 17th-century English explorer.
