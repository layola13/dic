engagingly:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪŋli/ | adv. | 迷人地，引人入胜地 | in a way that attracts and holds the attention; charmingly | She spoke engagingly about her travels. 她迷人地讲述了她的旅行。|  近义词: charmingly, captivatingly, interestingly, attractively | 反义词: dully, boringly, tediously | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From engage + -ing + -ly.  "Engage" comes from Old French "engager," meaning "to pledge."
