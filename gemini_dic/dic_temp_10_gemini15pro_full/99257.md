intercomplexity:/ˌɪntərˈkəmˈplɛksɪti/ | n. |相互复杂性，互联复杂性| The state or quality of being complex and interconnected with other complex systems or entities. It describes systems where multiple complex components interact and influence each other, leading to emergent behavior and intricate relationships. |例句：The intercomplexity of the global economy makes predicting market trends challenging. 全球经济的相互复杂性使得预测市场趋势具有挑战性。

例句：Understanding the intercomplexity of the human brain is a major goal of neuroscience. 了解人脑的相互复杂性是神经科学的主要目标。|近义词：interconnectedness, interdependence, intricacy |反义词: simplicity, independence |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "inter-" (prefix meaning "between, among, mutually") + "complexity" (from "complex" + "-ity"). "Complex" ultimately derives from the Latin "complexus," meaning "embraced, woven together."
