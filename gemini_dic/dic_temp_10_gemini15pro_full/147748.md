overproficient:/ˌoʊvərprəˈfɪʃənt/| adj.| 过于熟练的，能力过高的|  Having a level of skill or proficiency that exceeds what is considered necessary or typical for a particular task or role.  This word implies that the person's abilities are beyond the standard requirements and might even be a hindrance in some situations.| He's overproficient in Excel; he spends hours creating complex macros for simple tasks. 他在Excel方面过于精通；他花几个小时为简单的任务创建复杂的宏。

She's overproficient in French; she sometimes corrects native speakers, which can be a bit awkward. 她的法语过于熟练；她有时会纠正母语人士，这可能会有点尴尬。|近义词：overqualified, excessively skilled, overly competent|反义词：incompetent, unskilled, unqualified|词性:形容词
