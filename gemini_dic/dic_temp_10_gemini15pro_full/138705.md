nullification:/ˌnʌlɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/
n.
废除；无效；取消
The act of making something legally null and void; the state of being nullified.
The governor's veto led to the nullification of the law. 州长的否决导致了该法律的废除。
The treaty was declared a nullification. 该条约被宣布无效。
synonyms: annulment, revocation, repeal, invalidation
antonyms: enactment, validation, ratification
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin nullificatio, from Latin nullificare (“to make null”), from nullus (“none, no”) + facere (“to make, do”).
