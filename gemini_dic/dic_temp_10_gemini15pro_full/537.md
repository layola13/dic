abominators:/əˈbɒmɪneɪtəz/| n.|憎恶者，厌恶者|One who abominates; a detester; a hater.  指强烈憎恶或厌恶某事物或某人的人。|The abominators of cruelty rallied against the inhumane treatment of animals.  那些憎恶残忍行为的人们团结起来反对虐待动物。

Those who support the war are abominators of peace. 支持战争的人是和平的憎恶者。|近义词：detesters, haters, loathers, scorners|反义词：admirers, lovers, supporters|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *abominaten, from Late Latin abominārī (“to deprecate as an ill omen, to shudder at, to detest”), from Latin abōminor (“I deprecate as an ill omen”), from ab- (“away from”) + ōmen, ōminis (“omen, portent”).  Surface analysis is ab- +‎ -ominator.
