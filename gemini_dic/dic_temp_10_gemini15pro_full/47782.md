decanter's:/dɪˈkæntərz/
n.
醒酒器的，倾析器的
belonging to or associated with a decanter (a vessel for holding and serving decanted liquids, especially wine).
The decanter's stopper was missing. 醒酒器的塞子不见了。

反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bottle's" if contrasting with decanting)
词性:Possessive noun
