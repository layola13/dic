ostentatious:/ˌɑːstənˈteɪʃəs/ | adj. | 炫耀的，卖弄的 | characterized by vulgar or pretentious display; designed to impress or attract notice. | He drives an ostentatious sports car. 他开着一辆炫耀的跑车。|  She wore an ostentatious diamond necklace. 她戴着一条炫耀的钻石项链。 | synonyms: showy, pretentious, flamboyant, flashy | antonyms: modest, understated, unassuming, humble | adjective

ostentation:/ˌɑːstənˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 炫耀，卖弄 | pretentious and vulgar display, especially of wealth and luxury, intended to impress or attract notice. |  Her ostentation was evident in her designer clothes and expensive jewelry. 她的炫耀体现在她的名牌服装和昂贵的珠宝上。 | The ostentation of the wedding was breathtaking. 婚礼的奢华令人叹为观止。 | synonyms: showiness, pretentiousness, flamboyance, flashiness | antonyms: modesty, understatement, unassumingness, humility | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ostentātiō*, from *ostentāre* "to display," frequentative of *ostendere* "to show, exhibit," from *ob* "toward" + *tendere* "to stretch."
