deficient:/dɪˈfɪʃənt/
adjective
缺乏的，不足的
Lacking some element or characteristic; insufficient or inadequate.
例句：Their diet is deficient in vitamin C.  他们的饮食缺乏维生素C。

例句：The report was deficient in several key areas. 这份报告在几个关键领域存在不足。

近义词：lacking, insufficient, inadequate, meager, scant
反义词：sufficient, adequate, ample, abundant, plentiful
词性: adjective
