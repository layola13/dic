bonito:/bəˈniːtoʊ/ | n. |鲣鱼，金枪鱼的一种|A saltwater fish of the tuna family, often used in Japanese cuisine. |Example: We had some delicious bonito sashimi for lunch. 我们午餐吃了一些美味的鲣鱼刺身。

Example:  Bonito flakes are often used as a topping for okonomiyaki. 鰹魚片通常用作御好燒的配料。


|近义词：skipjack tuna, tuna|反义词:|词性:noun
