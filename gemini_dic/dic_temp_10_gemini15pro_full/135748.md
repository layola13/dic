nonproducible:/ˌnɒnprəˈdjuːsəbəl/ | /ˌnɑːnprəˈduːsəbəl/ | adj. | 不可生产的；无法复制的 | Not capable of being produced or reproduced.  | The original manuscript is nonproducible, so we must be careful with it. 原稿无法复制，所以我们必须小心处理。|  The artist's technique is so unique that it's practically nonproducible. 这位艺术家的技法非常独特，几乎无法复制。 |  Synonyms: unreproducible, irreplaceable, unique | Antonyms: reproducible, replaceable, common | adjective

nonproductive:/ˌnɒnprəˈdʌktɪv/ | /ˌnɑːnprəˈdʌktɪv/ | adj. | 非生产性的；徒劳的 | Not producing or capable of producing; not yielding results or profits. | The meeting was nonproductive and a waste of time.  这次会议没有成果，浪费了时间。|  He spent a nonproductive afternoon trying to fix the broken computer. 他花了一个毫无成果的下午试图修理坏掉的电脑。|  Synonyms: fruitless, futile, ineffective, inefficient, barren, sterile | Antonyms: productive, fruitful, effective, efficient, fertile | adjective
