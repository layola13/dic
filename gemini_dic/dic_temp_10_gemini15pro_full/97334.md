informer:/ɪnˈfɔːrmər/ /ɪnˈfɔːrmər/ | n. | 告密者，线人 |  A person who provides information, often secretly, to the police or other authorities. | The police relied on an informer within the gang to gather evidence. 警方依靠帮派内部的线人来收集证据。| synonym:  snitch, rat,  tipster, informant | antonym:  confidant,  loyalist | noun

informer:/ɪnˈfɔːrmər/ /ɪnˈfɔːrmər/ | n. |  通知者，报告者 | A person who gives information. | He acted as an informer for the company, reporting on market trends. 他担任公司的信息员，报告市场趋势。 | synonym:  reporter, advisor | antonym:  withholder | noun
