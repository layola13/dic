dedication:/ˌdedɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 奉献，献身；落成典礼；题献 | the quality of being dedicated or committed to a task or purpose; an act or ceremony of dedication |  The teacher's dedication to her students was inspiring.  这位老师对学生的奉献精神令人鼓舞。 | synonyms: commitment, devotion, allegiance | antonyms: apathy, indifference | noun

dedicate:/ˈdedɪkeɪt/ | v. | 奉献，献身；题献；致力于 | devote (time, effort, or oneself) to a particular task or purpose | She dedicated her life to helping others. 她毕生致力于帮助他人。 | synonyms: devote, commit, pledge | antonyms: forsake, abandon | verb
