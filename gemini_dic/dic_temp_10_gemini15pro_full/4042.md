aidant:/ɛdɑ̃/ (French) | n. | 助手；护理员；陪护人员 | A person who provides assistance and support to someone in need, particularly in the context of healthcare or social services. | Example: The aidant helped the elderly woman with her daily tasks.  护理员帮助老太太做日常工作。

Example:  My grandmother has an aidant who comes in a few hours a day. 我祖母有一个每天来几个小时的护理员。


|Synonym: helper, assistant, caregiver, supporter|Antonym: (No direct antonym, consider) dependent, charge | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "aidant," present participle of "aider" (to help), from Latin "adiutare" (to help, assist).
