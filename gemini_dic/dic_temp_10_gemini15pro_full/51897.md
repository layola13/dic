dharmic:/ˈdɑːrmɪk/| adj.|与佛法相关的，合乎佛法的|Relating to dharma, righteousness, or duty, especially in Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism, and Sikhism.  It describes actions, thoughts, and ways of life that are considered morally upright and in accordance with cosmic order.|例句：She leads a dharmic life, dedicated to helping others. 她过着合乎佛法的生活，致力于帮助他人。

|例句：His actions were considered dharmic and brought him good karma. 他的行为被认为是合乎佛法的，并给他带来了好的业力。|近义词：righteous, virtuous, ethical, moral|反义词：adharmic, unrighteous, immoral|词性:形容词
