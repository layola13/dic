geocronite:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈkrɒnaɪt/ | n. | 硫锑铅矿 | A lead-gray mineral, a sulfosalt of lead and antimony, Pb5(Sb,As)2S8. It occurs as prismatic or tabular crystals, or in granular to compact masses, in veins with other lead and antimony ores. |  Geocronite is a relatively rare mineral, usually found in association with stibnite. 硫锑铅矿是一种相对稀有的矿物，通常与辉锑矿共生。|  The museum's collection includes a fine specimen of geocronite. 该博物馆的藏品中有一块精美的硫锑铅矿标本。 | synonyms:  jordanite, meneghinite (similar sulfosalts) | antonyms: None (as it's a specific mineral, it doesn't have a direct opposite) | noun
