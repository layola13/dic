acetones:/ˈæsɪˌtoʊnz/| n.|丙酮|A colorless, volatile, flammable liquid ketone, (CH3)2CO, used as a solvent.|Nail polish remover often contains acetone.  指甲油去除剂通常含有丙酮。

The strong smell of acetone filled the lab.  实验室里充满了刺鼻的丙酮味。|近义词：dimethyl ketone (二甲基酮，化学名称), solvent (溶剂)|反义词:|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Aceton*, from *Aketon*, from Latin *acetum* ("vinegar") + *-on* (ketone suffix). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂éḱ- (“sharp; pointed; acid”).  来自德语 *Aceton*，源自 *Aketon*，来自拉丁语 *acetum*（“醋”）+ *-on*（酮后缀）。最终来自原始印欧语 *h₂éḱ-（“锋利的；尖的；酸的”）。
