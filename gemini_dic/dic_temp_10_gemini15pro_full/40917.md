coolest:/ˈkuːlɪst/ | adj. |最酷的，最冷静的| superlative form of cool; fashionable, stylish, impressive, excellent; calm, composed | Example: That's the coolest car I've ever seen. 那是我见过的最酷的车。| Example: He remained coolest under pressure. 他在压力下保持最冷静。|Synonyms: trendiest, hippest, chicest, most fashionable, calmest, most composed | Antonyms: uncoolest, lamest, dorkiest, nerdiest, most anxious, most agitated | Word Class: Adjective

Etymology:
From cool + -est (superlative suffix).  "Cool" has its origins in the Old English "col," meaning "moderately cold," and gradually evolved to encompass meanings related to calmness, composure, and eventually, stylishness and excellence.
