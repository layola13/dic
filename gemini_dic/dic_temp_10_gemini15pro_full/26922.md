calamite:/ˈkæləˌmaɪt/| n.|磁铁矿，天然磁石|A naturally magnetic mineral, specifically a variety of magnetite.|Lodestone, a type of calamite, was used in early compasses. 天然磁石，一种磁铁矿，曾用于早期的指南针。|Synonym: lodestone, magnetite|Antonym: (No direct antonym for a naturally occurring mineral)|Noun
