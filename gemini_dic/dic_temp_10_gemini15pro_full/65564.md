euxanthic:/juːˈzænθɪk/
adj.
euxanthic acid的；印度黄的
Relating to or containing euxanthic acid; of the colour of Indian yellow.
The artist used euxanthic pigments to create a luminous glow.  艺术家使用印度黄颜料来营造发光的效果。
近义词:Indian yellow, gamboge
反义词: (No direct antonym for a color.  Could consider contrasting colors like blue or violet.)
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From euxanth- + -ic. Euxanth- from Greek *εὐξανθής (euxanthēs, “deep yellow”) from εὖ (eu, “well”) + ξανθός (xanthos, “yellow”).
