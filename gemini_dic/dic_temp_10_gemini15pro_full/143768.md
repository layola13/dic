outmeasure:/aʊtˈmɛʒər/
verb
超过，胜过
to surpass in measure; to exceed in size, extent, degree, or quantity.
Our team outmeasured theirs in both skill and stamina.  我们的团队在技术和耐力上都超过了他们的团队。
The benefits outmeasure the risks. 好处大于风险。
synonyms: surpass, exceed, outdo, outperform, eclipse, overshadow
antonyms: fall short of, be inferior to, be surpassed by
