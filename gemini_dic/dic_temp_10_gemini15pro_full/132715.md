nondistributive:/ˌnɒndɪˈstrɪbjətɪv/ | adj. |非分配的| Not distributive; not following the distributive law.  A nondistributive operation is one where applying it to a combination of elements is not the same as applying it separately to each element and then combining the results. |The operation of averaging is nondistributive.  对数值进行平均是非分配运算。|  The concatenation operation on strings is nondistributive. 字符串的连接操作是非分配的。|  近义词: non-commutative (in some contexts)| 反义词: distributive| 词性: adjective
