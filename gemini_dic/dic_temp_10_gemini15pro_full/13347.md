atlantides:/ætˈlæntɪdiːz/
n.
亚特兰蒂斯（复数）
A mythical island continent that is said to have sunk beneath the Atlantic Ocean.
Plato's dialogues "Timaeus" and "Critias" tell the story of Atlantis, a powerful and advanced island nation that waged war against Athens but eventually sank into the ocean. 柏拉图的对话录《蒂迈欧篇》和《克里底亚篇》讲述了亚特兰蒂斯的故事，这是一个强大而先进的岛国，曾与雅典交战，但最终沉入海底。
Atlantis is often portrayed as a technologically advanced utopia. 亚特兰蒂斯经常被描绘成一个技术先进的乌托邦。
近义词:  lost city, utopia
反义词: reality
