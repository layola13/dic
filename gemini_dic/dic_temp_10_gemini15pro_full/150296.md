panentheism:/ˌpænˈɛnθiːɪzəm/| n. |泛神论|The belief that God is both immanent and transcendent, encompassing the universe but also existing beyond it.  God is in everything, and everything is in God, but God also transcends the universe.|例句：Panentheism offers a middle ground between theism and pantheism. 泛神论提供了一种介于有神论和泛神论之间的中间立场。

例句：She explored various spiritual philosophies, eventually finding resonance with panentheism. 她探索了各种精神哲学，最终与泛神论产生了共鸣。|近义词：panentheistic theory, process theism|反义词: atheism, deism|词性:noun
