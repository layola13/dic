murmurous:/ˈmɜːrmərəs/ (US), /ˈmɜːmərəs/ (UK)| adj.|低语的；喃喃的；潺潺的|Characterized by a low, continuous, indistinct sound, like that of murmuring.  Producing a murmur.|The murmurous sound of the stream was soothing. 小溪潺潺的流水声令人舒缓。|Synonyms: whispering, rustling, babbling, gurgling|Antonyms: loud, boisterous, silent, still|adjective
