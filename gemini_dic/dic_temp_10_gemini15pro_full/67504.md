extracranial:/ˌekstrəˈkreɪniəl/| adj.|颅外的，颅骨外的|Located or occurring outside the skull or cranium.|例句：Extracranial Doppler ultrasound can detect blockages in the arteries leading to the brain.  颅外多普勒超声可以检测通向大脑的动脉阻塞。|例句：The extracranial portion of the internal carotid artery is a common site for atherosclerosis. 颈内动脉的颅外部分是动脉粥样硬化的常见部位。|近义词：exocranial|反义词:intracranial|词性:形容词

词源:
extra- (prefix): outside, beyond  来自拉丁语 extra "outside of, beyond"
cranial (adjective): relating to the skull  来自拉丁语 cranium "skull," 来自希腊语 kranion "skull."
