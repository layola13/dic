abstractnesses:/ˈæbstræktnɪsɪz/
noun
抽象性；抽象
The state or quality of being abstract.  The level or degree to which something is abstract.
His paintings are known for their color and abstractnesses. 他的绘画以色彩和抽象性而闻名。
The abstractnesses of the concept made it difficult to grasp.  这个概念的抽象性使它难以理解。
