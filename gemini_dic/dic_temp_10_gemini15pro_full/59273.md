eagerly:/ˈiːɡərli/| adv.|热切地，渴望地|In a way that shows great enthusiasm and impatience.|She eagerly awaited his arrival. 她热切地等待着他的到来。| 近义词:keenly, avidly, enthusiastically|反义词:reluctantly, apathetically|词性:副词

词源: From Middle English *egerly, from Old French *aigreli, from Vulgar Latin *ācre, from Latin acer (“sharp, keen”) + -ly.
