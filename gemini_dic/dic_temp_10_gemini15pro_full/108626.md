leadhillite:/ˈlɛdɪlaɪt/
noun
铅矾
A sulfate-carbonate mineral of lead, typically occurring as colourless or white monoclinic crystals.
Leadhillite is a rare mineral found in oxidized lead deposits. 铅矾是一种罕见的矿物，发现于氧化的铅矿床中。
Leadhillite specimens can be quite beautiful and are prized by collectors. 铅矾标本非常漂亮，受到收藏家的珍视。
