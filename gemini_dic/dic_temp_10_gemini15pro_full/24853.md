brownite:/ˈbraʊnaɪt/| n. |褐锰矿|A brownish-black mineral consisting of manganese oxide, important as an ore of manganese.  It is a manganese oxide mineral with the formula Mn₂O₃.|Brownite is an important source of manganese. 褐锰矿是锰的重要来源。|Brownite deposits are found in several locations around the world. 全球多个地方都发现了褐锰矿矿床。|Synonyms:  manganese ore, hausmannite (similar mineral)|Antonyms: N/A (Minerals typically don't have direct antonyms)|noun
