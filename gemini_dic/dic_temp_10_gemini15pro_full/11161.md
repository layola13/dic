archetype:/ˈɑːrkɪtaɪp/| n.|原型，典型|An original model or pattern from which other things are copied; a perfect or typical example of something.|例句：The hero is an archetype of courage and strength. 英雄是勇气和力量的原型。|近义词：prototype, model, exemplar, paradigm|反义词：anomaly, aberration|noun

archetypal:/ˌɑːrkɪˈtaɪp(ə)l/| adj.|原型的，典型的|Of or relating to an archetype; very typical.|例句：The archetypal villain is often cruel and greedy. 典型的反派通常是残酷和贪婪的。|近义词：prototypical, exemplary, classic|反义词：atypical, unusual|adjective
