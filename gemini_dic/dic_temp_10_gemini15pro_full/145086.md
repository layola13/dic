overanxious:/ˌoʊvərˈæŋkʃəs/| adj.|过度焦虑的，过分担忧的|Excessively anxious; unduly apprehensive about actual or potential problems.|She was overanxious about the upcoming exam. 她对即将到来的考试过度焦虑。|He's overanxious about his health. 他过分担心自己的健康。|近义词:apprehensive, worried, uneasy, fretful|反义词:calm, relaxed, composed|词性:adjective

词源:
From over- + anxious.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Anxious" comes from the Latin "anxius," meaning "solicitous, uneasy, troubled in mind."
