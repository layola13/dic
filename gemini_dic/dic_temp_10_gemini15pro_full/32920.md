choosier:/ˈtʃuːziər/| adj.|更挑剔的，更讲究的|Comparative form of choosy: more careful in making a choice.|She's become choosier about the roles she accepts. 她对所接受的角色变得更加挑剔了。|近义词：pickier, fussier, more selective|反义词：less choosy, less selective|词性:形容词
Etymology: From choose + -y + -er.  Ultimately from Old French choisir.
