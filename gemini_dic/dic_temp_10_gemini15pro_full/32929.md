chop's:/tʃɑːps/| n.|食物，伙食，尤指某人的份额|A person's share of food, especially in a particular situation or place.  It's often used informally.  Derived from "chops," meaning jaws or mouthparts, and by extension, the act of eating or the food itself.|例句：Where's my chop's? 我的食物在哪？

例句：The dog wolfed down its chop's. 狗狼吞虎咽地吃掉了它的食物。|近义词：food, grub, meal, victuals|反义词: None (lack of food could be considered an antonym conceptually)|词性:名词
