graybeards:/ˈɡreɪˌbɪərdz/
noun
灰胡子，长老，年长而有智慧的人
Old men, especially those known for their wisdom and experience.
The graybeards of the council offered their sage advice.  议会的长老们提出了他们睿智的建议。
synonyms: elders, veterans, sages, old-timers
antonyms: youngsters, youths, novices
n.
