drawer:/ˈdrɔːr (US) / /ˈdrɔːə(r) (UK)| n. |抽屉；开票人，出票人|A sliding compartment in a piece of furniture, such as a desk or chest, used for storage.|I keep my socks in the top drawer. 我把袜子放在最上面的抽屉里。|Synonyms: compartment, tray, slot |Antonyms: cupboard, closet|noun

drawer:/ˈdrɔːr (US) / /ˈdrɔːə(r) (UK)| n. |出票人|A person who draws or issues a check, draft, or other order for payment.|The drawer of the check was identified as John Smith.  支票的出票人是约翰·史密斯。|Synonyms: issuer, payer |Antonyms: payee, recipient|noun
