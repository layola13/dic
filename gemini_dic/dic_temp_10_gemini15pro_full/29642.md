caters:/ˈkeɪtərz/ (US), /ˈkeɪtəz/ (UK)| v. |提供饮食；迎合；满足|provides food and drinks for a social event; satisfies the needs of a particular group or market| The restaurant caters for weddings and other special occasions. 这家餐厅承办婚礼和其他特殊场合的餐饮。|近义词：provides, serves, supplies, accommodates, satisfies|反义词: ignores, neglects | verb

caters:/ˈkeɪtərz/ (US), /ˈkeɪtəz/ (UK)| v. |迎合；投合|satisfies the needs of a particular group or market; tries to please someone by doing or providing what they want| The magazine caters to a young, female audience. 这本杂志迎合年轻女性读者。|近义词：pampers, indulges, gratifies |反义词: ignores, neglects| verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *catouren*, from Anglo-Norman *acatour*, from *acater* (“to buy”), from Late Latin *accaptare*, from Latin *ad* (“to”) + *captare* (“to seize, to try to get”), frequentative of *capere* (“to take, hold”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*kap-* (“to grasp”).
