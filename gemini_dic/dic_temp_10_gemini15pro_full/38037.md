comport:/kəmˈpɔːrt/| v. |相称；一致；举止；表现|To behave in a particular way, or to be in agreement with something.|例句：Her dignified behavior did not comport with his idea of a rock star's wife. 她的端庄举止与他对摇滚明星妻子的想象不符。|近义词：accord, agree, conform, harmonize|反义词：clash, disagree, conflict|词性：动词

comport with:/kəmˈpɔːrt wɪð/| phrasal verb |与...相符；与...一致|To be in agreement or harmony with something; to be suitable for or appropriate to something.|例句：His actions do not comport with his words. 他的言行不一。|近义词：accord with, agree with, conform to, be consistent with|反义词：clash with, disagree with, conflict with|词性：短语动词
