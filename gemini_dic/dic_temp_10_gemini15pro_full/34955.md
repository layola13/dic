clockwise:/ˈklɒkwaɪz/ (UK) /ˈklɑːkwaɪz/ (US)| adj. adv.|顺时针方向的；顺时针地|Moving in the same direction as the hands of a clock.|Turn the knob clockwise.  把旋钮顺时针旋转。|近义词：sunwise|反义词:anticlockwise, counterclockwise|词性:形容词, 副词

Etymology:
From clock +‎ -wise, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *klukkaną (“to cluck, strike like a hen”), thus relating to the sound of early clocks, and *weisaz (“way, manner, direction”).
