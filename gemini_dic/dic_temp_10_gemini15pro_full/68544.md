fane:/feɪn/
n.
庙宇，神殿，教堂
A temple or shrine, especially one dedicated to a particular deity.
The ancient fane was crumbling, yet still held an aura of reverence. 古老的神殿正在崩塌，但仍然拥有一种令人敬畏的气氛。
temple, shrine, sanctuary
---
Etymology:
From Middle English *fane, from Old French *fane, from Latin *fanum (“temple, shrine, sanctuary”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeh₂- (“to do, put, place”). Cognate with Ancient Greek θάνος (thános, “death”).
