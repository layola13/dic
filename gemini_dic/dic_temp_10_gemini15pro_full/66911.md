expeditive:/ɪkˈspiːdiətɪv/ /ɛkˈspiːdiətɪv/
adjective
权宜之计的；方便的；迅速的
done with speed and efficiency, but sometimes without thought about what is fair or right
例句：The government took some expeditive measures to control the spread of the virus. 政府采取了一些权宜之计来控制病毒的传播。

例句：Taking an expeditive route, they bypassed the traffic jam. 他们选择了一条便捷的路线，绕过了交通堵塞。
近义词：efficient, quick, swift, prompt, practical
反义词：slow, inefficient, cumbersome, deliberate
词性：adjective
