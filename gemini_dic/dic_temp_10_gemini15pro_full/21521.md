例句：In the 18th century, blue-stocking circles provided intellectual stimulation for women. 在18世纪，蓝袜子圈为女性提供了智力上的刺激。|近义词：intellectual, academic, bookworm, highbrow|反义词：philistine, lowbrow|词性: noun
