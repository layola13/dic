cimer:/ˈsiːmər/ (US), /ˈsaɪmər/ (UK)| interj. |谢谢 (非正式，网络用语)| Cimer is an internet slang term used primarily in Turkey, derived from "See you later," and used as a casual way of saying "Thank you." | Cimer for the coffee! 谢谢你的咖啡！| Cimer for the help, I really appreciate it. 谢谢你的帮助，我真的很感激。 | 近义词: thanks, thank you, teşekkürler (Turkish) | 反义词: N/A | 词性:感叹词
