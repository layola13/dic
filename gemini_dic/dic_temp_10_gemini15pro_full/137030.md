nonstationary:/ˌnɒnˈsteɪʃənəri/ /ˌnɑːnˈsteɪʃəneri/| adj.|非平稳的，非静止的|Not stationary; not constant or unchanging over time.  Referring to a process or time series whose statistical properties (like mean and variance) change over time.|The economist noted the nonstationary nature of the stock market. 经济学家指出了股票市场的非平稳性。|fluctuating, volatile, unstable, dynamic|stationary, stable, constant|adjective
