artichoke:/ˈɑːrtɪˌtʃoʊk/ (US), /ˈɑːtɪˌtʃəʊk/ (UK)| n.|朝鲜蓟|A thistle-like plant cultivated as a food crop for its large flower head, which has fleshy, edible scales and a heart.|I love steamed artichokes with a little butter. 我喜欢加一点黄油蒸朝鲜蓟。|Synonym: globe artichoke|Antonym: N/A|noun

artichoke heart:/ˈɑːrtɪˌtʃoʊk hɑːrt/ (US), /ˈɑːtɪˌtʃəʊk hɑːt/ (UK)| n.|朝鲜蓟心|The fleshy, edible center of an artichoke, considered the most desirable part.|Artichoke hearts are delicious marinated.  腌制的朝鲜蓟心很美味。|Synonym: heart of artichoke|Antonym: N/A|noun
