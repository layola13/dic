nonbiographical:/ˌnɒnˌbaɪəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
非传记的，非自传的
Not relating to or based on a person's life story.
Example: The novel is a nonbiographical work of fiction. 这部小说是一部非传记的虚构作品。
Example:  He preferred to write nonbiographical stories, focusing on universal themes rather than personal experiences. 他更喜欢写非自传体的故事，关注普遍的主题而不是个人经历。
Synonym: fictional, invented, imaginative
Antonym: biographical, autobiographical
词性: adjective
