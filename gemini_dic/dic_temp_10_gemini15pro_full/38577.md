concord:/ˈkɑːŋkɔːrd/ (US), /ˈkɒŋkɔːd/ (UK)| n.|和谐，一致，和睦；协议，条约|A state of agreement or harmony between people or things. |例句：There was complete concord among the delegates. 代表们意见完全一致。|近义词：harmony, agreement, unity, accord|反义词：discord, disagreement, conflict|noun
concord:/ˈkɑːŋkɔːrd/ (US), /ˈkɒŋkɔːd/ (UK)| n.|（语法）一致，呼应|Grammatical agreement between words, especially between a verb and its subject in person and number. |例句：The verb must be in concord with its subject. 动词必须与其主语一致。|近义词：agreement, accordance|反义词：disagreement, discordance|noun
