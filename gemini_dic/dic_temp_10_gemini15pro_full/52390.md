didacticity:/ˌdɪdækˈtɪsɪti/
n.
教导性，教训意味
the quality of being didactic; designed or intended to teach
The didacticity of the novel made it feel more like a textbook than a story. 这部小说的教导性使其感觉更像教科书而不是故事。
近义词：instructiveness, preachiness, moralizing
反义词：subtlety, nuance, suggestiveness
词性: noun

didactic:/daɪˈdæktɪk/
adj.
教诲的，说教的
intended to teach, particularly in having moral instruction as an ulterior motive
The professor's lectures were didactic, focusing on conveying information rather than encouraging discussion.  这位教授的讲座带有说教意味，侧重于传达信息而不是鼓励讨论。
近义词：instructive, educational, pedagogical
反义词：suggestive, subtle, nuanced
词性: adjective
