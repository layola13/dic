need-to-know:/ˌniːd tə ˈnoʊ/ | adj. | 需要知道的，必知的 | Essential information or knowledge that someone must have. |  Example: The CEO gave us a need-to-know briefing on the merger. 首席执行官向我们简要介绍了合并的必要信息。 | Example:  Access to this classified information is strictly on a need-to-know basis.  接触这些机密信息是严格按照需要知道的原则进行的。 | Synonym: essential, crucial, vital, critical | Antonym:  unnecessary, trivial, unimportant | adjective
