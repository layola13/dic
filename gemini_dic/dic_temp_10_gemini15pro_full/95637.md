incompetents: /ɪnˈkɒmpɪtənts/ /ɪnˈkɑːmpɪtənts/ | n. | 无能的人；不胜任的人；不合格的人 | People who lack the necessary skills or abilities to perform a task or job effectively.  Those who are not competent. | The project was hampered by a team of incompetents. 这个项目被一群无能的人阻碍了。| The company is riddled with incompetents who can't even perform the simplest tasks.  这家公司充斥着连最简单的任务都无法完成的无能之辈。 | synonyms: bunglers, duffers, inept, unskilled, unqualified | antonyms: experts, professionals, skilled, competent, qualified | Noun

Etymology:

From in- (not) + competent (capable). "Competent" comes from Latin "competens," present participle of "competere" (to be suitable, agree, coincide).
