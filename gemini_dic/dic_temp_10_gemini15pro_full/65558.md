eutrophication:/ˌjuːtrəfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |富营养化|Excessive richness of nutrients in a lake or other body of water, frequently due to runoff from the land, which causes a dense growth of plant life and death of animal life from lack of oxygen.|The eutrophication of the lake has led to a decline in fish populations.  湖泊的富营养化导致鱼类数量减少。| Lake Erie suffered from eutrophication due to phosphorus pollution. 伊利湖由于磷污染而遭受富营养化。|synonyms: hypertrophication|antonyms: oligotrophication|noun
