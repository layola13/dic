excelling:/ɪkˈsɛlɪŋ/
|verb (present participle of excel)| 擅长；胜过；优于
|Being outstanding or very good at something.
|She is excelling in her studies. 她学习很优秀。
|The company is excelling in customer service.  这家公司在客户服务方面表现出色。
|近义词：succeeding, thriving, flourishing, outperforming, surpassing
|反义词：failing, lagging, underperforming

词性: verb (present participle)
词源: From Latin excellere "to rise, surpass," from ex- "out" + -cellere "to rise high"
