antisyndication:/ˌæntiˌsɪndɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
反辛迪加化；反联合组织化
The act of opposing or preventing syndication, especially in the context of online content distribution.  This might involve technical measures or legal agreements.
Website owners often employ antisyndication measures to protect their original content. 网站所有者经常采取反联合组织措施来保护他们的原创内容。
News organizations sometimes use antisyndication clauses in their contracts.  新闻机构有时会在合同中使用反联合组织条款。
nonsyndication, content protection
syndication, distribution
noun
