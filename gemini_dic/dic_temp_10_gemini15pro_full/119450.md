micromanage:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmænɪdʒ/ | v. | 微观管理；事无巨细地管理 | to control every detail of a project or activity, often in a way that is annoying to the people involved |  The new boss is known for micromanaging his employees. 新老板以事无巨细地管理员工而闻名。

My previous manager micromanaged everything, which made it difficult to be creative.  我以前的经理对所有事情都进行微观管理，这使得很难有创造性。 | 近义词: overmanage, overcontrol | 反义词: delegate, empower | 词性: 动词

micromanagement: /ˌmaɪkroʊˈmænɪdʒmənt/ | n. | 微观管理；事无巨细的管理 | the act of controlling every detail of a project or activity, often in a way that is annoying to the people involved | The company suffered from excessive micromanagement.  这家公司因过度微观管理而受损。

Micromanagement can stifle creativity and innovation. 微观管理会扼杀创造力和创新。 | 近义词: overmanagement, overcontrol | 反义词: delegation, empowerment | 词性: 名词

micromanager: /ˌmaɪkroʊˈmænɪdʒər/ | n. | 微观管理者；事无巨细的管理者 | a person who micromanages | He's a notorious micromanager who constantly checks up on his staff. 他是一个臭名昭著的微观管理者，不断地检查他的员工。

Working for a micromanager can be incredibly frustrating. 为一个事无巨细的管理者工作会令人难以置信地沮丧。 | 近义词: control freak | 反义词: delegator | 词性: 名词
