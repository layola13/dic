averroism:/əˈvɛroɪzəm/
n.
阿威罗伊主义；阿维罗伊主义
The doctrine of Averroes, a 12th-century Islamic philosopher, which holds that there is only one intellect shared by all humans, and that philosophical truth can be achieved through reason independently of revelation.
Averroism was condemned by the Catholic Church.  阿威罗伊主义被天主教会谴责。
Averroism influenced many medieval thinkers. 阿威罗伊主义影响了许多中世纪思想家。
近义词: Aristotelianism, rationalism
反义词: mysticism, fideism
词性: noun
