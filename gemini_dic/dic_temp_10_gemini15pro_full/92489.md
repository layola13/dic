hypothecation:/haɪˌpɑːθɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 抵押，担保 | The act of pledging property as security for a loan without giving up possession of it. |  The bank required hypothecation of the factory as security for the loan.  银行要求将工厂抵押作为贷款担保。| pledge, mortgage, security | release, discharge | noun

hypothecate:/haɪˈpɑːθɪkeɪt/ | vt. | 抵押，担保 | To pledge something as security without giving up possession of it. | The farmer hypothecated his land to get a loan for new equipment. 农民抵押了他的土地以获得购买新设备的贷款。| pledge, mortgage, secure | release, redeem | verb
