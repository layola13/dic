orthodoxal:/ˌɔːrθəˈdɒksəl/
adjective
正统的，传统的
Conforming to established doctrine, especially in religion; conventional.
His orthodoxal views on marriage caused conflict with his more liberal children. 他在婚姻问题上的正统观点与他思想更开放的孩子们产生了冲突。
近义词: conventional, traditional, doctrinaire
反义词: unorthodox, heterodox, unconventional
词性: adjective
