guatemalans:/ˌɡwɑːtəˈmɑːlənz/
noun
危地马拉人
People from Guatemala, a country in Central America.
Many Guatemalans speak Spanish as their first language. 许多危地马拉人的母语是西班牙语。
Guatemalans are known for their vibrant textiles. 危地马拉人以其充满活力的纺织品而闻名。
Synonym: Guatemalan people
Antonym: N/A (no logical opposite)
词性: noun
