nonimplements:/ˌnɒnˈɪmplɪmɛnts/ | v. | 未实施，未执行 |  Fails to put into effect; does not carry out. | The company nonimplements the new safety regulations, leading to several accidents.  该公司未执行新的安全规定，导致了几起事故。 | 近义词: disregards, neglects, omits, ignores | 反义词: implements, executes, enforces, applies | 词性: 动词

nonimplements:/ˌnɒnˈɪmplɪmɛnts/ | n. | 未实施的项目/计划 | Things or plans that have not been implemented. | The report listed several nonimplements that needed to be addressed immediately. 报告列出了几项需要立即处理的未实施项目。| 近义词: omissions, neglected items | 反义词: implementations, completed tasks | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "implements" (verb meaning "puts into effect").  Derived from the Old French "implementer" and ultimately from the Latin "implere" meaning "to fill up" or "complete."
