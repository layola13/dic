inclusionary:/ɪnˈkluːʒənəri/ | adj. | 包容性的，接纳的 | Designed to include people of all kinds, especially those who have been excluded in the past. |  This is an inclusionary school that welcomes students of all abilities. 这是一所包容性的学校，欢迎各种能力的学生。

The city adopted an inclusionary zoning policy to create more affordable housing. 该市采取了包容性分区政策，以创造更多经济适用房。 | 近义词: inclusive, integrating, all-embracing | 反义词: exclusionary, discriminatory, segregating | 词性: 形容词
