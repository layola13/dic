firstperson:/ˈfɜːrst ˈpɜːsən/ (US) /ˈfɜːst ˈpɜːsən/ (UK)| n./adj.|第一人称|First person refers to the grammatical person used by a speaker to refer to themselves or a group including themselves.  As a noun, it refers to the perspective from which a story is told. As an adjective, it describes something from that perspective.|She wrote the novel in the first person. 她以第一人称写了这部小说。|近义词：I, me, we, us, myself, ourselves|反义词：second person, third person|词性: noun, adjective
