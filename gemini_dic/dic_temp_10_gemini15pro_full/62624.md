enforced:/ɪnˈfɔːrst/| v. adj.|执行，强制执行；强加的，强制的| compelled by force or authority.  |例句：The new law was strictly enforced. 新法律得到了严格执行。|近义词：implemented, imposed, applied, mandated|反义词:ignored, neglected, disregarded|词性:动词(past participle of enforce), 形容词

enforced:/ɪnˈfɔːrst/| adj.|强加的，强制的|made to happen, especially by law or by someone in authority|例句：Enforced idleness can be demoralizing. 强制无所事事可能会令人沮丧。|近义词：compulsory, mandatory, obligatory|反义词:optional, voluntary|词性:形容词

词源: From Old French *enforcier, from en- (“in, into”) + force (“force”).
