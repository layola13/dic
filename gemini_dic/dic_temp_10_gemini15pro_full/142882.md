out-of-state:/ˌaʊt əv ˈsteɪt/
adjective
州外的；外州的
Coming from or located in a different state.
例句：
She has to pay out-of-state tuition. 她必须支付州外学费。

He bought an out-of-state car. 他买了一辆外州的车。


近义词：nonresident, foreign
反义词：in-state, local
词性：adjective
