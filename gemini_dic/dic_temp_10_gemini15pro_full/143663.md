outlabour:/ˌaʊtˈleɪbər/
verb
胜过工作；比...更勤劳
to work harder or longer than someone else
The team outlaboured their opponents and secured a resounding victory.  这个团队比他们的对手更加努力，取得了决定性的胜利。
He outlaboured everyone else on the project. 他在这个项目上比其他任何人都努力。
近义词: outwork, surpass, outperform
反义词: underperform
