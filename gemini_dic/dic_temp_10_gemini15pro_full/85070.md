hard-hitting:/ˌhɑːrdˈhɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 强有力的，犀利的，毫不留情的 | delivering forceful blows; (of a speech, book, film, etc.) having a strong impact; uncompromisingly direct and critical |  The journalist wrote a hard-hitting article about corruption in the government. 这位记者写了一篇关于政府腐败的强有力的文章。|  The documentary was a hard-hitting exposé of the fashion industry. 这部纪录片是对时尚界的犀利揭露。 | 近义词: forceful, powerful, incisive, trenchant, impactful | 反义词: gentle, mild, soft, weak | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Compound of "hard" and "hitting," from the literal sense of delivering a strong physical blow, extended to figurative uses describing impactful speech, writing, or actions.
