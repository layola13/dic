ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + carbonate (from carbon, ultimately from Latin "carbo" meaning coal).  The "bi-" prefix refers to the single hydrogen ion in the compound (HCO3-), as opposed to two in carbonic acid (H2CO3).
