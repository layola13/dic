moron:/ˈmɔːrɑːn/ (US), /ˈmɔːrɒn/ (UK)| n. |笨蛋，傻瓜|A person who is considered foolish or stupid. |例句：He called me a moron, which I found offensive. 他骂我是笨蛋，我觉得很冒犯。

例句：Only a moron would do something like that. 只有傻瓜才会做那样的事。|近义词：idiot, imbecile, fool, dolt |反义词：genius, intellectual|词性:名词
