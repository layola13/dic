militant:/ˈmɪlɪtənt/ | adj. n. |好战的，激进的；斗士，激进分子|Engaged in warfare or combat;  combative and aggressive in support of a cause.  Also, a person who is militant.|例句：The militant group claimed responsibility for the bombing.  这个好战组织声称对爆炸事件负责。|近义词：aggressive, assertive, combative, activist|反义词：peaceful, passive, dovish|词性：形容词/名词

militant:/ˈmɪlɪtənt/ | n. |斗士，激进分子|A person engaged in warfare or combat; a combative and aggressive supporter of a cause.|例句：The militants were captured after a fierce firefight.  激进分子在激烈的交火后被俘。|近义词：activist, fighter, combatant|反义词：pacifist, moderate|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *militantem, present participle of militāre (“to serve as a soldier”), from Latin miles (“soldier”).
