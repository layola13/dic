kitbash:/ˈkɪtbæʃ/| n. v.|套件拼装，模型改造|The act of taking parts from different (usually commercial) kits and combining them to create a new model, or the resulting model itself.  It involves modifying, combining, and repurposing existing model kits or parts to create something new and original.|He kitbashed a spaceship model using parts from a tank and a plane kit. 他用坦克和飞机模型的零件拼装了一个宇宙飞船模型。|近义词：customize, modify, adapt|反义词:build from scratch|词性:noun, verb
