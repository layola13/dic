hygrodeik:/ˈhaɪɡroʊdaɪk/ | n. |湿度计，干湿表|An instrument that indicates the relative humidity of the air, typically by means of the difference in readings between two thermometers, one having a wet bulb and the other a dry bulb.|The hygrodeik showed a humidity of 60%. 干湿表显示湿度为60%。|Synonyms: hygrometer, psychrometer |Antonyms: None | Noun
