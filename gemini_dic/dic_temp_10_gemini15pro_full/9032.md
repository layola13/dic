antimasonry:/ˌæntiˈmeɪsənri/
n.
反对共济会
Opposition to Freemasonry, often based on suspicion of its secrecy and influence.
Antimasonry flourished in the United States during the 1820s and 1830s.  美国的反共济会运动在19世纪20年代和30年代盛行。
The antimasonry movement led to the formation of a political party. 反共济会运动导致了一个政党的形成。
近义词:anti-Freemasonry
反义词:Freemasonry
词性:noun
