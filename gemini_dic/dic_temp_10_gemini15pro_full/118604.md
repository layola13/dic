/ˈmɛsɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈmɛsɪdʒ/ (UK)| n. vt.|信息，消息；启示；(神的)旨意；(文艺作品的)寓意,主题；传达，告知|A piece of information or communication sent from one person to another.|例句：I left a message for him. 我给他留了个口信。|近义词：information, communication, news, word, notice|反义词: silence, concealment|词性:名词, 动词


/ˈmɛsɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈmɛsɪdʒ/ (UK)| vt.|传达，告知|To communicate a thought or idea.|例句：He messaged me the details. 他把细节传达给我了。|近义词：communicate, convey, inform, tell, notify|反义词: withhold|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French message, from Late Latin missaticum "something sent", from Latin missus, past participle of mittere "to send".
