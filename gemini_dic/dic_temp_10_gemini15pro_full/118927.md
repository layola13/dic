metempsychosis:/ˌmɛtəmˈsaɪkoʊsɪs/ /ˌmɛtɛmpˈsaɪkəʊsɪs/
n.
轮回；灵魂转世
The philosophical concept of metempsychosis suggests that the soul is reborn into a new body after death.  轮回的哲学概念表明，灵魂在死后重生到一个新的身体里。
The ancient Egyptians believed in metempsychosis and prepared elaborate tombs for their afterlife. 古埃及人相信轮回转世，并为他们的来世准备了精心制作的坟墓。
reincarnation, transmigration
