aspon: /ˈæspɒn/ (UK) /ˈæspən/ (US)
noun
白杨，山杨
a tall, fast-growing tree with smooth pale bark and leaves that tremble in the slightest breeze.
例句：The aspens quaked in the wind.  风中白杨树颤抖。

例句：A stand of aspens shimmered in the sunlight. 一片白杨树在阳光下闪闪发光。

近义词：poplar, aspen tree
反义词: N/A
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *æps, from Proto-Germanic *aspaz, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ep- “water, river” (due to aspens' preference for damp habitats).
