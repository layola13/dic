electroneutral:/ɪˌlektrəˈnjuːtrəl/
adjective
电中性的
Having no net electric charge; electrically neutral.
The solution is electroneutral because the positive and negative charges balance out.  这种溶液是电中性的，因为正负电荷相互抵消。
The compound formed is electroneutral.  形成的化合物是电中性的。
Synonym: neutral
Antonym: charged
adjective
