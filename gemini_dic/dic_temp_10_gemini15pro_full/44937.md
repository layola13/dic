cryptographic:/ˌkrɪptəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 密码的；密码学的 | Relating to or involving cryptography; the art of writing or solving codes. |  例句：Cryptographic techniques are used to protect sensitive information. 密码技术用于保护敏感信息。| 近义词：encrypted, coded, ciphered | 反义词：unencrypted, plain, deciphered | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From crypto- + -graphic.  Crypto- comes from the Greek word "kryptos," meaning "hidden" or "secret."  -Graphic comes from the Greek word "graphikos," meaning "of or relating to writing."
