anarchal: /ˈæn.ɑːr.kəl/ (US) /ˈæn.ɑː.kəl/ (UK)| adj.|无政府的，无政府主义的|Relating to or supporting anarchy; lacking order or control.|The country descended into an anarchal state after the coup. 政变后，该国陷入了无政府状态。|  The classroom was anarchal with the teacher absent. 老师不在，教室里一片混乱。|Synonyms: lawless, ungoverned, chaotic, disorderly|Antonyms: orderly, organized, lawful, governed|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *anarchique*, from Greek *anarkhos* "without a ruler," from *an-* "without" + *arkhos* "ruler."
