词源：From Old French convoier "to escort, accompany," from Vulgar Latin *conviare "to go along with," from Latin com- "together" (see com-) + via "way, road" (see via).


注意事项:  Convoy can be both a noun and a verb. As a verb, it primarily describes the act of escorting or protecting something, especially ships or vehicles, while traveling through a potentially dangerous area. As a noun, it refers to the group being escorted or the escort itself.
