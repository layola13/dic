hardships:/ˈhɑːrdʃɪps/
n.
艰难困苦；苦难
Severe suffering or privation.
They endured many hardships during the war.  他们在战争期间忍受了许多苦难。

近义词: difficulties, troubles, trials, adversity, privations
反义词: comforts, luxuries, ease, prosperity


词性: noun (plural)
词源: From "hardship," from hard + -ship (suffix denoting condition or state).  来自“hardship”，由 hard + -ship（表示条件或状态的后缀）组成。
