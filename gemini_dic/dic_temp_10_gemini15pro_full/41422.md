cornerer:/ˈkɔːrnərər/ | n. | 垄断者; 囤积者; 逼至绝境的人 | someone who has gained control of the trade in a particular commodity or class of goods, often by buying up all or most of the available supply | The government accused the company of being a cornerer of the wheat market. 政府指责该公司垄断了小麦市场。 | synonyms: monopolist, controller, dominator | antonyms: competitor, rival | noun

cornerer:/ˈkɔːrnərər/ | n. | 把…逼入困境的人 | someone who forces a person or animal into a place from which they cannot easily escape | The police cornered the suspect in an alley. 警察在一个小巷里把嫌疑人逼入绝境。 | synonyms: trapper, ensnarer | antonyms: liberator, rescuer | noun
