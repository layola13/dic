amoeba:/əˈmiːbə/ (US) /əˈmiːbə/ (UK)| n. | 变形虫，阿米巴 |A single-celled animal that catches food and moves about by extending fingerlike projections of protoplasm. Amoebas are either free-living in damp environments or parasitic.|Amoebas are simple organisms found in pond water. 变形虫是可以在池塘水中找到的简单生物。|
|The amoeba changed its shape as it moved. 变形虫在移动时改变了它的形状。|
|Synonyms: protozoan, microorganism|
|Antonyms: multicellular organism|
|noun|
