nonalienation: /ˌnɒnˌeɪliəˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 非异化，不疏离 | The state of not being alienated, especially from one's work, society, or oneself.  It implies a sense of connection, belonging, and engagement. |  Marxist theory emphasizes the importance of nonalienation in a truly communist society.  马克思主义理论强调在一个真正的共产主义社会中非异化的重要性。

|  The company's efforts to promote employee ownership are aimed at fostering nonalienation. 公司鼓励员工持股是为了促进员工的归属感，避免异化。 | 近义词: integration, connection, belonging, engagement | 反义词: alienation, estrangement, isolation, detachment | 词性: noun
