hypophosphoric:/ˌhaɪpəʊfɒsˈfɒrɪk/| adj.|次磷的，连二磷酸的|Relating to or containing phosphorus with a valence of four.|Hypophosphoric acid is used in some industrial processes.|近义词:tetraphosphoric (somewhat related in terms of phosphorus oxidation state) |反义词:phosphoric (higher oxidation state), perphosphoric (highest oxidation state)|词性:adjective


Etymology:
hypo- (Greek: under, less than) + phosphoric (relating to phosphoric acid, which contains phosphorus in its highest oxidation state)
