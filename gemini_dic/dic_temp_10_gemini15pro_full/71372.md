flagelliform:/fləˈdʒɛlɪfɔːrm/
adjective
鞭状的，鞭形的
Resembling a whip; long, slender, and tapering.
The plant has flagelliform leaves. 这种植物有鞭状的叶子。
The bacteria possess a flagelliform appendage.  这种细菌有一个鞭状的附属物。
近义词:whiplike, whip-shaped, lash-like
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider describing the opposite shape, e.g., broad, ovate, circular)
词性:adjective


flagellum:/fləˈdʒɛləm/
noun
鞭毛，鞭状体
A slender threadlike appendage, especially a whiplike extension of certain cells or organisms that functions as an organ of locomotion.
The bacterium propels itself using a flagellum.  细菌用鞭毛推动自己前进。
The paramecium uses its cilia and flagella for movement. 草履虫利用纤毛和鞭毛来移动。
近义词:cilium (in some contexts), appendage
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:noun
