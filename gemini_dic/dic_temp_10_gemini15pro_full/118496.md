mesmerize:/ˈmɛzməraɪz/ (US) /ˈmɛzməraɪz/ (UK)| vt.| 迷住，催眠，使着迷|To completely capture someone's attention and interest, often to the point of fascination or even a trance-like state.|The magician mesmerized the audience with his incredible illusions.  魔术师以其令人难以置信的幻象迷住了观众。

The beautiful sunset mesmerized us.  美丽的日落使我们着迷。|synonyms:captivate, enthrall, fascinate, hypnotize|antonyms:bore, repel, disenchant|verb
