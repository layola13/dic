field-test:/ˈfiːld tɛst/ | n. & v. | 现场测试，实地试验 |  A test conducted in the real-world environment for which a product or system is designed, rather than in a laboratory. | The company field-tested the new software before its official release.  公司在正式发布新软件之前对其进行了现场测试。| synonyms: trial run, beta test, pilot test | antonyms: laboratory test, simulation | noun

field-test:/ˈfiːld tɛst/ | v. | 现场测试，实地试验 | To test (something) in the real-world environment for which it is designed. |  They field-tested the new car in extreme weather conditions. 他们在极端天气条件下对新车进行了实地测试。 | synonyms: trial, pilot, beta test | antonyms: simulate | verb
