nephridial:/nɪˈfrɪdiəl/| adj.|肾管的，排泄器官的|Relating to or resembling a nephridium, a tubular excretory organ found in many invertebrates.|The earthworm has a nephridial system for waste removal. 蚯蚓有一个肾管系统来清除废物。|近义词:excretory|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider words related to retention like "retentive")|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *nephros* (kidney) + *-idial* (relating to).
