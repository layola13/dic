boskiness:/ˈbɒskɪnɪs/ | n. | 树木茂盛，多树 | The quality of being bosky; having abundant trees and shrubs.|  The boskiness of the area provided excellent cover for the deer.  这片区域的茂密树林为鹿提供了绝佳的掩护。 | synonyms: woodiness,  leafiness,  foliage, lushness | antonyms: barrenness, sparseness,  openness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bosky + -ness.  Bosky comes from Middle English *bosky*, from Middle French *bosquet*, diminutive of *bosc* "wood," from Late Latin *boscus* "wood, bush."
