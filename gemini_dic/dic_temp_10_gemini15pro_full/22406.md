boohoo:/ˈbuːhuː/
boohoo:v. int.|哭诉，假哭|To cry noisily, often in a way that seems exaggerated or insincere.|She boohooed about her lost phone, even though she knew her parents would buy her a new one. 她哭诉她丢了手机，即使她知道她父母会给她买一个新的。|近义词：cry, weep, sob, whine|反义词:laugh, rejoice
boohoo:n.|哭诉，假哭|A noisy display of crying, often exaggerated or insincere.|Her boohoo about the broken nail was completely over the top. 她因为指甲断了而哭闹，真是太夸张了。|近义词：crying, weeping, sobbing, whining|反义词:laughter, rejoicing
