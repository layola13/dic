curtailedly:/kɜːrˈteɪldli/
adv.
简略地，缩略地
in a curtailed manner; briefly.
The meeting was curtailedly summarized due to time constraints.  由于时间限制，会议被简略地总结了。
His explanation was curtailedly delivered, leaving many questions unanswered. 他的解释非常简略，留下许多问题没有解答。
近义词:briefly, concisely, succinctly, shortly
反义词:lengthily, extensively, comprehensively
词性:副词

词源:
From curtail + -ed + -ly.  Curtail comes from Old French *curtailler, frequentative of curtal "to dock, cut short," from curt.
