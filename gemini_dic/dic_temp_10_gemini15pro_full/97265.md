inflorescence:/ˌɪnfləˈrɛsəns/| n. |花序|The complete flower head of a plant including stems, stalks, bracts, and flowers.  The arrangement of flowers on a plant.| The lilac has a beautiful inflorescence. 紫丁香的花序很漂亮。|Synonyms: flower cluster, bloom, blossom|Antonyms: N/A|noun

inflorescence:/ˌɪnfləˈrɛsəns/| n. |花序|The arrangement and mode of development of flowers on an axis or stem.| Botanists study the different types of inflorescences to classify plants.  植物学家研究不同类型的花序来对植物进行分类。|Synonyms: flower head, floral arrangement|Antonyms: N/A|noun
