marsupialia:/ˌmɑːrsuːpiˈeɪliə/ | n. |有袋类动物|A subclass of mammals characterized by the presence of a pouch (the marsupium) in which the young are carried and nourished. |例句：Kangaroos and koalas are members of the marsupialia. 袋鼠和考拉是有袋类动物的成员。

例句：Marsupialia are found mainly in Australia and the Americas. 有袋类动物主要分布在澳大利亚和美洲。|近义词：metatheria|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑eutheria真兽类作为对比)|词性:noun
