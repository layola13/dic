chrysalises: /ˈkrɪsəlɪsiːz/
noun
蛹（复数）
The hard-shelled form that a caterpillar takes before it becomes a butterfly or moth.  This is the plural of chrysalis.
The children were fascinated watching the chrysalises hanging from the branch. 孩子们着迷地看着树枝上悬挂着的蛹。
Near synonyms: pupae, cocoons
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a stage in life cycle, but could consider) larva, caterpillar, adult (butterfly/moth)
词性: noun
