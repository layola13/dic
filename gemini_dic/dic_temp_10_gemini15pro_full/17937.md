beetling:/ˈbiːtlɪŋ/| adj.| 突出而醒目的；像甲虫一样突出的；阴沉的，怒容满面的 |Projecting or overhanging; like a beetle's brow; frowning or scowling. | The beetling cliffs towered above us.  突出醒目的悬崖高耸在我们上方。| synonyms: overhanging, projecting, jutting, prominent, frowning, scowling | antonyms: receding, sunken, cheerful, smiling | adjective
beetling:/ˈbiːtlɪŋ/| n.| 突出部分；悬垂物 |A projecting or overhanging part. | The beetling brow of the cliff cast a long shadow. 悬崖突出的边缘投下长长的阴影。| synonyms: overhang, projection, ledge | antonyms: recess, indentation | noun
