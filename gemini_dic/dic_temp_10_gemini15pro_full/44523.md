crossposted:/ˌkrɒsˈpəʊstɪd/ (British) /ˌkrɔːsˈpoʊstɪd/ (American)| adj.|交叉发布的|Posted in more than one online forum or newsgroup. This action is usually done to reach a wider audience or to solicit different perspectives on a topic.|I crossposted the job ad to several online platforms to attract more applicants. 我把招聘广告交叉发布到几个网络平台，以吸引更多求职者。

The article was crossposted on Reddit and Facebook. 这篇文章在Reddit和脸书上交叉发布了。|近义词：syndicated, reposted, shared|反义词: original post (implied, not a direct antonym)|词性: adjective
