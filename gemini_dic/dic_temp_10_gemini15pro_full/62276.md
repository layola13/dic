encyclopedist:/ɪnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdɪst/| n.|百科全书编纂者，百科全书家|A person who compiles or edits an encyclopedia, or who is a specialist in many fields of knowledge.|Denis Diderot was a prominent encyclopedist of the 18th century. 丹尼斯·狄德罗是18世纪著名的百科全书编纂者。|synonyms: lexicographer, compiler, editor|antonyms: specialist (in a narrow field)|noun
