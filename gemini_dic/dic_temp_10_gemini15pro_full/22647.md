boran:/ˈbɔːræn/ (US), /ˈbɒrən/ (UK)| n.|博兰牛; 博兰人|A breed of cattle originating in East Africa, known for their hardiness and ability to survive in harsh environments. Also, a member of the Borana Oromo people of southern Ethiopia and northern Kenya. |例句：The Boran cattle are well-adapted to arid and semi-arid regions. 博兰牛非常适应干旱和半干旱地区。

例句：The Boran people are traditionally pastoralists. 博兰人传统上是牧民。|近义词：zebu (for cattle)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
