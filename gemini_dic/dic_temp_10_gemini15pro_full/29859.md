causeful: /ˈkɔːzfʊl/
adjective
有原因的；有动机的；有意义的
Having a cause or reason; motivated by a purpose.
This behavior is causeful, stemming from a deep-seated fear of abandonment. 这种行为是有原因的，源于内心深处对被抛弃的恐惧。
The artist's brushstrokes were causeful, each one contributing to the overall effect.  艺术家的每一笔都富有意义，每一笔都对整体效果做出了贡献。

近义词:motivated, purposeful, reasoned, deliberate
反义词:random, aimless, pointless, causeless

词性:adjective
词源: "Cause" (from Old French "cause" and Latin "causa") + "-ful" (suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by").
