deflate:/dɪˈfleɪt/ (US), /diːˈfleɪt/ (UK)| v. | 放气；泄气；使缩小；使灰心；紧缩（通货）| to release air or gas from something; to make something smaller or less important; to cause someone to lose confidence or enthusiasm; to reduce the amount of money in an economy | The tires deflated slowly. 轮胎慢慢漏气了。| synonyms: flatten, collapse, shrink, depress | antonyms: inflate, expand, enlarge, encourage | verb

deflate:/dɪˈfleɪt/ (US), /diːˈfleɪt/ (UK)| adj. | 瘪的；泄气的| emptied of air or gas | a deflated balloon 一个瘪了的气球 | synonyms: flat, collapsed | antonyms: inflated, expanded | adjective
