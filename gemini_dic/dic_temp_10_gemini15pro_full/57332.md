downward:/ˈdaʊnwərd/ (US), /ˈdaʊnwəd/ (UK)| adj. adv.|向下，下降|Moving towards a lower place, level, or position.  Descending.|例句：The bird spiraled downward.  鸟儿盘旋而下。|近义词：descending, downhill, downwards|反义词:upward, ascending|词性:形容词, 副词

downwards:/ˈdaʊnwədz/ (UK)| adv.|向下，下降|Towards a lower place, level, or position.|例句：He looked downwards at his shoes. 他低头看着自己的鞋子。|近义词：downward, descending, downhill|反义词:upwards, ascending|词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dūnewearde (unattested), from dūne “down” + -weard “-ward”. Cognate with Dutch neerwaarts, German niederwärts.
