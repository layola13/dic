computerised:/kəmˈpjuːtəraɪzd/ (UK) /kəmˈpjuːtəraɪzd/ (US)| adj.|计算机化的，电脑化的|Controlled or operated by computer.  Formed by adding "-ised" (British English) or "-ized" (American English) to the word "computer".  This suffix indicates that something has been made or become something else, in this case, controlled by a computer.|The library has a computerized catalog. 图书馆有一个计算机化的目录。|The company uses a computerized system for payroll. 这家公司使用计算机化的系统来发工资。|近义词：automated, digital, electronic|反义词：manual, analog, mechanical|词性:形容词
