diaphonic:/ˌdaɪəˈfɒnɪk/ /ˌdaɪəˈfɑːnɪk/ | adj. |  全音程的，跨八度的 | Relating to or encompassing the entire range of an instrument or voice, especially an octave. | The diaphonic singing created a rich and resonant sound. 全音程的歌声营造出丰富而洪亮的音响。|近义词: octave-spanning, full-toned |反义词: monophonic |词性:形容词

diaphonic:/ˌdaɪəˈfɒnɪk/ /ˌdaɪəˈfɑːnɪk/ | n. | 全音程 | An interval of an octave.  | The piece featured a powerful diaphonic between the bass and soprano. 这首作品以低音和高音之间强有力的全音程为特色。|近义词: octave |反义词: unison |词性:名词
