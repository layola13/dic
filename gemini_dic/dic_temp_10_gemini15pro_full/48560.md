defamingly:/dɪˈfeɪmɪŋli/
adv.
诽谤地，中伤地
in a manner that damages someone's reputation by saying or writing false and unpleasant things about them.
He was defamingly accused of being a thief. 他被诽谤为小偷。
maliciously, slanderously, libelously
complimentarily, approvingly
词性: 副词
