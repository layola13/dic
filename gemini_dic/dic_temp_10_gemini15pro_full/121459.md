misjudge:/ˌmɪsˈdʒʌdʒ/ | v. |错误判断，误判|To form an incorrect opinion or conclusion about someone or something.| I misjudged the distance and drove off the road. 我错误估计了距离，把车开下了路。|He completely misjudged her character. 他完全误判了她的性格。|近义词：miscalculate, underestimate, overestimate, misread |反义词：judge correctly, assess accurately|词性:动词
