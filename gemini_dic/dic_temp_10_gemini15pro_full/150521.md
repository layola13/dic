pantophagy:/pænˈtɒfədʒi/ | n. | 杂食性 | The condition of being a pantophage; omnivorousness.  The practice of eating all kinds of food. | Example 1: Bears exhibit pantophagy, consuming berries, fish, and insects. 熊表现出杂食性，吃浆果、鱼和昆虫。

Example 2:  The pig's pantophagy makes it a valuable farm animal. 猪的杂食性使它成为一种有价值的农场动物。 | 近义词: omnivory, omnivorousness | 反义词: monophagous, stenophagy | 词性: noun

pantophagic:/ˌpæn.təˈfædʒ.ɪk/ | adj. | 杂食性的 | Eating all kinds of food; omnivorous. | Example 1:  Pigs are pantophagic animals, known for their diverse diet. 猪是杂食性动物，以其多样化的饮食而闻名。

Example 2:  The pantophagic nature of raccoons allows them to thrive in various environments. 浣熊的杂食性使它们能够在各种环境中茁壮成长。 | 近义词: omnivorous | 反义词: monophagous, stenophagous | 词性: adjective

pantophagous:/pænˈtɒfəɡəs/ | adj. | 杂食性的 |  Synonym of pantophagic. | Example 1: Humans are pantophagous and can adapt to a wide range of food sources. 人类是杂食性的，可以适应各种食物来源。

Example 2:  The pantophagous diet of the chimpanzee includes fruits, leaves, and insects. 黑猩猩的杂食性饮食包括水果、树叶和昆虫。 | 近义词: omnivorous, pantophagic | 反义词: monophagous, stenophagous | 词性: adjective
