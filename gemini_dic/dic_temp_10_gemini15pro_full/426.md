ablastemic: /ˌeɪbləˈstɛmɪk/
adjective
无胚层的; 非胚层的
Not germinating; without a germ or bud.  Relating to or designating tissues that are not part of the germ line and thus cannot give rise to gametes.
The ablastemic nature of the tumor indicated it was unlikely to spread.  该肿瘤的非胚层特性表明它不太可能扩散。
Ablastemic carcinomas are typically less aggressive. 非胚层癌通常不那么具有侵袭性。
近义词: agerminal, non-germinal
反义词: blastemic, germinal
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (not) + blastema (a mass of undifferentiated cells capable of growth and differentiation, from Greek blastēma, "sprout") + -ic (adjective suffix).
