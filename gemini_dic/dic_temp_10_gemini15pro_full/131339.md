noncharitableness:/ˌnɒnˈtʃærɪtəbəlnəs/
n.
缺乏仁慈；不慈善；刻薄
lack of charity; unkindness; meanness
Her noncharitableness made her unpopular. 她的刻薄让她不受欢迎。
近义词：unkindness, meanness, harshness, ruthlessness, cruelty
反义词：charitableness, kindness, generosity, compassion, empathy
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From non- + charitableness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "charitableness" comes from "charitable," which ultimately derives from the Late Latin "charitativus," meaning "loving, charitable."  This in turn comes from the Latin "charitas," meaning "dearness, high price, esteem, love, charity,"  from "carus," meaning "dear, costly."
