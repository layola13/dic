feeding-frenzies:/ˈfiːdɪŋ ˈfrɛnziːz/
|n.|疯狂进食; 抢食狂潮|A period of intense and often uncontrolled activity, typically involving a large number of people or animals competing for something.  A sudden surge of activity, often marked by competitiveness and a lack of restraint, especially in acquiring something.|The sharks went into a feeding frenzy when they smelled the blood in the water.  鲨鱼闻到水里的血腥味后，便开始疯狂进食。

The media feeding frenzy surrounding the celebrity scandal was relentless. 围绕着名人丑闻的媒体抢食狂潮是无情的。|近义词:  free-for-all, scramble, rush, melee|反义词:  calm, order, restraint|词性: 名词 (plural)
