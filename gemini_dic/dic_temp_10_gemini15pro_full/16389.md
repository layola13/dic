barbiturate: /bɑːrˈbɪtʃərət/ | n. | 巴比妥酸盐 |  A drug derived from barbituric acid that acts as a central nervous system depressant and is used as a sedative or hypnotic. |  The doctor prescribed a barbiturate to help her sleep. 医生给她开了巴比妥酸盐帮助她睡眠。| Synonyms: sedative, hypnotic, tranquilizer | Antonyms: stimulant | noun


barbiturate: /bɑːrˈbɪtʃərət/ | n. | 巴比妥类药物 | Any of a class of sedative and sleep-inducing drugs derived from barbituric acid. | Barbiturates were once commonly prescribed for anxiety and insomnia. 巴比妥类药物曾经普遍用于治疗焦虑和失眠。 | Synonyms: downer, sleeping pill, soporific | Antonyms: stimulant | noun
