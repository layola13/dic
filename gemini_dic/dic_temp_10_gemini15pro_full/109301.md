lend:/lend/ | v. | 借出，贷给 | To give something to someone for a short time, expecting it to be returned. | I lent my car to my brother. 我把我的车借给了我弟弟。 | He lent her some money. 他借给她一些钱。|近义词：loan, advance |反义词: borrow |词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *lænan* "to lend, give," from Proto-Germanic *lainjanan* (source also of Old Norse *læna*, Old Frisian *lena*, Dutch *lenen*, German *leihen* "to lend"), from PIE root *leikw-* "to leave, remain."
