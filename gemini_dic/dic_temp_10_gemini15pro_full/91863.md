hyperinflated:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˈfleɪtɪd/
adjective
过度膨胀的
(of a price or amount) excessively inflated or raised.
例句：The hyperinflated prices of basic goods made life difficult for ordinary citizens.  基本商品价格过度膨胀，使普通市民的生活变得困难。

例句：The hyperinflated currency quickly became worthless.  过度膨胀的货币很快就变得一文不值。

近义词：inflated, overvalued, exorbitant, astronomical
反义词：deflated, undervalued, reasonable, moderate
词性：adjective

Etymology:
From hyper- + inflated, past participle of inflate.  Hyper- comes from the Greek prefix "hyper-" meaning "over, above, beyond." Inflate comes from the Latin "inflare" meaning "to blow into, inflate."
