flapperish:/ˈflæpərɪʃ/| adj.|轻佻的，像轻佻女郎的|Resembling or characteristic of a flapper, a young woman in the 1920s who flouted conventional standards of behavior.|She had a flapperish haircut and wore a short skirt. 她留着轻佻女郎式的短发，穿着短裙。|Synonyms: frivolous, flighty, giddy, carefree|Antonyms: serious, demure, reserved|adjective
