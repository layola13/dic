nonagenarians:/ˌnɒnədʒɪˈnɛːriənz/ | n. | 九十多岁的人；九旬老人 |  A person who is between 90 and 99 years old. |  Many nonagenarians are still active and engaged in their communities. 许多九十多岁的人仍然活跃在社区中。|  The retirement home had a special celebration for its nonagenarians. 这家养老院为其九十多岁的老人举办了一场特别的庆祝活动。 |Synonyms:  ninety-year-olds | Antonyms: youngsters, children | Noun

Etymology: From nonagenarian (adjective), from Latin *nonagenarius, from nonaginta 'ninety'.
