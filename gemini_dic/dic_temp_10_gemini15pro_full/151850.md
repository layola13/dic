passings:/ˈpɑːsɪŋz/
n.
逝去；去世；消逝
instances of dying or ceasing to exist.
The memorial service commemorated the passings of several prominent community members.  这个追悼会是为了纪念几位杰出社区成员的逝世。
The museum exhibit documented the passings of traditional crafts. 博物馆的展览记录了传统工艺的消逝。
近义词: deaths, demises, losses
反义词: births, beginnings
