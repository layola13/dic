intercombination:/ˌɪntərˌkɒmbɪˈneɪʃən/ | /ˌɪntərˌkɑːmbɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |相互结合; 互化; （量子力学）禁戒跃迁 |A transition between two electronic states of different multiplicity.  This is a less probable transition than a combination. |The phosphorescence arises from intercombination to the triplet state. 磷光是由向三重态的禁戒跃迁产生的。 | synonym: transition, interaction | antonym: separation, division | noun
