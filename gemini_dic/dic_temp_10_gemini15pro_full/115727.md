Also a female given name. | The Marian shrine attracted many pilgrims.  玛丽安圣地吸引了许多朝圣者。|  She was christened Marian. 她被命名为玛丽安。| synonyms: Virgin Mary, Madonna (as epithets); blessed, holy, sacred (when referring to objects dedicated to Mary) | antonyms: secular, profane (when referring to objects dedicated to Mary) | word type: adjective, noun
