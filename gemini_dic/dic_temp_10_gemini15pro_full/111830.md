loggers:/ˈlɒɡəz/ (British) /ˈlɑːɡərz/ (American)| n.|伐木工人；记录器|People who cut down trees for timber; a device or program that records events.|The loggers worked tirelessly in the forest. 伐木工人在森林里不知疲倦地工作。 The server uses a logger to track all incoming requests. 服务器使用记录器来跟踪所有传入的请求。|近义词:lumberjacks, woodcutters (for people); recorder, monitor (for device)|反义词:conservationist (for people)|词性:名词
