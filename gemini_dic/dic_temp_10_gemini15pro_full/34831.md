clinging:/ˈklɪŋɪŋ/ | adj. | 粘着的；紧贴的；执着的；依赖的 | tending to cling; adhesive; clinging clothes 紧贴的衣服; a clinging child 依赖的孩子  | The wet shirt was clinging to his back. 湿衬衫紧贴在他的背上。| synonyms: adhesive, tenacious, persistent, dependent | antonyms: detached, independent, non-adhesive | adjective

clinging:/ˈklɪŋɪŋ/ | n. |  依附; 坚持 | The act of holding on tightly. |  The child's clinging to his mother was understandable after the scare.  孩子受到惊吓后，紧紧依偎着母亲，这是可以理解的。 | synonyms: attachment, adherence, tenacity | antonyms: detachment, release | noun
