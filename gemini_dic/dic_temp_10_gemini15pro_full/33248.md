chronometry:/krəˈnɒmətri/ /krəˈnɑːmətri/| n.|计时法; 测时法|The science of accurate time measurement.|例句：The chronometry of the race was precise, recording each runner's time to the hundredth of a second. 比赛的计时非常精确，记录了每个跑者精确到百分之一秒的时间。

例句：Advances in chronometry have led to the development of incredibly accurate atomic clocks. 计时法的进步导致了极其精确的原子钟的发展。|近义词：horology, timekeeping|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *chronométrie*, from Ancient Greek χρόνος (khrónos, “time”) + -μετρία (-metría, “measurement”).
