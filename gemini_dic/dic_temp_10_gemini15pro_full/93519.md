imager:/ˈɪmɪdʒər/| n.|成像器，成像设备|A device or system that produces images.  It can refer to various technologies, from cameras and scanners to medical imaging equipment and satellite sensors.|This new imager captures high-resolution images of distant galaxies.|近义词:scanner, camera, imaging device|反义词:None|noun

imager:/ˈɪmɪdʒər/| n.|显像剂|A substance used in medical imaging to enhance the visibility of internal structures.|The doctor injected a contrast imager to get a clearer picture of the blood vessels.|近义词:contrast agent, contrast medium|反义词:None|noun
