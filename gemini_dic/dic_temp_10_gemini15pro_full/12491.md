aspergilliform:/æsˌpɜːrdʒɪˈlɪfɔːrm/
adjective
曲霉状的，似曲霉的
Resembling or having the shape of Aspergillus, a genus of common molds.
The fungus exhibited an aspergilliform appearance under the microscope.  这种真菌在显微镜下呈现出曲霉状的外观。
The colony had an aspergilliform structure. 菌落具有曲霉状结构。
近义词:fungiform, mold-like
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider words describing dissimilar shapes, e.g., spherical, linear)
