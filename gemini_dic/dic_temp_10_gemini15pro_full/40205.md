contestation:/ˌkɒntɛˈsteɪʃən/ /ˌkɑːntɛˈsteɪʃən/| n. |争论，争议，竞赛|The contestation of the election results led to widespread protests.  选举结果的争议导致了广泛的抗议。|例句：The contestation over the new policy was fierce.  关于新政策的争论非常激烈。

例句：The contestation for the championship was intense.  冠军争夺赛非常激烈。|近义词：dispute, debate, argument, competition, challenge|反义词：agreement, harmony, acceptance, concession|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin contestatio, from Latin contestari "to call to witness," from con- "together" + testari "to bear witness," from testis "witness."
