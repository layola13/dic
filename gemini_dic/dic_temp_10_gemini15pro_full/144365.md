outstep:/ˈaʊtˌstɛp/| n. |越轨行为；过分的举动|An act of going beyond or exceeding the limits of something, especially of propriety or authority.|Her outstep cost her the job. 她的越轨行为让她丢了工作。|近义词： transgression, overstep, lapse|反义词: observance, compliance|词性:名词
