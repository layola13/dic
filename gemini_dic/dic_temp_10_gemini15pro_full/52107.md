diamond-in-the-rough:/ˈdaɪmənd ɪn ðə rʌf/
noun
未经琢磨的钻石；璞玉；潜力股
A person with great potential or talent but lacking polish or refinement.  A diamond in its natural state, before cutting and polishing, may appear dull or uninteresting, but it holds the potential for great brilliance. Similarly, a person described as a "diamond in the rough" may be unrefined or lack social graces, but possesses hidden qualities that, with development, could lead to great success or achievement.
He was a diamond in the rough, needing only some training to become a star salesman. 他是个璞玉，只需一些培训就能成为明星推销员。
Synonyms:  gem in the rough, unpolished gem, potential star, undiscovered talent, rough diamond
Antonyms: polished gem, finished product, refined individual
