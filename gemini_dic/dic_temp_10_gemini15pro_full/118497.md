mesmerized:/ˈmezməraɪzd/ | adj. | 着迷的，被迷住的 |  completely fascinated or hypnotized, as if by magic |  I was mesmerized by the dancer's graceful movements. 我被舞者优雅的动作迷住了。 |  captivated, enthralled, fascinated, spellbound |  bored, disinterested | adjective


mesmerize:/ˈmezməraɪz/ | vt. |  使着迷，迷住 | to fascinate or hypnotize completely | The magician mesmerized the audience with his tricks. 魔术师用他的戏法迷住了观众。 |  captivate, enthrall, fascinate, hypnotize | bore, disenchant | verb


Etymology: From the name of Franz Mesmer (1734-1815), an Austrian physician who developed a theory of animal magnetism, which involved inducing trance-like states in patients.  源自弗朗茨·梅斯梅尔（Franz Mesmer，1734-1815）的名字，他是一位奥地利医生，发展了动物磁力学理论，其中包括诱导患者进入恍惚状态。
