colloid:/ˈkɒlɔɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlɔɪd/ (US)| n. |胶体|A substance microscopically dispersed evenly throughout another substance.  The dispersed-phase particles have a diameter between about 5 and 200 nanometers. The mixture is called a colloidal system because it is intermediate between a solution and a suspension.|Milk is a colloid of liquid butterfat globules dispersed within a water-based fluid.|近义词: dispersion, suspension|反义词: solution|词性:n.

colloidal:/kəˈlɔɪdəl/ (UK) /kəˈlɔɪdl/ (US)| adj. |胶体的|Of, relating to, or having the properties of a colloid.|The colloidal particles are so small they don't settle out.|近义词: disperse, suspended|反义词: dissolved|词性:adj.
