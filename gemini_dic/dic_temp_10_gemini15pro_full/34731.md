clickstream:/ˈklɪkstriːm/
noun
点击流
A record of the links or pages a user clicks on while browsing the web.
The website analyzes the clickstream data to understand user behavior.  网站分析点击流数据以了解用户行为。
web traffic, browsing history
offline behavior
