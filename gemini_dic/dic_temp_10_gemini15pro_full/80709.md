gorily:/ˈɡɔːrɪli/ | adv. | 血腥地，残忍地 | In a gory or gruesome manner; involving bloodshed and violence. | The movie depicted the battle gorily, showing every wound in detail.  这部电影血淋淋地描绘了这场战斗，详细地展现了每一处伤口。| 近义词：bloodily, gruesomely, brutally | 反义词：peacefully, gently | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From "gory" + "-ly". "Gory" comes from the Old English "gōrig," meaning "covered with gore," ultimately from "gor," meaning "filth, dung, blood."
