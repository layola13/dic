clonky:/ˈklʌŋki/| adj.|笨重的，发出叮当声的，老旧的|Awkward, heavy, and inefficient; making a loud, metallic, or resonant sound.|The car was old and clonky. 这辆车又老又笨重。|近义词：clumsy, awkward, clunky, rickety, ramshackle|反义词:smooth, sleek, elegant|词性:形容词

Etymology:  Likely imitative of a metallic banging sound.
