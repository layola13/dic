hejira:/hɪˈdʒɪərə/ /hɪˈdʒɪrə/
noun
逃亡，出走；（尤指为摆脱困境的）迁徙
An escape from a dangerous or undesirable situation; specifically, Muhammad's flight from Mecca to Medina in A.D. 622.
The family made a hasty hejira to the country to avoid the creditors.  这家人为了躲避债主，匆忙逃往乡下。
exodus, flight, escape
return

词性: n.
