blenniid:/ˈblɛniɪd/| n. adj.|鳚鱼；鳚科鱼类的|A fish of the Blenniidae family, characterized by elongated bodies and a continuous dorsal fin.  These fish are typically found in intertidal zones and shallow waters.|The blenniid darted into a crevice in the rock. 这条鳚鱼飞快地钻进了岩石的缝隙里。|近义词:blenny, combtooth blenny|反义词:N/A|词性:noun, adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Blennius (genus name) + -id (suffix forming adjectives and nouns related to families or groups).  Blennius itself comes from the Greek "blennos" meaning "slime," referring to the slimy coating on the fish's skin.
