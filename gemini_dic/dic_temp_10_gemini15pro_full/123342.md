monolingually:/ˌmɒnəˈlɪŋɡwəli/ | adv. | 单语地；只用一种语言地 |  Using or knowing only one language.  It describes someone who speaks or uses only one language, or something that is expressed or available in only one language. | She grew up monolingually, speaking only English. 她从小只说英语长大。| synonyms: unilingually | antonyms: bilingually, multilingually | adverb
