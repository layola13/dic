broomstick:/ˈbruːmstɪk/ | n. |扫帚柄；飞天魔帚|The handle of a broom, traditionally made of wood.  Also often used figuratively, especially in the context of witches. |She flew on her broomstick. 她骑着她的扫帚飞走了。|Near synonyms: broom handle, besom|Antonyms: None (a broomstick is a specific object, not a concept with an opposite)|Noun

broomsticks:/ˈbruːmstɪks/ | n. |扫帚柄（复数）|The plural form of "broomstick," referring to multiple handles of brooms.  |The children were playing a game with broomsticks. 孩子们正在用扫帚柄玩游戏。|Near synonyms: broom handles, besoms|Antonyms: None|Noun
