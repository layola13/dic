doleful:/ˈdoʊlfəl/ | adj. | 悲伤的，忧郁的 | Expressing sorrow; mournful. | The dog gave a doleful howl. 那只狗发出一声悲鸣。| He had a doleful expression on his face. 他脸上带着悲伤的表情。 | 近义词：sad, mournful, melancholic, gloomy | 反义词：happy, cheerful, joyful | 词性: 形容词


词源: From Middle English *doleful, from dole (“grief, sorrow”) + -ful.  Ultimately from Old French doel, from Latin dolus (“pain, grief”).
