have-a-screw-loose:/hæv ə skruː luːs/| idiom |有点疯;行为古怪;神经不正常 |To be slightly crazy or eccentric; to have a mental or emotional instability. This idiom implies that someone is not thinking clearly or acting rationally.  It suggests mild craziness, not serious mental illness.|He's always talking to himself – I think he has a screw loose. 他总是自言自语——我觉得他有点神经不正常。|Synonyms: crazy, eccentric, nuts, off one's rocker, unhinged|Antonyms: sane, rational, normal|idiomatic expression
