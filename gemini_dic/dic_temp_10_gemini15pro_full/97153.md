infinities:/ɪnˈfɪnɪtiz/| n. |无限，无穷；无限大|An unlimited extent of time, space, or quantity. |There are infinities of stars in the universe. 宇宙中有无限多的星星。|Near synonyms: eternity, immensity, boundlessness|Antonyms: finiteness, limit|noun

infinities:/ɪnˈfɪnɪtiz/| n. |无穷大(数学)|A value greater than any assignable quantity or countable number. |Calculus deals with infinities and infinitesimals. 微积分处理无穷大和无穷小。|Near synonyms:  infinity|Antonyms: zero, infinitesimal|noun
