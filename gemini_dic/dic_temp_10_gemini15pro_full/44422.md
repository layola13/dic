cross-pollinate:/ˌkrɒs ˈpɒləneɪt/ /ˌkrɔːs ˈpɑːləneɪt/| v. |异花授粉，交叉传授|To transfer pollen from the anther of a flower of one plant to the stigma of a flower of another plant.| Bees cross-pollinate plants as they gather nectar. 蜜蜂在采集花蜜时会对植物进行异花授粉。|Synonyms: cross-fertilize, hybridize|Antonyms: self-pollinate|verb
cross-pollination:/ˌkrɒs ˌpɒləˈneɪʃən/ /ˌkrɔːs ˌpɑːləˈneɪʃən/| n. |异花授粉，交叉传授|The transfer of pollen from the anther of a flower of one plant to the stigma of a flower of another plant.|Cross-pollination helps to maintain genetic diversity within plant species. 异花授粉有助于保持植物物种内的遗传多样性。|Synonyms: cross-fertilization, hybridization|Antonyms: self-pollination|noun
