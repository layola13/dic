effloraison:/ˌɛflɔːˈreɪsən/| n.|风化，盐析，（尤指文学艺术的）繁荣，兴盛|The efflorescence of Romanticism in the early 19th century. 19世纪早期浪漫主义的繁荣。|近义词：blooming, blossoming, flourishing, thriving|反义词：decline, decay, deterioration|noun

Etymology: From French *effloraison*, from *effleurir* "to blossom," from Latin *efflorescere* "to blossom, bloom," from *ex-* "out" + *florescere* "to begin to blossom," from *flos* "flower."
