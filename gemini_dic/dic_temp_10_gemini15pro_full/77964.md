genteelly:/dʒɛnˈtiːli/
adverb|文雅地，有教养地|In a refined or polite manner, showing good breeding.|She genteelly sipped her tea, extending her pinky finger. 她优雅地抿着茶，伸出小指。|
近义词: politely, courteously, gracefully, elegantly|
反义词: rudely, crudely, vulgarly|
词性: adverb

genteelly:/dʒɛnˈtiːli/
adjective|文雅的，有教养的|Refined or polite, showing good breeding.  (While primarily an adverb, can be used adjectivally in some older texts)| Though rarely used this way now, one might find in older literature a description of "genteelly raised children." 虽然现在很少这样使用，但在旧文学作品中可能会发现“有教养的孩子”这样的描述。|
近义词: refined, polite, courteous, graceful, elegant|
反义词: rude, crude, vulgar|
词性: adjective (archaic)


词源:
From gentil + -ly. Ultimately from Latin gentilis (“of the same clan or tribe”).
