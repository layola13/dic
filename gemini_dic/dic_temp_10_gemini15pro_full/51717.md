devisions:/dɪˈvɪʒənz/| n. | 分配; 分歧; 分裂; 部门 | The act or process of dividing something into parts or portions; disagreement or discord among people; a separate part of a larger organization. | The company made devisions based on performance. 公司根据绩效进行分配。| 近义词：partitions, segments, disagreements, schisms, departments | 反义词: unions, agreements, accords | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From the Late Middle English *devisioun*, from Old French *division*, ultimately from Latin *divisio*, *division-*, from *dividere* "to divide".
