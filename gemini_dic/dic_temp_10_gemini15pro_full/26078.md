bushwhack:/ˈbʊʃwæk/| v. n.| 丛林中开路；伏击；（美）在农村地区作政治宣传 |To travel through dense forest or undergrowth by cutting a path; to ambush someone from a concealed position in the woods; (US) to campaign for political office in rural areas|  We had to bushwhack our way through the dense jungle. 我们不得不在茂密的丛林中开辟道路。|Synonyms: hack, clear, cut, ambush, waylay|Antonyms:  stroll, wander (for the travel sense), retreat (for the ambush sense)|verb

bushwhack:/ˈbʊʃwæk/| n.| 丛林中开路的旅程；伏击 |A journey through dense forest or undergrowth; an ambush| The bushwhack through the mountains was arduous. 穿越山区的丛林之旅十分艰辛。|Synonyms: trek, hike (for the journey sense), ambush, attack (for the ambush sense)|Antonyms:  rest, relaxation (for the journey sense), defense (for the ambush sense)|noun
