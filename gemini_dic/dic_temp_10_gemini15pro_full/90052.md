hospitalize:/ˈhɒspɪtəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈhɑːspɪtəlaɪz/ (US)| vt.|使住院|To cause (someone) to be admitted to a hospital for treatment.|例句：The accident hospitalized five people.  这起事故导致五人住院。|近义词：admit, intern|反义词:discharge, release|verb
