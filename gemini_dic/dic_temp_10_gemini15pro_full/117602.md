meganucleus:/ˌmɛɡəˈnjuːkliəs/| n. |巨核; 大核|A large, often irregularly shaped nucleus, characteristic of certain cells, especially in ciliate protozoans.|The meganucleus of the paramecium contains multiple copies of the genome. 草履虫的大核包含基因组的多个副本。|Synonyms: macronucleus|Antonyms: micronucleus|noun
