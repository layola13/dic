immovable:/ɪˈmuːvəbl/
adjective
不可移动的；固定的；不动产的
Not able to be moved, physically or emotionally.
The statue seemed immovable, firmly rooted to the ground.  这座雕像似乎一动不动，牢牢地扎根在地上。
His face was immovable, showing no sign of emotion.  他的表情一动不动，没有流露出任何情绪。
近义词:fixed, fast, steadfast, unyielding, inflexible
反义词:movable, mobile, flexible
词性:adjective

immoveable:/ɪˈmuːvəbl/
noun
不动产
(chiefly law) Real estate; property that cannot be moved.
The immovables included the land and the house built upon it.  不动产包括土地和建在上面的房子。
His wealth was primarily in immovables.  他的财富主要是不动产。
近义词:real estate, property, land
反义词:movables, chattels
词性:noun


词源:
Late Middle English: from Old French immoble, from Latin immobilis, from in- ‘not’ + mobilis (from movere ‘to move’).
