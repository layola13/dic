mosquitoes:/məˈskiːtəʊz/ (UK) /məˈskiːtoʊz/ (US)| n. |蚊子|A small flying insect that bites people and animals and sucks their blood. Some types of mosquitoes spread serious diseases like malaria.|例句：Mosquitoes are a common nuisance in the summer. 夏天蚊子很常见。

例句：Use mosquito repellent to protect yourself from bites. 使用驱蚊剂来防止蚊虫叮咬。|近义词：gnat, midge|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
