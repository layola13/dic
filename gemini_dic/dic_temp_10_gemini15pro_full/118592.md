mesozoa:/ˌmɛzəˈzoʊə/ | n. | 中生动物 | A small phylum of parasitic animals, generally considered to be positioned between protozoa and metazoa in evolutionary development. They are characterized by their simple body structure and lack of true tissues and organs. | Mesozoa are typically found parasitizing marine invertebrates. 中生动物通常寄生在海洋无脊椎动物身上。| Mesozoans are small, multicellular parasites. 中生动物是小型多细胞寄生虫。| synonym: dicyemida | antonym: N/A | noun


mesozoan:/ˌmɛzəˈzoʊən/ | adj. n. | 中生动物的；中生动物 | Relating to or belonging to the Mesozoa phylum; a member of the Mesozoa phylum. | The mesozoan life cycle is complex and involves multiple stages. 中生动物的生命周期很复杂，涉及多个阶段。|  This research focuses on the mesozoan nervous system. 这项研究的重点是中生动物的神经系统。| synonym: dicyemid | antonym: N/A | adjective, noun
