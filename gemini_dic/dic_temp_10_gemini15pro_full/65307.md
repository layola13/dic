eudiometer:/ˌjuːdiˈɒmɪtər/ | n. | 量气管；气体分析器 | An instrument for measuring the volume changes of gases during chemical reactions, used especially in the analysis of gases. |  The chemist used a eudiometer to analyze the composition of the air sample. 化学家使用量气管分析空气样本的成分。

The eudiometer showed a decrease in gas volume after the reaction. 反应后，量气管显示气体体积减少了。| 近义词: gasometer | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *eu-* (good, well) + *dia-* (through) + *-meter* (measure).  Originally referred to an instrument for measuring the "goodness" of air.
