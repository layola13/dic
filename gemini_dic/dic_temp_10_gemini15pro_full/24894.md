bruised:/bruːzd/
| adj.| 擦伤的，瘀伤的；受挫的；（水果等）碰伤的 |Injured by being struck or pressed; emotionally hurt or upset; (of fruit) damaged by being handled roughly.|  She had a bruised knee. 她膝盖擦伤了。|  近义词：battered, injured, hurt, damaged, scraped, sore, aching, painful, tender, sensitive, upset, offended, hurt|  反义词：uninjured, healed, unhurt, undamaged|词性:形容词


bruise:/bruːz/
| n.| 擦伤；瘀伤；（水果等的）碰伤；（感情上的）挫伤|An injury appearing as an area of discolored skin on the body, caused by a blow or impact rupturing underlying blood vessels; emotional hurt or upset; a mark like a bruise on fruit.| The bruise on her arm was turning purple. 她胳膊上的瘀伤正在变成紫色。|近义词：contusion, injury, lesion, mark, blemish, discoloration, spot, scrape, scratch,  hurt, upset, offense, insult |反义词: healing|词性:名词
