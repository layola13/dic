nonoppressiveness:/ˌnɑːnəˈprɛsɪvnəs/
|n.|无压迫性；非压迫性
|The state or quality of not being oppressive; the absence of oppression.|
|The community strives for nonoppressiveness in all its interactions.| 这个社区力求在其所有互动中做到无压迫性。

|The organization is committed to fostering a culture of nonoppressiveness and inclusivity.| 该组织致力于营造一种非压迫性和包容性的文化。

|Near Synonyms:  tolerance, acceptance, inclusivity, equity, fairness, justice, liberation, empowerment, respect, equality|
|Antonyms: oppression, tyranny, repression, subjugation, discrimination, prejudice, bias, bigotry, injustice, inequality|
