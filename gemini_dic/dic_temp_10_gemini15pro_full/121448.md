misinterpreter:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɜːrprɪtər/ | n. |误解者，曲解者|Someone who misinterprets something; someone who understands something incorrectly. | He was a misinterpreter of my intentions. 他误解了我的意图。| The journalist was accused of being a misinterpreter of the facts.  这位记者被指控曲解事实。|Synonym: misconstruer, distorter|Antonym: interpreter, elucidator|noun

misinterpret:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɜːrprɪt/ | vt. |误解，曲解|To understand something incorrectly. | She misinterpreted my silence as anger. 她把我的沉默误解为愤怒。| He misinterpreted the instructions and did it wrong. 他误解了指示，做错了。|Synonym: misconstrue, misunderstand, misapprehend|Antonym: understand, comprehend, grasp|verb
