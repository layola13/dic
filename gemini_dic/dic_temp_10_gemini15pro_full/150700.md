parable:/ˈpærəbəl/ | n. | 寓言，比喻 | A short allegorical story designed to illustrate or teach some truth, religious principle, or moral lesson. |  The Good Samaritan is a famous parable about helping others.  好撒玛利亚人是一个关于帮助他人的著名寓言。 | synonym: allegory, fable, story | antonym:  fact, history | noun

parable:/ˈpærəbəl/ | n. | (尤指类似的)事物; 典型 | Something that serves as a typical example of something else. |  Her life is a parable of courage and resilience. 她的人生是勇气和韧性的典范。 | synonym: example, model, archetype | antonym: anomaly, exception | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *parabola, from Latin parabola "comparison, parable," from Greek parabolē "a comparison, juxtaposition, parable," from paraballein "to throw beside, compare," from para- "beside" + ballein "to throw."
