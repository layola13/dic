gazetteer: /ˌɡæzɪˈtɪr/ | n. |地名词典；地名索引|A geographical index or dictionary. A book or website that lists the names of places, with information about each place, such as its location, population, and history.| I consulted the gazetteer to find the precise location of the village. 我查阅了地名词典以找到该村庄的精确位置。

|例句：The online gazetteer provided detailed information about the region's topography.  在线地名词典提供了该地区地形的详细信息。

|例句：She used a gazetteer to locate the obscure town mentioned in the old diary. 她使用地名词典来查找旧日记中提到的那个默默无闻的小镇。

|近义词：atlas, index, directory, lexicon|反义词: (No direct antonym exists. Perhaps "misinformation" in a very loose sense.) |词性: noun
