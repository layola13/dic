nonresistible:/ˌnɒnrɪˈzɪstəbl/ | /ˌnɑːnrɪˈzɪstəbl/ | adj. | 不可抗拒的 | Unable to be resisted; too tempting or appealing to withstand. |  Her smile was nonresistible. 她的笑容令人无法抗拒。|  The offer of a free trip to Hawaii was nonresistible.  免费去夏威夷旅行的提议太诱人了，无法拒绝。 | 近义词：irresistible, compelling, tempting, alluring | 反义词：resistible, avoidable, undesirable | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non.
resistible (adjective): From Latin resistere "to resist,"  from re- "back" + sistere "to stand, take a stand."
