oceanographically: /ˌoʊʃənəˈɡræfɪkli/ | adv. | 海洋学地 |  In a way that relates to oceanography, the study of the physical and biological aspects of the ocean. | The data were analyzed oceanographically to understand the currents.  这些数据用海洋学方法进行了分析，以了解洋流。| 近义词: marine, maritime | 反义词: terrestrially | 词性: 副词

oceanography: /ˌoʊʃənˈɑːɡrəfi/ | n. | 海洋学 | The branch of science that deals with the physical and biological properties and phenomena of the sea. | She's studying oceanography at the University of Washington. 她在华盛顿大学学习海洋学。| 近义词: marine science, hydrology (partially) | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: 名词

词源:  From ocean + -graphy.  "Ocean" comes from the Greek Ōkeanós, the name of the great river or sea surrounding the earth. "-graphy" comes from the Greek -graphia, meaning "description" or "writing."
