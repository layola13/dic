flyout:/ˈflaɪaʊt/| n.|弹出菜单，弹出式面板|A flyout is a small window that appears when you click or hover over an item in a software program or website, providing additional options or information.|When I clicked on the icon, a flyout appeared with several options.|近义词：popup, dropdown, menu, submenu|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "main menu" in some contexts)|noun
