bungee:/ˈbʌndʒi/| vi. |进行蹦极跳|To jump from a high place while attached to a bungee cord.|She bungeed off the Macau Tower. 她在澳门塔蹦极。|近义词：bungee jump|反义词: (无明确反义词)|v.
