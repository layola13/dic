demigorge:/ˌdɛmiˈɡɔːrdʒ/ /ˌdɛmiˈɡɔːrdʒ/| v. |呕吐；大量涌出；倾泻|To vomit or discharge violently; to eject (something) as from a gorge.|The volcano demigorged lava and ash. 火山喷出熔岩和火山灰。|Synonyms: spew, vomit, discharge, eject, emit|Antonyms: absorb, ingest, retain|verb

demigorge:/ˌdɛmiˈɡɔːrdʒ/ /ˌdɛmiˈɡɔːrdʒ/| n. |（城堡的）突出部，半月堡|A projecting part of a fortification.|The demigorge of the castle offered a strategic advantage. 城堡的突出部提供了战略优势。|Synonyms: bastion, bulwark, projection|Antonyms:  recess, indentation|noun
