matches:/ˈmætʃɪz/ (US), /ˈmætʃɪz/ (UK)| n. |比赛，火柴，相配的人或物|A small stick of wood or cardboard with a flammable tip, ignited by friction.  A contest in which people or teams compete against each other in a particular sport or game. Something similar or corresponding.|例句：The two teams played three matches. 这两支队伍进行了三场比赛。

例句：He struck a match to light the candle. 他划了根火柴点燃蜡烛。

近义词：games, contests (for competition), lighters (for ignition), counterparts, equivalents|反义词:mismatches|词性:名词

To be equal or similar to something else.|例句：The curtains and the carpet are a perfect match.  窗帘和地毯非常相配。

例句：Can you match this piece of fabric? 你能找到和这块布料一样的吗？
