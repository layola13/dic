hakeem:/hɑːˈkiːm/ | n. | 医师，医生；统治者，执政者 | A wise or learned person, especially a Muslim scholar of law, theology, and medicine.  Often used as a title of respect. | He consulted a hakeem for his chronic illness. 他向一位医师咨询了他的慢性病。|  The hakeem offered advice on both physical and spiritual health. 这位医师提供了身心健康方面的建议。 | 近义词: physician, doctor, healer, ruler, leader | 反义词: patient, follower | 词性: 名词

词源:
From Arabic حَكِيم (ḥakīm), meaning "wise", "judicious", "sage", "philosopher", "physician".  Derived from the root حَكَمَ (ḥakama), "to judge", "to rule", "to govern".
