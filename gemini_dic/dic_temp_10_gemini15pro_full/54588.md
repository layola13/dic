disman:/dɪsˈmæn/ | n. | 系统管理员；系统操作员 | A person responsible for managing and maintaining a computer system or network. | The disman was called in to fix the server crash.  系统管理员被叫来修复服务器崩溃。 | 近义词：system administrator, sysadmin, network administrator | 反义词：user | 词性: 名词

disman:/dɪsˈmæn/ | v. |  撤销人员编制; 裁员; 解雇 | To remove someone from a position or job, often as part of a reduction in workforce. | The company dismanned several employees due to budget cuts.  由于预算削减，公司裁掉了数名员工。 | 近义词：dismiss, fire, lay off, make redundant | 反义词：hire, employ, recruit | 词性: 动词
