decantation:/ˌdiːkænˈteɪʃən/
n.
倾析；滗析
The process of pouring a liquid from one container into another, typically in order to separate a sediment or other unwanted substance.
The wine underwent decantation to remove the sediment.  这葡萄酒经过倾析以去除沉淀物。

近义词:pouring, separation, filtration
反义词:mixing, combination
