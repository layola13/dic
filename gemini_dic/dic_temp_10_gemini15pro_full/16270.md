baphomet:/ˈbæfəmɛt/ (US), /ˈbæfəmet/ (UK)| n. |巴风特，羊头恶魔|A deity or demon, historically associated with the Knights Templar, and later adopted as a symbol by occultists.|The image of Baphomet adorned the altar during the occult ritual. 巴风特的形象装饰着神秘仪式的祭坛。|Synonyms: idol, deity, demon, icon|Antonyms: god, angel|noun
