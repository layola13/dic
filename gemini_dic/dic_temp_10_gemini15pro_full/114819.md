mamelon:/ˈmæməlɒn/ (UK) /ˈmæməlɑːn/ (US)| n. |乳头，乳突，圆丘|A nipple-like projection or protuberance, especially on a hill or mountain, or a rounded protuberance resembling a nipple.|The architect designed the building with a mamelon at the top, echoing the shape of the nearby hills. 建筑师设计的建筑顶部有一个圆丘，与附近山丘的形状相呼应。|Synonyms: nipple, teat, protuberance, knob, mound|Antonyms: depression, hollow, concavity|noun

mamelon:/ˈmæməlɒn/ (UK) /ˈmæməlɑːn/ (US)| adj. |乳头状的|Shaped like a nipple.|The mamelon-shaped hill was a distinctive landmark in the area.  乳头状的小山是该地区一个独特的标志。|Synonyms: nipple-shaped, mammillary, mammiform|Antonyms: flat, concave|adjective
