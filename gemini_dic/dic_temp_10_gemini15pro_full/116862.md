maximumly:/ˈmæksɪməli/| adv.|最大程度地，极其|This word is not standard English and is rarely used.  It is formed by adding the adverbial suffix "-ly" to the adjective "maximum."  Native speakers generally use "maximally" instead. |*He tried maximumly to achieve his goals. (Not standard English - better to use "He tried his utmost/He made every effort...")|*She was maximumly efficient. (Not standard English - better to use "She was extremely/highly efficient.")|近义词：maximally, extremely, highly, utterly, completely|反义词: minimally, slightly, hardly|词性: adverb
