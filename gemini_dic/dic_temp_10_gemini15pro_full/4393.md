alamites:/ˌæləˈmaɪtiːz/
n.
芦木；芦木化石
Extinct tree-like plants related to horsetails, dominant in Carboniferous swamps.  They had segmented, hollow stems and whorled leaves.
The fossilized remains of *alamites* are often found in coal deposits.  芦木化石经常在煤矿中被发现。
The museum displayed several impressive specimens of *alamites* fossils.  博物馆展出了几件令人印象深刻的芦木化石标本。
近义词:Calamites (often used synonymously, though technically a specific genus within the group)
反义词:N/A (No direct antonym for an extinct plant group)
词性:noun


词源: From Modern Latin *Alamites*, plural of *Alamites*, from Greek *κάλαμος* (kálamos, “reed”).
