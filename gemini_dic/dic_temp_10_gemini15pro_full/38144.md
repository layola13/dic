compressibility:/kəmˌprɛsɪˈbɪlɪti/
n.|可压缩性|The quality of being compressible; the extent to which something can be compressed.|The compressibility of gas is much higher than that of liquid.  气体的可压缩性比液体高得多。|compactability, density|incompressibility|noun

compress:/kəmˈprɛs/
vt.|压缩，压紧|To press or squeeze together; to reduce in size or volume.|The nurse compressed the wound to stop the bleeding. 护士按压伤口止血。|squeeze, condense, compact|expand, enlarge|verb

compressed:/kəmˈprɛst/
adj.|压缩的，被压缩的|Reduced in size or volume by pressure.|Compressed air is used in many industrial applications. 压缩空气在许多工业应用中使用。|compact, condensed, squeezed|expanded, enlarged|adjective
