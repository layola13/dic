battener:/ˈbætənər/ (US), /ˈbætnə(r)/ (UK)| n. |板条，压条|A thin strip of wood or metal used to strengthen or support something, or to fasten things together. |The old sails were reinforced with battens. 旧帆用板条加固了。|近义词:strip, slat, lath|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "support beam" for larger contrasting element)|noun
