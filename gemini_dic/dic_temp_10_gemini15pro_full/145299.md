overbravenesses: /ˌoʊvərˈbreɪvnɪsɪz/
noun
过度勇敢；鲁莽的行为(复数)
The state of being excessively brave, often to the point of recklessness.  It refers to multiple instances of this behavior.
His youthful overbravenesses led to several close calls with serious injury. 他年轻时的鲁莽行为让他几次险些重伤。
Their overbravenesses in the face of danger were admirable, but ultimately foolish.  他们在危险面前的过度勇敢令人钦佩，但最终是愚蠢的。
Synonyms: recklessnesses, rashnesses, audacities, temerities
Antonyms: cautiousnesses, timidities
