paranotum: /ˌpærəˈnəʊtəm/ (US) /ˌpærəˈnəʊtəm/ (UK)| n. |侧背板；翅基片|A lateral expansion of the notum of an insect thorax, especially of certain Hemiptera and Orthoptera, overlying the base of the wing.|The paranotum of this grasshopper species is particularly pronounced.  这种蚱蜢的侧背板特别明显。|Synonym:tegmen|Antonym: N/A|noun
