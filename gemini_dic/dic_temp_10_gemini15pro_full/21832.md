bode:/boʊd/ (US) /bəʊd/ (UK)| v.|预示，预兆|To indicate something, typically something that will happen in the future.|The dark clouds bode rain.  乌云预示着要下雨。|The economic indicators bode well for the future. 经济指标预示着未来会很好。|synonym: presage, portend, augur, foretell|antonym:  N/A|verb

Etymology:
From Old English *bodian "to announce, proclaim, foretell," from Proto-Germanic *bodjanan "to announce" (source also of Old Saxon bodian "to announce, command," Old Frisian bodia "to preach, command," Middle Dutch boeden "to command, offer," Old High German botan "to command, announce, preach," German bieten "to offer, bid," Gothic boda "messenger").
