lauraceous:/lɔːˈreɪʃəs/
adjective
月桂科的；似月桂的
Belonging to or resembling the laurel family (Lauraceae).
The lauraceous trees filled the air with a spicy fragrance. 月桂科的树木使空气中充满了辛辣的香味。
Synonyms:laurel-like, aromatic
Antonyms:non-aromatic
词性:adjective
