expungement:/ɪkˈspʌndʒmənt/ | n. |消除，删除，抹去|The act of removing something completely, especially from a record. |例句：He applied for expungement of his criminal record. 他申请消除他的犯罪记录。

例句：The expungement of her juvenile record allowed her to pursue a career in law. 她少年犯罪记录的消除使她能够从事法律事业。|近义词：deletion, removal, erasure, obliteration |反义词: retention, preservation, record |词性: 名词
