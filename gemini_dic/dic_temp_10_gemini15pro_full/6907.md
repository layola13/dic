anchovy:/ˈænˌtʃoʊvi/ (US), /ˈænˌtʃəvi/ (UK)| n. |凤尾鱼，鯷鱼|A small, common saltwater fish, often used as food, especially after being cured, salted, or canned.|例句：I like pizza with anchovies. 我喜欢凤尾鱼披萨。

例句：Anchovies are a key ingredient in Caesar salad dressing. 凤尾鱼是凯撒沙拉酱的主要成分。


近义词：sprat, sardine (similar small fish)|反义词: None (no direct opposite for a specific type of fish)|词性:noun
