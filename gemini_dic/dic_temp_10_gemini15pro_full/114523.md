malaise:/mæˈleɪz/ | n. | 不适， malaise; uneasiness; discomfort | A general feeling of discomfort, illness, or unease whose exact cause is difficult to identify. |  Many voters expressed a feeling of malaise about the current political climate. 许多选民表达了对当前政治气候的不安。| synonyms: uneasiness, discomfort, illness, sickness, unease, hardship | antonyms: comfort, well-being, health, energy | noun

malaises:/mæˈleɪzɪz/ | n. | 不适（复数）| Plural form of malaise, referring to multiple instances of a general feeling of discomfort or unease. | The report highlighted several economic malaises affecting the country.  这份报告强调了影响该国的几个经济困境。| synonyms: uneasinesses, discomforts, illnesses, sicknesses | antonyms: comforts, well-beings, healths | noun
