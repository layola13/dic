overhappily:/ˌoʊvərˈhæpɪli/| adv.| 过于快乐地，过分幸福地 |She laughed overhappily, almost hysterically. 她笑得过于开心，几乎歇斯底里。|例句：He overhappily accepted the invitation. 他欣然接受了邀请。|近义词：excessively happily, too happily|反义词：unhappily, sadly|词性:副词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer) + "happily" (from "happy" + "-ly"). "Over-" indicates excess or beyond a normal limit. "Happy" (from Middle English *happi) ultimately derives from a Proto-Germanic root meaning "lucky, fortunate."  "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives.
