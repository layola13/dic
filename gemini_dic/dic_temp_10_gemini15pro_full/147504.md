overpained:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪnd/| adj.|过度痛苦的，极度痛苦的|Experiencing an excessive amount of pain, beyond what is considered normal or bearable.  The word emphasizes the intensity and overwhelming nature of the pain.|She felt overpained after the surgery, requiring a higher dose of medication.  手术后她感到极度疼痛，需要更高剂量的药物。|近义词：agonized, excruciatingly pained, intensely pained|反义词: comfortable, pain-free|词性:形容词
词源: "Over-" (prefix indicating excess) + "pained" (past participle of "pain," meaning to cause suffering).
