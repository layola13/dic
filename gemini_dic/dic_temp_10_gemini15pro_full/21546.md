bluegrass:/ˈbluːɡræs/| n. adj.|蓝草音乐；蓝草|A type of country music that originated in the Appalachian Mountains of the United States, characterized by fast tempos, intricate harmonies, and the use of instruments such as the banjo, fiddle, guitar, mandolin, and double bass.|Example:  I went to a bluegrass festival last weekend. 我上周末去了一个蓝草音乐节。

Example:  She plays bluegrass music on her mandolin.  她用她的曼陀林演奏蓝草音乐。|近义词：country music, mountain music|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps pop music or classical music)|词性: noun, adjective
