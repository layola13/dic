endocarditis:/ˌɛndəʊkɑːˈdaɪtɪs/| n.|心内膜炎|Inflammation of the endocardium, the inner lining of the heart.  It is usually caused by a bacterial infection.|The patient was diagnosed with endocarditis after experiencing persistent fever and fatigue.  病人持续发烧和疲劳后被诊断为心内膜炎。|Near synonyms:  heart infection, infective endocarditis|Antonyms: None (Diseases don't typically have antonyms)|Noun
