brickest:/ˈbrɪkɪst/
adjective
最像砖的;最坚固的
Most resembling a brick; most solid.
This house is the brickest one on the street. 这房子是街上最坚固的。
近义词:solidest, firmest
反义词:weakest, flimsiest
词性:形容词


词源:
From brick + -est (superlative suffix).  The word "brick" itself comes from Middle Dutch *bricke, related to Old French brique.
