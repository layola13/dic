incinerated:/ɪnˈsɪnəreɪtɪd/
verb
焚化的，烧毁的
reduced to ashes; burnt to ashes.
The documents were incinerated after the investigation.  调查结束后，文件被焚毁了。
The waste was incinerated in a special furnace.  废物在特制炉中焚化。
Synonyms: burned, cremated, destroyed, consumed
Antonyms: preserved, saved, protected
