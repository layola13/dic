particularising:/pəˈtɪkjʊləraɪzɪŋ/
verb
详细说明；具体化
Giving specific details or examples to illustrate a point.
The report particularised the steps taken to improve safety.  这份报告详细说明了为提高安全而采取的步骤。
He particularised his objections to the plan. 他详细说明了他对该计划的反对意见。
synonyms: specifying, detailing, itemizing, enumerating
antonyms: generalising, summarising, abstracting
v.
