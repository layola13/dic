infirmnesses:/ɪnˈfɜːrmnəsɪz/ | n. | 虚弱，衰弱；疾病 |Physical and mental infirmnesses come with old age.  |  The elderly man's increasing infirmnesses made it difficult for him to live independently. | 近义词: weaknesses, frailties, ailments, disabilities | 反义词: strengths, robustness, health | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *infirmnes*, from Old French *infermete* (Modern French *infirmité*), from Latin *infirmitās*, from *infirmus* (“weak, not strong”).
