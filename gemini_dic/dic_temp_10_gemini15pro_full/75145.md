fritfly:/ˈfrɪtflaɪ/| n.|麦秆蝇|A small black fly (Oscinella frit), the larva of which feeds on the stems of cereals, especially oats, causing substantial crop losses. |The fritfly can cause significant damage to oat crops. 麦秆蝇会对燕麦作物造成重大损害。|The fritfly infestation decimated the oat yield this year. 今年，麦秆蝇的侵扰大大减少了燕麦的产量。|近义词：cereal fly|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From frit + fly.  "Frit" is of unknown origin, possibly imitative of the insect's buzzing.
