disorderly:/dɪsˈɔːdəli/| adj.|混乱的，无序的，妨碍治安的|Lacking order or tidiness; messy or untidy. Behaving in a way that disrupts public order.|The room was in a disorderly state after the party. 房间在派对后一片狼藉。|He was arrested for disorderly conduct. 他因扰乱治安行为被捕。|近义词：messy, untidy, chaotic, unruly, disruptive|反义词：orderly, tidy, organized, neat|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from disorder + -ly.
