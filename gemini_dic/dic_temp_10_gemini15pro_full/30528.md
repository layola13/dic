cephalically:/səˈfælɪkli/
adverb
头向地，头部地
With the head foremost; toward the head.
The baby was positioned cephalically for birth. 婴儿头位分娩。
近义词:head-first, headwards
反义词:caudally, feet first
词性:adverb
