collado:/kɔˈʎaðoʊ/ (US), /kɔˈʝaðo/ (Spain)| n.|山坳，山口；鞍部|A high pass or ridge between two mountains.  A col.  Derived from Spanish.|例句：The hikers reached the collado just before sunset.  徒步旅行者们在日落前到达了山口。

例句：The collado offered stunning views of the valley below. 山口提供了下面山谷的壮丽景色。|近义词：pass, saddle, ridge, gap|反义词:valley, basin|词性:noun
