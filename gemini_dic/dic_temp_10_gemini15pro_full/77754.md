gene:/dʒiːn/ | n. |基因|A unit of heredity which is transferred from a parent to offspring and is held to determine some characteristic of the offspring.|
Scientists have identified the gene responsible for the disease. 科学家已经确定了导致这种疾病的基因。|
Genes determine the characteristics of an organism. 基因决定了生物体的特征。|
synonyms: factor, determinant, element|
antonyms: N/A|
