changet: /ˈtʃeɪndʒt/ |v. | changed (simple past), changed (past participle) |改变了的，变化了的 | This is a past participle form of the verb "change," meaning something has been altered or transformed. | The changed landscape was barely recognizable after the flood.  洪水过后，改变了的景象几乎认不出来了。| The company's changed policies are more environmentally friendly. 公司改变后的政策更加环保。 | 近义词: altered, modified, transformed, converted | 反义词: unchanged, unaltered, static, constant | 词性: 动词(past participle)


Etymology:
From Middle English *chaungen, from Old French changier, from Late Latin cambiare (“to barter, exchange”), of uncertain origin. Perhaps related to Vulgar Latin *cambia (“exchange, truck”), ultimately from a pre-Roman substrate.
