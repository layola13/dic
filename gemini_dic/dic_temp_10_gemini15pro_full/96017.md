incute: /ɪnˈkjuːt/
adjective
灌输的; 注入的; 鼓吹的; 激发的
instilled, implanted, infused (especially of a quality or condition).
例句：He incited his followers to violence. 他煽动他的追随者使用暴力。

例句：The government's actions incited widespread protests. 政府的行动引发了广泛的抗议。

近义词：instill, implant, infuse, inspire, provoke, incite
反义词：suppress, quell, discourage, dissuade
词性: adjective (形容词)

词源:  From Latin incutere, from in- "in" + cutere "to strike."  Related to "incisive."  The meaning shifted from "strike in" to "impress upon" and then to "inspire."
