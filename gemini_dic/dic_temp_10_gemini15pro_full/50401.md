depositee:/dɪˌpɒzɪˈtiː/
n.
受托人；存款人
The person or organization to whom something is entrusted or deposited.
The depositee is responsible for safeguarding the assets. 受托人有责任保护资产。
The bank acted as depositee for the client's funds.  银行作为客户资金的受托人。
近义词: trustee, beneficiary, recipient
反义词: depositor, payer
词性: noun
