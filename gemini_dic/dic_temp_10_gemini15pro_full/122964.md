monacid:/məˈnæsɪd/ | adj. | 一元的；一碱价的 | Containing only one replaceable hydrogen atom or radical.  A monacid base is one containing only one hydroxyl group. |  Phosphoric acid can act as a monacid, diacid, or triacid. 磷酸可以作为一元酸、二元酸或三元酸。| Synonym: monoacid, monobasic | Antonym: polyacid, polybasic | adjective
