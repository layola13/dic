leatherette:/ˌlɛðəˈrɛt/| n. |人造革，仿皮|An imitation leather, typically made of plastic or coated fabric. |例句：My new car seats are upholstered in leatherette. 我的新车座椅用人造革装饰。

例句：She bought a leatherette handbag because it was cheaper than real leather. 她买了一个人造革手提包，因为它比真皮的便宜。|近义词：artificial leather, faux leather, imitation leather, synthetic leather|反义词:genuine leather, real leather|词性:noun
