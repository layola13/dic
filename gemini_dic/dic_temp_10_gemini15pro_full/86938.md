hempy:/ˈhɛmpi/| adj.|麻质的；似麻的；粗糙的；含大麻的|Made of, resembling, or containing hemp.  Relating to or suggestive of hemp, especially in being rough or coarse.|例句：The rope was hempy and strong.  绳子是麻制的，很结实。

例句：He had a hempy smell about him. 他身上有一股大麻味。|近义词：fibrous, coarse, cannabis-related|反义词:smooth, fine|词性:adjective
