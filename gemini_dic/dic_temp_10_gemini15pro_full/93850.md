immobilities:/ɪˌmoʊˈbɪlɪtiz/
|n.|不动的状态；不动产；固定资产|The state of being unable to move or be moved; lack of mobility.  Also refers to assets that are fixed and difficult to transfer.|
|例句：The patient's immobilities after the stroke were a major concern.  病人中风后的不动状态是一个主要问题。|
|例句：The company's immobilities included land, buildings, and machinery.  公司的固定资产包括土地、建筑物和机械。|
|近义词：inactivity, stillness, rigidity, fixity, assets, properties|
|反义词：mobility, movement, fluidity, activity, liabilities|
|词性：名词|
