melding:/ˈmɛldɪŋ/ | n. | 融合，混合物，结合 | A melding of different styles creates a unique look.  |  The architect achieved a beautiful melding of traditional and modern design. | 近义词: blend, mixture, fusion, amalgamation, synthesis | 反义词: separation, division, disjunction | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Low German *melden, from Old Saxon *meldōn, from Proto-Germanic *maldjaną (“to announce, proclaim, betray”). Cognate with Dutch melden (“to report, announce”), German melden (“to report, announce”). The noun is derived from the verb, referring to something that has been "announced" or "brought together".
