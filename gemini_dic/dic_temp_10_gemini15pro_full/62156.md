enclosure:/ɪnˈkloʊʒər/ /ɛnˈkloʊʒə(r)/ | n. | 附件, 围场,  enclosure |Something that is enclosed, such as a document or photograph included with a letter, or a piece of land surrounded by a fence or wall.  |例句：Please find the requested documents in the enclosure.  请在附件中找到所需文件。

例句：The sheep were grazing in the enclosure. 羊群在围场里吃草。


|近义词：attachment, document, paddock, compound, fence |反义词:openness, freedom | noun
