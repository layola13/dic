arrestee:/əˌresˈtiː/
n.
被捕者；被拘留者
A person who has been arrested.
The arrestee was taken to the police station for questioning.  被捕者被带到警察局接受讯问。
The arrestee exercised their right to remain silent.  被捕者行使了他们保持沉默的权利。
近义词：detainee, prisoner
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
词源: From arrest + -ee (suffix forming nouns denoting a person who is the object of the action of the verb).
