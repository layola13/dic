displeasurably:/dɪsˈpleʒərəbli/
adverb
不愉快地，令人不快地
in a way that causes displeasure or discomfort.
The afternoon passed displeasurably. 下午过得很不愉快。
近义词:unpleasantly, disagreeably
反义词:pleasantly, enjoyably
词性:副词

词源:
From displeasure + -ably.  Displeasure itself comes from dis- (expressing negation) + pleasure.
