atheisticalness:/ˌeɪθiˈɪstɪkəlnəs/
n.
无神论性质；不信神
The state or quality of being atheistic; atheism.
His atheisticalness was evident in his refusal to attend church. 他的无神论性质体现在他拒绝去教堂。
近义词: atheism, unbelief, godlessness, irreligion, nonbelief
