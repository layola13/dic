dilaceration:/dɪˌlæsəˈreɪʃən/
n.
撕裂；断裂；牙根弯曲
The tearing or laceration of tissues.  A deviation or bend in the root of a tooth.
The dentist identified a dilaceration in the patient's canine root.  牙医在病人的犬齿根部发现了一处弯曲。
The accident caused severe dilaceration of the muscles in his leg.  事故导致他腿部肌肉严重撕裂。
近义词: tearing, ripping, laceration, rupture, avulsion
反义词: joining, connection, union
词性: noun
