nariform:/ˈnɛərɪfɔːrm/| adj.|鼻形的，像鼻子的|Resembling a nose in shape.|The nariform appendage on the insect's head helped it to sense its surroundings. 昆虫头部像鼻子一样的附属物帮助它感知周围的环境。|近义词：nose-shaped, noselike|反义词:|adjective
词源: From Latin *nāriformis*, from nāris (“nostril”) + -formis (“-shaped”).
