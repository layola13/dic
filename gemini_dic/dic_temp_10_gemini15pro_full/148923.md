overwinter:/ˌoʊvərˈwɪntər/ | v. | 过冬；越冬 | To survive the winter; to remain alive or viable throughout the winter. |  Many birds overwinter in warmer climates. 许多鸟类在温暖的气候中过冬。

The Monarch butterfly overwinters in Mexico.  帝王蝶在墨西哥过冬。| 近义词: hibernate, winter | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 summer) | 词性: 动词


Etymology:
From "over" + "winter".  "Over" in this context means "through" or "during".
