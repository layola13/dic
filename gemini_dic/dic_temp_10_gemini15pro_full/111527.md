loathful:/ˈloʊθfʊl/ (US), /ˈləʊθfʊl/ (UK)| adj.|令人厌恶的；讨厌的；不愿意的|Feeling or showing dislike or aversion; reluctant.|He gave a loathful glance at the pile of dishes. 他厌恶地瞥了一眼那一堆盘子。|Synonyms: reluctant, unwilling, averse, disinclined, hesitant|Antonyms: willing, eager, keen, enthusiastic|adjective

loathful:/ˈloʊθfʊl/ (US), /ˈləʊθfʊl/ (UK)| adj.|充满厌恶的|Filled with dislike or aversion.|The room was filled with a loathful silence. 房间里充满了令人厌恶的沉默。|Synonyms: repugnant, repulsive, abhorrent, detestable|Antonyms: pleasant, agreeable, delightful|adjective
