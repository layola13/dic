eliminatory:/ɪˈlɪmɪnətɔːri/ | adj. |  淘汰的，预赛的 |Serving to eliminate or remove, especially from a competition.| The eliminatory rounds of the tournament were held last week.  上周举行了锦标赛的预赛。 | 近义词:qualifying, preliminary | 反义词:final, decisive | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *eliminatorius*, from Latin eliminare (“to turn out of doors, expel”) + -ory.  Eliminare is from ex- (“out”) + limen, liminis (“threshold”).
