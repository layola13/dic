cocoon:/kəˈkuːn/ | n. |茧;  保护层;  舒适安全的地方|A silky case spun by the larva of many insects, as protection for the pupa.  A state or situation of being protected and comfortable. | The caterpillar spun a cocoon around itself. 毛毛虫吐丝结茧把自己裹起来。|  She wrapped herself in a cocoon of blankets. 她用毯子把自己裹得像个茧一样。 |synonyms:  shell, case, chrysalis, sanctuary, refuge | antonyms: exposure, vulnerability | noun

cocoon:/kəˈkuːn/ | v. | 把…裹在茧中;  保护;  使(自己)舒适安全 |To wrap or enclose in or as in a cocoon. To protect or shelter. | The child was cocooned in blankets. 孩子被裹在毯子里。 |  He cocooned himself in his room, away from the noise and stress of the city. 他把自己关在房间里，远离城市的喧嚣和压力。| synonyms: envelop, protect, shelter, insulate | antonyms: expose, endanger | verb
