non-pluralistic:/ˌnɒnˈplʊərəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非多元化的，非多元主义的 | Not characterized by pluralism; lacking diversity of views, beliefs, or practices.  A system where only one ideology or perspective is dominant, excluding or marginalizing others. | The regime was criticized for its non-pluralistic approach to governance, suppressing dissenting voices.  这个政权因其非多元化的治理方式而受到批评，压制了异议。 | non-diverse, monolithic, uniform, homogenous | pluralistic, diverse, multicultural, heterogeneous | adjective
