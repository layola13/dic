deuteropathia:/ˌdjuːtərəˈpæθiə/| n. |继发性疾病，继发病|A disease or diseased condition that is secondary to another disease.|Example: The patient's deuteropathia was a result of the initial infection.  这位病人的继发性疾病是由最初的感染引起的。

Example:  The doctor explained that the skin rash was a deuteropathia caused by the underlying autoimmune disorder. 医生解释说，皮疹是由潜在的自身免疫性疾病引起的继发性疾病。|近义词:secondary disease, sequela, complication|反义词:primary disease, initial illness|词性:noun

Etymology:  From Greek *deuteros* (second) + *pathos* (suffering, disease).
