decalcify:/diːˈkælsɪfaɪ/
verb
去除钙化物；脱钙
To remove calcium salts from; to soften by removing calcium salts.
The dentist decalcified the patient's teeth to remove the plaque.  牙医去除病人牙齿上的牙菌斑以脱钙。
The bones were decalcified before being sectioned for microscopic study.  在被切片用于显微镜研究之前，骨头被脱钙了。
synonyms: delime, descale
antonyms: calcify
词性: 动词
