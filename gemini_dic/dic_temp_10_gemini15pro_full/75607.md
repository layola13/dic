fuga:/ˈfjuːɡə/ /ˈfuːɡɑː/ | n. |赋格; 遁走曲; |A contrapuntal composition in which a short melody or phrase (the subject) is introduced by one part and successively taken up by others and developed by interweaving the parts.| Bach's fugues are renowned for their complexity and beauty. 巴赫的赋格曲以其复杂性和优美而闻名。| The organist played a beautiful fuga.  风琴演奏家演奏了一首优美的赋格曲。|Synonyms: composition, piece, music|Antonyms: silence, discord| noun
