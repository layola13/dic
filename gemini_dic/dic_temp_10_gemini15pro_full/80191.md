godless:/ˈɡɒdləs/ (US) /ˈɡɑːdləs/ (UK)| adj.|无神的；不敬神的；邪恶的|Lacking belief in God or gods; wicked or immoral.|例句：He lived a godless life, indulging in every excess. 他过着无神的生活，沉溺于各种放纵。

例句：The villagers feared the godless stranger who had arrived in their midst. 村民们害怕来到他们中间的不敬神的陌生人。|近义词：atheistic, impious, irreligious, ungodly, wicked|反义词：religious, pious, devout, godly|词性:形容词
