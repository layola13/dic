avisely:/əˈvaɪzɪdli/
adverb
明智地，审慎地
In a careful and wise manner.
He avisely chose to remain silent. 他明智地选择了保持沉默。
She invested her money avisely. 她明智地投资了她的钱。
近义词: wisely, prudently, judiciously, sagaciously
反义词: foolishly, unwisely, imprudently, rashly
词性: adverb

词源:
From Middle English *avisedli, from Old French aviseé (“advised”), past participle of aviser (“to advise”), from Vulgar Latin *advidere (“to look at, consider”), from Latin ad (“to”) + vidēre (“to see”).
