madrasa:/məˈdræsə/ /ˈmædrəsə/ | n. |伊斯兰学校，经学院|A Muslim school, especially one attached to a mosque, for the study of Islam and other subjects. |  Example 1: He attended a madrasa in his village. 他在他村里的经学院上学。

Example 2:  The madrasa provided education for children in the community. 这所伊斯兰学校为社区里的孩子们提供教育。| synonym: Islamic school, religious school | antonym: secular school | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Arabic مدرسة (madrasa), meaning "school," from the root درس (darasa) "to study."
