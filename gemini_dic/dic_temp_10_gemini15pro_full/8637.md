anticompetitiveness:/ˌæntikəmˈpɛtɪtɪvnəs/| n. |反竞争性，反竞争行为|The state of being anticompetitive; actions or practices that suppress competition in a market.  It often refers to behaviors that limit consumer choice or inflate prices.|  The company was accused of anticompetitiveness after it bought out its main rival. 该公司在收购其主要竞争对手后被指控反竞争行为。|近义词：monopolistic, restrictive, exclusionary|反义词:competitive, pro-competition|词性:noun
