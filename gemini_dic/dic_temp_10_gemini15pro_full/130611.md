nonadhesions:/ˌnɒnədˈhiːʒənz/
noun
无粘连；不粘连
The absence of adhesions, typically referring to the absence of abnormal fibrous bands that can form between tissues and organs after surgery or inflammation.
After the laparoscopic procedure, the surgeon confirmed the nonadhesions of the abdominal organs. 腹腔镜手术后，外科医生确认腹部器官没有粘连。
The use of an anti-adhesion barrier during surgery promotes nonadhesions. 手术期间使用防粘连屏障可促进无粘连。
Synonyms: absence of adhesions, adhesion-free
Antonyms: adhesions
n.
