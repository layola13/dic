ape-like:/ˈeɪpˌlaɪk/
adjective
像猿的，类人猿的
Resembling an ape in appearance or behavior.
His posture was ape-like, hunched over with long arms dangling. 他的姿势像猿猴，驼背，长胳膊垂着。
The creature had an ape-like face and powerful limbs.  这个生物有一张像猿猴一样的脸和强壮的四肢。

synonym: simian, anthropoid, primate-like
antonym: human-like, humanoid
