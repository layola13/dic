color-force:/ˈkʌlər fɔːrs/| n.|色力|A fundamental force in particle physics that describes the strong interaction between quarks and gluons, responsible for holding protons and neutrons together.|Quarks interact through the exchange of particles called gluons, which carry the color force.|The color force is mediated by gluons.|近义词：strong force, strong nuclear force|反义词: weak force, weak nuclear force|名词
