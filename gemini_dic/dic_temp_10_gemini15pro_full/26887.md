cakeyest:/ˈkeɪkiɪst/| adj.|最像蛋糕的；最油腻的；最矫揉造作的|Having the qualities of cake to the greatest degree; excessively sweet, rich, or sentimental.  This word is often used informally and can be considered slang.|Her makeup was so cakeyest, it looked like she could frost a cupcake with it. 她的妆容太厚了，看起来像可以用它来给纸杯蛋糕上糖霜。|近义词：cloying, saccharine, overdone, artificial|反义词:natural, understated, subtle|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"Cakey" comes from "cake" + "-y" (a suffix forming adjectives meaning “full of” or “characterized by”).  "Cakeyest" adds the superlative suffix "-est" to "cakey."
