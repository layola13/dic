attributors:/əˈtrɪbjʊtərz/| n.| 属性；属性提供程序；归因者|People who give credit or ascribe something to a particular cause or source. |The attributors of the painting to Leonardo da Vinci were proven wrong. 这幅画的归属者被证明是错的，它并非出自达芬奇之手。|synonyms: assigner, imputer, credit givers|antonyms: deniers, refuters|noun
