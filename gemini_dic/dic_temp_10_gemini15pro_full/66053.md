exasperaters:/ɪɡˈzæspəˌreɪtərz/ | n. | 激怒者，令人恼火的人或事物 | People who constantly interrupt are exasperaters.  不断打断别人的人真是令人恼火。|  The constant noise from the construction site was one of the biggest exasperaters.  建筑工地持续不断的噪音是最令人恼火的事情之一。 |  irritants, annoyances |  soothers, comforters | noun


exasperate:/ɪɡˈzæspəreɪt/ | v. | 激怒，使恼怒 |  His constant complaining exasperated me. 他不停地抱怨，把我惹火了。 | The traffic delays exasperated drivers. 交通延误激怒了司机们。| annoy, irritate, infuriate | calm, soothe, placate | verb


词源:  From Latin exasperatus, past participle of exasperare "to roughen, irritate," from ex- "out" + asperare "to make rough," from asper "rough."
