nonpreferentially:/ˌnɒnprɛfəˈrɛnʃəli/
adverb
非优先地，无差别地
without preference; in a nonpreferential manner.  This means treating all options or items equally, without showing favoritism or bias towards any particular one.
The vaccine was distributed nonpreferentially to all residents.  疫苗被无差别地分发给所有居民。
Goods are imported nonpreferentially, meaning they are subject to the standard tariff rates.  货物是非优先进口的，这意味着它们需要缴纳标准关税。
近义词: equally, impartially, indiscriminately, uniformly
反义词: preferentially, selectively, discriminately
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not" or "opposite of"
preferential (adjective): from Latin "praeferre" (to prefer), meaning showing or giving preference.
-ly (suffix):  forming adverbs
