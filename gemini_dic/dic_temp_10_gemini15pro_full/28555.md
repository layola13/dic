noun
心动描记器
An instrument for recording the movements and force of the heart.
The cardiosphygmograph showed a significant increase in heart rate during the exercise.  心动描记器显示运动期间心率显著增加。

The doctor used a cardiosphygmograph to monitor the patient's heart function. 医生用心动描记器监测病人的心脏功能。

Synonyms: electrocardiograph, EKG, ECG
