consent:/kənˈsent/ /kənˈsɛnt/| n. vi.|同意，赞成；准许，许可|Agreement or permission to do something.|例句：She gave her consent to the marriage. 她同意了这婚事。|近义词：agreement, approval, permission|反义词：disagreement, disapproval, refusal|词性:名词, 动词

consent:/kənˈsent/ /kənˈsɛnt/| vi.|同意，赞成；准许，许可|To agree to something or give permission for something to happen.|例句：He consented to the proposal. 他同意了这项提议。|近义词：agree, approve, permit|反义词：disagree, disapprove, refuse|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French consentir, from Latin consentire ("to feel together, agree, be of the same mind"),  from com- (intensive prefix) + sentire ("to feel").
