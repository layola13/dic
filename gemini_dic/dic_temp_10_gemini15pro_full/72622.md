flyers:/ˈflaɪərz/ (US), /ˈflaɪəz/ (UK)| n.|传单；飞行物；飞行员|A printed sheet of paper containing advertising or information, usually distributed free to members of the public.|They were handing out flyers for a new restaurant. 他们在散发一家新餐馆的传单。|近义词：leaflet, handbill, circular, pamphlet|反义词: (For the "flyer" meaning "leaflet")  None.  A flyer is a physical object, so it doesn't have a direct opposite.|词性:名词 (plural of flyer)


flyer:/ˈflaɪər/ (US), /ˈflaɪə/ (UK)| n.|飞行员；飞行物；传单|Someone who flies an aircraft, or something that flies.|He's a trained flyer. 他是一个训练有素的飞行员。  The bird was a fast flyer. 这只鸟飞得很快。|近义词：(For pilot) aviator, pilot (For flying object) aircraft, bird|反义词: (For pilot) passenger  (For flying object) non-flying object|词性:名词

词源: From the verb "fly," with the suffix "-er" indicating an agent or something that performs the action.  Ultimately from Old English "fleogan."
