guicowar:/ɡiːkoʊˈwɔːr/ | n. | 吉科瓦尔; 古吉拉特邦的统治者 | A historical title for a ruler of Gujarat, particularly during the medieval period.  | The guicowar of Champaner built a magnificent fortress.  尚帕内尔的吉科瓦尔建造了一座宏伟的堡垒。 | synonym: ruler, sovereign, prince | antonym: subject, commoner | noun

etymology: From Gujarati *garvi gurjar*, meaning "proud Gurjar".  The Gurjars were a clan that ruled parts of western India. Over time, the term evolved into *guicowar*.
