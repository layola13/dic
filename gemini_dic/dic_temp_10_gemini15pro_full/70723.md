finched:/fɪntʃt/ | v. | 退缩，畏缩 | To avoid doing something because you are frightened or you do not want to |  He finched at the sight of the needle.  他看到针就畏缩了。| Synonyms: flinch, recoil, shrink, wince | Antonyms: brave, confront, face | verb

finched:/fɪntʃt/ | adj. | (rare)  捉到过的雀 | Having been caught; referring to a finch (bird). | The finched bird was carefully examined. 那只被捉住的雀被仔细检查了一下。| Synonyms: captured, caught | Antonyms: free, released | adjective
