enjoyingly:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪɪŋli/| adv.|愉快地，享受地|In a manner that gives pleasure or satisfaction.|She smiled enjoyingly as she listened to the music. 她听着音乐，脸上露出了愉悦的笑容。|
例句：He spent the afternoon enjoyingly reading in the garden. 他在花园里愉快地阅读了一下午。|近义词：pleasurably, happily, delightfully|反义词：unhappily, miserably, painfully|词性:副词
词源：From enjoy + -ing + -ly.  "Enjoy" comes from Old French "enjoir"  (to give joy to, delight).

PHRASE->
Not commonly used in phrases.  Adverbs modify verbs, and "enjoyingly" describes the *manner* of doing something. It's less common to have set phrases that dictate a specific manner of action.  It's more natural to use it directly with a verb.
