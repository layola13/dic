anti-crime:/ˌæntiˈkraɪm/| adj.|反犯罪的，打击犯罪的|Designed to prevent or reduce crime.  Relating to or directed against crime.|例句：The government implemented new anti-crime measures. 政府实施了新的打击犯罪措施。|近义词：crime-fighting, law-enforcement|反义词:pro-crime (this is rarely used and often considered inappropriate)|词性:adjective

anti-crime:/ˌæntiˈkraɪm/| n.|反犯罪|Actions or measures taken to prevent or reduce crime.|例句：Anti-crime initiatives are crucial for safe communities.  反犯罪措施对于安全的社区至关重要。|近义词：crime prevention, law enforcement|反义词:crime promotion (this is rarely used and often considered inappropriate)|词性:noun
