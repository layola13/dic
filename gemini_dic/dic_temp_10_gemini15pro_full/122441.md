mobbish:/ˈmɒbɪʃ/ (US) /ˈmɑːbɪʃ/| adj.|暴民似的，粗暴的，聚众闹事的|Characteristic of or resembling a mob; unruly and disorderly.|The mobbish behavior of the football fans after the game resulted in several arrests.  足球迷在比赛后的暴民行为导致多人被捕。|The protestors turned mobbish and started looting stores. 抗议者变成了暴民，开始洗劫商店。|近义词：rowdy, unruly, disorderly, riotous, tumultuous|反义词：orderly, peaceful, civilized, disciplined|词性:形容词
Etymology: From mob + -ish.  Mob itself comes from the Latin mobile vulgus, meaning "fickle crowd".
