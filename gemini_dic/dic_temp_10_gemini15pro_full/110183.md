lieu:/ljuː/
n.|代替；场所；位置|in lieu of something: instead of something, in place of something.  代替某物|The ceremony was held at the town hall in lieu of the cathedral.  仪式在市政厅举行，而不是在大教堂。|近义词: place, stead, position|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词
