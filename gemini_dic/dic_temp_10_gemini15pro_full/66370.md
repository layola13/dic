excuses:/ɪkˈskjuːsɪz/ /ɪkˈskjuːsəz/| n.|借口，理由；辩解|A reason or explanation given to justify a fault or offence.|He made excuses for his lateness. 他为他的迟到找借口。|近义词：justifications, apologies, pretexts, reasons, explanations|反义词: reasons, truth|Noun

excuse:/ɪkˈskjuːs/ /ɪkˈskjuːz/| vt.|原谅；为…辩解；使免除|To forgive someone for a minor fault or offence; to release someone from a duty or requirement.|Please excuse my interruption. 请原谅我的打扰。|近义词：forgive, pardon, absolve, exempt|反义词: blame, accuse|Verb
