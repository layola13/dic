epitomises:/ɪˈpɪtəmaɪzɪz/
verb
体现；是…的典型；概括
be a perfect example of something
Her work epitomises the struggle of artists against the odds. 她的作品体现了艺术家与命运抗争的精神。
Synonyms:  typifies, embodies, represents, exemplifies, symbolizes
Antonyms: misrepresents, distorts
词性: 动词
