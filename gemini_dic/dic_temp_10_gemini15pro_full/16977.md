bastardizing:/ˈbæstərdaɪzɪŋ/
v.
窜改；歪曲；降低…的格调；使…杂交；使…失去纯正性
corrupting or debased (something, typically something artistic or academic) by adding new elements or altering it in a way considered inappropriate.
The director has been accused of bastardizing the original play. 导演被指责窜改了原著戏剧。
近义词:corrupting, debasing, distorting, adulterating, degrading
反义词:preserving, purifying, refining, enhancing
词性:verb (动词)

bastardized:/ˈbæstərdaɪzd/
adj.
窜改的；歪曲的；降低格调的；杂交的；失去纯正性的
corrupted or debased (something, typically something artistic or academic) by adding new elements or altering it in a way considered inappropriate.
He presented a bastardized version of the theory. 他提出了一个被歪曲的理论版本。
近义词:corrupted, debased, distorted, adulterated, degraded
反义词:pure, authentic, genuine, unadulterated
词性:adjective (形容词)
