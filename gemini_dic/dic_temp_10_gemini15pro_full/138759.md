numerator:/ˈnjuːməˌreɪtər/ | n. | 分子 | The number above the line in a fraction, showing how many of the parts indicated by the denominator are taken. |  The numerator of the fraction 3/4 is 3.  分数 3/4 的分子是 3。 | synonym: top number (in a fraction) | antonym: denominator | noun
