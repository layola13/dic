deictics:/ˈdaɪktɪks/ | n. | 指示词 | Words or expressions that take their meaning relative to the time, place, and participants in the conversation (e.g., "here," "now," "I," "you," "this," "that").  | Example:  The word "there" is a deictic, as its meaning depends on where the speaker is.  那里是一个指示词，因为它的含义取决于说话者所在的位置。

| Example:  "This" and "that" are deictic expressions. “这个”和“那个”是指示语。 | Synonyms:  indexicals, shifters | Antonyms:  None | Noun
