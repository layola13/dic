half-title:/ˈhɑːfˌtaɪtl/ /ˈhæfˌtaɪtl/
n.
半书名页; 简略书名页
A page at the beginning of a book, usually preceding the title page, that bears only the book's title.
The half-title simply stated "The Raven." 这半书名页只写着“乌鸦”。
short title, bastard title
full title
noun
