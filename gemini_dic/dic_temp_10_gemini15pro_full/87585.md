heterosexualities:/ˌhɛtərəʊˌsɛkʃuˈælɪtiz/| n. |异性恋（性取向）|The plural form of heterosexuality, referring to the diverse expressions and experiences of attraction to the opposite sex.  It acknowledges that heterosexuality is not a monolithic entity but encompasses a range of individual and cultural variations.|Contemporary research explores the diverse heterosexualities across different cultures.|近义词：heterosexual expressions, heterosexual orientations|反义词: homosexualities, LGBTQ+ identities|词性:名词
