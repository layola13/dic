catchingly:/ˈkætʃɪŋli/| adv.|动人地；引人注目地；令人难忘地|In a way that is attractive or easily remembered.  It describes something that grabs your attention and stays with you.|Her song was catchingly melodic. 她的歌旋律动听，令人难忘。|近义词：memorably, attractively, appealingly|反义词：unremarkably, dully, boringly|词性:副词
Etymology: From "catching" + "-ly". "Catching" comes from the verb "catch," which has roots in Old North French "cachier," ultimately from Latin "capere" (to take, seize).
