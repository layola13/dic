outbulks:/ˌaʊtˈbʌlk/ | v. | 超过…的体积，在体积上超过 | To exceed in bulk; to be larger in volume than. |  The new warehouse outbulks the old one by a significant margin. 新仓库的体积比旧仓库大得多。|  The cruise ship easily outbulks the smaller yachts in the harbor.  这艘游轮的体积轻易超过了港口里的小型游艇。| 近义词: outweigh, surpass, exceed | 反义词:  be smaller than, be dwarfed by | 词性: 动词

outbulks:/ˌaʊtˈbʌlk/ | n. | (罕见)体积上的优势 |  (rare) Superiority in bulk or volume. | The sheer outbulks of the cargo ship made it difficult to maneuver.  货船庞大的体积使其难以操纵。(这个用法比较罕见，建议使用outbulking) | The outbulks of the new stadium is impressive. 新体育场的体积令人印象深刻。(这个用法比较罕见，建议使用outbulking/size/volume) | 近义词: size, volume, mass | 反义词: smallness, littleness | 词性: 名词 (罕用)


词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "bulk" (noun meaning large size or mass)
