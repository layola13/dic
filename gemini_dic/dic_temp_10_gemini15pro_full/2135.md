adaptivenesses:/əˌdæptɪvˈnɛsɪz/| n. |适应性，适应能力|The plural form of adaptiveness, referring to multiple instances or types of the ability to adjust to new conditions.|The varying adaptivenesses of these species to different environments are remarkable. 这些物种对不同环境的不同适应能力非常显著。
The study compared the adaptivenesses of several different learning algorithms.  这项研究比较了几种不同学习算法的适应性。|近义词：flexibility, plasticity, resilience|反义词：rigidity, inflexibility|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From adaptive + -ness.  Adaptive stems from adapt, from Latin *adaptare "to fit to," from ad- "to" + aptare "to fit."
