briquetting:/brɪˈkɛtɪŋ/
n.
压块，制成煤球
The process of forming fine material, such as coal dust or metal swarf, into compact blocks.
The briquetting of coal dust reduces dust pollution and makes transportation easier.  煤尘压块减少了粉尘污染，并使运输更容易。
compacting, agglomeration, pelleting
fragmentation, dispersion
noun
