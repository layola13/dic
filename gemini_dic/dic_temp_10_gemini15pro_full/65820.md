evilest: /ˈiːvɪlɪst/
adjective
最邪恶的
superlative adjective of evil; most wicked or morally bad.
He was the evilest man I'd ever met. 他是我见过的最邪恶的人。
近义词:wickedest, most villainous, most malevolent
反义词:kindest, best, most benevolent
词性:形容词

词源:
From Old English *yfelest, superlative of yfel (“evil”), from Proto-Germanic *ubilaz (“evil”). Cognate with Dutch euvel (“bad, wrong”), German übel (“evil, bad, ill”), Icelandic illr (“bad”).
