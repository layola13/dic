hesitate:/ˈhezɪteɪt/| v. |犹豫，踌躇|To pause before saying or doing something, especially because of uncertainty or nervousness.|She hesitated before answering the question. 她犹豫了一下才回答问题。|
hesitate:/ˈhezɪteɪt/| v. |不愿，不情愿|To be reluctant or unwilling to do something.|I hesitate to ask him for more money. 我不好意思再向他要钱了。|
hesitate:/ˈhezɪteɪt/| v. |停顿，迟疑|To stop briefly, especially because of uncertainty.|The engine hesitated before starting. 引擎启动前停顿了一下。|
Etymology: From Latin *haesitāre*, frequentative of haerēre "to stick, adhere, hold fast," from Proto-Indo-European *gheis-*  "to gape, be excited."|
Synonyms: pause, waver, falter, demur, vacillate|
Antonyms: decide, resolve, determine, proceed, continue|
Word type: verb
