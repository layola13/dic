chalkboards:/ˈtʃɔːkbɔːrdz/
n.
黑板
A dark, smooth surface for writing or drawing on with chalk.
The teacher wrote the equation on the chalkboard.  老师把公式写在黑板上。

近义词:blackboard, whiteboard
反义词:(无直接反义词)
词性:名词
