hulloes:/həˈloʊz/ | n. | 呼喊声，叫喊声 | A loud shout or cry, typically used to attract attention. | The sailors exchanged hulloes across the harbor. 水手们隔着港口互相呼喊。 | 近义词: shouts, cries, yells | 反义词: whispers, murmurs | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *hulloen*, *holloen*, from Anglo-Norman *huler*, from Old French *huler* (to howl), ultimately from Frankish *huwila*, from Proto-Germanic *hūlōną* (to hoot, howl).  Cognate with Dutch *huilen* (to cry, weep), German *heulen* (to howl).
