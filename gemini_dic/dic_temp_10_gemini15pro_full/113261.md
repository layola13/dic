lustrous:/ˈlʌstrəs/| adj.|有光泽的，光亮的|Having a sheen or glow; shining. |Her lustrous hair cascaded down her back. 她的光泽的头发瀑布般倾泻下来。|The car had a lustrous finish. 这辆车有着光亮的漆面。|近义词：shiny, glossy, gleaming, radiant, brilliant|反义词：dull, mat, lackluster|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *lustrouse*, from Anglo-Norman *lustrus*, from Medieval Latin *lustrōsus*, from Latin *lūster* (“light, brightness, brilliance”) + *-ōsus* (suffix forming adjectives indicating fullness or abundance).
