ineffective:/ˌɪnɪˈfɛktɪv/| adj.|无效的，无用的|Not producing any significant or desired effect.|例句：The medicine was ineffective against the virus.  这种药对病毒无效。

例句：His attempts to apologize were ineffective. 他试图道歉，但没有效果。|近义词：useless, futile, inefficient, unproductive|反义词：effective, efficient, productive|词性:形容词
Etymology:  Late 16th century: from in- (expressing negation) + effective.
