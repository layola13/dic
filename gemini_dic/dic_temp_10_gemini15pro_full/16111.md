banefully:/ˈbeɪnfəli/| adv.|有害地，恶毒地|Having a harmful or destructive effect; in a sinister way.  It describes actions or influences that are harmful and often have a sinister or evil quality. |The witch banefully cursed the village. 女巫恶毒地诅咒了村庄。|Synonyms: harmfully, destructively, malignantly, perniciously, malevolently|Antonyms: beneficially, helpfully, positively|Word type: adverb

Etymology->
From 'bane' (Old English 'bana' - destruction, killer) + '-ful' (full of) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
