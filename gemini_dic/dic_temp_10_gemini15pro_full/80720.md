gormandize:/ˈɡɔːrməndaɪz/ | v. |  暴饮暴食；狼吞虎咽 | To eat greedily or excessively; to indulge in a feast. | He gormandized on the buffet until he could barely move. 他在自助餐上暴饮暴食，直到几乎动弹不得。|  She gormandized the entire cake in one sitting. 她一口气吃掉了整个蛋糕。 | 近义词: binge, overeat, devour, stuff, pig out | 反义词: nibble, diet, fast | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *gourmandiser*, from *gourmand* ("glutton"), from Old French *gourmant*. Ultimately from Frankish **gurmant**.
