nonruminatingly:/nɒnˈruːmɪneɪtɪŋli/
adverb
非反刍地，不沉思地
Without ruminating; in a nonruminating manner.  This refers to not chewing cud (like a cow) or not pondering deeply.
The cow ate nonruminatingly due to illness. 这头牛因为生病没有反刍。
He spoke nonruminatingly, blurting out the first thing that came to mind. 他不假思索地脱口而出想到的第一件事。


近义词:thoughtlessly, unreflectively, impulsively
反义词:thoughtfully, reflectively, meditatively
词性:adverb
