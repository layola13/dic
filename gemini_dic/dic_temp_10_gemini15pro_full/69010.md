fattenable:/ˈfætənəbəl/
adjective
可肥育的; 可养肥的
Capable of being fattened.
The farmer raised fattenable pigs for market.  这位农民饲养可肥育的猪供市场销售。
These cattle are fattenable and will be ready for slaughter soon. 这些牛可以育肥，很快就可以宰杀了。
近义词:plump, fleshy,  
反义词:lean, thin
词性:adjective
