gyroscope: /ˈdʒaɪrəˌskoʊp/ (US) /ˈdʒaɪrəskəʊp/ (UK)| n.|陀螺仪，回转仪|An instrument containing a wheel mounted so that its axis can turn freely in any direction, used for measuring or maintaining orientation, based on the principles of angular momentum.|The gyroscope in the airplane helps maintain stability during flight. 飞机上的陀螺仪有助于在飞行中保持稳定。|Synonyms: gyro, gyrocompass, inertial guidance system|Antonyms: N/A|noun

gyroscopic: /ˌdʒaɪrəˈskɒpɪk/ (US) /ˌdʒaɪrəˈskɒpɪk/ (UK)| adj.|陀螺仪的，回转仪的|Relating to or characteristic of a gyroscope.|The gyroscopic effect keeps the bicycle upright. 陀螺效应使自行车保持直立。|Synonyms: rotational, spinning|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
