passivism:/ˈpæsɪvɪzəm/
n.
被动主义;消极主义;非暴力主义
The state or condition of being passive; lack of activity or resistance.
He advocated passivism as the only way to achieve peace.  他主张和平主义是实现和平的唯一途径。

Passivism is sometimes the best course of action in dangerous situations. 在危险情况下，被动有时是最好的行动方案。

近义词:inaction, inactivity, nonresistance, submissiveness
反义词:activism, aggressiveness, resistance

词性:noun

词源:From Latin passivus (“capable of suffering, liable to an action or impression from without”), from pati (“to suffer, endure, undergo, experience”).
