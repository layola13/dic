corporealism:/kɔːrˈpɔːriəˌlɪzəm/
n.
物质主义；肉体主义；唯物论
The belief that physical matter is the only reality.  The doctrine that only what is perceptible by the senses is real.
He rejected corporealism, believing in a spiritual dimension beyond the physical world. 他反对物质主义，相信在物质世界之外存在一个精神维度。
The philosopher argued against corporealism, stating that the mind is not simply a product of the brain. 这位哲学家反对唯物论， stating that the mind is not simply a product of the brain.
corporeal, materialism, physicalism
idealism, spiritualism
noun
