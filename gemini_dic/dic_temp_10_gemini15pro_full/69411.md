feel-to: /fiːl tuː/ | phrasal verb | 感到有义务去做; 倾向于; 想要 | To have an inclination or urge to do something.  It implies a less strong obligation than "have to" but more of a personal drive or desire. | I feel to tell you the truth, even though it's difficult. 我感到有义务告诉你真相，即使这很困难。|  I feel to go for a walk, the weather is so nice. 我想去散步，天气真好。 | synonyms: inclined to, disposed to, tempted to | antonyms: reluctant to, averse to | verb phrase
