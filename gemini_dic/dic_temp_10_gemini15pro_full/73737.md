forklike:/ˈfɔːrkˌlaɪk/| adj.|叉状的，像叉子的|Resembling a fork; forked.  Having the shape of a fork, with two or more prongs or branches.|The plant had forklike leaves.  这种植物的叶子是叉状的。|近义词：forked, branched, pronged|反义词:straight, unbranched|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From fork + -like.  "Fork" comes from Old English "forca," meaning "fork," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*furko," also meaning "fork." "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
