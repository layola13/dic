brocard:/ˈbrɒkɑːrd/ | n. | 格言；箴言；普遍真理 | A short, easily remembered saying of a proverbial nature; a maxim. |  Example 1:  The brocard "actions speak louder than words" is a timeless piece of wisdom.  行动胜于雄辩这句箴言是永恒的智慧。

Example 2:  Legal brocards offer concise summaries of complex legal principles.  法律格言对复杂的法律原则进行了简洁的概括。 | Synonym: maxim, proverb, adage, aphorism | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider "fallacy" or "misconception" as contrasting concepts.) | noun
