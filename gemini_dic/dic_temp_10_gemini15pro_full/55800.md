divertingly:/daɪˈvɜːrtɪŋli/
adverb
有趣地，娱乐地
in an entertaining or amusing way
The speaker divertingly recounted his travel experiences. 演讲者饶有兴致地讲述了他的旅行经历。
The children were divertingly playing in the park. 孩子们在公园里玩得很开心。
Synonyms: amusingly, entertainingly, enjoyably, pleasantly
Antonyms: boringly, dully, tediously
Word Type: Adverb

Etymology:
From divert + -ing + -ly.  Divert comes from Old French divertir "to turn aside, amuse, entertain," from Latin divertere "to turn aside, distract," from dis- "aside" + vertere "to turn."
