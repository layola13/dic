baloga: /bəˈloʊɡə/ | n. | 一种菲律宾香肠 | A type of Filipino sausage, typically made with cured pork and spices, often served at breakfast. |  Example: My favorite breakfast is baloga with garlic fried rice and a sunny-side-up egg. 我最喜欢的早餐是香肠配蒜蓉炒饭和单面煎蛋。

Example:  Street vendors often sell baloga wrapped in banana leaves. 街头小贩经常卖用香蕉叶包着的香肠。 | Synonym: sausage, longganisa (specifically Filipino sausage) | Antonym:  N/A | noun
