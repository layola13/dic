nonauthor:/ˌnɒnˈɔːθər/ | n. | 非作者；未署名者 |  A person who is not the author of a particular work.  A person who hasn't written a specific piece of writing. | The editor thanked the nonauthors for their contributions to the research. 编辑感谢了那些非作者对研究作出的贡献。

The nonauthor provided valuable feedback on the manuscript.  这位非作者对手稿提供了宝贵的反馈意见。 | near synonyms: contributor (if they contributed in a way other than writing), editor, researcher, assistant | antonym: author, writer | noun
