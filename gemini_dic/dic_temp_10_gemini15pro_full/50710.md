dermopteri:/ˌdɜːrmɒpˈtɛraɪ/
n.
皮翼目动物
Dermoptera, also known as colugos, are an order of gliding mammals.
The Sunda colugo (Galeopterus variegatus) is a dermopteran found in Southeast Asia.  巽他鼯猴（斑鼯猴）是一种分布在东南亚的皮翼目动物。
The Philippine flying lemur (Cynocephalus volans) is another species of dermopteran.  菲律宾鼯猴是皮翼目的另一种动物。
near synonyms: colugos, flying lemurs
Etymology:  From Greek derma "skin" + pteron "wing"
