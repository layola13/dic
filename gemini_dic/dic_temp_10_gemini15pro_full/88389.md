hireling:/ˈhaɪərɪŋ/| n.|雇佣兵，唯利是图的人|A person who is hired for a particular job, especially a mercenary soldier. hireling:/ˈhaɪərɪŋ/| adj.|被雇佣的，唯利是图的|Describing a person who is hired for a particular job, especially one who is motivated by personal gain. |He described the journalist's article as a piece of hireling journalism. 他将这位记者的文章描述为唯利是图的新闻报道。|Synonyms: mercenary, venal, corrupt, greedy, avaricious, grasping, acquisitive|Antonyms: altruistic, selfless, charitable, generous, benevolent|adjective
