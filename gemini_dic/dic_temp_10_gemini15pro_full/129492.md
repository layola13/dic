non-appreciation: /ˌnɒn əˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃn/ (US) /ˌnɒn əˌpriːsiˈeɪʃn/ (UK)| n.|缺乏欣赏，不重视，不理解|Lack of recognition or gratitude for something; failure to value or understand something properly.|Her non-appreciation of his efforts hurt his feelings. 她对他不屑一顾的态度伤害了他的感情。|近义词：disregard, neglect, indifference|反义词: appreciation, recognition, gratitude|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  From Latin non "not".
appreciation (noun): From Late Latin appretiationem,  from appretiare "to set a price to, value," from ad- "to" + pretium "price."
