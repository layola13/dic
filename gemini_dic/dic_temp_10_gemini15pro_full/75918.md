funest:/ˈfjuːnɪst/| adj.|最悲惨的，最不幸的，最不吉利的|Of or pertaining to death; mournful; sad; dismal; ill-omened.|例句：It was the funest day in the history of the nation. 这是这个国家历史上最悲惨的一天。|例句：The funest tidings reached the family.  最不幸的消息传到了这个家庭。|近义词：most mournful, saddest, most dismal, most tragic|反义词：happiest, most joyful, most fortunate|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *funestus (“fatal, deadly”), from funus (“funeral, death”).
