imitates:/ˈɪmɪteɪts/| v.|模仿，效仿|copies someone or something|He imitates his favorite singer perfectly. 他完美地模仿了他最喜欢的歌手。|mimics, copies, emulates|originates, innovates|verb
Etymology: From Late Latin *imītātus*, past participle of imitārī (“to imitate”), from Latin imitor, from imus (“lowest, bottommost”).
