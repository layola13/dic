coachee:/ˌkoʊˈtʃiː/| n.|受指导者，被辅导者|A person who receives coaching; a person who is being coached.|例句：The coachee made significant progress in her leadership skills.  这位受指导者在领导技能方面取得了显著进步。

例句：The coach worked with the coachee to identify their strengths and weaknesses. 教练与受指导者一起确定他们的优势和劣势。|近义词：client, student, trainee, mentee|反义词:coach, mentor|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From coach + -ee (suffix forming nouns denoting the recipient or beneficiary of an action). The word "coach" itself ultimately derives from the Hungarian town of Kocs, known for its horse-drawn carriages.
