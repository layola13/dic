eventuality:/ɪˌvɛn.tʃuˈæl.ɪ.ti/ /ɪˌven.tʃuˈæl.ə.ti/| n.|可能性；可能发生的事情；不测事件|a possible event or outcome, especially an unpleasant one|例句：We were prepared for every eventuality. 我们对各种可能发生的情况都做好了准备。|例句：We had to be ready for any eventuality. 我们必须为任何不测事件做好准备。|近义词：possibility, contingency, likelihood|反义词:impossibility, certainty|词性:noun
