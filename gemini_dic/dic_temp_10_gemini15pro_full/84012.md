half-done:/ˈhæf dʌn/
adjective
半完成的，未完成的
partially completed; not fully finished
The steak was half-done, still pink in the middle. 牛排半熟，中间还是粉红色的。
The project is half-done, with much work still to be completed.  这个项目只完成了一半，还有很多工作要做。
nearly done, partly completed
finished, completed
adjective
