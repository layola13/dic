motte:/mɒt/ | n. | 土丘，城堡主楼 | A raised earthwork forming the base of a castle. | The motte was surrounded by a deep ditch. 土丘被一条深沟包围着。| The motte offered a commanding view of the surrounding countryside.  土丘提供了周围乡村的绝佳视野。 | synonyms: mound, hill, fort, stronghold | antonyms: ditch, moat | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *motte* "clod, mound," from Vulgar Latin *mutta*, probably of Gaulish origin.
