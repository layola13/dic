crinicultural: /ˌkrɪnɪˈkʌltʃərəl/
adjective
毛发培养的; 与毛发培养有关的
Relating to the cultivation of hair.
Crinicultural techniques are being explored to treat baldness.  毛发培养技术正在被探索用于治疗秃顶。
Near synonyms: trichogenous,  hair-growing
No commonly used antonyms.
Etymology:  From Latin *crinis* (hair) + cultural (relating to cultivation).
Word Class: Adjective
