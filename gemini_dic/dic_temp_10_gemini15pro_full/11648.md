armiferous:/ɑːrˈmɪfərəs/
adjective
有纹章的；有盾形纹章的
Bearing arms or heraldic devices; entitled to bear arms.
The armiferous knight rode into battle.  这位有纹章的骑士骑马投入战斗。
近义词:heraldic, emblazoned
反义词:unarmed, plain
