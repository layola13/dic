gripless:/ˈɡrɪplɪs/| adj.|无握力的；抓不住的；不稳的|Lacking grip or the ability to grip; unable to hold on securely.|The climber found himself gripless on the smooth rock face. 登山者发现自己在光滑的岩壁上抓不住东西。|Synonyms: insecure, unsteady, slippery, precarious|Antonyms: firm, secure, steady, stable|adjective

gripless:/ˈɡrɪplɪs/| adj.|无能为力的；没有控制力的|Powerless; lacking control or influence.|He felt utterly gripless in the face of the overwhelming bureaucracy. 面对强大的官僚机构，他感到完全无能为力。|Synonyms: powerless, helpless, impotent|Antonyms: powerful, in control, influential|adjective
