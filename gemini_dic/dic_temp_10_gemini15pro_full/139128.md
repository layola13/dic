obeliscal:/ˌɒbɪˈlɪskəl/| adj.|方尖碑的；像方尖碑的|Resembling or relating to an obelisk, a tall, four-sided, tapering monument which ends in a pyramid-like shape at the top.|The museum's obeliskal entrance was quite imposing.  博物馆像方尖碑一样的入口十分宏伟。|Near synonyms: towering, monumental, tapering, pointed|Antonyms: short, squat, rounded|adjective
