ester:/ˈɛstər/| n. |酯|An organic compound made by replacing the hydrogen of an acid by an alkyl or other organic group. Many naturally occurring fats and essential oils are esters of fatty acids.| Ethyl acetate is a common ester used as a solvent. 乙酸乙酯是一种常用的酯类溶剂。|
Methyl salicylate is an ester with a minty smell. 水杨酸甲酯是一种有薄荷味的酯。|近义词: (for specific esters) acetate, formate, etc.|反义词:  (Generally no direct antonym for the chemical class "ester")|noun
