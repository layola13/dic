bugout:/ˈbʌɡaʊt/ | n. | 紧急撤退，逃亡 | A sudden, hasty departure, especially in response to an emergency or threat. |  Example 1: The family made a bugout when the wildfire approached their home.  这个家庭在野火逼近他们的家时紧急撤离了。

Example 2:  They had a bugout bag ready in case of a hurricane.  他们准备了一个紧急逃生包，以防飓风来袭。 | 近义词: evacuation, escape, flight | 反义词: arrival, return | 词性: 名词
