chase:/tʃeɪs/| v. n.|追逐，追赶；追求|To quickly follow someone or something in order to catch them.|The police chased the thief through the streets. 警察在街上追赶小偷。|近义词：pursue, follow, hunt|反义词：flee, escape|词性:动词,名词

chase:/tʃeɪs/| n.|追逐，追赶；追求|The act of pursuing someone or something.|The chase was long and tiring. 追逐漫长而累人。|近义词：pursuit, hunt|反义词：flight, escape|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *chacier* (Modern French *chasser*), from Vulgar Latin *captiāre*, frequentative of Latin *capere* "to take, seize".
