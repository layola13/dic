gemote:/jəˈmoʊt/ | n. | 集会，会议; (古)地方议会 | An assembly or meeting, especially a local council in Anglo-Saxon England. | The gemote decided on the new taxes.  集会决定了新的税收。| synonyms: assembly, meeting, council, gathering, congress, convention | antonyms: dispersal, separation, disunity | noun


gemote:/jəˈmoʊt/ | n. | (古)地方议会 | A local council in Anglo-Saxon England. |  The shire gemote met twice a year.  郡议会每年举行两次会议。| synonyms: council, assembly, moot | antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *gemōt, from Proto-Germanic *gamotą (“meeting”), from *ga- (“together”) + *motaną (“to meet”).  Cognate with Dutch gemeent (“municipality”), German Gemeinde (“community, municipality”).
