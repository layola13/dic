demonstrations:/ˌdemənˈstreɪʃənz/
|n.|游行示威，演示，证明|A public display of group opinion, often a march or rally; a practical exhibition and explanation of how something works or is done.|例句：The demonstrations against the new law continued for weeks.  针对新法律的示威活动持续了数周。

例句：The salesperson gave a demonstration of the new vacuum cleaner.  销售员演示了新的吸尘器。|近义词：protests, rallies, exhibitions, displays|反义词：suppression, concealment|词性:名词

demonstration:/ˌdemənˈstreɪʃən/
|n.|游行示威，演示，证明|A public display of group opinion, often a march or rally; a practical exhibition and explanation of how something works or is done.|例句：The teacher gave a demonstration of the experiment. 老师演示了这个实验。

例句：There was a large demonstration in the city center. 市中心有一场大规模的示威活动。|近义词：protest, rally, exhibition, display|反义词：suppression, concealment|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēmonstrātiōn- (stem of dēmonstrātiō), from Latin dēmonstrō (“I point out, show, demonstrate”), from dē (“from, down from, away”) + mōnstrō (“I show”).
