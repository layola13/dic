blister:/ˈblɪstər/| n. v.|水疱，气泡；使起水疱|A small bubble on the skin filled with serum and caused by friction, burning, or other damage.|例句：I got a blister on my heel from my new shoes. 我的新鞋把我的脚后跟磨出了水疱。|近义词：sore, bleb, vesicle|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|noun


blister:/ˈblɪstər/ |v. |起水疱，(使)起泡|To cause blisters to form on.|例句：The hot sun blistered the paint. 烈日使油漆起了泡。|近义词：scald, burn|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *blister*, *blester*, from Old Norse *blystr*, from Proto-Germanic *blustrō* (“swelling, boil”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰlew- (“to blow, swell”). Cognate with Icelandic *blystr* (“blister”), Swedish *blåsa* (“bladder, blister”).
