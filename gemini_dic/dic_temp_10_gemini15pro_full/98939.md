inter-applications:/ˌɪntərˌæplɪˈkeɪʃənz/| adj.| 应用程序间的，跨应用程序的 |Describing something that involves or relates to communication or interaction between different applications or software programs. It signifies the ability of different applications to work together or exchange information.|Example: The inter-applications communication protocol allows data to be seamlessly transferred between different software.  应用程序间通信协议允许数据在不同软件之间无缝传输。

Example: Inter-applications data sharing is crucial for a smooth user experience. 跨应用程序数据共享对于流畅的用户体验至关重要。|近义词: cross-application, application-to-application |反义词: intra-application, within-application |词性: adjective
