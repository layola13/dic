clarinet:/ˌklærəˈnɛt/ (US) /ˈklærɪnɛt/ (UK)| n.|单簧管；黑管|A single-reed woodwind instrument with a cylindrical tube and a flared bell.|Mozart wrote many concertos for the clarinet.|Synonyms:  reed instrument, woodwind|Antonyms: None|Noun


clarionet:/ˌklærəˈnɛt/ (US) /ˈklærɪnɛt/ (UK)| n.|单簧管；黑管|An older, less common spelling of "clarinet".  It refers to the same single-reed woodwind instrument.|The clarionet solo was particularly moving.|Synonyms: clarinet, reed instrument, woodwind|Antonyms: None|Noun
