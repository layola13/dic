embroidery:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdəri/ | n. | 刺绣 | The art or process of decorating fabric or other materials with needlework.  The product of such needlework.  Something resembling embroidery in richness or variety. | The tablecloth was adorned with delicate embroidery.  这桌布上装饰着精致的刺绣。 | 近义词: needlework, needlepoint, cross-stitch | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源:
From Middle English *embroudrie*, from Anglo-Norman *embrouderie*, from Old French *embroidure*, from *embrouder* ("to embroider"), from *en-* ("in, on") + *brouder* ("to prick, spur"), ultimately from Frankish *brozd*, related to Old High German *brozda*.
