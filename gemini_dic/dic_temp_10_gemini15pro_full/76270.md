fuzzy:/ˈfʌzi/ | adj. | 模糊的，绒毛的，失真的 | covered with soft short hairs or fibers; not clear or distinct | The picture was fuzzy.  照片很模糊。 |  近义词：blurred, indistinct, vague, downy, fluffy | 反义词：clear, sharp, distinct, smooth | 词性: 形容词

fuzzy:/ˈfʌzi/ | n. | 模糊不清的事物 | something that is fuzzy |  My memories of that night are a bit fuzzy. 我对那天晚上的记忆有点模糊。 | 近义词：blur | 反义词： clarity | 词性: 名词
