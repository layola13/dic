dopily:/ˈdoʊpɪli/| adv.| 愚蠢地，呆呆地 | In a stupid or foolish manner, often due to being under the influence of drugs or alcohol. | He grinned dopily at the camera. 他对着镜头傻笑着。| 近义词：stupidly, foolishly, vacantly, blankly | 反义词：intelligently, cleverly, sharply | 词性: 副词

词源:  From dope (meaning a drug or narcotic) + -ily (adverbial suffix).
