bait:/beɪt/| n. vt.|诱饵，饵；引诱|Something used to attract or lure, especially animals or fish to a trap or hook.  Also, to furnish with bait or to lure.|The fisherman baited his hook with a worm. 这位渔夫用蠕虫作钩上的诱饵。|
He baited the trap with cheese. 他用奶酪作陷阱的诱饵。|synonyms: lure, decoy, enticement, trap|antonyms: deterrent, repellent|noun, verb
