bowe:/boʊ/ | n. | bow, bend, curve | A curve or bend, especially in a river or road; an act of bending the head or upper body as a sign of respect or greeting. | The river takes a sharp bowe near the town. 这条河在小镇附近有一个急弯。| 近义词: bend, curve, arc | 反义词: straight | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle English *bowe, from Old English *boga, from Proto-Germanic *bugō. Cognate with Old Norse bogi (“bow”), Old High German bogo (“bow, arc”). Related to bow (weapon) and bough (tree branch).
