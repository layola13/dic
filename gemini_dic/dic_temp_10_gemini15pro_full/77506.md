gazin: /ˈɡeɪzɪn/
gazin | n. | 注视，凝视 |  A prolonged intent look.  Derived from "gaze" + "-ing" (present participle suffix), suggesting the act of gazing. |  She stood gazin at the beautiful sunset. 她站在那里凝视着美丽的日落。| 近义词: staring, gazing, peering | 反义词: glancing, ignoring | 词性: 名词 (derived from verb)
