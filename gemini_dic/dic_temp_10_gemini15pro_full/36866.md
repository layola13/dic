coloradans:/ˌkɒləˈrædənz/ /ˌkɑːləˈrædənz/| n.|科罗拉多州人；科罗拉多人|People who live in or are from the state of Colorado in the United States.  Residents of Colorado.|Many Coloradans enjoy skiing in the Rocky Mountains. 许多科罗拉多人喜欢在落基山脉滑雪。|Residents of Colorado, people from Colorado|N/A|noun
词源:  Derived from "Colorado" + "-ans" (suffix indicating inhabitants of a place).  "Colorado" itself comes from the Spanish word "colorado," meaning "colored red," referring to the reddish-brown color of the Colorado River.
