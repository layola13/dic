crunk:/krʌŋk/
adjective
疯狂的，热闹的，充满活力的
Very excited and full of energy, especially because of loud music and dancing.  Originating in the Southern US hip-hop scene.
例句：The club was crunk last night. 昨晚俱乐部气氛非常High.

例句：The DJ played some crunk music to get the party started. DJ播放了一些充满活力的音乐来启动派对。

近义词：wild, lively, energetic, hyped, amped
反义词：calm, subdued, mellow
词性：adjective
