evene: /ˈiːvɛn/ |adj.| 平滑的，均匀的；平坦的；平静的；偶数的；相等的；持平的；扯平的| smooth, uniform, flat; calm; divisible by two; equal; level; square |  The road was even and easy to drive on. 路很平坦，容易驾驶。| 近义词: smooth, level, flat, uniform, equal, balanced | 反义词: rough, uneven, bumpy, irregular, unequal, unbalanced | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Old French *evene (modern French uni), from Latin aequus (“equal, level”).
