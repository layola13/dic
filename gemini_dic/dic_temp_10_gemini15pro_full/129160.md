no-one:/ˈnoʊ wʌn/ | pron. | 没有人 |  No person; not a single person.  A more emphatic form of *nobody*. | No-one came to the party. 没有人来参加聚会。 | No-one answered the phone when I called.  我打电话时没有人接听。 | 近义词: nobody, not anyone | 反义词: everyone, everybody | 词性: 代词

词源: From *no one*, literally "no one person."  The spelling *no-one* is more common in British English.
