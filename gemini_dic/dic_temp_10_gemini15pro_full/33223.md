chronogram: /ˈkrɒnəɡræm/ (UK) /ˈkrɑːnəɡræm/ (US) | n. | 年表铭文；年代诗 | An inscription or other piece of writing in which certain letters, when interpreted as Roman numerals, express a date. | The chronogram on the building's cornerstone indicated the year of its construction.  这栋建筑基石上的年表铭文标明了它的建造年份。 | synonym: inscription, dated inscription | antonym: N/A | noun

chronogram: /ˈkrɒnəɡræm/ (UK) /ˈkrɑːnəɡræm/ (US) | n. | 年代诗 | A poem or other form of writing in which specific letters, usually capitalized, can be interpreted as Roman numerals to reveal a date or year that is significant in terms of the subject matter.  | He composed a chronogram to commemorate the centenary of the town's founding. 他创作了一首年代诗来纪念该镇成立一百周年。| synonym:  chronostich | antonym: N/A | noun
