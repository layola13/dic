all-the-same:/ɔːl ðə seɪm/
adverb
仍然；尽管如此；依然
nevertheless; in spite of this; yet
例句：He's not very reliable, but I like him all the same. 他不太可靠，但我仍然喜欢他。

例句：Although she was tired, she went to the party all the same. 虽然她很累，但她还是去了派对。

近义词：nevertheless, nonetheless, yet, still, however, notwithstanding
反义词：therefore, consequently
词性：副词
