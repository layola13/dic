margaritic:/ˌmɑːrɡəˈrɪtɪk/
adj.

Resembling a pearl; pearly.
The chemist created a margaritic substance for the new paint.  化学家为新油漆创造了一种珍珠状的物质。
The inside of the shell had a margaritic sheen. 贝壳内部有珍珠般的光泽。
近义词:pearly, nacreous, iridescent
反义词:dull, matte
词性:adjective
