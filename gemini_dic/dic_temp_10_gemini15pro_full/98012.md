inoperculate:/ɪnɒˈpɜːkjʊlət/ | adj. | 无盖的，无厣的 | Lacking an operculum; having an opening that is not covered by a lid or flap.  Specifically used in biology to describe organisms, especially mollusks or spores, that do not have a covering or lid. | The inoperculate snails are a diverse group. 无盖蜗牛是一个种类繁多的群体。| The fungal spores were observed to be inoperculate. 观察到真菌孢子是无盖的。 |Synonyms: lidless, uncovered, open | Antonyms: operculate, covered, closed | adjective
