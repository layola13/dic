nonmutability:/ˌnɑːnˌmjuːtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可变性 | The state or quality of not being mutable; immutability.  The inability to be changed after creation. |  The nonmutability of strings in Python can lead to performance improvements. Python中字符串的不可变性可以提高性能。 | immutability, unchangeability, invariance | mutability, changeability, alterability | noun
