beslave:/bɪˈsleɪv/
verb
奴役，使沦为奴隶
To make someone a slave; to enslave.
The conquerors beslaved the native population. 征服者奴役了当地居民。

He was beslaved by his desires. 他被自己的欲望所奴役。

近义词: enslave, subjugate, oppress
反义词: liberate, free, emancipate
词性: verb
