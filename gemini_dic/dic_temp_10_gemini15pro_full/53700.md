disclaiming:/dɪsˈkleɪmɪŋ/
verb
否认；放弃；拒绝承认
The company disclaimed all responsibility for the accident.  该公司否认对这起事故负有任何责任。
present participle of disclaim
放弃；拒绝承认；否认
He is disclaiming any knowledge of the crime. 他否认对这起犯罪有任何了解。
近义词: denying, renouncing, repudiating
反义词: acknowledging, admitting, claiming, confessing
词性: verb (present participle)


词源:
From late Middle English *disclamen, from Anglo-Norman desclamer, from Old French desclamer, from des- (expressing reversal) + clamer (to claim).
