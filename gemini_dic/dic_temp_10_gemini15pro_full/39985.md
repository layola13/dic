consulates:/ˈkɒnsjʊləts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsələts/ (US)| n. |领事馆|A consulate is an office that represents a country in a foreign city. It handles official matters and provides assistance to citizens of the represented country.|The US consulate in London issued new passports. 美国驻伦敦领事馆签发了新护照。|The French consulate assisted its citizens after the earthquake. 法国领事馆在地震后向其公民提供了援助。|Synonyms: embassy, diplomatic mission|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
