paleographer:/pəˈliːɒɡrəfər/ (US) /peɪˈliːɑːɡrəfər/ (UK)| n.|古文书学家；古文字学家|A scholar who studies ancient writing systems and deciphers historical manuscripts.|例句：The paleographer carefully examined the ancient scroll.  古文书学家仔细检查了古代卷轴。

例句：The paleographer was able to date the manuscript to the 12th century.  这位古文书学家能够将这份手稿的年代确定为12世纪。|近义词：scrivener, calligrapher, epigrapher|反义词: None|词性: Noun
