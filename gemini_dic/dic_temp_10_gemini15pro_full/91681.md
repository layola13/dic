hypercoagulability:/ˌhaɪpərkoʊˌæɡjələˈbɪləti/
n.
高凝状态，高凝性
A condition in which the blood has an increased tendency to clot.
Patients with cancer have a higher risk of hypercoagulability. 癌症患者有更高的血栓形成风险。
thrombophilia, increased coagulability
hypocoagulability
noun
