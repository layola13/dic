editorializing:/ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
n.|编辑评论;发表社论|The act of expressing opinions in an editorial, often in a way that is considered biased or intrusive.|His constant editorializing annoyed his colleagues. 他不断发表评论惹恼了他的同事。|commenting, opining| reporting, neutrality
词性: 名词 (gerund)

editorialize:/ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəˌlaɪz/
v.|发表社论;作评论|To express opinions in an editorial, often in a way that is considered biased or intrusive.|The news anchor was criticized for editorializing during the report.  新闻主播因在报道中发表评论而受到批评。|comment, opine|report, abstain
词性: 动词

词源: From "editorial" + "-ize," a suffix used to form verbs meaning "to make or become," "to subject to," or "to treat with." "Editorial" comes from "editor," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "ēditor," meaning "publisher."
