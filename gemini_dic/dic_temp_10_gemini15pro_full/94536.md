impossible: /ɪmˈpɒsəbl/ (UK) /ɪmˈpɑːsəbl/ (US) | adj. |不可能的，办不到的| not able to occur, exist, or be done | It is impossible to predict the future. 预测未来是不可能的。| It's impossible to finish this task in one day. 在一天内完成这项任务是不可能的。|近义词：unachievable, infeasible, unattainable|反义词：possible, feasible, achievable|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Old French *impossible (Modern French impossible), from Latin impossibilis (“not possible”), from in- (“not”) + possibilis (“possible”).
