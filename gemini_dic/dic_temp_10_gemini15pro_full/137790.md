nonvenomous:/ˌnɒnˈvenəməs/ | adj. |无毒的|Not venomous; not producing venom.|例句：The corn snake is a nonvenomous snake and makes a good pet. 玉米蛇是一种无毒的蛇，是很好的宠物。|例句：Many snakes are nonvenomous and pose no threat to humans. 许多蛇是无毒的，对人类没有威胁。|近义词：harmless, innocuous|反义词:venomous, poisonous|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- + venomous.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "venomous" comes from the Old French "venimeux," meaning "poisonous." Ultimately, it derives from the Latin "venenum," meaning "poison."
