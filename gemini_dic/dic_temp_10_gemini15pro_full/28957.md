carry-over:/ˈkæriˌoʊvər/ (US), /ˈkæriˌəʊvə(r)/ (UK)| n. adj.|结转，延滞，遗留物|Something that continues or is carried over from one time or situation to another.|The tax loss carry-over will reduce our tax burden next year. 这项税损结转将减少我们明年的税负。|The sense of unease from yesterday was a carry-over into today. 昨天的不安感延续到了今天。|synonyms: transfer, continuation, balance, surplus|antonyms: discontinuation, interruption|noun
