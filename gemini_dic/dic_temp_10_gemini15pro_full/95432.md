One could consider "composter" or "recycler" as antonymic processes.)|noun


incinerators: /ɪnˈsɪnəreɪtərz/ | n. |焚化炉（复数）|Multiple furnaces used for burning waste.|Several incinerators are in operation across the country. 全国有几座焚化炉在运行。|Synonym: burners, furnaces, destructors|Antonym: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Latin "incinerare," meaning "to reduce to ashes," from "in" (into) + "cinis" (ashes).
