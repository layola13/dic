fumigator:/ˈfjuːmɪɡeɪtər/ | n. |熏蒸员，熏蒸器 |A person or device that uses fumes to disinfect or kill pests. |The fumigator treated the house for termites. 熏蒸员对房子进行了白蚁处理。|fumigation device, exterminator |  | noun
fumigate:/ˈfjuːmɪɡeɪt/ | v. |熏蒸，以烟熏消毒 |To expose to fumes, especially for the purpose of disinfecting or killing pests. |They fumigated the building to get rid of the bedbugs.  他们熏蒸了大楼以清除臭虫。|disinfect, sterilize, exterminate | ventilate, aerate | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fumigare* "to smoke, fumigate," from *fumus* "smoke."
