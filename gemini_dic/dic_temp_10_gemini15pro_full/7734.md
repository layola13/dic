anophyte:/ˈænəˌfaɪt/
n.
一年生植物
a plant that completes its life cycle, from germination to the production of seed, within one year, and then dies.
The anophyte quickly sprouted, flowered, and produced seeds before the first frost. 这种一年生植物在初霜之前迅速发芽、开花和结籽。
annual, ephemeral
perennial
noun
