gemminess:/ˈdʒɛmɪnɪs/
n.
宝石般的光辉; 宝石的性质
The quality of resembling a gem, especially in brilliance or richness.
The gemminess of the deep blue sea captivated her. 深蓝色大海宝石般的光辉深深地吸引了她。
近义词：brilliance, radiance, luminosity, sparkle
反义词：dullness, opacity
