nought:/nɔːt/| n.|零；无；没有东西|A quantity of zero; nothing.|例句：He inherited nought but debts. 他只继承了债务。|近义词：zero, nothing, nil, aught|反义词: something, anything|名词

nought:/nɔːt/| adj.|无价值的；无用的|Of no value or importance.|例句：All his efforts were for nought. 他所有的努力都白费了。|近义词：worthless, useless|反义词: valuable, useful|形容词

词源: From Old English *nāwiht, *nōwiht (“nothing”), from *ne (“not”) + *āwiht, *ōwiht (“anything”). Cognate with Scots nocht, nowt (“nothing”), West Frisian neat (“nothing”), Dutch niets (“nothing”), German nichts (“nothing”). Related to naught.


注意事项: nought 虽然拼写不同，但和naught是同义词，可以互换使用。美式英语中naught更常见。
