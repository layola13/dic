fornix:/ˈfɔːrnɪks/ /ˈfɔːrnɪks/| n.|穹窿，拱形，(尤指大脑中的)穹窿|An arched structure or vault, especially the arching fibrous band connecting the hippocampus with the hypothalamus in the brain.|The fornix is a crucial part of the limbic system, involved in memory formation.|近义词：arch, vault|反义词:|noun
