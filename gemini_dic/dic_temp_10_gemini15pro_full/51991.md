diaglyphtic:/ˌdaɪɡlɪfˈtɪk/ | adj. | 雕刻的，阴刻的 |  Relating to or characterized by carving or engraving, especially intaglio.  Engraved, especially as a form of decoration. | The diaglyphtic gems were highly prized by collectors. 这些雕刻的宝石被收藏家们高度珍视。| The artist specialized in diaglyphtic techniques, creating intricate designs on precious stones. 这位艺术家擅长雕刻技术，在宝石上创作复杂的图案。 | 近义词: engraved, carved, incised | 反义词: embossed, raised | 词性: adjective
