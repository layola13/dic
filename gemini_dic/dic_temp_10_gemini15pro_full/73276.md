forebears:/ˈfɔːrˌbɛərz/ | n. |祖先，祖辈|Ancestors, especially those more remote than grandparents.|My forebears came to this country in the 19th century. 我的祖先在19世纪来到这个国家。|近义词：ancestors, forefathers, progenitors|反义词：descendants, offspring|词性: 名词

Etymology: From Middle English *foreberes*, from *fore* ("before") + *beran* ("to bear, carry").  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *forą* ("before") + *beraną* ("to bear"). Cognate with Old English *forebora* ("ancestor").
