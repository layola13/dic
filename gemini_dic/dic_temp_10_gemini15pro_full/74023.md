forwarding:/ˈfɔːrwərdɪŋ/ /ˈfɔːrwərdiŋ/
n.|转发，发送，转运|The act of sending something on to another destination.|例句：The forwarding of your mail to your new address is complete. 你的邮件已转发到你的新地址。|例句：The company handles the forwarding of goods overseas.  这家公司处理海外货物转运。|近义词：dispatch, shipment, transmission|反义词:receiving, holding|词性:名词

forwarding:/ˈfɔːrwərdɪŋ/ /ˈfɔːrwərdiŋ/
adj.|转发的，运输的|Relating to the process of sending something on.|例句：The forwarding address is incorrect. 转寄地址不正确。|例句：Forwarding charges will apply. 将收取转运费。|近义词：shipping, transmitting|反义词:receiving|词性:形容词
