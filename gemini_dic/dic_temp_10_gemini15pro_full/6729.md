Etymology->
From analyze + -ability.  Analyze comes from French *analyser*, from *analyse* "analysis," borrowed from Modern Latin *analysis*, from Greek *analysis* "a breaking up," from *analyein* "to unloose, undo," from *ana-* "up, throughout" + *lyein* "to loose."
