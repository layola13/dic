instigates:/ˈɪnstɪɡeɪts/
v.
煽动；唆使；激起
urges or encourages someone to do something, especially something that is wrong or illegal.
The government accused the opposition party of instigating the riots. 政府指责反对党煽动骚乱。
Synonyms: incite, provoke, foment, abet, goad
Antonyms: deter, discourage, prevent, suppress
词性: 动词


instigate:/ˈɪnstɪɡeɪts/
v.
煽动；唆使；激起
bring about or initiate (an action or event).
He instigated a refurbishment of the old house. 他着手翻新那栋老房子。
Synonyms: initiate, start, begin, launch, commence
Antonyms: stop, halt, end, finish, terminate
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin instigare "to incite, urge on," from in- "in" + stigare "to prick, goad."
