danceable:/ˈdænsəbl/ (US), /ˈdɑːnsəbl/ (UK)| adj.|可跳舞的，适合跳舞的|Suitable for dancing; easy to dance to.|The music was very danceable, and everyone was having a good time.  这音乐很适合跳舞，每个人都玩得很开心。

The band played a danceable tune. 乐队演奏了一首适合跳舞的曲子。

|近义词：rhythmic, groovy, catchy|反义词:undanceable, awkward|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dance + -able.  "Dance" comes from Old French "dancier." "-able" is a suffix meaning "capable of being."
