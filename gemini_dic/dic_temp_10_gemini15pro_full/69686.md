fenestra:/fɪˈnɛstrə/| n. |窗，孔，开口|An opening, especially in a bone or in the covering of a plant, such as a leaf.|The surgeon located the fenestra in the skull.  外科医生找到了头骨上的孔。|Synonyms: aperture, opening, orifice, window|Antonyms: closure, blockage|noun

fenestra:/fɪˈnɛstrə/| n. |(解剖)窗，小孔|A small opening, especially in bone or a membrane covering an internal organ.|The fenestra ovalis is a membrane-covered opening that leads from the middle ear to the inner ear. 卵圆窗是由膜覆盖的开口，从耳朵中部通向内耳。|Synonyms: aperture, orifice, hole|Antonyms: closure|noun
