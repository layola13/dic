overdebate:/ˌoʊvərdiˈbeɪt/ | v. | 过度辩论，过度讨论 | To debate or discuss something excessively, often to the point of being unproductive. | The issue had been overdebated, and everyone was tired of talking about it.  这个问题已经被过度讨论了，每个人都厌倦了谈论它。| They overdebated the merits of the new policy, ultimately reaching no consensus. 他们过度辩论了新政策的优点，最终没有达成共识。 |  近义词: overdiscuss, overanalyze | 反义词: underdiscuss | 词性: 动词
