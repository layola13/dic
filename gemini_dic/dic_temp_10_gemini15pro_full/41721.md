corrode:/kəˈroʊd/ (US), /kəˈrəʊd/ (UK)| v. |腐蚀，侵蚀|to destroy or damage slowly by chemical action|Acid corrodes metal. 酸腐蚀金属。|The salt water corroded the pipes. 盐水腐蚀了管道。|近义词：erode, rust, decay, wear away|反义词:preserve, protect, strengthen|词性:动词

corrode:/kəˈroʊd/ (US), /kəˈrəʊd/ (UK)| v. |(使)恶化，(使)败坏|(of a feeling or quality) to gradually make something weaker or worse|His confidence was corroded by repeated failures.  他因屡次失败而自信心受损。|Jealousy corroded their friendship. 嫉妒破坏了他们的友谊。|近义词：undermine, weaken, impair, damage|反义词:improve, enhance, strengthen|词性:动词
