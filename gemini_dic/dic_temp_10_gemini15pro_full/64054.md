epitomise:/ɪˈpɪtəmaɪz/ (UK) /ɪˈpɪtəˌmaɪz/ (US)| v. | 体现；是…的典型；概括 | to be a perfect example of something; to be a typical example of a quality or type of thing |  Her work epitomises the best of modern British art. 她的作品是现代英国艺术的最佳体现。

The athlete epitomises the spirit of sportsmanship.  这位运动员体现了体育精神。

近义词: exemplify, represent, embody, typify | 反义词: contradict, misrepresent | 词性: verb
