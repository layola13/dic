dismemberer:/dɪsˈmɛmbərər/ | n. |肢解者，分割者|A person who dismembers something, especially a body.|The dismemberer was apprehended by the police.  肢解者被警方逮捕了。|Synonyms: mutilator, butcher, dissector |Antonyms: assembler, constructor |noun

dismember:/dɪsˈmɛmbər/ | vt. |肢解，分割，拆散|To cut or tear (a body) into pieces; to take apart.|The gruesome crime scene showed a dismembered body.  可怕的犯罪现场显示一具被肢解的尸体。| The company was dismembered and sold off in parts. 公司被拆分并出售。|Synonyms: dissect, mutilate, dismantle |Antonyms: assemble, construct, unite |verb
