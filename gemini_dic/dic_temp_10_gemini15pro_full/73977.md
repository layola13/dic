fortitudes:/ˈfɔːrtɪtjuːdz/
n.
勇气，坚韧，刚毅
Mental and emotional strength in facing difficulty, adversity, danger, or pain courageously.
She showed great fortitude in the face of adversity.  她在逆境中表现出了极大的毅力。
近义词：courage, bravery, resilience, resolve, strength, tenacity
反义词：fear, cowardice, weakness, timidity
词性: 名词 (plural)


Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Old French fortitude, from Latin fortitudo, from fortis ‘strong’.
