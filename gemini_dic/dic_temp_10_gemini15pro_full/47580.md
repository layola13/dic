debasement:/dɪˈbeɪsmənt/ | n. | 降低，贬低；堕落 |  The act of reducing something in quality, value, or character; degradation. | The debasement of the currency caused widespread economic hardship. 通货贬值导致了普遍的经济困难。 | degradation, corruption, deterioration, decline, devaluation | elevation, improvement, enhancement | noun


debase:/dɪˈbeɪs/ | vt. | 降低，贬低；掺杂 | To reduce something in quality, value, or character. |  They debased themselves by accepting bribes. 他们接受贿赂，自甘堕落。| degrade, corrupt, demean, cheapen, adulterate | elevate, enhance, improve, ennoble, purify | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *debaser, from de- "down" + baser "to make low," ultimately from Late Latin bassus "low."
