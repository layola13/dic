nonpersecuting:/ˌnɑːnpɜːrsɪˈkjuːtɪŋ/
| adj. | 不迫害的，宽容的 | Not persecuting; tolerant. |  The government adopted a nonpersecuting policy towards religious minorities. 政府对宗教少数群体采取了不迫害的政策。|  The new regime promised a nonpersecuting approach to dissenters. 新政权承诺对持不同政见者采取不迫害的方式。 | 近义词: tolerant, accepting, liberal, forgiving | 反义词: persecuting, oppressive, intolerant, bigoted | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
persecuting (verb, present participle): from Latin persecutus, past participle of persequi "to follow persistently, pursue, hunt down, prosecute," from per- "thoroughly" + sequi "to follow."
