neckmould: /ˈnɛkˌmoʊld/ | n. |颈圈，颈托，颈模| A device used to support or immobilize the neck, often used after injury or surgery.  It can be made of various materials such as foam, plastic, or metal. |  The doctor prescribed a neckmould after her whiplash injury. 医生在她颈部扭伤后给她开了颈托。 | synonyms: neck brace, cervical collar, neck support | antonyms: N/A | noun
