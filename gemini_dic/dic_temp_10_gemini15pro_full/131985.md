nonconvertibles: /ˌnɑːnkənˈvɜːrtəblz/ | n. | 不可兑换证券；不可转换证券 | Securities, typically bonds or preferred stock, that cannot be converted into another form of security, such as common stock. |  Many investors prefer nonconvertibles because they offer a fixed income stream. 许多投资者更喜欢不可转换证券，因为它们提供固定的收入流。

The company issued nonconvertibles to raise capital. 这家公司发行不可转换证券来筹集资金。 | 近义词: non-exchangeable, non-transferable | 反义词: convertibles | 词性: 名词(plural)
