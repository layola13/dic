nonsolidified:/nɒnˈsɒlɪdɪfaɪd/ /nɑːnˈsɑːlɪdɪfaɪd/
adjective
未固化的，非固体的
Not having solidified; remaining in a liquid or gaseous state.
The nonsolidified lava flowed slowly down the volcano's side.  未固化的熔岩沿着火山侧面缓缓流下。
The mixture remained nonsolidified even after being cooled. 即使冷却后，混合物仍然未固化。
近义词:liquid, fluid, molten, unsolidified
反义词:solid, solidified, hardened
词性:adjective


词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"
solidify (verb): from Latin solidus "solid" + -ify (suffix meaning "to make or become")
