chicky:/ˈtʃɪki/| adj.|小鸡的；胆小的；娇气的|Resembling or characteristic of a chick, especially in being small, delicate, or timid.  Also used to describe something stylish or fashionable, particularly in a retro or vintage way.|例句：She wore a chicky little vintage dress. 她穿了一件时髦的复古小裙子。

例句：He's a bit chicky about going into the dark. 他有点害怕进黑暗的地方。|近义词：chicken, timid, stylish, fashionable|反义词：brave, bold, unfashionable|词性:形容词

chicky:/ˈtʃɪki/| n.|小鸡|A young chicken.|例句：The farmer brought home a basket of chickies. 农民带回家一篮子小鸡。

例句：Look at those fluffy little chickies! 看看那些毛茸茸的小鸡！|近义词：chick, hen (female), rooster/cock (male)|反义词： (No direct opposite, perhaps adult chicken/fowl)|词性:名词
