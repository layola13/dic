bismuth:/ˈbɪzməθ/ (US), /ˈbɪsməθ/ (UK)| n.|铋|A brittle, crystalline, white metal with a pinkish tinge, used in alloys and medicine. It is a chemical element with the symbol Bi and atomic number 83.|Bismuth is used in some medications to treat upset stomachs. 铋被用于一些治疗胃部不适的药物中。

Bismuth has a low melting point. 铋的熔点很低。|近义词:None (as it's an element)|反义词:None (as it's an element)|词性:noun
