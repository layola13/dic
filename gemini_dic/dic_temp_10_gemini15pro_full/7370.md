animadvert:/ˌænɪmædˈvɜːrt/
verb
批评；非难；苛责
To criticize or censure severely; to comment unfavorably upon.
The critic animadverted on the poor quality of the acting.  评论家严厉批评了糟糕的演技。
The teacher animadverted on the student's laziness. 老师批评了学生的懒惰。
近义词：criticize, censure, denounce, condemn, reprehend
反义词：praise, commend, laud, extol
词性:v.

词源:
From Latin animadvertere ("to turn one's mind to, consider, notice, perceive, remark upon"), from animus ("mind, spirit, courage, passion, feeling") + advertere ("to turn to"), from ad- ("to, toward") + vertere ("to turn").
