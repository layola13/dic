liveryman:/ˈlɪvərɪmən/| n. |送货员，(伦敦)同业公会会员|A member of a livery company in the City of London, originally a freeman of the city entitled to wear its livery, or uniform.  A person who delivers goods.|例句：The liveryman delivered the package on time. 送货员准时送达了包裹。

例句：He is a liveryman of the Worshipful Company of Fishmongers. 他是鱼贩同业公会的一名会员。|近义词：deliveryman, delivery driver, courier, guildsman|反义词: receiver, recipient|词性: noun
