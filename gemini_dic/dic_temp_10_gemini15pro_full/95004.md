in-waiting:/ɪn ˈweɪtɪŋ/
| adj.|等待中的，即将上任的|About to occupy a position or office; awaiting formal appointment. |The ambassador in-waiting is expected to arrive next week. 即将上任的大使预计下周抵达。|近义词：expectant, prospective, forthcoming|反义词:incumbent, current|词性:形容词
词源: "in" (介词，表示状态) + "waiting" (动名词，等待)
