drowsy:/ˈdraʊzi/| adj.|困倦的，昏昏欲睡的|Sleepy and lethargic; half asleep.|The warm room made me feel drowsy. 温暖的房间让我感到昏昏欲睡.|Synonyms: sleepy, lethargic, somnolent|Antonyms: alert, awake, wakeful|adjective
