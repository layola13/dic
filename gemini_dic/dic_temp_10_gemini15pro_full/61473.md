embraid:/ɪmˈbreɪd/ |v.| 编织；交织；缠绕 | To interweave or braid together; to decorate with braid. | She embraced the strands of hair and began to embraid them into a complex pattern. 她拿起几缕头发，开始把它们编成一个复杂的图案。|例句：The craftsman embraced the leather strips to embraid a beautiful whip.  工匠把皮条编成了一条漂亮的鞭子。

| 近义词: braid, plait, intertwine, weave | 反义词: unravel, untwist, disentangle |词性: 动词

词源:  From Middle English *embreiden, from Old French embraider, from en- + braider (“to braid”).

注意事项: embraid 虽然可以理解为“拥抱” (embrace) + “编织” (braid), 但其含义更侧重于编织，交织，而不是拥抱的动作。
