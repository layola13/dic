clarenceux: /ˈklærənsuː/
n.
克拉伦苏纹章官;英国纹章院中地位仅次于嘉德纹章官的纹章官
The second highest heraldic officer in England, subordinate to the Garter King of Arms.
Clarenceux King of Arms is responsible for the coats of arms of individuals and institutions located south of the River Trent in England.  克拉伦苏纹章官负责英格兰特伦特河南部个人和机构的盾徽。
The Clarenceux King of Arms granted the family a new crest. 克拉伦苏纹章官授予该家族一个新的饰章。
近义词: herald, officer of arms
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
