crook's:/krʊks/ | n. |骗子的（东西）; 坏蛋的（东西）|Something that belongs to a crook (a dishonest person, a criminal).  It's the possessive form of "crook." | That's a crook's game. 那是骗子的把戏。| The stolen jewels were found in the crook's hideout.  赃物在骗子的藏身处被找到了。| synonyms: criminal's, thief's, swindler's | antonyms: honest person's, law-abiding citizen's |  possessive noun
