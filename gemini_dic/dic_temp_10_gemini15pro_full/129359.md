nolle:/ˈnɒleɪ/
verb
不愿起诉；放弃诉讼；撤回起诉
(law) to declare that one will not prosecute a case; to withdraw a suit.
The district attorney decided to nolle the charges against the defendant due to lack of evidence. 由于缺乏证据，地方检察官决定撤销对被告的指控。
The prosecutor entered a nolle prosequi. 检察官提出了不起诉。
synonyms: withdraw, drop, dismiss, abandon
antonyms: prosecute, pursue, indict
