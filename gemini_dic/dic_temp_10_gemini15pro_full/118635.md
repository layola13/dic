messieurs:/meˈsjɜː(r)z/ /məˈsjɜːrz/
noun
先生们；各位
Plural form of monsieur, a French word meaning "mister" or "sir". Used as a polite form of address to a group of men.
Messieurs, dinner is served. 先生们，晚餐准备好了。
The letter began, "Messieurs, it is with great pleasure..." 这封信的开头是：“先生们，我非常高兴……”
gentlemen, sirs
ladies, mesdames
