fricando:/frɪˈkændoʊ/ /frɪˈkɑːndoʊ/
noun
fricassee, fricando
A dish of meat, fowl, or vegetables, cut into small pieces, stewed or fried, and served in a thick white sauce.
The chef's specialty is a veal fricando.  厨师的特色菜是小牛肉弗里坎多。
