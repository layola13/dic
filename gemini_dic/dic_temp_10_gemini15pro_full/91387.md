hydrotherapists:/ˌhaɪdrəˈθɛrəpɪsts/
n.
水疗师，水疗专家
Specialists who use water therapy (hydrotherapy) to treat various medical conditions.
Hydrotherapists work with patients of all ages, helping them improve their mobility and manage pain. 水疗师与各个年龄段的患者合作，帮助他们提高活动能力和控制疼痛。
Hydrotherapists often work in hospitals, clinics, or private practices. 水疗师通常在医院、诊所或私人诊所工作。
Synonyms: aquatic therapists, water therapists
Antonyms: None
