beaut:/bjuːt/| n.|美人;美好的事物|Something excellent or beautiful, especially a car.  Derived from "beauty," shortened and made informal.  Originally Australian slang, now more widespread.|That vintage Mustang is a real beaut! 那辆复古的野马真是一辆尤物！|近义词：beauty, stunner, looker, gem|反义词：dog, wreck, mess|名词
