comicality:/kəˈmɪkəlɪti/
n.
滑稽；诙谐；可笑
The comicality of the situation was not lost on us.
我们都注意到了当时情形的滑稽。
The play was filled with comicality and slapstick humor.
这部戏充满了滑稽和闹剧式的幽默。
Synonyms: humor, funniness, drollery, absurdity, ludicrousness
Antonyms: seriousness, solemnity, gravity
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From comical + -ity.  Comical comes from comic, from French comique, from Latin comicus, from Greek kōmikos, from kōmos (revel, merrymaking).
