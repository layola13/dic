haematinometric: /ˌhiːmətɪnəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
血色素测定的
Relating to the measurement of haematin (an oxidized form of heme, the iron-containing nonprotein part of hemoglobin) or hemoglobin, especially by means of a haematinometer.
Haematinometric methods are used to assess the concentration of hemoglobin in blood. 血色素测定法用于评估血液中血红蛋白的浓度。
The haematinometric analysis revealed a significant decrease in hemoglobin levels. 血色素测定分析显示血红蛋白水平显著降低。
Near Synonyms:  hemoglobinometric, colorimetric
