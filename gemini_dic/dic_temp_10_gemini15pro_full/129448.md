non-act:/ˈnɒnækt/ | n. | 不作为，拒绝行动 | An intentional failure or refusal to act; inaction. | The government's non-act on climate change is alarming. 政府在气候变化问题上的不作为令人担忧。|  The union's non-act during the negotiations cost the workers dearly. 工会在谈判期间的无所作为使工人付出了高昂的代价。 | 近义词: inaction, passivity, inactivity | 反义词: action, activity, involvement | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "act" (from Latin "actus," meaning "a doing, a driving, an impulse").  Essentially, "not acting."
