all-purpose:/ˌɔːlˈpɜːrpəs/ | adj. | 多用途的，万能的 | Suitable for many different uses or purposes. |  She bought an all-purpose cleaner for the kitchen. 她买了一种厨房多用途清洁剂。

All-purpose flour is good for most baking.  通用面粉适合大多数烘焙。


近义词: versatile, multipurpose, general-purpose | 反义词: specialized, single-purpose | 词性: adjective

词源:  "all" + "purpose".  "All" meaning for everything, and "purpose" referring to a use or intention.  The combination simply denotes something suitable for a variety of uses.
