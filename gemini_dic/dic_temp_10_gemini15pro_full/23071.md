boundary:/ˈbaʊndri/ (US) /ˈbaʊndəri/ (UK)| n.|边界，界限，分界线|A line that marks the limits of an area; a dividing line.|例句：The river forms the boundary between the two countries. 这条河是两国之间的边界。|近义词：border, limit, edge, frontier|反义词:center, interior|noun

boundary:/ˈbaʊndri/ (US) /ˈbaʊndəri/ (UK)| n.|界限，范围|The limit of something, especially an area or activity.|例句：Pushing the boundaries of science.  挑战科学的极限。|近义词：limit, confines, extent|反义词:freedom, limitlessness|noun
