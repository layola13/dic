lirelliform: /ˌlɪrɪˈlɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 琴形的，百合形的 | Shaped like a lyre or lily; narrow and pointed at both ends, with the widest point in the middle. |  The petals of the rare orchid were distinctly lirelliform. 这种稀有兰花的花瓣明显呈琴形。|  lyrate, spathulate | lanceolate, linear | adjective
