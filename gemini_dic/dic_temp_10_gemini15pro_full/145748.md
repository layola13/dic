overculled:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌld/
adjective
过度剔除的，过度选择的
Having been culled excessively; reduced in number beyond what is necessary or desirable.
The deer population was overculled last year, leading to a shortage of hunting licenses this season. 去年鹿群被过度剔除，导致本季度狩猎许可证短缺。
over-thinned, over-reduced
underpopulated

词性: adjective
