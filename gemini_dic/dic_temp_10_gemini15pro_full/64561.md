erroneousness:/ˌɛrəˈniːəsnəs/ |n.| 错误性，不正确 |The erroneousness of his assumptions led to the failure of the project.  他的假设的错误导致了项目的失败。|Synonyms: falsity, inaccuracy, incorrectness, fallaciousness |Antonyms: truth, accuracy, correctness, validity |noun
Etymology:  From erroneous + -ness.  Erroneous comes from Late Middle English, from Old French erronieux, from Latin errōneus, from errāre "to wander, err."
