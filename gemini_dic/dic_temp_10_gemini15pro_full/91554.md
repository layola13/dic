hyper-linked:/ˌhaɪpərˈlɪŋkt/ | adj. | 超链接的 | Connected to another source of information by means of a hyperlink. |  This word is hyper-linked to the dictionary.  这个词被超链接到字典。| 近义词: linked, connected | 反义词: unlinked, disconnected | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Hyper-" comes from the Greek prefix "hyper-" meaning "over, beyond," and "linked" comes from the Old English "hlincian," meaning "to join with rings."
