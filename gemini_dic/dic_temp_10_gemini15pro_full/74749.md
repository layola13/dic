freeway:/ˈfriːweɪ/ (US), /ˈfriːˌweɪ/ (UK)| n. |高速公路，快速公路|A wide road for fast-moving traffic, especially one with a limited number of places where you can enter or leave it.  A highway that you do not have to pay to use.|We took the freeway to Los Angeles. 我们走高速公路去了洛杉矶。|Synonyms: expressway, motorway, highway, thruway|Antonyms: side road, back road|noun
