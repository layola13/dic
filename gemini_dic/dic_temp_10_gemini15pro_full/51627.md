developmentally:/dɪˌveləpˈmentəli/| adv.|发展地，发育地|In a way that relates to the process of growing or changing or developing.|例句：Children develop developmentally at different rates. 孩子们的发育速度各不相同。|例句：The program is designed to help children learn developmentally appropriate skills. 该计划旨在帮助儿童学习适合其发展阶段的技能。|近义词：evolutionarily, progressively|反义词: regressively|词性:副词

词源: From "developmental" + "-ly". "Developmental" comes from "develop" (from Old French *desvoloper, meaning "to unfold, unwrap") + "-mental" (a suffix forming adjectives meaning "pertaining to").
