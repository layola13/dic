overconscientiousness:/ˌoʊvərˌkɑːnʃiˈɛnʃəsnəs/
n.|过度认真；过分谨慎|The state of being excessively conscientious, often to the point of being overly scrupulous or worried.|Her overconscientiousness made it difficult for her to delegate tasks. 她过分认真，很难把任务委托给别人。|近义词：scrupulousness, meticulousness, punctiliousness|反义词：negligence, carelessness|词性:名词
Etymology: over- + conscientious + -ness.  "Over-" denotes "excessively," "conscientious" refers to being guided by conscience and a strong sense of duty, and "-ness" forms a noun indicating a state or quality.
