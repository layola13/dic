dachshund:/ˈdæksˌhʊnd/, /ˈdɑːksˌhʊnt/ | n. |腊肠犬|A small dog with a long body and short legs, originally bred in Germany to hunt badgers. |例句：My dachshund loves to burrow under the blankets. 我的腊肠犬喜欢钻到毯子下面。

例句：Dachshunds are known for their stubborn personalities. 腊肠犬以其固执的个性而闻名。|近义词：sausage dog, wiener dog |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Dachs* (badger) + *Hund* (dog).  Literally "badger dog."
