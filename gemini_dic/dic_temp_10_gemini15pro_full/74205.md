fourscore:/ˈfɔːrskɔːr/
| adj. n.|八十；八十个；八十岁|A number equivalent to eighty (4 x 20).  Used especially in biblical contexts or elevated style.|The church was built fourscore years ago. 这座教堂建于八十年前。|近义词：eighty|反义词: twenty|词性:形容词，名词
Etymology: From Old English *fēowerscōr, from fēower (four) + scōr (score, twenty).
