covetousness:/ˈkʌvɪtəsnəs/| n. |贪婪，贪欲|An excessive or inordinate desire to possess something, typically something belonging to another.|  He was consumed by covetousness and wanted everything his neighbor owned. 他被贪婪吞噬，想要邻居拥有的一切。|
近义词：greed, avarice, cupidity, rapacity|反义词: generosity, contentment, satisfaction|词性: noun

covetous: /ˈkʌvɪtəs/ | adj. |贪婪的，垂涎的| Marked by or showing a great desire to possess something, typically something belonging to another. | She cast a covetous glance at her friend's new car. 她贪婪地瞥了一眼她朋友的新车。|近义词：greedy, avaricious, envious, desirous|反义词: generous, content, satisfied |词性: adjective
