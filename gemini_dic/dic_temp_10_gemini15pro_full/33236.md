chronography:/krəˈnɒɡrəfi/
n.|年代学;计时法|The study of time, especially the measurement of time or the making of timepieces. It also refers to a descriptive record or listing of events in their chronological order.|His new book is a detailed chronography of the American Civil War. 他的新书是美国内战的详细编年史。|近义词：chronology, annals, history|反义词: N/A
词性: 名词
