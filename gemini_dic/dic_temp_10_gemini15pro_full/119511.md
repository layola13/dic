microphyte:/ˈmaɪkroʊfaɪt/| n. |微植物；微生物|A microscopic plant, especially one that is aquatic.  A plant that can only be seen with a microscope, particularly those found in water. |例句：Microphytes form the base of the food chain in many aquatic ecosystems. 微植物在许多水生生态系统中构成食物链的基础。

例句：The researchers studied the diversity of microphytes in the lake. 研究人员研究了湖中微植物的多样性。|近义词：microorganism, microflora|反义词:macrophyte (大型植物)|词性:noun
