bailie:/ˈbeɪli/| n.|市政官，法警|A municipal officer in Scotland.  A magistrate or officer of justice in a particular town or district, typically having powers similar to those of a sheriff or alderman.|The bailie presided over the local court. 市政官主持了地方法庭。|Synonyms: magistrate, alderman, sheriff, officer, official|Antonyms: civilian, resident|noun

bailie:/ˈbeɪli/| n.|土地管理人|A person who manages an estate or farm, especially for an absentee landlord.|The bailie collected the rents from the tenant farmers.  土地管理人向佃农收取租金。|Synonyms: steward, manager, overseer, factor|Antonyms: owner, landlord|noun
