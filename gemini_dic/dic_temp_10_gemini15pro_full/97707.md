inimicalnesses:/ˌɪnɪˈmɪkəlnəsəz/
n.|敌意，不利，有害|The state or quality of being inimical; hostility or harmfulness.|His inimicalnesses towards the project surprised everyone.  他对这个项目的敌意令每个人都感到惊讶。|Near synonyms: hostilities, antagonisms, animosities|Near antonyms: friendliness, benevolence, goodwill|Noun

adj.|敌意的，不利的，有害的|Tending to obstruct or harm.|The policy was inimical to the interests of small businesses. 这项政策对小型企业的利益不利。|Near synonyms: hostile, antagonistic, unfriendly, adverse, harmful, detrimental, injurious|Near antonyms: friendly, benevolent, helpful, beneficial, advantageous|Adjective
