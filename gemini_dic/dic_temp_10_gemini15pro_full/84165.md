half-plucked:/ˌhɑːfˈplʌkt/
adjective
半拔的，未完全拔除的
Partially plucked, as in feathers or hair, with some remaining.
The half-plucked chicken lay on the counter, awaiting further preparation.  半拔毛的鸡躺在柜台上，等待进一步的处理。
The half-plucked eyebrows gave her a surprised look. 半拔的眉毛让她看起来很惊讶。
