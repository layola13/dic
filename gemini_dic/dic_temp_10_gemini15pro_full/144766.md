over-digested: /ˌoʊvər daɪˈdʒɛstɪd/ | adj. | 过度消化的；消化过度的 |  Having been digested excessively or to an undesirable degree. It often implies a feeling of discomfort or sluggishness after eating.  |  The rich, creamy meal left me feeling over-digested and bloated.  这顿丰盛的奶油餐让我感觉消化过度，肚子胀胀的。 |  overfull, bloated, stuffed, surfeited | under-digested | adjective

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over, beyond") + "digest" (from Latin digestus, past participle of digerere, meaning "to separate, distribute, arrange, dissolve," from dis- "apart" + gerere "to carry, bear").
