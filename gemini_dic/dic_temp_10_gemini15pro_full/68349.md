fallacies:/ˈfæləsiːz/
n.
谬误，谬论
A mistaken belief, especially one based on unsound argument.
Many people believe the fallacy that money brings happiness.  很多人相信金钱带来幸福的谬论。
Synonyms: misconception, delusion, error, untruth
Antonyms: truth, fact, reality
noun

fallacy:/ˈfæləsi/
n.
谬误，谬论
A mistaken belief, especially one based on unsound argument.
He exposed the fallacy of their reasoning. 他揭露了他们推理中的谬误。
Synonyms: misconception, delusion, error, untruth
Antonyms: truth, fact, reality
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin fallacia "deception, deceit, trick," from Latin fallax (genitive fallacis) "deceptive," from fallere "to deceive, trip, lead into error, disappoint, fail."
