注意事项:
"Birthings" is grammatically correct but less common than the singular "birth" or the plural "births," especially in everyday conversation.  It tends to be used in more formal or literary contexts when emphasizing the process or act of giving birth multiple times.  Using "births" is generally preferred in most situations.
