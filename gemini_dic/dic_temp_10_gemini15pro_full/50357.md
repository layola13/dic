depone:/dɪˈpoʊn/| v.|作证；宣誓作证；郑重声明|to testify or make a statement under oath, especially in a legal context.  To state something solemnly and formally.|The witness was asked to depone about what she had seen.  证人被要求就她所见之事作证。|Near Synonyms: testify, swear, attest, declare, affirm|Near Antonyms: recant, deny, repudiate|verb
