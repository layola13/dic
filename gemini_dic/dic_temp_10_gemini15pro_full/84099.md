half-intellectually:/ˌhæf ˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəli/
|adv.|半智力地；不完全理性地；|Engaging with something in a way that only partially utilizes or involves intellectual faculties; superficially intellectual.|He listened to the lecture half-intellectually, more concerned with what he was having for lunch.|Near Synonyms: superficially, cursorily, perfunctorily, absentmindedly|Antonyms: thoughtfully, attentively, diligently, intellectually|Adverb
