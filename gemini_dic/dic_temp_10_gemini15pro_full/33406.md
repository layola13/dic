churchiness:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃɪnɪs/
noun
教会气氛；宗教气息；虔诚做作
Excessive or affected piety; an ostentatious display of religious devotion.
Her churchiness was evident in her frequent pronouncements about morality. 她的虔诚表现在她经常发表关于道德的声明。
The churchiness of the event was off-putting to some attendees.  这次活动的宗教氛围让一些与会者感到反感。
近义词: piety, devoutness, religiosity, sanctimoniousness
反义词: irreverence, impiety, secularism
