dika:/ˈdiːkə/ | n. |  justice; right; what is right or just |  From Greek *dīkē* "justice, right, custom, usage."  It refers to a concept of justice, righteousness, and what is considered right or lawful. | The dika of the gods demanded retribution.  神明的正义要求报应。 |  justice, righteousness, fairness | injustice, unfairness | noun
