overmortgaging:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrɡɪdʒɪŋ/
|n.|过度抵押|The act of borrowing more money against a property than it is worth or more than one can realistically repay.|Overmortgaging can lead to foreclosure if the borrower defaults on payments. 过度抵押如果借款人违约，可能会导致丧失抵押品赎回权。|
|近义词: excessive borrowing, overleveraging|反义词: underleveraging, conservative financing|词性:名词 (derived from the verb "overmortgage")


overmortgage:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrɡɪdʒ/
|vt.|过度抵押|To secure loans against (a property) in excess of its value or of one's capacity to repay.|They overmortgaged their house and couldn't afford the repayments. 他们过度抵押了房子，无力偿还贷款。|近义词: overborrow, overleverage|反义词: underleverage|词性:动词
