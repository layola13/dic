colonized: /ˈkɒlənaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlənaɪzd/ (US)
|v. |殖民化的；被殖民的|To establish a colony or colonies in (a place). To settle (a place) as a colony. To send settlers to (a territory) and establish political control over it. To appropriate (a place or domain) for one's own use.|例句：The British colonized many parts of the world. 英国在世界许多地方建立了殖民地。|近义词：settled, occupied, populated, invaded, conquered|反义词：liberated, freed, independent|verb (past participle)

colonized: /ˈkɒlənaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlənaɪzd/ (US)
|adj. |殖民化的；被殖民的|Having been made into a colony; inhabited by colonists.|例句：India was a colonized country under British rule. 印度在英国统治下是一个被殖民的国家。|近义词：settled, occupied, dependent, subject|反义词：independent, sovereign, free|adjective
