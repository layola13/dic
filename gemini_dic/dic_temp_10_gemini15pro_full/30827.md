cha-cha-cha: /ˈtʃɑː tʃɑː tʃɑː/ | n. |恰恰恰（一种拉丁舞）| A fast rhythmic ballroom dance of Cuban origin, consisting of three steps and a shuffle. |  例句：They danced the cha-cha-cha all night. 他们整晚都在跳恰恰恰。

例句：The band played a lively cha-cha-cha. 乐队演奏了一曲活泼的恰恰恰。 | 近义词：rumba, mambo | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a dance) | 词性: noun

chas: /tʃɑːz/ (This is the singular form, less commonly used) | n. | 追逐，狩猎 | A hunt or chase. | 例句：The hounds were in full chas after the fox.  猎犬正在全力追捕狐狸。

例句：The chas ended with the capture of the deer. 追捕以捕获鹿而告终。 | 近义词：hunt, pursuit | 反义词: retreat, flight | 词性: noun
