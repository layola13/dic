anal-retentiveness:/ˌeɪnəl rɪˈtɛntɪvnəs/
n.
肛门滞留；执拗；一丝不苟
A personality trait characterized by excessive orderliness, attention to detail, and a tendency to control.  This term originates from Freudian psychoanalysis and is often used informally to describe someone overly preoccupied with rules, cleanliness, and perfection.
He showed signs of anal-retentiveness in his obsessive tidying. 他在强迫性整理方面表现出肛门滞留的迹象。
近义词：obsessiveness, compulsiveness, meticulousness, fastidiousness
反义词：sloppiness, carelessness, disorganization
词性：noun


anal-retentive:/ˌeɪnəl rɪˈtɛntɪv/
adj.
肛门滞留的；执拗的；一丝不苟的
Excessively orderly, attentive to detail, and controlling.
She's so anal-retentive about her work that she never makes a mistake. 她对自己的工作一丝不苟，从不出错。
近义词：obsessive, compulsive, meticulous, fastidious
反义词：sloppy, careless, disorganized
词性：adjective


词源:
From *anal* (relating to the anus) + *retentive* (tending to retain).  The term derives from Freudian psychoanalytic theory, which posits that this personality trait develops from fixation at the anal stage of psychosexual development.
