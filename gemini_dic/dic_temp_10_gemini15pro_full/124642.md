mouthier:/ˈmaʊðiər/ (US), /ˈmaʊθɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更爱说话的，更直言不讳的，更放肆的|More inclined to speak freely and boldly, often to the point of rudeness or excessive talkativeness.  Derived from "mouth" + "-y" (suffix indicating having the quality of) + "-er" (comparative suffix).|She's become much mouthier since she got the promotion. 她升职后变得更爱说话了。

He's a mouthier version of his brother. 他比他哥哥更口无遮拦。|近义词：talkative, outspoken, loquacious, garrulous, voluble|反义词：quiet, reserved, reticent, taciturn|词性:形容词
