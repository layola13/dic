bismuthous: /ˈbɪzməθəs/
adjective
亚铋的；铋(III)的
Containing or relating to bismuth, especially with a valency of three.  Relating to or containing bismuth with valence 3.
Bismuthous chloride is a highly reactive compound.  氯化亚铋是一种高活性化合物。
Bismuthous oxide is a yellowish solid. 氧化亚铋是一种淡黄色固体。
Near synonyms: trivalent bismuth
Antonyms: bismuthic (referring to bismuth with a valency of five)
词性: adjective
