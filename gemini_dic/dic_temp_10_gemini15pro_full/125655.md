mumming:/ˈmʌmɪŋ/ | n. | 哑剧表演；伪装；化装游行 | The act of wearing a disguise and not speaking, often as part of a traditional Christmas celebration.  It can also refer to any disguised performance, or the disguises themselves. |  Example: The Christmas mumming involved a colorful procession through the village.  圣诞哑剧表演包括一个穿过村庄的五彩缤纷的游行。

Example: The children enjoyed the mumming and the treats they received. 孩子们喜欢哑剧表演和他们收到的礼物。 | Synonym:  masquerade, charade, disguise, pantomime | Antonym:  disclosure, revelation | noun
