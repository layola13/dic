endowing:/ɪnˈdaʊɪŋ/
|vt.|赋予，捐赠，资助|provide with a quality, ability, or asset.|He endowed the university with a large sum of money. 他向这所大学捐赠了一大笔钱。|
近义词：bestowing, granting, donating, furnishing, investing|
反义词：depriving, stripping, divesting|
词性：动词


endowment:/ɪnˈdaʊmənt/
|n.|捐赠，天赋，才能|the action of endowing something or someone.|The university received a generous endowment. 这所大学收到了一笔慷慨的捐赠。|
近义词：gift, donation, grant, bequest, talent, aptitude, ability|
反义词：deprivation, lack|
词性：名词
