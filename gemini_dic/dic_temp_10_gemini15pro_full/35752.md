cochleariform: /ˌkɒkliˈɛərɪfɔːm/ /ˌkɑːkliˈɛrɪfɔːrm/
adjective
勺状的；蜗牛壳状的
Resembling the cochlea, a spiral cavity in the inner ear, or a spoon.
The cochleariform process is a bony projection in the temporal bone.  颞骨上的匙突是一个骨性突起。
The surgeon carefully examined the cochleariform area.  外科医生仔细检查了匙突区域。
Near Synonyms: spoon-shaped, spiral, cochleate
Antonyms: straight, linear
词性: adjective
