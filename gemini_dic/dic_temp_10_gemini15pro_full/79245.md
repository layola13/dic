glassen:/ˈɡlæsən/ | v. | 给…装玻璃; 用玻璃罩住 | To furnish or cover with glass. |  They glassed in the porch to create a sunroom. 他们把门廊装上玻璃，建成了一个日光浴室。

The gardener glassed the greenhouse to protect the plants. 园丁给温室装上玻璃以保护植物。 | 近义词: glaze, vitrify | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 remove the glass) | 词性: 动词

词源:  From the noun "glass" + the suffix "-en," which forms verbs from nouns or adjectives, meaning "to make or become (like the root word)."
