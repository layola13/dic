overawful:/ˌoʊvərˈɔːfəl/| adj.|极其糟糕的，非常可怕的| An informal, emphatic way of saying something is extremely bad or terrible.  It combines "over" suggesting excess, and "awful." |The traffic was overawful this morning; I was an hour late. 今早交通糟透了，我迟到了一个小时。|近义词：terrible, dreadful, horrible, atrocious, appalling|反义词: wonderful, excellent, fantastic|词性:形容词
