outreckoned:/ˌaʊtˈrɛkənd/
verb
算计过；胜过；在计算上超过
surpassed in reckoning or calculation; outmaneuvered or defeated by superior strategy or cleverness
The experienced chess player outreckoned his opponent and won the match.  这位经验丰富的棋手在计算上胜过了他的对手，赢得了比赛。
outwit, outmaneuver, outsmart, surpass
be outreckoned by, lose to
