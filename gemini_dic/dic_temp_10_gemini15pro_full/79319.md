glean:/ɡliːn/| v.|收集，拾取； glean (information)费力地收集(信息)|To gather bit by bit, as after a reaper; to gather (grain) left by reapers. gleanings:/ˈɡliːnɪŋz/| n.|拾遗；收集到的信息|Things gleaned; things acquired by gleaning, especially information or material gathered from various sources.|Her gleanings from foreign travels made for fascinating stories. 她从国外旅行中收集到的信息成就了引人入胜的故事。|collection, compilation, accumulation|loss, discard|noun
