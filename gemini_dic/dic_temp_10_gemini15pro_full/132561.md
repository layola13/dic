nondiaphanous:/ˌnɒnˌdaɪˈæfənəs/
adjective
不透明的，不透光的
Not allowing light to pass through; opaque.
The curtains were thick and nondiaphanous, blocking out all sunlight. 厚厚的窗帘不透光，挡住了所有的阳光。
近义词:opaque, nontransparent
反义词:diaphanous, transparent, translucent
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From non- + diaphanous.  "Diaphanous" comes from the Greek "diaphanēs" meaning "transparent, translucent," from "dia-" (through) + "phainein" (to show).  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not."
