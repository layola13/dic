misidentifying: /ˌmɪsɪˈdɛntɪfaɪɪŋ/
verb
错误识别，误认
The act of incorrectly identifying someone or something.
The police misidentified the suspect and arrested the wrong person. 警方错误地识别了嫌疑人，逮捕了错误的人。
He misidentified the bird as a robin, but it was actually a sparrow. 他把这只鸟误认为知更鸟，但实际上它是一只麻雀。
synonyms: mislabeling, misnaming, mistaking
antonyms: identifying, recognizing, classifying
