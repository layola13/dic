habiting:/ˈhæbɪtɪŋ/
v.
居住；栖息
The process of living in or occupying a place.
Fish are inhabiting the coral reefs. 鱼类栖息在珊瑚礁中。
inhabiting, residing, dwelling, occupying
leaving, deserting
verb
