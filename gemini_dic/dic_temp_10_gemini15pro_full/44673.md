crucifiers:/ˈkruːsəˌfaɪərz/
n.
十字架， распятие, 钉死于十字架
Objects, typically of wood, shaped like a cross and used in the Christian religion as a symbol of the crucifixion of Jesus Christ.
The priest held the crucifix high above his head.  神父高举着十字架。
We saw many crucifixes in the cathedral. 我们在大教堂里看到了许多十字架。
近义词:crosses, crucifix
反义词:None
词性:noun
