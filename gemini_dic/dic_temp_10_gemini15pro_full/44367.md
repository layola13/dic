cross-contamination:/ˌkrɒs kənˌtæmɪˈneɪʃən/ /ˌkrɔːs kənˌtæmɪˈneɪʃn/| n. |交叉污染|The unintentional transfer of bacteria or other microorganisms from one substance to another, often with harmful effects.  The spread of bacteria or other pathogens from one food item to another.|例句：Raw meat can cause cross-contamination if it touches other food. 生肉如果接触到其他食物，会导致交叉污染。

例句：Be careful to avoid cross-contamination when preparing food.  准备食物时要注意避免交叉污染。|近义词：cross-infection, contamination|反义词:sterilization, disinfection|词性:noun
