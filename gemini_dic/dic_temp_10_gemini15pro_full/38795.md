conductor:/kənˈdʌktər/ (US) /kənˈdʌktə(r)/ (UK)| n.|指挥家；售票员；导体|A person who directs the performance of an orchestra or choir. A person who collects fares or tickets on a bus or train. A substance that allows heat or electricity to pass through it.|
The conductor led the orchestra with passion. 指挥家充满激情地指挥着乐队。

The conductor asked to see our tickets. 售票员要求查看我们的车票。

Copper is a good conductor of electricity. 铜是良好的电导体。

|近义词：director, leader, guide, driver,  wire, cable|反义词:insulator|词性:noun


conductor:/kənˈdʌktər/ (US) /kənˈdʌktə(r)/ (UK)| n.|导体|A material or device that permits the flow of electricity.  Can also refer to a material that transmits heat.|
Copper is a good conductor of electricity. 铜是良好的电导体。

Aluminum is a lightweight conductor used in power lines. 铝是一种用于电力线的轻质导体。
|近义词：medium, transmitter|反义词:insulator, nonconductor|词性:noun
