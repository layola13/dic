doped:/doʊpt/ (US) /dəʊpt/ (UK)| adj.| 掺杂的；服用兴奋剂的；麻醉的；愚蠢的| treated with a dopant; under the influence of drugs; stupid or silly|
The semiconductor is doped with phosphorus.  这种半导体掺杂了磷。
The cyclist was disqualified for using doped substances.  这名自行车运动员因使用兴奋剂而被取消资格。

近义词:adulterated, contaminated, drugged, intoxicated, foolish, silly|
反义词:pure, undoped, sober, intelligent|
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From *dope* (n.), originally referring to a thick liquid or viscous substance.  The meaning expanded to include substances used to alter performance, and then to the act of adding such substances. Ultimately, it broadened to encompass the state of being under the influence of drugs.
