mar:/mɑːr/ /mɑːrd/| v. |损毁，玷污，破坏|To damage or spoil the appearance or quality of something.|例句：The beautiful scenery was marred by the ugly billboards.  美丽的风景被丑陋的广告牌破坏了。

例句：The accident marred his perfect driving record.  这事故破坏了他完美的驾驶记录。|近义词：blemish, spoil, ruin, impair, damage|反义词：enhance, improve, perfect, beautify|词性:动词


marred:/mɑːrd/ /mɑːrd/| adj. |受损的，有缺陷的|Damaged or spoiled.|例句：The marred surface of the table needed to be refinished.  桌子受损的表面需要重新修整。

例句：Her otherwise perfect essay was marred by a few spelling errors. 她的文章本来很完美，却被几个拼写错误破坏了。|近义词：damaged, spoiled, blemished, flawed, imperfect|反义词：perfect, flawless, pristine, immaculate|词性:形容词


词源: From Old English *mierran, from Proto-Germanic *marzjaną (“to hinder, confuse”). Cognate with Old Norse merja (“to bruise, crush”), Middle Dutch merren (“to hinder, trouble”).
