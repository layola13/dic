non-pragmatic:/ˌnɒn præɡˈmætɪk/ (US) /ˌnɑːn præɡˈmætɪk/| adj.|非实用主义的，不务实的|Not dealing with things sensibly and realistically in a way that is based on practical rather than theoretical considerations.|His non-pragmatic approach to the problem ultimately led to failure. 他不切实际的处理问题的方法最终导致了失败。|The artist's non-pragmatic pursuit of beauty over function frustrated his clients. 这位艺术家不切实际地追求美感而忽略功能，这使他的客户感到沮丧。| impractical, unrealistic, idealistic, unpractical|practical, realistic, pragmatic|adjective
