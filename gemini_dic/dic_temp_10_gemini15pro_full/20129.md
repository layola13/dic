bipartite:/baɪˈpɑːrtaɪt/
adjective
二分的，两分的
Consisting of two parts.  Divided into two parts.  Also, involving two groups or entities.
例句：The bipartite graph shows the relationships between two sets of nodes.  二分图显示了两组节点之间的关系。

例句：The agreement was a bipartite treaty between the two warring nations. 该协议是两个交战国之间的双边条约。
近义词：dual, two-part
反义词：multipartite, unified
词性: adjective
