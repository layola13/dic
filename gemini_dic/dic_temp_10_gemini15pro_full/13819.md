auditionee:/ɔːdɪʃəˈniː/
n.
試鏡者；參加試演的人
A person who is auditioning for a role or position.
The auditionees were all nervous before their turn. 試演者在輪到他們之前都很緊張。
The director spoke to each auditionee individually. 導演與每位試鏡者單獨交談。
