coco:/ˈkoʊkoʊ/ (US), /ˈkəʊkəʊ/ (UK)| n.|椰子; 可可; 可可树; 可可粉; 深棕色|A tropical palm tree bearing coconuts. The dried kernel of the coconut. A powder made from roasted and ground cacao seeds, used to make chocolate. A deep reddish-brown colour.|例句：I love the taste of fresh coco. 我喜欢新鲜椰子的味道。

例句：She added coco powder to the cake batter. 她在蛋糕糊里加了可可粉。

近义词：coconut (for the fruit), cocoa (for the powder), chocolate (for the product made from cocoa)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


coco:/ˈkoʊkoʊ/ (US), /ˈkəʊkəʊ/ (UK)| adj.|深棕色的|Of a deep reddish-brown colour.|例句：She wore a coco dress. 她穿了一件深棕色的连衣裙。

例句：He has coco hair. 他有深棕色的头发。

近义词：chocolate, brown, dark brown|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
