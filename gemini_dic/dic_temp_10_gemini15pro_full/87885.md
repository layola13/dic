hieing:/ˈhaɪɪŋ/
verb
急忙赶往，匆匆而去
Going quickly, hurrying.
例句：She was hieing home to tell her family the good news. 她急忙赶回家，把好消息告诉家人。

例句：Hieing to the station, he realized he'd forgotten his ticket.  他匆匆赶往车站，才发现忘记了车票。

近义词：hurrying, rushing, hastening, speeding
反义词：dawdling, lingering, delaying
词性：verb (present participle of "hie")


词源：
From Middle English hien, from Old English hīeġan (“to hasten, strive, endeavor”), from Proto-Germanic *haują (“to strike, hew”). Cognate with Dutch hijgen (“to pant, puff”), German hauen (“to hew, chop”).
