dentalization:/ˌdɛntələˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|齿音化；齿化|The pronunciation of a sound by placing the tongue against the upper teeth, as in some pronunciations of /n/ and /l/.  The process by which a sound becomes dental.|
例句：
The dentalization of /n/ in "tenth" is a common feature of English pronunciation.
“tenth”中/n/的齿音化是英语发音中的一个常见特征。

Dentalization can occur when a sound is next to a dental consonant.
当一个音素紧邻一个齿音辅音时，可能会发生齿音化。

|近义词: palatalization (腭音化)|反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it describes a specific phonetic process) |词性: noun|
