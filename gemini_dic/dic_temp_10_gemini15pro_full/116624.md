matronly:/ˈmeɪtrənli/ | adj. |  matronly的;像主妇的;稳重的 |  Resembling or characteristic of a matron; typically used to describe a woman who is older, dignified, and somewhat stout.  Often implies a settled, domestic image. |  She wore a matronly dress. 她穿着一件主妇般的连衣裙。 |  The matronly woman offered me a cup of tea.  这位像主妇一样的女人给我倒了杯茶。 |  motherly, stately, dignified | youthful, girlish | adjective
