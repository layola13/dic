creel:/kriːl/
n.
鱼篓，鱼笼
A wicker basket, especially one used for carrying fish.
The fisherman carried a creel full of trout. 渔夫提着满满一篓鳟鱼。
basket, container
trap
noun

creels:/kriːlz/
n.
鱼篓（creel的复数）
Wicker baskets, especially ones used for carrying fish.
The fishermen brought back their creels overflowing with fish. 渔民们带回了装满鱼的鱼篓。
