lubrication:/ˌluːbrɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|润滑，润滑作用|The lubrication of the engine is essential for its proper functioning.  发动机润滑对其正常运转至关重要。|例句：Regular lubrication is important for the smooth operation of machinery.  定期润滑对机器的平稳运行很重要。

例句：The lack of lubrication caused the gears to grind. 缺乏润滑导致齿轮磨损。|近义词：oiling, greasing|反义词:friction, abrasion|词性:noun

词源: From Late Latin *lubricatio, from Latin lubricatus, past participle of lubricare "to make smooth or slippery," from lubricus "slippery, smooth."
