milter:/ˈmɪltər/| n.|邮件过滤器|A mail filter is a program that processes email messages as they are being sent or received. It can be used to perform various actions, such as blocking spam, scanning for viruses, and modifying message headers.|Example 1: The milter blocked the spam email before it reached my inbox. 邮件过滤器在我收到垃圾邮件之前就将其拦截了。

Example 2:  We configured the milter to add a disclaimer to all outgoing emails. 我们配置了邮件过滤器，以便在所有发出的电子邮件中添加免责声明。|近义词:mail filter, email filter|反义词:|词性:noun
