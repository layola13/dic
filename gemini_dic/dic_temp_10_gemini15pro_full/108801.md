leatherette: /ˌlɛðəˈrɛt/ | n. | 人造革，仿皮 | Imitation leather; a fabric made to resemble leather. |  My new bag is made of leatherette. 我的新包是人造革做的。

She chose a leatherette sofa because it was cheaper than real leather. 她选择了一个人造革沙发，因为它比真皮的便宜。 | 近义词: artificial leather, faux leather, vinyl | 反义词: genuine leather, real leather | 词性: noun
