abelite: /ˈeɪbəˌlaɪt/
n.
阿贝立特（一种炸药）
A high explosive consisting of ammonium nitrate mixed with a small percentage of nitroglycerin and other ingredients.
Abelite was used in mining and quarrying. 阿贝立特曾用于采矿和采石。
Abelite is a powerful explosive. 阿贝立特是一种强大的炸药。
近义词: dynamite, explosive
反义词: None
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the name of its inventor, Hermann Abel, + -ite (a suffix used to form the names of minerals and rocks).
