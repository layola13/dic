nonproliferation:/ˌnɒnprəˌlɪfəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 核不扩散，防扩散 | the act of preventing something (such as nuclear weapons) from spreading to other countries or people |  The treaty aims at nuclear nonproliferation.  该条约旨在防止核扩散。 | synonyms: containment, restriction | antonyms: proliferation, expansion | noun
