clerkship: /ˈklɜːrkʃɪp/ (US) /ˈklɑːkʃɪp/ (UK)
n.
实习期；职员职位；文书工作
A period of time spent working as a clerk, especially as a training period for a lawyer or other professional. A position or job as a clerk. Clerical work.
After graduating from law school, she secured a clerkship with a Supreme Court justice.  从法学院毕业后，她在最高法院法官那里获得了一个实习职位。
He began his clerkship at a small law firm in his hometown. 他在家乡的一家小型律师事务所开始了他的实习期。
internship, apprenticeship, training
employment
noun
