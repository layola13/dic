alated:/ˈeɪleɪtɪd/| adj.|兴高采烈的，欢欣鼓舞的|Feeling or expressing great happiness or triumph.|例句：She felt elated after winning the race.  她赢得了比赛后感到欣喜若狂。

例句：He was elated by the news of his promotion. 他听到升职的消息欣喜若狂。|近义词：exhilarated, jubilant, thrilled, overjoyed|反义词：dejected, depressed, downcast|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *alatus* (“winged”), past participle of *alare* (“to furnish with wings”), from *ala* (“wing”).  The sense development is from the idea of being "lifted up," as if with wings.
