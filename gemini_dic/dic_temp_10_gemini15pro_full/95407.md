incurable: /ɪnˈkjʊrəbl/
adjective
无法治愈的;不可救药的
not able to be cured
His cancer is incurable. 他的癌症是无法治愈的。
The damage to their relationship was incurable.  他们之间关系的损害是无法弥补的。
Synonyms: irremediable, terminal, hopeless, irrecoverable
Antonyms: curable, treatable, remediable
词性: 形容词
