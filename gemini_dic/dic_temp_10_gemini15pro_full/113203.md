lurkingly:/ˈlɜːrkɪŋli/| adv.|潜伏地，暗中地|in a lurking manner; stealthily.|例句：A crocodile was lurkingly waiting for its prey beneath the surface of the water.  一条鳄鱼潜伏在水面下等待猎物。|例句：He lurkingly followed her home, staying hidden in the shadows. 他潜伏在阴影中，偷偷地跟着她回家。|近义词：stealthily, furtively, surreptitiously, covertly|反义词：openly, publicly, visibly|词性：副词

词源: From lurk (verb) + -ing (present participle suffix) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The verb "lurk" comes from Middle English lurken, of uncertain origin, possibly Scandinavian.
