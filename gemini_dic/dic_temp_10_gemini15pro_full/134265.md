noninflected:/ˌnɒnɪnˈflektɪd/ | adj. | 未屈折的，非活用 | Not inflected; unchanging in form.  A noninflected word does not change its form to show grammatical function or tense. | The word "sheep" is noninflected as it remains the same in singular and plural. 羊这个词是未屈折变化的，因为它的单复数形式相同。 | Example:  Chinese is largely a noninflected language. 中文很大程度上是一种非屈折语。| synonyms: uninflected, invariable, unchanging | antonyms: inflected, variable, changing |词性: adjective
