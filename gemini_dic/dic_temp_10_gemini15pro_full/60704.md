electro-acoustical:/ɪˌlɛktroʊ əˈkuːstɪkəl/
adjective
电声的
Relating to or involving both electrical and acoustical elements.  A device that converts electrical signals into sound waves, or vice versa.
例句：The development of electro-acoustical transducers revolutionized the music industry. 电声换能器的出现彻底改变了音乐产业。

例句：He specialized in electro-acoustical engineering. 他专攻电声工程。

近义词：electroacoustic
反义词：(No direct antonym, potentially "purely acoustic" or "purely electric")
词性: adjective
