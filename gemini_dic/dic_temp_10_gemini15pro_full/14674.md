avuncularity:/əˌvʌŋkjuˈlærɪti/
n.|叔伯般的慈爱；像伯父（或叔父、舅父、姑父、姨父）般地|Kindness and generosity, especially of an older man towards a younger one, that is like that of an uncle.|He exuded an avuncularity that put everyone at ease. 他散发出的叔伯般的慈爱让每个人都感到轻松自在。|Synonyms:  benevolence, kindness, generosity, paternalism|Antonyms:  hostility, coldness, indifference|noun
