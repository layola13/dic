fluentest:/ˈfluːəntɪst/ | adj. | 最流利的 |  Superlative form of fluent.  It means having the greatest ease and skill in speaking or writing a language. | She is the fluentest speaker of French I know. 她是我认识的法语说得最流利的人。 | Near Synonyms: most articulate, most eloquent, most proficient | Antonyms: least fluent, most hesitant, most halting | word property:adjective

fluentest:/ˈfluːəntɪst/ | adj. | 最流利的 |  The most fluent; speaking or writing with the greatest ease.  It's worth noting that while grammatically correct, "fluentest" is often considered awkward and "most fluent" is generally preferred. | He was the fluentest speaker at the conference. 他是会议上发言最流利的人。 | Near Synonyms: most fluent, most articulate | Antonyms: least fluent, most inarticulate | word property:adjective
