ectoplasmic:/ˌɛktəˈplæzmɪk/| adj.| 外质的；由外质组成的|Relating to or consisting of ectoplasm, a supposed viscous substance that emanates from the body of a medium during a spiritualistic trance.|The medium claimed to produce ectoplasmic hands during the séance. | 霊媒は降霊術の間に外質の手を作り出すと主張した。|Synonyms: spiritual, ethereal|Antonyms: material, physical|adjective

ectoplasm:/ˈɛktəˌplæzəm/| n.| 外质|A viscous substance that is supposed to emanate from the body of a medium during a spiritualistic trance.|Some believe ectoplasm is evidence of spirits, while others think it's a trick. | 一部の人は外質を霊の証拠と考えているが、トリックだと思う人もいる。|Synonyms: spiritual emanation|Antonyms: physical body|noun
