omnidirectional:/ˌɒmnɪdɪˈrɛkʃənəl/ | adj. | 全向的，多向的 | Receiving signals from or transmitting in all directions.  Able to pick up or send signals from or in all directions. | The security camera has an omnidirectional microphone so it can pick up sounds from anywhere.  安全摄像机有一个全向麦克风，因此可以拾取来自任何地方的声音。 |  The antenna is omnidirectional and receives signals from all directions.  这个天线是全向的，可以接收来自各个方向的信号。|  multidirectional, panoramic | directional, unidirectional | adjective

omnipresent:/ˈɒmnɪˈprɛzənt/ | adj. | 无所不在的 | Widespread; present everywhere.  Existing or being everywhere at the same time. |  The feeling of dread was omnipresent throughout the haunted house.  在整个鬼屋里，恐惧感无处不在。 | God is believed by many to be omnipresent.  许多人相信上帝无处不在。| ubiquitous, pervasive | rare, scarce | adjective
