mien:/miːn/
n.|风采；样子；态度|A person's look or manner, especially one of a particular kind, indicating their character or mood.|She had a haughty mien. 她神情高傲。|近义词：appearance, demeanor, bearing, air, look, aspect, countenance|反义词：None (Mien describes a specific kind of appearance related to character or mood, not a general opposite like "ugliness")|词性：noun

Etymology: From Middle English *mene*, from Old French *meine*, *miene*, from Late Latin *mina*, perhaps of Gaulish origin.
