catnapped:/ˈkætˌnæpt/| v.| 绑架（尤指小孩），打盹，小睡 |To kidnap (someone), especially a child. To have a short sleep, especially during the day.|例句：The kidnappers catnapped the mayor's son. 绑匪绑架了市长的儿子。|例句：I catnapped on the train. 我在火车上打了个盹。|近义词：kidnap, abduct, doze, nap|反义词:release, rescue|v.


catnapped:/ˈkætˌnæpt/| adj.| 被绑架的，短暂睡眠的 |Kidnapped, having a short sleep.|例句：The catnapped child was returned safely. 被绑架的孩子安全返回了。|例句：I felt refreshed after my catnapped on the plane. 在飞机上小睡了一会儿后，我感觉神清气爽。|近义词：kidnapped, abducted, dozing, napping|反义词:released, rescued|adj.
