liquidizing:/ˈlɪkwɪdaɪzɪŋ/
v.
液化；使溶解；清算；清除（债务或公司）
The process of making something liquid.  This can refer to blending something into a liquid state, or in a financial context, converting assets into cash.
She is liquidizing the frozen fruit for a smoothie. 她正在将冷冻水果打成果泥做冰沙。
The company is being liquidated to pay off its debts. 公司正在被清算以偿还债务。
liquefying, dissolving, melting
solidifying, freezing
verb
