normalism:/ˈnɔːrməlɪzəm/
n.
师范教育，师范主义
The principles and practices of teacher training, emphasizing professional preparation and pedagogical theory.
Normalism focuses on the practical aspects of teaching. 师范教育注重教学的实践方面。
The school of normalism believed in educating teachers in the best practices of pedagogy. 师范主义学派相信以最佳教学法教育教师。
近义词: teacher training, pedagogy, teacher education
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) informal education, self-learning
