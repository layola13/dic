concordanced:/kənˈkɔːrdənst/ | adj. |  协调的，一致的，和谐的 | Arranged in agreement or harmony; brought into concord. | The concordanced opinions of the experts reassured the public. 专家们一致的意见让公众安心。 | 近义词: harmonious, consistent, agreeable, unified | 反义词: discordant, conflicting, disagreeing | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the verb "concordance" + "-ed" suffix.  "Concordance" itself comes from Late Latin "concordantia,"  meaning "agreement," from "concordant-" (present participle stem of "concordare") + "-ia" (noun suffix). "Concordare" means "to agree," from "con-" (together) + "cord-" (heart).
