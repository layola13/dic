flu-like:/ˈfluːlaɪk/| adj.|类似流感的|Resembling influenza; having symptoms similar to those of influenza.|She had flu-like symptoms, including a fever and muscle aches. 她有类似流感的症状，包括发烧和肌肉酸痛。|I woke up with flu-like aches and pains. 我醒来时感到全身酸痛，像是得了流感。|Near synonyms:  influenzal, grippe-like|Near antonyms: healthy, well|adjective
