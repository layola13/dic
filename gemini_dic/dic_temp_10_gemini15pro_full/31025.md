词源:  Both "chalybean" and "chalybeate" derive from Latin *chalybs*, meaning "steel," which in turn comes from the Greek *khályps* (χάλυψ), referring to a hard iron or steel, possibly originating from the Chalybes, an ancient people of Asia Minor known for their ironworking skills.
