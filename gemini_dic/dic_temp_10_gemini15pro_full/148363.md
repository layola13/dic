overspeediness:/ˌoʊvərˈspiːdinəs/
noun
过度速度；超速
The state of being excessively speedy.
His overspeediness caused the accident. 他的超速导致了事故。
近义词:excessive speed, haste, hastiness, precipitateness, rashness
反义词:slowness, tardiness, carefulness, deliberation
词性:n.

overspeedy:/ˌoʊvərˈspiːdi/
adjective
超速的；过快的
Excessively speedy; too fast.
The overspeedy car was pulled over by the police.  这辆超速行驶的汽车被警察拦了下来。
近义词:too fast, excessively fast, hasty, rash
反义词:slow, careful, deliberate
词性:adj.


词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "speedy" (adjective meaning "fast") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).  "Speedy" comes from Old English "spēdig," meaning "successful, prosperous."  The sense of "fast" developed later.
