ghillie: /ˈɡɪli/ /ˈɡɪlɪ/ | n. | 吉利服；穿着吉利服的人 | A ghillie suit is a type of camouflage clothing designed to resemble heavy foliage.  A ghillie can also refer to a person wearing such a suit, especially a stalker or sniper. | The sniper, wearing a ghillie suit, blended seamlessly into the undergrowth.  狙击手穿着吉利服，完美地融入了灌木丛中。 |  The ghillie waited patiently for his target to appear. 穿着吉利服的人耐心地等待着他的目标出现。 | synonyms: camouflage suit, sniper suit | antonyms: uniform, bright clothing | noun
