outdesign:/ˌaʊt.dɪˈzaɪn/
verb
胜过设计，设计出更好的东西
to design something that is superior to something else.
例句：Company A outdesigned its competitors with its new smartphone. A公司凭借其新款智能手机在设计上胜过了竞争对手。
例句：She outdesigned everyone in the competition and won first prize. 她在比赛中设计的作品胜过所有人，并获得了一等奖。
近义词：surpass, outdo, eclipse
反义词：be inferior to, be outdone by
词性: v.

outdesign:/ˌaʊt.dɪˈzaɪn/
noun
更优越的设计
a superior design
例句：The new car's outdesign was praised by critics. 这款新车的卓越设计受到了评论家的赞扬。
例句：The company's outdesign gave them a competitive edge.  公司的优秀设计赋予了他们竞争优势。
近义词：superior design, better design
反义词：inferior design, worse design
词性: n.
