convector:/kənˈvɛktər/ | n. | 对流式加热器; 散热器 | A device that heats a room by warming the air and causing it to circulate. |  The convector quickly warmed up the chilly room. 对流式加热器很快使寒冷的房间暖和起来。| synonyms: heater, radiator | antonyms: cooler, air conditioner | noun

convectors:/kənˈvɛktərz/ | n. | 对流式加热器; 散热器(复数) |  Multiple devices that heat a room by warming the air and causing it to circulate. |  The convectors in the new building are very efficient. 新大楼里的散热器非常高效。 | synonyms: heaters, radiators | antonyms: coolers, air conditioners | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Latin "convehere" (to carry together), which combines "con-" (together, with) and "vehere" (to carry).  This refers to the way convectors carry heat through the air.
