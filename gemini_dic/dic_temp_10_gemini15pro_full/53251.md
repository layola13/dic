DIRECTV:/dəˈrɛktˌtiːviː/ /daɪˈrɛktˌtiːviː/
proper n.|DirecTV|A direct broadcast satellite service provider.|We just switched to DIRECTV. 我们刚刚换成了DirecTV。|近义词:Dish Network, cable television|反义词: terrestrial television|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
DIRECTV is a portmanteau of "direct" and "TV".  "Direct" refers to the direct-to-home satellite broadcasting technology, and "TV" is short for television.
