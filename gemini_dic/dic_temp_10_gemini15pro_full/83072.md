gurniad:/ˈɡɜːrniæd/| n.|鬼脸，怪相|A distorted facial expression, often grotesque or comical, typically made to amuse or shock.  A grimace, a face pulled in a playful or teasing way.|He made a gurniad at the baby to make her laugh. 他对婴儿做了个鬼脸，想逗她笑。|近义词：grimace, mug, face|反义词:smile|词性:noun
Etymology: From Scots *girn, to show the teeth, grin.
