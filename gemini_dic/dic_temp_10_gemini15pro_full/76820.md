ganache:/ɡəˈnɑːʃ/ | n. | 甘纳许 |A rich cream made of chocolate and heavy cream, used as a glaze or filling for pastries. | The chef poured the warm ganache over the cake.  厨师将温热的甘纳许淋在蛋糕上。 | The baker used a dark chocolate ganache to fill the truffles.  面包师用黑巧克力甘纳许填充松露巧克力。 | 近义词: frosting, icing | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

词源: French, from ganache 'blockhead,' perhaps from ganache 'clumsy.'  (法语，源自 ganache “笨蛋”，可能来自 ganache “笨拙的”。)
