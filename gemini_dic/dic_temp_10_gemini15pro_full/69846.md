ferriprussic: /ˌfɛriˈprʌsɪk/
adjective
铁氰的，亚铁氰化铁的
Containing or relating to the ferricyanide anion, [Fe(CN)6]3−.  Specifically, it refers to compounds containing iron in the +3 oxidation state complexed with cyanide. Often used interchangeably (though less correctly) with ferricyanide.
Potassium ferriprussic is a deep red crystalline solid.  亚铁氰化钾是一种深红色的结晶固体。
The ferriprussic acid is unstable in solution. 铁氰酸在溶液中不稳定。
Synonym: ferricyanide
Antonym: ferrocyanide
词性: adjective
