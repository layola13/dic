bucholzite:/ˈbuːkˌhɔːlzaɪt/
n.|硅线石|A fibrous variety of sillimanite, Al2SiO5, usually found in metamorphic rocks.|Bucholzite is often found in schists and gneisses. 硅线石经常出现在片岩和片麻岩中。|Synonyms: sillimanite, fibrolite|Antonyms: None|Noun

bucholzite:/ˈbuːkˌhɔːlzaɪt/
n.|硅线石|A mineral species belonging to the sillimanite group, typically occurring as fibrous aggregates.|Bucholzite can be used as a refractory material. 硅线石可以用作耐火材料。|Synonyms: sillimanite, fibrolite|Antonyms: None|Noun
