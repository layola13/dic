atrociously:/əˈtroʊʃəsli/
adverb
极其恶劣地，残暴地
in a terribly wicked or cruel way; outrageously badly
The play was atrociously acted and poorly directed.  这出戏演得极其糟糕，导演也很差。
The prisoners were atrociously treated. 囚犯受到了残暴的对待。
近义词：terribly, awfully, dreadfully, appallingly, horribly, shockingly
反义词：wonderfully, excellently, superbly
词性：副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From atrocious + -ly.  Atrocious comes from French atroce, from Latin atrōx "fierce, cruel, savage," figuratively "horrible, frightful," from ater "black, dark, gloomy."
