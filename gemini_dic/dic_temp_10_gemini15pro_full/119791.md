migrated:/ˈmaɪɡreɪtɪd/
verb
迁移，移居，(鸟类等)迁徙
past tense and past participle of migrate, meaning to move from one place to another.
Many birds have migrated south for the winter. 许多鸟已经南迁过冬。
The company migrated its servers to the cloud.  公司将其服务器迁移到云端。

近义词:moved, relocated, transferred, emigrated, immigrated
反义词:stayed, remained, settled

词性:verb


词源:From Latin migratus, past participle of migrare "to change one's abode, depart," from migrare "to move from one place to another."
