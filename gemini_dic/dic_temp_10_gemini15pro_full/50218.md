depasture:/dɪˈpæstʃər/ /diˈpæstʃər/ | vt. n.| 放牧，吃草；享受; 牧场|To graze; to feed on growing grass; to consume vegetation as food.  (verb) A pasture. (noun) | The shepherd depastured his flock on the hillside. 牧羊人在山坡上放牧他的羊群。| synonyms: graze, feed, browse, pasture | antonyms: starve, deprive (of food) | verb

depasture:/dɪˈpæstʃər/ /diˈpæstʃər/ | n.| 牧场|A field or area of land covered with grass or other vegetation suitable for grazing animals. | The cows were contentedly grazing in the depasture.  奶牛们心满意足地在牧场上吃草。| synonyms: pasture, meadow, grassland, grazing land | antonyms: barren land, desert | noun
