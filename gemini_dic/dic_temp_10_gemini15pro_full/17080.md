batiste:/bæˈtiːst/ /ˈbætɪst/| n.|巴迪斯布, 平纹细麻布|A fine, plain-woven fabric, typically linen or cotton, known for its lightweight and smooth texture.  Originally from Cambrai, France.|She wore a beautiful batiste dress to the summer garden party. 她穿着一件漂亮的巴迪斯布连衣裙去参加夏季花园派对。|近义词：cambric, lawn, voile|反义词:None|noun
