caduceus:/kəˈdjuːsiəs/
noun
双蛇杖；墨丘利神杖；（尤指美国）医学的象征
a staff with two snakes twined around it and two wings at the top, carried by Hermes/Mercury and traditionally associated with healing, and used as a symbol of medicine, especially in the US.
The caduceus is often mistakenly used as a symbol of medicine.  双蛇杖经常被误用作医学的象征。
The hospital's logo features a caduceus.  这家医院的标志以双蛇杖为特色。
synonyms:staff of Hermes, rod of Asclepius
antonyms: (No direct antonym, but the Rod of Asclepius is the correct symbol for medicine and could be considered a contrasting symbol)
