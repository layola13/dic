non-alluvial:/ˌnɒn əˈluːviəl/
adjective
非冲积的
Not composed of or deposited by alluvium (sediment deposited by flowing water).
The non-alluvial soils of the region are less fertile than the alluvial plains.  这个地区的非冲积土壤不如冲积平原肥沃。
The vineyard was planted on a steep, non-alluvial slope. 这片葡萄园种植在一个陡峭的非冲积斜坡上。
Synonyms: non-sedimentary, upland
Antonyms: alluvial, sedimentary
词性: adjective
