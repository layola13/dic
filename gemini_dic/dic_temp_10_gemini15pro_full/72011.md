flints:/flɪnts/| n.|燧石; 打火石; (旧式枪支的) 击锤;|A piece of hard, gray stone that produces sparks when struck against steel, formerly used to start fires.|例句：He struck the flints together to make a fire. 他敲击燧石生火。

例句：The early humans used flints to make tools. 早期人类用燧石制作工具。|近义词：stones, rocks, sparks|反义词:tinder (引火物, 虽然不是完全的反义词, 但在生火场景中是相对的概念)|词性:名词
