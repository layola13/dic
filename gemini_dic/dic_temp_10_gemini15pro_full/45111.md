cuckooing:/ˈkʊkuːɪŋ/
n.
杜鹃式寄生，鸠占鹊巢(指犯罪团伙利用弱势群体住所进行犯罪活动)
The act of taking over a vulnerable person's home and using it as a base for criminal activity, typically drug dealing.
The police are trying to tackle the growing problem of cuckooing. 警方正在努力解决日益严重的“鸠占鹊巢”问题。
exploitation, takeover, infiltration
protection, liberation
noun
