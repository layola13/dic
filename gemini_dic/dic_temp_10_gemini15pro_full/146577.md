overgratifies:/ˌoʊvərˈɡrætɪfaɪz/
verb
过分满足，过度纵容
to gratify (someone or something) to excess
例句：Overgratifying a child can lead to spoiled behavior. 过度满足孩子会导致他们被宠坏。

例句：He overgratified his desire for sweets and ended up with a stomachache. 他过度满足了自己对甜食的渴望，结果胃疼了。
近义词：spoil, indulge, pamper, coddle
反义词：deprive, neglect, restrain
词性：v.
