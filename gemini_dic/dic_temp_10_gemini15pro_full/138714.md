nullities: /nʌˈlɪtiz/ | n. | 无效；空虚；无价值的事物 | The contract was declared a nullity due to a technical error.  该合同由于技术错误而被宣布无效。 | The judge ruled that the marriage was a nullity. 法官裁定该婚姻无效。 | 近义词: invalidity, voidness, nothingness | 反义词: validity, legality | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *nullite*, from Old French *nullite*, from Latin *nullitās*, from *nullus* ("not any, no").
