groundsmen: /ˈɡraʊndzmən/ (UK), /ˈɡraʊndzmən/ (US)
| n. |场地管理员，场工|A person who takes care of a sports ground, especially a cricket or football pitch. |The groundsmen were busy preparing the pitch for the upcoming match. 场地管理员正忙着为即将到来的比赛准备场地。|  The groundsmen mowed the lawn meticulously.  场工一丝不苟地修剪草坪。|近义词：groundkeeper, caretaker|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps vandal if considering the act of damaging the ground)
