deliber:/dɪˈlɪbər/| v. |仔细考虑，商议|To think about or discuss something very carefully in order to make a decision.|The jury deliberated for two days before reaching a verdict. 陪审团商议了两天，才做出裁决。|近义词：consider, ponder, contemplate, discuss|反义词：rush, hasten|词性:动词
deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbərɪt/| adj. |故意的，深思熟虑的|Done or said on purpose; carefully thought out.|The attack was a deliberate act of terrorism. 这次袭击是一次蓄意的恐怖行为。|近义词：intentional, purposeful, conscious|反义词：accidental, unintentional, impulsive|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin  _dēlīberāre_ "to weigh, ponder," from _de- "from" + _lībra_ "balance, scales."
