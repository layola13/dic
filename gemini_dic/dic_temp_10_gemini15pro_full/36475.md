colead:/ˈkoʊliːd/| n. v.|共同领导，共同主持人|A person who shares leadership or主持 responsibilities with another.|例句：The project had two coleads to ensure its success. 这个项目有两个共同负责人以确保其成功。|例句：They decided to colead the workshop. 他们决定共同主持研讨会。|近义词：co-leader, joint leader, shared leader|反义词:follower, subordinate|词性:名词,动词

词源: co- (together) + lead (leader)
