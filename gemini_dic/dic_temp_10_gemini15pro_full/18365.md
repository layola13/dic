bellwether:/ˈbelˌweðər/ (US), /ˈbelˌweðə/ (UK)| n.|领头羊；前导；风向标|A bellwether is a leading indicator of future trends, often in a particular industry or market. It can also refer to a male sheep that leads the flock, often wearing a bell around its neck.|例句：The performance of the housing market is often seen as a bellwether for the overall economy. 房地产市场的表现通常被视为整体经济的风向标。

例句：Tech stocks are often considered a bellwether for the stock market as a whole. 科技股通常被认为是整个股市的领头羊。|近义词：leader, indicator, trendsetter, barometer|反义词：follower, laggard|词性: 名词
