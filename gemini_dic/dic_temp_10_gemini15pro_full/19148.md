bevvies:/ˈbɛviz/
n.
alcoholic drinks; beverages
An informal, chiefly British term for alcoholic drinks.
例句：Fancy a few bevvies after work? 下班后想喝几杯吗？

例句：We grabbed some bevvies from the off-licence for the party. 我们从非持牌酒商那里买了一些酒来参加派对。

近义词：drinks, booze, alcohol, refreshments
反义词:None (lack of drinks is not a direct antonym)
词性:Noun
