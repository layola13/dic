clarification:/ˌklærɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 说明，澄清，净化 | the act of making something clearer or easier to understand |  The government issued a clarification of its policy on immigration. 政府就其移民政策发布了说明。 | explanation, elucidation, clarification | obfuscation, confusion | noun

clarify:/ˈklærɪfaɪ/ | v. | 阐明，澄清 | to make something clearer or easier to understand | Could you clarify your earlier statement? 你能澄清一下你之前的声明吗？ | explain, elucidate, illuminate | confuse, obscure | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *clarificatio, from Latin clarificare "to make clear," from clarus "clear" + -ficare "-fy."
