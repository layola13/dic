clavicorn: /kləˈvɪkɔːrn/ | adj. n. | 棒角的；棒角甲虫 | Having or relating to clubbed antennae (antennae that thicken gradually toward the tip).  Also refers to a beetle with such antennae. | Example: The clavicorn beetle is easily recognizable by its distinctive antennae. 棒角甲虫很容易通过其独特的触角识别。| Example: The entomologist identified the insect as a clavicorn species. 昆虫学家将这种昆虫鉴定为棒角甲虫。| 近义词: club-horned | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider insects with different antenna types like 'serricorn' - saw-horned, 'lamellate' - plated antennae) | 词性: adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "clava" (club) + "cornu" (horn)
