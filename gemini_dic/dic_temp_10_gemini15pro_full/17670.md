becomed:/bɪˈkʌmd/ | v. (archaic) | 变得，成为 | This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English.  It was the past participle of "become" in older forms of the language. | He becomed a knight. (Archaic - He became a knight.) 他成为了一名骑士。| 近义词: become, gotten (US), got (UK) | 反义词:  None (as it's a past participle, not easily negated) | verb
