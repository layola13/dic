ballast:/ˈbæləst/ (US), /ˈbæləst/ (UK)| n. v.|压舱物，镇流器；使稳定，使平衡|Heavy material, such as gravel, sand, or iron, placed low in a vessel to improve its stability.  Also, a device that limits the current in an electrical circuit.|The ship took on ballast in the harbor.  这艘船在港口装载了压舱物。|synonyms: counterweight, stabilizer, weighting agent|antonyms: instability, imbalance|noun

ballast:/ˈbæləst/ (US), /ˈbæləst/ (UK)| v.|使稳定，使平衡；给…加压舱物|To provide or fill with ballast.|They ballasted the ship with rocks.  他们用石头给船加压舱物。|synonyms: stabilize, steady, balance|antonyms: destabilize, unbalance|verb
