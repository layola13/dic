consolatoriness:/kənˌsɒləˈtɔːrɪnɪs/
|n.|安慰，慰藉|The quality of providing comfort or solace. It refers to the state or property of being consolatory.|The consolatoriness of her presence was palpable. 她出现带来的慰藉是显而易见的。|近义词: comfort, solace, consolation|反义词: distress, misery, sorrow|词性:Noun

Etymology:
From "consolatory" + "-ness".  "Consolatory" comes from Late Latin "consolatorius," from Latin "consolari" (to console), from "con-" (with) + "solari" (to comfort).
