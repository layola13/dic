palimony:/ˈpælɪˌmoʊni/| n.| 姘居关系；类似婚姻的同居关系|A relationship resembling marriage but without a legal basis, especially one involving cohabitation.| After living together for ten years, their palimony ended acrimoniously.  他们同居十年后，他们的姘居关系不欢而散。| 近义词：cohabitation, common-law marriage| 反义词：marriage| 词性：名词

palimony:/ˈpælɪˌmoʊni/| n.| 共同生活财产；(姘居关系结束时)一方对另一方财产的共有权要求| The property accumulated by non-married partners in a relationship resembling marriage, or the claim by one partner against the other for a share of it.|  She sued him for palimony after their breakup.  他们分手后，她起诉他要求分割共同生活财产。| 近义词：joint property, shared assets| 反义词：separate property| 词性：名词
