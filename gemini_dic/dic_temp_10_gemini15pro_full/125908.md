muse:/mjuːz/| n. vi.|缪斯（希腊神话中掌管艺术和科学的九位女神之一）;  沉思; 灵感来源|A source of inspiration, especially in artistic or creative endeavors.  To think about something carefully and thoughtfully.|例句：The writer's muse was a mysterious woman he met in a cafe.  这位作家的灵感来源是他咖啡馆里遇到的一个神秘女子。|近义词：inspiration, contemplation, meditate, ponder|反义词:distraction|词性:noun, verb

muse:/mjuːz/| vi.|沉思，冥想|To think about something carefully and thoughtfully.|例句：He mused on the meaning of life. 他沉思着人生的意义。|近义词：meditate, ponder, contemplate, reflect|反义词:ignore, neglect|词性:verb
