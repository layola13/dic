deflater:/dɪˈfleɪtər/| n.|放气装置，压缩器|A device or program that deflates or compresses something, especially data or a file.|The programmer used a deflater to reduce the size of the image file.|近义词:compressor|反义词:inflater|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From deflate + -er.  Deflate comes from de- (down, away) + flare (to blow). Ultimately from Latin *flare, from fla- (to blow)
