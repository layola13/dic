engraftation:/ɪnˌɡræfˈteɪʃən/
n.
移植，嫁接；(思想、习惯等的)灌输
The process of grafting something, such as skin or a plant, onto something else; the process of establishing something firmly.
例句：The engraftation of the new skin was successful. 新皮肤的移植很成功。

例句：The engraftation of democratic ideals into the society took many years.  民主理念融入社会花费了多年时间。

近义词：implantation, transplantation, insertion, inculcation, instillation
反义词：removal, excision, expurgation
词性: 名词
