crowbarring:/ˈkroʊˌbɑːrɪŋ/
n.|撬锁；强行闯入；强行插入|The act of forcing something open or into place with a crowbar.  强行用撬棍打开或插入某物。|Burglars gained entry by crowbarring the back door.  窃贼撬开后门进入。|近义词:forcing, levering, prying|反义词:N/A|词性:noun (gerund or present participle)
crowbar:/ˈkroʊˌbɑːr/
n.|撬棍|A heavy iron bar with a flattened end, used as a lever.|He used a crowbar to open the crate. 他用撬棍打开了板条箱。|近义词:lever, pry bar|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
crowbar:/ˈkroʊˌbɑːr/
vt.|用撬棍撬开；强行插入|To force open or into place with a crowbar.|They crowbarred the door open. 他们用撬棍撬开了门。|近义词:force, pry, lever|反义词:N/A|词性:verb
