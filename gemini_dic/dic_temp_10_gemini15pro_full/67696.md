exulcerative:/ɪkˈsʌlsəreɪtɪv/
adjective
溃疡性的; 导致溃疡的
Causing, tending to cause, or characterized by ulceration.  Relating to or involving the formation of ulcers.
例句：Exulcerative colitis is a chronic inflammatory disease of the colon. 溃疡性结肠炎是一种结肠的慢性炎症性疾病。

例句：The doctor diagnosed the wound as exulcerative. 医生诊断伤口为溃疡性。
近义词：ulcerating, ulcerous, erosive
反义词：healing, restorative
词性: adjective
