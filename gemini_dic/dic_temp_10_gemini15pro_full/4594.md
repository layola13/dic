alcoometry:/ˌælkəˈɒmɪtri/ | n. | 酒精测定法 | The science or process of determining the alcoholic strength of liquids. |  Alcoometry is crucial in the production of alcoholic beverages. 酒精测定法在酒精饮料的生产中至关重要。

Alcoometry helps ensure consistent alcohol content in wine. 酒精测定法有助于确保葡萄酒中酒精含量的稳定。 | 近义词: alcoholmetry | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源:  From French *alcooométrie*, from alcool (“alcohol”) + -métrie (“-metry”). Ultimately from Arabic ﺍﻟﻜﺤﻮﻝ (al-kuḥūl, “kohl”).
