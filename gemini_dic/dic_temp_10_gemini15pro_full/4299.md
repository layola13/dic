aisleless:/ˈaɪl.ləs/
adjective
无通道的，没有走道的
Having no aisles.
The aisleless auditorium made it difficult to reach the seats in the middle rows.  无通道的礼堂使得很难到达中间排的座位。
The warehouse was an aisleless expanse, filled with boxes stacked haphazardly.  这个仓库是一片没有通道的广阔空间，堆满了随意堆放的箱子。
Near synonyms: unobstructed, open-plan
Antonyms: aisled
