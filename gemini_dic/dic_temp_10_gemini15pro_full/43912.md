crevasses:/krɪˈvæsɪz/| n. |裂缝，裂隙|A deep open crack, especially in a glacier or snowfield.|例句：The climbers navigated carefully around the dangerous crevasses. 登山者小心翼翼地在危险的裂缝周围航行。|近义词：fissures, cracks, chasms|反义词:ridges, mounds|词性:名词

crevasse:/krɪˈvæs/| n. |裂缝，裂隙|A deep open crack, especially in a glacier or snowfield.|例句：The earthquake created a massive crevasse in the earth. 地震在地球上造成了一条巨大的裂缝。|近义词：fissure, crack, chasm|反义词:ridge, mound|词性:名词
