cambric:/ˈkeɪmbrɪk/ | n. adj.|  细棉布，亚麻布;  用细棉布做的|A fine, closely woven white linen or cotton fabric.| She wore a dress of the finest cambric. 她穿了一件最好的细棉布连衣裙。|Synonyms: linen, cotton, muslin, batiste |Antonyms: coarse cloth, burlap| noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *cambrais*, from Cambrai, a city in northern France where the fabric was originally made.
