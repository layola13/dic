overknowing:/ˌoʊvərˈnoʊɪŋ/ | adj. | 过于自信的，自以为是的，故作聪明的 | Affecting to know more than one does; pretentious in one's knowledge. | Example: He had an overknowing smirk that irritated everyone. 他脸上挂着一种自以为是的假笑，惹恼了所有人。 | Example:  Her overknowing tone made it difficult to have a productive conversation. 她那自以为是的语气使得很难进行有效的对话。 | 近义词: pretentious, arrogant, condescending, superior, smug | 反义词: humble, modest, unassuming | 词性: adjective

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "knowing" (present participle of "know").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "knowing," suggesting an excessive or exaggerated display of knowledge.
