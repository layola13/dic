anabaptistry:/ˌænəˈbæptistri/ | n. | 再洗礼堂，施洗所 | A building or part of a church used for baptism by immersion. |  例句：The anabaptistry was a separate structure, octagonal in shape.  再洗礼堂是一个独立的建筑，呈八角形。

例句：The priest led the initiates into the anabaptistry for the ceremony.  神父带领初学者进入施洗所进行仪式。| 近义词：baptistery | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *anabaptisterium, from Greek anabaptizein "to rebaptize."  Ana- (again) + baptizein (to baptize).
