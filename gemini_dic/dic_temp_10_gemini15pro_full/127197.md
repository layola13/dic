naturistically:/ˌnætʃərˈɪstɪkli/
adverb
自然主义地
in a way that is based on the simple observation of nature, without moral or religious considerations
例句：They raised their children naturistically, allowing them to explore the world around them. 他们以自然主义的方式抚养孩子，让他们探索周围的世界。

例句：The artist painted the scene naturistically, capturing the beauty of the landscape.  这位艺术家以自然主义的方式描绘了这个场景，捕捉了风景的美丽。

近义词：naturally, simply, realistically
反义词：artificially, unnaturally, synthetically
词性：adverb
