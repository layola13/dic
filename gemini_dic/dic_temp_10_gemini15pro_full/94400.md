impli:/ɪmˈplaɪ/ | v. |暗示，意味|To suggest or hint at something indirectly; to express something in an indirect way.|Her silence implied consent. 她的沉默暗示了同意。| The dark clouds implied rain was coming. 乌云暗示着即将下雨。|近义词：suggest, hint, intimate|反义词：state, declare|词性:动词
