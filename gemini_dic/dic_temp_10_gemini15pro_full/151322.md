parishes:/ˈpærɪʃɪz/
n.
堂区；教区
An administrative district typically having its own church and priest or minister.
The church serves several different parishes. 这座教堂服务于几个不同的教区。

parish:/ˈpærɪʃ/
n.
堂区；教区
An administrative district typically having its own church and priest or minister.
He was born in the parish of St. Mary's. 他出生在圣玛丽教区。
近义词:diocese, community, congregation
反义词:None

Etymology:
From Old French *paroisse*, from Late Latin paroecia “diocese, parish”, from Ancient Greek παροικία (paroikía, “sojourning in a foreign land, a dwelling-place abroad”), from πάροικος (pároikos, “dwelling beside, neighboring”), from παρά (pará, “beside, near”) + οἶκος (oîkos, “house”).
