inculpatory:/ɪnˈkʌlpətɔːri/
adjective
控告的；归罪的；证明有罪的
Implying guilt; incriminating.
The prosecutor presented inculpatory evidence against the defendant.  检察官提出了控告被告的证据。
The discovery of the murder weapon in his car was inculpatory. 在他的车里发现凶器对他很不利。
Synonyms: incriminating, accusatory, implicating
Antonyms: exculpatory, absolving, vindicating
词性: adjective
