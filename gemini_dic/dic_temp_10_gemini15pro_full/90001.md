horticulturally:/ˌhɔːrtɪˈkʌltʃərəli/
adverb|园艺地；园艺学地|In a way that relates to horticulture; from the perspective of horticulture. |The gardens were horticulturally diverse, boasting a wide variety of plant species.  这些花园在园艺方面种类繁多，拥有各种各样的植物种类。|例句：The property is horticulturally significant because of its rare collection of orchids.  由于其稀有的兰花收藏，该房产在园艺方面意义重大。|近义词：agriculturally, botanically|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'wildly' or 'naturally' in certain contexts)|词性:adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From horticulture + -ally.  Horticulture comes from the Latin hortus "garden" + cultura "cultivation."
