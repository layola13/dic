otologist:/ˌoʊtɑːˈlɑːdʒɪst/ | n. |耳科医生|A physician who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the ear. |例句：I was referred to an otologist because of my persistent ear infections.  我因为持续的耳部感染被转诊到了一位耳科医生。

例句：The otologist performed surgery to repair my eardrum. 耳科医生为我做了手术来修复我的耳膜。|近义词：ear specialist, ear doctor |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From oto- + -logist.  Oto- comes from Greek *ōt-, *ōtos 'ear'. -logist means "one who studies" or "specialist".
