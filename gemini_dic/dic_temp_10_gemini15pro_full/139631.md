occluded:/əˈkluːdɪd/
adjective
闭塞的，堵塞的
blocked or obstructed in some way.
The artery was occluded by a blood clot.  这条动脉被血块堵塞了。
近义词:blocked, obstructed, closed
反义词:open, clear, unobstructed
词性:adjective

occluded:/əˈkluːdɪd/
adjective
（牙齿）咬合不正的
(of teeth) not properly aligned when the jaws are closed.
He has an occluded bite. 他咬合不正。
近义词:maloccluded, misaligned
反义词:aligned
词性:adjective

occluded:/əˈkluːdɪd/
adjective
遮挡的;被…遮挡的
hidden from view; concealed.
The moon occluded the sun during the eclipse.  月食期间，月亮遮住了太阳。
近义词:obscured, hidden, concealed
反义词:visible, exposed, revealed
词性:adjective


词源: From Latin occludere "to shut up, close up, enclose," from ob "against, in front of, toward" (see ob-) + claudere "to shut"
