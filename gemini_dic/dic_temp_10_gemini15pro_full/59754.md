economical:/ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪkl/ /ˌɛkəˈnɑːmɪkl/| adj.|经济的，节俭的，合算的|Giving or using the minimum amount of money, time, or resources necessary, avoiding waste.|例句：It's more economical to buy in bulk.  批量购买更划算。|近义词：thrifty, frugal, budget-friendly, cost-effective|反义词：expensive, extravagant, wasteful|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin oeconomicus, from Greek oikonomikos "of household management," from oikonomia "household management," from oikos "house" + nemein "to manage."
