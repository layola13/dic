comtist: /ˈkɒmtɪst/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmtist/ (US)| n. |孔德主义者；实证主义者|A follower of the philosophy of positivism as developed by Auguste Comte.| He was a staunch comtist, believing that scientific observation was the only path to true knowledge. 他是一位坚定的孔德主义者，相信科学观察是通往真知的唯一途径。|Synonyms: positivist, empiricist|Antonyms: idealist, metaphysician|Noun
