overlooseness: /ˌoʊvərˈluːsnəs/
n.
过度松弛；过度宽松
The overlooseness of the regulations allowed for too much ambiguity.  这些规定的过度宽松导致了太多的含糊不清。
The overlooseness of the soil made it difficult to build on. 土壤过度松弛，难以在其上建造房屋。
Etymology:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "loose" (adjective meaning "not tight") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)  "over-"（前缀，意为“过度”）+ "loose"（形容词，意为“不紧”）+ "-ness"（后缀，由形容词构成名词）
Synonyms: laxity, slackness, looseness, flexibility
Antonyms: tightness, firmness, rigidity, strictness
