choked:/tʃoʊkt/| v. adj.|窒息，哽咽，堵塞，抑制|To have difficulty breathing because your throat is blocked, or to cause someone to have this difficulty; also can mean suppressed or inhibited.|例句：He choked on a piece of bread. 他被一块面包噎住了。|近义词：suffocated, gagged, stifled, suppressed|反义词:breathed, expressed, released|词性:动词

choked:/tʃoʊkt/| adj.|窒息的，哽咽的，堵塞的|Describing something that is blocked, obstructed, or suppressed.|例句：The drain was choked with leaves. 排水沟被树叶堵塞了。|近义词：blocked, clogged, obstructed, suppressed|反义词:clear, open, unobstructed|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *choken*, from Old English *cēocian* (to choke), from Proto-Germanic *kēkōną (to suffocate, choke).  Cognate with Dutch *kichen* (to pant, gasp), German *keuchen* (to gasp, wheeze).
