blackmailers:/ˈblækˌmeɪlərz/
n.
勒索者；敲诈者
People who extort money or something else of value from a person by threats of exposing a criminal offense or discreditable information.
The blackmailers threatened to release the compromising photos unless they were paid a hefty sum. 勒索者威胁说，如果不付给他们一大笔钱，他们就会公布那些有损名誉的照片。

The journalist was approached by blackmailers who claimed to have incriminating evidence against him.  一名记者被勒索者找上门，他们声称掌握了对他不利的证据。


近义词:extortionists, racketeers
反义词:philanthropists, benefactors
词性:noun (plural)
