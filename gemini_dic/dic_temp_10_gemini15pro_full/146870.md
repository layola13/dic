overinflationary: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪʃəˌneri/ /ˌəʊvərɪnˈfleɪʃənəri/
adjective
过度通货膨胀的
Characterized by or causing excessive inflation.  Relating to or involving inflation of an excessive degree.
Example: The overinflationary policies of the government led to a sharp decline in the value of the currency. 政府过度通胀的政策导致货币价值急剧下降。

Example:  Economists warned about the dangers of overinflationary pressures on the economy. 经济学家警告了经济过度通胀压力的危险。

Synonym: hyperinflationary, excessively inflationary
Antonym: deflationary, disinflationary
词性: 形容词
