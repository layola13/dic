bespeaking:/bɪˈspiːkɪŋ/
v.|预示;表示;表明;证明;显示;订购;预订|
The clouds bespeak rain. 这些云预示着要下雨。|
His elegant attire bespoke a man of refined tastes. 他优雅的着装表明他是一个品味高雅的人。|
近义词:indicate, suggest, signify, order, reserve|
反义词: belie, contradict|
词性:动词

bespeaking:/bɪˈspiːkɪŋ/
n.|预订;显示;表明|
The bespeaking of the hotel rooms was done months in advance.  酒店房间的预订是几个月前完成的。|
The bespeaking of his anxiety was evident in his fidgeting. 他焦虑的表现很明显，因为他坐立不安。|
近义词: indication, suggestion, reservation |
反义词: concealment|
词性:名词
