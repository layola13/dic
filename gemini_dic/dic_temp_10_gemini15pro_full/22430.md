booked-out:/bʊkt ˈaʊt/
adjective
客满的；预订一空的
fully reserved or booked; having no vacancies
The hotel was completely booked-out for the weekend.  这家酒店周末全部客满了。
sold out, full
available, vacant
词性:形容词

booked-out:/bʊkt ˈaʊt/
verb
预订满
to reserve all available accommodations, tickets, etc.
The concert was booked-out within hours of tickets going on sale.  演唱会的门票在开售几小时内就被预订一空了。
reserve, sell out
cancel
词性:动词
