hookedness:/ˈhʊktnɪs/| n.|着迷，上瘾，入迷|The state of being hooked or addicted to something.  This refers to the degree to which someone is captivated or fascinated by something.|I can't explain the hookedness of this game; I just can't stop playing it. 我无法解释我对这款游戏的着迷，我就是停不下来。|近义词：addiction, obsession, fascination, captivation|反义词:indifference, apathy|词性:名词

词源: From "hooked" + "-ness".  "Hooked" originates from the image of being caught by a hook, implying being firmly attached or captivated. "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns expressing a state or quality.
