nonaxiomatical:/ˌnɒnˌæksiəˈmætɪkl/
adjective
非公理的
Not based on or derived from axioms; not self-evident or universally accepted as true.
This theory rests on a few nonaxiomatical assumptions.  这个理论基于一些非公理的假设。
His argument was deemed nonaxiomatical and therefore open to debate. 他的论点被认为是非公理的，因此可以公开辩论。
synonym:non-axiomatic, unprovable, undemonstrable
antonym:axiomatic, self-evident, demonstrable
词性:adjective
