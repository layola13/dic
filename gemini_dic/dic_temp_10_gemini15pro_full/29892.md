cauterises:/ˈkɔːtəraɪzɪz/
verb
烧灼；烙(伤口等)
burns (a wound or snakebite) with a hot iron or caustic substance to stop bleeding and prevent infection.
The doctor cauterised the wound to stop the bleeding. 医生烧灼伤口以止血。
Synonyms: sear, burn, scorch
Antonyms: heal, soothe
词性: 动词
