outplacement:/ˌaʊtˈpleɪsmənt/
n.
失业安置，转职咨询
The process of helping redundant employees find new jobs, often with the assistance of a specialist consultancy.
The company offers generous outplacement services to its laid-off workers.  这家公司为被解雇的员工提供优厚的转职安置服务。
近义词：career counseling, job placement, transition assistance
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "unemployment")
