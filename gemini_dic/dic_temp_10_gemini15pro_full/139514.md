obstupefactive:/ˌɒbstjʊˈpiːfæktɪv/| adj.|使人目瞪口呆的，令人惊愕的|Causing astonishment; stunning.|The magician's sudden disappearance was an obstupefactive feat.  魔术师突然消失是一个令人惊愕的壮举。

The news of the lottery win was obstupefactive.  彩票中奖的消息令人目瞪口呆。|近义词：astonishing, stunning, amazing, breathtaking, astounding|反义词：unimpressive, underwhelming, boring, mundane|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin obstupēfactus, past participle of obstupēfacere (“to amaze, stun”), from ob- (“against, towards”) + stupēfacere (“to stun, stupefy”), from stupēre (“to be stunned”) + facere (“to make, do”).  Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European *stupe- (“to be stiff, numb”).
