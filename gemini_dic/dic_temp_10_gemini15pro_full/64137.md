equably:/ˈiːkwəbli/| adv.|平静地；均匀地；公平地|In a calm, steady, and unvarying manner; evenly or uniformly; impartially or fairly.|He distributed the workload equably among his team. 他公平地将工作量分配给他的团队。|近义词：calmly, evenly, fairly, uniformly, steadily, consistently|反义词：unevenly, unfairly, erratically, fitfully|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin aequabilis, from Latin aequus (“equal, level, fair, calm”) + -abilis (suffix forming adjectives meaning “capable of being, worthy of being, tending to”).
