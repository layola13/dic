daoism:/ˈdaʊɪzəm/| n. |道教|A philosophical and religious tradition of Chinese origin which emphasizes living in harmony with the Tao (道), meaning "way", "path" or "principle".  It incorporates elements of Confucianism and Buddhism.|例句：Daoism emphasizes living in harmony with nature. 道教强调与自然和谐相处。

例句：Many people practice Daoism for its health benefits. 很多人为了健康而修习道教。|近义词：Taoism|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
