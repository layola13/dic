boscage:/ˈbɒskɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈbɑːskɪdʒ/| n. |树丛，灌木丛，丛林|A mass of trees or shrubs; a thicket. |例句：The birds sought refuge in the dense boscage. 鸟儿们在茂密的树丛中寻求庇护。

例句：The path led through a fragrant boscage of honeysuckle and roses. 小路穿过一片芬芳的金银花和玫瑰丛。|近义词：thicket, undergrowth, copse, woodland, grove|反义词：clearing, meadow, plain|词性: noun

boscage:| Etymology: From Middle French *boscage*, from Old French *boscage*, from *bosc* ("wood"), from Frankish *busk* (compare Dutch *bos*, German *Busch*), from Proto-Germanic *buskaz* ("bush"). |
