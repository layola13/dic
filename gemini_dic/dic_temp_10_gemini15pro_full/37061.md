combatants:/ˈkɒmbətənts/ (UK) /kəmˈbætənts/ (US)| n. | 参战者，战斗员 |  A person or group involved in fighting or conflict, especially war. | The two combatants faced each other across the battlefield.  两个参战者在战场上互相面对。| synonyms: fighters, warriors, soldiers, belligerents | antonyms: civilians, non-combatants | noun

combatants:/ˈkɒmbətənts/ (UK) /kəmˈbætənts/ (US)| n. | 竞争者 |  A person who takes part in a competition or contest. | The combatants in the chess tournament were highly skilled. 国际象棋比赛中的竞争者都非常熟练。 | synonyms: competitors, rivals, contestants | antonyms: allies, partners | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "combatant" (present participle of "combattre" - to fight), from Late Latin "combattere", from Latin "com-" (together) + "battuere" (to beat).
