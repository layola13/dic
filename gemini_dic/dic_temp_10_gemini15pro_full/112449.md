loudest:/ˈlaʊdɪst/| adj.|最响亮的，声音最大的|Superlative form of loud: most loud.  The loudest sound is the one with the highest volume or intensity.|The loudest cheer came from the home team's fans.  主队球迷的欢呼声最大。|Synonyms:  noisiest, strongest, booming, deafening|Antonyms: quietest, softest, faintest|adjective
