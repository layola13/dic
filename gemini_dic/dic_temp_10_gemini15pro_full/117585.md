megalomanias:/ˌmɛɡələˈmeɪniəz/ | n. | 夸大妄想症 | A mental disorder marked by delusions of grandeur, power, wealth, or omnipotence. | He suffers from megalomanias and believes he is the king of the world. 他患有夸大妄想症，认为自己是世界之王。|  Example: His megalomanias led him to believe he could single-handedly solve the world's problems. 他的夸大妄想症让他相信他可以独自解决世界上的所有问题。 | Synonyms: delusions of grandeur, grandiosity | Antonyms: humility, modesty | noun

megalomania:/ˌmɛɡələˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 夸大妄想 | An obsession with the exercise of power, especially in the political sphere.  A mental condition marked by delusions of great personal power and importance. | His megalomania led him to make increasingly reckless decisions. 他的夸大妄想导致他做出越来越鲁莽的决定。 | Example:  The dictator's megalomania was evident in his opulent palace and constant demands for praise.  独裁者的夸大妄想从他奢华的宫殿和不断要求赞扬中可见一斑。| Synonyms: egomania, narcissism, grandiosity | Antonyms: humility, self-doubt, modesty | noun
