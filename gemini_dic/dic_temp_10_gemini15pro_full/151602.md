partiality:/ˌpɑːrʃiˈæləti/ | n. | 偏袒，偏爱；偏心 |  A feeling of liking something or someone more than other things or people. |  She showed a marked partiality for expensive clothes. 她对昂贵的衣服表现出明显的偏爱。 | synonyms: bias, prejudice, favoritism, preference | antonyms: impartiality, fairness, objectivity | noun

partiality:/ˌpɑːrʃiˈæləti/ | n. | 特别的喜爱，偏好 |  A special liking for something. | He has a partiality for spicy food. 他偏爱辛辣食物。| synonyms: fondness, liking, taste, inclination | antonyms: aversion, dislike | noun
