hew:/hjuː/
verb
砍；劈；凿
to shape or smooth something by cutting away material from its surface, especially using an axe or other sharp tool.
He hewed a path through the jungle. 他在丛林中开辟了一条道路。
chop, hack, cleave
construct, build

hew:/hjuː/
noun
砍；劈；凿
the act of hewing something.
The statue was made with a few skillful hews of the chisel. 这座雕像是用凿子巧妙地凿几下完成的。
chop, hack, cut
construction, building

Etymology:
Old English hēawan "to cut, hew, chop," from Proto-Germanic *hauwan, from PIE root *keu- "to beat, strike."
