luncheonettes:/ˌlʌntʃəˈnɛts/
n.
小餐馆，简易午餐店
A small restaurant serving simple lunches.
例句：We grabbed a quick bite at the luncheonette down the street. 我们在街角的小餐馆里随便吃了点东西。

例句：The luncheonette was known for its delicious homemade pies.  这家小餐馆以其美味的自制馅饼而闻名。


近义词：cafe, diner, snack bar

词性：名词
