nonvoluble:/nɑːnˈvɑːljʊbəl/ | adj. | 不健谈的，沉默寡言的 | Not talkative; reticent. |  He was a nonvoluble man, preferring to listen rather than speak. 他是一个不健谈的人，宁愿倾听也不愿说话。

She gave a nonvoluble response, simply nodding her head. 她只是点点头，没有多说什么。 | 近义词: reticent, taciturn, quiet, reserved | 反义词: talkative, loquacious, voluble, garrulous | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *non volubilis*, from *non* (“not”) + *volubilis* (“easily turned, fluent”).  *Volubilis* is derived from *volvere* (“to roll, turn”).
