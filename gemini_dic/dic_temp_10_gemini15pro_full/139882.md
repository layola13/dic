odd: /ɑːd/ (US) /ɒd/ (UK)| adj.|奇怪的，古怪的；奇数的；临时的；剩余的；零散的|Something that is odd is unusual or strange. An odd number is not exactly divisible by two. Odd can also mean occasional or not regular. Finally, odd can refer to remaining or extra things.|例句：He has some odd habits. 他有一些奇怪的习惯。|近义词：strange, unusual, peculiar, weird, bizarre; (for numbers) uneven|反义词：normal, usual, ordinary; (for numbers) even|词性:形容词
