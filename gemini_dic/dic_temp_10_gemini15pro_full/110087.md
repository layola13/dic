lichens:/ˈlaɪkənz/ /ˈlɪtʃənz/| n. |地衣|A complex life form that is a symbiotic partnership of two separate organisms, a fungus and an alga.|Lichens grow on rocks and trees. 地衣生长在岩石和树木上.|Synonym: moss (though not biologically the same), airplant (for certain types) |Antonym: None (no direct opposite for a life form)|Noun


lichens:/ˈlaɪkənz/ /ˈlɪtʃənz/| n. |地衣|A composite organism that arises from algae or cyanobacteria living among filaments of multiple fungi in a symbiotic relationship.|The air quality is so pure here that even rare lichens thrive.  这里的空气质量非常好，即使是稀有的地衣也能茁壮成长。|Synonym: moss (in general appearance, though biologically different) |Antonym: None (no direct opposite for a life form)|Noun
