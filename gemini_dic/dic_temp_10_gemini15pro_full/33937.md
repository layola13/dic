cirrigrade:/ˈsɪrɪˌɡreɪd/
adjective
卷云状的，由卷云形成的
composed of cirrus clouds; resembling cirrus clouds.
Cirrigrade clouds often precede a change in weather. 卷云状的云通常预示着天气变化。
The cirrigrade formations were beautiful against the setting sun.  卷云的形状在夕阳的映衬下很美。

近义词:cirrous, wispy
反义词:cumuliform

词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *cirrigradus (“walking on cirri”), from cirrus (“curl of hair; tendril”) + gradus (“step; gait”).
