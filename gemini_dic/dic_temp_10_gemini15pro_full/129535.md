non-biblical:/ˌnɒnˈbɪblɪkəl/
adjective
非圣经的
Not found in or not derived from the Bible.
The story contains many non-biblical elements.  这个故事包含许多非圣经元素。
His beliefs are based on non-biblical sources. 他的信仰基于非圣经的来源。
近义词：secular, worldly, profane
反义词：biblical, scriptural
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): not
biblical (adjective): relating to the Bible
