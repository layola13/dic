immemorial:/ˌɪməˈmɔːriəl/
adjective
无法追忆的，古老的
reaching back beyond the bounds of memory; extremely old
例句：The custom has existed since time immemorial.  这个习俗自古以来就存在。

例句：These ceremonies have been performed since time immemorial. 这些仪式自古以来就一直在进行。

近义词：ancient, age-old, primeval, prehistoric
反义词：recent, modern, contemporary
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *immemorial, from Latin immemorialis ("that which is beyond memory, immemorial"), from in- ("not") + memor ("mindful, remembering") + -alis (adjective suffix).
