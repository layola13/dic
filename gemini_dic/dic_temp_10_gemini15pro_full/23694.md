brashnesses: /ˈbræʃnɪsɪz/
noun
鲁莽行为；傲慢行为；轻率行为
The plural form of brashness, referring to multiple instances of self-assertive and often noisy or disrespectful behavior.
His youthful brashnesses eventually subsided as he matured.  他年轻时的鲁莽行为随着他成熟而逐渐消失了。
The new recruit's brashnesses alienated his colleagues. 新员工的傲慢行为疏远了他的同事。

近义词: audacities, impertinences, impudences, insolences
反义词:  humility, modesty, politeness, reserve, diffidence


ETYMOLOGY->
From brash + -ness.  Brash itself likely originated as a variant of "brag" or related words, suggesting boastful or showy behavior.
