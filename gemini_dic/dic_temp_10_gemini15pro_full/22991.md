bouffancy:/ˈbuːfənsi/| n. |蓬松，膨胀，夸张|The bouffancy of her skirt made her look like a princess.  她的裙子蓬松得让她看起来像个公主。|例句：The bouffancy of the dress was quite impressive.  这件衣服的蓬松度相当令人印象深刻。

The 18th-century hairstyles were known for their extreme bouffancy. 18世纪的发型以其极端的蓬松而闻名。|近义词：fullness, puffiness, fluffiness|反义词:flatness, sleekness|词性:noun

Etymology: From French *bouffant* (puffing, puffed up), present participle of *bouffer* (to puff, to swell), from Old French *bouffer* (to blow, to puff).
