dawnlike:/ˈdɔːnlaɪk/| adj.|像黎明的，黎明般的|Resembling or evocative of the dawn; having the qualities of dawn, such as soft light and quiet tranquility.|The dawnlike glow in the east promised a beautiful sunrise.  东方黎明的微光预示着一个美丽的日出。|近义词：dawning, early, nascent, incipient|反义词：crepuscular, nocturnal, dusklike|词性:形容词

词源:
From dawn + -like.  由dawn（黎明）和-like（像...的）组成。
