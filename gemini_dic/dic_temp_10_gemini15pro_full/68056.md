facular:/ˈfækjʊlər/| adj.|光斑的，耀斑的|Bright or shining, especially relating to a facula (a bright region on the sun's surface).|The astronomer observed facular brightening near the sunspot. 天文学家观察到太阳黑子附近的光斑变亮。|近义词：bright, luminous, radiant|反义词：dim, dark|词性:形容词

facula:/ˈfækjʊlə/| n.|光斑，耀斑|A bright region on the sun's surface, usually near a sunspot.|Faculae are often associated with increased solar activity. 光斑通常与太阳活动的增加有关。|近义词：bright spot, flare|反义词：sunspot (in a contrasting sense)|词性:名词
