ferricyanic:/ˌfɛrɪsaɪˈænɪk/ | adj. | 铁氰的，高铁氰的 | Containing iron with a valence of three in the anion; specifically, designating the hexacyanoferrate(III) anion or a salt containing it.  Relating to or containing iron in the ferric state, especially in the anion of a compound. | Potassium ferricyanide is a deep red crystalline salt. 高铁氰化钾是一种深红色的结晶盐。| The ferricyanic ion reacts with ferrous ions to form Prussian blue. 铁氰离子与亚铁离子反应生成普鲁士蓝。| 近义词: hexacyanoferrate(III) | 反义词: ferrocyanic | 词性: adjective
