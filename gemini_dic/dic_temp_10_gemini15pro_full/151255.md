parenthetical:/ˌpærənˈθɛtɪkəl/
adjective
插入语的；括号内的
of, relating to, or inserted as a parenthesis
The book contains many parenthetical references to other works. 这本书包含许多对其他作品的插入式引用。
Synonyms: incidental, interjected, supplementary
Antonyms: essential, integral, main
词性: 形容词


parenthetical:/ˌpærənˈθɛtɪkəl/
noun
插入语；括号内的词语
a word, phrase, or sentence that is inserted as an explanation or afterthought into a passage which is grammatically complete without it, in writing usually within parentheses
He added a parenthetical remark about her hairstyle. 他补充了一句关于她发型的插入语。
Synonyms: aside, digression, interjection
Antonyms: main text, body
词性: 名词
