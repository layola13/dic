overbuilding: /ˌoʊvərˈbɪldɪŋ/
n.
过度建设，建造过多
The excessive construction of buildings, especially of a particular type, in a given area, leading to a surplus and potential economic problems.
The city suffered from overbuilding during the boom years, and now there are many vacant apartments.  这个城市在繁荣时期过度建设，现在有很多空置公寓。
oversupply, excess construction
undersupply, shortage of housing
noun
