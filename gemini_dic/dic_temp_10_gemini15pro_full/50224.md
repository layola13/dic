depeculation:/ˌdiːpekjʊˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 盗用公款；挪用公款；贪污 | The act of embezzling or misappropriating funds, especially public funds. |  The mayor was accused of depeculation of city funds. 市长被指控盗用市政资金。|  He lost his job because of depeculation. 他因为挪用公款丢了工作。| 近义词: embezzlement, misappropriation, fraud, theft | 反义词: honesty, integrity | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French dépéculation, from dépéculer "to peculate," from de- "from, away" + pécule "savings, private property," from Latin peculium "private property, savings," from pecu "cattle, flock," originally the individual's share of the common herd.  The notion is of taking money away from the common fund for private use.
