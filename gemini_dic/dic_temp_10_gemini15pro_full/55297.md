adverb
放荡地；放纵地

He lived dissolutely, indulging in every kind of excess. 他过着放荡的生活，沉溺于各种各样的过度行为。

词性: adverb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *dissolutus, past participle of dissolvere ("to dissolve, loosen, untie, disband, destroy, separate").  源自晚期拉丁语 *dissolutus，是 dissolvere（“溶解、放松、解开、解散、摧毁、分离”）的过去分词。
