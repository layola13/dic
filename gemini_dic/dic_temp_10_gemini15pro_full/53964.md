discredits:/dɪˈskredɪts/
verb (third-person singular simple present discredits, present participle discrediting, simple past and past participle discredited)
使丧失信誉，使丢脸
to harm the reputation of, make seem unreliable or untrue
The scandal discredited the politician. 这桩丑闻让这位政治家名誉扫地。
Synonyms: disgrace, dishonor, shame, tarnish
Antonyms: honor, praise, credit, extol
词性: 动词


discredit:/dɪˈskredɪt/
noun
丧失信誉，丢脸
loss or damage to someone's reputation
The allegations brought discredit on the entire organization. 这些指控给整个组织带来了耻辱。
Synonyms: disgrace, dishonor, shame, stigma
Antonyms: honor, prestige, reputation, esteem
词性: 名词
