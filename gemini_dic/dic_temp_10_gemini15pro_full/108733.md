leapfrog:/ˈliːpfrɒɡ/ (UK), /ˈliːpfrɔːɡ/ (US)| n. vi. vt.|跳背游戏，超越，蛙跳|The act of jumping over another person or thing that is bent over. Also, to surpass or overtake someone or something, often quickly or unexpectedly. |例句：The children were playing leapfrog in the park. 孩子们在公园里玩跳背游戏。

例句：The company leapfrogged its competitors by investing in new technology.  这家公司通过投资新技术超越了竞争对手。|近义词：bypass, overtake, outstrip, surpass |反义词: lag behind, trail|noun
