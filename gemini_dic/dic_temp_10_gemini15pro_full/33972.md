cissies:/ˈsɪsiz/| n.|胆小鬼，懦夫|A derogatory term for someone considered weak, cowardly, or effeminate.  Used to insult or belittle someone perceived as lacking courage or toughness. |He called the boys who wouldn't play football a bunch of cissies.|近义词：cowards, weaklings, wimps|反义词:brave, courageous|Noun
