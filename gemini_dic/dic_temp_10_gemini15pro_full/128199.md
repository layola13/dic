neuridin:/njʊˈraɪdɪn/ | n. | 神经氨酸 | A non-protein nitrogenous compound found in the brain and other tissues, especially during development.  It is believed to play a role in nerve cell growth and function. | Neuridin levels are highest during brain development.  神经氨酸水平在大脑发育过程中最高。|  Neuridin is important for neuronal differentiation. 神经氨酸对神经元分化很重要。 | synonyms: spermine, spermidine | antonyms: N/A | noun
