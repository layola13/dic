medication:/ˌmedɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |药物，药剂|A substance used for medical treatment, especially a drug or drugs taken to improve a condition or illness. |The doctor prescribed a new medication for her high blood pressure. 医生给她开了治疗高血压的新药。|synonyms: medicine, drug, remedy, treatment | antonyms: poison, toxin | noun

medication:/ˌmedɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |用药，治疗|The act or process of treating with medicine. | The medication of the patient was successful. 对病人的治疗很成功。| synonyms: treatment, therapy, cure | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the act of medicating) | noun
