frothy:/ˈfrɒθi/ (UK), /ˈfrɔːθi/ (US)| adj.|泡沫的，起泡的；（饮料）加奶的；轻浮的，华而不实的|Covered with or full of froth; (of a drink) containing frothed milk; frivolous and superficial.|The cappuccino was topped with a layer of frothy milk. 卡布奇诺上面覆盖着一层奶泡。|
近义词:foamy, bubbly, effervescent, frivolous, superficial|
反义词:flat, still, serious, substantial|
词性:adjective


Etymology:
From Old English *frothig, from froth + -ig.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *frauthiz.
