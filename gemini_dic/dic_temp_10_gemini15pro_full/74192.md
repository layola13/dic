ETYMOLOGY->
From four + -fold + -ness.  Four meaning "four times," from Old English *feower, from Proto-Germanic *feðwor; -fold from Old English -feald, meaning "times," related to "fold;" and -ness, a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, from Old English -nes.
