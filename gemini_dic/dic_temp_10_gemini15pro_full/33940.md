cirro:/ˈsɪroʊ/| adj.|卷云的，纤状的|A combining form meaning “curl of hair,” “tuft,” “fringe,” used in the formation of compound words:  cirrocumulus; cirrostratus.|Cirrus clouds are wispy and feathery. 卷云纤细如羽毛。|近义词: filamentous, wispy|反义词: dense, thick|词性: combining form (构词成分)
