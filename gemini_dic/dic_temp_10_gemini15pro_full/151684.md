partitionist:/pɑːrˈtɪʃənɪst/ | n. | 分裂主义者 | A person who advocates the division of a country or territory into separate political entities. |  The partitionists demanded the creation of a separate state. 分裂主义者要求建立一个独立的国家。| synonyms: separatist, divisionist | antonyms:  unionist, integrationist | noun


Etymology:

From partition + -ist.  "Partition" ultimately comes from the Latin "partitio," meaning "a dividing, sharing, portion." "-ist" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a person who adheres to a certain doctrine or system, or engages in a certain pursuit.
