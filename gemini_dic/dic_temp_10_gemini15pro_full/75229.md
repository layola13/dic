froise:/frɔɪˈzeɪ/ | n. |冰沙思乐,草莓冰沙 | A frozen dessert made with strawberries. Typically, it's a combination of blended frozen strawberries, ice, and often some type of alcohol like rosé wine. |  I ordered a froise to cool down on a hot summer day.  我点了一杯冰沙思乐来降暑。

 I love the combination of strawberries and rosé in a froise. 我喜欢冰沙思乐里草莓和桃红葡萄酒的组合。| 近义词:strawberry slushy, frozen cocktail, rosé slushy | 反义词:  hot chocolate, hot toddy | 词性: noun
