hyomental:/ˌhaɪoʊˈmɛntəl/| adj.|颏舌骨的，下颌舌骨的|Relating to the hyoid bone and the chin or mental region of the mandible.|The hyomental distance is an important measurement in cephalometric analysis. 颏舌骨距离是头影测量分析中的一个重要测量指标。|近义词:submental (this term refers to the area *under* the chin, not directly related to the hyoid bone)|反义词:None|词性:形容词

词源:
hy- (from Greek hyoeides, meaning "U-shaped", referring to the hyoid bone) + mental (from Latin mentum, meaning "chin")
