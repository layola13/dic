crosspollination:/ˌkrɒsˌpɒləˈneɪʃən/ |/ˌkrɔːsˌpɑːləˈneɪʃən/| n. |异花授粉；交叉传粉|The crosspollination of ideas between different cultures can lead to exciting new developments. 不同文化之间思想的交叉融合可以带来令人兴奋的新发展。| The bees play a crucial role in crosspollination of the apple trees. 蜜蜂在苹果树的异花授粉中起着至关重要的作用。| 近义词: cross-fertilization, hybridization | 反义词: self-pollination | 词性: noun

cross-pollinate:/ˌkrɒsˈpɒləneɪt/ |/ˌkrɔːsˈpɑːləneɪt/| v. |异花授粉；交叉影响 | The gardener cross-pollinated the flowers to create a new hybrid. 园丁将这些花进行异花授粉以创造一个新的杂交品种。 |  Different artistic styles can cross-pollinate and influence each other. 不同的艺术风格可以相互交叉影响。 | 近义词: cross-fertilize, hybridize | 反义词: self-pollinate | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From cross- + pollination.  "Pollination" comes from the Latin "pollen" (fine flour, dust).  "Cross-" indicates the transfer between different plants.
