ibidine:/ˈɪbɪdiːn/| n.|依比丁;|A dopamine D2 receptor agonist with potential antiparkinsonian activity. It acts by stimulating dopamine D2 receptors in the brain, thereby mimicking the effects of dopamine and potentially improving motor control in Parkinson's disease patients.|Ibopamine is metabolized to ibodine and ibibine, both of which are also active.|近义词：pramipexole, ropinirole|反义词:dopamine antagonist|noun
