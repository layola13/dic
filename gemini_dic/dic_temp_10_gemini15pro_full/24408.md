briquette:/brɪˈkɛt/| n.|煤球，煤饼，燃料块|A block of compressed coal dust or other combustible material used for fuel.|We use briquettes in our fireplace because they burn longer than logs. 我们壁炉里用煤球，因为它们比木柴燃烧得更久。|synonyms: block, cake, lump, pellet|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "loose material" or "powder")|noun

briquette:/brɪˈkɛt/| vt.|把…制成煤球|To form into briquettes.|The factory briquettes the sawdust into fuel. 这家工厂把锯末压制成燃料块。|synonyms: compress, compact, consolidate|antonyms:  separate, disperse, loosen|verb
