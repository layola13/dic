disputer:/dɪˈspjuːtər/ | n. | 争论者，辩论者 | A person who argues or debates a point. |  He was known as a skilled disputer, always ready for a debate. 他被称为一个熟练的辩论者，随时准备辩论。

The disputers couldn't agree on a solution. 这些争论者无法就解决方案达成一致。| 近义词：arguer, debater, contestant, litigant | 反义词：mediator, arbitrator | 词性: 名词
