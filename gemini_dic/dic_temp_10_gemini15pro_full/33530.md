cicuta:/saɪˈkjuːtə/ | n. | 毒芹；毒芹属植物 | A highly poisonous plant of the parsley family, especially the water hemlock. |  Cicuta is one of the most poisonous plants in North America. 北美毒芹是最毒的植物之一。

Cicuta poisoning can cause seizures and death.  毒芹中毒会导致癫痫发作和死亡。 | 近义词: water hemlock, cowbane | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a poisonous plant, could consider a medicinal plant as a conceptual opposite)  | 词性: noun
