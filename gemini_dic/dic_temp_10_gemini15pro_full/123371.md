monomaniacs:/ˌmɒnəˈmeɪnɪæks/ /ˌmɑːnəˈmeɪniˌæks/ | n. |偏执狂患者；独断专行者|A person who is obsessively preoccupied with one idea or subject; a person who suffers from monomania. |例句：The monomaniacs were convinced their theories were correct, despite evidence to the contrary. 偏执狂们坚信他们的理论是正确的，尽管有相反的证据。

例句：He became a monomaniac, focused solely on his quest for revenge. 他变成了一个偏执狂，一心只想着复仇。|近义词：fanatic, zealot, extremist|反义词：moderate, rationalist|词性:noun
