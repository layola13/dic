crusty:/ˈkrʌsti/| adj.|硬皮的，易怒的，脾气坏的|Covered with or having a hard crust; bad-tempered and irritable.|The bread had a wonderfully crusty exterior. 这面包的外皮非常酥脆。|近义词：crispy, brittle, grumpy, surly|反义词:soft, pliable, pleasant, agreeable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *crusted, past participle of crusten (“to form a crust”), from crust + -ed, from Old Norse *krustid, past participle of krusta (“to form a crust”), from Proto-Germanic *krustaną (“to form a crust”), from Proto-Indo-European *kreus- (“hard, firm”).
