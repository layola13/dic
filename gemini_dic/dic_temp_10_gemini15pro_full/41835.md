corvette:/kɔːrˈvet/| n.|轻巡洋舰，护卫舰|A small, fast warship, especially one used as an escort.|The corvette patrolled the coastal waters. 这艘轻型巡洋舰在沿海水域巡逻。|近义词：warship, frigate|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 civilian ship 民用船只)|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French "corvette," a small, fast warship, from Dutch "korvet," ultimately from Latin "corbita," a slow, cargo ship.  The meaning shifted from a slow cargo ship to a fast warship.
