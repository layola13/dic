partridges:/ˈpɑːrtrɪdʒɪz/
noun
鹧鸪
A medium-sized game bird with a short, curved bill and strong legs, often found in open country.
The hunter flushed a covey of partridges from the field.  猎人从田野里惊起一群鹧鸪。
synonyms: grouse, quail, pheasant
