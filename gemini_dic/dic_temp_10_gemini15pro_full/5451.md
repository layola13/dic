ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English ælmeshus (“almshouse”), from ælmesse (“alms”) + hūs (“house”).  The word "alms" comes from the Old English ælmesse, derived from the Greek  ἐλεημοσύνη (eleēmosynē, "mercy, pity, alms").
