hanukah:/ˈhɑːnəkə/ (US), /ˈhɑːnʊkə/ (UK)| n.|光明节|A Jewish festival, lasting eight days from the 25th of Kislev (in December), which celebrates the rededication of the Second Temple in Jerusalem after its desecration by the Syrian king Antiochus IV.  It is observed by the kindling of lights on a special menorah (candelabrum), one light being added each night.|We celebrate Hanukah by lighting candles on the menorah. 我们通过在烛台上点燃蜡烛来庆祝光明节。|The family gathered for a Hanukah dinner. 全家聚在一起吃光明节晚餐。|近义词:Festival of Lights|反义词:None|词性:noun
