furziest:/ˈfɜːrziɪst/| adj.|最多毛的，最毛茸茸的|Covered with the most fur; most hairy or shaggy.|例句：That's the furziest caterpillar I've ever seen. 那是我见过的毛毛虫中最毛茸茸的一只。

例句：My dog is the furziest of the litter. 我的狗是这窝幼犬中最毛茸茸的。|近义词：hairiest, shaggiest, fluffiest|反义词：smoothest, baldest|词性:形容词

词源: From 'fur' (Old French 'forre,' from Frankish 'fodr,' related to Old High German 'fodar' - lining, sheath) + '-y' (adjective-forming suffix) + '-est' (superlative suffix).
