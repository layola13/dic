nonrestorations:/ˌnɒnˌrɛstəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. | 未修复；未恢复；未重建（通常用复数形式） | The act or process of not restoring something to its former condition.  Absence of restoration. |  The museum curator lamented the nonrestorations of several important artifacts due to lack of funding. 博物馆馆长对由于缺乏资金而导致几件重要文物未能修复表示遗憾。| Near synonyms:  non-renewal, neglect, omission of restoration | Antonyms: restorations, renewals, renovations | Noun
