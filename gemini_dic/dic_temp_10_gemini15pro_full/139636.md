occlude:/əˈkluːd/
adjective
闭塞的，堵塞的
Stopped or blocked up; closed.
例句：The artery was occluded by a blood clot.  这条动脉被血块堵塞了。

例句：His vision was occluded by the thick fog. 浓雾遮蔽了他的视线。

近义词：block, obstruct, close, clog
反义词：open, clear
词性: adjective

occlude:/əˈkluːd/
verb
使闭塞，堵塞
Stop, close up, or obstruct (an opening, orifice, or passage).
例句：The police occluded the road to prevent the protesters from advancing. 警察封锁了道路以阻止抗议者前进。

例句：The tumor occluded his airway, making it difficult to breathe. 肿瘤阻塞了他的呼吸道，使他呼吸困难。

近义词：block, obstruct, close, clog
反义词：open, clear
词性: verb
