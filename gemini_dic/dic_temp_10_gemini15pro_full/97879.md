innards:/ˈɪnərdz/
noun
内脏；内部；深处
the internal organs of a person or animal; the internal parts of a machine or other object
例句：
The surgeon carefully examined the patient's innards.  外科医生仔细检查了病人的内脏。

The mechanic took apart the engine to examine its innards.  机械师拆开了引擎来检查它的内部构造。

近义词：viscera, entrails, guts, insides
反义词：exterior, outside
词性：n.
