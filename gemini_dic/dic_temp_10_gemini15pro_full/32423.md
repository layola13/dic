chiltern:/ˈtʃɪltərnz/
n.
奇尔特恩丘陵 (英格兰南部的一片丘陵地带)
a range of low hills in southern England, extending from Oxfordshire to Hertfordshire.
The Chiltern Hills are a popular destination for hikers. 奇尔特恩丘陵是徒步旅行者的热门目的地。
近义词:hills, downs
反义词:valleys, plains
词性:名词

chiltern:/ˈtʃɪltərn/
adj.
奇尔特恩的
relating to the Chiltern Hills.
The Chiltern Hundreds are a nominal office used by members of the UK Parliament to resign. 奇尔特恩百户区是一个名义上的职位，供英国议会议员辞职使用。
