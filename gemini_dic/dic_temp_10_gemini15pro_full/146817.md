overimporting: /ˌoʊvərɪmˈpɔːrtɪŋ/
n.|过度进口|The act of importing goods in excess of domestic demand or production capacity.  Excessive importation of goods. |The country's overimporting led to a trade deficit. 这个国家的过度进口导致了贸易逆差。|近义词：excessive importing|反义词: underimporting
词性: 名词 (noun)


overimport:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈpɔːrt/
v.|过度进口|To import goods in excess of domestic demand or production capacity.|The government is trying to discourage businesses from overimporting foreign products. 政府正在试图阻止企业过度进口外国产品。|近义词：import excessively|反义词: underimport
词性: 动词 (verb)
