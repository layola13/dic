dojo:/ˈdoʊdʒoʊ/| n. |道场；练习场所|A Japanese word for a formal training place for students of Japanese martial arts.|  He practices karate at the local dojo. 他在当地的道场练习空手道。|Synonym: training hall, gym, studio|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "home," "street" in the context of contrasting a place of training)|noun
