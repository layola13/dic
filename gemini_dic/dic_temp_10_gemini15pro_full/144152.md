outshaping:/ˌaʊtˈʃeɪpɪŋ/
|v.|胜过，在…方面超过|Outperform, surpass, especially in terms of giving something a better or more effective shape or form.|The new AI model is outshaping all its competitors in terms of accuracy.  这个新的人工智能模型在准确性方面胜过所有竞争对手。|
|v.|优于|Surpass, excel|Her performance outshaped everyone's expectations. 她的表现超出了所有人的预期。|
|近义词:|outperforming, surpassing, excelling, exceeding, outdoing, outstripping|
|反义词:|underperforming, lagging behind, falling short|
|词性:|Verb (present participle)|
