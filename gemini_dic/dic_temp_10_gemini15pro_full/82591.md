guardianage:/ˈɡɑːrdiənɪdʒ/
n.
监护,监护期
The state of being under guardianship; the period of time during which someone is a ward.
The child was under guardianage until he turned eighteen.  这个孩子在十八岁之前一直处于监护之下。
wardship, custodianship
majority

词性: 名词
