ochlocratic: /ˌɒkləˈkrætɪk/ | adj. | 暴民统治的；群众暴政的 | Relating to or characterized by ochlocracy (mob rule).  | The historian warned of the dangers of an ochlocratic society where the whims of the mob dictate policy.  历史学家警告了暴民统治的社会中，暴民的反复无常决定政策的危险。|  近义词: demagogic, mobocratic | 反义词: democratic, aristocratic | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *okhlokratía* "mob rule," from *ókhlor* "mob, crowd" + *-kratía* "rule, power."
