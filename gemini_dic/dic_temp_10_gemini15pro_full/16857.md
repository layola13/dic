basidium:/bəˈsɪdiəm/ (plural basidia /bəˈsɪdiə/)| n.|担子|A microscopic, club-shaped spore-bearing structure produced by certain fungi, typically the Basidiomycetes. It usually bears four spores called basidiospores.|The mushroom's gills are covered in basidia.|近义词: sporophore|反义词: None|词性: Noun

Etymology:
From New Latin *basidium, from Ancient Greek βάσις (básis, “base, pedestal”).
