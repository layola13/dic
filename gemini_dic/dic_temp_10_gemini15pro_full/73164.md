forbears:/ˈfɔːrˌbɛərz/| n. |祖先，祖辈|Ancestors or forefather. People from whom one is descended.|例句：His forbears were farmers. 他的祖先都是农民。

例句：She learned about her forbears through genealogy research. 她通过家谱研究了解了她的祖先。|近义词：ancestors, forefathers, predecessors|反义词:descendants, offspring|词性:名词
