ignitability:/ɪɡˌnaɪtəˈbɪləti/| n. |可燃性，易燃性|The quality of being ignitable; the ease with which something ignites. |The ignitability of dry leaves makes them a fire hazard. 干树叶的可燃性使它们成为火灾隐患。|近义词：flammability, combustibility |反义词: non-flammability, fire resistance|noun
