intergovernmentally:/ˌɪntərˌɡʌvərnˈmentəli/
adverb
政府间地；在政府之间
between or involving different governments
例句：The issue was discussed intergovernmentally. 这个问题在政府间进行了讨论。
例句：Intergovernmentally agreed standards are crucial for international trade. 政府间商定的标准对国际贸易至关重要。
近义词：between governments, among governments
反义词:  intragovernmentally (within a single government)
词性: adverb
