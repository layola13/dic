hi-tech:/ˌhaɪˈtɛk/ | adj. | 高科技的；尖端技术的 | Using advanced methods, processes, or equipment, especially in science or industry. |  This is a hi-tech company specializing in robotics.  这家公司是专门研究机器人技术的高科技公司。|  Example: They invested in hi-tech industries. 他们投资于高科技产业。| Synonyms: advanced, sophisticated, cutting-edge, state-of-the-art | Antonyms: low-tech, outdated, primitive | adjective


Etymology->
Clipped form of high technology, first recorded in 1958.
