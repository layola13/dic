oversweetly:/ˌoʊvərˈswiːtli/ /ˌəʊvəˈswiːtli/
adverb|过分甜地，甜得发腻地|Excessively sweet; too sweet.|The tea was oversweetly brewed. 这茶泡得过甜了.|Synonyms: cloyingly, saccharinely, sickeningly|Antonyms: blandly, tartly|词性: 副词

oversweetly:/ˌoʊvərˈswiːtli/ /ˌəʊvəˈswiːtli/
Etymology:  "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "sweet" (Old English swēte, from Proto-Germanic *swōtuz) + "-ly" (Old English -līce, adverbial suffix)|词源: 由"over-" (古英语 *ofer，源自原始日耳曼语 *uberi "在...之上，超过") + "sweet" (古英语 swēte，源自原始日耳曼语 *swōtuz) + "-ly" (古英语 -līce，副词后缀) 组成。|词性: 副词
