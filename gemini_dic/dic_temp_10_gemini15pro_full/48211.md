deconglomeration:/ˌdiːkənˌɡlɒməˈreɪʃn/
noun
反集团化；分解
The process of breaking up a large business conglomerate into smaller, independent companies.
The deconglomeration of the company led to increased competition.  公司的分解导致竞争加剧。
The 1980s saw a wave of deconglomeration in the US.  20世纪80年代，美国出现了一波反集团化浪潮。
近义词:  dismantlement, separation, division, splitting, break-up
反义词:  merger, consolidation, integration, combination
词性: noun

词源：de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + conglomerate (noun meaning "a large corporation formed by merging several smaller companies") + -ing/-ate/-ation (suffixes forming verb/verb/noun)
