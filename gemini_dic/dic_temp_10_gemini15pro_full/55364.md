distantly:/ˈdɪstəntli/ | adv. | 遥远地；冷淡地；疏远地 | In a distant manner; far away; remotely; aloofly |  She smiled distantly. 她淡淡地笑了笑。|  remotely, faintly, vaguely | closely, warmly, intimately | adverb

distantly:/ˈdɪstəntli/ | adv. | 遥远地；冷淡地；疏远地 |  He was related, however distantly, to the royal family.  尽管关系疏远，他也是皇室的亲戚。| far off, afar, remotely | nearly, closely, intimately | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *distantli, from Old French *distantment (modern French distamment), from Latin distantem (nominative distans) + -ment (suffix forming adverbs from adjectives), from distāre "to stand apart, be distant," from dis- "apart, away" + stāre "to stand."
