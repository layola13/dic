aepyornis:/ˌiːpaɪˈɔːrnɪs/
n.
象鸟
Aepyornis is an extinct genus of large flightless birds, also known as elephant birds, that once inhabited Madagascar.
象鸟是一种已灭绝的大型不会飞的鸟类，也称为象鸟，曾经生活在马达加斯加。
The Aepyornis egg was enormous, larger than any dinosaur egg discovered. 象鸟蛋非常巨大，比任何发现的恐龙蛋都大。
近义词:elephant bird
反义词: (No direct antonym for an extinct animal.  Perhaps "extant bird" could be considered a contrasting concept.)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek αἰπύς (aipús, “high, steep”) + ὄρνις (órnis, “bird”).  This likely refers to the bird's large size.
来自古希腊语 αἰπύς (aipús, “高的，陡峭的”) + ὄρνις (órnis, “鸟”)。这可能是指这种鸟体型巨大。
