immolate: /ˈɪməleɪt/
verb
献祭；牺牲；毁灭
to kill or destroy, especially by fire
The ancient Aztecs would immolate prisoners of war to their gods. 古代阿兹特克人会将战俘献祭给他们的神。
近义词: sacrifice, destroy, kill
反义词: preserve, save, protect
词性: Verb

immolation: /ˌɪməˈleɪʃn/
noun
献祭；牺牲；毁灭
the act of killing or destroying, especially by fire
The immolation of the wicker man was a central part of their pagan ritual.  焚烧柳条人是他们异教仪式的重要组成部分。
近义词: sacrifice, destruction, killing
反义词: preservation, salvation, protection
词性: Noun

Etymology:
From Latin *immolare* "to sprinkle with sacrificial meal; to sacrifice," from *in mola*" "in/on the sacrificial meal," from *in* "in, on" + *mola* "sacrificial meal, salt cake."
