cleistogamic:/ˌklaɪstəˈɡæmɪk/ | adj. |闭花受精的|Of or relating to cleistogamy; self-pollinating and fertilizing within an unopened flower.|Cleistogamic flowers are often small and inconspicuous. 闭花受精的花通常小而不起眼。|Violets produce both cleistogamic and chasmogamous flowers. 紫罗兰既产生闭花受精的花，也产生开放受精的花。| 近义词:self-pollinating, autogamous | 反义词:chasmogamous |词性:形容词

cleistogamy:/ˌklaɪstəˈɡæmi/ | n. |闭花受精|The production of small, unopened, self-pollinating flowers.|Cleistogamy is a common strategy in plants that grow in harsh environments. 闭花受精是生长在恶劣环境中的植物的常见策略.|Some plants exhibit both cleistogamy and chasmogamy. 一些植物既表现出闭花受精，也表现出开放受精。|近义词:self-pollination, autogamy | 反义词:chasmogamy |词性:名词
