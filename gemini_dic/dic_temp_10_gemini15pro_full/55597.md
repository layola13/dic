不统一，分裂，分歧
lack of unity or agreement; discord.
例句：The disunities within the party led to its downfall.  党内的不和导致了它的垮台。

例句：Tribal disunities have plagued the region for centuries.  部落纷争几个世纪以来一直困扰着这个地区。

近义词：discord, division, disharmony, friction, schism, split

词性: 名词 (plural noun)
