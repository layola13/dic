contemptible:/kənˈtɛmptɪbl/| adj.|卑鄙的，可鄙的，令人轻蔑的|Deserving contempt; despicable.|His behavior towards the homeless was utterly contemptible. 他对无家可归者的行为极其卑鄙。|近义词：despicable, abject, base, mean, vile, worthless|反义词：admirable, commendable, laudable, praiseworthy|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From Late Latin *contemptibilis, from Latin contemptus (“despised”), past participle of contemnere (“to despise, scorn”), from con- (“together”) + temnere (“to slight, scorn”).
