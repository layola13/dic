etchings: /ˈetʃɪŋz/
n.|蚀刻画，蚀刻版画|A print produced by the process of etching.  The process of producing etchings involves using acid to cut into a metal plate, which is then inked and used to create prints.|The museum had a beautiful collection of Rembrandt's etchings.|近义词: engravings, prints, inscriptions|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly paintings, sculptures if considering different art forms)|词性: Noun
