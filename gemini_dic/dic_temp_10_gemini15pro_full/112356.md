词源:
From Middle English *lorn, from Old English *loren (“lost, ruined, forsaken”), past participle of *lēosan (“to lose”). Cognate with Old Frisian lorin, Old Saxon loran, Middle Dutch verloren, Old High German lorēn, German verloren, Gothic 𐌻𐌰𐌿𐍃𐌰𐌽 (lausan). More at lose.
