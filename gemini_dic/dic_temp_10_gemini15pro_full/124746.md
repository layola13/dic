mq:/ɛmˈkjuː/ | n. | message queue (消息队列) | A system for passing messages between applications or processes.  It allows asynchronous communication, meaning that the sender and receiver don't need to interact at the same time. |  Example: We use an mq to handle the high volume of transactions. 我们使用消息队列来处理大量的交易。| Synonym: message broker, queue manager | Antonym: N/A | noun
