mesh:/mɛʃ/| n. v.|网格，啮合|A network fabric of uniform size formed by the intersection of threads, wires, etc. |The metal mesh protects the plants from deer. 金属网保护植物免受鹿的侵害。|Synonyms: network, grid, screen|Antonyms: solid, gap, opening|Noun

mesh:/mɛʃ/| v.|啮合，协调|To fit together or interlock.|The gears of the clock mesh perfectly. 时钟的齿轮完美啮合。|Synonyms: interlock, engage, connect|Antonyms: disengage, disconnect, separate|Verb
