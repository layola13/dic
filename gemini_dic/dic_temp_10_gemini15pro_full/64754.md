scroll:/skroʊl/| n. vi. vt.|卷轴，滚动条; (使)上下或左右移动|A long roll of paper or parchment, especially one with writing on it.  To move text or images up or down or across a computer screen.|例句：The ancient scroll contained a royal decree.  这古老的卷轴包含了一项皇家法令。

例句：I scrolled down the webpage to find the information. 我向下滚动网页来查找信息。|近义词：roll, parchment, scroll down/up|反义词: N/A|词性:noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *escroler, *escrouler ("to unroll"),  from Vulgar Latin *exrotulare ("to unroll"), from Latin ex ("out of, from") + rotula ("small wheel, roll").
