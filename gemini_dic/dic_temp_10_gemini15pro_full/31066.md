chamlet:/ˈtʃæmlɪt/ | n. | 小屋，茅舍；简陋的住所 | A small, mean dwelling; a hut. |  He lived in a chamlet on the edge of the forest. 他住在森林边缘的一个小屋里。 |  The chamlet provided meager shelter from the storm.  这间小屋勉强抵挡了风暴。| 近义词: hut, shack, cabin, cottage | 反义词: mansion, palace | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old French "chamelet," diminutive of "chamel," meaning "camel," likely referring to a small, hump-backed dwelling.
