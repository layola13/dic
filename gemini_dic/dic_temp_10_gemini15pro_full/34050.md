cityscapes:/ˈsɪtiskeɪps/| n. |城市景观，都市风景|A picture or view of a city, especially one which is striking or beautiful. |例句：The photographer captured the vibrant cityscapes of Hong Kong. 摄影师捕捉到了香港充满活力的城市景观。

例句：From the rooftop, you can enjoy breathtaking cityscapes. 从屋顶上，你可以欣赏到令人叹为观止的城市景观。|近义词：urban landscapes, city views, skylines|反义词:countryside, rural scenes|词性:noun
