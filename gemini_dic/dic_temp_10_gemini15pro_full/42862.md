courtesanship: /ˈkɔːrtiənʃɪp/ /ˈkɔːrtʃənʃɪp/
n.
交际手腕；逢迎；拉拢；（尤指）高级妓女的工作；妓女身份
The art or practice of behaving in a way intended to gain favor, especially by flattery; the profession of a high-class prostitute.
Her success depended as much on her political courtesanship as on her talent. 她的成功既取决于她的政治手腕，也取决于她的才能。
Synonyms: flattery, ingratiation, sycophancy, adulation, cajolery, wheedling, fawning, obsequiousness, subservience, sweet-talking, smooth-talking, blandishment, apple-polishing
Antonyms: frankness, honesty, candor, directness
noun
