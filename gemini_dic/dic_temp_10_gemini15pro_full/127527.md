necromancer:/ˈnekrəˌmænsər/| n. |死灵法师；巫师|A practitioner of necromancy; a sorcerer or magician who is believed to be able to communicate with the dead, especially in order to predict the future or to use their powers. |The necromancer summoned a skeletal warrior from the depths of the graveyard.| 近义词: sorcerer, wizard, magician| 反义词: priest, cleric |noun
