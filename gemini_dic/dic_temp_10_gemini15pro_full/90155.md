hotfoot:/ˈhɒtfʊt/ (British), /ˈhɑːtfʊt/ (American)| v. |匆匆忙忙，急忙|To go somewhere quickly, often because of something urgent or exciting. |He hotfooted it back to the office when he heard the news. 他听到消息后急忙赶回办公室。|近义词：hurry, rush, dash, hasten|反义词：dawdle, linger|词性: 动词

hotfoot:/ˈhɒtfʊt/ (British), /ˈhɑːtfʊt/ (American)| adj. |快速完成的，仓促的|Done or made quickly, often too quickly and without enough care. |It was a hotfoot decision that he later regretted. 这是一个他后来后悔的仓促决定。|近义词：hasty, rushed, hurried|反义词：considered, deliberate|词性: 形容词
