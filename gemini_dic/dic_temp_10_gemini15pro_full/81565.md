grazer:/ˈɡreɪzər/| n.|食草动物；放牧者；轻擦者|An animal that grazes, especially a herbivorous mammal.  A person who eats small amounts of food throughout the day rather than large meals. Something that grazes or touches lightly in passing.|Cows are grazers. 牛是食草动物。

The car was a grazer, barely touching the other vehicle. 汽车只是轻轻擦过另一辆车。

He's a grazer, constantly snacking throughout the day. 他是个喜欢随时吃点东西的人。|近义词：herbivore, browser, nibbler|反义词：predator, carnivore|词性:noun
