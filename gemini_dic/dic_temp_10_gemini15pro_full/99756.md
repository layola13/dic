intermeddling:/ˌɪntərˈmedlɪŋ/ | n. adj.| 干涉，干预; 爱管闲事的 | The act of interfering in something that is not one's concern. |  His constant intermeddling in his daughter's life caused her to resent him. 他不断干涉女儿的生活，让她对他很反感。| 近义词: interference, intrusion, meddling | 反义词: noninterference, nonintervention | 词性: 名词, 形容词


intermeddle:/ˌɪntərˈmedl/ | v. | 干涉，干预; 管闲事 | To interfere in something that is not one's concern. | He intermeddled in his neighbor's dispute. 他干预了邻居的纠纷。 | 近义词: interfere, intrude, meddle | 反义词: abstain, refrain | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *entremedler, from entre- "between" + medler "to mix."
