amniote:/ˈæmniˌoʊt/| n. adj.|羊膜动物|An amniote is a vertebrate animal whose embryo develops inside an amniotic sac. This sac contains amniotic fluid, which cushions and protects the developing embryo. Amniotes include reptiles, birds, and mammals.|Reptiles, birds, and mammals are all amniotes. 爬行动物、鸟类和哺乳动物都是羊膜动物。|Synonyms:  amniotic vertebrate|Antonyms: anamniote|noun, adjective
