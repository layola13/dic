noncategorical:/ˌnɒnkætəˈɡɒrɪkl/ | adj. | 非范畴的，非绝对的 | Not belonging to or relating to a particular category; not absolute or definite. |  Many mental health diagnoses are noncategorical, existing on a spectrum. 许多心理健康诊断是非范畴的，存在于一个谱系中。| The guidelines are noncategorical and allow for flexibility. 这些指导方针是非绝对的，允许灵活变通。 | 近义词: non-specific, imprecise, ambiguous, graded, dimensional | 反义词: categorical, definite, absolute, specific, precise | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "categorical" (from "category" + "-ical"). "Category" ultimately derives from the Greek word "katēgoria," meaning "accusation" or "predicate."
