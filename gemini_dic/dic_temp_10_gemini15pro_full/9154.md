antinaturally:/ˌæntɪˈnætʃərəli/
adverb|违反自然地；反常地|in a way that is not natural; in a way that goes against nature or human nature|例句：He was behaving antinaturally calmly after the accident. 事故发生后，他表现得异常平静。|例句：She smiled antinaturally wide, making him suspicious. 她笑得异常开阔，让他起了疑心。|近义词：unnaturally, abnormally, perversely|反义词：naturally, normally|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (against, opposite) + naturally (in a natural way).  The prefix "anti-" comes from Greek, meaning "against" or "opposite". "Naturally" comes from the Latin word "naturalis," meaning "of or pertaining to nature."
