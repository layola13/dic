endorhizal:/ɛndəˈraɪzəl/
adjective
内生菌根的
Characterized by the formation of endomycorrhizae, where the fungal hyphae penetrate the cortical cells of the plant roots.
The majority of flowering plants form endorhizal associations with fungi.  大多数开花植物与真菌形成内生菌根关联。
Many agricultural crops, such as corn and wheat, are endorhizal. 许多农作物，例如玉米和小麦，都是内生菌根的。
近义词: mycorrhizal
反义词: ectomycorrhizal
词性: adjective
