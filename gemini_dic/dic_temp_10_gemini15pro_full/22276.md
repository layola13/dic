boneset:/ˈboʊnˌsɛt/| n. | 骨碎补, 透骨草 |A North American plant (Eupatorium perfoliatum) of the daisy family, having hairy leaves and small white flowers. It has traditionally been used in herbal medicine. |  Early settlers used boneset to treat fevers. 早期定居者使用透骨草来治疗发烧。| Boneset tea was a common remedy for colds. 透骨草茶是治疗感冒的常用药物。| synonyms: thoroughwort, agueweed | antonyms: N/A | noun
