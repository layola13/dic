bland: /blænd/
adjective
温和的; 乏味的; 清淡的
Lacking strong features or characteristics and therefore uninteresting.
The soup was a bit bland. 这汤有点淡。

The doctor put her on a bland diet. 医生让她吃清淡的食物。


近义词: mild, tasteless, insipid, dull
反义词: spicy, flavorful, interesting, exciting
词性: adjective
