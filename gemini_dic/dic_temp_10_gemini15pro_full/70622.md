filmstrip: /ˈfɪlmˌstrɪp/ | n. | 胶卷，电影胶片；连环画片 | A length of film containing a sequence of images, especially one used to show still pictures or cartoons. |  We watched a filmstrip about the solar system in science class. 我们在科学课上看了一部关于太阳系的电影胶片。| synonyms: film, slide, strip, reel | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps digital image/video) | noun

filmstrip: /ˈfɪlmˌstrɪp/ | n. | 连环画片 | A series of related images, often cartoons, printed on a strip of film for projection. |  The filmstrip told the story of a little boy who lost his dog.  这部连环画讲述了一个小男孩丢失了他的狗的故事。| synonyms: comic strip, picture story | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun
