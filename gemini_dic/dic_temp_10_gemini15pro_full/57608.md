drastic:/ˈdræs.tɪk/| adj.|激烈的，猛烈的，极端的|Having a strong or far-reaching and usually sudden or violent effect.  Relating to or constituting a drastic purgative. |例句：The government took drastic measures to control inflation. 政府采取了严厉的措施来控制通货膨胀。

例句：They made drastic changes to their lifestyle to improve their health. 他们对生活方式做出了巨大的改变以改善健康状况。|近义词：severe, extreme, radical, harsh|反义词：mild, moderate, slight|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *drastikos* "active," from *dran* "to do, perform."
