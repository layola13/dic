criminal: /ˈkrɪmɪnl/ (US) /ˈkrɪmɪnəl/ (UK)| n. adj.| 罪犯，犯罪的|A person who has committed a crime.  Relating to crime. |例句：The criminal was sentenced to ten years in prison.  这个罪犯被判处十年监禁。|近义词：offender, culprit, felon, unlawful, illegal|反义词：law-abiding, innocent, legal|词性: noun, adjective

criminal: /ˈkrɪmɪnl/ (US) /ˈkrɪmɪnəl/ (UK)| adj.| 犯罪的|Relating to crime; involving or having the nature of a crime.|例句：The police are investigating a criminal act. 警方正在调查一起犯罪行为。|近义词：unlawful, illegal, illicit|反义词：lawful, legal, legitimate|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French criminel, from Late Latin criminalis (“relating to a crime”), from Latin crimen (“crime, accusation, charge”).
