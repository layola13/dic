dbms:/ˈdiː biː ɛmˈɛs/| n. |数据库管理系统(Database Management System)|A software system used for creating and managing databases. It provides a systematic way to create, retrieve, update and manage data.|例句：The company uses a robust DBMS to handle its customer data. 公司使用强大的数据库管理系统来处理其客户数据。

例句：Choosing the right DBMS is crucial for the success of any data-driven project. 选择合适的数据库管理系统对于任何数据驱动项目的成功至关重要。|近义词：database system, database software|反义词: None|词性:noun
