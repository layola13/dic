gastraea:/ɡæˈstriːə/| n. | 原肠胚 |A hypothetical ancestral form of multicellular animal with a single body cavity and a single opening serving as both mouth and anus, resembling the gastrula stage in embryonic development. | Haeckel proposed the gastraea theory, suggesting that all metazoans evolved from a gastraea-like ancestor. 海克尔提出了原肠胚理论，认为所有后生动物都是从类似原肠胚的祖先进化而来的。| Gastraea is a crucial concept in understanding the evolution of multicellular animals. 原肠胚是理解多细胞动物进化的关键概念。| synonyms: blastula, embryo | antonyms: adult, mature | noun
