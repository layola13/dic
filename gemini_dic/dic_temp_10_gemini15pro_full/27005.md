calculates:/ˈkælkjuleɪts/
verb
计算，估算
Determines the amount or number of something mathematically.
The computer calculates the trajectory of the missile. 计算机计算导弹的轨迹。
compute, determine, figure, reckon
estimate, guess
v.
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin calculatus, past participle of calculare (“to calculate”), from Latin calculus (“pebble, small stone used in reckoning”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱel- (“to strike, cut”).
