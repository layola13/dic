counters:/ˈkaʊntərz/ /ˈkaʊntəz/| n.|计数器，柜台|A device used for counting; a surface or table in a store or bank across which business is transacted. |The cashier rang up the groceries on the counter.  收银员在柜台上把食品杂货的价钱输进收银机。|Synonyms:  registers, desks, tables|Antonyms: None|Noun


counters:/ˈkaʊntərz/ /ˈkaʊntəz/| v.|反击，对抗|Speak or act in opposition to. |He countered her argument with a well-researched rebuttal. 他用经过充分研究的反驳来反驳她的论点。|Synonyms: opposes, contradicts, rebuts|Antonyms: supports, agrees, confirms|Verb
