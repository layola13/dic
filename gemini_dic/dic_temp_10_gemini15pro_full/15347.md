backstitch: /ˈbækˌstɪtʃ/ | n. v. |回针，倒缝；回针缝制| A sewing stitch that doubles back on the previous stitch to form a solid line, often used for outlines or emphasis.  A stitch made by inserting the needle a short distance behind the previous stitch and bringing it out ahead of that stitch, then inserting it again a short distance behind the last stitch and repeating the process. |  She used backstitch to outline the flower petals. 她用回针缝勾勒出了花瓣的轮廓。|  Backstitch the seam to make it stronger.  把接缝回针缝一下，使它更牢固。| synonyms:  backstitching, outline stitch | antonyms: running stitch | noun, verb
