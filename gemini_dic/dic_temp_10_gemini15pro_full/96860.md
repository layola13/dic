inexecutable:/ɪnˌɛksɪˈkjuːtəbl/
adjective
不可执行的
Not able to be executed or carried out; not capable of being put into effect.
The code was full of errors, making it inexecutable. 这段代码充满了错误，导致它无法执行。
The plan was deemed inexecutable due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该计划被认为是不可执行的。
近义词:unworkable, impracticable, infeasible
反义词:executable, workable, feasible
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From in- (not) + executable.  "Executable" comes from the Latin "executus," the past participle of "exequi" (to follow out, perform).
