dozier:/ˈdoʊʒər/ (US) /ˈdəʊziə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更懒惰的，更怠惰的|Comparative form of dozy: more dozy.  More sleepy, drowsy, or sluggish.|He felt dozier after the heavy meal. 他在吃完大餐后感到更困倦了。|Near synonyms: sleepier, drowsier, lazier, more sluggish|Near antonyms: more energetic, more active|adjective

dozier:/ˈdoʊʒər/ (US) /ˈdəʊziə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更容易被欺骗或利用的|More easily deceived or taken advantage of.  (Often used in the phrase "a dozier target")|The elderly can be a dozier target for scammers.  老年人更容易成为骗子的目标。|Near synonyms: more gullible, more vulnerable, more susceptible|Near antonyms: more cautious, more vigilant|adjective
