meiosis:/maɪˈoʊsɪs/
n.
减数分裂
A type of cell division that results in four daughter cells each with half the number of chromosomes of the parent cell, as in the production of gametes and plant spores.
Meiosis is essential for sexual reproduction. 减数分裂对有性生殖至关重要。
Meiosis produces gametes with half the number of chromosomes as the parent cell. 减数分裂产生的配子染色体数量是母细胞的一半。
synonym: reduction division
antonym: mitosis
noun
