imperturbably:/ˌɪmpərˈtɜːrbəbli/ | adv. | 泰然自若地，不动声色地 |  In a way that is calm and not disturbed by anything. | He listened imperturbably to the accusations. 他不动声色地听着那些指控。| calmly, serenely, placidly | excitedly, nervously | 副词

词源:  From imperturbable + -ly.  Imperturbable comes from French *imperturbable*, from Latin imperturbabilis, from in- 'not' + perturbare 'to disturb'.
