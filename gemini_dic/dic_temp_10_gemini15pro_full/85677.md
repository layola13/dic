have-a-hand-in:/ˌhæv ə ˈhænd ɪn/| idiom |参与；插手；对…施加影响|To be involved or influential in something; to participate in or contribute to something. |例句：It's clear that she had a hand in organizing the surprise party.  很明显，她参与组织了这次惊喜派对。

例句：I suspect he had a hand in the decision to close the factory. 我怀疑他插手了关闭工厂的决定。|近义词：be involved in, participate in, contribute to, influence, play a part in|反义词：abstain from, stay out of|phrase
