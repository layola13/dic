fertilize:/ˈfɜːrtɪlaɪz/ /ˈfɝːtəlɪz/| v. |使肥沃，使多产；施肥；使受精|To make (soil or land) richer or more productive by adding suitable substances such as manure or fertilizer. To cause an egg or seed to develop a new individual by uniting it with a sperm or pollen.|例句：
The farmer fertilized the fields with manure. 农民用肥料给田地施肥。

He fertilized the egg with his sperm. 他用自己的精子使卵子受精。

|近义词：enrich, nourish, inseminate, pollinate|反义词:sterilize|词性:verb
