obomegoid:/ˈəʊbəʊˈmiːɡɔɪd/| adj.|卵圆形的，卵形的|Resembling or shaped like an egg; ovoid.  Formed from "obo-" (inversely egg-shaped) + "-megoid" (like, resembling).|The obomegoid shape of the leaf was distinctive.  叶子的卵圆形很独特。|近义词：ovoid, oval, elliptic, egg-shaped|反义词:linear, elongated|词性:adjective
