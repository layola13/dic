algorithm: /ˈælɡərɪðəm/ | n. | 算法，计算程序 | A set of rules that are followed in calculations or other problem-solving operations, especially by a computer. |  Example 1:  The algorithm sorts the data efficiently.  这个算法高效地对数据进行排序。

Example 2:  Google uses complex algorithms to rank search results.  谷歌使用复杂的算法对搜索结果进行排名。 | synonyms: procedure, method, process, formula, recipe | antonyms: N/A (Algorithms don't have a direct opposite.) | noun
