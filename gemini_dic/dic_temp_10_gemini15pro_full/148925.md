overwintering:/ˌoʊvərˈwɪntərɪŋ/| n. adj.|过冬，越冬|The act of passing the winter in a particular place.  The process by which a plant or animal survives the winter.|例句：The monarch butterflies' overwintering grounds are in Mexico.  帝王蝶的越冬地在墨西哥。

The overwintering population of bees is smaller than in the summer.  蜜蜂的越冬种群比夏季小。|近义词：hibernation (for animals), dormancy (for plants)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, adjective

overwinter:/ˌoʊvərˈwɪntər/| v. |过冬，越冬|To spend the winter in a particular place.|例句：The birds overwinter in the south.  这些鸟在南方过冬。

Where do these insects overwinter?  这些昆虫在哪里过冬？|近义词：hibernate (for animals)|反义词: N/A|词性: verb
