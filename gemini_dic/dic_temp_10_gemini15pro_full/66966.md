experimentarian:/ɪkˌspɛrɪmənˈtɛəriən/
n.
实验者;  体验派; 乐于尝试新事物的人
A person who likes to try new experiences, especially in relation to food, travel, or lifestyle.
She's a real experimentarian, always trying new restaurants and cuisines. 她是一个真正的体验派，总是尝试新的餐馆和菜肴。
近义词: innovator, pioneer, adventurer, explorer
反义词: traditionalist, conservative
词性: 名词

Etymology:  From "experiment" + "-arian" (a suffix forming nouns meaning "one who practices or adheres to," from Latin -arius).  Essentially, "one who practices experimentation."
