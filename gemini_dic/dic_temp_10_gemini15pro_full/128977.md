nip-away:/nɪp əˈweɪ/
verb phrase
逐渐减少，蚕食，一点点地除去
to gradually remove or reduce something, bit by bit.
The company's profits have been nipped away by rising costs. 公司的利润已经被不断上涨的成本蚕食了。
The constant criticism nipped away at her confidence.  不断的批评逐渐削弱了她的自信心。
近义词: erode, diminish, whittle away, chip away
反义词: augment, increase, build up
词性: 动词短语

词源:  "nip" comes from Old English *hnipian "to castrate, pinch," related to Dutch knijpen "to pinch," German kneifen "to pinch, nip."  "Away" indicates removal or separation.
