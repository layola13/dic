DDT's: /ˌdiː diː ˈtiːz/ | n. |滴滴涕（的复数形式，Dichlorodiphenyltrichloroethane的缩写）| DDT is a powerful insecticide that was widely used in the past but is now banned in many countries due to its harmful effects on the environment and human health.  It's often used in its plural form, DDT's, when referring to multiple applications or instances of the chemical. | The use of DDT's was common in agriculture in the mid-20th century.  滴滴涕在20世纪中叶的农业中很常见。|  The negative impact of DDT's on bird populations led to its ban. 滴滴涕对鸟类种群的负面影响导致了它的禁用。 | synonyms: insecticide, pesticide | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider) natural predator, biological control | noun
