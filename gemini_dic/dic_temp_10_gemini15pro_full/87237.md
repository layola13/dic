heretical:/həˈretɪkəl/
adjective
异端的；异教的；旁门左道的
Holding beliefs or opinions that are contrary to or at variance with established or accepted doctrines, especially in religion.
His heretical views on the Trinity led to his excommunication from the church. 他对三位一体的异端观点导致他被逐出教会。
Synonyms: unorthodox, dissenting, apostate, blasphemous, iconoclastic
Antonyms: orthodox, conventional, conformist
词性: 形容词

heretical:/həˈretɪkəl/
adjective
异端的；异教的；旁门左道的
Characterized by departure from accepted beliefs or standards.
The professor's heretical approach to economics was met with skepticism.  这位教授对经济学的异端方法遭到了怀疑。
Synonyms: unconventional, nonconformist, heterodox
Antonyms: conventional, traditional, conformist
词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *haereticalis, from Latin haereticus (“heretical”), from Ancient Greek αἱρετικός (hairetikós, “able to choose”), from αἱρέομαι (hairéomai, “I choose”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵer- (“to grasp, take”).
