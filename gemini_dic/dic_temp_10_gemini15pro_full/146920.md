overintellectualized: /ˌoʊvərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəlaɪzd/
adjective
过度理智化的；过分理性分析的
Characterized by excessive intellectual activity or analysis, often to the point of impracticality or inaction.  Thinking too much and feeling too little.
His approach to the problem was overintellectualized and lacked common sense. 他处理这个问题的方法过于理性化，缺乏常识。
Synonyms: overanalyzed, overthought, cerebral, abstract
Antonyms: instinctive, intuitive, visceral, pragmatic
词性: 形容词
