movably:/ˈmuːvəbli/| adv.|可移动地|In a way that can be moved. |The screen is movably mounted on the wall. 屏幕可移动地安装在墙上。|Synonyms: portably, transportably|Antonyms: fixedly, immovably|Adverb

Etymology->
From "movable" + "-ly".  "Movable" comes from the Old French "movable," from "mover" (to move), ultimately from Latin "movēre" (to move).
