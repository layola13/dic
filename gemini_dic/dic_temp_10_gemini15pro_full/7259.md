anglomaniacs:/ˌæŋɡləˈmeɪniæks/
|n.|盎格鲁狂；亲英派；哈英族|People who are obsessed with English culture, customs, and the English way of life.|Those anglomaniacs insist on having afternoon tea every day. 那些哈英族坚持每天都要喝下午茶。|Synonyms: Anglophile|Antonyms: Anglophobe|noun
