angiocarpous:/ˌændʒiˈoʊkɑːrpəs/
|adj.|
|被子植物的，有封闭子房的|
|Having the fruit enclosed within a covering, as in the apple.|
|The apple tree is an example of an angiocarpous plant because its seeds are enclosed within the fruit.|苹果树是被子植物的一个例子，因为它的种子被包裹在果实内。

|Angiocarpous plants protect their developing seeds within a pericarp.|被子植物的种子在果皮内受到保护。

|近义词: covered-seeded, angiospermous|
|反义词: gymnocarpous|
|词性: adjective|
