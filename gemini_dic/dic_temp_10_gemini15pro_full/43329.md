cramoisy:/kræˈmwɑːzi/
adjective
深红色的，鲜红色的
Of a rich crimson color.
The king wore a cramoisy robe. 国王穿着一件深红色的长袍。

crimson, scarlet, ruby, vermilion
pale, dull, drab

Etymology:
From Middle French cramoisi, from Old Spanish cremesí, from Arabic قرمز qirmiz, from Sanskrit कृमिज kṛmija (“produced by a worm,” referring to the kermes insect, a source of crimson dye).
