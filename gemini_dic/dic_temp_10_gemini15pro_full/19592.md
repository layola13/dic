bigging:/ˈbɪɡɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
夸大；吹嘘；使…显得重要；
Making something seem more important or impressive than it really is, often by exaggerating.
He was bigging up his role in the project. 他一直在夸大自己在项目中的作用。
近义词: exaggerating, boasting, inflating, promoting, hyping
反义词: downplaying, minimizing, understating
词性: v.

bigging:/ˈbɪɡɪŋ/
adjective
重要的；大的；(非正式)重要的，有影响力的
Important, significant; (informal) influential.
It's not that bigging a deal. 这不是什么大不了的事。
近义词: important, significant, influential, major, substantial
反义词: unimportant, insignificant, minor, trivial
词性: adj.

词源：
"Bigging" comes from the verb "big," meaning large or important.  The "-ing" suffix turns it into a present participle, which can function as an adjective or verb.  Its informal usage as a verb meaning "to exaggerate or boast" likely developed through slang and colloquial speech.
