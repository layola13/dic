outpaced:/ˌaʊtˈpeɪst/ | v. | 超越，超过 | to go faster, achieve more, or develop more quickly than someone or something else |  The company outpaced its rivals in the race to develop new technologies.  这家公司在开发新技术的竞赛中超过了竞争对手。 | Example:  Inflation has outpaced wage growth this year. 今年通货膨胀超过了工资增长。| Synonyms: surpassed, exceeded, outran, overtook | Antonyms: lagged behind, trailed, fallen behind | verb

outpaced:/ˌaʊtˈpeɪst/ | v. | 超越，胜过 | to perform better than someone or something else |  She easily outpaced the other runners. 她轻松地超过了其他跑步者。 | Example: The smaller company quickly outpaced its larger competitors in sales. 这家小公司在销售额上很快就超过了更大的竞争对手。| Synonyms: outstripped, outdid, bested | Antonyms: underperformed, lost to | verb
