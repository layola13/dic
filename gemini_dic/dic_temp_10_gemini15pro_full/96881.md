inexorably:/ˌɪnˈɛksərəbli/| adv.|不可阻挡地，必然地|In a way that is impossible to stop or prevent.|The disease progressed inexorably.  这种疾病无情地发展。|例句：The country is sliding inexorably into recession. 这个国家正在不可避免地滑向衰退。|近义词：relentlessly, inevitably, unavoidably|反义词:avoidably, preventably|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin inexorabilis "unyielding," from in- "not" + exorabilis "able to be moved by entreaty," from ex- "out" + orare "to pray, plead."
