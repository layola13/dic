anglicisms:/ˈæŋɡlɪsɪzəmz/| n.|英语化；英语化的表达方式|A word or phrase borrowed from English by another language.|Many anglicisms have entered the French language. 许多英语词汇已经融入了法语。|loanwords, borrowings| |noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *anglicisme*, from *anglais* "English" (from Old French *engleis*) + *-isme* "-ism". Ultimately from Latin *Angli* "Angles".
