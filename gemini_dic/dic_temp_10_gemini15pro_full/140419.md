okapi:/oʊˈkɑːpi/ | n. | 霍加狓 |A central African mammal (Okapia johnstoni) related to the giraffe but having a much shorter neck and zebra-like stripes on the legs and hindquarters.|  The okapi, a shy and elusive creature, is native to the dense rainforests of central Africa.  霍加狓是一种害羞且难以捉摸的生物，原产于非洲中部茂密的雨林。| synonym:  forest giraffe | antonym: None (It's a unique species) | noun
