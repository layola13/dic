desilver:/diːˈsɪlvər/
verb
去除银
To remove silver from something, especially from a coin or other object made of silver.
The alchemists tried to desilver the coins. 炼金术士试图从硬币中去除银。
近义词: extract silver, remove silver
反义词: silver-plate, silver
