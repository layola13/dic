optimists: /ˈɒptɪmɪsts/ (UK) /ˈɑːptɪmɪsts/ (US) | n. |乐观主义者| People who tend to expect good outcomes and focus on the positive aspects of situations. |例句：The optimists believe the team will win the championship. 乐观主义者相信球队会赢得冠军。|例句：Despite the challenges, the optimists remained confident. 尽管面临挑战，乐观主义者仍然保持自信。|近义词：positive thinkers, hopeful people |反义词: pessimists |词性: noun


Etymology: From Late Latin optimus (“best”) + -ist.  "Optimus" itself comes from Latin ops ("power, resources, wealth") + -timus (superlative suffix).
