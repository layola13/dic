absolutenesses:/ˈæbsəluːtnəsɪz/
n.
绝对性；绝对状态
The state or quality of being absolute.
The absolutenesses of his pronouncements left no room for debate. 他的声明的绝对性不容置疑。
His pronouncements had an air of absolutenesses about them. 他的声明带有一种绝对的意味。

近义词:absoluteness, certainty, definiteness
反义词:relativity, uncertainty, indefiniteness
词性:noun (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From absolute + -ness.  "Absolute" comes from Late Latin absolut-us, past participle of absolvere "to set free, acquit," from ab- "from" + solvere "to loosen."  "-ness" is a native English suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being."
