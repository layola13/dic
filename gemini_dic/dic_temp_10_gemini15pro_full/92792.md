词源:
"Ickie" is likely a playful and childish derivation of "icky," which itself is an informal adjective meaning "sticky and unpleasant" or "disgusting."  It likely originated through reduplication and sound symbolism, mimicking the sound and feeling of something slimy or unpleasant.
