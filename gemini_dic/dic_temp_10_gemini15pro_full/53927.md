discoursing:/dɪˈskɔːsɪŋ/
verb
讲述；论述；谈论
present participle of discourse
He was discoursing on the subject of political economy. 他正在谈论政治经济学这个话题。
Synonyms: talking, speaking, conversing, discussing, holding forth, expatiating
Antonyms: listening, being quiet, silent
词性: 动词 (verb)
