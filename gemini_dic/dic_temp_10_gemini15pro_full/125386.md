multimolecular:/ˌmʌltiˈməˈlɛkjʊlər/ | adj. | 多分子的 | Formed by the aggregation of many molecules.  Composed of many molecules. | The formation of multimolecular micelles occurs above a certain surfactant concentration. | 近义词: polymeric, macromolecular | 反义词: monomolecular, unimolecular | 词性: adjective

Etymology: multi- (Latin: many, much) + molecular (from molecule, ultimately from Latin moles "mass, barrier")
