noir:/nwɑːr/(US) /nwaː(r)/(UK)| adj. n.|黑色的，黑色电影；黑色小说|A genre of crime film or fiction characterized by cynicism, moral ambiguity, and a sense of doom.  A dark or gloomy atmosphere or mood.|例句：The film is a classic example of film noir. 这部电影是黑色电影的经典范例。

The city has a noir atmosphere at night. 这座城市在夜晚有一种黑色电影般的氛围。|近义词：dark, gloomy, cynical, pessimistic|反义词：bright, cheerful, optimistic|词性：adjective, noun

Etymology: From French *noir*, meaning "black."
