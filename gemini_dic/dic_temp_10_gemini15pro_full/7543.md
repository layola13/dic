annexment:/əˈnɛksmənt/
n.
附件; 附加物; 合并
The annexment of the territory caused international concern.  这个地区的合并引起了国际关注。
The annexment of the smaller company was completed quickly.  对小公司的合并很快就完成了。
English Explanation:The act of annexing something, especially territory.  It refers to the process or result of adding something, typically land or a building, to something else that is already larger or more important.
例句：The annexment of the island to the kingdom expanded its territory. 该岛屿被王国吞并，扩大了其领土。

例句：The annexment of the new wing to the hospital provided much-needed space.  医院新翼楼的扩建提供了急需的空间。

近义词：acquisition, incorporation, appropriation, merger
反义词：cession, surrender, relinquishment
词性: noun
