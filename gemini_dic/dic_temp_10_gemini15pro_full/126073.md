mustier:/ˈmʌstiər/ (US), /ˈmʌstɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更霉的；更陈腐的；更过时的|Smelling or tasting stale, damp, and moldy; outdated, antiquated.|例句：The attic was mustier than I remembered.  阁楼比我记忆中更霉了。

例句：His ideas about education are getting mustier by the day. 他关于教育的观点越来越过时了。|近义词：stale, moldy, fusty, musty|反义词:fresh, airy, modern|词性:形容词
