drogher:/ˈdroʊɡər/ | n. | 平底驳船，拖船，小帆船 | A small sailing vessel, often square-rigged and used for carrying cargo, particularly in coastal waters or rivers, or a vessel towed by another.  A drogher could also refer to a West African coasting vessel. |  The drogher was laden with spices from the East Indies.  平底驳船装载了来自东印度群岛的香料。

 The fishing boats used a drogher to transport their catch back to the harbor.  渔船用拖船把捕获物运回港口。| synonym: barge, lighter, coaster, towboat | antonym: tugboat (sometimes, as a drogher can *be* towed), ship (in terms of size) | noun
