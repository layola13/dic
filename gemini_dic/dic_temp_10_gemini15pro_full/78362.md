gestatory:/ˈdʒɛstətɔːri/
adjective
妊娠的，怀孕的
Relating to pregnancy or gestation.
例句：
The gestatory period for elephants is about 22 months.
大象的妊娠期约为 22 个月。

The hormonal changes during the gestatory period can be quite dramatic.
妊娠期的荷尔蒙变化可能非常剧烈。

近义词：pregnant, gravid
反义词：non-pregnant
