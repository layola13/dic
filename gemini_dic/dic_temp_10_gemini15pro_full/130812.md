nonapostolical: /ˌnɒnˌæpəˈstɒlɪkəl/
adjective
非使徒的；不符合使徒传统的
Not of or relating to the apostles or their teachings; not derived from or conforming to the New Testament.
The group held nonapostolical beliefs, rejecting the authority of the apostles. 这个团体持有非使徒的信仰，拒绝使徒的权威。
Some scholars argue that certain texts are nonapostolical and should not be included in the biblical canon. 一些学者认为某些文本是非使徒的，不应该被包括在圣经正典中。
Near Synonyms: unorthodox, heterodox, heretical
Antonyms: apostolic, orthodox, canonical
word class: adjective
