Etymology: From Middle High German genuß, from Old High German ginuʒʒi, from Proto-Germanic *ganuzjaną (“to use, enjoy”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵenu- (“to make use of, to enjoy”).  Cognate with Dutch genot, German Genuss, Gothic 𐌲𐌰𐌽𐌿𐍄𐍃 (ganuts).
