enlarges:/ɪnˈlɑːrdʒɪz/
verb
扩大；增大；放大
makes something bigger or greater.
The company enlarged its market share. 公司扩大了市场份额。
synonyms: expands, increases, amplifies, augments, magnifies
antonyms: shrinks, reduces, diminishes, decreases, contracts
verb
