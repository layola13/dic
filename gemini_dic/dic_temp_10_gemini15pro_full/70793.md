fingerings:/ˈfɪŋɡərɪŋz/
n.
指法;指按法
The way in which a musician uses their fingers to play a musical instrument.
The guitarist's intricate fingerings brought the melody to life.  吉他手复杂的指法使旋律栩栩如生。
