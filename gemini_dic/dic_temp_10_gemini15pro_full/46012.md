Etymology:

* cybernetics (n.):  From French *cybernétique*, from Ancient Greek κυβερνητικός (kubernētikós, “good at steering, governor”)
* -ist (suffix):  Forms agent nouns.  From French -iste and Latin -ista, from Greek -ιστής (-istḗs).
* -'s (suffix):  Possessive suffix. From Old English -es.
