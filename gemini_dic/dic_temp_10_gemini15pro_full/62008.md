enamels:/ɪˈnæməlz/
enamels| n.|珐琅；瓷釉；指甲油|A glossy substance used to coat metal or pottery for protection or decoration, or for teeth.|The artist used vibrant enamels to decorate the vase.|近义词：lacquer, glaze, varnish, paint|反义词:None|Noun

enamels:/ɪˈnæməlz/
enamels| v.|涂珐琅；使光滑|To coat or decorate with enamel.|She enameled the jewelry with intricate designs.|近义词：lacquer, glaze, varnish, paint|反义词:strip, remove|Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *esmaillier (modern French émailler), from esmal (modern French émail), from Frankish *smalt, from Proto-Germanic *smaltaz ("smalt," a kind of blue enamel). Related to Old English smeltan (“to melt, refine”), German schmelzen (“to melt”).
