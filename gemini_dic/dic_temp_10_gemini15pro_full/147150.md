overlording:/ˌoʊvərˈlɔːrdɪŋ/| n. |专横跋扈；作威作福；过度控制|The act of dominating or controlling others excessively, often in a tyrannical or oppressive manner.|He resented his boss's overlording and constant micromanagement. 他憎恨老板的专横跋扈和事事干预。|The company's overlording of the market stifled competition. 该公司对市场的过度控制扼杀了竞争。|近义词：domination, tyranny, oppression, control|反义词：submission, cooperation, freedom|词性:名词

Etymology: From "overlord" + "-ing" (present participle suffix). "Overlord" comes from Old French "overe seignur," meaning "superior lord."
