disrout:/dɪsˈraʊt/
| v. | 击败, 溃败, 使混乱 | To defeat and cause to scatter in disorder; rout. | The army was completely disrouted by the enemy. 这支军队被敌人彻底击溃了。 |
| v. | 使混乱, 使狼狈 | To throw into confusion or disorganization. | The sudden storm disrouted our travel plans. 突如其来的暴风雨打乱了我们的旅行计划。 |
| n. | 溃败, 混乱 |  A complete defeat and disorderly retreat. | The battle ended in a disrout for the rebel forces. 这场战斗以叛军的溃败告终。|
| 近义词 | defeat, rout, conquer, overthrow, scatter, confuse, disorder |
| 反义词 | organize, order, rally |
| 词性 | 动词 (v.), 名词 (n.) |
| 词源 | From Old French *desrouter, from des- (expressing reversal) + route (“route, company”). |
