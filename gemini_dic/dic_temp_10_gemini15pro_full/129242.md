noctule:/ˈnɒktjuːl/ (US) /ˈnɒktʃuːl/ (UK)| n. |夜蝠，大黄蝠|A large bat (Nyctalus noctula) found in Europe and Asia, known for its fast, high flight.|例句：The noctule is one of the largest bat species in Europe.  欧洲最大的蝙蝠种类之一是大黄蝠。

例句：Researchers tracked the noctule's migration patterns using radio telemetry. 研究人员使用无线电遥测技术追踪了夜蝠的迁徙模式。|近义词：bat|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *noctula, diminutive of *nox, meaning "night".  The scientific name, Nyctalus, also derives from the Greek word for night, "nýx".
