macerators:/ˈmæsəˌreɪtərz/| n.|浸渍器，软化剂|A machine that reduces solids to a pulp or slurry.  These are commonly used in wastewater treatment plants and in the food processing industry. |The macerators ground the food waste into small pieces.  浸渍器将食物残渣磨成小块。|Synonyms: grinders, pulverizers, crushers|Antonyms: solidifiers, hardeners|noun

macerator:/ˈmæsəˌreɪtər/| n.|浸渍器，软化剂|A machine or substance that softens or breaks down something, typically by soaking.  This can refer to tools used in cooking, labs, or industrial processes. |The chef used a macerator to tenderize the meat.  厨师用浸渍器使肉变嫩。|Synonyms: softener, tenderizer, disintegrator|Antonyms: hardener, solidifier|noun
