Multiple factors contributed to the ineligiblenesses of several applicants.|Several ineligiblenesses prevented him from running for office. 几个不合格之处使他无法竞选公职。| 近义词: disqualifications, unsuitabilities| 反义词: eligibilities, qualifications| 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From ineligible + -ness.  Ineligible comes from in- (not) + eligible (suitable, qualified), which ultimately derives from the Latin eligere (to choose).
