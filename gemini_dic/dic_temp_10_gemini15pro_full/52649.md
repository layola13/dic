digitalized:/ˈdɪdʒɪtəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 数字化的 | Converted from analog to digital form; available in digital form. | The library has digitalized all its old manuscripts. 图书馆已将其所有旧手稿数字化。 |  The company has digitalized its entire archive.  公司已将其全部档案数字化。 | 近义词: digitized, electronic, computerized | 反义词: analog, non-digital | 词性:形容词

词源: From digital + -ize + -ed.  源自 digital (数字的) + -ize (使…化) + -ed (过去分词后缀).
