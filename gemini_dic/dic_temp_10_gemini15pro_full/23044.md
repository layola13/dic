ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *bondir, *bomber "to bump, swell," from Late Latin bombitire, frequentative of Latin bombus "a booming, humming sound."  The sense evolution likely went from a sound to the action of something hitting and making a sound, then to the rebounding motion.
