chanai: /tʃəˈnaɪ/ | n. |  Chennai (formerly Madras) | A city in southeastern India, the capital of Tamil Nadu, on the Bay of Bengal. Formerly known as Madras. |  Chennai is a major cultural and economic center.  钦奈是主要的文化和经济中心。|  Synonyms: Madras (archaic), City of Chennai | Antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology: From Madrasapatnam, the name of a fishing village north of Fort St. George.  The name Chennai is believed to be derived from the name of a local Telugu ruler, Chennappa Nayakudu.
