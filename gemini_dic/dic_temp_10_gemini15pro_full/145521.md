overcolor:/ˈoʊvərˌkʌlər/| v. n.|套色，套印，覆色|To print or color over something, especially with a different color; an additional color printed over another.|例句：He overcolored the photograph to make the sky look more dramatic. 他给照片套色，让天空看起来更引人注目。|例句：The overcolor gave the image a vintage feel. 覆色让图像有一种复古的感觉。|近义词：overlay, superimpose, tint|反义词：undercolor|词性：动词/名词
