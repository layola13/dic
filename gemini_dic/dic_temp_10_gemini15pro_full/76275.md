fyke:/faɪk/
noun
鱼梁，锥形网
A fyke is a type of fish trap, typically made of netting stretched over hoops, forming a cone-shaped structure with one or more chambers.
The fishermen set several fykes along the riverbank. 渔民们沿着河岸设置了几个鱼梁。
Synonyms: fish trap, fish weir, pound net
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider "open water" or "free-flowing river" as contrasting concepts)
