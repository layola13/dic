halos:/ˈheɪloʊz/ (US) /ˈheɪləʊz/ (UK)| n.|光环，晕，神像之光环|A circle of light appearing to surround the sun or moon, or a similar effect around other lights.  A bright circle depicted behind or above the head of a holy person, especially in a painting or sculpture.|例句：The sun had a halo around it. 太阳周围有一个光晕。|例句：The painting depicts an angel with a halo. 这幅画描绘了一个头顶光环的天使。|近义词：aura, nimbus, ring, corona|反义词: None| Noun
