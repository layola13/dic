guarantors:/ˈɡærənˌtɔːrz/
n.
保证人，担保人
A person or organization that promises to pay a debt or meet an obligation if the original party fails to do so.
The loan agreement requires two guarantors.  贷款协议需要两个担保人。
My uncle acted as guarantor for my student loan. 我叔叔为我的学生贷款做了担保人。
近义词: surety, sponsor, backer
反义词:  N/A (No direct antonym)
词性: noun (plural)
