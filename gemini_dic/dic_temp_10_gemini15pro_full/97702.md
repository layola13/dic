inimical:/ɪˈnɪmɪkəl/
adjective
有害的；不利的；敌意的
tending to obstruct or harm; unfriendly; hostile.
例句：Excessive bureaucracy is inimical to efficiency. 过多的官僚主义不利于效率。

例句：The policy was seen as inimical to the interests of small businesses. 这项政策被认为对小型企业的利益有害。

近义词：harmful, detrimental, hostile, antagonistic, adverse, unfriendly
反义词：beneficial, advantageous, friendly, helpful, supportive
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inimicalis, from Latin inimicus (“enemy, unfriendly”), from in- (“not”) + amicus (“friend”).
