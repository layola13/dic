insinuatory:/ɪnˈsɪnjuːətɔːri/ /ɪnˈsɪnjuːeɪtɔːri/
adjective
暗示的；含沙射影的；影射的
Suggesting or implying something bad or discreditable in an indirect way.
例句：He made an insinuatory remark about her past. 他含沙射影地说了她过去的事。

例句：Her insinuatory tone made him uncomfortable. 她那影射的语气让他感到不舒服。

近义词：suggestive, implying, hinting, allusive, innuendo
反义词：direct, explicit, forthright
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin insinuatorius, from insinuare "to introduce gently, insert by windings or turnings," from in- "into" + sinuare "to curve, bend," from sinus "curve, fold."
