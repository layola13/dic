first-ever:/fɜːrstˈevər/ | adj. | 首次的；空前的 |  Meaning "the very first," from "first" + "ever."  It emphasizes that something has never happened before. | This is the first-ever time I've baked a cake. 这是我第一次烤蛋糕。|  inaugural, initial, premier | subsequent, later | adjective

first-ever:/fɜːrstˈevər/ | n. | 首次发生的事情；空前事件 |  A noun form, less common, referring to the event itself. | The first-ever landing on the moon was a momentous occasion.  人类首次登月是一个重大的时刻。| debut, premiere |  repetition, sequel | noun
