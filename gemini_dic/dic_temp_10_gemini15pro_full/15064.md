ETYMOLOGY->
From babyish + -ness + -'s (possessive).  Babyish is derived from baby + -ish.  Baby ultimately comes from Middle English babi, likely imitative of baby talk.  The suffix -ness forms nouns denoting a state or quality.  The final -'s indicates possession.
