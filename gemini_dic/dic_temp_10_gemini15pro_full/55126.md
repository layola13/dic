disrupture:/dɪsˈrʌptʃər/| n.|分裂，瓦解，破坏|A disruption or interruption of a settled and peaceful condition.|The COVID-19 pandemic caused a major disruption to global travel. 新冠疫情对全球旅行造成了重大破坏。|近义词：disruption, upheaval, disturbance, chaos|反义词:order, stability, harmony|noun

disrupture:/dɪsˈrʌptʃər/| n.|扰乱因素，破坏性因素|Something that disrupts.|New technologies are often seen as disruptors in traditional industries. 新技术通常被视为传统行业的扰乱因素。|近义词：troublemaker, game-changer, revolutionary|反义词:stabilizer, maintainer|noun
