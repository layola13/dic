glimmered:/ˈɡlɪmərd/| v.|闪烁，微光|Shine faintly with a wavering light.|The candle glimmered in the dark room. 蜡烛在黑暗的房间里闪烁着。|The stars glimmered in the night sky. 星星在夜空中闪烁。|近义词：flickered, shimmered, twinkled|反义词：blazed, shone, glared|词性:动词
词源: From Middle English *glimeren, frequentative of glimen (“to gleam”), from Old English glīman (“to gleam, shine brightly”). Related to Dutch glimmeren (“to glimmer, glitter”), German glimmern (“to glimmer”), Swedish glimma (“to gleam, shine, flash”).
