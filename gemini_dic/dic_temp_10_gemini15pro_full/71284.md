fixed-wing:/ˈfɪkstˌwɪŋ/| adj.|固定翼的|Having wings that are fixed and do not move, like those of an airplane.|例句：Fixed-wing aircraft are more fuel-efficient than helicopters. 固定翼飞机比直升机更省油。|例句：The airport primarily caters to fixed-wing aircraft, not helicopters.  这个机场主要服务于固定翼飞机，而不是直升机。|近义词：conventional-wing|反义词:rotary-wing|词性:形容词

fixed:/fɪkst/| adj.|固定的|Fastened securely in position.|词源：From Middle English *fixen*, from Old French *fixer*, from Latin *fixus*, past participle of *figere* (“to fix, fasten”).|

wing:/wɪŋ/| n.|翅膀，翼|A modified forelimb that enables a bird or other creature to fly.|词源：From Middle English *winge*, from Old Norse *vængr*, from Proto-Germanic *wengaz* (“wing”).|
