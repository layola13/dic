childlikenesses:/ˈtʃaɪldˌlaɪknəsɪz/
n.
孩子气，幼稚的举动
The quality or state of being childlike; characteristics or behavior typical of a child, often in a positive sense, suggesting innocence, simplicity, or naiveté.
She retained a certain childlikeness in her old age, always eager for a new adventure.  她晚年仍然保持着某种孩子气，总是渴望新的冒险。
近义词：innocence, naiveté, simplicity, artlessness, ingenuousness
反义词：maturity, sophistication, worldliness
词性: Noun


childlike:/ˈtʃaɪldlaɪk/
adj.
像孩子一样的
Having qualities associated with a child, such as innocence, simplicity, or naiveté.
He had a childlike wonder about the world. 他对世界充满了孩子般的好奇。
近义词：innocent, naive, simple, artless, ingenuous
反义词：mature, sophisticated, worldly
词性: Adjective
