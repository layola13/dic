alternation:/ˌɔːltərˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 交替，轮流，间隔 | the action or process of alternating or being alternated | The alternation of day and night is a natural phenomenon.  白天和黑夜的交替是一种自然现象。 | synonyms: rotation, interchange, oscillation | antonyms: continuation, constancy, stability | noun

alternation:/ˌɔːltərˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 交替发生的事物；交替出现的两种状态之一 | one of a number of things that alternate with each other |  The bridge is open to traffic on alternate days. 这座桥每隔一天开放交通。 | synonyms: choice, option, alternative | antonyms:  singularity, uniformity | noun
