nonponderously:/ˌnɒnˈpɒndərəsli/
| adv. | 轻快地，不笨重地 | In a manner that is not ponderous; lightly and easily. |  She danced nonponderously across the stage, despite her elaborate costume. 她穿着精致的服装，轻盈地舞动着穿过舞台。 | Synonyms: lightly, nimbly, agilely, effortlessly | Antonyms: ponderously, heavily, clumsily | adverb

Etymology:
From non- (not) + ponderous (heavy, weighty) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Ponderous" comes from the Old French "ponderos" and ultimately from the Latin "ponderosus," meaning "weighty."
