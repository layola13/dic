generalise:/ˈdʒɛn(ə)rəlaɪz/ /ˈdʒɛnərəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 概括；推广；普遍化 | To infer or form a conclusion/principle from particular facts or instances; to make something more widespread or common. | Scientists are hesitant to generalise their findings from a small study. 科学家们不愿从小样本研究中概括出他们的发现。

She generalised her experience into a set of guidelines for other teachers. 她将自己的经验概括成一套供其他教师参考的指导方针。| 近义词: summarize, abstract, extrapolate, infer, deduce | 反义词: specify, particularize | 词性: 动词
