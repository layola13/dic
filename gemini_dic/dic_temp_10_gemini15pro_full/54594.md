dismantlers:/dɪsˈmæntlərz/
n.
拆卸者，拆除者
People or entities that dismantle something.  Those who take apart machines, structures, or organizations.
The dismantlers carefully removed the hazardous materials from the old factory.  拆卸人员小心地从旧工厂中移除了危险材料。
The dismantlers were experts at taking apart complicated machinery.  这些拆卸人员是拆卸复杂机器的专家。
Synonyms: deconstructors, disassemblers
Antonyms: assemblers, constructors
Noun

dismantle:/dɪsˈmæntl/
vt.
拆卸，拆除；解散
To take apart a machine or structure. To break down an organization or system.
They will dismantle the old bridge next week.  他们下周将拆除那座旧桥。
The government decided to dismantle the state-owned company. 政府决定解散这家国有企业。
Synonyms: disassemble, deconstruct, take apart
Antonyms: assemble, construct, build
Verb
