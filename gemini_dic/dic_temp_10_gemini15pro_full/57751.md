dreamiest:/ˈdriːmiɪst/| adj.|最梦幻的，最空想的|Superlative form of dreamy: most dreamy.  It means extremely pleasant and soothing, as if belonging to a dream; given to or characterized by daydreaming; vague; impractical.|例句：She had the dreamiest smile I'd ever seen. 她有着我见过的最梦幻的笑容。|例句：He planned a dreamiest vacation to a tropical island. 他计划了一个最梦幻的热带岛屿假期。|近义词：most fanciful, most romantic, most ideal, most idyllic|反义词：most realistic, most practical, harshest, starkest|词性:形容词
