luxuriously:/lʌɡˈʒʊəriəsli/
adv.
豪华地，奢侈地
in a rich and comfortable way, suggesting great expense
She stretched out luxuriously on the sofa. 她舒舒服服地躺在沙发上。
近义词：opulently, lavishly, sumptuously
反义词：simply, plainly, austerely
词性: 副词

词源:  From luxury + -ously.  "Luxury" comes from the Old French "luxurie" (excess, extravagance), ultimately from the Latin "luxuria" (abundance, profusion).
