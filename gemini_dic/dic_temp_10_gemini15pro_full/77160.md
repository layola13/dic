gasket:/ˈɡæskɪt/| n.|垫片，密封垫|A gasket is a mechanical seal which fills the space between two or more mating surfaces, generally to prevent leakage from or into the joined objects while under compression.|The engine's head gasket needs replacing.|Synonyms: seal, washer, packing|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "gap" in a loose sense)|noun


gasket:/ˈɡæskɪt/| n.|衬垫，填料|A gasket can also refer to material used as padding or filler.|He used a rubber gasket to protect the delicate item during shipping.|Synonyms: padding, cushioning, filler|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym)|noun
