outstating: /ˌaʊtˈsteɪtɪŋ/
adjective
夸大的，过分陈述的
Exaggerating the importance or quality of something.
例句：His claims about the product's effectiveness were outstating.  他关于产品功效的说法过于夸大。

例句：She has an outstating belief in her own abilities. 她对自己能力的认知过于夸大。

近义词：exaggerated, overstated, inflated
反义词：understated, modest

词性: adjective
