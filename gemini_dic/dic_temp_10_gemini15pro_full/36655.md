collegiately:/kəˈliːdʒiətli/ | adv. | 大学生似地；大学风气地；学会会员似地 | In a manner characteristic of college students or college life. |  He dressed collegiately, even after graduation.  他即使毕业后也穿得像个大学生。| 近义词: academically, scholastically | 反义词: unacademically, professionally | 词性: 副词
