hermetical:/hɜːˈmɛtɪkəl/ | adj. | 密封的；炼金术的；深奥的 | Completely sealed; airtight; relating to alchemy; obscure and difficult to understand. | The jar was hermetically sealed to prevent spoilage.  这个罐子被密封以防止腐败变质。 |  The ancient texts were full of hermetical knowledge.  这些古代文献充满了炼金术知识。 | airtight, sealed, impermeable, closed, esoteric, occult, mystical | open, permeable, exoteric | adjective

hermetically:/hɜːˈmɛtɪkli/ | adv. | 密封地；炼金术地 | In an airtight manner; relating to alchemy. | The documents were hermetically sealed in a vault. 文件被密封保存在金库中。 | The group studied the subject hermetically, delving into its mystical aspects.  这个小组深入研究了这个主题，钻研其神秘的方面。 | airtightly, tightly, impenetrably | openly, loosely | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hermeticus, from Greek Hermētikos, from Hermēs Trismegistos "Hermes the Thrice-Greatest," the supposed author of various mystical and alchemical writings. Ultimately from the name of the Greek god Hermes.
