ETYMOLOGY->
From French hémorragie, from Late Latin haemorrhagia, from Ancient Greek αἱμορραγία (haimorrhagía), from αἷμα (haîma, “blood”) +  ῥηγνύναι (rhēgnúnai, “to break, burst”).
