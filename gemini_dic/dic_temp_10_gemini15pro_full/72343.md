flowered:/ˈflaʊərd/ | adj. | 开花的；饰以花的；(脸色)红润的 | Covered or decorated with flowers; having a rosy complexion. | The meadow was flowered with wildflowers.  草地上开满了野花。| The little girl had a flowered, healthy complexion. 小女孩脸色红润健康。| 近义词: blooming, blossoming, flowery | 反义词: flowerless, bare | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the verb "flower" + "-ed" (suffix indicating a state or condition).  Ultimately from Old French "florir" and Latin "florere" (to bloom, blossom, flourish).
