adhortatory:/ədˈhɔːrtətɔːri/
adjective
劝告的；激励的
Serving or intended to exhort; urging to some course of action or conduct.
The general delivered an adhortatory speech to his troops before the battle. 将军在战斗前向他的部队发表了鼓舞士气的讲话。

The coach's adhortatory words inspired the team to victory. 教练激励人心的话鼓舞了球队走向胜利。

近义词:hortatory, encouraging, inspiring, urging, exhortative
反义词:discouraging, dissuasive, deterrent
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adhortātōrius, from Latin adhortārī (“to exhort”), from ad- (“to”) + hortārī (“to urge, encourage”).

USAGE->
Attributive Use (used before a noun):
He gave an adhortatory speech to the graduates. 他给毕业生们做了一个激励人心的演讲。
The adhortatory message in the book inspired many readers. 书中劝诫性的信息激励了许多读者。

Predicative Use (used after a linking verb):
Her tone was adhortatory, urging them to do their best. 她的语气是劝勉的，敦促他们尽力而为。
The sermon was deeply adhortatory, calling for peace and unity. 布道极具劝诫性，呼吁和平与团结。
