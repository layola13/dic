misremember:/ˌmɪsrɪˈmɛmbər/ | v. |记错，误记|To remember incorrectly; have a false memory of.| I misremembered the date of the meeting and arrived a day late. 我记错了会议日期，晚到了一天。|Example:  I thought I had locked the door, but I must have misremembered. 我以为我锁了门，但我一定是记错了。|Synonyms: misrecollect, forget, err |Antonyms: remember, recall, recollect|verb
