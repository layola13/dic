freedom-fighters:/ˈfriːdəm ˌfaɪtərz/| n.| 自由战士 | People who fight for the freedom of themselves or others, often against an oppressive regime or occupying force. |  Nelson Mandela was a famous freedom fighter who fought against apartheid in South Africa. 纳尔逊·曼德拉是一位著名的自由战士，他为反抗南非的种族隔离而斗争。

例句：
Many freedom fighters risked their lives for the cause of liberty.
许多自由战士为了自由事业冒着生命危险。

例句：
The freedom fighters were celebrated as heroes after the war.
战争结束后，自由战士被誉为英雄。

近义词：rebel, revolutionary, insurgent, partisan, resistance fighter
反义词：oppressor, tyrant, dictator
词性：noun (plural)
