folders:/ˈfoʊldərz/ (US) /ˈfəʊldəz/ (UK)| n.|文件夹|A folder is a container used to store documents, photographs, or other items, typically made of stiff paper or cardboard and folded in half.  It can also refer to a virtual location on a computer used to organize files.|I keep all my important documents in folders. 我把所有重要文件都放在文件夹里。|Synonyms: directory, file, portfolio|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "chaos," "disorganization" in a figurative sense) |Noun
