miasmology:/ˌmaɪæzˈmɒlədʒi/
n.
瘴气学;  污秽学
The study of miasmas, or bad smells, believed to be the cause of disease.
Miasmology was a dominant theory before the germ theory of disease.  瘴气学是疾病细菌理论出现之前的流行理论。
Miasmology is now considered an obsolete scientific theory. 瘴气学现在被认为是一种过时的科学理论。
近义词:obsolete theories of disease
反义词:germ theory
词性:noun
