inceration:/ɪnˌsɜːrˈeɪʃən/ | n. |焚烧；烧成灰烬 |The process of burning something completely.  The incineration of waste materials is a controversial method of waste disposal. |例句：The incineration of medical waste is strictly regulated.  医疗废物的焚烧受到严格监管。

例句：The city decided on incineration as the most efficient way to deal with its growing garbage problem.  这座城市决定将焚烧作为处理日益增长的垃圾问题的最有效方法。|近义词：combustion, burning |反义词: extinguishing |词性:noun
