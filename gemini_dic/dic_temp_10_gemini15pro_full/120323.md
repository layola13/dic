minifarms:/ˈmɪniˌfɑːrmz/
n.
小型农场
Small-scale farms, often located near urban areas, focusing on intensive production in a limited space.
Many young people are interested in starting minifarms to grow organic produce. 许多年轻人对开办迷你农场种植有机农产品感兴趣。

近义词:smallholdings, market gardens, urban farms
反义词:large-scale farms, agribusinesses
词性:noun
