lifted:/ˈlɪftɪd/| v. adj.|举起，抬起；提升；振奋；剽窃；解除；消散|The crane lifted the heavy container.|起重机吊起了沉重的集装箱。|近义词：raised, elevated, hoisted, boosted, stolen, removed, dispelled|反义词:lowered, dropped, depressed|词性:动词(v.), 形容词(adj.)

lifted:/ˈlɪftɪd/| adj.|心情愉快的，兴奋的；偷来的；抄袭的|I felt lifted after hearing the good news.|听到好消息后，我感到很振奋。|近义词：elated, cheerful, stolen, plagiarized|反义词:depressed, gloomy|词性:形容词(adj.)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *liften, from Old Norse lypta (“to lift”), from Proto-Germanic *luftijaną (“to lift”), from Proto-Indo-European *lewp- (“to peel, break off, flay”). Cognate with German lüften (“to air, lift”), Dutch luften (“to lift, air”).  The adjective sense "elated" is from the 19th century.
