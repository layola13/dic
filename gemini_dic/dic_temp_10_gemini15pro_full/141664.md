optionable:/ˈɒpʃənəbl/ |adj.| 可选择的，可决定的 | Available as an option; not compulsory. |  This car has optionable leather seats. 这款车可以选择真皮座椅。|  The software comes with several optionable features. 该软件附带几个可选功能。| 近义词: optional, elective, discretionary | 反义词: compulsory, mandatory, obligatory | 词性: adjective

词源:  From "option" + "-able". "Option" comes from the Latin "optio" meaning "choice".  "-able" is a suffix that forms adjectives meaning "capable of being" or "worthy of being".
