endemic:/ɛnˈdɛmɪk/ | adj.  n.| 地方性的；特有的；流行的 | Native to or confined to a particular region or people; characteristic of or prevalent in a particular field, area, or environment. | Malaria is endemic in many hot countries. 疟疾在许多炎热的国家流行。| synonyms: native, indigenous, aboriginal, local, restricted | antonyms: exotic, foreign, alien, pandemic, widespread | adjective


endemic:/ɛnˈdɛmɪk/ | n. | 地方病；风土病 | A disease regularly found among particular people or in a certain area.  |  The doctor was studying the endemic.  医生正在研究这种地方病。 | synonyms: local disease, regional disease | antonyms: pandemic | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French endémique and directly from New Latin endemicus, from Greek endēmos “native,” from en- “in” + dēmos “people.”
