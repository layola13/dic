bridle:/ˈbraɪdl/ /ˈbraɪdəl/| v. n.| 控制，约束；马勒，缰绳 | To restrain or control, especially one's feelings; a head harness for a horse. | She bridled at his suggestion, feeling it was an insult. 她对他的建议感到愤怒，觉得这是一种侮辱. | synonyms: restrain, curb, check, control | antonyms: unleash, indulge, vent | verb

bridle:/ˈbraɪdl/ /ˈbraɪdəl/| n.| 马勒，缰绳 | A head harness for a horse, used to control its movement. | The horse's bridle was adorned with silver studs.  这匹马的马勒上装饰着银钉。| synonyms: harness, reins | antonyms: N/A | noun

bridled:/ˈbraɪdld/ /ˈbraɪdəld/| adj.| 受约束的，控制的 | Showing restraint or controlled anger or resentment. | Her bridled anger was evident in her tightly clenched fists. 她紧握的拳头显示出她压抑的愤怒。| synonyms: restrained, controlled, curbed | antonyms: unrestrained, uncontrolled, unbridled | adjective
