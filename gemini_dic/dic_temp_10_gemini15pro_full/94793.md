impugners:/ɪmˈpjuːnərz/| n. |质疑者，非难者|People who challenge the honesty or validity of something or someone.  Those who attack or criticize someone's motives, integrity, or reputation.|例句：The politician's impugners questioned his commitment to environmental protection. 这位政治家的质疑者质疑他对环境保护的承诺。

例句：Her research findings were met with resistance from several impugners within the scientific community. 她的研究结果受到了科学界一些质疑者的抵制。|近义词：critics, detractors, challengers, attackers, doubters, skeptics|反义词：supporters, defenders, advocates, believers|词性:noun (plural)
