easylike:/ˈiziˌlaɪk/| adv.|轻松地，毫不费力地|Resembling or characteristic of something done easily; in a relaxed or casual manner.|He caught the ball easylike, as if he'd done it a thousand times. 他轻松地接住了球，就像他已经做过一千次一样。|近义词：easily, effortlessly, casually|反义词: laboriously, painstakingly|词性 副词
词源:  A combination of "easy" and the suffix "-like,"  meaning "similar to" or "in the manner of."  The word is informal and often considered dialectal, particularly associated with Southern American English.
