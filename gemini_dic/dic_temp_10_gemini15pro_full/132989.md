nonenergetic:/ˌnɑːnˌɛnərˈdʒɛtɪk/
adjective
无精打采的；不活跃的；缺乏能量的
Lacking in energy; not energetic.
The patient felt nonenergetic after the surgery.  这个病人在手术后感到浑身无力。
The team gave a nonenergetic performance.  这个团队的表现缺乏活力。

Synonyms: lethargic, listless, inactive, sluggish, apathetic, passive, inert, languid, dormant
Antonyms: energetic, active, vigorous, lively, dynamic, spirited, vibrant

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "energetic" (from Greek "energeia" meaning "activity, operation")
