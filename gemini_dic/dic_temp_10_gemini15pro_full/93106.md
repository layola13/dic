if-push-comes-to-shove:/ɪf pʊʃ kʌmz tə ʃʌv/
idiom
如果情况紧急；如果迫不得已；如果事态严重
If push comes to shove, I'll sell my car to pay for the surgery. 如果情况紧急，我会卖掉我的车来支付手术费用。
If push comes to shove, we can always borrow money from my parents. 如果迫不得已，我们总是可以向我父母借钱。
近义词: if necessary, if things get bad, in a pinch, as a last resort
反义词: under normal circumstances
词性: idiom
