nonexpeditiousness:/nɒnˌɛkspɪˈdɪʃəsnəs/| n.| 不迅速，不敏捷，拖拉 |Lack of speed or promptness; slowness. |The nonexpeditiousness of the bureaucracy frustrated the entrepreneur.  官僚主义的不迅速让企业家感到沮丧。|近义词：slowness, sluggishness, dilatoriness|反义词: speed, rapidity, swiftness|词性:名词

词源:
non- (prefix) + expeditious (adjective) + -ness (suffix)

"non-" 表示“不”或“非”
"expeditious" 源自拉丁语 "expeditus," 过去分词 "expedire," 意思是“释放，解开，准备”，引申为“迅速，敏捷”。
"-ness" 是一个名词后缀，用于将形容词转换为名词，表示状态或性质。
