insurance: /ɪnˈʃʊrəns/ (US), /ɪnˈʃɔːrəns/ (UK)| n.|保险|An arrangement by which a company or the state undertakes to provide a guarantee of compensation for specified loss, damage, illness, or death in return for payment of a specified premium.|I have health insurance through my employer. 我通过我的雇主获得了健康保险。|
insurance: | n.|保险|The act, business, or system of insuring.|The insurance industry is highly regulated. 保险业受到严格监管。|
insurance: | n.|安全措施|Something that provides protection against possible loss or harm.|Having savings is a good insurance against unemployment.  有储蓄是对失业的一种良好保障。|近义词：assurance, guarantee, protection, indemnity|反义词: risk, vulnerability|词性: noun
词源: From Old French enseurance, from enseurer "to make certain, assure," from en- "in" + seur "sure."
