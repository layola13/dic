hadrian:/ˈheɪdriən/| n. |哈德良; 哈德良皇帝 (罗马皇帝)|A Roman emperor (AD 117-138), noted for his extensive travels throughout the Roman Empire and for building Hadrian's Wall.  His reign was marked by consolidation and administrative reforms.|例句：Hadrian's Wall was built to defend the Roman Empire from the Picts.  哈德良长城是为了抵御皮克特人而建造的。

例句：Hadrian was a keen admirer of Greek culture. 哈德良非常仰慕希腊文化。|近义词：emperor, ruler |反义词:subject, citizen|词性:noun
