infuscated:/ɪnˈfʌsˌkeɪtɪd/ | adj. |  昏暗的；模糊的；混乱的；困惑的 | Darkened, obscured, made unclear or confusing.  Also, mentally clouded or confused. | The dense fog infuscated the landscape, making it difficult to see even a few feet ahead.  浓雾使景色变得昏暗，即使几英尺远也难以看清。

The complex legal jargon infuscated the jury, leaving them unsure of the defendant's guilt. 复杂的法律术语使陪审团感到困惑，他们不确定被告是否有罪。 | 近义词: obscured, darkened, clouded, confused, perplexed | 反义词: clear, bright, lucid, distinct, unambiguous | 词性: 形容词
