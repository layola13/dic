floriken:/ˈflɒrɪkən/ (US) /ˈflɔːrɪkən/ (UK)| n. | 花田鸡 |A large bird of the bustard family, found in open grasslands and scrublands of Africa, Asia, and Europe.  They are known for their elaborate courtship displays.| The male floriken performed a spectacular courtship display, leaping and fanning its tail. 雄性花田鸡进行了一场壮观的求偶表演，跳跃并展开尾巴。| The floriken is becoming increasingly rare due to habitat loss. 由于栖息地丧失，花田鸡变得越来越稀少。|近义词: bustard |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

Etymology: From French *floriken*, ultimately from Hindi *kar-rāya*, possibly imitative of its call.
