confederations: /kənˌfedəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. | 联盟，联邦 | A league or alliance, especially of states or nations. |  The southern states formed a confederation during the Civil War. 南方各州在内战期间组成了一个联盟。 | The Iroquois Confederacy was a powerful force in North America. 易洛魁联盟是北美一股强大的力量。 |近义词：alliances, leagues, federations, unions |反义词: divisions, separations | noun

confederation: /kənˌfedəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 联盟，联邦 | The act of confederating or the state of being confederated. | The Articles of Confederation were the first constitution of the United States. 《邦联条例》是美国的第一部宪法。|  The confederation of the thirteen colonies led to the formation of the United States. 十三个殖民地的联盟导致了美利坚合众国的成立。 | 近义词：alliance, league, federation, union | 反义词: division, separation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *confœderātiō, from confœderātus, past participle of confœderāre (“to unite by league”), from con- (“together”) + fœderāre (“to make a treaty”), from fœdus (“treaty, league, compact”).
