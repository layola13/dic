buffeter's:/ˌbʌfɪˈtɪərz/
n.
自助餐者的；自助餐馆的
A place belonging to or associated with a person who frequently eats at buffets, or a buffet restaurant itself.
The buffeter's plate was piled high with food.  自助餐者的盘子里堆满了食物。
The buffeter's selection was impressive. 自助餐馆的选择令人印象深刻。
Synonyms: buffet-goer's, buffet diner's
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider words like "gourmet's" or "connoisseur's" for contrast)
