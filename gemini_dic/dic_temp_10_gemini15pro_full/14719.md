awarenesses:/əˈwɛːrnəsɪz/
|n. (plural)|意识，认识；知道；觉察（awareness 的复数）|The program aims to raise public awarenesses of environmental issues.  这个项目旨在提高公众对环境问题的认识。|
|例句：The campaign aimed to increase awarenesses of the dangers of smoking. 这场运动旨在提高人们对吸烟危害的认识。|
|例句：Growing awarenesses of healthy eating habits are changing people's diets. 对健康饮食习惯日益增长的认识正在改变人们的饮食。|
|近义词：understandings, perceptions, comprehensions|
|反义词：ignorance, unawareness|
|词性：名词|

词源：From awareness + -es (plural suffix).  Awareness itself comes from aware (conscious of something) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Aware ultimately derives from Old English gewær.
