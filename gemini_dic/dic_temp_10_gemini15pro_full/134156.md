nonimpulsive:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpʌlsɪv/ | adj. | 非冲动的，不冲动的 | Not acting or done without forethought; controlled and deliberate. | She took a nonimpulsive approach to the problem, carefully considering all options. 她采取了一种非冲动的方式来处理这个问题，仔细考虑了所有选择。|  He made a nonimpulsive decision to invest his money wisely. 他做出了一个不冲动的决定，明智地投资了他的钱。 | 近义词: thoughtful, deliberate, considered, measured, cautious, circumspect, prudent | 反义词: impulsive, rash, reckless, impetuous, hasty | 词性: 形容词
