multi-tool:/ˈmʌltiˌtuːl/ /ˈmʌltaɪˌtuːl/| n. |多功能工具|A hand tool that combines several individual functions in a single unit. |Example:  He always carries a multi-tool on his camping trips. 他在露营旅行时总是随身携带一把多功能工具。

Example:  The multi-tool proved invaluable when we needed to fix the bike on the trail. 当我们需要在路上修理自行车时，多功能工具被证明是无价的。|近义词：multipurpose tool, Swiss Army knife|反义词:single-purpose tool|词性:名词
