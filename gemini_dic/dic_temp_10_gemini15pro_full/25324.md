bugling:/ˈbjuːɡlɪŋ/| n.|号角声，吹号|The sound produced by a bugle.  The act of playing a bugle.|例句：The bugling echoed across the valley. 号角声在山谷回荡。

例句：The bugling announced the arrival of the cavalry. 吹号声宣告了骑兵的到来。|近义词：trumpeting, sounding|反义词:silence|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bugle + -ing.  "Bugle" comes from Old French "bugle" (wild ox), later applied to the ox-horn used as a hunting horn, and then to the instrument itself.
