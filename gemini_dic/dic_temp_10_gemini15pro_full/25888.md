burlier:/ˈbɜːrlɪər/ | adj. | 更强壮的，更结实的 | More burly; sturdy and strongly built. |  He was much burlier than his brother. 他比他哥哥强壮多了。|  stouter, stronger, huskier | slighter, weaker, thinner | adjective

burly:/ˈbɜːrli/ | adj. | 强壮的，结实的 | Large and strongly built. | The burly bouncer stood guard at the door.  那个身材魁梧的保镖守在门口。|  stout, sturdy, husky | slight, weak, thin | adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *burlich, from Old English *burlic, of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Old English beorgan "to protect, defend, save," suggesting the idea of someone strong enough to offer protection.
