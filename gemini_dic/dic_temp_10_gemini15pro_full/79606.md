gloriole:/ˈɡlɔːriˌoʊl/ /ˈɡlɔːriəl/| n. |光环，荣光|A circle of light or glory, especially depicted around the head or body of a sacred personage; a halo.  An aura of glory, splendor, or magnificence surrounding something.|The saint was depicted with a gloriole around his head. 圣徒的头部被描绘成有一个光环。|Synonyms: halo, aura, nimbus, corona, radiance|Antonyms:  obscurity, dullness|noun
