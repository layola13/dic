boxier:/ˈbɑːksiər/| adj.|更四四方方的，更像盒子的|More box-shaped; more square and compact.|The new TV is boxier than the old one. 新电视比旧电视更方.|近义词：squarer, blockier|反义词：rounder, curvier|词性:形容词
Etymology: From box + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *buxus, from Greek pyxos "boxwood, box tree," also "box" (made of boxwood).
