例句：Researchers are studying the diverse species of ichneumonides to understand their role in the ecosystem. 研究人员正在研究姬蜂科昆虫的多样性，以了解它们在生态系统中的作用。|近义词：parasitic wasps 寄生蜂|反义词: None|词性: Noun
