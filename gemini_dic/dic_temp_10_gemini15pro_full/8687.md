anticorporatenesses:/ˌæntiˌkɔːrpərɪtnəsɪz/
noun
反公司主义，反企业精神
The state of being opposed to or hostile towards corporations, often expressed through activism or consumer choices.
There's a growing anticorporateness among young people. 年轻人中反企业情绪日益高涨。
The documentary explored the anticorporatenesses of the anti-globalization movement.  这部纪录片探讨了反全球化运动的反企业性质。
Synonyms: anti-corporatism, corporate skepticism, corporate distrust
Antonyms: pro-business, pro-corporate, corporate loyalty
