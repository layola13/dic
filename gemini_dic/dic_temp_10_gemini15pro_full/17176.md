batture:/ˈbætʃər/ (US), /bəˈtʊə(r)/ (UK)| n.|沙洲，河漫滩|An elevated area of land along a river that is formed by alluvial deposits and is not usually submerged except during floods.|The house was built on a batture overlooking the Mississippi River. 这座房子建在俯瞰密西西比河的沙洲上。|近义词：sandbar, shoal, alluvial plain|反义词:channel, deep water|词性:名词

词源: From Canadian French *batture*, from French *battre* "to beat," referring to the land beaten by the river.
