backlink:/ˈbækˌlɪŋk/| n.|反向链接|A link from one website to another.  It is also called an inbound link.|Example: High-quality backlinks can improve your website's search engine ranking. 高质量的反向链接可以提高你网站的搜索引擎排名。

Example:  She purchased backlinks to boost her site's visibility. 她购买了反向链接来提高她网站的知名度。|近义词：inbound link, incoming link|反义词:outbound link, external link|词性:noun
