dihedron:/daɪˈhiːdrən/| n.|二面角，二面体|A figure formed by two intersecting planes or half-planes meeting along a common edge.|The dihedral angle of an airplane wing affects its lift.  飞机机翼的二面角会影响其升力。|近义词：angle, corner|反义词: (No direct antonym for a geometric shape)|noun
