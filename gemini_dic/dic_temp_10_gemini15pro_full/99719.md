interluded:/ˌɪntərˈluːdɪd/
v.
插入，穿插
acted as an interlude between the two main events.  在两个主要事件之间充当了插曲。
The comedian interluded his jokes with songs.  这位喜剧演员在笑话中穿插了歌曲。
近义词: interspersed, inserted, intervened
反义词: continued,  proceeded
