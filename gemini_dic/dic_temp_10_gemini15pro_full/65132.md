ethicist:/ˈɛθɪsɪst/| n. |伦理学家；道德学家|A specialist in ethics.  Someone who studies or specializes in the principles of right and wrong conduct.|例句：The ethicist argued that the company's actions were morally reprehensible. 伦理学家认为该公司的行为在道德上应受谴责。

例句：The hospital consulted an ethicist about the difficult medical decision.  医院就这个棘手的医疗决定咨询了一位伦理学家。|近义词：moral philosopher, moralist|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone immoral or unethical in behavior) |词性:noun
