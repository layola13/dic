cider: /ˈsaɪdər/ (US) /ˈsaɪdə(r)/ (UK)| n.|苹果酒，苹果汁|An alcoholic drink made from fermented apple juice.  Sometimes used to refer to unfermented apple juice, especially in North America.|例句：
I like to drink cider in the fall. 我喜欢在秋天喝苹果酒。

We pressed our own cider from the apples in our orchard. 我们用自家果园的苹果榨了苹果汁。

|近义词：apple juice (for non-alcoholic), hard cider (for alcoholic)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: noun
