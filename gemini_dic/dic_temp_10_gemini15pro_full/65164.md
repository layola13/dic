ethnocentricities:/ˌeθnoʊsɛnˈtrɪsɪtiz/ | n. | 民族中心主义 | The belief in the inherent superiority of one's own ethnic group or culture.  A tendency to view alien groups or cultures from the perspective of one's own. |  The company's marketing campaign suffered due to its ethnocentricities, failing to resonate with diverse audiences. 该公司的营销活动因其民族中心主义而受挫，未能引起不同受众的共鸣。

Her ethnocentricities blinded her to the beauty of other cultures. 她的民族中心主义使她对其他文化的美丽视而不见。 | 近义词：cultural bias, prejudice, bigotry, narrow-mindedness | 反义词：cultural relativism, open-mindedness, tolerance | 词性：名词 (plural)
