nonclaimable:/ˌnɒnˈkleɪməbl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈkleɪməbəl/ (UK)
adjective
不可索赔的；不能认领的
Not able to be claimed or demanded as a right.
The airline stated that lost baggage after three months is considered nonclaimable.  这家航空公司声明，三个月后丢失的行李将被视为不可索赔。
The warranty is void for nonclaimable damages.  对于不可索赔的损害，保修无效。
近义词:unclaimable, irrecoverable, nonredeemable
反义词:claimable, recoverable, redeemable
词性:adjective
词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + claimable (adjective derived from the verb "claim")
