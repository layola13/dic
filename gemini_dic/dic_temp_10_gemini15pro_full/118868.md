metascientific:/ˌmɛtəsaɪənˈtɪfɪk/
adjective
元科学的
Relating to the philosophical and methodological study of science.  It examines the assumptions, methods, and goals of science, rather than the specific content of scientific theories.
Metascientific inquiries often explore questions about the nature of scientific explanation, the relationship between theory and observation, and the criteria for evaluating scientific claims.
例句：Metascientific debates about the objectivity of science are ongoing. 关于科学客观性的元科学争论一直在进行。
例句：His metascientific research focuses on the role of values in scientific practice. 他的元科学研究重点关注价值观在科学实践中的作用。
近义词：philosophical of science, epistemological of science
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective
