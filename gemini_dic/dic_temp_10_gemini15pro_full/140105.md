off-loading:/ˌɒfˈləʊdɪŋ/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊdɪŋ/
off-loading| n.|卸载，卸货|The act of unloading something, such as cargo from a ship or truck, or data from a computer.|The off-loading of the cargo took several hours. 货物的卸载花了几个小时。|近义词：unloading, discharging|反义词:loading, uploading|名词
off-load:/ˌɒfˈləʊd/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊd/
off-load| vt.|卸载，卸货；转嫁（责任等）|To unload something, or to transfer something to someone else.|They off-loaded the goods onto the dock. 他们把货物卸到码头上。 The manager off-loaded some of his work to his assistant. 经理把他的一些工作转嫁给了他的助手。|近义词：unload, discharge, transfer|反义词:load, take on|动词
