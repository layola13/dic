in charge:/ɪn ˈtʃɑːrdʒ/
n.|负责，主管|a person or group who is responsible for something or someone|例句：He's in charge of the marketing department. 他负责市场部。|近义词：responsible for, head of, supervisor, manager, director|反义词:subordinate, assistant
词性:名词短语 (prepositional phrase)


in charge:/ɪn ˈtʃɑːrdʒ/
adj.|负责的，主管的|responsible for something or someone|例句：Who's in charge here?  谁在这里负责？|近义词：responsible, leading, managing, directing|反义词:subordinate
词性:形容词短语 (adjectival phrase)


英文解析:The phrase "in charge" indicates having control, authority, or responsibility over something or someone. It implies leadership and oversight.

词源: "In" indicates position or state, while "charge" comes from Old French "charge" meaning "load, burden, expense, responsibility."  The combination suggests bearing the responsibility or burden of something.
