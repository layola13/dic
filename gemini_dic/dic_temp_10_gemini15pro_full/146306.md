overfeast:/ˌoʊvərˈfiːst/| v. n.|暴饮暴食；过量饮食|To eat too much; to indulge in excessive eating.  A meal eaten to excess.|He overfeasted during the holidays and gained weight. 他假期里暴饮暴食，体重增加了。|The Thanksgiving overfeast left everyone feeling stuffed. 感恩节的盛宴让每个人都感觉吃撑了。|近义词：gorge, overeat, overindulge|反义词: diet, fast|词性:动词/名词
