hapsburgs:/ˈhæpsbɜːrɡz/
noun
哈布斯堡王朝
A royal German family that provided rulers for several European states.
The Hapsburgs ruled Austria for over six centuries. 哈布斯堡家族统治奥地利超过六个世纪。
