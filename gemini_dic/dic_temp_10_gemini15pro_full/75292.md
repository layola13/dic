frontier:/ˈfrʌn.tɪər/ /frʌnˈtɪr/| n. |边界，边疆；前沿，新领域|a border between two countries, or (especially in the past) a region that forms the edge of a settled area
The soldiers guarded the frontier against invaders. 士兵们守卫着边疆，防止入侵者入侵。

Space is the final frontier. 太空是最终的疆域。

|近义词：border, boundary, edge, limit, perimeter, verge, brink, threshold, cutting edge, leading edge|反义词:center, interior, middle|词性:noun
