mannitol:/ˈmænɪˌtɒl/ (US) /ˈmænɪˌtɔːl/ (UK)| n.|甘露醇|A white crystalline alcohol C6H14O6 found in many plants and used as a diuretic and a sweetening agent.|Mannitol is used to reduce swelling in the brain. 甘露醇用于减轻脑肿胀。|Synonyms: osmotic diuretic, hexahydroxyhexane|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound, but potentially substances that cause water retention could be considered)|noun

mannitol:/ˈmænɪˌtɒl/ (US) /ˈmænɪˌtɔːl/ (UK)| n.|甘露糖醇|A sugar alcohol derived from mannose or fructose, used as a sweetener and in medicine as a diuretic.|Mannitol is commonly used in sugar-free chewing gum. 甘露糖醇常用于无糖口香糖中。|Synonyms: sugar alcohol, hexitol|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, similar reasoning as above)|noun
