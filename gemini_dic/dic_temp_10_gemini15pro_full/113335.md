luxuriance:/lʌɡˈʒʊəriəns/| n.| 繁茂，茂盛；丰富，奢华|The luxuriance of the tropical vegetation was breathtaking.  热带植被的繁茂令人叹为观止。|近义词：abundance, profusion, opulence|反义词：scarcity, barrenness|词性:名词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French luxuriance, from Latin luxuriantia, from luxurians, luxuriant- ‘growing profusely’ (see luxuriant).
