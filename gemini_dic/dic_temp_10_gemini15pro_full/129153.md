no-man's-land: /ˈnoʊˌmænzˌlænd/ | n. |无人区，真空地带| An area of land between two opposing armies, or a disputed area between two countries. |  The area between the two trenches became a dangerous no-man's-land.  两条战壕之间的区域变成了危险的无人区。 | Synonyms: wasteland, wilderness, buffer zone | Antonyms: safe zone, occupied territory | noun

no-man's-land: /ˈnoʊˌmænzˌlænd/ | n. |(比喻)三不管地带，模糊不清的领域| A situation or area of activity that is not clearly defined or controlled. | The regulations covering internet sales are still something of a no-man's-land.  关于互联网销售的法规仍然是一个三不管地带。 | Synonyms: gray area, limbo, undefined territory | Antonyms: defined area, regulated territory | noun
