copublishers:/ˌkoʊˈpʌblɪʃərz/
|n.| 共同出版商，联合出版者
|People or organizations that publish something together.|
|Example: The book was released by two copublishers, one in the US and one in the UK. 这本书由两家共同出版商发行，一家在美国，一家在英国。|
|Example: The copublishers worked closely together to ensure the book's success. 共同出版商紧密合作，以确保这本书的成功。|
|Synonym: joint publishers|
|Antonym: sole publisher|
|noun|
