aleconner:/ˈælɪkɒnər/| n.|酒类检查员，酒类鉴定师|An officer appointed to taste and assess the quality of ale or beer, especially to ensure it met certain standards.  This role was common in medieval and early modern England.|The aleconner would visit breweries and taverns to check the ale. 酒类检查员会去酿酒厂和酒馆检查麦芽酒。|The aleconner declared the brew fit for sale. 酒类鉴定师宣布该啤酒适合销售。|Synonym:  taster, inspector|Antonym: brewer, producer|noun
