oorial:/ˈɔːriəl/ | n. |盘羊,赤羊|A wild sheep (Ovis vignei) native to Central Asia, having reddish-brown fur and long, curved horns in the male.| The oorial is a skilled climber, often found on steep, rocky terrain. 盘羊是一种熟练的攀登者，经常在陡峭的岩石地形中被发现。|Synonyms: sha, urial |Antonyms: (No direct antonym for an animal species.
