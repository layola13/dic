intercombined:/ˌɪntərˈkəmˈbaɪnd/ | adj. |互相结合的，相互关联的 | Combined with each other; interconnected. |The various systems are intercombined to form a complex network. 各种系统相互结合，形成一个复杂的网络。|The gears are intercombined to ensure smooth operation. 齿轮相互结合以确保平稳运行。|近义词：interconnected, intertwined, integrated|反义词：separate, independent|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From inter- + combined, past participle of combine.  "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between," "among," or "together." "Combine" comes from the Old French "combiner," ultimately from the Latin "combinare," meaning "to join together."
