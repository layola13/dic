inseparate:/ɪnˈsɛprət/ | adj. | 不可分的，不能分离的 | Not able to be separated; permanently joined or united. |  The two friends were inseparable, always seen together. 这两个朋友形影不离，总是被看到在一起。|  The twins were inseparable in their childhood. 这对双胞胎在童年时期形影不离。 | 近义词: indivisible, unbreakable, inseparable,  indistinguishable | 反义词: separable, divisible, detachable | 词性: 形容词
