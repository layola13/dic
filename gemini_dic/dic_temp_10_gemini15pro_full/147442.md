overobjectify: /ˌoʊvərəbˈdʒɛktɪfaɪ/
verb
过度物化
To treat someone excessively as an object, especially sexually, reducing them to their physical attributes and disregarding their personality or dignity.
She felt overobjectified by the photographer's insistent focus on her body. 她觉得摄影师一直盯着她的身体，把她过度物化了。
The advertising industry often overobjectifies women to sell products.  广告业经常过度物化女性来销售产品。
近义词: sexualize, objectify, dehumanize
反义词: humanize, respect, value
词性: verb
