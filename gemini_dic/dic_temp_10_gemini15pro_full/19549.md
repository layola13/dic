bifurcate:/ˈbaɪfərˌkeɪt/ /ˈbaɪfərkeɪt/| v. adj.|分叉，分岔；使分叉|To divide into two branches or forks.  To have two branches or forks.|The river bifurcates a few miles downstream. 这条河在下游几英里处分叉了。|近义词：fork, divide, split, branch|反义词：merge, unite, join|verb

bifurcate:/ˈbaɪfərˌkeɪt/ /ˈbaɪfərkeɪt/| adj.|分叉的，分岔的|Having two branches or forks.|The bifurcate stem of the plant held two bright red flowers.  这种植物分叉的茎上有两朵鲜红色的花。|近义词：forked, branched, divided|反义词：unified, merged, joined|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin bifurcatus, past participle of bifurcare "to divide into two forks," from bi- "two" + furca "fork."
