dig-out:/ˈdɪɡ aʊt/ (US), /ˈdɪɡ aʊt/ (UK)| n. |挖掘出的东西；清理；困境|Something dug out; a rescue operation; a difficult situation.|例句：The archaeologists found a Roman dig-out filled with ancient artifacts. 考古学家发现了一个罗马时代的挖掘现场，里面充满了古代文物。

dig-out:/ˈdɪɡ aʊt/ (US), /ˈdɪɡ aʊt/ (UK)| v. |挖出；救出；找出|To excavate something; to rescue someone or something; to search for and find something.|例句：They had to dig out the car from the snowdrift. 他们不得不把车从雪堆里挖出来。
