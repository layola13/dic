atolls:/ˈætɒlz/ (US) /ˈætɔːlz/ (UK)| n. |环礁，珊瑚岛|A ring-shaped coral reef, island, or chain of islands formed of coral. |例句：The Maldives are a group of atolls in the Indian Ocean. 马尔代夫是印度洋上的一组环礁。

例句：Many atolls are popular tourist destinations because of their beautiful beaches and clear waters. 许多环礁因其美丽的海滩和清澈的海水而成为热门的旅游目的地。


|近义词：coral reef, island, cay|反义词:continent|词性:noun
