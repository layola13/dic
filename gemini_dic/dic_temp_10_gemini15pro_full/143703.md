outlet:/ˈaʊtlet/ (US), /ˈaʊtlɪt/ (UK)| n.|出口，排放口；经销店；发泄途径|A place where products are sold, often at a reduced price; a way of expressing or releasing something.|例句：The factory outlet sells clothes at a discount. 这家工厂直销店打折出售服装。|近义词：store, shop, vent, market|反义词:inlet|noun


outlet:/ˈaʊtlet/ (US), /ˈaʊtlɪt/ (UK)| n.|插座|A point on a wall where you can connect electrical equipment to the power supply.|例句：I plugged the lamp into the wall outlet. 我把灯插到墙上的插座里。|近义词：socket, receptacle|反义词:plug|noun
