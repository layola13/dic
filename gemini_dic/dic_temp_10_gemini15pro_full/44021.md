crimp:/krɪmp/| n. vt.|卷曲，褶皱；压制；限制；妨碍|A crimp is a curl or wave in something, especially hair.  As a verb, it means to press or pinch something to give it a curl or folded shape, or to restrict or hinder something.|Her hair had a slight crimp to it. 她的头发略微有些卷曲。|Synonyms: curl, wave, wrinkle, crease, inhibit, restrict|Antonyms: straighten, smooth, facilitate, encourage|noun, verb

crimp:/krɪmp/| n. vt.|（电线连接用的）金属箍；压制；限制；妨碍|A crimp is also a small metal ring used for connecting wires. As a verb, it can also mean to restrict or hinder something.|He used a crimp to connect the two wires. 他用金属箍连接了两根电线。|Synonyms: clamp, fastener, connector, inhibit, restrict|Antonyms: loosen, release, facilitate, encourage|noun, verb
