delighted:/dɪˈlaɪtɪd/| adj.|高兴的，欣喜的|Feeling or showing great pleasure.|例句：I was delighted to hear about your promotion. 我很高兴听到你升职的消息。|例句：She was delighted with the gift. 她对这份礼物很高兴。|近义词：pleased, glad, thrilled, joyful, elated|反义词：disappointed, unhappy, sad|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *deliten, from Old French deleiter, delyter (“to delight, please”), from Latin dēlectāre (“to allure, entice, charm, delight”), from dē- (“from”) + *lactāre, frequentative of lacere (“to entice, allure”).
