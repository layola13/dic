freshes:/ˈfrɛʃəz/| v.|使新鲜；恢复精神；变新鲜| The cool air freshes the room. 凉爽的空气使房间清新。|近义词：revives, refreshes, restores|反义词：tires, exhausts, weakens|verb (third-person singular simple present freshens, present participle freshening, simple past and past participle freshened)

freshes:/ˈfrɛʃəz/| n.|清新；新鲜空气|The freshes of the morning air invigorated her. 晨风的清新让她精神焕发。|近义词：freshness, coolness|反义词：staleness, mustiness|noun (plural only)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fresshen, from Old English *fersċian, from fersc (“fresh”).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *friskaz (“fresh”).
