It can refer to the process of communication, the systems used for communication, or the messages themselves. | Example:  The comm system went down during the storm. 通讯系统在暴风雨中瘫痪了。

| Example:  We need to improve comm between departments. 我们需要改进部门间的沟通。| Synonym: communication, contact, correspondence, intercourse | Antonym: silence, isolation, non-communication | noun
