half-tearfulness:/ˌhæfˈtɪəfʊlnəs/
|n.|
|半哭半笑;  快要哭出来的样子|
|A state characterized by a tendency towards tearfulness, but not full-blown crying.  It suggests a suppressed sadness or emotion close to the surface.|
|A half-tearfulness clouded her eyes as she listened to the sad news.  她听着这个悲伤的消息，眼里噙着泪水。|
|Near tears, close to tears, on the verge of tears, watery-eyed, misty-eyed|
|Cheerfulness, happiness, joy|
|noun|
