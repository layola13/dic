hyperdivision:/ˌhaɪpərˈdɪvɪʒən/ | n. | 超分割; 过度分割 | The act or process of dividing something excessively, often resulting in smaller, less effective units. | Excessive subdivision of land can lead to urban sprawl. 过度分割土地会导致城市扩张。  The hyperdivision of responsibilities hindered efficient decision-making. 责任的过度分割阻碍了高效的决策。 |近义词：fragmentation, subdivision, partitioning |反义词: consolidation, unification | noun
