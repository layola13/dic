词源:  "Falling-out" comes from the literal meaning of something falling out of place or out of alignment.  This sense was then extended metaphorically to describe a relationship falling apart.  “Falling-out”来自某物从适当的位置或排列中掉出来的字面意思。 然后，这个意义被隐喻地扩展到描述一段关系的破裂。
