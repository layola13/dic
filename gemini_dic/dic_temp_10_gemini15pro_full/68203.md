fair-trader:/ˈfeər ˌtreɪdər/ | n. | 公平贸易者；公平贸易倡导者 | A person who buys and sells goods produced under fair trade conditions, ensuring that producers receive a fair price and work in safe and ethical conditions. |  例句：She is a committed fair-trader and only buys coffee from cooperatives. 她是一位坚定的公平贸易支持者，只从合作社购买咖啡。

例句：The fair-trader explained the importance of supporting small-scale farmers.  这位公平贸易倡导者解释了支持小农的重要性。

| 近义词：ethical consumer, responsible shopper | 反义词：exploiter | 词性：名词
