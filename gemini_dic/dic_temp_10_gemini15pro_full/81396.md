grasscycle:/ˈɡræsaɪkəl/
verb
草循环，草回收利用
The act of leaving grass clippings on the lawn after mowing, allowing them to decompose and return nutrients to the soil.
I grasscycle every time I mow the lawn to reduce waste and fertilize the grass naturally.  我每次修剪草坪时都进行草循环，以减少浪费并自然地为草坪施肥。
近义词: mulch mow, compost
反义词: bagging grass clippings
词性: verb
