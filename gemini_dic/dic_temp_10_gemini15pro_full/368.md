abietene:/ˈeɪbiːtiːn/
noun
松香烯；冷杉烯
A hydrocarbon, C19H30, obtained from Canada balsam and other conifer resins.  It is a type of diterpene.
Abietene is a component of some essential oils. 松香烯是某些精油的成分。
Abietene can be used as a solvent. 松香烯可以用作溶剂。
