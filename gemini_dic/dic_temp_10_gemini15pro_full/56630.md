donjon:/ˈdɒndʒən/ (US) /ˈdʌndʒən/ (UK)| n. |地牢，主塔|The central tower of a medieval castle, formerly used as a keep and often as a prison.  A dungeon.|The king was imprisoned in the castle's donjon. 国王被囚禁在城堡的主塔里。|近义词：keep, stronghold, fortress, tower, dungeon|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: open field, outside)|noun
