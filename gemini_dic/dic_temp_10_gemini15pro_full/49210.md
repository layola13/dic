delectable:/dɪˈlɛktəbl/| adj.|美味的；令人愉悦的|Highly pleasing, especially to the sense of taste or smell.  Giving great pleasure.|例句：The restaurant served a delectable chocolate cake. 这家餐厅供应美味的巧克力蛋糕。|例句：The music was delectable and soothing.  这音乐优美而舒缓。|近义词：delicious, delightful, scrumptious, enjoyable|反义词：unpleasant, unappetizing, disgusting|词性:adjective
词源:From Late Latin *dēlectabilis, from Latin dēlectāre (“to delight”), from dē- + *lactāre, frequentative of laciō (“to entice, allure”).
