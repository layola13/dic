auto-destructive:/ˌɔːtəʊ dɪˈstrʌktɪv/
adjective
自动毁灭的；自毁的
Causing its own destruction.
例句：The auto-destructive behavior of the robot ensured it wouldn't fall into enemy hands.  机器人的自毁行为确保它不会落入敌人手中。

例句：His auto-destructive tendencies led him down a path of ruin. 他的自毁倾向导致他走向毁灭之路。

近义词：self-destructive, suicidal
反义词：self-preserving, constructive
词性：adjective
