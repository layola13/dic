nonaccessible:/ˌnɒnækˈsesəbl/ | adj. | 不可接近的，无法访问的 | Not able to be reached, entered, or obtained. |  例句：The remote island was nonaccessible by car.  这个偏远的岛屿无法开车到达。| 例句：The information on the website is nonaccessible to unauthorized users.  网站上的信息未经授权的用户无法访问。| 近义词：inaccessible, unreachable, unavailable | 反义词：accessible, reachable, available | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + accessible.  "Accessible" comes from Late Latin "accessibilis," meaning "approachable," from Latin "accedere" ("to approach"). The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning.
