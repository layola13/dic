mistranscription:/ˌmɪstrænˈskrɪpʃən/ | n. | 抄写错误，误写 |  An inaccurate written record of spoken or written words; a mistake made when copying something.  The student's mistranscription of the ancient text led to a misunderstanding of its meaning. | 近义词: miscopying, error, typo, inaccuracy | 反义词: accurate transcription, correct copy | 词性: noun

词源:  From mis- (meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + transcription (meaning "the act of copying something"). Ultimately from Latin trans- ("across") + scribere ("to write").
