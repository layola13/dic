modern:/ˈmɒdən/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdərn/ (US)| adj.|现代的，近代的|Of or relating to the present or recent times as opposed to the remote past.|例句：Modern art often breaks with traditional forms. 现代艺术经常打破传统形式。
