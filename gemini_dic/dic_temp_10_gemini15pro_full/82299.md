grouplet:/ˈɡruːplɪt/ | n. | 小组，小群 | A small group, especially one separated from a larger group. |  Example 1:  A grouplet of tourists broke away from the main tour to explore the back streets.  一群游客脱离了主旅行团去探索偏僻的街道。

 Example 2: Several grouplets formed within the larger organization, each with its own specific goals.  在更大的组织内形成了几个小组，每个小组都有自己的特定目标。| synonyms: cluster, clique, faction | antonyms: individual, whole, entirety | noun
