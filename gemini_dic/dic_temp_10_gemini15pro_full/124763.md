mrsas:/ˈmɜːrsæs/ (or /ˈmɜːrˌsæs/) | n. | MegaRAID Serial Attached SCSI |  A brand name for LSI Corporation's (now Broadcom Inc.'s) RAID controllers that connect to storage devices using Serial Attached SCSI (SAS) or Serial ATA (SATA) interfaces. These controllers manage multiple storage devices, allowing them to be configured into RAID arrays for redundancy and/or performance. |  Example: "The server uses an mrsas controller to manage the hard drives in a RAID 5 configuration."  服务器使用 mrsas 控制器以 RAID 5 配置管理硬盘驱动器。 |  Synonym: RAID controller, SAS controller, SATA controller | Antonym: N/A | noun
