filacer:/ˈfɪləsər/ | n. | 抄写员；档案保管员 | An officer of court in charge of filing records. |  The filacer prepared the documents for the judge. 抄写员为法官准备了文件。| synonym: clerk, recorder, archivist | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "deleter" in a metaphorical sense) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *filacer, from filer “to file, string together” (from Latin fīlum “thread”) + -cer (from Latin -ārius).
