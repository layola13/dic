count-coup:/ˌkaʊnt ˈkuː/ | n. | 击中敌人（但不杀死）| The act of striking an opponent in battle, especially by touching them with a coup stick or other object, without necessarily killing or seriously injuring them. This was considered a brave deed in some Native American cultures, demonstrating courage and skill. |  He counted coup on the enemy warrior by touching him with his coup stick.  他用他的击敌棍触碰了敌方战士，完成了击中敌人的壮举。


He had counted coup many times, proving his bravery in battle. 他曾多次击中敌人，证明了他在战斗中的英勇。 | Synonym:  touch, strike, tap | Antonym: kill, slaughter | noun
