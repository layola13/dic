nonimputativeness:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpjuːtətɪvnəs/
|n.|不可归罪性，非归罪性
|The nonimputativeness of an act means it cannot be attributed to a specific person or entity as a fault or responsibility.|
|The judge stressed the nonimputativeness of the child's actions due to their young age.|法官强调由于孩子年龄小，他们的行为不应被归罪。
|The nonimputativeness of the accident was due to an act of God.|这场事故的不可归责性是由于天灾。
|non-attribution, non-accountability|
|imputability, accountability|
|noun|
