elucidates: /ɪˈluːsɪdeɪts/ | v. | 阐明，解释 | makes something clear; explains |  The professor elucidates the complex theory with clear examples. 教授用清晰的例子阐明了这个复杂的理论。

The article elucidates the reasons behind the company's recent success. 这篇文章阐明了该公司近期成功背后的原因。

| 近义词：clarifies, explains, illuminates, expounds | 反义词：obscures, confuses, mystifies | 词性：动词
