natives:/ˈneɪtɪvz/| n.|本地人，土著，原住民|People who were born in a particular place; indigenous people.|The natives of this island are very friendly. 这个岛上的居民非常友好。|近义词：inhabitants, residents, locals, aborigines|反义词：foreigners, immigrants, aliens|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *natif*, from Old French *natif*, from Latin *nativus* "native, natural, innate," from *natus*, past participle of *nasci* "to be born."
