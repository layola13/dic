intermingling:/ˌɪntərˈmɪŋɡlɪŋ/| n. v.|混合，交织|The act of mixing or blending together.|例句：The intermingling of different cultures created a vibrant community. 不同文化的交融创造了一个充满活力的社区。

例句：The intermingling of the two rivers created a fertile delta. 两条河流的交汇形成了肥沃的三角洲。

近义词：mixing, blending, merging, integrating|反义词:separating, dividing, segregating|词性:noun, verb (present participle)


intermingle:/ˌɪntərˈmɪŋɡl/| v.|混合，交织|To mix or blend together.|例句：The scents of various spices intermingled in the air. 各种香料的气味在空气中混合在一起。

例句：The two families intermingled at the wedding reception. 两个家庭在婚宴上融为一体。
