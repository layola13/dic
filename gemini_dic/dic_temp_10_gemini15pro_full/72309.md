ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *florischen*, from Old French *florir*, *flouriss-* ("to blossom, bloom, flower, flourish"), from Vulgar Latin *florire*, from Latin *florēre* ("to blossom, bloom, flower, flourish"), from *flos*, *floris* ("flower").
