imbalm:/ɪmˈbɑːm/ | vt. | embalm, 防腐处理，保存尸体 |To preserve a corpse from decay, usually by treating it with chemicals or spices.| The ancient Egyptians embalmed their pharaohs. 古埃及人对他们的法老进行防腐处理。 |  The body was embalmed and placed in a tomb.  尸体经过防腐处理后被安放在墓穴中。 | 近义词: preserve, mummify | 反义词: decompose, rot | 词性: verb

Etymology: From Old French *embaumer, from en- "in" + baumer "balm."  Ultimately from Greek βάλσαμον (balsamon) "balsam."
