coiner:/ˈkɔɪnər/| n. |创造者，发明者（尤指新词或新短语的人）; 铸币者|A person who creates or invents something new, especially a new word or phrase; a person who mints coins.|例句：He is the coiner of the term "blogosphere". 他是“博客圈”一词的创造者。

例句：The coiner of this phrase remains unknown.  这个短语的创造者仍然不为人知。


|近义词：inventor, creator, originator|反义词:imitator, copier|词性: noun
