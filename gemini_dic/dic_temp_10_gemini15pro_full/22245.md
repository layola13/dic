bondings:/ˈbɑːndɪŋz/| n.|联系，关系，结合，粘合|The process of forming a close relationship or connection, often emotional, between people or groups. It can also refer to the physical joining of materials.|例句：The bonding between mother and child is very strong. 母子之间的联系非常牢固。

例句：The bonding of the two surfaces created a strong seal. 两个表面的粘合形成了一个牢固的密封。|近义词：connections, relationships, ties, attachments, adhesion, joining|反义词：separation, detachment, disconnection|词性:名词
