dunnish:/ˈdʌnɪʃ/
adjective
暗褐色的；鼠灰色的；略带灰褐色的
Of a slightly brownish dark grey colour.
The dunnish light of early dawn.  黎明时分昏暗的光线。
近义词:  mousy, greyish-brown, taupe
反义词: bright, colourful
词性: adjective
词源: From dun + -ish.
