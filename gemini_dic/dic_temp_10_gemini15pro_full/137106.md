nonsubjugable:/ˌnɒnsʌbˈdʒʌɡəbl/
adjective
不可征服的，不服从的，不屈服的
Not capable of being subjugated; not easily controlled or dominated.
The nonsubjugable spirit of the freedom fighters inspired others to join the rebellion.  自由战士不屈不挠的精神激励了其他人加入反抗。
The virus proved nonsubjugable despite the best efforts of the medical community. 尽管医学界尽了最大努力，但这种病毒仍无法被征服。
近义词:indomitable, unconquerable, rebellious, defiant, resistant
反义词:submissive, obedient, compliant, yielding, controllable
词性:adjective


词源:
From non- + subjugable, from subjugate + -able. Subjugate comes from the Latin subjugāre, from sub- "under" + jugum "yoke."
