dissimilarity:/dɪsˌsɪmɪˈlærɪti/
n.
不同, 差异, 相异
lack of resemblance or similarity
The dissimilarity between the twins was striking. 这对双胞胎的不同之处令人吃惊。

近义词:difference, unlikeness, distinction, divergence, variation, disparity, contrast
反义词:similarity, likeness, resemblance, similitude

词性:noun

词源:Late Middle English: from Old French dissimilarité, from dissimuler ‘disguise’ (see dissimular).
