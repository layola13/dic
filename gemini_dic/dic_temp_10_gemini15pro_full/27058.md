calendula:/kəˈlɛndʒʊlə/ | n. |金盏花；金盏菊 |A plant with bright orange or yellow flowers, used in herbal medicine.  A common garden flower known for its medicinal properties.| Calendula cream is often used to soothe minor skin irritations. 金盏花膏经常用来舒缓轻微的皮肤刺激。|Synonyms: pot marigold, English marigold | Antonyms: N/A | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *calendula, diminutive of calendae (“calends, first day of the month”), so called because it blooms throughout the year, especially in warm climates.
