brew:/bruː/
n.| 酿造的饮料；混合物；麻烦 | A brewed beverage; a mixture; trouble brewing. |  The pub brews its own beer. 这家酒吧自酿啤酒. | Synonyms: concoction, mixture, infusion | Antonyms: solution, separation | noun

brew:/bruː/
v.| 酿造；泡（茶）；酝酿；即将发生 | To make (beer) by soaking, boiling, and fermenting malt and hops; to make (tea or coffee) by mixing it with hot water; to develop; to be imminent. |  She brewed a pot of coffee. 她煮了一壶咖啡. | Synonyms: concoct, prepare, develop, ferment | Antonyms: distill, separate, prevent | verb
