overestimates:/ˌoʊvərˈɛstɪˌmeɪts/ | /ˌəʊvərˈɛstɪˌmeɪts/ | v. | 高估 | To estimate something as being larger, greater, better, or more important than it actually is. | He overestimates his abilities and often takes on tasks he can't handle. 他高估了自己的能力，经常承担他无法胜任的任务。| overvalues, overrates | underestimates | verb


overestimate:/ˌoʊvərˈɛstɪmət/ | /ˌəʊvərˈɛstɪˌmeɪt/ | n. | 高估 | An excessively high estimate. |  His overestimate of the project's cost caused delays. 他对项目成本的高估导致了延误。| overvaluation | underestimate | noun

词源: "over-" (excessive) + "estimate" (to judge the value of)
