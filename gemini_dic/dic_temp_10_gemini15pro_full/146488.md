overgambled:/ˌoʊvərˈɡæmbəld/
verb
过度赌博，赌博过度
Having bet or wagered excessively, often resulting in financial loss or other negative consequences.
He overgambled and lost his life savings. 他赌博过度，输光了毕生积蓄。
She overgambled on the stock market and lost a fortune. 她在股市上过度投机，损失惨重。
近义词:gambled too much, wagered excessively
反义词:gambled responsibly, played it safe
词性:verb
