expatiate:/ɪkˈspeɪʃieɪt/
verb
详述，细说
To speak or write at length or in detail.
The professor expatiated on the history of the Roman Empire. 教授详细阐述了罗马帝国的历史。
He expatiated on his plans for the future. 他详细阐述了他对未来的计划。
近义词:elaborate, expand, discuss, explain
反义词:summarize, condense, abbreviate
词性:verb

expatiate:/ɪkˈspeɪʃieɪt/
verb
细谈，详谈
To speak or write at length.
She expatiated on the benefits of a vegetarian diet. 她详细阐述了素食的好处。
The speaker expatiated upon the need for educational reform. 演讲者详细阐述了教育改革的必要性。
近义词:enlarge, dwell, descant
反义词:abridge, curtail
词性:verb
