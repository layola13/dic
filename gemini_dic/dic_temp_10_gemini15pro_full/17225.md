bawdrick: /ˈbɔːdrɪk/ | n. | 腰带，绶带;  | A baldric, also spelled bawdrick, baldrick, or bauldrick, is a belt worn over the shoulder or around the waist to support a sword, bugle, or other items. | The knight wore a bawdrick across his chest, from which his sword hung. 骑士的胸前系着一条绶带，剑就挂在上面。 |  The soldier's bawdrick held his bugle securely. 士兵的腰带稳稳地系着他的号角。 | Synonyms: belt, sash, baldric | Antonyms: None | Noun
