nondeadly:/ˌnɒnˈdɛdli/ /ˌnɑːnˈdɛdli/| adj.|非致命的，非致命性的|Not designed to kill; not capable of causing death.|The police used nondeadly force to subdue the suspect. 警方使用了非致命武力制服了嫌疑人。|例句：The protest was nondeadly. 抗议活动是非致命的。|近义词：nonlethal, harmless, innocuous|反义词：lethal, deadly, fatal|词性:形容词
词源:  "non-" (not) + "deadly" (capable of causing death).  "Deadly" comes from Old English "dēadlic," from "dēad" (dead).
