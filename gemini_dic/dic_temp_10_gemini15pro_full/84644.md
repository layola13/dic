hand-to-mouth:/ˌhænd tə ˈmaʊθ/| adj.|勉强糊口的，仅能维持生存的|Living a hand-to-mouth existence means having just enough money for basic necessities, often with no savings or security for the future.  It implies a precarious financial situation where income is barely sufficient to cover essential expenses.|They lived a hand-to-mouth existence, never knowing where their next meal was coming from. 他们过着勉强糊口的日子，从不知道下一顿饭在哪里。|The family's hand-to-mouth lifestyle made it difficult to afford any luxuries.  这个家庭勉强糊口的生活方式使得他们很难负担任何奢侈品。|precarious, struggling, impoverished|comfortable, affluent, prosperous|adjective
