ericaceous:/ˌɛrɪˈkeɪʃəs/
adjective
石南科的；杜鹃花科的
Relating to or belonging to the heath family (Ericaceae), typically having tough, evergreen leaves and bell-shaped or urn-shaped flowers.
The garden boasts a stunning collection of ericaceous plants, including rhododendrons and azaleas.  这个花园里种植了大量的石南科植物，包括杜鹃花和山茶花。

Heathers and rhododendrons are ericaceous plants. 石楠和杜鹃花都是杜鹃花科植物。

近义词:heath-like
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, as it refers to a specific plant family)
词性: adjective
