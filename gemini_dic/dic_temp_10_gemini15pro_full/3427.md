african-sleeping-sickness:/ˈæfrɪkən ˈsliːpɪŋ ˈsɪknɪs/
n.
非洲昏睡病
A parasitic disease transmitted by the tsetse fly, causing fever, lethargy, and eventually coma and death if left untreated.
Trypanosomiasis, also known as African sleeping sickness, is a serious public health concern in sub-Saharan Africa.  非洲锥虫病，也称为非洲昏睡病，是撒哈拉以南非洲地区一个严重的公共卫生问题。
The doctor diagnosed him with African sleeping sickness. 医生诊断他患有非洲昏睡病。
