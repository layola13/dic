indeterminate:/ˌɪndɪˈtɜːrmɪnət/ | adj. | 不确定的，不明确的 | The results of the experiment were indeterminate. 实验结果不确定。|  His future remains indeterminate. 他的未来仍然不确定。| uncertain, unclear, ambiguous | certain, definite, precise | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *indeterminatus, from in- "not" + determinatus "limited, determined," past participle of determinare "to limit, determine," from de- "completely" + terminare "to bound, limit," from terminus "boundary, limit."
