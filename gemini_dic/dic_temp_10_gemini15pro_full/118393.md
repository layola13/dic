meroblastic:/ˌmɛrəˈblæstɪk/
adjective
部分卵裂的，不完全卵裂的
Relating to or undergoing meroblastic cleavage, in which the cleavage furrows do not extend through the entire egg.  The yolk-rich part of the egg remains undivided.
例句：
The zebrafish embryo undergoes meroblastic cleavage.
斑马鱼胚胎进行不完全卵裂。

Birds' eggs exhibit meroblastic division.
鸟类的卵表现出不完全分裂。


近义词：discoidal (referring specifically to a type of meroblastic cleavage)
反义词：holoblastic


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *meros* "part" + *blastos* "germ, sprout"
