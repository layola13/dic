half-obliterated:/ˌhæf əˈblɪtəreɪtɪd/
adjective
半抹掉的，几乎消失的
partially erased or destroyed, almost gone
The inscription on the tombstone was half-obliterated by weathering. 墓碑上的铭文因风化而几乎消失不见。
The half-obliterated trail was difficult to follow.  几乎消失的小径很难跟随。
近义词:faded, worn, indistinct, erased, partially destroyed
反义词:clear, distinct, visible, preserved
词性:adjective
