losing:/ˈluːzɪŋ/| adj. v.|失败的，输掉的；损失的|Present participle of lose.  Describes the act of failing to win or maintain something.|He's been on a losing streak lately.  他最近一直走背运。|Synonyms: failing, forfeiting, defeated|Antonyms: winning, gaining, succeeding|verb/adjective

Etymology: From Middle English *losen*, from Old English *losian* (“to be lost, perish, escape”), from Proto-Germanic *lusianą* (“to lose”), from Proto-Indo-European *lewk- (“to tear apart, separate, damage”).
