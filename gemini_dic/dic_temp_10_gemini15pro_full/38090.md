composure:/kəmˈpəʊʒə(r)/ /kəmˈpoʊʒər/| n. |沉着；镇静；泰然自若|The state of being calm and in control of oneself, especially in a difficult situation.|He maintained his composure despite the intense pressure. 他在巨大压力下保持了镇静。|Synonyms: calmness, poise, self-control, equilibrium, aplomb|Antonyms: agitation, anxiety, panic, distress|noun
