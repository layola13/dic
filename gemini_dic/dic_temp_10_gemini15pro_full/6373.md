amphicarpous:/ˌæmfɪˈkɑːrpəs/
adjective
两型果实的，异果的
Producing fruit of two kinds, especially one above and one below ground.
例句：The amphicarpous plant produced both aerial and subterranean fruits.  这种两型果实的植物既能结出地上果实，也能结出地下果实。

例句：Amphicarpous species often have different dispersal mechanisms for their different fruit types. 两型果实的物种通常对不同类型的果实有不同的传播机制。

近义词：heterocarpous
反义词：无
词性: adjective
