multicircuited:/ˌmʌltiˈsɜːrkɪtɪd/
adjective
多回路的，多电路的
Having multiple circuits or paths for electrical current.
The multicircuited system provides redundancy in case of failure.  多回路系统在出现故障时提供了冗余。
The engineer designed a multicircuited power distribution network. 工程师设计了一个多回路配电网络。
近义词: multi-circuit, complexly wired
反义词: single-circuited, simply wired
词性: adjective
词源: multi- (many) + circuit + -ed (forming an adjective)
