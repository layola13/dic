depriving:/dɪˈpraɪvɪŋ/
|v.|剥夺，使丧失|Taking away something from someone.  The new law is depriving many people of their basic rights.|例句：He was deprived of sleep. 他被剥夺了睡眠。

例句：Depriving children of love can have devastating consequences. 剥夺孩子们的爱会造成毁灭性的后果。

|近义词：denying, stripping, divesting, bereaving|反义词：granting, giving, providing, bestowing|词性：动词(present participle of deprive)


deprive:/dɪˈpraɪv/
|vt.|剥夺，使丧失|To take something away from someone, especially something important or necessary.|例句：The drought deprived the region of its crops. 干旱使该地区颗粒无收。

例句：He was deprived of his liberty. 他被剥夺了自由。

|近义词：dispossess, rob, divest|反义词：provide, endow, furnish|词性：动词
