extravaganza:/ɪkˌstrævəˈɡænzə/ | n. | 盛大表演；铺张的活动；奢华的展示 | A large, elaborate, and spectacular entertainment or production. | The opening ceremony of the Olympics was a true extravaganza. 奥运会的开幕式是一场真正的盛会。

The company's Christmas party was an extravaganza, with a live band, gourmet food, and an open bar. 公司的圣诞晚会非常奢华，有现场乐队、美食和无限畅饮的酒吧。 | 近义词：spectacle, show, celebration, fete, pageant | 反义词：simplicity, modesty, austerity | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Italian *stravaganza*, from *stravagante* "extravagant," ultimately from Latin *extravagans*, present participle of *extravagari* "to wander outside or beyond," from *extra* "outside" + *vagari* "to wander."
