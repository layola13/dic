faithful:/ˈfeɪθfl/| adj.|忠诚的，忠实的；如实的，准确的|Staying true to commitments, beliefs, or a person; reliable and consistent. |例句：She has been a faithful employee for over twenty years. 她是一位忠诚的员工，已经工作了二十多年。

例句：The dog was a faithful companion to its owner.  这只狗是它主人的忠实伙伴。

近义词：loyal, constant, devoted, steadfast, true, accurate, precise|反义词：disloyal, unfaithful, treacherous, false, inaccurate|词性:形容词
