casualization:/ˌkæʒʊəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 零时工化；非正式化；休闲化 | The process of increasing the number of casual workers in an organization or workforce, often at the expense of permanent employees.  The trend towards more informal clothing and social behavior. | Example 1: The casualization of the workforce has led to job insecurity for many.  零时工化导致许多人的工作缺乏保障。

Example 2: The company embraced casualization, allowing employees to wear jeans to work.  公司接受了休闲化，允许员工穿牛仔裤上班。 | Synonyms: informalization, flexibilization, temporary employment, precarious employment | Antonyms: permanency, formalization, regularization | noun
