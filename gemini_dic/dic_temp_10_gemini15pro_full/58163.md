drove:/droʊv/ | v. | 驾驶，驱赶，迫使 | Past tense of "drive".  Meaning to operate and control the movement of a vehicle. Also means to force or urge someone to do something. | He drove the car to the airport. 他开车去了机场。

She drove the cattle across the field. 她把牛群赶过田野。

The stress of the job drove him to resign. 工作的压力迫使他辞职。| 近义词: operated, steered, propelled, compelled, forced | 反义词: rode, walked, followed, allowed | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old English *drīfan* (to push, drive, force), from Proto-Germanic *dreibaną* (to push, drive).  Cognate with Dutch *drijven*, German *treiben*, and Old Norse *drífa*.
