nonrelativity:/ˌnɒnˌrɛləˈtɪvɪti/ | n. |非相对性|The state or quality of not being relative; the absence of relativity.  This concept is usually used in contrast to Einstein's theory of relativity. |Classical physics deals with nonrelativity, where time and space are considered absolute.|近义词: absoluteness, classical mechanics (in context) |反义词: relativity |词性: noun
