easting:/ˈiːstɪŋ/| n.|东距，向东的距离|The easting of the point is 500 meters.  该点的东距为500米。|例句：The map reference gives the easting first, then the northing.  地图坐标先给出东距，然后是北距。

例句：The easting coordinate increases as you move east.  当你向东移动时，东距坐标会增加。|近义词：longitude, x-coordinate|反义词:westing|词性:noun
