demonized:/ˈdiːmənaɪzd/| v. |被妖魔化|To portray someone or something as wicked or threatening.|The media demonized the candidate, portraying him as a dangerous extremist. 媒体将这位候选人妖魔化，把他描绘成一个危险的极端分子。|例句：The refugees were demonized in the press.  难民在媒体上被妖魔化了。

例句：He was unfairly demonized by his political opponents. 他被他的政敌不公平地妖魔化了。|近义词：vilified, denigrated, slandered|反义词：praised, lauded, glorified|词性:动词
