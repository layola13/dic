immunodeficiencies: /ˌɪmjuːnoʊdɪˈfɪʃənsiz/ | n. | 免疫缺陷 | Conditions in which the body's immune system is not working properly, making individuals more susceptible to infections. | People with immunodeficiencies are more prone to infections. 免疫缺陷患者更容易感染。  HIV can lead to severe immunodeficiencies. 艾滋病毒会导致严重的免疫缺陷。 | 近义词: immune disorders, immune suppression | 反义词: immunocompetence | 词性: noun
