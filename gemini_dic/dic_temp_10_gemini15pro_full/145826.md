overdeliberate:/ˌoʊvərdiˈlɪbərət/| adj.|过分慎重的，过于深思熟虑的|Characterized by excessive deliberation; too cautious or painstaking.|His overdeliberate manner of speaking made him seem uncertain. 他过于慎重的说话方式让他看起来很不确定。|The committee's overdeliberate approach stalled the project. 委员会过于慎重的做法拖延了项目。|近义词：cautious, painstaking, meticulous, overcautious|反义词：rash, impulsive, hasty, reckless|词性:形容词
Etymology: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "deliberate" (from Latin "deliberatus," past participle of "deliberare" meaning "to weigh in the mind, consider").
