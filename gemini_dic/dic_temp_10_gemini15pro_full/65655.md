evaporator:/ɪˈvæpəreɪtər/ | n. | 蒸发器；蒸发皿 | A device used to transform a liquid into a vapor. |  The evaporator in the refrigerator cools the air. 冰箱里的蒸发器冷却空气。| synonym: vaporizer, still | antonym: condenser | noun

evaporate:/ɪˈvæpəreɪt/ | v. | 蒸发；消失 | To change from a liquid to a gas or vapor; to disappear. | The water evaporated quickly in the hot sun. 水在烈日下迅速蒸发。| synonym: vaporize, disappear, vanish | antonym: condense, materialize | verb
