bottlenecking:/ˈbɒtlˌnɛkɪŋ/| n. |瓶颈，瓶颈效应|A restriction on output or progress.|The bottlenecking of the production process caused delays in delivery. 生产过程中的瓶颈导致了交货延迟。|近义词：constraint, restriction, impediment, obstruction|反义词:advancement, progress|noun

bottleneck:/ˈbɒtlˌnɛk/| n. v.|瓶颈，阻塞；造成瓶颈|A narrow section of road or a point of congestion.|Traffic bottlenecks are common during rush hour. 交通瓶颈在高峰时段很常见。| The construction work is bottlenecking traffic. 施工造成了交通瓶颈。|近义词：obstruction, congestion, blockage|反义词: opening, expansion|noun, verb
