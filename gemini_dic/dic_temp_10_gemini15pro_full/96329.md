indignation:/ˌɪndɪɡˈneɪʃn/| n. |愤怒，愤慨，义愤|Anger or annoyance provoked by what is perceived as unfair treatment.|例句：She expressed her indignation at the unfair treatment. 她对不公平的待遇表示愤慨。

例句：His indignation at the accusation was evident. 他对指控的愤怒显而易见。

近义词：anger, fury, resentment, outrage|反义词:calmness, composure, pleasure, satisfaction|词性:noun
