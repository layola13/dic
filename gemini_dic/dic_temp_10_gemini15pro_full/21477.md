bls:/biː ɛl ɛs/ | abbreviation | 基本生命支持；基础生命支持 | Basic Life Support.  A level of medical care used for victims of life-threatening illnesses or injuries until they can be given full medical care at a hospital. | Example: All paramedics are trained in BLS. 所有护理人员都接受过基本生命支持培训。 | Example:  The lifeguard performed BLS on the unconscious swimmer. 救生员对失去知觉的游泳者进行了基本生命支持。 | Synonym:  first aid, CPR | Antonym: N/A | noun (abbreviation)
