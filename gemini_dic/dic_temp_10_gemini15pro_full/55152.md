disseize:/dɪˈsiːz/ /dɪsˈsiːz/ | vt. |剥夺，非法夺取（尤指土地）| To deprive of rightful or legal possession, especially of real estate. | The king disseized his rebellious barons of their lands. 国王剥夺了反叛男爵的土地。

The landlord attempted to disseize the tenant of his apartment. 房东试图非法剥夺房客的公寓。| synonyms: dispossess, oust, deprive, expropriate | antonyms: invest, endow, grant | verb
