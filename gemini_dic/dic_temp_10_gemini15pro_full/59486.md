easings:/ˈiːzɪŋz/
| n. |缓动, 缓动函数|A mathematical function that describes the speed of a transition over time, often used in animation and user interface design to create smooth and natural-looking movements. |The animation uses cubic bezier easings to create a smooth transition. 这段动画使用三次贝塞尔曲线缓动来创建平滑的过渡。|近义词:transitions, animations|反义词:N/A|名词
