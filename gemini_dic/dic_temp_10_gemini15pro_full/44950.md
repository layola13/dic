cryptopine:/krɪpˈtɒpaɪn/ | n. |隐品碱;  金罂粟碱|A crystalline alkaloid, C₂₁H₂₃NO₅, obtained from opium.|Cryptopine is one of the minor alkaloids found in opium. 隐品碱是鸦片中的一种次要生物碱。|Cryptopine exhibits weak analgesic properties. 隐品碱显示出微弱的镇痛特性。|Synonyms:  protopine (closely related, not a true synonym), allocryptopine|Antonyms: None (no direct chemical antonym)|Noun
