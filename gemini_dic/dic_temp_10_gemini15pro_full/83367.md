gyroma:/ˈdʒaɪroʊmə/ | n. |  希腊烤肉卷饼；希腊烤肉 | A Greek dish consisting of meat, typically lamb or pork, cooked on a vertical rotisserie and served wrapped in pita bread with various toppings such as tomatoes, onions, and tzatziki sauce. |  I ordered a gyroma for lunch. 我午饭点了一份希腊烤肉卷饼。|  Synonyms: gyro, souvlaki | Antonyms: N/A | noun
