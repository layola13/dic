bloaty:/ˈbloʊti/ (US), /ˈbləʊti/ (UK)| adj.|臃肿的，胀气的|Feeling full and uncomfortable, often due to gas or fluid retention.  Swollen or puffed up.|例句：I felt bloated after eating too much. 我吃太多后感到胀气。

例句：My stomach is bloated from all the salt I ate. 我的肚子因为我吃了太多盐而胀起来了。


近义词：swollen, distended, puffed up|反义词:slim, flat|词性:形容词

bloat:/bloʊt/ (US), /bləʊt/ (UK)| v. n.|（使）膨胀，肿胀|To swell or become swollen, typically as a result of gas or fluid retention.  The state of being bloated.|例句：The dead whale began to bloat on the beach. 死鲸鱼开始在海滩上膨胀。

例句：Eating too much salt can bloat you. 吃太多盐会让你胀气。


近义词：swell, distend, inflate|反义词:deflate, shrink|词性:动词 & 名词


ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle English *bloten* "to swell," from Old Norse *blótna* "to soften by soaking, to sacrifice," from Proto-Germanic *blautaną* "to soften, to sacrifice," from Proto-Indo-European *bʰlew-t-/*bʰlow-t- "to swell, bloom."
