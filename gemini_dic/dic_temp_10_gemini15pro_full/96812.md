inertly:/ɪˈnɜːrtli/ | adv. | 惰性地，迟钝地 | In a manner lacking the ability or tendency to move or react; inactive; sluggishly. | The gas hung inertly in the air.  这种气体惰性地悬浮在空中。 |  The knight stood inertly, seemingly unfazed by the dragon's roar. 骑士一动不动地站着，似乎没有被龙的咆哮吓到。 | 近义词: passively, sluggishly, inactively | 反义词: actively, energetically, responsively | 词性: 副词

inert:/ɪˈnɜːrt/ | adj. | 惰性的，不活泼的 | Lacking the ability or tendency to move or react; inactive. |  Inert gases rarely react with other elements. 惰性气体很少与其他元素发生反应。 | The medication made him feel inert and sleepy. 药物使他感到迟钝和困倦。| 近义词: inactive, passive, motionless | 反义词: active, reactive, dynamic | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *inert-, from iners "unskilled, inactive," from in- "not" + ars "skill, art."
