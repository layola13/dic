coprisoner:/koʊˈprɪzənər/| n.|狱友；难友|A fellow prisoner.  Someone imprisoned at the same time or in the same place as another person.|例句：He became friends with his coprisoner. 他和他的狱友成了朋友。|例句：The two coprisoners planned their escape together.  这两位狱友一起计划了他们的越狱。|近义词：fellow prisoner, inmate, cellmate|反义词:guard, warden|词性:noun
