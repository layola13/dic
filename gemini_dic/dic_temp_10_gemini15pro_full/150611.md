paperworks:/ˈpeɪpərwɜːrks/
n.
文书工作，文件；资料
the documents that are needed for something, especially something official or legal
例句：
The paperworks for the sale of the house are almost complete.  房屋出售的文书工作几乎完成了。

I'm still waiting for the paperworks to come through. 我还在等文件过来。

近义词：documentation, paperwork (singular), files, records
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
