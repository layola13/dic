gloat:/ɡloʊt/ (US) /ɡləʊt/ (UK)| vi.|幸灾乐祸；沾沾自喜|To dwell on one's own success or another's misfortune with smugness or malignant pleasure.|例句：She gloated over her rival's failure. 她对竞争对手的失败幸灾乐祸。

例句：He couldn't resist gloating at their misfortune. 他忍不住对他们的不幸幸灾乐祸。|近义词：revel, crow, exult, rejoice maliciously|反义词：commiserate, sympathize|词性：动词
