anantherous:/ænˈænθərəs/
adjective
无花药的；没有雄蕊的
Lacking anthers; without stamens.
Example 1: The anantherous flowers of this plant rely entirely on wind for pollination.  这种植物的无花药花完全依靠风来授粉。

Example 2:  Researchers studied the genetic basis of the anantherous mutation in the tomato plants. 研究人员研究了番茄植株中无花药突变的遗传基础。

synonym: stamenless
antonym: staminate

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀν- (an-, “without”) + ἀνθηρός (anthērós, “blooming, flowery”)  + -ous (English adjective suffix).
