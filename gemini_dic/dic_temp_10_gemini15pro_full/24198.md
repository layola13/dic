brideknot: /ˈbraɪdˌnɒt/ (US) /ˈbraɪdˌnɒt/ (UK)| n.|新娘结，爱情结|A knot, especially an ornamental one, tied as part of a wedding ceremony, symbolizing the union of the couple.  Historically, it also referred to a ribbon or lace tied around the bride's wrist, from which hung small ornaments. It was customary for the guests to pull off these ornaments, each paying a small sum for the privilege.|The brideknot was beautifully tied, symbolizing their lifelong commitment. 新娘结系得很漂亮，象征着他们一生的承诺。|Near synonyms: wedding knot, marriage knot|Antonyms: N/A|noun
