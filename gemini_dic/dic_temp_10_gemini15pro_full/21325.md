bloody-minded:/ˈblʌdiˈmaɪndɪd/
bloody-minded: adj.
bloody-minded: 故意刁难的；存心作对的；不合作的
bloody-minded: Deliberately uncooperative and awkward.
bloody-minded: He's so bloody-minded he'll refuse to help just to annoy you. 他这人就是爱抬杠，他会故意拒绝帮忙，就为了惹你生气。

bloody-minded: She was being bloody-minded about the whole thing. 她对整件事都故意刁难。
bloody-minded: 近义词: awkward, contrary, difficult, perverse, unreasonable, unhelpful
bloody-minded: 反义词: agreeable, amenable, cooperative, helpful, reasonable
bloody-minded: 词性: 形容词
