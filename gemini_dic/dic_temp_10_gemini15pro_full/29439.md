catallactics:/ˌkætəˈlæktɪks/ | n. |交换学；交易学| The study of exchanges, of how people allocate and distribute scarce resources through markets and pricing mechanisms. It emphasizes voluntary cooperation and mutual benefit in economic interactions. | Catallactics helps explain how prices are formed in a free market.  交换学有助于解释自由市场中价格是如何形成的。| Catallactics played a key role in the development of Austrian economics. 交换学在奥地利经济学的发展中起到了关键作用。 |Synonyms:  economics, praxeology | Antonyms:  N/A (It's a field of study, not a concept with a direct opposite.) | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *katallaktēs* (a money-changer, from *katallasso* "to exchange, reconcile")
