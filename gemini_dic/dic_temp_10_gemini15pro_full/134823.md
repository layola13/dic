nonministerially:/ˌnɒnmɪnɪˈstɪəriəli/
adverb
非部长地，非官方地
Not in a ministerial capacity; not through official channels.
The issue was handled nonministerially, through back channels.  这个问题是非官方地、通过秘密渠道处理的。
The decision was made nonministerially, bypassing the usual government procedures.  这个决定是绕过通常的政府程序，非正式地做出的。
Synonyms:  informally, unofficially, privately
Antonyms: officially, ministerially
词性: 副词
