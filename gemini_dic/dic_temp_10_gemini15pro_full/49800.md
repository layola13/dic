demonological:/ˌdiːmənəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ |adj.|恶魔学的，魔鬼学的|Relating to the study of demons or beliefs about demons. |The book delved into demonological lore, exploring the hierarchy and powers of various demonic entities. 这本书深入研究了恶魔学，探讨了各种恶魔实体的等级和力量。|例句：His research interests include demonological practices in medieval Europe. 他的研究兴趣包括中世纪欧洲的恶魔学实践。

例句：The museum exhibit featured a collection of demonological artifacts, including grimoires and amulets. 博物馆的展览展出了一系列恶魔学文物，包括魔法书和护身符。|近义词：demonic, satanic|反义词：angelic, divine|词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *daemonologicus, from Ancient Greek δαιμονολογία (daimonología, “treatise on demons”), from δαίμων (daímōn, “deity, spirit”) + -λογία (-logía, “-logy”).
