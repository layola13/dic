lie-about:/ˈlaɪ əˌbaʊt/
noun
无所事事的人；闲荡的人
a person who spends their time doing nothing or being unproductive
He's such a lie-about, always sleeping in and watching TV. 他真是个游手好闲的人，总是睡懒觉看电视。


近义词:loafer, idler, slacker
反义词:hard worker, busybody
