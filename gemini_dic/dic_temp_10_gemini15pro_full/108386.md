lawbreakings:/ˈlɔːˌbreɪkɪŋz/
n.
违法行为，犯罪行为
The act of breaking the law; illegal activities.
The city has seen a rise in lawbreakings in recent months.  这座城市最近几个月的违法行为有所增加。
Police are investigating a series of lawbreakings in the area. 警方正在调查该地区一系列的违法行为。
近义词: offenses, crimes, violations,  illegalities
反义词: law-abidingness, obedience, compliance
