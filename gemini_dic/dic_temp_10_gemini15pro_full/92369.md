hypochondriasis:/ˌhaɪpəʊkɒnˈdraɪəsɪs/ /ˌhaɪpəʊkɑːnˈdraɪəsɪs/| n.|疑病症，臆想病|A mental disorder characterized by excessive worry about having a serious illness, even when there is no medical evidence to support the concern.  A persistent belief that one has a serious medical condition despite reassurances from doctors that there is no physical cause for the symptoms.|She suffered from hypochondriasis and was constantly visiting the doctor. 她患有疑病症，经常去看医生。

He's a terrible hypochondriac and always thinks he's dying. 他是一个严重的疑病症患者，总是认为自己快要死了。|近义词：health anxiety, illness anxiety disorder|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider) robust health, well-being|词性: noun
