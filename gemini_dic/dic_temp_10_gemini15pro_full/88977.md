holily:/ˈhoʊlɪli/| adv.|神圣地，圣洁地|In a holy manner; righteously; piously.  Referring to actions or behavior performed with reverence, devotion, or in accordance with religious principles.|She lived holily, dedicating her life to prayer and service. 她过着圣洁的生活，将自己的一生奉献给祈祷和服务。|近义词：righteously, piously, devoutly, sacredly|反义词：wickedly, sinfully, impiously|词性:副词

词源: From Middle English *holily, from Old English *hāliglīce (“holily”), equivalent to holy +‎ -ly. Cognate with Dutch heiliglijk (“holily”), German heiliglich (“holily”).
