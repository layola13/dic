bulldozed:/ˈbʊldoʊzd/
verb|推平，摧毁，强迫|To clear (ground) by using a bulldozer. To force or coerce (someone) into doing something.|The construction crew bulldozed the old building to make way for the new shopping mall. 建筑工人用推土机推平了旧建筑，为新的购物中心腾出空间。|demolished, flattened, leveled, razed|protected, preserved|v.
bulldoze:/ˈbʊldoʊz/
verb|恐吓，胁迫|To intimidate or coerce (someone) into doing something.|The boss bulldozed his employees into working overtime. 老板强迫他的员工加班。|coerce, intimidate, pressure|persuade, encourage|v.
