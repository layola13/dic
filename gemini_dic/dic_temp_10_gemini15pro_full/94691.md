improbableness:/ɪmˌprɒbəb(ə)lnəs/
n.
不可能，未必会发生
The improbableness of his story made it hard to believe.  他故事的不可信度使人难以相信。
The sheer improbableness of the event made it all the more memorable.  这件事发生的极不可能反而使它更加令人难忘。
unlikelihood, implausibility
probability, likelihood
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From improbable + -ness.  Improbable from in- (not) + probable (likely).  Probable from Latin probabilis (provable, likely).
