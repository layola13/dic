overimitation: /ˌoʊvərɪmɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 过度模仿 | The action of copying another person’s actions or behaviours, even when those actions or behaviours appear unnecessary or irrelevant to achieving a goal. | Young children often display overimitation, meticulously copying every step of a demonstrated action, even if some steps are clearly superfluous.  小孩子经常表现出过度模仿，一丝不苟地复制示范动作的每一步，即使有些步骤明显是多余的。

| 近义词: mimicry, emulation,  | 反义词: innovation, originality | 词性: noun
