angering:/ˈæŋɡərɪŋ/| adj.|使人愤怒的，令人恼火的|Causing someone to become angry.  Derived from the verb "anger" with the suffix "-ing" to form a present participle adjective.|Her angering words made him storm out of the room. 她那些令人恼火的话让他冲出了房间。|Synonyms: infuriating, exasperating, provoking, irritating|Antonyms: soothing, calming, pleasing, placating|adjective
