overfishing: /ˌoʊvərˈfɪʃɪŋ/
n.
过度捕捞
The act of catching too many fish, so that there are not enough left to breed.
Overfishing has severely depleted fish stocks in the Atlantic.  过度捕捞已经严重耗尽了大西洋的鱼类资源。
Overfishing threatens the livelihoods of coastal communities. 过度捕捞威胁着沿海社区的生计。
近义词: depletion, exhaustion
反义词: sustainable fishing, conservation
