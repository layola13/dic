endocardium:/ˌɛndoʊˈkɑːrdiəm/| n. |心内膜|The thin, smooth membrane that lines the inside of the chambers of the heart and forms the surface of the valves.|The endocardium is essential for maintaining proper blood flow.|近义词:lining (of the heart)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps epicardium as an opposite layer)|noun
