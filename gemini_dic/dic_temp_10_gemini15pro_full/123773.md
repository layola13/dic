moory:/ˈmʊəri/| adj.|沼泽的，泥炭沼泽的|Of or relating to moors; resembling a moor; marshy. |The air smelled fresh and moory. 空气闻起来清新，带着沼泽的气息。|近义词：marshy, boggy, swampy, fenny|反义词：dry, arid|形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *mori, from Old English *mōrig (unattested), from mōr (“moor”). Cognate with Scots murie (“marshy”), West Frisian moarich (“moory”).
