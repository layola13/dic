contrabandism:/ˈkɑːntrəˌbændɪzəm/ /ˈkɒntrəˌbændɪzəm/
n.
走私；走私活动
The act or practice of smuggling illegal goods.
例句：The authorities are cracking down on contrabandism across the border.  当局正在打击跨境走私活动。

例句：Contrabandism of endangered species is a serious crime.  走私濒危物种是一项严重的罪行。

近义词：smuggling, trafficking, illicit trade
反义词：legal trade, legitimate commerce
词性：名词

contraband:/ˈkɑːntrəˌbænd/ /ˈkɒntrəˌbænd/
n.
走私货；违禁品
Goods that have been imported or exported illegally.
例句：Customs officials seized the contraband. 海关官员查获了走私货。

例句：The ship was carrying contraband weapons. 这艘船载着违禁武器。

近义词：smuggled goods, illicit goods, prohibited items
反义词：legal goods, permitted items
词性：名词

词源：From Italian contrabando, from contra + bando ‘proclamation, ban’, from Old High German bannan ‘to summon, proclaim’.
