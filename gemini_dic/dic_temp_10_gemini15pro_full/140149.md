offed:/ɔːft/
v. |杀死，干掉 (slang, informal)|To kill someone.  This is a slang/informal usage. |They offed him in the alley. 他们在小巷里把他干掉了。|近义词：killed, murdered, assassinated|反义词:saved, rescued
词性:动词
