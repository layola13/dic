flacked:/flækt/
verb
受到批评；遭到抨击；被大肆宣传
subjected to criticism, especially in the media; publicized heavily.
The new policy was flacked by the opposition party. 这项新政策遭到了反对党的抨击。
The movie was heavily flacked before its release. 这部电影在上映前被大肆宣传。
近义词: criticized, slammed, publicized, promoted
反义词: praised, lauded
