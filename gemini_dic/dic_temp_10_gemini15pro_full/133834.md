nongovernance:/ˌnɒnˈɡʌvərnəns/ |n.| 非治理；治理缺失|The state of lacking governance or having ineffective governance, often leading to instability and chaos.  This can refer to situations where there is no functioning government, a weak or corrupt government, or a lack of regulation in a particular area.|例句：The collapse of the state led to a period of nongovernance and widespread violence.  国家崩溃导致了一段时间的非治理和普遍的暴力。

例句：Nongovernance in the financial sector contributed to the economic crisis. 金融部门的治理缺失导致了经济危机。|近义词：misgovernance, lack of governance, anarchy, lawlessness|反义词：governance, rule of law, order|词性:名词
