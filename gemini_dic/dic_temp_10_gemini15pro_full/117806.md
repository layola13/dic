meliorator:/ˈmiːliəreɪtər/| n.|改良者，改进者|A person or thing that meliorates or improves something, especially land.|The farmer acted as a meliorator of the soil, enriching it with compost and other organic matter. 农民充当了土壤的改良者，用堆肥和其他有机物质丰富土壤。|近义词:improver, reformer, enhancer, ameliorator|反义词:worsener, degrader|noun
