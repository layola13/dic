faddishnesses:/ˈfædɪʃnɪsɪz/
noun
时尚；一时流行的风尚；流行一时的东西
The state or quality of being faddish; a fondness for or devotion to fads.  A collection of fads.
Her faddishnesses extended from clothes to diets. 她对时尚的追求从服装延伸到饮食。
Synonyms: trendiness, vogue, fashionableness, stylishness
Antonyms: timelessness, classicism, permanence
