convene:/kənˈviːn/ | v. |召集，集合|To come together formally for an official or public purpose; assemble.|The committee convened last week to discuss the budget. 委员会上周召开会议讨论预算。|近义词：assemble, gather, summon|反义词：adjourn, disperse, dismiss|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Old French *convenir*, from Latin *convenīre* "to come together, assemble, meet, agree," from *com-* "together" (see com-) + *venīre* "to come" (see come).
