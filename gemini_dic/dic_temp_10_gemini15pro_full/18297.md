belladonnas: /ˌbeləˈdɑːnəz/ | n. |颠茄（复数）| A perennial Eurasian herbaceous plant (Atropa belladonna) with reddish bell-shaped flowers and poisonous black berries. Also called deadly nightshade.  The drug prepared from its leaves and roots. |  Belladonnas are highly toxic and should never be ingested. 颠茄毒性很强，切勿吞食。|  The belladonnas were cultivated for medicinal purposes. 这些颠茄是为了药用而种植的。 | Synonyms: deadly nightshade, dwale | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a plant, perhaps consider "safe herb" in a specific context) | Noun
