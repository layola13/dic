intercapped:/ˌɪntərˈkæpt/ | adj. | 大小写间隔的，交错大小写的 |  Having alternating upper and lower case letters, typically with initial capitals.  This style is often used for trademarks or product names to create a distinctive visual appearance. | Example: The company's name, eBay, is intercapped.  该公司名称eBay是大小写间隔的。
