crouched:/kraʊtʃt/ | adj. | 蹲伏的，蜷缩的 |  Being in a low position with the legs bent and the upper body forward and down. | The cat crouched, ready to pounce on the mouse. 猫蹲伏着，准备扑向老鼠。|  squatting, hunched, stooped | standing, upright | adjective

crouch:/kraʊtʃ/ | vi. vt. | 蹲下，蜷缩 | To lower the body with the limbs close to the body, especially so as to escape observation; to stoop or lie close to the ground. | He crouched down to avoid being seen. 他蹲下来以免被看见。 He crouched behind a bush. 他蹲在一个灌木丛后面。 | squat, stoop, hunch | stand, straighten | verb
