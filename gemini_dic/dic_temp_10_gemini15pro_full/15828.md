ballader:/ˈbælədər/ | n. | 民谣歌手；演唱民谣的人 | A person who writes or sings ballads. |  He was a wandering ballader, traveling from town to town. 他是一位四处流浪的民谣歌手，从一个镇子到另一个镇子。| synonym: folk singer, minstrel, troubadour | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "pop star" for a contrasting genre) | noun

Etymology:  From "ballad" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Ballad" comes from Old French "balade," from Provençal "balada," meaning "dancing song." Ultimately from Late Latin "ballare" meaning "to dance."
