gloomful: /ˈɡluːmfʊl/ (UK) /ˈɡluːmfəl/ (US)| adj. | 忧郁的，阴沉的 | Filled with or expressing gloom; dismal. | The gloomful weather matched her mood.  阴沉的天气和她忧郁的心情很相配。 | The gloomful news cast a shadow over the celebration. 阴沉的消息给庆祝活动蒙上了一层阴影。 | 近义词: gloomy, depressing, melancholic, somber, sullen | 反义词: cheerful, bright, happy, joyful | 词性: 形容词

gloom: /ɡluːm/ | n. | 昏暗，忧郁 | Partial or total darkness; a state of depression or despondency. | 词源: From Middle English *gloumen*, from Old English *glom*, meaning "twilight".
