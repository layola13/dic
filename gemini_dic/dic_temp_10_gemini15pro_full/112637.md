low-rise: /ˈloʊˌraɪz/ (US), /ˈləʊˌraɪz/ (UK)
adjective
低腰的
Having a low waistband, sitting low on the hips.
These low-rise jeans are very fashionable. 这些低腰牛仔裤很时尚。
She prefers low-rise pants to high-waisted ones. 她喜欢低腰裤而不是高腰裤。
近义词: hip-hugging, low-slung
反义词: high-waisted, high-rise
词性: adjective
