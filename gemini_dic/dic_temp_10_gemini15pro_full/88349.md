hippophagi:/ˌhɪpəˈfædʒaɪ/
n.
食马者；吃马肉的人
People who eat horse meat.
The hippophagi of ancient Scythia were nomadic tribes known for their consumption of horseflesh.  古代斯基泰的食马者是游牧部落，以食用马肉而闻名。
Near synonyms: horse-eaters
No direct antonym
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek  ἱπποφάγος (hippopʰágos, “horse-eating”) from ἵππος (híppos, “horse”) + φαγεῖν (phageîn, “to eat”).
