The ritual was intended to forefend evil spirits.  这个仪式是为了避开邪灵。| 近义词: prevent, avert, preclude, obviate, forestall | 反义词: cause, incite, encourage, promote | 词性: 动词

词源： From Middle English *forefenden, from Old English forfēon (“to detest, despise; renounce, refuse, reject; avert, prevent”), from for- + fēon (“to hate, abhor; rejoice”). Cognate with Old High German firfāhan (“to despise, condemn”). More at for-, foe.
