ostracodermi:/ˌɒstrəˈkəʊdərmiː/ |n. | 甲胄鱼纲 | An extinct class of primitive jawless fish with bony armor covering the head and part of the body, considered to be the earliest known vertebrates. |  The ostracoderms flourished in the Silurian and Devonian periods. 甲胄鱼兴盛于志留纪和泥盆纪。|  Fossil ostracoderms provide valuable insights into the evolution of vertebrates. 甲胄鱼化石为脊椎动物的进化提供了宝贵的见解。 | 近义词: agnathan (无颌类) | 反义词:  gnathostomata (有颌类) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek óstrakon "shell" + dérma "skin."
