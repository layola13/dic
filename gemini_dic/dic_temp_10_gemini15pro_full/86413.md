heeded:/ˈhiːdɪd/| v.|注意，留心，听从|to pay attention to something, especially advice or a warning|例句：They heeded the warnings and left the area. 他们听从了警告，离开了该地区。

例句：She should have heeded her mother's advice. 她本应该听从母亲的建议。|近义词：obeyed, followed, observed, minded, noticed|反义词：ignored, disregarded, disobeyed|词性: 动词(Past Participle)


词源:
From Middle English heden, from Old English hēdan (“to heed, care for, observe, attend to, hold, keep, protect, entertain”), from Proto-Germanic *haidijan- (“to take care of, watch”), from Proto-Indo-European *kayt- (“to observe, see”).
