ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *expectacioun*, borrowed from Latin *exspectātiō*, *exspectātiōn-*, noun of action from past-participle stem of *exspectāre* (“to look out for, await, expect”).  Equivalent to *expect* +‎ *-ation*.
