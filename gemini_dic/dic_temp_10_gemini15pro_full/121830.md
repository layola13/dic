misrelied:/ˌmɪsrɪˈlaɪd/
verb
误信，错误地依赖
placed reliance on (someone or something) incorrectly.
I misrelied on the information I was given and made a costly mistake. 我误信了我得到的信息，犯了一个代价高昂的错误。
近义词:mistrusted, doubted
反义词:trusted, depended on
词性:verb

词源:
From mis- + relied, the past tense of rely.  The prefix "mis-" indicates something done wrongly or incorrectly.  "Rely" comes from Old French "relier" meaning "to rally, bring together, bind."
