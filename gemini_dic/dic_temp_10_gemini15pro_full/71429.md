flakier:/ˈfleɪkiər/ | adj. | 更薄的，更易碎的，更古怪的，更不可靠的 | more flaky; tending to flake or break off easily in small, thin pieces; also, more eccentric, unreliable, or temperamental. | The pastry was flakier this time, crumbling delightfully with each bite. 这回的糕点更酥了，每咬一口都碎得很好吃。

The politician's promises were becoming flakier, and fewer people believed him.  这位政治家的承诺越来越不可靠，相信他的人越来越少。 | 近义词：crumbly, brittle, eccentric, unpredictable, unreliable | 反义词：stable, consistent, reliable, dependable, solid | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From flake + -y.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *flakon.
