blister:/ˈblɪstər/ (US), /ˈblɪstə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|水疱，水泡；气泡，泡；(使)起泡|A small bubble on the skin filled with serum and caused by friction, burning, or other damage.  (A raised area on the surface of something resembling a blister.)  To form blisters or cause blisters to form.|例句：I got a blister on my heel from my new shoes. 我的新鞋把我脚后跟磨出了水泡。

|例句：The paint on the wall started to blister because of the damp.  因为潮湿，墙上的油漆开始起泡。|近义词：bubble, vesicle, bleb|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, verb
