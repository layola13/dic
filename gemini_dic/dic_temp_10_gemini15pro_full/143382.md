outfielded:/ˌaʊtˈfiːldɪd/
verb
在比赛中胜过，在…方面超过
surpassed in fielding; performed better than in the field (in sports, especially baseball or cricket)
The Red Sox outfielded the Yankees, making several spectacular catches. 红袜队在防守上胜过洋基队，做出几次精彩的接杀。
outplayed, surpassed, bested
underperformed
v.
