dihexagonal:/ˌdaɪhɛksˈæɡənəl/| adj.|双六角形的；十二边形的|Relating to or having the form of a twelve-sided polygon.  Composed of two hexagons. |The crystal structure is dihexagonal. 晶体结构是双六角形的。|The dihexagonal pyramidal class in crystallography exhibits this symmetry. 晶体学中的双六角锥体类展现了这种对称性。|duodecagonal, dodecagonal|n/a|adjective
