jinx:/dʒɪŋks/| n. vt.|厄运；霉运；不祥的人或事物；使倒霉；给…带来厄运|A jinx is something that is thought to bring bad luck.  As a verb, it means to cause someone or something to have bad luck. |例句：There's a jinx on this car! 这辆车真倒霉！|近义词：curse, hex, spell|反义词:blessing, charm|noun
jinx:/dʒɪŋks/| vt.|使倒霉；给…带来厄运|To jinx someone or something means to cause them to experience bad luck.|例句：I think I jinxed the game by talking about how well we were playing. 我想我说了我们打得多好，结果把比赛给搞砸了。|近义词：curse, hex|反义词:bless|verb
