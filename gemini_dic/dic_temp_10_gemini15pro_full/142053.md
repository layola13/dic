organs:/ˈɔːrɡənz/ (US) /ˈɔːɡənz/ (UK)| n. |器官;机构;风琴|A part of a body that has a particular function (such as the heart, liver, or lungs).  Also, a means of performing a function or accomplishing something; a medium of communication or information.  A musical instrument similar to a piano, but with pipes supplied with wind.|

The heart and lungs are vital organs. 心脏和肺是重要器官。

The newspaper is an important organ for public opinion. 报纸是公众舆论的重要机构。

He played a beautiful piece on the church organ. 他在教堂的风琴上演奏了一首优美的曲子。
