dirts:/dɜːrts/ | n. | 污垢，泥土 | Dirt in the plural form, referring to loose earth, soil, or grime. | The dirts under his fingernails suggested he'd been gardening. 他的指甲里的泥土表明他一直在园艺。| Synonym: grime, filth, soil | Antonym: cleanliness, purity | noun

dirts:/dɜːrts/ | v. | 弄脏 | To make something dirty.  This is a less common usage and usually refers to the act of revealing embarrassing information about someone (often in the phrase "to air someone's dirty laundry" or similar). |  He threatened to dirt on his former business partner. 他威胁要揭露他以前生意伙伴的丑闻。 | Synonym: soil, stain, tarnish, smear (reputation) | Antonym: clean, cleanse, purify | verb
