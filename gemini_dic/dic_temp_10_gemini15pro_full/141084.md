onondagas:/ˌɒnənˈdɑːɡəz/ | n. |奥农达加人|  A member of the Iroquois people formerly living south of Lake Ontario.  They are one of the original five nations of the Iroquois Confederacy (Haudenosaunee). | The Onondagas played a crucial role in the Iroquois Confederacy. 奥农达加人在易洛魁联盟中扮演着至关重要的角色。|  Iroquois, Haudenosaunee |  None | Noun
