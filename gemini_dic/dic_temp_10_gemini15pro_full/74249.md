foxholes:/ˈfɑːkshəʊlz/ (US) /ˈfɒkshəʊlz/ (UK)| n. |散兵坑，散兵壕，掩体|A hole dug in the ground as a shelter for one or two soldiers from enemy fire or bombardment.|The soldiers huddled in their foxholes, waiting for the shelling to stop. 士兵们挤在散兵坑里，等待炮击停止。|
The company commander checked each of the foxholes to ensure the men were prepared. 连长检查了每一个散兵坑，以确保士兵们做好了准备。|近义词：trenches, bunkers, dugouts|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "exposed position")|词性:noun
