drawrod:/ˈdrɔːrɒd/ |n.|拉杆，拉棒|A metal rod used for drawing or pulling something, often part of a mechanism.|The drawrod on the steam engine was damaged. 蒸汽机的拉杆损坏了。|Synonyms: tie rod, connecting rod|Antonyms: push rod|noun
