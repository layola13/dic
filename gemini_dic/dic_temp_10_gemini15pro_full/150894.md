paralinguistic:/ˌpærəˌlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk/ | adj.| 副语言的 | Relating to or denoting vocal or non-vocal elements of communication (such as facial expressions, gestures, and pitch of voice) that do not form part of verbal language but that may modify meaning or convey emotion. | She gave me a paralinguistic clue that she was unhappy. 她给了我一个副语言的暗示，表示她不开心。|  He noticed the paralinguistic cues, such as her fidgeting and averted gaze, which suggested she was lying.  他注意到了副语言的线索，比如她坐立不安和躲闪的目光，这表明她在撒谎。| nonverbal, extralinguistic | verbal, linguistic | adjective
