go-down:/ˈɡoʊˌdaʊn/ (US) /ˈɡəʊˌdaʊn/ (UK)| n.|仓库；地下室；储存窖；（尤指非法经营的）酒吧，夜总会|A go-down is a place where goods are stored.  A go-down can also refer to a basement or cellar, particularly in some regions. In some contexts, it can also mean a (often illicit) bar or nightclub.|The goods were stored in the go-down until they were shipped. 货物存放在仓库里，直到被运走。|近义词：warehouse, cellar, basement, storehouse|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

go-down:/ˈɡoʊˌdaʊn/ (US) /ˈɡəʊˌdaʊn/ (UK)| v. (informal)|下降;  被接受; 被记住（以某种方式）; （日、月）落下|To descend, to be received (in a certain way), to be remembered (in a certain way), or (of the sun or moon) to set.|The price of oil went down last week. 油价上周下跌了。|近义词：descend, fall, sink, decline, set (sun/moon)|反义词: rise, ascend, climb, increase|词性:动词
