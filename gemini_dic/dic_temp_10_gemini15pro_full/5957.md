ambrosia: /æmˈbroʊʒə/ /æmˈbroʊziə/ | n. | 神食；美味的食物 | (in Greek mythology) the food of the gods; something very pleasing to taste or smell. | The chef's signature dish was hailed as pure ambrosia.  这位厨师的招牌菜被誉为人间美味。| 近义词: delicacy, nectar | 反义词: swill, garbage | 词性: 名词

词源: From Ancient Greek ἀμβροσία (ambrosía, “immortality, ambrosia”), from ἄμβροτος (ámbrotos, “immortal”).
