giga:/ˈɡɪɡə/| adj.|十亿；千兆|From the Greek γίγας (gígas), meaning "giant."  Used as a prefix denoting a factor of one billion (10^9).|A gigabyte is a unit of information equal to one billion bytes.|例句：My new hard drive has a capacity of one terabyte, which is equivalent to 1024 gigabytes. 我的新硬盘容量为 1 TB，相当于 1024 GB。

例句：5G networks offer gigabit speeds. 5G 网络提供千兆比特的速度。|近义词：billion|反义词: N/A|词性: prefix (primarily), also used as a noun in informal contexts (e.g., "a giga of RAM")
