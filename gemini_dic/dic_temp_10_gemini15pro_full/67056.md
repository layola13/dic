explicating:/ˈɛksplɪkeɪtɪŋ/
verb
阐述；解释；说明
Making something clear by explaining it in detail.
The professor spent an hour explicating the poem to his students. 教授花了一个小时向学生们讲解这首诗。
explaining, interpreting, clarifying
obscuring, confusing
v.
