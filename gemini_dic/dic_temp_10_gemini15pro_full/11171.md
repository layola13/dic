archiblastula:/ˌɑːrkɪˈblæstʃʊlə/| n. |原肠胚|An embryonic stage of some invertebrates, especially certain arthropods, characterized by a hollow sphere of cells, the blastula, before gastrulation and the formation of germ layers.  It's a specific type of blastula where all cells are presumptive blastomeres and no blastocoel is present.|The archiblastula of this species develops rapidly.  该物种的原肠胚发育迅速。|近义词：blastula|反义词: N/A|名词
