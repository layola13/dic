crispily:/ˈkrɪspɪli/| adv.|酥脆地；干脆地|In a crisp manner; so as to be crisp.  Characterized by crispness.|The bacon was fried crispily. 培根煎得酥脆。|近义词：crunchily, crisply|反义词:soggy, softly|词性:副词
词源:From crisp + -ly.  Ultimately from the Proto-Germanic *kriusp-.
