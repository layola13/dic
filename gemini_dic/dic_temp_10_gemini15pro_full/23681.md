bransle:/ˈbrɑːnsl/ | n. | 布朗斯勒舞，一种古老的法国集体舞 | A lively, round dance popular in France from the 16th to the 18th centuries.  |  The musicians struck up a bransle, and the couples joined in the dance.  乐师们奏起了布朗斯勒舞曲，情侣们加入了舞蹈。 | near synonyms: dance, round dance, jig | antonym: stillness | noun

bransle:/ˈbrɑːnsl/ | v. | 跳布朗斯勒舞 | To dance the bransle. | The villagers bransled in the town square. 村民们在镇上的广场上跳布朗斯勒舞. | near synonyms: dance, jig, reel | antonym: stand still | verb
