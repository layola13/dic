ctenoidean: /ˌtiːnɔɪˈdɪən/ | adj. n. | 栉鳞的，栉鳞鱼 |  Relating to or denoting fishes belonging to the order Ctenoidei, characterized by scales with a comb-like edge. Also, a fish of this order.  | Example:  The perch is a ctenoidean fish.  鲈鱼是一种栉鳞鱼。

Example: Ctenoidean scales are rough to the touch. 栉鳞摸起来很粗糙。 | 近义词:  (For the noun)  spiny-rayed fish | 反义词: cycloidean | 词性: adjective, noun
