editorialization:/ˌɛdɪˌtɔːriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |  社论化，评论化 | The act of injecting personal opinions or bias into a supposedly objective piece of writing or reporting. | The documentary's heavy editorialization undermined its credibility. 这部纪录片浓厚的评论色彩损害了它的可信度。 | synonyms:  commentary, opinionizing, slanting, bias, spin | antonyms: objectivity, neutrality, impartiality | noun

editorialize:/ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəlaɪz/ | v. |  发表社论；评论 | To express opinions or bias in a supposedly objective piece of writing or reporting. | The news anchor was criticized for editorializing during the broadcast.  这位新闻主播因在广播中发表评论而受到批评。 | synonyms: comment, opine, offer an opinion, slant, spin | antonyms: report, state facts, remain neutral | verb
