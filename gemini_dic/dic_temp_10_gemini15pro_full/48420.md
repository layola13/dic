deducibilities:/dɪˌdʌktəˈbɪlətiz/ |n.| 可扣除性；可扣除额 |The deducibilities of certain expenses can significantly impact your tax liability. 某些费用的可扣除性会显著影响你的纳税义务。|  The new tax laws have changed the deducibilities for charitable donations. 新的税法改变了慈善捐款的可扣除额。|deductions, allowances| non-deductibles |noun


词源:  From the adjective "deductible" + "-ities" (suffix forming plural nouns). "Deductible" comes from the verb "deduct," from Latin "deductus," past participle of "deducere" (to lead down, subtract), from "de-" (down, away) + "ducere" (to lead).
