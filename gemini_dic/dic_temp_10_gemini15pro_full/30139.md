ceilidh:/ˈkeɪli/ (US), /ˈseɪli/ (UK)| n. |凯利舞会；爱尔兰或苏格兰的传统社交聚会|A ceilidh is a social gathering which involves traditional Gaelic folk music and dancing, originating in Scotland and Ireland. It often features lively group dances and is a popular form of entertainment.|We went to a ceilidh last night and had a fantastic time. 我们昨晚去了一个凯利舞会，玩得很开心。

The village hall hosts a ceilidh every month. 村礼堂每个月都会举办一次凯利舞会。|近义词：party, gathering, social, dance|反义词:|名词
