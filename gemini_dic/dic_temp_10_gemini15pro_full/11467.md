arguments:/ˈɑːrɡjʊmənts/| n.|争论，论点，论据|Reasons or evidence presented in support of a particular view or position.  A series of statements intended to persuade someone of something or to justify a course of action.  A disagreement, or the process of disagreeing.|例句：The arguments for and against the proposal were presented.  正反两方的论点都被提出来了。

例句：They had a heated argument about politics. 他们就政治问题进行了激烈的争论。|近义词：disputes, debates, reasons, grounds, evidence|反义词:agreements, accords, harmony|词性:noun
