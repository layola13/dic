grine:/ɡraɪn/| v. | 露齿而笑，咧嘴笑 | To smile broadly, often baring the teeth; to express pleasure, amusement, or sometimes scorn, by smiling. | He grinned when he saw his birthday present. 他看到生日礼物时咧嘴笑了。

She grinned mischievously. 她调皮地咧嘴一笑。


|近义词：smile, beam, smirk|反义词：frown, scowl|词性:动词
