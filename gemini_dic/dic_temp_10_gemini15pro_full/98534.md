instigatingly:/ˈɪnstɪɡeɪtɪŋli/
adverb
煽动地，怂恿地
In a way that incites or urges someone to do something, typically something that is unlawful or undesirable.
His words, spoken instigatingly, roused the crowd to violence. 他煽动性的话语激起了群众的暴力行为。
instigatingly, she whispered the plan to her accomplice. 她煽动地向她的同伙低语计划。
近义词:provocatively, incitingly, urgingly
反义词:dissuasively, calmingly
词性:adverb

词源: From instigate + -ing + -ly.  Instigate comes from the Latin instigare "to incite, urge forward," from in- "in" + stigare "to prick, goad."
