gestate:/ˈdʒɛsteɪt/ | v. | 孕育，酝酿，(想法、计划等)逐渐形成 | to carry in the womb during the period from conception to birth; to conceive and gradually develop something over a period of time |  The idea for the novel gestated for many years. 这部小说的构思酝酿了很多年。 | synonyms: develop, mature, incubate | antonyms: abort, stifle | verb

gestated:/ˈdʒɛsteɪtɪd/ | adj. |  酝酿的，孕育的 |  having been carried in the womb until fully developed; having been conceived and developed over a period of time |  His latest project is a well-gestated plan. 他的最新项目是一个经过充分酝酿的计划。 | synonyms: developed, mature, nurtured | antonyms: undeveloped, immature, premature | adjective
