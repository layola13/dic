nabbing:/ˈnæbɪŋ/| n. / v. (present participle of nab)|逮捕，抓获; 抢夺|The act of catching someone doing something wrong or illegal, or the act of grabbing something quickly. |Police made several nabbing for drug offenses. 警方因毒品犯罪逮捕了数人。|近义词：arresting, apprehending, seizing, grabbing, snatching|反义词: releasing, freeing|词性: noun / verb (present participle)
Etymology:  From the verb "nab," which originated in the late 18th century, likely from a Scandinavian source, possibly related to Swedish *nappa "to snatch, grab."
