magnitude:/ˈmæɡnɪtjuːd/| n. |大小；量级；震级；重要；重要性|a size or extent of something|例句：They don’t seem to grasp the magnitude of the problem. 他们似乎没有意识到这个问题的重要性。

例句：An earthquake of magnitude 7.2 struck the region. 该地区发生了7.2级地震。|近义词：size, extent, degree, scale, importance|反义词:insignificance, unimportance|词性:noun

magnitude:/ˈmæɡnɪtjuːdz/| n. |大小；量级；震级；重要；重要性（magnitude的复数形式）|sizes or extents of something|例句：The magnitudes of the two stars are quite different. 这两颗恒星的大小相差很大。

例句：We need to consider the magnitudes of the risks involved. 我们需要考虑所涉及风险的大小。|近义词：sizes, extents, degrees, scales, importances|反义词:insignificances, unimporances|词性:noun
