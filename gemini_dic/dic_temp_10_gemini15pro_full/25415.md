bulimics:/buːˈlɪmɪks/
|n.|暴食症患者|People with bulimia nervosa, characterized by binge eating followed by purging behaviors like vomiting or using laxatives.|Bulimics often struggle with body image issues. 暴食症患者经常与身体形象问题作斗争。|
|近义词: people with bulimia, bulimia sufferers|
|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially someone with anorexia)|
|词性: Noun (plural)|


bulimia:/buːˈlɪmiə/
|n.|暴食症|An eating disorder characterized by episodes of binge eating followed by compensatory behaviors like vomiting or using laxatives.|Bulimia can have serious health consequences. 暴食症会导致严重的健康后果。|
|近义词: bulimia nervosa, binge and purge syndrome|
|反义词: (No direct antonym)|
|词性: Noun|
