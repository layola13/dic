angeiotomy:/ˌændʒiˈɒtəmi/ | n. |血管切开术|Surgical incision into a blood vessel.  |The surgeon performed an angeiotomy to access the blocked artery. 外科医生进行了血管切开术以进入阻塞的动脉。|Synonym:  angiotomy|Antonym:  angiography (although not a direct opposite, it represents a different approach to vessels - visualizing rather than cutting)|noun
