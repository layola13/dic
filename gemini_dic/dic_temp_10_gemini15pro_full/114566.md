malaxator:/ˈmæləkseɪtər/ | n. | 揉捏器，软化器；按摩器 | A device used for kneading or softening substances, especially in industrial or scientific contexts. It can also refer to a massage device. | The malaxator mixed the clay and water thoroughly.  揉捏器将粘土和水彻底混合。

The malaxator was used to prepare the dough for baking.  揉捏器被用来准备烘焙面团。 | 近义词：kneader, mixer, softener, massager | 反义词: hardener | 词性: 名词
