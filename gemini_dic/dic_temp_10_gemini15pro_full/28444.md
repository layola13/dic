carbuncle: /ˈkɑːrbʌŋkəl/ (US) /ˈkɑːbʌŋkl/ (UK)| n.|痈;  红宝石; |A painful, localized bacterial infection of the skin and underlying tissues, larger than a boil, with multiple openings for the discharge of pus and dead tissue.
