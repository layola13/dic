overpayment:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪmənt/ | n. | 多付，过度支付 | An amount paid in excess of what is due. | Example: I received an overpayment on my salary this month and had to return the extra money. 这个月我的工资多付了，我不得不把多余的钱退回去。

Example: The overpayment was a result of a computer error. 多付款是由于电脑错误造成的。| Synonyms: excess payment, surplus payment | Antonyms: underpayment, short payment | noun
