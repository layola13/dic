coenesthesis:/ˌsɛnɪsˈθiːsɪs/
|n.|体感;  整体感觉;|The general feeling of inhabiting one's body; the totality of sensations of one's body.  It includes sensations such as hunger, thirst, and fatigue, as well as the sense of one's body in space.|Example 1:  She experienced a strange coenesthesis after the medication, feeling disconnected from her physical self. 她服药后体验到一种奇怪的体感，感觉与自己的身体脱节了。
