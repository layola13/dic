dravidians:/drəˈvɪdiənz/
noun
德拉维人
A member of any of a group of peoples indigenous to southern India and Sri Lanka and speaking Dravidian languages.
The Dravidians are believed to have been the original inhabitants of India. 德拉维人被认为是印度的原住民。
The majority of people in South India are Dravidians.  南印度的大多数人是德拉维人。
近义词: South Indians
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps Indo-Aryans in a historical/linguistic context)
词性: n.
