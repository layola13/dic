freshman's:/ˈfrɛʃmənzd/| n. |大学一年级学生的(东西)|Something that belongs to or is associated with a freshman. |例句：The freshman's dorm room was small but cozy. 大学一年级学生的宿舍房间虽小但很舒适。

例句：I accidentally took the freshman's textbook. 我不小心拿了那个大学一年级学生的课本。|近义词：first-year student's|反义词：senior's|词性：所有格


ETYMOLOGY->

From freshman + -'s (possessive suffix).  "Freshman" itself comes from fresh + man. "Fresh" meaning new and "man" referring to a person.  The term originated in the 16th century, referring to a new student at a university.
