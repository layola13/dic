cyclone-collector:/ˈsaɪkloʊn kəˈlɛktər/ | n. | 旋风收集器，旋风除尘器 | A device that uses centrifugal force to separate dust or other particles from a gas stream. |  The cyclone-collector efficiently removed the sawdust from the air. 旋风收集器有效地清除了空气中的锯末. | synonyms: cyclone separator, centrifugal separator | antonyms: filter, electrostatic precipitator | noun

cyclone:/ˈsaɪkloʊn/ | n. | 旋风，气旋，飓风 | A system of winds rotating inwards to an area of low barometric pressure. | The cyclone caused widespread damage. 气旋造成了大范围的破坏. | synonyms: hurricane, typhoon, tornado | antonyms: anticyclone | noun

collector:/kəˈlɛktər/ | n. | 收集者，收藏家 | A person who collects things of a particular type, professionally or as a hobby. | He is a stamp collector. 他是一位邮票收藏家. | synonyms: accumulator, gatherer, compiler | antonyms: distributor, disperser | noun
