outshop:/ˈaʊtʃɒp/ (UK), /ˈaʊtʃɑːp/ (US)| n.|外商店；外包商店；外协厂|A shop or factory, especially one independent of a main organization, which carries out work or supplies goods.|We decided to outsource the production to an outshop to reduce costs. 我们决定将生产外包给一家外协厂以降低成本.|Synonyms: subcontractor, external supplier, vendor, outside vendor|Antonyms: inshop, in-house|noun
