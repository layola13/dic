bullist: /ˈbʊlɪst/
n.
恃强凌弱者；恶霸
A person who uses their strength or power to frighten or hurt weaker people.
He was a bullist in school, always picking on smaller kids. 他在学校是个恶霸，总是欺负比他小的孩子。
近义词：bully, intimidator, oppressor, tormentor
反义词：victim, underdog


词性:
noun

词源:
From "bully" + "-ist" (suffix forming agent nouns).  "Bully" originates from Middle Dutch "boele" meaning "lover,"  later evolving to mean "pimp" and then acquiring its modern pejorative sense.
