fluidest:/ˈfluːɪdɪst/
adjective
最流畅的，最流动的
smoothest and most graceful; most easily flowing
Her movements were fluidest when she was dancing. 她跳舞时的动作最为流畅。

近义词:
most flowing, most graceful, smoothest, slickest, most liquid

反义词:
stiffest, most rigid, most viscous, clunkiest, most awkward

词性:
adjective (形容词)

词源:
From fluid + -est (superlative suffix).  "Fluid" comes from the Latin "fluidus," meaning "flowing."
