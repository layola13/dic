manitoba:/ˌmænɪˈtoʊbə/ (US), /ˌmænɪˈtəʊbə/ (UK)| n.|曼尼托巴省|A province located in central Canada, known for its prairies, lakes, and diverse wildlife. It is bordered by Ontario to the east, Saskatchewan to the west, Nunavut to the north, and the US states of North Dakota and Minnesota to the south.|Manitoba is known as the "Land of 100,000 Lakes". 曼尼托巴省被称为“十万湖之地”。
