befriended:/bɪˈfrɛndɪd/
verb
与...交朋友；对...友好
acted as a friend to.
She befriended the new student. 她和新来的学生交了朋友。
Synonyms: make friends with, pal up with, get friendly with
Antonyms: alienate, estrange
