misconstrues:/ˌmɪskənˈstruːz/
verb
曲解，误解
interprets (something, especially a person's words or actions) wrongly.
She completely misconstrued my words and thought I was insulting her. 她完全曲解了我的话，以为我在侮辱她。
misinterprets, misreads, misunderstands
understands, comprehends, interprets correctly
词性: 动词
