omnidirectionality:/ˌɒmnɪˌdaɪˌrekʃəˈnælɪti/ | n. | 全向性，全方位性 | The ability to operate or perceive in all directions. |  This microphone boasts omnidirectionality, making it ideal for recording group conversations.  这个麦克风拥有全向性，非常适合录制小组对话。

The security camera's omnidirectionality allows it to monitor the entire room.  这个安全摄像头的全向性使其能够监控整个房间。| synonyms: isotropy, spherical symmetry | antonyms: directionality, unidirectionality | noun
