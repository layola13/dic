alethoscope: /ˌeɪlˈɛθəskəʊp/ | n. | 万花筒；千变万化的东西 | An instrument containing mirrors and pieces of colored glass, etc., whose reflections produce changing patterns when the instrument is rotated.  A constantly changing pattern or sequence of objects or elements. | Example 1:  She peered through the alethoscope, mesmerized by the kaleidoscopic patterns. 她透过万花筒，被千变万化的图案迷住了。

Example 2: The city's nightlife is an alethoscope of vibrant music, dazzling lights, and diverse crowds.  这座城市的夜生活就像一个万花筒，充满活力四射的音乐、耀眼的灯光和形形色色的人群。 | Synonym: kaleidoscope | Antonym: monotony | Noun
