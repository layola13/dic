nonvertebrate:/nɒnˈvɜːtɪbrət/ /nɑːnˈvɜːrtəbrət/
|adj. n.|无脊椎动物的；无脊椎动物|An animal without a backbone. |例句：Sponges are nonvertebrate animals. 海绵是无脊椎动物。|近义词：invertebrate|反义词:vertebrate|词性:形容词, 名词
Etymology: non- + vertebrate
From Latin vertebratus, past participle of vertebrare (“to turn”), from vertebra (“joint, spinal joint”).  Non- is a prefix meaning "not". Thus, nonvertebrate means "not having vertebrae".
