dweeb:/dwiːb/| n. |书呆子，怪胎|A person who is perceived as socially awkward, unfashionable, or overly intellectual.  Someone who is excessively enthusiastic about a particular hobby or intellectual pursuit, often to the exclusion of other interests. |例句：He was such a dweeb in high school, always with his nose in a book. 他高中时就是个书呆子，总是埋头读书。

例句：Those computer dweebs spend all their time playing video games. 那些电脑怪胎整天都在玩电子游戏。|近义词：nerd, geek, wonk|反义词：cool kid, popular person|词性: Noun
