extrastapedial: /ˌekstrəstəˈpiːdiəl/
adjective
镫骨外的; 位于镫骨外的
Located or occurring outside the stapes (a small stirrup-shaped bone in the middle ear).
The extrastapedial portion of the facial nerve is susceptible to damage during ear surgery.  面神经的镫骨外部分在耳部手术中容易受损。
The infection spread to the extrastapedial spaces. 感染扩散到镫骨外间隙。
近义词: extratympanic
反义词: intrastapedial (if such a word exists - it's not commonly used)
词性: adjective
