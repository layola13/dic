adlocution:/ˌædloʊˈkjuːʃən/
noun
演讲; 说话方式; 措辞
An instance of formal speaking, a speech or address.  A manner of speaking; phrasing.
The president's adlocution was brief and to the point. 总统的讲话简短扼要。

The lawyer's adlocution was clear and persuasive.  律师的措辞清晰而有说服力。


近义词:speech, address, oration, discourse, pronouncements
反义词:silence, taciturnity


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin adlocutio (“address, speaking to”), from adloqui (“to speak to, address”), from ad- (“to”) + loqui (“to speak”).
