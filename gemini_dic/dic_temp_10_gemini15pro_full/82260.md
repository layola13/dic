groundhog:/ˈɡraʊndˌhɑːɡ/ (US), /ˈɡraʊndhɒɡ/ (UK)| n. |土拨鼠|A rodent of the squirrel family, Marmota monax, found in North America, known for its hibernation and its association with Groundhog Day.|例句：The groundhog saw its shadow, predicting six more weeks of winter. 土拨鼠看到了自己的影子，预示着还有六个星期的冬天。

例句：Groundhogs are known for digging burrows. 土拨鼠以挖洞而闻名。|近义词：woodchuck, marmot|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
