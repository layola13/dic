iffier:/ˈɪfiər/
adj.
更不确定的，更可疑的
more uncertain or doubtful; more questionable.
The weather forecast is iffier than yesterday, so we might have to cancel the picnic. 天气预报比昨天更不确定，所以我们可能不得不取消野餐。
近义词：more uncertain, more doubtful, riskier, more problematic
反义词：more certain, more definite, safer, more reliable
词性: 形容词

iffier:/ˈɪfiər/
词源:  Comparative form of iffy (informal adjective meaning uncertain or doubtful), which is likely a playful alteration of "if."
中文翻译: 更不确定的，更可疑的
英文解析: more uncertain or doubtful; more questionable.
例句：The situation is much iffier now than it was last week.  现在的情况比上周更加不确定。
例句：Investing in that company seems iffier now that the market is so volatile.  既然市场如此波动，投资那家公司现在看来更加冒险。
近义词：dicier, riskier, more uncertain, more doubtful
反义词：safer, more certain, more definite, more secure
词性: 形容词
