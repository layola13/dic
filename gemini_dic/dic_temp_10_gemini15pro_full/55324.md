dissolve:/dɪˈzɒlv/ (UK), /dɪˈzɑːlv/ (US)| v. |溶解；解散；消失；消除|To become incorporated into a liquid so as to form a solution; to bring to an end; to cause to disappear.|The sugar dissolved in the water. 糖溶解在水中。|The parliament was dissolved. 议会解散了。|synonyms: melt, liquefy, disintegrate, disperse, terminate|antonyms: solidify, freeze, congeal, assemble, establish|verb
