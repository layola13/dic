imperishable:/ɪmˈpɛrɪʃəbl/| adj.|不朽的，永恒的，不易腐烂的|Not subject to decay; enduring forever; not perishable.|The imperishable legacy of ancient Greece. 古希腊不朽的遗产。|近义词：eternal, everlasting, undying, indestructible|反义词：perishable, mortal, temporary|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French emperi(s)sable, from in- ‘not’ + perissable ‘perishable’ (from perir ‘perish’).
