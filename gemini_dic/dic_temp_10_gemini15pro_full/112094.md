longitude: /ˈlɒndʒɪtjuːd/ (UK) /ˈlɑːndʒɪtuːd/ (US) | n. | 经度 | The angular distance of a place east or west of the Greenwich meridian, or west of the standard meridian of a celestial object, usually expressed in degrees and minutes. |  The longitude of Beijing is approximately 116 degrees east. 北京的经度大约是东经116度。| synonyms: meridian | antonyms: latitude | noun
