almoravids:/ælˈmɒrəvɪdz/
| n. | 阿尔摩拉维德王朝 |  A Berber Muslim dynasty that ruled Morocco and parts of Spain and Portugal in the 11th and 12th centuries. | The Almoravids unified Morocco and extended their power into al-Andalus. 阿尔摩拉维德王朝统一了摩洛哥，并将他们的势力扩展到安达卢斯。| synonym: dynasty, empire | antonym: anarchy, disorder | noun
