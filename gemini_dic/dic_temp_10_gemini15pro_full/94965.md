in-step's:/ɪnˈstɛps/ |adj.|步伐一致的；协调的；同步的|in agreement or harmony with someone or something else.|例句：The dancers moved in-step with the music. 舞者们随着音乐的节奏翩翩起舞。

例句：The government's policy is in-step with public opinion. 政府的政策与公众舆论一致。|近义词：harmonious, coordinated, synchronized, in sync, in agreement, aligned|反义词：out-of-step, discordant, uncoordinated, asynchronous, in disagreement, misaligned|词性: 形容词


词源:  "in" + "step". "in"表示“在...之内”，“step”表示“步伐”。 合在一起表示“步伐一致”。
