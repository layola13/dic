decks:/deks/| n.| 甲板，层面；一副（纸牌）；录音卡座；磁带 |A flat surface that forms the main outside floor of a ship or boat; a level of a building; a pack of playing cards; a cassette player.| The ship has three decks. 这艘船有三层甲板。| synonyms: floors, levels, stories, tiers, platforms, stages, terraces, balconies, porches, verandas, patios, rooftops; pack, set; tape deck, cassette player | antonyms: hold (of a ship) | noun

decks:/deks/| v.| 装饰，打扮；击倒，打倒（deck someone）| To decorate; to knock someone down. |  They decked the halls with boughs of holly. 他们用冬青树枝装饰大厅。 He decked his opponent with a single punch. 他一拳就把对手打倒了。| synonyms: adorn, decorate, embellish, ornament, garnish, trim, beautify, enhance, grace; knock down, knock out, floor, flatten, fell, prostrate, level | antonyms: strip, denude; revive | verb
