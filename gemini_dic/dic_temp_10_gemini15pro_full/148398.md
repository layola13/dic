overstarch: /ˌoʊvərˈstɑːrtʃ/ | v. | 过浆；浆硬；给…上过多的浆 | To starch (clothing) excessively, making it stiff. |  She overstarched his shirts so much they could stand up on their own. 她把他的衬衫浆得太硬了，它们都可以自己立起来了。| The collar was so overstarched it felt like cardboard. 领子浆得太硬了，感觉像硬纸板一样。 | overstiffen | understiffen | verb
