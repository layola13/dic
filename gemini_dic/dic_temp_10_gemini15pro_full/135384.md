nonperiodicals:/ˌnɒnˌpɪəriˈɒdɪkəlz/ | n. | 非期刊出版物 | Publications that are not issued on a regular schedule, such as books, reports, and pamphlets. | The library has a vast collection of nonperiodicals, including rare manuscripts and first editions. 图书馆收藏了大量的非期刊出版物，包括珍稀手稿和初版书籍。| Synonyms: non-serials, monographs | Antonyms: periodicals, serials | Noun
