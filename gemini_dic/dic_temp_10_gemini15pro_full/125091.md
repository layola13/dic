mulligatawny:/ˌmʌlɪɡəˈtɔːni/
n.
Mulligatawny soup; Mulligatawny
一种用肉和蔬菜做的辣汤
This restaurant is famous for its mulligatawny. 这家餐厅以其肉汤而闻名。
Synonyms: soup, stew, broth
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific type of soup)


Etymology:
From Tamil  *miḷaku taṇṇīr* (மிளகு தண்ணீர்) "pepper water," from *miḷaku* (மிளகு) "pepper" + *taṇṇīr* (தண்ணீர்) "water."  Borrowed into English during the British Raj in India.
