helloing:/həˈləʊɪŋ/ (British) /həˈloʊɪŋ/ (American)|  informal, nonstandard | 打招呼，问候 (进行时态，非正式，不标准) | Expressing the act of saying hello.  This form is considered non-standard and is rarely used in formal writing. It is more common in casual spoken English, often used humorously or playfully. | Example: "I was helloing to everyone as I walked into the room."  我走进房间时跟每个人打招呼。 | 近义词: greeting, hailing | 反义词: ignoring, snubbing | Verb (present participle)
