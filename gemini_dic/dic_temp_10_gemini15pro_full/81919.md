grilled:/ɡrɪld/| adj.|烤的，烧烤的|Cooked over direct heat, usually on a grill.|例句：We had grilled chicken for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了烤鸡。|近义词：broiled, barbecued, roasted|反义词:boiled, steamed, fried|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the Old French word "griller," meaning "to burn," which ultimately comes from the Vulgar Latin "craticulare," meaning "to roast on a gridiron."
