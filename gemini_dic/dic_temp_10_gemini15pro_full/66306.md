excrementitiousness:/ˌɛkskrɪˈmɛnˈtɪʃəsnɪs/
n.
排泄物性质; 粪便状态
the quality of being excrementitious; consisting of or resembling excrement.
The excrementitiousness of the pigeon droppings on the statue was appalling.  鸽子粪便在雕像上的排泄物性质令人震惊。
近义词：feculence, filthiness
反义词：cleanliness, purity
词性：noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *excrementitius, from Latin excrementum (“excrement”) + -itious.
