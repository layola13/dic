eery:/ˈɪəri/ (US), /ˈɪəri/ (UK)| adj.|怪诞的，可怕的，令人毛骨悚然的|Strange and frightening, causing a feeling of mystery and fear.|The empty house had an eery silence. 空荡荡的房子里有一种怪诞的寂静。|近义词:creepy, spooky, unsettling, eerie|反义词:comforting, reassuring|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *ēri, from Old English *ēge, meaning "awe, fear, terror."  The modern spelling emerged in the 18th century.
