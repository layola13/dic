nonpermanence:/ˌnɒnˈpɜːmənəns/ | n. | 非永久性，无常 | The state or quality of not being permanent; impermanence. |  The Buddhist concept of nonpermanence emphasizes that everything is in constant flux. 佛教的无常观念强调一切都在不断变化。

The nonpermanence of online content makes archiving a challenge.  网络内容的非永久性使得存档成为一项挑战。| 近义词: impermanence, transience, ephemerality | 反义词: permanence, eternity, perpetuity | 词性: 名词

Etymology: non- + permanence.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "permanence" comes from the Late Latin "permanentia," meaning "a remaining, abiding, continuance."
