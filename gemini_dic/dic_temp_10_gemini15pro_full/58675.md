dummy:/ˈdʌmi/| adj. n.|假的，虚拟的；仿制品；笨蛋|A dummy is something that is not real but is made to look or act like something real.  A dummy can also refer to a stupid person.|例句：The store uses dummy cameras for security.  这家商店使用假摄像头来防盗。|近义词：fake, imitation, counterfeit, sham, mock, ersatz, phony, bogus, pseudo, simulated, artificial, synthetic, false, unreal, fictitious, pretend, feigned, hollow, insubstantial, nominal, figurehead, stooge, puppet, pawn, dupe, chump, simpleton, dolt, idiot, fool|反义词:real, genuine, authentic, intelligent, bright, smart|词性:形容词,名词

dummy:/ˈdʌmi/| n.|蠢人，笨蛋|A dummy is an informal term for a stupid person.|例句：He felt like a dummy for forgetting his keys. 他因为忘了带钥匙而觉得自己像个笨蛋。|近义词：idiot, fool, simpleton, dolt|反义词:genius, intellectual|词性:名词
