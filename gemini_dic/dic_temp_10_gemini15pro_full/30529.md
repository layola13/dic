cephalism:/ˈsɛfəlɪzəm/| n.| 头化，头部集中化|The concentration of sense organs, nervous control, etc., at the anterior end of the body, forming a head and brain, both during evolution and in the course of an embryo's development.  指感觉器官、神经控制等集中在身体的前端，形成头部和大脑，这在进化过程中和胚胎发育过程中都会发生。|Cephalism is a characteristic feature of vertebrates. 头化是脊椎动物的一个特征。|Cephalization| Decentralization| noun
