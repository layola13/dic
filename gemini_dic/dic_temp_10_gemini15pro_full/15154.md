back-heel:/ˌbækˈhiːl/| n. v.|后脚跟，用后脚跟着球|A back-heel is a way of kicking a ball using the back of your heel.  As a verb, it means to kick a ball in this way.|He scored with a brilliant back-heel. 他用一个精彩的后脚跟射门得分。

He back-heeled the ball to his teammate. 他用后脚跟着球传给了队友。|近义词：heel kick|反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps toe kick)|名词, 动词
