clickstreams:/ˈklɪkˌstriːmz/
noun
点击流
A series of mouse clicks made while using a Web site or other software, recorded and analyzed to track user behavior.
The company analyzes clickstreams to understand customer preferences. 公司分析点击流以了解客户偏好。
web analytics, site traffic, user activity
