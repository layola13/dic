conservant:/kənˈsɜːrvənt/| n. adj.|防腐剂，保存的|A substance used to preserve food or other materials from decay or decomposition.  A substance that prevents something, especially food, from decaying.|例句：This jam contains no artificial conservants.  这种果酱不含人工防腐剂。|近义词：preservative, additive|反义词:decomposer|词性:名词, 形容词

conservant:/kənˈsɜːrvənt/| adj.|保存的，有保存力的|Having the power of preserving; preservative.  Tending or having power to preserve; protecting from loss, decay, or injury.|例句：The museum has a conservant environment for its artifacts.  博物馆为其文物创造了一个有利于保存的环境。|近义词：protective, preservative|反义词:destructive|词性:形容词
