midterms:/ˈmɪdtɜːrmz/ (US) /ˈmɪdtɜːmz/ (UK)
n.|期中考试|Examinations administered in the middle of an academic term, typically a semester or quarter.  These exams often cover the material taught in the first half of the course.|例句：The midterms are next week and I'm not ready.  下周就要期中考试了，我还没准备好。|近义词：mid-term exams, mid-semester exams|反义词:finals, final exams
词性:名词
