overlooking:/ˌoʊvərˈlʊkɪŋ/| adj. |俯瞰的；忽略的；宽容的|Having a view of from above; failing to notice or consider; forgiving or tolerant.|例句：We booked a room overlooking the sea. 我们预订了一个可以俯瞰大海的房间。|近义词：ignoring, neglecting, forgiving, excusing, condoning|反义词：attentive to, noticing, meticulous, unforgiving, intolerant|词性:形容词

overlooking:/ˌoʊvərˈlʊkɪŋ/| prep.|俯瞰；忽略|From a position above; disregarding or failing to notice.|例句：The castle is situated overlooking the valley. 这座城堡坐落在俯瞰山谷的位置。|近义词：above, ignoring, disregarding|反义词：below, noticing, regarding|词性:介词


词源：From the verb "overlook," a combination of "over" and "look."  "Over" in this context means "from above" or "across," while "look" retains its basic meaning of directing one's gaze. The sense of "failing to notice" developed figuratively from the idea of looking over something without seeing it.
