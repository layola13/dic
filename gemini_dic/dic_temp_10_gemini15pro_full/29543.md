catchable:/ˈkætʃəbəl/| adj.|可以捕获的，可捉到的|Able to be caught.  |例句：The fish in this lake are very catchable. 这个湖里的鱼很容易钓到。|近义词：trappable, attainable, obtainable|反义词:uncatchable, elusive|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From "catch" + "-able". "Catch" comes from the Old North French "cachier" (to chase, seize). "-able" is a suffix of Latin origin, forming adjectives meaning "capable of being," "liable to be," or "worthy of being."
