miura:/ˈmjʊərə/ | n. | 三浦折叠，三浦折纸法 | A method of origami folding invented by Koryo Miura, notable for its rigid but foldable structure used in maps and solar panels. |  Miura-ori is used to fold maps efficiently. 三浦折叠被用于高效地折叠地图。

The Miura fold allows solar panels to be compactly stored and easily deployed. 三浦折叠使得太阳能板可以紧凑地存储并轻松展开。| 近义词: origami fold (泛指), tessellation (密铺, 类似的几何结构) | 反义词:  (无直接反义词) | 词性: noun
