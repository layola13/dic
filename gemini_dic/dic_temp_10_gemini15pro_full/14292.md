autokinetic:/ˌɔːtəʊkɪˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
自动运动的
Relating to or characterized by autokinesis, the apparent movement of a small stationary point of light in the dark.
The autokinetic effect can make a single, unmoving light appear to drift.
近义词:apparent motion, illusory motion
反义词:stationary, static
词性:adjective

词源: From Greek "auto-" (self) + "kinetikos" (moving).
