antiseparatists:/ˌæntiˈsɛpərətɪsts/ | n. | 反分裂主义者；反对分裂的人 |  People who oppose separatism, the advocacy of or attempt to achieve political separation from a larger group or nation. | The government took strong measures against the antiseparatists. 政府对反分裂主义者采取了强硬措施。 | The antiseparatists rallied in the capital to show their support for national unity. 反分裂主义者在首都集会，表达他们对国家统一的支持。 | 近义词: unionists, loyalists | 反义词: separatists, secessionists | 词性: 名词

词源:

anti- (prefix): against, opposed to  来自希腊语anti-, 意为"反对"
separatist (noun): a person who supports separatism 来自拉丁语separatus, 过去分词separare "to separate"
