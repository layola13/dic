in turn:/ɪn ˈtɜːrn/| adv.|依次，轮流，反过来，相应地|One by one, in order; as a result of something else.|例句：Each child in turn told the teacher what they had done during the summer vacation. 每个孩子轮流告诉老师他们在暑假期间做了什么。

例句：The stress of his job led to insomnia, which in turn affected his performance.  他工作的压力导致失眠，这反过来又影响了他的工作表现。|近义词：alternately, consecutively, consequently, subsequently|反义词：simultaneously, concurrently|词性:副词
