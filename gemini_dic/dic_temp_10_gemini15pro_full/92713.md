icequake:/ˈaɪskweɪk/| n. |冰震|A sudden, violent cracking or shaking of ice, often caused by thermal stress or movement of glaciers or ice sheets.|The icequake was so loud, it sounded like an explosion. 冰震的声音很大，听起来像爆炸。|The researchers recorded several icequakes during their expedition. 研究人员在探险期间记录了几次冰震。|近义词：cryoseism, frost quake|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially thaw)|词性:noun
