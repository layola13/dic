disinterment:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtɜːrmənt/
n.|掘尸；发掘|The disinterment of the body was ordered by the court. 法院下令掘尸。|例句：The disinterment of the king's remains caused a national controversy.  国王遗体的发掘引起了全国性的争议。

例句：The disinterment of the ancient artifacts provided valuable insights into the civilization.  古代文物的发掘为了解该文明提供了宝贵的见解。


近义词：exhumation, unearthing|
反义词：burial, interment|
词性：noun|

词源：From dis- "not, opposite of" + inter "bury" + -ment (suffix forming nouns of action or result). Ultimately from Latin dis- + in- + terra "earth."
