benefitting:/ˈbɛnɪfɪtɪŋ/
verb
受益，得益
Present participle of benefit. Receiving an advantage; being helped in some way.
The new policy is benefitting low-income families.  这项新政策使低收入家庭受益。
Many students are benefitting from the new library. 许多学生正从新图书馆中受益。
Synonyms: profiting, gaining, improving
Antonyms: harming, hindering, damaging
词性: 动词 (present participle)
