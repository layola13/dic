anti-natural:/ˌæntiˈnætʃrəl/
adjective
违反自然的；反自然的
Going against the natural order or what is considered normal in nature.
例句：
Genetic engineering is seen by some as anti-natural.
一些人认为基因工程是违反自然的。

Their anti-natural behavior disturbed the whole community.
他们违反自然的举动扰乱了整个社区。

近义词：unnatural, artificial, contrived
反义词：natural, normal, organic
词性：adjective
