forefather:/ˈfɔːrˌfɑːðər/ (US) /ˈfɔːˌfɑːðə(r)/ (UK)| n.|祖先，先辈|A person from whom one is descended, especially one who lived a long time ago; an ancestor.|Our forefathers fought for the freedom we enjoy today. 我们的祖先为我们今天享有的自由而战。|
synonyms: ancestor, progenitor, forebear|
antonyms: descendant, offspring|
