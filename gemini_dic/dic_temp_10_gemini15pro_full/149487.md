padre:/ˈpɑːdreɪ/ (US), /ˈpɑːdri/ (UK)| n.|神父，牧师，(尤指西班牙语国家或地区的)教士，(幽默或尊称)老兄，老爸|A priest, especially in a Spanish-speaking country or region. Also used informally or humorously to address a man, especially an older one.|例句：The padre gave a moving sermon. 神父做了一场感人的布道。

例句：Hey, padre, how's it going?  嘿，老兄，最近怎么样？|近义词：priest, minister, chaplain, father, parson|反义词:None (no direct opposite for a religious title)|词性:noun
