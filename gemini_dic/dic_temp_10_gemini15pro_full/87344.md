heroic:/hɪˈroʊɪk/ (US) /hɪˈrəʊɪk/ (UK)| adj.|英雄的；英勇的；崇高的|Very brave; showing great courage; exhibiting the qualities of a hero.  Relating to or resembling a hero in quality or character.|例句：He made a heroic attempt to rescue the drowning child. 他英勇地试图营救溺水的孩子。|例句：Her heroic deeds were celebrated throughout the land. 她的英雄事迹举国称颂。|近义词：brave, courageous, valiant, daring, bold|反义词：cowardly, timid, craven|词性:形容词
