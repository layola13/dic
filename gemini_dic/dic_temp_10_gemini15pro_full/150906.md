parallel-bar:/ˈpærəlel bɑːr/ | n. | 双杠 | Two horizontal bars fixed at equal height on upright supports, used for gymnastic exercises. | Gymnasts perform various exercises on the parallel bars.  体操运动员在双杠上进行各种练习。| synonyms: parallel bars, double bars | antonyms: N/A | noun

parallel-bar:/ˈpærəlel bɑːr/ | adj. | 平行的 | Extending in the same direction and always the same distance apart; having corresponding similarities. | The parallel-bar structure provides stability. 双杠结构提供了稳定性。 | synonyms: parallel, aligned, equidistant | antonyms: nonparallel, diverging, converging | adjective
