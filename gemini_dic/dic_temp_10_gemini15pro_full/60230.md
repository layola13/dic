effortlessness:/ˈɛfətləsnəs/| n.|轻松，毫不费力|The quality of doing something easily and without apparent effort.  It implies a natural grace or skill.|She danced with an effortless grace. 她跳舞轻松优雅。|synonyms: ease, facility, naturalness, fluency, smoothness|antonyms: awkwardness, clumsiness, difficulty, struggle, strain|noun
