cosied:/ˈkoʊ.zid/ (US), /ˈkəʊ.zid/ (UK)| v.|舒适地依偎；舒适地安顿|To make oneself or someone else warm and comfortable, often in a small space.| We cosied up by the fire on a cold winter night. 我们在一个寒冷的冬夜依偎在火炉旁。|  The children cosied down in their beds with their favorite books. 孩子们拿着他们最喜欢的书舒适地躺在床上。|近义词: snuggled, cuddled, nestled|反义词: separated, distanced|词性: verb (past tense and past participle of "cosy")

cosy:/ˈkoʊ.zi/ (US), /ˈkəʊ.zi/ (UK)| adj.|舒适的；温暖的|Giving a feeling of warmth, comfort and relaxation.| The room was cosy and inviting. 房间很舒适宜人。|  It's a cosy little cottage in the countryside. 这是一个舒适的乡村小屋。|近义词: comfortable, snug, warm|反义词: uncomfortable, cold, drafty|词性: adjective
