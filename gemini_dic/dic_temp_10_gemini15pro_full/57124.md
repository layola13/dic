dove:/dʌv/| n. |鸽子；和平鸽|A bird with a stout body and short legs, known for its soft cooing. It is often a symbol of peace.|例句：The dove landed gently on the branch. 鸽子轻轻地落在树枝上。

例句：He released a white dove as a symbol of peace. 他放飞了一只白鸽作为和平的象征。|近义词：pigeon|反义词:hawk|词性:noun

dove:/doʊv/| v. |(dive的过去式) 跳水，俯冲|Past tense of "dive," meaning to plunge headfirst into water or to descend steeply and rapidly.|例句：She dove into the pool. 她跳进了游泳池。

例句：The plane dove sharply to avoid the missile. 飞机急剧俯冲以避开导弹。|近义词：plunged, dived|反义词:ascended, surfaced|词性:verb
