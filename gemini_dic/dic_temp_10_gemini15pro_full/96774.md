inequitable:/ɪnˈɛkwɪtəbəl/
adjective
不公平的，不公正的
not fair; unjust
例句：The current healthcare system is inequitable, leaving many without access to affordable care.  当前的医疗保健体系不公平，导致许多人无法获得负担得起的医疗服务。

例句：The inequitable distribution of wealth is a major problem in many societies.  财富分配不均是许多社会面临的一个主要问题。
近义词：unfair, unjust, biased, discriminatory, prejudiced
反义词：fair, just, equitable, impartial
词性: adjective

词源:  Late 16th century: from in- ‘not’ + equitable.
