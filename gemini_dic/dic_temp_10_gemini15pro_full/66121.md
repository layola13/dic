excentral:/ɛkˈsɛntrəl/
adjective
外心的，离心的
Situated or occurring outside a center, especially a nerve center.  Relating to or proceeding away from a center.
The excentral position of the nucleus is a characteristic feature of this cell type.  细胞核的离心位置是这种细胞类型的特征。
Peripheral, outer, exterior
Central, interior, inner
