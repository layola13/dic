odiums:/ˈoʊdiəmz/
noun
憎恨，仇恨
intense hatred or dislike directed toward someone or something, usually as a result of their actions or qualities.
The dictator incurred the odiums of the people for his tyrannical rule.  独裁者因其暴虐的统治而招致人民的憎恨。
The politician resigned in a storm of public odiums.  这位政客在公众的强烈谴责中辞职了。
Synonyms: hatred, loathing, detestation, abhorrence, animosity, antipathy, aversion
Antonyms: love, admiration, respect, approval
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *odium* (hatred, aversion).
