computers:/kəmˈpjuːtərz/ (US) /kəmˈpjuːtəz/ (UK)| n.|计算机，电脑|Electronic devices that process information according to instructions given to them in the form of programs.|Example: Most offices now use computers to store and process information. 现在大多数办公室都使用电脑来存储和处理信息。|Synonym: machines, processors|Antonym: abacus, calculator|noun
