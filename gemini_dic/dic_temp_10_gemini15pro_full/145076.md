overanimated: /ˌoʊvərˈænɪmeɪtɪd/
adjective
过度活跃的，过于兴奋的
Excessively animated; showing too much excitement or enthusiasm in one's gestures, speech, or behavior.
Her overanimated performance felt forced and unnatural. 她过于活跃的表演感觉很做作，不自然。
The children were overanimated after eating too much sugar. 孩子们吃了太多糖后变得过于兴奋。
近义词: excitable, hyperactive, overexcited, boisterous
反义词: calm, subdued, quiet, restrained
词性: adjective
