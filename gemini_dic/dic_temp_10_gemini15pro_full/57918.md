drillers:/ˈdrɪlərz/| n.|钻工，训练者，钻床|People who operate drills or conduct training exercises. |The drillers worked tirelessly to reach the oil deposit. 钻工们不知疲倦地工作以到达油矿。 The army drillers prepared the recruits for combat. 军队训练者让新兵们为战斗做好准备。|近义词：operators, trainers, instructors|反义词:trainees, students|词性:名词

词源: From the verb "drill" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Drill" comes from Middle Dutch "drillen" meaning "to bore, to turn."
