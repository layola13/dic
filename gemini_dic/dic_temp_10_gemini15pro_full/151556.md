/pɑːrt/ |/pɑːrt/| n. |部分，零件，角色|A piece or segment of something larger; a component.  A role or character played by an actor. |例句：This is a part of the machine. 这是机器的一部分。

|例句：She played the part of Juliet. 她扮演了朱丽叶的角色。|近义词：piece, component, section, portion, role, character|反义词:whole, entirety| noun


/pɑːrt/ |/pɑːrt/| v. |分开，分离|To divide or separate something into parts. To leave or go away from someone or something.|例句：The curtain parted and the show began. 幕布拉开，演出开始了。

|例句：We parted ways at the crossroads. 我们在十字路口分道扬镳。|近义词：separate, divide, split, leave, depart|反义词:join, unite| verb


/pɑːrt/ |/pɑːrt/| adv. |部分地|To some extent; partly.|例句：The failure was due in part to his negligence. 失败部分原因是他的疏忽。

|例句：I agree with you, in part. 我部分同意你的观点。|近义词：partly, partially|反义词:wholly, completely| adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *part*, from Old French *part* (Modern French *part*), from Latin *pars* (genitive *partis*), meaning "part, share, portion".
