evinceable:/ɪˈvɪnsəbl/
adj.
可证实的，可表明的
capable of being evinced or shown clearly; demonstrable
His guilt was evinceable by the clear evidence presented in court.  他的罪行可以通过法庭上提出的明确证据得到证实。
The student's understanding of the topic was evinceable through her insightful essay.  这个学生对主题的理解可以通过她富有洞察力的文章得到证明。
近义词: demonstrable, provable, attestable, verifiable
反义词: unprovable, indemonstrable, questionable
