monosystemic:/ˌmɑːnəʊsɪˈstɪmɪk/ | adj. | 单一系统的；单系统性的 |  Relating to or characterized by a single system, especially a single political or economic system.  It often implies a lack of diversity or interconnectedness with other systems. |  The economist argued that the country's monosystemic approach to trade was ultimately unsustainable. 经济学家认为，该国单一系统的贸易方式最终是不可持续的。

The report criticized the monosystemic nature of the education system, advocating for more diverse approaches. 该报告批评了教育系统的单一系统性，主张采取更多样化的方式。 | 近义词：unified, homogenous, singular | 反义词：diverse, multisystemic, heterogeneous | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "mono-" (Greek: one, single) + "systemic" (from "system")
