man-up:/ˌmæn ˈʌp/ | phrasal verb | 拿出男子气概；振作起来；勇敢面对 | To behave in a traditionally masculine way, often involving courage, resilience, or self-control, especially when facing a difficult situation. | Example: "He needs to man up and take responsibility for his actions." 他需要拿出男子气概，为自己的行为负责。

man-up:/ˌmæn ˈʌp/ | n. | (俚语)  拿出男子气概的行为; 勇敢面对的行为 |  The act of behaving with courage and resilience.  | Example: "That was a real man-up moment for him." 那对他来说是一个真正展现男子气概的时刻。
