disarray's:/dɪsəˈreɪz/
noun
混乱的状态；紊乱；无秩序
The state of being disordered and disorganized; confusion.
The room was in complete disarray after the party. 房间在聚会后一片狼藉。
disorder, chaos, confusion, mess, muddle
order, organization, tidiness, neatness
词性: 名词所有格

词源: From Old French *desareiier, from des- (expressing reversal) + areiier "to array," from a- (from Latin ad-) + rei "row, line," of Germanic origin.
