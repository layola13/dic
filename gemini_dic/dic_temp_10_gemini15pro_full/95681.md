inconclusive:/ˌɪnkənˈkluːsɪv/ | adj. | 不确定的；无结果的；无定论的 | Not leading to a firm conclusion; not ending doubt or dispute. | The evidence was inconclusive, so the jury could not reach a verdict.  证据不确定，所以陪审团无法作出裁决。| inconclusive results =  无定论的结果 | synonyms: indecisive, indeterminate, ambiguous, equivocal | antonyms: conclusive, decisive, definite, certain | adjective
