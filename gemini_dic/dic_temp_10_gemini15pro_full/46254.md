cytologists:/saɪˈtɒlədʒɪsts/
n.
细胞学家
Scientists who study cells, their structure, function, and pathology.
Cytologists examine cells under microscopes to diagnose diseases like cancer.  细胞学家在显微镜下检查细胞以诊断癌症等疾病。
