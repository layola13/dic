nonsubstantialnesses:/ˌnɒnsəbˈstænʃəlɪsnəsɪz/
|n.|非实质性；无实体；不重要|The nonsubstantialnesses of his arguments became clear during the debate. 他的论点缺乏实质内容在辩论中变得很明显。|
|英文解析: The state or quality of being nonsubstantial; lack of substance, solidity, or importance.  It is the plural form of "nonsubstantiality."|
|例句：The nonsubstantialnesses of the rumors were quickly debunked. 这些谣言的虚假性很快就被揭穿了。|
|例句：He dismissed the nonsubstantialnesses of the objections raised by the opposition. 他驳回了反对派提出的无关紧要的反对意见。|
|近义词：insignificance, immateriality, unimportance, triviality|
|反义词：substantiality, importance, significance, materiality|
|词性: noun|
