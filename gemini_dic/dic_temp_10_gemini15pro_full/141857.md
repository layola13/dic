orchestrator:/ˈɔːrkɪstreɪtər/
n.|协调者，策划者，编曲者|A person who organizes or coordinates something, especially something complex.  The person who arranges or adapts a musical composition, especially for an orchestra.|The CEO was the orchestrator of the company's successful turnaround. 首席执行官是公司成功转型的主导者。

The orchestrator adapted the symphony for a smaller ensemble.  这位编曲家将交响曲改编成了适合小型乐团演奏的版本。|organizer, coordinator, planner, arranger, composer|manipulator, saboteur|noun
