bilk:/bɪlk/
verb
欺骗；诈骗
To cheat or defraud someone, especially of money.
The con man bilked several elderly people out of their savings. 那个骗子骗走了几个老人的积蓄。
近义词:cheat, defraud, swindle, con
反义词:repay, compensate
词性:v.
词源:Probably from bilk (n.), "nothing, a deception," from obsolete Dutch bilken "to polish," of uncertain origin.

bilk:/bɪlk/
noun
诈骗；骗局
An act of cheating or defrauding someone.
The investment scheme turned out to be a bilk. 这项投资计划结果是个骗局。
近义词:fraud, swindle, scam, con
反义词:legitimate business, honest deal
词性:n.
