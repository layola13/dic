illumined:/ɪˈluːmɪnd/ |v. |照亮的，启发的| lit up, brightened, made clear |The stained-glass windows illumined the church with a kaleidoscope of colors. 彩色玻璃窗用万花筒般的色彩照亮了教堂。| 近义词: illuminated, lit, brightened, enlightened | 反义词: darkened, dimmed, obscured |词性: 动词(过去分词形式，用作形容词)

illumined:/ɪˈluːmɪnd/ |adj. |照亮的，启发的| lit up, brightened, made clear |His face was illumined with joy. 他的脸上洋溢着喜悦。| 近义词: radiant, glowing, luminous | 反义词: dark, dull, gloomy |词性: 形容词


词源:  From Late Middle English *illumynen, from Latin illumināre (“to light up, enlighten”), from in- (“in, on”) + lūmināre (“to light”), from lūmen (“light”).
