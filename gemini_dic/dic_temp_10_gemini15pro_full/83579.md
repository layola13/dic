haematite:/ˈhiːmətaɪt/ (US) /ˈhemətaɪt/ (UK)| n. |赤铁矿，氧化铁|A common iron ore, Fe2O3, that is the main source of iron. It is typically black or reddish-brown in color.|The haematite deposits in this region are extensive. 这个地区的赤铁矿储量非常丰富。|Synonym: iron ore, red ochre|Antonym: N/A|Noun

haematite:/ˈhiːmətaɪt/ (US) /ˈhemətaɪt/ (UK)| n. |血石|A dark red gemstone variety of haematite.|She wore a necklace made of polished haematite. 她戴着一条由抛光血石制成的项链。|Synonym: bloodstone|Antonym: N/A|Noun
