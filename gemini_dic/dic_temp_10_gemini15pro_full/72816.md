foldless:/ˈfoʊldləs/
adjective
无褶的；不折叠的
Without folds or creases.  Lacking the ability or designed not to be folded.
The artist created a foldless sculpture from a single sheet of metal. 艺术家从一块金属板上创作了一个无褶的雕塑。
The new phone boasts a foldless screen, unlike its competitor's foldable design.  这款新手机拥有一个无褶的屏幕，与其竞争对手的可折叠设计不同。
Near Synonyms: smooth, flat, uncreased, seamless
Antonyms: folded, creased, wrinkled, pleated
词性: 形容词
