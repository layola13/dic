electro-optical:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈɒptɪkəl/
adjective
电光的
Relating to or involving the interaction between light and electric fields or currents.
Electro-optical devices are used in a variety of applications, including telecommunications, data storage, and medical imaging.
Electro-optical modulators are key components in fiber optic communication systems. 电光调制器是光纤通信系统中的关键部件。
The company specializes in the development of advanced electro-optical sensors.  这家公司专门开发先进的电光传感器。
近义词:optoelectronic, photonic
反义词:None
词性:adjective
