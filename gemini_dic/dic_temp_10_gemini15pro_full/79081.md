glace:/ɡleɪs/ /ɡlɑːs/| adj. n.|光滑的，冰状的；糖衣，冰衣|A smooth, glossy surface, often of ice; a thin, sugary coating.|The lake was glace with a thin layer of ice. 湖面覆盖着一层薄薄的冰，光滑如镜。|She dipped the cherries in glace. 她把樱桃蘸上糖衣。|近义词：glazed, icy, smooth, glossy|反义词:rough, matte|词性:形容词, 名词
glace:/ɡleɪs/ /ɡlɑːs/|v.|给...上糖衣，使光滑|To coat with a glace, to make smooth and glossy.|The chef glaced the cake with a thin layer of icing. 厨师在蛋糕上涂了一层薄薄的糖衣。|He glaced the carrots before serving them. 他在上菜之前给胡萝卜上了糖衣。|近义词：glaze, polish|反义词:roughen|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French "glace," meaning "ice," from Latin "glacies." The culinary sense referring to a sugar coating developed later, based on the smooth, ice-like appearance.
