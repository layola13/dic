carinate:/ˈkærɪneɪt/ /ˈkærɪnət/
adjective
龙骨状的；具龙骨的
Having a keel-like ridge or projection.  Resembling the keel of a boat.
The carinate sternum of birds provides a large surface area for the attachment of flight muscles. 鸟类具龙骨的胸骨为飞行肌肉的附着提供了很大的表面积。
The leaf was carinate, with a prominent central ridge. 叶子呈龙骨状，有一条突出的中心脊。
近义词:keeled, ridged
反义词:smooth, flat
词性:adjective

词源: From Latin *carīnātus, past participle of carīnāre (“to provide with a keel”), from carīna (“keel”).
