dendritic:/dɛnˈdrɪtɪk/
adjective|树枝状的，枝晶状的|Resembling a tree in form; branching.  Characterized by branching filaments or crystals.|The neuron's dendritic spines receive signals from other neurons.|近义词：branching, arborescent, ramified|反义词:linear, straight|词性:adjective
词源:From Greek *dendritēs* (relating to a tree), from *dendron* (tree).
