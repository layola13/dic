loner:/ˈloʊnər/ (US), /ˈləʊnə(r)/ (UK)| n.|独来独往的人，不合群的人|A person who prefers to be alone or avoids the company of others.|He was always a loner, preferring books to people. 他总是独来独往，喜欢看书胜过与人交往。|

例句：She's a bit of a loner and doesn't really socialize with her colleagues. 她有点不合群，不太和同事们交往。

例句：He was happy being a loner and enjoyed his own company. 他乐于独处，享受一个人的时光。

近义词：solitary, introvert, recluse|
反义词：extrovert, social butterfly|
词性：noun|

词源：From lone + -er, influenced by alone.  源自 lone 加后缀 -er，受 alone 的影响。
