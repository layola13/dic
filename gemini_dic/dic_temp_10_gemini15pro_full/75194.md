frizzly:/ˈfrɪzli/| adj.|卷曲的，蓬松的|Characterized by or having small, tight curls or waves, often wiry and bushy.  (以细小、紧密的卷发或波浪为特征，通常是金属丝状且浓密的)|Her frizzly hair made her look like she had been electrocuted. 她的卷发让她看起来像是被电击了一样。|The poodle's frizzly coat needed a good brushing. 这只贵宾犬的卷毛需要好好梳理一下。|近义词：curly, kinky, wiry|反义词:straight, smooth|词性:形容词

词源:
From frizz + -ly.  Frizz itself likely comes from a blend of fray and crisp.
