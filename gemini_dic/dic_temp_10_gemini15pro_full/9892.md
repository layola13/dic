apathetically:/ˌæpəˈθɛtɪkli/| adv.|冷漠地，无动于衷地|Showing or feeling no enthusiasm, interest, or concern; indifferently.|He stared apathetically at the television screen. 他冷漠地盯着电视屏幕。|例句：The audience responded apathetically to the politician's speech. 观众对政治家的演讲反应冷淡。

例句：She apathetically shrugged her shoulders when she heard the news.  她听到这个消息后无动于衷地耸了耸肩。|近义词：indifferently, listlessly, passively, uncaringly|反义词：enthusiastically, eagerly, passionately|词性: 副词
