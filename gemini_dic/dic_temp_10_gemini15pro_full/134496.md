nonlegato:/nɒnleˈɡɑːtəʊ/ (UK), /nɑːnleˈɡɑːtoʊ/ (US)| adj. adv.|非连奏的，断音的|Not smooth or flowing; detached. Played or sung with each note separate and distinct, as opposed to legato.|The pianist played the passage nonlegato, giving each note a crisp, percussive quality. 钢琴家用非连奏的方式演奏了这段乐句，使每个音符都清晰而有冲击力。

The melody should be played nonlegato, with a slight separation between the notes.  这段旋律应该用非连奏的方式演奏，音符之间略有间隔。|近义词：staccato, detached, separated|反义词：legato, smooth, connected|词性:形容词/副词
