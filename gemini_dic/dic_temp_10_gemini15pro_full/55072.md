disregards:/ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrdz/
| v. (third-person singular simple present disregards, present participle disregarding, simple past and past participle disregarded) | 忽视，漠视，不理会 | to ignore something; to treat something as unimportant | He disregards all the advice he's given. 他无视所有给他的建议。 | Synonyms: ignores, neglects, overlooks | Antonyms: heeds, respects, acknowledges
| verb |

disregard:/ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrd/
| n. | 忽视，漠视，不理会 | lack of attention or respect |  She treated my warnings with complete disregard. 她完全无视我的警告。 | Synonyms: neglect, indifference, contempt | Antonyms: respect, heed, regard
| noun |
