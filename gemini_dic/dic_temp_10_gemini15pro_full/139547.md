obtrusive:/əbˈtruːsɪv/| adj.|冒失的；突兀的；显眼的|Noticeably intrusive or unwelcome.  Pushing oneself or one's ideas forward or noticeable in an unwelcome or intrusive way.|The advertisements were very obtrusive and spoiled the look of the town.  这些广告非常显眼，破坏了城镇的景观。|Synonyms: conspicuous, noticeable, prominent, intrusive, blatant, obvious|Antonyms: inconspicuous, subtle, discreet, unobtrusive|adjective


obtrusive:/əbˈtruːsɪv/| adj.|唐突的；鲁莽的|Forward in manner or conduct; pushy.|He was very obtrusive in offering his help. 他主动提出帮助，显得有些唐突。|Synonyms: pushy, forward, officious, meddlesome|Antonyms: reserved, retiring, diffident, unassuming|adjective
