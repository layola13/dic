bombards:/bɒmˈbɑːdz/ (UK), /bɑːmˈbɑːrdz/ (US)| v.|轰炸，炮击；大量提问；涌入|To attack something or someone continuously with bombs, shells, or other missiles; to assail someone with questions, criticisms, or information.|The enemy bombarded the city for days. 敌人连续几天轰炸了这座城市。|近义词：shell, attack, assail, pepper|反义词: defend, protect|词性:动词

bombards:/bɒmˈbɑːdz/ (UK), /bɑːmˈbɑːrdz/ (US)| n.|轰击，炮击|A continuous attack with bombs, shells, or other missiles.|The city was under heavy bombard. 这座城市遭到猛烈轰炸。|近义词：barrage, shelling, attack|反义词: defense, protection|词性:名词


词源: From French bombarder, from Italian bombardare, from bombarda 'bombard' (a type of cannon).
