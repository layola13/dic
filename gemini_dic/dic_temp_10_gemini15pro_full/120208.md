mineralogical:/ˌmɪnərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ (US) /ˌmɪnərəˈlɒdʒɪkl/ (UK)
adjective
矿物学的
Relating to the scientific study of minerals, their physical properties, chemical composition, and formation.
The museum has a vast mineralogical collection.  博物馆有一个巨大的矿物收藏。
The geologist conducted a mineralogical analysis of the rocks. 地质学家对岩石进行了矿物学分析。

近义词:petrologic, geological
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective
