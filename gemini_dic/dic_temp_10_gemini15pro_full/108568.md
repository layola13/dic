lcs:/ˌɛlˌsiːˈɛs/ | n. | 最长公共子序列 (Longest Common Subsequence) |  A sequence of characters that appears in the same relative order, but not necessarily contiguous, in two or more other sequences.  It is a common problem in computer science, particularly in bioinformatics and dynamic programming. | The LCS of "ABCDGH" and "AEDFHR" is "ADH". | 近义词: N/A (This is a specific technical term) | 反义词: N/A | noun
