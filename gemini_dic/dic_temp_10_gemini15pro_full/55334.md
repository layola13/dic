dissuasive:/dɪˈsweɪsɪv/
adjective
劝阻的，阻止的
tending to dissuade; deterring.
例句：The high price of the tickets was dissuasive.  高昂的票价令人却步。

例句：His dissuasive arguments convinced her not to go. 他劝阻的话使她打消了去的念头。

近义词：deterrent, discouraging, cautionary
反义词：encouraging, persuasive, inciting
词性: adjective
