dogskin:/ˈdɒɡskɪn/ (UK) /ˈdɑːɡskɪn/ (US)| n. adj.|狗皮，狗皮革的|The tough skin of a dog, used for making leather goods.  It can also be used adjectivally to describe something made from dogskin.|例句：He wore a dogskin coat. 他穿着一件狗皮大衣。|近义词：leather, hide, calfskin|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps synthetic leather)|noun, adjective
