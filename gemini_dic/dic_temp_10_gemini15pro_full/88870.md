hoisters:/ˈhɔɪstərz/
n.
起重机操作员，起重工人
People who operate hoists or lifting devices.
The hoisters carefully lifted the heavy cargo onto the ship.  起重机操作员小心地将沉重的货物吊到船上。
The hoisters were skilled in maneuvering the crane.  起重工人们熟练地操纵着起重机。
Synonyms: crane operators, lift operators
Antonyms: N/A
Noun


hoist:/hɔɪst/
n.
起重机，升降机
A device used for lifting or raising something.
They used a hoist to lift the piano to the second floor.  他们用起重机把钢琴抬到二楼。
The building was equipped with a powerful hoist for moving materials.  这栋楼配备了一个强大的起重机来搬运材料。
Synonyms: crane, lift, winch
Antonyms: N/A
Noun


hoist:/hɔɪst/
vt.
吊起，升起
To raise or lift something, often using a mechanical device.
They hoisted the flag to the top of the pole.  他们把旗子升到旗杆顶端。
The sailors hoisted the sails of the ship.  水手们升起了船帆。
Synonyms: raise, lift, elevate
Antonyms: lower, drop
Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hoysten, from Middle Dutch hijschen, frequentative of Middle Dutch hissen (“to hoist”), from Proto-Germanic *hisjaną (“to hoist”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱeyH- (“to set in motion, to excite”).
