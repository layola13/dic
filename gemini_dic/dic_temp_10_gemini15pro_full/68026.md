factories:/ˈfæktəriz/| n. |工厂（factory 的复数形式）|Buildings or groups of buildings where goods are manufactured or assembled.|Many factories have closed down due to the economic downturn. 许多工厂由于经济衰退而关闭。|The factories are working overtime to meet the demand. 这些工厂正在加班加点以满足需求。|近义词：plants, mills, works|反义词: 无|名词

factory:/ˈfæktəri/| n. |工厂|A building or group of buildings where goods are manufactured or assembled.|The factory produces a wide range of electronic goods. 这家工厂生产各种电子产品。|She works in a shoe factory. 她在一家鞋厂工作。|近义词：plant, mill, works|反义词: 无|名词

词源: From Old French *factorie "trading post, factory," from Medieval Latin factoria "a factory, trading establishment," from Latin factor "maker, doer"
