overimmunizes: /ˌoʊvərˈɪmjʊnaɪzɪz/
verb
过度免疫
To immunize excessively, beyond what is necessary or recommended, potentially leading to adverse effects.
The doctor warned against overimmunizing children, as it could weaken their natural immune response.  医生警告不要对儿童过度免疫，因为这可能会削弱他们的自然免疫反应。
The study suggested that overimmunizing livestock might not necessarily increase their resistance to disease.  研究表明，过度免疫牲畜可能不一定会增强它们对疾病的抵抗力。
近义词: overvaccinate
反义词: underimmunize
词性: verb

overimmunize: /ˌoʊvərˈɪmjʊnaɪzɪz/
word origin: over- + immunize
"Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much."
"Immunize" comes from the Latin "immunis," meaning "exempt" or "free from burden."
