anconal:/æŋˈkəʊnəl/| adj.|肘的，尺骨的|Relating to the elbow, specifically the point of the elbow.  It refers to the region of the forearm relating to the olecranon (the bony prominence of the elbow).|The anconal fossa is a triangular depression on the posterior surface of the arm, superior to the olecranon.|Synonym: cubital, olecranal|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, consider terms relating to other parts of the arm, e.g., radial, carpal)|adjective
