bafflers:/ˈbæflərz/| n. |迷惑不解的人或物；难题；挡板|Something that baffles or obstructs; a puzzling or difficult problem; a device used to deflect air or sound.|The detective was stumped by the bafflers of the case.  侦探被这个案件的难题难住了。|The bafflers on the air conditioner direct the airflow. 空调上的挡板引导气流。|Synonyms: puzzles, riddles, enigmas, obstacles, deflectors|Antonyms: solutions, answers, aids, clarifications|noun
