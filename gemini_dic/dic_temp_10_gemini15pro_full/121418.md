misinfers:/ˌmɪsɪnˈfɜːrz/
verb
错误地推断，做出错误的推论
to infer incorrectly; to draw a wrong conclusion
He misinfers my silence as agreement. 他把我的沉默误解为同意。
近义词: misconstrues, misinterprets, misunderstands
反义词: understands, comprehends, deduces correctly
词性: 动词
