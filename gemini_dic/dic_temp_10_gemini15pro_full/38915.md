confessionalisms:/kənˈfɛʃənəlɪzəmz/
n.
忏悔主义，忏悔行为（尤指宗教上的）
systems of religious belief that emphasize personal confession of sins
The rise of confessionalisms in early modern Europe led to significant religious and political upheaval.  欧洲近代早期忏悔主义的兴起导致了重大的宗教和政治动荡。

The study explored the various confessionalisms of the 16th century. 这项研究探讨了16世纪的各种忏悔主义。
近义词:denominations, sects, creeds
反义词:secularism, non-belief
