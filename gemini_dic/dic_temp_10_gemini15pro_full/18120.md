behindhand:/bɪˈhaɪndhænd/| adj.|落后的，迟到的，拖欠的|Late or delayed in doing something; not up to date.|Example: He was behindhand with his rent. 他拖欠了房租。|Example:  She's always behindhand with her work. 她的工作总是拖延。|Synonyms: overdue, late, delayed, tardy, lagging, dilatory|Antonyms: ahead, early, prompt, punctual|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From "behind" + "hand".  "Behind" indicating a position of lateness or disadvantage, and "hand" referring to progress or completion of tasks.  Essentially, "not keeping up with the work at hand."
