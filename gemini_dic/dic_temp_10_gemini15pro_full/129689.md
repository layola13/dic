non-discursive:/ˌnɒn dɪˈskɜːsɪv/ | adj. | 非话语的，非论证的，非推论的 | Not proceeding by argument or reasoning; not discursive.  Communicating without the use of language, particularly in the usual sense of words, sentences, and connected discourse. |  Abstract art is often described as non-discursive, conveying meaning through form and color rather than narrative. 抽象艺术常被描述为非话语的，通过形式和颜色而非叙述来传达意义。

The ballet offered a non-discursive interpretation of the myth.  这出芭蕾舞对神话进行了一种非话语的诠释。 | 近义词: non-verbal, intuitive, expressive, implicit | 反义词: discursive, verbal, explicit, rational | 词性: adjective
