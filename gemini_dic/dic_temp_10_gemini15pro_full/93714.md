immaculatenesses:/ɪˈmækjələtɪsnɪsɪz/
noun
完美无瑕；洁净；纯洁
The state or quality of being immaculate; perfect cleanliness, purity, or freedom from flaw or error.
Her immaculateness was evident in her pristine white dress. 她的完美无瑕体现在她那洁白的连衣裙上。
The immaculatenesses of the hospital rooms impressed the inspectors. 医院房间的洁净程度给检查员留下了深刻的印象。
Synonyms: purity, spotlessness, flawlessness, impeccability
Antonyms: impurity, dirtiness, flawedness, imperfection
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *immaculatnes, from immaculate + -ness.  Immaculate itself comes from Latin immaculatus (“unstained, spotless”), from in- (“not”) + maculatus (“spotted, stained”), past participle of maculō (“I spot, stain”).
