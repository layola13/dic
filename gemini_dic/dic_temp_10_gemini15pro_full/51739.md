devolution:/ˌdiːvəˈluːʃn/ |n.|权力下放，移交；退化|The devolution of power from central government to the regions.  中央政府向地方权力下放。|Example 1: The devolution of power to Scotland has led to a resurgence in Scottish nationalism. 权力下放给苏格兰导致了苏格兰民族主义的复兴。

Example 2:  Many argue that devolution is essential for efficient governance. 许多人认为权力下放对于有效治理至关重要。|Synonyms: decentralization, delegation, transfer |Antonyms: centralization, consolidation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *devolutio, from Latin devolvere "to roll down," from de- "down" + volvere "to roll."  The sense of "transfer of power" arose from the idea of power "rolling down" from a higher to a lower authority.
