nanai:/ˈnɑːnaɪ/ /nəˈnaɪ/ | interj. n.| 再见；纳奈人；纳奈语 |  A word used as a farewell, particularly in informal British English. Also refers to an ethnic group indigenous to Eastern Russia and the language they speak.  | Example 1: "Right, I'm off. Nanai!" (好吧，我走了。再见！) | Example 2: The Nanai people traditionally lived along the Amur River. (纳奈人传统上居住在黑龙江沿岸。) | 近义词: bye, goodbye, cheerio, see ya | 反义词: hello | 词性: interjection, noun
