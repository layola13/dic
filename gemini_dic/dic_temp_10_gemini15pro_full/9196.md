antioptimisms: /ˌænˌtɪˈɒptɪmɪzəmz/ | n. | 反乐观主义，悲观主义 |  The feeling or attitude of expecting the worst possible outcome; the opposite of optimism.  It often involves a focus on negative aspects and a disbelief in positive potential. |  His antioptimisms about the future of the company was contagious. 他对公司未来的悲观情绪很有感染力。

His antioptimisms prevented him from seeing any opportunity in the crisis. 他的悲观情绪使他看不到危机中的任何机会。

| 近义词: pessimism, negativity, cynicism, defeatism | 反义词: optimism, hopefulness, positivity | 词性: 名词
