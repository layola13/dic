agal:/ˈɑːɡəl/ | n. | 阿格尔，阿拉伯男子戴的头巾 | A cord, usually of black goat hair, used to hold a man's headcloth (keffiyeh) in place, especially by Bedouins and other Arabs. | He wore a traditional keffiyeh held in place by an agal. 他戴着一顶用阿格尔固定的传统头巾。 | 近义词:  keffiyeh rope, head rope | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "bareheaded" in context) | 词性: noun
