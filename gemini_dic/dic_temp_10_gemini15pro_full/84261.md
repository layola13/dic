half-stocking:/ˌhɑːfˈstɑːkɪŋ/ | n. | 半截袜，短袜 | A stocking that reaches only to the calf or just below the knee. |  She wore half-stockings and a skirt. 她穿着半截袜和一条裙子。

| Example: In the 18th century, men often wore breeches and half-stockings. 在18世纪，男人经常穿马裤和半截袜。| Synonym: sock, ankle sock, knee sock (depending on length) | Antonym: stocking, thigh-high | noun



half-stocking:/ˌhɑːfˈstɑːkɪŋ/ | adj. | 半截袜的 | Relating to or characteristic of half-stockings. |  He sported a half-stocking look with his breeches. 他穿着马裤，展现出一种半截袜的风格。 | Example: The half-stocking fashion was popular among men of that era. 半截袜的时尚在那段时期很受男士欢迎。| Synonym: sock-like | Antonym: full-length, stockinged | adjective
