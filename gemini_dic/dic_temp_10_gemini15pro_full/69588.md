felting:/ˈfɛltɪŋ/ | n. | 毡合；制毡；毡制品 | The process of matting wool or other fibers together by applying moisture, heat, and pressure.  The resulting fabric is also called felt. |  She learned the art of felting from her grandmother. 她从她祖母那里学习了毡合的艺术。 | The felting process creates a dense and durable fabric. 毡合过程创造了一种致密耐用的织物。| synonyms: fulling, matting | antonyms: unraveling, separating | noun

felting:/ˈfɛltɪŋ/ | v. | 毡合；使…成毡状 | To make felt by matting wool or other fibers together with moisture, heat, and pressure. |  She felted the wool into a warm hat. 她把羊毛毡合成了一顶暖和的帽子。 | The artist felted the fibers together to create a unique sculpture.  这位艺术家将纤维毡合在一起，创作出一件独特的雕塑。 | synonyms: matting, fulling | antonyms: unraveling, separating | verb
