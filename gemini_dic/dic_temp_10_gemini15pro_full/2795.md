advancement:/ədˈvɑːnsmənt/| n.|进步，提升，晋升|The advancement of technology has changed our lives. 科技的进步改变了我们的生活。|例句：He received a promotion in recognition of his recent advancements in the field.  他因最近在该领域的进步而获得晋升。

例句：Her rapid advancement through the company ranks surprised everyone. 她迅速晋升公司各个级别，让每个人都感到惊讶。

|近义词：progress, development, promotion, improvement|反义词:regression, decline, stagnation, setback|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French avancement, from avancer "to advance." Ultimately from Latin abante "from before."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The advancement of medical technology improves patient care. = 医学技术的进步改善了病人护理。
His advancement to a senior position reflects his hard work. = 他晋升到高级职位反映了他的辛勤工作。

一般过去时:
The advancement of women's rights was a slow process. =  妇女权利的进步是一个缓慢的过程。
Their scientific advancements benefited society greatly. = 他们的科学进步极大地造福了社会。


现在完成时:
The company has seen significant advancement in recent years. = 近年来，该公司取得了显著的进步。
There have been remarkable advancements in artificial intelligence. = 人工智能领域取得了显著的进步。


过去完成时:
They had made significant advancements before the project was cancelled. = 在项目取消之前，他们已经取得了重大进展。
She had achieved considerable advancement in her career before she retired. = 在退休之前，她的事业取得了相当大的进步。

将来时:
Further advancements in this field will revolutionize the industry. =  该领域的进一步发展将彻底改变该行业。
Technological advancements will continue to shape our future. = 技术进步将继续塑造我们的未来。

被动语态:
Significant advancements were made in the development of a new vaccine. = 在开发新疫苗方面取得了重大进展。
Further advancements are being made in the field of renewable energy. = 可再生能源领域正在取得进一步的进展。
