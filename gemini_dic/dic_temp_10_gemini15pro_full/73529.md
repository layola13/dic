foresight:/ˈfɔːrsʌɪt/| n.|远见，先见之明|The ability to predict or plan for the future.|She had the foresight to book a hotel room before they arrived. 她有先见之明，在他们到达之前预订了酒店房间。|近义词：forethought, anticipation, vision, prudence|反义词：shortsightedness, myopia, imprudence|词性:名词

foresight:/ˈfɔːrsʌɪt/| n.|远见卓识|The act or faculty of foreseeing; provident care or provision.|His foresight in investing in the company paid off handsomely. 他对投资这家公司的远见卓识获得了丰厚的回报。|近义词：prescience, prediction|反义词：hindsight, afterthought|词性:名词
