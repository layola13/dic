collection:/kəˈlekʃən/ | n. |收藏品，收集物；募捐，募集的款项| The museum has a fine collection of antique toys.  博物馆收藏了精美的古董玩具。|例句：He has a large collection of stamps. 他收藏了大量的邮票。|例句：The art collection was donated to the museum.  这批艺术收藏品捐赠给了博物馆。|近义词：accumulation, gathering, assembly|反义词:dispersal, distribution|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *colleccion*, from Old French *colleccion*, from Latin *collectiō* (“a gathering together”), from *collectus*, past participle of *colligō* (“gather together”), from *com-* (“together”) + *legō* (“gather, collect”).
