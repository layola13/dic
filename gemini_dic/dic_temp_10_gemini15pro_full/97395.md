infrastructure:/ˈɪnfrəˌstrʌktʃər/ | n. | 基础设施；基础建设 |  The basic physical and organizational structures and facilities (e.g. buildings, roads, power supplies) needed for the operation of a society or enterprise. |  The country needs to invest in its infrastructure.  这个国家需要对其基础设施进行投资。 |  The aging infrastructure of the city needs urgent attention.  这座城市老化的基础设施急需关注。 | synonyms: base, foundation, framework, underpinnings | antonyms: superstructure | noun
