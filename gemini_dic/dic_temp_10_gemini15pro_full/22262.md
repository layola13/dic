bonedog:/ˈboʊnˌdɒɡ/ (US), /ˈbəʊnˌdɒɡ/ (UK)| n. | 骨瘦如柴的狗；瘦骨嶙峋的狗 | A dog that is very thin, often due to starvation or neglect.  It emphasizes the visibility of the dog's bones due to its thinness. | The stray bonedog scavenged for scraps of food in the alley. 那只流浪的骨瘦如柴的狗在小巷里寻找食物残渣。

The rescued bonedog was so weak it could barely stand.  被救出的骨瘦如柴的狗非常虚弱，几乎站不立。| 近义词：scrawny dog, emaciated dog | 反义词: well-fed dog, plump dog | 词性: noun
