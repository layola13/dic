example:/ɪɡˈzɑːmp(ə)l/ /ɪɡˈzæmpəl/ | n. |例子，样本，榜样 |Something that is typical of or characteristic of a group or set; an instance of something.  A person or thing that is regarded as a good model to be followed. | This painting is a good example of his early work. 这幅画是他早期作品的一个很好的例子。

The teacher used her own experience as an example to illustrate the point. 老师用她自己的经历作为例子来说明这一点。 | synonym: instance, illustration, specimen, model, paradigm | antonym: exception, anomaly | noun
