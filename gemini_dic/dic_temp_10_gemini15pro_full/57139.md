dovetailer:/ˈdʌvˌteɪlər/| n. |榫接工人，吻合的事物|A person or thing that dovetails or fits together perfectly.  A person skilled in dovetail joinery. Something that fits seamlessly with something else.|The carpenter was a skilled dovetailer, creating intricate joints.|近义词：joiner, fitter, coordinator, integrator|反义词:mismatch,  incongruity|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From dovetail (n.) + -er (agent suffix).  "Dovetail" itself comes from the resemblance of the joint to a dove's tail.
