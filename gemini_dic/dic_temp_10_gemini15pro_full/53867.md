discontinue:/ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuː/ (US), /ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuː/ (UK)| v. |停止，中断，终止|To stop doing or providing something, especially something that you have been doing or providing regularly.| The company discontinued the production of that model.  该公司停止了该型号的生产。| synonyms: cease, stop, terminate, abandon, halt| antonyms: continue, start, commence, initiate|verb

discontinuance:/ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjʊəns/ (US), /ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjʊəns/ (UK)| n. |停止，中断，终止|The act of stopping something; cessation.| The discontinuance of the service caused much inconvenience.  该服务的终止造成诸多不便。| synonyms: cessation, termination, stop, halt, discontinuation| antonyms: continuation, commencement, start, initiation|noun

Etymology: From Late Middle English discontinuen, from Old French discontinuer, from Late Latin discontinuare, from Latin dis- "apart, asunder" + continuare "to continue".
