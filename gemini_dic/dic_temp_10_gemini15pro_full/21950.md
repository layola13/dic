boggy:/ˈbɒɡi/ (UK) /ˈbɑːɡi/ (US)| adj.|沼泽的；泥泞的|Resembling a bog; wet and spongy.|The ground was boggy after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，地面变得泥泞不堪。|例句：The trail became boggy and difficult to traverse.  这条小路变得泥泞难行。

例句：We had to wear boots because the field was so boggy. 我们不得不穿靴子，因为田野太泥泞了。|近义词：marshy, swampy, miry, quaggy|反义词：dry, firm, solid|词性：形容词
