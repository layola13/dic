faisals:/ˈfeɪsəlz/ | n. | decisions, rulings, decrees (plural of faisal) | Decisions or rulings, often in a legal or official context.  The word is derived from Arabic. | The tribal council issued its faisals on land disputes. 部落委员会发布了关于土地纠纷的裁决。|  The judge's faisals were met with mixed reactions. 法官的裁决引起了不同的反应。 | synonyms: judgments, verdicts, decrees, rulings | antonyms: indecisions, appeals | noun
