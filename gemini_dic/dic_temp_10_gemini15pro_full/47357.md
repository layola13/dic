deaconed:/ˈdiːkənd/
v.
任命为执事；授予执事圣职
ordained or appointed as a deacon.
He was deaconed in 1985.  他于1985年被任命为执事。
ordained, invested
(Not typically used with an antonym as it describes a specific religious act.)
