boohoo: /ˈbuːhuː/ | interjection | 哭泣声；假哭；抱怨 | An exclamation imitating the sound of crying, often used to express mock sympathy or derision for someone complaining.  It suggests that the speaker finds the complaints trivial or insincere. |  "Boohoo, you lost your game. Get over it!" 你输了游戏，哭哭啼啼的。振作起来！ |  近义词: whine, moan, complain, whimper | 反义词: cheer, celebrate, rejoice | 词性: interjection

词源:  Imitative of the sound of crying.  Onomatopoeic.
