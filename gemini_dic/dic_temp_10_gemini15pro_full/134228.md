nonindustriousness:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdʌstriəsnəs/ | n. | 懒惰，不勤奋 | Lack of diligence or application to work; laziness. |  His nonindustriousness resulted in him losing his job. 他的懒惰导致他丢了工作。| Synonyms: laziness, indolence, idleness, sloth | Antonyms: industriousness, diligence, assiduity | noun

Etymology:  From non- (not) + industrious + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  The root "industrious" comes from the Latin "industrius," meaning "diligent," from "indu," within, + "struere," to build.
