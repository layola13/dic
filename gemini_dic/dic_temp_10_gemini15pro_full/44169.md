criticizers:/ˈkrɪtɪˌsaɪzərz/
n.
批评者，评论家
People who express disapproval or find fault with something or someone.
The film attracted many criticizers due to its controversial subject matter.  这部电影因其备受争议的主题吸引了许多批评家。
The president’s new policy has garnered a lot of criticizers. 总统的新政策引来了很多批评者。
critics, reviewers, detractors
supporters, advocates
noun
