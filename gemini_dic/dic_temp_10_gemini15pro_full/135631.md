The consultant identified several costly nonpractices in the manufacturing process.  顾问在制造过程中发现了几个代价高昂的未采用的做法。|近义词：inaction, omissions, defaults|反义词:practices, actions, implementations|词性:名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "practices" (plural noun from "practice")
