farmers:/ˈfɑːrmərz/ (US), /ˈfɑːməz/ (UK)| n.|农民；农场主|A person who owns or manages a farm.|The farmers are busy harvesting their crops. 农民们正忙着收割庄稼。|近义词：agriculturalists, growers, cultivators|反义词: consumers|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English fermer, from Anglo-French fermer, Old French fermeor (“lessee”), from Medieval Latin firmarius (“holder of a firm”), from Latin firmus (“firm, strong, steadfast”).
