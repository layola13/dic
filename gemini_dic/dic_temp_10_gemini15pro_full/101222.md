invitar:/ɪɱbiˈtɑːr/ (Spanish), /ɪnˈvaɪtər/ (anglicized)| vt.|邀请|To ask someone to go somewhere or do something with you.|例句：Te invito a cenar esta noche.  I invite you to dinner tonight. (我邀请你今晚共进晚餐。)|近义词：summon, ask, request|反义词:exclude, uninvite|verb

invitar:/ɪnˈvaɪtər/ (English pronunciation when borrowed)| vt.|邀请|To request someone's presence or participation.|例句：I invited her to my birthday party. 我邀请她参加我的生日派对。|近义词：ask, request, bid|反义词:exclude, disinvite|verb
