chary: /ˈtʃɛəri/ (US), /ˈtʃeəri/ (UK) | adj. | 谨慎的，小心的，吝啬的 | cautiously or suspiciously reluctant to do something; sparing or restrained about giving or using something |  She was chary of offering advice after her last suggestion backfired. 她上次的建议适得其反后，她就不太愿意提建议了。

He is chary of spending money. 他花钱很谨慎。

| 近义词: wary, cautious, circumspect, hesitant, reluctant, sparing, frugal | 反义词: reckless, extravagant, lavish, profuse | 词性: 形容词
