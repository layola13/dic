duvet:/ˈduːveɪ/ (US), /ˈdjuːveɪ/ (UK)| n.| 羽绒被，羽绒服|A soft quilt filled with down, feathers, or a synthetic fiber, used instead of an upper sheet and blankets.| I snuggled under the warm duvet. 我依偎在温暖的羽绒被下。|Synonym: comforter, quilt|Antonym: sheet|noun

duvets:/ˈduːveɪz/ (US), /ˈdjuːveɪz/ (UK)| n.| 羽绒被（复数）|Plural form of duvet, referring to more than one duvet.| We bought new duvets for all the beds. 我们为所有的床都买了新的羽绒被。|Synonym: comforters, quilts|Antonym: sheets|noun
