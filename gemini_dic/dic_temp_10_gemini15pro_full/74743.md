freethinking:/ˈfriːˌθɪŋkɪŋ/
adjective
自由思考的
Characterized by freedom of thought, especially in religious or political matters; not bound by traditional or conventional ideas.
She's known for her freethinking views on education. 她以其对教育的自由思考的观点而闻名。
synonyms:unconventional, unorthodox, independent, open-minded, liberal, progressive, radical, rebellious, nonconformist, dissident, heretical, agnostic, atheistic
antonyms:conventional, orthodox, conservative, dogmatic, conformist
词性:adjective


freethinking:/ˈfriːˌθɪŋkɪŋ/
noun
自由思想
The practice of forming opinions about religion and society based on reason and evidence, rather than accepting traditional beliefs.
Freethinking was discouraged in the strict religious community. 在严格的宗教社区，自由思想是不被鼓励的。
synonyms:unconventionality, unorthodoxy, independence, open-mindedness, liberalism, radicalism, heresy, agnosticism, atheism
antonyms:conventionality, orthodoxy, conservatism, dogmatism, conformism
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From free + thinking.  First recorded in the late 17th century.
