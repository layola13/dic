monkfish:/ˈmʌŋkˌfɪʃ/ | n. | 鮟鱇鱼 | A bottom-dwelling fish with a large head and wide mouth, found in coastal waters. It is considered a delicacy in some cuisines. | Monkfish is often called the "poor man's lobster" because of its firm, meaty texture. 鮟鱇鱼常被称为“穷人的龙虾”，因为它肉质紧实。

Monkfish liver is also considered a delicacy, similar to foie gras.  鮟鱇鱼肝也被认为是一种美味，类似于鹅肝。| 近义词: anglerfish, goosefish | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific type of fish) | 词性: noun
