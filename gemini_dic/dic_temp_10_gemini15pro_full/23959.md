breastplow: /ˈbrɛstˌplaʊ/ | n. | 胸犁式;俯卧式雪橇 | A face-down style of sledding where the person lies on their stomach and uses their arms to steer.  It's often seen as a more risky or reckless way to sled. | Example 1: He went down the hill on his sled using the breastplow technique. 他用胸犁式从山上滑了下来。

Example 2: Breastplowing can be dangerous if you're not careful. 如果你不小心，胸犁式滑雪可能会很危险。 | Near Synonyms: belly flop sledding, stomach sledding | Antonyms:  N/A (lack of a direct opposite action) | noun
