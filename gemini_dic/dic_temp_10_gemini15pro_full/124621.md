mousseline:/ˈmuːslɪn/ /muːslɪn/| n. |薄纱；平纹细布；慕斯林酱|A fine, sheer fabric resembling muslin.  A light sauce or purée, often made with cream or egg whites, used to coat or fill dishes. |The chef prepared a delicate salmon mousseline. 厨师准备了一份精致的三文鱼慕斯林酱。|近义词:chiffon, muslin,gauze|反义词:(No direct antonym for the fabric sense. For the culinary sense, perhaps "heavy sauce")|词性:noun

mousseline:/ˈmuːslɪn/ /muːslɪn/| adj. |薄纱的；慕斯林酱的|Made of or resembling muslin; made with a mousseline sauce. |She wore a mousseline dress to the party. 她穿着一件薄纱连衣裙去参加派对。|近义词:gauzy, sheer,chiffon|反义词:(No direct antonym)|词性:adjective
