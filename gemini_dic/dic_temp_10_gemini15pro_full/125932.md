mushiness:/ˈmʌʃɪnəs/
mushiness|n.|糊状，软绵绵，多愁善感|A soft, pulpy, or overly sentimental quality.|The mushiness of overcooked pasta is unappetizing.  煮过头的意大利面软绵绵的，让人没有食欲。|Synonyms: softness, pulpiness, sentimentality|Antonyms: firmness, hardness, practicality
mushiness|n.|糊状|The bananas had reached a state of mushiness. 香蕉已经变得糊状了。|Synonyms: pulp, sludge|Antonyms: firmness, solidity
