combativity:/kəmˈbætɪvɪti/
n.
好战性，斗志，战斗力
The quality of being eager to fight or argue.
He showed great combativity in the boxing ring.  他在拳击场上表现出极大的斗志。
The team's combativity helped them win the championship. 球队的斗志帮助他们赢得了冠军。
synonyms: aggressiveness, belligerence, pugnacity, fighting spirit
antonyms: passivity, docility, submissiveness
noun
