flux:/flʌks/| n. v.| 流动;变化;通量|A continuous change, passage, or flow.|The flux of information on the internet is overwhelming.  互联网上的信息流动量巨大。|synonyms: flow, fluctuation, change, variation|antonyms: stability, constancy|noun

flux:/flʌks/| v.| 熔化;使…熔化;用焊剂处理|To melt or become liquid; to treat with a flux.|The artist fluxed the metal to create the sculpture.  艺术家熔化金属来创作雕塑。|synonyms: melt, fuse, liquefy|antonyms: solidify, freeze|verb
