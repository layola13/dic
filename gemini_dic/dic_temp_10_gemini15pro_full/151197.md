parchments:/ˈpɑːrtʃmənts/| n. |羊皮纸，文稿，文件|A manuscript or document written on parchment.  These ancient parchments contain valuable historical information. 这些古老的羊皮纸包含珍贵的历史信息。|Synonyms: manuscripts, documents, scrolls |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps digital documents) |noun

parchment:/ˈpɑːrtʃmənt/| n. |羊皮纸|A material made from the skin of a sheep or goat, prepared for writing or painting. The Torah is traditionally written on parchment.  妥拉传统上是用羊皮纸书写的。|Synonyms: vellum (especially fine parchment) |Antonyms:  paper, papyrus |noun
