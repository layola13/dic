amortisement: /əˈmɔːtɪzmənt/ (UK), /ˌæmərtaɪˈzeɪʃn/ (US)| n.|摊销；分期偿还|The gradual reduction of a loan or debt through periodic payments. This process allocates the cost of an intangible asset over its estimated useful life.|The company uses straight-line amortisation for its intangible assets. 公司对其无形资产采用直线摊销法。|synonyms: depreciation, write-off|antonyms: appreciation, accrual|noun
amortize: /əˈmɔːtaɪz/ (UK), /ˈæmərtaɪz/ (US)| vt.|摊销；分期偿还|To gradually reduce a loan or debt through periodic payments.|The mortgage will be amortized over 25 years.  这笔抵押贷款将在 25 年内分期偿还。|synonyms: depreciate, write-off|antonyms: appreciate, accrue|verb
