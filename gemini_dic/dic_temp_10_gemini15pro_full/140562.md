oligarchal: /ˌɒlɪˈɡɑːkɪkəl/ | adj. | 寡头政治的；由少数人统治的 | Relating to or characteristic of an oligarchy, a form of government in which power is held by a small group of people. |  The country's oligarchal system concentrated wealth in the hands of a few families.  这个国家的寡头政治体系将财富集中在少数几个家族手中。 |  The oligarchal regime was overthrown by a popular uprising.  这个寡头政权被民众起义推翻了。 | Synonyms: autocratic, dictatorial, totalitarian, tyrannical | Antonyms: democratic, egalitarian | adjective
