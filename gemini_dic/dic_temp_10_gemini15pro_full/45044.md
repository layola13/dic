cubation: /kjuːˈbeɪʃən/
n.
孵化; 培养; 酝酿
the process of incubating eggs or developing something
例句：The cubation period for chicken eggs is 21 days.  鸡蛋的孵化期是21天。

例句：The project is still in its cubation phase.  这个项目仍处于酝酿阶段。
近义词：incubation, brooding, gestation, development
反义词：hatching
