give-away:/ˈɡɪvəˌweɪ/ (US), /ˈɡɪvəweɪ/ (UK)| n. adj.|赠品；泄露；暴露|Something that is given away free, especially something that a shop gives you when you buy goods; something that reveals a secret, often unintentionally.|例句：The shop had a give-away with every purchase.  这家商店每买一件商品都有赠品。|近义词：freebie, giveaway, prize, premium, bonus|反义词: purchase, sale|词性:noun

give-away:/ˈɡɪvəˌweɪ/ (US), /ˈɡɪvəweɪ/ (UK)| adj.|泄露秘密的；暴露的|Revealing a secret, often unintentionally.|例句：Her nervous laughter was a dead give-away. 她紧张的笑声完全暴露了秘密。|近义词：revealing, telling, incriminating|反义词: concealing, hiding|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the phrasal verb "give away," meaning to give something freely or to reveal a secret unintentionally. The noun and adjective forms evolved from this verb phrase.
