commensuration:/kəˌmenʃəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |相称; 等量; 同量; 测量; |The act of making things commensurate; a bringing into proportion or correspondence. 使事物相称的行为；使成比例或对应的行为。|  His commensuration of his expenses to his income allowed him to save money. 他根据收入调整支出，从而节省了开支。 |近义词：adjustment, equalization, balancing |反义词：mismatch,  incongruity |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin commensuratio, from Latin commensuratus (past participle of commensurare "to make commensurate"), from com- "together" + mensurare "to measure," from mensura "measure."
