defaceable:/dɪˈfeɪsəbl/
adjective
可污损的，可毁坏的
Susceptible to being defaced; capable of being marred or disfigured.
The statue was made of a soft, easily defaceable material.  这尊雕像是用一种柔软的、容易被污损的材料制成的。
The walls were covered in easily defaceable graffiti. 墙上满是容易被清除的涂鸦。
synonyms: vulnerable, damageable, marrable
antonyms: indestructible, impervious, resistant
词性: adjective
