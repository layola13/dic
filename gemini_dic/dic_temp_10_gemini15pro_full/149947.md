pallier:/ˈpæliˌeɪ/ (US), /ˈpælɪeɪ/ (UK)| v. n.| 减轻，缓解；权宜之计；掩盖；台阶；平台 |to mitigate, alleviate, or conceal something undesirable; a temporary solution; a landing between flights of stairs|The government announced measures to pallier the effects of the recession. 政府宣布了旨在减轻经济衰退影响的措施。|Synonyms: alleviate, mitigate, ease, assuage, lessen, diminish, reduce, abate, temper, moderate, soften, cushion, relieve, allay, soothe|Antonyms: aggravate, exacerbate, worsen, intensify, increase, heighten|verb

pallier:/ˈpæliˌeɪ/ (US), /ˈpælɪeɪ/ (UK)| n.| 台阶；平台；楼层 |a landing between flights of stairs; a level area|The pallier outside the apartment offered a stunning view of the city. 公寓外面的平台可以欣赏到令人惊叹的城市景观。|Synonyms: landing, platform, level, story, floor|Antonyms: None|noun
