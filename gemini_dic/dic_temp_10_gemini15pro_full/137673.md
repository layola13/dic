nonuniteable:/ˌnɒnjuːˈnaɪtəbl/ | adj. | 不可联合的，不能愈合的 | Not able to be united or joined together; specifically (of a broken bone) not capable of knitting or healing. | The doctor determined that the fracture was nonuniteable and would require surgery. 医生确定骨折无法愈合，需要手术。|  The nonuniteable bone fragments caused chronic pain. 无法愈合的骨碎片引起慢性疼痛。 | 近义词: unjoinable, inseparable, incurable (in the context of fractures) | 反义词:  joinable, uniteable, curable (in the context of fractures) | 词性: 形容词
