outflamed:/ˌaʊtˈfleɪmd/
verb
燃烧得更旺盛；胜过；在…方面超过
burned more brightly than; surpassed in fervor or brilliance
The setting sun outflamed the bonfire. 落日比篝火烧得更旺。
