bypassed:/ˌbaɪˈpæst/ /ˌbaɪˈpæsd/ | v. adj.|绕过，忽略，回避|To go around or avoid something, often an obstacle or a procedure.  Also used as an adjective to describe something that has been bypassed.|

例句：
The surgeon bypassed the blocked artery.  外科医生绕过了阻塞的动脉。

They bypassed the usual channels and went straight to the CEO.  他们绕过了通常的渠道，直接去找首席执行官。


近义词：circumvent, avoid, detour, ignore, neglect|
反义词：confront, address, engage|
词性: verb, adjective


bypassed:/ˌbaɪˈpæst/ /ˌbaɪˈpæsd/ | adj.|被绕过的，被忽略的|Something that has been bypassed or avoided.|

例句：
The bypassed town was now quiet and deserted. 被绕过的小镇现在安静而荒凉。

The bypassed security measures proved to be a weakness. 被忽略的安全措施被证明是一个弱点。


近义词：circumvented, avoided, neglected|
反义词：addressed, engaged|
词性: adjective


词源:  "by-" (meaning "near, aside") + "pass" (meaning "to go").  The meaning evolved from going alongside something to going around it and eventually to avoiding it altogether.
