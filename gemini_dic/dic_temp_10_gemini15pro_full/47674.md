debtee:/ˌdɛbˈtiː/ /ˌdɛtˈtiː/ | n. |债权人；贷方|A person or institution to whom a debt is owed. |Example 1: The debtee demanded full repayment of the loan. 债权人要求全额偿还贷款。

Example 2: The company listed all its debtees in its annual report.  该公司在其年度报告中列出了所有债权人。|Synonym: creditor, lender |Antonym: debtor, borrower |noun
