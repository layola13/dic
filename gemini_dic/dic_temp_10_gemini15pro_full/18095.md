behaviourist:/bɪˈheɪvjərɪst/ (US) /bɪˈheɪvjərɪst/ (UK)| n.|行为主义者|A psychologist who adheres to behaviorism, the theory that all behavior is learned through conditioning and environmental stimuli, rather than innate factors.|John B. Watson is considered a prominent behaviourist.  约翰·B·沃森被认为是一位杰出的行为主义者。|Synonym: behaviorist (US spelling)|Antonym: nativist|noun


behaviourist:/bɪˈheɪvjərɪst/ (US) /bɪˈheɪvjərɪst/ (UK)| adj.|行为主义的|Relating to behaviorism, the theory that all behavior is learned.|The behaviourist approach focuses on observable actions. 行为主义方法侧重于可观察的行为。|Synonym: behavioral|Antonym: cognitive|adjective
