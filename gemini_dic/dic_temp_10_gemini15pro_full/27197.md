callithump:/kəˈlɪθʌmp/ | v. |参加喧闹的游行；制造嘈杂的音乐；|To take part in or create a callithump.|  They callithumped through the streets after the game.  比赛结束后，他们喧闹地游行穿过街道。|Synonyms:  serenade (mockingly),  make a racket,  raise a din |Antonyms: be quiet, be silent | verb
