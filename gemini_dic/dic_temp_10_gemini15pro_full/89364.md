homonym:/ˈhɒmənɪm/ (UK) /ˈhɑːmənɪm/ (US)| n.|同音异义词|A word that sounds the same as another word but has a different meaning, regardless of spelling.  This broader definition is less commonly used.| "Two", "too", and "to" are sometimes considered homonyms. | “Two”、“too”和“to”有时被认为是同音异义词。| 近义词: homophone | 反义词: antonym, heteronym |词性: noun
