lumme:/ˈlʌmi/| int.|哎呀；我的天哪|An exclamation of surprise or dismay.  Expressing mild astonishment, alarm, or dismay.  Similar to "oh my God" or "oh dear" but milder and more common in British English. |例句：Lumme, that's a big spider! 哎呀，那是一只大蜘蛛！

例句：Lumme, I forgot my keys! 我的天，我忘记带钥匙了！|近义词：blimey, cor, gosh, crikey|反义词: (No direct antonym for an interjection)|词性:interjection
