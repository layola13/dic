motives:/ˈmoʊtɪvz/ (US) /ˈməʊtɪvz/ (UK)| n.|动机，目的|Something that causes a person to act in a particular way; a reason for doing something.|Her motives for leaving were never clear. 她离开的动机一直不清楚。|synonyms: reasons, intentions, aims, objectives, purposes, goals, drives, impulses|antonyms: deterrents, disincentives|noun


motivate:/ˈmoʊtɪveɪt/ (US) /ˈməʊtɪveɪt/ (UK)| v.|激励，激发|Provide (someone) with a motive for doing something.|The coach motivated the team to win. 教练激励球队赢得比赛。|synonyms: inspire, stimulate, encourage, drive, propel, prompt|antonyms: discourage, dissuade, deter|verb
