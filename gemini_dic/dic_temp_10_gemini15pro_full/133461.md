nonfeasible:/nɒnˈfiːzɪb(ə)l/
adjective
不可行的，办不到的
Not capable of being done or carried out; impracticable.
The project was deemed nonfeasible due to the exorbitant costs.  这个项目由于成本过高而被认为是不可行的。
The committee concluded that the proposal was nonfeasible given the current budget constraints. 委员会得出结论，鉴于目前的预算限制，该提案是不可行的。
近义词: impracticable, unworkable, impossible, unattainable
反义词: feasible, possible, achievable, viable
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  not, opposite of  (来自拉丁语 non)
feasible (adjective):  capable of being done  (来自法语 faisable,  最终来自拉丁语 facere,  "to do")
