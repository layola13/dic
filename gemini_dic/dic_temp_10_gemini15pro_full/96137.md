indenization:/ɪnˌdɪnɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |本土化；本地化；归化|The process of adapting something to local conditions or making it locally controlled. |The government is pushing for the indenization of the oil industry. 政府正在推动石油工业的本土化。|近义词：localization, nationalization, domestication |反义词: globalization, internationalization |词性: noun

Etymology:  From in- +‎ denizen +‎ -ization.  "Denizen" itself comes from Old French *denezein, from denz "within, inside" (from Latin de intus "from within") +‎ -ein, a suffix marking belonging.
