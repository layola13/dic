corneocalcareous:/ˌkɔːrnioʊkælˈkɛəriəs/
adjective
角质钙化的
composed of horn and calcareous matter; resembling horn and calcareous matter.  Formed of or resembling a mixture of horn and calcareous material.
The fossilized shell displayed a corneocalcareous texture. 这块化石贝壳呈现出角质钙化的结构。
Near synonyms:  horny, calcareous, shell-like, chitinous
Antonyms: soft, fleshy
词性: adjective
