ascendable:/əˈsɛndəbl/
adjective
可攀登的；可上升的
able to be ascended; climbable
The mountain is ascendable, but it's a challenging climb. 这座山可以攀登，但这是一个具有挑战性的攀登。
Experienced climbers found the icy cliff face ascendable.  经验丰富的登山者发现这面结冰的悬崖可以攀登。
近义词:climbable, scalable, surmountable
反义词:inaccessible, impassable, insurmountable
词性:adjective
词源:From ascend + -able.  Ascend comes from the Latin word *ascendere*, meaning "to climb up," from *ad- "to" + *scandere* "to climb."
