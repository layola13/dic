gadget:/ˈɡædʒɪt/| n.|小玩意儿；小器具；小装置|A small mechanical device or tool, especially an ingenious or novel one.|例句：He loves playing with his new gadget. 他喜欢玩他的新小玩意儿。

例句：This kitchen is full of handy gadgets.  这间厨房里充满了方便的小器具。|近义词：device, tool, appliance, instrument, contrivance|反义词: (gadget 没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用old-fashioned tool/equipment 表示老式工具/设备来对比)|词性:noun
