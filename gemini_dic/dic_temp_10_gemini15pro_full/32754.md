chloruret:/ˈklɔːraɪt/ | n. | 氯化物; 漂白剂 |A compound of chlorine with another element or radical, especially a salt of the anion Cl− or a covalent chloride. | Sodium chloruret is common table salt. 氯化钠是常见的食盐。|  Calcium chloruret is used as a drying agent. 氯化钙用作干燥剂。|synonyms: chloride,  bleach (when referring to a bleaching agent) | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, depends on the specific chloruret) | noun
