otheoscope:/ˈɒθiəˌskoʊp/ /ˈoʊθiəˌskoʊp/
noun
透视镜，热视觉仪
An instrument for detecting slight variations in temperature by means of the resulting variations in the radiation emitted.  A device that makes infrared radiation visible.
The otheoscope allowed the researchers to observe the heat patterns of the animals.  这台透视镜使研究人员能够观察动物的热模式。
synonyms: thermal imager, infrared camera
antonyms: N/A
