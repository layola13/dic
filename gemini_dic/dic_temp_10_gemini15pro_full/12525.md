aspherically:/eɪˈsfɛrɪkli/
adjective
非球面地
not spherical; deviating from the shape of a sphere.
The lens was ground aspherically to minimize distortion. 这块透镜被磨成非球面以减少失真。
近义词:non-spherical, anisometric
反义词:spherical, globular
词性:adjective

词源:
From a- (not) + spherical + -ly (adverbial suffix forming adjectives).  "Spherical" comes from the Greek "sphaira" meaning "ball, globe".
