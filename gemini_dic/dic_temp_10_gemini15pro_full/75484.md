frustrative:/ˈfrʌstreɪtɪv/| adj.|令人沮丧的；使人泄气的|Causing frustration; tending to frustrate.|The lack of progress on the project is very frustrating.  这个项目缺乏进展，令人非常沮丧。|例句：It's frustrating to have to wait so long.  不得不等这么久真是令人沮丧。|近义词：discouraging, annoying, exasperating|反义词：encouraging, satisfying, rewarding|词性:形容词
Etymology: From frustrate + -ive.  Ultimately from Latin frustra.
