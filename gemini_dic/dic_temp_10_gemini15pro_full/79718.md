glue-sniffing:/ˈɡluː snɪfɪŋ/ | n. | 吸胶 | The act of inhaling the fumes of glue or other solvents for intoxicating purposes.  | Glue-sniffing is a dangerous activity that can cause serious health problems. 吸胶是一种危险的行为，会导致严重的健康问题。 |  Glue-sniffing can lead to brain damage and even death. 吸胶会导致脑损伤甚至死亡。 |  solvent abuse, inhalant abuse |  (No direct antonym) | noun
