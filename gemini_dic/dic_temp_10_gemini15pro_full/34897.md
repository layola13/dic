cliqued: /klɪkt/ | adj. | 小集团的，派系的 | Divided into exclusive groups; clannish.  A group of people who form a small, exclusive group and tend to socialize only with each other. |  The office was very cliqued, making it difficult for newcomers to integrate.  办公室里小集团林立，新来的人很难融入。| Example:  The high school was notoriously cliqued, with the athletes, the nerds, and the popular kids rarely interacting. 这所高中以小集团众多而闻名，运动员、书呆子和受欢迎的孩子很少互动。| Synonyms: exclusive, clannish, insular | Antonyms: inclusive, open, welcoming | adjective


cliqued: /klɪkt/ | v. | 形成小集团 | past participle of clique |  They had cliqued up in high school and remained close friends ever since.  他们在高中时就形成了小团体，从那以后一直是亲密的朋友。| Example: The new students had already cliqued together, making it hard for others to join their group.  新来的学生已经形成了自己的小团体，其他人很难加入他们。| Synonyms: grouped, banded, united | Antonyms: separated, divided, dispersed | verb
