nonsecurities:/ˌnɑːnsɪˈkjʊrətiz/ | n. | 非证券 |  Assets that are not considered securities, such as real estate, commodities, or private business interests.  This term is used in finance and investing to distinguish between regulated financial instruments (securities) and other forms of investment. | Example: The investor diversified his portfolio by including nonsecurities like real estate and precious metals.  这位投资者通过纳入房地产和贵金属等非证券来使其投资组合多样化。| Synonyms: non-security investments, alternative investments | Antonyms: securities, stocks, bonds | Noun
