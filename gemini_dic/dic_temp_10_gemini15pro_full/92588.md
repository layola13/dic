-iable:/iəbl/
suffix|形容词后缀|capable of being; suitable for being; tending to; given to|
英文解析:This suffix forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," "suitable for being," "tending to," or "given to." It is often attached to verb stems.
例句:
Modifiable: The software is modifiable to suit individual needs.  这个软件可以修改以适应个人需求。

Reliable:  He is a reliable friend. 他是一个可靠的朋友。


近义词: -able, -ible
反义词: None (as a suffix)
词性: suffix (后缀)
