bares:/bɛərz/ | v. | 暴露，露出；展现，显露 |  To uncover or expose something that was previously hidden. | The dog bared its teeth aggressively. 狗凶狠地露出了牙齿。| reveals, exposes, uncovers | conceals, hides | verb

bares:/bɛərz/ | n. | (bare 的复数形式) | Plural form of "bare," referring to things that are uncovered or exposed. |  The winter winds left the trees' bares exposed to the sky. 冬天的风让树枝暴露在天空下。|  exposures, displays | coverings, concealments | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Old English *bær (adjective) meaning "bare, naked, uncovered," and *barian (verb) meaning "to make bare, uncover."  The Proto-Germanic root is *baraz, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰoso- meaning "naked, bare."
