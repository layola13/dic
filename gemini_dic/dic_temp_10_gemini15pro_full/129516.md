non-attachment:/ˌnɒn əˈtætʃmənt/ /ˌnɑːn əˈtætʃmənt/
n.
不执着，无执念，超脱
The state of not being emotionally attached to something or someone.
Buddhist philosophy emphasizes non-attachment to worldly possessions.  佛教哲学强调不执着于世俗的财产。
He practiced non-attachment to outcomes, focusing on the process instead. 他练习不执着于结果，而是专注于过程。
detachment, indifference, neutrality
attachment, dependence, clinging
noun
