dizzinesses:/ˈdɪzinɪsɪz/
noun
头晕，眩晕（复数）
A sensation of whirling and loss of balance, often accompanied by nausea.  The plural form of "dizziness."
She suffered from dizzinesses after the roller coaster ride. 她坐过山车后感到头晕。
近义词: lightheadedness, vertigo, giddiness
反义词: stability, balance
词性: noun
