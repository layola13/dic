oxheal:/ˈɒkshɪəl/ /ˈɑːkshɪl/ | n. | 牛舌草;  |A common European plant (Anchusa officinalis) of the borage family, having bright blue or purple flowers and bristly leaves. It was formerly believed to have medicinal properties, especially for healing wounds.|The oxheal grew wild along the edges of the field. |近义词：alkanet, bugloss |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
