disfiguringly:/dɪsˈfɪɡərɪŋli/
adv.
毁容地，丑陋地
in a way that spoils the appearance of something or someone; so as to cause disfigurement
The burn scarred her face disfiguringly. 烧伤在她脸上留下了丑陋的疤痕。
近义词:  hideously, grotesquely, repulsively
反义词:  beautifully, attractively, pleasingly
词性: adverb

disfigure:/dɪsˈfɪɡər/
vt.
损毁…的外形，使…变丑
to spoil the appearance of something or someone
The accident disfigured her face.  事故毁了她的容貌。
近义词:  mar, deform, mutilate
反义词:  beautify, enhance, adorn
词性: verb

figure:/ˈfɪɡər/
n.
数字；人物；身材；图形；价格；（雕塑或绘画作品的）人形；（尤指在特定时刻的）重要人物；（乐曲的）音型
v.
计算；认为；估计；象征；描绘；露头角；扮演角色
n. a shape or outline; a person; a number
v. to calculate or work out; to appear; to be important
She has a good figure. 她身材很好。
I figured that he would be late. 我估计他会迟到。
近义词:  (n.) shape, form, outline, number; (v.) calculate, compute, estimate
反义词:  (n.) shapelessness; (v.) miscalculate
词性: noun, verb
