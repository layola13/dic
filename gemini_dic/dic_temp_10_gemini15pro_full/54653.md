disobedient:/ˌdɪsəˈbiːdiənt/| adj.|不服从的，不顺从的|Not obeying rules or instructions. Failing to comply with an order or request.|例句：The disobedient child refused to go to bed.  不服从的孩子拒绝上床睡觉。

例句：The disobedient dog ran away from its owner. 不听话的狗从主人身边跑开了。|近义词：unruly, wayward, defiant, rebellious, insubordinate|反义词:obedient, compliant, submissive|词性:形容词
