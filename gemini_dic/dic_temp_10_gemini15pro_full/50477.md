depressing:/dɪˈprɛsɪŋ/| adj.|令人沮丧的，压抑的|Causing or tending to cause dejection. Making you feel very unhappy and without hope for the future.|The news was very depressing.  这个消息令人非常沮丧。|The weather has been depressing lately. 最近的天气一直很令人沮丧。| synonyms: disheartening, discouraging, dispiriting, demoralizing, upsetting, saddening | antonyms: uplifting, encouraging, cheering, exhilarating | adjective
