overcolors:/ˈəʊvəˌkʌləz/ (US) /ˈoʊvərˌkʌlərz/ (UK)
|n.|覆色，叠色|The act of applying one color over another, especially in painting or photography, to modify the existing hue or create a new one.|He used overcolors to give the photograph a vintage feel. 他用覆色给照片一种复古的感觉。|synonyms: overlays, glazes, tints, washes|antonyms: base colors, underpainting|noun
