heavy-set:/ˈhɛvi sɛt/| adj.|体格魁梧的，敦实的|Someone who is heavy-set has a large, strong, and solid body.  They are often considered overweight or stocky.  It can sometimes be considered a more polite way to say someone is overweight.|例句：The heavy-set man struggled to fit into the small sports car. 那个体格魁梧的男人费力地挤进那辆小型跑车。

例句：The police were looking for a heavy-set suspect in a blue jacket. 警察正在寻找一个穿着蓝色夹克的体格魁梧的嫌疑人。|近义词：stocky, stout, portly, hefty, bulky, big-boned|反义词：slim, thin, slender, slight, lean|词性:形容词
