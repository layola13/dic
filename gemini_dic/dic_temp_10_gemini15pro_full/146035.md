overdried:/ˌoʊvərˈdraɪd/| adj.|过度干燥的；干燥过度的|Excessively dried; dried beyond the desired or optimal point.|The overdried flowers crumbled in my hand.  这些过度干燥的花在我手中碎裂了。|The leather was overdried and cracked.  皮革过度干燥并开裂了。|近义词：parched, desiccated, dehydrated|反义词：moist, damp, hydrated|词性:形容词

词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "dried" (past participle of "dry")
"over-" 表示“过度”、“超过”
"dried" 是动词 "dry" 的过去分词，表示“干燥的”
