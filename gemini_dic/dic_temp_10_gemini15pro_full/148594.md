overteaching:/ˌoʊvərˈtiːtʃɪŋ/
n.|过度教学|The act of teaching too much, often resulting in confusion or a lack of independent learning.|The teacher's overteaching led to the students becoming passive learners. 老师的过度教学导致学生变得被动学习。|overinstruction, over-explanation|underteaching|noun
overteaching:/ˌoʊvərˈtiːtʃɪŋ/
v.|过度教导 (通常以被动语态出现)|The action of being taught too much.|The concepts were overtaught, leaving the students feeling overwhelmed. 这些概念被过度讲解，让学生感到不知所措。|overinstructing, drilling excessively|underteaching, neglecting|verb
