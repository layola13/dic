coaction:/koʊˈækʃən/| n.|共同行动，协作，合作|The project requires coaction between different departments. 这个项目需要不同部门之间的协作。|例句：The successful completion of the project was due to the coaction of all team members.  这个项目的成功完成归功于所有团队成员的共同努力。

例句：The coaction of several environmental factors led to the disaster.  几个环境因素的共同作用导致了这场灾难。

近义词：collaboration, cooperation, joint action, teamwork|反义词：inaction, opposition, antagonism|词性:名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin *coactio(n-), from Latin coactus ‘compelled’ (see coact).  最初的含义更接近“强迫联合行动”，后来演变为更普遍的“共同行动”。
