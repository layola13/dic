antifeudalisms:/ˌæntiˈfjuːdəlɪzəmz/
n.
反封建主义（复数）
opposition to or the destruction of a feudal system; systems of thought or belief opposed to feudalism.
The rise of antifeudalisms in Europe led to significant social and political changes. 欧洲反封建主义的兴起导致了重大的社会和政治变革。
近义词:antifeudalism,  modernism, liberalism
反义词:feudalism, conservatism
词性:名词
