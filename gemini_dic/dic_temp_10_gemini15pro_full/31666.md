chattily:/ˈtʃætɪli/| adv.|健谈地，饶舌地|In a chatty, informal manner; talkatively.  以健谈的、非正式的方式；饶舌地。|She wrote a chattily email to her friend about her day. 她给朋友写了一封健谈的邮件，讲述了她的一天。|Synonyms: talkatively, loquaciously, garrulously, volubly|Antonyms: taciturnly, quietly, reservedly, reticently|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From chat + -ly.  来自 chat（聊天）+ -ly（副词后缀）。
