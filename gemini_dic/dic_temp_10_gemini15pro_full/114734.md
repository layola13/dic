malmbrick: /ˈmɑːlmˌbrɪk/ | n. | 马尔姆砖 | A type of fired brick, typically pale yellow or light red in color, made from clay found near Malmö, Sweden, and commonly used in construction in the Nordic countries. | The house was built with classic yellow malmbrick. 这栋房子是用经典的黄色马尔姆砖建造的。 |  Malmbrick is a popular choice for building facades in Denmark. 马尔姆砖是丹麦建筑外墙的流行选择。 | synonyms: brick, facing brick | antonyms: N/A | noun
