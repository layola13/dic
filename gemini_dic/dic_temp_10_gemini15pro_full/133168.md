noneuphoniousnesses:/ˌnɒnjuːˈfəʊniəsnəsɪz/
noun
不悦耳, 刺耳的声音(复数)
The quality or state of being not euphonious; dissonance; harshness or unpleasantness of sound.
The noneuphoniousnesses of the construction site made it difficult to concentrate.  建筑工地的刺耳噪音让人难以集中注意力。
Near synonyms: dissonances, cacophonies, harshnesses
Antonyms: euphonies, melodies, harmonies
词性: 名词
