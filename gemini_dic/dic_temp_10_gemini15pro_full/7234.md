anglicizes:/ˈæŋɡlɪsaɪzɪz/
verb
使英语化；英国化
To make English in form or character; to adapt to English usage.
The author anglicizes foreign names in his novels. 作者在他的小说中把外国名字英语化了。
anglify, Anglise
n/a
