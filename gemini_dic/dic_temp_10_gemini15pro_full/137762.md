nonvariety:/ˌnɑːn vəˈraɪəti/
noun
非多样性；缺乏变化
Lack of variety; uniformity or monotony.
The nonvariety in the menu made every meal feel the same.  菜单缺乏多样性，使得每顿饭都感觉一样。
Near synonyms: uniformity, monotony, sameness, homogeneity
Antonyms: variety, diversity, heterogeneity
