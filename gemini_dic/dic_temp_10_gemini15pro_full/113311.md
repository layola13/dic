lutulence:/ˈluːtjʊləns/ | n. |浑浊；泥泞；污秽|The state of being muddy, turbid, or thick with suspended matter.|The lutulence of the river after the storm made it unsafe to drink.  暴风雨过后，河水浑浊，不能饮用。|Synonyms: turbidity, cloudiness, muddiness, thickness |Antonyms: clarity, clearness, transparency|noun
Etymology: From Latin *lūtulentus*, from *lūtum* "mud".|
