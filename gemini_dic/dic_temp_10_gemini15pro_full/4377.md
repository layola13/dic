aladdin:/əˈlædɪn/| n.|阿拉丁|A character from a Middle Eastern folk tale, a poor young man who obtains a magic lamp and ring that grant him wishes.  He is also the protagonist of the Disney animated film of the same name.|Example: Aladdin rubbed the lamp, and a genie appeared. 阿拉丁擦了擦神灯，一个精灵出现了。|Example:  The story of Aladdin is a classic tale of rags to riches.  阿拉丁的故事是一个经典的从赤贫到暴富的故事。|Synonym: protagonist, hero|Antonym: villain|Noun
