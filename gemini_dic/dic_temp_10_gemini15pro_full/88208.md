hind:/haɪnd/ | adj. | 后面的，后部的；（动物的腿）后面的 | Situated at the back; posterior. | The hind legs of a dog are stronger than its front legs. 狗的后腿比前腿更有力。| synonyms: rear, back, posterior | antonyms: front, fore, anterior | adjective

hind:/haɪnd/ | n. |  雌鹿（尤指赤鹿）| A female deer, especially a red deer. | The hind grazed peacefully in the meadow. 母鹿在草地上平静地吃草。| synonyms: doe, female deer | antonyms: stag, hart (male deer) | noun
