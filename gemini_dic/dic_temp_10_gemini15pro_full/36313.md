cohobate: /koʊˈhoʊbeɪt/ | v. | 蒸馏；反复蒸馏；精炼 | To repeatedly distill or redistill a liquid, often with the addition of a solvent, in order to purify or concentrate it. |  The alchemists cohobated the mixture several times to extract the essence.  炼金术士们反复蒸馏混合物数次以提取精华。

The chemist cohobated the solution to increase its potency.  化学家反复蒸馏溶液以提高其效力。 | 近义词: redistill, refine, purify | 反义词: contaminate, pollute | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Late Latin *cohobare, from Latin co- + hobare "to have a pot or kettle."
