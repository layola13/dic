mourn:/mɔːrn/| v. |哀悼，悲痛|To feel or express deep sorrow for someone who has died or something that has been lost.|She mourned the death of her husband for many years. 她为丈夫的去世哀悼了多年。|The entire nation mourned the loss of its leader. 整个国家都在为失去领导人而哀悼。|grieve, lament, sorrow|rejoice, celebrate|verb
