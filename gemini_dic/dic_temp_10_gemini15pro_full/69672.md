fender-bender:/ˈfɛndər bɛndər/
noun
轻微的交通事故; 小碰撞
A minor traffic accident, typically involving damage to the fenders of vehicles but no serious injuries.
My new car got into a fender-bender in the parking lot. 我的新车在停车场发生了轻微的碰撞。
近义词: minor accident, bump, scrape
反义词: major accident, collision, crash
词性: noun
