endoplasm:/ˈɛndəʊplæzəm/ |n.|内质|The inner portion of the cytoplasm of a cell, exclusive of the ectoplasm.  细胞质的内部部分，不包括外质。|The endoplasm contains the cell's organelles. 内质包含细胞的细胞器。|cytoplasm, protoplasm|ectoplasm|noun


endoplasms:/ˈɛndəʊplæzəmz/ |n.|内质（复数）|The plural form of endoplasm, referring to the inner portion of the cytoplasm of multiple cells. 指多个细胞的细胞质内部部分的复数形式。|The endoplasms of these cells show unusual activity. 这些细胞的内质显示出异常的活动。|cytoplasms, protoplasms|ectoplasms|noun
