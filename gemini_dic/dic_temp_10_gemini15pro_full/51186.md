destabilize:/diːˈsteɪbəlaɪz/
verb
使不稳定，动摇
to make something less firm or steady, especially a political system or a country
The rumors of a coup destabilized the government. 这些政变谣言动摇了政府。
近义词: weaken, undermine, unsettle
反义词: stabilize, strengthen, reinforce
词性:v.

destabilize:/diːˈsteɪbəlaɪz/
词源: de- (down, away) + stable (firmly fixed) + -ize (to make or become)  From Latin stabilis 'firm, steadfast'.
词性:v.
