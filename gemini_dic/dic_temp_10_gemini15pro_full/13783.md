audiobook:/ˈɔːdiəʊbʊk/ (UK) /ˈɑːdioʊbʊk/ (US)| n.|有声读物|A recording of a book being read aloud, typically available as a digital download.|I listened to the audiobook on my commute. 我在通勤路上听了有声读物。|Synonyms: talking book, audio recording, narrated book|Antonyms: printed book, ebook, hard copy|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
audio + book

* audio: from Latin *audīre* "to hear"
* book: from Old English *bōc* "book, writing, document"
