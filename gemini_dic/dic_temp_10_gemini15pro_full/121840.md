misrender:/ˌmɪsˈrɛndər/ | v. | 误释，曲解，错误地表达 | To represent or interpret something wrongly; to give a false or inaccurate rendering of. | The newspaper misrendered her comments, making her sound much more critical than she actually was. 这家报纸误解了她的评论，让她听起来比实际批评得多。 | The artist felt that the critics had misrendered his intentions in their reviews. 这位艺术家觉得评论家们在评论中曲解了他的意图。 | synonyms: misinterpret, misrepresent, distort, misconstrue | antonyms: interpret, represent, portray, depict | verb
