cimar:/siˈmɑr/ |n.|野马；未驯服的马|A wild or untamed horse, especially one of the small, hardy breed descended from Spanish horses and found in the western United States.|The cimars roamed freely across the plains. 野马在平原上自由漫步。|Synonyms: mustang, bronco, wild horse|Antonyms: domesticated horse|noun
