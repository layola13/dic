metagrammatism:/ˌmɛtəˈɡræmətɪzəm/
|n.|语法错乱; 语法错误|A disorder of speech, especially the misuse of words or grammatical forms, resulting from a brain lesion.|The patient exhibited metagrammatism, substituting "chair" for "table" and "walk" for "run". 病人表现出语法错乱，用“椅子”代替“桌子”，用“走”代替“跑”。|paragrammatism, dysgrammatism| correctness, accuracy|noun
