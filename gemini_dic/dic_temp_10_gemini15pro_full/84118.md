half-linen:/ˌhɑːf ˈlɪnɪn/
| adj.|亚麻布的；半亚麻的|Made of a blend of half linen and half another fiber, typically cotton. 由一半亚麻和一半其他纤维（通常是棉）混合制成。| This half-linen shirt is perfect for summer. 这件半亚麻衬衫非常适合夏天穿。|Synonyms: part-linen, blend-linen|Antonyms: pure-linen, all-linen|adjective
