|n.|虚假；谎言；不诚实|The mendaciousness of the politician's statements was apparent to everyone.  这个政客言论的虚假性对每个人来说都是显而易见的。|例句：The sheer mendaciousness of the propaganda was astounding.  宣传的纯粹谎言令人震惊。|近义词：dishonesty, falsehood, untruthfulness, deceit, duplicity|反义词：honesty, truthfulness, veracity|词性：名词

mendacious:/mɛnˈdeɪʃəs/
|adj.|撒谎的；虚假的；不诚实的|He was a mendacious and unscrupulous businessman. 他是一个虚伪且不择手段的商人。|例句：The mendacious rumors spread quickly through the town.  虚假的谣言迅速传遍了整个城镇。|近义词：lying, deceitful, dishonest, untruthful, false|反义词：honest, truthful, veracious|词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mendacitas, from Latin mendax (“lying, deceitful”), from mendum (“fault, defect”)  + -acious. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *mend- (“physical defect, fault”).
