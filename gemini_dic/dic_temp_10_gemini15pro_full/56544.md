dominator:/ˈdɒmɪneɪtə(r)/ (US) /ˈdɑːmɪneɪtər/ (UK)| n. |支配者，统治者，主导者|A person or thing that dominates or controls something or someone.|The company is a dominator in the global market. 这家公司在全球市场上占主导地位.|Synonyms: ruler, controller, leader, master, governor|Antonyms: subject, subordinate, follower|noun

dominators:/ˈdɒmɪneɪtəz/ (US) /ˈdɑːmɪneɪtəz/ (UK)| n. |支配者们，统治者们，主导者们|Plural form of dominator.|The new dominators quickly established their authority.  新的统治者们迅速确立了他们的权威.|Synonyms: rulers, controllers, leaders, masters, governors|Antonyms: subjects, subordinates, followers|noun
