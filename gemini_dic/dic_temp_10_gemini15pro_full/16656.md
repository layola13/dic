barracouta:/ˌbærəˈkuːtə/ | n. |梭鱼，巴racuda鱼|A saltwater fish found in temperate and tropical oceans, known for its elongated body and sharp teeth. It is a popular game fish and is also eaten. |例句：

The fisherman caught a large barracouta.
