bycatches:/ˈbaɪkætʃɪz/
n.
兼捕渔获，副渔获物
The unintentional capture of marine life during fishing for a different species.
例句：Bycatches are a serious problem for marine ecosystems. 兼捕渔获对海洋生态系统是一个严重的问题。

例句：The fishermen tried to minimize bycatches by using selective fishing gear. 渔民们尝试使用选择性渔具来减少兼捕渔获。
近义词：incidental catches, by-catch
反义词: target catch
词性: 名词(plural)
