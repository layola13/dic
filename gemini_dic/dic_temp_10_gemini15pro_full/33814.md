circumfluent:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈfluːənt/ | adj. | 环流的，周流的，包围流动的 | Flowing around; surrounding. | The circumfluent river encircled the ancient city. 这条环绕的河流包围着这座古城。 | The circumfluent waters protected the island. 环绕的水域保护着这个岛屿。 | 近义词: surrounding, encompassing, encircling | 反义词:  central, interior | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin circumfluentem (nominative circumfluens), present participle of circumfluere "to flow around," from circum "around" + fluere "to flow."
