nonsaccharine:/ˌnɑːnˈsækərɪn/ /ˌnɒnˈsækərɪn/
adjective
无糖的;不甜的;不含糖的
not containing or sweetened with sugar; not sugary.
Example:  She opted for a nonsaccharine sweetener in her coffee. 她选择在咖啡里加无糖甜味剂。

Example: The hospital provided nonsaccharine drinks for diabetic patients. 医院为糖尿病患者提供无糖饮料。

Synonym: sugar-free, unsweetened
Antonym: sugary, saccharine
