de-evolution:/ˌdiːɛvəˈluːʃən/
n.
退化；逆进化
The process of returning to an earlier or less advanced form or state, the opposite of evolution.
Some argue that reality TV represents the de-evolution of our culture.  一些人认为真人秀代表着我们文化的退化。
The film depicts a de-evolution of humanity into primal beasts.  这部电影描绘了人类退化成原始野兽的过程。
regression, devolution, decline
evolution, advancement, progress
noun
