discographies:/dɪˈskɒɡrəfiz/
n.
唱片目录；唱片分类目录
A list of all the recordings made by a particular performer or group.
例句：The band's discographies includes ten studio albums and numerous singles.  这支乐队的唱片目录包括十张录音室专辑和许多单曲。

例句：He has a comprehensive discographies of Beatles recordings. 他有一份完整的披头士乐队唱片目录。

近义词：catalog, list, compilation
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
