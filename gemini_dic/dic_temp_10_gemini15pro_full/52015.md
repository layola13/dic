diagramming:/ˈdaɪəˌɡræmɪŋ/| n.|图表绘制，图解|the act of representing something visually, especially by means of a diagram|She spent hours diagramming sentences for her English class. 她花了几个小时为英语课画句子结构图。|visualization, charting| - |noun
diagramming:/ˈdaɪəˌɡræmɪŋ/| v.|用图表表示，图解|to represent something visually, especially by means of a diagram|He diagrammed the process of photosynthesis for his students. 他为学生们绘制了光合作用过程的图表。|illustrating, charting| - |verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From diagram + -ing.  "Diagram" comes from the Greek "diagramma," meaning "geometric figure, outline, plan."
