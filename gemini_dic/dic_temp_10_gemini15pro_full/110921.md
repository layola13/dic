lipoma:/lɪˈpoʊmə/ | n. |脂肪瘤|A benign tumor composed of fatty tissue.  A lipoma is a slow-growing, fatty lump that's most often situated between your skin and the underlying muscle layer.| My doctor said the lump on my back is a lipoma. 我的医生说我背上的肿块是脂肪瘤。|The surgeon removed the lipoma in a simple procedure.  外科医生通过一个简单的程序切除了脂肪瘤。|近义词:adipoma, fatty tumor|反义词: N/A (Lipomas don't have a direct opposite.)|词性:noun
