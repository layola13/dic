counterdemands:/ˈkaʊntərˌdɪˈmændz/
noun
反要求，反诉求
demands made in response to previous demands.
The union responded to the company's offer with a series of counterdemands. 工会以一系列反要求回应公司的提议。
synonyms: counterclaims, counterproposals
antonyms: offers, concessions
n.
