allotment:/əˈlɒtmənt/ (US: /əˈlɑːtmənt/)
n.|分配，份额；小块菜园|A share or portion of something allocated to someone or something.  A plot of land rented by an individual for growing vegetables and fruit.|例句：Each family received an allotment of food. 每个家庭都领到了一份食物。|例句：He spends his weekends tending his allotment. 他周末都在照料他的小菜园。|近义词：allocation, share, portion, plot, patch|反义词:confiscation, seizure|词性:名词

allotments:/əˈlɒtmənts/ (US: /əˈlɑːtmənts/)
n.|分配，份额；小块菜园（复数）|Plural form of "allotment."  Refers to multiple shares or portions, or multiple plots of land for gardening.|例句：The allotments were distributed fairly among the residents. 菜园被公平地分配给了居民们。|例句：Many people in the city have allotments where they grow their own vegetables. 城市里的许多人都有自己种植蔬菜的小菜园。|近义词：allocations, shares, portions, plots, patches|反义词:confiscations, seizures|词性:名词


词源:
From Old French *alotir "to share, divide," from a- "to" + lot "share, portion."
