commissure:/ˈkɒmɪʃə(r)/ (US) /ˈkɑːmɪʃər/| n. | 连接；接合；接缝；连合| A point or line of union between two parts or things, especially the line of junction between two bones or parts of a bone, or a band of nerve tissue connecting the hemispheres of the brain or other parts of the nervous system.| The surgeon carefully stitched the commissure of the wound.  外科医生仔细地缝合了伤口的接缝。 |  The corpus callosum is the largest commissure in the brain. 胼胝体是大脑中最大的连合。 | synonyms: junction, joint, seam, connection | antonyms: separation, division | noun
