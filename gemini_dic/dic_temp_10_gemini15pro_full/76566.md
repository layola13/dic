galleys:/ˈɡæliz/| n.| 船上的厨房；狭长的厨房；（印刷厂的）版架|A galley is the kitchen on a ship or boat, or a long narrow kitchen.  It also refers to a metal tray used for holding type that has been set for printing.|The galley on the ship was small and cramped. 船上的厨房又小又挤。|近义词：kitchen, cookhouse|反义词: (no direct antonym)|noun

galleys:/ˈɡæliz/| n.| （印刷厂的）校样；单页印刷品|In printing, a galley is a proof or single printed page.|The editor reviewed the galleys before printing. 编辑在印刷前审阅了校样。|近义词：proof, page|反义词: (no direct antonym)|noun
