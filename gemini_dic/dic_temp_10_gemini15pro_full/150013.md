palmyra:/pælˈmaɪrə/ | n. | 棕榈树；巴尔米拉（叙利亚古城） | A tall, slender palm tree with large fan-shaped leaves, native to tropical Asia.  Also, an ancient Semitic city located in present-day Syria. | Example 1: The palmyra trees swayed gently in the breeze. 棕榈树在微风中轻轻摇曳。| Example 2:  The ruins of Palmyra are a UNESCO World Heritage Site. 巴尔米拉的遗迹是联合国教科文组织世界遗产。| Synonym: palm, Borassus flabellifer (for the tree) | Antonym: N/A | Noun
