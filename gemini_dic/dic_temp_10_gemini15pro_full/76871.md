gangly:/ˈɡæŋɡli/| adj.|瘦长的；细长的；笨拙的|Tall, thin, and awkward in movements.|例句：A gangly teenager bumped into me. 一个瘦高的青少年撞到了我。|例句：The foal was gangly and awkward. 小马驹又瘦又笨拙。|近义词：lanky, spindly, gawky|反义词：graceful, coordinated|词性:形容词
