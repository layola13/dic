dozy:/ˈdoʊzi/| adj.|昏昏欲睡的，半睡半醒的，懒散的|sleepy, drowsy; slow-witted, not alert|例句：I felt dozy after that heavy lunch.  午饭后我感到昏昏欲睡。|近义词：sleepy, drowsy, lethargic, sluggish|反义词：alert, awake, energetic, lively|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From doze + -y.  Ultimately from Old English *dūsian, from Proto-Germanic *dusōną (“to be sleepy or dazed”).
