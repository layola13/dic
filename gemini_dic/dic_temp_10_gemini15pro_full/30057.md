ccing:/ˈsiːsiːɪŋ/ | v. | 抄送 |  Present participle of "cc," which stands for "carbon copy." In email communication, it means to include someone in the recipient list, usually for informational purposes, not as a primary recipient. | I'm ccing my boss on this email so he's aware of the situation. 我正在把这封邮件抄送给我的老板，让他了解情况。 |  | include, forward | exclude | verb
