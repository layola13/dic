inter-coordinates:/ˌɪntərkoʊˈɔːrdɪneɪts/ | adj. |相互协调的，互相配合的|  Relating to or involving the coordination or interaction between different elements or systems, often implying a harmonious or synchronized relationship.  Derived from "inter-" (between) and "coordinates" (brings into a common action, movement, or condition). | The inter-coordinates movements of the dancers were mesmerizing. 舞蹈演员们相互协调的动作令人着迷。| Synonym: interconnected, integrated, synchronized | Antonym: uncoordinated, disjointed | adjective
