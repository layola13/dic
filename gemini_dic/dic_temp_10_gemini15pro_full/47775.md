decani:/dɪˈkeɪnaɪ/
noun
十人长，十分之一
A decani is a group of ten, especially a group of ten soldiers in the Roman army.
The decani were responsible for maintaining order and discipline within their units.  十人长负责维持其队伍中的秩序和纪律。
The centurion relied on his decani to relay orders and ensure they were followed. 百夫长依靠他的十人长来传达命令并确保命令得到遵守。
Synonyms: group, squad, team
Antonyms: individual, single
