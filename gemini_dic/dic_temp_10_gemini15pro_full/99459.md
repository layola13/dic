interfacial:/ˌɪntərˈfeɪʃəl/| adj.|界面间的，分界面的|Existing or occurring at the surface separating two phases (such as two immiscible liquids or a liquid and a solid).|The interfacial tension between oil and water is high. 油和水之间的界面张力很高。|The interfacial area between the two liquids is large. 两种液体之间的界面面积很大。|近义词：surface, boundary, |反义词:bulk, internal|adjective
