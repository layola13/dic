hypoxias:/haɪˈpɒksiəz/
n.
缺氧（复数）
A deficiency of oxygen reaching the tissues of the body.
例句：The climbers suffered from hypoxias at high altitude. 登山者在高海拔地区遭受缺氧。

例句：Multiple hypoxias can lead to organ damage. 多次缺氧会导致器官损伤。

近义词：anoxias, oxygen deficiencies
反义词：hyperoxias
词性：名词


hypoxia:/haɪˈpɒksiə/
n.
缺氧（单数）
A deficiency of oxygen reaching the tissues of the body.
例句：The pilot experienced hypoxia at 30,000 feet. 飞行员在30,000英尺高空经历了缺氧。

例句：Brain hypoxia can cause permanent damage. 脑缺氧会导致永久性损伤。

近义词：anoxia, oxygen deficiency
反义词：hyperoxia
词性：名词


词源：From Greek *hupóxia*, from *hupó* (under) + *oxús* (sharp, acid) - referring to the original belief that oxygen was involved in making substances acidic.  The original term referred to reduced oxygen tension in inspired air whereas now it commonly refers to reduced oxygen supply to tissues.
