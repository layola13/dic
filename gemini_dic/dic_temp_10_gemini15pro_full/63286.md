enthrones:/ɪnˈθroʊnz/
verb
使登基；使就任高位；把…奉为神圣
invests with sovereign power; places on a throne.
The ceremony enthrones the new king.  典礼上新国王登基了。
The new CEO was enthroned last week. 新任首席执行官上周就职了。
synonyms: crown, invest, install, inaugurate
antonyms: dethrone, depose, oust
词性: 动词
