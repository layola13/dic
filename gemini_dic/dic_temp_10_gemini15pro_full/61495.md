embroideries: /ɪmˈbrɔɪd(ə)riz/
noun
刺绣品，绣花，刺绣图案
Decorative needlework.

The museum displayed exquisite embroideries from the 18th century.  这家博物馆展出了18世纪精美的刺绣品。

She learned the art of embroideries from her grandmother. 她从祖母那里学习了刺绣艺术。

近义词: needlework, needlepoint, tapestry, stitchery
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a physical object.  Could consider "plain cloth" as a contrasting concept.)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *embroidure, from embroder "to embroider," from en- "in, on" + broder "to border, edge," from bord "border, edge."
