heterogenist: /ˌhɛtərəˈdʒɛnɪst/
|n.|异质主义者；主张人类起源于不同祖先的人|A person who believes in the theory of heterogenesis, which posits that different species arose from separate origins.|He was a staunch heterogenist, rejecting the idea of a common ancestor for all life forms.|近义词: polygenist|反义词: monogenist|词性: noun


Etymology:
From hetero- (different) + genesis (origin)
