outfast:/ˌaʊtˈfæst/
verb
超过…的速度；跑得比…快
to run or move faster than someone or something else
The hare outfasted the tortoise in the initial stages of the race. 兔子在比赛初期跑得比乌龟快。
He easily outfasted the other runners. 他轻松地超过了其他跑步者。
近义词: outrun, outstrip, surpass
反义词: lag behind, trail
词性: verb
