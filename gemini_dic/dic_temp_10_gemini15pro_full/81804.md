词源:  From Middle English *grene*, from Old English *grēne*, from Proto-Germanic *grōnijaz* (“green”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰreh₁- (“to grow, green”). Cognate with West Frisian *grien* (“green”), Dutch *groen* (“green”), German *grün* (“green”), Danish *grøn* (“green”), Swedish *grön* (“green”), Icelandic *grænn* (“green”).
