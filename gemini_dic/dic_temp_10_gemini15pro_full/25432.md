bulking:/ˈbʌlkɪŋ/ | n. |增重，增大，膨胀 |The process of increasing muscle mass, typically by consuming a calorie surplus and engaging in weight training. | He's been bulking up for his next bodybuilding competition.  他一直在为下一场健美比赛增重。|synonyms:  massing, gaining, growing, expanding | antonyms: cutting, slimming, shrinking, reducing | noun

bulking:/ˈbʌlkɪŋ/ | adj. |庞大的，巨大的 |Of great size or volume.  This meaning is less common than the noun form related to muscle growth. |They loaded the bulking cargo onto the ship. 他们把庞大的货物装上了船。| synonyms: large, massive, enormous, huge | antonyms: small, tiny, minuscule, insignificant | adjective
