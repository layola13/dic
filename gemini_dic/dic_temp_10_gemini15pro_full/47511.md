dears:/dɪərz/| n.|亲爱的们，各位|A plural form of "dear," used as a term of affection when addressing a group of people.|Dears, please listen carefully. 亲爱的们，请仔细听。|近义词：loved ones, beloveds, friends|反义词:enemies, foes|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "dēore," meaning "precious, valuable, costly, beloved."  Related to Old High German "tiuri" and Gothic "dius." The meaning shifted over time from primarily indicating monetary value to expressing affection.
