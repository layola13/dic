greekery:/ˈɡriːkəri/
noun
希腊文化（常含贬义），希腊风格（常含贬义）
Excessive or affected use of Greek language, customs, or style, often implying pretentiousness or artificiality.
He peppered his speech with greekery, hoping to impress the academics.  他演讲中充斥着希腊文化元素，希望给学者们留下深刻印象。
近义词:Hellenism, classicism
反义词:modernism
