filmily:/ˈfɪlmɪli/
adv.
薄膜状地，朦胧地
in a filmy manner; thinly and transparently.
The curtains hung filmily across the window. 窗帘薄薄地挂在窗户上。
近义词:thinly, gauzily, transparently, sheerly, diaphanously
反义词:thickly, opaquely
词性:副词

Etymology:
From film + -y.  "Film" comes from Old English "fylmen" meaning "membrane, thin skin,"  ultimately from Proto-Germanic *filmin-. The suffix "-y" forms adjectives and adverbs meaning "characterized by" or "full of."
