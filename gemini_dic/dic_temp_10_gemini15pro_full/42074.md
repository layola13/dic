cothurnated:/kəˈθɜːrneɪtɪd/
adjective
穿高底靴的；悲壮的；夸张的
Wearing cothurns; elevated in style; pompous or inflated.
The actor delivered his lines in a cothurnated style, reminiscent of classical tragedy. 演员以一种悲壮的风格念台词，让人想起古典悲剧。
近义词：stilted, pompous, lofty, inflated
反义词：natural, simple, unpretentious
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *cothurnatus*, past participle of *cothurnāre* "to wear cothurns," from *cothurnus* "buskin, tragic boot."
