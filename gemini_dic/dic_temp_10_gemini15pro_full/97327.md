informatively:/ɪnˈfɔːrmətɪvli/| adv.|提供信息的，信息丰富的|in a way that provides useful or interesting information|The guidebook describes the city informatively. 这本指南以信息丰富的方式描述了这座城市。|The article was informatively written, providing a lot of detail about the topic. 这篇文章写得很有信息量，提供了很多关于这个话题的细节。|近义词：instructively, helpfully, usefully, enlighteningly|反义词：uninformatively, meaninglessly, vaguely|词性:副词

词源: From informative + -ly.  "Informative" comes from the Late Latin informativus, from informare "to shape, train, instruct, educate," from in- "into" + formare "to form."
