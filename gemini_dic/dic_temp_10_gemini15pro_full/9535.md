antisensitizer:/ˌæntiˈsɛnsɪtaɪzər/
|n.|脱敏剂，抗敏剂|A substance that reduces or eliminates sensitivity, especially to an allergen.|Doctors may prescribe an antisensitizer to help manage allergic reactions. 医生可能会开一种脱敏剂来帮助控制过敏反应。|Example 2:  The new antisensitizer cream significantly reduced the itching and redness.  这款新的抗敏药膏显著减少了瘙痒和发红。|Synonyms: desensitizer, hyposensitizer|Antonyms: sensitizer|noun
