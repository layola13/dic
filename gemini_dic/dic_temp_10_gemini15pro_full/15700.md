balaclava:/ˈbæləˌklɑːvə/ | n. |巴拉克拉瓦帽，面罩|A close-fitting knitted garment covering the head and neck, often also covering most of the face.  It is typically worn in cold weather or for concealment.|The soldiers wore balaclavas to protect their faces from the biting wind. 士兵们戴着巴拉克拉瓦帽以保护他们的脸免受刺骨的寒风.|Synonyms: ski mask, face mask|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps uncovered face)|Noun
