crippling:/ˈkrɪplɪŋ/| adj.|严重削弱的，造成严重后果的；令人震惊的|Causing serious impairment or damage; severely damaging.  Relating to or characteristic of a cripple.|The country suffered a crippling economic downturn. 这个国家遭受了严重的经济衰退。|synonyms: devastating, debilitating, paralyzing, incapacitating|antonyms:  beneficial, helpful, restorative, strengthening|adjective

crippling:/ˈkrɪplɪŋ/| n.|跛行|The act of walking with a limp.|His crippling made it difficult to climb the stairs. 他的跛行使他爬楼梯很困难。|synonyms: limping, hobbling|antonyms: walking, running|noun
