arbiter's:/ˈɑːrbɪtərz/
noun
仲裁者的；裁决人的
belonging to or associated with an arbiter.
The arbiter's decision is final. 仲裁者的决定是最终的。
Synonyms: judge's, referee's, mediator's
Antonyms: disputant's, litigant's
词性: possessive noun (所有格名词)
