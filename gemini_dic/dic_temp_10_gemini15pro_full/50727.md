derogating:/dɪˈrɒɡeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
贬低的，减损的
 lessening the merit or reputation of a person or thing; disparaging.
His derogatory remarks about her angered everyone. 他对她说的贬低性的话激怒了所有人。
近义词: disparaging, belittling, denigrating, demeaning
反义词: complimentary, flattering, praising, laudatory
词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *dērogātus, past participle of dērogāre (“to repeal part of a law, detract from”), from dē- (“from, away”) + rogāre (“to ask, propose a law”).
