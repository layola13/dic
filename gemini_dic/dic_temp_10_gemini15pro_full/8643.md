anticonformist:/ˌæntiˈkənˈfɔːrmɪst/
|n.|反传统者；反从众者|A person who consciously and deliberately rejects prevailing social norms or behaviors.|He was an anticonformist, always challenging the status quo. 他是一个反传统主义者，总是挑战现状。|
|adj.|反传统的；反从众的|Opposed to or rejecting conformity, especially in social behavior.|She had an anticonformist attitude, preferring to stand out rather than fit in. 她有一种反传统的态度，喜欢标新立异而不是随波逐流。|
|近义词:|nonconformist, maverick, individualist, rebel, dissident|
|反义词:|conformist, traditionalist, follower|
|词性:|noun, adjective|
|词源:|anti- (against) + conformist (from conform, meaning to comply with rules or customs)|
