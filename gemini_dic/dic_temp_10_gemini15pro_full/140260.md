offshore's:/ˌɔːfˈʃɔːrz/ | n. |  离岸公司/机构的，离岸业务的 | Something that belongs to or is related to an offshore company or operation. |  The offshore's profits have increased significantly this year. 离岸公司的利润今年显著增长。| 近义词: offshore | 反义词: onshore | 词性: 所有格


offshore:/ˌɔːfˈʃɔːr/ | adj. adv. | 离岸的，近海的；向海 | Situated or operating at a distance from the shore; away from the shore. | Many offshore oil rigs operate in the Gulf of Mexico. 许多离岸石油钻井平台在墨西哥湾运营。| The wind blew offshore. 风吹向海面。 | 近义词: seaward | 反义词: onshore | 词性: 形容词/副词

词源: off + shore. "off" 表示 "远离", "shore" 表示 "海岸".
