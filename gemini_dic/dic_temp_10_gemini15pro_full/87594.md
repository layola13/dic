heterostylism: /ˌhetərəˈstaɪlɪzəm/ | n. | 异型花柱 | The condition of having styles of different lengths in the flowers of different plants of the same species, as a mechanism promoting outcrossing. |  Primroses exhibit heterostylism, with some plants having long styles and others short styles. 报春花表现出异型花柱，一些植物的花柱长，而另一些植物的花柱短。|  heterogony | isostyly | noun
