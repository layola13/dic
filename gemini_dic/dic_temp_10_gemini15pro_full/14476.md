avant:/əˈvɑːnt/ | adj. | 前卫的，先锋的 |  Unusually modern and experimental, especially in the arts, or not following traditional ways of behaving. |  Her fashion designs are quite avant-garde. 她的时装设计相当前卫。| synonyms:  advanced, progressive, innovative, cutting-edge, pioneering, unconventional, unorthodox, experimental | antonyms: traditional, conventional, conservative, orthodox | adjective

avant:/əˈvɑːnt/ | n. | 先锋，前锋 | The advance group in any movement. | The avant-garde of the art world. 艺术界的前锋。 | synonyms: vanguard, forefront, pioneers, innovators | antonyms: rearguard, followers | noun
