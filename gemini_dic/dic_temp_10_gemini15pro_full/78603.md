gibbers:/ˈdʒɪbərz/| n. |胡言乱语，快速而不清晰的话|Meaningless or unintelligible chatter; rapid and indistinct speech. |He was talking such gibberish that I couldn't understand a word. 他说得语无伦次，我一个字也听不懂。|近义词：babble, nonsense, drivel|反义词: sense, clarity |noun
gibber:/ˈdʒɪbər/| v. |快而不清楚地说，胡言乱语|To speak rapidly and indistinctly; to talk nonsense. |The man gibbered incoherently, seemingly lost in his own world.  那个男人语无伦次地说着，似乎沉浸在自己的世界里。|近义词：babble, chatter, ramble|反义词: articulate, enunciate |verb
