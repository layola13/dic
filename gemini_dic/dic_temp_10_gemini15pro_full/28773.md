carnous:/ˈkɑːrnəs/| adj.|肉色的；似肉的；肉质的|Resembling flesh in appearance, texture, or color; fleshy.|The fungus had a carnous texture, similar to a mushroom.|近义词：fleshy, meaty, pulpy|反义词：bony, thin, lean|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *carnōsus*, from *carō* ("flesh") + *-ōsus* (suffix forming adjectives of abundance).
