callisthenics:/ˌkælɪsˈθɛnɪks/| n.|健美操，徒手体操|Callisthenics are exercises done using your own body weight, without equipment. 健美操是利用自身体重进行的锻炼，无需器械。|She practices callisthenics every morning to stay fit. 她每天早上练习健美操以保持健康。|近义词：exercises, workout, bodyweight training|反义词:inactivity, sedentary lifestyle|词性:noun

callisthenic:/ˌkælɪsˈθɛnɪk/| adj.|健美操的，徒手体操的|Callisthenic exercises are a great way to build strength and flexibility. 健美操是增强力量和柔韧性的好方法。|He prefers a callisthenic workout to lifting weights. 他更喜欢徒手体操而不是举重。|近义词： gymnastic|反义词:weight-lifting|词性:adjective
