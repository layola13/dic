outskates:/ˌaʊtˈskeɪts/
verb
胜过滑冰; 滑冰比…好
to skate better or faster than someone
例句：He easily outskates everyone on the team. 他轻松地胜过队里的每一个人。

例句：She outskated her rivals and won the gold medal. 她滑冰胜过对手，赢得了金牌。

近义词：surpasses, outperforms, beats
反义词：loses to, is outskated by
词性：verb
