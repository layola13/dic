allotted: /əˈlɒtɪd/ (UK) /əˈlɑːtɪd/ (US)
|v. |分配的，指定的|Given as a share or portion.  Assigned or designated for a specific purpose.|例句：The teacher allotted each student 30 minutes to present. 老师给每个学生分配了30分钟的演讲时间。|近义词：assigned, allocated, apportioned, designated|反义词: withheld, denied
词性: 动词过去分词形式，用作形容词
