blare: /blɛər/ | n. | 刺耳的声音，喧闹声 | a loud, harsh sound | The blare of the trumpets announced the arrival of the king.  喇叭的喧闹声宣告了国王的到来。 | The sudden blare of the alarm startled everyone. 突然响起的刺耳的警报声吓了大家一跳。| synonyms: blast, din, clamor | antonyms: whisper, murmur | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *blaren, of uncertain origin, possibly from Old Norse *blása (“to blow”).
