compote:/ˈkɒmpəʊt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmpoʊt/ (US)| n. |蜜饯，果盘；炖水果|A dessert of fruit stewed in syrup.  It can also refer to a savory dish of meat or vegetables cooked slowly in syrup.|例句：She served a delicious compote of apples and berries for dessert. 她用苹果和浆果做了一道美味的蜜饯作为甜点。

例句：The pear compote was a perfect ending to the meal.  梨子蜜饯是这顿饭的完美结尾。|近义词：preserve, conserve, stew, fruit salad|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "fresh fruit")|词性: noun
