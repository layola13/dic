morani:/məˈrɑːni/| n.|莫拉尼（马赛族青年武士）|A young warrior of the Maasai people, typically a recently circumcised male who has not yet married and who traditionally dresses in elaborate ornamentation and ochre-dyed hair.|Example 1: The morani stood tall and proud, their ochre-dyed hair gleaming in the sun. 莫拉尼们昂首挺胸，赭石染成的头发在阳光下闪闪发光。

Example 2:  The morani performed a traditional jumping dance, showing off their strength and agility. 莫拉尼表演了传统的跳跃舞蹈，展示了他们的力量和敏捷。|Synonym: warrior, fighter|Antonym: civilian, elder|Noun
