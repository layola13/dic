hijras:/ˈhɪdʒrɑːz/ | n. | 海吉拉斯（印度及南亚地区的跨性别群体） |  A term used in India and South Asia to refer to a transgender community.  Hijras are often assigned male at birth but identify and live as women. They may also include intersex people. |  The hijras have a long and complex history in South Asian culture. 海吉拉斯在南亚文化中有着悠久而复杂的历史。|  In some areas, hijras perform at weddings and births. 在一些地区，海吉拉斯会在婚礼和出生时表演。 | synonyms: transgender people, third gender | antonyms: cisgender | noun
