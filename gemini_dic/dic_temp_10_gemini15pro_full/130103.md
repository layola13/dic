non-protestants:/nɒnˈprɒtɪstənts/ | n. |非新教徒|People who do not belong to a Protestant Christian denomination.  These could include Catholics, Orthodox Christians, Jews, Muslims, Buddhists, atheists, agnostics, and followers of other religions or no religion.|Many non-protestants attended the interfaith service. 许多非新教徒参加了跨宗教的礼拜。|Synonyms: non-Protestant Christians,  people of other faiths|Antonyms: Protestants|noun
