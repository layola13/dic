gyrencephala:/ˌdʒaɪrənˈsɛfələ/ | n. | 脑回动物 | Mammals having a brain with many convolutions, especially the primates and cetaceans. |  Gyrencephala are characterized by their folded brains, which allow for greater surface area and cognitive complexity. 脑回动物的特点是大脑有褶皱，这使得大脑表面积更大，认知更复杂。 | Synonym: convoluted brain animals | Antonym: lissencephala | noun

gyrencephalous:/ˌdʒaɪrənˈsɛfələs/ | adj. | 脑回的 | Having a gyrencephalic brain; possessing a brain with gyri (convolutions). |  Humans are gyrencephalous, meaning their brains have numerous folds. 人类是脑回动物，这意味着他们的大脑有很多褶皱。 | Synonym: convoluted | Antonym: lissencephalic | adjective
