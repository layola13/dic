overcompliance:/ˌoʊvər kəmˈplaɪəns/
noun
过度服从，过度遵从
The act of complying with rules, regulations, or requests to an excessive or unnecessary degree.
Overcompliance with safety regulations can sometimes be counterproductive.  过度遵守安全规定有时会适得其反。
The company's overcompliance with environmental regulations resulted in increased costs.  该公司对环境法规的过度遵守导致成本增加。
synonyms: over-obedience, over-conformity, excessive compliance
antonyms: noncompliance, disobedience, undercompliance
