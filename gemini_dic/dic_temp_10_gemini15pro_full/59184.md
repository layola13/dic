dysluite:/ˈdɪslʊˌaɪt/| n. |透辉石|A brownish-black to dark-brown or grayish-black mineral consisting of zinc aluminate and ferric oxide, occurring in isometric granular masses.| Dysluite is a rare mineral found in metamorphic rocks. 透辉石是一种稀有矿物，发现于变质岩中。|Synonyms: gahnite, spinel|Antonyms: N/A|noun
