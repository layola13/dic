diffusate:/ˈdɪfjuːzeɪt/ /ˈdɪfjuːsɪt/ | n. |弥散物，扩散物|A substance that has diffused, especially one that has passed through a membrane or other barrier. |The diffusate contained a high concentration of glucose. 扩散物中含有高浓度的葡萄糖。|The researchers analyzed the diffusate to determine its composition. 研究人员分析了扩散物以确定其成分。| synonyms: effluent, permeate, dialysate | antonyms: retentate | noun
