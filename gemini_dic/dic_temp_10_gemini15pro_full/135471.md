nonpestilential:/ˌnɒnpɛstɪˈlɛnʃəl/
adjective
无瘟疫的；非传染性的
Not pestilential; not tending to cause a pestilence or infectious disease.
The air in the mountains was cool and nonpestilential. 山里的空气凉爽，没有瘟疫。
Near Antonyms: pestilential, contagious, infectious
Antonyms: healthy, salubrious
adjective

Etymology:
From non- + pestilential.  Pestilential comes from Late Middle English: from Old French pestilentiel, or from late Latin pestilentialis, from Latin pestilentia ‘plague’.
