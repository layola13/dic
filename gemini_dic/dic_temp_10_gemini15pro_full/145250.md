overbleaching:/ˌoʊvərˈbliːtʃɪŋ/
adjective
过度漂白
The excessive bleaching of something, typically resulting in damage or an undesirable appearance.
The stylist overbleached her hair, leaving it dry and brittle.  发型师过度漂白了她的头发，使它变得干燥易碎。
Her teeth looked unnatural from overbleaching. 她的牙齿因为过度漂白而看起来不自然。
近义词: over-whitening, excessive bleaching
反义词: under-bleaching
词性: adjective
