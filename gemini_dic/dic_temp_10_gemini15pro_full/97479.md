ingeniously:/ɪnˈdʒiːniəsli/| adv.|巧妙地，精巧地，独创地|In a clever, original, and inventive manner.|He ingeniously solved the puzzle using a mirror. 他巧妙地用一面镜子解开了谜题。|cleverly, skillfully, inventively, resourcefully|clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly|adverb

词源:
From Late Latin *ingeniōsus, from Latin ingenium (“natural capacity, talent, intelligence”) + -ōsus.
