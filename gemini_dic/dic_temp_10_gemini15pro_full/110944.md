lipse:/lɪps/ | n. | 省略, 椭圆 | An omission or ellipsis of letters or words. | Example: The li pse in the sentence made it difficult to understand. 句子中的省略使得它难以理解。 | Example: The poet used li pse to create a sense of mystery. 诗人用省略来营造一种神秘感。 | Synonym: omission, abbreviation | Antonym: inclusion, addition | noun

lipse (alternative pronunciation):/ɪˈlɪps/ | n. | 椭圆 | A regular oval shape, traced by a point moving in a plane so that the sum of its distances from two other points (the foci) is constant, or resulting when a cone is cut by an oblique plane which does not intersect the base. | Example: The orbit of the planet is an ellipse. 行星的轨道是一个椭圆。 | Example:  Draw an ellipse with a major axis of 5cm and a minor axis of 3cm. 画一个长轴为5厘米，短轴为3厘米的椭圆。 | Synonym: oval | Antonym: circle, square | noun
