cartilaginous:/ˌkɑːrtɪˈlædʒɪnəs/| adj.|软骨的；软骨质的|Composed of cartilage; relating to cartilage.|The shark is a cartilaginous fish.  鲨鱼是一种软骨鱼。| The human ear has a cartilaginous structure. 人耳有一个软骨结构。|近义词：chondral|反义词：bony|词性: adjective

cartilage:/ˈkɑːrtɪlɪdʒ/| n.|软骨|A firm, flexible connective tissue.|词源：Late Middle English: from Old North French cartilage, based on Latin cartilago, from cart- (variant of carr- ‘car’) + suffix -lago.|
