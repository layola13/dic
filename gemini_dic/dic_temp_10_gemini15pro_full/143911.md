outpreach: /ˌaʊtˈpriːtʃ/
verb
胜过布道；在布道方面超过
to surpass in preaching; to preach more or more effectively than
He outpreached all the other ministers in the city with his charismatic sermons. 他以富有魅力的布道胜过城里所有其他牧师。
The young preacher tried to outpreach the bishop, but he failed.  年轻的传教士试图在布道方面超过主教，但他失败了。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel
反义词:  be outdone, be surpassed
词性: verb
