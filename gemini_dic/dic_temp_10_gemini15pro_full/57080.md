dough:/doʊ/ (US) /dəʊ/ (UK)| n.|生面团，钱(俚语)|A mixture of flour, water, and sometimes yeast, fat, or sugar, that is kneaded and baked to make bread, pastries, etc.  (informal) Money.|例句：She kneaded the dough for the bread. 她揉面包面团。|例句：He's got a lot of dough. 他很有钱(俚语)。|近义词：money (俚语), batter, paste|反义词: (无明显的反义词)|词性:名词
