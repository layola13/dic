electro-osmotic:/ɪˌlektroʊ ɒzˈmɒtɪk/ |adj.|电渗的|Relating to or caused by electro-osmosis, the movement of liquid through a porous material under the influence of an electric field.|Electro-osmotic flow is used in microfluidic devices. 电渗流用于微流体装置。|Synonym: electroendosmotic|Antonym: N/A|adjective


electro-osmosis:/ɪˌlektroʊ ɒzˈmoʊsɪs/ |n.|电渗|The movement of a liquid through a porous material under the influence of an electric field across the material.|Electro-osmosis is a useful technique for separating proteins. 电渗是一种用于分离蛋白质的有用技术。|Synonym: electroendosmosis|Antonym: N/A|noun
