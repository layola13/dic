capelin:/ˈkeɪplɪn/| n.|毛鳞鱼|A small, slender fish (Mallotus villosus) of the smelt family, found in large schools in the North Atlantic and North Pacific and used as food and bait.  It is also known as caplin.|例句：Capelin are an important food source for many seabirds. 毛鳞鱼是许多海鸟的重要食物来源。

例句：Fishermen use capelin as bait for cod. 渔民用毛鳞鱼作饵来钓鳕鱼。|近义词：sprat, smelt|反义词:|noun
