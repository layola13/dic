bobbinwork:/ˈbɒbɪnwɜːk/| n.|梭子花边；筒管工艺；绕线工艺|Work created using bobbins and thread, typically lace or similar decorative textiles.  It involves interlacing threads wound on bobbins (small spools) to create intricate patterns.|The tablecloth was adorned with delicate bobbinwork.|Synonyms: lacework, bobbin lace|Antonyms: N/A|noun
