overdrainage:/ˌoʊvərˈdreɪnɪdʒ/
n.|过度排水，排水过度|Excessive removal of water from an area, typically soil, often resulting in negative consequences for the environment or agriculture.|The overdrainage of the peat bog led to its degradation. 泥炭沼泽的过度排水导致其退化。|Over-irrigation can paradoxically lead to overdrainage in certain soil types. 过度灌溉反而会导致某些土壤类型的过度排水。|近义词：excessive drainage|反义词：underdrainage|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From 'over-' (prefix indicating excess) + 'drainage' (noun from 'drain' - to draw off liquid gradually).  'Drain' ultimately comes from the Old English 'drehnigean,'  meaning "to make dry," related to the Old Norse 'draugr' meaning "drying."
