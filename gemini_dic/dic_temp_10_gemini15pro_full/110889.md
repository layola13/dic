lip-like:/ˈlɪpˌlaɪk/
adjective
唇状的; 嘴唇似的
Resembling or suggesting lips in shape or form.
The orchid has a lip-like petal that attracts insects.  这株兰花有一片唇状的花瓣，可以吸引昆虫。
The petals were a delicate pink, almost lip-like in their softness.  这些花瓣是淡粉色的，柔软得几乎像嘴唇一样。
近义词:labial
反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly 'un-lip-like' in informal contexts)
词性:adjective
