hyalography:/ˌhaɪəˈlɒɡrəfi/
n.
透明质酸造影术
Radiographic examination of a joint cavity after injection of a radiopaque contrast medium into the joint.
Hyalography is used to assess the integrity of the articular cartilage and menisci. 透明质酸造影术用于评估关节软骨和半月板的完整性。
Hyalography can detect joint abnormalities that may not be visible on plain radiographs. 透明质酸造影术可以检测到普通X光片上可能看不到的关节异常。

近义词:arthrography (关节造影术)
反义词: 无

词性: noun
