canoness:/ˈkænənɪs/ | n. | 女教士；女律修会会长；（尤指科幻作品中的）女战士 | A woman who holds a position of authority in a religious order, especially a female superior in a convent of canonesses.  Often used in science fiction to denote a female warrior, particularly in the Warhammer 40,000 universe. | The canoness led her troops into battle. 女教士带领她的部队投入战斗。 |  The Sisters of Battle canoness wielded her power sword with righteous fury. 戰鬥修女團的女教士以正義之怒揮舞著她的動力劍。 | synonyms: prioress, abbess, superior, (in sci-fi context) warrior, commander | antonyms:  novice, follower (in general sense) | noun
