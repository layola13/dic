biodiversification:/ˌbaɪoʊdaɪˌvɜːrsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 生物多样化 | The process by which the diversity of life increases, typically through the evolution of new species.  | Example:  The biodiversification of the rainforest is astonishing.  雨林的生物多样性令人惊叹。| Synonym: diversification of life,  biological diversification | Antonym:  bio-homogenization, biotic homogenization | noun
