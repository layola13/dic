farers:/ˈfɛərərz/ | n. | 旅行者；航海者 | People who travel, especially by sea or air. |  Ancient mariners were skilled farers, navigating by the stars. 古代水手都是熟练的航海家，靠星星导航。

The farers set sail for a new land. 旅行者们启航前往一片新的土地。 | 近义词: travelers, voyagers, journeyers | 反义词: stay-at-homes, residents | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old English *faran "to journey, travel," related to German fahren "to drive, travel," from Proto-Germanic *faranan, from PIE root *per- (2) "to lead, pass over."  The "-er" suffix denotes "one who does something."
