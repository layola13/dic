lossy:/ˈlɒsi/ (UK) /ˈlɔːsi/ (US)| adj.|有损的，失真的|Characterized by the loss of data or quality, especially in data compression.|例句：Lossy compression reduces file size but sacrifices some image quality. 有损压缩减小了文件大小，但牺牲了一些图像质量。

例句：MP3 is a lossy audio format. MP3是一种有损音频格式。


近义词：imperfect, degraded, compressed|反义词:lossless, uncompressed|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From loss + -y.  Relatively recent, becoming common with the rise of digital media.
