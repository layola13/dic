oedematic:/iːdɪˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 水肿的，肿胀的 | Relating to or affected by edema; characterized by swelling due to excessive accumulation of fluid in the tissues. | The patient's legs were oedematic due to heart failure. 病人的腿因心力衰竭而水肿。 |  The oedematic tissues were painful to the touch.  水肿的组织一碰就疼。 | synonyms: swollen, puffy, bloated, edematous | antonyms: shrunk, shriveled, atrophied | adjective
