crabbinesses: /ˈkræbinəsɪz/
| n. |暴躁，易怒，脾气坏 (plural of crabbiness)|The constant rain and delays led to increasing crabbinesses among the travelers. 持續的雨天和延誤導致旅客們越來越暴躁。|  Multiple instances of a bad-tempered or irritable state.  指多次出现脾气暴躁或易怒的状态。|例句：The children's crabbinesses were exacerbated by hunger and tiredness. 孩子们的暴躁是由饥饿和疲劳加剧的。

|近义词：irritability, grumpiness, bad temper, ill humor, surliness, crossness |反义词：good humor, cheerfulness, pleasantness, amiability |词性: 名词
