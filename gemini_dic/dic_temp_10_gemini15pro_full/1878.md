acrostically: /əˈkrɒstɪkli/ /əˈkrɑːstɪkli/ | adv. | 以离合诗体; 以藏头诗体 |  In the manner of an acrostic; so as to form an acrostic.  The first letters of each line spell out a word or phrase. |  He wrote his girlfriend's name acrostically in his Valentine's Day poem. 他在情人节诗中以藏头诗的方式写了他女朋友的名字。

She arranged the flowers acrostically to spell out "I love you". 她将花朵排列成藏头诗，拼出“我爱你”。 | 近义词: N/A (as an adverb describing a specific form of writing) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adverb
