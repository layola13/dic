amid:/əˈmɪd/ | prep. | 在...中间，在...之中，被...包围 | surrounded by; in the middle of |  The flowers bloomed amid the ruins.  这些花在废墟中盛开。| amidst, among, amongst | outside, beyond | 介词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *amiddes, from Old English on middan "in the middle," from on "on" + middan (dative of middel "middle").
