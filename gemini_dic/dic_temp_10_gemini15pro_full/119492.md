microparasite:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈpærəsaɪt/ | n. |微寄生物，微型寄生虫 |A parasite, such as a bacterium or virus, that multiplies within its host, often inside its cells. |Malaria is caused by a microparasite transmitted by mosquitoes. 疟疾是由蚊子传播的一种微寄生物引起的。| Microparasites reproduce directly within their definitive host, generally within the host's cells. 微寄生物在其最终宿主体内直接繁殖，通常在宿主细胞内。| synonym: endoparasite, intracellular parasite | antonym: macroparasite | noun
