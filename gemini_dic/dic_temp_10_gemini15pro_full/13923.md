aulic: /ˈɔːlɪk/ | adj. | 宫廷的；朝廷的 | Relating to a royal court or to the emperor's court. | The aulic council advised the emperor on matters of state.  宫廷会议就国家事务向皇帝提供建议。|  例句：The aulic council advised the emperor on matters of state. 宫廷会议就国家事务向皇帝提供建议。

例句：The aulic chamber had opulent decorations.  朝廷的房间装饰华丽。 | 近义词：courtly, royal, imperial | 反义词：common, plebeian | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *aulic*, from Latin *aulicus*, from *aula* ("court, palace"). Ultimately from Ancient Greek *αὐλή* (*aulḗ*, “courtyard, hall”).
