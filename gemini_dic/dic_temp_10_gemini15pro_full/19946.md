biodegrading:/ˌbaɪoʊdɪˈɡreɪdɪŋ/
adjective
生物降解的
Undergoing the process of decomposition by natural biological agents, such as bacteria or fungi.
Example 1:  Biodegrading plastics are a promising solution to pollution. 生物降解塑料是解决污染的一个很有希望的方案。

Example 2: The company is developing new biodegrading packaging materials.  这家公司正在开发新的生物降解包装材料。
近义词: decomposing, biodegradable, composting
反义词: non-biodegradable, persistent
词性: adjective
