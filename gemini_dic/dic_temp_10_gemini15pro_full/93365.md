illegitimately:/ˌɪlɪˈdʒɪtɪmətli/
adv.
非法地，不合法地
in a way that is not allowed by law or the rules
He acquired the property illegitimately. 他非法获得了这处房产。
Synonyms: unlawfully, illegally, wrongly, improperly
Antonyms: legally, lawfully, legitimately
词性: 副词

illegitimate:/ˌɪlɪˈdʒɪtɪmət/
adj.
非法的，私生的
not allowed by law or the rules; born to parents not married to each other
The government condemned the illegitimate use of force. 政府谴责非法使用武力。
The king had several illegitimate children. 国王有几个私生子。
Synonyms: unlawful, illegal, bastard, unauthorized
Antonyms: legal, legitimate, authorized
词性: 形容词

词源:
From Latin illegitimus "unlawful," from in- "not" + legitimus "lawful."
