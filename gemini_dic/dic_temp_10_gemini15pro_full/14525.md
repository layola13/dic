averagely: /ˈævərɪdʒli/ | adv. | 平均地，一般地 | In a way that is not special or unusual; ordinarily. | He performed averagely in the exam. 他在考试中表现平平。| The food was averagely priced.  这食物的价格中等。 | 近义词: ordinarily, typically, normally, commonly, usually | 反义词: exceptionally, unusually, remarkably, extraordinarily | 词性: 副词

词源: From average + -ly.  "Average" comes from Old French "avarie," meaning "damage to ship or cargo," which in turn comes from Italian "avaria."  The sense shifted from shared financial loss to a general mean or median.
