cockaleekie:/ˌkɒkəˈliːki/| n.|苏格兰鸡汤|A traditional Scottish soup made with chicken, leeks, and often prunes or raisins.|My grandmother's cockaleekie is the best I've ever tasted. 我祖母做的苏格兰鸡汤是我吃过最好吃的。|Cock-a-leekie soup is a hearty and flavorful dish. 苏格兰鸡汤是一道丰盛而美味的菜肴。|近义词:chicken soup, broth|反义词:|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Scots *cock-a-leeky* "chicken and leek soup," from *cock* "fowl" + *leek* + *-ie*, diminutive suffix.
