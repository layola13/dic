half-wicket:/ˌhɑːf ˈwɪkɪt/ | n. | 半场板球赛; 半场门球 | A form of cricket played on a reduced pitch with fewer players, often for informal or practice purposes. It can also refer to the fall of five wickets in an innings. |  The village team often played half-wicket matches on summer evenings. 村里球队经常在夏夜进行半场板球赛。| Synonyms: limited-overs cricket, five-a-side cricket | Antonyms: full-wicket, test match | noun
half-wicket:/ˌhɑːf ˈwɪkɪt/ | n. | 五个门球的倒下 | The fall of five wickets in an innings. | England were 150 for 5 - half-wicket down. 英格兰队150分，五个门球倒下。| Synonyms: five down | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "all out") | noun
