chronologist: /krəˈnɒlədʒɪst/ /krəˈnɑːlədʒɪst/
n.|年代学家；编年史家|A person who studies or specializes in chronology; a person who arranges events in the order of their occurrence.|The chronologist meticulously documented the dynasty's reign.|近义词: historian, annalist|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin chronologista, from Ancient Greek χρόνος (khrónos, “time”) + -λόγος (-lógos, from λέγω (légō, “I say”)).
