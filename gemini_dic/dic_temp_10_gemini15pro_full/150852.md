paragons:/ˈpærəɡɒnz/ (UK), /ˈpærəɡɑːnz/ (US)| n.| 模范；典范；完美的人或事物|a person or thing regarded as a perfect example of a particular quality.|例句：She is a paragon of virtue. 她是有德行的典范。|例句：This car is a paragon of engineering excellence. 这辆车是工程学的典范。|近义词：model, exemplar, ideal, epitome|反义词：imperfection, flaw, blemish|词性: 名词
