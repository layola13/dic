band:/bænd/ | v. | 联合；结合；用带子绑扎 | To join together in a group or to tie something with a band. |  The survivors banded together for warmth. 幸存者们聚在一起取暖。 | synonyms: unite, combine, join, league, ally | antonyms: separate, divide | verb
