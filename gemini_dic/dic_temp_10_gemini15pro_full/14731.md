awayward:/əˈweɪwərd/
adjective
任性的，难以控制的；迷路的；不定的
disobedient, rebellious, and difficult to control; going in the wrong direction or to the wrong place.
The wayward child refused to listen to his parents.  这个任性的孩子拒绝听父母的话。
The ship was blown wayward by the storm.  这艘船被暴风雨吹得偏离了航线。
synonyms: disobedient, rebellious, unruly, headstrong, willful, contrary, perverse, difficult, troublesome, errant, wandering, straying, lost, off course, off track, aimless
antonyms: obedient, compliant, docile, well-behaved, on track
