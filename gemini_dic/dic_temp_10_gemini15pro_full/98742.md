insurrectionisms: /ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃənɪzəmz/
n.
叛乱主义，起义主义
The act of engaging in multiple insurrections or rebellions; the advocacy or promotion of such acts.  Multiple acts of revolt against civil authority or the established government.
The historian specialized in the study of 18th-century insurrectionisms across Europe.  这位历史学家专门研究18世纪整个欧洲的叛乱活动。
The government cracked down on all forms of insurrectionisms. 政府镇压了所有形式的叛乱活动。
Synonyms: rebellions, revolts, uprisings
Antonyms: obedience, submission, loyalty
词性: noun
