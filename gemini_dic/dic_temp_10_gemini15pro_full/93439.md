illuminational:/ɪˌluːmɪˈneɪʃənəl/
adj.|照明用的，启发的|Relating to illumination; providing enlightenment.|The illuminational design of the stage created a magical atmosphere. 舞台的照明设计营造了神奇的氛围。|近义词: enlightening, illuminating, clarifying|反义词: obscuring, darkening|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *illuminacioun*, from Latin *illuminatio*, from *illuminare* "to light up, enlighten," from *in-* "in" + *luminare* "to light," from *lumen* "light."
