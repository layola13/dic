garishness:/ˈɡɛərɪʃnəs/
noun
华而不实；俗丽；炫耀
The quality of being excessively bright and showy, typically in a way that is considered tasteless.
The garishness of the decorations was overwhelming.  装饰的俗丽令人难以忍受。
近义词: gaudiness, flashiness, tawdriness
反义词: subtlety, understatement, elegance
词性: noun

garish:/ˈɡɛərɪʃ/
adjective
俗丽的；花哨的；炫耀的
Excessively bright and showy, typically in a way that is considered tasteless.
She wore a garish pink dress. 她穿了一件俗气的粉红色连衣裙。
近义词: gaudy, flashy, tawdry
反义词: subtle, understated, elegant
词性: adjective
