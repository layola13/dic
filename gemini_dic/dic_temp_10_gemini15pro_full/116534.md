mathematics:/ˌmæθəˈmætɪks/ | n. |数学| The abstract science of number, quantity, and space, either as abstract concepts (pure mathematics), or as applied to other disciplines such as physics and engineering (applied mathematics). |例句：Mathematics is a fundamental subject for many scientific fields. 数学是许多科学领域的基础学科。

Mathematics is the language of the universe. 数学是宇宙的语言。|近义词: calculation, computation |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French mathematiques (plural), from Latin mathematica (plural), from Greek mathēmatikē (epistēmē) "(art or science) of mathematics," feminine of mathēmatikos "relating to learning, disposed to learn," from manthanein "to learn."
