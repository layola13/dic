boysenberries:/ˈbɔɪzənˌberiz/ | n. | 男孩森莓，波森莓 | A large, reddish-purple berry, similar to a blackberry, with a tart flavor. It is a hybrid of the blackberry, raspberry, and loganberry. | I made a boysenberry pie for dessert. 我做了一个波森莓派作为甜点。 | Boysenberries are my favorite berry to eat in the summer.  夏天我最喜欢吃波森莓了。 | 近义词：blackberries, raspberries, loganberries | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: Named after Rudolph Boysen, an American horticulturist who developed the berry in the early 20th century.
