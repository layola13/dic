blitzkrieg:/ˈblɪts.kriːɡ/| n.|闪电战|A swift, intensive form of warfare, especially using tanks and aircraft, first used by the Germans in World War II.|The German blitzkrieg quickly overwhelmed Poland. 德军的闪电战迅速击溃了波兰。|近义词：onslaught, blitz|反义词:stalemate|词性:名词

blitzkill:/ˈblɪts.kɪl/| n. vt.|闪电杀戮，快速击杀|Derived from "blitzkrieg," it refers to a swift and decisive defeat or elimination, often in gaming or competitive contexts.|The team executed a perfect blitzkill, wiping out the enemy base in minutes.  这支队伍执行了一次完美的闪电杀戮，在几分钟内摧毁了敌人的基地。| He blitzkilled his opponent in the chess tournament. 他在国际象棋锦标赛中快速击败了对手。|近义词：rout, annihilation, decimation|反义词:prolong, draw out|词性:名词, 动词
