diphyodont:/daɪˈfaɪəˌdɑːnt/| adj. n.|二齿性的，有两副牙齿的（动物）|Having two sets of teeth, one succeeding the other, as in most mammals.|Humans are diphyodont, meaning we have a set of baby teeth and a set of adult teeth.|近义词: heterodont|反义词: monophyodont|词性: adjective, noun


diphyodont:/daɪˈfaɪəˌdɑːnt/| n.|二齿动物|An animal having two successive sets of teeth.|Most mammals, including humans, are diphyodonts.|近义词: heterodont|反义词: monophyodont|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *di* (two) + *phuo* (to grow, produce) + *odous, odont-* (tooth).
