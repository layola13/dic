convivialist:/kənˈvɪviəlist/
noun
好交际的人，宴饮者
A person who enjoys convivial company or who is fond of feasting and merrymaking.
She was a renowned convivialist, always hosting lively dinner parties. 她是一位有名的社交家，总是举办热闹的晚宴。
The convivialist regaled his guests with stories and jokes.  这位好交际的人用故事和笑话款待他的客人。

近义词:socializer, bon vivant, party animal, extrovert, gregarious person
反义词:recluse, introvert, loner, hermit, wallflower

词性:n.

词源: From convivial + -ist.  Convivial from Latin convivialis "festal, social,"  from com vivere "to live" + com- "together."
