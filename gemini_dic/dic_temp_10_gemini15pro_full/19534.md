biflorous:/baɪˈflɔːrəs/
adjective
二花的；有两朵花的
Having or bearing two flowers.
The orchid is biflorous, displaying two delicate blossoms on a single stem. 这株兰花是二花的，一根茎上开着两朵精致的花朵。
The botanist identified the rare biflorous specimen.  植物学家鉴定出这株稀有的二花标本。
近义词: two-flowered
反义词: multiflorous, uniflorous
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *biflorus*, from bi- (two) + -florus (flowered), from flos (flower).
