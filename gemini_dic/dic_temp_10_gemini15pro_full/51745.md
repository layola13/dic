devolve:/dɪˈvɒlv/ /diˈvɑːlv/| v. |下放，移交，转让(权力、责任等);  衰落，退化|The federal government devolved responsibility for education to the states. |联邦政府将教育责任下放给各州。|近义词：delegate, transfer, pass down, deteriorate, degenerate|反义词：centralize, concentrate, improve, progress|词性:动词

devolved:/dɪˈvɒlvd/ /diˈvɑːlvd/| adj. |已下放的，已移交的|devolved powers 已下放的权力|The UK has a devolved government in Scotland. |英国在苏格兰有一个权力下放的政府。|近义词：delegated, transferred|反义词：centralized|词性:形容词


词源: From Latin *dēvolvere, from dē (“down from, away from”) + volvere (“to roll”).
