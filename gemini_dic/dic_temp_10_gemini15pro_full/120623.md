mis-cipher:/ˌmɪsˈsaɪfər/ | vt. | 错误地编码，解码，误译 | To encode or decode incorrectly, resulting in a garbled or meaningless message.  | He mis-ciphered the message, leading to a disastrous misunderstanding. 他错误地解码了信息，导致了灾难性的误解。 | misinterpret, misread, decode incorrectly | interpret correctly, decipher correctly | verb

Etymology:
From mis- (prefix meaning "wrongly, badly") + cipher (from Old French cifre, ultimately from Arabic ṣifr "zero," meaning a secret way of writing).
