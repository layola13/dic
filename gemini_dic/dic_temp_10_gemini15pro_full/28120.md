capitalnesses: /ˈkæpɪtəlnəsɪz/ | n. | 资本性，资本状态 | The state or quality of being capital; capital nature. This word is rarely used and may be considered non-standard. |  Due to its rarity, constructing natural examples is difficult.  A contrived example: "The capitalnesses of the investments were assessed." 资本投资的性质得到了评估。|Near synonyms: capitalization, capital nature | Antonyms:  insignificance, unimportance | noun
