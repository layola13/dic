lugubriously:/luːˈɡuːbriəsli/ | adv. | 悲哀地，忧郁地 | In a mournful or gloomy manner; sadly. | He shook his head lugubriously. 他悲哀地摇了摇头。| She sighed lugubriously as she looked at the pile of work. 她看着成堆的工作，忧郁地叹了口气。| 近义词：mournfully, sadly, gloomily, dolefully | 反义词：cheerfully, happily, joyfully | 词性: 副词

lugubrious:/luːˈɡuːbriəs/ | adj. | 悲哀的，忧郁的 | Looking or sounding sad and dismal. | He had a lugubrious expression on his face. 他脸上带着忧郁的表情。| The lugubrious sound of the cello filled the concert hall.  大提琴忧郁的声音充满了音乐厅。 | 近义词：mournful, sad, gloomy, doleful | 反义词：cheerful, happy, joyful | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *lūgubris* (“mournful, doleful”), from *lūgēre* (“to mourn”).
