crusader:/kruːˈseɪdər/| n.|十字军战士；改革者；拥护者；积极的倡导者|A person who campaigns vigorously for political, social, or religious change; a participant in one of the medieval Crusades.|例句：He was a crusader for civil rights. 他是一位民权运动的斗士。

例句：She is a crusader against animal cruelty. 她是一位反对虐待动物的斗士。|近义词：campaigner, advocate, reformer, champion|反义词：opponent, antagonist|词性: 名词
