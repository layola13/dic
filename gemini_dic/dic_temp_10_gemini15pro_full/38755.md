condonable:/kənˈdoʊnəbl/
adjective
可原谅的，可宽恕的
That can be condoned or overlooked; excusable.
His behavior was hardly condonable. 他的行为很难被原谅。
近义词: excusable, forgivable, pardonable, justifiable
反义词: inexcusable, unforgivable, unpardonable, unjustifiable
词性: adjective
