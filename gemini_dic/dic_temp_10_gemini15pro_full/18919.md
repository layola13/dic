besought:/bɪˈsɔːt/
verb
恳求；祈求
Asked (someone) earnestly or anxiously to do something.
The man besought the king for mercy.  这个男人恳求国王开恩。
He besought her to change her mind. 他恳求她改变主意。
近义词: implored, begged, pleaded, entreated
反义词: demanded, commanded, ordered
词性: verb (动词)

词源:
From Middle English *bisechen*, from Old English *besēcan* (“to beseech, implore, beg, ask, seek, require, demand”), from *be-* (intensive prefix) + *sēcan* (“to seek, search for, look for, visit, inquire”). Cognate with Dutch *bezoeken* (“to visit”), German *besuchen* (“to visit”).
