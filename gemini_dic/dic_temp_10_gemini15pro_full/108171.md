latrines:/ləˈtriːnz/
n.
厕所，茅坑
A toilet or outhouse, especially a communal one in a camp or barracks.
The soldiers dug latrines a safe distance from the camp. 士兵们在离营地安全距离的地方挖了厕所。
toilets, restrooms, lavatories
