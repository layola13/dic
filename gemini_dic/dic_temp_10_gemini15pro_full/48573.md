defay: /dɪˈfeɪ/ | v. | 击败，战胜 | To defeat or overcome, especially in a contest or competition. | He defayed his opponent in the final round. 他在最后一轮击败了对手。|  She defayed all expectations and won the race. 她战胜了所有人的预期，赢得了比赛。 | 近义词: defeat, conquer, overcome, vanquish | 反义词: lose, surrender, yield | 词性: 动词

词源：From Middle English *defeien, *defayen, from Anglo-Norman defeire, defaire, Old French desfaire (“to undo, destroy, defeat”), from des- (“dis-”) + faire (“to do, make”). Doublet of defeat.
