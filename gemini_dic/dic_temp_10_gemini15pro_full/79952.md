go-about:/ɡəʊ əˈbaʊt/| phrasal verb |着手；从事；忙于；处理；四处走动|To begin or carry out (a task or activity), especially in a determined or purposeful way. Also, to move from place to place, especially to socialize or conduct business.|例句：He went about his work with quiet efficiency. 他平静而高效地开展工作。|例句：She went about the town, visiting all her friends. 她走遍了全镇，拜访了所有的朋友。|近义词：undertake, commence, approach, tackle, circulate|反义词：neglect, abandon, avoid, shirk|词性：动词短语

词源：From Middle English *gon aboute, from Old English gān abūtan (“to go about, surround, perambulate”).  "Go" retains its original meaning of movement, while "about" indicates a general or encompassing activity or movement.
