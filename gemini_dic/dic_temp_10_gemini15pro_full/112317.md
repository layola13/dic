lords:/lɔːrdz/| n.| 贵族；君主；上帝；（Lord）用作称呼语 |A person of high rank in a feudal society or in one that retains feudal characteristics, especially a British nobleman.  The male head of a household or family. God or Christ. Used as a form of address to certain British officials, judges, and noblemen.| The lords of the realm assembled at the castle.  这个王国的贵族们聚集在城堡里。| synonyms: nobleman, peer, aristocrat, noble, grandee | antonyms: commoner, peasant | noun

lords:/lɔːrdz/| v.|  (lord it over)  作威作福；称王称霸 | (lord it over) Act in a domineering way over (someone).| He lords it over his employees. 他对他的员工作威作福。| synonyms: dominate, domineer, tyrannize, rule, control | antonyms: serve, obey | verb
