machineabilities:/məˌʃiːnəˈbɪlətiz/| n.|可加工性(复数)|The ease with which a material can be machined.  This encompasses factors like how easily it can be cut, drilled, shaped, and finished without excessive tool wear or damage to the workpiece.|The machineabilities of these new alloys are superior to those of traditional materials. 这些新合金的可加工性优于传统材料。|近义词:workability, machinability|反义词:unworkability|词性:名词

machineability:/məˌʃiːnəˈbɪləti/| n.|可加工性(单数)|The ease with which a material can be machined.
