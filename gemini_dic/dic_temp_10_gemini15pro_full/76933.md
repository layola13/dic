gaper:/ˈɡeɪpər/| n. | 呆呆地看的人；张口结舌的人；裂口；张开|Someone who stares open-mouthed, typically in amazement or wonder.|The tourists were gapers, staring at the unusual sight.  游客们都张大了嘴，呆呆地看着这奇特的景象。|Synonyms: gawker, onlooker, rubbernecker, starer | Antonyms: ignoramus|noun
gaper:| n. |裂口；开口|A wide opening or gap.|The gaper in the fence allowed the dog to escape. 篱笆上的裂口让狗逃跑了。|Synonyms: opening, gap, crack, fissure|Antonyms: closure, seal|noun
