outyelling:/ˌaʊtˈjɛlɪŋ/
verb
大声喊叫，盖过…的喊叫声
The two children were outyelling each other, each trying to get their mother's attention.  两个孩子互相大声喊叫，都想引起妈妈的注意。
The street vendor was outyelling the traffic noise, trying to sell his wares.  街头小贩大声叫卖，试图盖过交通噪音。
近义词: shouting over, drowning out
反义词: whispering, murmuring
词性: v.

Etymology:
From "out-" (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + "yell" (to shout loudly).  "Yell" comes from Old English "giellan" and is related to Old High German "gellan" (to sound loudly).
