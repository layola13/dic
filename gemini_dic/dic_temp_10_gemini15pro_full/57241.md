downily:/ˈdaʊnɪli/| adv.| 绒毛地，柔和地，沮丧地|Covered or resembling down; softly; in a depressed manner.|The goslings were downily covered in yellow fluff. 小鹅身上覆盖着柔软的黄色绒毛。|近义词：softly, fluffily, dejectedly|反义词：harshly, roughly, cheerfully|词性: 副词

Etymology:
From down (n.) + -y (suffix forming adjectives) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The word "down" referring to soft feathers ultimately comes from Old Norse *dūnn.
