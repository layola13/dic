convocationally:/ˌkɒnvəˈkeɪʃənəli/
adverb
以集会方式地; 以召集方式地
in a manner relating to or characteristic of a convocation or assembly
The matter was discussed convocationally. 这件事以集会的方式进行了讨论。
近义词: collectively, jointly, assembly-wise
反义词: individually, separately, privately
词性: adverb

词源:
From convocation + -al + -ly.  "Convocation" comes from Late Latin *convocātiōn- (stem of convocātiō ), from Latin convocātus (past participle of convocāre  "to call together"), from con- "together" + vocāre  "to call."
