domesman:/ˈdoʊmz.mən/| n. | 家臣；王室家臣；法官；审判官 | A man holding a position of authority in a royal household or a judge.  A historical term referring to a judge or member of a king's household. |  The king's domesmen were entrusted with upholding the law. 国王的法官们被委以维护法律的重任。|  The domesman delivered a harsh sentence. 法官作出了严厉的判决。|Synonyms: courtier, retainer, judge, magistrate | Antonyms: commoner, subject, defendant| noun
