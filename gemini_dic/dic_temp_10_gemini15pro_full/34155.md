clamation:/kləˈmeɪʃən/
n.
大声喊叫；强烈抗议；感叹；惊呼
A sudden loud utterance, especially in protest or alarm; an exclamation.
The audience erupted in clamations of delight. 观众爆发出欢呼声。
近义词: outcry, exclamation, shout, yell
反义词: whisper, murmur
词性: 名词
