blendwater:/ˈblendˌwɔːtər/ | n. |混合水，掺水| Water mixed with another substance, often for a specific purpose, such as diluting a drink or creating a solution. |  I added some blendwater to the whiskey to make it less strong. 我在威士忌里加了些掺水，使其不那么烈。

The artist used a blendwater of acrylic paints to achieve the desired shade. 艺术家使用丙烯酸颜料的混合水来达到预期的色调。 | 近义词: mixture, solution, dilution | 反义词:  pure water, concentrate | 词性: noun


词源:  blend (v.) + water (n.)  "blend" comes from Old Norse "blanda" meaning "to mix."
