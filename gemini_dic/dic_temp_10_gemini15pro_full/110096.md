licitation: /ˌlɪsɪˈteɪʃən/
n.
招标；投标；询价；恳求
The process of soliciting or inviting bids, offers, or proposals, typically for a contract or project.
The city issued a licitation for the construction of a new bridge.  该市就新桥的建设发布了招标公告。
The company submitted its bid in response to the licitation.  该公司提交了投标书以回应招标。
近义词: solicitation, invitation, bid, tender, request
反义词: offer, proposal
