anthologises:/ænˈθɒlədʒaɪzɪz/
verb
选集化；将…编成选集
To compile into an anthology; to include in an anthology.
This book anthologises the best poems of the 20th century. 这本书收录了20世纪的最佳诗歌。
The editor carefully anthologised the writer's short stories. 编辑仔细地将这位作家的短篇小说编成选集。
synonyms: compile, collect, gather, assemble
antonyms: disperse, scatter, separate
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From anthology + -ize.  "Anthology" comes from the Greek "anthologia" meaning "collection of flowers" or "garland of flowers," figuratively a collection of literary pieces.  "-ize" is a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become; to subject to; to engage in."
