echinaceas:/ˌɛkɪˈneɪsiəz/ | n. | 松果菊；紫锥菊 | A genus of herbaceous flowering plants in the daisy family, native to eastern and central North America.  Several species are used in herbal medicine. |  Echinaceas are popular in gardens for their vibrant colors. 松果菊因其鲜艳的颜色在花园中很受欢迎。

Echinaceas are believed by some to boost the immune system.  有些人认为松果菊可以增强免疫系统。 | 近义词: coneflowers | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a plant genus) | 词性: noun
