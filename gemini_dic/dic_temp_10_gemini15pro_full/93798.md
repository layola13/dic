immersible:/ɪˈmɜːsəbl/ /ɪˈmɜːrsəbl/ | adj. | 可浸入的；可沉入的；能沉浸的 | Able to be immersed in liquid.  Able to be deeply absorbed or involved in something. |  The device is completely immersible in water.  这个装置可以完全浸入水中。

The book is so immersible, I lost track of time. 这本书太引人入胜了，我都忘了时间。 | 近义词：submersible, submergible | 反义词：nonsubmersible | 词性: 形容词

immersible:/ɪˈmɜːsəbl/ /ɪˈmɜːrsəbl/ | n. |  可浸入的装置或设备 |  A device or piece of equipment that can be immersed. | The immersible pump is designed for underwater use.  这种潜水泵是为水下使用而设计的。| 近义词：submersible | 反义词：N/A | 词性: 名词 (较少使用)
