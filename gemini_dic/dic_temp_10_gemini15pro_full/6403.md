amphipodan:/ˈæmfɪpɒdən/ (US) /ˈæmfɪpoʊdən/| n. |片脚类动物|A small crustacean of the order Amphipoda, typically having a laterally compressed body and seven pairs of legs.  They are found in marine and freshwater habitats.|例句：The amphipod scurried across the seabed. 这只片脚类动物快速穿过海底。

例句：Amphipods are an important food source for many fish. 片脚类动物是许多鱼类的重要食物来源。|近义词：scud, sideswimmer|反义词:|noun
