monohemerous:/ˌmɒnəˈhɛmərəs/| adj.|一日生的，持续一天的|Lasting or occurring for only a single day.  Relating to a fever or illness that lasts for only one day.|The monohemerous flower bloomed brilliantly, then withered away by nightfall.|近义词：ephemeral, transient, fleeting|反义词:perennial, enduring, persistent|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Greek *mono-* "one" + *hēmera* "day"
