hydraulicity:/ˌhaɪdrɔːˈlɪsɪti/ | n. | 水力性，液压性 | The property of being operated by the force of liquid, especially water.  The hydraulicity of the system ensured efficient power transfer. | 例句：The dam's hydraulicity allows for the generation of clean energy. 大坝的水力性使其能够产生清洁能源。

例句：The hydraulicity of the fluid is a key factor in its performance. 液体的水力性是其性能的关键因素。 | 近义词：hydraulic capacity, hydraulic performance | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "inertness" in certain contexts) | 词性: noun
