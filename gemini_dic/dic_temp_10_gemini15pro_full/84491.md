haltless:/ˈhɔːltləs/| adj.|不停的，无休止的|Without halt or pause; incessant; continuous.|The haltless rain continued for days.  无休止的雨持续了好几天。|近义词:incessant, continuous, unremitting, ceaseless|反义词:intermittent, sporadic, occasional|词性:形容词

Etymology: From halt + -less.  "Halt" comes from Middle English *halten, from Old English *healdan "to hold, keep, guard," related to Old High German haltan "to hold."  The suffix "-less" means "without."
