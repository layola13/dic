non-intervene:/ˌnɒnˌɪntəˈviːn/ | adj. | 不干预的，不介入的 | Not intervening; characterized by a lack of intervention. | The UN adopted a non-intervene policy in the initial stages of the conflict. 联合国在冲突初期采取了不干预政策。| Synonyms: noninterventionist, hands-off, neutral | Antonyms: interventionist, intrusive, meddling | adjective

non-intervention:/ˌnɒnˌɪntəˈvenʃən/ | n. | 不干预，不介入 | The policy or practice of not intervening in the affairs of other countries or other people's business. | The principle of non-intervention is enshrined in the UN Charter.  不干预原则是载入联合国宪章的。| Synonyms: noninterference, neutrality | Antonyms: intervention, interference | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not".
intervene (verb): from Latin intervenire "to come between, interrupt," from inter- "between" + venire "to come."
