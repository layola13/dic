humiliators:/hjuːˈmɪlieɪtərz/
|n.|羞辱者，使人蒙羞的人|People who humiliate others; those who cause someone to feel ashamed or lose respect.|The bullies were cruel humiliators, preying on the weaker students. 这些恃强凌弱者是残酷的羞辱者，专门欺负弱小的学生。|Synonyms: degraders, belittlers, mortifyers|Antonyms: encouragers, supporters, uplifters|noun
