architect: /ˈɑːrkɪtekt/ (US), /ˈɑːkɪtekt/ (UK) | n. | 建筑师；设计师；缔造者 | A person who designs buildings and in many cases also supervises their construction. |  The architect designed a beautiful and functional building.  这位建筑师设计了一座既美观又实用的建筑。

The architect of this policy deserves praise. 这项政策的制定者值得称赞。


|近义词：designer, planner, builder|反义词: demolisher, destroyer |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French architecte, from Latin architectus, from Ancient Greek ἀρχιτέκτων (arkhitéktōn), from ἀρχι- (arkhi-, “chief”) + τέκτων (téktōn, “builder, carpenter”).
