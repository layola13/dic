delignate:/diːˈlɪɡneɪt/ | v. |  使腐朽, 使腐烂; 去除木质素 | To cause to decay and become soft or rotten; to remove lignin from (wood). | The fungus delignated the wood, making it spongy.  这种真菌使木头腐烂，变得像海绵一样。| Synonyms: rot, decay, decompose | Antonyms: preserve, strengthen | verb

delignification: /diːˌlɪɡnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 脱木质素; 去木质作用 | The process of removing lignin from wood. |  Delignification is a crucial step in the production of biofuels. 脱木质素是生物燃料生产中的一个关键步骤。| Synonyms: lignin removal | Antonyms: lignification | noun
