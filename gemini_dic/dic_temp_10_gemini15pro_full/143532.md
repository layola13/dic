outgrowth:/ˈaʊtˌɡroʊθ/| n.|结果；产物；副产品；发展；分支|Something that grows out of something else; a natural development or result.|例句：The civil war was an outgrowth of the long-standing tensions between the two regions. 内战是两个地区长期紧张关系的产物。
