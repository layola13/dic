controllably:/kənˈtrəʊləbli/
adverb
可控制地
in a way that can be controlled
The temperature can be controllably adjusted. 温度可以被可控地调节。
近义词: manageably, governably
反义词: uncontrollably, wildly
词性: adverb
词源: From "controllable" + "-ly". "Controllable" comes from "control" (from Old French "controle" and ultimately Latin "contra" (against) + "rotulus" (roll, list)) + "-able" (suffix indicating capability).
