nondeparture:/nɒn-dɪˈpɑːrtʃər/ /nɑːn-dɪˈpɑːrtʃər/| n.|未离开，未出发|The nondeparture of the flight was due to mechanical issues. 航班未起飞是由于机械故障。|例句：The nondeparture of the train caused significant delays for commuters. 火车未出发导致通勤者严重延误。|近义词：non-exit, remaining, stay|反义词:departure, exit|词性:名词
词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "departure" (from Old French "departir," meaning "to divide, separate, leave").
