nucellus:/nuːˈsɛləs/ /njuːˈsɛləs/
noun
珠心
The central part of an ovule, containing the embryo sac.
The nucellus is enclosed by the integuments and provides nutrients to the developing embryo sac. 珠心被珠被包围，为发育中的胚囊提供营养。
The nucellus is the tissue within the ovule of a seed plant surrounding the female gametophyte. 珠心是种子植物胚珠内包围雌配子体的组织。
Synonyms: none specifically, sometimes referred to as megasporangium (though technically distinct)
Antonyms: none
