nonmaliciously: /ˌnɒnməˈlɪʃəsli/ (British) /ˌnɑːnməˈlɪʃəsli/ (American)| adv.| 非恶意地|  In a way that is not intended to cause harm or offense.  It describes an action or behavior that is done without any malicious intent.  It suggests innocence or lack of harmful purpose.|  He nonmaliciously pointed out her mistake. 他并非恶意地指出了她的错误。|  She nonmaliciously teased him about his new haircut.  她没有恶意地取笑他的新发型。| 近义词: innocently, harmlessly, benignly, inoffensively | 反义词: maliciously, spitefully, malevolently | 词性: 副词
