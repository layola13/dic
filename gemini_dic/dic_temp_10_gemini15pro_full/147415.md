overnourish:/ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrɪʃ/| v. |过度营养；营养过剩|To provide with too much nourishment; overfeed.|The child was noticeably overnourished.  这个孩子明显营养过剩。|Synonyms: overfeed, stuff, gorge|Antonyms: undernourish, starve|Verb

overnourishment:/ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrɪʃmənt/| n. |营养过剩；过度营养|The condition of being overnourished.|Overnourishment can lead to obesity. 营养过剩会导致肥胖。|Synonyms: overfeeding, surfeit|Antonyms: undernourishment, malnutrition|Noun
