causational:/kɔːˈzeɪʃənəl/
adjective
因果的；原因的；由原因引起的
relating to or acting as a cause
The report explores the causational link between poverty and crime. 该报告探讨了贫困与犯罪之间的因果关系。
synonyms: causal, causative
antonyms: resultant, consequential
词性: adjective
