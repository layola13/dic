paranoiacally:/ˌpærənɔɪˈækəli/| adv.|偏执地，妄想地|In a manner characterized by or exhibiting paranoia; excessively suspicious or distrustful.|He looked at me paranoiacally, convinced I was plotting against him. 他偏执地望着我，确信我在密谋反对他。|近义词：suspiciously, distrustfully, apprehensively|反义词：trustingly, confidently|词性:副词

词源: From paranoia + -iac + -ally.  Paranoia comes from the Greek "para" (beside, beyond) + "nous" (mind, intellect).
