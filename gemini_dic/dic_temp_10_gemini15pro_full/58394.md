dubby:/ˈdʌbi/| adj.|低音沉重的，有回声的，重复的|Characterized by a strong bass and often reverberation; also, repetitive or duplicated. |The music was dubby and hypnotic.  这音乐低音沉重，令人着迷。|近义词：bass-heavy, resonant, repetitive|反义词：treble-heavy, thin, unique|词性:形容词

dubby:/ˈdʌbi/| n.|牙买加雷鬼乐的混音版本|A remix version of a reggae song, typically stripped down to the rhythm section and featuring heavy echo and other sound effects. |He played a dubby remix of the classic track. 他播放了这首经典歌曲的低音混音版。|近义词：remix, dub|反义词：original|词性:名词
