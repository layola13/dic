fruitfullest:/ˈfruːtfʊlɪst/ | adj. | 最富有成效的；最多产的 |  Superlative form of fruitful: producing good or abundant results; prolific.  | This has been the fruitfullest period of my career. 这是我职业生涯中最富有成效的时期。 | The fruitfullest branches of the apple tree were laden with ripe fruit.  苹果树上最多产的枝条上挂满了成熟的果实。 | 近义词: most productive, most fertile, most prolific | 反义词: barren, unproductive, sterile | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Middle English *frutefulest, from Old English *frēohtfulest (attested as frēohtfullīce (“fruitfully”)), equivalent to fruitful +‎ -est.
