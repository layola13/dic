apotelesmatic: /əˌpɒtəˌlɛzˈmætɪk/ /əˌpɑːtəˌlɛzˈmætɪk/
adjective
有效的；灵验的；(尤指)具有魔法的
Having the power to bring about a desired result; effective, especially magically.
例句：The witch claimed her potion was apotelesmatic and could cure any illness. 女巫声称她的药水很灵验，可以治愈任何疾病。

例句：He believed in the apotelesmatic power of certain rituals. 他相信某些仪式具有神奇的力量。

近义词：effective, potent, magical, efficacious
反义词：ineffective, impotent, powerless
词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *apotelesmatikos*, from *apotelesma* "result," from *apotelesein* "to complete, bring to an end," from *apo-* "from" + *telein* "to complete."
