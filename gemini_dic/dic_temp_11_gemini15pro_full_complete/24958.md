brushite:/ˈbrʌʃaɪt/
noun
透钙磷石
A hydrated acid calcium phosphate, occurring naturally as a mineral.
Brushite crystals were observed in the sample.  在样本中观察到了透钙磷石晶体。

近义词:monetite (单斜磷钙石)  (Both are calcium phosphate minerals, but with different hydration states)
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific mineral)
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
Named after the American mineralogist George Jarvis Brush (1831–1912).
