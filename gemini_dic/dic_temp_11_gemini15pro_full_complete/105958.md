kludging:/ˈklʌdʒɪŋ/
n.
修补；拼凑；粗制滥造
The act of kludging, or making a clumsy or inelegant solution to a problem, often using improvised or unsuitable materials.
He managed to get the broken pipe working again with a bit of kludging. 他用一些修补方法把破裂的管道重新修好了。
近义词: patching, improvising, bodging, jury-rigging, hacking
反义词:  perfecting, refining, optimizing
词性: 名词

kludge:/klʌdʒ/
n.
粗制滥造的东西；拼凑之物
A clumsy or inelegant solution to a problem, often improvised or unsuitable.
The software is a real kludge, but it gets the job done.  这个软件真是个粗制滥造的东西，但它能完成工作。
近义词:  patch, improvisation, botch, contraption
反义词: masterpiece,  perfect solution
词性: 名词

kludge:/klʌdʒ/
v.
修补；拼凑
To make a clumsy or inelegant solution to a problem, often using improvised or unsuitable materials.
He kludged together a temporary shelter from some old boards and plastic sheeting. 他用一些旧木板和塑料布拼凑了一个临时住所。
近义词: patch, improvise, bodge, jury-rig, hack
反义词: perfect, refine, optimize
词性: 动词


词源:  Probably from German *klotsen, to lump together, related to Klotz, a lump or block.
