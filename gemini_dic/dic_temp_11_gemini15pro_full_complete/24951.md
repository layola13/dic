brushed:/brʌʃt/| v. adj.|刷；擦过；掠过；（轻轻地）接触；|The cat brushed against my leg. 猫蹭了蹭我的腿。|近义词：swept, grazed, touched, skimmed|反义词:unbrushed, untouched|词性:动词(过去式/过去分词), 形容词

brushed:/brʌʃt/| adj.|刷过的；有刷痕的；|She wore a brushed wool coat. 她穿着一件刷过的羊毛外套。|近义词：smooth, polished|反义词:rough, unpolished|词性:形容词


词源: From Middle English *brushen, *brusshen, from Old French brosser, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Vulgar Latin *bruscia “heath, brushwood”.
