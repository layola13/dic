freebooters:/ˈfriːbuːtərz/
noun
海盗，掠夺者
A pirate or plunderer, especially one who operates in the Caribbean.
The freebooters sailed the seas, plundering any ship they encountered.  海盗们航行在海上，掠夺他们遇到的任何船只。
pirates, buccaneers, marauders, privateers
lawful citizens, merchants, traders
n.
