harped:/hɑːrpt/ | v. | 喋喋不休；反复讲；唠叨 | to talk or write persistently and tediously on a particular topic. | She harped on about her problems all night. 她整晚都在唠叨她的问题。| He kept harping on the same point. 他不停地重复同一个观点。 |  近义词: dwelt, reiterated, repeated, nagged | 反义词: ignored, disregarded | verb
