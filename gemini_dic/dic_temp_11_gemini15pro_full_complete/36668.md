collided:/kəˈlaɪdɪd/
verb
碰撞，冲突
hit by accident when moving
The two cars collided at the intersection. 两辆汽车在十字路口相撞。
Synonyms: crash, smash, bump, impact
Antonyms: avoid, miss, separate
verb
