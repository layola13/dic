intermodification:/ˌɪntərˌmɑːdɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. |相互修饰，互相影响 |The intermodification between syntax and semantics is a complex issue in linguistics. 句法和语义之间的相互修饰是语言学中的一个复杂问题。 | Interdependence, interaction | Independence, separation | noun

Etymology:  "inter-" (Latin, between) + "modification" (from Late Latin modificationem,  "a measuring, shaping," from modificare "to measure, limit, restrain," from modus "measure" + facere "to make"). Thus, it signifies the act of modifying each other.
