leang: /liːŋ/ | adj. | 瘦的，精瘦的; 吝啬的，小气的 | Lean means having little or no fat. It can also mean not generous, or unwilling to spend money. |  He has a lean, muscular physique. 他身材精瘦，肌肉发达。

She gave a lean donation to the charity. 她给慈善机构捐了一笔微薄的捐款。 | 近义词: thin, slender, slim, skinny, meager, stingy, parsimonious | 反义词: fat, obese, plump, generous, lavish | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Middle English *lene*, from Old English *hlǣne* (“lean, meager, slender, thin”), from Proto-Germanic *khlinaz* (“leaning, inclined”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱley- (“to lean, incline”).
