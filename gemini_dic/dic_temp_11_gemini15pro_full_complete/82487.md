grunhild:/ˈɡruːnhɪld/| n. | 格伦希尔德 | A female given name of Germanic origin, meaning "battle maiden" or "war battle".  It is composed of the elements *gruniz* "green" (symbolizing life and growth) and *hildjō* "battle".  It features prominently in Norse mythology and the *Nibelungenlied*. | Grunhild was a powerful Valkyrie in Norse mythology. 格伦希尔德在北欧神话中是一位强大的女武神。

Grunhild is the name of the Burgundian queen in the Nibelungenlied. 格伦希尔德是《尼伯龙根之歌》中勃艮第王后的名字。| 近义词: Kriemhild (sometimes considered a variant) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
