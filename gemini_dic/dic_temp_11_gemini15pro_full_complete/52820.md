dilutely:/daɪˈluːtli/| adv. |稀释地，淡薄地|In a diluted manner; weakly.  The paint was dilutedly applied, resulting in a pale, washed-out effect.|例句：The juice was dilutely mixed with water.  果汁用水稀释了。

例句：The chemist dilutely added the acid to the solution.  化学家将酸稀释后添加到溶液中。|近义词：weakly, thinly, faintly|反义词:strongly, concentratedly|词性:副词
Etymology: From "dilute" + "-ly".  "Dilute" comes from the Latin "diluere," meaning "to wash away," from "di-" (apart, asunder) + "luere" (to wash).
