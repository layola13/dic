awnless:/ˈɔːnlɪs/| adj.|无芒的，无颖的|Lacking awns (a bristle-like appendage extending from the tip of a plant structure, especially in grasses).|The farmer chose an awnless variety of wheat for easier harvesting.|近义词：smooth, beardless|反义词: awned|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From 'awn' + '-less'. 'Awn' comes from Old Norse 'ögn' meaning "chaff, husk".  '-less' is a suffix meaning "without".
