avertible:/əˈvɜːrtəbl/
adj.
可避免的，可防止的
capable of being averted or prevented
The accident was avertable. 这场事故是可以避免的。
The crisis was avertable with proper planning.  通过适当的规划，这场危机是可以避免的。
近义词: preventable, avoidable, evitable
反义词: inevitable, unavoidable, inescapable
词性: 形容词

词源:
From Late Latin *avertibilis, from Latin āvertō (“I turn away”), from ā (“away”) + vertō (“I turn”).
