multichrome: /ˈmʌltiˌkroʊm/ | adj. n. | 多色镀膜的，多色镀膜 | Having or exhibiting multiple colors, especially with an iridescent or shifting effect.  A substance or finish that displays multiple colors, especially with an iridescent or shifting effect. |  The multichrome eyeshadow shifted from green to gold to purple.  这款多色眼影从绿色变为金色再变为紫色。 | Her nails were painted with a multichrome polish. 她的指甲涂上了多色指甲油。 | 近义词: iridescent, opalescent, pearlescent | 反义词: monochrome, single-colored | 词性: adjective, noun

词源:  "multi-" (many) + "-chrome" (color). From Greek "polychrōmos" (many-colored).
