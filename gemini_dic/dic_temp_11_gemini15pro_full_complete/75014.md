friendlily:/ˈfrɛndlili/ | adv. | 友好地；亲切地 | In a friendly manner; amiably. | She smiled friendlily at the new student. 她对新同学友好地笑了笑。|  She greeted me friendlily and offered me a cup of tea. 她友好地跟我打招呼，并给我倒了一杯茶。 | 近义词: amiably, cordially, warmly | 反义词: unfriendlily, hostilely, coldly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "friendly" + "-ly."  "Friendly" comes from Old English "frēondlīc," meaning "friendly, showing friendship," from "frēond" (friend) + "-līc" (like).
