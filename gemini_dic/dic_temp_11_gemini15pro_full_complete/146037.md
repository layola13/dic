overdrily:/ˈoʊvərˌdraɪli/| adv.|过于干燥地，过于冷淡地|In an excessively dry or unemotional manner.  The comedian overdrily delivered the punchline, getting only a few scattered chuckles.|近义词: aridly, flatly, impassively, dully|反义词: emotionally, warmly, enthusiastically|词性:副词
词源:  From "over-" (excessively) + "dry" (lacking moisture or emotion) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
