antialcohol:/ˌæntiˈælkəhɔːl/
adjective
反酒精的; 禁酒的
Opposed to the consumption of alcohol; promoting abstinence from alcohol.
The antialcohol movement gained momentum in the early 20th century.  反酒精运动在20世纪初获得了动力。
The organization held an antialcohol rally.  该组织举行了一场反酒精集会。
近义词:temperance, teetotal, abstentionist
反义词:pro-alcohol
词性:adjective
