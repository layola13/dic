logician:/ləˈdʒɪʃən/ /ˈlɑːdʒɪʃən/| n.|逻辑学家；精于逻辑的人|A person skilled in logic. |例句：The logician presented a compelling argument.  逻辑学家提出了一个令人信服的论点。

例句：He is known as a brilliant logician. 他以才华横溢的逻辑学家而闻名。


近义词：reasoner, thinker, philosopher|反义词:illogical person, irrational person|词性:noun
