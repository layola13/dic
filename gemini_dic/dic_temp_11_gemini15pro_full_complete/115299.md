manna:/ˈmænə/ | n. | 天赐之物；意外收获；精神食粮 | Something beneficial that appears or is provided unexpectedly; spiritual nourishment. | The grant was manna from heaven for the struggling artist.  这笔资助对于这位苦苦挣扎的艺术家来说简直是天赐之物。| Synonyms: windfall, boon, blessing, godsend | Antonyms: curse, misfortune | noun
