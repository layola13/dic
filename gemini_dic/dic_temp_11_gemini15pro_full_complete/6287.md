amorous: /ˈæmərəs/ | adj. | 多情的，恋爱的，怀春的 | Showing, feeling, or relating to sexual desire. | She gave him an amorous glance. 她多情地瞥了他一眼。|  He cast an amorous look at her. 他向她投以爱慕的目光。 | 近义词：romantic, passionate, loving, affectionate | 反义词：platonic, indifferent, unloving | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *amorous, from Anglo-Norman *amurus, amuros, from Old French amoros, from Late Latin amorōsus (“loving”), from Latin amor (“love”).
