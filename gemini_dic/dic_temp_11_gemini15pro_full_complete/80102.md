gobbled:/ˈɡɑːbəld/ (US) /ˈɡɒbəld/ (UK)| v. |狼吞虎咽，贪婪地吃；(火鸡)咯咯叫 |to eat something quickly and noisily |He gobbled down his breakfast and rushed out the door. 他狼吞虎咽地吃完早餐，冲出门外。|synonyms: devour, gulp, guzzle |antonyms: nibble, savor|verb
Etymology-> From Middle English *gobben, of imitative origin.
