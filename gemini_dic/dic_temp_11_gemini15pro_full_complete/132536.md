nondeviations:/ˌnɒndiːviˈeɪʃənz/
noun
无偏差；无偏离；不变行
lack of deviations; adherence to a standard or norm.
The nondeviation from the plan ensured the project's success.  计划的严格执行确保了项目的成功。
nondeviation from the original design. 没有偏离最初的设计
Synonyms: conformity, adherence, compliance, consistency
Antonyms: deviation, divergence, digression, variance
词性: 名词
