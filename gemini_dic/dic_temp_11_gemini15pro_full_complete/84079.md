half-importantly:/ˌhæf ɪmˈpɔːrtəntli/
adv.
一半重要地；不太重要地
Modifying an adjective or adverb to indicate a moderate degree of importance.  It suggests that something is important to some extent, but not entirely or significantly.
He mentioned, half-importantly, that he had once met the president.  他不太重要地提了一句，他曾经见过总统。
Synonyms: moderately importantly, somewhat importantly, slightly importantly
Antonyms: very importantly, crucially, significantly
词性: 副词
