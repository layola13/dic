headsman:/ˈhedzmən/| n.|刽子手，行刑者|A person who carries out executions by beheading.|The headsman awaited the king's signal. 刽子手等待着国王的信号。
The headsman sharpened his axe. 刽子手磨利了他的斧头。|近义词：executioner|反义词:None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hedisman*, equivalent to *hed* (head) + *-es-* (genitive suffix) + *man* (man).  Essentially meaning "man of the head" (i.e., one who removes heads).
