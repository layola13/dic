anatomy:/əˈnætəmi/ | n. |解剖学；解剖结构；人体结构；（详细）分析|The study of the structure of living organisms, especially the human body.  The detailed analysis of something.|She's taking a class in anatomy. 她正在上解剖学课。| The anatomy of the crime was presented in court.  罪案的详细分析在法庭上进行了陈述。 | synonyms: structure, dissection, physiology | antonyms: synthesis, whole | noun
