fuzzboxes:/ˈfʌzbɒksɪz/| n.|失真效果器（复数）|A fuzzbox (or fuzz box) is an electronic effects pedal which clips the audio signal to produce a distorted, "fuzzy" sound, originally emulating the sound of overdriven vacuum tubes.|The guitarist stomped on his fuzzboxes and launched into a blistering solo. 吉他手猛踩他的失真效果器，开始了一段狂热的独奏。|近义词：distortion pedals, overdrive pedals|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "clean boost pedals")|noun
