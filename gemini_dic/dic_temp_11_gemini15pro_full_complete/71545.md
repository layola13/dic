flaring:/ˈflɛːrɪŋ/ | adj.  v. (flare的现在分词) | 燃烧的；闪耀的；张开的 |burning with a sudden bright light; spreading outwards | The flaring nostrils of the bull indicated its anger. 公牛张开的鼻孔表明它很愤怒。 | synonyms: blazing, flashing, widening, expanding | antonyms: contracting, shrinking, dimming | adjective

flaring:/ˈflɛːrɪŋ/ | n. |  闪光; 照明弹 | a sudden burst of bright light | The flares lit up the night sky. 照明弹照亮了夜空。 | synonyms: flash, blaze, burst | antonyms: darkness, gloom | noun
