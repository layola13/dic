outsiderishness:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪdərɪʃnəs/ | n. | 外来者身份，局外人的状态 | The state or quality of being an outsider; a feeling of not belonging. |  Her outsiderishness made it difficult for her to integrate into the close-knit community. 她格格不入，难以融入这个紧密的社区。| synonyms: alienation, estrangement, detachment, otherness | antonyms: belonging, inclusion, integration | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From outsider + -ishness.  "Outsider" comes from "outside" + "-er".  "Outside" derives from Old English "ūtan" (outside) + "sīde" (side). "-ishness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
