loimic:/ˈlɔɪmɪk/| adj.| 黏糊糊的; 黏滑的 |Covered with or resembling loam; sticky or slimy.| The loimic mud clung to my boots.  黏糊糊的泥巴粘在我的靴子上。|近义词：sticky, slimy, gooey, viscous|反义词：dry, smooth|词性:形容词

词源:  Likely derived from "loam" (fertile soil), with the suffix "-ic" added to form an adjective indicating a characteristic related to loam.  The sense of stickiness or sliminess is probably an extension of the texture of wet loam.
