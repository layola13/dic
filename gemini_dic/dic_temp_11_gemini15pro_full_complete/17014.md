batatas:/bəˈtɑːtəs/| n. |红薯，甘薯|A starchy tuberous root vegetable of a climbing plant, widely cultivated in tropical and subtropical countries.|例句：Batatas are a staple food in many cultures. 红薯是许多文化中的主食。|例句：She roasted the batatas with herbs and spices. 她用香草和香料烤了红薯.|近义词：sweet potato, yam|反义词: (无明显的反义词)|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Taino *batata*.  Borrowed into Spanish and Portuguese, and from there into English.
