apart:/əˈpɑːrt/| adj. adv.|分开的，相距的；除外；拆开；成碎片|separated by a distance; besides; into pieces|The two houses are three miles apart. 这两栋房子相距三英里。|近义词：separate, aside, distant, besides|反义词：together, joined, close|词性:形容词/副词
Etymology: From Middle English *apart*, from Old French *apart*, from *a part* (“aside, apart”), from Latin *ad partem* (“to the side”).
