decivilizes:/diːˈsɪvəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使丧失文明;使野蛮化
To cause to lose the qualities of civilization; to barbarize.
The prolonged war has decivilized the region, eroding basic human rights and fostering violence. 长时间的战争使该地区丧失了文明，侵蚀了基本人权，并助长了暴力。
The constant exposure to violence in the media can decivilize society by desensitizing people to suffering. 媒体上持续不断的暴力画面会使人们对苦难麻木，从而使社会丧失文明。
synonyms: barbarize, brutalize, corrupt, degrade
antonyms: civilize, refine, cultivate, enlighten
词性: 动词
