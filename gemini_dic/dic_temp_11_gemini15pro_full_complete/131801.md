nonconsecutive:/ˌnɑːnkənˈsekjətɪv/ /ˌnɒnkənˈsekjətɪv/ | adj. | 非连续的，不连续的 | Not following in a continuous sequence; separated by an interval or intervals. | The meetings were nonconsecutive, taking place over several weeks.  这些会议不是连续的，持续了好几周。| The patient experienced nonconsecutive days of fever. 病人经历了不连续几天的发烧。 |  近义词: intermittent, sporadic, discontinuous | 反义词: consecutive, continuous | 词性: adjective

nonconsecutive:/ˌnɑːnkənˈsekjətɪv/ /ˌnɒnkənˈsekjətɪv/ | noun | 非连续数（通常用于数学）|  A number that is not part of a consecutive sequence. |  While studying prime numbers, they also considered nonconsecutives. 在研究素数的同时，他们也考虑了非连续数。 |  Finding patterns in nonconsecutives can be challenging. 在非连续数中寻找规律可能具有挑战性。 | 近义词: non-sequential | 反义词: consecutive number | 词性: noun (less common)
