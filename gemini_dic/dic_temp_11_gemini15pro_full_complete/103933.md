judgey:/ˈdʒʌdʒi/| adj.|爱评判的，爱指责的|Having or showing a tendency to judge people harshly and critically.  It implies a critical and disapproving attitude, often based on superficial observations or personal biases.|例句：He's so judgey about everyone's clothes. 他对每个人的穿着都喜欢评头论足。

例句：She gave me a judgey look when I ordered dessert. 当我点甜点时，她给了我一个评判的眼神。|近义词：critical, judgmental, censorious, disapproving|反义词:accepting, tolerant, open-minded|词性:adjective
