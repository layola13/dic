|例句：Beware of mountebanks who promise quick riches.  小心那些承诺快速致富的江湖骗子。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *montimbanco*, from *montare in banco* "to mount a bench," referring to the platform used by street vendors and performers to sell their wares or perform their acts.
