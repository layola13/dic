hypocrites:/ˈhɪpəkɪts/ | n. | 伪君子；虚伪的人 |  A person who pretends to have virtues, moral or religious beliefs, principles, etc., that they do not actually possess, especially a person who acts in a way that contradicts their stated beliefs. |  Those hypocrites preach about charity but give nothing themselves. 那些伪君子鼓吹慈善，自己却什么也不捐。| synonyms: pretender, deceiver, charlatan, fraud, phony | antonyms: saint, moralist, genuine person | noun
