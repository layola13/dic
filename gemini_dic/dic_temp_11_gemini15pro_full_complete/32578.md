chirographical:/ˌkaɪrəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
手写的；笔迹的
Relating to handwriting; written by hand.
Her chirographical skills were impeccable, each letter perfectly formed.  她的书法技巧无可挑剔，每个字母都写得完美无缺。
The chirographical evidence suggested the letter was indeed authentic. 笔迹证据表明这封信确实是真实的。
Synonyms: handwritten, penned, script, calligraphic
Antonyms: typed, printed
词性: 形容词
