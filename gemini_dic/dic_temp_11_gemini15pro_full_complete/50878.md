desensitizing:/diːˈsɛnsɪtaɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
使脱敏;使减少敏感;使钝化
The process of making someone less sensitive to something, especially something unpleasant or upsetting.
Exposure therapy works by gradually desensitizing patients to their phobias.  暴露疗法通过逐渐使病人对他们的恐惧症脱敏来起作用。
The constant violence in the media is desensitizing us to real-life suffering.  媒体上不断的暴力使我们对现实生活中的苦难麻木不仁。
近义词:numbing, dulling, inuring
反义词:sensitizing, heightening
词性:v.

desensitize:/diːˈsɛnsɪtaɪz/
verb
使脱敏;使减少敏感;使钝化
To make someone less sensitive to something, especially something unpleasant or upsetting.
Repeated exposure to violence can desensitize people to it.  反复接触暴力会使人们对暴力麻木。
The aim of the program is to desensitize children to the sound of gunfire.  该计划的目的是让孩子们对枪声不敏感。
近义词:numb, dull, inure
反义词:sensitize, heighten
词性:v.

desensitization:/diːˌsɛnsɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
脱敏;减少敏感;钝化
The process of desensitizing someone or something.
Systematic desensitization is a common treatment for phobias.  系统脱敏是治疗恐惧症的常用方法。
The desensitization of society to violence is a worrying trend.  社会对暴力的麻木是一个令人担忧的趋势。
近义词:numbing, dulling, inuring
反义词:sensitization, heightening
词性:n.

词源:From de- + sensitize, from sensitive + -ize.
