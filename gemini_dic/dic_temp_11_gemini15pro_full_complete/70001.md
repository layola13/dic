fetes:/feɪts/ /fɛts/
| n. |庆祝活动，节日，盛宴|A large elaborate party or celebration, often outdoors. |The town held fetes to celebrate the summer solstice.  小镇举办庆祝活动来庆祝夏至。|Synonyms: celebrations, festivals, parties, carnivals|Antonyms: funerals, wakes|noun


fetes:/feɪts/ /fɛts/
| v. |宴请，款待|To entertain lavishly; to celebrate a person or event. |The king feted the visiting dignitaries with a grand banquet. 国王设盛宴款待来访的贵宾。|Synonyms: entertain, celebrate, honor, feast|Antonyms: neglect, ignore|verb
