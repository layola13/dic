mirthfulness:/ˈmɜːrθfəlnəs/ /ˈmɝːθfəlnəs/ | n. | 欢乐；高兴；愉快 | The state of being mirthful; full of mirth; merriment; gaiety. | Her mirthfulness was infectious, spreading joy throughout the room. 她的快乐很有感染力，让整个房间充满了喜悦。| synonyms:  joviality, cheerfulness, merriment, gaiety, hilarity | antonyms: gloom, sadness, melancholy, dejection, glumness | noun

mirthful:/ˈmɜːrθfʊl/ /ˈmɝːθfʊl/ | adj. | 欢乐的；高兴的；愉快的 | Full of mirth; merry; cheerful; jolly. |  The mirthful laughter of children filled the air. 孩子们欢乐的笑声充满了空气。 | synonyms:  jolly, jovial, merry, cheerful, joyous | antonyms: sad, gloomy, morose, sullen, somber | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mirthfulnes*, equivalent to mirthful +‎ -ness.  Mirth itself comes from Old English *myrġþ*  "joy, pleasure, bliss," related to Old High German *murgi*.
