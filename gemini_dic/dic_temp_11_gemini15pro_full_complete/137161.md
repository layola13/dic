nonsuburbans:/ˌnɑːnsəˈbɜːrbənz/ |n.| 非郊区居民，非市郊居民 | People who do not live in the suburbs.  These individuals might reside in urban city centers or rural areas. | Many nonsuburbans prefer the amenities and cultural opportunities of city life. 许多非郊区居民更喜欢城市的便利设施和文化机会。

The survey showed a growing trend of nonsuburbans moving to smaller towns.  调查显示，越来越多的非郊区居民搬到小城镇。| 近义词: urbanites, city dwellers, rural residents | 反义词: suburbanites | 词性: 名词


词源:

non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin *non < PIE *ne "not."
suburban (adjective/noun): From sub- "near" + urban "of a city." Ultimately from Latin sub "under, near" and urbs "city."
-s (suffix):  Plural marker.
