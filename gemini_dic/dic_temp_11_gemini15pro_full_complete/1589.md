achroodextrin:/ˌeɪkroʊˈdɛkstrɪn/| n.|非染色糊精|A type of dextrin that does not produce a color reaction with iodine.  It is a product of the breakdown of starch by amylase.|The achroodextrin was identified in the sample.|Synonyms: limit dextrin|Antonyms: starch, amylose|noun
