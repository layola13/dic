oligocene:/ˈɑlɪɡəˌsiːn/ |adj. n.| 渐新世的；渐新统 |Relating to or denoting the third epoch of the Tertiary period, between the Eocene and Miocene epochs. |The Oligocene epoch saw the rise of many modern mammal groups. |渐新世见证了许多现代哺乳动物群体的兴起。|近义词:Tertiary |反义词:None| noun, adjective

词源:
From French oligocène, from oligo- ‘few’ + Greek kainós ‘recent’.
