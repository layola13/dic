arminianism:/ɑːrˈmɪniənɪzəm/
n.
阿米念主义
A theological doctrine named after Jacobus Arminius, a Dutch theologian, that opposes the absolute predestination of Calvinism and emphasizes free will in salvation.
Arminianism emphasizes the importance of free will in accepting God's grace. 阿米念主义强调自由意志在接受上帝恩典方面的重要性。
Many Methodist churches adhere to Arminianism. 许多卫理公会教堂坚持阿米念主义。
近义词:free will theology
反义词:Calvinism
词性:noun
