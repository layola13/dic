inanimate:/ɪˈnænɪmət/| adj.|无生命的，无生气的|Not alive, not having the characteristics of life.  Lacking animation or liveliness.|The statue was inanimate, yet it seemed to possess a certain power. 这座雕像是无生命的，但它似乎拥有一种特殊的力量。|例句：The artist skillfully painted the inanimate objects, bringing them to life on the canvas.  艺术家巧妙地描绘了这些无生命的物体，使它们在画布上栩栩如生。

例句：She felt a strange connection to the inanimate landscape, as if the rocks and trees were communicating with her. 她感觉自己与这片无生命的景色有着一种奇特的联系，仿佛岩石和树木都在与她交流。|近义词：lifeless, inert, dead|反义词：animate, living, alive|词性：形容词
