nonreticent:/ˌnɒnˈrɛtɪs(ə)nt/
adjective
不沉默的；坦率的；健谈的
Not reticent; outspoken or frank.
She was surprisingly nonreticent about her past. 她对自己的过去坦率得令人惊讶。
近义词: outspoken, frank, communicative, talkative, loquacious, unreserved
反义词: reticent, reserved, taciturn, quiet
