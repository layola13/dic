incalculabilities:/ɪnˌkælkjʊləˈbɪlɪtiz/
| n. | 不可计算性；无法预测性；不可估量性 | The quality or state of being incalculable; things that are incalculable.  The numerous incalculabilities of the situation made it impossible to predict the outcome. | 例句：The incalculabilities of the stock market make investing a risky venture. 股市的不可预测性使得投资成为一项冒险的举动。

例句：The project faced numerous incalculabilities, including potential delays and unforeseen costs.  该项目面临着许多不可估量的因素，包括潜在的延误和不可预见的成本。
| 近义词：unpredictability, uncertainty, imponderables | 反义词：predictability, certainty, calculability | 词性: 名词 (plural noun)
