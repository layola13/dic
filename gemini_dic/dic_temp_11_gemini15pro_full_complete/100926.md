inuit: /ˈɪnjuɪt/ (UK) /ˈɪnuɪt/ (US)| n. |因纽特人|A member of an indigenous people inhabiting northern Canada, Greenland, Alaska, and eastern Siberia, traditionally living by hunting, fishing, and trapping.|例句：The Inuit have a deep understanding of the Arctic environment. 因纽特人对北极环境有深刻的理解。

例句：Inuit art often depicts animals and scenes from daily life. 因纽特艺术经常描绘动物和日常生活场景。|近义词：Eskimo (outdated and sometimes considered offensive)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
