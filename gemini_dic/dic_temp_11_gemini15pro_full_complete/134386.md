nonintervention:/ˌnɑːnɪntərˈvɛnʃən/ | n. | 不干涉，不介入 |  The policy of nonintervention in the affairs of other countries is a complex one.  | Example: The government's policy of nonintervention in the conflict was criticized by some. 政府对这场冲突的不干涉政策受到了一些人的批评。

Example:  The United Nations debated the principle of nonintervention in sovereign states. 联合国就主权国家不干涉原则进行了辩论。 | Synonym: noninterference, neutrality | Antonym: intervention, interference | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + intervention, from French intervention, from intervenir "to intervene," from Late Latin intervenire, from Latin inter- "between" + venire "to come."
