overprovoking:/ˌoʊvərprəˈvoʊkɪŋ/
adjective
过度刺激的，过分挑衅的
Causing excessive provocation; tending to provoke to an extreme degree.
His overprovoking behavior led to a fight. 他的过分挑衅的行为导致了一场斗殴。
Her overprovoking words angered everyone in the room. 她过分刺激的言辞激怒了房间里的每一个人。
近义词:inflammatory, incendiary, provocative, aggravating, exasperating
反义词:soothing, calming, placating, appeasing


词性: adjective


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "provoking" (present participle of "provoke," from Latin "provocare" - to call forth, challenge)
