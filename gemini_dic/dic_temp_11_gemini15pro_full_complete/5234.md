allness:/ˈɔːlnɪs/| n.|全部; 整体性; 完全; 绝对性|The state of assuming that one knows everything about something, or that what one knows about something is all there is to know.  The tendency to think in terms of "all" or "never". |He suffers from allness, believing his perspective is the only valid one.|近义词：totality, completeness, absoluteness|反义词: incompleteness, partiality|词性:noun
