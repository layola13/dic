breathing-spell:/ˈbriːðɪŋ spel/
n.
喘息的机会；短暂的休息
a short rest or period of relief from something difficult or unpleasant
After weeks of intense work, the weekend offered a welcome breathing-spell.  几周高强度的工作之后，周末提供了一个受欢迎的喘息机会。
The ceasefire provided a breathing-spell for the war-torn country. 停火为饱受战争蹂躏的国家提供了一个喘息的机会。
近义词：respite, break, lull, pause
反义词：strain, stress, pressure, burden
词性：名词
