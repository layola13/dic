kernel:/ˈkɜːrnl/ (US) /ˈkɜːnəl/ (UK)| n.|内核; 核心; 要点; 果仁|The central or most important part of something.  The soft, edible part of a nut, seed, or fruit stone contained within its hard shell.|

例句：
The kernel of the argument is that free trade benefits everyone.
论证的核心是自由贸易对每个人都有利。

例句：
He ate the apple, core and kernel.
他把苹果吃了，包括果核和果仁。


近义词：core, nucleus, essence, gist, heart, seed, nut, pip
反义词：periphery, exterior, shell, husk
词性：noun
