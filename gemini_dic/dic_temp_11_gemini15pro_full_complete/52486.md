dietine: /ˈdaɪɪˌtiːn/ | n. |二乙胺; N-乙基乙胺|A secondary amine, N-ethylethanamine, with the formula (C2H5)2NH. It is a colorless, volatile liquid with a strong fishy odor.|Diethine is used in the production of certain pesticides and pharmaceuticals. 二乙胺用于生产某些杀虫剂和药物。|Diethine reacted with the acid chloride to form the desired amide. 二乙胺与酰氯反应生成所需的酰胺。|Synonyms: N-ethylethanamine, diethylamine|Antonyms: N/A|noun
