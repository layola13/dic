disarranger:/dɪsəˈreɪndʒər/| n.|扰乱者，混乱制造者|Someone or something that disrupts order or arrangement.  A disarranger throws things into disarray.|The mischievous child was a disarranger of toys, leaving a chaotic mess in their wake.  这个淘气的孩子是一个玩具扰乱者，身后留下了一片混乱。|近义词：disruptor, disturber|反义词:organizer, arranger|Noun
