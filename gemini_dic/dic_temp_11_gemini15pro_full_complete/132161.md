noncurrent:/nɒnˈkʌrənt/ | adj. | 非流动的，非通用的 | Not currently in progress or belonging to the present period; not circulating. |  Example: Noncurrent assets are those not easily converted to cash. 非流动资产是那些不容易转换成现金的资产。 | Example:  The library has a vast collection of noncurrent periodicals. 图书馆收藏了大量的过期刊物。 | Synonym: long-term, fixed, non-circulating | Antonym: current, short-term, circulating | adjective
