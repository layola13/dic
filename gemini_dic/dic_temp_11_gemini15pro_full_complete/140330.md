ogygian:/oʊˈdʒɪdʒiən/
adjective
中文翻译：极其古老的；洪荒时代的
英文解析：Extremely old; of or belonging to the earliest ages.  Relating to a time long past, especially the early stages of the Earth's history. Often used hyperbolically or humorously.
例句：The ogygian redwood tree had witnessed centuries of history.  这棵极其古老的红杉树见证了几个世纪的历史。

例句：He pulled out an ogygian computer from his closet, covered in dust. 他从壁橱里拖出一台极其古老的电脑，上面满是灰尘。

近义词：ancient, archaic, primordial, primeval
反义词：modern, contemporary, recent
词性：adjective
