latinistic:/ˌlætɪˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
拉丁语的；拉丁风格的
Resembling or imitating Latin in form or style.
Her writing style is quite latinistic, full of long, complex sentences. 她的写作风格非常拉丁化，充满了长而复杂的句子。
The professor used a latinistic phrase that none of the students understood.  教授用了一个拉丁语的短语，学生们都没听懂。
近义词:Latin, classical
反义词:vernacular, colloquial
词性:adjective
