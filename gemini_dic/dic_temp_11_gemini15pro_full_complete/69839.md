ferricyanic:/ˌfɛriːsaɪˈænɪk/ | adj. | 铁氰的，含三价铁的氰化物的 | Containing or relating to iron in its +3 oxidation state, combined with cyanide. | Potassium ferricyanide is a bright red crystalline solid. 铁氰化钾是一种鲜红色的结晶固体。 | 近义词: hexacyanoferrate(III) (referring to the anion) | 反义词: ferrocyanic | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From ferri- (Latin, "iron") + cyanic (referring to cyanide).
