dialectally: /ˌdaɪəˈlɛktəli/ | adv. | 方言地；用方言 | In some areas, "ain't" is used dialectally. | 在某些地区，“ain't”是方言用法。| synonym: regionally, locally | antonym: formally, standardly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From dialect + -ally.  "Dialect" comes from the Greek "dialektos" meaning "conversation, discourse, language of a district," from "dialegesthai" meaning "to converse."  "-ally" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives, meaning "in a ... manner."
