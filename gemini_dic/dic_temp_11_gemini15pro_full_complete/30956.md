chak:/tʃæk/ | n. | 恰克（一种菲律宾的木制玩具陀螺）| A traditional Filipino wooden spinning top, usually played during town fiestas. |  例句：Children played with their colorful chaks during the fiesta. 孩子们在节日期间玩他们色彩鲜艳的恰克。

例句：He skillfully launched his chak, making it spin for a long time. 他熟练地发射了他的恰克，使它旋转了很长时间。| 近义词：spinning top, toy | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源:  Tagalog (菲律宾语)
