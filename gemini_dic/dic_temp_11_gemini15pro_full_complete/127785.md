neighbor:/ˈneɪbər/ (US), /ˈneɪbə/ (UK)| n.|邻居；邻国；邻近的事物|A person living next door or very near to another.|My neighbors are very friendly. 我的邻居们很友好。|近义词：resident, dweller|反义词：stranger, foreigner|noun

neighbor:/ˈneɪbər/ (US), /ˈneɪbə/ (UK)| v.|邻接；毗邻；与…为邻|To live or be situated next to or near another.|Their house neighbors the park. 他们的房子毗邻公园。|近义词：adjoin, abut|反义词：separate, divide|verb
