academicism: /ˌækəˈdɛmɪsɪzəm/ | n. |学院派；学院主义；学术主义| A style of painting or other art that conforms to the traditions taught in art schools, especially emphasizing skill in representing the human form and other traditional subjects accurately.  It often implies a lack of originality or creativity. | The painting was criticized for its dry academicism. 这幅画因其刻板的学院主义而受到批评。 |  formalism, traditionalism | originality, innovation | noun
