insuring:/ɪnˈʃʊrɪŋ/
|v.|投保，保险|The company is insuring against fire damage.  这家公司正在投保火灾险。|
|例句：Are you insuring your car against theft? 你给你的车投了盗窃险吗？|
|近义词：covering, protecting, safeguarding|
|反义词：risking, endangering|
|词性:动词|


insuring:/ɪnˈʃʊrɪŋ/
|n.|保险|Insuring your property is a wise decision.  为你的财产投保是一个明智的决定。|
|例句：The insuring of the cargo was handled by a specialist broker. 货物的保险是由一位专业经纪人处理的。|
|近义词：coverage, protection|
|反义词：risk, exposure|
|词性:名词(动名词)|

insure against (投保防范): Insure against all risks.  投保防范所有风险。
fully insured (全额保险): The car is fully insured.  这辆车是全额保险。
insure with (向...投保): We insure with a reputable company. 我们向一家信誉良好的公司投保。
