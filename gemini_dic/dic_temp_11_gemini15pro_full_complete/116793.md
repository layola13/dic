It can also refer to a long, rambling speech or chatter. |  The mawawal was soulful and captivating, expressing the singer's deep emotions.  那段吟唱深情而迷人，表达了歌手的深厚情感。 | synonyms: chant, song, incantation, rambling, chatter | antonyms: silence, quiet, conciseness, brevity | noun
