napha:/ˈnæfθə/ | n. | 石脑油，轻汽油 | A flammable, volatile, colorless liquid hydrocarbon mixture obtained from the fractional distillation of petroleum, used as a solvent and in the manufacture of plastics. |  Naptha is used as a solvent in many industrial processes. 石脑油在许多工业过程中用作溶剂。

Naptha was once used in lamps. 石脑油曾经用于灯具。| 近义词: petroleum ether, ligroin | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific substance) | 词性: noun
