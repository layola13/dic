elastomeric:/ɪˌlæstəˈmɛrɪk/| adj.|弹性体的，橡胶似的|Made of or resembling an elastomer; having elastic properties.  Having the characteristics of an elastomer, a polymer that can stretch and return to its original shape.|例句：The elastomeric material provides a comfortable and secure grip.  这种弹性体材料提供了舒适和安全的抓握。

例句：Elastomeric bearings are used in bridges to absorb vibrations.  弹性体轴承用于桥梁中吸收振动。|近义词：elastic, resilient, rubbery, flexible|反义词：rigid, stiff, inflexible|词性：adjective
词源：elastomer + -ic.  Elastomer comes from elastic + -mer (a combining form meaning "part" or "unit").  Ultimately from Greek *elastos* "malleable, ductile".
