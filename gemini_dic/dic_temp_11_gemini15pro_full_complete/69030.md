fatty:/ˈfæti/| adj. n.|肥胖的，脂肪的；胖子|Containing a lot of fat; plump or obese.  A fat person.|例句：Fatty foods are unhealthy.  高脂肪食物不健康。|近义词：plump, obese, chubby, greasy, oily|反义词：lean, thin, skinny|词性：形容词

fatty:/ˈfæti/| n.|胖子|A fat person.|例句：He called his overweight friend "fatty," which was unkind. 他叫他超重的朋友“胖子”，这很不友善。|近义词：chubby person, overweight person|反义词：thin person, skinny person|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
