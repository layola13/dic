anadiplosis: /ˌænədɪˈploʊsɪs/ | n. |首语重复法；衔接重复 | Repetition of the last word of a preceding clause. The word is used at the beginning of the next clause. | The mountains look on Marathon—And Marathon looks on the sea. |  Synonyms: reduplication, repetition, epizeuxis (when the repetition is immediate) | Antonyms: N/A | noun
