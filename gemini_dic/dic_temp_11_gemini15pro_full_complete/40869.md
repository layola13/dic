ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *coked*, past participle of *coken* (“to cook”), from Old English *cōcian* (“to cook”), from Proto-Germanic *kōkijaną* (“to cook”), from Proto-Indo-European *peh₂ḱʷ- (“to ripen, cook”).
