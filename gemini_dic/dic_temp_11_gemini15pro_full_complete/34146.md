clairaudient:/klɛrˈɔːdiənt/| adj.|有超自然听觉的；能听见灵界声音的|Having or claiming to have the power of hearing something not present to the ear; able to perceive sounds or words supernaturally.|She believes she is clairaudient and can hear the voices of angels.|近义词：psychic, clairvoyant|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词

Etymology: From French *clair* (clear) + *audient* (hearing), from Latin *clarus* (clear) and *audiens* (hearing).
