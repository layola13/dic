A centre can also be a place or building where a particular activity takes place.|The earthquake’s epicentre was 50 miles south of the city. 这场地震的震中位于这座城市以南 50 英里处。

center:/ˈsentər/ (US) /ˈsɛntə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|中心，中心点；中心机构；中锋;集中，居中|A center (US spelling) is the middle point or part of something. The center of a city or town is the part where there are a lot of shops and offices. A center can also be a place or building where a particular activity takes place.  As a verb, to center something means to position it in the middle.|The community center offers a variety of activities. 社区中心提供各种各样的活动。

Center the text on the page. 将文本在页面上居中。|近义词：middle, core, hub, focus (n.);  align, position (v.)|反义词:edge, periphery, boundary (n.)|noun, verb
