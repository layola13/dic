hedgerows:/ˈhɛdʒˌroʊz/ (US), /ˈhɛdʒˌrəʊz/ (UK)| n.|树篱，灌木篱墙|A row of bushes or small trees planted close together, typically to form a boundary or windbreak.|The hedgerows were bursting with berries. 树篱上长满了浆果。|Synonyms: hedge, fence, barrier|Antonyms: open field, clearing|Noun
