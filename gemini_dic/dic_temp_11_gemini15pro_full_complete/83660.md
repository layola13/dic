haemostatic:/ˌhiːməˈstætɪk/ /ˌheməˈstætɪk/ |  n.|止血药|A substance that arrests bleeding.|The doctor administered a haemostatic to control the bleeding. 医生使用了止血药来控制出血。|Synonyms:styptic, antihemorrhagic|Antonyms:coagulant, hemostatic|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek αἷμα (haîma) "blood" + στατικός (statikós) "causing to stand," from ἵστημι (hístēmi) "to make stand, to stop."
