blob:/blɒb/ (UK), /blɑb/ (US)| n.|一团，斑点，小球，水滴，（计算机）二进制大对象|A shapeless mass of something, especially a liquid or semiliquid substance; a small drop of a thick liquid or viscous substance; in computing, a Binary Large Object, a collection of binary data stored as a single entity in a database management system.|The paint had fallen on the floor and formed a sticky blob. 油漆掉在地板上，形成了一块黏糊糊的斑点。

The jelly wobbled like a big pink blob. 果冻像一大团粉红色的东西一样晃动着。

He transferred the blob of data to his computer. 他把那团数据传到了他的电脑上。

|近义词：drop, lump, mass, glob|反义词: - |词性:noun
