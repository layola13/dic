antialiasing:/ˌæntiˈeɪliəsɪŋ/ | n. | 抗锯齿，反锯齿 |  A technique used in computer graphics to smooth out the jagged edges of lines and curves by blending the pixels along the edges with the surrounding colors. |  The game looks much better with antialiasing enabled.  这个游戏启用抗锯齿后看起来好多了。 | synonyms: smoothing, de-jagging | antonyms: aliasing | noun
