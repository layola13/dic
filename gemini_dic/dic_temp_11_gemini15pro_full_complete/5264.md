allomerous:/əˈlɒmərəs/
adj.
异分节的，异节的
Having a different number of parts in successive whorls of a flower, or having the floral parts not in multiples of the same number.
Allomerous flowers have unequal numbers of sepals, petals, stamens, and carpels. 异分节的花具有数量不等的萼片、花瓣、雄蕊和心皮。
The plant's allomerous nature made it difficult to classify.  这种植物的异分节特性使其难以分类。

近义词: heteromerous
反义词: isomerous, homomerous
词性: adjective
