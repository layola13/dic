nonsaccharine:/ˌnɑːnˈsækərɪn/ /ˌnɒnˈsækərɪn/
adjective
不甜的；不含糖的；不讨好人的；不虚伪的
lacking in sugar; not sugary; not sentimental or saccharine
例句：She prefers a nonsaccharine breakfast cereal. 她更喜欢不甜的早餐麦片。
例句：He gave a nonsaccharine portrayal of the realities of war. 他对战争现实的描述并不虚伪。
近义词：unsweetened, dry, tart, savory, realistic, genuine
反义词：saccharine, sweet, sugary, sentimental, cloying
词性: adjective
