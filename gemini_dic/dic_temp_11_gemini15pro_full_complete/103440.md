jizya:/ˈdʒɪz.jə/ | n. | 人头税 | A per capita yearly tax historically levied by Islamic states on certain non-Muslim subjects, permanently residing in Muslim lands under Islamic law. |  The jizya was a significant source of revenue for early Islamic empires. 人头税是早期伊斯兰帝国的重要收入来源。|  The payment of the jizya afforded dhimmis protection under Islamic rule.  缴纳人头税使受保护的非穆斯林在伊斯兰统治下得到保护。 | synonyms: poll tax, head tax, capitation | antonyms:  zakat (obligatory alms giving in Islam)| noun
