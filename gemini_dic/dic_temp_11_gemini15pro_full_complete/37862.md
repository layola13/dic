compilation:/ˌkɒmpɪˈleɪʃn/ (UK), /ˌkɑːmpɪˈleɪʃn/ (US)| n. |汇编；编纂；合集|The process of gathering things together, especially information, music or other artistic works, into a single book, CD, etc.  |例句：This CD is a compilation of their greatest hits. 这张CD是他们的精选集。|近义词：collection, anthology, assemblage|反义词: division, separation |noun
