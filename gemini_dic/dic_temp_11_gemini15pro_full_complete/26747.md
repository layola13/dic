caducary:/kəˈdjuːkəri/
adjective
无人认领的; 抛弃的; 暂存的
Relating to or denoting property that lapses to the state in the absence of legal heirs.
The caducary nature of the abandoned mansion made it the subject of local legends.  这栋无人认领的豪宅因其暂存状态而成为了当地传说的主题。
近义词:escheated, forfeited, unclaimed
反义词:claimed, inherited
词性:adjective

caducary:/kəˈdjuːkəri/
noun
无人认领的财产; 抛弃物
Property that lapses to the state in the absence of legal heirs.
The state claimed the caducary consisting of the deceased's vast estate.  州政府认领了死者庞大遗产的无人认领部分。
近义词:escheat, forfeiture
反义词:inheritance
词性:noun
