harness:/ˈhɑːrnɪs/ (US) /ˈhɑːnɪs/ (UK)| n. vt.|马具，挽具；安全带；利用，控制|A set of straps and fittings by which a horse or other draught animal is fastened to a cart, plough, etc.  To control and make use of (natural resources), especially to produce energy.|The farmer put the harness on the horse.  农民给马套上挽具。|Synonyms: tackle, gear, equipment, apparatus, rigging, trappings; exploit, utilize, employ, apply|Antonyms: release, liberate|noun
