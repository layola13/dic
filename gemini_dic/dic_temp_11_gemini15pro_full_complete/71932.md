flicker's:/ˈflɪkərz/| n.|闪烁，闪现|A flicker's is the possessive form of "flicker," referring to something that belongs to or is associated with a flicker.  It can refer to the light produced by a flicker, or something else belonging to it.| The flicker's light briefly illuminated the dark room. 那盏忽明忽暗的灯光短暂地照亮了黑暗的房间。 |Synonyms: glimmer's, flash's, sparkle's|Antonyms: steadiness, constancy|Noun, Possessive
