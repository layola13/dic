moderato:/ˌmɒdəˈrɑːtoʊ/ (US), /ˌmɒdəˈrɑːtəʊ/ (UK)| adj. adv.| 中等的；适度的；温和的|Musical direction indicating a moderately slow tempo, faster than lento and slower than andante.  It can also be used more generally to mean moderate or restrained.|The composer marked the second movement "moderato."  作曲家将第二乐章标记为“moderato”。|The car was travelling at a moderato speed. 汽车以中等速度行驶。|近义词:moderate, temperate, restrained|反义词:fast, rapid, extreme|词性:adjective, adverb|词源:Italian, from Latin moderatus, past participle of moderari 'to regulate,' from modus 'measure.'
