aptitudes:/ˈæptɪtjuːdz/
n.|才能，天资，资质|A natural ability to do something.
|She showed a natural aptitude for music. 她展现出天生的音乐才能。
|近义词:talent, gift, skill, ability, flair, knack
|反义词:inability, incompetence, weakness
|词性:名词(plural)

aptitude:/ˈæptɪtjuːd/
n.|才能，天资，资质|A natural ability to do something.
|He has a remarkable aptitude for languages. 他有非凡的语言天赋。
|近义词:talent, gift, skill, ability, flair, knack
|反义词:inability, incompetence, weakness
|词性:名词(singular)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *aptitūdō, from Latin aptus (“fit, suitable”) + -itūdō (“-itude”).
