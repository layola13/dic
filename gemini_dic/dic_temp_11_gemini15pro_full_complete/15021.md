babish:/ˈbeɪbɪʃ/| adj.|幼稚的，孩子气的|Like a baby; childish.  Exhibiting qualities of a baby, such as innocence, naiveté, or helplessness. |He had a rather babish face for a man of his age.  他以他的年龄来说，脸庞显得相当孩子气。|近义词：childish, infantile, immature, naive, puerile|反义词：mature, adult, grown-up|词性:形容词
