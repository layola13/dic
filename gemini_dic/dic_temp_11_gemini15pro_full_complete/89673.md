hoopless:/ˈhuːpləs/| adj.|没有希望的，绝望的|Without hope; despairing.  Derived from "hoop" (a ring or circle, symbolizing completeness or wholeness) + "-less" (suffix indicating lack or absence). The original meaning referred to barrels lacking hoops and thus falling apart, extending metaphorically to situations or people lacking support or prospects.|The team seemed hoopless after losing their star player.  这支球队在失去他们的明星球员后似乎没有了希望。|hopeless, desperate, despondent, despairing|hopeful, optimistic, promising|adjective
