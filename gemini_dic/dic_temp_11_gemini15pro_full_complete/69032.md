fatuous:/ˈfætʃuəs/| adj.|愚蠢的，昏庸的，愚昧的|Lacking intelligence and good judgment; silly and pointless.|Her fatuous comments annoyed everyone in the room. 她愚蠢的评论惹恼了房间里的所有人。|例句：He gave a fatuous grin. 他傻傻地咧嘴一笑。

例句：The movie was filled with fatuous dialogue and predictable plot twists. 这部电影充斥着愚蠢的对话和老套的情节转折。


|近义词：silly, foolish, inane, asinine|反义词：sensible, intelligent, wise|词性:形容词
