overindexes:/ˌoʊvərˈɪndɛksɪz/ | v. |  过度索引；超额指数化；在…中占比过高 | To be disproportionately represented in a particular category or index.  A group overindexes if it appears more frequently than would be expected based on its size in the general population. |  Millennials overindex on social media usage. 千禧一代在社交媒体使用方面占比过高。| 近义词: overrepresents, dominates,  | 反义词: underindexes, underrepresents | verb
