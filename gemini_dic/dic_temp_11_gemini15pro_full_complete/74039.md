forgotten: /fərˈɡɒtn/ (British) /fərˈɡɑːtn/ (American) | adj. | 被遗忘的；忘记了的 |  No longer remembered or considered; neglected.  Comes from the past participle of the verb "forget". | The forgotten toys lay gathering dust in the attic. 被遗忘的玩具躺在阁楼里积满了灰尘。|  The forgotten hero was finally recognized for his bravery.  这位被遗忘的英雄最终因其勇敢而受到表彰。 | Synonym: unremembered, neglected, overlooked, abandoned | Antonym: remembered, cherished, valued | 词性: 形容词
