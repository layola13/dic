horopteric:/ˌhɔːrəpˈtɛrɪk/ | adj. | 眼horopter的，位于眼horopter上的 | Relating to the horopter, the curved line in space containing all points that are seen as single by the two eyes when the gaze is fixed on a particular point. |  Objects falling on the horopteric line are perceived as single. 落在horopter线上的物体被感知为单个物体。 |  The experiment measured the subject's horopteric sensitivity.  这个实验测量了受试者的horopter敏感度。 | 近义词: binocular (related to both eyes, but not strictly on the horopter) | 反义词:  None (lack of a direct antonym, possibly "non-horopteric" could be coined) | 词性: adjective
