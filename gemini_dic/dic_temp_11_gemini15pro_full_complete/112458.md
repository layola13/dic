loudmouths:/ˈlaʊdmaʊðz/ /ˈlaʊdmaʊθz/
noun
大声喧哗的人; 多嘴多舌的人; 吹牛大王
A person who talks too much, especially in a loud and boastful way.
He's a loudmouth who is always bragging about his accomplishments.
他是个多嘴多舌的人，总是吹嘘自己的成就。

The loudmouths in the bar were making it difficult to have a conversation.
酒吧里那些大声喧哗的人使得很难进行交谈。

近义词:braggart, boaster, show-off
反义词:quiet person, introvert
