basketry:/ˈbɑːskɪtri/ | n. | 编织工艺；篮子制品；柳条制品 | The art or craft of making baskets and other objects by weaving or interlacing pliable materials such as reeds, twigs, or grasses.  |  Example 1: Her basketry skills were admired by all.  她的编织技艺受到所有人的赞赏。

Example 2: The museum displayed a beautiful collection of Native American basketry.  博物馆展出了一批精美的美洲原住民编织工艺品。 |  Synonyms: wickerwork, weaving, plaiting | Antonyms: N/A | noun
