atlant:/ˈætlənt/| n.|阿特拉斯，擎天神|A titan condemned to support the heavens on his shoulders.  In Greek mythology, Atlas was one of the Titans who fought against Zeus. As punishment for his rebellion, Zeus condemned Atlas to hold up the sky for eternity.|Atlas shrugged, seemingly unbothered by the immense weight of the heavens. 阿特拉斯耸了耸肩，似乎并不为擎天重任所困扰。|Synonym: titan, giant|Antonym: dwarf, pygmy|noun
