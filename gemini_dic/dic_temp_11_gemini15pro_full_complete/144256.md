outsnored:/ˌaʊtˈsnɔːrd/
verb
胜过…的鼾声，比…更大声地打鼾
outdo someone in snoring.  To snore more loudly than someone else.
He outsnored his wife last night. 他昨晚的鼾声比他妻子的大。
近义词: outdo, surpass (in snoring)
反义词: be outsnores, snore less loudly than
词性: verb
