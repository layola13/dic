noncorruptly:/ˌnɑːnkəˈrʌptli/
adverb|
廉洁地，不腐败地|
In a manner that is free from corruption; honestly and ethically.|
The judge noncorruptly oversaw the trial, ensuring a fair verdict.  法官廉洁地监督了审判，确保了公正的判决。
He noncorruptly administered the funds, keeping meticulous records of every transaction. 他廉洁地管理资金，对每一笔交易都做了详细的记录。|
近义词:honestly, ethically, uprightly, with integrity|
反义词:corruptly, dishonestly, unethically, deceitfully|
词性:adverb


词源:
From non- (not) + corrupt + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The root "corrupt" comes from the Latin "corruptus," past participle of "corrumpere" (to break up, spoil, destroy).
