desolator:/ˈdesəleɪtər/| n.| 荒芜者，使人孤独的人或物，毁灭者，（尤指）扫荡者 |Something that causes great destruction and loss of life, especially in a war.| The nuclear bomb was a desolator of cities.| 近义词: destroyer, devastator, ravager | 反义词: creator, builder, preserver | 词性: noun
词源: From Latin *dēsōlātor*, from dēsōlātus (“abandoned, deserted, desolate”) + -tor.
