de-ice:/diːˈaɪs/
verb
除冰，去除冰
To remove ice from something, such as an aircraft, a road, or a ship.
The plane had to be de-iced before takeoff.  飞机起飞前必须除冰。
The city used salt to de-ice the roads after the snowstorm.  暴风雪过后，该市用盐来清除道路上的冰。
近义词:defrost, thaw
反义词:freeze, ice up
词性:verb


de-ice:/diːˈaɪs/
noun
除冰剂
A substance used to remove ice.
Apply de-ice to the windshield before driving in icy conditions. 在冰雪路面上驾驶之前，在挡风玻璃上涂抹除冰剂。
The airport ran out of de-ice during the blizzard.  暴风雪期间，机场的除冰剂用完了。
近义词:deicer, antifreeze
反义词:N/A
词性:noun
