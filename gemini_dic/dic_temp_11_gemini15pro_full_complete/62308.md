endeared:/ɪnˈdɪərd/ /ɛnˈdɪrd/| adj.| 使受喜爱；使受钟爱；| made someone like something or someone|Her kindness endeared her to everyone. 她的善良使她受到大家的喜爱。|近义词：beloved, cherished, adored, popular|反义词：detested, hated, despised|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From en- + dear + -ed.  "En-" acts as a prefix meaning "to put into" or "to cause to be." "Dear" means beloved or valued. "-ed" is a suffix forming the past participle of verbs, but here creates an adjective meaning "having been made dear."
