nonascendently:/nɒnəˈsɛndəntli/| adv.|非升序地，非递增地|Not in ascending order; in descending or non-increasing order.  This means arranged from largest to smallest, or in an order where subsequent elements are not greater than preceding elements.|The numbers were sorted nonascendently, from 10 to 1.  这些数字按非升序排列，从 10 到 1。|The data is presented nonascendently, starting with the highest value. 数据以非升序方式呈现，从最高值开始。| 近义词: descendently, nonincreasingly |反义词: ascendently, increasingly|词性:副词
