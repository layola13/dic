frogs:/frɒɡz/ (British), /frɑːɡz/ (American)|n.|青蛙|The plural form of "frog," referring to more than one frog.  A frog is a tailless amphibian with smooth skin, long hind legs for jumping, and webbed feet.|例句：The frogs croaked loudly by the pond. 池塘边的青蛙大声地叫着。|例句：We caught several frogs in the net. 我们在网里抓到了几只青蛙。 |近义词：toads, amphibians|反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps predators like snakes or birds) |Noun
