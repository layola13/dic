eye-catching:/ˈaɪˌkætʃɪŋ/
adjective
引人注目的；抢眼的；惹人注意的
Attracting attention; noticeable.
The dress had an eye-catching design. 这件连衣裙设计很抢眼。
Synonyms: attractive, noticeable, striking, conspicuous, arresting
Antonyms: dull, unremarkable, inconspicuous, bland
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From eye + catching (present participle of catch).  The earliest recorded use is from the mid-19th century.
