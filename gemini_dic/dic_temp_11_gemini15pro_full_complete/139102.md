obdurate:/ˈɒbdjʊrət/ (US: /ˈɑːbdərət/) | adj. | 顽固的，执拗的，冷酷无情的 |  Stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or course of action. |  The obdurate old man refused to listen to his children's pleas.  顽固的老人拒绝听他孩子们的恳求。 |  stubborn, adamant, inflexible, unyielding | compliant, yielding, amenable, tractable | adjective
