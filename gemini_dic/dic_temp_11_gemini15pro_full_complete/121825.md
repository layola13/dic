misrelates:/ˌmɪsrɪˈleɪts/ | v. | 误述；错误地联系 | To give an incorrect or misleading account of; to connect wrongly. | He misrelated the events of the evening, leaving out key details. 他错误地讲述了晚上的事情，省略了关键细节。 | She misrelated cause and effect, blaming the wrong person for the problem. 她错误地联系了因果关系，把问题归咎于错误的人。 | 近义词: misrepresents, distorts, falsifies | 反义词: represents accurately, portrays truthfully | 词性: 动词
