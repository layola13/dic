chunam:/ˈtʃuːnɑːm/| n.|灰泥，石灰浆，用石灰和沙子制成的建筑材料|A plaster or mortar made of lime and sand, used in building. |The walls were rendered with chunam. 墙壁是用灰泥粉刷的。|Synonyms: plaster, mortar, render, stucco|Antonyms: None|Noun

chunam:/ˈtʃuːnɑːm/| n.|磨光的贝壳粉|Powdered and polished shells. |Chunam is sometimes used in traditional cosmetics.  磨光的贝壳粉有时用于传统化妆品中。|Synonyms: shell powder, lime powder|Antonyms: None|Noun
