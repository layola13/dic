callowest:/ˈkæləwɪst/
adjective
最冷酷无情的；最无情的
Most unfeeling; most hard-hearted.
His callous indifference to her suffering shocked everyone. 他对她痛苦的冷酷无情震惊了所有人。
近义词: heartless, unfeeling, insensitive, cold-blooded, hard-hearted
反义词: compassionate, kind, sympathetic, warmhearted
词性: adjective

Etymology: From callus + -est.  "Callus" originally referred to hardened skin, and later came to mean emotionally hardened.
