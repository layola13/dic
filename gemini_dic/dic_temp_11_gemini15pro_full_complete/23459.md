ETYMOLOGY->
From Braggadocchio, a boastful character in Edmund Spenser's epic poem *The Faerie Queene* (1590-96).  The name itself is likely a coinage by Spenser, perhaps suggesting "brag" + Italian *doccio* (related to *doge*, a high-ranking official).
