daguerreotypers:/dəˈɡɛrəˌtaɪpərz/
noun
银版照相师
A person who takes daguerreotypes, an early type of photograph.
Louis Daguerre himself was one of the first daguerreotypers. 路易·达盖尔本人就是最早的银版照相师之一。
Daguerreotypers were highly skilled in the complex process. 银版照相师对这项复杂的过程非常熟练。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Daguerreotype + -er (agent suffix).  Daguerreotype is named after Louis-Jacques-Mandé Daguerre (1787-1851), a French artist and chemist who invented the process.  The word "type" ultimately comes from the Greek "typos" meaning "impression, mark".
