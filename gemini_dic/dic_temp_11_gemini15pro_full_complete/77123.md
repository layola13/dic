gas-cooled:/ˈɡæs kuːld/
adjective
气冷的
Cooled by gas, typically referring to a nuclear reactor or other engine where gas is used as a coolant.
The gas-cooled reactor design offers certain safety advantages.  气冷反应堆设计具有一定的安全优势。
Advanced Gas-cooled Reactors (AGRs) are the second generation of British nuclear reactors.  先进气冷堆 (AGR) 是英国的第二代核反应堆。
近义词:air-cooled
反义词:water-cooled
词性:adjective
