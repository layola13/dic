micromolar:/ˌmaɪkrəˈmoʊlər/| adj.|微摩尔的|A concentration of one millionth of a mole per liter.  Expressing a concentration of one millionth of a mole per liter. |The solution had a micromolar concentration of the toxin.  该溶液的毒素浓度为微摩尔。|Near synonyms:  micromol, μM |Antonym: molar (in the general sense of concentration, though not a direct opposite)|adjective
