grotesque: /ɡroʊˈtɛsk/ | adj. n. |怪诞的；荒谬的；丑陋的；风格怪异的（作品）| Comically or repulsively ugly or distorted. Characterized by bizarre distortions, especially in sculpture, painting, or architecture. |  The gargoyles on the old church were truly grotesque.  教堂上的滴水嘴兽真是怪诞。| synonyms: bizarre, distorted, deformed, misshapen, monstrous, hideous | antonyms: beautiful, attractive, normal, conventional | adjective

grotesque: /ɡroʊˈtɛsk/ | n. |怪诞的风格；怪诞的人物（或事物）| A style of decorative art characterized by bizarre or fantastic human and animal forms often interwoven with foliage. Something that is grotesque. | The play was a grotesque of modern life.  这出戏是对现代生活的一种怪诞的模仿。| synonyms: caricature, parody, travesty | antonyms:  beauty, normality | noun
