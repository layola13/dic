interpledge:/ˌɪntərˈpledʒ/| n.|相互保证，互相抵押|A mutual or reciprocal pledge or guarantee.|Example:  The two companies made an interpledge to support each other's ventures. 两家公司互相保证支持彼此的企业。

Example: The warring factions made an interpledge to cease hostilities. 交战各方互相保证停止敌对行动。|近义词：covenant, agreement, pact, contract|反义词: disagreement,  conflict|词性: 名词


词源:  From inter- (between, among) + pledge (a solemn promise or undertaking).
