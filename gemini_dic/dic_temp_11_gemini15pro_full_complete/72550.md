fluting:/ˈfluːtɪŋ/
n.
竖笛演奏；凹槽；褶皱
The sound of fluting filled the concert hall.  竖笛演奏的声音充满了音乐厅。
The columns were decorated with intricate fluting.  这些柱子装饰着复杂的凹槽。
The fabric had a delicate fluting along the edges.  这块布料的边缘有精致的褶皱。
Longitudinal grooves or channels, especially in a column or other architectural feature, or similar folds in fabric.  纵向的凹槽或通道，尤指在柱子或其他建筑特征上的，或织物上类似的褶皱。
The architect admired the fluting on the ancient Greek columns.  这位建筑师欣赏古希腊柱子上的凹槽。
The dress featured delicate fluting around the neckline.  这条裙子在领口周围有精致的褶皱。
grooves, channels, folds, ornamentation
smoothness, flatness
