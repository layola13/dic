millpond: /ˈmɪlpɒnd/ (UK) /ˈmɪlpɑːnd/ (US) | n. | 磨坊池塘；平静水面 | A body of water near a mill, often used to power the mill; figuratively, a very calm and still surface. | The lake was as smooth as a millpond.  湖面平静得像磨坊池塘。| 近义词: lake, pond, still water | 反义词: rough water, choppy water | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *myllepond, from *mylle (mill) + pond.  由mill(磨坊)和pond(池塘)组合而成。
