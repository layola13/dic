lawlessnesses: /ˈlɔːləsnəsɪz/
n.
违法行为，无法无天，混乱状态
The state of being without law; absence of government or control; disorder.
The city descended into lawlessness after the earthquake. 地震过后，这座城市陷入了混乱状态。

The recent lawlessnesses in the border towns have led to increased military presence.  边境城镇近来的违法行为导致了军队存在的增加。

Synonyms: anarchy, chaos, disorder, turmoil, violence, crime, iniquity, wickedness
Antonyms: order, lawfulness, peace, stability, control, harmony

词性: noun (名词)
