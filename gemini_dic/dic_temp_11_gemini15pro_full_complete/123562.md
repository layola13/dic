monsoonish:/mɒnˈsuːnɪʃ/ | adj. | 季风似的；多雨的；闷热的 | Resembling or characteristic of a monsoon; rainy and humid. | The weather has been very monsoonish lately, with heavy downpours and high humidity. 最近天气一直很像季风气候，暴雨不断，湿度很高。 |  The air felt heavy and monsoonish. 空气感觉很沉闷，像季风气候。 |近义词：humid, rainy, muggy, damp, sultry |反义词：dry, arid | 词性: 形容词
