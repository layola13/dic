euphoria:/juːˈfɔːriə/ /juːˈfɔːriə/
n.|欣快症；狂喜；异常的幸福感|A feeling or state of intense excitement and happiness.|She was in a state of euphoria after winning the lottery. 她中了彩票后欣喜若狂。|近义词：elation, joy, bliss, rapture|反义词：dysphoria, despair, misery|词性:名词
Etymology: From Greek *euphoría* "ability to bear easily, fertility," from *eûphoros* "bearing well, healthy," from *eû* "well" + *pherein* "to bear."
