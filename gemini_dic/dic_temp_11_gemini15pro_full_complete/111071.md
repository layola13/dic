listerial: /lɪˈstɪəriəl/ | adj. |  李斯特菌的；由李斯特菌引起的 | Relating to or caused by Listeria, a genus of bacteria that can cause serious infections, especially in pregnant women, newborns, and people with weakened immune systems. |  例句：Listerial infections can be very dangerous for pregnant women. 李斯特菌感染对孕妇来说非常危险。

例句：The outbreak was traced to listerial contamination of the cheese.  这次疫情的起因是奶酪受到李斯特菌污染。 | 近义词: None (specific to Listeria) | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

词源:  Derived from the genus name *Listeria*, named after Joseph Lister, a British surgeon known for his work in antisepsis.
