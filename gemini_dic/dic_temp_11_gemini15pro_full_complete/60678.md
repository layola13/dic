adjective|电的；与电有关的|relating to electricity; carrying or operated by electricity|The electrical system in this building is outdated.  这栋楼的电气系统已经过时了。|electric, powered|non-electrical
词性: adjective

electric:/ɪˈlɛktrɪk/
adjective|电的；用电的；电动的|Of, relating to, produced by, or operated by electricity.|An electric car is environmentally friendly.  电动汽车很环保。|electrical, powered|non-electric
词性: adjective

electricity:/ɪˌlɛkˈtrɪsɪti/
noun|电；电流；电学|A form of energy resulting from the existence of charged particles (such as electrons or protons), either statically as an accumulation of charge or dynamically as a current.|Electricity is essential for modern life. 电力对于现代生活至关重要。|power, current|none
词性: noun

electrically:/ɪˈlɛktrɪkli/
adverb|用电地；电气地|By means of electricity.|The gate is electrically operated.  这扇门是电动操作的。|electronically|manually
词性: adverb


词源: From Late Latin *electricus, from Latin electrum (“amber”), from Ancient Greek ἤλεκτρον (ḗlektron, “amber”).  The connection is due to the fact that amber acquires an electric charge when rubbed.
