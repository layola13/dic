carpeted:/ˈkɑːrpɪtɪd/ /ˈkɑːpətdɪd/
adjective
铺有地毯的；覆盖着…的
covered with a carpet or carpets.
The room was carpeted in a thick, luxurious pile.  这个房间铺着厚厚的豪华地毯。
The hills were carpeted in wildflowers.  山上覆盖着野花。
近义词:covered, floored
反义词:bare, uncarpeted
词性:adjective
