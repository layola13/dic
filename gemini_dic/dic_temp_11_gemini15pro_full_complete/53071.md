diota:/diˈoʊtə/ | n. |  大肚酒罐，双耳罐 | A large, two-handled earthenware jar, especially one used in ancient Greece or Rome for storing wine or oil. | The archaeologists unearthed a diota decorated with intricate geometric patterns. 考古学家出土了一个装饰着复杂几何图案的双耳罐。| synonym: amphora, jar, flagon | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "cup" or "glass" as contrasting vessels) | noun

diota:/diˈoʊtə/ | n. |  (古罗马)四分之一升 | A Roman liquid measure equal to one-fourth of a sextarius. | The recipe called for one diota of olive oil.  食谱要求四分之一升的橄榄油。 | synonym: measure, unit | antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun
