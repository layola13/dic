castable:/ˈkæstəbl/| adj. n.|可浇铸的，浇注料|Suitable for casting into a mold.  Can also refer to the material itself.|The concrete mixture is highly castable.  这种混凝土混合物非常易于浇铸。|Synonyms: moldable, pourable|Antonyms: non-moldable, rigid|adjective

castable:/ˈkæstəbl/| n.|浇注料|A material that can be cast into a mold.|This castable is ideal for high-temperature applications. 这种浇注料非常适合高温应用。|Synonyms: refractory, molding material|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "cast" (verb) + "-able" (suffix).  "Cast" comes from Old Norse "kasta" (to throw).  "-able" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being" or "worthy of being."
