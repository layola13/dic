bletting:/ˈblɛtɪŋ/ | n. | (果实的)软化，(尤指梨、柿子等的)后熟 |  The process of softening of fruit, especially pears or persimmons, after harvesting to improve its flavor. |  The bletting of the pears is essential for their sweetness to develop. 梨的软化对于其甜味的形成至关重要。 | synonyms: ripening, maturing, mellowing | antonyms:  unripening, hardening | noun


bletting:/ˈblɛtɪŋ/ | v. | (使果实)软化，后熟 | To allow fruit to soften after harvesting, typically pears or persimmons. |  He bletted the pears in a cool, dark place. 他把梨放在阴凉处使其软化。 | synonyms: ripen, mature, mellow | antonyms: harden, unripe | verb
