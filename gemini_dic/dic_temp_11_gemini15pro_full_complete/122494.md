mochi:/ˈmoʊtʃiː/| n. |麻糬，糯米糍|A Japanese rice cake made of glutinous rice pounded into paste and molded into various shapes. It is traditionally eaten during Japanese New Year. |例句：I love eating mochi with ice cream. 我喜欢吃麻糬配冰淇淋。

例句：Mochi is a popular dessert in Japan. 麻糬在日本是很受欢迎的甜点。|近义词：rice cake|反义词:|noun
