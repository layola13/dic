incivility:/ˌɪnsɪˈvɪləti/
n.
粗鲁，无礼
Rudeness, lack of courtesy; behavior that is not polite or respectful.
The customer service representative's incivility made me angry.  客服代表的无礼让我很生气。
His incivility towards the waiter was embarrassing to witness. 他对服务员的粗鲁行为令人尴尬。
近义词：rudeness, discourtesy, impoliteness, disrespect
反义词：courtesy, politeness, respect, civility
