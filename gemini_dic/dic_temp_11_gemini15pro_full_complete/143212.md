outcrops:/ˈaʊtkrɒps/ (US) /ˈaʊtkrɑːps/ (UK)| n.|露出地面的岩层；露头|A visible exposure of bedrock or other stratum at the earth's surface.|The granite outcrops were covered in moss. 花岗岩露头被苔藓覆盖。|近义词：exposure, outcropping|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 subsurface 地下的)|noun
