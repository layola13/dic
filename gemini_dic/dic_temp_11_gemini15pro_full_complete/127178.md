naturalizer:/ˈnætʃrəlaɪzər/| n.|归化者；入籍者；驯化者；使自然化的人（或物）|A person or thing that naturalizes something or someone.|例句：He became a naturalizer after living in the country for five years.  他在这个国家生活了五年后成为了归化公民。

例句：The naturalizer helped the immigrant family adjust to their new life.  归化协助员帮助这个移民家庭适应了他们的新生活。

近义词：citizen, national|反义词:foreigner, alien|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From naturalize + -er.  Naturalize comes from the Late Middle English *naturalizen*, from Old French *naturalizer*, and ultimately from Latin *naturalis* (natural).
