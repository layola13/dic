cyclograph: /ˈsaɪkləɡræf/ | n. | 周期图，循环图 | An instrument that records vibrations or oscillations as a wavy line.  It is often used in engineering and physics to study cyclical phenomena. | The engineer used a cyclograph to analyze the vibrations of the bridge. 工程师用周期图分析桥梁的振动。|  The cyclograph showed a clear pattern of oscillation. 周期图显示出清晰的振荡模式。 |  oscillograph, vibrograph | - | noun
