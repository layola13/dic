graunt: /ɡrɑːnt/ | v. | （尤指正式地）同意，准许；（勉强地）承认 | To graunt something is to agree to give or allow (something requested) to someone. | He graunted her request to leave early. 他同意她提前离开的请求。 | synonyms: concede, allow, permit | antonyms: refuse, deny, prohibit | verb
