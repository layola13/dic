outjuggled: /ˌaʊtˈdʒʌɡəld/
Verb
胜过，以智取胜
To surpass or defeat someone by using cleverness or trickery.
He outjuggled his opponent in the negotiations. 他在谈判中以智取胜，击败了对手。

She outjuggled the competition and secured the contract. 她智胜了竞争对手，拿到了合同。

Synonym: outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit
Antonym: be outwitted, be outsmarted

词性: verb
