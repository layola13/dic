overpersecutes:/ˌoʊvərˈpɜːrsɪˌkjuːts/
|v.|过度迫害，过分追究|To persecute excessively or beyond reasonable limits.|The regime overpersecutes its political opponents, imprisoning them for minor infractions.|近义词: persecute, harass, oppress|反义词: protect, defend, support|词性:动词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer) + "persecute" (from Latin persecutus, past participle of persequi "to follow persistently," from per- "thoroughly" + sequi "to follow").
