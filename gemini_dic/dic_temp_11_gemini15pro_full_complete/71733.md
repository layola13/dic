flawn:/flɔːn/
adjective
有缺陷的；有瑕疵的
having a defect or imperfection; not perfect
The diamond was flawless, but the setting was flawed.  这颗钻石完美无瑕，但镶嵌却有缺陷。
近义词: defective, imperfect, faulty
反义词: perfect, flawless, impeccable
词性: adjective

flaw:/flɔː/
noun
瑕疵；缺点；缺陷
a mark, fault, or other imperfection that mars a substance or object
The flaw in the vase made it less valuable. 花瓶上的瑕疵降低了它的价值。
近义词: defect, imperfection, blemish
反义词: perfection, strength, virtue
词性: noun

flaw:/flɔː/
verb
使有瑕疵；使无效；破坏；削弱
to cause a flaw in; mar; to invalidate; nullify
The scandal flawed his reputation. 这桩丑闻损害了他的名誉。
近义词: damage, impair, mar
反义词: perfect, enhance, improve
词性: verb

词源：From Old Norse *flaga (“flake, slab of stone”), from Proto-Germanic *flagō (“piece of flat material, board, flag”). Cognate with Icelandic flaga (“flake”), Norwegian flaga (“flake”), Swedish flaga (“flake”), Danish flag (“flag”). Related to flag. The verb is derived from the noun.
