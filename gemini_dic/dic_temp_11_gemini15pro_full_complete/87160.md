herborist:/ˈɜːrbəlɪst/ (US), /ˈhɜːbəlɪst/ (UK)| n. |草药学家，药草商|A person who is an expert in or sells herbs, especially for medicinal purposes.|例句：The herborist recommended a tea made from chamomile and lavender for relaxation.  这位草药学家推荐了一种用洋甘菊和薰衣草制成的茶来放松身心。

例句：She visited a local herborist to find a natural remedy for her cough. 她拜访了一位当地的草药学家，想找到一种治疗咳嗽的天然药物。

|近义词：herbalist, botanist|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
