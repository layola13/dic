billiard:/ˈbɪljərd/ (US), /ˈbɪliəd/ (UK)| n. adj.|台球，桌球；台球的，桌球的|A game played on a rectangular table covered with green cloth, in which two or more players use cues to hit balls against each other and into pockets around the edge of the table.  Also used as an adjective to describe things related to the game.|例句：He's a skilled billiard player. 他是一位熟练的台球选手。

例句：They spent the afternoon playing billiards. 他们一下午都在打台球。

|近义词：pool, snooker|反义词:|n. adj.
