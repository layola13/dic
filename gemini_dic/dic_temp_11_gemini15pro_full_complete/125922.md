mush:/mʌʃ/| n. vi. vt.|糊状物，软块；玉米粥；（尤指雪橇犬的）前进；傻瓜，蠢货|A soft thick mass or mixture, typically of plant matter. To travel or move (over snow) with a dog sled or team. A stupid or foolish person.|例句：The potatoes had turned to mush. 土豆已经变成糊状了。|近义词：porridge, pulp, sludge, fool, idiot|反义词:solid, genius|词性:名词,动词

mush:/mʌʃ/| vi. vt.|（尤指雪橇犬的）前进；快速前进；感情用事|To travel or move (over snow) with a dog sled or team. To proceed or travel quickly. To become overly sentimental or emotional.|例句：He mushed his dogs across the frozen lake. 他赶着他的狗穿过结冰的湖面。|近义词：travel, journey, advance, progress|反义词:retreat, withdraw|词性:动词
