bellowing:/ˈbeləʊɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbɛloʊɪŋ/ (US)| n.|吼叫，咆哮|The loud roar of a bull or other large animal, or a loud, angry shout. |The bellowing of the wounded lion echoed through the jungle. 受伤狮子的吼叫声在丛林中回荡。|近义词：roar, shout, yell, bawl |反义词: whisper, murmur |词性: Noun

bellowing:/ˈbeləʊɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈbɛloʊɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|吼叫的，咆哮的|Characterized by a deep, loud roar. |The bellowing wind rattled the windows. 呼啸的风拍打着窗户。|近义词：roaring, shouting, yelling, bawling |反义词: whispering, murmuring |词性: Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bellwen, from Old English *bellan ("to bellow, roar"), of imitative origin. Cognate with Dutch bellen ("to bark, low"), German bellen ("to bark, yell"), Old Norse bǫlla ("to bellow").
