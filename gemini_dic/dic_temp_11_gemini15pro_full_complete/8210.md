ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek *antí)
evolution (noun):  the process by which different kinds of living organism are thought to have developed and diversified from earlier forms during the history of the earth. (from Latin evolutio, "an unrolling")
-ist (suffix):  forms nouns denoting a person who adheres to a certain doctrine or system, or who performs a certain action.
