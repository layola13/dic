gongle:/ˈɡɒŋɡəl/ (British English), /ˈɡɑːŋɡəl/ (American English)| n.| 锣; (尤指)大型铜锣 |A large metal disc-shaped percussion instrument, especially one made of bronze, played by being struck with a soft-headed mallet.|The gongle resonated throughout the temple.  锣声在整个寺庙回荡。|近义词：gong, cymbal|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps silence)|noun
