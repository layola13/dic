nonchastisements: /nɒnˈtʃæstɪzmənts/ | n. |  没有惩罚; 免于责罚 | Absence of punishments or reprimands; freedom from scolding or discipline. |  His childhood was marked by a surprising lack of nonchastisements for his pranks. 他童年时期令人惊讶的是，他的恶作剧竟然没有受到任何惩罚。 | 近义词:  impunity, leniency, forgiveness, pardon | 反义词: punishments, reprimands, scoldings, discipline | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "chastisement" (from Old French "chastiement," from "chastier" - to punish, correct)
