deliberated:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪtɪd/
verb
仔细考虑过的，商议过的
considered at length; discussed formally.
The jury deliberated for three days before reaching a verdict. 陪审团商议了三天，才做出裁决。
近义词: considered, pondered, weighed, discussed, debated
反义词: impulsive, rash, hasty, unconsidered
词性: verb (past tense/past participle of deliberate)


deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/
verb
仔细考虑，商议
engage in long and careful consideration.
The committee deliberated on the proposal for several hours. 委员会对这项提议进行了几个小时的审议。
近义词: consider, ponder, weigh, discuss, debate
反义词: rush, hasten
词性: verb

deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/
adjective
故意的，蓄意的；慎重的，从容不迫的
done consciously and intentionally; slow and careful.
His movements were slow and deliberate. 他的动作缓慢而从容。
近义词: intentional, purposeful, conscious, calculated; careful, unhurried, measured
反义词: accidental, unintentional; hasty, rash
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin deliberatus, past participle of deliberare (“to weigh in mind, consider, consult, deliberate”), from de- (“thoroughly”) + librare (“to weigh”), from libra (“balance, scales”).
