finlet:/ˈfɪnlɪt/| n. |小鳍；鳍状突起|A small fin-like appendage, especially on a fish or other aquatic animal.|The tuna has small finlets between its dorsal and tail fins. 金枪鱼的背鳍和尾鳍之间有一些小鳍。|近义词：fin, appendage|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "body" in the context of contrasting with appendages)|noun
