coolingly:/ˈkuːlɪŋli/ | adv. | 冷静地；冷淡地 | In a manner that is cool, calm, and unemotional, often suggesting indifference or detachment. | She coolingly dismissed his apologies. 她冷淡地拒绝了他的道歉。 |  She coolingly assessed the situation before making a decision. 她冷静地评估了情况才做出决定。 | 近义词: calmly, dispassionately, indifferently, aloofly | 反义词: warmly, passionately, excitedly | 词性: 副词

词源: From "cooling" + "-ly." "Cooling" is the present participle of the verb "cool," which ultimately derives from the Proto-Germanic "*kōlaz." The suffix "-ly" forms adverbs from adjectives.
