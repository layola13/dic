expedite:/ˈɛkspɪdaɪt/| v. |加快；促进；迅速执行|To make a process happen more quickly.|例句：We'll do everything we can to expedite the delivery. 我们会尽一切可能加快交货速度。|例句：The company is looking for ways to expedite the production process. 该公司正在寻找加快生产过程的方法。|近义词：accelerate, hasten, quicken, facilitate|反义词：delay, hinder, obstruct|词性:动词
expedition:/ˌɛkspɪˈdɪʃən/| n. |远征；探险；考察；迅速|A journey undertaken by a group of people with a particular purpose, especially that of exploration, research, or war.|例句：The expedition to the North Pole was a dangerous undertaking.  北极探险是一项危险的任务。|例句：They mounted an expedition to explore the Amazon rainforest. 他们组织了一支探险队去探索亚马逊雨林。|近义词：journey, voyage, trek|反义词：delay, stagnation|词性:名词
expeditious:/ˌɛkspɪˈdɪʃəs/| adj. |迅速的；快捷的；效率高的|Done with speed and efficiency.|例句：The company is known for its expeditious handling of customer complaints. 这家公司以快速处理客户投诉而闻名。|例句：We need an expeditious solution to this problem. 我们需要一个快速解决这个问题的方案。|近义词：quick, swift, prompt, efficient|反义词：slow, inefficient|词性:形容词
