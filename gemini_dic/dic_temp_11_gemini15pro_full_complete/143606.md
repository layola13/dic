outintrigue: /ˌaʊtɪnˈtriːɡ/
verb
以智取胜，在阴谋诡计上胜过
to surpass or defeat in intrigue; to outmaneuver someone by using clever schemes or plots.
She outintrigued her rivals and secured the promotion. 她以智取胜，战胜了竞争对手，获得了晋升。
近义词: outmaneuver, outwit, outsmart
反义词: be outmaneuvered, be outsmarted
