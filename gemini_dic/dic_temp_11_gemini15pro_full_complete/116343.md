mastersinger:/ˈmɑːstərˌsɪŋər/ /ˈmæstərˌsɪŋər/| n.|大师歌手|A member of a guild of German lyric poets, singers, and composers, flourishing especially in the 15th and 16th centuries.|Wagner's opera "Die Meistersinger von Nürnberg" features a mastersinger competition. 瓦格纳的歌剧《纽伦堡的名歌手》以一场大师歌手比赛为特色。|synonyms: minstrel, troubadour, bard|antonyms: amateur, novice|noun
