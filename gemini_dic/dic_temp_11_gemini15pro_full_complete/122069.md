USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you usually do on weekends? 你周末通常做什么？
What does she like to eat for breakfast? 她早餐喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you working on right now? 你现在正在做什么？
What is he listening to at the moment? 他现在正在听什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she accomplished in her career? 她在事业上取得了什么成就？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been doing since I last saw you? 自从我上次见到你以来你一直在做什么？
What has he been studying at university? 他在大学里一直在学习什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left for work? 你去上班之前做了什么？
What had they discussed before making a decision? 他们在做决定之前讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been working on before the project was cancelled? 项目取消之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么长时间？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after you graduate? 你毕业后会做什么？
What will happen if we don't take action? 如果我们不采取行动会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到今年年底你将取得什么成就？
What will have changed by the time we get there? 等我们到那里的时候会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？
