crescentric: /krɛˈsɛntrɪk/
adjective
新月形的；逐渐增长的
shaped like a crescent; increasing in size or intensity
The baby’s fingernails were perfectly crescentric. 婴儿的指甲完美地呈新月形。
The company's profits showed a crescentric pattern over the last quarter. 该公司上一季度的利润呈增长趋势。
近义词: crescent-shaped, waxing, burgeoning, growing
反义词: waning, diminishing, decreasing, shrinking
词性: adjective

crescent: /ˈkrɛsnt/
noun
新月；月牙形
the curved shape of the waxing or waning moon
A silver crescent moon hung in the sky. 一弯银色的新月挂在天空。
The bakery makes delicious crescent rolls. 这家面包店做的新月形面包卷很好吃。
近义词: half-moon, lune
反义词: full moon
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *crescentis, present participle of crescere "to grow," from Latin crescere "to arise, grow," from PIE root *ker- (2) "to grow."
