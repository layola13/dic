diagnosticians:/ˌdaɪəɡnɒˈstɪʃənz/ | n. | 诊断专家，诊断医师 |  A diagnostician is a person skilled in making diagnoses, especially medical diagnoses. | The diagnosticians were unable to determine the cause of the illness. 诊断专家无法确定病因。| synonyms: analyst, specialist, expert | antonyms: amateur, novice | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From  "diagnosis" + "-ician".  "Diagnosis" comes from the Greek "diágnosis"  meaning "discernment, distinguishing," from "diagignṓskein"  "to distinguish," from "dia-" "through, across" + "gignṓskein" "to learn, know."  The suffix "-ician" denotes a specialist in a particular field.
