bounden:/ˈbaʊndən/
adjective
受约束的，有义务的
obligated, required by duty or law
He felt bounden to help his family. 他觉得有义务帮助他的家人。
近义词：obligated, compelled, duty-bound
反义词：free, unconstrained
词性：adjective
