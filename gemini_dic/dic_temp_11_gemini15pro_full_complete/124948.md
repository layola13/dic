muffish: /ˈmʌfɪʃ/ | adj. | 闷闷不乐的；生气的；愠怒的 | Annoyed and sullen; sulky. |  He gave me a muffish look when I interrupted his reading.  他当我打断他阅读时，给了我一个闷闷不乐的眼神。| 近义词：sullen, sulky, grumpy, petulant | 反义词：cheerful, happy, pleasant | 词性: 形容词
