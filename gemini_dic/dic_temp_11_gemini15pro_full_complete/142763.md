ottomanism:/ˈɒtəmənɪzəm/ | n. |奥斯曼主义，奥斯曼帝国体制|The ideology of equality for all citizens of the Ottoman Empire regardless of religion or ethnicity.  It aimed to create a unified Ottoman identity by minimizing religious and ethnic differences and promoting Ottoman Turkish culture and language.|The Tanzimat reforms were a key part of Ottomanism.  坦志麦特改革是奥斯曼主义的重要组成部分。|The empire promoted Ottomanism to counteract rising nationalism.  帝国推行奥斯曼主义以对抗日益高涨的民族主义。|Synonyms: Ottoman imperial ideology, Ottomanization|Antonyms: Nationalism, Balkanization|Noun
