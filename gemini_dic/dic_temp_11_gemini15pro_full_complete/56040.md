dobber:/ˈdɒbə(r)/ (UK), /ˈdɑːbər/ (US)| n. |浮标；钓鱼浮子；告密者，打小报告的人|A small float used in fishing, or a person who informs on others. |He watched the dobber bobbing gently on the surface of the lake. 他看着浮标在湖面上轻轻地上下摆动。| The kid was a real dobber, telling the teacher about every little thing. 这孩子真是个告密者，什么事都告诉老师。|近义词：float, informant, snitch, telltale, rat |反义词: None (for float); loyalist (for informant)| noun
