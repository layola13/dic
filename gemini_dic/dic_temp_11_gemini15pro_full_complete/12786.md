assimilations:/əˌsɪməˈleɪʃənz/| n.|同化，吸收|The process of becoming similar to something or someone else.  The assimilation of immigrants into American culture.|例句：The company's rapid assimilation of new technologies gave it a competitive edge.  公司快速吸收新技术使其具有竞争优势。

例句：Cultural assimilation can be a difficult process for immigrants. 文化同化对移民来说可能是一个艰难的过程。|近义词：integration, absorption, incorporation, adaptation|反义词：differentiation, separation, segregation|词性:noun
