intersomnious:/ˌɪntərˈsɒmniəs/ | adj. | 失眠的，患失眠症的 | Characterized by or tending to cause sleeplessness; relating to insomnia. |  She felt intersomnious due to the stress of her upcoming exams. 她因为即将到来的考试压力而感到失眠。 |  He experienced several intersomnious nights in a row. 他连续几个晚上都失眠。 | 近义词: sleepless, insomniac, restless | 反义词: sleepy, somnolent | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *intersomnis*, from *inter-* (between) + *somnus* (sleep).
