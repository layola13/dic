denouement:/ˌdeɪnuˈmɑːŋ/
n.
结局，结果
the final part of a play, movie, or narrative in which the strands of the plot are drawn together and matters are explained or resolved.
The denouement of the film was predictable and somewhat disappointing.  这部电影的结局是可以预见的，有点令人失望。
synonyms: outcome, conclusion, resolution, climax, culmination
antonyms: beginning, start, introduction, prelude
noun
