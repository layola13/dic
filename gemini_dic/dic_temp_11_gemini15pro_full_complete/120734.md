misalphabetizes:/ˌmɪsˌælfəbəˈtaɪzɪz/
verb
错误地按字母顺序排列
arranges (words or names) in alphabetical order incorrectly.
The librarian misalphabetized several books in the fiction section.  图书管理员把小说区的几本书按字母顺序排列错了。
misspell, misorder, misfile
alphabetize, order correctly
