nonentomological:/nɒnˌɛntəməˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
非昆虫学的
Not related to the study of insects.
Her research was nonentomological, focusing on the botany of the region. 她的研究与昆虫学无关，主要集中于该地区的植物学。
The museum's new exhibit is nonentomological; it features ancient pottery.  博物馆的新展览与昆虫学无关；它展出的是古代陶器。
Synonyms: non-insectological
Antonyms: entomological
