medlar:/ˈmedlər/ (US), /ˈmɛdlə/ (UK)| n. |欧楂果，欧楂树|A small deciduous tree of the rose family, with thorny branches, white flowers, and small, apple-shaped fruit. The fruit is edible only after it has begun to decay.|The medlars are ripe for picking in late autumn.  欧楂果在深秋成熟可以采摘。|Synonyms: none specific (fruit can be compared to loquat, crabapple)|Antonyms: N/A|Noun


medlar:/ˈmedlər/ (US), /ˈmɛdlə/ (UK)| adj. |欧楂树的|Relating to or made from the medlar tree or its fruit.|Medlar jelly is a traditional preserve. 欧楂果酱是一种传统的蜜饯。|Synonyms: N/A|Antonyms: N/A|Adjective
