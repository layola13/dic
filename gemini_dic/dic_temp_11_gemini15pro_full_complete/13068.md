astrolatry: /əˈstrɒlətri/ (US) /ˈæstrɒlətri/ (UK)| n. | 崇拜星体; 星辰崇拜 |The worship of the celestial bodies as deities.|  Ancient civilizations often practiced astrolatry, believing the stars controlled their destinies. 古代文明经常实行星辰崇拜，相信星星控制着他们的命运。

Astrolatry was a common practice in ancient Egypt. 在古埃及，星辰崇拜是一种普遍的做法。

|近义词：star-worship, Sabaism |反义词: atheism, iconoclasm |词性: noun
