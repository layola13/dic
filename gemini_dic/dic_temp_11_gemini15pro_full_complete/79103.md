glad-rags:/ˈɡlæd ˌræɡz/
noun
盛装；漂亮衣服
Fine or showy clothes, especially those worn for a special occasion.
She put on her glad-rags for the party. 她穿上盛装去参加派对。
近义词:finery, best clothes, Sunday best
反义词:everyday clothes, casual wear
词性:n.


glad-rags: /ˈɡlæd ˌræɡz/
词源:  A humorous and slightly derogatory term, "glad-rags" originated in the late 19th century. "Glad" in this context likely means "bright" or "showy," while "rags" ironically refers to clothing, even if expensive.  The combination suggests a playful dismissal of fancy attire.
