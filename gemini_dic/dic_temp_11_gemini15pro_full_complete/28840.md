carpellum:/ˈkɑːrpələm/ (US) /kɑːˈpɛləm/ (UK)| n. |心皮|The female reproductive organ of a flower, consisting of an ovary, a style, and a stigma. It may occur singly or as one of a group.|The flower contains five carpels. 这朵花包含五个心皮。|Synonyms: pistil (when carpels are fused), megasporophyll|Antonyms: stamen (male reproductive organ)|noun

carpels:/ˈkɑːrpəlz/ (US) /kɑːˈpɛlz/ (UK)| n. |心皮（复数）|Plural form of carpel.|The flower has numerous carpels. 这朵花有许多心皮。|Synonyms: pistils (when fused)|Antonyms: stamens|noun
