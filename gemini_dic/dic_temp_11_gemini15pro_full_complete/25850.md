burial: /ˈberiəl/ (US), /ˈbɛriəl/ (UK)
n.|埋葬，葬礼|The act of burying a dead person or animal.  The ceremony or act of placing a dead body in a grave. |The burial took place at dawn. 葬礼在黎明举行。| interment, funeral, sepulture |exhumation| noun
Etymology: From Old English *byrgel, from *byrgels, from Proto-Germanic *burgizlaz.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bhergh- (high, to raise).
