nonemendable:/ˌnɒnɪˈmɛndəbl/
adjective
不可修正的，无法补救的
Not capable of being mended, corrected, or improved.
The damage to the painting was nonemendable, leaving it permanently marred. 这幅画的损坏是不可修复的，使其永久性地留下瑕疵。
The judge declared the marriage nonemendable and finalized the divorce. 法官宣布婚姻无法挽回，并最终判决离婚。
近义词:irreparable, irremediable, incurable
反义词:repairable, mendable, fixable
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + emendable.  "Emendable" comes from the Latin "emendare," meaning "to free from faults, correct."  The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning.
