deploration:/ˌdɛpləˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 悲叹，痛惜，哀悼 |  The act of deploring; the expression of grief or sorrow; lamentation.  | The president expressed his deploration of the violence. 总统对暴力事件表示哀悼。 |  grief, sorrow, lamentation, mourning, regret, bemoaning | celebration, rejoicing | noun
