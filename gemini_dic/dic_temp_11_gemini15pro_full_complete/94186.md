imperativist:/ɪmˈpɛrətɪvɪst/
n.|命令主义者；绝对命令论者|Someone who believes in or follows imperativism, a philosophical view emphasizing imperatives, especially in ethics.|Kant was a prominent imperativist, believing morality stems from categorical imperatives.|Synonyms: prescriptivist, moralist|Antonyms: descriptivist, relativist|noun
