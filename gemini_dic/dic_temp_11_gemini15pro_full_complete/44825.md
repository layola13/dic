crushing:/ˈkrʌʃɪŋ/| adj.|压倒性的，毁灭性的；令人尴尬的；极严重的|Overwhelmingly powerful or effective; causing extreme embarrassment or humiliation; extremely severe.|例句：The team suffered a crushing defeat. 该队遭遇惨败。

例句：He received a crushing blow to his confidence. 他的自信心受到了沉重的打击。

近义词：devastating, overwhelming, overpowering, humbling, mortifying, severe, intense|反义词：minor, slight, insignificant, encouraging, uplifting|词性：形容词

crushing:/ˈkrʌʃɪŋ/| n.|压碎，碾碎|The act of crushing something.|例句：The crushing of the grapes is the first step in winemaking. 葡萄的压榨是酿酒的第一步。

例句：The crushing of the rebellion was swift and brutal.  镇压叛乱迅速而残酷。


近义词：grinding, milling, pulverizing|反义词：building, constructing|词性：名词
