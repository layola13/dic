invendibility:/ˌɪnˌvɛndɪˈbɪləti/
n.
不可销售性，滞销
The state or quality of being difficult or impossible to sell.
The invendibility of the product led to the company's bankruptcy.  该产品的滞销导致了公司的破产。
近义词：unsalability, unmarketability
反义词：salability, marketability
词性: noun

词源:
From in- (not) + vendible (saleable) + -ity (suffix forming abstract nouns).  Vendible comes from Latin vendere (to sell).
