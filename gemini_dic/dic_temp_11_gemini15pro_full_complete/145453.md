overcivil:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪvəl/| adj.|过分客气的，过分礼貌的，礼貌得虚假的|Excessively polite or civil, often to the point of seeming insincere or affected.|例句：His overcivil manner made her suspicious. 他过分客气的态度让她起了疑心。|例句：She found his overcivil tone rather irritating. 她觉得他过分客气的语气相当令人恼火。|近义词：unctuous, obsequious, smarmy, excessively polite|反义词：rude, impolite, discourteous|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + civil.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, often implying excess. "Civil" comes from the Latin "civilis," meaning "relating to citizens, befitting a citizen, courteous."
