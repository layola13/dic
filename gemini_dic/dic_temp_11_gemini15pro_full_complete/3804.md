agnominate:/əˈɡnɒmɪneɪt/| v. |命名，称呼；提名|To name or call; to nominate for office.|He was agnominated as the party's candidate for president. 他被提名为该党的总统候选人。|近义词：nominate, designate, appoint, name|反义词:dismiss, reject|词性:动词

词源: From Latin *agnominare*, from agnomen (additional name, nickname) + -are (to do).
