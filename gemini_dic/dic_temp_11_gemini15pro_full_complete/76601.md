gallium:/ˈɡæliəm/ | n. |镓|A rare silvery-white metallic element, found in trace amounts in bauxite and zinc ores.  It is liquid at temperatures slightly above room temperature. |Gallium arsenide is used in semiconductors. 砷化镓用于半导体。|Synonyms:  metal, element |Antonyms: nonmetal |noun
