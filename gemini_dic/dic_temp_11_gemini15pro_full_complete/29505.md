catarrhal:/kəˈtɑːrəl/ | adj. | 卡他性的，粘膜炎的 | Relating to or suffering from catarrh (inflammation of a mucous membrane, especially of the respiratory tract). |  He had a catarrhal cough. 他咳嗽带有粘液。| synonyms: mucous, phlegmy, rheumy | antonyms: healthy, clear | adjective

catarrhous:/kəˈtɑːrəs/ | adj. | 卡他性的，粘膜炎的 |  Affected with or relating to an excessive discharge of mucus from the nose and throat. | The catarrhous child sniffled constantly.  这个患感冒的孩子不停地抽鼻子。| synonyms: mucous, phlegmy, rheumy | antonyms: healthy, clear | adjective
