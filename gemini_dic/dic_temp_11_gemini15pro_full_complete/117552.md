megacephalous:/ˌmɛɡəˈsɛfələs/| adj.|巨头的，头大的|Having an abnormally large head.  Characterized by megacephaly, a condition in which the head is abnormally large.|The doctor diagnosed the child as megacephalous. 医生诊断这个孩子患有巨头症。|Synonyms: macrocephalic, large-headed|Antonyms: microcephalic, small-headed|adjective
