insolence: /ˈɪnsələns/ (US) /ˈɪnsələns/ (UK)| n.|傲慢；无礼；放肆|Rude and disrespectful behavior.|He responded with such insolence that he was fired on the spot. 他以如此傲慢的态度回应，当场就被解雇了。|近义词：rudeness, impudence, arrogance, haughtiness, disrespect|反义词：respect, courtesy, humility, politeness|词性:名词

insolent: /ˈɪnsələnt/ (US) /ˈɪnsələnt/ (UK)| adj.|傲慢的；无礼的；放肆的|Showing a rude and arrogant lack of respect.|Her insolent behavior towards her teacher was unacceptable. 她对她老师的无礼行为是不可接受的。|近义词：rude, impudent, arrogant, haughty, disrespectful|反义词：respectful, courteous, humble, polite|词性:形容词
