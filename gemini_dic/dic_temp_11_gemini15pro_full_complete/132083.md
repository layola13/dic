noncredible:/nɒnˈkrɛdəbl/ | adj. | 不可信的，不足信的 | Not believable; lacking credibility. |  The witness's testimony was deemed noncredible due to inconsistencies in his story.  证人的证词由于其故事中的前后矛盾而被认为不可信。

The noncredible evidence was dismissed by the judge.  法官驳回了不可信的证据。 | Synonyms: unbelievable, implausible, unconvincing, doubtful, dubious | Antonyms: credible, believable, plausible, convincing, trustworthy | word type: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + credible.  "Credible" comes from the Latin "credibilis," meaning "worthy of belief," from "credere," meaning "to believe."
