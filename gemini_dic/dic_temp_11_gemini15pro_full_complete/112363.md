los:/loʊs/ /lɒs/ | determiner, pronoun | 这些，那些（西班牙语）|  The plural form of the definite article "el" in Spanish, meaning "the". Used before masculine plural nouns. | Los libros están en la mesa. (The books are on the table.) | 近义词：(In English context) these, those | 反义词: N/A | 词性: Determiner, Pronoun
