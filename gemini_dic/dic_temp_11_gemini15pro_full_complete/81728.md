greens: /ɡriːnz/
n.
高尔夫球场上的果岭
The area of closely cut grass surrounding the hole on a golf course.
例句：
He chipped the ball onto the greens.
他把球切上了果岭。

The greens were fast and difficult to read.
果岭速度很快，很难判断。


近义词：putting green
反义词：(没有直接的反义词)
