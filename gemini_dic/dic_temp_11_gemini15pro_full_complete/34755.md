cliffed:/klɪft/ | adj. | 峭壁的，陡峭的 | Characterized by cliffs; steep. | The cliffed coastline was dramatic and beautiful. 峭壁林立的海岸线壮观而美丽。 | 近义词: steep, precipitous, sheer | 反义词: gentle, sloping | 词性: 形容词

cliffed:/klɪft/ | v. | （非正式）从悬崖上掉下来，突然失败 | (informal) To fall from a cliff, to fail suddenly and completely. |  The company cliffed after the CEO's disastrous decisions.  CEO灾难性的决定后，公司一落千丈。| 近义词: plummet, crash, fail | 反义词: succeed, thrive | 词性: 动词
