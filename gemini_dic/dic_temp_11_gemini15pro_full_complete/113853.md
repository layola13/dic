madeleine:/ˈmædlɪn/ | n. | 玛德琳蛋糕 | A small shell-shaped sponge cake. | Example:  I baked a batch of madeleines for the tea party. 我为茶会烤了一批玛德琳蛋糕。

Example:  The madeleines were light, airy, and delicious. 这些玛德琳蛋糕松软可口。 | Synonym: shell cake, petite madeleine | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "large cake" in terms of size) | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *madeleine*, named after Madeleine Paulmier, a 19th-century French cook who is said to have invented or popularized the recipe.
