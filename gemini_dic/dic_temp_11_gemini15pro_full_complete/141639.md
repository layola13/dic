optimise:/ˈɒptɪmaɪz/ (British) /ˈɑːptɪmaɪz/ (American)| v. |优化，最佳化|To make something as good as possible; to make the best use of something.|We need to optimise our website for mobile devices. 我们需要优化我们的网站以适应移动设备。|Synonyms: improve, enhance, refine, perfect|Antonyms: worsen, impair, degrade|verb
