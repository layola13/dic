kafkaesque:/ˌkɑːfəˈɛsk/| adj.|卡夫卡式的，荒诞的，噩梦般的|Characteristic of, or resembling, the literary work of Franz Kafka, especially in its nightmarish, surreal, and illogical qualities; marked by a senseless, disorienting, often menacing complexity.|例句：The bureaucratic process was kafkaesque, with endless forms and no clear explanation of the rules.  这个官僚程序就像卡夫卡小说一样，无尽的表格和不明确的规则说明。

例句：His experience with customer service was kafkaesque; he was transferred from department to department, never getting a resolution. 他与客服的经历简直是卡夫卡式的；他被从一个部门转到另一个部门，却始终得不到解决。|近义词：nightmarish, surreal, bizarre, absurd, illogical|反义词：ordinary, normal, rational, logical|词性:形容词
