chitinous:/ˈkaɪtɪnəs/| adj.|几丁质的；含几丁质的|Made of or containing chitin, a tough, semitransparent substance that forms part of the exoskeletons of insects and other arthropods.|The lobster's chitinous shell protected it from predators. 龙虾的几丁质外壳保护它免受捕食者的侵害。|近义词：horny, shell-like|反义词:soft, fleshy|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *chitineux*, from *chitine* (chitin) + *-eux* (-ous).  Ultimately from Greek *khitōn* (tunic, coat of mail).
