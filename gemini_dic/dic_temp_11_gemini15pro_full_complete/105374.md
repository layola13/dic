kickstarter:/ˈkɪkˌstɑːrtər/| n.|启动器；起动装置； Kickstarter（专有名词，指众筹平台）|A kickstarter is a device used to start an engine.  启动器是一种用来启动发动机的装置。| The project was launched on Kickstarter. 这个项目在Kickstarter上启动。|synonyms: initiator, catalyst, springboard|antonyms: obstacle, impediment|noun

kickstart:/ˈkɪkˌstɑːrt/| vt.|启动；促进；（使）快速开始|The government hopes to kickstart the economy with tax cuts. 政府希望通过减税来刺激经济。|We need to kickstart this project and get it moving. 我们需要启动这个项目，让它运转起来。|synonyms: boost, jump-start, launch|antonyms: hinder, obstruct|verb
