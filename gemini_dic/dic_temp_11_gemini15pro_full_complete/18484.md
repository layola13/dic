benchmark:/ˈbentʃmɑːrk/ (US) /ˈbentʃmɑːk/ (UK)| n. v.|基准；标准；衡量指标|A standard or point of reference against which things may be compared or assessed.|The company's profits this year serve as a benchmark for future performance.  该公司今年的利润是衡量未来业绩的基准。|synonyms: standard, criterion, gauge, yardstick, touchstone|antonyms:  deviation, anomaly|noun

benchmark:/ˈbentʃmɑːrk/ (US) /ˈbentʃmɑːk/ (UK)| v.|以…为标准衡量；评估|Evaluate or check (something) by comparison with a standard.|We need to benchmark our products against our competitors'. 我们需要将我们的产品与竞争对手的产品进行比较评估。|synonyms: assess, evaluate, measure, compare|antonyms: disregard, ignore |verb
