chumming:/ˈtʃʌmɪŋ/
noun
撒饵；chumming
The act of throwing chum (bait) into the water to attract fish.
Fishermen were chumming the waters to attract sharks. 渔民们正在撒饵以吸引鲨鱼。
近义词:baiting, luring
反义词:None
词性:n.

chumming:/ˈtʃʌmɪŋ/
verb
撒饵；chumming
To throw chum (bait) into the water to attract fish.
They were chumming for tuna. 他们在撒饵钓金鱼。
近义词:baiting, luring
反义词:None
词性:v.
