overafflicting:/ˌoʊvərəˈflɪktɪŋ/
adjective
过度折磨的，使极度痛苦的
Causing excessive affliction or suffering.  Intensifying or exacerbating affliction.
The overafflicting burden of grief overwhelmed her.  过度悲伤的重担压垮了她。
His overafflicting illness left him bedridden for months.  他极其痛苦的疾病让他卧床数月。
近义词: distressing, tormenting, harrowing
反义词: comforting, soothing, alleviating
词性: adjective
