non-intercourse:/ˌnɑnˈɪntərˌkɔːrs/ |n.| 不交往；中断往来；贸易禁运| The act of not having dealings or communication with another person, country, or organization, especially in trade or business. |  The Embargo Act of 1807 imposed non-intercourse between the United States and Great Britain.  1807年的禁运法案强制美国和英国之间中断贸易往来。| synonyms: embargo, boycott, sanction | antonyms:  intercourse, trade, exchange | noun
