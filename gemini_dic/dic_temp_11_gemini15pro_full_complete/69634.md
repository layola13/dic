feminize:/ˈfɛmɪnaɪz/ (US) /ˈfɛmɪnaɪz/ (UK)| v. |使女性化，使具有女性特征|To make something or someone more feminine or to give something feminine qualities. |例句：They tried to feminize the image of the army. 他们试图使军队的形象女性化。

例句：Exposure to certain chemicals can feminize male frogs. 接触某些化学物质会使雄蛙雌性化。

近义词：emasculate (反义词), soften, weaken|反义词: masculinize |词性:动词
