bloatware:/ˈbloʊtwɛər/| n.|预装软件，膨胀软件|Software included on a new computer or mobile device that is often considered unnecessary and unwanted, and that slows down the device's performance. It typically includes trial versions of applications or games, or applications specifically promoted by the manufacturer. |例句：My new laptop came with so much bloatware that I had to spend hours uninstalling it. 我的新笔记本电脑预装了太多膨胀软件，我不得不花几个小时卸载它们。

例句：The phone was slow because of all the bloatware pre-installed on it. 手机运行缓慢，是因为预装了太多膨胀软件。|近义词：crapware, shovelware|反义词: essential software, necessary software|词性:noun
