laissezfaireisms:/ˌlɛseɪˈfɛərizəmz/
|n.|自由放任主义|The belief in laissez-faireisms has led to deregulation in many industries. 自由放任主义导致许多行业放松管制。|
|词源: From French "laissez faire" (let do), meaning to allow to act without interference.|
|例句: The government's laissezfaireisms have been criticized for exacerbating inequality. 政府的自由放任主义因加剧不平等而受到批评。|
|例句:  Many economists argue that laissezfaireisms can stifle innovation and lead to market failures. 许多经济学家认为，自由放任主义会扼杀创新，并导致市场失灵。|
|近义词: deregulation, noninterventionism, free-market capitalism|
|反义词: interventionism, regulation, socialism, communism|
|词性: noun (plural)|
