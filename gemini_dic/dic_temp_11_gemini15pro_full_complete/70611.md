filminess:/ˈfɪlminɪs/| n.|薄膜状,薄而透明|The filminess of the fabric made it unsuitable for a winter coat.  这种布料薄如薄膜，不适合做冬衣。|例句：The wings of the butterfly had a delicate filminess. 蝴蝶的翅膀薄如蝉翼。

The old photographs had a certain filminess to them. 老照片有一种薄膜感。|近义词：thinness, transparency, gauziness|反义词: thickness, opacity|词性:名词
