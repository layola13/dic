nothingism:/ˈnʌθɪŋɪzəm/ | n. |虚无主义|The belief that life is meaningless and without objective value.  A philosophy that rejects all values and principles.|
例句：He embraced nothingism after the death of his wife. 他在妻子去世后皈依了虚无主义。
例句：The novel explores themes of existentialism and nothingism. 这部小说探讨了存在主义和虚无主义的主题。
近义词：nihilism, cynicism, pessimism |反义词: optimism, existentialism |词性: noun
