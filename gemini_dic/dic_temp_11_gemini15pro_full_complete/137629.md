nontutorially:/ˌnɒn.tjuːˈtɔː.ri.ə.li/ /ˌnɑːn.tuːˈtɔːr.i.ə.li/
adv.
非指导性地，非教程式地
Without following a tutorial or structured learning process; in a way that encourages independent exploration and discovery.
The software allows users to explore the features nontutorially.  这个软件允许用户非指导性地探索其功能。
The children learned the game nontutorially, by observing and imitating older players.  孩子们通过观察和模仿年长的玩家，非指导性地学习了这个游戏。

近义词: independently, experimentally, exploratively
反义词: tutorially, instructedly, guidedly
词性: adverb
