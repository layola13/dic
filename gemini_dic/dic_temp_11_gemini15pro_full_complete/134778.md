nonmetamorphoses: /ˌnɒnˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfəˌsiːz/ | n. | 非变形，不经历变态 | Absence of metamorphosis, the process of transformation from an immature form to an adult form in two or more distinct stages. | The nonmetamorphoses of certain insects is a fascinating area of study. 某些昆虫的非变态是一个引人入胜的研究领域。| 近义词: ametabolism, direct development | 反义词: metamorphosis, transformation | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "metamorphosis" (from Greek "meta" (change) + "morphē" (form)).
