burdening:/ˈbɜːrdənɪŋ/ | v. | 加重负担，使烦恼 |  Overloading something or someone with a heavy load, responsibility, or hardship. | The high taxes are burdening the small businesses in the area. 高额税款使该地区的个体户负担沉重。|  overloading, taxing, weighing down | relieving, easing | verb

burdening:/ˈbɜːrdənɪŋ/ | adj. | 繁重的，令人烦恼的 |  Used to describe something that is a burden or causes a burden. |  The burdening paperwork kept her at the office late. 繁重的文书工作让她在办公室待到很晚。| onerous, cumbersome, oppressive | light, easy | adjective
