neutralises:/ˈnjuːtrəlaɪzɪz/
verb
中和，抵消
to make something neutral, especially by adding a substance with the opposite chemical properties.  To counteract or cancel the effect of something.
Baking soda neutralises stomach acid. 小苏打能中和胃酸。
The company's losses were neutralised by gains in other areas. 公司在其他领域的收益抵消了损失。
synonyms: counteract, offset, balance, cancel out
antonyms: activate, enhance, intensify
词性: 动词
