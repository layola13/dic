discommodious:/ˌdɪskəˈməʊdiəs/
|adj.|
|不便的，不舒服的，不宽敞的|
|Lacking space or удобный; cramped; inconvenient.|
|The discommodious attic room was stuffy and cramped.|  那间阁楼房间又闷又窄，很不舒服。
|The small car was quite discommodious for five people.| 这辆小车对五个人来说很不方便。


|近义词：inconvenient, cramped, uncomfortable, restricted, poky|
|反义词：spacious, comfortable, convenient, roomy|
|词性: 形容词|
