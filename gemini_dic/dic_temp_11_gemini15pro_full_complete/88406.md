hirsuteness:/ˈhɜːrsjuːtnəs/ /hərˈsuːtnəs/| n. |多毛，毛发过多|The state of being hirsute; hairiness.|His hirsuteness was quite remarkable. 他的毛发非常浓密。|近义词：hairiness, villosity|反义词:smoothness, hairlessness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hirsūtusness*, from Latin hirsūtus (“rough, hairy, shaggy”) + -ness.  The Latin hirsūtus is related to hirtus (“rough, hairy”).
