balanceable:/ˈbælənsəbl/| adj.|可平衡的，可抵消的|Able to be brought into or maintained in a state of balance or equilibrium. |The gymnast executed a series of balanceable poses on the beam.  体操运动员在平衡木上完成了一系列可平衡的姿势。|The opposing forces are balanceable, resulting in a stable system. 相反的力是可平衡的，从而形成了一个稳定的系统。|近义词：equilibriable, counterpoiseable, stabilizable|反义词: unstable, unbalanced|词性:形容词

词源:  From balance (n.) + -able.  Balance itself comes from Old French "balance" (pair of scales), from Late Latin "bilancia," probably from "bis" (twice) + "lanx" (plate, scale pan).
