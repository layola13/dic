bolt-uprightness:/ˌboʊlt ˈʌpraɪtnəs/ | n. | 僵直, 笔直, 挺拔 |The state of being bolt upright; absolute straightness of posture.  It implies a rigid and often unnatural straightness. | She sat in bolt-uprightness, her back stiff and unyielding. 她僵直地坐着，背部僵硬不屈。|  The soldier maintained his bolt-uprightness during the entire ceremony.  士兵在整个仪式中保持着挺拔的姿势。|  stiffness, rigidity, erectness | slouch, laxity | noun
