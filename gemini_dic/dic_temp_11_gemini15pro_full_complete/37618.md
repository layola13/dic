communionist:/kəˈmjuːnjənɪst/ | n. | 共产主义者；圣餐信徒 | A person who believes in or supports communism; a person who partakes of Holy Communion. |  例句：The communist party gained power after the revolution. 共产党在革命后获得了权力。

例句：He is a devout communionist and attends church regularly. 他是一位虔诚的圣餐信徒，定期去教堂。 | 近义词：(For communism) Marxist, socialist, Bolshevik, red; (For Holy Communion) communicant | 反义词：(For communism) capitalist, anti-communist; (For Holy Communion)  None readily available | 词性: Noun
