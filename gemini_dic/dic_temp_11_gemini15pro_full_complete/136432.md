nonrevealing:/ˌnɒnriˈviːlɪŋ/ | adj. | 不暴露的，不泄露的 | Not revealing private or sensitive information; discreet. | Her dress was stylish yet nonrevealing. 她的裙子很时尚，但不暴露。 |  近义词: discreet, modest, concealing, uninformative | 反义词: revealing, transparent, explicit, telltale | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non, from ne (“not”) + oinom (“one”).  Indicates negation.
revealing (present participle of reveal): From Middle English revelen, from Old French reveler, from Latin revelare (“to unveil, reveal”), from re- (“back, again”) + velare (“to veil, cover”).
