demitint:/ˈdɛmɪtɪnt/| n. adj.|半色调，浅淡色|A printing technique that simulates continuous tone imagery through the use of dots, varying either in size or in spacing, thus generating a gradient-like effect. Also, a pale or subdued tint or color.|The artist used demitint to create a soft, hazy effect in the background of the painting. 艺术家用半色调在画作的背景中营造出柔和朦胧的效果。|近义词:halftone, mezzotint, wash|反义词:bright color, solid color|词性:名词, 形容词
Etymology: From French demi-teinte, from demi- "half" + teinte "tint".
