manufacturable:/ˌmænjuˈfæktʃərəbl/
adj.
可制造的；可生产的
Capable of being manufactured; suitable for manufacture.
This new material is lightweight and manufacturable.  这种新材料重量轻，可制造。
Synonyms: producible, makeable, fabricable
Antonyms: unproducible, non-manufacturable
