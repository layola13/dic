overbitterly:/ˌoʊvərˈbɪtərli/ | adv. | 过于苦涩地，极其怨恨地 |  In an excessively bitter manner, expressing strong resentment or cynicism. | He spoke overbitterly about his past experiences. 他极其怨恨地谈论他过去的经历。 | She complained overbitterly about the injustice. 她愤愤不平地抱怨不公。 | 近义词: resentfully, acrimoniously, bitterly | 反义词: sweetly, kindly, gently | 词性: 副词

over- (prefix): excessively, too much
bitter (adjective/noun): having a sharp, pungent taste or smell, not sweet; feeling or showing anger, hurt, or resentment
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs
