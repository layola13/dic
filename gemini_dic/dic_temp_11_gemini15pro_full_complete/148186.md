overshadow:/ˌoʊvərˈʃædoʊ/| vt.|使黯然失色；使相形见绌；遮蔽；使蒙上阴影|to make someone or something seem less important or impressive compared to someone or something else|Her success was overshadowed by her brother's. 她的成功被他哥哥的成就所掩盖。|synonyms: eclipse, obscure, dwarf|antonyms: highlight, emphasize|verb

overshadow:/ˌoʊvərˈʃædoʊ/| vt.|使蒙上阴影；使阴暗|to cast a shadow over something|The garden is overshadowed by tall trees.  花园被高大的树木遮蔽。|synonyms: darken, shade|antonyms: brighten, illuminate|verb
