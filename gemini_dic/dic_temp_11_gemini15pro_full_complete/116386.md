mastodon:/ˈmæstədɑːn/ (US) /ˈmæstədɒn/ (UK)| n. |乳齿象；庞然大物；巨物|An extinct elephant-like mammal of the Pliocene and Pleistocene epochs, typically larger than the modern elephant and with hairy skin and long curved tusks.  A person or thing of enormous size, power, or influence.|例句：The museum displayed a complete mastodon skeleton.  博物馆展出了一具完整的乳齿象骨架。

例句：The new skyscraper is a real mastodon, dominating the city skyline. 这座新的摩天大楼真是个庞然大物，占据了城市的天际线。|近义词：mammoth, behemoth, giant|反义词:dwarf, pygmy|词性:noun
