interpretable:/ɪnˈtɜːrprɪtəbl/ /ɪnˈtɜːrprɪtəbəl/| adj.|可解释的，可理解的|Capable of being explained or understood.  Something is interpretable if it can be given a meaning.|The results of the experiment were easily interpretable.  实验结果很容易解释。|Synonyms: understandable, comprehensible, explainable, decipherable|Antonyms: incomprehensible, unintelligible, uninterpretable|adjective
