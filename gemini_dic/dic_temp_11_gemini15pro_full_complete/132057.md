They often have a strong attachment to their local area or country and may be suspicious of foreign influences.| The small town was populated mainly by noncosmopolitans who rarely ventured beyond its borders. 这个小镇主要居住的是非世界主义者，他们很少冒险越过小镇的边界。

The noncosmopolitans in the village were wary of the new family from the city. 村里的非世界主义者对来自城市的新家庭持谨慎态度。 | 近义词: parochial, insular, provincial | 反义词: cosmopolitan, worldly, sophisticated | 词性: 名词
