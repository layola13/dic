kernelless:/ˈkɜːrnəlləs/| adj.|无核的，去核的|Lacking a kernel or core; having had the kernel or core removed.  Specifically referring to fruits like grapes or olives.|I prefer kernelless grapes because I don't like spitting out the seeds. 我更喜欢无核葡萄，因为我不喜欢吐籽。|Synonyms: seedless, pitless|Antonyms: seeded, pitted|adjective
