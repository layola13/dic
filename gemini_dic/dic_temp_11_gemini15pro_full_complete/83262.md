gymnolaema:/ˌdʒɪmnəˈliːmə/
n.
裸唇纲动物; 裸唇类; 裸唇类苔藓虫; 裸唇苔藓虫
An order of bryozoans having the mouth at the upper part of the lophophore, not overarched by a lip or epistome.  They are exclusively marine and mostly occur in shallow waters.
The gymnolaema are a large and diverse group of bryozoans. 裸唇纲苔藓虫是一个庞大而多样化的苔藓虫群体。
Gymnolaemata are characterized by a circular lophophore. 裸唇纲动物的特征是具有环状的触手冠。
近义词: bryozoan (苔藓虫),  ectoproct (外肛动物)
反义词: None (无明显的反义词，因为是指一个特定的生物分类)
词性: noun (名词)
