idlehood:/ˈaɪdlˌhʊd/| n.|懒惰，无所事事，闲散状态|A state of inactivity or unemployment, often characterized by laziness or a lack of purpose.|He spent his youth in idlehood, drifting from one meaningless job to another.  他年轻时无所事事，从一份毫无意义的工作漂到另一份。|近义词：inactivity, laziness, idleness, sloth, unemployment|反义词：activity, industry, diligence, work, employment|词性:名词

idlehood:/ˈaɪdlˌhʊd/| n.|头巾，兜帽（尤指中世纪妇女戴的那种）|A hooded garment, especially one worn by women in the Middle Ages.|The woman in the painting wore a simple idlehood. 画中的女人戴着一顶简单的兜帽。|近义词：hood, cowl, head covering|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑bareheaded 赤裸着头)|词性:名词
