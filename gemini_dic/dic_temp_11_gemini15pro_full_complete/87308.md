hermodactyl:/ˌhɜːrməˈdæktɪl/| n. |唐菖蒲球茎；蛇床子|A bulbous plant, variously identified, used medicinally in ancient and medieval times, especially as a purgative.  It is often identified with various species of Colchicum (meadow saffron).|The apothecary prescribed hermodactyl for his patient's gout.|Synonyms: meadow saffron, colchicum|Antonyms: N/A|noun
