omening:/ˈoʊmənɪŋ/ /ˈɒmənɪŋ/
n.
预兆，征兆
An event or sign that is believed to predict the future, typically something bad.
The dark clouds were an omening of the storm to come.  乌云是暴风雨即将来临的预兆。
foreboding, portent, presage
blessing, good sign
noun
