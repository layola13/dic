absurd:/əbˈsɜːrd/ /æbˈsɜːrd/| adj.|荒谬的，荒唐的，不合理的|Wildly unreasonable, illogical, or inappropriate.|The idea that he could fly was absurd. 他认为自己能飞的想法很荒谬。|近义词：ridiculous, preposterous, ludicrous, foolish, silly|反义词：reasonable, rational, sensible, logical|词性:形容词
absurdity:/əbˈsɜːrdəti/ /æbˈsɜːrdəti/| n.|荒谬，荒唐|The quality or state of being absurd.|The absurdity of the situation made us laugh.  情况的荒谬性让我们笑了。|近义词：nonsense, folly, ridiculousness|反义词：sense, rationality, logic|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French absurde, from Latin absurdus (“out of tune, discordant; incongruous, foolish”), from ab- (“from, away from”) + surdus (“deaf, dull, mute”).  The original meaning referred to something "out of harmony" or "discordant," later evolving to mean "illogical" or "ridiculous."
