jaggery: /ˈdʒæɡəri/ | n. | 粗糖，红糖 | A coarse dark brown sugar made from the sap of certain palm trees (such as the date palm, the coconut palm, or the Palmyra palm), and the sugarcane.  It is widely used in South Asia and Southeast Asia. |  Jaggery is a common ingredient in Indian sweets.  粗糖是印度甜食中常见的成分。

Jaggery is less refined than granulated sugar.  粗糖不如砂糖精制。| synonyms: gur, unrefined sugar, raw sugar | antonyms: refined sugar, white sugar | noun
