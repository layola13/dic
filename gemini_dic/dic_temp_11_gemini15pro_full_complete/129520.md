non-attributably:/ˌnɒn əˈtrɪbjʊtəbli/ | adv. |  非正式地；不具名地；匿名地 |  In a way that prevents the source of information from being known.  Used to indicate that information is provided without revealing the identity of the source. |  "The information was given non-attributably by a senior official."  一位高级官员匿名提供了这一消息。 | synonym: anonymously, off the record, unofficially | antonym: officially, on the record, attributably | adverb
