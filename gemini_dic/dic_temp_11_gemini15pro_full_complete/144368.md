outsteps:/ˌaʊtˈstɛps/
|v.|超越，胜过|To surpass or exceed someone in walking or taking steps; to go beyond the limits of something|She outstepped her authority when she made that decision. 她做那个决定的时候越权了。|
|v.|走出，迈出|To walk or step outside of something|He outstepped the bounds of decency with his rude remarks.  他粗鲁的言论超出了礼貌的界限。|
|近义词：surpasses, exceeds, transcends, outdoes|
|反义词：falls short of, lags behind|
|词性：动词|
|词源：out- + step.  "out-" 表示 "超过，在...之外"， "step" 表示 "步，步骤"|
