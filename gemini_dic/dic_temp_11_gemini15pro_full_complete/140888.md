on-wing:/ˈɒnˈwɪŋ/ /ˈɑːnˈwɪŋ/
adjective
机翼上的；在飞行中的
Relating to or carried out while an aircraft is in flight.
例句：On-wing maintenance is crucial for minimizing downtime. 机翼上的维护对于最大限度地减少停机时间至关重要。

例句：They performed an on-wing engine inspection. 他们在飞机机翼上进行了发动机检查。
近义词：airborne, in-flight
反义词：grounded
词性：adjective
