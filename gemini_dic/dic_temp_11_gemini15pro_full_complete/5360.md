alluringly:/əˈlʊərɪŋli/ | adv. | 诱人地，迷人地 | In a way that attracts or tempts someone strongly; charmingly. | She smiled alluringly at him across the room. 她隔着房间朝他妩媚一笑。|  The model posed alluringly for the camera.  模特儿摆出诱人的姿势供相机拍摄。 | 近义词: attractively, charmingly, temptingly, seductively, enticingly | 反义词: repulsively, unattractively, off-puttingly | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From allure + -ing + -ly. Allure comes from Old French alurer "to attract, entice," from a- "to" + lurer "to lure."
