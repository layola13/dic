empyesis: /ˌempɪˈiːsɪs/
noun
化脓,积脓;脓的形成
The formation or discharge of pus.
The doctor diagnosed empyesis in the patient's lungs. 医生诊断病人肺部积脓。
近义词: suppuration, festering
反义词: (No direct antonym for a pathological process)
