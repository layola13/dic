brachiopod:/ˈbrækɪəʊpɒd/ (UK) /ˈbrækioʊpɑːd/ (US)| n. |腕足动物|A marine invertebrate animal of the phylum Brachiopoda, which have a hinged shell and are superficially similar to bivalve molluscs but are not closely related.|例句：Brachiopods are often found as fossils in ancient marine rocks. 腕足动物经常以化石的形式出现在古老的海洋岩石中。

例句：The brachiopod anchored itself to the seabed with a fleshy stalk.  这只腕足动物用肉茎把自己固定在海床上。


近义词：lamp shell (informal)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
