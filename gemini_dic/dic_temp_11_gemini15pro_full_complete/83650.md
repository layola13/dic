haemorrhage:/ˈhemərɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈhɛmərɪdʒ/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|出血；大出血；（人才、资金等的）流失|A haemorrhage is a large loss of blood from a damaged blood vessel. It can also refer to a rapid loss of something valuable, like money or skilled workers.|The patient suffered a severe haemorrhage after the accident.  病人事故后大出血。|The company is haemorrhaging money due to poor management.  由于管理不善，公司资金大量流失。| synonym: bleeding, loss, outflow| antonym: clotting, inflow, gain| noun, verb
