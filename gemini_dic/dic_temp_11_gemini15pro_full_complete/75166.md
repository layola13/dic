fritzing-out:/ˈfrɪtsɪŋ aʊt/
verb phrase
行为失常；故障；发疯
acting strangely or malfunctioning, often due to technical difficulties.
My computer started fritzing-out in the middle of the presentation. 我的电脑在演示过程中开始出故障了。
The robot started fritzing-out after it fell into the water.  机器人掉进水里后开始行为失常了。
近义词: malfunctioning, glitching, going haywire, acting up, freaking out
反义词: working properly, functioning correctly
词性: verb phrase
