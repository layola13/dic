bump:/bʌmp/| n. v.|碰撞，肿块，隆起物；撞击，碰见|A bump is a raised area on a surface.  To bump into something is to hit it gently.  To bump into someone is to meet them unexpectedly.|I hit my head and got a bump. 我撞到了头，起了一个包。|近义词：knock, jolt, lump, swelling, encounter|反义词:dip, hollow, depression|词性:noun, verb

bump:/bʌmp/| v.|提升，增加|To bump something up is to increase it.|They bumped up the prices. 他们提高了价格。|近义词：increase, raise, elevate|反义词:decrease, lower, reduce|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
Likely of imitative origin, related to Dutch *bumsen “to make a hollow sound” and German bumsen “to drum, boom.”
