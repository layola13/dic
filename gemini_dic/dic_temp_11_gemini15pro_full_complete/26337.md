buzzed:/bʌzd/
adjective
兴奋的，激动的；微醉的
Excited, thrilled, or slightly intoxicated.
She was buzzed about the upcoming concert. 她对即将到来的演唱会感到兴奋。
He felt buzzed after a couple of beers.  喝了几杯啤酒后，他感到微醺。
近义词:excited, thrilled, tipsy, high
反义词:bored, depressed, sober
词性:adjective


buzzed:/bʌzd/
verb
(buzz的过去式和过去分词)发出嗡嗡声；(使)充满兴奋；打电话；快速移动
Made a buzzing sound; filled with excitement; telephoned; moved quickly.
The bee buzzed around the flower. 蜜蜂在花丛中嗡嗡作响。
The room buzzed with anticipation. 房间里充满了期待。
近义词:hum, vibrate, thrill, call, speed
反义词:quiet, still
词性:verb


词源:From the imitative word *buzz*.
