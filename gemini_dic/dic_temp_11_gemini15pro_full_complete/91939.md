hypermeter:/ˌhaɪpərˈmiːtər/ | n. | 超节拍 | A metrical unit composed of groups of measures, similar to how a measure is composed of beats.  It represents a larger-scale rhythmic structure in music. | The composer used hypermeter to create a sense of overarching phrasing in the symphony.  作曲家运用超节拍在交响曲中营造一种总体分句的感觉。 | The piece's hypermeter gives it a unique sense of propulsion. 这首曲子的超节拍赋予它一种独特的推进感。 |  meter, rhythm | N/A | noun
