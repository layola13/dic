estranger:/ɪˈstreɪndʒər/ | n. | 陌生人；异乡人；外地人 |  A person whom one does not know or with whom one is not familiar. |  He was a stranger in a strange land. 他身处异乡，举目无亲。 | 近义词: foreigner, alien, outsider | 反义词: friend, acquaintance, familiar | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old French estrange, estrangier (“foreign, strange, unfamiliar”), from Latin extraneus (“external, foreign, strange”), from extra (“outside, without”).
