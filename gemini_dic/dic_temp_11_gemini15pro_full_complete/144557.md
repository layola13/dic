outwalk:/ˌaʊtˈwɔːk/ | v. | 比…走得远；比…走得久；胜过 | To walk farther or longer than someone else; to surpass in walking. | He easily outwalked his companions on the hike. 他在徒步旅行中轻松地超过了他的同伴。|  She outwalked everyone in the group, even the experienced hikers. 她比团队里的每个人都走得远，甚至超过了有经验的徒步旅行者。 | 近义词：outpace, outhike, outdistance | 反义词：lag behind | 词性:动词
