oden:/ˈoʊdən/| n.|关东煮，杂烩菜肴|A Japanese winter dish consisting of various ingredients such as boiled eggs, processed fish cakes, and vegetables in a light broth flavored with soy sauce.|I had a bowl of hot oden on a cold winter night. 冬天晚上我吃了一碗热腾腾的关东煮。|近义词：stew, hot pot|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
