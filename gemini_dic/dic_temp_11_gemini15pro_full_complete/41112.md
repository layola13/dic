coproduced:/ˌkoʊprəˈduːst/| v. | 共同制作，联合制作 |  Produced together by two or more people or organizations. | The movie was coproduced by two major studios. 这部电影是由两家大型电影公司联合制作的。 |  The play was coproduced by the local theater and a touring company.  这个戏剧是由当地剧院和一个巡回演出公司共同制作的。 | synonyms: jointly produced, collaborated on | antonyms: solely produced, independently produced | verb

coproduction:/ˌkoʊprəˈdʌkʃən/| n. | 共同制作，联合制作 | The act of producing something together. | This film is a coproduction between France and Italy. 这部电影是法国和意大利的合拍片。 |  The international coproduction brought together actors from all over the world.  这次国际合作汇集了来自世界各地的演员。 | synonyms: joint production, collaboration | antonyms: solo production, independent production | noun
