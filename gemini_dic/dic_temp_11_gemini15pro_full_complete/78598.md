gibber:/ˈdʒɪbər/ (US), /ˈɡɪbə(r)/ (UK)| v. n.|快速而不清晰地说；胡言乱语；急促而不连贯的话|To speak rapidly and unintelligibly; to utter meaningless or incoherent sounds; rapid, incoherent talk.|He gibbered incoherently after the shock. 他在受到惊吓后语无伦次地胡说。|近义词：babble, chatter, prattle, ramble|反义词：articulate, enunciate|词性：动词, 名词

gibberish:/ˈdʒɪbərɪʃ/ (US), /ˈɡɪbərɪʃ/ (UK)| n.|胡言乱语；莫名其妙的话|Unintelligible or nonsensical talk or writing.|The message was just gibberish to me. 这条消息对我来说完全是胡言乱语。|近义词：nonsense, babble, drivel|反义词：sense, meaning|词性：名词
