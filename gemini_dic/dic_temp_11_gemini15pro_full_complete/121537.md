mismarking:/mɪsˈmɑːrkɪŋ/ | n. |错误标记，标记错误 |The mismarking of the price led to confusion at the checkout. 价格的错误标记导致结账时的混乱。| Example: The mismarking of the packages caused them to be sent to the wrong address. 包裹的错误标记导致它们被送到了错误的地址。|Synonyms: mislabeling, error, mistake, blunder | Antonyms: correct marking, accurate labeling | Noun

mismark: /mɪsˈmɑːrk/ | vt. |错误地标记 |  The cashier mismarked the item, charging me twice the price. 收银员错误地标记了商品，向我收取了两倍的价格。| Example:  I accidentally mismarked the exam papers and had to regrade them. 我不小心把试卷标记错了，不得不重新评分。| Synonyms: mislabel, label incorrectly | Antonyms: mark correctly, label correctly | Verb
