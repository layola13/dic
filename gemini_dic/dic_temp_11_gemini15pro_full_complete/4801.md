algometer:/ælˈɡɒmɪtər/ | n. | 痛觉计，测痛仪 | An instrument for measuring the sensitivity to pain, especially by determining the amount of pressure required to produce a sensation of pain. | The doctor used an algometer to assess the patient's pain threshold. 医生用痛觉计评估病人的痛阈。|  Synonyms: dolorimeter, pain meter | Antonyms: None | Noun
