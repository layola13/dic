nonsweating:/ˌnɒnˈswetɪŋ/ | adj. | 不出汗的，无汗的 | Not sweating; not producing or exuding sweat. |  The nonsweating skin of the lizard helps it conserve water. 蜥蜴不出汗的皮肤有助于它保存水分。

The scientist studied the nonsweating palms of the patient. 科学家研究了病人不出汗的手掌。| 近义词: anhidrotic, dry | 反义词: sweating, perspiring | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non "not".
sweating (present participle): From sweat (v.) + -ing.
sweat (v.): Old English swætan "to sweat," from Proto-Germanic *swaitan (source also of Old Saxon swetan, Old Norse sveita, Old Dutch swetan, Old High German sweizan, German schwitzen "to sweat").
