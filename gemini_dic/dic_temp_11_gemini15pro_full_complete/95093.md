inamissible:/ˌɪnəˈmɪsəbl/
adjective
不可接受的，不可容许的
not to be allowed, tolerated, or endured
The behavior of the fans was inamissible and resulted in several arrests.  球迷的行为是不可容许的，导致数人被捕。

近义词:unacceptable, intolerable, unforgivable, inexcusable
反义词:acceptable, tolerable, permissible, excusable
词性:adjective
词源:From French inadmissible, from in- + admissible.
