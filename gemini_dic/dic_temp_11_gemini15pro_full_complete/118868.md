metascientific:/ˌmɛtəsaɪənˈtɪfɪk/
adjective
元科学的
Relating to the philosophical study of the nature, methods, and limits of science.  It examines the assumptions, foundations, and implications of scientific inquiry.
Metascientific inquiries often explore questions about the objectivity, reliability, and social context of science. 元科学探究经常探索关于科学的客观性、可靠性和社会背景的问题。
The philosopher engaged in a metascientific analysis of the scientific method. 这位哲学家对科学方法进行了元科学分析。
Synonyms: meta-scientific, philosophy of science
Antonyms: N/A
词性: adjective
