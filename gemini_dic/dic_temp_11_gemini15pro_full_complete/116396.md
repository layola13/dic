masts: /mɑːsts/ /mæsts/ | n. |桅杆; 天线杆 |A tall, vertical spar, pole, or structure on a ship or boat, used to support sails, rigging, and other equipment. Also, a similar structure on land used to support antennas or other equipment. |The ship's masts were silhouetted against the sunset. 船的桅杆在日落映衬下形成剪影。| synonyms: spars, poles, pillars | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object. Could consider "base," "foundation" as conceptual opposites in terms of position.) | noun

masts: /mɑːsts/ /mæsts/ | n. | 天线杆 | A tall metal structure used to support antennas for radio, television, or mobile phone signals. | Several new masts have been erected to improve mobile phone coverage in the area. 为了改善该地区的手机信号覆盖范围，已经竖起了几根新的天线杆。| synonyms: towers, antennas, aerials | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun
