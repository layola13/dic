metamorphosing:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfəsɪŋ/ | adj. | 变形的，变质的，蜕变的 | undergoing metamorphosis; transforming |  The caterpillar is metamorphosing into a butterfly. 毛毛虫正在变成蝴蝶。| synonyms: transforming, changing, evolving, developing, mutating | antonyms: stagnating, fixating, regressing | verb (present participle)

metamorphosis:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfəsɪs/ | n. | 变形，变质，蜕变 | a change of the form or nature of a thing or person into a completely different one, by natural or supernatural means | The metamorphosis of the tadpole into a frog is a fascinating process. 蝌蚪变成青蛙是一个迷人的过程。| synonyms: transformation, change, alteration, mutation, conversion | antonyms: stagnation, fixity | noun

Etymology: From Greek *metamórphōsis*  "transformation," from *metamorphoun*  "to transform," from *meta* "change" + *morphē* "form."
