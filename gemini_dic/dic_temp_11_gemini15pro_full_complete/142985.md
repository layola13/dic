outbargained:/ˌaʊtˈbɑːrɡɪnd/
verb
在讨价还价中胜过; 比…会砍价
to obtain a better price or agreement than someone through bargaining
He outbargained the car salesman and got a great deal. 他与汽车销售员讨价还价，最终得到了一个非常划算的价格。
近义词: outsmart, outmaneuver, outtrade
反义词: be outmaneuvered, be outsmarted
