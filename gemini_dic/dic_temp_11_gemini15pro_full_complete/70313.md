fie:/faɪ/
interjection
呸; 啐; 唷（表示厌恶或不耐烦）
An exclamation of disgust or disapproval.
Fie! How could you say such a thing? 呸！你怎么能说出这种话？
Fie upon this terrible food! 对这糟糕的食物感到厌恶！
近义词:shame, for shame, pshaw, tsk, tut
反义词:bravo, well done, excellent


词性
interjection
