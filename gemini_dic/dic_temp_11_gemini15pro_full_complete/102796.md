jardins:/ʒaʁdɛ̃/| n. | gardens |A plural form of "jardin," meaning garden in French.  It refers to cultivated areas, often near a house, where flowers, shrubs, vegetables, or other plants are grown. |  She tends her jardins with great care. 她精心照料她的花园。|  The botanical jardins were filled with exotic plants. 植物园里充满了奇异的植物。| synonyms: gardens, yards, grounds | antonyms: wilderness, wasteland | noun
