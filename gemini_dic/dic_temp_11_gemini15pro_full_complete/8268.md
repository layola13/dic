anti-isolationism:/ˌæntiˌaɪsəˈleɪʃənɪzəm/ | n. | 反孤立主义 |  The doctrine or policy of opposing isolationism.  It advocates for international cooperation and engagement in global affairs. |  After World War II, anti-isolationism became the dominant foreign policy approach in the United States.  二战后，反孤立主义成为美国占主导地位的外交政策。

Anti-isolationism gained traction as people realized the dangers of ignoring global conflicts.  随着人们意识到忽视全球冲突的危险，反孤立主义获得了关注。 | 近义词：internationalism, globalism, interventionism | 反义词：isolationism | 词性: noun
