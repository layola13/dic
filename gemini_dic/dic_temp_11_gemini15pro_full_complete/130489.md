nonacceding:/ˌnɒnækˈsiːdɪŋ/
adjective
未加入的，未同意的，未签署的
Not agreeing to or signing a treaty or agreement; not joining a group or organization.
例句：Several nonacceding countries expressed interest in the trade agreement.  几个未加入的国家表示对该贸易协定感兴趣。

例句：The nonacceding parties were invited to observe the negotiations. 未加入的各方应邀观摩谈判。

近义词：non-signatory, dissenting, objecting, opposing
反义词：acceding, agreeing, signing, joining
词性: adjective
