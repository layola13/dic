informable:/ɪnˈfɔːrməbl/| adj.|可通知的，可告知的|Able to be informed; capable of being informed.  Something that can have information passed to it.  Often used in computer science contexts.|例句：The system is informable of any changes in the database. 系统可以被告知数据库中的任何变化。|近义词：notifiable, apprised|反义词:uninformed, ignorant|词性:形容词
词源: From "inform" + "-able". "Inform" comes from Old French "enformer," from Latin "informare"  meaning "to shape, train, instruct, educate."
