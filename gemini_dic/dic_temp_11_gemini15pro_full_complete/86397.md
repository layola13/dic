hedgingly:/ˈhedʒɪŋli/
adv.|谨慎地，规避风险地|In a way that avoids commitment or taking risks, often by using vague or noncommittal language.|He answered hedgingly, unwilling to commit to a definite position. 他回答得谨慎，不愿表明明确的立场。|Synonyms: cautiously, guardedly, evasively, ambiguously|Antonyms: directly, frankly, openly, forthrightly|词性: 副词
