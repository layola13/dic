bailment:/ˈbeɪlmənt/| n.|寄托;交付保管;保释|The delivery of goods to another person for a special purpose, with the understanding that they will be returned to the owner when the purpose has been fulfilled.|例句：The museum has the paintings on bailment.  博物馆以寄托方式持有这些画作。

例句：The dry cleaners lost my suit during the bailment.  干洗店在我寄存衣服期间把我的西装弄丢了。|近义词：safekeeping, custody, deposit|反义词:theft, larceny|noun
