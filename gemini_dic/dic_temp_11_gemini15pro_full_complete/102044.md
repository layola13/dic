isolator:/ˈaɪsəˌleɪtər/ | n. |隔离器，绝缘体|A device or material that isolates or separates something, especially preventing the transfer of electricity, heat, or sound.|The electrician installed isolators to prevent electrical shocks. 电工安装了隔离器以防止电击。|Synonyms: insulator, separator, barrier |Antonyms: conductor, connector|noun


isolators:/ˈaɪsəˌleɪtərz/ | n. |隔离器，绝缘体（复数）|Plural form of "isolator," referring to multiple devices or materials used for isolation.|The lab uses several isolators to prevent cross-contamination. 实验室使用多个隔离器来防止交叉污染。|Synonyms: insulators, separators, barriers |Antonyms: conductors, connectors|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From isolate + -or. Isolate comes from the Italian "isolato" (isolated), past participle of "isolare" (to isolate), from "isola" (island), from Latin "insula" (island).
