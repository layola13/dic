anticentralists:/ˌæntiˈsɛntrəlɪsts/
n.
反对中央集权者；地方分权主义者
People who oppose centralizing power, especially in government.  They advocate for greater autonomy and decision-making power at the local or regional level.
The anticentralists argued that concentrating power in the federal government would lead to tyranny. 反对中央集权者认为，将权力集中在联邦政府会导致暴政。
During the debates over the Constitution, anticentralists voiced concerns about a strong central government. 在关于宪法的辩论中，反对中央集权者表达了对强有力的中央政府的担忧。
近义词:decentralists, states' rights advocates
反义词:centralists, federalists
词性:noun
