markovian:/mɑːrˈkoʊviən/ | adj. | 马尔可夫的；马尔可夫过程的 | Relating to a process or system that satisfies the Markov property, where the future state depends only on the current state and not on the sequence of events that preceded it. |  The weather can be modeled as a Markovian process.  天气可以被建模为一个马尔可夫过程。| synonymous: memoryless, stochastic | antonymous: deterministic, non-Markovian | adjective

markovian:/mɑːrˈkoʊviən/ | n. | 马尔可夫模型/过程 | A Markov model or process, where the future state depends only on the current state. |  He's studying Markovian models in his probability class. 他在他的概率课上学习马尔可夫模型。| synonymous: Markov process, Markov chain | antonymous:  N/A (less common as a noun) | noun
