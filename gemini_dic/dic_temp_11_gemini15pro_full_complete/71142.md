fishtailed:/ˈfɪʃˌteɪld/| v.|鱼尾摆动，(车辆)蛇行，|To move with a swaying or swerving motion like the tail of a fish.|The car fishtailed on the icy road. 汽车在结冰的道路上蛇行。|近义词：swerve, skid, weave|反义词：(没有明确的反义词，可考虑steady, stabilize)|词性:动词
