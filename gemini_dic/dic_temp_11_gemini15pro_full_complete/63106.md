enshrouding:/ɪnˈʃraʊdɪŋ/
|adj.|笼罩的，覆盖的| enveloping completely; surrounding and concealing|A thick fog was enshrouding the city. 浓雾笼罩着城市。|近义词: enveloping, covering, obscuring, veiling|反义词: revealing, exposing|词性: Adjective

enshroud:/ɪnˈʃraʊd/
|vt.|笼罩，覆盖|to cover or enclose something completely|Mystery enshrouds the disappearance of the artifact.  文物的消失笼罩在神秘之中。|近义词: envelop, cover, conceal, veil|反义词: reveal, expose|词性: Verb

词源:
From Middle English *enshrouden, from en- + shroud.  Shroud originally referred to a garment used to wrap a body for burial, and later extended to mean any covering.
