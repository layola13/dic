full-scale:/ˌfʊlˈskeɪl/ | adj. | 全面的，大规模的，完全的 | involving or using all available resources or being at the maximum level of activity | The company launched a full-scale investigation into the incident.  公司对这起事件展开了全面调查。 |  Example: The army launched a full-scale invasion. 军队发动了全面入侵。| Synonyms: comprehensive, extensive, thorough, all-out, complete | Antonyms: limited, partial, small-scale, restricted | adjective

full-scale:/ˌfʊlˈskeɪl/ | adj. |  真实的，实际大小的 | having the same size as the original |  They built a full-scale model of the airplane. 他们建造了一个与飞机实际大小一样的模型。 | Example:  The architect presented a full-scale drawing of the building.  建筑师展示了建筑物的实际大小的图纸。 | Synonyms: life-size, actual-size | Antonyms: miniature, scaled-down | adjective
