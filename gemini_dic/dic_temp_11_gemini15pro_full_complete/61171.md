elucidative:/ɪˈluːsɪdeɪtɪv/ | adj. |  阐明的，解释性的 | Making something clear; explanatory. |  Her elucidative comments helped everyone understand the complex issue. 她的解释性评论帮助每个人理解了这个复杂的问题。| The professor's elucidative lecture clarified the confusing topic. 教授的阐明性讲座澄清了这个令人困惑的话题。| 近义词: explanatory, clarifying, illuminating, interpretive | 反义词: confusing, obscure, ambiguous, unclear | 词性: adjective

elucidate:/ɪˈluːsɪdeɪt/ | vt. | 阐明，解释 | To make something clear; explain. | Could you please elucidate your point?  你能解释一下你的观点吗？| The lawyer elucidated the complex legal jargon for the jury. 律师向陪审团解释了复杂的法律术语。| 近义词: explain, clarify, illuminate, expound | 反义词: confuse, obscure, obfuscate | 词性: verb

词源: From Latin *elucidatus*, past participle of *elucidare* "to make clear, explain," from *e-* "out" + *lucidus* "clear, bright" (from *lux* "light").
