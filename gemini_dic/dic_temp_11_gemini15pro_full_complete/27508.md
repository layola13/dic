camphene:/ˈkæmˌfiːn/ | n. |莰烯|A bicyclic monoterpene with a camphor-like odor, used in the manufacture of camphor and as a solvent. |Camphene is a component of many essential oils, including turpentine. 莰烯是许多精油的成分，包括松节油。|
Camphene is a solid at room temperature. 莰烯在室温下是固体。|
Synonyms: 2,2-dimethyl-3-methylidenebicyclo[2.2.1]heptane (IUPAC name) | Antonyms: None | noun
