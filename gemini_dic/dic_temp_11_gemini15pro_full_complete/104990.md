kennedys:/ˈkɛnɪdiz/| n.|肯尼迪家族；肯尼迪夫妇|A surname and the name of a prominent American political family.  Refers to the family of Joseph P. Kennedy Sr. and his descendants, including President John F. Kennedy, Senator Robert F. Kennedy, and Senator Edward M. Kennedy.|The Kennedys were a powerful force in American politics. 肯尼迪家族是美国政坛的一股强大力量。|Synonyms: Kennedy family|Antonyms: N/A|noun
