eucharistical:/ˌjuːkərˈɪstɪk(ə)l/
adjective
圣餐的，圣体的
relating to the Eucharist or Holy Communion.
The priest delivered a eucharistical sermon.  牧师发表了圣餐布道。
近义词:communion, sacramental
反义词:secular, profane
