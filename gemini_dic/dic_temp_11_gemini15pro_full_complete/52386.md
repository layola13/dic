didactical: /dɪˈdæktɪkəl/ /daɪˈdæktɪkəl/
adjective
教诲的; 说教的
Intended to teach, particularly in having moral instruction as an ulterior motive.  Overly instructive; tending to lecture others too much.
例句：The poem is overly didactic and lacks subtlety. 这首诗过于说教，缺乏含蓄。

例句：His didactic approach to teaching sometimes alienated his students. 他说教式的教学方法有时会疏远他的学生。

近义词：instructive, pedagogical, preachy, moralistic, edifying
反义词：nondidactic, heuristic, entertaining, amusing

词性: adjective
