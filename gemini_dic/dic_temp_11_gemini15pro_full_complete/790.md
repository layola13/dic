absorbents:/əbˈzɔːrbənts/| n.|吸收剂；吸收体|Substances that can absorb liquids or gases.|Paper towels are good absorbents for spills. 纸巾是很好的溢出物吸收剂。|近义词：absorbers, sponges|反义词:impermeables|noun

absorbents:/əbˈzɔːrbənts/| adj.|吸收性的|Having the ability to absorb liquids or gases.|The material is highly absorbent. 这种材料吸水性很强。|近义词：porous, permeable|反义词:impermeable|adjective
