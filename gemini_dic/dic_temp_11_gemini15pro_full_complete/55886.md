divisiveness:/dɪˈvaɪsɪvnəs/| n. | 分裂；分歧；不和 |The state or quality of being divisive; disagreement, discord.| The divisiveness of the issue threatened to split the party.  这个问题的分裂性威胁到要分裂该党。| 近义词：discord, disunity, schism, polarization | 反义词：unity, harmony, agreement, consensus | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From divisive + -ness.  Divisive comes from Late Latin dīvīsus, past participle of dīvidere "to divide," from dis- "apart" + -videre "to separate."
