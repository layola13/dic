desperado:/ˌdɛspəˈrɑːdoʊ/ (US), /ˌdɛspəˈreɪdəʊ/ (UK)| n. |亡命之徒；不顾一切的人|A desperate or reckless person, especially one who is a criminal.|例句：The desperado was cornered by the sheriff.  警长把那个亡命之徒逼到了绝路。

例句：He became a desperado after losing everything.  失去一切后，他变成了一个亡命之徒。|近义词：outlaw, bandit, gangster, rogue|反义词：law-abiding citizen|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *desesperado*, past participle of *desesperar* "to despair," from *des-* "dis-" + *esperar* "to hope," from Latin *sperare*.
