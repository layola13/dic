effectualness:/ɪˈfɛktʃuəlnəs/| n. |有效性，效力|The effectualness of the new policy is still under review.  新政策的有效性仍在审查中。|例句：The medicine's effectualness in treating the disease was remarkable.  这种药治疗这种疾病的效果显著。

The effectualness of the advertising campaign was evident in the increased sales.  广告活动的有效性在销售额的增长中显而易见。|近义词：effectiveness, efficacy, potency|反义词：ineffectiveness, inefficacy, impotence|词性:名词

词源: From effectual + -ness.  Effectual comes from Late Middle English, from late Latin effectualis, from Latin effectus (past participle of efficere ‘accomplish’)+ -al.
