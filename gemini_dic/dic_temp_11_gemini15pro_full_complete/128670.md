nicotic: /nɪˈkɒtɪk/ (US) /naɪˈkɒtɪk/ (UK)| adj.|尼古丁的，与尼古丁有关的|Relating to or containing nicotine, a stimulant and highly addictive alkaloid found in tobacco.| Nicotine is a highly addictive substance. 尼古丁是一种高度上瘾的物质。|Smoking is a major source of nicotic intake. 吸烟是尼古丁摄入的主要来源。|近义词: nicotine-containing, tobacco-related|反义词: nicotine-free|词性:形容词


词源:

From French *nicotique*, from *nicotine* + *-ique*. Ultimately from Latin *Nicotiana* (the tobacco plant), named after Jean Nicot, a French diplomat who introduced tobacco to France.
