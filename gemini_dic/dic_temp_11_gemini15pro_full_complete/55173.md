dissemination:/dɪˌsemɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. |传播，散布，普及|the act of spreading something, especially information, widely; circulation | The dissemination of information is crucial in a democracy. 信息的传播在民主社会中至关重要。| synonyms: spreading, distribution, circulation, propagation, diffusion | antonyms: suppression, concealment, withholding | noun

dissemination:/dɪˌsemɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. |散播，撒播（种子等）|the act of scattering or spreading something widely, like seeds | The dissemination of seeds is essential for plant reproduction.  种子的散播对于植物繁殖至关重要。| synonyms: scattering, dispersal, sowing | antonyms: gathering, collecting | noun
