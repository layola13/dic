mutual-funds: /ˈmjuːtʃuəl fʌndz/ | n. | 共同基金 | An investment program funded by shareholders that professionally manages a diversified portfolio of stocks, bonds, and other securities. |  Example: He invested a portion of his savings in mutual funds. 他将一部分积蓄投资于共同基金。

Example: Mutual funds offer a convenient way to diversify your investments. 共同基金提供了一种便捷的方式来分散你的投资。 | 近义词: investment fund, pooled fund | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps individual stocks/bonds) | noun
