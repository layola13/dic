gog:/ɡɒɡ/| n.|活力, 热情, 精力|A state of heightened activity, excitement, or energy.  Originates from the Welsh word "gog" meaning "lively" or "active".|例句：The team set off for the match with real gog.  这支队伍充满热情地出发去比赛。

例句：She approached the task with gog and determination. 她带着热情和决心来完成这项任务。|近义词：energy, enthusiasm, zeal, pep, vigor, spirit, verve, gusto|反义词：apathy, lethargy, indifference|词性:名词
