cruelnesses:/ˈkruːəltiːz/ /ˈkruːəltɪz/| n. |残忍，残酷行为|Instances of deliberate and unnecessary suffering inflicted on others.|The cruelnesses inflicted upon the prisoners of war were horrific.  战俘遭受的残酷行径骇人听闻。|
The history books documented the cruelnesses of the regime. 历史书记录了该政权的残酷行径。|近义词：atrocities, barbarities, brutalities|反义词: kindnesses, compassions, mercies |词性:名词(plural of cruelty)

cruelty:/ˈkruːəltiː/ /ˈkruːəltɪ/| n. |残忍，残酷|Behavior that causes pain or suffering to someone or something.|The cruelty of the dictator was well known.  独裁者的残忍是众所周知的。|He witnessed unimaginable cruelty during the war. 他在战争期间目睹了难以想象的残酷行为。|近义词：brutality, inhumanity, savagery|反义词: kindness, compassion, mercy |词性:名词
