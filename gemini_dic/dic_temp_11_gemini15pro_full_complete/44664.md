cruciation:/kruːʃiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 苦刑，折磨，拷问 |The act of torturing, or the state of being tortured, especially by means of a cross.  | The cruciation inflicted upon him was unbearable.  他遭受的酷刑难以忍受。 | 近义词：torture, torment, agony | 反义词：comfort, solace | 词性: noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *cruciatio, from Latin cruciatus, past participle of cruciare “to torture, torment,” from crux “cross.”
