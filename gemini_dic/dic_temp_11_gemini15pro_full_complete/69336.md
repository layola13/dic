fecundity:/fɪˈkʌndɪti/
n.
多产；富饶；肥沃
The ability to produce offspring, especially in large numbers; fruitfulness; fertility.
The fecundity of the rabbit is well known. 兔子的繁殖力是众所周知的。
fertility, productivity, prolificacy
sterility, barrenness, infertility
