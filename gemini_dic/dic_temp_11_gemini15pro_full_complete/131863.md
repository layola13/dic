nonconsumers:/ˌnɒnkənˈsjuːmərz/ | n. |非消费者|People who do not consume a particular product or service.  Those who abstain from purchasing or using certain goods or services, often for ethical, environmental, or economic reasons.| Example:  The marketing campaign failed to reach nonconsumers who were actively boycotting the brand. 这场营销活动未能触及那些积极抵制该品牌的非消费者。

Example:  The study focused on the motivations of nonconsumers in the organic food market.  这项研究的重点是有机食品市场中非消费者的动机。|Synonyms: non-users, abstainers|Antonyms: consumers, users|Noun
