architecturalist:/ˌɑːrkɪˈtɛktʃərəlɪst/
n.
建筑学家; 建筑理论家;
A person who specializes in the theory and history of architecture, or who strongly advocates a particular architectural style.  A person who studies or writes about architecture, or who designs buildings.
He's an architecturalist specializing in Gothic revival. 他是一位专门研究哥特式复兴的建筑学家。
The architecturalist argued for a return to classical principles. 这位建筑理论家主张回归古典原则。
近义词:architect, architectural historian, theorist
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) builder, developer (focus on practical aspects rather than theory)
词性: noun
