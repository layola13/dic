corporeally:/kɔːrˈpɔːriəli/
adverb
肉体地；身体上地
In a physical or bodily form; relating to the body.
The shaman claimed to be able to corporeally transport himself to the spirit world.  萨满声称能够将自己的肉身传送到精神世界。
He was corporeally present, but his mind seemed elsewhere. 他人虽然在场，但心似乎在别处。
近义词:physically, bodily, carnally
反义词:spiritually, mentally, immaterially
词性:adverb

词源:
From Late Latin *corporaliter, from Latin corporalis (“bodily”) + -iter (adverbial suffix). Equivalent to corporal +‎ -ly.
