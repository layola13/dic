mcman:/məkˈmæn/| n.|麦氏豪宅 (指一种大型、奢华，但通常被认为是建筑风格欠佳的郊区住宅)|A large, often ostentatiously designed suburban house, especially one regarded as architecturally undistinguished.  The term is often used derisively.|例句：They just built a huge mcmansion on the corner lot. 他们在拐角处建了一栋巨大的麦氏豪宅。

例句：That mcman is so big it blocks everyone's view. 那栋麦氏豪宅太大了，挡住了所有人的视线。|近义词：mansion, mega-home|反义词:bungalow, cottage|词性:noun
