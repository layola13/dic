energetic:/ˌɛnərˈdʒɛtɪk/ | adj. | 精力充沛的；充满活力的 | Showing or involving great activity or vitality. | She's a very energetic child. 她是一个精力非常充沛的孩子。 | The band gave an energetic performance. 乐队进行了充满活力的表演。 | 近义词: active, vigorous, dynamic, lively, spirited | 反义词: lethargic, inactive, apathetic, sluggish, passive | 词性: adjective

词源: Late 16th century: from French *énergétique or modern Latin energicus, from Greek energetikos, from energos ‘active’, from en- ‘in, at’ + ergon ‘work’.
