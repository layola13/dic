approach:/əˈproʊtʃ/ (US) /əˈprəʊtʃ/ (UK)| n. vt. vi.|方法，途径；接近，靠近|A way of dealing with something; to come near or nearer to something or someone.|例句：The new approach to teaching languages has been successful.  新的语言教学方法很成功。|近义词：method, way, access, near|反义词：avoid, retreat, depart|noun

approach:/əˈproʊtʃ/ (US) /əˈprəʊtʃ/ (UK)| vt. vi.|接洽，交涉；着手处理|To speak to someone about something, especially to ask for something or to offer something; to begin to deal with a task or problem in a particular way|例句：We approached the bank for a loan. 我们向银行申请贷款。|近义词：address, contact, engage, tackle|反义词：ignore, neglect, avoid|verb
