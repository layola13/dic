iodine:/ˈaɪədaɪn/ (US) /ˈaɪədiːn/ (UK)| n.|碘|A nonmetallic chemical element of the halogen group, atomic number 53, that is a grayish-black crystalline solid at ordinary temperatures, sublimes to a violet vapor, and is used in medicine as an antiseptic.|

例句：Iodine is used as an antiseptic. 碘被用作消毒剂。

例句：A lack of iodine can cause thyroid problems. 缺碘会导致甲状腺问题。

近义词：None (as an element) |反义词：None (as an element)|词性：noun
