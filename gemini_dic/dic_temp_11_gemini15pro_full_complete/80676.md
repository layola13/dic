gorge:/ɡɔːrdʒ/ (US) /ɡɔːdʒ/ (UK)| n. v.|峡谷；狼吞虎咽|A deep, narrow valley with steep, rocky sides.  To eat greedily and in large amounts.|例句：We hiked through the spectacular gorge. 我们徒步穿越了壮观的峡谷。|近义词：canyon, ravine, chasm, devour, gulp, gobble|反义词: none (for noun), nibble (for verb)|noun, verb

gorge:/ɡɔːrdʒ/ (US) /ɡɔːdʒ/ (UK)| v.|使吃饱；使厌烦|To fill completely or to the point of disgust.|例句：I gorged myself on the delicious buffet. 我在美味的自助餐上吃了个饱。|近义词：satiate, stuff, surfeit|反义词: starve|verb
