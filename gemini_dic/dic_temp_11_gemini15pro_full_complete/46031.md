cyberthreat:/ˈsaɪbərθrɛt/| n.|网络威胁|A threat that originates or occurs in cyberspace. This can include things like data breaches, denial-of-service attacks, malware infections, and phishing scams.|The company faced a serious cyberthreat that compromised customer data.  该公司面临严重的网络威胁，导致客户数据泄露。|Synonyms: cyberattack, cyber risk, online threat, digital threat|Antonyms: cybersecurity, cyber defense|noun
