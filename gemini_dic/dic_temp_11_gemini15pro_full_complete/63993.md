epistolar:/ɪˈpɪstəˌlər/ |adj.|书信体的，用书信表达的|Relating to or written in the form of letters. |Her novel is epistolar, told entirely through letters exchanged between the two main characters. 她的小说是书信体的，完全通过两个主要人物之间的书信往来讲述。|近义词：letter-based, correspondence-based|反义词：non-epistolar|词性：形容词
