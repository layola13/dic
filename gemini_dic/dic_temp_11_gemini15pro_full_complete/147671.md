overpotencies:/ˌoʊvərˈpoʊtənsiz/
| n. | 过度效力; 超效力; 过度增强 |  A state of having excessive potency or strength, especially in relation to homeopathic remedies.  It refers to the extremely high dilutions used in homeopathy, where substances are diluted to the point where there is theoretically no original substance left. These high dilutions are believed by homeopaths to have increased therapeutic power. | Homeopaths sometimes use overpotencies to treat chronic conditions. 顺势疗法有时会使用超效力来治疗慢性病。| synonyms: ultra-high dilutions, high potencies | antonyms: low potencies, crude substances | noun
