inbetweeners: /ˌɪnbɪˈtwiːnəz/ | n. | 中间人；夹在中间的人；青少年；介于两者之间的人 | People who are not in a particular group or category, often referring to adolescents who don't quite fit in with any of the established social groups. |  The show "The Inbetweeners" portrays the awkward lives of a group of teenage inbetweeners.  这部剧《中间人》描绘了一群青少年中间人的尴尬生活。 | Synonyms:  outsiders, misfits, adolescents, teenagers | Antonyms: insiders, conformists | noun

inbetweener: /ˌɪnbɪˈtwiːnə(r)/ | adj. | 中间的；过渡的 |  Located or existing between two points, stages, or things.  | He’s in that awkward inbetweener stage between childhood and adulthood. 他正处于童年和成年之间那个尴尬的过渡阶段。 | Synonyms:  intermediate, transitional, intermediary | Antonyms: extreme, ultimate | adjective
