nuisancey:/ˈnjuːsnsɪ/| adj.|讨厌的，令人讨厌的|Annoying, troublesome, or inconvenient.  Something that is slightly annoying or inconvenient, often in a persistent way.  It implies a mild form of nuisance.  It’s more informal than "nuisance" and often used colloquially. |That dripping faucet is getting really nuisancey.  那个滴水的水龙头真烦人。|近义词：annoying, irritating, bothersome, troublesome, pesky|反义词:pleasant, agreeable, convenient|词性:adjective
Etymology:  From "nuisance" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Nuisance" comes from Old French "nuisance" (harm, injury), ultimately from Latin "nocēre" (to harm).
