darned:/dɑːrnd/| adj. | 讨厌的，可恶的；（用来加强语气）非常的 | Used as a mild oath or to express annoyance or emphasis.  It's a euphemism for "damned".|  "This darned computer keeps crashing!" 这该死的电脑老是崩溃！ | Synonyms: cursed, blasted, confounded | Antonyms: blessed, fortunate | Adjective

darned:/dɑːrnd/| v. | 织补，缝补 | To mend a hole in fabric by weaving yarn across it.|  "She darned the hole in her sock." 她把袜子上的洞补好了。 | Synonyms: mend, repair, patch | Antonyms: tear, rip | Verb
