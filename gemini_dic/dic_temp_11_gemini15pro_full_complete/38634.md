conculcate:/ˈkɒŋkʌlkeɪt/ /ˈkɑːŋkʌlkeɪt/
verb
反复灌输；谆谆教诲
To impress (something) upon the mind of another by frequent repetition or persistent urging.
The teacher conculcated the importance of honesty in her students. 老师反复向学生们灌输诚实的重要性。
instill, implant, ingrain, inculcate
ignore, neglect, disregard
v.
