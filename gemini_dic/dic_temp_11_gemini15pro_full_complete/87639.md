hewn:/hjuːn/
adjective
凿成的；劈成的
shaped or cut with a sharp tool, especially an axe
The statue was hewn from a single block of marble. 这座雕像是由一整块大理石凿成的。

近义词:carved, sculpted, chipped, cut
反义词:uncut, natural, unshaped
词性:adjective
