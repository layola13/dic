outbuys:/ˌaʊtˈbaɪz/
|v.|胜过购买；出价高于|Outbidding someone in a purchase; spending more than another buyer. |The company outbuys its competitors to secure the best resources.  这家公司出价比竞争对手高以获得最好的资源。|Synonyms: outbids, surpasses, trumps|Antonyms: underbids, loses to|verb

outbuys:/ˌaʊtˈbaɪz/
|n.|胜过购买的行为；出价更高的行为|The act of outbidding someone; spending more than another buyer. |The company's outbuys have given them a significant advantage in the market.  该公司的胜过购买行为使其在市场上获得了显著优势。|Synonyms: outbidding, surpassing, trumping|Antonyms: underbidding, losing to|noun
