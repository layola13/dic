inspected:/ɪnˈspɛktɪd/
verb
检查过的，检验过的
past participle of inspect: having been examined closely.
The produce was carefully inspected before being shipped.  这些农产品在装运前都经过仔细检查。
近义词: examined, checked, scrutinized, reviewed
反义词: ignored, overlooked, neglected
词性:  Verb (past participle)

inspect:/ɪnˈspɛkt/
verb
检查，检验
to look at something or someone closely in order to discover information, especially about their quality or condition.
The health inspector inspected the kitchen for cleanliness.  卫生检查员检查了厨房的清洁情况。
近义词: examine, check, scrutinize, review
反义词: ignore, overlook, neglect
词性: Verb
