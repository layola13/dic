doctoress:/ˈdɒktrɪs/ (UK) /ˈdɑːktrɪs/ (US)| n. |女医生；女博士|A female doctor or a woman who holds a doctoral degree.  This term is now largely considered outdated and potentially sexist. It is generally preferable to use "doctor" for both men and women.|例句：In the 19th century, the term "doctoress" was common.  19世纪，“doctoress”这个词很常见。

例句：She was referred to as a doctoress in old medical journals. 在旧的医学期刊中，她被称为女医生。|近义词：doctor, physician (for medical doctors), Ph.D. holder (for doctoral degree holders)|反义词: patient (if referring to medical context), student (if referring to academic context)|词性:noun
