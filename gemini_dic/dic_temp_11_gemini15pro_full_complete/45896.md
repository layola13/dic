cutin:/ˈkʌtɪn/ | n. | 角质层; 表皮; | A fatty substance forming a protective layer on the surface of plants, fruits, insects, etc. | The apple's cutin made it shiny.  苹果的角质层使它看起来很亮。|Synonyms: cuticle, bloom | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "porous surface") | noun

cutin:/ˈkʌtɪn/ | n. | 插播; 插入镜头; 插嘴; |  An interruption in a broadcast for a news bulletin or advertisement; an abrupt transition from one film sequence to another. |  The news program was interrupted by a cutin about the breaking news.  新闻节目被一则突发新闻插播打断。 | Synonyms: interruption, interjection, insert | Antonyms: continuation,  seamless transition | noun
