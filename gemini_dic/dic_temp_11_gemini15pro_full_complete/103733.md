jostle: /ˈdʒɒsəl/ (British) /ˈdʒɑːsəl/ (American)
| v. n. | 推挤；争夺；竞争 | To push roughly against someone in a crowd; to compete strongly with someone for something. |  The shoppers jostled each other to get to the sale items. 顾客们互相推搡着去抢购打折商品。| synonyms: shove, push, elbow, bump, vie, contend, compete | antonyms: yield, give way, cooperate | verb
