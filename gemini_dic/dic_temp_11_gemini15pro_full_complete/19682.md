Bilderberg: /ˈbɪldərbɜːrɡ/ | n. | 比尔德伯格（集团）; 比尔德伯格会议 |  A secretive annual meeting of around 130 influential people from the fields of politics, business, finance, media, and academia.  The first meeting was held at the Hotel de Bilderberg in Oosterbeek, Netherlands, in 1954. | The Bilderberg meeting is shrouded in secrecy. 比尔德伯格会议笼罩在秘密之中。 |  The Bilderberg Group has been accused of manipulating global events. 比尔德伯格集团被指控操纵全球事件。 | synonyms: conference, meeting, summit, forum | antonyms: N/A | noun
