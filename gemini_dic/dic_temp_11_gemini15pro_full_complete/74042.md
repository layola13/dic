fosa: /ˈfoʊsə/ (US), /ˈfɒsə/ (UK) | n. | fossa | A cat-like mammal endemic to Madagascar. |  The fossa is the largest mammalian carnivore on the island of Madagascar.  福萨是马达加斯加岛上最大的哺乳类食肉动物。|  近义词: carnivore, predator | 反义词: prey | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Malagasy *fūsa, ultimately from Proto-Malayic-Polynesian *puSaŋ.
