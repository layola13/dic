nephron:/ˈnɛfrɒn/ (UK), /ˈnɛfrɑːn/ (US)| n. |肾单位|Each of the functional units in the kidney, consisting of a glomerulus and its associated tubule, through which the glomerular filtrate passes before emerging as urine.|The nephrons filter waste products from the blood. 肾单位过滤血液中的废物。|Synonyms: renal unit,  kidney unit |Antonyms: None|Noun


nephrons:/ˈnɛfrɒnz/ (UK), /ˈnɛfrɑːnz/ (US)| n. |肾单位（复数）|The plural form of nephron, referring to multiple functional units in the kidney.|Healthy kidneys contain millions of nephrons. 健康的肾脏包含数百万个肾单位。|Synonyms: renal units, kidney units|Antonyms: None|Noun
