hippuric:/hɪˈpjʊərɪk/| adj.|马尿酸的；由马尿酸构成的|Relating to or derived from hippuric acid, a crystalline acid formed in the liver of herbivorous animals and excreted in the urine.|Horses excrete large amounts of hippuric acid in their urine, hence the name. 马匹在尿液中排出大量的马尿酸，因此得名。| 近义词:  (No direct synonyms, but related terms include "benzamidoacetic" which is a chemical synonym for hippuric acid, and terms relating to equine urine excretion.) | 反义词:  None.|词性: Adjective

hippuric:/hɪˈpjʊərɪk/| n.|马尿酸|A crystalline acid formed in the liver of herbivorous animals and excreted in the urine.|Hippuric acid levels in urine can be used as a biomarker for toluene exposure. 尿液中马尿酸的水平可以用作甲苯暴露的生物标志物。| 近义词: benzamidoacetic acid| 反义词:  None.|词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *hippurique*, from Ancient Greek ἵππος (híppos, “horse”) + οὖρον (oûron, “urine”).  The acid was first isolated from horse urine.
