memorate: /ˈmɛməreɪt/ | v. | 纪念，庆祝 | To celebrate or commemorate an event or person, especially with a ceremony or by creating a memorial. |  We will memorate the anniversary of the founding of our city. 我们将庆祝我们城市成立的周年纪念日。

The statue was erected to memorate the fallen soldiers. 这座雕像是为了纪念阵亡的士兵而建立的。 | 近义词：commemorate, celebrate, observe | 反义词：forget, ignore | 词性: verb

Etymology:  From Latin *memoratus*, past participle of *memorare* "to bring to remembrance," from *memor* "mindful."
