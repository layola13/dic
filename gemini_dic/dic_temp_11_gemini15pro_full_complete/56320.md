dogmas:/ˈdɒɡməz/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡməz/ (US)| n. |教条，信条， dogma的复数形式|A dogma is a belief or set of beliefs that is accepted by the members of a group without being questioned or doubted. |例句：The politician challenged the established dogmas of the party.  这位政治家挑战了该党既定的教条。

例句：  Many religious dogmas are difficult for modern people to accept. 许多宗教教条对于现代人来说难以接受。|近义词：tenets, doctrines, beliefs, creeds|反义词：heresies, doubts, skepticism|词性: Noun (Plural)
