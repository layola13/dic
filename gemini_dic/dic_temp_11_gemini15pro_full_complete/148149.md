oversentimental: /ˌoʊvərˌsɛntɪˈmɛntəl/| adj.|过度感伤的，多愁善感的|Excessively sentimental; showing or expressing too much sentiment, often to the point of being maudlin or mawkish.|She wrote an oversentimental poem about the death of her goldfish.|近义词：maudlin, mawkish, saccharine, cloying, sentimental|反义词：unsentimental, pragmatic, hard-headed|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + sentimental.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Sentimental" comes from the French "sentiment," ultimately from the Latin "sentire," meaning "to feel." Thus, "oversentimental" literally means "too much feeling."
