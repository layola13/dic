countertradition:/ˌkaʊntərtrəˈdɪʃən/ |n.|反传统，对抗传统|A belief or practice that goes against the established traditions or norms.|The artist's work represents a countertradition to the prevailing artistic styles of the time. 这位艺术家的作品代表了对当时主流艺术风格的反传统。|近义词: rebellion, dissent, nonconformity|反义词: tradition, conformity, orthodoxy|词性:名词

countertradition:/ˌkaʊntərtrəˈdɪʃən/ |n.|对抗传统|A movement or set of ideas that opposes or challenges established traditions.|The feminist countertradition questioned traditional gender roles and expectations. 女权主义的反传统思潮质疑了传统的性别角色和期望。|近义词: challenge, opposition, resistance|反义词: tradition, conformity, orthodoxy|词性:名词
