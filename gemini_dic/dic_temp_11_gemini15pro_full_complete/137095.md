nonsubconscious:/nɒnˌsʌbˈkɒnʃəs/ | adj. | 非潜意识的，非下意识的 | Not pertaining to the subconscious; conscious or preconscious. |  Her nonsubconscious decision to wear red was a bold statement. 她非下意识地决定穿红色，这是一个大胆的声明。|  He made a nonsubconscious effort to remember her name. 他有意识地努力记住她的名字。 | 近义词: conscious, deliberate, intentional | 反义词: subconscious, unconscious, involuntary | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "subconscious" (adjective meaning "existing or operating in the mind beneath the level of conscious awareness").  The word "subconscious" itself comes from "sub-" (prefix meaning "under" or "below") + "conscious" (adjective meaning "aware of and responding to one's surroundings").
