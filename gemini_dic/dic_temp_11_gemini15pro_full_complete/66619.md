exilers:/ˈeksaɪlərz/
n.
流亡者，放逐者
People who are forced to live outside their own country.
The exilers longed to return to their homeland. 这些流亡者渴望回到自己的祖国。
refugees, deportees, displaced persons
natives, residents, citizens
