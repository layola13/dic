The tumor began to colliquate, spreading throughout the body. 肿瘤开始崩坏，扩散到全身。|近义词：melt, dissolve, liquefy, disintegrate, decay|反义词：solidify, harden, freeze|词性:动词

Etymology: From Latin *colliquāre*, from *com-* (together) + *liquāre* (to liquefy), from *liquidus* (liquid).
