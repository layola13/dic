cadilesker:/ˌkædɪˈlɛskər/ | n. | 下级官员；跟班；小人物 | A person of low rank or importance, especially a subordinate or hanger-on.  Derived from Turkish "kadiasker" meaning a military judge or administrator. | He was just a cadilesker, fetching coffee and making copies for his boss. 他只是个小喽啰，为老板端茶倒水，复印文件。 |  Synonym: underling, minion, lackey | Antonym: leader, boss, chief | noun
