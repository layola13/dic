loose-limbed:/ˌluːsˈlɪmd/| adj.|四肢放松的，行动笨拙的|Having relaxed and flexible limbs, often suggesting a casual or ungainly movement.|例句：The loose-limbed dancer moved with effortless grace.  四肢放松的舞者轻松优雅地舞动着。

例句：The young puppy, still loose-limbed and clumsy, tumbled across the lawn.  这只小狗仍然四肢无力，笨手笨脚地跌跌撞撞地穿过草坪。|近义词：relaxed, supple, lanky, gangly|反义词:stiff, rigid, tense|词性:adjective

Etymology:
"loose" (Old English *lēas) + "limbed" (from "limb" - Old English lim)
