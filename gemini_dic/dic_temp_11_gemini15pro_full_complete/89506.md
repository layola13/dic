hongi:/ˈhɒŋi/ (UK), /ˈhɔːŋi/ (US)| n.|碰鼻礼|A traditional Māori greeting in New Zealand, performed by pressing noses together.|Example 1: The hongi is a powerful symbol of unity and connection in Māori culture.  碰鼻礼是毛利文化中团结和联系的有力象征。

Example 2:  They exchanged a hongi as a sign of respect and welcome. 他们互碰鼻子以示尊重和欢迎。|近义词:greeting, salute|反义词:farewell, parting|Noun
