multi-articulated:/ˌmʌlti-ɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 多关节的；多节的 | Having many joints or articulations.  This refers to something that is composed of multiple segments connected by joints, allowing for a wide range of movement. |  A multi-articulated robot arm can perform complex tasks.  一个多关节的机械臂可以执行复杂的任务。

The millipede is a multi-articulated creature.  千足虫是一种多节的生物。 | Near Synonyms: jointed, segmented, articulated | Antonyms: rigid, inflexible | Word type: Adjective
