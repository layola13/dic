crustiest:/ˈkrʌstiɪst/| adj.|最硬的，最粗糙的，最坏脾气的|Superlative form of crusty: having or resembling a hard outer surface or crust.  Figuratively, it can also refer to a grumpy or bad-tempered person.|The crustiest bread I've ever tried was like biting into a rock. 我吃过的最硬的面包就像咬一块石头一样。|The crustiest old man yelled at the children playing in the street.  脾气最暴躁的老头对着街上玩耍的孩子们大喊大叫。|近义词：hardest, toughest, grumpiest, surliest|反义词:softest, smoothest, kindest, gentlest|词性:adjective
词源:From "crust" (Old French "crouste", from Latin "crusta") + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives) + "-est" (superlative suffix)
