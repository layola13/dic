acculturated: /əˈkʌltʃəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 文化适应的，融入的 | Having adapted to a new culture or adopted its customs and values. |  The acculturated immigrants easily blended into the new society. 融入新文化的移民很容易融入新社会。| synonyms: assimilated, adapted, integrated | antonyms: unacculturated, alienated, isolated | adjective

acculturate: /əˈkʌltʃəreɪt/ | v. | 使文化适应，使融入 | To adapt to a new culture or adopt its customs and values. |  Many immigrants strive to acculturate to the local customs. 许多移民努力适应当地风俗。| synonyms: assimilate, adapt, integrate | antonyms: alienate, isolate | verb

Etymology:  From Latin ad- "to" + cultura "culture."  The verb form "acculturate" appeared first, with the adjective "acculturated" derived from it.
