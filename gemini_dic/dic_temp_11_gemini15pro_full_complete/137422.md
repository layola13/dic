nontexturally:/nɒnˈtɛkstʃərəli/
adjective
非结构地，非质地地
Relating to aspects other than texture; not in terms of texture.
The artist explored the canvas nontexturally, focusing on color and form rather than the feel of the paint. 艺术家非结构地探索画布，专注于色彩和形式，而不是颜料的感觉。
Near Synonyms: non-structurally, non-materially
Opposite: texturally
词性: adjective
