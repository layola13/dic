drapabilities:/ˌdreɪpəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |悬垂性，垂褶性|The quality of a fabric that allows it to hang in folds; the way in which a fabric hangs.| The dress's drapabilities were exquisite, flowing gracefully around her. 这条裙子的悬垂性极佳，在她周围优雅地飘动。|近义词：flow, drape, hang|反义词：stiffness, rigidity|词性:noun

Etymology: From "drape" (v.) + "-ability" (suffix indicating capacity or quality). "Drape" comes from Old North French "draper" meaning "to arrange cloth."
