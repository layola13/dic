actinolitic:/ˌæktɪnəˈlɪtɪk/| adj.|阳起石的，含阳起石的|Relating to, containing, or resembling actinolite (a bright green or greyish-green amphibole mineral).|The rock sample contained actinolitic crystals. 这块岩石样本包含阳起石晶体。|The actinolitic schist glittered in the sunlight. 阳起片岩在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词:amphibolitic|反义词: (No direct antonym, could use terms describing rocks lacking actinolite, e.g., quartzose, feldspathic)|词性:adjective
