junky:/ˈdʒʌŋki/| n. adj.|瘾君子；破烂的；质量差的|A person addicted to drugs, especially heroin. Something of poor quality or in a deteriorated condition.|例句：He was a junky for most of his adult life. 他成年后大部分时间都是个瘾君子。|近义词：addict, junkie, trashy, shoddy|反义词：healthy, functional, high-quality|词性:名词, 形容词
junky:/ˈdʒʌŋki/| adj.|破烂的；质量差的|Something of poor quality or in a deteriorated condition.|例句：The car was a junky piece of metal. 这辆车是一堆破铜烂铁。|近义词：trashy, shoddy, cheap|反义词：high-quality, durable|词性:形容词
