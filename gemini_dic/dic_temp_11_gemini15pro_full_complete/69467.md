feistiest:/ˈfaɪstiɪst/
adjective
最活跃的；最有精神的；最易怒的
Most spirited, lively, or irritable.
She's the feistiest member of the team and always ready for a challenge. 她是最有活力的团队成员，随时准备迎接挑战。
The feistiest puppy in the litter was always the first to explore.  这一窝里最活泼的小狗总是第一个去探索的。
近义词: spirited, lively, energetic, spunky, fiery
反义词: lethargic, apathetic, listless, passive, calm
词性: adjective
词源: From feisty + -est (superlative suffix). Feisty itself is of uncertain origin, possibly from fisty (meaning "ready to use one's fists") or a dialectal alteration of ficey (a small, lively dog).
