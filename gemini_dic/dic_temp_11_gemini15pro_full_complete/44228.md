crocking:/ˈkrɒkɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkrɑːkɪŋ/ (US)| n. | 染色转移，掉色 | The transfer of dye from one fabric to another by rubbing. |  The red dye in my new jeans caused crocking onto my white sofa. 我的新牛仔裤上的红色染料蹭到了我的白色沙发上。| 近义词：bleeding, rubbing off | 反义词: colorfastness | 词性: noun

crock:/krɒk/ (UK) /krɑːk/ (US)| v. | (使)掉色，(使)染色转移 | To transfer dye from one fabric to another by rubbing. | The new towels crocked badly and stained the other laundry. 新毛巾掉色严重，弄脏了其他衣物。| 近义词：bleed, rub off | 反义词: be colorfast | 词性: verb
