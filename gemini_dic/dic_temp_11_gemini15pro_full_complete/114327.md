maist:/meɪst/ | adj. | 最多的，最大的 | Most; largest.  Primarily used in Scottish English. | The maist important thing is yer health.  最重要的东西是你的健康。 | 近义词: most, largest, biggest | 反义词: least, smallest | 词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *maist, *mest, from Old English *mǣst, a superlative form of *micel (“much, great”). Cognate with Scots maist (“most”), West Frisian meast (“most”), Dutch meest (“most”), German meist (“mostly”), Danish mest (“most”).
