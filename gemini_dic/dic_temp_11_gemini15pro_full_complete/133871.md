nongravitationally: /ˌnɒnˌɡrævɪˈteɪʃənəli/ (British) /ˌnɑːnˌɡrævɪˈteɪʃənəli/ (American)
adverb
非重力地
In a manner not influenced by gravity; excluding the effects of gravity.
The particles dispersed nongravitationally, driven by electromagnetic forces.  这些粒子非重力地分散，由电磁力驱动。
The spacecraft maneuvered nongravitationally using its thrusters. 飞船使用推进器进行非重力机动。
Near synonyms:  without gravity,  not gravitationally
Antonym: gravitationally
詞性: adverb
