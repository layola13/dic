chabuk:/tʃəˈbʊk/| n.| 马鞭；鞭打|A whip, typically made of leather or hide, used for driving animals or administering punishment. |He cracked the chabuk across the horse's back. 他用马鞭抽打马背。|Synonym: whip, lash, scourge|Antonym: caress, soothe|Noun
