bung-up:/ˌbʌŋˈʌp/ | adj. | 损坏的；受伤的；堵塞的 | Damaged, injured, or blocked. |  The car was pretty bunged-up after the accident.  这辆车在事故后损坏严重。| 近义词: damaged, broken, injured, blocked | 反义词: repaired, fixed, healed, clear | 词性: 形容词

bung-up:/ˌbʌŋˈʌp/ | n. | 碰撞；损伤；堵塞 | A collision, injury, or blockage. | He got a nasty bung-up on his knee playing football. 他踢足球时膝盖受了重伤。 | 近义词: injury, damage, blockage | 反义词: repair, healing, clearing | 词性: 名词


词源:  Likely a combination of "bung," meaning to close or stop up, and "up," intensifying the action.
