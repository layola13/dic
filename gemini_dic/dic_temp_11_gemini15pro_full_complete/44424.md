cross-pollinates:/ˌkrɒs ˈpɒləneɪts/ /ˌkrɑːs ˈpɑːləneɪts/| v.|异花授粉，互相影响|The wind cross-pollinates the flowers. 风给花朵异花授粉。|Synonyms: cross-fertilize, interbreed|Antonyms: self-pollinate, inbreed|verb

cross-pollination:/ˌkrɒs ˌpɒləˈneɪʃən/ /ˌkrɑːs ˌpɑːləˈneɪʃən/| n.|异花授粉，互相影响|Cross-pollination between different varieties of plants can lead to new hybrids. 不同品种植物之间的异花授粉可以产生新的杂交品种。|Synonyms: cross-fertilization, interbreeding|Antonyms: self-pollination, inbreeding|noun
