apaume:/əˈpɔːm/| adj.|手掌张开的，(尤指芭蕾舞姿)手心向外的|With hands turned outward, especially in ballet; having the palms turned outward. |She held her hands apaume, a classic ballet pose. 她手掌向外，摆出一个经典的芭蕾舞姿。|近义词:outward, open (regarding hand position)|反义词:inward, closed (regarding hand position)|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *à paume*, literally "to the palm".  From *à* (to) + *paume* (palm).  Ultimately from Latin *palma* (palm).
