circumspectly:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌspektli/ | adv. | 谨慎地，小心地 |  In a way that shows a careful consideration of any possible risks or consequences. | She moved circumspectly through the dark room, careful not to bump into anything. 她小心翼翼地穿过黑暗的房间，小心不要碰到任何东西。|  He circumspectly avoided answering the controversial question. 他谨慎地避免回答这个有争议的问题。 | cautiously, prudently, carefully, discreetly | recklessly, carelessly, heedlessly | adverb
