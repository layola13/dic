myriarch: /ˈmɪriɑːrk/ | n. | 指挥万人者；统治者；总督 | A ruler of ten thousand people; a commander of a large division of an army. |  The myriarch led his troops into battle.  这位万人统帅率领他的部队投入战斗。 | The myriarch oversaw the province with a firm hand.  这位总督以铁腕统治该省。 | 近义词: ruler, governor, commander, leader | 反义词: follower, subordinate | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek μυριάς (murías, “ten thousand”) +‎ -arch (“ruler”).
