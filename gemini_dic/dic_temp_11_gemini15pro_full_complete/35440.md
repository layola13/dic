co-regnant:/koʊˈreɪɡnənt/
| adj. |共同执政的；共同统治的|Sharing the throne or reigning at the same time.|Example: The co-regnant monarchs ruled wisely and justly. 共同执政的君主统治英明而公正。|Example:  The two emperors were co-regnant for a decade. 两位皇帝共同执政了十年。|近义词：jointly ruling, co-reigning|反义词：sole ruler, autocrat|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "co-" (together) + "regnant-" (reigning), present participle of "regnare" (to reign). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *reg- (to move in a straight line, to direct, to rule).
