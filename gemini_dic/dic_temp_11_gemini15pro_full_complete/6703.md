anallantoic:/ˌælənˈtɔɪɪk/
adjective
尿囊的；[胚] 尿囊膜的
Relating to or resembling the allantois, an extraembryonic membrane of reptiles, birds, and mammals that functions in respiration and excretion.
The anallantoic membrane plays a crucial role in embryonic development. 尿囊膜在胚胎发育中起着至关重要的作用。
The developing chick relies on the anallantoic sac for gas exchange. 发育中的小鸡依靠尿囊进行气体交换。
近义词: allantoidal
反义词: (No specific antonym)
词性: adjective

词源:
From Greek *allantoeides*, from *allanto*-  "sausage, allantois" + *-eides* "having the form of". The allantois is named for its sausage-like shape.
