nonprocreative:/ˌnɒnproʊˈkriːeɪtɪv/
adjective
非生育的，不生育的
Not involving or intended for procreation (the production of offspring).
例句：They chose to live a nonprocreative lifestyle. 他们选择过一种非生育的生活方式。

例句：Nonprocreative sex is becoming more accepted in modern society.  非生育性行为在现代社会越来越被接受。

近义词：childless, infertile, sterile
反义词：procreative, fertile
词性: adjective
