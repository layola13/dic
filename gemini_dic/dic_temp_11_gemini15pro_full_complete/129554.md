non-business:/ˌnɒnˈbɪznɪs/ /ˌnɑːnˈbɪznɪs/| adj.|非商业的，非业务的|Not related to business or commerce; not intended for profit or professional use.|例句：She made some non-business calls during work hours.  她在工作时间打了一些私人电话。

例句：The trip was for non-business purposes. 这次旅行不是为了公事。|近义词：personal, private, noncommercial|反义词：business, commercial|词性：形容词
