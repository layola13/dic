ethologist:/iːˈθɒlədʒɪst/ /iˈθɑːlədʒɪst/
n.
动物行为学家
A scientist who studies animal behavior, especially in their natural environment.
Jane Goodall is a renowned ethologist known for her work with chimpanzees. 简·古道尔是一位著名的动物行为学家，以她对黑猩猩的研究而闻名。
The ethologist observed the intricate mating rituals of the birds. 动物行为学家观察了鸟类复杂的交配仪式。

近义词:animal behaviorist, zoologist
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider someone who mistreats or disregards animals)
词性:noun
