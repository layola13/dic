hereditably:/hɪˈrɛdɪtəbli/
adverb
可遗传地，遗传性地
in a way that can be passed from parent to offspring genetically
Eye color is hereditably determined. 眼睛的颜色是由遗传决定的。
近义词:genetically, inheritedly
反义词: non-hereditarily, acquired

词性: adverb
