codirectors:/ˌkoʊdaɪˈrɛktərz/ /ˌkəʊdaɪˈrɛktərz/
词性|n.|
中文翻译|共同导演，联合导演|
英文解析|Two or more directors who share the responsibility for directing a film, play, or other creative project.|
例句|The codirectors worked well together, complementing each other's strengths. 联合导演合作得很好，彼此优势互补。|

例句|The codirectors had different visions for the film, which led to creative conflict.  联合导演对这部电影有不同的设想，导致了创作上的冲突。|

近义词|joint directors, collaborating directors|
反义词|(No direct antonym, but possibly "sole director")|
