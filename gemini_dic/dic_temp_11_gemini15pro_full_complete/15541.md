baggers:/ˈbæɡərz/| n.|装袋工；打包机|People who bag groceries, or machines that bag items. |The baggers at the grocery store are always so friendly. 超市里的装袋员总是很友好。|The automatic baggers quickly filled the bags with potatoes. 自动打包机迅速地将土豆装满袋子。|近义词：packers, cashiers|反义词:|noun

bagger:/ˈbæɡər/| n.|装袋工；打包机；（尤指在高尔夫球比赛中）低于标准杆两杆|A person who bags groceries, a machine that bags items, or (especially in golf) a score of two strokes under par on a hole. |The bagger helped me carry my groceries to the car. 装袋员帮我把杂货搬到车上。|He made a bagger on the 18th hole. 他在第18洞打出了低于标准杆两杆的成绩。|近义词：packer, cashier (for grocery baggers); eagle (in golf)|反义词:|noun
