entablature: /ɪnˈtæblətʃər/ | n. |  柱顶, 檐部 | A horizontal structure supported by columns or a wall, typically consisting of three parts: the architrave, frieze, and cornice. | The entablature of the temple was richly decorated.  寺庙的檐部装饰华丽。| Synonym: cornice, superstructure | Antonym: foundation, base | Noun
