capcase:/ˈkæpkeɪs/| n.|大写字母|The act of capitalizing the first letter of each word in a string of characters, especially in programming and text processing.  It's also known as "camel case" or "medial capitals".|Example:  The variable name "firstName" is written in capcase.|近义词: camel case, medial capitals, Pascal case (when the first letter is also capitalized)|反义词: lowercase, snake case|noun
