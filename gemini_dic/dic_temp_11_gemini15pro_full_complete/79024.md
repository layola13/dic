give: /ɡɪv/ | v. | 给予，赠送，提供 | to freely transfer the possession of (something) to (someone) |  He gave me a book. 他给了我一本书。 | 近义词: offer, grant, donate, bestow | 反义词: take, receive | verb

give: /ɡɪv/ | n. | 弹性，伸展性 | the ability of something to bend or stretch | The fabric has a lot of give. 这种织物很有弹性。 | 近义词: flexibility, elasticity | 反义词: rigidity, stiffness | noun
