lentigo:/lɛnˈtaɪɡoʊ/ /lenˈtiːɡəʊ/ | n. | 雀斑，老年斑 | A small, flat, dark-colored spot on the skin, typically caused by sun exposure. |  She had a few lentigines on her face from years of sunbathing. 她脸上因多年日光浴而长了一些雀斑。

Lentigines are common in older people. 老年斑在老年人中很常见。 | 近义词: freckle, age spot, sunspot | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider unblemished skin) | 词性: noun


lentigines (plural):/lɛnˈtaɪɡɪˌniːz/ /lenˈtiːɡɪniːz/ | n. | 雀斑，老年斑（复数） | Plural form of lentigo.  | She developed multiple lentigines on her hands and arms. 她的手和胳膊上长出了多处老年斑。

The doctor examined the lentigines on his back. 医生检查了他背上的雀斑。 | 近义词: freckles, age spots, sunspots | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun (plural)



词源: From Latin *lentīgin-, from lentus (“slow, viscous”).  So named because they develop slowly.
