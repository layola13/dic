chippier:/ˈtʃɪpiər/ | adj. | 更暴躁的，更易怒的 | More irritable or bad-tempered; more inclined to argue or complain.  |  例句：He's been much chippier since he lost his job.  他失业后变得更加暴躁了。

例句：She was chippier than usual this morning.  她今天早上比平时更易怒。

近义词：grumpier, crankier, testier, moodier | 反义词：happier, cheerier, more pleasant | 词性：形容词

chippy:/ˈtʃɪpi/ | adj. | 暴躁的，易怒的 | Irritable or bad-tempered; inclined to argue or complain. | 例句：He's a bit chippy today, so try not to annoy him. 他今天有点暴躁，所以尽量别惹他。

例句：Don't get chippy with me!  别跟我发脾气！

近义词：grumpy, cranky, testy, moody | 反义词：happy, cheerful, pleasant | 词性：名词

chippy:/ˈtʃɪpi/ | n. | 炸鱼薯条店 | A shop selling fish and chips. (British informal) | 例句：Let's get some fish and chips from the chippy. 我们去炸鱼薯条店买点炸鱼薯条吧。

例句：The chippy down the road is really good.  这条路尽头的炸鱼薯条店真的很棒。

近义词：fish and chip shop, fish bar (British) |  反义词： 无 | 词性：名词


词源：chippy (adj.) 源自 chip (n.)，指“木屑”，可能因为易怒的人像碎木片一样容易“爆发”。  chippy (n.) 则指销售炸鱼薯条的商店，因为炸鱼薯条通常用报纸包裹，报纸的纸屑很容易掉落。
