firebugs:/ˈfaɪərbʌɡz/
n.
纵火犯，玩火者
A person who deliberately sets fire to property, especially for malicious pleasure.
The firebugs were caught on camera setting fire to the abandoned warehouse. 纵火犯在废弃仓库纵火的画面被摄像头拍了下来。

firebugs:/ˈfaɪərbʌɡz/
n.
萤火虫
A firefly. (Chiefly US, informal)
Children ran around catching firebugs on a warm summer night. 孩子们在一个温暖的夏夜里跑来跑去抓萤火虫。


Etymology:
"fire" + "bug"  "bug" in the sense of an insect or an enthusiast.

Synonyms:
arsonist (for the "纵火犯" meaning)
firefly, lightning bug (for the "萤火虫" meaning)

Antonyms:
firefighter (for the "纵火犯" meaning)
