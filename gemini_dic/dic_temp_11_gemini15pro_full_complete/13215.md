一般现在时 (Simple Present):
She is often at work on Saturdays. 她经常星期六上班。
He is at work right now. 他现在在上班。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
She is being at work right now, so she can't come. 她现在正在上班，所以不能来。 (Note:  "being at work" in present continuous is less common and might sound slightly unnatural in some contexts. It emphasizes the temporary nature of being at work at this specific moment.)
He is currently at work on a new project. 他目前正在工作，进行一个新项目。(Again, using “at work” with present continuous is not as common.  This example uses "at work" to describe the general context of his current activity.)


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has been at work all day. 他一整天都在上班。
She has been at work since 8 am. 她从早上8点就一直在上班。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
She has been at work for eight hours straight. 她已经连续工作了八个小时. (Again, the continuous aspect with "at work" is less common. This emphasizes the duration of the action.)
He has been at work on this project for weeks. 他在这个项目上已经工作了几个星期。(Here, "at work on" means working on and not necessarily physically present at the workplace.)


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He was at work yesterday. 他昨天在上班。
She was at work when the fire alarm went off. 火警响起时，她在上班。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was working on the report while he was at work. 我在写报告的时候他在上班.  (This is a more natural way to use "at work" with past continuous - describing the context of another action.)
She was at work all day yesterday, so I didn’t see her. 她昨天一整天都在上班，所以我没有见到她。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had been at work for hours before he took a break. 他工作了几个小时才休息。
She had already been at work when I called. 我打电话时她已经在上班了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
He had been at work on that project for months before it was finished. 在这个项目完成之前，他已经工作了好几个月了。 (This emphasizes the duration of the action before a point in the past.)
She had been at work all day, so she was exhausted. 她工作了一整天，所以筋疲力尽。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
He will be at work tomorrow. 他明天将上班。
She will be at work next week. 她下周将上班。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
He will be at work this time tomorrow. 明天这个时候他会在上班。
She will be at work during the conference. 会议期间她将在上班。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By the end of the week, he will have been at work for 40 hours. 到本周末，他将工作满40个小时。
By next year, she will have been at work at this company for ten years. 到明年，她将在这家公司工作十年了。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By the time you arrive, I will have been at work for five hours.  当你到达时，我将已经工作了五个小时。
By the end of the month, she will have been at work on this project for six months. 到月底，她将在这个项目上工作了六个月。


虚拟语气:
If I were at work now, I would be finishing that report. 如果我现在在工作，我就会完成那份报告。
I wish I were at work instead of being stuck in traffic. 我希望我在工作而不是被困在交通堵塞中。

被动语态: (Not applicable - "at work" describes a state and isn't used in the passive voice.)



疑问句:
Is he at work today? 他今天上班吗？
Was she at work when you called? 你打电话时她在上班吗？

否定句:
He is not at work today. 他今天不上班。
She was not at work yesterday. 她昨天没上班。
