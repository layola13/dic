gem-like:/ˈdʒɛmˌlaɪk/
adjective
像宝石的；宝石般的
Resembling a gem, especially in brilliance, color, or preciousness.
Her eyes were gem-like, sparkling with an inner light. 她的眼睛像宝石一样，闪耀着内在的光芒。
The dew drops on the spiderweb looked gem-like in the morning sun.  晨曦中的蜘蛛网上的露珠看起来像宝石一样。
近义词: jeweled, brilliant, sparkling, glittering, precious
反义词: dull, lackluster, opaque
