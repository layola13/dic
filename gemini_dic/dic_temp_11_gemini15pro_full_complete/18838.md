besan:/beɪˈsɑːn/| n.|鹰嘴豆粉，克面粉|A flour made from ground chickpeas, used in Indian cuisine.|例句：She used besan to make pakoras. 她用鹰嘴豆粉做油炸饼。

例句：Besan is a key ingredient in many Indian dishes. 鹰嘴豆粉是许多印度菜肴的关键成分。|近义词：gram flour, chickpea flour|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps wheat flour as an alternative)|词性:noun
