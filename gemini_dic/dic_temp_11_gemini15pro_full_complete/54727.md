disparage:/dɪˈspærɪdʒ/ /dɪˈspæɹɪdʒ/ | vt. | 贬低，轻视，诋毁 | To speak of or treat slightingly; belittle.  To lower in rank or reputation; degrade. |  He disparaged his competitor's products. 他贬低了他竞争对手的产品。 | synonyms: belittle, denigrate, deprecate, detract, minimize, underestimate, undervalue | antonyms: praise, commend, extol, laud, compliment, appreciate | verb

disparage:/dɪˈspærɪdʒ/ /dɪˈspæɹɪdʒ/ | n. | 贬低，轻蔑 |  The act of disparaging; depreciation |  His constant disparage of his colleagues created a hostile work environment.  他不断地贬低同事，制造了敌对的工作环境。 | synonyms: denigration, depreciation, derogation | antonyms: praise, commendation, acclaim | noun
