nonleguminous:/ˌnɒnˈlɛɡjuːmɪnəs/
adjective
非豆科的
Not belonging to the Leguminosae (or Fabaceae) family of plants.  These plants are characterized by not having pods that split along two seams.
Example:  The farmer rotated his crops, planting nonleguminous plants after a season of soybeans.  这位农民轮作作物，在大豆季节之后种植非豆科植物。
Example:  Many nonleguminous trees are important sources of timber. 许多非豆科树木是重要的木材来源。
Synonym: non-legume
Antonym: leguminous
词性: adjective
