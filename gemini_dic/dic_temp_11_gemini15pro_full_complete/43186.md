crabbed:/ˈkræbɪd/| adj.|暴躁的，易怒的；（字迹）潦草的，难辨认的|Bad-tempered and irritable; (of handwriting) cramped and difficult to decipher.|His crabbed handwriting was almost illegible. 他的潦草的字迹几乎难以辨认。|The old man, known for his crabbed demeanor, yelled at the children playing in his yard.  这位以脾气暴躁闻名的老人对着在他院子里玩耍的孩子们大喊大叫。|Synonyms: grumpy, irritable, surly, ill-tempered, irascible, cantankerous, churlish, peevish, cross, fractious, waspish; (of handwriting) illegible, unreadable, indecipherable, spidery, scrawly, scribbled|Antonyms: good-natured, affable, amiable, genial, pleasant; (of handwriting) legible, readable, clear|adjective
