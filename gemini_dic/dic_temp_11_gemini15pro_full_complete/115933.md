marquis:/ˈmɑːrkwɪs/ | n. |侯爵；女侯爵|A nobleman ranking below a duke and above an earl/count. |例句：The marquis owned vast estates in the countryside. 侯爵在乡下拥有广阔的土地。

例句：She was married to a marquis. 她嫁给了一位侯爵。

近义词：nobleman, peer, lord |反义词: commoner|词性:noun

marquise:/mɑːrˈkiːz/ | n. |女侯爵；侯爵夫人；（尤指钻石的）榄尖形切工|A woman who holds the rank of marquis, often by marriage. Also, a marquise-cut diamond. |例句：The marquise hosted a grand ball at the palace. 女侯爵在宫殿里举办了一个盛大的舞会。

例句：She wore a necklace with a stunning marquise-cut diamond. 她戴着一条镶有令人惊艳的榄尖形切割钻石的项链。

近义词：marchioness |反义词: N/A |词性:noun


词源: From Old French *marchis "ruler of a border area," from marche "frontier, border," from Frankish *marka, from Proto-Germanic *markō "borderland." (source: etymonline.com)
