daca:/ˈdɑːkə/ | n. | 延期行动方案(Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals) | An American immigration policy that allows some undocumented immigrants who entered the country as minors to receive a renewable two-year period of deferred action from deportation and become eligible for a work permit.  |  Example: DACA has protected hundreds of thousands of young people from deportation. 延期行动方案保护了数十万年轻人免遭驱逐出境。 | Example:  The future of DACA remains uncertain.  延期行动方案的未来仍然不确定。 | Synonym:  dreamer (informal), undocumented immigrant status | Antonym:  lawful permanent resident, citizen | noun
