alkalization:/ˌælkəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 碱化；碱性化 | The process of making something alkaline, or increasing its alkalinity. |  The alkalization of the soil is important for certain crops. 土壤的碱化对于某些作物很重要。|  The lake underwent alkalization due to industrial waste.  由于工业废物，湖泊经历了碱化。 | synonyms: neutralization, basification | antonyms: acidification | noun


alkalize:/ˈælkəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 使碱化；使成碱性 | To make something alkaline, or increase its alkalinity. |  Farmers sometimes alkalize their soil to improve crop yields.  农民有时会碱化土壤以提高作物产量。 |  The addition of lime alkalized the water. 添加石灰使水碱化。 | synonyms: neutralize, basify | antonyms: acidify | verb
