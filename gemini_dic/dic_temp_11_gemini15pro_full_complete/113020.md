lumbricus:/lʌmˈbraɪkəs/
n.
蚯蚓
earthworm; a genus of earthworms.
例句：Lumbricus terrestris is a common species of earthworm. 蚯蚓是一种常见的蚯蚓。

例句：The lumbricus plays a vital role in soil aeration. 蚯蚓在土壤通气中起着至关重要的作用。

近义词：earthworm, nightcrawler
反义词:  (无明显的反义词)
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lumbricus*, from *lumbus* ("loin").  The connection may be due to the earthworm's segmented body resembling the muscles of the loin, or to its use as bait in fishing.
