overdeliberating:/ˌōvərdiˈlɪbəˌreɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度思考；过度审议
Spending too much time thinking about something, often to the point of inaction or anxiety.  The act of deliberating excessively, often leading to indecision or paralysis.
She was overdeliberating about which college to attend and missed several application deadlines. 她过度思考该上哪所大学，错过了几个申请截止日期。
He was so busy overdeliberating the potential consequences that he failed to act at all. 他忙于过度思考潜在的后果，以至于根本没有采取行动。
近义词: overthinking, agonizing, ruminating
反义词: acting decisively, being impulsive, taking action
词性: verb (present participle)
