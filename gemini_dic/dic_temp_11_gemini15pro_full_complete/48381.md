decyl: /ˈdesɪl/ | adj. n. | 十烷基；癸基 |  Relating to or containing ten carbon atoms.  Derived from decane, an alkane with ten carbon atoms in a straight chain.  Can refer to the decyl group (C10H21) or a decyl radical. |  Decyl alcohol is a fatty alcohol. 十烷醇是一种脂肪醇。|  Decyl sulfate is a surfactant. 癸基硫酸盐是一种表面活性剂。 | 近义词: capryl, nonyl, undecyl (referring to similar alkyl groups) | 反义词:  N/A (no direct opposite for a chemical term) | 词性: adjective, noun


词源:  From dec- (ten) + -yl (indicating a univalent radical). Ultimately from Greek *deka (ten).
