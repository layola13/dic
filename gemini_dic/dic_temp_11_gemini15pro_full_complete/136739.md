nonsensitized:/nɒnˈsɛnsɪtaɪzd/
adjective
未致敏的，未敏感化的
Not having been made sensitive or susceptible, especially to an allergen or antigen.
The patient received a nonsensitized blood transfusion.  病人接受了未致敏的输血。
The study used nonsensitized control animals. 该研究使用了未致敏的对照动物。
近义词: unexposed, naive
反义词: sensitized, allergic
词性: adjective
词源: non- + sensitized (sensitize 源自法语 sensibiliser, 意为 "make sensitive")
