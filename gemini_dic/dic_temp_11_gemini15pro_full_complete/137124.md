nonsubscription:/ˌnɒnsəbˈskrɪpʃən/ |n.|非订阅，未订阅|Absence or lack of a subscription.  This refers to a state where someone has not subscribed to something, such as a magazine, service, or online platform.|Example: Her nonsubscription to the magazine meant she missed the exclusive interview. 她没有订阅这本杂志，意味着她错过了独家采访。|Example:  The nonsubscription rate for the streaming service increased after the price hike.  提价后，该流媒体服务的未订阅率上升了。|Synonyms: non-membership, unsubscription|Antonyms: subscription, membership|noun
