courtliest:/ˈkɔːrtliɪst/ /ˈkɔːrtiɪst/
adjective
最彬彬有礼的，最殷勤的
Most polite and refined in manner; most courteous.
例句：He greeted her with the courtliest bow. 他以最优雅的鞠躬迎接她。

例句：His courtliest manners charmed everyone. 他彬彬有礼的态度迷住了所有人。

近义词：most courteous, most polite, most gracious, most refined, most civil
反义词：rudest, most impolite, most discourteous, most uncivil

词性
adjective
