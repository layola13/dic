intimately:/ˈɪntɪmɪtli/| adv.|深入地，详细地|deeply and thoroughly|He was intimately involved in the project. 他深入参与了这个项目。|deeply, thoroughly, profoundly|superficially, cursorily|adverb

词源:
From Late Latin *intimatus*, past participle of *intimare* "to make known, announce," from Latin *intimus* "inmost."
