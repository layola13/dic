emblazonment:/ɪmˈbleɪzənmənt/| n. |纹章装饰，装饰；颂扬|The act of emblazoning something, such as decorating it with bright colors or patterns, or praising it publicly. |The emblazonment of the knight's shield was intricate and beautiful. 骑士的盾牌装饰精美华丽。| The family crest was a prominent feature in the emblazonment of the hall.  家族的徽章是大厅装饰的一个显著特征。|Synonyms: adornment, decoration, ornamentation, embellishment, celebration, praise|Antonyms:  plainness, simplicity, understatement|noun

PHRASE->
emblazon with (用...装饰): The shield was emblazoned with a golden lion. 盾牌上饰有金狮。
emblazon on (装饰在...上):  His name was emblazoned on the trophy. 他的名字刻在奖杯上。
emblazon across (装饰在...各处): Banners were emblazoned across the city. 横幅装饰在城市各处。
emblazon something with something:  They emblazoned the walls with colorful murals. 他们用色彩鲜艳的壁画装饰墙壁。
emblazon a shield/banner/flag (装饰盾牌/旗帜): The knight emblazoned his shield with his family crest. 骑士用家族徽章装饰了他的盾牌。


词源: From Old French *emblazoner, from en- + blason “coat of arms.”
