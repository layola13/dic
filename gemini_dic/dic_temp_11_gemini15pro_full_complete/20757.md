blandiloquious:/ˌblændɪˈləʊkwɪəs/| adj.|甜言蜜语的，奉承的|Characterized by flattering or ingratiating speech; smooth-tongued.|The salesman used blandiloquious language to persuade us to buy the expensive car.  推销员用甜言蜜语劝说我们买那辆昂贵的汽车。|近义词：flattering, ingratiating, unctuous, smooth-tongued|反义词：blunt, frank, candid, forthright|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *blandiloquus*, from *blandus* ("flattering, charming, alluring") + *-loquus* (from *loqui*, "to speak").
