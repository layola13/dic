dratting:/ˈdrætɪŋ/
verb
诅咒; 咒骂
Cursing; expressing annoyance or frustration.
Dratting that broken washing machine!  这该死的洗衣机！
He was dratting his luck after missing the bus. 他没赶上公共汽车，咒骂自己的运气。
近义词:cursing, damning, blast, confound
反义词:blessing, praising
词性:v.
