ordinariest:/ˈɔːrdɪnɛriɪst/ /ˈɔːrdəˌnɛriɪst/| adj.|最普通的，最平凡的|Superlative form of ordinary: most ordinary.  It means the most common, usual, or unexceptional; not different or special in any way.|He lived the ordinariest life imaginable, working at the same job for thirty years. 他过着最平凡的生活，在同一份工作上干了三十年。|The restaurant served the ordinariest food I've ever tasted. 这家餐厅供应的食物是我吃过最普通的。|Synonyms: commonplace, mundane, usual, typical, standard, conventional|Antonyms: extraordinary, unusual, remarkable, exceptional, special, unique|adjective
