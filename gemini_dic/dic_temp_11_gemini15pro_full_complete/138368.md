notionalities:/ˌnəʊʃəˈnælɪtiz/| n. |国籍（复数）|The legal relationship between an individual and a state, granting the individual certain rights and protections under the state's laws and recognizing the individual's allegiance to the state. Also refers to the collective identities of people sharing a common national affiliation, cultural heritage, or sense of belonging to a particular nation.|The form asked for details of the applicants' notionalities.  该表格要求申请人详细说明其国籍。|Synonym: citizenships, nationalities|Antonym: statelessness|noun
