demount:/dɪˈmaʊnt/ (US), /diːˈmaʊnt/ (UK)| v.  n.|拆卸，卸下; 拆除之物|To remove something from its mounting, especially something large or heavy.  As a noun, it refers to the act of demounting or something that has been demounted.|The mechanic demounted the tire from the wheel. 技工把轮胎从轮辋上拆了下来。|近义词：dismount, dismantle, remove, detach|反义词: mount, attach, install|verb, noun


demount:/dɪˈmaʊnt/ (US), /diːˈmaʊnt/ (UK)| n.|拆除之物|Something that has been demounted. |The demount was carefully stored away. 拆下来的东西被小心地存放起来了。|近义词：removal|反义词: mount|noun
