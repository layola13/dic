nonreciprocally:/ˌnɒnˌrɛsɪˈprɒkli/ | adv. | 非互惠地，单向地 | In a nonreciprocal manner; without reciprocity.  Not mutually exchanged or felt. |  The two countries traded nonreciprocally for many years, with one benefiting far more than the other. 这两个国家多年来进行非互惠贸易，其中一个国家获益远大于另一个国家。|  The feelings between them were nonreciprocally, he loved her deeply, but she didn't feel the same. 他们之间的感情是非互惠的，他深深地爱着她，但她却没有同样的感觉。 | Synonyms: unilaterally, unidirectionally | Antonyms: reciprocally, mutually | Part of Speech: Adverb
