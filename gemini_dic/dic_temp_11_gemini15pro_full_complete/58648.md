dumbing:/ˈdʌmɪŋ/
adjective
使变蠢的，降低智商的
Making something simpler and easier to understand, often at the cost of accuracy or depth.  The act of making something less intelligent or sophisticated.
The movie studio insisted on dumbing down the script to appeal to a wider audience.  电影制片厂坚持要简化剧本以吸引更广泛的观众。
simplifying, reducing complexity
enriching, enhancing, improving
adjective


dumbing down:/ˈdʌmɪŋ daʊn/
verb phrase
简化；弱智化
The act of making something less intelligent or sophisticated.
Newspapers are often accused of dumbing down complex issues.  报纸经常被指责简化复杂的问题。
simplifying, oversimplifying, watering down
complicating, elaborating, sophisticating
