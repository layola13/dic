marblings:/ˈmɑːrblɪŋz/ | n. | 大理石花纹，大理石纹理;  (用颜料在水面作画，然后印在纸或织物上的) 水彩画 | The process of creating swirling patterns resembling marble, typically by floating paints on water and then transferring them to paper or fabric.
