ossification:/ˌɑːsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ /ˌɒsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.|骨化；硬化|The process by which bone is formed.  The hardening or solidifying, as of soft tissue into bone.|例句：The ossification of the cartilage begins in adolescence. 软骨的骨化始于青春期。

例句：The political ossification of the regime stifled any dissent.  该政权的政治僵化扼杀了任何异议。|近义词：calcification, hardening|反义词: softening
词性:名词
