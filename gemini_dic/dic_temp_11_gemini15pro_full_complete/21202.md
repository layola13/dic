blockheaded:/ˌblɒkˈhɛdɪd/ (UK), /ˌblɑːkˈhɛdɪd/ (US)| adj.|愚笨的，迟钝的|Stupid or slow-witted; dense.|He made a blockheaded decision to invest all his money in one stock. 他做了一个愚蠢的决定，把所有的钱都投资在一支股票上。|Synonyms: stupid, dense, obtuse, doltish, dim-witted|Antonyms: intelligent, smart, bright, clever, astute|adjective

blockheaded:/ˌblɒkˈhɛdɪd/ (UK), /ˌblɑːkˈhɛdɪd/ (US)| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A stupid or slow-witted person.|Don't be such a blockhead!  别这么傻！|Synonyms: fool, idiot, dunce, dolt|Antonyms: genius, intellectual|noun
