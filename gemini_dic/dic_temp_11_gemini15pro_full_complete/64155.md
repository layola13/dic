equalizes:/ˈiːkwəˌlaɪzɪz/
v.
使相等，使均衡
makes equal, balances
The new regulations equalize the playing field for smaller businesses.  新规定使小型企业处于平等的竞争环境。
The team equalized in the final minutes of the game.  球队在比赛的最后几分钟扳平了比分。
synonyms: balance, level, even, match
antonyms: unbalance, differentiate, vary
