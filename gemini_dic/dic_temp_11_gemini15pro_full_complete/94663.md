impressure:/ɪmˈprɛʃər/ |n.|印记，印象；压痕；压力|An impressure is a mark or indentation made by pressing something onto a surface. It can also refer to the pressure exerted or the influence felt.|The impressure of the seal was clear on the wax. 封蜡上印章的印记很清晰。|Synonyms: impression, imprint, mark, dent, indentation|Antonyms: smoothness, evenness|noun
