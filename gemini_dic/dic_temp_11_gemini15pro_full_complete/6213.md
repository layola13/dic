ammonium:/əˈmoʊniəm/| n.|铵|A positively charged ion, NH4+, formed by the addition of a proton to ammonia. It is the ionic form of ammonia and is found in many salts.|Ammonium chloride is commonly used in fertilizers. 氯化铵常用于肥料中。|近义词: N/A|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From ammonia + -ium.  Ultimately from the name of the ancient Egyptian god Amun, whose temple was near a deposit of ammonium chloride.
