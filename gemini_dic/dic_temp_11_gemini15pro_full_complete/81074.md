grains:/ɡreɪnz/ | n. | 谷物; 颗粒; 少量; 纹理 | Small, hard pieces of something, such as sand or salt; the seeds of crops such as wheat, corn, rye, oats, rice, etc., used for food. | The farmer scattered grains of wheat in the field.  农民在地里撒了小麦粒。| Synonyms: kernels, seeds, particles, bits | Antonyms: wholes, lumps, masses | noun

grains:/ɡreɪnz/ | n. | 纹理 | The arrangement of lines or fibers in wood, cloth, or other material. | The wood has a beautiful grain. 这块木头的纹理很漂亮。| Synonyms: texture, pattern, fiber | Antonyms: smoothness | noun
