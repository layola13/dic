excorticate:/ɛksˈkɔːrtɪkeɪt/
verb
剥皮；严厉批评；痛斥
To strip off the bark or rind; to denounce or berate severely.
The chef excoriated the orange before using the zest in the cake.  厨师在用橙皮做蛋糕前先剥了皮。
The critic excoriated the director for the film's poor plot and weak acting.  评论家严厉批评了导演糟糕的剧情和拙劣的演技。
近义词:denounce, condemn, criticize, flay, castigate
反义词:praise, commend, laud
词性:verb
