ftps:/ˌef tiː piː ˈes/| n. |文件传输协议安全|File Transfer Protocol Secure. A secure version of FTP that uses SSL/TLS encryption to protect data during transfer.|Example 1: We use FTPS to securely transfer sensitive files to our clients. 我们使用 FTPS 将敏感文件安全地传输给我们的客户。

Example 2:  FTPS is essential for protecting confidential information during online file transfers.  FTPS 对于在在线文件传输过程中保护机密信息至关重要。|近义词：SFTP, HTTPS, Secure FTP|反义词:FTP|词性:缩写词
