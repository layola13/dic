coussinet:/ˌkuːsɪˈneɪ/ /ˈkuːsɪnet/| n.|轴衬，轴瓦，垫块，软垫|A bearing, pad, or cushion, especially one in machinery or anatomy.  It often refers to a plain bearing. |例句：The coussinet in the engine needs replacing.  发动机里的轴瓦需要更换。

例句：The surgeon inserted a coussinet to protect the nerve.  外科医生插入了一个垫块来保护神经。|近义词：bearing, bush, pad, cushion, liner|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

词源: From French "coussinet," diminutive of "coussin" (cushion), from Latin "culcita" (mattress, cushion).
