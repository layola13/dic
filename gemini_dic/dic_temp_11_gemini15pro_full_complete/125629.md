mumbler:/ˈmʌmblər/ (US), /ˈmʌmblə(r)/ (UK)| n. |含糊不清说话的人；咕哝者|A person who speaks indistinctly, often in a low voice.|He's such a mumbler, I can never understand what he's saying. 他说话太含糊了，我从来都听不懂他在说什么。|近义词：mutterer, murmurer|反义词：articulator, orator|noun
