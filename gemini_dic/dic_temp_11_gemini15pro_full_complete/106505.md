krauts:/kraʊts/
n.
德国佬（贬义）
A derogatory term for Germans, derived from Sauerkraut, a type of fermented cabbage popular in German cuisine.
During World War II, "krauts" was commonly used as a derogatory term for German soldiers.
第二次世界大战期间，“krauts”通常被用作对德国士兵的贬义词。
近义词：Jerries, Huns (all derogatory)
反义词:None (avoiding derogatory terms)
词性:Noun
