overcommunicating:/ˌoʊvərˈkəˈmjuːnɪˌkeɪtɪŋ/
| v. (present participle)|过度沟通，沟通过度|The act of communicating excessively, often to the point of redundancy or annoyance.  It involves sharing more information than necessary or desired by the recipient.|She was worried about missing a deadline, so she started overcommunicating with her team.|近义词：oversharing, overexplaining|反义词: undercommunicating, withholding information|动词
