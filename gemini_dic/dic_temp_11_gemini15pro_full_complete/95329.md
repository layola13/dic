incensant:/ɪnˈsesənt/| adj.|不断的，不停的，持续的|Continuing without interruption; ceaseless.|The incessant noise of the traffic kept me awake all night. 交通的持续噪音让我整夜无法入睡。|近义词：constant, continuous, perpetual, unremitting|反义词：intermittent, occasional, sporadic|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin incessant-, from Latin incessans, present participle of incessare "to not cease," from in- "not" + cessare "to cease," frequentative of cedere "to yield, go."
