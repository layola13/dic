airburst:/ˈɛərbɜːrst/| n.|空中爆炸|An explosion that occurs in the air, before the projectile reaches the ground.|The airburst detonated hundreds of feet above the target. 空中爆炸在目标上方数百英尺处引爆。|Example: The missile was designed to airburst, maximizing its destructive power.  导弹被设计成在空中爆炸，以最大限度地发挥其破坏力。|Synonym: aerial explosion, midair explosion|Antonym: ground burst|noun

airburst:/ˈɛərbɜːrst/| v.|空中爆炸|To explode in the air, before reaching the ground.|The bomb airburst over the city. 炸弹在城市上空爆炸。|Example: The artillery shell was programmed to airburst, showering the area with shrapnel.  炮弹被设定为空中爆炸，向该地区倾泻弹片。|Synonym: explode midair, detonate midair|Antonym: ground burst|verb
