congruently:/kənˈɡruːəntli/
adv.
全等地，一致地
in a congruent manner; in agreement or harmony.
The two triangles are congruently placed on the plane. 这两个三角形在平面上全等放置。
The results of the two studies were congruently positive.  两项研究的结果一致地呈阳性。
近义词:correspondingly, similarly, identically
反义词:incongruently, differently, dissimilarly
词性:adverb

congruent:/ˈkɒŋɡruːənt/
adj.
全等的，一致的
(of figures, lines, or angles) identical in form; coinciding exactly when superimposed.
These two triangles are congruent. 这两个三角形全等。
Her actions are congruent with her values. 她的行为与她的价值观一致。
近义词:identical, matching, corresponding
反义词:incongruent, different, dissimilar
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin *congruentem (nominative congruens), present participle of congruere (“to come together, agree”), from com- (“together”) + -gruere, related to ruere (“to rush”).
