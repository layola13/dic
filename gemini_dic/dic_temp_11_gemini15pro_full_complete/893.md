abuelito:/ˌɑːbweɪˈlɪtoʊ/| n.|爷爷；外公 (昵称)|A term of endearment for "grandfather" in Spanish, often used in Latin American cultures.|例句：My abuelito told me stories about his childhood. 我的外公给我讲了他童年时代的故事。

例句：I love visiting my abuelito every Sunday. 我喜欢每个星期天去看望我的爷爷。

近义词：grandpa, grandfather|反义词:granddaughter, grandson|词性:noun
