blirt:/blɜːrt/| v. |脱口而出，冲口说出|To utter abruptly and impulsively.|He blurted out the secret before he could stop himself. 他还没来得及停下来就把秘密脱口而出了。|She blurted, "I'm pregnant!" 她脱口而出：“我怀孕了！”|近义词：blurt out, exclaim, ejaculate, burst out, utter, divulge|反义词：conceal, suppress, withhold|词性:动词
Etymology:  From late Middle English *blurten, probably of imitative origin.
