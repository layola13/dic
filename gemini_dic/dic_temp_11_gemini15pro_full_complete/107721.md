lanyard:/ˈlænjərd/ |n.|挂绳，系索，吊绳|A rope or cord worn around the neck, often for holding a whistle, ID card, or keys.|例句：He wore a lanyard around his neck with his ID card attached. 他脖子上戴着挂绳，上面挂着他的身份证。

例句：The sailor skillfully coiled the lanyard. 水手熟练地卷起系索。|近义词：cord, rope, strap|反义词: None|词性: noun
