excruciation:/ɪkˌskruːʃiˈeɪʃən/
n.
极度的痛苦；折磨
The process of inflicting severe pain or agony, both physical or mental.
The excruciation of waiting for the results was almost unbearable.  等待结果的煎熬几乎令人无法忍受。
近义词: torment, torture, agony, anguish
反义词: comfort, solace, relief
词性: 名词

excruciate:/ɪkˈskruːʃieɪt/
v.
折磨；使极度痛苦
To inflict severe pain or agony upon.
The memory of the accident continues to excruciate him. 事故的记忆一直折磨着他。
近义词: torment, torture, afflict
反义词: soothe, comfort, relieve
词性: 动词
