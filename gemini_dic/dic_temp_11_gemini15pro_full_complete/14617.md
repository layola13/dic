avocate:/ˈædvəkeɪt/| v. n. |提倡，拥护；支持者，拥护者|To publicly support or suggest an idea, development, or way of doing something; a person who publicly supports or suggests an idea, development, or way of doing something. |She advocates for women's rights. 她倡导妇女权利。|近义词：support, champion, proponent, backer|反义词：oppose, opponent, critic|词性:动词,名词
avocate:/ˈædvəkeɪt/| n. | (苏格兰法律)律师|(especially in Scotland) a lawyer|He is a well-known avocate in Edinburgh. 他是爱丁堡一位著名的律师。|近义词:lawyer, attorney, barrister|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *advocatus*, past participle of advocare (“to summon, call for”), from ad- (“to”) + vocare (“to call”).
