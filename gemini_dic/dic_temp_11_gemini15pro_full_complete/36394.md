coinvestigator:/ˌkoʊɪnˈvɛstɪɡeɪtər/| n. |共同研究者，共同调查者|A researcher or investigator who works with another on a project. | Dr. Smith is the principal investigator, and Dr. Jones is the coinvestigator on this project. 史密斯博士是这个项目的主要研究员，而琼斯博士是共同研究员。

Professor Lee and Professor Kim are coinvestigators on a new study about climate change. 李教授和金教授是一项关于气候变化的新研究的共同研究者。|近义词：co-researcher, collaborator|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "sole investigator")|词性:noun
