hibernal:/ˈhaɪbərnəl/ (US) /haɪˈbɜːnl/ (UK)| adj.|冬天的；冬令的|Characteristic of winter; wintry.|例句：The hibernal landscape was bleak and unforgiving. 冬天的景色荒凉而无情。

例句：Hibernal winds howled through the bare branches of the trees. 冬天的风呼啸着穿过光秃秃的树枝。|近义词：wintry, wintery, winterish|反义词：summery, estival|词性：形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *hibernālis*, from *hibernus* "of winter," related to *hiems* "winter."
