bevel:/ˈbevəl/| n. v. adj.|斜面，斜角；使成斜角；斜的|A bevel is a sloping edge or surface.  To bevel something means to cut or shape it at an angle.  Something that is bevelled has a sloping edge.|The mirror had a bevelled edge. 这面镜子有斜边。|近义词:slope, slant, chamfer|反义词:straight, level|词性:名词,动词,形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bivel, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old French biveau "oblique, sloping."
