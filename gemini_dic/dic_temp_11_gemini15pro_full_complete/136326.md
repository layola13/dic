nonrescissions:/ˌnɒn rɪˈsɪʒənz/
|n.| 不撤销，不废除 |The nonrescissions of the budget cuts mean the programs will continue to be underfunded.  预算削减的不撤销意味着这些项目将继续资金不足。| The governor's nonrescissions of several line-item vetoes surprised many legislators. 州长对几项单项否决的不撤销令许多立法者感到惊讶。 | near synonyms: continuations,  retentions | antonyms: rescissions, cancellations, revocations | noun

Etymology:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "rescission" (from Latin "rescindere,"  meaning "to cut back, repeal")
