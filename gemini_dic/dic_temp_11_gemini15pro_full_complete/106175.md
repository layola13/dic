knock-the-tar-out-of:/nɒk ðə tɑːr aʊt əv/ | idiom (v.) | 痛打；彻底击败；使筋疲力尽 | To beat someone severely or defeat them decisively.  It can also mean to exhaust someone completely. |  He knocked the tar out of his opponent in the boxing match. 他在拳击比赛中把对手打得落花流水。 | Synonyms: beat up, thrash, clobber, pummel, exhaust, wear out | Antonyms: lose to, surrender, give up | idiom

knock-the-tar-out-of:/nɒk ðə tɑːr aʊt əv/ | idiom (v.) | 使大吃一惊；使印象深刻 | To greatly impress or amaze someone. | Her performance knocked the tar out of the audience. 她的表演让观众惊叹不已。 | Synonyms: amaze, astound, impress, dazzle | Antonyms: disappoint, bore | idiom
