cowardish:/ˈkaʊərdɪʃ/| adj.|懦弱的，胆小的|Lacking courage and resolution, excessively afraid of danger or risk.|He made a cowardly decision to abandon his friends.|近义词: timid, craven, pusillanimous|反义词: brave, courageous, valiant|词性: adjective
Etymology: From coward + -ish.  Coward (n.) comes from Old French coart, from coe "tail," referring to an animal with its tail between its legs, a sign of fear.
