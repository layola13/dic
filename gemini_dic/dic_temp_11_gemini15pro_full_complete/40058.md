conta:/ˈkɒntə/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntə/ (US)| n. |账户；账目；（尤指银行账户）|A record of debit and credit entries relating to a particular item or a particular person or concern.|例句：She checked her bank conta regularly. 她定期查看她的银行账户。|例句：He opened a new conta at the local bank. 他在当地银行开了一个新账户。|近义词：account, record|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Latin "computare" (to count, reckon), which evolved into "contare" and eventually "conta."
