depurator:/ˈdɛpjʊˌreɪtər/ | n. |净化器；净化剂；清洁剂|A device or substance that cleanses or purifies something, especially a liquid.|The company installed a new depurator to remove impurities from the water supply.  公司安装了一个新的净化器来去除供水中的杂质。|Synonyms: purifier, cleanser, cleaner, filter |Antonyms: contaminant, pollutant|noun


depurate:/ˈdɛpjʊˌreɪt/ | v. |净化；清洁；去除杂质|To cleanse or purify something, especially a liquid.|The city depurates its wastewater before releasing it into the river.  这座城市在将废水排入河流之前对其进行净化。|Synonyms: purify, cleanse, clean, filter|Antonyms: contaminate, pollute|verb
