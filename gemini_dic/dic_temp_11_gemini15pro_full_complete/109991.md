libidinous:/lɪˈbɪdɪnəs/
adjective
好色的；性欲强的
Characterized by lust; lascivious. Showing excessive sexual drive; lustful.
例句：He gave her a libidinous look. 他色迷迷地看了她一眼。

例句：The libidinous lyrics of the song caused controversy. 这首歌好色的歌词引起了争议。

近义词：lustful, lascivious, lecherous, carnal, salacious
反义词：chaste, pure, virtuous, ascetic
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *libidinōsus, from Latin libido (“desire, lust, pleasure”), from libere (“to please”).
