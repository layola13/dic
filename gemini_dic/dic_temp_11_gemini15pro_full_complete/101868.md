isatis:/aɪˈseɪtɪs/| n. |菘蓝|A genus of plants in the family Brassicaceae, commonly known as woad. It is the source of a blue dye.|Woad (Isatis tinctoria) was historically an important source of blue dye in Europe. 菘蓝(Isatis tinctoria)在历史上是欧洲重要的蓝色染料来源。|近义词：woad|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially other dye sources like indigo)|词性:noun
