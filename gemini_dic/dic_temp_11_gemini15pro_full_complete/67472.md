extorter:/ɪkˈstɔːrtər/ | n. | 勒索者，敲诈者 | A person who obtains something, especially money, through coercion or intimidation. | The extorter threatened to release damaging information unless he was paid. 勒索者威胁说，如果不付钱，他就要公布破坏性的信息。 | He demanded a large sum of money, acting like a common extorter. 他像个普通的勒索者一样，索要一大笔钱。 | 近义词: blackmailer, racketeer | 反义词: benefactor, philanthropist | 词性: noun
