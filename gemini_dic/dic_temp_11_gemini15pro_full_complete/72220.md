floret:/ˈflɔːrɪt/ /ˈflɔːret/| n.|小花；小簇的花；（花椰菜等的）可食用的花状部分|A floret is one of the small flowers making up a composite flower head, such as a daisy or a head of broccoli.|The chef carefully separated the florets of the broccoli. 厨师小心地将西兰花的小花分开。|The dandelion clock is made up of many tiny florets. 蒲公英的绒球是由许多细小的绒毛状小花组成的。|Synonyms: floweret, blossom|Antonyms: N/A|noun
