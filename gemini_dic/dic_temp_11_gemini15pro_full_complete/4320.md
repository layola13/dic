aja:/ˈɑːhɑː/ | interj. |啊哈| An exclamation of triumph, satisfaction, or surprise, similar to "aha!". | Example: Aja! I found the missing piece! 啊哈！我找到丢失的碎片了！| Example: Aja, I knew you'd figure it out eventually. 啊哈，我就知道你最终会弄明白的。 | Synonym: aha, eureka, bingo | Antonym: (No direct antonym, expresses positive discovery) | Interjection

Etymology->
Likely a variant of "aha," which itself is an interjection of unknown origin, possibly imitative of a sound of sudden comprehension or discovery.

PHRASE->
(While "aja" itself doesn't form common phrases,  "aha" does, and they could be adapted)
Aha! I knew it! = 啊哈！我就知道！
Aha, I see what you mean. = 啊哈，我明白你的意思了。
Aha, now I understand. = 啊哈，现在我明白了。
Aha, that's the problem. = 啊哈，这就是问题所在。
Aha, I've got it! = 啊哈！我明白了！


注意事项:
"Aja" is not a common word in standard English.  Its use is primarily informal and expressive.  "Aha" is a more frequently used and accepted alternative.
