embroiderers:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdərərz/
noun
刺绣者；绣花工
People who embroider, creating decorative designs on fabric using needle and thread.
The embroiderers skillfully stitched intricate patterns onto the silk cloth.  刺绣者们熟练地将复杂的图案缝在丝绸上。
The guild of embroiderers held an annual exhibition of their work. 刺绣者协会每年举办一次作品展览。
synonyms: needleworkers, stitchers, sewers
antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps one could consider "machine operators" in a modern context where embroidery is often automated)
词性: noun
