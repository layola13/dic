modelled:/ˈmɒdld/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdld/ (US)| v. |模仿；塑造；（按模型）制作；展示|Based on the verb "model," meaning to shape or form something, often using a pattern or example.  It can also mean to display something, like clothing.| The architect modelled the building after a Greek temple. 建筑师模仿希腊神庙建造了这座建筑物。|  She modelled the clay into a small figure. 她用黏土塑造了一个小雕像。| synonyms: shaped, formed, fashioned, displayed, exhibited | antonyms: destroyed, demolished, deformed |verb
