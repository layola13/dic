equidimensional:/ˌiːkwɪdɪˈmɛnʃənəl/
adjective
等维的，维度相同的
Having all dimensions equal or uniform.  Relating to or being a topological space such that every point has arbitrarily small neighborhoods that are homeomorphic to an open set in a Euclidean space of a fixed dimension.
The crystal structure is equidimensional, with all sides roughly the same length.  这种晶体结构是等维的，所有边的长度大致相同。
The artist created an equidimensional sculpture, where height, width, and depth were equal.  这位艺术家创作了一个等维的雕塑，其高度、宽度和深度相等。
Synonyms: isometric, uniform, regular
Antonyms: anisotropic, irregular, non-uniform
词性: adjective
