gerent:/ˈdʒɪərənt/| n.| 代理人；统治者；管理者 | A person appointed to administer a kingdom or other territory in the absence of the monarch or other appointed ruler; a manager or administrator. | The king appointed a gerent to rule in his stead while he was away at war. 国王任命了一位摄政王在他外出征战期间代替他统治。|Synonyms: administrator, manager, governor, regent, deputy, proxy, representative| Antonyms: subordinate, employee, subject | noun

gerent:/ˈdʒɪərənt/| adj.| 代理的；管理的| Acting as a gerent; managing or administering.| He served as the gerent director until a permanent replacement could be found. 他担任代理主任，直到找到永久替代人选。|Synonyms: acting, managing, administering, governing| Antonyms: subordinate, subservient | adjective
