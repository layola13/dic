laccin:/ˈlæksɪn/
noun
树胶，漆
a resinous substance containing lac, used for making varnish and sealing wax.
The antique furniture was coated in a deep red laccin. 这件古董家具涂了一层深红色的漆。
synonyms: lacquer, varnish, shellac, resin
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a physical substance, could consider words like "solvent" if referring to removal)
