fondon: /ˈfɒndɒn/ (British) /ˈfɑːndən/ (American) | n. | 翻糖，软糖 | A thick, creamy sugar paste used for icing and decorating cakes, or a type of soft candy. | Example: The wedding cake was covered in a smooth white fondon. 婚礼蛋糕上覆盖着一层光滑的白色翻糖。

Example:  She bit into the soft, chocolate fondon. 她咬了一口软软的巧克力软糖。

近义词: icing, frosting (for cake decorating), confection (for candy) | 反义词:  None (no direct opposite for a specific type of icing or candy) | 词性: Noun
