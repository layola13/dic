loch:/lɒx (UK), /lɑːk (US)| n.|湖；狭长的海湾|A large, deep, inland body of water, typically one fed by rivers; a lake, especially a large one in Scotland.|例句：The monster of Loch Ness is a legend. 尼斯湖水怪是一个传说。

例句：We went sailing on the loch. 我们在湖上航行。|近义词：lake, inlet, firth|反义词:|名词
