intemperament: /ɪnˈtɛmpərəm(ə)nt/
n.
lack of self-control; immoderation
The state of lacking moderation or self-control; excessive indulgence of desires or passions.
His intemperament led him to make rash decisions. 他的缺乏自制力导致他做出轻率的决定。
Synonyms: immoderation, excess, intemperance, self-indulgence, overindulgence, lack of restraint, unrestraint, abandon, dissoluteness, debauchery, dissipation, profligacy, wildness
Antonyms: moderation, temperance, self-control, restraint
noun
