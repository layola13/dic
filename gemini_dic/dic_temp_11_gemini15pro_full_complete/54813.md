dispersal:/dɪˈspɜːrsəl/ | n. | 散布，分散，传播 | the action or process of distributing things or people over a wide area | The dispersal of seeds is crucial for plant reproduction. 种子的散布对植物繁殖至关重要。 | distribution, scattering, dissemination | concentration, gathering | noun


dispersals:/dɪˈspɜːrsəlz/ | n. | 散布，分散，传播（dispersal的复数形式）| multiple actions or processes of distributing things or people over a wide area | The wind aided in the dispersals of the dandelion seeds across the field. 风帮助蒲公英种子散布到整个田野。 | distributions, scatterings, disseminations | concentrations, gatherings | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *dispersen*, from Old French disperser, from Latin dispersus, past participle of dispergere (“to scatter”), from dis- (“apart, asunder”) + spargere (“to scatter, strew”).
