maximian: /ˌmæksɪˈmiən/ | n. |马克西米安| A Roman emperor (reigned AD 286-305). He was originally from Illyricum and rose through the military ranks.  He became Caesar under Diocletian and later co-emperor. Known for his persecution of Christians. | Maximian was a skilled military leader. 马克西米安是一位熟练的军事将领。 | Diocletian, Constantius | N/A | noun
