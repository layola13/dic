optimizers:/ˈɒptɪmaɪzəz/ (UK) /ˈɑːptɪmaɪzərz/ (US)| n.|优化器，优化程序|A program or algorithm designed to improve or maximize the performance of a system, process, or function.|The optimizers in the machine learning library helped improve the model's accuracy. 机器学习库中的优化器帮助提高了模型的准确性。|Synonyms: improvers, enhancers, refiners|Antonyms: degraders, diminishers|Noun

optimize:/ˈɒptɪmaɪz/ (UK) /ˈɑːptɪmaɪz/ (US)| v.|优化，最佳化|Make the best or most effective use of (a situation, opportunity, or resource).|We need to optimize our website for mobile devices. 我们需要优化我们的网站以适应移动设备。|Synonyms: improve, enhance, refine, perfect, maximize|Antonyms: worsen, degrade, diminish|Verb

optimal:/ˈɒptɪməl/ (UK) /ˈɑːptɪməl/ (US)| adj.|最佳的，最优的|Best or most favorable; optimum.|The optimal solution is often a compromise between different factors. 最佳解决方案通常是不同因素之间的妥协。|Synonyms: best, ideal, perfect, most favorable|Antonyms: worst, poorest, least favorable|Adjective
