dialogic:/ˌdaɪəˈlɒdʒɪk/ | adj. | 对话的，对话式的 | Relating to or characterized by dialogue; engaging in or based on an exchange of ideas and opinions. | Example: The play uses dialogic techniques to explore the theme of betrayal.  这出戏运用对话技巧来探讨背叛的主题。| Example: The teacher fostered a dialogic classroom environment. 老师营造了一个对话式的课堂环境。 |  Near Synonyms: conversational, interactive, communicative | Antonyms: monologic, unidirectional | Adjective


dialogic:/ˌdaɪəˈlɒdʒɪk/ | n. | 对话 | Dialogue or a system of dialogue. (Less common usage than the adjective form) | Example: The novel is structured around a series of dialogics. 这部小说是围绕一系列对话展开的。 | Example:  The philosopher explored the nature of dialogic in human communication. 这位哲学家探讨了人类交流中对话的本质。 | Near Synonyms: conversation, discourse, exchange | Antonyms: monologue, lecture | Noun
