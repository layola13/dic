churchwarden:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃˌwɔːrdən/ (UK) /ˈtʃɜːrtʃˌwɔːrdn/ (US)| n. |教区委员，教堂理事|An elected lay officer in the Church of England, responsible for the fabric and finances of a church. |例句：The churchwardens were responsible for organizing the annual fete. 教堂理事负责组织一年一度的游园会。

|例句：The churchwarden locked up the church after the service. 教堂理事在礼拜结束后锁上了教堂的门。
