issue:/ˈɪʃuː/ (US); /ˈɪsjuː/ (UK)| n. vt.|问题；议题；发行；期刊号；结果；子女|A point or matter in dispute or in doubt.  The act of officially distributing or supplying something.|The latest issue of the magazine is out now.  这本杂志的最新一期已经出版了。|近义词：problem, topic, release, publication|反义词:solution, resolution|词性:名词, 动词

issue:/ˈɪʃuː/ (US); /ˈɪsjuː/ (UK)| vt.|发行；发布；流出|To supply or distribute something officially.|The government issued a statement about the crisis. 政府就这场危机发表了一份声明。|近义词：distribute, release, publish|反义词:withdraw, withhold|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *issuer, from Latin exire "to go out," from ex- "out" + ire "to go."
