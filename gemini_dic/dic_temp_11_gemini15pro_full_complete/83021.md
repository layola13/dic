gunslingers:/ˈɡʌnslɪŋərz/ | n. | 神枪手；持枪歹徒 | A person, especially in the American West, who carries a gun and is quick to use it.  A gunslinger is typically a skilled shooter, often portrayed as a hired gun or an outlaw. |  The old western movies often featured gunslingers facing off in dusty streets.  那些老西部电影里经常有枪手在尘土飞扬的街道上对决。|  The sheriff faced the gunslinger in a tense standoff. 警长与持枪歹徒在紧张的对峙中对峙。| synonyms: gunman, shootist, outlaw, desperado | antonyms: pacifist, lawman, peacemaker | noun
