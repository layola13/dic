infinitival:/ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtɪvəl/| adj.| 非限定式的，不定式的 |Relating to or formed with an infinitive.  It describes something related to or formed using the infinitive form of a verb.|  The infinitival phrase "to eat the cake" acts as the subject of the sentence.  非限定词组“吃蛋糕”充当句子的主语。|  infinitive, non-finite| finite| adjective
