cross-promote:/ˌkrɒs prəˈməʊt/ (US) /ˌkrɔːs prəˈmoʊt/| v. |交叉推广，互相推广|To market (something, such as a product or service) together with another product or service, typically one considered complementary, in order to increase sales or awareness of both.|The two companies agreed to cross-promote each other's products. 这两家公司同意互相推广对方的产品。|
cross-promote:/ˌkrɒs prəˈməʊt/ (US) /ˌkrɔːs prəˈmoʊt/| v. |交叉推广，互相推广|The movie studio cross-promoted the film with a fast-food chain. 电影制片厂与一家快餐连锁店交叉推广了这部电影。|近义词:cross-market, jointly promote|反义词: N/A|词性: verb

cross-promotion:/ˌkrɒs prəˈməʊʃən/ (US) /ˌkrɔːs prəˈmoʊʃən/| n. |交叉推广，互相推广|The act of marketing two or more products or services together.|The cross-promotion between the two brands was very successful. 这两个品牌之间的交叉推广非常成功。|
cross-promotion:/ˌkrɒs prəˈməʊʃən/ (US) /ˌkrɔːs prəˈmoʊʃən/| n. |交叉推广，互相推广|The cross-promotion led to a significant increase in sales for both companies. 交叉推广使两家公司的销售额大幅增长。|近义词:cross-marketing, joint promotion|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
