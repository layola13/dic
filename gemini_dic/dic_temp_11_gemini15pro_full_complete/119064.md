metope: /ˈmɛtəʊp/ (UK) /ˈmɛtəp/ (US) | n. |  排档间饰，（希腊神庙中）排档间的方形图案 | A rectangular architectural panel between the triglyphs in a Doric frieze, often sculpted in relief. | The metope depicted a battle between the Lapiths and the Centaurs.  排档间饰描绘了拉皮斯人与半人马之间的战斗。|  Synonym: panel, slab | Antonym: triglyph | noun
