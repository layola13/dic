afforestations:/əˌfɔrɪˈsteɪʃənz/
|n.|造林，植树造林|The process of establishing a forest, especially on land that has not been forested for a long time.  建立森林的过程，尤指在长期未造林的土地上.|例句：The government has invested heavily in afforestations projects to combat desertification. 政府大力投资植树造林项目以对抗荒漠化。|例句：Afforestations efforts have helped to restore degraded landscapes. 植树造林的努力帮助恢复了退化的景观。|近义词：reforestation, forestation, tree planting|反义词:deforestation, logging|词性:名词

Etymology:
From French *afforestation*, from *afforester* "to convert into forest," from *a-* (from Latin *ad-*) "to" + *forest* "forest."  源自法语 *afforestation*，来自 *afforester* “转变为森林”，来自 *a-*（源自拉丁语 *ad-*）“到” + *forest* “森林”。
