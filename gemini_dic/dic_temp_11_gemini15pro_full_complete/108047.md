latchkey:/ˈlætʃkiː/
n., adj.|钥匙儿童（的）|A key to the outside door of a house, especially one given to a child who returns from school before their parents are home.|例句：He was a latchkey kid, letting himself into an empty house after school. 他是个钥匙儿童，放学后自己走进空荡荡的房子。|近义词：self-care, unsupervised|反义词：supervised, chaperoned|词性：名词, 形容词

latchkey:/ˈlætʃkiː/
adj.|钥匙儿童（的）|Relating to or characteristic of a child who returns from school to an empty home because their parents are out at work.|例句：Latchkey children are often left to their own devices after school. 钥匙儿童放学后经常只能自己照顾自己。|近义词：self-reliant, independent|反义词：dependent, cared-for|词性：形容词
