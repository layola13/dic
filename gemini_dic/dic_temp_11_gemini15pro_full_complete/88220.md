hindering:/ˈhɪndərɪŋ/| adj.|阻碍的，妨碍的|present participle of hinder, meaning "creating difficulties for someone or something, resulting in delay or obstruction."|The hindering traffic made me late for work. 阻碍的交通使我上班迟到了。|近义词：obstructing, impeding, hampering, inhibiting|反义词：helping, aiding, assisting, facilitating|词性: Adjective

hindering:/ˈhɪndərɪŋ/| n.|障碍，阻碍|The act of hindering; something that hinders.|The constant rain was a hindering to our progress.  持续的降雨是我们前进的障碍。|近义词：obstacle, impediment, barrier, obstruction|反义词：aid, assistance, help, support|词性: Noun (Gerund)
