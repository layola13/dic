financialist:/faɪˈnænʃəlɪst/ /fɪˈnænʃəlɪst/
n.
金融家；财务专家
A person who is skilled in or knowledgeable about finance.
Example: The financialist predicted a rise in interest rates.  这位金融家预测利率会上升。

Example: Several prominent financialists spoke at the conference. 几位著名的金融家在会议上发表了讲话。

近义词: financier, investor, banker, money manager
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) debtor, borrower
词性: noun
