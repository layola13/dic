heapy:/ˈhiːpi/| adj.|堆积的，凌乱的|A large, disordered pile of something.  Characterized by a large, messy, or overflowing collection of items.|例句：Her desk was heapy with papers and books. 她的桌子堆满了文件和书籍。

例句：The laundry basket was heapy with dirty clothes. 洗衣篮里堆满了脏衣服。|近义词：messy, cluttered, untidy, overflowing, piled high|反义词：tidy, neat, organized, orderly|词性:形容词
