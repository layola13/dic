infests:/ɪnˈfɛsts/| v. | 侵扰，骚扰；寄生于；大批滋生 | to be present (in a place or site) in large numbers, typically so as to cause damage or disease |  Rats infested the basement. 老鼠横行于地下室。|近义词：overrun, swarm, plague, beset |反义词: clear, cleanse, purify |词性:动词

infests:/ɪnˈfɛsts/| v. |遍布，充斥（常指不好的事物）| to spread or swarm in or over; to live in or on as a parasite | The abandoned house was infested with cockroaches. 这栋废弃的房子里蟑螂成灾。|近义词：pervade, permeate, fill |反义词: vacate, desert |词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin *infestare, from Latin infestus "hostile, unsafe," related to infestus "molested, disturbed," from in- "in" + a lost verb related to fendere "to strike."
