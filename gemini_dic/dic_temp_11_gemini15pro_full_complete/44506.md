crossjack: /ˈkrɒsˌdʒæk/ (British), /ˈkrɔsˌdʒæk/ (American) | n. | 后桅下帆；后桅下桁 | The lowest sail on the mizzenmast of a square-rigged ship.  It is a four-sided sail set on the crossjack yard. | The crossjack was ripped by the strong winds. 后桅下帆被强风撕裂了。 | Synonyms: mizzen course | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could consider other sails like mainsail, foresail) | noun
