anticommercialistic:/ˌæntiˌkəmɜːrʃəlˈɪstɪk/
| adj. |反商业主义的；反对商业化的|Opposed to commercialism or the pursuit of profit as the most important value.|He has a very anticommercialistic attitude towards art, believing it should be pure and not driven by market demands. 他对艺术持有非常反商业化的态度，认为艺术应该是纯粹的，而不应该被市场需求所驱动。|近义词:noncommercial, uncommercial|反义词:commercial, pro-business|词性:形容词

anticommercialism:/ˌæntiˌkəmɜːrʃəlˈɪzəm/
| n. |反商业主义|Opposition to commercialism or the pursuit of profit as the most important value.|The rise of anticommercialism in the 1960s was a reaction against the perceived excesses of consumer culture.  20世纪60年代反商业主义的兴起是对人们认为消费文化过度的一种反应。|近义词:anticonsumerism|反义词:commercialism, mercantilism|词性:名词
