agrichemical:/ˈæɡrɪˌkɛmɪkəl/
noun
农药，农业化学品
A chemical used in agriculture, such as a pesticide, herbicide, or fertilizer.
The overuse of agrichemicals can damage the environment. 过度使用农药会破坏环境。
synonyms: pesticide, herbicide, fertilizer, insecticide, fungicide
antonyms: organic fertilizer, biopesticide
n.
