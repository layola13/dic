deportation:/ˌdɪpɔːrˈteɪʃən/| n. |驱逐出境，递解|The act of deporting someone.|The deportation of the refugees caused international outrage.  难民的驱逐出境引起了国际社会的愤慨。|synonyms: expulsion, exile, banishment|antonyms: repatriation, return, admittance|noun

deport:/dɪˈpɔːrt/| vt. |驱逐出境，递解|To expel (a foreigner) from a country, typically on the grounds of illegal status or for having committed a crime.|The government deported him for his involvement in terrorist activities. 政府将他驱逐出境，因为他参与了恐怖活动。|synonyms: expel, banish, exile|antonyms: admit, repatriate, naturalize|verb
