moderatorship:/ˈmɒdərətəʃɪp/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdərətərʃɪp/ (US)| n. |版主职位；仲裁员职位；主持人职位|The position or role of a moderator, someone who presides over a discussion or debate, ensures fair and orderly proceedings, and often enforces rules.  A moderatorship can also refer to the term of office for a moderator.|She was elected to the moderatorship of the online forum. 她被选为在线论坛的版主。|近义词：chairmanship, presidency, leadership|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a position of authority)|词性: noun
