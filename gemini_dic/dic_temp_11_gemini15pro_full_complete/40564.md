contumaciousness:/ˌkɒntjʊˈmeɪʃəsnəs/ /ˌkɑːntjʊˈmeɪʃəsnəs/
n.
顽固，抗命，不服从
stubborn and willful disobedience to authority.  A persistent refusal to obey authority.
The lawyer was shocked by the contumaciousness of the defendant, who refused to cooperate with the court proceedings.  律师对被告的顽固感到震惊，被告拒绝配合法庭程序。
Near synonyms: rebelliousness, defiance, insubordination, recalcitrance
Near antonyms: obedience, compliance, submission, subservience
noun

contumacious:/ˌkɒntjʊˈmeɪʃəs/ /ˌkɑːntjʊˈmeɪʃəs/
adj.
顽固的，抗命的，不服从的
stubbornly disobedient; rebellious.  Persistently refusing to obey authority.
His contumacious behavior led to his expulsion from school. 他不服从的行为导致他被学校开除。
Near synonyms: rebellious, defiant, insubordinate, recalcitrant
Near antonyms: obedient, compliant, submissive, subservient
adjective
