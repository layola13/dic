nonfealty:/nɒnˈfiːəlti/
noun
不忠; 背信; 违誓;
Lack of loyalty or allegiance, especially to a feudal lord.  The state of not owing fealty.
The knight's nonfealty to the king resulted in his banishment.  这名骑士对国王的不忠导致了他的放逐。
Near synonyms: disloyalty, treachery, perfidy, infidelity
Antonyms: loyalty, fidelity, allegiance
