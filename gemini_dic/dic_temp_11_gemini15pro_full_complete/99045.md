interanimation:/ˌɪntərænɪˈmeɪʃən/ | n. |相互激励; 互相影响; 互动 |The interanimation between the two artists led to a collaborative project. 两位艺术家之间的互相激励促成了一项合作项目。|The interanimation of ideas within the group spurred creative solutions.  团队内部思想的互动激发了创造性的解决方案。|近义词：interaction, interplay, reciprocity|反义词：isolation, separation|词性:名词

词源:  From inter- + animation.  "Inter-" prefix meaning "between" or "among" and "animation" meaning "the state of being full of life or vigor; liveliness."  Therefore, interanimation refers to the lively interaction or exchange between entities.
