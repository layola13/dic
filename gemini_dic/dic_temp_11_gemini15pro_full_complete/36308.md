cohesively:/koʊˈhɪːsɪvli/| adv.|有凝聚力地；连贯地|In a cohesive manner; in a way that sticks together or forms a united whole.  Characterized by cohesion; forming a united whole.|The team worked cohesively to achieve their goal.  团队团结协作，实现了他们的目标。|Synonyms: unitedly, jointly, harmoniously, concordantly, consistently|Antonyms: disjointedly, incoherently, erratically, fragmentarily|词性: 副词
