alerts:/əˈlɜːrts/ | n. | 警报，警告；提醒 | something that makes you aware of a danger, problem, or opportunity | The system sends email alerts when new data is available. 系统在新数据可用时发送电子邮件警报。 | synonyms: warnings, alarms, notifications | antonyms: silences, assurances | noun

alerts:/əˈlɜːrts/ | v. | 使警觉，警告；提醒 | to warn someone of a danger, problem, or opportunity | The security guard alerted the police to the suspicious activity. 保安提醒警察注意可疑活动。 | synonyms: warns, notifies, cautions | antonyms: ignores, disregards | verb
