charco:/ˈtʃɑːrkoʊ/| n.|木炭; 炭; 炭笔|A black or dark gray porous solid consisting primarily of carbon, produced by heating wood or other organic materials in the absence of air.|Artists often use charco for sketching. 艺术家经常用木炭画素描。|Synonyms: coal, carbon, ember|Antonyms: ash, cinder|noun

charco:/ˈtʃɑːrkoʊ/| n.|炭画; 木炭画|A drawing made with charcoal.|The exhibition featured a collection of striking charcos. 展览展出了一系列引人注目的炭画。|Synonyms: charcoal drawing, sketch|Antonyms: oil painting, watercolor|noun
