counterview:/ˈkaʊntərˌvjuː/
n.|反驳，相反的观点|An opposing or contrasting view.|The government presented a counterview to the accusations of corruption. 政府提出了对腐败指控的相反观点。|近义词: rebuttal, rejoinder, counterargument|反义词: agreement, concurrence|词性:名词

Etymology:
From counter- + view.  "Counter-" acts as a prefix meaning "against" or "opposite." "View" refers to a particular way of considering something.
