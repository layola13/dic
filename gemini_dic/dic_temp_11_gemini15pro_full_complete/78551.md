ghosts:/ɡoʊsts/| n.|鬼魂；幽灵；鬼怪；（往事的）阴影；|The old house is said to be haunted by ghosts. 据说那栋老房子闹鬼。|例句：Many people believe in ghosts. 很多人相信鬼魂的存在。

例句：The ghosts of his past still haunted him. 他过去的阴影仍然困扰着他。|近义词：spirits, phantoms, specters, apparitions|反义词:reality|词性:名词

词源:From Old English *gāstas (plural), from Proto-Germanic *gaistaz.
