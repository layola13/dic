nonreceipts:/ˌnɑːn.rɪˈsiːts/
n.
未收到收据;没有收据
Lack of receipts or proof of purchase.
The IRS questioned the business expenses due to the nonreceipts.  由于没有收据，国税局质疑了业务支出。
The auditor flagged the nonreceipts as a potential issue. 审计员将缺少收据标记为一个潜在问题。
近义词:lack of receipts, absence of receipts
反义词:receipts, proof of purchase
词性:名词
