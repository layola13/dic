nettle-like:/ˈnɛtlˌlaɪk/| adj.|像荨麻的，有刺的|Resembling a nettle, especially in its stinging or irritating qualities.|The plant had nettle-like leaves that caused a rash when touched.|该植物有像荨麻一样的叶子，触摸时会引起皮疹。|近义词:prickly, stinging, irritating|反义词:smooth, soft|词性:形容词


Etymology:
From 'nettle' + '-like'. 'Nettle' comes from Old English 'netele', of Germanic origin; related to Dutch 'netel' and German 'Nessel'. '-like' is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling," "characteristic of," "in the manner of," from Old English '-lic'.
