antisocials: /ˌæntiˈsəʊʃəlz/ | n. | 反社会分子，反社会人士 | People who are antisocial avoid spending time with other people, often because they are shy or uncomfortable in social situations.  They may also exhibit behaviors that are harmful or disruptive to society. |  These antisocials vandalized the park last night. 这些反社会分子昨晚破坏了公园。|  The antisocials were known for their disruptive behavior in the neighborhood. 这些反社会人士因其在社区的破坏性行为而闻名。 | synonyms: loners, hermits, recluses, introverts (in some contexts), misfits, delinquents (if engaging in criminal behavior) | antonyms:  sociable people, extroverts, gregarious individuals | noun
