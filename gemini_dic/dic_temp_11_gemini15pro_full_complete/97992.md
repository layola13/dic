inoffensive:/ˌɪnəˈfɛnsɪv/| adj.|无害的，不冒犯的，平淡无奇的|Not objectionable or harmful; unlikely to cause offense or arouse strong feelings.|The joke was inoffensive, but not particularly funny.  这个笑话没有冒犯之意，但也不是特别好笑。|Synonyms: harmless, innocuous, unobjectionable, bland, mild|Antonyms: offensive, harmful, objectionable, provocative|adjective
