drift-off: /ˈdrɪft ɒf/ (British) /ˈdrɪft ɑːf/ (American)| vi.|渐渐入睡；不知不觉地睡着；慢慢离开；逐渐消失|To gradually fall asleep, or to slowly move away or disappear.|例句：I drifted off during the lecture. 我在讲座期间不知不觉地睡着了。|近义词：doze off, nod off, fall asleep|反义词：wake up|词性：动词

drift-off: /ˈdrɪft ɒf/ (British) /ˈdrɪft ɑːf/ (American)| n.|睡着；精神恍惚|The act of falling asleep or becoming mentally absent.|例句：The drift-off was so gradual I didn't even realize I was asleep.  我不知不觉地睡着了，甚至没有意识到自己睡着了。|近义词：sleep, doze|反义词：awakening|词性：名词
