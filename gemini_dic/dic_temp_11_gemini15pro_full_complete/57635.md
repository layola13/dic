draughtsperson:/ˈdrɑːftspɜːrsən/ /ˈdræftspɜːrsən/
noun
绘图员; 制图员
A person who makes detailed technical drawings or plans, especially architectural ones.
The draughtsperson carefully rendered the elevations of the proposed building.  绘图员仔细地绘制了拟建建筑物的立面图。
drafter, draftsman, draftswoman, designer
architect (更偏向建筑师)
noun
