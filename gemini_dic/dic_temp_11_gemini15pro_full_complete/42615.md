counterrally: /ˈkaʊntərˌræli/ | n. | 反集会，对抗性集会 | A public meeting or demonstration in opposition to another. |  The students organized a counterrally to protest the proposed tuition hike. 学生们组织了一场反集会抗议提议的学费上涨。

The opposition party held a counterrally to challenge the government's claims. 反对党举行了一场反集会，挑战政府的主张。

| 近义词：demonstration, protest, march | 反义词：support, agreement | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  "counter-" (against, opposite) + "rally" (a mass meeting).  "Counter-" comes from the Old French "contre-"  and ultimately from the Latin "contra" (against). "Rally" comes from the Old French "ralier" (to reunite).
