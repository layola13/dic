cassideous:/kæˈsɪdiəs/
adjective
乳酪状的; 干酪样的
Resembling cheese or curd; cheesy.  Specifically relating to the texture or appearance of cheese, particularly referring to a soft, white, and somewhat crumbly consistency.
The infection caused a cassideous discharge. 感染导致了乳酪状的分泌物。
近义词:cheesy, curdy
反义词:watery, liquid
词性:adjective
