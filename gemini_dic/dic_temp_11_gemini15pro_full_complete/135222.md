nonoxygenous:/ˌnɒnɒkˈsɪdʒɪnəs/ | adj. |非产氧的；不依赖氧气的|Not producing oxygen; not requiring oxygen for life.|Example:  Nonoxygenous photosynthesis is a type of photosynthesis that does not produce oxygen. 非產氧光合作用是一種不產生氧氣的光合作用。|Example:  Certain bacteria perform nonoxygenous photosynthesis. 某些細菌進行非產氧光合作用。|Synonym: anaerobic |Antonym: oxygenous |adjective
