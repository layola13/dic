acquir:/əˈkwaɪər/|v.|获得，取得，学到|to gain or obtain something, typically by one's own efforts or ability; to learn or develop a skill, habit, or quality.|She acquired a new car last week. 她上周买了一辆新车。|obtain, gain, get, learn, achieve|lose, forfeit|verb
Etymology: From Old French *aquerre, from Latin acquirere, from ad- (“to”) + quaerere (“to seek”).
