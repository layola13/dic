dactylotheca:/ˌdæktɪləˈθiːkə/| n. |指鞘；指骨套|A fossil sheath or covering of a finger or toe bone, especially of a reptile or amphibian.|The paleontologist discovered a well-preserved dactylotheca from the Jurassic period.  古生物学家发现了一个保存完好的侏罗纪时期的指骨套。|近义词:ungual sheath|反义词: (No direct antonym exists)|词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek *daktylos* (finger) + *theke* (case, sheath).
