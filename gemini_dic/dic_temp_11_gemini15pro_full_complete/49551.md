deme:/diːm/ | n. | 地方群体；同类群；族群 | A local population of organisms of one species that actively interbreed with one another and share a distinct gene pool. | Example: The deme of butterflies on this island has developed unique wing patterns.  这个岛上的蝴蝶族群已经形成了独特的翅膀图案。| Example:  Researchers studied the genetic diversity within the deme. 研究人员研究了该族群内的遗传多样性。| Synonyms: population, group, community | Antonyms: individual | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek δῆμος (dêmos, “country district, people, commoners”).
