bomar:/ˈboʊmɑːr/
noun
波玛, 靶心, 中心点
A target or bullseye, often used in the context of archery or shooting.  Specifically refers to the center ring or bullseye of a target used in archery.  Sometimes used figuratively to mean a central point or focus.
He aimed carefully and hit the bomar. 他小心地瞄准，射中了靶心。
Synonyms: bullseye, center, target, mark
Antonyms: periphery, edge, outskirts
n.
