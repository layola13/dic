kleptomaniac: /ˌklɛptəˈmeɪniæk/ | n. | 盗窃癖患者 | A person who has an uncontrollable urge to steal, typically without any need for the stolen items. |  例句：The kleptomaniac was caught stealing a candy bar, even though she had plenty of money.  盗窃癖患者被抓到偷糖果，即使她有很多钱。

例句：Despite her wealthy background, she was a kleptomaniac and often stole insignificant items. 尽管她出身富裕，但她是一个盗窃癖患者，经常偷一些无关紧要的东西。| 近义词：thief, shoplifter | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可用守法公民 law-abiding citizen) | 词性: noun
