hydroferrocyanic:/ˌhaɪdrəʊˌfɛrəʊsaɪˈænɪk/ | adj. |  氢铁氰化物的；六氰合铁(II)酸的 | Relating to or containing the anion [Fe(CN)₆]⁴⁻, derived from hydroferrocyanic acid (also called hexacyanoferrate(II)).  It is characterized by the presence of iron in the +2 oxidation state complexed with six cyanide ions. | Potassium hydroferrocyanide is used as an anticaking agent in road salt. 氢铁氰化钾用作道路盐的抗结块剂。| Synonyms: hexacyanoferrate(II), ferrocyanide | Antonyms: hexacyanoferrate(III), ferricyanide | adjective
