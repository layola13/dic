manifestive:/ˈmænɪˌfɛstɪv/| adj.|明显的，明白的；证明性的|clearly apparent to the sight or understanding; serving as clear evidence of something.|Her joy was manifestive in her bright smile. 她的喜悦从她灿烂的笑容中显露无疑。|Synonyms: obvious, apparent, evident, patent, clear, plain, unmistakable, palpable, demonstrable, pronounced, conspicuous, noticeable, discernible, perceptible, visible, overt, manifest, express, explicit, unambiguous, unequivocal|Antonyms: hidden, concealed, obscure, unclear, ambiguous, equivocal, subtle, inconspicuous|adjective

manifesto:/ˌmænɪˈfɛstoʊ/| n.|宣言，声明|a public declaration of policy and aims, especially one issued before an election by a political party or candidate.|The party released its election manifesto. 该党发布了其竞选宣言。|Synonyms: declaration, proclamation, pronouncement, statement, announcement, declaration of intentions, platform, program|Antonyms: retraction, denial|noun
