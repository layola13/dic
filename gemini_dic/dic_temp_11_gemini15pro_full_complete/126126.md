muti:/ˈmuːti/ | n. |  传统医药;  巫术;  魔法; (尤指南非的)传统疗法 | Traditional medicine, especially of African origin, involving the use of herbs and other natural substances.  It can also refer to witchcraft or magic, particularly in a Southern African context. |  The sangoma prepared a muti to heal the sick child.  桑戈马(南非传统医生)准备了一种传统药物来治疗生病的孩子。

The villagers believed the woman had used muti to curse her neighbour. 村民们认为那个女人用了巫术诅咒了她的邻居。 | 近义词:  remedy, medicine, charm, spell | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, depends on context. Could be conventional medicine) | 词性: noun
