archery: /ˈɑːrtʃəri/ (US) /ˈɑːtʃəri/ (UK)| n. |射箭；射箭运动|The sport or skill of shooting with a bow and arrows. |She took up archery when she was twelve. 她十二岁时开始学习射箭。| Robin Hood was skilled in archery. 罗宾汉精通射箭。| 近义词: toxophily | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Old French *archerie*, from archer "archer," from Latin *arcuarius* "bowman," from *arcus* "bow."
