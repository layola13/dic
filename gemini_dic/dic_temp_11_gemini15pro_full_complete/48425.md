deducibly:/dɪˈdjuːsəbli/
adverb|可推断地，可推导地|in a way that can be deduced; by deduction|The conclusion is deducibly correct from the premises.  从前提可以推断出结论是正确的。|The theorem is deducibly valid. 该定理可推导地有效。|近义词：inferably, logically, demonstrably|反义词：inductively, empirically|词性: adverb

词源: From deduce + -ible + -ly.  Deduce comes from the Latin dēdūcere, meaning "to lead down" or "to draw out."  The suffix -ible forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," and -ly forms adverbs.
