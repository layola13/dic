floral:/ˈflɔːrəl/ (US) /ˈflɒrəl/ (UK)| adj.|花的，花卉的；植物的|Relating to flowers; made of flowers. |The wedding cake was decorated with delicate floral patterns. 婚礼蛋糕上装饰着精致的花卉图案。|The floral arrangements in the church were stunning. 教堂里的插花美极了。|近义词：flowery, blossoming|反义词:plain, unadorned|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: florall, from Latin: floralis, from flos, flor- ‘flower’.
