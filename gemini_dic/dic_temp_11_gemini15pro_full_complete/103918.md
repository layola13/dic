judge's:/ˈdʒʌdʒɪz/
noun
法官的；裁判的
belonging to or associated with a judge.
The judge's decision is final. 法官的判决是最终判决。
The judge's gavel fell, silencing the courtroom. 法官的法槌落下，使法庭安静下来。
synonyms: judicial, magistrate's
antonyms: defendant's, accused's
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French juge (“judge”), from Latin iudex (“judge”).  The -'s is the possessive suffix.
