medicaid:/ˈmɛdɪkeɪd/| n. |医疗补助; 医疗補助|A U.S. government program financed by federal, state, and local funds that provides health care for low-income people.|Many low-income families rely on Medicaid for healthcare. 许多低收入家庭依靠医疗补助获得医疗保健。|Medicaid provides healthcare coverage for eligible low-income individuals and families. 医疗补助为符合条件的低收入个人和家庭提供医疗保健。|近义词：Medicare(for the elderly), welfare, public assistance|反义词: private insurance|词性: noun
