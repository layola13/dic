effodient:/ɪˈfoʊdiənt/
adjective
挖掘的; 适于挖掘的
adapted for digging; burrowing.
The effodient forelimbs of the mole are perfectly suited for its subterranean lifestyle. 鼹鼠适于挖掘的前肢非常适合其地下生活方式。
近义词:fossorial, burrowing
反义词:non-burrowing, surface-dwelling
词性:adjective

effodient:/ɪˈfoʊdiənt/
noun
穴居动物
a burrowing animal.
Moles are classic examples of effodients. 鼹鼠是穴居动物的典型例子。
近义词:burrower, fossorial animal
反义词:surface dweller
词性:noun

Etymology: From Latin *effodiēns*, present participle of *effodere* "to dig out, dig up," from *ex-* "out" + *fodere* "to dig".
