curious: /ˈkjʊəriəs/ (US) /ˈkjʊərɪəs/ (UK)| adj.| 好奇的，求知的；古怪的，奇特的| Eager to know or learn something. Strange; unusual. | She was curious about his past. 她对他的过去很好奇。| The museum has a curious collection of old toys.  博物馆收藏了一批奇特的古董玩具。| synonyms: inquisitive, nosy, inquiring, strange, unusual, odd, peculiar | antonyms: indifferent, apathetic, uninterested, normal, ordinary | adjective
