burgonet:/ˈbɜːrɡənɪt/ | n. | 轻盔，钢盔 | A type of helmet, lighter than a full helmet, popular in the 16th and 17th centuries.  It typically had a visor and cheek pieces that could be raised or lowered. |  The knight wore a gleaming burgonet into battle.  骑士戴着闪亮的轻盔上战场。| synonym: helmet, casque, headpiece | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bareheadedness" as a state) | noun
