encrusting:/ɪnˈkrʌstɪŋ/
adjective
结壳的; 包壳的; 覆盖着的
Covering with a hard crust.
The encrusting algae made the rocks slippery. 这些结壳的藻类使岩石很滑。
The sink was encrusting with limescale. 水槽上结了水垢。
synonyms: coating, covering, overlaying
antonyms: bare, exposed, uncovered
adjective

encrust:/ɪnˈkrʌst/
verb
使…结壳; 用…包壳
To cover or become covered with a hard crust.
Barnacles encrusted the ship's hull. 藤壶包覆了船体。
The jewels encrusted the crown. 宝石镶嵌在王冠上。
synonyms: coat, cover, inlay
antonyms: strip, uncover, expose
verb
