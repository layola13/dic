jump-jets:/ˈdʒʌmp dʒɛts/| n.|跳跃式喷气机；垂直/短距起落飞机|An aircraft capable of vertical or short take-off and landing (V/STOL), typically by directing thrust downwards.|The Harrier is a well-known example of a jump-jet.  鹞式战斗机是跳跃式喷气机的著名例子。|Synonyms: V/STOL aircraft, VSTOL plane|Antonyms: conventional aircraft, traditional airplane|noun

jump-jet:/ˈdʒʌmp dʒɛt/| n.|跳跃式喷气发动机；垂直/短距起落发动机|The engine of a jump-jet aircraft, enabling vertical or short take-off and landing.|The jump-jet's engine allows it to take off from almost anywhere.  跳跃式喷气发动机的设计使飞机几乎可以在任何地方起飞。|Synonyms: V/STOL engine|Antonyms: conventional aircraft engine|noun
