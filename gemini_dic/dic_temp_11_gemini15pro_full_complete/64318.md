equivoke: /ɪˈkwɪvəʊk/ (UK) /ɪˈkwɪvoʊk/ (US) | v. n. | 含糊其辞；模棱两可的话；双关语 | To use ambiguous language so as to conceal the truth or avoid committing oneself.  A statement or word with two or more possible meanings, usually used to mislead or deceive. |  He equivocated when asked about his involvement in the scandal, neither confirming nor denying it. 他在被问及是否卷入丑闻时含糊其辞，既不承认也不否认。| Synonyms: prevaricate, evade, hedge | Antonyms: be forthright, be direct | verb
