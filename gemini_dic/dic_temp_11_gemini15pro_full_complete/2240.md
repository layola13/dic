adducible:/əˈdjuːsəbl/
adjective
可引用的，可援引的
able to be adduced or cited as evidence or proof.
The lawyer presented adducible evidence to support his client's claim. 律师提供了可引用的证据来支持其委托人的主张。
Synonyms: citable, quotable, referable
Antonyms: inadmissible, irrelevant, unprovable
词性: adjective
