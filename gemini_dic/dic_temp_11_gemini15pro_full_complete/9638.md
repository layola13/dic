antistructuralist: /ˌæntiˈstrʌktʃərəlɪst/ | adj. n. | 反结构主义的；反结构主义者 |  Relating to or characteristic of antistructuralism; a person who opposes or rejects structuralism.  Antistructuralism questions the idea that there are underlying structures that determine meaning and emphasizes the instability and fluidity of meaning. |  His antistructuralist approach to literature challenged traditional interpretations. 他对文学的反结构主义方法挑战了传统解释。 |  The antistructuralist philosopher argued that language is not a fixed system.  这位反结构主义哲学家认为语言不是一个固定的系统。 |  poststructuralist, deconstructionist | structuralist | adjective, noun
