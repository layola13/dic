hooted:/ˈhuːtɪd/ | v. | 鸣叫；发出猫头鹰般的叫声；大声嘲笑 | made a loud, high-pitched sound like an owl; shouted in disapproval | The owl hooted from the tree. 猫头鹰在树上鸣叫。|近义词：screeched, shrieked, yelled, booed |反义词: whispered, murmured |词性: 动词
Etymology: From Middle English *houten, *hoten, from Old English *hōtian, from Proto-Germanic *hūtōną (“to shout”), from Proto-Indo-European *kewd- (“to shout, scream”).
