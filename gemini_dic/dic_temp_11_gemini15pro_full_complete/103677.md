jolterheaded:/ˈdʒoʊltərˌhɛdɪd/| adj.|愚笨的，傻头傻脑的|Stupid or slow-witted; having a thick skull.|He made a jolterheaded decision to invest all his money in a single stock. 他做了一个傻头傻脑的决定，把所有的钱都投资在一支股票上。|近义词：stupid, foolish, dense, doltish, blockheaded|反义词：intelligent, clever, smart, bright|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "jolterhead," an obsolete noun meaning a blockhead or stupid person.  "Jolter" likely referred to a heavy, jolting blow, suggesting a head too thick to register impact, thus implying stupidity.
