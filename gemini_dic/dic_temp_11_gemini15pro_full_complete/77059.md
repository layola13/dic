词源:
From Old French *grenat (influenced by Latin granatum “pomegranate”), from Medieval Latin granatus, from Latin granum "grain, seed".  So called from the resemblance of the crystals to the seeds of a pomegranate.
来自古法语 *grenat（受拉丁语 granatum “石榴” 的影响），来自中拉丁语 granatus，来自拉丁语 granum “谷物，种子”。之所以这样称呼，是因为晶体与石榴籽相似。
