expoundable:/ɪkˈspaʊndəbl/ | adj. | 可解释的，可阐述的 | Capable of being expounded; that can be explained in detail. | The professor's lectures were always clear and expoundable, making complex topics easy to understand. 教授的讲座总是清晰易懂，使复杂的主题易于理解。|  The theory is expoundable in simple terms.  这个理论可以用简单的术语来解释。 | synonyms: explainable, interpretable, elucidable | antonyms: inexplicable, obscure, uninterpretable | adjective
