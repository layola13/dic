nucleiform:/ˈnjuːkliəfɔːrm/
adjective
核形的，似核的
Resembling a nucleus in form.  Having the shape of a nucleus.
The nucleiform structure was observed under the microscope.  在显微镜下观察到了核形结构。
The amoeba formed a nucleiform body within its cytoplasm.  变形虫在其细胞质内形成了一个核形结构。
近义词: nuclear, nucleus-shaped
反义词: anuclear
