goeland:/ɡo.lɑ̃/| n. |海鸥|A large white and grey seabird.|例句：A goeland soared above the waves. 一只海鸥在波涛上翱翔。|例句：The goelands were crying loudly on the pier. 海鸥在码头上大声鸣叫。|近义词：seagull, gull|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From Old North French *goéland, from Old Norse *gaukr, "gull" + Old French *elant, "flying".
