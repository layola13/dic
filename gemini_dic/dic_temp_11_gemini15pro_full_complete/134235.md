noninertnesses:/ˌnɒnɪˈnɜːtnɪsɪz/ | n. | 非惰性;非稳定性 | The state or quality of being noninert; lack of inertness.  It refers to the tendency of a substance to react chemically. | The noninertnesses of the alkali metals makes them highly reactive with water.  碱金属的非惰性使它们与水发生剧烈反应。| Near synonyms: reactivity, instability, lability | Antonyms: inertness, stability, inactivity | noun
