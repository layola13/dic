binocularly:/bɪˈnɒkjələli/ /baɪˈnɑːkjələrli/
adverb
用雙眼地; 用雙筒望遠鏡地
Using both eyes at the same time; with binoculars.
She observed the bird binocularly through her high-powered binoculars. 她用高倍双筒望远镜用双眼观察那只鸟。
He studied the map binocularly, trying to find the hidden path. 他用双眼仔细研究地图，试图找到隐藏的小路。
近义词:stereoscopically, with both eyes
反义词:monocularly
词性:副词
