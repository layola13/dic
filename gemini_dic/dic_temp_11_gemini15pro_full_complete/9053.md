antimedieval:/ˌæntiˌmiːdiˈiːvəl/ | adj. | 反中世纪的，反对中世纪的 | Opposed to or hostile to the Middle Ages or its values and institutions. |  He held a strongly antimedieval view of history, believing the period to be a dark age. 他对历史持强烈的反中世纪观点，认为那个时期是一个黑暗时代。

| Example:  The Enlightenment fostered an antimedieval spirit, emphasizing reason and progress over tradition. 启蒙运动孕育了一种反中世纪的精神，强调理性与进步高于传统。

| Synonyms: anti-medieval, modern, progressive | Antonyms: medieval, medievalist, traditional | adjective
