dreamlessnesses:/ˈdriːmləsnəsɪz/
noun
无梦状态；无梦
the state of being without dreams
His dreamlessnesses were a blessing after weeks of nightmares.  他几周噩梦连连后，无梦的状态是一种幸福。
Near synonyms:  sleep without dreams, deep sleep
Antonyms: dreamfulness, vivid dreaming
