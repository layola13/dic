noncertifications:/ˌnɑːnsɜːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/
noun
未认证，未经证明
lack of certifications or official endorsements.

The company's noncertification in certain areas limited its ability to bid on some contracts.  该公司在某些领域未经认证，限制了其投标某些合同的能力。
non-accreditation, lack of credentials
