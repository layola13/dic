jurisdictional:/ˌdʒʊərɪsˈdɪkʃənəl/
adjective
管辖权的；司法权的
Relating to the power or right of a legal or political agency to exercise authority over a person, subject matter, or territory.
The court has jurisdictional limits and cannot try cases outside of its designated area.  该法院有管辖权限制，不能审理其指定区域以外的案件。
The dispute raises complex jurisdictional issues. 这一争议引发了复杂的管辖权问题。
synonyms:  authoritative, official, legal, governing
antonyms: unauthorized, unofficial, illegal
