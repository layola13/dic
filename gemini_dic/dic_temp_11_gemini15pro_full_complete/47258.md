/wɒt/ | pron. | 什么 | Used to ask for information about something. |
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you want for dinner? = 你晚饭想吃什么？
|近义词: which |反义词:  (What doesn't have a direct antonym) | 词性: pronoun


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你靠什么谋生？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing over there? = 他在那里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? = 你最近学了什么？
What has she been up to? = 她最近在忙什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你整个星期都在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? = 你说什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she wearing yesterday? = 她昨天穿什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they eaten for lunch? = 他们午饭吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the power went out? = 停电前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她转专业前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将取得什么成就？
What will they have finished by then? = 到那时他们将完成什么？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？



虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
What would she say if she were here? = 如果她在这里，她会说什么？

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？


否定句:
I don't know what to think. = 我不知道该怎么想。
She doesn't understand what he's saying. = 她不明白他在说什么。
