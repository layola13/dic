eburnification:/ˌiːbɜːrnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
骨质硬化，象牙质化
The process of becoming hard and dense like ivory, typically referring to bone tissue.
The eburnification of the bone made it brittle and prone to fracture.  骨质硬化使骨头变脆，容易骨折。
The advanced osteoarthritis led to eburnification of the joint surfaces.  晚期骨关节炎导致关节表面骨质硬化。
sclerosis, hardening
softening, demineralization
noun
