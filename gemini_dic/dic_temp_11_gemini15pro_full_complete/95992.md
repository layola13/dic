incurably:/ɪnˈkjʊərəbli/ | adv. | 不可治愈地；无法补救地 | Not able to be cured or remedied. |  He is incurably optimistic about the future. 他对未来抱有不可救药的乐观态度。 |  The disease is, sadly, incurable at this stage.  遗憾的是，这种疾病在这个阶段是无法治愈的。 | 近义词: irremediably, terminally, hopelessly | 反义词: curably, remediably | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *incurabili, from Late Latin incūrābilis, from Latin in- (not) + cūrābilis (curable).  The Latin cūrābilis is derived from the verb cūrō (to care for, attend to, heal).
