imperceptible:/ˌɪmpərˈseptəbl/ | adj. | 难以察觉的；感觉不到的 | Not perceptible by a sense or by the mind : extremely slight or subtle. | The change in her voice was almost imperceptible. 她的声音变化几乎难以察觉。 |  The difference in taste is imperceptible to me.  我感觉不到味道上的区别。 |  indistinguishable, undetectable, subtle, faint, minimal |  perceptible, noticeable, obvious, apparent, conspicuous | adjective
