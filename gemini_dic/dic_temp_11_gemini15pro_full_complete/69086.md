faunus:/ˈfɔːnəs/| n.| 福纳斯（罗马神话中的田野、森林和畜群之神）| In Roman mythology, Faunus was the god of the wild, of fields, forests, and flocks. He was also known as a prophetic god. 在罗马神话中，福纳斯是荒野、田野、森林和畜群之神。他也被称为预言之神。|例句：Faunus was often depicted with horns and goat's feet. 福纳斯通常被描绘成有角和山羊脚的形象。

|例句：The festival of Faunus, the Lupercalia, was celebrated in February. 福纳斯的节日，卢珀卡利亚节，在二月庆祝。|近义词：Pan, Silvanus|反义词: None|词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *faunus, related to favere ("to favor") and possibly to the deity Favonius. 最初源自拉丁语 *faunus，与 favere（“喜爱”）有关，也可能与神灵法温纽斯（Favonius）有关。
