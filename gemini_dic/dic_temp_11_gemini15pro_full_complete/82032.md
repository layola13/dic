grisamber:/ˈɡrɪsˌæmbər/ | n. |龙涎香|A waxy substance produced in the intestines of the sperm whale, used in perfumery as a fixative.|The perfume had a subtle base of grisamber. 这款香水有一种淡淡的龙涎香基调。|近义词:ambergris|反义词: None|词性:名词

词源:
From Old French *grisambre, from gris "gray" + ambre "amber".  The word literally means "gray amber".
