citylike:/ˈsɪtiˌlaɪk/
adjective
像城市的; 도시 같은 (Korean)
Resembling a city, especially in terms of  bustle,  density, or sophistication.
The small town had a surprisingly citylike atmosphere.  这个小镇有着令人惊讶的城市氛围。
The conference center had a citylike buzz. 会议中心有着像城市一样的喧嚣。
近义词:urban, metropolitan, cosmopolitan
反义词:rural, rustic, pastoral
词性:adjective
