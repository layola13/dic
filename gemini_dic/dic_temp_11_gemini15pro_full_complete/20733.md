blameworthiest:/ˈbleɪmwɜːrðiɪst/
adjective
最应该受责备的，最该谴责的
Most deserving of blame; most culpable.
He was judged the blameworthiest of all the conspirators. 他被认为是所有同谋者中最应受谴责的。
近义词: most culpable, most guilty, most reprehensible
反义词: most innocent, most blameless
词性: adjective

blameworthy:/ˈbleɪmwɜːrði/
adjective
应受责备的，该谴责的
Deserving of blame; culpable.
His conduct was clearly blameworthy. 他的行为显然应受谴责。
近义词: culpable, guilty, reprehensible
反义词: innocent, blameless
词性: adjective

blame:/bleɪm/
noun, verb
责备，归咎于
Responsibility for a fault or wrong; to hold responsible.
The blame for the accident rests squarely with the driver. 事故的责任完全在于司机。 /  They blamed him for the failure. 他们把失败归咎于他。
近义词: fault, responsibility, culpability (noun);  reproach, censure, condemn (verb)
反义词: praise, credit (noun);  exonerate, absolve (verb)
词性: noun, verb


词源:
From Old English *blāmwian "to blaspheme, slander," from blāma "disgrace, injury."  The sense shifted from "blaspheme" to "find fault with" in Middle English.
