deposited:/dɪˈpɒzɪtɪd/
verb (past tense and past participle of deposit)
存放；使沉积；付（定金）
put or set down; lay down; place
例句：
The flood waters deposited a layer of mud on the fields. 洪水在田野上沉积了一层泥。
She deposited the money in her bank account. 她把钱存进了她的银行账户。

近义词：placed, laid, put, stored, left, paid (a deposit)
反义词：withdrew, removed, took out

词性: verb

词源：From Latin depositus, past participle of deponere "to lay down, put aside," from de- "down" + ponere "to put, place."
