nomial:/ˈnəʊmiəl/ | n. | 项; 名目; (数学)项式 | A term or name, especially in mathematics, consisting of a sum or difference of two or more monomials.  |  A binomial is a polynomial with two terms. 二项式是一个有两个项的多项式。 |  The trinomial x² + 2x + 1 can be factored.  三项式 x² + 2x + 1 可以被分解因式。 | synonym: term, expression | antonym: (no direct antonym, perhaps consider "coefficient" or "constant" as conceptually different parts of a polynomial) | noun
