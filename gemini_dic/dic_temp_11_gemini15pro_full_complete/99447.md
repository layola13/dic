interesting:/ˈɪntrəstɪŋ/| adj.|有趣的，引起兴趣的|arousing curiosity or interest; holding or catching the attention.|例句：The book is very interesting. 这本书很有趣。|近义词：fascinating, engaging, captivating, intriguing, absorbing|反义词：boring, dull, tedious, monotonous, uninteresting|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *enteressen*, from Anglo-Norman *enteresser*, from Old French *entre intéresser* "to concern, affect," from *entre* "between, among" + *intéresser* "to interest."  Ultimately from Latin *inter* "between" and *esse* "to be."
