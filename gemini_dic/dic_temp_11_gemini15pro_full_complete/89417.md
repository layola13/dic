homotypal:/ˌhɒməˈtaɪpəl/ /ˌhoʊməˈtaɪpəl/
adjective
同型的，同类型的
Of the same type or structure.  Corresponding in structure, type, or otherwise.
The homotypal graft was accepted by the patient's body. 同型移植物被患者的身体接受了。
The researcher compared homotypal tissues from different species. 研究人员比较了来自不同物种的同型组织。
近义词: homologous, similar, isotypic, isomorphic
反义词: heterotypal, dissimilar, different
词性: adjective
