bloomery:/ˈbluːməri/| n.|锻铁炉，冶铁炉|A bloomery is a type of furnace used for smelting iron from its oxides. It is a simple furnace, typically built of clay or stone, in which iron ore is heated with charcoal and a forced draft of air.|The bloomery was the earliest type of furnace used to produce iron. 锻铁炉是最早用于生产铁的炉子。|The archaeological dig uncovered the remains of a medieval bloomery. 考古挖掘发现了中世纪锻铁炉的遗迹。|近义词：furnace, forge|反义词:|noun
