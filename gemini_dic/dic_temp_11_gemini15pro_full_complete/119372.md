microcosmically:/ˌmaɪkrəˈkɒzmɪkli/| adv.|以微观世界的方式；象征性地|Representing the whole world in miniature; symbolically.  It examines the issue microcosmically, focusing on a small group to represent larger trends.| He studied the ant colony microcosmically, seeing in its struggles a reflection of human society.  She explored the theme of love microcosmically through the relationship of two characters. | 近义词: representatively, symbolically, allegorically | 反义词: macroscopically, broadly | 词性:副词
