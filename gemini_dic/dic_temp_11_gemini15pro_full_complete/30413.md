centonism: /ˈsɛntənɪzəm/ | n. | 杂烩诗; 集锦诗; | The act or practice of composing a literary work made up of passages or lines from other authors.  A literary work composed in this way. |  His latest poem is an example of centonism, borrowing heavily from the Romantic poets.  The professor criticized the student's paper for its blatant centonism. | 近义词: patchwork, compilation, pastiche | 反义词: original work,  new creation | noun
