bemoaningly:/bɪˈmoʊnɪŋli/
adv.
悲叹地，哀怨地
in a way that expresses sadness or complaint
She bemoaningly recounted the loss of her beloved pet. 她悲叹地讲述了她心爱宠物的离去。
The old man sat on the park bench, bemoaningly muttering about the price of bread. 老人坐在公园的长椅上，悲叹着面包的价格。
Synonyms: plaintively, mournfully, sadly, dolefully
Antonyms: cheerfully, joyfully, happily
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From bemoan (verb) + -ingly (adverbial suffix).  "Bemoan" comes from the Old English "bemænan," meaning "to lament, complain about."  The "-ingly" suffix transforms the verb into an adverb, indicating the manner in which the action is performed.
