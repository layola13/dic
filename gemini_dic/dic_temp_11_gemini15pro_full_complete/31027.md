chalybeous:/kəˈlɪbiəs/
adjective
含铁的；似钢的；铁味的
containing or resembling iron or steel; impregnated with iron; having the taste or smell of iron

The chalybeous spring water is known for its high iron content.
这处含铁的泉水以其高铁含量而闻名。

The medicine left a chalybeous taste in my mouth.
这种药在我嘴里留下了一种铁的味道。

Near the volcano, the rocks had a distinctive chalybeous hue.
在火山附近，岩石呈现出一种独特的铁色调。


Synonyms: ferruginous, iron, steely
Antonyms: non-ferrous, iron-free

Etymology: From Latin *chalybs*, from Greek *khályps* (steel, hardened iron).  Ultimately of unknown, possibly pre-Greek, origin.
