oeconomical:/ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪkl/ /ˌɛkəˈnɑːmɪkl/| adj.|经济的；节俭的；实惠的|Avoiding waste and unnecessary expense; thrifty.  Relating to economics or the economy.|例句：She is an economical housekeeper. 她是一位节俭的家庭主妇。|例句：It's more economical to buy in bulk. 批量购买更划算。|近义词：thrifty, frugal, saving, sparing|反义词：extravagant, wasteful, lavish|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *oeconomicus*, from Greek oikonomikos ‘of household management’, from oikos ‘house’ + nemein ‘manage’.
