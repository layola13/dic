accountability:/əˌkaʊntəˈbɪləti/
n.|责任, 义务, 有义务, 可说明性|The state of being accountable; liability to be called on to render an account; responsibility.  The quality or state of being accountable; especially : an obligation or willingness to accept responsibility or to account for one's actions.|The CEO emphasized the importance of accountability within the company. 首席执行官强调了公司内部责任的重要性。|近义词：responsibility, liability, answerability|反义词：impunity, unaccountability|词性:名词

accountability:/əˌkaʊntəˈbɪləti/
n.|问责制|A system of holding individuals or organizations responsible for their actions, typically by requiring them to explain or justify what they have done.|The new accountability measures were put in place to improve performance. 新的问责制措施是为了提高绩效而制定的。|近义词：responsibility system, performance management|反义词: lack of oversight|词性:名词
