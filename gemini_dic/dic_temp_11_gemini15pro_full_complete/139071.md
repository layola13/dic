oath's: /oʊθs/
noun
誓言的；誓约的
Possessive form of "oath," meaning belonging to or associated with an oath.
The oath's solemnity impressed everyone present.  这个誓言的庄严性给所有在场的人留下了深刻的印象。
The judge reminded the witness of the oath's importance. 法官提醒证人誓言的重要性。

近义词: vow's, pledge's, promise's
反义词:  (An oath itself doesn't have a direct opposite, but breaking an oath is often associated with betrayal or treachery.)
