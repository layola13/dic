bragg:/bræɡ/| v. n.|吹嘘，自夸；吹牛大王|To talk boastfully about oneself or one's achievements.  Derived from the surname Bragg, popularized by the character Braggadocio in Edmund Spenser's *The Faerie Queene* (1590), a boastful character.|He bragged about his new car. 他吹嘘他的新车。|近义词：boast, vaunt, crow|反义词：be modest, downplay|词性:动词, 名词

braggart:/ˈbræɡərt/| n.|吹牛者，自夸的人|A person who brags.  Shares the same etymology as "bragg."|He's such a braggart, always talking about himself. 他真是个吹牛大王，总是谈论自己。|近义词：boaster, show-off|反义词：humble person|词性:名词
