breadbaskets:/ˈbredˌbæskɪts/
n.
粮仓；产粮区
regions or countries that produce large amounts of grain, especially wheat.
Kansas is one of the major breadbaskets of America.  堪萨斯州是美国主要的粮仓之一。
The war in Ukraine has disrupted one of the world's breadbaskets. 乌克兰战争扰乱了世界主要的粮仓之一。
