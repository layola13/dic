caretaker:/ˈkerˌteɪkər/ (US) /ˈkeəˌteɪkə(r)/ (UK)| adj.| 临时的；代理的|Acting as a caretaker; temporary.|She's the caretaker manager until they appoint a permanent one. 在他们任命一位长期经理之前，她是代理经理。|temporary, acting, interim|permanent, long-term|adjective
