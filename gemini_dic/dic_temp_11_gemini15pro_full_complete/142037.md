organography: /ˌɔːrɡəˈnɒɡrəfi/ /ˌɔːrɡəˈnɑːɡrəfi/
noun
器官学
The study of the structure and arrangement of organs in plants and animals.
Organography helps us understand the relationships between different plant species. 器官学帮助我们理解不同植物种类之间的关系。
The student is fascinated by the details revealed through plant organography.  这个学生被植物器官学揭示的细节所吸引。
近义词: morphology, anatomy
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider "physiology" as a contrasting field of study focusing on function rather than structure.)
词性: n.
