karagane:/kɑːrəˈɡɑːneɪ/
noun
雁，雁类
wild goose; geese collectively
The karagane fly south for the winter. 雁在冬天飞往南方。
近义词:goose, wildfowl
反义词: (No direct antonym for a type of bird)
词性:n.
