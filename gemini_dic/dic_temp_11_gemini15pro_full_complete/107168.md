lager: /ˈlɑːɡər/ (UK) /ˈlɑːɡər/ (US)| n.|拉格啤酒，窖藏啤酒|A type of beer, light in color and body, that is fermented and stored at a low temperature.|I prefer lager to ale. 我更喜欢拉格啤酒而不是麦芽啤酒。|Synonyms: pilsner, beer|Antonyms: ale, stout|noun

lager: /ˈlɑːɡər/ (UK) /ˈlɑːɡər/ (US)| adj.|窖藏的|Stored in a lager.|Lager beer is stored for several weeks. 窖藏啤酒要存放几个星期。|Synonyms: stored, cellared|Antonyms: fresh, unlagered|adjective
