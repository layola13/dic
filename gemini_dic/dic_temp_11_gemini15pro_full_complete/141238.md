oozy:/ˈuːzi/| adj.|渗出的；软泥的；泥泞的|
(of a thick liquid) flowing slowly and steadily; resembling or consisting of ooze.  (Cambridge Dictionary) |The wound was oozy and infected. 伤口渗出液体并感染了。|Synonyms: slimy, viscous, sticky, gooey|Antonyms: dry, solid, firm|adjective

oozy:/ˈuːzi/| adj.|（感情等）过度流露的；感伤的|Excessively or sentimentally emotional. (Lexico powered by Oxford)|His oozy sentimentality made her uncomfortable. 他过度流露的感伤让她感到不舒服。|Synonyms: mushy, mawkish, saccharine|Antonyms: unsentimental, reserved, stoic|adjective
