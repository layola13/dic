nonaccumulating:/nɒnəˈkjuːmjəleɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非累积的，不累加的
Not accumulating or increasing in quantity or intensity over time; not cumulative.
The medication has a nonaccumulating effect, so you need to take it regularly.  这种药物没有累积效应，所以你需要定期服用。
The interest on this account is nonaccumulating, meaning it's paid out rather than added to the principal.  这个账户的利息是非累积的，意味着它是支付出去而不是添加到本金中。
non-cumulative
