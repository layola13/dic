nectarize:/ˈnɛktəraɪz/| vt.|使充满甜蜜;分泌花蜜;采集花蜜|To fill with nectar; to secrete nectar; to collect nectar from.|Bees nectarize flowers. 蜜蜂从花朵中采集花蜜。|近义词：sweeten, dulcify|反义词: embitter, sour|verb

nectarization:/ˌnɛktərɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.|分泌花蜜;采集花蜜|The process of secreting or collecting nectar.|The nectarization of the flowers attracted many insects. 花蜜的分泌吸引了许多昆虫。|近义词：pollination|反义词: none|noun
