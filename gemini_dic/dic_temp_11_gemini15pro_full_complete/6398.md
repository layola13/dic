amphineura:/æm.fɪˈnjʊə.rə/
noun
石鳖目；石鳖
A class of marine mollusks, also known as chitons, with eight overlapping plates on their dorsal shell.
Amphineura are exclusively marine, mostly found in intertidal zones. 石鳖目动物 ausschließlich 海洋生物，主要分布于潮间带。

The amphineura clung tightly to the rock. 石鳖紧紧地贴在岩石上。

近义词: chiton
反义词: (No direct antonym for a taxonomic group)
词性: n.
