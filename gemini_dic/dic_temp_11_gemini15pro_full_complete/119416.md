microfilms:/ˈmaɪkrəʊfɪlmz/ (British) /ˈmaɪkroʊfɪlmz/ (American)
|n.|缩微胶片；缩微胶卷|A length of film containing microphotographs of documents, newspapers, etc., used for storing or copying them.|The library has a large collection of historical newspapers on microfilm.  图书馆收藏了大量历史报纸的缩微胶卷。|microfiche, microphotographs|photographs, enlargements|noun
