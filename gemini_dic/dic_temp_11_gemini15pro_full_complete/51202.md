destined:/ˈdɛstɪnd/| adj.|注定的，命定的|Intended (by fate or by some controlling power) for a particular purpose, activity, or end.|例句：She was destined for a life of poverty. 她注定要过贫困的生活。

例句：He was destined to become a great leader. 他注定要成为一位伟大的领袖。|近义词：fated, preordained, meant, intended|反义词：random, accidental, chance|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *destinatus*, past participle of *destinare* "to establish, determine, fix, designate," from *de-* "completely" + *stare* "to stand."
