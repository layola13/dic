consumerism:/kənˈsjuːmərɪzəm/| n. |消费主义|The preoccupation of society with the acquisition of consumer goods.|  Consumerism drives the economy in many countries. 消费主义推动了许多国家的经济发展。

Consumerism can lead to environmental problems. 消费主义会导致环境问题。

|近义词：materialism, consumption|反义词:frugality, asceticism|词性:noun
