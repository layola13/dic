hydriodic:/ˌhaɪdriˈɒdɪk/ | adj. | 氢碘酸的，碘化氢的 | Of or relating to hydroiodic acid or its salts; containing iodine and hydrogen. |  Hydrogen iodide is a colorless gas that dissolves in water to form hydriodic acid. 碘化氢是一种无色气体，溶于水形成氢碘酸。

Hydriodic acid is a strong acid. 氢碘酸是一种强酸。| 近义词: iodide (referring to the anion I⁻) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From hydro- + iodic (relating to iodine).  Ultimately from Greek *ὕδωρ (hýdōr, “water”) + ἰώδης (iṓdēs, “violet-colored”).
