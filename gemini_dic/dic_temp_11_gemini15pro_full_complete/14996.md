babe:/beɪb/| n.|宝贝，亲爱的，小孩，美女|A term of endearment, often used romantically or to address a child.  Can also refer to an attractive young woman.|例句：He called his girlfriend "babe." 他叫他的女朋友“宝贝”。|例句：Look at that babe over there! 看看那边那个美女！|近义词：baby, sweetheart, darling, honey, attractive woman|反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on context - perhaps "hag" if referring to an attractive woman, but this is very negative and should be used with extreme caution)|noun
