inferential:/ˌɪnfəˈrɛnʃəl/| adj.|推论的；推理的|Based on inference rather than direct observation.  Derived or capable of being derived by reasoning from premises or evidence.|例句：Inferential statistics are used to draw conclusions about a population based on a sample.  推论统计用于根据样本得出关于总体的结论。|近义词：deductive, reasoned, concluded|反义词：observed, factual, direct|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late 16th century: from late Latin *inferentialis, from inferre ‘bring in, infer’.
