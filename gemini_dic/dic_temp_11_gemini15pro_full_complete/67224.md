exprobrative:/ɪkˈsproʊbrətɪv/| adj.|责备的，斥责的|Expressing strong disapproval or reproach; censorious.|His exprobrative tone made it clear he wasn't happy with the situation. 他责备的语气表明他对情况很不满意。|近义词：reproachful, condemnatory, censorious, vituperative|反义词：laudatory, complimentary, approving|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *exprobrātīvus*, from Latin exprobrāre (“to reproach, upbraid”), from ex- (“out, forth”) + probrum (“reproach, disgrace”).
