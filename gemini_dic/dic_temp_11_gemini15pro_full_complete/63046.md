enricher:/ɪnˈrɪtʃər/ | n. |丰富者；改进者；浓缩器|Something or someone that enriches something else, making it better or more valuable. |The travel experience was an enricher of her life. 旅行经历丰富了她的人生。|Synonyms: improver, enhancer, booster|Antonyms: depleter, impoverisher|noun


enricher:/ɪnˈrɪtʃər/ | n. |浓缩器|A device or substance used to increase the concentration or quality of something. |The uranium enricher is a key component in nuclear power generation. 铀浓缩器是核能发电的关键组成部分。|Synonyms: concentrator, intensifier|Antonyms: diluter|noun
