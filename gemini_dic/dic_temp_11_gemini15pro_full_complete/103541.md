joggled:/ˈdʒɑːɡəld/ | v. | 轻摇；使…轻轻摇动；（使）略微移动 | To move or cause to move with a series of slight, rapid shakes or jerks. | The old woman joggled the baby on her knee. 老妇人把婴儿放在膝盖上轻轻摇晃。 |  The truck joggled along the bumpy road. 卡车在崎岖不平的道路上颠簸前行。 | 近义词: shake, jiggle, jolt | 反义词: steady, stabilize | 词性: 动词

joggled:/ˈdʒɑːɡəld/ | adj. | 摇晃的；不稳定的 | Shaky or unstable. |  The joggled table made it difficult to write. 摇晃的桌子使书写变得困难。 | The picture hung joggled on the wall.  这幅画摇摇晃晃地挂在墙上。| 近义词: shaky, unsteady, wobbly | 反义词: firm, stable, secure | 词性: 形容词
