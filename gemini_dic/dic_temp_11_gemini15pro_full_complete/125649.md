mummifications:/ˌmʌmɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
木乃伊化（过程），防腐处理，干尸化
the process of preserving a body by embalming it or drying it out
The mummifications of ancient Egypt are fascinating. 古埃及的木乃伊化过程令人着迷。
近义词:embalmment, preservation
反义词:decomposition, decay
词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *mummificatio, from mummificare "to make into a mummy," from mumia "mummy" + facere "to make."
