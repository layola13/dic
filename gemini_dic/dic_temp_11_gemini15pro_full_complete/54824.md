dispersibility:/dɪˌspɜːrsəˈbɪləti/ | n. |分散性，分散度|The dispersibility of the powder in water is excellent.  这种粉末在水中的分散性很好。|例句：The high dispersibility of the pigment is crucial for the paint quality.  颜料的高分散性对油漆质量至关重要。

The dispersibility of the nanoparticles affects their catalytic activity. 纳米颗粒的分散性会影响它们的催化活性。

|近义词：distribution, spreadability, diffuseness |反义词：agglomeration, clumping, coagulation |词性: noun

Etymology:  From disperse + -ibility. Disperse comes from Latin *dispersus, past participle of dispergere "to scatter," from dis- "apart" + spargere "to scatter."
