hurac:/ˈhʊəræk/ | n. | 呼拉克舞; 呼拉克; | A lively, traditional Czech folk dance.  A fast Bohemian folk dance performed in couples, with whirling and stamping movements. |  The villagers celebrated the harvest with a lively hurac. 村民们用欢快的呼拉克舞庆祝丰收。 | The band played a fast hurac, and everyone joined in the dance. 乐队演奏了一曲节奏欢快的呼拉克，每个人都加入了舞蹈。 |  folk dance, polka, redowa |  - | noun
