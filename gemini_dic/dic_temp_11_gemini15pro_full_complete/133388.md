nonfacetiously:/ˌnɒnfeɪˈsiːʃəsli/
adverb
并非开玩笑地；认真地
Seriously; not jokingly.
She nonfacetiously remarked that the situation was becoming dire. 她认真地说，情况正在变得糟糕。
近义词:seriously, earnestly, genuinely
反义词:facetiously, jokingly, playfully
词性:副词

Etymology:
From non- + facetious + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not".  "Facetious" comes from the French "facétieux", ultimately from Latin "facētus" meaning "witty, elegant, fine". The suffix "-ly" forms adverbs.
