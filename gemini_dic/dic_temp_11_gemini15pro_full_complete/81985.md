gringa:/ˈɡrɪŋɡə/ | n. |外国女人（尤指白人女子，在拉丁美洲国家使用，有时含贬义）|A foreign woman, especially a white woman, in Latin America, sometimes used disparagingly. |She was called a gringa by the locals. 她被当地人称为外国女人。| The shop owner tried to overcharge the gringa.  店主试图向这个外国女人多收钱。 |近义词：foreigner, outsider|反义词:local, native|词性:名词

词源:
From Spanish *gringa*, feminine of *gringo* (“foreigner”), ultimately a shortening of griego (“Greek”), from Latin Graecus.  源于西班牙语*gringa*，*gringo*（“外国人”）的阴性形式，最终是griego（“希腊人”）的缩写，源自拉丁语Graecus。
