overrestraint:/ˌoʊvərɪˈstreɪnt/
noun
过度约束；过度克制
Excessive restraint or control; too much limiting of one's actions or feelings.
His overrestraint in expressing his emotions led to misunderstandings. 他过度克制自己的情绪表达导致了误解。
synonyms: inhibition, repression, suppression
antonyms: unrestraint, abandon, impulsiveness
n.


overrestraint:/ˌoʊvərɪˈstreɪnt/
verb
过度约束；过度克制
To restrain or control excessively; to limit one's actions or feelings too much.  (Used passively more often)
He was overrestrained by his strict upbringing.  他被严格的教养过度约束了。
synonyms: curb, check, stifle
antonyms: indulge, liberate, unleash
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + restraint.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much."  "Restraint" comes from the Old French word "restreindre," meaning "to restrict" or "hold back."
