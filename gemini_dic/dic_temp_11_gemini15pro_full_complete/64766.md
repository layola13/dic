escutcheon:/ɪˈskʌtʃən/
n.
饰板; 盾形纹章; 铭牌; (尤指水龙头的)饰环
A shield or emblem bearing a coat of arms.  An ornamental or protective plate around a keyhole, light switch, or other fitting.
The knight's escutcheon displayed a fierce lion rampant. 骑士的盾形纹章上绘有一头凶猛的跃立雄狮。
The doorknob's escutcheon was tarnished with age. 门把手的饰板因年代久远而失去光泽。
synonym: shield, crest, coat of arms, plaque
antonym: (No direct antonym, but consider) plain surface, bare wall
noun
