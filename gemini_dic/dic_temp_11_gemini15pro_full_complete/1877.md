acrostical: /əˈkrɒstɪk(ə)l/ /əˈkrɑːstɪk(ə)l/
adjective
离合诗的; 首字母诗的
Relating to or in the form of an acrostic.
An acrostical poem is one in which the first letter of each line spells out a word, name, or phrase.


Example:
He wrote an acrostical poem for her birthday.  他为她的生日写了一首离合诗。

My friend composed an acrostical prayer. 我的朋友创作了一首离合诗体的祈祷文。


Synonym:
abecedarian, alphabetic

Antonym:
(No direct antonym for a descriptive word like acrostical)


Etymology:
From Greek *akrostikhis*  meaning "at the end of the line," from *akron* "end, tip" + *stikhos* "row, line of verse."
