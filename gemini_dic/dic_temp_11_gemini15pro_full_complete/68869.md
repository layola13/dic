fast-trackers:/ˈfæstˌtrækərz/ (US), /ˈfɑːstˌtrækəz/ (UK)| n. |快速晋升者，速成班学员|People who are promoted quickly in their jobs or who complete a course of study in a shorter time than usual.  Those who are on an accelerated path to success or advancement, often through a special program or method.|The company identifies high-potential employees and puts them on a fast track to leadership positions.  公司会识别出高潜力的员工，并将他们快速提升到领导职位。

The fast-trackers in the MBA program will complete their degree in one year instead of two.  这个MBA项目的速成班学员将在一年而不是两年内完成他们的学位。|近义词：high-fliers, go-getters, achievers|反义词：stragglers, laggards|Noun
