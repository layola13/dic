curmudgeonly:/kərˈmʌdʒənli/
|adj.|脾气坏的，爱抱怨的|bad-tempered, especially an old person; complaining and grumpy|例句：He's a curmudgeonly old man who complains about everything. 他是一个脾气暴躁的老人，总是抱怨一切。|例句：Despite her curmudgeonly exterior, she's actually quite kind. 尽管她外表脾气不好，但她实际上很善良。|近义词：bad-tempered, grumpy, cantankerous, irascible, surly|反义词：good-natured, affable, amiable, genial|词性:形容词
词源:
Origin is uncertain.  It may be imitative, perhaps influenced by the now obsolete *curmudgeon*, meaning "miser," of unknown origin.
