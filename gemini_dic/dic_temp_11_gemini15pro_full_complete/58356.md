dua:/duˈɑː/| n.|祈祷，恳求，祝福|A dua is a prayer or invocation in Islam.  Dua is a direct line of communication with God. |Example: He made dua for his family's safety. 他为家人的安全祈祷。

Example:  She recited a dua for good health. 她念了一段祈求健康的经文。|Synonym: prayer, invocation, supplication|Antonym: curse, malediction|noun
