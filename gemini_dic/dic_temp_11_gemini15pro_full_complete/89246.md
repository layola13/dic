hominem:/ˈhɒmɪnɛm/ |adj.| 人身的；针对个人的；| Appealing to personal considerations rather than to logic or reason.  针对个人，而不是逻辑或理性。|  His argument was a blatant ad hominem attack. 他的论点是明目张胆的人身攻击。|近义词: personal, individual |反义词: objective, impartial | 词性: adjective
