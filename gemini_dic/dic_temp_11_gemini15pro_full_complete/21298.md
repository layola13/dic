例句：The government is trying to prevent further bloodsheds. 政府正在努力防止进一步的杀戮。|近义词：carnage, slaughter, massacre|反义词：peace, harmony|词性:名词

词源:  From Old English *blōdsċed, from blōd "blood" + sċed(d) "shedding".  源自古英语 *blōdsċed，由 blōd “血” + sċed(d) “流出” 组成。
