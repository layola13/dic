glut:/ɡlʌt/| n. vt.|过剩，供过于求；充斥，充满|An excessive supply; an overabundance.  To supply or fill to excess.|例句：The market was glutted with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价进口商品。|近义词：surplus, excess, overabundance, plethora|反义词:shortage, scarcity, dearth, lack|noun
glut:/ɡlʌt/| vt.|使…过剩；使…充满|To supply or fill to excess.|例句：The company glutted the market with its new product.  这家公司用新产品使市场饱和。|近义词：flood, saturate, overload, inundate|反义词:deplete, drain, exhaust|verb
