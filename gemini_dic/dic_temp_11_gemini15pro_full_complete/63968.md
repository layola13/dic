episodic:/ˌɛpɪˈsɒdɪk/ (US) /ˌɛpɪˈzɒdɪk/ (UK)| adj.|间歇的；分集的； эпизодический (Russian)|Occurring in episodes or installments; not continuous.  Containing or consisting of a series of loosely connected parts or events.|The pain she experienced was episodic, coming and going without warning.|近义词：intermittent, sporadic, occasional|反义词：continuous, constant, persistent|词性:形容词

episodic:/ˌɛpɪˈsɒdɪk/ (US) /ˌɛpɪˈzɒdɪk/ (UK)| n.|插曲；一段情节|A distinct period or installment in a longer series of events; an episode.|Each episodic of the show featured a different guest star.|近义词：installment, chapter, part|反义词：whole, entirety, totality|词性:名词 (rarely used)
