fratricide:/ˈfræt.rɪ.saɪd/ | n. | 兄弟相残，杀害兄弟 | The act of killing one's brother. |  Cain's murder of Abel is a classic example of fratricide.  该隐谋杀亚伯是兄弟相残的典型例子。|  sibling murder, brother killing |  sororicide (killing one's sister) | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *frātrīcida* "killer of a brother," from *frāter* "brother" + *-cida* "killer," related to *caedere* "to kill."
