nongratuitousness:/ˌnɒnˌɡrætjuˈɪtəsnəs/
n.
无偿性；非无偿性；非免费
The state or quality of not being gratuitous; not being given or done free of charge.
The nongratuitousness of the service implied a certain level of obligation.  这项服务的非无偿性意味着某种程度的义务。
Near Synonyms: costliness, expensiveness,  chargeability
Antonyms: gratuitousness, free, complimentary
noun
