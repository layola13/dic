buzzworthy:/ˈbʌzwɜːrði/ (US) /ˈbʌzwɜːði/ (UK)
adjective
引起热议的，值得关注的
Generating significant buzz or excitement; likely to be talked about.
The new phone release is buzzworthy, with everyone talking about its camera.  这款新手机的发布引起了热议，大家都在谈论它的摄像头。
Her latest single is incredibly buzzworthy, topping the charts in several countries. 她的最新单曲非常火爆，在多个国家排行榜上名列前茅。
synonyms:  talked-about, hyped, trendy, hot, popular
antonyms:  unremarkable, unnoticed, obscure, ignored
