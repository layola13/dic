commissarial:/ˌkɒmɪˈsɛːriəl/ | adj. | 膳食的；补给的；军需的 | relating to the supply of food and other provisions | The commissarial arrangements for the festival were excellent.  节日食品的供应安排非常棒。 | synonyms: catering, provisioning, supply | antonyms:  lacking, deficient | adjective

commissariat:/ˌkɒmɪˈsɛːriət/ | n. | 军需部门；伙食；粮食补给 | a department or organization responsible for supplying food and other provisions, especially for an army | The army's commissariat was struggling to keep up with demand. 陆军的军需部门难以满足需求。| synonyms: supply chain, logistics, provisioning | antonyms: shortage, deficit | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *commissariat*, from *commissaire* (“commissary”), from Late Latin *commissarius* (“commissioner”), from Latin *committere* (“to commit, entrust”), from *com-* (“together”) + *mittere* (“to send”).
