margined:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnd/| adj.|有边的，有页边空白的，加了保证金的|Having a margin or border; provided with a margin.  Relating to buying securities on margin (using borrowed funds).|例句：The margined pages of the antique book were yellowed with age. 这本古书有页边空白的页面已经泛黄。

例句：He made a margined investment in the stock market. 他在股票市场进行了保证金交易。|近义词：bordered, edged, framed (for physical margins), leveraged (for financial margins)|反义词：unmargined, borderless (for physical margins), unleveraged, cash (for financial margins)|词性:adjective
