lavages:/ˈlɑːvɪdʒɪz/
n.
洗涤；灌洗
The act or process of washing or cleansing a body cavity, such as the stomach or bowel.
Gastric lavage was performed to remove the ingested poison.  对病人进行了洗胃以清除摄入的毒物。

近义词:washings, rinses
反义词:contaminations, pollutions
词性:名词


lavage:/ˈlɑːvɑːʒ/
n.
洗涤；灌洗
The fluid used for washing or cleansing.
The lavage fluid was clear. 灌洗液很清澈。


近义词:wash, rinse
反义词:contaminant, pollutant
词性:名词


词源:
From French lavage, from laver "to wash," from Latin lavāre "to wash."
