disparity:/dɪˈspærəti/| n.|不同，差异，不等|a great difference: The growing disparity between rich and poor. 贫富之间日益扩大的差距。|The disparity between the two accounts of the accident was alarming. 关于这起事故的两种说法差异之大，令人担忧。|inequality, difference, gap, imbalance|similarity, equality, uniformity|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *disparitas, from Latin dispar "unequal," from dis- "not, opposite of" + par "equal."
