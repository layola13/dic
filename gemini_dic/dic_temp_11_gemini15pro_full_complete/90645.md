humanizes:/ˈhjuːmənaɪzɪz/
verb
使人性化；赋予人性；使通俗易懂
To humanize something means to make it more humane or to give it human qualities.  It can also mean to make something more understandable or relatable to people.
The company tried to humanize its image by featuring employees in its advertising.  这家公司试图通过在广告中突出员工来使其形象更加人性化。
Synonyms: personalize, individualize, civilize
Antonyms: dehumanize, objectify
词性:动词
