overinstruct:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈstrʌkt/ |v.|过度指导，过分指导|To instruct someone excessively, often to the point of hindering their learning or performance.  It implies giving too much information, too much direction, or too much feedback, which can lead to confusion, anxiety, and reduced effectiveness.|The coach overinstructed the team, leading to their confusion on the field. 教练对球队进行了过度指导，导致他们在场上不知所措。

The teacher overinstructed the students, stifling their creativity. 老师对学生进行了过度指导，扼杀了他们的创造力。|近义词:overcoach, micromanage|反义词:underinstruct, neglect|词性:动词
