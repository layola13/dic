max:/mæks/| n. adj. v.|最大值，最大限度的；最大的；达到最大值|The car reached its max speed on the highway. 汽车在高速公路上达到了最高速度。|近义词：maximum, utmost, greatest, highest|反义词：min, minimum, least, lowest|词性:名词,形容词,动词

ETYMOLOGY->
Shortened form of *maximum*, from Latin *maximum* (neuter of *maximus* "greatest").
