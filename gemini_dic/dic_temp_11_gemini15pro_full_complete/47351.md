de-stress:/ˌdiːˈstrɛs/
verb
减压，缓解压力
To relieve stress or tension; to cause someone to feel less stressed.
Taking a vacation can de-stress you after a long period of hard work.  休假可以在长时间的辛苦工作后让你减压。
Yoga and meditation can help de-stress.  瑜伽和冥想可以帮助减压。
Synonyms: relax, unwind, decompress
Antonyms: stress, tense, pressure
词性:动词
