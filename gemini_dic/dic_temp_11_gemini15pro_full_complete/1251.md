accomptant:/əˈkaʊntənt/ | n. |会计师，会计人员|A person whose job is to keep or inspect financial accounts. |例句：The accomptant prepared the company's financial statements. 会计师准备了公司的财务报表。

例句：She works as an accomptant for a large multinational corporation.  她在一家大型跨国公司担任会计师。|近义词：accountant, bookkeeper |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French acomptant, present participle of acompter (“to count, reckon”), from Latin ad (“to”) + computare (“to count”).  The spelling "accomptant" is an older, now less common variant of "accountant."
