ironings:/ˈaɪərnɪŋz/
n.
熨烫（衣物）；熨好的衣物
the process of smoothing clothes with a heated iron; clothes that have been ironed
例句：I have a pile of ironings to do. 我有一堆衣服要熨。

例句：She spent the morning doing the ironings. 她早上都在熨衣服。

近义词：pressing
反义词：wrinkling, creasing
词性：名词
