hydatiform:/ˌhaɪdəˈtɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 葡萄状的，水泡状的 | Resembling a hydatid cyst; cystic; vesicular.  Relating to or characterized by a hydatidiform mole. |  The doctor identified a hydatiform mole during the ultrasound. 医生在超声波检查中发现了葡萄胎。| Synonym: cystic, vesicular | Antonym: solid, compact | adjective

hydatiform mole:/ˌhaɪdəˈtɪfɔːrm moʊl/ | n. | 葡萄胎 |  A rare mass or growth that forms inside the uterus at the beginning of a pregnancy.  It is a gestational trophoblastic disease. | The patient unfortunately experienced a hydatiform mole, requiring treatment.  这位病人不幸患上了葡萄胎，需要治疗。 | Synonym: molar pregnancy | Antonym: normal pregnancy | noun
