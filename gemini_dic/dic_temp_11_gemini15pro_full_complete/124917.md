mudless:/ˈmʌdləs/
adjective
无泥的；没有泥浆的
Free from mud; not muddy.
The new path was mudless, even after the rain.  雨后，这条新路依然没有泥泞。
近义词:clean, unsoiled, immaculate
反义词:muddy, miry, sludgy
词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From mud + -less (suffix meaning "without").
