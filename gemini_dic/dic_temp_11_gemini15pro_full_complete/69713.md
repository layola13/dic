fenowed:/fɛˈnoʊd/ | adj. | 沼泽的，多沼泽的 | Characterized by fens or bogs; marshy. | The fenowed landscape provided a unique habitat for various bird species. 这片沼泽地貌为各种鸟类提供了独特的栖息地。|  The expedition struggled through the fenowed terrain. 探险队艰难地穿过沼泽地带。|  marshy, boggy, swampy | dry, arid | adjective

Etymology:  From "fen" (low, marshy land) + "-owed" (a suffix meaning "characterized by").  "Fen" ultimately comes from Old English "fenn," related to Dutch "veen" and German "Fenn." "-owed" is a variant of "-ed," a common suffix used to form adjectives.
