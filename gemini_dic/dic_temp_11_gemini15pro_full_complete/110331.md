lightest:/ˈlaɪtɪst/| adj.|最轻的|Superlative form of light; having the least weight or density.|This feather is the lightest of all.|近义词：least heavy, airiest, slightest|反义词:heaviest, densest|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *lightest, from Old English lēohtest, superlative degree of lēoht (“light”).
