bulletproof:/ˈbʊlɪtpruːf/| adj.|防弹的，刀枪不入的|Impenetrable by bullets; able to withstand bullets.  Made to resist bullets.  Also used figuratively to mean invulnerable to criticism or other attacks.|The police officers wore bulletproof vests. 警察穿着防弹背心。|The company's reputation is bulletproof.  这家公司的声誉是无可指责的。|Synonyms: impenetrable, impervious, invulnerable|Antonyms: vulnerable, unprotected|adjective
