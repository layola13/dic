The arefaction process is crucial for preserving certain foods. 干燥过程对于保存某些食物至关重要。|近义词：drying, desiccation, dehydration |反义词：humidification, wetting |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *arefactus*, past participle of *arefacere* ("to make dry"), from *are-* (variant of *ārēre* "to be dry") + *facere* ("to make").
