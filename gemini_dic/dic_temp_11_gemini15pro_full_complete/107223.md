laidly:/ˈleɪdli/| adj.|丑陋的；令人厌恶的；可憎的|Loathsome; disgusting; repulsive.  Primarily used in Scottish and older English texts.  It describes something or someone as physically unattractive, often with connotations of being morally repugnant or even supernatural.|例句：The laidly worm devoured the knight.  丑陋的蠕虫吞噬了骑士。

例句：She transformed from a laidly hag into a beautiful princess. 她从一个丑陋的老巫婆变成了美丽的公主。|近义词：hideous, ugly, gruesome, repulsive, loathsome|反义词：beautiful, lovely, attractive, pleasant|词性:adjective
