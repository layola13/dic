decorous: /ˈdɛkərəs/ (US), /dɪˈkɔːrəs/ (UK) | adj. | 端庄的，得体的，有礼貌的 | Behaving politely and in a controlled way, suitable for a formal occasion.  Characterized by propriety and dignity; proper. |  Her decorous behavior at the funeral was admired by all.  她在葬礼上端庄的举止受到了所有人的赞赏。|  Synonyms: proper, appropriate, seemly, polite, courteous, respectful, dignified | Antonyms:  indecorous, improper, unseemly, impolite, rude, disrespectful, undignified | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin decōrus (“becoming, fitting, proper”), from décor (“grace, ornament”).
