bicameralists:/baɪˈkæmərəlɪsts/
n.
两院制拥护者；支持两院制的人
Supporters or advocates of a bicameral legislature (a legislature with two chambers or houses).
The bicameralists argued that two houses would provide checks and balances, preventing any one group from gaining too much power. 两院制拥护者认为，两院制可以提供制衡，防止任何一个团体获得过多的权力。

Bicameralists believe that a two-chamber system leads to more carefully considered legislation. 两院制拥护者认为，两院制会导致立法更加谨慎。

近义词:
supporters of bicameralism, advocates of a two-chamber system
反义词:
unicameralists
