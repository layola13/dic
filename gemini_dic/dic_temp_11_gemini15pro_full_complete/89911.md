horror-struck:/ˈhɒrə(r)ˌstrʌk/ | /ˈhɔːrərˌstrʌk/ | adj. | 震惊的，惊骇的 | Filled with horror; terrified.  Overcome with a feeling of shock and fear. |  She was horror-struck by the news of the accident. 她被事故的消息吓得目瞪口呆。| aghast, appalled, terrified, shocked |  calm, composed, unfazed | adjective
