laborers:/ˈleɪbərərz/ (US), /ˈleɪbərəz/ (UK)| n. |劳动者，工人|People who do manual labor, especially hard physical work.|例句：The laborers worked tirelessly in the fields. 工人们不知疲倦地在田里劳作。

例句：The construction project required hundreds of skilled laborers.  这个建筑项目需要数百名熟练工人。

近义词：workers, employees, workmen, hands|反义词：managers, employers, supervisors|词性：名词
