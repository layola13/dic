mangonize: /ˈmæŋɡənaɪz/ | v. | 芒果化，芒果处理 | To treat or process with mango; to impart the flavor or aroma of mango to something. Also, figuratively, to make something sweet and tropical, or to infuse it with a sense of exoticism. | The chef decided to mangonize the chicken by marinating it in mango puree.  这位厨师决定用芒果泥腌制鸡肉，使其具有芒果味。

The travel agent tried to mangonize the dreary English town, describing it as a hidden tropical paradise. 旅行社试图将沉闷的英国小镇芒果化，将其描述为一个隐藏的热带天堂。 | 近义词: tropicalize, exoticize | 反义词: None | 词性: verb

词源:  From "mango" + "-ize," a suffix used to form verbs meaning "to make or become like," "to treat with," or "to subject to the action of."
