goethite:/ˈɡʊθaɪt/| n. |针铁矿，褐铁矿|A common iron oxyhydroxide mineral, FeO(OH), occurring in radiating, acicular, or fibrous masses, often as brown or yellowish-brown coatings or concretions.|Goethite is an important ore of iron. 针铁矿是重要的铁矿石。|The goethite crystals were found embedded in the quartz matrix.  针铁矿晶体被发现嵌在石英基质中。|近义词：limonite (褐铁矿 - a general term for hydrated iron oxides), lepidocrocite (纤铁矿 - another iron oxyhydroxide)|反义词: None (No direct antonym for a mineral name)|词性:noun
