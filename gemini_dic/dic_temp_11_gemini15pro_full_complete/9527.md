antisegregation:/ˌæntiˌsɛɡrɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | adj. | 反种族隔离的 | Opposing or working against segregation, especially racial segregation. |  The antisegregation movement gained momentum in the 1960s.  反种族隔离运动在20世纪60年代蓬勃发展。

The antisegregation laws were a major victory for civil rights. 反种族隔离法是民权的重大胜利。 | 近义词：integrationist, desegregationist | 反义词：segregationist, racist | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
segregation (noun): the action or state of setting someone or something apart from other people or things or being set apart. (from Latin segregare 'to set apart')
