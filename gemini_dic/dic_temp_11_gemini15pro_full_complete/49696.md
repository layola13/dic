demob:/diːˈməʊb/ /diˈmoʊb/| n. |复员，退伍|The act of demobilizing or the state of being demobilized from military service.  Discharged servicemen and women collectively.|例句：Thousands of soldiers were demobbed after the war.  战争结束后，成千上万的士兵复员了。

例句：The demob were given train tickets home. 复员军人拿到了回家的火车票。|近义词：discharge, muster out|反义词：enlist, mobilize|词性：名词

demob:/diːˈməʊb/ /diˈmoʊb/| v. |复员，退伍|To demobilize (someone) from military service.|例句：He was demobbed in 1945. 他于1945年复员。

例句：The government plans to demob thousands of soldiers next year. 政府计划明年复员数千名士兵。|近义词：discharge, muster out|反义词：enlist, mobilize|词性：动词
