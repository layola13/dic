improper-fraction:/ɪmˈprɑːpər ˈfrækʃən/| n.|假分数|A fraction where the numerator is greater than or equal to the denominator.|Example: 11/7 is an improper fraction because 11 is greater than 7.|近义词: top-heavy fraction|反义词: proper fraction|词性:noun

improper:/ɪmˈprɑːpər/| adj.|不合适的，不正确的|Not suitable or appropriate; not correct.|Example: It is improper to talk loudly in a library. 在图书馆大声喧哗是不合适的。|近义词: inappropriate, unsuitable, incorrect|反义词: proper, appropriate, correct|词性:adjective

fraction:/ˈfrækʃən/| n.|分数，小部分|A numerical quantity that is not a whole number (e.g., 1/2, 0.5). A small or tiny part, amount, or proportion of something.|Example: Only a fraction of the students passed the exam. 只有少数学生通过了考试。|近义词: portion, part, segment|反义词: whole, entirety|词性:noun
