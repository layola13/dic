conservator: /kənˈsɜːrvətər/ (UK) /kənˈsɜːrvətɔːr/ (US) | n. |管理员，保护者，修复者| A person responsible for the preservation and restoration of works of art, buildings, or other items of cultural or environmental significance.  |  The museum hired a conservator to restore the ancient pottery.  博物馆聘请了一位管理员来修复古代陶器。 | synonyms: curator, guardian, restorer, preserver | antonyms: destroyer, vandal | noun


conservatorial: /kənˌsɜːrvəˈtɔːrɪəl/ (UK) /kənˌsɜːrvəˈtɔːriəl/ (US) | adj. |保护的，保存的，有关管理员的 | Relating to or characteristic of a conservator or their work. | The conservatorial process involved cleaning and stabilizing the painting.  保护过程包括清洁和稳定绘画。| synonyms: preservative, protective, restorative | antonyms: destructive, damaging | adjective
