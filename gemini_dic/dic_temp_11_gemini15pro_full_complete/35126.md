clouding:/ˈklaʊdɪŋ/ | n. | 混浊，模糊；阴暗 | The clouding of the lens in his eye impaired his vision. 他眼睛里晶状体的混浊损害了他的视力。 | 近义词：obscuring, blurring, dimming | 反义词：clearing, brightening | 词性：名词

Etymology: From cloud + -ing, from Old English clūd "mass of rock, hill," later "cloud," of uncertain origin.  The sense of "making cloudy" arose from the noun.
