caucusing:/ˈkɔːkəsɪŋ/
n.|核心会议，政党会议|A meeting of supporters or members of a specific political party or movement.
|The Democrats were caucusing to choose their candidate. 民主党人正在举行核心会议，以选择他们的候选人。
|The senators spent hours caucusing on the proposed legislation. 参议员们花了几个小时开核心会议讨论拟议的立法。
|meeting, conference, assembly
