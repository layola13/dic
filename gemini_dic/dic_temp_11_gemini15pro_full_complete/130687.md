nonaffiliations:/ˌnɒnəˌfɪliˈeɪʃənz/ | n. | 无关联；非附属关系 |  The state of not being affiliated with a particular group, organization, or cause.  Absence of affiliation; lack of formal connection or association. |  Many voters chose nonaffiliations in the recent election, preferring not to align with any political party. 许多选民在最近的选举中选择无党派，不愿与任何政党结盟。 | synonyms: independence, neutrality, nonalignment | antonyms: affiliation, membership, association | noun
