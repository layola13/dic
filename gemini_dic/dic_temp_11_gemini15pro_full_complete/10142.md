apologised: /əˈpɒlədʒaɪzd/ (UK), /əˈpɑːlədʒaɪzd/ (US)
verb
道歉，认错
expressed regret for something that one has done wrong.
例句：I apologised for being late. 我为迟到道歉。
例句：She apologised profusely for the misunderstanding.  她为这个误会深表歉意。
近义词：said sorry, expressed regret, made amends
反义词：blamed, accused
词性：verb (past tense of apologise)


apologize: /əˈpɒlədʒaɪz/ (UK), /əˈpɑːlədʒaɪz/ (US)
verb
道歉，认错
express regret for something that one has done wrong.
例句：I apologize for being late. 我为迟到道歉。
例句：He should apologize for his rude behavior. 他应该为他的粗鲁行为道歉。
近义词：say sorry, express regret, make amends
反义词：blame, accuse
词性：verb

词源：From Old French *apologiser, from Late Latin apologizāre "to make a defense," from Greek apologia "speech in defense," from apo- "away from" + logos "speech, word, reason."
