afterpiece:/ˈɑːftərˌpiːs/ | n. | 余兴节目；后续；收场白 | A short play, entertainment, or musical piece presented after the main performance. |  The afterpiece was a hilarious farce that left the audience in stitches.  余兴节目是一出滑稽的闹剧，让观众笑得前仰后合。| synonyms:  coda, finale, epilogue, postlude | antonyms:  prelude, prologue, preface | noun

afterpiece:/ˈɑːftərˌpiːs/ | n. | 后续；结果；后果| Something that follows or comes after a main event or occurrence, often as a consequence. | The economic downturn was an unfortunate afterpiece to the housing bubble. 经济衰退是房地产泡沫破灭后不幸的结果。 | synonyms:  sequel, consequence, result, aftermath | antonyms:  precursor, antecedent, cause | noun
