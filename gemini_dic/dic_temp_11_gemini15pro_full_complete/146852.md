overindulged: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒd/ | adj. | 放纵过度的，沉溺的 | Having indulged to excess.  |  He felt sick and sluggish after an overindulged weekend. 他放纵过度地过了一个周末后感到恶心和迟钝。| 近义词: pampered, spoiled, excessive | 反义词: moderate, temperate, abstemious | 词性: 形容词

overindulge: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒ/ | v. | 放纵，沉溺 | To allow oneself or someone else to have too much of something enjoyable, especially food or drink. | She overindulged in chocolate and felt sick. 她吃太多巧克力，感到恶心了。 | 近义词: pamper, spoil, coddle | 反义词: deprive, restrict, moderate | 词性: 动词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "indulge" (from Latin "indulgere" meaning "to be kind or tender to, yield to").
