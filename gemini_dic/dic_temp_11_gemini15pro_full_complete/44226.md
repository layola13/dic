crockett:/ˈkrɑːkɪt/| n.|克罗克特（姓氏或地名）; 一种边境英雄形象|A surname, or a place name. It can also refer to a frontiersman, especially of the American Old West, often idealized for their rugged individualism and skill in frontier life.  Specifically associated with Davy Crockett (1786-1836), a frontiersman, soldier, and politician.|Davy Crockett was a famous frontiersman. 戴维·克罗克特是一位著名的拓荒者。|The Crockett family settled in Tennessee. 克罗克特一家在田纳西州定居。|synonyms: frontiersman, pioneer, woodsman|antonyms: city-dweller, urbanite|noun
