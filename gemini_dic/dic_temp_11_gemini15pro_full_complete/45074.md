cubicular:/kjuːˈbɪkjʊlər/| adj.|卧室的；私人的；适合卧室的|Of, relating to, or resembling a cubicle, especially a small sleeping chamber.  Suitable for a bedroom; private.|例句：She preferred the cubicular comforts of her own room. 她更喜欢自己房间卧室般的舒适。

例句：He led a rather cubicular existence, rarely venturing out. 他过着相当私密的生活，很少外出。|近义词：private, personal, intimate, chamber, bedroom|反义词:public, open, shared|词性:adjective
Etymology->
From Latin *cubiculāris*, from *cubiculum* ("bedroom, sleeping chamber"), from *cubō* ("I lie down").
