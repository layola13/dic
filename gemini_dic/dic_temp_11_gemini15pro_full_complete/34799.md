climb-the-walls:/klaɪm ðə wɔːlz/| idiom |非常焦虑; 极其烦躁; 坐立不安|To be extremely anxious, agitated, or restless.|例句：The kids were climbing the walls by the time the babysitter arrived.  保姆到的时候，孩子们已经急得像热锅上的蚂蚁了。

例句：I've been stuck in this house for days, I'm climbing the walls! 我被困在这房子里好几天了，都快疯了！|近义词：go crazy, go stir-crazy, go bananas, be restless, be agitated|反义词：be calm, be relaxed, be patient|词性: idiom
