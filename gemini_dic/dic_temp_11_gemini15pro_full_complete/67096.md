explorations:/ˌɛkspləˈreɪʃənz/| n. |探索，探险，勘探|The act or process of exploring or investigating something thoroughly.  The explorations of space have yielded much new information about the universe. |太空探索已经产生了关于宇宙的很多新信息。|近义词：investigations, researches, discoveries, surveys, inquiries|反义词:inaction, idleness|词性:名词

词源: From Late Middle English exploracioun, from Late Latin explorātiō, from Latin explorāre (“to explore”).
