oversecurities: /ˌoʊvər səˈkjʊrətiz/
n. |过度担保，超额担保|The act of providing excessive collateral or security for a loan or other financial obligation, often beyond what is reasonably required.  This can tie up assets that could be used more productively elsewhere. | The company's oversecurities tied up valuable capital.  这家公司的过度担保占用了宝贵的资金。 | overcollateralization, excess security | under-collateralization | noun
