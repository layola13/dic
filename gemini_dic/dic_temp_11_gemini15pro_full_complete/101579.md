iroquois:/ˈɪrəkwɔɪ/ | n. | 易洛魁人；易洛魁语 | A member of a confederacy of North American Indian peoples speaking Iroquoian languages.  Originally inhabiting the region of present-day New York state and adjacent areas, they now live primarily in New York and Oklahoma, with smaller groups in Wisconsin, Ontario, and Quebec.  The Iroquoian language family. | The Iroquois Confederacy was a powerful force in North America.  易洛魁联盟是北美一股强大的力量。

The Iroquois have a rich cultural heritage.  易洛魁人拥有丰富的文化遗产。| 近义词: Haudenosaunee (official name of the Iroquois Confederacy)| 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a people group) | 词性: noun
