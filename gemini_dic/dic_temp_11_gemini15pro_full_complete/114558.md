malati:/ˈmɑːlətiː/ /məˈlɑːtiː/
n.
茉莉花 (尤指印度的一种白色茉莉花)
A fragrant white jasmine flower, especially Jasminum grandiflorum, often used in perfumes and religious ceremonies, particularly in India.
例句：
The scent of malati filled the air.
空气中弥漫着茉莉花的香味。

Malati flowers are often used to make garlands.
茉莉花经常被用来制作花环。


近义词：jasmine,  Arabian jasmine
反义词:  (无直接反义词，可以考虑一些气味难闻的花)
词性: noun
