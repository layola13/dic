overfed:/ˌoʊvərˈfɛd/| adj.|吃得过饱的，喂得过多的|Having eaten too much; given too much food.|The overfed cat refused to eat another bite. 这只吃得过饱的猫拒绝再吃一口了。|近义词：stuffed, gorged, satiated|反义词:underfed, starved|adjective

overfed:/ˌoʊvərˈfɛd/| v. (past tense and past participle of overfeed)|过量喂食|To give too much food to.|I think I overfed the fish. 我想我给鱼喂太多了。|近义词：gorge, stuff, satiate|反义词:starve, deprive|verb
