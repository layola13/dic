monistic:/məˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 一元论的 | Characterized by the doctrine of monism; asserting that there is only one ultimate kind of substance or principle in reality. |  Monistic philosophies often see the universe as a unified whole. 一元论哲学通常将宇宙视为一个统一的整体。

Monistic beliefs are common in some Eastern religions. 一元论信仰在一些东方宗教中很常见。| 近义词: unitary, singular | 反义词: dualistic, pluralistic | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Ancient Greek μόνος (mónos, “alone, only”) + -istic.
