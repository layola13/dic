demothing:/diːˈməʊθɪŋ/ | v. | 除蛾；去除（衣物等）的蛾子 |  To rid something of moths or moth larvae, especially clothes or other fabrics. |  She demothballed her winter coat and demothed it before putting it away. 她从樟脑球中取出她的冬大衣，在收起来之前先除掉了蛾子。| 近义词: mothproof | 反义词: infest (with moths) | 词性: 动词

词源:  "de-" (去除) + "moth" (蛾) + "-ing" (动名词/现在分词后缀)
