coachful /ˈkoʊtʃfəl/
adjective
充满指导的；富于指导性的
Full of coaching or guidance; providing a lot of coaching.  This word is not commonly used and may sound awkward or informal.
He gave a coachful lecture to the aspiring athletes. 他给有抱负的运动员们做了一场充满指导性的讲座。
近义词: instructive, mentoring, supportive, guiding
反义词: unhelpful, unsupportive, neglecting
