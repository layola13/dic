musts:/mʌsts/
| n.| 必需做的事；不可少的事物 | Essential things or duties; unavoidable obligations. |  These are the musts for any visitor to Paris. 这些是所有去巴黎的游客的必游之地。 | synonyms: essentials, necessities, requirements | antonyms: optionals, nonessentials | noun

musts:/mʌsts/
| v. |  (must 的第三人称单数现在式，很少使用，通常用 has to 或 have to 代替) 必须 | (Third-person singular simple present indicative form of must, rarely used, usually replaced by has to or have to)  Expressing obligation or necessity. | He musts leave now. (Rarely used, better: He has to leave now.) 他现在必须离开。| synonyms: has to, have to, is obliged to | antonyms: doesn't have to, isn't obliged to | verb

PHRASE->
There are no common phrases using "musts" as a plural noun.  The singular "must" is used in phrases.


PHRASE-> (using "must")
must-have = 必备品
must-see = 必看之物
must-do = 必做之事
must-read = 必读书籍
must-try = 必尝美食


注意事项:

"musts" 作为名词的复数形式使用频率不高，更多时候使用 "essentials", "necessities" 等同义词。 作为动词，几乎不用。
