gnosticism:/ˈnɒstɪsɪzəm/ (US) /ˈnɒstɪsɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |诺斯替主义，灵知主义|A religious movement or perspective prominent in the second century CE, characterized by the belief that matter is evil and that freedom comes from esoteric knowledge of the divine spark within oneself, leading to salvation.|Many scholars believe that gnosticism influenced early Christianity. 许多学者认为诺斯替主义影响了早期基督教。|近义词：esotericism, mysticism|反义词：materialism, orthodoxy|词性:noun

gnostical:/ˈnɒstɪkəl/ (US) /ˈnɒstɪkəl/ (UK)| adj. |诺斯替的，灵知的| Relating to or characteristic of gnosticism.|  The Nag Hammadi library contains several gnostical texts.  纳格·哈玛迪文库包含几部诺斯替文献。|近义词：esoteric, mystical|反义词：orthodox, conventional|词性:adjective
