bamboozler:/bæmˈbuːzlər/
n.|欺骗者，骗子|A person who deceives or tricks someone.|The bamboozler tricked the tourists out of their money. 那个骗子骗走了游客们的钱。|
近义词:swindler, cheat, con artist, fraudster, trickster|
反义词:honest person, dupe|
词性:noun


bamboozle:/bæmˈbuːzl/
vt.|欺骗，迷惑|To deceive or confuse someone.|The magician bamboozled the audience with his tricks. 魔术师用他的戏法迷惑了观众。|
近义词:deceive, trick, mislead, hoodwink, confuse, perplex|
反义词:clarify, enlighten, be honest|
词性:verb

词源:
Origin uncertain, possibly from dialectal English *bam*, a hoax, and *foozle*, to bungle.
