low-lying:/ˈloʊˌlaɪɪŋ/ /ˈləʊˌlaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 低洼的，地势低的 | Situated at a low elevation; likely to be flooded. | Example 1: Low-lying areas are prone to flooding. 低洼地区容易发生洪水。

Example 2: The low-lying coastal plains were evacuated before the hurricane.  飓风来临前，低洼的沿海平原上的居民被疏散了。| 近义词:low, lowland, bottom | 反义词: high, elevated, highland | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From 'low' (Old English 'lagu' - not high) + 'lying' (present participle of 'lie' - Old English 'licgan' - to recline).  The combination simply describes something that lies at a low level.
