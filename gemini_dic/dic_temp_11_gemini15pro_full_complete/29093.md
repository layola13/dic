caryophyllaceous:/ˌkæriəfɪˈleɪʃəs/
adjective
石竹科的；似石竹科植物的
Relating to or resembling plants of the pink family (Caryophyllaceae), typically having opposite leaves, swollen nodes, and flowers with five petals.
Carnations and baby's breath are caryophyllaceous flowers.  康乃馨和满天星都是石竹科植物。
The botanist identified the small white flower as a caryophyllaceous species.  植物学家将这朵小白花鉴定为石竹科植物。
Synonym:  pink-like
Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-caryophyllaceous)
词性: 形容词
