habitations:/ˌhæbɪˈteɪʃənz/
noun
住处，住所
places where people live; dwellings.
Many of the older habitations lacked basic amenities. 许多老房子缺乏基本设施。
synonyms: dwellings, residences, homes, abodes, domiciles
antonyms: wilderness, wasteland

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English habitacioun, from Old French habitacion, from Latin habitātiō (“a dwelling”), from habitō (“I dwell, inhabit”), frequentative of habeō (“I have, hold, possess”).
