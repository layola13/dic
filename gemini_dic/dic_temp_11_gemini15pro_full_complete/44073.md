crinkly:/ˈkrɪŋkli/ | adj. |皱巴巴的，有皱纹的|Having many small creases or wrinkles.  Characterized by a crinkling sound.|例句：The old woman's face was crinkly with age. 老妇人的脸因年老而皱巴巴的。

例句：The crinkly cellophane wrapping made a pleasant sound. 皱巴巴的玻璃纸包装发出悦耳的声音。|近义词：wrinkled, creased, crumpled|反义词：smooth, flat, ironed|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From crinkle + -y.  Crinkle itself likely imitative of the sound. 源自crinkle + -y。crinkle本身很可能模仿声音。
