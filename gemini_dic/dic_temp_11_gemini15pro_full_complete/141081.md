onomatopoeic:/ˌɒnəˌmætəˈpiːɪk/ | adj. | 拟声的 | Formed from a word that imitates the sound it represents. |  The word "buzz" is onomatopoeic, as it sounds like the noise a bee makes.  嗡嗡声这个词是拟声词，因为它听起来像蜜蜂发出的噪音。| synonyms: imitative, mimetic | antonyms: N/A | adjective
