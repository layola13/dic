advantaged:/ədˈvæntɪdʒd/
|adj.|享有优势的，处于有利地位的|Having an advantage or advantages, especially socially or economically.|Children from advantaged backgrounds are more likely to succeed in school. 享有优势背景的孩子更有可能在学校取得成功。|近义词：privileged, affluent, wealthy, fortunate, well-off|反义词：disadvantaged, underprivileged, deprived, poor|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From the verb "advantage" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Advantage" comes from Old French "avantage"  (now "avantage"),  ultimately from Late Latin "abante" "from before," from Latin "ab" (from) + "ante" (before).
