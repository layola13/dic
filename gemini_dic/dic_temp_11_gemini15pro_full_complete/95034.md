inaccuratenesses: /ɪnˈækjərətnəsɪz/ | n. | 不准确性；错误 |  The report was riddled with inaccuracies.  This plural noun refers to multiple instances of being inaccurate or incorrect, often in data, information, or measurements.  |  例句：The inaccuracies in the report led to a misunderstanding. 报告中的不准确之处导致了误解。

例句：The numerous inaccuracies in the data rendered the study useless. 数据中大量的错误使得这项研究毫无用处。

| 近义词：errors, mistakes, fallacies, inaccuracies | 反义词：accuracies, precision, correctness | 词性：名词
