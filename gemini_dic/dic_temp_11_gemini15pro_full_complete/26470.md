byzantine:/bɪˈzæn.taɪn/ /baɪˈzæn.taɪn/
adjective
拜占庭式的；错综复杂的
Characterized by intrigue, complexity, and deviousness, often in reference to political or administrative affairs.  Originating from the Byzantine Empire, known for its complex political and administrative systems.
例句：The byzantine plot of the novel was difficult to follow. 这部小说的错综复杂的故事情节很难理解。

例句：The company's byzantine organizational structure made it nearly impossible to get anything done.  这家公司错综复杂的组织结构使得几乎不可能完成任何事情。


近义词：convoluted, intricate, complex, labyrinthine
反义词：simple, straightforward, clear


词性: adjective
