enabler:/ɪˈneɪblər/| n.|使能者，促成者；赋能者|Someone or something that makes something possible.  Something that helps someone to continue in a bad habit, especially drinking alcohol or taking drugs. |Her wealth was an enabler, allowing her to pursue her passion projects. 她的财富让她能够追求她热爱的项目。 He considered her family an enabler of her drug addiction. 他认为她的家人是她吸毒成瘾的推手。|近义词：facilitator, supporter, catalyst|反义词：obstacle, hindrance, impediment|词性: noun
