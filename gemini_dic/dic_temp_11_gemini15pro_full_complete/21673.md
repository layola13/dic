blustrous:/ˈblʌstrəs/| adj.|喧闹的；狂暴的；大声嚷嚷的|Characterized by loud, noisy, and boisterous behavior, often accompanied by strong winds or storms.  It can also describe someone who is loud and aggressive in their speech or manner.|The blustrous wind howled through the trees. 喧闹的风在树林中呼啸。|The politician delivered a blustrous speech, full of empty promises. 这位政客发表了一通喧闹的演讲，充满了空洞的承诺。|近义词：noisy, boisterous, loud, clamorous, tumultuous, stormy, windy|反义词：quiet, calm, peaceful, serene, tranquil|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *blusterous, from blusteren + -ous.  Ultimately from Old Norse *blystra (to blow, puff).
