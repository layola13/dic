mismeasurements:/ˌmɪsˈmɛʒərmənts/
| n. |错误测量，测量不准|An act or instance of measuring incorrectly.  The dressmaker had made some mismeasurements and the dress was too tight.|例句：The mismeasurements in the construction project led to significant delays and cost overruns.  施工项目中的测量错误导致了严重的延误和成本超支。

例句：Due to several mismeasurements, the furniture did not fit properly in the room. 由于几次测量错误，家具在房间里放不下。|近义词：inaccurate measurements, incorrect measurements, faulty measurements|反义词:accurate measurements, precise measurements|词性:名词
