expurgation:/ˌekspərˈɡeɪʃn/ | n. |净化；删改；修订| the process of removing something considered harmful, offensive, or unsuitable from a book, film, etc. |例句：The expurgation of offensive content from the textbook sparked controversy. 从教科书中删除冒犯性内容引发了争议。

例句：The film underwent expurgation before its release to a wider audience. 这部电影在向更广泛的观众发行之前经过了删改。|近义词：censorship, bowdlerization, purging, cleansing |反义词:inclusion, addition, insertion |词性:noun
