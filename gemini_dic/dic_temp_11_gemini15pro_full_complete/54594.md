dismantlers:/dɪsˈmæntlərz/
n.
拆卸工；拆除者
People who dismantle things, such as machines, structures, or organizations.
The dismantlers carefully took apart the old factory piece by piece.  拆卸工小心地将旧工厂一件一件地拆卸开来。
The dismantlers began their work on the decommissioned power plant.  拆卸工开始拆除已停用的发电厂。
disassemblers, deconstructors
assemblers, constructors
noun
