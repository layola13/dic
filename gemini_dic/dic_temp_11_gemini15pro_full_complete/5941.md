amblygon:/ˈæmblɪɡɒn/ (UK) /ˈæmblɪɡɑːn/ (US)| n. |钝角, 优角 (大于90度小于180度的角)|An angle greater than 90 degrees and less than 180 degrees.|Example: An obtuse triangle contains one amblygon. 一个钝角三角形包含一个优角。|Example: The architect used amblygons in the design of the building to create a unique aesthetic. 建筑师在建筑设计中使用了优角，以创造独特的审美。|近义词:obtuse angle|反义词:acute angle|词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek "amblu-" (blunt, obtuse) + "gonia" (angle).
