hillstone:/ˈhɪlˌstoʊn/| n. |山石，页岩|A type of hard, fine-grained sedimentary rock that splits easily into thin layers. It is often used in building and paving.|The old house was built with hillstone walls. 这座老房子是用山石墙建造的。|The path was paved with hillstone. 这条小路是用页岩铺成的。|Synonyms: flagstone, shale, slate|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a type of rock)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hilston, from Old English *hyllstān, from hyll (“hill”) + stān (“stone”).  Literally "hill stone".
