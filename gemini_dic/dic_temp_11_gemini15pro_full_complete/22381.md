booed:/buːd/
| v.|嘘；发出嘘声|To express disapproval or contempt by shouting "boo."|The crowd booed the politician off the stage.  人群把政客嘘下台。|近义词：jeered, hissed, heckled|反义词：cheered, applauded|verb

Etymology:
From the interjection "boo," imitative of a sound of disapproval.
