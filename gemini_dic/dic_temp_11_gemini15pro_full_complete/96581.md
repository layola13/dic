inducible:/ɪnˈdjuːsəbl/ /ɪnˈduːsəbl/
adjective
可诱导的，可诱发的
capable of being induced; brought about or caused by some action or agent.
The enzyme is inducible by exposure to certain chemicals.  这种酶可以通过接触某些化学物质而被诱导。
The expression of this gene is inducible. 这个基因的表达是可诱导的。
近义词:activatable, expressible, triggerable
反义词:constitutive, repressed
词性:adjective
