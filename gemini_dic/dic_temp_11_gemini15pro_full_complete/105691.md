kink:/kɪŋk/| n. v.| 怪癖；扭结；绞缠；问题；毛病 |A sharp twist or curve in something that is otherwise straight; a flaw or difficulty in a plan or system.| The cable developed a kink and wouldn't pull through. 电缆打了个结，拉不动了。|近义词：twist, curl, bend, problem, glitch, snag, hitch|反义词：straightness, smoothness|词性:名词,动词

kink:/kɪŋk/| v. |(使)纽结, (使)绞缠, (使)弯曲；出现问题 |To form a kink or kinks.|  The hose kinked and stopped the water flow. 水管扭结，停止了水流。|近义词：twist, curl, bend|反义词：straighten, untwist|词性:动词
