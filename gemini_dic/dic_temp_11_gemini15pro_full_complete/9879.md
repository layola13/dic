apartheid:/əˈpɑːrtaɪt/
n.
种族隔离
A policy or system of segregation or discrimination on grounds of race.
例句：Apartheid was a brutal system of racial segregation enforced in South Africa. 种族隔离是南非强制执行的一种残酷的种族隔离制度。

例句：The struggle against apartheid lasted for decades. 反种族隔离斗争持续了几十年。

近义词：segregation, discrimination, racism
反义词：integration, equality, desegregation
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Afrikaans *apartheid* "separateness," from *apart* "separate" (from Dutch *apart*, from French *à part*) + *-heid* (equivalent to English *-hood*).
