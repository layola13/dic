outkills:/ˌaʊtˈkɪlz/
verb
胜过；击杀数超过
surpasses in the number of kills; exceeds in killing
例句：Team A outkilled Team B by a significant margin.  A队在击杀数上大幅领先B队。

例句：The experienced hunter outkilled all the novices in the competition.  这位经验丰富的猎人在比赛中击杀数超过了所有新手。
近义词：outmatches, surpasses, defeats
反义词：loses to, is defeated by
词性:v.
