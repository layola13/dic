dumpiest:/ˈdʌmpiɪst/
adjective
最矮胖的
superlative adjective of dumpy: short and stout
例句：He was the dumpiest little hamster I'd ever seen. 他是我见过最矮胖的小仓鼠。

例句：Of all the kittens, she picked the dumpiest one to cuddle. 在所有的小猫中，她挑了最矮胖的一只来抱。
近义词：chubbiest, stoutest, stockiest
反义词：thinnest, slimmest, skinniest
词性: adjective

词源:  From dumpy + -est (superlative suffix).  Dumpy itself likely comes from dump, referring to something short and compact like a heap.
