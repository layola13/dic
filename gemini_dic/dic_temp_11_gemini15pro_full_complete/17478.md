bearer:/ˈbeərər/ (US), /ˈbɛərə/ (UK)| n.|持票人；送信人；生育者；载体|A person or thing that carries or holds something else.|The bearer of the letter was unknown. 这封信的送信人身份不明。|synonyms: carrier, holder, messenger, conveyor,  possessor|antonyms: receiver, recipient|noun

bearer:/ˈbeərər/ (US), /ˈbɛərə/ (UK)| adj.|结果实的；含…的|Producing fruit or carrying something.|The tree was bearer of delicious apples. 这棵树结满了美味的苹果。|synonyms: fruitful, productive, fertile|antonyms: barren, infertile, unproductive|adjective
