communicably:/kəˈmjuːnɪkəbli/
adverb
可传达地；传染地
in a communicable way; so as to be communicable.
Measles is highly communicable. 麻疹具有高度传染性。
近义词:contagiously, infectiously
反义词:noncommunicable, noncontagious
词性:副词

词源:
From communicate + -able + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin communicare.
