ankled:/ˈæŋkəld/
adjective
有...踝的；踝关节形状的
having ankles of a specified kind
The deer is slender-ankled. 这只鹿踝骨纤细。
近义词: legged
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 crippled, lame 表示腿脚不便的)
词性: adjective


ankled:/ˈæŋkəld/
adjective
(腿)细长的，优美的
(of legs) slender and elegant
The dancer was long-ankled and graceful.  这位舞者有着修长的脚踝，十分优雅。
近义词: elegant, graceful, slender-legged
反义词: thick-ankled, clumsy
词性: adjective
