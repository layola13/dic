overpatriotisms: /ˌoʊvərˈpeɪtriətɪzəmz/
noun
过度爱国主义；极端爱国主义
Excessive patriotism; exaggerated or fanatical patriotism.

例句：
Overpatriotisms can lead to xenophobia and hostility towards other nations.
过度爱国主义会导致仇外心理和对其他国家的敌意。

Their overpatriotisms blinded them to the flaws in their own government's policies.
他们的极端爱国主义使他们看不到自己政府政策中的缺陷。


近义词：
chauvinism, jingoism, ultranationalism

反义词：
internationalism, cosmopolitanism

词性:
n. (noun)
