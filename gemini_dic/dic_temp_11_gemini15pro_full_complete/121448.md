misinterpreter:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɜːrprɪtər/ | n. |误解者，曲解者|Someone who interprets something wrongly, especially someone who interprets someone's words or actions wrongly. |例句：He was a misinterpreter of good intentions. 他误解了别人的好意。

例句：The journalist was accused of being a misinterpreter of the facts.  这位记者被指控曲解事实。

近义词：distorter, perverter, misconstruer |反义词: interpreter, elucidator | 词性: noun

misinterpret:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɜːrprɪt/ | vt. | 误解，曲解 | To understand (something, such as a word, deed, or intention) incorrectly. |例句：She misinterpreted his silence as anger. 她把他的沉默误解为生气。

例句：The data was misinterpreted, leading to false conclusions. 数据被误读，导致了错误的结论。

近义词：misunderstand, misconstrue, misapprehend |反义词: understand, comprehend, interpret correctly | 词性: verb
