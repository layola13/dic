catechistical:/ˌkætɪˈkɪstɪkəl/
adjective
教义问答式的；问答教学法的
Relating to, resembling, or consisting of question and answer, especially as used in religious teaching.
The priest used a catechistical method to instruct the children in the basic tenets of the faith.  神父用问答教学法指导孩子们学习信仰的基本原则。
catechistical instruction was common in Sunday schools.  主日学校里普遍采用问答式教学。
近义词: didactic, instructional, Socratic
反义词: n/a
词性: adjective
