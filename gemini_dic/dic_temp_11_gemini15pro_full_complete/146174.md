overexaggerated: /ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzædʒəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过分夸大的，夸张过度的 | Exaggerated to an excessive degree. |  Her reaction was completely overexaggerated. 她的反应完全是夸大其词了。| synonyms: overstated, overblown, inflated, amplified | antonyms: understated, downplayed, minimized | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + exaggerated, past participle of exaggerate, from Latin exaggeratus, past participle of exaggerare “to heap up, increase, aggravate,” from ex- “out” + aggerare “to heap up,” from agger “heap, mound.”
