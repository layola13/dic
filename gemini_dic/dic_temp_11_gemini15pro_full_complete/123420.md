monopneumona:/ˌmɒnəpnjuːˈməʊniə/ /ˌmɑːnənuːˈmoʊniə/ | n. |单侧肺炎|Inflammation of a single lung.  It is a type of pneumonia affecting only one lung. |Example 1: The patient was diagnosed with monopneumona after experiencing chest pain and difficulty breathing. 病人经历了胸痛和呼吸困难后被诊断为单侧肺炎。

Example 2: Monopneumona can be caused by various bacterial or viral infections. 单侧肺炎可能由各种细菌或病毒感染引起。|Synonym: single lung pneumonia, unilateral pneumonia|Antonym: bilateral pneumonia (though not a perfect opposite, it highlights the difference in scope)|noun
