countervote:/ˈkaʊntərˌvoʊt/| n. v. |反票，反对票；投反对票|The act of voting against something.  A vote that is cast in opposition to another vote.|例句：He cast his countervote against the new policy. 他投了反对票反对新政策。|近义词：oppose, vote against, reject|反义词: support, vote for, approve|词性:动词
