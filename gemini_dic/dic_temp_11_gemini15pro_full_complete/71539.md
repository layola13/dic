flare-up:/ˈfleərˌʌp/| n.|爆发，突发，加剧|A sudden outburst or intensification, as of a disease, violence, or anger.|The patient experienced a flare-up of his arthritis. 病人的关节炎突然发作了。|近义词：outbreak, eruption, surge, relapse|反义词: remission, abatement, decline|词性:名词

flare-up:/ˈfleərˌʌp/| v.|突然爆发，突然加剧|(Of a fire or light) burn suddenly with a bright flame.|(Of an illness or injury) recur or suddenly become worse. |His back injury flared up again.  他的背伤又发作了。|近义词：erupt, burst forth, intensify|反义词: subside, diminish, lessen|词性:动词
