digitated:/ˈdɪdʒɪteɪtɪd/
adjective
指状的，有指的，指形的
Having finger-like projections or divisions.
The maple leaf is digitated, resembling a hand with several fingers. 枫叶是指状的，像一只Several fingers的手。
The compound leaf of the horse chestnut tree is digitated. 七叶树的复叶是指状的。
