egalitarianism:/ɪˌɡælɪˈtɛəriənɪzəm/| n. | 平等主义 | A belief in the equality of all people, especially in political, social, or economic life. |  Example: The French Revolution was inspired by the ideals of egalitarianism. 法国大革命受到了平等主义理想的启发。

Example:  Her egalitarianism extends to believing that everyone should have equal opportunities. 她的平等主义延伸到相信每个人都应该有平等的机会。 | 近义词: equality, fairness, impartiality | 反义词: hierarchy, elitism, inequality | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *égalitaire*, from *égal* (“equal”) + *-itaire* (suffix forming adjectives and nouns of agent or quality), equivalent to English *equal* + *-itarian*.
