judicialness:/dʒuːˈdɪʃəl.nəs/
n.
司法公正;审慎;明断
The quality of being judicial; fairness and impartiality in reaching decisions.
His judicialness was evident in his careful consideration of all the facts. 他的审慎在他对所有事实的仔细考虑中显而易见。
近义词：impartiality, fairness, objectivity, neutrality
反义词：bias, prejudice, partiality, subjectivity
词性: 名词


词源：
From judicial + -ness.  Judicial comes from Late Latin judicialis, from Latin judicium "judgment,"  from judex "judge."
