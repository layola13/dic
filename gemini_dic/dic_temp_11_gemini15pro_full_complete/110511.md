limberest:/ˈlɪmbərɪst/| adj.| 最柔软的，最灵活的 |  Superlative form of "limber," meaning most flexible or supple. |  The gymnast was the limberest of all the competitors.  体操运动员是所有参赛者中最灵活的。|  most flexible, most supple, most agile | stiffest, least flexible | adjective

limber:/ˈlɪmbər/| adj. v. | 柔软的，灵活的；使柔软，使灵活 |  Flexible and supple; to make or become flexible.  | She did some stretches to limber up before the run.  她在跑步前做了一些伸展运动来活动筋骨。 | flexible, supple, agile | stiff, inflexible | adjective, verb
