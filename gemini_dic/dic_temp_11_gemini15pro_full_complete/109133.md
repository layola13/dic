legionary:/ˈliːdʒənəri/ | n. | 军团士兵；退伍军人 | A member of a legion, especially of the Roman army. |  The legionary stood guard at the gate.  军团士兵在门口站岗。| synonyms: soldier, trooper, legionnaire | antonyms: civilian | noun

legionary:/ˈliːdʒənəri/ | adj. | 军团的；大量的 | Relating to a legion or legions; extremely numerous. | The legionary forces of Rome were vast. 罗马的军团兵力非常庞大。 | synonyms: numerous, countless, myriad | antonyms: few, scarce | adjective
