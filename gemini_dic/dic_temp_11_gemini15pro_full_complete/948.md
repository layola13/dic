academias:/əˌkæˈdiːmiəz/
n.
学术机构，研究院，学会
A society or institution of distinguished scholars, artists, or scientists, that aims to promote and advance learning, art, or science.
The Royal Academy of Arts in London is a prestigious institution.  伦敦皇家艺术研究院是一个享有盛名的机构。
Several academies of science exist around the world, fostering collaboration and research. 世界各地都有几家科学院，促进合作和研究。
近义词:institutes, societies, schools
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "amateur groups," "non-academic organizations")

词性: noun (plural)

词源: From Latin academia, from Greek akadēmeia "Academy," the name of a gymnasium near Athens where Plato taught.
