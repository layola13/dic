mylohyoid: /ˌmaɪlɵˈhaɪɔɪd/ | adj. n. |  [解剖] 舌骨的；[解剖] 下颌舌骨肌 | A thin, flat muscle running from the mandible to the hyoid bone, forming the floor of the mouth. It elevates the hyoid bone and the tongue, and depresses the mandible. | The mylohyoid muscle plays a crucial role in swallowing. 下颌舌骨肌在吞咽过程中起着至关重要的作用。 |  Synonym: submental (referring to the region) | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific muscle) | noun, adjective
