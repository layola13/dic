gemmed:/dʒemd/| adj.|镶嵌宝石的；用宝石装饰的|Adorned or decorated with gems.  The word originates from the noun "gem" with the suffix "-ed" added to create an adjective. The suffix "-ed" often indicates that something has been done to the noun, in this case, decorated with gems.|例句：She wore a gemmed crown. 她戴着镶满宝石的王冠。|近义词： jeweled, bejeweled|反义词: plain, unadorned|词性:adjective
