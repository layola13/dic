nonpacifist:/ˌnɒnˈpæsɪfɪst/
n./adj.
非和平主义者；非和平主义的
A person who does not believe in pacifism; not pacifistic.  Someone who believes that violence or force is sometimes necessary or justifiable.
例句：The nonpacifist argued that war was sometimes unavoidable.  非和平主义者认为战争有时是不可避免的。

例句：Her nonpacifist stance made her unpopular with some groups.  她非和平主义的立场使她不受某些群体的欢迎。

近义词：warmonger, hawk, aggressor, militant
反义词：pacifist, dove, peacemaker
词性：noun, adjective
