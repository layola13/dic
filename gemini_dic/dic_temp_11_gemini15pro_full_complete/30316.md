censorian:/senˈsɔːriən/
n.
审查员，检察官
an official who examines books, films, news, etc. to remove anything considered offensive or harmful
The censorian removed several scenes from the film before its release. 审查员在电影上映前删除了几个场景。
synonym: censor, examiner, inspector
antonym:  promoter, advocate
noun

censorian:/senˈsɔːriən/
adj.
审查的，检查的
relating to censorship or a censor
The censorian board decided to ban the book. 审查委员会决定禁掉这本书。
synonym: censoring, expurgatory, restrictive
antonym: permissive, liberating, unrestrictive
adjective
