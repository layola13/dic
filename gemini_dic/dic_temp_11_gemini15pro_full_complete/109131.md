legionnaire:/ˈliːdʒəˈnɛər/
n.
军团士兵; 退伍军人;
a member of a legion, especially the French Foreign Legion.
Example: The legionnaire fought bravely in many battles. 这名军团士兵在许多战斗中英勇作战。
Example:  He was a proud legionnaire who served his country with honor. 他是一位自豪的军团士兵，光荣地为国家服务。
Synonym: soldier, veteran
Antonym: civilian
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *légionnaire*, from Latin *legiōnārius* (“legionary, soldier of a legion”), from *legiō* (“legion”) + *-ārius* (suffix forming nouns denoting persons connected with what is expressed by the base).
