nodes:/noʊdz/ (US) /nəʊdz/ (UK)| n.|节点，结点|A point of connection, intersection, or concentration in a network or system. |The network has several nodes, each representing a different computer. 网络有几个节点，每个节点代表一台不同的计算机。|The lymph nodes were swollen. 淋巴结肿大了。|synonyms: junction, point, vertex, intersection, nexus|antonyms: non-node (not a standard English word), gap, break|noun
