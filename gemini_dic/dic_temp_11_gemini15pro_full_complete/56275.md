dogg:/dɒɡ/ | n. |朋友, 哥们 (俚语) | A slang term, primarily used in hip-hop culture, to refer to a close friend or associate.  It's a variation of "dog." | Example: "Yo, what's up, dogg?" 哟，怎么了，哥们？ | Example:  He's my dogg, we've been through thick and thin. 他是我的哥们，我们一起经历了风风雨雨。 | 近义词:friend, pal, buddy, homeboy (slang), homie (slang) | 反义词:enemy, foe | 词性:名词
