conforming:/kənˈfɔːrmɪŋ/| adj.|一致的，顺从的，符合的|Acting in accordance with rules or standards; obedient or compliant.|例句：Conforming to social norms can be challenging. 顺应社会规范可能具有挑战性。|近义词：compliant, obedient, acquiescent, yielding, submissive|反义词:rebellious, nonconformist, defiant, dissenting, individualistic|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French conformer, from Latin conformare ("to form after, shape, fashion"), from com- ("together") + formare ("to form").
