ichnolite:/ˈɪknəˌlaɪt/| n. | 鱼石，遗迹化石 |A fossil consisting of the fossilized excrement (coprolite) or tracks of an organism.  It provides evidence of the activities of ancient organisms. | Example 1: The ichnolite discovered in the sandstone revealed the presence of dinosaurs in the area. 这块在砂岩中发现的鱼石揭示了该地区曾有恐龙的存在。

Example 2: Researchers studied the ichnolites to understand the locomotion patterns of extinct creatures. 研究人员研究了这些遗迹化石，以了解已灭绝生物的运动模式。 | 近义词: trace fossil, coprolite, fossil | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially "living organism" in a broad sense) | 词性: noun
