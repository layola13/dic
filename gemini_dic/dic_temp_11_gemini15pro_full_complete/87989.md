high-pressure:/ˌhaɪˈpreʃər/| adj.|高压的，高压力的|Characterized by or involving intense pressure, stress, or urgency.|He works in a high-pressure sales environment. 他在一个高压力的销售环境中工作。|Synonyms: stressful, demanding, intense|Antonyms: relaxed, low-pressure, easygoing|adjective

high-pressure:/ˌhaɪˈpreʃər/| n.|高压，高气压|A state of high or intense pressure, often related to stress or the atmosphere.|The high-pressure system brought clear skies. 高气压系统带来了晴朗的天空。|Synonyms: stress, strain, tension|Antonyms: low pressure, relaxation, calmness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "high" + "pressure". "High" meaning elevated or intense, and "pressure" referring to the force exerted on a surface or the burden of physical or mental distress.
