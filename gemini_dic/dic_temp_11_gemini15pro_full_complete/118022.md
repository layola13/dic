memorys:/ˈmeməriz/| n. |记忆（非标准英语，标准英语为memories）|This word is a non-standard plural form of "memory." The standard plural is "memories."  It refers to the faculty by which the mind stores and remembers information. |He cherishes his childhood memorys. (Non-standard) 他珍惜他的童年记忆。(非标准) | 近义词: recollections, reminiscences | 反义词: amnesia, oblivion | Noun
