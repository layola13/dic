antidomestic:/ˌænti dəˈmɛstɪk/ | adj. | 反家庭的，反对家庭生活的，厌恶家务的 | Opposed to domestic life or values; rejecting traditional family structures and roles. |  She had a very antidomestic attitude and preferred working outside the home. 她对家庭生活非常反感，更喜欢在外工作。 |  He led a rather antidomestic existence, rarely cooking or cleaning. 他过着相当反家庭的生活，很少做饭或打扫卫生。 | 近义词: unconventional, non-traditional, bohemian | 反义词: domestic, traditional, home-loving | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek "anti-")
domestic (adjective): relating to the home or family (from Latin "domesticus," meaning "belonging to the house")
