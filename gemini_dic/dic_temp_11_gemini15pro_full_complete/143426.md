outflies:/ˌaʊtˈflaɪz/
verb
超越飞行；比…飞得快；胜过
To fly faster or farther than.  To surpass in flight.
The new jet outflies all previous models.  这架新喷气式飞机比所有以前的型号飞得都快。
surpasses, exceeds
lags behind
