declaredly:/dɪˈklɛərdli/
adv.
公开地，明确地
openly and unambiguously; explicitly.
He declaredly supported the new policy. 他公开支持新政策。
Synonyms: openly, explicitly, plainly, avowedly, admittedly
Antonyms: secretly, covertly, implicitly
词性: 副词
