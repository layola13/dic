angels:/ˈeɪndʒəlz/ | n. |天使;守护神;安琪儿|A spiritual being believed to act as an attendant, agent, or messenger of God, conventionally represented in human form with wings and a long robe.|The angels sang praises. 天使们唱着赞歌。|Synonyms: seraph, cherub, spirit, guardian angel|Antonyms: demon, devil|Noun

angels:/ˈeɪndʒəlz/ | n. |(非正式)善良或无辜的人，尤指孩子|A person, especially a child, who is kind or innocent.|She's such a little angel. 她真是个小天使。|Synonyms: darling, sweetheart, cherub|Antonyms: devil, brat|Noun
