ciliated:/ˈsɪlieɪtɪd/
adjective
有纤毛的；具纤毛的
Covered with cilia (small hair-like structures).
The ciliated cells lining the trachea sweep mucus and foreign particles out of the lungs.  气管内壁的纤毛细胞将粘液和异物清除出肺部。

The paramecium is a single-celled, ciliated organism. 草履虫是一种单细胞的纤毛生物。

近义词: fimbriated, hairy
反义词: non-ciliated, smooth

词性: adjective
