brownie:/ˈbraʊni/| n.|布朗尼蛋糕；女童子军；深褐色|A small, square, rich chocolate cake.|例句：I baked a batch of brownies for the bake sale. 我烤了一批布朗尼蛋糕用于烘焙义卖。

例句：She joined the Brownies when she was seven. 她七岁时加入了女童子军。|近义词：chocolate cake, brownie scout (for a girl scout)|反义词: (No direct antonym, could be considered opposite of a "blondie" in baking context) |词性:noun


brownie:/ˈbraʊni/| adj.|深褐色的|Having a dark brown color.|例句：She has brownie eyes. 她有一双深褐色的眼睛。

例句：He bought a brownie uniform for his daughter. 他为女儿买了一套深褐色的制服。|近义词：dark brown, chocolate-colored|反义词: light brown, blonde|词性:adjective
