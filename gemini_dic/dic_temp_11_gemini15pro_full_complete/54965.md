disproperty:/dɪsˈprɒpəti/
noun
剥夺财产; 剥夺权利

He suffered the disproperty of his lands. 他遭受了被剥夺土地的厄运。
Synonyms: deprivation, divestiture, forfeiture
Antonyms:  acquisition, endowment, bestowal
