learnt:/lɜːnt/ /lɜːrnd/ | v. | 学会,得知 | Past tense and past participle of learn.  It means to gain knowledge or skill by study, experience, or being taught. | I learnt to ride a bike when I was six. 我六岁时学会了骑自行车。|  He learnt that his friend had moved away. 他得知他的朋友搬走了。| 近义词: learned, studied, discovered | 反义词:  ignored, forgot | 词性: 动词
