nidificate:/ˈnɪdəˌfeɪt/ /ˈnɪdɪˌfeɪt/| v.|筑巢, 做窝, 定居|To build a nest; to settle down in a place.|The robins nidificated in the apple tree. 知更鸟在苹果树上筑巢。|The couple nidificated in a cozy cottage by the sea. 这对夫妇在海边舒适的小屋里安顿下来。|近义词：nest, build a nest, settle, reside|反义词:migrate, roam, wander|词性:动词

Etymology: From Latin *nīdificāre* "to make a nest," from *nīdus* "nest" + *-ficāre* (akin to *facere* "to make, to do").
