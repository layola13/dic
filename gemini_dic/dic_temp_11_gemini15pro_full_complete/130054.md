non-physical:/ˌnɒnˈfɪzɪkəl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈfɪzɪkl/ (UK)| adj.|非物质的，非实体的|Not having a material existence; not relating to the physical world.|例句：Many religions believe in a non-physical soul. 很多宗教信仰非物质的灵魂。|近义词：immaterial, spiritual, metaphysical, abstract|反义词：physical, material, tangible|词性:形容词
