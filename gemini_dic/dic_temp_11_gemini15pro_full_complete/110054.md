licentiateship:/laɪˈsɛnʃɪətʃɪp/
n.
执照；许可证；特许；获得许可的身份
The status of being a licentiate; a professional qualification between a bachelor's degree and a doctorate.
She's studying for her licentiateship in dentistry. 她正在攻读牙科执照。
Synonyms: license, certification, authorization, accreditation, diploma
Antonyms: prohibition, disqualification, ban
