giraffe:/dʒəˈræf/ | n. | 长颈鹿 | A tall African mammal with a very long neck and legs, patterned with brown patches separated by lighter lines.  It is the tallest living animal. |  I saw a giraffe at the zoo. 我在动物园看到了一只长颈鹿。 | The giraffe reached up to eat the leaves. 长颈鹿伸长脖子吃树叶。 | 近义词:  (没有完美的近义词，因为长颈鹿是独一无二的) | 反义词: (没有反义词) | 词性: noun

Etymology->
From Old French *girafe, from Italian giraffa, from Arabic zarāfah (زرافة).
