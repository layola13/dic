aired:/erd/ | adj. | 通风的，公开的 | Exposed to the air; ventilated; made public. | The room felt fresh and aired. 房间感觉清新通风。| Synonyms: ventilated, breezy, open, public | Antonyms: stuffy, closed, private | adjective
