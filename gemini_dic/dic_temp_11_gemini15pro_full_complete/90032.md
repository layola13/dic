hosiery:/ˈhoʊʒəri/ (US), /ˈhɒʒəri/ (UK)| n. |袜子，针织品|Articles of clothing made from knitted or woven material, such as socks, stockings, and tights.|She bought new hosiery for the winter. 她买了新的冬季袜子。|Example: The store specializes in high-quality hosiery. 这家商店专营高品质的针织品。|Synonyms: stockings, socks, tights, legwear|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "bare legs" or "shoes")|noun
