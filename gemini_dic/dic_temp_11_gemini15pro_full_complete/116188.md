mashie:/ˈmæʃi/| n.|马什杆; 五号铁; 中距离铁杆|A golf club with a lofted face, used for short approaches to the green.  It is roughly equivalent to a modern five-iron.|He used his mashie to chip the ball onto the green. 他用他的五号铁将球切上果岭。|近义词:five-iron, mid-iron|反义词:driver, putter|词性:noun

Etymology:  Shortened from *mashie-niblick*, from Scots *mashie*, of uncertain origin + *niblick* "a short iron with a deep, angled face"
