headhunting:/ˈhedˌhʌntɪŋ/| n.|猎头; 物色人才|The act of recruiting a person of senior management or technical level from another company or organization.|The company is known for its aggressive headhunting practices.  这家公司以其激进的猎头行为而闻名。|Synonyms: recruitment, executive search|Antonyms: dismissal, firing|noun

headhuntings:/ˈhedˌhʌntɪŋz/| n.|猎头行为（复数）|Multiple instances of the act of recruiting a person of senior management or technical level from another company or organization.|The recent headhuntings have left several companies short-staffed. 最近的猎头行为导致几家公司人手不足。|Synonyms: recruitments, executive searches|Antonyms: dismissals, firings|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"Headhunting" originally referred to the practice of some tribal cultures of literally taking and preserving the heads of enemies.  This term was then metaphorically applied to the practice of recruiting executives from other companies, implying a kind of "capture" of valuable personnel.
