nonlaminated:/ˌnɒnˈlæmɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
未层压的，非层压的
Not having been covered with a thin protective layer of plastic or other material.
The restaurant menus were printed on cheap, nonlaminated paper.  这家餐厅的菜单印在廉价的未覆膜纸上。
The nonlaminated wood quickly warped in the humid climate. 未层压的木材在潮湿的气候中很快变形。
近义词:uncoated, untreated
反义词:laminated, coated
词性:adjective

nonlaminated:/ˌnɒnˈlæmɪneɪtɪd/
noun
未层压材料
Material that has not been covered with a thin protective layer of plastic or other material.
They used nonlaminated cardboard for the packaging.  他们用未层压的纸板做包装。
近义词:uncoated material
反义词:laminated material
词性:noun


词源:
non- (prefix) meaning "not" + laminated (past participle of laminate, from Latin lamina "thin plate")
