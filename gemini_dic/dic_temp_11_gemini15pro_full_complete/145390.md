overcasualness:/ˌoʊvərˈkæʒuəlnəs/| n.| 过于随意；过分随便|The overcasualness of his attire was inappropriate for the formal event. 他的穿着过于随意，不适合这种正式场合。|例句：His overcasualness in the meeting offended the clients.  他在会议上过于随便，冒犯了客户。

例句：Her overcasualness about the deadline led to the project being delayed. 她对截止日期的漫不经心导致项目延期。|近义词：informality, casualness, nonchalance|反义词：formality, seriousness, meticulousness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + casualness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much". "Casualness" is derived from "casual" which comes from the Late Latin "casualis" meaning "happening by chance."
