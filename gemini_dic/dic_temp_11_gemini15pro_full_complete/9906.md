ape-like:/ˈeɪpˌlaɪk/
adjective
像猿的；类人猿似的
Resembling an ape in appearance or behavior.
His posture was ape-like, hunched and slightly awkward. 他的姿势像猿猴一样，弓着背，略显笨拙。
The creature had long, ape-like arms.  这个生物有着长长的、像猿猴一样的胳膊。
近义词: simian, anthropoid, primate-like
反义词: human-like, hominid
词性: adjective
