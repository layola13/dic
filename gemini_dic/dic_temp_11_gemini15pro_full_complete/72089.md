floatingly:/ˈfloʊtɪŋli/| adv.|飘浮地，轻盈地|In a manner resembling something that floats; lightly and gracefully.|The ballerina moved floatingly across the stage.  芭蕾舞演员轻盈地在舞台上移动。|近义词：gracefully, airily, lightly|反义词：heavily, clumsily|词性:副词

词源: From float + -ing + -ly.  来自 float（漂浮） + -ing（现在分词后缀） + -ly（副词后缀）。
