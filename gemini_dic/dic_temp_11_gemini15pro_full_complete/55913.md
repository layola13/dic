divulgements: /daɪˈvʌldʒmənts/ | n. | 泄露，披露（复数） | The act of revealing something previously secret or unknown.  The plural form of divulgement. |  The recent divulgements about the company's finances have caused a scandal. 最近关于公司财务状况的披露引发了一场丑闻。

The divulgements of classified information put national security at risk.  泄露机密信息危及国家安全。 | 近义词：disclosures, revelations, publications | 反义词：concealments, suppressions | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old French divulguer "to reveal, publish," from Latin divulgare "to make public, spread among the people," from dis- "widely" + vulgare "to make common or public," related to vulgus "the common people, multitude."
