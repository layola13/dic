hooding:/ˈhʊdɪŋ/ | n. |学位授予仪式，加冕仪式|The act of placing a hood on a graduate, especially as part of a graduation ceremony, or a similar act of investiture.|The hooding ceremony marked the culmination of years of hard work.  学位授予仪式标志着多年辛勤工作的顶点。| 近义词:investiture, ceremony | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 divestment - 剥夺) | noun

hooding:/ˈhʊdɪŋ/ | n. | (通常指鸟类)戴上头罩的行为 | The act of covering the head of a bird of prey with a hood.| The falconer practiced hooding the bird before the hunt.  猎鹰人练习在狩猎前给鸟戴上头罩。 | 近义词: covering, concealing | 反义词: uncovering, revealing | noun
