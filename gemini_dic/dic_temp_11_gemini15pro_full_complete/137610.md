nontropically:/ˌnɒnˈtrɒpɪk(ə)li/ | adv. | 非向性地，非趋向性地 |  In a way that is not directed towards or influenced by a particular stimulus or goal; not tropically.  It describes something that doesn't exhibit tropism, meaning it doesn't grow or move in response to an external stimulus like light, gravity, or touch. |  The crystals formed nontropically, spreading evenly across the surface. 晶体非向性地形成，均匀地分布在表面。| The particles dispersed nontropically throughout the solution.  颗粒在整个溶液中非向性地分散。 | synonym: randomly, haphazardly, evenly | antonym: tropically, directionally | adverb
