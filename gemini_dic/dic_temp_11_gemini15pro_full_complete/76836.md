Ganesa:/ɡəˈneɪʃə/ | n. |象头神，伽内什|A Hindu deity with an elephant's head, son of Shiva and Parvati, and god of wisdom, beginnings, and the remover of obstacles.|例句：Ganesa is widely worshipped in India. 象头神在印度被广泛崇拜。

例句：Many Hindus pray to Ganesa before starting a new venture. 许多印度教徒在开始新事业之前会向象头神祈祷。|近义词：Ganesha|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Ganesh:/ɡəˈneɪʃ/ | n. |象头神，伽内什|Another spelling for Ganesa, a Hindu deity with an elephant's head, revered as the remover of obstacles and god of beginnings.|例句：Ganesh Chaturthi is a major Hindu festival celebrating his birthday. 象头神节是一个重要的印度教节日，庆祝他的生日。

例句：Statues of Ganesh are often placed at entrances for good luck.  象头神的雕像经常被放置在入口处以求好运。|近义词：Ganesa|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源:
From Sanskrit गणेश (gaṇeśa), meaning "lord of categories," from गण (gaṇa) "category, multitude, troop, host, flock, tribe, series, class" and ईश (īśa) "lord, master, ruler, controller."
