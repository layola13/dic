cubs:/kʌbz/
|n.|幼崽（尤指熊、狮、虎、狼等）;  (Chicago Cubs) 芝加哥小熊队（美国职棒大联盟球队）|Young animals, especially of bears, lions, tigers, or wolves. Also, refers to the Chicago Cubs baseball team.|The mother bear fiercely protected her cubs. 母熊拼命保护她的幼崽。|The Chicago Cubs won the World Series in 2016. 芝加哥小熊队在2016年赢得了世界大赛。|young, offspring, babies, whelps (specifically for dogs)|adults|noun
