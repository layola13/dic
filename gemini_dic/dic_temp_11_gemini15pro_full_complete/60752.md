electrofocusing:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈfoʊkəsɪŋ/| n.|电聚焦|A technique used to separate molecules, especially proteins, by their isoelectric point (pI) in a stable pH gradient generated by an electric field.|Electrofocusing is a powerful technique for separating proteins.|近义词：isoelectric focusing|反义词:|noun
