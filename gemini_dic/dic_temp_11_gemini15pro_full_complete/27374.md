came-around:/keɪm əˈraʊnd/
verb
苏醒；恢复知觉；回心转意；造访
regain consciousness; change one's mind or position; visit
He came around after a few minutes. 他几分钟后就苏醒了。
The committee eventually came around to my point of view. 委员会最终同意了我的观点。
She promised to come around later this week. 她答应这周晚些时候会来拜访。
近义词:recover, revive, change one's mind, visit, drop by
反义词:pass out, faint, remain opposed
