footstalk:/ˈfʊtˌstɔːk/| n. |叶柄；花梗；果柄|The slender stem that supports a leaf, flower, or fruit.|The apple hung from its footstalk. 苹果悬挂在它的果柄上。|近义词：pedicel, peduncle, stem|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From foot + stalk.  "Foot" referring to the base or support, and "stalk" referring to a stem or slender support.
