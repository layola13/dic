matchwoods:/ˈmætʃˌwʊdz/
noun
火柴木;引火柴;火柴梗
small sticks of wood used for making matches
Early matches were made with matchwoods dipped in sulfur. 早期的火柴是用浸过硫磺的火柴木制成的。
