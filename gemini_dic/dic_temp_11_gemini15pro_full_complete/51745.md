devolve:/dɪˈvɒlv/ /dɪˈvɑːlv/| v. |转移（权力、责任等）；退化；衰落|To transfer or delegate (power or responsibility) to a lower level, especially from central government to local or regional administration; to pass on or down (something, such as rights, duties, or powers) from one person or entity to another. Also, to degenerate or deteriorate.|The federal government devolved some of its powers to the states. 联邦政府将部分权力下放给各州。|delegate, transfer, pass down|centralize, retain|verb


devolved:/dɪˈvɒlvd/ /dɪˈvɑːlvd/| adj. |已下放的；衰落的|Having had power transferred or delegated to a lower level.|The devolved government in Scotland has responsibility for education and health.  苏格兰下放政府负责教育和卫生。|decentralized, delegated|centralized|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin devolvere "to roll down," from de- "down" + volvere "to roll."
