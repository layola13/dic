flinchingly:/ˈflɪntʃɪŋli/
adv.
退缩地，畏缩地
in a flinching manner; showing a tendency to flinch.
She flinchingly accepted the criticism. 她畏缩地接受了批评。
近义词：hesitantly, timidly, nervously
反义词：boldly, bravely, courageously
词性: 副词

Etymology: From flinch + -ing + -ly.  "Flinch" comes from Old French "flenchir" meaning "to bend, give way."

1. He flinchingly accepted the what if scenario. 他畏缩地接受了如果...会怎么样的设想。
