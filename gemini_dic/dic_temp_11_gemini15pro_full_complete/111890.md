logophile:/ˈlɒɡəfaɪl/ (UK), /ˈlɑːɡəfaɪl/ (US)| n. | 爱词者；词汇爱好者 | A lover of words; a person who enjoys learning and using new words. |  She's a true logophile, constantly expanding her vocabulary. 她是一位真正的词汇爱好者，不断地扩大她的词汇量。
