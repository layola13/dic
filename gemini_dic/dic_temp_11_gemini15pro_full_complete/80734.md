gory:/ˈɡɔːri/| adj.|血淋淋的，充满血污的|Covered in blood; involving much bloodshed.|The battlefield was a gory scene. 战场是一片血腥的景象。|Synonyms: bloody, bloodstained, blood-soaked|Antonyms: clean, pristine|adjective
