USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are they watching on TV? = 他们在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? = 你最近学到了什么？
What has she accomplished this year? = 今年她完成了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been studying for the past few months? = 过去几个月他一直在学习什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had they discussed prior to the conference? = 会议之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? = 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been working on for so long? = 她这么长时间以来一直在做什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们会在下次会议上讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 年底你将完成什么？
What will they have finished by then? = 到那时他们将完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将学习什么专业多久了？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
If I knew what to say, I would tell you. = 如果我知道该说什么，我会告诉你。

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what he's saying. = 她不明白他在说什么。
