glycinin:/ˈɡlaɪsɪnɪn/| n.|大豆球蛋白|A major storage protein of soybeans, comprising about 80% of the total protein. It's a globulin, meaning it's soluble in dilute salt solutions but insoluble in pure water.|Glycinin is an important source of protein in soy-based foods. 大豆球蛋白是大豆食品中重要的蛋白质来源。|Synonyms: soy globulin|Antonyms: None|noun

glyconin:/ɡlaɪˈkoʊnɪn/| n.|大豆伴球蛋白|A glycoprotein found in soybeans, often associated with glycinin. It's a minor storage protein compared to glycinin.|Glyconin contributes to the nutritional value of soybeans. 大豆伴球蛋白有助于大豆的营养价值。|Synonyms: None|Antonyms: None|noun
