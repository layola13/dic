nonequestrians:/ˌnɒnɪˈkwɛstriənz/
noun
非骑马者；非马术爱好者
people who do not ride horses or engage in equestrian activities.
The nonequestrians in the group were offered a hayride instead of joining the trail ride.  这群非骑马者被安排乘坐干草车，而不是参加骑马旅行。
The stable offered lessons for both equestrians and nonequestrians.  马厩为骑马者和非骑马者都提供课程。
Synonyms: non-riders
Antonyms: equestrians, riders
n.
