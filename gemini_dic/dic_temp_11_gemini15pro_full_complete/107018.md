lactations:/lækˈteɪʃənz/
|n.|哺乳期; 泌乳期|The period of milk production in mammals, typically associated with pregnancy and childbirth.  The process of producing milk.|She experienced difficulties during her first few lactations. 她头几次哺乳期都遇到了困难。|
近义词: breastfeeding, nursing, milk production
|反义词: weaning|
词性: noun (plural)

词源: From Latin *lactātiō (“sucking”), from lactō (“I suckle, give milk to”), from lac (“milk”).
