ministering:/ˈmɪnɪstərɪŋ/
adjective
服侍的，照料的
Attending to the needs of others; providing care, aid, or religious service.
例句：
She was ministering to the sick. 她正在照顾病人。

The nurses were ministering to the wounded soldiers. 护士们正在照料受伤的士兵。


近义词：serving, tending, attending, caring
反义词：neglecting, ignoring

词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *ministren, from Old French ministrer, from Latin ministrāre (“to serve, attend, furnish”), frequentative of ministrare (“to serve, wait upon”), from minister (“servant, attendant”).
