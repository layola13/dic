ovalnesses:/ˈoʊvəlnɪsɪz/
n.
椭圆度，卵形
the quality or state of being oval.
The artist emphasized the ovalnesses of the faces in the portrait.  这位艺术家强调了肖像中脸的椭圆形。
ovality, roundness, ellipticity
angularity, sharpness
