bulker:/ˈbʌlkər/| n. |散货船，散装货船|A ship designed to carry bulk cargo, such as grain, ore, or coal.|The bulker was loaded with iron ore in Australia. 这艘散货船在澳大利亚装载了铁矿石。|Synonyms: bulk carrier, freighter, cargo ship|Antonyms: container ship, tanker|noun
