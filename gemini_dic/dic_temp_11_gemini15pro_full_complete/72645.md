flyspeck:/ˈflaɪˌspɛk/| n.|蝇粪; 小污点; 微不足道的东西|A flyspeck is a tiny spot of excrement from a fly. It is also used metaphorically to describe something small and insignificant.|The document was covered in flyspecks.|近义词：speck, dot, blemish, trifle|反义词: something substantial, something significant|词性:noun

Etymology:
From fly + speck.  First recorded in the late 16th century.
