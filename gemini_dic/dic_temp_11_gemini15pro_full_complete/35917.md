cocreators:/ˌkoʊkriˈeɪtərz/| n. |共同创造者，合作者|People who create something together.  Those who share in the act of creation, often working collaboratively.|例句：The cocreators of the project were recognized for their innovative work.  该项目的共同创造者因其创新工作而受到表彰。

例句：The game's cocreators designed it to be both challenging and rewarding.  这款游戏的共同创造者将其设计得既有挑战性又有回报。|近义词：collaborators, partners, joint authors|反义词:competitors, rivals|词性:noun

Etymology:
co- (prefix): from Latin "com-" meaning "with," "together"
creator: from Latin "creare" meaning "to create,"  "creator" meaning "one who creates"
