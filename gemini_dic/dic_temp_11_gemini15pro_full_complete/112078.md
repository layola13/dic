longing:/ˈlɒŋɪŋ/ (British), /ˈlɔːŋɪŋ/ (American)| n. adj.|渴望，向往；渴望的，热望的|A strong feeling of wanting something or someone.|She felt a longing for her homeland. 她对故乡充满了渴望。|近义词：yearning, craving, desire, wish|反义词：indifference, apathy|noun

longing:/ˈlɒŋɪŋ/ (British), /ˈlɔːŋɪŋ/ (American)| adj.|渴望的，热望的|Having a strong desire for something or someone.|He gave her a longing look. 他深情地望着她。|近义词：yearning, wistful|反义词：indifferent, apathetic|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *langung, verbal noun of langian "to yearn," from Proto-Germanic *langōną "to grow long," from the root *langaz "long." The sense evolution is from "grow long (for something)" to "yearn."
