nonrequisite:/nɒnˈrɛkwɪzɪt/
nonrequisite| n.|非必修科目；非先决条件|A course that is not required for a particular degree or program.  A condition that is not necessary for something else to happen.|Students can choose from a range of nonrequisite electives.|近义词：optional, elective|反义词:required, compulsory, mandatory|词性:名词

nonrequisite:/nɑːnˈrɛkwɪzɪt/
nonrequisite| adj.|非必需的；非必修的|Not required; optional.|Attendance at the workshop is nonrequisite but encouraged.|近义词：optional, elective, nonessential|反义词:required, compulsory, mandatory, essential|词性:形容词
