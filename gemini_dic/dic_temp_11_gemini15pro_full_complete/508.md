abolishers:/əˈbɒlɪʃəz/
n.
废除者， abolitionist的复数形式
People who seek to abolish a system, practice, or institution.  Those who advocate for the abolition of something.
The abolitionists fought tirelessly to end slavery. 废奴主义者不知疲倦地奋斗以结束奴隶制。
synonyms: abolitionists, reformers, activists, campaigners
antonyms: supporters, proponents, advocates (of the thing being abolished), conservatives
noun
