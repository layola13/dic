nondeflation:/ˌnɒndɪˈfleɪʃən/ | n. | 非通货紧缩 | The state of an economy where prices are not falling.  Absence of deflation. |  The government's policies aimed at nondeflation helped stabilize the economy. 政府旨在避免通货紧缩的政策帮助稳定了经济。 | Near synonyms: price stability, disinflation | Antonyms: deflation, price decrease | noun


Etymology: "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "deflation" (from Latin "deflare," meaning "to blow away," in economic context, a decrease in the general price level of goods and services).
