overly:/ˈoʊvərli/| adv.|过度地，过分地|Too; to an excessive degree.  It emphasizes that something is done more than is necessary or desirable.|She was overly concerned about her appearance. 她过分在意自己的外表。|近义词：excessively, unduly, too|反义词: moderately, reasonably|词性:副词
词源: From Old English *oferlic "upper, superior," from ofer "over" + -lic "-ly."  The meaning shifted to "excessive" in Middle English.
