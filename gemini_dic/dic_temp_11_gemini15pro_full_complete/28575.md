careen:/kəˈriːn/
verb
倾斜，摇晃；(尤指车辆)高速危险地行驶
to (cause a vehicle to) lean over or swerve while moving at high speed

The car careened around the corner. 汽车在转弯处摇晃。
The bus careened down the hill, out of control. 公共汽车失控地摇晃着冲下山坡。

近义词:swerve, tilt, lurch, veer, career
反义词:stabilize

词性:v.
