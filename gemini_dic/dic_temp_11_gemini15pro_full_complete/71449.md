flame:/fleɪm/| n. vi.|火焰，热情，光辉|a hot glowing body of ignited gas that is generated by something on fire|The campfire flames danced in the night. 篝火的火焰在夜里跳动。|近义词：blaze, fire, inferno|反义词:calm, peace|noun
flame:/fleɪm/| vi.|燃烧，爆发，发怒|to burn with a bright flame or to suddenly become angry or excited|His face flamed with anger. 他的脸因愤怒而涨红。|近义词：blaze, flare, erupt|反义词:extinguish, die down|verb
