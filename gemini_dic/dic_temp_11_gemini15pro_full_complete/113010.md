lumberless:/ˈlʌmbərlɪs/
adjective
无木材的；没有木材的
lacking lumber; without lumber
The small island nation was lumberless, relying on imports for all its wood needs. 这个小岛国没有木材，所有的木材需求都依赖进口。
The lumberless workshop had to find alternative materials for their projects.  这间没有木材的工作坊不得不为他们的项目寻找替代材料。
近义词: woodless, timberless
反义词: wooded, timbered
词性: adjective

词源:  "lumber" (timber) + "-less" (suffix indicating absence).  The suffix "-less" comes from Old English "-lēas."
