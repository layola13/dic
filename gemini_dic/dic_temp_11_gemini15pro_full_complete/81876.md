grievancer:/ˈɡriːvənsər/| n.|申诉人，抱怨者|A person who has a grievance or complaint.|The grievancer filed a formal complaint with the human resources department. 申诉人向人力资源部门提交了正式投诉。|近义词：complainant, objector, protester|反义词:approver, supporter|noun

grievance:/ˈɡriːvəns/| n.|委屈，抱怨，不平|A real or imagined wrong or other cause for complaint or protest, especially unfair treatment.|He has a grievance against his employer. 他对他的雇主心怀不满。|近义词：complaint, injustice, wrong|反义词:satisfaction, contentment|noun
