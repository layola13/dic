ghostiest: /ˈɡoʊstiɪst/ | adj. | 最鬼魅的；最像鬼的 |  Superlative form of ghostly: most ghostly.  Having the most characteristics of a ghost; most spectral or eerie. | The ghostiest room in the house was the attic. 房子里最像鬼屋的房间是阁楼。| The abandoned asylum had a ghostiest atmosphere I've ever experienced. 这座废弃的收容所弥漫着我从未经历过的最鬼魅的气氛。 | Synonyms: spectral, eerie, spooky, haunted, unearthly | Antonyms:  cheerful, bright, lively, earthly, tangible | adjective
