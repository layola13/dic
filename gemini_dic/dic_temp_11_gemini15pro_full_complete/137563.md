nontransmission:/ˌnɑːntrænzˈmɪʃən/ |/ˌnɒntrænzˈmɪʃən/| n. |不传播，非传播，不传递|The nontransmission of the virus is crucial for public health. 病毒的不传播对公共卫生至关重要。|例句：The nontransmission of cultural knowledge can lead to its extinction. 文化知识的不传播会导致其消亡。|近义词：non-proliferation, containment, suppression|反义词: transmission, spread, proliferation|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + transmission.  "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not". "Transmission" comes from the Late Latin "transmissio," meaning "a sending across" or "a passing on."
