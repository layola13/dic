outfumbling:/ˌaʊtˈfʌmblɪŋ/
verb
笨手笨脚地摸索;  笨拙地找出
The act of fumbling something out, typically in a clumsy or awkward manner.  Implies a struggle to retrieve or produce something.
He was outfumbling for his keys in the dark. 他在黑暗中笨手笨脚地摸索着钥匙。
clumsily searching, groping, awkwardly retrieving
skillfully retrieving, smoothly handling
v.
