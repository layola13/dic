cottier:/ˈkɒtiə(r)/ (US: /ˈkɑːtiər/) | n. | 佃农；小屋农 | A cottier is a peasant farmer who rents a small plot of land for a living, often living in a simple cottage. |  The cottiers worked hard to cultivate their small plots of land. 佃农们辛勤耕作着他们的小块土地。

The cottiers lived in simple cottages provided by the landlord. 佃农们住在由地主提供的简陋小屋里。 | 近义词: peasant, tenant farmer, smallholder | 反义词: landlord, landowner | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *coter, from Old French *cotier, from cote (“cottage”), from Old Norse kot (“hut, cottage”). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kutą (“hut, hovel”).
