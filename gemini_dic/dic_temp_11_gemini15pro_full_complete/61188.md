elusion:/ɪˈluːʒən/ | n. | 逃避；躲避；回避|The act of escaping or avoiding something, especially cleverly or skillfully.  An instance of this. | He managed an elusion of the police. 他设法躲避了警察。|  近义词：evasion, avoidance, escape | 反义词: confrontation, facing | noun

elusion:/ɪˈluːʒən/ | n. | 错觉；谬见；幻觉 | A deceptive belief or impression; a delusion. | He was under the elusion that he was popular. 他误以为自己很受欢迎。 | 近义词: illusion, delusion, misconception | 反义词: reality, truth | noun
