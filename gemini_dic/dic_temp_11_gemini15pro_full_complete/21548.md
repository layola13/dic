blueish:/ˈbluːɪʃ/| adj.|浅蓝色的，略带蓝色的|Slightly blue; tinged with blue.|The sky was a blueish grey. 天空是蓝灰色的。|The walls are painted a blueish green. 墙壁被漆成了蓝绿色。|近义词：bluish, light blue, pale blue|反义词：reddish, yellowish|词性:形容词

词源:  From blue + -ish.  The suffix "-ish" is used to form adjectives meaning "somewhat" or "slightly".  So, "blueish" literally means "somewhat blue".
