coffering:/ˈkɒfərɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkɔːfərɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|格子天花板; 凹格装饰; 镶板|A series of recessed panels in a ceiling, vault, or arch.  A decorative sunken panel in a ceiling, vault, or other structure.|The coffering in the cathedral ceiling was intricate and beautiful. 教堂天花板上的格子装饰精美绝伦。|synonyms: paneling, recessed panels, coffers|antonyms: smooth surface|noun
