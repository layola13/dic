ekarin: /ɛkəˈrɪn/
noun
鱼精蛋白，鱼精蛋白硫酸盐
A protein found in fish sperm, especially salmon and herring sperm, that is rich in arginine and has various uses, including as a pharmaceutical to reverse the effects of heparin.
Ekarin is used to reverse the effects of heparin after surgery.  鱼精蛋白用于手术后逆转肝素的作用。
Synonyms: protamine sulfate (most common synonym in a pharmaceutical context)
Antonyms: heparin (in terms of its anticoagulant effect)
n.
