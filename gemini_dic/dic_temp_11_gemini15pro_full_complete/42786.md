coupé:/kuːˈpeɪ/ (US) /ˈkuːpeɪ/ (UK)| n.|双门轿跑车|A car with a fixed roof, two doors, and usually a sloping back.|He drove off in his sleek new coupé.|近义词:sports car, two-door|反义词:sedan, four-door|词性:noun
Etymology: French, from couper 'to cut,' past participle of couper. Originally referred to a horse-drawn carriage with the front section cut away.
