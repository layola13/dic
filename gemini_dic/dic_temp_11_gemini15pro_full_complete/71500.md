flangers:/ˈflændʒərz/| n.|镶边效果器，镶边器|An electronic device or software that creates a flanging effect, a sweeping, whooshing sound, by mixing a slightly delayed copy of an audio signal with the original.|The guitarist used flangers to create a psychedelic sound. 吉他手使用镶边效果器来创造迷幻的声音。|近义词：phaser, chorus|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "clean sound" or "dry signal")|noun
