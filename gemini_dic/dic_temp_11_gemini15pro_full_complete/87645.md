hexacanth:/ˈhɛksəkænθ/| n. |六钩蚴，六钩胚|A larval stage of a tapeworm, characterized by six hooks. It is the oncosphere after it has developed hooks.  It is the infective stage for the intermediate host.|The hexacanth burrows through the intestinal wall of the intermediate host.|近义词：oncosphere (though technically a slightly earlier stage)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
