drowsy: /ˈdraʊzi/ | adj. | 昏昏欲睡的；困倦的 | Feeling sleepy and lethargic. |  I felt drowsy after taking the medication.  我服药后感到昏昏欲睡。

|  The warm room made everyone drowsy. 温暖的房间让每个人都昏昏欲睡。 |  sleepy, lethargic, tired | alert, awake, energetic | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *drusian, from *drusi "heaviness, sluggishness."  Related to Old English dreosan "to fall, sink, decline."
