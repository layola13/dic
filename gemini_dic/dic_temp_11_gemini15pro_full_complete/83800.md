hairpin:/ˈhɛərˌpɪn/| adj.|急转弯的，发夹形的|Sharply curved, like a hairpin.|The hairpin turn on the mountain road was dangerous. 山路上急转弯很危险。|Synonyms: sharp, tight|Antonyms: straight, wide|adjective
