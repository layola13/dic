hyperintellectualnesses:/ˌhaɪpərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəlˌnɛsɪz/
|n.|过度理智；过度沉溺于智力活动|The state of being excessively intellectual or overly concerned with intellectual pursuits.|His hyperintellectualnesses made him seem aloof and out of touch with everyday concerns. 他过度的理智让他显得冷漠，与日常事务脱节。|近义词: intellectualism, cerebrality|反义词: practicality, pragmatism|词性: 名词

Etymology:
hyper- (Greek): over, excessive
intellectual (Latin intellectus, from intelligere "to understand"): relating to the intellect
-ness (Old English -nes): state or quality of being
