overdebilitate: /ˌoʊvər dəˈbɪlɪˌteɪt/
verb
过度削弱，使极度衰弱
To weaken severely; to cause someone or something to become extremely feeble.
The long illness overdebilitated him, making even simple tasks difficult.  长期疾病使他极度衰弱，甚至连简单的任务都难以完成。
The economy was overdebilitated by the trade war. 贸易战使经济极度衰弱。
近义词: enfeeble, weaken, debilitate, exhaust, sap
反义词: strengthen, invigorate, empower
