isolating:/ˈaɪsəleɪtɪŋ/ /ˈaɪsəˌleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 隔离的，孤立的 | Causing someone to be or feel alone and without friends or help. |  The isolating nature of his work made him feel lonely. 他的工作的孤立性使他感到孤独。|


isolating:/ˈaɪsəleɪtɪŋ/ /ˈaɪsəˌleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 导致隔离的 |  Creating a situation where something is separate and alone. | The government implemented isolating measures to contain the spread of the virus. 政府实施了隔离措施以遏制病毒的传播。


ETYMOLOGY->
From isolate + -ing. Isolate comes from the Italian "isolato" (past participle of "isolare," meaning "to isolate"), derived from "isola," meaning "island," from the Latin "insula."
