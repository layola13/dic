breaming:/ˈbriːmɪŋ/ | adj. | 洋溢的，满面的，盈满的 | Filled to the point of overflowing, especially with happiness, pride, or good health. |  She was beaming with pride after her son's graduation. 她儿子毕业后，她脸上洋溢着骄傲。 | synonyms: radiant, glowing, joyful, bright | antonyms: gloomy, sad, depressed | adjective

Etymology: From Middle English *bremen, from Old English brēman (“to make famous, celebrate, extol, roar, resound, rage”), from Proto-Germanic *brōmijaną (“to roar, rage, be noisy”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰrem- (“to roar, resound, hum”).
