expansionist:/ɪkˈspænʃənɪst/
adj. n.|扩张主义的；扩张主义者|Relating to or characteristic of a policy of territorial or economic expansion.  (adj.) A person who advocates or practices expansionism. (n.)|The government pursued an expansionist foreign policy. (adj.)  He was known as an aggressive expansionist. (n.)|近义词：imperialist, aggressive, acquisitive|反义词:pacifist, isolationist|词性:adjective, noun
Etymology: Late 18th century: from expansion + -ist.
