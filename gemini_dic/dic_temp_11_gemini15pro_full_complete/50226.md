depend:/dɪˈpend/| v. |依靠，依赖，取决于|To rely on someone or something for support, help, or supply; to be contingent on.|例句：My success depends on hard work. 我的成功取决于努力工作。|近义词：rely, count on, hinge on|反义词:support, sustain|词性:动词

depend:/dɪˈpend/| v. |信赖，信任|To trust or have confidence in.|例句：You can depend on him to be there for you. 你可以相信他会支持你。|近义词：trust, believe in|反义词:distrust, doubt|词性:动词
