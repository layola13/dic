herschelian:/hɜːrˈʃɛliən/
adjective
赫歇耳式的，赫歇耳的
Of or relating to Sir William Herschel, his work, or his telescope.  Relating to or characteristic of Herschel or his work.  Specifically referring to a telescope design with a single concave mirror tilted to reflect light to an eyepiece at the side, avoiding the need for a secondary mirror to intercept the light path.
The observatory boasted a massive herschelian telescope, allowing astronomers to peer deeper into the cosmos.  该天文台拥有一台巨大的赫歇耳式望远镜，使天文学家能够更深入地观察宇宙。
Herschel's observations of Uranus were made using a herschelian telescope of his own design. 赫歇尔利用自己设计的赫歇耳望远镜观测了天王星。
近义词:reflecting (reflecting telescope)
反义词:refracting (refracting telescope)
词性:adjective
