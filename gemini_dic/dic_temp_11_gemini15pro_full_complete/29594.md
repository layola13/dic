catechu: /ˈkætɪtʃuː/ | n. | 儿茶，儿茶膏 | A reddish-brown extract from acacia trees used for tanning and dyeing and medicinally as an astringent. |  Catechu was once an important ingredient in traditional medicine. 儿茶曾经是传统医学中的重要成分。

Catechu gives a rich brown color to textiles. 儿茶赋予纺织品丰富的棕色。| 近义词: tannin, cutch | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly a bleaching agent) | 词性: 名词
