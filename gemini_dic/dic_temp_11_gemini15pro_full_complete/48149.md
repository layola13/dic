decolorant:/diːˈkʌlərənt/ | n. | 脱色剂；漂白剂 |A chemical substance that removes color or bleaches something. | The decolorant quickly removed the stain from the fabric.  这种脱色剂很快地去除了织物上的污渍。| synonyms: bleach, bleaching agent | antonyms: colorant, dye | noun

decolorant:/diːˈkʌlərənt/ | adj. |  脱色的；漂白的 | Having the ability to remove color or bleach. |  A decolorant solution was used to prepare the sample.  使用脱色溶液制备样品。 | synonyms: bleaching, decoloring | antonyms: coloring, dyeing | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + colorant (noun meaning "a substance that imparts color").
