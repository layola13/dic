imparting:/ɪmˈpɑːtɪŋ/
verb
传授，告知；给予，赋予
communicating information or knowledge; giving a particular quality to something
The teacher is imparting knowledge to her students. 老师正在向她的学生传授知识。
He imparted a sense of urgency to the meeting. 他给会议增添了一种紧迫感。
Synonyms: communicating, conveying, transmitting, bestowing, conferring
Antonyms: withholding, concealing, suppressing
v.
