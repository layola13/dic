commandingness:/kəˈmændɪŋnəs/| n. |威严，气势， commanding 的名词形式|The commandingness of her presence silenced the room. 她威严的气场让整个房间鸦雀无声。|authority, dominance, presence, majesty |submissiveness, meekness |noun

Etymology->

From commanding + -ness.  Commanding ultimately derives from the Latin word *commandāre*, meaning "to entrust, commit, order". The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
