epiphyllospermous:/ˌɛpɪˈfɪləʊspɜːməs/
adjective
附生种子的，叶上生种子的
(of a plant) bearing seeds on the leaves, as certain ferns.  A plant that develops seeds on its leaves.
例句：Epiphyllospermous ferns reproduce by producing spores on their leaves. 附生种子的蕨类植物通过在其叶子上产生孢子来繁殖。

例句：The researcher discovered a new species of epiphyllospermous plant in the rainforest.  这位研究人员在雨林中发现了一种新的附生种子植物。
近义词：foliicolous (叶生的)
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, as it describes a specific reproductive strategy.)
词性: adjective
