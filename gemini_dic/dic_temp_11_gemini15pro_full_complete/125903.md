musculocutaneous: /ˌmʌskjʊloʊkjuˈteɪniəs/ | adj. | 肌皮的 | Relating to both muscles and skin, or to the muscles and the skin. | The musculocutaneous nerve supplies the biceps and brachialis muscles and the skin of the lateral forearm. 肌皮神经支配肱二头肌、肱肌和前臂外侧的皮肤。 |  近义词: myocutaneous | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

musculocutaneous: /ˌmʌskjʊloʊkjuˈteɪniəs/ | n. | 肌皮神经 | The nerve that supplies the biceps and brachialis muscles and the skin of the lateral forearm. | Damage to the musculocutaneous nerve can result in weakness in flexing the elbow and loss of sensation on the lateral forearm.  肌皮神经损伤会导致屈肘无力和前臂外侧感觉丧失。| 近义词: N/A | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
