formul:/ˈfɔːrmjʊl/ | n. | 公式，方程式；配方；准则；方案 | A set of symbols that expresses a mathematical rule or principle; a recipe or prescription; a fixed way of doing something. | The chemist followed a specific formula to create the compound.  化学家遵循特定的公式来制造这种化合物。| synonyms: equation, recipe, prescription, method, procedure | antonyms: improvisation, guesswork | noun

formul:/ˈfɔːrmjʊl/ | adj. | 公式的；程式化的 | Relating to or expressed by a formula; following a fixed or conventional method. |  The speaker gave a rather formulistic response. 演讲者给出了一个相当程式化的回答。 | synonyms: conventional, standard, routine, fixed | antonyms: original, innovative, creative | adjective
