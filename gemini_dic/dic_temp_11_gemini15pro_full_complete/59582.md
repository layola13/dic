ebracteolate: /iːbrækˈtiːəˌleɪt/
adjective
无苞片的
Lacking bracteoles.  Small leaf-like structures at the base of a flower stalk.
The ebracteolate flowers of this species are a key identifying feature.  这种植物无苞片的花朵是其关键识别特征。
ebracteolate inflorescence: 无苞片花序

Synonyms:  bracteole-less
Antonyms:  bracteolate, bracteate
word type: adjective
