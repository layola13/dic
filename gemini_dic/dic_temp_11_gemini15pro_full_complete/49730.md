democratizers:/dɪˈmɒkrətaɪzərz/
noun
民主化者，民主推动者
People or things that promote or cause democratization.
The internet is considered by some to be one of the greatest democratizers of information.  互联网被一些人认为是信息最伟大的民主化者之一。
New technologies are powerful democratizers in the world of art. 新技术是艺术世界强有力的民主化推动者。
synonyms: reformers, liberals, advocates
antonyms: authoritarians, dictators, oppressors
n.
