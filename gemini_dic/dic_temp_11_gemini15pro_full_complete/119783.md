mignonette:/ˌmɪnjəˈnɛt/ | n. | 香 mignonette; 木犀草; 小巧可爱的人或物 | A delicate annual plant (Reseda odorata) with spikes of small, fragrant, greenish-white flowers. Also: its fragrance.  |  The air was sweet with the scent of mignonette.  空气中弥漫着木犀草的香味。 | synonyms: sweet alyssum, sweet reseda | antonyms: N/A | noun


mignonette:/ˌmɪnjəˈnɛt/ | adj. | 小巧玲珑的；精致的 |  Dainty, delicate. (Less common usage) |  She wore a mignonette silver locket. 她戴着一个精巧的银质小盒式吊坠。| synonyms: dainty, delicate, petite | antonyms: large, bulky, coarse | adjective
