concentratedly:/ˈkɒntrənsteɪtɪdli/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsəntreɪtɪdli/ (US)| adv.|专心地，集中地|In a manner showing great concentration; intently.|She listened concentratedly to the lecture, taking detailed notes. 她专心致志地听着讲座，做了详细的笔记。|近义词：attentively, intently, earnestly, fixedly|反义词：inattentively, distractedly|词性:副词
词源: From the adjective "concentrated" + the adverbial suffix "-ly." "Concentrated" comes from the verb "concentrate," which derives from the Latin "concentrare," meaning "to bring to a center."
