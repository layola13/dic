lamaist:/ˈlɑːmeɪst/ | adj. n. | 拉迈佛教的；喇嘛教徒 | Relating to or characteristic of Lamaism, a form of Buddhism practiced in Tibet and Mongolia.  A follower of Lamaism. |  Example:  The lamaist monks chanted ancient prayers.  喇嘛教僧侣吟诵着古老的祈祷文。

Example: He is a devout lamaist. 他是一位虔诚的喇嘛教徒。 | 近义词: Tibetan Buddhist,  | 反义词: None (lack of a widely accepted direct antonym) | 词性: adjective, noun

词源: From Lama + -ist.  Lama comes from the Tibetan word "bla ma" meaning "superior one" or "guru".
