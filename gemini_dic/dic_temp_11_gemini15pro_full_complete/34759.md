cliffiest: /ˈklɪfiɪst/
adjective
最陡峭的，悬崖最多的
Having the most or steepest cliffs.
The cliffiest part of the coast was inaccessible by foot.  海岸线最陡峭的部分无法步行到达。
The cliffiest terrain posed a challenge for the hikers.  最陡峭的地形对徒步旅行者构成了挑战。
近义词: steepest, most precipitous
反义词: flattest, gentlest
词性: adjective

词源:
cliff (n.) + -y (adj. suffix) + -est (superlative suffix)

cliff (n.) from Old English *clif, clif, from Proto-Germanic *kliban (source also of Old Norse klif "cliff, rock," Middle Dutch clif "cliff, shore," Old High German klepfa "cliff," German Klippe "cliff, rock").

-y (adj. suffix) from Old English -ig.

-est (superlative suffix) from Old English -est.
