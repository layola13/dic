overexploiting: /ˌōvərɪkˈsplɔɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度开发，过度利用
excessively exploiting something, typically a natural resource, to the point of diminishing returns or damage.
The fishing industry is overexploiting the ocean's resources. 渔业正在过度开发海洋资源。

Overexploiting the soil can lead to desertification. 过度开发土壤会导致荒漠化。

近义词: overusing, overharvesting, abusing
反义词: conserving, protecting, preserving
词性: verb (present participle)

词源:
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "exploiting" (present participle of "exploit," from Old French "exploitier" meaning "to accomplish, achieve, make use of," ultimately from Latin "explicitare" meaning "to unfold, explain").
