excommunicant:/ˌɛks kəˈmjuːnɪkənt/
n.
被绝罚者；开除教籍者
A person who has been excommunicated, excluded from the fellowship of a church or religious community.
The excommunicant was no longer allowed to participate in religious services. 这个被逐出教会的人不再被允许参加宗教仪式。
近义词: outcast, pariah, heretic
反义词: member, believer, adherent
词性: 名词


excommunicate:/ˌɛks kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/
vt.
把…逐出教会；开除…的教籍
To officially exclude someone from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church.
The church excommunicated him for his heretical beliefs. 教会因为他异端的信仰而将他逐出教会。
近义词: banish, expel, ostracize
反义词: admit, include, welcome
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *excommunicant-, present participle stem of excommunicare "to excommunicate," from ex- "out" + communicare "to share, communicate," from communis "common."
