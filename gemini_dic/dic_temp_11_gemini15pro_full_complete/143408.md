outflanker:/ˌaʊtˈflæŋkər/
noun
包抄者，侧翼攻击者
Someone who outflanks, especially in a military context.  This person maneuvers around the side of an opponent to gain a strategic advantage.
The general sent out a group of outflankers to surprise the enemy. 将军派出一队侧翼包抄者突袭敌人。
近义词:flanker, attacker
反义词:defender
