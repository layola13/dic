neuropsychological:/ˌnʊəroʊˌsaɪkəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective|神经心理学的|Relating to the relationship between the nervous system and mental processes, especially in terms of brain function and behavior.|例句：Neuropsychological testing can help identify cognitive deficits after a stroke.  神经心理学测试可以帮助识别中风后的认知缺陷。|例句：The neuropsychological assessment revealed impairments in memory and attention. 神经心理学评估揭示了记忆力和注意力方面的损伤。|近义词：cognitive, mental|反义词:physical|词性:adjective

neuropsychological:/ˌnʊəroʊˌsaɪkəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
noun|神经心理学|The branch of psychology that deals with the relationship between the nervous system and mental processes, especially in terms of brain function and behavior.|例句：Neuropsychological research has shed light on the neural basis of language. 神经心理学研究揭示了语言的神经基础。|例句：Advances in neuropsychological imaging techniques have revolutionized the field. 神经心理学成像技术的进步彻底改变了该领域。|近义词：cognitive neuroscience|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
