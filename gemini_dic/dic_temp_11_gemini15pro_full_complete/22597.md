bootstrapped:/ˈbuːtˌstræpt/
v.|用自己的资源创业；自力更生|built or started with limited resources, often financial, and relying on one's own efforts rather than external help. |The company was bootstrapped from the ground up. 这家公司是白手起家的。|synonyms: self-funded, self-made, independent|antonyms: subsidized, funded, dependent|verb (past participle)

adj.|自力更生的；用有限资源建立的|built or started with limited resources, often financial, and relying on one's own efforts rather than external help.|They built a bootstrapped business without any outside investment. 他们在没有任何外部投资的情况下建立了一个自力更生的企业。|synonyms: self-funded, self-made, independent|antonyms: subsidized, funded, dependent|adjective

词源：来源于短语 "pull oneself up by one's bootstraps"，指的是一种不可能完成的任务，比喻通过自己的努力克服困难，取得成功。尽管这个短语的本意是指不可能的事情，但 "bootstrapped"  这个词被用来形容在没有外部帮助的情况下，依靠自己的有限资源创业或发展。
