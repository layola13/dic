griper:/ˈɡraɪpər/| n.|抱怨者，爱发牢骚的人|A person who complains constantly or habitually. |例句：He's such a griper, always finding something to moan about. 他真是个爱抱怨的人，总是能找到东西抱怨。

例句：Don't be such a griper; try to look on the bright side. 别总是抱怨了，试着看看好的一面。

|近义词：complainer, whiner, grumbler|反义词: optimist, enthusiast|词性: noun
