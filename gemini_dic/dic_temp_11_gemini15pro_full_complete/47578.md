debasedness:/dɪˈbeɪsɪdnəs/
n.
堕落；卑鄙；贬值
The state of being debased; moral corruption or degradation; a decline in value or quality.
The debasedness of the currency caused widespread economic hardship. 货币贬值造成了广泛的经济困难。
degradation, corruption, depravity, vileness
nobility, virtue, purity
noun


debase:/dɪˈbeɪs/
v.
降低；贬低；贬值
To reduce the quality or value of something; to lower the moral character of someone.
The politician was accused of debasing the office he held.  这位政治家被指控降低了他所担任的职位的尊严。
degrade, corrupt, demean, cheapen
elevate, enhance, ennoble, improve
verb
