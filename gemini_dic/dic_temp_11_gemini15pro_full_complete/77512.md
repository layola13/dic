gazon:/ɡæˈzɔːn/ (UK), /ɡəˈzɑːn/ (US)| n.|草坪，草地|A stretch of short, mown grass, typically found in a lawn or park.  A smooth expanse of short grass.|The children were playing on the gazon. 孩子们在草坪上玩耍。|The groundskeeper meticulously maintained the gazon.  场地管理员一丝不苟地维护着草坪。|Synonyms: lawn, turf, sward|Antonyms: wasteland, barren land|noun
