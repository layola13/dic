detected:/dɪˈtɛktɪd/ | v. |  已检测到，已发现 | Discovered or noticed something, usually something hidden or not easily obvious. | The software detected a virus on my computer.  该软件检测到我的电脑上有病毒。 |  discovered, found, identified, noticed, observed | missed, overlooked | verb

detected:/dɪˈtɛktɪd/ | adj. |  被检测到的，被发现的 |  Something that has been discovered or noticed. | The detected error was minor.  检测到的错误很小。 |  discovered, found, identified, noticed, observed | undetected, hidden | adjective
