lutulent: /ˈluːtjʊlənt/ | adj. |浑浊的；污秽的；含糊不清的 | Muddy; turbid; thick or opaque; also, figuratively, unclear or confused. |The river, swollen by the recent rains, was brown and lutulent.  这条河由于最近的降雨而上涨，变得浑浊不堪。| The speaker's lutulent prose made it difficult to understand his point.  演讲者含糊不清的散文使人们难以理解他的观点。| 近义词: turbid, muddy, murky, opaque | 反义词: clear, transparent, limpid | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *lūtulentus*, from *lūtum* "mud."
