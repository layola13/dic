outrival:/ˌaʊtˈraɪvəl/
verb
胜过，超越
to surpass or defeat in competition or rivalry
例句：He outrivaled his competitors in the race and won the gold medal. 他在比赛中胜过对手，赢得了金牌。

例句：She strives to outrival her brother in academic achievements. 她努力在学业成就上超越她的哥哥。

近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, outstrip, beat, defeat
反义词：lose to, be defeated by, be inferior to
词性：v.
