overassertiveness:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɜːrtɪvnəs/
| n. | 过分自信; 过分武断;  |The state of being excessively assertive.  Asserting oneself in a manner that is overly forceful, aggressive, or presumptuous.|Her overassertiveness in the meeting alienated some of her colleagues. 她会议上过分的自信疏远了一些同事。|近义词：aggressiveness, pushiness, presumptuousness |反义词: meekness, humility, diffidence|noun
