inhume:/ɪnˈhjuːm/ /ɪnˈhjuːm/ | vt. |埋葬，安葬|To bury a corpse in the earth; inter.|例句：The king was inhumed with great ceremony. 国王被隆重安葬。

例句：They plan to inhume the time capsule next week. 他们计划下周埋葬时间胶囊。

近义词：bury, inter, entomb |反义词:exhume, disinter |词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin inhumāre ("to bury, inter"), from in ("in, into") + humāre ("to bury"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰem- ("earth").
