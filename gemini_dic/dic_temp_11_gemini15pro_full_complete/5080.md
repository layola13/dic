allahs:/ˈæləz/ | n. | 阿拉们（Allah 的复数形式，通常被认为是不正确的用法）|  A grammatically incorrect pluralization of Allah, the Arabic word for God.  The concept of Allah in Islam is strictly monotheistic, therefore a plural form is inappropriate and theologically incorrect. | This usage is generally considered incorrect and disrespectful.
