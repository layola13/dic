dryad:/ˈdraɪæd/ | n. | 树精，树仙 | A mythological spirit or nymph inhabiting a tree. |  The ancient Greeks believed that dryads protected the trees they lived in. 古希腊人相信树精保护着它们居住的树木。

 The dryad emerged from the oak, her form shimmering in the moonlight.  月光下，树精从橡树中出现，身影闪烁。| 近义词：nymph, wood nymph, tree spirit | 反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑与破坏自然的形象对应，例如：logger 伐木工人) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek δρυάς (druás), from δρῦς (drûs, “tree, oak”).
