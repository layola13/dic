acrotarsium:/əˌkroʊˈtɑːrsiəm/
noun
顶端膨大
The swollen or club-shaped tip of something, especially a fungal hypha.
The acrotarsium of the fungus was covered in spores.  这种真菌的顶端膨大布满了孢子。
Synonyms: apex, tip, extremity
Antonyms: base, bottom
词性: 名词
