contemporaneously:/kənˌtɛmpəˈreɪniəsli/
adverb
同时地
happening or existing at the same time
The two events occurred contemporaneously. 这两件事同时发生。

近义词:simultaneously, concurrently, at the same time
反义词:separately, at different times
词性:adverb
