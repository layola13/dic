macrophytic:/ˌmækroʊˈfɪtɪk/ | adj. | 大型植物的；肉眼可见植物的 | Relating to or consisting of macrophytes; large enough to be seen with the naked eye. | The lake was choked with macrophytic growth.  这个湖里长满了大型植物。| Synonym: macroscopic, mega, large | Antonym: microscopic, micro, small | adjective

macrophyte:/ˈmækroʊfaɪt/ | n. | 大型植物；肉眼可见植物 | An aquatic plant large enough to be seen with the naked eye. | Water lilies are a type of macrophyte. 睡莲是一种大型水生植物。| Synonym:  macroalga, aquatic plant | Antonym: microalga, phytoplankton | noun
