longinus:/lɒnˈdʒaɪnəs/ | n. |朗吉努斯|  Referring to Cassius Longinus, a rhetorician and philosophical critic of the 1st century AD, or to the anonymous author of *On the Sublime*, a treatise on literary criticism traditionally attributed to him. | Longinus is considered one of the most important figures in the history of literary criticism.  朗吉努斯被认为是文学批评史上最重要的人物之一。| synonym:  Cassius Longinus, Pseudo-Longinus | antonym: N/A | noun
