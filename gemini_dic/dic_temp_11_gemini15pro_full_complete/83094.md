gusset: /ˈɡʌsɪt/ | n. | 三角形衬布；腋下衬布；角撑板 | A piece of fabric inserted into a garment to strengthen or enlarge a part of it, such as the crotch of trousers or the underarm of a shirt. |  The gusset in the trousers allows for greater freedom of movement. 裤子上的三角形衬布可以提供更大的活动自由。

The tent had a gusset to make it more spacious.  帐篷有一个角撑板，使它更宽敞。 | synonyms: insert, reinforcement, patch, panel | antonyms:  N/A (no direct antonym) | noun
