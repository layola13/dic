over-soaped:/ˌoʊvərˈsoʊpt/
adjective
过度肥皂的；肥皂用得过多的
Having used too much soap.
The over-soaped sweater felt stiff and scratchy after it dried.  这件毛衣肥皂用得太多了，干了之后又硬又痒。
The dog was over-soaped and smelled strongly of coconut. 这只狗被过度肥皂清洗，浑身散发着浓浓的椰子味。
synonyms: lathery, soapy
antonyms: rinsed, clean (in the sense of free from soap)
词性: 形容词
