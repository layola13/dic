deludedly:/dɪˈluːdɪdli/
adverb
妄想地，受骗地
In a deluded manner; under the influence of a delusion.
He deludedly believed he could fly. 他妄想地认为自己能飞。
She deludedly clung to the hope that he would return. 她痴迷地抱着他会回来的希望。
近义词:  erroneously, mistakenly, foolishly, wrongly
反义词: realistically, rationally, sanely, wisely
词性: adverb
词源: From the adjective "deluded" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Deluded" comes from the verb "delude," from Latin "deludere" (to mock, deceive).
