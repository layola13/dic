deistic: /deɪˈɪstɪk/ /diːˈɪstɪk/
adjective
自然神论的
Pertaining to or characteristic of deism; acknowledging the existence of a God but denying revealed religion, supernatural intervention, etc.
Thomas Jefferson held deistic beliefs. 托马斯·杰斐逊持有自然神论的信仰。
近义词: rationalistic, freethinking
反义词: theistic, orthodox
词性: adjective
