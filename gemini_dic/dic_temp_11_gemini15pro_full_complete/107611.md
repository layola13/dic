landdrost:/ˈlɑːnddrɒst/| n. |地方行政官，地方官|A local magistrate or administrator in South Africa during the Dutch colonial period.  A landdrost was a government official responsible for administering a district, similar to a magistrate or prefect.|例句：The landdrost heard the case and made his judgment. 地方行政官听取了案件并作出了判决。

例句：The landdrost was responsible for collecting taxes and maintaining order. 地方行政官负责收税和维持秩序。|近义词：magistrate, administrator, prefect|反义词:citizen, resident|词性:noun
