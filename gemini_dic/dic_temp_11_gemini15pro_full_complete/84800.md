handspring:/ˈhændsprɪŋ/| n.|手翻，翻筋斗|An acrobatic movement in which the body turns over forward or backward, supported by the hands on the ground.|She did a perfect handspring on the mat. 她在垫子上做了一个完美的手翻。|近义词：cartwheel, flip, somersault|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "stand still")|noun
