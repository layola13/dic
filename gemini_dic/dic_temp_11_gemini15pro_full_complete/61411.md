embodiment:/ɪmˈbɒdimənt/ (US) /ɪmˈbɑːdimənt/ (UK)| n.| 体现;化身;具体表现|a tangible or visible form of an idea, quality, or feeling|例句：She is the embodiment of kindness. 她就是善良的化身。

例句：This painting is an embodiment of the artist's vision. 这幅画是艺术家愿景的体现。

近义词：personification, incarnation, representation, manifestation, exemplar, epitome|反义词:abstraction|词性:名词
