constituter:/ˈkɒnstɪtjuːtər/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnstɪtuːtər/ (US)| n.|构成者，组成者|A person or thing that constitutes something.|The constituents of the mixture are sugar, flour, and eggs. 这种混合物的成分是糖、面粉和鸡蛋。|Synonyms: component, element, ingredient|Antonyms: N/A|noun
