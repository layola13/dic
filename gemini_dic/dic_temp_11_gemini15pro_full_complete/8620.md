anticolonialist:/ˌæntiˈkəˈloʊniəlɪst/
n. adj.|反殖民主义者；反殖民主义的|A person who opposes colonialism; opposed to colonialism. |例句：The anticolonialist movement gained momentum after World War II. 二战后，反殖民主义运动势头强劲。|近义词：nationalist, freedom fighter, independence advocate|反义词：colonialist, imperialist|词性: 名词，形容词

anticolonialist:/ˌæntiˈkəˈloʊniəlɪst/
