gaining:/ˈɡeɪnɪŋ/| v. |获得，赢得；增加；(钟表)走快；到达|The company is gaining market share. 这家公司正在赢得市场份额。|近义词：acquiring, obtaining, earning, increasing, achieving, winning|反义词：losing, forfeiting, decreasing, failing|词性:动词
词源: From Middle English *gaynen, *gainen, from Anglo-Norman gaigner, geigner (“to gain, win, conquer, earn, obtain”), from Old North French gaainier (“to procure, obtain”), from Frankish *waidanjan (“to forage, hunt, graze, pasture”), from Proto-Germanic *waidjanōną (“to graze, pasture, forage, hunt”), from *waiþiz (“hunt”) + *-anōną (suffix forming verbs). Cognate with Old English wāþian (“to hunt, wander, roam”). More at wade.
