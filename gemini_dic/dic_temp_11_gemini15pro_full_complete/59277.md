eagers:/ˈiːɡərz/ | adj. | 热切的，渴望的 |  The eager students listened attentively to the lecture. | The children were eager to open their presents. 孩子们渴望打开他们的礼物。 | 近义词: enthusiastic, keen, avid, impatient | 反义词: apathetic, indifferent, reluctant | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *eger, from Old French eigre, egre (Modern French aigre), from Latin acer (“sharp, pungent, keen, severe”).
