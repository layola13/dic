nigraniline:/ˌnaɪɡrəˈænɪlɪn/| n. |  苯胺黑;  黑色苯胺; |A dark green crystalline powder, C6H4(NH)2,  formed by oxidation of aniline, used especially as a base for dyes.|Nigraniline is a crucial component in certain conductive polymers.|近义词:aniline black|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:

* "nigr-" comes from Latin "niger," meaning "black."
* "-aniline" refers to the chemical compound aniline, from which nigraniline is derived.
