beamlike:/ˈbiːmlaɪk/
adjective
像光束的，光束状的
Resembling a beam of light; radiating or extending in a straight, narrow line.
The lighthouse emitted a beamlike ray of light across the dark waters. 灯塔在黑暗的水面上发出光束般的光线。
The searchlight cast a beamlike light into the night sky. 探照灯向夜空投射出一束光柱般的光。
近义词:raylike, shaftlike, linear
反义词:diffuse, scattered, spread out
词性:adjective
