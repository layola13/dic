dynamometry: /ˌdaɪnəˈmɒmətri/ (US) /ˌdaɪnəˈmɒmɪtri/ (UK)| n.|测力法；测力学|The measurement of force or power, especially of muscles.|Example: Handgrip dynamometry is a simple way to assess muscle strength.  握力测力法是评估肌肉力量的简单方法。|Example:  The use of dynamometry in assessing athletic performance is becoming increasingly common. 在评估运动员表现方面，测力法的使用越来越普遍。|Synonym: ergometry, dynamometrics|Antonym: N/A|Noun

dynamometer: /ˌdaɪnəˈmɒmɪtər/ (US) /ˌdaɪnəˈmɒmɪtər/ (UK)| n.|测力计；功率计|An instrument for measuring force or power, especially of muscles or engines.|Example: The engineer used a dynamometer to test the engine's output. 工程师使用测力计来测试发动机的输出功率。|Example:  A dynamometer is used to measure the torque and rotational speed of an engine. 测力计用于测量发动机的扭矩和转速。|Synonym: ergometer|Antonym: N/A|Noun
