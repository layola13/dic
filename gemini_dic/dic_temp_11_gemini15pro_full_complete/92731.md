ichnoscopy: /ɪkˈnɒskəpi/ /ɪkˈnɑːskəpi/ | n. | 足迹学；遗迹学 | The study of trace fossils, such as footprints, burrows, and trails, to understand past life and environments. |  Ichnoscopy can reveal details about the behavior and ecology of extinct animals.  足迹学可以揭示已灭绝动物的行为和生态的细节。|  近义词: paleoichnology | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | noun
