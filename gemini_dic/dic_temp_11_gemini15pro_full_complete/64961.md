estheticizes:/ɛsˈθɛtɪsaɪzɪz/
verb
美化；使审美化
To represent something in a way that emphasizes its beauty or artistic qualities; to make something aesthetically pleasing.
The photographer estheticizes the harsh realities of war by focusing on the resilience of the human spirit. 摄影师通过聚焦人类精神的韧性来美化战争的残酷现实。

The film estheticizes violence, making it seem stylish and glamorous. 这部电影美化了暴力，使其显得时尚和迷人。

Synonym: beautifies, glorifies, romanticizes
Antonym: uglifies, deforms, distorts
词性: 动词
