coccobacterium:/ˌkɒkəʊbəˈsɪliəm/ | n. | 球杆菌 | A bacterium that is intermediate in form between a coccus and a bacillus.  A short, thick, slightly oval, rod-shaped bacterial cell. | Coccobacilli can cause various infections. 球杆菌可以引起各种感染。 |  The lab identified the bacteria as a type of coccobacterium.  实验室鉴定出这种细菌是一种球杆菌。 | near synonyms:  coccoid rod, ovoid rod | antonyms: coccus, bacillus | noun
