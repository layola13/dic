endocardium:/ˌɛndəˈkɑːrdiəm/| n. |心内膜|The thin, smooth membrane which lines the inside of the chambers of the heart and forms the surface of the valves.|The endocardium is inflamed in cases of endocarditis.|近义词:lining (generic), inner layer|反义词:epicardium (outer layer of the heart)|noun
