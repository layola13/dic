amphigamous:/æmˈfɪɡəməs/
adjective
两性生殖的
Reproducing sexually, involving the fusion of male and female gametes.
Amphigamous reproduction is common in most plants and animals. 两性生殖在大多数动植物中很常见。
The amphigamous life cycle of ferns involves both spores and gametes. 蕨类植物的两性生殖生命周期包括孢子和配子。
近义词:  sexual
反义词: asexual, agamous
词性: adjective
