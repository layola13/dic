cavelike:/ˈkeɪvˌlaɪk/
adjective
像洞穴的；洞穴般的
Resembling a cave; cavernous.
The room was dark and cavelike. 房间里很暗，像洞穴一样。
The narrow passage led into a cavelike chamber. 狭窄的通道通向一个像洞穴一样的房间。
近义词: cavernous, dark, dim, gloomy
反义词: bright, light, airy, open
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From cave + -like.  "Cave" comes from Old French "cave"  and Latin "cava" (feminine of "cavus" meaning "hollow"). "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling, similar to."
