nondeceptivenesses:/ˌnɒndɪˈsɛptɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
无欺骗性；真实性；诚实
The quality or state of being nondeceptive; truthfulness; honesty.
The contract emphasized the nondeceptivenesses of the advertising claims.  该合同强调了广告声明的真实性。
The company prides itself on the nondeceptivenesses of its business practices.  该公司以其商业行为的诚实为荣。
近义词:honesty, truthfulness, sincerity, integrity, candor
反义词:deceptiveness, dishonesty, deceit, fraud, guile
词性:noun

词源:  "nondeceptivenesses" comes from "non-" (not) + "deceptive" (tending to mislead) + "-ness" (forming nouns expressing state or quality) + "-es" (plural suffix).  "Deceptive" ultimately derives from the Latin word "capere" (to take, seize).
