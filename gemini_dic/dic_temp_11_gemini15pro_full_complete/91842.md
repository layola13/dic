词源:  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, above, beyond," derived from Greek. "Hilarious" comes from the Greek word "hilaros," meaning "cheerful, merry."  "Hyperhilarious" is a combination of these two, intensifying the meaning of "hilarious."
