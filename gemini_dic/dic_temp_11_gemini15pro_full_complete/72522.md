flusher:/ˈflʌʃər/ (US), /ˈflʌʃə/ (UK)| n.|冲洗器；脸红的人|A person or device that flushes something, especially a toilet.  A person whose face flushes easily.|例句：The plumber installed a new toilet flusher. 水管工安装了一个新的马桶冲水器。

例句：He's a terrible flusher; any slight embarrassment turns his face bright red. 他很容易脸红；任何轻微的尴尬都会让他脸红得发亮。|近义词：cleaner, purger (for devices), blusher (for people)|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
