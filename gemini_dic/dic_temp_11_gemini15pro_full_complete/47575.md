debars:/dɪˈbɑːrz/
verb
禁止，阻止，排除
officially prevents someone from doing something
The committee debarred him from running for office. 委员会禁止他竞选公职。
Synonyms: prohibit, forbid, preclude, prevent, exclude
Antonyms: allow, permit, authorize
词性: 动词
