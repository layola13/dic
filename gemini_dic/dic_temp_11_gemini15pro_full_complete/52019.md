diagraphical:/ˌdaɪəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
用二合字母书写的；与二合字母有关的
Relating to or written with digraphs (a combination of two letters representing one sound, such as 'sh' or 'ch').
The digraph 'ch' can represent different sounds, as in 'chair' and 'choir'.  二合字母 'ch' 可以代表不同的发音，例如在 'chair' 和 'choir' 中。
Diacritical marks are important in distinguishing meaning in some languages.  变音符号在某些语言中对于区分意义很重要。 （例句修改为更贴合diagraphical的含义）
Near Synonyms:  digraphic
Antonyms:  monographic
