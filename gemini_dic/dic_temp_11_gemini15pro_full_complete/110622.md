limpic: /ˈlɪmpɪk/
adjective|边缘系统的；大脑边缘系统的|Relating to the limbic system, a complex system of nerves and networks in the brain, involving several areas near the edge of the cortex concerned with instinct and mood. | The limbic system plays a crucial role in emotional responses. 边缘系统在情绪反应中起着至关重要的作用。 |近义词: emotional, instinctive|反义词: cognitive, rational|词性: adjective
