calescence:/kæˈlɛsəns/ /kəˈlɛsəns/
n.
加热；发热；白热化
The process of becoming hot; the state of being hot.
The calescence of the metal during forging made it malleable.  金属在锻造过程中发热使其具有延展性。

近义词:heating, warming, incandescence
反义词:cooling, refrigeration

词性:noun
