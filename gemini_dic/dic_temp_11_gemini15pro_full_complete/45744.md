curvograph: /ˈkɜːrvəɡrɑːf/ | n. | 曲线图，曲线记录器 | An instrument for drawing curves, especially one for automatically recording variations in a quantity such as temperature or blood pressure. | The curvograph showed a steady increase in the patient's heart rate. 曲线图显示病人的心率在持续上升。 | The curvograph recorded the fluctuating temperatures throughout the day.  曲线记录器记录了一整天温度的波动。 |近义词：graph, chart, plotter |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "table" or "data list" for contrasting representation methods) |词性: noun
