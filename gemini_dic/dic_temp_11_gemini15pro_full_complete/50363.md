depopularizing:/diːˈpɒpjələˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
使不受欢迎；降低...的受欢迎程度
reducing the popularity of something
The new tax policy is depopularizing the government. 新的税收政策使得政府越来越不受欢迎。
The mayor's controversial decision is depopularizing him with voters. 市长备受争议的决定使他在选民中失去了人气。
近义词: marginalizing, alienating, isolating
反义词: popularizing, promoting
词性: verb (present participle)

depopularize:/diːˈpɒpjələˌraɪz/
verb
使不受欢迎；降低...的受欢迎程度
to make something less popular
The rising crime rate is depopularizing the neighborhood.  上升的犯罪率使这个社区越来越不受欢迎。
The celebrity's scandalous behavior depopularized his brand.  这位名人的丑闻行为损害了他的品牌形象。
近义词: undermine, discredit, damage
反义词: popularize, boost, enhance
词性: verb

词源: de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + popularize (verb meaning "to make popular")
