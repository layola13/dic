aliphatic:/ˌælɪˈfætɪk/| adj.|脂肪族的|Relating to or denoting organic compounds in which carbon atoms form open chains (as in the alkanes), not aromatic rings.|例句：Methane is an aliphatic hydrocarbon. 甲烷是一种脂肪烃。|例句：Aliphatic compounds are commonly found in petroleum. 脂肪族化合物常见于石油中。|近义词：non-aromatic, acyclic|反义词:aromatic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *aleiphar, *aleiphat- ‘fat, oil’ + -ic.
