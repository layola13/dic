词源：
"gnattier" is the comparative form of "gnatty," which means annoying or irritating like a gnat.  "Gnatty" comes from "gnat," the small flying insect, plus the suffix "-y" which forms adjectives.  The comparative suffix "-er" is added to form "gnattier."
