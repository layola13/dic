maaden:/ˈmɑːdən/| n. |矿，矿山，矿藏|A maaden is a place where minerals or ores are extracted from the earth.|The copper maaden employed hundreds of workers.|近义词：mine, quarry, pit|反义词:None|词性:noun
maden: /ˈmɑːdən/ | adj. |由矿物制成的，矿物的|Made of or containing minerals.|Maden deposits are a valuable resource.|近义词：mineral, metallic, earthy|反义词:None|词性:adjective
