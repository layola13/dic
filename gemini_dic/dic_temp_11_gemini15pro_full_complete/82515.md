如果你是想了解单词 what 的用法，我可以提供以下信息：

what: /wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American) | pron. det. adv. | 什么，多么 | used to ask for information about something |

英文解析:  A word used to ask a question about something or to refer to something when you do not know its name or when the name is not important.  It can function as a pronoun, determiner, or adverb.

例句：
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you want for dinner? = 你晚餐想吃什么？

近义词: which, whatever
反义词:  (What doesn't have a direct antonym as it's a question word.)
词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb
