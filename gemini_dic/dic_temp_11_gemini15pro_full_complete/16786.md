basanite:/ˈbæsəˌnaɪt/ | n. | 玄武岩,试金石 |A hard, dark, fine-grained volcanic rock, similar to basalt, but containing a small amount of feldspathoids. It was used in ancient times as a touchstone to test the purity of gold. |Basanite is a common volcanic rock found in many parts of the world. 玄武岩是一种常见的火山岩，在世界许多地方都能找到。| The jeweler used a piece of basanite to test the gold. 珠宝商用一块玄武岩来测试黄金。 |Synonyms: basalt, touchstone| Antonyms: N/A | Noun

basanite:/ˈbæsəˌnaɪt/ | n. | 试金石 |A hard black siliceous stone, such as Lydian stone, formerly used to test the purity of gold and silver by observing the color of the streak produced on it. |The alchemist used basanite to verify the quality of his gold. 炼金术士用试金石来验证他的黄金的质量。| The basanite revealed that the coin was not pure gold. 试金石显示这枚硬币不是纯金。| Synonyms: touchstone, Lydian stone| Antonyms: N/A | Noun
