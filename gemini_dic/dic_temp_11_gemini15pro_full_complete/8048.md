anthropoidal:/ˌænθrəˈpɔɪdəl/
adjective
类人的；似人的
resembling a human, especially an ape.
例句：The robot had an anthropoidal form.  这个机器人具有人形。

例句：The creature's anthropoidal features were striking.  这个生物的类人特征很引人注目。

近义词：humanoid, hominoid, apelike, simian
反义词：non-human, beastly, animalistic
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *anthropoïde*, from *anthropo-* (from Ancient Greek ἄνθρωπος (ánthrōpos, “human being”)) + *-oïde* (from Ancient Greek -ειδής (-eidḗs, “-like, resembling”)).
