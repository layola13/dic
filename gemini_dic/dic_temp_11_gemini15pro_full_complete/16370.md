barbermonger:/ˈbɑːrbərmʌŋɡər/| n. |理发师；庸医；江湖郎中|A barber-surgeon; a person who pretends to skill in surgery; a quack.  (Often used contemptuously.)|He called himself a doctor, but everyone knew he was just a barbermonger, peddling useless remedies.|Near synonyms: quack, charlatan, mountebank|Antonyms:  physician, surgeon, doctor|noun
