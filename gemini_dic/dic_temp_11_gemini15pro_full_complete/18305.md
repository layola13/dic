bellbird:/ˈbelbɜːrd/ (US) /ˈbelbɜːd/ (UK)| n. |铃鸟|A bird known for its bell-like song.|例句：The bellbird's song echoed through the forest. 铃鸟的歌声在森林中回荡。

例句：We heard the distinctive call of a bellbird. 我们听到了铃鸟独特的叫声。|近义词：honeyeater (some species) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bell + bird.  The name refers to the clear, resonant song of these birds, which resembles the ringing of a bell.
