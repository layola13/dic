anhinga:/ænˈhɪŋɡə/| n.|蛇鸟，美洲蛇鸟|A large, dark-colored waterbird with a long, slender neck and sharp bill, also known as the American darter or snakebird.  It is known for its habit of swimming with its body submerged, leaving only its head and neck above water, giving it a snake-like appearance.|例句：The anhinga patiently stalked fish in the shallows.  蛇鸟在浅滩耐心地潜行捕鱼。

例句：We saw an anhinga drying its wings on a branch. 我们看到一只蛇鸟在树枝上晾晒翅膀。|近义词：darter, water turkey|反义词: (No direct antonym, as it refers to a specific species.)|词性:noun
