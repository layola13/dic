girdlelike:/ˈɡɜːrdlˌlaɪk/| adj.| 腰带状的，环状的|Resembling a girdle; belt-shaped.  Having the form or appearance of a girdle; encircling like a belt.|The snake coiled around the branch in a girdlelike fashion.|近义词：beltlike, bandlike, ringlike|反义词: linear, straight|词性: adjective
Etymology: From girdle + -like.  Girdle comes from Old English *gyrdel, from Proto-Germanic *gurdilaz.
