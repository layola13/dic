moat:/moʊt/| n. |护城河，壕沟|A deep, wide ditch surrounding a castle, fort, or town, typically filled with water and intended as a defense against attack. |例句：The castle was protected by a deep moat. 这座城堡被一条深深的护城河保护着。

例句：The moat was once filled with alligators. 这条护城河曾经满是鳄鱼。|近义词：ditch, trench, canal, fosse|反义词: bridge (not a true antonym, but represents the opposite function)|词性:noun
