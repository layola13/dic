nugation:/njuːˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 无稽之谈；无聊的事；琐事 |  An act, statement, or belief that has no value or basis in truth; nonsense.  |  His claims about the conspiracy were pure nugation. 他关于阴谋的说法纯属无稽之谈。 | synonyms: nonsense, absurdity, drivel, balderdash, twaddle | antonyms: sense, truth, fact | noun

nugatory:/ˈnjuːɡətɔːri/ | adj. | 无效的；无价值的；琐碎的 | Of no value or importance; trifling; useless. | The agreement was rendered nugatory by the change in law.  法律的变更使得协议失效。 | synonyms: invalid, void, worthless, futile, trivial | antonyms: valid, effective, important, significant | adjective
