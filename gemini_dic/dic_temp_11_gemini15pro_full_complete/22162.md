bombardments:/bɒmˈbɑːdmənts/ /bɑːmˈbɑːrdmənts/| n. |轰炸，炮击|The act or process of bombarding; a continuous attack with bombs, shells, or other missiles.|The city endured weeks of relentless bombardments.  这座城市遭受了数周无情的轰炸。|Synonyms: attacks, shelling, barrages, onslaughts|Antonyms: ceasefires, truces|noun

bombardment:/bɒmˈbɑːdmənt/ /bɑːmˈbɑːrdmənts/| n. |轰炸，炮击|An overwhelming quantity of something, as questions or requests.|He faced a bombardment of questions from the reporters. 他面临记者的连珠炮似的提问。|Synonyms: barrage, deluge, volley|Antonyms: trickle, dearth|noun
