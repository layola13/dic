hieroglyphic:/ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪk/
adjective|象形文字的|relating to or consisting of hieroglyphs.|例句：The ancient Egyptians used a hieroglyphic writing system. 古埃及人使用象形文字书写系统。|近义词：pictorial, symbolic|反义词:alphabetic|词性:adjective
hieroglyphic:/ˈhaɪərəɡlɪf/
noun|象形文字|a character used in hieroglyphic writing systems.|例句：The Rosetta Stone helped scholars decipher Egyptian hieroglyphics. 罗塞塔石碑帮助学者们破译了埃及象形文字。|近义词：symbol, ideogram|反义词:letter|词性:noun
