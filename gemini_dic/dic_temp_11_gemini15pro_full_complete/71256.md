five-star:/ˌfaɪv ˈstɑːr/ | adj. |五星级的；一流的|  Of the highest quality; rated as excellent.  It is derived from the rating system that uses stars to indicate quality, with five stars representing the best. | This is a five-star hotel with excellent service.  这是一家服务一流的五星级酒店。

| The restaurant received five-star reviews. 这家餐厅获得了五星好评。| 近义词：top-notch, first-class, deluxe, premium, luxury | 反义词：low-quality, inferior, poor | 词性：形容词
