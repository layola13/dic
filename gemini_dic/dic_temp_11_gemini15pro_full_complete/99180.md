intercirculated:/ˌɪntəˈsɜːkjəleɪtɪd/
adjective
相互流通的，互相循环的
distributed or circulated among a group or system, often reciprocally.
例句：The intercirculated funds helped stabilize the economies of both countries. 相互流通的资金帮助稳定了两国的经济。

例句：Intercirculated information ensured everyone was aware of the project's progress. 互相流通的信息确保每个人都了解项目的进展。
近义词：shared, exchanged, distributed, circulated
反义词：hoarded, restricted, confined, isolated
词性：adjective
