overlusciously: /ˌoʊvərˈlʌʃəsli/ | adv. | 过分甜美地，过分华丽地 | Excessively luscious; too sweet or rich, often to the point of being unpleasant or cloying.  Derived from "over" (meaning excessively) and "luscious" (meaning appealingly rich and sweet). | The dessert was overlusciously decorated with sugary flowers and excessive frosting.  这个甜点装饰得过于甜腻，满是糖花和过多的糖霜。| The romance novel was written overlusciously, filled with unrealistic declarations of love. 这部爱情小说写得过于甜腻，充满了不切实际的爱情宣言。 | 近义词: cloyingly, excessively, sickeningly | 反义词: plainly, simply, subtly | 词性: 副词
