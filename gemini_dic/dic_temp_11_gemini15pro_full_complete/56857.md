dorsiferous:/dɔːrˈsɪfərəs/
adjective
背部有…的；背上长有…的
Bearing or having something on the back.  Specifically, often used in biology to describe organisms carrying something on their dorsal surface.
The dorsiferous beetle carries its young on its back.  这只背部有幼虫的甲虫背着它的幼崽。
近义词: dorsal-bearing
反义词: ventral-bearing
词性: adjective

dorsiferous:/dɔːrˈsɪfərəs/
词源: From Latin *dorsum* (back) + *-fer* (bearing, carrying) + *-ous* (adjective ending)
