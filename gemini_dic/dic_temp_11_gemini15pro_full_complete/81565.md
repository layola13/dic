grazer:/ˈɡreɪzər/| n.|食草动物；放牧者；轻擦者|An animal that grazes; someone who grazes livestock; something that grazes (lightly touches or scrapes).|The cows are grazers in the field. 奶牛在田里吃草。|近义词：herbivore, feeder, browser|反义词:predator, carnivore|noun

graze:/ɡreɪz/| v. |放牧；吃草；轻擦；擦伤|To feed on growing grass; to feed livestock; to touch or scrape lightly in passing.|The sheep grazed peacefully in the meadow. 羊群在草地上平静地吃草。|近义词：browse, nibble, scrape|反义词: starve|verb

graze:/ɡreɪz/| n. |擦伤|A minor abrasion or scrape on the skin.|He suffered a graze on his knee when he fell. 他摔倒时膝盖擦伤了。|近义词：scratch, scrape, abrasion|反义词: gash|noun
