literati:/ˌlɪtəˈrɑːti/
noun
文人; 文人墨客;
Well-educated people who are interested in literature.
The event was attended by many of the town's literati.  这个活动有许多城里的文人墨客参加。
synonyms: intellectuals, scholars, men/women of letters
antonyms: illiterates, philistines
n.
