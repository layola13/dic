hungrier:/ˈhʌŋɡriər/ (US), /ˈhʌŋɡriə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更饿的，更渴望的|Comparative form of "hungry," meaning experiencing a greater desire or need for food, or metaphorically, for something else. |例句：I'm hungrier than I thought I would be. 我比我想象的要饿。

例句：He's hungrier for success than ever before. 他比以往任何时候都渴望成功。|近义词：more hungry, ravenous, starving (for food), desirous, eager (metaphorical)|反义词：less hungry, satiated, full (for food), apathetic, indifferent (metaphorical)|词性:形容词
