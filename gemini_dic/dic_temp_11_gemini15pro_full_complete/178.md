abature:/əˈbætʃər/ |n.|  屠宰场废物，（尤指）屠宰的下脚料|Offal or waste parts of slaughtered animals, especially the entrails.|The abature from the slaughterhouse was disposed of properly.| 近义词:offal, entrails, viscera, refuse | 反义词: (无明确的反义词，可考虑meat, carcass表示可食用的部分) | 词性: 名词


Etymology:
From French *abatture*, from abattre "to knock down, slaughter," from Old French abatre, from a- "down" + batre "to beat."
