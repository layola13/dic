baptism:/ˈbæptɪzəm/| n.|洗礼，浸礼|The act of baptizing someone, usually with water, as a religious rite or sacrament. It symbolizes purification, initiation, or admission into a faith.|Her son's baptism will be held next Sunday. 她儿子的洗礼将于下周日举行。|近义词：christening, initiation|反义词:None|词性:名词
