nonnegligent:/ˌnɑːnˈnɛɡlɪdʒənt/
adjective
无过失的；未疏忽的；非玩忽职守的
Not negligent; characterized by the absence of negligence.
The court found the driver nonnegligent in the accident. 法院认定司机在此次事故中没有过失。
The company was found nonnegligent in the handling of the hazardous materials.  公司被认定在处理危险材料方面没有过失。
近义词:blameless, innocent, faultless, irreproachable
反义词:negligent, careless, irresponsible
词性:adjective

词源: non- (not) + negligent (failing to take proper care)
