antecedent:/ˌæn.tɪˈsiː.dənt/| n. adj.| 先行词，先例；祖先|Something that existed before or logically precedes another|His antecedent in the family business was his grandfather. 他在家族企业中的先驱是他的祖父。|synonyms: predecessor, precursor, forerunner|antonyms: descendant, successor|noun

antecedent:/ˌæn.tɪˈsiː.dənt/| adj.| 先前的|Existing, happening, or coming before something else|The antecedent events led to the current crisis. 先前发生的事件导致了目前的危机。|synonyms: prior, previous, earlier|antonyms: subsequent, later, following|adjective
