hypotenuse:/haɪˈpɒtənjuːz/
n.
斜边
The longest side of a right-angled triangle, opposite the right angle.
The Pythagorean theorem states that the square of the hypotenuse is equal to the sum of the squares of the other two sides. 毕达哥拉斯定理指出，斜边的平方等于其他两条边的平方和。
近义词:longest side (in a right triangle)
反义词:leg (of a right triangle), cathetus
词性:名词
