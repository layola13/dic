词源：From Middle English *halteren, from Old English *healftrian (“to hobble, tie the legs together”), from Proto-Germanic *halb- (“half”) + *-tr- (“tree, wood”)  + *-janą (“to do, to make”). Related to halter (n.).  The sense development is likely from tying one leg halfway up.
