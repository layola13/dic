improvability:/ɪmˌpruːvəˈbɪləti/| n. |可改进性；改进的可能性|The improvability of the system is a key factor in its long-term success.  这个系统的可改进性是其长期成功的关键因素。|例句：The improvability of her writing skills is evident. 她的写作技巧的进步是显而易见的。|近义词：malleability, flexibility, plasticity|反义词：fixity, rigidity, immutability|词性:名词

Etymology: From "improve" + "-able" + "-ity".  "Improve" comes from Old French "emprover" (to make a profit from, to utilize), from "en-" (in) + "pro" (for) + "veir" (to turn). "-able" is a suffix meaning "capable of being," and "-ity" is a suffix forming nouns of quality or state.
