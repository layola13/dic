ETYMOLOGY->
From blue + -ish + -ness.  "Blue" traces back to Old French "bleu," ultimately from a Germanic source. "-ish" is a suffix indicating a slight degree of a quality. "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality.
