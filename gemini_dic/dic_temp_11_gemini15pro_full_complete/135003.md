nonnegation:/ˌnɑːnɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n.|非否定; 不否定|The principle of nonnegation states that a proposition cannot be both true and false at the same time.|This theory relies on the principle of nonnegation. 这个理论依赖于非否定的原则。|近义词:affirmation, assertion|反义词:negation|词性:名词

词源:  Formed from "non-" (not) + "negation" (the act of denying). "Negation" ultimately derives from Latin "negare" (to deny).
