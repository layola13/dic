kinglet:/ˈkɪŋlɪt/| n.| 戴菊鸟 |A very small songbird with a brightly coloured crown, found chiefly in coniferous forests.  A tiny king; a petty monarch. | We saw a golden-crowned kinglet flitting through the branches. 我们看到一只金冠戴菊在树枝间飞来飞去。|  Synonyms: wren, tit,  monarch(in the petty sense)| Antonyms:  giant, behemoth| noun

kinglet:/ˈkɪŋlɪt/| n.| 小国王; 不重要的君主|A person who holds royal power but has limited authority or influence.  |He was considered a mere kinglet, ruling over a small and insignificant territory. 他被认为只是一个统治着小而无足轻重领土的小国王。| Synonyms:  ruler, sovereign, petty king | Antonyms: emperor, high king | noun
