conditional:/kənˈdɪʃənəl/| adj. n.|有条件的；条件句；条件语|Depending on a condition; a grammatical mood used to express a proposition whose validity is dependent on some condition.|例句：Attendance at the meeting is conditional upon your submitting a report beforehand.  参加会议的条件是你事先提交一份报告。|近义词：dependent, contingent, provisional|反义词:unconditional, absolute|词性:形容词

conditional:/kənˈdɪʃənəl/| n.|条件句；条件语|A sentence or clause that expresses a condition, typically introduced by "if" or "unless".|例句：He used a lot of conditionals in his speech, exploring various hypothetical scenarios.  他的演讲中使用了大量的条件句，探讨了各种假设的场景。|近义词：proviso, stipulation|反义词:N/A|词性:名词
