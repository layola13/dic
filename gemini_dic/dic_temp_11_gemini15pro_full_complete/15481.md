badigeon:/bəˈdɪʒən/ | n. vt.|灰泥，涂料；粉饰，掩盖|A mixture of whiting, size, and water, used for coloring or coating walls or other surfaces; also, any similar mixture used as a coating.  The act of applying such a mixture. To coat or paint with badigeon.|The workers badigeoned the old wall with a fresh coat of white. 工人们用一层新的白色涂料粉刷了旧墙。|近义词:whitewash, plaster, paint, coat, cover, conceal, disguise|反义词:expose, reveal|词性:noun, verb

Etymology: From French *badigeon*, from Old French *bagier* "to moisten, soak," of uncertain origin.
