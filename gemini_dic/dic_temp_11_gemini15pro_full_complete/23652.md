brandisher:/ˈbrændɪʃər/| n. |挥舞者，炫耀者|Someone who brandishes something, typically a weapon.|The knight was a skilled brandisher of the sword.  这位骑士是一位熟练的舞剑者。|例句：The brandisher of the protest sign seemed determined to make his point.  举着抗议标语的挥舞者似乎决心要表达他的观点。

例句：The brandisher of the knife was quickly apprehended by the police.  挥舞着刀子的歹徒很快被警察逮捕了。|近义词：waver, wielder, flourisher|反义词: concealer, hider|词性: noun
