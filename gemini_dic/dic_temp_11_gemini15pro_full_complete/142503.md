osmund:/ˈɒzmənd/ | n. |紫萁|A fern of the genus Osmunda, having large bipinnate fronds and sporangia borne in branched clusters.|The royal osmund is a particularly striking fern. 紫萁是一种特别引人注目的蕨类植物。|Synonyms: fern, brake |Antonyms: N/A | Noun
