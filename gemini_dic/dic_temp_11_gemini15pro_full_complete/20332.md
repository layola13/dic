bishops:/ˈbɪʃəps/| n.|主教们|A senior member of the Christian clergy, usually in charge of a diocese and empowered to confer holy orders.|The bishops met to discuss the future of the church. 主教们开会讨论教会的未来。|Near synonyms: prelates, archbishops, clergy|Antonym: laity|Noun
