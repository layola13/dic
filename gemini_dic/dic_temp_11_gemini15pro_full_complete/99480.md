interferingness:/ˌɪntərˈfɪərɪŋnəs/
n.
干涉性，干预性
The quality or degree of being interfering; the tendency to interfere.  This word describes the inherent characteristic of something that is prone to causing interference or disruption.
His interferingness made it difficult for the team to work independently. 他的干涉性使团队难以独立工作。
Near Synonyms: interference, intrusion, meddling, intervention
Antonyms: noninterference, respect, autonomy
