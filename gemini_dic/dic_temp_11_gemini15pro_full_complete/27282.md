calumnious: /kəˈlʌmniəs/ | adj. | 诽谤的，中伤的 | Containing or constituting calumny; slanderous. Making false and defamatory statements about someone. |  The newspaper published a calumnious article about the politician. 这家报纸发表了一篇关于这位政治家的诽谤性文章。

Her calumnious remarks damaged his reputation. 她诽谤性的言论损害了他的名誉。

| 近义词: slanderous, defamatory, libelous, false, untrue, malicious,  | 反义词: complimentary, flattering, praising, laudatory | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *calumniōsus, from Latin calumnia (“false accusation, malicious prosecution, trickery”) + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives meaning ‘full of,’ ‘prone to,’ ‘characterized by,’ ‘like’).
