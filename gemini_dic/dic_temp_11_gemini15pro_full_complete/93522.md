imageries:/ˈɪmɪdʒəriz/
n.
意象，图像，比喻
mental images; the use of figurative language, especially in poetry or prose, to evoke a feeling or idea; descriptions and figures of speech
例句：The poet's imageries are vivid and evocative. 诗人的意象生动且引人入胜。

例句：The novel's rich imageries transported the reader to another world. 小说丰富的意象将读者带到了另一个世界。

近义词：images, imagery, figures of speech, metaphors, similes
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑"literal language" 字面语言)
词性: 名词(plural)

词源: From Old French *imagerie, from image + -erie. Ultimately from Latin imago.
