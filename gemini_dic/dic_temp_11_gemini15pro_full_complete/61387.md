emblazon:/ɪmˈbleɪzən/
verb
中文翻译：纹章装饰；宣扬；使华丽夺目
英文解析：To adorn or decorate with prominent markings or bright colors, often heraldic devices; to celebrate or proclaim publicly.
例句：The knight's shield was emblazoned with a golden lion. 骑士的盾牌上饰有金色的狮子。

例句：Her name was emblazoned across the headlines.  她的名字赫然印在头条新闻上。


近义词：adorn, decorate, ornament, celebrate, proclaim
反义词：obscure, conceal, hide
词性：v.
