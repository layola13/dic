corrode:/kəˈroʊd/ /kɒˈrəʊd/| v. |腐蚀，侵蚀|To destroy or damage slowly by chemical action.|Acid corrodes metal. 酸腐蚀金属。|The salt water corroded the pipes. 盐水腐蚀了管道。|近义词:erode, wear away, eat away|反义词:preserve, protect|verb

corrode:/kəˈroʊd/ /kɒˈrəʊd/| v. |(逐渐)损害，削弱|To gradually destroy or weaken something.|Confidence in the government has been corroded by the series of scandals.  一系列丑闻损害了政府的信誉。|His health was corroded by worry and anxiety. 他的健康被担忧和焦虑所损害。|近义词:undermine, weaken, impair|反义词:strengthen, bolster|verb
