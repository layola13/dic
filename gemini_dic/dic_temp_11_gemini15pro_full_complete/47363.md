deacons:/ˈdiːkənz/| n.|执事，助祭|A deacon is a member of the clergy who is ordained but not a priest.  A deacon typically assists a priest in their duties.|The deacons helped distribute communion. 执事们帮助分发圣餐。|The deacons collected the offerings. 执事们收集了捐款。|synonyms: church officer, church leader, minister, clergyman|antonyms: laity, congregation|noun
