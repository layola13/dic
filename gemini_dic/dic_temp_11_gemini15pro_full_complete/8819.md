antiestablishmentarianism: /ˌæntiɪˌstæblɪʃmənˈtɛəriənɪzəm/ /ˌæntiɪˌstæblɪʃmənˈtɛəriənɪzəm/
n.
反建制主义；反传统主义
Opposition to the establishment; the belief that established institutions and social norms should be challenged and changed.
The punk rock movement of the 1970s was fueled by antiestablishmentarianism.  20世纪70年代的朋克摇滚运动受到反建制主义的推动。

Her antiestablishmentarianism led her to protest against government policies. 她的反建制主义立场促使她抗议政府政策。
近义词: iconoclasm, nonconformity, radicalism, dissidence
反义词: conformism, traditionalism
词性: noun
