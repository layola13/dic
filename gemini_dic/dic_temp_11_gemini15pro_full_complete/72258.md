florulent:/ˈflɔːrələnt/ | adj. | 开花的；华丽的；辞藻华丽的 | Covered with flowers; flowery; ornate.  Characterized by a flowery or ornate style of writing or speaking. | The florulent meadows in springtime were a sight to behold. 春天里开满鲜花的草地是一道美丽的风景。 | synonyms: blooming, blossoming, flowery, ornate, flamboyant | antonyms: barren, plain, simple, unadorned | adjective
