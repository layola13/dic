gauntry:/ˈɡɔːntri/| adj.| 笨拙的；粗陋的；简陋的|Awkward and clumsy; crudely made or finished; roughly or poorly made.|The gauntry furniture looked out of place in the elegant room.  那笨重的家具在雅致的房间里显得格格不入。|近义词：clumsy, awkward, ungainly, crude, rough, rustic|反义词：elegant, refined, graceful, sophisticated|词性:形容词
Etymology:  Likely from dialectal English *gaunt, "lean, thin," influenced by words like "country" suggesting rustic simplicity and roughness. The connection might be that gaunt objects appear unfinished and roughly made.
