infraterritorial:/ˌɪnfrətɛrɪˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
领土内的；国内的
Existing or occurring within a territory, especially within the boundaries of a state or country.
The infraterritorial laws apply only to citizens within the country's borders.  领土内的法律只适用于该国境内的公民。

The government implemented new infraterritorial regulations regarding commerce. 政府实施了新的国内商业法规。

近义词:domestic, internal, within the country
反义词:extraterritorial, international, foreign

词性:adjective
