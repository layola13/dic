frizzly:/ˈfrɪzli/ /ˈfrɪzli/| adj.|卷曲的，毛糙的|Characterized by or having small, tight curls or waves, often in a messy or unruly way; wiry and brittle.|Her frizzly hair made it difficult to style.|近义词：kinky, wiry, nappy, fuzzy, unruly|反义词:straight, smooth, sleek|词性:形容词
词源:From frizz + -ly.  Frizz itself likely comes from a blend of "frieze" (a coarse woolen cloth) and words like "frazzle" suggesting a frayed or worn appearance.
