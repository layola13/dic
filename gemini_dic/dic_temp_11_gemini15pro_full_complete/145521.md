overcolor:/ˈoʊvərˌkʌlər/| v. n.|套色，覆盖颜色，过浓的色彩|To apply a transparent color over a base color; a color applied in this way.|The artist used an overcolor of blue to create a sense of depth. 艺术家用蓝色套色来营造一种深度感。|The overcolor gave the painting a richer tone.  套色使这幅画的色调更加丰富。|近义词：overlay, tint, glaze|反义词：undercolor, basecoat|词性：动词，名词
