lowerer:/ˈloʊərər/ (US), /ˈləʊərə(r)/ (UK)| n.|降低者，减低者|Someone or something that lowers something else. |The lowerer of the flag during the ceremony was a young veteran.  仪式上降旗的是一位年轻的退伍军人。

The automatic lowerer carefully placed the heavy box on the conveyor belt. 自动下降器小心地将沉重的盒子放在传送带上。|近义词：reducer, diminisher, decreaser|反义词：raiser, increaser, elevator|词性:noun
