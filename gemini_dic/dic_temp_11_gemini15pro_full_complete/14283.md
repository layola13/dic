autogrid:/ˈɔːtəʊɡrɪd/
noun|自动网格|A software tool used in computational chemistry to prepare input files for molecular simulations by generating a grid of points around a molecule to represent the potential energy surface.|The researcher used AutoGrid to prepare the system for docking studies.|Synonyms: grid generator, mesh generator|Antonyms: N/A|n.
