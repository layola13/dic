ineluctabilities:/ˌɪnɪˌlʌktəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |不可避免性；必然性；无法逃避的事|The quality or state of being ineluctable; inevitability.  Things that are unavoidable or inescapable.|The ineluctabilities of aging are a source of anxiety for many.  衰老的必然性是许多人焦虑的来源。|Synonyms: inevitabilities, necessities, certainties|Antonyms: avoidabilities, possibilities, uncertainties|noun
