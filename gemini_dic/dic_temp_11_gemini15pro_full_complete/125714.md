municipalization:/mjuːˌnɪsɪpəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
市政化; 城市化
The process of bringing something under the control or ownership of a municipality (a city or town government).
The municipalization of the water supply improved its quality and accessibility for all residents.  城市供水系统市政化后，提高了水质，所有居民都能方便地用水。

The city council voted for the municipalization of public transport. 市议会投票决定将公共交通市政化。

近义词:nationalization, socialization
反义词:privatization
词性:noun
