lustrum:/ˈlʌstrəm/ | n. | 五年期，庆典 | A period of five years, especially as reckoned in ancient Rome for taking a census and performing purification rites.  | The city held a lustrum to celebrate its founding.  这座城市举行了一个五年庆典来庆祝它的建立。 | 近义词: quinquennium, five-year period | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lustrum*, "purification," from *luere*, "to wash, purify, expiate." Originally referred to a purification ceremony performed every five years in ancient Rome.
