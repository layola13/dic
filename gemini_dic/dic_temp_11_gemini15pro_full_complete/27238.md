calmy:/ˈkɑːmli/| adv.|平静地，镇定地|In a calm manner; peacefully; quietly.|She spoke calmly, despite the chaos around her. 她平静地说着，尽管周围一片混乱。|例句：He calmly assessed the situation before acting. 他在行动前冷静地评估了局势。|近义词：peacefully, serenely, tranquilly|反义词：frantically, hysterically, agitatedly|词性:副词
词源: From calm + -ly.  Ultimately from the Latin *cauma, meaning "heat of the sun," then "rest at noon," then "tranquility."
