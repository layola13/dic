grassed:/ɡræst/
|v. (grassed, grassing)|
|覆盖草皮；告密，告发|
|The field was newly grassed. 这块田新铺了草皮。|
|He grassed on his friends to the police. 他向警方告发了他的朋友们。|
|近义词: turfed, snitched, informed|
|反义词: unearthed,  kept silent|
|词性: 动词|

|adj.|
|覆盖着草的|
