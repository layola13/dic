ETYMOLOGY->
Abbreviation of *miles per hour*.  *Mile* comes from Old English *mil*, from Latin *milia passuum* "thousand paces". *Per* comes from Latin *per* "through, by means of". *Hour* comes from Old French *hore*, ultimately from Latin *hora*.
