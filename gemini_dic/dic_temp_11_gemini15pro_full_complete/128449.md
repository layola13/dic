newscast: /ˈnjuːzkɑːst/ (US), /ˈnjuːzkɑːst/ (UK)| n. |新闻广播；新闻节目|A program that broadcasts news reports.|例句：I watched the evening newscast to catch up on current events. 我看了晚间新闻广播，了解时事。

例句：The newscast reported on the latest developments in the political scandal.  新闻节目报道了政治丑闻的最新进展。

近义词：news bulletin, news report, news program|反义词: None|词性: noun
