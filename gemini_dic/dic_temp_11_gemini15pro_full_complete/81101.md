graminivorous:/ˌɡræmɪˈnɪvərəs/ | adj. | 食草的 | Feeding on grass and other plants; herbivorous. | Cows are graminivorous animals. 牛是食草动物。|  Zebras are graminivorous and graze on the savanna.  斑马是食草动物，在热带草原上吃草。 |  herbivorous, vegetarian | carnivorous | adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *graminivorus*, from gramen ("grass") + -vorus ("-eating").
