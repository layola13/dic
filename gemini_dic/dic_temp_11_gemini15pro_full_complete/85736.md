hawkers:/ˈhɔːkəz/
n.
小贩；叫卖者
a person who travels from place to place selling goods, typically advertising them by shouting
The hawkers loudly advertised their wares in the bustling marketplace.  小贩们在熙熙攘攘的市场上大声吆喝着他们的商品。

近义词: vendors, peddlers, merchants, traders
反义词: buyer, customer

词性: noun (plural)
词源: From Middle English *haukere, from Anglo-Norman haukere, variant of Old Northern French *halchier, from Frankish *halkōn (“to hawk, peddle”), from Proto-Germanic *halkōną (“to hawk”). Cognate with Middle Dutch halken (“to hawk”).
