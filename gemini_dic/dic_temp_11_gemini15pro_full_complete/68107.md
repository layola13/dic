faff:/fæf/ | vi. | 瞎忙；瞎折腾 | To spend time doing unimportant things. |  Stop faffing and get ready! 别瞎忙了，赶紧准备! | synonyms: dither, procrastinate, delay | antonyms: expedite, hasten | verb
