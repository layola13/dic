gourmets:/ˈɡʊrmeɪz/ /ˈɡɔːrmeɪz/| n.|美食家；讲究饮食的人|A gourmet is a person who knows a lot about food and cooking, and who enjoys eating high-quality food.|The restaurant is popular with gourmets.  这家餐厅很受美食家欢迎。|Synonyms: connoisseur, epicure, foodie|Antonyms: glutton, gourmand|Noun
