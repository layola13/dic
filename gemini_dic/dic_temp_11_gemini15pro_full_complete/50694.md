dermatophyte: /ˌdɜːrˈmætəˌfaɪt/ | n. | 皮肤癣菌 | A fungus that causes skin diseases, especially ringworm. |  Ringworm is caused by a dermatophyte.  癣是由皮肤癣菌引起的。| synonyms: tinea, ringworm fungus | antonyms: antifungal (referring to treatment, not the organism itself) | noun

dermatophyte: /ˌdɜːrˈmætəˌfaɪt/ | n. | 皮肤癣菌 | A type of fungus that thrives on keratin, a protein found in skin, hair, and nails, causing infections like athlete's foot, ringworm, and jock itch. |  The doctor diagnosed the rash as a dermatophyte infection.  医生诊断皮疹为皮肤癣菌感染。| synonyms: tinea fungus | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the organism itself, but antifungal refers to treatments that combat it) | noun
