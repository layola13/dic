deloo:/dɪˈluː/ |  n. |  delusion（的非正式说法）; 错觉；妄想 | A shortened, informal way of saying "delusion," referring to a false belief or opinion.  |  He's got some crazy deloo about being a superhero.  他有一些关于自己是超级英雄的疯狂错觉。 | 近义词: delusion, illusion, misconception, fallacy | 反义词: reality, truth, fact | 词性: 名词


词源: "Deloo" is a slang shortening of "delusion." The word "delusion" comes from the Late Latin "delusio," meaning "deception,"  from the Latin "deludere," meaning "to deceive, mock, play false."
