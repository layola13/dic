malnourishment: /ˌmælnəˈrɪʃmənt/ | n. | 营养不良 |  The state of being poorly nourished; lack of proper nutrition, caused by not having enough to eat, not eating enough of the right things, or being unable to use the food that one does eat. | Many children in developing countries suffer from malnourishment. 许多发展中国家的儿童营养不良。| synonyms: undernourishment, malnutrition | antonyms: nourishment, healthy diet | noun

malnourishment: /ˌmælnəˈrɪʃmənt/ | n. | 营养不良 |  A condition resulting from insufficient or unbalanced intake of nutrients or their impaired assimilation or utilization. |  The doctor diagnosed the child with malnourishment due to a poor diet. 医生诊断这个孩子因为饮食不良而营养不良。| synonyms:  undernutrition, starvation | antonyms:  overeating, healthy diet | noun
