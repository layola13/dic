bloomingness:/ˈbluːmɪŋnɪs/
noun
盛开的状态；繁荣；兴旺
The state or quality of being in bloom; flourishing, thriving.
The bloomingness of the garden in spring was breathtaking. 春天花园的繁荣景象令人叹为观止。
flourishing, thriving, blossoming, prosperity
decline, decay, withering, fading
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From bloom + -ing + -ness.  "Bloom" comes from Old Norse *blōma. "-ing" is a suffix forming present participles and verbal nouns. "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being."
