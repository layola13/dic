basalts:/bəˈsɔːlts/ | n. | 玄武岩 |A dark, fine-grained volcanic rock that makes up most of the Earth's oceanic crust. It is typically composed primarily of plagioclase and pyroxene minerals.|  The Hawaiian Islands are largely composed of basalts. 夏威夷群岛主要由玄武岩构成。

The cliffs were made of dark basalts. 这些悬崖由深色的玄武岩构成。

|近义词：volcanic rock, igneous rock, lava rock|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific rock type) |词性: noun
