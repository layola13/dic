impracticably:/ɪmˈpræktɪkəbli/ | adv. | 不切实际地；无法实行地 | In a way that is not practical or possible to do. |  The plan was impractically expensive.  这个计划贵得 impractical. | 近义词: impossibly, unrealistically, unfeasibly | 反义词: practically, feasibly, realistically | 词性: 副词

词源:  From impracticable + -ly.  Impracticable comes from in- (not) + practicable (able to be put into practice), from practic, the stem of practicare (to practice), ultimately from Greek praktikos.
