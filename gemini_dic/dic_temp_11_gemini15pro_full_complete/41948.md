cosmorama:/ˌkɒzməˈrɑːmə/ | /ˌkɑːzməˈræmə/ | n. | 全景画; 世界奇观; 包罗万象的展览 | A picture, building, or other work of art designed to give the viewer the impression of looking at an extensive landscape or scene.  A complete and varied collection of things. |  例句：The cosmorama offered a breathtaking view of the Himalayas. 全景画提供了喜马拉雅山脉令人叹为观止的景色。

例句：The museum boasted a cosmorama of ancient artifacts.  博物馆拥有一个包罗万象的古代文物展览。 | 近义词：panorama, diorama, Cyclorama | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用limited view, narrow perspective) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cosmorama*, from *cosmo-* (from Ancient Greek *κόσμος* (kósmos, “world, universe, order”)) + *-orama* (from Ancient Greek *ὅραμα* (hórama, “sight, spectacle”)).
