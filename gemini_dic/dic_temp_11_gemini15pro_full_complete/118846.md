metaphrase:/ˈmetəfreɪz/| n.|逐字翻译，直译|A translation that renders the text literally, word for word.|The translator produced a metaphrase, sticking closely to the original text's wording, which unfortunately resulted in an awkward and unnatural-sounding translation.|近义词：literal translation, word-for-word translation|反义词:paraphrase, free translation|noun
