faintly:/ˈfeɪntli/| adv.|微弱地，模糊地，隐约地|in a faint manner; indistinctly; slightly|例句：She smiled faintly. 她淡淡一笑。|近义词：slightly, dimly, vaguely, subtly|反义词：strongly, clearly, distinctly|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle English *feintly, from Old French *feintisse (“weakness, faintness”), from feint (“feigned, weak, faint”), past participle of feindre (“to feign”), from Latin fingō (“I shape, form, fashion, pretend”).
