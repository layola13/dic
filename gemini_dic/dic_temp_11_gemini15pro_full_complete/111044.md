lispingly:/ˈlɪspɪŋli/| adv.|口齿不清地，咬着舌说话地|In a manner characterized by lisping; with a lisp.  Speaking with a difficulty in pronouncing sibilants, especially s and z, substituting the sounds of th.|She lispingly told him the secret. 她口齿不清地告诉他这个秘密。|Synonyms: with a lisp, imperfectly, indistinctly|Antonyms: clearly, articulately|Adverb
