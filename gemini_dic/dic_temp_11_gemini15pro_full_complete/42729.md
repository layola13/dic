counterworld:/ˈkaʊntərˌwɜːrld/| n.|反世界，平行世界，对立面|A world that is the opposite of the real or ordinary world, often existing in imagination or fiction; a contrasting world or domain.|She dreamt of a counterworld where magic was real and science was fiction. 她梦到一个反世界，在那里魔法是真实的，科学是虚构的。|synonyms: alternate world, parallel universe, opposite world|antonyms: real world, actual world|noun
