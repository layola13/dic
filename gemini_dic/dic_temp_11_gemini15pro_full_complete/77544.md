gdc:/dʒiː diː siː/ | abbreviation | 最大公约数 (greatest common divisor) |  An abbreviation for "greatest common divisor," which is the largest number that divides two or more numbers without leaving a remainder. | The GDC of 12 and 18 is 6.  12和18的最大公约数是6。|  synonym: GCF (greatest common factor), HCF (highest common factor) | antonym: LCM (least common multiple) | noun
