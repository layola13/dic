misshipment: /ˌmɪsˈʃɪpmənt/ | n. |错运，误运|The act of shipping goods to the wrong destination or recipient.  It can also refer to the goods that have been misshipped.| Example: The misshipment of the parts delayed production.  零件的错运延迟了生产。

Example:  The customer service team had to deal with the misshipment and send the correct order. 客服团队不得不处理错运的货物并发送正确的订单。|Synonyms: misdirection, misrouting,  wrong delivery|Antonyms: correct shipment, accurate delivery|noun
