doucine:/ˈduːsiːn/| n.| 圆线，凹圆线，反曲线|A concave moulding, especially a moulding at the top of a pedestal or base; a cyma reversa.|The doucine on the base of the column was delicately carved.  柱子底座上的凹圆线雕刻精美。|近义词:cyma reversa, cavetto, scotia|反义词:ovolo, cyma recta|noun
