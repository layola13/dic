adoring:/əˈdɔːrɪŋ/
adjective
崇拜的，爱慕的
Showing or expressing adoration.
She gave him an adoring look. 她给了他一个爱慕的眼神。
近义词:loving, devoted, worshipping
反义词:critical, hateful, disapproving
词性:adjective
