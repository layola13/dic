jalousie:/ˈʒælʊzi/ (US), /ˈʒaluːzi/ (UK)| n.|百叶窗；妒忌；猜忌|A window blind made of slats that can be adjusted to admit light and air or to provide privacy. Also, a feeling of resentment towards another's success, possessions, or advantages.|例句：The jalousies were closed to keep out the afternoon sun. 百叶窗关上了，以遮挡下午的阳光。

例句：She was consumed by jalousie when she saw her rival's success. 当她看到对手的成功时，她充满了嫉妒。|近义词：blinds, shutters (for window covering); envy, jealousy (for the emotion)|反义词：generosity, admiration (for the emotion)|词性:noun
