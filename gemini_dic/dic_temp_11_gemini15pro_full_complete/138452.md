novas:/ˈnoʊvəz/ (US), /ˈnəʊviː/ (UK)| n. |新星|A nova is a sudden burst of brightness from a star.  It is not a new star, but a temporary increase in the brightness of an existing star.|Astronomers observed several novas last year.  去年天文学家观察到几颗新星。|Synonyms: supernova (for a much larger explosion), transient|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly "dwarf star" in a loose sense)|noun
