mulching:/ˈmʌltʃɪŋ/
n.
覆盖
The act of applying mulch.
Applying mulch helps retain moisture in the soil.  覆盖有助于保持土壤水分。

近义词:covering, protecting
反义词:exposing

词性:noun (名词)
词源:From mulch + -ing.  Ultimately from Middle English *molsh, from Old French mol, soft.


mulch:/mʌltʃ/
n.
覆盖物
A protective covering of organic or inorganic material spread on the ground.
The gardener used bark mulch around the roses. 园丁在玫瑰周围使用了树皮覆盖物。
v.
覆盖
To apply mulch.
He mulched the flowerbeds to prevent weeds. 他给花坛铺上覆盖物以防止杂草生长。

近义词:cover, protect
反义词:expose

词性:noun (名词), verb (动词)
词源:From Middle English *molsh, from Old French mol, soft.
