cry wolf: /kraɪ wʊlf/ | v. | 谎报军情；发出假警报 | To raise a false alarm; to warn of a danger that doesn't exist. | The boy cried wolf so many times that no one believed him when the wolf really did appear. | 这个男孩多次谎报狼来了，所以当狼真的出现时，没有人相信他。 | exaggerate, lie, fabricate | tell the truth, be honest | verb
