concepting:/ˈkɒnseptɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːnseptɪŋ/| n.|概念化，构思|The process of developing and refining ideas, especially in advertising or design.|The team spent weeks concepting the new ad campaign.  团队花了数周时间构思新的广告活动。|synonyms: brainstorming, ideating, developing, formulating|antonyms: executing, implementing|noun
