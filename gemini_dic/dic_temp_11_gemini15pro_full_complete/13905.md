augustinian:/ɔːɡʌˈstɪniən/ | adj. n. |奥古斯丁的；奥古斯丁教派的；奥古斯丁修会的；奥古斯丁教派修士 | Relating to Saint Augustine of Hippo, his theological doctrines, or the religious orders following his rule.
