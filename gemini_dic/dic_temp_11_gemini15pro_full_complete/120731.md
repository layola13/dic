misallotting:/ˌmɪs.əˈlɑː.tɪŋ/
verb
错误分配，分配不当
The act of allotting something wrongly or improperly.
The committee was accused of misallotting funds. 委员会被指控资金分配不当。
misallocate, misuse, misappropriate
allocate correctly, appropriate correctly
