cocultured:/koʊˈkʌltʃərd/
adjective
共同培养的
grown together in a laboratory environment; jointly cultured.
The cells were cocultured with fibroblasts. 这些细胞与成纤维细胞共同培养。
近义词:co-cultivated
反义词:monocultured
词性:adjective

coculture:/koʊˈkʌltʃər/
noun
共同培养
the cultivation of two or more different kinds of cells together.
Coculture of different cell types can mimic in vivo conditions. 不同细胞类型的共培养可以模拟体内条件。
近义词:co-cultivation
反义词:monoculture
词性:noun

coculture:/koʊˈkʌltʃər/
verb
共同培养
to cultivate two or more different kinds of cells together.
We cocultured the bacteria with human cells. 我们将细菌与人类细胞共同培养。
近义词:co-cultivate
反义词:monoculture
词性:verb
