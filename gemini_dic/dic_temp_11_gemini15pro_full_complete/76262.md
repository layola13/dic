fuzzier:/ˈfʌziər/ (US), /ˈfʌzɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更模糊的，更绒毛的|Comparative form of fuzzy: more fuzzy.  Meaning more covered with fluff or fuzz; or more indistinct, unclear, or vague.|例句：The picture became fuzzier as I zoomed in.  我放大图片时，它变得更模糊了。|例句：The peach felt fuzzier than the nectarine.  这个桃子摸起来比油桃的绒毛更多。|近义词：blurrier, vaguer, hazier, unclearer|反义词：clearer, sharper, distincter|词性:形容词
