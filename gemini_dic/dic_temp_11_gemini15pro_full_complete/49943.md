dendroclimatologist: /ˌdɛndroʊklaɪməˈtɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 树木气候学家 | A scientist who studies the climate of past ages by examining the rings of trees. | The dendroclimatologist analyzed the tree rings to determine the rainfall patterns over the past century.  树木气候学家分析了树木年轮，以确定过去一个世纪的降雨模式。 | Synonym:  paleoclimatologist, tree-ring analyst | Antonym: N/A | Noun
