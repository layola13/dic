over-willingly: /ˌoʊvərˈwɪlɪŋli/
| adv. | 过于乐意地，过分积极地 | Excessively willingly; with undue eagerness or readiness. | She over-willingly agreed to help, even though she was already overloaded with work. 她过于乐意地答应帮忙，即使她已经工作 overload 了。| Synonym: too eagerly, overzealously | Antonym: reluctantly, hesitantly |词性: 副词
