distributary:/dɪˈstrɪbjutəri/ /dəˈstrɪbjəˌtɛri/| n. |分流，支流|A branch of a river that does not return to the main stream but flows directly to the sea or a lake. |The Mississippi River has several distributaries that flow into the Gulf of Mexico.|近义词：branch, offshoot, tributary|反义词: mainstream, tributary (when referring to a stream flowing *into* a larger river)|noun
