clypeus:/ˈklɪpiəs/| n. |唇基; 上唇; (昆虫的)头盾|A shield-like plate forming the front of an insect's head above the mouthparts.  The corresponding part in arachnids and crustaceans is also sometimes called a clypeus.|The clypeus of this beetle is particularly prominent.  这只甲虫的唇基特别突出。|Synonyms: shield, plate|Antonyms: N/A|noun


clypeus:/ˈklɪpiəs/| n. |(古罗马战士用的)大圆盾|A large round shield used by ancient Roman soldiers.|The clypeus protected the soldier from enemy attacks.  大圆盾保护士兵免受敌人攻击。|Synonyms: shield, buckler|Antonyms: N/A|noun
