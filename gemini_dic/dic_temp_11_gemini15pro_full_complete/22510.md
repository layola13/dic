boomeranger's:/ˈbuːməræŋərz/| n.|回旋镖投掷者(的), 回飞镖拥有者(的)|A boomeranger's skill is crucial for the hunt.  回旋镖投掷者的技巧对于狩猎至关重要。|The boomeranger's collection was impressive.  这位回飞镖拥有者的收藏令人印象深刻。|近义词: thrower (if referring to the action of throwing), owner (if referring to possession) |反义词: N/A|词性: possessive noun (所有格名词)
Etymology: From "boomerang" + "-er" (agent suffix) + "-'s" (possessive suffix). "Boomerang" itself is borrowed from Dharug (an Aboriginal Australian language), where it referred to a particular type of returning throwing stick.
