de-employed:/ˌdiːɪmˈplɔɪd/
adjective
失业的;被解雇的
Deprived of employment, typically as a result of company downsizing or restructuring.  Having lost one's job, often due to economic reasons beyond one's control, rather than misconduct or poor performance.
例句：Many skilled workers found themselves de-employed after the factory closed. 许多熟练工人发现工厂关闭后自己失业了。

例句：The de-employed engineer struggled to find a new position in his field.  这位失业的工程师努力在他的领域找到一个新的职位。

近义词：unemployed, jobless, laid-off, redundant, out of work
反义词：employed, working
