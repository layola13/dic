hosen:/ˈhoʊzən/ | n. | 长袜，软管 | Hose, especially long stockings or tights.  From the German word "Hosen" meaning trousers or pants. Originally referred to breeches or trousers, and later came to mean stockings or hose. |  He wore hosen and a doublet. 他穿着长袜和紧身上衣。 |  stockings, tights, hose | pants, trousers | noun
