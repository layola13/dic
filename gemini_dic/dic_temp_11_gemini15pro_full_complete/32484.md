chine:/tʃaɪn/ | n. | 裂缝，裂口；山脊，狭窄的山谷 | A narrow ravine, a deep fissure or cleft. | The climbers found a handhold in a chine in the rock face. 攀登者在岩壁的裂缝中找到了一个支撑点。| Synonyms: crack, fissure, cleft, gorge, ravine | Antonyms: closure, seal | noun

chine:/tʃaɪn/ | n. | 猪脊骨肉；(尤指)猪背脊肉 | A cut of meat including the backbone, especially of a pork loin. |  We roasted a chine of pork for Sunday dinner. 星期天晚餐我们烤了一块猪脊骨肉。 | Synonyms: backbone, loin, back | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a cut of meat) | noun
