areligious:/ˌeɪrɪˈlɪdʒəs/
adjective
无宗教信仰的；世俗的
Not religious; having or showing no religious beliefs.
He considered himself areligious, preferring scientific explanations over spiritual ones. 他认为自己没有宗教信仰，更喜欢科学解释而不是精神解释。
She grew up in an areligious household. 她在一个没有宗教信仰的家庭长大。
近义词:nonreligious, secular, unbelieving, irreligious
反义词:religious, devout, pious
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (not) + religious. The prefix "a-" comes from Greek and means "not" or "without."  "Religious" comes from the Latin "religiosus," meaning "careful, conscientious, scrupulous," later acquiring the sense of "related to religion."
