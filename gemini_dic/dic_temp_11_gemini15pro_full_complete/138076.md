normalize:/ˈnɔːrməlaɪz/ (US) /ˈnɔːməlaɪz/ (UK)| v. |使正常化，使标准化|To make something standard or regular; to bring or return something to a usual or expected state or condition.|The hospital worked to normalize the patient's blood pressure. 医院努力使病人的血压恢复正常。|synonyms: standardize, regulate, adjust, adapt|antonyms:  distort, skew, corrupt, abnormalize|verb

normalize:/ˈnɔːrməlaɪz/ (US) /ˈnɔːməlaɪz/ (UK)| n. |正常化，标准化|The process of making something standard or regular.|The normalization of relations between the two countries was a long and difficult process. 两国关系正常化是一个漫长而艰难的过程。|synonyms: standardization, regularization|antonyms: distortion, corruption|noun
