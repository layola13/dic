officiated: /əˈfɪʃieɪtɪd/
verb
主持，执行，担任裁判
acted as an official in charge of something, especially a sporting event or religious ceremony
The referee officiated the football game. 裁判主持了这场足球比赛。
He officiated at their wedding. 他主持了他们的婚礼。
synonyms: referee, umpire, preside, administer, conduct, chair, moderate
antonyms:  observe, spectate
词性: 动词(verb)
