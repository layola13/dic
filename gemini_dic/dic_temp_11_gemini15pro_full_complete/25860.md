burka's:/ˈbɜːrkəz/
noun
罩袍(尤指穆斯林妇女穿的)
A long, loose garment covering the whole body from head to feet, worn in public by many Muslim women.
The woman wore a burka that concealed her entire face.  那个女人穿着一件遮住整个脸的罩袍。

burka's (Technically incorrect, should be burkas)
noun
罩袍(尤指穆斯林妇女穿的) (复数形式，虽然语法上更推荐burkas)
Plural form referring to multiple burkas.  While "burkas" is generally preferred, "burka's" might be encountered in informal contexts.
Several women in burka's walked down the street.  几个穿着罩袍的女人走在街上。(语法上更推荐: Several women in burkas walked down the street.)


Synonym:niqab, veil, chador
Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps Western clothing)
词性: 名词
