outhearing: /ˌaʊtˈhɪərɪŋ/
n.
胜过听力，听力更胜一筹
The act of hearing better or further than another person.
He demonstrated his outhearing by identifying the distant bird song before anyone else. 他通过比任何人都先识别出远处鸟鸣来展示他胜过他人的听力。
近义词: superior hearing, sharper hearing
反义词: poor hearing, deafness
词性: 名词

Etymology:  "out-" (prefix denoting exceeding or surpassing) + "hearing" (the faculty of perceiving sounds)
