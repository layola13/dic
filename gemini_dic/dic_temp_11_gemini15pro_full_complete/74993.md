fried:/fraɪd/| adj.|油炸的；炒的|cooked in hot fat or oil.|例句：I love fried chicken. 我喜欢炸鸡。|近义词：cooked, roasted, sauteed|反义词:raw, uncooked|adjective

fried:/fraɪd/| v.|油炸；炒|(past tense and past participle of fry)|例句：He fried the potatoes until they were golden brown. 他把土豆炸至金黄色。|近义词：cooked, roasted, sauteed|反义词:raw, uncooked|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *frien, from Old English frīġan (“to fry”), from Proto-Germanic *frijjaną (“to fry”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰerǵʰ- (“to roast, fry, parch”). Cognate with West Frisian frie (“to fry”), Dutch frijten (“to fry”), German braten (“to roast”).
