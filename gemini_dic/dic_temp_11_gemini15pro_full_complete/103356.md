jimi:/ˈdʒɪmi/| adj. n.| 极好的，很棒的；吉米（男子名）| From the name Jimi Hendrix, a famous guitarist known for his innovative and exciting music.  It's used as slang to describe something excellent, exciting, or innovative, often in music or related fields. | That guitar solo was jimi! 那段吉他独奏真是太棒了! |My friend Jimi is a talented musician. 我的朋友吉米是一位才华横溢的音乐家。|近义词：awesome, excellent, fantastic, brilliant, superb|反义词：awful, terrible, bad|词性: 形容词, 名词
