aphanipterous:/ˌeɪfəˈnɪptərəs/| adj.|无翅的；属于蚤目的|Relating to or belonging to the order Siphonaptera, which comprises the fleas; wingless.|Fleas are aphanipterous insects that feed on the blood of mammals and birds. 跳蚤是一种无翅昆虫，以哺乳动物和鸟类的血液为食。|Synonyms: wingless, apterous|Antonyms: winged, alate|adjective
