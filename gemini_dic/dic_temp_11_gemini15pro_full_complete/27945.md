canto:/ˈkæntəʊ/ (US), /ˈkæntoʊ/ (UK)| n.| 篇章；诗章；(长诗的)篇；(歌剧的)幕|A canto is one of the sections into which a long poem is divided.  A canto is also a major division of a long poem. |Dante’s Inferno is divided into cantos.  但丁的地狱篇被分成几章。

The second canto of the poem describes a beautiful garden.  这首诗的第二章描述了一个美丽的花园。|近义词: chapter, section, part, division |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
