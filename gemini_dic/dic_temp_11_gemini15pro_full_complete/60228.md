effortless:/ˈɛfərtlɪs/| adj.|毫不费力的，轻松的|Achieved or done without apparent effort.|She danced with effortless grace. 她舞姿优雅，毫不费力。|近义词：easy, simple, painless, smooth|反义词：difficult, strenuous, laborious|词性:形容词
Etymology: From effort + -less.  Effort comes from Old French esforcier "to strive, endeavor," from Vulgar Latin *exfortiare "to strengthen," from Latin ex- "out" + fortis "strong."
