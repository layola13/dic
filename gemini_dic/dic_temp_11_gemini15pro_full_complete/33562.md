ciggie's:/ˈsɪɡiz/ | n. | 香烟（非正式，通常用复数形式指多支香烟） | Informal, often plural, for cigarette.  A shortened and more casual way to refer to cigarettes.  The apostrophe-s indicates possession, but it's often used informally even when not strictly possessive.  It can be understood as "cigarette's" shortened or as a plural possessive like "cigarettes'." |  例句：I'm going out for a ciggie's. 我出去抽根烟。

例句：He always bummed ciggie's off his friends. 他总是向朋友讨烟抽。
