ETYMOLOGY->

From non- (prefix meaning "not") + trespass (noun meaning "an unlawful act committed on the person, property or rights of another").  Ultimately derived from Old French *trespas (“to pass across, to die”), from Latin trans- ("across") + passus ("step").
