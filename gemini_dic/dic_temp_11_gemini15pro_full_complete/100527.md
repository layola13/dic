例句：The intervisitation program allowed doctors from different hospitals to observe each other's practices.  互访计划允许来自不同医院的医生互相观摩彼此的做法。|近义词：exchange visits, cross-visits, reciprocal visits|反义词:isolation, seclusion|词性:noun
