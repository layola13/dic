entangling: /ɪnˈtæŋɡlɪŋ/ | adj. | 纠缠的，使人难以摆脱的 |  Causing something to become twisted together with something else in a complicated way, so that it is difficult to separate them. | The fishing net was entangling the coral reef.  渔网缠住了珊瑚礁。 |  tangling, snarling, intertwining | disentangling, separating | adjective

entangle: /ɪnˈtæŋɡl/ | vt. |  使纠缠；卷入；使陷入 | Cause to become twisted together with or caught in something.  Involve (someone) in difficulties or complicated circumstances from which it is difficult to escape. |  The rope became entangled in the propeller. 绳子缠在了螺旋桨上。 He became entangled in a complicated lawsuit. 他卷入了一场复杂的官司。| involve, embroil, implicate | extricate, disentangle | verb
