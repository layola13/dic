methionine:/məˈθaɪəniːn/
noun|蛋氨酸;甲硫氨酸|An essential amino acid that is important for growth and many bodily functions. It is not synthesized by the body and must be obtained through diet.|Methionine is found in meat, fish, and dairy products. 蛋氨酸存在于肉类、鱼类和奶制品中。|synonyms: essential amino acid|antonyms: N/A|n.
