orthodoxal:/ˌɔːrθəˈdɒksəl/
adjective
正统的，传统的
Conforming to established doctrine especially in religion; conventional.
例句：His views on art are strictly orthodoxal. 他对艺术的看法非常传统。
例句：The orthodoxal approach to treating this disease has been challenged by new research.  治疗这种疾病的传统方法受到了新研究的挑战。

近义词：orthodox, conventional, traditional, conservative
反义词：unorthodox, unconventional, heterodox, radical
词性：adjective
