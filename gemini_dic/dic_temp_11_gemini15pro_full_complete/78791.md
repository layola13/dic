gilly:/ˈɡɪli/| n.| 溪边钓鱼人助手；渔夫的助手；诱饵鱼；(苏格兰)峡谷；(苏格兰)溪谷；|A gilly is a person who helps anglers, especially fly-fishers, by carrying equipment, providing local knowledge, and rowing or poling the boat. In Scotland, a gilly can also refer to a narrow ravine or a mountain stream.|The gilly helped the angler land a large salmon. 钓鱼助手帮助垂钓者钓到了一条大鲑鱼。|Synonym: attendant, guide, helper|Antonym: angler, fisherman|noun

gilly:/ˈɡɪli/| adj.| 狡猾的；诡计多端的|Covered with or made of camouflage material, especially netting and foliage. |The sniper wore a gilly suit.狙击手穿了一件吉利服。|Synonym: cunning, crafty, wily|Antonym: honest, straightforward|adjective
