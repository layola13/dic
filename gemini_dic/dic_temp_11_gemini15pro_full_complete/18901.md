besmircher:/bɪˈsmɜːrtʃər/
n.|玷污者，诽谤者|Someone who besmirches; someone who stains, soils, or sullies something, especially someone's reputation.|The besmircher spread lies about the candidate to ruin his campaign.  这个诽谤者散布关于候选人的谎言以破坏他的竞选活动。|Synonyms: slanderer, defamer, calumniator, libeler, traducer|Antonyms: defender, advocate, supporter|noun

besmirch:/bɪˈsmɜːrtʃ/
vt.|玷污，诽谤|To damage the reputation of (someone or something) by saying or suggesting something bad and untrue about them; to stain or soil something.|The scandal besmirched the reputation of the entire family. 这桩丑闻玷污了整个家族的名声。|Synonyms: sully, tarnish, taint, smear, disgrace|Antonyms: honor, praise, extol, laud|verb
