dexterity:/dɛkˈstɛrəti/| n. |灵巧，敏捷，熟练|Skill and grace in physical movement, especially in using the hands; adroitness.|She played the piano with remarkable dexterity. 她弹钢琴非常灵巧。|近义词：adroitness, skill, proficiency, agility, deftness|反义词：clumsiness, awkwardness, ineptness|noun
