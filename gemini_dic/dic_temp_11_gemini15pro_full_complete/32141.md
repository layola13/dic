chevroned:/ˈʃɛvrənd/ | adj. | V 形的，人字形的 | Having a pattern of chevrons. Decorated with a chevron or chevrons. | The chevroned pattern on the sweater was very stylish. 毛衣上的人字形图案非常时尚。 | The general wore chevroned insignia on his uniform. 将军的制服上有V形徽章。 |striped, zigzagged | plain, solid | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *chevroné (“marked with a rafter or chevron”), from chevron (“rafter”), from Vulgar Latin *capriōnem, accusative of *capriō (“rafter”), from Latin caper (“goat”), from Proto-Indo-European *keh₂p- (“to seize”).  The pattern resembles the shape of a goat's horns.
