hellenist:/ˈhɛlənɪst/| n.|希腊文化爱好者，希腊化主义者|A person who admires and studies ancient Greek culture or language, or a supporter of Hellenism.|例句：He was a renowned hellenist, deeply versed in the classics. 他是一位著名的希腊文化爱好者，精通古典文化。

例句：The hellenist translated the ancient texts with great care.  这位希腊文化研究者非常仔细地翻译了古代文献。|近义词：classicist, philhellene|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


hellenistic:/ˌhɛləˈnɪstɪk/| adj.|希腊化的，希腊文化影响的|Relating to the period of Greek history and culture after the death of Alexander the Great, or influenced by ancient Greek culture.|例句：The museum houses a fascinating collection of Hellenistic art.  这家博物馆收藏了引人入胜的希腊化时期艺术品。

例句：The Hellenistic period saw the spread of Greek language and culture throughout the Mediterranean world. 希腊化时期见证了希腊语言和文化在地中海世界的传播。|近义词：Greek, Hellenic|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective
