mandala:/ˈmændələ/ (US), /ˈmændələ/ (UK)| n.|曼荼罗;坛场|A geometric figure representing the universe in Hindu and Buddhist symbolism. A complex circular design that is usually brightly colored.  It is used as an aid to meditation.|She created a beautiful sand mandala. 她创作了一个美丽的沙曼荼罗。|The monks meticulously crafted a mandala from colored grains of rice.  僧侣们用彩色的米粒精心制作了一个曼荼罗。|近义词：diagram, symbol, pattern|反义词: (No direct antonym)|Noun
