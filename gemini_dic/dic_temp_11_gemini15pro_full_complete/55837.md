dividing:/dɪˈvaɪdɪŋ/
v. |分割，划分|The river divides the city into two parts. 这条河把城市分成两部分。|例句：They are dividing the cake into six pieces. 他们正在把蛋糕分成六块。|近义词：separating, splitting, apportioning|反义词：uniting, joining, merging|词性:动词(present participle of divide)
dividing:/dɪˈvaɪdɪŋ/
adj. |分隔的，分裂的|The dividing line between the two countries is heavily guarded.  两国之间的分界线戒备森严。|例句：The dividing wall between the apartments is very thin. 公寓之间的隔墙很薄。|近义词：separating, partitioning|反义词：uniting, joining|词性:形容词

词源：From Old French *deviser (modern French diviser), from Latin dīvīdere, from di- (“apart”) + vīdere (“to separate”).

注意事项:  dividing 作为动词是divide的现在分词形式，作为形容词表示“分隔的”。
