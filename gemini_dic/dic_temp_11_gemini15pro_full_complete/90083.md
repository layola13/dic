hostessy:/ˈhəʊstɪs/ (UK) /ˈhoʊstɪs/ (US)| n.|女主人，女服务员，女招待|A woman who acts as a host, especially one who greets and looks after guests at a restaurant, club, or other social event.|The hostess showed us to our table. 女服务员带我们到餐桌旁。|近义词：host, waitress, server|反义词:guest, customer|noun

hostess:/ˈhəʊstɪs/ (UK) /ˈhoʊstɪs/ (US)| n.|女主持人|A woman who introduces and interviews guests on a television or radio program.|The hostess of the show was very charismatic. 节目女主持人非常有魅力。|近义词：presenter, anchor, MC|反义词:guest, interviewee|noun
