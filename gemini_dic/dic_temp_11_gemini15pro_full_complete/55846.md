divinatory:/ˈdɪvɪnətɔːri/ |adj.|占卜的；预言的|Used to describe something related to divination or prophecy; predicting the future by supernatural means.|The ancient Romans used divinatory rituals before major battles. 古代罗马人在重大战役前会进行占卜仪式。|Synonyms: prophetic, oracular, predictive, prognostic, mantic|Antonyms: mundane, ordinary, empirical|adjective

divinatory:/ˈdɪvɪnətɔːri/ |n.|占卜者；预言者 (较少用作名词)|A person who practices divination.|The divinatory offered cryptic pronouncements about the king's future. 占卜者对国王的未来做出了神秘的预言。(名词用法较少见)|Synonyms: seer, prophet, oracle, soothsayer|Antonyms: skeptic, realist|noun
