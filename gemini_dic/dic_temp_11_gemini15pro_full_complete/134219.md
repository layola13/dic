nonindulgences: /ˌnɒnɪnˈdʌldʒənsɪz/
词性: noun (名词)
中文翻译:  克制；禁欲；节制（尤指饮食方面）；不放纵
英文解析: The act or practice of refraining from indulging in something, especially pleasures or luxuries.  It implies a conscious decision to abstain from something considered enjoyable or desirable.
例句：Her nonindulgence in sweets helped her maintain a healthy weight. 她不吃甜食，这帮助她保持了健康的体重。

例句：His nonindulgences were legendary; he lived a life of strict austerity. 他的节制行为是传奇性的；他过着极其简朴的生活。

近义词: abstinence, self-denial, moderation, austerity, restraint
反义词: indulgence, excess, intemperance, self-gratification
