inthirst:/ɪnˈθɜːrst/ | n. |渴望，极度口渴 | A strong craving for something, especially drink; extreme thirst. | He felt an inthirst for knowledge after the inspiring lecture.  他听完那场鼓舞人心的演讲后感到对知识的渴望。 |  Near synonyms: craving, yearning, thirst, longing | Near antonyms: apathy, indifference, satiety | noun

inthirst:/ɪnˈθɜːrst/ | vt. |使渴望，使极度口渴 | To cause to have a strong craving, especially for drink; to make extremely thirsty. | The desert sun inthirsted the travelers. 沙漠的太阳使旅行者们感到极度口渴。 | Near synonyms: parch, dehydrate, desiccate | Near antonyms: satiate, quench, satisfy | verb
