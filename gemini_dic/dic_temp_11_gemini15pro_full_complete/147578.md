overpessimism: /ˌoʊvərˈpɛsɪmɪzəm/ | n. |过度悲观|Excessive pessimism; the tendency to expect the worst possible outcome.|His overpessimism about the future prevented him from taking any risks. 他对未来的过度悲观使他不敢冒险。|The pervasive overpessimism in the office was starting to affect morale. 办公室里普遍存在的过度悲观情绪开始影响士气。| 近义词: defeatism, negativity, cynicism | 反义词: optimism, hopefulness | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "pessimism" (from French pessimisme, from Latin pessimus "worst").
