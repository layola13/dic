heptarchic:/hɛpˈtɑːrkɪk/
adjective
七王国时代的；七个统治者的
Relating to a heptarchy, a government of seven rulers.  Specifically refers to the period in Anglo-Saxon England, traditionally considered to have been ruled by seven kingdoms.
The heptarchic period of Anglo-Saxon England was a time of frequent conflict.  七王国时代的盎格鲁-撒克逊英格兰是一个冲突频发的时期。
The historian challenged the traditional view of a heptarchic system in Anglo-Saxon England.  这位历史学家挑战了盎格鲁-撒克逊英格兰七王国体系的传统观点。
近义词: seven-kingdom, sevenfold
反义词: unified, single
