chiminage:/ˈtʃɪmɪnɪdʒ/ | n. |烟道使用费，壁炉税|A fee paid for the use of a chimney; hearth money.|In medieval times, chiminage was a significant source of income for landlords. 在中世纪，烟道使用费是地主的重要收入来源。|The family struggled to pay the chiminage each year. 这家人每年都难以支付烟道使用费。|Synonyms: hearth tax, chimney tax|Antonyms: None|Noun


chiminage:/ˈtʃɪmɪnɪdʒ/ | n. |通行费（尤指通过森林）|A toll or fee paid for passage, especially through a forest.|In some regions, travelers had to pay chiminage to use certain roads. 在某些地区，旅行者必须支付通行费才能使用某些道路。|The chiminage for crossing the king's forest was quite steep. 穿越国王森林的通行费相当高。|Synonyms: toll, passage fee|Antonyms: None|Noun
