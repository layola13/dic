intershifting:/ˈɪntərˌʃɪftɪŋ/
adjective
交替变换的，交互转换的
Changing or alternating between different states, positions, or forms.
The intershifting patterns of light and shadow created a mesmerizing effect. 交替变换的光影图案营造出迷人的效果。
The artist's work explores the intershifting relationship between humans and nature.  这位艺术家的作品探索了人与自然之间不断变化的关系。
synonyms: alternating, fluctuating, oscillating, varying, shifting
antonyms: static, fixed, constant, unchanging
词性: adjective
