nongolder: /nɒnˈɡəʊldər/ (British) /nɑːnˈɡoʊldər/ (American) | n. | 非淘金者；非淘金热时期的人 | A person who is not a gold digger, or someone who lived outside a gold rush period. |  Many nongolders settled in California for its agricultural potential. 许多非淘金者在加利福尼亚定居是为了发展农业。| Near synonyms: settler, resident, inhabitant | Antonym: gold digger (in a historical context) | noun

nongolder: /nɒnˈɡəʊldər/ (British) /nɑːnˈɡoʊldər/ (American) | adj. | 非淘金的；非淘金热时期的 | Not related to gold digging or a gold rush period. | The nongolder population focused on farming and trade. 非淘金人口专注于农业和贸易。| Near synonyms: non-mining, non-prospecting | Antonym: gold-mining (in a historical context) | adjective


Etymology: "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "golder" (derived from "gold digger").  The term likely arose during or after the major gold rushes of the 19th century, particularly the California Gold Rush.
