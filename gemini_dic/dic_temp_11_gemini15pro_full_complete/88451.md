hɪˈstɒlədʒi (British) | hɪˈstɑːlədʒi (American)
n.
组织学
The study of the microscopic structure of tissues.
Histology helps us understand how the body works at a cellular level. 组织学帮助我们了解身体在细胞层面的运作方式。
Histology revealed the presence of cancerous cells. 组织学检查揭示了癌细胞的存在。

近义词: microscopic anatomy
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps macroscopic anatomy)
词性: noun
