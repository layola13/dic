disproportion:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃən/ |n.| 不均衡，不成比例 |A lack of balance or equality between things.|  There is a huge disproportion between the number of male and female employees in this company. 这家公司男女员工数量极度不均衡。|Synonyms: imbalance, disparity, inequality | Antonyms: balance, equality, proportion | noun

disproportions:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənz/ |n.|  不均衡，不成比例（复数）|  Lack of balance or equality between things (plural). | The report highlighted the disproportions in funding between urban and rural schools.  这份报告强调了城乡学校之间资金分配的不均衡。| Synonyms: imbalances, disparities, inequalities | Antonyms: balances, equalities, proportions | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *disproportion-, from dis- + proportion-  ("proportion").  The prefix "dis-" indicates negation or reversal, meaning "not" or "apart." "Proportion" comes from Latin "proportio," meaning "comparative relation between things or magnitudes."
