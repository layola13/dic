babbages:/ˈbæbɪdʒɪz/| n.|巴贝奇（复数）| Refers to Charles Babbage (often pluralized when discussing his machines), a 19th-century British mathematician and inventor considered a "father of the computer" for his conceptual and partially constructed calculating machines, the Difference Engine and the Analytical Engine. |The Babbages were groundbreaking inventions for their time. 巴贝奇的机器在当时是突破性的发明。|  Museum visitors marveled at the intricate workings of Babbages' machines.  博物馆的参观者对巴贝奇机器的复杂运作惊叹不已。|Synonym: (no direct synonym, could use "early computers," "mechanical calculators," "proto-computers")|Antonym: (no direct antonym)|noun
