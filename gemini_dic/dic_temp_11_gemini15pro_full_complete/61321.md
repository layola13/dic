embassy: /ˈembəsi/ | n. |大使馆；大使及其随员|An embassy is the official residence or offices of an ambassador.|The American Embassy in Paris is a grand building. 美国驻巴黎大使馆是一座宏伟的建筑。|近义词: consulate, legation |反义词: N/A |词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Old French *embassade (unattested), from Provençal ambaissada, from ambaissador "ambassador", from Late Latin ambaxiator, from ambactus "servant, vassal" (source also of Old High German ambahteo, ambahtscalo, German Amt "office"), of Gaulish origin.
