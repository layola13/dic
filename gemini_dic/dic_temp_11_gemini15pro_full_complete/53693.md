discipling:/dɪˈsaɪplɪŋ/
n.|训练，训导，管教|The act of training someone in obedience and self-control, often in a religious context.|The pastor focused on discipling new members of the church. 牧师专注于训练教会的新成员。|
近义词:training, instructing, mentoring, teaching
反义词:neglecting, spoiling, indulging
词性:noun (gerund or present participle of the verb "disciple")


disciple:/dɪˈsaɪpəl/
n.|门徒，弟子，信徒|A follower or student of a teacher, leader, or philosophy.|He was a disciple of the famous philosopher. 他是那位著名哲学家的弟子。|
近义词:follower, student, adherent, believer
反义词:opponent, critic, dissenter
词性:noun


disciple:/dɪˈsaɪpəl/
vt.|教导，训练，使成为门徒|To teach and train someone in the principles of a particular belief or way of life.|The guru discipled many young people in his ashram.  这位古鲁在他的静修处教导了许多年轻人。|
近义词:teach, train, instruct, mentor
反义词:mislead, corrupt, misguide
词性:verb
