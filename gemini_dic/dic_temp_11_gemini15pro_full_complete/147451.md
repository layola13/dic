overocclusion: /ˌoʊvərəˈkluːʒən/ | n. | 过度咬合，深覆合 | A condition in which the upper teeth overlap the lower teeth excessively when the jaws are closed.  This can cause problems with chewing, speaking, and jaw pain. |  The dentist diagnosed her with overocclusion and recommended braces. 牙医诊断她过度咬合，建议她戴牙套。 |  Her overocclusion made it difficult to bite into an apple. 她的深覆合让她很难咬苹果。 | Synonyms: deep bite, closed bite, overbite | Antonyms: open bite, underbite | noun

overocclusion: /ˌoʊvərəˈkluːʒən/ | n. | 过度闭塞 | Excessive occlusion or blockage, often referring to blood vessels. (Less common usage than in dentistry) | The overocclusion of the artery led to a stroke.  动脉过度闭塞导致了中风。| The report mentioned an overocclusion in the patient's femoral artery. 报告提到了病人股动脉的过度闭塞。| Synonyms: blockage, obstruction | Antonyms: patency | noun
