half-life:/ˈhɑːf laɪf/ (US) /ˈhɑːf laɪf/ (UK)| n.|半衰期|The time taken for the radioactivity of a specified isotope to fall to half its original value.|The half-life of carbon-14 is 5,730 years. 碳-14的半衰期是5730年。

The half-life of this drug is about four hours.  这种药物的半衰期大约是四个小时。|近义词: half-time|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
