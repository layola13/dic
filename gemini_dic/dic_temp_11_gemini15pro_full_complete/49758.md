demolishers:/dɪˈmɒlɪʃəz/
n.|拆除者，拆迁者|People employed in the act of demolishing buildings or other structures.  These individuals often operate heavy machinery and are skilled in demolition techniques.|The demolishers arrived early in the morning to begin tearing down the old factory.|近义词：wreckers, destroyers|反义词:builders, constructors|词性:名词

demolish:/dɪˈmɒlɪʃ/
v.|拆除，摧毁;驳倒|To tear down or break apart a building or other structure; to completely refute an argument or theory.|They hired a company to demolish the old building.  他们雇佣了一家公司来拆除旧楼。 The lawyer demolished the witness's testimony. 律师驳倒了证人的证词。|近义词：destroy, wreck, raze|反义词:build, construct|词性:动词

词源: From Old French *demolir, from Latin demoliri "to tear down," from de- "down" + moliri "to build, to work," from moles "mass, pile."
