free-spirited:/ˌfriːˈspɪrɪtɪd/| adj.|自由奔放的，无拘无束的|Not bound by conventional social norms; independent and unconventional in thought and action.  A person who lives according to their own wishes and beliefs, not caring what other people think.|She's a free-spirited artist who lives life on her own terms. 她是一位自由奔放的艺术家，按照自己的方式生活。|Synonyms: bohemian, unconventional, independent, nonconformist, uninhibited|Antonyms: conventional, conformist, inhibited, restrained|adjective

Etymology:
From free + spirited.  "Spirited" meaning full of spirit or energy, often implying boldness and independence.
