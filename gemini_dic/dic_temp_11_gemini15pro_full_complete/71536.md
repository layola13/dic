flap:/flæp/ | n. v. | 拍打，襟翼，副翼 | A piece of something thin and flat, attached to something else on one side only, that can move freely.  To move something up and down or back and forth. | The bird flapped its wings and flew away. 鸟儿拍打着翅膀飞走了。 | The flaps on the wings of the airplane helped it to land safely. 飞机机翼上的襟翼帮助它安全着陆。 | synonyms: flutter, wag, swing | antonyms: still, motionless | noun, verb

flap:/flæps/ | n. | 拍打，襟翼，副翼（复数）| Plural form of "flap." | The bird's flaps were damaged. 鸟的翅膀受伤了。|  The plane lowered its flaps for landing.  飞机放下襟翼准备着陆。| synonyms:  wings, ailerons (in aviation context) | antonyms: N/A | noun
