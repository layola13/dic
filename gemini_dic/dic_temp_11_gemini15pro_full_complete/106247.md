knowinger:/ˈnoʊɪŋər/ | n. | 知情者；心照不宣的人 | Someone who has special knowledge of something, especially something that is not generally known, and who sometimes behaves in a way that suggests they know more than other people.  | He gave me a knowinger look, as if we shared a secret. 他意味深长地看了我一眼，好像我们之间有什么秘密似的。 | 近义词: insider, expert,  connoisseur | 反义词:  outsider, ignoramus | 词性: 名词


词源:  From "knowing" (adjective) + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting persons associated with the quality or action of the verb). "Knowing" ultimately derives from the Old English verb "cnāwan" (to know).
