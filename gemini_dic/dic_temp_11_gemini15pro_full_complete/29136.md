case-hardening:/ˈkeɪsˌhɑːrdnɪŋ/
n.|表面硬化，表面硬化处理|The process of hardening the surface of a metal object while leaving the interior relatively soft.|The gears were subjected to case-hardening to improve their wear resistance. 齿轮经过表面硬化处理以提高其耐磨性。|surface hardening, face hardening|annealing, softening|noun
case-harden:/ˈkeɪsˌhɑːrdn/
v.|使表面硬化|To harden the surface of a metal object while leaving the interior relatively soft.|They case-hardened the steel to make it more durable.  他们对钢进行了表面硬化处理，使其更耐用。|surface harden, face harden|soften, anneal|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From case + harden.  "Case" refers to the outer layer or "case" of the metal.
