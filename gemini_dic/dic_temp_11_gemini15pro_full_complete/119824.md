miladies:/ˌmɪˈleɪdiz/ | n. | 女士们；小姐们 | A plural form of milady, used as a polite form of address to a group of women.  It is now archaic or humorous. | Miladies, welcome to our annual luncheon. 女士们，欢迎参加我们的年度午餐会。 | Miladies, please be seated. 小姐们，请就座。 | Near synonyms: ladies, women, females | Antonyms: gentlemen, men, males | noun
