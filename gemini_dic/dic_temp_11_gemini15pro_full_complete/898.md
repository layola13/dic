abundancy:/əˈbʌndənsi/
n.
丰富，充裕
The state or quality of being abundant; plentifulness.
The garden displayed an abundancy of colorful flowers.  花园里展示着五彩缤纷的花朵的丰盛。
近义词: abundance, profusion, plenty, copiousness
反义词: scarcity, lack, dearth, paucity
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *abundantia, from Latin abundant- (stem of abundans), present participle of abundare ‘to overflow,’ from ab- ‘from’ + unda ‘wave.’
