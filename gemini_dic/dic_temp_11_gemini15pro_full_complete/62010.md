enamor:/ɪˈnæmər/ /ɪˈneɪmər/| vt.|使迷恋，使倾心|To inspire with love; to charm; to captivate. |例句：The prince was enamored of the beautiful princess. 王子被美丽的公主迷住了。

例句：She was completely enamored with the idea of traveling the world. 她完全被环游世界的想法迷住了。|近义词：captivate, charm, fascinate, enchant|反义词：repel, disgust, disenchant|词性:动词
