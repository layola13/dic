blitzkrieg:/ˈblɪts.kriːɡ/| n.|闪电战|A swift, intensive military attack, especially using tanks and aircraft, designed to defeat the enemy quickly.|The German blitzkrieg of Poland in 1939 was devastatingly effective. 1939年德国对波兰的闪电战具有毁灭性的效力。|近义词：onslaught, blitz, assault|反义词:stalemate, defense|名词

blitzkill:/ˈblɪts.kɪl/| n.  v.|闪电杀戮，快速击杀|A swift and decisive kill, often in a video game or similar context, inspired by the concept of "blitzkrieg".  As a verb, it means to quickly and decisively kill (someone or something).|The gamer executed a perfect blitzkill, eliminating the entire opposing team.  这个玩家执行了一次完美的闪电杀戮，消灭了整个对方队伍。
He blitzkilled the final boss with a powerful combo attack. 他用一个强大的组合攻击闪电般地杀死了最终boss。|近义词：annihilate, obliterate, destroy|反义词:revive, resurrect|名词，动词
