destructiveness:/dɪˈstrʌktɪvnəs/
noun
破坏性，毁灭性
The quality of being destructive; the capacity to destroy.
The hurricane's destructiveness left the coastal town in ruins.  飓风的破坏性使沿海城镇沦为废墟。
destructivity, devastation, ruin, damage
construction, creation, repair, preservation
