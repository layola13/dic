niggardness:/ˈnɪɡərdnəs/ | n. |吝啬，小气 | Extreme unwillingness to spend money; stinginess. |  He showed his niggardness by refusing to leave a tip. 他拒绝付小费，表明了他的吝啬。| synonyms: stinginess, parsimony, miserliness, meanness, tightness | antonyms: generosity, liberality, munificence | noun

Etymology: From Middle English *nigardnesse*, from Old Norse *hnǫggr* (stingy) + *-ness* (suffix forming nouns of quality or state).  Related to Old English *hnēaw* (stingy).
