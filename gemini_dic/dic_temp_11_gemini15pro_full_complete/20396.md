bitchiness:/ˈbɪtʃɪnɪs/ | n. | 刻薄；毒舌；恶毒 | The bitchiness of her remarks surprised everyone. |  Her constant criticism and sarcasm revealed her underlying bitchiness. | 近义词：malice, spite, nastiness, cattiness, meanness | 反义词：kindness, niceness, sweetness, generosity | 词性: 名词

词源: 源自形容词bitchy（刻薄的，恶毒的），加上名词后缀-ness构成。Bitchy又源自名词bitch（母狗，泼妇），该词最初指母狗，后来引申指“坏女人”，再后来发展出“刻薄的，恶毒的”含义。
