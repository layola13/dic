gliders:/ˈɡlaɪdərz/| n.|滑翔机; 滑翔员; 滑翔动物|Aircraft or animals that fly using air currents without an engine.  People who fly gliders are also called gliders.|The gliders soared effortlessly through the sky. 滑翔机毫不费力地在空中翱翔。|近义词：sailplanes, soaring machines|反义词：(For the aircraft) powered aircraft|词性:Noun
