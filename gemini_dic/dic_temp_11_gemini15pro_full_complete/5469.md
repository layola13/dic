aloha's:/əˈloʊhəz/
noun
阿罗哈的；阿罗哈所属的
Possessive form of "aloha," meaning belonging to or associated with aloha.
Example: Aloha's spirit is felt throughout the islands. 阿罗哈的精神遍布整个岛屿。
近义词: Hawaiian's, island's
反义词: none
词性: noun (possessive)

aloha's:/əˈloʊhəz/
noun
阿罗哈（複数形，不太常见）
A less common plural form of "aloha," referring to multiple instances of the greeting or sentiment.  This usage is less grammatically standard.
Example:  He sent his aloha's from afar. 他从远方表达了他的祝福。(不太常见的用法)
近义词: greetings, regards
反义词: none
词性: noun (plural, non-standard)
