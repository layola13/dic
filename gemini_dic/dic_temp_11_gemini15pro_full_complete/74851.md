frequentatives:/ˈfriːkwəntətɪvz/ | n. |  频繁发生的动作；重复动词；频率词 |Words formed from a verb and denoting repeated or habitual action. |Frequentatives are often used in children's language. 重复动词经常用于儿童语言。|  derivatives, iteratives |  --- | noun

frequentative:/ˈfriːkwəntətɪv/ | adj. | 频繁的；重复的 |  Indicating repeated or habitual action. |He has a frequentative cough. 他经常咳嗽。|  repetitive, habitual, recurring | occasional, infrequent | adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *frequentativus, from Latin frequentare "to visit often," from frequens "crowded, frequent."
