co-ordinated:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪd/ /kəʊˈɔːdɪneɪtɪd/ | adj.|协调的，配合的|Working together efficiently.|Their movements were perfectly co-ordinated.|synonyms: organized, harmonious, synchronized|antonyms: disorganized, uncoordinated, chaotic|adjective
