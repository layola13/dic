immanent:/ˈɪmənənt/| adj.|内在的；固有的|Existing or operating within; inherent.|例句：God is immanent in all creation. 上帝存在于所有造物之中。|例句：The principles of justice are immanent in the legal system.  正义的原则固存于法律体系之中。|近义词：inherent, intrinsic, ingrained|反义词：external, extrinsic|词性:形容词
Etymology:  Late Middle English: from late Latin immanent- ‘remaining within’, from the verb immanere, from in- ‘in’ + manere ‘remain’.
