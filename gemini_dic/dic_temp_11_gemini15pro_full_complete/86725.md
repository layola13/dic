helminthologist:/ˌhɛlmɪnˈθɒlədʒɪst/ (US) /ˌhɛlmɪnˈθɒlədʒɪst/ (UK)| n. |蠕虫学家；寄生虫学家|A scientist who specializes in the study of helminths (parasitic worms).|例句：The helminthologist studied the lifecycle of the intestinal worm.  这位蠕虫学家研究了肠道蠕虫的生命周期。

例句：The helminthologist presented her research on parasitic worms at the conference.  这位蠕虫学家在会议上展示了她对寄生虫的研究。|近义词：parasitologist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
