anchylotic:/ˌæŋkɪˈlɒtɪk/ | adj. |强直的，僵硬的 |Relating to or characterized by ankylosis; stiff or rigid.| The anchylotic joints made movement difficult. 强直的关节使活动困难。|Synonyms: stiff, rigid, immobile, inflexible |Antonyms: flexible, mobile, supple |adjective

anchylosed:/ˌæŋkɪˈloʊzd/ | adj. |强直的，僵硬的 |Affected by ankylosis; stiff or rigid.|  His fingers were anchylosed from arthritis. 他的手指因关节炎而僵硬。|Synonyms: stiff, rigid, immobile, inflexible |Antonyms: flexible, mobile, supple |adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ankylos* meaning "bent, crooked,"  + -otic (suffix forming adjectives).  The word ultimately describes a joint that is bent or crooked in a fixed position.
