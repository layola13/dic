abnormality:/ˌæbnɔːrˈmælɪti/ /ˌæbnɔːrˈmæləti/| n.|异常，反常；异常情况，畸形|A departure from what is normal or expected; irregularity.|The doctor noticed an abnormality in the patient's blood test. 医生注意到病人的血液检测结果异常。|近义词：anomaly, irregularity, deviation, aberration|反义词：normality, regularity, standard|词性:名词

abnormal:/æbˈnɔːrml/ /æbˈnɔːrməl/| adj.|反常的，异常的；不正常的|Deviating from what is normal or usual, typically in a way that is undesirable or worrying.|The abnormal weather patterns have caused widespread flooding.  异常的天气模式导致了大范围的洪水。|近义词：unusual, atypical, irregular, deviant|反义词：normal, typical, usual, regular|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin abnormalis, from ab- ‘away from’ + normalis (see normal).
