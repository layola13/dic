{arg} 替换为 what

what: /wɑːt/ (US) /wɒt/ (UK) | pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么，|What is your name? 你叫什么名字？ | synonyms: which, how much, how many | antonyms:  (What doesn't typically have direct antonyms as it's a question word) | pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你觉得怎么样？
What does she want for lunch? = 她午饭想吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? = 他现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she eaten so far? = 到目前为止她吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你这周一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? = 你说了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before he arrived? = 在他到达之前你做了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision? = 决定之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do this weekend? = 你这个周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing at 8 pm tonight? = 今晚8点你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? = 会议上他们将要展示什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by Friday? = 到星期五你会完成什么？
What will have changed by next year? = 到明年会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习什么五年了？
What will she have been working on by the deadline? = 到截止日期前她将一直在做什么？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to do in this situation. = 我希望我知道在这种情况下该怎么办。

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the issue? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are your plans for the summer? = 你暑假的计划是什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to think. = 我不知道该怎么想。
There's nothing what I can do. = 我无能为力。



PHRASE->

what if = 如果...会怎么样？
what for = 为了什么？
so what? = 那又怎样？
