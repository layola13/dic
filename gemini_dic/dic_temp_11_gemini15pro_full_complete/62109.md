enchants:/ɪnˈtʃænts/ /ɛnˈtʃænts/| vt.|使着迷，使陶醉|fills (someone) with great delight; charms.|The magician enchanted the audience with his tricks. 魔术师用他的戏法使观众着迷。|近义词：captivates, fascinates, charms, bewitches|反义词：repels, disgusts, bores|verb

enchants:/ɪnˈtʃænts/ /ɛnˈtʃænts/| n.|魔法，魅力 (罕用，通常用 enchanting)|a magical spell; a charm.|The enchants of the forest held her captive. 森林的魔法让她身陷其中。(罕用)|近义词：spells, charms, magic|反义词：curses, hexes|noun (rare)
