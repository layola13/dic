inseparability:/ɪnˌsɛpərəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可分割性；不可分离性
The quality or condition of being inseparable.
The inseparability of mind and body is a complex philosophical issue.  身心不可分割性是一个复杂的哲学问题。

inseparable:/ɪnˈsɛpərəbl/
adj.
不可分离的；形影不离的
Not able to be separated.
The two friends were inseparable during their childhood. 这两个朋友童年时形影不离。
近义词: indivisible, unbreakable, inseparable
反义词: separable, divisible
词性: 名词(n.)
