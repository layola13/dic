affective:/əˈfɛktɪv/| adj.| 情感的；表达感情的；由感情引起的 | Relating to moods, feelings, and attitudes. | Her affective response to the music was overwhelming. 她对音乐的情感反应非常强烈。| synonyms: emotional, feeling, sensitive | antonyms: unemotional, apathetic, indifferent | adjective

affective:/əˈfɛktɪv/| n.| （语法）表达说话者情绪的词语或形式 | A word or form expressing the speaker's attitude. (Less common usage) |  The use of "alas" is an affective.  使用“唉”是一个表达情感的词语。 | synonyms: emotive (adjective), interjection | antonyms:  N/A (This sense of affective rarely has direct antonyms) | noun
