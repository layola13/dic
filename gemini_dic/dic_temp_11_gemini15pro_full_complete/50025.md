denominations:/dɪˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃənz/| n.|面额，名称，教派|A class or kind of people or things distinguished by a specific name, especially a religious group.  A name or designation; an appellation. A unit of currency.|The cashier counted out the denominations of bills. 出纳员数出了不同面额的钞票。|synonyms: classes, categories, designations, sects, appellations, units|antonyms: whole, entirety, aggregate|noun
