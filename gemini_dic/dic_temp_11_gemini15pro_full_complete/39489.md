connotatively:/ˌkɑːnəˈteɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 含蓄地，隐含地 |  In a way that suggests a particular idea or feeling in addition to its literal meaning. |  "Home" connotatively suggests warmth, comfort, and family. “家”这个词隐含着温暖、舒适和家庭的含义。 |  implicitly, suggestively |  explicitly, literally | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *connotare, from Latin com- "together" + notare "to mark."  The adverb form is derived by adding the suffix "-ly."
