Etymology:
Cyberpunk is a portmanteau of "cybernetics" and "punk".  "Cybernetics" refers to the science of communication and control systems in both machines and living things, while "punk" suggests a rebellious and counter-cultural attitude.  The term was coined by Bruce Bethke in his 1980 short story of the same name.
