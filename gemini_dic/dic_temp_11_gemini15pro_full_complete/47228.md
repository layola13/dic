dazing:/ˈdeɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人眼花缭乱的，令人眩晕的 |  Causing someone to feel unable to think clearly. | The dazzling sunlight made me feel dazed.  耀眼的阳光让我感到头晕目眩。| synonyms: bewildering, stunning, overwhelming, confusing | antonyms: calming, soothing, clarifying | adjective

daze:/deɪz/ | n. vt. | 昏迷，茫然；使（某人）感到眩晕或迷茫 | A state of being confused and unable to think clearly; to stun or confuse someone. |  He was in a daze after the accident. 事故发生后，他处于一种茫然的状态。 The blow to his head dazed him.  击中他头部的打击使他晕眩。| synonyms: stupor, confusion; bewilder, stun | antonyms: alertness, clarity; awaken, enliven | noun, verb
