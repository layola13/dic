expressing:/ɪkˈsprɛsɪŋ/
adjective
表达的，表示的
conveying a thought or feeling in words or by actions or appearance.
Her face was expressing a mixture of surprise and delight. 她的脸上流露出惊喜交加的表情。
近义词: conveying, communicating, articulating, showing, demonstrating
反义词: suppressing, concealing, hiding
