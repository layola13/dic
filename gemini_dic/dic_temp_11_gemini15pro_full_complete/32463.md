china:/ˈtʃaɪnə/| n. |瓷器; 瓷; 中国|A hard, white, shiny substance made by baking clay at a high temperature.  Also refers to the country, China.|例句：This teacup is made of fine china.  这个茶杯是用上好的瓷器做的。

例句：She's just returned from China. 她刚从中国回来。|近义词：porcelain, ceramic, tableware (for the material sense); PRC, People's Republic of China (for the country sense) |反义词: (None for either sense)|名词
