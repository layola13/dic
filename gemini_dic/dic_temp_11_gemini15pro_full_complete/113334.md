luxullianite:/lʌkˌsʌliəˈnaɪt/ | n. | 勒克斯里安岩 | A rare type of granite containing tourmaline, known for its ornamental value.  A variety of granite from Luxulyan, Cornwall, England, consisting of quartz, orthoclase, tourmaline, and cassiterite. |  The collector proudly displayed his piece of luxullianite.  这位收藏家自豪地展示了他的一块勒克斯里安岩。 | Synonym: tourmaline granite | Antonym: N/A | noun
