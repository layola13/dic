cd-roms:/ˌsiː diː ˈrɒmz/ | n. | 光盘（复数） | Compact Disc Read-Only Memory, a type of optical disc used to store digital data. CD-ROMs are read-only, meaning that data can be accessed but not modified or erased.  | I have a collection of old games on CD-ROMs. 我有一堆CD-ROM光盘的老游戏。 |  The software comes on a CD-ROM.  这款软件是通过CD-ROM光盘提供的。 | Synonym: discs, optical discs | Antonym: hard drives, USB drives | Noun
