exalt:/ɪɡˈzɔːlt/ | vt. |  提升；赞扬；使得意 | To raise in rank, power, or character; to elevate by praise or in estimation; to enhance the activity of. |  The victory exalted the nation. 胜利使国家振奋。| 近义词:elevate, praise, glorify | 反义词:debase, degrade | verb
