forewarner:/fɔːrˈwɔːrnər/ | n. | 预警者；预言者 | Someone or something that forewarns. | The dark clouds were a forewarner of the coming storm.  乌云预示着即将到来的暴风雨。 | 近义词：harbinger, precursor, omen | 反义词: None (lack of a forewarner implies surprise) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From forewarn + -er.  Forewarn from fore- + warn.  Fore- from Old English *fore-, from Proto-Germanic *fura-, meaning "before." Warn from Old English warnian, from Proto-Germanic *warnōną, meaning "to warn, take heed."
