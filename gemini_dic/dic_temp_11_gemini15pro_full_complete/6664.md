anagogy:/ˈænəɡɒdʒi/ /ˈænəˌɡɑːdʒi/| n.|神秘的解释，精神上的解释，寓意|A mystical or spiritual interpretation (especially of a text).  The interpretation of a word, passage, or text that finds a higher or spiritual meaning in addition to the literal meaning.|The preacher used anagogy to connect the story of Jonah to the resurrection of Christ.|近义词：allegory, spiritual interpretation, mystical interpretation, symbolism|反义词: literal interpretation|词性:noun
