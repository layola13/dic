glades:/ɡleɪdz/| n. | 树林中的空地，林间空地 | An open space in a wood or forest. |例句：Sunlight streamed into the forest glades. 阳光倾泻进森林的空地。|例句：Deer grazed peacefully in the glades. 鹿群在林间空地里安静地吃草。| 近义词：clearing, meadow, opening | 反义词: thicket, forest | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old English *glæd "bright, shining," later "open space in a wood," related to glad.
