iconize: /ˈaɪkənaɪz/ | vt. | 使成为偶像；赋予偶像地位；把…当作偶像崇拜 | To treat someone or something as an icon; to make an icon of. |  They iconized their leader, hanging posters of him everywhere. 他们把他们的领袖奉为偶像，到处都挂着他的海报。

|  The media iconized her for her beauty and talent. 媒体因她的美貌和才华而把她奉为偶像。 | 近义词: idolize, worship, deify | 反义词: vilify, condemn, denigrate | 词性: verb

词源: From icon + -ize.  "Icon" comes from the Greek word "eikōn" meaning "image" or "likeness."
