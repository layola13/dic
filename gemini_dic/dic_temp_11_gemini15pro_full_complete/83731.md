haiku: /ˈhaɪkuː/ | n. |俳句|A Japanese poem of seventeen syllables, in three lines of five, seven, and five, traditionally evoking images of the natural world.|例句：She wrote a haiku about the cherry blossoms. 她写了一首关于樱花的俳句。

例句：The haiku captured the essence of the autumn evening. 这首俳句捕捉到了秋夜的精髓。


近义词：poem, verse |反义词: novel, prose |词性: noun
