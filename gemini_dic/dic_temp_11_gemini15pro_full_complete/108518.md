lays:/leɪz/ | n. | (诗歌或歌曲)短叙事诗，短歌；放置，产卵（lay 的复数） | a short narrative poem or song; plural of lay |  The minstrels sang lays of ancient heroes.  吟游诗人歌颂古代英雄的叙事诗。 |近义词：ballads, songs, poems, verses |反义词: (No direct antonym for this sense of lays) | noun
