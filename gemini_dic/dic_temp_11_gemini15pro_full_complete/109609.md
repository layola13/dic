lette:/ˈlɛtə/| n.| 字母; 信; 证书; 文学; |A letter is a character representing one or more of the sounds used in speech; any of the symbols of an alphabet. A letter is also a written, typed, or printed communication, especially one sent in an envelope by mail or messenger.|例句：Please write me a letter. 请给我写封信。|例句：She received a letter from her mother. 她收到了她妈妈的来信。|近义词：note, message, character, alphabet, communication|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑number 数字, symbol 符号 根据语境选择) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lettre*, from Old French *lettre*, from Latin *littera* (“letter of the alphabet; literature, writing; epistle”), related to *linere* (“to smear, besmear”). Doublet of *liter*.
