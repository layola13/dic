antiformalist:/ˌæntiˈfɔːrməlɪst/
adjective
反形式主义的；非形式主义的
Opposed to formalism, especially in art or literature.  Rejecting or reacting against established forms or conventions.
The antiformalist movement in the 1960s challenged traditional artistic norms.  20世纪60年代的反形式主义运动挑战了传统的艺术规范。
