cross-ruff: /ˈkrɒs rʌf/ (British), /ˈkrɔːs rʌf/ (American)| n. |交叉将牌,交叉垫牌| In card games like whist and bridge, a cross-ruff is a situation where both partnerships repeatedly trump each other's suits in order to win tricks. |

例句：
The cross-ruff continued for several tricks, with neither side able to gain an advantage.
交叉垫牌持续了好几墩，双方都无法获得优势。

Their skillful cross-ruff secured them the game.
他们熟练的交叉垫牌技术确保了他们赢得比赛。
