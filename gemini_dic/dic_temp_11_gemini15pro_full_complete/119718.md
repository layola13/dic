midsopolis: /ˌmɪdzəˈpɒlɪs/ | n. | 中型城市；中等规模的城市 | A city of medium size, typically between a metropolis and a small town.  It often serves as a regional center. |  Example 1: The midsopolis of Springfield is known for its thriving arts scene. 斯普林菲尔德这座中型城市以其繁荣的艺术景象而闻名。

Example 2:  Many young professionals are choosing to live in midsopolises due to their lower cost of living. 许多年轻的专业人士选择居住在中等规模的城市，因为那里的生活成本较低。 | 近义词: mid-sized city, regional center | 反义词: metropolis, megacity, small town | 词性: 名词


词源:

"midsopolis" is a relatively recent coinage, likely formed by analogy to "metropolis."  "Mid-" indicates "middle" or "medium," and "-polis" comes from the Greek word "polis," meaning "city."  Therefore, "midsopolis" literally translates to "middle city."
