fripperer:/ˈfrɪpərər/
n.
旧货商；穿着俗丽的人；轻浮的人
A person who deals in frippery; one who is frivolously and showily dressed; a frivolous person.
The fripperer tried to sell me a moth-eaten coat for a ridiculous price.  这个旧货商想以荒谬的价格把一件蛀虫的衣服卖给我。
He was known as a fripperer, always decked out in the latest fashions. 他被称为穿着俗丽的人，总是穿着最新的时装。

近义词:
dandy, fop, coxcomb
反义词:
pragmatist

词性:
noun
