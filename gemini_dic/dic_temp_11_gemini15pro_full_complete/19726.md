bilk:/bɪlk/
verb
欺骗；诈骗
To cheat or swindle someone out of money or other valuable possessions.
He bilked investors out of millions of dollars. 他骗取了投资者数百万美元。
近义词: cheat, defraud, swindle, con
反义词: compensate, reimburse
词性: verb

bilk:/bɪlk/
verb
逃避支付；赖账
To evade payment of a debt.
He bilked the taxi driver out of his fare. 他骗了出租车司机的车费。
近义词: evade, dodge, avoid
反义词: pay
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain. Possibly from Middle English *bilken, of Scandinavian origin; compare with dialectal Swedish bylka “to heap up,” Norwegian bulke “to swell.”  The earliest senses seem to relate to cheating at cards.
