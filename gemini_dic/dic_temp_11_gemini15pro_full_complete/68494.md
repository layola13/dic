famous:/ˈfeɪməs/| adj.|著名的，出名的|Known by many people; celebrated.|例句：She is a famous actress. 她是一位著名的女演员。|例句：The city is famous for its beautiful beaches. 这座城市以其美丽的海滩而闻名。|近义词：renowned, celebrated, well-known, prominent, distinguished|反义词：unknown, obscure, anonymous|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *famosus* "celebrated, notorious," from *fama* "fame, report, rumor."
