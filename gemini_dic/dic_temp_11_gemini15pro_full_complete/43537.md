crawlingly:/ˈkrɔːlɪŋli/ | adv. | 爬行地，缓慢地 | In a slow, crawling manner, like a crawling insect.  | The traffic moved crawlingly through the rush hour. 交通在高峰期缓慢地移动。|  The snail crawled crawlingly across the leaf.  蜗牛缓慢地爬过树叶。| 近义词：slowly, sluggishly, gradually | 反义词：quickly, rapidly, swiftly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From crawl (verb) + -ing (present participle suffix) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Crawl" comes from Old Norse *krafla, meaning "to scratch, claw."
