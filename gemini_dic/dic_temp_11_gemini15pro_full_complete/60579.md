elatedly: /ɪˈleɪtɪdli/
adverb
欣喜若狂地，兴高采烈地
In a manner expressing great happiness and excitement.
She danced elatedly after winning the competition. 她赢了比赛后欣喜若狂地跳了起来。
He accepted the award elatedly. 他兴高采烈地接受了奖项。
近义词: joyfully, jubilantly, ecstatically, rapturously
反义词: dejectedly, sadly, gloomily
词性: adverb

词源:
From the adjective "elated" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Elated" comes from the Latin word "elatus," the past participle of "efferre," meaning "to carry out" or "to uplift."  The sense of emotional upliftment led to the modern meaning of "elated."
