communicators:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtərz/
n.|传播者，沟通者|People who transmit information or messages; those who communicate effectively.|例句：The company hires skilled communicators to manage its public relations. 公司聘请了熟练的传播者来管理其公共关系。|近义词：speakers, messengers, orators, presenters|反义词：receivers, listeners|词性:名词

communicators:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtərz/
n.|交流者|People who exchange information, ideas, or feelings.|例句：Effective communicators build strong relationships. 有效的交流者建立牢固的关系。|近义词：conversationalists, interlocutors|反义词：isolates|词性:名词
