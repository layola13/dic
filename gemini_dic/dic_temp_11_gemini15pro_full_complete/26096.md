businessman:/ˈbɪznəsmæn/ (US), /ˈbɪznɪsmən/ (UK)| n. |商人，实业家|A man who works in commerce or industry, especially one who has a high position in a company. |He's a shrewd businessman with a knack for making deals. 他是一位精明的商人，很有做交易的诀窍。

|近义词：entrepreneur, executive, industrialist, merchant|反义词: employee, worker|noun
