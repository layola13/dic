idiomorphic:/ˌɪdioʊˈmɔːrfɪk/| adj.|自形的，真形的|Having its own characteristic form; fully developed in crystalline form.  A crystal that has developed its characteristic external shape is said to be idiomorphic.|The quartz crystals in the granite are idiomorphic.  花岗岩中的石英晶体是自形的。|euhedral, automorphic|anhedral, subhedral|adjective
