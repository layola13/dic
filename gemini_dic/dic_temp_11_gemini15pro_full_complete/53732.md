discography:/dɪˈskɒɡrəfi/ /dɪˈskɑːɡrəfi/
n.|唱片目录；唱片集|A list of all the recordings made by a particular musician or singer.|例句：The band's discography includes ten studio albums and numerous singles.  这支乐队的唱片目录包括十张录音室专辑和许多单曲。|例句：She has a vast discography spanning several decades. 她的唱片作品浩瀚，跨越数十年。|近义词：catalog, collection, oeuvre|反义词:|名词
