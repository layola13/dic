finched:/fɪntʃt/ | v. | 退缩，畏缩 | To shrink back or flinch, as from fear or pain.  | He finched when the doctor gave him the injection. 他在医生给他打针时畏缩了。|  The horse finched at the sudden loud noise. 马儿因突如其来的巨响而畏缩。 | Synonyms: flinch, recoil, shrink, wince, cower | Antonyms:  brave, confront, face | verb
