lay-for:/leɪ fɔːr/
verb phrase
埋伏以攻击；伺机报复
To lie in wait for someone with the intention of attacking or confronting them.
He was laying for me after I accidentally embarrassed him in front of his friends. 他在我当着他朋友的面让他意外出丑后，就一直伺机报复我。
