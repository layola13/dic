half-exposed:/ˌhæf ɪkˈspoʊzd/
adjective
半暴露的，部分暴露的
partly uncovered or unprotected; visible but not completely revealed
The building was left half-exposed to the elements after the storm.  暴风雨过后，这座建筑有一半暴露在风雨中。
The photographs revealed a half-exposed secret. 这些照片揭露了一个半公开的秘密。
near synonyms: partially exposed, partly visible, semi-concealed
antonyms: fully exposed, completely covered, hidden, concealed
adjective
