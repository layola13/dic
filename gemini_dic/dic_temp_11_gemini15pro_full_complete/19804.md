biltong:/ˈbɪltɒŋ/ (UK), /ˈbɪlˌtɔŋ/ (US)| n. |风干肉，肉干 |A form of cured meat that originated in Southern African countries. Various types of meat can be used to produce biltong, but beef is most common.  It is typically air-dried and seasoned with spices such as coriander, salt, and pepper.|  I brought back some biltong from my trip to South Africa. 我从南非旅行带回了一些风干肉。| Synonyms: jerky, dried meat | Antonyms: fresh meat | Noun
