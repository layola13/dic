communalists:/kəˈmjuːnəlɪsts/
n.
宗派主义者；地方自治主义者；公社主义者
People who advocate or participate in communalism, often characterized by prioritizing the interests of their own community or religious group over others, sometimes leading to tension or conflict.
The communalists were blamed for instigating the riots.  宗派主义者被指责煽动骚乱。
The rise of communalists in the region has led to increased tensions. 该地区宗派主义者的兴起导致紧张局势加剧。
synonyms:sectarians, separatists, partisans
antonyms:integrationists, unitarians, universalists
