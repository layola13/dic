egotheism:/ˈiːɡoʊθiːɪzəm/ | n. | 自我神论，极端利己主义 | The belief that oneself is God or the supreme being; extreme egotism or self-centeredness.  | He exhibited such egotheism that he believed all his actions were divinely inspired. 他表现出如此的自我神论，以至于他相信他所有的行为都是神圣的启示。 |  Example: His egotheism blinded him to the needs of others. 他的极端利己主义使他看不到别人的需要。| Synonyms:  egotism, narcissism, self-worship, solipsism | Antonyms:  altruism, humility, selflessness | noun
