classiest:/ˈklæsɪɪst/| adj.|最高级的，最有品位的|Most stylish and elegant; having the most class.|She always throws the classiest parties. 她总是举办最高雅的派对。|近义词：most elegant, most stylish, most refined, sophisticated|反义词：tackiest, trashiest, cheapest, gaudiest|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "classy" + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Classy" originates from "class" (social status), which ultimately comes from Latin "classis" (division, fleet).  The meaning evolved from referring to high social standing to denoting elegance and sophistication.
