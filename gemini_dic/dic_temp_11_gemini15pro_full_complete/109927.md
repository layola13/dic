libellous:/ˈlaɪbələs/ (US) /ˈlaɪbələs/ (UK)| adj.|诽谤的，中伤的|Containing libel; defamatory.  A statement is libellous if it is false and published with malicious intent and causes harm to the reputation of the person it refers to.|The newspaper article was deemed libellous and the publisher was sued. 这篇报纸文章被认为是诽谤性的，出版商被起诉了。

The politician claimed the accusations were libellous and intended to damage his career.  这位政治家声称这些指控是诽谤性的，意在损害他的职业生涯。|近义词：defamatory, slanderous, calumnious|反义词：laudatory, complimentary|词性: adjective
