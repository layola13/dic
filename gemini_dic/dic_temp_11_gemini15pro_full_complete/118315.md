merenchyma:/ˈmerənˌkaɪmə/ | n. | 薄壁组织 |  A tissue of plants made up of thin-walled living cells that remain capable of cell division even after maturity, and form the soft parts of leaves, stems, fruits, and other plant organs. | Example: The merenchyma of the fruit was soft and juicy.  水果的薄壁组织柔软多汁。| Example: Photosynthesis primarily occurs in the merenchyma cells of leaves. 光合作用主要发生在叶子的薄壁组织细胞中。 | Synonym: parenchyma | Antonym: sclerenchyma | Noun
