hallucinogenic:/həˌluːsɪnəˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
致幻的；引起幻觉的
Causing hallucinations; relating to or resembling hallucinations.
Some mushrooms have hallucinogenic properties.  一些蘑菇有致幻性。
The drug is known for its hallucinogenic effects. 这种药物以其致幻作用而闻名。

近义词:psychedelic, psychotomimetic
反义词:sobering

词性:adjective


词源:
From hallucination + -genic.  来自hallucination（幻觉）+ -genic（产生……的）。
