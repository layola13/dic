odorous:/ˈoʊdərəs/| adj.|有气味的，有香味的，通常指难闻的气味|Having a distinct smell, often unpleasant.|The odorous fumes from the factory caused complaints from nearby residents. 工厂散发出的难闻气体引起了附近居民的投诉。|Synonyms: smelly, fragrant (if pleasant), aromatic (if pleasant), pungent, musty|Antonyms: odorless, scentless, unscented|adjective
