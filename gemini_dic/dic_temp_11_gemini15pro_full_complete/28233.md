capstone:/ˈkæpˌstoʊn/ (US) /ˈkæpˌstəʊn/ (UK)| n.|顶石，顶峰，毕业项目|a final project in a student's academic program, typically involving independent research or design, that serves as a culminating experience|例句：Her capstone project was a study of the effects of social media on teenage mental health. 她的毕业项目是研究社交媒体对青少年心理健康的影响。

例句：The capstone course required students to integrate knowledge from all their previous coursework. 这门顶点课程要求学生整合他们之前所有课程的知识。


近义词：culmination, climax, pinnacle, apex|反义词:base, foundation, beginning|词性:noun
