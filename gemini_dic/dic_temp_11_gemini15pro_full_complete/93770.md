immelodious:/ɪməˈləʊdiəs/
adjective
不悦耳的；不和谐的
not melodious; unmusical; harsh-sounding.
The immelodious sound of the rusty swing set grated on her nerves. 生锈的秋千发出刺耳的不和谐声音，让她心烦意乱。
The beginner's violin practice was quite immelodious. 初学者练习小提琴的声音很不悦耳。
近义词:discordant, cacophonous, inharmonious, tuneless
反义词:melodious, harmonious, tuneful, euphonious
词性:adjective
词源:  "im-" (not) + "melodious" (pleasant-sounding). "Melodious" comes from the Latin "melodia" and ultimately from the Greek "melos" (song) + "oide" (song).
