bab:/bæb/ | n. | 婴儿；幼兽；孩子气的人 | A very young child, or a baby animal.  A person who behaves in a childish way. |  The bab cried all night. (婴儿整夜都在哭。) He's a real bab when he doesn't get his way. (当他达不到目的时，他就像个孩子。) | 近义词: baby, infant, child | 反义词: adult, grown-up | 词性: 名词

bab:/bæb/ | v. |  闲聊；胡扯 | To talk foolishly or idly; chatter. |  They babbed on about nothing in particular. (他们闲聊些无关紧要的事情。) Stop babbing and get to work! (别闲聊了，快去工作！) | 近义词: chatter, prattle, gabble | 反义词: be quiet, be silent | 词性: 动词

词源： 
"bab" likely originates as an onomatopoeia, mimicking the sounds babies make. Its use as a verb, meaning to chatter or talk foolishly, extends from the association of babies with incoherent speech.  The exact etymology is uncertain, but this imitative origin is widely accepted.
