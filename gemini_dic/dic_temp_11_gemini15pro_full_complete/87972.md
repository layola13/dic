high-handed:/ˌhaɪˈhændɪd/| adj. |高压的，专横的，霸道的|acting without considering the feelings or opinions of others; domineering|例句：The government's high-handed approach to the protests drew widespread criticism. 政府对示威活动的高压态度引来广泛批评。|例句：She was high-handed in her dealings with her employees, often ignoring their suggestions. 她对员工很专横，经常无视他们的建议。|近义词：arbitrary, dictatorial, overbearing, imperious, tyrannical|反义词:considerate, democratic, humble, respectful|词性:adjective

词源: From "high hand," referring to playing a strong card in a card game without regard for others.  Later, the term evolved to describe domineering behavior.
