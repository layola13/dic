intuitively:/ɪnˈtuːɪtɪvli/
adv.
直觉地，直观地
based on feelings rather than facts or proof
She intuitively knew that something was wrong. 她直觉地感到有些不对劲。
近义词: instinctively, naturally
反义词: logically, rationally
词性: 副词

词源: From Late Latin intuitivus, from intueri "to look at, contemplate," from in- "in, on" + tueri "to look at, watch, guard, defend."
