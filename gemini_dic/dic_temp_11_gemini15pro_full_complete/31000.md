chalkier:/ˈtʃɔːkiər/ (US), /ˈtʃɔːkɪə(r)/ (UK)
adjective
更白垩的，更像粉笔的
Resembling chalk, especially in color or texture; containing more chalk.
The soil in this area is chalkier than in the valley.  这个地区的土壤比山谷里的更白垩。
The pills left a chalkier taste in my mouth. 这些药丸在我的嘴里留下了一种更像粉笔的味道。
近义词:  whiter, paler, dustier
反义词: darker, less chalky
词性: adjective
