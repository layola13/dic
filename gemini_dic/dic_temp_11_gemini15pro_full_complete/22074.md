bolix:/ˈbɒlɪks/| n. v. |胡说八道，搞砸|Nonsense; to mess something up; (vulgar slang, chiefly Irish) testicles. |He bolixed the whole operation. 他把整个行动都搞砸了.|近义词：mess up, ruin, screw up, balls (vulgar)|反义词:fix, repair|词性:名词，动词

词源: From Irish *bolg* (“scrotum, belly, bag”), from Proto-Celtic *bolgos (“belly, bag”). Cognate with Welsh *bolg*, Breton bolc'h (“pod”).  The slang usage likely arose from its association with "balls" (testicles).
