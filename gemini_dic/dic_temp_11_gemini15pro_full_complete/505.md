abolishable:/əˈbɒlɪʃəbl/
adjective
可废除的
Capable of being abolished; that can be done away with.
Slavery is an abolishable practice.  奴隶制是一种可以废除的做法。
The death penalty is an abolishable punishment. 死刑是一种可以废除的刑罚。
近义词:terminable, eradicable, revocable
反义词:irrevocable, ineradicable, permanent
词性:adjective
