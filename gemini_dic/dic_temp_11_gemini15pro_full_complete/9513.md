antischolastic:/ˌænti-skəˈlæstɪk/ | adj. | 反学院派的，反对学究式的 | Opposed to traditional academic values or methods; rejecting or hostile to formal education, especially that based on a narrow curriculum. |  The artist's antischolastic approach to art making prioritized experimentation over traditional techniques.  这位艺术家的反学院派艺术创作方法优先考虑实验而不是传统技术。| Synonyms: unorthodox, unconventional, heterodox, iconoclastic | Antonyms: scholastic, academic, conventional, orthodox | adjective

antischolastics:/ˌænti-skəˈlæstɪks/ | n. | 反学院派人士 |  People who oppose traditional academic values or methods. | The antischolastics believed that true learning came from experience, not from books. 反学院派人士认为真正的学习来自经验，而不是书本。 | Synonyms: nonconformists, rebels, mavericks | Antonyms: scholars, academics, traditionalists | noun
