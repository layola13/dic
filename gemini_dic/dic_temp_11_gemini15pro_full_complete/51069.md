despair:/dɪˈspeər/| n. v.|绝望，丧失希望|The complete loss or absence of hope.|例句：She felt a deep sense of despair. 她感到深深的绝望。|近义词：hopelessness, despondency|反义词:hope, optimism|词性:名词，动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *desperer, from Latin desperare ("to be without hope, give up hope"), from de- ("without") + sperare ("to hope").
