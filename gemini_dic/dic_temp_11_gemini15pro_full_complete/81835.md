greying:/ˈɡreɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 变灰白的，变白的 | Becoming grey or white, especially of hair. | Her greying hair made her look older than she was. 她的灰白头发使她看起来比实际年龄大。| 近义词：graying, silvering, whitening | 反义词：darkening, blackening | 词性: 形容词

词源: From grey + -ing.  The suffix "-ing" is used to form present participles and adjectives, often indicating a process or state. "Grey" comes from the Old English "græg," of Germanic origin, related to Dutch "grauw" and German "grau."
