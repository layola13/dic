isohelic:/ˌaɪsəˈhiːlɪk/
adjective
等螺旋的，同螺距的
Having the same helix or helical structure, especially of a polymer or crystal.
Isohelic viruses have capsid proteins arranged in the same helical symmetry. 等螺旋病毒的衣壳蛋白排列成相同的螺旋对称。
The isohelic arrangement of the molecules contributes to the stability of the crystal structure. 分子的等螺旋排列有助于晶体结构的稳定性。
近义词: isotactic (for polymers)
反义词: anisotactic (for polymers)
词性: adjective

词源:  From iso- (Greek: equal) + helical
