misreliance:/ˌmɪsrɪˈlaɪəns/
n.
过度依赖；错误的依赖
An act of depending on someone or something too much, or in a way that is wrong.
His misreliance on his GPS led him to a dead end. 他过度依赖GPS导航，结果把他带到了死胡同。
Overdependence, overreliance, undue reliance
Independence, self-reliance
noun
