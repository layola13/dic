diabetes insipidus:/ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz ɪnˈsɪpɪdəs/| n.|尿崩症|A rare disorder that causes frequent urination and excessive thirst due to the kidneys' inability to conserve water.|The doctor diagnosed him with diabetes insipidus after he complained of constant thirst and frequent urination. 医生在他抱怨持续口渴和频繁排尿后，诊断他患有尿崩症。|近义词:water diabetes|反义词: N/A|名词
