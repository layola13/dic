kingliness:/ˈkɪŋlɪnəs/| n.|王者风范，王者气质|The quality of being kingly; royal bearing or behavior; majestic appearance or manner.|He carried himself with a certain kingliness. 他举手投足间流露出一股王者风范。|近义词：majesty, regality, nobility, grandeur|反义词：humbleness, meekness, modesty|词性:名词
Etymology: From kingly + -ness.  Kingly from king + -ly.  King from Old English *cyning, from Proto-Germanic *kuningaz.
