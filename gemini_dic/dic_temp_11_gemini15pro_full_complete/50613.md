deregulation:/diːˌreɡjəˈleɪʃn/
n.
解除管制，撤销管制
The removal of regulations or restrictions, especially in a particular industry.
The deregulation of the airline industry has led to increased competition and lower fares. 航空业的解除管制导致竞争加剧和票价降低。

The deregulation of the financial markets has had a profound impact on the global economy. 金融市场的放松管制对全球经济产生了深远的影响。

近义词: liberalization, decontrol
反义词: regulation, control
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning 'removal, reversal') + regulation.  The prefix "de-" originates from Latin, meaning "down from, away from, off."  "Regulation" comes from the Late Latin word "regulatio," meaning "a directing," derived from the verb "regulare," meaning "to control by rule, direct."
