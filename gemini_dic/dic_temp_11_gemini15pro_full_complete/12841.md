associationistic:/əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃənɪstɪk/ | adj. | 联想主义的 | Relating to or based on the theory of associationism, which emphasizes the role of experience and association in learning and memory. |  His learning style was very associationistic, connecting new concepts to things he already knew.  他学习的方式非常联想主义，将新概念与他已经知道的事情联系起来。 |  Example: The psychologist employed associationistic principles in his therapy. 这位心理学家在他的治疗中运用了联想主义的原则。 |Synonyms:  associative, connectionist | Antonyms:  n/a | word type: adjective

associationistic:/əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃənɪstɪk/ | n. | 联想主义者 | A person who believes in or practices associationism. | While less common, the term can also refer to a person who adheres to associationism.  虽然不太常见，但该词也可以指坚持联想主义的人。| Example:  He considered himself an associationistic, believing all knowledge stems from sensory experience. 他认为自己是一个联想主义者，相信所有的知识都源于感官经验。| Synonyms: associationist | Antonyms: n/a | word type: noun
