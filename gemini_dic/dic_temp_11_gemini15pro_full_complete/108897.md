led-on:/lɛd ˈɑːn/ | adj. | 被欺骗的，被误导的 | Deceived or misled, especially in a romantic context, into believing that someone has feelings for you when they do not. |  He led her on for months, making her think he was serious about their relationship.  他让她空欢喜了好几个月，让她以为他对他们的关系是认真的。| Synonyms: strung along, deceived, misled | Antonyms: honest, truthful | adjective
