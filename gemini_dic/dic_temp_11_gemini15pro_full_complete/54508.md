disjointed:/dɪsˈdʒɔɪntɪd/ | adj. | 脱节的，不连贯的，杂乱无章的 | Lacking a coherent sequence or connection; disconnected. |  The novel felt disjointed, as if chapters were missing. 这部小说感觉脱节，好像缺了几章。 |  The speaker's disjointed presentation left the audience confused. 演讲者杂乱无章的陈述让听众感到困惑。 | 近义词:incoherent, disconnected, rambling, fragmented, illogical | 反义词:coherent, connected, unified, consistent, logical | 词性:形容词

disjoint:/dɪsˈdʒɔɪnt/ | vt. |  使脱节，使分离 | To separate or disconnect the parts of something. | The company's restructuring disjointed the sales and marketing teams. 公司的重组使销售和营销团队脱节了。 | The accident disjointed the railway line. 事故使铁路线脱节了。 | 近义词:separate, disconnect, disunite, sever | 反义词:join, connect, unite, link | 词性:动词
