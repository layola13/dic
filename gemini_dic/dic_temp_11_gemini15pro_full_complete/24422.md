briskness:/ˈbrɪsknɪs/| n. |活泼；轻快；爽快；兴隆|The briskness of the autumn air was invigorating. 秋天的空气清新爽快，令人振奋。|Synonyms: liveliness, quickness, activity, energy, vigor|Antonyms: sluggishness, slowness, inactivity, lethargy, dullness|noun
brisk:/brɪsk/| adj. |活泼的；轻快的；敏捷的；兴隆的|He walked at a brisk pace. 他步伐轻快地走着。|Synonyms: lively, quick, active, energetic, vigorous|Antonyms: sluggish, slow, inactive, lethargic, dull|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *brisk*, probably from Old French *brisque* (“nimble, quick”), perhaps related to Italian *brusco* (“tart, rough”) and ultimately of Germanic origin.
