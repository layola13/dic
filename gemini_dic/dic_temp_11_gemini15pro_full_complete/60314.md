ego-surfing:/ˈiːɡoʊ sɜːrfɪŋ/
n.
自我搜索；网络自恋
the practice of searching the Internet for instances of one's own name or other information relating to oneself.
例句：He spends hours ego-surfing, looking for mentions of himself online. 他花了几个小时在网上搜索自己的名字。

例句：She admitted to occasionally indulging in a bit of ego-surfing. 她承认偶尔会沉迷于自我搜索。

近义词：vanity searching, self-googling
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
