overjoyously: /ˌoʊvərˈdʒɔɪəsli/ | adv. | 非常高兴地，欣喜若狂地 |  In a manner expressing extreme joy and happiness.  Derived from "overjoyous" (over + joyous), which itself combines "over" (excessively) and "joyous" (full of joy). The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective into an adverb. | She danced overjoyously when she heard the good news. 她听到这个好消息后欣喜若狂地跳了起来。|  happily, joyfully, ecstatically, blissfully | sadly, sorrowfully, miserably | adverb
