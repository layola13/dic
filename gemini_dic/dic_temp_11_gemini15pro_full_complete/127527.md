necromancer:/ˈnɛkrəmænsər/| n. |亡灵法师，巫师|A person who practices necromancy; a sorcerer or magician who claims to be able to communicate with the dead, especially in order to predict the future or to use their power. |例句：The necromancer summoned a skeletal warrior from the depths of the graveyard. 亡灵法师从墓地深处召唤出一个骷髅战士。

例句：In the fantasy novel, the necromancer sought to raise an army of the undead. 在这部奇幻小说中，亡灵法师试图组建一支亡灵大军。|近义词：sorcerer, wizard, magician|反义词:priest, paladin|词性:noun
