archaeal:/ɑːrˈkiːəl/
adjective
古菌的
of or relating to archaea
Archaea are single-celled microorganisms.  古菌是单细胞微生物。
Archaeal membranes contain ether linkages. 古菌的细胞膜含有醚键。
近义词: archaean
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly bacterial or eukaryotic depending on context)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *Archaealis, from archae- +‎ -al.  Ultimately from Ancient Greek ἀρχαῖος (arkhaîos, “ancient”).
