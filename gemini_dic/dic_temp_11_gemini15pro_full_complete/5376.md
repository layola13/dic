alluviate:/əˈluːvieɪt/
verb
冲积；淤积
to deposit or form by alluvion
The Nile River has alluviated its delta for centuries. 尼罗河几个世纪以来一直淤积着它的三角洲。

alluviate:/əˈluːviət/
adjective
冲积的
pertaining to, composed of, or deposited by alluvion
The alluviated soil is very fertile. 冲积土非常肥沃。


近义词:deposit, accumulate, silt up
反义词:erode, abrade
词性:v. adj.
