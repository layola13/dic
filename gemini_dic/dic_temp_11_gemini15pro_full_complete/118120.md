menopauses:/ˈmɛnəˌpɔːzɪz/ /ˈmɛnəˌpɔːziz/| n. |更年期（复数）|The biological stage in a woman's life when menstruation ceases permanently, marking the end of her reproductive years. It is typically accompanied by various physical and emotional changes due to declining estrogen levels.|例句：Many women experience hot flashes during menopause. 许多女性在更年期会出现潮热。

menopauses:/ˈmɛnəˌpɔːzɪz/ /ˈmɛnəˌpɔːziz/| n. |绝经期（复数）|The ceasing of menstruation.|例句：The average age for menopause is around 51. 绝经的平均年龄在51岁左右。

menopauses:/ˈmɛnəˌpɔːzɪz/ /ˈmɛnəˌpɔːziz/| n. |停经期（复数）|A period of cessation of menstruation.|例句：These menopauses are often accompanied by hormonal fluctuations. 这些停经期通常伴随着激素波动。
