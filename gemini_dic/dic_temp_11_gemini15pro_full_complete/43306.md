craggy: /ˈkræɡi/ (US) /ˈkræɡi/ (UK) | adj. | 崎岖的，陡峭的 | Having many steep, rough rocks; rugged. | The craggy cliffs offered a breathtaking view of the ocean. 崎岖的悬崖提供了令人叹为观止的海景。| The hikers carefully navigated the craggy terrain.  徒步旅行者小心翼翼地在崎岖的地形上前行。| 近义词: rugged, rough, jagged, uneven | 反义词: smooth, even, flat | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: From crag + -y.  Crag comes from the Middle English cragge, from a Celtic source akin to Welsh craig "rock."
