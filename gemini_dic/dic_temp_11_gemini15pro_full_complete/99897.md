internasal:/ˌɪntərˈneɪzəl/
adjective
鼻内的；鼻间的
Situated or occurring between the nasal cavities.
The septum is an internasal structure. 鼻中隔是一个鼻间结构。
The doctor diagnosed an internasal infection. 医生诊断出鼻内感染。
近义词: intranasal
反义词: None
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From inter- + nasal. Inter- is a prefix meaning "between," "among," "in the midst of," "mutually," "reciprocally," "together," "during."  Nasal refers to the nose or nasal cavity.
