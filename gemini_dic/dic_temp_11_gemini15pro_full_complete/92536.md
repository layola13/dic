hypsometric:/ˌhɪpsəˈmetrɪk/ | adj. | 测高的，高度测量的 | Relating to the measurement of elevation above sea level. |  The hypsometric curve shows the distribution of land area according to elevation.  测高曲线显示了土地面积随海拔的分布情况。

The hypsometric equation relates pressure to height in the atmosphere. 测高方程将大气中的压力与高度联系起来。 | 近义词: altitudinal, elevation | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "bathymetric" which relates to underwater depth) | 词性: adjective
