debasing:/dɪˈbeɪsɪŋ/
adjective
贬低的；堕落的；降低身份的
lowering the quality or character of something; degrading
例句：
The debasing effect of poverty can be seen in many areas.
贫困带来的堕落影响在很多地方都能看到。

He felt that the work was debasing and beneath his dignity.
他觉得这份工作有损人格，有失体面。


近义词：degrading, humiliating, corrupting, demoralizing
反义词：ennobling, uplifting, elevating
词性：adjective
