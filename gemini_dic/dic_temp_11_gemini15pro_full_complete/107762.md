lapidarists:/ˌlæpɪˈdɛərɪsts/
n.
宝石工匠，玉石雕刻师
A person who cuts, polishes, or engraves precious stones.
The lapidarists carefully shaped the raw diamonds into sparkling jewels. 宝石工匠小心翼翼地将未加工的钻石雕琢成闪闪发光的珠宝。
synonyms: gem cutter, jeweler, lapidary
antonyms: N/A
