nonnegotiable:/ˌnɒn.nɪˈɡəʊ.ʃi.ə.bəl/ (US) /ˌnɑːn.nɪˈɡoʊ.ʃi.ə.bəl/
nonnegotiable: adj.|不可协商的，没有商量余地的|Not open to discussion or modification; fixed and unchangeable.|The price is nonnegotiable.  价格不可协商。|non-negotiable, inflexible, unalterable|negotiable, flexible|adjective
nonnegotiables:/ˌnɒn.nɪˈɡəʊ.ʃi.ə.bəlz/ (US) /ˌnɑːn.nɪˈɡoʊ.ʃi.ə.bəlz/
nonnegotiables: n.|不可协商的事项|Things that are not open to discussion or compromise.|Safety standards are nonnegotiables in this company.  安全标准在这家公司是不可协商的。|essentials, must-haves, requirements|options, preferences|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + negotiable.  "Negotiable" comes from the Late Latin negotiabilis, meaning "capable of being transacted," from negotiari "to transact business."
