antisabbatarian:/ˌæntiˌsæbəˈtɛəriən/
|adj. n.|反对安息日(尤其指基督教安息日)的(人)；非守安息日的(人)|Opposed to the observance of the Sabbath, especially the Christian Sabbath.|He held strong antisabbatarian views, believing that every day should be treated equally. 他持有强烈的反对安息日的观点，认为每一天都应该被平等对待。|
|n.|反对安息日主义者|A person who opposes the observance of the Sabbath.|The antisabbatarian argued that rest was important but shouldn't be confined to a single day.  这位反对安息日主义者认为休息很重要，但不应该局限于一天。|
|近义词:non-sabbatarian|反义词:sabbatarian|词性:adjective, noun
