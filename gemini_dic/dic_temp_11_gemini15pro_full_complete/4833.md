alibiing:/ˈælɪbaɪɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
提供不在场证明; 为...辩解
The act of providing an alibi, or offering an excuse.
He was alibiing for his friend who was suspected of the robbery. 他在为被怀疑抢劫的朋友提供不在场证明。
近义词: excusing, justifying, defending
反义词: accusing, blaming, incriminating
词性: verb (present participle)

alibi:/ˈælɪbaɪ/
noun
不在场证明; 托辞; 辩解
A claim or piece of evidence that one was elsewhere when an act, typically a criminal one, is alleged to have taken place.
He had a solid alibi for the night of the murder.  他有充分的不在场证明，证明谋杀案发生当晚他在别处。
近义词: excuse, pretext, justification
反义词: accusation, blame, incrimination
词性: noun


词源: From Latin *alibi, from alibī 'elsewhere'.
