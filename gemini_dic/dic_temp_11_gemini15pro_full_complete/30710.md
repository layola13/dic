certs:/sɜːrts/ | n. | 证书；凭证 | Certificates (plural form).  A document serving as evidence or as written testimony, as of status, qualifications, privileges, or the truth of something. | She finally received her teaching certs. 她终于拿到了教师资格证书。| synonyms: credentials, qualifications, licenses | antonyms: disqualifications | noun
