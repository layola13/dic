deemphasizing: /ˌdiːˈɛmfəˌsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |  降低…的重视程度;淡化 | Reducing the emphasis on something; making something seem less important. | The new CEO is deemphasizing the importance of individual sales targets in favor of a team-based approach.  新任首席执行官正在淡化个人销售目标的重要性，转而支持基于团队的方法。| The company is deemphasizing its reliance on traditional advertising and focusing more on digital marketing. 公司正在降低对传统广告的依赖，更多地关注数字营销。 |  downplaying, minimizing, understating | emphasizing, highlighting, stressing | verb
