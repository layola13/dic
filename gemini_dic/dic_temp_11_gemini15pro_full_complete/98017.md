词源:  From in- (not) + opportune (suitable or convenient).  "Opportune" comes from Latin "opportunus,"  meaning "fit, convenient," from "ob" (towards) + "portus" (harbor).  The idea is that a favorable wind blowing towards a harbor is "opportune."
