nonelectrical:/ˌnɒnɪˈlɛktrɪk(ə)l/
adj.
非电的；不使用电的
Not operated by electricity; not producing electricity.
例句：They used a nonelectrical can opener. 他们使用了一个手动开罐器。
例句：The campsite offers nonelectrical tent sites.  这个营地提供不带电的帐篷营地。
近义词：manual, mechanical
反义词：electrical, electric
词性：形容词

词源：
Prefix "non-" (meaning "not") + "electrical" (related to electricity).  The prefix "non-" comes from Latin.  "Electrical" ultimately derives from the Greek word "elektron" meaning "amber," as early experiments with static electricity involved rubbing amber.
