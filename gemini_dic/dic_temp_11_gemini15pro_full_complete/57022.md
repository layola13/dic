doubleminded:/ˌdʌbəlˈmaɪndɪd/ | adj. | 三心二意的，犹豫不决的 | Having two minds; undecided; wavering between two opinions or courses of action; unstable in purpose. | Example 1: He was doubleminded about accepting the job offer, torn between the higher salary and the longer commute. 他对接受这份工作犹豫不决，在更高的薪水和更长的通勤时间之间左右为难。

Example 2:  A doubleminded man is unstable in all his ways.  三心二意的人，他的道路步步不稳。(引自圣经雅各书) | 近义词: ambivalent, irresolute, uncertain, wavering, hesitant | 反义词: resolute, decisive, determined, steadfast, single-minded | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From double + minded.  "Minded" in this context means having a certain mental disposition or intention.  The prefix "double" indicates two such dispositions or intentions, hence the meaning of being divided in mind or purpose.
