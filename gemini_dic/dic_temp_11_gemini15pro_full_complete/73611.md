forewarners:/fɔːrˈwɔːrnərz/
n.
预警者，预先警告的人
People who give warning of something bad or unpleasant that is going to happen.
The forewarners of the economic crisis were ignored.  那些对经济危机发出预警的人被忽视了。
synonyms: harbingers, heralds, messengers, prophets
antonyms: followers, reactors
