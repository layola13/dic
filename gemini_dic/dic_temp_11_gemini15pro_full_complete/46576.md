damageabilities:/ˌdæmɪdʒəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |损害性，易损性|The damageabilities of different materials vary greatly. 不同材料的损害性差异很大。|例句：The report assessed the damageabilities of various infrastructure components to extreme weather events.  这份报告评估了各种基础设施组件在极端天气事件中的易损性。

The damageabilities of coastal communities to rising sea levels are a major concern.  沿海社区对海平面上升的易损性是一个主要问题。|近义词：vulnerability, fragility, susceptibility|反义词：resistance, durability, resilience|词性:名词
