fasttrack:/ˈfæstˌtræk/ (US), /ˈfɑːstˌtræk/ (UK)| n. v. adj.|快速通道，快速处理，加速|To expedite a process or project.  A route or method that allows something to be done or achieved more quickly than usual. |

例句：
The project was fasttracked to meet the deadline.
这个项目被快速推进以赶上截止日期。

The government is fasttracking the new legislation.
政府正在加速新立法的进程。


近义词：expedite, accelerate, rush, hasten|
反义词：delay, hinder, obstruct, slow down|
词性: noun, verb, adjective
