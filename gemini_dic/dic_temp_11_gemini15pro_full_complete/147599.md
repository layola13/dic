overplants:/ˌoʊvərˈplænts/ | n. | 过量种植的植物;  过密种植的植物 | Plants that have been planted too close together or in excessive numbers, leading to overcrowding. | The farmer removed some of the overplants to give the remaining seedlings more space to grow. 农民移除了一些过密种植的植物，以便给剩余的幼苗更多的生长空间。| 近义词: overcrowded plants | 反义词: sparsely planted plants | 词性: 名词

overplants:/ˌoʊvərˈplænts/ | v. |  过量种植; 过密种植 | To plant too many plants in a given area. | She overplanted the garden, and now the seedlings are struggling for resources. 她在这个花园里种植了过多的植物，现在幼苗都在争夺资源。| 近义词: overcrowd, overseed | 反义词: underplant | 词性: 动词
