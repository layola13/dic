naphthene:/ˈnæfθiːn/| n.|环烷烃|A type of hydrocarbon with a ring structure, also known as a cycloalkane.|Naphthenes are important components of some crude oils. 环烷烃是一些原油的重要成分。|The stability of naphthenes makes them valuable in lubricant production. 环烷烃的稳定性使其在润滑剂生产中具有重要价值。|近义词：cycloalkane|反义词:|名词
