godot:/ˈɡɒdoʊ/ (US), /ˈɡɒdəʊ/ (UK)| n.|戈多（人名；指虚无缥缈地等待的人或物）|Refers to the title character in Samuel Beckett's play "Waiting for Godot," symbolizing something or someone that is awaited but never arrives, representing hope, despair, and the human condition.|例句：They waited for their Godot, a solution that never materialized. 他们等待着他们的戈多，一个永远不会实现的解决方案。

例句：The promised funding was their Godot, always hoped for but never received.  承诺的资金就是他们的戈多，总是充满希望却从未收到。


近义词：phantom, illusion, chimera|反义词:reality, certainty|词性:noun
