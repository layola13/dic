nonacademician:/ˌnɒnˌækədəˈmɪʃən/ |n.|非院士；非学会会员|A person who is not a member of an academy or learned society. |例句：He was a respected scientist, although a nonacademician.  他是一位受人尊敬的科学家，尽管他不是院士。

例句：Many nonacademicians contribute significantly to their fields. 许多非院士对各自的领域做出了重大贡献。|近义词：non-member, outsider|反义词：academician, member|词性: noun
