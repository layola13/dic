overformalize:/ˌoʊvərˈfɔːrməˌlaɪz/ | v. | 过度形式化，过分拘泥形式 | To make something excessively formal, often to the point of hindering effectiveness or naturalness. | They overformalized the process, creating so much paperwork that it slowed everything down. 他们过度形式化了这个流程，创建了如此多的文书工作，以至于拖慢了一切。|  The company's communication style is overformalized, making it difficult for employees to connect with each other.  这家公司的沟通方式过于正式，使员工难以相互联系。 | synonyms: over-regulate, ritualize, bureaucratize | antonyms: simplify, streamline, informalize | verb

overformalize:/ˌoʊvərˈfɔːrməˌlaɪz/ | adj. (less common) | 过于正式的，过分拘泥形式的 | Characterized by excessive formality. |  The overformalized language of the contract made it difficult to understand.  合同中过于正式的语言使其难以理解。| Her overformalized approach to teaching alienated the students. 她过于正式的教学方法使学生疏远了她。| synonyms: excessively formal, rigid, stilted | antonyms: informal, casual, relaxed | adjective
