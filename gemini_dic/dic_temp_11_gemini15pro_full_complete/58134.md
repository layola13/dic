drose:/droʊz/| n.| 残花，凋谢的花朵;  (古语)渣滓，糟粕|A drose is a faded flower, often one that has fallen from its stem. It can also refer to dregs or refuse (archaic usage).|The ground was littered with droses from the rose bushes.|近义词: withered flower, dead flower, faded blossom|反义词: fresh flower, blooming flower|词性:名词


词源:  From Middle English *dros, probably from Old Norse *dros, meaning "dregs, lees."  Related to Old English *drōs, meaning "dregs."
