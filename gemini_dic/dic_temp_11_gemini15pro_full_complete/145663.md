overcontritely:/ˌoʊvərˈkɒnˌtraɪtli/ | adv. | 过分忏悔地，过度懊悔地 |  In a manner that expresses excessive or exaggerated contrition.  It suggests an apology that goes beyond what is necessary or sincere, perhaps for manipulative purposes. | He apologized overcontritely, hoping to avoid further punishment. 他过分忏悔地道歉，希望能避免进一步的惩罚。|  She overcontritely begged for forgiveness, batting her eyelashes dramatically. 她过度懊悔地乞求原谅，夸张地眨着睫毛。 | excessively apologetic, overly repentant, exaggeratedly remorseful | unapologetic, unrepentant | adverb
