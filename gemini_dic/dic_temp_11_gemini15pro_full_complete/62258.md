encumbrancer:/ɪnˈkʌmbrənsər/
n.
妨碍者；阻碍者；累赘
A person or thing that encumbers; something that hinders or burdens.
The heavy backpack was an encumbrancer on the long hike.  这个沉重的背包在长途徒步旅行中是个累赘。
The old regulations are an encumbrancer to progress.  旧的规章制度阻碍了进步。
Synonym: hindrance, burden, obstacle, impediment
Antonym: aid, help, assistance, facilitator
词性: 名词
