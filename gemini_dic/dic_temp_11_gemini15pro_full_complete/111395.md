living:/ˈlɪvɪŋ/| adj. n.|活着的，现存的；生计|Something that is living is alive now.  A living thing is something that is alive, such as a plant or animal.  Living can also refer to the way that someone earns money to support themselves.|He makes a living by selling handmade jewelry. 他靠卖手工珠宝为生。|近义词：alive, existent, surviving|反义词：dead, extinct, deceased|词性:形容词

living:/ˈlɪvɪŋ/| n.|生活，生计|The way that you earn money in order to live.|They earn their living from farming. 他们以务农为生。|近义词：livelihood, subsistence, existence|反义词：death|词性:名词
