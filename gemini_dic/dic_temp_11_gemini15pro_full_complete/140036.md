n.
the science and study of wine and winemaking
He studied oenology at university. 他在大学学习葡萄酒酿造学。
The region is renowned for its oenology. 该地区以其葡萄酒酿造而闻名。
Synonyms: viniculture, viticulture, winemaking
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a field of study)
词性: Noun


Etymology: From French *œnologie*, from Ancient Greek *οἶνος* (*oînos*, “wine”) + *-λογία* (*-logía*, “-logy”).
