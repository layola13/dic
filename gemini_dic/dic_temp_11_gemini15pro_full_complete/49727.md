democratize: /dɪˈmɒkrətaɪz/ (UK) /dɪˈmɑːkrətaɪz/ (US) | v. |使民主化；使大众化|To make something accessible to everyone, or to make a country or organization more democratic.|The internet has democratized access to information. 互联网使获取信息大众化了。|The government is trying to democratize the education system. 政府正试图使教育系统民主化。|synonyms: popularize, broaden, universalize |antonyms: restrict, limit, monopolize |verb

democratization: /dɪˌmɒkrətɪˈzeɪʃən/ (UK) /dɪˌmɑːkrətɪˈzeɪʃən/ (US) | n. |民主化；大众化|The process of making something accessible to everyone, or of making a country or organization more democratic.|The democratization of technology has empowered individuals. 科技的民主化赋予了个人力量。|The democratization of art has led to a wider range of artistic expression. 艺术的大众化导致了更广泛的艺术表达。|synonyms: popularization, liberalization |antonyms: restriction, limitation |noun
