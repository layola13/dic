empiricisms:/ɪmˈpɪrɪsɪzəmz/| n.|经验主义，经验主义理论|The theory that all knowledge is derived from sense-experience. Stimulated by the rise of experimental science, it developed in the 17th and 18th centuries, expounded in particular by John Locke, George Berkeley, and David Hume.|His philosophy is based on empiricisms. 他的哲学是基于经验主义的。|近义词：experientialism, pragmatism|反义词：rationalism, idealism|词性: 名词
