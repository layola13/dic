impinge:/ɪmˈpɪndʒ/ /ɪmˈpɪnʤ/ | v. | 撞击，冲击；侵犯，影响 | to have an effect, especially a negative one; to hit or dash against something | The noise of the traffic impinged on our conversation. 交通噪音干扰了我们的谈话。| synonyms: encroach, infringe, intrude, affect, influence | antonyms: avoid, dodge, assist, aid | verb

impinge:/ɪmˈpɪndʒ/ /ɪmˈpɪnʤ/ | v. | 对…有强烈影响 | to have a strong effect on someone or something |  Personal problems began to impinge on her work. 个人问题开始影响她的工作。| synonyms: impact, affect, influence | antonyms: detach, disconnect, separate | verb
