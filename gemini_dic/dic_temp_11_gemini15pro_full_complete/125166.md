multibillions:/ˌmʌltiˈbɪljənz/
|n.|数十亿，数百亿| An extremely large, but unspecified, number of billions.  Referring to a sum of money or a quantity in the billions, but not specifying the exact number.  It implies a vast amount, significantly more than just a few billion. |The company is worth multibillions of dollars.  这家公司价值数百亿美元。|Synonyms: countless billions, vast billions, numerous billions|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider) a small amount, a few million, negligible|Noun

multibillions:/ˌmʌltiˈbɪljənz/
|adj.|数十亿的，数百亿的 | Worth or costing many billions of dollars or other units; extremely expensive. |The multibillion-dollar project was completed ahead of schedule.  这个耗资数百亿美元的项目提前完成了。|Synonyms: immensely costly, exorbitantly priced, astronomically expensive|Antonyms: inexpensive, cheap, affordable|Adjective
