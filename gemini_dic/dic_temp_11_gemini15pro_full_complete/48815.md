defluxion:/dɪˈflʌkʃən/
n.
流出；下泻；分泌物
a flowing down; a discharge of mucus or other fluid, especially from the nose or eyes.
A severe cold caused a defluxion from his nose.  严重的感冒导致他流鼻涕。
近义词:discharge, flow, effusion, catarrh
反义词:influx, absorption
词性:noun

defluxion:/dɪˈflʌkʃən/
n.
(旧)炎症;卡他性炎
(archaic) inflammation; catarrh
The doctor diagnosed a defluxion of the lungs. 医生诊断出肺部炎症。
近义词:inflammation, catarrh
反义词: None
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēfluxio, from Latin dēfluere (“to flow down”), from dē (“down, away from”) + fluere (“to flow”).
