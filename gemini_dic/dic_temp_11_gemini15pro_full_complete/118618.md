Messerschmitt:/ˈmɛsəʃmɪt/ | n. | 梅塞施密特 |A German aircraft manufacturing corporation, especially known for its fighter aircraft designs during World War II.  The name is derived from the founder Willy Messerschmitt.| The Messerschmitt Bf 109 was one of the most important fighters of the Luftwaffe. 梅塞施密特Bf 109是德国空军最重要的战斗机之一。|Synonym:  Bf 109 (often used interchangeably, although technically a specific model),  Focke-Wulf (another German aircraft manufacturer),  Heinkel (another German aircraft manufacturer) | Antonym:  Spitfire (a major British fighter aircraft, often pitted against the Bf 109),  P-51 Mustang (a major American fighter aircraft) | noun
