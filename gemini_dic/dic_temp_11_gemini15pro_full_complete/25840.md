burgoo:/ˈbɜːrɡuː/
n.
浓稠的炖菜；大杂烩
A thick stew or porridge, often made with various meats and vegetables.
Example: The annual burgoo festival is a popular event in Kentucky.  肯塔基州一年一度的浓汤节是一个很受欢迎的活动。

Example:  They served a hearty burgoo at the campsite.  他们在营地供应了丰盛的浓汤。
近义词: stew, chowder, soup
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a light broth or clear soup)
词性: noun
