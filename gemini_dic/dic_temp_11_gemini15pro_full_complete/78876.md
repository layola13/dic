ginglymus:/ˈɡɪŋɡləməs/| n. | 铰链关节|A hinge joint; a joint that allows movement in only one plane, like the elbow or knee.|The elbow is a classic example of a ginglymus, permitting flexion and extension.|近义词：hinge joint|反义词: ball and socket joint|词性:noun
ginglymoid:/ˈdʒɪŋɡlɪˌmɔɪd/ | adj. | 铰链状的|Resembling or functioning as a ginglymus; hinge-like.|The ginglymoid joint of the jaw allows for efficient chewing.|近义词：hinge-like|反义词: spheroidal|词性:adjective
