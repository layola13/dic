disaffirmation:/ˌdɪsˌæfərˈmeɪʃən/
n.
否认，拒绝承认，废除，撤销
The act of disaffirming; denial; refusal to affirm; revocation.
The minor's disaffirmation of the contract rendered it void.  未成年人对合同的否认使其无效。
近义词：repudiation, denial, rejection, revocation
反义词：affirmation, confirmation, acceptance
词性：名词

disaffirm:/ˌdɪsəˈfɜːrm/
v.
否认，拒绝承认，废除，撤销
To deny or contradict; to refuse to confirm or ratify; to annul or revoke.
The teenager disaffirmed the contract he had signed while still a minor.  这名青少年否认了他在未成年时签署的合同。
近义词：repudiate, deny, reject, revoke
反义词：affirm, confirm, accept
词性：动词

词源：
From Late Latin *disaffirmāre, from Latin dis- (“not, un-”) + affirmāre (“to affirm, confirm”).
