Orwell: /ˈɔːrwɛl/ | n. | 奥威尔 |  Primarily known as the pen name of Eric Arthur Blair, a British novelist, essayist, journalist, and critic.  | Example: Orwell's "Nineteen Eighty-Four" is a dystopian classic. 奥威尔的《一九八四》是一部反乌托邦经典著作。 | Synonym: Blair (his real name) | Antonym: N/A | noun

Orwellian: /ɔːrˈwɛliən/ | adj. | 奥威尔式的；极权主义的 | Characteristic of the totalitarian or manipulative social practices depicted in George Orwell's writings, especially in his novel "Nineteen Eighty-Four." | Example: The government's surveillance program felt Orwellian. 政府的监控计划让人感觉像是奥威尔式的。| Synonym: dystopian, totalitarian, authoritarian | Antonym: utopian, democratic, libertarian | adjective
