ecumenical:/ˌɛkjuˈmɛnɪkəl/ /ˌiːkjuˈmɛnɪkəl/
adjective
普遍的，世界的；基督教各教派合一的
promoting or relating to unity among the world's Christian churches.

The Pope called for greater ecumenical cooperation.
教皇呼吁加强基督教各教派的合作。


The conference was attended by representatives of many different faiths, demonstrating a truly ecumenical spirit.
许多不同信仰的代表参加了这次会议，展现了真正的普世精神。


Synonym:universal, global, worldwide, general, catholic
Antonym:parochial, local, sectarian, divisive
