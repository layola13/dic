chimney:/ˈtʃɪmni/ | n. | 烟囱; 壁炉 |A vertical channel or pipe that conducts smoke and combustion gases up from a fireplace, furnace, or other device and releases them into the atmosphere.| Smoke curled from the chimney. 烟从烟囱里袅袅升起。 | synonyms: flue, stack, vent | antonyms: None | noun
