on-the-pad:/ɒn ðə pæd/ /ɑːn ðə pæd/ | adj. | 记账的，未付款的，赊账的 |  Refers to expenses or debts that have been incurred but not yet paid.  It implies that the amount is recorded or "on the pad" of paper awaiting payment.  Primarily used in informal contexts. | I've got a few drinks on the pad at the pub. 我在酒吧里还有一些赊账的酒。| The company car repairs are on the pad until next month. 公司的汽车维修费用记在账上，到下个月才付。 | synonyms: outstanding, unpaid, due, owed | antonyms: paid, settled, cleared | adjective
