agonizer:/ˈæɡənaɪzər/| n.| 苦思冥想者，深思熟虑者| someone who spends a lot of time thinking about a difficult decision or problem.  He was an agonizer, endlessly debating the pros and cons of every decision. 他是一个苦思冥想者，无休止地争论每一个决定的利弊。|近义词：worrier, fretter, brooder|反义词: decider, resolver|词性: noun

agonizer:/ˈæɡənaɪzər/| n.| 使人痛苦的事; 令人苦恼的事 | something that causes great pain or suffering. The agonizing decision was an agonizer for everyone involved.  这个令人痛苦的决定对所有参与者来说都是一件令人苦恼的事。|近义词：ordeal, torment, tribulation|反义词: comfort, solace|词性: noun
