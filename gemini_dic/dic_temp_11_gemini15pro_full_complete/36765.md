colluding:/kəˈluːdɪŋ/
verb
共谋；勾结；串通
acting together secretly or illegally in order to deceive or cheat someone
The two companies were accused of colluding to fix prices.  这两家公司被指控串通操纵价格。

Several officials were accused of colluding with the enemy. 几名官员被指控与敌人勾结。

conspiring, plotting, conniving, scheming
cooperating, collaborating
