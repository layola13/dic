overfrightens:/ˌoʊvərˈfraɪtənz/| v. |过度惊吓|To frighten excessively.  The loud noise overfrightened the children.|例句：The horror movie overfrightened the young child.  这部恐怖电影把小孩子吓坏了。

例句：The sudden appearance of the dog overfrightened the cat. 狗的突然出现把猫吓坏了。

近义词：terrify, scare, alarm|反义词:calm, soothe, comfort|词性:动词
