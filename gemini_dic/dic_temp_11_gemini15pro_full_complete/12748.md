assiduous:/əˈsɪdʒuəs/| adj.|勤勉的，刻苦的|Showing great care and perseverance.|She is an assiduous student, always studying hard. 她是一个勤奋的学生，总是努力学习。|近义词：diligent, industrious, hardworking, persistent|反义词：lazy, indolent, negligent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *assiduus*, from *assidere* "to sit beside," from *ad-* "to, at" + *sedere* "to sit."  The original sense implied constant attendance or devotion to a task.
