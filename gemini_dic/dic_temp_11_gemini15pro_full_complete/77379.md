Also, a challenge or ordeal.|Running the gauntlet was a common form of punishment in the past.  跑过两排持械士兵之间是一种旧时的常见惩罚。| 近义词:glove, mitten, challenge, trial, ordeal | 反义词:surrender, acceptance | 词性:名词


词源: From Old French *gantelet, diminutive of gant ("glove"), from Frankish *want, from Proto-Germanic *wantuz.
