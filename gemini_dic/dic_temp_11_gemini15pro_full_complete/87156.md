herbivorous:/hɜːrˈbɪvərəs/ | adj. | 食草的 | Feeding on plants; plant-eating. |例句：Cows are herbivorous animals. 奶牛是食草动物。|例句：Herbivorous dinosaurs roamed the earth millions of years ago.  数百万年前，食草恐龙在地球上漫游。| 近义词：vegetarian, phytophagous | 反义词：carnivorous |词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *herbivorus*, from *herba* (herb) + *-vorus* (eating), related to *vorare* (to devour).
