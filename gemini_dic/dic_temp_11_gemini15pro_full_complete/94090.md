impatient:/ɪmˈpeɪʃnt/ | adj. | 不耐烦的，急躁的 | Easily annoyed or irritated by delay or opposition; restless. |  He became impatient waiting for her to finish. 他等她做完有些不耐烦了。| synonyms: restless, fidgety, edgy, anxious, eager, keen | antonyms: patient, tolerant, calm, composed | adjective

impatience:/ɪmˈpeɪʃəns/ | n. |  不耐烦，焦躁 | The tendency to be easily annoyed or irritated by delay or opposition. |  Her impatience was evident in the way she tapped her foot.  她焦躁不安，不停地用脚敲着地板。| synonyms: restlessness, irritability, edginess, anxiety | antonyms: patience, tolerance, calmness, composure | noun
