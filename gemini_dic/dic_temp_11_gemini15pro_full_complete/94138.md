impedimental:/ɪmˌpedɪˈmentl/
adjective
阻碍的，妨碍的
hindering or obstructing progress.
The cumbersome bureaucracy was impedimental to the project's success.  繁琐的官僚机构阻碍了项目的成功。
impedimental regulations  妨碍性的规章
synonyms: hindering, obstructing, hampering, inhibiting, restrictive, deterrent
antonyms: helpful, facilitating, aiding, assisting
词性: adjective
