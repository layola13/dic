gulper:/ˈɡʌlpər/| n.|吞食者；大口吞咽的人（或动物）；一种深海鱼|Someone or something that gulps, especially a deep-sea fish with a large mouth.|The pelican is a prodigious gulper of fish. 鹈鹕是一种吞食大量鱼类的动物。|Synonyms: glutton, gorger|Antonyms: nibbler|noun


gulper:/ˈɡʌlpər/| n.|吞食者；狼吞虎咽的人；一种深海鱼|A deep-sea fish with a large mouth and stomach, capable of swallowing prey larger than itself.  Also refers to anyone who gulps food or drink.|The gulper eel can unhinge its jaw to swallow enormous prey.  大嘴鳗可以张开它的下巴吞下巨大的猎物。|Synonyms: deep-sea fish, glutton, gorger|Antonyms: nibbler|noun
