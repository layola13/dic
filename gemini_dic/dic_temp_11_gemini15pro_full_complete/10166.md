apophlegmatizant: /əˌpɒflɛɡˈmætɪzənt/ /əˌpɑːflɛɡˈmætɪzənt/
noun
祛痰剂; 促痰药
A medicine that promotes the discharge of phlegm from the respiratory tract.
Example: The doctor prescribed an apophlegmatizant to help clear the patient's congested lungs. 医生给病人开了祛痰药，以帮助清除病人拥塞的肺部。
Example:  The apophlegmatizant helped to loosen the mucus in her chest. 这种祛痰药帮助她疏通了胸部的粘液。
synonym: expectorant, mucolytic
antonym:  None (no direct antonym for a medicine with this specific function)
