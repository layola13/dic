|n.|神仙鱼|A group of brightly colored, laterally compressed marine fish found in tropical and subtropical waters.  They are known for their vibrant colors and intricate patterns.|We saw a school of angelfishes while snorkeling in the coral reef. 我们在珊瑚礁浮潜时看到了一群神仙鱼。|
