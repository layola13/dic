improvable:/ɪmˈpruːvəbl/
adjective
可改进的
Capable of being improved or made better.
This essay is improvable with a little more effort. 这篇文章稍加努力就可以改进。
Her writing skills are definitely improvable. 她的写作技巧肯定可以提高。
synonyms:  amenable, betterable, corrigible, enhancible, refinable
antonyms:  fixed, immutable, incorrigible, perfect, unalterable
词性: adjective
