libatory:/ˈlaɪbəˌtɔːri/ /ˈlɪbəˌtɔːri/
adjective
奠酒的；祭酒的；庆祝的
Relating to or suitable for the pouring out of a drink as an offering to a god.  Also, pertaining to drinking or feasting.
The libatory ritual was performed at dawn.  黎明时分举行了奠酒仪式。

The family gathered for a libatory celebration of their grandfather's life. 全家聚在一起，举杯庆祝祖父的一生。
近义词:ceremonial, ritualistic, festive, celebratory
反义词:abstemious, sober
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin lībātōrius (“pertaining to libation”), from lībātiō (“libation”), from lībō (“I pour out, I take a little from”), from Proto-Italic *leibō, from Proto-Indo-European *leyb- (“to pour, to libate”).
