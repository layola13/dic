outnumber:/ˌaʊtˈnʌmbər/
verb
在数量上超过
to be greater in number than someone or something
例句：The girls outnumber the boys in our class. 我们班女生比男生多。

例句：They outnumber us two to one. 他们的数量是我们的两倍。

近义词：exceed, surpass, outweigh
反义词：be outnumbered by
词性：v.
