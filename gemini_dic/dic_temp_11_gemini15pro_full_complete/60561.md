elasmobranchii:/ɪˌlæzməˈbræŋkiaɪ/| n. |板鳃类|A subclass of cartilaginous fishes (Chondrichthyes) that includes sharks, rays, skates, and sawfish, characterized by having five to seven pairs of gill openings on each side of the head, plate-like gills, and a skeleton made of cartilage rather than bone.|Sharks and rays are members of the elasmobranchii subclass.|近义词：chondrichthyes (软骨鱼类) |反义词: (No direct antonym; could consider bony fish - Osteichthyes)|noun
