humanics:/hjuːˈmænɪks/
n.
人文科学; 人文学科;
The study of subjects such as literature, philosophy, and history that are concerned with human thought and culture.
例句：She majored in humanics in college. 她大学主修人文科学。

例句：The university has a strong humanics department. 这所大学有人文科学系很强。

近义词：humanities, liberal arts
反义词: science, technology
词性: noun
