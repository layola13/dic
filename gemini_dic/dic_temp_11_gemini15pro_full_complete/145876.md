overdesire:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈzaɪər/
n.|过度欲望，过分的渴望|An excessive or inordinate desire for something, often beyond what is reasonable or healthy. |His overdesire for power ultimately led to his downfall. 他对权力的过度渴望最终导致了他的垮台。|近义词: lust, greed, avarice, covetousness|反义词: contentment, moderation, indifference|词性:名词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer-, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "desire" (from Old French desirer, from Latin desiderare "long for, wish for, miss").  The prefix "over-" indicates an excess or surpassing of a normal or acceptable limit.
