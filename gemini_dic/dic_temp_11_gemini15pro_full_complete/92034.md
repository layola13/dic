hyperpatriotisms:/ˌhaɪpərˈpeɪtriətɪzəmz/
|n.|极端爱国主义|Excessive patriotism; exaggerated or fanatical patriotism.|The rise of hyperpatriotisms across the globe is a worrying trend. 全球极端爱国主义的兴起是一个令人担忧的趋势.|His speeches were filled with hyperpatriotisms, bordering on jingoism. 他的演讲充满了极端爱国主义，近乎沙文主义。|近义词: ultranationalism, jingoism, chauvinism|反义词: internationalism, cosmopolitanism|词性: 名词

Etymology:
hyper- (Greek): over, excessive + patriotism (from patriot + -ism)
