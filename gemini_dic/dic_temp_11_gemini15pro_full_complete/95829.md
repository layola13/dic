incorporator:/ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪtər/| n. |公司创办人；注册登记人|A person or entity that applies for and receives a corporate charter from a government, bringing the corporation into legal existence.|Example: The incorporators filed the necessary paperwork with the state to establish the new company. 公司创办人向州政府提交了必要的文件以建立新公司。

Example:  The original incorporators of the charity were a group of local philanthropists.  这家慈善机构最初的注册登记人是当地的一群慈善家。|Synonym: founder, organizer, charter member|Antonym: N/A|Noun
