jerrymander: /ˈdʒɛriˌmændər/ (US), /ˈdʒɛriˌmændə(r)/ (UK)| n. vt.|不公正地划分选区；操纵选区|To divide a state, county, etc., into election districts so as to give one political party a majority in many districts while concentrating the voting strength of the other party into as few districts as possible.|The opposition party accused the government of gerrymandering to maintain its power.|近义词:manipulate, rig|反义词:fair apportionment|词性:名词, 动词
