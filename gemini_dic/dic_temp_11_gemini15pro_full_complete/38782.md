conduct's:/ˈkɒndʌkts/ | n. | 行为，举止；管理，经营；引导 | conduct's is the possessive form of conduct, referring to the behavior, management, or guidance belonging to or associated with something or someone. | The company's conduct in this matter has been questionable.  这家公司在这件事上的行为令人质疑。| synonyms: behavior, demeanor, management, guidance | antonyms: misconduct, mismanagement | noun

conduct's:/kənˈdʌkts/ | v. |  (第三人称单数)引导；指挥；带领；传导(电或热) | conduct's is the third-person singular present tense form of the verb "conduct," meaning to lead, guide, manage, or transmit. | He conducts the orchestra with great passion. 他以极大的热情指挥乐队。 | synonyms: lead, guide, manage, direct, transmit | antonyms: follow, obstruct | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French conduire, from Latin conducere ‘bring together, lead’, from con- ‘together’ + ducere ‘to lead’.
