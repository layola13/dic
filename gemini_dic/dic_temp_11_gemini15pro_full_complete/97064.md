inferiorities: /ɪnˌfɪəriˈɔːrətiz/
n.
自卑感；劣等感；缺点
feelings of inadequacy and lack of confidence; shortcomings or weaknesses.

She struggled with feelings of inferiority.
她与自卑感作斗争。

His inferiority complex stemmed from his childhood experiences.
他的自卑情结源于他的童年经历。

The product's inferiorities were quickly apparent.
该产品的缺点很快显现出来。


Synonyms: shortcomings, weaknesses, flaws, defects, inadequacies, drawbacks
Antonyms: strengths, advantages, merits, virtues, superiorities

Etymology: From Late Middle English: from late Latin inferioritas, from Latin inferior ‘lower’.
