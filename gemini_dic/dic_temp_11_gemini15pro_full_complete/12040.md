articulated:/ɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪtɪd/
articulated| adj.|清晰表达的，口齿伶俐的；有关节的| expressed clearly and fluently; having joints or jointed segments.|She delivered a well-articulated speech about the importance of education. 她发表了一篇关于教育重要性的精彩演讲。|The skeleton is an articulated framework.  这副骨架是一个有关节的框架。|近义词：clear, eloquent, distinct, jointed|反义词：inarticulate, mumbled, unclear, fused|词性:形容词

articulate:/ɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪt/
articulate| v.|清晰地表达；连接|to express (an idea or feeling) fluently and coherently; to form a joint |He struggled to articulate his feelings about the loss. 他难以表达他对失去亲人的感受。|The bones in the hand articulate with each other. 手部的骨头相互连接。|近义词：express, voice, pronounce, connect|反义词：mumble, slur, disconnect|词性:动词
