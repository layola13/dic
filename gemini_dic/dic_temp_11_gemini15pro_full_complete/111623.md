localism:/ˈloʊkəlɪzəm/| n.|地方主义，地方观念|Preference for one's own area or region, especially when this results in limited outlook or hostility to outsiders. |The localism of the community made it difficult for newcomers to integrate. 这个社区的地方主义使得新来者很难融入。|近义词：regionalism, provincialism|反义词:globalism, internationalism|词性:noun

localism:/ˈloʊkəlɪzəm/| n.|方言，土语|A word, expression, or custom peculiar to a particular locality.|The use of localisms can make it hard for people from other areas to understand the conversation. 使用方言会使其他地区的人难以理解对话。|近义词：dialect, vernacular|反义词:standard language|词性:noun
