justificator:/ˌdʒʌstɪfɪˈkeɪtər/
noun
辩护者，证明者
One who justifies or defends something or someone.
He saw himself as a justificator of the company's actions. 他认为自己是公司行为的辩护者。
Synonyms: defender, advocate, apologist, vindicator
Antonyms: accuser, critic, opponent
n.

justificator:/ˌdʒʌstɪfɪˈkeɪtər/
noun
正当化者，使…合理化的人
One who makes something seem just or right.
The report served as a justificator for the increased budget. 这份报告为增加预算提供了合理的解释。
Synonyms: rationalizer, explainer
Antonyms:  detractor, disparager
n.
