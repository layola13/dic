bothies:/ˈbɒθiz/ (UK), /ˈboʊθiz/ (US)| n. |小屋，简陋的小屋（尤指苏格兰高地的）|Small, simple shelters, usually found in remote mountainous areas, especially in the Scottish Highlands, available for free use by hikers and climbers. |We stayed in a bothy overnight during our hike through the Cairngorms. 我们在穿越凯恩戈姆山脉的徒步旅行中在一个小屋里过夜。

|Hikers often rely on bothies for shelter in bad weather.  徒步旅行者经常依靠小屋来躲避恶劣天气。|近义词：huts, shelters, cabins|反义词: hotel, mansion|词性: 名词
