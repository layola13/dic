macrophage: /ˈmækɹəˌfeɪdʒ/ | n. | 巨噬细胞 | A type of white blood cell that engulfs and digests cellular debris, foreign substances, microbes, and cancer cells.  |  Macrophages play a crucial role in the immune system. 巨噬细胞在免疫系统中起着至关重要的作用。 |  Activated macrophages can release cytokines that influence the activity of other immune cells. 激活的巨噬细胞可以释放细胞因子，影响其他免疫细胞的活性。 |  monocyte, phagocyte | N/A | noun
