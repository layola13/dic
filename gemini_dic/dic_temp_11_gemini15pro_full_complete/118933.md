meteorical:/ˌmiːtiˈɔːrɪkəl/ /ˌmiːtiˈɑːrɪkəl/
adjective
流星般的，急速的，昙花一现的
Relating to a meteor or meteors; resembling a meteor in speed or brilliance; transiently brilliant or spectacular.
His meteoric rise to fame surprised everyone. 他迅速成名让每个人都感到惊讶。
Near synonyms: rapid, swift, speedy, fast, quick, accelerated, express, breakneck, blistering, lightning, whirlwind, sudden, abrupt, precipitate, hasty, hurried, headlong, impetuous, brisk
Antonyms: slow, gradual
词性: adjective

meteorical:/ˌmiːtiˈɔːrɪkəl/ /ˌmiːtiˈɑːrɪkəl/
