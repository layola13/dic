homelinesses:/ˈhoʊmlɪnɪsɪz/
noun
简陋；朴素；不修边幅；家的感觉（通常复数）
The state of being homely, simple, and unpretentious, often in a way that evokes a sense of comfort and familiarity.  Often used in the plural to refer to multiple instances or aspects of this quality.
Her homelinesses, while unconventional, gave her apartment a unique charm. 她的简陋虽然不拘一格，却赋予了她的公寓一种独特的魅力。
The homelinesses of the countryside cottage appealed to her. 乡村小屋的朴素吸引了她。
synonyms:simplicities, unpretentiousness, plainness, modesty
antonyms:elegance, sophistication, grandiosity, ostentation
