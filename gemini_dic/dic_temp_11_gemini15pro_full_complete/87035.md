hepatocele:/ˌhɛpətəˈsiːl/| n. |肝突出；肝疝|A hernia or swelling containing part of the liver.  It's a protrusion of the liver through an abnormal opening in the abdominal wall or diaphragm.|The patient presented with a large, painful hepatocele.|Synonym: hepatic hernia|Antonym: None|noun
