only:/ˈoʊnli/ (US), /ˈəʊnli/ (UK)| adj. adv.|唯一的，仅有的，只是|Restricted to a single person, thing, or group; exclusively.|She was the only person who could help. 她是唯一能帮忙的人。|近义词:sole, single, alone, merely, just|反义词:many, several, numerous|词性:形容词/副词

only:/ˈoʊnli/ (US), /ˈəʊnli/ (UK)| conj.|但是，可是，不过|Introducing a sentence, phrase, or clause that limits, contradicts, or contrasts with a preceding one.|He wanted to go, only he didn't have enough money. 他想去，只是没有足够的钱。|近义词:but, however, except|反义词: (无直接反义词)|词性:连词
