muscallonge:/ˈmʌskəlʌndʒ/ (US), /ˈmʌskəlɒndʒ/ (UK)| n.|北美狗鱼，麝香鱼|A large freshwater game fish (Esox masquinongy) of the pike family, native to North America and noted for its size and voracity.|例句：The angler landed a massive muscallonge after a long fight.  钓鱼者经过一番搏斗钓到了一条巨大的北美狗鱼。

例句：Muscallonge are apex predators in their freshwater habitats.  北美狗鱼是其淡水栖息地的顶级掠食者。|近义词：muskie, muskellunge, great pike|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ojibwe *maškinoše *great fish*.  Ultimately from Proto-Algonquian *mašk-* *big* + *-kin-* *fish* + *-ooše* *ugly, deformed*.
