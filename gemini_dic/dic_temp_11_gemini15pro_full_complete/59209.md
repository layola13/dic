dyspnoic:/dɪsˈpnɔɪk/ /dɪsˈpniːɪk/
adjective
呼吸困难的
experiencing difficulty in breathing
The patient presented as dyspnoic and tachycardic.  病人表现为呼吸困难和心动过速。
The asthmatic child was noticeably dyspnoic.  患哮喘的孩子明显呼吸困难。
近义词:breathless, short of breath
反义词: eupneic
