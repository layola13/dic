isomorphism:/ˌaɪsəˈmɔːrfɪzəm/ | n. | 同构，同形 | A one-to-one correspondence between two sets, which preserves the relations existing between elements in the sets.  |  The isomorphism between the two groups allows us to study their properties interchangeably. 这两个群之间的同构性使我们可以互换地研究它们的性质。| synonym: similarity, correspondence | antonym:  heteromorphism | noun
