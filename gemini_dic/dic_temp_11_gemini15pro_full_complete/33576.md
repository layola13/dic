cilic:/ˈsɪlɪk/ | adj. | 纤毛状的，有纤毛的 | Covered with cilia; resembling cilia. |  The paramecium is a ciliated protozoan. 草履虫是一种有纤毛的原生动物。| The surface of the cell is ciliated.  细胞表面有纤毛。| 近义词: ciliate, hairy | 反义词: smooth, glabrous | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *cilicium "eyelid, eyelash," from cilium "eyelid, eyelash."
