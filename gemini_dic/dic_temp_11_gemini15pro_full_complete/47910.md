decentralists:/diːˈsɛntrəlaɪsts/| n.|去中心化主义者；权力下放主义者|People who advocate for decentralization, distributing power away from a central authority.|Many decentralists believe that blockchain technology can empower individuals and communities.|近义词：autonomists, federalists|反义词：centralists, authoritarians|词性:noun

decentralist:/diːˈsɛntrəlaɪst/| adj.|去中心化的；权力下放的|Relating to or supporting decentralization.|A decentralist approach to governance could lead to more local autonomy.|近义词：distributed, autonomous|反义词：centralized, authoritarian|词性:adjective
