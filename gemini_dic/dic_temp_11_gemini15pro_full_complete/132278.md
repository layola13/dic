词源：

"Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not".

"Defective" comes from the Latin word "defectus", meaning "fault" or "imperfection".

"-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.

Therefore, "nondefectively" means "in a way that is not defective".
