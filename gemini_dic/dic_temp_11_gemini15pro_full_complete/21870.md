body-blow:/ˈbɒdi bləʊ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːdi bloʊ/ (US)| n.|沉重打击，重创|A damaging blow, either physical or metaphorical, that weakens or demoralizes someone or something.|The loss of their biggest client was a real body-blow to the small company.  失去他们最大的客户对这家小公司来说是一个真正的打击。

The team suffered a body-blow when their star player was injured.  当他们的明星球员受伤时，球队受到了重创。|近义词：setback, shock, jolt, wallop|反义词:boost, advantage, benefit|词性:名词
