indigestible:/ˌɪndɪˈdʒɛstəbl/ | adj. | 难以消化的；难理解的 |Not easily digested; difficult to understand. |例句：The steak was tough and indigestible. 牛排又硬又难消化。

例句：The professor's lectures were often long and indigestible. 教授的讲座通常又长又难理解。

近义词：unpalatable, inedible, incomprehensible | 反义词：digestible, palatable, comprehensible | 词性：形容词
