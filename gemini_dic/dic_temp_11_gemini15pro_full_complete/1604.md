acid-test:/ˈæsɪd tɛst/| n.|决定性考验，严峻的考验|A conclusive test that reveals the true worth or effectiveness of something.  It originates from the use of nitric acid to test gold for purity.|The real acid test of the new car will be how it handles in icy conditions. 这辆新车的真正考验将是它在冰雪路面上的操控性能。|synonyms:  crucible, trial, ordeal, litmus test|antonyms:  walk in the park, cakewalk|noun

acid-tests:/ˈæsɪd tɛsts/| n.|决定性考验，严峻的考验（复数）|Plural form of "acid test," referring to multiple conclusive tests that reveal the true worth or effectiveness of something.|The company faced several acid tests during the economic downturn. 在经济低迷时期，该公司面临着几项严峻的考验。|synonyms: trials, ordeals, litmus tests|antonyms: easy tasks, simple matters|noun
