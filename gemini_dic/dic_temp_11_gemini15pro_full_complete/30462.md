centrifugality: /ˌsɛntrɪˈfjuːɡælɪti/ | n. | 离心力，离心倾向 | The tendency to move away from a center. |  The centrifugality of the spinning top kept it upright.  旋转陀螺的离心力使它保持直立。| synonyms: centrifugal force, outward tendency | antonyms: centripetality, inward tendency | noun


centrifugality: /ˌsɛntrɪˈfjuːɡælɪti/ | n. | 离心趋势，分散趋势 | The tendency to move away from a central authority or focus. | The centrifugality of the empire led to its eventual fragmentation.  帝国的离心趋势导致了它最终的分裂。| synonyms: decentralization, fragmentation | antonyms: centralization, unification | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *centrifugus* (“fleeing from the center”) (from *centrum* (“center”) + *fugere* (“to flee”)) + *-alis* + *-ity*.
