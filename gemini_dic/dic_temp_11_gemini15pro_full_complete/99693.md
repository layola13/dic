interlocations:/ˌɪntərloʊˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |相互作用，相互关联，交织|The complex interlocations of social and economic factors make it difficult to isolate the cause of the problem.  社会和经济因素复杂的相互作用使得难以找出问题的根源。|例句：The interlocations of art and science are increasingly apparent in contemporary society. 艺术与科学的相互关联在当代社会越来越明显。

例句：The project explores the interlocations between language, culture, and identity. 该项目探讨了语言、文化和身份之间的相互关联。|近义词：interactions, interrelationships, connections, linkages|反义词: separations, disconnections|词性:名词
