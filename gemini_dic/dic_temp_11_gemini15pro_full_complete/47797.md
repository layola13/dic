decapod:/ˈdekəpɒd/ (UK) /ˈdekəˌpɑːd/ (US)| n. |十足目动物；十足动物|An animal, such as a crab, lobster, shrimp, or prawn, that has ten legs, typically five pairs of appendages on the thorax.|例句：The decapod crustaceans include crabs, lobsters, and shrimps. 十足目甲壳类动物包括螃蟹、龙虾和虾。

例句：The giant squid is a formidable predator, even for large decapods. 巨型乌贼即使对大型十足目动物来说也是可怕的捕食者。

近义词：crustacean|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
