nonspinning:/ˌnɒnˈspɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 非旋转的，无自旋的 | Not spinning; having no spin.  This refers to the absence of rotation, especially in the context of subatomic particles or celestial bodies. |  Electrons in certain orbitals are nonspinning.  某些轨道上的电子是非旋转的。|  The nonspinning black hole absorbed all matter around it.  这个不旋转的黑洞吸收了周围所有的物质。 | stationary, motionless, static | rotating, spinning | adjective
