laager:/ˈlɑːɡər/ (UK), /ˈlɑːɡər/ (US)| n.|营地，防御阵地|A camp or encampment, especially one formed by a circle of wagons for defense.|The pioneers formed a laager to protect themselves from attack.  拓荒者们围成防御阵地，以保护自己免受攻击。|The Boers defended their laager against the British. 布尔人保卫他们的营地抵御英国人。|Synonyms: encampment, camp, stockade, fort|Antonyms: dispersal, scattering|noun

laager:/ˈlɑːɡər/ (UK), /ˈlɑːɡər/ (US)| v.|扎营，组成防御阵地|To form or encamp in a laager.|The settlers laagered their wagons at night.  定居者们晚上把他们的马车围成营地。|The troops laagered up for the night. 部队扎营过夜。|Synonyms: encamp, camp, fortify|Antonyms: decamp, disperse|verb
