foreland:/ˈfɔːrlænd/| n.|前陆，岬角；沿海地区|A foreland is a point of land that extends into a body of water.|The ship rounded the rocky foreland and entered the bay.  这艘船绕过岩石岬角驶入海湾。|synonyms: headland, promontory, cape, peninsula|antonyms: hinterland|noun

forelands:/ˈfɔːrləndz/| n.|前陆，岬角；沿海地区|Forelands are points of land that extend into a body of water. |The ship navigated carefully through the treacherous forelands.  这艘船小心翼翼地航行过危险的岬角。|synonyms: headlands, promontories, capes, peninsulas|antonyms: hinterlands|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *forland*, from Old English *forland* (“foreland, promontory, headland”), from *fore* (“before, in front”) + *land* (“land”). Cognate with Dutch *voorland* (“foreland”), German *Vorland* (“foreland”), Swedish *förland* (“foreland”).
