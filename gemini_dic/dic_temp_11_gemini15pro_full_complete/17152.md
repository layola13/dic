battledoor:/ˈbætldɔːr/ (US) /ˈbætlˌdɔː(r)/ (UK)| n.|羽毛球拍；板羽球游戏|A light bat used in the game of battledore and shuttlecock, or the game itself.|Children played battledore and shuttlecock in the garden. 孩子们在花园里玩板羽球。|Synonyms: racket, bat|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for the object, perhaps "net" for the game as an opposing element)|noun

battledoor:/ˈbætldɔːr/ (US) /ˈbætlˌdɔː(r)/ (UK)| adj. (rare, archaic)|像球拍的；来回的|Resembling or characteristic of a battledore, typically in a back-and-forth motion.|The battledore debate continued for weeks. (Figurative use, referring to a back-and-forth argument)  来回的辩论持续了几个星期。|Synonyms: reciprocating, oscillating|Antonyms: static, stationary|adjective
