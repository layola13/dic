ishango:/ɪˈʃɑːŋɡoʊ/
n.|伊尚戈骨|A bone tool discovered in the Ishango region of the Democratic Republic of Congo, dating back to the Upper Paleolithic era. It is believed to be one of the earliest known examples of mathematical notation.|The Ishango bone is a fascinating artifact. 伊尚戈骨是一件引人入胜的文物。|近义词:artifact, relic|反义词: (None -  it's a unique object)|词性:noun
