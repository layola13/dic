enamel:/ɪˈnæml/ /ɛˈnæməl/| n. vt.|珐琅，搪瓷；指甲油|A glassy substance, usually opaque, applied by fusion to the surface of metal, glass, or pottery as a decoration or for protection.  The hard, glossy surface of a tooth.|The artist painted the vase with brightly colored enamels. 艺术家用色彩鲜艳的珐琅彩绘了花瓶。|She chipped the enamel on her tooth. 她磕掉了牙齿上的珐琅质。|近义词：glaze, lacquer, varnish, polish|反义词: - |n. vt.

enamel:/ɪˈnæml/ /ɛˈnæməl/| v.|涂珐琅，上釉|To coat or decorate with enamel.|She enameled the copper bowl with a delicate floral design.  她在铜碗上涂上了精致的花卉图案珐琅。|The factory enamels kitchen appliances. 这家工厂给厨房用具上釉。|近义词：glaze, lacquer, varnish|反义词: - |v.
