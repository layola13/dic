enravishingly:/ɪnˈrævɪʃɪŋli/| adv.|令人销魂地，迷人地|in a way that fills someone with delight.|She smiled enravishingly at him. 她对他露出了迷人的微笑。|近义词：charmingly, captivatingly, delightfully, alluringly|反义词：unattractively, repellently|词性: 副词

Etymology: From enravish + -ing + -ly. Enravish comes from the Old French enravir, meaning "to seize, to carry away," from en- "in" + ravir "to seize, to ravish."
