outrowed:/ˌaʊtˈroʊd/
verb
胜过划船
rowed faster than someone else.
例句：
The experienced team easily outrowed the novices in the competition.
经验丰富的队伍在比赛中轻松地胜过了新手。

He outrowed his opponent and won the race.
他划得比对手快，赢得了比赛。

近义词：outpaced, surpassed, outran
反义词：lost to, defeated by
词性：动词
