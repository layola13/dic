integrated:/ˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪd/ | adj. |综合的，完整的，集成的|Combined into a unified whole; incorporating all elements.|例句：The company offers an integrated software solution.  公司提供了一个集成的软件解决方案。|例句：The school has an integrated curriculum. 学校拥有一个综合的课程体系。|近义词：combined, unified, incorporated, consolidated|反义词：separate, segregated, disjointed|词性:形容词

Etymology: From the Latin word "integratus," past participle of "integrare" (to make whole), which is derived from "integer" (whole, complete).
