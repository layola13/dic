hubbies:/ˈhʌbiz/| n. |丈夫们（复数，非正式）|Informal plural of "hubby," which itself is an informal term for "husband."  It's a term of endearment or a casual way to refer to husbands.|例句：My hubbies are going golfing this weekend.  我的老公们这周末要去打高尔夫球。|近义词：husbands, spouses, partners|反义词:wives (as a group, not individually to each hubby)|noun
