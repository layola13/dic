chics: /ʃiːks/
n.|时髦女子；漂亮女孩|Stylish and fashionable young women; attractive girls.|The chics were dressed in the latest fashions. 这些时髦女子穿着最新的时装。|近义词:fashionistas, trendsetters, stylish women|反义词:frumps, dowdies|词性:名词

词源:
From French *chic*, meaning "skill, knack," from German *Geschick*, "fate, destiny, skill." The meaning shifted in French to "stylish, elegant" in the late 19th century.
