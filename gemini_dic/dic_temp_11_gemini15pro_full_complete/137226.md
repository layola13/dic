nonsurgically:/ˌnɑːnˈsɜːrdʒɪkli/ /ˌnɒnˈsɜːdʒɪkli/
adjective
非手术地；不用手术地
Without the use of surgery; using methods other than surgery.
The doctor treated the patient nonsurgically. 医生对病人进行了非手术治疗。
She was able to correct the problem nonsurgically. 她能够非手术地纠正这个问题。

近义词:medically, therapeutically
反义词:surgically
词性:adjective
