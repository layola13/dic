fitfulness:/ˈfɪtfʊlnəs/
noun
断续;  一阵一阵;  时断时续

His sleep was plagued by fitfulness and nightmares. 他的睡眠受到断续和噩梦的困扰。
intermittence, irregularity, spasmodicity
continuity, steadiness, regularity
