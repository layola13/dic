consultum:/kənˈsʌltəm/ | n. | 决议，决定，商议的结果 |  A decision, decree, or result of deliberation.  It's the neuter form of the perfect passive participle of the Latin verb "consulere," meaning "to consult." | The consultum of the senate was to declare war. 元老院的决议是宣战。| Near synonyms: decision, decree, resolution, judgment | Antonyms: indecision, disagreement | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *consultum*, the neuter form of the perfect passive participle of *consulere* "to consult," from *con-* "together" (see com-) + *-sulere*, related to Old English *solor* "house floor," literally "bottom, foundation."
