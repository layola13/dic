attiring:/əˈtaɪərɪŋ/
verb
穿着，打扮
The act of dressing or clothing someone.
She was attiring herself in a beautiful gown for the ball. 她正为自己穿上美丽的礼服准备参加舞会。
The king was attiring himself for the coronation. 国王正在为加冕礼穿戴盛装。
近义词:dressing, clothing, arraying, adorning
反义词:undressing, disrobing
词性:v.
