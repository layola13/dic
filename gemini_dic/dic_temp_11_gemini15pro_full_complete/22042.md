bolder:/ˈboʊldər/ (US) /ˈbəʊldə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更大胆的；更明显的；更粗体的|Having more courage and daring; more noticeable or prominent; printed in a heavier typeface.|The new design is bolder and more modern.  新设计更加大胆和现代。|The headline was printed in a bolder font.  标题是用更粗的字体印刷的。|近义词：braver, more daring, more prominent, brighter, stronger|反义词：timider, more hesitant, fainter, lighter, weaker|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *boldere, comparative of bold, from Old English beald (“bold, brave, confident, strong”).  The -er suffix is a comparative ending.
