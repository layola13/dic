blockaded:/blɒˈkeɪdɪd/ | adj. |被封锁的|Sealed off by a blockade, preventing entry or exit.|The blockaded city was running out of supplies. 被封锁的城市物资即将耗尽。|近义词：besieged, surrounded, closed off|反义词:open, accessible|形容词

blockade: /blɒˈkeɪd/ | n. vt. |封锁，封锁线；阻塞；封锁(港口等)|The act or means of sealing off a place to prevent goods or people from entering or leaving; an obstruction.|The navy enforced a blockade of the enemy port. 海军封锁了敌人的港口. |近义词：siege, obstruction, barrier|反义词:opening, access|名词，动词
