inspirations:/ˌɪnspəˈreɪʃənz/| n.|灵感，鼓舞人心的人或事|Something that makes someone want to do something or that gives someone an idea about what to do or create.  It can also refer to the people or things that provide this stimulus.|Her biggest inspirations were her parents and her teachers. 她最大的灵感来源是她的父母和老师。|Synonyms: influences, motivations, stimuli, muses|Antonyms: deterrents, discouragements|noun

inspiration:/ˌɪnspəˈreɪʃən/| n.|灵感，鼓舞人心的人或事|The process of being mentally stimulated to do or feel something, especially in a creative way.  It can also refer to the people or things that provide this stimulus.|He needed some inspiration for his next novel. 他需要一些灵感来创作他的下一部小说。|Synonyms: influence, motivation, stimulus, muse|Antonyms: deterrent, discouragement|noun
