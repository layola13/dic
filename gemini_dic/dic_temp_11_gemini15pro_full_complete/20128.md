bipartisanship: /ˌbaɪˈpɑːrtɪzənʃɪp/
n.
两党合作；跨党派合作
agreement or cooperation between two political parties that usually oppose each other's policies.
Example:  Bipartisanship is essential for effective governance. 两党合作对于有效治理至关重要。

Example:  The bill passed with strong bipartisan support.  该法案在两党的强烈支持下获得通过。

近义词: cooperation, collaboration, unity, agreement
反义词: partisanship, division, discord, antagonism
词性: noun

词源:  bi- (two) + partisan + -ship (state of being)  来源于拉丁语词根bi-（两个）和partisan（党派支持者），加上后缀-ship（状态），表示两个党派合作的状态。
