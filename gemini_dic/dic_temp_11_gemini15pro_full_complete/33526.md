ciclatoun:/ˈsɪkləˌtuːn/| n. |骑兵披风; 一种昂贵的丝绸或锦缎; |A rich silk or satin fabric, often used for costly garments, especially in the Middle Ages.  A kind of over-garment worn by knights.|The knight wore a ciclatoun over his armor. 骑士在他的盔甲外面穿着一件骑兵披风。|近义词：surcoat, mantle, cloak |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "undergarment")|词性:noun
