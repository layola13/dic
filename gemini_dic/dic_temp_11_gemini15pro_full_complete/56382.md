dolcemente:/doltʃeˈmɛnte/ | adv. | 甜蜜地；温柔地；轻柔地 | Sweetly, softly, gently. It describes a manner of doing something in a tender, delicate, or pleasant way. | She sang the lullaby dolcemente, soothing the baby to sleep. 她温柔地唱着摇篮曲，哄宝宝入睡。|  The pianist played the piece dolcemente, creating a calming atmosphere. 钢琴家轻柔地演奏着这首曲子，营造出一种宁静的氛围。 | 近义词: softly, gently, tenderly, sweetly | 反义词: harshly, roughly, loudly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Italian dolcemente, derived from dolce (sweet) + -mente (adverbial suffix). Ultimately from Latin dulcis (sweet).
