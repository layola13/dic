misentreat:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtriːt/ | vt. | 虐待；苛待 | To treat badly or cruelly. |  He misentreats his animals, leaving them without food or water. 他虐待他的动物，不给它们食物和水。

| The prisoners were misentreated by their captors. 囚犯们受到了俘虏者的虐待。 | 近义词: abuse, maltreat, mistreat | 反义词: treat well, cherish, nurture | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (badly) + entreat (to treat).  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English, meaning "wrongly" or "badly." "Entreat" comes from Old French "entraitier," meaning "to treat."
