antirecession:/ˌænti rɪˈsɛʃən/ | adj. | 抗衰退的，反衰退的 | Designed to prevent or counteract a recession.  | The government introduced an antirecession budget. 政府推出了一个抗衰退的预算。 | The antirecession measures seem to be working.  这些抗衰退措施似乎正在奏效。 | 近义词: countercyclical,  contractionary (in some contexts) | 反义词: procyclical | 词性: adjective
