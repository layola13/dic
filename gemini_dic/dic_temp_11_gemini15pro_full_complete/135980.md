nonradiations:/ˌnɒnˌreɪdiˈeɪʃənz/
|n.|非辐射|The process of energy transfer that does not involve the emission of electromagnetic radiation.  It includes conduction, convection, and other forms of heat transfer.| The building uses nonradiations methods for heating, like underfloor heating. 这栋建筑使用非辐射方式供暖，例如地板采暖。|Near synonyms: non-radiative processes, non-emissive transfers|Antonyms: radiations, emissions|noun
