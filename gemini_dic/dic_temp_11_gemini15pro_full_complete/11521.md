arillated:/ˈærɪleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 假种皮的，有假种皮的 | Having an aril (an outer covering of a seed). | The yew tree has bright red arillated seeds. 紫杉树有鲜红色的假种皮种子。 | Near synonyms: arillode, carunculate | Antonyms: exarillate | adjective
