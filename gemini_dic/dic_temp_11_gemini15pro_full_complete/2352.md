adhortatory:/ədˈhɔːrtətɔːri/ | adj. | 劝告的；激励的 | Serving to exhort; urging to some course of conduct or action. | The coach delivered an adhortatory speech to his team before the final game. 教练在决赛前对他的团队发表了激励人心的讲话。 | The general issued an adhortatory message to the troops, urging them to fight bravely for their country. 将军向部队发出了劝告性的信息，敦促他们为国家勇敢作战。|  Synonyms: hortatory, encouraging, inspiring, urging, exhortative | Antonyms: discouraging, dissuading, deterring | adjective
