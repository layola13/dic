confusing:/kənˈfjuːzɪŋ/| adj.|令人困惑的，难以理解的|Causing confusion; bewildering.|The instructions were so confusing that I couldn't assemble the furniture. 说明书太令人困惑了，我无法组装家具。|近义词：bewildering, perplexing, puzzling, unclear, ambiguous|反义词：clear, obvious, straightforward, simple, understandable|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *confusen*, from Anglo-Norman *confuser*, from Latin *confundere* ("to pour together, mix up, confuse, confound"), from *con-* ("together") + *fundere* ("to pour, melt").
