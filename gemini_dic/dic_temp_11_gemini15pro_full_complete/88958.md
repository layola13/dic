词源: From *holh, from Proto-Germanic *hulną (“hole”). Cognate with Old Saxon hol, Old Frisian hol, Dutch hol, Old High German hol, German hohl, Old Norse holr. Related to hollow.


注意事项: holey有时可以用来比喻充满缺陷或漏洞的东西，例如 "a holey argument" (一个漏洞百出的论点)。
