laevulose:/ˈliːvjuːloʊs/ /ˈlɛvjuːloʊs/
noun
果糖，左旋糖
A simple sugar found in honey and many fruits.  It is an isomer of glucose.
Laevulose is sweeter than glucose. 果糖比葡萄糖甜。
synonyms: fructose, fruit sugar
antonyms: None (Laevulose is a specific type of sugar; it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
