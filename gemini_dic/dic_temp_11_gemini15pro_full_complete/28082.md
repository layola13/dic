capillaries:/ˈkæpɪˌlɛriz/ | n. | 毛细血管 | Very small blood vessels that connect arteries and veins.  They are the site of exchange of oxygen, nutrients, and waste products between blood and tissues.| Oxygen and nutrients pass from the blood in the capillaries to the body tissues. 氧气和营养物质从毛细血管中的血液输送到身体组织。| synonyms: blood capillaries, capillary vessels | antonyms: arteries, veins | noun


capillary:/ˈkæpɪˌlɛri/ | adj. | 毛细管的; 毛细作用的 | Relating to or resembling a capillary or capillaries. | Capillary action causes the water to rise in the thin tube. 毛细作用使水在细管中上升。| synonyms: fine, hairlike | antonyms: large, wide | adjective
