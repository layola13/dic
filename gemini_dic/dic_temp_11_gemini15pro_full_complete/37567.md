commotive:/kəˈmoʊtɪv/
adjective
煽动的，激动的
Relating to, or tending to excite motion or action; having power to excite.
The commotive speech roused the crowd to action.  这番煽动性的演讲激起了群众的行动。
近义词:  agitating, exciting, stimulating, provocative
反义词:  soothing, calming, pacifying
词性: adjective

commotive:/kəˈmoʊtɪv/
noun
骚动，暴动
A disturbance, commotion. (This usage is less common.)
The commotive in the streets led to several arrests. 街上的骚动导致数人被捕。
近义词:  turmoil, unrest, upheaval, disturbance
反义词:  peace, tranquility, calm
词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *commotīvus, from Latin commovēre (“to move violently, disturb, excite”), from com- (“together, with”) + movēre (“to move”).
