exclaiming:/ɪkˈskleɪmɪŋ/
| adj. | 惊叹的；呼喊的；感叹的 | Expressing strong emotion, especially surprise, anger, or pain. | She gave an exclaiming cry when she saw the spider.  她看到蜘蛛时发出一声惊叫。| synonyms: crying out, shouting, yelling, shrieking,  | antonyms: whispering, murmuring, muttering | adjective

exclaim:/ɪkˈskleɪm/
| v. | 呼喊；惊叫；大声说出 | Cry out or speak suddenly and vehemently. | "You did what?" he exclaimed in surprise. “你做了什么？”他惊讶地喊道。| synonyms: cry out, shout, yell, declare, pronounce | antonyms: whisper, murmur, mutter | verb

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French exclamer, from Latin exclamare ‘cry out’, from ex- ‘out’ + clamare ‘to shout’.
