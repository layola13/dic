cookmaid: /ˈkʊkmeɪd/ | n. | 女佣；厨娘 | A female servant employed to do both cooking and general housework. | The cookmaid prepared a delicious roast for dinner.  女佣准备了一顿美味的烤肉晚餐。 | 近义词: maid, servant, housekeeper | 反义词: employer, master | 词性: noun

Etymology:  A compound of "cook" and "maid". "Cook" comes from the Old English "coc" meaning "one who cooks". "Maid" comes from Old English "mægden" meaning "virgin, girl, female servant".
