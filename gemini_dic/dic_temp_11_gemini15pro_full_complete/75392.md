frozenly:/ˈfroʊzənli/| adv.|冰冷地，冷淡地|In a way that suggests being emotionally or physically frozen.  The king looked frozenly at his advisor. 国王冰冷地看着他的顾问。|近义词：icily, coldly, frigidly|反义词：warmly, kindly, affectionately|词性:副词

词源: From "frozen" (past participle of "freeze") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Freeze" comes from Old English "freosan."
