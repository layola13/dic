dints:/dɪnts/ | n. | 凹痕；凹陷；（数量的）减少 | small marks or depressions in the surface of something; a reduction in the amount of something | The car was covered in dints after the hailstorm.  这辆车在冰雹过后布满了凹痕。 | synonyms: dents, hollows, depressions, reductions | antonyms: bulges, protrusions, increases | noun

Etymology:  From the verb *dint*,  which comes from Old English *dynt* "blow, stroke," related to Old Norse *dyntr* and Dutch *deunt*.  Originally referred to the mark left by a blow.
