inexpensivenesses:/ˌɪnɪkˈspɛnsɪvnɪsɪz/ | n. | 廉价；低廉；物美价廉 | The state of being inexpensive; affordability.  This plural form is rare and often considered non-standard.  It's more common to use "instances of inexpensiveness" or similar phrasing. | Due to the inexpensivenesses of materials in that region, manufacturing costs are low. (This sentence is grammatically correct but sounds unnatural.  It would be better to say: "Due to the low cost of materials in that region, manufacturing costs are low.") |  近义词: affordability, cheapness, economy | 反义词: expensiveness, costliness | noun
