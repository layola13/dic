light:/laɪt/ /laɪt/| adj. n. v.|轻的，光，点燃|Something that makes things visible or affords illumination.|The sun provides light for the earth. 太阳为地球提供光。|近义词：bright, luminous, illuminate, kindle, ignite|反义词:dark, heavy, extinguish|词性:adjective, noun, verb

light:/laɪt/ /laɪt/| adj. n. v.|少量的，轻松的，点燃|Small in quantity, amount, or degree; not serious or important; to start burning.|He packed a light suitcase for the weekend trip. 他为了周末旅行打包了一个轻便的行李箱。|近义词：slight, easy, gentle, ignite, kindle|反义词:heavy, serious, extinguish|词性:adjective, noun, verb

Etymology: From Old English *lēoht (adjective and noun), from Proto-Germanic *leuhtą (“light, brightness”), from Proto-Indo-European *leuk- (“light, brightness”).
