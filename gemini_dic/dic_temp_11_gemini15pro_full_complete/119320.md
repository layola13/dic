microbic:/maɪˈkroʊbɪk/| adj.|微生物的，由微生物引起的|Relating to or caused by microbes; microscopic. |The microbic contamination caused the food to spoil. 微生物污染导致食物变质。|近义词：microbial, bacterial, germinal|反义词:sterile, abiotic|词性:形容词
词源: From microbe + -ic. "Microbe" comes from French, coined by Charles-Emmanuel Sédillot in 1878, from Greek mīkros "small" + bios "life."
