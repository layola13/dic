forkbeard:/ˈfɔːrkˌbɪərd/| n. |叉须鱼，一种海鱼|A marine fish with a pair of barbels on its chin resembling a fork. |例句：The forkbeard is a popular fish for anglers. 叉须鱼是钓鱼爱好者喜爱的鱼。

例句：Forkbeards are bottom-dwelling fish found in the Atlantic Ocean. 叉须鱼是大西洋底栖鱼类。|近义词：fish, gurnard (some species) |反义词: None (specific)|词性: noun

Etymology:
From 'fork' + 'beard', referring to the forked barbels on the chin of the fish.
