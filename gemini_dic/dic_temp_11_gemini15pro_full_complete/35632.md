coating:/ˈkəʊtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkoʊtɪŋ/ (US)| n.|涂层，覆盖层|A layer of a substance spread over a surface for protection or decoration.|The car has a new coating of paint. 汽车有一层新的油漆涂层。|layer, film, covering|exposure, bareness|noun

coating:/ˈkəʊtɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkoʊtɪŋ/ (US)| n.|（食物的）裹料|A layer of breadcrumbs, batter, etc., around food.|The chicken has a crispy coating. 鸡肉有一层酥脆的裹料。|crust, breading, batter| - |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *coting, from Old French *cotier, from Vulgar Latin *cottāre, frequentative of Latin *cottus “a tunic, felt coat,” of uncertain origin.
