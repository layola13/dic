keelraking:/ˈkiːlˌreɪkɪŋ/
n.
严厉斥责；苛刻批评；翻旧账
A severe reprimand or criticism; the act of bringing up old grievances.
The boss gave him a real keelraking for missing the deadline. 老板因为他错过了截止日期而狠狠地训斥了他。
He subjected his employees to a painful keelraking. 他对员工进行了痛苦的批评。
reprimand, castigation, censure, upbraiding
praise, commendation
noun
