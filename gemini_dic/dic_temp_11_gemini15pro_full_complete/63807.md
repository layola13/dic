epidermal:/ˌɛpɪˈdɜːrməl/ | adj. | 表皮的；外皮的 | Relating to the epidermis; of or forming the outer layer of skin. |  The epidermal cells protect the underlying tissues. 表皮细胞保护下面的组织。| Example: Sunburn is damage to the epidermal layer of the skin. 晒伤是对皮肤表皮层的损伤。 | Synonyms: cutaneous, dermal, outer | Antonyms: internal, inner | adjective
