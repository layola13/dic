nonincrimination:/ˌnɒnɪnˌkrɪmɪˈneɪʃən/
|n.| 免罪，不控告 |The Fifth Amendment guarantees the right against self-incrimination.  第五修正案保障了免于自证其罪的权利。|
|英文解析: The act of not incriminating or the state of not being incriminated; exoneration.  The Fifth Amendment of the U.S. Constitution guarantees that no person "shall be compelled in any criminal case to be a witness against himself." This prevents authorities from compelling a person to provide information that might lead to their criminal prosecution.|
|例句：He received a grant of nonincrimination from the government. 他获得了政府的免罪保证。|
|例句：She invoked her right of nonincrimination when questioned by the police.  她在接受警方讯问时援引了她的不自证其罪的权利。|
|近义词：exoneration, acquittal, immunity|
|反义词：incrimination, accusation|
|词性：名词|
