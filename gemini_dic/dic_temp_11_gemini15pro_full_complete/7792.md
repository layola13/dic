answerers:/ˈænsərərz/ | n. | 回答者；答复者 | People who provide answers or respond to questions. | The answerers on the online forum were very helpful. 在线论坛上的回答者非常 helpful. | synonyms: respondents, repliers | antonyms: questioners, inquirers | noun

Etymology:  From the verb "answer" + "-er" (suffix forming agent nouns). "Answer" comes from Old English *andswarian "to swear in opposition to, reply," from and- "against" + swerian "to swear."
