caulicle: /ˈkɔːlɪkəl/
n.
幼茎；胚轴；上胚轴
The part of a plant embryo or seedling below the cotyledons that develops into the stem.
The caulicle emerged from the seed first. 幼茎最先从种子中长出来。
hypocotyl, plumule
radicle
