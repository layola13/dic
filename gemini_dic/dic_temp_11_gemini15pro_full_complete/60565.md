elastically:/ɪˈlæstɪkli/
adverb|弹性地，有弹性地|in a way that can stretch and return to its original size and shape; flexibly.|The rubber band stretched elastically.  橡皮筋有弹性地伸展。|flexibly, resiliently, springily|rigidly, inflexibly

elastically:/ɪˈlæstɪkli/
adverb|经济学术语：富有弹性地|In economics, referring to a situation where a small change in price causes a large change in quantity demanded or supplied.|Demand for luxury goods is often elastically responsive to price changes. 奢侈品的需求通常对价格变化富有弹性。|responsively, sensitively|inelastically, unresponsively
