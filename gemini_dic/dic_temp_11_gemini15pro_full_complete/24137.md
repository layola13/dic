ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English brere, from Old English brǣr (“thorn bush”), from Proto-Germanic *brēraz, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰrēHg- (“to prick, sting”). Cognate with Dutch braam (“bramble”), German Brombeere (“blackberry”), Icelandic brár (“eyelash”).
