cave-like:/ˈkeɪvˌlaɪk/
adjective
像洞穴的；洞穴似的
Resembling a cave; dark, hollow, or suggestive of a cave.
The room was dark and cave-like.  这个房间阴暗得像洞穴一样。
The narrow passage had a cave-like atmosphere.  这条狭窄的通道有一种像洞穴般的气氛。
cavernous, hollow
open, airy
adjective
