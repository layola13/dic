idolises:/ˈaɪdəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
崇拜；把…当作偶像
admires, worships, or loves someone or something very much
She absolutely idolises her older brother. 她非常崇拜她的哥哥。
worship, revere, adore
criticize, condemn
词性: 动词
