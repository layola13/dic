calcification:/ˌkæl.sə.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən/| n.|钙化|The process of hardening by deposition of calcium salts in the tissues of a body.|Arterial calcification can increase the risk of heart attack. 动脉钙化会增加心脏病发作的风险。|近义词:hardening, ossification|反义词:decalcification|词性:名词

calcification:/ˌkæl.sə.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən/| n.|钙化作用|The process in which calcium builds up in body tissue, causing it to harden.|Calcification of the heart valves can lead to serious health problems. 心脏瓣膜钙化会导致严重的健康问题。|近义词:mineralization, petrification|反义词:demineralization|词性:名词
