cotyledonary:/ˌkɒtɪˈliːdənəri/ | adj. | 子叶的，胚胎的 | Relating to or resembling a cotyledon; of the nature of a cotyledon.  | The cotyledonary leaves of the bean plant are thick and fleshy.  蚕豆植物的子叶又厚又多肉。 |  近义词: embryonic, seminal | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

cotyledon:/ˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/ | n. | 子叶 |  The first leaf or one of the first pair or whorl of leaves developed by the embryo of a seed plant. |  The bean seed has two cotyledons.  豆子有两个子叶。 | 近义词: seed leaf | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
