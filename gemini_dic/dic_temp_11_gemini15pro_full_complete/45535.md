curiet:/ˈkjʊəriət/ | n. |  好奇心; 好奇的人或物 |  A strong desire to know or learn something.  A person or thing that excites curiosity. |  Her curiet about the world led her to travel extensively. 她对世界的好奇心使她广泛旅行。 The antique shop was full of curiets. 这家古董店里摆满了稀奇古怪的东西。| 近义词：curiosity, inquisitiveness, inquirer | 反义词：apathy, indifference | 词性: 名词

词源:  From earlier *curiosite, from French curiosité, from Latin cūriōsitās (“inquisitiveness, curiosity”), from cūriōsus (“careful, inquisitive”).  While the standard spelling is "curiosity," "curiet" appears to be a misspelling or archaic variant.  It's not commonly used in modern English.
