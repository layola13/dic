lickety-split:/ˈlɪkɪti splɪt/
adv.
快速地，飞快地
very quickly; at great speed.
例句：She finished her homework lickety-split and went out to play. 她飞快地完成了作业，然后出去玩了。

例句：The repairman fixed the problem lickety-split. 修理工快速解决了问题。

近义词：quickly, rapidly, swiftly, speedily
反义词：slowly, gradually
