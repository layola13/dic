half-reasoning:/ˌhɑːfˈriːzənɪŋ/ | adj. | 半推理的；缺乏逻辑的 | Characterized by faulty or incomplete reasoning; based on partial or flawed logic.  | His half-reasoning argument didn't convince anyone. 他的缺乏逻辑的论点没能说服任何人。|  Her half-reasoning explanation left more questions than answers. 她半推理的解释留下了更多的问题而不是答案。 | Near synonyms: illogical, flawed, unsound, specious, fallacious | Antonyms: logical, sound, rational, well-reasoned | adjective
