go-back-on:/ɡəʊ bæk ɒn/| phrasal verb |违背（诺言、协议等）；食言；背弃|to fail to keep a promise, agreement, etc.|例句：He went back on his word and refused to help. 他食言了，拒绝帮忙。

例句：She went back on her promise to visit her grandmother. 她违背了去看望她祖母的诺言。


近义词：renege on, break, default on|反义词：keep, honor, fulfill|词性:动词短语
