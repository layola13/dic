idlest:/ˈaɪdlɪst/| adj.|最闲的；最懒惰的|Most idle; most lazy.  Characterized by the greatest degree of inactivity or laziness.|He was the idlest boy in the class, always daydreaming. 他是班里最懒的孩子，总是做白日梦。|近义词：laziest, most inactive|反义词：busiest, most industrious|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *idelest, superlative of *idel, from Old English īdel (“empty, vain, unemployed, useless”), from Proto-Germanic *īdilaz (“empty”), from Proto-Indo-European *oydʰ- (“empty”).
