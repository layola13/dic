intertangle:/ˌɪntərˈtæŋɡl/ | v. | 缠结，纠结，使…交织 | To twist or twine together; interweave. | The branches of the trees were intertangled, creating a dense canopy. 树枝交错缠绕，形成茂密的树冠。 | The climbers' ropes became intertangled during the ascent. 登山者的绳索在攀登过程中缠结在一起了。 | 近义词: intertwine, interweave, entangle | 反义词: disentangle, separate, unravel | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
From inter- + tangle.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "tangle" comes from the Middle English "tanglen," likely of Scandinavian origin.
