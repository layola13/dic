bergschrund:/ˈbɛərɡʃrʊnd/ | n. | 冰斗裂缝，冰川裂隙 | A large crevasse that separates flowing glacial ice from stagnant ice or firn above.  It forms at the head of a glacier where the moving ice pulls away from the back wall of the cirque. |  The mountaineers had to carefully navigate the bergschrund to reach the summit.  登山者必须小心地绕过冰斗裂缝才能到达顶峰。| Synonym: crevasse, rimaye | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "solid ice" or "glacier face") | noun
