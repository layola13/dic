myspace: /ˈmaɪspeɪs/ | n. | 我的空间; 个人主页 | A social networking website popular in the early 2000s where users could create profiles, connect with friends, share photos and videos, and blog. |  I used to spend hours customizing my MySpace profile. 我过去常常花好几个小时定制我的MySpace主页。| synonyms: social media platform, online community, profile page | antonyms: offline, physical space | noun

etymology: "my" + "space" -  A combination of the possessive pronoun "my" indicating personal ownership and "space" referring to a designated area, in this context, a virtual area on the internet.
