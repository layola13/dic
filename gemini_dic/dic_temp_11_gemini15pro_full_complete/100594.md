inthronization:/ɪnˌθroʊnɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 就任；登基； enthronement|The inthronization of the new king was a grand ceremony. 新国王的登基仪式非常隆重。| Synonyms: enthronement, inauguration, investiture | Antonyms: abdication, deposition | noun

inthronize:/ɪnˈθroʊnaɪz/ |vt.| 使登基；使就任|The archbishop inthronized the new bishop. 大主教主持了新主教的就任仪式。|Synonyms: install, inaugurate, invest | Antonyms: depose, dethrone | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *inthronisacioun*, from Old French enthronization, from enthroniser  ("to enthrone"), from en- + throne.
