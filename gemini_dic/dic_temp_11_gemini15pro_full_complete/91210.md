hydriot:/ˈhaɪdriˌɒt/ | n. |氢离子|A positively charged hydrogen atom (H⁺), essentially a proton. |The concentration of hydriots determines the pH of a solution. 氢离子浓度决定溶液的pH值。| The hydriot is crucial in acid-base reactions. 氢离子在酸碱反应中至关重要。| synonyms: proton, hydrogen ion | antonyms: hydroxide ion | noun
