nonborrowed:/ˌnɒnˈbɒrəʊd/ (British) /ˌnɑːnˈbɑːroʊd/ (American)| adj.|非借用的，本土的|Not borrowed from another language; native or indigenous.|例句：The word "house" is a nonborrowed word in English.  英语单词“house”是一个非借用词。

例句：Many nonborrowed English words have Germanic roots. 许多非借用的英语单词有日耳曼语词根。|近义词：indigenous, native|反义词：borrowed, loanword|词性:形容词
