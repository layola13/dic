beer:/bɪr/ | n. |啤酒|An alcoholic drink made from yeast-fermented malt flavored with hops.|I like to drink beer on a hot day. 我喜欢在大热天喝啤酒。|Near synonyms: ale, brew, lager|Antonym:  abstinence (referring to the act of not drinking alcohol)|noun

Etymology: From Old English *bēor, from Proto-Germanic *beuwoz, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰew- (“to drink, imbibe”). Cognate with Dutch bier, German Bier.
