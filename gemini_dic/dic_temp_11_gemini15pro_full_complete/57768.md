dreamtimes:/ˈdriːmtaɪmz/| n.| 梦幻时期，梦想时代|A term used to refer to the ancient time of creation in Aboriginal Australian mythology, when ancestral spirits roamed the earth and shaped the landscape and its inhabitants.|The stories of the Dreamtime explain the origins of the world. 梦幻时期的故事解释了世界的起源。|近义词:mythological period, creation period|反义词:present, reality|词性:noun
