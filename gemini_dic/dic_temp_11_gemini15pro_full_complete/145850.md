overdependent:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈpɛndənt/ | adj. | 过度依赖的，过分依赖的 | Excessively reliant on someone or something.  Characterized by excessive dependence. | He became overdependent on his parents after the accident. 事故发生后，他变得过度依赖父母。 | She is overdependent on her phone for everything. 她做什么事都过度依赖手机。 | 近义词: dependent, reliant, clingy | 反义词: independent, self-reliant, autonomous | 词性: adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "dependent" (adjective meaning "relying on someone or something else for support").  The word "dependent" ultimately comes from the Latin "dependere" meaning "to hang down from."
