kosho:/ˈkoʊʃoʊ/ | n. | 交涉，交涉权；香料 |  Negotiation, the right to negotiate; spice (archaic/rare).  From Japanese 交渉 (kōshō). | The kosho between the two companies lasted for months.  两家公司之间的交涉持续了数月。 |  The union has the kosho for its members. 工会拥有其成员的交涉权。 | synonyms: negotiation, bargaining, parley | antonyms:  impasse, deadlock | noun
