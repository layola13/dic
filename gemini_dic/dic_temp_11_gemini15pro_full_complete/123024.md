monaxial:/ˌmɒnˈæksiəl/ /ˌmɑːnˈæksiəl/ | adj. | 单轴的 | Having only one axis; rotating around a single axis. |  A monaxial joint, like the elbow, allows movement in only one plane.  像肘部这样的单轴关节只允许在一个平面上运动。 | synonym: uniaxial | antonym: biaxial, triaxial | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From *mono- + axial. Mono- comes from Greek monos "single, alone."  Axial comes from Latin axis "axle, pivot."
