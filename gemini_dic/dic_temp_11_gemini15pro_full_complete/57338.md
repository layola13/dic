downway:/ˈdaʊnweɪ/| n. |下坡路，下降趋势|A downward slope or trend.  This word is not commonly used and might be considered archaic or dialectal.  It's more common to use phrases like "downward slope" or "downward trend."|The path took a sharp downway towards the valley. 这条小路陡峭地向下通往山谷。|近义词：decline, descent, downslope|反义词:upward trend, ascent, upslope|词性:noun
