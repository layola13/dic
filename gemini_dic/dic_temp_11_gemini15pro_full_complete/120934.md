miscellany: /ˌmɪsəˈleɪni/ | n. | 杂项；混合物；杂集 | A collection of different items or a mixture of various things. |  Example: The museum housed a miscellany of artifacts from different eras.  博物馆收藏了来自不同时代的各种文物。

Example:  She keeps a miscellany of small trinkets on her desk. 她在桌子上摆放着各种各样的小饰品。 | Synonym: assortment, mixture, collection, medley, variety, jumble | Antonym: uniformity, homogeneity | noun



miscellaneous: /ˌmɪsəˈleɪniəs/ | adj. | 杂项的；各种各样的 | Consisting of diverse things or members; having various qualities or aspects. | Example: The attic contained a miscellaneous collection of old furniture and boxes.  阁楼里堆放着各种各样的旧家具和箱子。

Example:  He worked at various miscellaneous jobs before finding his career path. 在找到自己的职业道路之前，他做过各种各样的杂活。 | Synonym: diverse, varied, assorted, sundry, mixed | Antonym: uniform, homogeneous, specific | adjective
