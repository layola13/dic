delecto:/dɪˈlɛktoʊ/ /dəˈlɛktoʊ/
verb
使高兴，使愉快；取悦
To delight or please someone greatly.
She was delected by the unexpected gift. 她对这意外的礼物感到非常高兴。
The performance delected the audience. 表演让观众很开心。
近义词: delight, please, charm, enchant, gratify
反义词: displease, annoy, irritate, bore
词性: verb
