bonneting:/ˈbɒnɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 装引擎罩; (旧)戴帽子的行为; (鸟类)张开翅膀像引擎罩 | The act of putting on a bonnet (a type of hat), or covering something with a bonnet-like structure. In ornithology, it refers to a bird spreading its wings and holding them out to the sides like a bonnet. |  The female bird was observed bonneting while protecting her chicks. 这只雌鸟在保护小鸡时被观察到张开翅膀像引擎罩一样。 | Synonyms: hooding, covering, shielding | Antonyms: uncovering, exposing | noun
