amortizing:/ˈæmərˌtaɪzɪŋ/
|v.|分期偿还，摊销|Gradually reducing or writing off the cost or value of an asset over a period of time.|The company is amortizing the cost of the new equipment over five years.  该公司正在五年内摊销新设备的成本。|Synonyms: depreciating, expensing, writing off|Antonyms: appreciating, increasing|Verb

amortize:/ˈæmərˌtaɪz/
|v.|分期偿还，摊销|Gradually reducing or writing off the cost or value of an asset over a period of time.|The loan will be amortized over 15 years. 这笔贷款将在15年内分期偿还。|Synonyms: depreciate, expense, write off|Antonyms: appreciate, increase|Verb

amortization:/ˌæmərtaɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|摊销，分期偿还|The action or process of gradually writing off the initial cost of an asset.|The amortization of the loan will take 20 years.  这笔贷款的摊销将需要20年。|Synonyms: depreciation, expensing, write-off|Antonyms: appreciation, increase|Noun
