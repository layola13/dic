onomatology:/ˌɒnəməˈtɒlədʒi/ /ˌɑːnəməˈtɑːlədʒi/| n.|命名学；专有名词学|The study of the origins and forms of proper names.  It encompasses personal names, place names, and other kinds of names.|Onomatology is a fascinating field of study, revealing much about history and culture. 命名学是一门引人入胜的研究领域，揭示了许多关于历史和文化的信息。|synonyms: name study, nomenclature|antonyms: N/A|noun

onomatology:/ˌɒnəməˈtɒlədʒi/ /ˌɑːnəməˈtɑːlədʒi/| n.|专有名词|A proper name or term.|While "dog" is a common noun, "Fido" is an onomatology. “dog”是普通名词，而“Fido”是专有名词。|synonyms: proper noun, name|antonyms: common noun|noun
