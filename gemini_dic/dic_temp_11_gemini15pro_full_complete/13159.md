asystolism: /eɪˈsɪstəlɪzəm/ /əˈsɪstəlɪzəm/
|n.| 心搏停止；心脏停搏|Cardiac standstill; cessation of heartbeats.|The patient experienced asystolism during surgery, requiring immediate resuscitation. 病人在手术过程中出现心脏停搏，需要立即进行复苏。|Asystole can be caused by various factors, such as heart attack or drug overdose. 心脏停搏可能是由多种因素引起的，例如心脏病发作或药物过量。|近义词：cardiac arrest, heart stoppage|反义词：normal sinus rhythm, heartbeat|词性：名词

Etymology:  From a- (without) + systole (contraction of the heart) + -ism (condition). Ultimately from Greek a- (without) + systolē (contraction).
