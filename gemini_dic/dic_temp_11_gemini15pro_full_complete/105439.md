kieselguhr:/ˌkiːzəlˈɡʊər/ /ˌkaɪzəlˈɡʊr/ | n. |硅藻土|A soft, white, porous sedimentary rock formed from the fossilized remains of diatoms. It is used as a filter aid, abrasive, absorbent, and in other industrial applications.|Diatomaceous earth, sometimes referred to as kieselguhr, is commonly used in swimming pool filters.  硅藻土，有时被称为kieselguhr，通常用于游泳池过滤器。| The gardener used kieselguhr to improve the drainage of the soil. 园丁用硅藻土来改善土壤的排水。|Synonyms: diatomaceous earth, diatomite, infusorial earth|Antonyms: None|Noun
