monetarism: /ˈmʌnɪtərɪzəm/ (US) /ˈmʌnɪtərɪzəm/ (UK)| n.|货币主义|A school of economic thought that emphasizes the role of the money supply in controlling inflation and promoting economic growth. It advocates controlling the money supply as the primary means of stabilizing the economy.|例句：Monetarism was popular in the 1980s. 货币主义在20世纪80年代很流行。

例句：The government's economic policy is based on the principles of monetarism. 政府的经济政策是基于货币主义的原则。|近义词：monetary policy,  quantitative easing (in some contexts)|反义词: Keynesianism, fiscal policy|词性:noun
