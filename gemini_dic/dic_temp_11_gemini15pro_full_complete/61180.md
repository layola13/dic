eluded:/ɪˈluːdɪd/| v. (past tense)|逃避，躲避|To evade or escape from, especially by cunning, skill, or speed.  It refers to something intangible, like grasp, understanding, or memory.|The answer eluded him for hours.  这个答案让他苦思冥想几个小时。|近义词：evade, avoid, escape, dodge|反义词:face, confront, meet|verb

eluded:/ɪˈluːdɪŋ/| adj. |难以捉摸的，难以理解的|Difficult to find, catch, or achieve.|The cause of the disease has remained elusive despite years of research. 这种疾病的病因尽管经过多年的研究仍然难以捉摸。|近义词：evasive, slippery, intangible|反义词:obvious, clear, concrete|adjective
