dull:/dʌl/ | adj. | 迟钝的，无趣的，暗淡的 | lacking interest or excitement; not bright or shiny | The knife was too dull to cut the rope. 这把刀太钝了，切不了绳子。 | 近义词: boring, tedious, uninteresting, blunt | 反义词: sharp, bright, interesting, exciting | 词性: 形容词
