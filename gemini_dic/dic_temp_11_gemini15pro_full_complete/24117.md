brewhaha:/ˌbruːˈhaːhɑː/ /ˌbruːˈhɑːhɑː/| n. |喧闹，骚动；吵嚷；争吵|A noisy and overexcited reaction or response to something, often involving a disagreement or argument.|例句：The media created a brewhaha over the politician's comments. 媒体对这位政治家的评论大肆渲染。

例句：There was a huge brewhaha when the wrong winner was announced at the awards ceremony.  颁奖典礼上宣布了错误的获奖者，引起了一片哗然。

近义词：commotion, uproar, fuss, hubbub, hullabaloo|反义词:calm, peace, quiet|词性:noun
