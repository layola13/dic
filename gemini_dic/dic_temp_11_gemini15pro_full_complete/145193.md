overbaited:/ˌoʊvərˈbeɪtɪd/
adjective
过度诱饵的，上饵过多的
having too much bait (used in fishing)
The hook was overbaited, which scared away the fish. 鱼钩上的饵太多，把鱼吓跑了。
overloaded, excessive
