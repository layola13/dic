monosepalous:/ˌmɒnəˈsɛpələs/ | adj. | 单萼片的 | Having sepals united into a single structure, forming a tube or cup. |  The monosepalous calyx of the flower was a striking deep red.  这朵花的单萼片花萼是醒目的深红色。 | Synonym: gamosepalous | Antonym: polysepalous | Adjective
