fumbling:/ˈfʌmblɪŋ/| adj.  n.|笨手笨脚的，摸索；摸索，笨拙的动作|awkward and clumsy, especially in handling something; the act of groping or handling something clumsily|He made a fumbling attempt to tie his shoelaces. 他笨手笨脚地试图系鞋带。|Synonyms: clumsy, awkward, inept, maladroit|Antonyms: deft, skillful, adroit, dexterous|adjective

fumbling:/ˈfʌmblɪŋ/| n.|摸索，笨拙的动作|the act of groping or handling something clumsily|Her fumbling in the dark for the light switch was unsuccessful.  她在黑暗中摸索电灯开关，但没有成功。|Synonyms: groping, searching, scrambling|Antonyms: precision, accuracy|noun
