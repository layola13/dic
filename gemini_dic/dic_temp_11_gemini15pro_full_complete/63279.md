enthralment:/ɪnˈθrɔːlmənt/
n.
迷住，吸引
the state of being enthralled; fascination.
The children watched the magician with enthralment. 孩子们着迷地看着魔术师。
近义词：captivation, fascination, enchantment
反义词：boredom, disinterest, apathy
词性：名词

enthral:/ɪnˈθrɔːl/
vt.
迷住，吸引
to hold spellbound; charm.
The storyteller enthralled the children with his tales. 讲故事的人用他的故事吸引了孩子们。
近义词：captivate, fascinate, charm
反义词：bore, repel, disgust
词性：动词

词源：From en- + thrall, ultimately from a Norse word meaning "slave."
