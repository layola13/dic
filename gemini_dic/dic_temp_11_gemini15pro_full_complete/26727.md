cadger:/ˈkædʒər/| n.|乞丐；骗子；揩油的人|Someone who tries to get something from someone else without paying for it, or by persuading them to give it.|He's such a cadger, always borrowing money and never paying it back. 他真是个揩油的人，总是借钱不还。|近义词：beggar, sponger, scrounger|反义词：giver, donor|noun

cadger:/ˈkædʒər/| v.|乞讨；骗取；揩油|To try to get something from someone else without paying for it, or by persuading them to give it.|He's always cadging cigarettes from his friends. 他总是从朋友那里揩油香烟。|近义词：beg, sponge, scrounge|反义词：give, donate|verb
