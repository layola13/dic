localisable:/ˌloʊkəˈlaɪzəbl/ | /ˌləʊkəˈlaɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可本地化的，可局部化的 | Able to be adapted to local conditions or languages.  |  This software is localisable into multiple languages.  这个软件可以本地化成多种语言。 |  The error message is localisable, allowing for a more user-friendly experience.  错误消息是可本地化的，允许更友好的用户体验。 |  translatable, adaptable | non-localisable, untranslatable | adjective

Etymology->
From localize + -able. Localize itself comes from local + -ize.  "Local" ultimately derives from the Latin "locus" meaning "place." "-ize" and "-able" are suffixes of French and Latin origin, respectively, indicating ability or capability.
