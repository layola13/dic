allured:/əˈlʊərd/| v.|引诱，诱惑|to attract someone very strongly and make them want to have or do something|The traveler was allured by the promise of adventure. 旅行者被冒险的承诺所诱惑。|近义词：tempted, enticed, captivated, charmed|反义词：repelled, disgusted|verb

alluring:/əˈlʊərɪŋ/| adj.|诱人的，迷人的|powerfully attractive or charming; tempting|The restaurant had an alluring atmosphere. 这家餐厅的氛围很诱人。|近义词：attractive, tempting, enticing, captivating|反义词：repulsive, unappealing|adjective
