holsteins:/ˈhoʊlˌstaɪnz/| n.|荷斯坦奶牛（复数）|Holstein Friesians, a breed of dairy cattle originating in the Netherlands and northern Germany, known for their high milk production.  They are predominantly black and white.|The farmer milks his holsteins twice a day. 农场主一天两次挤他养的荷斯坦奶牛的奶。|近义词：dairy cows, cattle|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 beef cattle 肉牛)|词性:noun
