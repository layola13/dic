chalchihuitl: /ˌtʃɑːltʃiˈwiːtl/ | n. | 绿松石，翡翠 | A precious green stone, especially jade or turquoise, highly valued by the Aztecs and other pre-Columbian Mesoamerican peoples. |  The Aztec ruler wore a chalchihuitl pendant.  阿兹特克统治者戴着绿松石吊坠。

| Synonym: jade, turquoise, precious stone | Antonym: worthless stone | noun
