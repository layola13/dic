counterweight:/ˈkaʊntərˌweɪt/| n.|平衡重，平衡锤，抗衡力|a weight that balances another weight, especially one that moves up and down, or a force that counterbalances another force|The crane uses a large concrete block as a counterweight.  起重机使用一个大的混凝土块作为平衡重。|The government's policy of higher interest rates acted as a counterweight to inflation. 政府提高利率的政策对通货膨胀起到了抗衡作用。|Synonyms: balance, counterpoise, offset|Antonyms: imbalance, instability|noun

counterweights:/ˈkaʊntərˌweɪts/| n.|平衡重（复数）|plural form of counterweight|The counterweights on the elevator ensure smooth operation. 电梯上的平衡重确保平稳运行。|The system of checks and balances serves as counterweights to prevent any one branch of government from becoming too powerful.  制衡制度作为一种抗衡力量，防止任何一个政府部门变得过于强大。|Synonyms: balances, counterpoises, offsets|Antonyms: imbalances, instabilities|noun
