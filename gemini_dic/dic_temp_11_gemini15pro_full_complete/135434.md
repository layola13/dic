nonpersisting:/ˌnɒnpərˈsɪstɪŋ/
adjective
非持久性的，不持续的
Not persisting; not continuing to exist or endure.
The nonpersisting nature of the data meant it had to be constantly refreshed.  这些数据的非持久性意味着它必须不断刷新。
The symptoms were nonpersisting, disappearing within a few hours. 症状是非持久性的，几小时内就消失了。
synonym:transient, temporary, ephemeral, fleeting
antonym:persistent, enduring, permanent, lasting
word type:adjective
