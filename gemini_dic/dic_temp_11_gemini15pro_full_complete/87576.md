heteropod:/ˈhɛtərəpɒd/ (US) /ˈhɛtərəpɑːd/ (UK)| n. |异足动物，异足类|A pelagic snail of the order Heteropoda, having a laterally compressed foot adapted for swimming.|Heteropods are found in tropical and subtropical waters around the world. 异足类动物发现于世界各地的热带和亚热带水域。|Synonyms: sea elephant snail, sea butterfly|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

heteropodous:/ˌhɛtərəˈpɒdəs/ (US) /ˌhɛtərəˈpɑːdəs/ (UK)| adj. |异足的|Having different feet or legs.|Some crustaceans are heteropodous. 一些甲壳类动物是异足的。|Synonyms: N/A|Antonyms: N/A|Adjective
