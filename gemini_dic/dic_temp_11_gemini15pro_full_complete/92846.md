icterical: /ɪkˈtɛrɪk(ə)l/
adjective
黄疸的；患黄疸病的
Affected by jaundice, which causes yellowing of the skin and eyes.
The patient's skin appeared icterical, suggesting liver problems.  病人皮肤呈黄疸色，提示可能有肝脏问题。
The doctor noted the icterical tinge to the baby's skin. 医生注意到婴儿皮肤的黄疸色调。

近义词: jaundiced
反义词: healthy

词性: adjective
词源: From Greek ikteros "jaundice" + -ical.
