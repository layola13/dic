cellule:/seˈlyl/ (French), /ˈsɛlˌjuːl/ (anglicized pronunciation, less common)| n. |细胞; 小房间; 小组|A small cavity or compartment, especially in a honeycomb or a plant or animal body.  Also, a small group of people forming a nucleus of a larger political or revolutionary movement. |例句：The monks lived in individual cellules.  僧侣们住在各自的小房间里。

例句：The cellule was responsible for distributing propaganda. 这个小组负责分发宣传资料。|近义词：cell, compartment, chamber, cavity, group, unit|反义词: organism (in the biological sense) | noun
