coelentera:/sɪˈlɛntərə/ | n. |腔肠动物|Aquatic invertebrate animals characterized by a saclike body cavity with a single opening serving as both mouth and anus, radial symmetry, and tentacles with stinging cells (cnidocytes). Includes jellyfish, corals, and sea anemones.|Jellyfish and corals are coelenterates. 水母和珊瑚是腔肠动物。|近义词:cnidaria|反义词:None|名词


coelenterate:/sɪˈlɛntərət/ | adj. |腔肠动物的|Relating to or characteristic of the coelenterates.|The coelenterate body plan is relatively simple. 腔肠动物的身体构造相对简单。|近义词:cnidarian|反义词:None|形容词
