curvingly:/ˈkɜːrvɪŋli/
adv.
弯曲地，曲线地
in a curving manner; with curves
The road wound curvingly through the mountains. 这条路蜿蜒曲折地穿过群山。
近义词: sinuously, windingly
反义词: straightly, linearly
词性: 副词

Etymology:  From curve + -ing + -ly.  "Curve" comes from the Latin "curvus" meaning "bent, crooked".  The "-ing" suffix forms a present participle, and "-ly" transforms it into an adverb.
