deinternationalized:/diːɪntərˈnæʃənəlaɪzd/
verb
去国际化
To reverse the process of internationalization; to make something less international in character or scope.
The company deinternationalized its operations after the economic downturn.  这家公司在经济衰退后将其业务去国际化。
Governments sometimes deinternationalize education curricula to emphasize national values. 政府有时会将教育课程去国际化，以强调民族价值观。
近义词: localize, nationalize
反义词: internationalize, globalize
词性: verb


deinternationalization:/diːɪntərˌnæʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
去国际化
The process of reversing internationalization; the act of making something less international.
The deinternationalization of the economy has led to increased protectionism. 经济的去国际化导致了保护主义的抬头。
Some argue that deinternationalization of cultural institutions can lead to greater artistic diversity. 一些人认为文化机构的去国际化可以带来更大的艺术多样性。
近义词: localization, nationalization
反义词: internationalization, globalization
词性: noun
