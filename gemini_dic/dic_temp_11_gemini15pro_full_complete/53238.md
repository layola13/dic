directorial:/ˌdɪrɛkˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
导演的；指导的
Of or relating to the work of a director.
Her directorial debut was a critical success. 她的导演处女作获得了评论界的好评。
近义词: directing, leading
反义词:  (No clear antonym, perhaps "subordinate" in a managerial context)
词性: adjective
