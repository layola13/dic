crump:/krʌmp/ | n. | 脆响声；爆炸声；（尤指炮弹的）爆炸 | a loud, dull sound, especially of an explosion (especially of a shell) | The crump of distant artillery fire could be heard. 可以听到远处炮火的隆隆声。 | 近义词:bang, boom, explosion | 反义词:whisper, murmur | noun

crump:/krʌmp/ | v. | 发出低沉的爆炸声；（使）破碎；（使）倒塌； | to make a loud, dull sound; to break or collapse with a loud, dull sound |  The building crumped in the earthquake.  这栋建筑在地震中倒塌了。| 近义词:collapse, crumble, explode | 反义词:build, construct | verb
