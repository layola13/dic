hydro-electric:/ˌhaɪdrəʊɪˈlɛktrɪk/| adj.|水力发电的|Relating to or denoting the generation of electricity using flowing water (typically from a reservoir held behind a dam or other barrier) to drive turbines that activate generators.|The Three Gorges Dam is a massive hydro-electric project. 三峡大坝是一个大型水力发电项目。|Hydro-electric power is a clean energy source. 水力发电是一种清洁能源。|近义词：hydropower, waterpower|反义词: fossil fuel, nuclear|词性:形容词
