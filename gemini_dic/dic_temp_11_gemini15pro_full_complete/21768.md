boatswain:/ˈboʊtsən/ (US), /ˈbəʊsən/ (UK)| n.| 水手长，甲板长| A petty officer on a ship, in charge of the deck crew and responsible for the maintenance of the ship's hull, rigging, anchors, cables, and boats.| The boatswain piped the crew to quarters.  水手长吹哨召集船员到岗位。| The boatswain supervised the raising of the anchor. 水手长监督起锚工作。| synonyms: deck officer, bosun| antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific job title.  Could consider "landlubber" as a humorous opposite in terms of maritime experience.)| noun
