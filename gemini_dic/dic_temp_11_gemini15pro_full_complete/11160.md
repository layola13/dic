archetypally:/ˌɑːrkɪˈtaɪpəli/ | adv. |典型地；作为原型地| in a way that represents a perfect example of something; in a very typical way |  The hero in the story is archetypally brave and selfless. 故事中的英雄典型地展现了勇敢和无私。 | Examples:  The villain was archetypally evil, twirling his mustache and plotting world domination.  这个反派典型地邪恶，捻着胡子策划着统治世界。

The landscape was archetypally English, with rolling green hills and quaint villages. 这片景色典型地体现了英格兰的特点，连绵起伏的绿色山丘和古朴的村庄。|Synonyms: typically, classically, representatively, quintessentially | Antonyms: atypically, unusually, unconventionally | 词性: 副词
