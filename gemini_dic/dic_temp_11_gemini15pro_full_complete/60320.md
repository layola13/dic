egocentrically:/ˌiːɡoʊˈsɛntrɪkli/ | adv. | 以自我为中心地 | In a manner that is focused excessively or exclusively on oneself and one's own needs or desires; self-centeredly. | He egocentrically assumed everyone was interested in his story. 他以自我为中心地认为每个人都对他讲的故事感兴趣。 |  She egocentrically interrupted the conversation to talk about herself. 她自私地打断谈话，开始谈论自己。 | selfishly, narcissistically, self-centeredly | altruistically, empathetically | adverb
