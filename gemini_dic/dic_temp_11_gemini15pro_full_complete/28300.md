capybara:/ˌkæpiˈbɑːrə/ (US), /ˌkæpɪˈbɑːrə/ (UK)| n.|水豚|A large South American rodent with partially webbed feet, short hair, and no tail. It is the largest living rodent.|例句：We saw a capybara swimming in the river. 我们看到一只水豚在河里游泳。|例句：The capybara grazed peacefully on the riverbank. 水豚在河岸边平静地吃草。|近义词：rodent|反义词:|noun
