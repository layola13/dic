nationless:/ˈneɪʃənləs/
adjective
无国籍的；没有国家的
Without a nation; not belonging to any nation.
Many refugees end up stateless and nationless. 许多难民最终变得无国籍，没有国家。
nationless:/ˈneɪʃənləs/
adjective
无国籍的；没有国家的
Lacking national identity or a sense of belonging to a particular nation.
He felt nationless after living abroad for so many years. 在国外生活了这么多年后，他感到自己没有国籍。

Synonyms: stateless, denationalized, expatriated
Antonyms: national, citizen
