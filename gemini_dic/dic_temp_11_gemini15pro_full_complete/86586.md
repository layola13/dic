heliochromic:/ˌhiːliəˈkrəʊmɪk/
adjective
日光变色的，光致变色的
Exhibiting heliochromism; undergoing a reversible color change on exposure to sunlight.
The heliochromic ink changes from clear to colored in sunlight.  日光变色墨水在阳光下由透明变为彩色。
The artist used heliochromic paints to create a mural that transforms throughout the day.  这位艺术家使用日光变色颜料创作了一幅壁画，它会随着时间的推移而改变。
近义词: photochromic
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective

词源:
From helio- "sun" + -chromic "color-changing."
