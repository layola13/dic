octandrous: /ɒkˈtændrəs/
adjective
具八雄蕊的；八雄蕊的
Having eight stamens.
例句：The octandrous flower attracted the bee. 这朵具八雄蕊的花吸引了蜜蜂。

例句：Octandrous plants are common in certain families.  某些科的植物通常是八雄蕊的。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *octandrus, from octo (“eight”) + Ancient Greek ἀνήρ (anḗr, “man”), which in botany refers to the stamen.
