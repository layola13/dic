agrochemicals:/ˈæɡroʊˌkɛmɪkəlz/
noun
农用化学品
Chemicals used in agriculture, such as fertilizers, pesticides, and herbicides.
The overuse of agrochemicals can pollute water sources. 过度使用农用化学品会污染水源。
Synonyms: agricultural chemicals, pesticides, fertilizers, herbicides
Antonyms: organic fertilizers, biopesticides
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From agro- (combining form meaning "agriculture," from Latin ager "field") + chemicals (plural of chemical).
