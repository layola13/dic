hoochi:/ˈhuːtʃiː/
n.
劣质酒；私酿酒；非法制造的酒精饮料
Illicitly distilled or brewed alcoholic beverage, often of poor quality.
He offered me a swig of some hoochi he'd made in his bathtub. 他给了我一口他在浴缸里酿的私酒。
近义词：moonshine, white lightning, rotgut
反义词: (无明确反义词，可考虑用 legally produced alcohol 合法生产的酒)
词性: 名词
