altruism:/ˈæltruɪzəm/
n.|利他主义；无私|the belief in or practice of disinterested and selfless concern for the well-being of others.|Mother Teresa was known for her altruism.  特蕾莎修女以其利他主义而闻名。|synonyms: selflessness, benevolence, charity|antonyms: selfishness, egoism|noun

altruisms:/ˈæltruɪzəmz/
n.|利他行为；无私行为 (altruism的复数形式，较少使用)|specific acts or instances of selfless concern for others.|Her altruisms extended beyond her immediate family to the entire community.  她的利他行为不仅惠及她的直系亲属，也惠及了整个社区。|synonyms: selfless acts, benevolent deeds, charitable actions|antonyms: selfish acts, egoistic deeds|noun
