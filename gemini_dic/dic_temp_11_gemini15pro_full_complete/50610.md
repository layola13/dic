deregulate:/diːˈreɡjʊleɪt/
verb
解除管制，撤销管制
to remove government controls or rules from a business or other activity
The government has decided to deregulate the airline industry. 政府决定解除对航空业的管制。
近义词:liberalize, loosen
反义词:regulate, control
词性:v.

deregulated:/diːˈreɡjʊleɪtɪd/
adjective
解除管制的，撤销管制的
freed from government controls or rules
The deregulated banking sector led to increased competition. 解除管制的银行业导致竞争加剧。
近义词:liberalized, uncontrolled
反义词:regulated, controlled
词性:adj.

deregulation:/diːˌreɡjʊˈleɪʃn/
noun
解除管制，撤销管制
the act of removing government controls or rules from a business or other activity
Deregulation of the financial markets can have both positive and negative consequences. 金融市场的放松管制既有积极的后果，也有消极的后果。
近义词:liberalization
反义词:regulation
词性:n.
