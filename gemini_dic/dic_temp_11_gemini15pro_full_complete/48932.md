defuelled:/diːˈfjuːəld/
verb
卸除燃料
To remove fuel from a vehicle or aircraft.
The aircraft was defuelled before maintenance.  飞机在维护前被卸除了燃料。
The team defuelled the rocket after the launch was scrubbed.  发射取消后，团队从火箭中卸除了燃料。
synonym: drain, empty
antonym: fuel, refuel
word type: verb

defuelled:/diːˈfjuːəld/
adjective
已卸除燃料的
Having had fuel removed.
The defuelled aircraft was towed to the hangar.  卸除燃料后的飞机被拖到机库。
The defuelled rocket posed no further explosion risk.  卸除燃料后的火箭不再有爆炸风险。
synonym: emptied, drained
antonym: fuelled, refueled
word type: adjective
