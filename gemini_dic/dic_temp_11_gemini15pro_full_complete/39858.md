constantia:/kɒnˈstænʃɪə/ /kɑːnˈstænʃiə/
noun
坚定, 坚韧, 恒心, 毅力
Steadfastness, firmness of purpose, resoluteness, perseverance.
She showed great constantia in the face of adversity.  她在逆境中表现出了极大的毅力。
Synonyms: perseverance, tenacity, fortitude, resolve, steadfastness, determination
Antonyms:  irresolution, vacillation, weakness, instability, fickleness
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From Latin constantia ("steadfastness"), from constans ("standing firm"), present participle of constare ("to stand firm"), from com- ("together") + stare ("to stand").
