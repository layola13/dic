novatianism:/ˌnoʊveɪˈʃiənɪzəm/
n.
诺瓦森主义（基督教早期的一个异端派别）
A schismatic movement within the early Christian church, named after Novatian, a Roman presbyter, who held that the church should not readmit those who had lapsed during persecution, particularly those who had sacrificed to pagan deities.
The council condemned Novatianism and upheld the practice of allowing lapsed Christians back into the church after a period of penance.  诺瓦森主义被教会谴责，教会坚持允许犯错的基督徒在忏悔一段时间后重返教会。
Novatianism challenged the Church's authority to grant forgiveness. 诺瓦森主义挑战了教会给予宽恕的权力。
近义词:heresy, schism
反义词:orthodoxy
词性:noun
