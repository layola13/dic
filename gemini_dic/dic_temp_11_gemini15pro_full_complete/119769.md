mightier: /ˈmaɪtɪər/ | adj. | 更强大的，更强有力的 | Comparative form of "mighty."  Having or showing greater power, strength, or importance. |  A mightier force than we could imagine stopped them.  一股比我们能想象到的更强大的力量阻止了他们。 | synonyms: stronger, more powerful, more potent, greater | antonyms: weaker, less powerful, feebler | adjective
