glonoine:/ˈɡlaɪnəʊɪn/| n.|硝酸甘油; 甘油三硝酸酯|A colorless, oily, explosive liquid used in medicine as a vasodilator, especially in the treatment of angina pectoris. It is also a major component of dynamite.|She was prescribed glonoine for her angina. 她被开了硝酸甘油治疗心绞痛。|近义词：nitroglycerin, trinitroglycerin|反义词: None|词性:noun


Etymology:  From German *Glonoïn*, from Ancient Greek *γλυκύς* (glukús, “sweet”) + *nitrogen* (referring to the element contained in the compound)
