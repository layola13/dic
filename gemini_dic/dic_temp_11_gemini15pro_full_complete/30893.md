chaffs:/tʃæfs/| n.|谷壳，糠，碎屑；无价值的东西；戏弄, 开玩笑|The chaffs are separated from the grain. 谷壳与谷粒分离。|近义词：husks, debris, banter, jesting|反义词:grain, kernels, seriousness|noun

chaffs:/tʃæfs/| v.|戏弄，开玩笑|He chaffed her about her new haircut. 他取笑她的新发型。|近义词：tease, banter, joke|反义词:compliment, praise|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ċeaf (attested only in compounds like *ċeaf-finc "chaffinch"), from Proto-Germanic *kafō (source also of Old Saxon kaf, Old High German cheva, German Kaff "chaff"), from Proto-Indo-European *gōbh- (source also of Latin gabalum "ear of corn"). The verb is derived from the noun.
