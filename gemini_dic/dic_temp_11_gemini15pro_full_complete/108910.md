ledging:/ˈlɛdʒɪŋ/
n.
壁架；岩架；窗台；横档
A narrow horizontal surface projecting from a wall, cliff, or other vertical structure.
The climber found a precarious ledging on the cliff face. 攀岩者在悬崖面上找到了一个危险的岩架。
shelf, ledge, sill, projection
none
