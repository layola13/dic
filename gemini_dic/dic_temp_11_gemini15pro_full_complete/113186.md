lurers:/ˈlʊrərz/
n.
引诱者；诱惑者
People who tempt others into something, often something illegal or immoral.
The online game uses lurers to entice players to spend more money.  这款网络游戏用引诱者来诱惑玩家花更多的钱。
近义词: tempters, seducers, enticers
反义词: protectors, guardians
词性: noun

词源: From the verb "lure," which comes from Old French "luerre" (a falconer's lure), ultimately from Germanic origin.
