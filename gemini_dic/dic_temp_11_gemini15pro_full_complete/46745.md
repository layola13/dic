dandruff:/ˈdændrəf/| n. |头皮屑|Small white flakes of dead skin from the scalp.|例句：He's got a lot of dandruff. 他有很多头皮屑。|例句：The shampoo is designed to control dandruff. 这款洗发水旨在控制头皮屑。|近义词：scurf|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dandruffe*, of unknown origin. Possibly related to *dander* (anger, temper) + *druff, riff, ruff* (dirt, scurf).
