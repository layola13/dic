nonperpetuity:/ˌnɒnpɜːrpəˈtjuːɪti/
n.
非永久性，有限期限
A nonperpetuity is something that does not last forever.
The lease on the apartment is a nonperpetuity; it expires in a year.  公寓的租约并非永久，一年后就到期了。
temporary, finite, limited duration
perpetual, everlasting, permanent
noun
