debasingly:/dɪˈbeɪsɪŋli/
adverb
堕落地；卑鄙地
in a way that lowers the quality or esteem of something or someone; in a degrading manner.
He spoke debasingly of his former colleagues. 他贬低他的前同事。
Synonyms: degradingly, ignominiously, shamefully, basely
Antonyms: nobly, honorably, respectfully
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From debase + -ing + -ly. Debase comes from the Old French *debaser*, from de- "down" + bas "low".
