flumes:/fluːmz/
n.|水槽，斜槽，引水槽，水道|A sloping channel or chute for carrying water, especially one used to transport logs or irrigate land. Also, a deep narrow ravine with a stream running through it.|The logs floated down the flumes to the sawmill. 原木顺着水槽漂流到锯木厂。|近义词：channels, chutes, troughs, conduits, aqueducts|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "dam" in some contexts)|词性:noun

flumes:/fluːmz/
n.|（游乐场的）滑水道|A water slide or channel at an amusement park or swimming pool.|The kids loved going down the flumes at the water park. 孩子们喜欢在水上乐园玩滑水道。|近义词：water slides, chutes|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
