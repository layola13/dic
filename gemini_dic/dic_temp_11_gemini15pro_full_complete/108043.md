latched:/lætʃt/ | adj. | 闩住的；扣紧的；迷上的；抓住的 |  in a fastened or secured position; obsessed with something |  He was latched onto the idea of buying a boat. 他迷上了买船的想法。 | 近义词: fastened, secured, hooked, obsessed, captivated | 反义词: unlatched, open, free, indifferent | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *lacchen, from Old English læccan (“to seize, grasp”), from Proto-Germanic *lakkōną (“to play, jump, move quickly”).
