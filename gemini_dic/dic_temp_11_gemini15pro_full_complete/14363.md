autoplasty:/ˈɔːtəˌplæsti/
noun
自体成形术; 自体移植
Surgical reconstruction of a part of the body using tissues taken from another part of the same body.
The surgeon performed an autoplasty using bone from the patient's hip.  外科医生使用病人髋部的骨骼进行了自体成形术。

autoplasty:/ˈɔːtəˌplæsti/
noun
自体修复
The process of self-repair or regeneration.
The body's natural autoplasty helped heal the wound.  身体的自然自体修复帮助伤口愈合。

Etymology: From auto- (self) + -plasty (surgical repair or reconstruction).

Synonyms:  autograft, autotransplantation
Antonyms: allograft, heterotransplantation
Word type: Noun
