chards:/tʃɑːrdz/| n.| 菜梗，叶柄；(尤指烤肉后留下的)烧焦物，炭渣|The chards of the Swiss chard are often cooked separately from the leaves.  瑞士甜菜的叶柄通常与叶子分开烹饪。| The fire left behind a pile of black chards. 火留下了一堆黑色的炭渣。 | 近义词: stalks, stems, cinders, embers | 反义词:  (无明确的反义词) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old French *charde, from Late Latin *carda, from Latin carduus (“thistle”). Related to cardoon.
