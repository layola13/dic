outbrags:/ˌaʊtˈbræɡz/
verb
吹嘘胜过；夸耀超过
boasts more extravagantly than; surpasses in bragging
He outbragged his friends about his new car. 他对他的新车吹嘘得比他的朋友们还厉害。
近义词: outboasts, surpasses
反义词: downplays, understates
词性: verb
