distance-education:/ˈdɪstəns ɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 远程教育 | Education that takes place without the students being physically present in a traditional classroom setting. It often involves online learning, correspondence courses, and other forms of remote instruction. |  Many universities now offer distance-education programs. 许多大学现在都提供远程教育课程。|  She completed her master's degree through distance education. 她通过远程教育完成了硕士学位。 | synonyms: online learning, e-learning, distance learning, correspondence education | antonyms: traditional education, face-to-face learning | noun

distance-educations: /ˈdɪstəns ɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 远程教育（项目/课程）（复数形式，较少使用）|  Multiple programs or instances of distance education.  While grammatically correct, it's more common to use "distance-education programs" or "distance-learning opportunities." | Several universities offer various distance-educations focused on different fields.  几所大学提供针对不同领域的各种远程教育。 |  He researched different distance-educations before choosing one. 他在选择之前研究了不同的远程教育。 | synonyms: online learning programs, e-learning courses, distance learning programs, correspondence courses | antonyms: traditional education programs, face-to-face learning experiences | noun
