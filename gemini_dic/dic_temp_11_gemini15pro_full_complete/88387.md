hirees:/haɪəˈriːz/
noun
被雇佣者；新员工
people who have recently been hired for a job
The company welcomed its new hirees with a luncheon. 公司为新员工举办了欢迎午宴。
Synonyms: recruits, new employees, appointees
Antonyms: retirees, leavers, ex-employees
n.
