ancestor:/ˈænsɛstər/ (US), /ˈænsɛstə(r)/ (UK)| n. |祖先，先人|A person from whom one is descended, especially one who lived a long time ago.|My ancestors came from Ireland. 我的祖先来自爱尔兰。|
近义词：forefather, progenitor, antecedent|反义词:descendant|词性:名词

ancestor:/ˈænsɛstər/ (US), /ˈænsɛstə(r)/ (UK)| n. |原型，先驱|something that existed before or gave rise to something else|This clock is the ancestor of all modern clocks.  这个钟是所有现代钟表的原型。|近义词：predecessor, prototype|反义词:successor|词性:名词
