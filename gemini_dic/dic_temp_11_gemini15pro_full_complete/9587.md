antisocietal:/ˌæntaɪsəˈsaɪɪtəl/
adjective
反社会的
opposed to the norms and values of society.
His antisocial behavior led to his expulsion from school.  他的反社会行为导致他被学校开除。
synonyms: antisocial,  asocial, misanthropic,  nonconformist, rebellious,  deviant,  unconventional
antonyms:  prosocial,  sociable,  conformist,  conventional
词性: 形容词
