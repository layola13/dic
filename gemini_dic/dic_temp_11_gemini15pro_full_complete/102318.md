ivory-like:/ˈaɪvəriˌlaɪk/
adjective
象牙似的；象牙色的
Resembling ivory in color, texture, or appearance.
Her skin was smooth and ivory-like. 她的皮肤光滑，象牙色。
Near Antonyms: ebony, jet-black
Near Synonyms: creamy, alabaster, bone-white
词性: 形容词
