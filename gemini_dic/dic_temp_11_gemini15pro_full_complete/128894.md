nimble:/ˈnɪmbl/| adj.|敏捷的，灵活的|Quick and light in movement or action; agile.|The nimble fingers of the pianist flew across the keys.  钢琴家灵活的手指在琴键上飞舞。|近义词：agile, deft, quick, swift, spry|反义词：clumsy, awkward, slow|词性:形容词
nimbleness:/ˈnɪmblnəs/| n.|敏捷，灵活性|The quality of being nimble; agility.|The gymnast's nimbleness was breathtaking. 体操运动员的敏捷性令人叹为观止。|近义词：agility, dexterity, quickness, swiftness|反义词：clumsiness, awkwardness, slowness|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *nembil, from Old English *numol (“capable of catching, taking, receiving”), from Proto-Germanic *numalaz (“capable of catching”), equivalent to numen +‎ -le. Cognate with Old Saxon numal, Old Frisian nemel, Dutch nemen (“to take”), German nehmen (“to take”), Old Norse nema (“to take”). More at numen.
