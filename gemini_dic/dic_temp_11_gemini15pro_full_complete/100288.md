intersentimental:/ˌɪntərˌsɛntɪˈmɛntəl/| adj.|相互感伤的，共同感伤的|Characterized by shared sentimental feelings or experiences between individuals or groups. It describes a sentimentality that is reciprocal and connects people through shared emotions.|例句：The two old friends shared an intersentimental moment as they reminisced about their childhood.  两位老朋友在追忆童年时，共享了一段相互感伤的时刻。

例句：The movie evoked an intersentimental response from the audience, many of whom had experienced similar family struggles. 这部电影引起了观众的共同感伤，因为许多观众都经历过类似的家庭困境。|近义词：mutual, shared, reciprocal, collective|反义词：individual, personal, private|词性:形容词
词源: "inter-" (prefix meaning "between, among, mutually") + "sentimental" (adjective related to feelings and emotions).
