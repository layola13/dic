bawl:/bɔːl/
bawl:v.|大声哭喊；放声痛哭；叫卖；大声斥责|To cry loudly and unrestrainedly; to shout or call out loudly.|The child bawled when his toy broke. 孩子玩具坏了就大哭起来。|近义词:cry, weep, shout, yell, scream|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:动词
bawl:n.|大声哭喊；叫卖声|A loud cry or shout.|The trader's bawl could be heard across the market. 商贩的叫卖声响彻整个市场。|近义词:cry, shout, yell|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:名词
