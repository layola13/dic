iridescent:/ˌɪrɪˈdɛsənt/ | adj. | 彩虹色的，闪光的 | Displaying luminous colors that seem to change when seen from different angles. |  The iridescent feathers of the peacock are truly stunning.  孔雀彩虹般的羽毛真是令人叹为观止。| synonyms: shimmering, opalescent, pearly, lustrous | antonyms: dull, matte | adjective

iridescence:/ˌɪrɪˈdɛsns/ | n. | 彩虹色，闪光 | The quality of showing luminous colors that seem to change when seen from different angles. | The iridescence of the soap bubble captivated the child. 肥皂泡的彩虹色迷住了孩子。| synonyms: shimmer, opalescence, pearlescence, luster | antonyms: dullness, matteness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *irisdescent*, from *iris* (rainbow) (from Latin *iris*, from Greek *ῑ̃́ρις*) + *-escent* (becoming) (from Latin *-ēscent*). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *weyh₁-* (to turn, wind, bend).
