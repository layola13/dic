divvies:/ˈdɪviz/| n. |分红，股息；赃物（尤指平分的）|Shares of something, especially money or profits, that are divided between a number of people.|The robbers split the divvies evenly. 强盗们平分了赃物。|近义词：shares, proceeds, loot, spoils|反义词:losses, debts|词性:名词

词源:
From the verb "divide".  Shortened form of dividends, first recorded in 1886.
