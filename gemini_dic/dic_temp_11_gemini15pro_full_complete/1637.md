acinetiform: /əˈsɪnɪtɪfɔːrm/
adjective
无运动的；似不动的；不活动的
Resembling Acineta, a genus of stalked ciliate protozoans characterized by their lack of motility in the adult stage.  Having a form or structure similar to Acineta, typically immobile or sessile.
The acinetiform organism remained attached to the substrate.  这种无运动的生物体仍然附着在基质上。
Synonyms: sessile, immobile, fixed, attached
Antonyms: motile, mobile, moving, active
word type: adjective


Etymology:  From "Acineta" (a genus of ciliates) + "-form" (having the form of).  "Acineta" derives from the Greek "akinetos," meaning "motionless."
