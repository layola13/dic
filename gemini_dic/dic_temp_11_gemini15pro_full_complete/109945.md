liberalities:/ˌlɪbəˈrælɪtiz/
n.
慷慨；大方；宽宏大量
acts of generosity; the state of being liberal in giving
He was known for his liberalities to the poor. 他以对穷人的慷慨而闻名。
Synonyms: generosities, bounties, munificence, largesse
Antonyms: stinginess, miserliness, parsimony
