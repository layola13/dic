frequentable:/frɪˈkwɛntəbl/
adjective
可常去的；可出入的；可光顾的
Suitable for frequenting; pleasant or agreeable enough to visit often.
The restaurant is frequentable and the food is delicious.  这家餐厅可以常去，而且食物很美味。
This bar is frequentable by students and locals alike.  这家酒吧学生和当地人都经常光顾。
近义词: accessible, approachable, visitable, resort to
反义词: unfrequented, inaccessible, undesirable, unpleasant
词性: adjective

词源:
From French *frequentable*, from frequenter (“to frequent”) + -able.  Ultimately from Latin frequentō (“I visit often, resort to, haunt”).
