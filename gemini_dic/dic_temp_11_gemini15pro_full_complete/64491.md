erodabilities:/ɪˌroʊdəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 可蚀性，侵蚀性 |  The susceptibility of different soil types to erosion.  It refers to the ease with which soil particles are detached and transported by erosive agents like wind or water. | The erodabilities of the different soils were compared in the study.  这项研究比较了不同土壤的可蚀性。| 近义词: erodibility, vulnerability to erosion | 反义词: resistance to erosion | 词性: 名词
