miaowing:/miˈaʊɪŋ/
| adj.|喵喵叫的，（猫）哀叫的|The sound of a cat crying or mewing.  It describes the characteristic vocalization of a cat, particularly when it's in distress, seeking attention, or expressing discomfort.  It can also be used figuratively to describe a similar sound made by a person or other animal. |例句：The kitten was miaowing pitifully because it was lost. 小猫可怜地喵喵叫，因为它迷路了。|近义词：mewing, caterwauling (for loud, unpleasant meows)|反义词: purring, growling|词性:形容词


miaowing:/miˈaʊɪŋ/
| n.|喵喵叫，（猫）哀叫|The act or sound of a cat miaowing. |例句：The constant miaowing of the cat kept me awake all night. 猫不停地喵喵叫让我整夜没睡。|近义词：mewing, caterwauling (for loud, unpleasant meows)|反义词: purring, growling|词性:名词
