jewellike:/ˈdʒuːəlˌlaɪk/
adjective
像宝石的；珠宝般的
Resembling a jewel; brilliant or precious.
The car had jewellike headlights. 这辆车有着像宝石般的大灯。
The hummingbird's feathers were jewellike in their brilliance.  蜂鸟的羽毛光彩夺目，像宝石一样。
近义词：gemlike, brilliant, sparkling, dazzling, precious
反义词：dull, drab, lackluster
词性: adjective

词源: jewel (n.) + -like (suffix)
"jewel" comes from Old French "jouel," ultimately from Latin "jocale," meaning "plaything."
"-like" is a suffix forming adjectives and adverbs, meaning "resembling," "characteristic of."
