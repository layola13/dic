brahmo:/ˈbrɑːmoʊ/
n.
Brahmo
A follower of Brahmoism, a monotheistic reformist Hindu religious tradition.
Raja Ram Mohan Roy founded the Brahmo Samaj. 拉贾·拉姆·莫汉·罗伊创立了梵社。
follower, believer, adherent
atheist, non-believer
noun

Etymology:
From Bengali ব্রাহ্ম (brāhma), from Sanskrit ब्रह्मन् (brahman).
