neozoic:/ˌniːəˈzoʊɪk/ | adj. n. | 新生代的；新生代 | Relating to or denoting the current geological era, which began 66 million years ago and continues to the present.  |  The neozoic era is characterized by the dominance of mammals.  新生代以哺乳动物的统治地位为特征。 | Example:  The fossil record shows a dramatic increase in mammal diversity during the Neozoic Era. 化石记录显示新生代哺乳动物多样性急剧增加。| Synonyms:  Cenozoic | Antonyms:  Paleozoic, Mesozoic | adjective, noun
