neelghau:/ˈniːlɡaʊ/ | n. | 蓝牛,印度羚 | A large Asian antelope (Boselaphus tragocamelus) with short horns, a sloping back, and a tufted tail, the male being bluish gray. |  例句：The neelghau grazed peacefully in the meadow. 蓝牛在草地上平静地吃草。

例句：We saw a herd of neelghau while on safari. 我们在旅行时看到了一群蓝牛。| 近义词：blue bull, nilgai | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Hindi नीलगाय (nīlgāy), from नील (nīl, “blue”) + गाय (gāy, “cow”).
