bondar:/ˈbɒndɑːr/ (UK), /ˈbɑːndɑːr/ (US)| n.|桶匠；制桶工人；合作社成员|A craftsman who makes barrels or casks.  A cooper.|例句：The bondar carefully assembled the oak staves to form a barrel. 桶匠小心地组装橡木板条做成一个桶。

例句：The bondar's workshop was filled with the scent of wood shavings. 桶匠的工作间里充满了刨木屑的香味。

近义词：cooper, barrel-maker|反义词: None|词性:Noun
