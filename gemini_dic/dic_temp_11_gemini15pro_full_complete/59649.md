echelonments:/ˈeʃəlɒnmənts/ | n. |梯次配置，阶梯状排列|The arrangement of troops, ships, aircraft, etc., in echelon formation. The process of arranging something in echelon formation.|The general ordered the echelonments of his troops along the ridge. 将军命令他的部队沿着山脊梯次配置。|The echelonments of the fleet allowed for broad coverage of the area. 舰队的梯次配置使其能够广泛覆盖该区域。|Synonym:  arrangements, dispositions, deployments|Antonym:  disorganization, scattering|noun
