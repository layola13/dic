decomplex:/diːkəmˈplɛks/
verb
简化；化解…的复杂性
to make something less complicated
The new software decomplexes the process of filing taxes.  这个新软件简化了报税的流程。
simplify, streamline, clarify
complicate, confuse
词性:动词
