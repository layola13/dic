delineator:/dɪˈlɪnɪeɪtər/| n.|描绘者; 画线者; 定界符; 分界线|something that serves to delineate; something that marks a boundary|The river serves as a natural delineator between the two countries. 这条河是两国之间的天然分界线。|近义词：marker, boundary, separator, divider|反义词：connector, unifier|noun

delineator:/dɪˈlɪnɪeɪtər/| n.|轮廓线; 略图; |a line or set of lines enclosing or indicating the shape of an object in a sketch or diagram.|The architect used a delineator to sketch the building's outline. 建筑师用绘图笔勾勒出建筑物的轮廓。|近义词：outline, contour, sketch|反义词：whole, complete|noun
