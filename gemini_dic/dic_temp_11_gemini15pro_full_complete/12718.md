assessing:/əˈsesɪŋ/
v.
评估，评定
evaluating or judging the nature, ability, or quality of something or someone.
The teacher is assessing the students' work. 老师正在评估学生的作业。
We are assessing the damage caused by the storm. 我们正在评估风暴造成的损失。
