nonductile:/nɒnˈdʌktaɪl/ | adj. | 非延展性的，不韧的 | Not ductile; incapable of being permanently stretched or drawn out into a wire or thread without breaking. | The ceramic material proved to be nonductile and shattered under pressure.  这种陶瓷材料被证明是非延展性的，在压力下破碎了。|  The nonductile metal snapped rather than bent. 这种非延展性金属断裂而不是弯曲。 | 近义词: brittle, fragile, inelastic | 反义词: ductile, malleable, flexible | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "ductile" (from Latin "ducere," meaning "to lead" or "to draw")
