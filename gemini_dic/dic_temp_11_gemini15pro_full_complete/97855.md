inleaguer:/ˈɪnˌliːɡər/
n.
同盟者；同伙；同党
A person who belongs to the same league, group, or team as another.
例句：He's an inleaguer of mine from college. 他是我大学时的同窗好友。

例句：She found an inleaguer in her fight against injustice. 在她对抗不公的斗争中，她找到了一个盟友。

近义词：ally, associate, partner, teammate, confederate
反义词：opponent, adversary, enemy, foe
词性: 名词
