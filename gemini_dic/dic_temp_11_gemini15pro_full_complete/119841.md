mildewed:/ˈmɪlˌdjud/ | adj. |发霉的；长霉的|Affected with mildew; musty.|The old books in the basement were mildewed. 地下室里的旧书发霉了。|Synonyms: moldy, musty, fusty|Antonyms: clean, fresh|adjective

mildew:/ˈmɪlˌdjuː/ | n. |霉；霉菌|A superficial growth of minute fungi forming in moist warm conditions, especially on cloth or paper or leather.|The dampness caused mildew to grow on the walls. 潮湿使墙上长霉了。|Synonyms: mold, fungus|Antonyms: cleanliness|noun
