obsoleteness: /ˌɒbsəˈliːtnəs/ (US) /ˌɒbsəˈliːtnɪs/ (UK)| n. | 过时，陈旧，废弃 |The obsoleteness of their equipment put them at a disadvantage.  他们的设备过时，使他们处于劣势。|例句：The rapid obsoleteness of technology is a challenge for many companies.  技术的快速过时对许多公司来说是一个挑战。|近义词：outdatedness, obsolescence, antiquity, archaism |反义词：currency, modernity, newness |词性: 名词

Etymology: From obsolete + -ness.  Obsolete comes from the Latin word obsoletus, past participle of obsolescere "to fall into disuse," from ob- "away" + solere "to be accustomed."
