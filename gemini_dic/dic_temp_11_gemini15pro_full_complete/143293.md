outdream:/ˌaʊtˈdriːm/
verb
超越梦想；在梦中胜过
to surpass in dreaming; to have a more fantastic or vivid dream than
例句：She outdreamed him with visions of flying through galaxies. 她梦到自己飞越星系，比他的梦更奇妙。

例句：He outdreamed everyone in the competition with his fantastical tale of a city built on clouds. 在比赛中，他梦到一座建立在云端的城市，这个奇幻的故事超越了所有人的梦想。

近义词：surpass, exceed, transcend, outfantasize
反义词：(No direct antonym, could consider "underdream" - to dream less vividly or ambitiously)
词性: verb
