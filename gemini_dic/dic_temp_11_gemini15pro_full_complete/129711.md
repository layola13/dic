non-ego:/nɒnˈeɪɡəʊ/| n.|非我; 无我|Absence of the ego or self; the concept of selflessness or the absence of individual identity.|例句：In some Eastern philosophies, the goal is to achieve a state of non-ego. 在一些东方哲学中，目标是达到无我的境界。|例句：Meditation can help you transcend the ego and experience a sense of non-ego. 冥想可以帮助你超越自我，体验无我的感觉。|近义词：selflessness, altruism, egolessness|反义词:ego, self, selfishness|noun

non-ego:/nɒnˈeɪɡəʊ/| adj.|无我的|Characterized by the absence of the ego or self; selfless.|例句：Non-ego behavior involves acting without consideration for personal gain. 无我的行为包括不考虑个人利益的行动。|例句：She demonstrated a non-ego approach to leadership, prioritizing the needs of her team. 她展示了一种无我的领导方式，优先考虑团队的需求。|近义词：selfless, altruistic, egoless|反义词:egoistic, selfish, self-centered|adjective
