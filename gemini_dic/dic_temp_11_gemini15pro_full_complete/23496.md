brahminism:/ˈbrɑːmɪnɪzəm/
n.|婆罗门教；婆罗门主义|A complex system of religious and social beliefs and practices based on the Vedas and centered on the Brahmins, the priestly caste in Hinduism.  It emphasizes ritual purity, the authority of the Vedas, and the hierarchical caste system.|The ancient texts offer insights into brahminism and its evolution into Hinduism. 古代文献让我们得以了解婆罗门教及其演变为印度教的过程。|Hinduism, Vedic religion|casteless society
