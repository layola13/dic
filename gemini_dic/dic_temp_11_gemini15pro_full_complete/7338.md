anhelose:/ænˈheloʊz/ | v. |渴望，热望 |To long for something intensely; to yearn. |She anhelosed for a life of peace and tranquility. 她渴望过上平静安宁的生活。|The travelers anhelosed for the sight of home after their long journey.  旅行者在长途旅行后渴望看到家。|近义词：yearn, crave, long for, desire |反义词：dislike, hate, despise |词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin *anhelosus, from Latin anhēlus (“panting, breathless”), from anhēlāre (“to pant”), from an- (“toward, up to”) + hālāre (“to breathe”).
