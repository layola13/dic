extrapolatable:/ɪkˈstræpəˌleɪtəbl/ | adj. | 可外推的，可推断的 | Able to be extrapolated.  Inferring something from known facts or data. |  The results of the small study are extrapolatable to larger populations.  这项小型研究的结果可以推断到更大的人群。|  The trend is not necessarily extrapolatable to the future. 这种趋势不一定会延续到未来。 | predictable, projectable, inferable | non-extrapolatable | adjective
