associability:/əˌsoʊʃiəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可交际性；合群性；亲和力 | The quality of being associable; the ability to be easily associated or connected with others.  |  Her natural associability made her popular. 她天生的亲和力使她很受欢迎。 | synonyms: sociability, gregariousness, affability, friendliness | antonyms: unsociability, aloofness, introversion | noun

associability:/əˌsoʊʃiəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可结合性；可联想性 | The capability of being readily connected or linked in the mind or in experience. | The associability of the two ideas made them easy to remember. 这两个想法的可联想性使它们很容易记住。 | synonyms: connectability, linkability, relatability | antonyms: disconnection, unrelatedness | noun
