colorings:/ˈkʌlərɪŋz/
n.
着色，染色，颜色；（尤指食物或药品中添加的）色素，着色剂
the act of adding color to something, or the colors that are added. Especially artificial colors added to food or medicine.
The colorings in the candy made it look appealing to children.  这些糖果里的色素使它看起来对孩子们很有吸引力。

近义词:coloration, pigmentation, tints, hues, dyes, shades
反义词:bleach, fading, dullness
词性:noun
