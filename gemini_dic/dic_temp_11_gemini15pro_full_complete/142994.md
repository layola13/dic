outbawling: /ˌaʊtˈbɔːlɪŋ/
verb
大声哭喊，胜过…的哭喊
crying louder than someone or something else
The baby was outbawling the other children in the nursery.  这个婴儿的哭声比托儿所里其他孩子的哭声都大。
The distraught mother outbawled everyone at the funeral.  那位悲痛欲绝的母亲在葬礼上哭得比谁都大声。
Synonyms: outcrying, outshouting, outweighing, overpowering
Antonyms: whispering, whimpering, muting, quieting
词性: 动词
