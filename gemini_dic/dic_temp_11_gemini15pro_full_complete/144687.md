ooze:/uːz/
verb
渗出；泄漏
to flow slowly out of something; to (cause to) pass gradually or leak through or as if through small openings
例句：
Blood was oozing from a cut on her head.
鲜血从她头上的伤口渗出。

Pus oozed from the wound.
伤口里渗出脓液。

近义词：seep, leak, discharge
反义词：gush, spurt
词性：v.

ooze:/uːz/
noun
软泥；淤泥
soft, watery mud, especially at the bottom of a river or lake
例句：
The riverbed was thick with ooze.
河床淤泥很厚。

The snail left a trail of glistening ooze.
蜗牛留下了一道闪闪发光的粘液痕迹。

近义词：slime, mud, muck
反义词：N/A
词性：n.
