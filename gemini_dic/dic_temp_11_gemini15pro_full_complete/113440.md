lyncher:/ˈlɪntʃər/| n.|私刑者，施以私刑的人|A person who takes part in lynching; someone who participates in the illegal killing of a person by a mob. |The lynchers were never brought to justice. 那些施以私刑的人从未被绳之以法。|Example: The sheriff tried to stop the angry mob from becoming lynchers. 警长试图阻止愤怒的暴徒变成私刑者。|Synonyms: vigilante, executioner (in this context), mob member|Antonyms: law enforcer, peacekeeper|noun
