overattached:/ˌoʊvərəˈtætʃt/| adj.|过度依恋的，过分依赖的|Excessively attached or dependent, often to a person, to an unhealthy or extreme degree.  Characterized by clinginess, neediness, and difficulty separating.|She's overattached to her boyfriend and gets anxious when he's not around. 她过度依赖她的男朋友，当他不和她在一起时，她就会焦虑。|近义词：clingy, needy, dependent, possessive|反义词：independent, detached, aloof|词性:形容词

词源: The word "overattached" is formed by combining the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessively" or "too much") with the past participle of the verb "attach" (meaning "to connect or fasten").
