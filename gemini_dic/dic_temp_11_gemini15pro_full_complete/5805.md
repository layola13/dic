amaryllises: /ˌæməˈrɪlɪsiːz/
n.
朱顶红（复数）
Amaryllises are bulbous plants known for their large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers, typically red or pink.
例句：The amaryllises in the garden are blooming beautifully.  花园里的朱顶红开得很漂亮。

例句：She planted several amaryllises in pots for the winter. 她冬天在花盆里种了几株朱顶红。

近义词：hippeastrums (although technically a different genus, often used interchangeably)
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a flower name)
词性: Noun
