nonselective:/ˌnɑːnsɪˈlɛktɪv/| adj.|非选择性的，不加选择的|Not selecting or distinguishing between things or people; indiscriminate.|The herbicide is nonselective and will kill any plant it touches. 这种除草剂是非选择性的，会杀死任何接触到的植物。|Synonyms: indiscriminate, unselective, general, broad, universal|Antonyms: selective, discriminating, particular, specific|adjective
