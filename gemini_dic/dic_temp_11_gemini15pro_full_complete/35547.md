coalescence:/ˌkoʊəˈlɛsəns/| n. |合并，结合，联合|The act or process of coming together to form one mass or whole.|The coalescence of the two companies created a powerful new entity. 两家公司的合并创造了一个强大的新实体。|amalgamation, fusion, union, merger|separation, division, fission|noun


coalescences:/ˌkoʊəˈlɛsənsiz/| n. |合并，结合，联合（复数）|Plural form of coalescence, referring to multiple instances of the act or process of coming together.|The various coalescences of small tribes eventually led to the formation of a nation.  众多小部落的合并最终导致了一个国家的形成。|amalgamations, fusions, unions, mergers|separations, divisions, fissions|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin coalescentia, from coalescent- (stem of coalescens), present participle of coalescere "to grow together," from com- "together" (see com-) + alescere "to grow up," inchoative of alere "to nourish" (see old).
