indo-abyssinian:/ˌɪndoʊˌæbɪˈsɪniən/
|adj.|印埃塞俄比亚的；印度和埃塞俄比亚动植物区系的|Relating to or denoting a biogeographical region comprising the Indian subcontinent and parts of Africa, including Ethiopia.|The indo-abyssinian region is known for its diverse flora and fauna. 印埃塞俄比亚地区以其多样化的动植物群而闻名。|Indo-Ethiopian| N/A |adjective
Etymology:
indo- (prefix): from Latin Indus, meaning "Indus River", later used to refer to India.
Abyssinian:  referring to Abyssinia, a historical name for Ethiopia.
