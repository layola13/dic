hokinesses:/ˈhoʊkinɪsɪz/
n.
老套, 俗气, 陈腐
The quality of being hokey; mawkish sentimentality or artificiality.
The movie was full of hokinesses, like the dramatic sunset and the overly-sweet love story.  这部电影充满了老套的桥段，比如戏剧性的日落和过于甜腻的爱情故事。
近义词：banalities, clichés, platitudes, corniness
反义词：originality, sophistication, genuineness
词性: 名词 (plural of hokiness)

hokey:/ˈhoʊki/
adj.
老套的, 俗气的
Obviously sentimental or artificial.
The plot of the movie was incredibly hokey.  这部电影的情节极其老套。
近义词：corny, sentimental, clichéd, hackneyed
反义词：original, sophisticated, genuine
词性: 形容词
