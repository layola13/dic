cacodoxy:/ˈkækəˌdɑːksi/
n.
异端邪说，谬见，错误的教义
A belief or opinion that goes against accepted religious doctrine or a generally accepted principle.
例句：His sermons were full of cacodoxy, leading to his excommunication. 他的布道充满了异端邪说，导致他被逐出教会。

例句：The teacher warned her students against the cacodoxies of pseudoscience. 老师警告她的学生们要警惕伪科学的谬论。
近义词：heresy, heterodoxy, fallacy, error, misconception
反义词：orthodoxy, dogma, truth, correctness


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cacodoxia, from Greek kakodoxia "bad opinion," from kakos "bad" + doxa "opinion, glory."
