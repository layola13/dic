dozy:/ˈdoʊzi/| adj.|昏昏欲睡的，困倦的|Feeling sleepy, drowsy.|例句：I felt dozy after lunch. 午饭后我感到昏昏欲睡。

例句：The warm room made everyone feel dozy.  温暖的房间让每个人都昏昏欲睡。|近义词：sleepy, drowsy, lethargic|反义词：alert, awake, energetic|词性:形容词

词源: From doze + -y.  Ultimately from Old English *dūsian "become stupefied, be amazed."
