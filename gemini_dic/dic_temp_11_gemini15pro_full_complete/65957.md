exacts:/ɪɡˈzækts/ | v. | 要求；强求；急需 | demands and obtains (something) from someone. |  He exacted a promise from them. 他强迫他们作出承诺。 | 近义词: demands, requires, extorts | 反义词: gives, offers, relinquishes | 词性: 动词

exacts:/ɪɡˈzækts/ | adj. (古语，罕用) | 准确的；精确的 | Precise; accurate. | They made exact copies of the documents. 他们制作了文件的精确副本。 | 近义词: precise, accurate, correct | 反义词: inaccurate, imprecise, incorrect | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Late Middle English *exacten*, from Latin *exactus*, past participle of *exigō* (“I demand, require, drive out”).  The adjective is from Latin *exactus* (“driven out, exact”).
