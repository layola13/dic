floodwaters:/ˈflʌdwɔːtərz/
n.
洪水，泛滥的河水
The floodwaters inundated the town. 洪水淹没了小镇。
The floodwaters receded slowly after the storm.  暴风雨过后，洪水慢慢退去。
inundation, deluge
low water, drought
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "flood" (Old English "flod," meaning "a flowing, stream, flood") + "water" (Old English "wæter").
