hards:/hɑːrdz/ | n. | 矿渣，尾矿；（尤指亚麻或大麻的）粗纤维 | The hards are the waste material left after ore has been processed. 矿渣是矿石加工后剩下的废料。 |  The hards of flax are used to make rope. 亚麻的粗纤维用来制作绳索。 | synonyms: tailings, waste, refuse, dregs, leftovers | antonyms: valuables, treasures | noun

Etymology:  From Middle English *hardes, plural of hard (adjective).  Originally referred to the coarse part of flax or hemp. The sense of "mining waste" developed later.
