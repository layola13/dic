dulce:/ˈdʊlseɪ/ (US), /ˈdʌlsi/ (UK)| adj. n.|甜的；甜食|Sweet; a sweet food or candy.|

例句：
The cake was incredibly dulce.
蛋糕甜得不可思议。

The bakery sells a variety of dulces.
这家面包店出售各种甜食。

近义词：sweet, sugary, candied|
反义词：bitter, sour, savory|
词性: adjective, noun|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dulcis*, from Proto-Indo-European *dl̥kʷis* (sweet). Cognates include Greek γλυκύς (glykýs), Sanskrit धृत्वी (dhṛtvī), and Old English *sōfte*.
