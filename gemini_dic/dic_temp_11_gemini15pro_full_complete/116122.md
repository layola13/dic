marvels:/ˈmɑːrvəlz/ /ˈmɑːrvlz/| n.|奇迹；令人惊奇的事物；奇观|Something that causes wonder; an astonishing person or thing.  A wonderful or astonishing person or thing.|例句：The Grand Canyon is one of the natural marvels of the world. 大峡谷是世界自然奇观之一。

例句：The new bridge is an engineering marvel. 这座新桥是一项工程奇迹。|近义词：wonders, miracles, prodigies|反义词：normalities, commonalities|词性:名词(plural)

marvel:/ˈmɑːrvəl/ /ˈmɑːrvl/| n. vi.|奇迹；令人惊奇的事物；奇观；感到惊奇|Something that causes wonder; an astonishing person or thing. To be filled with wonder or astonishment.|例句：It's a marvel that he survived the crash. 他在车祸中幸存下来真是个奇迹。

例句：I marvel at her strength and resilience. 我惊叹于她的力量和韧性。|近义词：wonder, miracle, prodigy|反义词：normality, commonality|词性:名词/动词

词源: From Old French *merveille*  (Modern French merveille), from Vulgar Latin *miribilia*, neuter plural of *mirabilis*  "wonderful," from Latin *mirari* "to wonder at."
