dourer: /ˈdaʊərər/ | n. | 皱眉者；苦瓜脸的人 |  A person who habitually frowns or wears a sullen expression. | He's such a dourer; he never seems to smile. 他真是个苦瓜脸，好像从来不笑。| 近义词：grouch, curmudgeon, pessimist | 反义词：optimist, cheerful person | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From the adjective "dour" + "-er" (suffix forming agent nouns). "Dour" comes from the Scots word "dour," ultimately from Latin "durus" meaning "hard."
