brimful:/ˈbrɪmfʊl/| adj.|满怀的|Full of a particular quality or emotion.|She was brimful of enthusiasm for the project. 她对这个项目充满了热情。|Synonyms: full of, filled with, brimming with|Antonyms: lacking, devoid of, empty of|adjective
