citizens:/ˈsɪtɪzənz/| n.|公民；市民；居民|A legally recognized subject or national of a state or commonwealth, either native or naturalized.|例句：The citizens of the country have the right to vote. 这个国家的公民有投票权。|近义词：inhabitants, residents, nationals|反义词:aliens, foreigners|noun

citizens:/ˈsɪtɪzənz/| n.|公民；市民；居民|Members of a particular city or town.|例句：The citizens of London are proud of their city. 伦敦市民为他们的城市感到骄傲。|近义词：townspeople, townsfolk|反义词:N/A|noun
