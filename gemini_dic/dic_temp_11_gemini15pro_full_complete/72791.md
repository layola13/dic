foist:/fɔɪst/ | vt. | 欺骗；强加；硬塞给 | to force something unwanted onto someone |  He foisted his unwanted opinions on everyone. 他把他不受欢迎的意见强加于每个人。|  The salesman foisted a damaged car on the unsuspecting customer.  推销员把一辆破损的汽车硬塞给了毫无戒心的顾客。 | synonyms: impose, inflict, palm off, pass off | antonyms:  accept, request, welcome | verb

foisted:/ˈfɔɪstɪd/ | v.(foist的过去式和过去分词) | 欺骗；强加；硬塞给 |  The outdated equipment was foisted on the new branch. 过时的设备被强加给了新的分公司。 | The extra work was foisted on me. 额外的工作强加给了我。 |  synonyms: imposed, inflicted, palmed off, passed off | antonyms: accepted, requested, welcomed | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From obsolete French *foister "insert a false die," from Old French fausser "falsify, forge, counterfeit," from Late Latin falsare "to falsify," from Latin falsus "false."
