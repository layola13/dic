leggier:/ˈlɛɡɪər/ | adj. | 更轻快的，更苗条的，更容易的 | Having longer or more slender legs; more agile or nimble; less burdensome or demanding. |  Her new hairstyle made her look leggier.  她的新发型使她看起来腿更修长了。 | The climb up the second hill was leggier than the first.  爬第二座山比第一座山更容易些。 | Near synonyms: slimmer, slenderer, more graceful, easier, simpler | Near antonyms: heavier, thicker, more difficult, more cumbersome | adjective

leggier:/ˈlɛɡɪər/ | adj. | （通常指表演）衣着暴露较多的 | (especially of a show) characterized by scanty costumes.  | The showgirl's costume was definitely leggier than last year's.  那位歌舞女郎的服装肯定比去年的暴露得多。| Near synonyms: skimpier, more revealing | Near antonyms: more modest, more concealing | adjective
