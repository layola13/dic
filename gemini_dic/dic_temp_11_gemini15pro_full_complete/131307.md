noncertified:/ˌnɑːnˈsɜːrtɪfaɪd/
adjective
未经认证的，未经证明的
not having received a certificate of qualification or proficiency.
The software is noncertified and may not meet industry standards.  这个软件未经认证，可能不符合行业标准。
The technician was noncertified and therefore unable to perform the repair.  该技术员未经认证，因此无法进行维修。
synonyms: uncertified, unlicensed, unaccredited, unauthorized
antonyms: certified, licensed, accredited, authorized
词性: adjective
