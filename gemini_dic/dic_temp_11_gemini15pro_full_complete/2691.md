adrogate:/ˈædrəɡeɪt/| vt.|擅取；冒称；僭称|to claim or take something that is not rightfully yours, especially a position or title; to adopt (a person) into one's family.|He adrogated the leadership of the group after the former leader resigned.|近义词：usurp, appropriate, assume, adopt|反义词：relinquish, renounce|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adrogare* (to ask or propose (a law) to the people; to adopt), from *ad-* (to) + *rogare* (to ask).
