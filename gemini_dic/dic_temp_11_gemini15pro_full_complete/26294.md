butylene:/ˈbjuːtɪliːn/ /ˈbjuːtəlˌin/
noun
丁烯
An unsaturated hydrocarbon, C4H8, obtained from petroleum and used in the manufacture of synthetic rubber.
Butylene is a key ingredient in the production of butadiene, which is used to make synthetic rubber.  丁烯是生产丁二烯的关键成分，丁二烯用于制造合成橡胶。
Butylene is also used in the production of various plastics. 丁烯也用于生产各种塑料。
