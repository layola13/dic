词源:
From Middle French *eminence (unattested), from Latin eminentia (“a standing out, projection”), from eminent- (“standing out”), present participle of eminēre, from ex- (“out”) + -minēre, related to mons (“mountain”).

注意事项:
eminency 虽然可以指高地，但这个用法在现代英语中已经过时，主要用于文学或古语中。 通常情况下，eminency 指的是地位或成就的显赫。
