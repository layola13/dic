amphibolous:/æmˈfɪbələs/
| adj. |模棱两可的，含糊不清的|Open to more than one interpretation; having double meaning.|The oracle's amphibolous prophecy could be interpreted in two completely different ways.|近义词：ambiguous, equivocal, unclear|反义词：clear, unambiguous, unequivocal|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *amphibolus*, from Greek *amphíbolos* "ambiguous," from *amphí* "on both sides" + *bállō* "I throw, cast."
