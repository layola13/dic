denudes:/dɪˈnjuːdz/ /dɪˈnuːdz/| v. |脱去，使裸露，剥夺|to strip (something) of its covering, possessions, or assets; to make bare. | The flood denuded the land of its topsoil. 洪水冲走了土地的表层土壤。| The invading army denuded the city of its treasures. 入侵的军队掠夺了城市中的宝藏。 | synonyms: strip, bare, divest, deprive | antonyms: cover, clothe, enrich | verb

denude:/dɪˈnjuːdz/ /dɪˈnuːdz/| n. |（罕见）裸露，剥夺的状态| (rare) The act of denuding; the state of being denuded. | The denude of the forest floor after the fire was shocking. 大火过后森林地面的裸露景象令人震惊。 |  | synonyms: barrenness, desolation | antonyms: covering, abundance | noun (rare usage)
