ilixanthin:/ɪlɪˈzænθɪn/ | n. |  伊利黄素 | A carotenoid pigment found in certain berries, particularly holly berries, giving them their characteristic red color. | The bright red color of holly berries is due to the presence of ilixanthin. 冬青果鲜艳的红色是由于存在伊利黄素。| synonym: carotenoid | antonym: N/A | noun
