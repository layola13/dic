antistimulations:/ˌæntiˌstɪmjʊˈleɪʃənz/| n.|抗刺激，反刺激|The process of reducing or suppressing stimulation, or the measures taken to achieve this. It can refer to reducing external stimuli like noise and light, or internal stimuli like anxiety and cravings.| The doctor recommended antistimulations techniques for managing his chronic pain. 医生建议采用抗刺激技术来控制他的慢性疼痛。|Near synonyms: desensitization, inhibition, suppression|Antonyms: stimulation, excitation|Noun
