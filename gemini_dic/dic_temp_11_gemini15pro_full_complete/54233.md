disengage:/dɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/
v.
脱离，解开，退出
to separate or release (something) from something else that it is connected to; to become separated or released
The soldiers disengaged from the enemy. 士兵们脱离了敌人。
He disengaged the clutch and shifted gears. 他松开离合器并换挡。
detach, disconnect, withdraw
engage, connect, attach
verb
