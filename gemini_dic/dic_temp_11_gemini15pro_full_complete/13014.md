astoundingly:/əˈstaʊndɪŋli/
adverb
令人震惊地，惊人地
extremely surprisingly
The results were astoundingly good. 结果好得惊人。
The scenery was astoundingly beautiful. 景色美得令人震惊。
Synonyms: surprisingly, amazingly, shockingly, incredibly, remarkably
Antonyms: ordinarily, commonly, unexceptionally, typically
词性: 副词
