disconfirmatory:/ˌdɪskənˈfɜːrmətɔːri/
adjective
不证实的，反证的
Serving to disprove or invalidate (a hypothesis, theory, or belief).
The experiment yielded disconfirmatory results, challenging the prevailing theory.  这个实验产生了反证结果，挑战了盛行的理论。
The evidence was disconfirmatory to their hypothesis.  这个证据反驳了他们的假设。
近义词:contradictory, refuting, invalidating
反义词:confirmatory, validating, supporting
词性:adjective
