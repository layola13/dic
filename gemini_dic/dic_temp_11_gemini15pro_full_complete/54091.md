disembed:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbed/
verb
取出，拔出; 挖出
To remove something from where it is embedded or fixed.
The archaeologists carefully disembedded the ancient pottery shards from the earth. 考古学家小心翼翼地从泥土中挖出古代陶器碎片。
The surgeon had to disembed the splinter from the patient's finger.  外科医生不得不把病人手指上的碎片取出来。
extricate, extract, remove
embed, insert, implant
