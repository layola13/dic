feloniousness:/fəˈloʊniəsnəs/
|n.| 邪恶; 重罪性 |The feloniousness of the crime shocked the community.  这起罪行的邪恶程度震惊了整个社区。|The judge commented on the sheer feloniousness of the act. 法官评论了该行为的纯粹邪恶性。|近义词：criminality, wickedness, evil, depravity|反义词：goodness, righteousness, virtue|词性: noun

Etymology:
From felonious + -ness.  Felonious comes from Anglo-French felun, felon "evil-doer, scoundrel," from Medieval Latin fellonem (nominative fello) "traitor, villain, cruel person," of uncertain origin, perhaps from Frankish *fillo "rogue, knave."
