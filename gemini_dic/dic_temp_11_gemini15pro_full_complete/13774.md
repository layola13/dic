audiences:/ˈɔːdiənsɪz/
n.
观众，听众
A group of people who have gathered to watch or listen to something (a play, concert, somebody speaking, etc.)
The singer captivated the audience with her powerful voice. 歌手用她浑厚的嗓音迷住了观众。
