godliest:/ˈɡɒdliɪst/| adj.|最虔诚的，最圣洁的|Superlative form of godly: most godly.  Characterized by piety or devoutness; most reverent or religious.|例句：He was considered the godliest man in the village. 他被认为是村里最虔诚的人。

例句：She lived a godliest life, devoted to prayer and charity. 她过着最虔诚的生活，致力于祈祷和慈善。|近义词：holiest, most devout, most pious, most religious|反义词：wickedest, most sinful, most ungodly|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *gōdlīc (attested only as gōdlīce "piously"), from gōd "good" + -līc "-ly."  The superlative suffix -est is a common Germanic superlative ending.
