clankingnesses:/ˈklæŋkɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
叮当声，铿锵声（通常指金属碰撞的声音的复数形式）
The repeated clankingnesses from the factory kept me awake all night.
工厂反复的叮当声让我整夜无法入睡。
The clankingnesses of the chains added to the eerie atmosphere of the dungeon.
锁链的叮当声增加了地牢的恐怖气氛。
近义词:clangs, rattles, jangles
反义词:silences, quietness
词性:noun

Etymology: From "clank" (verb) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ness" (noun suffix forming abstract nouns of quality or state) + "-es" (plural suffix).  The word "clank" itself likely originates from imitative origin, echoing the sound it describes.
