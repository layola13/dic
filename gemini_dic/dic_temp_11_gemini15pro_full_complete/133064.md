nonequably:/nɒnˈiːkwəbli/
| adv. |不均匀地，不平衡地|Not equally; in an uneven or unbalanced manner.  The paint was applied nonequably, resulting in a patchy finish.|例句：The resources were distributed nonequably among the different departments. 资源在不同部门之间分配不均匀。

例句：The heat was nonequably distributed throughout the room, with some areas much warmer than others.  房间里的热量分布不均匀，有些区域比其他区域暖和得多。|近义词：unevenly, disproportionately, irregularly|反义词:equally, evenly, uniformly
|词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + equably (evenly).  "Equably" comes from the Latin "aequabilis," meaning "equal, uniform, consistent," from "aequus" (equal).
