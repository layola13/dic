baptise:/bæpˈtaɪz/ | v. | 受洗；施洗 | to perform the religious rite of baptism |  They baptised their baby daughter Sarah. 他们给他们的宝贝女儿莎拉施洗。| 近义词: christen | 反义词:  | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French baptiser, from Late Latin baptizare, from Greek baptizein "to immerse, dip, wash," from baptos "dipped, dyed."
