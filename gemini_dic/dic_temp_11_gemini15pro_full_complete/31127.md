ETYMOLOGY->
From "chancellor" + "-ship."  "Chancellor" comes from Old French "chancelier," ultimately from Late Latin "cancellarius" (a court usher), from "cancelli" (latticework, barrier). The suffix "-ship" forms abstract nouns denoting state, condition, office, or skill.
