deprivers:/dɪˈpraɪvərz/| n.|剥夺者，使人丧失者|People who deprive others of something are called deprivers.  那些剥夺他人事物的人被称为剥夺者。| The deprivers took away their basic rights. 剥夺者夺走了他们的基本权利。|  synonyms: dispossessors, despoilers| antonyms: providers, givers|noun

deprive:/dɪˈpraɪv/| v.|剥夺，使丧失|The new law will deprive us of our freedom. 新法律将剥夺我们的自由。|He was deprived of food and water for three days. 他被剥夺了食物和水三天。|synonyms: dispossess, divest, strip|antonyms: provide, give, endow|verb
