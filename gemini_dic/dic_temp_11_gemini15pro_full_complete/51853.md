dextrality:/dɛkˈstrælɪti/
n.
right-handedness; the dominance of the right hand
The prevalence of dextrality in humans is a fascinating area of study.  人类右撇子的普遍存在是一个引人入胜的研究领域。
His dextrality made it difficult for him to use left-handed tools. 他的右撇子习惯让他很难使用左手工具。
Synonym: right-handedness
Antonym: left-handedness, sinistrality
