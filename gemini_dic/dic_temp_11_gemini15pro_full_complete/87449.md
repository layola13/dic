hesitancy:/ˈhezɪtənsi/ (US), /ˈhezɪtənsi/ (UK)| n.|犹豫，迟疑|The state of being hesitant; reluctance.|There was a slight hesitancy in her voice. 她的声音略带迟疑。|Synonyms: hesitation, indecision, reluctance, doubt|Antonyms: decisiveness, confidence, certainty|noun
