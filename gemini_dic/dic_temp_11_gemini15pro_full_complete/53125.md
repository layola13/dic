diplogenic:/ˌdɪploʊˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj.|双基因的，由两个基因产生的|Of or relating to the production of a double or diploid amount of genetic material.|Diplogenic inheritance is a complex topic in genetics. 双基因遗传是遗传学中的一个复杂课题。|Example: Diplogenic mutations can contribute to various diseases.  双基因突变会导致各种疾病。|Synonym: diploid, biallelic|Antonym: haploid, monoallelic|adjective
