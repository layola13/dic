irreplaceable:/ˌɪrɪˈpleɪsəbl/ | adj. | 不可替代的，无法取代的 | Not able to be replaced; too valuable or special to be replaced. |  This antique vase is irreplaceable.  这个古董花瓶是不可替代的。

|  A good mother is irreplaceable.  一位好母亲是无法取代的。

| 近义词: essential, invaluable, crucial, vital, indispensable | 反义词: replaceable, dispensable, expendable | 词性: adjective


词源:

From in- (not) + replaceable.  "Replaceable" comes from the French "remplacer" meaning "to replace," ultimately from Latin "re-" (again) + "placere" (to please).
