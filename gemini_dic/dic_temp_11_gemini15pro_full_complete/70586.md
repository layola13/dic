filliped:/ˈfɪlɪpt/ | v. |轻击，轻弹，轻拂 | To strike or tap lightly. | He filliped the dust off his jacket. 他轻弹掉夹克上的灰尘。 |  She filliped her fingers against the table. 她用手指轻轻敲击桌子。 | tap, flick, flip | strike, hit | verb

Etymology:
Likely a blend of "flick" and "flip," both suggesting a light, quick movement.
