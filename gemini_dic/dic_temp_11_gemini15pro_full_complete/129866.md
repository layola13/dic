non-inflammable:/ˌnɒnɪnˈflæməbl/ | adj. |不易燃的，非易燃的|Not easily set on fire; not flammable. Although seemingly contradictory, "non-inflammable" means the same as "nonflammable."  The "in-" prefix can sometimes intensify meaning (as in invaluable), but in this case leads to confusion.  "Nonflammable" is the preferred term. |This material is non-inflammable and safe to use near heat sources. 这种材料不易燃，可在热源附近安全使用。| The costumes are made of non-inflammable material. 这些服装是用不易燃的材料制成的。 |Synonyms: nonflammable, fire-resistant, fireproof |Antonyms: flammable, inflammable, combustible |adjective
