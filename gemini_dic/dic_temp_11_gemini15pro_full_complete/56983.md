double-crosser:/ˈdʌblˌkrɔsər/
noun|双面间谍，背叛者，出卖朋友的人|A person who deceives or betrays someone, especially someone who trusts them.|He promised to help me, but he turned out to be a double-crosser.|Synonyms: traitor, betrayer, backstabber|Antonyms: loyalist, friend, ally|n.

double-cross:/ˌdʌbl ˈkrɔs/
verb|欺骗，背叛，出卖|To deceive or betray someone, especially someone who trusts you.|She double-crossed him by revealing his secret to the police.|Synonyms: betray, deceive, cheat|Antonyms: support, help, assist|v.
