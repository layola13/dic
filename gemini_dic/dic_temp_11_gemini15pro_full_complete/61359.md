embezzle:/ɪmˈbezəl/| vt.| 盗用，挪用，贪污 | To illegally take money that you are responsible for, often from a company or organization. | The accountant was caught embezzling funds from the company. 会计被发现挪用公司资金。

He embezzled millions of dollars from his clients. 他从客户那里盗用了数百万美元。| 近义词: misappropriate, pilfer, defraud, steal | 反义词: donate, contribute | verb
