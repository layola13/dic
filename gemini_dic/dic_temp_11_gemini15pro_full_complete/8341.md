anti-platonic:/ˌænti pləˈtɑːnɪk/ | adj. | 反柏拉图式的，非柏拉图式的 | Opposed to or rejecting Platonic ideals, especially in the sense of rejecting a purely spiritual or non-sexual relationship.  It describes a relationship that is not based on the philosophical ideals of Plato, and often implies a relationship that is romantic or sexual. |  Example 1: Their relationship was decidedly anti-platonic, filled with passionate embraces and whispered secrets.  他们的关系显然不是柏拉图式的，充满了热情的拥抱和窃窃私语。

Example 2:  Despite their insistence that their bond was purely platonic, their constant flirting suggested an anti-platonic undercurrent. 尽管他们坚称他们的关系纯粹是柏拉图式的，但他们不断的调情暗示着一种非柏拉图式的潜流。 | 近义词：romantic, intimate, passionate | 反义词：platonic, chaste, non-sexual | 词性: adjective
