committal:/kəˈmɪtl/ /kəˈmɪtəl/| n. |承诺，保证；收监，监禁；委托，托付|An act of committing someone or something to a particular course of action, place, or condition.  The state of being committed to a course of action or to a person or institution.|The judge ordered the committal of the defendant to prison. 法官下令将被告收监。|近义词：consignment, commitment, pledge|反义词：release, freedom|noun

committal:/kəˈmɪtl/ /kəˈmɪtəl/| adj. |投入的，坚定的；需要承担义务的|Characterized by commitment. Involving obligation or commitment.|She had a committal expression on her face. 她脸上带着坚定的表情。|近义词：dedicated, devoted, committed|反义词：noncommittal, uncommitted|adjective
