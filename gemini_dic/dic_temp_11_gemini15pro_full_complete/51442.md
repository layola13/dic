dethrone:/diˈθroʊn/
verb
废黜，罢免
To remove a king or queen from power; to remove someone from a position of authority or dominance.
The king was dethroned and exiled. 国王被废黜并流放了。
The champion was dethroned by the young challenger.  冠军被年轻的挑战者击败了。
depose, oust, unseat
crown, enthrone
