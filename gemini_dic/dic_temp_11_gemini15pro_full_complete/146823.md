overimpress: /ˌoʊvərɪmˈprɛs/ | vt. | 过分留下印象；使过分钦佩 | To impress excessively; to create an exaggeratedly favorable impression. | He overimpressed the interviewers with his elaborate answers, making them suspicious. 他用精心准备的答案给面试官留下了过分的印象，反而让他们起了疑心。

The marketing campaign overimpressed potential customers, leading them to believe the product was too good to be true.  市场营销活动给潜在客户留下了过分的印象，让他们觉得产品好得令人难以置信。

| 近义词: overwhelm, overawe | 反义词: underwhelm, disappoint | 词性: 动词
