articulatorily:/ɑːrˌtɪkjʊˈleɪtərɪli/
| adv. |清晰地，表达流畅地|In a manner characterized by clear and distinct articulation; expressively.  It describes the way something is expressed, particularly speech, emphasizing clarity and fluency.|She spoke articulatorily, carefully enunciating each syllable. 她说话清晰流畅，每个音节都发音清晰。|Synonyms: eloquently, fluently, clearly, distinctly, expressively|Antonyms: inarticulately, unclearly, vaguely, mumblingly|词性:副词
