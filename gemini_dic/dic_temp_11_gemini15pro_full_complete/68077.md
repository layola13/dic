faddists:/ˈfædɪsts/
n.
时尚追随者；赶时髦的人
People who enthusiastically follow fads, often short-lived trends or crazes.
Faddists are always chasing the next big thing, whether it's a diet, a fashion trend, or a new gadget.  时尚追随者总是追逐下一个热门事物，无论是节食、时尚潮流还是新玩意。

The faddists quickly abandoned the platform shoes once they went out of style.  一旦松糕鞋过时了，那些赶时髦的人就很快放弃了它们。


近义词:trendsetters, fashionistas, followers
反义词:traditionalists, conservatives
词性:noun
