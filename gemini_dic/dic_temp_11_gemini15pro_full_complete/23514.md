braillists:/ˈbreɪlɪsts/
noun
盲文书写者；精通盲文的人
People who are skilled in reading and writing Braille.
例句：The braillists transcribed the novel into Braille. 盲文书写者将小说转录成盲文。

例句：Braillists are essential for making information accessible to the visually impaired. 盲文书写者对于使视障人士能够获取信息至关重要。


近义词：Braille readers, Braille writers, Braille transcribers
反义词:  (No direct antonym; perhaps "sighted readers/writers")
词性:noun
