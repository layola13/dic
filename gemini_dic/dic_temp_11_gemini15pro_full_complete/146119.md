overemotionally: /ˌoʊvərɪˈmoʊʃənəli/ | adv. | 过于情绪化地，感情过度地 | In a way that shows too much emotion. | She reacted overemotionally to the news. 她对这个消息反应过度情绪化。| 近义词: excessively emotionally, hysterically, dramatically | 反义词: calmly, rationally, composedly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) + "emotional" (from "emotion" -  late Middle English: from Old French, or from medieval Latin emotiōn-, from Latin ēmovēre ‘move out, remove, excite’)+ "-ly" (Old English -līce, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch -lijk and German -lich)
