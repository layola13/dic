Simple Present:
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? 他做什么工作？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? 她晚饭在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned recently? 你最近学了什么？
What has he done to his hair? 他对他的头发做了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been reading lately? 你最近一直在读什么？
What has she been working on all day? 她一整天都在忙什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say to you? 她对你说了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? 她参加聚会穿了什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you left? 你离开之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I arrived? 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  她换专业之前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after you graduate? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will she buy with the money? 她要用这笔钱买什么？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be studying next semester? 她下学期会学什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have finished by the end of the day? 你到今天结束前会完成什么？
What will she have accomplished by the time she retires? 她退休时会取得什么成就？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she gets her PhD? 她拿到博士学位时将已经学习什么多久了？
