belayer:/bɪˈleɪər/| n.|保护员，确保者|A person who controls the rope for a climber, ensuring their safety in case of a fall.  The belayer uses a belay device to create friction on the rope and stop the climber's fall.|The experienced belayer kept a close watch on the climber.  经验丰富的保护员密切注视着攀登者。|近义词：protector, safeguarder|反义词: None|Noun

belay:/bɪˈleɪ/| v.|保护，确保；停止，固定|1. To secure a climber by controlling the rope attached to them.  2. (Nautical) To secure (a rope) by winding it round a cleat or pin. 3. To stop or cease.|1.  She belayed her partner as he climbed the difficult route.  她在她同伴攀登艰难路线时为他保护。 2. The sailor belayed the rope to the mast. 水手把绳子固定在桅杆上。 3.  Belay that order!  停止执行那项命令！|近义词：secure, fasten, stop, cease|反义词: release, loosen|Verb
