broad:/brɔːd/ /brɑːd/| adj.|宽阔的，广阔的；广泛的；明显的；大概的|Wide, extensive; covering a large number of subjects or areas; obvious; general, not detailed.|The river is very broad at this point. 这条河在这里很宽。|近义词：wide, extensive, vast, comprehensive|反义词：narrow, limited, restricted|adjective

broad:/brɔːd/ /brɑːd/| n.|女人，妓女 (俚语，冒犯)|A woman, often derogatory or offensive slang.|That broad over there looks familiar.  那边那个女人看起来很眼熟。(俚语，冒犯)|近义词：woman (正式), lady (正式), gal (非正式), chick (俚语)|反义词：man (性别对立)|noun
