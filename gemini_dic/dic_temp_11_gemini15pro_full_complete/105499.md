kilobit:/ˈkɪləbɪt/ /ˈkɪləʊbɪt/| n. |千比特|A unit of information equal to 1,000 bits or 125 bytes.  It is often used to measure data transmission speeds. |The file downloaded at a speed of 50 kilobits per second. |该文件以每秒 50 千比特的速度下载。|Synonyms: kb, kbit|Antonyms: megabit, gigabit|noun
