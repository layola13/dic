defamer:/dɪˈfeɪmər/| n.|诽谤者，中伤者|A person who attacks the reputation of another by slander or libel.  Someone who harms another's reputation by spreading false and malicious statements.|The defamer was sued for damaging the actor's career.  这个诽谤者因损害演员的职业生涯而被起诉。|近义词：slanderer, libeler, calumniator|反义词：supporter, advocate|词性:noun

defame:/dɪˈfeɪm/| vt.|诽谤，中伤|To harm the reputation of by false or malicious statements. To attack the good name of someone.|The tabloid defamed the politician with unfounded accusations.  这家小报用毫无根据的指控诽谤了这位政治家。|近义词：slander, libel, malign, vilify|反义词：praise, commend, extol|词性:verb
