disorient:/dɪsˈɔːriənt/ /dɪsˈɔrˌiɛnt/| vt. |使迷失方向；使不知所措|To cause to lose one's sense of direction, position, or relationship with one's surroundings; to confuse.|The flashing lights disoriented me. 闪爍的灯光使我迷失了方向。|Synonyms: confuse, bewilder, perplex|Antonyms: orient, guide, clarify|Verb
