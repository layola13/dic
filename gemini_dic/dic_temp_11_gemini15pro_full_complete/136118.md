nonreductions:/ˌnɒnrɪˈdʌkʃənz/ | n. | 非减少，不减少 | Absence of reductions; instances of not reducing something.  This word typically refers to situations where something is *not* reduced in size, quantity, price, or complexity. | Example: The nonreductions in staff led to increased workload for everyone.  员工人数没有减少导致每个人的工作量都增加了。

| Example:  The policy of nonreductions in benefits was welcomed by the employees. 不减少福利的政策受到了员工的欢迎。 | Near synonyms:  non-decrease, maintenance,  preservation | Antonyms: reductions, decreases, cuts | noun
