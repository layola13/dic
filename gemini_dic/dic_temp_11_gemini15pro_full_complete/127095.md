nationalises:/ˈnæʃənəlaɪzɪz/
verb
国有化
To transfer (a major branch of industry or commerce) from private to state ownership or control.
The government nationalised the railways. 政府将铁路国有化了。
The new regime swiftly nationalized all private land. 新政权迅速将所有私有土地国有化。
nationalize, expropriate
privatize, denationalize
