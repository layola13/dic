lockdown: /ˈlɒkdaʊn/ (UK) /ˈlɑːkdaʊn/ (US)| v.| 封锁，禁闭|To put a place or community under lockdown.|The government decided to lockdown the entire region. 政府决定封锁整个地区。|近义词：confine, restrict, isolate|反义词: open, free| verb
