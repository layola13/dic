basilicas:/bəˈsɪlɪkəz/| n.|教堂，圣殿|A large oblong hall or building with double colonnades and a semicircular apse, used in ancient Rome as a court of law or for public assemblies. Later it became the architectural model for Christian churches.|例句：St. Peter's Basilica in Vatican City is a magnificent example of Renaissance architecture. 梵蒂冈城的圣彼得大教堂是文艺复兴时期建筑的宏伟典范。

例句：Many ancient basilicas have been converted into churches. 许多古代的巴西利卡已被改建成教堂。|近义词：cathedral, church, temple|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
