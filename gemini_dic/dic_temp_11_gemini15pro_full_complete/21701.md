board's:/bɔːrdz/ |n. |董事会的；委员会的；木板的；（提供膳食的）寄宿处的|It refers to something that belongs to or is associated with a board, which can be a group of people managing an organization, a piece of wood, or a place providing meals and lodging.|The board's decision was final. 董事会的决定是最终的。|Synonyms: council's, committee's, panel's (when referring to a group)|Antonyms: individual's, personal (when referring to ownership)|Determiner/Possessive Pronoun
