all-nighter: /ˌɔːlˈnaɪtər/ | n. | 夜以继日；通宵；通宵活动 | An event or activity that lasts all night, especially one involving studying or working. |  I pulled an all-nighter to finish my essay. 我通宵达旦地完成了我的论文。

| 近义词: late night, overnight session | 反义词:  daytime work, early night | 词性: 名词


all-nighter: /ˌɔːlˈnaɪtər/ | n. | 通宵的人 | A person who stays up all night, especially to study or work. |  He's a regular all-nighter during exam season. 考试期间，他经常通宵学习。


| 近义词: night owl | 反义词:  early bird | 词性: 名词

词源:  Compound formed from "all" and "nighter" (a night), likely influenced by similar terms like "all-dayer."
