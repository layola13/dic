nondeclarative:/ˌnɒndɪˈklærətɪv/ | adj. | 非陈述性的，非声明性的 | Relating to knowledge that cannot be consciously recalled but that manifests itself in behavior.  This type of memory is often expressed through performance rather than recollection. |  Riding a bike is a nondeclarative memory. 骑自行车是一种非陈述性记忆。|  Nondeclarative memory influences our actions without us being aware of it. 非陈述性记忆会在我们不知不觉中影响我们的行为。| synonyms: implicit, procedural, unconscious | antonyms: declarative, explicit, conscious | adjective
