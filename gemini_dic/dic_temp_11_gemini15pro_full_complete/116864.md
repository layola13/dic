maximus:/ˈmæksɪməs/| adj.|最大的，极大的|Greatest; largest; highest.  It derives from the Latin word "magnus" meaning "large" or "great", with the superlative suffix "-imus" added to indicate the highest degree. It's often used in titles or names to signify the greatest example of something. |例句：He achieved maximus success in his career. 他在事业上取得了最大的成功。

|例句：The Roman general was known as Maximus Decimus Meridius.  这位罗马将军被称为马克西姆斯·德西姆斯·梅里迪乌斯。|近义词：greatest, largest, utmost, supreme, paramount|反义词:minimum, smallest, least|词性:adjective
