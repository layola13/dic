admonish:/ədˈmɑnɪʃ/ (US) /ədˈmɒnɪʃ/ (UK)| v.|劝诫；警告；责备|to warn or reprimand someone firmly|例句：The teacher admonished the students for being late. 老师责备学生迟到。|例句：She was admonished for her careless work. 她因工作粗心而受到警告。|近义词：reprimand, rebuke, caution, warn|反义词：praise, commend, encourage|词性:动词
Etymology: From Middle English *amoneschen*, from Old French *amonester*, from Late Latin *admonestāre*, from Latin *admonēre* (“to remind, advise, warn”), from *ad* (“to”) + *monēre* (“to warn”).
