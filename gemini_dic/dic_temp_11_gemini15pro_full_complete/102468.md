jackrabbits:/ˈdʒækræbɪts/| n. |野兔，长耳大野兔|A large hare of western North America, with very long ears and long powerful hind legs, noted for its high leaps.  西部北美的一种大型野兔，耳朵很长，后腿有力，以其高跳跃而闻名。|例句：Jackrabbits are known for their incredible speed.  野兔以其惊人的速度而闻名。

例句：We saw several jackrabbits bounding across the desert. 我们看到几只野兔跳跃着穿过沙漠。|近义词：hare, rabbit|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
