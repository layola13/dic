cantabrians:/kænˈtæbriənz/
noun
坎塔布连人；坎塔布里亚居民
People from Cantabria, an autonomous community in northern Spain.
Many Cantabrians are proud of their region's history and culture. 许多坎塔布连人以他们地区的悠久历史和文化为豪。
inhabitants, residents, locals
outsiders, foreigners
