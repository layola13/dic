intertingeing:/ˌɪntərˈtɪndʒɪŋ/ | adj. |相互混合的，相互影响的 |  Blending together; coloring slightly; affecting reciprocally. | The intertingeing of different cultures created a vibrant new art form. 不同文化的相互交融创造了一种充满活力的新艺术形式。 |  intermingling, blending, fusing, merging | separating, dividing, isolating | adjective

intertinge:/ˌɪntərˈtɪndʒ/ | v. |  使混合，使略带色彩 | To blend together; to tint slightly. | The sunset intertinged the clouds with shades of pink and orange.  日落给云彩染上了粉色和橙色的阴影。| blend, mix, mingle, tinge, colour | separate, divide, isolate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From inter- + tinge.  "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between" or "among." "Tinge" comes from the Old French "teindre," meaning "to dye" or "to color."
