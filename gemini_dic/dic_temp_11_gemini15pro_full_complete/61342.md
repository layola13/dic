embedding:/ɪmˈbedɪŋ/ | n. | (机器学习)嵌入; |A low-dimensional vector representation of a discrete variable, such as a word, sentence, or image, often learned through machine learning techniques. |Word embeddings are used in natural language processing to represent words in a way that captures their semantic meaning. 词嵌入在自然语言处理中用于表示单词，以捕捉它们的语义。|  | representation, vectorization | - | noun
