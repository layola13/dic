barysphere:/ˈbæriˌsfɪr/ | n. | 重圈; 地核 |The barysphere, also known as the core, is the innermost part of Earth. 重圈，也称为地核，是地球最内部的部分。| The Earth's barysphere is primarily composed of iron and nickel. 地球的重圈主要由铁和镍组成。| synonyms: core, centrosphere | antonyms: atmosphere, crust | noun
