nondescriptiveness:/ˌnɒndɪˈskrɪptɪvnəs/ | n. | 无特色；难以描述；平淡无奇 |  The nondescriptiveness of the building made it easy to overlook.  | Example: The nondescriptiveness of the room made it feel impersonal and cold.  房间的毫无特色使它感觉没有人情味，很冷清。| Synonyms: anonymity, ordinariness, blandness, featurelessness | Antonyms: distinctiveness, uniqueness, remarkableness | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + descriptive + -ness.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not," descriptive means "serving to describe," and -ness is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives.
