noyade:/nɔɪˈɑːd/| n.|溺杀，集体溺毙|The noyades were a horrific period in French history.  集体溺毙是法国历史上恐怖的一段时期。|The French Revolution included the horrific noyades in the Loire. 法国大革命时期包括了卢瓦尔河令人发指的集体溺毙事件。|近义词：drowning, massacre, execution|反义词:rescue, saving|词性:noun

Etymology: From French *noyade*, from *noyer* "to drown," from Vulgar Latin *necare* "to kill," from Latin *necare* "to kill, put to death."
