chuggers: /ˈtʃʌɡərz/
n.
慈善募捐者（尤指在街上劝人定期捐款的人）
People who solicit donations for charities, especially on the street, by asking people to sign up for regular donations.
例句：The chuggers were out in force today, trying to get people to sign up for monthly donations. 今天慈善募捐者倾巢出动，试图让人们注册每月捐款。

例句：I try to avoid eye contact with chuggers when I walk down the high street. 当我走在商业街上时，我尽量避免与慈善募捐者眼神接触。

近义词：fundraisers, solicitors, canvassers
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "benefactor" or "donor")
