intervarsity:/ˌɪntərˈvɑːrsɪti/ | adj. |校际的，大学间的| Existing or occurring between universities. |  Example: The intervarsity debate competition was fiercely contested. 校际辩论赛竞争激烈。

Example:  She participated in the intervarsity rowing championships. 她参加了校际划船锦标赛。| 近义词: intercollegiate | 反义词: intravarsity (within a university) | 词性: adjective
