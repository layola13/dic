impressionism:/ɪmˈprɛʃənɪzəm/| n.|印象主义，印象派|A style of painting developed in France in the late 19th century, characterized by short brushstrokes and an emphasis on capturing the fleeting impression of a scene rather than precise detail. It also refers to a similar style in other arts, such as music and literature.|The museum has a wonderful collection of French Impressionism.|近义词：post-impressionism|反义词:realism|noun
