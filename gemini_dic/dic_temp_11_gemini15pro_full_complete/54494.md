disinvest:/ˌdɪsɪnˈvɛst/ | v. |撤资，减少投资| To withdraw investment from (something).  To reduce or stop investing in (something).| The company decided to disinvest from the failing project.  该公司决定从失败的项目中撤资。

The government is disinvesting in public services. 政府正在减少对公共服务的投资。|Synonyms: divest, withdraw, pull out| Antonyms: invest, fund, finance| Verb
