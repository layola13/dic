debian:/ˈdɛbiən/ | n. | Debian | A popular distribution of Linux, known for its adherence to free software principles and its large software repository.| I'm running Debian on my server. 我在我的服务器上运行 Debian。 | 近义词: Linux distribution, Ubuntu, Fedora | 反义词: Windows, macOS | 词性: 名词

词源:  A portmanteau of the names Debra (Ian Murdock's then-girlfriend, later wife) and Ian (Murdock himself).
