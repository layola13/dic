immorigerous:/ˌɪmɒˈrɪdʒərəs/
adjective
不守规矩的，放荡的
Lacking in restraint or discipline; unruly.
The immorigerous child refused to listen to his parents.  这个不守规矩的孩子拒绝听父母的话。
The teacher struggled to control the immorigerous class. 老师努力控制这个放荡不羁的班级。
Synonyms: unruly, undisciplined, wild, rambunctious, uncontrolled, insubordinate
Antonyms: obedient, disciplined, well-behaved, compliant, docile
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From Latin *immoriger (not obedient) < in- (not) + moriger (obedient) < mos (custom, manner) + -ger (bearing).
