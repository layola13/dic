hull-less:/ˈhʌl.ləs/
adjective
无壳的，无苞的
Lacking a hull, husk, or outer covering.
The farmer developed a new strain of hull-less barley.  这位农民开发了一种新的无壳大麦品种。
The hull-less pumpkin seeds are easier to eat.  这种无壳南瓜子更容易食用。
近义词: shell-less, huskless
反义词: hulled, shelled
词性: adjective

词源:
From "hull" (meaning outer covering) + "-less" (suffix indicating absence). "Hull" comes from Old Norse "hul" meaning "covering."
