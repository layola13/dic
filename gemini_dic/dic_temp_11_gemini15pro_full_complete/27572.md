canalization:/ˌkænəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
引导，限制，约束
The process of restricting or limiting development to a particular path or direction.
例句：Canalization ensures that development proceeds along predictable pathways despite environmental perturbations. 引导确保了尽管环境扰动，发育仍沿着可预测的路径进行。

例句：Genetic canalization buffers against the effects of mutations. 遗传引导缓冲了突变的影响。
近义词：restriction, limitation, direction, channeling, constraint
反义词：diversification, expansion, broadening
词性: noun


词源:  From the Latin "canalis" (channel, groove) + "-ize" (to make) + "-ing" (present participle).
