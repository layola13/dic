clayier:/ˈkleɪ.i.ər/
adjective
更黏土的；黏土含量更高的
Containing more clay; having a greater proportion of clay.
The soil in this area is clayier than in the other field.  这个地区的土壤比另一块田地里的土壤黏土含量更高。
The further down you dig, the clayier the soil becomes. 你挖得越深，土壤的黏土含量就越高。
近义词：denser, heavier, stickier
反义词：sandier, looser, lighter
词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From clay + -ier (comparative suffix).  Clay comes from Old English *clæg.  The suffix -ier derives from Middle English -ier, -ir, from Old French -ier, ultimately from Latin -ārius.
