med's:/mɛdz/
noun
药物（复数，非正式）
Short for "medications."  These are the meds the doctor prescribed. 这是医生开的药。
She's on a lot of meds these days. 她最近在服用很多药物。
medications, drugs, pills
(No direct antonym for medication)
n.
