double-faulted:/ˈdʌbəl ˈfɔːltɪd/ | v. | 双误; 发球双误 |  A tennis term. It means hitting two consecutive faults on a serve, resulting in the loss of the point.  The server first misses the service area entirely with their first serve, then repeats the error on their second serve. |  She double-faulted on match point, losing the game.  她在赛点上双误，输掉了比赛。 | Synonyms: faulted (twice), served two faults | Antonyms: aced, served an ace | verb

double-faulted:/ˈdʌbəl ˈfɔːltɪd/ | adj. |  双误的 | Describes a serve in tennis where two faults have been committed. |  He lost the game on a double-faulted serve. 他在一记双误的发球上输掉了比赛。 | Synonyms: failed serve | Antonyms: successful serve | adjective
