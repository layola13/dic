ammonal:/ˈæməˌnɑːl/
noun
铵炸药
An explosive made of ammonium nitrate, powdered aluminium, and TNT.
Ammonal was widely used in World War I. 铵炸药在第一次世界大战中被广泛使用。
Synonyms: explosive, blasting agent
