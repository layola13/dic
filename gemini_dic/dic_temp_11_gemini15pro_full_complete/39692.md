consideration:/kənˌsɪdəˈreɪʃn/| n.|考虑；体贴；报酬；重要性|Careful thought, typically over a period of time. Something taken into account.|例句：After careful consideration, he decided to accept the offer.  经过仔细考虑后，他决定接受这个提议。

例句：Safety is a primary consideration in the design of any aircraft. 安全是任何飞机设计中的首要考虑因素。|近义词：thought, deliberation, reflection, contemplation, attention, regard, concern, solicitude, care, heed, notice, respect, courtesy, kindness|反义词：disregard, inconsideration|词性:名词
