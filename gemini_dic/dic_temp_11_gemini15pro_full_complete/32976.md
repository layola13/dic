choree:/ˈkɔːriː/ /ˈkɔːreɪ/
noun
舞蹈动作; 舞蹈短句; 舞蹈组合
A choreographic sequence, a short dance, or a dance figure.
The ballet featured a series of intricate chorees.  这个芭蕾舞剧以一系列复杂的舞蹈动作/短句为特色。
The choreographer taught the dancers a new choree.  编舞老师教了舞者们一个新的舞蹈组合。
近义词:step, figure, movement, phrase
反义词: (No direct antonym for a choreographic unit)
