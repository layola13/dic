coveting: /ˈkʌvɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 贪恋，觊觎 | The act of desiring something that belongs to someone else. |  She couldn't help coveting her neighbor's beautiful garden. 她忍不住觊觎邻居漂亮的花园。 | Synonyms: desiring, yearning, craving, envying | Antonyms:  disinterested, indifferent | noun

coveting: /ˈkʌvɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 贪婪的，渴望的 |  Marked by a strong desire to possess something, especially something that belongs to another. |  He cast a coveting glance at his friend's new sports car. 他贪婪地瞥了一眼他朋友的新跑车。| Synonyms: desirous, greedy, envious | Antonyms: content, satisfied | adjective
