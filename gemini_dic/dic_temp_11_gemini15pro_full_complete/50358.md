deponent:/dɪˈpəʊnənt/
n.|证人；宣誓作证者；陈述者|A person who makes a deposition or affidavit under oath. |The deponent testified that she saw the defendant leaving the scene of the crime. 证人作证说她看到被告离开了犯罪现场。|witness, testifier| accuser, defendant|noun
deponent:/dɪˈpəʊnənt/
adj.|（语法）被动语态的|Having a passive form but an active meaning. |Latin verbs like "conor" are deponent verbs. 像“conor”这样的拉丁语动词是被动语态的。|passive|active|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *deponent*, from Latin *dēpōnēns*, present participle of *dēpōnere* (“to lay down, deposit, testify”), from *dē* (“down, away”) + *pōnere* (“to put, place”).
