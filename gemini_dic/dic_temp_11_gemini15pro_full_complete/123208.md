noun
单色，单色画法
The quality or state of being monochromatic; the use of a single color, or variations of a single color, in a work of art.
The artist explored monochromy's expressive potential in a series of charcoal drawings.  这位艺术家在一系列木炭画中探索了单色画的表达潜力。
Near synonyms: monochrome, monochromaticity
