hotaka: /hoʊˈtɑːkɑː/ | n. | 穂高岳 (Hotaka Mountain Range) |  A mountain range in the Japanese Alps, located in Nagano and Gifu Prefectures. It is known for its stunning scenery and challenging hiking trails. | Example:  Hotaka Mountain is a popular destination for climbers. 穂高岳是登山者们的热门目的地。

Example: The Hotaka range offers breathtaking views. 穂高連峰的景色令人叹为观止。 | Synonym: Japanese Alps (partially), North Japan Alps (partially) | Antonym: N/A | noun
