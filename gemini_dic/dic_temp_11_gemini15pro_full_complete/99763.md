intermediary:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdieri/ | n. adj.| 中间人，媒介；中间的 |Someone who acts as a link between two or more people or sides; relating to an intermediate stage or level.| The lawyer acted as an intermediary between the buyer and the seller. 律师在买方和卖方之间充当中间人。| synonyms: mediator, go-between, broker, agent | antonyms: principal, direct | noun, adjective

intermediary:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdieri/ | adj.| 中间的，媒介的 |Being between two other things, levels, or stages.| He enrolled in an intermediary English course. 他报名参加了一个中级英语课程。| synonyms: middle, intermediate, median | antonyms: extreme, ultimate | adjective
