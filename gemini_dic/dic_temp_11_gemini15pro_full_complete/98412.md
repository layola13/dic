insonorous:/ɪnˈsɒnərəs/ /ɪnˈsɑːnərəs/| adj.|无声音的，沉闷的|Not resonant or sonorous; lacking resonance or full, rich sound.|The insonorous voice of the speaker made it difficult to stay awake. 演讲者沉闷的声音让人昏昏欲睡。|近义词：dull, muted, quiet, hushed, subdued|反义词：resonant, sonorous, loud, clear|词性:形容词

词源:
Late 16th century: from Latin in- ‘not’ + sonorus (from sonor ‘sound’) + -ous.
