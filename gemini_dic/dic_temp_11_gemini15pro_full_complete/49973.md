denigrations:/ˌdɛnɪˈɡreɪʃənz/
|n.| 诽谤；诋毁；贬低 |
|The politician's speech was filled with denigrations of his opponent.|这位政治家的演讲充满了对对手的诋毁。|
|The constant denigrations of her work made her feel demoralized.|对她工作的不断贬低让她感到 demoralized。|
|Synonyms: defamation, slander, disparagement, vilification, calumny|
|Antonyms: praise, commendation, acclaim, applause, exaltation|
|noun|
