bibliopolar:/ˌbɪbliəˈpoʊlər/ | n. | 书商；爱书人；藏书家 | A seller of rare or antiquarian books; a person who loves or collects books. |  He was a true bibliopolar, his shelves overflowing with first editions. 他是一位真正的藏书家，他的书架上摆满了初版书籍。| synonym: booklover, book collector, book dealer, bibliophile | antonym: biblioclast (one who destroys books) | noun
