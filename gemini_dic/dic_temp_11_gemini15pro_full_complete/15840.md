ballast: /ˈbæləst/ (US) /ˈbæləst/ (UK)| n. v.|压舱物，镇流器；稳定，使稳定|Heavy material, such as gravel, sand, or iron, placed low in a ship or the bottom of a hot-air balloon to increase stability. Also, anything that gives stability or steadiness.|The ship took on ballast in the harbor.  这艘船在港口装载了压舱物。|Synonyms: counterweight, stabilizer, anchor|Antonyms: instability, volatility|noun

ballast: /ˈbæləst/ (US) /ˈbæləst/ (UK)| v.|给…加压舱物，使稳定|To provide with ballast or something that gives stability.|They ballasted the ship with rocks. 他们用石头给船加压舱物。|Synonyms: stabilize, steady, anchor|Antonyms: destabilize, unsettle|verb
