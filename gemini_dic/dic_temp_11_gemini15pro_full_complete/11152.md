archeress: /ˈɑːrtʃərɪs/ | n. | 女弓箭手 | A female archer.  |  The archeress notched an arrow and took aim. 女弓箭手搭上箭，瞄准目标。 |  The archeress let fly a swift arrow. 女弓箭手射出一支飞快的箭。| 近义词: bowwoman, female archer | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps non-combatant) | noun

词源:

From *archer* + *-ess*, a suffix denoting feminine gender. *Archer* comes from Old French *archier*, from Latin *arcarius*, meaning "bowman," derived from *arcus*, meaning "bow."
