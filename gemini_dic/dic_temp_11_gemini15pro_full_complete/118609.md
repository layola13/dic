messengers:/ˈmesɪndʒərz/
n.
信使，使者，报信者
People carrying messages or information.  They can be people, animals, or even things.
The king sent messengers to all parts of the kingdom. 国王派遣信使到王国各地。
The company uses bicycle messengers to deliver packages.  这家公司用自行车信使递送包裹。
couriers, envoys, heralds
recipients
noun
