nonconsonances:/ˌnɑːnkɑːnsənənsɪz/
|n.|不和谐音；非辅音|A combination of sounds that are not harmonious or do not share a consonant sound.|The piece was full of nonconsonances, making it jarring to the ear. 这首曲子充满了不和谐音，让人听起来很不舒服。|近义词：dissonances, discords|反义词：consonances, harmonies|词性:Noun

nonconsonance:/ˌnɑːnkɑːnsənəns/
|n.|不和谐音；非辅音|Lack of harmony or agreement; a combination of sounds that are not harmonious or do not share a consonant sound.|The nonconsonance between the two instruments was distracting. 这两件乐器之间的不和谐音让人分心。|近义词：discord, dissonance|反义词：consonance, harmony|词性:Noun
