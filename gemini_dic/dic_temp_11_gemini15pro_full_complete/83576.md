haematin:/ˈhiːmətɪn/ /ˈhemətɪn/ | n. |血红素，正铁血红素|A dark bluish or brownish pigment containing iron, derived from oxidized heme.  It is found in old blood stains and is one of the end products of hemoglobin breakdown. |The haematin stained the bandage brown. 血红素把绷带染成了棕色。| 近义词:hemin | 反义词: N/A | noun

haematin:/ˈhiːmətɪn/ /ˈhemətɪn/ | n. |血晶素，高铁血红素|A crystalline form of heme, containing ferric iron. It is less common than hematin and is sometimes considered a synonym. |The analysis revealed traces of haematin crystals. 分析显示有微量血晶素结晶。| 近义词:hemin | 反义词: N/A | noun
