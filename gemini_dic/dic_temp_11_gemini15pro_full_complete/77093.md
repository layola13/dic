garrote:/ɡəˈrɒt/ (UK), /ɡəˈrɑːt/ (US)| n. v.| 绞刑，勒死；金属丝或绳索|A device, typically a length of wire or cord, used for strangulation.  Also, the act of strangling someone with such a device.|The dictator was executed by garrote.  独裁者被绞死了。|Synonyms: strangle, choke, asphyxiate|Antonyms: resuscitate, revive|noun, verb
