nonthematically:/ˌnɒnθəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 非主题地；不按主题地 |  Not related to or organized by a particular theme or subject.  | The books were arranged nonthematically, simply by size. 这些书不是按主题排列的，只是按大小排列的。 | The museum displayed the artifacts nonthematically, simply in the order they were acquired.  博物馆没有按主题陈列文物，只是按照获得的顺序陈列。 | synonym: randomly, haphazardly, unsystematically | antonym: thematically, systematically, topically | adverb
