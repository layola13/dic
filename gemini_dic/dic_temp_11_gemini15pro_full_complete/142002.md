organism:/ˈɔːɡənɪzəm/ | n. |生物体；有机体 |A living thing, such as a plant, animal, or bacterium, that can function independently.|例句：Single-celled organisms are the foundation of life on Earth. 单细胞生物是地球生命的基础。|例句：The human body is a complex organism. 人体是一个复杂的生物体。|近义词：being, creature, life form, entity|反义词: non-living, inanimate | noun
