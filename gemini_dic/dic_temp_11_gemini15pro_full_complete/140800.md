on-board:/ˌɑːnˈbɔːrd/ /ˌɒnˈbɔːrd/| adj. adv.|在船（或飞机、火车、公共汽车）上的；内置的；一体化的|Located or integrated within a vehicle, especially a ship, aircraft, train, or bus; included as part of a system or structure.|例句：The aircraft has on-board entertainment systems. 飞机上有机载娱乐系统。|近义词：integrated, built-in, included, onboard|反义词：external, off-board, separate|词性:形容词/副词

on-board:/ˌɑːnˈbɔːrd/ /ˌɒnˈbɔːrd/| v.|让…上船（或飞机、火车）；吸收（新成员）|To take or bring onto a vehicle, especially a ship, aircraft, train, or bus; to integrate (a new member) into an organization or team.|例句：They on-boarded the new employees quickly. 他们很快让新员工加入了团队。|近义词：embark, enroll, integrate|反义词：disembark|词性:动词
