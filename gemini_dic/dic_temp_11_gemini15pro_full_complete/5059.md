all-rounder:/ˈɔːlˌraʊndə(r)/ (UK), /ˈɔːlˌraʊndər/ (US)| n.|全能选手，多面手|A person who is competent in many different things; a versatile person.| He's a real all-rounder – he can cook, fix the car, and even play the piano. 他是个真正的多面手——他会做饭，会修车，甚至还会弹钢琴。| synonym: versatile person, jack-of-all-trades| antonym: specialist, expert| noun

all-rounders:/ˈɔːlˌraʊndəz/ (UK), /ˈɔːlˌraʊndərz/ (US)| n.|全能选手们，多面手们|Plural form of all-rounder.|  The team needs more all-rounders, not just specialists.  这个团队需要更多的多面手，而不仅仅是专家。| synonym: versatile people, jacks-of-all-trades| antonym: specialists, experts| noun
