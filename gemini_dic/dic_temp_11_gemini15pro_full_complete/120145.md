mince:/mɪns/| n.|肉馅|Ground meat, typically beef or a mixture of beef and pork.|The mince was used to make a delicious pie. 这肉馅被用来做了一个美味的派。|近义词：ground meat|反义词: N/A|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "mincier" meaning "to cut into small pieces," derived from Vulgar Latin "minutiare," from Latin "minutus" meaning "small, minute."
