conniption fit:/kəˈnɪpʃən fɪt/ /kəˈnɪpʃn fɪt/ | n. | 歇斯底里的发作 | A sudden outburst of anger or hysteria. | He threw a conniption fit when he realized he'd lost his wallet. 他意识到自己丢了钱包，顿时勃然大怒。| synonyms: tantrum, outburst, fit of rage | antonyms: calmness, composure | noun
