as-heck:/æz hɛk/| adv. | 非常；极其；真的；很；|Used to emphasize the preceding adjective. It intensifies the meaning, often implying a playful or slightly ironic tone.  Origin: A combination of "as" and "heck," with "heck" serving as a euphemism for "hell."| That cake was as-heck delicious! 那个蛋糕真的非常好吃！|近义词：very, extremely, really, totally, absolutely |反义词: slightly, mildly |词性:副词
