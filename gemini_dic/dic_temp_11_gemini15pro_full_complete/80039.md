go-with:/ɡoʊ wɪð/ /ɡəʊ wɪð/ | phrasal verb |  搭配；伴随；与…相配；与…协调 | To match or coordinate with something else; to be included or provided together with something else; to choose to support or agree with someone or something. |

例句：
Does this tie go with this shirt?
这条领带和这件衬衫搭配吗？

The fries go with the burger.
薯条和汉堡一起提供。

近义词：match, complement, accompany, suit, harmonize, agree with, side with
反义词：clash, disagree with, oppose
词性：动词短语
