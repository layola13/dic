nielsen:/ˈniːlsən/| n. |尼尔森|A surname of Scandinavian origin, also the name of a global marketing research firm known for its media audience measurement services.  It is often used to refer to the company itself or its data and ratings.|The latest Nielsen ratings show a decline in viewership for network television. 最新尼尔森收视率显示电视网络收视率下降。|近义词: (For the company) Arbitron, comScore|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
