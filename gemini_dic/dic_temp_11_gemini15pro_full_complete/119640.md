middle-of-the-roadism: /ˌmɪdl əv ðə ˈroʊdɪzəm/ | n. | 中庸主义，中间路线主义 | The avoidance of extremes, especially in political views; moderation.  A policy of compromise. |  The party's platform reflected a bland middle-of-the-roadism that appealed to few voters. 该党的纲领反映了一种温和的中间路线主义，很少有选民对此感兴趣。 |  Middle-of-the-roadism can sometimes be seen as a lack of conviction. 中庸之道有时会被视为缺乏信念。 | Synonyms: centrism, moderation, compromise | Antonyms: extremism, radicalism, partisanship | noun
