disinvestment:/dɪsɪnˈvɛstmənt/
n.
撤资，减少投资
The act of reducing investment in something.
The government's disinvestment in public services has led to a decline in their quality. 政府对公共服务的撤资导致其质量下降。
The company announced its disinvestment in the failing project.  该公司宣布撤出对这个失败项目的投资。
