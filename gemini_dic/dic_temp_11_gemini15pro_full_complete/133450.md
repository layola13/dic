nonfatalnesses:/ˌnɑːnˈfeɪtlnəsɪz/ | n. | 非致命性，非致命事件 |  The report highlighted the nonfatalnesses of the injuries sustained in the accident. 该报告强调了事故中受伤的非致命性。 | 例句：The study focused on the nonfatalnesses of the disease.  这项研究的重点是这种疾病的非致命性。| 近义词: survivals, non-lethality | 反义词: fatalities, lethality | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "fatal" (adjective meaning "causing death") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).
