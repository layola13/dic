flexors:/ˈfleksərz/ /ˈfleksɔːrz/
n.
屈肌
Muscles that bend a part of the body, such as an arm or a leg.
The flexors in her forearm were tight after the workout.  她前臂的屈肌在锻炼后很紧。

The hand flexors allow us to grip objects. 手部的屈肌使我们能够抓住物体。


近义词:  bending muscles, flexor muscles
反义词: extensors
词性: noun
