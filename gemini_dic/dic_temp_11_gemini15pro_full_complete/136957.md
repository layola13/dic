ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + specified.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "specified" comes from the Latin "species" (kind, sort) + "-ficare" (to make), meaning "to make specific."  Therefore, "nonspecified" literally means "not made specific."
