nongarrulous: /ˌnɒnˈɡærʊləs/ | adj. | 不爱说话的，沉默寡言的 | Not talkative; reticent.  | He was a nongarrulous man, preferring to listen rather than speak. 他是个不爱说话的人，宁愿倾听也不愿说话。| Synonym: taciturn, quiet, reserved, reticent | Antonym: garrulous, loquacious, talkative | adjective

Etymology:
From non- (not) + garrulous (talkative). Garrulous ultimately comes from the Latin word *garrulus*, meaning "chattering" or "talkative."
