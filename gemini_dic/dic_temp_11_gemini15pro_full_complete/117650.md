megayacht:/ˈmɛɡəˌjɑːt/| n. |超级游艇|A very large, luxurious yacht, typically over 100 feet (30 meters) in length.|例句：The megayacht was anchored in the harbor, gleaming in the sun. 这艘超级游艇停泊在港口，在阳光下闪闪发光。

例句：He made his fortune in tech and bought a megayacht to cruise the Mediterranean. 他在科技行业发了财，买了一艘超级游艇在地中海巡游。|近义词：superyacht, luxury yacht|反义词:dinghy, small boat|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Mega- (from Greek μέγας mégas 'great, large') + yacht (from Dutch jacht 'hunt, light fast sailing vessel')
