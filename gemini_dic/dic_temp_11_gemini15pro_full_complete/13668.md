attractingly:/əˈtræktɪŋli/ | adv. | 吸引人地，迷人地 | In a manner that attracts; charmingly. | She was attractingly dressed in a flowing silk gown. 她穿着一件飘逸的丝绸长袍，十分迷人。|  She smiled attractingly at him. 她对他嫣然一笑，十分迷人。 | 近义词：charmingly, appealingly, enticingly, alluringly | 反义词：unattractively, repellently | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From attract + -ing + -ly.  "Attract" comes from the Latin *attractare*, frequentative of *attrahere* "to draw to," from *ad-* "to" + *trahere* "to draw."
