honorable:/ˈɒnərəbl/ (British), /ˈɑːnərəbl/ (American)| adj.|值得尊敬的，荣誉的，光荣的|Deserving of honor and respect; bringing honor or distinction.|He led a long and honorable life. 他过着漫长而光荣的一生。|近义词：respected, noble, esteemed, reputable, virtuous|反义词：dishonorable, shameful, disgraceful|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French *onorable, from Latin honorabilis ("worthy of honor"), from honor ("honor").
