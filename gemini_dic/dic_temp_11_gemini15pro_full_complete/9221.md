antipacifists:/ˌæntiˈpæsɪfɪsts/
noun
反和平主义者，主战者
People who oppose pacifism and believe that war or violence can sometimes be justified or necessary.
Anti-pacifists argued that appeasement would only embolden the aggressor. 反和平主义者认为，绥靖只会助长侵略者的气焰。
Synonyms: warmongers, hawks, jingoists
Antonyms: pacifists, peacemakers, doves
n.

antipacifist:/ˌæntiˈpæsɪfɪst/
adjective
反和平主义的，主战的
Opposed to pacifism; believing that war or violence can sometimes be justified or necessary.
He took an antipacifist stance during the debate. 在辩论中，他采取了反和平主义的立场。
Synonyms: pro-war, militaristic, hawkish
Antonyms: pacifistic, peace-loving, dovish
adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + pacifist.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to." "Pacifist" comes from the Latin "pacificus" meaning "peacemaking," ultimately from "pax" (peace) and "facere" (to make).
