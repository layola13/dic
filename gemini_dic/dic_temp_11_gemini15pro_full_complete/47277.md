de-cultural:/diːˈkʌltʃərəl/
adj.
去文化的，非文化的
Removed or stripped of cultural influences or characteristics; having lost or been deprived of one's culture.
The de-cultural process often leads to a loss of identity. 去文化的过程通常会导致身份认同的丧失。
Indigenous communities are often subjected to de-cultural practices through assimilation policies. 原住民社区经常通过同化政策而遭受去文化习俗。
近义词:  acculturated, assimilated, westernized
反义词:  cultural, enculturated, indigenous
词性: 形容词
