organised:/ˈɔːɡənaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈɔːrɡənaɪzd/ (US)| adj.|有条理的，有组织的|arranged or structured in a systematic way|例句：She has a very organised desk. 她的桌子非常整洁有序。|例句：The event was well organised and ran smoothly.  这次活动组织得很好，进行得很顺利。|近义词：structured, ordered, systematized, methodical, efficient, arranged, planned|反义词：disorganised, chaotic, messy, cluttered|词性:形容词

词源:
From the verb "organise" (UK) or "organize" (US), which comes from French "organiser," ultimately from Late Latin "organizare," meaning "to furnish with organs," from Latin "organum" meaning "instrument, tool."
