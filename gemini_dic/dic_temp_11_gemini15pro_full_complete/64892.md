essen:/ˈɛsn̩/
verb
to eat (German)
This verb is the German infinitive form of "to eat".  It's used in various contexts depending on the grammatical construction.
Wir müssen essen. = We have to eat.
Ich esse gerne Brot. = I like to eat bread.
近义词:  (in German) speisen, verzehren
反义词: (in German) hungern, fasten
词性: verb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Ich esse jeden Tag Obst. = I eat fruit every day.
Er isst gern Fleisch. = He likes to eat meat.

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Ich bin gerade am Essen. = I am currently eating.  (Note: German doesn't have a direct present continuous equivalent, this uses a construction with "am" + infinitive.)
Wir sind am Essen, komm schnell! = We are eating, come quickly!

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Ich habe gegessen. = I have eaten.
Sie hat schon gegessen. = She has already eaten.

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
(Not directly applicable in German in the same way as English.  Emphasis on duration would be conveyed differently.)
Ich esse schon seit einer Stunde. = I've been eating for an hour.
Er isst die ganze Zeit. = He's been eating the whole time.

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Ich aß gestern Abend Pizza. = I ate pizza last night.
Wir aßen im Restaurant. = We ate in the restaurant.

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Ich war am Essen, als das Telefon klingelte. = I was eating when the phone rang.
Sie war gerade am Essen. = She was just eating.


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Ich hatte schon gegessen, bevor er kam. = I had already eaten before he came.
Wir hatten im Restaurant gegessen, bevor wir ins Kino gingen. = We had eaten in the restaurant before we went to the cinema.


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
(Not directly applicable in German.  Emphasis on duration would be conveyed differently.)
Ich hatte schon eine Stunde gegessen, als er kam. = I had already been eating for an hour when he came.
Wir hatten die ganze Zeit gegessen. = We had been eating the whole time.


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Ich werde morgen frühstücken. = I will have breakfast tomorrow.
Wir werden heute Abend im Restaurant essen. = We will eat in the restaurant tonight.

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
(Not used in the same way as English. German uses simple future or other constructions.)
Ich werde morgen um diese Zeit essen. = I will be eating at this time tomorrow.
Sie wird später essen. = She will be eating later.

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Ich werde bis morgen Abend gegessen haben. = I will have eaten by tomorrow evening.
Wir werden bis dahin gegessen haben. = We will have eaten by then.


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
(Not directly applicable in German.)
Bis morgen werde ich schon lange gegessen haben. = By tomorrow, I will have long since finished eating.
Bis dahin werden wir die ganze Zeit gegessen haben. = By then, we will have been eating the whole time.


虚拟语气:
Wenn ich Hunger hätte, würde ich essen. = If I were hungry, I would eat.
Ich wünschte, ich könnte jetzt essen. = I wish I could eat now.

被动语态:
Es wird gegessen. = It is being eaten. (Impersonal passive)
Die Suppe wurde gegessen. = The soup was eaten.


疑问句:
Was isst du? = What are you eating?
Wann essen wir? = When are we eating?

否定句:
Ich esse nicht. = I am not eating / I don't eat.
Wir essen kein Fleisch. = We don't eat meat.
