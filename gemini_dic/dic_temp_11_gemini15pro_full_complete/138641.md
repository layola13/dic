nudation:/njuːˈdeɪʃn/| n.|裸露，剥蚀|The process of stripping bare, or making naked; the state of being stripped bare; exposure; specifically (Geol.), the laying bare of rock masses by the weathering away of superficial deposits.|The prolonged nudation of the land surface resulted in the formation of a vast plateau.|近义词：exposure, denudation, stripping|反义词：covering, concealment|noun
