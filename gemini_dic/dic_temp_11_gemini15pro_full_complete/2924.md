advisor:/ədˈvaɪzər/| n.|导师|A teacher or other adult who gives help and advice to a student.|My academic advisor helped me choose my classes. 我的学术导师帮我选择了课程。|Synonyms: mentor, tutor, counselor, guide|Antonyms: advisee, pupil, student|noun
