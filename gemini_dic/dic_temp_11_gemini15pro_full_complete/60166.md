effendi:/ɪˈfɛndi/| n.|先生，老爷，阁下 (title of respect, formerly used in Turkey and some other parts of the Ottoman Empire)|A title of respect or courtesy, equivalent to "sir" or "Mr.," formerly used in the Ottoman Empire.  It was applied to educated men or government officials.|The effendi greeted us warmly at the door.  老爷在门口热情地迎接我们。|Near Synonyms: sir, master, gentleman, lord|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a title of respect)|Noun
