haustellate: /ˈhɔːstəˌleɪt/ | adj. | 吸管状的，具有吸管的 | Adapted for sucking; having a haustellum.  | Example: The mosquito's haustellate mouthparts allow it to pierce skin and draw blood. 蚊子的吸管状口器使其能够刺穿皮肤并吸取血液。

Example:  Butterflies have a haustellate proboscis for sipping nectar. 蝴蝶有一个吸管状的喙来吸食花蜜。 | 近义词: sucking, proboscidial | 反义词: chewing, biting | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *haustellātus, past participle of *haustellāre ‘to draw up, suck’, from haustor, haustus ‘one who draws’, from hauriō ‘I draw’.
