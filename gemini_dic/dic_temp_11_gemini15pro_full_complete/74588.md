freak:/friːk/
noun|怪人，怪胎；狂热爱好者；畸形生物|a person or animal that is markedly unusual or deformed|例句：He was a fitness freak. 他是个健身狂。|近义词：oddity, anomaly, enthusiast, fanatic|反义词：norm, regular|词性:n.

freak:/friːk/
verb|使震惊，使害怕；（使）发生故障，（使）出毛病|to become or cause someone to become extremely emotional|例句：The accident freaked me out.  这起事故把我吓坏了。|近义词：frighten, scare, malfunction|反义词：calm, soothe|词性:v.

freak:/friːk/
adjective|奇特的，反常的|very unusual or unexpected|例句：It was a freak accident. 那是一起离奇的意外事故。|近义词：bizarre, strange, unusual|反义词：normal, common|词性:adj.
