outinfluences:/ˌaʊtˈɪnflʊənsɪz/
verb
超过…的影响力; 比…更有影响力
To exert greater influence than someone or something else.
The new CEO quickly outinfluenced the former one in board decisions.  这位新任首席执行官在董事会决策中迅速超过了前任的影响力。
The marketing campaign outinfluenced all previous efforts, leading to record sales.  这次营销活动的影响力超过了以往所有的努力，带来了创纪录的销售额。
Synonyms: surpass, outweigh, override, eclipse
Antonyms:  be overshadowed by, be dominated by
词性: 动词
