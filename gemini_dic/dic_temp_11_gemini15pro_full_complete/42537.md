countermatch:/ˈkaʊntərˌmætʃ/| n.|对抗赛，对等比赛|A match or contest arranged in opposition to another, typically as a response or retaliation. |  The team demanded a countermatch after their controversial defeat.  该队在备受争议的失利后要求进行一场对抗赛。| synonym: rematch, return match| antonym: walkover (noun) forfeit (verb) |noun


countermatch:/ˌkaʊntərˈmætʃ/| v.|对抗，对等比赛|To arrange or engage in a countermatch. | They countermatched the opponent's strategy with a surprising tactic. 他们用一个令人惊讶的策略来对抗对手的策略。 |synonym: oppose, retaliate| antonym: concede, submit |verb
