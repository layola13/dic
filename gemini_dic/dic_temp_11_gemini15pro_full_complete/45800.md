custodialisms:/kʌˈstoʊdiəlɪzəmz/
|n.|保管主义；监护主义；监禁主义|The tendency to prioritize incarceration and other forms of custodial care over alternative approaches to social problems, such as rehabilitation or community-based solutions.  It often refers to a system or mindset that favors confinement and control over treatment and reintegration. |The report criticizes the increasing custodialisms in the juvenile justice system.  这份报告批评了青少年司法系统中日益增长的监禁主义。|近义词：incarceration, confinement, detention|反义词：rehabilitation, reintegration, community-based care|Noun
