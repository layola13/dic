marsh:/mɑːrʃ/ | n. |沼泽，湿地| An area of low-lying land that is flooded in wet seasons or at high tide, and typically remains waterlogged at all times. |例句：The marsh was teeming with wildlife. 沼泽里充满了野生动物。

|例句：We walked across the marsh on a wooden boardwalk. 我们沿着木板路走过沼泽。| 近义词：swamp, bog, fen | 反义词：desert, upland | noun
