fasciculus:/fəˈsɪkjʊləs/ | n. | 小束，小捆，分册 | A small bundle or cluster, especially of nerve or muscle fibers; a section of a book published in installments. | The surgeon carefully identified the fasciculus of nerves before making the incision.  外科医生在做切口前仔细地识别了神经束。 | bundle, cluster, tract | dispersion, scattering | noun

fasciculus:/fəˈsɪkjʊləs/ | n. | 册，分册 | One of the divisions of a book published in parts. | The fasciculus contained the first two chapters of the novel. 这分册包含了小说的前两章。| installment, part, section | whole, complete | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fasciculus*, diminutive of *fascis* "bundle".
