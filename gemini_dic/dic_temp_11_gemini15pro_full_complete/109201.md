leguminous:/ləˈɡjuːmɪnəs/ | adj. | 豆科的；豆类的 | Relating to or denoting plants of the pea family (Leguminosae), typically having seeds in pods, distinctive flowers, and root nodules containing nitrogen-fixing bacteria. |  Leguminous plants like beans and peas are important for soil health. 豆类植物如豆类和豌豆对土壤健康很重要。| synonyms:  pod-bearing, leguminous, papilionaceous | antonyms: non-leguminous | adjective

leguminous:/ləˈɡjuːmɪnəs/ | n. | 豆科植物 | A plant of the pea family (Leguminosae). |  Soybeans are a type of leguminous. 大豆是一种豆科植物。| synonyms: legume, pulse | antonyms:  N/A | noun
