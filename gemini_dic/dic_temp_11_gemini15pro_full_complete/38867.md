confederationist:/kənˌfedərˈeɪʃənɪst/
n.|邦联主义者；支持邦联制的人|A supporter of a confederation, especially a supporter of the Confederate States of America during the American Civil War.|Example: The confederationist argued for states' rights.  这位邦联主义者主张州权。|Example:  His grandfather was a confederationist during the Civil War. 他的祖父在内战期间是一位邦联主义者。|Synonym: secessionist, rebel|Antonym: unionist, federalist|noun


confederationist:/kənˌfedərˈeɪʃənɪst/
