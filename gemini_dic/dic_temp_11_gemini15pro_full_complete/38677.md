condemning:/kənˈdemɪŋ/
verb
谴责; 宣判; 迫使…接受困境
expressing strong disapproval of; officially declaring unfit for use; forcing someone to accept a difficult or unpleasant situation
The judge was condemning the thief for stealing. 法官谴责小偷的偷窃行为。
The building was condemned after the fire. 这栋楼在火灾后被宣布不适宜居住。
His poor health condemned him to a life indoors. 他糟糕的健康状况迫使他只能在室内生活。
synonyms: criticizing, denouncing, censuring, sentencing, dooming
antonyms: praising, commending, approving, acquitting, exonerating
