dissertationist:/ˌdɪsəˈteɪʃənɪst/
n.
论文作者，撰写论文的人
A person who writes a dissertation, especially one who has submitted a dissertation for a doctoral degree.
She's a budding dissertationist, working on her thesis about the impact of social media on political discourse. 她是一位崭露头角的论文作者，正在撰写关于社交媒体对政治话语影响的论文。
The dissertationist spent countless hours in the library researching their topic.  这位论文作者在图书馆花了无数时间研究他们的主题。
synonyms: doctoral candidate, PhD student, researcher, scholar
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, could consider someone who avoids academic work)
noun
