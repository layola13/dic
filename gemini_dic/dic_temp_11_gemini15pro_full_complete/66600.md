exsiccation:/ˌɛksɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
干燥，脱水
the process of removing moisture from something
The exsiccation of the plant specimens allowed for their long-term preservation.  植物标本的干燥使其能够长期保存。

近义词: dehydration, drying, desiccation
反义词: hydration, wetting

词性: noun
词源: From Latin exsiccātus, past participle of exsiccāre (“to dry up”), from ex- (“out”) + siccāre (“to dry”).
