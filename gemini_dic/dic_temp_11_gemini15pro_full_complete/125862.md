muscat:/ˈmʌskət/ /ˈmʌsˌkæt/ | n. |麝香葡萄；麝香葡萄酒|A sweet aromatic grape used for making wine and raisins. Also refers to the wine made from this grape.|例句：I enjoy a glass of chilled muscat after dinner. 我喜欢在晚餐后喝一杯冰镇麝香葡萄酒。

例句：The muscat grapes grown in this region are particularly flavorful.  这个地区种植的麝香葡萄特别美味。

近义词：Moscato, Muscat Blanc, Frontignan |反义词: N/A |词性:noun

muscat:/ˈmʌskət/ /ˈmʌsˌkæt/ | adj. | 麝香葡萄的； 麝香葡萄酒的|Relating to or characteristic of muscat grapes or wine.|例句：The muscat aroma filled the air.  空气中弥漫着麝香葡萄的香味。

例句：This is a delicious muscat dessert wine. 这是一款美味的麝香甜酒。

近义词：Muscatel |反义词: N/A |词性:adjective
