hypoderm: /ˈhaɪpəʊdəːm/ (US) /ˈhaɪpəʊdəːm/ (UK)| n. |皮下组织；皮下注射|The layer of tissue beneath the epidermis, especially in invertebrates.| The hypoderm of some parasitic worms is syncytial.  一些寄生蠕虫的皮下组织是合胞体的。|Synonyms: subcutaneous tissue, hypodermis|Antonyms: epidermis|noun


hypodermic: /ˌhaɪpəˈdɜːmɪk/ (US) /ˌhaɪpəˈdɜːmɪk/ (UK)| adj. n. |皮下的；皮下注射的；皮下注射器|Relating to the region immediately beneath the skin.|A hypodermic needle is used to administer the drug. 皮下注射针用于给药。|Synonyms: subcutaneous|Antonyms: surface, topical|adjective, noun
