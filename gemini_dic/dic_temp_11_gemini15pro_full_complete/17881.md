beeline:/ˈbiːlaɪn/| n.|直线，捷径，最短路线|A straight line or direct route to something, especially the quickest or shortest route.|He made a beeline for the refrigerator. 他径直走向冰箱。|synonyms: direct route, shortest route, straight line, bee line|antonyms: detour, indirect route|noun
