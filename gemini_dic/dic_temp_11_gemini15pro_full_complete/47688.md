debugs:/ˌdiːˈbʌɡz/
verb
调试，除错
identifies and removes errors from (computer hardware or software).
The programmer debugs the code line by line.  程序员逐行调试代码。
He spent hours debugging the new software. 他花了几个小时调试新软件。
synonyms: troubleshoot, fix, repair, correct, rectify
antonyms: break, damage, corrupt
v.
