creative:/kriˈeɪtɪv/ | adj. | 创造性的，有创造力的 | Having the ability or power to create.  | She's a very creative person, always coming up with new ideas. 她是一个很有创造力的人，总是能想出新点子。 | innovative, imaginative, ingenious, original | uncreative, unimaginative, unoriginal | adjective

creative:/kriˈeɪtɪv/ | n. |  有创造力的人; 具有创造性的事物 | A person who is creative, especially in a profession or art. |  The creatives in the advertising agency came up with a brilliant campaign. 广告公司的创意人员想出了一个很棒的活动。 | innovator, artist, designer, inventor | - | noun
