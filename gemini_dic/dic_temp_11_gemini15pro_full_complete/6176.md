Amish: /ˈɑːmɪʃ/ (US), /ˈæmɪʃ/ (UK)| adj. n.| 阿米什人的，阿米什人|A member of a strict religious group in the US, known for its simple lifestyle and rejection of modern technology.  They are descended from Swiss Anabaptists.|  Example:  The Amish community maintains its traditional way of life.  阿米什社区保持着其传统的生活方式。

Example:  She bought an Amish quilt at the market. 她在市场上买了一条阿米什手工缝制的被子。 | 近义词: Mennonite (similar religious group) | 反义词: modern, contemporary | 词性: adjective, noun
