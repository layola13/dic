idiographic:/ˌɪdɪəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 特质的，个体化的 |  Relating to the study of particular facts or processes, as distinct from general laws. Focused on the individual and unique, rather than general or universal. |  Idiographic research focuses on individual cases rather than general patterns.  特质性研究侧重于个别案例而非一般模式。 |  Idiographic approaches are valuable in understanding the complexity of human behavior.  特质性方法对于理解人类行为的复杂性很有价值。 |  individual, particular, unique, distinctive | nomothetic, general | adjective
