elviate: /ɪˈliːvieɪt/
verb
减轻，缓解
to make (something) easier to bear : to lessen the pain or trouble of (something)
例句：He took aspirin to elviate the pain. 他服用阿司匹林来缓解疼痛。

例句：The medicine elviated her symptoms.  这种药减轻了她的症状。

近义词：alleviate, mitigate, lessen, reduce, relieve, ease
反义词：aggravate, worsen, exacerbate, intensify
词性：v.
