clerical-error:/ˈklerɪkəl ˈerər/ (US), /ˈklerɪkəl ˈerə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 书写错误；笔误|A mistake made in an office, especially one made while writing something.|There was a clerical error in the invoice, so the total was incorrect. 发票上有一个笔误，所以总额不正确。|Synonyms: misprint, typo, slip, slip-up, lapse, oversight, mistake, error, inaccuracy, fault, flaw, blunder, howler|Antonyms: accuracy, correctness|noun


clerical error:/ˈklerɪkəl ˈerər/ (US), /ˈklerɪkəl ˈerə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|  文书的；办事员的|Relating to clerks or office work. (This usage is less common for the phrase "clerical error," which typically appears as a noun.)|The clerical staff handles all the paperwork. 文书人员处理所有的文书工作. (This exemplifies the adjective "clerical," not the noun phrase "clerical error.")|Synonyms: administrative, office, secretarial|Antonyms: executive, managerial|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "clerical" (relating to clerks) + "error" (mistake). "Clerical" comes from the Latin "clericus" (clerk, clergyman). "Error" comes from the Latin "error" (wandering, mistake).
