oncometer:|/ɒnˈkɒmɪtər/ /ɑːnˈkɑːmɪtər/| n. |肿瘤计，体积描记器|An instrument that measures and records changes in the volume of a part of the body, typically an organ or a tumor, often by measuring changes in fluid displacement. |An oncometer was attached to the kidney to monitor changes in its volume during the experiment. 在实验过程中，将肿瘤计连接到肾脏以监测其体积的变化。|Synonym:  volume recorder, plethysmograph|Antonym: N/A|noun
