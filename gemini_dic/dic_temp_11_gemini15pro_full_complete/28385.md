carbinol:/ˈkɑːrbɪnɒl/ (US) /ˈkɑːrbɪnɔːl/ (UK)| n. |甲醇; 羟甲基|An alcohol containing the hydroxymethyl group (CH₂OH).  Specifically, methanol.|例句：Methanol is the simplest carbinol. 甲醇是最简单的甲醇。

例句：The carbinol group is a key structural element in many organic compounds. 羟甲基是在许多有机化合物中的关键结构元素。|近义词：methanol, methyl alcohol|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
