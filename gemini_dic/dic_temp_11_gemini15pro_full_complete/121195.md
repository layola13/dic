misdrawing:/ˌmɪsˈdrɔːɪŋ/
n.
错误的绘图; 歪曲的描述
an incorrect or distorted drawing or representation.
The architect noticed a misdrawing in the blueprint that could have caused a major structural problem.  建筑师注意到蓝图中有一个绘图错误，可能会导致严重的结构问题。
misrepresentation, distortion, inaccuracy
accurate portrayal, correct depiction
noun
