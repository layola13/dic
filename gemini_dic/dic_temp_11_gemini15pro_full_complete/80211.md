godness:/ˈɡʊdnɪs/ | n. | 仁慈，善良，美德 | The goodness of her heart was evident in her charitable work. 她的善良从她的慈善工作中可见一斑。 |  例句：She radiated goodness and kindness. 她浑身散发着善良和仁慈。 | 近义词：kindness, benevolence, virtue, morality | 反义词：evil, wickedness, badness, cruelty | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *godnes*, equivalent to *god* + *-nes*. Cognate with Scots *guidness* (“goodness”).
