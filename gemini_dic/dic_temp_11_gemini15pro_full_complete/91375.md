hydrosulphite:/ˌhaɪdrəˈsʌlfaɪt/
n.
连二亚硫酸盐;保险粉
a salt of hydrosulfurous acid.  A powerful reducing agent, especially in alkaline solution, used commercially as a bleach for wood pulp, sugar, and other substances.
Sodium hydrosulphite is used in dyeing. 连二亚硫酸钠用于染色。
