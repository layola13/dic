delimited:/dɪˈlɪmɪtɪd/
adjective
限定的；界定的
Marked off by or as if by a boundary; confined within limits.  Something that is delimited has clearly defined boundaries or limitations.
The garden is delimited by a low fence. 花园被矮栅栏围了起来。
Their roles were strictly delimited. 他们的角色被严格限定。
近义词: bounded, defined, restricted, limited
反义词: unlimited, unbounded, undefined
词性: adjective

delimit:/dɪˈlɪmɪt/
verb
限定；界定
To fix or mark the limits or boundaries of.  To delimit something is to establish its boundaries or limits.
The property is delimited by a fence. 这块地产用栅栏界定。
The powers of the president are delimited by the constitution. 总统的权力受宪法限制。
近义词: define, bound, demarcate, circumscribe
反义词: expand, broaden

词源:
From the Latin word "dēlimitāre," meaning "to mark out the boundaries of," from "dē-" (from, away from) + "līmitāre" (to limit, from "līmes," meaning "boundary").
