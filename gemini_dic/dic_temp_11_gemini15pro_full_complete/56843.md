dorsad:/ˈdɔːrsæd/| adv. adj.| 背侧地，向背侧的 | Towards the back or dorsal surface, especially of an animal.|The hairs on the caterpillar are arranged dorsad.  毛毛虫身上的毛向背侧排列。|近义词：posteriorly, backward |反义词: ventrad, ventrally|词性: 副词，形容词

dorsal:/ˈdɔːrsəl/| adj. n.| 背部的，背侧的；背鳍 | Relating to the back or upper surface of an animal, plant, or organ; a dorsal fin or similar anatomical feature. |The dorsal fin of a shark is often visible above the water.  鲨鱼的背鳍经常露出水面。|例句：The dorsal side of the leaf is darker green. 叶子的背面颜色更深。|近义词：posterior, back |反义词: ventral, anterior|词性: 形容词，名词


词源:  From Latin *dorsālis*, from *dorsum* (“back”).
