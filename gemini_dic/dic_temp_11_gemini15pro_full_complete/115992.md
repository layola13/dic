ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "marshal," which comes from Old French "mareschal" (horse servant), ultimately from Frankish *marhskalk "horse servant," from *marha- "horse" + *skalk- "servant."  The meaning evolved from managing horses to managing people and things in general.
