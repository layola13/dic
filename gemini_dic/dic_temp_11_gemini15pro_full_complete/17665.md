becky:/ˈbɛki/ | n. | 贝基（女子名） | A diminutive of Rebecca, often used as a stereotype for a white, basic, and privileged woman, particularly in online slang.  This stereotype is often considered derogatory. |  例句：Becky didn't understand why her pumpkin spice latte was so expensive.  贝基不明白为什么她的南瓜香料拿铁这么贵。

例句：The meme made fun of Beckys who only listened to pop music.  这个表情包取笑那些只听流行音乐的贝基们。 | 近义词：Rebecca (formal), Becca | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
