amiral:/ˈædmərəl/ | n. | 海军上将；旗舰；凤蝶 | A high-ranking naval officer, typically one in command of a fleet or squadron. amiral:/ˈædmərəl/ | adj. | 海军上将的；旗舰的 | Of or relating to an admiral or a flagship. |  The admiral's cabin was luxurious. 海军上将的船舱很豪华。 | synonyms: naval, maritime, nautical | antonyms: land-based | adjective
