nonavoidable:/ˌnɒnəˈvɔɪdəbl/ /ˌnɑːnəˈvɔɪdəbl/| adj.|不可避免的|Not able to be avoided; inevitable.|例句：The consequences of his actions were nonavoidable. 他行为的后果是不可避免的。|近义词：inevitable, unavoidable, inescapable|反义词：avoidable, preventable|词性:形容词
nonavoidable:|词源: non- (not) + avoidable (able to be avoided).  The prefix "non-" negates the meaning of "avoidable," which comes from the verb "avoid" ultimately derived from Old French "esvuidier".|
