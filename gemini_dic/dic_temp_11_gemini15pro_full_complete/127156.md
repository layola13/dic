natural-born:/ˈnætʃrəlˈbɔːrn/| adj.|天生的，生来的|Born in a particular place or having a particular characteristic from birth.  Relating to the place or circumstances of one's birth.|例句：He is a natural-born leader. 他是一位天生的领袖。|例句：She has a natural-born talent for music. 她有与生俱来的音乐天赋。|近义词：innate, inherent, congenital|反义词：acquired, learned|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From natural + born.  "Born" here functions as a past participle used adjectivally.  "Natural" traces back to Latin "naturalis," meaning "of or pertaining to nature."
