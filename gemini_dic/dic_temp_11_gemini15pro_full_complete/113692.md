mackle:/ˈmækəl/| n.| 模糊的印刷; 叠印; 双晶;  孪晶|A blurred impression in printing; an overlapping impression; a twin crystal; a mineralogical twin.|The old book had a mackle on page 57.  这本旧书的第57页有一个模糊的印刷。|近义词：blur, smudge, twin, double|反义词: single, distinct| noun

mackle:/ˈmækəl/| v.| 模糊地印刷; 叠印; 使成双晶|To blur in printing; to make an overlapping impression; to twin.|The printer accidentally mackled several pages.  打印机意外地模糊地印了几页。|近义词：blur, smudge, twin, double|反义词: clarify, single| verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *macle*, from Old French *mascle* ("spot, mesh"), from Vulgar Latin *macula* ("spot, mesh"), from Latin *macula* ("spot, stain, blemish").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *meh₂ḱ-/*meh₂k- ("long, thin").
