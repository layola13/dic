nonchurchgoer:/ˌnɒnˈtʃɜːrtʃɡəʊər/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈtʃɜːrtʃɡoʊər/ (US)
noun
不去教堂的人；非经常去教堂的人
A person who does not attend church regularly or at all.
He's a nonchurchgoer, but he still considers himself spiritual. 他不去教堂，但他仍然认为自己有灵性。
Synonyms: non-attender, secularist
Antonyms: churchgoer, worshipper
词性: 名词
