idiomatically:/ˌɪdiəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 地道地；惯用地 |  In a way that is typical of a particular language, especially in using idioms. | She speaks English idiomatically. 她英语说得地道。| 近义词: naturally, fluently | 反义词: awkwardly, unnaturally | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From idiom + -atically.  "Idiom" comes from Greek "idioma" meaning "peculiarity, special property," from "idios" meaning "one's own."
