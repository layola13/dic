forestries:/ˈfɒrɪstrɪz/ | n. | 林学; 林业 | The science, art, and practice of managing and using forests and their resources. |  The government is investing heavily in forestries to promote sustainable timber production. 政府正在大力投资林业以促进可持续木材生产。 | 近义词: forestry, silviculture | 反义词: deforestation | 词性: 名词 (plural)


Etymology:  From forestry + -es (plural suffix). Forestry itself comes from Old French forestrie "forestry," from forest + -erie. Ultimately from Late Latin forestis "belonging to a forest," from foris "outside."
