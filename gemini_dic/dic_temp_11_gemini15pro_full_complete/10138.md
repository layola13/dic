apologiae:/əˌpɒləˈdʒiːiː/
n.
formal defenses, apologies
A written defense of one's opinions or conduct; a formal apology.
Justin Martyr’s writings are considered early Christian apologetics, defending the faith against paganism.
近义词：defenses, justifications, explanations, vindications
反义词：condemnations, accusations, indictments
词性：noun (plural)

apologia:/əˌpɒləˈdʒiːə/
n.
formal defense, apology
A formal written defense of one's opinions or conduct.
He wrote an apologia for his beliefs.
近义词：defense, justification, explanation, vindication
反义词：condemnation, accusation, indictment
词性：noun (singular)


词源：From Late Latin *apologia, from Ancient Greek ἀπολογία (apología, “speech in defense”), from ἀπολογέομαι (apologéomai, “to speak in defense”).
