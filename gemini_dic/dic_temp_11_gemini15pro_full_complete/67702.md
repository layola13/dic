exultantly:/ɪɡˈzʌltəntli/
adverb
欣喜若狂地；得意洋洋地
in a way that shows great happiness or triumph
She threw her arms in the air and laughed exultantly. 她举起双臂欣喜若狂地大笑。
近义词:triumphantly, jubilantly, joyously
反义词:mournfully, sadly, dejectedly
词性:adverb

exultant:/ɪɡˈzʌltənt/
adjective
欣喜若狂的；非常高兴的
feeling or expressing great happiness or triumph
The exultant fans cheered their team to victory.  欣喜若狂的球迷为他们的球队取得胜利而欢呼。
近义词:jubilant, triumphant, joyful
反义词:dejected, sad, miserable
词性:adjective

exult:/ɪɡˈzʌlt/
verb
欢欣鼓舞；兴高采烈
to feel or express great joy or triumph
They exulted at/over their victory. 他们为自己的胜利而欢欣鼓舞。
近义词:rejoice, triumph, revel
反义词:mourn, grieve, lament
词性:verb

词源: From Latin exsultare "to leap up, jump for joy," from ex- "out" + saltare "to leap, dance"
