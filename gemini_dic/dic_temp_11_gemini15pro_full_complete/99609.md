interjectionally:/ˌɪntərˈdʒɛkʃənəli/
adverb
感叹地，插入感叹词地
Used to describe words or phrases spoken as an interjection.
"Wow," she said interjectionally, "that's amazing!" “哇，”她感叹道，“太神奇了！”
