cringeworthy:/ˈkrɪndʒˌwɜːrði/| adj.|令人尴尬的，难为情的|Causing feelings of embarrassment or awkwardness.|Her attempt at a joke was cringeworthy. 她试图讲个笑话，结果令人尴尬。|近义词:embarrassing, awkward, uncomfortable|反义词:comfortable,  amusing,  pleasant|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From "cringe" (meaning to shrink back in fear or embarrassment) + "-worthy" (a suffix meaning deserving of).  "Cringe" ultimately comes from the Old English "cringan," meaning to bend or yield.
