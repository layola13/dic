dramaturgy:/ˈdrɑːmətɜːrdʒi/ /drəˈmætɜːrdʒi/
n.
戏剧学；剧本结构；剧作法
The theory and practice of dramatic composition.  The art or technique of dramatic composition and theatrical representation.
例句：He studied dramaturgy at university. 他在大学学习戏剧学。

例句：The play's dramaturgy is weak, with poorly developed characters and a predictable plot.  这出戏的剧本结构很弱，人物塑造单薄，情节老套。

近义词：playwriting, dramatic composition, theater arts
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider concepts like "improvisation" or "unstructured performance")
词性: noun
