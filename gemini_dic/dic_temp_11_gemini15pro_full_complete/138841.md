ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *nuptialis*, from *nupta* ("bride, married woman"), perfect passive participle of *nubere* ("to marry, to veil oneself"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*sneubh-* ("to twist, turn, wrap").
