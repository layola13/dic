nuke's:/nuːks/| n.|核武器的；核能的| possessive form of nuke, meaning "belonging to a nuclear weapon" or "related to nuclear energy". It refers to something owned by or associated with a nuclear weapon or nuclear power.| The nuke's destructive power was immense.  这枚核武器的破坏力是巨大的。|  The nuke's core was damaged. 核武器的内核损坏了。 |synonyms: atomic's, nuclear's| antonyms: conventional's, non-nuclear's| possessive noun
