diabetes mellitus:/ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz məˈlaɪtəs/ | n. | 糖尿病 | A group of metabolic disorders characterized by hyperglycemia resulting from defects in insulin secretion, insulin action, or both.  It is marked by persistent elevated blood glucose levels. | My grandfather has diabetes mellitus and has to monitor his blood sugar levels regularly. 我的祖父患有糖尿病，必须定期监测血糖水平。 |  hyperglycemia, high blood sugar | hypoglycemia, low blood sugar | noun
