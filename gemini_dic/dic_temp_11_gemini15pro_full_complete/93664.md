imbuement:/ɪmˈbjuːmənt/
noun
灌输；浸染；充满
the act of imbuing something with a quality or feeling
例句：The imbuement of the team with a winning spirit was key to their success.  这支队伍被灌输了求胜精神，这是他们成功的关键。

例句：The artist's work reflects an imbuement with the spirit of the Renaissance.  这位艺术家的作品反映了文艺复兴精神的浸染。
近义词：instillation, infusion, permeation, impregnation
反义词：drainage, depletion
词性：n.

imbuing:/ɪmˈbjuːɪŋ/
verb
灌输；使充满
inspire or permeate with a feeling or quality
例句：He imbued his students with a love of learning.  他向学生们灌输了热爱学习的精神。

例句：The painting was imbued with a sense of mystery. 这幅画充满了神秘感。
近义词：instill, infuse, permeate, impregnate
反义词：drain, deplete
词性：v.
