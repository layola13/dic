faradization:/ˌfærədaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 法拉第电疗 | The therapeutic use of a faradic current.  It involves applying intermittent pulses of electrical current to stimulate muscles or nerves. |  Faradization was a common treatment in the 19th century for various neurological conditions. 法拉第电疗在 19 世纪是治疗各种神经系统疾病的常用方法。 | electrotherapy, galvanization | N/A | noun
