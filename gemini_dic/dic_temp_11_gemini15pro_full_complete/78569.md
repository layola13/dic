ghz:/ɡɪɡəhɜːrts/| n.| 千兆赫兹 |A unit of frequency equal to one billion hertz, used especially in reference to electromagnetic waves.  It is a measure of how many cycles per second a wave completes, often used to describe the speed of computer processors.|My new computer has a 3.5 GHz processor. 我的新电脑有一个3.5 GHz的处理器。|近义词:gigahertz|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement) |noun
