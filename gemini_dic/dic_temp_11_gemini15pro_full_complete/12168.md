aryanization:/ˌɛəriənɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
雅利安化；种族清洗
The systematic removal of Jews and other non-Aryans from positions of power and influence in society, typically through expropriation, forced emigration, or genocide.
The Nazi regime pursued a policy of Aryanization, seizing Jewish businesses and property.  纳粹政权奉行雅利安化政策，夺取犹太人的企业和财产。

The Aryanization of German society was a key component of Nazi ideology. 德国社会的雅利安化是纳粹意识形态的一个关键组成部分。
近义词:racial cleansing, ethnic cleansing,  discrimination, persecution
反义词:integration, inclusion, diversity

词性: noun
