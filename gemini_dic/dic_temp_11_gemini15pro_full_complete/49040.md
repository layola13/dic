degustation:/ˌdeɡəˈsteɪʃən/ | n. | 品尝，品酒 | the careful and appreciative tasting of something, especially food or wine |  We attended a wine degustation at the vineyard. 我们在葡萄园参加了一场品酒会。| synonyms: tasting, sampling, savoring | antonyms: gorging, guzzling | noun

Etymology: From French *dégustation*, from *déguster* "to taste," from Latin *degustare* "to taste," from *de-* "down" + *gustare* "to taste."
