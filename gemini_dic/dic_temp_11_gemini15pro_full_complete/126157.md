mutterer:/ˈmʌtərər/ (US), /ˈmʌtərə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 咕哝者；低声抱怨者 |A person who mutters; someone who speaks in a low, indistinct voice, often complaining. |He was a known mutterer, always complaining under his breath about something. 他是一个众所周知的爱咕哝的人，总是低声抱怨些什么。|

The old woman was a constant mutterer, never seeming to be satisfied.  这个老妇人总是不断地咕哝，似乎永远都不满意。|近义词：grumbler, complainer, murmurer |反义词:  proclaimer, announcer |词性: noun
