outfloated:/ˌaʊtˈfloʊtɪd/
verb
胜过，漂浮超过
surpassed in floating; floated beyond
The larger log outfloated the smaller one down the river.  较大的木头漂浮在河上超过了较小的木头。
近义词：surpassed, outstripped, exceeded
反义词：outdone, lagged behind
词性：v.

词源：
"Outfloat" is a combination of the prefix "out-" and the verb "float." "Out-"  generally indicates exceeding or surpassing something. "Float" refers to resting or moving on the surface of a liquid.
