interweft: /ˈɪntərˌweft/
noun
交织纬线; 交织物
A decorative weft yarn interwoven with the basic weft of a fabric.  A fabric woven with an interweft.
The intricate pattern was created using a contrasting interweft.  这复杂的图案是使用对比鲜明的交织纬线织成的。
The interweft added a subtle shimmer to the silk. 交织纬线为丝绸增添了微妙的光泽。
Synonyms: crossweave, interwoven weft
Antonyms: N/A (no direct antonym for a technical weaving term)
词性: n.
