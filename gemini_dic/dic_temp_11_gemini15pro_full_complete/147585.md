overphilosophizes:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪləˌsɒfaɪzɪz/
verb
过度哲学化; 过分思辨
thinks or discusses something in a way that is too theoretical and complicated
He tends to overphilosophize simple matters, making them seem more complex than they are. 他倾向于过度思辨简单的事情，使它们看起来比实际更复杂。
overthink, overanalyze, intellectualize, ruminate
underthink, simplify, skim
v.
