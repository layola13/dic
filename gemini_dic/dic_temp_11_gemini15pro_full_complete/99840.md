intermittently:/ˌɪntərˈmɪtəntli/| adv.|间歇地，断断续续地|occurring at irregular intervals; not continuously or steadily. |The rain fell intermittently throughout the day. 一整天都断断续续地下着雨。|近义词：sporadically, occasionally, periodically|反义词：continuously, constantly|词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *intermittentem (nominative intermittens), present participle of intermittere "to discontinue, leave off; cease; interrupt; put between, interpose," from inter- "between" + mittere "to send, let go."
