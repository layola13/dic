detrude:/dɪˈtruːd/
verb
推下；挤出；黜落
To force down or out from a position.
The invading army detruded the king from his throne. 入侵的军队将国王从王位上赶了下来。
synonyms: displace, oust, expel, eject
antonyms: install, reinstate, enthrone
verb

detrude:/dɪˈtruːd/
verb
推下；挤出；黜落
To thrust or push down or out.
The rocks were detruded from the cliff face by the heavy rain.  暴雨将岩石从悬崖面上冲了下来。
synonyms: dislodge, remove
antonyms: embed, insert
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *detrudere*, from *de-* (down, away from) + *trudere* (to thrust, push).
