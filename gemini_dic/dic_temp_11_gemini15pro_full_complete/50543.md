depurition:/ˌdiːpjʊˈrɪʃən/
n.
净化，清除
The process of purifying or cleansing something, especially the body.
The depurition of the blood is essential for good health.  血液净化对健康至关重要。

近义词:purification, cleansing, purging

词性:noun

词源:From Late Latin *depuritiōn-, from Latin depurit- ‘cleansed’, from the verb depurire, from de- ‘from, away’ + purus ‘pure’.
