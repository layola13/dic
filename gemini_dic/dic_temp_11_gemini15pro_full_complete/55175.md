disseminator:/dɪˈsɛmɪneɪtər/
n.|传播者，散布者|A person or thing that disseminates something, especially information or knowledge.|The internet has become a powerful disseminator of information. 互联网已成为强大的信息传播工具。|synonyms: distributor, spreader, propagator|antonyms: suppressor, withholder|noun

disseminator:/dɪˈsɛmɪneɪtər/
n.|宣传者|A person who promotes or spreads an idea, doctrine, etc.|He was a disseminator of revolutionary ideas. 他是革命思想的宣传者。|synonyms: promoter, advocate, publicist|antonyms: opponent, critic|noun
