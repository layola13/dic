outshrills:/ˌaʊtˈʃrɪlz/
verb
胜过(某人)的喊叫声；盖过(某人)的喊叫声
to shout louder than someone else
The protestors outshrilled the police, their chants echoing through the streets. 抗议者的呼喊声盖过了警察的声音，他们的口号在街道上回荡。
The soprano outshrilled the other singers, her voice soaring above the chorus. 女高音的歌声盖过了其他歌手，她的声音在合唱团之上翱翔。
Synonyms: outshout, drown out
Antonyms: be drowned out, be outshouted
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + shrill (v.).  "Out-" prefix signifies exceeding or surpassing.  "Shrill" comes from Middle English *schrillen*, of imitative origin.
