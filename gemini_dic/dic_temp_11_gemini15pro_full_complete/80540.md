goodliness:/ˈɡʊdlɪnɪs/
n.
美好；善良；美貌；漂亮
moral excellence or beauty
Her goodliness was apparent to all who met her. 她的美好品质在她遇到的每个人身上都显而易见。

近义词:beauty, loveliness, grace, charm, elegance, virtue, goodness
反义词:ugliness, badness, evil, wickedness


词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *godlinesse*, equivalent to goodly +‎ -ness.  The word "goodly" itself comes from Old English *gōdlīc*, from *gōd* ("good") + *-līc* (like).
