enervators:/ˈɛnərveɪtərz/| n. |使人衰弱的人或物；泄气的人或物|Something that enervates reduces the mental or physical energy of someone or something.  It makes them feel weak and tired.|The humid, tropical heat was a powerful enervator.|近义词：weakeners, debilitators, drainers|反义词：energizers, invigorators|词性: Noun (plural)

enervate:/ˈɛnərveɪt/| v. |使衰弱；使无力|To enervate is to deprive of force or strength; weaken.|The long illness had enervated him.|近义词：weaken, debilitate, sap|反义词：energize, invigorate, strengthen|词性: Verb
