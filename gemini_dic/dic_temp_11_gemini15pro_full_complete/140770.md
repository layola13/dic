omohyoid:/ˌoʊmoʊˈhaɪɔɪd/| n. |肩胛舌骨肌|A long slender digastric muscle extending from the scapula to the hyoid bone and drawing the hyoid bone caudad and laterally.|The omohyoid muscle is important for swallowing and speaking.|Synonyms:  infrahyoid muscle (more general), strap muscle (more general) |Antonyms:  N/A (Muscles don't typically have direct antonyms)|Noun
omohyoid:/ˌoʊmoʊˈhaɪɔɪd/| adj. |肩胛舌骨肌的|Relating to or pertaining to the omohyoid muscle. | The omohyoid fascia covers the omohyoid muscle. | Synonyms: infrahyoid (more general),  |Antonyms: N/A|Adjective
