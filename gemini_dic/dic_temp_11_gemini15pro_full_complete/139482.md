obstreperously: /əbˈstrepərəsli/ /ɑːbˈstrepərəsli/
| adv. | 喧闹地；吵闹地；任性地 | marked by unruly or aggressive noisiness : clamorous | The children were behaving obstreperously in the restaurant. 孩子们在餐馆里表现得吵闹喧哗。|  The protesters chanted obstreperously outside the government building. 抗议者在政府大楼外喧闹地 chanting chanting。 | 近义词：noisily, clamorously, boisterously, loudly, vociferously | 反义词：quietly, calmly, peacefully | 词性: 副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *obstreperus* "noisy," from *ob* "against, toward" + *strepere* "to make a noise."
