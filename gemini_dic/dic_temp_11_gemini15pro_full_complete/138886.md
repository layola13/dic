nusayba:/nuːˈsaɪbə/ | n. |努赛芭 (女子名)| Nusayba is an Arabic female given name.  It is derived from the Arabic word "nasa'ib" meaning "lineage" or "ancestry".  In Islamic history, Nusayba bint Ka'ab al-Ansariyyah was a prominent female companion of the Prophet Muhammad known for her bravery and skill in battle.| Nusayba is a beautiful name.  努赛芭是个好听的名字。| 近义词: (无直接近义词，可以列举其他阿拉伯女性名字，例如:Aisha, Fatima, Khadija) | 反义词: (人名无反义词) | 词性: 名词
