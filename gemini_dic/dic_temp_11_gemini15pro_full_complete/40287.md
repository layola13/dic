continuer:/kɒnˈtɪnjuːə(r)/ (British), /kənˈtɪnjuːər/ (American)| v. |继续，持续|To persist in an action or process.|He continued to work despite the noise.  他尽管噪音很大，仍然继续工作。|近义词：persist, proceed, go on, carry on, keep on|反义词:stop, cease, halt|verb
