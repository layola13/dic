avuncular:/əˈvʌŋkjələr/
adjective
像伯父（叔父）的；慈爱的；宽厚的
Kind and friendly towards a younger person, like an uncle.
He had an avuncular manner that made him popular with his students. 他有一种慈祥的举止，使他很受学生欢迎。
Synonyms: kind, friendly, benevolent, paternal, fatherly, warm, caring
Antonyms: cold, distant, unfriendly, hostile
词性: adjective
