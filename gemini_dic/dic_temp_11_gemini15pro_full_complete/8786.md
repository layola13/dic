antiegoism:/ˌæntiˈiːɡoʊɪzəm/

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposite of  (from Greek _anti_)
egoism (noun): excessive concern for oneself; selfishness (from Latin _ego_ meaning "I")
-ism (suffix): forming nouns denoting action, practice, or condition
