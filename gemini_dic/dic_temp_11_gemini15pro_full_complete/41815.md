cortina:/kɔːrˈtiːnə/ | n. |帷幕，幔帐；菌膜|A curtain or covering, especially one of membranous tissue in mushrooms or other fungi. |The mushroom had a delicate cortina covering its gills.| 近义词:veil, membrane | 反义词: (None -  lack of a cortina is simply described as absent or missing) | noun
