blossoms: /ˈblɑːsəmz/ (US) /ˈblɒsəmz/ (UK)| n. v.|花朵；开花|The state of flowering, especially of fruit trees.  The flowers of a tree or plant.|例句：The cherry blossoms are beautiful in spring. 春天的樱花很漂亮。|近义词：flowers, blooms|反义词:fade, wither|词性:名词复数；动词第三人称单数

blossoms: /ˈblɑːsəmz/ (US) /ˈblɒsəmz/ (UK)| v.|开花|To produce or yield blossoms; to flourish or thrive.|例句：The apple tree blossoms in May.  苹果树在五月开花。|近义词：bloom, flower, flourish|反义词:wither, die|词性:动词第三人称单数
