arraign:/əˈreɪn/| v. |传讯；控告；责难|to call or bring (someone) before a court to answer to an indictment|The suspect was arraigned on charges of murder. 嫌疑犯被传讯，被控谋杀罪。|近义词：indict, prosecute, charge, accuse|反义词：exonerate, acquit|词性:动词

词源: From Old French *araisnier, from Vulgar Latin *adrationare "to call to account," from Latin ad "to" + rationare "to reason, reckon" (from ratio "reckoning, account, reason").
