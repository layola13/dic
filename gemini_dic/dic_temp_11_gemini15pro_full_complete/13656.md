attornment:/əˈtɜːrnmənt/
n.
承认新业主;承认新地主;转让租赁物所有权的契约
The act of a tenant acknowledging a new landlord.  The transfer of ownership of leased property.
The attornment of the tenants to the new landlord was completed smoothly. 租户对新房东的承认顺利完成。
The attornment clause in the lease protected the tenant's rights. 租约中的承认条款保护了租户的权利。
近义词: acknowledgment, recognition, acceptance
反义词: denial, rejection, repudiation
词性: noun

词源: From Old French *atornement, from aturner ‘to turn to, assign’, from a- (from Latin ad ‘to’) + turner ‘to turn’ (from Latin tornare).
