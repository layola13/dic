infotainments: /ˌɪnfoʊˈteɪnmənts/ | n. | 信息娱乐 |  A blend of "information" and "entertainment," referring to systems, typically in vehicles, that provide both information (like navigation, traffic updates) and entertainment (like music, radio, video). | The car's infotainment system includes a large touchscreen display. 这辆车的信息娱乐系统包括一个大的触摸屏显示器。 |  multimedia systems, in-car entertainment | N/A | noun
