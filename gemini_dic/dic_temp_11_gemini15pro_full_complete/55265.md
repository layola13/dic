dissipatedly:/ˈdɪsɪpeɪtɪdli/
adverb
放荡地，挥霍无度地
In a dissipated manner; characterized by excessive indulgence in pleasure or vice.
He lived dissipatedly, squandering his fortune on wine, women, and song. 他过着放荡的生活，把自己的财产挥霍在酒、女人和歌曲上。
The young heir dissipatedly gambled away his inheritance.  这位年轻的继承人挥霍无度地赌博，输光了遗产。

近义词:  profligately, wantonly, recklessly, extravagantly, intemperately
反义词:  temperately, moderately, frugally, prudently, responsibly
