charmander:/ˈtʃɑːrmændər/| n.|小火龙|A fictional creature, a fire-type Pokémon from the Pokémon franchise. It is a small, lizard-like Pokémon that has a flame burning at the tip of its tail.  This flame is an indicator of its health and mood.|Charmander is the first Pokémon that Ash catches in the anime. 小火龙是动画中小智抓到的第一只宝可梦。

Charmander can learn powerful fire moves like Flamethrower. 小火龙可以学习像喷射火焰这样强大的火系招式。|近义词： (As a fire-type Pokémon) Cyndaquil, Torchich, Litten|反义词: (As a fire-type Pokémon) Squirtle, Totodile, Piplup |词性:noun
