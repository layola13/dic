insinuating:/ɪnˈsɪnjuːeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
暗示的，旁敲侧击的，谄媚的
Suggesting or implying something bad or unpleasant in an indirect way.
She made some insinuating remarks about his past. 她旁敲侧击地评论了他的过去。
synonyms: suggestive, implying, hinting, intimate, innuendo
antonyms: direct, forthright, blunt, explicit
词性: 形容词

insinuate:/ɪnˈsɪnjuːeɪt/
verb
暗示，旁敲侧击地指出，潜入，钻营
To suggest or hint (something bad or reprehensible) in an indirect and unpleasant way.
He insinuated that she had cheated on the test. 他暗示她在考试中作弊了。
synonyms: imply, suggest, hint, intimate
antonyms: declare, state, assert
词性: 动词
