bassorin:/ˈbæsərɪn/| n.|巴索林，树胶质|A complex gummy carbohydrate found in gum tragacanth. It swells in water but does not dissolve.|Bassorin is used as a thickening agent in food and pharmaceuticals. 巴索林在食品和药品中用作增稠剂。|Bassorin is a key component of gum tragacanth, giving it its unique viscous properties. 巴索林是黄蓍胶的关键成分，赋予其独特的粘性。|Synonyms: tragacanthin, gum tragacanth|Antonyms: N/A|noun
