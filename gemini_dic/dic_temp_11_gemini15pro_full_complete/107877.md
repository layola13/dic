largifluous: /lɑːrˈdʒɪfluːəs/
adjective
慷慨的；大量的；丰富的
Characterized by profuse or copious flow; flowing freely.  Often used to describe language or style as being extravagant or overly embellished.
The largifluous prose of the novelist filled pages with elaborate descriptions.  这位小说家丰富的散文充满了详尽的描述。
Synonyms: copious, abundant, profuse, effusive, extravagant
Antonyms: sparse, meager, scanty, terse, concise
词性: adjective
