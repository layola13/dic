dopeynesses:/ˈdoʊpinəsɪz/
noun
愚蠢；迟钝；麻木；昏沉
The state of being dopey; sluggishness, dullness, or stupidity.
His dopeyness made it difficult for him to follow the complex instructions. 他的迟钝让他难以理解复杂的指令。
Near Synonyms:  stupidity, dullness, sluggishness, apathy, lethargy, torpor
Antonyms:  intelligence, alertness, energy, vivacity
词性: 名词
