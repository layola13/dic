The nonvaporosities of the substance make it suitable for use in high-temperature environments.  这种物质的非挥发性使其适合在高温环境中使用。
Etymology: non- (not) + vapor (steam, gas) + -osity (forming abstract nouns of quality)
Example: The nonvaporosities of the compound are important for its stability.  该化合物的非挥发性对其稳定性很重要。
