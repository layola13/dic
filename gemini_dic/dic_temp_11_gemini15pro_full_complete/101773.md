irretrievably:/ˌɪrɪˈtriːvəbli/
adv.
不能挽回地，无可挽回地
In a way that cannot be retrieved or put right.
The papers were irretrievably lost. 这些文件无可挽回地丢失了。
近义词: irrevocably, permanently, irreparably
反义词: retrievably, recoverably
词性: 副词

irretrievably:/ˌɪrɪˈtriːvəbli/
词源:  From ir- "not" + retrievable (from retrieve, from Old French retrover "find again") + -ly.  Essentially meaning "not able to be found or recovered again".
中文翻译:  不能挽回地；无可挽回地
英文解析: In a way that is impossible to recover or regain; permanently lost.
例句: The data was irretrievably lost when the hard drive crashed.  硬盘崩溃时，数据已无可挽回地丢失。
例句:  His reputation was irretrievably damaged by the scandal. 他的名誉因丑闻而无可挽回地受损。
近义词: permanently, irrevocably, irreparably
反义词: retrievably, recoverably, reversibly
词性: adverb
