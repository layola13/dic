infusion:/ɪnˈfjuːʒən/| n.|灌输，注入，浸泡，泡制(的饮料)|The introduction of new ideas or practices. The act of steeping or soaking something in liquid to extract its flavor or medicinal properties.  |例句：The infusion of new talent into the team improved morale. 新人才的注入提高了团队士气。

|例句：She enjoys a relaxing infusion of herbal tea in the evening. 她晚上喜欢喝一杯放松身心的凉茶。|近义词：injection, introduction, insertion, immersion, steeping|反义词:extraction, removal|noun

infuse:/ɪnˈfjuːz/| vt.|灌输，注入，使充满|To fill something or someone with a particular quality or feeling. To steep or soak something in liquid to extract its flavor or medicinal properties. |例句：The coach infused the team with confidence. 教练给球队注入了信心。

|例句：She infused the water with sliced cucumbers and mint. 她在水中加入了黄瓜片和薄荷。|近义词：instill, imbue, inject, permeate, steep|反义词:drain, extract|verb
