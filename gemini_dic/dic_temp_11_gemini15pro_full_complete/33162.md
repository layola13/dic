chromatoscope: /krəˈmætəskoʊp/ /krəˈmætəskəʊp/
noun|色谱仪，色度计|An instrument for exhibiting colours, or analyzing light by separating it into its component wavelengths, especially one in which a picture is produced by combining lights of various colours.|The scientist used a chromatoscope to analyze the spectrum of the star. 科学家用色谱仪分析了这颗恒星的光谱。|spectroscope, colorimeter| - |noun
Etymology:  From Greek *chrōma* (color) + *-skopion* (instrument for viewing)
