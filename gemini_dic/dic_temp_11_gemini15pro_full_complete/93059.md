idoling: /ˈaɪdəlɪŋ/ | n. | 偶像崇拜；无所事事；怠速运转 | The act of excessively admiring or obsessing over someone, often a celebrity or public figure. Also refers to wasting time or the act of a vehicle's engine running slowly while stationary. | She spends hours idoling over her favorite pop star. 她花了几个小时崇拜她最喜欢的流行歌星。 | Synonyms: worshipping, adoring, fawning, lazing, loafing | Antonyms: working, ignoring, neglecting | noun


idoling: /ˈaɪdəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 闲散的；空转的 | Being inactive or unproductive; running an engine slowly while stationary. | The idoling engine wasted a lot of fuel. 怠速运转的发动机浪费了大量燃料。| Synonyms: inactive, unproductive, lazy, stationary | Antonyms: active, productive, busy, moving | adjective


Etymology: From the verb "idle" + "-ing" suffix.  "Idle" comes from Old English "īdel" meaning "empty, vain, useless."
