directly:/dɪˈrektli/ /daɪˈrektli/| adv.|直接地，立即，坦率地| without anything else being involved or in between.  In a straight line or the shortest route.|
例句：
He went directly to the airport. 他直接去了机场。

The sunlight fell directly on her face. 阳光直射在她的脸上。

近义词：immediately, straight, instantly, frankly, openly
反义词:indirectly, circuitously

词性:副词
