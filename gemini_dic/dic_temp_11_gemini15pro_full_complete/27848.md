canonising:/ˈkænənaɪzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkænəˌnaɪzɪŋ/ (US)
|v.|册封为圣徒，使经典化|The act of declaring someone a saint or officially recognizing a religious text as genuine and authoritative. |The Church is considering canonising Mother Teresa. 教会正在考虑册封特蕾莎修女为圣徒。|The process of canonising a literary work often involves extensive scholarly debate.  将一部文学作品经典化的过程通常涉及广泛的学术争论。| 近义词: sanctifying, glorifying, enshrining, consecrating | 反义词: deconsecrating, denouncing, condemning |词性: 动词(verb)
