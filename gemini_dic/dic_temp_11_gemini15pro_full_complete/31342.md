characterism: /ˈkærəktərɪzəm/ | n. | 特性，特征，特色 | A distinguishing feature or quality.  A characteristic is a more commonly used word. |  This vibrant color is a characterism of his paintings.  这种鲜艳的色彩是他绘画的一个特点。 | Synonym: characteristic, feature, trait, attribute | Antonym:  The word "characterism" doesn't have a direct antonym, but one could consider words that represent a lack of distinguishing features like "commonality," "ordinariness," or "blandness." | noun
