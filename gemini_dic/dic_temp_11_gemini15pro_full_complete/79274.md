glauberite:/ˈɡlɔːbəraɪt/| n. |芒硝|A mineral consisting of hydrous sodium calcium sulfate, Na2Ca(SO4)2. It is typically found in evaporite deposits.|Glauberite is often found alongside other evaporite minerals like gypsum and halite.|近义词:bloedite, polyhalite|反义词: N/A|名词
