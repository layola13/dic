如果您想了解单词 "what" 的用法，我可以提供以下信息：

what: /wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American) | pronoun, determiner, adverb | 什么，多么 | used to ask for information about something |
例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

例句：What do you want? 你想要什么？

近义词: which, how, whatever
反义词: (What 没有直接的反义词)
词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you usually eat for breakfast? 你早餐通常吃什么？
What does he do for a living? 他以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner?  她晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has he done to his hair? 他对他的头发做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading this week? 她这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you eat for lunch yesterday? 你昨天午饭吃了什么？
What did she say to you? 她对你说了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing last night at 8 pm? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party?  她参加聚会穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she told him before the meeting? 在会议之前她告诉他什么了？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the accident? 事故发生前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  在她换专业之前，她一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? 你下周末要做什么？
What will he bring to the picnic? 他会带什么去野餐？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be wearing to the wedding? 她婚礼上会穿什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have accomplished by next year? 到明年你会完成什么？
What will he have learned by the end of the course?  到课程结束时，他将学到什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习什么多久了？



PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What the heck? = 搞什么鬼！（语气比 what the hell 轻一些）
What if...? = 如果...会怎么样？
What for? = 为了什么？
So what? = 那又怎样？
