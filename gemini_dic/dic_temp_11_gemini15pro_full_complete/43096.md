cowweed:/ˈkaʊˌwiːd/| n. |紫茎泽兰;飞机草|A tall, weedy plant (Erigeron canadensis) with numerous small white flower heads, native to North and Central America and naturalized elsewhere as a weed.|The fields were overrun with cowweed.|Synonyms: horseweed, Canadian fleabane, butterweed|Antonyms: N/A|noun
