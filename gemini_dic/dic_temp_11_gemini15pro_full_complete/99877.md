intern:/ˈɪntɜːrn/ (US) /ˈɪntɜːn/ (UK)| n.  |实习生，见习生 |A student or recent graduate who works for a period of time in order to gain experience in a particular field.| She worked as an intern at a law firm during the summer. 她夏天在一家律师事务所实习。|Synonyms: trainee, apprentice, probationer|Antonyms: experienced worker, professional, expert|noun

intern:/ɪnˈtɜːrn/ (US) /ɪnˈtɜːn/ (UK)| vt.  |拘留，扣留|To confine someone, especially during a war.| Many enemy aliens were interned during World War II.  二战期间许多敌国侨民被拘留。|Synonyms: detain, confine, imprison|Antonyms: release, liberate, free|verb
