auditorily:/ˌɔːdɪˈtɔːrɪli/| adv.|听觉上地，通过听觉|Perceived or experienced through hearing.  She learns auditorily, meaning she retains information best when she hears it. 她学习是通过听觉的，这意味着当她听到信息时，她能更好地记住它。|Synonym: acoustically, by ear|Antonym: visually|词性:副词
