gulpier:/ˈɡʌlpiər/ | adj. | 更贪婪的，更轻信的 | More gullible; more easily tricked or cheated. | He's even gulpier than his brother, believing every tall tale he's told. 他比他哥哥更容易受骗，对他说的每一个荒诞的故事都信以为真。 |  He fell for the scam because he was so gulpier. 他上当受骗了，因为他太容易轻信了。 | 近义词: more credulous, more naive, more trusting | 反义词: more skeptical, more cynical, more discerning | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From gulp (verb) + -y (adjective suffix) + -er (comparative suffix).  "Gulp"  imitates the sound of swallowing large amounts quickly, implying a certain eagerness or lack of caution.
