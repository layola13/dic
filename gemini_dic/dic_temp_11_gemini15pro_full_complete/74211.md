fourteens:/ˈfɔːrˈtiːnz/
noun
十四（fourteen的复数形式）
The number fourteen repeated multiple times or a group of fourteen items.
He counted by fourteens. 他以十四为单位计数。
The bakery sold cookies in packs of fourteens. 这家面包店出售十四个一包的饼干。
