arrowless:/ˈærəʊləs/ (UK) /ˈɛroʊləs/ (US)| adj.|无箭的，没有箭的|Lacking arrows; without arrows.  This describes something that normally has or uses arrows, but currently doesn't. |The arrowless quiver hung uselessly on the wall.  墙上挂着无箭的箭袋，毫无用处。|The archer found himself arrowless in the midst of the battle. 那位弓箭手发现自己在战斗中没有了箭。|Near synonyms: unarmed, defenseless|Near antonyms: armed, equipped|adjective
