outbirth:/ˈaʊtbɜːθ/
n.
出生，起源，成果

The novel is an outburst of her creativity. 这部小说是她创造力的成果。
synonyms: birth, origin, genesis, inception, beginning
antonyms: death, end, demise, conclusion
