dichroscope:/ˈdaɪkrəˌskoʊp/| n.|二色镜|A pocket-sized instrument used in mineralogy to examine the pleochroism of gemstones, by transmitting polarized light through them in two directions at right angles.|Gemologists use a dichroscope to determine if a gemstone is doubly refractive. 宝石学家使用二色镜来确定宝石是否是双折射的。

The jeweler used a dichroscope to verify the authenticity of the emerald. 珠宝商用二色镜验证祖母绿的真伪。|近义词:polariscope|反义词:None|词性:noun
