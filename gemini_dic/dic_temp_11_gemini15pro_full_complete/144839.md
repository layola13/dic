over-manage:/ˌoʊvərˈmænɪdʒ/| v. |过度管理，管理过细|To manage too closely or excessively, often stifling creativity and initiative.|例句：He over-managed the project, leading to demotivation among the team. 他对项目管理过细，导致团队成员失去动力。

例句：By over-managing her children, she inadvertently hindered their development of independence.  由于过度管理孩子，她无意中阻碍了他们独立性的发展。|近义词：micromanage, over-control|反义词: under-manage, neglect|词性:动词
