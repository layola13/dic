disacceptance:/ˌdɪsəkˈseptəns/
n.
不接受；拒收；拒付
lack of acceptance; rejection.
The disacceptance of the offer caused a delay in the project.  这个提议的拒收导致了项目的延误。

His disacceptance of the award shocked everyone. 他拒绝领奖让每个人都感到震惊。

近义词: rejection, refusal, decline, denial
反义词: acceptance, approval, adoption
