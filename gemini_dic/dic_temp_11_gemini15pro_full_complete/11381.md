arete:/ˈærəteɪ/ /əˈreɪti/ | n. | 美德，卓越，优秀，山脊，刃岭 |  Excellence of any kind, especially moral excellence.  In mountaineering, a sharp mountain ridge. | He demonstrated arete in his handling of the difficult situation.  他处理困境的方式展现了他的美德。 The climbers traversed a narrow arete.  登山者们穿过了一条狭窄的山脊。 | 近义词：excellence, virtue, merit, prowess, ridge, crest | 反义词：mediocrity, weakness, deficiency, valley | 词性: 名词

词源: From Ancient Greek ἀρετή (aretḗ, “excellence”).
