dichogamous:/daɪˈkɒɡəməs/ | adj. | 雌雄异熟的 | Having the male and female reproductive organs mature at different times, thus preventing self-fertilization. |  Many orchid species are dichogamous, which promotes cross-pollination. 许多兰花种类是雌雄异熟的，这促进了异花授粉。

| Example 2: Dichogamy is a common strategy in plants to avoid inbreeding. 雌雄异熟是植物避免近亲繁殖的常见策略。 | Synonym:  heterogamous | Antonym: homogamous | Word type: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *dikhos* (double, twofold) + *gamos* (marriage).
