baleless:/ˈbeɪllɪs/| adj.|无包的，无捆的|Lacking bales; not formed into bales.|The baleless cotton was difficult to transport. 无包的棉花很难运输。|近义词:loose, unpacked|反义词:baled, packed|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From bale + -less.  "Bale" comes from Old French "bale" meaning "package, bundle," ultimately from Late Latin "balla."  "-less" is a Germanic suffix meaning "without."
