outbabble: /ˌaʊtˈbæbəl/
verb
胜过…的饶舌；比…更健谈；用饶舌压倒
to talk more loudly, longer, or more persuasively than someone else
He outbabbled his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中以滔滔不绝压倒了对手。
Synonyms: outtalk, outargue
Antonyms: be quiet, listen
