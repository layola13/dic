huck:/hʌk/| v. |兜售；叫卖；努力推销|To peddle or hawk goods, especially aggressively. |例句：He was hucking his wares on the street corner. 他在街角兜售他的商品。

例句：She hucked the magazines door-to-door. 她挨家挨户地推销杂志。|近义词：peddle, hawk, tout, vend|反义词: buy, purchase|词性:动词
