desiccator:/ˈdɛsɪkeɪtər/ | n. |干燥器；干燥剂 | a closed container in which something can be dried and kept dry | Silica gel is a common desiccant used in desiccators. 硅胶是干燥器中常用的干燥剂。 |  The botanist kept rare plant specimens in a desiccator to preserve them. 植物学家将稀有植物标本放在干燥器中保存。 | 近义词: dehydrator, drier | 反义词: humidifier | 词性: noun

词源: From Latin *desiccatus*, past participle of desiccare (“to dry up”), from de- (“thoroughly”) + siccare (“to dry”), from siccus (“dry”).
