frutescent:/fruːˈtɛsənt/| adj.| 灌木状的，亚灌木的| Resembling a shrub; shrubby. Having the form of a shrub, especially a small shrub. | Example 1:  The frutescent plant grew only a few feet tall, but it was covered in vibrant flowers.  这株灌木状的植物只有几英尺高，但却开满了鲜艳的花朵。

Example 2:  The arid landscape was dotted with frutescent bushes that had adapted to the harsh conditions.  干旱的景观中点缀着适应恶劣环境的灌木丛。 | Near synonyms: shrubby, bushy, fruticose | Antonyms: herbaceous, arboreal, tree-like | Word Type: Adjective
