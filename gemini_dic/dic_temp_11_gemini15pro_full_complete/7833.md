antalkaline:/ˌæntælˈkəlaɪn/
adjective
抗碱的; 中和碱性的
Reduces or neutralizes alkalinity.  This term is less commonly used than "antacid".
Antacids are often antalkaline substances.  抗酸剂通常是抗碱物质。
The medication had an antalkaline effect, reducing the pH of the stomach.  这种药物具有抗碱作用，可以降低胃的pH值。
近义词:antacid, neutralizing, alkaline-neutralizing
反义词:alkaline, basic
词性:adjective
