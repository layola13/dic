n.
不可食性，不能吃
The inedibility of the plant was well known to the locals. 这种植物不能吃是当地人众所周知的。

例句：The chef tested the inedibility of the raw dough. 厨师测试了生面团的不可食用性。


近义词：uneatableness, nonedibility
反义词：edibility
词性：noun
