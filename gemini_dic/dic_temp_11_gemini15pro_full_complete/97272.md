influenceable:/ɪnˈfluːənsəbl/| adj.|易受影响的，可被左右的|Susceptible to being influenced; easily persuaded or swayed.|例句：He is influenceable and tends to follow the crowd. 他很容易受影响，往往会随大流。

例句：Young children are particularly influenceable by their peers. 年幼的孩子特别容易受到同龄人的影响。|近义词：suggestible, impressionable, susceptible, persuadable|反义词：uninfluenceable, resistant, steadfast, independent|词性:形容词
