atoner:/əˈtəʊnər/ (UK) /əˈtoʊnər/ (US)| n.|赎罪者，补偿者|A person who makes amends or reparations for a wrong or injury.|例句：He sought to be an atoner for the sins of his father. 他试图为他父亲的罪孽赎罪。

例句：She dedicated her life to being an atoner for the injustices of the past. 她毕生致力于弥补过去的冤屈。|近义词：expirator, propitiator|反义词: sinner, offender|词性:noun
