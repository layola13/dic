discretized:/dɪˈskriːtaɪzd/| adj.|离散化的，离散的|Converted from continuous values to discrete values.  Transforming a continuous range of values into a set of separate, distinct values.|The data was discretized into age groups. 数据被离散化到不同的年龄组。|The temperature readings were discretized for easier analysis. 温度读数被离散化以便于分析。|近义词：quantized, digitized|反义词: continuous, analog|词性:形容词
