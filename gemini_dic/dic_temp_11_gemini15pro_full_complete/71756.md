flea-marketeers:/ˈfliː mɑːrkɪtərz/
n.
跳蚤市场摊贩，在跳蚤市场卖东西的人
People who sell goods at flea markets.
Many flea-marketeers arrive before dawn to set up their stalls. 许多跳蚤市场摊贩在天亮前就到达，开始摆摊。
The flea-marketeers were haggling with customers over prices. 跳蚤市场摊贩们正在与顾客讨价还价。
vendors, sellers, traders
buyers, customers
