altimeter:/ˈæltɪmiːtər/ | n. |高度计；测高仪|An instrument that measures altitude, especially an aircraft's height above sea level. |The pilot checked the altimeter to ensure they were at the correct cruising altitude. 飞行员检查了高度计，以确保他们在正确的巡航高度。|Synonyms:  hypsometer, altitude gauge |Antonyms: depth gauge, bathymeter|noun
