orthostade: /ˈɔːrθəˌsteɪd/ | n. | 正投影图; 正射投影图 | A drawing of an upright object, such as a building or a column, projected orthogonally.  It represents the true dimensions of the object without perspective distortion. | Example: The architect presented an orthostade of the proposed skyscraper to the city council.  建筑师向市议会提交了拟建摩天大楼的正投影图。

Example: The orthostade clearly showed the intricate details of the ancient column's carvings.  正投影图清晰地展现了古代柱子上雕刻的复杂细节。 | Synonym: elevation, projection | Antonym: perspective drawing | noun
