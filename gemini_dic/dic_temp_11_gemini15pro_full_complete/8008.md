anthozoic:/ˌænθəˈzoʊɪk/
adjective
食花的；以花为食的
Feeding on flowers; flower-eating.
Certain insects are anthozoic, deriving their sustenance from the nectar and pollen of flowers. 某些昆虫以花为食，从花蜜和花粉中获取营养。
近义词:nectarivorous, pollinivorous
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider carnivorous, herbivorous depending on context)
词性:adjective


anthozoic:/ˌænθəˈzoʊɪk/
noun
食花动物
An animal that feeds on flowers.
The anthozoic butterfly flitted among the blossoms.  这只食花蝴蝶在花丛中飞舞。
近义词: nectarivore, pollinivore
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider carnivore, herbivore depending on context)
词性:noun
