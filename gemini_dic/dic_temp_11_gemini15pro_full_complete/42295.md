counter-interrogation:/ˌkaʊntər ɪnˌtɛrəˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. |反讯问，反审讯|The process of questioning someone who has already interrogated you, often to challenge or refute their claims.  It can also refer to techniques used to resist or deflect interrogation.| The suspect used counter-interrogation techniques to confuse the detective. 嫌疑人使用反讯问技巧来迷惑侦探。|Synonyms: cross-examination, counter-questioning | Antonyms: interrogation, questioning | noun
