lancepesade:/ˈlænsɪpəˌseɪd/ /ˈlɑːnsɪpəˌsɑːd/ | n. | 轻骑兵,枪骑兵 | A soldier of a class originating in the 16th century, intermediate between light and heavy cavalry.  A light horseman armed with a lance. |  The lancepesade charged the enemy lines. 轻骑兵向敌军阵线冲锋。 |  The lancepesade held his lance steady. 轻骑兵稳稳地握着他的长枪。 | 近义词：light horseman, lancer, cavalryman | 反义词：foot soldier, infantryman | 词性：noun

词源:

From French *lancepessade*, from Italian *lancia spezzata* (“broken lance”), referring to a light horseman originally armed with a short or broken lance.
