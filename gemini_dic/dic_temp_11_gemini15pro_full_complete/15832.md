balladize:/ˈbælədaɪz/ | v. | 将…改编成民谣；以民谣形式表达 | To compose or rewrite something in the style of a ballad. | He balladized the story of the local hero. 他将当地英雄的故事改编成了民谣。 |  The poet balladized the tragic love story. 诗人将这个悲惨的爱情故事写成了民谣。 |  versify, poetize |  -| verb
ballad:/ˈbæləd/ | n. | 民谣，歌谣 | A poem or song narrating a story in short stanzas. |  She sang a beautiful ballad about a lost love. 她唱了一首关于失恋的优美民谣。 | The band played a traditional Irish ballad. 乐队演奏了一首传统的爱尔兰民谣。 | song, poem, ditty | - | noun
