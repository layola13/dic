interdevouring:/ˌɪntərdiˈvaʊərɪŋ/
adjective
相互吞食的，互相残杀的
Engaging in mutual destruction by consuming one another.
The interdevouring nature of the political factions weakened the country.  这个国家政治派别的相互倾轧使其国力衰弱。
The interdevouring fish in the tank eventually left only one survivor.  鱼缸里互相残杀的鱼最终只剩下一条幸存者。
近义词:cannibalistic, internecine
反义词:cooperative, symbiotic
词性:adjective

词源:
From "inter-" (between, among) + "devouring" (consuming, eating up).  源自"inter-"（在...之间，在...之中）+ "devouring"（消耗，吞噬）。
