fallacies:/ˈfæləsiːz/
noun
谬误，谬论
A mistaken belief, especially one based on unsound argument.
Many people believe the fallacy that money brings happiness. 很多人相信金钱带来幸福的谬论。
synonyms: misconception, delusion, error, untruth, falsehood
antonyms: truth, fact, reality
n.

fallacy:/ˈfæləsi/
noun
谬误，谬论
A mistaken belief, especially one based on unsound argument.
The idea that the earth is flat is a common fallacy.  地球是平的这种想法是一个常见的谬误。
synonyms: misconception, delusion, error, untruth, falsehood
antonyms: truth, fact, reality
n.
