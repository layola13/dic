exudate: /ˈɛksjʊdeɪt/ | n. | 渗出物；分泌物 | A substance that has slowly oozed out from a plant or animal tissue, typically as a result of disease or injury. |  The wound was covered in a thick, yellow exudate.  伤口上覆盖着一层厚厚的黄色渗出物。 | 近义词: discharge, secretion, effusion | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "absorption") | noun

exudate: /ɛksˈjuːdeɪt/ | v. | 渗出；分泌 |  To discharge (a substance) slowly and steadily. | The tree exuded a sticky resin. 这棵树分泌出粘稠的树脂。 | 近义词:  secrete, ooze, discharge | 反义词: absorb | verb
