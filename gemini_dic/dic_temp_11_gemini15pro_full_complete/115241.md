manilla:/məˈnɪlə/ | n. | 马尼拉麻, 马尼拉纸, 马尼拉雪茄 | A strong fiber obtained from the leaves of a Philippine plant (Musa textilis), used for making rope, paper, etc. Also refers to paper or cigars made from this fiber. |  The sailors used manilla rope to secure the ship. 水手们用马尼拉麻绳固定船只。

Manilla envelopes are quite durable. 马尼拉纸信封非常耐用。 | synonym: abaca, Manila hemp | antonym: N/A | noun

manilla:/məˈnɪlə/ | adj. | 马尼拉的，用马尼拉麻制成的 | Made of or relating to Manila hemp. | He bought a manilla folder for his documents. 他买了一个马尼拉纸文件夹来装他的文件。

The manilla rope was strong enough to hold the weight.  这马尼拉麻绳足够结实，可以承受住重量。 | synonym: abaca, hemp | antonym: N/A | adjective
