oligospermous: /ˌɒlɪɡəʊˈspɜːməs/ /ˌɑːlɪɡoʊˈspɜːrməs/
adjective
少精子的，精子减少的
Having a low concentration of sperm in the semen.
Example: The doctor informed him that he was oligospermous, which could make conception difficult. 医生告诉他，他患有少精症，这可能会使受孕变得困难。

Example:  Oligospermous men may face challenges in fathering children naturally. 少精症男性可能在自然生育孩子方面面临挑战。

近义词: hypofertile, subfertile
反义词: fertile, polyspermous
词性: adjective
