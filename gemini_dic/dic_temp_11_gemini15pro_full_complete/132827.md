nonecumenical:/ˌnɒnˌɛkjuːˈmɛnɪk(ə)l/
adjective
非普世教会的；不具普世性的
Not ecumenical; not promoting or tending toward worldwide Christian unity or cooperation.
The conference focused on nonecumenical issues specific to the denomination.  这次会议集中讨论了该教派特有的非普世性问题。
His nonecumenical views caused friction within the church. 他的非普世性观点在教会内部引起了摩擦。
Synonyms: sectarian, denominational, parochial
Antonyms: ecumenical, universal, catholic
词性: 形容词
