croaked:/kroʊkt/| v. |发出低沉沙哑的声音；呱呱叫；暴毙；惨败|To make a deep hoarse sound, like a frog or a raven; to die; to fail miserably. |The old man croaked a greeting. 老人低沉沙哑地打了个招呼。

The frog croaked by the pond. 青蛙在池塘边呱呱叫。

"I'm dying," he croaked. “我要死了，”他沙哑地说。

The business croaked after only a year. 这家公司才一年就倒闭了。|近义词：rasp, wheeze, growl, die, fail|反义词:thrive, succeed|词性:动词

croaked:/krəʊkt/| adj. |沙哑的|Having a deep hoarse voice.|He had a croaked voice from shouting too much. 他喊得太多，嗓子都沙哑了。|近义词：hoarse, raspy|反义词:clear, smooth|词性:形容词
