overoptimist: /ˌoʊvərˈɑːptɪmɪst/ | n. | 过度乐观者；盲目乐观者 | Someone who is excessively optimistic, often to an unrealistic degree. |  He was such an overoptimist that he invested all his savings in a risky venture. 他是一个过度乐观的人，把所有的积蓄都投资到了一项风险很大的企业中。| 近义词: Pollyanna, utopian | 反义词: pessimist, cynic | 词性: noun

overoptimistic: /ˌoʊvərˌɑːptɪˈmɪstɪk/ | adj. | 过度乐观的；盲目乐观的 | Excessively optimistic, often to an unrealistic degree. | Her overoptimistic projections for sales growth proved to be inaccurate. 她对销售增长的过度乐观预测被证明是不准确的。 | 近义词: excessively optimistic, unrealistically positive | 反义词: pessimistic, realistic | 词性: adjective
