forewheel:/ˈfɔːrˌwiːl/| n. |前轮|The forewheel of the bicycle was buckled. |The front wheel of a vehicle, especially a bicycle or motorcycle. |例句：

The forewheel of his bike got stuck in a rut.
他的自行车前轮卡在了车辙里。

The impact bent the forewheel of the motorcycle.
撞击使摩托车的前轮弯曲了。

|近义词：front wheel|反义词:rear wheel, back wheel|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From fore- + wheel.  "Fore-" comes from Old English "fore," meaning "before, in front." "Wheel" comes from Old English "hweol,"  meaning "wheel, circle."
