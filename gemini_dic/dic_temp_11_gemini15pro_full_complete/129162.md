no-show:/ˈnoʊ ʃoʊ/
n.| 缺席者；未出现的人；预订后未入住的客人 | a person who fails to arrive for an appointment, performance, etc., especially after having made a reservation |  The restaurant was full of no-shows last night. 昨晚这家餐馆有很多预订了却没来的人。| absentee, defaulter | attendee, present | noun

no-show:/ˈnoʊ ʃoʊ/
adj.| 缺席的；未出现的 | failing to keep an appointment or reservation | The no-show rate for the conference was surprisingly high.  这次会议的缺席率出奇地高。 | absent, missing | present, attending | adjective
