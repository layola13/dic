cupola:/ˈkjuːpələ/ (US), /ˈkjuːpələ/ (UK)| n.|穹顶，圆顶，小圆屋|A small dome, especially one forming a roof or ceiling over a circular or polygonal area.  A cupola can also refer to a furnace for melting metals.|The cupola atop the town hall offered a stunning view. 市政厅顶上的穹顶可以欣赏到迷人的景色。|Synonyms: dome, turret, rotunda|Antonyms: base, foundation|noun


cupola:/ˈkjuːpələ/ (US), /ˈkjuːpələ/ (UK)| n.|熔铁炉|A furnace used for melting metals, especially iron, in a foundry.|Molten iron flowed from the cupola into the molds. 熔化的铁水从熔铁炉流入模具中。|Synonyms: furnace, smelter|Antonyms: N/A|noun
