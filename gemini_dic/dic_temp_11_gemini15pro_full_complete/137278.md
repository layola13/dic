nonsymptomatic:/ˌnɒnsɪmptəˈmætɪk/ (US) /ˌnɒnsɪmptəˈmætɪk/ (UK)| adj.|无症状的|Not showing or experiencing symptoms of a disease, even though the disease may be present.| Many people with COVID-19 are nonsymptomatic, which makes it difficult to control the spread. 许多新冠肺炎患者是无症状的，这使得控制传播变得困难。|例句：

The study found a significant number of nonsymptomatic carriers of the virus.
这项研究发现了大量的病毒无症状携带者。

She was nonsymptomatic but still tested positive for the disease.
她没有症状，但疾病检测结果仍然呈阳性。

|近义词：asymptomatic|反义词: symptomatic|词性: adjective
