oarsweed: /ˈɔːrzwiːd/ | n. | 桨草，一种生长在水中的大型藻类 | A large seaweed, typically kelp, that can become entangled in oars.  It often refers to any seaweed that impedes rowing. | The oarsweed was so thick around the boat that they could barely row. 桨草在船周围太厚了，他们几乎划不动。| The oarsweed made it difficult to navigate the narrow channel. 桨草使得在狭窄的航道中航行变得困难。 | synonyms: kelp, seaweed, sea tangle | antonyms: N/A | noun
