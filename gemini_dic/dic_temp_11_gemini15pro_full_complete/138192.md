nosebanded:/ˈnoʊzbændɪd/| adj.|戴着鼻羁的，被束缚的|Having a noseband (a strap that goes around the nose of a horse, usually part of a bridle) fitted. |The nosebanded horse tossed its head impatiently. 戴着鼻羁的马不耐烦地甩了甩头。|近义词：restrained, bridled|反义词:unbridled, unrestrained|词性:形容词
词源: nose (鼻子) + band (带子) + -ed (形容词后缀，表示“戴着…的”)
