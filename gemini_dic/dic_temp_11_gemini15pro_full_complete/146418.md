overfondness:/ˌoʊvərˈfɑːndnəs/ | n. | 过分喜爱，溺爱 | Excessive fondness or affection; doting. | She displayed an overfondness for sugary treats. 她对甜食表现出过分的喜爱。|  His overfondness for his dog was well known.  他对他的狗的溺爱是众所周知的。 | 近义词: doting, indulgence,  partiality | 反义词: aversion, dislike, indifference | 词性: noun

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer-, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above, beyond") + "fondness" (from "fond" (Middle English *fonnen "to be foolish," from Old English *fonnian "to be foolish," of unknown origin) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives))
