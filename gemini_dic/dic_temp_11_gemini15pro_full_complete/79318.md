gleamy:/ˈɡliːmi/| adj.|闪亮的，光亮的|Shining brightly; lustrous.|The car had a gleaming new paint job. 这辆车拥有闪亮的新漆。|近义词：shiny, bright, sparkling, glistening|反义词：dull, matte, tarnished|词性:形容词

Etymology: From gleam + -y.  "Gleam" comes from Old English *glæm, from Proto-Germanic *glaimaz, related to Old Norse gleima "to gleam." The suffix "-y" forms adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of."
