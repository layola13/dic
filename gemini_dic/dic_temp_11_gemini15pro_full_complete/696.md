abseil: /ˈæbseɪl/ (UK), /əˈbseɪl/ (US) | vi. vt. n. | 绳降；绕绳下降 | To descend a rock face or other near-vertical surface by using a doubled rope coiled around the body and fixed at a higher point.  | The climbers abseiled down the cliff face.  登山者们沿崖壁绳降。 |  近义词: rappel, descend, lower | 反义词: ascend, climb | 词性: verb, noun

abseil: /ˈæbseɪl/ (UK), /əˈbseɪl/ (US) | n. | 绳降 | The act of abseiling. |  The abseil down the waterfall was exhilarating. 瀑布下的绳降令人振奋。 | 近义词: rappel, descent | 反义词: ascent, climb | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *abseilen*, from ab-* 'down' + Seil 'rope'.
