lycanthropia:/ˌlaɪkənˈθrəʊpiə/ (US) /ˌlɪkənˈθrəʊpiə/ (UK)| n. |狼人化；变形狂|The clinical term for the mythical ability to transform into a wolf, or a belief that one has done so.  It also refers to a form of madness involving the delusion that one is a wolf.| He suffered from lycanthropy and truly believed he could change into a wolf.|Synonyms: shape-shifting, therianthropy|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
