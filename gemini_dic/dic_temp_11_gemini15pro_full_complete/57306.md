downsteepy:/ˈdaʊnˌstiːpi/| adj.|陡峭向下倾斜的|Characterized by a steep downward slope.  Describing a terrain that slopes sharply downwards.|The downsteepy path was treacherous to navigate. 这条陡峭向下倾斜的小路走起来很危险。|The downsteepy hillside made it difficult to climb.  陡峭向下倾斜的山坡使攀登变得困难。|近义词：precipitous, steep, sheer|反义词:gentle, gradual|词性:形容词

词源: "downsteepy" is a descriptive compound word formed from "down" and "steepy,"  "down" indicating a downward direction and "steepy" being an adjectival form of "steep" referring to a sharp incline.
