imperiling:/ɪmˈpɛrəlɪŋ/
verb
使处于危险，危及
putting someone or something in danger
The rising floodwaters were imperiling the town.  上涨的洪水正危及着这个城镇。
Synonyms: endangering, jeopardizing, threatening
Antonyms: protecting, safeguarding
词性: 动词
