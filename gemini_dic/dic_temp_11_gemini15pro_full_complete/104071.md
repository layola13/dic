jumblingly:/ˈdʒʌmblɪŋli/
adverb
混乱地，杂乱地
in a jumbled or confused manner.
The toys were scattered jumblingly across the floor. 玩具杂乱地散落在地板上。
The thoughts jumbled jumblingly in his head. 各种想法在他脑子里乱成一团。
confusedly, disorderly, haphazardly
orderly, neatly, methodically
词性: 副词
