chameleon:/kəˈmiːliən/ |n.|变色龙; 见风使舵的人;|A lizard that changes skin colour to match what surrounds it. A person who changes their behaviour or opinions according to the situation.|The chameleon blended in perfectly with the leaves. 变色龙完美地融入树叶中。 He's a political chameleon, changing his views to suit the prevailing opinion. 他是个政治变色龙，见风使舵。|Synonyms: adapter, conformist|Antonyms: nonconformist, maverick|noun
