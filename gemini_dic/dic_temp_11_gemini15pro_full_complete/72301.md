flour:/ˈflaʊər/ (US), /ˈflaʊə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 面粉；粉状物质 |A fine powder obtained by grinding grain, especially wheat, and used to make bread, cakes, and pastry.|She used two cups of flour to make the cake. 她用了两杯面粉做蛋糕。|Synonyms: meal, powder|Antonyms: grain, whole wheat|noun
