gulfs:/ɡʌlfs/ | n. | 海湾;深渊;分歧 | a deep inlet of the sea almost surrounded by land, with a narrow mouth.  A very large difference or division between two people or groups, or between viewpoints, concepts, etc. | The boat sailed into the gulf.  船驶进了海湾。|近义词：bay, abyss, chasm, divide |反义词: closure, agreement, unity | 词性: 名词

gulfs:/ɡʌlfs/ | v. | 吞噬;吞没 | (of water) flow over and submerge (land).  (of a large amount or number of things) engulf (someone or something). | The rising tide gulfed the beach.  上涨的潮水吞没了海滩。|近义词：engulf, swallow, inundate |反义词: disgorge, expel | 词性: 动词
