callously:/ˈkæləsli/| adv.|无情地，冷酷地，麻木不仁地|Showing or having an insensitive and cruel disregard for others.|He callously ignored her pleas for help. 他无情地无视了她寻求帮助的请求。|例句：She spoke callously about the suffering of the refugees. 她冷漠地谈论难民的苦难。

例句：He callously dismissed their concerns. 他冷酷地驳回了他们的担忧。|近义词：heartlessly, cruelly, unfeelingly|反义词：compassionately, kindly, sympathetically|词性:副词
