nonefficacies:/ˌnɒnˈɛfɪkəsiz/ | n. |无效; 无能; 无效率| Lack of effectiveness or efficacy; the state of being ineffective. | The nonefficacies of the proposed solutions led to further frustration. 提出的解决方案无效导致了进一步的失望. | The nonefficacies of the new drug were apparent in the clinical trials. 这种新药在临床试验中效果不佳.| 近义词: inefficacy, ineffectiveness, impotence, futility | 反义词: efficacy, effectiveness, potency | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "efficacy" (from Late Latin *efficacia*, from Latin *efficax* "effective").
