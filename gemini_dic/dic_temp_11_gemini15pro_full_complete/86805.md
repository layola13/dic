hemathermal:/ˌhiːməˈθɜːrməl/ /ˌheməˈθɜːrməl/ | adj. | 温血的，恒温的 |  Maintaining a relatively constant body temperature regardless of the surrounding environment.  Relating to or characteristic of a warm-blooded animal. | Example 1: Humans are hemathermal creatures, able to regulate their internal temperature. 人类是恒温动物，能够调节自身的内部温度。

Example 2: Hemathermal animals, like birds and mammals, can thrive in a wider range of climates. 像鸟类和哺乳动物这样的恒温动物，可以在更广泛的气候范围内生存。 | 近义词: homeothermic, endothermic | 反义词: poikilothermic, ectothermic | 词性: adjective


Etymology:  From Greek *haima* (blood) + *thermē* (heat).
