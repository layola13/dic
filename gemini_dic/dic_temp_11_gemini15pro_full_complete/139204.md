objective:/əbˈdʒektɪv/ | adj. n. | 客观的；目标；物镜 | Based on facts rather than feelings or opinions; a goal or aim |  The journalist tried to remain objective in her reporting. 记者努力在报道中保持客观。 | synonyms: unbiased, impartial, fair, neutral | antonyms: subjective, biased, prejudiced | adjective

objective:/əbˈdʒektɪv/ | n. | 目标；物镜 | A goal or aim; the lens in a telescope or microscope that is closest to the object being viewed |  Her primary objective was to secure funding for the project. 她的主要目标是确保项目资金。 | synonyms: goal, aim, target, purpose | antonyms: subjectivity | noun
