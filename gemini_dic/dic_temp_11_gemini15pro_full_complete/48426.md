deduce:/dɪˈduːs/
verb
推断，演绎
To reach a conclusion from something known or assumed.
Sherlock Holmes deduced the killer's identity from the evidence.  夏洛克·福尔摩斯从证据中推断出凶手的身份。
infer, conclude, reason, gather
surmise, guess, speculate
v.

deducing:/dɪˈduːsɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
正在推断，正在演绎
The act of reasoning from known facts or principles.
He is deducing the answer from the given information.  他正在从给定的信息中推断答案。
inferring, concluding, reasoning
guessing, speculating, surmising
v.
