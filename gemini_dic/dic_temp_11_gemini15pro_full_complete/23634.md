branchiura: /bræŋˈkɪʊrə/
n.
鳃尾类
A subclass of crustaceans characterized by a broad, shield-like carapace, sessile compound eyes, and a pair of large suckers derived from the second maxillae.  These are fish parasites.
The Branchiura, commonly known as fish lice, are parasites of fish.
近义词: Arguloida (sometimes considered a synonym)
反义词: N/A
词性: Noun


branchiura: /bræŋˈkɪʊrə/
n.
鱼虱
A group of small crustaceans that are parasitic on fish.
Fish lice, belonging to the subclass Branchiura, are common parasites found on both freshwater and marine fish.
近义词: Arguloida, fish lice
反义词: N/A
词性: Noun


Etymology:
From Greek βράγχια (branchia) "gills" + οὐρά (oura) "tail"
