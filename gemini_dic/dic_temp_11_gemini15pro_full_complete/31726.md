cheapener:/ˈtʃiːpənər/
noun
降低价格的人或物; 使…便宜的东西
something that reduces the price of something else.
The introduction of new technology was a cheapener for consumers. 新技术的引入降低了消费者的价格。
synonyms: reducer, discounter
antonyms: increaser, inflater
n.
