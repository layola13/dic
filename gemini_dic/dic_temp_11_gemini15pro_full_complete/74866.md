frescoed:/ˈfrɛskoʊd/ | adj. | 壁画的，绘有壁画的 | Decorated with frescoes. Painted in fresco. | The ceiling was frescoed with scenes from mythology. 天花板上有神话场景的壁画。 | The walls of the church were frescoed with biblical figures. 教堂的墙壁上绘有圣经人物的壁画。 | 近义词:muraled, painted | 反义词:bare, unadorned | 词性:形容词

词源: From Italian *affrescato*, past participle of *affrescare* "to paint in fresco," from *a fresco* "in fresco," from *fresco* "fresh."  Ultimately from Frankish *frisk* "fresh," from Proto-Germanic *friskaz*. Related to Old English *fersc* "fresh."
