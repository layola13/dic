overjacket:/ˈoʊvərˌdʒækɪt/| n. |外套，罩衣|A jacket worn over other clothes, especially for protection from cold or rain. |例句：He put on his overjacket before going out into the cold. 他出门前穿上了外套。

例句：She wore a stylish overjacket over her dress. 她在连衣裙外面穿了一件时尚的外套。|近义词：coat, jacket, topcoat|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps undershirt if considering layering)|词性:noun
