defluoridations:/diːˌflʊərɪˈdeɪʃənz/

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix indicating removal or reversal) + fluoridation (the process of adding fluoride to something).  "Fluoridation" itself comes from  "fluoride" + "-ation" (suffix forming nouns of action or process).  "Fluoride" ultimately derives from "fluorine" (the element) + "-ide" (suffix forming nouns denoting binary compounds).
