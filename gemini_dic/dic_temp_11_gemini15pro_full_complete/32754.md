chloruret:/ˈklɔːraɪt/ |n.| 氯化物; 漂白剂 |A compound of chlorine with another element or radical, especially a salt of the anion Cl− or an organic compound containing chlorine bonded to an alkyl group.  |Sodium chloruret is commonly known as table salt. 氯化钠通常被称为食盐。|Bleach is a chloruret solution used for whitening clothes. 漂白剂是一种用于漂白衣物的氯化物溶液。|synonyms: chloride, bleach (when referring to a bleaching solution)|antonyms: N/A|noun
