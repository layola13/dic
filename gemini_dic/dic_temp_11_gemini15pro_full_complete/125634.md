mumblingly:/ˈmʌmblɪŋli/
adv.
含糊地，咕哝地
in a low, indistinct manner, as if unable or unwilling to speak clearly.
She answered mumblingly, barely audible. 她含糊地回答，几乎听不见。
近义词: indistinctly, incoherently, unclearly
反义词: clearly, distinctly, articulately
词性: 副词

词源: From the verb "mumble" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Mumble" comes from Middle Dutch or Middle Low German "mommelen,"  frequentative of "mommen" (to mumble), probably imitative.
