downbeats:/ˈdaʊnˌbiːts/| n.|低落的情绪；不顺利的事情；弱拍|A downbeat is an unaccented beat in music, particularly the first beat of a measure. It can also refer to a pessimistic or negative feeling or event.|The song started with a strong downbeat. 这首歌以强烈的弱拍开始。|近义词：disappointments, setbacks, downsides|反义词:upbeats, highlights|noun


downbeat:/ˈdaʊnˌbiːt/| adj.|悲观的；消极的|Downbeat means pessimistic or negative.|The economic forecast was rather downbeat. 经济预测相当悲观。|近义词：pessimistic, gloomy, negative|反义词:optimistic, positive|adjective
