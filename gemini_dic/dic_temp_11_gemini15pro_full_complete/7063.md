anemometry:/ˌænɪˈmɒmɪtri/ | n. |风速测定；风速测定法|The study of wind and its properties is called anemometry.  对风及其特性的研究称为风速测定。| Anemometry plays a crucial role in weather forecasting. 风速测定在天气预报中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: wind measurement, aerology| Antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology: From Greek *ἀνεμος* (ánemos) "wind" + *-μετρία* (-metría) "-metry", from *μέτρον* (métron) "measure".
