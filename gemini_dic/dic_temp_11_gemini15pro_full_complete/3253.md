affined:/əˈfaɪnd/| adj.|密切相关的，有亲和力的|Having a natural liking or affinity for something; related by affinity.|例句：They were affined by their shared love of music. 他们因共同热爱音乐而关系密切。

例句：The two companies are affined in their business strategies. 这两家公司的商业战略密切相关。|近义词：related, connected, kindred, similar|反义词：unrelated, dissimilar, different|词性:形容词
