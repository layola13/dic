errands:/ˈerəndz/ (US), /ˈerəndz/ (UK)
n.|差事，跑腿|Short trips that you make in order to do something, such as buying or delivering things.|I ran some errands after work today. 我今天下班后跑了一些差事。|synonyms: tasks, chores, jobs|antonyms: leisure, relaxation
词性: 名词
