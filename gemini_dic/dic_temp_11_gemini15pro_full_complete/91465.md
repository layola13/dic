hylism:/ˈhaɪlɪzəm/
n.
物质主义；唯物论
The philosophical doctrine that matter is the only reality.
Hylists believe that everything can be explained by physical processes. 物质主义者认为一切都可以用物理过程来解释。
Hylists reject the idea of a separate soul or spirit. 物质主义者反对独立的灵魂或精神的概念。
materialism, physicalism
idealism, dualism
noun
