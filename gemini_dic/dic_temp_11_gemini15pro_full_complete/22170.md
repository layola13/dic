bombastic:/bɒmˈbæstɪk/ /bɑːmˈbæstɪk/| adj.|夸大的，言过其实的|High-sounding but with little meaning; inflated.|His bombastic speech was full of long words, but it said very little of substance.  他夸夸其谈，说了很多华丽的词藻，但实质内容却很少。|Synonyms: pompous, inflated, overblown, pretentious|Antonyms: understated, simple, modest, unpretentious|adjective

bombastically:/bɒmˈbæstɪkəli/ /bɑːmˈbæstɪkəli/| adv.|夸大地，言过其实地|In a bombastic manner.|He bombastically declared himself the greatest writer of all time. 他夸夸其谈地宣称自己是史上最伟大的作家。|Synonyms: pompously, pretentiously|Antonyms: modestly, simply, humbly|adverb

bombast:/ˈbɒmbæst/ /ˈbɑːmbæst/| n.|夸大的言辞，浮夸的语言|High-sounding language with little meaning, used to impress people.|The politician's speech was full of bombast, but lacked any real substance. 这位政客的演讲充满了夸夸其谈，但缺乏任何实际内容。|Synonyms: rhetoric, bluster, grandiloquence|Antonyms: understatement, simplicity, conciseness|noun
