all-consuming:/ˌɔːl kənˈsuːmɪŋ/ | adj. | 极耗费时间的；全身心投入的；令人着迷的 | Occupying all of one's time and attention. |  His work became all-consuming, leaving him little time for family. 他的工作变得非常耗时，几乎没有时间陪伴家人。

|  Her passion for dance was all-consuming. 她对舞蹈的热情极度狂热。 | 近义词: absorbing, engrossing, immersive, obsessive | 反义词:  superficial, casual, perfunctory | 词性: 形容词


词源: "all-" (completely) + "consuming" (taking up completely).  From the Latin "consumere," meaning "to take up completely, devour."
