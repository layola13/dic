digression:/daɪˈɡrɛʃən/ /dɪˈɡrɛʃən/
n.
离题；枝节；漫笔；脱轨
a temporary departure from the main subject in speech or writing.
The professor's lecture was full of humorous digressions. 教授的讲座中充满了幽默的离题话。
After a long digression, he finally returned to the main point.  在一段冗长的离题之后，他终于回到了要点。
近义词:deviation, detour, tangent, aside
反义词:focus, concentration, adherence, mainstream
词性:名词
