misgivingly:/mɪsˈɡɪvɪŋli/
adverb
心存疑虑地，忐忑不安地
With doubt or apprehension; uneasily.
He accepted the offer misgivingly, unsure if it was the right decision.  他心怀顾虑地接受了这个提议，不确定这是否是正确的决定。
She misgivingly opened the door, fearing who might be on the other side. 她忐忑不安地打开门，害怕另一边会是谁。
近义词:apprehensively, doubtfully, hesitantly, uneasily
反义词:confidently, assuredly, resolutely
词性:adverb

词源: From misgiving + -ly.  Misgiving from mis- + giving.
