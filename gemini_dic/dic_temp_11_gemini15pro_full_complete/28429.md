carbonless:/ˈkɑːrbənləs/
adj.
无碳的
Without carbon paper; designating paper treated to produce copies without the use of carbon paper.
This form is carbonless, so you don't need to worry about smudging.  这张表格是无碳的，所以你不用担心弄脏。

She made a carbonless copy of the receipt. 她用无碳复写纸复印了收据。
近义词:self-copying, NCR (No Carbon Required)
反义词:carbonated, carbon-based (these are not direct opposites, but represent contrasting concepts)
词性:adjective
