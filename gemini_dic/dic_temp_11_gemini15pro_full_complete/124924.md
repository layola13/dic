mudslinging:/ˈmʌdˌslɪŋɪŋ/
noun
诽谤，恶意中伤
The act of making malicious and untrue statements about someone, typically in an attempt to damage their reputation.
The election campaign descended into mudslinging. 选举活动沦为互相诽谤。
近义词:smear, slander, libel, defamation, character assassination
反义词:praise, commendation, acclaim
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From mud + slinging (present participle of sling, meaning "to throw").  The image is of throwing mud at someone to soil or damage them.
