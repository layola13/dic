jellied:/ˈdʒɛlid/| adj.|果冻状的，凝胶状的|Having the consistency of jelly; set into a jelly.|The jellied eels were a local delicacy.  果冻状的鳗鱼是当地的一道美味佳肴。|例句：The dessert was topped with jellied cranberries. 甜点上覆盖着果冻状的蔓越莓。|近义词：gelatinous, jelly-like|反义词:liquid, runny|词性:形容词

词源: From "jelly" (n.) + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). "Jelly" comes from Old French "gelee" meaning "frost, jelly," from "geler" meaning "to freeze," ultimately from Latin "gelare" meaning "to freeze."
