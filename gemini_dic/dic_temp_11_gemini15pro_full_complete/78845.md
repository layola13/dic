ginga:/ˈʒɪŋɡə/| n.|  金加（巴西战舞/卡波耶拉的基本动作）| A fluid, rocking motion in capoeira, the Brazilian martial art, used to maintain balance, create momentum, and deceive the opponent.  It's the foundation of capoeira movements and is characterized by constant motion and shifting of weight.  It can be translated as "sway," "swing," or "rock."|  The ginga is the heart and soul of capoeira. 金加是卡波耶拉的核心和灵魂。| The capoeirista's ginga was mesmerizing.  这位卡波耶拉表演者的金加令人着迷。| Synonyms: sway, swing, rock (in the context of capoeira) | Antonyms: stillness, rigidity | noun
