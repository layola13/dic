counteracters:/ˈkaʊntərˌæktərz/
noun
抵消者；对抗者；反作用剂
Those who counteract something; things that neutralize or oppose the effects of something else.
The government introduced counteracters to mitigate the effects of the new policy. 政府引入了反作用剂来减轻新政策的影响。
synonyms: neutralizers, antagonists, remedies, antidotes
antonyms: agonists, catalysts, enhancers
