beset:/bɪˈsɛt/| v. |困扰，围攻|To trouble persistently; harass.  To surround; hem in.|例句：The project was beset by difficulties from the start.  这个项目从一开始就困难重重。|近义词：trouble, harass, plague, beleaguer, besiege|反义词:aid, assist|verb

beset:/bɪˈsɛt/| v. |镶嵌，点缀|To stud, cover, or decorate all over (as with jewels). (This usage is less common).|例句：The crown was beset with diamonds.  皇冠上镶满了钻石。|近义词：adorn, decorate, embellish, ornament|反义词:strip, bare|verb
