diploblastic:/ˌdɪpləˈblæstɪk/
adjective
双胚层的
Having two germ layers, the ectoderm and endoderm, as the embryos of sponges and coelenterates.
Sponges and jellyfish are diploblastic animals. 海绵和水母是双胚层动物。
近义词: two-layered
反义词: triploblastic
词性: adjective
