nonanimate:/ˌnɒnˈænɪmət/ | adj. |无生命的，非生物的 | Not having the characteristics of life; not alive. |  The statue is a nonanimate object. 这座雕像是无生命的物体。 | Rocks and minerals are nonanimate. 岩石和矿物质是无生命的。 | 近义词:inanimate, lifeless | 反义词: animate, living | 词性:形容词

词源:
From non- + animate.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "animate" comes from the Latin word "anima," meaning "soul" or "breath."
