indaba:/ɪnˈdɑːbə/
noun
会议，讨论，特别指南非的部落会议
A conference or meeting, especially a traditional Zulu or Xhosa assembly for discussion.
The tribal elders held an indaba to discuss the drought. 部落长老们召开了一次indaba会议来讨论旱情。
