imagist:/ˈɪmɪdʒɪst/| n.|意象派诗人；意象主义者|A member of a school of early 20th-century poets who emphasized the use of precise imagery and clear, sharp language.|Ezra Pound was a leading figure in the Imagist movement.  埃兹拉·庞德是意象派运动的领军人物。|Synonyms:  modernist, poet|Antonyms: traditionalist|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *imagiste*, from *image* (image) + *-iste* (-ist). Ultimately from Latin *imago* (image).
