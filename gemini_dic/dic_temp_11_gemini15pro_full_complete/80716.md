gorma:/ˈɡɔːrmə/| n.|盛宴，美食，饕餮|A gorma is a grand feast, especially one featuring elaborate or exotic dishes.  It emphasizes abundance and enjoyment of fine food and drink.|The king held a gorma to celebrate his victory. 国王举行盛宴庆祝他的胜利。|近义词：feast, banquet, repast|反义词:fast, starvation|词性:名词
gorma:/ˈɡɔːrmə/| adj.|美食的，美味佳肴的|Relating to or characteristic of a gorma; luxurious or elaborate in food and drink. |The gorma spread included delicacies from around the world.  盛宴的菜肴包括来自世界各地的美味佳肴。|近义词：gourmet, epicurean, luxurious|反义词:plain, simple, basic|词性:形容词
