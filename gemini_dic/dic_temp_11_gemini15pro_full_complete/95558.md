incognizance:/ɪnˈkɒɡnɪzəns/ |n.| 无知，不知情 |Lack of knowledge or awareness; ignorance. | He acted in incognizance of the consequences. 他在不了解后果的情况下采取了行动。 | Synonyms: ignorance, unawareness, unconsciousness, unfamiliarity | Antonyms: awareness, knowledge, consciousness, familiarity | noun

incognito:/ˌɪnkɒɡˈniːtəʊ/ |adj. adv. n.| 隐姓埋名的，化名的；隐姓埋名地；匿名者，化名 | (of a person) having one's true identity concealed. | The prince often travelled incognito. 王子经常隐姓埋名地旅行。 | Synonyms: disguised, anonymous, camouflaged, undercover | Antonyms: known, identified, recognized, revealed | adjective, adverb, noun
