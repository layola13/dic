launderability:/ˌlɔːndərəˈbɪləti/
n.
可洗性，耐洗性
The ease with which a fabric can be washed and retain its original appearance and quality.
This shirt has excellent launderability.  这件衬衫耐洗性很好。
The launderability of this fabric is poor. 这块布料的耐洗性很差。
washability, cleanability
non-washable, fragility (of fabric)
noun
