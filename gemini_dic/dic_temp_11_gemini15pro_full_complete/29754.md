cataract:/ˈkætərækt/| n.|白内障; 瀑布; 洪水; 大雨|A cataract is a clouding of the lens in the eye which leads to a decrease in vision.|例句：He developed a cataract in his left eye. 他的左眼得了白内障。|例句：The Niagara Falls is a famous cataract. 尼亚加拉瀑布是一个著名的瀑布。|近义词：waterfall, cascade, rapids (for the "waterfall" meaning); opacity, clouding (for the "eye condition" meaning)|反义词: clarity (for the "eye condition" meaning)|词性:noun
