infirming:/ɪnˈfɜːrmɪŋ/
|v.|使弱，使不坚定|Making something weaker or less certain.  The new evidence was infirming his previously held beliefs.|例句：The constant criticism was infirming her confidence.  不断的批评削弱了她的自信心。

例句：The lack of funding is infirming the project's chances of success. 缺乏资金削弱了该项目成功的机会。|近义词：weakening, undermining, debilitating|反义词:strengthening, reinforcing, bolstering|词性:动词(Verb)

Etymology: From Late Latin *infirmare, from Latin infirmus "weak".  Ultimately from in- "not" + firmus "strong".
