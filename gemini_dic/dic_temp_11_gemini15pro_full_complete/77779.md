generalise:/ˈdʒɛn(ə)rəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈdʒɛnərəˌlaɪz/ (US)| v. |概括，归纳；推广，普及|To infer or form a conclusion from particular instances; to make a general statement or form a general principle from specific cases; to extend a particular idea, principle, or method to a larger group or situation.|Scientists are careful not to generalise from limited experimental data. 科学家们很谨慎，不会从有限的实验数据中概括结论。

The findings of the study can be generalised to the wider population. 这项研究的结果可以推广到更广泛的人群。|近义词：infer, deduce, extrapolate, broaden, expand|反义词：specify, particularise, restrict|词性：动词
