concessionary:/kənˈseʃənəri/ | n.|受让人，特许经营者|A person who has been granted a concession.|例句：The concessionary operates the food stalls at the festival.  这个特许经营者在节日里经营食品摊位。|近义词：franchisee, licensee|反义词：licensor, franchisor|词性:名词
