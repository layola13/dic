lane:/leɪn/| n.|车道，小路，航线|A narrow road, especially in the country. |We drove down a quiet country lane. 我们沿着一条安静的乡村小路行驶。|近义词：path, road, alley, avenue, street|反义词:highway, expressway|noun

lane:/leɪn/| n.|泳道，跑道，赛道|A designated route or course for vehicles or athletes.|She won the race in the outside lane. 她在外侧跑道赢得了比赛。|近义词：track, course, path|反义词: - |noun
