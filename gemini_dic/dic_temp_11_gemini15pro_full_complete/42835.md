courser:/ˈkɔːrsər/ (US) /ˈkɔːsə(r)/ (UK)| n.|骏马；猎狗；猎鹰|A swift and spirited horse, especially one used in hunting or racing; a hound used for coursing; a falcon trained to pursue game.|例句：The courser galloped across the field. 骏马飞驰过田野。

例句：The king's courser was a magnificent Arabian stallion. 国王的骏马是一匹壮丽的阿拉伯种马。|近义词：steed, charger, hunter, hound, falcon|反义词:nag (驽马)|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *`corsier` "racehorse, warhorse," from `corsier` "to run," from Late Latin `cursiare`, frequentative of Latin `currere` "to run."
