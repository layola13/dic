know-it-all: /ˈnoʊɪtɔːl/ (US) /ˈnəʊɪtɔːl/ (UK)| n. |自以为是的人，无所不知的人| A person who acts as if they know everything.| He's such a know-it-all, always correcting everyone. 他真是个自以为是的人，总是纠正别人。|  Synonyms: smart aleck, wise guy, know-all | Antonyms: humble person, modest person | Noun

know-it-alls: /ˈnoʊɪtɔːlz/ (US) /ˈnəʊɪtɔːlz/ (UK)| n. |自以为是的人们，无所不知的人们 (know-it-all的复数形式) | Plural form of know-it-all, referring to multiple people who act as if they know everything. | The meeting was dominated by a group of know-it-alls who wouldn't listen to anyone else's ideas. 会议被一群自以为是的人控制了，他们根本不听别人的意见。 | Synonyms: smart alecks, wise guys, know-alls | Antonyms: humble people, modest people | Noun
