overinsistence:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈsɪstəns/| n. |过度坚持，过分强调|The overinsistence on the rules made the workplace stifling.  过分强调规则使得工作场所令人窒息。|例句：Her overinsistence on the importance of organic food became tiresome. 她过分强调有机食品的重要性，令人厌烦。|近义词：persistence, insistence, pertinacity |反义词: acquiescence, compliance, submissiveness |词性:noun


Etymology:
From over- + insistence.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much". "Insistence" comes from the Late Latin insistentia, from insistere "to stand upon, persist," from in- "on" + sistere "to stand".
