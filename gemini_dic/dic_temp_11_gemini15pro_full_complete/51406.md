determinist:/dɪˈtɜːmɪnɪst/
n.|决定论者|A person who believes in determinism, the philosophical doctrine that all events are predetermined by antecedent causes.|例句：The determinist argued that free will is an illusion. 决定论者认为自由意志是一种幻觉。|近义词：fatalist|反义词：libertarian|词性:noun
determinist:/dɪˈtɜːmɪnɪstɪk/
adj.|决定论的|Relating to determinism; determined.|例句：A deterministic system is one in which the future state is completely determined by the present state. 决定论系统是指未来状态完全由现在状态决定的系统。|近义词：predetermined, inevitable, predictable|反义词：indeterministic, random, stochastic|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From determine + -ist.  Determine comes from Old French determiner, from Latin determinare "to limit, fix, bound," from de- "completely" + terminare "to set bounds to," from terminus "boundary mark, limit."
