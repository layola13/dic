ariman:/ˈærɪmən/ | n. | 阿里曼; 恶神; | Ariman is the Zoroastrian spirit of destruction and darkness, the source of all evil in the world.  阿里曼是琐罗亚斯德教中的毁灭和黑暗之神，是世界上所有邪恶的根源。|  Ariman represents the forces of evil opposed to Ahura Mazda. 阿里曼代表与阿胡拉·马兹达对立的邪恶势力。| synonyms: Angra Mainyu, Devil, Satan | antonyms: Ahura Mazda, God, good | noun
