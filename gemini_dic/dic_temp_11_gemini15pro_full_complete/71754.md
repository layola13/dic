flea-bitten:/ˈfliːˌbɪtn/| adj.|被跳蚤咬过的；破旧的；肮脏的|Infested with fleas; shabby and run-down.|The flea-bitten dog scratched constantly. 那只被跳蚤咬过的狗不停地挠痒。|近义词：shabby, scruffy, mangy, seedy|反义词：pristine, immaculate, clean|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From flea + bitten, the past participle of bite.  The literal meaning refers to an animal covered in flea bites. The figurative meaning developed to describe something shabby and run-down, likely due to the association of fleas with poverty and neglect.
