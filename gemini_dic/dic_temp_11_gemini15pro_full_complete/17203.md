baunscheidtism:/ˈbaʊnʃaɪtɪzəm/| n. |放血疗法|A form of alternative medicine in which the skin is pricked with a needle-studded device and then irritated with oil, purportedly to draw toxins out of the body.|Baunscheidtism was once a popular treatment for rheumatism. 放血疗法曾经是治疗风湿病的流行疗法。|Synonyms: counter-irritation, derivation|Antonyms: None|Noun
