overadjustments: /ˌoʊvərəˈdʒʌstmənts/
noun
过度调整；过度校正
Adjustments that go too far or are excessive, often resulting in a worse situation than the original problem.
The government's overadjustments to the economy led to a recession. 政府对经济的过度调整导致了经济衰退。
Overadjustments to the medication dosage can be harmful.  药物剂量的过度调整可能是有害的。
synonyms: overcorrections, overcompensations
antonyms: underadjustments, undercorrections
n.
