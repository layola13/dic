moulds:/məʊldz/ (British) /moʊldz/ (American)| n. |模具，模型，霉菌|A hollow container used to give shape to molten or hot liquid material when it cools and hardens; a growth of minute fungi forming on vegetable or animal matter.|The jelly was set in a heart-shaped mould.  果冻被放在一个心形的模具里。|Synonyms: cast, form, matrix, die, mold (American)|Antonyms: shapelessness|Noun

moulds:/məʊldz/ (British) /moʊldz/ (American)| v. |塑造，形成，影响|To shape or form (something) from a pliable material; to influence the development or character of.|He moulded the clay into a figure. 他用黏土塑造了一个人物。|Synonyms: shape, form, fashion, model|Antonyms: deform, distort|Verb
