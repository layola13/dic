arrowworm:/ˈærəʊwɜːm/| n. |箭虫|A small, transparent, predatory marine worm with horizontal fins and grasping spines around the mouth, belonging to the phylum Chaetognatha.|例句：Arrowworms are important predators in the plankton community. 箭虫是浮游生物群落中的重要捕食者。

例句：The arrowworm darted forward and captured a copepod. 箭虫向前冲去，捕获了一只桡足类动物。|近义词：chaetognath|反义词: None|词性:noun
