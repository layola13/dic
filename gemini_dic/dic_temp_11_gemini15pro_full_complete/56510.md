domesticities:/ˌdoʊˌmɛsˈtɪsɪtiz/ | n. | 家庭生活；家务事 |  The routines and activities associated with running a home and family life. |  She found solace in the simple domesticities of life, like baking bread and tending her garden. 她在家务琐事中找到了慰藉，比如烤面包和照料花园。 | 近义词: household chores, home life, family life | 反义词: outside world, public life | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From "domesticity" + "-ies" (plural suffix).  "Domesticity" comes from the Latin "domesticus," meaning "belonging to the house."
