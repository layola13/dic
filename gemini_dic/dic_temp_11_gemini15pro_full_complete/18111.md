behemoth:/bɪˈhiːməθ/ |n.|巨兽，庞然大物|Something of immense size or power.  A huge or monstrous creature.|例句：The multinational corporation is a real behemoth in the industry.  这家跨国公司是该行业的真正巨头。|例句：The new aircraft carrier is a behemoth of the seas. 这艘新的航空母舰是海上巨兽。|近义词：giant, monster, leviathan|反义词：dwarf, pygmy|词性：名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *behemot*, *behemoth*, from Biblical Hebrew *בְּהֵמוֹת* (*bəhēmōṯ*), plural of *בְּהֵמָה* (*bəhēmā*) "beast, cattle, brute beast" (interpreted as singular in English and other languages), perhaps an intensive plural of *בהמה* (*behem*) "mute beast", or possibly from Egyptian *p-ehe-mau* "water-ox".
