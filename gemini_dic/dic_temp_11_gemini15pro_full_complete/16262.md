banyan:/ˈbænjən/ | n. | 印度榕树，菩提树 | A large East Indian tree (Ficus benghalensis) that puts out aerial roots that grow down into the ground to form additional trunks. |  Example 1: We sat in the shade of a giant banyan tree.  我们坐在一棵巨大的菩提树的树荫下。

Example 2: The banyan tree is considered sacred in many cultures. 菩提树在许多文化中被认为是神圣的。| 近义词: fig tree (无花果树 - 更广泛的类别) | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑一些小型植物)shrub, bush | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Gujarati *bāṇiyõ*, a merchant, from Sanskrit *vaṇij*, from *vanij*, merchant.  The name was originally applied to the tree because Hindu merchants often set up their stalls in the shade of such trees.  葡萄牙语拼写为 *banian*, 法语拼写为 *banian*.
