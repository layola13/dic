demotivators:/ˌdiːˈmoʊtɪveɪtərz/ | n. |  desmotivadores（西班牙语）， 令人沮丧的事物；打击积极性的事物 |  Something that reduces motivation and enthusiasm.  These are often humorous images or posters with captions that highlight the futility or absurdity of certain tasks or situations. | Those demotivators really got me down. 那些打击士气的东西真的让我沮丧。| 近义词: discouragements, deterrents | 反义词: motivators, incentives | noun

demotivator:/ˌdiːˈmoʊtɪveɪtər/ | n. |  令人沮丧的事物；打击积极性的事物 | Something that reduces motivation and enthusiasm. Often a humorous image or poster with a caption that highlights the futility or absurdity of a certain task or situation. | Seeing that demotivator poster about work made me question my career choices. 看到那张关于工作的消极海报，让我开始质疑我的职业选择。 | 近义词: discouragement, deterrent | 反义词: motivator, incentive | noun


词源:

From demotivate (de- + motivate) + -or.  The word "demotivate" itself arose in the late 20th century as the antonym of "motivate."  The "-or" suffix denotes an agent or something that performs the action of the verb.
