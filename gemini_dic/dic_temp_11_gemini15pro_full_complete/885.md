absurdly:/əbˈsɜːrdli/ /æbˈsɜːrdli/
adverb
荒谬地，荒唐地
In a manner that is ridiculously unreasonable, illogical, or inappropriate.
That's an absurdly low price for such a valuable item.  那件贵重物品的价格低的荒唐。
He was absurdly dressed for the occasion. 他穿的衣服与场合极不相称。
Synonyms: ridiculously, preposterously, ludicrously, foolishly, senselessly
Antonyms: reasonably, sensibly, logically, rationally
词性: 副词
