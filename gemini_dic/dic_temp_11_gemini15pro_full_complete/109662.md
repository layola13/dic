leucocythemia:/ˌluːkəsaɪˈθiːmiə/| n. |白血病|A malignant progressive disease in which the bone marrow and other blood-forming organs produce increased numbers of immature or abnormal leucocytes. These suppress the production of normal blood cells, leading to anemia and other symptoms.|He was diagnosed with leucocythemia last year. 他去年被诊断出患有白血病。|Synonym: leukemia, cancer of the blood |Antonym: health, wellness|noun
