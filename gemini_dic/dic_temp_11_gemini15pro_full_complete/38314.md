concealing:/kənˈsiːlɪŋ/
Verb (present participle)
隐藏，掩盖
The act of hiding or keeping something secret.
He was concealing a weapon under his coat. 他把武器藏在外套下面。

concealing:/kənˈsiːlɪŋ/
Adjective
隐藏的，掩蔽的
Serving to hide or keep something secret.
She wore a concealing scarf to hide her identity. 她戴着一条遮掩身份的围巾。


Etymology:
From Late Middle English *concelen, from Anglo-Norman conceler, Old French conceler, from Latin concēlāre ("to hide, conceal"), from com- ("together, completely") + cēlāre ("to hide").

Synonyms:
hiding, covering, disguising, masking, camouflaging, obscuring, suppressing

Antonyms:
revealing, exposing, uncovering, disclosing, divulging, showing, displaying

Word type:
Verb (present participle), Adjective
