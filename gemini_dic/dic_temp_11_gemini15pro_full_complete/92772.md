ichthyotomist:/ˌɪkθiˈɒtəmɪst/ | n. | 鱼类解剖学家 | A person who dissects or studies the anatomy of fish. |  Example 1: The ichthyotomist carefully examined the fish's skeletal structure. 鱼类解剖学家仔细检查了鱼的骨骼结构。

Example 2: The museum hired a skilled ichthyotomist to prepare the new specimens for display. 博物馆聘请了一位熟练的鱼类解剖学家来准备新的标本以供展示。 | Near synonyms: fish anatomist, fish biologist | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps a fish taxonomist who focuses on classification rather than dissection) | Noun
