jolly:/ˈdʒɒli/ (UK) /ˈdʒɑːli/ (US)| adj. adv.|快乐的，高兴的；宜人的，令人愉快的；adv. 非常，很|Full of or showing high spirits; cheerful.  Conveying or intended to convey happiness and amusement. (adv.) Very; extremely.|例句：We had a jolly good time at the party. 我们在派对上玩得很开心。|近义词：cheerful, merry, happy, joyful, mirthful|反义词:sad, gloomy, depressed, miserable|词性:形容词, 副词
jolly:/ˈdʒɒli/ (UK) /ˈdʒɑːli/ (US)| n.|欢乐，愉快；（尤指圣诞节期间的）庆祝活动|A festive gathering or celebration, especially at Christmas time.|例句：The Christmas jolly was a great success. 圣诞庆祝活动非常成功。|近义词：celebration, festivity, party|反义词:wake, funeral|词性:名词

词源:
From Old French *jolif, joli "festive, merry, pretty," from Old Norse jól "Yule, Christmas," with the suffix -if (from Frankish *-ing) added.
