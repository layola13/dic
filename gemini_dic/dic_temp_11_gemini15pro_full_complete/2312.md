aderly:/ˈædərli/| adj.|年老的，老年的|Elderly is used to describe people who are old, especially politely or respectfully.  It suggests a more advanced age than 'old'.|例句：The elderly gentleman offered me his seat on the bus.  那位老先生在公交车上给我让座。

例句：The government provides special services for the elderly. 政府为老年人提供特殊服务。|近义词：old, aged, senior|反义词:young, youthful|词性:adjective
