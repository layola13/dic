contractedness:/kənˈtræktɪdnəs/ /kənˈtræktɪdnɪs/
|n.|收缩性；收缩状态；简略；紧凑|The contractedness of the muscle fibers allows for efficient movement. 肌肉纤维的收缩性使运动更有效率。|The contractedness of his writing style made it difficult to understand. 他紧凑的写作风格使人难以理解。|近义词: contraction, constriction, shortness, compactness, terseness|反义词: expansion, dilation, lengthiness, diffuseness|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From contract + -ed + -ness.  "Contract" comes from the Latin "contractus," the past participle of "contrahere," meaning "to draw together, to conclude a bargain."  "-ed" forms the past participle, and "-ness" forms a noun denoting a state or quality.
