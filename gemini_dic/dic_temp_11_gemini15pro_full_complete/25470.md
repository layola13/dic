bulletin board system:/ˈbʊlətɪn bɔːrd ˈsɪstəm/
noun|电子公告板系统，简称BBS|A computer system that allows users to connect and exchange messages, files, and other information.|People used bulletin board systems to connect with others long before the internet became popular.  人们在互联网普及之前就使用电子公告板系统与他人联系。|forum, online community|offline community|n.
ETYMOLOGY->
bulletin (from French, ultimately from Italian *bullettino*, diminutive of *bulla* 'seal, edict') + board (from Old English *bord* 'plank, table') + system (from Latin *systema*, from Greek *sustēma* 'organized whole')
