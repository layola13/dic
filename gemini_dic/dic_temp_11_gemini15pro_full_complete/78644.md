gibstaff:/ˈɡɪbstæf/ | n. |戟杆;戟柄;长矛杆 | A long staff or pole, especially the wooden shaft of a halberd or other similar weapon. | The gibstaff was too heavy for the young soldier to wield effectively. 年轻士兵觉得戟杆太重了，无法有效挥舞。| The knight lowered his lance, its gibstaff gleaming in the sunlight. 骑士放低了他的长矛，它的长杆在阳光下闪闪发光。 | synonym: staff, pole, shaft | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "tip" or "head" if referring to the opposite end of the weapon) | noun
