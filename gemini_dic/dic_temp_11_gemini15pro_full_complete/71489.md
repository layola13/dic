flanch:/flæntʃ/ | n. |凸缘，轮缘，法兰盘 |A projecting flat rim, collar, or rib on an object, such as a wheel or a pipe, used to strengthen or attach it to another object. |The plumber tightened the bolts on the pipe flanch. 水管工拧紧了管道法兰上的螺栓。|The train wheels have a flanch to keep them on the track. 火车轮有轮缘以保证其在轨道上行驶。|rim, edge, flange, lip | - | noun
flanch:/flɑːntʃ/ | v. |给…装上凸缘 |To provide with a flanch. |The workers flanched the steel beams together. 工人们将钢梁用凸缘连接在一起。|They flanched the ends of the pipes before welding them. 他们在焊接管道之前给管道的末端装上了法兰。 |attach, connect, join |detach, separate | verb
