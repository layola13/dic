diandrian:/daɪˈændriən/| adj.|雌雄同体的，兼具两性的|Having both male and female sexual characteristics or organs.  Derived from the Greek words "di-" (two) and "aner" (man), with the suffix "-ian" indicating belonging to or relating to.  Originally referred to hermaphroditism in plants, but later extended to animals and humans.|The diandrian flower has both stamens and pistils.|近义词：hermaphroditic, bisexual, monoecious|反义词：unisexual, dioecious|词性:形容词
