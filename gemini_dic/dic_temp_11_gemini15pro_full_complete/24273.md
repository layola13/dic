brigades:/brɪˈɡeɪdz/| n.|旅；大队；消防队|A group of people organized for a particular purpose, especially for social or political action or work.|例句：The international brigades fought against fascism in the Spanish Civil War. 国际纵队在西班牙内战中与法西斯主义作战。

例句：Volunteer brigades helped to clean up the oil spill. 志愿队伍帮助清理了石油泄漏。|近义词：teams, groups, units, squads, forces|反义词:individuals|词性:名词

brigades:/brɪˈɡeɪdz/| v.|把…編成旅；把…編成队|To organize people into brigades.|例句：The workers were brigaded to harvest the crops. 工人们被组织起来收割庄稼。

例句：The army brigaded the soldiers for the upcoming battle. 军队将士兵们編成旅，准备即将到来的战斗。|近义词：organize, assemble, group, unite|反义词:disperse, scatter|词性:动词
