onces:/ˈwʌnsɪz/ | adv. | 一次; 从前; 曾经 |Happening or existing only once; formerly; at one time. | She only goes there once a year. 她一年只去那里一次。 | 近义词: formerly, previously | 反义词: never, always | 词性: 副词


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He goes to the gym once a week.  他每周去一次健身房。
She visits her grandmother once a month. 她每月去看望她祖母一次。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am only explaining this once. 我只解释一次。
He is doing this experiment only once. 他只做一次这个实验。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have only been there once. 我只去过那里一次。
She has tried that dish only once. 她只尝过一次那道菜。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been thinking about that once. 我曾经想过那件事一次。 (不太常用)
He has been considering that option once. 他曾经考虑过那个选项一次。 (不太常用)

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I went there once. 我去过那里一次。
She saw him only once. 她只见过他一次。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was only saying that once. 我只是说了一次。
He was only trying it once. 他只是试了一次。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had only been there once before. 我以前只去过那里一次。
She had only seen him once before. 她以前只见过他一次。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been considering that once before. 我以前曾经考虑过那件事一次。(不太常用)
He had been thinking about that once before. 他以前曾经想过那件事一次。(不太常用)

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will go there only once. 我只会去那里一次。
She will try it only once. 她只会试一次。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be going there only once. 我将只会去那里一次。 (强调动作的短暂性)
She will be trying it only once. 她将只会试一次。 (强调动作的短暂性)

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, I will have gone there only once. 到明年，我将只去过那里一次。
By next week, she will have tried it only once. 到下周，她将只试过一次。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous): (不太常用在这个语境)


虚拟语气:
If I had gone there once, I would have known. 如果我去过那里一次，我就知道了。
I wish I had gone there once. 我希望我去过那里一次。

被动语态: (不太适用)

疑问句:
Have you been there once? 你去过那里一次吗？
Did you go there once? 你去过那里一次吗？

否定句:
I have never been there, not even once. 我从未去过那里，一次也没有。
She didn't go there once. 她一次也没去过那里。


PHRASE->
at once  立刻，马上
once upon a time 从前
once in a while 偶尔
once and for all  一劳永逸地
once more 再一次

注意事项:  onces 不是一个标准的英文单词。 应该是 once。 以上所有例句和解释都是基于 once 这个词。
