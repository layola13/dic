nonaseptic:/ˌnɒnəˈsɛptɪk/| adj.|非无菌的，未经消毒的|Not aseptic; not free from microorganisms that cause disease or fermentation.  Refers to an environment or substance that is not sterile and may contain bacteria, fungi, or other microorganisms.  It essentially means the opposite of aseptic.|The wound was treated in a nonaseptic environment, leading to infection.| 近义词: contaminated, unsterile, septic | 反义词: aseptic, sterile | 词性: adjective
