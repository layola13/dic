distasteful:/dɪsˈteɪstfʊl/ /dɪsˈteɪstfəl/| adj.|令人不快的，讨厌的|Causing dislike or disgust; unpleasant or offensive.|例句：The idea of eating insects is distasteful to many people. 吃昆虫的想法让很多人感到厌恶。

例句：He found the comedian's jokes about disability distasteful. 他觉得那个喜剧演员关于残疾的笑话很令人反感。

近义词：unpleasant, offensive, disagreeable, objectionable, repugnant, repulsive|反义词：pleasant, agreeable, appealing, attractive|词性:adjective
