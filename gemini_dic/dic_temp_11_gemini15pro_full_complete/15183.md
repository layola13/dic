backbiting: /ˈbækˌbaɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 恶意诽谤，背后说坏话 | The act of saying mean or spiteful things about someone behind their back. |  He lost his job because of the backbiting of his colleagues.  他因为同事的背后诽谤而丢了工作。

| Example: The constant backbiting in the office created a toxic work environment. 办公室里不断的恶意诽谤制造了有毒的工作环境。 | Synonyms: slander, defamation, gossip, libel | Antonyms: praise, compliment, commendation | noun
