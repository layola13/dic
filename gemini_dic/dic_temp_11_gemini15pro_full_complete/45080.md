cubism:/ˈkjuːbɪzəm/| n.|立体主义，立方派|A style of art, especially painting, in which objects and people are represented as geometric shapes, especially cubes.|Picasso's paintings from his Cubist period are highly valued.  毕加索立体主义时期的画作非常珍贵。|synonyms:  modernism, avant-garde|antonyms: realism, classicism|noun
