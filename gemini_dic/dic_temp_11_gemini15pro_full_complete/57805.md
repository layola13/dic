dreibund:/ˈdraɪbʊnd/
noun
三国同盟
A triple alliance; specifically, the alliance between Germany, Austria-Hungary, and Italy from 1882 to 1915.
The Dreibund was formed to counterbalance the Triple Entente.  三国同盟的形成是为了抗衡三国协约。
The Dreibund ultimately proved ineffective in preventing World War I. 三国同盟最终未能阻止第一次世界大战的爆发。
synonyms:triple alliance, coalition
antonyms:Triple Entente
