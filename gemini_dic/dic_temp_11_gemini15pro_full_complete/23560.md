brainstorming: /ˈbreɪnstɔːrmɪŋ/ | n. | 头脑风暴，集体讨论 | a group problem-solving technique that involves the spontaneous contribution of ideas from all members of the group |  We had a brainstorming session to come up with new marketing ideas. 我们进行了一次头脑风暴会议，提出了新的营销理念。| synonyms: idea generation, spitballing | antonyms: analysis, criticism | noun

brainstorm: /ˈbreɪnstɔːrm/ | v. | 集思广益，集体讨论 | to hold a brainstorming session |  We brainstormed ways to improve customer service. 我们集思广益，讨论如何改进客户服务。 | synonyms: collaborate, ideate | antonyms: analyze, criticize | verb
