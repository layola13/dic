babblements:/ˈbæblmənts/| n. |含糊不清的话；呓语；胡言乱语|The babblements of a young child are often difficult to understand. 小孩子的咿呀学语通常很难理解。|例句：The feverish patient's babblements made no sense.  发烧病人的胡言乱语没有任何意义。

例句：The babblements of the brook created a soothing sound.  小溪的潺潺流水声令人舒缓。


近义词：chatter, prattle, gibberish, nonsense|反义词:sense, clarity|词性:noun

词源：From Middle English *babelyng, from Old French babillier (“to babble”), frequentative of baber (“to open the mouth wide, gape”), of imitative origin.|
