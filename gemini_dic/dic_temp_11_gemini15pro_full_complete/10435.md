applauders:/əˈplɔːdərz/
n.
鼓掌者，喝彩者
People who clap their hands or express approval.
The applauders cheered enthusiastically after the performance. 演出结束后，鼓掌的观众热情地欢呼。
近义词:clappers, supporters, fans
反义词:critics, detractors
词性:Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From applaud + -er (agent suffix).  "applaud" comes from the Latin "applaudere," from "ad-" (to) + "plaudere" (to clap).
