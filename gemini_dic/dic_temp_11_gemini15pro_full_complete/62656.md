engagedness:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒdnəs/| n. |参与度，投入度|The state of being engaged or involved; the extent to which someone is actively involved or committed to something.|The teacher praised the students for their engagedness in the classroom discussion. 老师称赞学生们在课堂讨论中的积极参与。|Near synonyms: engagement, involvement, commitment, dedication|Near antonyms: apathy, indifference, detachment|noun
