interpledges:/ˌɪntərˈpledʒɪz/
| n. |相互保证，相互抵押，联合担保|A mutual or reciprocal pledge or guarantee.  A system where individuals or groups guarantee each other's obligations.|The two families made interpledges of support in times of trouble. 这两个家庭承诺在困难时期互相支持。|近义词：guarantees, assurances, commitments|反义词: breaches, betrayals|noun
