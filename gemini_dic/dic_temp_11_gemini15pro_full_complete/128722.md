niftiest:/ˈnɪf.ti.ɪst/| adj.|最漂亮的，最棒的，最巧妙的|superlative of nifty: most nifty.  Stylish, attractive, or pleasing, especially in a clever or witty way.| That's the niftiest little gadget I've ever seen! 那是我见过的最巧妙的小玩意儿！|近义词：smartest, coolest, best, slickest, nattiest|反义词：clumsiest, ugliest, worst, dullest|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From nifty + -est. Nifty originated in African American Vernacular English in the early 20th century. Its exact origins are uncertain, but it may be related to the word "magnificent."
