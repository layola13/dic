jilting:/ˈdʒɪltɪŋ/| n. |抛弃，拒绝，失约|The act of suddenly rejecting or abandoning a lover or a sweetheart, especially after a long or serious romantic relationship.|She was devastated by his jilting. 她被他抛弃后伤心欲绝。|近义词：abandonment, desertion, rejection, leaving, walking out on|反义词：acceptance, commitment|词性:名词

jilting:/ˈdʒɪltɪŋ/| adj. |抛弃的，拒绝的|Descriptive of the act of jilting; causing emotional distress through sudden rejection.|The jilting experience left her scarred. 这段被抛弃的经历给她留下了伤疤。|近义词：rejecting, abandoning, deserting|反义词：accepting, committing|词性:形容词
