guillotining:/ˈɡɪləˌtiːnɪŋ/| n. |断头，斩首，腰斩 (尤指用断头台)；强制终止，突然中断|The guillotining of the king marked the end of an era. 国王的斩首标志着一个时代的结束。|Synonyms: beheading, decapitation, execution, termination, curtailment|Antonyms: sparing, reprieving|noun

guillotine:/ˈɡɪləˌtiːn/| n. vt.|断头台；(比喻)强制终止，突然中断|The guillotine was used during the French Revolution. 断头台在法国大革命时期被使用。  They guillotined the project due to budget cuts. 由于预算削减，他们突然终止了这个项目。|Synonyms: (n.) beheading machine, execution device; (v.) terminate, curtail|Antonyms: (v.) initiate, commence|noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French guillotine, named after Joseph-Ignace Guillotin, a French physician who proposed its use, ultimately derived from Guillaume, a given name.
