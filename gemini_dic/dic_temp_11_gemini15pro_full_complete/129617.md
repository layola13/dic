non-conjunction:/ˌnɒn kənˈdʒʌŋkʃən/ | n. | 非合取, 非连接 |  A logical operation that outputs true only when both of its inputs are false. In other words, it is the negation of a conjunction. | The non-conjunction of A and B is true if and only if either A or B (or both) are false. | 近义词: NAND, Sheffer stroke | 反义词: conjunction | 词性: noun
