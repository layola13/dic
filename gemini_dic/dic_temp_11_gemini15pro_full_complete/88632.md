hmm:/hm/, /hʌm/ | interj. | 嗯，哼，唔 | Used to express hesitation, doubt, or thoughtfulness.  | Example: Hmm, I'm not sure about that. 嗯，我不确定。 | Synonym: uh, er, um | Antonym:  (Interjections typically don't have antonyms) | interjection

hmm:/hm/, /hʌm/ | n. | 嗯，哼，唔(表示犹豫或思考的声音) | The sound made when thinking or hesitating. | Example:  He let out a thoughtful "hmm" before answering. 他在回答之前若有所思地“嗯”了一声。 | Synonym: murmur, grunt | Antonym: (Nouns representing sounds typically don't have antonyms) | noun
