gladden:/ˈɡlædn/| v. |使高兴，使快乐|To make someone feel happy or joyful.|The sunshine gladdened our hearts after the long winter. 冬天过后，阳光温暖了我们的心。|The good news gladdened me. 好消息让我很高兴。|近义词：cheer, delight, please|反义词：sadden, depress|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *glæddian, from glæd "glad."
