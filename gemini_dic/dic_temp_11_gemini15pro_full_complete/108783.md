leasing:/ˈliːsɪŋ/| n.|租赁，出租|The company specializes in car leasing. 这家公司专门从事汽车租赁。|Example:  Leasing a car can be more affordable than buying one. 租赁一辆汽车比买一辆更实惠。

Example:  The leasing agreement lasts for three years. 租赁协议为期三年。|Synonyms: renting, hiring|Antonyms: buying, purchasing|noun
