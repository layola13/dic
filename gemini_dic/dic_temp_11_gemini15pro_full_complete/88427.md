hispanicists:/hɪˈspænɪsɪsts/
n.
西班牙研究专家；西班牙文化学者
A specialist in Hispanic studies; a scholar of Spanish language, literature, history, and culture.
例句：The conference brought together hispanicists from around the world.  这次会议汇集了来自世界各地的西班牙研究专家。

例句：Many hispanicists are fascinated by the works of Cervantes. 许多西班牙文化学者对塞万提斯的作品着迷。
近义词：Hispanists, Latin Americanists, Iberianists
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone specializing in a completely different field like Sinologists)
词性:Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hispanic + -ist.  Hispanic comes from the Latin "Hispanicus," relating to Hispania (the Roman name for the Iberian Peninsula). The suffix "-ist" denotes a person who studies or specializes in a particular subject.
