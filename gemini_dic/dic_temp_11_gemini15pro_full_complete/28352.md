carat:/ˈkærət/| n. |克拉；开|A unit of weight for precious stones and pearls, equal to 200 milligrams.  Also, a measure of the purity of gold, pure gold being 24 carats.|This diamond is a three-carat stone. 这颗钻石重三克拉。|The ring is made of 18-carat gold. 这枚戒指是由18K金制成的。|Synonym: karat|Antonym: N/A|noun
