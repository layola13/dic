improbability:/ɪmprɒbəˈbɪləti/ /ɪmprɑːbəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可能，不太可能发生的事|The improbability of the event made it all the more remarkable.  这个事件发生的可能性极小，使得它更加引人注目。|例句：The improbability of winning the lottery is well known.  众所周知，赢得彩票的可能性微乎其微。

例句：He stressed the improbability of such a scenario. 他强调了这种情况发生的可能性极低。|近义词：unlikelihood, implausibility, doubtfulness|反义词：probability, likelihood, possibility|词性:名词

Etymology->
From im- (not) + probability.  The "im-" prefix comes from Latin, meaning "not." "Probability" comes from the Latin word "probabilitas," meaning "plausibility, likelihood."
