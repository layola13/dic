overboastful: /ˌoʊvərˈboʊstfəl/ | adj. | 过分夸耀的，自吹自擂的 | Excessively boastful; bragging too much.  Characterized by excessive boasting or bragging. | He was so overboastful about his achievements that everyone started to avoid him. 他对自己的成就如此自吹自擂，以至于每个人都开始躲避他。|  boastful, bragging, vainglorious, arrogant, conceited | modest, humble, unassuming | adjective
