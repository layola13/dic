flea-marketer:/ˈfliː ˌmɑːrkɪtər/| n.|跳蚤市场摊贩；经常逛跳蚤市场的人|A person who sells goods at a flea market or frequently attends flea markets. |例句：The flea-marketer had a wide array of vintage jewelry.  跳蚤市场摊贩摆放着琳琅满目的复古珠宝。

例句：My aunt is a seasoned flea-marketer, always finding hidden treasures. 我阿姨是一位经验丰富的跳蚤市场常客，总能找到隐藏的宝藏。|近义词：vendor, seller, bargain hunter|反义词:buyer, purchaser|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "flea market" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person or thing associated with a particular activity). "Flea market" itself is a calque (loan translation) of the French marché aux puces ("market of the fleas"), referring to the possibility of second-hand goods being infested with fleas.
