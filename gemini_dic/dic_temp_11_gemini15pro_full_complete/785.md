absorbedness:/əbˈzɔːrbdnəs/ /əbˈsɔːrbdnɪs/
|n.|全神贯注，专注|The state of being completely engrossed or preoccupied; deep concentration.|Her absorbedness in the book made her oblivious to the world around her. 她对这本书的全神贯注使她忘记了周围的世界。|近义词：engrossment, preoccupation, immersion, concentration|反义词：distraction, inattention|词性:名词

absorbedness:/əbˈzɔːrbdnəs/ /əbˈsɔːrbdnɪs/
|n.|吸收性|The ability of a material to absorb something, such as a liquid or gas.|The absorbedness of the sponge is remarkable. 这块海绵的吸水性非常好。|近义词：absorbency, porosity|反义词：impermeability|词性:名词
