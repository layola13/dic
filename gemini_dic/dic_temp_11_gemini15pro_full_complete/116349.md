mastery:/ˈmɑːstəri/ /ˈmæstəri/| n. |掌握，精通，控制|Comprehensive knowledge or skill in a particular subject or accomplishment.  The state of being in control of something.|She has achieved mastery of several languages. 她精通好几门语言。|近义词：expertise, proficiency, command, control, dominance|反义词：ineptitude, incompetence, weakness, inability|noun
