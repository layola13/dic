calciform:/ˈkæl.sɪ.fɔːrm/ | adj. | 杯状的，似杯状的 | Shaped like a cup or chalice. |  The calciform corolla of the flower attracted the hummingbird.  这朵花的杯状花冠吸引了蜂鸟。| 近义词: cup-shaped, cupulate, cyathiform | 反义词: (No direct antonym, consider shapes like 'flat', 'linear' depending on the context) | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *calciformis*, from *calix* (cup, chalice) + *-formis* (having the form of).
