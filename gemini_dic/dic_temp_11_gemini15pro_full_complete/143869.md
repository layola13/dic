outpointed:/ˌaʊtˈpɔɪntɪd/
verb
以点数击败
defeated by scoring more points in a competition, especially boxing or other sports judged by points.
例句：The young boxer outpointed the champion.  年轻的拳击手以点数击败了冠军。

例句：She was outpointed by her opponent in the fencing final.  她在击剑决赛中被对手以点数击败。
近义词：outdo, surpass, defeat, beat
反义词：lose to
词性：v.
