intercomplexity:/ˌɪntər kəmˈpleksɪti/ | n. |相互复杂性，交互复杂性|The state of being intricately interconnected and mutually influencing, where multiple complex systems interact and give rise to emergent properties and behaviors.|The intercomplexity of global markets makes predicting economic trends challenging. 全球市场的相互复杂性使得预测经济趋势具有挑战性.|Synonyms: interconnectedness, interdependence, interaction, entanglement|Antonyms: simplicity, independence, isolation|noun
