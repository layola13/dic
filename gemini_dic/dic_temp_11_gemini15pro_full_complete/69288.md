feature-length:/ˈfiːtʃə(r) leŋθ/
adj.
长片的，足够长的
Having the length of a full-length film, typically over 70 minutes.
This documentary is feature-length, clocking in at 90 minutes.  这部纪录片是长片，时长90分钟。
The director is known for his feature-length animated movies.  这位导演以其长篇动画电影而闻名。
近义词:full-length
反义词:short
