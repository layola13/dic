histogenesis:/ˌhɪstoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
组织发生;组织形成
The formation and development of the tissues of an organism.
The histogenesis of bone involves the differentiation of mesenchymal cells into osteoblasts.  骨的组织发生涉及间充质细胞分化为成骨细胞。
The study of histogenesis helps us understand how different tissues develop and function. 对组织发生的研究有助于我们理解不同组织是如何发育和运作的。
近义词: tissue development, tissue formation
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 tissue degeneration 组织退化)
词性: noun


Etymology: histo- (tissue) + genesis (origin, creation)
From Greek histos "tissue" + genesis "origin, creation"
