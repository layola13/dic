nationalising:/ˈnæʃnəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
国有化
The act of bringing an industry or assets under the control of the government.
The government is nationalising the railways. 政府正在将铁路国有化。
The nationalising of key industries was a central part of their policy. 将关键产业国有化是他们政策的核心部分。
nationalization, state ownership
privatization, denationalization
