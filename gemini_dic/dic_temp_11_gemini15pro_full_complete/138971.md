nyctibune: /ˈnɪktɪˌbjuːn/ | n. |夜猫子，夜行动物 | A person or animal that is active at night. |  例句：Owls are nyctibunes, hunting their prey in the darkness. 猫头鹰是夜行动物，在黑暗中捕猎。

例句：He's a real nyctibune, staying up late every night. 他是个真正的夜猫子，每天晚上都熬夜。| 近义词：night owl, nocturnal animal | 反义词：early bird, diurnal animal | 词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *nyctibi*, from Latin *nyctibius*, from Ancient Greek *νυκτίβιος* (nuktibios, “night-living”), from *νύξ* (nux, “night”) + *βίος* (bios, “life”).
