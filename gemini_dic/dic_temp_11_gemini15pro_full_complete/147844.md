overqualified:/ˌoʊvərˈkwɑːlɪfaɪd/ | adj. |资历过高的，能力过剩的 | Having qualifications or experience that are too high or extensive for a particular job. |  She was overqualified for the assistant's position, having previously worked as a manager. 她资历过高，不适合助理的职位，因为她之前做过经理。
