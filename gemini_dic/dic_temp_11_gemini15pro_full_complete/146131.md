overempirically:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈpɪrɪkli/ | adv. | 过于经验地，过度依赖经验地 | Relying excessively or solely on observation and experiment, without sufficient regard for theory or deduction. |  His approach to the problem was criticized as being overempirical, neglecting the established theoretical framework. 他解决问题的方法被批评为过于经验主义，忽略了已建立的理论框架。|  The study was overempirically based, lacking a strong theoretical foundation. 这项研究过度依赖经验，缺乏坚实的理论基础。| 近义词: excessively empirical, overly practical | 反义词: theoretical, deductive | 词性: 副词
