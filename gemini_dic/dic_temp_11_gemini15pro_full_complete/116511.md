mateship:/ˈmeɪtʃɪp/ | n. | 友情；伙伴关系；同志情谊 |  A close bond of friendship and loyalty, particularly associated with Australian culture. It emphasizes equality, mutual support, and camaraderie. |  Example 1: Their mateship was forged during their time in the army.  他们的友谊是在他们在军队服役期间建立的。

Example 2:  Mateship is a core value in Australian society.  伙伴关系是澳大利亚社会的一个核心价值观。 |  Synonyms: camaraderie, fellowship, friendship, brotherhood, sisterhood | Antonyms: enmity, hostility, rivalry | noun
