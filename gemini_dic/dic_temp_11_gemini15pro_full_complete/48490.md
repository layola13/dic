deepeningly:/ˈdiːpənɪŋli/
adverb|加深地，更深地|In a manner that becomes increasingly deep or profound.|The crisis is deepeningly affecting the global economy. 这场危机正日益加深地影响着全球经济。|increasingly, progressively, more and more|superficially, lightly
Etymology: From "deepen" + "-ing" + "-ly". "Deepen" comes from Old English "dēopian," meaning "to make deep."
