anaemia:/əˈniːmiə/ (US) /əˈniːmɪə/ (UK)| n. |贫血|A condition in which there is a deficiency of red cells or of haemoglobin in the blood, resulting in pallor and weariness.|She suffered from anaemia after the surgery. 她手术后患上了贫血。|近义词:iron deficiency|反义词: plethora (of red blood cells)|词性:noun

anaemic:/əˈniːmɪk/ (US) /əˈniːmɪk/ (UK)| adj. |贫血的|Suffering from anaemia.|He looked anaemic and tired. 他看起来面色苍白，很疲惫。|近义词:pale, wan, sickly|反义词:ruddy, healthy|词性:adjective
