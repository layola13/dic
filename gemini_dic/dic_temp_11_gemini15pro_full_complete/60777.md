electromagnetic:/ɪˌlektroʊmæɡˈnɛtɪk/| adj.|电磁的|Relating to the interrelation of electric currents or fields and magnetic fields.|例句：Electromagnetic waves travel at the speed of light. 电磁波以光速传播。

例句：The Earth's magnetic field protects us from electromagnetic radiation from space. 地球磁场保护我们免受来自太空的电磁辐射。|近义词：magnetic, electric|反义词:non-magnetic|词性:形容词
