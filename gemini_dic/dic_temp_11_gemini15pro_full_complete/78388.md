get-a-life:/ɡɛt ə laɪf/ | idiom |  过点儿正常生活;  找点正经事做;  别再浪费生命了 |  Used to tell someone to stop wasting their time on trivial or unproductive activities and to do something more worthwhile.  It often implies that the person is obsessed with something unimportant or socially unacceptable. | "He spends all day playing video games. Someone needs to tell him to get a life!" 他整天都在玩电子游戏。得有人告诉他该过点儿正常生活了！ | 近义词:  grow up,  snap out of it | 反义词:  waste time | idiom
