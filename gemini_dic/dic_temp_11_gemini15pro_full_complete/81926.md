grills:/ɡrɪlz/| n. v.|烧烤架，烤肉架；烧烤，拷问|A framework of metal bars used for cooking food over an open fire; the act of cooking food on a grill.  The act of questioning someone intensely.|He cooked hamburgers on the grills. 他在烤架上烤汉堡。|近义词：barbecues, rotisseries|反义词: freezers, refrigerators|n.

grills:/ɡrɪlz/| v.|烧烤，拷问|To cook food on a grill; to question someone intensely.|The police grilled the suspect for hours. 警察对嫌疑人进行了数小时的盘问。|近义词：barbecue, roast, interrogate|反义词: freeze, chill|v.

词源：From Old French *griller, from grille "gridiron," from Vulgar Latin *gratila, diminutive of cratis "wickerwork."


注意事项:  “grill” 作动词时，也可以指“盘问”或“追问”，这时与 “interrogate” 或 “question” 近义。
