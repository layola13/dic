nonsale:/ˈnɒnˌseɪl/ | adj. | 非卖品的，不出售的 | Not for sale; not available for purchase. |  The antique clock was marked "nonsale." 这座古董钟被标注为“非卖品”。|  This prototype is nonsale and only for demonstration purposes.  这个原型是非卖品的，仅用于演示。 |近义词：unavailable, not for sale |反义词: for sale, available |词性:形容词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "sale" (the exchange of a commodity for money).  由表示否定的前缀 "non-" 和 "sale"（销售）组成。
