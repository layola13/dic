frower: (This word is likely a misspelling of "flower") /ˈflaʊər/
n.|花；开花植物；精华；盛时|A reproductive structure in angiosperms, typically colorful and fragrant, that produces seeds.|例句：The flower is beautiful. 这朵花很漂亮。

例句：The cherry blossoms are in full flower. 樱花盛开了。
近义词：bloom, blossom|反义词:weed
词性:名词


flower: /ˈflaʊər/
v. |开花；繁荣；成熟|To produce flowers; to develop fully or richly.|例句：The roses flowered in June. 玫瑰在六月开花。

例句：Her talent flowered early. 她的天赋很早就展现出来了。
近义词：bloom, blossom|反义词:wilt, wither
词性:动词
