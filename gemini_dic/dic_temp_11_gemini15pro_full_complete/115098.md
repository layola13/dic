manganesious:/ˌmæŋɡəˈniːʃəs/
adjective
亚锰的；二价锰的
Containing or relating to divalent manganese.  Characterized by manganese having a valence of two.
Manganese(II) oxide is a manganesious compound. 氧化亚锰是一种亚锰化合物。
Manganesious chloride is light pink in color.  氯化亚锰呈浅粉色。
近义词:manganese(II), divalent manganese
反义词:manganic (referring to manganese(III) or higher oxidation states)
词性:adjective
