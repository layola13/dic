epipharynx:/ˌɛpɪˈfærɪŋks/| n. |咽上部，鼻咽|The part of the pharynx lying above the soft palate, connecting the nasal cavity to the oropharynx.  It is also known as the nasopharynx.|The doctor examined his epipharynx for signs of infection. 医生检查了他的鼻咽部是否有感染迹象。|Synonyms: nasopharynx, rhinopharynx|Antonyms: None|Noun
epipharynx:/ˌɛpɪˈfærɪŋks/| n. |鼻咽|The epipharynx plays a crucial role in breathing and speech. 鼻咽在呼吸和言语中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: nasopharynx, rhinopharynx|Antonyms: None|Noun
