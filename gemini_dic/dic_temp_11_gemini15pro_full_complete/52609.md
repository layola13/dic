digestible:/daɪˈdʒɛstəbl/ /dɪˈdʒɛstɪbl/
adjective
易消化的；可理解的
Easily digested or understood.
例句：
The food was light and digestible.  食物清淡易消化。

The information was presented in a clear and digestible format.  信息以清晰易懂的格式呈现。

近义词：easy to digest, palatable, comprehensible, understandable
反义词：indigestible, incomprehensible
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *digestibilis, from Latin digestus (past participle of digerere "to separate, distribute, arrange; digest"), from dis- "apart" + gerere "to carry, bear."
