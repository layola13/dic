half-english:/ˌhɑːf ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ/ | adj. | 半英语的；夹杂英语的 |  Someone who is half-English has one English parent and one parent of another nationality.  It can also refer to something expressed using a mixture of English and another language. |  Her father is French, so she's half-English. 她父亲是法国人，所以她是半个英国人。|  He spoke in a half-English, half-French patois. 他说着半英语半法语的混合语。 | synonyms: bilingual (when referring to a person), mixed-language | antonyms: monolingual, unilingual | adjective
