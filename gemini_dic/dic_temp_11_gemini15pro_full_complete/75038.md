friezy:/ˈfriːzi/| adj.|雕饰繁复的，饰以雕带的|Decorated with a frieze or friezes; resembling a frieze.|The friezy architecture of the ancient temple was breathtaking. 这座古老寺庙雕饰繁复的建筑令人叹为观止。|Synonyms: ornate, decorated, embellished|Antonyms: plain, simple, undecorated|adjective

frieze:/friːz/| n.|雕带，饰带|A broad horizontal band of sculpted or painted decoration, especially on a wall near the ceiling.|The frieze depicted scenes from Greek mythology.  雕带上描绘了希腊神话中的场景。|Synonyms: band, border, strip|Antonyms: none|noun
