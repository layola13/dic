epigraphic:/ˌɛpɪˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
碑文的；题词的
of or relating to epigraphs or inscriptions.
The epigraphic evidence suggests the temple was built in the 3rd century BC.  碑文证据表明这座寺庙建于公元前3世纪。
The archaeologist carefully documented the epigraphic markings on the ancient tomb. 考古学家仔细记录了古墓上的碑文标记。
近义词:inscribed,  inscriptive
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "uninscribed," "blank")
词性:adjective
