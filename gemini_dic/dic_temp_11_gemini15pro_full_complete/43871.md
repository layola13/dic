cresses:/ˈkrɛsɪz/| n.|西洋菜，水田芥|A small, pungent-tasting plant of the mustard family, often eaten raw in salads.|例句：I added some fresh cresses to my sandwich. 我在三明治里加了一些新鲜的西洋菜。
