fissional:/ˈfɪʃənəl/| adj.|裂变的，分裂的|Relating to or causing fission, especially nuclear fission.|例句：The fissional process releases tremendous energy. 裂变过程释放巨大的能量。

例句：Uranium-235 is a fissional material. 铀-235是一种可裂变材料。|近义词：fissile, divisible, cleavable|反义词：fusional|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From fission + -al.  Fission comes from Latin *fissionem,  the accusative of *fissio ("a cleaving, splitting"), derived from fissus, the past participle of findere ("to cleave, split").
