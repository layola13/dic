areopagite:/ˌæriˈɒpəɡaɪt/
noun
雅典最高法院法官；亚略巴古的成员
A member of the Areopagus, the ancient Athenian council.
Paul addressed the Areopagites in Athens. 保罗在雅典向亚略巴古的法官们发表了演讲。
Synonyms: judge, councilor, magistrate
Antonyms: commoner, civilian
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *Areopagita, from Ancient Greek Ἀρεοπαγίτης (Areopagítēs), from Ἄρειος πάγος (Áreios págos, “Areopagus”).
