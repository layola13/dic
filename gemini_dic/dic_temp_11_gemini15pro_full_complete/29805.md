caudicle:/ˈkɔːdɪkəl/| n.|小柄，花柄，花粉块柄|A small stalk, stem, or support, especially the stalk that attaches the pollen masses in orchids to the viscidium.|The caudicle of the orchid is delicate and easily broken.|Synonym: stalk, stem, pedicel|Antonym: N/A|noun
