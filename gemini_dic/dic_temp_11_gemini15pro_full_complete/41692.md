corrie:/ˈkɔːri/ (UK), /ˈkɔːri/ (US)| n.|山间盆地，圈谷|A circular hollow on the side of a mountain, formed by glacial erosion.|The climbers found shelter in a small corrie.|Synonyms: cirque, cwm|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, could consider peak, summit)|Noun
