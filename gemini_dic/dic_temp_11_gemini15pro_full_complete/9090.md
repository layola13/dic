antimodernnesses:/ˌæntiˈmɒdərnɪsnɪsɪz/ /ˌæntiˈmɑːdərnɪsnɪsɪz/
noun
反现代性（复数）
The state or quality of being opposed to modernism, often characterized by a preference for traditional values and practices.  The various antimodernnesses of the different groups made it hard to form a cohesive movement.
例句：
The artist's work explored the antimodernnesses present in contemporary society.  这位艺术家的作品探讨了当代社会中存在的反现代性。

His writings reflect the antimodernnesses of his rural upbringing. 他的作品反映了他乡村教育中的反现代性。

近义词：traditionalism, conservatism, reaction
反义词：modernism, modernity, progressivism
词性：n.
