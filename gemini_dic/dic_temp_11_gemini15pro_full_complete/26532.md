cabernet:/ˌkæbərˈneɪ/ /ˌkæbərˈnet/ | n. | 赤霞珠（一种红葡萄及其酿造的葡萄酒） | A red wine grape variety originally from Bordeaux, France, widely grown elsewhere and known for producing full-bodied, tannic wines.  It is one of the six varieties permitted in the red blends of Bordeaux. |  例句：This Cabernet Sauvignon has a rich, complex aroma.  这赤霞珠红葡萄酒有着浓郁复杂的香气。

例句：We enjoyed a bottle of Cabernet with dinner. 我们晚餐喝了一瓶赤霞珠。| 近义词：Bordeaux (often refers to blends containing Cabernet), Merlot (another Bordeaux grape), red wine | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider white wine as a contrasting category) | 词性: noun
