frivolity:/frɪˈvɑːləti/| n.|轻浮；轻佻；无聊的事；琐事|Lack of seriousness; behavior that is silly or lighthearted.  The state of being frivolous.|Her frivolity annoyed her serious colleagues. 她的轻浮惹恼了她严肃的同事们。|Synonyms: levity, flippancy, silliness, triviality|Antonyms: seriousness, gravity, solemnity|noun

frivolous:/ˈfrɪvələs/| adj.|轻浮的；轻佻的；无聊的；琐碎的|Not having any serious purpose or value.|He made a frivolous complaint. 他提出了一个无聊的投诉。|Synonyms: silly, foolish, trifling, petty|Antonyms: serious, important, significant|adjective
