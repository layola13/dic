nonallegation:/ˌnɒnˌæləˈɡeɪʃn/ | n. | 非指控; 不是指控 | The statement was a nonallegation, simply a recounting of the facts.  这个陈述并非指控，只是对事实的叙述。 |  A careful reading shows this is a nonallegation. 仔细阅读表明这不是指控。 | 近义词: denial, refutation, contradiction | 反义词: allegation, accusation, charge | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "allegation" (from Late Latin *allegationem, accusative of allegatio, meaning "citation," from Latin allegare "to send as a deputy," related to Latin legare "to send as an ambassador, bequeath")
