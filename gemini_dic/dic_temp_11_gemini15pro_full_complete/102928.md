jaywalked:/ˈdʒeɪwɔːkt/ | v. | 乱穿马路 | To cross a street illegally or carelessly, not at a designated crosswalk or intersection. |  I saw him jaywalked across the busy street. 我看到他乱穿马路。| Synonyms: cross illegally, cross carelessly | Antonyms: cross safely, use crosswalk | verb
