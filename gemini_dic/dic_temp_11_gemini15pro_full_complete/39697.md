considerer:/kənˈsɪdərər/
n.
考虑者；思考者
One who considers; a person who thinks carefully about something.
She is a careful considerer of all options before making a decision.  她是一位在做决定前会仔细考虑所有选项的人。
近义词: thinker, ponderer, contemplator, deliberator
反义词: none (lack of consideration is implied by other words, not a direct opposite of the person)
词性: noun

Etymology:
From the verb "consider" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Consider" comes from Old French "considerer" and ultimately from Latin "considerare" meaning "to contemplate, observe attentively," literally "to observe the stars," from "com-" (together) + "sidus" (star, constellation).
