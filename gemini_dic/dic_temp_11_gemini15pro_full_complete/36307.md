cohesive:/koʊˈhiːsɪv/ /kəʊˈhiːsɪv/| adj.|粘着的，有结合力的，团结的|Characterized by or causing cohesion; tending to stick together; united.|The team demonstrated a cohesive spirit throughout the tournament. 这支队伍在整个锦标赛中展现了团结的精神。|近义词：united, close-knit, coherent, integrated|反义词:incoherent, disunited, fragmented|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin cohaesivus, from cohaerere "to cleave together," from com- "together" + haerere "to stick."
