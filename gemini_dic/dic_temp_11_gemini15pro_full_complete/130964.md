nonassonant:/nɒnˈæsənənt/
adjective
不谐音的；非谐音的
lacking assonance; not assonant.
The poet deliberately used nonassonant words to create a jarring effect. 诗人故意使用不谐音的词语来制造一种不和谐的效果。
近义词: dissonant, inharmonious
反义词: assonant, harmonious
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + assonant, from French assonant, from assoner "to assonate," from Latin assonare "to respond," from ad- "to" + sonare "to sound."
