discovering:/dɪˈskʌvərɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
发现，发觉
the act of finding something unexpectedly or during a search.
例句：We are discovering new species all the time. 我们一直在发现新的物种。

例句：She is discovering the joys of gardening. 她正在发现园艺的乐趣。

近义词：finding, unearthing, exploring, learning, detecting
反义词：hiding, concealing, ignoring, overlooking
词性：动词(现在分词)


discover:/dɪˈskʌvər/
verb
发现，发觉
find unexpectedly or during a search.
例句：Scientists discovered a new planet. 科学家们发现了一颗新的行星。

例句：I discovered a great new restaurant. 我发现了一家很棒的新餐厅。

近义词：find, unearth, explore, learn, detect
反义词：hide, conceal, ignore, overlook
词性：动词

词源：From Old French *descovrir, from des- (“dis-”) + covrir (“to cover”), from Latin cooperīre.
