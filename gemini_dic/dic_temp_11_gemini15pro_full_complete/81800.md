grenadillo:/ˌɡrɛnəˈdɪloʊ/ (US), /ˌɡrɛnəˈdɪləʊ/ (UK)| n.|百香果，西番莲果，格拉纳迪拉木|A tropical American passion flower with edible fruit; the fruit of this plant.  The wood of certain trees of the genus Dalbergia, used in making musical instruments.|例句：I love the flavor of grenadillo. 我喜欢百香果的味道。

例句：The grenadillo wood gives the clarinet a warm tone. 格拉纳迪拉木赋予单簧管温暖的音色。|近义词：passion fruit, maracuja|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
