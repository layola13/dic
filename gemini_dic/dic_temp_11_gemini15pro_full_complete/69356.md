federated:/ˈfɛdəˌreɪtɪd/
adjective
联邦的；联合的；同盟的
Constituting or formed into a federation.  Joined in a league or federation.
例句：The federated states of Micronesia. 密克罗尼西亚联邦州。

例句：A federated republic.  一个联邦共和国。

近义词：united, allied, confederated, leagued, joined
反义词：independent, separate, disunited
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin foederatus (“leagued, allied”), past participle of foederare (“to make a treaty, to ally”), from Latin foedus (“treaty, league, alliance”) + -atus (past participle suffix).
