assegai:/ˈæsɪɡaɪ/ /ˈæsəɡaɪ/| n.| 刺矛，短矛 |A slender, iron-tipped spear, used chiefly in southern Africa.| The Zulu warriors charged with their assegais raised.  祖鲁武士们高举着他们的刺矛冲锋陷阵。|例句：The hunter skillfully threw the assegai, bringing down the antelope. 猎人熟练地投掷刺矛，击倒了羚羊。

|近义词：spear, javelin, lance|反义词: shield (not a direct opposite, but represents defense) |词性: noun
