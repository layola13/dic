dextrose:/ˈdɛkstroʊs/| n. |葡萄糖|A simple sugar (a monosaccharide) also known as glucose or grape sugar; it is a crystalline dextro-rotatory sugar found in plants and honey and is readily absorbed by the body. It is an important carbohydrate and is used in the body as a source of energy and as a metabolic intermediate.|Dextrose is often given intravenously to patients who need a quick energy boost.  葡萄糖经常被静脉注射给需要快速补充能量的病人。

Dextrose solutions are commonly used in baking. 葡萄糖溶液常用于烘焙。|近义词：glucose, grape sugar|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
