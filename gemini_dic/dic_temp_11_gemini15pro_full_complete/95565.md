incoherent:/ˌɪnkəʊˈhɪərənt/ (US) /ˌɪnkəˈhɪrənt/ (UK)| adj.|不连贯的，语无伦次的，无条理的|unable to express yourself clearly, or expressed in a way that is difficult to understand|例句：His speech was rambling and incoherent. 他的讲话杂乱无章，语无伦次。|近义词：confused, rambling, illogical, disjointed|反义词：coherent, lucid, articulate|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *incohaerent-* "not sticking together," from *in-* "not" + *cohaerent-* (see coherent).
