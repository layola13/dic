fuelers:/ˈfjuːələrz/
noun
加油员；燃料添加者
People who supply fuel to vehicles or machinery.
The fuelers quickly filled the airplane's tanks. 加油员迅速加满了飞机的油箱。
The fuelers were busy during the rush hour.  加油员在高峰时段很忙。
synonyms: gas attendants, refuelers
antonyms:  N/A (lack of a direct antonym)
