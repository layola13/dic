monocrotism:/ˈmɒnəkrɒtɪzəm/ /ˈmɑːnəkrɑːtɪzəm/
n.
单搏脉
A condition characterized by a single beat or a single prominent peak in the pulse wave.
The patient exhibited monocrotism, indicating a serious cardiovascular issue.  病人表现出单搏脉，表明存在严重的心血管问题。
Near synonyms: single-peaked pulse, single-beat pulse
Antonyms: dicrotism, tricrotism
noun
