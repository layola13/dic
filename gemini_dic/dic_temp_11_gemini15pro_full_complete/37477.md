commissure:/ˈkɒmɪʃə(r)/ /ˈkɑːmɪʃər/
n.
接合处；连合； commissure
a point or line at which two things join or intersect, especially the point at which the corners of the lips meet or the eyelids join at either corner of the eye.
The surgeon repaired the commissure of the lips.  外科医生修复了嘴唇的接合处。
The anterior commissure connects the two cerebral hemispheres. 前连合连接左右大脑半球。
joint, junction, connection, seam, juncture
separation, disjunction, division
noun
