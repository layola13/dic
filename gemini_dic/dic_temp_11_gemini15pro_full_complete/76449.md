gainsay:/ɡeɪnˈseɪ/ /ˌɡeɪnˈseɪ/| v.|否认，反驳，否定|To deny or contradict; to dispute or oppose.|例句：No one could gainsay the evidence. 没有人可以否认证据。|例句：It is difficult to gainsay his claims. 很难反驳他的说法。|近义词：deny, dispute, contradict, controvert|反义词：confirm, admit, acknowledge, concede|词性:动词
