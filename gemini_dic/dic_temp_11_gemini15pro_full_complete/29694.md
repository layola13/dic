cathode:/ˈkæθoʊd/ (US) /ˈkæθəʊd/ (UK)| n.|阴极|The negatively charged electrode in an electrochemical cell or electron tube. It attracts positively charged cations.|Electrons flow from the anode to the cathode. 电子从阳极流向阴极。|The cathode ray tube is a vacuum tube containing one or more electron guns, and a fluorescent screen used to view images.  阴极射线管是一种真空管，包含一个或多个电子枪和一个用于查看图像的荧光屏。|Synonyms: negative electrode, negative pole|Antonyms: anode, positive electrode|noun
