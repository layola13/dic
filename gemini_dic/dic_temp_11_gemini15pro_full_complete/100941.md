inundate: /ˈɪnʌndeɪt/ | vt. | 淹没，泛滥，使应接不暇 | to flood, overflow; to overwhelm with something | The town was inundated after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，小镇被淹没了。 | The office was inundated with applications for the new position.  办公室收到了大量新职位的申请。 |  近义词：flood, overwhelm, swamp, deluge | 反义词：drain, empty | 词性：动词

inundation: /ˌɪnʌnˈdeɪʃn/ | n. | 淹没，泛滥，大量 | the rising of a body of water and its overflowing onto normally dry land; an overwhelming abundance of something | The inundation caused by the broken dam destroyed many homes.  溃坝造成的洪水摧毁了许多房屋。 |  The company received an inundation of complaints after the product recall. 产品召回后，公司收到了大量的投诉。|  近义词：flood, deluge, torrent | 反义词：drought, lack | 词性：名词

词源: From Latin *inundatus, past participle of inundare "to flood, overflow," from in- "in, into" + unda "wave."
