multisulcate:/ˌmʌltiˈsʌlkeɪt/
adjective
多沟的，多槽的
Having many grooves or furrows.
The multisulcate pollen grains were easily identifiable under the microscope.  这些多沟的花粉粒在显微镜下很容易识别。
The archaeologist discovered a multisulcate seed from an ancient plant. 考古学家发现了一颗来自古代植物的多沟种子。

近义词: grooved, furrowed, ribbed
反义词: smooth, unfurrowed

词性: adjective
