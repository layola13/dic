dermoid:/ˈdɜːrmɔɪd/| adj. n.|皮样囊肿的，皮样囊肿|A dermoid cyst is a sac-like growth containing structures such as hair, fluid, teeth, or skin glands that are normally found in the dermal layer of the skin.|例句：The doctor diagnosed a dermoid cyst on her ovary. 医生诊断出她的卵巢上有一个皮样囊肿。

|例句：Dermoid cysts are usually benign. 皮样囊肿通常是良性的。|近义词：teratoma|反义词:|词性: adjective, noun
