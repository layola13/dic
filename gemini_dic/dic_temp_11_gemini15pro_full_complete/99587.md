interinstitutional:/ˌɪntərˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃənl/
adjective
机构间的
Existing or occurring between institutions.
例句：The project requires interinstitutional collaboration.  这个项目需要机构间的合作。

例句：Interinstitutional agreements facilitate student exchange programs. 机构间协议促进了学生交换项目。

近义词：interagency, collaborative, joint
反义词：intrainstitutional, internal
词性: adjective
