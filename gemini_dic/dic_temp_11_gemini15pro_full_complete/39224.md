conglutinant:/kənˈɡluːtɪnənt/
adjective
粘合的；胶凝的
Having the power of uniting or gluing together.
The carpenter used a conglutinant substance to bond the two pieces of wood. 木匠用一种粘合剂把两块木头粘在一起。
synonyms: adhesive, binding, cementing, gluing, sticking
antonyms: separating, dividing, detaching
词性: adjective

conglutinant:/kənˈɡluːtɪnənt/
noun
粘合剂
A substance that unites or glues together.
Egg white can act as a conglutinant in some recipes. 蛋清在某些食谱中可以起到粘合剂的作用。
synonyms: adhesive, glue, binder, cement
antonyms: solvent, thinner
词性: noun
