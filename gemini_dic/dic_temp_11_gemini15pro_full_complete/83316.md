gyp:/dʒɪp/| n. v. |欺骗；敲诈；抱怨；(使)难受|A swindle or fraud; an act of cheating or overcharging someone. Also, to complain or fret, or to cause someone distress or discomfort.|

例句：
He felt he'd been gypped when he saw the price on the bill.
他看到账单上的价格时觉得自己被骗了。

例句：
Stop gypping about the weather, we can't change it.
别抱怨天气了，我们改变不了它。

近义词：swindle, cheat, defraud, overcharge, gripe, complain, fret|
反义词：be honest, be fair|
词性: noun, verb|


gyp:/dʒɪp/| v. |(使)难受；(使)痛苦|To cause someone distress or discomfort.|

例句：
