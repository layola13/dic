conformal:/kənˈfɔːrməl/ | adj. | 保形的，共形的 | Preserving the angle between curves; relating to or involving a conformal map. | Example 1: The Mercator projection is a conformal map, preserving angles between lines of longitude and latitude. 麦卡托投影是一种保角地图，保留了经纬线之间的角度。

Example 2: Conformal geometry is the study of geometric properties that are preserved under conformal transformations. 共形几何学是研究在共形变换下保持不变的几何性质。 | Synonym: isogonal, angle-preserving | Antonym: non-conformal | word type: adjective
