/ˌhaɪpərˌɡæməˌɡlɒbjʊlɪˈniːmiə/ (British) /ˌhaɪpərˌɡæməˌɡlɑːbjʊləˈniːmiə/ (American)| n. |高球蛋白血症|A condition characterized by an increased level of gamma globulins in the blood.  Gamma globulins are a type of antibody, and elevated levels can indicate various underlying conditions, including infections, autoimmune diseases, and certain cancers.|Example: Hypergammaglobulinaemia can be a sign of chronic infection. 高球蛋白血症可能是慢性感染的迹象。|Example: The doctor ordered further tests to determine the cause of the patient's hypergammaglobulinaemia. 医生要求进一步检查以确定患者高球蛋白血症的原因。|Synonym:  hypergammaglobulinemia (American spelling), gammopathy |Antonym: hypogammaglobulinaemia (low levels of gamma globulins)|Noun
