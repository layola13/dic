bondabilities:/ˌbɒndəˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 担保能力；可担保性 |  The capacity of an entity or asset to be used as collateral for a loan or bond.  It refers to the perceived creditworthiness and value that allows it to secure financing. | The bondabilities of the company's assets were assessed before the loan was approved. 该公司资产的担保能力在贷款获批前得到了评估。 | Synonyms: creditworthiness, collateral value, security | Antonyms: insolvency, uncreditworthiness | noun
