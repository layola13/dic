anders:/ˈɑːndərz/ | proper n. | 安德斯（人名） | A Scandinavian given name, a variant of Andreas, meaning "manly," "brave," or "warrior." Ultimately derived from the Greek name Ἀνδρέας (Andreas), meaning "man" or "masculine."  | Anders Celsius was a famous Swedish astronomer.  安德斯·摄尔修斯是一位著名的瑞典天文学家。 |  |  | noun
