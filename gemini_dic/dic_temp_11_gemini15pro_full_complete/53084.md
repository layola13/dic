diphtheria:/dɪfˈθɪəriə/ /dɪpˈθɪriə/| n. |白喉|An acute, highly contagious bacterial disease causing inflammation of the mucous membranes, formation of a false membrane in the throat which hinders breathing and swallowing, and potentially fatal heart and nerve damage.|Diphtheria is a serious disease that can be prevented by vaccination. 白喉是一种可以通过接种疫苗来预防的严重疾病。| Diphtheria outbreaks still occur in countries with low vaccination rates. 在疫苗接种率低的国家，白喉疫情仍然时有发生。 |Synonym:  none (Specific medical term)|Antonym: none (Specific medical term)|Noun
