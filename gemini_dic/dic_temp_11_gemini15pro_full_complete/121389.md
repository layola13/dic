mishearing:/ˌmɪsˈhɪərɪŋ/ | n. | 误听; 听错 | An act of hearing something incorrectly. | I thought she said "cat," but it was a mishearing; she actually said "hat." 我以为她说的是“猫”，但那是听错了；她实际上说的是“帽子”。 |  misinterpretation, misunderstanding | correct hearing | noun
