downcastness: /ˌdaʊnˈkæstnɪs/
n.
沮丧，垂头丧气，闷闷不乐
A state of being sad, discouraged, or dejected.
Her downcastness was evident in her slumped posture and quiet voice. 她的沮丧在她低垂的姿势和安静的声音中显而易见。
The team's downcastness after the loss was palpable.  球队输球后的沮丧情绪是显而易见的。
近义词：dejection, despondency, gloominess, sadness, melancholy
反义词：cheerfulness, happiness, elation, joy, optimism
