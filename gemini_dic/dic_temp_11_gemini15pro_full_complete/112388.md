lossful:/ˈlɔːsfʊl/
adjective
有损失的，导致损失的，徒劳的
causing or resulting in loss; unprofitable
例句：The company made a lossful investment. 这家公司做了一项亏损的投资。

例句：It was a lossful endeavor, costing time and resources with no return. 这是一项徒劳的努力，耗费了时间和资源却没有任何回报。

近义词：unprofitable, losing, wasteful, detrimental, damaging
反义词：profitable, gainful, beneficial, advantageous
词性: adjective
