galena:/ɡəˈliːnə/| n.|方铅矿|A common lead sulfide mineral (PbS) that is the chief ore of lead.|Galena is a heavy, metallic mineral that is easily recognizable by its cubic crystals and bright, silvery-gray color. 方铅矿是一种重金属矿物，很容易通过其立方晶体和明亮的银灰色来识别。

Galena is the most important ore of lead and is mined extensively throughout the world. 方铅矿是最重要的铅矿石，在世界各地被广泛开采。|近义词：lead glance, lead sulfide|反义词: 无|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *galena, from Latin galēna (“lead ore”), possibly from Ancient Greek γαλήνη (galḗnē, “lead dross, lead ore”).
