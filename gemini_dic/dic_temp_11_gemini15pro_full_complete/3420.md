afraid: /əˈfreɪd/ (UK), /əˈfreɪd/ (US)| adj. |害怕的，担心的| Feeling fear or anxiety; worried.|  I'm afraid of spiders. 我很怕蜘蛛。| 近义词: scared, frightened, fearful, apprehensive | 反义词: brave, courageous, bold, unafraid | 词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *afraid, *afrayed, past participles of affrayen (“to frighten”), from Anglo-Norman afrayer, Old French esfreer (“to frighten”), from Vulgar Latin *exfridāre, from Latin frigidus (“cold”).  The original sense was thus "chilled with fear."
