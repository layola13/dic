incontaminate:/ɪnkənˈtæmɪneɪt/
adjective
未受污染的；洁净的
Not contaminated; free from contamination.
The scientists worked in an incontaminate environment. 科学家们在一个无污染的环境中工作。
The water source is incontaminate and safe to drink. 水源未受污染，可以安全饮用。
近义词:pure, pristine, unpolluted, unblemished, immaculate
反义词:contaminated, polluted, tainted, defiled
词性:adjective

incontaminate:/ɪnkənˈtæmɪneɪt/
verb
不污染；使洁净
To make free from contamination.
They incontaminated the area after the chemical spill.  他们在化学品泄漏后净化了该地区。
We must incontaminate the water supply. 我们必须净化水源。
近义词:purify, cleanse, sanitize, sterilize
反义词:contaminate, pollute, taint, defile
词性:verb


词源:
From in- (not) + contaminate (from Latin contaminare, to pollute).
