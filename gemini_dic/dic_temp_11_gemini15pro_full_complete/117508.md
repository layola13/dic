meekest:/ˈmiːkɪst/| adj.|最温顺的，最谦恭的|Superlative form of meek: most meek.  Having or showing a quiet and gentle nature: not wanting to fight or argue with other people.|例句：He was the meekest man I ever knew. 他是我认识的最温顺的人。|例句：She spoke in the meekest of voices. 她用最谦卑的声音说话。|近义词：gentlest, mildest, humblest, quietest|反义词：boldest, proudest, most arrogant|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *meke*, from Old Norse *mjúkr* "soft, mild, gentle," from Proto-Germanic *meukaz*, from PIE *meug- "soft, pliant."
