depresses:/dɪˈprɛsɪz/| v.| 使沮丧，使消沉，降低，压下|  makes (someone) feel unhappy and without hope; reduces the level or strength of something; presses something down. |The news of the job losses depressed everyone.  裁员的消息使每个人都感到沮丧。|Synonyms: saddens, discourages, dejects, lowers, diminishes |Antonyms: elates, encourages, heartens, raises, increases|verb


depressed:/dɪˈprɛst/| adj.| 沮丧的，消沉的，萧条的| (of a person) in a state of general unhappiness and low spirits; (of a place or economy) suffering from a lack of activity or prosperity; (of a part of a surface) pushed or forced downwards. |He felt depressed after losing his job. 他失业后感到沮丧。|Synonyms: sad, dejected, downcast, gloomy, miserable|Antonyms: happy, cheerful, elated, joyful, buoyant|adjective
