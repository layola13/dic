hypersensitizing:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnsɪtaɪzɪŋ/
verb
过敏化；超敏化
Making something excessively sensitive.
The photographer hypersensitized his film to capture images in low light. 摄影师使他的胶卷超敏化以便在低光下拍摄图像。
近义词:sensitizing, heightening
反义词:desensitizing, lessening
词性:verb


hypersensitized:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnsɪtaɪzd/
adjective
过敏的；超敏化的
Having been made excessively sensitive.
The hypersensitized film allowed for stunning night photography. 超敏化的胶卷可以拍摄出令人惊叹的夜景照片。
近义词:sensitized, heightened
反义词:desensitized, lessened
词性:adjective


hypersensitivity:/ˌhaɪpərˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪti/
noun
过敏性；超敏性
The state of being excessively sensitive.
He showed hypersensitivity to certain foods. 他对某些食物表现出过敏反应。
近义词:sensitivity, reactivity
反义词:insensitivity, tolerance
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (meaning "excessive") + sensitizing (from sense + -ize + -ing)
