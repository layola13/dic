isochronous:/aɪˈsɒkrənəs/ /aɪˈsɑːkrənəs/| adj.|等时的，同步的|Occurring at the same time or intervals.  Characterized by or pertaining to isochronism.|例句：The dancers' movements were perfectly isochronous. 舞蹈演员的动作完全同步。

例句：Isochronous data transmission ensures a steady flow of information. 等时数据传输确保信息的稳定流动。|近义词：synchronous, simultaneous, concurrent|反义词：asynchronous, non-synchronous|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *isochrone* (now *isochrone*), from Ancient Greek *ἰσόχρονος* (isókhronos, “equal in time, taking the same time”), from *ἴσος* (ísos, “equal”) + *χρόνος* (khrónos, “time”).
