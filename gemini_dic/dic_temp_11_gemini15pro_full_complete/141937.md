ordinator:/ˈɔːrdɪneɪtər/ | n. | 协调员；纵坐标 |A person who organizes or coordinates something.  Also, the vertical axis on a graph. |The event coordinator made sure everything ran smoothly. 活动协调员确保一切顺利进行。 The y-coordinate is 3.  y坐标是3。| synonyms: organizer, planner, manager, director, administrator, facilitator, y-axis | antonyms:  subordinate, assistant | noun
