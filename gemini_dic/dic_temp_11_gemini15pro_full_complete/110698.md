linearity:/ˌlɪnɪˈærəti/ | n. |线性，线性度|The property of being linear.  A linear relationship exists between two variables if one variable can be expressed as a constant multiple of the other plus a constant term. |The linearity of the sensor's response is crucial for accurate measurements. 传感器的线性响应对于精确测量至关重要。| linearity: The graph demonstrates a clear linearity. 这张图表展示了清晰的线性关系。|近义词：straightness, directness|反义词：nonlinearity, curvature|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *linearitas, from Latin linearis (“of or pertaining to a line”), from linea (“line”).
