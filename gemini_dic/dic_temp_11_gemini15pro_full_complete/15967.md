balsamy: /ˈbɔːlsəmi/ | adj. | 香脂的，有香脂味的，芳香的 | Having the pleasant smell of balsam; fragrant. | The air was balsamy with the scent of pine needles. 空气中弥漫着松针的香味，如同香脂一般。 | The room was filled with a balsamy fragrance. 房间里充满了香脂的香味。 | 近义词: fragrant, aromatic, sweet-smelling | 反义词: fetid, foul, acrid | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French *balsamin, from late Latin balsamum (see balsam).
