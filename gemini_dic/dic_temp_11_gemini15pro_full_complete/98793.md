integrational:/ˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃənəl/
adjective
融合的，一体化的
Relating to or involving integration, the act of combining or bringing together different parts into a whole.
The government implemented new integrational policies to unite the diverse communities. 政府实施了新的融合政策，以团结不同的社区。
The company is undergoing a period of integrational change as it merges with its competitor.  公司正在经历一段融合变革时期，因为它正在与竞争对手合并。
Synonyms: unifying, combining, consolidating, merging
Antonyms: separating, dividing, segregating
词性: 形容词
