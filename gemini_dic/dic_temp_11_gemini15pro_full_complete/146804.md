overimitating: /ˌoʊvərˈɪmɪteɪtɪŋ/
| v. | 过度模仿 | Copying or imitating actions or behaviors excessively, even when they are unnecessary or irrelevant to achieving a goal.  It often refers to children's tendency to meticulously copy all actions of an adult demonstrator, even those that are clearly irrelevant to the task. |  The child was overimitating the adult's actions, even the unnecessary ones.  孩子过度模仿了成年人的动作，甚至包括不必要的动作。| mimic, ape, copy, emulate |  under-imitating, ignoring | verb
