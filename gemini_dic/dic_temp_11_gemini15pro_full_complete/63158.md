ensured:/ɪnˈʃʊərd/| v. |确保，保证|To make certain that something will happen.|例句：He ensured the safety of the passengers. 他确保了乘客的安全。|近义词：guaranteed, secured, ascertained|反义词：jeopardized, risked|verb

ensured:/ɪnˈʃʊərd/| adj. |确定的，得到保证的|(Rarely used as an adjective) Certain to happen.|例句：His victory was ensured. 他的胜利已成定局。|近义词：certain, guaranteed, secure|反义词：uncertain, doubtful, risky|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *ensuren*, from Old French *enseurer*, from *en-* (in) + *seur* (sure), from Latin *securus* (secure).
