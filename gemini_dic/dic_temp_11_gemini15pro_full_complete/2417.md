adjoins:/əˈdʒɔɪnz/| v. |毗邻，邻接|be next to and joined with (something).|例句：Her house adjoins the church. 她的房子紧邻教堂。|例句：The two rooms adjoin. 这两个房间相邻。|近义词：abuts, borders, neighbors, touches, connects|反义词:separates, detaches|verb

Etymology:
From Middle English *ajoinen, from Old French ajoindre, from Latin adjungō (“I join to, attach, unite”), from ad (“to”) + jungō (“I join”).
