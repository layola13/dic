halogens:/ˈhælədʒənz/
n.
卤素
Elements that form group 17 of the periodic table (fluorine, chlorine, bromine, iodine, and astatine) and that are highly reactive.
Fluorine, chlorine, bromine, and iodine are halogens. 氟，氯，溴和碘都是卤素。
reactive nonmetals, Group 17 elements
noble gases
noun
