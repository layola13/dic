hypnagogic:/ˌhɪpnəˈɡɒdʒɪk/ /ˌhɪpnəˈɡɑːdʒɪk/| adj.|入睡前的，睡前幻觉的|Relating to the state immediately before falling asleep, especially the drowsy state characterized by vivid mental imagery.|例句：I often experience hypnagogic hallucinations just before I drift off. 我经常在快要睡着之前经历睡前幻觉。

例句：The hypnagogic state can be a creative time for some people. 对于某些人来说，睡前状态可能是一个富有创造力的时刻。|近义词：presleep, pre-dormant|反义词：post-sleep, waking|词性:形容词
