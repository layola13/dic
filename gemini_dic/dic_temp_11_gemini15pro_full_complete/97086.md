infertileness: /ˌɪnfərˈtɪlnəs/ | n. |不孕，不育|The state of being unable to reproduce; sterility.| The couple struggled with infertility for years. 这对夫妇多年来一直在与不孕症作斗争。| 近义词: sterility, barrenness | 反义词: fertility, fecundity | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From in- (not) + fertile + -ness (noun suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives).  "Fertile" comes from Latin "fertilis" meaning "bearing, producing," from "ferre" meaning "to bear, carry."
