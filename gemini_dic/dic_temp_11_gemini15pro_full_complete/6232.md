amnigenous:/æmˈnɪdʒɪnəs/
adjective
由河流产生的；河生的
born or produced in or by a river
例句：The amnigenous flora thrived along the riverbanks.  河生的植物群沿着河岸繁茂生长。

例句：Amnigenous sediments were deposited at the mouth of the river. 河流沉积物沉积在河口。
近义词：riverine, fluvial
反义词：marine, oceanic
词性: adjective
