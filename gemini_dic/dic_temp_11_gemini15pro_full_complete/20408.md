bite-your-tongue:/ˌbaɪt jər ˈtʌŋ/ | idiom | 忍住不说；保持沉默 | To refrain from speaking, especially when tempted to say something inappropriate or critical. |  Example 1: I wanted to tell her she was wrong, but I bit my tongue. 我想告诉她她是错的，但我忍住了没说。

Example 2:  He bit his tongue to avoid arguing with his boss. 他为了避免和老板争吵而忍住了没说。 | Synonyms: hold your tongue, keep silent, refrain from speaking | Antonyms: speak out, speak your mind, express yourself | idiom
