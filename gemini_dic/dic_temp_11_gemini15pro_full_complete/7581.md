annotine:/ˈænətɪn/ |n.| 毒草亭碱; 一种从毛茛科植物中提取的生物碱|A toxic alkaloid extracted from plants of the Ranunculaceae family, specifically Aconitum anthora. | The annotine in this plant can be lethal.  这种植物中的毒草亭碱可能是致命的。|Synonyms: aconitine (though chemically distinct), alkaloid|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly antidote, remedy) | noun
