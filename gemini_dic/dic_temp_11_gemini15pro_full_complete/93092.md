idyllic:/ɪˈdɪlɪk/ /aɪˈdɪlɪk/
adjective
田园诗般的；牧歌式的；美好的
extremely happy, peaceful, or picturesque.  Characterized by pleasant simple life in natural surroundings.
例句：We spent an idyllic week in the countryside. 我们在乡下度过了一个田园诗般的一周。

例句：The village is an idyllic retreat. 这个村庄是一个田园诗般的世外桃源。

近义词：peaceful, serene, tranquil, picturesque, charming, utopian
反义词：unpleasant, stressful, hectic, chaotic, dystopian
词性：adjective
词源：From Late Latin *idyllicus, from Greek eidyllion ("short poem, especially a pastoral poem"), diminutive of eidos ("form, shape").
