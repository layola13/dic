acceptingness:/əkˈsɛptɪŋnɪs/
n.
接纳; 接受; 宽容; 包容
The state or quality of being accepting; willingness to tolerate or embrace something.
Her acceptingness of his flaws made their relationship stronger. 她对他缺点的接纳使得他们的关系更加牢固。
acceptance, tolerance, receptiveness
resistance, rejection, intolerance
