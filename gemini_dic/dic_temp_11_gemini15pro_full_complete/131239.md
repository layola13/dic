noncapsizable:/ˌnɒnkæpˈsaɪzəbl/ | adj. | 不可倾覆的 |  Not able to be capsized (overturned in the water).  Designed or built in a way that prevents it from turning over. | This new sailboat is designed to be noncapsizable, making it safer for novice sailors. 这艘新的帆船被设计成不可倾覆的，对新手水手来说更安全。| The noncapsizable life raft provided a sense of security during the storm.  这个不可倾覆的救生筏在暴风雨中提供了一种安全感。 | synonyms: unsinkable, stable, buoyant | antonyms: capsizable, unstable, tippy | adjective
