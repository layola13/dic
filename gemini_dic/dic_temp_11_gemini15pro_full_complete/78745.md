gigot:/ˈdʒɪɡət/ (US), /ˈʒiːɡɒt/ (UK)| n. |羊腿；腿肉|A leg of lamb or mutton, especially one roasted or braised.|例句：The gigot of lamb was perfectly cooked. 羊腿烤得恰到好处。

例句：We had a delicious gigot for Easter dinner.  我们复活节晚餐吃了一顿美味的羊腿。|近义词：leg of lamb, haunch|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *gigot*, from Old French *gigue*, meaning "leg," likely of Germanic origin.
