fibreless:/ˈfaɪbələs/| adj.|无纤维的，不含纤维的|Lacking fibers; having no fibrous structure.|The flesh of the fruit was surprisingly fibreless and smooth.|近义词：smooth, pulpy, non-fibrous|反义词:fibrous, stringy|adjective

词源:
From fiber + -less.  "Fiber" comes from the Old French "fibre" and ultimately from the Latin "fibra" meaning "fiber, filament."  The suffix "-less" is an Old English suffix meaning "without."
