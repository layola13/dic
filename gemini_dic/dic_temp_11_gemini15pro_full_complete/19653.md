bilabiate:/ˌbaɪˈleɪbiˌeɪt/ /ˌbaɪˈleɪbiət/
adjective
双唇的，两唇形的
Having two lips, especially of a corolla or calyx, one projecting over the other.  The shape resembles two lips.
The bilabiate corolla of the snapdragon is a striking feature.  金鱼草的两唇形花冠是一个显著的特征。
The sage flower has a distinctly bilabiate calyx. 鼠尾草的花有一个明显的双唇形花萼。
近义词: two-lipped
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly regular, rotate, or other corolla shapes)
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bi- (“two”) + labiatus (“lipped”), past participle of labiō (“to provide with lips”). Ultimately from labium (“lip”).
