homogenized:/həˈmɒdʒənaɪzd/ /həˈmɑːdʒənaɪzd/| adj.|均质化的，同质化的|Made uniform or consistent throughout a substance or mixture.|The milk we buy from the supermarket is usually homogenized. 我们从超市买的牛奶通常是均质化的。|synonyms: uniform, consistent, standardized, unvaried|antonyms: heterogeneous, varied, diverse|adjective

homogenize:/həˈmɒdʒənaɪz/ /həˈmɑːdʒənaɪzd/| v.|使均匀，使均质|To make something uniform or consistent throughout.|The process homogenizes the milk so that the cream doesn't separate.  这个过程使牛奶均质化，这样奶油就不会分离。|synonyms: blend, mix, combine, standardize|antonyms: separate, differentiate, diversify|verb
