muscle: /ˈmʌs(ə)l/ (US), /ˈmʌs(ə)l/ (UK)| vt.|  硬挤，强行推进|To move (something) in a particular direction by using one’s physical strength.| He muscled his way to the front of the crowd. 他挤到了人群的最前面。| synonyms: force, shove, push, thrust | antonyms: yield, concede | verb
