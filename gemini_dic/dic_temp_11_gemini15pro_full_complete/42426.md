counterculturalisms: /ˌkaʊntərˈkʌltʃərəlɪzəmz/
|n.| 反文化主义（复数） |The beliefs and practices of a counterculture, a group that rejects or opposes the dominant values and norms of society.|  The 1960s saw a rise in various counterculturalisms, challenging traditional societal structures.  20世纪60年代出现了各种反文化主义的兴起，挑战了传统的社会结构。|Synonyms:  countercultures,  anti-establishment movements |Antonyms:  mainstream cultures,  established norms| noun
