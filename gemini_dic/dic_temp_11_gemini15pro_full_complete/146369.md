overflog:/ˌoʊvərˈflɒɡ/ /ˌoʊvərˈflɑːɡ/ | vt. |过度鞭笞，过分鞭打| To whip or flog excessively; to punish too harshly by whipping. | The cruel master overflogged his slaves for minor infractions.  残酷的主人因为轻微的违规而过度鞭打他的奴隶。

The captain was accused of overflogging the sailors. 船长被指控过度鞭打水手。


|近义词：flog, whip, scourge, lash|反义词: coddle, pamper |词性: 动词
