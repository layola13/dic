kilohertz:/ˈkɪləhɜːrts/ (US), /ˈkɪləhɜːtz/ (UK)| n. |千赫兹|A unit of frequency equal to one thousand hertz.|The processor runs at a speed of 3.2 kilohertz.  处理器以3.2千赫兹的速度运行。|Synonym: kHz|Antonym: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement.  Could consider extremely low frequencies as a conceptual opposite.)|noun
Etymology: kilo- (thousand) + hertz (unit of frequency named after Heinrich Hertz)
