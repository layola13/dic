distributed: /dɪˈstrɪbjuːtɪd/ /dɪˈstrɪbjətɪd/ | adj. | 分布的，分散的 | spread out over a wide area or among a large number of people | The company has a widely distributed network of offices.  这家公司的办事处网络分布广泛。| synonyms: spread, scattered, dispersed | antonyms: concentrated, localized | adjective

distributed: /dɪˈstrɪbjuːtɪd/ /dɪˈstrɪbjətɪd/ | v. (past participle of distribute) | 分发的，分配的 |  having been shared or allocated | The donated food was distributed among the homeless.  捐赠的食物分发给了无家可归的人。| synonyms: allocated, apportioned, dispensed | antonyms: collected, gathered, hoarded | verb (past participle)
