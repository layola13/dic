nonregulated:/ˌnɒnˈrɛɡjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. |不受监管的，无管制的|Not regulated; without regulations or rules.|  The cryptocurrency market is largely nonregulated, posing risks for investors. 加密货币市场很大程度上不受监管，给投资者带来了风险。|  Many online businesses operate in a nonregulated environment. 许多在线企业在不受监管的环境中运营。| 近义词:unregulated, uncontrolled, unsupervised | 反义词: regulated, controlled, supervised | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
regulated (past participle of regulate): from Late Latin regulatus, past participle of regulare "to control by rule, direct," from Latin regula "rule," from regere "to keep straight, guide, lead, rule."
