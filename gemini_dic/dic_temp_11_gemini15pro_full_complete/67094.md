exploration:/ˌɛkspləˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 探险，探索，勘探 | the act of traveling through an unfamiliar area in order to learn about it |  Example: The exploration of space has led to many discoveries.  太空探索带来了许多发现。

Example:  His latest book details his explorations in the Amazon rainforest. 他最新的书详细描述了他在亚马逊雨林的探险。| 近义词: discovery, investigation, search, quest | 反义词:  concealment, hiding | 词性: noun
