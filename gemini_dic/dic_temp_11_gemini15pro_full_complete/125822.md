murmurousness:/ˈmɜːməɹəsnəs/ | n. |低语声，喃喃声 |The state or quality of being murmurous; a low, indistinct, continuous sound, like that of running water or whispering voices.|The murmurousness of the stream lulled me to sleep. 小溪的潺潺声使我昏昏欲睡。|Synonyms: whispering, muttering, rustling, babbling |Antonyms: silence, quiet, stillness| noun

murmur:/ˈmɜːmə(r)/ | n. v. |低语，喃喃自语;小声抱怨|A low, continuous, indistinct sound; a softly spoken word or words; a complaint uttered in a low voice.|He spoke in a low murmur. 他低声喃喃自语。
The crowd murmured their disapproval.人群低声表示反对。|Synonyms: whisper, mutter, grumble, complain |Antonyms: shout, yell, proclaim| noun, verb
