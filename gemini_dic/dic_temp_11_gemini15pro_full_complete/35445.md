co-sovereign:/koʊˈsɒvrɪn/ /koʊˈsɑːvrɪn/
adjective
共同主权的；共同享有主权的
Sharing sovereign power; exercising sovereignty jointly.
The two nations acted as co-sovereign entities in the disputed territory. 这两个国家在争议地区作为共同主权实体行事。
Co-sovereign states share responsibility for governing a particular territory. 共同主权国家共同承担管理特定领土的责任。

近义词:jointly sovereign, shared sovereignty
反义词:subordinate, dependent
词性:adjective
