grandeur:/ˈɡrændʒər/| n. |宏伟，壮丽，伟大|Splendor and impressiveness, especially of appearance or style.|The grandeur of the mountains took my breath away. 山脉的壮丽让我叹为观止。|近义词：magnificence, majesty, splendor, glory|反义词：insignificance, humility, modesty|词性:名词
Etymology: From Old French "grandeur," from Late Latin "grandor," from Latin "grandis" (great).
