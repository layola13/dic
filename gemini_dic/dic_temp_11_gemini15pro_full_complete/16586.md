barnstorming:/ˈbɑːrnstɔːrmɪŋ/| adj. n.|巡回演出的，轰动一时的；巡回演出，巡回政治演说|Originally referring to theatrical performances in barns, now often used to describe energetic and dramatic campaigning or promotion.|The candidate's barnstorming tour of the state drew huge crowds.  这位候选人在该州的巡回政治演讲吸引了大批群众。|近义词：touring, campaigning, stumping, whirlwind|反义词:static, stationary|adjective, noun

barnstorming:/ˈbɑːrnstɔːrmɪŋ/| n.|巡回演出，巡回政治演说|The act of touring, especially in rural areas, giving performances or speeches.|His barnstorming across the country energized his supporters. 他在全国的巡回演讲激励了他的支持者。|近义词：touring, campaigning, stumping|反义词: staying put|noun
