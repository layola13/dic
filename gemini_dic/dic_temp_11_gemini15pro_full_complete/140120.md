off-side:/ˌɒfˈsaɪd/ /ˌɔːfˈsaɪd/ | adj. n.|越位的，越位位置|Being in a position in a game (especially soccer/football) where it is illegal to play the ball.| The referee blew the whistle for offside. 裁判吹哨示意越位。|Synonym: illegal position|Antonym: onside|adjective, noun

off-side:/ˌɒfˈsaɪd/ /ˌɔːfˈsaɪd/ | adj. n.|偏僻的，远离中心的|Situated away from the main or central part; remote.|  The off-side rule is often misunderstood. 越位规则经常被误解。|Synonym: remote, outlying|Antonym: central, main|adjective, noun
