orthorhombic:/ˌɔːrθəˈrɒmbɪk/ | adj. |斜方晶系的；正交晶系的| Relating to a crystal system characterized by three mutually perpendicular axes of unequal lengths.  A crystal system where the three crystallographic axes are all at right angles to each other, and are all of different lengths.| The mineral olivine commonly crystallizes in the orthorhombic system.  橄榄石通常在斜方晶系中结晶。|Synonyms:  rectangular, orthogonal |Antonyms: cubic, isometric|adjective
