mucilaginous:/ˌmjuːsɪˈlædʒɪnəs/| adj.| 粘液的；粘性的|Covered with or containing mucilage; slimy. |例句：The okra pods were mucilaginous and slippery.  秋葵荚粘糊糊的，滑溜溜的。
