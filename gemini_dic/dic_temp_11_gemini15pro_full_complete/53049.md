dionysus:/daɪ.əˈnaɪ.səs/ | n. |狄俄尼索斯; 酒神 | The Greek god of wine, vegetation, festivity, and theatre.  | Dionysus is often depicted with a thyrsus and surrounded by satyrs. 狄俄尼索斯通常被描绘成手持酒神杖，周围环绕着萨提尔。| Dionysus is associated with grapes and winemaking. 狄俄尼索斯与葡萄和酿酒有关。 |  Synonyms: Bacchus, Liber | Antonyms:  N/A (as a proper noun, it doesn't typically have antonyms) | Noun
