climbed /klaɪmd/ | v. | 爬，攀登 | Past tense of "climb."  It indicates the action of ascending something, such as a mountain, stairs, or a ladder. |  She climbed the mountain. 她爬上了那座山。 |  He climbed the stairs quickly. 他快速地爬上楼梯。 | synonyms: ascended, scaled, mounted | antonyms: descended, dropped, fell | verb

climbed /klaɪmd/ | v. | 上升，增长 | Past tense of "climb." It indicates an increase in value, number, or level.  | The temperature climbed steadily throughout the day.  气温一整天都在稳步上升。 |  Sales climbed by 20% this year. 今年销售额增长了20%。| synonyms: increased, rose, soared | antonyms: decreased, fell, dropped | verb
