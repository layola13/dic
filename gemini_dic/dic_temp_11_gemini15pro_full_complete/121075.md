miscontinuance:/ˌmɪskənˈtɪnjuəns/
n.|中止，中断，停止|The miscontinuance of the treatment led to a relapse. 治疗的中断导致了复发。|例句：The miscontinuance of the medication caused her symptoms to return.  药物的中止导致她的症状复发。|近义词：discontinuation, cessation, interruption, suspension|反义词：continuance, continuation, resumption|词性:名词

Etymology:
From mis- + continuance.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English and Old French origin meaning "wrong," "bad," or "wrongly." "Continuance" comes from the Old French "continuance" and the Latin "continuare," meaning "to continue."
