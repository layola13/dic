emblem:/ˈembləm/ | n. | 象征；标志；徽章 |A visual symbol, design, or figure that represents something else, such as a group, an idea, or a quality. | The eagle is a national emblem of the United States.  鹰是美国的国家象征。 | symbol, logo, badge, insignia, crest | antonym: Not applicable (Emblems represent something; there isn't a direct opposite.) | noun

emblems:/ˈembləmz/ | n. | 象征（复数）；标志（复数）；徽章（复数）| Plural form of "emblem," referring to multiple visual symbols. | The Olympic emblems represent the spirit of the games. 奥运会的会徽代表着奥运精神。 | symbols, logos, badges, insignia, crests | antonym: Not applicable | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *emblome*, from Late Latin *emblema* “inlaid work, mosaic,” from Greek *émblēma* "something inserted, inlaid ornament," from *en-*, "in" + *ballein*, "to throw."
