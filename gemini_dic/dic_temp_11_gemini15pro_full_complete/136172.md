nonreimbursed:/ˌnɒnriːɪmˈbɜːst/ /ˌnɑːnriːɪmˈbɜːrst/ | adj. | 未报销的，未补偿的 | Not reimbursed; not compensated for expenses. |  Example: Nonreimbursed business expenses can be deducted from your taxes.  未报销的商务费用可以从你的税款中扣除。

Example: She kept track of all her nonreimbursed travel costs. 她记录了所有未报销的旅行费用。 | Synonyms: unreimbursed, uncompensated, out-of-pocket | Antonyms: reimbursed, compensated, paid | adjective
