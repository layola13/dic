halfmeant:/ˌhæfˈment/| adj.|半心半意的，不完全认真的|Meaning something only partially or not fully intending it. It describes an action or statement that is not entirely sincere or wholehearted.|She gave a halfmeant apology, not truly sounding remorseful. 她做了一个半心半意的道歉，听起来并不真心悔过。|近义词：insincere, lukewarm, perfunctory|反义词：wholehearted, sincere, genuine|词性:形容词
