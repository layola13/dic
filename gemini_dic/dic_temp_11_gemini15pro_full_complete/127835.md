nemertean:/nɪˈmɜːrtiən/ | adj. n. | 纽形动物的；纽形动物 |Relating to or denoting nemerteans, a phylum of invertebrate worms, also called ribbon worms or proboscis worms.  | Nemertean worms are fascinating creatures found in marine environments. 纽形蠕虫是在海洋环境中发现的迷人生物。 |  Nemertean worms are known for their ability to stretch their bodies to incredible lengths. 纽形蠕虫以能够将身体伸展到令人难以置信的长度而闻名。 | synonyms: ribbon worm, proboscis worm | antonyms: N/A | noun, adjective

Etymology: From Modern Latin *Nemert(e)a*, named after the Greek sea-nymph Nemertes.
