argumental:/ˌɑːrɡjəˈmɛntəl/ | adj. | 好争辩的，爱争论的 | Given to argument; tending to argue or dispute. |  He's very argumental and always ready for a debate. 他很好争辩，随时准备辩论。

She became quite argumental when I challenged her opinion. 当我质疑她的观点时，她变得非常爱争论。

| 近义词: quarrelsome, contentious, disputatious | 反义词: agreeable, amiable, conciliatory | 词性: 形容词
