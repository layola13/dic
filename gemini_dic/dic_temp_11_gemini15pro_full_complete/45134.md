cuddling:/ˈkʌdlɪŋ/| n. |拥抱，依偎|The act of holding someone close in a loving or affectionate way.|例句：They spent the evening cuddling by the fire. 他们依偎在火炉旁度过了一个晚上。|近义词：hugging, snuggling, embracing|反义词:separating, distancing|noun
