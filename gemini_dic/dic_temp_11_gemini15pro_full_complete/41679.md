correspondent:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɒndənt/ (UK) /ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndənt/ (US)| n. |通讯员，记者；通信者|A person employed by a news organization to report on a particular subject or from a particular place; a person who writes letters to another person.|The BBC's foreign correspondent filed a report from the war zone.  英国广播公司的驻外记者从战区发回报道。|Synonyms: reporter, journalist, writer, contributor, communicator|Antonyms: recipient, receiver|noun

correspondent:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɒndənt/ (UK) /ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndənt/ (US)| adj. |相应的，符合的；一致的|Similar or analogous; agreeing in character, form, or function.|The punishment should be correspondent to the crime.  惩罚应该与罪行相符。|Synonyms: corresponding, equivalent, similar, analogous, matching|Antonyms: dissimilar, different, contrasting|adjective
