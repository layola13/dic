diversiloquent:/daɪˌvɜːrsɪˈloʊkwənt/
adjective
说话多样的；谈论各种话题的；多言善辩的
Speaking in different ways or on various subjects; versatile in speech.
The diversiloquent professor kept the students engaged with his wide range of topics. 这位博学多才的教授用他广泛的话题吸引了学生的注意力。
近义词:  versatile, eloquent, voluble, loquacious
反义词:  taciturn, reticent, laconic
词性:  adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *diversiloquus, from diversus (“different”) + loquor (“to speak”).
