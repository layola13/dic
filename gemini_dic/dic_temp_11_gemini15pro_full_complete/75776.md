fulvia:/ˈfʊlviə/| n.|芙尔维亚 (女子名)|A Roman feminine personal name, notably borne by Fulvia Flacca Bambula, the wife of Mark Antony.  It is ultimately derived from the Roman family name Fulvius, meaning "tawny" or "reddish-yellow".|例句：Fulvia is a beautiful and classic name. 芙尔维亚是一个美丽而经典的名字。
