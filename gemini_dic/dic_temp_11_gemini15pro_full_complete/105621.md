kinesthetic:/ˌkɪnɪsˈθɛtɪk/ /ˌkɪnəsˈθɛtɪk/
adjective
运动觉的；动觉的
Relating to a person's awareness of the position and movement of the parts of the body by means of sensory organs within the muscles and joints.
Dancers have a highly developed kinesthetic sense.
舞者有高度发达的动觉。

Kinesthetic learners learn best by doing.
动觉型学习者通过动手操作学习效果最好。

近义词:motor, tactile, haptic
反义词:cognitive, intellectual
词性:adjective
