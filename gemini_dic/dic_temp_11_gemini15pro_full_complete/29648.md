caterwaulings:/ˌkætərˈwɔːlɪŋz/
| n. |猫叫声，刺耳的叫声 |A loud, harsh cry, like that of a cat in heat.  It can also refer to any similar, unpleasant noise. |The caterwaulings of the alley cats kept me awake all night.  巷子里野猫的叫声让我整夜没睡。|近义词：howls, shrieks, wails, screams |反义词: whispers, murmurs |词性:名词

caterwauling:/ˌkætərˈwɔːlɪŋ/
| n. |猫叫春声，刺耳的叫声 |A loud, harsh cry, like that of a cat in heat.  It can also refer to any similar, unpleasant noise. |The caterwauling from next door sounded like a tortured animal. 隔壁传来的刺耳叫声听起来像一只受折磨的动物。|近义词：howling, shrieking, wailing, screaming |反义词: whispering, murmuring |词性:名词

caterwaul:/ˌkætərˈwɔːl/
| v. |发出猫叫春般的声音，发出刺耳的叫声| To make a loud, harsh cry, like that of a cat in heat. |The band caterwauled their way through a set of poorly-written songs.  乐队演奏了一组写得很差的歌曲，声音刺耳难听。|近义词：howl, shriek, wail, scream |反义词: whisper, murmur |词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "cater" (Middle English, probably from Old North French *cater, cat) + "waul" (Middle English waulen, probably of imitative origin).
