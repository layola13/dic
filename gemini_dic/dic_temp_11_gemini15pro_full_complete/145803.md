overdecorative: /ˌoʊvərˈdɛkərətɪv/ | adj. | 过于装饰的，装饰过度的 | Excessively or excessively elaborately decorated; having too much decoration. | The Christmas tree was overdecorative, with tinsel draped over every branch.  圣诞树装饰过度，每个树枝上都挂满了金丝。 |  The wedding cake was a bit overdecorative for my taste. 婚礼蛋糕对我来说有点装饰过度。 | 近义词: ornate, fussy, flamboyant, ostentatious | 反义词: simple, plain, understated, minimalist | 词性: 形容词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + decorative (adjective meaning "serving to decorate")
