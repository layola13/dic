esloin:/ɪˈslɔɪn/ |v.| 疏远；使远离；移居 |To remove or withdraw to a distance; to retire or depart.|He esloined himself from the bustling city and sought refuge in the countryside. 他远离了喧嚣的城市，到乡下寻求宁静。|近义词: withdraw, remove, depart, retreat, seclude |反义词: approach, join, engage|词性: 动词

Etymology: From Old French *esloignier, from loing "far," from Latin longe.
