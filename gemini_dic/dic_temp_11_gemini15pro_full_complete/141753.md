orange-tawny:/ˈɔːrɪndʒ ˈtɔːni/| adj.|橙黄色|A color between orange and light brown, resembling the coat of a tawny owl.|The leaves turned a beautiful orange-tawny in the autumn. 秋天，树叶变成了美丽的橙黄色。|近义词：fulvous, reddish-brown,  russet|反义词:blue, green|词性:形容词

词源:
"orange" comes from Old French "orenge," ultimately from Arabic "nāranj," from Persian "nārang," from Sanskrit "nāraṅga" (orange tree). "tawny" comes from Middle English "taune," from Anglo-French "tauné," past participle of "taner" (to tan), from Medieval Latin "tannare," of uncertain origin.
