overexpects:/ˌōvərɪkˈspɛkts/
verb
过度期望，期望过高
to expect too much from someone or something
例句：He overexpects from his employees and is often disappointed. 他对员工期望过高，经常感到失望。

例句：She overexpects her children to excel in everything they do. 她过度期望她的孩子们在所有事情上都表现出色。

近义词：expects too much, has unrealistic expectations
反义词：underexpects, has low expectations
词性：v.
