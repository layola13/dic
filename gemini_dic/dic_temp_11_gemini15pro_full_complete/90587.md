hullabaloo:/ˈhʌləbəluː/| n.|喧闹，骚动，吵闹; 混乱|a loud confused noise, especially one caused by a large mass of people.|The hullabaloo from the street kept me awake all night. 街上的喧闹声让我整夜没睡。|近义词：commotion, uproar, racket, din, clamor|反义词：silence, quiet, peace, tranquility|noun
