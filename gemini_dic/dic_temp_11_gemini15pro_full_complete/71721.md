flaw:/flɔː/| n.|缺点，瑕疵，缺陷|a mark, fault, or other imperfection that mars a substance or object.|The vase had a flaw in it.  这个花瓶有个瑕疵。|Synonyms: defect, imperfection, blemish, fault, error, |Antonyms: perfection, strength, virtue|noun
flaw:/flɔː/| v.|使有缺陷，使无效，(使)破裂|to cause a fault or weakness in; make imperfect.|The witness's inconsistent testimony flawed the case. 目击者前后矛盾的证词使案件出现了缺陷。|Synonyms: impair, mar, damage, spoil, ruin|Antonyms: perfect, enhance, improve|verb
