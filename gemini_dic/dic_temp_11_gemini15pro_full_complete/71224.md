fitment:/ˈfɪtmənt/| n.|安装; 装配; 配件; (尤指家具的)固定装置|the act of fitting something or the way in which it fits|The fitment of the new kitchen cabinets took longer than expected. 新厨柜的安装比预期的要长.|近义词：installation, fitting, fixture, equipment|反义词:removal, detachment|词性:名词

Etymology: From the verb "fit" + the suffix "-ment," which forms nouns indicating the result or product of an action. "Fit" comes from Old French "fiter," ultimately from Vulgar Latin *fīctāre, frequentative of Latin fīgere "to fix, fasten."
