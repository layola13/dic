mechanographist:/məˈkænəˌɡræfɪst/
n.
机械记录员；打字员
A person who operates a keyboard machine, especially a typewriter or a data entry device.
The mechanographist typed up the report quickly and efficiently.  机械记录员快速高效地打好了报告。

The mechanographist entered the data into the computer. 机械记录员将数据输入计算机。
近义词:typist, keyboard operator, data entry clerk
反义词:None (This occupation is largely obsolete due to technological advancements, making a direct antonym less relevant.)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *mechano-* (relating to machines) + *-graph-* (writing) + *-ist* (a person who does something)
