bristle:/ˈbrɪsl/ (US) /ˈbrɪs(ə)l/ (UK)| n. v.|刚毛，鬃毛；(毛发等)竖起；发怒|A short, stiff hair, typically one of those on an animal's skin or a man's face.  To (cause to) stand up stiffly, like bristles.|The cat's fur bristled when it saw the dog. 猫看到狗时毛都竖起来了。|近义词：hair, quill, spine|反义词:smoothness|词性:名词, 动词
