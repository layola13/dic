gunocracy: /ɡʌˈnɒkrəsi/
n.
gunocracy (rule by force of arms) 枪杆子统治，武力统治
A gunocracy is a government ruled by force of arms.
例句：The small island nation became a gunocracy after the military coup.  这个小岛国在军事政变后变成了一个枪杆子统治的国家。

例句：The gunocracy maintained its power through fear and intimidation. 这个枪杆子政权通过恐惧和恐吓来维持其权力。
近义词：dictatorship, tyranny, despotism
反义词：democracy, republic
词性：名词
