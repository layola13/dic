outsmiling:/ˌaʊtˈsmaɪlɪŋ/
|v. | 以微笑胜过; 用微笑表示友好|  outsmiling someone means smiling more than them, especially in order to show that you are more friendly or confident. | She outsmiled him, radiating warmth and confidence. 她对他报以更灿烂的微笑，展现出热情和自信。 |  outcharm, outshine | frown, grimace | verb
