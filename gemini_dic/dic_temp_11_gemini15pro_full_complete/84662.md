handbooks:/ˈhændbʊks/ | n. | 手册，指南 | A concise reference book providing specific information or instructions on a subject. |  Example: The student consulted the physics handbook for the formula. 学生查阅了物理手册上的公式。

Example:  She keeps a handbook of grammar rules on her desk. 她桌子上放着一本语法规则手册。| 近义词:manuals, guides, handbooks | 反义词:  None (no direct opposite for a physical object) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "hand" + "book".  "Hand" in this context refers to something small and easily held.
