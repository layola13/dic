officialism: /əˈfɪʃəlɪzəm/
n.
官僚主义；官僚作风；形式主义
Excessive reliance on or reverence for official rules and regulations; the rigid adherence to official formalities.
The project was bogged down by officialism and red tape.  这个项目因官僚主义和繁文缛节而停滞不前。
Bureaucracy in government is often associated with officialism. 政府的官僚机构常常与官僚作风联系在一起。
近义词: bureaucracy, red tape, formalism
反义词:  flexibility, informality
