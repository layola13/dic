benzaldehyde:/ˈbɛnzˈældɪhaɪd/ (US) /ˈbɛnzˈældɪhaɪd/ (UK)| n. |苯甲醛|An aromatic aldehyde formed by the removal of two hydrogen atoms from benzyl alcohol; the simplest aromatic aldehyde. It is a colorless liquid with a characteristic almond-like odor.|Benzaldehyde is used in the manufacture of perfumes and flavorings. 苯甲醛用于制造香水和调味剂。|Synonyms: phenylmethanal|Antonyms: N/A|noun

benzaldehyde:/ˈbɛnzˈældɪhaɪd/ (US) /ˈbɛnzˈældɪhaɪd/ (UK)| n. |苯甲醛|The chemical compound C6H5CHO, the simplest aromatic aldehyde, used in perfumes, dyes, and flavorings.|The characteristic almond odor of benzaldehyde can be detected even in small concentrations.  即使是小浓度的苯甲醛也能闻到其特有的杏仁气味。|Synonyms:  benzoic aldehyde|Antonyms: N/A|noun
