latinize:/ˈlætɪnaɪz/| v. |拉丁化；使拉丁化|To convert (a word or text) into Latin form or to make something conform to Latin models.|The company decided to latinize its name for a more international feel.  该公司决定将其名称拉丁化，以使其更具国际化。|The spelling of many place names was latinized during the Middle Ages.  许多地名的拼写在中世纪被拉丁化了。|近义词：romanize|反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially "vernacularize")|词性:动词
