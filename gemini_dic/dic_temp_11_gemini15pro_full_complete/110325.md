lighter:/ˈlaɪtər/| n. |打火机；点火器；驳船|A portable device used to create a flame, typically fueled by butane or other flammable liquid.  A barge or other vessel used to transfer cargo to or from a larger ship.|例句：He used a lighter to light his cigarette. 他用打火机点燃了香烟。

例句：The cargo was transferred to the ship via a lighter. 货物通过驳船转移到船上。|近义词：igniter, kindler, barge (for cargo transfer)|反义词:extinguisher|词性:noun

lighter:/ˈlaɪtər/| adj. (比较级 lighter, 最高级 lightest)| 更轻的；更明亮的；更淡的|Less heavy; having a brighter or paler color.|例句：The feather is lighter than the rock. 羽毛比石头轻。

例句：The walls are painted a lighter shade of blue. 墙壁被漆成了更浅的蓝色。|近义词：paler, brighter, less heavy|反义词:darker, heavier|词性:adjective
