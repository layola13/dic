conspicuous-consumption:/kənˈspɪkjuəs kənˈsʌmpʃən/ | n. | 炫耀性消费 |  Expenditure on or acquisition of goods or services primarily for the purpose of displaying one's wealth or social status.  It is a way to show off one's purchasing power and impress others, often involving luxury goods or experiences that are not necessary for basic survival. |  He engaged in conspicuous consumption, buying a sports car he couldn't afford just to impress his neighbors. 他进行了炫耀性消费，买了一辆他买不起的跑车只是为了给邻居留下深刻印象。

The lavish parties were a clear example of conspicuous consumption. 这些奢华的派对是炫耀性消费的明显例子。 | 近义词：ostentatious consumption, extravagant spending | 反义词：frugal living, thrift | 词性: noun
