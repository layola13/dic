melicerous:/mɪˈlɪsərəs/
adjective
似蜜的；产蜜的；甜蜜的
Containing or producing honey; sweet like honey.
The bees produced a melicerous substance. 蜜蜂产生了一种像蜜一样的物质。
The melicerous scent of the flowers filled the air. 花的蜜香弥漫在空气中。
近义词: honeyed, sweet, nectarous
反义词: bitter, sour
词性: adjective
