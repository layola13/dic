overexpends:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspɛndz/
verb
过度支出，超支
to spend more than one can afford or more than is necessary
He overexpends every month and then has to borrow money. 他每个月都过度支出，然后不得不借钱。
She overexpends on clothes and shoes. 她过度花费在衣服和鞋子上。
overspend, squander, lavish
underspend, save, economize
v.
