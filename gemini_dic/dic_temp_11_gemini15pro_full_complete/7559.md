annihilatory:/əˈnaɪəˌleɪtəri/
adjective
毁灭性的，歼灭性的
Causing destruction or complete ruin; tending to annihilate.
The annihilatory power of nuclear weapons is a constant threat to world peace. 核武器的毁灭性力量是对世界和平的持续威胁。

The asteroid impact had an annihilatory effect on the dinosaurs.  小行星撞击对恐龙产生了毁灭性的影响。


近义词:destructive, devastating, ruinous, exterminating
反义词:creative, constructive, beneficial, preservative
