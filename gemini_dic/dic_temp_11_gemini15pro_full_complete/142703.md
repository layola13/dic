otherways:/ˈʌðərˌweɪz/| adv.|以其他方式；在其他方面|Otherwise means in a different way from the one mentioned or implied. It can also mean in other respects or aspects.|She didn't study, otherwise she would have passed the exam. 她没有学习，否则她会通过考试。|Near synonyms: differently, alternatively, or else|Opposite: similarly, likewise|adverb
