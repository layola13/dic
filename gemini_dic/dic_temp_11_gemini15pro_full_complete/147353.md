overname:/ˌoʊvərˈneɪm/ | n. | 覆盖名;别名;假名 | An overname is a name used in addition to or instead of a person's real name, often for privacy or security reasons.  | Example:  He used the overname "John Smith" to protect his identity. 他用“约翰·史密斯”这个假名来保护自己的身份。

Example:  She registered for the conference under an overname. 她用化名注册参加了会议。

| 近义词: alias, pseudonym, assumed name, false name | 反义词: real name, birth name, legal name | 词性: noun
