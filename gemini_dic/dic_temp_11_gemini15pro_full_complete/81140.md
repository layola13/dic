granada:/ɡrəˈnɑːdə/ /ɡrəˈneɪdə/| n.|石榴；手榴弹|A round fruit with a tough reddish outer skin and many small seeds with juicy red pulp.  A small bomb thrown by hand or launched mechanically.|
The market was full of fresh pomegranates. 市场里到处都是新鲜的石榴。

The soldier pulled the pin on the grenade. 士兵拔掉了手榴弹的保险销。


近义词：(fruit) pomegranate; (weapon) bomb, explosive|
反义词: N/A|
词性:noun|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *granate* (modern French *grenade*), from Medieval Latin *grānāta*, feminine of *grānātus* (“having many seeds”), from Latin *grānum* (“seed, grain”).  The sense of "bomb" developed from the resemblance of the exploding bomb scattering fragments to a pomegranate scattering seeds.
