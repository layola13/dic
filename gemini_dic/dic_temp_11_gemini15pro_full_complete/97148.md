infinitesimality:/ɪnˌfɪnɪtəˈsɪmələti/ | n. |无限小；无穷小 |The concept of infinitesimality is crucial in calculus. |
The infinitesimality of the change made it practically unnoticeable.  这微乎其微的变化使得它几乎难以察觉。|
Synonyms: minuteness, tininess, insignificance |
Antonyms: immensity, vastness, enormity |
noun


infinitesimal:/ˌɪnˌfɪnɪˈtɛsɪməl/ | adj. | 极小的；无限小的；无穷小的 | The difference between the two values is infinitesimal. |
The chances of winning the lottery are infinitesimal.  赢得彩票的几率微乎其微。|
Synonyms: minute, tiny, negligible |
Antonyms: huge, immense, enormous |
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *infinitesimalis, from infinit(us) (infinite) + -esimus (suffix forming ordinal numbers) + -alis (adjectival suffix).
