guiltier:/ˈɡɪltiər/ (US), /ˈɡɪltiə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更内疚的，更有罪的|Comparative form of "guilty," meaning feeling or showing a sense of guilt to a greater degree.|例句：He felt guiltier than he had ever felt before. 他感到比以往任何时候都更加内疚。

例句：She looked guiltier after being confronted with the evidence.  在她面对证据后，她看起来更内疚了。


近义词：more culpable, more responsible|反义词:less guilty, more innocent|词性:adjective

guilt:/ɡɪlt/ (US), /ɡɪlt/ (UK)| n.|内疚，罪行|The fact of having committed a specified or implied offense or crime.|例句：He admitted his guilt. 他承认了自己的罪行。

例句：She felt a pang of guilt. 她感到一阵内疚。


近义词：remorse, shame|反义词:innocence|词性:noun
