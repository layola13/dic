mouthguard:/ˈmaʊθɡɑːrd/| n.|护齿套，牙套|A protective device worn over the teeth, typically by athletes, to prevent injury to the mouth.|Boxers wear mouthguards to protect their teeth. 拳击运动员佩戴护齿套来保护他们的牙齿。|He forgot his mouthguard at home. 他把护齿套忘在家里了。|Synonyms: gum shield, mouth protector|Antonyms: N/A|noun
