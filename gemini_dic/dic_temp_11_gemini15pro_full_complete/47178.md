daydream: /ˈdeɪdriːm/ (US) /ˈdeɪdriːm/ (UK)| vi.|做白日梦；幻想|To have pleasant thoughts that distract one's attention from the present.|例句：He spent the afternoon daydreaming about his future. 他一下午都在幻想他的未来。|例句：She daydreamed of a life of luxury and ease. 她幻想着过着奢华安逸的生活。|近义词：fantasize, muse|反义词：focus, concentrate|词性:动词


词源：From "day" + "dream".  "Day" referring to being awake during the daytime, and "dream" referring to the fanciful thoughts.
