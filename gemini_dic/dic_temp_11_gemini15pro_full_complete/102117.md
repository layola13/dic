isotonicity:/ˌaɪsɒtəˈnɪsɪti/ | n. | 等渗性；等张性 | The state of having the same osmotic pressure as another solution, especially one in a cell or a body fluid.  |  The isotonicity of the saline solution is crucial for intravenous drips. 静脉输液中生理盐水的等渗性至关重要。 |  isomotic, isosmoticity | hypertonicity, hypotonicity | noun
