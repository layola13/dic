outstations:/ˌaʊtˈsteɪʃənz/
n.| 分站；派出机构；外围站点|
A secondary station; a station in the outlying districts, away from a central point or main station.
|The company has several outstations in rural areas.  这家公司在农村地区有几个派出机构。
|The mining company operates several outstations throughout the region.  这家矿业公司在整个地区经营着几个分站。
|近义词: branch offices, field offices, satellite offices, remote locations, substations
|反义词: headquarters, main office, central office
|词性: 名词
