inescapableness:/ˌɪnɪˈskeɪpəbəlnəs/
|n.|不可避免性，无法逃避|The inescapableness of death is a difficult concept for many to grasp.  死亡的不可避免性对许多人来说是一个难以理解的概念。|The inescapableness of his fate weighed heavily upon him. 他命运的不可避免性沉重地压在他身上。|
|近义词: inevitability, unavoidability|
|反义词: escapability, avoidability|
|词性: 名词|


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + escapable + -ness.  Escapable from escape (v.) from Old French escaper, from Late Latin excappare "to escape," literally "to get out of one's cape," from ex- "out of" + cappa "cape."
