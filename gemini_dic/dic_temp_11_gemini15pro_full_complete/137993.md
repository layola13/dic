noodleism:/ˈnuːdɪlɪzəm/
n.
面条主义; (尤指对复杂问题的) 过度简化
An instance of oversimplifying a complex issue, especially by drawing a superficial analogy to something else.
The politician's noodleism compared the national debt to a household budget, failing to address the complexities of government finance.  这位政客用面条主义将国家债务比作家庭预算，未能解决政府财政的复杂性。

Noodleism is a relatively recent term, gaining traction in online discussions and informal settings. Its exact etymology is unclear, but it likely derives from the image of noodles, long and tangled, representing the convoluted and often superficial explanations offered for complex issues.  面条主义是一个相对较新的术语，在在线讨论和非正式场合中得到广泛应用。它的确切词源尚不清楚，但它很可能源于面条的形象，长而缠结，代表了对复杂问题提出的复杂且往往肤浅的解释。

EXAMPLE->
His explanation of the economic crisis was pure noodleism. 他对经济危机的解释纯粹是面条主义。

EXAMPLE->
The commentator's noodleism about the conflict oversimplified a complex geopolitical situation.  评论员对面条主义冲突的解释过于简化了复杂的地缘政治局势。

SYNONYM->
oversimplification, superficiality, reductionism, simplistic explanation
ANTONYM->
nuance, complexity, in-depth analysis, thorough explanation
