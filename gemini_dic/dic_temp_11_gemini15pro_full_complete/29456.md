catalysing:/ˈkætəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
催化；促进
acting as a catalyst; accelerating a process.
The enzyme catalyses the breakdown of fats in the body.  这种酶催化体内脂肪的分解。
catalyzing:/ˈkætəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
催化；促进
acting as a catalyst; accelerating a process.
The presence of a foreign power can catalyze political change. 外来势力的存在可以催化政治变革。
synonyms: accelerating, expediting, furthering, precipitating, promoting, stimulating
antonyms: hindering, inhibiting, obstructing, retarding
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From catalyst + -ing.  Catalyst from Greek katalusis "dissolution," from kataluein "to dissolve," from kata- "down" + luein "to loosen."
