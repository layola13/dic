jorum:/ˈdʒɔːrəm/| n.|大酒杯；一大杯（尤指啤酒或其他酒精饮料）|A large drinking bowl or vessel, especially for ale or other liquor.|He raised his jorum and toasted the king. 他举起大酒杯向国王敬酒。|Synonyms: tankard, flagon, pitcher, jug|Antonyms: thimble, shot glass|noun

jorum:/ˈdʒɔːrəm/| n.|（非正式）大量；很多（尤指酒）|A large quantity of something, especially liquor (informal).|He consumed a jorum of ale before dinner. 晚饭前，他喝了大量的麦酒。|Synonyms: lot, abundance, plenty|Antonyms: little, bit, drop|noun
