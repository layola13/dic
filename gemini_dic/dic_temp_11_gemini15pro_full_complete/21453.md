blowpipe:/ˈbloʊpaɪp/ (US) /ˈbləʊpaɪp/ (UK)| n.|吹管；喷焊枪|A tube through which air is blown into a fire to make it burn more strongly, or used by glassblowers to shape molten glass. Also, a tube for shooting darts or pellets by blowing.|The glassblower used a blowpipe to create a delicate vase.| 近义词: blowgun, blow tube| 反义词: N/A| noun
