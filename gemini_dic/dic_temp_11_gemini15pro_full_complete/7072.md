anencephalic:/ˌænənˌsɛˈfælɪk/| adj.|无脑的|Born without a major portion of the brain, skull, and scalp. |The anencephalic baby tragically died shortly after birth.  这个无脑婴儿出生后不久就悲惨地夭折了。|Synonyms: brainless, acephalic|Antonyms: normal, healthy|adjective

anencephaly:/ˌænənˈsɛfəli/| n.|无脑畸形|A serious birth defect in which a baby is born without parts of the brain and skull.|Anencephaly is a devastating condition that is incompatible with life. 无脑畸形是一种毁灭性的疾病，与生命不相容。|Synonyms: anencephalia|Antonyms: N/A|noun
