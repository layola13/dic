bunge:/ˈbʌndʒi/| n. |蹦极，橡皮筋|A type of strong, elastic cord.  It also refers to the activity of jumping from a high place while attached to such a cord.|He went bungee jumping off a bridge. 他从桥上蹦极跳了下去。|近义词：elastic cord, shock cord|反义词:|noun

bungee jump:/ˈbʌndʒi dʒʌmp/| v. |进行蹦极跳|To jump from a high place while attached to a bungee cord.|He bungee jumped off a cliff in New Zealand. 他在新西兰的一个悬崖上蹦极跳。|近义词:|反义词:|verb
