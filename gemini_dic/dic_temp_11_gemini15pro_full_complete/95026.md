inaccessibly:/ˌɪnækˈsɛsəbli/
adjective
难以接近地，难以理解地
not easily reached or understood; remotely
The summit of the mountain was inaccessibly high. 山顶高不可攀。
The author's writing style was dense and inaccessibly complex. 作者的写作风格浓厚而难以理解。
近义词:unreachably, impenetrably, untouchably
反义词:accessibly, easily, readily
词性:adverb

inaccessible:/ˌɪnækˈsɛsəbəl/
adjective
难以接近的，难以理解的
not easily reached or understood; remote
The remote village was inaccessible during the winter.  这个偏远的村庄在冬天难以到达。
His philosophical ideas are inaccessible to most people. 他的哲学思想对大多数人来说难以理解。
近义词:unreachable, impenetrable, untouchable
反义词:accessible, reachable, approachable
词性:adjective

access:/ˈækses/
noun
通道，入口；使用权；机会
a means of approaching or entering a place; the right or opportunity to use or see something
The only access to the island is by boat. 到达该岛的唯一途径是乘船。
Students have access to the library's online resources. 学生可以使用图书馆的在线资源。
近义词:entry, admittance, approach
反义词:exclusion, restriction, inaccessibility
词性:noun

access:/ˈækses/
verb
访问，进入；使用
approach or enter (a place); obtain, examine, or retrieve (data or a file)
He accessed the confidential files without permission. 他未经许可访问了机密文件。
I can access the internet from my phone. 我可以用手机上网。
近义词:obtain, retrieve, reach
反义词:block, restrict, prevent
词性:verb


词源:
From in- (not) + accessible.  Accessible comes from Late Middle English, from late Latin accessibilis, from accedere ‘to approach’ (see accede).
