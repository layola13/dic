limpid:/ˈlɪmpɪd/| adj.|清澈的，透明的；清晰的，易懂的|Characterized by transparency or clarity; easily understood.|The limpid waters of the Caribbean are breathtaking. 加勒比海清澈的海水令人叹为观止。|Synonyms: clear, transparent, pellucid, crystalline|Antonyms: murky, cloudy, opaque, obscure|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French limpide, from Latin limpidus ("clear, transparent, liquid"), from lympha ("water").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *lendʰ- (“liquid”).
