architecture's: /ˈɑːrkɪtɛktʃərz/ | n. | 建筑风格；建筑学；体系结构 |  The possessive form of "architecture," referring to something belonging to or associated with architecture.  | The architecture's influence on the city is undeniable.  这座城市的建筑风格对其影响不可否认。 | Near synonyms: design, structure, framework | Antonym: formlessness |  noun (possessive)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *architectura, from Latin architectura (“architecture”), from architectus (“architect”), from Ancient Greek ἀρχιτέκτων (arkhitéktōn, “master builder, director of works”), from ἀρχι- (arkhi-, “chief”) + τέκτων (téktōn, “builder, carpenter”).
