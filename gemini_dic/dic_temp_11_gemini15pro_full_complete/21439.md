blowhole:/ˈbloʊhoʊl/ (US) /ˈbləʊhəʊl/ (UK)| n.|呼吸孔；喷水孔；通风孔|A hole in the top of the head of a whale or dolphin, through which it breathes.  Also, a hole in the ice or ground that allows air or water to escape from a cavity below.|The whale surfaced and spouted water from its blowhole.  鲸鱼浮出水面，从它的呼吸孔喷出水柱。

The hikers discovered a blowhole emitting steam from a geothermal vent.  徒步旅行者发现了一个从地热喷口喷出蒸汽的通风孔。|近义词：vent, nostril (for whales/dolphins), opening, aperture|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps blockage, obstruction)|noun
