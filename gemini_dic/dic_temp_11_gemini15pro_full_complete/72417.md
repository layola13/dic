fluffiest:/ˈflʌfiɪst/| adj.|最毛茸茸的，最蓬松的|Superlative form of fluffy: covered with soft, light hair or fibers, giving a light and airy appearance.  It implies a greater degree of fluffiness than anything else being compared.|例句：This is the fluffiest kitten I've ever seen. 这是我见过最毛茸茸的小猫。|近义词：softest, downiest, most feathery|反义词:smoothest, flattest, hardest|词性:形容词

词源:
From fluff + -y + -est.  Fluff comes from Middle English *fluffen, of imitative origin.
