objectionably: /əbˈdʒɛkʃənəbli/
adverb
令人反感地，讨厌地
in a way that is unpleasant and likely to cause criticism or protest.
His behavior was objectionably loud and disruptive. 他的行为吵闹且具有破坏性，令人反感。
The film was objectionably violent. 这部电影暴力程度令人难以接受。
近义词: offensively, repulsively, disgustingly, unpleasantly
反义词: pleasantly, acceptably, agreeably
词性: adverb

objectionable: /əbˈdʒɛkʃənəbəl/
adjective
令人反感的，讨厌的
unpleasant and likely to cause criticism or protest.
The comedian made several objectionable jokes.  这位喜剧演员讲了几个令人反感的玩笑。
Some people found the content of the book objectionable.  有些人觉得这本书的内容令人反感。
近义词: offensive, repugnant, disgusting, unpleasant
反义词: pleasant, acceptable, agreeable
词性: adjective


词源: From "objection" + "-able". "Objection" comes from the Latin "obiectio," meaning "a throwing against," from "ob-" (against) + "iacere" (to throw).
