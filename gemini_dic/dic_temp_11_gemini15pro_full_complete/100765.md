adjective

Situated within a heart valve.

adjective
瓣膜内的

Intravalvular stenosis can impede blood flow. 瓣膜内狭窄会阻碍血液流动。


近义词:  endocardial, valvular

词源: intra- (within) + valvular (relating to a valve)
