corollaries:/ˈkɔːrəˌlɛriz/| n.|推论，必然结果|A proposition that follows from one already proved.  A natural consequence or result.|例句：One corollary of the rise in inflation was an increase in prices. 通货膨胀上升的一个必然结果是物价上涨。

例句：Increased taxes are a corollary of higher government spending. 税收增加是政府支出增加的必然结果。|近义词：consequences, results, deductions, inferences|反义词:causes, premises|词性:noun
