notional:/ˈnəʊʃənəl/ | adj. | 概念上的；名义上的；想象的 | Existing only in theory or as a suggestion; not real or actual. | The figures are notional and do not represent actual expenditure. 这些数字是名义上的，并不代表实际支出。 |  The threat was notional rather than real.  这种威胁是想象的，而不是真实的。 |近义词：theoretical, hypothetical, conceptual, abstract, imaginary | 反义词：actual, real, concrete, tangible | 词性: adjective

notion:/ˈnəʊʃən/ | n. | 概念；想法；见解 | A belief or idea. | I have a notion that he's not telling us the whole truth. 我感觉他没有告诉我们全部真相。 | She had a vague notion about what happened. 她对发生的事情有一个模糊的概念。 |近义词：idea, belief, concept, thought, perception | 反义词：fact, reality | 词性: noun
