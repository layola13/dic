interproportional:/ˌɪntərprəˈpɔːʃənl/ | adj. |相互成比例的，互相成比例的 |  Relating to two or more quantities that vary proportionally to each other.  If one quantity doubles, the other doubles as well. | If A and B are interproportional, then when A increases, B increases at the same rate. 如果A和B相互成比例，那么当A增加时，B也以相同的速率增加。 |The growth of the economy and the increase in employment are often interproportional. 经济增长和就业增加通常是成比例的。|  proportional, directly proportional | inversely proportional | adjective
