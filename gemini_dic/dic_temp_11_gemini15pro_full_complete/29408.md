cataclysmist:/ˈkætəkˌlɪzmɪst/| n.|灾变论者，大灾难预言者|A person who believes in or predicts cataclysms.|The cataclysmist predicted the end of the world was near.  这位灾变论者预言世界末日即将来临。|Synonyms: doomsayer, pessimist, alarmist|Antonyms: optimist|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cataclysmiste*, from *cataclysme* ("cataclysm") + *-iste* ("-ist").  *Cataclysme* from Ancient Greek *κατακλυσμός* (kataklusmós, “flood, deluge”).
