mixogamous:/mɪkˈsɒɡəməs/| adj.|异配生殖的，混合配合的|Relating to or exhibiting mixogamy; characterized by the fusion of gametes differing in size and/or form.  Specifically referring to oogamy, where a large non-motile female gamete (egg) is fertilized by a small, motile male gamete (sperm).|Example 1:  The evolution of mixogamous reproduction was a key step in the development of complex multicellular organisms. 进化到异配生殖是复杂多细胞生物发展的关键一步。

Example 2: Many algae exhibit a mixogamous life cycle, alternating between sexual and asexual reproduction. 许多藻类表现出异配生殖的生命周期，在有性和无性生殖之间交替。|Synonym: oogamous|Antonym: isogamous|Word type: adjective
