overrefinement:/ˌoʊvər rɪˈfaɪnmənt/ | n. | 过度精炼；过分细化；矫揉造作 |  The act of refining something excessively, often to the point of losing its essential qualities or becoming unnecessarily complicated or subtle. |  The overrefinement of his prose made it difficult to understand. 他的散文过于精雕细琢，反而难以理解。 |  sophistication, elaboration, overelaboration | simplicity, naturalness | noun
