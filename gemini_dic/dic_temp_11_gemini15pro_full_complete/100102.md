词源:
From inter- + pour.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between," "among," "mutually," or "reciprocally." "Pour" comes from the Old French word "purer," which means "to cleanse, purify, refine."  Thus, "interpoured" literally suggests a mutual or reciprocal pouring or flowing together.
