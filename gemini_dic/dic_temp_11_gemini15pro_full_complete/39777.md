consonance:/ˈkɑːnsənəns/ /ˈkɒnsənəns/| n.|一致，和谐，调和，（音韵）和音|Agreement or compatibility between opinions or actions; the recurrence of similar sounds, especially consonants, in close proximity.|The consonance between the two melodies created a beautiful harmony. 这两段旋律之间的和声创造了一种美丽的和谐。|近义词：harmony, agreement, accord, concord, unity|反义词：dissonance, discord, conflict, disagreement|词性:名词

consonances:/ˈkɑːnsənənsɪz/ /ˈkɒnsənənsɪz/| n.|一致，和谐，调和，（音韵）和音（复数）|Agreement or compatibility between opinions or actions; the recurrence of similar sounds, especially consonants, in close proximity (plural).|The consonances between their political views were surprising. 他们的政治观点之间的一致性令人惊讶。|近义词：harmonies, agreements, accords, concords|反义词：dissonances, discords, conflicts|词性:名词
