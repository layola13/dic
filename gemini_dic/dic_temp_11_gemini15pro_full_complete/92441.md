hypophosphataemia: /ˌhaɪpəʊfɒsfəˈtiːmiə/ | n. | 低磷血症 | An abnormally low level of phosphates in the blood. |  Severe hypophosphataemia can lead to serious complications. 严重的低磷血症会导致严重的并发症。 | hypophosphatemia (US spelling), low phosphate | hyperphosphataemia | noun
