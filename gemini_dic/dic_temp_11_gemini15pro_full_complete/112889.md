ludditism:/ˈlʌd.ɪ.tɪ.zəm/
n.
卢德主义；反对技术进步
Opposition to new technology, especially automation, often expressed through the destruction of machinery.
The resurgence of ludditism in some sectors is a reaction to job losses caused by automation.  一些行业卢德主义的复兴是对自动化造成失业的反应。
Ned Ludd's followers engaged in acts of ludditism, destroying textile machinery they believed threatened their livelihoods. 内德·卢德的追随者从事卢德主义活动，破坏他们认为威胁他们生计的纺织机械。
近义词:  anti-technologism,  technophobia
反义词:  technophilia,  modernism
词性: 名词
