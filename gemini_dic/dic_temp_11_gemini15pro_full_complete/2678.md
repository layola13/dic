adragant:/ˈædrəɡænt/
n.|黄蓍胶, 黄蓍树胶|A gummy exudation obtained from certain Asiatic plants of the genus Astragalus, used as a thickening agent in textile printing and other industrial processes.|The pharmacist used adragant to bind the ingredients of the medicine.|Synonyms: gum tragacanth, tragacanth|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
