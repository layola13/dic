ballasting:/ˈbæləstɪŋ/
n.
压舱物，镇重物；稳定；（铁路）铺道碴
The act or process of furnishing with ballast.
The ship needs ballasting for stability in rough seas.  这艘船需要加压舱物以在波涛汹涌的海面上保持稳定。
近义词：stabilizing, weighting
反义词：unbalancing, destabilizing
词性：名词

ballast:/ˈbæləst/
n.
压舱物，镇重物
Heavy material, such as gravel, sand, or iron, placed low in a vessel to improve its stability.
The ship took on ballast in the harbor. 这艘船在港口装载了压舱物。
近义词：weight, counterweight
反义词：buoyancy
词性：名词

ballast:/ˈbæləst/
vt.
给…加压舱物；使稳定
To furnish with ballast.  
They ballasted the ship with rocks. 他们用石头给船加压舱物。
近义词：stabilize, steady
反义词：destabilize
词性：动词


词源：From Middle Dutch *ballast, from Old Norse *barhlastr (“bare load”), referring to the practice of using stones as cargo when a ship sailed unloaded.
