megapixel:/ˈmɛɡəˌpɪksəl/ |n.| 百万像素 |A unit of graphic resolution equivalent to one million pixels.  A pixel is the smallest unit of a digital image.  The more megapixels, generally the higher the resolution of the image. |This camera has a 24-megapixel sensor. 这款相机有2400万像素的传感器。|Synonyms: resolution, definition |Antonyms: N/A |noun
