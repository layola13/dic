displeasuring:/dɪsˈpleʒərɪŋ/
adjective
令人不快的，使人不高兴的
Causing displeasure; offensive or annoying.
Her displeasuring tone made it clear she wasn't happy. 她不悦的语气表明她不高兴。
The displeasuring news of the budget cuts upset everyone. 预算削减的坏消息让每个人都很沮丧。
近义词:unpleasant, annoying, irritating, offensive
反义词:pleasing, agreeable, delightful, satisfying
词性:adjective

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + pleasure + -ing (forming present participles and adjectives). Ultimately from Latin placēre "to please."
