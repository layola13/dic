nonstudious: /ˌnɒnˈstjuːdiəs/ (British) /ˌnɑːnˈstuːdiəs/ (American)
adjective
不爱学习的，不用功的
Not studious; not given to studying.
He was a nonstudious child, preferring to play outdoors.  他是个不爱学习的孩子，更喜欢在户外玩耍。
She's bright but nonstudious. 她很聪明，但不爱学习。
synonyms:  idle, lazy, indolent, unscholarly
antonyms: studious, diligent, scholarly
词性: adjective
