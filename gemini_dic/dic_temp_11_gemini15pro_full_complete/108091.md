lath:/læθ/ | n. | 板条，狭板 | A thin, narrow strip of wood, especially one of a series forming a foundation for plaster or tiles. | The roof was made of laths covered with thatch. 屋顶是用盖着茅草的板条做成的。

lath:/læθ/ | n. | (集)板条，格栅 | Laths collectively or a framework of laths. | The plaster is applied over laths. 灰泥涂在板条上。


lath:/læθ/ | vt. | 用板条覆盖 | To cover or overlay with laths. | They lathed the ceiling before plastering it. 他们在抹灰前用板条覆盖了天花板。


Etymology: Old English *lætt, from Proto-Germanic *latto (source also of Old High German latta "lath," Old Norse latta "lath, rafter," Dutch lat "lath"), perhaps from a PIE root meaning "to split, cleave."

Synonyms: strip, slat, batten

Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider "solid piece" or "beam" for contrasting concepts)

词性: noun, verb
