disinformation:/ˌdɪsɪnfərˈmeɪʃən/
n.
虚假信息，假情报
The spread of disinformation can have serious consequences. 虚假信息的传播会造成严重的后果。
Deliberately misleading or biased information; manipulated narrative or facts; propaganda.  故意误导或带有偏见的信;被操纵的叙述或事实;宣传。
The government was accused of spreading disinformation about the war. 政府被指控散布关于战争的虚假信息。
近义词: misinformation, propaganda, lies, falsehoods
反义词: truth, facts, accuracy
