ichnite:/ˈɪknaɪt/| n. |足迹，化石足迹|A fossilized footprint.  A track or footprint of an extinct animal, preserved in rock. |例句：The ichnite provided valuable clues about the dinosaur's size and gait. 这块足迹化石为了解恐龙的体型和步态提供了宝贵的线索。

例句：Researchers discovered a new ichnite in the sandstone formation. 研究人员在砂岩地层中发现了一个新的足迹化石。|近义词：fossil footprint, track, trace|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, potentially "unfossilized footprint")|词性:noun
