epotation:/ˌiːpoʊˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 豪饮，痛饮 | The act of drinking large quantities of liquid, especially alcoholic beverages. | He engaged in an epotation of beer after winning the game. 他赢了比赛后痛饮啤酒。 | 近义词: carousal, binge, drinking bout | 反义词: abstinence, sobriety | 词性: noun
Etymology: From Late Latin ēpōtātiōn- (stem of ēpōtātiō ), from Latin ēpōtāre "to drink deeply," from ē- (variant of ex- ) "out" + pōtāre "to drink."
