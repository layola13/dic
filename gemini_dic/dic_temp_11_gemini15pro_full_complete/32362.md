childproofed:/ˈtʃaɪldˌpruːft/| adj.|防儿童开启的，对儿童安全的|Designed to prevent children from opening or using something potentially dangerous.  Made safe for children.|例句：The medicine bottle is childproofed.  药瓶是防儿童开启的。

例句：They childproofed the house before the baby started crawling.  在婴儿开始爬行之前，他们对房子进行了安全防护。|近义词：child-resistant, tamper-proof, safety-proof|反义词:unsafe, accessible|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From child + proof + -ed.  "Proof" in this context means resistant or impervious, as in waterproof or bulletproof.
