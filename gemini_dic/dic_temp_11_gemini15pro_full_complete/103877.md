jubilation:/ˌdʒuːbɪˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 欢腾，喜悦，庆祝 |A feeling of great happiness and triumph, especially due to a success. |The team erupted in jubilation when they won the championship. 球队赢得冠军时爆发出欢呼声。|  The crowd cheered in jubilation as the fireworks began. 当烟花开始燃放时，人群欢呼雀跃。| synonyms: rejoicing, exultation, elation, triumph, euphoria | antonyms: grief, sorrow, despair, despondency | noun
