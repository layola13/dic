fourierism:/ˈfʊrieɪˌɪzəm/ | n. | 傅立叶主义; 傅里叶主义 |A system of socialist ideas, particularly those of the French social theorist Charles Fourier (1772–1837), advocating the transformation of society into self-sufficient, cooperative communities.|  Fourierism gained popularity in the 19th century.  傅立叶主义在19世纪 gained popularity. |Synonyms:  Fourieristic socialism, utopian socialism| Antonyms: capitalism, individualism| noun
