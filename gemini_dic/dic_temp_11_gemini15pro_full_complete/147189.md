ETYMOLOGY->

From over- + luxuriousness + -es.  "Over-" acts as a prefix indicating excess. "Luxuriousness" is the noun form of "luxurious," deriving from the Latin "luxuria" meaning "luxury, abundance, excess."  The suffix "-es" creates the plural form.
