collado:/koˈʎaðo/ (Spanish), /kɔˈlɑːdoʊ/ (Anglicized)| n.|山坳，山口，鞍部|A high point of land between two mountains, resembling a neck or saddle.|例句：The hikers reached the collado just as the sun began to set.  徒步旅行者在太阳开始落山时到达了山坳。

例句：The collado offered stunning views of the valley below.  从山坳可以看到下面山谷的壮丽景色。|近义词：pass, saddle, gap, col|反义词:peak, summit|词性:noun
