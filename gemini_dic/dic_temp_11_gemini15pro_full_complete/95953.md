inculcate:/ɪnˈkʌlkeɪt/
verb
灌输，谆谆教诲
instill (an idea, attitude, or habit) by persistent instruction.
The teacher inculcated in her students the importance of hard work. 老师向她的学生灌输努力学习的重要性。
instill, implant, ingrain, infuse
eradicate, extinguish
v.
