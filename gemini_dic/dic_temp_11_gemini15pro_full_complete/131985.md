nonconvertibles:/ˌnɒnkənˈvɜːrtɪblz/ | n. | 不可兑换证券；不可转换债券 |  Securities, typically bonds or preferred stock, that cannot be exchanged for another type of security, usually common stock.  |  The company issued nonconvertibles to raise capital.  该公司发行不可转换债券以筹集资金。 |  Nonconvertibles offered a higher interest rate than the convertibles. 不可转换债券提供的利率高于可转换债券。 | synonyms: straight bonds, debentures | antonyms: convertibles, exchangeable securities | noun
