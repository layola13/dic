meanings:/ˈmiːnɪŋz/| n.|意思，含义；意义，重要性|Something that is meant or conveyed, especially by language; a significance.|The meaning of "red" is a color.  红色的意思是是一种颜色。|synonyms: significance, sense, import, gist|antonyms: meaninglessness, insignificance|noun

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do these symbols mean? = 这些符号是什么意思？
The red light means "stop". = 红灯表示“停止”。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you meaning by that? = 你那样说是什么意思？ (不太常用，更常用"What do you mean by that?")
What is this word meaning in this context? = 在这种语境下，这个词是什么意思？(不太常用，更常用"What does this word mean in this context?")

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What has this experience meant to you? = 这段经历对你来说意味着什么？
His words have meant a lot to me. = 他的话对我意义重大。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
