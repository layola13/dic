dunked:/dʌŋkt/ | v. | 浸泡，扣篮 | To submerge something briefly in liquid.  In basketball, to shoot the ball down through the basket with force. | He dunked his biscuit in his tea. 他把饼干浸泡在茶里。|  He dunked the ball over two defenders. 他越过两名防守队员扣篮。 | synonyms: dipped, submerged, slammed (in basketball) | antonyms: removed, withdrew | verb

dunked:/dʌŋkt/ | adj. | 被浸泡的 |  Having been briefly submerged in liquid. | The dunked donut was dripping with glaze.  被浸泡的甜甜圈上滴着糖霜。| synonyms: soaked, dipped | antonyms: dry | adjective
