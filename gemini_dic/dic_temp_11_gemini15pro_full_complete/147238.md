overmeekly: /ˌoʊvərˈmiːkli/ | adv. | 过分温顺地，过分谦恭地 |  In an excessively meek or submissive manner; too humbly or timidly. | She overmeekly accepted the criticism, even though she knew it was unfair. 她过分温顺地接受了批评，即使她知道这是不公平的。 |  He overmeekly apologized for interrupting, even though he hadn't realized he was doing so. 他过分谦恭地为打断别人道歉，即使他没有意识到自己这样做。| 近义词:submissively, timidly, humbly | 反义词: assertively, boldly, confidently | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over + meek + -ly.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Meek" comes from Old Norse *mjukr, meaning "soft" or "pliant." "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.
