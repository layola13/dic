dipsomania:/ˌdɪpsəˈmeɪniə/| n.| 酒狂，嗜酒症|An uncontrollable craving for alcoholic beverages.|He suffered from dipsomania and could not control his drinking. 他患有嗜酒症，无法控制自己的饮酒。|近义词：alcoholism, drunkenness|反义词：sobriety, temperance|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *dipsa* (thirst) + *-mania* (madness).
