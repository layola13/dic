banns:/bænz/
n.
结婚公告，婚讯
a public announcement of a proposed marriage, made in church three Sundays in a row
The banns of marriage were read in church for three weeks.  结婚公告在教堂连续宣读了三个星期。
近义词：announcement, proclamation
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *banes (plural), from Frankish *ban "proclamation, order", from Proto-Germanic *bannaną "to summon, proclaim, command".
