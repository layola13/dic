overculling:/ˌəʊvərˈkʌlɪŋ/ | n. | 过度捕杀，过度剔除 | The act of culling too many animals or plants, often leading to negative ecological consequences. |  Overculling of deer has led to an imbalance in the forest ecosystem. 过度捕杀鹿导致森林生态系统失衡。| Overharvesting, overhunting | Underculling, sustainable harvesting | noun
