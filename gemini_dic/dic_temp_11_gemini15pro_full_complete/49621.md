demicastor:/ˈdemɪˌkæstər/ | n. |  半水獭 | An extinct genus of beavers known from fossils found in North America.  It represents a transitional form between the older Palaeocastor and the modern Castor. |  The demicastor fossils provided valuable insights into beaver evolution. 半水獭化石为了解海狸进化提供了宝贵的见解。 |  synonym:  paleocastor (somewhat), ancestor (in a broader sense) | antonym: Castor (modern beaver) | noun
