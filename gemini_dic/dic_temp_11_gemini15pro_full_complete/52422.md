didjeridoo:/ˌdɪdʒəriˈduː/
n.
迪吉里杜管
A wind instrument developed by Indigenous Australians of northern Australia. It is a long wooden tube, traditionally made from a eucalyptus tree hollowed out by termites.
He played a haunting melody on the didjeridoo. 他用迪吉里杜管吹奏了一曲萦绕心头的旋律。
近义词:dronepipe (虽然不完全相同，但音色相似)
反义词: 无 (没有直接的反义词，因为它是一种独特的乐器)
词性:名词
