counter-player:/ˈkaʊntərˌpleɪər/| n. |对手; 对弈者|A person who plays against another, especially in a game or sport.  Someone who acts in opposition to another.|例句：He's a formidable counter-player in chess. 他是一位可怕的国际象棋对手。

例句：In negotiations, she proved to be a shrewd counter-player. 在谈判中，她被证明是一个精明的对手。|近义词：opponent, adversary, rival, competitor|反义词:ally, teammate, partner|词性:noun
