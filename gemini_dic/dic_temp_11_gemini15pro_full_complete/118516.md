mesencephalon:/ˌmɛsɛnˈsɛfəlɒn/, /ˌmɪdˈbrɪn/ | n. | 中脑 | The mesencephalon, or midbrain, is the portion of the brainstem that connects the hindbrain and the forebrain.  It plays a crucial role in functions such as vision, hearing, motor control, sleep/wake, arousal (alertness), and temperature regulation. | The mesencephalon plays a vital role in eye movement. 中脑在眼球运动中起着至关重要的作用。

The doctor explained that damage to the mesencephalon could lead to Parkinson's disease. 医生解释说，中脑损伤可能导致帕金森病。 | synonym: midbrain | antonym: N/A | noun
