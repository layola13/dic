mestizoes:/mɛˈstiːzoʊz/
|n.|混血儿（尤指西班牙人和美洲印第安人的混血后代）|A person of mixed racial ancestry, especially of Spanish and Indigenous American descent.|Many mestizoes in Mexico City speak both Spanish and Nahuatl. 墨西哥城的许多混血儿既说西班牙语又说纳瓦特尔语。|Synonyms: hybrid, mixed-race|Antonyms: purebred, full-blooded|noun

mestizo:/mɛˈstiːzoʊ/
|n.|混血儿（尤指西班牙人和美洲印第安人的混血后代）|A person of mixed racial ancestry, especially of Spanish and Indigenous American descent.|The mestizo population is a significant demographic group in many Latin American countries. 混血人口在许多拉丁美洲国家都是一个重要的人口群体。|Synonyms: hybrid, mixed-race|Antonyms: purebred, full-blooded|noun
