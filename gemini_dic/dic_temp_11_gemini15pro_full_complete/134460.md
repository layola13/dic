nonjournalistically: /ˌnɒnˌdʒɜːrnəˈlɪstɪkli/ /ˌnɑːnˌdʒɜːrnəˈlɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 非新闻性地，不像新闻报道那样 |  In a manner that avoids the typical characteristics of journalistic writing, such as objectivity, factual accuracy, and concise reporting.  It implies a more personal, subjective, or perhaps embellished style. | He spoke nonjournalistically about his experiences, focusing on emotions rather than facts. 他非新闻性地讲述了他的经历，侧重于情感而不是事实。 |  She described the event nonjournalistically, adding her own opinions and interpretations. 她非新闻性地描述了这件事，加入了她自己的观点和解释。 |  informally, personally, subjectively | journalistically, objectively, factually | adverb
