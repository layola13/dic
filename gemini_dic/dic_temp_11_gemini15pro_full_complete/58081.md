droop:/druːp/
|v.  n.|下垂，萎靡；低垂|To bend or hang downward; to become weakened or depressed. |The flowers drooped in the heat.  花在高温下垂了下来。|近义词：sag, wilt, decline, flag|反义词：rise, perk up, revive|词性：动词
droop:/druːp/
|n.|下垂，萎靡|The act or condition of drooping. |There was a noticeable droop to his shoulders. 他的肩膀明显地垂了下来。|近义词：sag, wilt|反义词：rise, perk up|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *drupa ("to droop, sink"), from Proto-Germanic *drupaną ("to drip, drop, sink"). Cognate with Old English drēopan ("to drip").
