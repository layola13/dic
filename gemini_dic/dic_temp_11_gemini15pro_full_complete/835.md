abstract-away-from:/ˌæbˈstrækt əˈweɪ frəm/ | phrasal verb | 从...抽象出来，脱离... | To generalize or conceptualize something by removing specific details or context.  It involves focusing on the essential characteristics and ignoring the irrelevant ones. |  Programmers often abstract away from the underlying hardware when writing software. 程序员在编写软件时经常会从底层硬件中抽象出来。

|  The artist abstracted away from the real-life scene to create a more impressionistic painting. 艺术家从现实生活中抽象出来，创作了一幅更具印象派风格的画作。 | synonyms: generalize, conceptualize, simplify, distill | antonyms: specify, detail, concretize | verb phrase
