inking:/ˈɪŋkɪŋ/ | n. | 墨迹，着墨，上墨 | the act of putting ink onto something | The inking of the contract was a momentous occasion.  合同的签署是一个重要的时刻。 | 近义词：coloring, painting, drawing | 反义词: erasing, wiping | noun

inking:/ˈɪŋkɪŋ/ | adj. | 墨色的，涂墨的 | covered or marked with ink |  He had inky fingers after signing the document.  签署文件后，他的手指沾满了墨水。 | 近义词： inky, black | 反义词: clean, blank | adjective
