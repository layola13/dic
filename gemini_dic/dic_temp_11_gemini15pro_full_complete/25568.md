bombard:/bɒmˈbɑːd/ (UK), /bɑːmˈbɑːrd/ (US)| v. |轰炸，炮击；大量提问；连续发送信息|To attack a place with continuous heavy fire from artillery or aircraft; to assail persistently with questions, criticisms, or information.|The enemy bombarded the city for days. 敌人连续几天轰炸了这座城市.|synonyms: shell, attack, assail, pepper, barrage|antonyms: defend, protect|verb

bombard:/bɒmˈbɑːd/ (UK), /bɑːmˈbɑːrd/ (US)| n. |(古代)一种短炮|A type of short cannon used formerly.|The bombard was a powerful weapon in siege warfare.  这种短炮是攻城战中的有力武器。|synonyms: cannon, mortar|antonyms: shield, armor|noun
