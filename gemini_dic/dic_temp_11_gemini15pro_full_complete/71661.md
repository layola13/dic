flatties:/ˈflætiz/| n. |平底鞋；芭蕾平底鞋|Flat shoes, especially ballet flats.|She prefers wearing flatties to heels. 她更喜欢穿平底鞋而不是高跟鞋。|Example: I bought a new pair of red flatties for the summer. 我夏天买了一双新的红色平底鞋。|Synonyms: flats, ballet flats, ballerinas|Antonyms: heels, high heels|Noun

flatties:/ˈflætiz/| n. |比目鱼；鰈形目魚類（尤指在英國水域發現的）|A general term for flatfish, especially those found in British waters.|He caught a few flatties while fishing off the coast. 他在海岸边钓鱼时捕到几条比目鱼。|Example: Flatties are a popular dish in many coastal regions. 比目鱼是许多沿海地区的热门菜肴。|Synonyms: flatfish, flounder, sole, plaice|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could consider round fish)|Noun
