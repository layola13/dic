etherealizes:/ɪˈθɪəriəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使...变得空灵；使...变得超凡脱俗；使...精神化；使...升华
To make something ethereal; to give something a delicate and unearthly quality.
The music etherealizes the mundane experience of waiting for the bus.  这音乐使等公交车的平凡体验变得超凡脱俗。
The artist's brushstrokes etherealized the landscape, giving it a dreamlike quality.  艺术家的笔触使风景变得空灵，赋予它梦幻般的品质。
Synonyms: spiritualize, refine, sublimate, rarify
Antonyms: materialize, solidify, coarsen
词性: 动词
