heliometry:/ˌhiːliˈɒmɪtri/ | n. | 日径测量法；太阳测量法 | The measurement of the sun's diameter, or of the angular distance of any two celestial objects, by means of a heliometer. | Heliometry played a crucial role in early astronomical observations. 日径测量法在早期天文观测中发挥了至关重要的作用。 |  Heliometry provided precise measurements of the positions and movements of celestial bodies. 日径测量法提供了天体位置和运动的精确测量。 | synonyms: astrometry | antonyms: N/A | noun
