antimonarchs:/ˌæntiˈmɑːnərkists/ | n. | 反君主制者；反对君主制的人 | People who oppose monarchy are called antimonarchs.  Those who believe that monarchy is an outdated and unfair system are often antimonarchs. |  The antimonarchs protested outside the palace. 反君主制者在王宫外抗议。

Antimonarchs believe that hereditary power is undemocratic. 反君主制者认为世袭权力是不民主的。| 近义词: republicans, revolutionaries | 反义词: monarchists, royalists | 词性: 名词(plural)
