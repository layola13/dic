infeasible:/ɪnˈfiːzəbl/
adjective
不可行的，办不到的
not capable of being carried out or put into practice; not practicable or workable
The project was deemed infeasible due to the high costs involved.  这个项目由于涉及高昂的成本被认为是不可行的。
The proposed solution was technically infeasible. 提出的解决方案在技术上是不可行的。
近义词:impractical, unworkable, impossible, unattainable
反义词:feasible, possible, practical, achievable
词性:adjective

infeasibility:/ɪnˌfiːzəˈbɪləti/
noun
不可行性
the state or quality of being infeasible
The infeasibility of the plan became apparent after careful analysis.  经过仔细分析，该计划的不可行性变得显而易见。
The report highlighted the infeasibility of completing the project on time. 报告强调了按时完成项目的不可行性。
近义词:impracticability, impossibility
反义词:feasibility, possibility
词性:noun
