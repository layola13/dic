deceivably:/dɪˈsiːvɪŋli/| adv.|欺骗地，貌似地|in a way that is deceptive; so as to deceive|Her cakes are deceptively simple to make. 她的蛋糕看起来很容易做，其实不然。|近义词：misleadingly, deceptively, falsely|反义词：honestly, truthfully|词性:副词
词源: From deceive + -ably.  Ultimately from Latin *dēcipere*, from *dē-* (down, away from) + *capere* (to take).
