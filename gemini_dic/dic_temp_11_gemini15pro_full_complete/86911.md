hemmed:/hemd/ | v. | 包边，缝边，包围，限制 | to fold back and sew down the edge of a piece of cloth or clothing, or to surround something closely, preventing movement or escape. | She hemmed the dress before wearing it. 她穿之前给裙子缝了边。| The soldiers were hemmed in on all sides. 士兵们被团团包围。 | synonyms: enclose, confine, surround, border, edge | antonyms: free, release, liberate | verb

hemmed:/hemd/ | adj. | 被包围的，被限制的 | surrounded or restricted |  The hemmed-in troops were forced to surrender. 被包围的军队被迫投降。|  She felt hemmed in by her family's expectations. 她感到被家人的期望所束缚。| synonyms: confined, restricted, trapped, enclosed | antonyms: free, liberated, unrestricted | adjective
