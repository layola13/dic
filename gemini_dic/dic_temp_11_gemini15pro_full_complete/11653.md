armillary:/ˈɑːrmɪˌlɛri/| adj. n.|浑仪；球仪的；环形的|An armillary sphere is a model of the celestial sphere, consisting of a spherical framework of rings, centred on Earth or the Sun, that represent the circles of the celestial sphere, such as the horizon, meridian, and the ecliptic. |The museum displayed a beautiful antique armillary sphere.  博物馆展示了一个美丽的古董浑天仪。|近义词：celestial globe, sphere|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun
