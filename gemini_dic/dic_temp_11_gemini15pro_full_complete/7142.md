angelic:/ænˈdʒɛlɪk/ | adj. | 天使的；似天使的 | Resembling or characteristic of an angel, especially in innocence, beauty, or goodness. | The child had an angelic face.  这个孩子有一张天使般的脸庞。|  The choir sang with angelic voices.  唱诗班的声音如同天使一般。|  近义词：divine, seraphic, heavenly | 反义词：diabolic, demonic, devilish |词性: 形容词
