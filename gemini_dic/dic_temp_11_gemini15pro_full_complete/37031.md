comanage:/ˌkoʊˈmænɪdʒ/
verb
共同管理；协同管理
to manage something together with someone else
We decided to comanage the project to share the workload. 我们决定共同管理这个项目以分担工作量。
The department is comanaged by two directors.  这个部门由两位主管共同管理。
Synonyms: co-administer, share management, jointly manage
Antonyms: solely manage, independently manage
词性: 动词

comanager:/ˌkoʊˈmænɪdʒər/
noun
共同管理者；共同经营者
a person who manages something together with someone else
The comanagers have different strengths that complement each other.  这两位共同管理者有着不同的优势，彼此互补。
They appointed two comanagers to oversee the transition. 他们任命了两名共同管理者来监督过渡期。
Synonyms: co-administrator, joint manager
Antonyms: sole manager, independent manager
词性: 名词
