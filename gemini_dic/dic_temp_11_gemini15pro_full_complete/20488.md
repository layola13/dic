bituminizing:/bɪˈtʃuːmənaɪzɪŋ/ /bɪˈtuːmənaɪzɪŋ/
verb
沥青化；用沥青处理
The process of coating or impregnating something with bitumen.
The road was being bituminized to improve its surface. 这条道路正在进行沥青化处理以改善路面。
The driveway was bituminized to prevent water damage.  车道被沥青化以防止水损坏。
近义词:asphalting, tarring
反义词:None
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From bitumen + -ize.  Bitumen comes from Latin.
