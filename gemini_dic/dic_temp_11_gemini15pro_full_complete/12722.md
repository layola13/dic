assessor:/əˈsesər/| n.|顾问，助理|Someone who advises or assists a judge or magistrate.|The judge consulted with his assessor before making a ruling. 法官在作出裁决前咨询了他的顾问。|近义词：advisor, consultant, assistant|反义词:|noun
