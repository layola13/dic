lored: /lɔːrd/ | adj. | 诱人的；有吸引力的 | Alluring; tempting, often in a mysterious or slightly dangerous way.  |例句：The lored traveler ventured deeper into the enchanted forest.  被诱惑的旅行者冒险深入到迷人的森林中。|例句：The siren's lored song drew the sailors closer to the rocks.  塞壬诱人的歌声吸引着水手们靠近岩石。|近义词：alluring, enticing, tempting, seductive |反义词：repulsive, unappealing, off-putting |词性: 形容词

词源:  Likely a poetic or archaic variant of "lured," past participle of the verb "lure."  "Lure" comes from Old French "luerre" (a decoy for a hawk), from Frankish *lōthr, related to Old High German lōthra (bait).
