calcinable:/kælˈsaɪnəbl/ | adj. | 可煅烧的 | Able to be calcined; capable of being reduced to a powder or friable substance by the action of heat. | The limestone is calcinable and can be used to make cement. 石灰石是可煅烧的，可以用来制造水泥。| The ore is highly calcinable, making it easy to process. 这种矿石很容易煅烧，因此很容易加工。 |  combustible, burnable | non-combustible, incombustible | adjective
