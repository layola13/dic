hawthorny:/ˈhɔːθɔːrni/| adj.|山楂的；多刺的；棘手的|Resembling or characteristic of a hawthorn; thorny; difficult or awkward to deal with.|例句：The path was hawthorny and difficult to traverse.  这条小路长满了荆棘，很难通行。

例句：The hawthorny issue of immigration reform remains a contentious topic. 棘手的移民改革问题仍然是一个有争议的话题。|近义词：thorny, prickly, spiky, difficult, tricky|反义词:smooth, easy, simple|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *hawthorny, from Old English *haguþornig, equivalent to hawthorn +‎ -y.
