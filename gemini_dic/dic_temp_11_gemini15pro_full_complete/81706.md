greener:/ˈɡriːnər/| adj.|更绿的，更环保的|Comparative form of "green."  Indicates a greater degree of greenness, either in color or in environmental friendliness. |The grass is greener on the other side of the fence.  篱笆另一边的草更绿。|近义词：more environmentally friendly, more ecological|反义词：browner, less green|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *grener, comparative of grene (“green”), from Old English grēnra, comparative of grēne (“green”).
