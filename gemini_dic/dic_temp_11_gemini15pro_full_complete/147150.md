overlording:/ˌoʊvərˈlɔːrdɪŋ/ | n. | 专横跋扈；作威作福；称王称霸 | The act of dominating or ruling over others tyrannically. |  The overlording of the small nations by their larger neighbors caused much resentment.  较大的邻国对小国的霸凌引起了很多怨恨。 |Synonyms: dominating, tyranny, oppression, subjugation | Antonyms: submission, subservience, yielding | noun

overlord:/ˈoʊvərˌlɔːrd/ | n. | 宗主；霸主；最高统治者 | A ruler, especially one who has or claims the right to rule over other rulers. | The feudal overlord demanded absolute loyalty from his vassals. 封建领主要求他的诸侯绝对忠诚。 | Synonyms: ruler, lord, master, sovereign, potentate | Antonyms: subject, servant, underling | noun
