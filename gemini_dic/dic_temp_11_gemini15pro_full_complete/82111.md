groggified:/ˈɡrɒɡɪfaɪd/| adj.|醉醺醺的，昏昏沉沉的|Intoxicated, dazed, or stupefied, especially from alcohol.|After a few pints, he was completely groggified and couldn't stand up straight. 几品脱啤酒下肚后，他醉醺醺的，站都站不稳了。|近义词：drunk, intoxicated, inebriated, tipsy|反义词:sober, clear-headed|词性:形容词

词源:  From "grog" (an alcoholic drink) + "-ified" (a suffix meaning "made into" or "caused to become").  Ultimately from "Old Grog," a nickname for British Admiral Edward Vernon, who introduced diluted rum rations to his sailors.
