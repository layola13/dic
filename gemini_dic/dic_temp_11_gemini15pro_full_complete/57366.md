doxologically:/dɒksəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adv.|颂赞地，赞美地|In a manner befitting a doxology; giving praise or glory to God.|He ended the prayer doxologically, praising God for his mercy and grace.|近义词：worshipfully, reverently, adoringly|反义词：blasphemously, irreverently|词性:副词
词源: From the Greek word "doxologia" (δοξολογία), meaning "a word of praise," from "doxa" (δόξα) meaning "glory" and "logia" (λογία) meaning "speaking."
