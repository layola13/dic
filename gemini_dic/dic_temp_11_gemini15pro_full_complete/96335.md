indignly: /ɪnˈdɪɡnəntli/ | adv. | 愤慨地，义愤填膺地 | In a manner expressing strong displeasure at something considered unjust, offensive, insulting, or base. | He indignantly denied the accusations. 他愤慨地否认了这些指控。 | indignantly rejected the offer.  他愤慨地拒绝了提议。| 近义词: angrily, furiously, resentfully | 反义词: calmly, patiently, submissively | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Late Latin *indignant-, from Latin indignans, present participle of indignari "to deem unworthy," from in- "not" + dignus "worthy."
