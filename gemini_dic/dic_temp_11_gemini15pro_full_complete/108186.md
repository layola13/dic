latticed:/ˈlætɪst/| adj.|格子状的，构成格子形的，用格子装饰的|Made of or covered with latticework.  A structure of crossed wooden or metal strips usually arranged to form a diagonal pattern of open spaces between the strips.|The gazebo was latticed and covered in climbing roses.  凉亭是格子状的，上面爬满了玫瑰。|近义词：crisscrossed, gridded, meshed|反义词:solid, unlatticed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From late Middle English *latisen, from Old French latis, from latte “lath” < Frankish *latta, of uncertain origin.|
