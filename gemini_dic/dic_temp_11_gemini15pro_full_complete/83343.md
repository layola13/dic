gypsylike:/ˈdʒɪpsiˌlaɪk/
adjective
像吉普赛人的；流浪的；自由奔放的
Resembling or characteristic of a Gypsy, especially in being nomadic, unconventional, or colorful.
Her gypsylike lifestyle involved traveling from town to town, selling handmade jewelry. 她像吉普赛人一样的生活方式让她辗转于各个城镇，出售手工珠宝。
The caravan had a gypsylike appearance, brightly painted and decorated with tassels. 这辆大篷车外观像吉普赛人的，漆成鲜艳的颜色，并用流苏装饰。
synonyms: nomadic, wandering, roving, bohemian, free-spirited, unconventional
antonyms: settled, conventional, sedentary, conformist
词性: 形容词
