hamitic:/ˈhæmɪtɪk/ | adj. n.|含米特人的，含米特语的；含族人；含语系|Relating to the Hamites, a group of North African peoples postulated by 19th-century anthropologists, or their languages. Now generally considered outdated and inaccurate due to its racist implications.|The term "Hamitic" was used in a now-discredited theory of racial classification.|近义词: Afro-Asiatic (more accurate), Cushitic, Berber|反义词:  (No direct antonym due to the term's problematic nature) |词性: adjective, noun
