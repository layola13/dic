cosseted:/ˈkɒsətɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːsətɪd/ (US)| adj.|娇生惯养的，宠溺的|Overly pampered and protected.|The cosseted child had never had to do chores.  这个娇生惯养的孩子从来不用做家务。|近义词：pampered, indulged, spoiled|反义词：neglected, deprived|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "cosset," which comes from Middle English "cosset," meaning a pet lamb, ultimately from Old North French "coste," a rib, side, hence something held close to the side, like a pet.
