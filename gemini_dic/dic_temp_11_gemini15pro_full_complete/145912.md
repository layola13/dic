overdignifying: /ˌoʊvərˈdɪɡnɪfaɪɪŋ/
| adj. | 过分抬举的，过分尊重的 | Treating something or someone with more dignity or importance than is warranted.  Making something seem more important or serious than it really is. |  He felt that calling him 'Professor' was overdignifying a man who barely scraped through high school.  他觉得称他为“教授”是过分抬举一个勉强读完高中的人。 |  overestimating, exaggerating, aggrandizing, inflating, overstating | understating, belittling, downplaying, minimizing | adjective
