discontentedly:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛntɪdli/
adverb
不满地，不高兴地
In a manner expressing dissatisfaction or lack of contentment.
She sighed discontentedly and turned away. 她不满地叹了口气，转过身去。
He muttered discontentedly about the long wait. 他不满地嘀咕着等待时间太长。
近义词:unhappily, grumblingly, sullenly
反义词:happily, contentedly, cheerfully
词性:adverb

discontent:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛnt/
noun
不满，不满足
A feeling of dissatisfaction or unhappiness.
There is widespread discontent among the workers. 工人们普遍不满。
The new policy has fueled discontent among the population. 新政策加剧了民众的不满。
近义词:dissatisfaction, unhappiness, restlessness
反义词:contentment, satisfaction, happiness
词性:noun

discontent:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛnt/
verb
使不满，使不满足
To make someone feel dissatisfied or unhappy.
The decision to close the factory discontented the workers. 关闭工厂的决定让工人们感到不满。
The lack of progress discontented the investors. 缺乏进展让投资者感到不满。
近义词:dissatisfy, disappoint, frustrate
反义词:satisfy, please, content
词性:verb


词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + content (satisfied, happy).  Ultimately from Latin contentus, past participle of continēre "to hold together, contain."
