outcavil: /ˌaʊtˈkævəl/
verb
胜过诡辩；以诡辩胜过
to surpass or overcome in caviling; to raise frivolous objections to
He outcaviled his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中以诡辩胜过了对手。
近义词: outargue, quibble, nitpick
反义词: concede, agree
词性: verb

词源:
From out- + cavil.  "Out-" acts as a prefix meaning "to surpass or exceed" and "cavil" means "to make petty or unnecessary objections."
