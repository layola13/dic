length:/leŋθ/ /leŋkθ/
noun|长度，长，一段时间，篇幅|The length of the table is two meters. 桌子的长度是两米。|近义词：extent, duration, distance|反义词: shortness, brevity|n.

length:/leŋθ/ /leŋkθ/
adjective|长的|It was a length debate. 这是一场冗长的辩论。(不常用用法，通常用long)|近义词：long, extended|反义词: short, brief|adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *lenþu (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *langþō, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)lengʰ- (“long”). Cognate with Dutch lengte, German Länge.
