outstorm: /ˈaʊtˌstɔːrm/
verb
战胜暴风雨，比…更猛烈
To surpass in storminess; to outdo in violence or fury (as a storm).
The ship managed to outstorm the hurricane. 这艘船设法战胜了飓风。
The new captain outstormed the previous one in bravery. 新船长在勇气上胜过前任。
Synonyms: outweather, withstand, endure
Antonyms: succumb, yield
词性: 动词
