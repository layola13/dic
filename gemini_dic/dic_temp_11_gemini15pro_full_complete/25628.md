bunch: /bʌntʃ/ | n. | 一束，一群，一堆 | A collection of things, typically of the same kind, growing or fastened together. |  I bought a bunch of flowers for my mother. 我给妈妈买了一束花。

She gave me a bunch of grapes from her garden. 她给了我一把从她花园里摘的葡萄。

近义词: group, cluster, collection, bundle | 反义词: individual, single | 词性: noun

bunch: /bʌntʃ/ | v. |  聚集，捆绑 | To gather or form into a bunch. | The children bunched together for warmth. 孩子们挤在一起取暖。

He bunched his fists in anger. 他愤怒地攥紧了拳头。

近义词: gather, cluster, group | 反义词: separate, disperse | 词性: verb
