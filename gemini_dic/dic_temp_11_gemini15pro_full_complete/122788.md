moldabilities:/ˌmoʊldəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可塑性，成型性|The capacity of a material to be molded or shaped into different forms.  This refers to how easily a substance can be deformed permanently without cracking or breaking. |This clay has excellent moldabilities, making it ideal for sculpting. 这种黏土具有极佳的可塑性，非常适合雕塑。|近义词：plasticity, malleability, ductility, flexibility |反义词: rigidity, stiffness, inflexibility| noun
