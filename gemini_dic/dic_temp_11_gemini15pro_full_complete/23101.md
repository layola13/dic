bounty:/ˈbaʊnti/| n.|慷慨；奖金；赏金|Something given liberally or generously; a reward or sum, especially one offered by a government, for killing or capturing a person or accomplishing something.|The government offered a bounty for the capture of the fugitive. 政府悬赏捉拿逃犯。|Synonyms: reward, premium, prize, grant|Antonyms: penalty, fine|noun

bounty:/ˈbaʊnti/| n.|大量；丰富|A plentiful supply; abundance.|The bounty of the harvest ensured that no one went hungry. 丰收保证了没有人挨饿。|Synonyms: abundance, profusion, plenty, wealth|Antonyms: scarcity, lack, dearth|noun
