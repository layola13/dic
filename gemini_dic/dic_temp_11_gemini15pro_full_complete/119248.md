micelle:/maɪˈsɛl/ /mɪˈsɛl/| n. |胶束；微团|A tiny spherical aggregate of surfactant molecules dispersed in a liquid, with the hydrophilic heads facing outwards and the hydrophobic tails inwards.|The formation of micelles is essential for the absorption of fats in the digestive system. 胶束的形成对于消化系统吸收脂肪至关重要。

|近义词：aggregate, cluster|反义词: None|词性:noun


micellar:/maɪˈsɛlər/ /mɪˈsɛlər/| adj. |胶束的|Relating to or consisting of micelles.|Micellar water is a popular cleanser. 胶束水是一种流行的清洁剂。|近义词：None|反义词: None|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *micelle*, diminutive of Latin *mica* ("crumb, grain").
