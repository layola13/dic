discussion:/dɪˈskʌʃən/ | n. | 讨论，商讨 |  A conversation or debate about a particular topic. |  We had a long discussion about the project. 我们对这个项目进行了长时间的讨论。

The discussion went on for hours. 讨论持续了几个小时。

| 近义词：conversation, debate, dialogue, talk, deliberation | 反义词：silence, quiet, disagreement | 词性：名词
