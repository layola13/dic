galligaskins:/ˌɡælɪˈɡæskɪnz/ | n. | 宽松的马裤；灯笼裤 | Loose-fitting breeches or trousers.  They were a type of wide breeches fashionable from the 16th to the early 17th centuries. | He strutted about in his galligaskins, imagining himself a dashing cavalier. 他穿着灯笼裤昂首阔步，把自己想象成一个潇洒的骑士。 |  breeches, slops, trousers |  jeans, skinny jeans | noun
