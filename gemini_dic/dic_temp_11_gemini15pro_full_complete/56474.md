doltishly:/ˈdoʊltɪʃli/| adv.|愚蠢地，笨拙地|In a stupid or foolish manner; like a dolt.|He doltishly left his keys in the car. 他愚蠢地把钥匙忘在了车里。|Synonyms: stupidly, foolishly, idiotically, obtusely|Antonyms: intelligently, wisely, cleverly, astutely|词性:副词
Etymology: From doltish + -ly.  Doltish comes from dolt, meaning a stupid person, which has uncertain origins, possibly related to dull.
