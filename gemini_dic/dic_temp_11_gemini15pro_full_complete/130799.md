nonantagonistic:/ˌnɒnænˌtæɡəˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非对抗性的 | Not antagonistic; characterized by the absence of antagonism or opposition. | The two countries maintained a nonantagonistic relationship despite their differing political ideologies.  这两个国家尽管政治意识形态不同，但仍保持着非对抗性关系。

The negotiation was surprisingly nonantagonistic, leading to a mutually beneficial agreement.  这次谈判出人意料地没有对抗性，最终达成了互利的协议。 | 近义词: cooperative, collaborative, harmonious, peaceful, friendly | 反义词: antagonistic, hostile, adversarial, conflicting, opposing | 词性: 形容词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + antagonistic (from Greek *antagonistes* "opponent, rival")
