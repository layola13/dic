noun
埃尔文岩，一种浅成岩
A fine-grained igneous rock, typically light-colored, found in dikes and sills.  It's a type of rhyolite or quartz porphyry.
Elvanite is often used for building materials due to its hardness. 埃尔文岩由于硬度高，常被用作建筑材料。
