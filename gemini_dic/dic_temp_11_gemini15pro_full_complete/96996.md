infantlike:/ˈɪnfəntˌlaɪk/| adj.|像婴儿的；幼稚的|Resembling or characteristic of an infant, especially in behavior or appearance.  Characterized by innocence, naivety, or dependence.|Her infantlike trust in everyone she met was both charming and concerning.|近义词：childlike, babyish, naive, innocent|反义词：mature, adult, sophisticated|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From infant + -like.  "Infant" comes from the Latin "infans," meaning "unable to speak" (in- "not" + fans "speaking," present participle of fari "to speak").  "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
