n.
Licensed Practical Nurses
执业实习护士
Licensed practical nurses (LPNs) provide basic nursing care under the supervision of registered nurses (RNs) or physicians.
执业实习护士 (LPN) 在注册护士 (RN) 或医生的监督下提供基本的护理。
The LPNs monitored the patients' vital signs.  执业实习护士监测病人的生命体征。

近义词:LVN (Licensed Vocational Nurse)
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider contrasting roles like physician or surgeon)
