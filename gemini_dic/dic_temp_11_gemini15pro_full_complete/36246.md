cognizee:/ˌkɑːɡnɪˈziː/ | n. |被认知者；认知对象|The person who is the object of cognition.  The one about whom something is known or perceived. | The speaker is the cognizor, and the audience is the cognizee. 演讲者是认知者，而听众是被认知者。| The teacher explained the concept clearly, ensuring every cognizee understood. 老师清晰地解释了这个概念，确保每个被认知者都理解了。|  近义词: recipient, perceiver, subject (of perception) | 反义词: cognizor, perceiver | 词性: noun
