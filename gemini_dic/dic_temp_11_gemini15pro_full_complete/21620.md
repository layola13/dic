blur:/blɜːr/ (US) /blɜː/ (UK)| n. v.| 模糊，模糊不清的事物；使模糊 |The fog blurred the outlines of the buildings. 浓雾使建筑物的轮廓变得模糊不清。|The photographer blurred the background of the photo. 摄影师把照片的背景虚化了。|近义词：smear, haze, obscure|反义词：sharpen, clarify, focus|词性:名词，动词
Etymology: From Middle English *blurre, *blurren, probably of imitative origin, related to blear ("dim-sighted").
