honchoing:/ˈhɒntʃəʊɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːntʃoʊɪŋ/ (US)| v. (present participle)|负责；掌管；指挥|Acting as the honcho, taking charge or leading.|Example: He's been honchoing the project since the beginning. 他从一开始就负责这个项目。|Example: She's honchoing the marketing department now. 她现在掌管市场部。|Synonyms: managing, directing, leading, overseeing, supervising, heading, running, commanding|Antonyms: following, obeying, assisting|verb

honcho:/ˈhɒntʃəʊ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːntʃoʊ/ (US)| n.|头儿；负责人；领班|A person in charge or the leader of a group.|Example: She's the honcho around here. 她是这儿的头儿。|Example:  He's the honcho of the sales team. 他是销售团队的负责人。|Synonyms: boss, chief, leader, manager, supervisor|Antonyms: subordinate, follower|noun
