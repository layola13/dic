axiom:/ˈæksiəm/ | n. | 公理；格言；自明之理 | A statement or proposition which is regarded as being established, accepted, or self-evidently true. |  The axioms of Euclidean geometry. 欧几里得几何的公理。 | synonym:  postulate, principle, theorem, maxim,  truism | antonym: fallacy, paradox | noun

axiom:/ˈæksiəm/ | n. | 定律；准则 | A rule or principle that many people accept as true. | It's a widely held axiom that governments should not negotiate with terrorists. 普遍接受的准则是政府不应该与恐怖分子谈判。| synonym:  rule, law, tenet, precept | antonym:  heterodoxy | noun
