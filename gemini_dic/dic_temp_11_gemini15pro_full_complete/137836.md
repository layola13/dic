nonvicarious:/ˌnɒnvɪˈkɛəriəs/
adj.
非替代的，非代理的，亲身经历的
Not experienced or felt through another person; personal, direct.
His grief was nonvicarious, stemming from his own personal loss.  他悲伤并非感同身受，而是源于自身的损失。
近义词: firsthand, direct, personal, immediate
反义词: vicarious, indirect, secondhand, empathetic
词性: 形容词
