hobbly:/ˈhɑːbli/ (US), /ˈhɒbli/ (UK)| adj.|蹒跚的，跛行的|Moving with a limp; lame.|The old man walked hobblily down the street. 老人一瘸一拐地走在街上.|近义词：lame, halting, limping|反义词:agile, nimble|词性:形容词
Etymology: From hobble (v.) + -ly (adv. suffix), from Middle English hobelen "to limp," probably of imitative origin.
