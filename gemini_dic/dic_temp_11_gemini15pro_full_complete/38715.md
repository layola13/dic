condimental:/ˌkɒndɪˈmentl/ /ˌkɑːndɪˈmentl/
|adj.|调味用的；作调味品的|Used for seasoning; serving as a condiment. |The chef added several condimental herbs to the dish. 厨师在菜里加了几种调味香草。|近义词：seasoning, flavoring, spicy, savory|反义词:bland, tasteless|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *condimentālis, from Latin condimentum (“spice, seasoning”), from condīre (“to preserve, pickle, season”), from com- + -dīre, present active infinitive of dō (“give”).
