cricketer:/ˈkrɪkɪtər/| n.|板球运动员|A person who plays cricket.|例句：He is a talented cricketer. 他是一位才华横溢的板球运动员。

例句：The cricketer scored a century in the match.  这名板球运动员在比赛中得了100分。


近义词：batsman (击球手), bowler (投球手), fielder (守场员) (These are more specific roles within cricket).|反义词: N/A (No direct antonym for a person who plays a sport)|词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From cricket (the sport) + -er (suffix denoting a person who performs an action).  "Cricket" itself likely originates from a Middle Dutch word, "krick(-e)," referring to a stick or crooked staff.
