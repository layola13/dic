induces:/ɪnˈd(j)uːsɪz/
verb
引起，导致
causes something to happen
The drug induces sleep.  这种药能引起睡眠。
persuades someone to do something
Nothing could induce me to climb a mountain.  没有什么能引诱我去爬山。
Synonyms: cause, bring about, stimulate, persuade, convince
Antonyms: prevent, deter, dissuade
词性:动词
