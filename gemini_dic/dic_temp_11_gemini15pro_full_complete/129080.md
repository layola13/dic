nitromethane:/ˌnaɪtroʊˈmɛθeɪn/ | n. |硝基甲烷|A colorless, oily, flammable liquid, CH3NO2, used as a solvent, rocket fuel, and racing fuel.  It is the simplest organic nitro compound.|Nitromethane is often used as a fuel in top fuel drag racing. 硝基甲烷常被用作顶级燃油赛车中的燃料。|Synonyms:  nitro, CH3NO2 |Antonyms: None | Noun
