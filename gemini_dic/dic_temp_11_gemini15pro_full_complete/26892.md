cala:/ˈkɑːlə/ | n. |小海湾; 小港湾|A small, sheltered bay or inlet, often found along a rocky coastline. | We anchored the boat in the cala, protected from the open sea. 我们把船停泊在小海湾里，避开大海的波涛汹涌。|Synonym: cove, inlet, bay | Antonym: open sea| noun


/ˈkɑːlə/ | n. |卡拉（重量单位）| A unit of weight used in South Asia, particularly for gold and other precious metals.  | The necklace weighed 10 calas. 这条项链重10卡拉。 | Synonym: tola (in some regions) | Antonym: N/A | noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *cala* "cove, bay," from Catalan *cala,* perhaps of pre-Roman origin.  For the weight unit, from Tamil *kalam,* ultimately from Sanskrit *kala* "small part, minute."
