extorters:/ɪkˈstɔːrtərz/
n.
勒索者；敲诈者
People who obtain something, especially money, through coercion or intimidation.
The extorters threatened to release damaging information unless they were paid a large sum of money. 勒索者威胁说，如果不付给他们一大笔钱，他们就公开破坏性的信息。

The extorters were eventually caught and brought to justice.  这些勒索者最终被抓获并绳之以法。

近义词:blackmailers, racketeers
反义词:donors, philanthropists
词性:noun
