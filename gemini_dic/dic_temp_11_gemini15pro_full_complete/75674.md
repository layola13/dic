fulfilled:/fʊlˈfɪld/| adj.|满足的，实现的|Satisfied or happy because of fully developing one's abilities or character. Having fulfilled a desire, dream, or ambition.|例句：She felt fulfilled in her career. 她对自己的事业感到满足。|近义词：satisfied, content, gratified, realized, achieved|反义词：unfulfilled, dissatisfied, frustrated|词性:形容词

fulfilled:/fʊlˈfɪld/| v.|履行，实现|To do or achieve what was hoped for or expected. To satisfy (a requirement or condition).|例句：He fulfilled his promise to visit his grandmother. 他履行了去看望他祖母的承诺。|近义词：accomplish, achieve, execute, perform, satisfy|反义词：fail, neglect, default|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *fulfillen*, from Old English *fullfyllan* (“to fill full, fulfill”), equivalent to *full* + *fill*. Cognate with Dutch *vervullen* (“to fulfill”), German *erfüllen* (“to fulfill”).
