diktat: /dɪkˈtæt/ /ˈdɪktɑːt/ | n. |  命令; 指示; (尤指) 专横的命令 | An order or decree imposed by someone in power without popular consent. |  The company president issued a diktat that all employees must work overtime. 公司总裁发布命令，所有员工必须加班。|Synonyms: decree, edict, mandate, order, command | Antonyms: request, plea, suggestion | noun

diktat: /dɪkˈtæt/ /ˈdɪktɑːt/ | n. |  (尤指不受欢迎的) 规定; 要求 | A rule or principle that is imposed forcefully or arbitrarily.  | The fashion magazine's latest diktat is that everyone must wear bright pink.  时尚杂志最新的规定是每个人都必须穿亮粉色。 | Synonyms:  rule, regulation, precept | Antonyms:  guideline, recommendation | noun
