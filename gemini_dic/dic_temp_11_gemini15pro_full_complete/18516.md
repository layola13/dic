benedictionary: /ˌbɛnɪˈdɪkʃənɛri/ | n. | 祝福;祝祷;恩赐;幸事 |  A prayer asking for God's blessing, especially one given at the end of a religious service. Something that is good and helpful. | The priest pronounced the benediction.  神父做了祝祷。|  The rain was a benediction for the farmers.  这场雨对农民来说是天赐之物。 | synonyms: blessing, invocation, grace, boon | antonyms: curse, malediction | noun
