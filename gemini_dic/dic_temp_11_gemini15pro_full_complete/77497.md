gazetted:/ɡəˈzɛtɪd/
adjective
已公布的；已公告的
officially published or announced, especially in a government gazette
The new law was gazetted last week.  新法律已于上周公布。
近义词:announced, published, proclaimed, official
反义词:unpublished, unannounced, withdrawn
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the French "gazette", meaning a newspaper, especially an official journal. Ultimately derived from "gazzetta," a Venetian coin, the price of an early newspaper.
