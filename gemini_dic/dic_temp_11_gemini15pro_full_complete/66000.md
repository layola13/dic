examination: /ɪɡˌzæmɪˈneɪʃn/ /ɪɡˌzæməˈneɪʃn/
n.
考试, 检查, 审查
A detailed inspection or investigation.  The act of looking at something closely and carefully.
The students are preparing for their final examinations. 学生们正在准备期末考试。
He underwent a thorough medical examination. 他接受了全面的体检。
test, quiz, assessment, inspection, scrutiny
checkup, investigation, analysis
noun
