ichnographical:/ˌɪknəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
平面图的；地形图的
Representing a ground plan; descriptive of a ground plan.
The ichnographical layout of the ancient city was meticulously detailed.  这座古城的平面布局被精心绘制出来。
The architect presented an ichnographical drawing of the proposed building. 建筑师展示了拟建建筑的平面图。
近义词: planimetric, topographical
反义词: elevational, perspectival
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *ikhnos* (track, footprint) + *graphein* (to write)
