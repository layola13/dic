misclassified:/ˌmɪsˈklæsɪfaɪd/| adj.|错误分类的，分类错误的|Incorrectly assigned to a category.  The algorithm misclassified several images as spam.|例句：The email was misclassified as spam and ended up in the junk folder. 这封电子邮件被错误地归类为垃圾邮件，最终进入了垃圾邮件文件夹。

例句：Several documents were misclassified and filed in the wrong cabinet.  一些文件被错误分类并归档到了错误的柜子里。|近义词：mistakenly categorized, wrongly labeled, inaccurately classified|反义词：correctly classified, accurately categorized|词性:形容词

词源:
mis- (prefix):  Meaning "wrong," "bad," or "incorrect." From Old English and related to German *miss-.
classify (verb):  Meaning "to arrange or organize by categories." From French classifier and ultimately from Latin classis "class."
-ed (suffix):  Forms the past participle of regular verbs.
