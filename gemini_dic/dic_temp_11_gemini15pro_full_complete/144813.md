over-heaps:/ˌoʊvərˈhiːps/ | adv. | 过度地，过多地 | Loads or piles excessively; to a very great degree or in large quantities.  | The gardener over-heaps the flowerpots with soil. 园丁在花盆里堆了太多的土。 |  The company over-heaps its employees with work.  这家公司给员工的工作太多了。 | 近义词: excessively, profusely, abundantly | 反义词: sparsely, moderately, slightly | 词性:副词
