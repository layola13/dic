decommission:/ˌdiːkəˈmɪʃən/
verb
使退役；使停止使用
To officially take something out of service, such as a ship, aircraft, or nuclear power station.
The Navy is planning to decommission several of its older ships. 海军正计划让几艘老旧的舰艇退役。

The power plant will be decommissioned next year.  这家发电厂明年将停止使用。

近义词: retire, withdraw, deactivate
反义词: commission, activate
词性: verb
