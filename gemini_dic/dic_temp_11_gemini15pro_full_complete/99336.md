interdeal:/ˌɪntərˈdiːl/ | n. |互相交易；相互往来；暗中勾结|A secret or private agreement or understanding between two or more parties, often involving business or political dealings.|例句：The interdeal between the two companies raised suspicions of insider trading.  两家公司之间的秘密交易引起了内幕交易的怀疑。

例句：Their interdeal ensured that both sides profited from the arrangement. 他们的相互交易确保了双方都能从协议中获利。|近义词：collusion, conspiracy, bargain, pact|反义词：disagreement, dispute|词性:名词


词源:  From inter- + deal.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "deal" refers to a transaction or agreement.
