fencelessnesses:/ˈfɛnsləsnɪsɪz/
n.
无栅栏状态；无围栏状态；无防御状态
The state of being without fences or boundaries; lack of protection or defense.
The fencelessnesses of the vast plains gave a sense of freedom.  广阔平原的无边无际给人一种自由的感觉。
The fencelessnesses of their personal information online made them vulnerable to identity theft.  他们的个人信息在网上的无保护状态使他们容易受到身份盗窃。
近义词:unprotectedness, vulnerability, openness
反义词: security, enclosure, protection
词性: 名词

Etymology:
fence (n.)  from Old French "fense" (fence, defense),  from "defendre" (to defend), from Latin "defendere"
-less (suffix)  meaning "without"
-ness (suffix) forming nouns expressing state or condition
