distribution:/ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən/ | n. | 分配，分发；分布，散布；销售 | the act of sharing something among a number of people, or spreading something over an area | The distribution of food and medical supplies is crucial after a natural disaster.  食品和医疗用品的分配在自然灾害后至关重要。| synonyms: allocation, apportionment, dispersion, dissemination | antonyms: collection, gathering, concentration | noun

distribution:/ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən/ | n. | 分布状态 | the way in which something is spread over an area | The uneven distribution of wealth is a major problem in many countries. 财富分配不均是许多国家的主要问题。| synonyms: arrangement, spread, dispersal | antonyms: concentration | noun
