cheery-bye: /ˈtʃɪəri baɪ/ | interjection | 再见；快乐的告别 |  A cheerful way of saying goodbye. It combines "cheery" (happy) and "bye" (goodbye). | "Cheery-bye, everyone! Have a wonderful day!" 再见，各位！祝你们有美好的一天！ |  Synonyms: goodbye, farewell, so long, see you later | Antonyms: hello, greetings | interjection
