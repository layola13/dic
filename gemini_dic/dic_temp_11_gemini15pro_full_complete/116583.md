matriarchy: /ˈmeɪtriˌɑːrki/ (US), /ˈmeɪtriˌɑːki/ (UK)| n. |母系社会; 女族长制|A system of social organization in which descent and inheritance are traced through the female line and power is held by women.| The anthropologist studied the Minangkabau people of Indonesia, a well-known example of a matriarchy.  这位人类学家研究了印度尼西亚的米南加保人，这是一个著名的母系社会例子。|  The novel depicts a futuristic society organized as a matriarchy. 这部小说描绘了一个未来社会，它被组织成一个母系社会。|synonyms: matriarchate|antonyms: patriarchy|noun
