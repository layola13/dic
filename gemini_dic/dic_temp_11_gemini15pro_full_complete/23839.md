breadboard:/ˈbred.bɔːrd/| n.| 面包板 |A board for making temporary electrical connections to test circuits, typically used in electronics prototyping.  A rectangular plastic block with rows of interconnected metal clips, used for creating temporary electronic circuits.|  I used a breadboard to prototype my circuit before soldering it to a PCB. 我在将电路焊接到 PCB 板之前，用面包板制作了原型。| synonyms: prototype board, circuit board | antonyms: printed circuit board (PCB), perfboard (less common as an antonym, but implies a more permanent solution)|noun

breadboards:/ˈbred.bɔːrdz/| n.| 面包板（复数） |Multiple boards for making temporary electrical connections to test circuits, typically used in electronics prototyping. | I have several breadboards of different sizes for various projects. 我有几个不同尺寸的面包板，用于各种项目。| synonyms: prototype boards, circuit boards | antonyms: printed circuit boards (PCBs)|noun
