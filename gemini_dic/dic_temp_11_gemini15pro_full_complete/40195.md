conterminous:/kənˈtɜːrmɪnəs/ /kɑːnˈtɜːrmɪnəs/
adjective
相邻的；接壤的；有共同边界的
Sharing a common boundary or border; contiguous.
The 48 conterminous states of the United States.  美国本土 48 个州。
The property is conterminous with the park. 这块地产与公园毗邻。
近义词: adjacent, bordering, contiguous, neighboring
反义词: distant, separate, nonadjacent
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin conterminus (“bordering upon, having a common boundary”), from com- (“together”) + terminus (“boundary, limit”).
