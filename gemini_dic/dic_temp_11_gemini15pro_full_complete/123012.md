monasterial:/ˌmɒnəˈstɪəriəl/
adjective
修道院的； монастырский (Russian); monastique (French); klösterlich (German)
Of, relating to, or characteristic of a monastery or monastic life.
The library contained numerous monasterial manuscripts.  图书馆藏有大量的修道院手稿。
The quiet, simple life held a monasterial appeal for him.  平静、简单的生活对他有一种修道院般的吸引力。
Synonyms:  monastic, cloistered, conventual, abbey
Antonyms:  secular, worldly, lay
词性: adjective
