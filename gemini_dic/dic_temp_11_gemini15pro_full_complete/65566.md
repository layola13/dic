euxenite:/juːˈzɛnaɪt/
noun
 euxenite, euxinite
A brownish-black mineral, a complex niobate, titanate, and tantalate of yttrium, cerium, erbium, uranium, and calcium.
Euxenite is a radioactive mineral found in granitic pegmatites.  它是一种放射性矿物，发现于花岗伟晶岩中。
Euxenite is a source of rare earth elements.  euxenite是稀土元素的一种来源。
