nongovernance:/ˌnɒnˈɡʌvənəns/| n.|非治理，缺乏治理|The state of lacking governance structures or effective governance.  This often results in chaos, corruption, or inefficiency.|The country's descent into nongovernance led to widespread instability.|Synonyms: misgovernance, mismanagement, lack of governance, anarchy, lawlessness|Antonyms: governance, government, administration, order, rule of law|Noun
