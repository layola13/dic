customary:/ˈkʌstəməri/ (US), /ˈkʌstəmri/ (UK)| adj.|习惯的，惯常的；合乎习俗的|According to the customary practice, we exchange gifts at Christmas.  Based on established traditions or usual practices.|It is customary to shake hands when you meet someone for the first time. 第一次见面握手是惯例。|近义词：usual, traditional, habitual, conventional|反义词: unusual, unconventional, atypical|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *custumarie*, from Old French *coustumier* (“habitual, usual”), from *coustume* (“custom”), from Latin *consuetūdō* (“custom, habit, usage”), from *consuēscō* (“I accustom myself”), from *con-* (with) + *suēscō* ("I become accustomed").
