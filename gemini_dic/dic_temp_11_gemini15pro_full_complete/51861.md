dextrogyrate:/ˈdɛkstroʊˌdʒaɪreɪt/| adj.|右旋的，向右旋转的|Rotating to the right, especially of polarized light.  A dextrogyrate substance rotates the plane of polarized light clockwise.|This chemical is known to be dextrogyrate.  这种化学物质已知是右旋的。|Synonyms: clockwise, right-handed|Antonyms: levogyrate, left-handed|adjective

dextrogyrate:/ˈdɛkstroʊˌdʒaɪreɪt/| n.|右旋物质|A substance that rotates plane-polarized light to the right (clockwise).|Dextrogyrate is the opposite of levogyrate. 右旋物质与左旋物质相反。|Synonyms: dextrorotatory|Antonyms: levogyrate, levorotatory|noun
