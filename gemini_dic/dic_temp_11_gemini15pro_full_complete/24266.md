brieriest:/ˈbraɪəriɪst/
adjective
最简短的；最简洁的
shortest, most concise
The chairman kept his remarks to the brieriest possible explanation. 主席的发言力求简洁，只做了最简短的解释。
近义词:shortest, briefest, tersest, most concise, most succinct
反义词:longest, most verbose, most prolix
词性:adjective

brier:/ˈbraɪər/
noun
荆棘；野蔷薇
a thorny bush or shrub
The path was overgrown with briers. 小路被荆棘覆盖了。
近义词:thorn, bramble, prickly bush
反义词:(no clear single antonym)
词性:noun

-iest:/iːɪst/
suffix
最高级后缀
suffix forming the superlative degree of adjectives and adverbs
Used to form the superlative of adjectives, as in "briefest," "happiest," etc. 用于构成形容词的最高级，如“最简短的”、“最快乐的”等。
近义词:(No synonyms for suffixes)
反义词:(No antonyms for suffixes)
词性:suffix

词源:
From Middle English *brerest, from Old English brērest, superlative of brǣr (“briar”). Cognate with Dutch braam (“bramble”), German Brombeere (“blackberry”).  The "-iest" suffix is a common superlative ending in English, tracing back to Old English.
