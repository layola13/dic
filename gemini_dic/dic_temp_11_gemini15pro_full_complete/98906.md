intensities: /ɪnˈtɛnsɪtiz/ | n. |强度；烈度|The plural form of intensity, referring to the magnitude or strength of something.  The intensities of the earthquakes were measured on the Richter scale.|The intensities of the two lights were noticeably different. 这两盏灯的强度明显不同。| synonyms: strengths, magnitudes, forces| antonyms: weaknesses, mildnesses | noun
