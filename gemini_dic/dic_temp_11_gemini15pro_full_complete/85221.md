harl:/hɑːrl/ | v. | 拖，拉，拽| To drag or pull something, often with difficulty. |  He harled the heavy box across the floor. 他费力地把沉重的箱子拖过地板。 | They harled the fishing net onto the deck.  他们把渔网拖到甲板上。| 近义词：drag, haul, pull, tug | 反义词：push, shove |词性：动词
