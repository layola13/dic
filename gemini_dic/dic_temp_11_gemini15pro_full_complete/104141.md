juneating: /ˈdʒuːnˌiːtɪŋ/
n.
六月吃; 六月饮食;  (指在六月期间的饮食习惯或特定食物)
The practice of eating specific foods or having particular dietary habits during the month of June.
Juneating involves consuming fresh, seasonal produce like strawberries and cherries. 六月饮食包括食用新鲜的时令农产品，如草莓和樱桃。
Many cultures celebrate juneating with special feasts and picnics. 许多文化通过特殊的节日和野餐来庆祝六月的饮食。
近义词: summer eating, seasonal eating
反义词: winter eating
词性: 名词

词源:  A neologism (newly coined word) combining "June" and "eating."  It likely emerged with the increasing popularity of seasonal eating and focusing on local produce.
