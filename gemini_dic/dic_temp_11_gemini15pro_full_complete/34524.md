cleanliest:/ˈkliːnlɪɪst/| adj.|最干净的|Superlative form of clean.  Refers to something being the most clean out of a group of things being compared.|例句：This is the cleanest room in the house. 这是房子里最干净的房间。|例句：She keeps her kitchen the cleanest of all her friends. 她的厨房是她所有朋友中最干净的。|近义词：tidiness, immaculateness, spotlessness|反义词：dirtiest, filthiest|词性:形容词
