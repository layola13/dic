agouti: /əˈɡuːti/ | n. | 刺豚鼠；刺豚鼠的毛色 | A small, short-eared, South American rodent with speckled, grizzled fur. | The agouti darted into the undergrowth. 刺豚鼠飞快地窜进了灌木丛。| Synonyms: rodent, cavy | Antonyms: None | Noun

agouti: /əˈɡuːti/ | adj. | 刺豚鼠毛色的；灰褐色的 | Having the speckled, grizzled fur characteristic of an agouti. | The horse had an agouti coat. 这匹马的毛色是灰褐色的。| Synonyms: grizzled, speckled | Antonyms: solid-colored | Adjective
