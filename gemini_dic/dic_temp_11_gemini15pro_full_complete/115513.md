例句：The manzanilla flowers are blooming in the garden. 花园里的甘菊花开了。|近义词：chamomile tea, chamomile|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps other herbal teas like peppermint or ginger tea could be considered opposites in terms of flavor profile) |词性:noun
