lcs:/ɛl siː ɛs/ | n. | 最长公共子序列 (Longest Common Subsequence) |  The longest subsequence common to all sequences in a set of sequences (often just two sequences). It is a classic computer science problem. | The lcs of "ABCDGH" and "AEDFHR" is "ADH".  "ABCDGH" 和 "AEDFHR" 的 lcs 是 "ADH"。 |近义词: N/A |反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
