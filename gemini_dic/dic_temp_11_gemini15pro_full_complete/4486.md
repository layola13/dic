albiggeois:/æl.bɪˈʒɔɪ/ | n. adj.| 阿尔比派教徒，阿尔比派的 |A person who was a member of a heretical Christian sect that flourished in southern France in the 12th and 13th centuries.  Also used as an adjective to describe things related to this sect.|  The Albigensian Crusade was a brutal suppression of the Albiggeois.  阿尔比十字军运动是对阿尔比派教徒的残酷镇压。 | Near Synonyms: Cathar, heretic | Antonyms: orthodox, Catholic | Noun, Adjective
