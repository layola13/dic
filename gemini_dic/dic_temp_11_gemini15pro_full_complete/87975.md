high-hatted:/ˌhaɪˈhætɪd/ | adj. | 傲慢的，自命不凡的 |  Snobbish, supercilious, behaving as if one is superior to others.  Derived from the wearing of a high hat, traditionally associated with formal attire and upper-class status. | He gave me a high-hatted look. 他傲慢地看了我一眼。 |  Snobbish, arrogant, condescending, supercilious, haughty | Modest, humble, down-to-earth | adjective
