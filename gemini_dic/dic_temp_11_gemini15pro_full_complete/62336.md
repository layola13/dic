endemical: /ɛnˈdɛmɪk(ə)l/ | adj. | 地方性的；特有的 | Native or confined to a particular region or people; characteristic of or prevalent in a field. | Malaria is endemic in many hot countries. 疟疾在许多炎热的国家流行。|  synonyms: native, indigenous, aboriginal, local | antonyms: foreign, alien, exotic | adjective

endemics: /ɛnˈdɛmɪks/ | n. | 地方病 | A disease regularly found among particular people or in a certain area. |  The spread of endemics can be difficult to control.  地方病的传播可能难以控制。| synonyms: endemic disease | antonyms: pandemic | noun
