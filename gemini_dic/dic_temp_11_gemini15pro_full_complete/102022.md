isography:/aɪˈsɒɡrəfi/ /aɪˈsɑːɡrəfi/
n.
等值线图; 等雨量线图; 等高线图
A line on a map connecting points having the same value of some phenomenon (such as rainfall, temperature, or elevation).
The isography shows areas with the same average annual rainfall.  等雨量线图显示了年平均降雨量相同的地区。
Isography helps visualize the distribution of temperature across a region. 等高线图有助于可视化一个区域内的温度分布。
contour line, isoline, isopleth
