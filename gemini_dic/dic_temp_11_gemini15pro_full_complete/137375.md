nontemperateness:/nɒnˈtɛmpərɪtnəs/
noun
缺乏节制; 不温和; 不节制
lack of moderation or restraint; immoderation; intemperance.
His nontemperateness in eating led to health problems. 他饮食上的不节制导致了健康问题。
Near synonyms: immoderation, intemperance, excessiveness, unrestraint, lack of control
Near antonyms: moderation, temperance, restraint, self-control
n.
