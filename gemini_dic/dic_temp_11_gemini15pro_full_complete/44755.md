crumblingness:/ˈkrʌmblɪŋnɪs/| n. |易碎性；崩塌性|The state or quality of being easily crumbled or broken into small fragments.|The crumblingness of the old building made it unsafe. 这栋老建筑的易碎性使它变得不安全。|Synonyms: fragility, friability, brittleness|Antonyms: strength, solidity, durability|noun


crumbling:/ˈkrʌmblɪŋ/| adj. |崩溃的；破碎的；衰落的|Breaking or falling apart into small fragments.|The crumbling walls of the castle were a testament to its age. 城堡 crumbling 的墙壁证明了它的年代久远。|Synonyms: decaying, disintegrating, deteriorating|Antonyms: strong, stable, solid|adjective
