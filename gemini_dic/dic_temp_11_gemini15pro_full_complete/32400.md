chillim:/ˈtʃɪlɪm/| n.|水烟筒，一种烟管|A pipe used for smoking, especially in India and the Middle East, with a long flexible tube connected to a container of water through which smoke is drawn.|He took a long drag from the chillim. 他深深地吸了一口烟筒。|近义词：hookah, narghile, water pipe|反义词:|名词
