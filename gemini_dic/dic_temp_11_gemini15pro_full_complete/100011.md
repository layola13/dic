interpenetrates:/ˌɪntərˈpɛnɪtreɪts/
verb
互相渗透，相互穿透
to penetrate mutually; to pervade each other; to extend into or through one another.
The roots of the two trees interpenetrated.  两棵树的树根互相缠绕。
permeates, pervades, infiltrates
separates, isolates
v.
