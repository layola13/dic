authorities: /əˈθɔːrətiz/ | n. | 当局；官方；权威人士 | People who have the power to make decisions or enforce rules. | The local authorities are responsible for maintaining the roads. 地方当局负责维护道路。|  The health authorities issued a warning about the virus. 卫生当局发布了关于该病毒的警告。 | synonyms: officials, government, administration, experts, specialists | antonyms: subordinates, civilians, public | noun

authorities: /əˈθɔːrətiz/ | n. | 权威；专家 | A person who is considered an expert in a particular field. |  She is a leading authority on medieval literature. 她是中世纪文学方面的权威。 | He is an authority on ancient Greek history. 他是古希腊历史方面的专家。| synonyms: expert, specialist, pundit, scholar | antonyms: amateur, novice, layman | noun
