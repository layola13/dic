fistulose:/ˈfɪstʃʊləs/ /ˈfɪstjʊləs/| adj.|瘘管的；有瘘管的|Characterized by an abnormal passage or fistula, a tube-like connection between two body cavities or between a body cavity and the surface.|The patient presented with a fistulose tract between the colon and the skin.  病人出现结肠和皮肤之间的瘘管。|近义词：fistular|反义词: healthy, normal|词性: adjective

fistula:/ˈfɪstʃʊlə/ /ˈfɪstjʊlə/| n.|瘘管|An abnormal passage or tube-like connection between two body cavities or between a body cavity and the surface.|The surgeon repaired the fistula in the patient's intestine.  外科医生修复了病人肠道中的瘘管。|近义词：fistulose tract|反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it represents an abnormal condition)|词性: noun
