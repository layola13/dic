bloodiness:/ˈblʌdɪnɪs/
noun

The bloodiness of the battle shocked even the veteran soldiers. 这场战斗的血腥程度甚至让老兵们都感到震惊。
bloodthirstiness, goriness, carnage
peacefulness, gentleness
