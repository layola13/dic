creaks:/kriːks/
creak:/kriːk/ | n. vi. | 嘎吱声；吱吱作响 | a harsh scraping or squeaking sound | The old stairs creaked under his weight. 老楼梯在他的重压下嘎吱作响。 | 近义词：squeak, groan | 反义词: silence, quiet | 词性:名词,动词
creaks:/kriːks/ | v. | 嘎吱作响；发出吱吱声 | to make a harsh scraping or squeaking sound | The door creaked open. 门吱呀一声开了。 | 近义词：squeak, groan | 反义词: be silent, be quiet | 词性:动词
