bellicose: /ˈbelɪˌkoʊs/ (US) /ˈbelɪkəʊs/ (UK)| adj.|好战的，好斗的|Inclined or eager to fight; aggressively hostile.|例句：His bellicose rhetoric stirred up the crowd. 他好战的言辞煽动了人群。|例句：The bellicose nation was constantly involved in conflicts.  这个好战的国家不断卷入冲突。|近义词：pugnacious, aggressive, belligerent, hostile, warlike|反义词：peaceful, pacifistic, conciliatory, amicable|词性: 形容词
Etymology: From Latin *bellicosus*, from bellicus (“of war”) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of" or "given to").  Ultimately from bellum (“war”).
