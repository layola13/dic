ashokas:/əˈʃoʊkəz/| n.|阿育王（复数）|Plural form of Ashoka, referring to more than one emperor named Ashoka or to multiple depictions or representations of the emperor Ashoka.  Ashoka was an Indian emperor of the Maurya Dynasty who ruled almost all of the Indian subcontinent from c. 268 to 232 BCE. |Example: The ashokas depicted on the pillars reflect the emperor's commitment to Buddhism.  这些柱子上描绘的阿育王反映了这位皇帝对佛教的虔诚。

Example:  Several museums showcased exhibitions featuring the ashokas of ancient India.  几家博物馆举办了以古印度阿育王为主题的展览。|近义词: Mauryan emperors, Indian rulers|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词
