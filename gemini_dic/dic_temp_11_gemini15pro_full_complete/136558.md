nonsacrificial:/ˌnɒnsækrɪˈfɪʃəl/
adjective
非牺牲的; 不需祭祀的
Not involving or requiring sacrifice.

例句：
The company adopted a nonsacrificial approach to environmental protection, finding ways to reduce pollution without hindering production.
该公司采取了一种非牺牲性的环境保护方法，找到了在不阻碍生产的情况下减少污染的方法。

The ritual was nonsacrificial, involving only prayer and meditation.
该仪式是非牺牲性的，只涉及祈祷和冥想。

近义词: non-immolating, sparing
反义词: sacrificial

词性: adjective


词源：
non- (prefix meaning "not") + sacrificial (adjective from sacrifice)
