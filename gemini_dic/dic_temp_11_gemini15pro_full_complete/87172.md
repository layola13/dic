hercynian:/hɜːrˈsɪniən/
adjective
海西的；海西期的；[地质] 海西造山运动的
Of or relating to the Hercynian orogeny.  Relating to a mountain-building period of the late Paleozoic era, about 250 million years ago, in which mountains were formed across central Europe.
The Hercynian folds are well exposed in Devon and Cornwall. 海西褶皱在德文郡和康沃尔郡暴露得很好。
Synonyms: Variscan
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a geological term.  Could consider "recent" or "modern" in a geological context, but not true opposites.)
词性: adjective
