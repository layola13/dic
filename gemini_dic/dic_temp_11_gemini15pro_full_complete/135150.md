nonomissions:/ˌnɒnəˈmɪʃənz/ | n. | 不遗漏; 全部包括 | The policy of nonomissions ensures that all relevant data is included.  不遗漏政策确保所有相关数据都被包括在内。 | The report was praised for its thoroughness and nonomissions. 这份报告因其全面性和不遗漏而受到称赞。 | 近义词: inclusion, completeness | 反义词: omission, exclusion | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "omission" (the act of leaving something out). "Omission" comes from the Latin "omissio," from the verb "omittere" (to leave out).
