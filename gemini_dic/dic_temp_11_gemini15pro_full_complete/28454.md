carburetor:/ˈkɑːrbəreɪtər/ (US) /ˈkɑːbərɛtər/ (UK)| n. |化油器|A device in an internal combustion engine for mixing air with a fine spray of liquid fuel.|The carburetor in my old car needs to be replaced. 我的旧车的化油器需要更换了。|synonyms:  carburettor (UK spelling), mixing chamber|antonyms: fuel injector|noun
