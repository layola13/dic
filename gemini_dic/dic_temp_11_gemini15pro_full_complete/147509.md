overpaint:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪnt/| v. n.|过度涂抹，覆涂；覆涂颜料|To paint over something, often obscuring the original layer.  As a noun, it refers to the layer of paint applied over another.|例句：He overpainted the graffiti on the wall. 他在墙上的涂鸦上涂了一层油漆。

例句：The overpaint concealed the original mural. 覆涂的颜料遮盖了原来的壁画。|近义词：repaint, cover, coat|反义词: uncover, expose|词性:动词,名词


ETYMOLOGY->

From over- + paint.  "Over-" signifies "above" or "excessively". "Paint" comes from Old French "peindre," ultimately from Latin "pingere" meaning "to paint."
