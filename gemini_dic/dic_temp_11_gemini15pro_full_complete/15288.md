backlog:/ˈbækˌlɒɡ/ (US) /ˈbækˌlɒɡ/ (UK)| n.|积压的工作，待办事项|A backlog is an accumulation of unfinished work or unprocessed items. It often refers to a list of tasks or projects that need to be completed.|例句：The company has a huge backlog of orders. 这家公司积压了大量订单。

例句：She faced a backlog of emails after her vacation. 她度假回来后发现邮箱里堆积了大量邮件。

近义词：accumulation, arrears, queue|反义词：surplus, excess|词性：noun
