endorses:/ɪnˈdɔːrsɪz/
verb
(第三人称单数) 赞同；认可；支持；签名；背书
Gives support or approval to someone or something.

例句：
The newspaper endorsed the candidate for mayor.
这家报纸支持这位市长候选人。

The doctor endorsed the new treatment.
医生认可了新的治疗方法。

近义词：supports, approves, backs, champions, sanctions, authorizes
反义词：opposes, disapproves, rejects, condemns, denounces


词性:v.


词源:
From Old French endosser "to write on the back of," from en- "on" + dos "back," from Latin dorsum. Originally meant to write one's name on the back of a document, hence "to sign a check," then broadened to "give support to."
