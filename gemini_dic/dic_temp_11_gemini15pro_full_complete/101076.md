inventorial:/ˌɪnvənˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
存货的，清单的
Relating to or of the nature of an inventory.
The company conducted an inventorial audit. 公司进行了存货审计。
The inventorial records were meticulously maintained.  存货记录被一丝不苟地维护着。
synonyms: inventory-related, stock-related
antonyms: non-inventorial, intangible

词性: adjective
