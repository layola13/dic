cylinderlike:/ˈsɪlɪndərˌlaɪk/
adjective
圆柱状的，像圆柱的
Resembling a cylinder in form.  Having the shape of a cylinder.
The bacteria are small and cylinderlike. 这些细菌小而呈圆柱状。
The spaceship had a long, cylinderlike body. 宇宙飞船有一个长长的圆柱形机身。
近义词:cylindrical, columnar
反义词:spherical, cubical
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From cylinder + -like.  "Cylinder" comes from the Greek "kylindros" meaning "roller," from "kylindein," meaning "to roll."  The suffix "-like" forms adjectives meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
