monoplast:/ˈmɒnəˌplæst/ /ˈmɑːnəˌplæst/| n. |单层塑料；单塑料|A plastic consisting of a single layer, or a single film or sheet of plastic. | This inexpensive shopping bag is made of monoplast.  这个便宜的购物袋是用单层塑料制成的。| The monoplast cover protected the goods from dust.  这层单塑料膜保护货物免受灰尘侵蚀。| synonym: single-layer plastic, monolayer plastic | antonym: multilayer plastic,  multi-ply plastic | noun
