convolvulin:/kənˈvɒlvjʊlɪn/| n.|旋花苷，旋花树脂|A purgative glycoside, C₅₄H₉₆O₂₇, obtained from the root of jalap.|The convolvulin in jalap root acts as a powerful purgative.  泻根中的旋花苷是一种强力泻药。|Synonyms: resin, glycoside|Antonyms: None|noun

convolvulin:/kənˈvɒlvjʊlɪn/| n.|旋花苷，旋花树脂|A purgative glycoside found in plants of the Convolvulaceae family, especially jalap.|Convolvulin can cause severe diarrhea if ingested in large quantities. 如果大量摄入旋花苷会导致严重的腹泻。|Synonyms: jalapin|Antonyms: None|noun
