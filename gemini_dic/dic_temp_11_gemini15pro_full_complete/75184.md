frizzes:/ˈfrɪzɪz/| v. (third-person singular simple present frizzles, present participle frizzling, simple past and past participle frizzled)|卷曲，使卷曲；使起泡|To form tight curls or make something form tight curls, often due to heat or moisture.  It can also refer to making something sizzle or fry.|The humidity frizzles my hair. 湿气使我的头发卷曲。|Synonyms: curl, kink, crinkle, crisp|Antonyms: straighten, smooth|Verb

frizz:/frɪz/| n.|卷发，鬈发|Tight curls or a halo of short, curly hairs.|My hair has so much frizz in the summer. 我的头发夏天有很多卷毛。|Synonyms: curl, kink, crinkle|Antonyms: straight hair|Noun
