anthropogenic:/ˌænθrəpəˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. |人为的，人类起源的|  Originating from human activity.  Relating to or resulting from the influence of human beings on nature. | The anthropogenic emissions of greenhouse gases are a major contributor to climate change. 人为排放的温室气体是气候变化的主要原因。|  The study focused on the anthropogenic impact on the rainforest ecosystem. 这项研究集中在人类活动对雨林生态系统的影响。|  human-induced, human-caused, man-made | natural, non-anthropogenic | adjective
