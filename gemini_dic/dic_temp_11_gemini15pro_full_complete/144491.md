outthunders:/ˌaʊtˈθʌndərz/
verb
胜过…的雷声; 以更大的雷声盖过
to surpass in loudness of thunder; to drown out with a louder sound of thunder
The rock band outthundered the storm.  这支摇滚乐队的演奏声盖过了雷声。
