guimpe:/ɡɪmp/ | n. | 女式高领衬衫，胸衣，（修女的）头巾|A high-necked, long-sleeved blouse or bodice, often worn under a low-necked dress, or a nun's headdress.|例句：She wore a guimpe under her sheer dress. 她穿了一件高领衬衫在透明连衣裙里面。|近义词：blouse, bodice, dickey |反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly low-cut top) |词性:noun

guimpe:/ɡɪmp/ | n. | （修女的）头巾|A nun's headdress, usually white and starched, framing the face.|例句：The nun's guimpe was impeccably starched. 修女的头巾浆洗得一丝不苟。|近义词：headdress, wimple |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "guimpe," ultimately from Frankish *wimpil, related to Old High German wimpil "veil, shawl."
