irenics: /aɪˈrɛnɪks/
noun
和平学；和解学
The study or advocacy of peace and conciliation, especially between different denominations or religions.
例句：He dedicated his life to the study of irenics, hoping to foster understanding between warring factions. 他毕生致力于和平学研究，希望促进交战派别之间的理解。

例句：The conference on irenics brought together religious leaders from around the world. 和平学会议汇集了来自世界各地的宗教领袖。
近义词：pacifism, peacemaking, conciliation, mediation
反义词：polemic, eristic, disputation
词性: n.


Etymology:
From Greek εἰρηνικός (eirēnikos, “peaceful, pacific”), from εἰρήνη (eirēnē, “peace”).
