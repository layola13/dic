minim:/ˈmɪnɪm/| n.| 最小量，微量; (音乐) 二分音符; (旧时) 液量单位|A minim is a very small amount of something, especially liquid. |The recipe calls for a minim of vanilla extract.  这食谱只需要一丁点香草精。|Synonyms: drop, bit, speck, iota|Antonyms: maximum, abundance|noun

minim:/ˈmɪnɪm/| n.| (音乐) 二分音符|A minim is a note in music that has the time value of two crotchets or half a semibreve. |The piece is full of minims and crotchets. 这首曲子充满了二分音符和四分音符。|Synonyms: half note (US)|Antonyms: N/A|noun


minim:/ˈmɪnɪm/| n.| (旧时) 液量单位 (约一滴)|A minim is a former unit of liquid volume equal to about one drop.  |Apothecaries used to measure liquids in minims. 药剂师过去用“分”来计量液体。|Synonyms: drop|Antonyms: N/A|noun
