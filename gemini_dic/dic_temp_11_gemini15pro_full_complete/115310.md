mannerisms:/ˈmænərɪzəmz/
noun
习惯；癖性；特殊举动
a way of speaking or moving that is typical of a particular person
He has the same mannerisms as his father. 他和他父亲有相同的举止习惯。

近义词:affectations, habits, peculiarities, traits
反义词:normalities
