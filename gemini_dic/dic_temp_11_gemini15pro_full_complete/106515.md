kremlins: /ˈkrɛmlɪnz/
n.
克里姆林宫（复数）; 俄罗斯政府（喻）
citadels within Russian cities, especially the one in Moscow, housing the offices of the Russian government (often used as a synonym for the Russian government itself).
The policies emanating from the Kremlin have been met with international concern.  来自克里姆林宫的政策受到了国际社会的关注。
The Kremlin denied any involvement in the incident.  克里姆林宫否认参与了这起事件。
Synonym: Russian government, Moscow government
Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "opposition" or "dissidents")
