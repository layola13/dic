delineable:/dɪˈlɪniəbl/
adjective
可描绘的，可勾勒的
able to be delineated; capable of being outlined or clearly described.
The boundaries of the property were clearly delineable on the map.  这块地产的边界在地图上清晰可辨。
The artist's style is delineable by its bold use of color.  这位艺术家的风格以其大胆的用色而著称。
近义词: definable, describable, discernible, distinct
反义词: vague, indistinct, blurred, ambiguous
词性: adjective

词源:  From delineate + -able.  Delineate comes from the Latin dēlineāre, meaning "to sketch out," from dē- "completely" + lineāre "to draw lines," from līnea "line."
