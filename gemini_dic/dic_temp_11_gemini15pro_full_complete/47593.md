debatefully:/dɪˈbeɪtfəli/
adv.
争论地，可争辩地
in a manner open to debate; arguably.
例句：The issue was debatefully discussed in the meeting.  这个问题在会议上进行了辩论。

例句：Debatefully, the new policy will benefit the economy.  可以争论的是，新政策将有利于经济。
近义词：arguably, disputably, contentiously
反义词：undeniably, indisputably, irrefutably
词性：副词
