convene:/kənˈviːn/ /kənˈvin/ | v. |召集，集合|To come together formally for an official or public purpose; assemble.|The committee will convene next week to discuss the budget. 委员会将于下周召开会议讨论预算。|近义词：assemble, gather, summon|反义词: adjourn, disperse, dismiss|词性:动词
Etymology: From Latin *convenīre*, present active infinitive of *conveniō* (“I come together, assemble”), from *con-* (“together”) + *veniō* (“I come”).
