assignably:/əˈsaɪnəbli/
adjective
可分配地，可指定地
Capable of being assigned; transferable.  Something that can be allocated or designated to someone or something else.
The rights to this property are assignably.  这处房产的权利是可以转让的。
The tasks are assignably to different teams. 这些任务可以分配给不同的团队。
近义词: transferable, allocatable
反义词: non-transferable, inalienable
词性: adjective
词源: From "assign" + "-able" (suffix indicating capability). "Assign" comes from Old French "assigner" and ultimately from Latin "assignare" (to mark out, allot).
