befits:/bɪˈfɪts/| v.|适合，相称|It is a role that befits her perfectly.  这是一个非常适合她的角色。|Example:  A grand celebration befits the occasion. 盛大的庆祝活动与这一场合相称。|Synonyms: suits, becomes, is appropriate for|Antonyms: misfits, clashes with|verb

Etymology->
From Middle English *befiten, from Old English *befetian (unattested), from be- + fetian (“to fetch”). Compare Dutch befieten (“to become”).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bitaną (“to bite”).
