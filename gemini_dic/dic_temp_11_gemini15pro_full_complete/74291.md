fracted:/ˈfræktɪd/| adj.|破碎的，分裂的|Broken or split into fragments, either literally or metaphorically.  Describes something that is not whole, either physically or emotionally.|The mirror lay on the floor, fractured into a thousand pieces.  镜子碎成一千片，散落在地上。|The party was deeply fractured by internal disagreements. 该党因内部意见分歧而严重分裂。|近义词：broken, fragmented, splintered, shattered, divided|反义词：whole, united, intact|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *fractus*, past participle of *frangere* "to break."
