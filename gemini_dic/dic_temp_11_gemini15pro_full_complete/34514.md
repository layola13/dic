词源:  "Clean" comes from Old English *clæne, from Proto-Germanic *klainą (“clean, pure”). "Out" comes from Old English ūt, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *ūt (“out”).  The combination "cleaned out" developed the figurative sense of being emptied or depleted.
