concordance:/kənˈkɔːrdəns/| n.|一致，和谐， concord, agreement, harmony|The concordance between the two reports suggests they are based on the same data.  两份报告的一致性表明它们基于相同的数据。|例句：There is a general concordance of opinion on this matter. 在这个问题上，意见普遍一致。|近义词：agreement, harmony, unity, accord|反义词：discord, disagreement, conflict|noun
