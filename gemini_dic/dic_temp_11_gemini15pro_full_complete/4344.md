akinesia: /ˌeɪkaɪˈniːʒə/ /ˌeɪkaɪˈniːziə/ | n. | 运动不能，运动迟缓 | Loss or impairment of the power of voluntary movement. |  Parkinson's disease can cause akinesia, making it difficult for sufferers to initiate movement.  帕金森病会导致运动不能，使患者难以开始运动。

The patient exhibited akinesia after the stroke, struggling to even lift a cup.  中风后病人表现出运动不能，即使是举起杯子也很困难。 | 近义词: hypokinesia, bradykinesia | 反义词: hyperkinesia | 词性: noun
