occasion: /əˈkeɪʒən/ | vt. | 引起，造成 | to cause something to happen | His rude remarks occasioned a quarrel.  他的粗鲁言论引发了一场争吵。 | synonyms: cause, create, produce, bring about, give rise to, prompt | antonyms: prevent, avert, stop | verb
