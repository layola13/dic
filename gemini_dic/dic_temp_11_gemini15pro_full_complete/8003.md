anthophyllite:/ænˈθɒfɪlaɪt/| n. |直闪石|A metamorphic mineral, a variety of amphibole, usually occurring in fibrous or columnar masses.|Anthophyllite is commonly found in metamorphic rocks. 直闪石常见于变质岩中。|
Anthophyllite is a key indicator mineral for certain metamorphic facies. 直闪石是某些变质相的关键指示矿物。|近义词：amphibole, gedrite|反义词: None|词性:noun
