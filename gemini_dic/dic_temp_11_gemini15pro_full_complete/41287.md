cordin:/ˈkɔːrdɪn/ | n. |  Cord, rope, string (often dialectal or archaic) | A thick string or thin rope. | The sailor used a cordin to tie the boat to the dock. 水手用绳子把船拴在码头上。 | 近义词: rope, string, cord, twine | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Old French "cordon" (string, cord), from Latin "chorda" (string of a musical instrument, catgut), from Greek "chordē" (gut, string).
