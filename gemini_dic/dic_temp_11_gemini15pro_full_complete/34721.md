clickbait:/ˈklɪkbeɪt/
n.
点击诱饵；标题党
content whose main purpose is to attract attention and encourage visitors to click on a link to a particular web page.
The article title was pure clickbait, promising a shocking secret revealed. 这篇文章的标题纯粹是点击诱饵，承诺会揭露一个令人震惊的秘密。
近义词：sensationalism, linkbait
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider:  factual reporting, objective journalism)
词性: noun


clickbait:/ˈklɪkbeɪt/
adj.
标题党的；诱导点击的
designed to make people click on a link, especially when the link leads to content of dubious value or interest.
The website is full of clickbait articles designed to generate ad revenue.  这个网站充满了旨在产生广告收入的标题党文章。
近义词：sensationalist, attention-grabbing
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider:  factual, objective)
词性: adjective
