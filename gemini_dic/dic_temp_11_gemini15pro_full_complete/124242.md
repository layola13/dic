mossiest:/ˈmɔːsiɪst/ | adj. | 生满苔藓的；布满青苔的 | Covered with moss, especially to a greater degree than something else. | The mossiest rock I've ever seen was in a dark, damp corner of the forest. 我见过的苔藓最多的岩石是在森林中一个黑暗潮湿的角落里。| 近义词: moss-covered, mossy | 反义词: bare, clean | 词性: 形容词

词源: From moss + -y + -est.  "Moss" comes from Old English *mos, from Proto-Germanic *mosa, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *meus- "moist."
