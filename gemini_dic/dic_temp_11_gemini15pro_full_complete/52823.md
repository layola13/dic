diluter:/daɪˈluːtər/ | n. |稀释剂；稀释器|A substance used to dilute something, typically a solvent.  A device used to dilute something, especially a gas or vapour.|The chemist added a diluter to the concentrated solution. 化学家在浓缩溶液中加入了稀释剂。| synonyms: thinner, weakening agent | antonyms: concentrator, thickener | noun

dilute:/daɪˈluːt/ | v. |稀释；冲淡；削弱|Make (a liquid) thinner or weaker by adding water or another solvent. To make something weaker or less effective.|She diluted the juice with water. 她用水稀释了果汁。| synonyms: weaken, thin, reduce | antonyms: concentrate, strengthen, intensify | verb
