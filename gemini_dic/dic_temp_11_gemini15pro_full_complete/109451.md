lepers:/ˈlɛpərz/| n.|麻风病人；受蔑视的人|A person with leprosy, a chronic infectious disease that mainly affects the skin, nerves, limbs, eyes, lining of the nose, and upper respiratory tract.  Figuratively, it can refer to someone who is ostracized or marginalized.|例句：In the Bible, Jesus healed lepers. 圣经中，耶稣治愈了麻风病人。

例句：They were treated as lepers by the community. 他们被社区视为麻风病人一样对待。|近义词：outcasts, pariahs, untouchables|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) accepted, included|词性:Noun
