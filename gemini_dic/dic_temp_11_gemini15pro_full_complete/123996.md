morningless:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋləs/| adj.|没有早晨的，缺乏早晨的|Lacking a morning; without a morning.  This word describes a situation where there is no discernible morning period, or a state of being without the usual experience of a morning.  It could be metaphorical, referring to a feeling of disorientation or a lack of a fresh start.|例句：The long polar night felt morningless. 漫长的极夜感觉没有早晨。

例句：After working the night shift, I felt utterly morningless.  上完夜班后，我感觉完全没有早晨的感觉。|近义词：bleak, dreary, dark, gloomy|反义词：bright, sunny, cheerful|词性:形容词
