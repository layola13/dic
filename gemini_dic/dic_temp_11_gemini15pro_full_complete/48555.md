defame:/dɪˈfeɪm/| vt.|诽谤，中伤|To harm someone's reputation by saying or writing bad things about them that are not true.|The article defamed the actor by accusing him of drug use. 这篇文章诽谤了这位演员，指控他吸毒。|近义词：slander, libel, malign, disparage|反义词：praise, commend, extol|verb

defamed:/dɪˈfeɪmd/| v. (past tense & past participle of defame) |被诽谤的，被中伤的 (作为形容词使用时通常用于被动语态)|Having one's reputation harmed by false statements.|The defamed politician sued the newspaper for damages.  被诽谤的政治家起诉了这家报纸，要求赔偿损失。|近义词：slandered, libeled, maligned|反义词：praised, commended|verb, adjective (used as past participle)
