louder: /ˈlaʊdər/ (US), /ˈlaʊdə(r)/ (UK) | adj., adv. | 更大声地，更响亮地 | comparative form of "loud," meaning producing or capable of producing much noise.  It can be used as both an adjective and an adverb. | Please speak louder, I can't hear you. 请大声点说话，我听不见你。|  Turn the music up louder. 把音乐开大声点。| synonyms: more loudly, noisier, stronger | antonyms: quieter, softer | adjective, adverb
