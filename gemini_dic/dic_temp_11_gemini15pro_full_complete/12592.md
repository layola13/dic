assagai:/ˈæsəɡaɪ/ /ˈæsəɡaɪ/| n. |投矛，短矛|A light spear or javelin, typically having a thin, iron blade, used chiefly by Southern African peoples.|The warrior hurled the assagai with deadly accuracy.  战士以惊人的准确度投掷了投矛。|近义词：spear, javelin, lance|反义词:shield, armor|词性:noun
Etymology: From the Berber *zaghāya via Arabic *azzāḡaya and Portuguese azagaia.
