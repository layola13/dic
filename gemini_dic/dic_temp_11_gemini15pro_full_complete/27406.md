cameralistic: /ˌkæmərəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
财政学的；国库的
Relating to the administration of public finances; relating to a system of public finance administration in which the state is regarded as the supreme landlord and the revenue system is a form of estate management.
The government adopted a cameralistic approach to budgeting. 政府采用了财政学的预算方法。
The cameralistic system treated the state as a large estate. 财政制度将国家视为一个大型庄园。
近义词: fiscal, financial
反义词: None


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *kameralistisch*, from *Kameralwissenschaft* "science of public finance," from Late Latin *camera* "chamber, treasury." Ultimately from Greek *kamara* "vaulted chamber."
