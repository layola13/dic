matchwood:/ˈmætʃˌwʊd/| n.|火柴木；易碎的木头；碎片|Small pieces of wood, especially those used for lighting fires; wood that is so dry or rotten that it breaks easily. |The old chair crumbled into matchwood.  这把旧椅子碎成了火柴木。|Synonyms: kindling, tinder, splinters|Antonyms: hardwood, solid wood|noun
