antimysticisms: /ˌæntiˈmɪstɪˌsɪzəmz/ | n. | 反神秘主义 | Doctrines or practices opposed to mysticism.  The rejection of mystical beliefs or practices. |  His antimysticisms led him to reject any explanation that couldn't be empirically verified. 他的反神秘主义让他拒绝任何无法用经验验证的解释。|  The scientist's antimysticisms were evident in his insistence on data-driven conclusions.  这位科学家的反神秘主义体现在他坚持以数据驱动的结论。|近义词: rationalism, skepticism | 反义词: mysticism, spiritualism | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + mysticism.  "Anti-" is a prefix of Greek origin meaning "against" or "opposite of." "Mysticism" comes from the Greek "myein," meaning "to close" (especially the eyes or mouth), referring to the secretive nature of mystical initiation rites.
