loaning:/ˈloʊnɪŋ/| v.| 借出，贷出|The act of lending something to someone.  It implies a temporary transfer of possession with the expectation of return.|例句：She is loaning him her car for the weekend. 她周末把车借给他。

例句：They were loaning money to small businesses. 他们向小企业提供贷款。|近义词：lending, advancing|反义词：borrowing|词性: 动名词 (gerund)

注意事项:

* "Loaning" 虽然是动名词形式，但更常作为动词使用，尤其是在美式英语中。英式英语更倾向于使用 "lending"。
*  虽然有borrowing作为反义词，但更精确的反义动作取决于上下文。例如，loaning的反义词可以是 borrowing,  receiving a loan, or taking a loan.
