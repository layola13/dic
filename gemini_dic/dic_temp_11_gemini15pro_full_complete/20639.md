blackmailers:/ˈblækmeɪlərz/
n.
勒索者；敲诈者
People who extort money or other favors from someone by threatening to reveal embarrassing or damaging information about them.
The blackmailers threatened to release the photos unless they were paid a large sum of money. 勒索者威胁说，如果不支付一大笔钱，他们就会公布这些照片。
extortionists, racketeers
victims, targets
noun
