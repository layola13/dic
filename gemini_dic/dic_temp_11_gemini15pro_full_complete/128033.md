n.|书呆子气；怪癖；痴迷|The extent to which someone is a nerd.  It encompasses a passion for intellectual pursuits, often combined with social awkwardness or eccentricity.|His nerdiness was endearing. 他的书呆子气很可爱。|近义词: geekiness, dorkiness|反义词: worldliness, sophistication|词性:名词

词源:
From nerd + -iness.  "Nerd" likely originated in the 1950s, possibly from Dr. Seuss's book "If I Ran the Zoo" (1950), which features a creature called a "nerd." The suffix "-iness" forms nouns indicating a state or quality.
