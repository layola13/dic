lozenged:/ˈlɒzɪndʒd/ /ˈlɑːzɪndʒd/
adjective
菱形的；刻成菱形图案的；似菱形的
shaped like a lozenge; decorated with a lozenge pattern
The window was lozenged with small panes of glass. 窗户上镶嵌着菱形的小玻璃窗格。

近义词: diamond-shaped, rhombic, rhomboidal
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) shapeless, amorphous
词性: adjective
