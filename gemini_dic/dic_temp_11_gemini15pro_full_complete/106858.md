labours:/ˈleɪbərz/ (British) /ˈleɪbərz/ (American)| n. v.| 劳动，劳工，分娩| Work, especially hard physical work. The process of childbirth.|

例句：
The labourers worked tirelessly in the fields.  工人们不知疲倦地在田里劳作。

The fruits of their labours were evident in the bountiful harvest.  他们辛勤劳作的成果在丰收中显而易见。

近义词：work, toil, effort, exertion, childbirth, delivery
反义词：rest, leisure, idleness

词性: noun (plural), verb (third person singular present)

词源: From Middle English *labouren*, from Old French *labourer*, from Latin *laborāre* (“to work, toil, exert oneself”), from *labor* (“labor, toil, hardship”).
