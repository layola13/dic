ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hwæt, nominative and accusative neuter singular of hwā (“who”), from Proto-Germanic *hwat, from Proto-Indo-European *kʷód. Cognate with Scots what, West Frisian wat, Dutch wat, German was, Danish hvad, Swedish vad, Icelandic hvað.
