noncognizance:/nɒnˈkɒɡnɪzəns/ /nɑːnˈkɑːɡnɪzəns/| n.|不知情；不认知；不承认|Lack of awareness or acknowledgment; failure to take notice of something.|The judge expressed noncognizance of the previous case.|近义词：ignorance, unawareness, disregard|反义词: awareness, acknowledgment, recognition|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + cognizance.  Cognizance comes from Old French *conoissance, from conoissant "knowing," present participle of conoistre "to know," from Latin cognoscere "to get to know, learn," from com- "together" + gnoscere "to know."
