inconclusiveness: /ˌɪnkənˈkluːsɪvnɪs/
| n. | 不确定性；无结果；无定论 | The inconclusiveness of the meeting frustrated everyone. 会议没有结果，每个人都很沮丧。|  The report highlighted the inconclusiveness of the data. 报告强调了数据的无定论性。 | 近义词：indeterminacy, ambiguity, uncertainty | 反义词：conclusiveness, certainty, definiteness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From inconclusive + -ness.  Inconclusive comes from in- (not) + conclusive (leading to a definite result). Conclusive derives from the Latin concludere (to conclude).
