destabilized: /diːˈsteɪbəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 不稳定的 | made less stable or safe | The country's political system is destabilized. 这个国家的政治体系不稳定。| synonyms: unstable, insecure, precarious | antonyms: stable, secure, firm | adjective
