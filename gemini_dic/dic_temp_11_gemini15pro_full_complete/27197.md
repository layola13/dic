callithump:/kəˈlɪθʌmp/ | n. |喧闹的游行；嘈杂的音乐；喧闹的庆祝 | A boisterous parade or noisy celebration, often with improvised instruments and costumes, typically intended as a mock serenade or charivari. |  The students staged a callithump after their team won the championship. 学生们在他们的球队赢得冠军后举行了一场喧闹的庆祝游行。 | Synonyms: charivari, shivaree, hullabaloo | Antonyms: silence, quiet | noun

callithump:/kəˈlɪθʌmp/ | v. |举行喧闹的游行；发出嘈杂的声音 | To participate in or create a callithump. | They callithumped through the streets after the game. 比赛结束后，他们在街上喧闹地游行庆祝。 | Synonyms:  carouse, revel | Antonyms:  be quiet, be silent | verb
