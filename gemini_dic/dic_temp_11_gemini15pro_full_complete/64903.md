essentialness:/ɪˌsɛnʃəlˈnɛs/| n.|必要性，本质，重要性|The essentialness of clean water for survival cannot be overstated.  清洁水对于生存的必要性怎么强调都不为过。|近义词：importance, necessity, cruciality, significance|反义词：insignificance, unimportance, triviality|词性:noun
词源: From essential + -ness.  Essential comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin essentialis, from essentia ‘being’, from esse ‘to be’.
