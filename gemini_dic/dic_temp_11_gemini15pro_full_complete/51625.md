developmentalism:/dɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntəlɪzəm/ | n. | 发展主义 | The idea that societies progress through defined stages of economic growth and development. |  Many economists criticized the country's adherence to strict developmentalism. 许多经济学家批评该国坚持严格的发展主义。

Developmentalism has led to significant industrial growth in some countries. 发展主义导致一些国家显著的工业增长。


| 近义词: modernism, progressivism, industrialism | 反义词: stagnation, traditionalism | noun
