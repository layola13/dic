demonlike:/ˈdiːmənlaɪk/| adj.|像恶魔的，恶魔般的|Resembling a demon in appearance or behavior; fiendish, devilish.|Her demonlike rage frightened everyone in the room. 她那恶魔般的愤怒吓坏了房间里的所有人。|近义词：diabolic, demonic, satanic, infernal, hellish|反义词：angelic, divine, godly|词性:形容词
Etymology: demon + like.  "Demon" comes from the Late Latin *daemon* and ultimately from Greek *daimōn*, meaning "divinity, spirit."  "Like" comes from Old English *gelīc*.
