nonobjection:/ˌnɒnəbˈdʒekʃən/ | n. | 不反对; 无异议 |  The absence of an objection;  acceptance or acquiescence.  It implies passive agreement rather than enthusiastic support. | We proceeded with the plan, given the board's nonobjection.  董事会没有反对，我们就继续执行了这个计划。 | The company's nonobjection to the merger allowed it to proceed.  公司对合并没有异议，使得合并得以进行。 | 近义词: acceptance, acquiescence, approval, consent | 反义词: objection, opposition, disapproval, dissent | 词性: noun

词源:  Comes from "non-" (not) + "objection" (an expression or feeling of disapproval or opposition).  "Objection" comes from the Latin "obiectus," past participle of "obicere" (to throw against), from "ob-" (against) + "iacere" (to throw).
