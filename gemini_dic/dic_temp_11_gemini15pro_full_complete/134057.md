nonigneous:/ˌnɒnˈɪɡniəs/
adjective
非火成的
Not formed by igneous processes; sedimentary or metamorphic.
Limestone is a nonigneous rock. 石灰岩是一种非火成岩。
The geologist studied the nonigneous formations in the valley. 地质学家研究了山谷中的非火成岩地层。
Synonyms: sedimentary, metamorphic
Antonyms: igneous
词性: adjective
