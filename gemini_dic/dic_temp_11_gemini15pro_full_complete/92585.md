ia:/ɪə/| n.|状态，病情|A suffix used to form abstract nouns, often denoting a pathological condition.|例句：Insomnia is a common sleep disorder. 失眠是一种常见的睡眠障碍。|近义词：condition, state|反义词:health|noun

ia:/ɪə/| n.|地区，领域|A suffix used to form nouns denoting regions or areas of study.|例句：The Amazonia is a vast rainforest. 亚马逊是一片广袤的雨林。|近义词：region, area|反义词: |noun
