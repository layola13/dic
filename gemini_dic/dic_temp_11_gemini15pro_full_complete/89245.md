homin:/ˈhɒmɪn/ | n. | 人；人类 |A human being, especially a member of the family Hominidae (which includes humans, chimpanzees, gorillas, and orangutans).  It is a shortened and informal form of "hominid". | Early homins used simple tools. 早期人类使用简单的工具。| 近义词: hominid, human | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps animal) | noun
