overindebtedness:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈdɛtɪdnəs/
Noun
过度负债
A state of having more debt than one can reasonably be expected to manage or repay.
The farm was sold because of the family's overindebtedness. 由于家庭过度负债，农场被卖掉了。
Synonyms: excessive debt, insolvency, debt overload
Antonyms: solvency, financial stability, affluence
ETYMOLOGY->
over- (prefix) + indebted + -ness (suffix)
"over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much"
"indebted" means owing money
"-ness" forms a noun from an adjective, indicating a state or condition
