concisely: /kənˈsaɪsli/ /kənˈsaɪsli/ | adv. | 简洁地，简要地 | In a brief and to-the-point manner; without unnecessary words or details.  | She explained the complex issue concisely. 她简洁地解释了这个复杂的问题。 | He concisely summarized the main points of the meeting.  他简要地总结了会议的要点。 | 近义词: briefly, succinctly, tersely | 反义词: verbosely, lengthily, at length | 词性: 副词

词源: From Latin concīsus, past participle of concīdere "to cut down, cut short," from com- "together" (see com-) + caedere "to cut" (see -cide).
