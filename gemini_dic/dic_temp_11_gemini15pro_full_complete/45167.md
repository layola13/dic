cuesta:/ˈkwɛstə/ | n. | 斜面， cuesta | A cuesta is a hill or ridge with a gentle slope on one side and a steep slope on the other.  |  We hiked up the steep side of the cuesta. 我们沿着斜坡的陡峭一面向上爬。 | The cuesta offered stunning views of the valley below.  这个斜坡提供了下面山谷的壮丽景色。 |  slope, escarpment | cliff, precipice | noun
