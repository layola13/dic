dystopic:/dɪˈstɒpɪk/ /dɪˈstɑːpɪk/
adjective
反乌托邦的；反面乌托邦的
Relating to or denoting a dystopia.  A dystopia is an imagined state or society in which there is great suffering or injustice, typically one that is totalitarian or post-apocalyptic.
Example: The movie portrays a dystopic future where the environment is ruined and people live under a totalitarian regime.  这部电影描绘了一个反乌托邦的未来，环境被破坏，人们生活在极权统治之下。

Example:  The novel presents a dystopic vision of a society controlled by technology. 这部小说展现了一个被科技控制的社会，一个反乌托邦的景象。


近义词: nightmarish, apocalyptic, Orwellian
反义词: utopian, idyllic, ideal
词性: adjective
