microprocessors:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌprɑːsesərz/| n. |微处理器|A microprocessor is a central processing unit (CPU) contained on a single integrated circuit (IC) chip. It performs the arithmetic, logic, and control functions of a computer. |Modern computers rely heavily on microprocessors to execute instructions. 现代计算机严重依赖微处理器来执行指令。

Microprocessors are the brains of most electronic devices. 微处理器是大多数电子设备的大脑。|近义词：CPU, central processing unit|反义词: N/A|Noun
