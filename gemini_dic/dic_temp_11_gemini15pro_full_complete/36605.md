/kəˈlekts/ | n. |收藏品；收藏家|  Belongings, especially when they are rare or valuable.  "collect's" is usually seen as a shortening of "collect's item" or similar. | This is a rare stamp, a true collect's item.  这是一枚稀有的邮票，一件真正的收藏品。| synonyms: collection, accumulation | antonyms: disposal, discarding | noun

/kəˈlekts/ | adj. (attributive only) |收藏家的；收藏的| Relating to or suitable for a collector.|"collect's" used attributively usually implies a high value or rarity designed for collectors. | He boasts a collect's edition of the first Superman comic. 他收藏了第一版超人漫画的收藏版，并以此为荣。 | synonyms: collectible | antonyms: common, ordinary | adjective
