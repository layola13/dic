careful:/ˈkɛərfəl/| adj.|小心的，仔细的，谨慎的|Making sure of avoiding potential danger or harm; cautious.|例句：Be careful when crossing the road. 过马路时要小心。|近义词：cautious, wary, attentive, prudent|反义词：careless, reckless, negligent|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *`carful`, from Old English `cearful` ("full of care, anxious, sorrowful"), equivalent to `care` + `-ful`.
