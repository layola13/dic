hancock: /ˈhænˌkɑːk/ | n. | 签名, 约翰·汉考克 (美国独立宣言的签署者之一) | A person's signature; specifically, a bold and flamboyant signature.  It alludes to John Hancock, whose large signature on the Declaration of Independence is famous. | He signed the document with a flourish, a true hancock.  他潇洒地在文件上签了名，真是个漂亮的签名。 | 近义词: signature, autograph | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a signature itself, perhaps forgery as an opposite concept) | 词性: noun
