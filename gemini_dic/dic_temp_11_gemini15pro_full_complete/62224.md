encrinoidea:/ˌɛŋkrɪˈnɔɪdiə/ | n. |海百合纲|A class of marine animals, in the phylum Echinodermata, commonly known as sea lilies and feather stars.  They are characterized by a cup-shaped body with branching arms. | The encrinoidea are an ancient group, with fossils dating back millions of years.| 近义词:sea lilies, feather stars | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἐγ- (en-) + κρίνον (krínon, “lily”) + -oidea.
