humboldt:/ˈhʌmbəʊlt/ (US) /ˈhʌmbɒlt/ (UK)| n. adj.|洪堡;洪堡的|Referring to Alexander von Humboldt, a Prussian geographer, naturalist, explorer, and influential proponent of Romantic philosophy and science.  It can also refer to places, institutions, and things named after him.|例句：The Humboldt Current is a cold, low-salinity ocean current that flows north along the west coast of South America. 洪堡洋流是一股沿着南美洲西海岸向北流动的寒冷低盐度洋流。

例句：He studied at the Humboldt University of Berlin. 他在柏林洪堡大学学习。|近义词：(depending on context - for the person) explorer, scientist, naturalist, polymath; (for the current) Peruvian Current|反义词: N/A|词性:Noun, Adjective
