asiphonida:/eɪˈsaɪfənɪdə/
n.
Asiphonida
a taxonomic order of marine bivalve molluscs characterized by the absence of a siphon.  This group includes windowpane oysters and scallops.
Asiphonida are a diverse group of bivalves found in various marine habitats.
近义词:  Pteriomorphia (a larger class containing Asiphonida)
反义词:  Siphonida (bivalves with siphons)
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἀ- (a-, 'without') + σίφων (síphōn, 'tube, siphon') + -ida (a taxonomic suffix denoting an order).
