hoazin:/ˈhoʊɑːtsɪn/ | n. |麝雉|A South American bird (Opisthocomus hoazin) noted for the clawed wings of its young and for its musky odor.  It is the only member of its order (Opisthocomiformes). |The hoazin is a unique bird found in the Amazon basin. 麝雉是一种在亚马逊盆地发现的独特鸟类。| The hoazin's chicks have claws on their wings, which they use for climbing. 麝雉的幼鸟翅膀上有爪子，用来攀爬。 | synonyms: stinkbird, Canje pheasant | antonyms: N/A | noun
