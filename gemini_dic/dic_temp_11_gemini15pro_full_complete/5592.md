alterant:/ˈɔːltərənt/| n. adj.|改变剂，变化的|Something that alters; a substance that alters the function of the body or mind, typically a drug.|The doctor prescribed an alterant to help stabilize his mood. 医生开了一种改变剂来帮助稳定他的情绪。|近义词:modifying, changing, transformative|反义词:stabilizing, fixing, unchanging|词性:名词, 形容词
词源: Late 18th century: from Latin alterant- ‘changing’, from the verb alterare, from alter ‘other’.
