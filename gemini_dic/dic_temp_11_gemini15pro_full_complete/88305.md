HIPAA: /ˈhɪpə/ (American English) | n. | 健康保险流通与责任法案 (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act) |  A US law designed to provide privacy standards to protect patients' medical records and other health information provided to health plans, doctors, hospitals and other health care providers. | Example:  The hospital was fined for violating HIPAA regulations.  这家医院因违反 HIPAA 法规而被罚款。| Synonym:  health information privacy law | Antonym: N/A | noun

HIPAA: /ˈhɪpə/ (American English) | abbreviation | 健康保险流通与责任法案 (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act) |  A shortened form of the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act. | Example:  All employees must undergo HIPAA training. 所有员工都必须接受 HIPAA 培训。 | Synonym: N/A | Antonym: N/A | abbreviation
