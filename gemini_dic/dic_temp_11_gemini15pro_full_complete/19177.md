bewildering:/bɪˈwɪldərɪŋ/| adj.|令人迷惑的，令人困惑的|Extremely confusing or perplexing.|The city was a bewildering maze of narrow streets. 这座城市是一个令人迷惑的狭窄街道的迷宫。|Synonyms: confusing, perplexing, baffling, mystifying, disorienting|Antonyms: clear, understandable, lucid|adjective

bewilder:/bɪˈwɪldər/| vt.|使迷惑，使困惑|To confuse or puzzle someone greatly.|The sudden change in policy bewildered everyone. 政策的突然改变使每个人都感到困惑。|Synonyms: confuse, puzzle, baffle, mystify, disorient|Antonyms: clarify, enlighten, explain|verb
