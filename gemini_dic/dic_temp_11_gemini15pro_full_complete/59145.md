dynamometrical:/ˌdaɪnəmɒˈmetrɪkl/ | adj. | 测力计的；测力的 | Relating to or measured by a dynamometer (an instrument for measuring force, torque, or power). |  This dynamometrical test will determine the engine's horsepower.  这个测力测试将确定发动机的马力。|  The dynamometrical readings showed a significant increase in power output. 测力计读数显示功率输出显着增加。 |近义词: force-measuring, power-measuring | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Greek "dynamis" (power) + "metron" (measure) + "-ical" (adjectival suffix).
