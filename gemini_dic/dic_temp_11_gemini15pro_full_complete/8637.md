anticompetitiveness:/ˌæntiˌkəmˈpɛtɪtɪvnəs/
n.|反竞争性，反竞争行为|The state of being anticompetitive; actions or practices that hinder competition in a market.  It often involves practices that limit consumer choice or inflate prices.|The company was accused of anticompetitiveness after it bought out its main rival.|synonyms:  monopolistic behavior, unfair competition, restrictive practices|antonyms:  fair competition, open market, competitiveness
