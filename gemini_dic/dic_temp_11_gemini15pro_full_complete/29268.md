cassonade:/ˌkæsəˈnɑːd/ /ˌkæsəˈneɪd/| n.|红糖，卡索那德糖|A coarse, brown, unrefined cane sugar.  A type of sugar originally from France, similar to turbinado sugar. It is less refined than white sugar and has a slightly moist texture and a molasses flavor.|I used cassonade in the cookies for a richer flavor. 我在饼干里用了红糖，味道更浓郁。|近义词：turbinado sugar, brown sugar, raw sugar|反义词: white sugar, refined sugar|词性:noun
