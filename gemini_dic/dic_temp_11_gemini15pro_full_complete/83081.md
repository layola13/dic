guru-ships:/ˈɡʊruːʃɪps/
n.
导师身份；导师地位；精神领袖地位
The state or position of being a guru; the collective body of gurus.
Many young entrepreneurs seek guru-ships in the tech industry. 许多年轻的企业家在科技行业寻求导师身份。
The conference brought together guru-ships from various fields.  这次会议汇集了来自各个领域的专家。
近义词: mentorships, leadership, guidance
反义词: apprenticeship, followership, discipleship
词性: 名词

词源:
From "guru" (from Hindi guru, from Sanskrit guru "heavy, weighty, venerable") + "-ship" (a suffix forming abstract nouns of state, condition, or quality).
