disestablishment's:/dɪsɪˈstæblɪʃmənts/
noun
解除建立关系；撤销教会特权
The act of disestablishing something, especially a church.  The noun form of "disestablish," referring to the ending of an official status or recognition.
The disestablishment's impact on the Church of England was profound. 解除教会特权对英国教会的影响是深远的。
The disestablishment's consequences were felt throughout the country. 解除教会特权的后果在全国各地都能感受到。
Synonym: abolition, termination, dissolution
Antonym: establishment, founding, institution
词性: 名词
