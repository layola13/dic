brigandism:/ˈbrɪɡəndɪzəm/
n.
土匪行为；盗匪活动；抢劫
The act of being a brigand; highway robbery; banditry.
Brigandism was rampant in the mountains, making travel dangerous.  山区盗匪猖獗，出行危险。
banditry, robbery, outlawry, highway robbery, pillage
