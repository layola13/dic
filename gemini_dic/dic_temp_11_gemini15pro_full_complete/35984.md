codes:/koʊdz/ | n. | 代码; 密码; 编码; 法典 |  A system of words, letters, figures, or symbols used to represent something. |  The programmer wrote efficient codes for the software. 程序员为软件编写了高效的代码。| synonyms: ciphers, symbols, signals | antonyms: plain text, clear message | noun


/koʊdz/ | v. | 编码 | To convert (information) into a particular form or system of symbols, especially for the purposes of secrecy or security. | The message was coded to prevent interception.  这条消息被加密以防止被截获。 | synonyms: encrypt, encode, cipher | antonyms: decode, decipher | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *code*, from Late Latin *codex*, meaning "book, code of law."
