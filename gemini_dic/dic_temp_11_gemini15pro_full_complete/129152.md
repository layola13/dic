no-love-lost:/noʊ lʌv lɒst/ (British) /noʊ lʌv lɔːst/ (American)| idiom |没有好感；互相敌视；关系恶劣|A situation in which two people or groups dislike each other.  It implies a history of animosity or rivalry. |There's no love lost between the two rival teams. 这两支竞争队伍之间没有好感。| There is no love lost between them after their bitter divorce.  他们离婚后关系恶劣。|Synonyms: animosity, antipathy, bad blood, hostility, ill will | Antonyms: affection, amity, friendship, goodwill, rapport | idiom
