entangledness:/ɪnˈtæŋɡəldnəs/| n. |纠缠，缠绕，复杂性|The state of being entangled; intricacy or complexity.|The entangledness of the political situation made it difficult to find a solution. 政治局势的复杂性使得很难找到解决方案。|近义词：complexity, intricacy, involvement|反义词:simplicity, clarity|词性:名词
Etymology: From entangle + -ness.  "Entangle" comes from the Old French "entangler," meaning "to entrap in a net," from "en-" (in) + "tangler" (to entangle).
