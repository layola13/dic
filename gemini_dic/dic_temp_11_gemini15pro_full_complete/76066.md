furnishes:/ˈfɜːrnɪʃɪz/
verb
提供，供应；装备，布置
supplies (with furniture and fittings); provides; equips
The apartment is furnished with modern appliances. 这套公寓配备了现代化的电器。
The company furnishes its employees with laptops and cell phones.  这家公司为员工提供笔记本电脑和手机。
近义词: equip, supply, provide, outfit
反义词: strip, deprive
词性: verb
