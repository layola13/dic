boringnesses:/ˈbɔːrɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
无聊；乏味
The sheer boringnesses of the lecture made me want to fall asleep.
这场讲座的纯粹乏味让我昏昏欲睡。
Etymology: From boring + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Ultimately from bore (v.) + -ing (suffix forming present participles and adjectives) + -ness.
Synonyms: tediousness, monotony, dullness, vapidity
Antonyms: excitement, interest, fascination
