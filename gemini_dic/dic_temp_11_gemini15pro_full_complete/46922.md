darling:/ˈdɑːrlɪŋ/ /ˈdɑːrlɪŋ/| n. adj.|亲爱的，宠儿，心肝宝贝|A term of endearment, expressing affection. Can also be used ironically or condescendingly.|例句：He called her "darling" affectionately. 他亲切地叫她“亲爱的”。

例句：She's such a darling child. 她真是个可爱的孩子。|近义词：dear, sweetheart, honey, love, beloved|反义词：enemy, foe|词性:名词

darling:/ˈdɑːrlɪŋ/ /ˈdɑːrlɪŋ/| adj.|可爱的，讨人喜欢的|Pleasing, charming, or attractive.|例句：What a darling little cottage! 多么可爱的小屋！

例句：She wore a darling dress to the party. 她穿了一件可爱的连衣裙去参加聚会。|近义词：adorable, charming, sweet, lovely|反义词：unpleasant, disagreeable|词性:形容词
