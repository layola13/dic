ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "fetch," meaning "to go and bring back," with the suffix "-ing" used to create an adjective meaning "having the quality of fetching or attracting."  The verb "fetch" comes from Old English "feccan," related to German "fassen" (to grasp).
