azoic:/eɪˈzoʊɪk/ /əˈzoʊɪk/
adjective
无生命的，无生物的；含氮的
Relating to or denoting the geological eons preceding the earliest known fossils, representing a time when there was no life on earth/ containing nitrogen, especially in a combined state.
The geologist explained that the azoic rocks contained no fossils.  地质学家解释说，这些无生命的岩石中不含化石。
Nitrogen is an essential component of many azoic compounds. 氮是许多含氮化合物的重要组成部分。
近义词: lifeless, inanimate, abiotic
反义词: biotic, living
词性: adjective
