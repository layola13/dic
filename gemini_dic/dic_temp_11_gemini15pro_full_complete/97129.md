infiltrate:/ˈɪnfɪltreɪt/| v. |渗透，潜入|To enter or pass through something secretly or gradually, especially a territory or organization.|例句：The rebels infiltrated the government army. 叛军渗透了政府军。|例句：The company was infiltrated by spies. 公司被间谍渗透了。|近义词：penetrate, permeate, invade|反义词：exfiltrate, withdraw|词性：动词

infilter:/ˈɪnfɪltreɪt/| v. |(液体或气体)渗入，渗透|To pass into or through (something) by filtering or permeating.|例句：Rainwater infiltrated the soil. 雨水渗入了土壤。|例句：The gas infiltrated the building through cracks in the foundation.  气体通过地基的裂缝渗入了建筑物。|近义词：percolate, seep, permeate|反义词：drain, dry|词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- "into" + filter "to strain" (from Medieval Latin filtrare).
