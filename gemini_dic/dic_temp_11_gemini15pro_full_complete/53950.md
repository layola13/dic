discovers:/dɪˈskʌvərz/
| v. |发现，发觉| to find something unexpectedly or while looking or searching for something else|例句：Scientists discovered a new species of frog in the Amazon. 科学家在亚马逊发现了一种新的青蛙物种。|例句：She discovered her talent for painting at a young age. 她很小的时候就发现了自己的绘画天赋。|近义词：find, locate, unearth, detect, ascertain|反义词：conceal, hide, lose, overlook|词性:动词

词源:
From Old French *descovrir, from des- (“dis-”) + covrir (“to cover”). Equivalent to dis- + cover.
