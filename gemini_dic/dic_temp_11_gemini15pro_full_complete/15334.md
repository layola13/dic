backspace:/ˈbækspeɪs/ | n. |退格键，回退键|A key on a keyboard that moves the cursor one position to the left, deleting the character in that position.|She hit the backspace key to correct her typo. 她按了退格键来纠正她的打字错误。|synonyms: delete key, back key|antonyms:  N/A (functionally, the opposite might be 'enter' or any key that inputs characters)|noun
