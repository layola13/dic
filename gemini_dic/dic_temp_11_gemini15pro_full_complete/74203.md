fours:/fɔːrz/
n.
四；四个一组；四岁；（纸牌游戏）四张同点数的牌
The number four, or a group of four.
He rolled the dice and got two fours. 他掷骰子掷出了两个四。
Synonym: quartet, quadruplet
Antonym: one, two, three, five...(Context-dependent)
词性: 名词
