absolve:/əbˈzɒlv/ /əbˈsɑːlv/| v. |赦免，免除，宣告无罪|to free someone from guilt, blame, or responsibility |The priest absolved him of his sins.  神父赦免了他的罪过。|
absolve:/əbˈzɒlv/ /əbˈsɑːlv/| v. |免除，解除|to release someone from a duty or obligation|He was absolved from all further responsibility. 他被免除了一切进一步的责任。|
