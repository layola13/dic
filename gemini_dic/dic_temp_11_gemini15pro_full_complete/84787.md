handshaker:/ˈhændˌʃeɪkər/| n.|握手者；热衷于握手的人|A person who shakes hands, often excessively or enthusiastically.|He's a real handshaker, always greeting everyone with a firm grip. 他真是个热衷于握手的人，总是紧紧地握着每个人的手。|近义词：greeter, welcomer|反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps someone who avoids physical contact)|noun
