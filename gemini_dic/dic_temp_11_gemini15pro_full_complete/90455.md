huarache:/wəˈrɑːtʃi/ /wəˈrætʃi/
n.
凉鞋; 华拉契凉鞋
A sandal, typically made of woven leather thongs.
She wore a pair of huaraches on her feet. 她脚上穿着一双华拉契凉鞋。
synonyms: sandal, flip-flop, thong
antonyms: boot, shoe
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *huarache*, from  Purépecha *kwarachi*.
