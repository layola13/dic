inner:/ˈɪnər/| adj.|内部的，里面的；内心的，精神的|situated inside or further in; relating to the mind or spirit|例句：The inner lining of the coat is made of silk.  这件外套的内衬是丝绸做的。|例句：He struggled with his inner demons. 他与内心的恶魔作斗争。|近义词：internal, inside, interior|反义词：outer, external|词性:形容词

inner:/ˈɪnər/| n.|内部，里面|the inner or interior part|例句：The inner of the fruit was soft and juicy. 水果的内部柔软多汁。|例句：Only a few people know the inner workings of the organization. 只有少数人知道这个组织的内部运作。|近义词：interior, inside|反义词：exterior, outside|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *innerra, comparative of inne (“within, inside”), from Proto-Germanic *innaraz, from Proto-Indo-European *en (“in”). Cognate with Old Frisian innera, Old Saxon innara, Old High German innaro, Old Norse innri, Gothic 𐌹𐌽𐌽𐌰𐍂𐌰 (innara).
