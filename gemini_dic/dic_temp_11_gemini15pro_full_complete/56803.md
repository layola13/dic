dorgon:/ˈdɔːrɡən/| n.|多尔衮|A Manchu prince who was instrumental in the Manchu conquest of China and the establishment of the Qing dynasty.  He was the regent for the young Shunzhi Emperor.|Dorgon played a crucial role in the early Qing dynasty. 多尔衮在清朝初期扮演了至关重要的角色。|Synonyms: regent, prince, leader|Antonyms: follower, subordinate|noun
