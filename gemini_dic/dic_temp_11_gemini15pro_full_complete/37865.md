compile:/kəmˈpaɪl/ (US) /kəmˈpaɪl/ (UK)| v. |编译，汇编，编纂|To convert (a program) into machine language that a computer can read and execute; to gather together (texts, information, or data) into a single work or publication.|The programmer compiled the code into an executable file. 程序员将代码编译成可执行文件。|synonyms: assemble, build, synthesize|antonyms: decompile, disassemble|verb

compile:/kəmˈpaɪl/ (US) /kəmˈpaɪl/ (UK)| n. |编译，汇编|The act of compiling something, especially a program or a book.|The compile of the new dictionary took several years. 新词典的编纂花了数年时间。|synonyms: compilation, assembly|antonyms: decomposition, disassembly|noun
