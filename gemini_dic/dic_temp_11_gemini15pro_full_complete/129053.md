nitrifies:/ˈnaɪtrɪfaɪz/
verb
硝化；使硝化
converts (ammonia or another nitrogen compound) into nitrates or nitrites, especially by the action of bacteria.
Nitrifying bacteria nitrifies ammonia in the soil. 土壤中的硝化细菌将氨硝化。

reduces
