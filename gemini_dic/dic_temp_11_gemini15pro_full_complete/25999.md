bursty:/ˈbɜːrsti/| adj.|突发的，爆发性的，一阵阵的|Characterized by short periods of intense activity followed by periods of inactivity.  Something that happens in bursts or is characterized by bursts.|例句：The network experienced bursty traffic during peak hours. 网络在高峰时段经历了突发性流量。

例句：Bursty communication can make it difficult to maintain a consistent flow of information.  突发性的通信会使保持一致的信息流变得困难。|近义词：intermittent, sporadic, irregular, fitful, jerky|反义词：continuous, steady, consistent, regular, smooth|词性:形容词
