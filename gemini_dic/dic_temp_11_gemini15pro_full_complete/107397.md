laminarian:/ˌlæmɪˈnɛəriən/
n.|昆布，海带|Large brown seaweed, any of numerous kelps of the order Laminariales, usually having a large, flat, leathery thallus and found in colder oceans. |Kelp forests, dominated by laminarian species, provide habitat for a variety of marine life.  海带森林以昆布为主，为各种海洋生物提供栖息地。|
synonyms: kelp, seaweed, algae
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a type of organism)
noun

laminarian:/ˌlæmɪˈnɛəriən/
adj.|海带的，昆布的|Relating to or resembling laminaria; of or pertaining to the order Laminariales.|The laminarian zone is the area where these kelps thrive.  昆布区是这些海带繁盛的区域。|
synonyms: kelp, seaweed, algal
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a type of organism)
adjective
