linerless:/ˈlaɪnərlɪs/
adjective
无衬里的，无内衬的
Relating to or denoting a label that does not require a backing liner. Linerless labels are applied directly to the product or packaging.
Linerless labels are more sustainable because they reduce waste. 无衬里标签更具可持续性，因为它们减少了浪费。
Supermarkets are increasingly using linerless labeling systems. 超市越来越多地使用无衬里标签系统。
Near Synonyms: liner-free, backingless
Antonyms: lined, with liner
词性: adjective
