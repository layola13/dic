antlered:/ˈæntlərd/ | adj. | 有角的；生鹿角的 | Having antlers (the branched bony growths on the head of a deer, elk, moose, etc.) |  The antlered stag stood majestically in the clearing. 有角的雄鹿雄赳赳地站在空地上。| 近义词: horned | 反义词: antlerless | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From antler + -ed.  "Antler" comes from Old French "antoillier," ultimately from Latin "anteocularis," meaning "before the eye."
