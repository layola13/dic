outblunder:/ˌaʊtˈblʌndər/
verb
犯更大的错误；在失误方面超过
to surpass in blundering; to make more or bigger mistakes than
He outblundered his opponent in the chess match, making three critical errors that cost him the game. 他在象棋比赛中比对手犯了更大的错误，三个关键的失误让他输掉了比赛。

outblundering (present participle)
正在犯更大的错误

By outblundering the competition, the company managed to snatch defeat from the jaws of victory.  由于犯了比竞争对手更大的错误，该公司设法从胜利的边缘走向了失败。
outblunder (base form)
犯更大的错误
make bigger mistakes than
Politicians often try to outblunder each other in debates, hoping to discredit their opponents.  政客们经常试图在辩论中比对方犯更大的错误，希望以此败坏对手的名誉。
近义词:outdo in errors, surpass in mistakes, blunder more than
反义词:outsmart, outmaneuver

词源:
"out-" (prefix meaning "to surpass") + "blunder" (verb meaning "to make a stupid or careless mistake")
