epidemically:/ˌɛpɪˈdɛmɪkli/| adv.| 流行地，像瘟疫般地|In a manner characteristic of an epidemic; spreading rapidly and extensively.|The flu spread epidemically through the school. 流感在学校里迅速蔓延。|近义词：widely, rapidly, extensively|反义词:locally, sporadically|词性:副词

词源:
From epidemic + -ally.  Epidemic comes from the Greek epidēmios, meaning "prevalent among the people," from epi- "upon, among" + dēmos "people."
