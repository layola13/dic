overdiscreetly: /ˌoʊvər dɪˈskriːtli/ | adv. | 过分谨慎地，过分小心地 | Excessively discreet; too cautious or reserved in one's actions or speech.  Characterized by an overabundance of caution or reserve. | She overdiscreetly avoided mentioning his name, fearing it would cause a scene. 她过分谨慎地避免提及他的名字，担心这会引起一场风波。|  He overdiscreetly tiptoed around the sensitive subject. 他过分小心地回避了这个敏感的话题。 | Synonyms: overcautiously, overcarefully, too reservedly | Antonyms: indiscreetly, carelessly, openly, boldly | Adverb
