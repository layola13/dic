enaliosaurian:/ɛˌnælioʊˈsɔːriən/ |adj. n.| 蛇颈龙类的；蛇颈龙类动物 |Of or relating to the Enaliosauria, an extinct order of marine reptiles including plesiosaurs and ichthyosaurs.  |  Example: The enaliosaurian fossil was remarkably well-preserved. 这块蛇颈龙类的化石保存得非常好。|  Example:  Enaliosaurians thrived in the Mesozoic seas. 蛇颈龙类动物在中生代的海洋中繁盛一时。| 近义词:plesiosaur, ichthyosaur, marine reptile | 反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly terrestrial animal) | 词性: adjective, noun | 词源: From Greek *enalios* "marine" + *sauros* "lizard".
