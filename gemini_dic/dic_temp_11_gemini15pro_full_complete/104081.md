jump-aboard:/ˌdʒʌmp əˈbɔːrd/ | phrasal verb | 加入；登上；赶快行动 | To join in an activity, project, or trend enthusiastically, or to literally board a vehicle. |  例句：Many investors are jumping aboard the electric car bandwagon. 许多投资者正纷纷加入电动汽车的潮流。

例句：We jumped aboard the train just as it was about to leave. 我们在火车即将开动时跳了上去。 | 近义词：join in, participate, get involved, climb aboard, get on, embark | 反义词：opt out, withdraw, disembark, get off | 词性: 动词短语
