multielectrode:/ˌmʌltiɪˈlɛktrəʊd/ (US) /ˌmʌltiɪˈlɛktroʊd/| adj. n.|多电极的，多电极|Having or using multiple electrodes.  A device or system incorporating multiple electrodes.|The multielectrode array allowed for precise recording of neuronal activity. 多电极阵列允许精确记录神经元活动。|multichannel, multiple electrode|single electrode|adjective, noun
etymology->
multi- (prefix): from Latin multus "much, many"
electrode: from electr- + -ode.  Electr- is a combining form representing electricity, ultimately from Greek ēlektron "amber". -ode is a combining form denoting a path or electrode, from Greek hodos "way".
