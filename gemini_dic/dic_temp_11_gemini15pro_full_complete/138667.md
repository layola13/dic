nudism: /ˈnjuːdɪzəm/ (US), /ˈnjuːdɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |裸体主义|The practice of going without clothes, especially in a social setting where others are also naked, often for reasons of health, freedom, or self-expression.| Some people find nudism liberating while others find it uncomfortable.  一些人觉得裸体主义很解放，而另一些人觉得它不舒服。| 近义词:naturism| 反义词: clothedness,  prude|词性: noun

nudisms:  This word is not typically used in English.  The singular form, "nudism," is the standard way to refer to the practice.  While grammatically a plural form is possible, it is not commonly used and might sound odd to native speakers. It would suggest multiple instances or types of nudism.
