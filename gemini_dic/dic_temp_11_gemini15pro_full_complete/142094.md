orientalize: /ˌɔːriˈentəlaɪz/ | v. | 使东方化，使具有东方色彩 | To make something have characteristics or features that are considered typical of the cultures of East and Southeast Asia, often in a way that is stereotypical or romanticized. |  The author orientalizes the Middle East in his novels, portraying it as a mystical and exotic land.  作者在他的小说中将中东东方化，将其描绘成一个神秘而奇异的土地。 |  The movie orientalized Japanese culture by focusing on geishas and samurai. 这部电影通过聚焦于艺伎和武士而将日本文化东方化了。 |  exoticize, romanticize | Westernize | verb
