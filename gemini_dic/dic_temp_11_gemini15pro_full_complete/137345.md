nontaxables:/ˌnɒnˈtæksəblz/
noun
非应税项目；免税品
Items or income that are not subject to taxation.
Many retirement accounts hold nontaxables. 许多退休账户持有免税品。
The store specializes in nontaxables like food and medicine. 这家商店专门经营食品和药品等非应税商品。
synonyms: tax-exempt items, duty-free goods
antonyms: taxable items, dutiable goods
词性: noun (plural)
