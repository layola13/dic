bewailer:/bɪˈweɪlər/| n. | 哀悼者，恸哭者 |A person who wails or laments, especially loudly and publicly.  Someone who expresses great sadness or disappointment about something.|  The funeral was filled with bewailers, their cries echoing through the church.  葬礼上挤满了哀悼者，他们的哭声在教堂里回荡。| synonyms: mourner, griever, lamenter, weeper | antonyms: celebrant, rejoicer | noun
