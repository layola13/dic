graphiscope:/ˈɡræfɪskoʊp/
noun
图形镜; 录影机
An instrument for enlarging and projecting images of opaque objects, such as photographs or book pages.
The lecturer used a graphiscope to show the detailed illustrations in the old book. 演讲者使用图形镜展示了旧书中详细的插图。
Synonyms: opaque projector, episcope
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps: microscope)
n.
