graphed:/ɡræft/ | v. | 绘制成图表；用图表表示 | represented by a graph; plotted on a graph | The researchers graphed the results of the experiment. 研究人员将实验结果绘制成图表。 | plotted, charted, diagrammed |  | verb (past tense)
Etymology: From graph + -ed (past tense suffix). Ultimately from Greek *gráphō* (to write, draw).
