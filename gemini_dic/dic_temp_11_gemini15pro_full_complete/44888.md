cryogenic:/ˌkraɪəˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj.|低温的，低温学的|Relating to or involving very low temperatures, especially temperatures approaching absolute zero.|例句：Cryogenic freezing is used to preserve biological samples.  低温冷冻用于保存生物样本。

例句：Scientists are exploring cryogenic sleep as a means of long-distance space travel. 科学家们正在探索低温睡眠作为长途太空旅行的一种手段。|近义词：low-temperature, freezing|反义词：high-temperature, hot|词性：形容词

cryogenics:/ˌkraɪəˈdʒɛnɪks/| n.|低温学|The branch of physics that deals with the production and effects of very low temperatures.|例句：Advances in cryogenics have led to the development of new materials and technologies. 低温学的进步导致了新材料和新技术的开发。

例句：He specialized in cryogenics and its application in space exploration. 他专门研究低温学及其在太空探索中的应用。|近义词：low-temperature physics|反义词：high-temperature physics|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *kryos* (frost, ice) + *-genic* (producing).
