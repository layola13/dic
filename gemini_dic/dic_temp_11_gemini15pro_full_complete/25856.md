burinist: /ˈbɜːrɪnɪst/ | n. | 钻孔工，雕刻工 | A person who uses a burin, a tool for engraving or carving. |  The burinist carefully etched the intricate design onto the metal plate.  钻孔工小心翼翼地在金属板上刻蚀复杂的图案。| 近义词: engraver, carver, sculptor | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: Noun


Etymology: From French *buriniste*, from *burin* (burin, graver) + *-iste* (suffix forming agent nouns). Ultimately from Frankish *bor* (auger, drill).
