horizontalities: /ˌhɔːrɪzɒnˈtælɪtiz/ | n. | 水平性，水平状态；横向性；横向关系 | The state or quality of being horizontal; the arrangement or alignment of things along a horizontal plane; relationships or connections that exist on a horizontal level (e.g., between individuals or institutions of equal status). |  The architect emphasized the horizontalities of the prairie landscape in his design.  这位建筑师在他的设计中强调了草原景观的水平性。

The painting explored the horizontalities of human connection. 这幅画探索了人与人之间横向联系。 | synonyms: levelness, flatness, horizontals | antonyms: verticalities, perpendicularities | noun
