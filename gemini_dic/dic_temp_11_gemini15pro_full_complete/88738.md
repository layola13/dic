hoboy:/ˈhɔɪbɔɪ/ | int. | 好家伙；哎哟；哎呀 | An exclamation expressing surprise, dismay, or excitement. | Example: "Hoboy, that was a close call!" 好家伙，那真是千钧一发！ | Example: "Hoboy, look at all that food!" 哎哟，看看这么多食物！ | 近义词: wow, gee, gosh, oh boy, my goodness | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an interjection) | 词性: Interjection

(Hoboy is generally used standalone and doesn't form common phrases.  Here are some similar interjections in phrases)

My goodness, look at that! = 我的天哪，看看那个！
Gosh darn it! = 该死的！（Mild oath)
Good heavens, what a mess! = 天哪，真是一团糟！


注意事项:
* "Hoboy" is an informal interjection.
*  It can express a range of emotions, so context is important.
