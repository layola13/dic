huckstress:/ˈhʌkstrəs/ | n. | 女商贩；叫卖女；泼妇 | A woman who sells small items, often food, either in a market or by going from house to house. Can also refer to a loud, aggressive, or boisterous woman. |  She was a huckstress, selling her wares in a booming voice at the market. 她是一个女商贩，在市场上用洪亮的声音叫卖她的商品。 | synonym: vendor, hawker, peddler, shrew | antonym: buyer, customer, quiet person | noun

huckstress:/ˈhʌkstrəs/ | n. |  粗俗女人；泼妇 | A loud, aggressive, or boisterous woman. |  She behaved like a huckstress, shouting and swearing at everyone. 她的举止像个泼妇，对每个人又喊又骂。 | synonym: shrew, virago, termagant, fishwife | antonym: lady, gentlewoman, refined woman | noun
