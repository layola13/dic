overgrowth:/ˌoʊvərˈɡroʊθ/ | n. |过度生长，蔓生，疯长|Excessive growth, often of vegetation.| The garden suffered from overgrowth of weeds. 花园里杂草丛生。|近义词：overabundance, proliferation, excess|反义词:undergrowth, scarcity|词性:名词

overgrowth:/ˌoʊvərˈɡroʊθ/ | n. |过度增殖，增生|Excessive growth of tissue or cells in the body.|The doctor discovered an overgrowth of tissue in his lung. 医生在他的肺里发现了组织增生。|近义词：hyperplasia, hypertrophy|反义词:atrophy, hypoplasia|词性:名词
