earthiest:/ˈɜːrθiɪst/
adj.
最朴实的，最粗俗的，最世俗的
Resembling or suggestive of earth; crude and unrefined.
例句：He told the earthiest jokes I've ever heard. 他讲了我听过的最粗俗的笑话。

例句：Her earthiest comments shocked the refined guests. 她粗俗的评论震惊了那些有教养的客人。

近义词：earthy, coarse, vulgar, crude, unrefined, down-to-earth
反义词：refined, sophisticated, elegant, polished, cultured
词性: 形容词
