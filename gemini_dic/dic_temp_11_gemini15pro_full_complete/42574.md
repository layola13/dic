counterpicketed:/ˌkaʊntərˈpɪkɪtɪd/
verb
对抗性纠察，反纠察
To picket in opposition to another picket line.
The union members counterpicketed the company's headquarters after management hired replacement workers. 工会成员在管理层雇佣了替代工人后，到公司总部进行反纠察。
Synonyms: counter-protest, counter-demonstrate
Antonyms: support, endorse
verb

counterpicket:/ˌkaʊntərˈpɪkɪt/
noun
对抗性纠察，反纠察
A picket line established in opposition to another picket line.
The counterpicket was organized by a rival union. 对抗性纠察是由一个敌对的工会组织的。
Synonyms: counter-protest, counter-demonstration
Antonyms: support, endorsement
noun
