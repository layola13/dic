outroaring:/ˌaʊtˈrɔːrɪŋ/
adjective
胜过咆哮的，声音更大的
Surpassing in sound or vehemence; louder than a roar.
The outroaring thunder shook the house. 震耳欲聋的雷声震动了房子。
近义词:deafening, thunderous, resounding
反义词:quiet, silent
词性:adjective


词源:
"outroar" comes from "out-" (meaning surpassing or exceeding) + "roar" (a loud, deep sound).  It's a combination emphasizing a sound that is even louder than a roar.
