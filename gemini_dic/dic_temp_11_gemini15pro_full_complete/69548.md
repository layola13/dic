fellowed:/ˈfeləʊd/
verb
结交,成为…的会员,授予…会员资格
admitted as a fellow; granted fellowship.
He was fellowed at Trinity College, Cambridge. 他被授予剑桥大学三一学院的研究员职位。

近义词: admitted, inducted, initiated
反义词: expelled, excluded

词性: verb (past tense and past participle of "fellow")
词源: From Middle English *felaghen, from Old English *gefēolagan (“to join as a fellow”), from fēolaga (“fellow, companion”).
