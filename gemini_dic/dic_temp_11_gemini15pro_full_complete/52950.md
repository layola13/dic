dinful:/ˈdɪnfʊl/| adj.|喧闹的，嘈杂的|Characterized by a loud, unpleasant, and prolonged noise.|The dinful construction site made it impossible to concentrate.  建筑工地嘈杂的声音让人无法集中注意力。|例句：The dinful bar made it difficult to hear our conversation. 喧闹的酒吧让我们很难听到彼此的谈话。|近义词：noisy, clamorous, raucous|反义词：quiet, peaceful, silent|词性:形容词
词源: From 'din' (noise) + '-ful' (full of).  来自“din”（噪音）+“-ful”（充满）。
