kaling:/ˈkɑːlɪŋ/ | n. | 卡林加人；卡林加地区；卡林加语 |  Kaling refers to a group of Austronesian peoples primarily from the northern Philippines, particularly in the provinces of Kalinga and Apayao. It also refers to the region they inhabit and their language. | The Kaling are known for their intricate woven textiles. 卡林加人以其复杂的编织纺织品而闻名。|  The rice terraces of Kalinga are a UNESCO World Heritage Site. 卡林加的梯田是联合国教科文组织世界遗产。 | synonyms: Kalinga people, Kalinga tribe | antonyms: N/A | noun
