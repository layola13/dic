arboriculturists:/ˌɑːrbərɪˈkʌltʃərɪsts/
n.
树艺师，树木栽培家
A person who cultivates trees.  One who is skilled in arboriculture.
例句：The arboriculturists carefully pruned the branches of the old oak tree.  树艺师们仔细地修剪了老橡树的枝条。

例句：Arboriculturists play a vital role in maintaining the health and beauty of urban forests.  树艺师在维护城市森林的健康和美丽方面发挥着至关重要的作用。

近义词：tree surgeon, tree expert, horticulturist
