hilly:/ˈhɪli/| adj.|多山的，丘陵起伏的|Characterized by many hills; rolling and uneven land.|例句：The region is known for its hilly terrain. 这个地区以其丘陵地带而闻名。

例句：They went for a hike in the hilly countryside. 他们在丘陵起伏的乡村远足。|近义词：mountainous, undulating, rolling|反义词:flat, level, plain|词性:adjective
词源:From hill + -y.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hulniz ("small hill").
