multiheaded:/ˌmʌltiˈhɛdɪd/ | adj. | 多头的，多首的 | Having multiple heads. |  The multiheaded hydra was a fearsome beast in Greek mythology.  多头水螅是希腊神话中可怕的野兽。 | multi-headed, many-headed | single-headed | adjective

Etymology:
"multi-" (from Latin "multus" meaning "many, much") + "-headed" (from Old English "hēafod" meaning "head")
