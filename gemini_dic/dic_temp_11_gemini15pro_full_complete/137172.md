nonsuccessionally:/nɒnˌsəkˈseʃənəli/
adverb
非连续地，不连续地
not in a successive manner; not following in order or sequence.
The events occurred nonsuccessionally, with significant gaps in between. 这些事件发生得非连续性，中间有明显的间隔。
The numbers were arranged nonsuccessionally. 这些数字排列得非连续。
近义词: intermittently, sporadically, discontinuously
反义词: successively, consecutively, sequentially
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"
succession (noun): from Latin "successio" meaning "a following after"
-al (suffix): forming adjectives
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs
