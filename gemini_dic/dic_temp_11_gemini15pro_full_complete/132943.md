nonemendations: /ˌnɒnɪˈmɛndeɪʃənz/ | n. | 未修改；未更正；未改进 |  The absence of emendations or corrections; the state of being unamended or uncorrected.  It refers to a text or document that remains in its original state, without any alterations or improvements. |  The editor's decision to publish the manuscript with nonemendations caused controversy.  编辑决定出版未经修改的手稿，引发了争议。

The manuscript is remarkable for its nonemendations, providing a pure view of the author's original intent. 这份手稿因其未经修改而引人注目，提供了作者原意的纯粹视角。 | 近义词: unaltered state, original version | 反义词: emendations, corrections, revisions | 词性: noun
