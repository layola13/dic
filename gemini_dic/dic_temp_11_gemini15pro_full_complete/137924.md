nonvolitional:/ˌnɒnˌvɒˈlɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非自愿的，非意志的 | Not done by choice; involuntary.  Resulting from or characterized by the absence of volition. |  His hand trembled in a nonvolitional spasm. 他的手非自愿地一阵痉挛。| synonyms: involuntary, automatic, reflex, instinctive, unconscious | antonyms: voluntary, conscious, deliberate, intentional | adjective
