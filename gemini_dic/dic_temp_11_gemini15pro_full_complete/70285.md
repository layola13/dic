fidejussion:/ˌfɪdəˈdʒʌʃən/
n.
保证；担保；保证书
A pledge or guarantee by a third party to be responsible for a debt or engagement of another.
The bank required a fidejussion from her parents before granting her the loan.  这家银行在她获得贷款前要求她父母提供担保。

近义词:guaranty, surety, bond, pledge
反义词:None (lack of fidejussion implies no guarantee)
词性:Noun

词源: From Latin *fidejussio*, from *fidejussus*, past participle of *fidejubēre* "to order to give surety", from *fidēs* "faith, trust" + *jubēre* "to order, command."
