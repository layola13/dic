noncolonial:/nɒn.kəˈləʊ.ni.əl/ | adj. | 非殖民的 | Not colonial; not belonging to or characteristic of a colony or colonies; not subject to or characterized by colonialism. |  The conference focused on noncolonial interpretations of history.  这次会议的重点是非殖民主义对历史的诠释。 |  Example: The rise of noncolonial literature challenged traditional perspectives. 非殖民文学的兴起挑战了传统观点。 | Synonyms: independent, sovereign, autonomous, postcolonial | Antonyms: colonial, imperial, dependent | adjective
