cyberspatial:/ˈsaɪbərˈspeɪʃəl/| adj.|网络空间的|Relating to or existing within cyberspace.  Existing or occurring in cyberspace; of or relating to cyberspace.|例句：Cyberspatial interaction is becoming increasingly important in modern society. 网络空间互动在现代社会变得越来越重要。

例句：The cyberspatial environment presents both opportunities and challenges for businesses. 网络空间环境为企业带来了机遇和挑战。|近义词：virtual, online, digital|反义词:physical, real-world|词性:adjective

词源:  "Cyber-" (from cybernetics) + "spatial" (relating to space).  The prefix "cyber-" refers to computer or information technology, while "spatial" relates to the dimensions of space. Thus, "cyberspatial" describes something related to the space of computer networks and virtual reality.
