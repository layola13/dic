词源：From Middle English *lym, from Old English līm (“sticky substance, birdlime, glue”), from Proto-West Germanic *līm, from Proto-Germanic *līmaz (“lime, birdlime”), from Proto-Indo-European *leiH- (“to smear, stick, stick together”). Cognate with West Frisian līm (“lime, glue”), Dutch lijm (“glue”), German Leim (“glue”), Danish lim (“glue”), Swedish lim (“glue”).  The citrus fruit sense is a shortening of lime fruit, from French lime, from Arabic لَيْمَة‎ (layma).
