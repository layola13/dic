counterfire:/ˈkaʊntərˌfaɪər/| n. v.|还击，反火力|Fire returned at an enemy's position.  The act of returning fire. |The artillery launched a counterfire against the enemy's bombardment. 炮兵向敌人的炮击发起反击。| The troops responded with heavy counterfire. 部队以猛烈的还击作为回应。 |Synonyms: return fire, retaliatory fire, counterattack, riposte|Antonyms: ceasefire, truce, surrender|noun
