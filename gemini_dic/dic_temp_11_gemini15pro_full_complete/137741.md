nonvalues:/ˈnɒnˌvæljuːz/ (British) /ˈnɑːnˌvæljuːz/ (American)| n.| 无价值之物；非价值观|Things considered unimportant or without worth.  It often refers to things that a person or society rejects or considers detrimental. |例句：The company decided to discard these nonvalues to focus on its core mission.  这家公司决定放弃这些无价值之物，专注于其核心使命。

例句：He argued that pursuing fame and fortune were nonvalues and that true happiness lay elsewhere. 他认为追求名利是毫无价值的，真正的幸福在于别处。|近义词：worthlessness, trivialities, insignificance|反义词:values, principles, ideals|词性:名词
