laton:/ˈleɪtɒn/ (UK), /ˈleɪtɑːn/ (US)| n.| 拉顿, 黄铜的一种|Laton is a type of brass, a metal alloy made primarily of copper and zinc. It often has a higher zinc content than other brasses, making it harder and more durable.  It was historically a common material for decorative objects and utensils.|The antique clock was made of laton.|近义词: brass, bronze|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
