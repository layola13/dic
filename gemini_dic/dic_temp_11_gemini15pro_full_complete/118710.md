metacromion:/ˌmɛtəˈkreɪmiən/| n.|肩峰外侧|The metacromion process is a bony projection on the scapula (shoulder blade) in some mammals, located at the top of the spine of the scapula and extending laterally.  它是一些哺乳动物肩胛骨（肩胛骨）上的骨突，位于肩胛骨脊柱的顶部并向侧面延伸。|The metacromion provides an attachment point for muscles involved in shoulder movement. 肩峰外侧为参与肩部运动的肌肉提供附着点。|Synonyms: acromion process (in some contexts), metacromial process|Antonyms:  (No direct anatomical antonym)|noun
