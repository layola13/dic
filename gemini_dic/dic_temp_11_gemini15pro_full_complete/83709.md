hagiography:/ˌhæɡiˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. |圣徒传记；理想化的传记；(对某人过分赞扬的) 吹捧文章 |An idealized biography, especially of a saint or religious figure. Also, excessively flattering or uncritical writing about someone. |例句：The campaign biography was pure hagiography, glossing over any of the candidate's flaws. 这本竞选传记完全是吹捧，掩盖了候选人的所有缺点。

例句：Many ancient hagiographies are more legend than fact. 许多古代圣徒传记与其说是事实，不如说是传说。|近义词：biography, life story, eulogy, panegyric |反义词：exposé, critique, debunking |词性:noun
