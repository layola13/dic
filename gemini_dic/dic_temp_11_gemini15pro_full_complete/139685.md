ocean-going:/ˈoʊʃənˌɡoʊɪŋ/| adj.|远洋的，适于远洋航行的|Designed or equipped for traveling on the ocean.  Used to describe ships or other vessels that are built and equipped to travel across oceans.|例句：The ocean-going vessel was built to withstand harsh weather conditions. 这艘远洋船舶是为了抵御恶劣天气条件而建造的。

例句：Ocean-going tankers transport vast quantities of oil across the world. 远洋油轮在世界各地运输大量的石油。|近义词：seaworthy, seafaring|反义词：coastal, inland|词性: adjective
