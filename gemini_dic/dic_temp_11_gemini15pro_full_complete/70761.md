finery:/ˈfaɪnəri/| n.|华丽的服饰；盛装；精美的东西|finery refers to elaborate or showy clothing and accessories, often worn for special occasions. It can also refer to anything elegant or beautiful.|She wore her finest finery to the ball. 她穿着她最华丽的服饰去参加舞会。|The queen's finery was dazzling. 女王的盛装令人眼花缭乱。|近义词：attire, garments, apparel, raiment, best clothes|反义词：rags, casual wear|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *finerie*, from Old French *finerie* (“refinement, delicacy”), from *fin* (“fine”).
