extrusions: /ɪkˈstruːʒənz/ | n. | 挤压物，挤出物，突出物 |  The process of shaping material by forcing it through a die. Also refers to the shaped object produced by this process. | The aluminum extrusions were used to create the frame of the building.  这些铝挤压件被用来建造建筑物的框架。 | synonyms: protrusions, projections, pressings | antonyms: indentations, depressions, recesses | noun


extrude: /ɪkˈstruːd/ | v. | 挤压，压出，推出 | To shape (material such as metal or plastic) by forcing it through a die. |  The machine extrudes plastic tubing at a rapid rate.  这台机器快速挤压塑料管。 | synonyms: force out, press out, squeeze out | antonyms:  draw in, pull in, suck in | verb
