circumcisions: /ˌsɜːrkəmˈsɪʒənz/ | n. | 割礼，包皮环切术 |The surgical removal of the foreskin of the penis.  It is a common religious ritual in Judaism and Islam, and is also performed for medical reasons.|  Male circumcisions are performed for religious or health reasons. 男性割礼是出于宗教或健康原因进行的。| Circumcisions are less common in some cultures. 在某些文化中，割礼不太常见。|  近义词:  excision | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

Etymology: From Late Middle English *circumcisioun*, from Old French circumcision, from Latin circumcisiō (“a cutting around”), from circumcidō (“I cut around”), from circum (“around”) + caedō (“I cut”).
