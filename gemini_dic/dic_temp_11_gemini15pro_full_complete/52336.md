dicta:/ˈdɪktə/| n.|陈述；声明；断言；命令；指示|An authoritative pronouncement; a decree.  A judge's incidental expression of opinion, not essential to the decision, not establishing precedent.|The dicta of the Supreme Court influenced lower courts.|近义词：pronouncement, decree, assertion, edict|反义词:request, plea|noun
