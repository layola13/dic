emulsify:/ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪ/| v. |乳化|To combine two liquids that don't normally mix, like oil and water, into a stable mixture.|  The chef emulsified the oil and vinegar to make a salad dressing.  厨师将油和醋乳化，制成沙拉酱。|synonyms: blend, mix, homogenize|antonyms: separate, divide|verb

emulsion:/ɪˈmʌlʃən/| n. |乳液，乳剂|A mixture of two or more liquids that are normally immiscible (unblendable).| Milk is an emulsion of fat and water. 牛奶是脂肪和水的乳浊液。|synonyms: suspension, colloid|antonyms: solution, mixture|noun

emulsionize:/ɪˈmʌlsɪnaɪz/| v. |乳化|To make into an emulsion; emulsify.|  She emulsionized the cream and butter for the frosting. 她将奶油和黄油乳化做糖霜。|synonyms: emulsify, blend, mix, homogenize|antonyms: separate, divide|verb
