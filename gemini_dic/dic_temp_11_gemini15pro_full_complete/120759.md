misapplier:/ˌmɪsəˈplaɪər/| n.|误用者；错误应用者|Someone who misapplies something, meaning they use it incorrectly or for the wrong purpose.|He was a misapplier of funds, using the charity money for his own personal gain. 他滥用了资金，把慈善捐款用于个人利益。|近义词：misuser, abuser|反义词: proper user, appropriate user|noun
