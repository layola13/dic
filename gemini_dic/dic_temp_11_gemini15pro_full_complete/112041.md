longan:/ˈlɒŋɡən/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋɡɑːn/ (US)| n. |龙眼|A tropical tree native to Southeast Asia, or its small, round, sweet, brownish-yellow fruit with a translucent white flesh and a hard, dark brown seed.|例句：I love the sweet taste of fresh longans. 我喜欢新鲜龙眼的味道。

例句：Longan trees thrive in warm climates. 龙眼树在温暖的气候中生长茂盛。|近义词：lychee, rambutan|反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Cantonese lùhng ngáahn 龍眼, literally "dragon eye", referring to the appearance of the fruit's flesh and seed.
