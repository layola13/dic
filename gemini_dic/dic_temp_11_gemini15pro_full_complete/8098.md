anthropopsychism:/ˌænθrəpəˈsaɪkɪzəm/
n.
拟人论, 赋予动物、植物或无生命物体以人类情感和心理特征的倾向
The attribution of human emotions and mental characteristics to animals, plants, or inanimate objects.
例句：Anthropopsychism is a common tendency in children's stories, where animals talk and think like humans.  拟人论在儿童故事中很常见，动物像人一样说话和思考。

例句：Her anthropopsychism led her to believe that her dog was truly communicating with her. 她的拟人论使她相信她的狗真的在和她交流。
近义词：anthropomorphism, personification
反义词:objectification
词性:noun
