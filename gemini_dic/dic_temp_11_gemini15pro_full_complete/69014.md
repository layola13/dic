fattening:/ˈfætnɪŋ/ | adj. | 使人发胖的；容易使人长胖的 | (of food) likely to make you fat |  Example: Avoid fattening foods like cakes and pastries. 避免食用蛋糕和糕点等容易使人发胖的食物。 | Synonyms: rich, calorie-laden, unhealthy | Antonyms: slimming, diet, healthy | adjective

fattening:/ˈfætnɪŋ/ | adj. | 肥育的 |  Making or tending to make fat; fatted or fit for fattening. | Example: The farmer kept his pigs in fattening pens. 农民把他的猪养在肥育栏里. | Synonyms: plumping, enriching | Antonyms: slimming, diet, healthy | adjective
