outjested:/ˌaʊtˈdʒɛstɪd/
verb
胜过(某人)的玩笑;以更巧妙的玩笑胜过
surpassed in jesting; outdone in wit or humor
He outjested his rivals at the king's court. 他在宫廷里以玩笑胜过了他的对手们。
Synonyms: outwitted, surpassed, bested
Antonyms: defeated, outdone, lost to
词性: 动词
