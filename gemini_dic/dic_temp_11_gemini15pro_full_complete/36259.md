cogon:/ˈkōˌɡōn/| n. |茅草，白茅|A tall, perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical Asia, often used for thatching and weaving.  It is known for its sharp-edged leaves and ability to spread rapidly, often considered invasive.|The roof of the hut was made of cogon.|近义词：blady grass, thatch grass|反义词: N/A (no single antonym for a type of grass)|noun
