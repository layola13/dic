chemists:/ˈkɛmɪsts/| n.|化学家；药剂师|A chemist is a scientist who studies the composition, structure, properties, and reactions of matter, especially of atomic and molecular systems.|Many chemists are working on developing new drugs.|Synonyms: scientist, researcher, analyst|Antonyms: N/A|noun

chemists:/ˈkɛmɪsts/| n.|药店；药房 (British English)|In British English, a chemist's is a shop where medicines and other toiletries are sold.|I need to go to the chemist's to pick up my prescription.|Synonyms: pharmacy, drugstore (American English)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
