corkwood:/ˈkɔːrkwʊd/ | n. | 软木树，软木木材 | A type of tree known for its light and buoyant wood, similar to cork.  Specifically refers to several species within the genera *Endiandra* and *Duboisia*. | The corkwood was so light it floated easily on the water. 软木很轻，很容易漂浮在水上。 | Corkwood is often used for fishing floats. 软木经常被用来制作钓鱼浮标。 | synonyms: cork, balsa, lightwood | antonyms: hardwood, ironwood | noun

corkwood:/ˈkɔːrkwʊd/ | n. | 软木，软木制品 | Objects made from the wood of the corkwood tree.  |  He carved a small bird from the corkwood. 他用软木雕刻了一只小鸟。| The corkwood handle on the knife provided a good grip. 刀上的软木手柄提供了良好的抓握力。| synonyms: cork, balsa | antonyms: hardwood, metal | noun
