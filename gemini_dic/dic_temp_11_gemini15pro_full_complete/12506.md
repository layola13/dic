asperser:/əˈspɜːrsər/
n.|诽谤者，污蔑者|Someone who attacks the reputation of another by slander or libel.|The anonymous asperser was never identified.  匿名诽谤者从未被确认身份。|slanderer, defamer, calumniator|defender, advocate
词性: noun


asperse:/əˈspɜːrs/
vt.|诽谤，污蔑，中伤|To attack the reputation of someone by slander or libel.|His political opponents aspersed his character. 他的政敌诽谤他的人格。|slander, defame, calumniate|praise, commend
词性: verb


词源:
From Late Latin *aspersāre, frequentative of Latin aspergere "to sprinkle, scatter," from ad- "to, toward" + spargere "to scatter."  The figurative sense of "to besmirch, slander" arose from the notion of sprinkling or scattering something unpleasant.
