corporatism:/ˈkɔːrpərətɪzəm/ /ˈkɔːrpərəˌtɪzəm/
n.
社团主义，统制主义
A political ideology which advocates the organization of society by corporate groups, such as agricultural, labour, military, scientific, or guild associations, on the basis of their common interests.  These groups may often have representation in government or may directly control government policy.
Corporatism is often viewed as a third way between capitalism and socialism.

Italy under Mussolini was an example of corporatism.  墨索里尼统治下的意大利是社团主义的一个例子。

The influence of powerful corporate lobbies on government policy could be seen as a form of corporatism.  强大的企业游说团体对政府政策的影响可以被视为一种社团主义形式。

近义词:syndicalism, statism, collectivism
反义词:individualism, liberalism, laissez-faire
词性:noun
