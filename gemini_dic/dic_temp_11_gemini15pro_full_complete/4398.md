alamohods:/ˌæləˈmoʊdz/
|n.|阿拉莫哈德人（12世纪到13世纪统治北非和西班牙部分地区的柏柏尔穆斯林王朝）|A member of a Berber Muslim dynasty that ruled North Africa and parts of Spain in the 12th and 13th centuries.|The Almohads conquered much of Spain. 阿拉莫哈德人征服了西班牙的大部分地区。|The Almohad empire stretched from Morocco to Seville. 阿拉莫哈德帝国从摩洛哥延伸到塞维利亚。|近义词:Berbers, Muslims|反义词:Christians|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *almohades*, plural of *almohade*, from Arabic *al-Muwaḥḥidūn*, "the Unitarians," or "those who affirm the unity of God."
