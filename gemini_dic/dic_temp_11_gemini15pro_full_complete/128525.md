nexus:/ˈnɛksəs/ (US), /ˈnɛksəs/ (UK)| n.|连接，联系；中心，枢纽|A connection or series of connections linking two or more things.|The nexus between poverty and crime is complex. 贫困与犯罪之间的联系错综复杂.|Synonyms: link, connection, bond, tie, junction, intersection|Antonyms: division, separation|noun

nexi:/ˈnɛksaɪ/ (US), /ˈnɛksaɪ/ (UK)| n.|连接，联系；中心，枢纽 (nexus的复数形式)|Plural form of nexus, referring to multiple connections or centers.|The various nexi of transportation in the city are crucial for its economy. 城市中的各种交通枢纽对其经济至关重要.|Synonyms: links, connections, bonds, ties, junctions, intersections|Antonyms: divisions, separations|noun
