misconstruable:/ˌmɪskənˈstruːəbl/ | adj. | 可被误解的 | Open to misinterpretation or liable to be wrongly understood. |  His statement was easily misconstruable and caused a lot of confusion. 他的声明很容易被误解，引起了很多混乱。

The contract's wording was intentionally misconstruable to allow for loopholes. 合同的措辞故意含糊不清，以便留下漏洞。

Synonyms: ambiguous, misleading, equivocal, vague | Antonyms: clear, unambiguous, precise, unequivocal | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (prefix meaning 'wrongly' or 'badly') + construable (from construe 'to interpret' + -able 'capable of being').  The root "construe" comes from Latin "construere," meaning "to build together," implying an interpretation built from the parts of a text or statement.
