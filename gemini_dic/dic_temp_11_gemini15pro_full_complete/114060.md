magnetizability:/məɡˌnɛtɪzəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 磁化率，磁化性 | The magnetizability of a material indicates how easily it can be magnetized in an external magnetic field.  一种材料的磁化率表示它在外磁场中被磁化的容易程度。 | The magnetizability of iron is much higher than that of aluminum. 铁的磁化率比铝的磁化率高得多。 |  susceptibility, permeability | non-magnetizability (less common) | noun
