coelom:/ˈsiːləm/| n. |体腔|The main body cavity in most multicellular animals. It is positioned inside the body to surround and contain the digestive tract and other organs.  It is lined with mesoderm.|The earthworm has a well-developed coelom.|近义词:body cavity|反义词:None|词性:名词

coelomic:/siːˈlɒmɪk/ |adj.|体腔的|Relating to the coelom, the main body cavity in most animals.|The coelomic fluid helps to protect the internal organs.|近义词:peritoneal (referring specifically to the abdominal coelom)|反义词:None|词性:形容词
