disappropriate:/ˌdɪsəˈproʊprieɪt/
verb
中文翻译：剥夺；不适当地使用；使不适合
英文解析：To deprive of something that one has appropriated or acquired; to use inappropriately; to make unsuitable.
例句：The government disappropriated the land for public use. 政府征用了这块土地用于公共用途。

例句：He disappropriated the company funds for his personal use. 他挪用了公司资金供个人使用。

近义词：misappropriate, misuse, divest, expropriate
反义词：appropriate, allocate, grant, bestow
词性：v.
