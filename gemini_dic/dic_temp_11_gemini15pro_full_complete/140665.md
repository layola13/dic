omelet:/ˈɑːmlɪt/ (US) /ˈɒmlɪt/ (UK)| n.|煎蛋卷，蛋饼|A dish of beaten eggs fried and folded over, often with a filling. |例句：I had a delicious cheese omelet for breakfast. 我早餐吃了一个美味的奶酪煎蛋卷。

例句：She made an omelet with mushrooms and onions. 她做了一个蘑菇洋葱煎蛋卷。

近义词：fritatta|反义词: None|词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *omelette*, earlier *alumelle* and *alumete*, from *alemelle* "thin plate," diminutive of *aleme* "blade," from Old French *aleme*, from Latin *lamina* "thin plate, leaf." The spelling with a *t* was influenced by Latin *lamella*.
