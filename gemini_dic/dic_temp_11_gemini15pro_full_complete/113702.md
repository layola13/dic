macrameing:/ˈmækrəˌmeɪɪŋ/
n.|编绳工艺; 用绳结编织;|The act of creating decorative or functional items by knotting cord or string in patterns.  It's a craft that involves making things by tying knots.|She enjoys macrameing in her spare time. 她喜欢在空闲时间编绳。|近义词：knotting, weaving, braiding|反义词: unraveling, untying|词性: noun (gerund)
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *macramé*, from Arabic *miqrāmah* (مِقْرَمَة) meaning "embroidered napkin" or "fringed cloth," ultimately from Turkish *makrama*.
