epanody:/ɪˈpænədi/ | n. | 回指, 重复; (修辞)反复 |  A figure of speech in which the same word or phrase is repeated at the beginning and end of a clause or sentence, for emphasis or effect. | "Rejoice in the Lord always: and again I say, Rejoice."
