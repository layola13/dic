mochila: /moʊˈtʃiːlə/ (US), /məʊˈtʃiːlə/ (UK) | n. | 背包 | A bag carried on the back, typically made of canvas or other strong fabric and used for carrying supplies, especially by hikers and climbers. |  Example: She packed her mochila with food and water for the hike. 她把食物和水装进背包里准备徒步旅行。| Synonym: backpack, rucksack, knapsack | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps suitcase for a different type of luggage) | Noun

Etymology: From Spanish *mochila*, ultimately from Arabic *muḫalla*, meaning "provision bag."
