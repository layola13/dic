affably:/ˈæfəbli/| adv.|和蔼地；亲切地|In a friendly and easy-going manner.|He greeted me affably and offered me a cup of tea. 他和蔼地跟我打招呼，并给我倒了杯茶。|例句：She smiled affably at the guests. 她和蔼可亲地对客人微笑。

例句：He affably agreed to help with the project. 他欣然同意帮忙做这个项目。|近义词：amiably, genially, pleasantly, cordially|反义词：unfriendly, coldly, curtly|词性:副词

词源:
From Late Latin *affabilis, from Latin ad- (“to, towards”) + fari (“to speak”).
