dele:/ˈdiːliː/
noun
删除，删节
an instruction written on a manuscript or proof to delete something
The editor marked several deles in the manuscript.  编辑在原稿上标注了几个删除的地方。
近义词:deletion, erasure, expunction
反义词:insertion, addition
