nonzoologically:/ˌnɒnˌzuːəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adjective
非动物学地
Not related to or concerned with zoology; from a non-zoological perspective.
His research, although related to animal behavior, was conducted nonzoologically, focusing on the physical environment rather than the animals themselves. 他虽然研究动物行为，但却不是从动物学的角度，而是关注物理环境，而不是动物本身。
non-zoologically
not zoologically
