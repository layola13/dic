crystallic:/krɪˈstælɪk/ | adj. | 晶体的；水晶般的 |  Relating to or resembling a crystal; crystalline. | The cave walls were covered in crystallic formations. 洞穴的墙壁上覆盖着晶体结构。|The geode revealed a breathtaking array of crystallic structures. 晶洞展现出一系列令人叹为观止的晶体结构。| 近义词: crystalline, glassy, clear | 反义词: amorphous, opaque | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From French *crystallin,* from Latin *crystallinus,* from *crystallum* "crystal," from Greek *krystallos* "ice, crystal."
