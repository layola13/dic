jigsaw:/ˈdʒɪɡsɔː/ | n. | 拼图；谜；难题 | A puzzle consisting of a picture printed on cardboard or wood and cut into various pieces of different shapes that have to be fitted together. |  I spent hours trying to complete the jigsaw. 我花了几个小时试图完成拼图。

The investigation is like a jigsaw, with each piece of evidence helping to complete the picture.  这项调查就像一个拼图，每一条证据都有助于拼凑出完整的画面。 | synonyms: puzzle, conundrum, riddle | antonyms: solution, answer | noun
