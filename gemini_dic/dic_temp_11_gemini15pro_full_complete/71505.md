flanker:/ˈflæŋkər/| n.|侧翼队员，侧卫，侧翼攻击者|A flanker is a person positioned on the side of something, especially a military formation or a sports team, often with the aim of attacking or defending. |The flanker intercepted the ball. 侧卫拦截了球。|近义词:wingman, wing forward, winger|反义词:center|词性:noun

flanker:/ˈflæŋkər/| v.|包抄，位于…的侧面，从侧面攻击|To flank or attack from the flank. |The enemy flanked our troops. 敌人从侧翼包抄了我们的部队。|近义词:outflank, surround, encircle|反义词:confront|词性:verb
