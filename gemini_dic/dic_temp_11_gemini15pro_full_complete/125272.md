multifaith:/ˈmʌltiˌfeɪθ/ | adj. | 多信仰的；多种宗教的 | Involving or relating to several different religions or faiths. |  The city has a multifaith community center that welcomes people of all backgrounds. 这个城市有一个多信仰的社区中心，欢迎来自不同背景的人们。

The school has a multifaith prayer room for students of various religions. 这所学校有一个多信仰的祈祷室，供不同宗教的学生使用。 | 近义词: interfaith, multireligious | 反义词: single-faith, monoreligious | 词性: adjective

Etymology: "multi-" (from Latin "multus" meaning "many, much") + "faith" (from Old French "fei" meaning "faith, belief").
