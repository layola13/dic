classrooms:/ˈklɑːsruːmz/ /ˈklæsruːmz/
n.
教室（复数）
A room in a school or university where classes are held.
The students gathered in their classrooms for the first day of school. 学生们在开学第一天聚集在教室里。
The university is building new classrooms to accommodate the growing student body.  大学正在建造新的教室以容纳不断增长的学生人数。
近义词:lecture halls, seminar rooms, studios (depending on context)
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) outdoors, hallways
词性:Noun
