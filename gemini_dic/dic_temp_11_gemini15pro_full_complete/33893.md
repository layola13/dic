circumstantiate: /sərˈkʌmstənʃieɪt/ /sərˈkʌmstænʃieɪt/
verb
证实；用事实说明
To provide detailed information about something, giving the facts and circumstances
The lawyer asked the witness to circumstantiate his claim. 律师要求证人详细说明他的主张。
近义词: verify, corroborate, substantiate, detail, specify
反义词: disprove, refute, deny
词性: verb

circumstantiate: /sərˈkʌmstənʃieɪt/ /sərˈkʌmstænʃieɪt/
词源: From Late Latin circumstantiatus, past participle of circumstantiare "to place in particular circumstances," from Latin circum- "around" + stantia "stance."
