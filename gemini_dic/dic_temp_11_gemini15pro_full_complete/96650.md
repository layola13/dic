industrialises:/ɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzɪz/
verb
工业化
To develop industries in (a country or region) on a wide scale.
Many countries in Asia successfully industrialized in the latter half of the 20th century. 许多亚洲国家在20世纪后半叶成功地实现了工业化。
The government is trying to industrialize the rural areas. 政府正在努力实现农村地区的工业化。
synonyms: develop, modernize, advance
antonyms: ruralize, deindustrialize
词性:动词
