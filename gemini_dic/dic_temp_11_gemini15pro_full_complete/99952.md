interobserver:/ˌɪntərəbˈzɜːrvər/| adj.|观察者间的，不同观察者之间的|Relating to or involving different observers.  It describes the variation or agreement between the results obtained by different people observing the same phenomenon.|The study examined interobserver reliability in diagnosing the disease. 这项研究检验了诊断这种疾病时观察者间的一致性。|近义词：interrater|反义词:intraobserver|词性:形容词
