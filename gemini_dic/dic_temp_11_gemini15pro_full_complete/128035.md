nerding:/ˈnɜːrdɪŋ/ | n. | 书呆子行为，沉迷于某事 | The act of engaging in nerdy pursuits or behaving like a nerd.  | He spent the weekend nerding out on his new computer. 他整个周末都沉迷于他的新电脑。| 近义词: geeking out, obsessing, indulging | 反义词: socializing, partying | 词性:名词

词源: Derived from the noun "nerd" + "-ing" suffix, forming a gerund or present participle.  "Nerd" itself has uncertain origins, possibly related to "nert," meaning "fool" or "crazy person."
