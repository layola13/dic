overbejeweled: /ˌoʊvərbiˈdʒuːəld/ | adj. | 过度装饰珠宝的；珠宝过多的 | Covered with too many jewels; excessively adorned with jewels. |  The overbejeweled socialite drew gasps from the crowd. 珠光宝气的社会名流引来人群的惊叹。| The actress wore an overbejeweled gown to the premiere.  这位女演员穿着缀满珠宝的礼服出席了首映式。| 近义词: gaudy, ostentatious | 反义词: simple, understated | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "bejewel" (verb meaning "to adorn with jewels").  "Bejewel" itself comes from "be-" (prefix meaning "to make or cause to be") + "jewel" (noun).
