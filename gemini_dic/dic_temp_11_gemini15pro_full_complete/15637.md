bait-and-switch:/ˈbeɪt ən swɪtʃ/ | n. | 诱饵调包，挂羊头卖狗肉 | A deceptive commercial practice where customers are lured in with a promise of a low-priced item but are then pressured to buy a more expensive one. | The store advertised a great deal on a TV, but it was a bait-and-switch tactic; they were out of the advertised TV and tried to sell me a much more expensive model.  这家商店宣传电视机很划算，但这是一个诱饵调包的策略；他们没有广告中的电视机，并试图向我推销更昂贵的型号。| Synonyms: deceptive advertising, false advertising, scam, fraud | Antonyms: honest advertising, ethical marketing | noun


bait-and-switch:/ˈbeɪt ən swɪtʃ/ | adj. | 诱饵调包的，挂羊头卖狗肉的 | Describing a deceptive commercial practice. | The bait-and-switch tactics of the car dealership left many customers feeling cheated.  汽车经销商的诱饵调包策略让许多顾客感到上当受骗。 | Synonyms: deceptive, misleading, fraudulent | Antonyms: honest, ethical | adjective
