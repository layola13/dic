geognost:/ˈdʒiːəɡnɒst/| n. |地质学家；地质学者|A person skilled in geognosy; one who has a knowledge of the structure of the earth.|The geognost studied the rock formations to understand the history of the area.  这位地质学家研究了岩层构造以了解该地区的历史。|近义词：geologist, earth scientist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From German *Geognost*, from Greek *gē* "earth" + *gnōstēs* "one who knows," from *gignōskein* "to know."
