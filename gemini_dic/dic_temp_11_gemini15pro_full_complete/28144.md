capitulate: /kəˈpɪtʃʊleɪt/ /kəˈpɪtʃəleɪt/
verb
投降，屈服，停止抵抗
cease to resist an opponent or an unwelcome demand; surrender.  The act of surrendering or ceasing to resist.
例句：
The enemy finally capitulated after weeks of fighting.  敌人经过数周的战斗后最终投降了。

After a long argument, he capitulated and let her have her way.  经过长时间的争论，他妥协了，让她随心所欲。

近义词：surrender, yield, give in, succumb
反义词：resist, defy, oppose
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *capitulātus, past participle of capitulāre (“to draw up in chapters, to treat upon articles, to surrender on terms”), from Latin capitulum (“chapter, article, heading”), diminutive of caput (“head”).
