derange:/dɪˈreɪndʒ/| v. |使精神错乱，扰乱|to disturb the order or arrangement of; to make insane|His mind was deranged by grief.  他的精神被悲痛折磨得错乱了。

The violent storm deranged the traffic system in the city.  暴风雨扰乱了城市的交通系统。|近义词：disrupt, unsettle, unbalance, disorder, confuse, madden|反义词：organize, order, calm, soothe|词性：动词

deranged:/dɪˈreɪndʒd/| adj. |精神错乱的|insane; mentally disturbed|The deranged man wandered the streets. 那个精神错乱的男人在街上游荡。

The police apprehended the deranged suspect. 警察逮捕了那个精神错乱的嫌疑人。|近义词：insane, mad, lunatic, demented|反义词：sane, rational, lucid|词性：形容词

derangement:/dɪˈreɪndʒmənt/| n. |精神错乱，扰乱|the act of deranging or the state of being deranged; a disorder or disturbance|He suffered from a mental derangement. 他患有精神错乱。

The derangement of the files caused chaos in the office. 文件的混乱导致了办公室的混乱。|近义词：disorder, chaos, confusion, madness, insanity|反义词：order, organization, sanity|词性：名词
