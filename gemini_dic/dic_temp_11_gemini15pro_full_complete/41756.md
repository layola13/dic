corruptedly:/kəˈrʌptɪdli/
| adv. | 腐败地，堕落地 |  In a dishonest or immoral manner; in a way that shows a willingness to act dishonestly in return for money or personal gain. |  The officials acted corruptedly, accepting bribes and manipulating contracts. 官员们腐败行事，接受贿赂和操纵合同。| 近义词：dishonestly, fraudulently, illegally, immorally | 反义词：honestly, ethically, morally | 词性: 副词

corrupt:/kəˈrʌpt/
| adj. | 腐败的，堕落的 |  Having or showing a willingness to act dishonestly in return for money or personal gain. | The corrupt politician was eventually exposed and removed from office. 腐败的政客最终被揭露并被免职。| 近义词：dishonest, crooked, unscrupulous | 反义词：honest, upright, ethical | 词性: 形容词

corrupt:/kəˈrʌpt/
| v. | 使腐败，使堕落 | Cause to act dishonestly in return for money or personal gain. | The offer of a bribe corrupted the official.  贿赂的提议腐蚀了这位官员。 | 近义词：bribe, subvert, pervert | 反义词：purify, reform, improve | 词性: 动词
