distortion:/dɪˈstɔːrʃn/ | n. | 扭曲，变形；失真；歪曲 | a change in the shape, sound, or appearance of something so that it seems strange or unpleasant | The loud music caused a distortion of the sound.  喧闹的音乐导致声音失真。 | synonyms: deformation, contortion, warping, falsification, misrepresentation | antonyms: accuracy, clarity, truth, precision | noun

distortions:/dɪˈstɔːrʃənz/ | n. | 扭曲，变形；失真；歪曲（复数）|  plural form of distortion | The distortions in the image made it difficult to recognize the person. 图像的扭曲使得难以辨认这个人。| synonyms: deformations, contortions, warpings, falsifications, misrepresentations | antonyms: accuracies, clarities, truths, precisions | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *distortionem, from Latin distortus (past participle of distorquere “to twist different ways”), from dis- “apart, away” + torquere “to twist.”
