inadmissibility:/ˌɪnədˌmɪsəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可受理性，不许可入境|The state of being inadmissible; not allowable.|His inadmissibility to the country was due to a previous criminal conviction. 他因以前的犯罪记录而被禁止入境。|近义词：unacceptability, ineligibility|反义词：admissibility, eligibility|词性:名词

inadmissible:/ˌɪnədˈmɪsəbl/| adj. |不可接受的，不许可入境的|Not admissible; not allowable.|The evidence was inadmissible in court.  该证据在法庭上不被采纳。|近义词：unacceptable, ineligible|反义词：admissible, eligible|词性:形容词

词源:  From in- (not) + admissible.  Admissible comes from Latin admittere (to allow to enter).
