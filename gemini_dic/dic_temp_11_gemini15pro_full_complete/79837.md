glyphic:/ˈɡlɪfɪk/| adj.|雕刻的，象形的|Relating to or resembling a glyph or glyphs; carved or engraved.  Having the appearance of being carved or engraved.|The weathered stone had a glyphic quality, like ancient runes.|近义词：carved, engraved, inscribed, sculpted|反义词:smooth, plain|词性:adjective
glyphic:/ˈɡlɪfɪk/| n.|象形文字|A symbolic figure or character, especially one used in hieroglyphic writing.|The ancient Egyptian glyphic depicted a falcon.|近义词：symbol, character, hieroglyph|反义词: |词性:noun
