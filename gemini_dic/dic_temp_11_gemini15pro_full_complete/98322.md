insignificant:/ˌɪnsɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt/ | adj. | 无关紧要的；微不足道的 | Not important or significant; too small or unimportant to be worth consideration. |  The cost was insignificant compared to the potential profits.  成本与潜在利润相比微不足道。 | Synonyms: trivial, negligible, minor, petty, paltry | Antonyms: significant, important, substantial, major, crucial | adjective

insignificants:/ˌɪnsɪɡˈnɪfɪkənts/ | n. | 无关紧要的人或事（复数）|  People or things that are not important or significant. | He dismissed the insignificants and focused on the main issue. 他忽略了无关紧要的事情，专注于主要问题。| Synonyms: trifles, trivialities, nonessentials | Antonyms: essentials, necessities, importants | noun
