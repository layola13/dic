barley-break:/ˈbɑːrli breɪk/| n.|大麦棋; 一种古老的乡村游戏|A rustic game played by three couples, who are stationed in three compartments made by chalk lines on the ground; the middle compartment being called hell.  Two of the couples chase the other, who, when caught, change places with their captors. The game continues until all are in "hell".|We played barley-break on the village green. 我们在村里的草坪上玩大麦棋。|Synonyms:  prisoner's base, tag|Antonyms: None.|noun
