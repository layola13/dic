hydropult:/ˈhaɪdrəˌpʌlt/ | n. | 水上飞机弹射器；水上飞机起飞装置 | A device used to launch seaplanes from the water. | The hydropult propelled the seaplane into the air. 水上飞机弹射器将水上飞机推进空中。| The hydropult launched the aircraft smoothly. 水上飞机弹射器平稳地发射了飞机。| 近义词: catapult, launcher | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From hydro- (water) + -pult (Latin: pulsare, to push).
