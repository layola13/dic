broodier:/ˈbruːdiər/| adj.|更想孵蛋的，更闷闷不乐的|More inclined to brood; more moody or contemplative.|The hen has become broodier lately, spending more time in the nest.  这只母鸡最近变得更想孵蛋了，花更多时间在窝里。|The artist seemed broodier than usual, lost in thought.  这位艺术家看起来比平时更忧郁，陷入了沉思。|近义词：gloomier, moodier, sulkier, more contemplative|反义词：happier, cheerier, more outgoing|词性:形容词
词源: From brood + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  Ultimately from Old English brōd.
