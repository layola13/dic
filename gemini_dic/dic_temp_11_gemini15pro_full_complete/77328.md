n.
短吻鳄（复数）
Short form of alligators, large reptiles resembling crocodiles but with broader, shorter heads.
Those gators are basking in the sun. 那些短吻鳄正在晒太阳。
近义词:alligators, crocs (informal)
反义词:None (Animals don't typically have antonyms)
词性:noun
