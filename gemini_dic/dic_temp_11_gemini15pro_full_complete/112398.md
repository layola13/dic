lost-properties: /lɒst ˈprɒpətiz/ (British) /lɔːst ˈprɑːpərtiz/ (American)| n.| 失物招领处/失物| a place where lost items are kept until their owners claim them; items that have been lost and not yet reclaimed |例句：I went to the lost-properties office to see if my umbrella had been handed in. 我去失物招领处看看我的伞是否被交上去了。

例句：The lost-properties included a laptop, a suitcase, and a teddy bear. 失物包括一台笔记本电脑、一个行李箱和一只泰迪熊。|近义词：lost and found, lost baggage office|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


lost: /lɒst/ (British) /lɔːst/ (American) | adj.| 丢失的，迷路的 | unable to find one's way; no longer possessed | 词源: From Old English *lostian "be lost, perish," from Proto-Germanic *losaną (source also of Old Saxon los, Old Norse losa "to loosen, detach," Middle Dutch losen "to lose"), from PIE root *leu- "to loosen, divide, cut apart."
