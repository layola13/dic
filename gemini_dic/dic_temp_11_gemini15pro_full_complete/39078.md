confounded:/kənˈfaʊndɪd/ | adj. | 困惑的，糊涂的；被搞混的；该死的 | confused, bewildered; mixed up; damned. |  The witness's testimony was so confounded that the jury couldn't make sense of it. 证人的证词非常混乱，陪审团无法理解。| synonyms: bewildered, perplexed, puzzled, confused | antonyms: clear, lucid, understandable | adjective

confound:/kənˈfaʊnd/ | v. | 使困惑，使糊涂；混淆；挫败 | to confuse, bewilder; to mix up; to frustrate. | His sudden change of heart confounded everyone. 他突然改变主意，使每个人都感到困惑。| synonyms: confuse, bewilder, perplex, frustrate, thwart | antonyms: clarify, enlighten, explain | verb
