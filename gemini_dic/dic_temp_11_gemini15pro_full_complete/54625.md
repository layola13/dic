词源:  From Old French *desmettre, from Latin dīmittere (“to send away, let go, discharge, release”), from dī- (“away, apart”) + mittere (“to send”).


注意事项:  Dismiss can be used in both formal and informal contexts.  Be mindful of the specific meaning depending on the context.
