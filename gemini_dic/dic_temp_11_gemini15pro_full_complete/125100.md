mullioning:/ˈmʌljəˌnɪŋ/
n.
竖框，窗棂，直棂
The vertical bars that divide the panes of a window or the panels of a screen or other structure.
The mullioning in the old cathedral windows was intricate and beautiful.  古老教堂窗户上的竖框复杂而美丽。
近义词:muntins, glazing bars
反义词:(No direct antonym, could consider "open space" in context)
词性:noun


mullion:/ˈmʌljən/
n.
竖框，窗棂，直棂
A vertical bar or division between the lights or panes of a window, between panels in wainscoting, etc.
The stone mullion was cracked and needed repair. 石头竖框裂了，需要修理。
近义词:muntin, glazing bar
反义词:(No direct antonym)
词性:noun
