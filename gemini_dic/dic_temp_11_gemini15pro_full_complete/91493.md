hymn:/hɪm/ | n. |赞美诗，圣歌|A religious song or poem of praise to God or a god.|例句：The congregation stood and sang a hymn. 教众起立唱赞美诗。

例句：They sang hymns of praise. 他们唱赞美诗。|近义词：carol, psalm, anthem, song|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑blasphemy(亵渎神明) 但语境差异较大) |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ymne, from Latin hymnus, from Ancient Greek ὕμνος (humnos, “song of praise”).
