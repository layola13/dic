advertizings: /ˈædvərˌtaɪzɪŋz/
n.
广告活动；宣传活动
The act or practice of calling public attention to one's product, service, need, etc., especially by paid announcements in newspapers and magazines, over radio or television, on billboards, etc.
The company launched a series of advertizings to promote their new product.  这家公司开展了一系列广告活动来推广他们的新产品。
Synonyms:  promotion, publicity, marketing, campaign
Antonyms:  concealment, suppression

词性: 名词 (Noun)
