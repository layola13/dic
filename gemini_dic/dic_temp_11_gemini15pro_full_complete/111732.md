loculicidal: /ˌlɒkjuːlɪˈsaɪdəl/ | adj. | 沿室背开裂的 | Splitting along the dorsal suture of a carpel or fruit, allowing the seed to escape.  |  The ripe seed pod of the lily is loculicidal. 成熟的百合果荚是沿室背开裂的。 |  septicidal, valvular | indehiscent | adjective
