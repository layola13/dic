caregivings:/ˈkeərˌɡɪvɪŋz/
n.
照護；照顧工作；護理
the work of caring for a child or sick, elderly, or disabled person.
例句：The demands of caregivings can be overwhelming.  照顾工作的需求可能会让人不堪重负。

例句：She balanced her career with the caregivings of her aging parents. 她兼顾着自己的事业和照顾年迈父母的责任。
近义词：care, support, assistance, nursing, tending
反义词：neglect, abandonment
词性：名词

caregiving:/ˈkeərˌɡɪvɪŋ/
n./adj.
照護；照顧工作；護理/提供照護的
the work of caring for a child or sick, elderly, or disabled person./ relating to the work of caring for a child or sick, elderly, or disabled person.
例句：Caregiving can be a rewarding experience. 照顾他人可以是一段有益的经历。

例句：The caregiving responsibilities fell on her shoulders. 照顾的责任落在了她的肩上。
近义词：care, support, assistance, nursing, tending/caring, supportive

词性：名词/形容词
