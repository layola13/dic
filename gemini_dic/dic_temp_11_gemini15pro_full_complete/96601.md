inductivenesses:/ɪnˈdʌktɪvnəsɪz/
n.
归纳性（复数）
The quality of being inductive; the process of reasoning from specific observations to general principles.
The inductivenesses of the different studies varied considerably, leading to different conclusions. 不同研究的归纳性差异很大，导致结论不同。
The scientist emphasized the inductivenesses of their research methods.  科学家强调了他们研究方法的归纳性。
近义词:inductivity, induction
反义词:deductiveness, deduction
词性:noun
