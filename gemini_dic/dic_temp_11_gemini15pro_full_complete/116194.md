mashy:/ˈmæʃi/ | adj. | 糊状的；软烂的 | Soft and pulpy; resembling mash. | The potatoes were too mashy. 土豆太烂了。| The overcooked carrots were unpleasantly mashy.  煮过头的胡萝卜烂糊糊的，令人不快。| 近义词: pulpy, mushy, soft | 反义词: firm, hard, solid | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From mash + -y.  "Mash" comes from Old Norse *maska "to mix, stir."
