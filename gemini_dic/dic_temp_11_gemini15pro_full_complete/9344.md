antiproductively:/ˌæntiprəˈdʌktɪvli/
adverb
适得其反地，反效果地
in a way that prevents something from being produced or achieved as effectively as it could be
Working late into the night can be antiproductive if you're too tired to focus.  熬夜工作如果让你太累而无法集中注意力，可能会适得其反。
He realized that arguing with her was antiproductively and decided to compromise. 他意识到与她争论适得其反，于是决定妥协。
Synonyms: counterproductively, inefficiently, ineffectively, unproductively
Antonyms: productively, effectively, efficiently
词性: 副词
