assistantship:/əˈsɪstəntʃɪp/| n.|助教职位；研究生助教奖学金|A position as an assistant, especially to a professor, that involves part-time teaching or research duties in exchange for financial aid.|She was awarded an assistantship in the physics department. 她获得了物理系的助教职位。|Synonyms: fellowship, scholarship, grant|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider non-funded position)|noun

assistantship:/əˈsɪstəntʃɪp/| n.|助手的工作；助理的职位|The work or position of an assistant.|His assistantship involved grading papers and leading discussion sections. 他的助教工作包括批改论文和主持讨论课。|Synonyms: assistantship, aideship|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider leadership position, directorship)|noun
