jinnee:/ˈdʒɪni/ /ˈdʒiːni/| n.|精灵，妖怪|A supernatural creature in Arabian and Muslim folklore that can appear in human or animal form and grant wishes.  Derived from the Arabic word "jinn," referring to a spirit or demon.|例句：The jinnee emerged from the lamp in a puff of smoke. 精灵在一股烟雾中从神灯里冒了出来。

例句：He rubbed the lamp, hoping a jinnee would appear. 他擦了擦神灯，希望一个精灵会出现。|近义词：genie, djinn|反义词: None|词性:noun
