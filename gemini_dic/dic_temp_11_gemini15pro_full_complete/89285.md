homodromous:/ˌhɒməˈdrɒməs/| adj.|同向旋转的，顺行的|Running or turning in the same direction. Referring specifically to leaf arrangement on a stem where the genetic spiral follows the same direction as the spiral formed by connecting the bases of successively higher leaves.|The homodromous arrangement of leaves is common in many plant species.|近义词：concurrent, codirectional|反义词：antidromous|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Greek *homós* "same" + *drómos* "running, course".
