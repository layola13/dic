failingnesses:/ˈfeɪlɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
缺点，不足之处
The tendency to fail or the state of being deficient.
His greatest failingnesses were impatience and lack of attention to detail. 他最大的缺点是不耐烦和缺乏对细节的关注。
shortcomings, weaknesses, deficiencies, flaws
strengths, virtues
