bejumble:/bɪˈdʒʌmbəl/ | vt. | 使混乱；使困惑 | To mix things up in a confused or disordered way. | The strong winds bejumbled the papers on my desk.  大风把我桌上的文件吹得乱七八糟。| Synonyms: confuse, muddle, disarrange, disorder, jumble | Antonyms: organize, arrange, order, tidy | verb

bejumble:/bɪˈdʒʌmbəl/ | adj. | 混乱的；困惑的 |  Mixed up in a confused or disordered way. (Less common as an adjective) | The bejumbled wires made it difficult to find the right connection. 杂乱的电线使得很难找到正确的连接。 | Synonyms: confused, muddled, disarranged, disordered, jumbled | Antonyms: organized, arranged, ordered, tidy | adjective
