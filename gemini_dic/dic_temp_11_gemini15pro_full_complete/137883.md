nonvisible:/nɒnˈvɪzəbl/ | adj. | 看不见的，非可见的 | Not visible; incapable of being seen. | The bacteria are nonvisible to the naked eye. 这些细菌肉眼是看不见的。 |  The spy used nonvisible ink to write the secret message.  这个间谍用隐形墨水写了密信。 | 近义词: invisible, unseen, imperceptible | 反义词: visible, seen, perceptible | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + visible (from Latin *visibilis, from videre "to see")
