aureate:/ˈɔːriət/| adj.|金色的，华丽的，（文风）绚丽的|Characterized by ornate and elaborate language; resembling gold.|Her aureate prose was reminiscent of the Victorian era. 她华丽的散文让人想起维多利亚时代。|近义词：golden, gilded, ornate, flowery, flamboyant|反义词：plain, simple, unadorned, austere|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *aureatus*, past participle of *aureare*  "to gild," from *aureus* "golden," from *aurum* "gold."
