adorement:/əˈdɔːrmənt/ | n. | 敬爱，爱慕，崇拜 | The feeling of adoration; worshipful love. |  She looked at him with adorement in her eyes. 她眼里充满了对他的爱慕。 | 近义词: adoration, worship, reverence, devotion | 反义词: disdain, contempt, hatred | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *adorement*, from adorer (to adore) + -ment (suffix forming nouns from verbs).  Ultimately from Latin adorare (to worship).
