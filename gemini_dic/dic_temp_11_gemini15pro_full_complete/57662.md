Derived from a character in George Villiers, 2nd Duke of Buckingham's play, *The Rehearsal* (1671), who boasts of his prowess and vanquishes multiple opponents at once. | He acted like a real drawcansir, boasting about his accomplishments to anyone who would listen. 他表现得像个十足的夸夸其谈的武士，向任何愿意听的人吹嘘自己的成就。 |  The new manager was a real drawcansir, ordering everyone around and taking credit for their work.  这位新经理真是个傲慢自大的人，对每个人颐指气使，还把他们的功劳据为己有。| synonym: braggart, bully, swaggerer, blusterer | antonym: coward, milksop | noun
