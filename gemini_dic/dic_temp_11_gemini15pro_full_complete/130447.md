nonabsolutistic:/ˌnɒnˌæbsəˈluːtɪstɪk/
adjective
非绝对主义的
Not characterized by absolutism; rejecting absolute principles or the exercise of unlimited power.
The philosopher advocated for a nonabsolutistic approach to morality, arguing that ethical principles should be flexible and adaptable to different situations. 哲学家提倡一种非绝对主义的道德方法，认为伦理原则应该是灵活的，能够适应不同的情况。
The government adopted a nonabsolutistic stance on freedom of speech, acknowledging that some restrictions might be necessary in certain circumstances. 政府对言论自由采取了非绝对主义的立场，承认在某些情况下可能需要一些限制。
近义词: relativistic, flexible, adaptable, pragmatic
反义词: absolutist, dogmatic, rigid, inflexible
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + absolute + -ist (suffix forming nouns and adjectives) + -ic (suffix forming adjectives)
