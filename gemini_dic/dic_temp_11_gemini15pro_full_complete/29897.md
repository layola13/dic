cauterize:/ˈkɔːtəraɪz/| v. | 烧灼；使麻木；使无感觉 | To burn (tissue) with a hot iron, electric current, or a caustic substance, typically to stop bleeding or prevent infection. | The doctor cauterized the wound to stop the bleeding. 医生烧灼伤口以止血。

The intense heat cauterized the nerve endings, reducing the pain.  高温烧灼了神经末梢，减轻了疼痛。 | 近义词：burn, sear, singe | 反义词: heal | 词性: 动词
