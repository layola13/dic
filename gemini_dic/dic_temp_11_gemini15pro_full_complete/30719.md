cerumen:/səˈruːmən/ /sɪˈruːmən/
n.
耳垢，耳屎
A yellowish waxy substance secreted in the ear canal of humans and other mammals.
Earwax is a natural substance that helps protect the ear canal from dust, dirt, and other foreign particles.  耳垢是一种天然物质，有助于保护耳道免受灰尘、污垢和其他异物的侵害。
Cerumen impaction can cause hearing loss. 耳垢堵塞会导致听力损失。
近义词:earwax
反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "clean ear canal")
