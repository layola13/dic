decatoic: /dɪˈkeɪtɔɪk/ | adj. |癸酸的; 十碳的| Relating to or derived from decanoic acid (capric acid), a saturated fatty acid with ten carbon atoms. | Decatoic acid is found in coconut oil and palm kernel oil. 癸酸存在于椰子油和棕榈仁油中。|  Decatoic acid is also known as capric acid. 癸酸也称为羊脂酸。| 近义词: capric | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific fatty acid) | 词性: adjective
