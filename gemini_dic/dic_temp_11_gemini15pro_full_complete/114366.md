majorism:/ˈmeɪdʒərɪzəm/| n.|多数主义，多数统治|The principle or practice of majoritarianism, where the decisions of a group are made by the majority of its members.|Majorism can lead to the marginalization of minority groups. 多数主义可能导致少数群体边缘化。| The country's political system is based on majorism. 这个国家的政治制度建立在多数主义之上。 |近义词：majority rule|反义词：minority rights|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *majorismus, from Latin major "greater".
