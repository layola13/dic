onal:/ˈoʊnəl/ | adj. | 拥有的，所属的 (通常不单独使用，而是作为后缀) |  "-onal" is a suffix used to form adjectives meaning "relating to" or "of the nature of". It is not typically used as a standalone word. | This suffix appears in words like "national" (relating to a nation), "personal" (relating to a person), and "seasonal" (relating to a season). | 近义词: belonging to, relating to | 反义词: unrelated to | suffix
