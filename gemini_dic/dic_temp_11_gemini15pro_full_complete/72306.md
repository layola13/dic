flouring:/ˈflaʊərɪŋ/| adj. | 开花的；繁荣的；兴盛的|Characterized by flowering or flourishing.|The flowering plants attract many bees. 这些开花植物吸引了许多蜜蜂。|近义词：blooming, blossoming, thriving, prospering|反义词:withering, declining, decaying|词性:adjective
