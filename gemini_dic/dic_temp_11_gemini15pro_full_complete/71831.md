fleetingness:/ˈfliːtɪŋnəs/ | n. |短暂，转瞬即逝 |The fleetingness of youth is a common theme in literature. 青春的短暂是文学中的常见主题。|例句：The fleetingness of the moment made it all the more precious.  这一刻的短暂让它更加珍贵。

The fleetingness of happiness makes us cherish it even more. 幸福的短暂让我们更加珍惜它。


Synonyms: transience, ephemerality, brevity, impermanence
Antonyms: permanence, eternity, perpetuity
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From fleeting + -ness.  Fleeting comes from Middle English *fletinge, from Old English flēotende (“floating, swimming, flowing”), present participle of flēotan (“to float, swim, flow”).
