at:/æt, ət/| prep.|在, 于, 向, 对准, 从事于, 因为, 以(某种价格或速度)|
Etymology: From Old English æt, from Proto-Germanic *at, from Proto-Indo-European *ad- (“to, at”). Cognates include Old Frisian et, Old Saxon at, Old High German az, Gothic 𐌰𐍄 (at), Latin ad.
|例句：The meeting is at 3 p.m. 会议在下午3点举行。

|例句：He's good at math. 他擅长数学。

|近义词：in, on, by, near, toward|
|反义词: away from|
|词性: 介词 (preposition)|
