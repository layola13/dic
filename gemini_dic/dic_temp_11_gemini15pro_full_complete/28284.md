capturable:/ˈkæptʃərəbl/| adj.|可捕获的，可俘虏的|Able to be captured or taken.|例句：The escaped prisoner was considered highly capturable.  这名逃犯被认为很容易被抓获。

例句：The city was capturable due to its weak defenses.  由于防御薄弱，这座城市很容易被攻陷。|近义词：seizable, vulnerable, conquerable|反义词:uncapturable, impregnable, invincible|词性:adjective
词源:From capture (v.) + -able. Capture from Latin *captiō, *captāre, frequentative of capere "to take, hold, seize".
