expansionism:/ɪkˈspænʃənɪzəm/| n. |扩张主义，扩张政策|A policy or doctrine of expanding a country's territory or influence, often by aggressive means.|例句：The late 19th century was a period of intense European expansionism. 19世纪晚期是欧洲强烈扩张主义的时期。

例句：Many critics see American foreign policy as driven by expansionism. 许多批评家认为美国外交政策是由扩张主义驱动的。|近义词：imperialism, colonialism, annexationism|反义词:isolationism, pacifism|词性:名词

词源: From expand + -sion + -ism.  Ultimately from Latin expandere "to spread out."
