distillation:/ˌdɪstɪˈleɪʃn/ | n. | 蒸馏；提炼；精粹 | the action of purifying a liquid by a process of heating and cooling. |  The whisky undergoes a double distillation process.  这威士忌经过两次蒸馏过程。| purification, refinement, extraction | dilution, contamination | noun


distil:/dɪˈstɪl/ | v. | 蒸馏；提纯；渗出；提取…的精华 | extract the essential meaning or most important aspects of. |  The notes I took at the lecture distilled the essence of his argument. 我在讲座上做的笔记提取了他论证的精髓。| purify, refine, extract, concentrate | contaminate, dilute | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *distillationem, from Latin distillare "to drip down," from dis- "down" + stillare "to drip," from stilla "drop."
