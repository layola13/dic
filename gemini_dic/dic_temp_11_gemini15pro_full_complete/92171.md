hyperspace:/ˈhaɪpərspeɪs/| n. |超空间|A fictional space of more than three dimensions, through which spacecraft in science fiction stories can travel faster than light.|The spaceship jumped to hyperspace to escape the enemy. 飞船跳跃到超空间以躲避敌人。|Synonyms: subspace, warp space|Antonyms: normal space|noun
