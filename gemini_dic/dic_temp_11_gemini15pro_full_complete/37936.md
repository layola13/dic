completest:/kəmˈpliːtɪst/ | adj. | 最完整的，最完全的 |  Superlative form of "complete," meaning having all necessary parts or elements; entirely finished.  It suggests the highest degree of completeness. | This is the completest collection of antique toys I've ever seen. 这是我见过的最完整的古董玩具收藏。|  She gave the completest account of the accident. 她对事故做了最完整的描述。 |  Near synonyms: most complete, total, absolute, thorough, utter | Near antonyms: incomplete, partial, deficient, lacking | adjective
