heroicities:/ˌhɛrəʊˈɪsɪtiz/
|n.|英雄事迹；英勇行为|Instances of great bravery or courage, often pluralized to emphasize multiple acts or qualities.|The soldier's heroicities during the battle were celebrated by his comrades.  这位士兵在战斗中的英勇行为受到了战友们的赞扬。|Synonyms: acts of heroism, brave deeds, valorous feats|Antonyms: cowardices, acts of cowardice|noun

heroicity:/ˌhɛrəʊˈɪsɪti/
|n.|英雄品质；英勇|The quality of being heroic; bravery, courage.|The heroicity of the firefighters saved countless lives. 消防员的英勇精神拯救了无数生命。|Synonyms: heroism, bravery, valor, courage|Antonyms: cowardice, timidity|noun
