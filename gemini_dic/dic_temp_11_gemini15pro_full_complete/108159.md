latitudinally:/ˌlætɪˈtjuːdɪnəli/| adv.|纬度地，横向地|In a latitudinal direction; along a parallel of latitude; from east to west or west to east.|The temperature varies latitudinally. 温度会随着纬度变化。|Synonyms: horizontally, east-west, across latitudes|Antonyms: longitudinally, north-south, along meridians|adverb
