hackamore:/ˈhækəmɔːr (US), ˈhækəmɔː(r) (UK)| n.|无勒缰绳; hackamore bitless bridle|A hackamore is a type of bridle that does not have a bit. It is often used for training young horses or for horses with mouth injuries.|The trainer used a hackamore on the young colt.  训练员在小马驹上使用了无勒缰绳。|

The hackamore allows for more subtle communication with the horse. 无勒缰绳可以与马进行更微妙的沟通。|近义词: bitless bridle, bosal|反义词: bridle (with a bit)|词性:noun
