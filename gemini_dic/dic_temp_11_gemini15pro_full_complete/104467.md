kaiserism: /ˈkaɪzərɪzəm/
| n. |
| 皇帝主义；专制统治；军国主义 |
| A policy of extending an empire by the use of military force, characterized by strong centralized government and aggressive nationalism, as practiced by the German Empire under Kaiser Wilhelm II before and during World War I. |
|  Historians debate the extent to which Kaiserism was responsible for the outbreak of World War I.  历史学家们争论皇帝主义对第一次世界大战爆发的责任有多大。 |

|  The rise of Kaiserism in Germany led to an arms race with Britain. 德国皇帝主义的兴起导致了与英国的军备竞赛。 |
| imperialism, militarism, authoritarianism, despotism |
| democracy, pacifism |
| noun |
