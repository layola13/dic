drawee:/drɔːˈiː/
n.
付款人；受票人
The person or bank ordered to pay a check or draft.
The drawee of the check is the bank. 这张支票的付款人是银行。

The drawee is responsible for paying the draft upon presentation. 付款人有责任在出示汇票时付款。

近义词:payer, payee (in certain contexts)
反义词:drawer

词性:noun
