cooperage:/ˈkuːpərɪdʒ/ /ˈkɑːpərɪdʒ/
n.
制桶业；桶匠职业；桶匠工场；（总称）桶
The art or craft of making barrels and casks.  A place where barrels and casks are made. Barrels and casks collectively.
The cooperage produced hundreds of oak barrels for the winery. 这家制桶厂为酒厂生产了数百个橡木桶。

近义词:barrel making, cask making
反义词:None

词性:noun
