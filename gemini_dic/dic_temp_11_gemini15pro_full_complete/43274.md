craftless:/ˈkræftləs/
adjective
无技巧的，笨拙的，拙劣的
Lacking skill, dexterity, or art; clumsy or inartistic.
Her craftless attempt at knitting resulted in a tangled mess of yarn. 她笨拙的编织尝试导致一堆乱糟糟的纱线。
The painting was a craftless imitation of a masterpiece. 这幅画是对杰作拙劣的模仿。
近义词: clumsy, awkward, unskilled, inept, maladroit
反义词: skillful, adept, dexterous, artful, adroit
词性: adjective
