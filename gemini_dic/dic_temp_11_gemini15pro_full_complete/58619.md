duluth:/dəˈluːθ/| n. |德卢斯（美国明尼苏达州城市）|A port city in eastern Minnesota, on Lake Superior.  It is the westernmost point of the Great Lakes.|例句：I visited Duluth last summer. 我去年夏天去了德卢斯。

|例句：Duluth is known for its scenic beauty. 德卢斯以其风景秀丽而闻名。|近义词：city, port|反义词:rural area|词性:noun


词源: Named after Daniel Greysolon, Sieur du Lhut (or Duluth), a French explorer and trader who visited the area in the late 17th century.
