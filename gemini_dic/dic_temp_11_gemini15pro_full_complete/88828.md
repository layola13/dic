hogget:/ˈhɒɡɪt/ (UK) /ˈhɑːɡɪt/ (US)| n. |一岁半到两岁的绵羊|A young sheep between the ages of one and two years, or one that has not yet been shorn.|例句：The farmer sold all his hoggets at the market. 农夫在市场上卖掉了所有的一岁羊。

例句：The hoggets grazed peacefully in the field.  那些一岁羊在田野里平静地吃草。|近义词：sheep, yearling, lamb (younger)|反义词: (no direct opposite - perhaps mature sheep, ewe, ram)|词性:noun
