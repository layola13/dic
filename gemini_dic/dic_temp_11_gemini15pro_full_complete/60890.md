elementary:/ˌelɪˈmentri/ | adj. | 基本的，初级的，简单的 | Relating to the basic or fundamental principles of a subject; of or relating to the first stages of a process or course of study. |  This is an elementary question in mathematics. 这是数学中的一个基本问题。

Elementary school teaches children the basics of reading and writing. 小学教孩子们基本的阅读和写作。| 近义词: basic, fundamental, rudimentary, introductory, primary | 反义词: advanced, complex, complicated, sophisticated | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin elementarius, from Latin elementum ‘element’.
