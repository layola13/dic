boon:/buːn/
n.
恩惠，好处，福气
something that is very helpful or beneficial.
The rain was a boon to the farmers. 这场雨对农民来说是场及时雨。

近义词:blessing, advantage, benefit, windfall
反义词:bane, curse, disadvantage, misfortune
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *bón "a petition, prayer," from Proto-Germanic *bōniz "prayer, request."  The sense evolution went from "something requested" to "something granted as a favor" to "a blessing, benefit."
