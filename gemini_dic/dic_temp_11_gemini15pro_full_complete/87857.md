hideous:/ˈhɪdiəs/ | adj. | 可怕的，丑陋的 | Extremely ugly or unpleasant; causing horror or disgust. | The room was decorated in a hideous shade of green. 房间被装饰成一种可怕的绿色。|  The special effects in the horror movie were truly hideous. 这部恐怖电影里的特效真是太可怕了。| 近义词:ugly, frightful, ghastly, horrific, monstrous, repulsive | 反义词:beautiful, attractive, lovely, pleasant | 词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *hidous, from Anglo-Norman hidous, from Old French hideus (“horrible, dreadful”), from hisde (“horror, dread”), of uncertain origin.
