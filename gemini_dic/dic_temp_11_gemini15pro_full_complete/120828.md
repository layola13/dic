misauthorizes:/ˌmɪsˈɔːθəˌraɪzɪz/
verb
未经授权；错误授权
to give authorization incorrectly or without proper authority.
The clerk misauthorized the refund and the customer received twice the amount due.  店员错误地授权了退款，顾客收到了两倍的金额。
The intern misauthorized the release of confidential information. 实习生未经授权发布了机密信息。
synonyms: misapprove, disallow, refuse, reject
antonyms: authorize, approve, permit, sanction
词性: verb
