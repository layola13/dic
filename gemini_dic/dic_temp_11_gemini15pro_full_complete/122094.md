mistilled:/mɪˈstɪld/ | adj. | 蒸馏错误的；不正当地酿造的 | Incorrectly or improperly distilled. |  The mistilled whiskey had a strange, unpleasant taste. 这蒸馏错误的威士忌有一种奇怪的、令人不快的味道。| The moonshiners were arrested for producing mistilled spirits.  这些私酒贩子因生产非法蒸馏的烈酒而被捕。 | 近义词: improperly distilled, wrongly distilled | 反义词: properly distilled, correctly distilled | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + "distilled" (past participle of "distill").
