lilted:/ˈlɪltɪd/| v.|轻快地抑扬顿挫地说,用轻快的抑扬顿挫的语调说|Her voice lilted as she described her trip to Ireland. 她描述爱尔兰之旅时，语调轻快抑扬。|Synonyms: sing-song, melodic, modulated|Antonyms: monotone, flat, droning|verb

Etymology:  From lilt + -ed.  "Lilt" itself likely comes from Scottish Gaelic or Irish, related to words for singing or humming.
