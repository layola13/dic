bobance: /bəˈbɑːns/ /ˈbɑːbəns/ | vi.| 昂首阔步；炫耀 | To move or walk with a bobance; to display oneself ostentatiously. |  He bobanced into the room, full of self-importance. 他趾高气扬地走进房间，充满了自负。 | 近义词：swagger, strut, prance, boast, brag | 反义词：cringe, recoil | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *bobancer*, of uncertain origin, perhaps related to *bombancer* "to make a loud noise," from *bombe* "bomb."  The word suggests a puffed-up or inflated quality, both literally in terms of gait and figuratively in terms of demeanor.
