nationalisms:/ˈnæʃənəlɪzəmz/
n.
民族主义（复数）
Doctrines or movements promoting the interests of a particular nation, especially with the aim of gaining and maintaining sovereignty (self-government) over its homeland.  Nationalisms can also refer to the various forms that nationalism can take.
The rise of nationalisms in the 19th century led to the creation of many new nation-states. 19世纪民族主义的兴起导致了许多新民族国家的建立。

Nationalisms across the globe are complex and varied. 全球各地的民族主义都很复杂且各不相同。

近义词: patriotism, nationalist sentiment
反义词: internationalism, globalism
词性: noun (plural)
