forgo:/fɔːrˈɡoʊ/ (US) /fɔːˈɡəʊ/ (UK)| v.|放弃，摒弃|To abstain from; to give up (something pleasant or valuable).|She decided to forgo dessert in order to lose weight. 她为了减肥决定不吃甜点。

He had to forgo his vacation to finish the project. 他不得不放弃休假来完成这个项目。|近义词：relinquish, sacrifice, abstain from, waive|反义词：indulge in, partake of|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *forgān "to go without, relinquish," from for- "away, without" + gān "to go."
