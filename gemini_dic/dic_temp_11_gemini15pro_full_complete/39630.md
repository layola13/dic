consequent-on:/ˈkɒnsɪkwənt ɒn/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsɪkwent ɑːn/ (US)| adj. |作为…的结果；随之发生的|Resulting from something; following as a natural effect. |例句：The flooding was consequent on the heavy rain. 洪水是暴雨造成的。|例句：His dismissal was consequent on his persistent lateness. 他被解雇是因为他经常迟到。|近义词：resulting from, following, ensuing, subsequent to, as a result of |反义词:prior to, preceding |词性:形容词
