nabobically:/ˌneɪˈbɑːbɪkəli/
adverb|以富豪的方式；奢侈地|In a manner characteristic of a nabob; luxuriously.
The retired couple lived nabobically, traveling the world and indulging in fine dining. 这对退休夫妇过着富豪般的生活，环游世界，享受精致的餐饮。|
近义词:luxuriously, lavishly, opulently, extravagantly
反义词:frugally, modestly, simply, plainly
词性:adverb

词源:
From nabob + -ical + -ly.  Nabob comes from Hindi nawwāb, from Arabic nuwwāb (plural of nā’ib, “deputy”).
