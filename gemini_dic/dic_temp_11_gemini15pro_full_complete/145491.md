overcloak:/ˈoʊvərˌkloʊk/
n.
掩饰；伪装；幌子
something that hides, covers, or disguises something else.
The spy used an overcloak of normalcy to blend in with the crowd.  间谍用平常的外表作为掩饰，混入人群中。
He used charity work as an overcloak for his illegal activities. 他用慈善工作来掩盖他的非法活动。
近义词：disguise, cover, cloak, veil, facade
反义词：revelation, exposure, disclosure
词性: 名词

overcloak:/ˈoʊvərˌkloʊk/
v.
掩盖；掩饰；遮掩
to hide, cover, or disguise something.
Her smile overcloaked her true feelings of sadness.  她的笑容掩盖了她真正的悲伤。
The company attempted to overcloak the scandal, but the truth eventually emerged.  公司试图掩盖丑闻，但真相最终还是浮出水面了。
近义词：conceal, disguise, mask, hide, obscure
反义词：reveal, expose, uncover
词性: 动词


词源:
From "over-" (meaning "above" or "excessively") + "cloak" (a garment worn over other clothes for warmth or protection, also used figuratively to mean "something that conceals").  由"over-"（意为“在...之上”或“过度地”）和"cloak"（一件穿在外衣外面的衣服，用于保暖或保护，也用于比喻意义上的“掩盖某物的东西”）组成。
