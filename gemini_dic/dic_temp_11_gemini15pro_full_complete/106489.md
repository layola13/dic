krabby:/ˈkræbi/| adj.|暴躁的，易怒的|Bad-tempered and irritable.  Relating to or resembling a crab, especially in being irritable or difficult to deal with.|例句：He's been very krabby lately due to lack of sleep.  他最近因为睡眠不足而非常暴躁。

例句：She gave me a krabby look when I asked her to work overtime. 当我让她加班时，她给了我一个恼怒的眼神。|近义词：grumpy, irritable, crabby, cantankerous, surly|反义词：pleasant, cheerful, amiable, good-natured|词性:形容词
词源:  Likely a blend of "crab" and "crabbed" (meaning ill-tempered), influenced by the perceived grumpy demeanor of crabs.
