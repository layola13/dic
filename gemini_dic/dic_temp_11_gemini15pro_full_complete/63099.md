enshrined:/ɪnˈʃraɪnd/ /ɛnˈʃraɪnd/| v. | 供奉，珍藏；铭记；把…奉为神圣 | to preserve (a right, tradition, etc.) in a form that ensures it will be protected and respected | The right to free speech is enshrined in the First Amendment.  言论自由权被载入美国宪法第一修正案。| synonyms: sanctify, consecrate, hallow | antonyms: desecrate, profane | verb
