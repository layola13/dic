energises:/ˈenədʒaɪzɪz/
verb
使充满活力，使活跃，激励
Gives energy to; invigorates.
The coach's speech energised the team before the final game. 教练的讲话在决赛前激励了球队。
energizes (American English)
verb
使充满活力，使活跃，激励
Gives energy to; invigorates.
The new CEO's vision energizes the entire company. 新任首席执行官的愿景激励了整个公司。

synonyms: invigorates, stimulates, revitalizes, enlivens, boosts, empowers
antonyms: weakens, drains, exhausts, fatigues, enervates
词性: 动词


词源: From energy + -ize.  Ultimately from Greek energeia.
