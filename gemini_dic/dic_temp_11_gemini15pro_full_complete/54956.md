disprince:/dɪsˈprɪns/
v. | 剥夺王子身份；废黜 | To deprive of the rank or status of a prince; to depose from princely power. | The king disprinced his rebellious son. 国王废黜了他叛逆的儿子。|  depose, dethrone, oust |  enthrone, crown | verb

disprince:/dɪsˈprɪns/
n. | 被废黜的王子 | A prince who has been deprived of his rank or status. | The disprince lived a quiet life after being deposed.  被废黜的王子在被罢免后过着平静的生活。 | ex-prince, deposed prince | reigning prince, crown prince | noun
