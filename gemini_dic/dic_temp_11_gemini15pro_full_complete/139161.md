obi: /ˈoʊbiː/ | n. | 腰带，腰带（尤指日本和服的宽腰带）| A broad sash, worn around the waist, especially as part of a Japanese kimono. | She tied her obi in a beautiful knot. 她把腰带系成了一个漂亮的结。|  The obi was made of silk and intricately embroidered. 这条腰带是用丝绸做的，绣得很复杂。 | 近义词: sash, belt, girdle | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词

词源: From Japanese 帯 (obi).
