leaner:/ˈliːnər/| adj.|更瘦的，更苗条的；更精简的，更有效的|Someone or something that is leaner is thinner or contains less fat than before.  A leaner organization is one that has been made smaller or more efficient by reducing the number of people working for it.|She's much leaner now that she's exercising regularly. 她现在经常锻炼，瘦多了。|近义词:thinner, slimmer, slenderer, more efficient, streamlined|反义词:fatter, heavier, plumper, less efficient, bloated|adjective

leaner:/ˈliːnər/| n.|学习者|Someone who is learning something.|He's a quick learner and picked up the new software easily. 他学得很快，轻松掌握了新的软件。|近义词:student, pupil, apprentice, novice, beginner|反义词:expert, master, professional, teacher, instructor|noun
