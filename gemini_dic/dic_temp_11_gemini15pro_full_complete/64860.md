espousement:/ɪˈspaʊzmənt/
n.
拥护；信奉；支持；结婚
the act of supporting or adopting a cause, belief, or way of life; marriage.
She announced her espousement of the new political party. 她宣布拥护这个新的政党。
His espousement of socialist ideals led him to a life of activism. 他对社会主义理想的信奉使他走上了积极分子的生活道路。
近义词:adoption, embracement, support, advocacy, championship, marriage, wedding
反义词:rejection, renunciation, opposition, antagonism
