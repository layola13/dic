overfurthest:/ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrðɪst/ | adj. | 最远的；极远的 |  Located at the most distant point; furthest. This word emphasizes the extreme distance and is somewhat informal and less common than "furthest." |  He lives in the overfurthest corner of the county. 他住在该县最远的角落。|  The expedition reached the overfurthest point of their journey. 探险队到达了他们旅程的最远点。 | Synonyms: furthest, remotest, uttermost | Antonyms: nearest, closest | Word Class: Adjective
