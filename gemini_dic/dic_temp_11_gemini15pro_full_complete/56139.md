documentaries:/ˌdɒkjuˈmɛnt(ə)riz/ |n.|纪录片|Films or television or radio programmes that provide a factual record or report on a particular subject.|I enjoy watching nature documentaries. 我喜欢看自然纪录片。|近义词:films, non-fiction films|反义词:fictional films|noun

documentary:/ˌdɒkjuˈmɛnt(ə)ri/ |adj.|纪录的，纪实的|Consisting of or based on official documents.|The film is a documentary account of the war. 这部电影是对战争的纪实描述。|近义词:factual, non-fictional|反义词:fictional, imaginary|adjective
