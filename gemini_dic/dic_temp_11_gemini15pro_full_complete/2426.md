adjudged:/əˈdʒʌdʒd/| v. |判决；裁定；宣布；认为|Considered or declared to be, typically after some deliberation.|He was adjudged guilty of the crime. 他被判犯有此罪。|Synonyms: deemed, considered, pronounced, decreed, ruled, judged|Antonyms: acquitted, pardoned|verb

adjudged:/əˈdʒʌdʒd/| adj. |已判决的；已裁定的|Having been formally judged or decided.|The adjudged winner will receive a prize. 已判定的获胜者将获得奖品。|Synonyms: decided, determined, settled|Antonyms: undecided, undetermined, unsettled|adjective
