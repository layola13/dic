doake: /doʊk/ | n. | 沼泽，泥潭 | A bog or marsh, typically one that is treacherous. |  The horse sank deep into the doake. 马深深地陷入了沼泽。|  近义词: bog, marsh, swamp, mire | 反义词:  dry land | 词性: 名词


词源:  Likely of dialectal origin, possibly related to "dike" or "ditch," referencing areas where water collects.  The precise etymology is uncertain.
