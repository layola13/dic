nonplausible:/ˌnɒnˈplɔːzɪbl/
adjective
难以置信的，不可信的
not seeming reasonable or probable; failing to convince
例句：His explanation for his absence was nonplausible.  他缺席的解释令人难以置信。

例句：The plot of the movie was so nonplausible that I couldn't suspend my disbelief. 这部电影的情节太令人难以置信了，我无法让自己相信。

近义词：implausible, unbelievable, improbable, unconvincing
反义词：plausible, believable, credible, probable
词性: adjective
