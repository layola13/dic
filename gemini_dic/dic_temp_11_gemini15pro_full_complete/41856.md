coryphodont:/kɔːˈrɪfədɒnt/ | n. | 冠齿兽 | An extinct ungulate mammal of the Eocene epoch, having large, heavy teeth.  |  Coryphodonts were among the largest mammals of their time.  冠齿兽是它们那个时代最大的哺乳动物之一。| Example 2:  The coryphodont fossil was remarkably well-preserved. 这块冠齿兽化石保存得非常好。 | synonyms:  Eocene mammal, prehistoric mammal | antonyms:  N/A | noun
