lengthen:/ˈlɛŋθən/ (US), /ˈlɛŋθən/ (UK)| v.|加长，延长，变长|To make something longer in space or time.|例句：The days are lengthening. 白天变长了。|例句：They lengthened the road by five miles. 他们把道路延长了五英里。|近义词：extend, prolong, stretch|反义词:shorten, reduce, curtail|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From length + -en.  From Middle English *lengthnen, from Old English *lenȝþnian, from lenȝþu (“length”).
