knee-jerk:/ˈniː dʒɜːrk/ (US), /ˈniː dʒɜːk/ (UK)| adj.|下意识的，条件反射的，未经思考的|A knee-jerk reaction is one that is automatic and unthinking, often in response to a particular stimulus.|例句：He has a knee-jerk reaction against any form of regulation. 他对任何形式的监管都有下意识的抵触情绪。

例句：Her knee-jerk response was to deny everything. 她的下意识反应是否认一切。

近义词：automatic, instinctive, reflex, unthinking, impulsive|反义词：considered, deliberate, reasoned, thoughtful|词性：形容词

knee-jerk:/ˈniː dʒɜːrk/ (US), /ˈniː dʒɜːk/ (UK)| n.|膝跳反射|A sudden involuntary reflex extension of the leg in response to a sharp tap on the patellar tendon.|例句：The doctor tested her knee-jerk reflex. 医生检查了她的膝跳反射。

例句：A sharp tap just below the knee elicits the knee-jerk. 在膝盖下方轻敲一下会引起膝跳反射。

近义词：reflex|反义词：(没有直接的反义词)|词性：名词
