outbound:/ˈaʊtbaʊnd/| adj.|向外的，往外的；离开的；驶往外国的；发出的|Traveling away from a particular place, especially returning home.|例句：Outbound flights are often cheaper than return flights. 出发的航班通常比返程航班便宜。|近义词：departing, outgoing, egress, exiting|反义词:inbound, incoming, returning|adjective

outbound:/ˈaʊtbaʊnd/| n.|外发邮件；向外航线|Something that is outbound, such as a flight or a message.|例句：Outbound traffic is heavy during rush hour.  高峰时段外出交通拥堵。|近义词：departure, exit|反义词:arrival, entry|noun
