fabulize:/ˈfæbjʊlaɪz/| v. |编造；虚构；把…编成故事|To invent or create a story or a narrative, often based on slight or no foundation in reality.  |She fabulized a whole series of events that never actually happened. 她虚构了一系列从未发生过的事情。|He fabulized his childhood adventures to impress his friends. 他编造了童年冒险故事来打动他的朋友们。|近义词：invent, fabricate, fictionalize, romanticize|反义词：report, document, verify|词性:动词
