centurion:/sɛnˈtʊəriən/| n.|百夫长，百人队长|An officer commanding a century (originally a unit of a hundred men) in the ancient Roman army.|The centurion ordered his men to advance. 百夫长命令他的士兵前进。|例句：The film depicted the bravery of a Roman centurion. 这部电影描绘了一位罗马百夫长的英勇事迹。|近义词：commander, captain, officer|反义词:soldier, private|noun
