chafeweed:/ˈtʃeɪfˌwiːd/| n. |紫茎泽兰|A highly invasive plant species (Ageratina adenophora) native to Mexico and Central America, known for its aggressive growth and negative impacts on biodiversity.|The chafeweed has taken over the pasture, making it unusable for grazing. 紫茎泽兰侵占了牧场，使其无法放牧。|The spread of chafeweed is a serious threat to native ecosystems. 紫茎泽兰的蔓延对本地生态系统构成严重威胁。|Synonyms: crofton weed, eupatory, Mexican devil|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
