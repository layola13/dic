headwater:/ˈhɛdwɔːtər/ (US), /ˈhɛdwɔːtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |源头，上游，河源|The source of a stream or river.|例句：The dam was built near the headwaters of the river.  大坝建在河的源头附近。

例句：Protecting the headwaters is crucial for maintaining a healthy river ecosystem. 保护河流源头对于维护健康的河流生态系统至关重要。

近义词：source, origin, fountainhead|反义词: mouth, estuary|词性:名词
