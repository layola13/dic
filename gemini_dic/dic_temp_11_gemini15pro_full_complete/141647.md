optimistic: /ˌɒptɪˈmɪstɪk/ | adj. | 乐观的，乐观主义的 | Expecting good things to happen or something to be successful; hopeful. |  She's optimistic about the outcome of the election. 她对选举结果很乐观。|  He remained optimistic in the face of adversity. 面对逆境，他仍然保持乐观。 | 近义词: hopeful, positive, sanguine | 反义词: pessimistic, negative, gloomy | 词性: adjective | 词源: From Latin optimus (“best”) + -istic.
