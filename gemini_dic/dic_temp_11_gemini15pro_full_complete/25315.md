buggy's:/ˈbʌɡiz/ | n. | 轻便马车；婴儿车；购物车 |  A buggy is a small, light vehicle, originally horse-drawn, now usually with an engine. It can also refer to a baby carriage or a shopping cart. |  I saw a farmer driving his buggy down the road. 我看到一个农民开着他的轻便马车在路上行驶。 | The family took the buggy to the park. 这家人带着婴儿车去了公园。 | synonyms: carriage, stroller, cart | antonyms: truck, car | noun

buggy's:/ˈbʌɡiz/ | n. |  有故障的程序或系统的 (informal) | Buggy software or systems are full of errors and don't work properly.  This is an informal usage, especially common in computing contexts. | The new software is still a bit buggy. 新软件仍然有一些bug。 |  The game is so buggy it keeps crashing. 这个游戏bug太多了，总是崩溃。 | synonyms: flawed, glitchy, faulty | antonyms: perfect, flawless, functional | noun
