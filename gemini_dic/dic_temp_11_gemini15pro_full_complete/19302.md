bibasic:/baɪˈbeɪsɪk/
adj.
二碱的；二元的
Containing two atoms of hydrogen replaceable by basic atoms or radicals.
This bibasic acid can react with two molecules of sodium hydroxide.  这种二元酸可以和两分子的氢氧化钠反应。

The chemist studied the properties of the bibasic compound.  这位化学家研究了二元化合物的性质。
近义词:dibasic
反义词:monobasic
词性:adjective
