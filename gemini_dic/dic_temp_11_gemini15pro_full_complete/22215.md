bonapartean:/ˌboʊnəˈpɑːrtiən/
adjective
波拿巴的；拿破仑式的；专横的
Relating to or characteristic of Napoleon Bonaparte or his family, especially his authoritarian style of government.
His bonapartean ambition led him to seek absolute power. 他的拿破仑式野心使他追求绝对权力。
Synonyms: authoritarian, dictatorial, autocratic, tyrannical, despotic
Antonyms: democratic, egalitarian
词性: adjective
