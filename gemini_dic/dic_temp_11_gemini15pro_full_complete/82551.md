guaniferous:/ˌɡwɑːˈnɪfərəs/
adjective
含鸟粪的; 富含鸟粪的
Containing or rich in guano (bird droppings).
The guaniferous deposits on the islands were a valuable source of fertilizer.  这些岛屿上富含鸟粪的沉积物是宝贵的肥料来源。
The miners worked tirelessly extracting the guaniferous earth. 矿工们不知疲倦地开采着富含鸟粪的土壤。
Near synonyms:  guano-rich, nitrogenous, phosphatic
Antonyms:  barren, infertile
词性: adjective
