by-election:/ˌbaɪ ɪˈlɛkʃən/ | n. |补选|An election held to choose a new representative to replace one who has died or resigned before the end of their term.|The party is hoping to gain a seat in the upcoming by-election.  该党希望在即将到来的补选中获得一个席位。|Synonyms: special election, supplementary election|||noun
