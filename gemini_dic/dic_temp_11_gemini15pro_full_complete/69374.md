fee:/fiː/
n.
费用，酬金
A payment made to a professional person or to a professional or public body in exchange for advice or services.
例句：The lawyer's fee was very high. 律师的费用很高。
例句：There is no entrance fee to the museum.  博物馆不收门票。
近义词：charge, price, cost, payment
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps) waiver (if the fee is waived), free (if no fee is required)
词性: noun
