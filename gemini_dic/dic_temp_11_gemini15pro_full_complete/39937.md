constructable:/kənˈstrʌktəbl/ | adj. | 可建造的，可构造的 | Able to be constructed or built; capable of being put together or formed. | The architect assured us that the building was constructable within the given budget. 建筑师向我们保证，这座建筑物可以在给定的预算内建造。 | The bridge is constructable using prefabricated sections. 这座桥可以用预制构件建造。 |  near synonyms: buildable, erectable, makeable, feasible | antonyms:  unbuildable, impracticable | adjective

constructible:/kənˈstrʌktɪbl/ | adj. | 可作图的 (数学) |  (Mathematics) Able to be constructed using a compass and straightedge. |  A regular pentagon is constructible using a compass and straightedge. 正五边形可以用圆规和直尺作图。 |  Not all polygons are constructible with classical methods.  并非所有多边形都可以用经典方法作图。 | near synonyms: drawable (in geometry) | antonyms: non-constructible | adjective
