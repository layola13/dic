bolsterer:/ˈboʊlstərər/| n.|支持者，鼓励者|Someone or something that bolsters or supports something else.  A person who gives support or encouragement.|The new trade deal was a much-needed bolsterer for the struggling economy. 新的贸易协议对苦苦挣扎的经济来说是急需的支持。 The coach was a great bolsterer of team morale. 教练极大地鼓舞了团队士气。|近义词：supporter, advocate, proponent, champion|反义词：opponent, critic, detractor|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From bolster + -er.  Bolster comes from Old English *bolster, from Proto-Germanic *bulstraz. Ultimately from the same root as "belly."
