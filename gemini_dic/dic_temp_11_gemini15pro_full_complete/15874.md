ballistician:/ˌbælɪˈstɪʃən/| n. |弹道学家；弹道专家|An expert in ballistics. A person who studies the science of projectiles and firearms.|The ballistician examined the bullet trajectory to determine the location of the shooter. 弹道学家检查了子弹的轨迹以确定射击者的位置。|Synonyms: ballistics expert, firearms expert|Antonyms: N/A|noun
