USAGE->
Simple Present:
What do you do for a living? = 你靠什么谋生？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? = 他现在在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? = 她用这笔钱做了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading this week? = 他这周一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had happened before the police arrived? = 警察到达之前发生了什么事？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到今年年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做什么五年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
