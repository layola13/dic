affect-to:/əˈfɛkt tuː/
| v. |假装；佯装|To pretend to have or feel (something). To make a show of; feign.|He affected to be indifferent to her criticism, but I knew he was hurt. 他假装对她批评漠不关心，但我知道他很受伤。|例句：She affected to not hear him. 她假装没听见他说话。|近义词：pretend, feign, simulate, assume|反义词：reveal, expose|词性：动词

affect-to:/əˈfɛkt tuː/
