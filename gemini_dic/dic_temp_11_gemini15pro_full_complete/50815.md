describers:/dɪˈskraɪbərz/| n.|描述者，描述词|People who describe something or something that describes.  Those who give an account or representation of something in words.|The travel writer used vivid describers to paint a picture of the exotic locale.|Synonyms: narrators, depicters, reporters, commentators|Antonyms: listeners, observers (in a passive sense)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "describe" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Describe" comes from the Latin "describere," meaning "to write down" or "to copy."
