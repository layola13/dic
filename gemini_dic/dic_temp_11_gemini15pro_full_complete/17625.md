beblubber:/bɪˈblʌbər/
verb
哭诉，痛哭
To weep loudly and uncontrollably.
She beblubbered her story to anyone who would listen. 她向任何愿意倾听的人哭诉她的故事。
The child beblubbered when he lost his toy. 孩子丢了玩具后哭了起来。
近义词:cry, weep, sob, wail
反义词:laugh, chuckle, giggle
词性:v.

Etymology:
From be- + blubber, from Middle English bluberen, probably of imitative origin.
