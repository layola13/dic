bloomer:/ˈbluːmər/| n.|灯笼裤；完全失败的人（或事物）；大错；（尤指女子）初露头角的运动员；开花植物|A bloomer is a type of women's pants gathered at the knee or lower. It can also refer to a mistake, a failure, or a promising young athlete, particularly a woman.  In a botanical context, it refers to a flowering plant.|例句：
She wore a pair of bloomers for the cycling race. 她穿着一条灯笼裤参加自行车比赛。

Her first attempt at baking a cake was a complete bloomer. 她第一次尝试烤蛋糕完全失败了。

She's a real bloomer in the world of tennis. 她在网球界是一位真正崭露头角的运动员。

The garden is full of bloomers in the springtime. 春天花园里开满了鲜花。|近义词：pants, trousers, failure, flop, mistake, rising star, prospect|反义词:success, triumph, veteran|词性:noun
