overcrowdedly:/ˌoʊvərˈkraʊdɪdli/
|adv.|过于拥挤地；挤满地|In a way that is too full of people or things. |The refugees were overcrowdedly packed into the small boats.  难民们过于拥挤地挤在小船里。|近义词：densely, thickly|反义词: sparsely, thinly
|词性:副词
