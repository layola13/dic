nontolerablenesses:/nɒnˈtɒlərəbəlˌnɛsɪz/ | n. | 不可容忍性；无法忍受的事情 | The state or quality of being nontolerable; things that are nontolerable. |  The nontolerablenesses of his behavior led to his dismissal. 他的行为的不可容忍性导致他被解雇。 | Synonyms: intolerances, unacceptabilities | Antonyms: tolerances, acceptabilities | noun

Etymology:
From non- (not) + tolerable + -ness (state of being).  "Tolerable" comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin tolerabilis, from Latin tolerare ‘endure’.
