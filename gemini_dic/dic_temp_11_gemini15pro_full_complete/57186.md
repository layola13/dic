down-and-outer:/ˌdaʊn ən ˈaʊtər/| n. |潦倒的人；失意的人|A person who has suffered a decline in fortune and social position, often homeless or destitute.|He was a down-and-outer, sleeping on park benches and begging for change. 他是一个潦倒的人，睡在公园的长椅上，乞讨零钱。|近义词：bum, derelict, vagrant|反义词:successful person, prosperous individual|Noun

down-and-out:/ˌdaʊn ən ˈaʊt/| adj. |潦倒的；失意的|Destitute, impoverished, and having lost hope or status.|She felt down-and-out after losing her job and apartment. 她失去了工作和公寓后感到非常失意。|近义词：destitute, impoverished, ruined|反义词:prosperous, successful, affluent|Adjective
