beautiful: /ˈbjuːtɪfʊl/ (US) /ˈbjuːtɪfəl/ (UK) | adj. |美丽的，美好的|Pleasing the senses or mind aesthetically. |例句：She wore a beautiful dress. 她穿了一件漂亮的连衣裙。|例句：The scenery was absolutely beautiful.  景色美极了。|近义词：pretty, gorgeous, stunning, attractive, handsome|反义词：ugly, hideous, unattractive|词性: adjective
词源: From Middle English *beautevous, from Anglo-Norman *beautevous, bel, beau (“beautiful”) + -ous (“-ous”). Equivalent to beauty + -ful.
