corncrib:/ˈkɔːrnkrɪb/| n.|玉米仓，玉米 crib|A ventilated structure for storing ears of corn.|The farmer stored the harvest in the corncrib.|granary, silo|barn|noun
Etymology->
From corn + crib (meaning a rack or manger).  The word "crib" in this context originally referred to a structure with slatted sides allowing for ventilation, essential for drying and preserving corn.
