heretic:/ˈherɪtɪk/| n.|异教徒，异端邪说者|A person who holds religious beliefs that are contrary to the established doctrines of their church or religion. | Galileo was considered a heretic for his belief in heliocentrism.  伽利略因其日心说信仰被认为是异教徒。| synonym: dissenter, nonconformist, apostate, sectarian| antonym: orthodox, conformist | noun

heretical:/həˈretɪkl/| adj.|异端的，异教的|Relating to or characterized by heresy.|  His heretical views led to his excommunication from the church. 他的异端观点导致他被逐出教会。|synonym: unorthodox, dissenting, nonconformist, apostate, blasphemous| antonym: orthodox, conformist | adjective
