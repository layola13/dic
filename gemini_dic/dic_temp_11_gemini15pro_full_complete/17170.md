battling:/ˈbæt(ə)lɪŋ/
adjective|斗争的，搏斗的|engaged in a battle or struggle; fighting against something|The two armies were battling fiercely for control of the city. 两支军队为了控制这座城市而激烈战斗。|近义词:fighting, struggling, combating, contending|反义词:yielding, surrendering, submitting|词性:形容词
battling:/ˈbæt(ə)lɪŋ/
verb|斗争，搏斗|present participle of "battle"|They are battling against the disease. 他们在与疾病作斗争。|近义词:fighting, struggling, combating, contending|反义词:yielding, surrendering, submitting|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *batelen, from Old French bataillier, from Late Latin battualiare (“to fight, fence”), from Latin battuere (“to beat, strike”).
