fissigemmation:/ˌfɪsɪdʒɛˈmeɪʃən/ | n. |裂殖生殖|Asexual reproduction by fission or splitting, seen in some invertebrates like corals and sea anemones. |Coral reefs grow through fissigemmation, where polyps divide to form new individuals.|Synonyms: fission, splitting, fragmentation|Antonyms: sexual reproduction|noun
