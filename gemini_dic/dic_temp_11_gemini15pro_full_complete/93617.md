imbibed:/ɪmˈbaɪbd/ | v. |吸收，吸取（液体），接受（思想、知识等）| To drink (alcohol); to absorb or assimilate (ideas or knowledge). |  He imbibed a large quantity of beer. 他喝了大量的啤酒。

She imbibed the wisdom of her teachers. 她汲取了老师们的智慧。| 近义词：absorb, assimilate, drink, consume, ingest | 反义词：excrete, expel, reject |词性:动词
