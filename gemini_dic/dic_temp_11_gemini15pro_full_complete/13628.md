attired:/əˈtaɪərd/
adjective
穿着…的；打扮的
dressed in a particular way, especially in fine or special clothes
例句：She was attired in a long, flowing gown. 她穿着一件飘逸的长袍。

例句：The king was attired in his royal robes. 国王穿着他的皇袍。

近义词：dressed, clad, clothed, arrayed, garbed
反义词：undressed, naked, unclothed
词性: adjective
