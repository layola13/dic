frustum:/ˈfrʌstəm/| n.|平截头体；截头锥体；截锥体|The frustum of a cone or pyramid is the part that remains after its upper part has been cut off by a plane parallel to its base.  圆锥或棱锥的平截头体是指其上部被平行于底面的平面截去后剩余的部分。|The lampshade was shaped like a frustum.  灯罩的形状像一个平截头体。|近义词:section, segment|反义词: (No direct antonym for a geometric shape)|noun
