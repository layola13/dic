anneal:/əˈniːl/| v. |使退火；使缓和；使变坚韧|To heat (metal or glass) and allow it to cool slowly, in order to remove internal stresses and toughen it.  To strengthen or toughen (something, especially metal or glass) by heating and then cooling it slowly. To reduce brittleness and improve toughness by heating and slowly cooling.|The glass is annealed to make it stronger.  玻璃经过退火处理以增强强度。|Synonyms: temper, toughen, harden, strengthen|Antonyms: weaken, soften, brittle|verb

annealed:/əˈniːld/| adj. |退火的；缓和的|Heated and then cooled slowly to make it less brittle. Having been subjected to annealing.|Annealed steel is less brittle. 退火钢的脆性较小。|Synonyms: tempered, toughened, hardened, strengthened|Antonyms: weakened, softened, brittle|adjective
