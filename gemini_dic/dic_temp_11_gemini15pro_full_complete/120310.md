minicyclone:/ˈmɪniˌsaɪkloʊn/| n. |小型气旋，微型气旋|A small-scale atmospheric vortex, resembling a cyclone but smaller in size and intensity.  It is often used to describe dust devils or whirlwinds.|Example 1: A minicyclone of dust swirled across the dry field. 一股小型尘卷风席卷了干燥的田野。

Example 2: The sudden minicyclone picked up leaves and debris from the garden.  突然出现的微型气旋卷起了花园里的树叶和杂物。|近义词：whirlwind, dust devil, vortex|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
