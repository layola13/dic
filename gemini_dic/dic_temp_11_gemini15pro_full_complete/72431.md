fluidest:/ˈfluːɪdɪst/
adjective
最流畅的，最流动的
smoothest, most graceful and effortless
Her movements were fluidest when she was dancing. 她跳舞时动作最流畅。
近义词:  smoothest, slickest, most graceful, most flowing
反义词:  stiffest, most rigid, clunkiest, least flowing
词性: adjective

fluidest:/ˈfluːɪdɪst/
词源: From fluid + -est (superlative suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *fluidus, from fluere "to flow."
中文翻译:最流畅的，最流动的
英文解析:Superlative form of fluid.  Used to describe something that flows or moves with the greatest ease and grace.  Often used to describe liquids, movements, or styles.
例句:The fluidest dancer in the company captivated the audience.  该舞蹈团中最流畅的舞者吸引了观众。

例句:His writing style is fluidest when he's writing about nature. 当他写关于自然的东西时，他的写作风格最为流畅。
