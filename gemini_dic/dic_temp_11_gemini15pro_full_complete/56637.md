donna:/ˈdɑːnə/ (US), /ˈdɒnə/ (UK)| n.|女士，夫人；（意大利语）女士，夫人|A respectful title for a woman, especially in Italian.  Donna is the Italian equivalent of Mrs. or Ms.|例句：Donna Lucia is a renowned opera singer.  露西亚女士是一位著名的歌剧演唱家。|例句：In Italy, it's polite to address a woman as "Donna" followed by her first name. 在意大利，称呼女性为“Donna”加上她的名字是礼貌的。|近义词：woman, lady, madam, signora (Italian)|反义词:man, gentleman, signore (Italian)|词性:noun
