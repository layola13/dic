insipient:/ɪnˈsɪpiənt/| adj.|初期的，刚开始的|Beginning to exist or appear; in an initial stage.|例句：He showed incipient signs of a cold. 他出现了感冒的初期症状。|例句：The project is still in its incipient stages. 这个项目还处于起步阶段。|近义词：beginning, nascent, developing, embryonic, initial, commencing|反义词：advanced, developed, mature, established|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *incipientem*, present participle of *incipere* "to begin," from *in-* "in, on" + *capere* "to take, seize."
