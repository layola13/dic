inflectionless:/ɪnˈflɛkʃənləs/| adj.|无屈折变化的，无词形变化的|Lacking inflections; not undergoing inflection.  Referring to a word or language that does not change form to express grammatical relations such as tense, number, or case.|The language is largely inflectionless, relying on word order to convey meaning.|近义词：uninflected, analytic|反义词: inflected, synthetic|词性: adjective
