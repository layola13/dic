nonsecession:/ˌnɑːnsɪˈseʃən/ | n. | 不脱离，不分离 | The principle of nonsecession ensures the territorial integrity of a nation. | The government upheld the policy of nonsecession, refusing to allow any region to break away. | 近义词: unionism, indivisibility | 反义词: secession, separation | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "secession" (from Latin "secessio," meaning "a going apart, withdrawal").
