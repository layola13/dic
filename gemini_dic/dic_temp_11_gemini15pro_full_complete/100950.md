inunderstanding: /ɪnˌʌndərˈstændɪŋ/ | adj. | 在理解中，在相互理解中 | in the process of understanding something; having a mutual agreement or empathy | We are inunderstanding about the terms of the contract. 我们正在就合同条款进行理解。|  近义词: agreeing, sympathizing, empathizing | 反义词: disagreeing, misunderstanding | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "in" (表示状态) + "understanding" (名词，理解)

英文解析: This word is less commonly used compared to "understanding" or "in the process of understanding."  It implies an ongoing state of seeking comprehension or establishing common ground. It can also suggest a sense of empathy or agreement.

例句：
They are inunderstanding regarding the complexities of the project.
他们正在理解项目的复杂性。

We are currently inunderstanding about the new company policy.
我们目前正在理解新的公司政策。
