digital-signatures:/ˈdɪdʒɪtəl ˈsɪɡnətʃərz/ |n.|数字签名|A digital code attached to an electronically transmitted document that uniquely identifies the sender.  It ensures the authenticity and integrity of the document.|Example: Digital signatures are used to verify the sender of emails and other electronic documents. 数字签名用于验证电子邮件和其他电子文档的发送者。

|Example:  The contract was signed with digital signatures to make it legally binding.  这份合同使用了数字签名，使其具有法律约束力。|Synonym: electronic signatures, e-signatures|Antonym: N/A|Noun
