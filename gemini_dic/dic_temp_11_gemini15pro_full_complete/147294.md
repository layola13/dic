overmodesties:/ˌoʊvərˈmɑːdɪstiz/ |n.| 过分谦虚; 过分自谦 |Excessive or affected modesty; exaggerated claims of humility.| Her overmodesties about her achievements were charming, but also a little frustrating. 她对她成就的过分谦虚很迷人，但也让人有点沮丧。| 近义词: humility, self-deprecation | 反义词: arrogance, boastfulness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From "over-" (excessive) + "modesty" (humility).  "Modesty" ultimately comes from Latin "modestus" meaning "moderate, keeping due measure."
