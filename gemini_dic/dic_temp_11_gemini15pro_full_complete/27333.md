camaradas:/ˌkɑːməˈrɑːdəs/ | n. |同志们，伙伴们| Comrades; a term of address used among socialists, communists, or other left-wing groups, expressing solidarity and shared ideals. It originates from the Spanish word "camarada," meaning "roommate" or "companion." |例句：The president addressed the assembled camaradas. 总统向聚集的同志们发表了讲话。

例句：The camaradas worked together to achieve their common goal. 同志们一起努力实现他们的共同目标。

近义词：comrades, colleagues, associates, companions, friends|反义词：enemies, adversaries, opponents|词性:名词
