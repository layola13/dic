cosmopolite:/ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪt/ /ˌkɑːzməˈpɑːlɪt/| n. |世界公民；四海为家的人|A person who is at home in any part of the world and/or has wide experience of different cultures.|She's a real cosmopolite, she's lived in five different countries.|近义词：citizen of the world, internationalist, globetrotter|反义词:provincial, local|noun

cosmopolite:/ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪt/ /ˌkɑːzməˈpɑːlɪt/| adj. |世界性的；国际性的|Familiar with and at ease in many different countries and cultures.|London is a very cosmopolitan city.|近义词：international, global, multicultural|反义词:insular, parochial|adjective
