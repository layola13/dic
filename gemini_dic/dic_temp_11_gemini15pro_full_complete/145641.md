overcontentedness:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˈtɛntɪdnəs/| n. |过度满足，过分知足|A state of being excessively content or satisfied, often to the point of complacency or lack of ambition.|He seemed to be lost in a state of overcontentedness, unmotivated to strive for anything more. 他似乎沉浸在过度满足的状态中，没有动力去争取更多。|The overcontentedness of the team led to their downfall. 球队的过度满足导致了他们的失败。|近义词：complacency, satisfaction, contentment|反义词：discontent, dissatisfaction, ambition|词性:名词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + content (adjective meaning "satisfied") + -edness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
