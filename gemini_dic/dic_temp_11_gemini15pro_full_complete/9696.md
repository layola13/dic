antithyroid:/ˌæntiˈθaɪrɔɪd/ | adj. | 抗甲状腺的 | Relating to or denoting substances that inhibit the production of thyroid hormones. |  She was given antithyroid medication to control her overactive thyroid. | Synonyms: goitrogenic, goitrogen | Antonyms: thyromimetic | Adjective
