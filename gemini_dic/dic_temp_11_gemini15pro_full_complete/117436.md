mediocrity:/ˌmiːdiˈɒkrəti/ | n. | 平庸，平凡；平庸之才 | the quality or state of being mediocre; of only moderate quality; not very good | His acting career started brilliantly, then sank into mediocrity. 他的演艺生涯起初辉煌灿烂，后来变得平庸。 | synonyms: ordinariness, averageness, commonplaceness | antonyms: excellence, brilliance, superiority | noun

mediocre:/ˌmiːdiˈoʊkər/ | adj. | 平庸的，普通的；二流的 | of only moderate quality; not very good | The film's plot is predictable and the acting is mediocre.  这部电影的情节是可以预测的，而且表演也很平庸。 | synonyms: average, ordinary, undistinguished | antonyms: excellent, outstanding, exceptional | adjective
