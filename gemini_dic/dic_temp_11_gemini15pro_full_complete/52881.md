diminute:/daɪˈmɪnjuːt/ /dɪˈmɪnjut/ | adj. | 极小的，微小的 | Extremely small; tiny. |  The hummingbird is a diminutive bird.  蜂鸟是一种极小的鸟。

She has diminutive hands. 她有一双纤细的小手。

| 近义词: tiny, minuscule, miniature, small, slight | 反义词: huge, enormous, gigantic, massive, large | 词性: 形容词

diminute:/daɪˈmɪnjuːt/ /dɪˈmɪnjut/ | n. |  微小的东西；极小量 | A very small thing or amount. |  The bakery specialized in diminutives of classic pastries.  这家面包店专门制作经典糕点的微型版本。

The dose of medicine was a mere diminutive.  药量极小。


| 近义词: speck, jot, iota, bit, trifle | 反义词: abundance, mass, lot, heap, pile | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diminuutus, past participle of diminuere "to lessen, diminish," from Latin de- "down, from" + minuere "to lessen."
