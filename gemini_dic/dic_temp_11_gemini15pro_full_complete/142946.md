out-winging:/ˌaʊtˈwɪŋɪŋ/
verb
胜过，以更宽的翼展飞行
surpassing or defeating someone, especially in a political context, often by appealing to a broader range of people.  Can also literally refer to having a wider wingspan than another bird.
The candidate out-winged his opponent by focusing his campaign on the rural vote.  候选人通过将竞选重点放在农村选票上，胜过了他的对手。
The eagle out-winged the hawk in the soaring competition.  在这场翱翔比赛中，鹰的翼展超过了老鹰。
outsmart, outmaneuver, surpass
be outdone by, lose to
