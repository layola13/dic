improvidently:/ɪmˈprɒvɪdəntli/
adverb
轻率地，不顾后果地
in a way that does not show foresight or care for the future
He had improvidently spent all his savings. 他轻率地花光了所有积蓄。
Synonyms: rashly, carelessly, recklessly, thoughtlessly, imprudently
Antonyms: prudently, wisely, carefully, thoughtfully
词性: 副词
