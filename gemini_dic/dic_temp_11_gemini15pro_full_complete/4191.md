airfield:/ˈɛərfild/ (US) /ˈɛːfiːld/ (UK)| n. |飞机场；简易机场|A place where aircraft can take off and land, often with limited facilities. |The small airfield was used primarily by private pilots. 这个小型飞机场主要供私人飞行员使用。|The pilot landed the plane safely on the airfield. 飞行员安全地将飞机降落在机场。| synonym: airport, airstrip, landing field| antonym: None| noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From air + field.  "Air" referring to aircraft and "field" denoting a flat open space.
