chromophotolithograph: /ˌkroʊmoʊfoʊtoʊˈlɪθəˌɡræf/ | n. | 彩色石印照片 | A picture produced by a process combining chromolithography and photography.  It involves creating a color image on a lithographic stone or plate using photographic methods. | Example: The antique book contained beautiful chromophotolithographs of exotic birds. 这本古董书里包含精美的异国鸟类彩色石印照片。

Example: Chromophotolithographs were a popular method of reproducing artwork in the 19th century. 彩色石印照片是19世纪复制艺术作品的流行方法。 | 近义词: chromolithograph, color lithograph | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

chromo: /ˈkroʊmoʊ/ | n. | 彩色图片;尤指廉价的 | A colored picture, especially a cheap one. | Etymology: Shortening of chromolithograph. |

photo: /ˈfoʊtoʊ/ | n. | 照片 | A photograph. | Etymology: Shortening of photograph. |

litho: /ˈlɪθoʊ/ | n. | 石版画 | A lithograph. | Etymology: Shortening of lithograph.  From Greek lithos "stone" + graphein "to write." |

graph: /ɡræf/ | n. | 图表;图形 | A diagram or chart. | Etymology: From Greek graphein "to write." |
