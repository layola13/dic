hemiopsia:/ˌhɛmiˈɒpsiə/ | n. |偏盲，半盲|Blindness or defective vision in half the visual field of one or both eyes.| After the stroke, she experienced hemiopsia and could only see half of her visual field.|Synonyms:  hemianopsia, partial blindness|Antonyms:  normal vision|noun
