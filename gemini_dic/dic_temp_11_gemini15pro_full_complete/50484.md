depressiveness:/dɪˈprɛsɪvnəs/
|n.|抑郁，沮丧，消沉|The pervading depressiveness of the economic situation worried many. 经济形势普遍低迷，令许多人担忧。|例句：Her depressiveness made it difficult for her to enjoy life. 她的抑郁让她难以享受生活。

例句：The depressiveness of the weather matched his mood. 阴沉的天气与他的心情相符。|近义词：depression, gloom, melancholy, dejection|反义词：cheerfulness, happiness, elation|词性：名词

词源: From *depressive* + *-ness*.  *Depressive* comes from Late Latin *dēpressīvus*, from Latin *dēpressus*, past participle of *dēprimere* "to press down, depress," from *de-* "down" + *premere* "to press."  The suffix *-ness* forms nouns denoting a state or quality.
