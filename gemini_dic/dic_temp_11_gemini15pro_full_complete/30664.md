cerolite:/ˈsɪrəˌlaɪt/
noun
水镁石
A hydrated magnesium silicate mineral, usually found as a soft, earthy, white or pale-colored mass.
Cerolite is often found in serpentine rocks. 水镁石常发现于蛇纹岩中。
Synonyms: magnesium silicate hydrate, kerolite
Antonyms: N/A
词性: noun
