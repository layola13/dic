logos:/ˈlɒɡɒs/ (UK), /ˈloʊɡoʊs/ (US)| n.|逻各斯；理性；言语；神|The divine Word or reason incarnate in Jesus Christ, according to Christian theology.  In rhetoric, it refers to the appeal to logic or reason.  More generally, it can refer to a principle of order and intelligibility.|

例句：
The company's logos is instantly recognizable.  这家公司的标志一眼就能认出来。

The philosopher explored the concept of logos.  这位哲学家探讨了逻各斯的概念。

近义词：reason, word, principle, intellect, doctrine
反义词：pathos, ethos, irrationality
词性：noun
