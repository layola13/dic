balloonists:/bəˈluːnɪsts/
noun
热气球驾驶员；乘热气球的人
A person who pilots or travels in a hot air balloon.
The balloonists prepared for their ascent at dawn.  热气球驾驶员们在黎明时分准备升空。
The balloonists waved to the crowd below as they drifted across the valley.  热气球驾驶员们漂过山谷时向下面的人群挥手致意。
Synonyms: aeronauts, air travelers
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "ground crew")
