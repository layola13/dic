amphitheatrically:/ˌæmfɪθiˈætrɪk(ə)li/
adverb
像圆形剧场一样地; 以圆形剧场的方式
in the manner of an amphitheater; in a way that resembles an amphitheater in shape or arrangement.
The seats were arranged amphitheatrically, rising in tiers around the stage. 座位像圆形剧场一样排列，围绕着舞台层层升高。
similarly, circularly, concentrically
linearly, flatly

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀμφιθέατρον (amphitheatron, “amphitheater”) + -ικος (-ikos, adjective-forming suffix) + -ως (-ōs, adverbial suffix) + -ly (adverbial suffix)
