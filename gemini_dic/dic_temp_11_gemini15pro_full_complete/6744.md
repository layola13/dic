analytically:/ˌænəˈlɪtɪkli/| adv.|分析地；解析地|In a way that separates something into its parts in order to examine and understand it.|She examined the data analytically. 她分析地审查了数据。|例句：He approached the problem analytically.  他分析性地处理这个问题。

例句：The report analytically assesses the impact of the new policy.  该报告分析性地评估了新政策的影响。|近义词：logically, rationally, systematically, methodically|反义词：intuitively, emotionally, subjectively|词性:副词
词源: From analytic + -ally.  Ultimately from the Greek analutikos.
