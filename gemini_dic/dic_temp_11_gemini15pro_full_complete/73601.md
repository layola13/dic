foretold:/fɔːrˈtəʊld/
verb
预言，预示
predicted something that later happened.
The fortune teller foretold a great journey in my future.  算命先生预言我未来会有一次伟大的旅程。
foretold his own death. 他预言了自己的死亡。

synonym: predicted, prophesied, forecast
antonym: unpredicted, unforeseen
