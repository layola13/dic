dispirited:/dɪˈspɪrɪtɪd/
adjective
沮丧的，气馁的
Lacking in spirit or enthusiasm; discouraged.
The team was dispirited after losing the championship game. 球队在输掉冠军赛后士气低落。
discouraged, disheartened, dejected, downcast
encouraged, cheerful, optimistic
adjective
