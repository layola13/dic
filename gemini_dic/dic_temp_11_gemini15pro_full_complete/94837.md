imputation:/ˌɪmpjuˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 归罪，归咎；污名；推诿 | the act of saying or suggesting that someone is guilty of something bad |  The imputation of dishonesty angered him.  归咎于他不诚实让他很生气。 | synonyms: accusation, charge, attribution, ascription | antonyms: exoneration, acquittal | noun

imputation:/ˌɪmpjuˈteɪʃn/ | n. |  估算，推算；插补(统计学) |  a value that is substituted for a missing data point in statistical analysis.  | The researchers used imputation to fill in the missing values in the dataset. 研究人员使用插补法填充了数据集中缺失的值。| synonyms: estimation, inference, calculation | antonyms: omission | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin imputatio, from Latin imputare (“to charge, ascribe”), from in- + putare (“to reckon, think”).
