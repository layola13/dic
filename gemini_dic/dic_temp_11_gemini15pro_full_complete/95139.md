inapprehensible:/ˌɪnæprɪˈhensəbl/ | adj. | 不可理解的，难以领会的 | Not able to be understood; beyond grasp. |  The concept of infinity is often inapprehensible to children.  无限的概念对孩子们来说通常是难以理解的。

The philosopher's lecture was so dense and abstract as to be almost inapprehensible.  这位哲学家的讲座既深奥又抽象，几乎难以理解。

| 近义词：incomprehensible, unfathomable, inscrutable, obscure | 反义词：comprehensible, understandable, clear, lucid | 词性: adjective
