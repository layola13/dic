linguacious:/lɪŋˈɡweɪʃəs/ | adj. | 健谈的，流利的，语言能力强的 |  Fluent and articulate; having a good command of language. | She is a linguacious woman who can speak five languages fluently. 她是一位语言能力很强的女性，能流利地说五种语言。| synonyms: articulate, eloquent, fluent, voluble | antonyms: inarticulate, taciturn, reticent | word type: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *linguax, linguacis (“talkative”), from lingua (“tongue, language”).
