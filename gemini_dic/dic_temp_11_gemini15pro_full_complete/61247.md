emanatively:/ˈemənətɪvli/ | adv. | 流出地，散发地 | In a manner that emanates; radiatingly. |  The scent of lavender emanatively filled the room. 薰衣草的香味弥漫了整个房间。| 近义词：radiantly, diffusively, exhalingly | 反义词：absorptively, reservedly | 词性：副词

词源: From emanate + -ive + -ly.  Emanate comes from Latin emanare "to flow out," from ex- "out" + manare "to flow."
