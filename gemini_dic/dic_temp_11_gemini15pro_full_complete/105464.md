kill two birds with one stone:/kɪl tuː bɜːdz wɪð wʌn stoʊn/ | idiom | 一石二鸟；一箭双雕 | To achieve two aims with a single action. |  I needed to go to the bank and the post office, so I killed two birds with one stone and did both on the same trip. 我需要去银行和邮局，所以我一石二鸟，在同一次旅行中完成了这两件事。 | synonyms: achieve two things at once, accomplish two things at once, get two for the price of one | antonyms:  waste time, spin one's wheels | idiom
