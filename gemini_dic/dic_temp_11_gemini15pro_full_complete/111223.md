lithotome: /ˈlɪθətəʊm/ (UK), /ˈlɪθəˌtoʊm/ (US)
noun
碎石刀，切石刀
A surgical instrument used for cutting into the bladder to remove stones.
The surgeon used a lithotome to remove the bladder stone.  外科医生用碎石刀取出膀胱结石。
Synonyms:  cystotome
Antonyms:  None (Specific surgical instruments rarely have direct antonyms.)
词性: noun
