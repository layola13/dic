overhandles:/ˌoʊvərˈhændəlz/ | n. | (自行车、摩托车等的)上弯车把 | handlebars that curve upwards and towards the rider |  He prefers a bike with overhandles for a more upright riding position. 他更喜欢用上弯车把的自行车，骑行姿势更直立。| synonyms:  upright handlebars,  North Road handlebars | antonyms: drop handlebars, flat handlebars | noun

overhandle:/ˌoʊvərˈhændəl/ | v. | 过度处理，过分干预 | to manage or control something too much, often causing problems | The manager overhandled the situation, creating more tension among the staff. 经理对情况过度处理，反而在员工之间制造了更多的紧张气氛。| synonyms: micromanage, interfere, meddle | antonyms: neglect, ignore | verb
