gecko:/ˈɡekoʊ/ (US) /ˈɡekəʊ/ (UK)| n.|壁虎|A small lizard with soft skin, short legs, and adhesive pads on its feet that allow it to climb walls and ceilings.|The gecko scurried across the ceiling. 壁虎飞快地爬过天花板。|Synonyms: lizard, house lizard|Antonyms: N/A|noun
