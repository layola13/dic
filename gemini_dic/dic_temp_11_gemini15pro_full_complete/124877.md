mudded:/ˈmʌdɪd/ | adj. | 泥泞的；沾满泥的 | Covered or stained with mud. | The car's tires were mudded after driving through the unpaved road. 汽车的轮胎在穿过未铺砌的道路后沾满了泥。| The children's clothes were mudded after playing in the garden. 孩子们在花园里玩耍后，衣服上沾满了泥。| 近义词: muddy, soiled, dirty | 反义词: clean, spotless | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the verb "mud" (to cover or stain with mud) + "-ed" (suffix forming past participles and adjectives).  The verb "mud" comes from Old English "mud" meaning "mud, mire."
