dandify:/ˈdændɪfaɪ/| vt.|使...变得像花花公子；使...讲究衣着；打扮|To make someone or something more like a dandy; to make someone or something more fashionable or stylish.|He dandified himself before going out to the club. 他在去俱乐部之前把自己打扮得花枝招展。|The new haircut dandified his appearance. 新的发型让他看起来更时髦了。|近义词：spruce up, groom, embellish|反义词：unkempt, slovenly|词性:动词

词源: From dandy + -fy.  "Dandy" originated in the late 18th century, possibly from a Scottish nickname. The "-fy" suffix is of Latin origin, meaning "to make."
