delivered: /dɪˈlɪvərd/ | v. |递送，发表，履行，接生|convey (goods or letters) to the intended recipient.|The courier delivered the package.|近义词：sent, transported, distributed, presented, uttered, executed, performed|反义词: withheld, kept, retained|词性:动词

Etymology: From Old French *deliverer, from Latin dēliberāre (“to liberate, free, deliver”), from dē (“from”) + līberāre (“to free”), from līber (“free”).
