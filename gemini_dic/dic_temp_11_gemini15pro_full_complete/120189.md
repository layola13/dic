mindsets:/ˈmaɪndˌsɛts/| n. |心态，思维模式|A mindset refers to a set of beliefs or a way of thinking that determines someone's behavior, outlook, and mental attitude. |Example 1:  A growth mindset is essential for learning and development.  成长型思维模式对于学习和发展至关重要。

Example 2: Her fixed mindset prevented her from taking on new challenges.  她固定的思维模式阻碍了她接受新的挑战。|Synonyms: attitude, outlook, perspective, frame of mind, belief system|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but contrasting concepts include) open-mindedness, flexibility, adaptability|noun
