cystoidean: /ˌsɪstɔɪˈdiːən/
| adj. n.| 囊状的；海百合纲的；海百合纲动物| Relating to or resembling a cystoid; belonging to the class Cystoidea, an extinct group of echinoderms. |  This fossil is a cystoidean, an extinct type of echinoderm.  这个化石是海百合纲动物，一种已灭绝的棘皮动物。| The museum has a large collection of cystoidean fossils.  博物馆收藏了大量的海百合纲化石。| 近义词：cystid, cystoid | 反义词: None| 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Greek *kystis* "bladder, pouch" + *-oeides* "-oid."
