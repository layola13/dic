attractability:/əˌtræktəˈbɪləti/
n.
可吸引性；吸引力
The quality of being attractive or capable of attracting.
Her attractability stems from her confidence and kindness. 她的吸引力源于她的自信和善良。
Synonyms: attractiveness, appeal, allure, charm
Antonyms: unattractiveness, repulsiveness
