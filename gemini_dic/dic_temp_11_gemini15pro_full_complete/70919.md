fireboard:/ˈfaɪərˌbɔːrd/| n.|防火板，炉边挡板|A board or screen placed in front of a fireplace to protect the floor or carpet from sparks and embers.  It can also be decorative.|The ornate fireboard depicted scenes of rural life.|近义词：firescreen, hearth guard|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From fire + board.  "Fire" comes from Old English "fȳr," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*fūr-" and Proto-Indo-European "*péh₂wr-."  "Board" comes from Old English "bord," meaning a plank or table, ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*burdą."
