misregulated:/ˌmɪsˈreɡjuleɪtɪd/| adj.|失调的；管理不善的|Not properly regulated; poorly controlled or managed.  It implies a failure or inadequacy in the regulatory process.|The misregulated financial markets contributed to the economic crisis. 失调的金融市场导致了经济危机。|Synonyms: uncontrolled, disordered, chaotic, poorly managed, badly run|Antonyms: regulated, controlled, ordered, well-managed, properly functioning|adjective
