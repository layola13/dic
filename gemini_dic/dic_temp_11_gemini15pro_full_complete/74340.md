fragmentarinesses:/ˌfræɡmənˈtɛrɪnəsɪz/| n. |碎片状态，片段性，不完整性|The state of being fragmented; consisting of fragments.  A collection of separate parts that do not form a complete whole.|The fragmentarinesses of the historical record make it difficult to reconstruct a complete picture of the past. 过去历史记录的碎片化使得重建完整的历史画面变得困难。|The artist intentionally embraced the fragmentarinesses of memory in her work. 这位艺术家在她的作品中有意地接受了记忆的碎片化。|fragmentation, disjointedness, incompleteness|completeness, wholeness|noun
