abruptness:/əˈbrʌptnəs/| n.|突然；唐突；急躁|The abruptness of his departure surprised everyone. 他的突然离去让所有人都感到惊讶。|Etymology: From abrupt + -ness.  "Abrupt" comes from Latin abruptus, past participle of abrumpere "to break off," from ab- "off, away from" + rumpere "to break."|例句：The abruptness of the stop caused several passengers to fall. 突然停车导致几名乘客摔倒。

The abruptness of her tone offended him. 她语气唐突，冒犯了他。|近义词：suddenness, sharpness, bluntness, curtness|反义词:gradualness, smoothness, gentleness|词性:noun
