optional: /ˈɑːpʃənəl/ | adj. | 可选择的，非强制的 |  Available as a choice but not required. | You can add optional extras, such as a sunroof. 你可以添加一些额外的选择，比如天窗。

optional: /ˈɑːpʃənəl/ | adj. | 可选择的，非强制的 |  Available as a choice but not required. |  Attendance at the meeting is optional. 参加会议是可选的。


近义词: elective, voluntary, discretionary
反义词: compulsory, mandatory, required
词性: adjective

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin *optionalis, from Latin optio(n-) ‘choice, wish’ (see option).
