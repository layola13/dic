nawabs:/nəˈwɑːbz/ | n. | 纳瓦卜（印度的穆斯林统治者或高官） | A governor in India during the Mogul empire; a Muslim nobleman or person of high rank. |  The nawabs of Awadh were known for their lavish lifestyle.  阿瓦德的纳瓦卜以其奢华的生活方式而闻名。

The nawabs held considerable power in their regions.  这些纳瓦卜在他们各自的地区掌握着相当大的权力。| 近义词：rulers, princes, nobles | 反义词：commoners, subjects | 词性: noun
