mumbo:/ˈmʌmboʊ/ (US) /ˈmʌmbəʊ/ (UK)| n. |含糊不清的话; 莫名其妙的话; 咒语; 骗人的把戏|Unintelligible or meaningless speech; a ritualistic incantation or spell; a deceptive trick or sham. |例句：He muttered some mumbo jumbo about needing more time. 他含糊其辞地说需要更多时间。|近义词：gibberish, nonsense, jargon, balderdash|反义词:sense, clarity|noun

mumbo-jumbo:/ˈmʌmboʊ ˈdʒʌmboʊ/ (US) /ˈmʌmbəʊ ˈdʒʌmbəʊ/ (UK)| n. |繁文缛节; 莫名其妙的话|Complicated and confusing language or ritual; nonsense.|例句：The contract was full of legal mumbo-jumbo.  这份合同充满了法律上的繁文缛节。|近义词：gibberish, nonsense, jargon, balderdash|反义词:sense, clarity|noun
