cistern:/ˈsɪstərn/| n.|蓄水池；水箱|A cistern is a tank for storing water, especially rainwater collected from the roof of a building.|The old house had a cistern in the basement for collecting rainwater. 这栋老房子地下室里有一个蓄水池，用来收集雨水。|reservoir, tank, water tank|tap, faucet|noun
