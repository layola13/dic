hypercorrection:/ˌhaɪpərˈkərɛkʃən/ | n. |过度纠正，矫枉过正 | An instance of the production of a non-standard linguistic form by speakers attempting to produce prestige or standard forms.  It is often caused by the speaker's awareness of a real or perceived difference between their own variety of language and the standard variety. |  Example: Pronouncing "nuclear" as "nucular" is a common example of hypercorrection. 将“nuclear”发音为“nucular”是过度纠正的常见例子。 | Example:  His insistence on using "whom" even when it's grammatically incorrect is a form of hypercorrection. 他即使在语法错误的情况下也坚持使用“whom”，这是一种过度纠正的形式。 | 近义词: overcorrection, overcompensation | 反义词:  undercorrection | 词性: noun
