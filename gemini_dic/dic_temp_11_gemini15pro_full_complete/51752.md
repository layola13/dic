devonian:/dɪˈvoʊniən/ | adj. n. |泥盆纪的，泥盆系的；泥盆纪，泥盆系|Relating to or denoting the fourth period of the Paleozoic era, between the Silurian and Carboniferous periods, or the corresponding system of rocks, characterized by the development of fishes and the appearance of amphibians and land plants. |例句：The Devonian period is known as the "Age of Fishes". 泥盆纪被称为“鱼的时代”。

|例句：Devonian rocks are often rich in fossils. 泥盆纪的岩石通常富含化石。|近义词：Paleozoic |反义词: (No direct antonym for a geologic period) |词性: adjective, noun
