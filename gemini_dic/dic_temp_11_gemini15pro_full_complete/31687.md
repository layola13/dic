chaunter:/ˈtʃɔːntər/ | n. | 吟唱者，歌唱者；街头歌手 | A person who chants, sings, or recites, often in a public place. | The chaunter entertained the market crowd with his lively ballads.  市场上的吟唱者用他活泼的民谣款待人群。 | 近义词: singer, vocalist, minstrel | 反义词: listener, audience | noun

chaunter:/ˈtʃɔːntər/ | v. | 吟唱，歌唱；反复地说 | To sing or recite in a monotonous or repetitive tone. | He chaunted the same prayer over and over. 他一遍又一遍地吟诵着同样的祈祷。| 近义词: chant, intone, recite | 反义词: whisper, murmur | verb
