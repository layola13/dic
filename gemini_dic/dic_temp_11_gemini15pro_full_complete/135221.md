nonoxygenated:/ˌnɒnˈɒksɪdʒəneɪtɪd/
adjective
未氧化的，不含氧的
Not containing oxygen; deoxygenated.
Example: Nonoxygenated blood returns to the heart through the veins.  未氧化的血液通过静脉回流到心脏。
Example:  They studied the effects of nonoxygenated environments on marine life. 他们研究了无氧环境对海洋生物的影响。
Synonym: deoxygenated, anoxic
Antonym: oxygenated, aerated
词性: 形容词
