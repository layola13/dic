nogging:/ˈnɒɡɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈnɑːɡɪŋ/ (US)| n.|填充木料；砖块间的填充物；矮墙；隔墙|Horizontal timbers fixed between studs to give extra support to a wall or strengthen a partition. Also, the act of filling in the space between studs with these timbers.|The builder used nogging to strengthen the wall. 建筑工人用填充木料加固墙壁。|Synonyms: filling, packing, stuffing|Antonyms: emptying, removal|noun


nogging:/ˈnɒɡɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈnɑːɡɪŋ/ (US)| v.|用填充木料加固|To fill in the space between studs with horizontal timbers.|He was nogging the partition wall. 他正在用填充木料加固隔墙。|Synonyms: fill, pack, stuff|Antonyms: empty, remove|verb
