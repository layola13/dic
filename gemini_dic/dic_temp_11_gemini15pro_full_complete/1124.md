accessorising:/əkˈsesəˌraɪzɪŋ/
noun
配饰，装饰
The act of adding accessories to something, typically clothing or a room, to enhance its appearance.
She spent hours accessorising her outfit for the party. 她花了几个小时为聚会的服装搭配配饰。
adornment, decoration, embellishment
plainness, simplicity
n.
