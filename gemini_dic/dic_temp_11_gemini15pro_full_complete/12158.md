arvicole: /ɑːrˈvɪkoʊl/ (US), /ɑːˈvɪkəʊl/ (UK)| n. |田鼠|A small rodent of the subfamily Arvicolinae (voles and lemmings), found in grassy habitats.|The arvicole scurried through the tall grass.|近义词：vole, field mouse, meadow mouse|反义词: None|词性: noun
Etymology: From New Latin *arvicola, from Latin arvum (“field”) + colō (“I inhabit”).
