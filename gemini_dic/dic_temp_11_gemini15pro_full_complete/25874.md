burlapped:/bərˈlæpt/
adjective
包着粗麻布的；用粗麻布覆盖的
Covered or wrapped with burlap.
The burlapped roots of the young tree were carefully placed in the hole.  这棵小树包着粗麻布的根被小心地放进了洞里。
The artist used a burlapped canvas for his latest painting.  这位艺术家在他的最新画作中使用了包着粗麻布的画布。
近义词:covered, wrapped
反义词:uncovered, exposed
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From burlap + -ed, the past participle suffix, indicating a state or condition.  Burlap itself comes from the town of Burlap, in India, where the fabric was originally manufactured.
