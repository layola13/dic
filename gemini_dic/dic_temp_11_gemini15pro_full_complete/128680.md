nicotinization:/ˌnɪkətɪnɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 尼古丁化；尼古丁增高；尼古丁作用|The process of treating or impregnating something with nicotine, or increasing the level of nicotine in something.  The researchers studied the nicotinization of tobacco leaves. 研究人员研究了烟叶的尼古丁化。|例句：The nicotinization of the body can lead to addiction.  尼古丁对身体的作用会导致成瘾。

例句：Excessive nicotinization of crops can be harmful to consumers.  农作物中过量的尼古丁对消费者有害。|近义词: nicotine impregnation, nicotine treatment|反义词: nicotine reduction, denicotinization|词性: noun
