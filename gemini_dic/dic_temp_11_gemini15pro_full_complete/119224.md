miargyrite:/maɪˈɑːrdʒɪˌraɪt/
n.
硫锑银矿
A silver antimony sulfide mineral, AgSbS₂,  crystallizing in the monoclinic system. It is an important ore of silver.
Miargyrite is a common mineral found in silver deposits. 硫锑银矿是一种常见的银矿床矿物。
Miargyrite occurs as dark red to black, metallic crystals. 硫锑银矿以暗红色到黑色、金属光泽的晶体形式出现。
