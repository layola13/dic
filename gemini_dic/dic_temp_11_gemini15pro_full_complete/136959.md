nonspeciously:/ˌnɒnsˈpiːʃəsli/
adjective
非虚伪地，真诚地
without speciousness; genuinely; sincerely.  Not deceptively attractive.
His arguments were presented nonspeciously, relying on facts and logic. 他提出的论点并非虚饰，而是依靠事实和逻辑。
He spoke nonspeciously about his failings, admitting his mistakes openly. 他真诚地谈论了自己的缺点，公开承认了自己的错误。
近义词:genuinely, sincerely, honestly, truthfully, candidly
反义词:speciously, deceptively, falsely, dishonestly, disingenuously
词性:adjective
词源:From non- + specious + -ly.  "Specious" comes from Latin *speciōsus "fair in appearance, plausible," from speciēs "appearance, form, kind"
