marshiness:/ˈmɑːrʃɪnəs/ /ˈmɑːrʃinəs/
n.
沼泽性；湿软
The marshiness of the ground made it difficult to walk.  这块地湿软难行。
The marshiness of the soil is due to the high water table. 土壤的湿软是由于地下水位高造成的。
近义词：sogginess, swampiness, wetness
反义词：dryness, firmness, hardness
词性：名词
