lithiophilite:/lɪθiˈɒfɪlaɪt/
n.
锂磷锰矿
A rare phosphate mineral containing lithium, manganese, and iron, typically occurring in granitic pegmatites.
Lithium iron manganese phosphate mineral.  It is often associated with other lithium-bearing minerals.
Lithiophilite is a relatively rare mineral found in pegmatite deposits. 锂磷锰矿是一种相对稀有的矿物，发现于伟晶岩矿床中。

Lithiophilite specimens can be attractive to collectors.  锂磷锰矿标本对收藏家很有吸引力。
