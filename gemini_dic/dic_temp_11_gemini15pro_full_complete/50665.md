derive:/dɪˈraɪv/ /dəˈraɪv/| v.|获得，取得；起源于，源自|to get or receive something from something else; to have something as an origin|Many English words derive from Latin. 许多英语单词源自拉丁语.|近义词：obtain, acquire, originate, stem, descend|反义词: forfeit, lose |词性:动词

derive:/dɪˈraɪv/ /dəˈraɪv/| n.|（化学）衍生物|a substance that is formed from another substance|This product is a petroleum derive. 这个产品是石油衍生物。 |近义词：derivative, byproduct |反义词: source, origin |词性:名词
