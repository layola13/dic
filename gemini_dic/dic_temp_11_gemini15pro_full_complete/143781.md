outmouthing:/ˌaʊtˈmaʊθɪŋ/
verb
胜过(某人)的言辞;说得比...更大声,更自信或更具说服力
To defeat or surpass someone in a verbal contest; to speak more loudly, confidently, or persuasively than someone else.
He outmouthed his opponent in the debate and won the argument. 他在辩论中胜过对手，赢得了辩论。
近义词: outspeak, outargue, outtalk
反义词: be silenced, be outdone

outmouthing:/ˌaʊtˈmaʊθɪŋ/
noun
胜过(某人)言辞的行为;大声,自信或有说服力的讲话
The act of defeating or surpassing someone in a verbal contest; loud, confident, or persuasive speech.
His outmouthing of the other candidates secured him the nomination. 他胜过其他候选人的言辞为他赢得了提名。
近义词:outspeaking, outarguing
反义词: silence, reticence
