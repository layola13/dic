composite:/ˈkɒmpəzɪt/ (UK), /kəmˈpɑːzɪt/ (US)| adj. n.|复合的，合成的；复合材料|Made up of various parts or elements.|例句：The material is a composite of plastic and metal. 这种材料是由塑料和金属复合而成的。|近义词：compound, combined, complex|反义词：simple, basic|词性:形容词

composite:/ˈkɒmpəzɪt/ (UK), /kəmˈpɑːzɪt/ (US)| n.|复合材料，合成物|A material made from two or more different materials, combined to improve strength, stability, or other properties.|例句：Carbon fiber composites are used in aircraft construction. 碳纤维复合材料用于飞机制造。|近义词：compound, mixture|反义词：element|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin compositus, past participle of componere "to put together," from com- "together" + ponere "to put, place."
