overreduction:/ˌoʊvərɪˈdʌkʃən/ | n. | 过度还原；超还原 | The process of reducing a chemical compound beyond the desired point, often resulting in an undesired product. | The overreduction of the ketone resulted in an alcohol instead of the desired alkane. 过度还原酮导致生成了醇而不是所需的烷烃。| Over-reduction can occur in chemical reactions involving metal hydrides. 金属氢化物参与的化学反应中可能发生过度还原。 | hyperreduction | underreduction | noun
