biparietal:/ˌbaɪpəˈraɪɪtəl/
| adj.|双顶的；顶骨间的|Relating to the two parietal bones, or the two sides of the head.  Located between the parietal bones of the skull.|The biparietal diameter is a measurement of the fetal head obtained by ultrasound.  双顶径是通过超声波获得的胎儿头部测量值。|
|synonym:interparietal|antonym:N/A|adjective|

biparietal diameter:/ˌbaɪpəˈraɪɪtəl daɪˈæmɪtər/
| n.|双顶径|The distance between the two parietal bones of the skull, used especially as a measurement of fetal head size during pregnancy.|The biparietal diameter measurement helped determine the gestational age of the fetus. 双顶径测量有助于确定胎儿的孕龄。|
|synonym:BPD|antonym:N/A|noun|
