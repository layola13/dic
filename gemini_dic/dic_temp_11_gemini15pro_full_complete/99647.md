interlaying:/ˌɪntərˈleɪɪŋ/
verb
交错放置；插入；夹放
The process of placing something between layers of something else.
Workers were interlaying sheets of plastic between the layers of fabric. 工人们正在把塑料薄片夹在织物层之间。
interleaving, inserting, layering
--- (no clear antonym)
