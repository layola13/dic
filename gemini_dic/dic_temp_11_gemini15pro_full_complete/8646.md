anticonformity:/ˌæntiˈkənˈfɔːrməti/| n.|反从众，逆从|The act of opposing or rejecting the accepted social norms or behaviors of a group.  Going against the grain of popular opinion or action.|He showed his anticonformity by dressing in all black when everyone else wore bright colors. 他通过穿一身黑来表达他的反从众，而其他人则穿着鲜艳的衣服。|nonconformity, dissent, rebellion, counterculture|conformity, obedience, compliance|noun
