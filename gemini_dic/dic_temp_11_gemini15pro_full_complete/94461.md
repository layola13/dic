impolitic:/ˌɪmˈpɑːlətɪk/ | adj. | 不明智的，失策的 | Not showing good judgment or wisdom; unwise, inexpedient. |  It was impolitic of him to criticize the boss in front of everyone.  在他面前批评老板是不明智的。 | Synonyms: unwise, imprudent, injudicious, undiplomatic, tactless | Antonyms: wise, prudent, judicious, diplomatic, tactful | adjective
