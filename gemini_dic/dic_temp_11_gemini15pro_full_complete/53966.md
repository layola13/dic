discreeter:/dɪˈskriːtər/
adjective
更谨慎的，更小心的
more careful and prudent in one's speech or actions, especially in order to avoid causing offense or attracting attention.
She was always discreeter than her sister. 她总是比她姐姐更谨慎。
近义词: more cautious, more prudent, more reserved, more tactful
反义词: indiscreet, careless, imprudent
词性: adjective (比较级)

discreet:/dɪˈskriːt/
adjective
谨慎的，小心的
careful and prudent in one's speech or actions, especially in order to avoid causing offense or attracting attention.
He is very discreet in his personal life.  他在个人生活中非常谨慎。
近义词: cautious, prudent, reserved, tactful
反义词: indiscreet, careless, imprudent
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin discretus, past participle of discernere "to separate, distinguish," from dis- "apart" + cernere "to sift, distinguish."
