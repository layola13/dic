bas-relief: /ˌbɑː rɪˈliːf/ /ˌbæs rɪˈliːf/
noun
浅浮雕
A sculpture technique in which figures and/or other design elements are just slightly raised from a background surface.  The figures project only slightly from the background, giving a three-dimensional effect while remaining close to the original plane.
The Parthenon frieze is a famous example of bas-relief.  帕台农神庙的雕带是著名的浅浮雕的例子。

近义词: low relief, basso-rilievo
反义词: high relief, alto-rilievo
词性: n.
