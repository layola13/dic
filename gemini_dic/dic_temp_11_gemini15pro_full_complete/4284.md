airwing:/ˈɛərˌwɪŋ/| n.|空军联队；舰载机联队|A group of aircraft operating together, typically as part of a larger organization such as a navy or air force.  A self-contained tactical and administrative unit composed of aircraft squadrons and supporting units required for a specific mission.|The airwing was deployed to the carrier.|Synonyms: air group, squadron|Antonyms: N/A|noun
