grindelia: /ɡrɪnˈdiːliə/
n.
gumweed, rosinweed, tarweed
A genus of North American plants of the daisy family (Asteraceae), with resinous leaves and stems and yellow flower heads.  Many species are used medicinally.
Grindelia squarrosa is used to treat asthma and bronchitis.  格林德利亚被用来治疗哮喘和支气管炎。
synonyms: gumplant, rosinweed
