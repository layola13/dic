limbos:/ˈlɪmboʊz/| n.| 困境，边缘状态；地狱边境|A state of uncertainty or neglect, especially one in which someone or something has been forgotten or relegated to a lower priority. Also, in some Christian beliefs, the place where the souls of unbaptized but innocent babies go after death.|The project has been in limbo for months.  这个项目已经搁置好几个月了.|Synonyms: oblivion, purgatory, abeyance|Antonyms: certainty, activity, inclusion|Noun
