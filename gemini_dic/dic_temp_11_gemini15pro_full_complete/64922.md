establishing: /ɪˈstæblɪʃɪŋ/ | v. | 建立，设立；确定；证实 |  The company is establishing a new branch in Tokyo.  该公司正在东京设立新的分公司。| 近义词: founding, creating, setting up, proving, confirming | 反义词: abolishing, destroying, disproving | 词性: 动词 (present participle of establish)

词源: From Middle English *establissen, borrowed from Old French establiss-, stem of establir (“to establish, fix, settle”), from Latin stabiliō (“make stable”), from stabilis (“stable, firm, steadfast”), from stō (“stand”)
