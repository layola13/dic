euphemism: /ˈjuːfəmɪzəm/ | n. |委婉语，委婉说法 | A mild or indirect word or expression substituted for one considered to be too harsh or blunt when referring to something unpleasant or embarrassing. |  She used a euphemism for death, referring to it as "passing away." 她用“去世”这个委婉语来指代死亡。

She called her dismissal a "career change opportunity," which is clearly a euphemism. 她把自己的解雇称为“职业转变机会”，这显然是一种委婉语。

| 近义词: circumlocution, understatement | 反义词: dysphemism | 词性: noun
