agatiferous:/ˌædʒəˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
多刺的；产刺槐胶的
Producing or bearing agati (Sesbania grandiflora), a type of small tree with edible flowers and pods, also known for producing a gum.
The agatiferous tree provided shade and a source of food for the villagers.  这个产刺槐胶的树为村民提供了阴凉和食物来源。
The botanist studied the agatiferous properties of the Sesbania grandiflora.  植物学家研究了大花田菁的产刺槐胶特性。
近义词: thorny, prickly, spiny
反义词: smooth, soft
