call-back:/ˈkɔːlbæk/ (US), /ˈkɔːlbæk/ (UK)| n.|回电，召回，复试|A request to return, or the act of returning, especially a telephone call made in response to a message left or a previous call.  A later audition for an acting role.|I received a call-back for the second interview. 我收到了第二次面试的复试通知。|synonyms: return call, recall, second audition, follow-up|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "rejection" in some contexts)|noun

call-back:/ˈkɔːlbæk/ (US), /ˈkɔːlbæk/ (UK)| n.|回调函数|In computer programming, a function that is passed as an argument to another function and is executed later.|The button's click event triggers a call-back function.  按钮的点击事件触发了一个回调函数。|synonyms: callback function, handler|antonyms:  (No direct antonym)|noun
