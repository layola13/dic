incultivation:/ˌɪnkʌltɪˈveɪʃən/
noun
教化; 培养; 陶冶; 开垦
The act or process of cultivating or improving something, especially land or the mind.
The incultivation of virtues is essential for a harmonious society.  培养美德对于和谐社会至关重要。
The incultivation of the land led to increased crop yields.  土地的开垦导致了作物产量的增加。
cultivation, development, nurture
neglect, abandonment
