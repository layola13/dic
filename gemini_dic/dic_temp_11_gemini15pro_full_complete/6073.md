amerasian:/əˈmɛrɪʒən, əˈmɪərˌeɪʒən/
n.|亚裔美国人；美亚混血儿|A person of mixed Asian and American descent, specifically a child born of an Asian mother and a white American serviceman father, typically during or after the Korean War or Vietnam War.|Many Amerasians faced discrimination in both Asia and the United States.|近义词:multiracial, mixed-race|反义词:None (lacks a direct opposite)|词性:noun


Etymology:
A blend of "American" and "Asian," coined in the mid-20th century.
