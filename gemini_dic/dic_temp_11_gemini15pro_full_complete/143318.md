outdwell:/ˌaʊtˈdwɛl/
verb
居住在外面; 比...住得更久
to live beyond (a place or time); outlive.  To dwell outside.
He outdwelt all his siblings, living to the ripe old age of 98. 他比他所有的兄弟姐妹都活得长，活到了98岁高龄。
The hermit outdwelt in the forest, far from civilization.  这位隐士居住在远离文明的森林里。
近义词: outlive, survive
反义词: predecease
词性: verb
