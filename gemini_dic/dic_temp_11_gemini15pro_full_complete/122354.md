mixtec: /ˈmɪkstɛk/ | adj. n.| 米斯特克人的；米斯特克语；米斯特克人 |Relating to the Mixtec people, their language, or their culture.  A member of an indigenous people of Mexico. | Example: The Mixtec codices are important historical documents.  米斯特克人的手抄本是重要的历史文献。 He is a Mixtec from Oaxaca. 他是来自瓦哈卡的米斯特克人。 | 近义词: Zapotec (related indigenous group) | 反义词:  N/A (lacks a direct opposite) | 词性: adjective, noun
