antambulacral:/ˌæntæmˈbjʊləkrəl/ | adj. n. |管足的；步带沟的；管足|Relating to the ambulacral areas of an echinoderm, which bear tube feet used for locomotion, feeding, and respiration.  Located on the underside of a starfish arm, containing the tube feet. | The antambulacral groove houses hundreds of tube feet. 管足沟里有数百个管足。| The starfish uses its antambulacral feet to grip and move across surfaces. 海星用它的管足抓住并在表面移动。|近义词: ambulacrarian |反义词: abambulacral |词性: adjective, noun
