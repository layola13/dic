filberts:/ˈfɪlbərts/ | n. |榛子|A hazelnut, especially one cultivated for its nuts. |例句：I love to snack on roasted filberts. 我喜欢吃烤榛子。

例句：The squirrels were busy burying filberts for the winter. 松鼠们正忙着为冬天埋藏榛子。|近义词：hazelnuts, cobnuts |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


Etymology:
From Old French *filbert, from Saint Philibert, whose feast day (August 20th/22nd) coincided with the ripening of the nuts.
