laparoscopists:/ˌlæpərəˈskɒpɪsts/ | n. | 腹腔镜医师 | A surgeon who performs laparoscopy, a minimally invasive surgical procedure. |  The laparoscopists successfully removed the gallbladder through a small incision.  腹腔镜医师通过一个小切口成功地切除了胆囊。 |  surgeons, laparoscopic surgeons |  open surgeons | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From laparoscopy + -ist. Laparoscopy from  laparo-  "flank, abdominal wall," from Greek *lapara (plural laparoi "flanks, loins"), related to lapein "to lick, lap up" + -scopy "viewing," from Greek skopein "to look at."
