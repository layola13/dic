以下提供单词 what 的详细解析，因为题目中多次出现 what，并且格式要求与 what 的解析相关：

what: /wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American) | pronoun, determiner, adverb | 什么，多么 | Used for asking for information specifying something. |

例句：
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？

What do you want for dinner? = 你晚餐想吃什么？


近义词: which, who | 反义词:  (What doesn't have a direct antonym) | 词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb



USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you doing right now? = 你现在正在做什么？
What is he cooking in the kitchen? = 他在厨房里煮什么？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she accomplished this year? = 她今年完成了什么？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading all day? = 他一整天都在看什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say to you? = 她对你说了什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病之前吃了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? = 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
What will she study in college? = 她上大学后会学什么？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be presenting at the conference? = 她将在会议上展示什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by next month? = 你到下个月会完成什么？
What will she have learned by the end of the course? = 到课程结束时她会学到什么？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past five years? = 过去五年你一直在做什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候，她将一直在学习什么？


虚拟语气:
If I knew what to say, I would tell you. = 如果我知道该说什么，我会告诉你。
I wish I knew what she was thinking. = 我希望我知道她在想什么。


被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？


疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what I'm saying. = 她不明白我在说什么。
