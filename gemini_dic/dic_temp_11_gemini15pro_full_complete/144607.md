outweighs:/ˌaʊtˈweɪz/
verb
超过，胜过
be greater or more significant than
The benefits of this treatment far outweigh the risks. 这种治疗的好处远远大于风险。
Synonyms:  exceed, surpass, override, prevail over, preponderate
Antonyms:  be outweighed by, lose to, be inferior to
词性: 动词
