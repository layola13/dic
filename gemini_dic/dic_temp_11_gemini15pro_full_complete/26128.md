busters:/ˈbʌstərz/| n.|破坏者，打败者，破案者|People who break things, defeat opponents, or solve mysteries.  Specifically used in compounds like "ghostbusters" or "mythbusters".|The MythBusters tested the theory that a penny dropped from the Empire State Building could kill someone.  《流言终结者》测试了从帝国大厦掉下来的一分钱可以杀死人的理论。|近义词：breakers, demolishers, solvers, detectives|反义词: creators, builders, losers|词性:noun
