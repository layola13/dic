monoculturally:/ˌmɒnəˈkʌltʃərəli/ | adv. | 单一文化地 |  In a way that relates to or is characterized by a single culture, especially in a way that ignores or excludes other cultures.  |  The issue was approached too monoculturally, failing to consider the perspectives of other groups.  这个问题处理得过于单一文化，未能考虑其他群体的观点。 |  homogeneously, uniformly |  multiculturally, diversely | adverb
