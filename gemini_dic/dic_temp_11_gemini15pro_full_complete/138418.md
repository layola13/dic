what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) | pron. det. adv. int.| 什么 | Used to ask for information about something. |
What is your name? 你叫什么名字？
What time is it? 现在几点了？

近义词: which, who, how | 反义词: N/A | 词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection | 词源: From Old English _hwæt_, neuter singular nominative and accusative of _hwā_ "who, what."


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? 你在看什么？
What is he doing now? 他现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned so far? 你到目前为止学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? 她用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? 我来之前你做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting? 会议前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the accident? 事故发生前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? 她转专业前一直在学什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow? 你明天要做什么？
What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting? 会议上他们会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have accomplished by next year? 到明年你会完成什么？
What will have changed by then? 到那时会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做这件事十年了？
What will she have been studying by graduation? 到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
What if I told you the truth? 如果我告诉你真相会怎么样？

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to solve the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is your favorite color? 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are you thinking about? 你在想什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what I'm saying. 她不明白我在说什么。
