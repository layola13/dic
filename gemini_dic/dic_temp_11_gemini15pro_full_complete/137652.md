nonunderstandingly:/ˌnɒnʌndəˈstændɪŋli/ | adv. | 不理解地，缺乏理解地 | In a way that shows a lack of understanding. | She stared at him nonunderstandingly, unsure of what he meant. 她茫然地盯着他，不明白他的意思。| He shook his head nonunderstandingly at the complex instructions.  他对着复杂的指示一头雾水地摇了摇头。 | 近义词：blankly, uncomprehendingly, confusedly | 反义词： knowingly, intelligently, perceptively | 词性：副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non < PIE *ne "not"
understanding (noun): from Old English understandan "comprehend," from under + standan "to stand"  (The original notion is perhaps of "standing close to" something.)
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
