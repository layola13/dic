mockers:/ˈmɑːkərz (US) /ˈmɒkəz (UK)| n. |嘲笑者，嘲弄者|People who mock or ridicule others.  Those who express contempt or derision through mimicry, sarcasm, or scornful laughter.|The mockers in the crowd hurled insults at the speaker.  人群中的嘲笑者向演讲者大声辱骂。|synonyms: scoffers, jeerers, teasers, taunters|antonyms: admirers, praisers, supporters|noun
