bleaching:/ˈbliːtʃɪŋ/| n.|漂白，脱色|The process of whitening or removing color from something, typically using chemicals. |The bleaching of coral reefs is a serious environmental problem. 珊瑚礁的漂白是一个严重的环境问题。|synonyms: whitening, decolorizing|antonyms: dyeing, coloring|noun
bleach:/bliːtʃ/| v.|漂白，脱色|To whiten or remove color from something, typically using chemicals. |She bleached her hair blonde. 她把头发漂白成了金色。|synonyms: whiten, decolorize|antonyms: dye, color|verb
bleached:/bliːtʃt/ |adj.| 漂白的，褪色的|Whitened or faded in color, especially due to exposure to sunlight or chemicals.|The bleached bones lay scattered across the desert.  漂白的骨头散落在沙漠中。|synonyms: whitened, faded, pale|antonyms: dyed, colored, bright|adjective
