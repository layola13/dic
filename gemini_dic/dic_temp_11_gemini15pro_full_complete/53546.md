disbalance:/dɪsˈbæləns/ | n. |不平衡，失衡 |lack of balance or symmetry; instability. |The disbalance of power in the region led to conflict. 该地区力量的失衡导致了冲突。|近义词：imbalance, inequality, disproportion, asymmetry |反义词：balance, equilibrium, symmetry |词性: 名词

disbalance:/dɪsˈbæləns/ | vt. |使失去平衡，使失调 |to upset the balance or stability of. |Stress can disbalance your hormones. 压力会使你的荷尔蒙失调。|近义词：unsettle, destabilize, disturb |反义词：balance, stabilize, regulate |词性: 动词
