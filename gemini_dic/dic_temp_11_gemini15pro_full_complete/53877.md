discontinuousness:/ˌdɪskɒntɪˈnjuːɪtnəs/ | n. |不连续性，间断性|The state or characteristic of being discontinuous; lack of continuity.|The graph showed the discontinuousness of the function. 图表显示了该函数的不连续性。|近义词：discontinuity, interruption, brokenness|反义词：continuity, continuousness|词性:名词
Etymology: From discontinuous + -ness.  Discontinuous from dis- "not, opposite of" + continuous. Continuous from Latin continuus "holding together," from continere "hold together"
