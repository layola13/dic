candet: /ˈkændɪt/
n.
candied fruit peel; a piece of this.
蜜饯果皮；一块蜜饯果皮。
The candied peel added a burst of citrus flavor to the cake. 蜜饯果皮为蛋糕增添了一股柑橘的香味。
近义词:  candied peel, succade
反义词: None (no direct opposite for a specific type of confection)
