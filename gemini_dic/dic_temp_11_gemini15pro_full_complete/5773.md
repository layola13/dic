amalgamator:/əˈmælɡəmeɪtər/| n.|合并者，混合者，融合器|A person or thing that amalgamates (combines or unites things, especially companies or organizations, into one).|The company president acted as the amalgamator, bringing together several smaller businesses to form a larger corporation. 公司总裁担任了合并者的角色，将几家小企业合并成一家大公司。|Synonyms: uniter, merger, consolidator|Antonyms: separator, divider|noun

amalgamator:/əˈmælɡəmeɪtər/| n.|汞齐化器，混汞器|A machine used in gold mining to extract gold from ore by combining it with mercury.|The old amalgamator was still in use, despite concerns about mercury pollution. 尽管人们担心汞污染，那台旧的混汞器仍在使用。|Synonyms: mixer, blender|Antonyms: separator|noun
