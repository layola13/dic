officerless:/ˈɒfɪsələs/ /ˈɑːfɪsərlɪs/
adjective
无官员的；无长官的
Lacking officers; without officers in charge.
The small, officerless unit was left to fend for itself.  这个小而无领导的单位只能自生自灭了。
The mutiny left the ship officerless and adrift.  兵变后这艘船没有了官员，漂流在海上。
leaderless, unsupervised
led, commanded
