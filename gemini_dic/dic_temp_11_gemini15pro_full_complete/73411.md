foreknower:/fɔːrˈnoʊər/
n.
预知者，先知者
Someone who knows something in advance, especially something in the future.
The ancient prophets were seen as foreknowers of great events. 古代的先知被视为重大事件的预知者。
近义词：seer, prophet, oracle
反义词：None

ETYMOLOGY->
From fore- (before) + know + -er (one who performs an action)
