oathbreaking:/ˈoʊθˌbreɪkɪŋ/
n.|违背誓言，背信弃义|The act of breaking an oath or solemn promise.|His oathbreaking shocked the entire community. 他的背信弃义震惊了整个社区。|近义词：perjury, betrayal, treachery, disloyalty|反义词：fidelity, loyalty, trustworthiness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From oath + breaking (present participle of break).  "Oath" comes from Old English "āð," related to Old High German "eid" (oath), ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*aiþaz" (oath). "Break" comes from Old English "brecan," related to Old High German "brehhan," from Proto-Germanic "*brekaną" (to break).
