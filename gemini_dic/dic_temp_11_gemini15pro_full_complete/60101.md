eeriest:/ˈɪəriɪst/ | adj. | 最怪诞的，最阴森的 |  Superlative form of eerie: most eerie.  Describes something that inspires fear, especially in a strange or mysterious way. | The eeriest sound I ever heard was the wind whistling through the abandoned house. 我听过的最怪诞的声音是风穿过废弃房屋的呼啸声。|  The abandoned amusement park was the eeriest place I'd ever been.  这个废弃的游乐园是我去过的最阴森的地方。 | synonyms: creepiest, spookiest, most unsettling, most sinister | antonyms: comforting, reassuring, pleasant, cheerful | adjective
