lupuses:/ˈluːpʊsiːz/
n.
狼疮（复数）
Plural form of lupus.  It refers to more than one instance of the disease lupus.
Lupuses can manifest in various ways, affecting different organs. 狼疮可以以各种方式表现出来，影响不同的器官。
The doctor diagnosed both sisters with different types of lupuses. 医生诊断两姐妹患有不同类型的狼疮。
Synonyms: (No direct synonym for the plural form, but for lupus singular)  lupus erythematosus, SLE
Antonyms: (No antonyms for diseases)
