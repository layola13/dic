jokeless:/ˈdʒoʊklɪs/
adjective
没有玩笑的；不开玩笑的；严肃的
Without jokes; serious.
The atmosphere in the room was jokeless and tense. 房间里的气氛严肃而紧张。
The meeting was a jokeless affair, focused entirely on business.  这次会议是一场严肃的事情，完全专注于业务。
Synonyms: serious, solemn, grave, earnest, humorless
Antonyms: humorous, playful, jocular, lighthearted
词性: 形容词
