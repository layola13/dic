ascendantly:/əˈsɛndəntli/
adverb
上升地，优势地
In a manner of ascending; rising upward.  Characterized by increasing power or influence.
His career progressed ascendantly, quickly reaching the top position. 他的职业生涯蒸蒸日上，迅速达到了最高职位。
The company's stock price has been moving ascendantly for the past year.  这家公司的股价在过去一年里一直在上涨。
近义词: upwardly, increasingly, progressively
反义词: descendingly, decreasingly, declining
词性: adverb

ascendant:/əˈsɛndənt/
adjective, noun
adj. 上升的，占优势的
n.  优势，支配地位
词源: From Latin ascendens, present participle of ascendere "to climb up, mount, ascend," from ad- "to" (see ad-) + scandere "to climb" (see scan (v.)).
adj.  The ascendant political party is expected to win the next election.  预计占优势的政党将赢得下次选举。
n.  He is in the ascendant in his company. 他在他的公司里处于优势地位。
近义词: (adj.) rising, dominant, prevailing; (n.) dominance, superiority
反义词: (adj.) declining, subordinate; (n.) subordination, inferiority
词性: adjective, noun
