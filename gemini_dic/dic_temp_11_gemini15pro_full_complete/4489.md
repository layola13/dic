albinism:/ˈælbɪnɪzəm/
n.
白化病
A genetic condition characterized by a deficiency or absence of pigment in the skin, hair, and eyes.
People with albinism are sensitive to sunlight and should take precautions. 白化病人对阳光敏感，应该采取预防措施。
近义词: achromia, achromasia
反义词: pigmentation
词性: noun
