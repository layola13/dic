manam:/ˈmɑːnəm/| n.|魔力，法力；精神力量；效力|A supernatural force, power, or influence; spiritual power; efficacy|例句：The shaman used his manam to heal the sick. 萨满用他的法力治病。|例句：The ancient sword was said to possess a powerful manam.  据说这把古老的剑拥有强大的魔力。|近义词：magic, power, force, energy, potency|反义词: weakness, impotence|词性:名词

词源: From Proto-Oceanic *mana. Cognates include Hawaiian mana, Māori mana, Samoan mana, Tongan mana, Fijian mana, and Rapanui mana.
