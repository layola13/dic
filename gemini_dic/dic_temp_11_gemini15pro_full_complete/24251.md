briefers:/ˈbriːfərz/
n.
提供指示的人;指示者
People who give instructions or briefings.
The briefers explained the mission objectives to the team. 指示者向团队解释了任务目标。
The president's briefers kept him updated on the latest developments.  总统的指示者让他了解最新的发展情况。
Synonyms: instructors, advisors, informants
Antonyms: trainees, learners
