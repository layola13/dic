extractor:/ɪkˈstræktər/| n.|提取器，抽出器，拔取器|A device or tool used for extracting something, such as a juice extractor or a tooth extractor.|例句：The dentist used an extractor to remove the damaged tooth. 牙医用拔牙钳拔掉了那颗坏牙。

例句：She used a juice extractor to make fresh orange juice. 她用榨汁机榨了新鲜的橙汁。|近义词：remover, device, tool|反义词:inserter|noun
