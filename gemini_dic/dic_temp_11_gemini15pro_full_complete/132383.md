nondemises:/ˌnɒndɪˈmaɪzɪz/
n.
非租赁财产，非转租财产
Parts of a property not included in a lease.  Specifically refers to areas like common hallways, lobbies, mechanical rooms, and exterior grounds that are maintained by the landlord but not leased to individual tenants.
The nondemises were clearly marked on the building plans.  大楼平面图上清楚地标明了非租赁财产。
Synonyms: common areas, shared spaces
Antonyms: leased premises, demised premises
noun
