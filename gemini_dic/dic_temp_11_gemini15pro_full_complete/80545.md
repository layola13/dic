goodman:/ˈɡʊdmən/| n.|好人；家主；丈夫|A man of substance and respectability, often the head of a household.  Archaic or dialectal term.|例句：The goodman of the house welcomed us warmly.  这家的男主人热情地欢迎我们。|例句：In the old ballads, the goodman often went hunting. 在古老的民谣中，男主人经常去打猎。|近义词：husband, master, householder|反义词:badman, villain|noun
