disuniting:/dɪsjuˈnaɪtɪŋ/
|v.|使分离，使不团结|Causing disunity or separating something into parts.|The new policy is disuniting the country. 新政策正在分裂国家。|The ongoing conflict is disuniting the community.  持续的冲突使社区变得四分五裂。|近义词：dividing, separating, alienating|反义词：uniting, joining, connecting
|动词|
