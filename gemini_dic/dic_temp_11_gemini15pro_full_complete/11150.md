archeology's:/ˌɑːrkiˈɒlədʒiz/
noun
考古学的（所有格）
Possession form of "archeology," referring to something belonging to or associated with the study of human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites and the analysis of artifacts and physical remains.
Archeology's findings often rewrite history.  考古学的发现经常改写历史。
The museum houses archeology's greatest treasures.  这家博物馆收藏着考古学上最伟大的宝藏。
synonyms:archaeology's (no direct synonyms for a possessive form, but synonyms for archeology itself are: anthropology, paleontology)
antonyms:(no direct antonyms)
n.(noun)
