flatlets:/ˈflæt.ləts/| n.|小型公寓；套房|A flatlet is a small apartment, often with only one or two rooms.  A flatlet is typically self-contained and may be part of a larger building or house that has been divided into separate units.|She couldn't afford a large apartment, so she rented a flatlet in the city center. 她负担不起大公寓，所以在市中心租了一套小公寓。

The flatlet was perfect for a single person, with a small kitchen and living area combined.  这套小公寓非常适合单身人士，有一个小厨房和起居区合为一体。|近义词：studio apartment, apartment, bedsit|反义词:house, mansion|词性:noun
