onlinenlan: /ˌɒnlaɪnˈlæn/ | n. | 网上局域网; 在线局域网 | A local area network (LAN) that is accessible over the internet. It allows users to connect and play games or share files as if they were on the same physical network. | Example:  Gamers often use onlinenlan services to play with friends who live far away. 玩家经常使用网上局域网服务与远方的朋友一起玩游戏。

Example:  Some onlinenlan platforms offer file sharing and collaboration features. 一些网上局域网平台提供文件共享和协作功能。 | 近义词: virtual LAN, online network | 反义词: physical LAN, offline network | 词性: noun
