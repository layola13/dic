cowry:/ˈkaʊri/ | n. | 贝壳，宝贝，子安贝 | a small, often brightly colored, glossy shell, formerly used as money in parts of Africa and Asia |  The necklace was made of cowries. 这条项链是用贝壳制成的。 | synonyms: shell, seashell | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "currency" in the context of its former use as money) | noun

cowry:/ˈkaʊri/ | n. | 贝壳货币，贝币 | a small shell formerly used as money in parts of Africa and Asia | Cowries were once a common form of currency. 贝壳曾经是一种常见的货币形式。| synonyms: shell money | antonyms:  modern currency | noun
