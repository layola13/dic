hedgehoggy:/ˈhedʒˌhɒɡi/ | adj. | 像刺猬的；多刺的；易怒的 | Resembling a hedgehog in appearance or character; prickly; irritable. | The old man had a hedgehoggy beard.  这位老人的胡子像刺猬一样。|  prickly, spiky, bristly, irritable, grumpy | smooth, soft, amiable, friendly | adjective

Etymology:
From hedgehog + -y.  The word "hedgehog" comes from Middle English heggehoge, from Old English hegehog, where hege means "hedge" and hog means "hog," referring to its pig-like snout and its habit of rooting in hedges.
