accusatorial:/əˌkjuːzəˈtɔːriəl/| adj.|控诉的，谴责的，指责的|Characterized by or expressing accusation.  Indicating or suggesting that one believes someone has done something wrong.|Her accusatorial tone made him feel uncomfortable. 她指责的语气让他感到不舒服。|Synonyms: accusing, incriminating, blaming, condemnatory, reproving|Antonyms: forgiving, understanding, exculpatory, apologetic|adjective
