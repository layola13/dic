negotiate:/nɪˈɡoʊʃieɪt/ (US) /nɪˈɡəʊʃɪeɪt/ (UK)| v. | 谈判，协商；商定；成功越过 | to discuss something formally in order to make an agreement |  We negotiated a price for the car. 我们协商了汽车的价格。| synonyms: bargain, discuss, confer | antonyms: dictate, impose | verb

negotiate:/nɪˈɡoʊʃieɪt/ (US) /nɪˈɡəʊʃɪeɪt/ (UK)| n. | 谈判，协商 | an act of negotiating | The negotiation lasted for several hours.  谈判持续了几个小时。| synonyms: bargaining, discussion, conference | antonyms: dictation, imposition | noun
