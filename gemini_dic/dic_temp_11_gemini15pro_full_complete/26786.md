cafeneh:/ˌkæfəˈneɪ/ | n. | 咖啡馆，咖啡店 | A small, informal restaurant serving coffee and other refreshments.  Derived from the French word "café."  Often used in British English, especially in contexts relating to the Middle East or South Asia. |  例句：Let's meet at the cafeneh for a chat and some baklava.  我们去咖啡店聊聊天，吃点果仁蜜饼吧。

例句：The cafeneh was bustling with activity, filled with the aroma of cardamom coffee.  咖啡馆里熙熙攘攘，充满了豆蔻咖啡的香味。 | 近义词：cafe, coffee shop, coffeehouse | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps restaurant if contrasting a larger, more formal establishment) | 词性: noun
