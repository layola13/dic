increscent:/ɪnˈkrɛsənt/| adj.|渐强的，新月形的|Waxing; increasing in strength, intensity, or size; shaped like a crescent with the points turned upward.|The increscent moon shone brightly in the night sky.  新月在夜空中闪耀。|近义词：waxing, growing, burgeoning, escalating|反义词:decrescent, waning, diminishing|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin "increscens," present participle of "increscere" (to grow, increase), from "in-" (in, into) + "crescere" (to grow).
