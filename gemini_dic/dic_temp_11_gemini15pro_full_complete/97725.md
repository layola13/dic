inirritable: /ɪnˈɪrɪtəbl/
adjective
不易怒的，温和的
Not easily irritated; calm and even-tempered.
He has an inirritable disposition, rarely getting upset about anything. 他性情温和，很少因为任何事生气。
Synonyms: calm, placid, serene, tranquil, even-tempered, imperturbable, unflappable
Antonyms: irritable, irascible, touchy, testy, choleric
词性: 形容词
