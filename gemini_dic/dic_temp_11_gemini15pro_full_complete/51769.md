devotion:/dɪˈvəʊʃn/| n.|奉献，忠诚，热爱|love, loyalty, or enthusiasm for a person, activity, or cause.|例句：His devotion to his wife was unwavering. 他对妻子的爱始终如一。|例句：She has a deep devotion to her art. 她对她的艺术有着深深的热爱。|近义词：commitment, dedication, allegiance, piety, faithfulness|反义词：disloyalty, infidelity, apathy|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *devotion, devociun (“solemn dedication to a religious purpose, consecration, devoutness, piety”), from Late Latin devotionem (nominative devotio) (“a devoting, dedication, giving up”), from Latin devotus (“devoted”).
