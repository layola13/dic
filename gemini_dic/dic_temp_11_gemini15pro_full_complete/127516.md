neckweed:/ˈnɛkˌwid/| n. |颈草，毒芹|A poisonous plant, specifically *Conium maculatum*, also known as hemlock. It is highly toxic to humans and animals.|例句：The ancient Greeks used neckweed to execute prisoners. 古希腊人用毒芹处决囚犯。

例句：Neckweed can be mistaken for edible plants, so be cautious when foraging. 毒芹很容易被误认为是可食用的植物，所以在觅食时要小心。|近义词：hemlock, poison hemlock|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "antidote" in a medicinal context)|词性:noun
