harlequin:/ˈhɑːrlɪkwɪn/ | n. adj.|丑角，滑稽角色；小丑服；五颜六色的|A comic character in traditional pantomime, typically masked and dressed in a diamond-patterned costume.|The harlequin skipped across the stage. 小丑跳过舞台。|近义词：clown, jester, buffoon|反义词:serious character|词性:noun, adjective

harlequin:/ˈhɑːrlɪkwɪn/|adj.|五颜六色的，杂色的|Having a pattern of brightly colored diamond shapes.|Harlequin ducks have striking plumage. 小丑鸭有着鲜艳的羽毛。|近义词：multicolored, variegated, motley|反义词:monochrome, plain|词性:adjective
