aristotelian:/ˌærɪstəˈtiːliən/
adjective
亚里士多德的；亚里士多德哲学的
Relating to the philosophy of Aristotle.
例句：His arguments were based on Aristotelian logic. 他的论点基于亚里士多德的逻辑。

例句：The Aristotelian view of the universe placed Earth at the center. 亚里士多德的宇宙观把地球放在中心。

近义词：peripatetic
反义词：Platonic


aristotelic:/ˌærɪstəˈtɛlɪk/
adjective
目的论的；有明确目标的；以目标为导向的
Having to do with purposes or goals.  Specifically, referring to the philosophy of Aristotle, which emphasizes that everything has a purpose or final cause.
例句：An Aristotelian universe is a teleological universe. 一个亚里士多德式的宇宙是一个目的论的宇宙。

例句：The artist's work seemed driven by an aristotelic purpose, striving towards a pre-conceived ideal.  这位艺术家的作品似乎是由一个目的论的目的驱动的，努力朝着一个预先设想的理想前进。

近义词：teleological, purposive
反义词：mechanistic
