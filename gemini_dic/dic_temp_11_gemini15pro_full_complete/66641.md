existentially:/ˌɛɡzɪˈstɛnʃəli/
adverb|存在地，生存地；就存在而言|Relating to existence, especially human existence and experience; in a way that relates to or affects your existence.|He felt existentially lost after losing his job and his wife in the same year. 在同一年失去工作和妻子后，他感到生存的意义都消失了。|
synonym:philosophically, fundamentally, essentially
antonym:inessentially, superficially
词性:副词
