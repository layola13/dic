microprocessing:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈprɑːsesɪŋ/ | n. |微处理|The execution of computer instructions by a microprocessor.  It involves fetching instructions from memory, decoding them, and performing the operations they specify. It is the core function of a computer's central processing unit (CPU).|Microprocessing has revolutionized the computer industry. 微处理彻底改变了计算机行业。|  | 近义词：information processing, data processing | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider manual processing) | noun

microprocessor:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈprɑːsesər/ | n. |微处理器|A small chip that contains the central processing unit (CPU) of a computer. It performs arithmetic and logic operations and controls the flow of data within the computer system.|The speed of your computer depends largely on the microprocessor. 你电脑的速度很大程度上取决于微处理器。|  | 近义词：CPU, chip | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | noun
