adjective
无离婚的; 没有离婚的
Without divorce; not having experienced a divorce.
They lived a long and divorceless life together. 他们一起过着漫长而没有离婚的生活。
近义词:unseparated, unmarried (if never married), enduring
反义词:divorced, separated
词性:adjective
