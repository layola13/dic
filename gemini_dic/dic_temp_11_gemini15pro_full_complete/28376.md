carbazole: /ˈkɑːrbəˌzəʊl/ (US) /ˈkɑːbəzɒl/ (UK) | n. |咔唑|A crystalline organic compound used in the manufacture of dyes, pigments, and pharmaceuticals.  A nitrogen-containing aromatic compound with the chemical formula C12H9N. It features a tricyclic structure consisting of two six-membered benzene rings fused on either side of a five-membered nitrogen-containing ring.|Carbazole derivatives are used in organic light-emitting diodes (OLEDs). 咔唑衍生物用于有机发光二极管 (OLED) 中。| Carbazole is a heterocyclic aromatic compound. 咔唑是一种杂环芳香化合物。 | synonym: dibenzopyrrole, diphenylenimine | antonym: N/A | noun
