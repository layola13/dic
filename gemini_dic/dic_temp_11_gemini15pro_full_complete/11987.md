artfulnesses:/ˈɑːrtfəlnəsɪz/
n.
狡猾；诡诈；巧妙
The quality of being artful; cunning, craftiness, or skill.
Her artfulnesses were evident in the way she manipulated the situation to her advantage. 她的狡猾在她操纵局势为自己谋利的方式中显而易见。
Synonyms:  cunning, craftiness, guile, slyness, trickery, deception, chicanery
Antonyms:  honesty, sincerity, forthrightness, ingenuousness, artlessness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From artful + -ness.  Artful itself comes from art (skill) + -ful (full of).  Ultimately from Latin ars (skill).
