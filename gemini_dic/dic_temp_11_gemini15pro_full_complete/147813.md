overprovision:/ˌoʊvərprəˈvɪʒən/ | n. | 过度供应，超额配置 | The act of supplying more resources than are actually needed, often as a precaution against future demand or failure. |  We overprovisioned server capacity to ensure smooth performance during peak hours. 我们超额配置了服务器容量，以确保在高峰时段的流畅性能。| 近义词: oversupply, overstock | 反义词: underprovision, undersupply | noun

overprovision:/ˌoʊvərprəˈvɪʒən/ | v. | 过度供应，超额配置 | To supply more resources than are actually needed. | The IT department overprovisioned the network bandwidth. IT部门过度配置了网络带宽。| 近义词: oversupply, overstock | 反义词: underprovision, undersupply | verb
