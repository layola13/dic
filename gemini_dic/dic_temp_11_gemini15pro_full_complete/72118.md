flogged:/flɒɡd/ (UK) /flɑːɡd/ (US)| v. |鞭打，重打，严厉批评，过度使用|To beat with a whip or stick as punishment or to drive or urge on; to criticize severely; to promote or sell something vigorously.|The slave was flogged for disobedience.  这个奴隶因不服从而被鞭打。

The horse was flogged to make it run faster. 这匹马被鞭打以使其跑得更快。

He flogged his old car until it finally died. 他把他的旧车过度使用，直到它最终报废。

The critics flogged the new play mercilessly.  评论家们毫不留情地批评了这部新剧。

The salesman flogged the product relentlessly.  推销员不懈地推销产品。|近义词：beat, whip, lash, chastise, criticize, overwork, overuse|反义词：praise, commend|词性:动词
