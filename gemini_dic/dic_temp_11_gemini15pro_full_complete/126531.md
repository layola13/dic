naboberies:/ˈneɪbəbəriːz/
n.
恶作剧，胡闹；无聊的小玩意儿；无价值的东西
frivolous actions, gadgets, or things of little value
He wastes his time on childish naboberies. 他把时间浪费在幼稚的胡闹上。
近义词:frivolities, trifles, gimmicks, gadgets, toys
反义词:necessities, essentials, valuables


词性:noun (plural)

词源:Likely from Dutch *nabobberij, a derivative of nabob ("person of great wealth; governor of a province in India under the Mogul empire"), from Urdu nawāb ("viceroy; governor"). The semantic shift likely occurred via an association with the perceived frivolous spending habits of wealthy individuals.
