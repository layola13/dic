heam:/hiːm/ | n. | 膜 | A thin membrane, especially one enclosing an organ or part.  Specifically, it can refer to the caul, the membrane enclosing a fetus. | The heam of the eye was damaged. 眼睛的膜受损了。| synonyms: membrane, film, caul | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical membrane.  Could consider "opening," "gap," or "tear" in a specific context.) | noun
