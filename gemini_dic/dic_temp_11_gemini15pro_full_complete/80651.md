goramy:/ɡʊˈrɑːmi/ /ɡəˈrɑːmi/| n. | 鲤科鱼，吻嘴鱼，丝足鱼，格拉米鱼 |A freshwater fish of the family Osphronemidae, native to South and Southeast Asia, several species of which are popular in aquariums. |例句：The goramy is a popular aquarium fish.  这种吻嘴鱼是一种很受欢迎的观赏鱼。

例句：We saw several colorful goramies in the fish tank. 我们在鱼缸里看到几条色彩鲜艳的吻嘴鱼。

近义词：fish, gourami |反义词: None|词性:noun


Etymology->
From Malay *gurami*. Ultimately from Proto-Malayo-Polynesian *gurami*.
