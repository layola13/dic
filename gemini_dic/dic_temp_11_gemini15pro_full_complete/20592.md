blackfaces:/ˈblækˌfeɪsəz/ | n. | 黑脸 minstrel（尤指白人演员扮成黑人的） |  A form of theatrical makeup used predominantly by non-black performers to represent a caricature of a black person. This practice is now widely considered offensive due to its origins in racist minstrel shows. |  Minstrel shows, featuring performers in blackfaces, were popular in the 19th century.  黑脸艺人在19世纪很受欢迎。| synonyms: minstrel makeup | antonyms:  N/A | noun

blackface: /ˈblækˌfeɪs/ | n. | 黑脸（尤指白人演员扮成黑人的） |  The makeup itself used in blackface minstrelsy, typically involving blackened skin, exaggerated red lips, and often a wig or other exaggerated features. |  The use of blackface is now seen as a deeply offensive act.  现在使用黑脸被视为一种极具冒犯性的行为。| synonyms: minstrel makeup | antonyms: N/A | noun
