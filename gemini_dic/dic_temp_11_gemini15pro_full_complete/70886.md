finway:/ˈfɪnweɪ/| n. |鱼道; 鱼路|A structure that allows fish to swim upstream past a dam or other obstacle.|The salmon used the finway to reach their spawning grounds. 鲑鱼利用鱼道到达它们的产卵地。|近义词：fish ladder, fish pass|反义词:dam, barrier|词性:名词

词源: "fin" (鱼鳍) + "way" (路)  A compound word directly describing the function of the structure.
