electrosurgical:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈsɜːrdʒɪkəl/
adjective
电外科的
Relating to or used in electrosurgery, a surgical technique using high-frequency electric current to cut or coagulate tissue.
The surgeon used an electrosurgical unit to cauterize the bleeding vessel.  外科医生使用电外科设备烧灼出血的血管。
Electrosurgical instruments are sterilized before each use.  每次使用前，电外科器械都要进行消毒。
Synonyms: electrocautery
Antonyms: None
词性: adjective
