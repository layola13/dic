ethylenically:/ˌɛθɪˈlɛnɪkli/
adverb
以乙烯基方式
in a manner related to or involving ethylene or its derivatives, often referring to polymerization or other chemical reactions.
The monomers are ethylenically unsaturated. 这些单体是乙烯基不饱和的。
