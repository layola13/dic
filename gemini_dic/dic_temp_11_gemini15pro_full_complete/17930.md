beetled:/ˈbiːtld/| adj.|甲虫似的；有眉毛的；突出的|Having overhanging eyebrows; projecting or scowling. Also, resembling a beetle, especially in having a hard, rounded shell or a projecting brow.|His beetled brows gave him a perpetually angry look. 他突出的眉毛使他看起来总是很生气。|Synonyms: prominent, protruding, overhanging, scowling, beetle-browed|Antonyms: flat, smooth, receding|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From beetle + -ed.  "Beetle" itself comes from Old English *bīetel, meaning "biter," related to bite.
