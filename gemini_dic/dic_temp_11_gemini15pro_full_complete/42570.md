counterpetitioned:/ˌkaʊntər.pəˈtɪʃ.ənd/
v. | 反诉，对抗性请愿 | to file a petition in opposition to another petition. | The company counterpetitioned, arguing that the original claims were unfounded.  该公司提出反诉，认为最初的索赔是没有根据的。 | The residents counterpetitioned the city council's decision to build a new highway. 居民们对市议会修建新高速公路的决定提出了反诉。 | appealed, protested, challenged | conceded, acquiesced | verb
