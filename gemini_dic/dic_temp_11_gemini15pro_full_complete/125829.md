murrah:/ˈmʊrə/| n.|摩拉水牛|A breed of water buffalo (Bubalus bubalis) originating in India and Pakistan, known for its high milk production.|The Murrah buffalo is prized for its rich milk. 摩拉水牛因其丰富的牛奶而备受珍视。|Synonyms: water buffalo, buffalo|Antonyms: None|Noun
