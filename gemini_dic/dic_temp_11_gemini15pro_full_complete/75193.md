frizzling:/ˈfrɪzəlɪŋ/| adj. v. (present participle of frizzle)|卷曲的，鬈曲的；发出咝咝声|The bacon is frizzling in the pan.  培根在锅里发出咝咝声。|近义词：curling, crisping, sizzling|反义词:straightening, flattening|词性:形容词，动词(现在分词)
frizzle:/ˈfrɪzl/| v. n.|（使）卷曲，（使）鬈曲；发出咝咝声|The hot sun frizzled my hair.  炎热的太阳晒得我的头发卷曲了。|近义词：curl, crisp, sizzle|反义词:straighten, flatten|词性:动词，名词

Etymology:  From the Middle French *frizer, frequentative of Old French friser "to curl, crisp," probably from Frankish *frisojan.
