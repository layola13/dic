dig:/dɪɡ/ | n. | 挖掘；考古挖掘；刺，戳 | an act of digging; an archaeological excavation; a poke or thrust | The archaeological dig unearthed a Roman villa. 考古挖掘出土了一座罗马别墅。 | synonyms: excavation, unearthing | antonyms: burial, interment | noun
