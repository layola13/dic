absenting:/ˈæbsəntɪŋ/
verb
缺席，使不在；使不参与
The act of being absent or causing someone to be absent.
He was absenting himself from work without permission. 他未经许可就旷工。
近义词: withdrawing, skipping, avoiding
反义词: attending, presenting, participating
词性: verb (present participle of absent)

absenting:/ˈæbsəntɪŋ/
gerund
缺席，不参与
A noun formed from a verb (absenting)
Absenting yourself from class too often will affect your grades.  经常缺课会影响你的成绩。
近义词: absence, nonattendance, truancy
反义词: attendance, presence, participation
词性: gerund/noun
