overanalyses:/ˌoʊvərəˈnæləˌsiːz/| n.|过度分析|An excessive or unnecessary amount of analysis, often resulting in a misinterpretation or complication of the original issue.|例句：His overanalyses of the data led him to a false conclusion. 他对数据的过度分析导致他得出了错误的结论。

例句：Her overanalyses of his behavior created unnecessary anxiety. 她对他行为的过度分析造成了不必要的焦虑。|近义词：overthinking, overinterpretation|反义词: underanalysis, simplification|词性:名词
