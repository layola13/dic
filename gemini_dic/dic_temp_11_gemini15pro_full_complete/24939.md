brunsick:/ˈbrʌnzˌwɪk/ | n. |不伦瑞克 (Brunswick) | Brunswick is a city in Lower Saxony, Germany. It is located on the Oker River, about 29 kilometres (18 mi) east of Wolfsburg. |  Brunswick is known for its rich history and culture. | 近义词:  (As a place name, it has no true synonyms.) | 反义词: (As a place name, it has no true synonyms.) | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Middle Low German *Brunswik, from Brun(i) (a personal name) + wik (a trading settlement).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *brūną ("brown") and *wīkaz ("village, dwelling").
