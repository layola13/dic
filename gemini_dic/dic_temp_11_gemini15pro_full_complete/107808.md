lapstrake:/ˈlæpˌstreɪk/ | adj. n. | 叠板的，搭接板；叠板式船 |  (of a boat) having external planks overlapping each other, or (of planks) overlapping in this way.  A boat built with such planks. |  The boat was built using the traditional lapstrake method. 这艘船是用传统的叠板方法建造的。 |  clinker-built, overlapping |  carvel-built | adjective, noun
