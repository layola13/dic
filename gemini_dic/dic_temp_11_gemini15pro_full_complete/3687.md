aggravation:/ˌæɡrəˈveɪʃən/
n.|加重，恶化；恼怒，烦躁|An increase in severity or intensity; something that causes annoyance or frustration.|The traffic noise was a constant aggravation.|近义词:irritation, annoyance, exasperation, worsening|反义词:improvement, alleviation, relief|词性:名词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin aggravatio(n-), from the verb aggravare (see aggravate).
