festive:/ˈfɛstɪv/| adj.|节日的，喜庆的，欢乐的|Relating to or characteristic of a festival, especially Christmas. Marked by festivity and rejoicing.|例句：The whole town had a festive atmosphere. 整个城镇都充满了节日气氛。|例句：She wore a festive red dress. 她穿了一件喜庆的红色连衣裙。|近义词：joyful, merry, celebratory, jovial|反义词：somber, gloomy, mournful|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *festivus, from Latin festum "festival, feast."
