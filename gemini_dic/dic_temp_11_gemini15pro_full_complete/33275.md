chrysoidine:/krɪˈsɔɪdiːn/
n.
金胺;菊红;金黄色
a synthetic azo dye that gives a golden-yellow or orange colour.
Chrysoidine is used to dye wool, silk, and leather. 金胺用于染羊毛、丝绸和皮革。
Chrysoidine was once used as a food coloring. 金胺曾被用作食品色素。
Synonyms: basic orange 2, C.I. 11270
Antonyms: None (color, not typically having direct opposites)
noun
