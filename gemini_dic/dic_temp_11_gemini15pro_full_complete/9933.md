aperture:/ˈæpərtʃər/ (US), /ˈæpətʃə/ (UK)| n. |孔，缝隙，光圈|An opening, hole, or gap, typically one that allows light or something else to pass through.|The camera's aperture controls how much light enters the lens. 相机的孔径控制进入镜头的光量。|synonyms: opening, hole, gap, orifice, slit, vent|antonyms: closure, blockage, obstruction|noun


apertures:/ˈæpərtʃəz/ (US), /ˈæpətʃəz/ (UK)| n. |孔，缝隙，光圈（aperture的复数形式）|Openings, holes, or gaps, typically ones that allow light or something else to pass through.|The telescope's multiple apertures allowed for a wider field of view. 望远镜的多个孔径允许更宽的视野。|synonyms: openings, holes, gaps, orifices, slits, vents|antonyms: closures, blockages, obstructions|noun
