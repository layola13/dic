infinitesimalness:/ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtɛsɪməlnəs/| n.|无穷小；极微小；无限小|The infinitesimalness of the particles makes them difficult to detect. 这些粒子的极微小性使它们难以被检测到。|例句：The infinitesimalness of the change was barely perceptible. 这种微乎其微的变化几乎难以察觉。

例句：The scientist discussed the infinitesimalness of the atom. 科学家讨论了原子的无限小。|近义词：minuteness, tininess, exiguity, microscopicity|反义词：immensity, vastness, enormity|词性:noun

infinitesimal:/ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtɛsɪməl/| adj.| 极小的；无穷小的|The chances of winning the lottery are infinitesimal.  赢得彩票的几率极其微小。|例句：An infinitesimal amount of poison can be deadly.  极少量的毒药也可能是致命的。

例句：The difference between the two measurements was infinitesimal.  两次测量之间的差异微乎其微。|近义词：tiny, minute, negligible, microscopic|反义词：huge, enormous, vast, immense|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From late 17th century: from French infinitésimal, from médiéval Latin infinitesimālis, from infinit(us) ‘infinite’ + -esim- (as in centesimus ‘hundredth’) + -al.
