circumlittoral:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈlɪtərəl/| adj.|环岸的，近岸的|Relating to the shallow water zone extending from the low tide mark to the edge of the continental shelf, typically to a depth of about 200 meters.  It is characterized by constant salinity and temperature, and is affected by wave action and light penetration.|The circumlittoral zone is home to a diverse range of marine life. 环岸带是各种海洋生物的家园。|The study focused on the circumlittoral fish communities. 这项研究的重点是环岸鱼类群落。|近义词：sublittoral, neritic|反义词：littoral, supralittoral|词性:形容词

Etymology:
circum- (Latin): around, about
littoral (Latin litus, litoris): shore
