gatecrashing: /ˈɡeɪtˌkræʃɪŋ/
gatecrashing: n.
gatecrashing: 擅自闯入；不请自来
gatecrashing: The act of entering a party or other event without an invitation.
gatecrashing: They tried gatecrashing the wedding, but were politely turned away by security. 他们试图擅自闯入婚礼，但被保安礼貌地拒之门外。
gatecrashing: He was known for gatecrashing parties and making a nuisance of himself.  他以擅自闯入派对和制造麻烦而出名。
gatecrashing: 近义词: intruding, barging in, crashing
gatecrashing: 反义词: being invited, attending (with invitation)
gatecrashing: 词性: 名词 (gerund)
