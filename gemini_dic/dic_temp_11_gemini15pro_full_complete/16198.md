banned:/bænd/ | adj. | 被禁止的，被取缔的 | officially or legally prohibited | The book was banned in several countries. 这本书在几个国家被禁。| prohibited, forbidden, outlawed, proscribed | permitted, allowed, legal | adjective

Etymology:
From the Old English *bannan* "to proclaim, summon," which also meant "to curse, prohibit."  It's related to the Old High German *bannan* "to command, summon," and ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bannaną*. The sense of "prohibit" developed from the idea of issuing a decree or proclamation against something.
