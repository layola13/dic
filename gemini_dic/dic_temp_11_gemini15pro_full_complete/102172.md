虚拟语气:

被动语态:
(It'd is less common in passive voice but possible in reported speech):
He said it'd been decided already. 他说已经决定好了。
She told me it'd been done the previous week. 她告诉我上周已经做好了。

注意事项:  it'd can be ambiguous as it can contract both "it would" and "it had". Context is usually needed to clarify the meaning.
