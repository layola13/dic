arietta:/ˌærɪˈɛtə/ | n. |小咏叹调，短抒情调|A short song or air, usually light and graceful in character. |She sang a charming arietta by Mozart. 她演唱了一首莫扎特迷人的小咏叹调。|Example: The composer's arietta was simple yet elegant.  这位作曲家的小咏叹调简洁而优雅。|Synonym: air, melody, tune, song|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps cacophony or dissonance as opposites in terms of musical quality) |noun
