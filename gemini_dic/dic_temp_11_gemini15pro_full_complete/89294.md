homoeomorphism:/ˌhoʊmiːoʊˈmɔːrfɪzəm/| n. |同胚|A continuous function between topological spaces that has a continuous inverse function.  A one-to-one correspondence between the points of two figures that is continuous in both directions. A homeomorphism preserves topological properties.|The coffee cup and the donut are homeomorphic. 咖啡杯和甜甜圈是同胚的。|Synonyms: isomorphism (in a more general context), topological equivalence|Antonyms: non-homeomorphic|noun
