biogenesis:/ˌbaɪoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ /ˌbaɪəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.
生命起源; 生源说
The production of living organisms from other living organisms.
例句：Biogenesis is the theory that living things can only come from other living things.  生源说是指生物只能来自其他生物的理论。

例句：The principle of biogenesis replaced the earlier theory of spontaneous generation. 生源论原则取代了早期的自然发生理论。

近义词：abiogenesis (in older texts)
反义词：abiogenesis
词性：noun
