ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin encyclopaedia, from Greek enkyklios paideia "general education," from enkyklios "circular, recurring" (from en "in" + kyklos "circle") + paideia "education, rearing of a child."  The sense evolution was from "general course of instruction" to "work containing such a course."
