empoisonment:/ɪmˈpɔɪzənmənt/ /ɛmˈpɔɪzənmənt/| n.|中毒，毒害；投毒|The investigation concluded that the cause of death was accidental food empoisonment. 调查结论是死因是意外食物中毒。|Example: The symptoms suggested empoisonment. 症状表明中毒了。

Example:  He was charged with the empoisonment of his wife. 他被指控毒害了他的妻子。

|近义词：poisoning, contamination, tainting|反义词:cure, detoxification|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *empoisonement, from empoisonner (to poison), from em- (in, into) + poison (poison).  Ultimately from Latin potio (drink).
