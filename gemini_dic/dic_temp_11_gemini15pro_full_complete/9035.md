antimaterialist:/ˌænti məˈtɪəriəlɪst/
adjective
反物质主义的；非物质主义的
Opposed to or rejecting materialism, the belief that material possessions and physical comfort are the most important things in life.
She's become quite antimaterialist since traveling the world and seeing how little some people have. 她周游世界，看到一些人拥有的东西如此之少后，变得相当反物质主义。
近义词:spiritual, ascetic, non-materialistic
反义词:materialistic, consumerist
词性:adjective
