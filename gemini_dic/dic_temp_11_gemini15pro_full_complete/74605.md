freaky:/ˈfriːki/| adj.|古怪的，怪异的，反常的|Strange or unusual in a way that is unpleasant or frightening. | That's a freaky coincidence! 那真是个怪异的巧合！|近义词：weird, bizarre, strange, odd, unusual, eccentric|反义词：normal, ordinary, conventional, typical|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From freak + -y.  "Freak" originally referred to a sudden capricious change of mind, whim, or fancy.  The modern sense of "abnormal person or thing" developed in the 19th century.
