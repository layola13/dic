glorioser:/ˌɡlɔːriˈoʊsər/ | adj. | 更光荣的，更辉煌的 | More glorious; more splendid. | The glorioser reign of the new king began with a grand celebration. 新国王更辉煌的统治以盛大的庆典开始。|  The sunrise was even glorioser than yesterday. 今天的日出比昨天更壮观。 | 近义词: more glorious, grander, more magnificent, more splendid | 反义词: less glorious, humbler, simpler | 词性:形容词

词源:  Comparative form of *glorios*, from Latin *gloriosus* (“glorious”), from *gloria* (“glory”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱlew- (“hear, fame”).
