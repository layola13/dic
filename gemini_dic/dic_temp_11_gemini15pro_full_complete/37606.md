communications:/kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 沟通；通讯；通信；传达；交流；交通；通路 | the process by which people exchange information, ideas, feelings, and emotions | The company needs to improve its internal communications.  这家公司需要改进其内部沟通。| synonyms: correspondence, interaction, intercourse, contact | antonyms: isolation, silence, seclusion | noun

communications:/kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | (plural) 通讯系统；通讯工具；通信设备；交通设施 | systems and methods used to send information, messages, etc. |  The storm knocked out all communications.  暴风雨摧毁了所有的通讯设施。| synonyms:  telecommunications, media, networks | antonyms:  - | noun
