diabetes mellitus:/ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz məˈlaɪtəs/ | n. |糖尿病|A group of metabolic disorders characterized by hyperglycemia resulting from defects in insulin secretion, insulin action, or both.| My grandmother has diabetes mellitus and has to monitor her blood sugar levels regularly. 我的祖母患有糖尿病，必须定期监测血糖水平。|Synonyms: diabetes, sugar diabetes|Antonyms: None|Noun
