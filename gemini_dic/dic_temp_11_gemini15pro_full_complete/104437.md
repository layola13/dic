kagu:/ˈkɑːɡuː/| n.|卡古鸟，鹭鹤|A flightless bird (Rhynochetos jubatus) endemic to New Caledonia, with grey plumage, a red bill and legs, and a prominent erectile crest.|The kagu is the only surviving member of its family.|近义词:flightless bird|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From New Caledonian French *cagou*, from a native Kanak language.
