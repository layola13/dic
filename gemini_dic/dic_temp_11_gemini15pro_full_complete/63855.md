epigrammatize:/ˌɛpɪˈɡræmətaɪz/ | v. |  用警句表达；使…简洁 | To express concisely, often in a witty or paradoxical way, as in an epigram. | He epigramatized the situation with a clever remark. 他用一句机智的话概括了情况。 |  summarize, condense, encapsulate | expand, elaborate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *épigrammatiser*, from épigramme (“epigram”) + -iser (suffix forming verbs meaning “to make into, to treat as”), from Latin epigramma, from Ancient Greek ἐπίγραμμα (epígramma, “inscription”), from ἐπιγράφω (epigráphō, “I inscribe”), from ἐπί (epí, “upon, on”) + γράφω (gráphō, “I write, scratch, draw”).
