hegemony:/hɪˈdʒɛməni/ /hɪˈɡɛməni/ | n. | 霸权，支配权，领导权 | Leadership or dominance, especially by one country or social group over others. |  The United States has struggled to maintain its hegemony in the region. 美国一直在努力维持其在该地区的霸权地位。

The company's hegemony over the market was threatened by the arrival of a new competitor.  这家公司对市场的霸权地位受到了新竞争对手的威胁。

| 近义词：dominance, supremacy, leadership, control, authority | 反义词：subordination, weakness, impotence | 词性: noun
