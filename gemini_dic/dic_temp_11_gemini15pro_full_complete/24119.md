brewhouses:/ˈbruːˌhaʊzɪz/
n.|啤酒厂；酿酒坊|A building or part of a building where beer is brewed.
例句：The old brewhouses of the city are now popular tourist destinations. 城市里的老啤酒厂现在是热门的旅游目的地。

例句：Many brewhouses offer tours and tastings. 许多啤酒厂提供参观和品尝服务。
近义词：brewery, microbrewery
反义词: None
词性: Noun
