half-vellum:/ˌhɑːfˈvɛləm/ | n. | 半羊皮纸 | A bookbinding material consisting of a binding in which the spine and corners are covered in vellum or leather, and the sides are covered in cloth. | The antiquarian bookstore had several first editions bound in half-vellum.  这家古董书店有几本精装半羊皮纸面的初版书籍。| Synonyms: half-leather, quarter-bound | Antonyms: full-vellum, full-leather | noun

half-vellum:/ˌhɑːfˈvɛləm/ | adj. | 半羊皮纸的 | Bound with a spine and corners of vellum and sides of cloth. |  He purchased a half-vellum copy of Dickens' "Great Expectations". 他买了一本半羊皮纸面的《远大前程》。| Synonyms: half-leather, quarter-bound | Antonyms: full-vellum, full-leather | adjective
