overpowerfully:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊərfəli/
adverb
以压倒性的力量，极其强大地
with overwhelming power; extremely strongly.
The champion overpowerfully defeated his opponent. 这位冠军以压倒性的力量击败了他的对手。
The music overpowerfully filled the concert hall.  音乐极其强烈地充满了音乐厅。
Synonym: overwhelmingly, dominantly, irresistibly
Antonym: weakly, feebly, mildly
词性: 副词
