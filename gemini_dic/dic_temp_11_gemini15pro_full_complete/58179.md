drownings:/ˈdraʊnɪŋz/
noun
溺水事件（复数）
instances of people dying from suffocation in water.
There have been several drownings at the lake this summer. 今年夏天这个湖里发生了几起溺水事件。
synonyms: asphyxiations (in water), submersions (fatal)
antonyms: rescues (from water), resuscitations

词性：noun
