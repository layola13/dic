guiser:/ˈɡaɪzər/| n.| (苏格兰、爱尔兰)在万圣节或其他节日挨家挨户唱歌或表演以获取食物或钱的人|Especially in Scotland and Ireland, a person, usually a child, who goes from house to house at Halloween or other festivals in disguise, singing or performing in exchange for treats or money. |The guisers came to our door dressed as ghosts and witches.|Synonym: carol singer (in some contexts)|Antonym:  (No direct antonym)|noun
