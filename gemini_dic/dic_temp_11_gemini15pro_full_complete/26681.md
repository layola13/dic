cadaverine:/kəˈdævəˌriːn/
noun
尸胺
A foul-smelling diamine, NH2(CH2)5NH2, formed by the bacterial decomposition of animal tissue.
例句：Cadaverine is one of the compounds responsible for the putrid smell of decaying flesh. 尸胺是导致腐肉腐臭气味的原因之一。

例句：The presence of cadaverine can indicate the time of death in forensic investigations. 在法医调查中，尸胺的存在可以指示死亡时间。

近义词:putrescine
反义词:(None -  no direct opposite for a chemical compound)
词性:n.
