explicitness:/ɪkˈsplɪsɪtnəs/| n. |明确性；清晰性；直白|The explicitness of the instructions left no room for misinterpretation.  这些指示非常明确，没有误解的余地。|例句：The explicitness of the movie's violence shocked some viewers.  这部电影中暴力场面的直白程度震惊了一些观众。

例句：She appreciated the explicitness of his feedback, even though it was negative. 她很感激他直白的反馈，即使反馈是负面的。

近义词：clarity, directness, precision, unambiguousness|反义词：vagueness, ambiguity, obscurity, implicitness|词性:名词
