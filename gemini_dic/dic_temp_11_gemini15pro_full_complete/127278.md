navelwort:/ˈneɪvəlˌwɜːrt/| n. |脐景天，脐叶景天|A succulent plant of the genus *Umbilicus*, especially *Umbilicus rupestris*, with rounded, fleshy leaves and a spike of greenish flowers. It is also known as wall pennywort. Its leaves resemble a belly button, hence the name.|The navelwort thrives in dry, rocky environments.|近义词：pennywort, wall pennywort|反义词:|名词
