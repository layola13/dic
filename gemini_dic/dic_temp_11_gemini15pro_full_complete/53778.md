discommodity:/ˌdɪskəˈmɒdəti/ | n. | 不便之处，不利条件；商品价格下跌 | something that causes inconvenience, disadvantage, or trouble; a decline in the price of goods |  The traffic jams during rush hour are a real discommoditiy for commuters. 上下班高峰时间的交通堵塞对通勤者来说真的是很不方便。| The recent discommoditiy in oil prices has negatively impacted the economy. 最近石油价格下跌对经济产生了负面影响。 | 近义词：inconvenience, disadvantage, drawback, hindrance, burden, nuisance | 反义词：advantage, benefit, convenience, boon | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation) + commodity.  The original meaning referred to a decline in the price of goods.  Over time, it broadened to include anything that causes inconvenience.
