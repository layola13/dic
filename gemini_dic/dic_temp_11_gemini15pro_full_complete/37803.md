compendia:/kəmˈpɛndiə/| n. |概要，纲要，简编|A compendium is a concise collection of information on a particular subject, often presented in a book or other publication.  It typically summarizes a larger body of work.|This compendium of medical knowledge will be an invaluable resource for students. 这本医学知识概要将是学生们宝贵的资源。|synonyms: summary, abstract, synopsis, digest, overview|antonyms: expansion, elaboration, detail|noun

compendium:/kəmˈpɛndiəm/| n. |概要，纲要，简编|A compendium is a brief, comprehensive summary of a larger work or of a field of knowledge. |The professor assigned a compendium of Roman history for the midterm. 教授布置了一本罗马史概要作为期中考试的内容。|synonyms: compilation, collection, anthology|antonyms:  None (lack of a concise summary isn't a single word)|noun
