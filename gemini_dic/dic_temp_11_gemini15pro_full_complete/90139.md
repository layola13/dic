hotchpotch:/ˈhɒtʃpɒtʃ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtʃpɑːtʃ/ (US)| n. |大杂烩；混合物；混杂 |A confused mixture of different things.| The play was a hotchpotch of various dramatic styles.  这出戏是各种戏剧风格的大杂烩。|Synonyms: mixture, medley, jumble, mishmash, farrago, potpourri, gallimaufry |Antonyms: order, uniformity, consistency| Noun


hotpot:/ˈhɒtpɒt/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtpɑːt/ (US)| n. |火锅；大杂烩 |A stew of meat and vegetables, typically cooked in a pot on the stove or oven; (figuratively) a mixture of things.|  We had a delicious lamb hotpot for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了一顿美味的羊肉火锅。  The film was a hotpot of clichés and tired jokes. 这部电影是陈词滥调和老掉牙笑话的大杂烩。|Synonyms: stew, casserole, (figuratively) mixture, medley, jumble |Antonyms: (figuratively) order, uniformity, consistency | Noun
