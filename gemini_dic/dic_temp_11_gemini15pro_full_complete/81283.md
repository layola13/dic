grantee:/ɡrænˈtiː/
n.
受让人，被授予者
a person to whom something is granted (such as land or a right)
The grantee will receive the deed to the property. 受让人将收到房产的契据。
近义词:recipient, beneficiary
反义词:grantor
词性:名词
