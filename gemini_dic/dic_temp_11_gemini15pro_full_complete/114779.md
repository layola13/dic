malting:/ˈmɔːltɪŋ/ | n. | 麦芽制造; 制麦芽| The process of converting grain into malt, especially barley, by steeping, germinating, and drying. | The malting process takes several days.  制麦芽的过程需要几天时间。|  The brewery uses traditional floor malting. 这家啤酒厂采用传统的落地制麦芽法。 | 近义词: germination | 反义词:  None | 词性: Noun

malting:/ˈmɔːltɪŋ/ | adj. | 用于制麦芽的 | Related to or used for malting. |  Malting barley is a key ingredient in beer production. 制麦芽的大麦是啤酒生产的关键成分。 |  The farmer grows malting varieties of grain. 这位农民种植用于制麦芽的谷物品种。 | 近义词: brewing (in some contexts) | 反义词: None | 词性: Adjective
