forwardnesses:/ˈfɔːrwərdnəsɪz/
n.
过于大胆;鲁莽;冒失;唐突
The quality or state of being forward; boldness, presumptuousness, or impertinence.
Her forwardness in addressing the CEO directly surprised everyone. 她直接与首席执行官交谈的冒失行为让每个人都感到惊讶。
近义词: boldness, audacity, presumption, impertinence, pushiness
反义词: shyness, timidity, reticence, reserve, modesty
词性: 名词 (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From forward + -ness.  "Forward" traces back to Old English "foreweard," meaning "directed towards the front." The suffix "-ness" forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
