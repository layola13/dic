intuited:/ɪnˈtjuːɪtɪd/ | v. | 直觉到，凭直觉感知 | understood or worked something out based on feelings rather than facts |  She intuited that something was wrong. 她直觉到有些不对劲。|  sensed, perceived, divined | reasoned, deduced | verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *intuire, from Latin intueri "to look at, contemplate," from in- "in, on" + tueri "to watch, look at."
