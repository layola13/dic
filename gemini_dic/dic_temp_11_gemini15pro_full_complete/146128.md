overemphasizing: /ˌoʊvərˈɛmfəˌsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 过分强调；过分重视 | Placing too much importance or emphasis on something. | He was overemphasizing the importance of the test. 他过分强调了这次测试的重要性。 | The media is overemphasizing the negative aspects of the story. 媒体过分强调了故事的负面方面。 | 近义词: overstressing, exaggerating, overplaying | 反义词: understating, downplaying | 词性: 动词 (present participle of overemphasize)

overemphasize: /ˌoʊvərˈɛmfəˌsaɪz/ | v. | 过分强调；过分重视 | To give something too much importance or emphasis. |  Don't overemphasize the importance of money. 不要过分强调金钱的重要性。 |  She tends to overemphasize her achievements. 她倾向于过分强调她的成就。 | 近义词: overstress, exaggerate, overplay | 反义词: understate, downplay | 词性: 动词


词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "emphasize" (from French "emphasiser," ultimately from Greek "emphasis" meaning "significance, stress").
