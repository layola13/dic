Hittites:/ˈhɪtaɪts/
n.
赫梯人
A member of an ancient people who established an empire in Asia Minor and Syria between the 18th and 13th centuries BC.
The Hittites were known for their advanced ironworking skills. 赫梯人以其先进的铁器加工技术而闻名。
近义词: Anatolians (更广泛的地理术语)
词性: 名词
