congratulations: /kənˌɡrætʃuˈleɪʃənz/ | n. | 祝贺，恭喜 | an expression of joy in the success or good fortune of another. | Example: Congratulations on your graduation! 恭喜你毕业！ | Example:  Congratulations on the new baby! 恭喜你喜得贵子！| Synonym:  felicitations, best wishes | Antonym: condolences | noun
