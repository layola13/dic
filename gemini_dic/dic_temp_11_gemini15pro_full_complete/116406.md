masula:/məˈsuːlə/ | n. | 麻索拉，masula船 | A masula boat is a type of traditional, handcrafted boat from India, particularly associated with the Coromandel Coast. It is known for its unique construction and shallow draft, making it suitable for navigating shallow waters and estuaries. The term "masula" may also refer to the specific type of stitching or lashing used in the construction of these boats. | Example 1: The fishermen used a masula to navigate the shallow backwaters. 渔民们使用麻索拉船在浅水航行。
