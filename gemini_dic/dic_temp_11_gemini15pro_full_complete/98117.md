insalivation:/ɪnˌsælɪˈveɪʃən/
n.
分泌唾液；流涎
The process of producing saliva.
The sight and smell of the food caused insalivation.  看到和闻到食物的味道引起了唾液分泌。
Pavlov's dogs are famous for their insalivation in response to a bell. 巴甫洛夫的狗因听到铃声而分泌唾液而闻名。
近义词:salivation, drooling
反义词:xerostomia (口干症)
词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *insalivationem, from insalivatus "moistened with saliva," past participle of insalivare "to moisten with saliva," from in- "into, in" + saliva "saliva."
