antiquers:/ˈæntɪkwərz/
n.
古董商；古董收藏家
People who collect or deal in antiques.
例句：The antiquers flocked to the estate sale, hoping to find hidden treasures.  古董商们涌向房产拍卖会，希望能找到隐藏的宝藏。

例句：Seasoned antiquers can spot a fake a mile away.  经验丰富的古董商一眼就能看出赝品。

近义词：antique collectors, antique dealers
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


antique:/ænˈtiːk/
n.
古董；古物
An object that is old and often valuable.
例句：The museum houses a vast collection of Chinese antiques.  这家博物馆收藏了大量的中国古董。

例句：She inherited an antique clock from her grandmother. 她从祖母那里继承了一座古董钟。

近义词：relic, artifact
反义词: modern, contemporary
词性: noun, adjective


antiquing:/ænˈtiːkɪŋ/
n.
搜寻古董；古董收藏
The activity of searching for and buying antiques.
例句：Antiquing is a popular pastime for many people.  对许多人来说，搜寻古董是一种流行的消遣。

例句：We spent the weekend antiquing in the countryside. 我们在乡下度过了一个周末，寻找古董。

近义词：antique hunting, antique collecting
反义词: N/A
词性: noun, gerund
