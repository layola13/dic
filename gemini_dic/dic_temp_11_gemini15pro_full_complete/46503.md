dairymen:/ˈdeərimen/ | n. |奶农；乳品工人|A person, especially a man, who works in a dairy or operates a dairy farm.  A dairyman is responsible for the care and milking of cows, and the processing and distribution of milk and dairy products.| The dairymen were up early to milk the cows. 奶农们早起挤牛奶。| The dairymen delivered fresh milk to the local store every morning. 奶农们每天早上都把新鲜牛奶送到当地商店。| 近义词: milkman, farmer, agricultural worker | 反义词: consumer | 词性: 名词

词源:
From dairy + -men (plural suffix). Dairy itself comes from Middle English deyerie, from Old French deirie "dairy," from deie "dairymaid," from Vulgar Latin *deia, from Latin dea "goddess," used in Late Latin as "female servant."
