innovative: /ˈɪnəveɪtɪv/ | adj. | 创新的，革新的 | Introducing something new and original.  Characterized by new methods, ideas, or products. |  Example: The company is known for its innovative designs. 这家公司以其创新设计而闻名。

Example:  They developed an innovative approach to teaching mathematics.  他们开发了一种创新的数学教学方法。

近义词:  creative, original, groundbreaking, pioneering, ingenious, inventive, novel, cutting-edge, state-of-the-art

反义词:  traditional, conventional, outdated, old-fashioned, unoriginal

词性: adjective


词源:  Late Middle English: from Latin *innovativus, from innovare ‘renew, alter’ (see innovate).
