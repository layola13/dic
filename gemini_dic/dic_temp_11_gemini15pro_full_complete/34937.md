clobberer:/ˈklɒbərər/ (UK) /ˈklɑːbərər/ (US)| n. |痛击者，狠揍者|Someone who hits or defeats someone or something severely.|He's a real clobberer on the tennis court, his opponents rarely win a set. 他在网球场上是个狠角色，他的对手很少赢一盘。|The boxer was a feared clobberer in the ring.  这名拳击手在拳击场上是个令人畏惧的狠角色。|近义词：thumper, basher, hitter|反义词: loser, weakling|词性:noun

词源: From the verb "clobber," which means to hit hard, ultimately from a 19th-century underworld slang term.
