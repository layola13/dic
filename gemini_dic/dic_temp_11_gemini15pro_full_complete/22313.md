bonk:/bɒŋk/ (British), /bɑːŋk/ (American)| n. v. int.|碰撞，敲击；(俚语)性交|A dull, hollow sound, as of a blow against something; to hit or strike with a dull, hollow sound; (slang) to have sexual intercourse.|

例句：
I heard a bonk from the other room.
我听到另一个房间传来砰的一声。

The car bonked into the lamppost.
汽车撞上了路灯杆。

(slang) They bonked all night.
(俚语) 他们整晚都在做爱。


近义词：thump, knock, bang, hit, strike (for the non-slang meanings);  have sex, make love, sleep with (slang)|
反义词:(No direct antonym for the sound or action itself.  For the slang meaning, "abstain" could be considered an opposite.)|
词性: noun, verb, interjection|


bonk:/bɒŋk/ (British), /bɑːŋk/ (American)| v.| 变得精疲力竭；(俚语)性交|To become extremely tired or exhausted, often suddenly;  (slang) to have sexual intercourse.|


例句：
I bonked on the last mile of the marathon.
我在马拉松的最后一英里累垮了。

The runner bonked due to dehydration.
跑步者因脱水而体力不支。

(slang) They bonked after the party.
(俚语) 他们在派对后做爱了。



近义词：collapse, crash, give out,  (for exhaustion); have sex, make love, sleep with (slang)|
反义词:(No direct antonym for exhaustion. For the slang meaning, "abstain" could be considered an opposite.)|
词性: verb|
