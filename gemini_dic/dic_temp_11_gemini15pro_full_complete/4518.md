albuminiparous:/ˌælbjʊˈmɪnɪpərəs/
adjective
产卵蛋白的；卵白生的
Producing eggs in which the embryo is nourished by albumin.
The platypus is an albuminiparous mammal, laying eggs that are rich in albumin.  鸭嘴兽是一种产卵蛋白的哺乳动物，产的卵富含蛋白。
The echidna, like the platypus, is also albuminiparous.  针鼹和鸭嘴兽一样，也是产卵蛋白的。
近义词:oviparous
反义词:viviparous
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *albuminiparus (“producing albumin”), from albumin (“albumen”) + -parus (“-bearing, -producing”).
