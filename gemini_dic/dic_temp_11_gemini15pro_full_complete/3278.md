affirmingly:/əˈfɜːrmɪŋli/
adverb
肯定地；断然地
in a way that shows agreement or support
She nodded affirmingly. 她肯定地点了点头。
近义词:positively, assertively, definitely
反义词:negatively, uncertainly
词性:adverb
