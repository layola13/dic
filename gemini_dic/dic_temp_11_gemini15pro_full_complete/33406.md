churchiness:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃɪnɪs/
noun
虔诚；宗教气息；教会气氛
The quality of being excessively or ostentatiously devoted to church or religious practices.  A strong or excessive emphasis on the outward forms and rituals of religion.
The churchiness of the service made some attendees uncomfortable.  这个仪式过于宗教化，让一些参加者感到不舒服。
近义词: piety, devoutness, religiosity, sanctimoniousness
反义词: irreverence, impiety, secularism
词性: n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From church + -iness (a suffix forming nouns meaning "state or quality of being").  Church comes from Old English *cirice, from Proto-West Germanic *kirika, borrowed from Greek kyriakón "the Lord's (house)," neuter of kyriakós "of the Lord."
