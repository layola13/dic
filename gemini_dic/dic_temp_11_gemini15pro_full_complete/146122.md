overemphasize: /ˌoʊvərˈɛmfəˌsaɪz/ (American) | v. | 过分强调；过分重视 |  To place too much importance or emphasis on something; to exaggerate the importance or significance of something. |  Don't overemphasize the importance of money; happiness is more important. 不要过分强调金钱的重要性；幸福更重要。| She overemphasized her role in the project, making it seem like she did all the work. 她过分强调了她在项目中的作用，让人觉得她做了所有的工作。|近义词: exaggerate, overstate, overstress | 反义词: understate, downplay | 词性: 动词


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "too much") + "emphasize" (from French "emphasiser" and ultimately from Greek "emphasis" meaning "significance").
