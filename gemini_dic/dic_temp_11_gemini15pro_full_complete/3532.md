against the grain:/əˈɡenst ðə ɡreɪn/ | adv. |逆着潮流，违反意愿，格格不入|Going against the grain means doing something that is difficult or unpleasant because it contradicts your natural inclinations or the accepted way of doing things. It can also refer to wood that is difficult to work with because its fibers are not aligned.|例句：It goes against the grain to accept charity. 接受施舍让我很不自在。

例句：Sanding against the grain can ruin the finish of wood. 逆着纹理打磨会破坏木材的光洁度。|近义词：against one's nature, counterintuitive, awkward, difficult, unnaturally|反义词：naturally, with the grain, easily, smoothly, effortlessly|词性：副词
