hypostoma:/ˈhaɪpəˌstoʊmə/| n. |口下板；唇基|A plate-like or shield-like structure on the ventral side of the head in various arthropods, especially trilobites and certain crustaceans, often covering the mouth.|The hypostoma of this trilobite is well-preserved.  这只三叶虫的口下板保存完好。|Synonym: labrum (in some contexts)|Antonym: (No direct antonym)|noun
