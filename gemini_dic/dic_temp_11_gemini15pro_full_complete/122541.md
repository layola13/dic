modeler:/ˈmɒdələr/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdələr/ (US)| n.|建模者，模型制作者|A person who creates models, often using computer software or physical materials.  A modeler can work in various fields, such as engineering, architecture, fashion, or finance.|She's a 3D modeler for video games. 她是一名电子游戏3D建模师。|Synonyms: designer, creator, builder, architect, sculptor|Antonyms: destroyer, demolisher|noun


modeler:/ˈmɒdələr/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdələr/ (US)| n.|模拟者|A person who uses models to simulate or represent real-world systems or processes.|The economic modeler predicted a recession. 这位经济模拟者预测了经济衰退。|Synonyms: simulator, analyst, forecaster|Antonyms: N/A|noun
