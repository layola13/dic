distances:/ˈdɪstənsɪz/ /ˈdɪstənsəz/ | n. | 距离，路程 | The distance between two points is the length of the shortest path connecting them. |  The distance between New York and London is about 3,470 miles. 纽约到伦敦的距离大约是3470英里。

The marathon runner covered a long distance. 马拉松运动员跑完了很长的一段距离。 |近义词：length, space, gap, remoteness | 反义词：closeness, nearness, proximity | 词性: noun

distance:/ˈdɪstəns/ /ˈdɪstənsəz/ | v. |  使疏远，保持距离 | To distance oneself from someone or something is to become less involved or emotionally connected with them. | He distanced himself from the controversy. 他使自己远离了这场争议。

She has distanced herself from her family. 她与家人疏远了。 |近义词：separate, detach, alienate | 反义词：approach, engage | 词性: verb
