moonstricken:/ˈmuːnˌstrɪkən/| adj.|月光下的, 受月神影响的; 神经错乱的, 疯狂的|Affected by the moon; lunatic; crazed.  Driven to madness, supposedly by the moon.|例句：The moonstricken lovers wandered through the forest.  月光下的情侣在森林里漫步。|例句：He had a moonstricken look in his eyes. 他的眼神看起来有些疯狂。|近义词：lunatic, insane, mad, demented|反义词：sane, rational|词性:形容词

词源: From moon + stricken (past participle of strike). Originally referred to a condition thought to be caused by the moon, such as sleepwalking or madness.
