banalizes:/bəˈnəˌlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 使…平庸；使…陈腐；使…老套 | to make something seem less important, impressive, or serious by making it too familiar or common |  Overexposure to violence on television banalizes it, making viewers less sensitive to real-life suffering.  电视上过度曝光的暴力使其变得平庸，使观众对现实生活中的苦难不那么敏感。| synonyms: trivializes, vulgarizes, cheapens, degrades | antonyms: elevates, ennobles, dignifies | verb

banal:/bəˈnɑːl/ | adj. | 平庸的；陈腐的；老套的 | so lacking in originality as to be obvious and boring | The plot of the movie was banal and predictable.  这部电影的情节平庸且可预测。| synonyms: trite, hackneyed, clichéd, commonplace, stale | antonyms: original, fresh, novel, innovative, unique | adjective
