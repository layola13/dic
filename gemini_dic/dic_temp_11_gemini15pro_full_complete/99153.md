interchangeability:/ˌɪntərˌtʃeɪndʒəˈbɪləti/ | n. |可互换性，互换性 |The interchangeability of parts in a machine makes repairs easier. 机器零件的可互换性使维修更容易。|例句：The interchangeability of these two modules simplifies the design process. 这两个模块的可互换性简化了设计过程。

例句：The lack of interchangeability between the two systems caused significant problems.  这两个系统之间缺乏互换性导致了重大问题。

|近义词：substitutability, replaceability, exchangeability |反义词：incompatibility, non-interchangeability|词性:名词


interchangeable:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒəbl/ | adj. |可互换的，可交换的 |These parts are interchangeable, so you can use either one. 这些零件是可互换的，所以你可以使用其中任何一个。|例句：The batteries in these cameras are interchangeable. 这些相机里的电池是可互换的。

例句：The terms "soda" and "pop" are often interchangeable in American English.  在美式英语中，“soda”和“pop”这两个词通常可以互换使用。

|近义词：substitutable, replaceable, exchangeable|反义词：non-interchangeable, incompatible|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (between) + changeable (capable of being changed).  The suffix -ability forms nouns meaning "the quality or state of being able to be...".
