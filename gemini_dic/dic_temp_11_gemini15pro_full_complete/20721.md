blamable:/ˈbleɪməbl/| adj.|应受责备的，可责备的|Deserving censure; culpable.  Worthy of blame.|He was blamable for the accident because he was driving too fast. 他对这起事故负有责任，因为他开车太快了。|近义词：culpable, guilty, responsible, accountable, answerable|反义词：blameless, innocent, faultless, impeccable|词性:形容词
词源: From blame + -able.  Ultimately from the Old French word "blasmer" meaning "to blame".
