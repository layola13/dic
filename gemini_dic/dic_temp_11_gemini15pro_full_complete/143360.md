outfalls:/ˈaʊtfɔːlz/
n.
出水口；排污口
A place where water or sewage flows out from a pipe or channel.
The city's sewage outfalls discharge treated wastewater into the ocean.  这座城市的污水排放口将处理过的废水排入海洋。

The outfalls of the dam are carefully monitored for water quality. 大坝的出水口受到仔细监测，以确保水质。

近义词： outlets, discharges, exits
反义词： intakes, inlets
词性：名词
