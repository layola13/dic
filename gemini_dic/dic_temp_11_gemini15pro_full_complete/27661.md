candidatures:/ˈkændɪdəˌtʃʊəz/
n.
候选资格；申请；候选人身份
the fact of being a candidate in an election or for a job or position.
Many people submitted their candidatures for the vacant position. 许多人提交了这个空缺职位的申请。
synonyms: applications, nominations, submissions
antonyms: withdrawals, resignations
noun
