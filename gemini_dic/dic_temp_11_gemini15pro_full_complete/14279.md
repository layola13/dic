autography:/ɔːˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 亲笔，手稿 | The autography of the letter proved it was genuine. 这封信的亲笔证明了它是真的。| Example:  The historian studied the autography of the ancient manuscript. 历史学家研究了古代手稿的亲笔。| Synonym: handwriting, penmanship, chirography | Antonym: typing, printing | Noun
