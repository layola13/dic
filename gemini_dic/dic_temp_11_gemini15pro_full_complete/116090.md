martyrish:/ˈmɑːtɪrɪʃ/| adj.|殉道者似的，装作殉道者的|Having or showing the qualities or behavior of a martyr, especially an exaggerated or affected seriousness or sense of suffering.|She had a martyrish expression on her face as she washed the dishes. 她洗碗时脸上带着一副殉道者的表情。|近义词：self-sacrificing, long-suffering, victimized|反义词：selfish, uncomplaining|词性:形容词
词源: From martyr + -ish.
