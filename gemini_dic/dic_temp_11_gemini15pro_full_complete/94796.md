impugnment:/ɪmˈpʌɡnmənt/
|n.|质疑，非难，攻击|An act of impugning something; a calling into question; an attack on the integrity or validity of something.|The lawyer's impugnment of the witness's testimony cast doubt on the entire case. 律师对证人证词的质疑使整个案件蒙上了阴影。|近义词：challenge, question, dispute, attack, criticism|反义词：defense, support, endorsement, praise|词性:名词

impugn:/ɪmˈpjuːn/
|vt.|质疑，非难，攻击|dispute the truth, validity, or honesty of (a statement, character, or motive); call into question.|The politician's opponent impugned his motives.  政客的对手质疑他的动机。|近义词：challenge, question, dispute, attack, criticize|反义词：defend, support, endorse, praise|词性:动词
