eneid:/ˈiːniɪd, ˈɛnɪɪd/| n. |《埃涅阿斯纪》|An epic poem by Virgil, relating the adventures of Aeneas, a Trojan hero, after the fall of Troy. He eventually journeyed to Italy and became the ancestor of the Romans. |例句：The Aeneid is considered one of the greatest works of Latin literature.  《埃涅阿斯纪》被认为是拉丁文学最伟大的作品之一。

例句：Virgil's Aeneid tells the story of the founding of Rome. 维吉尔的《埃涅阿斯纪》讲述了罗马的建国故事。|近义词：epic, poem|反义词: None|词性: Noun
