cloaking:/ˈkloʊkɪŋ/| n.|掩盖，隐瞒，伪装|The act of concealing or disguising something, often for deceptive purposes.  It can refer to hiding something physical, like a weapon, or something more abstract, like one's true intentions.|The wizard used a cloaking spell to become invisible.  巫师使用隐身咒使自己隐形。

The company was accused of cloaking its illegal activities. 这家公司被指控掩盖其非法活动。|近义词：concealing, disguising, masking, hiding, camouflaging|反义词：revealing, exposing, unmasking, showing|词性:noun
