dandydom:/ˈdændɪdəm/
|n.|花花公子界，纨绔子弟的气派，讲究衣着的男士群体|The world of dandies; the characteristic behavior, style, or attitudes of dandies.|He was welcomed into the dandydom of London's elite. 他受到了伦敦精英花花公子界的欢迎。|近义词：foppery, stylishness, elegance|反义词：shabbiness, unstylishness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From dandy + -dom.  "Dandy" originates from the late 18th century, of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Andrew, or a pet-form of the given name Andrew. "-dom" is an Old English suffix meaning "state, condition, dignity, office, realm."
