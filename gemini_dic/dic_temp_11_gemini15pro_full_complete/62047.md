encapsulate:/ɪnˈkæpsjʊleɪt/ /ɛnˈkæpsjʊleɪt/ | v. |封装，概括，囊括|To enclose something in or as if in a capsule. To express the essential features of something succinctly.|The artist encapsulated the entire history of the war in one painting. 艺术家将整个战争史浓缩在一幅画中。|synonyms: summarize, condense, epitomize, abstract|antonyms: expand, elaborate, detail|verb

encapsulate:/ɪnˈkæpsjʊleɪt/ /ɛnˈkæpsjʊleɪt/ | n. |胶囊|A small container, typically of gelatin, enclosing a dose of medicine.|The medication is available in capsule form. 这种药物有胶囊剂型。|synonyms: capsule, tablet, pill|antonyms: N/A|noun
