hocus-pocus:/ˈhoʊkəs ˈpoʊkəs/| n.  |戏法；花招；胡言乱语；欺骗|An expression used when performing a trick, suggesting that something magical is happening, or to describe deceptive or misleading talk.|The magician waved his wand and muttered "hocus pocus" before pulling a rabbit out of the hat. 魔术师挥舞着魔杖，低声说着“胡言乱语”，然后从帽子里拉出一只兔子。|近义词：trickery, deception, sleight of hand, magic, mumbo jumbo|反义词: honesty, truth, reality|词性:名词

hocus-pocus:/ˈhoʊkəs ˈpoʊkəs/| v.  |变戏法；施魔法；欺骗|To trick or deceive someone, often using misleading or nonsensical language.|He hocus-pocused his way into getting a promotion. 他用欺骗的手段得到了晋升。|近义词：trick, deceive, mislead, bamboozle|反义词: be honest, be truthful|词性:动词
