brandied:/ˈbrændid/ | adj. | 浸过白兰地的; 白兰地味的 | Preserved in or flavored with brandy. | The brandied cherries were a delicious addition to the dessert. 浸过白兰地的樱桃是这道甜点美味的补充。| Synonyms: alcohol-soaked, liquor-infused | Antonyms: unflavored, plain | word type: adjective

Etymology:  From brandy + -ed. Brandy itself comes from the Dutch word "brandewijn," meaning "burnt wine."
