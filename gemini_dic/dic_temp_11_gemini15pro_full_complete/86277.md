heaviness:/ˈhɛvinəs/| n.|沉重, 沉闷, 阴沉|The heaviness in her heart made it difficult to breathe. 她心中的沉重让她难以呼吸。|例句：The heaviness of the wet clothes made it hard to walk. 湿衣服的沉重使行走困难。

例句：A feeling of heaviness settled over him as he heard the bad news.  听到坏消息后，一种沉重的感觉笼罩着他。|近义词：weight, burden, gravity, oppression, gloom, dullness|反义词：lightness, buoyancy, cheerfulness, brightness|词性:名词

Etymology: From Middle English *hevinesse, from Old English *hefignes, from hefian (“to make heavy”) + -nes (“-ness”). Cognate with Dutch hevigheid.
