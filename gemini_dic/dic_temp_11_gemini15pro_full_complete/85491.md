hastated:/ˈheɪsteɪtɪd/| adj.|戟形的，矛状的|Shaped like a spearhead with barbs projecting at an angle from the base.|The leaf of that plant is hastated.  那种植物的叶子是戟形的。|The hastated markings on the butterfly's wings were striking. 蝴蝶翅膀上的戟形斑纹引人注目。|近义词：spear-shaped, lanceolate|反义词:rounded, ovate|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *hastatus, from hasta (“spear”).
