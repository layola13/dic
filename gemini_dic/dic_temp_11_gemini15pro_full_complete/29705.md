catholicalnesses: /ˌkæθəˈlɪkəlnəsɪz/ | n. | 普遍性，广泛性; 包容性 | The state or quality of being catholic or universal; broadmindedness; comprehensiveness. |  His philosophical writings are marked by a catholicalness of taste. 他的哲学著作的特点是品味广泛。 | 近义词: universality, comprehensiveness, broad-mindedness, tolerance, ecumenism | 反义词: narrowness, intolerance, bigotry, parochialism | 词性: 名词

词源:  From "catholical" + "-ness."  "Catholical" comes from Late Latin "catholicalis,"  from Greek "katholikos" meaning "universal, general."  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality.
