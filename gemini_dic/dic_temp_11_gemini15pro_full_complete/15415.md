bactericidal: /ˌbækˌtɪrɪˈsaɪdəl/
adjective
杀菌的
Capable of killing bacteria.
This new disinfectant is highly bactericidal.  这种新的消毒剂杀菌力很强。
近义词: germicidal, disinfectant, antiseptic
反义词: bacteriostatic
词性: adjective
