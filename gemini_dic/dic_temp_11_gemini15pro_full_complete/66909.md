ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *expeditus* (past participle of *expedire* "to extricate, disengage, set free; prepare, make ready"), from *ex-* "out" + *pes* (genitive *pedis*) "foot."  The suffix *-ness* forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
