breaststroke: /ˈbrɛstˌstroʊk/ | n. v. | 蛙泳 | A style of swimming in which both arms move forward together and then sweep back at the same time, while the legs kick outward and backward in a frog-like motion. |  She won the gold medal in the breaststroke. 她在蛙泳比赛中获得了金牌。|  近义词: None | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词, 动词

词源: breast + stroke. "breast" refers to the movement of the arms resembling a hug, and "stroke" refers to a single complete movement of the arms and legs in swimming.
