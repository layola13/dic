abscission:/æbˈsɪʒən/ | n. |脱落，分离|The abscission of leaves in the autumn is a natural process. 秋天树叶的脱落是一个自然过程。|例句：The abscission of the fruit from the tree was caused by the wind.  水果从树上脱落是风造成的。

例句：The surgeon performed an abscission of the damaged tissue.  外科医生切除了受损的组织。|近义词：separation, detachment, shedding|反义词：attachment, adherence|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin abscissio, from Latin abscindere "to cut off," from ab- "off, away from" + scindere "to cut, split, cleave."
