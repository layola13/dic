jamlike:/ˈdʒæmlaɪk/
adjective
像果酱的; 果酱状的
Resembling jam in consistency, texture, or appearance.
The cake had a jamlike filling.  这个蛋糕有一个像果酱一样的馅。
近义词:jellylike, preserves-like
反义词:runny, watery
词性:形容词
