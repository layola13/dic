counterbids:/ˈkaʊntərˌbɪdz/ | n. |还价，反要约，竞争性报价|An offer made in response to a previous bid, typically a higher offer in an auction or negotiation. |  The company received several counterbids after their initial offer was rejected.  这家公司在最初的报价被拒绝后收到了几份还价。| synonyms: competing bids, rival offers, higher bids | antonyms: offers, bids | noun


counterbid:/ˈkaʊntərˌbɪd/ | v. |还价，反要约，竞争性报价|To make an offer in response to a previous bid, typically a higher offer in an auction or negotiation. |  She counterbid with a price that surprised everyone. 她以一个令所有人惊讶的价格还价。| synonyms: outbid, offer more, raise the bid | antonyms: accept, concede, give in | verb
