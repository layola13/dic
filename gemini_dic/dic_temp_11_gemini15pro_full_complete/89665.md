hoopa:/ˈhuːpə/ | n. |胡帕|A mythical Pokémon introduced in Generation VI. It is a Psychic/Ghost or Psychic/Dark type. Hoopa is known for its mischievous nature and ability to create portals with its rings. |Hoopa appeared from a ring and stole all the food! 胡帕从一个环里出现，偷走了所有的食物！| 近义词: (As a mischievous being) imp, gremlin | 反义词: (Considering its dual typing and lore) guardian, protector | 词性: noun
