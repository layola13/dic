outlasting:/ˌaʊtˈlæstɪŋ/
verb|比...持久；比...活得长；经受住|surviving or enduring longer than. |The pyramids have outlasted many empires. 金字塔比许多帝国存在的时间都要长。|近义词：outlive, survive, endure|反义词:succumb, perish|v.

outlasting:/ˌaʊtˈlæstɪŋ/
adjective|持久的；耐久的|That lasts longer than something else. |This building has an outlasting foundation. 这栋建筑的地基非常耐久。|近义词：durable, long-lasting, enduring|反义词:short-lived, temporary|adj.
