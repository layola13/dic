inconstant:/ɪnˈkɒnstənt/ (US: /ɪnˈkɑːnstənt/)
adjective
反复无常的；易变的；不稳定的
Not constant; changeable.
The weather has been inconstant this spring, changing from sunshine to rain several times a day.
今年春天天气变化无常，一天之内晴雨交替好几次。
Synonyms: fickle, capricious, erratic, mercurial, volatile
Antonyms: constant, steady, stable, unwavering, steadfast
词性: 形容词
