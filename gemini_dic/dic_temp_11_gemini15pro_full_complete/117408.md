medicinally:/məˈdɪsɪnəli/
adverb
药用地；从医学角度
in a way that relates to medicine
例句：The herb can be used medicinally. 这种草药可以药用。

例句：Garlic is used medicinally to reduce cholesterol. 大蒜药用可降低胆固醇。
synonym: therapeutically, remedially
antonym: harmfully, detrimentally
词性: 副词
