empanelled:/ɪmˈpænəld/ |v. |组成陪审团；选定（陪审团）|to enroll or list (jurors) on a panel. |The judge empanelled a jury of twelve citizens.|近义词：enroll, select, list, constitute|反义词: discharge, dismiss|词性:动词

Etymology:
From en- + panel + -ed.  "Panel" comes from Old French "panel" meaning a piece of cloth, parchment, or wood.
