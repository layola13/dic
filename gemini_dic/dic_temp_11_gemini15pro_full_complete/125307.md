multigranular:/ˌmʌltiˈɡrænjʊlər/
adjective
多粒度的，多层次的
Having multiple levels of granularity; involving or characterized by several different levels of detail or scope.
Multigranular computing allows for flexible and efficient data processing. 多粒度计算允许灵活高效的数据处理。
The multigranular approach to security ensures data protection at various levels. 多粒度安全方法确保了不同级别的数据保护。
Near Synonyms: hierarchical, layered, stratified, tiered
Antonyms: uniform, homogeneous
word type: adjective
