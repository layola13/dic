colorimetry:/ˌkʌlərˈɪmətri/ | n. | 比色法，色度学 | The science of measuring and specifying colours, especially using a colorimeter. | Colorimetry is used to ensure consistent colour in products like textiles and paints. 比色法用于确保纺织品和油漆等产品的颜色一致。 | synonyms: colorimetric analysis, spectrophotometry | antonyms: N/A | noun

colorimetric:/ˌkʌlərɪˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 比色的，色度的 | Relating to or involving colorimetry. | Colorimetric assays are commonly used in biochemistry. 比色测定法常用于生物化学。| synonyms: colorimetrical | antonyms: N/A | adjective
