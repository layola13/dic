furziest:/ˈfɜːrziɪst/| adj.|最多毛的，最毛茸茸的|Covered with the most fur; most furry.|例句：He has the furziest cat I've ever seen. 他拥有我见过毛最多的猫。

例句：That blanket is the furziest and warmest one in the store. 那条毯子是店里最毛茸茸、最暖和的。|近义词：hairiest, shaggiest, fluffiest|反义词：smoothest, baldest|词性:形容词

词源:  From *furry* + *-est* (superlative suffix).  *Furry* comes from *fur* + *-y*.  *Fur* ultimately derives from Old French *forre*, meaning "sheath, lining."
