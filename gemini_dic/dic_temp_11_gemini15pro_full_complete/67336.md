extendability:/ɪkˌstendəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可扩展性，可延伸性 | The quality of being extendable; the capacity to be extended or enlarged. | The extendability of the software allows for easy addition of new features.  该软件的可扩展性允许轻松添加新功能。 | 近义词：scalability, flexibility, adaptability | 反义词：inelasticity, rigidity | 词性: noun

Etymology: From "extendable" + "-ity". "Extendable" comes from "extend" (from Latin "extendere" - to stretch out) + "-able" (suffix indicating capability).
