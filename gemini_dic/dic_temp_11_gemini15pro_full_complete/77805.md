generally:/ˈdʒen(ə)rəli/ /ˈdʒɛnərəli/| adv.|通常地，一般地，普遍地|Usually; in most cases; widely.|例句：Generally, I go to bed early. 我通常很早睡觉。|近义词：usually, typically, normally, commonly, ordinarily|反义词：rarely, seldom, unusually, exceptionally|词性：副词

Etymology: From Late Latin *generaliter*, from Latin *generalis* (general) + *-iter* (adverbial suffix).
