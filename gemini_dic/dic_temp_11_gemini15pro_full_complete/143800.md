outorganize:/ˌaʊtˈɔːrɡənaɪz/
verb
胜过组织；以更好的组织战胜
to organize something more effectively than someone else
例句：They outorganized their competitors and gained a larger market share. 他们比竞争对手组织得更好，获得了更大的市场份额。

例句：The smaller army outorganized the larger one and won the battle.  规模较小的军队组织得比规模较大的军队更好，赢得了这场战斗。
近义词：outmaneuver, outsmart, surpass
反义词：mismanage, disorganize
词性：v.
