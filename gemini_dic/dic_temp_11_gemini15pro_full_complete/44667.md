crucifer: /ˈkruːsɪfər/ | n. | 十字花科植物；十字花科蔬菜 | A plant of the family Cruciferae (Brassicaceae), such as cabbage, cauliflower, or mustard.  These plants have flowers with four petals in the shape of a cross. |  Broccoli is a crucifer and is very healthy. 西兰花是十字花科植物，非常健康。| Kale, a leafy green crucifer, is packed with nutrients. 羽衣甘蓝，一种绿叶十字花科蔬菜，富含营养。 | synonyms: brassica, cruciferous plant | antonyms: N/A | noun

cruciferous: /kruːˈsɪfərəs/ | adj. | 十字花科的 | Belonging to the Cruciferae (Brassicaceae) family; having flowers with four petals arranged in a cross shape. |  Cruciferous vegetables are known for their health benefits. 十字花科蔬菜以其健康益处而闻名。 | Many cruciferous plants are grown for food. 许多十字花科植物被种植用作食物。| synonyms: brassicaceous | antonyms: N/A | adjective
