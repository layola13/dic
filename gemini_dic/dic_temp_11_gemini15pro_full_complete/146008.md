overdosing:/ˌoʊvərˈdoʊsɪŋ/ | n. | 过量服用；过量用药 | The act of taking more of a drug or medication than is prescribed or recommended, often leading to harmful or fatal consequences. | He was hospitalized for overdosing on sleeping pills. 他因过量服用安眠药而住院。 | overdose (n.), overdosage (n.) | underdose (n.), moderate dose (n.) | noun

overdose:/ˈoʊvərdoʊs/ | n. | 过量；过量服用 | An excessive and dangerous amount of a drug or medication. |  She took an overdose of painkillers. 她服用了过量的止痛药。| excess, surfeit | deficiency, underdose | noun
