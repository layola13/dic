outslandering: /aʊtˈslændərɪŋ/
Verb
诽谤，诋毁
The act of making false and damaging statements about someone.
He is outslandering his political opponents with lies and misinformation. 他用谎言和虚假信息诽谤他的政治对手。
She was accused of outslandering her former employer. 她被指控诽谤她的前雇主。
Synonyms: defaming, libeling, slandering, vilifying, maligning, disparaging, denigrating
Antonyms: praising, commending, lauding, extolling, complimenting
