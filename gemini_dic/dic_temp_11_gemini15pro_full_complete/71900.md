flexer:/ˈflɛksər/| n.|炫耀者，摆阔者|Someone who boasts about their possessions or achievements, often on social media.  A person who shows off.|He's such a flexer, always posting pictures of his expensive watch. 他真是个爱炫耀的人，总是晒他昂贵的手表的照片。|近义词：show-off, braggart, boaster|反义词：humble person, modest person|noun
