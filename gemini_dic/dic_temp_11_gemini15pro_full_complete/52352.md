dictatorship:/dɪkˈteɪtərʃɪp/ | n. |独裁统治; 专政|A form of government in which one person or a small group possesses absolute power without effective constitutional limitations.|The country suffered under a brutal dictatorship for decades. 这个国家在残酷的独裁统治下遭受了几十年的苦难。|Synonyms: tyranny, despotism, autocracy|Antonyms: democracy, republic|Noun


dictate:/dɪkˈteɪt/ | v. |口述; 命令; 支配; 决定|To say or read aloud words to be typed, written down, or recorded; to give orders authoritatively; to impose or determine.|He dictated a letter to his secretary. 他向秘书口述了一封信。
The weather will dictate our plans for the weekend. 天气将决定我们周末的计划。|Synonyms: command, order, prescribe, impose|Antonyms: suggest, request, obey, comply |Verb
