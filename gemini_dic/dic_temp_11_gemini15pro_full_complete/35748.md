cochlea:/ˈkɒkliə/ (UK) /ˈkoʊkliə/ (US)| n. |耳蜗|The spiral cavity of the inner ear containing the organ of Corti, which produces nerve impulses in response to sound vibrations.|The cochlea is a vital part of the auditory system. 耳蜗是听觉系统的重要组成部分。|The damage to her cochlea resulted in hearing loss. 她的耳蜗受损导致听力损失。|近义词:inner ear (not a direct synonym, but refers to the location)|反义词:None|词性:noun

cochlear:/ˈkɒkliə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkoʊkliər/ (US)| adj. |耳蜗的|Relating to the cochlea.|Cochlear implants can restore hearing in some deaf individuals.  人工耳蜗可以帮助一些失聪人士恢复听力。|He underwent cochlear surgery to repair the damage. 他接受了耳蜗手术来修复损伤。|近义词:aural, auditory|反义词:None|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cochlea* "snail, snail shell, screw," from Greek *kokhlias* "spiral, snail, screw," related to *kokhlos* "spiral shell."  The word reflects the spiral, snail-shell-like shape of the cochlea.
