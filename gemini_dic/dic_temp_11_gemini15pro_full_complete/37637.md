commutation:/ˌkɑːmjuˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 交换；减刑；通勤 | the act of exchanging one thing for another; the reduction of a punishment to a less severe one; the act of traveling regularly between one's home and one's place of work |  The commutation of his death sentence to life imprisonment was a controversial decision.  他的死刑被减为无期徒刑是一个有争议的决定。 | synonyms: exchange, substitution, reduction, remission, pardon, commute | antonyms:  increase,  aggravation, extension | noun

commute: /kəˈmjuːt/ | v. | 交换；减刑；通勤 | to exchange one thing for another; to reduce a punishment to a less severe one; to travel regularly between one's home and one's place of work | He commuted his prison sentence to community service. 他将刑期改为社区服务。| synonyms: exchange, substitute, reduce, remit, pardon | antonyms:  increase, aggravate, extend | verb
