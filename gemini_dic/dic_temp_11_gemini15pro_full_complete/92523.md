hypotrichous:/ˌhaɪpəˈtrɪkəs/| adj.|少毛的，毛发稀疏的|Having a deficient amount of hair or trichomes.  Relating to or characteristic of the Hypotrichida, a group of ciliate protozoans.|Hypotrichous animals, like the naked mole rat, are adapted to subterranean life. 裸鼹鼠等少毛动物适应地下生活。|Near synonyms: hairless, glabrous, bald|Antonyms: hirsute, hairy, pilose|adjective
