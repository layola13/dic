fossils:/ˈfɑːsəlz/ /ˈfɒsɪlz/ | n. | 化石 | The remains or impression of a prehistoric plant or animal embedded in rock and preserved. |  The museum has a fascinating collection of dinosaur fossils.  这家博物馆收藏了令人惊叹的恐龙化石。 | synonyms: remains, relics, vestiges | antonyms:  living organisms, extant species | noun
