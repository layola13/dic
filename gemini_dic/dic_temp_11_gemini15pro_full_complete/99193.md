interclash:/ˌɪntərˈklæʃ/
noun
冲突，抵触
a violent clash or conflict between two or more groups, especially one that recurs.
The interclash between the two rival gangs resulted in several injuries. 两个敌对帮派之间的冲突导致数人受伤。
近义词：conflict, clash, collision, encounter
反义词：harmony, agreement, peace
词性：n.

Etymology:  "inter-" (prefix meaning "between, among") + "clash" (from Old Norse *klasa "to make a noise").  The word suggests a clashing *between* things.
