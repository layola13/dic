changelings:/ˈtʃeɪndʒlɪŋz/| n. |婴灵，被替换的孩子，被偷偷替换的孩子|A child believed to have been secretly substituted by fairies for the parents' real child; an impostor or substitute.|例句：In folklore, changelings were often blamed for unexplained illnesses or misfortunes within a family.  在民间传说中，婴灵经常被认为是家庭中不明疾病或不幸的原因。

例句：The mother feared her sickly child was a changeling, swapped in the night by mischievous fairies.  母亲担心她体弱多病的孩子是妖精在夜里偷偷替换的婴灵。|近义词：impostor, substitute, double, replacement|反义词:original, genuine|词性:名词
