gingerbread: /ˈdʒɪndʒərbred/ (US) /ˈdʒɪndʒəbred/ (UK)| n. adj.| 姜饼, 姜饼色的 |A type of cookie or cake flavored with ginger and other spices, often made in decorative shapes, especially at Christmas. Also used as an adjective to describe something that resembles gingerbread in color or style. |例句：We decorated gingerbread houses for Christmas. 我们在圣诞节装饰了姜饼屋。

The house had charming gingerbread trim. 这房子有迷人的姜饼色装饰。|近义词：ginger snap, cookie|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "plain biscuit" for the noun form) |词性: noun, adjective
