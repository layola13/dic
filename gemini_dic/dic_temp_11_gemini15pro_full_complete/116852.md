maximization:/ˌmæksɪmɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |最大化，最大限度|The process of making something as large or great as possible. |The company's goal is the maximization of profit.  这家公司的目标是利润最大化。| 近义词: optimization, enhancement, amplification | 反义词: minimization, reduction | noun

maximise (verb form): /ˈmæksɪmaɪz/ | v. |最大化，使…最大限度|To increase something as much as possible. |We need to maximise our resources. 我们需要最大限度地利用我们的资源。|近义词: optimize, enhance, amplify | 反义词: minimize, reduce | verb
