mazily:/ˈmeɪzɪli/| adv.|茫然地，恍惚地，懒洋洋地|In a dreamy, absent-minded, or indolent manner.  Derived from "maze" suggesting a state of being lost or confused, extended to imply a lack of focus or energy.|She smiled mazily, lost in thought. 她茫然地笑着，陷入了沉思。|Synonyms: dreamily, vaguely, languidly, listlessly|Antonyms: alertly, energetically, attentively|词性:副词
