break-out:/ˈbreɪkaʊt/| n. adj.|爆发，突围，越狱|A sudden escape from prison or confinement; a sudden increase in the incidence of a disease.|例句：There was a breakout at the local prison last night. 昨晚当地监狱发生了一起越狱事件。|近义词：escape, eruption, outbreak|反义词:imprisonment, containment|noun

break-out:/ˈbreɪkaʊt/| adj.|爆发的，突围的|Relating to or involving a sudden escape or increase.|例句：The breakout performance of the young actor surprised everyone.  这位年轻演员的突破性表演让所有人感到惊讶。|近义词：eruptive, sudden|反义词:contained, controlled|adjective
