nongravitational: /ˌnɒnˌɡrævɪˈteɪʃənəl/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˌɡrævɪˈteɪʃənəl/ (US) | adj. | 非引力的；非重力的 | Relating to or denoting forces other than gravity. | The nongravitational forces acting on the spacecraft include solar radiation pressure and atmospheric drag.  航天器受到的非重力包括太阳辐射压力和大气阻力。|  The asteroid's trajectory is influenced by nongravitational perturbations. 这颗小行星的轨迹受到非引力扰动的影响。 |Synonyms:  agravitational, anemobaric (specifically referring to pressure differences)| Antonyms: gravitational |词性: 形容词
