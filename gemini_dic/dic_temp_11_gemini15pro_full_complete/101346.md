iodyrite:/ˈaɪoʊdəˌraɪt/ | n. |碘银矿|A naturally occurring mineral form of silver iodide (AgI). It is a yellowish, hexagonal crystal.  It darkens on exposure to light.|The miners discovered a vein of iodyrite. 矿工们发现了一条碘银矿脉。|Synonyms:  silver iodide, mineral silver iodide|Antonyms: N/A (as it is a specific mineral, it doesn't have a direct opposite)|noun
