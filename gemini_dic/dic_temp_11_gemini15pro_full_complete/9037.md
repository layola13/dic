antimaterialistically:/ˌæntiˌməˈtɪəriəˈlɪstɪkli/
| adv. |反物质主义地|  Opposing or rejecting materialism, which is the focus on acquiring material possessions and valuing them over spiritual, intellectual, or cultural values.| She lived antimaterialistically, valuing experiences over possessions. 她过着反物质主义的生活，重视体验而不是物质财富。|近义词：spiritually, ascetically, simply|反义词：materialistically, consumeristically|词性: 副词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek "anti-")
materialistic (adjective): excessively concerned with material possessions; relating to materialism (from Late Latin "materialis")
-ally (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives (from Old English "-lice")
