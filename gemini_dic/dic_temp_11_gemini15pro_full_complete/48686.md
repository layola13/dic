defeudalize:/diːˈfjuːdəlaɪz/
verb
废除封建制度；消除封建残余
To abolish feudalism; to free from feudalism.
The French Revolution aimed to defeudalize the country and establish a republic. 法国大革命旨在废除国家的封建制度，建立共和国。
The government implemented policies to defeudalize land ownership. 政府实施政策以消除土地所有制的封建残余。
近义词:  abolish, dismantle, eradicate
反义词: feudalize, strengthen
词性: verb
