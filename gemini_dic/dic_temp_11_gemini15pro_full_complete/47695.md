debunkery:/diːˈbʌŋkəri/
n.
揭穿；揭露；拆穿
The act of exposing false claims or myths.
The book is a work of thorough debunkery, exposing the fraudulent claims of the so-called "miracle cure."  这本书是一部彻底的揭穿之作，揭露了所谓的“神奇疗法”的欺诈性说法。
The website is dedicated to the debunkery of popular conspiracy theories.  这个网站致力于揭穿流行的阴谋论。
Synonyms: exposure, debunking, refutation
Antonyms:  promotion, advocacy, support
noun
