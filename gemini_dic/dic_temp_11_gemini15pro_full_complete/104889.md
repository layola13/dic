keeperless:/ˈkiːpərlɪs/
adjective
无管理员的；无人照管的；无保管人的
lacking a keeper; not kept or guarded; unattended
The keeperless zoo animals roamed free after the earthquake. 地震后，无人看管的动物园动物四处游荡。
Synonyms: unattended, unguarded, neglected, abandoned
Antonyms: guarded, protected, attended, supervised
词性: 形容词
