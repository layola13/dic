baying:/ˈbeɪɪŋ/| n. |吠叫，咆哮；强烈要求|a deep prolonged howl, as of a hound on the scent; a clamor of voices, esp. in protest or opposition|The baying of the hounds echoed through the forest. 猎犬的吠叫声在森林中回荡。|近义词：howling, barking, clamoring|反义词: whimpering, whispering|noun

baying:/ˈbeɪɪŋ/| adj. |吠叫的，咆哮的|giving or uttering a deep prolonged howl; clamoring|The baying mob demanded justice.  咆哮的暴徒要求正义。|近义词：howling, barking, clamoring|反义词: quiet, silent|adjective
