cantata:/kænˈtɑːtə/ | n. |清唱剧; 康塔塔|A medium-length narrative piece of music for voices with instrumental accompaniment, typically with solos, chorus, and orchestra.| Example: Bach's cantatas are masterpieces of the Baroque period. 巴赫的清唱剧是巴洛克时期的杰作。| Example: The choir performed a beautiful Christmas cantata.  唱诗班表演了一首优美的圣诞清唱剧。|Synonym: oratorio, choral work| Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps instrumental piece) | Noun
