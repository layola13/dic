bisulcate:/baɪˈsʌlkeɪt/ | adj. |二沟的，双沟的| Having two grooves or furrows.  Specifically referring to hooves of ruminant animals (like cows, sheep, deer) that are cloven or divided into two parts. It can also be used more generally to describe anything with two grooves. | The bisulcate hoof of the cow left a distinct print in the mud.  牛的偶蹄在泥里留下了清晰的印记。| The archaeologist identified the bone as belonging to a bisulcate mammal. 考古学家鉴定这块骨头属于一种偶蹄哺乳动物。 |  cloven, forked, divided | undivided, whole | adjective
