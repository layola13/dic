characterized:/ˈkærəktəˌraɪzd/| v. |具有…的特征；以…为特征| This area is characterized by its hot, dry summers and mild winters. 这个地区的特点是夏季炎热干燥，冬季温和。|例句：Her work is characterized by meticulous attention to detail. 她的工作特点是一丝不苟地注重细节。|近义词：distinguished, marked, typified|反义词：uncharacteristic, atypical|词性:动词

characterized:/ˈkærəktəˌraɪzd/| adj. |被赋予…特征的|His reign was characterized by peace and prosperity. 他的统治以和平与繁荣为特征。|例句：The disease is characterized by a high fever and a persistent cough. 这种疾病的特征是高烧和持续咳嗽。|近义词：defined, identified, known|反义词：undefined, unknown|词性:形容词
