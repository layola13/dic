insemination:/ɪnˌsɛmɪˈneɪʃən/| n.|授精，人工授精|The act of introducing semen into the vagina or uterus, especially for the purpose of reproduction.|Artificial insemination is an option for couples struggling with infertility. 人工授精是难以受孕的夫妇的一种选择。|Near synonyms: impregnation, fertilization|Antonym: none|noun


insemination:/ɪnˌsɛmɪˈneɪʃən/| n.|播种|The introduction of seeds or other propagules into the ground for propagation.|The insemination of the field with wheat seed took place in the fall. 秋天进行了小麦种子的播种。|Near synonyms: sowing, planting|Antonym: none|noun
