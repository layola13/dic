de minimis:/ˌdeɪ ˈmɪnɪmɪs/| adj.|微不足道的，最低限度的|Of too little importance to be considered.  Referring to something so insignificant that it can be ignored or disregarded.  Often used in legal contexts to describe negligible amounts or matters.|The error was de minimis and did not affect the overall result.  这个错误微不足道，并不影响总体结果。|近义词: negligible, insignificant, trifling, immaterial|反义词: significant, substantial, material|词性:形容词

Etymology: Latin, literally "of minimum things," ablative plural of *minimus*, superlative of *parvus* "small."
