disinherited:/dɪsɪnˈhɛrɪtɪd/
adj.
被剥夺继承权的, 被排除在外的
Deprived of an inheritance, especially one that is legally or rightfully due.  Excluded or rejected.
He felt disinherited by the rapid changes in technology. 他感觉自己被技术的快速发展排除在外了。
The disinherited son vowed revenge. 被剥夺继承权的儿子发誓要报复。
Synonyms: disowned, excluded, rejected, cut off
Antonyms:  inherited, included, accepted
词性: 形容词

词源:
From dis- "not, opposite of" + inherit, from Old French enheriter "to cause to inherit," from Latin inhereditare "to appoint as heir," from in- "in" + heres (genitive heredis) "heir."  The notion is of being excluded from inheritance.
