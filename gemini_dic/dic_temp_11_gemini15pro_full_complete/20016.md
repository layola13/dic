biolytic:/ˌbaɪəˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
生物溶解的; 通过生物作用分解的
Relating to or causing biolysis; the destruction of organic material by microorganisms.
Biolytic enzymes are used in some detergents to break down stains.  生物溶解酶被用于一些洗涤剂中以分解污渍。
Biolytic processes are important for nutrient cycling in ecosystems. 生物溶解过程对于生态系统中的营养循环很重要。
近义词:biodegradable, biodegradative
反义词:non-biodegradable
词性:adjective
