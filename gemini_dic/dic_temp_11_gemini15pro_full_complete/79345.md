gleeman:/ˈɡliːmən/| n.|吟游诗人，游方歌手|A medieval minstrel or traveling musician who sang songs and recited poems.|The gleeman entertained the king's court with tales of bravery and love. 游方歌手用勇敢和爱情的故事娱乐了国王的宫廷。|近义词：minstrel, bard, singer, musician|反义词: audience, listener|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Old English *glīwmann, from glīw (music, mirth) + mann (man).  The word is related to "glee."
