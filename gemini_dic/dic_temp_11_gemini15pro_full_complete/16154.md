banjo:/ˈbændʒoʊ/ (US) /ˈbænˌdʒəʊ/ (UK)| n. |班卓琴；五弦琴|A stringed musical instrument with a long neck and a round body like a drum, played by plucking the strings.|He played a lively tune on his banjo. 他用班卓琴演奏了一首活泼的曲子。|Synonyms: five-string, stringed instrument |Antonyms: None|noun

banjo:/ˈbændʒoʊ/ (US) /ˈbænˌdʒəʊ/ (UK)| v. |弹奏班卓琴|To play the banjo.|He banjoed a cheerful melody. 他用班卓琴弹奏了一首欢快的旋律。|Synonyms: play (banjo), strum, pick |Antonyms: None|verb
