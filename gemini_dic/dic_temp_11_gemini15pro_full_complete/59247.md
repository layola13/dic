e-learning:/ˈiː lɜːrnɪŋ/ | n. | 电子学习 | Learning conducted via electronic media, typically on the Internet. |  Our company is investing heavily in e-learning resources for employee training.  我们公司正在大力投资员工培训的电子学习资源。


| 近义词: online learning, distance learning, web-based learning | 反义词: traditional learning, classroom learning | 词性: noun

e-learning:/ˈiː lɜːrnɪŋ/ | adj. | 电子学习的 | Relating to or involving learning conducted via electronic media. | The e-learning platform offers a variety of courses. 这个电子学习平台提供各种课程。


| 近义词: online, digital | 反义词: traditional, conventional | 词性: adjective
