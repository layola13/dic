ironclad:/ˈaɪərnˌklæd/ | adj. n. | 装甲的，铁甲的；坚固的，不可违逆的（协议等）| Covered or protected with iron plates, especially of a warship; unbreakable, rigidly enforced (especially of an agreement or contract). | The two parties signed an ironclad agreement.  双方签署了一份不可违逆的协议。| The ironclad ship was impervious to enemy fire.  这艘铁甲舰不受敌人炮火的伤害。 |近义词：inviolable, unbreakable, armored, impregnable |反义词：breakable, vulnerable, weak |词性：形容词，名词

ironclad:/ˈaɪərnˌklæd/ | n. | 铁甲舰 | A warship covered with iron plates. | The ironclad revolutionized naval warfare. 铁甲舰彻底改变了海战。 | The monitor was a new type of ironclad.  Monitor号是一种新型铁甲舰。|近义词：battleship, warship |反义词： (No direct opposite, perhaps "unarmed vessel")|词性：名词
