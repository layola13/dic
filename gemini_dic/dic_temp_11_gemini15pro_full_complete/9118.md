antimoralistic:/ˌæntiˌmɔːrəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
反道德的，非道德主义的
Opposing or rejecting traditional morality or moral principles.
His antimoralistic stance often put him at odds with the more conservative members of society. 他反道德的立场经常使他与社会上更保守的成员发生冲突。
synonyms:  unconventional, nonconformist, iconoclastic, rebellious, dissident,  libertine, amoral, immoral
antonyms: moralistic, conventional, orthodox, conformist
词性: 形容词
