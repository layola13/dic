hyades:/ˈhaɪəˌdiːz/ /ˈhaɪədiz/| n.|毕星团;雨星|A V-shaped cluster of stars in the constellation Taurus, considered to presage rain in ancient times.|The Hyades are visible to the naked eye.  毕星团肉眼可见。|The rising of the Hyades marked the start of the rainy season for the ancient Greeks. 对古希腊人来说，毕星团的升起标志着雨季的开始。|近义词:Taurus (as a constellation), star cluster|反义词: None (Specific astronomical object)|词性:noun

hyades:/ˈhaɪəˌdiːz/ /ˈhaɪədiz/| n.|Hyades (Greek mythology)|许阿得斯仙女; 雨仙女|In Greek mythology, the Hyades were nymphs associated with rain.|The Hyades were said to be the daughters of Atlas.  据说许阿得斯仙女是阿特拉斯的女儿们。|The Hyades wept for their brother Hyas, and their tears became rain. 许阿得斯仙女为她们的兄弟许阿斯哭泣，她们的眼泪变成了雨水。|近义词:nymphs|反义词: None (Specific mythological figures)|词性:noun
