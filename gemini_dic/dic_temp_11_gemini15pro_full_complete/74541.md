fratricide:/ˈfrætrɪsaɪd/
n.
兄弟相残；杀害兄弟者
The act of killing one's brother.
Cain's murder of Abel is a classic example of fratricide.
凯恩谋杀亚伯是兄弟相残的典型例子。

Romulus's killing of Remus is a legendary act of fratricide.
罗慕路斯杀死雷穆斯是一个传说中的兄弟相残行为。

近义词：sibling murder, brother killing
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, potentially "brotherly love")
