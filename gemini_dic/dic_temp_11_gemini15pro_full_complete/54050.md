disdainer:/dɪsˈdeɪnər/| n. |轻蔑者，鄙视者|A person who disdains or treats someone or something with contempt.|He is a disdainer of popular culture. 他是一个流行文化的鄙视者。|Synonyms: scorner, despiser, contemner|Antonyms: admirer, respecter|Noun

Etymology->
From Middle English desdeigner, from Old French desdaigner, from des- (expressing negation) + deigner (“to deign”).
