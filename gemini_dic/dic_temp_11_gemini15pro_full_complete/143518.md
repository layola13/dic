outgreen:/ˌaʊtˈɡriːn/| v. | 比...更环保；在环保方面胜过| To surpass someone in terms of environmental friendliness or sustainability. |  Tesla aims to outgreen all other car manufacturers with its electric vehicles. 特斯拉的目标是凭借其电动汽车在环保方面胜过所有其他汽车制造商。

My neighbor tries to outgreen me by composting, but I've installed solar panels! 我的邻居试图通过堆肥在环保方面胜过我，但我已经安装了太阳能电池板！| 近义词：surpass, outdo, eclipse (in environmental practices)| 反义词：pollute more than, be less environmentally friendly than |词性：动词
