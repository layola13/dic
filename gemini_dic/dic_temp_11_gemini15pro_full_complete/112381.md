losings:/ˈluːzɪŋz/| n. |损失；亏损|Money lost, especially in gambling or business.  The act or process of losing something, typically money.|His losings at the casino were substantial.|近义词：losses, deficits, damages|反义词:winnings, gains, profits|词性:名词
Etymology: From the verb "lose" (Old English *lēosan), from Proto-Germanic *leusaną.  The "-ings" suffix forms a gerund or present participle, but in this case, "losings" acts as a plural noun.
