contemplator:/ˈkɒntəmpleɪtə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntəmpleɪtər/ (US)| n.|沉思者；冥想者；凝视者|A person who contemplates; one given to contemplation.  A person who looks at something thoughtfully for a long time.|He sat by the river, a contemplator of nature's beauty. 他坐在河边，凝视着大自然的美景。|Synonyms:  meditator, thinker, ponderer, observer, muser|Antonyms:  doer, actor, activist|Noun

contemplate:/ˈkɒntəmpleɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntəmpleɪt/ (US)| v.|沉思；冥想；凝视；打算|To think profoundly and at length; meditate.  To look at attentively or thoughtfully.|She contemplated the meaning of life. 她沉思着人生的意义。  He is contemplating a career change. 他正在考虑换工作。|Synonyms: consider, ponder, meditate, muse, reflect|Antonyms: disregard, ignore, neglect|Verb
