acquirer:/əˈkwaɪərər/ | n. | 收购者，获得者 | A person or company that buys another company or a part of another company. |  The company was sold to a foreign acquirer.  这家公司被卖给了一家外国收购商。 | Synonyms: buyer, purchaser, investor | Antonyms: seller, vendor | noun

acquirer:/əˈkwaɪərər/ | n. | 获得者 | A person who obtains or gains something. | He is an acquirer of rare books. 他是一位珍本书的收藏家。 | Synonyms: obtainer, gainer, recipient | Antonyms: loser, forfeiter | noun
