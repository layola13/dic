counter-agent:/ˈkaʊntər ˌeɪdʒənt/ /ˈkaʊntər ˌeɪdʒnt/
noun
对策剂; 反作用剂; 反代理人; 对抗剂
a person or thing that opposes or counteracts another.
The new drug acts as a counter-agent to the poison.  这种新药可以作为这种毒药的解毒剂。
synonyms: antidote, remedy, neutralizer,  opponent, adversary, rival
antonyms:  agonist, catalyst,  ally, supporter
n.
