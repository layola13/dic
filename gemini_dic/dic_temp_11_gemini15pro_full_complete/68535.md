fancying:/ˈfænsiɪŋ/ | v. | 幻想；自负 |  Believing something, often mistakenly; having an overly high opinion of oneself. |  He was fancying himself as a bit of a ladies' man. 他自以为很受女人欢迎。 |  imagining, believing, conceited, vain |  modest, humble | verb
