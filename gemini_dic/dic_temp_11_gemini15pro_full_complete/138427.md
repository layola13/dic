nourishable:/ˈnʌrɪʃəbl/| adj.|营养的，滋养的|Containing substances necessary for growth and health; able to be nourished.|例句：A nourishing diet is essential for good health.  营养丰富的饮食对健康至关重要。

例句：The soil is rich and nourishable, perfect for growing vegetables. 这片土壤肥沃且营养丰富，非常适合种植蔬菜。|近义词：nutritious, wholesome, healthful|反义词：unhealthy, unwholesome, innutritious|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: nurrisshable, from nurrisshen (see nourish) + -able.
