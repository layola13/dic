cock-eyed:/ˌkɒkˈaɪd/ /ˌkɑːkˈaɪd/ | adj. |歪斜的；荒谬的，不合逻辑的||例句：He wore his hat at a cock-eyed angle. 他把帽子戴歪了。

例句：She had some cock-eyed notion that she could fly. 她有一种荒谬的想法，认为自己能飞。|近义词：askew, lopsided, awry, absurd, irrational|反义词:straight, symmetrical, rational, logical|词性:adjective

Etymology: From "cock" (meaning tilted or turned awry) + "eyed" (referring to the appearance of something tilted or askew).  The suffix "-ness" turns the adjective "cock-eyed" into the noun "cockeyedness," indicating the state or quality of being cock-eyed.
