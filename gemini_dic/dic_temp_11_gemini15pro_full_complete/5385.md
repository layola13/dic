ally's:/ˈælaɪz/
noun
盟友的；同盟者的
Possessive form of ally, indicating ownership or association.  Belonging to or associated with an ally.
例句：Our ally's support is crucial to our success. 我们的盟友的支持对我们的成功至关重要。

例句：The ally's troops arrived just in time.  盟军的部队及时到达。

近义词：friend's, partner's, supporter's
反义词：enemy's, opponent's, foe's
词性：possessive noun (所有格名词)
