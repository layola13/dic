ambassadorially:/ˌæmbæsəˈdɔːriəli/
| adv. |大使地；以大使的身份| In a manner befitting an ambassador; diplomatically. | He acted ambassadorially, smoothing over the conflict with grace and tact. 他以大使的身份行事，以优雅和机智化解了冲突。|近义词：diplomatically, officially, formally |反义词: informally, unofficially |词性:副词
词源: From ambassador + -ial + -ly. Ultimately from the Late Latin ambactiator.
