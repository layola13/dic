cannibalize:/ˈkænɪbəlaɪz/| v. |同类相食，拆用配件，损害自身利益|To take parts from one machine or system in order to use them in another, or to reduce sales of one product by introducing a similar one.|The company cannibalized its older models to produce the new one. 公司拆用了旧型号的配件来生产新的型号。|synonyms: appropriate, usurp, strip, dismantle|antonyms: assemble, construct, build|verb

cannibalizes:/ˈkænɪbəlaɪzɪz/| v. |同类相食，拆用配件，损害自身利益|Third-person singular present tense form of cannibalize.  It describes the action of taking parts from something to use elsewhere, or reducing sales of one product by introducing a similar, competing product.|The new phone model cannibalizes sales of the older one.  这款新手机蚕食了旧款手机的销量。|synonyms: appropriates, usurps, strips, dismantles|antonyms: assembles, constructs, builds|verb
