burro:/ˈbɜːroʊ/ (US), /ˈbʌrəʊ/ (UK)| n.|驴子；毛驴|A small donkey, especially one used as a pack animal.|例句：The burro carried supplies up the steep mountain trail. 这头驴子驮着补给品沿着陡峭的山路向上走。

例句：The prospector and his burro were a common sight in the old west.  在旧西部，探矿者和他的驴子是常见的景象。|近义词：donkey, ass|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *burro*, meaning "donkey," ultimately from Late Latin *burricus*, meaning "small horse" or "pony."
