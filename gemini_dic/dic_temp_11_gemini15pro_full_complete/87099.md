herald: /ˈherəld/ (US) /ˈhɛrəld/ (UK)| n. vt.| 预兆，先兆；传令官；信使| a person or thing viewed as a sign that something is about to happen. To be a sign that (something) is about to happen.|The robin is a herald of spring. 知更鸟是春天的预兆。| synonyms: harbinger, precursor, forerunner, omen | antonyms: aftermath, conclusion, end | noun

herald: /ˈherəld/ (US) /ˈhɛrəld/ (UK)| vt.| 预示，预告；宣布| To make known publicly or officially. To be a sign that (something) is about to happen. | The warm weather heralds the coming of spring.  温暖的天气预示着春天的到来。| synonyms: announce, proclaim, declare, portend | antonyms: conceal, hide, suppress | verb
