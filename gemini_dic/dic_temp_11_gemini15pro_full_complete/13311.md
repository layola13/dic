athleticism: /æθˈlɛtɪsɪzəm/
n.|运动能力，体育精神，强健的体魄|The quality of being athletic, especially in terms of physical strength, agility, and stamina.  |例句：She displayed impressive athleticism on the basketball court. 她在篮球场上展现了惊人的运动能力。|近义词：fitness, athletic ability, prowess, agility, strength, stamina |反义词:inactivity, weakness, clumsiness| noun
