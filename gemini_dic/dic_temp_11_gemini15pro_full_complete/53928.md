discoursive: /dɪˈskɜːsɪv/ /dɪˈskɔːrsɪv/
adjective
散漫的，推理的，论证的
Proceeding by argument or reasoning rather than intuition.
例句：The book takes a discoursive approach to the subject, exploring various perspectives. 这本书对这个主题采取了一种散漫的方式，探讨了各种观点。

例句：Her discoursive style of writing can make it difficult to follow her train of thought. 她散漫的写作风格让人很难跟上她的思路。
近义词：argumentative, rambling, digressive, analytical, reasoned
反义词：concise, succinct, pithy, intuitive
词性: adjective
