mraa:/ˈmrɑː/ | n. | 低级别硬件访问库 |  A low-level library that provides an abstraction layer for interacting with hardware components like GPIOs, I2C, PWM, SPI, UART, and analog inputs. It is primarily used for embedded systems and IoT development, often in conjunction with single-board computers like the Intel Edison and other similar platforms. |  Example:  The mraa library simplifies access to the GPIO pins on the Intel Edison.  mraa库简化了对英特尔爱迪生开发板上GPIO引脚的访问。| Synonym:  WiringPi, libgpiod | Antonym: N/A | noun
