jargle:/ˈdʒɑːrɡəl/
v.
发出刺耳的声音；发出格格声；争吵；胡言乱语
To make a harsh, grating sound; to utter a discordant sound; to argue noisily; to talk nonsense.
The rusty gate jargleed as it opened. 生锈的大门打开时发出刺耳的声音。
He jargleed on about the unfairness of it all. 他喋喋不休地抱怨这一切的不公平。
近义词：grate, screech, rasp, squawk, gibber
反义词：melodize, harmonize
词性：动词
