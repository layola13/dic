outmarched:/ˌaʊtˈmɑːrtʃt/
verb
超过，胜过（行军速度或距离）
to march further, faster, or more effectively than someone else
The smaller army outmarched its larger opponent and surprised them at dawn.  这支规模较小的军队行军速度超过了规模较大的对手，并在黎明时分突袭了他们。
The refugees outmarched their pursuers and reached the border. 难民们比追捕他们的人走得更快，到达了边境。
surpass, outstrip, outrun
lag behind, fall behind
