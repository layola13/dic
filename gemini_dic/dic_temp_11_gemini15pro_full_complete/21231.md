blogosphere:/ˈblɒɡəsfɪə(r)/ /ˈblɑːɡəsfɪr/| n. |博客圈；博客世界|The blogosphere is buzzing with news about the upcoming election. 博客圈对即将到来的选举议论纷纷。|The company is actively engaging with the blogosphere to promote its new products. 该公司积极与博客圈互动以推广其新产品。|近义词：blogging community, online community|反义词: None|词性: Noun

Etymology:  "blog" + "-sphere" (a suffix denoting a domain or area of activity). "Blog" is a shortened form of "weblog," a term coined in the late 1990s.  "-sphere" comes from Greek "sphaira," meaning "ball" or "globe."
