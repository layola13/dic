brickyard:/ˈbrɪk.jɑːrd/ | n. |砖厂；制砖厂|A place where bricks are made.|The old brickyard on the edge of town is now a shopping mall.|近义词：brick factory, brickworks|反义词: None|词性: Noun

Etymology:
From *brick* + *yard*.  *Brick* comes from Middle Dutch *bricke* "brick," related to Old French *brique*. *Yard* comes from Old English *geard* "enclosure," related to Old High German *gart* "garden," from Proto-Germanic *gardaz*.
