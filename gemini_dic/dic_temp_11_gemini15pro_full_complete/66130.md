excepted:/ɪkˈsɛptɪd/| adj.|被排除的，除外的|Excluded, not included.  Specifically mentioned as not included in a category or group.|Everyone is invited, excepted John.  除了约翰，每个人都被邀请了。|近义词：excluded, barring, save, apart from, aside from|反义词：included|词性:形容词

excepted:/ɪkˈsɛptɪd/| v.|除外；把…除外（except的过去分词）|Past participle of "except." To exclude; to leave out.|He excepted his brother from the list of invitees. 他把他的兄弟从被邀请者名单中除外了。|近义词：excluded, omitted|反义词：included|词性:动词 (过去分词)
