anticarcinogens:/ˌæntiˌkɑːrsɪˈnədʒənz/
n.
抗癌物
Substances that inhibit or prevent the development of cancer.
Cruciferous vegetables are believed to contain potent anticarcinogens.
十字花科蔬菜被认为含有强效的抗癌物质。

Studies suggest that certain antioxidants act as anticarcinogens.
研究表明某些抗氧化剂具有抗癌作用。

近义词:cancer inhibitors, cancer preventing agents
反义词:carcinogens
