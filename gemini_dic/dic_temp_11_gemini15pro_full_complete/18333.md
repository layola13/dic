bellicose: /ˈbelɪˌkoʊs/ (US) /ˈbelɪkəs/ (UK)| adj. | 好战的，好斗的 | Demonstrating aggression and willingness to fight. |  The bellicose general was eager for war.  好战的将军渴望战争。

The politician's bellicose rhetoric inflamed tensions between the two countries.  这位政客的好战言辞加剧了两国之间的紧张关系。

| 近义词: aggressive, pugnacious, belligerent, hostile, warlike | 反义词: peaceful, pacifistic, conciliatory, amicable | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bellicosus*, from bellum ("war") + -osus (suffix forming adjectives indicating fullness or abundance).
