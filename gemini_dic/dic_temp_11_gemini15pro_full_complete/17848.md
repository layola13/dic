beechy:/ˈbiːtʃi/| adj.|山毛榉的，多山毛榉树的|Characterized by or abounding in beech trees.|The path led through a beechy wood. 这条小路穿过一片山毛榉树林。|近义词：beech-covered, beech-shaded|反义词:treeless, barren|词性:形容词
Etymology: From beech + -y.  Beech comes from Old English *bēce (attested in compounds), from Proto-Germanic *bōkjōn, from Proto-Indo-European *bhāgós.
