infixations:/ɪnˌfɪkˈseɪʃənz/| n. |插入词缀，中缀|The process of inserting an affix within a word stem.  The addition of an affix inside a word stem. |Example:  "Absogoddamlutely" uses the infix "goddamn".  "Fan-bloody-tastic" uses the infix "bloody".|近义词:interfixes, tmesis (although tmesis involves inserting a whole word, not just an affix)|反义词:prefixes, suffixes|词性:noun
