mavournin:/məˈvɔːrnɪn/| n.|爱人，亲爱的|A term of endearment, often used in Irish English, meaning "my darling" or "my love".  Derived from the Irish Gaelic phrase "mo mhúirnín," meaning "my little darling."|例句：

"Oh, mavournin," she whispered, "I've missed you so."
“哦，亲爱的，”她低声说，“我很想你。”

"Come here, mavournin, and let me give you a hug."
“过来，亲爱的，让我抱抱你。”

|近义词：darling, sweetheart, love, honey|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:名词
