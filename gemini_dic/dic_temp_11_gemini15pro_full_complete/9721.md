antiunion:/ˌæntiˈjuːnjən/
adjective
反工会的；反对工会的
Opposed to labor unions or their activities.
The company's antiunion stance drew criticism from employees and labor advocates. 该公司反工会的立场引来了员工和劳工倡导者的批评。
The antiunion policies were designed to discourage workers from organizing. 这些反工会的政策旨在阻止工人组织起来。

synonyms: anti-labor, anti-trade union
antonyms: pro-union, pro-labor
adjective
