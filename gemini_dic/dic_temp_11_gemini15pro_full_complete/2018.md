actuary:/ˈæktʃʊˌɛri/ | n. | 精算师 | A person who compiles and analyzes statistics and uses them to calculate insurance risks and premiums. |  An actuary calculates how much an insurance policy should cost.  精算师计算保险单的费用。| synonyms: statistician, analyst | antonyms:  N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *actuarius (“recorder, clerk”), from Latin actus (“a doing, a driving, an impulse”).
