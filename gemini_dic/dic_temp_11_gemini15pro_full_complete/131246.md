noncarbonated:/ˌnɒnkɑːrbəˈneɪtɪd/ (US) /ˌnɒnkɑːbəˈneɪtɪd/ (UK)| adj.|不含碳酸的，无气的|Not containing carbon dioxide; not carbonated.  It refers to beverages that do not have the fizz or bubbles created by dissolved carbon dioxide.| I prefer noncarbonated drinks like juice and water. 我更喜欢不含碳酸的饮料，如果汁和水。|Near Synonyms: still, flat, uncarbonated|Antonyms: carbonated, fizzy, sparkling|Adjective
