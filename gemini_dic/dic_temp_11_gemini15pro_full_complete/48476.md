deep-sea:/ˌdiːpˈsiː/| adj.|深海的，深海产的|Of, relating to, or inhabiting the deeper parts of the sea.|例句：Deep-sea exploration is fascinating. 深海探险引人入胜。|例句：Deep-sea creatures are often bizarre. 深海生物通常很奇特。|近义词：abyssal, pelagic|反义词：shallow, coastal|词性:形容词
Etymology: Compound of "deep" (from Old English *dēop) and "sea" (from Old English *sæ).
