ctc:/siː tiː siː/ |abbr.|Connectionist Temporal Classification (连接时序分类)|A neural network output method commonly used for speech recognition and other sequence labeling tasks. It allows the network to output sequences of labels without needing aligned input-output pairs during training.|Example: The CTC algorithm significantly improved the accuracy of our speech recognition system.| 近义词:  HMM (Hidden Markov Model - sometimes used as an alternative),  sequence transduction| 反义词: N/A|  abbr.
