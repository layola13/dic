carbonizes:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪzɪz/
verb
使碳化；烧焦
to change into carbon by burning or intense heating
The intense heat carbonizes the wood, turning it into charcoal.  高温使木头碳化，变成了木炭。
char, scorch, burn
oxidize, decay
