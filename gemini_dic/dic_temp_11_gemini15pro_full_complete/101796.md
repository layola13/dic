irrigates:/ˈɪrɪɡeɪts/
verb
灌溉
supplies water to (land or crops) to help growth, typically by means of channels.
The farmer irrigates his fields using a complex system of canals. 农民用复杂的灌溉渠系统灌溉田地。
Water from the dam irrigates thousands of acres of farmland. 大坝的水灌溉了数千英亩的农田。
water, moisten
drain, dry
