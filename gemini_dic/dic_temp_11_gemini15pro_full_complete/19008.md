bestrode:/bɪˈstrəʊd/| v.|跨坐，跨过，支配|To sit or stand with one leg on each side of something; to dominate or control.|例句：He bestrode the horse with confidence. 他自信地跨坐在马上。|例句：The colossal statue bestrode the valley. 巨大的雕像矗立在山谷之上，仿佛跨坐在其间。|近义词：straddled, dominated, controlled|反义词: submitted, yielded|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bestridan, from be- (prefix meaning "around," "on all sides") + stridan (to stride).
