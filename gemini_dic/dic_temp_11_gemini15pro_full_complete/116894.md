mayes:/meɪz/ | n. | 迷宫，曲径，错综复杂的事物 | A complex network of paths or passages; a labyrinth. | She got lost in the mayes of the old city. 她迷失在古城的迷宫里。| The legal system can be a mayes for those unfamiliar with it.  对于不熟悉的人来说，法律体系可能像个迷宫。 | 近义词: labyrinth, maze, network | 反义词: simplicity, clarity | 词性: 名词

词源：来自古法语 *mais,  "混乱，纠缠"， 可能与古法语 meslier "混合" 有关。
