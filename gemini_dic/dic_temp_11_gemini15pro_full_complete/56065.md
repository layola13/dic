docility:/doʊˈsɪləti/ /dəʊˈsɪləti/ | n. | 温顺；顺从；驯服 | The docility of the dog made it easy to train.  这条狗的温顺使它很容易训练。| 近义词：obedience, compliance, submissiveness | 反义词：rebelliousness, defiance, intransigence | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *docilite*, from Old French *docilite*, from Latin *dōcilitās*, from *dōcilis* (“teachable, docile”), from *docēre* (“to teach”).
