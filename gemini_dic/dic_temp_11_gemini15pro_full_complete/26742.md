ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cadmia*, from Greek *kadmeia*, meaning "calamine", a zinc ore often containing cadmium.  The name is ultimately derived from Cadmus, a figure in Greek mythology credited with introducing the alphabet to Greece, as calamine was associated with the region of Thebes, which Cadmus founded.
