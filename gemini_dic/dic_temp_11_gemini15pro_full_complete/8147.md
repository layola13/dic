anti-confucian:/ˌænti-kənˈfjuːʃən/
adj.
反儒家的，反对儒家思想的
Opposed to or rejecting Confucianism, its principles, or its influence.
The anti-Confucian movement gained traction during the May Fourth Movement. 五四运动期间，反儒家运动得到了发展。
The government implemented several anti-Confucian policies. 政府实施了几项反儒家政策。
近义词:non-Confucian, anti-traditional
反义词:Confucian, pro-Confucian
词性:adjective
