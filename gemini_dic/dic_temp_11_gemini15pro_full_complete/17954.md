befittingly:/bɪˈfɪtɪŋli/
adverb
适当地；恰当地
in a way that is suitable or right for a particular occasion or situation
She was befittingly dressed for the funeral, in a simple black dress. 她穿着朴素的黑色连衣裙，穿着得体地参加了葬礼。
近义词: appropriately, suitably, properly, fittingly
反义词: inappropriately, unsuitably, improperly
