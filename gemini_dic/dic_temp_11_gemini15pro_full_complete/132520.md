nondeterministic:/ˌnɑːndɪˌtɜːrmɪˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非确定性的 | Not determined in advance; having several possible outcomes or solutions.  |  A nondeterministic algorithm may produce different results on different runs with the same input.  非确定性算法在使用相同输入的不同运行中可能会产生不同的结果。

A nondeterministic Turing machine can explore multiple computational paths simultaneously. 非确定性图灵机可以同时探索多个计算路径。 | synonyms:  probabilistic, stochastic, random, unpredictable | antonyms: deterministic, predictable, fixed, definite | word type: adjective
