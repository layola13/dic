overcommended: /ˌoʊvər.kəˈmɛndɪd/
| adj. | 过度推荐的；被吹捧过头的 | Excessively praised or recommended, often beyond what is deserved. | The movie was overcommended by the critics; it wasn't that good. 这部电影被评论家过度吹捧了，其实并没有那么好。 | The restaurant was overcommended online; the food was mediocre. 这家餐厅在网上被过度推荐；食物很一般。 | 近义词: overpraised, overhyped, overrated | 反义词: underrated, underappreciated | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "commend" (verb meaning "to praise").  "Commend" comes from the Latin "commendare" (to entrust, recommend).
