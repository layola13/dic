ichthin:/ˈɪkθɪn/ | n. | 鱼毒，鱼素 | A poisonous nitrogenous base, C10H13N2O2, found in the flesh and organs of many carangoid fishes, especially the black skipjack Euthynnus yaito. |  Exposure to ichthin can cause severe gastrointestinal distress.  接触鱼毒会导致严重的肠胃不适。| Synonym: ciguatoxin | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific toxin) | noun
