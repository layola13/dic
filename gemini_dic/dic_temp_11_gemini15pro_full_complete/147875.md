overreact:/ˌoʊvərriˈækt/ | v. |反应过度，过激反应 |To react or respond more strongly than is necessary or appropriate. |She overreacted to his innocent comment and started shouting. 她对他的无心之言反应过度，开始大喊大叫。|  He tends to overreact to criticism. 他容易对批评反应过激。 |近义词：overdo, exaggerate |反义词: underreact |词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + react.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much."  "React" comes from the Latin "reagere," meaning "to act back" or "to respond."
