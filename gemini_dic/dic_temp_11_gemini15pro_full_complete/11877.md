arroba:/@ˈroʊbə/ (US), /əˈrɒbə/ (UK)| n.|艾特符号，商业单位(西班牙语)，阿罗巴(重量单位)|A symbol used in email addresses to separate the user name from the domain name. Also, a Spanish and Portuguese unit of weight, approximately 11.5 kg (25.4 lb), and a unit of liquid volume. |例句：Please send the email to myname@example.com. 请将邮件发送到myname@example.com。

例句：In Spain, the arroba was a unit of weight. 在西班牙，阿罗巴是一个重量单位。|近义词：(for @ symbol) at sign|反义词: (no direct antonym)|词性:noun

例句：Arroba as a liquid measure varied regionally. 阿罗巴作为液量单位在不同地区有所不同。|近义词：(no direct equivalent in other systems)|反义词: (no direct antonym)|词性:noun
