abstemiousnesses:/ˌæbstɪˈmiːəsnəsɪz/
n.
节制, 禁欲
The practice of abstaining from indulging in something, typically alcohol.
Her abstemiousnesses extended to all forms of indulgence, not just food and drink. 她不仅在饮食方面，在所有形式的放纵方面都非常节制。
Synonyms:  abstinence, sobriety, temperance, moderation
Antonyms:  indulgence, intemperance, excess, gluttony
noun
