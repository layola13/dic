horses:/ˈhɔːrsɪz/ (US) /ˈhɔːsɪz/ (UK)| n.|马|Plural form of "horse," referring to more than one horse.  A horse is a large, hoofed mammal, domesticated for riding, racing, and working. |The horses galloped across the field. 马儿在田野上奔驰。|例句：Wild horses roamed the plains. 野马在平原上漫游。|近义词：steeds, mounts, equines|反义词: N/A|名词
