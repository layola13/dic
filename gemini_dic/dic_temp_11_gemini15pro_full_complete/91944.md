hypermilitantly:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɪlɪtəntli/ | adv. | 极端好战地 |  In an extremely militant manner; with excessive or extreme aggression and combativeness, especially in support of a cause. |  The group acted hypermilitantly, resorting to violence at the slightest provocation.  该组织行动极端好战，稍有挑衅就诉诸暴力。 | 近义词: aggressively, belligerently, violently | 反义词: peacefully, passively, diplomatically | 词性: 副词

hypermilitantly:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɪlɪtəntli/ | adv. | 极端好战地 |  Characterized by extreme militancy, going beyond what is considered normal or reasonable in terms of aggressive behavior and advocacy.  |  He hypermilitantly defended his beliefs, alienating even his supporters. 他极端好战地捍卫自己的信仰，甚至疏远了他的支持者。 | 近义词: fanatically, zealously, extremely | 反义词: moderately, reasonably, calmly | 词性: 副词
