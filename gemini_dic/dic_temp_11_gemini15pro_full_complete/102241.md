/ˈaɪtəmz/ | n. | 物品，项目 |  A collection of objects or articles considered as a group.  Derived from the Old French word "item," meaning "likewise, also." It ultimately traces back to the Latin "ita," meaning "so, thus." | I need to buy a few items at the grocery store. 我需要在杂货店买几样东西。| synonyms: goods, articles, belongings, possessions | antonyms: nothing, lack | noun
