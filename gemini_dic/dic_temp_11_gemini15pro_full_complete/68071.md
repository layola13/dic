faddiness:/ˈfædɪnɪs/
n.|时尚；流行；一时狂热|The state or quality of being faddish; a tendency to follow fads.|The faddiness of teenage fashion is well documented. 青少年时尚的流行趋势是有据可查的。|trendiness, volatility, ephemerality|permanence, stability|noun


faddish:/ˈfædɪʃ/
adj.|时尚的；流行的；一时狂热的|Following fads; fashionable for a short time only.|Platform shoes were a faddish trend in the 70s. 厚底鞋是70年代的一种流行趋势。|trendy, fashionable, stylish|classic, timeless, enduring|adjective
