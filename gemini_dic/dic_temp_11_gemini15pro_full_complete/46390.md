daedal:/ˈdiːdəl/ /ˈdɛdəl/| adj.|精巧的，巧夺天工的；复杂的，曲折的|skillfully and elaborately made; intricate or maze-like|The architect designed a daedal structure, a masterpiece of interwoven arches and columns. 建筑师设计了一座精巧的建筑，它是拱门和柱子交织在一起的杰作。|Synonyms: intricate, complex, elaborate, ingenious, artistic|Antonyms: simple, plain, unadorned, crude, artless|adjective

daedal:/ˈdiːdəl/ /ˈdɛdəl/| n.|复杂的事物；迷宫|something intricate or maze-like|The ancient city was a daedal of narrow streets and hidden courtyards. 这座古城是由狭窄的街道和隐藏的庭院组成的迷宫。|Synonyms: labyrinth, maze, network, web|Antonyms: simplicity, clarity|noun
