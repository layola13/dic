mesocuneiform:/ˌmɛsoʊˈkjuːnɪfɔːrm/
adjective
中楔形文字的
Relating to or denoting a simplified form of cuneiform writing used in Mesopotamia during the second millennium BC, intermediate between the earlier and later forms.
The mesocuneiform texts provide valuable insights into the economic and social life of the period.  中楔形文字文献为了解那个时期的经济和社会生活提供了宝贵的见解。
The library contained numerous clay tablets inscribed with mesocuneiform script.  图书馆里藏有许多刻有中楔形文字的泥板。
Near synonyms: Middle Babylonian cuneiform
No direct antonym.  (Antonyms would relate to other periods or types of cuneiform, not a direct opposite meaning.)
词性: adjective
