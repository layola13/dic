harvester:/ˈhɑːrvɪstər/ (US), /ˈhɑːvɪstə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 收割机；收割者|A machine or person that harvests crops. |The farmer bought a new combine harvester. 农民买了一台新的联合收割机。|Synonyms: reaper, collector, gatherer|Antonyms: sower, planter|noun

harvester:/ˈhɑːrvɪstər/ (US), /ˈhɑːvɪstə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 收割蚁|An ant that gathers seeds and other food. |Harvester ants are known for their large colonies. 收割蚁以其庞大的群体而闻名。|Synonyms: foraging ant|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider parasitic ant)|noun
