nephrostome: /ˈnɛfrəˌstoʊm/ | n. | 肾口；肾孔 | The funnel-shaped opening of a nephridium.  It is the opening of the kidney tubule through which waste is filtered from the body cavity. |  The nephrostome collects waste products from the coelom. 肾口从体腔收集废物。|  The cilia on the nephrostome create a current that draws fluid into the nephridium. 肾口上的纤毛产生一股将液体吸入肾管的水流。| synonyms: nephridial opening, renal opening | antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *nephros* (kidney) + *stoma* (mouth)
