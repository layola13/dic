overpraises: /ˌoʊvərˈpreɪzɪz/
verb
过分赞扬, 过誉
praises someone or something too much
He overpraised her performance in the play. 他过分赞扬了她在剧中的表演。

synonyms: exaggerate, overstate, laud excessively, overestimate
antonyms: criticize, underestimate, downplay
词性: 动词
