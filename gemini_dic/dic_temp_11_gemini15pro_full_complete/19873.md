bingo:/ˈbɪŋɡoʊ/ (US) /ˈbɪŋɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. int. exclam.|宾果游戏;中了！;太好了！|A game of chance in which each player has a card printed with different numbers on which they cover the numbers called out by the caller.  When a row of numbers is covered, the player calls out "Bingo!". Also used as an exclamation of sudden discovery or success. |例句：I shouted "Bingo!" when I finally found my lost keys. 当我终于找到丢失的钥匙时，我喊道“宾果！”

例句：My grandma loves playing bingo at the community center. 我奶奶喜欢在社区中心玩宾果游戏。

|近义词：jackpot, success, eureka|反义词:failure, loss|noun, interjection, exclamation
