gobbed:/ɡɒbd/ (British English), /ɡɑːbd/ (American English)| v. |吐口水; 涂抹; 覆盖|Covered or smeared with something, often saliva or a sticky substance.  Derived from "gob," meaning a lump or mass, especially of something viscous.  Often used in a derogatory or informal context. |He gobbed on the floor. 他往地板上吐口水。| 近义词: spat, spit, daubed, smeared | 反义词: cleaned, wiped |词性: 动词
