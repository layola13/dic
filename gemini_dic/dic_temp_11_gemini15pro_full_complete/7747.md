anorexic: /ˌænəˈreksɪk/ | adj. n. | 厌食的；患厌食症的；厌食症患者 |  Relating to, characterized by, or suffering from anorexia nervosa, an eating disorder characterized by an abnormally low body weight, an intense fear of gaining weight, and a distorted perception of body weight and shape. | She looked pale and anorexic. 她看起来面色苍白，像是得了厌食症。 | The anorexic patient refused to eat.  这位厌食症患者拒绝进食。 | 近义词: emaciated, gaunt, thin, underweight | 反义词: obese, overweight | 词性: 形容词，名词

词源: From Greek *anorektos*, from *an-* (not) + *orektos* (having appetite), from *orexis* (appetite).
