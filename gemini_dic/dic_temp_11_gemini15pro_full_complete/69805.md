ferocious:/fəˈroʊʃəs/| adj.|凶猛的，残暴的；激烈的，猛烈的|Exhibiting or given to extreme fierceness and unrestrained violence. Characterized by intense energy and unrestrained force.|The ferocious lion stalked its prey. 凶猛的狮子悄悄地跟踪猎物。

The competition in the market is ferocious. 市场竞争非常激烈。|近义词：fierce, savage, brutal, violent, intense|反义词：gentle, mild, tame, calm|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *ferōcius, comparative of ferōx (“fierce, wild, savage”), from ferus (“wild”) + -ōx (suffix forming adjectives indicating a fullness of something).
