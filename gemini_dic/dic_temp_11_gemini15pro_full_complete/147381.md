overnervously:/ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrvəsli/ | adv. | 过分紧张地，过度神经质地 | In an excessively nervous manner; too nervously. | She overnervously clutched her purse as she walked through the dark alley. 她走过黑暗的小巷时，紧张地抓着钱包。|  He overnervously tapped his foot during the job interview.  面试时，他紧张地不停地敲着脚。| 近义词: anxiously, nervously, apprehensively | 反义词: calmly, confidently, composedly | 词性: 副词

詞源->
From "over-" (excessively) + "nervous" (easily agitated or alarmed) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Nervous" ultimately derives from Latin "nervus" (sinew, nerve).
