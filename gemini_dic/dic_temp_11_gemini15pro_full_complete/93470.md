illusoriness:/ɪˌluːsərɪnɪs/
n.
虚幻性；错觉
the state or quality of being illusory; unreal or deceptive
The illusoriness of her dreams made her feel lost and confused. 她梦想的虚幻性让她感到迷茫和困惑。
近义词：unreality, delusion, deception, fallacy
反义词：reality, actuality, truth
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From illusory + -ness.  Illusory comes from Late Latin illusus, past participle of illudere "to mock," from in- "at" + ludere "to play."
