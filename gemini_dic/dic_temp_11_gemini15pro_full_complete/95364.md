incessant:/ɪnˈsesənt/| adj.|不停的，不断的|Continuing without pause or interruption.|The incessant noise of the traffic kept me awake all night. 交通不停的噪音让我整夜无法入睡。|continuous, constant, perpetual, unremitting|intermittent, occasional, sporadic|adjective

incessable:/ɪnˈsesəbl/| adj.|不停的，不断的|Not ceasing; continuing without interruption.|The incessant rain caused flooding in the area.  不停的雨造成了该地区的洪水泛滥。|continuous, constant, perpetual, unremitting|intermittent, occasional, sporadic|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin incessabilis, from Latin incessans, present participle of incessare (“to attack, assail”), from in- (“in, on, upon”) + cessare (“to cease, stop”).
