Maxentius: /mækˈsɛnʃəs/ | n. | 马克森提乌斯 |  Roman emperor from AD 306 to 312. He usurped power after the death of his father, Maximian, and was defeated and killed by Constantine I at the Battle of the Milvian Bridge. |  Maxentius ruled Rome with an iron fist. 马克森提乌斯用铁腕统治罗马。| Synonyms: ruler, emperor, sovereign | Antonyms: subject, citizen | Noun
