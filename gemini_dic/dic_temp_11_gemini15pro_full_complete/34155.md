clamation:/kləˈmeɪʃən/
n.
惊叫，呼喊，感叹
a sudden loud cry or utterance, especially in fear, surprise, or joy.
She uttered a exclamation of surprise. 她惊呼了一声。
cry, shout, yell
whisper, murmur
noun
