forgeries:/ˈfɔːrdʒəriz/
n.
伪造品；赝品
A copy of a document, painting, etc. that is intended to deceive people into believing that it is the original.
The painting was later discovered to be a forgery.  这幅画后来被发现是赝品。
synonyms: counterfeits, fakes, imitations, reproductions
antonyms: originals, authentics
noun

forgery:/ˈfɔːrdʒəri/
n.
伪造（行为）
The crime of making a copy of something, such as a document, painting, etc., in order to deceive people into believing that it is the original.
He was found guilty of forgery. 他被判伪造罪名成立。
synonyms: counterfeiting, faking, imitation, reproduction
antonyms: authentication
noun
