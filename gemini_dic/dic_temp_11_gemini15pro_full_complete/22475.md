bookmobiles:/ˈbʊkməʊbiːlz/ (US), /ˈbʊkməʊbaɪlz/ (UK)| n. |流动图书馆；图书车|A vehicle designed as a traveling library, carrying a selection of books to areas where library services are limited.|The bookmobile visits our rural community every Tuesday.  流动图书馆每周二到我们乡下来。|The bookmobile parked near the school, allowing children easy access to books. 图书车停在学校附近，方便孩子们借阅书籍。|Synonyms: mobile library, traveling library|Antonyms: brick and mortar library, stationary library|Noun
