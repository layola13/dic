nonreproductiveness:/ˌnɑːnˌriːprəˈdʌktɪvnəs/ | n. | 不可重复性；不可复制性 | The inability of a scientific experiment or study to be duplicated to achieve the same results.  | The nonreproductiveness of the initial findings led to further investigation. 初始发现的不可重复性导致了进一步的调查。 |  Synonyms: irreproducibility, nonrepeatability | Antonyms: reproducibility, repeatability | noun
