hotbedding:/ˈhɒtˌbɛdɪŋ/ (US) /ˈhɒtˌbedɪŋ/ (UK)
|n.|轮用床位；共用办公桌；温床；滋生地|The practice of having workers share desks or workstations, especially when they work different shifts.  It can also refer to a situation that fosters the growth or development of something, often something negative.|The company uses hotbedding to maximize office space.  这家公司采用轮用办公桌的方式最大限度地利用办公空间。|synonyms: desk-sharing, hoteling,  incubator (figurative)|antonyms: dedicated desk, private office|noun
