词源: electrifier comes from the verb "electrify," which in turn comes from "electric" + "-fy."  "Electric" ultimately derives from the Greek word "elektron" meaning "amber," as static electricity was first observed by rubbing amber. "-fy" is a suffix meaning "to make or cause to be."
