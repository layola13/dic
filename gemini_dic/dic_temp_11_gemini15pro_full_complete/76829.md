ganders:/ˈɡændərz/| n.|雄鹅（复数）|Male geese.  A gander is an adult male goose. Ganders are typically larger than female geese and have a more prominent knob at the base of their bill.|The ganders were protective of their goslings.|近义词：male geese|反义词:female geese (geese)|noun
