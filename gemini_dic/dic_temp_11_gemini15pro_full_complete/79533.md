globulite:/ˈɡlɒbjʊlaɪt/| n. |球粒, 小球体|A small spherical body; a globule.  Specifically, a rounded crystalline body occurring in vitreous volcanic rocks.|Volcanic rock sometimes contains globulites. 火山岩有时含有球粒。|近义词：globule, spherule|反义词: - |noun
