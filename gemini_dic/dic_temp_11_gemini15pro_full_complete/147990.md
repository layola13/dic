override: /ˌoʊvərˈraɪd/ (US), /ˌəʊvəˈraɪd/ (UK)| v. n.| 覆盖，推翻，否决，优先于 |To use one's authority to reject or cancel (a decision, view, etc.).  As a noun, it refers to the act of overriding.|
The president used his veto to override the decision of Congress. 总统用他的否决权推翻了国会的决定。

The manager's decision overrides the recommendations of the committee. 经理的决定否决了委员会的建议。

近义词：overrule, cancel, nullify, supersede, supplant|
反义词： uphold, sustain, confirm, ratify|
词性：verb, noun


override: /ˈoʊvərraɪd/ (US), /ˈəʊvəraɪd/ (UK)| n.| 覆盖，推翻，否决 |The act of overriding; a rejection or cancellation by someone in authority.|
The president's override of the bill was unexpected. 总统对该法案的否决出乎意料。

The system allows for manual override of the automatic settings. 该系统允许手动覆盖自动设置。

近义词：cancellation, rejection, veto|
反义词： approval, confirmation|
词性：noun
