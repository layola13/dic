misthrowing:/ˌmɪsˈθroʊɪŋ/
n.|投掷失误，投错|The misthrowing of the ball cost the team the game. 投球失误导致球队输掉了比赛。|English explanation: An act of throwing something incorrectly or to the wrong place.|例句：His misthrowing led to an interception. 他的投掷失误导致球被拦截。|近义词：miscast, mishandle|反义词：accurate throw, good throw
詞性:名詞
