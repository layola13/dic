heuristic:/hjʊˈrɪstɪk/ | adj. n. | 启发式的；启发法；| A heuristic technique, or a heuristic, is any approach to problem solving or self-discovery that employs a practical method that is not guaranteed to be optimal, perfect, or logical, yet is sufficient for reaching an immediate, short-term goal. |  Example 1: Using a rule of thumb is a heuristic method. 使用经验法则是一种启发式方法。
Example 2: He employed a heuristic approach to solve the complex puzzle. 他采用了一种启发式方法来解决这个复杂的谜题。 | synonyms: exploratory, trial-and-error, intuitive, speculative | antonyms: algorithmic, systematic, exhaustive, rigorous | adjective, noun
