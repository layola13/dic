centenarianism:/ˌsɛntɪˈnɛəriənɪzəm/
n.
百岁老人现象；人瑞现象
The condition or state of being a centenarian; the increasing prevalence of centenarians.
The study of centenarianism is growing as more people live to be 100. 百岁老人现象的研究随着越来越多的人活到100岁而发展。
The conference explored various aspects of centenarianism, from genetics to lifestyle. 会议探讨了百岁老人现象的各个方面，从遗传学到生活方式。
近义词: longevity, long life
反义词: short lifespan, premature death
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From centenarian + -ism.  Centenarian comes from Latin *centenarius "containing a hundred," from centum "hundred."  The suffix -ism denotes a condition, state, or doctrine.
