overrepresentative:/ˌoʊvərˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv/ | adj. | 过度代表的；代表性过高的 | Having a greater representation than is justified or accurate, typically of a particular group within a larger population. |  This sample is overrepresentative of white, middle-class families.  这个样本过度代表了白人中产阶级家庭。| Overrepresented, disproportionately represented | Underrepresented | adjective
