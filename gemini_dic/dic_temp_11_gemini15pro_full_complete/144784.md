over-feels:/ˌoʊvərˈfiːlz/ |v. (informal)|过度感受，反应过激| To experience or express emotions too intensely, often in a dramatic or exaggerated way. | Example 1: He tends to over-feels everything, so a small setback can feel like the end of the world to him. 他往往对所有事情都反应过激，所以一个小小的挫折对他来说就像世界末日一样。

Example 2: She over-felt when her favorite band broke up, crying for days and refusing to listen to their music. 当她最喜欢的乐队解散时，她反应过度，哭了几天，并拒绝听他们的音乐。| 近义词: overreact, exaggerate, dramatize | 反义词: underreact, downplay |词性: 动词
