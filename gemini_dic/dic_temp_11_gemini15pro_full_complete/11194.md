architect: /ˈɑːrkɪtekt/ (US) /ˈɑːkɪtekt/ (UK) | n. | 建筑师；设计师；缔造者 | A person who designs buildings and in many cases also supervises their construction. |  Frank Lloyd Wright was a famous architect.  弗兰克·劳埃德·赖特是一位著名的建筑师。

The software architect designed the system.  这位软件架构师设计了该系统。

| 近义词: designer, planner, builder, creator | 反义词: destroyer, demolisher | 词性: noun
