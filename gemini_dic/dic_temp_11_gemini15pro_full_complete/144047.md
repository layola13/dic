outrightnesses:/ˌaʊtˈraɪtnəsɪz/| n. |直率，坦率，彻底|The quality or state of being outright; directness, bluntness, completeness.  It refers to instances of being completely straightforward and open, leaving no room for doubt or ambiguity.|His outrightnesses about the company's failings surprised everyone. 他对公司缺点的坦率让每个人都感到惊讶。|近义词：frankness, candor, bluntness, directness, completeness|反义词:indirectness, evasiveness, subtlety, incompleteness|noun

Etymology:
From outright + -ness. Outright itself comes from Old English *ūtriht, from ūt (out) + riht (right).
