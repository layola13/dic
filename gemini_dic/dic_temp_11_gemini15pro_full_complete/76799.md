gammer:/ˈɡæmər/| n.|老妇人，村妇|An elderly woman, especially a gossipy or talkative one.  Originally, a respectful term for an older woman, it later acquired a slightly negative connotation.|例句：The gammer sat by the fire, knitting and telling stories. 老妇人坐在火炉旁，一边织毛衣一边讲故事。

例句：The village gammers would gather at the well each morning to exchange gossip. 村里的老妇人们每天早上都会聚集在井边交换八卦。|近义词：old woman, matron, dame|反义词:young woman, lass, maiden|词性:noun
