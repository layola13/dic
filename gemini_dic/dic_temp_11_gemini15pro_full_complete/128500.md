newsreels: /ˈnjuːzriːlz/ (US), /ˈnjuːzriːlz/ (UK)
n.
新闻短片；新闻电影
Short films of news and current affairs, formerly shown in cinemas.
Example: We used to watch newsreels before the main feature at the cinema. 我们过去常常在电影正片放映前观看新闻短片。
Example: Newsreels captured many important historical events.  新闻短片记录了许多重要的历史事件。

近义词: newsfilms, documentaries
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps fictional films)
词性: noun
