infer:/ɪnˈfɜːr/ (US) /ɪnˈfɜː(r)/ (UK)| v. |推断，推论|to reach a conclusion based on evidence and reasoning|From her silence, I inferred that she was angry. 从她的沉默中，我推断她生气了。|deduce, conclude, gather|surmise, guess, assume|verb
Etymology: From Latin *inferre* "bring in, carry in," from *in-* "in" + *ferre* "to carry."  The sense evolution is from "bring in (an idea) to the mind."
