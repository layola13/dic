mispredict:/ˌmɪsprɪˈdɪkt/ | v. | 错误地预测；预测失误 | To predict incorrectly; to make a wrong prediction about something. | The economist mispredicted the rise in inflation. 经济学家错误地预测了通货膨胀的上升。| The weather forecast mispredicted the storm's path. 天气预报错误地预测了风暴的路径。| synonyms: forecast wrongly, miscalculate, misjudge | antonyms: predict accurately, foresee correctly | verb
