mafia: /ˈmɑːfiə/ (US), /ˈmæfiə/ (UK)| n. |黑手党；秘密犯罪组织|A criminal organization originating in Sicily, characterized by its strong family ties, code of silence, and involvement in illegal activities such as extortion, racketeering, and drug trafficking.  It also refers to similar organizations in other countries.|The police are investigating the mafia's involvement in the drug trade. 警方正在调查黑手党参与毒品交易的情况。|syndicate, mob, gang|law enforcement, authorities|noun
