absonant:/ˈæbsənənt/ | adj. | 刺耳的，不和谐的，格格不入的 |  Sounding harsh and discordant; incongruous.  Derived from Latin "absonus" (dissonant), from "ab-" (away from) + "sonus" (sound). |  The absonant music made everyone uncomfortable.  刺耳的音乐让每个人都感到不舒服。 | The absonant colors clashed horribly.  不和谐的颜色可怕地冲突着。 | 近义词：discordant, jarring, cacophonous, inharmonious, incongruous | 反义词：harmonious, euphonious, consonant, concordant, congruous | 词性: adjective
