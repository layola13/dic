antimechanization:/ˌæntiˌmɛkənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 反机械化；抵制机械化 |  The opposition to the use of machinery, especially in warfare or agriculture.  It often involves tactics or strategies designed to negate the advantages of mechanized forces or equipment. |  The farmers' antimechanization protests delayed the introduction of combine harvesters for years. 农民的反机械化抗议活动使得联合收割机的引进推迟了数年。 | Synonyms:  anti-technology, Luddism | Antonyms: mechanization, automation | noun

antimechanize:/ˌæntiˈmɛkənaɪz/ | v. | 反机械化；抵制机械化 | To oppose or resist the use of machinery. |  Some groups sought to antimechanize agriculture, believing it would lead to job losses. 一些团体试图抵制农业机械化，认为这会导致失业。 | Synonyms: resist mechanization, oppose automation | Antonyms: mechanize, automate | verb
