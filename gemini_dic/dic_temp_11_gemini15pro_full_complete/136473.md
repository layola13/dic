nonrevoltingly:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈvəʊl.tɪŋ.li/
adjective
不令人作呕地，不令人厌恶地
Not causing revulsion or disgust.
Her surprisingly nonrevolting burp after the large meal was a testament to her impeccable manners.  她吃完大餐后令人惊讶地打了一个不令人作呕的嗝，证明了她无可挑剔的举止。
He described the scene of the accident in a nonrevolting, factual manner. 他以一种不令人反感、实事求是的态度描述了事故现场。
近义词:inoffensive, pleasant, agreeable
反义词:revolting, disgusting, nauseating
词性:adverb

词源:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "revolting" (adjective meaning "causing intense disgust") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
