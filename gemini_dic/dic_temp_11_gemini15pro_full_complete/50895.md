deserting:/dɪˈzɜːtɪŋ/
v.
遗弃，抛弃，擅离
Leaving or abandoning someone or something.
He was court-martialed for deserting his post. 他因擅离职守而被军事法庭审判。
Synonyms: abandoning, forsaking, leaving, quitting
Antonyms: supporting, staying, helping, aiding
词性: 动词

deserting:/dɪˈzɜːtɪŋ/
adj.
荒芜的，无人居住的
Barren or desolate; empty of inhabitants.
The deserting landscape stretched for miles. 荒凉的景色绵延数英里。
Synonyms: desolate, barren, deserted, empty
Antonyms: populated, inhabited, fertile, lush
词性: 形容词
