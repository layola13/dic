arty:/ˈɑːrti/ /ˈɑːrdi/ | adj. |附庸风雅的，装腔作势的；爱好艺术的|  Affecting or pretending to have an interest in the arts, typically in a way that is regarded as pretentious or superficial; of or characteristic of artists or the arts.  |She's very arty and hangs out with a lot of painters and musicians. 她很附庸风雅，经常和很多画家和音乐家混在一起。|  The café had a very arty atmosphere. 这家咖啡馆很有艺术氛围。|  pretentious, affected, bohemian, sophisticated| down-to-earth, unpretentious, simple| adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From art + -y.  The suffix "-y" is used to form adjectives from nouns, often implying "characterized by" or "full of."
