guerrillaism:/ɡəˈrɪləɪzəm/
|n.|游击主义；游击战术；游击战|The use of unconventional warfare tactics, typically involving small, mobile groups of fighters operating against a larger, more established force.|The rebels resorted to guerrillaism in their fight for independence. 叛军在争取独立的斗争中采用了游击战术。|近义词：guerrilla warfare, unconventional warfare, partisan warfare|反义词:conventional warfare|词性:名词

guerrillaisms:/ɡəˈrɪləɪzəmz/
|n.|游击活动；游击战术（guerrillaism的复数）|Instances or examples of guerrilla warfare tactics.|The general outlined several guerrillaisms employed by the enemy. 将军概述了敌人使用的几种游击战术。|近义词：guerrilla tactics, unconventional warfare tactics|反义词:conventional warfare tactics|词性:名词
