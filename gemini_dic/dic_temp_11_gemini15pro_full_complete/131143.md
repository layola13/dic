nonblundering:/ˌnɒnˈblʌndərɪŋ/ | adj. | 不犯错的，不出差错的 |  Not making blunders; proceeding without mistakes.  Derived from "blunder" (meaning a stupid or careless mistake) with the prefix "non-" indicating negation. | The nonblundering surgeon performed the complex operation flawlessly.  这位不出差错的外科医生完美地完成了复杂的手术。 |  accurate, precise, faultless, impeccable | blundering, clumsy, error-prone | adjective
