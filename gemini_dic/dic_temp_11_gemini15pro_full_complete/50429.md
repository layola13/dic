deprave:/dɪˈpreɪv/| vt. |使堕落；使腐败；败坏|to make someone morally bad; to corrupt|Exposure to violence can deprave young people. 接触暴力会使年轻人堕落。|The city was depraved by vice. 这座城市因恶习而堕落。|近义词：corrupt, pervert, debase, vitiate|反义词：purify, elevate, ennoble|词性:动词
词源:From Latin *depravare*, from *de-* (“down, away from”) + *pravus* (“crooked, perverse”).
