distinguishable:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbl/| adj.|可区分的，可辨别的|Capable of being distinguished or recognized as different; discernible.|The twins are so alike that they are barely distinguishable. 这对双胞胎长得太像了，几乎无法区分。|Synonyms: discernible, perceptible, noticeable, recognizable, identifiable|Antonyms: indistinguishable, identical, similar, alike|adjective

distinguishable:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbl/| adj.|可辨认的，可分辨出的|Able to be clearly identified or differentiated.|The counterfeit money was barely distinguishable from the real thing.  这假币几乎和真的一模一样，很难分辨。|Synonyms: differentiable, recognizable, identifiable|Antonyms: indistinguishable, unidentifiable, imperceptible|adjective
