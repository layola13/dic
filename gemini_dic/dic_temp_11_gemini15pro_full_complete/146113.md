overembroidering: /ˌoʊvərɪmˈbrɔɪdərɪŋ/
verb
过度刺绣，过分修饰
Embroidering something too much, adding excessive decoration.
The dress was overembroidering, making it look gaudy rather than elegant. 这件衣服绣得太多了，看起来很俗气而不是优雅。
She overembroidered the cushion with sequins, beads, and ribbons. 她用亮片、珠子和丝带过度装饰了靠垫。
近义词: overdecorating, embellishing excessively
反义词: understating, simplifying
词性: Verb (present participle)
