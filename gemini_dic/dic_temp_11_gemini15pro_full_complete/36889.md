colorfully:/ˈkʌlərfəli/
adverb|丰富多彩地，艳丽地|In a way that uses a lot of bright colors.|例句：The birds were colorfully feathered. 这些鸟的羽毛色彩艳丽。|近义词：brightly, vividly, gaily|反义词：dully, plainly, drably|词性: 副词
Etymology: From "colorful" + "-ly". "Colorful" comes from "color" (from Old French "color," ultimately from Latin "color") + "-ful" (a suffix meaning "full of").
