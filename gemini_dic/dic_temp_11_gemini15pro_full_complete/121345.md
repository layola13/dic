misgovernance:/ˌmɪsˈɡʌvərnəns/
n.
管理不善，治理不当
The country suffered from years of misgovernance. 这个国家遭受了多年的管理不善。
Example: The misgovernance of the company led to its bankruptcy. 公司的管理不善导致其破产。

Example:  The report highlighted the misgovernance and corruption within the organization.  这份报告强调了该组织内部的管理不善和腐败。

Synonym: mismanagement, maladministration, misrule
Antonym: good governance, sound management

词性: noun
