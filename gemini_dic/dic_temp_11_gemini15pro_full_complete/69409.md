feel-oneself:/fiːl wʌnˈsɛlf/ |v. |感到自在；觉得身体正常；恢复自信|To be aware of one's identity, personality, or existence; to feel healthy and normal again.|例句：After a long illness, she finally began to feel herself again.  久病之后，她终于开始感觉像自己了。

例句：He didn't feel himself after the surgery.  手术后，他感觉不太舒服。|近义词：be oneself, be back to normal, recover, be comfortable in one's own skin |反义词：be out of sorts, feel unwell, be ill at ease |词性：动词
