agazed:/əˈɡeɪzd/
| adj. | 凝视的，注视的 | Filled with wonder and often open-mouthed admiration; staring intently. |  She stood agazed at the magnificent sunset. 她凝视着壮丽的日落。 | synonyms: astonished, awestruck, bewildered, dazzled, dumbfounded, fascinated, flabbergasted, open-mouthed, spellbound, startled, stunned, thunderstruck, wide-eyed | antonyms: indifferent, unconcerned, uninterested | adjective
