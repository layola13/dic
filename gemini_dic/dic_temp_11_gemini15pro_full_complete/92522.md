hypotrichosis:/ˌhaɪpoʊtraɪˈkoʊsɪs/
noun
少毛症
A condition characterized by a deficiency of hair on the body.
Hypotrichosis can be inherited or acquired due to various factors. 少毛症可能是遗传性的，也可能是由于各种因素后天获得的。
Congenital hypotrichosis can be caused by genetic mutations. 先天性少毛症可能是由基因突变引起的。
近义词:atrichia, alopecia
反义词:hypertrichosis
词性:n.
