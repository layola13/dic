cadetship:/kəˈdɛtʃɪp/
n.
学员身份；见习期
A period of training for a cadet, especially a trainee officer in the armed forces or merchant navy.
He gained a cadetship with the Royal Navy.  他获得了皇家海军的学员身份。
traineeship, apprenticeship, internship
 
词性: 名词
