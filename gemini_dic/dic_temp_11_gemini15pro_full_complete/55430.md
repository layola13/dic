distinct-from:/dɪˈstɪŋkt frəm/ | adj. | 不同的，有区别的 | Separate and different; not the same.  Being clearly different or of a different kind. |例句：The two species are distinct from each other. 这两个物种彼此不同。

例句：His voice is quite distinct from his brother's. 他的声音和他兄弟的完全不同。

近义词：different, separate, discrete, unlike, dissimilar |反义词：similar, alike, identical, same |词性：形容词
