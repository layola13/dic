lay-byes:/ˈleɪbaɪz/ (UK), /ˈleɪbaɪz/ (US)
n.
分期付款购买;预留商品;路边停车带
A system of paying a deposit to reserve an item and then making regular payments until the full price is paid. A designated area by the side of a road where vehicles can stop temporarily.
I put the furniture on lay-by. 我把家具分期付款买了。
There's a lay-by just up ahead where we can pull over and admire the view. 前面有个路边停车带，我们可以停下来欣赏风景。
近义词: installment plan, layaway, reservation, roadside parking area
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) immediate purchase, full payment
词性: 名词 (plural)
