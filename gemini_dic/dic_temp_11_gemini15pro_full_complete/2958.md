adynamia:/ˌeɪdaɪˈneɪmiə/ /ˌædɪˈneɪmiə/| n. |无力，虚弱|Lack or loss of strength; weakness.|例句：The patient experienced severe adynamia after the surgery. 手术后病人感到严重的无力。

例句：Prolonged bed rest can lead to adynamia.  长时间卧床休息会导致无力。|近义词：weakness, debility, asthenia|反义词: strength, vigor, energy|词性:noun
Etymology: From Greek *ἀδυναμία (adynamia)  "powerlessness," from *ἀ- (a-)  "not" + *δύναμις (dynamis) "power, strength".
