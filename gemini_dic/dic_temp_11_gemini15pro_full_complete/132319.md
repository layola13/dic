nondefiniteness:/ˌnɒndɪˈfɪnɪtnəs/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈfɪnɪtnəs/
n.
不确定性，非限定性
lack of definiteness; the quality of being indefinite or imprecise.
The nondefiniteness of the contract terms led to disputes.  合同条款的不确定性导致了纠纷。
indefiniteness, uncertainty, vagueness
definiteness, certainty, precision
noun
