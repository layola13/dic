mushed:/mʌʃt/| adj.|糊状的，软烂的|Reduced to a soft, pulpy mass; crushed.|The potatoes were overcooked and mushed.|近义词：pulpy, mashed, squishy|反义词:firm, solid|词性:形容词

词源: From mush (n.) + -ed.  Ultimately from Old French *moschier "to wet, soak," perhaps from Vulgar Latin *mocciare, related to mucus "mucus."
