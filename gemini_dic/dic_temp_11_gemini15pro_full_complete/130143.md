non-regardance:/ˌnɒn rɪˈɡɑːdəns/ | n. |漠视，忽视，不理会|The state of not regarding or considering something; disregard. |His non-regardance of the rules resulted in his dismissal. 他对规则的漠视导致了他的解雇。|
|Synonyms: disregard, neglect, indifference, inattention, slighting |Antonyms: regard, attention, consideration, heed, notice |noun
