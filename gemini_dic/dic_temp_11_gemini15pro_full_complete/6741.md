analytic:/ˌænəˈlɪtɪk/ | adj. |分析的，解析的| Relating to or using analysis or logic.|例句：He has a very analytic mind. 他有很强的分析能力。|例句：The report provided an analytic overview of the market. 这份报告对市场进行了分析性的概述。|近义词：logical, reasoning, systematic|反义词：intuitive, synthetic, unreasoning|词性:形容词

analytic:/ˌænəˈlɪtɪk/ | n. |分析方法，分析结果|(often analytics) the process of using statistical methods to analyse data, especially in large amounts, in order to discover or predict patterns and trends.|例句：Data analytics can help businesses make better decisions. 数据分析可以帮助企业做出更好的决策。|例句：The use of website analytics is crucial for online marketing. 网站分析的使用对于在线营销至关重要。|近义词：analysis, examination, investigation|反义词: synthesis|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *analyticus*, from Ancient Greek ἀναλυτικός (analutikós, “fit for dissolving, resolving”), from ἀναλύω (analúō, “I dissolve, resolve”)
