icing:/ˈaɪsɪŋ/| n.|糖霜；结冰|A sweet coating used on cakes or biscuits. Also, the formation of ice on a surface.|"The cake had a thick layer of icing." 蛋糕上有一层厚厚的糖霜。 "The car's windshield had a layer of icing." 汽车挡风玻璃上结了一层冰。|近义词:frosting, glaze (for cakes); freezing (for ice formation)|反义词:thawing (for ice formation)|词性:名词
