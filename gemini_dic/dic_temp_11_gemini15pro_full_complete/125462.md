multiplicative:/ˌmʌltɪˈplɪkətɪv/ | adj. | 乘法的，倍增的 |  Relating to multiplication; tending to multiply. | The multiplicative inverse of 2 is 0.5.  2的乘法逆元是0.5。| synonyms: multiplying,  | antonyms: dividing, additive | adjective


multiplicative:/ˌmʌltɪˈplɪkətɪv/ | n. | 乘数 | A number by which another number is multiplied. |  The multiplicative is 3 in the expression 3x. 在表达式3x中，乘数是3。| synonyms: multiplier, factor | antonyms: divisor, dividend | noun
