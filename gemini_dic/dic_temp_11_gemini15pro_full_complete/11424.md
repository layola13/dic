argillous:/ɑːrˈdʒɪləs/| adj.|粘土的，含粘土的|Consisting of or containing clay; clayey.|The soil in this region is argillous and difficult to cultivate. 这个地区的土壤是粘土质的，难以耕种。|近义词：clayey, loamy|反义词：sandy|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *argillosus, from Latin argilla (“clay”).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek ἄργιλλος (árgillos, “white clay”).
