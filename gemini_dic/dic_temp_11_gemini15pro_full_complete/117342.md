media's:/ˈmiːdiəz/
noun|媒体的，媒介的|Belonging to or associated with the media.  It is the possessive form of "media," which itself is treated as a singular noun despite appearing plural.|
The media's coverage of the election was extensive. 媒体对选举的报道非常广泛。
The media's influence on public opinion is undeniable. 媒体对公众舆论的影响是不可否认的。
|近义词: press's, journalism's|反义词: N/A|词性: possessive noun (名词所有格)
