gypseous:/ˈdʒɪpsiəs/| adj.|含石膏的，石膏质的|Relating to, resembling, or containing gypsum.|The soil in this area is gypseous. 这个地区的土壤含石膏。|Synonyms: chalky, calcareous|Antonyms: non-gypseous|adjective

gypseous:/ˈdʒɪpsiəs/| adj.|像石膏的|Resembling gypsum in appearance or texture.|The walls had a gypseous texture. 墙壁的纹理像石膏。|Synonyms: chalky, powdery|Antonyms: smooth, glossy|adjective
