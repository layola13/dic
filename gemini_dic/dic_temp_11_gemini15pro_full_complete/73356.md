foregate:/ˈfɔːrɡeɪt/| n.|前门，外城门|A gate in the outer wall of a city or fortified place, often leading to a main road. |The old foregate of the city was still standing.  这座城市古老的前门仍然屹立着。|The enemy breached the foregate and entered the city. 敌人攻破了前门，进入了城市。|Synonyms: gateway, entrance, portal|Antonyms: back gate, rear entrance|noun
