overslow:/ˌoʊvərˈsloʊ/| v. |放慢；使…减速；行动过慢|To reduce the speed of something; to go too slowly.|The driver overslowed as he approached the intersection. 司机在接近十字路口时放慢了速度.|近义词：decelerate, slow down|反义词:accelerate, speed up|词性:动词
Etymology: "over-" + "slow"  "over-" as a prefix indicating "excessive" or "too much," and "slow" meaning moving at a low speed.
