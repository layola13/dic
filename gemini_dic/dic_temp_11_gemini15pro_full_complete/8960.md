antihydrophobic:/ˌæntiˌhaɪdrəˈfoʊbɪk/
adjective
抗疏水的，亲水的
Resisting or counteracting hydrophobicity; having an affinity for water.
This new coating is antihydrophobic, allowing the surface to stay wet. 这种新的涂层是抗疏水的，可以让表面保持湿润。
The researchers developed an antihydrophobic material for use in water filtration. 研究人员开发了一种用于水过滤的抗亲水材料。
Synonyms: hydrophilic, water-loving, water-attracting
Antonyms: hydrophobic, water-repelling, water-fearing
word class: adjective
