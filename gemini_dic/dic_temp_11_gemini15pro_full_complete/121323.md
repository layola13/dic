misframes:/mɪsˈfreɪmz/
| v. | 误读；曲解；歪曲 | To interpret (something, such as a situation or someone's words or actions) incorrectly. | The media misframed the politician's comments, taking them out of context. 媒体曲解了这位政治家的评论，断章取义。 |  He misframed my intentions, thinking I was trying to sabotage him. 他误解了我的意图，以为我想破坏他的计划。 | misinterprets, distorts, misrepresents | accurately represents, correctly interprets | verb
