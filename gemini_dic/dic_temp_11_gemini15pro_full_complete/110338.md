ETYMOLOGY->
From "light" + "haller" (an agent noun derived from the verb "hall," meaning "to pull" or "to drag," likely referring to the act of hauling supplies or equipment up to the lighthouse). Although "haller" isn't a standard word in modern English, it likely evolved from older forms of the word "haul."
