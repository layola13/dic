battiest:/ˈbætiɪst/ | adj. | 最疯狂的，最古怪的 |  Superlative adjective of "batty," meaning extremely eccentric or foolish.  It describes someone or something as being the most crazy or strange out of a group. | That's the battiest idea I've ever heard! 那是我听过的最疯狂的想法！|  crazy, insane, eccentric, wacky, bizarre | sane, sensible, rational | adjective

battiest:/ˈbætiɪst/ | adj. | 最疯狂的，最古怪的 |  Derived from "bat," slang for "crazy" or "insane," likely related to the erratic flight of bats.  "Batty" became an adjective meaning "crazy," and "battiest" is its superlative form. | He had the battiest collection of garden gnomes I've ever seen.  他收藏的那些花园侏儒是我见过的最古怪的。 |  mad, daft, loopy | normal, ordinary, conventional | adjective
