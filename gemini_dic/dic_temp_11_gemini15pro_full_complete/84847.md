hangability:/ˌhæŋəˈbɪləti/
n.
可绞死性，可被处以绞刑的性质
The hangability of the defendant was a key point of the trial.  被告是否可以被处以绞刑是审判的关键点。
English Explanation: The quality or state of being suitable for hanging (as a form of execution).  This refers specifically to whether a person can be legally executed by hanging, not to the physical act of hanging itself.
Example: The judge questioned the hangability of the convicted murderer. 法官质疑了被判有罪的杀人犯是否可以被处以绞刑。
Synonym: executability (by hanging)
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, consider "reprieve" or "pardon" as antonyms for the outcome)
词性: 名词
