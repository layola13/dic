nonobstructive:/ˌnɑːnəbˈstrʌktɪv/
adjective
非阻塞性的；无梗阻的
Not causing or characterized by obstruction, especially of a bodily passage or duct.
His asthma was classified as nonobstructive. 他的哮喘被归类为非阻塞性哮喘。
The patient presented with nonobstructive jaundice. 病人表现为非梗阻性黄疸。
近义词:nonblocking, unobstructed, patent
反义词:obstructive, blocked
词性:adjective
