motivity:/moʊˈtɪvɪti/
n.
动力;积极性
The driving force or impulse behind an action; motivation.
His motivity for learning is his desire to get a better job. 他学习的动力是他想找一份更好的工作。

近义词: motivation, drive, impetus, incentive
反义词: apathy, inertia, indifference
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *motivitas, from Latin motus (“motion”), past participle of movēre (“to move”).
