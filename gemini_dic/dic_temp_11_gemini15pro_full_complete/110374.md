lignify:/ˈlɪɡnɪfaɪ/| v. |木质化，木化|To become wood or woody; to convert into wood.  The plant cells began to lignify, adding strength and rigidity to the stem.|例句：The cell walls lignify, providing structural support to the plant. 细胞壁木质化，为植物提供结构支撑。

例句：As the tree grew older, its branches continued to lignify. 随着树龄的增长，它的枝干继续木质化。|近义词：harden, solidify, petrify|反义词:soften, decay|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin lignum ("wood") + -ify.
