double-breasted:/ˌdʌbəlˈbrɛstɪd/
adj.
双排扣的
having two parallel rows of buttons and overlapping front flaps
He wore a double-breasted navy blue suit. 他穿着一套双排扣的藏蓝色西装。
She prefers double-breasted coats for winter. 她冬天更喜欢穿双排扣的大衣。
近义词:double-buttoned
反义词:single-breasted
词性:形容词
