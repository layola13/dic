What are you doing now? = 你现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you done today? = 你今天做了什么？
What has she learned in this class? = 她在这节课学到了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been doing all day? = 你一整天都在做什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did he say to you? = 他对你说了什么？
What happened yesterday at the park? = 昨天在公园发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when I called? = 我打电话时你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会时她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had happened before I arrived? = 在我到达之前发生了什么事？
What had they discussed before the meeting? = 会议前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the accident? = 事故发生前你一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for so long? = 他们一直在讨论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow after work? = 你明天下班后要做什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? = 明天这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting next Friday? = 下周五的会议上他们会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
