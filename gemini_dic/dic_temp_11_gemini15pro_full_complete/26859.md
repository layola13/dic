cajones:/kəˈhoʊneɪz/| n.| 卡洪鼓;  勇气; 胆量 (slang, informal)|A box-shaped percussion instrument played by slapping the front or sides with the hands.  Figuratively, it refers to courage or nerve, especially in a challenging situation.  Originates from the Spanish word "cajón" meaning "box," "drawer," or "crate."|He played a lively rhythm on the cajones. 他用卡洪鼓演奏了一段活泼的节奏。|Synonyms: box drum, nerve, guts, courage, bravery|Antonyms: cowardice, fear, timidity|noun
