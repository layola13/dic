fancy-free:/ˈfænsiˌfriː/
adjective
无拘无束的，自由自在的
Free from emotional ties or responsibilities, especially romantic ones; carefree.
She felt fancy-free after graduating from college. 她大学毕业后感到无拘无束。
He lived a fancy-free life, traveling the world with no attachments. 他过着自由自在的生活，无牵无挂地环游世界。
近义词:carefree, untroubled, unburdened
反义词:burdened, responsible, attached
词性:adjective

词源:
From "fancy" (meaning "love, inclination") + "free."  The original sense was "free from the constraints of love," implying a lack of romantic entanglement.
