hoarded:/ˈhɔːrdɪd/
verb|囤积，贮藏| accumulated and stored away, often excessively|The squirrel hoarded nuts for the winter. 松鼠为冬天囤积坚果。|近义词：stockpiled, amassed, accumulated, cached|反义词:squandered, spent, dispersed|v.

hoard:/hɔːrd/
noun|贮藏，囤积物|a supply or accumulation that is hidden or carefully guarded|He had a hoard of old coins in his attic. 他在阁楼里藏了一堆旧硬币。|近义词：stockpile, cache, store, reserve|反义词:lack, dearth, scarcity|n.
