outlived:/ˌaʊtˈlɪvd/ | v. | 比...活得长; 比...持久;  | to live longer than someone or something; to continue to exist after something else has ended or become less important |  My grandmother outlived her husband by ten years. 我的祖母比她丈夫多活了十年。

The usefulness of this technology has outlived itself. 这项技术的实用性已经过时了。 | 近义词：survived, outlasted | 反义词：predeceased | 词性: 动词
