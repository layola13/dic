atlanticist:/ætˈlæntɪsɪst/
n.| 大西洋主义者|A supporter of the political, economic, and military alliance between North America and Western Europe, particularly the United States and the countries of the European Union. |She's a staunch Atlanticist, believing in the importance of transatlantic cooperation.|近义词：transatlanticist, Westernizer |反义词:isolationist, protectionist
词性:名词

atlanticist:/ætˈlæntɪsɪst/
adj.|大西洋主义的| Relating to or supporting Atlanticist policies or principles.  |The new president took a decidedly Atlanticist stance in foreign policy.| 近义词：transatlantic, Western-oriented |反义词:isolationist, protectionist
