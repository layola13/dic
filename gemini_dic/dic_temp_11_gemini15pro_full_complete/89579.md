hoo-ha:/ˈhuːhɑː/
noun
喧闹，骚动；争吵；麻烦，困境
A commotion or fuss; an uproar.
例句：There was a big hoo-ha about the new parking regulations.  关于新的停车规定有很多的争吵。

例句：What's all the hoo-ha about?  吵什么吵？


近义词：fuss, commotion, uproar, disturbance, ado, rumpus, to-do
反义词：calm, peace, quiet
词性：n.
