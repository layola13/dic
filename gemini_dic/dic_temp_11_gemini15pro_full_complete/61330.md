embathe:/ɪmˈbeɪð/ /ɛmˈbeɪð/| v. |浸泡，沐浴，沉浸|To immerse or suffuse something, often in liquid or light; to bathe.|The setting sun embathed the mountains in a golden glow. 落日将群山沐浴在金色的光辉中.|近义词：bathe, immerse, submerge, drench, suffuse|反义词:dry, dehydrate|词性:动词

词源:
From em- + bathe.  "Em-" is a variant of "en-" which acts as a prefix meaning "in" or "into".  "Bathe" comes from Old English *baþian, from Proto-Germanic *baþōną, meaning "to warm oneself, bathe".
