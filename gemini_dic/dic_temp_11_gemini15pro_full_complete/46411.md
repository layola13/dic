daftest:/ˈdeɪfɪst/| adj.|最愚蠢的|Most foolish or silly.|That's the daftest idea I've ever heard! 那是迄今为止我听过的最愚蠢的主意！|例句：Wearing a bikini in the snow is the daftest thing you could do. 在雪地里穿比基尼是你能做的最愚蠢的事情。|近义词：silliest, stupidest, most foolish|反义词：wisest, smartest, most intelligent|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From *daft* (foolish, silly) + *-est* (superlative suffix).  *Daft* likely originates from the Old Norse *daftr* meaning "numb" or "lethargic," later evolving to mean "foolish" in Middle English.
