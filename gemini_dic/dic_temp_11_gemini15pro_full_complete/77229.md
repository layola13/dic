gastrocolic: /ˌɡæstrəˈkɒlɪk/ /ˌɡæstroʊˈkɑːlɪk/
adjective
胃结肠的
Relating to the stomach and the colon.  Specifically, referring to the reflex that triggers increased colonic motility after food enters the stomach.
The gastrocolic reflex is stronger in infants and can cause bowel movements shortly after feeding. 胃结肠反射在婴儿身上更强，会在喂食后不久引起排便。
Eating a large meal can stimulate the gastrocolic reflex.  吃大餐会刺激胃结肠反射。
Synonyms:  None specifically for the medical term.  One might use "digestive" in a broader context.
Antonyms:  None.
词性: adjective
