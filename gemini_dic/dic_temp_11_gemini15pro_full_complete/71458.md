flamenco: /fləˈmɛŋkoʊ/ (US), /fləˈmeŋkəʊ/ (UK) | n. adj. |  弗拉门戈舞/弗拉门戈风格的 | A style of Spanish music and dance, characterized by vigorous strumming, singing, and dancing.  It originated in Andalusia, Spain. |  例句：
She's a passionate flamenco dancer. 她是一位充满激情的弗拉门戈舞者。

The guitarist played a lively flamenco piece.  吉他手演奏了一首活泼的弗拉门戈曲子。

| 近义词：fandango（凡丹戈舞，也是西班牙的一种舞蹈） | 反义词:  (No direct antonym.  Could consider contrasting genres like classical ballet or waltz) | 词性: noun, adjective
