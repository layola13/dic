aestivate: /ˈɛstɪveɪt/ | v. | 夏眠，度夏 | To spend the summer in a state of dormancy or inactivity, especially to avoid heat and drought. |  Example 1: Desert tortoises aestivate during the hottest months. 沙漠龟在最热的几个月里夏眠。

Example 2:  Several species of snails aestivate to survive dry conditions.  几种蜗牛夏眠以度过干燥的环境。 | Synonym: hibernate, sleep, rest | Antonym: be active, awaken | verb
