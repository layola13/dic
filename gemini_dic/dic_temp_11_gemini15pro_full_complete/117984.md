memorative:/kəˈmɛmərətɪv/ /kəˈmɛmˌəˌreɪtɪv/ | adj. | 纪念的；追悼的 | Serving to commemorate or serving as a memorial. |  This commemorative stamp was issued to mark the centenary of her birth. 这枚纪念邮票是为了纪念她诞辰一百周年而发行的。

The museum houses a commemorative exhibition of his works.  博物馆举办了他的作品纪念展。 | 近义词：memorial, remembrance, celebratory | 反义词: n/a | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin commemorativus, from commemorare "to call to mind, remember solemnly," from com- "together" + memorare "to bring to mind," from memor "mindful."
