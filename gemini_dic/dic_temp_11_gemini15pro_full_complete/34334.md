clashingly:/ˈklæʃɪŋli/
adverb
冲突地，不协调地
in a way that clashes; jarringly.
Her bright pink dress clashed clashingly with the somber atmosphere of the funeral. 她鲜艳的粉红色连衣裙与葬礼的肃穆气氛很不协调。
Near synonyms: jarringly, discordantly, disharmoniously, cacophonously
Near antonyms: harmoniously, congruously, compatibly
词性: 副词

clash:/klæʃ/
verb
冲突，碰撞，抵触
(of colours) look unpleasant together.
The red walls clashed horribly with the orange carpet.  红色的墙壁和橙色的地毯极其不协调。
Near synonyms:  jar, grate, conflict, disagree
Near antonyms: harmonize, match, blend
词性: 动词

clash:/klæʃ/
noun
冲突，撞击声，抵触
a loud, harsh, metallic sound.
The clash of cymbals marked the end of the piece.  钹的撞击声标志着这首曲子的结束。
Near synonyms:  conflict, collision, disagreement, argument
Near antonyms: agreement, harmony, concord
词性: 名词
