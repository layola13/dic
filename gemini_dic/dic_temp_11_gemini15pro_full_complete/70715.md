financist:/ˈfaɪnænsɪst/ /fɪˈnænsɪst/
n.
金融家；理财专家
A person skilled in or occupied with finance.
例句：The financist predicted a rise in interest rates. 金融家预测利率会上升。

例句：She's a respected financist on Wall Street. 她在华尔街是一位受人尊敬的金融家。
近义词：financier, banker, investor
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "debtor" in some contexts)
词性: noun
