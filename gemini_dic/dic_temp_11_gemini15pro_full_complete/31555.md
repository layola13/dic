chartulary: /ˈtʃɑːrtjʊləri/ | n. |文献集；图表集；（尤指中世纪的）寺院财产目录；契据登记簿 | A collection of charters or other documents, especially relating to the property or privileges of a monastery, cathedral, or other corporate body. | The chartulary of Westminster Abbey provides invaluable insights into medieval London.  威斯敏斯特教堂的文献集为了解中世纪伦敦提供了宝贵的资料。| synonyms: archive, register, record, annals | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "dispersion" in a figurative sense) | noun
