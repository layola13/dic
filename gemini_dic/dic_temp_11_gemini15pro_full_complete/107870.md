largess:/lɑːrˈdʒes/ |n.|慷慨赠予，施舍；赠品，赏金|generosity in bestowing money or gifts upon others.  A gift or donation. |The king distributed largess to the poor. 国王向穷人分发赏赐。|Synonyms: generosity, bounty, munificence, liberality,  donation, gift, present |Antonyms: stinginess, meanness, parsimony, miserliness|noun
