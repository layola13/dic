burnable:/ˈbɜːrnəbl/| adj.|可燃的，可燃烧的|Something that is burnable can be burned. |例句：The waste is burnable. 这些废物是可燃的。|例句：Only burnable materials should be put in the incinerator. 只有可燃材料才能放入焚化炉。|近义词：combustible, flammable, ignitable|反义词：non-burnable, non-flammable, fireproof|词性:形容词
词源: From "burn" (verb) + "-able" (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being"). "Burn" comes from Old English "bærnan," of Germanic origin.
