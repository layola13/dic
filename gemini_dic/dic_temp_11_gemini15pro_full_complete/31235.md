chanterships:/ˈtʃæntərʃɪps/
n.
chanter职位；领唱者职位；圣咏团领唱职位
The position or office of a chanter, especially in a cathedral, church, or chapel.
He held the chantership at the cathedral for over twenty years. 他在教堂担任领唱职位超过二十年。
近义词: precentorship, cantorship
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "choir member" in a broad sense)
词性: noun
