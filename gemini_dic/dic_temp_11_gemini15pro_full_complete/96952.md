infallible:/ɪnˈfæləbəl/| adj.| 绝对正确的，不会犯错的，万无一失的|incapable of making mistakes or being wrong|例句：No one is infallible. 没有人是不会犯错的。|例句：The Pope is considered infallible by Catholics. 天主教徒认为教皇是不会犯错的。|近义词：unerring, faultless, impeccable, perfect|反义词：fallible, erring, flawed, imperfect|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin infallibilis, from in- ‘not’ + fallere ‘deceive, err’.
