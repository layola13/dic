kobalt: /ˈkoʊbɔːlt/ (US), /ˈkəʊbɔːlt/ (UK)| n. |钴|A hard, silvery-white metallic element, symbol Co, atomic number 27, used in alloys, magnets, and paints.|Cobalt blue is a popular color for pottery. 钴蓝色是陶器常用的颜色。|Synonyms: none (as an element)|Antonyms: none (as an element)|noun


kobalt: /ˈkoʊbɔːlt/ (US), /ˈkəʊbɔːlt/ (UK)| adj. |钴的，含钴的|Made of or containing cobalt.|The vase had a beautiful kobalt blue glaze.  这个花瓶上釉有美丽的钴蓝色。|Synonyms: cobaltous|Antonyms: none|adjective
