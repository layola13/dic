gulpers:/ˈɡʌlpərz/| n.|大口吞咽者；贪婪者|People who gulp, often referring to those who drink or eat quickly and in large amounts.  Those who are eager to consume or acquire something.|The gulpers at the buffet were making short work of the roast beef.  自助餐上那些狼吞虎咽的人很快就吃光了烤牛肉。|近义词：gorger, glutton, devourer|反义词: nibbler, sipper| noun
