astrocytes:/ˈæstrəˌsaɪts/| n. |星形胶质细胞|A star-shaped glial cell in the brain and spinal cord that provides support for neurons, regulates the extracellular environment, and participates in various physiological processes.|Astrocytes play a crucial role in maintaining the blood-brain barrier. 星形胶质细胞在维持血脑屏障中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: astroglia|Antonyms: None|Noun
