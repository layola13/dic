collotype:/ˈkɒlətaɪp/ | n. |珂罗版，照相凹版|A printing process that uses a gelatin-coated glass plate to produce high-quality reproductions of images.  It is known for its ability to capture subtle tonal variations.| The museum displayed a series of collotypes of famous paintings.  博物馆展出了一系列名画的珂罗版印刷品。|synonyms: photogelatin, heliotype |antonyms: N/A |noun


collotype:/ˈkɒlətaɪp/ | n. |照相凹版印刷品|A print made using the collotype process. |  The collotype reproductions of the photographs were remarkably detailed.  这些照片的珂罗版复制品非常精细。|synonyms: print, reproduction |antonyms: N/A |noun
