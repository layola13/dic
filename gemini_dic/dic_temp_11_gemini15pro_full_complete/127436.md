nebbish:/ˈnɛbɪʃ/| n.|懦弱的人；无用的人；胆小鬼|A timid, meek, or ineffectual person; a nobody.|He was such a nebbish, he couldn't even stand up for himself when they cut in line. 他真是个懦夫，别人插队他都不敢吭声。|近义词：wimp, milquetoast, doormat, pushover|反义词：go-getter, extrovert, alpha|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Yiddish נעבעך (nebekh), literally "poor thing," from Hebrew נִבְכֶּה (niḇkeh), feminine singular of נִבְכֶּה (niḇkeh), "it is regrettable".
