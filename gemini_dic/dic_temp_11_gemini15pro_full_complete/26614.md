caching:/ˈkæʃɪŋ/ | n. | 缓存，高速缓存 | The process of storing data in a temporary storage area (a cache) to speed up access to it. |  The browser's caching significantly improved website loading times. 浏览器缓存显著提高了网站加载速度。 | synonyms: buffering, storing, memorizing | antonyms: retrieving, discarding | noun
