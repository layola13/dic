cornflour:/ˈkɔːrnflaʊər/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrnˌflaʊər/ (US)| n. |玉米淀粉；玉米粉|A fine flour ground from dried corn kernels, used as a thickening agent in cooking.|She used cornflour to thicken the sauce. 她用玉米淀粉使酱汁变稠。|Synonym: cornstarch (US), corn starch|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps wheat flour as an alternative thickener)|noun
