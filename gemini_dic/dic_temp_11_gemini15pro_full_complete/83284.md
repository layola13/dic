gynaecium:/ɡaɪˈniːsiəm/ |n.| 雌蕊群；雌蕊 |The gynoecium is the female reproductive part of a flower.| The botanist examined the gynoecium of the orchid.  植物学家检查了兰花的雌蕊群。|Synonym: pistil | Antonym: androecium | noun

gynoecium:/dʒaɪˈniːsiəm/ |n.| 雌蕊群；雌蕊 |The gynoecium consists of one or more pistils.|  Damage to the gynoecium can prevent seed formation. 对雌蕊的损害会阻止种子的形成。|Synonym: pistil | Antonym: androecium | noun
