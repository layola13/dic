nonlegume:/nɒnˈleɡjuːm/ /nɑːnˈleɡjuːm/
noun
非豆科植物
a plant that is not a legume, especially one used for animal fodder or green manure.
Example:  Farmers often rotate nonlegumes with legumes to improve soil health.  农民经常将非豆科植物与豆科植物轮作以改善土壤健康。

Example:  Rye is a common nonlegume cover crop. 黑麦是一种常见的非豆科覆盖作物。
synonym:  forage crop (when used as fodder), cover crop (when used for soil improvement)
antonym: legume
词性: noun
