devoids:/dɪˈvɔɪdz/| v. |使…缺乏；剥夺|To make something completely lacking in something.|He **devoids** the room of all furniture. 他把房间里的所有家具都搬空了。|近义词：strips, deprives, empties|反义词:fills, provides, endows|词性:动词

词源: From Old French *desvoidier*, from *des-* (expressing removal) + *voidier* "to empty," from *voide* "empty."
