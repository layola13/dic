lustful: /ˈlʌstfəl/ (US) /ˈlʌstfʊl/ (UK)| adj. |好色的；淫荡的；充满性欲的 | Having or showing strong sexual desire. | He gave her a lustful look. 他带着淫邪的目光看着她。| The novel is full of lustful descriptions. 这部小说充满了淫秽的描写。| 近义词: lecherous, lascivious, libidinous, concupiscent | 反义词: chaste, virtuous, pure | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *lustful, from Old English *lustfull (“full of pleasure, delightful”), equivalent to lust +‎ -ful.  The sense shifted from "full of pleasure" to specifically sexual pleasure.
