computable:/kəmˈpjuːtəbl/| adj.|可计算的|Capable of being computed or calculated.  This often refers to problems or functions that can be solved by an algorithm or computer program.  A computable function has a well-defined procedure that can be followed to obtain its output for any valid input.|例句：Determining the area of a circle is a computable problem. 计算圆的面积是一个可计算的问题。|近义词：calculable, solvable, determinable|反义词:incomputable, unsolvable, undecidable|adjective
