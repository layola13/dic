overmorally:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrəli/
adjective
过分道德的，过分拘泥于道德的
Excessively or overly concerned with morality; applying moral principles too rigidly or strictly.
He criticized the overmorally judgmental attitudes of some religious groups.  他批评了一些宗教团体过分拘泥于道德评判的态度。
She was known for her overmorally strict upbringing. 她以其过分拘泥道德的严格家教而闻名。
近义词:  puritanical, moralistic, self-righteous
反义词:  amoral, immoral, unethical
词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "moral" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix forming an adjective)
