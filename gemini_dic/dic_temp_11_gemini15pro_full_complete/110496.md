liman:/ˈliːmən/ | n. | 河口湾，潟湖 | A shallow coastal lagoon or lake, typically separated from the sea by a sandbar or barrier beach and connected by a narrow channel. | The Dniester Liman is a large liman in Ukraine.  德涅斯特河口湾是乌克兰的一个大型潟湖。 | estuary, lagoon, inlet | bay, gulf | noun

Etymology:
From Russian лима́н (limán), from Turkish liman (“port, harbor”), ultimately from Greek λιμήν (limḗn, “harbor, bay”).
