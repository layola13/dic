antiaggressive:/ˌæntiəˈɡrɛsɪv/
adjective
抗攻击性的；抑制攻击行为的
counteracting or diminishing aggression; tending to inhibit aggression.
The doctor prescribed antiaggressive medication for the patient. 医生给病人开了抗攻击性药物。
He took an antiaggressive stance during the negotiation. 在谈判中，他采取了不具攻击性的姿态。

synonym:  nonaggressive, pacifistic, appeasing, conciliatory
antonym: aggressive, belligerent, hostile, provocative
词性: 形容词
