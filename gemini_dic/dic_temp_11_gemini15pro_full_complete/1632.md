acinaceous: /ˌæsɪˈneɪʃəs/ | adj. | 簇生的，腺状的 | Resembling small berries or drupes; arranged in clusters like grapes.  Relating to or resembling the acini of a gland. | The acinaceous glands secreted a thick, milky substance. 这些腺状腺体分泌出一种浓稠的乳状物质。| The plant's acinaceous fruits were a vibrant shade of purple.  这种植物簇生的果实呈现出鲜艳的紫色。 |  clustered, grape-like, glandular | N/A | adjective
