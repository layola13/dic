marlin:/ˈmɑːrlɪn/ (US), /ˈmɑːlɪn/ (UK)| n.| 枪鱼，马林鱼 |A large, fast-swimming fish found in warm seas, popular as a game fish. |例句：The fisherman struggled for hours before finally reeling in the massive marlin.  这位渔夫奋斗了几个小时才最终钓到这条巨大的马林鱼。

例句：Marlin are highly prized by sport fishermen.  枪鱼是运动钓鱼者非常珍视的鱼类。

近义词：sailfish, spearfish|反义词: (No direct antonym for a type of fish) |词性:noun
