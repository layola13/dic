nonequalization:/ˌnɒnˌiːkwəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |不均衡，不均等化 |The nonequalization of resources led to significant disparities in educational outcomes. 资源分配的不均衡导致了教育成果的显著差异。|  Lack of nonequalization means everyone gets the same, regardless of need.  缺乏不均衡意味着每个人都得到相同的待遇，而不管需求如何。 | 近义词: disparity, imbalance, inequality | 反义词: equalization, parity, balance | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + equalization (from equalize, meaning "to make equal")
