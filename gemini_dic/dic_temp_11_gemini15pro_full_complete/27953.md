cantonese: /ˌkæn.təˈniːz/ (US), /ˌkæn.təˈniːz/ (UK)| n. adj.| 广东话；广东人；广东的|A Chinese dialect spoken in Guangdong and Guangxi provinces, Hong Kong, Macau, and by many overseas Chinese communities. Also refers to the people or culture of Guangdong province.  It is mutually unintelligible with Mandarin.|Example: She speaks fluent Cantonese. 她能说一口流利的广东话。|Example:  Cantonese cuisine is known for its dim sum. 广东菜以点心闻名。|近义词:Guangdong dialect, Yue Chinese|反义词: (No direct antonym for a language)|词性: noun, adjective
