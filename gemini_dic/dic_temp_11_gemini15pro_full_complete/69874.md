ferruminate:/ˈfɜːrmɪneɪt/ | v. | 沉思；反复思考 | To think deeply and at length; to ponder; to meditate.  |  He spent hours ferruminating on the meaning of life. 他花了几个小时思考人生的意义。 |  She continued to ferruminate on the problem, unable to sleep. 她继续思考这个问题，无法入睡。 |  ponder, meditate, contemplate, muse, ruminate |  dismiss, ignore, neglect | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ferruminatus, past participle of ferruminare (“to solder, cement, join”), from Latin ferrumen (“cement, solder”), from ferrum (“iron”).  The sense development is from the idea of joining or connecting thoughts.
