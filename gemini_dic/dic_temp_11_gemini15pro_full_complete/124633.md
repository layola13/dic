mouthed:/maʊðd/ | adj. | 嘴巴的，说出的，用嘴做的 | Having or showing a (specified kind of) mouth or mouth expression. | He gave a tight-mouthed smile. 他勉强地笑了笑。|Synonyms: oral, spoken, uttered | Antonyms: silent, unspoken | adjective

mouthed:/maʊðd/ | v. | 做口型，装腔作势地说 | To form words with the lips without speaking; To utter or pronounce in a specified way | She mouthed the words "I love you" across the crowded room.  她隔着拥挤的房间用口型说出“我爱你”。 | Synonyms: articulate, pronounce, enunciate | Antonyms: mumble, murmur | verb
