overcoaching:/ˌoʊvərˈkoʊtʃɪŋ/
n.|过度指导，过度训练|Excessive coaching or instruction, often to the detriment of the person being coached.|The team's performance suffered from overcoaching; the players were so stressed about following every instruction that they couldn't play naturally.  团队的表现受到了过度指导的影响；球员们过于强调遵循每一条指令，以至于无法自然地发挥。|micromanagement, overtraining, excessive instruction|undercoaching, neglect|noun
