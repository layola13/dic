effected:/ɪˈfɛktɪd/| adj. (obsolete, rare) |做作的；不自然的| artificial, contrived, or pretentious in manner |  She spoke in an effected tone, trying to sound sophisticated. 她说话的语气很做作，试图让自己听起来很老练。 | synonyms: artificial, contrived, pretentious, affected, mannered | antonyms: natural, genuine, sincere | adjective
