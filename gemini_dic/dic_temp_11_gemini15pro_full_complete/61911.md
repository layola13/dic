empyrean: /ˌɛmpaɪˈriːən/ /ˌɛmpaɪˈriən/ | n. adj.| 最高天，苍天；九霄；天空的，天上的 |The highest heaven or sphere, once thought to be composed of pure fire; the visible heavens; the sky. |  The poet described the eagle soaring into the empyrean. 诗人描绘了雄鹰翱翔于九霄云外的情景。| 近义词: celestial, heavenly, ethereal | 反义词: terrestrial, earthly | 词性: 名词，形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French empire or late Latin empyreus, from Greek empyros ‘fiery, in or from fire.’
