cowedly:/ˈkaʊɪdli/| adj.|胆怯的，懦弱的|Exhibiting or characterized by fear and timidity; lacking courage.|He cast a cowedly glance at his angry boss.|近义词：timid, fearful, craven, pusillanimous|反义词：brave, courageous, bold|词性:形容词
词源: From cow (verb) + -ly (adverbial suffix). The verb "cow" originally meant to subdue or intimidate, often by harsh treatment.  This meaning evolved from Old Norse *kúga "to oppress, tyrannize."
