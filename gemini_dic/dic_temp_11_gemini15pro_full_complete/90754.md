hummus:/ˈhʊməs/ (US), /ˈhʌməs/ (UK)| n. |鹰嘴豆泥|A Middle Eastern dip or spread made from cooked, mashed chickpeas or other beans, blended with tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, salt and garlic.|I love to eat hummus with pita bread. 我喜欢用皮塔饼蘸鹰嘴豆泥吃。|Synonyms: chickpea spread, dip|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a food item)|noun
