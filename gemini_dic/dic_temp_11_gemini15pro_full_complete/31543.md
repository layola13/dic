chartism:/ˈtʃɑːtɪzəm/ | n. |宪章运动|A working-class movement for political reform in Britain, from 1838 to 1848, seeking universal suffrage for men, equal electoral districts, vote by ballot, annually elected parliaments, payment of members of Parliament, and abolition of the property qualification for membership.|Chartism was a significant movement in 19th-century Britain. 宪章运动是19世纪英国的一场重要运动。| The Chartist movement ultimately failed to achieve its aims directly, but it laid the groundwork for later reforms. 宪章运动最终未能直接实现其目标，但它为后来的改革奠定了基础。|近义词: reform movement, political movement|反义词: conservatism, counter-revolution|词性: noun
