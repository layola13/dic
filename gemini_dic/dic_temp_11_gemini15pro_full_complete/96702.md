ineffably:/ɪnˈɛfəbli/
adjective
难以形容地；妙不可言地
too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words.
The beauty of the sunset was ineffably sublime.  日落的美丽难以形容地壮丽。
Synonyms:indescribably, inexpressibly, unutterably
Antonyms:describable, expressible, utterable
词性: 形容词
