enliven:/ɪnˈlaɪvən/| v.|使活跃，使生动，使振奋|To make something more lively, interesting, or exciting.|The music enlivened the party.  音乐使派对气氛活跃起来。|近义词：animate, invigorate, stimulate, cheer, brighten|反义词：dampen, deaden, dull, depress|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (make, cause to be) + live (alive) + -en (make, cause to be).  Ultimately from Latin vivere (to live).
