kleptomania: /ˌklɛptəˈmeɪniə/
noun
盗窃癖
An irresistible urge to steal, typically without any need or economic motive.
He was diagnosed with kleptomania after being caught stealing small items from stores. 他在从商店偷了一些小东西后被诊断为盗窃癖。
kleptomania: /ˌklɛptəˈmeɪniə/
n.
盗窃癖
A mental disorder characterized by a recurrent failure to resist impulses to steal objects not needed for personal use or for their monetary value.
She struggled with kleptomania for years, often stealing things she didn't even want. 她多年来一直在与盗窃癖作斗争，经常偷一些她甚至不想要的东西。
Synonyms: stealing compulsion, shoplifting addiction
Antonyms: honesty, integrity
