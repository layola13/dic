hymnology:/hɪmˈnɑːlədʒi/
n.
赞美诗学; 赞美诗集
The study of hymns, their origin, use, and meaning.  A collection of hymns.
He is a renowned scholar of hymnology. 他是一位著名的赞美诗学学者。
The church's hymnology reflected the changing times. 教堂的赞美诗集反映了时代的变化。
近义词: hymnody
反义词: (无明显的反义词)
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Ancient Greek  ὕμνος (humnos, “hymn, song of praise”) + -λογία (-logia, “study of”).
