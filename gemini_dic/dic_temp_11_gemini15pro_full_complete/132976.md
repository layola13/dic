nonencyclopaedic:/ˌnɒnɪnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdɪk/ | adj. | 非百科全书式的；不适合百科全书的 | Not suitable for or characteristic of an encyclopedia; not containing comprehensive information on a wide range of topics. | This blog post is interesting, but it's a bit too nonencyclopaedic for our website. 这篇博客很有趣，但对我们的网站来说有点太非百科全书式了。|  The style of writing was too informal and nonencyclopaedic. 这篇文章的写作风格太不正式，不适合百科全书。|  informal, unsuitable, unscholarly | encyclopaedic, scholarly, formal | adjective
