enthused: /ɪnˈθjuːzd/ | adj. | 热情的，充满激情的 | Filled with enthusiasm; eager. |  She was enthused by the prospect of traveling to Europe. 她对去欧洲旅行的前景充满热情。|  He was enthused about the new project. 他对这个新项目充满热情。 | 近义词: enthusiastic, excited, eager, passionate | 反义词: apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic | 词性:形容词
词源: From enthuse + -ed. Enthuse comes from the Greek "enthousiazein," meaning "to be inspired or possessed by a god," from "en" (in) + "theos" (god).
