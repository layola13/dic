nonsimulative:/ˌnɒnˈsɪmjʊlətɪv/
adjective
非模拟的
Not simulating; not imitative or based on simulation.  A nonsimulative training exercise focuses on real-world practice rather than simulated scenarios.
The team conducted a nonsimulative test of the new software, using real data and conditions.  团队使用真实数据和条件对新软件进行了非模拟测试。
Near Synonyms: actual, real, genuine, authentic, practical
Antonyms: simulated, imitative, artificial, synthetic
词性: adjective
