gynantherous:/ˌɡaɪnænˈθərəs/
| adj. |
|雌雄蕊合生的；具合生雌雄蕊的|
|Having stamens adnate to the gynoecium; having the stamens and pistils united.|
|Gynantherous flowers are characteristic of the milkweed family.|  雌雄蕊合生的花是萝藦科的特征。

|The gynantherous structure of the flower makes pollination more efficient.| 这种花的雌雄蕊合生结构使授粉更有效。
|近义词：synantherous|
