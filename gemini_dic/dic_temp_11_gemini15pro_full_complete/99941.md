interns:/ˈɪntɜːrnz/| n. |实习生|A student or recent graduate who works for a period of time in order to gain practical experience in a particular profession.|The company hired three interns for the summer.  公司夏天雇佣了三个实习生。|Synonyms: trainee, apprentice, probationer|Antonyms:  professional, expert|Noun


intern:/ɪnˈtɜːrn/| vt. |拘留；扣押|To confine (someone, such as a prisoner of war) within certain limits.|The government interned enemy aliens during the war.  战争期间，政府拘留了敌国的侨民。|Synonyms: confine, detain, imprison|Antonyms: release, liberate|Verb
