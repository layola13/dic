gymnopaedic: /ˌdʒɪmnəˈpiːdɪk/ | adj. n. | 裸体的，裸舞的；裸舞曲 | Of or relating to gymnopaedia, an ancient Spartan festival in which naked youths danced and performed athletic exercises. Can also refer to a musical piece evocative of these dances, particularly those composed by Erik Satie. |  The gymnopaedic movements of the dancers were mesmerizing. 舞蹈演员的裸体动作令人着迷。 | Synonyms: nude, naked, bare, unclothed | Antonyms: clothed, dressed, covered | Adjective/Noun

gymnopaedia: /ˌdʒɪmnəˈpiːdɪə/ | n. | 裸舞，裸舞节 | An ancient Spartan festival featuring athletic displays by naked youths. | The gymnopaedia was a significant cultural event in Sparta. 裸舞节是斯巴达的一项重要的文化活动。 | Synonyms: nude festival, naked dance | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could be a festival featuring clothed participants) | Noun
