fancymonger:/ˈfænsiˌmʌŋɡər/| n.| 幻想贩子；空想家；不切实际的人 |A person who deals in fantasies or unrealistic ideas; a dreamer or idealist.| He's a bit of a fancymonger, always coming up with these elaborate schemes that never come to fruition. 他有点像个幻想贩子，总是想出这些永远不会实现的复杂计划。 |Synonyms: dreamer, idealist, visionary, fantasist, romantic, Don Quixote |Antonyms: realist, pragmatist, cynic| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From fancy (n.) + monger (n.).  "Fancy" in this older sense means "fantasy, imagination, illusion." "Monger" refers to a dealer or trader, often with negative connotations, suggesting someone who promotes or spreads something undesirable.  Thus, a "fancymonger" is someone who "deals" in fantasies.
