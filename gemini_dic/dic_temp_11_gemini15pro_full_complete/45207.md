cullers:/ˈkʌlərz/
noun
剔除者，拣选者
People who select or sort things, removing those considered undesirable.
The cullers quickly sorted the good apples from the bad. 拣选者迅速地将好苹果和坏苹果分开。
selectors, sorters, pickers
rejecters, keepers (if considering the opposite action)
n.
