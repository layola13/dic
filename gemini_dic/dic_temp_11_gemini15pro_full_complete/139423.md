obsessionalism: /əbˈseʃənəlɪzəm/
|n.|强迫症|A state of being obsessed or preoccupied with something, often to an excessive or compulsive degree, sometimes associated with obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD).|Her obsessionalism with checking the locks made her late for work every day. 她对检查锁的强迫症让她每天上班迟到。|近义词: compulsiveness, OCD tendencies|反义词: calmness, tranquility|词性: 名词
