camisard:/ˌkæmɪˈzɑːr/ | n. | 卡米撒派教徒 | A member of a group of French Protestants in the Cévennes region who revolted against Louis XIV's revocation of the Edict of Nantes in the early 18th century.  |  The camisards fought bravely against the royal troops. 卡米撒派教徒勇敢地与皇家军队作战。 | synonyms: Huguenot, Protestant rebel | antonyms: Catholic, Royalist | noun

camisard:/ˌkæmɪˈzɑːr/ | adj. | 卡米撒派的 | Relating to the Camisards or their revolt. | The camisard uprising was a brutal and bloody conflict. 卡米撒派起义是一场残酷血腥的冲突。 | synonyms: Huguenot, Protestant rebel | antonyms: Catholic, Royalist | adjective
