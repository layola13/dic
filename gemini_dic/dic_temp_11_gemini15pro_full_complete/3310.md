affluence: /ˈæfluəns/ | n. | 富裕，富足 |  The state of having a great deal of money; wealth. |  They live in an area of great affluence.  他们住在非常富裕的地区。| synonyms: wealth, prosperity, opulence, richness | antonyms: poverty, destitution, indigence | noun

affluent: /ˈæfluənt/ | adj. | 富裕的，丰富的 | Having a great deal of money; wealthy.  |  Affluent families can afford to send their children to private schools.  富裕的家庭能够负担得起送孩子上私立学校的费用。 | synonyms: wealthy, rich, prosperous, opulent | antonyms: poor, impoverished, destitute | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *affluentia* "abundance, plenty," from *affluentem* (nominative *affluens*), present participle of *affluere* "to flow to," from *ad-* "to" + *fluere* "to flow."
