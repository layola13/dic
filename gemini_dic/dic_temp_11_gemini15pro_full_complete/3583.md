agatelike: /ˈæɡətˌlaɪk/
adjective
像玛瑙的；玛瑙状的
Resembling agate in appearance or quality.  Having the banded or variegated appearance of agate.
The polished stone had an agatelike pattern.  这块抛光的石头有像玛瑙一样的图案。
The mineral formed agatelike bands in the rock. 这种矿物在岩石中形成了玛瑙状的条带。
近义词: agateous, agatine
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "uniform" or "monochromatic" for contrasting appearances)
词性: adjective
