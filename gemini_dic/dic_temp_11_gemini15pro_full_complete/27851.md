canonization:/ˌkænənɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |册封圣徒；认可；推崇|The official act of the Church in declaring a deceased person a saint. The process of recognizing someone or something as important or worthy of respect. |例句：The canonization of Joan of Arc was a long and complex process.  圣女贞德的册封是一个漫长而复杂的过程。

例句：The canonization of Shakespeare as the greatest English writer is almost universal.  莎士比亚被尊为最伟大的英国作家，这几乎是普遍的共识。|近义词：deification, glorification, sanctification, exaltation |反义词：condemnation, denunciation |词性:noun
