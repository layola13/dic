breechblock:/ˈbriːtʃblɒk/ (UK) /ˈbriːtʃblɑːk/ (US)| n.|炮闩；枪机|The part of a gun that closes the breech (the rear end of the barrel) against the force of the explosion.|The soldier checked the breechblock of his rifle before firing.|Synonyms: breech mechanism, breech closure|Antonyms: muzzle (opposite end of the barrel)|noun
