fenestral:/fɛˈnɛstrəl/
adjective

The fenestral arrangements allowed ample sunlight into the room.  房间的窗户布置让充足的阳光照射进来。
The butterfly's wings had fenestral markings. 蝴蝶的翅膀上有像窗户一样的斑纹。

近义词: windowed
反义词: windowless
词性: adjective
