intransigent:/ɪnˈtrænsɪdʒənt/ | adj. | 不妥协的，不让步的 | Unwilling or refusing to change one's views or to agree about something. |  The intransigent leader refused to negotiate with the opposition.  这位不妥协的领导人拒绝与反对派谈判。 |  The government remained intransigent on the issue of tax cuts. 政府在减税问题上仍然不让步。 | Synonyms: uncompromising, inflexible, unyielding, obstinate, stubborn | Antonyms: compromising, flexible, yielding, accommodating, cooperative | Adjective
