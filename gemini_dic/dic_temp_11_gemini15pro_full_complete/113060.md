lumpiest:/ˈlʌmpɪɪst/
adjective
最块状的；最粗糙的
superlative adjective of lumpy: having lumps or bumps
The lumpiest mashed potatoes I've ever seen. 我见过最块状的土豆泥。
The road was the lumpiest I'd ever driven on.  这条路是我开过最颠簸的。
近义词:bumpy, uneven, knobbly
反义词:smooth, even, flat
词性:adjective
