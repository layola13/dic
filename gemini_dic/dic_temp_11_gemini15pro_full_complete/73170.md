forbiddenly:/fərˈbɪdnli/
adverb
禁止地，严禁地
in a forbidden manner; in a way that is not allowed.
She smiled forbiddenly. 她露出了禁忌的微笑。
Near synonyms: illicitly, wrongly, sinfully
Antonyms: permissibly, lawfully, acceptably
词性: 副词
