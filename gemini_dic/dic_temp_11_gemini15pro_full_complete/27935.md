cantillate:/ˈkæntɪleɪt/ | v. |吟诵，咏唱 |to chant or intone a passage of religious text, typically in a Gregorian style. | The priest cantillated the prayers. 神父吟诵着祈祷文。| Example: The cantor cantillated the verses beautifully.  领唱者优美地吟诵着诗句。 | Synonyms: chant, intone, recite, sing | Antonyms: whisper, mumble | verb

cantillation:/ˌkæntɪˈleɪʃn/ | n. |吟诵，咏唱 |the act of chanting or intoning a passage of religious text, typically in a Gregorian style. | The cantillation of the psalms filled the cathedral. 圣诗的吟诵充满了大教堂。| Example: The rabbi's cantillation was mesmerizing. 拉比的吟诵令人着迷。 | Synonyms: chanting, intonation, recitation | Antonyms: whispering, murmuring | noun
