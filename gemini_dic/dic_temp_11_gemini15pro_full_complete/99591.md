interinvolved:/ˌɪntərɪnˈvɒlvd/ | adj. |相互关联的，相互牵连的 | Mutually involved or implicated; intertwined.  Describes a situation where multiple entities or factors are intricately connected and influence one another. | The economies of the two countries are now deeply interinvolved. 这两个国家的经济现在紧密相连。| The various departments within the organization are interinvolved in a complex web of procedures. 该组织内部的各个部门相互关联，形成一个复杂程序网络。 |  interconnected, intertwined, interrelated, interdependent | independent, separate, detached | adjective
