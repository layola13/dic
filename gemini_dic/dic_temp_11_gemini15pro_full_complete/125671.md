munchausenism:/mʌnˈtʃaʊzənɪzəm/
n.
孟乔森综合征，诈病
A factitious disorder in which a person repeatedly and deliberately acts as if he or she has a physical or mental illness when he or she is not really sick.
Munchausen syndrome is a psychological disorder where someone pretends to be ill or deliberately produces symptoms of illness in themselves. 孟乔森综合征是一种心理障碍，患者会假装生病或故意在自己身上制造疾病症状。
He was diagnosed with Munchausenism after doctors discovered he was self-inflicting injuries.  医生发现他是自残后，诊断他患有孟乔森综合征。
近义词:factitious disorder, fabricated illness, pathomimia
反义词:genuine illness
词性:noun


munchausen syndrome by proxy:/mʌnˈtʃaʊzən ˈsɪndroʊm baɪ ˈprɒksi/
n.
代理型孟乔森综合征
A factitious disorder in which a person acts as if an individual he or she is caring for has a physical or mental illness when the person is not really sick.
Munchausen syndrome by proxy is a form of child abuse. 代理型孟乔森综合征是一种虐待儿童的形式。
The mother was suspected of Munchausen syndrome by proxy when her child's unexplained illnesses stopped whenever the child was away from her. 当孩子不明原因的疾病只要孩子离开母亲就会停止时，母亲被怀疑患有代理型孟乔森综合征。
近义词:factitious disorder by proxy, medical child abuse
反义词:genuine caregiving
词性:noun
