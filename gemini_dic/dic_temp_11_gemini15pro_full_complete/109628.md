letterpress:/ˈletərˌpres/| n. adj.|凸版印刷（术）；凸版印刷品|A printing method in which raised type is inked and pressed against paper to create an impression.  Also refers to the printed material produced by this method.|例句：The wedding invitations were beautifully printed using letterpress.  婚礼请柬用凸版印刷精美地印制出来。

例句：She collects antique letterpress equipment. 她收藏古董凸版印刷设备。|近义词：relief printing, typography, printing|反义词：offset printing, digital printing|词性:名词, 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "letter" (referring to the individual characters) + "press" (the machine used for printing).
