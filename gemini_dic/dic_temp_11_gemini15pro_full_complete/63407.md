entrain:/ɪnˈtreɪn/ | v. |  带动，夹带，使同步，(生理节奏)与…同步 | To cause something to be carried along in a current of air, water, etc.  To synchronize the rhythm of a physiological process with an external rhythm. | The logs were entrained in the river's current. 原木被卷入河水的水流中。 The circadian rhythm is entrained by the light-dark cycle. 昼夜节律与光暗循环同步。 |  近义词: synchronize, carry, incorporate | 反义词: desynchronize | 词性: 动词

entrainment:/ɪnˈtreɪnmənt/ | n. | 夹带，带动，同步 | The process of entraining something. | The entrainment of air bubbles in the concrete weakened its structure. 混凝土中夹带的气泡削弱了其结构。| 近义词: synchronization, incorporation | 反义词: desynchronization | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *entraîner*, from en- (“in, into”) + traîner (“to drag, to pull”).  Ultimately from Latin *trahere* ("to draw, pull").
