courteous:/ˈkɜːrtiəs/ (US) /ˈkɜːtɪəs/ (UK)| adj.|有礼貌的，谦恭的|Polite, respectful, or considerate in manner.|例句：He was courteous and attentive to her needs. 他彬彬有礼，对她关怀备至。|近义词：polite, respectful, gracious, civil, well-mannered|反义词：rude, impolite, discourteous, disrespectful|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *corteis* "courtly, elegant," from Vulgar Latin *(cōrtēnsis)* "pertaining to the court," from Latin *(cohors, cohortis)* "enclosed area, courtyard, retinue."  The evolution of meaning reflects the refined manners expected in a royal court.
