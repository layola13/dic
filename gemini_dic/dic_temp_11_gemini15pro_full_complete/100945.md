inundate:/ˈɪnʌndeɪt/ | v. | 淹没；泛滥；大量涌入；使应接不暇 | To overwhelm (someone) with things or people to be dealt with. To flood. |  We were inundated with requests for help after the hurricane.  飓风过后，我们收到了大量的求助请求。| overwhelm, flood, deluge, swamp | drain, dry, empty | verb
