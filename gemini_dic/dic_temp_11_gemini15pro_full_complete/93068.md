idolize:/ˈaɪdəˌlaɪz/ | vt. | 崇拜；把…当作偶像 | To admire, revere, or love someone or something to an excessive degree. | She idolizes her older brother. 她崇拜她的哥哥。|  He idolized the famous scientist. 他把那位著名的科学家当作偶像。 | 近义词: worship, adore, revere | 反义词: despise, loathe | verb
