moroccos:/məˈrɒkəʊz/
n.
摩洛哥皮革; (尤指山羊皮)摩洛哥(Morocco)制造的皮革制品
A fine, flexible leather made from goatskin, originally tanned with sumac, and used especially for bookbindings, upholstery, and shoes.  It was traditionally produced in Morocco, hence the name.
These shoes are made of fine moroccos. 这些鞋是由优质的摩洛哥皮革制成的。
近义词:leather, goatskin
反义词: (无明确的反义词，可考虑材质上不同的例如：synthetic leather 人造革)
词性:名词
