opinionated: /əˈpɪnjəneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 固执己见的；武断的 | Holding stubbornly to one's own opinions, often without considering the views of others. |  She's very opinionated and never hesitates to share her views, whether you want to hear them or not. 她很固执己见，从不犹豫分享她的观点，无论你想不想听。

He's so opinionated that it's impossible to have a reasonable discussion with him. 他太固执己见了，根本不可能和他进行理性的讨论。 | 近义词: dogmatic, inflexible, stubborn, biased | 反义词: open-minded, receptive, flexible | 词性: 形容词
