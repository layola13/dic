goitrous:/ˈɡɔɪtrəs/ | adj. | 甲状腺肿的；患甲状腺肿的 | Affected with or exhibiting goiter; swollen. |  The doctor examined the patient's goitrous neck. 医生检查了病人肿大的脖子。 |  The goitrous swelling was clearly visible. 甲状腺肿胀清晰可见。 | 近义词: swollen, enlarged | 反义词: normal, healthy | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *gutturosus, from guttur (“throat”) + -osus.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰewd- (“to pour, libate”).
