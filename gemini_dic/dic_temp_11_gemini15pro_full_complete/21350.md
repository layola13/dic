blooped:/bluːpt/
verb
失误；搞砸
to make a mistake or blunder.
He blooped the easy catch and cost his team the game. 他失误了简单的接球，使他的球队输掉了比赛。
近义词：blundered, messed up, botched, flubbed
反义词：succeeded, aced, nailed
词性：v.


bloop:/bluːp/
noun
失误；愚蠢的错误
a mistake or blunder.
That bloop cost us the game. 那次失误让我们输掉了比赛。
近义词：blunder, mistake, error, gaffe
反义词：success, triumph
词性：n.
