antigenic:/ˌæntiˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. | 抗原的；产生抗体的 |  Capable of stimulating the production of antibodies or interacting with an antibody. |  The virus has undergone antigenic drift, making previous vaccines less effective. 病毒经历了抗原漂移，使得以前的疫苗效果降低。| synonyms: immunogenic, immunizing | antonyms: non-antigenic, non-immunogenic | adjective

antigen:/ˈæn.tɪ.dʒən/ | n. | 抗原 | A toxin or other foreign substance which induces an immune response in the body, especially the production of antibodies. | The body produces antibodies to fight off antigens. 身体产生抗体来对抗抗原。| synonyms: immunogen | antonyms: antibody | noun
