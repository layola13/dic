kleptomania:/ˌklɛptəˈmeɪniə/| n. |盗窃癖，偷窃狂|A mental disorder marked by a compulsive desire to steal, typically without any economic motive.|She was diagnosed with kleptomania after repeatedly stealing items she didn't need.|Near synonyms: stealing compulsion, shoplifting addiction|Antonym: honesty|Noun
