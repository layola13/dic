hyphenated:/ˈhaɪfəneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 带连字符的，用连字符连接的 |  Joined by a hyphen.  Formed with a hyphen. Characterized by the presence of hyphens. |  The compound word "self-esteem" is hyphenated.  复合词“self-esteem”带有连字符。|  connected, joined, linked | separated, detached, unlinked | adjective

Etymology:
From hyphen + -ate (suffix forming verbs meaning "to provide with" or "to make into") + -ed (suffix forming adjectives from verbs).  The word "hyphen" itself comes from the Greek "huphen" meaning "under one," referring to words joined together.
