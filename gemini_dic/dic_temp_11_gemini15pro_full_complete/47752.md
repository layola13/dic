decalling:/diːˈkælɪŋ/
n.
移印；贴花；去花纸
The process of transferring a printed image from a carrier film onto a surface.
The company specializes in decaling ceramic tiles. 这家公司专门从事瓷砖贴花。
transfer printing, applying decals
removal of decals
noun

decall:/diːˈkæl/
n.
贴花；移印花纸
A design or image that is transferred from a special paper onto another surface, usually by rubbing.
He put a decal of a sports car on his window. 他在窗户上贴了一张跑车的贴花。
sticker, transfer, label
removal
noun

decall:/diːˈkæl/
v.
用贴花装饰；移印
To decorate something with a decal.
They decalled the car with racing stripes. 他们在车上贴了赛车条纹。
decorate with decals, transfer a design
remove a decal
verb
