glomerule:/ˈɡlɒmərjʊl (British), ɡləˈmɛrʊləs (American)| n. |肾小球|A cluster of capillaries around the end of a kidney tubule, where waste products are filtered from the blood.|The glomerule filters waste products from the blood. 肾小球过滤血液中的废物。|The glomeruli are essential for proper kidney function. 肾小球对肾脏的正常功能至关重要。|近义词:renal corpuscle|反义词:None|词性:Noun
