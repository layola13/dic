divisibilities:/dɪˌvɪzɪˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |可除性；整除性|The property of being divisible by a number.  The plural of divisibility.  It refers to the various ways in which a number can be divided evenly by other numbers.|The divisibility rules help us quickly determine if a number is divisible by another. 可除性规则帮助我们快速确定一个数能否被另一个数整除。|Synonym: factorization properties|Antonym: indivisibility|Noun
