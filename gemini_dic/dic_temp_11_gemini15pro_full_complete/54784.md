dispensably:/dɪˈspɛnsəbli/
adv.
可有可无地，不必要地
in a way that is not essential; in a way that one can do without
She brought a lot of dispensably luxurious items on the trip. 她旅行带了很多不必要的奢侈品。
近义词: unnecessarily, nonessentially
反义词: essentially, necessarily
词性: 副词

dispensable:/dɪˈspɛnsəbəl/
adj.
可有可无的，不必要的
not necessary
Many people consider a landline phone dispensable these days. 很多人认为如今固定电话可有可无。
近义词: unnecessary, nonessential
反义词: essential, necessary, indispensable
词性: 形容词


词源:
From Late Latin dispensabilis "that can be dispensed with," from Latin dispensare "to dispense" (see dispense). The suffix -able is from Latin -abilis.
