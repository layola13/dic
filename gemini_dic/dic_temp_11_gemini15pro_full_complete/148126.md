overseeding:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːdɪŋ/
n.
播撒覆盖
The process of sowing seed over an existing lawn or pasture to improve its density, color, or disease resistance.
We did some overseeding in the spring to thicken up the lawn. 我们在春天进行了一些覆播来使草坪更茂密。

近义词:reseeding, topsowing
反义词:N/A
词性:noun
