nonpneumatically:/ˌnɒn.njuːˈmæt.ɪ.kəl.i/
adjective

not using air pressure; solid.
例句：Nonpneumatically tired wheels are more resistant to punctures. 非充气轮胎更耐刺穿。

例句：The vehicle utilizes nonpneumatically designed tires for enhanced durability.  这辆车采用非充气设计的轮胎以增强耐用性。

近义词：solid, airless
反义词：pneumatic, inflated
词性: adjective
