jamboree:/ˈdʒæmbəriː/| n. |欢庆活动，盛大集会|A large celebration or party, especially one involving a lot of people.|例句：The annual scout jamboree attracts thousands of young people.  年度童子军大会吸引了成千上万的年轻人。

例句：The town held a jamboree to celebrate its bicentennial.  该镇举行了一场盛大的活动来庆祝其二百周年纪念。|近义词：celebration, festival, fiesta, gathering, rally|反义词：wake, funeral|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain, possibly from a fanciful blend of *jam* (meaning a crowded gathering) and *boree* (a boisterous party), influenced by similar-sounding words like *jabber* and *tambour*. First recorded in the late 19th century.
