artilize:/ˈɑːrtɪlaɪz/
verb
使艺术化；赋予艺术性
To give something artistic qualities; to make something into a work of art.
They artilized the everyday objects by painting them in bright colors. 他们通过用鲜艳的颜色绘制日常物品，使它们艺术化。
The designer artilized the simple dress with intricate embroidery.  设计师用复杂的刺绣将这条简单的连衣裙艺术化。
Synonyms: aestheticize, stylize, embellish
Antonyms: simplify, de-emphasize
词性: 动词
