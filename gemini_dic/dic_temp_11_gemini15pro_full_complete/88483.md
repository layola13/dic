historically:/hɪˈstɔːrɪkli/
adverb
历史上地，从历史角度来看
From a historical point of view; in terms of history.
Historically, this area has been prone to flooding. 从历史上看，这个地区很容易发生洪水。
This building has historically been used as a school. 从历史上看，这座建筑一直被用作学校。
Synonyms: traditionally, formerly, previously, in the past
Antonyms: currently, presently, contemporarily
词性: 副词
