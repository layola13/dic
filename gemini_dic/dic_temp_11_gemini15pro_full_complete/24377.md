brinier:/ˈbraɪniər/| adj.|更咸的，盐分更高的|Containing a higher concentration of salt.  More saline.|The Dead Sea is brinier than the Mediterranean Sea. 死海比地中海更咸。|The soup was too briny, so I added some water.  这汤太咸了，所以我加了些水。|近义词：saltier, more saline|反义词：less salty, fresher|词性:形容词
词源: From brine (salty water) + -ier (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *brunjo- (a spring, well).
