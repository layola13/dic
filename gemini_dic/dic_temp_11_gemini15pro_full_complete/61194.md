elutriate:/ɪˈluːtriˌeɪt/| v.|淘洗，洗净；漂洗；分离|To separate lighter particles from heavier ones by washing or decanting.|The chemist elutriated the precipitate to remove impurities. 化学家淘洗沉淀物以去除杂质。|Synonyms: wash, rinse, separate, purify|Antonyms: combine, mix, contaminate|verb

elutriation:/ɪˌluːtriˈeɪʃən/| n.|淘洗，洗净；漂洗；分离|The process of separating lighter particles from heavier ones by washing or decanting.|The elutriation of the sample removed unwanted debris. 样品的淘洗去除了不需要的碎片。|Synonyms: washing, rinsing, separation, purification|Antonyms: combination, mixture, contamination|noun
