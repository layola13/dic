commute: /kəˈmjuːt/ (US), /kɒˈmjuːt/ (UK)
| v. | 通勤；减刑；交换；改变 | to travel regularly between work and home | She commutes to London every day. 她每天通勤到伦敦。 | synonyms: travel, journey | antonyms: stay, reside
| n. | 通勤 | the journey between work and home | My commute takes about an hour. 我的通勤时间大约一个小时。 | synonyms: journey, travel | antonyms: stay, residence
| adj. | commuted | 减刑的；交换的 | His death sentence was commuted to life imprisonment. 他的死刑被减为无期徒刑。 | synonyms: reduced, exchanged | antonyms: increased, maintained
