overexpands:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspændz/
verb
过度扩张；过度膨胀
to expand too much
The company overexpanded during the boom years and subsequently went bankrupt.  这家公司在繁荣时期过度扩张，随后破产了。
The balloon overexpanded and burst. 气球过度膨胀爆炸了。
Synonyms: overextend, overreach
Antonyms: contract, shrink
词性: 动词
