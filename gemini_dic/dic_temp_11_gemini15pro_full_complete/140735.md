omnipowerful:/ˌɑːmˈnɪˈpaʊərfl/
adj.
无所不能的，全能的
Having unlimited or very great power.
God is often described as omnipowerful. 上帝常被描述为无所不能的。
The omnipowerful CEO controlled every aspect of the company.  这位权力无限的首席执行官控制着公司的方方面面。

近义词: almighty, all-powerful, supreme
反义词: powerless, impotent, weak
词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin omni- "all" + powerful.
