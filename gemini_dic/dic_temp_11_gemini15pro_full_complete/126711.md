/ˈneɪmər/ | n. |命名者; 题名者; 提名者| A person who gives a name to something or someone. |  John was the namer of the newly discovered species of beetle.  约翰是新发现甲虫物种的命名者。| Synonyms: name-giver, baptizer, nominator | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider anonymizer in some contexts) | noun


/ˈneɪmər/ | n. |姓名; 名称| Name itself. (Less common usage) |  What is the namer of this street? 这条街的名称是什么? | Synonyms: name, appellation, designation | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | noun


Etymology: From the verb "name" + "-er" (suffix forming agent nouns).  Ultimately from Old English "nama" and Proto-Germanic "*namō".
