depersonalized:/diːˈpɜːrsənəlaɪzd/ /diːˈpɝːsənəˌlaɪzd/ | adj. | 非个人化的，失去人情味的，客观的 |  Divested of personal character or individuality; made impersonal or objective.  Characterized by a loss of identity or feeling of detachment from oneself. | The hospital environment felt cold and depersonalized.  医院的环境让人感觉冰冷且没有人情味。| The artist's style is so depersonalized that it's hard to connect with the work emotionally.  这位艺术家的风格如此客观，以至于很难与作品产生情感上的共鸣。 | 近义词: impersonal, objective, detached, anonymous | 反义词: personal, subjective, intimate | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + personal + -ize (suffix meaning "to make or become") + -ed (past participle suffix).  The word "personal" ultimately derives from the Latin "persona," meaning "mask" or "character."
