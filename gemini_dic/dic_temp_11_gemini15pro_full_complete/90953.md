hurons:/ˈhjʊərɒnz/ /ˈhʊrɑːnz/ | n. |休伦人|A member of a North American Indian people formerly living around Lake Huron and Georgian Bay.  They were one of the Iroquoian-speaking peoples.  |例句：The Hurons were skilled agriculturalists. 休伦人是熟练的农业家。

例句：The French established trading posts in Huron territory. 法国人在休伦领地建立了贸易站。|近义词：Wyandot (This refers to the descendants of the Huron who relocated.)|反义词: N/A (There's no direct opposite for a specific tribe.)|词性:noun
