figurative: /ˈfɪɡjərətɪv/ (US), /ˈfɪɡərətɪv/ (UK)| adj.| 比喻的，象征的|Departing from literal use of words; metaphorical.  Representing by a figure or resemblance; typical, emblematic.|例句：The expression "raining cats and dogs" is figurative. “倾盆大雨”这个表达是比喻性的。|近义词：metaphorical, symbolic, emblematic|反义词：literal, concrete|词性：形容词

figurative: /ˈfɪɡjərətɪv/ (US), /ˈfɪɡərətɪv/ (UK)| n.|形象化|The use of figures of speech.|例句：The poem is rich in figurative language. 这首诗充满了比喻性的语言。|近义词：imagery, symbolism|反义词：literalness|词性：名词 (罕见)
