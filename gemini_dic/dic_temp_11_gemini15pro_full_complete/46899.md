darker:/ˈdɑːrkər (US) / ˈdɑːkə(r) (UK)| adj.|更暗的，更黑暗的；更深色的|Comparative form of dark.  Means more dark than something else.  Can refer to literal darkness (lack of light), or to colors that are deep or closer to black. Can also have figurative meanings, such as more gloomy, sinister, or secret.|The room grew darker as the sun set. 随着太阳落山，房间变得更暗了。|near synonyms: dimmer, gloomier, shadier|antonyms: lighter, brighter|adjective
