itchinesses:/ˈɪtʃinəsɪz/
noun
痒，发痒，瘙痒（itchiness 的复数形式）
The plural form of "itchiness," referring to multiple instances or areas of itching sensation.
The itchinesses on my arms and legs were driving me crazy. 我胳膊和腿上的瘙痒快把我逼疯了。
The various itchinesses he experienced suggested an allergic reaction. 他经历的各种瘙痒表明是过敏反应。
synonyms: itches, pruritus
antonyms: comfort, numbness
词性: 名词

itchiness:/ˈɪtʃinəs/
noun
痒，发痒，瘙痒
The sensation of wanting to scratch.
The itchiness of the mosquito bite was unbearable.蚊子叮咬的痒让人难以忍受。
He complained of itchiness all over his body. 他抱怨全身发痒。
synonyms: itching, pruritus
antonyms: comfort, numbness
词性: 名词

itch:/ɪtʃ/
noun
痒；渴望；疥癣
An irritating sensation that causes a desire to scratch; a restless desire or craving.
I have an itch on my back. 我背上很痒。
He has an itch to travel the world. 他渴望环游世界。


verb
发痒；渴望
To have or cause an irritating sensation that prompts scratching; to have a restless desire or craving.
My mosquito bites itch terribly. 我被蚊子咬的地方痒得厉害。
She's itching to tell everyone the good news. 她渴望告诉所有人这个好消息。


adjective
渴望的，急切的
Having or causing a restless desire or craving. (Informal)
She's itch to start her new job. 她渴望开始她的新工作。(非正式用法)
He's got itchy feet and wants to move abroad.  他渴望搬到国外去。(非正式用法, itchy feet 指的是渴望旅行或搬迁)
synonyms: (n.) irritation, yearning; (v.) tickle, crave; (adj.) restless, impatient
antonyms: (n.) comfort, contentment; (v.) soothe, satisfy; (adj.) content, satisfied
词性: 名词，动词，形容词
