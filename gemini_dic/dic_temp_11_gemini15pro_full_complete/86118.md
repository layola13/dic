heartburned:/ˈhɑːrtbɜːrnd/| adj. n.|胃灼热,烧心;妒忌,眼红|A burning sensation in the chest, caused by acid refluxing from the stomach into the esophagus. Also, a feeling of jealousy or resentment.|例句：I ate too much spicy food and now I'm heartburned. 我吃了太多辛辣的食物，现在胃灼热。

例句：He felt heartburned when he saw his colleague get promoted.  看到同事升职，他感到十分妒忌。|近义词：jealous, envious, resentful (for the figurative meaning); acid indigestion, pyrosis (for the literal meaning)|反义词: contented, satisfied (for the figurative meaning)|词性:形容词, 名词
