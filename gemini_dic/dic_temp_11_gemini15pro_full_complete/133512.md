nonfestive:/ˌnɒnˈfɛstɪv/ | adj. | 非节日的，不喜庆的 | Not related to or suitable for a festival or celebration; lacking a festive spirit. | The decorations were surprisingly nonfestive for a Christmas party.  圣诞派对的装饰出奇地没有节日气氛。|  The library had a nonfestive atmosphere during the exam period. 考试期间图书馆的气氛一点也不喜庆。| 近义词: somber, subdued, uncelebratory | 反义词: festive, celebratory, joyous | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "festive" (from Latin "festivus," meaning "festive, joyous," related to "festum," meaning "festival").
