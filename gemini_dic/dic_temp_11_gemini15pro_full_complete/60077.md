eejits:/ˈiːdʒɪts/
noun
笨蛋，傻瓜
Informal, often humorous term for idiots or fools.
"Those eejits left the door unlocked again!" 那些笨蛋又忘了锁门！
Synonyms: idiots, fools, morons, imbeciles, nitwits
Antonyms: geniuses, intellectuals, smart people
