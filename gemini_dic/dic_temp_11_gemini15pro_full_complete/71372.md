flagelliform:/fləˈdʒɛlɪfɔːrm/
| adj. |鞭状的，鞭形的|Resembling a whip or flagellum; long, slender, and tapering. |The plant has long, flagelliform leaves.  这种植物有长长的鞭状叶子。|近义词：whiplike, whip-shaped, lash-like|反义词: broad, wide|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *flagellum* ("whip, scourge") + *-formis* ("-shaped").
