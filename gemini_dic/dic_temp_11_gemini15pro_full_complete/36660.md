collenchyma: /kəˈlɛŋkɪmə/ /kəˈlɛŋkaɪmə/ | n. | 厚角组织 | A plant tissue that provides support and flexibility, composed of elongated cells with unevenly thickened walls, especially in young stems and leaves. |  Collenchyma cells help support the plant without restraining growth. 厚角组织细胞帮助支撑植物而不抑制生长。|  The strings of celery are composed primarily of collenchyma. 芹菜的茎主要由厚角组织组成。 |  近义词: supporting tissue, strengthening tissue | 反义词: sclerenchyma | 词性: noun

collenchymatous: /kəˌlɛŋˈkaɪmətəs/ /kəˌlɛŋˈkɪmətəs/ | adj. | 厚角组织的 | Relating to or consisting of collenchyma. |  The stems of many herbaceous plants have a collenchymatous layer beneath the epidermis. 许多草本植物的茎在表皮下有一层厚角组织。 |  The young shoots exhibited collenchymatous characteristics. 幼芽表现出厚角组织的特征。 | 近义词: none | 反义词: none | 词性: adjective
