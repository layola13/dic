malacissation:/ˌmæləsɪˈseɪʃən/
n.
软化; 变软; 柔化; (尤指食物的)软化烹调
softening; the process of making something soft, especially by cooking.
The malacissation of the carrots made them easier to eat for the baby.  胡萝卜软化后婴儿更容易食用。
The chef achieved the perfect malacissation of the meat through slow braising.  厨师通过慢炖使肉达到了完美的软化程度。
近义词: softening, tenderizing
反义词: hardening, toughening
词性: 名词
