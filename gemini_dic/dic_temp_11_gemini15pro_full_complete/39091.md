confraternities:/ˌkɒnfrəˈtɜːnɪtiz/
n.
宗教团体，兄弟会，联谊会
A confraternity is a lay association of men and women in the Catholic Church or other Christian denominations, gathered for pious practice or to work for some charitable purpose.
The confraternity met every month to pray the rosary.  这个宗教团体每个月都聚在一起念玫瑰经。
The confraternity raised funds for the local orphanage.  这个兄弟会为当地的孤儿院筹集资金。
synonyms: brotherhood, fellowship, association, society, league, guild, order
antonyms:  isolation, solitude
