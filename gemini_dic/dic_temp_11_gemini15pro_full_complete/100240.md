interrupt's:/ˌɪntəˈrʌpts/ | n. | 中断，打断 |  The possessive form of "interrupt," meaning something belonging to or associated with an interruption.  It's less common than the verb form. |  The interrupt's timing was unfortunate.  这个中断的时机很不巧。 | Near synonyms: interruption's, disruption's | Antonyms: continuation's |  noun
