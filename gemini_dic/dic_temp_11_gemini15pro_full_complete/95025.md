词源:

"inaccessible" comes from the Latin "in-" (not) + "accessibilis" (approachable), which is derived from "accedere" (to approach).  "Inaccessiblenesses" is formed by adding the suffix "-ness" to "inaccessible" to create a noun indicating the state or quality of being inaccessible.
