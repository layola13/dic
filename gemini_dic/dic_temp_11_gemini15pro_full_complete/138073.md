normalizable:/ˈnɔːməlaɪzəbl/| adj.|可归一化的|Able to be normalized; capable of being made normal or brought into conformity with a standard.  This often refers to mathematical functions or vectors that can be scaled so their magnitude is equal to one.|The wave function is normalizable, ensuring a valid probability distribution.  该波函数是可归一化的，确保了有效的概率分布。|近义词：standardizable, regularizable|反义词: non-normalizable|词性:adjective
