instigatingly:/ˈɪnstɪɡeɪtɪŋli/
adverb
煽动地；教唆地；怂恿地
In a way that incites or urges someone to do something, especially something bad.
His words were delivered instigatingly, clearly aimed at provoking a fight.  他煽动性地说了那些话，显然是为了挑起争端。
She smiled instigatingly, urging him to join the prank. 她怂恿地笑了笑，催促他加入恶作剧。
近义词:provocatively, incitingly, urgingly
反义词:dissuasively, calmingly
词性:adverb

词源: From the verb "instigate" + "-ing" (present participle) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Instigate" comes from the Latin "instigare," meaning "to goad, urge on."
