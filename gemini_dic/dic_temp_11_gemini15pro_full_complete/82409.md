grudge:/ɡrʌdʒ/ | n. vt. |怨恨，妒忌；吝惜，勉强给| a feeling of resentment or ill will because of a past insult or injury | She still holds a grudge against him for breaking up with her. 她仍然对他和她分手怀恨在心。| synonyms: resentment, animosity, malice, rancor | antonyms: forgiveness, benevolence, goodwill | noun
grudge:/ɡrʌdʒ/ | vt. |怨恨，妒忌；吝惜，勉强给| be unwilling to give or allow (something), begrudge | I don't grudge her success. 我并不妒忌她的成功。| synonyms: begrudge, resent, envy | antonyms: grant, bestow, give willingly | verb
