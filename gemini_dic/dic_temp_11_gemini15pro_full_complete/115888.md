marmalade:/ˈmɑːməleɪd/ (US), /ˈmɑːməleɪd/ (UK)| n.|橘子酱，果酱|A preserve made from citrus fruit, especially bitter oranges, prepared by boiling the pulp with sugar.|I like marmalade on my toast in the morning. 我早上喜欢在吐司上涂橘子酱。|Synonyms: jam, preserve, conserve|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "savory spread")|Noun
