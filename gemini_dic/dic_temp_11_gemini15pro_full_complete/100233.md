interruling:/ˌɪntərˈruːlɪŋ/
adjective
相互裁决的，互相干扰的
Making, or having the nature of, a decision between or among other rulings.  A ruling that interrupts, interferes with, or influences another ruling.
The interruling decisions of the various courts created confusion. 各个法院相互矛盾的裁决造成了混乱。
The interruling influence of the two powerful factions stalled progress. 两个强大派系的相互干扰影响阻碍了进展。
近义词:interfering, interacting, conflicting
反义词:independent, separate, distinct
词性:adjective

词源:
From inter- + ruling.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between, among, mutually, reciprocally." "Ruling" comes from the verb "rule," ultimately from Latin *regula, "straight stick, ruler."
