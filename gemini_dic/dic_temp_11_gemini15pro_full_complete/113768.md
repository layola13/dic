macroprism:/ˈmækroʊprɪzəm/
noun|宏观棱镜; 宏观视角|A theoretical lens or perspective through which to view large-scale phenomena, encompassing multiple facets or dimensions.  It suggests a comprehensive and nuanced understanding of complex systems or situations.|Looking at the global economy through a macroprism reveals the interconnectedness of various markets.|Synonyms: macroscopic view, broad perspective, holistic approach|Antonyms: microcosm, narrow view, limited perspective|n.
