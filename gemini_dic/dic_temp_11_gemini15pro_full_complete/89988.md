horsily:/ˈhɔrsɪli/| adv.|像马一样地；粗鲁地；笨拙地|In a manner resembling a horse; in a coarse or clumsy way. |He sat horsily in the armchair, sprawling his legs. 他笨拙地坐在扶手椅上，张开双腿。|The old farmer laughed horsily. 老农夫粗声粗气地笑了。|近义词:clumsily, awkwardly, coarsely, roughly|反义词:gracefully, elegantly, refinedly|词性:副词
词源: From horse + -ly.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hursa-.
