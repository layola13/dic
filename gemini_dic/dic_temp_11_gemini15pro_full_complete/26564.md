cabler:/ˈkeɪblər/ | n. | 布线工，电缆安装工 | A person who installs or repairs cables. |  The cabler finished installing the network cables in the new office building. 布线工完成了在新办公楼中安装网络电缆。

The cabler spliced the two fiber optic cables together. 布线工将两条光缆拼接在一起。 | 近义词: cable installer, wireman | 反义词: None | Noun
