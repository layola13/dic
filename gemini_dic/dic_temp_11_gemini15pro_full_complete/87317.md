herniae:/ˈhɜːrniːiː/
n.
疝气(复数)
A hernia is a condition in which part of an organ is displaced and protrudes through the wall of the cavity containing it.  Herniae is the plural form of hernia.
The surgeon repaired multiple hernias.  外科医生修复了多处疝气。
近义词: ruptures
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 "normal tissue integrity" 组织完整性)
词性: 名词(复数)
