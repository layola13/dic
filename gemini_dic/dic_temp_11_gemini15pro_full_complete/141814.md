orbited:/ˈɔːrbɪtɪd/
verb
绕轨道运行，环绕
moved around a celestial body in an orbit.
The satellite orbited the Earth for several years.  这颗卫星绕地球运行了好几年。
The Earth orbits the sun. 地球绕太阳运行。
synonym: circled, revolved, rotated
antonym: crashed, landed, descended
词性: 动词
