biographical:/ˌbaɪəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
传记的；传记体的
Relating to the facts and events of a person's life.
Example:  She wrote a biographical novel about her grandmother. 她写了一部关于她祖母的传记体小说。

Example:  The museum has a biographical exhibit on the life of Albert Einstein.  博物馆有一个关于爱因斯坦生平的传记展览。
synonym:  life-history,  biographic
antonym:  fictional,  unhistorical
词性: adjective
