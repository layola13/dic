overappraised: /ˌoʊvərəˈpreɪzd/
overappraised: adj.
overappraised: 估价过高的
overappraised:  Estimated or assessed at a value that is too high.  It implies that the assigned value exceeds the true or fair market value.
overappraised: The house was significantly overappraised, leading to difficulties in selling it. 这房子被严重高估了，导致难以出售。
overappraised: The antique dealer overappraised the vase, hoping to make a larger profit. 古董商高估了花瓶的价格，希望能赚取更大的利润。
overappraised: 近义词: overvalued, inflated
overappraised: 反义词: undervalued, underpriced
overappraised: 词性: 形容词
