arras:/ˈærəs/ /ˈærɑːz/| n.|挂毯；(尤指)织锦壁毯；(复数)定金，保证金|A large tapestry, typically one woven with historical or mythological scenes.  Money or other valuables given by a groom to his bride on their wedding day, traditionally in some parts of France.|The king's chambers were adorned with rich arras depicting scenes of the hunt.|近义词：tapestry, wall hanging, deposit, pledge|反义词: (For the financial meaning) debt|词性:noun
