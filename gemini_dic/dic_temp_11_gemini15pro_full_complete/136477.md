nonrevolutions:/ˌnɒnˌrevəˈluːʃənz/| n. |非革命，不彻底的变革|A process of gradual change that does not involve a sudden or violent overthrow of a government or social order.  It refers to transformations that occur incrementally, without radical upheaval.|The government implemented a series of nonrevolutions to modernize the economy.|Synonyms: evolution, reform, gradualism, transition|Antonyms: revolution, upheaval, rebellion, revolt|noun
