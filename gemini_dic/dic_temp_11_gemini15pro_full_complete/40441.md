contrasters:/ˈkɒntrɑːstəz/ | n. | 对比者，形成对比的人或物 | People or things that contrast with each other, highlighting their differences. | The black and white squares on the chessboard are effective contrasters. 棋盘上的黑白方格是有效的对比物。 |  The bright colors of the flowers served as striking contrasters against the green foliage.  鲜艳的花朵与绿叶形成了鲜明的对比。 |近义词：foils, opposites |反义词:similarities, parallels | noun

contraster:/kənˈtrɑːstər/ | n. | 对比者，形成对比的人或物 |  A person or thing that contrasts or creates a contrast. |  He acted as a contraster to her outgoing personality with his quiet demeanor. 他安静的举止与她外向的性格形成了对比。 |  The dark background served as a contraster to make the bright colors of the painting pop.  深色的背景起到了对比的作用，使画作的鲜艳色彩更加突出。 |近义词：foil, opposite |反义词:complement, parallel | noun
