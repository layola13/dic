disbranch:/dɪsˈbrɑːntʃ/
verb
剥去树枝; 去枝; 修剪
To remove branches from a tree or plant.
The gardener disbranched the apple tree to improve its shape. 园丁修剪了苹果树以改善其形状。
The storm disbranched several trees in the park.  暴风雨折断了公园里几棵树的树枝。
prune, trim, lop
grow, cultivate
