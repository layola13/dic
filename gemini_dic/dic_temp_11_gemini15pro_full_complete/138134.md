northeasterly: /ˌnɔːrθˈiːstərli/ /ˌnɔːrθˈiːstərli/
adjective|东北方的；从东北方来的|Coming from the northeast; situated in or towards the northeast.|The wind was northeasterly. 风来自东北方。|northeastern, easterly|southwesterly, westerly
adverb|向东北|Towards the northeast.|The ship sailed northeasterly. 船向东北航行。|northeastward, eastward|southwestward, westward

ETYMOLOGY->
From north + east + -erly (suffix forming adjectives and adverbs of direction).
