kinship:/ˈkɪnʃɪp/| n.|亲属关系，血缘关系，亲切感|A sharing of characteristics or origins.|例句：I felt a kinship with the other artists. 我和其他艺术家感到一种亲切感。|例句：The bonds of kinship are strong in many traditional societies.  在许多传统社会中，亲属关系的纽带很强。|近义词：relationship, affinity, connection|反义词：estrangement, alienation|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cynscipe "kinship, family, tribe, race," from cyn "kin" (see kin) + -scipe (see -ship).
