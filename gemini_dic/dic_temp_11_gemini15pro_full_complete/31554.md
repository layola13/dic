charts:/tʃɑːrts/ | n. |图表; 曲线图; 海图|A graphical representation of data, often using lines, bars, or other symbols to show the relationship between different quantities or values. Also, nautical maps. |The sales charts showed a significant increase in profits. 销售图表显示利润大幅增长。|Navigational charts are essential for safe sailing. 航海图对于安全航行至关重要。| graphs, diagrams, maps | tables, lists | noun
