dingo:/ˈdɪŋɡoʊ/ (US), /ˈdɪŋɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. |澳洲野犬|A wild or half-wild dog of Australia, having a sandy-colored coat.|The dingo howled at the moon.  澳洲野犬对着月亮嚎叫。|Synonyms: wild dog, feral dog|Antonyms: domesticated dog|noun

dingo:/ˈdɪŋɡoʊ/ (US), /ˈdɪŋɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. |野狗（泛指）|Can sometimes refer to any wild or feral dog, not exclusively Australian.|Locals warned us about the dingos in the area. 当地人警告我们该地区有野狗。|Synonyms: wild dog, stray dog|Antonyms: domesticated dog, pet dog|noun
