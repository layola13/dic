hypersensitively:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnsɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 过度敏感地，过敏地 | in a way that is too easily upset, offended, or affected |  She reacted hypersensitively to the criticism. 她对批评反应过度敏感。| 近义词: oversensitively, excessively sensitively | 反义词: insensitively, unfeelingly | 词性: 副词

词源: From hypersensitivity + -ly. Hypersensitivity is formed from hyper- (meaning "excessive") + sensitivity.
