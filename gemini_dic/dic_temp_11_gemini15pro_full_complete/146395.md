overfluency: /ˌoʊvərˈfluːənsi/ | n. | 过度流利; 言语流畅过度 |  An excessive fluency of speech, sometimes to the point of making communication less effective due to rapid or unclear articulation, or inclusion of unnecessary details.  It can be a symptom of some neurological conditions. |  His overfluency made it hard to follow his train of thought. 他的过度流利使得很难跟上他的思路。| Synonyms: volubility, logorrhea | Antonyms: hesitancy, dysfluency | noun
