neuropathic:/ˌnjʊərəˈpæθɪk/ /ˌnʊrəˈpæθɪk/| adj.|神经病的，神经性的|Relating to, or affected by, neuropathy; resulting from damage to the nerves.|例句：She suffers from neuropathic pain in her feet. 她双脚患有神经性疼痛。|例句：Neuropathic ulcers are a common complication of diabetes. 神经性溃疡是糖尿病的常见并发症。|近义词：neural, neurological|反义词: healthy, normal|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From neuro- + -pathic.  Neuro- comes from Greek *neuron* "nerve, sinew". -pathic comes from Greek *pathos* "suffering, disease".
