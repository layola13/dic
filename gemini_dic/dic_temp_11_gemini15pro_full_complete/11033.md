arcadians:/ɑːrˈkeɪdiənz/
n.
阿卡迪亚人；田园诗人；过着田园般生活的人
Inhabitants of Arcadia, a region in ancient Greece, often idealized as a place of rustic simplicity and pastoral happiness, or people who live a simple, peaceful life close to nature.
The arcadians lived a simple life, tending their flocks and playing music in the fields. 阿卡迪亚人过着简朴的生活，照料他们的羊群，在田野里演奏音乐。

The poet described the arcadians as living in perfect harmony with nature. 诗人将阿卡迪亚人描述为与自然和谐相处。

近义词:rustics, pastoralists, country dwellers
反义词:city dwellers, urbanites
词性:noun
