jaunted:/ˈdʒɔːntɪd/ | adj. | 愉快的；洋洋得意的；轻快的 | Having or expressing a lively, cheerful, and self-confident manner. |  She walked with a jaunty step. 她步伐轻快地走着。| synonyms: cheerful, lively, perky, buoyant, airy, brisk, sprightly, spirited, carefree, nonchalant | antonyms:  depressed, gloomy, downcast, dejected, despondent | adjective


Etymology:
From French *janter "to jest," perhaps from Old French genter "to entertain."  The meaning shifted from "stylish" to "easy and sprightly in manner" in the 18th century.
