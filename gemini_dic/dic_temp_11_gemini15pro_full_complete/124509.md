moundbuilder:/ˈmaʊndˌbɪldər/
n.
土墩建造者，指美洲原住民中建造土墩的族群
A member of one of the various Native American peoples who built mounds in the eastern United States.
The moundbuilders left behind fascinating earthworks.  这些土墩建造者留下了令人惊叹的土方工程。

近义词:ancient American, prehistoric American
反义词:(无直接反义词)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *mound* + *builder*. *Mound* comes from Middle English *mount*, from Old French *mont*, ultimately from Latin *montem*, accusative of *mons* ("mountain"). *Builder* comes from Middle English *bilder*, from Old English *byldere*, an agent noun from *byldan* ("to build").
