compatible:/kəmˈpætɪbl/ | adj. | 兼容的；可共存的；相容的 | (of two things) able to exist or occur together without conflict.  | This software is compatible with most computers. 这个软件与大多数电脑兼容。 | The new printer is compatible with all operating systems.  这台新打印机与所有操作系统兼容。 |近义词：consistent, agreeable, harmonious, congruent |反义词：incompatible, conflicting, discordant | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *compatibilis, from Latin compati, present active infinitive of compātor “I suffer with, sympathize with,” from com- “together” + patior “I suffer, endure.”
