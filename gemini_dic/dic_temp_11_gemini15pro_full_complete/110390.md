ligroin: /ˈlaɪɡroʊɪn/ | n. | 石油醚，轻汽油 | A flammable, volatile fraction of petroleum, distilling at 60–90°C, consisting of a mixture of alkanes and used as a solvent. | Ligroin was used to extract the organic compounds from the mixture.  石油醚被用来从混合物中提取有机化合物。| synonyms: petroleum ether, petroleum spirit | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a specific chemical substance. One might consider "water" as an antonym in the context of solubility, since ligroin is nonpolar.) | noun
