codlin:/ˈkɒdlɪn/ | n. |未成熟的苹果; 一种烹饪苹果|An immature apple; a kind of apple suited for cooking.|She made a delicious codlin pie. 她做了一个美味的未熟苹果派。|Synonym: cooking apple|Antonym: ripe apple|noun


Etymology->
From Middle English *codlyng, from Old French *codlin, of uncertain origin. Possibly from Vulgar Latin *coto, from Latin *cotoneum 'quince'.
