inappropriate:/ˌɪnəˈproʊpriət/| adj.|不恰当的，不合适的|It would be inappropriate to discuss such matters in public. 在公共场合讨论这种事情是不合适的。|例句：His inappropriate joke caused a lot of embarrassment. 他不合时宜的笑话引起了很多尴尬。|近义词：unsuitable, improper, unfit|反义词: suitable, appropriate, fit|词性:adjective
