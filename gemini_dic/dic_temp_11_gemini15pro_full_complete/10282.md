apparatchik: /ˌæpəˈrætʃɪk/ | n. | 党政官员；官僚 | A member of a communist party apparatus, typically one who holds an administrative post and adheres rigidly to party doctrine. |  The apparatchiks were quick to denounce the new reform proposals. 这些党政官员迅速谴责了新的改革提案。| The film satirizes the apparatchiks of the old Soviet regime. 这部电影讽刺了旧苏联政权的官僚们。 | Synonyms: bureaucrat, official, functionary | Antonyms: rebel, dissident | Noun

Etymology: From Russian аппара́тчик (apparátchik), from аппара́т (apparát, “apparatus, machinery, administrative body”), from French appareil.
