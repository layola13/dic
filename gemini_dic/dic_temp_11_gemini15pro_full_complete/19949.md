biodiverse:/ˌbaɪoʊdaɪˈvɜːrs/
adjective
生物多样性的
Possessing a high level of biodiversity; having many different types of plants and animals.  Characterized by a great variety of living organisms.
例句：The Amazon rainforest is a biodiverse ecosystem. 亚马逊雨林是一个生物多样性的生态系统。

例句：Costa Rica is known for its biodiverse landscapes. 哥斯达黎加以其生物多样性的景观而闻名。


近义词：species-rich, varied, multifarious
反义词：homogeneous, uniform, barren
词性：adjective
