dumbfounding:/dʌmˈfaʊndɪŋ/| adj.|令人震惊的，使人目瞪口呆的|Extremely surprising and shocking, so that one is temporarily unable to react.|The magician's skill was so dumbfounding that the audience was speechless. 魔术师的技艺令人叹为观止，观众都 speechless 了。|Synonyms: astonishing, astounding, bewildering, stunning|Antonyms: unsurprising, expected, ordinary|adjective
