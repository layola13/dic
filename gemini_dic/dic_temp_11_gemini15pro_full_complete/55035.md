disquieter:/dɪsˈkwaɪətər/| n.|扰乱安宁的人; 令人不安的人|Someone or something that causes anxiety or disturbs peace. |The constant construction noise outside her window was a real disquieter.|近义词：troublemaker, agitator, alarmist|反义词:pacifier, calmer|词性:noun

disquiet:/dɪsˈkwaɪət/| n. vt.|不安; 焦虑; 忧虑; 使不安; 使焦虑; 使忧虑|A feeling of anxiety or uneasiness; to make someone anxious or uneasy. |The news of the layoffs filled her with disquiet. / The sudden silence disquieted her.|近义词:unease, anxiety, worry, disturb, upset|反义词:calm, peace, tranquility, soothe, reassure|词性:noun, verb
