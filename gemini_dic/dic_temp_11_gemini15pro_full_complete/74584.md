frazzles:/ˈfræzəlz/| n. | 碎布; 疲惫状态 |  Small, loose threads or fibers that have worn away from a fabric or rope; a state of being extremely tired or worn out, especially mentally or emotionally. |  The carpet was so old, it was covered in frazzles. 这地毯太旧了，上面全是毛边。 | synonyms: shreds, tatters, exhaustion, fatigue | antonyms: wholeness, intactness, energy, vigor | noun

frazzle:/ˈfræzl/| v. | 磨损; 使疲惫 | To wear out or fray into small, loose threads or fibers; to exhaust or wear out, especially mentally or emotionally. | The constant stress of the job began to frazzle her nerves. 这份工作的持续压力开始让她神经紧张。 | synonyms: fray, unravel, exhaust, fatigue | antonyms: mend, repair, energize, invigorate | verb
