oems:/ˈoʊiːɛmz/ | n. | Original Equipment Manufacturers (原始设备制造商) | Companies that manufacture products that are then sold by another company under their own brand.  |  Example: The OEMs provide the parts and another company assembles the final product. 原始设备制造商提供零件，而另一家公司组装最终产品。 | Example: Many computer companies rely on OEMs for components like hard drives and motherboards. 许多计算机公司依赖原始设备制造商提供硬盘和主板等组件。 | Synonyms:  manufacturers, producers, suppliers | Antonyms:  retailers, distributors | noun
