boogeyman:/ˈbʊɡimæn/ (US) /ˈbʊɡɪmæn/ (UK)| n. |妖怪，鬼怪；吓唬小孩的假想怪物|An imaginary evil creature used to frighten children.|例句：The boogeyman will get you if you don't behave. 如果你不乖，妖怪就会来抓你。

例句：She used the boogeyman as a way to get her kids to go to bed. 她用妖怪来吓唬她的孩子上床睡觉。

近义词：monster, goblin, bogey, bugbear|反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "protector," "guardian") |词性:noun
