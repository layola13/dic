crescendo: /krəˈʃendoʊ/ (US) /krəˈʃendəʊ/ (UK)
crescendo: n. | 渐强，高潮 | a gradual increase in loudness, or the moment when a sound is at its loudest | The music reached a crescendo and then faded away.  音乐声渐强，然后逐渐消失。| synonyms: climax, peak, culmination | antonyms: diminuendo, decrescendo | noun
crescendo: adv. | 渐强地 | with a gradual increase in loudness | The orchestra played crescendo until the final note.  管弦乐队的声音逐渐增强，直到最后一个音符。| synonyms: increasingly, escalating | antonyms: decreasingly, diminishing | adverb
crescendo: v. |  (声音)渐强，(局势)加剧 | to increase gradually in loudness or intensity | The applause crescendoed as the performer took a bow.  随着表演者鞠躬，掌声逐渐增强。 | synonyms: build, intensify, swell | antonyms: diminish, lessen, subside | verb
