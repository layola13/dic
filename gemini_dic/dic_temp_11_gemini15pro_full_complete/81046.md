grafted:/ˈɡræftɪd/ | adj. |嫁接的，移植的|Joined or fixed to something else, typically in a way considered unnatural or artificial.  Combined or integrated.|例句：A grafted rose bush. 一株嫁接的玫瑰丛。|例句：His head seemed grafted onto his body. 他的头看起来像是嫁接到身体上的。|近义词：attached, joined, implanted, spliced|反义词：detached, separated|词性:形容词
词源: From graft (v.) + -ed.  Ultimately from the Greek *γράφειν (graphein) meaning "to scratch, write, draw."
