esthetic:/ɛsˈθɛtɪk/ /iːsˈθetɪk/
adj.|审美的，美学的，美感的|Relating to the philosophy of aesthetics; concerned with notions such as the beautiful and the ugly.|The painting is very esthetic. 这幅画很有美感。|近义词：artistic, beautiful, creative|反义词：ugly, unattractive|词性: adjective

esthetic:/ɛsˈθɛtɪk/ /iːsˈθetɪk/
n.|美感，审美观|A set of principles underlying and guiding the work of a particular artist or artistic movement.|He has a keen esthetic. 他有敏锐的审美观。|近义词：taste, appreciation, perception|反义词：disinterest, indifference|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *aisthētikos* "perceptive, sensitive," from *aisthēta* "perceptible things," from *aisthanesthai* "to perceive."
