monopoloid:/ˈmɒnəplɔɪd/ /ˈmɑːnəplɔɪd/| adj. n.|单倍体(的)；一倍体(的)|Having a single set of chromosomes in each cell nucleus.  A cell or organism having a single set of chromosomes.|例句：The monopoloid plant showed reduced vigor.  这种单倍体植物表现出活力降低。

例句：Scientists are studying the monopoloid form of the fungus. 科学家正在研究这种真菌的单倍体形态。|近义词：haploid|反义词：diploid, polyploid|词性:adjective, noun

Etymology:  From mono- + -ploid, from Greek -ploos "-fold," related to haploid and diploid.
