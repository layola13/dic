multiplexing: /ˈmʌltɪˌplɛksɪŋ/ | n. | 多路复用，多路传输 | The process of combining multiple signals into one shared channel. |  Modern telecommunications rely heavily on multiplexing to transmit large amounts of data efficiently. 现代电信严重依赖多路复用技术来高效地传输大量数据。

Multiplexing allows multiple users to share a single internet connection. 多路复用允许多个用户共享一个互联网连接。 | 近义词: multiple access | 反义词: demultiplexing | 词性: 名词
