organics:/ɔːrˈɡænɪks/ | n. | 有机物；有机食品；有机农产品 |  Substances or products that are produced or grown without the use of synthetic pesticides, fertilizers, or genetically modified organisms. |  Example: She only buys organics at the grocery store. 她只在杂货店买有机食品。 | Example: The demand for organics has increased in recent years. 近年来对有机产品的需求有所增加。| Synonyms: organic produce, organic food, ecological, natural | Antonyms: inorganic, synthetic, non-organic | noun

organics:/ɔːrˈɡænɪks/ | adj. | 有机的 | Relating to or derived from living matter.  Produced or involving production without the use of chemical fertilizers, pesticides, or other artificial agents. | Example:  Organic farming is better for the environment. 有机农业对环境更有利。 | Example:  She prefers organic cotton clothing. 她更喜欢有机棉服装。 | Synonyms: biological, natural, ecological | Antonyms: inorganic, synthetic, non-organic | adjective
