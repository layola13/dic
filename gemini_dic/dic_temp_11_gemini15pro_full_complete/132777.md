nondualism:/nɒnˈdʒuːəlɪzəm/ | n. | 非二元论 | The philosophical, spiritual, and religious concept that there is ultimately no distinction between subject and object, or between the self and the universe.  It emphasizes the interconnectedness and unity of all things. |  Example 1: Nondualism is a core concept in Advaita Vedanta. 非二元论是吠檀多不二论的核心概念。

Example 2:  Many mystical traditions embrace nondualism. 很多神秘主义传统都拥护非二元论。 | 近义词: monism, oneness | 反义词: dualism | 词性: noun
