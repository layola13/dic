nonnegativity:/ˌnɒnˌnɛɡəˈtɪvɪti/ |n.| 非负性；非负性约束 |The condition of being greater than or equal to zero.  This concept is crucial in mathematics, particularly in optimization problems where variables are restricted to non-negative values.|The nonnegativity constraint ensures that the solution is physically meaningful. 非负性约束确保解在物理意义上是有意义的。

The algorithm maintains the nonnegativity of all variables throughout the iterations. 该算法在整个迭代过程中保持所有变量的非负性。|近义词: positiveness, non-negativeness |反义词: negativity |词性: noun
