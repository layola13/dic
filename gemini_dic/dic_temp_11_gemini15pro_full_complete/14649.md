avow:/əˈvaʊ/
|v.|公开承认；公开声明；坦白|To declare or admit something openly and frankly.|She avowed her love for him. 她坦白了她对他的爱。|He avowed that he was guilty. 他承认自己有罪。|近义词：admit, confess, declare, proclaim|反义词：deny, disavow|verb
