figuratively: /ˈfɪɡjərətɪvli/ /ˈfɪɡjərətɪvli/ | adv. | 比喻地，象征性地 |  In a way that uses figures of speech; not literally. |  He used the word "killed" figuratively, meaning the performance was very impressive.  他用“杀死”这个词是比喻，意思是表演非常令人印象深刻。| synonym: metaphorically, symbolically, allegorically | antonym: literally, directly | adverb

Etymology:  From Late Latin *fīgurātīvus, from Latin fīgurātus (“formed, shaped”), past participle of fīgurāre (“to form, shape”), from fīgura (“figure, shape”).
