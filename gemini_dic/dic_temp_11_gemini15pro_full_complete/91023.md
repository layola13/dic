hushes:/ˈhʌʃɪz/ | n. | 寂静，安静；嘘声 |  The sound of hushes fell over the crowd as the speaker began. |  A hush fell over the audience as the curtain rose.  观众席上鸦雀无声，大幕拉开了。 | synonyms: silences, quiets, stills | antonyms: noises, commotions, uproars | noun

hushes:/ˈhʌʃɪz/ | v. | 使安静，使沉默 | The mother hushed her crying baby. |  He hushed the audience with a gesture. 他做了个手势让观众安静下来。 | synonyms: silences, quiets, stills | antonyms: excites, agitates, stirs up | verb



ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *huschen, *husshen, from Old French huchier (“to hush”), of uncertain origin. Perhaps of imitative origin.
