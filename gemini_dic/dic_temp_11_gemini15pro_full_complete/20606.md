blackguard:/ˈblækɡɑːrd/| n.|恶棍，无赖，流氓|A person, particularly a man, who behaves in a dishonest or contemptible way; a scoundrel.|That blackguard stole my purse! 那个无赖偷了我的钱包！|近义词：villain, rogue, scoundrel, rascal|反义词：gentleman, hero|词性: 名词

blackguard:/blækˈɡɑːrd/| vt.|辱骂，诽谤|To abuse or vilify someone.|He blackguarded his opponent during the debate. 他在辩论中辱骂了他的对手。|近义词：revile, slander, defame|反义词：praise, commend|词性: 动词
