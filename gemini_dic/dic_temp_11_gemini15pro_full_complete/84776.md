hands-on:/ˈhændzˈɒn/ /ˈhændzˈɑːn/ | adj. |亲力亲为的；实际操作的；| involving or requiring personal experience, skill, or attention, rather than theoretical knowledge or impersonal observation. |  例句：This workshop provides hands-on training in software development.  这个工作坊提供软件开发的实践培训。

例句：She prefers a hands-on approach to management. 她更喜欢亲力亲为的管理方式。|近义词：practical, active, involved, experiential |反义词：theoretical, passive, uninvolved | 词性: 形容词

hands-on:/ˈhændzˈɒn/ /ˈhændzˈɑːn/ | n. |实际操作；实际经验 | practical experience | 例句：The internship gave him valuable hands-on.  这次实习让他获得了宝贵的实践经验。

例句：Many employers value hands-on over academic qualifications. 许多雇主重视实践经验胜过学术资格。|近义词：experience, practice |反义词：theory |词性: 名词
