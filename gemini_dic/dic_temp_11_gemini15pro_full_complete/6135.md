amiablest:/ˈeɪmiəblɪst/ | adj. | 最和蔼可亲的 | Most amiable; having or showing pleasant, good-natured personal qualities; sociable. |  She was the amiablest person I'd ever met, always ready with a kind word and a smile. 她是我见过的最和蔼可亲的人，总是面带微笑，和蔼可亲。| 近义词: friendliest, most agreeable, most affable, most genial | 反义词: unfriendliest, most disagreeable, most hostile | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Middle French amiable, from Late Latin amicabilis (“friendly”), from Latin amicus (“friend”) + -abilis.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂eḱ- (“sharp; pointed”).
