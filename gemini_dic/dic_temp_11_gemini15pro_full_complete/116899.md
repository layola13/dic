mayfly:/ˈmeɪflaɪ/| n. |蜉蝣|A short-lived insect with delicate wings, especially abundant near water.|例句：Mayflies filled the air around the lake. 湖边飞满了蜉蝣。

例句：The mayfly's life is brief but beautiful. 蜉蝣的生命短暂而美丽。

近义词：shadfly|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps long-lived insect)|词性:noun
