homely:/ˈhoʊmli/ (US) /ˈhəʊmli/ (UK)| adj.|家常的；朴素的；不好看的|Simple and unpretentious; lacking in physical attractiveness (especially of a person).|例句：She had a homely face, but a warm and kind personality. 她长相普通，但性格热情善良。

例句：The restaurant had a homely atmosphere. 这家餐馆有一种家的氛围。|近义词：plain, simple, unattractive, unprepossessing|反义词：beautiful, attractive, glamorous, sophisticated|词性:形容词

homely:/ˈhoʊmli/ (US) /ˈhəʊmli/ (UK)| adj.|思乡的|Experiencing a longing for one's home.|例句：He felt homely after being away for so long.  离开这么久后，他感到很思念家乡。
