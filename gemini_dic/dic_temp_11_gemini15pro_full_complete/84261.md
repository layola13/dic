half-stocking:/ˈhæf ˈstɒkɪŋ/ (US) /ˈhɑːf ˈstɒkɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|半截袜，短袜|A stocking that reaches only to the calf or just below the knee.  It's shorter than a full-length stocking.|She wore half-stockings with her Mary Jane shoes. 她穿着玛丽珍鞋搭配半截袜。|Synonyms: ankle sock, sock, bobby sock|Antonyms: stocking, thigh-highs|noun
