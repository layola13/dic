hornlessnesses:/ˈhɔːrn.ləs.nɪ.sɪz/ | n. |无角状态; 没有角 |The state or quality of being hornless; lack of horns. | The hornlessnesses of the polled cattle were a result of selective breeding.  这些 polled 牛的无角状态是选择性育种的结果。| Near Synonyms: hornless condition, lack of horns | Antonyms: hornedness, presence of horns | noun

Etymology:
From hornless + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  "Hornless" derives from "horn" (Old English *horn) + "-less" (Old English -lēas).
