amylase:/ˈæmɪleɪz/ /ˈæmɪleɪs/| n.|淀粉酶|An enzyme that catalyses the hydrolysis of starch into sugars.|Saliva contains amylase, which begins the chemical process of digestion. 唾液含有淀粉酶，它开始了消化过程的化学反应。

Amylase is essential for breaking down carbohydrates. 淀粉酶对分解碳水化合物至关重要。

|近义词：diastase|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑抑制剂 inhibitor)|词性: noun
