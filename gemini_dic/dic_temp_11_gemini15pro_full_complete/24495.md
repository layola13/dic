bro's:/broʊz/| n.|兄弟们；伙计们|Informal, contracted form of "brothers."  Used to address a group of male friends or acquaintances.  Can also be a shortened form of "brother's," indicating possession.|例句：Hey bro's, what are you up to tonight? 嘿，兄弟们，今晚你们要做什么？

例句：I'm going over to my bro's house. 我要去我兄弟家。|近义词：guys, dudes, pals, mates|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially enemies or rivals depending on context)|词性:noun
