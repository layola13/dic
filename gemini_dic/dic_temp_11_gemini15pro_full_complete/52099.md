diametric:/ˌdaɪəˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 直径的，完全相反的 | Relating to or along a diameter; completely opposite or contrary. | The two politicians held diametrically opposed views on the issue. 这两位政治家在这个问题上持完全相反的观点。 | synonyms: opposite, contrary, antithetical, conflicting, clashing | antonyms: similar, alike, identical, consistent, harmonious | adjective

diametrically:/ˌdaɪəˈmɛtrɪkli/ | adv. |  直径地，完全相反地 | In a diametrically opposite way. | Their views on the subject were diametrically opposed. 他们在该主题上的观点截然相反。 | synonyms: completely, absolutely, totally, utterly, entirely | antonyms: partially, slightly, somewhat, moderately | adverb

From Late Latin *diametricus, from Ancient Greek  διαμετρικός (diametrikós, “diagonal”), from διά (diá, “across, through”) + μέτρον (métron, “measure”).
