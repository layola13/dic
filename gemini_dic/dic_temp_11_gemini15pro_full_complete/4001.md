ahchoo: /ɑːˈtʃuː/ (also /əˈtʃuː/) | interjection | 啊啾（拟声词，模拟打喷嚏的声音） |  The sound made when sneezing.  It is an onomatopoeic representation of the sound. |  "Ahchoo!" she sneezed, grabbing a tissue.  啊啾！她打了个喷嚏，抓起一张纸巾。 | Example: He let out a loud "ahchoo!" in the middle of the quiet library. 他在安静的图书馆里大声地打了个“啊啾！”|Synonyms: achoo, atchoo | Antonyms:  (No real antonyms for a sound) | Part of Speech: Interjection
