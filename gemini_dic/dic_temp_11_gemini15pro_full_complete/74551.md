fraud:/frɔːd/| n.|欺诈，诈骗，骗子|wrongful or criminal deception intended to result in financial or personal gain|He was found guilty of tax fraud. 他被判犯有税务欺诈罪。|近义词：deception, swindle, cheat, scam, hoax|反义词：honesty, integrity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French fraude "fraud, deceit, trickery," from Latin fraudem (nominative fraus) "injury, harm; offense; deceit, treachery," of unknown origin.
