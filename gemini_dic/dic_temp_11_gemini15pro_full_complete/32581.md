chirogymnast:/ˌkaɪroʊˈdʒɪmnæst/ | n. | 手体操教练，手部体操师 | A specialist in chirogymnastics, a system of exercises for the hands. |  A skilled chirogymnast can help improve dexterity and hand strength.  一位熟练的手体操教练可以帮助提高手的灵巧性和力量。| synonym: hand therapist, physical therapist (for hands) | antonym: N/A | noun

Etymology:  From Greek *kheir* (χειρ) "hand" + *gymnastēs* (γυμναστής) "trainer, coach."
