nondefining:/ˌnɒndɪˈfaɪnɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈfaɪnɪŋ/
adj.
非限定性的, 非定义的
Giving extra information about something, without being essential to its meaning.
My brother, who lives in London, is a doctor. 我的哥哥，住在伦敦的，是个医生。(非限定性定语从句，去掉逗号之间的部分，句子的主要意思仍然完整)
Her house, which she bought last year, is very modern. 她去年买的房子非常现代化。(非限定性定语从句)
近义词:nonrestrictive, parenthetical
反义词:defining, restrictive
词性:adjective
