colligate:/ˈkɒlɪɡeɪt/ (British) /ˈkɑːlɪɡeɪt/ (American)| v. | 整理；校对；汇集 | To gather into an orderly system; to verify or check, as by comparison; to bind or fasten together. | He colligated the data from several different sources for his research paper. 他从不同的来源汇集数据用于他的研究论文。 | The editor carefully colligated the different versions of the manuscript. 编辑仔细校对了不同版本的稿件。 | 近义词: assemble, gather, collect, compile, collate | 反义词: disperse, scatter, separate | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "colligatus," past participle of "colligare," from "com-" (together) + "ligare" (to bind).
