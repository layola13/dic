misdiet:/ˌmɪsˈdaɪət/ | n. |  不良饮食；不健康的饮食 | An unhealthy diet, characterized by insufficient or excessive intake of certain nutrients, or by consumption of food that is harmful to health. |  Her misdiet of fast food and sugary drinks led to health problems.  她不健康的快餐和含糖饮料饮食导致了健康问题。 | Synonyms: poor diet, unhealthy diet, unbalanced diet | Antonyms: healthy diet, balanced diet | noun
