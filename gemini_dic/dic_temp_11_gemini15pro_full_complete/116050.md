martialists:/ˈmɑːrʃəlɪsts/
n.
尚武者，武术家，好战分子
People who advocate or practice martial arts or have a strong belief in military solutions.
The martialists believed that strength and discipline were the keys to a successful society. 尚武者们相信力量和纪律是成功社会的关键。
The group of martialists trained rigorously every day. 这群武术家每天都进行严格的训练。
synonyms: warriors, fighters, soldiers, militarists
antonyms: pacifists, doves, peacemakers
noun
