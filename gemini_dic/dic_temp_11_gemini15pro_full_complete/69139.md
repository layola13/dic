favour:/ˈfeɪvər/ (US) /ˈfeɪvə(r)/ (UK)| n. vt.|好感，喜爱；恩惠；帮助；赞同；支持| a kind action that you do for someone | Could you do me a favour and open the window?  你能帮我个忙打开窗户吗？|近义词：kindness, help, support, benefit, approval|反义词: disfavor, opposition|noun

favour:/ˈfeɪvər/ (US) /ˈfeɪvə(r)/ (UK)| vt.|赞成；喜爱；有利于；偏袒| to support or prefer one particular possibility |  The warm weather favoured our plans for a picnic.  温暖的天气有利于我们的野餐计划。|近义词：support, prefer, promote, encourage|反义词: oppose, hinder|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *faveur, favor "kindness, pity, help; privilege granted," from Latin favor "goodwill, kindness, support, popularity," related to favere "to be favorable to," from PIE root *ghau- "to rejoice, take pleasure in."
