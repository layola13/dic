n.

The northerliness of the region is reflected in its harsh winters.  该地区的北方性体现在其严酷的冬季。
The house had a certain northerliness about it, with its small windows and thick stone walls. 这座房子有一种北方特有的感觉，窗户很小，石墙很厚。
Etymology: northern + -li- + -ness. From "northern," referring to the north, with "-li-" forming an adjective and "-ness" forming a noun indicating a state or quality.  词源：northern + -li- + -ness。来自“northern”，指的是北方，“-li-”构成形容词，“-ness”构成名词，表示状态或性质。
Near Synonyms: northernness, northerly character, northward tendency
Antonyms: southerliness, southern character, southward tendency
