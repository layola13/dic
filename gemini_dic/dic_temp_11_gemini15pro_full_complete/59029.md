dwarfing:/ˈdwɔːrfɪŋ/
adjective
使相形见绌的；使显得矮小的
Making something seem small or insignificant by comparison.
The skyscrapers dwarfed the surrounding buildings. 这些摩天大楼使周围的建筑物显得矮小。
近义词: diminishing, eclipsing, overshadowing
反义词: enlarging, magnifying, exaggerating
词性: adjective

dwarfing:/ˈdwɔːrfɪŋ/
noun
矮化；变矮
The process of making something smaller or stunted in growth.
The dwarfing of the trees was caused by a lack of nutrients in the soil. 土壤中缺乏营养导致树木矮化。
近义词: stunting, shrinking, reduction
反义词: growth, enlargement, expansion
词性: noun

词源: From the verb "dwarf" + "-ing" suffix. "Dwarf" comes from Old English *dweorh, of Germanic origin, related to Old Norse dvergr and Dutch dwerg.
