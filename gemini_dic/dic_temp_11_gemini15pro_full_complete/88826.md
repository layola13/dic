hoggerpipe:/ˈhɒɡəpaɪp (British) / ˈhɑːɡərpaɪp (American)| n. | 排水管，污水管；（尤指）厕所的排水管 | A large pipe for carrying sewage or waste water, especially from a toilet. |  The hoggerpipe was blocked, causing a nasty overflow.  排水管堵塞了，造成了严重的溢流。| Example:  The plumber had to snake the hoggerpipe to clear the blockage. 水管工不得不使用通管器疏通排水管。 | Synonyms:  sewer pipe, drainpipe, waste pipe, soil pipe | Antonyms: None (It's a specific type of pipe, not a concept with an opposite.) | noun
