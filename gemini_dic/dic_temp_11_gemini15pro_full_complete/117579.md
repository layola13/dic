megalocephaly:/ˌmɛɡəloʊˈsɛfəli/| n. |巨头畸形，大头症|A medical condition characterized by an abnormally large head.  It is often a symptom of underlying medical conditions affecting brain development.|The child was diagnosed with megalocephaly shortly after birth.  这个孩子出生后不久就被诊断出患有巨头畸形。|近义词：macrocephaly|反义词：microcephaly|词性:noun

Etymology:
From mega- (large) + -cephaly (head), derived from Greek *megálē kephalḗ*.
