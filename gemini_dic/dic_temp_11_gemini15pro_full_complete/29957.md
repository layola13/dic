caverned:/ˈkævənd/ | adj. | 洞穴状的；似洞穴的；多洞穴的 | Resembling a cavern; having caverns or hollows.  | The caverned halls of the ancient castle echoed with the sound of our footsteps. 古堡洞穴般的大厅回荡着我们脚步的声音。 | The caverned depths of the ocean. 海洋深处洞穴般的深度。| 近义词: cave-like, hollowed, concave | 反义词: convex, smooth, flat | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the noun "cavern" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Cavern" comes from Old French "caverne" from Latin "caverna" meaning "cave," related to "cavus" meaning "hollow."
