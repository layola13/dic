disinsure:/ˌdɪsɪnˈʃʊr/ | v. | 取消保险 | To terminate or cancel insurance coverage on something or someone. |  The company decided to disinsure the fleet of old trucks because the premiums were too high.  该公司决定取消这批旧卡车的保险，因为保费太高了。

The homeowner decided to disinsure the shed as it was no longer in use.  房主决定取消棚屋的保险，因为它不再使用了。 | 近义词: cancel (insurance), terminate (coverage) | 反义词: insure, ensure | 词性: 动词

词源:  "dis-" (prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of") + "insure" (to provide or obtain insurance).  Comes from the Latin "in-" (meaning "in" or "on") and "securus" (meaning "secure" or "safe").
