/wɒt/ |pron. | 什么 |used to ask for information about something| What is your name? 你叫什么名字？| which, who | - | pronoun

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你靠什么谋生？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you working on? = 你在做什么？
What is he doing here? = 他在这里做什么？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? = 她最近在忙什么？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been reading lately? = 你最近一直在读什么？
What has he been doing since he quit his job? = 他辞掉工作以来一直在做什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会的时候她在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision was made? = 做决定之前他们讨论了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been working on before you got interrupted? = 你被打断之前一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气怎么样？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议会讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? = 到2033年你将已经做这件事十年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将学习什么专业多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to do. = 我希望我知道该做什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


疑问句:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are your plans for the summer? = 你夏天的计划是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
She didn't say what she wanted. = 她没说她想要什么。

what for? = 为了什么？
