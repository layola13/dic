heaviest:/ˈhɛviɪst/| adj.|最重的|Superlative form of heavy.  Having the greatest weight.|This is the heaviest box I've ever lifted. 这是我举过的最重的箱子。|Synonyms: weightiest, bulkiest, massive|Antonyms: lightest, slightest|adjective

heavier:/ˈhɛviər/| adj.|更重的|Comparative form of heavy. Having greater weight.|This box is heavier than that one.  这个箱子比那个重。|Synonyms: weightier, bulkier, more massive|Antonyms: lighter, slighter|adjective

heavy:/ˈhɛvi/| adj.|重的|Having great weight; difficult to lift or move.|The piano is very heavy. 钢琴很重。|Synonyms: weighty, bulky, massive|Antonyms: light, slight|adjective
