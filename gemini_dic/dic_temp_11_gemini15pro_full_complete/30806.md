ceylanite:/ˈseɪləˌnaɪt/ | n. | 锡兰石 |A dark green, blue-black, or black variety of spinel.  It is a magnesium aluminium oxide mineral (MgAl₂O₄).| Ceylanite is a beautiful gemstone often used in jewelry. 锡兰石是一种美丽的宝石，常用于珠宝。| The museum displayed a collection of rare ceylanite crystals.  博物馆展出了一系列罕见的锡兰石晶体。 |Synonyms: pleonaste, spinel | Antonyms: None | Noun
