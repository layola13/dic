fasted:/ˈfɑːstɪd/ /ˈfæstɪd/| v. adj.|禁食，斋戒（动词）；禁食的，斋戒的（形容词）|The act of abstaining from all or some kinds of food or drink, especially as a religious observance (verb).  Having abstained from food or drink (adjective).|例句：They fasted for three days as part of a religious ritual.  他们为了宗教仪式禁食了三天。|近义词：abstained, refrained|反义词：feasted, gorged|词性:verb, adjective

fasted:/ˈfɑːstɪd/ /ˈfæstɪd/| adj.|禁食的，斋戒的|Having abstained from food or drink.|例句：She felt weak and dizzy, having fasted for over 24 hours.  她禁食了超过24小时，感到虚弱和头晕。|近义词：hungry, starving|反义词：fed, satiated|词性:adjective

词源：From Old English *fæstan "to fast," from Proto-Germanic *fastanan "to hold firm, observe," from PIE root *past- "to fasten; firm, solid." The original sense was "to observe, keep, hold to," later specialized to "abstain from food."
