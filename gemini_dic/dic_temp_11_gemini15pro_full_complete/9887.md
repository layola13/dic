apastron:/ˈæpəstrɒn/ /əˈpæstrən/| n. |远星点; 拱点 |The apastron is the point of greatest separation between two stars orbiting each other.| The comet reached its apastron, the furthest point from the star in its orbit. 这颗彗星到达了它的远星点，也就是它轨道上离恒星最远的点。|近义词: apoapsis |反义词: periastron|noun
