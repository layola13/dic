arbitrarious:/ˌɑːrbɪˈtreəriəs/ | adj. | 任意的；武断的；专横的 | Based on random choice or personal whim, rather than any reason or system. |  The choice of restaurant was completely arbitrary.  餐厅的选择完全是随意的。| Example: The dictator's arbitrary rule caused widespread unrest.  独裁者的专横统治引起了广泛的动乱。 | Synonyms: capricious, random, whimsical, despotic, tyrannical | Antonyms: reasoned, rational, justifiable, objective, fair | word type: adjective
