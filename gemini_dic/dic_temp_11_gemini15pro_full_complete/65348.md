eulogises:/ˈjuːlədʒaɪzɪz/
verb
赞扬；称颂；歌颂
praises; extols; speaks or writes in high praise of.
The speaker eulogised the bravery of the firefighters. 演讲者称颂了消防员的英勇。
Synonyms:  praise, extol, laud, commend, celebrate, acclaim
Antonyms: criticize, condemn, denounce, disparage
词性: verb
