nonretractions:/ˌnɒnriˈtrækʃənz/ | n. | 非撤回，非收回（通常指声明、文章等） |  The journal's policy on nonretractions is very strict.  该期刊关于不撤回的政策非常严格。 |  Absence of retractions,  non-withdrawal | retractions, withdrawals | noun

Etymology:
* non-: Prefix meaning "not," from Latin *non*.
* retraction:  From Late Latin *retractionem* (nominative *retractio*), noun of action from past-participle stem of Latin *retrahere* "draw back," from *re-* "back" + *trahere* "to draw."
