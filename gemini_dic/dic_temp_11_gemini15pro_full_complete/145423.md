overcharged:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑːrdʒd/ | adj. | 过度充电的，收费过高的 |  Having been charged too much money for goods or services.  | Example: I think I was overcharged for my groceries. 我想我的杂货收费过高了。 | Example:  The hotel overcharged us for the room service.  酒店的客房服务对我们收费过高。 | Synonyms:  inflated, exorbitant, excessive | Antonyms: undercharged, discounted, cheap | Word type: adjective

overcharge:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑːrdʒ/ | v. |  收费过高，过度充电，过量装载 | To charge someone too much money for goods or services; to load something excessively. | Example: The taxi driver overcharged me. 出租车司机向我多收了钱。 | Example: Don't overcharge the battery. 不要给电池过度充电。 | Synonyms:  gouge, fleece, soak, overload | Antonyms: undercharge, discount | Word type: verb
