chaddars:/ˈtʃɑːdərz/ | n. | 印度大披巾; 印度薄棉被; |A large piece of thin cotton cloth, typically used as a bed covering in India. | She wrapped herself in a warm chaddar. 她用一条温暖的印度大披巾把自己裹了起来。| The chaddar provided little warmth against the cold desert night. 这条薄棉被抵御不住沙漠夜晚的寒冷。 |近义词: shawl, blanket, wrap |反义词: (No direct antonym) | noun
