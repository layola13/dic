outblustering: /aʊtˈblʌstərɪŋ/
outblustering | v. | 以更大的声势压倒; 比...更气势汹汹 | overpowering or intimidating someone by being louder and more aggressive | He outblustered his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中以更大的声势压倒了对手。| 近义词：outshout, intimidate, browbeat, cow | 反义词：submit, yield, concede | 词性: 动词

outblustering | adj. | 气势汹汹的; 咆哮的 | characterized by loud, aggressive, and blustering behavior | His outblustering manner alienated many of his colleagues. 他气势汹汹的态度疏远了许多同事。| 近义词：blusterous, boisterous, overbearing | 反义词：quiet, meek, subdued | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From 'out-' (meaning exceeding or surpassing) + 'bluster' (meaning to talk in a loud, aggressive, and empty way).  'Bluster' ultimately derives from a Scandinavian word related to 'blast'.
