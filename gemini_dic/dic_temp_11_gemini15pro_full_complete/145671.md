overcontrols:/ˌoʊvər kənˈtroʊlz/
verb
过度控制
to control something too much
The company overcontrols its employees, stifling creativity and innovation.  这家公司过度控制员工，扼杀了创造力和创新。
He overcontrols his children, dictating every aspect of their lives. 他过度控制他的孩子们，支配他们生活的方方面面。
近义词: micromanage, domineer, overmanage
反义词: empower, trust, delegate
词性: verb

overcontrol:/ˌoʊvər kənˈtroʊl/
noun
过度控制
excessive control
The overcontrol of the government stifles economic growth. 政府的过度控制扼杀了经济增长。
Parental overcontrol can lead to rebellious behavior in teenagers. 父母的过度控制会导致青少年的叛逆行为。
近义词: micromanagement, domination
反义词: freedom, autonomy
词性: noun
