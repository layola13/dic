compensator:/ˈkɒmpənseɪtə(r)/ (US) /ˈkɑːmpənseɪtər/| n. |补偿器；校正器；平衡器|A device or system that compensates for something else, typically something undesirable.|The aircraft's flight control system includes compensators for turbulence.|近义词: equalizer, counterbalance, adjuster, regulator|反义词: aggravator, amplifier|词性: noun

compensators:/ˈkɒmpənseɪtəz/ (US) /ˈkɑːmpənseɪtərz/| n. |补偿器；校正器；平衡器(复数)|Plural form of compensator. Refers to multiple devices or systems that compensate for something.|The engineers designed new compensators to improve the stability of the bridge.|近义词: equalizers, counterbalances, adjusters, regulators|反义词: aggravators, amplifiers|词性: noun
