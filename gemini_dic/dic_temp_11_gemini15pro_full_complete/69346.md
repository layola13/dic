federalistic:/ˌfɛdərəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 联邦主义的；拥护联邦制的 | Relating to or advocating federalism; supporting the principles of federalism. | The United States has a federalistic system of government, dividing powers between the national and state levels. 美国有一个联邦主义的政府体系，在国家和州两级之间划分权力。

The senator's federalistic stance earned him support from states' rights advocates.  参议员的联邦主义立场为他赢得了州权倡导者的支持。 | 近义词: federal, decentralized | 反义词: unitary, centralized | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From *federalist* + *-ic*.  *Federalist* comes from *federal* + *-ist*. *Federal* ultimately derives from Latin *foedus* "treaty, covenant, league, alliance".
