circumventor:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈvɛntər/
noun
规避者；绕行者；欺骗者
Someone who finds a way around a rule or law, especially by using deceit.
The tax circumventor was caught and penalized heavily.  这个逃税者被抓获并受到了重罚。
He was a master circumventor of the rules. 他是一个精于规避规则的大师。
synonyms: dodger, evader, cheat, fraudster, swindler
antonyms: conformist, follower, law-abider
n.
