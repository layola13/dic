composable:/kəmˈpoʊzəbl/| adj.|可组合的，可组成的|Able to be combined or put together, especially in different arrangements, to form a more complex whole.|Modular furniture is highly composable, allowing for countless configurations.|近义词:modular, combinable, adaptable, flexible|反义词:fixed, rigid, inflexible|adjective

composable:/kəmˈpoʊzəbl/| n.|可组合的组件|A composable element or module.|This software platform utilizes composables to build custom applications.|近义词:module, component, element|反义词:monolith|noun
