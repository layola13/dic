anti-Birmingham:/ˌæntiˈbɜːrmɪŋəm/ | adj. | 反伯明翰的，反对伯明翰政策的 | Opposed to the policies or practices associated with Birmingham, especially during the Civil Rights Movement.  |  Example:  The anti-Birmingham sentiment was strong among civil rights activists. 民权活动家中的反伯明翰情绪很强烈。

Example:  His anti-Birmingham stance cost him his job. 他的反伯明翰立场使他丢了工作。 | Near synonyms: anti-segregationist, anti-racist | Antonyms: pro-Birmingham, segregationist | adjective
