includible:/ɪnˈkluːdəbl/
adjective
可包括的，可列入的
able to be included
All meals are includible in the price of the holiday. 所有餐费都包含在度假费用中。
Near synonyms: includable, incorporable
Antonyms: excludable, inadmissible
词性: adjective
