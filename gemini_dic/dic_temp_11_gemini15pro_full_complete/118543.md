mesomorph:/ˈmɛsəmɔːrf/ | n. | 中胚层体型者；肌肉发达者 | A person with a well-developed muscular body and a heavy frame.  | Example: Mesomorphs tend to gain muscle easily and have a naturally athletic build. 中胚层体型的人往往容易增肌，天生就具有运动员的体格。

Example: He was a classic mesomorph, broad-shouldered and muscular. 他是典型的中胚层体型，肩膀宽阔，肌肉发达。 | Synonym: athletic build, muscular type | Antonym: ectomorph, endomorph | noun
