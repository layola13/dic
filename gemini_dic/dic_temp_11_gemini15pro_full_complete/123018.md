monasticism: /məˈnæstɪsɪzəm/
n.
禁欲主义，修道主义，僧侣生活
The state or condition of being a monk or nun; the monastic life or system.  A way of life characterized by prayer, contemplation, and self-denial, lived in seclusion under religious vows.
He chose a life of monasticism.  他选择过禁欲主义的生活。

The monastery is a place of peace and monasticism. 这座修道院是一个宁静和修道生活的地方。


近义词: asceticism, contemplation, religious seclusion
反义词: worldliness, secularism
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin monasticismus, from monachus "monk" (from Greek monakhos "solitary, monk," from monos "alone") + -ismus (from Greek -ismos).
