ill-equipped:/ˌɪlɪˈkwɪpt/
adjective
装备不良的，准备不足的
lacking the necessary tools, skills, or knowledge to do something effectively
例句：
The expedition was ill-equipped for such a challenging climb.
探险队装备不足，无法应对如此具有挑战性的攀登。

They were ill-equipped to deal with the sudden influx of refugees.
他们没有准备好应对难民的突然涌入。

近义词：unprepared, unequipped, inadequate, deficient
反义词：well-equipped, prepared, ready, competent
词性: adjective
