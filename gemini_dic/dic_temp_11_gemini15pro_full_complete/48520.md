deescalated:/diːˈɛskəleɪtɪd/
verb
缓和，降低，减轻
Reduced the intensity of a conflict or problem.
The police deescalated the situation by speaking calmly to the protesters. 警察通过平静地与抗议者交谈，缓和了局势。
The company deescalated the production of the unpopular product. 公司降低了不受欢迎产品的产量。
synonyms: calmed, eased, mitigated, reduced, lessened
antonyms: escalated, intensified, aggravated, worsened
