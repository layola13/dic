indecency:/ɪnˈdiːsnsi/ | n. |不体面；猥亵；下流|The state or quality of being indecent; lack of decency, propriety, or modesty.  Behavior or language that is offensive to accepted standards of decency. |The comedian was criticized for the indecency of his jokes. 这位喜剧演员因其笑话的猥亵内容而受到批评。|Near Synonyms: impropriety, immodesty, obscenity, lewdness, vulgarity|Antonyms: decency, propriety, modesty|noun

indecent:/ɪnˈdiːsnt/ | adj. |不体面的；猥亵的；下流的|Not conforming with generally accepted standards of decency or propriety; immodest or improper.  Offensive to good taste, especially in sexual matters. |He made indecent remarks about her appearance. 他对她的外貌说了些下流的话。|Near Synonyms: improper, immodest, obscene, lewd, vulgar|Antonyms: decent, proper, modest|adjective
