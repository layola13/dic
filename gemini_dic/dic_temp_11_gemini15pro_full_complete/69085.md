fauns:/fɔːnz/
n.
森林之神; 牧神; 半人半羊的生物
A mythical woodland creature, part human and part goat, with pointed ears, short horns, and a goat's tail.
The painting depicted a group of fauns dancing in a forest glade.  这幅画描绘了一群牧神在森林空地上跳舞。
synonyms: satyr, sylvan, wood nymph
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms exist, as fauns are mythological figures.  One might consider "human" as a loose antonym.)
