disoxygenation:/diːˌɒksɪdʒəˈneɪʃən/ /diːˌɑːksɪdʒəˈneɪʃən/
noun
脱氧；去氧
The process of removing oxygen from a substance or environment.
The disoxygenation of the ocean can lead to dead zones. 海洋的脱氧会导致死亡区域。
deoxygenation, anoxia
oxygenation
