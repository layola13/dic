ichthyocol:/ˈɪkθiəˌkɑːl/ | n. | 鱼胶，鱼鳔胶 |A form of gelatin obtained from the dried swim bladders of fish, especially sturgeon.  It is used as a clarifying agent for wine and beer, and in various other applications. |  Isinglass, also known as ichthyocol, is used in traditional bookbinding.  鱼胶，也称为鱼鳔胶，用于传统的书籍装订。

The brewery uses ichthyocol to clarify their beer.  这家啤酒厂使用鱼鳔胶来澄清他们的啤酒。 | 近义词: isinglass, gelatin, collagen | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
