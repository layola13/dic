breakwater: /ˈbreɪkˌwɔːtər/ (US) /ˈbreɪkˌwɔːtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |防波堤;防浪堤|A structure built in the sea to protect a harbor or coast from the force of waves.|The breakwater protected the boats from the storm. 防波堤保护船只免受风暴的袭击。|Synonyms: jetty, mole, seawall, groyne, barrier|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "open sea")|noun
