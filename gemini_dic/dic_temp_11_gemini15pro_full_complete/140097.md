off-label:/ˈɔːfˈleɪbl/ /ˈɑːfˈleɪbl/
adjective
标签外的；未经核准的；非适应症的
Use of a drug for a condition not listed on its official label.  A medication is used in a manner not specified in the FDA's approved packaging label, or insert.
Doctors sometimes prescribe drugs "off-label"—that is, for conditions other than those the drugs were officially approved to treat. 医生有时会“超适应症”开药——也就是说，用于治疗药物未被正式批准的疾病。

The drug is being used off-label to treat migraines.  这种药物正被超适应症用于治疗偏头痛。

Synonym:unapproved, unlicensed, unsanctioned
Antonym:approved, licensed, on-label
词性:adjective
