hexamerous:/hɛkˈsæmərəs/
| adj. |六数的，六基数的|Having six parts, especially six parts in each whorl of a flower (sepals, petals, stamens, etc.). |例句：The hexamerous flower is a characteristic of the lily family.  百合科的特征是六数花。

例句：Hexamerous symmetry is common in monocots.  单子叶植物中六基数对称很常见。|近义词：six-parted|反义词:|词性:形容词
