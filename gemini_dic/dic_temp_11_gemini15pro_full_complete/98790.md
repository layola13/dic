integrating:/ˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪŋ/| adj. v.|整合的，一体化的; 整合，合并|combining two or more things so that they work together; becoming part of a group or society|例句：The company is integrating its new software into its existing systems.  公司正在将其新软件整合到现有系统中。

例句：Integrating diverse perspectives is crucial for innovation. 整合不同的观点对于创新至关重要。|近义词：combining, merging, unifying, incorporating, assimilating|反义词：separating, dividing, segregating|词性: adjective, present participle of "integrate"

integrate:/ˈɪntɪɡreɪt/| v.| 整合，合并; （使）融入（社会）|to combine two or more things so that they work together; to become part of a group or society|例句：The school is working to integrate students with special needs. 学校正在努力使有特殊需要的学生融入进来。

例句：He found it difficult to integrate into the new community. 他发现很难融入新的社区。|近义词：combine, merge, unify, incorporate, assimilate|反义词：separate, divide, segregate|词性: verb


词源: From Latin integratus, past participle of integrare "to make whole," from integer "whole."
