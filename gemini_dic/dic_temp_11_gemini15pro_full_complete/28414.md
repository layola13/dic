carboniferous:/ˌkɑːrbəˈnɪfərəs/| adj. n.|石炭纪的，含碳的；石炭纪|Relating to or denoting the fifth period of the Palaeozoic era, between the Devonian and Permian periods, lasting from about 359 to 299 million years ago.  Also means containing carbon or coal.|The Carboniferous period is known for its vast coal deposits. 石炭纪以其大量的煤炭沉积而闻名。|Synonyms: coal-bearing, carbon-rich|Antonyms: non-carbonaceous|adjective, noun
