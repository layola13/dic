double-faults:/ˈdʌbəl fɔːlts/ | n. | 双误；两次发球失误 |  In tennis, a double fault occurs when a player fails to legally serve two consecutive attempts. |  He lost the match after serving two double-faults in the final game. 他在最后一局比赛中两次双误，输掉了比赛。 | synonyms: double service faults | antonyms: ace, successful serve | noun

double-faults:/ˈdʌbəl fɔːlts/ | v. | 双误；两次发球失误(较少用作动词，通常用名词形式) | To serve two consecutive faults in tennis. | She double-faulted on match point. 她在赛点上双误了。 | synonyms: serve two faults | antonyms: serve an ace | verb
