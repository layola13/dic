belittler:/bɪˈlɪtlər/| n.|轻视者，贬低者|A person who belittles or disparages others or things.  Someone who makes others feel small or unimportant.|He's a belittler who constantly criticizes his colleagues' work. 他是一个轻视者，经常批评同事的工作。|Synonyms: detractor, disparager, critic, denigrator|Antonyms: admirer, praiser, supporter, encourager|noun
