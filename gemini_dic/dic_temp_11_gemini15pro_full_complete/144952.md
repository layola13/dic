overabstracted:/ˌoʊvəræbˈstræktɪd/ | adj. | 过于抽象的，过度抽象化的 |  Characterized by excessive abstraction; removed from concrete realities or specific examples. | The theory was so overabstracted that it was difficult to apply to real-world situations.  这个理论过于抽象，难以应用于现实情况。 | Example: His overabstracted explanation left the students confused. 他过于抽象的解释让学生们感到困惑。| synonyms:  abstract, theoretical, conceptual, notional, intangible | antonyms: concrete, specific, tangible, practical, applied | word type: adjective
