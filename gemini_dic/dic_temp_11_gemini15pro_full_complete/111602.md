lobsters:/ˈlɒbstəz/ (UK) /ˈlɑːbstərz/ (US)| n.|龙虾|A large marine crustacean with five pairs of legs, the first pair of which are modified into large claws. Lobsters are highly valued as food.|例句：We had lobsters for dinner last night. 昨晚我们晚餐吃了龙虾。

例句：The lobsters were kept in a tank at the restaurant. 龙虾被养在餐厅的水箱里。


近义词：crayfish, langoustine|反义词: None (No direct opposite for an animal)|词性: Noun
