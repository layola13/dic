ga-ga:/ˈɡɑːɡɑː/| adj.|痴呆的，糊涂的；过分热衷的，着迷的|Infatuated or obsessed to the point of silliness; also, senile or mentally impaired. |She's completely ga-ga over the new boy band. 她完全迷上了那个新的男孩乐队。|Synonyms: crazy, infatuated, obsessed, dotty, senile|Antonyms: sane, lucid, rational|Adjective

ga-ga:/ˈɡɑːɡɑː/| n.|婴儿语；胡话|Baby talk; nonsense.|The baby was gurgling ga-ga. 婴儿在咯咯地说着婴儿语。|Synonyms: babble, gibberish|Antonyms: sense, logic|Noun
