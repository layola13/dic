bipinnate:/baɪˈpɪneɪt/ | adj. | 二回羽状的 | Twice pinnate; having the primary divisions pinnate, and the pinnules also pinnate.  | The fern had delicate, bipinnate fronds.  这种蕨类植物有精致的二回羽状叶子。 | synonyms: doubly pinnate, twice-pinnate | antonyms: simple, entire | adjective

bipinnated:/baɪˈpɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 二回羽状的 | Twice pinnate; having the primary divisions pinnate, and the pinnules also pinnate.  | The leaves of the Jacaranda tree are bipinnated. 蓝花楹树的叶子是二回羽状的。 | synonyms: doubly pinnate, twice-pinnate | antonyms: simple, entire | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bi- (“two, twice”) + pinnātus (“feathered, winged, pinnate”), perfect passive participle of pinnō (“furnish with feathers or wings”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *pet- (“to fly, to feather”).
