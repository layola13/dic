annexed:/əˈnekst/ |v. | 附加，吞并 | To attach, append, or add, especially to something larger or more important.  To incorporate (territory) into the domain of a city, country, or state. |例句：The small country was annexed by its larger neighbor. 这个小国被其强大的邻国吞并了。

|例句：He annexed a codicil to his will. 他在遗嘱上附加了一个附录。|近义词：attached, appended, added, incorporated, subjoined, appropriated, seized, conquered |反义词:detached, separated, relinquished, ceded |词性:v.
annexed:/əˈnekst/ |adj. | 被吞并的，附加的 | Attached or added, especially to something larger or more important. | 例句：The annexed territories were quickly integrated into the empire. 被吞并的领土很快就被并入了帝国。

|例句：The annexed documents provided further evidence of the conspiracy. 附加的文件提供了进一步的阴谋证据。|近义词：attached, appended, added, incorporated, subjoined |反义词:detached, separated |词性:adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *annexus*, past participle of *annectere* "to tie or bind to," from *ad-* "to" + *nectere* "to bind."
