lithography:/lɪˈθɑːɡrəfi/ /lɪˈθɒɡrəfi/
n.|平版印刷术，光刻技术|A method of printing from a flat stone or metal plate on which the image areas are worked using a greasy substance so that the ink will adhere to them by, while the non-image areas are made ink-repellent.|Lithography is widely used in the printing industry. 平版印刷术在印刷行业被广泛使用。
|printing, engraving|photocopying|noun
lithography:/lɪˈθɑːɡrəfi/ /lɪˈθɒɡrəfi/
n.|光刻|A method of printing integrated circuits on silicon chips.|Modern computer chips are made using lithography. 现代计算机芯片是用光刻技术制造的。
|microfabrication, nanofabrication|photography|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *lithographie*, from Ancient Greek *λίθος* (líthos, “stone”) + *γράφειν* (gráphein, “to write”).
