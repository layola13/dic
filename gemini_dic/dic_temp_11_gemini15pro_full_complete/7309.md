anguish:/ˈæŋɡwɪʃ/| n.|极度痛苦，苦恼|Extreme mental or physical suffering.|She cried out in anguish when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息时痛苦地哭了出来。|Synonyms: agony, torment, distress, suffering|Antonyms: joy, comfort, peace, relief|noun

anguish:/ˈæŋɡwɪʃ/| v.|使极度痛苦，使苦恼|To cause or feel extreme mental or physical suffering.|The thought of her missing child anguished her. 她失踪孩子的念头使她痛苦不堪。|Synonyms: torment, torture, distress|Antonyms: comfort, soothe, relieve|verb
