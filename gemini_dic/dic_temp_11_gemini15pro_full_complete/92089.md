hyperrealize:/ˌhaɪpərˈriːəlaɪz/ | v. |过度实现；超现实化；过度认识到|To realize or conceptualize something to an excessive or exaggerated degree, often to the point of distortion or artificiality.| The media has hyperrealized the dangers of air travel, making people more fearful than they need to be. 媒体过度渲染了航空旅行的危险，使人们比实际情况更加恐惧。|  The constant exposure to edited photos online can lead people to hyperrealize beauty standards.  在网上不断接触编辑过的照片会导致人们对美的标准过度认知。 |近义词：exaggerate, overemphasize, distort, amplify |反义词: downplay, minimize, understate |词性:动词
