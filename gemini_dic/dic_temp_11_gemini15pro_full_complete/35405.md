co-optation:/ˌkoʊˌɑːpˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 收编，纳入；增选 | The act of adding someone to a group, especially a governing body, by the invitation of existing members rather than by election or appointment by an outside authority.  |  The co-optation of outside experts can bring valuable perspectives to the board. 收编外部专家可以为董事会带来宝贵的视角。 |  absorption, assimilation, incorporation | exclusion, rejection | noun

co-optations:/ˌkoʊˌɑːpˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 收编，纳入；增选（复数）| Plural form of co-optation, referring to multiple instances of adding someone to a group by invitation. | Several co-optations to the committee were made this year. 今年委员会增选了几名成员。 | absorptions, assimilations, incorporations | exclusions, rejections | noun
