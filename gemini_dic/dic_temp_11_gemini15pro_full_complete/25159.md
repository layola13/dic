buckshot: /ˈbʌkʃɒt/ (UK) /ˈbʌkʃɑːt/ (US)| n. |鹿弹，霰弹|A type of large lead or steel pellet used in shotgun shells.  It is larger than birdshot and is designed to hunt larger game such as deer.|The hunter loaded his shotgun with buckshot. 猎人用鹿弹装填了他的猎枪。|He bagged a deer with a single blast of buckshot. 他一枪霰弹就打到了一只鹿。|Synonyms:  shot, pellets|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
