disserve:/dɪˈsɜːrv/
verb
损害，伤害
to fail to serve (someone or something) adequately or properly; to do harm to
The new policy disserves the interests of the working class.  这项新政策损害了工人阶级的利益。
近义词: harm, hurt, injure, damage, prejudice
反义词: serve, help, benefit, aid, assist
词性: verb

disserve:/dɪˈsɜːrv/
词源:  From dis- + serve.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of".  Therefore, "disserve" literally means "not to serve" or "to serve badly".
中文翻译: 损害，伤害
