charioteers:/ˈtʃæriəˈtɪərz/| n.|战车驾驶员；马车夫|A person who drives a chariot, a two-wheeled horse-drawn vehicle used in ancient warfare and racing.|The charioteers skillfully maneuvered their horses around the track.|近义词：driver, rider, coachman|反义词:passenger|noun
