funnellike:/ˈfʌn(ə)lˌlaɪk/
adjective
漏斗状的;像漏斗的
Resembling a funnel in shape; tapering toward one end.
The tornado formed a funnellike cloud.  龙卷风形成了一个漏斗状的云。
The flower had a funnellike center.  这朵花的花心呈漏斗状。
近义词:funnel-shaped, cone-shaped, tapering
反义词:cylindrical, widening, expanding
词性:adjective

词源:
From "funnel" + "-like."  "Funnel" comes from the Old French "funel," ultimately from the Latin "infundibulum" (funnel).  "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
