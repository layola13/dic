gated:/ˈɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 有门的，有闸门的，封闭式的 | Having a gate or gates; enclosed by a gate or gates. |  例句：The gated community offered a sense of security.  封闭式社区提供了一种安全感。

例句：Access to the gated area is restricted to residents.  只有居民才能进入封闭区域。| 近义词：enclosed, restricted, secure | 反义词：open, public, unrestricted | 词性：形容词

词源: From "gate" (n.) + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). "Gate" comes from Old English "geat" (n.), from Proto-Germanic "*gatan" (n.), related to Old Norse "gat" (n.). The suffix "-ed" derives from Old English "-ed," "-od," or "-ad," used to form adjectives from nouns.
