antifashion:/ˌæntiˈfæʃən/ | n. | 反时尚，非主流时尚 |  A style of dressing or behaving that deliberately avoids or rejects the prevailing fashions. |  She embraced antifashion, preferring vintage clothing and unique accessories. 她崇尚反时尚，喜欢复古服装和独特的配饰。 |  Example:  He wore mismatched socks as a statement of antifashion. 他穿着不配套的袜子以表明他对反时尚的态度。 | Synonyms:  nonconformity, unconventionality, counterculture | Antonyms:  fashion, trend, vogue | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + fashion.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to".  "Fashion" comes from Old French "façon"  meaning "manner," "shape," "make."
