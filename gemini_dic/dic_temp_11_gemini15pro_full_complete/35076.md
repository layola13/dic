closure:/ˈkloʊʒər/ (US) /ˈkləʊʒə(r)/ (UK)| n.|关闭，结束；了结；（情感上的）解脱；闭包|An act of closing something; the condition of being closed.  A feeling that an emotional or unpleasant experience has been resolved.|例句：The closure of the factory resulted in hundreds of job losses. 工厂的关闭导致数百人失业。

例句：She needed closure after the death of her husband. 她需要在丈夫去世后得到解脱。|近义词：ending, completion, conclusion, finish, resolution|反义词:opening, beginning, commencement, start|词性:noun
