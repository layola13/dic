filename:/ˈfaɪlneɪm/| n. |文件名|The name of a file, including the extension, such as document.docx or image.jpg.  A filename allows the operating system and other software to identify and access the specific file.|Please tell me the filename so I can find it.  请告诉我文件名，以便我找到它。|Synonym: file identifier|Antonym: N/A|Noun
