epicurean: /ˌɛpɪˈkjʊəriən/ /ˌɛpɪˈkjʊəriːən/
epicurean: adj. | 好享乐的；讲究饮食的；美食的 | Devoted to the pursuit of pleasure; fond of good food and comfort. | He is an epicurean, always seeking the finest wines and cheeses. 他是个享乐主义者，总是追求最好的葡萄酒和奶酪。 | 近义词: hedonistic, gourmet, gourmand, luxurious, sybaritic | 反义词: ascetic, austere, abstemious | 词性: 形容词

epicurean: n. | 好享乐的人；美食家 | A person devoted to sensual enjoyment, especially good food and drink. |  He considered himself an epicurean, sampling delicacies from all over the world. 他自认为是个美食家，品尝来自世界各地的美味佳肴。 | 近义词: hedonist, gourmet, gourmand | 反义词: ascetic | 词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin *Epicurēus, from Latin Epicūrus + -eus, from Ancient Greek Ἐπίκουρος (Epíkouros, “Epicurus”).  Named after the Greek philosopher Epicurus, whose philosophy emphasized pleasure and happiness.
