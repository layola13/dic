monger:/ˈmʌŋɡər/| n.|贩子，传播者|A person who promotes or spreads something (usually something undesirable).|He's a rumour monger, always spreading gossip. 他是个散布谣言的人，总是传播流言蜚语。|The warmonger was finally brought to justice.  战争贩子最终被绳之以法。|synonyms: dealer, trader, trafficker, peddler, purveyor, promoter, disseminator|antonyms: opponent, critic, detractor|noun
