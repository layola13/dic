layovers:/ˈleɪoʊvərz/ (US), /ˈleɪˌəʊvəz/ (UK)| n. |中途停留，过境停留|A temporary stop in a journey, especially during a flight, often requiring a change of planes or a long wait.|例句：My flight to London has two layovers, one in Paris and one in Frankfurt. 我的飞往伦敦的航班有两个中途停留，一个在巴黎，一个在法兰克福。|例句：I had a long layover in Chicago, so I decided to explore the city. 我在芝加哥有一个很长的中途停留，所以我决定去探索一下这个城市。|近义词：stopover, transfer, connection|反义词:direct flight|词性:名词
