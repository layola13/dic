moev:/muːv/| v.|移动，感动 |To change the position of something or someone; to cause someone to have strong feelings, such as sadness or sympathy.|例句：Could you please move your car?  你能把你的车挪一下吗？

例句：The story moved me to tears.  这个故事让我感动得流泪。|近义词：shift, transfer, stir, touch|反义词:stay, remain, freeze|词性:动词
