durga:/ˈdʊrɡə/| n.|杜尔迦，难近母|A principal Hindu goddess, wife of Shiva, worshipped as the benevolent protector of the righteous and destroyer of the wicked.  Durga is often depicted riding a lion or tiger, with many arms each wielding a weapon.|Example 1:  The festival of Durga Puja is celebrated with great fervor in India.  印度人民热烈庆祝杜尔迦节。

Example 2:  Durga is a powerful symbol of feminine strength and power. 杜尔迦是女性力量和权力的强大象征。|近义词：Devi, Parvati, Shakti|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
