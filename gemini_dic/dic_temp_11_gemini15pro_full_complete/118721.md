metagastric:/ˌmetəˈɡæstrɪk/
adjective
胃后的；位于胃后的
Situated behind the stomach.
The metagastric pouch in the crayfish stores food. 小龙虾的胃后囊储存食物。
近义词:postgastric
反义词:pregastric
词性:adjective
