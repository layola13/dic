debiting:/ˈdɛbɪtɪŋ/
verb
借记；记入借方
The act of recording a debit entry.  This involves increasing the balance of asset, expense, or dividend accounts, and decreasing the balance of liability, owner's equity, or revenue accounts.
The accountant is debiting the customer's account.  会计正在记入客户账户的借方。
charging, entering
crediting
