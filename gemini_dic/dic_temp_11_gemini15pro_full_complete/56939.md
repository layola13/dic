dottable:/ˈdɒtəbl/ (US) /ˈdɒtəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|可加点的，可以打点的|Able to be dotted; suitable for being marked with dots.  This refers to the physical act of marking something with a dot, or to the suitability of a letter for having a diacritic dot placed above or below it.|The lowercase 'i' and 'j' are dottable letters. 小写的'i'和'j'是可加点的字母。| The soft clay was dottable and perfect for making decorative patterns.  这种软粘土可以打点，非常适合制作装饰图案。|markable, pointable|N/A|adjective
