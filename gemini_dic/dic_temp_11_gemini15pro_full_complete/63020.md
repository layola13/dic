enquiry's:/ɪnˈkwaɪəriz/| n.|询问，查询（复数）|This is the possessive form of "enquiry," meaning "belonging to an enquiry" or "of an enquiry."  It refers to something related to a question or investigation. |Example: The enquiry's findings were inconclusive.  该调查的结果尚无定论。|近义词：investigation's, query's, question's|反义词:answer's, solution's|词性:名词


enquiry:/ɪnˈkwaɪəri/| n.|询问，查询|An act of asking for information.|Example: We received numerous enquiries about the job vacancy. 我们收到了许多关于这个职位空缺的询问。|近义词：investigation, query, question|反义词:answer, solution|词性:名词
