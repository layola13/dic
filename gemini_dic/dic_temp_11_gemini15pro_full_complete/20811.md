blasphemously: /blæsˈfɛməsli/ /blæsˈfiːməsli/ | adv. | 亵渎地，不敬地 | In a way that shows disrespect toward something considered sacred. | He spoke blasphemously about the church. 他亵渎地谈论教会。|  She blasphemously cursed the gods. 她亵渎地诅咒了众神。 | 近义词: profanely, sacrilegiously, irreverently | 反义词: reverently, piously, respectfully | 词性: 副词

词源: From Middle English *blasfemously, from Old French blasphemousement, from blasphemer (“to blaspheme”) + -ment (adverbial suffix). Ultimately from Late Latin blasphēmia, from Greek βλασφημία (blasphēmía, “slander, blasphemy”), from βλάσφημος (blasphēmos, “slanderous, blasphemous”), from βλάπτω (bláptō, “I harm, hurt”) + φήμη (phḗmē, “speech, rumor”).
