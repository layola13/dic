confessionalism: /kənˈfɛʃənəlɪzəm/
n.
忏悔主义；教派主义
A system of government in which different religious groups are given political power and influence, often leading to sectarian tensions.  Also, the tendency to divide along religious lines.
The confessionalism of Lebanese politics has often led to conflict. 黎巴嫩政治的教派主义经常导致冲突。

The 19th-century historian Leopold von Ranke analyzed the role of confessionalism in the Thirty Years' War. 19世纪的历史学家利奥波德·冯·兰克分析了忏悔主义在三十年战争中的作用。

近义词: sectarianism, denominationalism
反义词: secularism, non-sectarianism
词性: noun
