cloudest:/ˈklaʊdɪɪst/ | adj. | 最多云的 | Superlative form of cloudy: most cloudy.  The day with the least amount of sunshine due to cloud cover. |  例句：Yesterday was the cloudiest day of the month. 昨天是这个月最多云的一天。

例句：This week has been the cloudiest I can remember. 这周是我记忆中最阴沉的一周。 | 近义词：gloomiest, darkest, overcast | 反义词：brightest, sunniest, clearest | 词性: 形容词


词源: From Middle English *cloudiest, from Old English *cloudigest, superlative of cloudig (“cloudy”). Equivalent to cloudy +‎ -est.
