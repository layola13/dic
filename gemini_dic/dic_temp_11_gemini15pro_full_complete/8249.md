anti-inductiveness:/ˌænti-ɪnˈdʌktɪvnəs/ | n. | 反感应性，抗感应性 | The property of a circuit element or material that opposes the change in current flow, especially the opposition to the establishment of an induced current.  It's often associated with resistance and capacitance. | The anti-inductiveness of the capacitor helped stabilize the circuit. 电容器的反感应性有助于稳定电路。| 近义词: reactance, impedance | 反义词: inductiveness | 词性: 名词
