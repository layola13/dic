myelin: /ˈmaɪəlɪn/ (US), /ˈmaɪəlɪn/ (UK)| n.|髓磷脂;髓鞘|A fatty white substance that surrounds the axon of some nerve cells, forming an electrically insulating layer. It is essential for the proper functioning of the nervous system.|The myelin sheath helps speed up nerve impulses. 髓鞘有助于加速神经冲动。|Multiple sclerosis is a disease that damages myelin. 多发性硬化症是一种损害髓磷脂的疾病。|近义词: myelin sheath|反义词: (No direct antonym, but demyelination refers to the loss of myelin)|词性:noun
