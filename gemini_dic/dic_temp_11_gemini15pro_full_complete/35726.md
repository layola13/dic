coccosteus:/kɒˈkɒstiːəs/ | n. |节颈鱼|An extinct genus of placoderm fish that lived during the Devonian period, known for its large size and bony head shield.|Coccosteus was a fearsome predator of the Devonian seas. 节颈鱼是泥盆纪海洋中可怕的捕食者。|Coccosteus fossils have been found in Scotland. 在苏格兰发现了节颈鱼化石。| 近义词: placoderm (更广义的盾皮鱼纲), Dunkleosteus (另一种盾皮鱼) | 反义词:  无 (因为是已灭绝生物，没有直接的反义词) | 词性: noun

coccostean:/kɒˈkɒstiːən/ | adj. |节颈鱼的|Relating to or characteristic of Coccosteus.|The coccostean fossil was remarkably well-preserved.  这块节颈鱼化石保存得非常完好。|They studied the coccostean jaw structure. 他们研究了节颈鱼的颌骨结构。|近义词: placoderm | 反义词: 无 | 词性: adjective
