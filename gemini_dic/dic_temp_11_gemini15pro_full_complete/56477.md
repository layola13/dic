dolts: /doʊlts/
n.
笨蛋，傻瓜
A stupid or foolish person.
Those dolts locked their keys in the car. 那些笨蛋把钥匙锁在车里了。
synonyms: idiot, fool, moron, imbecile, nitwit, simpleton
antonyms: genius, intellectual, savant
noun
