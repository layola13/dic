calendars: /ˈkælɪndərz/ (US), /ˈkælɪndəz/ (UK) | n. | 日历；日历表 | A system for fixing the beginning, length, and divisions of the civil year and arranging days and longer divisions of time (such as weeks and months) in a definite order.  A chart or register showing the days, weeks, and months of a particular year, or a similar table, often with added information. | I bought two new calendars for next year. 我买了两本日历准备明年用。| synonyms: almanac, schedule, timetable | antonyms: None | noun
