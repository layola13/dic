interferingly:/ˌɪntərˈfɪərɪŋli/
adverb
干扰地；妨碍地
In a way that interferes or obstructs.  Acting as a hindrance or obstacle.
His loud music played interferingly while I was trying to study. 他大声播放音乐干扰了我学习。
Synonyms: obstructively, disruptively, hinderingly, intrusively
Antonyms: helpfully, supportively, constructively
词性: 副词
