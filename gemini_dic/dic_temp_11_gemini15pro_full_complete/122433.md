moating:/ˈmoʊtɪŋ/| n. |护城河，壕沟；形成护城河|The act of surrounding a place with a moat for protection.  用护城河围绕一个地方以保护它。|例句：The moating of the castle made it almost impossible to attack. 城堡周围的护城河使它几乎不可能被攻破。

例句：The moating around the ancient city was still visible. 古城周围的护城河仍然清晰可见。|近义词：ditching, trenching|反义词:filling, bridging|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *motyng, verbal noun from mote (verb) "to dig a moat," from Old French moter "to dig a moat, move earth," from Vulgar Latin *mutāre "to move, change," from Latin mūtāre "to change."
