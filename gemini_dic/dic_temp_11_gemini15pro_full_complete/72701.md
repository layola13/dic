foci's:/ˈfoʊsaɪz/| n.|焦点（复数的所有格）|The possessive form of "foci," which is the plural of "focus." It refers to the belonging of the points called foci.  It implies ownership or association with the foci.|The ellipse's foci's locations determine its shape. 椭圆的焦点的定位决定了它的形状。|近义词:centers' , focal points'|反义词: peripheries'|词性:名词
