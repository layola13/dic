meditater:/ˈmɛdɪˌteɪtər/| n.|冥想者，沉思者|A person who practices meditation.|She is a devoted meditater, spending hours each day in quiet contemplation. 她是一位虔诚的冥想者，每天花几个小时静静地沉思。|Synonyms: contemplator, reflector, ponderer|Antonyms: agitator, disrupter|noun

meditate:/ˈmɛdɪteɪt/| v.|冥想，沉思|To engage in contemplation or reflection.|He meditated on the meaning of life. 他沉思着生命的意义。|Synonyms: contemplate, ponder, reflect, muse|Antonyms: disregard, ignore|verb
