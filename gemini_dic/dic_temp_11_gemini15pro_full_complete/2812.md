advantage:/ədˈvæntɪdʒ/ | vt. | 有利于，使处于优势 |To put in a favorable or more desirable position.| The new trade agreement advantaged both countries. 新的贸易协定对两国都有利。| benefit, help, assist | harm, hinder | verb

advantages:/ədˈvæntɪdʒɪz/ | n. (plural) | 优势，有利条件，益处（复数） |The plural form of "advantage".|  There are many advantages to living in a big city. 住在大城市有很多好处。| benefits, gains, assets | disadvantages, drawbacks | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *avantage* "advantage, profit, superiority,"  from *avantager* "to give an advantage to," from *avant* "before, forward" (from Latin *abante*, from *ab* "from" + *ante* "before") + *-age*.
