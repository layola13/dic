aciculate:/əˈsɪkjʊleɪt/ | adj. | 针状的；细针状的 | Covered with small needle-like prickles. | The aciculate leaves of the cactus help it conserve water. 仙人掌的针状叶子有助于它保存水分。 |  Example 2: The insect was identified by its aciculate antennae.  这种昆虫通过其针状触角被识别出来。 | synonyms: needle-like, needle-shaped, prickly, spiny | antonyms: smooth, soft, rounded | adjective
