interartistic:/ˌɪntərɑːrˈtɪstɪk/
adjective
跨艺术的，艺术间的
Existing or occurring between different forms of art; involving a relationship or interaction between different art forms.
The museum hosted an interartistic exhibition, showcasing the connections between music, painting, and dance.  博物馆举办了一个跨艺术展览，展示了音乐、绘画和舞蹈之间的联系。
The interartistic project combined elements of film, theatre, and visual arts. 这个跨艺术项目结合了电影、戏剧和视觉艺术的元素。
近义词:interdisciplinary, cross-disciplinary, multidisciplinary
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly) specialized, single-discipline
