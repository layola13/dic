irresponsibilities:/ɪˌrɪˌspɒnsɪˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. |不负责任，失责；轻率的行为|lack of responsibility; failure to act responsibly or with due care.  A state or instance of being irresponsible.|  His youthful irresponsibilities cost him dearly later in life. 他年轻时的轻率行为在他日后生活中付出了高昂的代价。| 近义词: carelessness, recklessness, negligence, flippancy | 反义词: responsibility, accountability, diligence, conscientiousness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From irresponsible + -ities.  Irresponsible is formed from in- (not) + responsible.  Responsible ultimately derives from Latin respondēre "to answer, promise."
