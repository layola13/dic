PHRASE->
fork out: 付出（一大笔钱）  He had to fork out a lot of money for the repairs. 他不得不花一大笔钱来修理。
fork over: 交出，让出  Fork over the cash! 把钱交出来！
fork in the road:  岔路  He came to a fork in the road and had to make a decision. 他来到了一个岔路口，不得不做出决定。
fork lift: 叉车  The warehouse uses fork lifts to move heavy pallets. 仓库使用叉车来搬运沉重的货盘。
fork up:  勉强付出  I had to fork up the money for a new tire. 我不得不掏钱买一个新轮胎。
