assimilate:/əˈsɪməleɪt/ | v. | 吸收；同化 | to absorb and integrate (people, ideas, or culture) into a wider society or culture. | The immigrants quickly assimilated into the local community. 移民们很快融入了当地社区。| synonyms: absorb, integrate, incorporate, adapt, adjust | antonyms:  segregate, isolate, reject | verb

assimilate:/əˈsɪməleɪt/ | v. | (食物)消化吸收 | to absorb (food) into the body after digestion. | The body assimilates nutrients from food. 身体从食物中吸收营养。| synonyms: digest, absorb, metabolize | antonyms: excrete, expel | verb


assimilates:/əˈsɪməleɪts/ | n. | 被吸收或同化的事物（通常用复数）| things that have been absorbed or integrated. | The assimilates of different cultures create a vibrant society. 不同文化的融合创造了一个充满活力的社会。| synonyms: acquisitions, incorporations | antonyms: rejections, exclusions | noun
