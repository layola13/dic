desaturating:/diːˈsætʃəreɪtɪŋ/
verb
脱色；降低饱和度
reducing the saturation of a color; making a color less vivid
The software is desaturating the image to make it look more vintage.  这个软件正在降低图像的饱和度，使其看起来更具复古感。
The artist desaturated the painting's colors to create a somber mood.  艺术家降低了画作的色彩饱和度，以营造一种忧郁的氛围。
synonyms: fading, dulling, muting, bleaching, toning down
antonyms: saturating, brightening, intensifying, enhancing
verb

desaturated:/diːˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/
adjective
脱色的；降低饱和度的
having had its saturation reduced; less vivid in color
The desaturated photograph evoked a sense of nostalgia. 这张褪色的照片唤起了一种怀旧之情。
The designer chose a desaturated color palette for the website. 设计师为网站选择了一个低饱和度的调色板。
synonyms: faded, dull, muted, bleached, toned down
antonyms: saturated, bright, intense, vibrant
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reduce") + saturate (verb meaning "to fill completely") + -ing (present participle suffix).  Ultimately from Latin saturare "to fill full."
