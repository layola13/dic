confute:/kənˈfjuːt/
verb
驳倒，反驳
To prove (a person or an assertion) to be wrong.
He confuted their arguments. 他驳倒了他们的论点。
近义词:refute, disprove, debunk
反义词:prove, confirm, corroborate
词性:v.
