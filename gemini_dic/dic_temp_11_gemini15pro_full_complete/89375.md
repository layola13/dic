homophobe:/ˈhɒməfəʊb/ (UK) /ˈhoʊməfoʊb/ (US)| n.| 恐同者，恐同症患者 |A person who has a strong dislike of or prejudice against homosexual people.|He was accused of being a homophobe after making derogatory comments about gay marriage. 他因发表贬低同性婚姻的言论而被指责为恐同者。|近义词：bigot, prejudiced person|反义词：ally, supporter|词性: noun

Etymology: homo- (from Greek homos "same") + -phobe (from Greek phobos "fear")
