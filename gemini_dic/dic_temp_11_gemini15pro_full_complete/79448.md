glitteringly:/ˈɡlɪtərɪŋli/| adv.|闪闪发光地，灿烂地|In a manner that glitters; with brilliance or sparkle.|The diamonds glittered glitteringly under the spotlights. 钻石在聚光灯下闪闪发光。|近义词：sparklingly, brilliantly, dazzlingly|反义词:dully, dimly|词性:副词

词源: From glitter (v.) + -ing (present participle suffix) + -ly (adverb suffix).  The verb "glitter" comes from Old Norse *glitra "glitter, sparkle."
