carpools:/ˈkɑːrpuːlz/ (US), /ˈkɑːpuːlz/ (UK)| n. v.|拼车；共乘；|A system of transportation in which several people share a car, especially to and from work.|Many companies encourage their employees to join carpools to reduce traffic congestion. 许多公司鼓励员工拼车以减少交通拥堵。|近义词：rideshare, car-sharing, lift-sharing (UK)|反义词:driving alone|noun

carpools:/ˈkɑːrpuːlz/ (US), /ˈkɑːpuːlz/ (UK)| v.|拼车；共乘；|To travel together in a car, usually to and from work, sharing the costs and responsibilities.|They carpool to work every day to save money on gas. 他们每天拼车上班以节省汽油钱。|近义词：rideshare, share a ride|反义词:drive alone|verb
