nonfeminists:/ˌnɒnˈfɛmɪnɪsts/ | n. | 非女权主义者 | People who do not identify as feminists or who actively oppose feminist ideals. |  Many nonfeminists misunderstand the goals of the feminist movement. 许多非女权主义者误解了女权运动的目标。 | non-feminists | feminists | noun


Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
feminist (noun): from French féministe, from féminin "feminine"
