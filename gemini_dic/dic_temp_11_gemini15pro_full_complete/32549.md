chippendale: /ˈtʃɪpənˌdeɪl/ | n. adj. |齐本德尔式, 齐本德尔式的家具/家具风格 | A style of furniture and interior decoration popular in England in the mid-18th century, characterized by elaborate carving, cabriole legs, and claw-and-ball feet.  |  例句：The dining room was furnished with exquisite Chippendale chairs.  餐厅里摆放着精美的齐本德尔式椅子。 | 近义词：Rococo, Georgian | 反义词: Modern, minimalist | 词性: 名词, 形容词
