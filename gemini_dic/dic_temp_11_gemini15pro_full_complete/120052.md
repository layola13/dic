millrea: /mɪlˈriːə/ | n. | 千分之一英寸 | A unit of length equal to one thousandth of an inch (0.001 inch).  It is also sometimes referred to as a "thou." | The diameter of the wire is measured in millreas.  这根线的直径是用千分之一英寸来测量的。| The thickness of the sheet metal was only a few millreas. 这块金属板的厚度只有几千分之一英寸。 | synonyms: thou, mil | antonyms: N/A | noun
