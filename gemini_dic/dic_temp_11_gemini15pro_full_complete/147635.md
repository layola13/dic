overpolemical: /ˌoʊvər pəˈlɛmɪkəl/
adjective
过于论战的，过度争论的
Excessively or unnecessarily argumentative or controversial; engaging in excessive polemics.
His overpolemical style alienated even his supporters.  他过于论战的风格甚至疏远了他的支持者。
The blog post was criticized for being overpolemical and lacking substance. 这篇博客文章因过于论战且缺乏实质内容而受到批评。
Synonyms:  contentious, disputatious, quarrelsome, belligerent
Antonyms:  conciliatory, peaceable, agreeable, amicable
词性: adjective
