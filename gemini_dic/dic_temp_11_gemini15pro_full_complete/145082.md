overannotates:/ˌoʊvərˈænəˌteɪts/| v. |过度注释，注释过多|To provide an excessive amount of annotations, often to the point of obscuring the original text or making it difficult to read.| The editor overannotates the manuscript, making it difficult for the author to follow.|Synonyms: overcomment, overexplain|Antonyms: underannotate, underspecify|Verb
