downgrade:/ˌdaʊnˈɡreɪd/ | v.  n. | 降级，降低，贬低 |  to reduce something to a lower grade, rank, or level of importance |  The airline downgraded me from business class to economy. 航空公司把我从商务舱降级到经济舱。 | synonyms: demote, lower, reduce, devalue | antonyms: upgrade, promote, elevate | verb

downgrade:/ˈdaʊnˌɡreɪd/ | n. | 降级，降低 | a reduction in the quality or importance of something | The hotel described the change as a minor downgrade. 酒店将这一变化描述为轻微的降级。 | synonyms: demotion, reduction, devaluation | antonyms: upgrade, promotion, elevation | noun
