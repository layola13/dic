matriarchs:/ˈmeɪtriɑːrks/| n. |女族长，女家长|A woman who is the head of a family or tribe.|例句：The matriarchs of the village held a council to discuss the upcoming harvest. 村里的女族长们召开会议讨论即将到来的收成。

例句：She was a powerful matriarch who ruled her family with an iron fist. 她是一位强势的女族长，以铁腕统治着她的家族。|近义词：materfamilias, matron|反义词:patriarch|词性:noun
