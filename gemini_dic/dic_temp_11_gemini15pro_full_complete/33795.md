circumduct:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌdʌkt/ | vt. |环绕，绕行，旋转|To move (a limb or eye) in a circular motion.|The doctor asked the patient to circumduct his shoulder. 医生让病人旋转他的肩膀。|例句：He circumducted his arm to demonstrate the range of motion. 他旋转他的手臂以展示运动范围。|近义词：rotate, swivel, pivot|反义词:fix, immobilize|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin circumductus, past participle of circumducere ("to lead around"), from circum ("around") + ducere ("to lead").
