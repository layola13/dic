brutism:/ˈbruːtɪzəm/| n.|蛮力主义；野兽主义|A philosophy that emphasizes strength, aggression, and disregard for intellectual or artistic refinement.  It often manifests as a preference for simple, functional designs and raw materials in architecture and other arts.|The stark concrete facade of the building is a classic example of brutism. 这栋建筑的朴素混凝土立面是野兽派的典型例子。|近义词：brutalism,  primitivism|反义词: refinement, elegance|词性:noun
