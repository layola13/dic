half-tearfully: /ˌhɑːfˈtɪrfəli/ | adv. | 半含泪地，几乎要哭地 | Partly tearfully; with tears welling up but not fully flowing. | She smiled half-tearfully as she accepted the award. 她接过奖项时半含泪地笑了笑。|  She waved goodbye half-tearfully. 她半含泪地挥手告别。| 近义词: tearfully, almost crying, weepily | 反义词: cheerfully, joyfully | 词性: 副词

词源:  "half-" (Old English healf) + "tearfully" (tear + -ful + -ly). "Tear" comes from Old English "tear," and "-ful" and "-ly" are suffixes indicating "full of" and "in a manner," respectively.
