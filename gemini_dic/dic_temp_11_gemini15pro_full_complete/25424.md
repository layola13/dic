bulkhead:/ˈbʌlkhed/ | n. | 隔壁; 防水舱壁; |A dividing wall or barrier between compartments in a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle, or in a building.  | The bulkhead between the two cabins was thin. 两个船舱之间的隔板很薄。| synonyms: partition, wall, divider, barrier | antonyms: opening, gap | noun


bulkhead:/ˈbʌlkhed/ | n. | (尤指路边的)挡土墙 | A retaining wall, especially one built at the edge of a road. |  The road was bordered by a high bulkhead. 路边有一堵高高的挡土墙。 | synonyms: retaining wall, embankment, rampart | antonyms: ditch, trench | noun
