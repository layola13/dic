garlicky:/ˈɡɑːrlɪki/ | adj. | 大蒜味的；有蒜臭的 | Having a strong taste or smell of garlic. |  The steak had a distinctly garlicky aroma. 牛排有一股明显的大蒜香味。

The garlicky sauce was perfect with the pasta.  蒜味酱汁和意大利面很搭。 | 近义词: pungent, alliaceous | 反义词: bland, mild | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From garlic + -y.  The suffix "-y" is used to form adjectives indicating possession or likeness.
