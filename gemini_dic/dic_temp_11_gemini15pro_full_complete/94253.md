impermanence:/ɪmˈpɜːrmənəns/| n. |无常，短暂，易变性|The state or fact of not lasting forever; transience.|例句：The impermanence of life is a constant theme in Buddhist philosophy.  人生的无常是佛教哲学中永恒的主题。

例句：She was struck by the impermanence of beauty. 她被美丽的短暂所震撼。|近义词：transience, ephemerality, mutability, instability|反义词：permanence, eternity, stability, immutability|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *impermanentia, from in- (“not”) + permanentia (“permanence”). Ultimately from Latin permanēre (“to continue, endure”) + -ence.
