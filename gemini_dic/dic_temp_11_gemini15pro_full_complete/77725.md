gemmosity:/dʒɛˈmɒsɪti/ | n. | 宝石的性质; 宝石般的光泽 | The quality of being gemmy; resembling a gem, especially in brilliance or richness of color. | The gemmosity of the ruby was breathtaking. 红宝石的光泽令人叹为观止。 | 近义词： brilliance, sparkle, luster, radiance | 反义词： dullness, opacity | 词性: 名词

词源:  From gem + -osity.  "Gem" comes from Old French "geme" and ultimately from Latin "gemma" meaning "bud, jewel". "-osity" is a suffix forming abstract nouns of quality or state.
