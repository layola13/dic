cipolin:/ˈsɪpəlɪn/| n.|青葱石，葱纹大理石|A metamorphic rock, often used decoratively, characterized by green or greenish-white bands or stripes, resembling the layers of an onion. It is a type of crystalline limestone or marble.|The palace floors were made of polished cipolin.|Synonyms: green marble, onion stone|Antonyms: N/A|noun
