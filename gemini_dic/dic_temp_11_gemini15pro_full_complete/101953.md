isn't:/ˈɪzənt/ (US), /ˈɪznt/ (UK)| v.| 不是 | Contraction of "is not".  It's the negative form of the present tense verb "to be" for the third person singular (he, she, it). | He isn't coming to the party. 他不会来参加派对了。| She isn't happy with the results. 她对结果不满意。 | synonyms: ain't (informal), is not | antonyms: is | verb (auxiliary verb, contraction)


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He isn't a doctor. 他不是医生。
The cat isn't sleeping. 猫没有在睡觉。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
She isn't working today. 她今天不工作。
He isn't playing football right now. 他现在没有在踢足球。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
She isn't finished with her homework. 她还没有完成作业。
He isn't been to Paris before. 他以前没去过巴黎。（错误用法，应用hasn't been）

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
He isn't been sleeping well lately.（错误用法，应用hasn't been sleeping）他最近一直没有睡好。
She isn't  been working on that project for very long. （错误用法，应用hasn't been working）她做那个项目的时间并不长。


一般过去时 (Simple Past): (isn't 用于现在时, 以下例句使用wasn't)
He wasn't at home yesterday. 他昨天不在家。
She wasn't happy with the gift. 她对礼物不满意。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous): (isn't 用于现在时, 以下例句使用wasn't)
He wasn't sleeping when I called. 我打电话时他没有在睡觉。
She wasn't watching TV at that time. 她当时没有在看电视。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect): (isn't 用于现在时, 以下例句使用hadn't)
He hadn't finished his work before I arrived. 在我到达之前，他没有完成工作。
She hadn't eaten anything all day. 她一整天都没吃东西。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous): (isn't 用于现在时, 以下例句使用hadn't been)
He hadn't been feeling well for a few days. 他这几天一直感觉不舒服。
She hadn't been working there for very long. 她在那里工作的时间不长。

一般将来时 (Simple Future): (isn't 用于现在时, 以下例句使用won't be)
He won't be attending the meeting. 他不会参加会议。
She won't be happy about this. 她对此不会感到高兴。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous): (isn't 用于现在时, 以下例句使用won't be)
He won't be working tomorrow. 他明天不工作。
She won't be traveling next week. 她下周不旅行。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect): (isn't 用于现在时, 以下例句使用won't have)
He won't have finished the project by next month. 他下个月之前不会完成这个项目。
She won't have arrived by then. 到那时她不会到达。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous): (isn't 用于现在时, 以下例句使用won't have been)
He won't have been working there for long. 他在那里工作的时间不会很长。
She won't have been studying for very long. 她学习的时间不会很长。

It is not my fault. 这不是我的错。
