amelioration:/əˌmiːliəˈreɪʃn/ | n. |改善，改进，改良|The amelioration of working conditions has led to increased productivity. 工作条件的改善导致了生产力的提高。|例句：The government implemented policies for the amelioration of poverty. 政府实施了改善贫困的政策。  The doctor prescribed medication for the amelioration of his symptoms. 医生开了药来缓解他的症状。|近义词：improvement, betterment, enhancement|反义词：deterioration, worsening, decline|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *amelioratio*, from Latin ameliorare "to make better," from ad- "to" + melior "better."
