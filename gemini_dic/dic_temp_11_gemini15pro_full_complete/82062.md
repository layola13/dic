gritstone:/ˈɡrɪtˌstoʊn/ (US) /ˈɡrɪtˌstəʊn/ (UK)| n.|砂岩|A hard, coarse-grained sandstone, typically containing small particles of quartz or feldspar.|The Peak District is known for its dramatic gritstone edges. 皮克区以其壮观的砂岩边缘而闻名。|近义词:sandstone, rock|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider softer rocks like claystone)|noun
