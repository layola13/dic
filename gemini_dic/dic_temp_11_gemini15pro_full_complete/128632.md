nickelized:/ˈnɪkəlaɪzd/
adjective
镀镍的
coated or plated with nickel.
The car had nickelized bumpers. 这辆车的保险杠镀了镍。
synonyms: nickel-plated, nickel-clad
antonyms: uncoated, bare
词性: adjective
