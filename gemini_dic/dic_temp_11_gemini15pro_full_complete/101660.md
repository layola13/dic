irrejectable:/ˌɪrɪˈdʒɛktəbl/
adjective
不可拒绝的，不容置疑的
Not capable of being rejected; impossible to reject.
His offer was irrejectable, given the substantial benefits it promised. 考虑到它承诺的巨大好处，他的提议是不可拒绝的。
The judge's ruling was irrejectable and binding on all parties. 法官的裁决是不可拒绝的，对所有各方都有约束力。
近义词:unimpeachable, undeniable, incontestable
反义词:rejectable, questionable, disputable
词性:adjective

词源:
From ir- (not) + rejectable (capable of being rejected), from reject (to refuse to accept) + -able (capable of being). The root "reject" comes from the Latin "reicere," meaning "to throw back."
