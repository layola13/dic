misbecoming:/ˌmɪsbɪˈkʌmɪŋ/| adj.|不得体的，不合适的， unbecoming|Not appropriate or proper; unsuitable, especially in appearance or behavior.|Her loud laughter was misbecoming in such a solemn setting.  她在如此庄严的场合大声笑，真是不得体。|
The short skirt was misbecoming on her. 这条短裙在她身上不太合适。|Synonyms: inappropriate, unseemly, indecorous, improper, unsuitable|Antonyms: appropriate, fitting, proper, seemly, suitable|adjective
