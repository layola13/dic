contractional:/kənˈtrækʃənəl/| adj.|收缩的，缩略的|Relating to or involving contraction, especially of muscles or words.|The contractional force of the heart pumps blood throughout the body. 心脏的收缩力将血液泵送到全身。|近义词：contractive, shortening|反义词: expansive, dilatory|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *contractionalis*, from Latin *contractio* (contraction) + *-alis* (suffix forming adjectives).  The Latin *contractio* comes from *contrahere* (to draw together), from *com-* (together) + *trahere* (to draw).
