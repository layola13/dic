fufu:/ˈfuːfuː/| n.|富富（西非和中非的一种主食，由木薯或山药捣碎而成）|A staple food in West and Central Africa, made from boiled starchy vegetables such as cassava, yams, or plantains that are pounded into a dough-like consistency.|Fufu is often served with stews and soups. 富富通常与炖菜和汤一起食用。|Near synonyms: pounded yam, fufu flour, dough, paste|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps consider contrasting textures like "liquid," "crispy")|noun

fufu:/ˈfuːfuː/| n.|（俚语）兴奋，激动，狂热|A slang term expressing excitement, enthusiasm, or obsession.|I'm so fufu about this new band! 我对这个新乐队非常着迷！|Near synonyms: excited, enthusiastic, obsessed, thrilled|Antonyms: bored, apathetic, indifferent|noun
