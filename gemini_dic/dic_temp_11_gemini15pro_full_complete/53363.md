disaffiliate:/dɪsəˈfɪlieɪt/
verb
脱离关系，断绝联系
To officially end a connection with a larger group or organization.
The local chapter disaffiliated itself from the national organization.  当地分会脱离了国家组织。
The church disaffiliated from the denomination over theological differences.  由于神学分歧，该教堂脱离了教派。
近义词:disconnect, separate, sever, withdraw
反义词:affiliate, join, unite
词性:v.

disaffiliation:/dɪsəˌfɪliˈeɪʃən/
noun
脱离关系，断绝联系
The act of officially ending a connection with a larger group or organization.
The disaffiliation of the local branch caused a lot of controversy.  当地分支机构的脱离引起了很多争议。
The disaffiliation vote was close, but ultimately successful.  脱离的投票结果很接近，但最终还是成功了。
近义词:separation, severance, withdrawal
反义词:affiliation, union
词性:n.
