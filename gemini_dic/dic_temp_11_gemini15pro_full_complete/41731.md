corrodible:/kəˈrəʊdəbl/ (US) /kəˈroʊdəbl/ (UK)| adj.|可腐蚀的，易腐蚀的|Susceptible to corrosion; able to be corroded or eaten away by chemical action.|Example: Iron is a corrodible metal and will rust if not protected. 铁是一种易腐蚀的金属，如果不加以保护就会生锈。

Example:  The corrodible pipes needed to be replaced with PVC.  这些易腐蚀的管道需要更换成PVC管道。|近义词：rustable, erodible, oxidizable|反义词：corrosion-resistant, non-corrodible, impervious|词性:形容词
