encroachment:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃmənt/ | n. |侵占，侵入，蚕食|An act of slowly or gradually taking away someone else's rights, property, etc. |例句：The encroachment of the sea is threatening the coastal villages.  海水侵蚀正在威胁沿海村庄。

例句：Urban encroachment is destroying valuable farmland. 城市扩张正在破坏宝贵的农田。|近义词：intrusion, invasion, infringement |反义词: retreat, withdrawal |词性: noun
