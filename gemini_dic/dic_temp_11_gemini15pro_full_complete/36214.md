cogitative:/ˈkɒɡɪtətɪv/ /ˈkɑːɡnɪteɪtɪv/ | adj. | 思考的，深思熟虑的 | Relating to the process of thinking and reasoning; thoughtful. |  She spent a cogitative hour contemplating her next move. 她花了整整一个小时深思熟虑她的下一步行动。|Synonyms:  reflective, meditative, contemplative, pensive, introspective | Antonyms: unthinking, impulsive, rash, heedless | 词性: 形容词

cogitate:/ˈkɒdʒɪteɪt/ /ˈkɑːɡnɪteɪt/ | v. | 思考，考虑 | To think deeply about something; meditate or reflect. | He cogitated on the problem for days before finding a solution.  他仔细思考了这个问题好几天才找到解决办法。 | Synonyms: ponder, contemplate, deliberate, muse, ruminate | Antonyms: ignore, disregard, neglect | 词性: 动词

词源: From Latin cogitatus, past participle of cogitare "to think," from co- "together" + agitare "to set in motion, drive, discuss."
