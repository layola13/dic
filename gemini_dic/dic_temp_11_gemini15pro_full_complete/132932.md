nonemanating:/ˌnɒnɪˈmeɪnɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非散发出的，非发射出的 | Not emanating; not emitting or sending out. |  The cave was dark and nonemanating, giving off no light or heat. 这个洞穴黑暗且不散发任何光和热。

The object was strangely nonemanating, showing no signs of radioactivity.  这个物体奇怪地没有发出任何辐射，没有显示出任何放射性的迹象。 | 近义词: non-emitting, non-radiating | 反义词: emanating, emitting, radiating | 词性:形容词

词源:
"Non-" (prefix) + "emanating" (present participle of "emanate")

"Non-" comes from Latin "non" meaning "not."
"Emanate" comes from Latin "emanare," meaning "to flow out," from "e-" (out) + "manare" (to flow).
