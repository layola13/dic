hamperer:/ˈhæmpərər/| n.|阻碍者，妨碍者|Someone or something that hampers someone or something else.  A person or thing that hinders or impedes.|The heavy rain was a real hamperer to our picnic plans.  这场大雨真是阻碍了我们的野餐计划。|Synonyms: hinderer, obstructor, impediment, obstacle|Antonyms: helper, facilitator|noun

Etymology->
From the verb "hamper" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Hamper" comes from the Old North French "hanapier" (to seize, grasp), ultimately from Frankish or another Germanic source.
