lampers:/ˈlæmpərz/
noun
煤油灯; (尤指旧时的)油灯;  (尤指旧时的)路灯; 矿灯;
a lamp, especially an oil lamp, or a light such as a streetlight or miner’s lamp, especially one of an old type
The old lampers flickered in the wind.  那些旧路灯在风中摇曳。
synonyms: lamp, lantern, light
antonyms: darkness
