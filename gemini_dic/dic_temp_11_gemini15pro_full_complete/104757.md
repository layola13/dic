kayak:/ˈkaɪæk/| n. vi.|皮划艇，独木舟|A small, narrow boat with a covered deck, propelled by a double-bladed paddle.|例句：I rented a kayak and paddled around the lake. 我租了一艘皮划艇，绕着湖划行。

kayak:/ˈkaɪæk/| vi.|划皮划艇|To travel in a kayak.|例句：We kayaked down the river for three hours. 我们沿着河划了三个小时的皮划艇。

|近义词：canoe, boat|反义词: None|词性:noun, verb
