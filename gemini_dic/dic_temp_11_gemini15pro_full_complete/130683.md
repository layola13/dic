nonaffiliated:/ˌnɒnəˈfɪlieɪtɪd/
adjective
非附属的；无党派的；独立的
Not officially attached to or connected with an organization or group.
The survey included responses from both affiliated and nonaffiliated voters.  这项调查包括了来自附属政党和无党派选民的回应。
She prefers to remain nonaffiliated with any political party. 她更喜欢保持无党派身份。
近义词:independent, unattached, unaffiliated
反义词:affiliated, connected, associated
词性:adjective

词源:
Prefix "non-" (meaning "not") + "affiliated" (past participle of "affiliate," from Latin "affiliare," meaning "to adopt as a son").  "non-"（表示“不”）+“affiliated”（“affiliate”的过去分词，源自拉丁语“affiliare”，意为“收养为儿子”）。
