classicist: /ˈklæsɪsɪst/ | n. | 古典主义者；古典学者 | A person who studies or is an expert in the classics, especially ancient Greek and Roman literature, art, and culture. |  He is a renowned classicist and has published several books on ancient Greek philosophy. 他是一位著名的古典学者，出版了多本关于古希腊哲学的书籍。

classicist: /ˈklæsɪsɪst/ | adj. | 古典主义的；古典的 | Relating to or characteristic of classicism. |  Her classicist approach to architecture emphasizes symmetry and proportion. 她对建筑的古典主义方法强调对称和比例。

Etymology:  Late 18th century: from French *classiciste*, from classique ‘classical’.

Synonyms: scholar, humanist, academic, intellectual, traditionalist (for noun); classical, traditional, conventional (for adjective)

Antonyms: modernist, avant-garde (for both noun and adjective)
