incriminating:/ɪnˈkrɪmɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
控告的；显示有罪的；牵连的
Making someone appear guilty of a crime.
The police found incriminating evidence in his car. 警方在他的车里发现了显示他有罪的证据。
Synonyms: implicating, accusing, compromising, damaging
Antonyms: exculpatory, exonerating
词性: 形容词
