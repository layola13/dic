cocatalyst:/koʊˈkætəlɪst/| n.|助催化剂；共催化剂|A substance that increases the rate of a chemical reaction without itself undergoing any permanent chemical change, but which is not consumed in the reaction. It works in conjunction with a catalyst.|The cocatalyst enhanced the activity of the primary catalyst, leading to a faster reaction.|Synonyms: promoter, activator|Antonyms: inhibitor, poisoner|Noun
