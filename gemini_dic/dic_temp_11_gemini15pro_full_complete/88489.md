historicists:/hɪˈstɔːrɪsɪsts/ | n. | 历史主义者 |  A person who believes that history is governed by discernible laws, and that it is possible to predict future developments.| The historicists believed they could chart the inevitable course of history. 历史主义者认为他们可以绘制出历史的必然进程。 | 近义词: determinists, historicism advocates | 反义词: presentists, ahistoricists | 词性: noun

historicist:/hɪˈstɔːrɪsɪst/ | adj. | 历史主义的 | Of or relating to historicism; characterized by the belief that history is governed by discernible laws.|  He took a historicist approach to understanding the conflict. 他采取了一种历史主义的方法来理解这场冲突。| 近义词: deterministic, historical | 反义词: presentist, ahistorical | 词性: adjective
