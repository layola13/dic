hematomata's:/ˌhiːməˈtoʊməˌtəz/ |n. | 血肿（复数的所有格）| A hematoma is a collection of blood outside of blood vessels.  Hematomata is the plural form. Hematomata's indicates possession, belonging to the hematomas. | The hematomata's discoloration was alarming. 血肿的变色令人担忧。| Near synonyms: bruises' (more common for surface hematomas), contusions' | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps) healthy tissue | Noun, possessive
