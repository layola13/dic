chessil:/ˈtʃɛsɪl/
n.
Chesil Beach; a pebble beach
A bank or accumulation of pebbles or shingle, especially on the seashore.
The Chesil Beach stretches for 18 miles along the Dorset coast.  切西尔海滩沿着多塞特郡海岸绵延 18 英里。
