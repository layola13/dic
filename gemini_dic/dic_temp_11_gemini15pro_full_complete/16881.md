basilius:/bəˈsɪliəs/| n.|巴兹尔; 巴西略（男子名）|A male given name, from Greek, meaning "king" or "royal". It is a variant of Basil.|Basil was a popular name in the early church. 巴兹尔在早期教会中是一个流行的名字。|近义词：Basil|反义词:N/A|名词

basilius:/bəˈsɪliəs/| adj.|帝王的; 国王的; 王室的|Of or relating to a king or kings; royal. Derived from the Greek word "basileus".|The basilius authority of the emperor was unquestioned. 皇帝的王权不容置疑。|近义词：Royal, Kingly, Regal|反义词:Common, Plebeian|形容词
