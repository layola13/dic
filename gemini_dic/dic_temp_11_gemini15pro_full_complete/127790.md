neighbourer: /ˈneɪbərər/ (British) /ˈneɪbər/ (American)| n. |邻居|A person who lives next door to or very near to another.|例句：My neighbourer often helps me with my garden. 我的邻居经常帮我打理花园。

例句：We had a barbecue with our neighbourers last weekend. 上周末我们和邻居一起烧烤了。


近义词：neighbor (American spelling), next-door neighbor|反义词: stranger|词性: noun
