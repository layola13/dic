luller:/ˈlʌlər/| n.| 哄骗者; 安抚物; 使人平静的东西; 间歇期; 暂时的平静; |Something that lulls or soothes.  A period of calm or quiet. |The gentle rocking of the boat lulled the baby to sleep. 船的轻轻摇晃使婴儿入睡了。|Synonyms: soother, pacifier, calming influence, respite, break|Antonyms: agitator, irritant, disturbance, escalation|noun
