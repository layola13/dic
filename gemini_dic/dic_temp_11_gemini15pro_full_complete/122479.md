mobocratic:/ˌmɑːbəˈkrætɪk/ | adj. |暴民统治的；民众专政的 | Characterized by or subject to rule by the mob; resembling or characteristic of a mobocracy. | The mobocratic atmosphere made reasoned discussion impossible. 暴民统治的氛围使得理性讨论不可能。 | The mobocratic regime quickly descended into chaos. 暴民统治的政权迅速陷入混乱。 | 近义词：anarchic, lawless, tumultuous, unruly | 反义词：democratic, orderly, lawful | 词性:形容词

Etymology: From mob + -cratic (from Greek -kratia, meaning "rule, power").  The word "mob" originally referred to a disorderly crowd and is shortened from mobile vulgus, Latin for "fickle crowd."
