electropositively:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈpɒzɪtɪvli/
adjective
正电性地；电阳性地
In a manner that exhibits electropositivity; tending to lose electrons and form positive ions.
The alkali metals react electropositively with halogens. 碱金属与卤素发生电阳性反应。
Sodium reacts electropositively with chlorine to form sodium chloride. 钠与氯发生电阳性反应形成氯化钠。
Synonyms: readily oxidized
Antonyms: electronegatively
Part of Speech: adverb
