disagreeing:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːɪŋ/
adjective
不同意的；不一致的
Holding a different opinion or having a conflict with someone or something.
They are disagreeing about the best way to proceed. 他们在就最佳的进行方式产生分歧。
The two experts are disagreeing on the cause of the problem. 两位专家对问题的起因意见不一。
近义词:differing, dissenting, disputing, objecting, conflicting
反义词:agreeing, concurring, consenting
词性:adjective
词源:From disagree + -ing, ultimately from dis- + agree.
