marathons:/ˈmærəθɑːnz/| n.|马拉松，耐力比赛|A long-distance running race of 26 miles and 385 yards (42.195 kilometers). Also used figuratively to describe any long, arduous undertaking.|She ran her first marathon last year. 她去年跑了人生中第一个马拉松。|近义词：endurance test, trial, ordeal|反义词:sprint, dash|词性:noun

marathon:/ˈmærəθɑːn/| n. adj.|马拉松，耐力比赛; 马拉松式的，漫长的|A long-distance running race of 26 miles and 385 yards (42.195 kilometers). Also used figuratively to describe any long, arduous undertaking.
