domineer:/ˌdɑːməˈnɪr/| v. |支配；控制；专横跋扈|to assert one's will over another in an arrogant way| He domineered the conversation, not letting anyone else get a word in edgewise. 他控制了整个谈话，不让别人插嘴。|Synonyms: control, dominate, overpower, tyrannize|Antonyms: submit, obey, yield|verb

domineering:/ˌdɑːməˈnɪrɪŋ/| adj. |专横的；跋扈的；盛气凌人的|asserting one's will over another in an arrogant way|She has a domineering personality that makes it difficult to work with her. 她专横跋扈的个性使人很难与她共事。|Synonyms: bossy, tyrannical, overbearing, authoritarian|Antonyms: submissive, meek, humble|adjective
