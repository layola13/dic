annihilate:/əˈnaɪɪleɪt/
verb
消灭，歼灭
To destroy completely; to reduce to nothing.
The enemy army was annihilated in the final battle. 敌军在最后一场战斗中被歼灭了。

The meteor strike annihilated the dinosaurs.  陨石撞击导致恐龙灭绝。

destroy, obliterate, eradicate, wipe out
create, build, establish, preserve
