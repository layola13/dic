hylopathist:/ˈhaɪləʊpeɪθɪst/
n.
森林疗法者；树木崇拜者
A believer in the healing power of trees or forests; a person who practices hylopathy.
The hylopathist led the group on a meditative walk through the woods.  森林疗法者带领大家在树林里进行冥想散步。
