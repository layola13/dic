orca:/ˈɔːrkə/ (US) /ˈɔːkə/ (UK)| n.|虎鲸，逆戟鲸|A toothed whale that is black and white and known for its intelligence and complex social behavior. It is a top predator in the ocean.|例句：The orca breached, its black and white body glistening in the sun. 虎鲸跃出水面，黑白相间的身体在阳光下闪闪发光。

例句：Orcas are highly social animals, living in pods. 虎鲸是高度社会化的动物，群居生活。|近义词：killer whale|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific animal)|词性:noun
