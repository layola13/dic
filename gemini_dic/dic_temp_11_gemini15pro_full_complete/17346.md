beachhead:/ˈbiːtʃhɛd/| n. |滩头阵地，立足点，桥头堡|A position gained by an attacking force which allows further advances to be made; a secure initial position that has been gained and can be used as a base for further advancement. |The company established a beachhead in the European market by buying a small local firm.  这家公司通过收购一家当地小公司，在欧洲市场建立了滩头阵地。

The soldiers secured a beachhead after a fierce battle. 士兵们经过激烈的战斗，确保了滩头阵地。|近义词：foothold, base, stronghold, bridgehead|反义词: None|词性: noun
