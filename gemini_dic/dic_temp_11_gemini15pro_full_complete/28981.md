cartage:/ˈkɑːtɪdʒ/| n.|运费；货物运输；运送|The cartage on the furniture was more than we expected. 家具的运费比我们预期的要多。|The company paid for the cartage of the goods to the warehouse. 公司支付了货物到仓库的运输费用。|近义词：freight, haulage, transportation, shipping|反义词: N/A|noun

Etymology->
From Middle English *cartage, from Anglo-Norman and Old North French *cartage, from Vulgar Latin *carraticum, from Late Latin carrus (“car, cart”).
