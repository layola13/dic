delayering:/diːˈleɪərɪŋ/
noun
去层级化;扁平化
The reduction of the number of levels of staff in a company or organization.
Many companies are now undergoing a process of delayering. 许多公司正在经历一个去层级化的过程。
The delayering of management has led to increased efficiency. 管理层级的精简提高了效率。

近义词: flattening, streamlining, downsizing
反义词:  hierarchizing, layering
