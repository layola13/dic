abasement:/əˈbeɪsmənt/
noun
屈辱，贬低，降低
The state of being brought low in rank, prestige, or esteem; humiliation.
He endured the abasement of having to apologize publicly. 他忍受了必须公开道歉的耻辱。
humiliation, degradation, shame, dishonor, debasement
elevation, exaltation, dignity, honor
n.
