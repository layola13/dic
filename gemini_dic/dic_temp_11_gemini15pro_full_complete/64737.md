escutcheon:/ɪˈskʌtʃən/
n.
盾形纹章，饰板，孔罩
A shield or emblem bearing a coat of arms.  A decorative plate or shield around a keyhole, light switch, etc.
The knight's escutcheon displayed a fierce lion rampant. 骑士的盾形纹章上绘有一只凶猛的跃立雄狮。
The escutcheon around the doorknob was tarnished. 门把手上周围的饰板已经失去光泽了。
Synonym: shield, crest, coat of arms, backplate
Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "blank space" in context of a keyhole cover)
