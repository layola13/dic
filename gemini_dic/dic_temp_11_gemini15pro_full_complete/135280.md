nonparochially:/ˌnɒnpəˈrəʊkiəli/
adverb
不狭隘地，不局限于地方的
Not limited to a narrow or local perspective; open-minded and inclusive.
The university president encouraged students to think nonparochially and engage with global issues.  大学鼓励学生不狭隘地思考，参与全球性议题。
The museum curator sought to acquire art nonparochially, representing diverse cultures and styles.  博物馆馆长试图不狭隘地收购艺术品，以代表不同的文化和风格。
Synonym: broadly, open-mindedly, liberally, inclusively
Antonym: narrowly, provincially, parochially, locally
词性: 副词
