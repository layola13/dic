affirmable:/əˈfɜːməbl/ | adj. | 可证实的，可断言的 |  Capable of being affirmed; that can be declared to be true. | The witness's statement was affirmable.  证人的陈述是可证实的。| Synonyms: verifiable, attestable, confirmable | Antonyms:  unverifiable, deniable, refutable | adjective

affirmable:/əˈfɜːməbl/ | adj. | 可断言的 |  That can be stated positively; assertable. | His claim is affirmable. 他的说法是可以断言的。 | Synonyms: assertable, declarable, maintainable | Antonyms: unassertable,  undeniable, irrefutable | adjective
