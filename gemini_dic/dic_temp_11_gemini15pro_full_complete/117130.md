meandrina:/miˈændrɪnə/| n. |脑珊瑚|A genus of stony corals characterized by large, brain-like structures formed by their meandering ridges and valleys.|Divers admired the massive meandrina coral on the reef. 潜水员欣赏了礁石上巨大的脑珊瑚。|Synonyms: brain coral|Antonyms: N/A|noun

meandrina:/miˈændrɪnə/| n. |蜿蜒曲折|A winding or convoluted course or pattern, resembling the ridges of a brain coral.|The river's meandrina carved a picturesque path through the valley.  这条河蜿蜒曲折地穿过山谷，形成了一幅如画的景象。|Synonyms: meander, convolution, sinuousity|Antonyms: straightness, linearity|noun
