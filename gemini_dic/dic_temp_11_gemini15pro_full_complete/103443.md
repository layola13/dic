一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她正在做什么晚餐？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has he accomplished this year? = 他今年完成了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has she been studying at university? = 她一直在大学里学什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last weekend? = 你上周末做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会时她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they discussed before making a decision? = 他们在做决定之前讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been working on before she got sick? = 她生病前一直在做什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after you graduate? = 你毕业后会做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们在下次会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by the time we get back? = 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
If I knew what he wanted, I would give it to him. = 如果我知道他想要什么，我就会给他。
I wish I knew what the answer was. = 我希望我知道答案是什么。


被动语态:
What was discussed at the conference? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the summer? = 你暑假的计划是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
He didn't tell me what happened. = 他没有告诉我发生了什么。



PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/你好吗？
What the heck? = 搞什么鬼？（语气比 what the hell 轻）
What about...? = ...怎么样？/ ...怎么办？
What for? = 为了什么？
So what? = 那又怎样？


注意事项:  jk 通常只在非正式的网络或短信交流中使用。
