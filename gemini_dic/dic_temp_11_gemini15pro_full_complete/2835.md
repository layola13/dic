adventuresomeness:/ədˌvɛntʃərəsnəs/
|n.|
|冒险精神；爱冒险；大胆|
|The quality or state of being adventuresome; a willingness to take risks and try new things.|
|Her adventuresomeness led her to travel the world solo.|她爱冒险的精神促使她独自环游世界。

|Her adventuresomeness took her on many exciting journeys.|她的冒险精神带她踏上了许多激动人心的旅程。

|Synonym: daring, boldness, intrepidity, adventurous spirit|
|Antonym: timidity, cautiousness, fearfulness|
|noun|
