mitochondrial:/ˌmaɪtəˈkɒndriəl/ /ˌmaɪtoʊˈkɑːndriəl/| adj.|线粒体的|Relating to or characteristic of mitochondria, the organelles in cells responsible for energy production.|Mitochondrial DNA can be used to trace maternal lineage. 线粒体DNA可以用来追踪母系血统。|近义词:None (highly specific)|反义词:None|词性:形容词

mitochondrion:/ˌmaɪtəˈkɒndriən/ /ˌmaɪtoʊˈkɑːndriən/| n.|线粒体|An organelle found in large numbers in most cells, in which the biochemical processes of respiration and energy production occur. It has a double membrane, the inner layer being folded inward to form layers (cristae).|The mitochondrion is the powerhouse of the cell. 线粒体是细胞的能量工厂。|近义词:None (highly specific)|反义词:None|词性:名词
