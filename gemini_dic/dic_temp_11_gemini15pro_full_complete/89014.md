hollowing:/ˈhɒləʊɪŋ/ (US: /ˈhɑːloʊɪŋ/)| v. n. adj.|挖空，掏空；空洞化；（声音）空洞的|The process of making something hollow, or the state of being hollow.  It can also describe a hollow sound.|
The wind was hollowing out the sand dunes. 风正掏空沙丘。
The artist is hollowing out the wood to create a sculpture. 艺术家正在挖空木头来创作雕塑。

The hollowing sound of the wind was eerie. 风的空洞声很怪异。
The economic downturn led to the hollowing out of the manufacturing sector. 经济衰退导致制造业空洞化。

The hollowing of the tree trunk provided a home for several small animals. 树干的空洞为几只小动物提供了一个家。
Years of neglect resulted in the hollowing out of the once vibrant community. 多年的忽视导致曾经充满活力的社区空洞化。


近义词:excavating, scooping, emptying, gutting, hollow|
反义词:filling, packing, stuffing|
词性:verb, noun, adjective


词源:From Middle English *holwen, from Old English holgian (“to hollow, excavate, pierce”), from Proto-Germanic *hulwijaną (“to hollow”), from *hulwaz (“hollow”). Cognate with Old Frisian holgia (“to hollow”), Old Saxon holgian (“to hollow”), Dutch uithollen (“to hollow out”), German aushöhlen (“to excavate”), Swedish hölja (“to cover, sheath”). Related to hollow.
