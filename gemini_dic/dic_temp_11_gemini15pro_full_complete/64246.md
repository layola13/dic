equipendency:/ˌiːkwɪˈpɛndənsi/ | n. |均势;均力;等势;等势性|The state or condition of being equipotent or equally potent.  A balance of power or influence.|The equipendency between the two nations prevented either from gaining a decisive advantage. 这两个国家之间的均势阻止了任何一方获得决定性优势。|近义词：equilibrium, balance, parity|反义词:imbalance, disparity|noun
