disjunction:/dɪsˈdʒʌŋkʃən/| n. |分离，分裂，析取|A disjunction is a lack of connection between two things.|The disjunction between theory and practice is a common problem.|近义词：separation, division, disunion, gap, disconnect|反义词：conjunction, union, connection|noun

disjunction:/dɪsˈdʒʌŋkʃən/| n. |（逻辑）析取，逻辑“或”|In logic, disjunction is a logical connective typically notated as ∨ whose meaning either refines or corresponds to that of natural language expressions such as "or".|The statement "A ∨ B" is true if A is true, or if B is true, or if both A and B are true.|近义词：OR|反义词：AND, conjunction|noun
