malayalams:/ˌmɑːləˈjɑːləmz/ | n. | 马拉雅拉姆语（复数形式，通常用单数）| Malayalam is a Dravidian language spoken in the Indian state of Kerala. |  Many Keralites speak Malayalams. 许多喀拉拉邦人说马拉雅拉姆语。| synonym: Malayalam (singular form) | antonym: N/A | noun

Etymology: From Malayalam മലയാളം (malayāḷam), from Malaya (mountain) + āḷam (region, depth).  Ultimately from Proto-Dravidian *mala (mountain).
