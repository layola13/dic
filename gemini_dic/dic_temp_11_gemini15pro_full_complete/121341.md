misgiving:/ˌmɪsˈɡɪvɪŋ/
n.
疑虑，担忧
a feeling of doubt or apprehension about the outcome or consequences of something.
例句：I had some misgivings about accepting the job. 我对接受这份工作有些疑虑。

例句：She expressed misgivings about the new plan. 她对新计划表示担忧。

近义词：doubt, apprehension, qualm, reservation, suspicion, worry, anxiety
反义词：confidence, certainty, assurance
词性：名词
