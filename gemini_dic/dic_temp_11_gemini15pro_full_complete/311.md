abet:/əˈbɛt/ | vt. | 教唆，煽动，怂恿 | To encourage or assist (someone) to do something wrong, in particular, to commit a crime or other offense. | He was abetted in the deception by his wife. 他妻子怂恿他进行欺骗。 |  近义词: encourage, support, assist, aid, promote, incite, instigate | 反义词: discourage, dissuade, hinder, obstruct, impede | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *abeter "to bait," from à "to" (from Latin ad) + beter "to bait."  The original sense was "to cause a dog to bite by setting it on," hence, "to incite."
