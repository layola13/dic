ghess:/ɡɛs/ | n. | 推测，猜测 | A guess is an attempt to form an opinion or give an answer about something when you do not know much or anything about it. |  I took a guess at her age. 我猜了猜她的年龄。

| 近义词: conjecture, supposition, surmise, hypothesis, estimate | 反义词: certainty, knowledge | 词性: 名词


ghess:/ɡɛs/ | v. | 猜测，估计 | To guess is to form an opinion or give an answer about something when you do not know much or anything about it. | I guessed her age. 我猜了她的年龄。

| 近义词: conjecture, surmise, speculate, estimate, hypothesize | 反义词: know, ascertain | 词性: 动词


词源:  From Middle English *gessen, from Old Norse *geta ("to get, obtain, beget, guess"), from Proto-Germanic *getaną (“to get, obtain”). Cognate with Scots get (“to guess”), Swedish gissa (“to guess”), Icelandic geta (“to be able to, can, may, guess”).  Related to get.
