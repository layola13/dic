cawing:/ˈkɔːɪŋ/ (US), /ˈkɔːɪŋ/ (UK)| n. v.| 鸦叫声，发出鸦叫声|The sound a crow makes; the act of making that sound.|The cawing of the crows woke me up early this morning. 清晨，乌鸦的叫声把我吵醒了。|The crows were cawing loudly in the trees. 乌鸦在树上大声地叫着。|近义词：croaking, squawking|反义词:chirping, tweeting|词性:noun, verb
Etymology:  From the imitative verb "caw."  Ultimately of Germanic origin.
