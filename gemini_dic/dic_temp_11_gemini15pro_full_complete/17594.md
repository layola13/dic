beautifies:/ˈbjuːtɪfaɪz/
verb
美化，使变美
to make something beautiful or more beautiful.
The flowers beautify the garden.  这些花美化了花园。
She beautifies herself with makeup. 她用化妆品来美化自己。
synonyms: embellish, adorn, enhance, decorate, ornament
antonyms: uglify, mar, spoil, disfigure
