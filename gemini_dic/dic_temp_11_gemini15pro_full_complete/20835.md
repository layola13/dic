blastodermatic:/ˌblæstəˈdɜːrmətɪk/
adjective
胚盘的, 囊胚层的
Relating to or derived from the blastoderm.  The blastoderm is a layer of cells formed in the early development of an embryo, forming the wall of the blastula.
The blastodermatic vesicle develops into the embryo.  胚盘囊泡发育成胚胎。
The researchers studied the blastodermatic stage of chicken development. 研究人员研究了鸡胚胎发育的囊胚层阶段。
Synonyms: blastodermic
Antonyms: None
