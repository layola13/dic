jaundice: /ˈdʒɔːndɪs/ (UK) /ˈdʒɑːndɪs/ (US)| n.|黄疸|A medical condition in which the skin, whites of the eyes, and mucous membranes turn yellow because of a high level of bilirubin in the blood.|The baby was born with jaundice.  这个婴儿出生时患有黄疸。|Synonyms: icterus|Antonyms: None|Noun
