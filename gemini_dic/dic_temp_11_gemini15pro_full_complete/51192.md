distemper:/dɪˈstɛmpər/ /dɪsˈtɛmpər/ | n. vt.|犬瘟热；缓和；坏脾气；颜料 |A viral disease affecting dogs and other animals, typically marked by fever, coughing, and vomiting. Also, a method of painting using pigments mixed with water and glue, or a state of ill humor. |The puppy died of distemper.  这只小狗死于犬瘟热。
The artist used distemper to paint the mural.  这位艺术家用蛋彩画绘制了壁画。
He was in a distemper after losing his job. 他失业后脾气很坏。|近义词：illness, disease (for the disease sense); paint, pigment (for the art sense); bad mood, anger (for the ill humor sense)|反义词：health, wellness (for the disease sense); good mood, happiness (for the ill humor sense)|词性: 名词, 动词

distemper:/dɪˈstɛmpər/ /dɪsˈtɛmpər/ | vt.|使缓和；使平静；调和（颜色）|To moderate or subdue; to mix (pigments).|He tried to distemper his anger. 他试图平息自己的怒火。
The artist distempered the paint with water.  艺术家用水调和颜料。|近义词：moderate, subdue, soothe, temper, blend, mix|反义词：aggravate, intensify, incite|词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *destemprer* "to upset the proper mixture or balance of humors," from *des-* "dis-" + *temprer* "to temper," from Latin *temperare* "to mix in due proportion, qualify, moderate."
