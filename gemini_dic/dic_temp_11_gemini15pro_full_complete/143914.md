outpreaching: /ˌaʊtˈpriːtʃɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的讲道；在讲道方面超过
Surpassing someone in preaching; exceeding someone in the fervor or effectiveness of preaching.
He was known for outpreaching all the other ministers in the area. 他以讲道胜过该地区所有其他牧师而闻名。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel
反义词:  underperform, fall short of
词性: verb (动词)
