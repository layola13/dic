dutiful:/ˈdjuːtɪfəl/ | adj. | 尽职的，顺从的 | Conscientiously performing one's duties; obedient and respectful. | She is a dutiful daughter who always takes care of her parents. 她是一个孝顺的女儿，总是照顾她的父母。|  He was a dutiful employee, always arriving on time and completing his tasks efficiently. 他是一位尽职的员工，总是准时上班，高效地完成任务。 | 近义词: obedient, respectful, responsible, conscientious | 反义词: disobedient, disrespectful, irresponsible, negligent | 词性: 形容词
词源: From duty + -ful.  Ultimately from Latin debitum "that which is owed."
