discourtship: /dɪˈskɔːrtʃɪp/ /dɪˈskɔːrtʃɪp/
n.
求爱；追求；求婚；（鸟类、昆虫等的）求偶
The period of time during which a couple develop a romantic relationship before getting married, especially including the period between when they start going out together and when they get engaged.
例句：Their discourtship was brief and passionate. 他们的恋爱期短暂而热烈。

例句：The male bird performed an elaborate discourtship dance. 雄鸟表演了一场精心设计的求偶舞。


近义词：courtship, wooing, pursuit, romance
反义词：rejection, spurning, estrangement
词性：名词
