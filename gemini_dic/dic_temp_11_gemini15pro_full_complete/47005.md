dashiki: /dəˈʃiːkiː/
n.
达西基, 一种宽松的，通常色彩鲜艳的衬衫或外衣，起源于西非。
A loose-fitting, often brightly colored pullover shirt or outer garment, originating in West Africa.
例句：He wore a vibrant dashiki to the festival.  他穿着一件鲜艳的达西基去参加节日。

例句：The dashiki is a symbol of African pride. 达西基是非洲自豪的象征。

近义词：shirt, tunic, garment
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "Western clothing" in a broad context)
词性: noun
