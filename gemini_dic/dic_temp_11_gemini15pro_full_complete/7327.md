angustate:/æŋˈɡʌsteɪt/
verb
使狭窄；使收缩；使痛苦
to make narrow or constricted; to cause distress
The drought angustated the river, reducing it to a mere trickle.  干旱使河流变窄，只剩下一条细流。
His chest felt angustated, making it difficult to breathe. 他感到胸部收缩，呼吸困难。
近义词: constrict, narrow, compress, restrict, contract, straiten, shrink
反义词: widen, broaden, expand, dilate
词性: verb
