nonvexatiously: /ˌnɒnˌvɛkˈseɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 无恶意地，不骚扰地 | Without causing vexation or annoyance; in a manner that does not harass or provoke. |  He nonvexatiously pointed out the errors in her report. 他善意地指出了她报告中的错误。|  He handled the dispute nonvexatiously, seeking a peaceful resolution. 他以不引起争端的方式处理了这场纠纷，力求和平解决。 | 近义词: peaceably, amicably, inoffensively | 反义词: vexatiously, annoyingly, provocatively | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Latin, meaning "not"
vexatious (adjective): From Late Latin vexatiosus, from Latin vexare "to shake, jolt, toss, agitate, trouble, distress, harass," frequentative of vehere "to carry, convey."
-ly (suffix): Forms adverbs from adjectives.
