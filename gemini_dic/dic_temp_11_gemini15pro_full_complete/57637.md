draughtswomen:/ˈdrɑːftsˌwɪmɪn/
n.
女绘图员，女制图员
A woman who makes detailed technical drawings or plans, typically of machinery or buildings.
The draughtswomen meticulously rendered the blueprints for the new bridge.  女绘图员一丝不苟地绘制了新桥的蓝图。
The company employed several draughtswomen to prepare the architectural drawings.  该公司雇佣了几位女绘图员来绘制建筑图纸。
近义词: draftswomen, draftsperson, drafter, designer
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "architect" or "engineer" if referring to the person giving instructions to the draughtswoman)
词性: noun
