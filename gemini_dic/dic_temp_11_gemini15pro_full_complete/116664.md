mattins:/ˈmætɪnz/ | n. |晨祷，早课|A service of morning prayer, especially in the Anglican Church.|例句：The monks gathered for mattins at dawn. 修士们黎明时分聚集做晨祷。

例句：She always attended mattins on Sundays. 她星期天总是参加晨祷。|近义词：morning prayer, lauds|反义词: vespers, evensong|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *matines*, from Late Latin *matutinum* (office), neuter of *matutinus* "of the morning," from *Matuta*, goddess of the dawn.
