goat:/ɡoʊt/ (US) /ɡəʊt/ (UK)| n.|山羊；替罪羊|A domesticated ruminant mammal with backward-curving horns, a beard, and a short tail.|The farmer has a herd of goats.  那个农民有一群山羊。|
goat:/ɡoʊt/ (US) /ɡəʊt/ (UK)| n.|替罪羊|Someone who is blamed for the mistakes or failures of others.|He was made the goat for the team's loss. 他成了球队失利的替罪羊。|
