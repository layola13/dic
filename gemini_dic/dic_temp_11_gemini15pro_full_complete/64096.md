eponymous:/ɪˈpɒnɪməs/ /əˈpɑːnɪməs/| adj.|同名的；以此命名的|Giving their name to something; named after someone or something.|The museum is dedicated to the eponymous founder.  这座博物馆是为了纪念与其同名的创始人而建的。|例句：The Ford Foundation, established by the eponymous Henry Ford, is a philanthropic organization. 福特基金会，由同名的亨利·福特创立，是一个慈善组织。

例句：The restaurant is eponymous with its owner, a famous chef. 这家餐厅与其所有者同名，他是一位著名的厨师。|近义词：namesake, titular|反义词:|adjective
