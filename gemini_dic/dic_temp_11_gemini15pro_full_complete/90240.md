house-warming:/ˈhaʊsˌwɔːrmɪŋ/
n.
乔迁庆宴，温居宴
A party given by someone who has just moved into a new house.
We're having a house-warming party next Saturday.  我们下周六要举行一个乔迁派对。

We went to a house-warming party at their new place. 我们去他们的新家参加了一个乔迁派对。

近义词：housewarming party,  celebration
反义词:  (No direct antonym)
词性: 名词
