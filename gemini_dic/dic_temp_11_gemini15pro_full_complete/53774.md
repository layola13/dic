discommission:/ˌdɪskəˈmɪʃən/
verb
使退役；使停用
To officially take something, especially a ship, aircraft, or machine, out of active service.
The aircraft carrier was decommissioned in 1998.  这艘航空母舰于1998年退役。
The power plant was decommissioned after the accident.  事故发生后，这家发电厂被停用。
deactivate, retire, withdraw
commission, activate
