humidnesses:/hjuːˈmɪdnəsɪz/ | n. | 湿度；潮湿 | The plural form of "humidness," referring to multiple instances or levels of humidity.  It's not commonly used; "humidity" is generally preferred. | The humidnesses of the different rainforests varied significantly. 不同雨林的湿度差异很大。| 近义词: humidities (more common), dampness, moisture | 反义词: dryness, aridity | 词性: Noun


Etymology:  From humid + -ness.  "Humid" comes from Late Latin "humidus" meaning "moist," ultimately from Latin "humere" meaning "to be moist."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
