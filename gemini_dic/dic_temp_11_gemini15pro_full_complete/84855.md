hangdog:/ˈhæŋdɒɡ/ (US) /ˈhæŋdɒɡ/ (UK)| adj.| 愧疚的；沮丧的；垂头丧气的 | Having a dejected or guilty appearance, like a dog with its tail between its legs. | He had a hangdog expression on his face after being caught cheating.  他作弊被抓后，脸上带着愧疚的表情。 |  synonyms: ashamed, guilty, dejected, downcast | antonyms: cheerful, happy, upbeat | adjective
