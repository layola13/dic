lizards:/ˈlɪzərdz/
n.
蜥蜴
reptiles that typically have long bodies and tails, four legs, movable eyelids, and a rough, scaly, or spiny skin.
Lizards are common in warm climates.  蜥蜴在温暖的气候中很常见。
The lizard darted across the rock.  蜥蜴飞快地穿过岩石。
reptiles, geckos, skinks
(无常用反义词)
