conicity:/kəˈnɪsɪti/ | n. |锥度，锥形度|The degree to which something is conical; the ratio of the difference in diameter between the two ends of a cone to its length.|The conicity of the drill bit was too high, causing it to bind in the hole.|近义词: taper, slope, incline |反义词: cylindricality |词性: noun
