mile:/maɪl/| n.|英里|A unit of linear measure equal to 5,280 feet (approximately 1.609 kilometers).|例句：The marathon is 26.2 miles long. 马拉松全程26.2英里。

例句：We drove for miles and miles. 我们开了好几英里。|近义词：league, kilometer|反义词:inch, millimeter|词性:noun
