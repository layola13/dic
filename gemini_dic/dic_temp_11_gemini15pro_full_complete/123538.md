monotype:/ˈmɒnəˌtaɪp/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəˌtaɪp/ (US)| n. adj.|单一字体；单版；单型印刷|A single typeface, a single specimen, or a print made by a process involving painting or drawing on a smooth, non-absorbent surface and then transferring the image to paper.|The artist created a beautiful monotype using vibrant inks.|近义词:unique print, one-off print|反义词:edition, multiple|词性: noun, adjective

monotype:/ˈmɒnəˌtaɪp/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəˌtaɪp/ (US)| adj.|单型的|Of or relating to a single type or specimen.|The exhibition featured a collection of monotype prints.|近义词:single, unique|反义词:multiple, varied|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From mono- (one) + type (kind, sort).  Ultimately from Greek monos (single, alone) and typos (impression, mark, type).
