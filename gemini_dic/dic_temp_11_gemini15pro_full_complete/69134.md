favorless:/ˈfeɪvərlɪs/
adjective
无味的；淡而无味的；不可口的
Lacking flavor; bland or insipid.
The soup was utterly favorless, like dishwater. 这汤寡淡无味，像洗碗水一样。
Near Synonyms: bland, insipid, tasteless, unflavored, unseasoned
Antonyms: flavorful, tasty, savory, delicious, palatable
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From favor (meaning "taste, flavor") + -less (suffix indicating absence of).  The word "favor" in this sense is now archaic, replaced by "flavor."
