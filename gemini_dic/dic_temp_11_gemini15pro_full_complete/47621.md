deber:/deˈber/| v. |应该，必须 (Spanish)| This word is Spanish for "should" or "must," expressing obligation or duty.| Debes estudiar para el examen. You should/must study for the exam.| 近义词: tener que, necesitar| 反义词: no deber| 词性: verb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Debo estudiar todos los días. = I should study every day.
Él debe trabajar para mantener a su familia. = He must work to support his family.

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Estoy debiendo mucho dinero. = I am owing a lot of money. (This usage is less common for expressing obligation and more common for expressing debt)
¿Estás debiendo algún favor? = Do you owe any favors?

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He debido estudiar mucho este semestre. = I have had to study a lot this semester.
Ella ha debido trabajar horas extras. = She has had to work overtime.

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
He estado debiendo dinero por mucho tiempo. = I have been owing money for a long time. (Again, this refers to debt, not obligation)
Hemos estado debiendo favores a nuestros vecinos. = We have been owing favors to our neighbors.

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Debí estudiar más para el examen. = I should have studied more for the exam.
Ella debió llegar temprano a la reunión. = She should have arrived early to the meeting.

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Estaba debiendo dinero cuando perdí mi trabajo. = I was owing money when I lost my job. (Debt context)
Estaban debiendo favores a sus amigos. = They were owing favors to their friends.

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Había debido estudiar antes de la fiesta. = I should have studied before the party.
Habían debido trabajar horas extras para terminar el proyecto. = They had had to work overtime to finish the project.

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Había estado debiendo dinero durante años. = I had been owing money for years. (Debt context)
Habían estado debiendo favores a sus familiares. = They had been owing favors to their relatives.

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Deberé estudiar mucho para el próximo examen. = I will have to study a lot for the next exam.
Ella deberá trabajar el fin de semana. = She will have to work on the weekend.

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Estaré debiendo dinero durante un tiempo. = I will be owing money for a while. (Debt context)
Estarán debiendo favores a sus colegas. = They will be owing favors to their colleagues.


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Habré debido terminar el proyecto para el viernes. = I will have had to finish the project by Friday.
Habrán debido trabajar muchas horas para lograrlo. = They will have had to work many hours to achieve it.

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
Habré estado debiendo dinero por cinco años. = I will have been owing money for five years. (Debt context)
Habrán estado debiendo favores a sus mentores. = They will have been owing favors to their mentors.


虚拟语气:
Si debiera ir, iría. = If I should go, I would go.
Ojalá debiera saber la respuesta. = I wish I should know the answer.

被动语态 (Not applicable in its core meaning of obligation.  Can be used in passive voice when referring to debt: "El dinero es debido." = "The money is owed.")


疑问句:
¿Debo ir a la fiesta? = Should I go to the party?
¿Debe ella trabajar mañana? = Must she work tomorrow?

否定句:
No debo ir a la fiesta. = I should not go to the party.
Ella no debe trabajar mañana. = She must not/should not work tomorrow.



PHRASE->
deber de + infinitive = probably, must (e.g., Debe de estar cansado. = He must be tired.)
deber + infinitive = should, ought to, must
no deber + infinitive = should not, must not
debería + infinitive = should (more polite/less forceful than debo)
¿Qué debo hacer? = What should I do?


注意事项:  "Deber" is a very common and important verb in Spanish. Understanding its various uses and tenses is essential for fluency. Note that the continuous tenses are less common when expressing obligation.
