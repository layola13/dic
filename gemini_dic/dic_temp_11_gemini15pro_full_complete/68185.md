faintling:/ˈfeɪnt.lɪŋ/
n.|弱者，胆小鬼|A timid or feeble person.|He was such a faintling, he couldn't even stand up to a bully. 他真是个胆小鬼，连面对恶霸都站不起来。|近义词:weakling, coward|反义词:hero, strongman|词性:名词

Etymology: From "faint" (weak) + "-ling" (a diminutive suffix indicating smallness or insignificance).
