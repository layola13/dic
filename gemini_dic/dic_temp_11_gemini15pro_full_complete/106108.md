knightess:/ˈnaɪtɪs/
n.
女骑士，女性骑士
A female knight; a woman who holds the rank of knight.
She was dubbed a knightess for her bravery in battle. 她因在战斗中的英勇而被封为女骑士。
The knightess rode her valiant steed into the fray.  这位女骑士骑着她的骏马冲入战场。
近义词:female knight, dame
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly squire or knave for a male counterpart of lower rank)
词性: noun
