bell-out:/ˈbel aʊt/ | n. |紧急财政援助；紧急救助；保释|An act of giving financial assistance to a failing business or economy.|The government provided a bell-out to the struggling airline. 政府向陷入困境的航空公司提供了紧急财政援助。|Example: The bank received a government bell-out. 这家银行得到了政府的紧急救助。|Synonyms: bailout, rescue, lifeline|Antonyms: refusal, denial|noun

bell-out:/ˈbel aʊt/ | v. |保释；帮助…摆脱困境|To rescue someone or something from a difficult situation, often financial.|The government belled out the failing bank. 政府救助了这家濒临倒闭的银行。|Example:  They had to bell out their daughter when her business failed. 当女儿的生意失败时，他们不得不帮助她摆脱困境。|Synonyms: rescue, save, bail out|Antonyms: abandon, forsake|verb
