evanescent:/ˌɛvəˈnɛsənt/ | adj. | 短暂的，转瞬即逝的 | Quickly fading or disappearing; lasting for only a short time. | The beauty of the sunset was evanescent, lasting only a few minutes. 夕阳的美是短暂的，只持续了几分钟。| synonyms: fleeting, transient, ephemeral, momentary | antonyms: permanent, enduring, lasting | adjective
