briared:/ˈbraɪərd/
adjective
荆棘丛生的；多刺的；充满荆棘的
Covered in or full of briars; thorny.
The briared path was difficult to navigate.  荆棘丛生的小路很难通行。
The fox disappeared into the briared thicket.  狐狸消失在荆棘丛生的灌木丛中。
近义词: thorny, prickly, brambly
反义词: smooth, clear

词性: adjective

词源: From briar + -ed, from Old English brǣr.
