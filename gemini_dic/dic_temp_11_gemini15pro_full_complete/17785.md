bedphere: /ˈbɛdfɪə(r)/ | n. | 床圈;睡眠氛围 | A coined term referring to the immediate environment of a bed, encompassing the bedding, mattress, and surrounding atmosphere conducive to sleep.  It can also refer to the general feeling or atmosphere associated with sleep and bedtime. |  She created a calming bedphere with soft lighting and lavender scents. 她用柔和的灯光和薰衣草的香味营造了一个宁静的睡眠氛围。 |  My cluttered bedroom doesn't exactly promote a restful bedphere.  我凌乱的卧室并不能营造一个宁静的睡眠氛围。 | synonyms: sleep environment, sleep sanctuary, bedroom atmosphere | antonyms: wakefulness, insomnia | noun
