accomplishing: /əˈkʌmplɪʃɪŋ/
Verb
完成，实现
the process of finishing something successfully; succeeding in doing something.
She is accomplishing her goals one by one. 她正在逐一实现她的目标。
The team is accomplishing its mission. 团队正在完成它的使命。
Synonyms: achieving, attaining, fulfilling, realizing
Antonyms: failing, neglecting, abandoning
