fruiter:/ˈfruːtər/ | n. |水果商，水果贩|A person who sells fruit, especially from a stall or shop. |例句：The fruiter had a colourful display of fresh produce. 水果商摆放着五颜六色的新鲜农产品。

例句：She bought a basket of strawberries from the local fruiter. 她从当地的水果商那里买了一篮草莓。|近义词：greengrocer, fruit seller, fruit vendor |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From fruit + -er (agent suffix).  The suffix "-er" is used to form nouns denoting a person or thing that performs a particular action, in this case, someone who sells fruit.  The word "fruit" ultimately comes from the Latin "fructus," meaning "enjoyment, delight, fruit, produce."
