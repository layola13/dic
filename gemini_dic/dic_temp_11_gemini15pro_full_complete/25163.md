buckskins:/ˈbʌkskɪnz/| n.|鹿皮裤; 鹿皮衣服; 鹿皮|Clothes made of buckskin, typically worn by Native Americans and frontiersmen.  Also refers to the tanned hide of a deer or similar animal.|The frontiersman wore buckskins.  那个拓荒者穿着鹿皮衣服。|Near Synonyms: deerskin, leathers, hides|Antonyms: synthetics, fabrics|noun
