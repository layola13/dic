commingle:/kəˈmɪŋɡəl/| v. |混合，掺杂|To mix together; to blend.|例句：The company commingled customer funds with its own.  这家公司把客户的资金和自己的资金混在一起了。

例句：Don't commingle your personal expenses with business expenses. 不要把你的个人开支和商务开支混在一起。|近义词：mix, blend, merge, combine|反义词：separate, divide, segregate|词性:动词


Etymology->
From Late Latin *commisculāre, from Latin com- + misculāre “to mix,” frequentative of miscēre “to mix.”
