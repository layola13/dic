decrypted:/diːˈkrɪptɪd/| adj.|解密的，破译的|Converted from a coded or encrypted form into plain text or an understandable format.  The message was successfully decrypted.|例句：The spy decrypted the enemy's message.  间谍破译了敌人的信息。

例句：The encrypted files were decrypted using a complex algorithm.  加密的文件使用复杂的算法被解密。|近义词：decoded, deciphered|反义词：encrypted, encoded|词性:形容词

词源: From de- + encrypt, from en- + crypt.  "Crypt" comes from the Greek "kryptos," meaning "hidden" or "secret."
