acing-it:/ˈeɪsɪŋ ɪt/| adj.|表现出色，轻松应对|Succeeding or performing exceptionally well, often with ease and confidence.  Origin:  "Ace" means to achieve something perfectly, especially in a test or exam. "Acing it" extends this meaning to any situation where someone performs exceptionally well. |例句：She aced the exam. 她考试考得很好。|例句：He's acing it in his new role. 他在新岗位上表现出色。|近义词：excelling, mastering, conquering, nailing it, killing it |反义词:failing, struggling, bombing |词性:形容词
