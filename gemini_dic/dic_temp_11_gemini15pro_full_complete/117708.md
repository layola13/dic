melancholiae:/ˌmɛlənˈkəʊliːiː/
n.
忧郁症，悲哀，忧思
A state of profound sadness and depression.
Melancholiae was once a common medical diagnosis. 忧郁症曾经是一种常见的医学诊断。
His melancholiae deepened during the winter months.  他的忧郁在冬季加深了。
近义词:depression, sadness, gloom, melancholy
反义词:joy, happiness, elation, cheerfulness
词性:名词

词源:
From Late Latin melancholia, from Greek melankholía "black bile," from melas (genitive melanos) "black" + kholé "bile."  The ancient Greeks believed that an excess of black bile caused melancholia.
