almanac: /ˈɔːlmənæk/ (US) /ˈɔːlmənæk/ (UK)| n.|年鉴；历书|A publication containing astronomical data, tide tables, and other information often arranged according to the days, weeks, and months of the year.|The farmer consulted the almanac to determine the best time to plant his crops. 农民查阅了年鉴，以确定种植作物的最佳时间。|Example: I checked the almanac for the high tide times. 我查阅了年鉴，查看了涨潮时间。|Synonyms: calendar, yearbook, annual, register|Antonyms: N/A|noun
