coadjutant:/koʊˈædʒətənt/| n. adj.|助手，副官；辅助的|A person who is an assistant to someone else, especially in the army.  A coadjutant can also describe something that assists or supports something else.|例句：The general relied heavily on his coadjutant. 将军非常依赖他的副官。

The new drug proved coadjutant in boosting the immune system. 这种新药被证明有助于增强免疫系统。|近义词：assistant, aide, helper, auxiliary|反义词:opponent, enemy|词性:名词, 形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *coadiuvant-, present participle stem of coadiuvare "to assist," from Latin com- "together" + adiuvare "to help."
