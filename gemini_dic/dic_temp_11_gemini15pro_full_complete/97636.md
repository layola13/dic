inheritability:/ɪnˌhɛrɪtəˈbɪləti/ |n.| 遗传性，可遗传性 |The degree to which a particular characteristic is genetically rather than environmentally determined.  The inheritability of eye color is high. |例句：Scientists are studying the inheritability of intelligence. 科学家正在研究智力的遗传性。

例句：The inheritability of height is well established. 身高的遗传性已被证实。

|近义词：heredity, heritability |反义词：acquired characteristic|词性：名词
