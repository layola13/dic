opisthography:/ˌɒpɪˈθɒɡrəfi/
noun
反面书写，背面书写
Writing or inscription on the back of something, especially a coin or tablet.
The opisthography on the ancient coin revealed a hidden message. 古钱币背面的文字揭示了一条隐藏的信息。

The curator pointed out the opisthography on the clay tablet.  策展人指出了泥板上背面的文字。
Synonym: inscription, writing
Antonym: blank, unwritten
