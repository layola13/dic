alternativeness:/ɔːlˈtɜːrnətɪvnəs/ | n. | 替代性，可选择性 | The alternativeness of the proposed solutions allows for flexibility in decision-making.  提出的解决方案的可选择性使得决策具有灵活性。 | Example: The report highlighted the alternativeness of renewable energy sources.  这份报告强调了可再生能源的替代性。
Example:  She appreciated the alternativeness of transportation options in the city. 她很欣赏这个城市交通方式的多样性。| Synonyms: optionality, choice, variability, diversity | Antonyms: singularity, uniformity, inflexibility, rigidity | noun
