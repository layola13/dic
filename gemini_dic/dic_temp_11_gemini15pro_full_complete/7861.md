anteater:/ˈæntˌiːtər/ | n. |食蚁兽|An animal that feeds primarily on ants and termites, characterized by a long snout, sticky tongue, and strong claws. |例句：The anteater used its long tongue to lap up the ants. 食蚁兽用它长长的舌头舔食蚂蚁。

例句：Anteaters are native to Central and South America. 食蚁兽原产于中美洲和南美洲。|近义词：antbear |反义词: None | 词性: noun
