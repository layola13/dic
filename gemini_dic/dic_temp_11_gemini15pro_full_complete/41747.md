corrugate:/ˈkɔːrəɡeɪt/ (US), /ˈkɒrəɡeɪt/ (UK)| v. adj.|使起皱纹，起皱纹的|to contract or cause to contract into wrinkles or folds; wrinkled or folded.|The intense heat corrugated the plastic.  高温使塑料起皱。|Synonyms: wrinkle, crease, crinkle, furrow|Antonyms: smooth, flatten|verb

corrugate:/ˈkɔːrəɡeɪt/ (US), /ˈkɒrəɡeɪt/ (UK)| adj.|起皱纹的，波纹的|shaped into alternating ridges and grooves.|Corrugated cardboard is widely used for packaging. 瓦楞纸板广泛用于包装。|Synonyms: wrinkled, ridged, grooved, furrowed|Antonyms: smooth, flat|adjective
