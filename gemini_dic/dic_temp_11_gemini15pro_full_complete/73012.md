footbinding:/ˈfʊtˌbaɪndɪŋ/ /ˈfʊtˌbaɪndɪŋ/
n.
裹脚
The act or practice of tightly binding the feet of young girls to prevent further growth, a custom formerly practiced in China.
Footbinding was a painful and debilitating practice. 裹脚是一种痛苦且使人衰弱的习俗。
The museum displayed exhibits on the history of footbinding in China.  博物馆展出了关于中国裹脚历史的展品。
近义词: foot-binding
反义词: natural feet, unbound feet
词性: 名词
