crosspiece:/ˈkrɒspiːs/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːspiːs/ (US)| n. |横木，横档，横撑|A horizontal piece in a structure, such as a bar, beam, or rung of a ladder.|The carpenter secured the crosspiece to the uprights.  木匠把横木固定在立柱上。|
The cat walked along the narrow crosspiece of the fence.  猫沿着围栏狭窄的横档走着。|
Synonyms: bar, beam, rung, rail, crossbar|
Antonyms: upright, vertical|
