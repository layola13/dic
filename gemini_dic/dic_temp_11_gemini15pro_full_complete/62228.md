encroach:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃ/| v. |侵入，侵占，蚕食|to advance gradually beyond usual or proper limits; trespass|例句：The suburbs encroached steadily on the rural areas. 市郊逐步蚕食了农村地区。

例句：The factory encroached on the neighboring farmland. 工厂侵占了邻近的农田。|近义词：intrude, infringe, trespass|反义词：retreat, withdraw|词性：动词
