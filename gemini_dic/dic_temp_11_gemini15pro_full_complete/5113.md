allegers:/əˈlɛdʒərz/| n.| 指控者，宣称者 |People who claim something is true, typically without proof.| The allegers claimed the company was polluting the river, but they had no concrete evidence.  指控者声称该公司污染了河流，但他们没有确凿的证据。 | accusers, claimants | deniers, defenders | noun

allegers:/əˈlɛdʒərz/| v.|  (allege 的第三人称单数形式)  断言，宣称 |To assert or declare without proof.| He alleges that he saw the suspect at the scene of the crime. 他声称在犯罪现场看到了嫌疑人。| assert, claim, maintain | deny, refute | verb
