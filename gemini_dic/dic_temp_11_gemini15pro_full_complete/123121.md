monifier:/ˈmɒnɪfaɪər/ | n. | 货币主义者; |A person who believes in or advocates monetarism (the theory that changes in the money supply directly affect the economy). | Example: The monifier argued that controlling inflation was the central bank's primary responsibility.  货币主义者认为，控制通货膨胀是中央银行的主要职责。

| Example:  Some monifiers believe that government intervention in the economy is harmful. 一些货币主义者认为政府干预经济是有害的。 | 近义词: monetarist | 反义词: Keynesian (凯恩斯主义者) | 词性: noun


词源:  Derived from "monetary" (relating to money) + "-ifier" (a suffix denoting a person who does something or causes something to happen).
