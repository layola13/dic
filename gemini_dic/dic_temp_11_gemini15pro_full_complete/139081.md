例句：The oaxacanita we had last night was particularly smooth. 我们昨晚喝的瓦哈卡尼塔特别顺滑。 | 近义词：mezcal, agave spirit | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could be contrasted with a non-smoky spirit like vodka) | 词性: noun
