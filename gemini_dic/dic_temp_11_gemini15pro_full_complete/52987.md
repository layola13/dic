dinking:/ˈdɪŋkɪŋ/| n.|轻击，(尤指在排球、网球等运动中)轻扣，吊球;  休闲活动; 消遣;  (非正式)双职工无子女(DINK = Double Income, No Kids);|The act of hitting a ball softly and skillfully so that it just goes over the net in sports like volleyball and tennis.  Also refers to leisure activities or pastimes. Informally, it can also refer to the lifestyle of a couple with double income and no kids.|He won the point with a clever dinking shot. 他用一记巧妙的吊球赢得了这一分。

She enjoys dinking around in the garden. 她喜欢在花园里消遣。

They're a DINK couple, so they have a lot of disposable income. 他们是丁克一族，所以有很多可支配收入。|近义词：tapping, dabbing, patting (for the sports meaning); relaxing, unwinding, leisure (for the pastime meaning); childfree, childless (for the DINK meaning) |反义词：smashing, spiking (for the sports meaning); working, laboring (for the pastime meaning)|词性:名词
