overpedal:/ˌoʊvərˈpɛdl/ | v. | 过分使用踏板（尤指钢琴），过度渲染，过分强调 | To use the sustaining pedal of a piano excessively, resulting in a blurred or muddy sound. To exaggerate or overemphasize something. | The pianist overpedaled throughout the piece, creating a wash of sound.  钢琴家在整首曲子中过度使用了踏板，制造了一种模糊的声音。

The politician overpedaled his message, and voters became skeptical.  这位政治家过分强调了他的信息，选民们变得 skeptical 了。 | 近义词：exaggerate, overemphasize, overdo | 反义词：understate, downplay | 词性: 动词
