amateurisms:/ˈæmətərɪzəmz/
n.
业余爱好；非专业行为；外行做法
The performance was marred by several amateurisms. 表演中有一些业余的瑕疵。
Amateurisms in the painting were obvious to the trained eye.  这幅画中的业余之处在外行眼里显而易见。
近义词: unprofessionalism, ineptitude, clumsiness
反义词: professionalism, expertise, skill
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From amateur + -ism.  Amateur comes from French amateur "lover of," from Old French amatour, from Latin amator "lover," from amare "to love."  The suffix -ism denotes a doctrine, belief, or practice.
