lubricator:/ˈluːbrɪkeɪtər/| n.|润滑器；加油器；润滑油壶|A device that applies a lubricant to a machine or engine.  A lubricator keeps moving parts working smoothly and prevents wear and tear.|The mechanic used a lubricator to oil the gears. 机械师用润滑器给齿轮上油。|Synonyms: oiler, greaser|Antonyms: desiccant, dryer|noun

lubricate:/ˈluːbrɪkeɪt/| vt.|润滑；使…顺利进行|To apply a lubricant to something in order to reduce friction and allow smooth movement. To make something easier to operate or progress.|He lubricated the hinges with oil. 他用油润滑了铰链。|Synonyms: oil, grease, smooth|Antonyms: dry, roughen|verb
