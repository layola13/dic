amniocentesis: /ˌæmniəʊsɛnˈtiːsɪs/ (US) /ˌæmniəʊsɛnˈtiːsɪs/ (UK)
n.|羊膜穿刺术|A medical procedure in which a small amount of amniotic fluid, which surrounds the fetus, is extracted from the uterus for testing. This test can detect chromosomal abnormalities and certain genetic disorders.|Amniocentesis is offered to women at increased risk of having a baby with a genetic condition.|Synonym: amniotic fluid test|Antonym: N/A|Noun
