negotiability:/nɪˌɡoʊʃiəˈbɪləti/
n.|可转让性，可流通性|The quality of being transferable by endorsement or delivery, to give the transferee the right to enforce the instrument.|The negotiability of the bond makes it a liquid asset.  这债券的可转让性使其成为流动资产。|transferability, assignability|non-negotiability|noun

negotiabilities:/nɪˌɡoʊʃiəˈbɪlətiz/
n.|可转让性，可流通性（复数）|The plural form of negotiability, referring to the various aspects or instances of being transferable by endorsement or delivery.|The negotiabilities of different financial instruments vary. 不同金融工具的可转让性各不相同。|transferabilities, assignabilities|non-negotiabilities|noun
