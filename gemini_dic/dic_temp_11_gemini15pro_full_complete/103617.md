jointress:/ˈdʒɔɪntrɪs/
noun
女性共同继承人; 女共有人;寡妇共同继承人
A woman who holds a jointure or has a life interest in the estate of her deceased husband.
The jointress of the estate had the right to live in the manor house for the rest of her life.  这位女性共同继承人有权在庄园里度过余生。
Synonyms: coheiress, co-owner (female)
Antonyms: sole heir, sole owner
n.
