about-turning:/əˈbaʊt ˈtɜːrnɪŋ/
adjective
转向的，调头的，转变方向的
Relating to the act of turning around or changing direction.
The car made an about-turning maneuver in the narrow street. 汽车在狭窄的街道上做了一个调头动作。
The about-turning point in his life was when he decided to quit his job and travel the world.  他人生的转折点是他决定辞掉工作去环游世界。
近义词: turning, reversing, U-turning
反义词: straightforward, continuing
