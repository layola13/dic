outgazing:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪzɪŋ/
n.
凝视; 注视; 以目光胜过
The act of looking steadily and intently, often with the implication of surpassing or dominating through gaze.
He held her in an outgazing of deep love. 他深深地凝视着她，充满了爱意。
近义词: staring, gazing, watching
反义词: glancing, looking away
词性: 名词

outgazing:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪzɪŋ/
v.
以目光胜过; 凝视
To look steadily and intently, often with the implication of surpassing or dominating through gaze.
She outgazed her opponent, unwavering in her determination. 她以坚定的目光凝视着对手，毫不动摇。
近义词: outstare, outface
反义词: avert one's eyes, look away
词性: 动词
