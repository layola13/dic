conceptive:/kənˈsɛptɪv/| adj.|概念上的，构思的|Relating to or based on mental concepts.  Concerned with the formation of ideas or concepts.|例句：Conceptive skills are crucial for problem-solving. 概念技能对于解决问题至关重要。|例句：The artist's work is highly conceptive and abstract. 这位艺术家的作品概念性很强，很抽象。|近义词：conceptual, ideational, abstract, theoretical|反义词：concrete, practical, tangible|词性:adjective
Etymology:  Late 16th century: from late Latin conceptivus, from Latin concept- ‘conceived’, from the verb concipere.
