ambulacral:/ˌæmbjʊˈleɪkrəl/| adj.|步带的，管足的|Relating to or resembling an ambulacrum, a water vascular system in echinoderms used for locomotion, respiration, and feeding.|The starfish uses its ambulacral feet to move across the ocean floor. 海星用它的管足在海底移动。|近义词：tube foot (specifically for echinoderms)|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider terms related to other locomotion systems, e.g., "skeletal", "muscular")|词性: adjective

ambulacral:/ˌæmbjʊˈleɪkrəl/| n.|管足|A tube foot, part of the water vascular system of echinoderms.|The ambulacral are essential for the starfish's ability to grip surfaces. 管足对于海星抓握表面的能力至关重要。|近义词：tube foot|反义词:  (No direct antonym)|词性: noun
