enticing:/ɪnˈtaɪsɪŋ/
adjective
诱人的；有吸引力的
Attractive or tempting; alluring.
The enticing aroma of freshly baked bread wafted from the kitchen.  厨房里飘来 freshly baked bread 诱人的香味。
The travel brochure showed enticing pictures of tropical beaches.  旅游手册上展示了热带海滩诱人的图片。
近义词: alluring, tempting, attractive, appealing, captivating
反义词: repulsive, unappealing, off-putting, repellent, disgusting
词性: adjective

词源:
From late Middle English *enticen, from Anglo-Norman enticier, Old French enticier, from en- ‘in’ + a derivative of tise ‘enticement’.
