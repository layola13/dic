nonverbal:/ˌnɒnˈvɜːbəl/ (British) /ˌnɑːnˈvɜːrbəl/ (American)| adj.|非语言的；不用言语的|Not involving or using words; communicating without speech.|Her nonverbal communication skills are excellent; she can convey so much with just a look. 她的非语言沟通能力很出色；她只需一个眼神就能表达很多。|She gave him a nonverbal cue to be quiet. 她给了他一个让他安静的非语言提示。|近义词：unspoken, wordless, silent|反义词：verbal, spoken|词性:形容词

nonverbal:/ˌnɒnˈvɜːbəl/ (British) /ˌnɑːnˈvɜːrbəl/ (American)| n.|非语言交际；非语言行为|Communication without the use of words.|Body language is a powerful form of nonverbal.  肢体语言是一种强有力的非语言交流形式。|The psychologist studied the patient's nonverbals for clues about his emotional state. 心理学家研究了病人的非语言行为，以寻找有关他情绪状态的线索。|近义词：body language, gestures|反义词：speech, verbal communication|词性:名词
