dyne:/daɪn/| n. |达因|A unit of force in the centimeter-gram-second system of physical units, equal to the force that would give a free mass of one gram an acceleration of one centimeter per second per second.|The force required to accelerate a one-gram mass at a rate of one centimeter per second squared is one dyne.|近义词: force unit|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|词性:noun
