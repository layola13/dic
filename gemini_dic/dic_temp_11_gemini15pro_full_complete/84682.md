handed-over: /ˈhændɪd ˈoʊvər/ (UK) /ˈhændɪd ˈoʊvər/ (US)
adjective
已移交的，已交付的
having been formally transferred from one person or organization to another.
The handed-over documents contained classified information.  已移交的文件包含机密信息。
The project was handed-over to the new team last week.  该项目已于上周移交给新团队。
Synonyms: transferred, delivered, relinquished, ceded
Antonyms: retained, kept, withheld
词性: adjective
