blurringly:/ˈblɜːrɪŋli/| adv.| 模糊地，含糊地 |In a way that makes something unclear or difficult to see or remember. |The camera focused blurringly on the moving car. 相机模糊地对焦在移动的汽车上。|The events of that night remained blurringly in his memory. 那晚的事件在他的记忆中模糊不清。|近义词：vaguely, indistinctly, hazily|反义词：clearly, sharply, distinctly|词性:副词

词源: From blur (v.) + -ing (present participle) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Blur comes from Middle English blurre, probably of imitative origin.
