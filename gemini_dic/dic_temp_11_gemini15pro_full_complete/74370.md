fraise:/freɪz/ (UK), /freɪz/ (US)| n.|草莓; 草莓色; 钻头;|A small, red, juicy fruit that grows on a plant close to the ground.  Also, a bright pink-red color, resembling the fruit. In a technical context, it can refer to a cutter or a reamer.|

例句：
The cake was decorated with fresh fraises.
蛋糕上装饰着新鲜的草莓。

We picked fraises at the farm yesterday.
我们昨天在农场摘了草莓。


近义词：strawberry|
反义词: N/A|
词性: noun|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *fraise, from Vulgar Latin *fraga, plural of Latin fraga “strawberry,” related to fragum “strawberry,” of uncertain origin.
