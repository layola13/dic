goray:/ˈɡɔːreɪ/| n.|戈瑞, 照射量的单位|A unit of absorbed dose of ionizing radiation, equal to one joule of energy absorbed per kilogram of matter.|The patient received a dose of 50 gorays. 病人接受了50戈瑞的剂量。|近义词:None (as it's a specific unit)|反义词:None (as it's a specific unit)|词性:noun

Etymology:  Named after Louis Harold Gray, a British physicist who made significant contributions to the field of radiation dosimetry.
