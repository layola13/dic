creolizes:/ˈkriːəlaɪzɪz/
verb
克里奥尔化；使…克里奥尔化
To cause to become Creole, especially with respect to language or culture. The process by which a pidgin becomes the native language of a speech community.
English has creolized in Jamaica, creating Jamaican Patois.  英语在牙买加克里奥尔化，形成了牙买加帕托瓦语。
The language creolized over time, incorporating elements from various African languages. 随着时间的推移，这种语言克里奥尔化，融合了各种非洲语言的元素。

近义词:hybridize, blend, mix, merge
反义词:purify, separate, segregate
词性:v.
