diageotropism:/ˌdaɪəˌdʒiːəˈtrɒpɪzəm/ | n. | 横向地性 | The growth of a plant part at right angles to the stimulus of gravity; horizontal growth. | Example 1: The branches of the tree exhibited strong diageotropism, growing outwards rather than upwards. 这棵树的树枝表现出强烈的横向地性，向外生长而不是向上生长。

Example 2:  The rhizomes of the ginger plant show diageotropism, spreading horizontally underground. 姜植物的根茎表现出横向地性，在地下水平蔓延。 | Synonym: plagiotropism | Antonym: orthotropism | noun
