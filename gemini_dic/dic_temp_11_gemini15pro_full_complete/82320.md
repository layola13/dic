grouting: /ˈɡraʊtɪŋ/ /ˈɡraʊdɪŋ/
grouting: n.
grouting: 灌浆；勾缝；填缝
grouting: The process of filling gaps or spaces between tiles, bricks, or other construction materials with a mortar-like mixture.
grouting: The tiles were laid and the grouting was done the next day.  瓷砖铺好了，第二天进行了灌浆。
grouting: After the tiles were set, he carefully applied the grouting.  瓷砖铺好后，他仔细地涂抹了勾缝剂。
grouting: cementing, sealing, filling
grouting: removal, excavation
