faker:/ˈfeɪkər/| n.|造假者，骗子|A person who pretends to be someone or something they are not, typically in order to deceive others. |He was exposed as a faker, pretending to be a qualified doctor. 他被揭露是一个骗子，假装是一位合格的医生。|Synonyms: imposter, fraud, charlatan, phony, pretender|Antonyms: genuine person, authentic individual|noun

faker:/ˈfeɪkər/| adj. (informal)|假的，伪造的|Not genuine; counterfeit. |He tried to pass off a faker Rolex as the real thing. 他试图把一只假劳力士当真货卖掉。|Synonyms: fake, counterfeit, bogus, sham, phony, ersatz|Antonyms: genuine, real, authentic|adjective
