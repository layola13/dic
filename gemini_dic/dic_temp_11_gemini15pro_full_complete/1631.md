aciform:/ˈæsɪfɔːrm/
adjective
针状的，针形的
shaped like a needle; needle-shaped
The aciform leaves of the pine tree help it conserve water. 松树针状的叶子有助于其保存水分。
近义词: needle-shaped, acicular, acerose
反义词: broad, wide, rounded
词性: adjective
