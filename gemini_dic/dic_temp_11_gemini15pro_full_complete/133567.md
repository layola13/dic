nonflagellated:/ˌnɒnˈflædʒəleɪtɪd/
adjective
无鞭毛的
Lacking flagella (whip-like appendages used for locomotion).
Nonflagellated bacteria rely on other mechanisms for movement, such as gliding or twitching.  无鞭毛细菌依靠其他机制移动，例如滑行或抽搐。
Some nonflagellated protozoa move by amoeboid motion. 一些无鞭毛原生动物通过变形虫运动来移动。
nonmotile, aflagellate
flagellated, motile
