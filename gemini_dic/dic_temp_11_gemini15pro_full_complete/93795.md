immersed:/ɪˈmɜːrst/| adj.|沉浸的，专心的，浸入的|deeply involved in something; submerged in a liquid|例句：He was completely immersed in his work. 他完全沉浸在他的工作中。|例句：The statue was immersed in the acid bath. 雕像被浸在酸浴中。|近义词：engrossed, absorbed, preoccupied, submerged|反义词：distracted, uninvolved, superficial|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: past participle of immerge, from Latin immergere, from in- ‘into’ + mergere ‘plunge’.
