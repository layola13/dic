dittoed:/ˈdɪtoʊd/ | adj. | 同上，同上所述 | Identical; the same as what has just been said or written. | The figures for 2021 were good, and the forecast for 2022 is dittoed. 2021年的数据很好，2022年的预测也一样。 | 近义词：repeated, copied, duplicated, identical | 反义词：different, contrasting, dissimilar | 词性:形容词

ditto:/ˈdɪtoʊ/ | n. | 同上 | The same as what has just been said. | "And the same goes for you," he added, "ditto." “你也一样，”他补充道，“同上。” | 近义词：the same, aforesaid | 反义词：different | 词性:名词
