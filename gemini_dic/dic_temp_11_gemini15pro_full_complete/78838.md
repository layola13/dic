gimp:/ɡɪmp/| n. vi. vt.|跛子，瘸子；图像处理软件；(使)变跛，(使)瘸|A person who limps. To cause someone to limp.  A free and open-source raster graphics editor used for image retouching and editing, free-form drawing, resizing, cropping, photomontages, converting between different image formats, and more specialized tasks.|例句：The gimp struggled to climb the stairs. 跛子艰难地爬上楼梯。

例句：He gimped his ankle playing basketball. 他打篮球时扭伤了脚踝。

例句：I used GIMP to edit my photos. 我使用GIMP编辑我的照片。|近义词：cripple, lame, hobble (v.), limp (v.)|反义词：able-bodied, healthy|词性:名词，动词
