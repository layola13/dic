hemicarp:/ˈhemɪkɑːrp/| n. |分果爿，半果，小果|One of the two carpels of an umbelliferous plant, each containing a single seed.  指伞形科植物的两个心皮之一，每个心皮含有一粒种子。|The hemicarps of the plant are easily separated.  这种植物的分果爿很容易分开。|近义词:mericarp, carpel|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "whole fruit")|n.
