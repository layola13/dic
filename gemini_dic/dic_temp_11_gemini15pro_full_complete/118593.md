mesozoic:/ˌmɛzəˈzoʊɪk/ | adj. n.| 中生代的，中生代 |Relating to or denoting the era between the Paleozoic and Cenozoic eras, comprising the Triassic, Jurassic, and Cretaceous periods.  Characterized by the dominance of reptiles, especially dinosaurs. | The Mesozoic Era saw the rise and fall of the dinosaurs. 中生代见证了恐龙的兴衰。| The museum has a fantastic exhibit on Mesozoic fossils.  博物馆有一个很棒的中生代化石展览。| synonym: Secondary | antonym: Paleozoic, Cenozoic | adjective, noun
