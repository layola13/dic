japanning:/dʒəˈpænɪŋ/
n.|漆器，上漆，漆器工艺|The process of applying a hard, glossy varnish to an object, typically in imitation of Japanese lacquerware.|The antique table had beautiful japanning.|近义词：lacquering, varnishing|反义词:|noun

japan:/dʒəˈpæn/
vt.|涂日本漆，使…具有光泽|To coat or varnish an object in imitation of Japanese lacquerware.|He japanned the wooden box to give it a shiny finish.|近义词：lacquer, varnish|反义词:|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Japanese, ultimately from "Japan" + "-ing". The term arose because the technique of applying lacquer was associated with Japan.
