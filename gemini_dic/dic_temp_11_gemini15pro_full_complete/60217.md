effluvial:/ɪˈfluːviəl/ |adj.|恶臭的；难闻的；由散发物引起的| Relating to or arising from effluvium (an unpleasant or harmful odor, gas, or vapor).|The effluvial smell from the sewage plant made the neighborhood unbearable. 污水处理厂散发出的恶臭气味使附近无法忍受。| The effluvial nature of the swamp discouraged exploration. 沼泽散发出的难闻气味让人望而却步。|Synonyms: noxious, fetid, foul, mephitic|Antonyms: fragrant, aromatic, sweet|Adjective

effluvium:/ɪˈfluːviəm/ |n.|散发物；(尤指难闻的)气味，臭气|An unpleasant or harmful odor, gas, or vapor, especially one that is given off by decaying matter.|The effluvium from the landfill was overwhelming. 垃圾填埋场散发出的臭气令人难以忍受。| The detective noticed a strange effluvium emanating from the basement.  侦探注意到地下室散发出一股奇怪的气味。|Synonyms: stench, stink, reek, miasma|Antonyms: fragrance, perfume, aroma|Noun
