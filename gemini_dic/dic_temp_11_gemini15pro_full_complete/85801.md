hazarders:/ˈhæzərdərz/
n.|冒险者；敢于冒险的人|People who take risks or engage in hazardous activities.
例句：The hazarders set off on their perilous journey across the mountains. 冒险者们开始了他们穿越山脉的危险旅程。

例句：Only the bravest hazarders dared to enter the dark cave. 只有最勇敢的冒险者才敢进入黑暗的洞穴。
近义词：risk-takers, gamblers, adventurers, daredevils
反义词：cautious people, conservatives


词性:名词(plural of hazarder)

词源: From the verb "hazard" (meaning "to risk or endanger"), which comes from the Old French "hasard" (meaning "game of dice, chance").  Ultimately, the word has Arabic origins.
