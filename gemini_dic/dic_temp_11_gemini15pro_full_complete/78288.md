germinal:/ˈdʒɜːrmɪnl/| adj.|萌芽的；初期的；胚胎的|Relating to a germ or seed; rudimentary; being in the earliest stage of development.|The germinal stage of pregnancy is the first two weeks after conception. 怀孕的萌芽阶段是指受孕后的前两周。|Synonyms: nascent, incipient, embryonic, rudimentary|Antonyms: mature, developed, advanced|Adjective

germinal:/ˈdʒɜːrmɪnl/| n.|萌芽；胚芽；起源|A germ or seed; the origin or source of something.|The idea was the germinal for her later research. 这个想法是她后来研究的萌芽。|Synonyms: origin, source, root, seed|Antonyms: end, conclusion, result|Noun
