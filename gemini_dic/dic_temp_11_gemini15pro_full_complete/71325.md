fjord:/fjɔːrd/ (US) /ˈfjɔːrd/ (UK)| n. |峡湾|A long, narrow, deep inlet of the sea between high cliffs, typically formed by submergence of a glaciated valley.|The Geirangerfjord in Norway is a stunning example of a fjord.  挪威的盖朗厄尔峡湾是一个令人惊叹的峡湾例子。|近义词：inlet, sound|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 open sea 或 plain)|词性: 名词
