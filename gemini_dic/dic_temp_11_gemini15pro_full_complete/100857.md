introspectiveness:/ˌɪntrəˈspektɪvnəs/
n.|内省，反省|The quality of examining one's own thoughts and feelings.|Her introspectiveness allowed her to understand her motivations. 她的内省让她能够理解自己的动机。|近义词：reflectiveness, thoughtfulness, contemplation|反义词：extroversion, superficiality|词性: 名词
introspective:/ˌɪntrəˈspektɪv/
adj.|内省的，反省的|Characterized by examination of one's own thoughts and feelings.|He was an introspective young man, always analyzing his own behavior. 他是一个内省的年轻人，总是分析自己的行为。|近义词：reflective, thoughtful, contemplative|反义词：extroverted, superficial|词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *introspectus*, past participle of *introspicere* "to look into," from *intro-* "inward, within" + *specere* "to look."
