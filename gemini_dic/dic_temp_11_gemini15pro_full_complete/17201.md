baume:/boʊˈmeɪ/| n.|波美度，一种比重计量单位|A scale for measuring the specific gravity of liquids, invented by Antoine Baumé. It is calibrated in degrees Baumé (°Bé). There are separate scales for liquids heavier than water and liquids lighter than water.|The winemaker checked the baume of the grape juice. 酿酒师检查了葡萄汁的波美度。|近义词：specific gravity, density|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|noun
