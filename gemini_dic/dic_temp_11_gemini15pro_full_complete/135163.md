nonopinionatedness:/nɒnəˈpɪnjəneɪtɪdnəs/| n.|无偏见; 不固执己见; 开放性|The state of being nonopinionated, characterized by a willingness to consider different perspectives and a lack of rigid adherence to one's own views.|Her nonopinionatedness made her an ideal mediator. 她的无偏见使她成为理想的调解人。|近义词：open-mindedness, impartiality, neutrality, objectivity|反义词：dogmatism, bias, prejudice|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + opinionated + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
"Opinionated" comes from "opinion" (from Old French opinion, from Latin opiniōn-, opiniō, from opinārī "to think, believe") + -ated (suffix forming adjectives).
