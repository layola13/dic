doggerelizer:/ˈdɒɡərəlaɪzər/ (US) /ˈdɒɡərəlaɪzə/ (UK)| n.|打油诗作者，蹩脚诗人|A person who writes doggerel, which is poorly written verse, often characterized by clumsy rhyming and forced meter.|He was known as the office doggereliser, churning out rhymes for every occasion.|近义词：versifier, rhymester, poetaster|反义词：poet, bard|词性:noun

doggerelize:/ˈdɒɡərəlaɪz/ (US) /ˈdɒɡərəlaɪz/ (UK)| v.|写打油诗，写蹩脚诗|To write doggerel; to compose poorly written verse.|He doggerelises constantly, filling notebooks with his clumsy rhymes.|近义词：rhyme (badly), versify (poorly)|反义词：compose (poetry), write (verse)|词性:verb
