nonrecuperativeness:/ˌnɒnriːkuːpərətɪvnəs/
noun
无法恢复; 不可恢复性
The nonrecuperativeness of the ecosystem after the oil spill was devastating.  石油泄漏后，生态系统的不可恢复性是毁灭性的。
The patient's nonrecuperativeness worried the doctors. 病人的无法恢复令医生们担忧。
Near synonyms: irrecoverability, irretrievability, irreversibility
Antonyms: recoverability, restorability, reversibility
词性: noun
