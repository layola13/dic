inflectedness:/ɪnˈflɛktɪdnəs/ | n. | 屈折性，词形变化性 | The degree to which a word changes its form to express grammatical relations such as tense, number, case, etc. | The high inflectedness of Latin makes it challenging for beginners. 拉丁语高度的屈折性使初学者感到困难。 |  inflection, variability | invariability, uniformity | noun
