neologist:/niˈɒlədʒɪst/ /niˈɑːlədʒɪst/ | n. |新词语学家；造词者|A person who coins or uses new words. |例句：The neologist created a new term for the emerging technology.  这位新词语学家为这项新兴技术创造了一个新术语。

例句：Shakespeare was a prolific neologist, adding hundreds of words to the English language.  莎士比亚是一位多产的造词者，为英语增添了数百个词汇。

近义词：wordsmith, innovator, inventor (of words) |反义词:  traditionalist, purist |词性:noun
