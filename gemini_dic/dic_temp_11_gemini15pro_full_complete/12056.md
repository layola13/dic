artifact:/ˈɑːrtɪfækt/| n.|人工制品，手工艺品，史前古器物|An object made by a human being, typically an item of cultural or historical interest.|The museum displays many artifacts from ancient Egypt.  博物馆展出许多来自古埃及的文物。|synonyms: relic, object, antique, item|antonyms: natural object|noun


artifact:/ˈɑːrtɪfækt/| n.|（尤指科技产品测试中的）假象，人为现象|something observed in a scientific investigation or experiment that is not naturally present but occurs as a result of the preparative or investigative procedure.|The distortions in the image were simply artifacts created by the scanner. 图像中的扭曲仅仅是扫描仪产生的假象。|synonyms: anomaly, error, glitch, aberration|antonyms: reality, truth|noun
