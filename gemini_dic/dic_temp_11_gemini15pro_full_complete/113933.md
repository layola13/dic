mafia:/ˈmɑːfiə/ (US), /ˈmæfiə/ (UK)| n.|黑手党，秘密犯罪组织|A secret criminal organization originating in Sicily, characterized by its use of violence and intimidation to control businesses and other activities.|The mafia controlled the city's construction industry.  黑手党控制了这个城市的建筑业。|The local businesses were forced to pay protection money to the mafia. 当地企业被迫向黑手党支付保护费。|synonyms: organized crime, syndicate, mob, gang|antonyms: law enforcement, police|noun
