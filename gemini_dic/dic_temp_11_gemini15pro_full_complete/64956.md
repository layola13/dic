esthetical: /ɛsˈθɛtɪk(ə)l/ /ɛsˈθɛtɪkl/
| adj. | 美学的；审美的；关于美的 | Concerned with beauty or the appreciation of beauty. |  The painting was considered an esthetical masterpiece. 这幅画被认为是美学上的杰作。|  The design of the building is very esthetical.  这座建筑的设计非常美观。 | 近义词: artistic, beautiful, elegant | 反义词: ugly, unattractive, displeasing | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *aesthēticus, from Ancient Greek αἰσθητικός (aisthētikós, “of sense perception, perceptive”), from αἴσθησις (aísthēsis, “sensation, perception”).
