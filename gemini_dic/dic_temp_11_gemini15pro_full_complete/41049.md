compile: /kəmˈpaɪl/ (US) /kəmˈpaɪl/ (UK)| v. |编译；汇编；收集|To gather together from various sources and combine (material, such as texts or data) into one work, usually a book or list. To convert (a program) into a machine-code or lower-level form in which the program can be executed by a computer.|She compiled a list of names and addresses.|她汇编了一份姓名和地址的清单。|synonyms: assemble, collect, gather, amass|antonyms: disperse, scatter|verb

compile: /kəmˈpaɪl/ (US) /kəmˈpaɪl/ (UK)| v. |编译（计算机程序）|To convert (a program) into a machine-code or lower-level form in which the program can be executed by a computer.|The program took several hours to compile.|这个程序花了几个小时才编译完成。|synonyms: build, translate (in computing)|antonyms: decompile|verb
