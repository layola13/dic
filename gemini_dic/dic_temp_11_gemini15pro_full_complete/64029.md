epitheca:/ˈɛpɪˌθiːkə/
n.|上壳，瓣壳|The outer or upper part of a theca, as of a diatom or dinoflagellate.  (硅藻或甲藻等)壳的外部或上部。|The epitheca of this diatom species is ornamented with delicate spines.  这种硅藻的上壳饰有精致的刺。|近义词:valve, frustule|反义词:hypotheca|词性:名词
