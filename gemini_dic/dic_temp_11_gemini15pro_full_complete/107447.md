lampooned:/læmˈpuːnd/
verb
讽刺的，被嘲笑的
publicly criticized or made fun of, especially by means of a satire
The politician's latest policy was lampooned in the newspapers. 这位政客的最新政策在报纸上受到了讽刺。
The movie lampooned the pretensions of the Hollywood elite. 这部电影讽刺了好莱坞精英的做作。
satirized, ridiculed, mocked, parodied
praised, lauded, commended
verb
