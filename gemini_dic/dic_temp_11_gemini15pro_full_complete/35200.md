clubhaul: /ˈklʌbˌhɔːl/
noun
俱乐部搬运;  (尤指)将高尔夫球杆从车上卸下
The act of unloading golf clubs from a car, especially at a golf club.
After parking, the caddy assisted with the clubhaul.  泊车后, 球童协助卸下球杆。
近义词: unloading, removal
反义词: loading
