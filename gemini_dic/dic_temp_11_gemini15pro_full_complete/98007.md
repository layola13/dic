inoperation:/ɪnˌɑːpəˈreɪʃn/ | adj. | 运行中，生效，实施中 | in or into operation; functioning; working effectively. | The new system is now in operation. 新系统现在正在运行中。|  The traffic laws are in operation. 交通法规正在生效。 | 近义词: functioning, working, active, effective, running, valid | 反义词: out of order, broken, inactive, ineffective, not working, invalid | 词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin in operationem, from in- ‘in, into’ + operatio(n-) ‘working’, from the verb operari (see operate).
