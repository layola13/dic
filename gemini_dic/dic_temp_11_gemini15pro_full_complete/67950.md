facie:/ˈfeɪʃii/ /ˈfeɪʃɪˌiː/| adj.|表面上的，初步看来是|Appearing to be something, usually before all the facts are known.|He has a prima facie case against the company. 他对该公司有一个表面上看来成立的案件。|近义词：apparent, seeming, ostensible|反义词：actual, real|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *facie*, ablative singular of *facies* "face, appearance, form, shape, outward aspect," related to *facere* "to make, do."
