djibba:/ˈdʒɪbə/
n.
Djellaba, Jellaba, Jebba
A long, loose-fitting hooded robe with full sleeves, traditionally worn by men in North Africa, particularly Morocco.
He wore a djibba of heavy wool to protect him from the desert wind. 他穿着一件厚羊毛的长袍以抵御沙漠的风。
近义词: robe, cloak, kaftan
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "modern clothing")
词性: noun
