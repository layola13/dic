diatomous:/ˌdaɪəˈtɒməs/
adjective
硅藻的；由硅藻土构成的
Relating to or consisting of diatoms.
Diatomaceous earth is a naturally occurring, soft, siliceous sedimentary rock that is easily crumbled into a fine white to off-white powder. 硅藻土是一种天然存在的、柔软的硅质沉积岩，很容易粉碎成细小的白色到灰白色粉末。
The filter is made of diatomaceous earth.  这个过滤器是由硅藻土制成的。
Synonyms:  siliceous
Antonyms: N/A
词性: adjective
