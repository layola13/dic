naturalistic:/ˌnætʃrəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 自然主义的；写实主义的；逼真的 | Representing something as it appears in nature; true to life. |  例句：The painting was praised for its naturalistic portrayal of the countryside. 这幅画因其对乡村的自然主义描绘而受到赞扬。

例句：The director aimed for a naturalistic style in his films, avoiding dramatic exaggeration.  这位导演在他的电影中追求自然主义风格，避免戏剧性的夸张。


近义词：realistic, lifelike, true-to-life, representational | 反义词：abstract, idealized, stylized, unrealistic | 词性：adjective
