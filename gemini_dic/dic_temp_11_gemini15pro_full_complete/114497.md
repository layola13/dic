malacostracous:/ˌmæləˈkɒstrəkəs/| adj.|甲壳纲的，属于甲壳纲的|Relating to or belonging to the Malacostraca, a large class of crustaceans, including crabs, lobsters, shrimps, and woodlice.  These animals are characterized by a segmented body, typically with a head, thorax, and abdomen, and jointed appendages.|Lobsters are malacostracous crustaceans. 龙虾是甲壳纲的甲壳类动物。|Synonyms: crustacean|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-crustacean)|adjective

malacostracan:/ˌmæləˈkɒstrəkən/| n. adj.|甲壳纲动物，甲壳纲的|A crustacean of the class Malacostraca.  Also used as an adjective with the same meaning as malacostracous.|The study focused on several malacostracan species. 这项研究集中在几个甲壳纲物种上。|Synonyms: crustacean|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-crustacean)|noun, adjective
