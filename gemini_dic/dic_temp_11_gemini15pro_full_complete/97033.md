infectiveness:/ɪnˈfɛktɪvnəs/| n.|传染性，感染力|The infectiveness of a disease is determined by several factors, including the pathogen's ability to survive outside the host. 一种疾病的传染性是由几个因素决定的，包括病原体在宿主外生存的能力。|例句：The new variant of the virus has increased infectiveness. 这种病毒的新变种传染性增强了。|近义词：contagiousness, transmissibility|反义词:resistance, immunity|词性:名词

词源: From infect + -ive + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin inficere "to stain, dye, tinge, taint, infect," from in- "into, in, on, upon" + facere "to make, do."
