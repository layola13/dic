hypocretin:/ˌhaɪpəˈkriːtɪn/ | n. |食欲素; 下丘脑泌素 | A neuropeptide hormone produced in the hypothalamus that plays a crucial role in regulating wakefulness, appetite, and arousal.  It is also known as orexin. |  Narcolepsy is often associated with a deficiency in hypocretin. 嗜睡症通常与食欲素缺乏有关。 |  Hypocretin plays a vital role in maintaining stable sleep-wake cycles. 食欲素在维持稳定的睡眠-觉醒周期中起着至关重要的作用。 | synonym: orexin | antonym: N/A | noun
