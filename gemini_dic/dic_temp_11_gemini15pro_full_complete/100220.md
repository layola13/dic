interrogational:/ˌɪntəˈrɑːɡətɔːriəl/ /ˌɪntəˈrɒɡətɔːriəl/| adj.|疑问的，讯问的|Of, relating to, or conveying a question.  Used to describe something that asks a question.|The teacher used an interrogational tone when asking the student about their homework. 老师在询问学生作业情况时使用了疑问的语气。|近义词：inquiring, questioning, inquisitive|反义词：declarative, affirmative|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin interrogātiōnālis, from Latin interrogātiō, from interrogāre (to question), from inter- (between) + rogāre (to ask).
