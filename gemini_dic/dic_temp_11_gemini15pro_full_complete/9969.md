aphidophagous:/ˌeɪfɪˈdɒfəɡəs/
adj.
食蚜的; 以蚜虫为食的
Feeding on aphids.
例句：The ladybug is an aphidophagous insect. 瓢虫是一种食蚜昆虫。

例句：Aphidophagous insects are beneficial to gardens. 食蚜昆虫对花园有益。

近义词：aphid-eating
反义词：N/A
词性：adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *aphis*, *aphid-* "aphid" + *-phagos* "eating".
