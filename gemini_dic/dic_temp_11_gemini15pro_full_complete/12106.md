artlessness:/ˈɑːrtləsnəs/| n.|天真烂漫，单纯，朴实|Lack of guile or artificiality; naturalness and simplicity.|Her artlessness was both charming and disarming. 她的天真烂漫既迷人又令人放松警惕。|Synonyms: innocence, naiveté, ingenuousness, simplicity, candor|Antonyms: artfulness, cunning, guile, sophistication, worldliness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From artless + -ness.  Artless itself comes from art (meaning "skill, cunning") + -less (meaning "without").  Therefore, artlessness literally means "without cunning."
