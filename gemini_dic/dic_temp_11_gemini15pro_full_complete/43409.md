crannied:/ˈkrænɪd/| adj.|充满裂缝的，多洞的|Having many crannies; full of crevices or nooks.|例句：The old, crannied walls of the castle stood against the sky.  古老城堡布满裂缝的墙壁耸立在天空中。|例句：We explored the crannied coastline, discovering hidden coves and beaches. 我们探索了裂缝密布的海岸线，发现了隐藏的海湾和海滩。|近义词：creviced, fissured, cracked|反义词：smooth, unblemished|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From cranny + -ed.  "Cranny" comes from the Old North French word "cran" meaning "notch" or "chink."
