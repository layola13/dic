contradicts:/ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪkts/ | v. |反驳，与…矛盾，与…抵触|To assert the opposite of; deny the truth of (a statement, etc.); to be in conflict with.|His account contradicts hers. 他的说法与她的说法相矛盾。|近义词：deny, oppose, refute, gainsay|反义词：confirm, support, corroborate|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin contradicere, from contra- "against" + dicere "to speak."
