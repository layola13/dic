knab:/næb/| n. |小丘，小山 |A small hill or rounded elevation.|The sheep grazed on the knab overlooking the valley. 羊群在可以俯瞰山谷的小山上吃草。|近义词：knoll, hillock, mound|反义词:valley, depression|名词
