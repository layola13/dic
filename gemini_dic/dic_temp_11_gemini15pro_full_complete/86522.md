hejira:/hɪˈdʒɪərə/ /hɪˈdʒɪrə/
noun
逃亡；出走；移居；（尤指为摆脱困境的）迁徙
An exodus or migration, especially one undertaken to escape a difficult situation.
The family made their hegira to the country to escape the city's noise and pollution.  这家人为了躲避城市的噪音和污染，迁往乡下。
exodus, migration, flight, escape
return, arrival

词性:n.
