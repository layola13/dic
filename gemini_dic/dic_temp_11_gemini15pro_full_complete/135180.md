nonoptionally:/ˌnɑːnˈɑːpʃənəli/
adv.
无选择地，强制地，必须地
Without choice; obligatorily; necessarily.
Attendance at the meeting is nonoptional.  参加会议是强制性的。
Near Synonyms: obligatorily, necessarily, mandatorily, compulsorily
Antonyms: optionally, voluntarily
词性: 副词
