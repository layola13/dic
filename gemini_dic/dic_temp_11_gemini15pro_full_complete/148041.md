overrule: /ˌoʊvərˈruːl/ (US), /ˌəʊvəˈruːl/ (UK)| vt.| 驳回；否决；宣布无效；统治；支配|to reject or disallow a decision, argument, etc., made by someone with less authority| The judge overruled the objection. 法官驳回了反对意见。|The president overruled the decision of the committee. 总统否决了委员会的决定。|近义词: overturn, reject, override, invalidate, cancel|反义词: uphold, sustain, approve, ratify|词性:动词

Etymology: From Middle English *overreulen*, from Old French *surruler*, from *sur-* (over) + *ruler* (to rule).
