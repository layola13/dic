amentum:/ˈæməntəm/ /əˈmɛntəm/
noun
柔荑花序;葇荑花序
A drooping spike of unisexual flowers, as in the willow, hazel, or birch.  A catkin.
The male flowers of the hazel are borne in aments.榛树的雄花长在柔荑花序上。

The amentum of the willow tree swayed gently in the breeze. 柳树的柔荑花序在微风中轻轻摇曳。

synonym: catkin, inflorescence
antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific botanical term like amentum)
