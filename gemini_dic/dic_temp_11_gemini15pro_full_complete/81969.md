grindability:/ˌɡraɪndəˈbɪləti/ | n. |可磨性，易磨性 |The quality of a substance that allows it to be ground easily. The ease with which a solid substance can be reduced to powder or smaller particles by grinding.  |The grindability of the clinker affects the energy consumption of the cement mill. 熟料的可磨性会影响水泥厂的能耗。|Synonyms: pulverizability, friability |Antonyms: hardness, toughness| noun
