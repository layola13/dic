catamenia: /ˌkætəˈmiːniə/
noun
月经; 月事
The menses, or menstrual flow.  The discharge of blood, secretions, and tissue debris from the uterus that recurs in nonpregnant women at monthly intervals.
例句：The onset of catamenia typically occurs during puberty. 月经初潮通常发生在青春期。

例句：Irregular catamenia can be a sign of hormonal imbalance. 月经不调可能是荷尔蒙失调的迹象。

近义词：menses, period, menstruation
反义词:  None (This is a biological process without a direct opposite.)
词性：noun
