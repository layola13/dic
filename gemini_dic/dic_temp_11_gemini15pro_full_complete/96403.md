indispensably:/ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəbli/| adv.|不可或缺地，绝对必要地|Essential, absolutely necessary; in a way that cannot be done without.|例句：Water is indispensably important for survival. 水对于生存至关重要。|近义词：essentially, necessarily, vitally, crucially|反义词:unnecessarily, needlessly|词性:副词

indispensable:/ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəbəl/| adj.|不可缺少的，绝对必要的|Essential, absolutely necessary; something that cannot be done without.|例句：A good dictionary is an indispensable tool for learning a new language. 一本好词典是学习一门外语必不可少的工具。|近义词：essential, crucial, vital, necessary|反义词:unnecessary, dispensable, nonessential|词性:形容词


词源:
Late Middle English: from late Latin indispensabilis, from in- ‘not’ + dispensare ‘dispense with’ (see dispense).
