indistinguishable:/ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbl/
adjective
难以区分的，无法辨别的
Not able to be identified as different or distinct.
The twins are so alike that they are virtually indistinguishable. 这对双胞胎如此相似，几乎无法区分。
The counterfeit money was indistinguishable from the real thing.  这些假币与真钞难以区分。
近义词:identical, alike, similar, same
反义词:distinguishable, different, distinct
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + distinguishable (able to be distinguished).  Distinguish comes from Latin distinguere (to separate, distinguish), from dis- (apart) + -stinguere (to prick, mark).
