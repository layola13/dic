arabist: /ˈærəbɪst/ | n. | 阿拉伯学者，阿拉伯问题专家 | A specialist in Arab affairs, language, or culture.  A person with expert knowledge of Arab countries or culture. | She's a renowned arabist and has written extensively on the region. 她是一位著名的阿拉伯学者，撰写了大量关于该地区的著作。|  Example: The arabist translated the ancient manuscript.  这位阿拉伯学者翻译了这份古代手稿。 | Synonym:  Arab scholar, Orientalist (somewhat outdated and potentially offensive), Middle East expert, Islamic scholar (if focus is on Islamic aspects of Arab culture) | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but possibly) non-specialist, layman | noun
