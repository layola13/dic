fertilizers:/ˈfɜːrtəlaɪzərz/ (US) /ˈfɜːtɪlaɪzəz/ (UK)| n. |肥料|Substances added to soil or water to increase its productivity by supplying essential nutrients for plant growth.|Farmers use fertilizers to enrich the soil.  农民使用肥料来肥沃土壤。|
The garden thrived after the application of fertilizers.  施用肥料后，花园欣欣向荣。|
Synonyms: plant food, manure, compost, nutrients|
Antonyms: depletes,  sterilizers|
