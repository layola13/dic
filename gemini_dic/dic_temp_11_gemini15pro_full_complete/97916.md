innocent:/ˈɪnəsənt/| adj. n.|无辜的，清白的；无罪的人；天真的人|Free from moral wrong; not guilty of a crime or offense.  Also, lacking experience or knowledge of the world.|The jury found the defendant innocent. 陪审团认定被告无罪。|Synonyms: blameless, guiltless, pure, harmless|Antonyms: guilty, culpable, sinful|adjective
