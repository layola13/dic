nonfragrant:/nɒnˈfreɪɡrənt/ | adj. | 无香味的，不香的 |  Lacking fragrance; not giving off a pleasant smell. | The nonfragrant flowers were still beautiful despite their lack of scent. 这些无香味的花朵虽然没有香味，但仍然很美丽。| The mushrooms were nonfragrant but delicious. 这些蘑菇没有香味，但却很美味。 | 近义词: odorless, scentless | 反义词: fragrant, aromatic | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "fragrant" (from Latin "fragrantem," present participle of "fragrare" - to emit a sweet smell).
