iroko:/ɪˈroʊkoʊ/ /aɪˈrəʊkəʊ/
| n. | 非洲柚木; 伊洛科木 | A large hardwood tree native to tropical Africa, yielding a durable timber resistant to termites. |  例句：The iroko tree can grow to immense heights.  非洲柚木可以长到很高的高度。

| | | 例句： Iroko wood is prized for its durability and resistance to insects. 伊洛科木因其耐用性和抗虫性而备受珍视。 | 近义词：African teak, odum (Yoruba) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
