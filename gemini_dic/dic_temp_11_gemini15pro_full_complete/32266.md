chid:/tʃaɪd/| vt.|责骂，斥责|To scold or rebuke.|Example: The mother chided her child for misbehaving. 母亲责备孩子行为不端。|Example:  He was chided for his carelessness. 他因粗心大意而受到责备。|近义词：scold, rebuke, reprimand, reproach|反义词:praise, commend|动词

Etymology:
From Old English *cīdan, from Proto-Germanic *kīdaną (“to chide, complain, lament”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱeyd- (“to sorrow, complain”).  Cognate with Old Norse kveða (“to say, speak, tell, recite”), Gothic (qeþan, “to say”).
