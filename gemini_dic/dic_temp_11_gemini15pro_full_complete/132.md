abandoning: /əˈbændənɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
放弃，抛弃
The act of leaving something or someone behind, usually permanently.
They are abandoning the old car. 他们正在抛弃那辆旧车。
He was charged with abandoning his post. 他被指控擅离职守。
synonyms: leaving, deserting, forsaking, relinquishing
antonyms: keeping, retaining, cherishing, supporting
