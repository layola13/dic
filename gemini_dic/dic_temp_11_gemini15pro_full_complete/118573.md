mesosiderite:/ˈmɛzoʊˈsaɪdəˌraɪt/| n.|中铁陨石|A stony-iron meteorite composed of approximately equal parts of metallic nickel-iron and silicate minerals, predominantly pyroxene, plagioclase, and olivine.|Mesosiderites are rare meteorites that offer a glimpse into the early solar system. 中铁陨石是稀有的陨石，让我们得以一窥早期太阳系。|Synonyms: stony-iron meteorite|Antonyms: N/A|noun
