icehouse:/ˈaɪshaʊs/ | n. |冰窖，冷藏库，冰屋|A building used to store ice, especially before refrigeration was common.  A place that is very cold. |例句：The icehouse kept the food fresh for months. 冰窖让食物保持了数月的新鲜。

例句：The cabin felt like an icehouse in the middle of winter.  小屋在隆冬时节感觉像个冰窖。 |近义词：cold storage, freezer, refrigerator |反义词: furnace, oven | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *īs-hūs (attested only as īsærn "iron house for ice"), from īs "ice" + hūs "house".  Cognate with German Eishaus.
