northwester:/ˌnɔːrθˈwɛstər/ | n. adj. |西北风；来自西北的|A strong wind from the northwest.  A person or thing from the northwest. |The northwester blew fiercely all night. 西北风整夜猛烈地吹。|近义词：northwest wind, northerly gale |反义词:southeaster, southerly gale|词性:名词, 形容词
northwesterly:/ˌnɔːrθˈwɛstərli/ | adj. adv. |西北的；向西北的|From or toward the northwest. |The ship sailed in a northwesterly direction. 船向西北方向航行。|近义词：northwest, towards the northwest |反义词:southeasterly, southeast|词性:形容词, 副词
