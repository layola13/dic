emasculate: /ɪˈmæskjʊleɪt/
| vt. | 阉割；削弱；使无男子气概 | To deprive a male of his male role or identity, either literally by castration or figuratively by weakening or diminishing his power, strength, or spirit. |  Critics charged that the new law would emasculate the power of the governor. 批评人士指责新法律将削弱州长的权力。| 近义词： castrate, weaken, diminish | 反义词： empower, strengthen | 词性: 动词

词源: From Latin emasculatus, past participle of emasculare "to castrate," from ex- "out" + masculus "male."
