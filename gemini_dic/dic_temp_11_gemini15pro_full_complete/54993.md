disproving:/dɪsˈpruːvɪŋ/
verb
反驳；证明…是假的
The evidence effectively disproved the defendant's alibi.  证据有效地反驳了被告的不在场证明。
gerund or present participle of disprove
反驳的；正在反驳的
His theory was disproving by recent research. 他的理论被最近的研究反驳了。
Synonyms: refuting, debunking, contradicting
Antonyms: proving, confirming, validating
v.
