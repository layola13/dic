meteorites:/ˈmiːtiəraɪts/| n. |陨石|A piece of rock or metal that has fallen to the earth's surface from outer space.|例句：Scientists studied the meteorites to learn more about the early solar system. 科学家们研究陨石以了解更多关于早期太阳系的信息。

例句：The museum has a large collection of meteorites from around the world.  这家博物馆收藏了来自世界各地的大量陨石。|近义词：meteoroid, bolide|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
