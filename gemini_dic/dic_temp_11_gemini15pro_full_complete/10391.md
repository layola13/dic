appendicular:/ˌæpənˈdɪkjʊlər/
adjective
附属的; 附肢的
Relating to or consisting of an appendage or appendages, especially the limbs.
The appendicular skeleton includes the bones of the limbs. 附属骨骼包括四肢的骨骼。
近义词:  limb, extremity, attached, adjuvant
反义词: axial, central
词性: adjective

appendicular:/ˌæpənˈdɪkjʊlər/
noun
附属物
An appendage or anything that is attached to something larger. (This noun usage is less common than the adjective.)
The sea star's appendiculars helped it cling to the rock. 海星的附属物帮助它紧紧抓住岩石。
近义词: appendage, attachment, adjunct
反义词: core, main body
词性: noun
