nonblendings:/nɒnˈblɛndɪŋz/ | n. | 非混合物；不混合的事物 |  Substances or things that do not blend or mix together.  This word is likely a neologism, formed by adding the prefix "non-" to the present participle "blending".  It describes materials or concepts that resist integration or combination. | The artist experimented with nonblendings, creating a mosaic of distinct colors.  艺术家尝试了非混合物，创造了由不同颜色组成的马赛克。| near synonyms: separates, distinct entities, unmixed substances | antonyms: mixtures, blends, compounds | noun
