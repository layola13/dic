missing-out:/ˈmɪsɪŋ aʊt/ | n. | 错过；错失良机 | The feeling of missing out (FOMO) is a common experience in today's social media-driven world. |  错过（FOMO）的感觉在当今社交媒体驱动的世界中是一种普遍的体验。 | 近义词: loss, omission | 反义词: gain, inclusion | 词性: 名词

missing out:/ˈmɪsɪŋ aʊt/ | phrasal verb | 错过；错失 | I felt like I was missing out by not going to the party. | 我觉得没去参加聚会很遗憾。| 近义词: losing out on, overlooking | 反义词: taking part in, attending | 词性: 动词短语

ETYMOLOGY->

From the verb "miss" (Old English "missan," meaning "to fail to hit, go astray") and the adverb/preposition "out" (Old English "ūt," meaning "outward, outside").
