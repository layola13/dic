mappa:/ˈmæpə/ | n. |地图；（尤指）战役地图；（解剖学）脑图；（基因）图谱|A visual representation of an area, often drawn to scale and showing features of interest.  A detailed diagram or chart illustrating the relationships between parts of something, especially the brain or the genome.|例句：The general studied the mappa of the battlefield. 将军研究了战场地图。

例句：The scientists created a mappa of the human genome. 科学家们绘制了人类基因组图谱。
