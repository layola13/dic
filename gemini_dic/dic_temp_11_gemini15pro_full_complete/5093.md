allay:/əˈleɪ/|vt.|减轻，缓和，平息|to reduce the intensity of (something, such as fear or pain) : RELIEVE|The government is trying to allay public fears about the spread of the disease. 政府正在努力减轻公众对疾病传播的恐惧。|近义词:mitigate, alleviate, assuage, lessen, reduce, relieve, soothe, calm|反义词:aggravate, exacerbate, intensify, increase|verb

allay:/əˈleɪ/|n.|减轻，缓和，平息 (罕见用法，通常用作动词)|the action of allaying something|The medicine provided some allay from the pain.  这种药减轻了一些疼痛。|近义词:mitigation, alleviation, assuagement|反义词:aggravation, exacerbation, intensification|noun
