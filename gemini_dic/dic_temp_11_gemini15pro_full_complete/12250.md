ascendency:/əˈsɛndənsi/ | n. |优势，支配地位，权势|A position of dominant power or influence.|例句：The ascendency of the new political party surprised many.  新政党的崛起让很多人感到惊讶。

例句：Her ascendency in the company was due to her hard work and innovative ideas. 她在公司的优势地位源于她的努力工作和创新思维。|近义词：dominance, supremacy, control, power, authority |反义词：subordination, weakness, impotence |词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ascendentia, from Latin ascendens (present participle of ascendere “to ascend”), from ad- (“to”) + scandere (“to climb”).
