latakia:/ˌlætəˈkiːə/ | n. |拉塔基亚烟草; 拉塔基亚(叙利亚一港市)|A richly flavored, dark, sun-cured tobacco, originally produced in Syria, used especially in pipe tobacco blends.  It is known for its smoky, slightly spicy, and leathery aroma.|例句：He enjoys a pipe tobacco with a strong latakia blend. 他喜欢一种混合了浓烈拉塔基亚烟草的烟斗烟。

例句：Latakia is prized for its unique flavor profile. 拉塔基亚因其独特的风味而备受推崇。|近义词：tobacco, Syrian tobacco |反义词: None |词性:noun
