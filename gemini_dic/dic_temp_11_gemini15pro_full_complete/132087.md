noncreditable:/nɑːnˈkrɛdɪtəbl/
adjective
不可信的；不值得称赞的；不能获得学分的
Not believable or trustworthy; not deserving of praise or admiration; not eligible for academic credit.
例句：His claims about the incident were deemed noncreditable by the investigators.  调查人员认为他对该事件的说法不可信。

例句：The university offers a variety of noncreditable courses for personal enrichment.  这所大学提供各种不计学分的课程，以供个人充实自己。
synonym:unbelievable, dubious, questionable, untrustworthy, disreputable
antonym: credible, believable, trustworthy, reputable, praiseworthy
词性: adjective
