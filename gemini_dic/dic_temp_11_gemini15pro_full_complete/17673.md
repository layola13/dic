becoming:/bɪˈkʌmɪŋ/ | adj.| 合适的，相称的；好看的，吸引人的 | Suitable, appropriate, and pleasing in appearance; attractive. |  That dress is very becoming on you. 那件衣服你穿很合适。| synonyms:  attractive, flattering, suitable, appropriate, fitting, apt, seemly | antonyms: unbecoming, unflattering, inappropriate, unsuitable | adjective

becoming:/bɪˈkʌmɪŋ/ | n.|  行为，举止；风度 | The process of coming to be something or of passing into a new state. | Her becoming a doctor was a great achievement. 她成为一名医生是一项伟大的成就。| synonyms: development, evolution, growth, maturation | antonyms: regression, decline, deterioration | noun
