inconceptible:/ˌɪnkənˈsɛptəbl/ | adj. | 不可思议的；难以理解的；不能想象的 | Not capable of being conceived or imagined; unimaginable. | The vastness of space is almost inconceivable to the human mind.  浩瀚的宇宙对人类的思想来说几乎是不可想象的。

Her sudden change in attitude was inconceivable to me. 她态度的突然转变让我难以理解。 | 近义词: unimaginable, unthinkable, incomprehensible, unbelievable | 反义词: conceivable, imaginable, understandable, believable | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin inconcepabilis, from in- ‘not’ + concepibilis (from concipere ‘conceive’).
