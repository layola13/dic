briariest:/ˈbraɪəriɪst/
adjective
最棘手的，最困难的
Covered with or consisting of briars; thorny.  Figuratively, most difficult or troublesome.
The briariest problem we faced was funding the project.  我们面临的最棘手的问题是项目资金。
近义词:thorniest, prickliest, most difficult
反义词:easiest, simplest, smoothest
词性:adjective
词源:From briar + -iest (superlative suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *brǣmiz.
