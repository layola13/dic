analogously:/əˈnæləɡəsli/
adverb
类似地; 同样地
in a similar way
例句：The brain processes information analogously to a computer. 大脑处理信息的方式类似于计算机。

例句：Just as the heart pumps blood throughout the body, analogously, the engine circulates oil throughout the car. 正如心脏将血液泵送到全身一样，发动机也类似地将油输送到汽车的各个部位。

近义词：similarly, likewise, correspondingly, comparably
反义词：differently, dissimilarly, distinctively
词性：副词
