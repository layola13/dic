coestate:/koʊɪˈsteɪt/
n.
共同继承人; 同地产所有人
A person who inherits property jointly with another.
John and his sister are coestates of their father's farm.
约翰和他的妹妹是他们父亲农场的共同继承人。

The two brothers were coestates of a considerable fortune.
两兄弟是巨额财产的共同继承人。

heir, joint heir, co-heir
