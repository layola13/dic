disavowers:/dɪsəˈvaʊərz/
|n.|否认者；拒绝承认者；不承认者
|People who disavow something; those who deny responsibility or knowledge of something.|
|The disavowers of the conspiracy theory were quickly silenced.|阴谋论的否认者很快就被噤声了。
|synonyms: repudiators, deniers, rejectors
|antonyms: supporters, advocates, endorsers
|noun


disavowal:/ˌdɪsəˈvaʊəl/
|n.|否认；拒绝承认；不承认
|An act of denying responsibility for or knowledge of something.|
|His disavowal of the allegations was met with skepticism.|他对这些指控的否认遭到了怀疑。
|synonyms: denial, repudiation, rejection
|antonyms: acknowledgement, acceptance, admission
|noun
