noncalculable:/ˌnɒnkælkjʊləbl/ | adj. | 不可计算的，无法估计的 | Not able to be calculated or estimated. |  The long-term effects of the policy are noncalculable. 这项政策的长期影响是不可估量的。|  The damage to his reputation was noncalculable. 对他名誉的损害是无法估量的。| 近义词: immeasurable, inestimable, unpredictable | 反义词: calculable, predictable, estimable | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From non- + calculable, from calculate (from Latin calculatus, past participle of calculare "to reckon, compute," from calculus "pebble," diminutive of calx "limestone, pebble").  The prefix non- means "not."
