noce:/noʊs/| n. |婚礼; 婚宴|A wedding feast or reception. |例句：They had a lavish noce after the church ceremony.  他们在教堂仪式后举行了盛大的婚宴。|例句：The noce was held in a beautiful garden overlooking the sea. 婚宴在一个可以俯瞰大海的美丽花园里举行。|近义词：wedding, marriage, nuptials|反义词:divorce, separation|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin "nuptae" (bride), past participle of "nubere" (to marry). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*snubh-" (to marry).
