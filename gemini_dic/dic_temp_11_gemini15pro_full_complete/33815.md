circumfluous:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈfluːəs/ | adj. | 冗长的，绕圈子的 | Flowing around; encompassing; surrounding; also, roundabout, indirect, or redundant in speech or writing. | The professor's circumfluous explanation confused the students more than it helped. 教授冗长的解释让学生们更加困惑，而不是有所帮助。

The politician's circumfluous answer avoided the real issue.  这位政客拐弯抹角的回答避开了真正的问题。| 近义词: wordy, verbose, prolix, rambling | 反义词: concise, succinct, terse, brief | 词性: adjective
