acaroid:/ˈækərɔɪd/| adj. n.| 类似树脂的，金树脂，禾木树脂 |Resembling resin; a resin from certain Australian trees (xanthorrhoea) used in varnishes, stains, and medicine. |The acaroid resin gave the varnish a rich, golden hue.  金树脂使清漆呈现出浓郁的金色色调。| The furniture was polished with an acaroid-based varnish. 这件家具是用一种以禾木树脂为基料的清漆抛光的。 |近义词:resinous, gummy|反义词: non-resinous|词性: adjective, noun|词源: From Greek *ákari* (mite) + *-oeidēs* (-like, resembling).  Initially referring to the resin's resemblance to the secretions of certain mites.
