coparceny:/koʊˈpɑːrsəni/
n.
共同继承；共同遗产；共同继承权
Joint heirship or ownership of property; the state of being a coparcener.
例句：The sisters held the property in coparceny. 姐妹们共同继承了这处房产。

例句：Coparceny was a common form of inheritance in medieval England.  共同继承是中世纪英格兰常见的继承形式。

近义词：joint tenancy, joint ownership, co-ownership
反义词：sole ownership, severalty
词性: 名词
