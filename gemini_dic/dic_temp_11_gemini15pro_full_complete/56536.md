dominate:/ˈdɒmɪneɪt/ (UK) /ˈdɑːmɪneɪt/ (US)| v. |控制，支配，统治；占主导地位|to have control or power over someone or something, or to be the most important or noticeable part of something|例句：The company dominates the market for computer software.  这家公司在计算机软件市场占据主导地位。

|近义词：control, govern, rule, lead, command|反义词：submit, obey, follow|词性：动词

dominate:/ˈdɒmɪneɪt/ (UK) /ˈdɑːmɪneɪt/ (US)| adj. |（基因）显性的|A dominant gene causes a person to have a particular physical characteristic, even if the gene that they inherited from their other parent would normally cause them to have a different characteristic.|例句：Brown eyes are dominant over blue eyes. 褐色眼睛是显性基因，蓝色眼睛是隐性基因。


|近义词：predominant, controlling|反义词：recessive, subordinate|词性：形容词
