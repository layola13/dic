misplants:/ˌmɪsˈplɑːnts/
|n. |移植错误；错位；|An act of planting something in the wrong place or position.  The misplanting of the seedlings resulted in stunted growth.|
|v. |(misplant的第三人称单数)移植错误；错位；|He misplants the seeds every year, resulting in a poor harvest. |
|词性|动词(第三人称单数) 名词|
|中文翻译|移植错误；错位|
|英文解析|To plant (something) in the wrong place or position.|
|例句|The gardener misplants the rose bush in a shady spot. 园丁把玫瑰丛错种在阴凉处。|

|例句|The misplants in the garden were obvious. 花园里移植错误的植物很明显。|

|近义词|misplace, mislocate|
|反义词|transplant correctly, position correctly|
