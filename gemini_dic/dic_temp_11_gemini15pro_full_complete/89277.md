homochromy:/ˌhəʊməˈkrəʊmi/ | n. |保护色，拟态|The homochromy of the chameleon allows it to blend in with its surroundings. 变色龙的保护色使其能够与周围环境融为一体。|The insect's homochromy made it almost invisible against the bark of the tree. 昆虫的保护色使它在树皮上几乎看不见。|近义词：camouflage, mimicry|反义词：conspicuousness|词性: noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek ὁμός (homós, “same”) +  χρῶμα (khrôma, “color”).
