cancels:/ˈkænsəlz/ (US), /ˈkænslz/ (UK)| v.|取消，撤销|to decide that an arranged event will not happen; to stop an order for goods or services that you no longer want|The concert was cancelled due to bad weather.  音乐会因天气恶劣而取消。| synonyms: aborts, revokes, terminates, countermands, voids, rescinds, | antonyms: approves, confirms, permits, authorizes|verb

cancels:/ˈkænslz/ (US), /ˈkænslz/ (UK)| n.|取消，撤销（通常用复数形式）|the act of cancelling something|There have been several flight cancellations due to the storm. 由于暴风雨，有几架航班被取消。| synonyms: annulments, revocations, terminations| antonyms: confirmations, authorizations|noun
