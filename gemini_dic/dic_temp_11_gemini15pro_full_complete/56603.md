donator:/ˈdoʊneɪtər/ (US), /dəʊˈneɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|捐赠者，捐助者|A person who donates something, especially money, to a charity or other cause.|例句：The donator wished to remain anonymous.  这位捐赠者希望保持匿名。

例句：The hospital received a large donation from a generous donator.  这家医院收到了一位慷慨捐赠者的大笔捐款。|近义词：donor, contributor, benefactor|反义词: recipient|词性: 名词
