empathist:/ˈɛmpəθɪst/| n.|同情者；共情者|A person who has the ability to understand and share the feelings of another.|She's a natural empathist and always knows how to comfort her friends. 她是一个天生的共情者，总是知道如何安慰她的朋友。|近义词：sympathizer, compassionate person|反义词：narcissist, sociopath|词性: noun

empathist:/ˈɛmpəθɪst/| n.|移情者；神入者|Someone who can share another person's feelings, as if experiencing those feelings themselves. |He is an empathist and feels the pain of others deeply. 他是一个移情者，能深深地感受到别人的痛苦。|近义词：intuitive, sensitive|反义词：apathetic, unfeeling|词性: noun
