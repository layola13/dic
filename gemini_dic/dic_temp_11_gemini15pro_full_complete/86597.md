heliography:/ˌhiːliˈɒɡrəfi/ /ˌhiːliˈɑːɡrəfi/
n.
日光摄影术；日光通信
A method of transmitting messages by reflecting sunlight with a mirror.  A photographic process using sunlight.
The heliograph was an important tool for communication in the 19th century.  19世纪，日光通信是重要的通讯工具。
He learned heliography while serving in the army.  他在军队服役期间学会了日光反射信号。
近义词:sun-signaling, semaphore
反义词:None
词性:noun


Etymology:  From Greek *hēlios* (sun) + *-graphia* (writing).
