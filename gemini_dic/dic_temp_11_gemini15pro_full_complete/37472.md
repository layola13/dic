commissions:/kəˈmɪʃənz/
n.|佣金，委托，委任|A fee paid for services, especially to a salesperson or agent; the act of authorizing someone to do something|The salesperson earns a 10% commission on every sale. 销售人员每笔销售可获得 10% 的佣金。|synonyms: fees, charges, payments, allowances|antonyms: salaries, wages
n.|委员会|A group of people officially charged with a particular function|The city council set up a commission to study the problem. 市议会设立了一个委员会来研究这个问题。|synonyms: committee, board, council, panel|antonyms: individual
vt.|委任，委托|To formally authorize someone to do something|The artist was commissioned to paint a mural. 艺术家受委托绘制壁画。|synonyms: authorize, appoint, delegate, entrust|antonyms: dismiss, fire
