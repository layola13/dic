jailed:/dʒeɪld/| adj.|被监禁的，入狱的|Imprisoned; confined in jail.|He was jailed for five years for robbery. 他因抢劫罪被判入狱五年。|近义词：imprisoned, incarcerated, confined|反义词：released, freed|词性:形容词
词源：From the verb "jail," which comes from the Old French word "jaiole" meaning "cage."
