endorhizal:/ɛndəˈraɪzəl/| adj.|内生菌根的|Having fungal hyphae that penetrate the cortical cells of the roots.  This forms a symbiotic relationship where the fungus receives carbohydrates from the plant and the plant receives enhanced nutrient uptake, especially phosphorus, from the fungus.|Many orchids are endorhizal, relying on specific fungi for germination and growth.|近义词:mycorrhizal|反义词:ectomycorrhizal|词性:形容词
