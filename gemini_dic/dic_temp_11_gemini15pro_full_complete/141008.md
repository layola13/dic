onesie:/ˈwʌnzi/ (US), /ˈwʌnsi/ (UK)| n. |连体衣，连身衣|A loose-fitting one-piece garment combining a top and trousers, often worn as pajamas or loungewear, typically by babies, children, or adults seeking comfort.|例句：My baby looks adorable in his dinosaur onesie. 我的宝宝穿着他的恐龙连体衣看起来很可爱。

例句：I love wearing a onesie on a lazy Sunday. 我喜欢在慵懒的周日穿连体衣。|近义词：jumpsuit, romper, playsuit|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps separates)|词性:noun
