corb:/kɔːrb/| n.| 突出物，托架，乌鸦 |A projecting bracket or support, often in stone or brick, usually to support a cornice, beam, or other architectural element. Also, a raven (archaic).|The corbels supporting the balcony were intricately carved.  阳台的托架雕刻精美。|近义词:bracket, support, console, raven|反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on specific meaning, e.g., recess for architectural meaning)|名词
