loanable:/ˈloʊnəbəl/| adj.|可借出的，可贷的|Something that is loanable can be lent to someone.  |例句：Are there any loanable books in the library? 图书馆里有没有可以借的书？

例句：The museum has a collection of loanable artifacts for educational purposes.  博物馆有一批可供教育用途的文物。|近义词：lendable, available for loan|反义词：unlendable, non-circulating|词性:形容词
