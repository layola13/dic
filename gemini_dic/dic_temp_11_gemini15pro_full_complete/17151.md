battled:/ˈbætld/ | v. | 战斗，搏斗，奋战 | fought, struggled, contended against someone or something. | The two armies battled for control of the city.  两支军队为争夺这座城市的控制权而战。

The firefighters battled the blaze for hours. 消防队员与大火搏斗了几个小时。 | 近义词：fought, struggled, contended, competed, vied | 反义词：yielded, surrendered, submitted | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *batallé, past participle of bataller "to fight, skirmish, battle," from Late Latin battualiare "to fight," from battuere "to beat."
