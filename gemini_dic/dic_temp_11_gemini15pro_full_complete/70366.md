fierily:/ˈfʌɪərɪli/ /ˈfaɪərɪli/| adv.|激烈地，火热地，暴躁地|In a fiery manner; passionately, intensely, angrily.|She delivered a fierily passionate speech about the injustice. 她就这件不公正的事发表了慷慨激昂的演说。|Synonyms: passionately, vehemently, intensely, ardently, fervently, heatedly|Antonyms: calmly, coolly, placidly, apathetically|Word Class: Adverb

Etymology: From "fiery" + "-ly". "Fiery" comes from Middle English "fyry", from Old English "fȳren" ("fiery, on fire"), from Proto-Germanic "*fūrijaną" ("to make fire"), from "*fūr-" ("fire").
