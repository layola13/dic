bragging:/ˈbræɡɪŋ/| n. |吹嘘，炫耀|The act of boasting about oneself or one's achievements.|He couldn't stop bragging about his new car. 他不停地吹嘘他的新车。|近义词：boasting, showing off, vaunting|反义词：modesty, humility|noun

bragging:/ˈbræɡɪŋ/| adj. |吹嘘的，自夸的|Given to boasting; boastful.|He had a bragging manner that annoyed many people. 他有一种自夸的举止，惹恼了很多人。|近义词：boastful, swaggering, vainglorious|反义词：modest, humble|adjective
