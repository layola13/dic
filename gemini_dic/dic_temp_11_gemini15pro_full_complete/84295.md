half-veiled:/ˌhɑːfˈveɪld/
adjective
半遮掩的；含蓄的；朦胧的
Partially concealed; obscured or suggested rather than explicitly stated.
Her face was half-veiled by a lace shawl. 她的脸被蕾丝披肩半遮着。
He gave a half-veiled threat about what would happen if she betrayed him. 他含蓄地威胁了她，如果她背叛他将会发生什么。
near synonyms: partially hidden, obscured, veiled, subtle, implied, intimated
antonyms: exposed, revealed, overt, explicit
词性: 形容词
