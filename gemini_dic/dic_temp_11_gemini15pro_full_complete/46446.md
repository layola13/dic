daguerreotype:/dəˈɡɛrəˌtaɪp/ /dəˈɡer.ə.taɪp/| n.|银版照相，早期照片|A photograph taken by an early photographic process employing an iodine-sensitized silvered plate and mercury vapor.|例句：The museum has a fascinating collection of daguerreotypes from the 19th century.  这家博物馆收藏了19世纪引人入胜的银版照片。

例句：The daguerreotype captured a fleeting moment in time, preserving it for posterity. 这张银版照片捕捉到了一个稍纵即逝的瞬间，并将其保存了下来。|近义词：photograph, image, picture|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a specific photographic process)|词性:noun
