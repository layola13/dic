gluts:/ɡlʌts/| n. v.|供过于求；过剩；充斥|An excessive quantity of something, especially so much that it cannot be sold or used.  A situation where there is more of something than is needed or wanted.|The market was glutted with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价的进口商品。|近义词：surplus, excess, oversupply, plethora|反义词: shortage, scarcity, dearth, lack|n.
gluts:/ɡlʌts/| v. |使…充满；使…过量；狼吞虎咽|To supply or fill to excess. To eat or consume to excess.|The warehouses were glutted with unsold goods. 仓库里堆满了未售出的货物。 He glutted himself on chocolate cake. 他狼吞虎咽地吃巧克力蛋糕。|近义词：flood, saturate, gorge, overfill|反义词: deprive, starve, deplete|v.
