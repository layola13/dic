below-the-fold:/bɪˈloʊ ðə foʊld/ | adj. |线下内容，网页折叠线以下的内容| Refers to content on a webpage that is only visible after scrolling down.  Originally a newspaper term referring to the lower half of the front page, less prominent than the top half ("above the fold").  In the digital age, it refers to any content that requires scrolling to be seen. | The most important news stories are placed above the fold, while less crucial articles are below the fold.  最重要的新闻放在首屏，而次要的文章则放在线下。

The call to action button was placed below the fold, resulting in fewer clicks.  行动号召按钮被放在了线下内容区域，导致点击次数减少。 | synonyms:  less prominent, secondary, lower down | antonyms: above the fold, prominent, leading, primary | adjective
