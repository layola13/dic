communism: /ˈkɒmjʊnɪzəm/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmjənɪzəm/ (US)| n. |共产主义|A political theory derived from Karl Marx, advocating class war and leading to a society in which all property is publicly owned and each person works and is paid according to their abilities and needs.|The spread of communism was a major concern during the Cold War.| 近义词: Marxism, socialism | 反义词: capitalism, individualism | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *communisme*, from *commun* (“common”) + *-isme* (English *-ism*). *Commun* is ultimately from Latin *commūnis*.
