multimedias:/ˌmʌltiˈmiːdiəz/ | n. | 多媒体（资源，内容，系统） |  Refers to content that uses a combination of different content forms such as text, audio, images, animations, video and interactive content.  It can also refer to the systems used to create, store, and deliver such content. |  The website offers a rich variety of multimedias, including videos, podcasts, and interactive games. 该网站提供丰富的多媒体内容，包括视频、播客和互动游戏。 | synonyms: multimedia resources, multimedia content, multimedia systems | antonyms: single medium, unimodal | noun

multimedia:/ˌmʌltiˈmiːdiə/ | adj. | 多媒体的 | Designed to incorporate or utilize multiple forms of media, such as text, audio, video, and graphics. |  The museum has a multimedia exhibit on the history of flight. 博物馆有一个关于飞行史的多媒体展览。| synonyms: interactive, digital, integrated | antonyms: traditional, analog, single-medium | adjective
