commensurately:/kəˈmɛnʃərətli/
adverb
相应地，相称地
In a manner that is proportionate or corresponding in size, amount, or degree.
Her salary increased commensurately with her responsibilities. 她的薪水随着她的责任增加而相应增加。

The reward was commensurate with the effort involved.  报酬与所付出的努力相称。

近义词: proportionately, correspondingly, appropriately, suitably, accordingly
反义词: disproportionately, inadequately, insufficiently
词性: adverb
词源: From Late Latin commensuratus, past participle of commensurare "to measure in agreement with," from com- "together" + mensurare "to measure."
