dreaminess:/ˈdriːminəsnɪs/
n.|梦幻状态; 空想癖; 朦胧;|The quality of being dreamy or given to daydreaming. A state of being pleasantly unrealistic or impractical.|She had a certain dreaminess about her that made her seem otherworldly. 她身上有一种梦幻般的气质，让她看起来像是不属于这个世界的人。|近义词：reverie, fantasy, daydreaming, absent-mindedness|反义词：realism, practicality, alertness|词性: 名词
