charitably:/ˈtʃærɪtəbli/
adverb
慈善地；宽厚地
in a charitable way; kindly.
She charitably overlooked his rudeness. 她宽厚地忽略了他的粗鲁。
Near Synonyms: kindly, generously, benevolently, compassionately, humanely, leniently, tolerantly, forgivingly
Antonyms: unkindly, ungenerously, malevolently, cruelly, harshly, severely
词性: 副词
