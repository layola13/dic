nerdy:/ˈnɜːdi/ (US) /ˈnɜːdi/ (UK)| adj.| 书呆子气的；笨拙的；不合时宜的|Characterized by great enthusiasm for and knowledge about a particular subject, especially one of specialist or niche interest. Often associated with a lack of social skills or a preoccupation with intellectual pursuits over social interaction.|He's a bit nerdy, always talking about computers. 他有点书呆子气，总是谈论电脑。|Synonyms: geeky, dorky, bookish, studious|Antonyms: cool, popular, fashionable|adjective
