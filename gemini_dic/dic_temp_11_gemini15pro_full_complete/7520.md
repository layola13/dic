annelid:/ˈænəlɪd/| n. |环节动物|A segmented worm, such as an earthworm or leech, belonging to the phylum Annelida.  These worms are characterized by their segmented bodies.|例句：Earthworms are a common type of annelid found in gardens. 蚯蚓是花园里常见的一种环节动物。

例句：Leeches, also annelids, were historically used in medicine. 水蛭也是环节动物，历史上曾用于医学。|近义词：segmented worm, worm|反义词: None|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin Annelida, from Latin annellus (“little ring”) + -ida (animal order suffix), diminutive of Latin anus (“ring, circle”).  Relates to the segmented, ring-like appearance of these worms.
