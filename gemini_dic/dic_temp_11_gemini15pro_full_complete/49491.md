delves:/dɛlvz/| v. (third-person singular simple present delves, present participle delving, simple past delved or dove, past participle delved or dove)|探究，钻研；深入研究|To search deeply and thoroughly into something; to investigate or research something intensively.|He delved into the archives looking for information about his ancestors. 他深入档案馆寻找关于他祖先的信息。|Synonyms: explore, investigate, probe, examine, research|Antonyms: skim, ignore, neglect|verb

delves:/dɛlvz/| n. (plural of delve)|探究，钻研 (较少用作名词)|The act of delving; a deep search or investigation. |His delves into history unearthed some fascinating facts.  他对历史的探究发现了一些引人入胜的事实。|Synonyms: explorations, investigations, probes, examinations, researches|Antonyms: glances, oversights, neglects|noun
