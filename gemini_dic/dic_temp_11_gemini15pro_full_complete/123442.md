monopolizers:/məˈnɒpəlaɪzəz/
n.
垄断者，独占者
People or companies that monopolize something, gaining exclusive control over it and preventing others from participating.
Microsoft was accused of being a monopolizers in the operating system market. 微软被指控在操作系统市场上垄断。
The government is trying to break up the monopolizers in the telecommunications industry. 政府正在努力打破电信行业的垄断。
Synonyms:cornerers, controllers, dominators
Antonyms:competitors, sharers
