oversentimentalize: /ˌoʊvərˌsɛntɪˈmɛntəlaɪz/
verb
过度感伤化; 过于多愁善感
To indulge in excessive sentimentality; to make something seem more sentimental than it actually is.
She tends to oversentimentalize the death of her cat, treating it like the loss of a child. 她倾向于过度感伤化猫的死亡，把它当作失去一个孩子一样对待。
He oversentimentalized his childhood, remembering only the good times and ignoring the difficulties. 他过度美化了自己的童年，只记得美好的时光，而忽略了其中的困难。
近义词: overdramatize, exaggerate, romanticize
反义词: understate, downplay
词性: verb
