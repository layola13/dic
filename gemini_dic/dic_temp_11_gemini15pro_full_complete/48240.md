decontaminator:/ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪtər/ | n. | 去污剂，净化器，消毒器 |A substance or device used to remove contaminants, such as radioactive material, bacteria, or chemical agents.| The decontaminator quickly neutralized the spilled acid.  去污剂迅速中和了溢出的酸。|  近义词：cleaner, purifier, sterilizer | 反义词: contaminant, pollutant | noun

decontaminate:/ˌdiːkənˈtæmɪneɪt/ | v. | 去污，净化，消毒 | To remove contaminants, making something safe to handle or use. |  They decontaminated the area after the chemical spill.  化学品泄漏后，他们对该区域进行了净化。| 近义词：cleanse, purify, sanitize | 反义词: contaminate, pollute | verb
