knoller:/ˈnɒlər/| n.|小丘；圆丘；小土山|A small hill or mound.|We climbed to the top of the knoller to admire the view. 我们爬上了小山的顶部欣赏风景。|近义词：hill, mound, hillock|反义词：valley, hollow|词性:名词
词源: From Middle English *knollere, from knolle + -er. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *knūllaz.
