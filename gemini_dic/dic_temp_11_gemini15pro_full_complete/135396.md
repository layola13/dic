nonpermeative:/ˌnɒnpɜːrˈmiːətɪv/
adjective
不可渗透的
Not allowing fluids or gases to pass through.
The nonpermeative membrane prevented the diffusion of the solute.  这层不可渗透的膜阻止了溶质的扩散。
The protective coating is nonpermeative to water.  这层保护涂层不透水。

Synonym:
impermeable, impervious, impenetrable

Antonym:
permeable, pervious, porous

词性: adjective
