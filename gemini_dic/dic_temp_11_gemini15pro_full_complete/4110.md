aims:/eɪmz/
| n.| 目标，目的 |Goals or intentions; the things you hope to achieve.|  Her main aim in life is to be happy. 她人生的主要目标是快乐。| synonyms: goals, objectives, targets, intentions, purposes | antonyms:  none (lack of aims might be considered the opposite conceptually) | noun
aims:/eɪmz/
| v. | 瞄准，致力于；旨在，目的是 | To direct something at a target; to intend to achieve something. | He aimed the gun at the target. 他用枪瞄准目标。 | synonyms: point, direct, target, aspire, strive | antonyms: miss, deviate | verb

注意事项: aims 既可以是 aim 的复数形式（名词），也可以是 aim 的第三人称单数形式（动词）。上下文语境很重要。
