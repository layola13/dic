ineffervescible:/ˌɪnɪfərˈvɛsəb(ə)l/
adj.
无泡的；沉闷的；不兴奋的
Lacking effervescence; not bubbly or lively.  Not giving off bubbles, as a liquid. Figuratively, lacking spirit or liveliness; dull.
His ineffervescible personality made him a poor choice for a party host. 他沉闷的个性使他成为一个糟糕的派对主办人选择。
The lecturer's ineffervescible delivery made the complex topic even more difficult to grasp. 演讲者沉闷的表达方式使这个复杂的话题更加难以理解。
Synonyms: flat, dull, lifeless, vapid, insipid, phlegmatic
Antonyms: effervescent, lively, bubbly, sparkling, vivacious
词性: 形容词
