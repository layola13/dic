exabyte:/ˈɛksəbaɪt/| n. |艾字节|A unit of information equal to one quintillion (10^18) bytes or one billion gigabytes.  It's used to measure extremely large amounts of data.|The world's data centers are estimated to store several exabytes of information. 全球的数据中心估计存储了数个艾字节的信息。|近义词:zettabyte (larger)|反义词:kilobyte (smaller)|noun
