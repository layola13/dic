humped:/hʌmpt/
adjective
驼背的，隆起的
having a hump or humps; rounded or arched
例句：The camel is a humped animal. 骆驼是一种驼背的动物。

例句：They drove over a humped bridge. 他们开车过了一座拱桥。
近义词：arched, rounded, hunched, humpbacked
反义词：flat, level
词性: adjective

humped:/hʌmpt/
verb
(使)隆起，(使)弓起
to arch or bend into a hump
例句：The cat humped its back when it saw the dog. 猫看到狗时弓起了背。

例句：The road humped slightly where the culvert ran underneath. 在涵洞的下方，道路略微隆起。
近义词：arch, bend, curve
反义词：straighten, flatten
词性: verb
