accessors:/ˈæksɛsɚz/ (US), /ækˈsɛsəz/ (UK)| n.|访问器，取值器，设值器|Methods used to access or modify the state of an object, typically without directly exposing its underlying data.|Example:  Accessors provide a controlled way to interact with private variables. 访问器提供了一种控制访问私有变量的方式。

Example:  In Java, getters and setters are common accessors. 在Java中，getter和setter是常见的访问器。|近义词：getters, setters, mutators|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
