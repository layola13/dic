hyperlipidaemic:/ˌhaɪpərˌlɪpɪˈdiːmɪk/ | adj. | 高脂血症的 |  Relating to or characterized by hyperlipidemia, an abnormally high concentration of fats or lipids in the blood. |  Example 1: The patient's hyperlipidaemic condition required dietary changes and medication. 病人的高脂血症需要改变饮食和药物治疗。

Example 2:  Hyperlipidaemic individuals are at increased risk of heart disease. 高脂血症患者患心脏病的风险增加。 | 近义词: hyperlipidemic | 反义词: normolipidemic, hypolipidemic | 词性: adjective

hyperlipidemia:/ˌhaɪpərˌlɪpɪˈdiːmiə/ | n. | 高脂血症 |  An abnormally high concentration of fats or lipids in the blood. | Example 1:  Hyperlipidemia is a major risk factor for cardiovascular disease. 高脂血症是心血管疾病的主要危险因素。

Example 2:  The doctor diagnosed him with hyperlipidemia and prescribed medication to lower his cholesterol. 医生诊断他患有高脂血症，并开了降低胆固醇的药物。 | 近义词: hyperlipemia | 反义词: hypolipidemia | 词性: noun


Etymology:
hyper- (Greek: over, excessive) + lip(o)- (Greek: fat) + -emia (Greek: blood condition)
