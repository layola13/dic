bewake: /bɪˈweɪk/
Verb
唤醒；使觉醒
To rouse from sleep; to awaken.
The rooster's crow bewake the farmer.  公鸡的啼叫唤醒了农民。
Near synonyms: awaken, rouse, waken
Antonyms: lull, soothe
词性: 动词
