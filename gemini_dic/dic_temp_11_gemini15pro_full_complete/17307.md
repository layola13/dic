bcg:/ˌbiː siː ˈdʒiː/| n.|卡介苗; BCG疫苗|Bacillus Calmette–Guérin (BCG) vaccine, a vaccine primarily used against tuberculosis. It is the most widely used vaccine worldwide. BCG is also sometimes used in the treatment of bladder cancer.|Doctors recommend that all babies should have the BCG vaccination. 医生建议所有婴儿都应该接种卡介苗。|Synonyms: BCG vaccine, TB vaccine|Antonyms: None|noun
