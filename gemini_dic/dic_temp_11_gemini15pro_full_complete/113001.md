lumberer:/ˈlʌmbərər/ (US), /ˈlʌmbərə(r)/ (UK)| n. |伐木工人；木材商|A person who cuts down trees, especially for timber; a person who deals in lumber.|The lumberer felled the giant redwood. 伐木工人砍倒了巨大的红杉树。|Synonyms: logger, woodsman, timberman|Antonyms: conservationist, environmentalist|noun

lumberer:/ˈlʌmbərər/ (US), /ˈlʌmbərə(r)/ (UK)| n. |笨拙行走的人；缓慢移动的人|A person who moves in a clumsy or heavy way.|He lumbered across the room, his heavy boots thudding on the floor. 他笨拙地走过房间，沉重的靴子在地板上发出咚咚声。|Synonyms: plodder, shuffler|Antonyms: sprinter, dancer|noun
