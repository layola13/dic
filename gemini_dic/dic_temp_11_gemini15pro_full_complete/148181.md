overseverely:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪˈvɪərli/

The child was overseverely scolded for spilling his juice.  孩子因为把果汁洒了而被过度责骂。 2.  Don't judge him overseverely; he's still learning. 不要过于严厉地评判他；他还在学习中。 | 近义词: too harshly, excessively strictly, unduly severely | 反义词: leniently, mildly | 词性: 副词
