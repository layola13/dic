hyperpatriotically:/ˌhaɪpərˌpeɪtriˈɒtɪkli/
| adv. |极端爱国地|In an excessively or exaggeratedly patriotic manner.  Showing extreme patriotism, often to an excessive or unreasonable degree. | He spoke hyperpatriotically about the nation's destiny, claiming it was divinely ordained to rule the world. 他极端爱国地谈论国家的命运，声称其统治世界是神圣的旨意。|
|The politician hyperpatriotically declared that any criticism of the government was treason.  这位政客极端爱国地宣称，任何批评政府的行为都是叛国。|
|Synonyms: ultranationally, jingoistically, chauvinistically|
|Antonyms: unpatriotically, disloyally, treasonously|
|词性: 副词 (Adverb)|
