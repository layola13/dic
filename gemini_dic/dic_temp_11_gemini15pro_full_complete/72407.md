fluently:/ˈfluːəntli/ /ˈfluːəntli/| adv.|流利地，流畅地|In a fluent manner; easily and articulately.  Speaking or writing in a smooth, effortless way.|She speaks French fluently. 她法语说得非常流利。|Synonyms: effortlessly, smoothly, easily, readily, flawlessly|Antonyms: haltingly, awkwardly, hesitantly|Adverb
