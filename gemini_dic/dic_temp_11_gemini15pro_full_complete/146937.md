overintensity:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈtɛnsɪti/| n.|过度强度，过度紧张|An excessive or exaggerated degree of intensity.  The state of being too intense, often referring to emotions, effort, or focus.|The overintensity of her gaze made him uncomfortable. 她过于强烈的注视让他感到不舒服。|近义词：excess, extreme, overkill|反义词: mildness, moderation, calmness|词性:名词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "intensity" (noun). "Intensity" comes from the Late Latin "intensitās,"  from "intensus" (past participle of "intendere" - to stretch out, strain),  ultimately from "in-" (in, into) + "tendere" (to stretch).
