malingering:/məˈlɪŋɡərɪŋ/| n.|装病，诈病|The act of feigning illness, especially to avoid work or duty.|He was accused of malingering to get out of military service. 他被指控装病以逃避兵役。|Synonyms: feigning, faking, shamming|Antonyms: diligence, sincerity|noun

malinger:/məˈlɪŋɡər/| v.i.|装病，诈病|To pretend to be ill, especially to avoid work or duty.|He malingered for weeks, hoping to avoid the exam. 他装病数周，希望逃避考试。|Synonyms: feign, fake, sham|Antonyms: work, strive|verb
