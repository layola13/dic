haemosiderosis:/ˌhiːmoʊˌsɪdərˈoʊsɪs/| n. |血铁质沉着症|A condition characterized by the excessive deposition of iron, stored as haemosiderin, in the tissues.|He was diagnosed with haemosiderosis after experiencing persistent fatigue and joint pain. 他在经历持续疲劳和关节疼痛后被诊断出血铁质沉着症。|Synonym: iron overload|Antonym: iron deficiency|noun
