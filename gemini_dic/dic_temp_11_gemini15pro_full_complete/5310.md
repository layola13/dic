allotting:/əˈlɒtɪŋ/
verb
分配，拨给
The process of distributing or assigning something, such as resources, tasks, or time.
The government is allotting $10 million for education. 政府将拨款一千万美元用于教育。
The manager is allotting tasks to each team member. 经理正在给每个团队成员分配任务。
synonyms: assigning, distributing, allocating, apportioning
antonyms: withholding, denying, refusing
v.
