agroforester:/ˈæɡroʊˈfɔːrɪstər/| n.|农林复合经营者，农林学家|A person who practices or studies agroforestry, a land use management system in which trees or shrubs are grown around or among crops or pastureland.|例句：The agroforester explained the benefits of planting nitrogen-fixing trees alongside crops.  农林学家解释了在农作物旁边种植固氮树木的好处。

agroforestry:/ˌæɡroʊˈfɔːrɪstri/| n.|农林复合经营，农林业|A land use management system in which trees or shrubs are grown around or among crops or pastureland, combining agriculture and forestry.|例句：Agroforestry helps to prevent soil erosion and improve water quality.  农林复合经营有助于防止水土流失和改善水质。

|例句：The organization promotes agroforestry as a sustainable way to increase food production and protect biodiversity. 该组织提倡农林复合经营，将其作为一种可持续的方式来增加粮食产量和保护生物多样性。|近义词：agro-silviculture, forest farming|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
