hyperproducers:/ˈhaɪpərprəˈd(j)uːsərz/
noun
超级生产者，高产者
Individuals or entities that produce an exceptionally large amount of something, often content or information.  These individuals often leverage technology and platforms to reach large audiences.
Hyperproducers on YouTube consistently upload multiple videos a week.  YouTube上的超级生产者每周都会上传多个视频。

Hyperproducers flooded the market with low-quality content. 超级生产者向市场注入了大量低质量的内容。

近义词: prolific producers, high-output producers, power users
反义词: low producers, underachievers
词性: n.
