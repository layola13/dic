midday:/ˈmɪddeɪ/ (US), /ˈmɪddeɪ/ (UK)| n.|正午，中午|The temperature is highest at midday. 中午的温度最高。|We met for lunch at midday. 我们中午见面吃午饭。|近义词: noon, lunchtime|反义词: midnight|词性: 名词
Etymology: From Middle English *middai, from Old English middæg (“midday”), from midd (“mid, middle”) + dæg (“day”). Cognate with Dutch middag (“afternoon”), German Mittag (“noon”), Swedish middag (“noon, dinner”).
