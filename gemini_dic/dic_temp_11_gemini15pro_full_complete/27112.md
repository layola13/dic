caligo:/kəˈlaɪɡoʊ/
noun
黑暗，朦胧，失明
darkness; dimness; blindness
例句：A thick caligo descended upon the valley.  浓厚的黑暗笼罩着山谷。

例句：The caligo of early morning made it difficult to see. 清晨的朦胧使人难以看清。

近义词：darkness, obscurity, gloom, blindness
反义词：light, brightness, clarity, vision
词性：noun
