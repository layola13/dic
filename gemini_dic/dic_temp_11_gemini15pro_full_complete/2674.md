adown:/əˈdaʊn/
adverb
向下地，往下地
From a higher place to a lower place; downwards.
例句：Her hair flowed adown her back. 她的头发垂在背上。

例句：Tears streamed adown her cheeks. 眼泪顺着她的脸颊流了下来。

近义词：down, downwards, downhill
反义词：up, upwards, uphill
词性：副词

Etymology:
From Middle English *adoun, from Old English ofdūne (“downwards, from above, from a higher place; utterly, completely”), equivalent to a- +‎ down. Cognate with Old Frisian adūn (“down”), Middle Dutch adoen (“down”), Old High German adūna, aduno (“down”), Gothic 𐌰𐌸 (“at, to”) + 𐌿𐌽𐌳𐌰𐍂 (“under”).
