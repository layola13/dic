misnarrate:/ˌmɪsnəˈreɪt/
verb
错误地讲述；歪曲叙述
To narrate or tell (a story, for example) incorrectly or inaccurately.
He misnarrated the events of the evening, leaving out key details. 他错误地讲述了当晚发生的事件，遗漏了关键细节。
The witness misnarrated what she saw, confusing the jury. 目击者歪曲了她看到的事实，使陪审团感到困惑。
misrepresent, distort, falsify
relate accurately, report truthfully
v.
