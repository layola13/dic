expiator:/ˈɛkspiˌeɪtər/ | n. |赎罪者，补偿者 |A person who makes amends or atones for a wrong or sin.  |例句：He saw himself as an expiator, seeking to make amends for the sins of his past. 他视自己为一个赎罪者，试图弥补过去的罪孽。

例句：The community looked to the expiator to cleanse them of their collective guilt. 社区指望赎罪者来洗清他们的集体罪恶。|近义词：atoner, redeemer, propitiator |反义词: sinner, wrongdoer |词性: noun
