exulcerative:/ɛkˈsʌlsəreɪtɪv/
adjective
溃疡性的；加重溃疡的
causing or tending to cause ulcers; exacerbating an ulcer.
The patient presented with exulcerative lesions on his lower leg.  病人小腿上出现了溃疡性病变。
Exulcerative colitis is a type of inflammatory bowel disease. 溃疡性结肠炎是一种炎症性肠病。
近义词: ulcerative, ulcerating,  corrosive
反义词: healing, soothing
