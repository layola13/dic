overpractices:/ˌoʊvərˈpræktɪsɪz/ | v. | 过度练习，练习过度 | To practice something excessively, often to the point of detriment.  The pianist overpractices to the point of injury.  钢琴家过度练习以致受伤。 |例句：
He overpractices the piano and now has tendonitis. 他过度练习钢琴，现在得了肌腱炎。

She overpractices her lines and they sound unnatural. 她过度练习台词，听起来很不自然。

| 近义词：overtrain, overdo | 反义词：underpractice |词性: 动词
