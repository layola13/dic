gamify:/ˈɡeɪmɪfaɪ/ /ˈɡeɪmfaɪ/ | vt. |游戏化|To make something, typically a task or process, more engaging by applying game mechanics and design principles.|例句：The company decided to gamify its employee training program to improve engagement.  公司决定游戏化其员工培训计划以提高参与度。

例句：They gamified the sales process by introducing a points-based reward system. 他们通过引入基于积分的奖励制度来游戏化销售流程。|近义词：ludify, make a game of|反义词: N/A |词性: verb
