localhost:/ˈloʊkəlˌhoʊst/ | n. | 本地主机 | A hostname that refers to the current computer used to access a network service locally.  It is a standard hostname given to the address 127.0.0.1. | Example:  You can access the development website on your localhost. 你可以在你的本地主机上访问开发网站。 | Example:  Testing the application on localhost is crucial before deploying it to a server. 在将应用程序部署到服务器之前，在本地主机上进行测试至关重要。 | Synonym: loopback address, home computer | Antonym: remote host | noun
