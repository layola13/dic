cloyless:/ˈklɔɪləs/| adj.|不腻人的，不会使人感到过分的甜或丰富的|Not overly sweet, rich, or excessive; not causing satiety or disgust.  Not cloying.|The cloyless dessert was a refreshing end to the meal. 这道不甜腻的甜点为这顿饭画上了清爽的句号。|近义词：light, refreshing, delicate|反义词:cloying, sickly, rich|词性:形容词
词源: From cloy + -less.  Cloy comes from Old French encloyer "to nail up, stop up, clog," from en- "in" + cloer "to nail shut".
