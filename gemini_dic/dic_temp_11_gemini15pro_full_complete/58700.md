dumpish: /ˈdʌmpɪʃ/ | adj. | 沮丧的，闷闷不乐的 | Feeling or showing gloominess or sullenness. |  He sat there with a dumpish expression on his face after hearing the bad news.  他听到坏消息后，脸上带着沮丧的表情坐在那里。 | Synonyms: gloomy, glum, sullen, morose, downcast, dejected | Antonyms: cheerful, happy, joyous, merry, upbeat | adjective


Etymology: From dump (n.) + -ish.  The noun "dump" originally referred to a state of melancholy or depression, and the suffix "-ish" adds the meaning "somewhat" or "having the quality of."
