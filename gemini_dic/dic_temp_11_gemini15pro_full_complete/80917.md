gowning:/ˈɡaʊnɪŋ/
n.
穿手术衣；穿防护服
The act of putting on a gown, especially a surgical gown or protective clothing.
The doctor began gowning for the surgery. 医生开始穿手术衣准备手术。
Nurses are required to follow strict gowning procedures before entering the operating room.  护士进入手术室前必须遵守严格的穿衣程序。
donning, dressing
undressing, disrobing
noun
