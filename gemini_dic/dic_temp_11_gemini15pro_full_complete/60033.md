educate:/ˈɛdʒʊkeɪt/ (US), /ˈɛdʒʊkeɪt/ (UK)| vt.|教育，培养|To give someone intellectual, moral, or social instruction, typically at a school, college, or university. |例句：They educate their children at home. 他们在家里教育孩子。|例句：The program aims to educate people about the dangers of drug abuse. 该项目旨在教育人们吸毒的危害。|近义词：train, teach, instruct, school, tutor|反义词：mislead, misinform|词性: verb

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Latin educat- ‘brought up, led forth’, from the verb educare, from e- (variant of ex-) ‘out of, from’ + ducere ‘to lead’.
