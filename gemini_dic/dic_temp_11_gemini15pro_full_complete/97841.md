inlaid:/ɪnˈleɪd/ /ˈɪnˌleɪd/| adj. |镶嵌的；嵌入的|Decorated or furnished by inserting pieces of contrasting material. |The wooden box was inlaid with mother-of-pearl.  木盒镶嵌着珍珠母。|近义词：embedded, inset, inserted|反义词: plain, unembellished|adjective
inlay:/ˈɪnleɪ/ /ˈɪnˌleɪ/| n. |镶嵌物；镶嵌工艺|Material inlaid or the process of inlaying. |The inlay on the guitar was intricate and beautiful. 吉他的镶嵌工艺复杂而精美。|近义词：insetting, insertion|反义词: - |noun
inlay:/ɪnˈleɪ/ /ˈɪnˌleɪ/| vt. |镶嵌；嵌入|To decorate by inserting pieces of contrasting material.|He inlaid the table with silver wire. 他用银丝镶嵌桌子。|近义词：embed, insert, set in|反义词: extract, remove|verb
