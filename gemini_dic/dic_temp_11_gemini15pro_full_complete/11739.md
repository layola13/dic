aromatically:/ˌærəˈmætɪkli/
adverb|芳香地，芬芳地|In a way that has a pleasant and distinctive smell.|The dish was aromatically spiced. 这道菜加了香料，闻起来很香。|近义词：fragrantly, sweetly, pleasingly|反义词:unpleasantly, offensively|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From aroma + -atically.  "Aroma" comes from the Greek 'arōma', meaning 'spice, fragrant herb'.  "-atically" is a suffix forming adverbs from adjectives ending in "-atic."
