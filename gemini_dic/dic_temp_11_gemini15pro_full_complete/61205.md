elves:/ɛlvz/
n.
精灵（elf的复数形式）
Plural form of elf, a mythical creature of folk tales, typically having magical powers, pointed ears, and a capricious nature.
The elves danced in the moonlight. 精灵在月光下跳舞。
The children left cookies out for Santa's elves. 孩子们为圣诞老人的精灵留下了饼干。
近义词：fairies, sprites, pixies
反义词：(No direct antonym for a mythical creature)
词性：Noun
