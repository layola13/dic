councilist: /ˈkaʊnsəlɪst/ | n. | 市政主义者；地方议会议员 | A person who advocates for or is a member of a local council, especially one who emphasizes local control and interests.  |  The councilist argued for greater autonomy for the city.  这位市政主义者主张赋予城市更大的自治权。 |  Example: The councilist voted against the regional development plan. 这位地方议会议员投票反对区域发展计划。 | Synonym: council member, councilor, local politician | Antonym: centralist | Noun
