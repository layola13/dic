monovalent:/ˌmɒnəˈveɪlənt/ /ˌmɑːnəˈveɪlənt/| adj.|单价的，一价的|Having a valency of one; capable of bonding with only one other atom or radical.|Sodium is a monovalent element. 钠是一种单价元素.|Synonyms: univalent|Antonyms: multivalent, polyvalent|adjective

monovalent:/ˌmɒnəˈveɪlənt/ /ˌmɑːnəˈveɪlənt/| n.|单价物质|A monovalent atom, radical, or element.|The reaction requires a monovalent cation. 这个反应需要一个一价阳离子。|Synonyms: univalent|Antonyms: multivalent, polyvalent|noun
