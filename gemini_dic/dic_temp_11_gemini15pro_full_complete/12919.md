Assyria:/əˈsɪriə/ | n. | 亚述 | An ancient empire in northern Mesopotamia, existing from approximately the 25th century BC to its destruction in the 7th century BC.  It was known for its powerful military, sophisticated administration, and cultural achievements. |  The Assyrian empire was vast and powerful. 亚述帝国幅员辽阔，势力强大。

Assyria controlled a large part of the Near East. 亚述控制了近东的大部分地区。| 近义词: Mesopotamia (in its broadest sense), Ancient Near East | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun


Etymology:
From Latin *Assyria*, from Ancient Greek *Ἀσσυρία (Assuría)*,  from Akkadian *Aššur*,  the name of the city and its chief god. Ultimately of uncertain origin.
