atrophous:/ˈætrəfəs/| adj.|萎缩的，发育不全的|Wasted away, diminished in size and function due to disease, injury, or lack of use.|The patient's leg muscles were atrophous after being in a cast for months. 病人的腿部肌肉在打几个月石膏后萎缩了。|近义词：shrunken, withered, wasted, underdeveloped|反义词：developed, enlarged, healthy|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek *átrophos*, from *a-* (without) + *trophḗ* (nourishment).
