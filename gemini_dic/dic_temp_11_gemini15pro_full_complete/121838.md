misremembers:/ˌmɪsrɪˈmɛmbərz/ | v. | 记错，误记 | To remember incorrectly; to have a false or inaccurate memory of something. | She misremembers the details of the accident. 她记错了事故的细节。|  He often misremembers names and faces. 他经常记错名字和面孔。 | synonyms: misrecollects, forgets, distorts | antonyms: remembers, recalls, recollects | verb
