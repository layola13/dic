avowers:/əˈvaʊərz/
n.
公开承认者；公开声明者
People who openly declare or acknowledge something, especially a belief or opinion.
The avowers of the new religion were persecuted.  这些新宗教的公开承认者遭到了迫害。
近义词:proclaimers, declarers, affirmers
反义词:deniers, concealers
词性:名词

avow:/əˈvaʊ/
v.
公开承认；公开声明
To declare or acknowledge openly, frankly, and publicly.
He avowed his love for her. 他公开承认爱她。
近义词:declare, admit, confess, affirm
反义词:deny, conceal
词性:动词
