oracularity:/ˌɔːrækjuˈlærɪti/ |n.|神谕性，预言能力|The oracularity of her pronouncements was both impressive and unnerving. 她的预言能力既令人印象深刻，又令人不安。|The ancient Greeks believed in the oracularity of the Delphi Oracle. 古希腊人相信德尔斐神谕的神谕性。|近义词：prophecy, prediction, prescience|反义词：uncertainty, vagueness|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *oracularitas, from Latin oraculum (“oracle”) + -itas (“-ity”).
