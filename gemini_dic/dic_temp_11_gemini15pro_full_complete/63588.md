PHRASE->
envisage doing something: 设想做某事
He envisaged travelling the world after retirement. 他设想退休后环游世界。

envisage that: 设想…
I envisage that the project will be completed on time. 我预计项目将按时完成。

envisage someone doing something: 设想某人做某事
She envisaged him becoming a successful doctor. 她设想他成为一名成功的医生。

difficulty envisaging something: 难以设想某事
I have difficulty envisaging life without my family. 我很难想象没有家人的生活。

can/could envisage: 可以/能够设想
I can envisage a future where everyone has access to clean water. 我可以设想一个每个人都能获得清洁水的未来。
