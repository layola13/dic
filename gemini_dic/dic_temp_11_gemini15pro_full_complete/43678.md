credentialed:/krɪˈdɛnʃəld/
| adj. |有资格证书的；获得证书的；经认证的|Having the necessary credentials, qualifications, or certifications.|Only credentialed journalists were allowed into the press conference. 只有持有记者证的记者才能进入新闻发布会。|近义词：certified, qualified, licensed, authorized|反义词：unqualified, unlicensed, unauthorized|词性: 形容词
词源: From credential + -ed, meaning "provided with credentials."  The word "credential" comes from the Latin "credentia" meaning "belief, trust."
