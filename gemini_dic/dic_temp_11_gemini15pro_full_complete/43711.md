creditworthiness:/ˈkrɛdɪtˌwɜːrðiːnɪs/| n.|信誉;信用可靠性;资信;信用度|The ability of a person or company to repay borrowed money.  A lender will consider your creditworthiness before approving a loan.|The bank assessed the creditworthiness of the applicant before granting the loan.  银行在批准贷款前评估了申请人的信誉。|Synonyms: credit standing, credit rating, solvency, reliability|Antonyms: insolvency, unreliability|noun
