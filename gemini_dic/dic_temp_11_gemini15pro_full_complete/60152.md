effectuous: /ɪˈfɛktʃuəs/
adjective
有效的，生效的
Producing a desired effect; effective.  Having the power to produce an outward effect.
例句：His pleas were effectuous, and the king granted him pardon. 他的恳求生效了，国王赦免了他。

例句：The effectuous medicine quickly relieved her pain.  这有效的药迅速缓解了她的疼痛。

近义词：effective, potent, efficacious
反义词：ineffective, futile, useless
词性: adjective

词源：From Late Latin *effectuosus, from Latin effectus (“effect”) + -osus (“-ous”).
