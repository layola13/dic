accede:/əkˈsiːd/| vi.|同意，加入，就任|To agree to a demand, request, or treaty.  To assume an office or position.|The company finally acceded to the workers' demands for a pay raise.  这家公司最终同意了工人加薪的要求。|Synonyms: consent, comply, acquiesce, assent|Antonyms: refuse, reject, decline, dissent|verb

accede:/əkˈsiːd/| vi.|同意，加入，就任|To come into a position of power. |She acceded to the throne after the death of her father.  在她父亲去世后，她继承了王位。|Synonyms: succeed, inherit, assume|Antonyms: abdicate, resign|verb
