Example:  Researchers are studying nonvolitions in patients with Tourette syndrome. 研究人员正在研究图雷特综合征患者的非自主行为。 | 近义词: involuntary movements, spasms, tics, impulses | 反义词: voluntary actions, conscious actions | 词性: noun
