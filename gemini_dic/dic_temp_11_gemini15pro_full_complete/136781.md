nonseriousness:/nɒnˈsɪəriəsnəs/ | n. | 轻浮, 不严肃, 不认真 |  lack of seriousness; the state of not being serious.  |  His nonseriousness about the situation angered his parents.  他对于这种情况不认真的态度激怒了他的父母。 | synonyms: frivolity, levity, lightheartedness, flippancy | antonyms: seriousness, gravity, solemnity | noun

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "seriousness" (noun derived from "serious") The prefix "non-" comes from Latin, meaning "not." "Serious" comes from the Latin "serius," meaning "grave, weighty, important."
