one-to-one:/ˌwʌn tə ˈwʌn/ | adj. | 一对一的，个别的 | Characterized by a direct relationship between two individuals or things, with each element of one set corresponding to exactly one element of another set. | Each student gets one-to-one tuition. 每个学生都接受一对一教学。|  We offer one-to-one support for students with learning difficulties. 我们为学习有困难的学生提供一对一的支持。 | individual, private, personal | group, collective | adjective

one-to-one:/ˌwʌn tə ˈwʌn/ | n. | 一对一的关系，一对一的会议 | A meeting or relationship between just two people. | I had a one-to-one with my manager about my performance. 我和我的经理进行了一对一的绩效面谈。 | The therapist offers one-to-ones to clients. 治疗师为客户提供一对一的服务。 | meeting, session, consultation | group meeting, group session | noun
