cabado:/kəˈbɑːdoʊ/ | adj. | 完成的，结束的 | Finished, completed.  Derived from the Spanish word "acabado," meaning finished or completed.  Often used in a context related to art or projects. | The painting is finally cabado. 这幅画终于完成了。| The project is cabado and ready for presentation.  这个项目已经完成，可以进行演示了。|Synonyms: finished, complete, concluded, done|Antonyms: unfinished, incomplete, ongoing, pending|Adjective
