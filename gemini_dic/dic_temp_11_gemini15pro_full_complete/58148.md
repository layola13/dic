drossy:/ˈdrɒsi/ | adj. | 渣滓的；劣质的；无价值的 | Containing or resembling dross; of inferior quality; worthless. | The wine had a drossy sediment at the bottom.  这酒底部有渣滓沉淀。 |  The metal was drossy and unusable. 这金属满是杂质，无法使用。 | 近义词: impure, inferior, worthless, trashy | 反义词: pure, refined, valuable, high-quality | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: From dross + -y. Dross (n.) comes from Middle English dros, from Old English drós "dregs, lees, scum," of uncertain origin.
