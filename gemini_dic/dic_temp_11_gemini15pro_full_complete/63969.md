episodical:/ˌɛpɪˈsɒdɪkəl/ | adj. | 分散性的；插曲式的；偶然发生的；情景记忆的 | Occurring or appearing in episodes; not continuous or regular. Relating to episodic memory, which is the memory of autobiographical events (times, places, associated emotions, and other contextual who, what, when, where, why knowledge) that can be explicitly stated or conjured. |  She had only episodical memories of her childhood. 她对童年的记忆只是片段式的。| synonyms: sporadic, intermittent, occasional, irregular, discontinuous | antonyms: continuous, regular, constant, persistent | adjective

episodic:/ˌɛpɪˈsɒdɪk/ | adj. | 分散性的；插曲式的；偶然发生的；（尤指电视连续剧）由多集组成的| Occurring or appearing in episodes; not continuous or regular. (especially of a television or radio series) consisting of a number of connected parts. |  The show is episodic, with each episode telling a self-contained story.  这部剧是分集的，每一集都讲述一个独立的故事。| synonyms: serial, serialized | antonyms: continuous, regular, constant, persistent | adjective
