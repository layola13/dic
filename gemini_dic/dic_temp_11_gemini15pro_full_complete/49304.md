delict:/ˈdiːlɪkt/| n.| 违法行为，民事侵权行为| An act or omission that gives rise to a civil action; a tort.  A breach of duty in civil law. |例句：The delict of negligence caused significant harm.  过失侵权行为造成了重大损害。

例句：He was sued for the delict of defamation. 他因诽谤侵权行为被起诉。|近义词：tort, offense, misdemeanor, wrongdoing|反义词: lawful act, compliance|词性:noun
