backsword:/ˈbæksɔːrd/| n.|反手刀，单刃剑|A single-edged sword, typically with a basket hilt, used for cutting and thrusting, especially in the 17th and 18th centuries.|He skillfully wielded his backsword in the duel. 他在决斗中熟练地挥舞着他的反手刀。|近义词：sword, blade, cutlass|反义词:shield, armor|noun

backsword:/ˈbæksɔːrd/| v.|用反手刀击剑|To fence or fight with a backsword.|They backsword every Saturday in the park. 他们每个星期六都在公园里用反手刀练习击剑。|近义词：fence, duel, fight|反义词:defend, protect|verb
