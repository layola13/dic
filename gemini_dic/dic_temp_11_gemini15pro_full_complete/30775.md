cestoid:/ˈsɛstɔɪd/| n. adj.|绦虫，绦虫样的|A parasitic flatworm, the adult of which lives in the intestines of vertebrates.  It has a segmented body and lacks a digestive tract.  (一种寄生扁形虫，其成虫生活在脊椎动物的肠道中。它有分节的身体，缺乏消化道。)|The veterinarian diagnosed the dog with a cestode infection. 兽医诊断这只狗感染了绦虫。|The cestode larva burrowed into the muscle tissue. 绦虫幼虫钻入了肌肉组织。|近义词：tapeworm|反义词: None|词性: noun, adjective

cestoid:/ˈsɛstɔɪd/| adj.|绦虫样的|Resembling or characteristic of a cestode. (类似于或具有绦虫特征的)|The creature had a long, segmented body, a cestode characteristic.  这种生物有一个长的、分段的身体，这是绦虫的特征。|The microscopic image revealed cestode-like structures. 显微图像显示出类似绦虫的结构。|近义词：tapeworm-like|反义词: None|词性: adjective
