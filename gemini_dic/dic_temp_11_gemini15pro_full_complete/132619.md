nondisagreement:/ˌnɒndɪsəˈɡriːmənt/ | n. | 非分歧，缺乏分歧 | Absence of disagreement; a state where parties involved do not express opposing views, often implying tacit agreement or acceptance. |  The nondisagreement on the budget proposal allowed the meeting to proceed quickly.  预算提案没有异议，会议得以迅速进行。

The nondisagreement about the plan suggests everyone is on board.  对于该计划缺乏分歧表明大家都同意。 |  accord, concord, harmony, consensus | disagreement, dissent, discord, dispute | noun

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "disagreement" (from "dis-" (prefix indicating reversal or negation) + "agreement" (from Old French "agreement," ultimately from Latin "aggregare" - to gather, unite))
