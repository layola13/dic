non-denial:/ˌnɒn dɪˈnaɪəl/ | n. |不否认;间接承认|An act of not denying something, which can be interpreted as an implicit admission of guilt or responsibility.  It's a statement that avoids directly confirming or denying something, often used in political or diplomatic contexts.| The government's non-denial of the rumors fueled further speculation. 政府对传闻的不否认进一步加剧了猜测。|Near synonyms: implicit admission, tacit acknowledgment, evasion, equivocation | Antonyms: denial, refutation, confirmation, affirmation | noun
