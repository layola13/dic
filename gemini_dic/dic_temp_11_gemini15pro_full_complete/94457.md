impolite:/ɪmpəˈlaɪt/| adj.|不礼貌的，粗鲁的|Not having or showing good manners; rude.|例句：It is impolite to interrupt someone while they are speaking. 打断别人说话是不礼貌的。

例句：His impolite behavior offended many guests. 他的无礼行为冒犯了许多客人。|近义词：rude, disrespectful, discourteous, uncivil|反义词：polite, courteous, respectful, civil|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + polite (from Latin politus, past participle of polire "to polish, refine").  Meaning "not polished, lacking refinement" led to the meaning "rude, discourteous."
