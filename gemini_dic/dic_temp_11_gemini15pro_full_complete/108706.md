lean-on:/ˈliːn ɒn/ | phrasal verb | 依靠，依赖；(非正式)施加压力；逼迫 | to depend on someone or something for support, or to try to influence someone by putting pressure on them |  I always lean on my family for support when things get tough.  我总是在遇到困难时依靠我的家人支持。|  synonyms: depend on, rely on, count on | antonyms: support, assist | verb phrase
