dying:/ˈdaɪɪŋ/| adj. v.|垂死的，临终的；渴望的，极想的|The dying man whispered his last words. 垂死的人低声说了最后一句话。|例句：The dying embers glowed in the fireplace. 壁炉里快要熄灭的余烬发出微光。

例句：She has a dying wish to see her children one last time. 她临终的愿望是再见她的孩子们一面。

近义词： expiring, terminal, fading, yearning, longing|反义词：living, thriving, flourishing|词性:形容词，动词(die的现在分词)

dying:/ˈdaɪɪŋ/| v.|死，死亡 (die的现在分词)|例句：He is dying of cancer. 他快要死于癌症了。

例句：Plants are dying from lack of water. 植物因缺水而枯死。

近义词：perishing, expiring |反义词：living, surviving|词性:动词

词源：From Old English *dēonde, present participle of *dēan  "to die," from Proto-Germanic *daujanan, from Proto-Indo-European *dheu-  "to die, disappear."
