co-educational:/ˌkoʊˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃənl/
adj.
男女同校的，男女合校的
Educating both male and female students together.
My children attend a co-educational school. 我的孩子们在一所男女同校的学校上学。
The university has been co-educational since 1975. 这所大学自1975年以来一直是男女同校的。
近义词:mixed, integrated
反义词:single-sex, segregated
词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From co- (together) + educational (relating to education).  The "co-" prefix comes from the Latin "cum" meaning "with" or "together".
