不服从; 顺从;  不反抗

His nondefiantness surprised everyone, considering his previous rebellious nature.  他顺从的态度让每个人都很惊讶，考虑到他以前叛逆的性格。

Antonyms: defiance, resistance, rebelliousness, disobedience, insubordination
