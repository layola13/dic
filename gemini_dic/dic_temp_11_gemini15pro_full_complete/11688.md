armoured:/ˈɑːməd/ (UK), /ˈɑːrmərd/ (US)| adj.|装甲的，披甲的|Protected with armour; covered with protective metal plates.|例句：The knights rode armoured horses into battle. 骑士们骑着披甲的战马冲入战场。

例句：The armoured car protected the president from attack.  这辆装甲车保护总统免受袭击。


近义词：protected, shielded, fortified|反义词:unprotected, vulnerable|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *armure*, from Old French *armure*, from Latin *armātūra* ("arms, armour"), from *armāre* ("to arm"), from *arma* ("arms, weapons").
