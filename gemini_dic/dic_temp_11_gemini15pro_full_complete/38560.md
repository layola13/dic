concoct:/kənˈkɒkt/ (UK) /kənˈkɑːkt/ (US)| v.| 捏造；编造；调制；配制 | To create or devise something, typically a story, excuse, or plan, especially by combining various elements or ingredients. Also, to make (a drink or meal) by mixing various ingredients.| She concocted a story about missing her train. 她编了个故事说她错过了火车。|synonyms: devise, invent, fabricate, formulate, create, prepare, mix|antonyms: expose, reveal, discover, uncover, unmask|verb

concoction:/kənˈkɒkʃən/ (UK) /kənˈkɑːkʃən/ (US)| n.| 混合物；调合物；捏造的故事 | Something concocted, especially a food or drink made by mixing various ingredients, or an elaborate story or plan. | The drink was a strange concoction of fruit juices and spices. 这种饮料是用果汁和香料调制成的奇怪的混合物。|synonyms: mixture, blend, compound, brew, fabrication, invention|antonyms: component, element, ingredient|noun
