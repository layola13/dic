dewberry:/ˈdjuːbɛri/ /ˈduːberi/| n.|露莓，悬钩子|A small, dark edible berry similar to a blackberry, usually growing on a trailing prickly plant.|例句：The dewberry bushes were laden with ripe fruit. 露莓灌木丛上挂满了成熟的果实。

例句：I picked a handful of dewberries and ate them straight from the vine. 我摘了一把露莓，直接从藤上吃掉了。|近义词：blackberry, bramble|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

dewberry:/ˈdjuːbɛri/ /ˈduːberi/| adj.|露莓的，像露莓的|Resembling or characteristic of a dewberry.|例句：The dewberry jam had a sweet and tart flavor. 露莓酱的味道酸甜可口。

例句：She made a dewberry pie using berries from her garden. 她用自家花园里的浆果做了一个露莓派。|近义词：blackberry-like, bramble-like|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
