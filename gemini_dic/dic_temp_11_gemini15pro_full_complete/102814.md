jargonistic:/ˌdʒɑːrɡəˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 行话的；术语的；含糊不清的 | Using or containing jargon; technical terminology that is difficult for ordinary people to understand. |  Example: The contract was full of jargonistic legal terms.  合同充满了专业的法律术语。 | Synonyms: technical, specialized, esoteric, obscure, abstruse | Antonyms: simple, clear, straightforward, plain, understandable | word property:adjective
