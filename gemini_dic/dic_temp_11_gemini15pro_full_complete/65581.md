evacuee:/ɪˌvækjuˈiː/| n. |撤离者，被疏散者|A person who has been evacuated from a place, especially because of war or disaster.|The evacuees were housed in temporary shelters.  被疏散者被安置在临时住所里。|
evacuee:| n. |撤离者，被疏散者|Someone forced to leave their home because of a dangerous situation.|Many evacuees lost everything in the flood. 许多撤离者在洪水中失去了一切。|近义词:refugee, displaced person|反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps 'resident', 'inhabitant')|词性:noun
