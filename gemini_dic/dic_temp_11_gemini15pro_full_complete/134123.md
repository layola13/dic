nonimpeachable:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpiːtʃəbl/ | adj. | 不可弹劾的，无可指责的 | Not liable to impeachment; not subject to accusation; irreproachable.  | The judge's conduct was deemed nonimpeachable.  法官的行为被认为是无可指责的。 |  His integrity is nonimpeachable. 他的正直无可指责。 | 近义词: blameless, irreproachable, unimpeachable | 反义词: impeachable, culpable, blameworthy | 词性: adjective

词源:
From non- (not) + impeachable (from impeach, from Old French empeechier, from Late Latin impedicare "to fetter," from Latin in- "in" + pedica "fetter, shackle").
