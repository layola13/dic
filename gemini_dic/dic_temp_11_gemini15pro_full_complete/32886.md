chondrodite:/ˈkɒndrəˌdaɪt/ | n. | 硅镁石 |A mineral, magnesium silicate, typically occurring as yellow to reddish-brown granular masses in metamorphic rocks. |Chondrodite is a nesosilicate of magnesium. 硅镁石是一种镁的岛状硅酸盐。| Chondrodite can be found in contact metamorphic zones. 硅镁石可以在接触变质带中找到。 |Synonyms: humite (a related mineral), clinohumite (a related mineral) | Antonyms: None (as it's a specific mineral, it doesn't have a direct opposite) | noun
