exceptant:/ɪkˈspektənt/
adjective
期待的，怀孕的
expecting something, especially something pleasant and exciting; pregnant.
例句：The expectant crowds waited for the parade to begin.  期待的人群等待着游行开始。

例句：The expectant mother felt the baby kick. 准妈妈感觉到胎儿踢了一下。

近义词：expecting, hopeful, anticipating, pregnant (if referring to pregnancy)
反义词：uncertain, doubtful, pessimistic, nonpregnant (if referring to pregnancy)
词性: adjective
