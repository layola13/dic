misbuilds:/ˌmɪsˈbɪldz/
|n.|错误构建，构建错误|An act or instance of building something incorrectly or poorly.|The bridge's collapse was attributed to numerous misbuilds during its construction. 这座桥的倒塌归咎于其建造过程中的 numerous misbuilds. |Example: Software misbuilds can lead to crashes and security vulnerabilities. 软件构建错误会导致崩溃和安全漏洞。|Synonyms:  errors, flaws, defects, glitches, bugs|Antonyms: correct builds, proper constructions|Noun

misbuild:/ˌmɪsˈbɪld/
|v.|错误地构建，构建错误|To build something incorrectly or poorly.|The contractors misbuilt the foundation, causing the entire structure to be unstable. 承包商错误地构建了地基，导致整个结构不稳定。 |Example:  They had to demolish the wall because it was misbuilt.  他们不得不拆除这堵墙，因为它构建错误。|Synonyms:  misconstruct, bungle, mess up|Antonyms: construct correctly, build properly|Verb
