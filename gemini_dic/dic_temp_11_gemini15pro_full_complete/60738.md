electrocute:/ɪˈlɛktrəkjuːt/ /ɪˈlɛktroʊkjuːt/
verb
处死，电死
To kill someone by passing a strong electric current through their body.
The murderer was electrocuted. 谋杀犯被处以电刑。
He was accidentally electrocuted when he touched a live wire. 他不小心碰到一根带电的电线而触电身亡。
Synonyms: execute, kill, assassinate
Antonyms: revive, resuscitate
词性: 动词
