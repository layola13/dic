defalcate:/ˌdiːˈfɔːlkeɪt/ | v. | 挪用；盗用；贪污 | To embezzle or misappropriate (funds placed in one's trust). | The treasurer defalcated $10,000 from the club's funds.  财务主管挪用了俱乐部1万美元的资金。| Synonyms: embezzle, misappropriate, peculate | Antonyms: reimburse, repay | verb

defalcation:/ˌdiːfɔːlˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 挪用；盗用；贪污 | The fraudulent appropriation of funds or property entrusted to one's care. | The company's downfall was caused by the defalcation of its CEO. 公司的倒闭是由其首席执行官的贪污造成的。| Synonyms: embezzlement, misappropriation, peculation | Antonyms: restitution, reimbursement | noun
