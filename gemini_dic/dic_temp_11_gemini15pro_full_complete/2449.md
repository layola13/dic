adjunct:/ˈædʒʌŋkt/ | n. | 附属物；助手；修饰语 | something added or joined to something else, but not essentially a part of it.  A person who is added to a staff or group, especially temporarily. A word or phrase that adds information to a clause or sentence. |  The laboratory is an adjunct to the hospital.  实验室是医院的附属机构。| synonym: addition, accessory, supplement, attachment | antonym: primary, essential | noun

adjunct:/ˈædʒʌŋkt/ | adj. | 附属的；辅助的 |  added or connected in a subordinate capacity; attached to a faculty or staff in a temporary or auxiliary capacity | He worked as an adjunct professor at the university. 他在大学担任兼职教授。| synonym: auxiliary, supplementary, subordinate | antonym: primary, main | adjective
