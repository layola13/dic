canfuls: /ˈkænˌfʊlz/
n.
罐fuls; 满罐;
The amount that a can will hold.  A plural of "canful".
She brought three canfuls of water from the well. 她从井里提了三罐水。
近义词: canfuls, cans
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "empty cans")
词性: noun
