broadspreading:/brɔːdˈsprɛdɪŋ/| adj.|广泛传播的，散布的|Characterized by spreading widely or extensively.|The broadspreading branches of the oak tree provided ample shade. 橡树宽阔的树枝提供了充足的荫凉.|近义词：widespread, far-reaching, extensive|反义词：localized, confined, limited|词性:形容词
Etymology:  "broad" (wide) + "spreading" (present participle of "spread").  "Broad" comes from Old English "brād," and "spread" from Old English "sprǣdan."
