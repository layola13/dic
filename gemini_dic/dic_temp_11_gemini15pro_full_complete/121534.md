mismanaging: /ˌmɪsˈmænɪdʒɪŋ/
verb
管理不善，处置失当
Failing to manage or control something effectively or well.
The company was mismanaging its resources and losing money.  这家公司管理不善，亏损严重。
The project was mismanaged from the start. 这个项目从一开始就管理不善。
mismanaging
近义词: mishandling, misgoverning, bumbling, bungling
反义词: managing, administering, directing
