defiantly:/dɪˈfaɪəntli/
adverb|挑衅地，对抗地，不服从地|In a manner that shows open resistance or bold disobedience.|She defiantly refused to apologize. 她坚决拒绝道歉。|例句：He defiantly crossed his arms and glared at her. 他挑衅地交叉双臂，怒视着她。|近义词：rebelliously, boldly, audaciously|反义词：obediently, submissively, meekly|词性: 副词

defiantly:/dɪˈfaɪəntli/
adverb|对抗地，挑战地|Showing resistance or opposition, often in a bold or provocative way.|The protesters stood defiantly in front of the police. 抗议者挑衅地站在警察面前。|例句：Despite the threats, she defiantly continued her work. 尽管受到威胁，她还是毅然决然地继续她的工作。|近义词：resistantly, oppositionally, provocatively|反义词：cooperatively, submissively, yielding|词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *defiauntly*, from Old French *defiant*, present participle of *defier* (“to defy, challenge”), from Latin *dēfidō* (“I renounce, distrust, defy”), from *dē* (“down, away”) + *fidō* (“I trust”).
