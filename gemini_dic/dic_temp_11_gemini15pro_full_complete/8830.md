antiexpansion:/ˌæntiɪkˈspænʃən/
adjective
反扩张的，反对扩张的
Opposing or resisting expansion, typically territorial or economic.
The senator voiced his antiexpansion sentiments during the debate.  参议员在辩论中表达了他反对扩张的情绪。
The antiexpansion policy helped to stabilize the region. 反扩张政策帮助稳定了该地区。

近义词:anti-growth, contractionary, restrictionist
反义词:expansionist, pro-growth, expansionary
词性:adjective
