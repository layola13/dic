loop:/luːp/ | n. v. | 环，圈，循环，回路；使成环，使成圈，循环 |A shape produced when a line curves round and crosses itself.  | The road makes a wide loop around the lake. 这条路绕着湖形成了一个大圈。 | loop around, loop back, loop the loop, loop through, in the loop, out of the loop | 近义词: circle, ring, circuit, cycle | 反义词: line, straight | 词性: 名词，动词

loop:/luːp/ | v. |  使成环，使成圈，循环 | To form or bend something into a loop. | He looped the rope around the pole. 他把绳子绕在杆子上。 | loop around, loop back, loop the loop, loop through, in the loop, out of the loop | 近义词:  circle, curl, twist | 反义词: straighten, uncoil | 词性: 动词


词源:  Middle English loupe, from Old French, probably of Germanic origin; akin to Old High German luppi ring.
