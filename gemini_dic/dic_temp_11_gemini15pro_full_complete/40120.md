contemplate:/ˈkɒntɛmpleɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntəmpleɪt/ (US)| v. | 沉思；考虑；注视；打算 | To think profoundly and at length; to look at thoughtfully; to have in mind as a possibility. | She sat by the window, contemplating the falling leaves. 她坐在窗边，凝视着落叶。|  He contemplated the meaning of life. 他沉思着人生的意义。| consider, ponder, meditate, muse, ruminate | disregard, ignore | verb
