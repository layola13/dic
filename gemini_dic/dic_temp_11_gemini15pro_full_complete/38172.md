compromissorial:/ˌkɒmprəˈmaɪsəʊriəl/ /ˌkɑːmprəˈmaɪsəʊriəl/
adjective
妥协的；和解的；妥协案的
Relating to or involving compromise.  Characterized by or resulting from compromise.
例句：The senator offered a compromissorial solution to the budget debate. 参议员对预算辩论提出了一个妥协的解决方案。

例句：The compromissorial agreement allowed both sides to claim a victory. 妥协协议允许双方都宣称胜利。

近义词：conciliatory, mediating, compromising
反义词：uncompromising, intransigent
词性：adjective

compromise:/ˈkɒmprəmaɪz/ /ˈkɑːmprəmaɪz/
noun
妥协；折衷；和解
An agreement or settlement of a dispute that is reached by each side making concessions.
例句：The two sides reached a compromise after hours of negotiation. 双方经过数小时的谈判达成了妥协。

例句：They were forced to make a compromise. 他们被迫做出妥协。

近义词：agreement, settlement, understanding
反义词：deadlock, stalemate, impasse
词性：noun

compromise:/ˈkɒmprəmaɪz/ /ˈkɑːmprəmaɪz/
verb
妥协；折衷；损害；危及
Settle a dispute by mutual concession.  Expose or make liable to danger, suspicion, or disrepute.
例句：We are not willing to compromise on quality. 我们不愿意在质量上妥协。

例句：His actions compromised the safety of the mission. 他的行为危及了任务的安全。

近义词：jeopardize, endanger, undermine
反义词：maintain, uphold, protect
词性：verb
