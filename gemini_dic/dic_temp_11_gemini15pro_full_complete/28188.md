capreolate: /ˈkæpriəˌleɪt/ | adj. | 卷须状的，有卷须的 | Having tendrils; resembling a tendril. |  The plant's capreolate stems clung to the trellis.  这种植物的卷须状茎紧紧 clinging to the trellis. | 近义词: tendriled, cirrate | 反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑 smooth, untendriled) | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *capreolatus, from capreolus (“tendril”), diminutive of caper (“goat”).  The connection is likely due to the resemblance of tendrils to a goat's horns.
