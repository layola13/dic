hastiness:/ˈheɪstinəs/| n. |匆忙，仓促，草率|The hastiness of his decision led to disastrous consequences. 他的草率决定导致了灾难性的后果。|例句：The hastiness of the wedding arrangements suggested that they were trying to hide something. 婚礼安排的仓促表明他们试图隐瞒什么。|近义词：rush, hurry, speed, precipitation|反义词：carefulness, deliberation, caution, patience|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle English *hastinesse, from Old French hastinesse, from hastif (“hasty”) + -nesse, equivalent to hasty + -ness.  Ultimately from Frankish *haifstiz.
