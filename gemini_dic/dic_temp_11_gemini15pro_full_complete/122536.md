modding:/ˈmɒdɪŋ/ (US) /ˈmɒdɪŋ/ (UK)| n.| 模组制作；修改；改装 | The act of modifying something, typically a video game, to alter its content or functionality.  This involves changing the game's code, assets, or other files to create new features, characters, levels, or gameplay mechanics. | Example: He spends hours modding his favorite game, adding new weapons and characters.  他花费数小时修改他最喜欢的游戏，添加新的武器和角色。

Example: Modding can extend the lifespan of a game by introducing fresh content. 模组制作可以通过引入新内容来延长游戏的寿命。 | 近义词: customizing, tweaking, altering, hacking (in some contexts) | 反义词:  leaving as is, preserving, maintaining | 词性: noun (gerund)
