caterwaulers:/ˈkætərˌwɔːlərz/
n.
嚎叫者；发出刺耳声音的人或动物
A person or animal that makes a loud, harsh, and unpleasant cry, especially at night.
The caterwaulers in the alley kept me awake all night.
巷子里的嚎叫声让我整夜无法入睡。

近义词:shriekers, screamers, wailers
反义词:songbirds, melodists
