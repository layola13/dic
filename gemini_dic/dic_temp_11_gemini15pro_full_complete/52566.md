diffusely:/dɪˈfjuːslɪ/ | adv. |弥漫地，扩散地|In a spread-out or scattered way, not concentrated.|The light diffused softly through the frosted glass. 光线透过磨砂玻璃柔和地散开。|近义词：widely, extensively, broadly, spread out|反义词：concentratedly, narrowly, focally|词性:副词

Etymology: From Late Latin diffusus, past participle of diffundere "to pour out, spread," from dis- "out" + fundere "to pour."
