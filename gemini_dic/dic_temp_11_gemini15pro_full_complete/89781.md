ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *orizon* (Modern French *horizon*), from Late Latin *horizontem*, accusative of *horizōn*, from Ancient Greek ὁρίζων (horízōn, “limiting”), present participle of ὁρίζω (horízō, “I limit, define, separate”), from ὅρος (hóros, “boundary, limit, landmark”).
