nonvisionaries:/ˌnɒnˈvɪʒənəriz/ | n. | 非远见卓识者；缺乏远见的人 | People who lack foresight or imagination, those who cannot envision future possibilities. | The nonvisionaries were content with the status quo and resisted any proposed changes. 那些缺乏远见的人满足于现状，抵制任何提出的改变。 |  Example 2: The company suffered because the nonvisionaries in leadership failed to anticipate market trends.  由于领导层中缺乏远见的人未能预见市场趋势，公司遭受了损失。 | 近义词: pragmatists, traditionalists, conservatives | 反义词: visionaries, innovators, pioneers | 词性: 名词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
visionary (noun): from Late Latin visionarius "one who sees visions," from visio "sight, vision"
