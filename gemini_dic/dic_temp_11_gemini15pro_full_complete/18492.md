bend over backward:/bɛnd ˈoʊvər ˈbækwərd/ | idiom | 竭尽全力，不遗余力 | To make a great effort to do something, especially to be helpful or accommodating. |  Example: The hotel staff bent over backward to make our stay enjoyable.  酒店员工竭尽全力让我们住得开心。| Synonym: go the extra mile, go out of one's way, pull out all the stops | Antonym:  do the bare minimum, lift a finger (sarcastically) | idiom

bend over backward:/bɛnd ˈoʊvər ˈbækwərd/ | idiom | 竭尽全力，不遗余力 | To strive to please or accommodate someone to an unusual degree.  | Example: She bent over backward to help her clients. 她竭尽全力帮助她的客户。 | Synonym:  fall over oneself, do everything in one's power | Antonym:  do nothing, be unhelpful | idiom
