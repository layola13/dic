headiest:/ˈhedɪɪst/| adj.|最令人兴奋的；最令人陶醉的；最强烈的| (of a feeling or experience) extremely exciting, exhilarating, or intoxicating.|The headiest days of our youth. 我们青春最令人兴奋的日子。|Synonyms: intoxicating, exhilarating, exciting, thrilling, stimulating, invigorating, heady|Antonyms: dull, boring, mundane, sobering|adjective

headiest:/ˈhedɪɪst/| adj.|（酒）最浓烈的，酒精含量最高的| (of alcoholic drink) having a high alcohol content and therefore quickly intoxicating.|A headiest wine. 酒精度最高的葡萄酒。|Synonyms: strong, potent, intoxicating|Antonyms: weak, mild, light|adjective
