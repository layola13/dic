dipterous:/ˈdɪptərəs/| adj.|双翅目的，有双翅的|Having two wings, or belonging to the order Diptera (true flies).|例句：Mosquitoes are dipterous insects. 蚊子是双翅目昆虫。

例句：The dipterous fly landed on the flower. 这只双翅目的苍蝇落在花上。|近义词：two-winged|反义词:apterous (无翅的)|词性:adjective
词源:From Greek di- (two) + pteron (wing)
