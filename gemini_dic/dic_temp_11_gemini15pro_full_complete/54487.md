disinter:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtɜːr/ (US), /ˌdɪsɪnˈtɜː/ (UK)| v. | 掘出；发掘；使重现 | To dig up (something, especially a corpse) from the earth; to unearth; to bring back from obscurity or disuse. | The archaeologists disinterred a Viking longship. 考古学家挖出了一艘维京长船。 |  The historian disinterred forgotten documents that shed new light on the case.  历史学家发掘出被遗忘的文件，为这个案件提供了新的线索。 | 近义词: exhume, unearth, uncover, excavate | 反义词: bury, inter | 词性: 动词
