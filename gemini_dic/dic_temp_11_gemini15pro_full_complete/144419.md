outsuffer:/ˌaʊtˈsʌfər/
verb
比...更长久地受苦;比...活得更长（及物动词）
To suffer or endure longer than (someone or something else).  He outsuffered his illness and lived for another ten years. 他战胜了病魔，又活了十年。
He outsuffered his rivals and won the marathon. 他比他的对手坚持的时间更长，赢得了马拉松比赛。
