bedeguar:/ˈbɛdɪɡɑːr/| n. |蔷薇瘿, 蔷薇 Bedeguar|A mossy or bristly gall found on roses, caused by a gall wasp.  It's also known as a Robin's pincushion.|The bedeguar on the rose bush looked like a fuzzy, reddish ball.|Synonym: Robin's pincushion, rose gall, mossy gall|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps healthy rose tissue)|noun
