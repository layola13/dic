inexterminable:/ˌɪnɪkˈstɜːmɪnəbl/
adjective
不能根除的，无法消灭的
Not able to be destroyed; impossible to kill or get rid of.
The weeds in my garden are seemingly inexterminable.  我花园里的杂草似乎无法根除。
The dictator faced inexterminable resistance from freedom fighters.  独裁者面临着来自自由战士的无法消灭的抵抗。
近义词: indestructible, ineradicable, unquenchable
反义词: eradicable, destructible, extinguishable
词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *inexterminabilis, from in- ‘not’ + exterminabilis (from exterminare ‘to drive out, banish’) + -able.
