asai:/ɑːˈsaɪ/ | n. |巴西莓 |Açaí (pronounced ah-sigh-EE) is a small, dark purple berry that comes from the açaí palm tree, native to the Amazon rainforest. It is known for its high antioxidant content. | I had an açaí bowl for breakfast. 我早餐吃了一碗巴西莓。|  Açaí berries are a popular ingredient in smoothies. 巴西莓是冰沙中很受欢迎的原料。 | synonyms:  açaí berry | antonyms: N/A | noun
