honorarium:/ˌɒnəˈreərɪəm/ /ˌɑːnəˈreriəm/ | n. |酬金，谢礼|A payment given for professional services that are rendered nominally without charge.|例句：He received a small honorarium for his work on the committee. 他因在委员会的工作获得了一笔小小的酬金。

例句：The speaker was given an honorarium of $500. 演讲者获得了500美元的酬金。|近义词：fee, payment, stipend, compensation|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
