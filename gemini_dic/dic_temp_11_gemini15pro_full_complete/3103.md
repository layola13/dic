aerosolization:/ˌɛəroʊˌsɒləˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌɛroʊˌsɑːlɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
气雾化；雾化
The process of converting a liquid or solid substance into a fine mist or spray, suspended in the air as an aerosol.
The aerosolization of the medication allows for direct inhalation into the lungs.  药物的雾化使其可以直接吸入肺部。
atomization, nebulization
