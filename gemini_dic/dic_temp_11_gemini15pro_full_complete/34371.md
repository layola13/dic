classics: /ˈklæsɪks/ | n. | 经典作品；古典文学；古典音乐；杰作；一流的东西 |  Works of literature, art, or music that are widely recognized as having lasting value and importance.  |  Example:  She studied the classics at university. 她大学时学习古典文学。

Example:  Mozart's symphonies are considered classics. 莫扎特的交响曲被认为是经典之作。 | 近义词: masterpieces, masterworks | 反义词: trash, junk | 词性: noun
