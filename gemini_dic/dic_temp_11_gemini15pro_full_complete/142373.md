orthopraxy:/ˈɔːrθəˌpræksi/
n.
正统实践，正确行为
Right conduct, as opposed to right belief or correct doctrine.  Emphasis on conduct and practice rather than faith or belief.
The sect stresses orthopraxy over orthodoxy, emphasizing actions over beliefs.  这个教派强调正统实践胜过正统信仰，注重行为而不是信仰。
近义词: observance, practice
反义词: heresy, heterodoxy
词性: noun
