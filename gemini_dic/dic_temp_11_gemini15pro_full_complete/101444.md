irately:/ˈaɪrətli/ | adv. |愤怒地，生气地 | In an angry and irritated manner. | She slammed the door irately. 她愤怒地摔门而去。|  He irately demanded an explanation. 他愤怒地要求解释。| 近义词：angrily, furiously, wrathfully | 反义词：calmly, peacefully | 词性: 副词

词源:
From Latin īrātus (“angered”), past participle of īrāscor (“to grow angry”).
