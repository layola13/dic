footers:/ˈfʊtərz/
noun
页尾；脚注；底端
The part of a page or document that appears at the bottom, typically containing information such as page numbers, dates, or copyright notices.
The footers on each page display the company logo. 每页的页脚都显示公司标志。
The document's footers contained the author's name and the date.  文档的页脚包含作者姓名和日期。
synonyms: bottom, base, end
antonyms: header, top
