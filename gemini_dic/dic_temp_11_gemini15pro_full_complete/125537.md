multisources:/ˈmʌltiˌsɔːrsɪz/ |n. |多来源，多渠道 |Information gathered from multisources is often more reliable. 从多渠道收集的信息通常更可靠。| The report draws on multisources, including interviews, surveys, and official documents. 这份报告借鉴了多种来源，包括访谈、调查和官方文件。 |近义词: multiple sources, various sources, diverse sources |反义词: single source, sole source |词性: 名词


Etymology:
multi- (prefix): from Latin multus "much, many"
source: from Old French source "a rising, spring, fountainhead; origin, cause"
