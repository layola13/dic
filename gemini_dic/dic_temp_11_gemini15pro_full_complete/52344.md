dictates:/ˈdɪkteɪts/ (UK), /ˈdɪkteɪts/ (US)| v. (第三人称单数)|支配；规定；口述；命令|to give orders, or tell someone exactly what they must do, with total authority;  to say words aloud for someone to write down|The UN will dictate the terms of troop withdrawal.|联合国将规定撤军的条件。|synonyms: orders, commands, decrees, prescribes, imposes, lays down|antonyms: suggests, requests, obeys, follows|verb

dictates:/ˈdɪkteɪts/ (UK), /ˈdɪkteɪts/ (US)| n. (复数)|命令；指示；要求| orders or commands given with authority|The dictates of fashion are constantly changing.|时尚的规则总是在变化。|synonyms: orders, commands, decrees, instructions|antonyms: suggestions, requests, pleas|noun
