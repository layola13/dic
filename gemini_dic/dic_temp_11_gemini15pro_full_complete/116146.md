masala:/məˈsɑːlə/| n.|玛萨拉，混合香料|A mixture of ground spices used in South Asian cooking.|例句：This masala is perfect for chicken.  这种玛萨拉粉非常适合用来做鸡肉。

例句：She added a spoonful of masala to the curry.  她在咖喱里加了一勺玛萨拉粉。|近义词：spice blend, spice mix|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bland seasoning")|词性:noun
