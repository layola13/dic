词源:
From Middle English *grene hil, from Old English grēne "green" + hyll "hill".  The word is a compound of two common Germanic elements.

用法说明:
"Greenhill" is a descriptive noun that refers to a hill covered in green vegetation. It can be used in various contexts, such as nature writing, poetry, or everyday conversation.  It's a straightforward and easily understood term.
