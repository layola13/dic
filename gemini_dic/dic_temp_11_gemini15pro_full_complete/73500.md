foreseeability:/fɔːrˌsiːəˈbɪləti/
n.
可预见性
the ability to anticipate that something might happen in the future
The accident was caused by a lack of foreseeability on the part of the driver.  由于驾驶员缺乏预见性，导致了这起事故。

近义词：predictability,  anticipation
反义词：unforeseeability,  unexpectedness
词性：noun
