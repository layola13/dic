epsomite:/ˈ엡səˌmaɪt/| n.|泻石; 七水硫酸镁|A naturally occurring hydrous magnesium sulfate mineral (MgSO4·7H2O). It is used as a source of magnesium and sulfur, and in medicine as a laxative.|Epsomite is often found in caves and dry lake beds. 泻石常发现于洞穴和干涸的湖床中。|Synonyms: Epsom salt, magnesium sulfate heptahydrate|Antonyms: None|Noun
