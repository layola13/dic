denotative:/dɪˈnəʊtətɪv/ (US) /dɪˈnəʊtətɪv/ (UK)| adj.|指示的，字面的，明确的|Relating to the literal meaning of a word, not its associated meanings; explicit.|The denotative meaning of "home" is simply "a place where one lives," but it can also connote feelings of warmth and comfort.|Synonyms: literal, explicit, exact, precise|Antonyms: connotative, suggestive, implied|Adjective
