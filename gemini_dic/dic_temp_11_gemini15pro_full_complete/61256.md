emancipationist:/ɪˌmænsɪˈpeɪʃənɪst/
n.
解放主义者；废奴主义者
A person who advocates for emancipation, especially the abolition of slavery.
Abraham Lincoln is considered a great emancipationist for his role in freeing the slaves.  亚伯拉罕·林肯因其在解放奴隶中扮演的角色而被认为是一位伟大的解放主义者。
Frederick Douglass was a prominent emancipationist who spoke out against the injustices of slavery.  弗雷德里克·道格拉斯是一位著名的解放主义者，他公开反对奴隶制的不公正。
近义词:abolitionist, liberator, reformer
反义词:slaveholder, enslaver
