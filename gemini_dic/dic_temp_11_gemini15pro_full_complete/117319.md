meddest:/ˈmɛdɪst/| adj.|最爱干涉的，最爱管闲事的|Superlative form of "meddling": most meddling.  Characterized by interfering in other people's affairs without being asked or needed.|例句：She's the meddest person I know, always sticking her nose where it doesn't belong. 她是我认识的最爱管闲事的人，总是把鼻子伸到不该伸的地方。

例句：He's got the meddest mother-in-law in the world. 他有一个世界上最爱干涉的岳母。|近义词：interfering, intrusive, nosy, officious|反义词：respectful, unintrusive, minding one's own business|词性:形容词
