glue-up:/ˈɡluː ʌp/| v.|用胶水粘合|He glued up the broken chair leg. 他用胶水粘好了断了的椅腿。|近义词：bond, adhere, cement|反义词:separate, detach|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "glue" + "up".  "Glue" comes from Old French "glu" (birdlime), ultimately from Late Latin "glus" (glue). "Up" indicates completion or thoroughness of the action.
