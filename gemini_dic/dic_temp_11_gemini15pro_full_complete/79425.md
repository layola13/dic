glistened:/ˈɡlɪsənd/ | v. | 闪耀，闪烁 | To shine with a soft reflected light, often due to a wet or smooth surface. | The dew glistened on the grass. 露水在草地上闪闪发光。| The car glistened in the sunlight. 汽车在阳光下闪闪发光。 | synonyms: sparkled, shimmered, glittered | antonyms: dulled, dimmed | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *glisnian, frequentative of glīsan "to glisten, shine," of Germanic origin; related to Dutch glinsteren and German glimmern.
