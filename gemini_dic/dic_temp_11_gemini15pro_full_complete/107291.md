lamaic:/ləˈmeɪɪk/
adjective
喇嘛的；喇嘛教的
Relating to lamas or Lamaism (Tibetan Buddhism).
The Dalai Lama is the spiritual leader of Lamaic Buddhism. 达赖喇嘛是喇嘛教的精神领袖。
The temple was decorated with intricate lamaic artwork. 这座寺庙装饰着复杂的喇嘛教艺术品。
Synonyms: Tibetan Buddhist
Antonyms: None
word type: adjective
