flimsies:/ˈflɪm.ziz/| n. | 薄纸；廉价印刷品；不值钱的东西 | Thin sheets of paper, especially those used for making carbon copies; cheaply printed matter; things of little value. | The architect's plans were printed on flimsies. 建筑师的图纸印在薄纸上。 |  The newspapers were full of flimsies about the scandal. 报纸上充斥着关于这桩丑闻的廉价印刷品。 | synonyms: cheap copies, throwaways, ephemera | antonyms: valuables, treasures | noun

flimsy:/ˈflɪm.zi/| adj. | 薄弱的；脆弱的；不牢靠的 | Weak; fragile; insubstantial; not strongly made. | The evidence against the suspect was flimsy.  针对嫌疑人的证据很薄弱。 | The bridge was a flimsy structure. 这座桥的结构很不牢固。| synonyms: fragile, weak, thin, insubstantial, slight | antonyms: strong, sturdy, robust, solid | adjective
