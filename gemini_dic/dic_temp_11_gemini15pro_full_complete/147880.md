overreactive:/ˌoʊvərriˈæktɪv/| adj.|反应过度的，过度敏感的|Showing an exaggerated response to something; responding more strongly than is necessary or appropriate.|例句：He's a bit overreactive to criticism. 他对批评有点反应过度。

例句：She tends to be overreactive in stressful situations.  她在压力大的情况下容易反应过度。|近义词：hypersensitive, oversensitive, touchy|反义词：calm, composed, impassive|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + reactive.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much". "Reactive" comes from the verb "react," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "reagere," meaning "to act back" or "to respond."
