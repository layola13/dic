carlock: /ˈkɑːrlɒk/ (UK) /ˈkɑːrlɑːk/ (US)| n.|车锁;方向盘锁|A steering wheel lock that prevents a car from being driven.|例句：He fitted a carlock to his steering wheel as an extra security measure. 他在方向盘上安装了一个方向盘锁作为额外的安全措施。

例句：The carlock was difficult to remove, even with the key. 即使有钥匙，方向盘锁也很难拆卸。|近义词：steering wheel lock, wheel clamp, security device|反义词: None|词性:noun
