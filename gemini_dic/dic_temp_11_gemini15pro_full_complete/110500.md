limax:/ˈlaɪmæks/| n. |蛞蝓; 鼻涕虫|A terrestrial gastropod mollusk without a shell, having a slimy elongated body.  A slug.|The limax left a silvery trail on the pavement.  蛞蝓在人行道上留下了一条银色的痕迹。|近义词：slug, gastropod|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially snail as it has a shell)|noun
