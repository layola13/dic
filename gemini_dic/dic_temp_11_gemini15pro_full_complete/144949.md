overabstemiousness:/ˌoʊvəræbˈstiːmiəsnəs/| n.| 过度节制; 过分禁欲|  The state of being excessively abstemious, characterized by extreme moderation or abstinence, especially in eating and drinking.  It implies going beyond what is considered healthy or reasonable self-control.|His overabstemiousness led to nutritional deficiencies. 他的过度节制导致了营养不良。|Near synonyms: asceticism, austerity, self-denial|Near antonyms: gluttony, indulgence, excess|noun
