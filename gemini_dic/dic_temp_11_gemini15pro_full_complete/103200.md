jet-lagged:/ˈdʒɛtˌlæɡd/| adj.|时差反应的；受时差影响的|Experiencing the tiredness and other physical effects caused by travelling quickly across several time zones.|I was jet-lagged for a week after my trip to Tokyo. 我去东京旅行后，有一个星期都处于时差反应中。|Synonyms:  fatigued, exhausted, weary, tired|Antonyms:  rested, refreshed, energized|Adjective
