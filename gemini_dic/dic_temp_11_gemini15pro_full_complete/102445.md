jacketlike:/ˈdʒækɪtˌlaɪk/
adjective
像夹克的，类似夹克的
Resembling a jacket in form or function.
The garment was jacketlike in its appearance, with a zipper and two pockets. 这件衣服外形像夹克，有拉链和两个口袋。
He wore a jacketlike shirt with buttons down the front. 他穿着一件类似夹克的衬衫，前面有纽扣。
近义词: jacket-style, jacket-shaped
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider describing the opposite appearance)
