chlorophyll: /ˈklɔːrəfɪl/ (US) /ˈklɒrəfɪl/ (UK)| n. |叶绿素|A green pigment, present in all green plants and in cyanobacteria, responsible for the absorption of light to provide energy for photosynthesis.|例句：Chlorophyll is essential for photosynthesis in plants. 叶绿素对植物的光合作用至关重要。|近义词：pigment|反义词: None|Noun

chlorophyll: /ˈklɔːrəfɪl/ (US) /ˈklɒrəfɪl/ (UK)| n. |叶绿素|The green coloring matter of leaves and plants, essential to the production of carbohydrates by photosynthesis, and occurring in a bluish-black form (chlorophyll a) and a dark-green form (chlorophyll b).|例句：The leaves change color in the fall as the chlorophyll breaks down. 秋天树叶变色是因为叶绿素分解了。|近义词：pigment|反义词: None|Noun
