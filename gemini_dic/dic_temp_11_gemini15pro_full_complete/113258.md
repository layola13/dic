lustreless:/ˈlʌstələs/| adj.|无光泽的，暗淡的|Lacking shine or gloss; dull.|The old painting had become lustreless over time. 这幅古老的画作随着时间的推移变得暗淡无光。|近义词：dull, mat, lackluster, dim, drab|反义词：shiny, glossy, bright, lustrous, radiant|词性:形容词
词源: From lustre + -less.  Lustre (from Old French lustre) ultimately derives from Latin lustrare "to brighten, purify".
