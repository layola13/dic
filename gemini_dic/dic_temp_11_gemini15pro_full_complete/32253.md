chickweed:/ˈtʃɪkwiːd/| n.| 繁缕；鸡草|A low-growing plant of the pink family, often considered a weed. It has small white flowers and is a common food source for birds, especially chicks.|Chickweed is often found growing in gardens and lawns. 繁缕经常生长在花园和草坪上。|The chickens pecked at the chickweed in the yard.  鸡在院子里啄食繁缕。|Synonyms: stitchwort, mouse-ear chickweed|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

chickweeds:/ˈtʃɪkwiːdz/| n.| 繁缕（复数）|Plural form of chickweed.|The chickweeds were spreading quickly through the flowerbed. 繁缕在花坛里迅速蔓延。|Pull out the chickweeds before they take over the garden.  把繁缕拔掉，以免它们占领花园。|Synonyms: stitchworts, mouse-ear chickweeds|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
