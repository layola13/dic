nonpolemical:/ˌnɒnpəˈlɛmɪkəl/
adjective
非争论性的；不引起争论的
Not controversial or argumentative; avoiding or refraining from polemics.
The book offers a nonpolemical account of the historical events. 这本书对历史事件进行了非争论性的描述。

The speaker delivered a nonpolemical address, focusing on shared values rather than divisive issues. 演讲者发表了非争论性的讲话，重点关注共同价值观，而不是分裂性问题。


近义词:conciliatory, pacific, irenic, non-confrontational
反义词:polemical, controversial, argumentative, contentious
词性:adjective
