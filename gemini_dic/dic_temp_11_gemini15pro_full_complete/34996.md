cloisterlike: /ˈklɔɪstərˌlaɪk/
adjective
像修道院的; 幽静的
Resembling or suggestive of a cloister; secluded and quiet.
The library had a cloisterlike atmosphere, quiet and conducive to study.  图书馆有一种修道院般的气氛，安静且有助于学习。
The long, dimly lit hallway felt cloisterlike.  这条狭长昏暗的走廊感觉像修道院一样。
近义词: secluded, quiet, monastic, serene, peaceful
反义词: bustling, noisy, public, open, lively
词性: adjective

词源:
From cloister + -like.  Cloister comes from Old French cloistre, from Latin claustrum "enclosed place," from claudere "to close."
