disaffiliated:/dɪsəˈfɪliˌeɪtɪd/
| adj. |脱离的，断绝关系的|No longer affiliated; having broken off an affiliation. |The disaffiliated group formed its own independent organization.  这个脱离的团体组建了自己的独立组织。|Synonyms: unconnected, detached, independent, separate, severed |Antonyms: affiliated, connected, associated, joined, united
|词性:形容词


disaffiliate:/dɪsəˈfɪliˌeɪt/
| vt. |脱离，断绝关系|To officially end a connection or affiliation with a larger group or organization.|The local chapter disaffiliated from the national organization due to disagreements over policy.  由于在政策上的分歧，地方分会脱离了国家组织。|Synonyms: detach, separate, sever, disconnect, disassociate |Antonyms: affiliate, connect, associate, join, unite
|词性:动词


disaffiliation:/dɪsəˌfɪliˈeɪʃən/
| n. |脱离，断绝关系|The act of breaking off an affiliation.|The disaffiliation of the branch caused a significant loss of membership for the national organization. 分支机构的脱离导致国家组织的成员大量流失。|Synonyms: separation, detachment, severance, disconnection, disassociation |Antonyms: affiliation, connection, association, union
|词性:名词


词源:  dis- (prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of") + affiliate + -ed/-ion (suffixes indicating past participle/noun form)
