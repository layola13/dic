dirties:/ˈdɜːrtiz/
verb|弄脏，使变脏|Makes something dirty; soils.
The dog dirties the carpet every time it rains. 狗每次下雨都会弄脏地毯。
He dirtied his hands working in the garden. 他在花园里干活弄脏了手。
近义词:soils, stains, contaminates
反义词:cleans, washes, purifies
词性:verb

dirty:/ˈdɜːrti/
adjective|脏的|Not clean.
My clothes got dirty playing in the mud. 我的衣服在泥里玩耍弄脏了。
The dishes were dirty after the party. 聚会后盘子很脏。
近义词:filthy, soiled, unclean
反义词:clean, pure, spotless
词性:adjective

dirt:/dɜːrt/
noun|污垢，泥土|A substance, such as dust, mud, or grime, that makes something unclean.
There was dirt all over the floor. 地板上到处都是灰尘。
He wiped the dirt off his shoes. 他擦掉了鞋上的污垢。
近义词:grime, filth, soil
反义词:cleanliness, purity
词性:noun
