ell:/ɛl/
noun
长度单位, 英尺
a former measure of length (about 45 inches) used mainly for cloth
The tailor measured out four ells of silk.  裁缝量出了四英尺的丝绸。
近义词: yard, cubit
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑表示短的单位，例如inch)
