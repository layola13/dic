battleaxe:/ˈbætlaks/| n. |战斧；泼妇；悍妇|A weapon consisting of a handle with a bladed axe head.  Figuratively, a formidable, domineering, or aggressive woman, especially an older one.|The museum displayed a Viking battleaxe. 博物馆展出了一把维京战斧。|近义词：axe, hatchet (for the literal meaning); shrew, harridan, virago (for the figurative meaning)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


battleaxes:/ˈbætˌlæksiːz/| n. |战斧（复数）；泼妇；悍妇（复数）|Plural form of "battleaxe," referring to multiple weapons or, figuratively, to more than one formidable, domineering, or aggressive woman.|The warriors brandished their battleaxes. 战士们挥舞着他们的战斧。|近义词：axes, hatchets (literal); shrews, harridans, viragos (figurative)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

词源:
From Middle English *batel-ax, equivalent to *batel (“battle”) + *ax (“axe”).  The figurative meaning of a domineering woman emerged in the 20th century.
