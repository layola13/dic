esquimau: /ˈeskɪmoʊ/ (US) /ˈɛskɪməʊ/ (UK)| n. |爱斯基摩人 (现已弃用，被认为带有贬义)|A term formerly used to refer to the Inuit and Yupik peoples of the Arctic. This term is now considered outdated and offensive, and Inuit or Yupik are the preferred terms. |例句：The term "esquimau" is no longer considered appropriate.  “爱斯基摩人”这个词现在被认为不再合适。

例句：It is important to use respectful and accurate terms like Inuit instead of "esquimau." 使用像因纽特人这样尊重和准确的词语来代替“爱斯基摩人”是很重要的。
