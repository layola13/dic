mccarthyites: /məˈkɑːrθiˌaɪts/
|n.|麦卡锡主义者；麦卡锡的追随者|A supporter of Senator Joseph McCarthy and his anti-communist crusade in the 1950s, often characterized by aggressive and unsubstantiated accusations.|During the 1950s, many innocent people were accused of being McCarthyites and lost their jobs and reputations.|近义词：red-baiters, anti-communists|反义词：communists, leftists|词性: 名词

mccarthyite: /məˈkɑːrθiˌaɪt/
|adj.|麦卡锡主义的；属于麦卡锡主义的；|Characteristic of or relating to Senator Joseph McCarthy and his methods of accusing people of communist sympathies without sufficient evidence.|The McCarthyite era was a dark period in American history.|近义词：anti-communist, red-baiting|反义词：pro-communist, leftist|词性: 形容词
