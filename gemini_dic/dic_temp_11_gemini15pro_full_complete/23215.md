bowfin:/ˈboʊfɪn/| n. |弓鳍鱼|A primitive, air-breathing fish (Amia calva) of North America, having a long dorsal fin and a rounded tail. It is the only surviving member of the Amiiformes order.|例句：The bowfin is a tenacious predator. 弓鳍鱼是一种顽强的捕食者。

例句：Bowfin can survive in oxygen-depleted water. 弓鳍鱼可以在缺氧的水中生存。|近义词：mudfish, dogfish (in some regions)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
etymology->
From bow (curved) + fin, referring to the long, continuous dorsal fin.
