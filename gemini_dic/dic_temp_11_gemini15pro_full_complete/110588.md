limiters:/ˈlɪmɪtərz/
noun
限制器；限制者；限幅器
A device or factor that limits something.
The governor acts as a limiter, preventing the engine from exceeding a certain speed. 调速器充当限制器，防止发动机超过一定速度。
New regulations are acting as limiters on corporate growth. 新法规正在限制企业的发展。
synonyms:  restraint, restriction, constraint, curb, check, control
antonyms:  accelerator, catalyst, enhancer
n.
