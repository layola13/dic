jokery:/ˈdʒoʊkəri/| n.|玩笑; 戏谑; 滑稽行为|An act, utterance, or writing intended to be funny.  The act of telling or writing jokes. |He enjoyed their jokery and banter. 他喜欢他们的玩笑和戏谑。|Synonyms: jesting,  banter,  raillery,  badinage,  jocularity,  fun,  clowning,  horseplay|Antonyms: seriousness, solemnity|noun
