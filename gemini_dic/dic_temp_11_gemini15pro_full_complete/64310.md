equivocates:/ɪˈkwɪvəkeɪts/
|v.|含糊其辞，支吾|To use ambiguous language so as to conceal the truth or avoid committing oneself.|The politician equivocated when asked about his stance on the issue.  这位政客在被问及他对这个问题的立场时含糊其辞。|近义词：prevaricates, evades, hedges|反义词：states clearly, articulates, is forthright|词性: 动词

equivocate:/ɪˈkwɪvəkeɪt/
|n.|含糊其辞，支吾|The use of ambiguous language to conceal the truth or avoid committing oneself.|His equivocation on the subject made it difficult to understand his true intentions. 他在这个问题上的含糊其辞使得很难理解他的真实意图。|近义词：prevarication, evasion, hedging|反义词：clarity, directness, forthrightness|词性: 名词


词源: From Late Latin aequivocatus, past participle of aequivocare (“to call by the same name, use ambiguous language”), from Latin aequus (“equal”) + vocare (“to call”).
