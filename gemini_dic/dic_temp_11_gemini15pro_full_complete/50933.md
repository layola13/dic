desideratum:/dɪˌzɪdəˈreɪtəm/| n.| 渴望得到的东西；必需品|Something that is needed or wanted.|Patience is a desideratum for a teacher. 耐心是教师必备的品质。|近义词:requirement, necessity, essential, requisite, need|反义词:nonessential, luxury|名词
