diorite:/ˈdaɪəraɪt/| n. |闪长岩|A coarse-grained igneous rock composed primarily of plagioclase feldspar, hornblende, and sometimes pyroxene or biotite. It is intermediate in composition between gabbro and granite.|The countertop was made of polished diorite.  这个台面是用抛光的闪长岩制成的。|Synonyms:  andesite (extrusive equivalent)|Antonyms: rhyolite, granite (felsic counterparts)|Noun
