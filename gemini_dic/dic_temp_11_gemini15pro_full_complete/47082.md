datum:/ˈdeɪtəm/, /ˈdɑːtəm/ | n. | 数据；资料；论据 | A single piece of information or fact, often used as a basis for calculation or discussion.  A single measurement or observation. | The datum used to calculate the average rainfall was inaccurate.  用于计算平均降雨量的数据不准确。 | synonyms: fact, figure, statistic, information, detail | antonyms: misinformation, falsehood | noun
