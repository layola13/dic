词源：Okey is a variant spelling of OK, which has a disputed etymology.  One popular theory suggests it originated as an abbreviation of "oll korrect," a humorous misspelling of "all correct" popular in the 1830s.  Another theory links it to the Choctaw word "okeh," meaning "it is so."
