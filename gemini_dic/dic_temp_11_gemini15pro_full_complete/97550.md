ingratitudes:/ɪnˈɡrætɪtjuːdz/
n.
忘恩负义； ingratitude的复数形式
Lack of thankfulness or appreciation for kindness shown.
He showed his ingratitudes by ignoring those who had helped him. 他无视那些帮助过他的人，表现出忘恩负义。
ingratitude, thanklessness, ungratefulness
gratitude, thankfulness, appreciation
noun
