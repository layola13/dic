italics:/ɪˈtælɪks/ | n. | 斜体字 | A style of printing in which the letters slope to the right. |  The title of the book was printed in italics.  这本书的标题是用斜体字印刷的。| synonyms: italic type, slanted letters | antonyms: roman type | noun

italics:/ɪˈtælɪks/ | adj. | 斜体的 | Printed in or using characters that slope to the right. | The italicized words are important.  斜体字是重要的。| synonyms: italicized, slanted | antonyms: roman, upright | adjective
