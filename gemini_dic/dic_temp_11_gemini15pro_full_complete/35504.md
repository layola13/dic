coadjutress:/ˌkoʊəˈdʒuːtrɪs/
noun
女性助手；女帮手
A woman who assists a senior colleague, especially in church work.
例句：The bishop appointed a coadjutress to help him with administrative tasks. 主教任命了一位女助手来帮助他处理行政事务。

例句：She served as a coadjutress to the pastor for many years. 她多年来一直担任牧师的助手。

近义词：assistant, helper, aide, colleague
反义词：opponent, adversary
