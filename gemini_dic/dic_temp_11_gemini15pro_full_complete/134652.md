nonmanagerial:/ˌnɑːnˌmænəˈdʒɪəriəl/
adjective
非管理的；非管理人员的
Not involved in management; not having management responsibilities.
The nonmanagerial staff were unhappy with the new pay scales. 非管理人员对新的工资标准不满意。
Synonyms: nonsupervisory, rank-and-file, subordinate, worker, employee
Antonyms: managerial, executive, administrative, supervisory
词性: 形容词
