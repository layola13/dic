lakeside:/ˈleɪksaɪd/| n. adj.|湖边，湖畔|The area beside a lake.  A lakeside location is attractive to tourists. |例句：We had a picnic by the lakeside. 我们在湖边野餐了。

例句：The lakeside cottage offers stunning views. 湖畔小屋的景色美不胜收。|近义词：shore, waterfront, beach|反义词:inland|noun
