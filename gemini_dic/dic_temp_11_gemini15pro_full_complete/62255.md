encumberment:/ɪnˈkʌmbərmənt/| n.|负债，债务|The state of being encumbered with debt or other financial obligations. |He struggled under the encumberment of heavy debt. 他在沉重的债务负担下挣扎。|近义词：debt, liability, obligation|反义词:asset|noun
