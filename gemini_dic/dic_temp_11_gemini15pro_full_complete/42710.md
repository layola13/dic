countertrippant:/ˌkaʊntərˈtrɪpənt/
adjective
逆行的，反向行走的 (尤指纹章学中的动物)
Walking in the opposite direction, especially of an animal in heraldry.
The lion rampant is a common heraldic symbol, but a countertrippant lion is much rarer.  这头跃立的狮子是常见的纹章符号，但逆行的狮子则更为罕见。
Near Synonyms:  retrograde, reversed
Opposite:  trippant, passant
词性: adjective
