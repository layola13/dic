cat:/kæt/ | n. |猫 |A small, domesticated carnivorous mammal with soft fur, short snout, and retractile claws. It is widely kept as a pet or for catching mice, and several breeds have been developed. |例句：My cat likes to chase laser pointers. 我的猫喜欢追逐激光笔。

例句：The cat sat on the windowsill, basking in the sun.  这只猫坐在窗台上晒太阳。
