disguisements:/dɪsˈɡaɪzmənts/
n.
伪装，掩饰
acts or instances of disguising something or someone; things used to disguise
The detective saw through the criminal's clever disguisements.  侦探识破了罪犯巧妙的伪装。
She used various disguisements to avoid being recognized. 她用各种伪装来避免被认出。
synonyms:  camouflages, masks, covers, cloaks, pretenses
antonyms: revelations, exposures
