arcturus:/ɑːrkˈtjʊərəs/ | n. | 大角星 | A red giant star in the constellation Boötes, the fourth-brightest star in the night sky. | Arcturus is easily visible in the Northern Hemisphere's spring sky.  大角星在北半球的春季天空中很容易看到。| Arcturus is located in the constellation of Bootes. 大角星位于牧夫座。 |Synonyms: Alpha Boötis | Antonyms: N/A | noun
