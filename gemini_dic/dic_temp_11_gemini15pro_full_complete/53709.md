discloister:/dɪsˈklɔɪstər/
verb
使…走出隐居生活；使…公开；揭露
To bring out of seclusion or confinement, especially to release from a monastery or convent.  To reveal or expose something previously hidden or secret.
The historian discloistered a long-forgotten manuscript about the king's secret life. 这位历史学家揭露了一份关于国王秘密生活的长期被遗忘的手稿。
The internet has discloistered many private lives. 互联网揭露了许多私人生活。
近义词: reveal, expose, uncover, unveil
反义词: conceal, hide, secrete, cloister
词性: verb
