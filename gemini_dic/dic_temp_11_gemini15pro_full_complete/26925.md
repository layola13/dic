calamitously:/kəˈlæmɪtəsli/
adverb
灾难性地；悲惨地
in a disastrous or calamitous way.
The project failed calamitously, costing the company millions. 这个项目灾难性地失败了，使公司损失数百万美元。
The bridge collapsed calamitously. 桥梁灾难性地倒塌了。

近义词:disastrously, catastrophically, ruinously
反义词:fortunately, luckily, successfully
词性:adverb
