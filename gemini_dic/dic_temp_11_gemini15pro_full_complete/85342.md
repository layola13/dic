harpy:/ˈhɑːrpi/ | n. |  鹰身女妖；泼妇；凶恶的女人 | A mythical monster with the head and breasts of a woman and the body of a bird, known for its shrill cries and rapaciousness. Also, a shrewish and grasping woman. |  The mythical harpies were said to snatch food from the tables of the Phineus.  神话中鹰身女妖会从菲纽斯的餐桌上抢走食物。

The old woman was a real harpy, always complaining and criticizing.  那个老妇人真是个泼妇，总是抱怨和批评。| 近义词：shrew, virago, vixen | 反义词: angel | 词性: 名词
