irredeemabilities:/ɪˌrɪˈdiːməbɪlɪtiz/
|n.|不可救赎性；无法弥补性；不可挽救性|The state or quality of being irredeemable; multiple instances of this quality.|The irredeemabilities of his past haunted him.  他过去的不可救赎性一直困扰着他。|Synonyms: incorrigibility, depravity, wickedness|Antonyms: redeemability, rectifiability, corrigibility
|词性|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From irredeemable + -ities (suffix forming plural nouns).  Irredeemable itself comes from in- (not) + redeemable (capable of being redeemed), ultimately from Latin redimere (to buy back).
