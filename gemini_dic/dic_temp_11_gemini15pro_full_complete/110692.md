lineament:/ˈlɪniəmənt/| n. | 特征；面貌；轮廓；线条 | A distinctive feature or characteristic, especially of the face.  A line, especially one that is distinctive or characteristic. |  Her face, with its strong lineaments, was instantly recognizable. 她的脸，轮廓分明，一眼就能认出来。

The lineaments of the mountains were softened by the mist.  山脉的轮廓被薄雾柔化了。 | 近义词：feature, characteristic, contour, outline, trait | 反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "blur" in context of visual lineaments) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *lineament*, from Late Latin *lineamentum*, from Latin *linea* ("line") + *-mentum* (suffix forming nouns).
