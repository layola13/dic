nonchurchgoing:/ˌnɑːnˈtʃɜːrtʃˌɡoʊɪŋ/
adjective
不经常去教堂的；非经常去教堂的
Not regularly attending church services.
Many nonchurchgoing people still hold religious beliefs. 许多不经常去教堂的人仍然持有宗教信仰。
The survey targeted both churchgoing and nonchurchgoing individuals. 这项调查针对经常去教堂和不经常去教堂的个人。
synonyms: irreligious, secular, worldly
antonyms: churchgoing, devout, pious
adjective
