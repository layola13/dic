outcrop: /ˈaʊtkrɒp/ (UK) /ˈaʊtkrɑːp/ (US) | n. |露出地面的岩层；露出；出现|A visible exposure of bedrock or other stratum at the earth's surface.|例句：The quarry face revealed a massive limestone outcrop.  采石场表面露出大片石灰岩层。

例句：Outcrops of granite are common in this region. 花岗岩露头在这个地区很常见。

近义词：exposure, protrusion, outthrust |反义词: subduction |词性：名词

outcrop: /ˈaʊtkrɒp/ (UK) /ˈaʊtkrɑːp/ (US) | v. |露出；显露|To appear or become visible; to crop out.|例句：The veins of gold outcropped near the surface. 金矿脉露出地表附近。

例句：The bedrock outcrops in several places along the river. 基岩沿着河流多处露出。


近义词：emerge, appear, surface |反义词: disappear, submerge |词性：动词
