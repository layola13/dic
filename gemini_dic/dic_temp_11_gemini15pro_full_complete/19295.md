biaxal:/baɪˈæksəl/| adj.|双轴的|Having two axes; especially : having two optic axes.|例句：Biaxial crystals have two optic axes. 双轴晶体有两个光轴。

例句：The biaxial stress state is common in thin films. 薄膜中双轴应力状态很常见。|近义词：anisotropic|反义词：uniaxial, isotropic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + axial (relating to an axis). Ultimately from Latin axis (axle, axis).
