growthest: /ˈɡroʊθɪst/
noun
增长主义者;  信奉增长主义的人
A person who believes in or advocates for continuous economic growth, often without regard for its environmental or social consequences.
Example: Some growthests argue that technological advancements will always solve the problems created by economic expansion.  一些增长主义者认为，技术进步总是能解决经济扩张带来的问题。

Example: Critics argue that growthests fail to account for the finite resources of the planet.  批评人士认为，增长主义者没有考虑到地球资源的有限性。

Synonym:  pro-growth advocate, expansionist
Antonym: environmentalist, conservationist, sustainability advocate
词性: 名词
