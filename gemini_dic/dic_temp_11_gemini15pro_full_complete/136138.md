nonreformational:/ˌnɒnˌrɛfərˈmeɪʃənəl/
| adj. |非改革的，非改良的|Not related to or characterized by reformation; not intended to reform.  It describes something that doesn't aim to change or improve an existing system, institution, or practice.| The government's nonreformational approach to the education system has been criticized. 政府对教育系统不进行改革的做法受到了批评。|Synonyms: conservative, traditional, orthodox|Antonyms: reformist, progressive, revolutionary|adjective
