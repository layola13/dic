marathons:/ˈmærəθɒnz/ (UK), /ˈmærəθɑːnz/ (US)| n. |马拉松，耐力赛|A long-distance running race of 26 miles 385 yards (42.195 kilometres). Also used figuratively to describe any long and arduous undertaking.|例句：He ran his first marathon in under four hours. 他在四小时内跑完了他的第一个马拉松。

例句：The negotiations turned into a marathon, lasting over 12 hours.  谈判变成了一场马拉松，持续了12个多小时。|近义词：endurance race, long-distance race|反义词:sprint, dash|词性:noun
