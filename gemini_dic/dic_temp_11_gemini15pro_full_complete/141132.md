onwardness:/ˈɒnwədnəs/ /ˈɑːnwərdnəs/
n.
前进; 进取心; 上进心
The quality or state of moving forward or progressing.  The quality of being eager to advance.
例句：Her onwardness and determination impressed everyone. 她的进取心和决心给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。

例句：The company's onwardness in developing new technologies is commendable.  该公司在开发新技术方面的进取精神值得称赞。

近义词：progress, advancement, drive, ambition, initiative
反义词：stagnation, inertia, apathy
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From onward + -ness.  "Onward" itself comes from Old English "onweard" (toward, forward), from "on" (on, in) + "weard" (toward).
