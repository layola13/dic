controlled:/kənˈtroʊld/ (US), /kənˈtrəʊld/ (UK)| adj.|受控制的；克制的；控制的|Governed or regulated, restrained, deliberate.|例句：The experiment was carried out under controlled conditions. 实验是在受控条件下进行的。|例句：He spoke in a controlled voice, trying not to betray his anger. 他用克制的声音说话，努力不流露出他的愤怒。|近义词：managed, regulated, restrained, governed, contained, curbed|反义词：uncontrolled, unrestrained, wild, free, unchecked|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "control," which comes from the Old French "contreroller" (to check, verify, compare with a copy), from "contre-" (against) + "roller" (to enroll).  Ultimately from Latin "contra" (against) and "rotulus" (a little wheel, roll).
