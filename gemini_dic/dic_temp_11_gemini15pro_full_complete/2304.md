adeptship:/əˈdɛptʃɪp/
n.
熟练,精通;专家的地位,专家身份
The state or quality of being an adept; proficient skill.
He demonstrated his adeptship in calligraphy. 他展示了他在书法方面的精通。
近义词: mastery, proficiency, expertise, skill, competence
反义词: incompetence, inability, ineptitude
词性: 名词

词源: From adept + -ship.  Adept itself comes from Latin *adeptus "obtained," past participle of adipisci "to obtain."
