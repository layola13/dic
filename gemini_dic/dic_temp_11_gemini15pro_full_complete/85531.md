hatchability:/ˌhætʃəˈbɪləti/| n.|孵化率，出雏率|The percentage of fertile eggs that hatch successfully.  The hatchability of the eggs was affected by the temperature and humidity.|The hatchability of the chicken eggs was over 90%.  这些鸡蛋的孵化率超过了90%。|近义词：incubation rate, hatching success|反义词:infertility|词性:noun
