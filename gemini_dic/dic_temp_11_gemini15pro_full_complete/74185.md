fourbe:/furb/| n.|骗子，诡计多端的人|A deceitful and cunning person; a rogue. | He was a fourbe, always looking for ways to cheat people. 他是个骗子，总是想方设法欺骗别人。|  He tricked her out of her inheritance; what a fourbe! 他骗走了她的遗产；真是个骗子！|近义词：deceiver, cheat, trickster, rogue, scoundrel |反义词: honest person, dupe |词性: noun

Etymology: From Old French *forbe*, from Frankish *forbo* (“cunning, sly”).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *frabaudiz* (“deceitful, crafty”).  Related to Old High German *firbeit* (“cunning, deceit”).
