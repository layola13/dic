archives:/ˈɑːrkaɪvz/| n.|档案，档案馆|A collection of historical documents or records providing information about the past.  The city's archives contain birth certificates dating back to the 1800s.  这个城市的档案馆保存着可追溯到19世纪的出生证明。|synonyms: records, annals, chronicles, documents|antonyms:  None (Archives represent preservation, the opposite would be destruction or loss, not a single word.)|noun
archive:/ˈɑːrkaɪv/| vt.|存档，归档|To store or file (something) in an archive. I archived all my old emails. 我把所有旧邮件都存档了。|synonyms: file, store, catalog|antonyms: delete, erase, discard|verb
