endeavored:/ɪnˈdɛvərd/| v.|努力，尽力，尝试|past tense of endeavor; tried hard to do or achieve something.|例句：They endeavored to find a peaceful solution. 他们努力寻找和平的解决方案。|近义词：tried, attempted, strived, aimed|反义词：neglected, ignored, abandoned|词性:动词

endeavor:/ɪnˈdɛvər/| n. v.|努力，尽力；努力，尝试|An attempt to achieve a goal.|例句：His endeavor to climb Mount Everest was successful. 他攀登珠穆朗玛峰的努力成功了。|近义词：attempt, effort, try, strive|反义词：neglect, inaction|词性:名词/动词

词源: From Middle English *endeveren, from Anglo-Norman en dever (“in duty”), equivalent to en (“in”) + dever (“duty”).  Ultimately from Latin dēbēre (“to owe”).
