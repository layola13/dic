empathize: /ˈɛmpəθaɪz/ (US) /ˈɛmpəθʌɪz/ (UK)| v. |同情，共情，感同身受 | To understand and share the feelings of another. | I can empathize with your frustration because I've been in a similar situation. 我能理解你的沮丧，因为我也有过类似的经历。| It's important to empathize with victims of natural disasters. 对自然灾害的受害者表示同情是很重要的。| synonyms: sympathize, relate to, understand, share the feelings of | antonyms: disregard, ignore, be indifferent to | verb
