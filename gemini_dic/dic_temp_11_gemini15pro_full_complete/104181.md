junketer:/ˈdʒʌŋkɪtər/| n.|免费旅游；游览；外出考察|A trip or excursion, especially one that is free or paid for by someone else, often with the implication that it is for pleasure or self-promotion rather than serious business.|The company sponsored a junket to Las Vegas for its top salespeople.  这家公司赞助其顶级销售人员前往拉斯维加斯旅游。|近义词：trip, excursion, outing, jaunt|反义词:work, business trip|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French "jonquette," meaning "cream cheese," or from Italian "giuncata," a kind of fresh cheese, later referring to a basket made of rushes used to carry cheese. The word evolved to mean a feast or banquet, and then to a pleasure trip, often with connotations of extravagance at public expense.
