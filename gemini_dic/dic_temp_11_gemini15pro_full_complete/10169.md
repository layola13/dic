apophthegmatical:/ˌæpəˈθɛɡmətɪk(ə)l/
adjective
格言式的；警句式的；箴言式的
Of the nature of an apophthegm; concise and sententious.
His apophthegmatical pronouncements were often quoted. 他的格言式言论常被引用。

apophthegmatical:/ˌæpəˈθɛɡmətɪk(ə)l/
adjective
精辟的；简洁的
Pithy; terse.
She delivered an apophthegmatical speech, full of wisdom and wit. 她发表了一场精辟的演讲，充满了智慧和机智。


近义词:pithy, terse, concise, epigrammatic, sententious, proverbial
反义词:prolix, verbose, rambling, diffuse

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin apophthegmaticus, from Greek apophthegmatikos, from apophthegma (aphorism), from apophthengesthai (to speak out).
