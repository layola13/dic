hematinon:/ˌhiːməˈtaɪnɒn/ /ˌheməˈtaɪnɒn/ | n. |血凝素| A substance that promotes blood clotting. |  Hematinon is crucial for stopping bleeding. 血凝素对于止血至关重要。| Hematinon helped to clot the blood quickly after the injury. 受伤后，血凝素帮助血液快速凝结。| 近义词: coagulant, clotting factor | 反义词: anticoagulant | 词性: noun

词源:  Derived from Greek *haîma*, *haimatos* meaning "blood."
