hydantoin:/haɪˈdæntɔɪn/ | n. |乙内酰脲；海因|A crystalline heterocyclic compound with the formula C4H4N2O2.  It is an anticonvulsant drug. |Hydantoin is used in the production of some anti-epileptic medications.|近义词:glycolylurea|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology:  From German *Hydantoin*, from *Hydant* (“allantoin”) + *-oin*.  *Hydant* is from Ancient Greek ὑδαντόν (hydantón, “glassy, transparent”) (because allantoin forms glassy crystals) + German *-oin* (chemical suffix for heterocyclic compounds related to furans).
