in-so-many-words:/ɪn ˌsoʊ ˈmɛni ˈwɜːrdz/
|adv.|确切地说；明白地说；简洁地说|explicitly; plainly; directly|He didn't say so in so many words, but he implied that I was lying. 他没有明说，但暗示我在撒谎。|Synonyms: explicitly, directly, plainly, bluntly, frankly|Antonyms: indirectly, implicitly, vaguely, evasively|adverb
