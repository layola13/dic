celticism: /ˈkɛltɪsɪzəm/ | n. |凯尔特主义，凯尔特文化特色|A characteristic of the Celts or their languages, especially in terms of style or subject matter. |例句：The intricate knotwork designs are a striking example of Celticism in early medieval art.  这些复杂的结饰图案是中世纪早期艺术中凯尔特主义的一个突出例子。

例句：The love of nature and the supernatural is a recurring theme in Irish literature, reflecting a strong element of Celticism. 对自然和超自然的热爱是爱尔兰文学中反复出现的主题，反映了凯尔特主义的强烈元素。|近义词：Celticity, Celtic culture, Druidism (in some contexts)|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "non-Celtic culture") |词性: noun
