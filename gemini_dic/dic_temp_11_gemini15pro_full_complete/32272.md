chiders:/ˈtʃaɪdərz/
n.
爱责骂的人;吹毛求疵的人
People who find fault with others; nitpickers.
Those chiders are always complaining about something. 那些吹毛求疵的人总是抱怨个不停。
近义词:critics, faultfinders, nitpickers
反义词:approvers, praisers
