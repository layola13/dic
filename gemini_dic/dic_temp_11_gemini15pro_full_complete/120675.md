misaddressed:/ˌmɪsəˈdrɛst/ | adj. | 写错地址的；寄错地址的 | Incorrectly addressed. | The misaddressed letter eventually reached its intended recipient. 这封写错地址的信最终到达了预定的收件人手中。 |  The package was misaddressed and sent to the wrong city.  这个包裹地址写错了，被送到了错误的城市。 | 近义词: wrongly addressed, incorrectly addressed | 反义词: correctly addressed | 词性:形容词
misaddress:/ˌmɪsəˈdrɛs/ | v. | 写错地址；寄错地址 | To write the wrong address on (an envelope, letter, etc.). | I misaddressed the letter and it was returned to sender. 我把信的地址写错了，它被退回给寄件人了。 | I misaddressed the email and it went to the wrong person. 我把邮件地址写错了，结果邮件发给了错误的人。| 近义词: address incorrectly, address wrongly | 反义词: address correctly | 词性:动词

词源:
mis- (prefix): meaning "wrong" or "badly".  来自古英语，意为“错误的”或“坏的”。
address (verb/noun): from Old French *adresser, meaning "to direct" or "to straighten". 源自古法语 *adresser，意为“引导”或“拉直”。
