isometric-drawing:/ˌaɪsəˈmɛtrɪk ˈdrɔːɪŋ/
n.
等轴测投影图；等角投影图
A type of pictorial drawing in which all three dimensions are drawn at full scale rather than foreshortening them to their actual projected lengths.  It represents a three-dimensional object on a two-dimensional surface, with all lines parallel to the three principal axes drawn to scale and at 120 degrees to one another.
The architect presented an isometric drawing of the proposed building. 建筑师展示了拟建建筑的等轴测图。

近义词: isometric projection, axonometric drawing, 3D drawing
反义词: orthographic projection, plan view, elevation view
词性: noun
