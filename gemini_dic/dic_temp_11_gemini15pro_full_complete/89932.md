horsefly:/ˈhɔːrsflaɪ/ (US) /ˈhɔːsflaɪ/ (UK)| n. |马蝇；虻|A large fly that bites horses and other mammals, including humans. It delivers a painful bite.|例句：The horseflies were swarming around the horses, making them restless. 马蝇成群地围着马飞，使它们烦躁不安。

例句：I got a nasty bite from a horsefly while hiking. 我在徒步旅行时被马蝇狠狠地咬了一口。|近义词：gadfly, cleg, deerfly|反义词: N/A |词性: noun

Etymology: From Old English *horsflege, from hors "horse" + fleoge "fly."
