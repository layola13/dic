mechanizable:/ˌmɛkəˈnaɪzəbl/ |adj.| 可机械化的，可机械操作的 |Capable of being mechanized; adaptable to the use of machines. |例句：Farming is becoming increasingly mechanizable.  农业正变得越来越机械化。

例句：Many tasks in the factory are now mechanizable, reducing the need for manual labor. 工厂里的许多任务现在都可以机械化，减少了对手工劳动的需求。

近义词：automatable, machine-driven, automated |反义词：manual, labor-intensive| 词性: adjective
