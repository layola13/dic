bad-mouth: /ˌbædˈmaʊθ/ | v. | 诋毁，说坏话 | to criticize someone or something unfairly, often spreading untrue rumors | He bad-mouthed his former employer to anyone who would listen. 他向任何愿意听的人诋毁他的前任雇主。|  She's always bad-mouthing her colleagues. 她总是说同事的坏话。 | 近义词: criticize, denigrate, slander, malign, disparage | 反义词: praise, commend, compliment | 词性: 动词

bad-mouth: /ˌbædˈmaʊθ/ | n. | 诋毁，诽谤 | the act of criticizing someone or something unfairly |  I've heard enough of your bad-mouth about him. 我已经听够了你对他的诋毁。 | His constant bad-mouth was starting to annoy everyone. 他不断的诋毁开始惹恼每个人。 | 近义词: criticism, denigration, slander, defamation | 反义词: praise, commendation, compliment | 词性: 名词
