franklinite:/ˈfræŋklɪˌnaɪt/ | n. |  锌铁尖晶石 | A black isometric mineral consisting of oxides of iron, zinc, and manganese.  It is a valuable ore of zinc. | Franklinite is often found associated with zincite and willemite. 锌铁尖晶石常与红锌矿和硅锌矿伴生。 | Synonyms:  zinc iron oxide, gahnite (similar composition) | Antonyms: N/A | noun
