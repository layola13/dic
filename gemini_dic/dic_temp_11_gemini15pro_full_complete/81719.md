greenlier:/ˈɡriːnlɪər/ | adj. | 更绿的，更环保的 |Comparative form of "green," meaning exhibiting more of the qualities associated with the color green (lush vegetation, environmental friendliness, etc.). | The park is greenlier this year than last.  这个公园今年比去年更绿了。|  The company is adopting greenlier practices to reduce its carbon footprint.  这家公司正在采取更环保的做法来减少碳足迹。 |  more environmentally friendly, more sustainable, lusher | less green, browner, less sustainable | adjective
