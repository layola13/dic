metely:/ˈmiːtli/| adv.|适度地，适当地|Moderately; fittingly; suitably.  Meaning "in a measured or proportionate way". Derived from the word "mete," which means "to measure" or "to allot."|He metely praised the performance, avoiding excessive enthusiasm. 他适度地赞扬了表演，避免了过分的热情。|The judge metely sentenced the defendant, considering all the circumstances. 法官适当地判了被告的刑，考虑了所有的情况。|近义词:moderately, appropriately, suitably, fittingly|反义词:excessively, immoderately, inappropriately|词性:副词
