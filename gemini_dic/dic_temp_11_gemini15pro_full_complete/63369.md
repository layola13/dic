entomophaga:/ˌɛntəˈmɒfəɡə/ | n. | 食虫动物；食虫植物 | An organism that feeds on insects. |例句：The Venus flytrap is a well-known entomophaga.  捕蝇草是一种著名的食虫植物。

例句：Many bats are nocturnal entomophaga, playing a vital role in insect control. 许多蝙蝠是夜间活动的食虫动物，在控制昆虫方面起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词：insectivore | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Greek entomon "insect" + -phaga "eater," from phagein "to eat."
