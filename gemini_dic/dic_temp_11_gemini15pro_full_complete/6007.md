amebae's:/əˈmiːbiːz/
n.

belonging to or associated with an ameba (or amoeba), a single-celled organism.
The amebae's pseudopods extended towards the food particle. 变形虫的伪足向食物颗粒伸展。

词性:Possessive noun (名词所有格)
