bondsman:/ˈbɑːndzmən/| n.|保释人，保证人，奴隶|A person who acts as surety for another, especially one who pledges money or property to secure the release of a prisoner.  A slave or serf.|例句：The bondsman paid the bail to secure the defendant's release. 保释人支付了保释金以确保被告获释。

例句：In feudal times, the bondsman was tied to the land and his lord. 在封建时代，奴隶被束缚于土地和他的领主。|近义词：surety, guarantor, bailer, slave, serf|反义词:freeman|词性:noun
