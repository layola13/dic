grubbing:/ˈɡrʌbɪŋ/
grubbing| n. | 觅食; 搜寻; 翻掘; | the act of digging in the ground for food or other things |  The pigs were grubbing for roots in the forest. 猪在森林里觅食树根。| 近义词：searching, foraging, rummaging, digging | 反义词: planting, sowing | 词性: 名词

grubbing:/ˈɡrʌbɪŋ/
grubbing| adj. |  卑鄙的; 贪婪的; | involving digging in the ground; concerned with petty or sordid matters | He had a grubbing nature, always looking for ways to exploit others. 他生性卑鄙，总是想方设法剥削他人。 | 近义词：greedy, avaricious, sordid, grasping | 反义词: generous, altruistic | 词性: 形容词
