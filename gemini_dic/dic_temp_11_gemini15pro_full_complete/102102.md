isostemonous:/ˌaɪsoʊˈstɛmənəs/| adj.|等雄蕊的，同数雄蕊的|Having the same number of stamens as petals.  Referring to a flower where the number of stamens equals the number of petals.|Example: The lily is an isostemonous flower, with six stamens and six petals. 百合花是等雄蕊的，有六个雄蕊和六个花瓣。|Example: Isostemonous flowers are common in many plant families. 等雄蕊的花在许多植物科中很常见。|近义词:homostemonous|反义词:anisostemonous|词性:adjective
