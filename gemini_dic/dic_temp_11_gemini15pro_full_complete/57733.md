dreadness:/ˈdrɛdnəs/| n.|可怕; 令人恐惧|The dreadness of the situation overwhelmed her.  这种情况的可怕让她不知所措。|The movie conveyed a sense of utter dreadness. 这部电影传达了一种极度恐惧的感觉。|近义词：terror, fearfulness, frightfulness, awfulness|反义词：calmness, peacefulness, tranquility|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dread (n.) + -ness.  Dread comes from Old English *drǣdan "to fear greatly," related to Old Saxon and Old High German intratan "to threaten, urge, compel."
