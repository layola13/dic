mandilion: /ˈmændɪljən/ | n. |披风，斗篷，无袖短外套|A loose, sleeveless garment, worn over the shoulders, often with openings for the arms; a type of cape or cloak.  | He wore a mandilion over his doublet. 他在紧身上衣外套了一件披风。| 近义词: cape, cloak, shawl | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "jacket" as representing a more fitted garment) | noun
