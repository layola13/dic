ETYMOLOGY->
From *bromine* + *-ite*.  Bromine gets its name from the Greek word *βρῶμος (brōmos)* meaning "stench," referring to its strong and unpleasant odor.  The suffix *-ite* is commonly used in mineralogy to denote a mineral containing a specific element.
