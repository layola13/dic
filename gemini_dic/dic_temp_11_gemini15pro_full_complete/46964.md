darting:/ˈdɑːrtɪŋ/ | adj. | 迅速移动的，飞奔的 | Moving suddenly and rapidly. |  The lizard's tongue darted out to catch the fly.  蜥蜴的舌头飞快地伸出来抓住苍蝇。

| 近义词：flashing, dashing, swift, rapid | 反义词：slow, sluggish | 词性: 形容词

darting:/ˈdɑːrtɪŋ/ | n. | 猛冲，飞奔 | A sudden rapid movement. | The fish made a darting movement towards the surface. 鱼儿猛地向水面窜去。


| 近义词：dash, bolt, sprint | 反义词：creep, crawl | 词性: 名词


词源: From the verb "dart," from Old Norse *darta "to throw, thrust," related to Old English *daroþ "javelin, dart."
