john-bullish:/ˌdʒɒnˈbʊlɪʃ/ | adj. | 约翰牛式的；英国式的；顽固的；爱国的 | Resembling or characteristic of John Bull, a personification of England or the English, typically represented as a stout, red-faced man.  It often implies a sense of patriotism, stubbornness, and a belief in British superiority.  |  He had a rather john-bullish attitude towards foreigners.  他对外国人有一种相当典型的英国式的态度。 | Synonyms: patriotic, nationalistic, chauvinistic, insular, bull-headed | Antonyms: cosmopolitan, internationalist, open-minded | word property: adjective
