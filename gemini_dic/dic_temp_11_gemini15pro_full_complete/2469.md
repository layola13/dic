adjustive: /əˈdʒʌstɪv/
adjective
调整的；调节的；适应的
Relating to or causing adjustment; tending to adjust to a situation or environment.
The child displayed adjustive behavior when starting at the new school.  这个孩子在新学校开始上学时表现出了适应性行为。
Adjustive mechanisms help animals survive in changing environments.  调节机制帮助动物在变化的环境中生存。
synonyms: adaptive, accommodating, flexible, malleable
antonyms: inflexible, rigid, unadaptable
词性: 形容词
