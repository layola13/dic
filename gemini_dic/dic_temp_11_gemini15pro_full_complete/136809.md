nonshipper:/ˌnɑnˈʃɪpər/ | /ˌnɒnˈʃɪpə(r)/ | n. | 非同人爱好者，不热衷于虚构人物配对的人 | A person who does not participate in or enjoy the activity of "shipping" fictional characters, that is, imagining and discussing romantic relationships between them. |  She's a nonshipper, so she doesn't understand the appeal of fanfiction. 她不是同人爱好者，所以她不明白同人小说的吸引力。 | Example: He's such a nonshipper; he just doesn't get why people are so invested in these fictional pairings. 他完全不是个同人爱好者；他不明白为什么人们对这些虚构人物的配对如此投入。 | Near synonyms:  non-fan, casual viewer | Antonyms: shipper, fan | noun
