flogger:/ˈflɒɡər (UK), ˈflɑːɡər (US)| n.| 鞭打者；皮鞭；拍打器 |A person who flogs, especially one who whips another as punishment.  A whip used for flogging.  Something that beats or strikes with repeated blows, like heavy rain or waves. |The slave owner was a cruel flogger. (奴隶主是一个残忍的鞭打者。) |  The heavy rain flogged against the windows. (大雨拍打着窗户。) | synonyms: whipper, scourger, lasher| antonyms: protector, defender | noun
