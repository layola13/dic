matrilineality:/ˌmætrɪˈlɪniˈælɪti/ | n. |母系社会，母系继承 |The tracing of descent through the female line.  A system in which lineage, inheritance, and succession are traced through the mother's line.| The Minangkabau people of Indonesia practice matrilineality.  米南佳保人是印度尼西亚的母系社会。|  matriliny, matriarchal system | patrilineality, patriarchy | noun
