largition:/lɑːrˈɡɪʃən/
n.
慷慨赠予； lavish giving (especially of money)
The king's largition to the church ensured his place in heaven, or so he believed.  国王对教会的慷慨捐赠确保了他在天堂的地位，或者说他是这么认为的。
He gained popularity through largition to the poor. 他通过对穷人的慷慨解囊而赢得了声望。
近义词: generosity, munificence, bounty, liberality
反义词: stinginess, parsimony, miserliness
词性: noun

词源:  From Latin *largitionem*, accusative singular of *largitio*, from largire (“to give bountifully”), from largus (“abundant”).
