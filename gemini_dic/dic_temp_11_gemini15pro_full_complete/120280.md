miniaturist:/ˈmɪnɪətʃərɪst/| n.|微型画家；微雕艺术家|A person who paints very small pictures, especially portraits.|She was a renowned miniaturist, her works being highly sought after by collectors.|近义词:mini painter, limner|反义词:muralist|词性:noun

Etymology:
From miniature + -ist. Miniature from French miniature "miniature painting," from Italian miniatura, from Medieval Latin miniatura "illumination of manuscripts," from Latin miniare "to color with minium (red lead)," from minium "red lead."
