jurat:/ˈdʒʊəræt/| n.|宣誓证明；宣誓证明书|A jurat is a part of an affidavit where the affiant swears that the contents of the affidavit are true.|Example 1: The notary public signed the jurat after the witness swore the affidavit was true.  公证人在证人宣誓证词属实后签署了宣誓证明书。

Example 2:  The lawyer carefully reviewed the jurat to ensure it was properly executed. 律师仔细审查了宣誓证明书，以确保其正确执行。|Synonym: oath, affirmation, affidavit|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps denial, refutation)|noun
