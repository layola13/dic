acrolein: /ˈækrəliɪn/ (US) /ˈækrəʊliːn/ (UK)| n. |丙烯醛|A colorless, pungent liquid aldehyde, CH₂=CHCHO, used in the manufacture of resins, pharmaceuticals, and other organic substances. It is highly irritating to the eyes and mucous membranes.|Acrolein is a highly toxic byproduct of burning organic materials, such as wood and plastic.|近义词: propenal, acrylaldehyde |反义词: None| Noun
