congratulated: /kənˈɡrætʃəleɪtɪd/
verb
祝贺过的，恭喜过的
past tense and past participle of "congratulate" - having expressed pleasure to someone at their success or good fortune.
He congratulated her on her promotion. 他祝贺她升职。
Near Synonyms:  commended, lauded, praised
Antonyms: criticized, condemned, reprimanded
词性: 动词(过去式/过去分词)
