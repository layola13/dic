celibatist:/ˈsɛləbətɪst/| n.|独身主义者，禁欲者|A person who abstains from marriage and sexual relations, especially for religious reasons.|He lived as a celibatist for his entire adult life. 他成年后一直过着独身生活。|近义词：abstainer, single person|反义词：married person|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *caelibatista*, from Latin caelibatus ("unmarried state"), from caelebs ("unmarried"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱóy(e)lo- ("alone").
