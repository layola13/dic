overgratifying: /ˌoʊvərˈɡrætɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
过度满足的；过分令人高兴的
Providing excessive gratification; overly satisfying.
His overgratifying lifestyle ultimately led to unhappiness. 他的过度满足的生活方式最终导致了不快乐。
The dessert was rich and overgratifying. 这甜点很浓郁，太令人满足了。
近义词: oversatisfying, cloying, excessive
反义词: unsatisfying, underwhelming, insufficient
词性: adjective

over-: /ˈoʊvər/  prefix
过度；过于；超过
From Old English *ofer (cognate with Old High German ubar, Old Norse yfir).
over-achiever: someone who achieves more than expected.
over-confident: excessively confident.


gratifying: /ˈɡrætɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
令人高兴的；令人满意的
Giving pleasure or satisfaction.
It was gratifying to see her so happy. 看到她如此快乐，真是令人欣慰。
The results of the experiment were gratifying. 实验结果令人满意。
近义词: satisfying, pleasing, rewarding
反义词: disappointing, frustrating, unsatisfying
词性: adjective

-ing: /ɪŋ/  suffix
构成现在分词和动名词
Used to form the present participle and gerund of verbs.
walking, running, singing.
