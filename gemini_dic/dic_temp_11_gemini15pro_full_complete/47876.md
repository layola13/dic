deceleration:/diːˌseləˈreɪʃn/
noun
减速，减速作用
a reduction in speed or rate
The deceleration of the car was smooth. 汽车的减速很平稳。

近义词:slowing, retardation, decrease in speed
反义词:acceleration, speeding up

词性:n.


词源:
From Latin de- (down, away) + celer (swift) + -ation (forming nouns of action or process).
