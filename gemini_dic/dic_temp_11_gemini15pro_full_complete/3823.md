agonic:/eɪˈɡɒnɪk/| adj.|无磁偏角的，无偏角的|Relating to or situated on an agonic line (a line of zero magnetic declination).  An agonic line is a line on a chart joining points of zero magnetic declination. It connects locations where the magnetic north and true north are aligned.|The agonic line passes through several countries.|近义词:non-magnetic, non-deviating|反义词:magnetic, deviating|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek *a-* (not, without) + *gonia* (angle).
