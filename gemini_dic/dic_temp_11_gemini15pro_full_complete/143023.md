outbleat: /ˌaʊtˈbliːt/
词性: verb
中文翻译: 比...叫得更响亮；胜过
英文解析: To bleat louder than; to surpass or outdo in bleating.  Often used figuratively to mean outdo someone in complaining or whining.
例句：The lamb outbleated all the others in the pen.  这只小羊羔的叫声比围栏里所有其他的羊羔都响亮。

例句：He outbleated everyone else in the meeting, complaining about the new policy.  他在会议上比任何人都抱怨得厉害，抱怨新政策。
近义词: outshout, outwhine, outcomplain
反义词: be outdone, lose to
词性: verb
