earmarkings:/ˈɪərmɑːrkɪŋz/
n.
专款；指定用途的款项；标记
funds or resources that are set aside or designated for a particular purpose; the act of setting aside or designating funds for a particular purpose
Congress approved $5 million in earmarkings for the new library. 国会批准了500万美元用于新图书馆的专项拨款。
allocations, appropriations, designations
None
noun
