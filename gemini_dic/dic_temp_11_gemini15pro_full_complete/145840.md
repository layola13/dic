overdelighted:/ˌoʊvərdiˈlaɪtɪd/| adj.|欣喜若狂的，非常高兴的|Overjoyed; extremely delighted.  It implies a greater degree of delight than simply "delighted."|She was overdelighted to receive the scholarship. 她收到奖学金欣喜若狂。|近义词：ecstatic, overjoyed, thrilled, elated|反义词：disappointed, unhappy, sad|词性:形容词

词源:
"Over-" (prefix) + "delighted" (adjective). "Over-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "to a great degree". "Delighted" comes from the Old French "deliter" and ultimately from the Latin "delectare" meaning "to charm, delight."
