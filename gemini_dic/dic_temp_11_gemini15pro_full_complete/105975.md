kmer:/ˈkeɪmər/| n. |k元聚体，k-mer|A k-mer is a substring of length k contained within a biological sequence. In computational genomics, k-mers refer to all the possible substrings of length k that are contained in a string.  |The software identified all the 6-mers in the DNA sequence.|近义词：oligonucleotide, substring|反义词: - |noun
