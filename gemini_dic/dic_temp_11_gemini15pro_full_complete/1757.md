acquiring:/əˈkwaɪərɪŋ/
Verb|获得，取得|The process of obtaining or gaining possession of something; learning or developing a skill or habit.|例句：She is acquiring a reputation as a talented artist. 她正逐渐获得才华横溢的艺术家的名声。|近义词：obtaining, gaining, learning, developing|反义词：losing, forfeiting, abandoning|v.
acquiring:/əˈkwaɪərɪŋ/
Adjective|收购的，获得的|Used to describe something that is in the process of being acquired.|例句：The acquiring company will merge with the smaller firm. 收购公司将与较小的公司合并。|近义词：purchasing, obtaining|反义词：selling, divesting|adj.
