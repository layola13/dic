dextrorotatory:/ˌdɛkstroʊˈroʊtətɔːri/ /ˌdɛkstrəˈrəʊtəri/ | adj. | 右旋的 | Rotating the plane of polarized light to the right, clockwise. |  The solution was dextrorotatory, indicating the presence of a specific sugar.  该溶液是右旋的，表明存在一种特定的糖。

The dextrorotatory form of glucose is commonly found in nature.  葡萄糖的右旋形式在自然界中很常见。 | 近义词: clockwise | 反义词: levorotatory | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *dexter* (right) + *rotatorius* (rotating).
