jaywalking:/ˈdʒeɪˌwɔːkɪŋ/ | n. | 乱穿马路 | The act of crossing a street at a place other than a designated crosswalk, or without regard for traffic signals. | Jaywalking is illegal in many cities. 在许多城市，乱穿马路是违法的。|  Jaywalking can be dangerous, especially during rush hour. 乱穿马路很危险，尤其是在高峰时段。 |  crossing against the light, illegally crossing | obeying traffic signals | noun
