adjoining:/əˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ/
adjective
邻近的，毗邻的
located next to or joined with something else
The adjoining rooms were connected by a door. 这两个相邻的房间由一扇门相连。
adjacent, neighboring, contiguous
distant, separate
adjective
