nonseizure:/nɒnˈsiːʒər/ | n. | 非扣押，非癫痫发作 | Absence of a seizure (e.g., epileptic seizure).  It refers to a state where a seizure does not occur. | The patient experienced a period of nonseizure after starting the new medication.  病人开始服用新药后经历了一段时间的非癫痫发作。| The EEG confirmed a state of nonseizure during the episode. 脑电图证实了这段时间内没有癫痫发作。 | Synonyms: seizure-free, absence of seizure | Antonyms: seizure, convulsion, fit | noun
