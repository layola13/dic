deodorant:/diˈoʊdərənt/ /ˌdiːˈəʊdərənt/| n.|除臭剂，体香剂|A substance applied to the body to prevent or mask body odor.|I apply deodorant every morning after my shower. 我每天早上淋浴后都会涂抹除臭剂。|近义词：antiperspirant|反义词:|noun

deodorant:/diˈoʊdərənt/ /ˌdiːˈəʊdərənt/| adj.|除臭的|Having the property of eliminating or masking unpleasant odors.|This deodorant soap leaves my skin feeling fresh. 这款除臭皂让我的皮肤感觉很清爽。|近义词：anti-odor|反义词:|adjective
