enforest:/ɪnˈfɔːrɪst/ | vt. | 植树造林，使…长满树林 | To establish a forest on land not previously forested; to cover with trees. |  The organization is working to enforest the barren hillsides.  该组织正在努力在贫瘠的山坡上植树造林。

The government plans to enforest large areas of the country. 政府计划在该国大面积植树造林。 | 近义词: afforest, plant, reforest | 反义词: deforest, clear | 词性: 动词
