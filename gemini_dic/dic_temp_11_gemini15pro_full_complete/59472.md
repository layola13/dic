easeled:/ˈiːzəld/| v. |放置于画架上|To place (a painting, etc.) on an easel.|She easeled her latest masterpiece, a vibrant landscape of the Tuscan countryside. 她把她最新的杰作——一幅充满活力的托斯卡纳乡村风景画——放在了画架上。|近义词：display, exhibit, mount|反义词: remove, take down|词性: 动词

词源:  From easel + -ed.  "Easel" comes from the Dutch word "ezel," meaning "donkey," likely referring to the easel's support function, like a beast of burden.
