acceptable: /əkˈseptəbl/ | adj. | 可接受的，合意的 | satisfactory and able to be agreed to or approved of. |  This behavior is not acceptable in our society.  这种行为在我们的社会中是不可接受的。

The quality of the work is acceptable. 这份工作的质量是可以接受的。

近义词：satisfactory, adequate, tolerable, agreeable, pleasing

反义词：unacceptable, unsatisfactory, inadequate, intolerable, disagreeable

词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin acceptabilis, from acceptare ‘accept’.
