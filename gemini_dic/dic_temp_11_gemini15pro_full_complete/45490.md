curdier:/ˈkɜːrdiər/| adj.|更凝乳的，更像凝乳的|More like curd; resembling curd more closely.|This cheese is curdier than the one we bought last week.  这种奶酪比我们上周买的更凝乳。|近义词：coagulated, thicker, firmer|反义词:runnier, thinner, smoother|词性:形容词
词源:From curd + -y + -er (comparative suffix). Ultimately from the Middle English *crudden, from the Old English *crudu.
