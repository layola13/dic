gathering:/ˈɡæðərɪŋ/| n.|聚会，集会，聚集；采集，收集|A gathering is a meeting of people for a particular purpose.|例句：We had a family gathering for Thanksgiving. 我们感恩节举行了一次家庭聚会。

例句：The gathering of storm clouds warned of the approaching rain. 聚集的风暴云预示着即将到来的雨。|近义词：assembly, meeting, collection, congregation|反义词:dispersal, scattering|词性:noun
