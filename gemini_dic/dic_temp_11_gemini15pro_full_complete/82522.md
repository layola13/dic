gtg:/ˈɡɒt tuː ɡəʊ/ (British English), /ˈɡɑːt tuː ɡoʊ/ (American English)| abbreviation | 我得走了 | Short for "got to go," meaning "I have to leave." | Example: Gotta finish this email, gtg!  得完成这封邮件，我得走了！ | Example: Sorry, gtg, meeting in 5.  不好意思，我得走了，5分钟后开会。 | 近义词: gotta go, have to leave, bye | 反义词: I'm staying, I'm here to stay | abbreviation

He gtgs early every day. 他每天都走得很早。

I'm gtg right now. 我现在就得走。
He's gtg in a minute.  他马上要走了。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I've gtg already. 我已经走了。 (This usage is less common and might sound unnatural)

(Not typically used with gtg, as it implies an ongoing action of leaving which doesn't fit the immediacy of "gotta go.")
