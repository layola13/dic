The attractile force of the magnet drew the iron filings towards it. 磁铁的吸引力把铁屑吸了过去。

近义词: appealing, alluring, charming, magnetic, captivating | 反义词: repulsive, repellent, unappealing, unattractive | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *attractilis, from Latin attract-, past participle stem of attrahere "to draw to," from ad- "to" + trahere "to draw."
