landscapes:/ˈlændskeɪps/| n. |风景，景色；山水画；地形；<计>横向|A landscape is all the visible features of an area of land, often considered in terms of their aesthetic appeal.  It can also refer to a painting of such a view or, in computing, to a horizontal page orientation.|The landscapes of Tuscany are breathtaking. 托斯卡纳的风景令人叹为观止。|Synonyms: scenery, view, vista, panorama, terrain|Antonyms: cityscape, interior|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Dutch *landschap*  (literally "land-shape"), from *land* ("land") + *-schap* (suffix denoting "condition, state, quality"). Cognate with German *Landschaft*.
