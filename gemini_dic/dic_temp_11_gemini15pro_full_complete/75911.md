funeral:/ˈfjuːnərəl/ (US) /ˈfjuːn(ə)rəl/ (UK)| n. |葬礼；丧葬仪式|A ceremony or service held shortly after a person's death, typically including the burial or cremation of the body.|We attended the funeral of a close friend. 我们参加了一位挚友的葬礼。|Synonyms: burial, interment, obsequies, cremation service|Antonyms: birth, wedding|noun
