free-base:/ˈfriː beɪs/ | n. |游离碱; 自由碱 | A form of a substance, especially a drug, that is not combined with another substance, such as an acid or salt. |  Cocaine free-base is a more potent form of the drug. 可卡因游离碱是这种药物的一种更强效的形式。| synonyms: pure form, uncombined form | antonyms: salt, acid | noun

free-base:/ˈfriː beɪs/ | v. | 将…制成游离碱 | To convert a substance, especially a drug, into its free-base form. | Drug dealers free-base cocaine to increase its potency. 毒贩将可卡因制成游离碱以增加其效力。| synonyms: convert, purify | antonyms: combine, synthesize | verb
