bluster:/ˈblʌstər/| n.|咆哮；怒吼；狂风；|A loud, aggressive, or boastful way of talking, often with little effect. A violent gust of wind.|His bluster didn't scare anyone. 他的咆哮并没有吓到任何人。  A bluster of wind slammed the door shut. 一阵狂风把门关上了。|近义词：roar, rage, storm, boasting, bragging|反义词：whisper, murmur, calm, stillness|词性:名词
