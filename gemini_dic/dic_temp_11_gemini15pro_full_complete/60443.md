einstein:/ˈaɪnstaɪn/| n.|爱因斯坦|A German-born theoretical physicist who developed the theory of relativity, one of the two pillars of modern physics. His work is also known for its influence on the philosophy of science.|Einstein is famous for his theory of relativity. 爱因斯坦以他的相对论而闻名。|Synonym: genius, physicist|Antonym: N/A|noun
