dishumour:/dɪsˈhjuːmə(r)/
n.
不幽默；缺乏幽默感；扫兴；败兴
lack of humour; a tendency to be serious or gloomy.
He responded to the joke with complete dishumour. 他对这个笑话完全没有幽默感。
近义词：seriousness, gloom, solemnity, dourness
反义词：humour, wit, cheerfulness
词性: 名词
