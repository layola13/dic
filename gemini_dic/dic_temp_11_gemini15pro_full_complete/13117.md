astucity:/əˈstjuːsəti/ /əˈstuːsəti/
n.|机敏；狡黠；诡诈|Shrewdness and cunning, especially in managing or maneuvering.
He showed considerable astucity in avoiding the trap. 他在避开陷阱方面表现出了相当的机敏。
Her astucity allowed her to quickly climb the corporate ladder. 她的狡黠使她能够迅速晋升。
近义词: shrewdness, cunning, guile, craftiness, wiliness
反义词: naiveté, innocence, artlessness
词性: noun

astute:/əˈstjuːt/ /əˈstuːt/
adj.|精明的；敏锐的；狡猾的|Having or showing an ability to accurately assess situations or people and turn this to one's advantage.
She was astute enough to realize that he was lying. 她很精明，意识到他在撒谎。
an astute businessman  一位精明的商人
近义词: shrewd, sharp, perceptive, insightful, clever, intelligent
反义词: naive, foolish, unintelligent
词性: adjective


词源: From French *astuce* "skill, cleverness," from Italian *astuzia*, ultimately from Latin *astutia* "craft, cunning."
