lagune:/ləˈɡuːn/ /ləˈɡʊn/| n. |泻湖；环礁湖|A stretch of salt water separated from the sea by a low sandbank or coral reef.|例句：We sailed across the calm waters of the lagoon. 我们航行穿过平静的泻湖水域。

例句：The lagoon was teeming with colorful fish. 泻湖里充满了五颜六色的鱼。|近义词：inlet, bay, cove|反义词:ocean, sea|词性:noun
