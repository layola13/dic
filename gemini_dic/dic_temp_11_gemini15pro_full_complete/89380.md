homophones:/ˈhɒməfəʊnz/ (UK), /ˈhɑːməfoʊnz/ (US)| n. |同音异义词|Words that are pronounced the same but have different meanings, origins, or spellings.|例句：The words "to," "too," and "two" are homophones.  单词"to"、"too"和"two"是同音异义词。

例句： "Meat" and "meet" are homophones. "Meat" 和 "meet" 是同音异义词。|近义词：homonyms|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
