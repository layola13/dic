clergiable:/ˈklɜːrdʒɪəbəl/
adjective
可担任圣职的；适合担任神职人员的
suitable for or allowed to be a member of the clergy.
He was deemed clergiable by the bishop. 他被主教认为适合担任神职人员。
近义词: ordained, ecclesiastic
反义词: secular, lay
词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin clergiabilis, from clericus ‘cleric’ + -abilis ‘-able’.
