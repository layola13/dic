gaucho-pants:/ˈɡaʊtʃoʊ pænts/ | n. | 高乔裤 | Wide-legged pants, often cropped, inspired by the traditional trousers worn by gauchos (South American cowboys). |  She wore a pair of stylish gaucho-pants to the party. 她穿了一条时髦的高乔裤去参加派对。 |  She paired her gaucho-pants with a fitted top. 她把高乔裤和一件合身的衣服搭配在一起。| synonyms: wide-leg pants, palazzo pants, culottes | antonyms: skinny jeans, leggings | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "gaucho" (referring to South American cowboys) + "pants" (trousers).  "Gaucho" ultimately derives from Quechua "huachu," meaning "orphan" or "vagabond."
