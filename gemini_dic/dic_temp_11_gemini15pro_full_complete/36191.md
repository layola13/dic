confounding:/kənˈfaʊndɪŋ/ | adj.| 令人困惑的；混杂的；惊人的；讨厌的 | causing surprise or confusion, especially by acting against your expectations |  The confounding results of the study left the researchers puzzled. 研究结果令人困惑，让研究人员感到不解。| synonyms: perplexing, bewildering, baffling, surprising, astonishing, frustrating, annoying | antonyms: clear, obvious, straightforward, expected | adjective


confound:/kənˈfaʊnd/ | v. | 使困惑；使惊讶；证明…有误；挫败；诅咒 | to confuse and surprise someone; to prove something wrong; to defeat a plan, hope, or expectation | The magician confounded the audience with his amazing tricks. 魔术师用他神奇的戏法使观众感到困惑。| synonyms: confuse, bewilder, baffle, perplex, surprise, astonish, frustrate, thwart | antonyms: clarify, explain, enlighten, help, aid | verb
