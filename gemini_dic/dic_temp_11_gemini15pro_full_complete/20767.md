blandnesses:/ˈblændnəsəz/
noun
平淡；乏味；温和
lack of strong features or characteristics; uninteresting mildness
The blandnesses of the hospital food made him lose his appetite.  医院食物的平淡无味让他食欲不振。

The blandnesses of the music made it perfect for background noise. 这音乐的平淡无奇使它非常适合做背景音乐。

synonym
insipidity, dullness, vapidity, monotony
antonym
excitement, zest, piquancy, flavor, interest
