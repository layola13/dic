clearest:/ˈklɪərɪst/| adj.|最清晰的,最明白的|The clearest explanation is often the simplest. |例句：This is the clearest picture I've ever taken. 这是我拍过的最清晰的照片。

例句：He gave the clearest explanation of the complex issue. 他对这个复杂的问题作出了最清晰的解释。

|近义词：most obvious, most transparent, most lucid, most distinct|反义词：most obscure, most vague, most ambiguous, murkiest|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *clerest*, *cleriste*, from Old English *clēorost*, superlative of *clēoro* (“clear, bright, pure, shining”). Cognate with Dutch *klaarst*, German *klarst*, Swedish *klarast*.  The superlative form of clear.
