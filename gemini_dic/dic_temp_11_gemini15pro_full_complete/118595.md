mesquite:/mɛˈskiːt/ | n. | 牧豆树；牧豆树木材 | A thorny tree or shrub of the genus Prosopis, native to arid regions of the Americas and having small flowers, spiny branches, and pods rich in sugar. |  The mesquite tree provided shade for the weary travelers.  疲惫的旅行者在牧豆树下乘凉。| synonym:  algarroba, honey mesquite | antonym: N/A | noun

mesquite:/mɛˈskiːt/ | n. | 牧豆树木材（尤指用于烧烤）| The wood of the mesquite tree, often used for smoking and grilling food due to its distinct flavor.  | The steak was grilled over mesquite, giving it a smoky flavor. 牛排是用牧豆树木材烤制的，带有一股烟熏味。| synonym: firewood, smoking wood | antonym: N/A | noun
