at-the-worst:/æt ðə wɜːrst/
adverbial phrase
最坏的情况; 最糟的时候
in the worst possible case; if the worst happens
At the worst, we'll have to spend the night in the car. 最坏的情况是，我们得在车里过夜。
Synonyms: if worst comes to worst, in the worst-case scenario
Antonyms: at best, at the very least
phrase
