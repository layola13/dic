overdisciplines:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪsəplɪnz/| v. |过度训练，过分纪律约束|To discipline excessively or too rigorously.|  The coach overdisciplined the athletes, leading to burnout and resentment. 教练对运动员进行了过度训练，导致他们精疲力竭和怨恨。

The parents overdisciplined their child, stifling his creativity. 父母对孩子管教过严，扼杀了他的创造力。


|近义词：overtrain, overcontrol, overregulate |反义词: underdiscipline, neglect |词性: verb
