accommodate:/əˈkɒmədeɪt/ (UK) /əˈkɑːmədeɪt/ (US)| v.|容纳，提供住宿，适应，顾及|to provide lodging or sufficient space for; to adjust to; to consider and satisfy|The hotel can accommodate up to 500 guests. 这家酒店最多可容纳500位客人。|近义词：house, lodge, adapt, adjust, fit, suit|反义词:disagree, refuse|词性:动词

accommodate:/əˈkɒmədeɪt/ (UK) /əˈkɑːmədeɪt/ (US)| n.(罕用)|住宿，膳宿|lodging, accommodations|(This meaning is less common)  The accommodate provided by the host was excellent.  (这个意思比较少用) 主人提供的住宿非常棒。|近义词：lodging, housing, accommodations|反义词:None|词性:名词


词源:  From Late Latin accommodātus, past participle of accommodāre (“to make fit, adapt”), from Latin ad (“to”) + commodāre (“to make suitable”), from commodus (“suitable, convenient”).
