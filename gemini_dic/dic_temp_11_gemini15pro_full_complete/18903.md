besmirch:/bɪˈsmɜːrtʃ/
verb
玷污，败坏（名声）
To damage the reputation of (someone or something) in the opinion of others.
The scandal besmirched the reputation of the entire company. 这桩丑闻玷污了整个公司的名声。
近义词: tarnish, sully, smear, stain, discredit
反义词: honor, praise, extol, laud
词性: verb

besmirch:/bɪˈsmɜːrtʃ/
词源: From Middle English *bismerchen, from Old French besmirchier (“to soil, blacken”), from be- (“thoroughly”) + smirchier (“to dirty”).
词性: verb
