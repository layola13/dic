lollop:/ˈlɒləp/ (UK) /ˈlɑːləp/ (US)| v. n.| 晃动；慢跑；笨拙地行动|To move in a clumsy, rolling way; to run with a long, bounding stride.  A clumsy, rolling gait or stride.|The dog lolloped across the field. 狗笨拙地在田野里跑.|近义词：lumber, bound, gallop, canter|反义词：dart, sprint, scurry|词性:动词,名词
Etymology:  Probably imitative of the sound of heavy footsteps.
