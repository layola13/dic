coloring:/ˈkʌlərɪŋ/ | n. | 着色，涂色，染色；颜料；（尤指人造的）色泽，色彩 | the act of adding color to something; the colors used; the appearance of something with regard to its color |  She enjoys coloring in her coloring book. 她喜欢在她的涂色书上涂色。 | synonyms: pigmentation, tint, hue, shade, dye | antonyms: bleaching, fading | noun

coloring:/ˈkʌlərɪŋ/ | n. | （食品的）色素，着色剂 | a substance used to give color to food | The cake had artificial coloring in it.  蛋糕里有人工色素。| synonyms: dye, pigment | antonyms:  None | noun
