insufficientness:/ˌɪnsəˈfɪʃəntnəs/| n. |不足，不够，缺乏|The insufficientness of the evidence led to the case being dismissed.  证据不足导致案件被驳回。|例句：The insufficientness of funding hampered the project.  资金不足阻碍了项目进展。

The insufficientness of her explanation left him with more questions. 她解释的不足让他产生了更多疑问。|近义词：inadequacy, deficiency, scarcity, lack, shortage|反义词：sufficiency, adequacy, abundance, plenty|词性:noun
Etymology: From insufficient + -ness.  Insufficient comes from Late Latin insufficient-, from in- "not" + sufficient-, from sufficere "to suffice," from sub "up to" + facere "to make, do."
