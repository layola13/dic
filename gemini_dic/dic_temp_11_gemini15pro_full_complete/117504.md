meedfully:/ˈmiːdfəli/| adv.|恰当地；应得地；适当地|In a manner that satisfies a need or reward; deservedly.|He was meedfully rewarded for his efforts. 他因努力而得到了应有的回报。|近义词：deservedly, justly, appropriately, rightfully, fittingly|反义词：undeservedly, unjustly, inappropriately|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *medefulli*, equivalent to *mede* (reward, merit) + *-fulli* (suffix denoting "in a manner characterized by").  *Mede* comes from Old English *mēd* (reward), from Proto-Germanic *mēdaz* (reward), from Proto-Indo-European *médʰ-/*méh₂dʰ- (to measure, to consider worthy of).
