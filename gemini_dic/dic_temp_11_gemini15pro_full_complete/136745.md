nonsensuous:/ˌnɒnˈsɛnʃuəs/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɛnʃuəs/| adj.|非感官的；非感觉的；理智的|Not perceived by or affecting the senses; appealing to the intellect rather than the emotions; intellectual.|His poetry is nonsensuous and philosophical. 他的诗是非感官的，具有哲理性的。|近义词：intellectual, cerebral, abstract, unemotional|反义词：sensuous, sensual, emotional|词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): not
sensuous (adjective): relating to or affecting the senses
