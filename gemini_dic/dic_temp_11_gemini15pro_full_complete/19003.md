bestride:/bɪˈstraɪd/
verb
跨坐, 跨立, 统治, 影响
to sit or stand with one leg on each side of something; to have control or influence over something
例句：He bestrode his horse like a seasoned warrior. 他像个老战士一样跨坐在马上。

例句：The Colossus of Rhodes bestrode the harbor entrance. 罗德岛的巨像跨立在港口入口。

近义词：straddle, span, dominate, influence
反义词： (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑根据语境使用 "submit to"  屈服于，或"be controlled by" 被...控制)
词性：v.


bestride:/bɪˈstraɪd/
verb
跨越
to extend across or over
例句：A huge mountain range bestrides the border between the two countries. 一座巨大的山脉横跨两国边界。

例句：The Roman Empire bestrode much of the known world. 罗马帝国跨越了当时已知世界的大部分地区。
