igneous:/ˈɪɡniəs/| adj.| 火成的；岩浆的| Formed by or having the nature of fire; produced under conditions involving intense heat, especially from molten rock.|  Example: Granite is an igneous rock. 花岗岩是一种火成岩。| Example: The volcano spewed forth igneous debris. 火山喷出了火成碎屑。| 近义词: volcanic, magmatic | 反义词: sedimentary, metamorphic | 词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *igneus*, from ignis (“fire”).
