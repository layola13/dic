listerian:/lɪˈstɪəriən/
adjective|李斯特菌的；李斯特菌病的|Relating to or caused by Listeria, a type of bacteria that can cause food poisoning.|The listerian outbreak was traced back to contaminated cantaloupe. 李斯特菌的爆发可以追溯到被污染的哈密瓜。|Related: bacterial, microbial|Antonyms: N/A
noun|感染李斯特菌的人或动物|A person or animal infected with Listeria.|The listerian was treated with antibiotics. 感染李斯特菌的患者接受了抗生素治疗。|Related: patient, victim|Antonyms: N/A
