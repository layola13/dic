eddy: /ˈedi/ (US), /ˈɛdi/ (UK)| n. v.| 涡流，漩涡；(使)起漩涡|A circular movement of water, counter to a main current, causing a small whirlpool.|The canoe was caught in an eddy and spun around. 独木舟被卷入漩涡中旋转起来。|近义词: swirl, whirlpool, vortex|反义词: current, stream|词性:名词,动词

eddy: /ˈedi/ (US), /ˈɛdi/ (UK)| v.|(使)起漩涡，旋转|To move in an eddy; whirl.|The wind eddied the fallen leaves. 风使落叶旋转。|近义词: swirl, whirl, spin|反义词: straighten, smooth|词性:动词
