erratical:/ɪˈrætɪkəl/
adjective
古怪的，反复无常的，不稳定的
Deviating from the usual or proper course in conduct or opinion; eccentric; queer.  Characterized by lack of consistency, regularity, or uniformity.
His erratic behavior concerned his friends. 他的古怪行为让他的朋友们很担心。
synonyms: unpredictable, inconsistent, capricious, fickle, volatile, whimsical
antonyms: consistent, regular, predictable, steady, stable
词性: 形容词
