bludhorn:/ˈblʌdhɔːrn/
noun
血角, 血角号 (一种乐器)
A bludhorn is a type of musical instrument, typically made from an animal horn, used for signaling or ritual purposes.
The chieftain sounded the bludhorn to signal the start of the battle. 族长吹响血角号，标志着战斗的开始。
synonyms: horn, trumpet, bugle
