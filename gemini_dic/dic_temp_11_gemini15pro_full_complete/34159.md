clambake: /ˈklæmˌbeɪk/ | n. | 蛤蜊烘烤; 海鲜野餐; (非正式)热闹的聚会 | A social event, typically held outdoors, where clams and other seafood are baked, often accompanied by other food and entertainment.  A lively, informal gathering or celebration. | We're having a clambake on the beach this weekend. 我们这个周末要在海滩上举行蛤蜊烘烤。 | The annual company picnic was a real clambake. 公司的年度野餐真是热闹非凡。 | synonyms: beach party, cookout, feast, picnic, party | antonyms: meeting, conference | noun
