abutter:/əˈbʌtər/| n.| 邻接土地所有人，比邻而居者|Someone whose property abuts or adjoins another's; a neighbor, especially one whose property borders on another's.|例句：The abutters were notified of the proposed construction.  比邻而居者被告知了拟议中的建筑工程。

例句：The abutters filed a lawsuit to stop the development.  邻接土地所有人提起诉讼阻止开发。|近义词：neighbor, adjacent landowner|反义词:distant resident|词性:noun
