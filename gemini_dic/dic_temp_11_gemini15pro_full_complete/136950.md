nonspecific:/ˌnɑːnspəˈsɪfɪk/ /ˌnɒnspəˈsɪfɪk/ | adj. |非特异性的，非具体的|Not specific to a particular thing, condition, or area; general.|The patient presented with nonspecific symptoms, such as fatigue and muscle aches. 病人表现出非特异性症状，例如疲劳和肌肉酸痛。|Synonyms: general, vague, imprecise, undefined, broad|Antonyms: specific, precise, definite, particular|adjective

nonspecifically:/ˌnɑːnspəˈsɪfɪkli/ /ˌnɒnspəˈsɪfɪkli/ | adv. |非特异性地，非具体地|In a nonspecific manner; generally.|The drug acts nonspecifically on the immune system. 该药物对免疫系统的作用是非特异性的。|Synonyms: generally, vaguely, imprecisely|Antonyms: specifically, precisely, definitely|adverb
