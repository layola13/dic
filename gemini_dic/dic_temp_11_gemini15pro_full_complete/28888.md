carpophore:/ˈkɑːrpəfɔːr/| n.|子实体柄，担子果柄|A stalk that supports the fruiting body of a fungus, such as a mushroom.|The mushroom had a long, slender carpophore.|Synonyms: stipe, stalk|Antonyms: None|noun

carpophore:/ˌkɑːrpəˈfɔːr/| n.|果柄，果托|The slender stalk that supports the fruit of certain plants.|The carpophore of the dandelion holds the seeds aloft.|Synonyms: pedicel, stalk|Antonyms: None|noun
