overimpressing:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈprɛsɪŋ/ | v. | 过分给人留下深刻印象，用力过猛 |  Trying too hard to impress someone, often with negative results. |  He overimpressed the interviewer by reciting his entire resume. 他背诵了整份简历，给面试官留下了用力过猛的印象。| 近义词: overdoing, exaggerating | 反义词: understating, downplaying | 词性: 动词(present participle)

overimpressed:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈprɛst/ | adj. | 过分留下深刻印象的，被用力过猛所影响的 | Having been impressed excessively, often to the point of skepticism or discomfort. | I was overimpressed by his flashy car, but then I found out he was deeply in debt.  他那辆炫目的车让我印象深刻，但我后来发现他负债累累。|近义词: overwhelmed, bowled over | 反义词: unimpressed, indifferent | 词性: 形容词
