brimmed:/brɪmd/
adj.
充满的，盈满的
filled to the brim; full to overflowing.
The cup was brimmed with hot coffee.  杯子装满了热咖啡。

近义词:full, overflowing, topped
反义词:empty, vacant

词性:adjective
词源:From brim (n.) + -ed.  The noun "brim" refers to the upper edge or rim of a container, and the suffix "-ed" forms an adjective indicating a state of being filled to the brim.
