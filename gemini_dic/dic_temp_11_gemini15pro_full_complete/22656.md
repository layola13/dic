borcan:/ˈbɔrkən/ (Romanian pronunciation)| n.|罐子，坛子|A borcan is a type of container, typically made of glass or ceramic, used for storing food, especially preserves or pickles.  It is similar to a jar.  This word is primarily used in Romanian.|例句：Bunica a pus murăturile în borcan.  祖母把腌菜放在罐子里。|例句：Am cumpărat un borcan de miere de albine. 我买了一罐蜂蜜。|近义词：jar, container, pot|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
