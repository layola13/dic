/kroʊm/ (US) /krəʊm/ (UK)| n. adj. vt.|铬黄; 铬颜料|A vivid yellow pigment made from lead chromate. |例句：The artist used chrome yellow in the painting.  艺术家在画中使用了铬黄色。|近义词：(for the color) yellow, golden|反义词: (no direct antonym)|词性: Noun, Adjective
