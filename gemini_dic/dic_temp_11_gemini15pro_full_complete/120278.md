miniaturizes:/ˈmɪnɪətʃəraɪzɪz/
verb
使小型化；微型化
To make something much smaller in size.
They've miniaturized the camera so it can fit inside a pen. 他们把相机小型化了，可以装在钢笔里。
miniaturize, shrink, reduce, compress
enlarge, expand, magnify
v.
