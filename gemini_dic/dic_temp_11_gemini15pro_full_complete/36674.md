collie: /ˈkɒli/ (UK) /ˈkɑːli/ (US) | n. | 牧羊犬，柯利牧羊犬 | A breed of dog, originally from Scotland, used for herding sheep, known for its intelligence and long coat. |  Lassie, the famous movie dog, was a Collie.  莱西，那只著名的电影狗，是一只柯利牧羊犬。

The farmer used a collie to round up the sheep.  农民用一只柯利牧羊犬把羊群赶到一起。 | 近义词：sheepdog, herding dog | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
