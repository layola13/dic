modernity:/məˈdɜːrnəti/ /məˈdɑːrnəti/| n. |现代性；现代化；现代社会|The condition of being modern; modern character or qualities.  A modern way of thinking or working. |例句：The art exhibit explored themes of modernity and tradition. 这个艺术展览探讨了现代性与传统的主题。

例句：The architecture reflected the modernity of the city.  这栋建筑反映了这座城市的现代化。|近义词：contemporaneity, modernism, present, newness|反义词：antiquity, tradition, past|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *modernitas, from Latin modernus ("modern").
