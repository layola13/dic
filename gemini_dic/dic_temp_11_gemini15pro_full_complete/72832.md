folily:/ˈfoʊlɪʃli/| adv.|愚蠢地；傻气地|In a foolish manner; unwisely.  It describes an action done in a way that lacks good sense or judgment.|He folily invested all his money in a single stock. 他愚蠢地把所有的钱都投资在一支股票上。|She folily believed everything he said. 她愚蠢地相信了他所说的一切。|近义词：stupidly, unwisely, rashly, imprudently|反义词：wisely, prudently, sensibly|词性:副词
