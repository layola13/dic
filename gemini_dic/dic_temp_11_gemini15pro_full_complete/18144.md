beige:/beɪʒ/| adj. n.|米黄色，浅褐色|A pale sandy fawn colour.|The walls were painted a warm beige.|近义词:buff, cream, ecru, off-white, tan|反义词:black, vibrant|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology:
From French *beige*, from the Italian *bigio* "grayish," from an alteration of Vulgar Latin *badius* "bay-colored, brown."
