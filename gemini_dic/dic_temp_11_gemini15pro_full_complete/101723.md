irreptitious:/ˌɪrəpˈtɪʃəs/
adjective
偷偷摸摸的；秘密的
done secretly, without anyone noticing
He made an irreptitious glance at his watch. 他偷偷看了一眼手表。

近义词:
clandestine, covert, furtive, stealthy, surreptitious
反义词:
open, overt, public

词源:
From Latin *irreptus*, from in- ‘not’ + reptus ‘seized’, past participle of rapere ‘to seize’.
