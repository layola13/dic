opifice: /ˈɒpɪfɪs/ (UK) /ˈɑːpɪfɪs/ (US)| n. |工作室，工场；营业场所|A place of work, especially one devoted to a specific occupation or industry.  A more formal or literary term than "workshop" or "office."|例句：The artist labored tirelessly in his opifice, surrounded by canvases and paints. 艺术家在他的工作室里不知疲倦地工作，周围都是画布和颜料。

例句：The alchemist's opifice was filled with strange and exotic ingredients. 炼金术士的工作室里摆满了奇异而外来的材料。|近义词：workshop, studio, office, workplace|反义词: leisure, home, vacation|词性:noun
