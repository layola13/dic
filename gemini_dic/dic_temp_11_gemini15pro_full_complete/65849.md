evitation: /ˌiːvɪˈteɪʃən/
noun
逃避，躲避
the act of avoiding something or someone
He practiced evitation of his responsibilities. 他逃避自己的责任。
近义词：avoidance, evasion, eluding, dodging
反义词：confrontation, facing, embracing
词性：名词
