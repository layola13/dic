bracteal:/ˈbræktiəl/ | adj. |苞片的，苞叶的|Relating to or resembling a bract; bractlike.| The bracteal leaves are small and inconspicuous. 苞叶很小，不显眼。| Near synonyms: bracted, bracteate | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider terms like "apetalous" (lacking petals) or "asepalous" (lacking sepals) if discussing flower parts in general) | word type: adjective

bract:/brækt/ | n. |苞片，苞叶|A modified leaf, usually small, located near the base of a flower or inflorescence.| The bract protects the developing flower bud. 苞片保护发育中的花芽。| Near synonyms: bractlet, scale | Antonyms: (No direct antonym exists) | word type: noun
