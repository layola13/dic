furtherer:/ˈfɜːrðərər/ | n. | 推进者；促进者 | Someone who furthers something, promotes or advances it. |  He was a furtherer of education in the community. 他是社区教育的推动者。 |  The company is a furtherer of technological innovation. 这家公司是技术创新的推动者。 | Synonyms: promoter, advocate, supporter, champion | Antonyms: hinderer, obstructer | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "further" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Further" comes from Middle English *furtheren*, from Old English *fyrðran* "to promote, advance," from *furðor* "further."
