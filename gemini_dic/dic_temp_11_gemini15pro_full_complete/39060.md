conformational:/ˌkɑːnfɔːrˈmeɪʃənəl/ | adj. |构象的；构型的| Relating to or involving the spatial arrangement of atoms in a molecule that can be changed by rotation about single bonds, without breaking any bonds. |The conformational change of the protein allows it to bind to the receptor. 蛋白的构象变化使其能够与受体结合。|conformative, configurational | constitutional | adjective
