nonrestorations:/ˌnɑːnˌrɛstəˈreɪʃənz/
|n.|未修复，未恢复，未重建|The nonrestoration of the historic building led to its eventual collapse.  这座历史建筑的未修复导致它最终倒塌。|The museum displayed artifacts in their state of nonrestoration, highlighting the effects of time.  博物馆展出了未修复状态的文物，突出了时间的影响。|近义词: neglect, disrepair|反义词: restoration, renovation|词性:名词


词源:
non- (prefix) + restoration (noun)

"non-" 源自拉丁语，意思是 "not" 或 "without".
"restoration" 源自拉丁语 "restaurare," 意思是 "to restore," "to renew," or "to rebuild."
