denumbers:/dɪˈnʌmbərz/

denumbers:/dɪˈnʌmbərz/
|v.|减少...的数量|Reduces the numbers of. The new policy denumbers the workforce. 新政策减少了职工人数。|近义词: reduces, diminishes, decreases|反义词: increases, augments|词性:动词
