mimetically:/mɪˈmɛtɪkli/ | adv. | 模仿地，拟态地 | In a way that imitates or mimics something else.  The parrot mimetically reproduced the sounds of the human voice. | 例句：The robot moved mimetically, copying the actions of the human operator.  机器人模仿地移动，复制人类操作员的动作。

例句：She mimetically recreated the painting, capturing every detail of the original. 她模仿地重现了这幅画，捕捉了原作的每一个细节。 | 近义词：imitatively, apishly,模拟地 | 反义词：originally, creatively, 独特地 | 词性：副词
