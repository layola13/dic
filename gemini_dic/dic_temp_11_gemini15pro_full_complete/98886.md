The pain was so intenible that she had to go to the hospital. 疼痛难以忍受，她不得不去医院。 | 近义词: indefensible, unsustainable, unjustifiable, unbearable, intolerable | 反义词: tenable, defensible, justifiable, bearable, tolerable | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *intenibilis, from in- "not" + tenibilis "that can be held," from tenere "to hold."
