murkily:/ˈmɜːrkɪli/ /ˈmɝːkɪli/
adverb|昏暗地，朦胧地，模糊地|in a murky way; darkly and gloomily; obscurely|The details of the agreement remain murkily defined.  协议的细节仍然模糊不清。|The river flowed murkily beneath the bridge. 河水在桥下浑浊地流淌。|近义词：dimly, obscurely, vaguely, darkly|反义词：clearly, brightly, distinctly|词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From murky + -ly. Murky itself comes from the Old English *myrce, of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old Norse myrkr "darkness."
