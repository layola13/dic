arnotto:/əˈnɑːtoʊ/ /əˈrɒtəʊ/| n.|胭脂树橙；胭脂树的种子|A reddish-orange dye or condiment made from the seeds of the achiote tree.|The chef used arnotto to give the rice a vibrant yellow color.  厨师用胭脂树橙给米饭增添鲜艳的黄色。|Synonyms: achiote, annatto|Antonyms: N/A|noun

arnotto:/əˈnɑːtoʊ/ /əˈrɒtəʊ/| adj.|胭脂树橙色的|Having the reddish-orange color of arnotto.|The arnotto-colored cheese was a local delicacy.  胭脂树橙色的奶酪是当地的一道美味佳肴。|Synonyms: achiote-colored, annatto-colored|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
