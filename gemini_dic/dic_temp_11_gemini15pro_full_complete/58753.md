dungmeer:/ˈdʌŋˌmɪər/
n.
粪海，粪堆
A large accumulation of dung, especially animal manure.  Often used figuratively to describe a situation that is extremely unpleasant or dirty.
The stable was a veritable dungmeer.  马厩简直就是一个粪海。
近义词:muckheap, manure pile, dunghill
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "clean area," "pristine environment")
词性:noun
