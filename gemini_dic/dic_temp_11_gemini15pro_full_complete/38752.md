condominium:/ˌkɒndəˈmɪniəm/ (US) /ˌkɒndəˈmɪniəm/ (UK)| n. | 公寓；一套公寓房；共同统治；共管 |A building or complex of buildings containing a number of individually owned apartments or houses.|  I live in a condominium overlooking the park. 我住在一套可以俯瞰公园的公寓里。| synonyms: apartment, flat, unit | antonyms: house, detached house | noun
