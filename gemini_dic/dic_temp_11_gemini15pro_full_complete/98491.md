instamp:/ˈɪnstæmp/
verb
盖章；冲压；铭刻
to impress or imprint something, especially a mark or seal, onto a surface.
The official instamped the document with the seal.  官员在文件上盖了章。
The image was instamped on my memory. 这幅图像铭刻在我的记忆中。
Synonyms: imprint, impress, stamp, engrave
Antonyms: erase, remove
词性: 动词
