hydrosalt:/ˈhaɪdroʊsɔːlt/
noun
水盐
A salt containing water of crystallization.  A hydrate of a salt.
The hydrosalt was carefully weighed and added to the solution.  这种水盐被仔细称重并加入到溶液中。
hydrate, hydrated salt
anhydrous salt
