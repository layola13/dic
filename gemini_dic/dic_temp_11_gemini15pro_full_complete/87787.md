hibernation:/ˌhaɪbərˈneɪʃn/
n.
冬眠
A state of inactivity and metabolic depression in endotherms.
例句：Bears go into hibernation during the winter. 熊在冬天进入冬眠。

例句：The groundhog's hibernation lasts for several months. 土拨鼠的冬眠持续几个月。


近义词：dormancy, sleep
反义词：activity, wakefulness
词性：noun

hibernating:/ˈhaɪbərˌneɪtɪŋ/
adj.
冬眠的
Being in a state of hibernation.
例句：The hibernating bear didn't move for months. 冬眠的熊几个月没动了。

例句：Scientists study hibernating animals to learn about metabolic processes. 科学家研究冬眠的动物以了解代谢过程。


近义词：dormant, sleeping
反义词：active, awake
词性：adjective

hibernate:/ˈhaɪbərneɪt/
v.
冬眠
To spend the winter in a dormant state.
例句：Some animals hibernate to survive the cold winter months. 一些动物冬眠以度过寒冷的冬季。

例句：The hedgehog hibernates in a nest of leaves. 刺猬在树叶窝里冬眠。


近义词：sleep, overwinter
反义词：awaken
词性：verb
