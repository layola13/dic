inscribed:/ɪnˈskraɪbd/| adj.|刻写的，铭刻的|Marked or written on a surface, especially carved or engraved.|例句：The ring was inscribed with their initials.  戒指上刻着他们的姓名首字母。|近义词：engraved, etched, carved|反义词:erased, unmarked|词性:形容词
inscribe:/ɪnˈskraɪb/| vt.|题写，铭刻|To write, carve, or engrave (words or symbols) on something.|例句：His name was inscribed on the monument. 他的名字刻在了纪念碑上。|近义词：engrave, etch, carve|反义词:erase|词性:动词
