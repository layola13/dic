hypochondriasis:/ˌhaɪpəʊkɒnˈdraɪəsɪs/ /ˌhaɪpəʊkɑːnˈdraɪəsɪs/
n.
疑病症，臆想病
A psychological disorder characterized by excessive worry about having a serious illness, even in the absence of medical evidence.
He suffers from hypochondriasis and is always convinced he has a terminal illness. 他患有疑病症，总是确信自己得了绝症。
近义词：health anxiety, illness anxiety disorder
反义词:  robust health, wellness (These are antonyms of the symptom, not the condition itself)
词性: noun
