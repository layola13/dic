altho:/ɔːlˈðoʊ/| conj.|虽然，尽管| A shortened, informal form of "although." It introduces a subordinate clause that expresses a contrast or exception to the main clause.| Altho I was tired, I went to the party. 虽然我很累，但我还是去参加了聚会。|近义词：although, though, even though, while, albeit|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) yet, still, however|词性: conjunction
