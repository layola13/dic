ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "hold on," which itself is a phrasal verb composed of the verb "hold" (Old English *haldan*, from Proto-Germanic *haldaną) and the adverb "on" (Old English on, an, from Proto-Germanic *ana). The gerund form "holding-on" nominalizes the action of holding on.
