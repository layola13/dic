nonciteable:/ˌnɒnsaɪˈteɪbl/ | adj. | 不可引用的，不能引证的 | Not suitable or allowed to be cited or quoted as evidence or support. |  The preliminary report is nonciteable and should not be distributed. 这份初步报告不可引用，不应分发。|  The data in this draft version are nonciteable.  这个草稿版本中的数据不可引用。| 近义词: unquotable, unusable | 反义词: citeable, quotable | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "cite" (from Latin "citare," meaning "to summon, quote") + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being")
