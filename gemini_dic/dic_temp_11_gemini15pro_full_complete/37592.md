communicability:/kəˌmjuːnɪkəˈbɪləti/
n.|可传达性，传播性|The communicability of an idea depends on its clarity and simplicity. 一个想法的可传达性取决于它的清晰度和简洁性。|例句：The communicability of the virus is a major concern for public health officials. 病毒的传播性是公共卫生官员的主要担忧。|近义词：transmissibility, spreadability|反义词：noncommunicability, incontagiousness|词性:名词

communicability:/kəˌmjuːnɪkəˈbɪləti/
n.|感染性|The communicability of the disease is high. 这种疾病的传染性很高。|例句：Measles has high communicability. 麻疹传染性很强。|近义词：contagiousness, infectivity|反义词：non-contagiousness|词性:名词
