materiate:/məˈtɪəriˌeɪt/ | adj.  v. | 物质的，实体的；赋予形体，使具体化 | Composed of matter; to give material form to; to embody. | The artist materiated his vision in a stunning sculpture. 艺术家将他的愿景具体化在一座令人惊叹的雕塑中。|  The spirit supposedly materialized before their eyes. 据说，灵魂在他们眼前显现了。 | 近义词: embody, materialize, substantialize, reify | 反义词: immaterialize, dematerialize, spiritualize | 词性: adjective, verb

词源: From Late Latin *materiatus, past participle of materiare "to make of matter," from Latin materia "matter."
