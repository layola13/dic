agroindustrial:/ˌæɡroʊɪnˈdʌstriəl/
adjective
农业工业的；农产工业的
Relating to or involved in both agriculture and industry, typically referring to the processing of agricultural products.
The country's agroindustrial sector is booming.  这个国家的农产工业蓬勃发展。
Agroindustrial development is key to economic growth. 农产工业发展是经济增长的关键。
近义词:agribusiness, agricultural-industrial
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "artisanal" or "traditional" in certain contexts)
词性:adjective
