landfilling:/ˈlændfɪlɪŋ/
n.
垃圾填埋
The process of disposing of waste material by burying it in a designated area.
The city is looking for alternatives to landfilling.  这个城市正在寻找垃圾填埋的替代方案。
dumping, burying
incineration, composting
noun

landfill:/ˈlændfɪl/
n.
垃圾填埋场
A designated area where waste is buried.
The landfill is nearing capacity. 垃圾填埋场快要满了。
dump, tip
recycling center
noun
