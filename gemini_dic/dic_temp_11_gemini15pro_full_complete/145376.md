overcapacity:/ˌoʊvər kəˈpæsəti/ | n. | 生产过剩，产能过剩 | A situation in which the supply of something exceeds the demand for it. | The factory was operating at overcapacity to meet holiday demand. 工厂为了满足节假日需求而超负荷运转。 | Example: The airline industry suffered from overcapacity after the pandemic. 航空业在大流行后产能过剩。| Synonyms: glut, surplus, excess | Antonyms: shortage, deficit, undersupply | noun
