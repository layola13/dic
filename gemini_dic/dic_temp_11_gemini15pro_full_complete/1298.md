accounter:/əˈkaʊntər/| n.|会计；会计师|A person whose job is to keep or check financial accounts. |The company hired a new accounter to manage their finances. 公司聘请了一位新的会计来管理他们的财务。|Example: The accounter meticulously recorded every transaction.  会计一丝不苟地记录了每一笔交易。|Synonym: accountant, bookkeeper|Antonym: N/A|noun
