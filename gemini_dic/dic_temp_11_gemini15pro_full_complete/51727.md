devoe:/dɪˈvoʊ/ | n. |  奉献，热爱；虔诚 |  Devotion; ardent attachment.  A misspelling of "devotion."  |  His devoe to his work was admirable, though perhaps a bit excessive. 他对工作的热爱令人钦佩，尽管可能有点过头了。 | 近义词: devotion, dedication, commitment, zeal, fervor | 反义词: apathy, indifference, neglect | 词性: 名词 (Noun)

词源:  Derived from the Old French word "devotion," ultimately from the Latin "devotio," meaning "a vowing away, dedication."
