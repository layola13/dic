all-for-the-best:/ɔːl fɔr ðə best/ | adj. phrase |  一切都会好起来的；为了更大的利益；出于好意 |  Used to suggest that although a situation seems bad or unpleasant, it is probably the best thing that could have happened.  It implies a positive outlook on a potentially negative situation. |  It's hard now, but it's all for the best in the long run.  现在很艰难，但从长远来看，一切都会好起来的。 | Synonyms: beneficial, advantageous, fortunate | Antonyms: unfortunate, disastrous, detrimental | phrase
