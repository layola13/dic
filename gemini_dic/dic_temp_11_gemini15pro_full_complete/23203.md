bowenite:/ˈboʊəˌnaɪt/| n.|鲍文石|A massive, compact variety of serpentine, usually apple-green, but sometimes darker green or almost black, that takes a high polish and is used as an ornamental stone.|The carver chose a piece of bowenite for his next project. 雕刻家选择了一块鲍文石用于他的下一个项目。|近义词：serpentine, jade|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific mineral)|词性:noun
