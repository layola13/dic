ferrocyanate:/ˌfɛroʊˈsaɪəneɪt/
noun
亚铁氰化物，六氰合铁(II)酸盐
a salt of ferrocyanic acid containing the tetravalent anion [Fe(CN)6]4−.
Potassium ferrocyanate is used in the production of Prussian blue. 亚铁氰化钾用于生产普鲁士蓝。
Synonyms: hexacyanoferrate(II)
Antonyms: ferricyanate
