iffy:/ˈɪfi/
adjective
不确定的，可疑的
uncertain or doubtful
The future of the company is iffy. 公司的前途未卜。
It's iffy whether he'll be able to come. 他能不能来还很难说。
近义词:uncertain, doubtful, risky, dicey
反义词:certain, definite, safe, sure
词性:adjective

词源:
"iffy" is an informal word that likely originated in the mid-20th century. It's thought to be derived from "if," reflecting the sense of conditionality and uncertainty. "iffier" is simply the comparative form of "iffy."
