cineaste:/ˈsɪniːæst/ /ˈsɪnɪæst/| n.|电影爱好者；电影迷；影迷|A cineaste is a devotee of motion pictures, especially one who is knowledgeable or enthusiastic about technique and artistry.  A person who is seriously interested in the cinema, especially as an art form.|The renowned director is considered a true cineaste, known for his deep appreciation of film history.  这位著名的导演被认为是一位真正的电影爱好者，以其对电影史的深刻理解而闻名。

The local cineaste queued for hours to see the premiere of the new independent film.  这位当地的电影爱好者排了几个小时的队才看到这部新的独立电影的首映。|近义词：film buff, movie lover, film enthusiast, connoisseur of film |反义词:philistine (对艺术无兴趣的人)|词性:noun
