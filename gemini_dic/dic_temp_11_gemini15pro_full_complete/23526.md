brainage: /ˈbreɪnˌeɪdʒ/
n.
脑龄; 心智年龄
A measure of brain health based on cognitive performance, often compared to the average cognitive performance of individuals of the same chronological age.
Her brainage is much younger than her actual age. 她的脑龄比她的实际年龄年轻得多。
Synonyms: mental age, cognitive age
Antonyms: chronological age
