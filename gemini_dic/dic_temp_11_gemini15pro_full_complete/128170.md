network:/ˈnɛtwɜːk/ (US) /ˈnɛtwɜːk/ (UK)| n. v.|网络，网状物；广播网，电视网；人际网|A group or system of interconnected people or things.|The company has a network of offices across the country.  这家公司在全国各地都有办事处网络。|近义词：system, web, grid|反义词:isolation, disconnection|词性:名词

network:/ˈnɛtwɜːk/ (US) /ˈnɛtwɜːk/ (UK)| v. |建立联系，建立人际网；联网|To connect with other people or organizations, especially professionally.|He's trying to network with potential clients. 他正试图与潜在客户建立联系。|近义词：connect, link, interact|反义词:isolate, disconnect|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: *net* + *work*, denoting something made of net-like fabric. The modern sense dates from the mid 19th century.
