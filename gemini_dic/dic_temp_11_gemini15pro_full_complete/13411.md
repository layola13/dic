atomizer:/ˈætəmaɪzər/ (US), /ˈætəmaɪzə/ (UK)| n.|雾化器，喷雾器|A device that converts a liquid into a fine spray or mist.|例句：She used an atomizer to apply perfume. 她用喷雾器喷香水。

例句：The paint was applied using an atomizer for an even coat.  油漆是用喷雾器喷的，涂层很均匀。

|近义词：sprayer, nebulizer, vaporizer|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly condenser)|词性: noun
