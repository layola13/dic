mingers:/ˈmɪŋərz/ | n. |闲逛者；混日子的人；不务正业的人 | People who spend their time idly, especially hanging around in public places without any apparent purpose.  Often carries a negative connotation, suggesting they are unproductive or undesirable. |  例句：The mingers were hanging around the shopping centre all day. 那些闲人在购物中心闲逛了一整天。

例句：I don't want to spend my Saturday with a bunch of mingers. 我不想和一群游手好闲的人一起度过我的周六。 | 近义词：loafers, idlers, time-wasters, layabouts | 反义词：hard workers, busybodies | 词性：名词(Noun)
