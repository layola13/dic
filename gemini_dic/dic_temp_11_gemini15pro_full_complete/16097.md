bandstand:/ˈbændˌstænd/ | n. |  乐队演奏台；露天音乐台 | A raised platform, often covered, for a band or orchestra to perform on, typically in an outdoor setting. | The band played a lively tune on the bandstand in the park. 乐队在公园的露天音乐台上演奏了一首轻快的曲子。 | The town's bandstand was the focal point of the summer concerts.  小镇的露天音乐台是夏季音乐会的中心。 | 近义词: platform, stage, podium | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "audience seating area") | 词性: noun
