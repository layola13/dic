dagswain:/ˈdæɡˌsweɪn/ | n. | 船长，指挥员 | A boatswain, a petty officer on a ship in charge of rigging, anchors, cables, etc. and summoning the crew. | The dagswain ordered the crew to raise the anchor. 船长命令船员起锚。| The dagswain piped the crew to quarters. 船长用哨子召集船员到岗位。 | 近义词: boatswain, bosun | 反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑 subordinate, crewman) | 词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *daggesweyn, from Old Norse *daggsveinn (“day-servant”), equivalent to dagr (“day”) + sveinn (“swain, servant”). Cognate with Danish dagsven, Icelandic dagsveinn, Swedish dagsvän.
