miscutting:/mɪsˈkʌtɪŋ/
n.
错误切割，切割失误
The act or result of cutting something incorrectly or inaccurately.
The miscutting of the fabric ruined the entire garment.  布料的切割错误毁了整件衣服。
The miscutting led to a significant loss of material.  切割失误导致了大量的材料损失。
近义词: mis-cut, incorrect cutting, inaccurate cutting
反义词: accurate cutting, precise cutting
词性: noun

miscut:/ˌmɪsˈkʌt/
v. (past tense: miscut, past participle: miscut, present participle: miscutting)
错误地切割
To cut something incorrectly or inaccurately.
He miscut the wood, making it too short.  他把木头切错了，切得太短了。
The tailor miscut the cloth, ruining the suit.  裁缝把布料切错了，毁了这套西装。
近义词: cut wrong, cut incorrectly, cut inaccurately
反义词: cut correctly, cut accurately, cut precisely
词性: verb

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly") + "cut" (verb meaning "to divide or separate with a sharp-edged tool")
