alleviate:/əˈliːvieɪt/
verb
减轻，缓和
to make something bad such as pain or problems less severe
The doctor gave me some medicine to alleviate the pain. 医生给了我一些药来减轻疼痛。

近义词:ease, lessen, relieve, mitigate, soothe
反义词:aggravate, worsen, exacerbate
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *alleviatus, past participle of alleviare "to lighten, relieve," from Latin ad- "to" + levis "light"
