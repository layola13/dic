judiciousness:/ˌdʒuːˈdɪʃəsnəs/| n.|明智；审慎；判断力|The quality of being judicious; sound judgment.|She demonstrated great judiciousness in handling the delicate situation. 她处理微妙局面的能力非常明智。|Synonyms: wisdom, prudence, sagacity, discernment, discretion|Antonyms: foolishness, imprudence, recklessness, indiscretion|noun

judicious:/dʒuˈdɪʃəs/| adj.|明智的；审慎的；有判断力的|Having, showing, or done with good judgment or sense.|He made a judicious choice of career. 他做出了明智的职业选择。|Synonyms: wise, sensible, prudent, discreet, sagacious|Antonyms: unwise, foolish, imprudent, reckless, indiscreet|adjective
