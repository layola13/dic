hydrant:/ˈhaɪdrənt/| n.|消防栓，消防龙头|A pipe, usually located on a street, with a connection for a fire hose, providing access to a water supply for firefighting.|The firefighters quickly connected the hose to the hydrant. 消防员迅速地将水管连接到消防栓上。|Synonyms: fireplug, standpipe|Antonyms: None|noun
