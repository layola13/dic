lemurid:/ˈlɛmjərɪd/ | n. |狐猴；狐猴类动物 |A primate of the suborder Strepsirrhini, native to Madagascar, Comoros, and other islands in the area.  They are characterized by their large eyes, long tails, and often nocturnal habits.| Lemurs are fascinating creatures found only on Madagascar. 狐猴是只在马达加斯加发现的迷人动物。|Synonyms:  prosimian, primate |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider anthropoid for contrasting primate group) | Noun
