fanfoot:/ˈfænˌfʊt/| n. |扇足；蹼足|A descriptive term for a bird with lobed or webbed feet, adapted for swimming or wading.  It refers to the shape of the foot resembling a fan.|The coot, with its distinctive fanfoot, easily navigates the marshy waters.  白骨顶鸡以其独特的扇足轻松地在沼泽地带航行。|近义词：webfoot|反义词:|n.
