gummer:/ˈɡʌmər/| n.|牙龈；无牙老人；粘胶；橡胶工人|A person who has lost most or all of their teeth, especially an elderly person.  One who gums food. One who applies gum or glue. A worker in a rubber factory.|The old gummer could only eat soft foods.|近义词：toothless person, denture wearer|反义词: (For the sense of a person) dentate person|noun
