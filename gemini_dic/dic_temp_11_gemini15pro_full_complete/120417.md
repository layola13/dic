minitour:/ˈmɪniˌtʊr/| n. |小型巡回演出，短期旅行|A short tour, often involving a limited number of locations or performances.|The band embarked on a minitour of the East Coast to promote their new album. 乐队开始在东海岸进行小型巡回演出，以宣传他们的新专辑。|近义词：short tour, brief tour, small tour|反义词: world tour, extended tour|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "mini-" (meaning small) + "tour."  "Mini-" is a borrowing from Latin "minimus" (smallest). "Tour" comes from Old French "tour," ultimately from Latin "tornus" (a lathe, something that turns).
