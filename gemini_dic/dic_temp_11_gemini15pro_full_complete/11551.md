aristocracies:/ˌærɪˈstɒkrəsiːz/
n.
贵族统治；贵族（aristocracy的复数）
The power was concentrated in the hands of a few wealthy aristocracies. 权力集中在少数富裕贵族手中。
Aristocracies throughout Europe were threatened by the rising tide of revolution. 整个欧洲的贵族都受到革命浪潮的威胁。
近义词:nobilities, elites
反义词:commoners, masses
词性:名词


aristocracy:/ˌærɪˈstɒkrəsi/
n.
贵族；贵族统治；上层社会；贵族阶层
The aristocracy owned vast tracts of land. 贵族拥有大片土地。
He married into the aristocracy. 他与贵族联姻。
近义词:nobility, elite, upper class
反义词:commoners, masses
词性:名词


词源：
From Old French *aristocracie*, from Late Latin aristocratia, from Greek aristokratia, from aristos (best) + -kratia (power, rule). Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *kretis (judge, ruler).
