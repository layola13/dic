counterinflation:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈfleɪʃən/ | n.| 反通货膨胀 |  The act or process of preventing or reducing inflation. | Counterinflation is a critical concern for central banks. 反通货膨胀是各国央行的重要关切事项。 | synonyms: disinflation, price stability | antonyms: inflation | noun
