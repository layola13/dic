incommunicable:/ˌɪnkəˈmjuːnɪkəbl/
adjective
无法表达的；难以言喻的
not capable of being communicated or imparted; inexpressible
Her joy was so profound, so incommunicable. 她的喜悦如此深沉，难以言喻。
Synonyms: inexpressible, indescribable, unutterable, ineffable
Antonyms: communicable, expressible, describable, utterable
词性: 形容词


incommunicado:/ˌɪnkəˌmjuːnɪˈkɑːdoʊ/
adjective/adverb
禁止通讯的；单独监禁的
not able, allowed, or willing to communicate with other people
He was held incommunicado for several weeks. 他被单独监禁了几个星期。
Synonyms: isolated, secluded, sequestered
Antonyms: connected, accessible
词性: 形容词/副词
