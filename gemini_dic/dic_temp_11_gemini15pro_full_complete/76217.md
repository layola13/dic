futilely:/ˈfjuːtaɪlli/ /ˈfjutl̩i/| adv.|徒劳地，无效地|in a futile manner; unsuccessfully.  Characterized by having no useful result.|He tried futilely to open the locked door. 他徒劳地试图打开锁着的门。|Synonyms: vainly, unsuccessfully, pointlessly, fruitlessly|Antonyms: effectively, successfully, productively|词性:副词

futile:/ˈfjuːtaɪl/ /ˈfjutl̩i/| adj.|徒劳的，无效的|incapable of producing any useful result; pointless.|It was futile to argue with him.  和他争论是徒劳的。|Synonyms: vain, useless, pointless, fruitless|Antonyms: effective, successful, productive|词性:形容词
