culvert:/ˈkʌlvərt/| n.|涵洞，排水管|A culvert is a tunnel carrying a stream or open drain under a road or railroad.|The culvert under the highway was blocked, causing flooding. 公路下的涵洞堵塞了，造成了洪水。|近义词：drain, conduit, channel|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 dam, barrier  -  这些词阻挡水流，与涵洞的功能相反)|词性: 名词
