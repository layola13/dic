flashiness:/ˈflæʃɪnɪs/
n.| 华丽，炫耀，俗艳|The flashiness of his clothes was rather off-putting. 他的衣服过于华丽，让人有点反感。|flashy, flamboyant, ostentatious, showy|understated, subtle, modest|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From flashy + -ness.  Flashy itself comes from flash (verb), meaning to shine brightly and suddenly.  The verb flash ultimately derives from Old Norse *flasa.
