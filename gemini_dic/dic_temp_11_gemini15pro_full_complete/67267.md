exquisite:/ɪkˈskwɪzɪt/ /ɛkˈskwɪzɪt/| adj.|精致的；精美的；剧烈的；异常的|Characterized by intricate and beautiful craftsmanship or design; intensely felt; acute.|The museum displayed exquisite porcelain vases from the Ming Dynasty.  博物馆展出了明朝的精美瓷花瓶。|Synonyms: delicate, elegant, refined, superb, perfect, intense, acute|Antonyms: crude, coarse, rough, ordinary, dull|adjective


exquisite:/ɪkˈskwɪzɪt/ /ɛkˈskwɪzɪt/| n.|穿着讲究的男子|A man who is exquisitely dressed and refined in his manners.|He was considered an exquisite by his peers due to his impeccable style. 他因其无可挑剔的风格而被同龄人视为穿着讲究的男子。|Synonyms: dandy, fop|Antonyms: slob|noun (rare)
