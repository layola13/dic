americiums: /ˌæməˈrɪsiəmz/ | n. |镅|A synthetic radioactive metallic transuranic element of atomic number 95, used in smoke detectors.  It is the only synthetic element to have found its way into the household, where one common type of smoke detector uses 241Am in the form of americium dioxide as its source of ionizing radiation.|Americiums are used in some types of smoke detectors. 镅被用在某些类型的烟雾探测器中。|Synonyms: transuranic elements, actinides|Antonyms: None (as it's a specific element)|noun
