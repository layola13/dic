abelian:/əˈbiːliən/| adj.|阿贝尔的；交换的；可交换的|Of or relating to Niels Henrik Abel or his mathematical discoveries, especially his demonstration of the impossibility of solving the general quintic equation by radicals.  Involving operations that are commutative.  (of a group) such that the result of applying the group operation to two group elements does not depend on the order in which they are written.|The abelian groups are all commutative.  阿贝尔群都是可交换的。|commutative, commutable|noncommutative, nonabelian|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
Named after Niels Henrik Abel (1802-1829), a Norwegian mathematician.
