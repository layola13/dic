marshy:/ˈmɑːrʃi/ /ˈmɑːrʃi/| adj.|沼泽的，湿软的|Characterized by swamps or marshes; boggy.  Resembling a marsh in consistency.|The path led through marshy ground. 这条小路穿过一片沼泽地。|近义词：swampy, boggy, miry, fenny|反义词：dry, arid|词性:形容词
Etymology: From marsh + -y.  "Marsh" comes from Old English *mersc (related to Dutch moeras, German Marsch), of uncertain origin, perhaps from a PIE root meaning "sea, lake."
