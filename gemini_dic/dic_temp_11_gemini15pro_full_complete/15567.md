baguette:/bæˈɡɛt/ | n. | 法式长棍面包 | A long, thin loaf of French bread with a crisp crust. |例句：I bought a fresh baguette from the bakery this morning. 我今天早上从面包店买了一条新鲜的法棍面包。

例句：The baguette was still warm from the oven. 这法棍面包还带着烤箱的余温。| 近义词：French bread, loaf | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French baguette ("stick, rod, wand"), diminutive of bague ("ring"). Ultimately from Vulgar Latin *baca ("strap, tie").
