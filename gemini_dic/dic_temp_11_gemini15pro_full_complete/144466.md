outteased: /ˌaʊtˈtiːzd/
verb
戏弄得更厉害; 比...更会戏弄; 胜过...的戏弄
to tease or mock someone more effectively or cleverly than someone else.
He outteased his brother, leaving him speechless. 他比他弟弟更会戏弄人，让他哑口无言。
近义词: outwit, outsmart, outmaneuver
反义词: be outdone, lose to
