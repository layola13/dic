inadmissible:/ˌɪnədˈmɪsəbl/ | adj. | 不可接受的； inadmissible在法律上指不能作为证据的 | Not allowable as evidence; not to be admitted or allowed. |  The evidence was deemed inadmissible in court.  证据被认为在法庭上不可采纳。|  The judge ruled the confession inadmissible because it was coerced. 法官裁定供词不可接受，因为它是被胁迫的。 |  unacceptable, invalid, ineligible, improper | admissible, acceptable, valid, legitimate | adjective
