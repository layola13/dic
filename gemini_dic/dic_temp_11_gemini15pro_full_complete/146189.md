overexerted:/ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzɜːrtɪd/| adj.|过度劳累的，用力过度的|Having exerted oneself too much; strained.|例句：He felt overexerted after the marathon. 他在马拉松比赛后感到筋疲力尽。|例句：She was overexerted from working double shifts.  她因为连上两班而过度劳累。|近义词：exhausted, fatigued, worn out, spent|反义词:rested, refreshed|词性:形容词
词源: From over- + exert.  Over- is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much".  Exert comes from the Latin *exserere*, meaning "to thrust out" or "put forth".
