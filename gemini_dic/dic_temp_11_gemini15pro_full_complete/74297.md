fractionary:/ˈfrækʃənəri/| adj.|碎片的，零碎的；不足整数的|Relating to, containing, or constituting a fraction; fractional.  Broken into separate parts. Involving or expressed as a fraction, especially a small one.|The fractionary reserve banking system can lead to financial instability. 部分准备金银行体系可能导致金融不稳定。|Synonyms: fractional, fragmented, incomplete, partial|Antonyms: whole, complete, entire|adjective
