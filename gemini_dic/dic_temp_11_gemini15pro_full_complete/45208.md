cullet:/ˈkʌlɪt/
n.
碎玻璃，玻璃渣，废玻璃片
Broken or waste glass, especially that which is to be remelted.
The cullet is added to the batch to facilitate melting.  废玻璃片被添加到配料中以促进熔化。
The factory uses cullet to make new bottles.  这家工厂用碎玻璃制造新瓶子。
synonyms: broken glass, glass shards, waste glass
antonyms: new glass, intact glass
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *culette (“bottom, backside”), from Old French cuel (“bottom, backside”).  Ultimately from Latin cūlus (“anus”).  The sense development is perhaps from the idea of the base or bottom of a bottle.  Compare French cul (“bottom, backside”).
