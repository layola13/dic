kupfernickel:/ˈkʊpərnɪkəl/ | n. | 矿镍,假镍 | An old German name for niccolite (nickel arsenide), alluding to its deceptive copper-like appearance. Miners believed it contained copper but could not extract it. |  Kupfernickel was a source of frustration for early miners. 早期矿工对矿镍感到很沮丧。

The name kupfernickel reflects the challenges of early metallurgy. 矿镍这个名称反映了早期冶金学的挑战。 | 近义词: niccolite, nickel arsenide | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps copper ore since it was mistaken for it) | 词性: noun
