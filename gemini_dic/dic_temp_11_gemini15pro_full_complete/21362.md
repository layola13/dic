blossomer:/ˈblɑːsəmər/ /ˈblɒsəmə(r)/| n.|开花植物；处于发展期的人或事物|Something that blossoms; a plant that is blossoming or about to blossom. A person or thing that is developing and flourishing.|The apple tree is a beautiful blossomer in the spring.  这棵苹果树在春天开花很漂亮。|近义词：bloomer, flowerer|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用withered plant, dying plant)|词性:noun
