frequenting:/ˈfriːkwentɪŋ/
|v.|经常光顾，常去|The act of visiting a place or person often.|He was known for frequenting the local pubs. 他以经常光顾当地的酒吧而闻名。|近义词：visiting, attending, haunting|反义词：avoiding, shunning|Verb

frequents:/ˈfriːkwents/
|v.|经常光顾，常去|Visits a place or person often.|She frequents the library. 她经常去图书馆。|近义词：visits, attends, haunts|反义词：avoids, shuns|Verb

frequent:/ˈfriːkwənt/
|adj.|频繁的，经常的|Occurring or done often.|There were frequent power outages during the storm.  暴风雨期间经常停电。|近义词：regular, common, habitual|反义词：rare, infrequent, occasional|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *frequentyn, from Old French frequenter, from Latin frequentāre (“to visit often, resort to, haunt, crowd, people, visit, celebrate”), from frequēns (“crowded, numerous, frequent”).
