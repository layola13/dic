long-distance:/ˌlɔːŋ ˈdɪstəns/ | adj. | 长途的，远距离的 |  Covering a large distance.  Relating to or traveling over a long distance. |  I have a long-distance relationship with my girlfriend. 我和我的女朋友处于异地恋。

long-distance:/ˌlɔːŋ ˈdɪstəns/ | n. | 长途，远距离 |  A long distance. |  He prefers running the long-distance. 他更喜欢跑长跑。
