bush-league: /ˈbʊʃˌliːɡ/ | adj. |  二流的；业余的；不专业的 | Characteristic of a minor league, especially in baseball; unsophisticated, unprofessional, or second-rate.  | That was a real bush-league move, forgetting your passport. 那是真不专业，居然忘了带护照。| 近义词: amateur, unprofessional, second-rate, minor-league | 反义词: professional, major-league, sophisticated, first-class | 词性: 形容词

bush-league: /ˈbʊʃˌliːɡ/ | n. |  二流水平；业余水平 | The minor leagues, especially in baseball; a low or unprofessional level of performance. |  He's been playing bush-league ball for years and will never make it to the majors. 他已经在二流联赛打了多年球，永远也进不了大联盟。 | 近义词: minor leagues, amateurism | 反义词: major leagues, professionalism | 词性: 名词


词源:  Originated in American baseball, referring to teams in rural areas with less skilled players compared to the major leagues.  "Bush" referred to the rural setting of these leagues.
