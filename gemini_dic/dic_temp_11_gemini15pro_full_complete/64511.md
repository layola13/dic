erosive:/ɪˈroʊsɪv/ /ɪˈrəʊsɪv/| adj.|腐蚀的；侵蚀的；磨蚀的|tending to erode or to induce erosion|Acid rain is erosive to limestone buildings. 酸雨对石灰岩建筑物有腐蚀作用。|近义词：corrosive, destructive, abrasive|反义词：constructive, protective|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *ērōsīvus*, from ērōsus (past participle of ērōdere ‘gnaw away’), from ex- ‘out of’ + rōdere ‘gnaw’.
