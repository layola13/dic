overgambles:/ˌoʊvərˈɡæmbəlz/
verb
过度赌博
Gambles excessively or more than one can afford.
He overgambles and often finds himself in debt. 他赌博过度，经常负债累累。
She overgambles every weekend and loses a lot of money. 她每个周末都赌博过度，输了很多钱。
Synonyms: gambles too much, bets excessively, wagers excessively
Antonyms: gambles responsibly, bets moderately, wagers cautiously
词性: 动词
