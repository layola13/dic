hesitators:/ˈhezɪteɪtərz/
n.
犹豫者，迟疑者
People who hesitate; those who are indecisive or reluctant to act.
The hesitators stood back while the bolder ones jumped into the pool.  犹豫不决的人们站在后面，而胆大的人跳进了游泳池。
The group of hesitators finally made their decision. 这群犹豫不决的人终于做出了决定。
近义词: waverers, doubters, procrastinators
反义词:  decision-makers, doers
