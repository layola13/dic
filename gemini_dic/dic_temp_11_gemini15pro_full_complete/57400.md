dozzled:/ˈdɒzəld/ (UK) /ˈdɑːzəld/ (US)| adj.| 眼花缭乱的，昏昏沉沉的|Confused and unable to think clearly, especially because of a shock or blow to the head.| I was dozzled by the bright lights. 我被明亮的灯光照得眼花缭乱。|Synonyms: dazed, stunned, bewildered|Antonyms: clear-headed, alert|adjective

dozzled:/ˈdɒzəld/ (UK) /ˈdɑːzəld/ (US)| v.| 使眼花缭乱，使昏昏沉沉|To make someone feel confused and unable to think clearly.| The sudden flash of lightning dozzled me. 突然的闪电让我眼花缭乱。|Synonyms: daze, stun, bewilder|Antonyms: clarify, enlighten|verb
