discomforted: /dɪsˈkʌmfərtɪd/ | adj. | 不安的，尴尬的，不舒服的 | Feeling uneasy, embarrassed, or slightly ashamed.  |  The pointed question left him feeling quite discomfited.  尖锐的问题让他感到非常不安。 | synonyms: uneasy, embarrassed, awkward, ill at ease, self-conscious, abashed, disconcerted, flustered, perturbed | antonyms: comfortable, at ease, relaxed, confident, composed | adjective

discomfited: /dɪsˈkʌmfərtɪd/ | v. | 使不安，使尴尬 | To make someone feel uneasy or embarrassed.  | The unexpected news discomfited her. 这突如其来的消息让她感到不安。 | synonyms:  unsettle, disconcert, perturb, ruffle, discompose, faze, rattle, unnerve | antonyms: comfort, reassure, soothe, calm | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + comfort + -ed (past participle suffix).  Ultimately from Latin com- (together) + fortis (strong).
