irradiation:/ɪˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 辐射，照射 |  The process of exposing something to radiation.  The act of radiating something, such as light or heat. The apparent enlargement of a bright object seen against a dark background. |  The irradiation of the tumor shrank it significantly.  肿瘤的辐射治疗使其明显缩小。| synonyms: exposure, radiation, illumination | antonyms: shielding, protection | noun

irradiate:/ɪˈreɪdieɪt/ | v. | 照射，辐射；照亮；使焕发 | To expose to radiation. To shine brightly; to illuminate. To spread out from a central point. | The sun irradiated the Earth.  太阳照射着地球。 The diamond irradiated a dazzling light. 钻石发出耀眼的光芒。| synonyms: illuminate, shine, beam | antonyms: darken, obscure | verb
