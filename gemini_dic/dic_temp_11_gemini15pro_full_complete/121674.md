misplanted:/ˌmɪsˈplɑːntɪd/
adjective|移植错误的，种植在错误地方的|Planted in the wrong place or position.
The misplanted tree struggled to grow in the shady spot.|这棵移植错误的树在阴凉处挣扎着生长。
misplaced, wrongly positioned|correctly planted, well-situated
adjective

misplant:/ˌmɪsˈplɑːnt/
verb|移植错误，种植在错误的地方|To plant in the wrong place or position.
I misplanted the roses, and they didn't bloom.|我把玫瑰种错了地方，它们没有开花。
misplace, wrongly position|correctly plant, properly site
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
mis- (prefix): from Old English, meaning "wrongly," "badly," or "unsuitably."
plant (verb): from Old French planter, ultimately from Latin plantare "to fix in place, set," from planta "sprout, slip, cutting."
