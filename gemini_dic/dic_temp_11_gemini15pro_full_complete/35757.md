cochrane:/ˈkɒk.rən/ | n. | Cochrane (通常指 Cochrane Collaboration) |  A global independent network of researchers, professionals, patients, carers, and people interested in health. They produce systematic reviews of healthcare interventions, which are internationally recognized as the highest standard in evidence-based healthcare. |  The Cochrane review showed no clear benefit of the treatment.  Cochrane综述显示该治疗方法没有明显的益处。 | synonyms: Cochrane Collaboration, Cochrane Library, Cochrane Database of Systematic Reviews | antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but could consider biased research, anecdotal evidence) | noun

cochrane:/ˈkɒk.rən/ | adj. | Cochrane's (as in relating to Cochrane or his/her work) | Related to or produced by the Cochrane Collaboration. |  The Cochrane methodology is rigorous and transparent. Cochrane的方法论是严谨和透明的。 | synonyms:  (Often no direct synonym, context-dependent: evidence-based, systematic) | antonyms: (No direct antonyms, context-dependent: unsystematic, biased) | adjective
