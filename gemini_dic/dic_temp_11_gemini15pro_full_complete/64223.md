equimomental:/ˌiːkwɪməˈmɛntəl/
adjective
等力矩的；等惯性矩的
Having the same moments of inertia about all axes. Two bodies are equimomental if they have the same total mass and the same centroid, and their moments of inertia about any axis are equal.
Two rotating bodies are said to be equimomental when their kinetic energies are equal for all speeds of rotation. Two laminae are equimomental if they have the same mass, the same center of gravity and the same principal axes at the center of gravity, and their moments of inertia about the principal axes are equal.
The two bodies are equimomental. 这两个物体是等力矩的。
A thin circular ring and four equal particles placed at the corners of a square inscribed in the circle, if all have equal masses, are equimomental. 一个薄的圆环和四个质量相等的质点放置在内接于该圆的正方形的四个角上，如果它们的质量都相等，则它们是等力矩的。

synonym: dynamically equivalent, isokinetic
antonym: (No direct antonym, but one could consider "differently momental" or "having dissimilar moments of inertia" as conceptually opposite)
词性: adjective
