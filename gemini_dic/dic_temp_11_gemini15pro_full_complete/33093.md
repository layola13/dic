christen:/ˈkrɪsən/ /ˈkrɪsn/ |v.|命名；施洗；作为…的教名|To give a name to something, especially at a ceremony such as a baptism or naming; to officially name a ship or aircraft.  Also, to use something for the first time.|例句1：They christened the ship "The Mayflower". 他们将这艘船命名为“五月花号”。


例句2：The child was christened John.  这个孩子被命名为约翰。


近义词：name, baptize, dedicate|反义词:|词性:verb
