mouthes:/maʊðz/ /maʊθz/| n. |嘴；河口|The plural form of "mouth".  It refers to multiple mouths of people or animals, or the openings of rivers or other bodies of water.|The babies opened their mouthes, waiting to be fed. 婴儿们张开嘴巴，等着喂食。 The river has two mouthes where it flows into the sea. 这条河在入海口有两个河口。|近义词：(for river mouths) estuaries, outlets, deltas|反义词: (no direct antonym for mouths in general, for river mouths consider) source, origin|词性:Noun
