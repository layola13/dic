bezoardic:/ˌbezɔːˈɑːrdɪk/
adjective
胃石的；由胃石形成的
Of or relating to a bezoar.
The veterinarian discovered a bezoardic mass in the horse's stomach.  兽医在马的胃里发现了一块胃石。
The museum displayed a fascinating collection of bezoardic objects. 博物馆展出了一个引人入胜的胃石藏品。
近义词:  bezoar-related
反义词:  None
词性: adjective


词源:
From French *bézoardique*, from bézoard (“bezoar”) + -ique.  The word "bezoar" ultimately comes from Persian *pād-zahr* (پادزهر), literally “antidote, that which counteracts poison”.
