a little bird:/ə ˈlɪtl bɜːrd/ | idiom |  小道消息；秘密来源 |  This idiom refers to receiving information from a secret or anonymous source. It implies that the speaker doesn't want to reveal who told them the information. | A little bird told me you're getting a promotion.  有人私下告诉我你要升职了。|  rumor, grapevine, hearsay |  official announcement, confirmed report | noun phrase

little:/ˈlɪtl/ | adj. | 小的；少量的；幼小的 |  Small in size, amount, or degree; young. |  The little girl played with her doll. 小女孩玩她的洋娃娃。| small, tiny, miniature | large, big, huge | adjective

bird:/bɜːrd/ | n. | 鸟 |  A feathered, winged animal. |  The bird sang a beautiful song. 这只鸟唱了一首优美的歌。| avian, fowl | - | noun
