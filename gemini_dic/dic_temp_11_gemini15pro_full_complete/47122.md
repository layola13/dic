dauntlessly:/ˈdɔːntləsli/| adv.|无畏地，勇敢地|In a fearless and determined manner; without intimidation. |She dauntlessly faced the challenge, despite the risks. 她无畏地面对挑战，尽管有风险。|例句：He dauntlessly climbed the steep mountain. 他勇敢地爬上了陡峭的山峰。|近义词：fearlessly, bravely, courageously, boldly, intrepidly|反义词：fearfully, timidly, cowardly|词性:副词
词源: From *dauntless* + *-ly*. *Dauntless* comes from the Middle English *daunten*, meaning "to tame or subdue," ultimately from Old French *danter*.
