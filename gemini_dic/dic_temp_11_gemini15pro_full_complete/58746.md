dungeons:/ˈdʌndʒənz/
n.
(尤指城堡中的)地牢，地下城
underground prisons, especially in castles
The hero explored the dark dungeons of the castle. 英雄探索了城堡黑暗的地牢。
近义词:jails, prisons, underground cells
反义词:freedom, liberty
词性:名词

词源：
From Old French donjon, ultimately from Late Latin dominionem "lordship, domain."  Originally referring to the main tower of a castle, which often housed the prison.
