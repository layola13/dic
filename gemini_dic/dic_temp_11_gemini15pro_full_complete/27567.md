canalising:/ˈkænəlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
引导; 限制; 规范化;  (英式拼写) canalizing
restricting (something) to a particular course or direction.
例句：Genes canalising development ensure the robust formation of an organism despite environmental and genetic perturbations.  引导发育的基因确保了生物体尽管受到环境和遗传扰动的影响也能稳健地形成。

例句：The government is trying to canalise the flow of traffic away from the city centre. 政府正试图引导交通流离开市中心。
近义词：directing, guiding, channeling, restricting, confining
反义词：diverting, spreading, expanding
词性: adjective
