briquets:/brɪˈkɛts/
n.
煤球，煤饼，引火块
A block of compressed flammable material used for lighting fires.
He lit the charcoal briquets in the barbecue. 他在烧烤架里点燃了煤球。

近义词: briquette, coal, charcoal, firelighter, fire starter
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "fuelwood" as a less processed alternative)
词性: noun


briquette:/brɪˈkɛt/
n.
煤球，煤饼，引火块
A block of compressed material, especially coal dust or charcoal, used for fuel.
She used a briquette to start the fire. 她用一块煤饼生火。

近义词: briquet, coal, charcoal, firelighter, fire starter
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
