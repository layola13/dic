enforce:/ɪnˈfɔːrs/ (US) /ɪnˈfɔːs/ (UK)| vt.|执行，实施，强制|to make people obey a law or rule, or to make a particular situation happen or be accepted|The police are responsible for enforcing the law. 警察负责执法。|近义词：implement, apply, execute, carry out|反义词：neglect, ignore|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *enforcier "to strengthen, reinforce," from en- "in" + force "force."
