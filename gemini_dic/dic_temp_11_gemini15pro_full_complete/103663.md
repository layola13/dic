jollily:/ˈdʒɒləli/ (US) /ˈdʒɑːləli/ (UK)| adv.|快乐地，高兴地|In a jolly manner; merrily; cheerfully.|He sang jollily as he walked down the street. 他沿着街道走着，快乐地唱着歌。|She jollily skipped down the path. 她欢快地沿着小路跳跃。| 近义词: merrily, cheerfully, happily, gaily, joyfully|反义词: sadly, gloomily, miserably, morosely|词性:副词

词源:
From the adjective "jolly" + the adverbial suffix "-ly". "Jolly" comes from the Old French "joli" meaning "pretty, pleasant, amusing". Ultimately from the Old Norse "jól" meaning "Yule", a midwinter feast.
