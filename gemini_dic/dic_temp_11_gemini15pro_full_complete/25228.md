budgie:/ˈbʌdʒi/| n. |虎皮鹦鹉|A small, colorful parrot, often kept as a pet. It is short for budgerigar.|例句：My budgie loves to sing. 我的虎皮鹦鹉喜欢唱歌。

例句：The budgie flew around the room. 虎皮鹦鹉在房间里飞来飞去。|近义词：parakeet,  budgerigar|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


budgy:/ˈbʌdʒi/| adj. |矮胖的，圆胖的 (Informal, Australian)|Short and plump; podgy.|例句：He's a bit budgy around the middle. 他的腰有点粗。

例句：She has a budgy little face. 她有一张圆嘟嘟的小脸。|近义词：chubby, plump, podgy, dumpy|反义词:thin, slim, slender|词性:adjective
