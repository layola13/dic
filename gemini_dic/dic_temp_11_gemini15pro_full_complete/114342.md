majesticalness:/məˈdʒɛstɪkəlnəs/ | n. | 雄伟, 庄严, 壮丽 | The state or quality of being majestic; grandeur. | The majesticalness of the mountains awed the hikers. 群山的雄伟使徒步旅行者感到敬畏。 | 近义词: majesty, grandeur, magnificence, splendor | 反义词: insignificance, commonness, ordinariness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From majestic + -ness.  "Majestic" comes from the Old French "majesté," ultimately from the Latin "maiestas" (greatness, dignity).  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns expressing a state or quality.
