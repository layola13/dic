metacenter:/ˈmetəˌsentər/| n. |稳心|The point of intersection of the lines of action of the buoyant force acting on a floating body when the body is tilted and the vertical line through the center of buoyancy of the body when it is not tilted.  一个漂浮物体倾斜时作用在其上的浮力的作用线与物体未倾斜时通过其浮心垂线的交点。|The metacenter of the ship is crucial for its stability. 这艘船的稳心对于它的稳定性至关重要。| The position of the metacenter determines whether a vessel will remain upright. 稳心的位置决定了一艘船是否会保持直立。|Synonyms: center of buoyancy, stability point|Antonyms: N/A|noun
