intervisible:/ˌɪntərˈvɪzəbl/
adjective
互相可见的
able to be seen one from the other.
The two mountain peaks are intervisible. 这两座山峰互相可见。
近义词: visible from each other, mutually visible
反义词: invisible, obscured, hidden
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From inter- (meaning "between, mutually") + visible (from Latin visibilis, meaning "able to be seen").
