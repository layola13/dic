intervertebral:/ˌɪntərˈvɜːrtɪbrəl/| adj.|椎间盘的，脊椎间的|Located or occurring between two vertebrae.  Relating to the area between two vertebrae.|例句：Intervertebral discs act as cushions between the vertebrae. 椎间盘充当椎骨之间的缓冲垫。

例句：Intervertebral fusion is a surgical procedure.  椎间融合术是一种外科手术。|近义词：spinal, vertebral|反义词: None|词性: adjective
