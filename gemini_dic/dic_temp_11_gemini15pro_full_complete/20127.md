bipartisanship: /ˌbaɪˈpɑːrtɪzənʃɪp/
n.
两党合作；跨党派合作
agreement or cooperation between two political parties that normally oppose each other's policies.
Example:  Bipartisanship is crucial for effective governance.  两党合作对于有效的治理至关重要。

Example: The bill passed with strong bipartisan support.  该法案在两党的强烈支持下获得通过。

近义词: cooperation, collaboration, unity, concord
反义词: partisanship, division, discord, polarization
词性: noun

Etymology:
From bi- (meaning "two") + partisan + -ship.  "Partisan" ultimately comes from the Italian "partito," meaning "party."
