einsteinian:/aɪnˈstaɪniən/
adjective
爱因斯坦的; 极其聪明的
Relating to Albert Einstein, his theories, or his method of scientific thought; exceptionally intelligent.
His theories were so revolutionary they could only be described as Einsteinian. 他的理论如此具有革命性，只能用“爱因斯坦式的”来形容。
synonyms: brilliant, genius, revolutionary, groundbreaking, innovative
antonyms: unoriginal, conventional, mundane, ordinary
词性: 形容词

einsteinian:/aɪnˈstaɪniən/
noun
极其聪明的人
A person of exceptional intelligence.
He was considered an Einsteinian in the field of astrophysics. 他在天体物理学领域被认为是一位极其聪明的人。
synonyms: genius, intellectual, prodigy, savant
antonyms: simpleton, dullard
词性: 名词
