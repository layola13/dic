instituter:/ˈɪnstɪˌtjuːtər/ | v. | 建立；设立；制定；开始；实行 | To establish, organize, and set in operation; to initiate; to begin | The government instituted a new policy on education. 政府制定了一项新的教育政策。 |  He instituted legal proceedings against his neighbor. 他对邻居提起法律诉讼。| 近义词：establish, found, inaugurate, initiate, commence | 反义词：abolish, terminate, discontinue | 词性: 动词

instituter:/ˈɪnstɪˌtjuːtər/ | n. | 创立者；设立者；创始人 | A person who institutes something; a founder |  He was the instituter of the new program. 他是新项目的创始人。 |  The instituters of the company are still involved in its management. 公司的创始人们仍然参与公司的管理。 | 近义词：founder, establisher, originator | 反义词： N/A | 词性: 名词
