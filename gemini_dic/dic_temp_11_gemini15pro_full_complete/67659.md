extruct:/ɪkˈstrʌkt/| v. |提取，取出|To remove or take out, often with effort or by a special method.|The dentist extracted my wisdom tooth. 牙医拔掉了我的智齿。|近义词：remove, extract, pull out, take out|反义词:insert, implant|动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin extrāctus, past participle of extrāhere ("to draw out"), from ex ("out of") + trahere ("to draw, pull").
