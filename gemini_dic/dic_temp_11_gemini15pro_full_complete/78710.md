gigantism:/dʒaɪˈɡænˌtɪzəm/| n. |巨人症；巨大发育；巨大化|A condition characterized by excessive growth and height significantly above average, often caused by overproduction of growth hormone during childhood.|The boy's gigantism was evident in his towering height. 这个男孩的巨人症从他高耸的身高中就可以看出来。|Synonyms: acromegaly, giantism|Antonyms: dwarfism|noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *gigantismus, from Latin gigant-, gigas "giant" + -ismus -ism.
