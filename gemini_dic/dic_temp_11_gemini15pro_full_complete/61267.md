emargination:/iːˌmɑːrdʒɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |边缘化；缺边；切迹|The act of removing a margin or edge; a notched appearance. The social process of becoming marginalized. |The emargination of the indigenous population has led to widespread poverty and social unrest. 原住民的边缘化导致了普遍的贫困和社会动荡。| The leaf showed signs of emargination at its tip.  这片叶子的尖端显示出缺边的迹象。| 近义词：marginalization, exclusion, isolation | 反义词：inclusion, integration | 词性: noun

词源: From Latin emarginatus, past participle of emarginare ("to deprive of a margin, to take away the edge"), from ex- ("out of, from") + marginare ("to furnish with a border"), from margo ("edge, border, margin").
