deadliness:/ˈdɛdlɪnəs/
n.
致命性；危险性
The deadliness of the virus shocked the world.
这种病毒的致命性震惊了世界。
The deadliness of the new weapon is a major concern.
这种新武器的致命性是一个主要担忧。
Synonym: lethality, fatality, dangerousness, perniciousness
Antonym: harmlessness, safety, innocuousness


Etymology:
From deadly + -ness.  "Deadly" comes from Old English *dēadlic, from dēad "dead."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
