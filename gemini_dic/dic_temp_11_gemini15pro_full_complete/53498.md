disassemblers:/dɪsəˈsɛmblərz/| n.|反汇编器，反汇编程序|A disassembler is a computer program that translates machine language into assembly language—the inverse operation to that of an assembler.  |Example: The programmer used a disassembler to understand the malware's functionality. 程序员使用反汇编器来理解恶意软件的功能。

Example:  Disassemblers are useful tools for reverse engineering. 反汇编器是逆向工程的有用工具。|近义词：decompiler (for higher-level languages)|反义词：assembler|词性:noun
