foundries:/ˈfaʊndriz/| n. |铸造厂；铸造车间|A factory or workshop where metal or glass is melted and poured into molds to make things.|例句：The town's economy was based on its iron foundries.  这个城镇的经济以其钢铁铸造厂为基础。

例句：Several large foundries closed down during the recession.  经济衰退期间，几家大型铸造厂倒闭了。|近义词：casting plants, smelters|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *fondrie "a casting," from fondre "to melt, cast," from Latin fundere "to pour, melt."
