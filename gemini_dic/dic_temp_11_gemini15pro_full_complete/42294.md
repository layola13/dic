counter-intelligence:/ˌkaʊntər ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns/
noun
反情报，反间谍
Activities designed to prevent or thwart spying by an enemy.
The CIA's counter-intelligence unit is working tirelessly to protect national security. 中央情报局的反情报部门正孜孜不倦地保护国家安全。
The agency's counter-intelligence efforts were crucial in uncovering the plot. 该机构的反情报工作对揭露阴谋至关重要。

Synonyms: counterespionage, countersurveillance
Antonyms: espionage, intelligence
