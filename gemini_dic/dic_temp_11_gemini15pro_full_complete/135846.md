nonprosaically:/ˌnɒnprəˈzeɪɪkli/ /ˌnɑnprəˈzeɪɪkli/
adverb
非散文地；不平淡地；不寻常地
Not in a prosaic or dull manner; imaginatively; unusually.
She described the event nonprosaically, using vivid imagery and metaphors. 她用生动的比喻和隐喻，非同寻常地描述了这件事。
She danced nonprosaically, her movements full of passion and grace. 她舞姿优美，充满激情和优雅，一点也不平淡。
Synonyms: imaginatively, poetically, lyrically, vividly, colorfully, creatively
Antonyms: prosaically, dully, plainly, simply, ordinarily, conventionally
词性: 副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + prosaic (adjective meaning "ordinary, dull") + -ally (suffix forming adverbs)
