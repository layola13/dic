acuminate:/əˈkjuːmɪneɪt/ | adj. | 渐尖的，锐尖的 | Tapering to a long point; pointed. | The acuminate leaves of the willow tree swayed gently in the breeze. 柳树尖尖的叶子在微风中轻轻摇曳。 | The plant has long, acuminate leaves. 这种植物有细长的尖叶。 | 近义词: pointed, sharp, acute, tapered | 反义词: obtuse, blunt | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Latin "acuminatus," past participle of "acuminare"  "to sharpen," from "acus" "needle."
