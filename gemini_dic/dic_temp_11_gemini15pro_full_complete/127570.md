nectareous:/nɛkˈtɛəriəs/
adjective
甜蜜的，如花蜜的
Resembling nectar; sweet; delicious.  Having the qualities of nectar, especially sweetness.
例句：The nectareous aroma of the honeysuckle filled the air.  金银花的甜蜜香气弥漫在空气中。

例句：The bees buzzed around the nectareous blossoms. 蜜蜂在甜蜜的花朵周围嗡嗡作响。

近义词：sweet, sugary, honeyed, luscious, delicious
反义词：bitter, sour, acrid

Etymology: From Latin *nectareus*, from nectar "nectar" + -eus.
