hospitable:/ˈhɑːspɪtəbl/ /hɒˈspɪtəbl/| adj.|好客的，殷勤的；适宜的|Friendly and welcoming to guests or strangers; (of an environment) pleasant and favorable for living in.|例句：The local people were very hospitable to us. 当地人对我们非常热情好客。|近义词：welcoming, friendly, genial, convivial, agreeable|反义词：inhospitable, unfriendly, unwelcoming|词性:形容词
hospitable:|词源: From Latin *hospitālis*, from hospes (“guest, host”).|
