forgivingly:/fərˈɡɪvɪŋli/
adv.
宽恕地，原谅地
in a forgiving way; readily pardoning offenses.
She smiled forgivingly at him. 她宽恕地对他笑了笑。
近义词:mercifully, compassionately, leniently
反义词:unforgivingly, relentlessly, vindictively
词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From forgive + -ing + -ly.  Forgive comes from Old English *forgiefan (attested only in forgiefenes "forgiveness"), from for- "away" + giefan "to give".  The suffix -ly forms adverbs from adjectives.
