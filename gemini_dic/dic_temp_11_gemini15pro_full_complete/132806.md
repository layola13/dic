noneccentric:/ˌnɒnɪkˈsentrɪk/
adjective
非偏心的，正常的
Not eccentric; conventional or normal.
Her style is noneccentric and classic. 她的风格很传统，并不古怪。
近义词: conventional, normal, ordinary, regular, typical, standard
反义词: eccentric, unconventional, unusual, odd, strange, peculiar
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + eccentric (from Greek "ekkentros" meaning "out of center")
