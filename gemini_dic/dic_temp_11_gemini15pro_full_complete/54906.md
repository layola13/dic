disposable: /dɪˈspoʊzəbl/ /dɪˈspəʊzəbl/
adjective|一次性的，可任意处理的|Designed to be thrown away after use.|例句：Disposable diapers are convenient for busy parents.  一次性尿布对忙碌的父母来说很方便。|近义词：expendable, throwaway|反义词：reusable, permanent|词性: adjective
disposable: /dɪˈspoʊzəbl/ /dɪˈspəʊzəbl/
noun|一次性用品|Something designed to be thrown away after use.|例句：Medical disposables, like syringes and gloves, are essential.  医疗一次性用品，如注射器和手套，是必不可少的。|近义词：consumable|反义词：reusable|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the late Middle English "disponere" meaning to dispose, arrange, or settle, derived from the Latin "disponere", from "dis-" (apart, asunder) + "ponere" (to place, put). The modern usage relating to discarding after use emerged in the 20th century.
