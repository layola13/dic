ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *graten, from Old French grater (“to scrape, scratch”), from Frankish *kratōn, from Proto-Germanic *kratōną (“to scratch, scrape”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰredʰ- (“to scrape, scratch”).
