bedew:/bɪˈdjuː/
verb
沾湿；使覆盖露水
To cover with drops of dew or as if with dew.
The grass was bedewed with tiny droplets.  草地上沾满了小水滴。
The spider's web was bedewed with morning mist.  蜘蛛网上沾满了晨雾。
近义词:moisten, dampen, drench
反义词:dry
词性:v.
