come to your senses:/kʌm tu jər ˈsɛnsɪz/ |phrase|恢复理智；醒悟过来|To start thinking and behaving reasonably after a period of folly or irrationality.|Example 1: He finally came to his senses and realized he was making a terrible mistake. 他终于醒悟过来，意识到自己犯了一个可怕的错误。

Example 2:  It's time you came to your senses and stopped spending so much money. 你该醒悟过来了，别再花那么多钱了。|近义词: regain your sanity, see reason, think clearly, wise up|反义词: lose your mind, go crazy, act irrationally|phrase
