outpocketing:/ˈaʊtˌpɑːkɪtɪŋ/
n.
外袋；突出部；膨胀
A part that swells or bulges outwards from a larger structure.
The small outpocketing of the intestine was identified as a diverticulum.  肠道的小外袋被确诊为憩室。
protrusion, bulge, swelling
indentation, depression, concavity
noun
