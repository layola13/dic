cosurety:/ˌkoʊˈʃʊrəti/ | n. |共同担保人；连带保证人 | A person who joins with another or others in becoming surety for a debt or obligation.  A joint surety. |  John and Jane acted as cosureties for the loan, meaning they were both equally responsible for repaying it if the borrower defaulted.  约翰和简作为这笔贷款的共同担保人，这意味着如果借款人违约，他们两人都有同等责任偿还。| synonym: joint surety, co-obligor | antonym:  debtor, borrower | noun

cosurety:/ˌkoʊˈʃʊrəti/ | n. |共同担保；连带保证 | The state or fact of being a cosurety. | The cosurety agreement clearly outlined the responsibilities of each party involved. 共同担保协议明确规定了每一方的责任。 | synonym: joint liability, shared responsibility | antonym: individual liability, sole responsibility | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From co- + surety.  "Co-" is a prefix meaning "jointly," "together," or "mutually." "Surety" comes from Old French "seurte" meaning "safety, security, certainty," ultimately from Latin "securus" meaning "free from care, safe, secure."
