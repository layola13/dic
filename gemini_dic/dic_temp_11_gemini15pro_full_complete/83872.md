half-acre:/ˌhɑːf ˈeɪkər/ (US) /ˌhɑːf ˈeɪkə(r)/ (UK)| n.|半英亩|A unit of area equal to half an acre (approximately 2,178 square feet or 0.2 hectares).  It's often used to describe the size of a plot of land, especially residential property.|They bought a half-acre lot to build their dream home. 他们买了一块半英亩的地来建造他们的梦想家园。

|The garden occupies a half-acre behind the house.  花园占地半英亩，位于房子后面。
|近义词:half an acre|反义词:(no direct antonym - consider using larger or smaller units of area like 'acre', 'square foot', 'hectare')|词性:noun
