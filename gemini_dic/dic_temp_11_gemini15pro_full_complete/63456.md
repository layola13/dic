entrepreneured:/ˌɑːntrəprəˈnɜːrd/
|v.|以企业家身份经营；创业|To operate as an entrepreneur; to start or run a business.
|She entrepreneured her way to success by starting a small online store.|她通过开设一家小型在线商店，以企业家身份创业成功。
|He entrepreneured several businesses before finding one that truly thrived.|他在找到一个真正蓬勃发展的企业之前，创办了几家企业。
|近义词：started, launched, initiated, pioneered|
|反义词：abandoned, ceased, discontinued|
|词性：动词|
