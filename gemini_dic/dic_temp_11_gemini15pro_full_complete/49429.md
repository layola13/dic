deloused:/diːˈlaʊst/
verb
除去虱子；清除害虫
freed from lice; rid of infestation.
The refugees were deloused at the border. 难民们在边境被除虱。
Synonyms: disinfected, cleansed, sanitized
Antonyms: infested
词性:动词
