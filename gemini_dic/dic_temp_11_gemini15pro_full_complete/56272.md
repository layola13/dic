dogfish:/ˈdɒɡfɪʃ/ (UK), /ˈdɔːɡfɪʃ/ (US)| n. |角鲨，狗鲨|A small shark, various species of which are found in many seas. They are sometimes used for food. |例句：The fishermen caught a dogfish in their nets. 渔民在他们的网里捕到了一条角鲨。

例句：Dogfish are often used in fish and chips. 角鲨经常被用来做炸鱼薯条。

近义词：shark, houndshark|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly larger fish like tuna or marlin if contrasting size) |词性: noun


dogfish:/ˈdɒɡfɪʃ/ (UK), /ˈdɔːɡfɪʃ/ (US)| n. |棘皮油鱼，白斑狗鱼|A type of small elongated scaleless marine fish of the family Squalidae, with two dorsal fins each preceded by a spine. |例句：The dogfish is known for its rough skin. 棘皮油鱼以其粗糙的皮肤而闻名。

例句：  Some species of dogfish can be found in deep waters.  一些种类的棘皮油鱼可以在深水中找到。


近义词：spiny dogfish, Squalus acanthias|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: noun
