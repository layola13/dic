overkeep:/ˌoʊvərˈkiːp/ | v. | 过度保存；过度饲养；过度维护 | To maintain or preserve in excess; to keep more of something than is necessary or desirable. |  He overkept the documents, filling his office with useless paperwork. 他过度保存了文件，办公室里堆满了无用的文书工作。

The farmer overkept his livestock, leading to overcrowding and disease. 农民过度饲养牲畜，导致过度拥挤和疾病。 | 近义词：overstock, hoard | 反义词： neglect, undermaintain | 词性: 动词
