borneol:/ˈbɔːrniɒl/ (US) /ˈbɔːniɒl/ (UK)| n. |冰片，龙脑|A white, crystalline, bicyclic terpene alcohol, C₁₀H₁₈O, with a minty camphoraceous odor, found in certain essential oils, especially from the borneol tree ( *Dryobalanops aromatica* ), and used in perfumery, medicine, and incense.|Borneol is used in some traditional Chinese medicines.|近义词：camphor|反义词:|名词
