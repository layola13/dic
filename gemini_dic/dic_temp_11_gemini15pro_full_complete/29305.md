casteless:/ˈkɑːstləs/ /ˈkæstləs/| adj.|无种姓的，不分种姓的|Without caste; not belonging to a caste system.  Refers to a society or individual not bound by the social hierarchy of a caste system.|India is striving to become a more casteless society. 印度正在努力成为一个更加不分种姓的社会.|Synonyms: classless, egalitarian|Antonyms: caste-based, hierarchical|adjective
