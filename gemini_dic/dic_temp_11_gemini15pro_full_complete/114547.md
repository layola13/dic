malarial:/məˈleriəl/| adj.|疟疾的，患疟疾的|Relating to, caused by, or infected with malaria.  Characterized by or suffering from malaria.|例句：He suffered from malarial fevers. 他患了疟疾。|例句：Malarial mosquitoes are a serious problem in this region. 疟蚊是该地区的严重问题。|近义词：aguish|反义词:|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *malarialis, from malaria ("bad air"), from mala ("bad") + aria ("air").
