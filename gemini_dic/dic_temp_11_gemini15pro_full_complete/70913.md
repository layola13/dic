firearms:/ˈfaɪərɑːrmz/
n.
火器；枪械
portable guns
The police found a cache of firearms and ammunition in the suspect's car. 警方在嫌疑人的车里发现了一批枪支弹药。

近义词:guns, weapons, ordnance, arms
反义词:(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑peace tools 和平工具)
词性:名词(复数)

ETYMOLOGY->
From "fire" + "arm," referring to weapons using gunpowder.  The word "fire" ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *fōr- and Proto-Indo-European *pūr- (to burn). "Arm" comes from Old French arme and Latin arma (weapons), related to the Proto-Indo-European root *ar- (to fit together).
