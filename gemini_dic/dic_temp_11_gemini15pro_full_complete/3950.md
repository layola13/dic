agroforestry:/ˈæɡroʊˈfɔːrɪstri/
n.|农林复合经营;农林业|A land use management system in which trees or shrubs are grown around or among crops or pastureland. This intentional combination of agriculture and forestry has diverse benefits, such as increased biodiversity, improved soil health, and reduced erosion.|Agroforestry systems can provide both food and timber. 农林复合系统可以同时提供食物和木材。|
近义词:permaculture, sustainable agriculture|
反义词:monoculture|
词性:noun
