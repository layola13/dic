hemihedrism:/ˌhɛmiˈhɛdrɪzəm/ | n. |半面象; 半面晶形 |The development of only half the planes required by the full symmetry of a crystal form, resulting in a crystal with lower symmetry.  A crystal form exhibiting hemihedrism.|

例句：
The hemihedrism of the crystal was evident under the microscope.
在显微镜下，晶体的半面象很明显。

例句：
The study of hemihedrism is crucial in crystallography.
半面象的研究在晶体学中至关重要。


|近义词： merohedrism |反义词: holohedrism |词性: noun|
