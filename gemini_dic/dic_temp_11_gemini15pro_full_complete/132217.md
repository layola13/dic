nondecadencies: /ˌnɒndɪˈkeɪdənsiz/ | n. | 非衰退，非堕落 |  The absence of decay or decline, especially moral or cultural decline.  This word is the plural form of "nondecadence." | The historian noted the nondecadencies of the Roman Republic during its early period.  历史学家注意到罗马共和国在其早期阶段的非衰退。| The museum showcased the art and architecture of several nondecadencies.  博物馆展示了几个非衰退时期的艺术和建筑。| 近义词:  flourishing, thriving, progress, advancement | 反义词: decadence, decline, decay, degeneration | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
"Non-" (prefix):  From Latin "non" meaning "not."
"Decadence": From French "décadence," ultimately from Latin "cadere" meaning "to fall."
