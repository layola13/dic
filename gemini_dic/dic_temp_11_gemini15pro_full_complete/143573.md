outhomer:/ˌaʊtˈhoʊmər/| n.|本垒打|A home run in baseball.|例句：He hit an outhomer in the bottom of the ninth. 他在第九局下半击出了一支本垒打。|例句：The outhomer tied the game.  这支本垒打追平了比分。|近义词：home run, dinger, round-tripper, four-bagger|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用out, strikeout等表示未能击球)|词性:n.

词源:  A compound of *out-* and *homer*, short for *home run*.
