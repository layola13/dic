essenism:/ˈɛsɪnɪzəm/
n.|艾赛尼主义；艾赛尼教派|A Jewish religious sect or school of thought of the Second Temple period, known for their strict piety, communal living, and belief in ritual purity.  They are believed by some scholars to have written the Dead Sea Scrolls.|The professor's lecture focused on the tenets of Essenism.|近义词:Jewish sectarianism|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
