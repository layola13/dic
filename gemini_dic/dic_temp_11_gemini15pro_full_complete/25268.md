buffie:/ˈbʌfi/| n.|浅黄色皮革, 浅黄色, 巴菲（女子名）|A strong, undyed leather, originally made from buffalo skin, now also from other animal hides.  Also, a pale yellowish color resembling this leather.  Can also be a female given name, short for Elizabeth.|She wore a buffie jacket. 她穿着一件浅黄色皮革夹克。|近义词：yellowish, tan, beige, Buffy|反义词:dark, black|名词
