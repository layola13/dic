dreams: /driːmz/ /driːmz/ | n. | 梦，梦想，理想 |A series of thoughts, images, and sensations occurring in a person's mind during sleep.  A cherished aspiration, ambition, or ideal.| Last night I had vivid dreams about flying. 昨晚我做了关于飞翔的栩栩如生的梦。|  She has dreams of becoming a doctor. 她梦想成为一名医生。 | synonyms: aspirations, fantasies, visions, hopes | antonyms: realities, nightmares | noun
