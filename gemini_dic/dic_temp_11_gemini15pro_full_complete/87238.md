heretically:/hɪˈrɛtɪkli/| adv.|异端地，异教地|In a way that goes against the accepted beliefs or practices of a particular religion or group.  Holding or expressing opinions that are contrary to established beliefs, especially in religious matters.|He spoke heretically about the church's teachings. 他异端地谈论教会的教义。|Synonyms: unorthodoxly, dissidently, blasphemously|Antonyms: orthodoxly, conventionally, piously|词性:副词
