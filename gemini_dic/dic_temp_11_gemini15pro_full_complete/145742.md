overcrowded: /ˌoʊvərˈkraʊdɪd/ | adj. | 过于拥挤的，人满为患的 | Filled with too many people or things. | The train was overcrowded and uncomfortable. 火车过于拥挤，很不舒服。| The beach was overcrowded with tourists. 海滩上挤满了游客。 | 近义词: congested, packed, jammed | 反义词: spacious, uncrowded, empty | 词性: 形容词

overcrowd: /ˌoʊvərˈkraʊd/ | vt. |  使过度拥挤，使人满为患 | To fill a space with too many people or things. |  Don't overcrowd the pan when frying vegetables.  炒蔬菜时不要把锅装得太满。 | They overcrowded the bus with passengers. 他们让公共汽车上挤满了乘客。 | 近义词: overfill, pack, cram | 反义词: thin out, depopulate | 词性: 动词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "too much") + crowd (noun meaning "a large number of people gathered closely together")
