nonoperative:/ˌnɒnˈɒpərətɪv/ | adj. | 非手术的；无效的；不工作的 | Not involving or requiring surgery; not functioning or effective. | The patient received nonoperative treatment for his knee injury.  这位病人接受了非手术治疗他的膝盖损伤。|  The old machinery is now nonoperative.  旧机器现在不能工作了。 | 近义词: nonsurgical, inoperative, inactive | 反义词: operative, surgical, active | 词性: adjective

词源:  From non- + operative.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "operative" refers to something that functions or works.
