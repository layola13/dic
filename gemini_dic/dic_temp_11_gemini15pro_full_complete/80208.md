godmothering:/ˈɡɒdˌmʌðərɪŋ/
n.|教母职责；像教母一样照顾|The act of being a godmother, or behaving in a way that is characteristic of a godmother, typically involving care, guidance, and support.|She enjoyed the godmothering of her friend's children, taking them on outings and offering advice. 她很喜欢像教母一样照顾朋友的孩子，带他们出去玩，给他们提建议。|近义词：mentoring, guiding, nurturing|反义词：neglecting|词性:名词

godmothering:/ˈɡɒdˌmʌðərɪŋ/
v.|担任教母；像教母一样照顾|Acting as a godmother or behaving like one.|She's been godmothering her niece since she was born. 她自从侄女出生以来就一直像教母一样照顾她。|近义词：mentoring, guiding, nurturing|反义词：neglecting|词性:动词
