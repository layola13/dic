over-reducing:/ˌəʊvər rɪˈdjuːsɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度还原，过度简化 | Reducing something too much, typically simplifying it to the point where it loses essential information or functionality.  This often applies to chemical reactions, data analysis, or explanations. | The model was over-reducing the complexity of the climate system, leading to inaccurate predictions.  这个模型过度简化了气候系统的复杂性，导致了不准确的预测。 | Over-simplifying, overgeneralizing | nuanced, comprehensive | adjective
