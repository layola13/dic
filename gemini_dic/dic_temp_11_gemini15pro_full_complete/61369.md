embitter:/ɪmˈbɪtər/ | v. | 使怨恨；使痛苦 | To cause someone to feel bitter or resentful. |
The loss of his job embittered him towards his former employer.  失去工作使他对前雇主怀恨在心。

The experience embittered her and made her distrustful of others.  这段经历使她痛苦，并使她不信任别人。

| 近义词:resent, anger, sour, poison | 反义词:sweeten, please, delight | Verb
