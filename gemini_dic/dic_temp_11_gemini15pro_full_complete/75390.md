froze:/froʊz/| v.|冻结，凝固|(past tense of freeze)  The lake froze over in winter. |例句：The water froze solid overnight. 水一夜之间冻成了固体。

The ground froze hard during the winter months.  地面在冬季的几个月里冻得很硬。|近义词：congealed, solidified, hardened|反义词:thawed, melted|词性:动词
