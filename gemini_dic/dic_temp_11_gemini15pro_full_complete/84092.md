half-ingenuousness:/ˌhɑːf ˌɪndʒɛnˈjuːəsnɪs/ |n.| 半坦率；半真诚；半直率 | A state or quality of being partly ingenuous, implying a mixture of innocence or candor with some degree of shrewdness or calculation. |  He spoke with a half-ingenuousness that made her wonder if he was hiding something. 他半真半假地说着，让她怀疑他是否隐瞒了什么。| near synonyms: partial candor, affected naivety, calculated innocence | antonyms: guile, cunning, duplicity | noun

ingenuous: /ɪnˈdʒɛnjuəs/ | adj. | 天真的；坦率的；真诚的 |  (of a person or action) innocent and unsuspecting. | She gave him an ingenuous smile. 她给了他一个天真的微笑。 | near synonyms: innocent, naive, artless, guileless, candid, frank, open, honest, sincere, truthful, straightforward, upfront, direct | antonyms: cunning, sly, artful, calculating, devious, wily | adjective
