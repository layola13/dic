acrid:/ˈækrɪd/| adj.|辛辣的，刺鼻的，刻薄的|Having a strong and unpleasant taste or smell that is sharp and bitter; (of a person's remarks or tone of voice) bitter and sharp.|The acrid smell of burning plastic filled the air. 燃烧塑料的刺鼻气味弥漫在空气中。|Synonyms: pungent, bitter, sharp, caustic, harsh|Antonyms: sweet, mild, pleasant|adjective

acrider:/ˈækrɪdər/| adj.|更辛辣的，更刺鼻的，更刻薄的|(comparative adjective of acrid) More sharp and bitter or unpleasant in taste or smell; more bitter and sharp in tone.|The smoke from the second fire was even acrider than the first. 第二场火产生的烟比第一场更刺鼻。|Synonyms: more pungent, more bitter, sharper, more caustic, harsher|Antonyms: sweeter, milder, more pleasant|adjective
