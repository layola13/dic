esotericism:/ˌɛsəˈtɛrɪsɪzəm/ | n. | 秘传；奥义；深奥难懂的事物 | The study of esotericism explores hidden knowledge and mystical practices. 对秘传的研究探索隐藏的知识和神秘的实践。|  Esotericism often involves secret rituals and teachings. 秘传通常涉及秘密仪式和教义。| 近义词: mysticism, occultism, | 反义词: exotericism | 词性: noun

esoteric:/ˌɛsəˈtɛrɪk/ | adj. | 秘传的；深奥的；难懂的 |  The esoteric teachings of the group were only revealed to initiates.  该团体的秘传教义只向新入教者透露。 |  He spoke in an esoteric language that few could understand.  他用一种很少有人能理解的深奥语言说话。| 近义词: arcane, obscure, cryptic | 反义词: exoteric, common, accessible | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *ésotérisme*, from Late Latin *ēsōtericus*, from Greek *esōterikos* "inner," from *esō* "within."
