interradiated:/ˌɪntərˈreɪdieɪtɪd/
adjective
相互照射的；互相辐射的
emitting rays of light that affect each other; illuminated by each other.
The two stars interradiated, creating a brilliant spectacle in the night sky. 两颗星星互相照耀，在夜空中创造了壮观的景象。
Near synonyms: interilluminated, interconnected (in a radiant way)
Antonyms: shaded, obscured
词性: adjective
