outplods:/ˌaʊtˈplɒdz/ | v. | 缓慢而费力地走;  | to walk slowly and heavily | The weary traveler outplods his way across the desert.  疲惫的旅行者缓慢而费力地穿过沙漠。 |  trudges, slogs | sprints, races | verb

Etymology:
"out-" (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + "plod" (to walk heavily or laboriously)
