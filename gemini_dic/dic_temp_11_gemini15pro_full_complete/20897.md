bleach:/bliːtʃ/ | n. v. |漂白剂，漂白；使变白|A chemical used to whiten clothes or hair, or to kill germs. To make something white or lighter in color.|She used bleach to remove the stain from the carpet. 她用漂白剂去掉了地毯上的污渍。|Synonyms: whitener, disinfectant|Antonyms: dye, darken|noun, verb

bleach:/bliːtʃ/ | v. |使褪色；使（颜色）变淡|To cause something to lose its color or become pale.|The sun has bleached the curtains. 太阳把窗帘晒褪色了。|Synonyms: fade, lighten|Antonyms: darken, color|verb
