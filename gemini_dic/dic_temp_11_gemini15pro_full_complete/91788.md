hyperexcitement:/ˌhaɪpərɪkˈsaɪtmənt/
n.|过度兴奋，极度兴奋|A state of extreme excitement.
The child's hyperexcitement before the birthday party was contagious.  孩子生日派对前的过度兴奋很有感染力。
The hyperexcitement of the fans before the concert was palpable.  演唱会前歌迷的极度兴奋是显而易见的。
近义词:overexcitement, frenzy, mania
反义词:calmness, tranquility, apathy
词性:名词

词源:
From hyper- + excitement.  由 hyper- + excitement 组成。
hyper- : prefix meaning "over, above, excessive"  前缀，意思是“过度，超过，极度”
excitement: n. a feeling of great enthusiasm and eagerness.  名词，一种极大的热情和渴望的感觉。
