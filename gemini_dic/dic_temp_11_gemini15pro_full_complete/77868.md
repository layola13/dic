genetic: /dʒəˈnɛtɪk/ (British) /dʒəˈnɛdɪk/ (American) | adj.| 遗传的；基因的| Relating to genes or heredity.| 例句：Genetic factors can influence a person's height. 遗传因素会影响一个人的身高。| 近义词：hereditary, inherited, inborn, congenital | 反义词: acquired | 词性: adjective

genetic: /dʒəˈnɛtɪk/ (British) /dʒəˈnɛdɪk/ (American) | n.| 基因| A unit of heredity which is transferred from a parent to offspring and is held to determine some characteristic of the offspring. (Usually used in plural form: genetics) | 例句：The study of genetics is crucial for understanding diseases. 基因研究对于理解疾病至关重要。| 近义词：gene, chromosome | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun (usually used in plural form)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *genētikos* "genitive, relating to origin," from *genesis* "origin, source, creation, generation," from *gignesthai* "to be born, become, happen."
