crossbows:/ˈkrɒsbəʊz/ (UK) /ˈkrɑːsbəʊz/ (US)| n. |十字弓，弩|A medieval weapon consisting of a bow fixed transversely on a stock, having a groove for the bolt and a trigger to release it.  It's more powerful and accurate than a regular bow, but slower to reload.|The hunter silently aimed his crossbow at the deer. 猎人默默地用十字弓瞄准了鹿。|Synonyms: arbalests|Antonyms: bows, slingshots|noun
