cwe:/ˌsiːˌdʌbəlˈjuːˈiː/ | n. | Common Weakness Enumeration (通用弱点枚举) | A community-developed list of software and hardware weakness types.  It serves as a common language for describing security flaws. | Example: The software vulnerability scanner identified several CWEs in the code.  该软件漏洞扫描器在代码中识别出几个CWE。 | Example:  Researchers use CWEs to categorize and analyze security vulnerabilities. 研究人员使用 CWE 对安全漏洞进行分类和分析。 | 近义词: vulnerability, flaw, weakness | 反义词: strength, security | 词性: noun
