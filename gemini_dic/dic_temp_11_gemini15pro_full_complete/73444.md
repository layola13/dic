foremostly:/ˈfɔːrmoʊstli/| adv.|首先，最重要的是|Primarily; most importantly. It emphasizes something as being the most important or primary consideration.|Foremostly, I want to thank my family for their support. 首先，我要感谢我的家人对我的支持。|近义词:primarily, chiefly, mainly, principally, first and foremost|反义词:lastly, finally|词性:副词
词源:foremost + -ly.  Foremost comes from Old English *foremest, superlative of fore.
