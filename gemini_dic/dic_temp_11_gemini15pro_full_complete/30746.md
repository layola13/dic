cesaro:/ˈtʃɛzəroʊ/ | adj. n. |切萨罗的; 切萨罗求和（法）| Relating to Ernesto Cesàro, an Italian mathematician, or his methods, particularly in the context of Cesàro summation, a method for assigning values to some infinite sums that do not converge in the usual sense.  Cesàro can also refer to the summation method itself (as a noun). | The Cesàro mean often provides a useful way to assign a value to divergent series. 切萨罗平均值通常提供一种有用的方法来为发散级数赋值。

The Cesàro sum of the Grandi's series (1 - 1 + 1 - 1 + ...) is 1/2. 格兰迪级数 (1 - 1 + 1 - 1 + ...) 的切萨罗和为 1/2。| 近义词: (For the summation method)  Riesz mean, Abel summation | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective, noun
