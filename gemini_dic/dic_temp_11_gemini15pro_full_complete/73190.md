force:/fɔːrs/ (British), /fɔːrs/ (American)| n. vt.|力量，武力，强迫| strength, power, compulsion; to compel, to oblige|
例句：The force of the wind blew the tree down. 风的力量把树刮倒了。
例句：He was forced to resign. 他被迫辞职。
近义词：strength, power, compel, oblige|反义词:weakness, impotence, persuade|词性:名词，动词

force:/fɔːrsɪz/ (plural for noun)|n.|军队，部队| troops, army |
例句：The air force is a vital part of national defense. 空军是国防的重要组成部分。
例句：The security forces were deployed to maintain order.  安全部队被部署以维持秩序。
近义词：troops, army, military|反义词:civilians|词性:名词
