dumps's:/dʌmps/| n.| 垃圾场;  (尤指计算机的)内存转储; (pl.) 抑郁, 沮丧| A dump is a place where rubbish is left. (dumps's is not standard English. The possessive form of "dumps" would normally be "dump's," referring to something belonging to a single dump.  "Dumps" itself can mean a state of depression, but "dumps's" is not grammatically correct in this sense.)  | The city built a new dump outside of town.  这个城市在城外建了一个新的垃圾场。 |近义词: junkyard, landfill, waste disposal site, (for the emotional meaning) blues, low spirits |反义词: (for physical dump) recycling center, (for emotional dump) elation, joy |词性: noun (primarily)
