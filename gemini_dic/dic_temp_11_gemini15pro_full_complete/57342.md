downwinder:/ˈdaʊnˌwɪndər/| n.|顺风人；下风处居民；受放射性尘埃影响的人|A person or community located downwind from a source of airborne pollutants, especially radioactive fallout.|例句：The downwinders suffered health problems from the nuclear tests. 核试验导致下风处居民健康问题。

例句：Many downwinders were exposed to radiation from the Nevada Test Site. 许多下风处居民受到内华达试验场的辐射。|近义词：victim, sufferer|反义词: upwinder (less common)|词性:noun
