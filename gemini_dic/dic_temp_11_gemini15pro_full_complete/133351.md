nonextenuatingly:/ˌnɒnɪkˈstɛnjuːeɪtɪŋli/
| adv. | 无可辩解地，毫不留情地 | without any mitigating circumstances; in a way that offers no excuse or justification |  He was nonextenuatingly condemned for his actions. 他因其行为受到了毫不留情的谴责。|  He nonextenuatingly criticized her performance. 他毫不留情地批评了她的表现。 | 近义词：unmitigatedly,  uncompromisingly, harshly | 反义词： leniently, mercifully | 词性:副词

词源:
nonextenuatingly  是由 "non-" (not) + "extenuating" + "-ly" (adverb suffix) 组成。 "extenuating" 来自拉丁语 "extenuare"，意思是 "to make thin, diminish, lessen".  因此，nonextenuatingly 的字面意思是 "not lessening or mitigating".
