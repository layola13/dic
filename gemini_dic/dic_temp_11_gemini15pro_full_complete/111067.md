listeningless:/ˈlɪsənɪŋlɪs/
adj.
不倾听的，不留心的
Not listening; inattentive.
He gave a listeningless ear to her complaints. 他对她的抱怨充耳不闻。
近义词:inattentive, unheeding, deaf
反义词:attentive, listening, heedful
词性:形容词

Etymology:
From listening + -less (suffix meaning "without").  The word "listen" comes from Old English *hlysnan, from Proto-Germanic *hlusnōną, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱlew- (“to hear”).
