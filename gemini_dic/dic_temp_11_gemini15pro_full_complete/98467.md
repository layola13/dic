inspographic: /ˌɪnspəˈɡræfɪk/
n.
灵感图；信息图表
A portmanteau of inspiration and infographic, referring to a visually appealing image, often found on social media, that provides inspiration or motivation, typically related to lifestyle, fashion, or design.
I found some great inspographics on Pinterest for my living room makeover. 我在Pinterest上找到了一些很棒的灵感图，用于改造我的客厅。
近义词: mood board, vision board, infographic
反义词:  (No direct antonym)
词性: noun
