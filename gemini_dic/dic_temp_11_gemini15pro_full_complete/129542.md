non-bolsheviks:/nɒnˈbɒlʃəvɪks/ | n. |非布尔什维克派；孟什维克派|  Those who were not members of the Bolshevik party, often including Mensheviks, Socialist Revolutionaries, and other opponents of Lenin's faction. |  After the October Revolution, many non-bolsheviks were persecuted. 十月革命后，许多非布尔什维克派人士遭到迫害。| Near synonyms: Mensheviks, oppositionists | Antonym: Bolsheviks | Noun

non-bolshevik:/nɒnˈbɒlʃəvɪk/ | adj. |非布尔什维克的| Relating to or characteristic of those who were not members of the Bolshevik party. | The non-bolshevik parties were gradually suppressed. 非布尔什维克的政党逐渐被压制。 | Near synonyms: anti-Bolshevik, non-communist | Antonym: Bolshevik | Adjective
