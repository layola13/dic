intertinge:/ˌɪntərˈtɪndʒ/
verb
使略带…颜色；使微微着色；轻微染色；使带有一点…气息
to slightly affect something with a feeling or quality
Her joy was intertinged with sorrow. 她的喜悦中夹杂着一丝悲伤。
