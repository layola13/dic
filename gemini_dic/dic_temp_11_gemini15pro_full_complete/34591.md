clearway:/ˈklɪəweɪ/ | n. | 清除路段，畅通无阻的路段 | A stretch of road, especially near an airport, where parking is prohibited to ensure free flow of traffic. |  The police kept the clearway free of parked cars. 警方确保清除路段上没有停放的车辆。| synonym: throughway, expressway | antonym: traffic jam, bottleneck | noun

clearway:/ˈklɪəweɪ/ | n. | （空中）航线，航道 | A designated route in the air for aircraft, ensuring safe and efficient movement. | The pilot requested a clearway for landing. 飞行员请求一条畅通的着陆航道。 | synonym: airway, flight path | antonym: no-fly zone | noun
