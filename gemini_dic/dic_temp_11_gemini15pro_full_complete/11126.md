archduchesses:/ˈɑːrtʃˌdʌtʃɪsɪz/
n.
女大公；大公爵夫人
The plural form of archduchess, referring to more than one wife or widow of an archduke, or a female holding the rank of archduchess in her own right.
The archduchesses attended the coronation. 女大公们参加了加冕典礼。

The archduchesses were known for their philanthropy.  这些女大公以她们的慈善事业而闻名。


近义词:princesses (a more general term), noblewomen
反义词:commoners
词性:noun (plural)
