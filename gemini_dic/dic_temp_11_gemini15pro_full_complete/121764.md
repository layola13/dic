misquote:/ˌmɪsˈkwoʊt/ | n. | 错误的引语 | An inaccurate repetition of someone's words. | The article was full of misquotes, which made it unreliable. 这篇文章充满了错误的引用，这使得它不可靠。| misrepresentation, distortion | accurate quotation, faithful representation | noun
