habit:/ˈhæbɪt/| n.|习惯，习性|A settled or regular tendency or practice, especially one that is hard to give up.|She has a habit of biting her nails when she's nervous. 她紧张的时候有咬指甲的习惯。|近义词:custom, practice, routine|反义词:infrequent, irregular|noun

habit:/ˈhæbɪt/| vt. (obsolete)|给…穿上衣服；居住于|Dress; clothe; reside in (archaic).|He was habituated in fine robes. 他穿着华丽的礼服。|近义词:clothe, dress, attire|反义词:undress, disrobe|verb (obsolete)
