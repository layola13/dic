formosa:/fɔrˈmoʊsə/ | adj. n. |美麗的；台灣(舊稱) | An archaic term for Taiwan, meaning "beautiful" in Portuguese.  It is derived from the Portuguese Ilha Formosa. | Example: The sailors were awestruck by the beauty of Formosa. 水手們被福爾摩沙的美麗所震懾。| Example: Formosa was once a major producer of sugar. 台灣(福爾摩沙)曾經是主要的糖產地。 | 近义词: beautiful, stunning, Taiwan | 反义词: ugly, hideous | 词性: adjective, noun
