misspenders:/ˌmɪsˈspɛndərz/
|n.|挥霍者，浪费者|People who spend money unwisely or wastefully are called misspenders.  挥霍金钱或浪费金钱的人被称为挥霍者。|The government was accused of being a misspender of public funds. 政府被指控浪费公共资金。|近义词：squanderers, wasters, profligates|反义词：savers, economizers|词性: 名词

词源:
From "misspend" + "-er".  "Mis-" is a prefix indicating wrong or bad. "Spend" comes from Old English "spendan," meaning "to expend, pay out."  "-er" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a person who performs the action of the verb. 由"misspend" + "-er"组成。"Mis-"是一个表示错误或不好的前缀。"Spend"来自古英语"spendan"，意思是“花费，支出”。"-er"是一个后缀，构成表示执行动词动作的人的名词。
