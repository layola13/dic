acknowledger:/əkˈnɒlɪdʒər/ |əˈknɑːlɪdʒər/| n. |承认者；确认者|A person who acknowledges something, especially a debt or receipt.|The lawyer acted as the acknowledger for the transaction.  律师担任了这笔交易的确认者。|Synonyms: recognizer, admitter, acceptor|Antonyms: denier, disputer, repudiator|noun
