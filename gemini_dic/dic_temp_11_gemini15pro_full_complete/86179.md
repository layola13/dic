heartwood:/ˈhɑːrtˌwʊd/ | n. | 心材 |The dense inner part of a tree trunk, yielding the hardest timber.  The heartwood is no longer living and provides structural support to the tree. | The craftsman preferred using heartwood for his furniture because of its durability.  工匠喜欢用心材做家具，因为它很耐用。| heart, core, duramen | sapwood | noun
