oestrus:/ˈiːstrəs/ (US) /ˈiːstrəs/ (UK)| n. |发情期；动情期|A recurring period of sexual receptivity and fertility in many female mammals; heat.|The lioness was in oestrus and attracting the attention of several males. 母狮正处于发情期，吸引了几只雄狮的注意。|Synonyms: heat, rut, season|Antonyms: anoestrus|Noun

oestrous:/ˈiːstrəs/ (US) /ˈiːstrəs/ (UK)| adj. |发情的；动情的|Relating to or characteristic of oestrus.|The oestrous cycle in cats can be quite frequent. 猫的发情周期可能相当频繁。|Synonyms: in heat|Antonyms: non-oestrous|Adjective
