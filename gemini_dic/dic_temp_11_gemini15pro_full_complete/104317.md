justos:/ˈhustos/| adj.|公正的，正义的，正直的|Just and fair; righteous.  Derived from Latin "iustus" meaning "just, lawful, rightful".  Originally referred to conforming to law or divine will, later broadened to encompass ethical and moral uprightness.|例句：They fought for a just cause. 他们为正义的事业而战。|近义词：righteous, equitable, fair, upright, honest, moral|反义词：unjust, unfair, wicked, corrupt, dishonest|词性:形容词
