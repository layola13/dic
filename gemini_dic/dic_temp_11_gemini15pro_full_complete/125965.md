musicomania:/ˌmjuːzɪkəˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 狂热的音乐爱好 | An excessive or abnormal love of music. | He suffers from musicomania, spending all his money on concerts and records. 他患有音乐狂热症，把所有的钱都花在了音乐会和唱片上。 | synonyms: melomania | antonyms: apathy towards music | noun
