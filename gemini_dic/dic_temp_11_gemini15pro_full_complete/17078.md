batista:/bɑːˈtiːstə/ | n. | 咖啡师；咖啡调配师 | A person who makes and serves coffee, especially in a coffee shop. |  例句：The batista expertly poured the latte art. 咖啡师熟练地倾倒着拿铁拉花。| 近义词：barista (Note: "barista" is the more common and preferred spelling) | 反义词: N/A | noun

batista:/bɑːˈtiːstə/ | n. | 巴蒂斯塔（古巴前领导人） | Fulgencio Batista y Zaldívar (1901-1973), a Cuban military officer and politician who served as the elected president of Cuba from 1940 to 1944 and as its U.S.-backed dictator from 1952 to 1959, before being overthrown during the Cuban Revolution. |  例句：Batista's regime was overthrown by Fidel Castro. 巴蒂斯塔政权被菲德尔·卡斯特罗推翻。 | 近义词：dictator, autocrat | 反义词: democrat, revolutionary | noun
