laxatively:/ˈlæksətɪvli/
adverb|通便地；泻药地|In a way that relieves constipation.  Having the effect of a laxative.|He smiled laxatively. 他露出了轻松的笑容 (比喻义，表示缓解紧张).|Synonyms: purgatively, cathartically|Antonyms: astringently, constipatingly|词性: 副词

laxative:/ˈlæksətɪv/
adjective|通便的；缓泻的|Having the effect of relieving constipation.|Prunes are known for their laxative properties. 西梅以其通便的特性而闻名。|Synonyms: purgative, aperient, cathartic|Antonyms: astringent, constipating|词性: 形容词

laxative:/ˈlæksətɪv/
noun|泻药|A medicine or agent for relieving constipation.|He took a laxative to relieve his constipation. 他服用了泻药来缓解便秘。|Synonyms: purgative, aperient, cathartic|Antonyms: antidiarrheal, constipating agent|词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *laxativus, from Latin laxare (“to loosen, open”), from laxus (“loose, slack”).
