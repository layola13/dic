overcensoring:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsərɪŋ/
noun
过度审查
The act of censoring something too much, often to the point of absurdity or harm.
The overcensoring of the media stifled free speech. 过度审查媒体扼杀了言论自由。
overcensorship, excessive censorship
undercensoring, lack of censorship
n.
