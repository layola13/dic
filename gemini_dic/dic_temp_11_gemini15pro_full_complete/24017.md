breechcloth:/ˈbriːtʃklɔːθ/ /ˈbriːtʃklɒθ/
n.
缠腰布；遮羞布
A garment worn around the hips, covering the genitals.  It is typically a single piece of cloth or animal skin.
The tribesmen wore simple breechcloths made of animal hide. 部落男子穿着简单的兽皮缠腰布。

The ancient warrior's only clothing was a leather breechcloth.  古代战士唯一的衣服是一块皮革缠腰布。

loincloth, waistcloth
(No clear antonym)
noun
