exculpatory:/ɪkˈskʌlpətɔːri/ /ɛkˈskʌlpəˌtɔri/
adjective
辩解的，开脱的，免罪的
Tending or serving to clear from alleged fault or guilt.  Showing that someone is not guilty of wrongdoing.
The defendant offered exculpatory evidence in an attempt to clear his name.  被告提供了开脱罪责的证据，试图证明自己的清白。
The lawyer sought exculpatory evidence to prove her client's innocence. 律师寻找有利的证据来证明她委托人的清白。
近义词:absolving, clearing, vindicating, exonerating
反义词:incriminating, accusatory
