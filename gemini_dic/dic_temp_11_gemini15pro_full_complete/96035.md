indebt:/ɪnˈdɛt/ | adj. |负债的，受恩惠的|Owing gratitude or recognition to another, especially for help or support; obliged.  Placed in a position of obligation because of a favor, service, or monetary loan.|例句：I am deeply indebted to my parents for their support. 我深深地感激父母的支持。|例句：The company is heavily indebted to the bank. 这家公司欠银行很多钱。|近义词：obligated, beholden, grateful|反义词：unobligated, free|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From in- + debt.  The "in-" prefix here carries the sense of "in a state of," similar to "in bloom" or "in love."  Thus, "indebted" literally means "in a state of debt."
