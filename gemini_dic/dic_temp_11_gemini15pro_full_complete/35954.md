code-naming:/ˈkoʊd neɪmɪŋ/ |n.|代码命名，代号命名|The process of assigning code names to projects, operations, or individuals, often for secrecy or brevity.  This is common in military, intelligence, and corporate settings.|The code-naming of the new product was "Project Phoenix".  新产品的代号命名为“凤凰计划”。|synonyms:  labeling, designation, classification|antonyms: disclosure, revelation|noun

code-naming:/ˈkoʊd neɪmɪŋ/ |adj.|代码命名的，代号命名的|Used to describe something that is related to the process of code-naming.|The code-naming conventions are strict. 代码命名约定很严格。|synonyms: classified, confidential, secret|antonyms: public, open, revealed|adjective
