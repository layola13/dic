overaggravate: /ˌoʊvərˈæɡrəveɪt/
|v.| 过分加重，过分夸大| To aggravate to an excessive degree; to exaggerate the seriousness of. | He tends to overaggravate his injuries to get sympathy. 他往往夸大自己的伤势以博取同情。|Synonym: exaggerate, overstate, overemphasize| Antonym: downplay, minimize, understate| Verb

overaggravate: /ˌoʊvərˈæɡrəveɪt/
|v.|使恼怒，激怒|To irritate or annoy to an excessive degree.|Her constant complaining overaggravated him. 她不断的抱怨让他很恼火。|Synonym: exasperate, irritate, annoy| Antonym: soothe, calm, placate| Verb
