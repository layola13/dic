billow:/ˈbɪloʊ/ (US) /ˈbɪləʊ/ (UK)| n. v.| 巨浪；波涛汹涌；像波浪般起伏；鼓起 |A large wave or surge, or something that swells and rises like a wave.  It can also be used as a verb meaning to swell or surge outwards.|The smoke billowed from the burning building. 浓烟从燃烧的建筑物中滚滚而出。|近义词：surge, swell, wave, bulge, balloon|反义词: deflate, shrink, recede|noun, verb
