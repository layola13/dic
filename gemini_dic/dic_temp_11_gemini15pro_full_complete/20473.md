bittour: /ˈbɪt.tʊr/
noun
比特之旅; 数字之旅; (可能指与比特币或加密货币相关的旅行或体验)
A journey or experience related to Bitcoin or cryptocurrency, perhaps involving travel or online exploration.

Example: He embarked on a bittour, exploring different countries that accept Bitcoin as payment. 他开始了一场比特之旅，探索不同接受比特币作为支付方式的国家。

Example: Her bittour involved attending cryptocurrency conferences and meeting key figures in the industry. 她的比特之旅包括参加加密货币会议和会见业内关键人物。

Synonym: crypto journey, digital currency tour
Antonym: physical journey, traditional travel
词性: 名词
