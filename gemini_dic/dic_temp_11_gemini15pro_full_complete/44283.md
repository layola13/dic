crony:/ˈkroʊni/| n.|亲信，密友，狐朋狗友|A close friend or companion, especially one who frequents taverns or joins in noisy revelry; a longtime close friend or associate, especially of someone powerful (such as a politician).|He was surrounded by his cronies at the bar. 他在酒吧里被他的狐朋狗友包围着。

The mayor appointed several of his cronies to city jobs. 市长任命了他的几个亲信担任市政工作。|近义词：friend, pal, chum, associate, companion|反义词：enemy, foe, opponent|词性:noun
