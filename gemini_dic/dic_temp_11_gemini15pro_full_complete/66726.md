exorbitant:/ɪɡˈzɔːrbɪtənt/| adj.|过高的，过分的，离谱的|Going beyond reasonable or proper limits; excessive.  Relating to something, especially a price or amount charged, that is unreasonably high.|The prices at that resort are exorbitant.  那个度假胜地的价格高得离谱。|近义词：excessive, outrageous, unreasonable, preposterous|反义词:reasonable, moderate, fair, cheap|adjective
