amin:/ˈeɪmɪn/, /ˈɑːmiːn/ | n. | 阿门; 安拉 | An expression used in various religions at the end of a prayer or hymn, expressing affirmation or assent.  Also spelled "Ameen".  Derived from Hebrew and Aramaic. | Example: The congregation responded with a resounding "Amen."  会众以响亮的“阿门”回应。 | Example:  He ended his prayer with a quiet "Amen". 他以一个轻声的“阿门”结束了他的祈祷。| Synonym: so be it, verily | Antonym:  (No direct antonym exists as it is an affirmation.  Perhaps a refutation or denial could be considered an opposite concept.) | noun
