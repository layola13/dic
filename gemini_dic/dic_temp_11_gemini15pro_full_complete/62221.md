encrinite:/ˈɛŋkrɪˌnaɪt/| n. |海百合，海百合茎化石|A fossil of a crinoid, a marine animal related to starfish and sea urchins.  These fossils often resemble small discs stacked in a column.|The museum displayed a beautiful collection of encrinites from the local quarry.|近义词：crinoid, stone lily|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *encrine* (modern *encrine*), from Ancient Greek *ἐγκρινής* (*enkrinḗs*), meaning "lily-like". The word ultimately derives from *ἐν* (*en*), meaning "in", and *κρίνον* (*krínon*), meaning "lily".
