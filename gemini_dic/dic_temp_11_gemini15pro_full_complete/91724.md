hyperdeifies:/ˌhaɪpərˈdiːɪfaɪz/ | v. | 过度神化，极度神化 | To treat or regard someone or something as a god to an excessive degree, beyond normal deification. |  The cult hyperdeifies its leader, attributing supernatural powers to him.  这个邪教过度神化了它的领袖，认为他拥有超自然的力量。

The marketing campaign hyperdeifies the new product, making it seem like a miracle cure.  这场营销活动过度神化了新产品，让它看起来像是一种神奇的疗法。 | 近义词: idolizes, exalts, glorifies | 反义词:  denigrates, belittles, disparages | 词性: 动词
