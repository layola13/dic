inextinguishable:/ˌɪnɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbl/
adjective
不可磨灭的，无法扑灭的
Not able to be extinguished; unquenchable.
His inextinguishable enthusiasm inspired everyone around him. 他不灭的热情鼓舞了周围的每一个人。
The inextinguishable fire raged for days. 无法扑灭的大火持续了数天。
synonyms: unquenchable, insatiable, enduring, persistent
antonyms: extinguishable, quenchable, temporary, fleeting
词性: 形容词
