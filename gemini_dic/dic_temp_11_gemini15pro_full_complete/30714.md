cerulein: /sɪˈruːliɪn/ | n. | 蓝绿素；蛙皮肽 | A polypeptide similar in structure and action to gastrin and cholecystokinin, found in the skin of certain frogs. | Cerulein is used in scientific research to study pancreatic function. 蓝绿素用于科学研究以研究胰腺功能。| synonyms: caerulein | antonyms: N/A | noun


cerulean: /səˈruːliən/ | adj. | 天蓝色的；蔚蓝的 | Deep sky blue. | The cerulean sky was a breathtaking sight. 蔚蓝的天空令人叹为观止。| synonyms: azure, sky-blue, sapphire | antonyms: murky, dark | adjective
