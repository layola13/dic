overmourn:/ˌōvərˈmôrn/| v. |过度哀悼，过分悲痛|To mourn excessively or for too long a period.|She overmourned the death of her cat for months.|近义词：grieve excessively, lament overly|反义词: celebrate, rejoice|词性:动词
词源: From over- + mourn.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "too much". "Mourn" comes from Old French "morne," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*murnan," meaning "to care, worry, be anxious."
