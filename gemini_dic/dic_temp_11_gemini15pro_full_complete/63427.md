entrappingly:/ɪnˈtræpɪŋli/
adverb
诱捕地；陷害地
in a way that traps someone or something
Her words were entrappingly sweet, hiding a sinister motive. 她的言语甜得像陷阱，隐藏着险恶的用心。
The lawyer entrappingly questioned the witness, hoping for a slip-up. 律师设下圈套盘问证人，希望能让他说漏嘴。
近义词:deceitfully, insidiously, cunningly
反义词:honestly, openly, frankly
词性:adverb

词源:
From entrap + -ing + -ly.  "Entrap" comes from the Old French "entraper," meaning "to catch in a trap."
