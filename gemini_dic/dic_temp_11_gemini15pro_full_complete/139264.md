obligingness:/əˈblaɪdʒɪŋnɪs/ | n. |乐于助人;  亲切;  殷勤; |The quality of being happy and willing to help someone.|  Her obligingness made her a favorite among her colleagues.  她的乐于助人使她成为同事们的最爱。 | synonyms: helpfulness, kindness, courtesy, amiability, generosity | antonyms: unhelpfulness, rudeness, unkindness, hostility | noun

obliging:/əˈblaɪdʒɪŋ/ | adj. |乐于助人的;  亲切的;  殷勤的; | willing to do a service or kindness; helpful and accommodating. | He was very obliging and offered to carry my bags. 他非常乐于助人，主动提出帮我提行李。| synonyms: helpful, accommodating, kind, considerate, courteous | antonyms: unhelpful, unaccommodating, unkind, inconsiderate, discourteous | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *obligeance*, from Old French obligeance, from obliger (“to oblige”), from Latin obligāre (“to bind, oblige”), from ob- (“towards”) + ligāre (“to bind”).
