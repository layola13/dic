nonapproachable:/ˌnɒnəˈprəʊtʃəbl/ | adj. |难以接近的，冷淡的 | Not easy to talk to; unfriendly or distant.  |  She seemed nonapproachable, so I didn't try to talk to her. 她看起来难以接近，所以我没有尝试和她说话。| Example: The CEO was known for his nonapproachable demeanor, making employees hesitant to approach him with concerns.  这位CEO以其难以接近的举止而闻名，这让员工们不敢向他提出自己的担忧。 | Synonyms: aloof, distant, unfriendly, unapproachable, intimidating, forbidding, cold, stand-offish | Antonyms: approachable, friendly, warm, welcoming, accessible | Word type: Adjective
