fortnightly: /ˈfɔːrtˌnaɪtli/ (UK) /ˈfɔːrtˌnaɪtli/ (US)| adj. adv.|两周一次的，每两周一次地|Happening or produced every two weeks.|The magazine is published fortnightly. 这本杂志两周发行一次。|近义词：biweekly, semimonthly|反义词: weekly, monthly|词性: 形容词/副词
fortnightly: /ˈfɔːrtˌnaɪtli/ (UK) /ˈfɔːrtˌnaɪtli/ (US)| n.|两周一次的出版物|A publication issued every two weeks.|I subscribe to a fortnightly gardening magazine. 我订阅了一份两周一次的园艺杂志。|近义词：biweekly|反义词: weekly, monthly|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From "fourteen nights," reflecting the original meaning of a period of two weeks.  Old English *fēowertīene nihta*, from fēowertīene "fourteen" + niht "night."
