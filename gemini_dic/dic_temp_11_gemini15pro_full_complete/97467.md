ingemination:/ɪnˌdʒɛmɪˈneɪʃən/
n.
重复，重叠（尤指辅音）
the doubling or repetition of a consonant or vowel sound in a word.
The word "better" demonstrates gemination of the consonant "t".  单词"better"展现了辅音"t"的重复。
Repetition, duplication, reduplication
Weakening, reduction
