anticipators:/ænˈtɪsɪpeɪtərz/
n.
预料者，预期者
People who anticipate something; those who foresee or expect something.
The anticipators of the new technology were excited about its potential.  新技术的预期者对其潜力感到兴奋。
近义词：expectants, foreseers
反义词：doubters, skeptics
词性：名词


anticipate:/ænˈtɪsɪpeɪt/
v.
预期，预料
To expect or predict.
We anticipate a large crowd at the concert. 我们预计演唱会上会有很多人。
I anticipate finishing the project by next week. 我预计下周完成这个项目。
近义词：expect, foresee, predict
反义词：doubt
词性：动词
