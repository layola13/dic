ooecium:/oʊˈiːsiəm/
|n.|卵鞘，卵胞，生殖体|A brood pouch or capsule in which eggs are produced, incubated, and sometimes hatched, as in bryozoans.|The bryozoan's ooecium protects the developing larvae.  苔藓虫的卵鞘保护着发育中的幼虫。|Synonyms: brood pouch, capsule, gonangium|Antonyms: N/A
