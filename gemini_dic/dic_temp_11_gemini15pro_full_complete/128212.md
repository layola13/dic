neurobiotaxis:/ˌnʊəroʊˌbaɪoʊˈtæksɪs/| n. |神经生物趋向性|The tendency of nerve cell bodies to move toward the source of stimulation.|The researchers observed neurobiotaxis in the developing nervous system of the fruit fly. 研究人员在果蝇发育中的神经系统中观察到了神经生物趋向性。|Synonyms: neuronal migration, chemotaxis|Antonyms: None|noun

Etymology:  neuro- (Greek: nerve) + bio- (Greek: life) + -taxis (Greek: arrangement, order, from *tag-ti-s, from the root *tag- "to touch, handle")
