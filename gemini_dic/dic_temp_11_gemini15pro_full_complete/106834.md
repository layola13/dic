laboriously:/ləˈbɔːriəsli/
adverb|辛苦地，费力地|in a way that requires a lot of effort and time; with difficulty|例句：He labored laboriously on the project for months. 他在这个项目上辛辛苦苦地工作了几个月。|近义词：strenuously, arduously, painstakingly|反义词：easily, effortlessly|词性: 副词

laboriously:/ləˈbɔːriəsli/
adverb|吃力地|with difficulty and much effort|例句：She climbed laboriously up the steep hill. 她吃力地爬上了陡峭的山坡。|近义词：with difficulty, with effort, with exertion|反义词：easily, effortlessly|词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *laboriōsus, from Latin labor "work, toil, hardship" + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives with the sense ‘full of, prone to’).
