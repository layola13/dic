dirigibility:/dɪˌrɪdʒəˈbɪləti/ /ˌdaɪrɪdʒəˈbɪləti/| n.|可操纵性， dirigibleness|The dirigibility of the new airship was tested in a series of flights.  这艘新飞艇的可操纵性在一系列飞行中得到了测试。|The aircraft's dirigibility was crucial for navigating the narrow canyon. 这架飞机的可操纵性对于在狭窄的峡谷中航行至关重要。|近义词：controllability, maneuverability|反义词：unwieldiness, intractability|词性:noun

词源: From French *dirigibilité*, from diriger (“to direct”) (from Latin dirigō, present active infinitive of dirigere (“to direct, manage, arrange, steer”)) + -ibilité, from Latin -ibilitas, from habilis (“able”).
