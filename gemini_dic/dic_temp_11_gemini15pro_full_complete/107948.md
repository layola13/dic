noun
喉气管切开术
A surgical incision into the larynx and trachea.  The creation of an opening into the larynx and trachea through the neck, performed to relieve upper airway obstruction.
The patient underwent an emergency laryngotracheotomy after experiencing severe swelling in his throat.  病人喉咙严重肿胀后，接受了紧急喉气管切开术。
