ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *disputer* (Modern French *disputer*), from Latin *disputare* "to discuss, examine, argue, debate," literally "to reckon apart, consider separately," from *dis-* "apart" + *putare* "to reckon, think, believe, suppose."
