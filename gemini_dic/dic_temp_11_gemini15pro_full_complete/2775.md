adumbrative:/ˌædʌmˈbreɪtɪv/| adj.|预示的；略述的；模糊的|Giving a slight indication or vague suggestion of something to come; foreshadowing.|The dark clouds were adumbrative of the coming storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨即将到来。|Synonyms: foreshadowing, prefigurative, suggestive, indicative, ominous|Antonyms: explicit, clear, definite, revealing|adjective
