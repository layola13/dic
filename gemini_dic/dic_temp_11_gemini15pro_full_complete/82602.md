guardrail:/ˈɡɑːrdreɪl/ /ˈɡɑːrdˌreɪl/| n.|护栏，栏杆|A barrier erected along the edge of a road, bridge, or other dangerous area to prevent people or vehicles from falling over the edge.|The guardrail prevented the car from plunging down the cliff.  护栏阻止了汽车坠落悬崖。|近义词：barrier, railing, fence|反义词: (no direct antonym)|noun
