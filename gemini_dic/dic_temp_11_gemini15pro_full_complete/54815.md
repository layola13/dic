dispersed:/dɪˈspɜːrst/ | adj. | 分散的，散布的 | distributed or spread over a wide area | The seeds were dispersed by the wind. 种子被风吹散了。 | 近义词: scattered, disseminated, spread out | 反义词: concentrated, gathered, clustered | 词性:形容词

disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/ | v. | 分散，散布，驱散 | distribute or spread over a wide area | The police dispersed the crowd. 警察驱散了人群。| 近义词: scatter, disseminate, break up | 反义词: gather, collect, assemble | 词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dispersus, past participle of dispergere "to scatter," from dis- "apart" + spargere "to strew, scatter."
