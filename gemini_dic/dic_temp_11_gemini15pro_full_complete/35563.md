coalitioners:/ˌkoʊəˈlɪʃənərz/
n.
联盟成员，联合政府成员
Members of a coalition, especially a political one.
The coalitioners agreed on a common platform despite their differing ideologies. 联合政府成员尽管意识形态不同，但还是在一个共同的平台上达成了一致。
synonyms: allies, partners, collaborators
antonyms: opponents, adversaries
noun
