furus:/ˈfʊərəs/| n.| 沟渠，水道|A ditch or channel for carrying off water, especially one used for irrigation.|The furus carried water from the river to the fields. 沟渠将水从河流引到田地。|近义词：ditch, channel, canal|反义词:ridge, mound|词性:名词


Etymology: From Latin *fūrus, related to *fuō (“to flow”).  Cognate with Old English fōr (“furrow, track”).
