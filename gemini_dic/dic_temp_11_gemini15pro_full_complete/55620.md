diswitted:/dɪsˈwɪtɪd/
adj.
愚蠢的，笨的，缺乏智慧的
Lacking wit or good sense; foolish.
He made a diswitted remark that offended everyone in the room. 他说了一句愚蠢的话，冒犯了房间里的所有人。
近义词：foolish, stupid, silly, witless, unwise
反义词：clever, intelligent, wise, astute, shrewd
词性: 形容词
