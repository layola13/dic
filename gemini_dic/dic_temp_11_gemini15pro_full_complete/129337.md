noisier:/ˈnɔɪziər (US), ˈnɔɪzɪə(r) (UK)| adj.|更吵闹的, 噪音更大的|Comparative form of noisy: producing a lot of noise. |The city is much noisier than the countryside. 城市比乡村吵闹得多。

The construction site next door is getting noisier every day. 隔壁的建筑工地一天比一天吵。

|近义词：louder, more boisterous, more clamorous, more rambunctious|反义词:quieter, calmer, more peaceful|词性:形容词
