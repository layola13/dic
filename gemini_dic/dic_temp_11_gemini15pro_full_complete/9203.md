antiorganizations:/ˌæntiˌɔːrɡənɪˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.|反组织；反体制（通常指对传统等级制度或官僚机构的抵制）|Groups or movements characterized by their opposition to traditional hierarchical or bureaucratic organizations, often favoring decentralized and non-hierarchical structures.  They prioritize individual autonomy and resist centralized control.|Example: The rise of antiorganizations reflects a growing distrust of established institutions. 反组织的兴起反映了人们对现有机构日益增长的不信任。

Example:  Many antiorganizations advocate for self-governance and participatory decision-making. 许多反组织倡导自治和参与式决策。
|近义词:decentralized networks, grassroots movements, autonomous collectives|反义词:hierarchical organizations, bureaucracies, traditional institutions|词性:名词
