communication:/kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 沟通，交流；通讯；信息 | the process by which information is exchanged between individuals through a common system of symbols, signs, or behavior |  Effective communication is key to a successful relationship. 有效的沟通是成功关系的关键。 | correspondence, interaction, exchange |misunderstanding, silence | noun

communication:/kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən/ |n.| 传达，传递 | the imparting or exchanging of information or news | The communication of the bad news was handled poorly.  坏消息的传达处理得很糟糕。 | transmission, conveyance | withholding, suppression | noun
