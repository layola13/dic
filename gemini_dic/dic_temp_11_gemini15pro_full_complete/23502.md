braided:/ˈbreɪdɪd/| adj.| 编织的，辫状的| interwoven or plaited together; made by braiding. | Her hair was braided into a long plait. 她的头发编成了一条长辫。|  The rug was made of braided strips of fabric. 这块地毯是由编织的布条制成的。|  interwoven, plaited, intertwined | loose, unbraided| adjective
