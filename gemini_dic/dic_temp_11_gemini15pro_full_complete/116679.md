matura:/məˈtjʊərə/ (UK), /məˈtʊrə/ (US)| n.| 高中毕业考试；毕业考试文凭；成熟；(尤指女孩的)性成熟|A final examination in secondary school, qualifying successful candidates for higher education, especially in some European countries.  The certificate awarded on passing this examination.  The state or quality of being mature, especially sexually.|例句：She passed her matura with flying colors. 她以优异的成绩通过了高中毕业考试。

例句：He received his matura certificate last week. 他上周收到了高中毕业证书。

近义词：exam, examination, finals, school-leaving exam, Abitur (German), baccalauréat (French)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
