ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *don, *doen, from Old English dōn (“to do, make, perform, cause, put”), from Proto-Germanic *dōną (“to do”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeh₁- (“to put, place, set”). Cognate with West Frisian dwaan, Dutch doen, German tun, Danish gøre.
