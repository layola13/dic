fallacious:/fəˈleɪʃəs/
adjective
谬误的，错误的
Containing or based on a fallacy; misleading.
例句：His argument was based on fallacious reasoning. 他的论证是基于错误的推理。

例句：The fallacious advertisement misled many consumers.  这个虚假的广告误导了许多消费者。

近义词：false, misleading, deceptive, erroneous, spurious, untrue, invalid, illogical, unsound
反义词：true, valid, sound, logical, correct, accurate

词性
adjective
