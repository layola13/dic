debouche:/dɪˈbaʊtʃ/ /deɪˈbuːʃ/| v. n.|流出，涌出; 出口，河口|To emerge from a confined space, especially of troops or a liquid.  (Especially of a river or road) to flow or lead into a larger body or area. |The river debouches into the sea. 这条河流入大海。|近义词：emerge, issue, flow, discharge, empty |反义词:enter, ingress|verb
debouche:/dɪˈbaʊtʃ/ /deɪˈbuːʃ/| n.|出口，河口|An outlet or exit.|The debouche of the tunnel was hidden by trees. 隧道的出口被树木遮蔽了。|近义词：outlet, exit, mouth, opening |反义词:entrance, inlet|noun
