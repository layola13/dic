boulevarded:/ˈbuːləvɑːrdɪd/
adjective
林荫大道化的；有林荫大道的
lined with boulevards; having boulevards
The boulevarded streets of Paris are famous. 巴黎林荫大道化的街道很有名。
The newly boulevarded avenue gave the city a more modern feel. 新建的林荫大道使这座城市更加现代化。
Synonyms: tree-lined, landscaped
Antonyms: barren, desolate
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From boulevard + -ed.  "Boulevard" comes from Middle Dutch  bolwerc, meaning "bulwark" or "rampart."  Over time, the meaning shifted to refer to the broad, often tree-lined avenues that replaced city fortifications.
