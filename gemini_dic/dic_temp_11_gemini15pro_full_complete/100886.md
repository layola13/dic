intrust:/ɪnˈtrʌst/ | vt. | 委托；信托；托付 | To give something to someone to take care of or be responsible for. | I entrusted my lawyer with the task of preparing my will. 我委托我的律师准备我的遗嘱。 |  He entrusted the money to his friend. 他把钱托付给了他的朋友。 | 近义词: confide, commit, assign | 反义词: withhold, retain | 词性: verb
词源: From in- + trust.  "Trust" comes from Old Norse "traust," meaning "confidence, support."
