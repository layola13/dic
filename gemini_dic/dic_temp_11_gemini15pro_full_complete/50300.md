deplant:/diːˈplænt/ | v. | 移植；迁移；使离开家园 | To remove a plant from the ground or from a pot. To remove someone from their accustomed place or surroundings, often by force. |  The gardener carefully deplanted the rose bush. 园丁小心地把玫瑰丛移植了。 The war deplanted thousands of refugees. 战争使成千上万的难民背井离乡。| synonyms: uproot, displace, remove, transplant | antonyms: plant, settle, establish | verb
