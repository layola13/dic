discursory:/dɪˈskɜːrsəri/
adj.
粗略的，草率的，散漫的
passing quickly from one subject to another without careful attention to detail; superficial.
The lawyer gave a discursory glance at the document. 律师粗略地瞥了一眼文件。

discursory:/dɪˈskɜːrsəri/
adj.
漫谈式的，离题的
covering a wide range of topics in a rambling way.
His discursory remarks touched on several subjects but never went into depth. 他漫谈式的发言涉及了几个主题，但从未深入探讨。

Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin discursorius ‘running to and fro’, from the verb discurrere (see discourse).
词源：中古英语晚期：源自晚期拉丁语 discursorius “来回跑”，来自动词 discurrere（参见 discourse）。


Synonym: superficial, cursory, perfunctory, hasty, hurried, brief, fleeting, shallow, sketchy, desultory
近义词：表面的，粗略的，敷衍的，仓促的，匆忙的，简短的，飞逝的，肤浅的，粗略的，散漫的

Antonym: thorough, detailed, comprehensive, in-depth, meticulous, painstaking, exhaustive
反义词：彻底的，详细的，全面的，深入的，细致的，艰苦的，详尽的
