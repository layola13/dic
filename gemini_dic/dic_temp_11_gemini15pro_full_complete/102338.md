iwi:/ˈiːwiː/
n.
tribe, people, nation, bone
A Māori word referring to a tribe or extended kinship group.
The iwi gathered for their annual meeting.  这个部落聚集起来参加他们的年度会议。
The local iwi has strong connections to the land. 当地的部落与这片土地有着紧密的联系。
synonyms: tribe, clan, people, nation, kin, family
antonyms: individual, outsider, foreigner
