argonauta:/ˌɑːrɡəˈnɔːtə/ | n. |船蛸；纸鹦鹉螺；漂浮者|A genus of pelagic octopuses, the females of which secrete a paper-thin, spiraled shell.  The name comes from Greek mythology, referring to the Argonauts who sailed with Jason in search of the Golden Fleece. | The argonaut, unlike other cephalopods, builds a delicate shell. 船蛸不像其他头足类动物，它会建造一个精致的壳。

The paper nautilus, or argonaut, is not a true nautilus but an octopus.  纸鹦鹉螺，或船蛸，并不是真正的鹦鹉螺，而是一种章鱼。| 近义词: paper nautilus, | 反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a specific species) | 词性: noun
