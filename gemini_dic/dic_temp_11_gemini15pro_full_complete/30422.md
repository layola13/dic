centralises:/ˈsɛntrəlaɪzɪz/
verb
集中；中央集权
To bring something under the control of a central authority.
The government centralised the education system. 政府将教育系统集中管理。
The company centralised its operations in London. 公司将其业务集中在伦敦。
centralize, consolidate, unify
decentralize, distribute, disperse
v.
