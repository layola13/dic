intervenes:/ˌɪntərˈviːnz/
verb
干预，介入，插手，调停
comes between so as to prevent or alter a result or course of events.
例句：The central bank intervened in the currency markets. 中央银行干预了货币市场。

例句：She intervened in the argument between her two sons. 她介入到两个儿子之间的争论中。

近义词：interfere, mediate, arbitrate
反义词：ignore, disregard
词性：v.
