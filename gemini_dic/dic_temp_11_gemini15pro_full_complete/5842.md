amazedly:/əˈmeɪzɪdli/
adverb
惊讶地，惊奇地
In a manner showing great surprise or wonder.
She stared amazedly at the magician. 她惊讶地盯着魔术师。
He listened amazedly to the incredible story. 他惊奇地听着这个不可思议的故事。
近义词:astonishingly, surprisingly, incredibly, unbelievably
反义词:apathetically, indifferently, calmly
