gastrovascular: /ˌɡæstrəʊˈvæskjʊlə(r)/ /ˌɡæstroʊˈvæskjələr/
| adj.| 消化循环的；胃管的 |Relating to a gastrovascular cavity.|The jellyfish has a simple gastrovascular cavity. 水母有一个简单的消化循环腔。|近义词: digestive |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider terms related to separate circulatory and digestive systems) |词性: adjective
gastrovascular: /ˌɡæstrəʊˈvæskjʊlə(r)/ /ˌɡæstroʊˈvæskjələr/
| n.| 消化循环腔 |A cavity that serves both digestive and circulatory purposes in some invertebrates.| The gastrovascular cavity of the hydra allows it to both digest food and transport nutrients.  水螅的消化循环腔既能消化食物又能运输营养物质。|近义词: digestive cavity|反义词: (No direct antonym, as above) |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From  gastro-  + vascular.  "Gastro-" comes from Greek "gaster" (stomach). "Vascular" comes from Latin "vasculum" (small vessel).
