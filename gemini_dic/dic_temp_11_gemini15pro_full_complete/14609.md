aviso:/əˈviːsoʊ/ (US), /əˈviːzəʊ/ (UK)| n.|通知，警告，预告|A formal notice or warning of something about to happen. |The captain received an aviso of an approaching storm. 船长收到了风暴即将来临的通知。|The company issued an aviso about the upcoming merger. 公司发布了关于即将进行的合并的公告。|近义词：notice, warning, alert, announcement|反义词: None|noun
