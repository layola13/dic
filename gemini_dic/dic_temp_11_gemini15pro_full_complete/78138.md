geometrid:/ˌdʒiːəˈmetrɪd/
noun
尺蛾；尺蠖蛾
A moth of the family Geometridae, whose larvae are called inchworms or loopers.
The geometrid caterpillar inched its way along the branch.  尺蛾幼虫沿着树枝一寸一寸地爬行。
近义词:inchworm, looper, measuring worm
反义词: (No specific antonym)
词性:n.


geometrid:/ˌdʒiːəˈmetrɪd/
adjective
尺蛾科的
Relating to or belonging to the family Geometridae.
The geometrid moth has distinctive wing patterns.  这种尺蛾科的飞蛾有独特的翅膀图案。
近义词: (No specific synonym)
反义词: (No specific antonym)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *Geometridae, from Greek geōmetrēs "geometer, land-measurer," from gē "earth" + metron "measure."  Referring to the looping movement of the larvae, as if measuring the earth.
