neons:/ˈniːɒnz/ (UK) /ˈniːɑːnz/ (US)| n.|霓虹灯；霓虹颜色|Bright, fluorescent colours, typically resembling those produced by neon lighting.  Plural form of "neon".|The city at night was a blaze of neons. 夜晚的城市霓虹灯闪烁。|The designer used vibrant neons in her latest collection.  这位设计师在她的最新系列中使用了鲜艳的霓虹色。|近义词：fluorescent colours, bright colours, vivid colours|反义词：pastel colours, muted colours|词性:noun


neon:/ˈniːɒn/ (UK) /ˈniːɑːn/ (US)| n. adj.|氖；霓虹灯；霓虹色的|A colorless, odorless gaseous element, present in minute amounts in the air, used in electric lamps and signs.|The neon sign buzzed faintly. 霓虹灯发出微弱的嗡嗡声。|She wore a neon pink dress. 她穿了一件霓虹粉色的连衣裙。|近义词：(as adjective) fluorescent, bright, luminous|反义词：(as adjective) dull, dark, muted|词性:noun, adjective
