asura:/ˈɑːʃʊrə/ | n. | 阿修罗 | A powerful demigod or demon in Hindu and Buddhist mythology, often depicted as engaged in perpetual conflict with the gods. | Example: The asura attacked the heavens, wielding a mighty weapon. 阿修罗手持强大的武器攻击了天界。

Example: In Buddhist cosmology, asuras represent beings driven by envy and jealousy. 在佛教宇宙观中，阿修罗代表着被嫉妒和忌妒驱使的众生。| 近义词: demon, titan | 反义词: deva, god | 词性: noun
