jub:/dʒʌb/| n.|欢庆，庆祝活动；周年纪念（尤指25周年或50周年）|A jubilation or celebration, especially a jubilee or anniversary.|例句：The town held a jub to mark the 50th anniversary of its founding.  这个小镇举行了庆祝活动，以纪念其成立50周年。

例句：Their 25th wedding anniversary was a real jub. 他们的25周年结婚纪念日是一个真正的庆祝活动。|近义词：celebration, festival, anniversary|反义词:mourning, grieving|词性:名词
