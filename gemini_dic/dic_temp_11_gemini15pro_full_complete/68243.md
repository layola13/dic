fairylands:/ˈfeəriˌlændz/
n.
仙境；奇幻世界
A magical, enchanting place, often imaginary.
She dreamed of visiting faraway fairylands. 她梦想着去遥远的仙境。
近义词:wonderlands, enchanted realms, magical kingdoms
反义词:reality, mundane world
词性:名词
