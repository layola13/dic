hungered:/ˈhʌŋɡərd/ | adj. | 渴望的，饥饿的 | Feeling a strong desire or craving for something, especially food. |  The refugees were hungered for news from their families. 难民们渴望得到家人的消息。| 近义词: starving, ravenous, famished, yearning, craving | 反义词: satiated, full, satisfied | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the Old English *hungrian, from Proto-Germanic *hungrōną, from Proto-Indo-European *kenk- (“to long for”). Related to hunger (n.).
