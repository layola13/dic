almagra:/ælˈmɑːɡrə/ | n. | 红赭石，赭红色 | A deep red ochre used as a pigment. | The artist used almagra to create the warm tones in the painting. 艺术家用红赭石来创作画作中的暖色调。 |  近义词: red ochre, ochre, ruddle | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Spanish "almagra," from Arabic "al-maghra" ( المغرة ) meaning "the ochre."
