bushfires:/ˈbʊʃˌfaɪəz/ (US) /ˈbʊʃˌfaɪəz/ (UK)| n. |森林火灾，山火|A fire that burns in scrub or a forest, especially a large, destructive one.|例句：The bushfires in Australia were devastating. 澳大利亚的森林火灾是毁灭性的。|近义词：wildfires, forest fires|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可用 controlled burn 受控燃烧)|noun
