acrophony:/əˈkrɒfəni/ | n. | 首音缩略法 | A mnemonic device or method of abbreviation in which the initial letters of a series of words form a pronounceable word or phrase. | Example:  LASER (Light Amplification by Stimulated Emission of Radiation) is an example of acrophony. 激光（受激辐射光放大）是首字母缩略法的一个例子。

Example:  NATO (North Atlantic Treaty Organization) is another common example of acrophony. 北约（北大西洋公约组织）是首字母缩略法另一个常见的例子。 | 近义词: abbreviation, initialism, acronym | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek  ἄκρον (ákron, “tip, extremity”) + φωνή (phōnḗ, “sound, voice”)
