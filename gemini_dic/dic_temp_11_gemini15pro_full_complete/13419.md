atonal:/eɪˈtoʊnl/ /ˈeɪtəʊnl/| adj.|无调性的|Lacking a tonal center or key; not based on the traditional system of tonality in music.|The composer experimented with atonal music, creating a jarring but intriguing sound.  作曲家尝试了无调性音乐，创造了一种刺耳但引人入胜的声音。|The piece was so atonal it was difficult to follow. 这首曲子太无调性了，很难理解。|近义词：nontonal, dissonant|反义词：tonal, harmonic|词性:形容词

Etymology:  a- (not) + tonal (relating to musical tone)  来自a-（不）+ tonal（与音乐音调有关）
