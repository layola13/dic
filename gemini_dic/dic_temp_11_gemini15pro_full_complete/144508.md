outtrading:/ˌaʊtˈtreɪdɪŋ/
verb
胜过交易；在交易中胜过
outperform in trading or business dealings
例句：The company succeeded by outtrading its competitors in the emerging markets.  这家公司通过在新兴市场上胜过竞争对手的交易而获得成功。

例句：Their strategy involved outtrading their rivals by offering lower prices and better terms. 他们的策略包括通过提供更低的价格和更好的条款来胜过竞争对手。

近义词：outmaneuvering, outsmarting, surpassing
反义词：being outmaneuvered, being outsmarted

词性：v.
