depredation:/ˌdɛprɪˈdeɪʃən/| n. |掠夺，破坏，蹂躏|The act of preying upon or plundering; robbery; spoliation.  The state of being preyed upon or plundered.|The depredations of the invaders left the city in ruins. 入侵者的掠夺使这座城市沦为废墟。|Synonyms:ravage, plunder, pillage, loot, despoil|Antonyms:preservation, protection, construction|noun
