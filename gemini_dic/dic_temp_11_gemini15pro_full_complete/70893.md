fioritura:/ˌfjɔːrɪˈtuːrə/ | n. | 开花，盛开; (音乐)装饰音 |A blossoming or flowering, especially a profuse one.  A florid ornamentation in music. | The cherry trees are in full fioritura. 樱花盛开。The soprano's fioritura in the aria was breathtaking. 女高音在咏叹调中的装饰音令人叹为观止。| 近义词: bloom, blossom, flowering, efflorescence (for plants); ornamentation, embellishment (for music) | 反义词:  withering, fading (for plants); simplicity, plainness (for music) | 词性: 名词

词源:  Italian, from fiorire 'to flower,' from fiore 'flower,' from Latin florere, from flos 'flower.'
