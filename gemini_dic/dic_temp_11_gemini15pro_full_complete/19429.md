词源:  Shortening of "biscuit," influenced by the tendency in Australian and New Zealand English to use diminutives ending in "-ie" or "-y."  "Biscuit" itself comes from Old French "bescuit" (twice-cooked), ultimately from Latin "bis" (twice) + "coctus" (cooked).
