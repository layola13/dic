词源:
"Jointure" comes from Old French "jointure," meaning "joining, junction."  The suffix "-less" indicates absence, so "jointureless" means "without joints or seams." “Jointure”来自古法语“jointure”，意思是“连接，接合”。后缀“-less”表示没有，所以“jointureless”的意思是“没有接缝或接缝”。
