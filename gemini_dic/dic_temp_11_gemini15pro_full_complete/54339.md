disheritor:/dɪsɪnˈhɛrɪtər/
n.
剥夺继承权的人
One who disinherits another; one who cuts off from inheritance.
The disheritor left his nephew nothing but a worn-out coat.  剥夺继承权的人只给他的侄子留下一件破旧的外套。
近义词:depriver, excluder
反义词:heir, inheritor
词性:noun

disheritor:/dɪsɪnˈhɛrɪtər/
n.
剥夺继承权者
Someone who prevents another from inheriting property or a title.
His father, the disheritor, left him with nothing. 他的父亲，剥夺继承权者，什么也没留给他。
近义词:divester
反义词:benefactor, testator
词性:noun
