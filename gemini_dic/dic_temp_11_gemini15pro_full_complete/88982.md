holis:/ˈoʊlis/| interjection |你好；嗨|A colloquial and informal greeting, primarily used online and in some Spanish-speaking communities. It's a variation of "hola" (hello) with a playful or affectionate tone. |Example 1: Holis! How are you doing today? 你好！你今天过得怎么样？

Example 2: Holis, everyone! Welcome to the stream! 嗨，大家好！欢迎来到直播间！|近义词：hi, hey, hello, what's up, hola |反义词: goodbye, farewell |词性: interjection
