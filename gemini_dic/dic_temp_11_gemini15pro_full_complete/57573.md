dramatize:/ˈdræməˌtaɪz/ /ˈdrɑːməˌtaɪz/| v. |戏剧化;编写剧本;夸张|to present something in a dramatic or exaggerated way; to adapt something for a play| The author dramatized his experiences in the war in his latest novel. 作者在他最新的长篇小说中将他经历过的战争戏剧化了。

The news report dramatized the plight of the refugees.  这则新闻报道戏剧化地描述了难民的困境。

|近义词：exaggerate, overstate, sensationalize, theatricalize |反义词: downplay, understate, minimize |词性:动词
