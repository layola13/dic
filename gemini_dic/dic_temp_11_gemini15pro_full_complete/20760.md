blandish:/ˈblændɪʃ/| v. | 奉承；哄骗；甜言蜜语地劝诱 | To coax with flattery, soothing words, or promises. | He blandished her with flowers and compliments, hoping to win her favor. 他用鲜花和恭维话奉承她，希望赢得她的好感。

He blandished the child with promises of sweets to get him to behave. 他用糖果的承诺来哄骗孩子，让他守规矩。

| 近义词：coax, cajole, flatter, wheedle | 反义词：threaten, coerce, criticize | 词性：动词
