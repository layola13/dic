overbarren:/ˈoʊvərˌbærən/
adjective
过度贫瘠的，极其荒芜的
Excessively barren; unproductive; too barren.  Land that is overbarren cannot support any life.
例句：The overbarren landscape offered little hope for the settlers.  这片极其荒芜的土地给定居者带来了很少的希望。

例句：Due to the drought, the fields became overbarren. 由于干旱，田地变得极其贫瘠。
近义词：infertile, sterile, desolate, unproductive
反义词：fertile, productive, fruitful, lush
词性：adjective

overbarren:/ˈoʊvərˌbærən/
adjective
过度贫瘠的
Excessively barren; unproductive.
例句：The overbarren desert stretched for miles. 这片极其贫瘠的沙漠绵延数英里。

例句：The farmer abandoned the overbarren fields. 农民放弃了这片过度贫瘠的土地。
近义词：arid, desolate, sterile
反义词：fertile, lush, productive
词性：adjective


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "barren" (adjective meaning "too poor to produce much or any vegetation").  来源于"over-"（前缀，意为“过度地”）和"barren"（形容词，意为“贫瘠的，不毛的”）。
