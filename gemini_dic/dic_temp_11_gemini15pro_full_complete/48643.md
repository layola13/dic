defensative: /dɪˈfɛnsətɪv/
adjective
防御性的，防守的
Protecting oneself from criticism or attack; adopted for the purpose of defense.
He immediately became defensative when I questioned his decision.  他一听我质疑他的决定，立刻就摆出防御姿态。
She had a defensative tone in her voice. 她的语气中带着防御的意味。

近义词: protective, guarded, apologetic
反义词: aggressive, offensive
