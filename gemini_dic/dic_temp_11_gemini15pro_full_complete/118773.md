metallogist:/məˈtælədʒɪst/
n.
冶金学家，金属学家
A scientist who studies metals, their properties, and their applications.
The metallogist analyzed the composition of the alloy.  冶金学家分析了合金的成分。

The metallogist developed a new process for refining the ore. 冶金学家开发了一种新的矿石精炼工艺。

metallurgist, materials scientist
chemist, physicist
noun
