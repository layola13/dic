limitate:/ˈlɪmɪteɪt/
verb
限制，限定
To restrict or confine within limits.
He felt limitated by his lack of experience. 他觉得缺乏经验限制了他的发展。
近义词: restrict, confine, limit, circumscribe
反义词: expand, broaden, enlarge
词性:v.


limitate:/ˈlɪmɪteɪt/
词源: From Latin *limitatus, past participle of *limitare (“to limit”), from limes (“boundary, limit”).
用法说明: Limitate is a less common synonym for "limit". While both words convey the idea of setting boundaries or restrictions, "limit" is much more frequently used in modern English.  "Limitate" might be encountered in older texts or in specific technical contexts.  Generally, using "limit" is preferred.
