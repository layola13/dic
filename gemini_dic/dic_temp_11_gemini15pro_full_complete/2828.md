adventured:/ədˈvɛntʃərd/
verb (past tense)
冒险(过去式)
Engaged in adventures; took risks.
They adventured into the unknown wilderness. 他们冒险进入了未知的荒野。
He adventured his life savings on a risky business venture. 他将毕生积蓄冒险投入一项有风险的商业项目。
近义词: dared, risked, ventured, gambled
反义词: played it safe, stayed put
词性: verb (past tense)


adventure:/ədˈvɛntʃər/
noun
冒险；奇遇
An unusual and exciting or daring experience.
She had many exciting adventures during her travels. 她在旅行中经历了许多激动人心的冒险。
Going to the Amazon rainforest was a real adventure. 去亚马逊雨林是一次真正的冒险。
近义词: escapade, exploit, undertaking
反义词: routine, boredom
词性: noun

adventure:/ədˈvɛntʃər/
verb
冒险
Engage in daring or risky activity.
He wanted to adventure in the mountains. 他想在山里冒险。
They decided to adventure forth into the blizzard. 他们决定冒险闯入暴风雪中。
近义词: venture, explore, dare, risk
反义词: refrain, avoid
词性: verb


词源:
From Old French *aventure (Modern French aventure), from Latin adventūra (“that which happens, occurrence, event”), from adventurus (“about to happen”), future active participle of advenīre (“to come to, arrive, befall, happen”), from ad- (“to, toward”) + venīre (“to come”).
