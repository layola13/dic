Bollandist: /ˈbɒləndɪst/ | n. | 博兰主义者；耶稣会学者(尤指研究圣徒传记的) | A Jesuit scholar, especially one who studies the lives of saints.  Specifically, a member of the Society of Jesus who contributes to the *Acta Sanctorum*. | The Bollandists have been working on the *Acta Sanctorum* for centuries. 博兰主义者几个世纪以来一直在研究《圣徒行传》。

|  The meticulous research of the Bollandists is renowned in hagiographical studies. 博兰主义者的严谨研究在圣徒传记研究中享有盛誉。 | 近义词: hagiographer,  Jesuit scholar | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "skeptic" in a broader context) | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From proper noun *Bolland*, from John van Bolland (Johannes Bollandus), a 17th-century Jesuit scholar who began the *Acta Sanctorum* project.
