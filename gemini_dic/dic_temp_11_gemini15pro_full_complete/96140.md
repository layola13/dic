indent:/ɪnˈdɛnt/ | n. | 缩进，缩排；凹痕 |  A blank space at the beginning of a line of writing; a notch, recess | The indent makes the paragraph easier to read. 缩进使段落更容易阅读。| synonyms: notch, recess, indentation | antonyms: projection, protrusion | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *indenter, from in- "in" + dent "tooth," from Latin dens (genitive dentis).  Originally meant "to furnish with teeth," later "to cut into points," and finally "to notch" or "to make a recess."
