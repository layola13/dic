ounce: /aʊns/ | n. | 盎司 | A unit of weight equal to one sixteenth of a pound (approximately 28.35 grams).  Also a fluid ounce (approximately 29.57 milliliters). |  I need eight ounces of flour for this recipe.  我需要八盎司的面粉来做这个食谱。

|近义词: dram, grain (for small weights) |反义词: pound, ton (larger units of weight)|词性: noun


ounce: /aʊns/ | n. |少量，少许|A small amount of something. |  He didn't have an ounce of common sense.  他一点常识都没有。

|近义词: bit, drop, smidgen |反义词: lot, abundance|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *unce (Modern French once), from Latin uncia "twelfth part," related to unus "one."  Originally referred to a twelfth part of a Roman pound (libra) or a twelfth part of a Roman foot (pes).
