counters:/ˈkaʊntərz/ | n. | 柜台，计数器 | surfaces or devices used for counting or serving; things that oppose or contrast with something else |  The bank tellers worked busily behind their counters.  银行出纳员在柜台后面忙碌地工作着。| synonyms: worktops, surfaces, bar,  opposites: none | noun


counters:/ˈkaʊntərz/ | v. | 反击，对抗，抵消 | speaks or acts in opposition to something; meets or opposes something with equal force | The government countered the opposition's claims with strong evidence. 政府用强有力的证据反驳了反对派的指控。 | synonyms: opposes, contrasts, retaliates,  opposites: supports, agrees | verb
