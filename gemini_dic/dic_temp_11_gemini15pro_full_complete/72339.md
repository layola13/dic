flower:/ˈflaʊər/ (US), /ˈflaʊə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|花；开花；繁盛|A reproductive structure in plants, typically colorful and fragrant, that produces seeds.|例句：The flower is beautiful.  这朵花很漂亮。

The cherry trees flowered early this year. 今年樱花开得早。|近义词：blossom, bloom|反义词：weed|词性:名词, 动词
