antimodernistic:/ˌæntiˌmɑːdərnˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 反现代主义的 | Opposed to or rejecting modernism, especially in the arts, culture, or literature.  Characterized by a preference for traditional values and styles. |  The architect's antimodernistic designs incorporated classical elements.  这位建筑师的反现代主义设计融合了古典元素。

The antimodernistic movement championed a return to traditional craftsmanship. 反现代主义运动倡导回归传统工艺。| 近义词: traditional, conservative, reactionary | 反义词: modern, modernist, avant-garde | 词性: adjective
