agitprop:/ˈædʒɪtprɒp/| n.| 政治宣传|Agitprop is a portmanteau word of "agitation" and "propaganda." It refers to politically motivated and directed art, especially theater, literature, and cinema, aiming to influence public opinion.|The play was a blatant piece of agitprop designed to stir up anti-government sentiment. 这部戏剧是一部公然的政治宣传作品，旨在煽动反政府情绪。|synonyms: propaganda, indoctrination, brainwashing|antonyms: education, enlightenment, objectivity|noun

agitprop:/ˈædʒɪtprɒp/| adj.| 宣传鼓动的|Used attributively to describe something that functions as or resembles agitprop.|The agitprop theatre group toured factories and farms.  这个宣传鼓动剧团在工厂和农场巡回演出。|synonyms: propagandistic|antonyms: objective, unbiased|adjective
