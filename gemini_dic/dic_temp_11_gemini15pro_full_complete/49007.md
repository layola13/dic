degradations:/ˌdɛɡrəˈdeɪʃənz/| n. | 退化，降解，堕落 |  The process of something becoming worse or being destroyed, especially over time.  | The building showed signs of degradation due to years of neglect. 这栋建筑由于多年失修，呈现出退化的迹象。

The environmental degradations caused by pollution are severe. 污染造成的环境退化很严重。 | synonyms: deterioration, decline, decay, worsening, impairment | antonyms: improvement, enhancement, amelioration, betterment | noun
