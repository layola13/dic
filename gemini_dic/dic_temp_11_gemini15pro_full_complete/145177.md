overattentively: /ˌoʊvərəˈtɛntɪvli/ | adv. | 过于专注地，过分注意地 | Paying attention to something or someone more than is necessary or usual. | She listened overattentively to every word he said, afraid of missing any nuance. 她过分专注地听着他说的每一个字，生怕错过任何细微的差别。| 近义词: excessively attentively, too attentively | 反义词: inattentively, carelessly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
over- (prefix) + attentively
over-: from Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi (meaning "above, over, beyond")
attentively: from attentive + -ly
attentive: from Latin attentus (meaning "attentive"), past participle of attendere (meaning "to attend to")
-ly: adverbial suffix
