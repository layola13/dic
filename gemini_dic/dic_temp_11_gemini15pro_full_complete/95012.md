in your wheelhouse: /ɪn jər ˈwiːlhaʊs/ | idiom | 在你的掌控范围内；擅长的事情；你的领域 | Something that is in your wheelhouse is something you are good at or know a lot about. It refers to your area of expertise or comfort zone. |  Negotiating is definitely in her wheelhouse. 她绝对擅长谈判。| Synonyms: forte, specialty, strength, area of expertise, domain | Antonyms: weakness, shortcoming | idiom
