abysmally:/əˈbɪzməli/| adv.| 极糟地，非常差地 | In an extremely bad way; terribly. | The movie was abysmally boring.  这部电影糟糕透顶，无聊至极。| The team played abysmally in the first half.  该队上半场表现得非常糟糕。| 近义词: terribly, awfully, dreadfully, appallingly | 反义词: excellently, superbly, wonderfully | 词性: 副词

词源: From Late Latin *abyssalis, from Latin abyssus (“bottomless pit, the deep”), from Ancient Greek ἄβυσσος (ábyssos, “bottomless, unfathomable”).
