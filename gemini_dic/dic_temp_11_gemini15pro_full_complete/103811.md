jovinianist:/ˌdʒoʊvɪˈneɪənɪst/
n.
信奉约维尼安主义的人; 约维尼安主义信徒
A follower of the doctrines of Jovinian.
Jovinian was a 4th-century monk who challenged the ascetic ideals of the church.  His followers, the Jovinianists, were condemned as heretics.  约维尼安是一位四世纪的僧侣，他挑战了教会的禁欲主义理想。他的追随者，约维尼安主义者，被谴责为异端。
The jovinianists believed that married people were as holy as virgins. 约维尼安主义者认为已婚人士和处女一样神圣。

近义词: heretic, dissenter
反义词: ascetic
词性: noun
