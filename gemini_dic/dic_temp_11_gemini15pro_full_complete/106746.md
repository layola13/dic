l-plate:/ˈɛl pleɪt/| n.|临时驾照标志牌（learner plate的缩写）|A sign that must be displayed on the front and rear of a vehicle driven by a learner driver.  A learner driver is someone who is learning to drive but has not yet passed their driving test.|例句：The car had L-plates on it, so I knew the driver was inexperienced. 这辆车上有临时驾照标志牌，所以我知道司机没有经验。|例句：  He finally took the L-plates off his car after passing his test.  他通过考试后终于把车上的临时驾照标志牌摘下来了。|近义词：learner plate, provisional plate|反义词:|noun
