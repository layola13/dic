manura: /məˈnʊrə/ | n. | 肥料，粪肥 |  Manure is organic matter used as fertilizer.  It typically consists of animal feces, but can also include other decomposed organic materials such as straw and hay. | The farmer spread manure on his fields. 农民在地里撒了肥料。 | synonyms: fertilizer, compost, dung | antonyms: none | noun
