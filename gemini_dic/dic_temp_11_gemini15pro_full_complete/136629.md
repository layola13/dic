nonscarcities:/nɒnˈskɛəsɪtiz/
noun
非稀缺性；充足
The state or quality of not being scarce; abundance.
Due to technological advancements, we are entering an era of nonscarcities for certain resources. 由于技术的进步，我们正在进入某些资源非稀缺性的时代。
The development of synthetic meat could lead to nonscarcities of protein.  人造肉的发展可能导致蛋白质的非稀缺性。
Synonyms: abundance, plentifulness, profusion, surplus, plethora
Antonyms: scarcity, lack, dearth, shortage, deficiency
