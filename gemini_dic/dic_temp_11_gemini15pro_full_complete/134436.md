nonirrevocableness:/ˌnɒnɪrɪˈvɒkəbəlnəs/
n.
不可撤销性
The state or quality of not being irrevocable; the possibility of being reversed or undone.
The contract's nonirrevocableness allowed either party to withdraw.  这份合同的不可撤销性允许任何一方撤回。
non-revocability, reversibility
irrevocableness, finality
noun
