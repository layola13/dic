overfrighten:/ˌoʊvərˈfraɪtn/| vt.|过度惊吓，吓坏|To frighten excessively; to cause extreme fear in someone.|The loud noise overfrightened the children. 巨大的噪音吓坏了孩子们。|The horror movie overfrightened her so much she couldn't sleep. 这部恐怖电影把她吓得够呛，她都睡不着了。|近义词:terrify, scare, alarm|反义词:calm, soothe, comfort|词性:动词
词源: over- + frighten. "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Frighten" comes from the Old English word "fryhto," meaning "fear."
