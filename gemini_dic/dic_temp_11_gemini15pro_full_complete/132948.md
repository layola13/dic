nonemigrants:/ˌnɒnˈɛmɪɡrənts/ | n. |非移民|People who enter a country legally for a temporary period, such as tourists, students, or temporary workers, without the intention of establishing permanent residency. | The US issues various types of visas for nonemigrants. 美国为非移民签发各种类型的签证。| Tourists and students are examples of nonemigrants.  游客和学生是非移民的例子。| visitors, aliens, foreigners| immigrants, residents| noun
