modestest:/ˈmɑːdɪstɪst/ | adj. | 最谦虚的 | Superlative form of modest: having or showing a moderate or humble estimate of one's merits, importance, etc. |  She is the modestest person I know, always downplaying her accomplishments. 她是我认识的最谦虚的人，总是轻描淡写她的成就。|  He gave the modestest of bows after his performance.  演出结束后，他非常谦虚地鞠了一躬。 | 近义词: humblest, unpretentious | 反义词: proudest, arrogant | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Middle French *modeste (modern French modeste), from Latin modestus (“moderate, keeping due measure”), from modus (“measure, manner, mode”).
