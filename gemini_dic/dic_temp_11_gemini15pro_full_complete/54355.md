dishonor:/dɪsˈɑːnər/ /dɪsˈɒnə(r)/| n. vt.|耻辱，丢脸；侮辱；拒付|A state of disgrace or low repute. To deprive of honor or respect; disgrace. To refuse to accept or pay (a check, bill, etc.).|
He felt it was a dishonor to his family. 他觉得这让他家族蒙羞。


He dishonored his family by his actions. 他的行为让他的家族蒙羞。


The bank dishonored the check. 银行拒付了这张支票。
