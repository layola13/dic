a'as:/əz/ |conj.| as | used to indicate that something happens at the same time as something else | Example: As I walked down the street, I saw a cat. 当我走在街上的时候，我看到了一只猫。| Synonym: while, when | Antonym: before, after | word class: pronoun/conjunction/adverb

a'as:/əz/ |adv.| as | used to indicate the degree to which something is true | Example: She's not as tall as her sister. 她没有她姐姐高。 | Synonym: so, equally | Antonym: less, unequally | word class: pronoun/conjunction/adverb
