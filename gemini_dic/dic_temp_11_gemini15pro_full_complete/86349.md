hectic:/ˈhɛktɪk/| adj.| 忙碌的，紧张的，狂热的| Full of incessant or frantic activity.|例句：I've had a hectic week at work. 我这周工作非常忙碌。|例句：The store was hectic on the day of the sale.  大减价那天，商店里熙熙攘攘，一片繁忙。|近义词：frantic, feverish, busy, chaotic|反义词：calm, peaceful, quiet, relaxing|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek *hektikos* "habitual," from *hexis* "habit," related to *ekhein* "to have, hold." Originally referred to a continuous fever, later extended to any kind of feverish or restless activity.
