alveoli: /ælˈviːəlaɪ/ (plural), /ælˈviːələs/ (singular)
n.
肺泡
Tiny air sacs in the lungs where the exchange of oxygen and carbon dioxide takes place.
The alveoli are essential for respiration. 肺泡对于呼吸至关重要。
The walls of the alveoli are very thin, allowing for efficient gas exchange.  肺泡壁非常薄，有利于气体交换。
synonyms: air sacs
antonyms: N/A
noun
