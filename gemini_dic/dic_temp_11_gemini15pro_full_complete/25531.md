bullshit:/ˈbʊlʃɪt/ /ˈbʊlʃɪt/| n. interj. adj.|胡说八道，废话；胡扯；|nonsense; lies.|He told me some story about being mugged, but I think it's all bullshit. 他跟我讲了一些关于他被抢劫的故事，但我认为全是胡说八道。|近义词：nonsense, rubbish, crap, lies|反义词：truth, fact|词性:名词,感叹词,形容词
bullshit:/ˈbʊlʃɪt/ /ˈbʊlʃɪt/| v. |胡扯，瞎说；|talk nonsense.|Stop bullshitting me! 别跟我胡扯了！|近义词：lie, deceive, prevaricate|反义词：tell the truth|词性:动词

词源:
From bull +‎ shit.  The term refers to the large amount of manure produced by bulls.  The figurative sense developed by 1915, originally referring to deceptive or misleading language or actions, and later extended to general nonsense.
