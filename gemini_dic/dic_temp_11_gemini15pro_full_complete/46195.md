cypris:/ˈsaɪprɪs/| n.|介形虫；淡水介形亚纲动物；（希臘神話）賽普勒斯女神|A small freshwater crustacean of the subclass Ostracoda, typically enclosed in a hinged carapace.  Also, in Greek mythology, another name for Aphrodite, associated with the island of Cyprus.|例句：The cypris larva of the barnacle attaches itself to a hard surface. 藤壶的介形幼虫附着在坚硬的表面上。

例句：Cypris, in Greek mythology, is associated with love and beauty. 在希腊神话中，賽普勒斯女神与爱和美丽有关。|近义词：ostracod|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
