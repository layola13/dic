cliquier:/kliːˈkiː/ | n. |小圈子，派系 |A small, exclusive group of people. |例句：The office was dominated by a cliquier of senior managers. 办公室被一个由高级经理组成的小圈子所掌控。

例句：It's hard to break into that cliquier if you're not already part of their social circle. 如果你还不是他们社交圈的一部分，很难打入那个小圈子。|近义词：clique, coterie, inner circle, faction |反义词: inclusive group, community |词性: noun
