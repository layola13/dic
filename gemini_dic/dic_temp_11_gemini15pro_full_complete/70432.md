figment:/ˈfɪɡmənt/| n. |虚构的事物，幻觉|something that someone has imagined or invented and that is not real|例句：The story was a complete figment of his imagination.  这个故事完全是他想象出来的。

例句：Ghosts and fairies are figments of the imagination. 鬼怪和仙女都是虚构的。|近义词：invention, fabrication, illusion, fantasy|反义词：reality, truth, fact|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *figmentum "a fashioning, feigning," from Latin fingere "to shape, fashion, pretend, invent," from PIE root *dheigh- "to form, build."
