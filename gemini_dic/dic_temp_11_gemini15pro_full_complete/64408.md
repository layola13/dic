ergative:/ˈɜːrɡətɪv/ | adj. n.| 作格的，作格语言；作格|An ergative verb takes a subject that is not an agent but is affected by the verb’s action; in English, many verbs can be used this way.  An ergative language is one that treats the subject of intransitive verbs like the object of transitive verbs.|The window broke. (Ergative use - the window is affected by the action but doesn't perform it) | 近义词: absolutive (for the noun) | 反义词: nominative, accusative |词性: adjective, noun
