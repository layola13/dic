epigrammist: /ɪˈpɪɡræmɪst/ | n. | 警句家；善写警句的人 | A person who composes or uses epigrams. |  He was a renowned epigrammist, known for his witty and concise sayings. 他是一位著名的警句家，以其机智简洁的格言而闻名。

Oscar Wilde was a brilliant epigrammist. 奥斯卡·王尔德是一位才华横溢的警句家。 | 近义词: wit, pundit, quipster | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly someone verbose or inarticulate) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *épigrammatiste*, from Latin *epigramma* (epigram), from Greek *epigramma*  "inscription," from *epigraphein* "to write on, inscribe," from *epi* "upon" + *graphein* "to write."
