executrix:/ɪɡˈzɛkjətrɪks/| n. |女遗嘱执行人|A woman appointed by a testator in a will to carry out the terms of the will.|Example 1:  Mary was named the executrix of her mother's will. 玛丽被指定为她母亲遗嘱的执行人。

Example 2: The executrix oversaw the distribution of the estate. 女遗嘱执行人监督了遗产的分配。|近义词：executor (if gender is not relevant), administrator|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English executrice, from Anglo-Norman executrice, feminine of executor, ultimately from Latin exsecūtor (“executor, performer”), agent noun from exsecūtus, past participle of exsequor (“I follow out, execute”).
