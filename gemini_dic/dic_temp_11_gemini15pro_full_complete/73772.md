formalism:/ˈfɔːrməlɪzəm/ | n. |形式主义；拘泥形式；形式体系| Excessive adherence to prescribed forms or conventions, often at the expense of substance or meaning. A theory that stresses adherence to prescribed forms or external rules. |  Many artists rebelled against the formalism of the academy. 许多艺术家反抗学院的刻板形式主义。|  The company's rigid formalism stifles creativity. 公司死板的繁文缛节扼杀了创造力。|近义词: ritualism, conventionalism, rigidity |反义词: informality, flexibility, pragmatism |词性: noun

Etymology: Late 19th century: from French *formalisme*, from formel ‘formal’.
