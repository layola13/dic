humid:/ˈhjuːmɪd/ (US), /ˈhjuːmɪd/ (UK)| adj.|潮湿的|Containing or characterized by a large amount of water vapor; noticeably moist.|例句：The air was thick and humid. 空气又闷又潮湿。

例句：It's very humid today; I'm sweating a lot. 今天很潮湿；我出了很多汗。


近义词：damp, moist, muggy, sticky, sultry|反义词:arid, dry|词性:adjective
