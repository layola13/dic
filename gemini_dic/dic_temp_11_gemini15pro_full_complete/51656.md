deviator:/ˈdiːvieɪtər/| n.|偏离者，偏差者，偏向器|Someone or something that deviates (strays) from a norm, standard, or expectation.|The deviator in the experiment was the one data point that fell far outside the expected range.  实验中的偏差值是一个远远超出预期范围的数据点。|例句：The road deviator redirects traffic away from the construction zone. 道路导流器将交通从施工区域引导出去。

例句：Statistical analysis helps identify deviators from the average performance. 统计分析有助于识别与平均绩效的偏差者。|近义词：nonconformist, maverick, dissident, outlier, anomaly|反义词:conformist, traditionalist|词性:noun
