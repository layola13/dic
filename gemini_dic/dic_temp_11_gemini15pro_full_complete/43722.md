credulousness:/krɪˈdʒuːləsnəs/| n.|轻信，易受骗|A tendency to be too ready to believe that something is true. |Her credulousness made her an easy target for scams. 她的轻信使她很容易成为骗局的目标。|Synonyms: gullibility, naivety, innocence|Antonyms: skepticism, cynicism, disbelief|noun

credulous:/ˈkrɛdʒʊləs/| adj.|轻信的，易受骗的|Having or showing too great a readiness to believe things.|He was credulous enough to believe the stranger's story. 他很容易相信陌生人的故事。|Synonyms: gullible, naive, trusting|Antonyms: skeptical, cynical, suspicious|adjective
