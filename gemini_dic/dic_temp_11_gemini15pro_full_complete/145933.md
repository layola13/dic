overdiscount:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪskaʊnt/ | n. | 过度折扣，超额折扣 | A discount that is larger than usual or necessary. |  The store offered an overdiscount on last season's clothing to clear out inventory.  这家商店对上一季的服装提供超额折扣以清理库存。 | Example: The overdiscount attracted many customers, but significantly reduced the profit margin.  超额折扣吸引了许多顾客，但大大降低了利润率。 | Synonyms: excessive discount, steep discount | Antonyms: premium, surcharge | noun

overdiscount:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪskaʊnt/ | vt. | 过度打折，超额打折 | To offer a discount that is larger than usual or necessary. | The retailer overdiscounted the items to stimulate sales. 零售商对商品进行了超额打折以刺激销售。 | Example:  Management decided to overdiscount slow-moving products. 管理层决定对滞销产品进行超额打折。 | Synonyms: discount excessively, heavily discount | Antonyms: mark up, upcharge | verb
