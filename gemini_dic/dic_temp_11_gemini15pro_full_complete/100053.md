interpledged:/ˌɪntərˈpledʒd/
adjective
相互保证的，相互抵押的
mutually pledged or guaranteed; having made a mutual pledge or agreement.
The two nations made an interpledged agreement to defend each other in times of war. 这两个国家签署了相互保证的协议，承诺在战争时期互相保卫。
Near Synonyms: interguaranteed, mutually assured, covenanted
Antonyms:  unbound, independent, separate
词性: adjective
