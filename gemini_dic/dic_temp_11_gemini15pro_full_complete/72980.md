foolhardiness:/ˈfuːlhɑːrdɪnɪs/| n.|鲁莽；蛮勇； |Reckless bravery; foolish daring; a tendency to take unnecessary risks. |His foolhardiness led him to climb the mountain without proper equipment. 他的鲁莽让他在没有适当装备的情况下攀登山峰。|Her foolhardiness in confronting the armed robber was shocking. 她竟然敢于对抗持械抢劫犯，真是令人震惊。|近义词：recklessness, rashness, temerity, audacity|反义词：caution, prudence, circumspection|词性:名词
