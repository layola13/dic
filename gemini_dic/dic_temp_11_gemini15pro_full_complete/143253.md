outdespatched:/ˌaʊt.dɪˈspætʃt/
verb
超越派遣，胜过派遣
To despatch more quickly or effectively than another.
Example:  Our team outdespatched their couriers and delivered the package first. 我们的团队比他们的快递员更快地送达了包裹。

Example:  Despite their efforts, they were outdespatched by a rival company. 尽管他们尽了努力，他们还是被一家竞争对手公司超越了。
Synonym: outstrip, surpass, outperform
Antonym: lag behind, fall behind
词性:动词

词源:
"out-" (prefix meaning "surpassing" or "exceeding") + "despatch" (verb meaning "to send off with speed").  The word "despatch" itself comes from the Old French word "despescher," meaning "to hasten" or "to expedite."
