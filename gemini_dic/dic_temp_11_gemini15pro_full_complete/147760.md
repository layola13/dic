overprolificness:/ˌoʊvərproʊˈlɪfɪknəs/
noun
过度生育;过度多产
The state of being excessively prolific; overproduction, especially of offspring.
The rabbit's overprolificness led to an overpopulation problem in the area.  兔子的过度繁殖导致该地区兔子数量过剩。
The author's overprolificness resulted in a decline in the quality of his later works.  这位作家过度创作导致他后期作品质量下降。
近义词:overabundance, superabundance, excess, plethora
反义词:scarcity, dearth, paucity
词性:n.

Etymology:
From over- + prolific + -ness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Prolific" means producing offspring or fruit abundantly; fertile. "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
