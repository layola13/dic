in conclusion:/ɪn kənˈkluːʒən/| adv.|总之，最后，结论是|Used to introduce the concluding section of a piece of writing or speech.  It signals that the speaker or writer is summarizing the main points or giving a final thought. |In conclusion, the study showed a clear correlation between the two variables. 总之，这项研究表明这两个变量之间存在明显的相关性。

|In conclusion, I'd like to thank everyone for their hard work on this project. 最后，我要感谢大家在这个项目上的辛勤工作。|近义词:finally, lastly, to sum up, in summary, to conclude|反义词:initially, at first, to begin with|词性:副词
