cranker:/ˈkræŋkər/ | n. |怪人，脾气坏的人；手摇曲柄；|A cranker is an eccentric or bad-tempered person, often with strong, unconventional beliefs.|He's a bit of a cranker, always complaining about something. 他有点怪，总是抱怨个不停。|Synonyms: eccentric, grouch, curmudgeon, crank, oddball |Antonyms: agreeable person, pleasant person |noun

cranker:/ˈkræŋkər/ | n. |手摇装置；启动装置|A cranker is a device, especially one with a crank, used for starting an engine or other machinery.|The old car needed a few pulls on the cranker to get it started. 这辆老爷车需要摇几下手柄才能启动。|Synonyms: starter, winder |Antonyms: N/A |noun
