corrodibilities:/kəˌroʊdəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. |腐蚀性, 被腐蚀的可能性|The corrodibilities of different metals vary greatly. 不同金属的耐腐蚀性差异很大。|例句：The engineer is studying the corrodibilities of various alloys for use in a harsh marine environment.  工程师正在研究各种合金在恶劣海洋环境中的耐腐蚀性。

The high corrodibilities of these materials make them unsuitable for use in acidic environments. 这些材料的高腐蚀性使其不适合在酸性环境中使用。 | 近义词：corrosion resistance (反义词), susceptibility to corrosion| 反义词：corrosion resistance, inertness | 词性: 名词(plural)
