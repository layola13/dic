opulence:/ˈɒpjʊləns/ (US) /ˈɑːpjələns/ (UK)| n. | 富裕，奢华，丰富 |Great wealth or luxuriousness.  A display of showy extravagance.|例句：The opulence of the palace was breathtaking. 宫殿的富丽堂皇令人叹为观止。|例句：She lived a life of opulence, surrounded by luxury. 她过着奢华的生活，周围都是奢侈品。|近义词：luxury, lavishness, richness, affluence, grandeur|反义词：poverty, austerity, simplicity, modesty|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *opulentia* "wealth, abundance," from *opulentus* "wealthy," related to *ops* "power, resources, wealth."
