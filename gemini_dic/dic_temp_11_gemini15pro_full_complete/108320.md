laurite:/ˈlɔːraɪt/| n.|硫钌矿|A rare sulfide mineral, RuS₂, found in igneous rocks. It is a source of ruthenium.|Laurite was discovered in Borneo. 硫钌矿是在婆罗洲发现的。|Synonyms: ruthenium sulfide|Antonyms: N/A|noun
