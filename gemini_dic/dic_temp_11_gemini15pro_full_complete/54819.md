disperseness:/dɪˈspɜːs(ə)nɪs/
n.
分散; 分散性; 零散
the state of being spread over a wide area.

The disperseness of the population made communication difficult.
人口的分散使得沟通变得困难。


The disperseness of the data made it hard to analyze.
数据的零散性使得难以分析。

Synonyms: scattering, diffusion, dissemination, spread
Antonyms: concentration, centralization, compactness, gathering
词性: 名词
