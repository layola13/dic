colloquialisms:/kəˈloʊkwɪəlɪzəmz/| n. |俗语，口语|Informal words or phrases used in everyday conversation.|Example:  "Wanna" and "gonna" are common colloquialisms. “Wanna” 和 “gonna” 是常见的口语。|Synonyms: idioms, slang, informal language|Antonyms: formal language, standard English|noun
