aspirated:/ˈæspəreɪtɪd/| adj.|送气的，吸气的|Pronounced with a puff of air, as the consonants 'p,' 't,' and 'k' in English.  Characterized by the release of a puff of air.|例句：The 'p' in 'pen' is aspirated. “pen”中的“p”是送气的。

例句：In some languages, the difference between aspirated and unaspirated consonants can change the meaning of a word. 在某些语言中，送气和不送气辅音的区别可以改变单词的含义。|近义词：plosive, breathed|反义词：unaspirated|词性:adjective
