blue-collar:/ˈbluː ˈkɑːlər/ (US) /ˈbluː ˈkɒlə(r)/ (UK)| adj. n.|蓝领的，蓝领工人|Relating to manual work or workers, especially in industry.|例句：Blue-collar workers are essential to the economy. 蓝领工人对经济至关重要。
