dislive: /dɪsˈlɪv/
verb
使不快乐，使不满意
to make unhappy or dissatisfied.
He was dislived by the outcome of the election. 他对选举结果感到不满意。
近义词: displease, upset, disappoint
反义词: please, satisfy, delight
词性: verb

词源:  "dis-" (expressing negation) + "live" (to experience life).  The meaning evolved from the original sense of "not to live fully or happily."
