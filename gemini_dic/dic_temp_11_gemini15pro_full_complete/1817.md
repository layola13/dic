acritude: /ˈækrɪtjuːd/
noun
辛辣；尖刻；刻薄
Sharpness or bitterness of speech or temper.
Her acritude surprised everyone; she was normally so polite. 她的尖刻让每个人都感到惊讶；她通常很有礼貌。
近义词: bitterness, sharpness, acrimony, asperity, mordancy, tartness
反义词: sweetness, gentleness, mildness, kindness
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *acrītūdō, from Latin ācer (“sharp, pungent, keen”) + -itūdō (“-ness, -ity”).
