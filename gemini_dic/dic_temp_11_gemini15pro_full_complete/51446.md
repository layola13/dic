dethroners:/diˈθrəʊnərz/
n.
废黜者，取代者
Those who remove someone from a position of power or dominance.
The dethroners of the old king quickly established a new regime.  老国王的废黜者迅速建立了新的政权。
The new technology became the dethroners of traditional methods. 新技术取代了传统方法。
synonyms: usurpers, ousts, displacers, successors
antonyms: incumbents, defenders, supporters
noun
