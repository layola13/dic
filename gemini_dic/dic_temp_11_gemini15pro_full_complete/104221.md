jured:/ˈdʒʊərd/ | adj. | 受伤的；委屈的；受损害的 | Injured means harmed, damaged, or wounded, either physically or emotionally.  It often implies a sense of injustice or wrong. |  The jured athlete had to withdraw from the competition. 受伤的运动员不得不退出比赛。| synonyms: wounded, hurt, harmed, damaged, impaired | antonyms: healed, healthy, sound, undamaged | adjective
