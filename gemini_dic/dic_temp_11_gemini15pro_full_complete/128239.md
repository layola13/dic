neurography:/nʊˈrɒɡrəfi/ /nʊˈrɑːɡrəfi/
noun
神经造影术；神经描写
A technique for visualizing peripheral nerves.  It involves injecting a contrast agent around the nerve and then using imaging techniques, such as X-ray, CT, or MRI, to create detailed images.
Neurography helped doctors identify the location of the nerve compression. 神经造影帮助医生确定了神经受压的部位。
Synonyms: nerve conduction study, electromyography
Antonyms: None (lack of a direct antonym for a diagnostic procedure)
词性: noun
