equipper:/ɪˈkwɪpər/ | n. | 装备者，配备者 | someone or something that equips | The company is a leading equipper of hospitals.  这家公司是医院的主要设备供应商。| 近义词: outfitter, supplier, provider | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From the verb "equip," which comes from Old French "equiper" (to fit out a ship), ultimately from a Germanic source.
