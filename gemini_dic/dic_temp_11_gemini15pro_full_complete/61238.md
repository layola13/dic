emails:/ˈiːmeɪlz/ | n. |电子邮件（复数）| Messages distributed by electronic means from one computer user to one or more recipients via a network. | I check my emails every morning. 我每天早上都会查看我的电子邮件。|  I received hundreds of emails after the announcement.  公告发布后，我收到了数百封电子邮件。 | 近义词：messages, correspondence | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps traditional mail/letters) | 词性: noun
