bushfowl:/ˈbʊʃfaʊl/| n. |灌丛鸡|A type of bird found in Africa, belonging to the family Numididae. They are known for their speckled plumage and ground-dwelling habits.|Example 1: The bushfowl scurried through the undergrowth. 灌丛鸡快速穿过灌木丛。

Example 2: We saw a flock of bushfowl foraging near the watering hole. 我们看到一群灌丛鸡在水坑附近觅食。|近义词：guineafowl, francolin|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology:
"bush" + "fowl"
"bush" (Old English *bysc*, meaning a shrub or thicket) + "fowl" (Old English *fugol*, meaning bird)
