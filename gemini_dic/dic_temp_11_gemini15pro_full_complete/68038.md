factorship:/ˈfæktərʃɪp/
n.
代理权;代理商的身份;代理商的业务
The state or position of being a factor or agent.
He held the factorship for a large textile company. 他担任一家大型纺织公司的代理商。
近义词: agency, dealership, distributorship
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "client" or "customer" from the opposite perspective)
词性: noun
