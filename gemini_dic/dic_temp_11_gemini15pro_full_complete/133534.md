nonfiguratively:/ˌnɒnˈfɪɡərətɪvli/ /ˌnɑːnˈfɪɡjərətɪvli/
adv.
字面上地，非比喻地
literally, not figuratively; in a way that is not metaphorical
The term "cold-blooded" is used nonfiguratively to describe reptiles.  “冷血”一词用于字面上地描述爬行动物。
She described the events nonfiguratively, sticking to the facts. 她如实地描述了这些事件，坚持事实。
近义词: literally, directly, plainly
反义词: figuratively, metaphorically
词性: adverb

词源:
From non- + figurative + -ly.  Non- 表示否定，figurative 表示比喻的，-ly 构成副词。
