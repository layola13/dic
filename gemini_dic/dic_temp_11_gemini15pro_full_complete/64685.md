escapee:/ɪˌskeɪˈpiː/ /əˈskeɪpi/
noun
逃亡者；逃跑者
A person who has escaped from captivity or confinement.
The escapee was quickly apprehended by the authorities.  逃亡者很快就被当局逮捕了。
The escapee blended into the crowd and disappeared.  逃犯混入人群消失了。
Synonyms: fugitive, runaway, refugee
Antonyms: captive, prisoner
词性: 名词
