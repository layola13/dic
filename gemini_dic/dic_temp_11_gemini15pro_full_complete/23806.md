brazenly:/ˈbreɪzənli/| adv.|厚颜无耻地，肆无忌惮地|In a bold and shameless manner, without feeling embarrassed or ashamed.|He brazenly lied to his parents about where he had been. 他厚颜无耻地向父母撒谎自己去过哪里。|近义词：boldly, shamelessly, impudently, audaciously|反义词：shyly, timidly, modestly, discreetly|词性: 副词
