corpora:/ˈkɔːrpərə/ (US), /ˈkɔːpərə/ (UK)| n. |语料库，文集，全集|A large collection of written or spoken texts, especially one compiled electronically and used in the study of language and literature.|Example: Researchers used a massive corpora of online text to study the evolution of slang. 研究人员使用大量的在线文本语料库来研究俚语的演变。|Synonym: collection, compilation, dataset|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "fragment" or "sample")|Noun
