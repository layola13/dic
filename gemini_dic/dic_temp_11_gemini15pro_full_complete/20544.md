blabbier:/ˈblæbiər/| adj.|更爱闲聊的，更饶舌的|Comparative form of blabby: more blabby.  More prone to talk excessively, especially about unimportant matters.|She's become much blabbier since she started that new job. 她自从开始那份新工作后变得更爱闲聊了。|He's so blabbier now than he used to be. 他现在比以前健谈多了。|近义词：talkative, loquacious, garrulous|反义词：reticent, taciturn|词性:形容词
词源: From blab + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  Blab itself comes from Middle English blabben.
