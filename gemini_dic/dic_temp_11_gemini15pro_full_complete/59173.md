dysfunctionally:/dɪsˈfʌŋkʃənəli/
adv.
功能失调地，失常地
in a way that is not normal or does not work properly
The family was operating dysfunctionally due to a lack of communication.  这个家庭由于缺乏沟通而运作失常。
The team was so dysfunctionally organized that they couldn't meet a single deadline.  这个团队组织得如此混乱，以至于他们无法完成任何一个截止日期。
近义词: abnormally, improperly, erratically
反义词: normally, properly, healthily
词性: 副词

dysfunctional:/dɪsˈfʌŋkʃənəl/
adj.
功能失调的，失常的
not working normally or properly
The dysfunctional family was the subject of a documentary.  这个功能失调的家庭是纪录片的主题。
He grew up in a dysfunctional environment. 他在一个不正常的环境中长大。
近义词: broken, impaired, unhealthy
反义词: functional, healthy, working
词性: 形容词

dysfunction:/dɪsˈfʌŋkʃən/
n.
功能障碍，机能不良，失调
abnormality or impairment in the function of a specified bodily organ or system
Erectile dysfunction is a common problem among men.  勃起功能障碍是男性中的常见问题。
The patient exhibited signs of cognitive dysfunction.  该病人表现出认知功能障碍的迹象。
近义词: malfunction, disorder, impairment
反义词: function, health, normality
词性: 名词

词源:  "dys-" (Greek: bad, impaired) + "function"  (Latin: functio, performance)
