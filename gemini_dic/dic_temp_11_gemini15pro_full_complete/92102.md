hyperrhythmical:/ˌhaɪpərˈrɪðmɪkəl/
adjective
超节律的，极有节奏感的
Characterized by a very fast or irregular rhythm.
Her hyperrhythmical heartbeat was a cause for concern. 她心跳过快且不规律，令人担忧。
The hyperrhythmical drumming captivated the audience. 极有节奏感的鼓声吸引了观众。
近义词: arrhythmic, irregular, fast-paced
反义词: rhythmic, regular, steady
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (meaning "over, excessive") + rhythmical (from rhythm, ultimately from Greek rhythmos "measured motion, time, proportion").
