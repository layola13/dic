impressing:/ɪmˈprɛsɪŋ/| v. | 给…留下深刻印象；使铭记 | Making someone feel admiration or respect.| Her knowledge of the subject impressed me. 她对这门学科的了解给我留下了深刻印象. | synonyms: influencing, affecting, swaying | antonyms:  disappointing, disheartening | verb (present participle)

impressing:/ɪmˈprɛsɪŋ/| adj. |  给人深刻印象的;  感人的|  making a strong or vivid impression; producing a strong effect |  The sheer size of the building was impressing. 这栋建筑的庞大规模令人印象深刻. | synonyms:  striking, remarkable, memorable | antonyms:  unremarkable, unimpressive | adjective (present participle)
