debilitate:/dɪˈbɪlɪteɪt/
verb
使衰弱；削弱
to make someone or something physically weak
The illness debilitated him for months.  这种病使他虚弱了好几个月。

近义词:weaken, enfeeble, incapacitate
反义词:strengthen, invigorate, fortify
词性:verb
