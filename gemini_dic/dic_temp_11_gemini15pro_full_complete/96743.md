ineligibly:/ɪnˈɛlɪdʒɪbli/
adverb
不合格地，无资格地
in a way that makes someone or something not eligible
His name was written ineligibly on the form. 他的名字在表格上写得难以辨认，导致不合格。
近义词: illegibly, unacceptably, improperly
反义词: legibly, acceptably, properly
词性: adverb

词源:
From in- + eligible + -ly.  "Eligible" comes from the Old French "eligible" and ultimately from the Latin "eligere" (to elect, choose).  The prefix "in-" negates the meaning.
