nonstorages:/ˈnɒnˈstɔːrɪdʒɪz/
|n.| 非存储，非存储器| Areas designated as nonstorages are crucial for maintaining clear pathways. 指定为非存储区的区域对于保持通道畅通至关重要。|Nonstorages are often overlooked in warehouse design. 在仓库设计中，非存储区常常被忽视。|近义词: non-storage areas, open areas, clear zones|反义词: storage areas, storage facilities, warehouses|词性: 名词

Etymology:
"Non-" (prefix): From Latin "non" meaning "not".
"Storage": From Old French "estorage" meaning "storehouse".  Ultimately from Latin "stare" "to stand".
