odists:/ˈəʊdɪsts/
noun| 憎恨者；厌恶者|People who feel intense dislike or hatred towards something or someone.|The odists of modern art protested the new exhibition. 现代艺术的憎恨者抗议了新的展览。|近义词：haters, detractors, opponents|反义词:lovers, admirers, supporters|词性:n.

Etymology:
From Greek *odeo*  "I hate" + -ist
