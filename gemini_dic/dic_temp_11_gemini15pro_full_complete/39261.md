congregativenesses:/kənˌɡrɛɡəˈtɪvnəsɪz/
n.
聚集性，群居性
The state or quality of being congregative; a tendency to congregate.
The congregativenesses of certain animal species, like meerkats, contribute to their survival.  某些动物物种，如猫鼬，它们的群居性有助于它们的生存。
Synonym: sociability, gregariousness
Antonym: solitariness, aloofness
词性: 名词
