halsening:/ˈhɑːlsnɪŋ/ (Swedish)
n.
greeting, salutation
A halsening is a greeting or salutation, typically used in Swedish.
Example:  Han gav mig en varm halsening. = He gave me a warm greeting.

Example:  Hon skickade en halsening till sin familj. = She sent a greeting to her family.

近义词: greeting, salutation, welcome
反义词: farewell, goodbye
词性: noun
