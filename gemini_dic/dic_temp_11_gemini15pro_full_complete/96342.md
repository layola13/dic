indigos:/ˈɪndɪɡoʊz/
n.
靛蓝;靛蓝色染料; 
A dark blue color, like that of the indigo dye.
例句：Her dress was a deep indigo. 她的裙子是深靛蓝色的。

例句：The artist used natural indigos in his paintings.  这位艺术家在他的绘画中使用了天然靛蓝颜料。

近义词：blues,  sapphires
反义词：reds, yellows
词性：名词(Noun)

词源: From Spanish *índico*, from Latin *indicum*, neuter of *indicus* (“Indian”), from *India* (“India”).  The dye was originally imported from India.
