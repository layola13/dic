backhoes:/ˈbækˌhoʊz/ (US), /ˈbækˌhəʊz/ (UK)| n.|反铲挖掘机|A backhoe is a type of excavating equipment, or digger, consisting of a digging bucket on the end of a two-part articulated arm. It is typically mounted on the back of a tractor or front loader.  The word "backhoe" refers to the action of the bucket, digging by drawing earth backwards, rather than lifting it with a forward motion.|The construction crew used a backhoe to dig the foundation for the new building. 建筑施工队用反铲挖掘机挖新建筑的地基。

The backhoe efficiently cleared the debris after the storm. 反铲挖掘机在风暴过后有效地清除了碎片。
