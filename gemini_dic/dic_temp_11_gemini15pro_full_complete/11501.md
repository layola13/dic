aridify:/əˈrɪdɪfaɪ/
verb
使干燥；使荒芜
To make or become arid; dry up.
The ongoing drought is continuing to aridify the region, turning fertile land into desert.  持续的干旱正在使该地区变得更加干旱，将肥沃的土地变成沙漠。
The climate change has aridified the land, making it difficult for crops to grow. 气候变化使土地变得干旱，作物难以生长。

近义词:desiccate, parch, dehydrate
反义词:moisten, irrigate, humidify
词性:v.
