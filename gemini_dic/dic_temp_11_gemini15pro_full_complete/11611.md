arm-twistings:/ˈɑːrmˌtwɪstɪŋz/
noun
施压；强迫；劝说
The act of trying to persuade someone to do something they do not want to do, by using strong arguments or by threatening them.
He finally agreed to do it after a lot of arm-twisting.  经过一番强迫之后，他最终同意了。
pressure, coercion, persuasion
cooperation, willingness
n.
