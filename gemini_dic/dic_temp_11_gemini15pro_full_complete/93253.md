ikebana:/ɪˈkɑːnə/ | n. |插花；花道|The Japanese art of flower arrangement, also known as kadō. It is a disciplined art form in which nature and humanity are brought together.  |例句：She has been studying ikebana for many years. 她学习花道很多年了。

例句：The ikebana arrangement was a beautiful centerpiece for the table.  花道作品是餐桌上美丽的中心装饰。|近义词：flower arrangement, floral design|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology: From Japanese 生け花 (ikebana), from 生ける (ikeru, “to arrange (flowers); to keep alive”) + 花 (hana, “flower”).
