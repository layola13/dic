outwiggle: /ˌaʊtˈwɪɡəl/
verb
胜过…的扭动；比…扭动得更厉害
To wiggle or wriggle more or better than someone or something else.
The baby outwiggled his older brother in the dance competition.  这个婴儿在舞蹈比赛中扭动得比他哥哥更厉害。
Her fish outwiggled all the others to get the food. 她的鱼比所有其他的鱼扭动得更厉害，从而得到了食物。
Synonyms: outmaneuver, surpass, beat, outperform
Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
word type: verb
