hyperresponsive:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈspɑːnsɪv/| adj.|过度反应的，高度敏感的|Excessively or abnormally responsive to stimuli.|例句：The child was hyperresponsive to loud noises.  这孩子对巨大的噪音过度反应。|例句：The patient is hyperresponsive to certain medications.  这个病人对某些药物高度敏感。|近义词：oversensitive, hypersensitive, reactive|反义词：hyposensitive, unresponsive, insensitive|词性:形容词
词源: hyper- (过度) + responsive (反应的)  "hyper-" comes from Greek meaning "over, beyond, excessive." "Responsive" comes from Latin "respondere" meaning "to answer."
