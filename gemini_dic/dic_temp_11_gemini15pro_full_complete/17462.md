bearbind:/ˈberˌbaɪnd/| n. |旋花科植物；黑草莓；|A twining plant, such as bindweed, especially black bindweed (Fallopia vulgaris, syn. Polygonum vulgare), with heart-shaped leaves and small greenish-white flowers. It is a common weed. |The bearbind choked the garden flowers.  旋花扼住了花园里的花。|近义词：bindweed, convolvulus|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From 'bear' (probably referring to the rough, clinging nature of the plant) + 'bind' (meaning to tie or fasten).  The plant "binds" like a bear hug.
