closet:/ˈklɒzɪt/ (UK) /ˈklɑːzɪt/ (US)| n. adj.|衣橱，壁橱；密室，秘密的；私下的|A cupboard or small room used for storage, especially clothes. Can also be used figuratively to describe something kept secret or hidden.|She hung her dress in the closet. 她把连衣裙挂在衣橱里。|Synonyms: wardrobe, cupboard, cabinet|Antonyms: open, public|noun

closet:/ˈklɒzɪt/ (UK) /ˈklɑːzɪt/ (US)| adj.|秘密的，私下的；隐蔽的|Kept secret or hidden, not openly acknowledged.|He's a closet alcoholic. 他是个隐藏的酗酒者。|Synonyms: secret, hidden, private|Antonyms: open, public|adjective
