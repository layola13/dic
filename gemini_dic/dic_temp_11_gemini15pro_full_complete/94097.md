impavid:/ɪmˈpævɪd/| adj.|无畏的，勇敢的|fearless; undaunted|例句：The impavid soldier faced the enemy without flinching.  这位无畏的士兵毫不畏惧地面对敌人。

例句：She remained impavid in the face of danger.  面对危险，她依然毫不畏惧。|近义词：fearless, intrepid, dauntless, undaunted, valiant, courageous|反义词：fearful, timid, cowardly, apprehensive|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *inpavidus, from in- "not" + pavidus "fearful," from pavere "to be afraid."
