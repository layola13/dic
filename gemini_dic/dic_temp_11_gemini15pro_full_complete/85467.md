hasn't: /ˈhæzənt/ (hæz-uhnt)
|v.|没有|Contraction of "has not."  It indicates the third person singular present tense negative form of the verb "have."|She hasn't finished her homework yet. 她还没有完成她的家庭作业。|近义词: doesn't have, hasn't got|反义词: has|词性:动词(auxiliary verb - 辅助动词)

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He hasn't any money. = 他没有钱。
She hasn't a clue what's going on. = 她一点也不知道发生了什么事。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
This usage is incorrect. "Hasn't" cannot be used in present continuous.  Use "isn't having" instead.
He isn't having any trouble with the project. = 他做这个项目没有任何困难.
She isn't having a good time at the party. = 她在派对上玩得不太开心。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He hasn't eaten his lunch. = 他还没有吃午饭。
She hasn't seen that movie yet. = 她还没有看过那部电影。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
This usage is incorrect. "Hasn't" cannot be used in present perfect continuous. Use "hasn't been having" instead if expressing a state.  More commonly, a different verb would be used.
He hasn't been having any luck finding a job. = 他一直没有找到工作的运气.
She hasn't been having any problems with her new car. = 她的新车一直没有任何问题。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
This usage is incorrect. Use "didn't have."
He didn't have any money yesterday. = 他昨天没有钱。
She didn't have a car back then. = 那时候她没有车。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
This usage is incorrect. Use "wasn't having."
He wasn't having any fun at the party. = 他在派对上玩得不开心.
She wasn't having any trouble with the assignment. = 她做作业没有任何困难。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
This usage is incorrect. Use "hadn't had."
He hadn't had breakfast before he left. = 他离开前还没有吃早餐。
She hadn't had a chance to speak to him. = 她还没有机会和他说话。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
This usage is incorrect. Use “hadn’t been having.”
He hadn't been having any problems with his neighbors until recently. = 直到最近，他与邻居一直没有发生任何问题。
She hadn't been having much luck finding an apartment. = 她一直没怎么找到公寓。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
This usage requires restructuring. Consider using “won’t have.”
He won't have any time tomorrow. = 他明天没有时间。
She won't have the necessary skills for the job. = 她将不具备这份工作所需的技能。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
This usage requires restructuring.
He won't be having any visitors next week. = 他下周不会有访客。
She won't be having any time off work next month. = 她下个月没有休假时间。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
This usage requires restructuring.
He won't have finished his work by Friday. = 他到星期五不会完成他的工作。
She won't have graduated by next summer. = 到明年夏天，她将不会毕业。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
This usage requires restructuring. Consider a form like "won't have been having". This tense is rarely used in negative contractions with "have."
By next week, she won’t have been having any contact with him for a month. = 到下周，她将有一个月没有和他联系了。
By the end of the year, he won’t have been having any issues with his car. = 到年底，他的车将没有任何问题。


虚拟语气:
If she hasn't called by now, she probably won't come. = 如果她现在还没打电话，她可能不会来了。
I wish he hasn't spent all his money. = 我希望他没有花光所有的钱。

被动语态:
This is generally incorrect with "hasn't."  Passive voice usually requires a form of "be" with the past participle. The closest example may be something like:
The decision hasn't been made yet. = 尚未作出决定.
The work hasn't been completed. = 工作还没有完成.


疑问句:
Hasn't she finished her work yet? = 她还没完成工作吗？
Hasn't he arrived? = 他还没到吗？

否定句:
He hasn't gone to school today. = 他今天没去上学。
She hasn't read that book. = 她没有读过那本书。

PHRASE->

It hasn't been easy. = 这并不容易。
He hasn't got a chance. = 他没有机会。
