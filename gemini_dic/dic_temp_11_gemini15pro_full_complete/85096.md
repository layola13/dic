hardboards:/ˈhɑːrdbɔːrdz/
n.
硬纸板，纤维板
A stiff board made of compressed wood fibers.
The shelves were made of hardboard.  这些架子是用硬纸板做的。
近义词：fiberboard, chipboard, pressboard
反义词：(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑softwood, plywood等表示不同材质的词)
词性: 名词
