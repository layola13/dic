beareth:/ˈbɛərɪθ/| v.|承受，忍受，承担，结果实，生育|This is an archaic form of the third-person singular present tense of the verb "bear." It means "carries," "supports," "endures," "produces," or "gives birth to."|The tree beareth much fruit. 这棵树结果很多。|近义词：carries, supports, endures, produces, yields|反义词:drops, fails, avoids, refuses|词性:动词
