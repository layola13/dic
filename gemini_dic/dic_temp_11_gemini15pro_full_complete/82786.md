guiltylike: /ˈɡɪltiˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 内疚似的，像是做了错事一样 |  Resembling or suggestive of guilt; having the appearance of someone who has done something wrong. | He had a guiltylike expression on his face after breaking the vase.  他打碎花瓶后脸上带着一副内疚的表情。 | 近义词: ashamed, sheepish, remorseful | 反义词: innocent, unashamed, brazen | 词性: 形容词

词源:  A combination of "guilty" and the suffix "-like," meaning "resembling" or "having the characteristics of." "Guilty" comes from the Old English "gyltig," meaning "liable to payment of a fine."
