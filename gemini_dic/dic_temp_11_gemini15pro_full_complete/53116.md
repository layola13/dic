dipleidoscope:/daɪˈpliːɪdəˌskoʊp/ /daɪˈpliːɪdəskəʊp/| n. |双影日晷|An instrument that uses two images of the sun, one reflected from still water and the other from a mirror, to determine the precise time of apparent noon.|Astronomers used a dipleidoscope to accurately determine midday. 天文学家使用双影日晷来精确地确定正午。|近义词: sundial, heliometer|反义词: None|noun
