berserk: /bərˈsɜːrk/ (US), /bəˈzɜːk/ (UK)| n.|狂暴状态|A state of violent or destructive rage.|例句：He flew into a berserk rage and started smashing things. 他勃然大怒，开始砸东西。|近义词：frenzy, rage, fury|反义词：calm, peace, tranquility|词性:名词

词源：From Old Norse *berserkr, from bjǫrn "bear" + serkr "shirt, coat of mail". Originally referred to Norse warriors who fought with frenzied rage, possibly under the influence of drugs or a form of self-induced hysteria.
