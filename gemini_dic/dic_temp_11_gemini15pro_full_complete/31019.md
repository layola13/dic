challenging:/ˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋ/| adj.|挑战性的，考验人的，有挑战性的|Stimulating, demanding, and testing one's abilities.|Climbing Mount Everest is a challenging experience. 攀登珠穆朗玛峰是一项具有挑战性的经历。|近义词：difficult, demanding, arduous, strenuous, tough|反义词：easy, simple, straightforward|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the verb "challenge," which comes from Old French "chalengier" (to accuse, claim, dispute), ultimately from Latin "calumniare" (to accuse falsely).
