crumpliest:/ˈkrʌmpliɪst/
adjective
最皱的
most wrinkled; having the most wrinkles.
The crumpliest shirt I've ever seen. 我见过最皱的衬衫。
近义词: most wrinkled, most creased
反义词: smoothest, flattest
词性: adjective
词源:  "crumple" (from Old English "crump") + "-y" (adjective suffix) + "-est" (superlative suffix)
