ochreous:/ˈoʊkəriəs/| adj.|赭色的，土黄色的|Of a pale brownish-yellow color; resembling ochre.|The walls were painted an ochreous hue, reminiscent of the desert sands.|近义词：yellowish-brown, buff, sandy, tan|反义词：blue, green|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *ochreus, from Latin ōchra (“ochre”), from Ancient Greek ὤχρα (ṓkhra, “pale yellow”).
