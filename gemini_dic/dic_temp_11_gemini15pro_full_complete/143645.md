outkilled:/ˌaʊtˈkɪld/
verb
比...杀死更多(敌人)
To kill more (enemies) than someone else.
例句：Team A outkilled Team B by a significant margin in the final match.  A队在决赛中比B队杀死了更多的敌人。

例句：The experienced hunter outkilled all the novices in the competition.  经验丰富的猎人在比赛中比所有新手杀死的猎物都多。
近义词：outmatched, surpassed, defeated
反义词：lost to, was defeated by
词性:v.
