lockjaw:/ˈlɒkˌdʒɔː/ (UK) /ˈlɑːkˌdʒɔː/ (US)| n.|破伤风；牙关紧闭|A serious bacterial infection that causes painful muscle spasms, especially in the jaw and neck.|Example 1:  The doctor diagnosed him with lockjaw after he stepped on a rusty nail. 医生在他踩到生锈的钉子后诊断他患有破伤风。

Example 2:  Lockjaw can be fatal if left untreated.  如果不及时治疗，破伤风可能会致命。|近义词：tetanus|反义词: None|词性:noun


Etymology:
From "lock" (referring to the locking of the jaw) + "jaw".  The term describes the prominent symptom of tetanus.
