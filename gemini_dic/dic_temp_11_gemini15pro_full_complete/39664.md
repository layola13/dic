conservatorial:/kənˌsɜːrvəˈtɔːriəl/| adj.|音乐学院的；温室的；保护的|Of, relating to, or characteristic of a conservatory, especially a music conservatory. Also, relating to or intended for conservation. |例句：The conservatorial training provided a strong foundation for her musical career.  音乐学院的训练为她的音乐生涯奠定了坚实的基础。

例句：The conservatorial approach to wildlife management aims to protect endangered species.  保护性的野生动物管理方法旨在保护濒危物种。|近义词：protective, preserving, academic (for music), greenhouse (for plants)|反义词：destructive, damaging|词性:adjective
