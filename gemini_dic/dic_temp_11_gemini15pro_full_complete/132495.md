nondesignative:/ˌnɒnˈdezɪɡnətɪv/ | adj. | 非指示的，非特指的 | Not serving to designate or specify; not restrictive or limiting. |  This term is nondesignative and can refer to a wide range of things. 这个术语是非指示性的，可以指代广泛的事物。 |  The use of "they" as a singular pronoun is becoming increasingly nondesignative.  使用“they”作为单数代词正变得越来越不限制性别。 | Synonyms: general, generic, nonspecific | Antonyms: specific, designative, particular | adjective
