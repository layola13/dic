machine-made:/məˈʃiːnˈmeɪd/ | adj. |机器制造的，机制的 | Made by a machine, rather than by hand. |  This carpet is machine-made, so it's less expensive than a handmade one. 这块地毯是机器制造的，所以比手工制作的便宜。

| Example: Machine-made goods are often cheaper than handcrafted items. 机器制造的商品通常比手工制品便宜。| Synonym: factory-made, mass-produced | Antonym: handmade, handcrafted | adjective
