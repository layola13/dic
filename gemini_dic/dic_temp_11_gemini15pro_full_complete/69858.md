ferromagnetically:/ˌfɛroʊmæɡˈnɛtɪkli/| adv. | 铁磁地 | In a way that exhibits ferromagnetism; exhibiting or relating to ferromagnetism.  A property of certain materials that are strongly attracted to magnets and can retain their magnetic properties after being exposed to a magnetic field. | The material behaves ferromagnetically below the Curie temperature.  这种材料在居里温度以下表现出铁磁性。| The compass needle aligned ferromagnetically with the Earth's magnetic field. 指南针的指针与地球磁场铁磁性地对齐。 | Synonyms: magnetically (in a broader sense) | Antonyms: diamagnetically, paramagnetically | adverb
