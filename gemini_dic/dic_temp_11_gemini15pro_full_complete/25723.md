buntings:/ˈbʌntɪŋz/ | n. | 彩旗；燕雀类鸣鸟 | Small flags or pieces of brightly colored cloth used for decoration, especially at festivals and parties; also, a group of small, seed-eating songbirds. | The street was decorated with colorful buntings for the festival. 街道上装饰着五彩缤纷的彩旗，迎接节日。| Synonyms: banners, flags, streamers (for decorative buntings); finches, sparrows (for bird buntings) | Antonyms: None | noun

buntings:/ˈbʌntɪŋz/ | n. | 燕雀 | Any of a number of small, seed-eating songbirds, typically having short, conical bills and brightly colored plumage.  | We saw a flock of buntings in the garden. 我们在花园里看到了一群燕雀。| Synonyms: finches, sparrows | Antonyms: None | noun
