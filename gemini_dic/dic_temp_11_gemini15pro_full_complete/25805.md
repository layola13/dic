burgeoning:/ˈbɜːrdʒənɪŋ/
adjective
迅速发展的，蓬勃发展的
Beginning to grow or increase rapidly; flourishing.
The company is experiencing burgeoning sales in the Asian market.  这家公司在亚洲市场的销售额正在迅速增长。

The burgeoning population puts a strain on resources.  迅速增长的人口给资源带来了压力。

近义词: expanding, growing, flourishing, thriving, mushrooming
反义词: shrinking, declining, dwindling, contracting
词性: adjective

词源:  From the Middle English word *burjonen*, meaning "to bud," from Old French *burjoner*. Ultimately from Vulgar Latin *burrionare*, from Latin *burrio* "a sprout, shoot."
