down-payment:/ˌdaʊnˈpeɪmənt/| n.|预付款，首付|An initial payment made when something is bought on credit. The rest of the amount is usually paid in installments.|例句：They made a down payment of $10,000 on the house. 他们在房子上支付了1万美元的首付款。

例句：A larger down payment will reduce your monthly mortgage payments.  更高的首付会减少你的每月抵押贷款还款额。|近义词：deposit, initial payment|反义词: final payment|词性:noun
