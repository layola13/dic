auto-exposure:/ˌɔːtəʊ ɪkˈspəʊʒə(r)/ |n.|自动曝光|The automatic adjustment of the aperture and/or shutter speed of a camera to maintain correct exposure.|My camera has auto-exposure, so I don't need to worry about the settings in most situations. 我的相机有自动曝光功能，所以在大多数情况下我不需要担心设置。|The auto-exposure compensated perfectly for the changing light conditions. 自动曝光完美地补偿了不断变化的光线条件。|近义词:automatic exposure|反义词:manual exposure|词性:名词

Etymology:
auto- (Greek origin, meaning "self") + exposure (Latin origin, meaning "a setting forth")
