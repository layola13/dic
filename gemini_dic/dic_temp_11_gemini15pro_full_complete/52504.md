differencing:/ˈdɪfərənsɪŋ/
n.|差分，求差|The process of finding the difference between two values or sets of data.  This is commonly used in time series analysis to identify trends and seasonality.|Economists use differencing to analyze changes in economic indicators over time. 经济学家使用差分来分析经济指标随时间的变化。|synonyms: subtraction, deduction|antonyms: addition, summation|noun

differencing:/ˈdɪfərənsɪŋ/
adj.|差分的，作差分的|Relating to or involving the process of finding differences.|Differencing techniques are crucial in various fields, including mathematics and computer science. 差分技术在包括数学和计算机科学在内的各个领域都至关重要。|synonyms: subtractive|antonyms: additive|adjective
