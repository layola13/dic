blacksmiths:/ˈblækˌsmɪθs/ /ˈblækˌsmɪðz/| n.|铁匠（复数）|A person who makes and repairs things made of iron, especially horseshoes.  A blacksmith traditionally works by heating pieces of iron in a forge and shaping them with a hammer and anvil.|The blacksmiths worked diligently, shaping the glowing metal. 铁匠们辛勤地工作，塑造着发光的金属。|The village blacksmiths were essential to the community. 村里的铁匠对社区至关重要。|近义词：metalworker, farrier|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑与其工作相反的职业，例如software developer)|词性:noun
