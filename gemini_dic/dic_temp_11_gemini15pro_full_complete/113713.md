macrobenthic:/ˌmækrəʊˈbɛnθɪk/ | adj. | 大型底栖生物的 |  Relating to or denoting the flora and fauna found on the bottom of a body of water, and large enough to be visible without magnification. |  The study focused on the macrobenthic community structure of the river. 这项研究集中于河流的大型底栖生物群落结构。|  benthic, macrofauna |  - | adjective
macrobenthos:/ˌmækrəʊˈbɛnθɒs/ | n. | 大型底栖生物 | The large, usually invertebrate, animals that live on or in the seabed. | The macrobenthos of the lake was diverse and abundant.  这个湖的大型底栖生物种类繁多且数量丰富。| benthos, macrofauna | - | noun
