lamella:/ləˈmɛlə/| n.|薄片，薄板， lamella (plural lamellae or lamellas)|A thin plate, scale, or layer, especially in bone tissue or gills.|The gills of a fish are composed of numerous lamellae. 鱼的鳃是由许多薄片组成的。|The lamellae of bone tissue provide strength and flexibility. 骨组织的薄片提供了强度和灵活性。|plate, layer, scale|thickness|noun
