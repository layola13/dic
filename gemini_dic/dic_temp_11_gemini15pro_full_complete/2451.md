adjuratory:/ˌædʒʊˈreɪtəri/
adjective
恳求的；誓言的；起誓的
Relating to or containing an adjuration; expressing earnest entreaty.
The defendant gave an adjuratory statement, swearing his innocence. 被告做了一个恳求的陈述，发誓他是无辜的。
The priest delivered an adjuratory sermon, urging the congregation to repent. 牧师发表了一篇恳求的布道，敦促会众忏悔。
Synonyms: beseeching, imploring, supplicatory, entreating
Antonyms: demanding, commanding, dictatorial
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin adjuratorius, from Latin adjurare ("to swear to"), from ad- ("to") + jurare ("to swear").
