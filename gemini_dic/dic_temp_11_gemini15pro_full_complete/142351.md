orthokinesis:/ˌɔːrθoʊkɪˈniːsɪs/
n.|正趋性|The movement of an organism in response to a stimulus, with the speed of the movement being directly proportional to the intensity of the stimulus.  An organism moves faster in unfavorable conditions and slower in favorable conditions.|Woodlice exhibit orthokinesis, moving faster in dry areas and slower in humid ones.  潮虫表现出正趋性，在干燥的地方移动得更快，在潮湿的地方移动得更慢。|kinesis, orthotaxis|—|noun
