feedbacking:/ˈfiːdbækɪŋ/
verb
反馈；提供反馈
The process of giving feedback.  This is often considered a gerund or present participle used as a noun.
We are feedbacking on the new proposals. 我们正在就新提案提供反馈意见。
The team spent an hour feedbacking to the manager. 团队花了一个小时向经理反馈。
synonyms: responding, reacting, reviewing, commenting, reporting
antonyms: ignoring, disregarding
