let rip:/lɛt rɪp/
verb phrase
突然释放；尽情发泄；火力全开
to suddenly release something forcefully; to do something with great energy and enthusiasm
He let rip with a volley of angry words. 他突然发泄出一连串愤怒的话语。
The band let rip with a powerful performance. 乐队火力全开的表演燃爆全场。
近义词: unleash, let loose, let fly, go all out
反义词: hold back, restrain, suppress
词性: 动词短语

词源:  "Let rip" originates from the nautical term "let rip a sail," meaning to release a sail quickly. This sense of sudden release then broadened to encompass other forms of unrestrained action or expression.
