overpessimistically: /ˌoʊvərˌpɛsɪˈmɪstɪkli/
| adv. | 过于悲观地 | In an excessively pessimistic manner; with undue negativity. | He overpessimistically predicted the company's imminent demise. 他过于悲观地预测了公司即将倒闭。| Example: She overpessimistically assessed the situation, assuming the worst possible outcome. 她过于悲观地评估了形势，设想了最坏的结果。|Synonyms: unduly pessimistically, excessively negatively, too gloomily | Antonyms: optimistically, hopefully, positively | 词性: 副词


Etymology:
over- (prefix): excessive, too much
pessimistic (adjective): tending to see the worst aspect of things or believe that the worst will happen
-ally (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
