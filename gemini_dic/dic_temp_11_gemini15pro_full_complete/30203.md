celidography:/ˌsiːlɪˈdɒɡrəfi/ | n. |  腹腔镜检查术；腹腔镜描记术 | The description of the abdominal cavity.  It also refers to the surgical procedure of examining the abdominal cavity using a laparoscope. |  Celidography was performed to assess the extent of the damage.  腹腔镜检查被用来评估损伤的程度。 | Synonym: laparoscopy | Antonym: N/A | noun
