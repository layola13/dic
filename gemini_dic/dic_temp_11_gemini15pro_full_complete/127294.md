navigational:/ˌnævɪˈɡeɪʃənəl/
adjective
导航的；航行的
Of or relating to navigation; concerned with the determination of position and direction.
The ship had sophisticated navigational equipment. 这艘船拥有先进的导航设备。
Synonyms: directional, guiding, piloting
Antonyms: lost, adrift
词性: 形容词
