cymbling:/ˈsɪmblɪŋ/
n.
小钟，铃铛，铙钹
A small bell, cymbal, or a similar tinkling instrument.
The cymbling announced the start of the ceremony. 小钟宣布仪式开始。
The dancer wore cymblings on her ankles. 舞蹈演员的脚踝上戴着铃铛。

近义词:bell, chime, cymbal, jingle
反义词:silence

词性:noun

词源:From Middle English *simblyng, *cimblyng, from Old French cimbal and Latin cymbalum (“cymbal”).
