fondues:/fɒnˈdjuːz/ (UK), /fɑːnˈduːz/ (US)| n.|火锅，奶酪火锅|A dish in which pieces of food are dipped into a hot sauce or melted cheese.|例句：We had a cheese fondue for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了奶酪火锅。|例句：The fondue was bubbling away on the table. 火锅在桌上咕嘟咕嘟地冒着泡。|近义词：dip, hotpot|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
