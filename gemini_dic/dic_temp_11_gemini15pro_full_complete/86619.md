heliozoa:/ˌhiːliəˈzoʊə/
n.
太阳虫类
Microscopic, spherical freshwater protozoans with stiff radiating pseudopods (axopodia) supported by microtubules and used for capturing food.
Heliozoa are fascinating creatures to observe under a microscope. 在显微镜下观察太阳虫是件令人着迷的事情。
近义词:sun animalcule
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially other protists like amoebas or paramecia as contrasting examples)
词性:noun
