millineries:/ˈmɪlɪnəriːz/
n.
女帽; 女帽业; 女帽店;
Women's hats; the business of designing, making, or selling women's hats; a shop that sells women's hats.
The millineries on the street displayed a wide array of stylish hats. 街上的女帽店展示了各种各样的时尚帽子。
She inherited her grandmother's millineries business. 她继承了祖母的女帽生意。
近义词:hat shops, hat stores
反义词:None (No direct antonym for a specialized shop)
词性:Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *millinerie*,  from *milliner* (originally meaning a dealer in Milan goods, especially silks and hats), which comes from *Milan*, the Italian city.
