overfreighted: /ˌoʊvərˈfreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过载的，装载过重的 | overloaded with freight; carrying too much cargo. | The ship was overfreighted and sank in the storm. 这艘船装载过重，在暴风雨中沉没了。|  The truck was overfreighted and struggled up the hill.  这辆卡车装载过重，费力地爬上山坡。 | 近义词: overloaded, overburdened | 反义词: underloaded | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "freight" (noun meaning "goods transported by a carrier") + "-ed" (suffix forming past participles and adjectives).
