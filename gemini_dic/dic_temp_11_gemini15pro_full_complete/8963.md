antihygienically:/ˌæntihaɪdʒiˈɛnɪkli/ | adv. |不卫生地，违反卫生地 |  In a manner that is contrary to the principles of hygiene; uncleanly. | He prepared the food antihygienically, leading to food poisoning. 他准备食物的方式很不卫生，导致了食物中毒。|The raw meat was stored antihygienically next to the vegetables. 生肉不卫生地理放在蔬菜旁边。| synonym: unsanitarily, unhealthily | antonym: hygienically, sanitarily | adverb
