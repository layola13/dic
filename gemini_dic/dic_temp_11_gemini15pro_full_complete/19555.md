bifurcous:/baɪˈfɜːrkəs/| adj.|分叉的，分两股的|Divided into two branches or forks.|The bifurcous root of the tree provided a stable base.|近义词：forked, branched, divided|反义词:unified, single, joint|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Latin "bifurcus" (two-forked), from "bi-" (two) + "furca" (fork).
