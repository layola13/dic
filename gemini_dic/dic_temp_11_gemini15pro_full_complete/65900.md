evulgate:/ˈiːvʌlɡeɪt/ /ˈɛvʌlɡeɪt/ | vt. | 泄露，传播，公布 | To make something publicly known, especially something that was previously secret or private. |  The company evulgated the news of the merger. 公司公布了合并的消息。

The scientist was reluctant to evulgate his findings before they were peer-reviewed.  这位科学家不愿在同行评审之前公布他的发现。

| 近义词：divulge, disclose, reveal, publish,  | 反义词：conceal, hide, suppress | 词性: 动词
