fumigate:/ˈfjuːmɪɡeɪt/| v. |熏蒸，烟熏消毒|To apply fumes, especially of certain chemicals, to (an area) for the purpose of disinfecting or ridding it of vermin.|例句：They fumigated the house to get rid of the termites. 他们熏蒸房子以清除白蚁。

例句：The warehouse was fumigated after the discovery of rats.  仓库在发现老鼠后进行了熏蒸。|近义词：disinfect, sterilize, sanitize|反义词: contaminate, infect|词性: verb


Etymology: From Latin *fumigare*  "to smoke, fumigate," from *fumus* "smoke."
