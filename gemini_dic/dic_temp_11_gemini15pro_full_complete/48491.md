deepens:/ˈdiːpənz/
verb
加深，深化
makes something deeper or more profound.
The river deepens as it flows towards the sea.  河流在流向大海的过程中逐渐加深。
His voice deepened with emotion. 他的声音因激动而变得低沉。
intensifies, strengthens, enhances
lessens, weakens, decreases
