bibliolater:/ˈbɪblɪɒleɪtər/ (US) /ˈbɪbliːɒleɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |书籍崇拜者；过度尊崇书籍的人|A person who excessively reveres or worships books, often to the point of prioritizing them over practical experience or reason.|He was a bibliolater, surrounding himself with stacks of books but rarely venturing out into the real world. 他是一个书籍崇拜者，周围堆满了书，但很少涉足现实世界。|近义词：bookworm, bibliophile|反义词：philistine|词性:noun
