bacillar:/bəˈsɪlər/| adj.|杆菌的，杆状的|Relating to, caused by, or containing bacilli (rod-shaped bacteria).|例句：Bacillar dysentery is a type of dysentery caused by bacilli. 杆菌性痢疾是一种由杆菌引起的痢疾。

例句：The doctor identified a bacillar infection in the patient's blood sample. 医生在病人的血液样本中发现了杆菌感染。|近义词：bacterial, rod-shaped|反义词:coccal (referring to spherical bacteria)|词性:adjective
