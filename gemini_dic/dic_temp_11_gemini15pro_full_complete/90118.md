hot-tempered:/ˌhɒtˈtɛmpəd/ /ˌhɑːtˈtɛmpərd/| adj.|易怒的，暴躁的|Easily angered; quick to lose one's temper; irascible.|He's known for being hot-tempered, so it's best to avoid arguing with him.  他以脾气暴躁而闻名，所以最好避免和他争吵。

She has a hot-tempered personality and can flare up at the slightest provocation. 她性格暴躁，稍有挑衅就会勃然大怒。|近义词：irascible, short-tempered, fiery, choleric, volatile|反义词：calm, patient, even-tempered, placid, serene|词性:形容词
