overglamourizes: /ˌoʊvərˈɡlæməˌraɪzɪz/
verb
过分美化，过分渲染
presents something as more glamorous or exciting than it actually is.
The movie overglamourizes the life of a rock star.  这部电影过分美化了摇滚明星的生活。
The advertisement overglamourizes the effects of the new diet pill.  这则广告过分渲染了这款新的减肥药的效果。
近义词: romanticizes, idealizes, glorifies
反义词: deglamourizes, downplays, understates
词性: verb


Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "glamour" (from Scottish "gramarye," meaning "magic, enchantment") + "-ize" (suffix forming verbs from nouns or adjectives)
