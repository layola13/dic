bodily:/ˈbɒdɪli/ (UK) /ˈbɑːdəli/ (US)| adj. | 身体的；肉体的 | Relating to the body, physical; in person, physically. | She suffered bodily harm in the accident.  她在事故中遭受了人身伤害。|  The king appeared bodily before his subjects. 国王亲自出现在他的臣民面前。 | 近义词: physical, corporal, somatic | 反义词: mental, spiritual | 词性: adjective
词源: From Middle English *bodili, *bodilych, from Old English bōdlic (“bodily, physical, material”), equivalent to bōd (“body”) +‎ -lic (“-ly”). Cognate with German *bodleich (Middle High German bōdelīch).
