miked /maɪkt/
verb
装上麦克风; 用麦克风录制
equipped or recorded with a microphone.
例句：The band miked the drums to get a better sound.  乐队给鼓装上麦克风以获得更好的声音。

例句：The singer was miked up before the performance. 歌手在表演前装上了麦克风。
近义词：amplified, recorded
反义词:unmiked, unaided
