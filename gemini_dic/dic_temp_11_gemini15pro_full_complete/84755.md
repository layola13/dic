handmade:/ˌhændˈmeɪd/| adj.|手工制作的；手工的|Made by hand, not by machine.|例句：This sweater is handmade.  这件毛衣是手工制作的。|例句：She sells handmade jewelry at the market. 她在市场上卖手工制作的珠宝。|近义词：handcrafted, artisanal|反义词：machine-made, factory-made|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From hand + made, the past participle of make.  The word has been in use since the late 16th century.
