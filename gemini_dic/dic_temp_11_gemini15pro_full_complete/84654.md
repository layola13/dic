handbasin: /ˈhændˌbeɪsɪn/ (UK) /ˈhændˌbeɪsn/ (US)
n.
洗手盆；洗脸盆
A bowl typically fixed to a wall or on a pedestal, used for washing one's hands and face.
She washed her hands in the handbasin. 她用洗手盆洗了手。
The handbasin was cracked and stained. 洗手盆有裂缝和污渍。

近义词: sink, washbasin, basin
反义词: (没有直接的反义词)
词性: 名词
