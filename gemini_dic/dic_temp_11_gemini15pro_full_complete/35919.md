coctile:/ˈkɒktaɪl/ /ˈkɑːktaɪl/| adj.|烧制的，烘制的；可烧制的|Baked, roasted, or capable of being baked or roasted.  Relating to or involving baking or roasting.|The coctile properties of clay make it suitable for pottery.  黏土的烧制特性使其适合制陶。|近义词：baked, roasted, fired|反义词: raw, uncooked|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *coctilis, from coctus (past participle of coquere ‘to cook’).
