appropriated:/əˈproʊprieɪtɪd/
verb
占用，挪用，拨款
past tense and past participle of appropriate: to take something for one's own use, typically without the owner's permission.
The government appropriated land for the new highway. 政府征用了土地来修建新的高速公路。
Congress appropriated $10 million for the project. 国会为该项目拨款1000万美元。
synonyms: seize, confiscate, annex, commandeer, usurp, expropriate
antonyms: return, relinquish, give back
v.
