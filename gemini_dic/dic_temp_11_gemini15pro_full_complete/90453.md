huanaco:/wɑːˈnɑːkoʊ/ |n.| 南美驼，原驼 |A wild South American ruminant mammal (Lama guanicoe), related to the llama and alpaca, having long, soft, reddish-brown fur.|  The huanaco is native to the Andes Mountains. 南美驼原产于安第斯山脉。|近义词:guanaco, llama (loosely)|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider domesticated camelids like alpaca or llama as conceptually opposite in terms of wildness.)|词性:noun

huanaco:/wɑːˈnɑːkoʊ/ |n.|原驼毛，原驼呢 |The wool or fabric made from the fur of a huanaco.| The coat was made of luxurious huanaco. 这件外套是用奢华的原驼毛制成的。|近义词:llama wool, alpaca wool (similar)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
