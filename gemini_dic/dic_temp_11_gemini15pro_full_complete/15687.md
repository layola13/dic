bakshish:/ˈbɑːkʃiːʃ/ /ˈbækʃiːʃ/
n.
小费，赏钱，贿赂
A tip or gratuity, especially in some Middle Eastern and Asian countries; also, a bribe.
He offered the porter a small bakshish to carry his bags. 他给了搬运工一点小费让他搬运行李。
tip, gratuity, reward, gift
bribe, extortion
noun
