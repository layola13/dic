immensurable:/ɪˈmɛnʃərəbəl/| adj.|不可估量的，无限的|Too great to be measured; limitless.|例句：The universe is immeasurable. 宇宙是浩瀚无垠的。|例句：Her contribution to the project was immeasurable. 她对这个项目的贡献是不可估量的。|近义词：incalculable, infinite, boundless, vast|反义词：measurable, finite, limited|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French incommensurable or late Latin immensurabilis, from in- ‘not’ + mensurabilis (from mensurare ‘to measure’).
