nonseasonably:/nɒnˈsiːzənəbli/ /nɑːnˈsiːzənəbli/
adjective
不合时宜地；反常地；不适时地
Not typical of the time of year; unseasonable.
The weather was nonseasonably warm for February.  二月的天气异常暖和。
It snowed nonseasonably in May. 五月不合时宜地下雪了。
近义词:unseasonable, untimely, inappropriate, unsuitable
反义词:seasonable, timely, appropriate, suitable
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"
seasonable (adjective):  suitable for the particular season
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
