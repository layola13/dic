lanthana:/ˈlænθənə/| n. |氧化镧|A white crystalline oxide of lanthanum.|Lanthana is used in some high-refractive-index glass formulations. 氧化镧用于某些高折射率玻璃配方中。|Synonyms: lanthanum oxide, La2O3|Antonyms: None|noun

lanthana:/ˈlænθənə/| n. |镧土|Historically, lanthana referred to a mixture of rare earth oxides, primarily lanthanum oxide.|The old texts refer to lanthana, but modern usage specifies individual rare earth oxides. 旧文献提到了镧土，但现代用法指定了单个稀土氧化物。|Synonyms: rare earth oxides (historical)|Antonyms: None|noun
