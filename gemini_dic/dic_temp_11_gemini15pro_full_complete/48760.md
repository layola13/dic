definitude:/ˈdɛfɪnɪtjuːd/
n.|确定性，限定性|The state or quality of being definite; definiteness.|The definitude of the legal terms provided clarity in the contract.  合同中法律术语的确定性使其更加清晰明了。|近义词：definiteness, precision, clarity, certainty|反义词：indefiniteness, vagueness, ambiguity, uncertainty|词性:名词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin *definitudo, from Latin definitus ‘defined’.
