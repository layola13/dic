overcautiousness:/ˌoʊvərˈkɔːʃəsnəs/| n. |过度谨慎，过分小心|The overcautiousness of the general led to a missed opportunity for a decisive victory. 将军的过度谨慎导致错失了决定性胜利的机会。|例句：Her overcautiousness prevented her from taking any risks. 她的过度谨慎使她不敢冒险。|近义词：carefulness, cautiousness, prudence, timidity|反义词：recklessness, impulsiveness, daring, boldness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From over- + cautiousness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much."  "Cautiousness" is the noun form of "cautious," deriving from the Latin "cautio" meaning "caution" or "provision against danger."
