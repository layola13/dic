overdignifies: /ˌoʊvərˈdɪɡnɪˌfaɪz/
verb
过分抬举；过分重视；过分尊崇
To treat (something) with more respect or seriousness than it deserves.
The ceremony overdignified the occasion.  这个仪式把这个场合搞得过于隆重了。
The lengthy introduction overdignified the speaker.  冗长的介绍过分抬举了演讲者。
近义词: exaggerate, inflate, overemphasize, overstate
反义词: downplay, minimize, underestimate, undervalue
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + dignify.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much." "Dignify" comes from the Latin word "dignitas" meaning "worth," "merit," "dignity."
