distinguish-by:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ baɪ/| v. | 通过...区分，凭借...辨别 | To differentiate or recognize something based on a particular characteristic or feature. |  例句：We can distinguish the twins by their hairstyles. 我们可以通过他们的发型来区分这对双胞胎。

例句：The expert could distinguish the real painting from the fake by the brushstrokes. 专家可以通过笔触区分真画和假画。

近义词：differentiate by, identify by, recognize by| 反义词：confuse, mistake |词性:动词
