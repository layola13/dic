ootooid:/ˈoʊ.tə.tɔɪd/
adjective
卵形的；卵状的
Resembling or shaped like an egg.
The ootooid grains were microscopic and ovoid in shape. 这些卵形粒是微观的，呈卵形。
近义词: ovoid, egg-shaped, oval
反义词:  amorphous, shapeless


Etymology:  From Greek *ōoeidēs* "egg-shaped," from *ōon* "egg" + *-eidēs*, adjectival suffix meaning "having the form of."
