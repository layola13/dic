overabounding:/ˌoʊvərəˈbaʊndɪŋ/
adjective
极其丰富的，过剩的
Existing or supplied in excessive abundance.
The garden was overabounding with fragrant roses.  花园里开满了芬芳的玫瑰。
The river was overabounding with fish. 河里鱼很多。
近义词: overflowing, superabundant, profuse, copious, exuberant
反义词: scarce, meager, deficient, lacking
词性: adjective

词源: From over + abounding, from abound, from Old French abonder, from Latin abundare "to overflow, abound," from ab- "from" (see ab-) + undare "to surge, overflow," from unda "wave."
