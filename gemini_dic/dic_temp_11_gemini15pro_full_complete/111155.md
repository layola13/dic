lithest:/ˈlaɪðɪst/| adj.|最轻盈的，最柔软的，最敏捷的|Most agile and graceful; easily bent.|例句：She was the lithest dancer in the troupe. 她是剧团里舞姿最轻盈的舞蹈演员。|近义词：agile, supple, nimble, graceful|反义词：stiff, clumsy, awkward|词性:形容词
词源：From Middle English *lithiest, superlative of lithi (“flexible, supple”), from Old English līðe (“gentle, mild; pleasant, agreeable; soft, mild, gentle; flexible, pliant”), from Proto-Germanic *linþjaz (“soft, yielding, weak”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)lento- (“pliant”). Cognate with Dutch lijdzaam (“patient, gentle”), German lind (“mild, soft”).
