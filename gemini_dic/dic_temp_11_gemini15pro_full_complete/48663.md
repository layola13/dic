defensively:/dɪˈfɛnsɪvli/
adverb|防御地，保护地|In a way that defends or protects someone or something.|He spoke defensively about his decision. 他为自己所做的决定进行辩护。|近义词:protectively, guardedly|反义词:offensively, aggressively|词性:副词
词源: From defense + -ive + -ly. Ultimately from Latin dēfendere.
