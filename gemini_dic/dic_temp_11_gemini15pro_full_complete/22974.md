bottony:/ˈbʌtəni/| adj.|像纽扣的；多纽扣的；钮扣状的|Resembling a button or buttons; having many buttons; shaped like a button.|The dress had a bottony design down the front. 这件连衣裙的正面有一个像纽扣一样的设计。|近义词：button-like, buttoned, studded|反义词: smooth, plain|词性:形容词

bottony:/ˈbʌtəni/| adj.|（植物学）花序密集的，头状花序的| (botany) Having flowers arranged in dense clusters resembling buttons.|The plant had small, bottony flowers. 这种植物开着小的、头状花序的花。|近义词：clustered, compact, dense|反义词: sparse, scattered|词性:形容词
