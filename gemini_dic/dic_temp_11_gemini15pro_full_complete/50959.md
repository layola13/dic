designers:/dɪˈzaɪnərz/ | n. | 设计师；设计者 | People who create and plan the look or workings of something. | The designers of the new car focused on fuel efficiency.  这款新车的设师注重燃油效率。| synonyms: creators, planners, architects, stylists | antonyms: users, consumers | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "design," which comes from the Old French "desseigner" (to mark out, designate), ultimately from the Latin "designare" (to mark out). The "-er" suffix denotes a person who performs the action of the verb.
