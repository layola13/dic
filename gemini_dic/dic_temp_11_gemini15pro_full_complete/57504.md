dragoman:/ˈdræɡəmən/ | n. | 拖曼，译员，通译 | An interpreter or guide, especially in countries speaking Arabic, Turkish, or Persian. | Example 1: The dragoman translated the sultan's words for the foreign diplomats.  苏丹的翻译员为外国外交官翻译了苏丹的话。

Example 2:  Hiring a dragoman is essential for navigating the bustling markets of Marrakech.  在熙熙攘攘的马拉喀什市场，雇佣一名翻译是必不可少的。 | 近义词: interpreter, translator, guide | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun


词源: From the medieval Latin *dragumannus, from the Arabic tarjumān, meaning "interpreter."
