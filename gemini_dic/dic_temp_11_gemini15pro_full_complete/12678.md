assentant:/əˈsɛntənt/| adj. n.| 同意的，赞成的；赞成者，同意者 | Agreeing; a person who agrees. |  The assentant murmur of the crowd indicated their approval.  人群赞同的低语表明了他们的认可。| 近义词: agreeing, approving, concurring, consenting; supporter,  advocate | 反义词: dissenting, disagreeing, opposing; opponent, objector | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *assentāns, present participle of assentāre (“to assent”), from Latin assentārī (“to assent to, agree with”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + sentīre (“to feel, think, perceive”).
