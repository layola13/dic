orgiasticism: /ˌɔːrdʒiˈæstɪsɪzəm/ /ˌɔːrdʒiˈæstɪˌsɪzəm/
n.
狂欢; 放荡; 恣意; 纵情
The state of being orgiastic; excessive indulgence in emotion or sensation.
The festival descended into orgiasticism as the night wore on.  随着夜幕降临，节日变成了狂欢。
His art is characterized by a kind of orgiasticism of color and form. 他的艺术以色彩和形式的某种狂欢为特征。
近义词：frenzy, revelry, bacchanalia, debauchery, excess
反义词：moderation, restraint, control, asceticism
词性：noun

Etymology:  From orgiastic + -ism.  Orgiastic from orgies + -astic.  Orgies from French orgies, from Latin orgia, from Greek orgia.
