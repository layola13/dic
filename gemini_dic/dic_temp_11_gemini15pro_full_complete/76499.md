galaxies:/ˈɡæləksiz/| n. |星系|A system of millions or billions of stars, together with gas and dust, held together by gravitational attraction. |例句：The Milky Way is our galaxy. 银河系是我们的星系。

例句：Astronomers study distant galaxies to learn about the universe. 天文学家研究遥远的星系以了解宇宙。|近义词：nebulae (in some contexts), star systems|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps void, nothingness)|词性:noun
