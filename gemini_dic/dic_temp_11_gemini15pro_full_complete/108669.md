leafs:/liːfs/
n.
树叶 (plural of leaf, less common)
The strong wind blew the leafs off the trees.
强风把树叶从树上吹落了。
The leafs of the oak tree are turning brown.
橡树的叶子正在变成褐色。
近义词:leaves
反义词:(None - Leafs refers to a physical object; it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性:noun


leafs:/liːfs/
v.
翻页；浏览 (third-person singular simple present tense of leaf)
He leafs through the magazine quickly.
他快速地翻阅杂志。
She leafs through the photo album, reminiscing.
她翻阅着相册，回忆往事。
近义词:browses, skims, flips
反义词:(None - Leafs as a verb describes an action, and doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *lēaf, of Germanic origin, from a base meaning ‘grow’; related to Dutch loof and German Laub.
