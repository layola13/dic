confiscable:/kənˈfɪskəbəl/ | adj. |可没收的，可充公的 |  Something that is confiscable can be legally taken away by the government or other authority. | The government declared that all illegal drugs were confiscable. 政府宣布所有非法毒品均可没收。| The court ordered that the criminal's assets were confiscable. 法院下令没收罪犯的资产。 | synonyms: seizable, forfeitable, appropriatable | antonyms: unseizable,  returnable | adjective
