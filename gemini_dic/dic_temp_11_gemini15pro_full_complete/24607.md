broil:/brɔɪl/ (US) /brɔɪl/ (UK)| v.| 烤；炙；灼热；被烤 |To cook food by exposing it to direct, intense heat, typically from above. To be subjected to intense heat. |例句：I'm going to broil some chicken for dinner. 我打算烤些鸡肉做晚餐。
