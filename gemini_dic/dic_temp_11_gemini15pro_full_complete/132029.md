noncorroborative:/ˌnɒnkəˈrɒbəreɪtɪv/
adjective
未经证实的，不支持的
Not providing supporting evidence; not corroborating.
The detective found some noncorroborative evidence at the crime scene.  侦探在犯罪现场发现了一些未经证实的证据。
His alibi was noncorroborative, leading the police to suspect him. 他的不在场证明未经证实，导致警方怀疑他。
近义词:unsubstantiated, unconfirmed, unsupported
反义词:corroborative, confirming, supporting
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix): not
corroborative (adjective): from Latin corroboratus, past participle of corroborare "to strengthen," from com- "together" + roborare "to strengthen," from robur "strength, oak."
