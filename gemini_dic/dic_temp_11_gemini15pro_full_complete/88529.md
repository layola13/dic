hit-it-off:/hɪt ɪt ɔːf/ /hɪt ɪt ɑːf/
phrasal verb
一拍即合；相处融洽
to quickly become good friends with someone
We hit it off immediately and have been friends ever since. 我们一见如故，从此成了朋友。
click, get along, connect
clash, disagree
