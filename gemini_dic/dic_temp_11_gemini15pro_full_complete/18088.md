behaviorally:/bɪˈheɪvjərəli/
| adv. |行为上地，行为方面地|
Relating to or concerned with behavior.  Pertaining to the way someone acts or conducts themselves.
|例句：The drug was shown to behaviorally modify the rats.  这种药物被证明可以改变老鼠的行为。|

|例句：Behaviorally, the children responded well to the new reward system. 从行为上看，孩子们对新的奖励制度反应良好。|
|近义词：externally, outwardly|
|反义词：internally, inherently|
|词性：副词|
