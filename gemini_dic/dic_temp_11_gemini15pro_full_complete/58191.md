ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *drousen, from Old English *drūsian (“to become sluggish, grow feeble”), from Proto-Germanic *drūsaną (“to be troubled, cloudy, muddy, drooping”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰrewh₁- (“to become muddy, troubled”). Related to dregs.
