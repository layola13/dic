admission:/ədˈmɪʃən/| n.|允许进入；承认；入场费；坦白|The act of allowing someone to enter a place or become a member of something; a confession or acknowledgment of something.|例句：Her admission to the university was a great achievement. 她被大学录取是一项伟大的成就。

例句：The admission fee to the museum is $10.  博物馆的门票是10美元。|近义词：entrance, entry, acceptance, confession, acknowledgment|反义词：rejection, denial, refusal|noun
