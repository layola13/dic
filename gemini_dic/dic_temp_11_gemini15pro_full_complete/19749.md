billeter's:/ˌbɪləˈtɪərz/ | n. |  售票员的（东西）; 钱包的; | Something that belongs to a billeter (ticket seller) or relates to a wallet. | The billeter's booth was closed. 售票亭关门了。|  近义词: ticket seller's, cashier's (depending on context), wallet owner's | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词 (possessive)

词源:

From French "billetier," meaning "ticket seller." The "-'s" suffix indicates possession. The word "billeter" itself is derived from "billet," meaning "ticket" or "note."
