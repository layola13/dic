intensest:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪst/| adj.|最强烈的，最紧张的|Superlative form of "intense," meaning of extreme degree or strength.|The intensest heat of the summer made it difficult to work outdoors. 夏天最酷热的时候使得在户外工作变得很困难。|The competition was at its intensest moment. 比赛正处于最激烈的时刻。|近义词：strongest, greatest, highest, utmost|反义词：weakest, mildest, slightest|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *intens*, from Late Latin *intensus* "stretched out, strained, violent," past participle of *intendere* "to stretch out, strain," from *in-* "toward" + *tendere* "to stretch."
