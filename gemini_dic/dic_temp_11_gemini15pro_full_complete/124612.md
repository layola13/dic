mousinesses:/ˈmaʊsɪnɪsɪz/
|n.|羞怯，胆怯；谨小慎微|The state or quality of being mousy; timidness or shyness. |Her mousiness made it difficult for her to speak up in class. 她的胆怯使她在课堂上难以发言。|Synonyms: timidity, shyness, meekness, diffidence|Antonyms: boldness, confidence, assertiveness|Noun

Etymology:
From mousy + -ness.  "Mousy" itself comes from "mouse," referring to the quiet, unobtrusive nature of a mouse.
