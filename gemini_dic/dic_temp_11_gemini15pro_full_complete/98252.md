inseparable:/ɪnˈsɛpərəbl/| adj.|不可分的，分不开的|not able to be separated|例句：The two friends were inseparable. 这两个朋友形影不离。

例句：Love and pain are often inseparable in life. 在生活中，爱与痛常常是不可分割的。|近义词：indivisible, attached, close, united|反义词:separable, divisible, detached|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French inseperable or late Latin inseparabilis, from in- ‘not’ + separabilis (from separare ‘to separate’).
