overharden:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrdn/| v. |过度硬化；淬火过度|To harden excessively, making something brittle and prone to cracking.|The blacksmith overhardened the sword, making it too brittle for battle. 铁匠把剑淬火过度，使它太脆，不适合战斗.|overtemper, overcure|soften, anneal|verb
overharden:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrdn/| adj. |过度硬化的|Excessively hardened, brittle.|The overhardened steel snapped under pressure.  过度硬化的钢在压力下断裂了。|overtempered, overcured|soft, annealed|adjective
