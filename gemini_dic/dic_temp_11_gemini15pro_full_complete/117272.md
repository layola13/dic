mechanistic:/ˌmɛkəˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
机械论的; 机制的
Relating to theories that explain phenomena in purely physical or deterministic terms.  Focusing on the workings of parts in a machine-like way, rather than the meaning or purpose of the whole.
The professor criticized the student's mechanistic interpretation of the poem, arguing that it ignored the emotional and symbolic aspects. 教授批评了学生对这首诗的机械式解读，认为它忽略了情感和象征意义。
The doctor explained the body's functions in a purely mechanistic way, without considering the role of the mind or emotions. 医生以纯粹的机械论方式解释了身体的功能，而没有考虑精神或情绪的作用。
synonyms: mechanical, deterministic, unthinking, automatic
antonyms: holistic, teleological, vitalistic, organic
词性: 形容词
