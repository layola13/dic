arclike:/ˈɑːrkˌlaɪk/
adjective
弧形的，弓形的
Resembling an arc; curved.
The bridge had an arclike structure.  这座桥有一个弧形的结构。
The cat stretched its back into an arclike position. 猫把背伸成弓形。
近义词:arched, curved, bow-shaped
反义词:straight, linear
词性:adjective

词源:
From *arc* + *-like*.  *Arc* comes from the Latin *arcus*, meaning "bow, arch."  *-like* is a suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
