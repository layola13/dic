interchanger:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒər/ | n. | 交换器；立交桥；换乘站 |A device or system that exchanges something. A road junction that uses grade separation to allow traffic on intersecting roads to cross without stopping. A place where passengers can change from one form of transport to another. |  The heat exchanger transfers heat from the hot fluid to the cold fluid. 热交换器将热量从热流体传递到冷流体。| synonyms: exchange, junction, interchange, terminal | antonyms: N/A | noun

interchanger:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒər/ | n. | 交换者 | A person who exchanges something. | The two collectors were interchangers of rare stamps. 这两位收藏家交换了稀有的邮票。 | synonyms: exchanger, trader, swapper | antonyms: N/A | noun
