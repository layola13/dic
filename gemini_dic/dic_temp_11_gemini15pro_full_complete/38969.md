confine:/kənˈfaɪn/ /ˈkɑːnfaɪn/| v. n.|限制，禁闭；范围；边界|to keep someone or something within limits; the limits or borders of something|例句：The illness confined him to his bed.  这种疾病使他卧床不起。|近义词：restrict, limit, restrain, imprison|反义词：liberate, free, release|词性：动词, 名词

confine:/kənˈfaɪn/ /ˈkɑːnfaɪn/| n.|限制，禁闭；范围；边界|the limits or borders of something|例句：The refugees were confined within the camp.  难民被限制在营地内。|近义词：limits, boundaries, restrictions|反义词：expanse, freedom|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *confine (modern French confine "border, frontier," noun, and confiner, verb), from Latin confinis "bordering, adjacent," from con- "together" + finis "end, limit, boundary."
