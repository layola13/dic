nodosarine:/ˌnəʊdəˈsɑːriːn/ /ˌnoʊdəˈsɑːriːn/
adjective
结节状的；念珠状的
Relating to or resembling a nodosaria, a genus of foraminifera characterized by chambers arranged in a linear series.
These nodosarine foraminifera are common in shallow marine environments. 这些结节状的有孔虫在浅海环境中很常见。

synonym:moniliform, beaded
antonym:smooth, unsegmented
词性:adjective
