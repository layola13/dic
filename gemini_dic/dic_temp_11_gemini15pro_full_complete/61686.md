emo:/ˈiːmoʊ/ (US), /ˈɛːməʊ/ (UK)| adj. n.| 情绪化的；伤感摇滚；情绪硬核|Relating to emo, a style of rock music characterized by emotional lyrics and expressive vocals.  Also, a person who identifies with this subculture, often characterized by introspective and expressive behavior.|He was really into the emo scene in high school. 他高中时非常喜欢情绪硬核的场景。|近义词：emotional, melancholic, sensitive|反义词：unemotional, apathetic|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: Shortened from "emotional hardcore," a subgenre of punk rock.
