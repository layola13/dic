ambusher:/ˈæmˌbʊʃər/| n.|埋伏者；伏击者|A person who ambushes someone or something.|The ambushers lay hidden in the trees, waiting for their victims. 埋伏者藏在树林里，等待着他们的受害者。|Synonyms: attacker, assailant, waylayer|Antonyms: defender, protector|noun

ambush:/ˈæmˌbʊʃ/| n. vt.|埋伏；伏击|A surprise attack by people lying in wait in a concealed position.|The soldiers were ambushed on their way back to base. 士兵们在返回基地的途中遭到伏击。|Synonyms: trap, snare, waylay|Antonyms: defend, protect|noun, verb
