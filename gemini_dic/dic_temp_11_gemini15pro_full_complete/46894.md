darkened:/ˈdɑːrkənd/ |v. adj.|变暗，使...暗下来；暗的，昏暗的|To make or become dark or darker.  It can also be used as an adjective to describe something that has become dark or darker.|The room darkened as the sun set. 房间随着日落而变暗。|近义词：dimmed, obscured, shadowy, black, dusky|反义词：brightened, illuminated, lightened|词性:动词/形容词
词源: From dark + -en, from Old English *dearcian "to become dark," from deorc "dark."
