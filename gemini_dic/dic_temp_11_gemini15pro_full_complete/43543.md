crayfish:/ˈkreɪfɪʃ/ (US) /ˈkreɪfɪʃ/ (UK)| n. |小龙虾；螯虾|A freshwater crustacean resembling a small lobster, with pincers and a hard shell.|例句：We caught crayfish in the creek. 我们在小溪里捉小龙虾。|例句：Crayfish are a popular dish in Louisiana. 小龙虾是路易斯安那州的热门菜肴。|近义词：crawfish, crawdad, yabby|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crevis, from Old French crevice (Modern French écrevisse), from Frankish *krebiz, from Proto-Germanic *krebitjaz (“crawfish”), from *kreb- (“to crawl”). Cognate with Dutch kreeft, German Krebs (“crab, crayfish”), Old English crabba (“crab”).
