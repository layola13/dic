memoir:/ˈmɛmwɑːr/ (US), /ˈmɛmwɑː/ (UK)| n.|回忆录；自传|A historical account or biography written from personal knowledge or experience.|Nelson Mandela's memoir, "Long Walk to Freedom," is a powerful and moving story.  纳尔逊·曼德拉的回忆录《漫漫自由路》是一个充满力量和感人的故事。

|近义词：autobiography, reminiscence, chronicle, account|反义词:fiction|noun
