forearming:/fɔːrˈɑːrmɪŋ/
verb
预先武装；预先准备；预先告知
Preparing or equipping oneself or others in advance, especially for a dangerous or challenging situation.  Providing with information or knowledge beforehand.
Forearming the villagers about the impending flood allowed them to evacuate in time.  预先告知村民即将到来的洪水，使他们能够及时撤离。
The hikers forearmed themselves with maps, compasses, and extra food.  徒步旅行者预先准备了地图、指南针和额外的食物。
近义词: preparing, equipping, briefing, alerting
反义词: neglecting, ignoring, surprising
