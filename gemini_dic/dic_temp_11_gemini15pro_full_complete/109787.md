levoglucoses:/ˌlevəˈɡluːkoʊsiz/ | n. |左旋葡萄糖 |A monosaccharide (simple sugar) that is a pyrolysis product of cellulose and other carbohydrates. It is a six-carbon sugar with a cyclic structure.  | Levoglucoses are important markers for biomass burning in atmospheric aerosols. 左旋葡萄糖是大气气溶胶中生物质燃烧的重要标志物。| synonyms: 1,6-anhydro-β-D-glucopyranose | antonyms: N/A | noun
