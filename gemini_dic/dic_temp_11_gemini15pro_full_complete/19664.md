bilateralisms:/ˌbaɪˈlætərəlɪzəmz/
n.
双边主义，双边关系
The practice or principle of cooperation between two nations, groups, or entities.  The state of having or involving two sides.
Many countries are now promoting bilateralisms to strengthen economic ties. 许多国家现在都在推动双边主义以加强经济联系。
The bilateralisms between the two countries have been strengthened in recent years.  近年来两国之间的双边关系得到了加强。
近义词:bilateral agreements, two-sidedness, partnerships
反义词:multilateralism, unilateralism
词性:名词

Etymology:
From bilateral + -ism.  "Bilateral" comes from the Latin "bi-" (two) + "lateralis" (of or belonging to the side). "-ism" is a suffix forming nouns of action, state, condition, doctrine, etc.
