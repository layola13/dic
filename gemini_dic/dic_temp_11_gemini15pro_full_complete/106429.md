korfball:/ˈkɔːrfbɔːl/| n. | 科夫球 | A mixed-gender team sport, similar to netball and basketball, played on a rectangular court with two baskets. |  Korfball is a fast-paced and dynamic sport. 科夫球是一项快节奏的动态运动。

Korfball is played by teams of eight, with four male and four female players. 科夫球由八人组成的队伍进行比赛，其中包括四名男性和四名女性球员。 | 近义词: netball, basketball | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific sport) | 词性: noun
