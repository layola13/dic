ethnocentricities:/ˌɛθnoʊˈsɛnˈtrɪsɪtiz/
| n. |种族中心主义|The belief in the inherent superiority of one's own ethnic group or culture.  A tendency to view alien groups or cultures from the perspective of one's own.|
Her writing displays troubling ethnocentricities, assuming her cultural norms are universal. 她 writings 显示出令人不安的种族中心主义，假设她的文化规范是普遍的。
The company's marketing campaign was criticized for its ethnocentricities, failing to resonate with diverse audiences. 该公司的营销活动因其种族中心主义而受到批评，未能引起不同受众的共鸣。
|近义词：bigotry, prejudice, bias, discrimination, racism, xenophobia |
|反义词：cultural relativism, multiculturalism, tolerance|
|词性: noun (plural)|

The study examined the ethnocentricity of the participants' responses. 这项研究调查了参与者回答中的种族中心主义。
|近义词：bigotry, prejudice, bias, discrimination, racism, xenophobia |
|反义词：cultural relativism, multiculturalism, tolerance|

词源:  From ethno- + centric + -ity.  "Ethno-" refers to ethnicity or culture, "centric" means centered, and "-ity" forms a noun denoting a state or quality.
