cybernetics: /ˌsaɪbərˈnetɪks/ | n. | 控制论 | The science of communications and automatic control systems in both machines and living things. | Example 1: He's studying cybernetics at university. 他在大学学习控制论。

Example 2:  Cybernetics plays a crucial role in the development of artificial intelligence. 控制论在人工智能的发展中起着至关重要的作用。 | Near synonyms: control theory, systems theory, information theory | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "chaos" or "disorder" in a philosophical sense) | Noun
