accessioning:/ækˈsɛʃənɪŋ/| n.|登录，登记，加入|The process of adding a new item to a collection, such as a museum or library.  将新物品添加到收藏品（例如博物馆或图书馆）的过程。|例句：The accessioning of the new artifacts took several weeks.  新文物的登录工作花了几个星期。

例句：The museum is currently accessioning a large collection of photographs.  博物馆目前正在登记一大批照片。|近义词：cataloging, registering, acquiring|反义词：deaccessioning, disposal|词性:noun


Etymology:  From the verb "accession" + "-ing" suffix. "Accession" comes from the Late Latin "accessio" meaning "addition," from the verb "accedere" meaning "to approach, to be added."  源自动词“accession”+“-ing”后缀。“Accession”来自晚期拉丁语“accessio”，意思是“添加”，来自动词“accedere”，意思是“接近，添加”。
