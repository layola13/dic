foil:/fɔɪl/ | n. | 箔，金属薄片；陪衬物，衬托；花剑 | A very thin sheet of metal, especially used to wrap food in to keep it fresh.  A person or thing that makes another seem better by contrast. A light fencing sword. | The chocolate was wrapped in aluminum foil. 巧克力用铝箔包着。 | synonyms: sheet, film, layer, lamina; contrast, complement | antonyms: highlight, emphasize | noun

foil:/fɔɪl/ | vt. | 挫败，阻止；衬托 | To prevent someone or something from being successful. To back with foil. |  The police foiled the robbers' attempt to escape. 警方挫败了劫匪的逃跑企图. | synonyms: thwart, frustrate, defeat, obstruct; enhance, set off | antonyms: aid, assist, abet; detract from | verb
