meibomian: /maɪˈboʊmiən/ |adj.| 睑板的；麦氏腺的 |Relating to the meibomian glands, which are sebaceous glands in the eyelids that secrete an oily substance called meibum. |The meibomian glands are crucial for maintaining a healthy tear film.  睑板腺对维持健康的泪膜至关重要。 | Meibomian glands produce oils that prevent tears from evaporating too quickly. 睑板腺产生的油脂可以防止眼泪蒸发过快。|Synonym: tarsal |Antonym: None| adjective
