candescent:/kænˈdɛsənt/ | adj. | 白炽的；发光的；感情强烈的 | Emitting light as a result of being heated; glowing with heat; passionate or inspiring. | The candescent light bulb illuminated the room. 白炽灯照亮了房间。 |  The speaker's candescent delivery captivated the audience. 演讲者热情洋溢的演讲吸引了观众。 | 近义词:glowing, luminous, incandescent, radiant, fervent | 反义词:dull, dim, dark, cold, apathetic | 词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin "candēscere" (to become white-hot), from "candēre" (to glow white).
