colonise:/ˈkɒlənaɪz/ (British) /ˈkɑːlənaɪz/ (American)| vt.|殖民，拓殖|To establish a colony or colonies in (a territory); to settle among and establish political control over (the indigenous people of a place).|The British colonised many countries around the world. 英国在世界各地殖民了许多国家。

The company is looking to colonise new markets overseas. 这家公司正在寻求开拓海外新市场。

|近义词：settle, populate, conquer, occupy|反义词：decolonise, liberate|词性:动词(verb)

colonisation:/ˌkɒlənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ (British) /ˌkɑːlənəˈzeɪʃən/ (American)| n.|殖民，殖民地化|The action or process of settling among and establishing control over the indigenous people of a place.|The colonisation of Africa had a profound impact on the continent. 非洲的殖民化对这块大陆产生了深远的影响。

The colonisation of Mars is a long-term goal for some scientists.  火星殖民是某些科学家的长期目标。

|近义词：settlement, occupation, conquest|反义词：decolonisation, liberation|词性:名词(noun)
