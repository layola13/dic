narrational:/nəˈreɪʃənəl/| adj.|叙述的，讲述的|Relating to narration; characteristic of a narrator.  Used to describe something that relates to the act or process of telling a story, or the way a story is told.|The film's narrational style was unique, switching between different characters' perspectives. 这部电影的叙事风格很独特，在不同角色的视角之间切换。|synonyms: narrative, descriptive, expository|antonyms: dramatic, performative|adjective
