bookended:/ˈbʊkɛndɪd/
verb (past tense: bookended, past participle: bookended, present participle: bookending, third person singular present tense: bookends)
以...开始和结束；夹住
to occur or be positioned at the beginning and end of (something)
The film is bookended by two powerful scenes. 这部电影以两个强有力的场景开始和结束。
Synonyms: enclose, bracket, flank
Antonyms: separate, divide
词性:动词

bookended:/ˈbʊkɛndɪd/
adjective
以...开始和结束的；夹住的
occurring or positioned at the beginning and end of (something)
Her career was bookended by two major awards. 她的职业生涯以两个主要奖项开始和结束。
Synonyms: enclosed, bracketed, flanked
Antonyms: separated, divided
词性:形容词

词源:  "Bookend" originally referred to a support placed at the end of a row of books to keep them upright. The figurative use, meaning to enclose or bracket something, developed later.
