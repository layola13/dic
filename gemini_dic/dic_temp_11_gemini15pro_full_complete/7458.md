anisopleura:/ˌænɪsoʊˈplʊərə/ | n. |异脉蟌属|A genus of damselflies in the family Euphaeidae. |Anisopleura comes from the Greek "anisos" meaning unequal and "pleura" meaning side, referring to the unequal sides of the quadrilateral in the wing venation.  This genus is found in Asia. |The anisopleura zhengi is a recently discovered species of damselfly.  郑氏异脉蟌是最近发现的一种豆娘。|Synonym: None (scientific name)|Antonym: None (scientific name)|noun
