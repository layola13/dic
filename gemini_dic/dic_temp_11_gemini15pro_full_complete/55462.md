/dɪsˈtaɪtl/ | v. | 剥夺称号；贬低 | To deprive of a title or status; to belittle or demean. |  He was distitled of his knighthood after the scandal.  他因丑闻被剥夺了骑士爵位。

/dɪsˈtaɪtl/ | v. | 使失去资格 | To disqualify or make ineligible. | The new regulations distitle him from competing.  新规定使他失去参赛资格。

/dɪsˈtaɪtl/ | v. |  使失去权利 | To deprive of a right or privilege. | The law distitles felons from voting.  法律剥夺了重罪犯的投票权。
