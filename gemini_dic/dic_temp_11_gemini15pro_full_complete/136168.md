nonrehabilitation:/ˌnɒnˌriːəˌbɪlɪˈteɪʃən/
n.
未能康复；未恢复原状；无法恢复
The nonrehabilitation of the injured athlete ended his career.  这位受伤运动员的未能康复结束了他的职业生涯。
The nonrehabilitation of the historic building led to its demolition. 这座历史建筑未能修复，导致其被拆除。
近义词: nonrecovery, failure to rehabilitate
反义词: rehabilitation, recovery, restoration
词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): not, absence of (from Latin)
rehabilitation (noun): the action of restoring someone to health or normal life through training and therapy after imprisonment, addiction, or illness. (from Late Middle English: from late Latin rehabilitatio(n-), from the verb rehabilitare, from re- ‘again’ + habilitare ‘make fit’)
