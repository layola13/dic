amianthoid:/ˌeɪmiˈænθɔɪd/| adj.|石棉状的，似石棉的|Resembling asbestos; having a fine, fibrous texture.|例句：The mineral exhibited an amianthoid appearance.  这种矿物呈现出石棉状的外观。

例句：The rock contained amianthoid inclusions. 这块岩石包含石棉状内含物。|近义词：asbestiform, fibrous|反义词: compact, dense|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Greek *amiantos* ("undefiled, pure," referring to asbestos's resistance to fire) + *-oid* ("resembling").  词源：来自希腊语 *amiantos*（“未受污染的，纯净的”，指的是石棉的耐火性）+ *-oid*（“类似的”）。
