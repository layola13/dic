beards:/bɪərdz/| n.|胡须，络腮胡子|The hair that grows on the chin, cheeks, and upper lip of a man, sometimes also on the neck.|He had a long, white beard. 他留着长长的白胡子。|近义词：facial hair, whiskers|反义词: clean-shaven|Noun
