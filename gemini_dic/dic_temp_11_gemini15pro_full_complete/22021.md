boisterously:/ˈbɔɪstərəsli/| adv.|喧闹地； boisterously|The children played boisterously in the garden. 孩子们在花园里喧闹地玩耍。|The crowd cheered boisterously for their team.  人群喧闹地为他们的队伍欢呼。|近义词：noisily, loudly, clamorously, tumultuously|反义词：quietly, softly, calmly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From boisterous + -ly.  Boisterous comes from Middle English *boistous, probably from Anglo-Norman boistous "rough, clumsy," perhaps from a Scandinavian source.
