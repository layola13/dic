draftsmanship: /ˈdræftsmənʃɪp/
n.
绘图术; 制图技巧; 草图绘制; 
The skill of making drawings, especially technical drawings, or the quality of a drawing.
Her draftsmanship is impeccable, capturing every detail with precision. 她的绘图技巧无可挑剔，精确地捕捉了每一个细节。
The architect's draftsmanship was evident in the detailed blueprints. 建筑师的制图技巧体现在详细的蓝图中。
近义词: drawing skill, artistry, draughtsmanship (British spelling)
反义词: ineptitude, clumsiness

词性: noun
词源:  From draft +‎ -manship,  "draft" meaning a preliminary sketch or version, ultimately from the verb "to draw".
