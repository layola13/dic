crawling:/ˈkrɔːlɪŋ/| adj.  n.|爬行的，缓慢的；匍匐前进；（交通）缓慢行驶；起鸡皮疙瘩的| moving slowly; on all fours; creeping.|例句：The baby is crawling on the floor. 婴儿在地板上爬行。|近义词：creeping, wriggling, inching|反义词：running, sprinting|词性: adjective, noun
crawling:/ˈkrɔːlɪŋ/| n.|爬行；匍匐前进；（交通）缓慢行驶；|The act of moving slowly on hands and knees.|例句：The traffic was crawling along the highway. 高速公路上的车辆行驶缓慢。|近义词：creeping, wriggling, inching|反义词：running, sprinting|词性: noun
