juices:/ˈdʒuːsɪz/
n.|果汁，菜汁；精髓，精华；精力，活力|The liquid part of a fruit, vegetable, or meat that can be squeezed out; the essential part of something.|She enjoys drinking fresh fruit juices. 她喜欢喝新鲜的果汁。
|近义词: beverages, drinks, extracts, essences|反义词: pulp, solids
词性:名词(plural of juice)


juice:/dʒuːs/
n.|果汁，菜汁；精髓，精华；精力，活力|The liquid part of a fruit, vegetable, or meat that can be squeezed out; the essential part of something.|He squeezed the juice from an orange. 他从橙子里榨出了汁。
|近义词: beverage, drink, extract, essence|反义词: pulp, solid
词性:名词

juice:/dʒuːs/
vt.|榨取…的汁液；使充满活力；给…通电|To extract juice from; to invigorate; to energize.|She juiced some oranges for breakfast. 她榨了一些橙汁做早餐。
|近义词: extract, energize, invigorate|反义词: drain, deplete
词性:动词


词源:
From Middle English *jus, from Old French jus, juis (“juice, sap, gravy”), from Latin jūs (“broth, gravy, juice, liquid”).
