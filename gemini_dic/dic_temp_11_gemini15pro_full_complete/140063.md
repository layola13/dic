oestrogenization:/ˌiːstrədʒənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |雌激素化；雌激素增多|The process of becoming feminized due to exposure to oestrogens.  This can occur naturally during puberty in females, or artificially through medication or environmental exposure.|  Increased oestrogenization during puberty leads to the development of secondary sexual characteristics in females. 青春期雌激素增加导致女性第二性征的发育。

The oestrogenization of male fish in polluted rivers is a growing concern. 受污染河流中雄鱼的雌性化是一个日益严重的问题。|近义词: feminization |反义词:  masculinization |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From oestrogen + -ization.  Oestrogen itself comes from the Greek *oistros* (gadfly, frenzy) + *genos* (birth, kind).
