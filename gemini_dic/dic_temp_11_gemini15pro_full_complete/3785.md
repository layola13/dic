aglitter:/əˈɡlɪtər/| adj.|闪闪发光的，闪耀的|Covered with or reflecting many small, sparkling lights. |The Christmas tree was aglitter with ornaments. 圣诞树上装饰着闪闪发光的饰品。|近义词：sparkling, glittering, shimmering, twinkling|反义词:dull, dark, dim|词性:形容词
词源: a- (prefix meaning "on" or "in") + glitter (verb meaning "to shine with many small flashes of light").  The word "glitter" itself comes from the Old Norse "glitra," meaning "to sparkle."
