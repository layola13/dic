noncultural:/ˌnɒnˈkʌltʃərəl/ | /ˌnɑːnˈkʌltʃərəl/ | adj. | 非文化的 | Not related to or derived from culture; not having to do with the customs, beliefs, art, etc., of a particular society. | The scientist argued that the behavior was noncultural, based on instinct rather than learned behavior.  科学家认为这种行为是非文化的，基于本能而不是习得的行为。 |例句：The scientist argued that the behavior was noncultural, based on instinct rather than learned behavior. 科学家认为这种行为是非文化的，基于本能而不是习得的行为。
|例句：Noncultural factors, such as climate and geography, also play a role in shaping a society.  非文化因素，如气候和地理，也在塑造社会中发挥作用。| 近义词：acultural, non-ethnic | 反义词：cultural, ethnic | 词性：adjective
