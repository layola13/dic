leukaemia:/luːˈkiːmiə/ /luːˈkimiə/| n.|白血病|A malignant progressive disease in which the bone marrow and other blood-forming organs produce increased numbers of immature or abnormal leucocytes. These suppress the production of normal blood cells, leading to anaemia and other symptoms.|My uncle was diagnosed with leukaemia last year. 我叔叔去年被诊断出患有白血病。| Leukemia is a type of blood cancer that affects the bone marrow and blood cells.  白血病是一种影响骨髓和血细胞的血癌。|近义词:cancer, blood cancer|反义词:health, wellness|词性:noun
