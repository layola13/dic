moneymaking:/ˈmʌniˌmeɪkɪŋ/| adj.|赚钱的；营利的|The process or activity of earning money, especially in a way that is clever or effective.  Relating to or designed to make money.|He has a moneymaking scheme. 他有一个赚钱的计划。|Synonyms: profitable, lucrative, remunerative|Antonyms: unprofitable, loss-making|adjective
