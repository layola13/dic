hatstands:/ˈhætstændz/
n.
帽架；衣帽架

The hallway was furnished with a sturdy oak hatstand.  走廊里摆放着一个结实的橡木帽架。
synonyms: hat rack, coat stand, coat rack
antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a physical object)
