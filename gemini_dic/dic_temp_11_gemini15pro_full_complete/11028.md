arcadia:/ɑːrˈkeɪdiə/ | n. | 阿卡迪亚；世外桃源；理想的地方 | A region in Greece, traditionally associated with pastoral simplicity and idyllic happiness.  It is often used to represent any real or imaginary place offering peace and simplicity. |  Example 1:  He dreamed of escaping to an arcadia of peace and tranquility. 他梦想着逃到一个和平宁静的世外桃源。

Example 2:  The valley was a veritable arcadia, filled with wildflowers and the sound of birdsong.  这个山谷是一个名副其实的世外桃源，开满了野花，鸟鸣声声。| 近义词：utopia, paradise, Eden | 反义词：dystopia, hell | 词性：名词
