commandingly:/kəˈmɑːndɪŋli/ | adv. | 命令式地，威严地 | In a manner that shows authority and demands obedience; in a way that impresses or inspires. | She spoke commandingly, silencing the entire room. 她威严地说着，让整个房间安静下来。

The general addressed his troops commandingly. 将军以命令的口吻向他的部队讲话。| 近义词: authoritatively, assertively, imperiously | 反义词: meekly, submissively, timidly | 词性: 副词
