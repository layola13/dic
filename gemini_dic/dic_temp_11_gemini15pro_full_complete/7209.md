angles:/ˈæŋɡlz/| n.|角，角度|The sum of the angles in a triangle is 180 degrees. 三角形内角和为180度。|Example: Measure the angles of the square.  测量正方形的角度。|Synonyms: corners, intersections|Antonyms: curves, rounds|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *angle (Modern French angle), from Latin angulus "corner, angle," related to angustus "narrow."
