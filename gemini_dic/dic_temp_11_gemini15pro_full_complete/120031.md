millionaire:/ˌmɪljəˈneər/ | n. | 百万富翁；大富豪 | A person whose assets are worth one million or more units of currency. |  He became a millionaire overnight after winning the lottery. 他中了彩票，一夜之间成了百万富翁。

| Example:  She invested wisely and became a millionaire before she was 30. 她投资明智，30岁之前就成了百万富翁。| Synonym: tycoon, magnate, wealthy person | Antonym: pauper, bankrupt | noun
