interminableness:/ɪnˈtɜːrmɪnəblənəs/
|n.| 无休止，无限，没完没了 |The interminableness of the journey made the children restless.  旅途的漫长让孩子们焦躁不安。|English Explanation: The state or quality of being interminable; endlessness.|例句：The interminableness of the lecture bored the students. 演讲的冗长乏味让学生们感到无聊。|近义词：endlessness, perpetuity, infinity|反义词：brevity, shortness, finiteness|词性: 名词

词源: From interminable + -ness.  Interminable comes from Late Middle English: from Old French, or from medieval Latin interminabilis, from in- ‘not’ + terminabilis ‘capable of being limited’ (from terminare ‘to limit’).
