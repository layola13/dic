bounce lighting:/ˈbaʊns laɪtɪŋ/| n.|反跳照明，跳闪|A lighting technique where light is reflected off a surface (like a ceiling or wall) to create a softer, more diffused light.|Example 1: The photographer used bounce lighting off the ceiling to avoid harsh shadows on the subject's face. 摄影师利用天花板的反光进行跳闪，以避免在拍摄对象脸上出现难看的阴影。

Example 2: Bounce lighting is a great way to achieve a natural-looking light in indoor photography. 跳闪是在室内摄影中获得自然光线的好方法。|近义词：indirect lighting, diffused lighting, reflected lighting|反义词:direct lighting, hard light|名词
