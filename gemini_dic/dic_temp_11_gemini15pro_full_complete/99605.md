interjecting:/ˌɪntərˈdʒektɪŋ/| v. |插嘴，打断；突然插入|The teacher was constantly interjecting with helpful comments. 老师不断地插话进行有益的评论。|例句：He kept interjecting while I was trying to explain. 我试图解释时，他不停地插嘴。

例句：She interjected a question during the presentation.  她在演讲中插了一个问题。|近义词：interrupting, inserting, interposing|反义词：listening, withholding|词性:动词(present participle of interject)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin interiectus, past participle of intericere (“to throw between”), from inter (“between”) + iacere (“to throw”).
