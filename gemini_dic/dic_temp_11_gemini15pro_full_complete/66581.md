exhortative:/ɪɡˈzɔːrtətɪv/
adj.
劝告的；勉励的
Serving or intended to exhort; hortatory.
The coach gave an exhortative speech to his team before the final game. 教练在决赛前给他的队员们做了一次激励的演讲。
近义词：hortatory, encouraging, urging, inspiring, motivational
反义词：discouraging, dissuading, deterrent
词性: adjective

exhort:/ɪɡˈzɔːrt/
vt.
劝告；勉励
Strongly encourage or urge (someone) to do something.
The teacher exhorted her students to study hard for the exam. 老师劝勉学生们努力学习，准备考试。
近义词：urge, encourage, advise, counsel, admonish
反义词：discourage, dissuade, deter
词性: verb

词源：From Late Latin exhortativus, from exhortat- ‘urged’, from the verb exhortari, from ex- ‘out’ + hortari ‘encourage’.
