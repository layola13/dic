bounteousnesses:/ˈbaʊntiəsnɪsɪz/
noun
慷慨；大方；丰饶
The state or quality of being bounteous; generosity; abundance.
The bounteousnesses of the harvest ensured no one went hungry.  丰收的慷慨确保没有人挨饿。
Synonyms: generosity, liberality, munificence, abundance, bounty, profusion
Antonyms: stinginess, meanness, scarcity, meagerness, paucity
词性: 名词


bounteous:/ˈbaʊntiəs/
adjective
慷慨的；丰富的；充足的
Characterized by bounty; giving freely; abundant.
The king held a bounteous feast for his subjects. 国王为他的臣民举行了一场盛大的宴会。
Synonyms: generous, liberal, plentiful, abundant, copious, ample
Antonyms: stingy, meager, scarce, scanty, sparse
词性: 形容词
