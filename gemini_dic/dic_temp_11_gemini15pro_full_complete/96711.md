ineffectualities:/ˌɪnɪˈfɛktʃuˈælɪtiz/
n.
无效性；无效；低效
lack of effectiveness; the state of being unable to produce any significant or desired effect
The report highlighted the ineffectualities of the current system.  这份报告强调了当前系统的无效性。
The ineffectualities of the government's policies led to widespread discontent. 政府政策的无效导致了普遍不满。
ineffectiveness, inefficiency, futility, uselessness
effectiveness, efficiency, potency
noun
