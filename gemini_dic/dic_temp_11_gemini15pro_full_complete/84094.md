half-inherited:/ˌhæf ɪnˈhɛrɪtɪd/ | adj. | 半继承的；部分继承的 | Partly inherited; inherited to some degree but not completely.  This describes traits or characteristics that are influenced by both genetic and environmental factors. |  His musical talent is half-inherited, half-cultivated through years of practice. 他的音乐天赋一半是遗传的，一半是多年练习培养的。

The dog's temperament seemed half-inherited, half-learned from its previous owner.  这只狗的性情似乎一半是遗传的，一半是从它以前的主人那里学来的。 | 近义词: partially inherited, partly genetic | 反义词: fully inherited, wholly genetic | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
"half-" (Old English healf) + "inherited" (past participle of "inherit," from Old French enheriter, ultimately from Latin inhereditare).
