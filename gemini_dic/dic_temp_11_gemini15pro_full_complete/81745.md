greenweed:/ˈɡriːnwiːd/| n. |金雀花，染料木|A European shrub (Genista tinctoria) of the legume family, having clusters of yellow flowers and yielding a yellow dye.|The field was covered with bright yellow greenweed.|近义词：dyer's greenweed, dyer's broom|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Old English *grēn* (green) + *wēod* (weed, plant).
