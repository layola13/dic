non-linear:/ˌnɒnˈlɪnɪə(r) (UK), /ˌnɑːnˈlɪniər (US)| adj.|非线性的|Not linear; not describing a straight line.  Relating to or denoting a function or relationship in which the change in output is not proportional to the change in input.|The graph shows a non-linear relationship between the two variables. 这张图显示了两个变量之间的非线性关系。|nonproportional, curvilinear|linear|adjective
