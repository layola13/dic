nonrepresentative:/ˌnɒnˌreprɪˈzentətɪv/ | adj. | 非代表性的，不典型的 | Not representative; not typical of a particular group or thing. | This small group is nonrepresentative of the population as a whole. 这个小组并不能代表整体人口。|  The survey results are nonrepresentative because the sample size was too small.  这项调查结果不具有代表性，因为样本量太小。 |近义词: atypical, uncharacteristic, biased, skewed |反义词: representative, typical, characteristic |词性: adjective
