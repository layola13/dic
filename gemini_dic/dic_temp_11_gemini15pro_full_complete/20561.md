blackballing: /ˌblækˈbɔːlɪŋ/
n.|排斥；投票反对；反对票|The act of rejecting someone, usually by secret ballot, from a club, group, or other organization.|He faced blackballing from the exclusive club because of his controversial views. 他因为有争议的观点而面临被这个高级俱乐部排斥。|ostracization, exclusion, rejection|acceptance, inclusion|noun

blackballing: /ˌblækˈbɔːlɪŋ/
v.|投票反对；排斥|To reject someone, usually by secret ballot, from a club, group, or other organization.|The committee blackballed his application for membership. 委员会投票否决了他的会员申请。|ostracize, exclude, reject|accept, include|verb
