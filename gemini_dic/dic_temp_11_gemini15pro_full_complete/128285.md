neuropteran:/ˌnjʊərˈɒptərən/ |n.|神经翅目昆虫；脉翅目昆虫|An insect of the order Neuroptera, such as a lacewing or an antlion.|Lacewings are common neuropterans found in gardens. 草蛉是花园里常见的神经翅目昆虫。|Synonyms: lacewing, antlion, alderfly, dobsonfly|Antonyms: N/A|noun

neuropterous:/njʊəˈrɒptərəs/ |adj.|神经翅目的；脉翅目的|Relating to or belonging to the insect order Neuroptera.|Neuropterous insects are characterized by their intricately veined wings. 神经翅目昆虫的特点是它们错综复杂的翅脉。|Synonyms: N/A|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
