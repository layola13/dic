ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gredire, from Old North French *gredire (“grid, grate”), from Vulgar Latin *grāticula, diminutive of Latin crātis (“wickerwork, hurdle”). The sporting sense arose from the resemblance of early football fields marked with grid lines to a gridiron used for cooking.
