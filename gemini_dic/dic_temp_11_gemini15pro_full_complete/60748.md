electrodynamics: /ɪˌlɛktroʊdaɪˈnæmɪks/ /ɪˌlektrəʊdaɪˈnæmɪks/
noun
电动力学
The branch of physics concerned with the interaction of electric and magnetic phenomena.
Electrodynamics is a core subject in physics. 电动力学是物理学的核心科目。
The principles of electrodynamics explain the behavior of electromagnetic waves. 电动力学原理解释了电磁波的行为。
synonyms: electromagnetism
antonyms: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "statics" in the context of contrasting dynamic and static electricity)
词性: 名词
