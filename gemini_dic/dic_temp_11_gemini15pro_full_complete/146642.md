overhardens:/ˌōvərˈhɑːrdnz/ | v. | 过度硬化，硬化过度 |  To harden excessively, making something brittle or inflexible. | The blacksmith overhardened the sword, making it prone to shattering. 铁匠把剑过度硬化了，使它容易破碎。| The clay overhardened in the sun and cracked.  黏土在阳光下过度硬化并开裂了。 | over-temper, overbake | soften, anneal | verb
