coursed:/kɔːrst/ /kɝst/| v.|流淌，快速移动，追逐|The blood coursed through his veins. 血液在他的血管里奔流。|例句：The river coursed through the valley.  河流流经山谷。

例句：Tears coursed down her cheeks.  泪水顺着她的脸颊流了下来。

|近义词：flowed, rushed, sped, ran, streamed|反义词:stagnated, trickled|词性:动词

coursed:/kɔːrst/ /kɝst/| adj.|(猎狗)追逐(野兽)的|(of hounds) pursuing game|The coursed hare darted across the field. 被猎狗追逐的野兔飞奔穿过田野。|例句：The coursed fox sought refuge in the woods. 被猎狗追赶的狐狸在树林里寻找 refuge。

例句：They watched the coursed deer leap over the fence. 他们看着被追逐的鹿跳过栅栏。


|近义词：chased, hunted, pursued|反义词:protected, sheltered|词性:形容词
