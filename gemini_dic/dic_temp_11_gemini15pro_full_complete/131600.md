noncomplacencies:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpleɪsənsiz/
n.
不满足，不懈怠，居安思危
lack of complacency; the state of being noncomplacent.  A feeling of unease about something.  A refusal to be satisfied with current achievements, often leading to a continued striving for improvement.
The company's noncomplacencies about its market share led to continuous innovation.  公司对其市场份额的不满足促成了持续的创新。
Near synonyms: vigilance, alertness, uneasiness, restlessness, dissatisfaction
Near antonyms: complacency, satisfaction, contentment
noun
