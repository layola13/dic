glint:/ɡlɪnt/| n. vi.|闪烁，闪光；闪现；一丝|A brief flash of light, often reflected from a smooth surface.  A brief, sudden flash of an emotion or quality.|The glint of sunlight on the water was blinding.  阳光在水面上闪烁，令人目眩。  There was a glint of mischief in his eyes. 他的眼里闪过一丝狡黠。|近义词：flash, gleam, sparkle, glitter, twinkle|反义词:dullness, darkness|词性:名词,动词

词源:From Middle English glenten, glinten (“to shine, glitter”), of uncertain origin; perhaps related to Old English glitnian (“to glitter, shine”).
