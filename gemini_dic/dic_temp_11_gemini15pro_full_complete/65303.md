eudemonist:/ˌjuːdɪˈmɒnɪst/ | n. | 快乐主义者；幸福论者 | A person who believes in eudaemonism (the ethical theory that happiness or welfare is the chief good). |  He considered himself a eudemonist, always striving for personal fulfillment and well-being. 他认为自己是一个快乐主义者，总是努力追求个人的满足和幸福。| 近义词: hedonist | 反义词: ascetic | 词性: noun

eudemonistic:/ˌjuːdɪˈmɒnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 快乐主义的；幸福论的 | Of or relating to eudaemonism. |  He took a eudemonistic approach to life, prioritizing experiences that brought him joy and meaning. 他对生活采取了一种快乐主义的态度，优先考虑那些给他带来快乐和意义的经历。| 近义词: hedonistic | 反义词: ascetic | 词性: adjective
