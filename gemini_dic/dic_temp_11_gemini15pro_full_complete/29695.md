cathodegraph:/ˈkæθəʊdɡræf/ | n. |阴极射线示波器;示波图|An instrument that records oscillations of an electrical current by means of a spot of light moving across a fluorescent screen.  It's essentially an older term for an oscilloscope.| The engineer used a cathodegraph to analyze the signal. 工程师使用阴极射线示波器分析信号。 |Synonym: oscilloscope, oscillograph | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps analog meter) | noun
