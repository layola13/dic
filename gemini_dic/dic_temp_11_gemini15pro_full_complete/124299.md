motherboards:/ˈmʌðərbɔːrdz/
n.
主板
The main circuit board of a computer, containing the CPU, memory, and other essential components.  A motherboard connects all the parts of a computer together.
The technician replaced the faulty motherboard.  技术员更换了故障主板。
mainboard, system board
(No real antonym for a physical component)
noun
