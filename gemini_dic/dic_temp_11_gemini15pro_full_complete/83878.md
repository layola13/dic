half-afraid:/ˌhɑːf əˈfreɪd/| adj.|有些害怕，略微害怕|Slightly afraid; apprehensive.|She was half-afraid to open the door. 她有点害怕打开门。|近义词: apprehensive, uneasy, slightly afraid|反义词: brave, bold, fearless|词性:形容词
Etymology:  "half" (Old English healf) + "afraid" (from Old French afraier, ultimately from Latin exfridare "to take peace from")
