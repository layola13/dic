hydrodynamical:/ˌhaɪdrəʊdaɪˈnæmɪkəl/ |adj.|流体动力学的|Relating to the motion of fluids (liquids or gases) and the forces acting on them.  It describes the behavior of fluids in motion and their interaction with solid boundaries.|The hydrodynamical properties of the ship's hull were carefully analyzed. 这艘船船体的流体动力学特性经过了仔细分析。|
The hydrodynamical forces acting on the submarine were significant. 作用在潜艇上的流体动力学力量非常显著。|Synonyms: fluid-dynamic, fluid-mechanical|Antonyms: static, motionless|adjective
