antivaccinist:/ˌæntivaˈksɪnɪst/ | n. | 反疫苗者，疫苗接种反对者 | A person who opposes vaccination or who advocates against the use of vaccines. |  Many antivaccinists believe that vaccines are dangerous. 许多反疫苗者认为疫苗是危险的。|  The antivaccinist movement has been linked to outbreaks of preventable diseases. 反疫苗运动与可预防疾病的爆发有关。| near synonyms:  vaccine skeptic, anti-vaxxer | antonym: pro-vaccinist, vaccine proponent | noun
