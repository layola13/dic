dumbshow:/ˈdʌmʃoʊ/ (US), /ˈdʌmʃəʊ/ (UK)| n.|哑剧；哑剧表演|A dramatic performance without dialogue, using only gestures and action.|The clown performed a hilarious dumbshow about getting stuck in a box.|Synonyms: mime, pantomime, silent acting|Antonyms: dialogue, spoken drama|noun
