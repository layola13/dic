disassimilate:/dɪsəˈsɪməleɪt/
verb
使异化；使不同化；[生理学]异化
to make or become dissimilar; to cause to become less similar
The immigrants were encouraged to disassimilate and retain their cultural heritage.  移民被鼓励不要被同化，要保留他们的文化遗产。
The body disassimilates food into energy and waste products.  身体将食物异化成能量和废物。
近义词:differentiate, diverge, vary
反义词:assimilate, homogenize, integrate
词性:v.

disassimilation:/dɪˌsɪməˈleɪʃən/
noun
异化；异化作用；[生理学]异化作用
the process of making or becoming dissimilar
Disassimilation of cultural groups can lead to social fragmentation. 文化群体的异化会导致社会分裂。
The disassimilation of nutrients provides the body with energy.  营养物质的异化作用为身体提供能量。
近义词:differentiation, divergence, variation
反义词:assimilation, homogenization, integration
词性:n.
