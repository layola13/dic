docketing:/ˈdɑːkɪtɪŋ/
n.
归档；登记；记录
The act of placing a document in a docket or entering it into a formal record.
The docketing of the case took longer than expected.  案件的归档比预期的要长。
record-keeping, filing, registering, logging
N/A
noun
