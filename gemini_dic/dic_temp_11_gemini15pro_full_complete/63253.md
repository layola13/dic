entertainable:/ˌɛntərˈteɪnəbl/ | adj. | 有趣的，娱乐的 | Providing amusement or enjoyment; capable of holding one's attention agreeably. | The movie was highly entertaining, with plenty of action and humor.  这部电影非常有趣，有很多动作和幽默的场景。|  近义词: amusing, enjoyable, diverting, engaging, interesting | 反义词: boring, tedious, dull, uninteresting | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *enterteynable, from entertein + -able. Equivalent to entertain + -able.
