misalignments: /ˌmɪsəˈlaɪnmənts/ | n. | 未对准，偏差，错位 |  A state of being out of alignment or incorrect relative position.  It refers to situations where things are not positioned or arranged correctly in relation to each other. | The misalignments in the gears caused the machine to malfunction.  齿轮未对准导致机器故障。

The misalignments of the telescope prevented accurate observations. 望远镜的偏差阻碍了精确的观测。 | synonyms: discrepancies, disparities, inconsistencies, variations, deviations | antonyms: alignments, agreements, harmonies, consistencies | noun
