malthouses:/ˈmɔːlthaʊzɪz/
n.
麦芽房，制麦场
A building or place where malt is made.
The malthouses were busy during the barley harvest.  麦芽房在大麦收获季节很忙碌。
The old malthouses have been converted into apartments.  老麦芽房已经被改建成公寓。
synonyms: maltster, brewery, kiln
antonyms: N/A
