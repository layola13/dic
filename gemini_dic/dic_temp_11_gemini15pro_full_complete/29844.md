causally:/ˈkɔːzəli/| adv.|因果地，原因上地|in a way that relates to cause and effect|例句：The two events are not causally related. 这两件事没有因果关系。|近义词：consequently, therefore, thus|反义词:randomly, accidentally|词性:副词
词源: From "causal" + "-ly."  "Causal" comes from Late Latin "causalis," from Latin "causa" (cause).
