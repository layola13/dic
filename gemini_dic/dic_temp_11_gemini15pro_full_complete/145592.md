overcondensation:/ˌoʊvərˌkɒndenˈseɪʃən/ | /ˌoʊvərˌkɑːndenˈseɪʃn/ | n. | 过度冷凝，过饱和| The process of condensation occurring to an excessive degree, resulting in supersaturation or the formation of more condensate than is desired or expected. | Excessive humidity led to overcondensation on the windows. 过高的湿度导致窗户上过度冷凝。|  The overcondensation in the air conditioning system caused water damage. 空调系统中的过度冷凝导致了水损。 | supersaturation, excess condensation | undercondensation, insufficient condensation | noun
