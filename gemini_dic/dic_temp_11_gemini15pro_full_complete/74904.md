freshness:/ˈfrɛʃnɪs/| n.|新鲜，清新|The freshness of the air invigorated us. 空气的清新使我们精神焕发。|近义词：newness, novelty, crispness|反义词：staleness, mustiness|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle English *fersknesse*, equivalent to fresh +‎ -ness.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *friskaz.
