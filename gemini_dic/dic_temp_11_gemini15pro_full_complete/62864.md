enjoys:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪz/ | v. | 享受，喜爱 |  Derives from Old French "enjoir" (to give joy to, delight), from "en-" (in) + "joir" (to rejoice). It means to take pleasure or satisfaction in something. | She enjoys reading books in her spare time.  她空闲时间喜欢看书。| likes, appreciates, relishes | dislikes, hates | verb
