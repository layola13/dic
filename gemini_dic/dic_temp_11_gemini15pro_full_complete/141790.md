orb:/ɔːrb/ | n. | 球体，天体，圆形物 | A spherical body; a celestial body; something circular or spherical. | The full moon hung in the sky like a giant silver orb.  满月像一个巨大的银球一样挂在天空。 | synonyms: sphere, globe, ball | antonyms:  - | noun

orb:/ɔːrb/ | v. | 形成球体，环绕 | To form into a sphere or circle; to encircle. | The magician orbed his hands around the crystal ball. 魔术师将他的双手环绕在水晶球周围。 | synonyms:  encircle, surround, encompass | antonyms: - | verb
