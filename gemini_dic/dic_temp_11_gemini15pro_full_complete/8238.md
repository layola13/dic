anti-ideological:/ˌæntiˌaɪdiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adj.
反意识形态的
Opposed to or lacking a strong ideology.
He took an anti-ideological stance, preferring pragmatism over doctrine. 他采取了反意识形态的立场，更喜欢实用主义而不是教条。
近义词:non-ideological, pragmatic
反义词:ideological, doctrinaire
词性:形容词
