balata:/bəˈlɑːtə/| n.|巴拉塔树胶，巴拉塔树|A tough, elastic, non-vulcanizable rubber-like material obtained from the milky sap of the bully tree (Manilkara bidentata), native to South America and the West Indies. It is used for belting, golf ball covers, and other products.|The golf balls used to be made of balata. 过去的高尔夫球是用巴拉塔树胶做的。|近义词：gutta-percha, chicle|反义词: None|noun

balata:/bəˈlɑːtə/| n.|巴拉塔树|The bully tree (Manilkara bidentata), a tropical American tree yielding balata.|The balata tree is native to South America. 巴拉塔树原产于南美洲。|近义词：bully tree|反义词: None|noun
