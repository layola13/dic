convictible:/kənˈvɪktɪbl/
adjective
可定罪的; 可证明有罪的; 易被定罪的
Capable of being convicted of a crime; demonstrably guilty.
The evidence made him easily convictible. 证据使他很容易被定罪。
Synonyms: guilty, culpable, indictable
Antonyms: innocent, blameless
词性: 形容词


convictible:/kənˈvɪktɪbl/
noun
敞篷车
A car with a roof that can be folded back or removed.
He drove his convictible along the coast. 他开着敞篷车沿着海岸行驶。
Synonyms: convertible, cabriolet, roadster
Antonyms: hardtop, sedan
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *convictibilis, from Latin convictus (past participle of convincere "to prove wrong, refute, convict") + -ibilis (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being").  The noun meaning "convertible car" is a shortening of convertible.
