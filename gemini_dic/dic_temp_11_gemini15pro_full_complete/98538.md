instigator:/ˈɪnstɪɡeɪtər/| n. |煽动者，教唆者|A person who initiates or incites something, especially something negative like violence or unrest.|例句：He was identified as the main instigator of the riot. 他被认定为这场骚乱的主要煽动者。|例句：The police are still searching for the instigator of the bomb threat. 警方仍在搜寻炸弹威胁的煽动者。|近义词：agitator, provoker, inciter|反义词：peacemaker, mediator|词性：名词

词源：From Late Latin instigator, from Latin instigare "to incite," from in- "in" + stigare "to prick, goad."
