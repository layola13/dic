microlight:/ˈmaɪkrəʊlaɪt/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊlaɪt/ (US)| n. | 微型飞机；超轻型飞机 | A small, lightweight aircraft with a flexible wing. |  He took flying lessons and bought a microlight. 他上了飞行课，买了一架微型飞机。| synonym: ultralight | antonym: jumbo jet | noun

microlight:/ˈmaɪkrəʊlaɪt/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊlaɪt/ (US)| adj. | 微光的；超轻的 | Relating to or denoting a microlight aircraft. | The microlight aircraft soared through the air.  这架超轻型飞机翱翔在空中。 | synonym: ultralight | antonym: heavy | adjective
