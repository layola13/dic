joystick:/ˈdʒɔɪstɪk/ | n. |操纵杆，控制杆 |A lever that can be moved in several directions to control the movement of an image on a computer or similar display screen, or to control the movement of an aircraft or other vehicle. | He used the joystick to control the plane in the flight simulator. 他使用操纵杆在飞行模拟器中控制飞机。|  The drone is controlled by a joystick.  这架无人机是用操纵杆控制的。 | synonyms: control stick, lever, controller | antonyms: N/A | noun
