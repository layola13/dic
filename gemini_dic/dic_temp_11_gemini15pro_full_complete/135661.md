nonpreferences:/ˌnɑːnˈprɛfrənsɪz/ | n. | 非偏好，不偏爱 | Lack of preference or aversion; absence of a strong feeling for or against something. |  Many questionnaires about food choices now include a "nonpreferences" section. 许多关于食物选择的问卷现在都包含一个“非偏好”部分。|  The survey revealed consumer nonpreferences for certain product features.  这项调查揭示了消费者对某些产品特征的不偏爱。 | 近义词: indifferences, neutrality, apathy | 反义词: preferences, favorites | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + preferences (noun from Latin "praeferre" meaning "to set before, prefer")
