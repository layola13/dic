emersion:/ɪˈmɜːrʒən/ | n. | 浮现，出现；露出(水面) |The act of emerging or coming out of something (especially a fluid).| The emersion of the submarine after 24 hours underwater was a welcome sight.  潜艇在水下24小时后浮出水面，真是令人欣喜的景象。| 近义词：emergence, appearance, surfacing | 反义词：immersion, submersion | 词性: 名词

emersion:/ɪˈmɜːrʒən/ | n. |(尤指礼拜仪式后的)再现，重现 |Reappearance, especially after a ritual immersion.|  The emersion from the mikveh symbolizes spiritual cleansing and renewal. 从浸礼池中出来象征着精神的净化和更新。| 近义词：reappearance, return | 反义词：disappearance | 词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin *ēmersiōn-, from Latin ēmersus, past participle of ēmergere ‘emerge’.
