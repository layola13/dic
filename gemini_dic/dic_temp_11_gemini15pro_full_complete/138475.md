novellike:/ˈnɒvəlˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像小说的；新奇的 | Resembling a novel, especially in being lengthy, complex, or fictitious. |  The movie had a novellike plot, full of twists and turns. 这部电影的情节像小说一样，充满了曲折。| synonyms: fictional, imaginative, inventive, creative  | antonyms: factual, real, true, unimaginative | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From novel + -like.  "Novel" originates from the Italian word "novella," meaning "new," "news," or "short story of something new."  "-like" is a suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
