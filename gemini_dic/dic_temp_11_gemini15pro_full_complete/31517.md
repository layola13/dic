charrs:/tʃɑːrz/| n.|红点鲑；炭火烤鱼|A group of freshwater fish in the salmon family (Salmonidae) found in cold, clear lakes and streams.  They are closely related to trout and salmon.  Sometimes the singular form is also "charr".|We caught several charrs in the alpine lake. 我们在高山湖泊中捕到了几条红点鲑。|近义词：trout, salmon|反义词:|noun
