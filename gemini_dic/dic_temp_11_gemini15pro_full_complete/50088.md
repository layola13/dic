dentes:/ˈdɛntiːz/
n.
teeth
The plural form of "dens" (tooth), meaning teeth in Latin.  It is used primarily in a scientific or medical context.
The dentist examined the patient's dentes. 牙医检查了病人的牙齿。
synonyms: teeth
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a physical body part)
