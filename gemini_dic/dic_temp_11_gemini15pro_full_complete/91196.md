hydraulics: /haɪˈdrɔːlɪks/ | n. | 液压系统，液压学 | The study and application of the mechanical properties of liquids, particularly confined liquids used to generate, control, and transmit power.| The car's brakes use hydraulics. 这辆车的刹车使用液压系统。 | Synonyms: fluid power, fluid mechanics | Antonyms: pneumatics | noun
