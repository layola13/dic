outechoed:/ˌaʊtˈɛkoʊd/
|v.|胜过回声；盖过回声；在回声中超越|To surpass or exceed in echoing; to be louder or more resonant than an echo.|Her voice outechoed the thunder. 她的声音盖过了雷声。|近义词：outdo, surpass, exceed|反义词：muffled, subdued|词性:动词
词源:  A combination of the prefix "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) and the verb "echoed" (the past participle of "echo").
