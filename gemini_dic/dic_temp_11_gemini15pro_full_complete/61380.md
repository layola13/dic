emblazon:/ɪmˈbleɪzən/ | v. | 装饰；纹饰；炫耀；宣扬 | To decorate or adorn richly, especially with prominent markings; to display or celebrate publicly. | The knight's shield was emblazoned with his family crest. 骑士的盾牌上装饰着他的家族徽章。 | The company's logo was emblazoned on the side of the building. 公司的标志装饰在大楼的侧面。 | 近义词: adorn, decorate, ornament, embellish | 反义词: obscure, conceal, hide | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "emblazoner," from "en-" (in, on) + "blason" (coat of arms). Ultimately from Frankish "*blaso" (shield).
