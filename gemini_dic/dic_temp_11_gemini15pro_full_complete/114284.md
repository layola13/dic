mainlines:/ˈmeɪnlaɪnz/ | n. | 主要线路；干线；主流；（复数）基本方针；主要策略 |Main transportation routes, the most important lines in a system or network; the basic approaches or strategies. |The mainlines of the company's new strategy were presented at the meeting. 公司新战略的主要方针在会议上进行了介绍。| synonyms: trunk lines, principal routes, core strategies, main policies | antonyms: branch lines, secondary routes, minor strategies, peripheral policies | noun

mainline:/ˈmeɪnlaɪn/ | v. | 把…纳入主流；将…置于重要地位 | To bring something into the mainstream, to make something central or important. |The government is mainlining environmental concerns into its policy. 政府正将其环境问题纳入其政策的主流。| synonyms: prioritize, emphasize, highlight, centralize | antonyms: marginalize, downplay, minimize, sideline | verb
