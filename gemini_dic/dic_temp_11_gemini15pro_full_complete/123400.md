monophanous: /mɒˈnɒfənəs/ | adj. | 单调的，千篇一律的 | Having a uniform structure or composition throughout; homogeneous.  Characterized by a lack of variation; monotonous. | The monophanous landscape of the desert stretched for miles. 沙漠单调的景色绵延数英里。| The speaker's monophanous tone made the lecture incredibly boring. 演讲者单调的语气使讲座令人难以置信的无聊。 | 近义词: homogeneous, uniform, monotonous, unvaried | 反义词: heterogeneous, varied, diverse, multifaceted | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Greek "monos" (single, one) + "phainein" (to show, appear).  Meaning "appearing as one," hence uniform or unvaried.
