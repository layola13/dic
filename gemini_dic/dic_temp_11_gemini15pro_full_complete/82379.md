grozing:/ˈɡroʊzɪŋ/| n.| 凿槽；开榫；|The grozing of the wood was precise and clean. 木头的凿槽精确而干净。|英文解析：The act of shaping or finishing a material, especially wood or metal, by means of a grozing iron.|例句：The carpenter used a grozing iron for the fine detail work on the chair legs. 木匠用凿子对椅腿进行精细的加工。

The precise grozing ensured a tight fit for the joints. 精确的凿槽确保了接缝紧密贴合。|近义词：chiseling, carving, shaping|反义词:smoothing, filling|词性:noun
