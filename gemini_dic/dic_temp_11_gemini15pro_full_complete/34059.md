civic:/ˈsɪvɪk/| adj.|公民的，市民的，城市的|Relating to a city or town, or the people who live there; municipal. Concerned with the affairs of citizens.|例句：Civic pride is important for a thriving community.  公民的自豪感对于一个繁荣的社区很重要。|例句：The civic center hosts a variety of community events. 市政中心举办各种社区活动。|近义词：municipal, urban, community|反义词：rural, private, national|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From Latin *civicus*, from *civis* "citizen".
