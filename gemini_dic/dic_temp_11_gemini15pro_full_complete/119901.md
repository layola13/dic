militarizing:/ˈmɪlɪtəˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
军事化
The act of equipping with military forces and defenses or of giving a military character to.
The government is militarizing the police force. 政府正在将警察部队军事化。
militarizing:/ˈmɪlɪtəˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
军事化
Giving a military character to something; equipping with military forces and defenses.
They are militarizing the border region. 他们正在将边境地区军事化。
militarize (verb)
近义词: arm, fortify, strengthen
反义词: demilitarize, disarm, weaken
