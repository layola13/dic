nondualistic:/ˌnɒnˈdʒuːəlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非二元论的 |  Relating to or characterized by nondualism; the philosophical view that reality is not divided into two fundamental categories, such as mind and matter, or subject and object.  It emphasizes the interconnectedness and unity of all things. |  "His nondualistic perspective allowed him to see the inherent connection between himself and the universe." 他非二元论的视角让他能够看到自己与宇宙之间的内在联系。| synonyms: monistic, holistic, unified | antonyms: dualistic, binary | adjective
