donative:/ˈdoʊnətɪv/ /ˈdɒnətɪv/| adj. n.|捐赠的，捐赠物|Relating to or constituting a donation; given as a gift.  A gift or donation.|例句：The donative intent was clear. 捐赠意图很明确。|近义词：gift, present, contribution, offering|反义词：earned, purchased|词性：adjective, noun
donative:/ˈdoʊnətɪv/ /ˈdɒnətɪv/| n.|捐赠物|Something given as a gift. |例句：The museum gratefully accepted the donative. 博物馆感激地接受了这份捐赠。|近义词：gift, present, contribution, offering|反义词：purchase|词性：noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *dōnātīvus, from Latin dōnātus, past participle of dōnāre (“to give, present”), from dōnum (“gift”).
