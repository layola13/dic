cloop:/kluːp/
verb
发出汩汩声；发出类似液体流动的声音
to make a sound like liquid flowing or bubbling
The water clooped as it drained from the sink. 水从水槽里流出来，发出汩汩的声音。
gurgling, bubbling, splashing
dripping, trickling
v.
