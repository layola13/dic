dissidents:/ˈdɪsɪdənts/| n.|持不同政见者，异议者|A person who publicly disagrees with and criticizes their government.|例句：The government imprisoned several dissidents for speaking out against the regime. 政府囚禁了几名公开反对政权的持不同政见者。

例句：Many dissidents were forced into exile. 许多持不同政见者被迫流亡。|近义词：rebel, protester, objector, nonconformist, iconoclast|反义词：conformist, loyalist|词性:Noun


dissident:/ˈdɪsɪdənt/| adj.|持不同政见的，异议的| disagreeing, especially with an established religious or political system, organization, or belief.|例句：Dissident voices are becoming increasingly common on social media.  在社交媒体上，持不同政见的声音越来越普遍。

例句：The dissident group was quickly silenced by the authorities.  异议团体很快就被当局压制了。|近义词：rebellious, dissenting, unorthodox, nonconformist|反义词：conformist, orthodox, loyal|词性:Adjective
