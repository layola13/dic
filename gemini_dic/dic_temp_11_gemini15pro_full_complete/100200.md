interresistibility:/ˌɪntərɪˌzɪstəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可抗拒性，极大的吸引力 | The quality of being so attractive or compelling as to be impossible to resist. |  Her interresistibility charmed everyone in the room. 她的魅力让房间里的每个人都为之倾倒。|  His interresistibility made him a natural leader. 他不可抗拒的魅力使他成为天生的领袖。 | 近义词：allure, magnetism, charm, appeal | 反义词：repulsiveness, unattractiveness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From inter- (meaning "between, among, mutually, reciprocally") + resist (from Latin resistere "to stand back, withstand") + -ible (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of, fit for, tending to") + -ity (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).
