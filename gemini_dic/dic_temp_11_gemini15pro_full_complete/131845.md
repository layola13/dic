nonconstituents:/ˌnɒnkənˈstɪtʃuənts/| n.| 非成分，非构成成分 |Elements of a sentence that do not form a syntactic unit.  A sequence of words that does not function as a unit in a sentence.  |The words "the dog" are constituents, while "dog barked" in "The dog barked loudly" are nonconstituents if taken in isolation from "loudly". 这句话里，“the dog”是成分，而如果把“dog barked”从“loudly”中孤立出来，那么“dog barked”就不是成分。

The sequence "ran quickly" in "The dog ran quickly" is a constituent, whereas "dog ran" is a nonconstituent. 在“The dog ran quickly”这句话中，“ran quickly”是一个成分，而“dog ran”则不是。|近义词: non-units, fragments|反义词: constituents, components|词性: noun
