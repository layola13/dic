assertive:/əˈsɜːtɪv/ | adj. | 断言的；自信的；坚决主张的 | Having or showing a confident and forceful personality.  | She was very assertive in the meeting.  她在会议上非常自信坚定。 |  confident, forceful, decisive, self-assured | timid, hesitant, passive, submissive | adjective

assertiveness:/əˈsɜːtɪvnəs/ | n. | 坚定自信；果断 |  The quality of being confident and expressing your opinions forcefully. | Her assertiveness helped her get the promotion. 她的自信果断帮助她升职了。| confidence, self-assurance, decisiveness | timidity, hesitancy, passivity | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *assertīvus, from Latin assertus (past participle of asserere ‘claim, maintain’), from ad- ‘to’ + serere ‘join’.
