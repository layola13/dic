balefire:/ˈbeɪlˌfaɪər/| n.|魔火，毁灭之火，信标|A magically destructive fire, often associated with cleansing or destruction so complete that it leaves no trace.  It's often mentioned in fantasy literature, drawing from its use in Tolkien's works.|The balefire consumed the enemy army, leaving no bodies behind. 魔火吞噬了敌军，没有留下任何尸体。|Near synonyms: hellfire, wildfire, conflagration|Near antonyms:  holy fire, sacred flame|noun
