nonreviewability:/ˌnɑːnriˌvjuːəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可审查性，不可复审性
The state or quality of being exempt from judicial review.  This often applies to certain decisions made by government agencies or other bodies.
The nonreviewability of the agency's decision frustrated the plaintiffs.  该机构决定的不可审查性使原告感到沮丧。
近义词：immunity, exemption
反义词：reviewability, appealability
词性: noun


Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + review (verb meaning "to examine again") + -able (suffix forming adjectives) + -ity (suffix forming nouns)
