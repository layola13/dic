dogmatist:/ˈdɒɡmətɪst/ (UK), /ˈdɔːɡmətɪst/ (US)| n. |教条主义者，独断论者|A person who asserts opinions as if they were unquestionable truths.|He was known as a dogmatist, unwilling to consider any viewpoint but his own. 他被称为教条主义者，不愿考虑除自己以外的任何观点。|近义词：doctrinaire, authoritarian, rigid, inflexible|反义词：freethinker, skeptic, open-minded|noun
