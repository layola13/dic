hyphenates:/ˈhaɪfənˌeɪts/| n.|连字符连接的词，用连字符连接|A compound word formed by joining two or more words with a hyphen. |Examples of hyphenates include "mother-in-law" and "up-to-date."|Synonyms: compound words, hyphenated words|Antonyms: single words, unhyphenated words|noun
