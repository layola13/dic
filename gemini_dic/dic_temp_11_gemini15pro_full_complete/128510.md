newsworthiness:/ˈnjuːzwɜːrðinəs/ | n. | 新闻价值；报道价值 | The quality of being sufficiently interesting to be reported in news bulletins. | The newsworthiness of the story was questioned by some.  这个故事的新闻价值受到了一些人的质疑。 | synonyms:  relevance, significance, importance, interest, value | antonyms: insignificance, unimportance, triviality | noun
