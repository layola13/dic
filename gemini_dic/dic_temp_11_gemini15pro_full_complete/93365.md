illegitimately:/ˌɪlɪˈdʒɪtɪmətli/
adv.
非法地，不正当地
in a way that is not allowed by law or the rules
He acquired the property illegitimately. 他非法获得了这笔财产。
Synonym: unlawfully, wrongly, illegally
Antonym: legitimately, legally, lawfully
词性: 副词
