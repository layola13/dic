concerned:/kənˈsɜːrnd/ /kənˈsərnd/| adj.|担心的，有关的，关心的|Worried, anxious, or troubled about something. Also, involved in or affected by something.|例句：I am concerned about your health. 我很担心你的健康。|近义词：worried, anxious, apprehensive, solicitous, involved|反义词：unconcerned, indifferent, apathetic|词性:形容词
concerned:/kənˈsɜːrnd/ /kənˈsərnd/| adj.|相关的，有牵连的|Related to or involved with something.|例句：The report is concerned with the effects of pollution. 这份报告是关于污染的影响的。|近义词：related, relevant, connected, pertinent|反义词：unrelated, irrelevant|词性:形容词

词源: From the Latin "concernere" (to mix together, to sift), which later evolved into Old French "concerner" and then Middle English "concernen". The original sense of "mixing together" gradually shifted to "relate to" and "be involved in."
