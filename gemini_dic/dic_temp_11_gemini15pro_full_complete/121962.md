missions:/ˈmɪʃənz/
n.
任务，使命；代表团；布道
an important assignment carried out for political, religious, or commercial purposes, typically involving travel.
The diplomat was sent on a mission to China.  这位外交官被派往中国执行任务。
assignments, tasks, duties, undertakings
holidays, vacations, leisure
