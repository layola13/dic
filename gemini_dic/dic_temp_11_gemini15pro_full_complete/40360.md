contraction:/kənˈtrækʃn/| n.|（单词或短语的）缩略形式| "Can't" is a contraction of "cannot". "Can't"是"cannot"的缩写形式。|abbreviation, shortening|expansion, lengthening|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *contractiōn- (stem of contractiō), from Latin contractus, past participle of contrahere (“to draw together, contract”), from con- (“together”) + trahere (“to draw, pull”).
