birders:/ˈbɜːrdərz/
noun
观鸟者
People who engage in the activity of observing birds, typically as a hobby.
例句：The birders gathered at dawn to spot the rare migratory birds. 观鸟者们聚集在黎明时分，为了观测稀有的候鸟。

例句：Many birders travel long distances to add new species to their life lists. 许多观鸟者长途跋涉，只为将新物种添加到他们的观鸟记录中。
近义词：birdwatchers, ornithologists (更专业)
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词)
词性: noun (名词)
