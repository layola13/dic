celebrated: /ˈsɛləˌbreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 著名的，有名的，受人称赞的 | Widely known and admired; famous. |  This restaurant is celebrated for its exquisite French cuisine. 这家餐厅以其精致的法国菜肴而闻名。 | The celebrated author signed copies of his latest book.  这位著名的作家签署了他最新著作的副本。 | 近义词：renowned, famous, acclaimed, distinguished | 反义词：unknown, obscure, infamous | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the Latin "celebrare," meaning "to frequent, honor, praise."  The past participle "celebratus" became the source of the English word.
