earphone:/ˈɪərfəʊn/ (UK), /ˈɪrfoʊn/ (US)| n.|耳机|A device that converts electrical signals into sound and is worn in or over the ear, typically in pairs connected by a wire or wirelessly, for listening to music, radio broadcasts, etc.|I listen to music with my earphones on the bus. 我在公交车上用耳机听音乐。|Synonyms: headphones, earbuds|Antonyms: loudspeaker, speaker|noun
