formalizer:/ˈfɔːrməlaɪzər/ | n. | 形式化者;规范化者 |Someone or something that formalizes something (makes it more formal or official).| The treaty acted as a formalizer of the peace agreement. 该条约使和平协议正式化。|  近义词: codifier, systematizer, regulator | 反义词: informalizer (less common) | 词性: noun

formalize:/ˈfɔːrməlaɪz/ | v. | 使正式化；使规范化; 形式化 |To make something more formal or official.|  We need to formalize this agreement with a written contract. 我们需要签订书面合同，使这项协议正式化。| 近义词: codify, systematize, regulate, standardize | 反义词: informalize (less common) | 词性: verb
