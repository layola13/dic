antimonite:/ˈæn.tɪ.mə.naɪt/ | n. |锑矿，辉锑矿|A metallic mineral consisting of antimony trisulfide (Sb2S3), the principal ore of antimony. |The miners discovered a rich vein of antimonite. 矿工们发现了一条富含锑矿的矿脉。|Synonym: stibnite|Antonym: N/A|Noun
