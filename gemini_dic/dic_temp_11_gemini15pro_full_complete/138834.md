nunnery:/ˈnʌnəri/ | n. | 女修道院；尼姑庵 | A building or group of buildings where nuns live as a religious community. |  例句：The young woman decided to devote her life to God and entered a nunnery.  这位年轻女子决定献身于上帝，进入了一家女修道院。

例句：The nunnery provided a peaceful sanctuary away from the bustling city. 这座女修道院提供了一个远离喧嚣城市的宁静圣地。

近义词：convent, monastery | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑世俗场所) secular institution | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *nonnerie*, from Anglo-Norman *nonerie*, from Old French *nonerie*, from *none* (“nun”) + *-erie* (suffix forming nouns denoting places, occupations, etc.). *None* from Latin *nonna* (“nun”).
