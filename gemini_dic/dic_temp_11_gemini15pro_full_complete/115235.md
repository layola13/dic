manikin:/ˈmænɪkɪn/| n.|人体模型；时装模特儿；侏儒|A life-size model of the human body, typically used for displaying clothes or for anatomical study.  Also, a very small person; a dwarf.|The designer draped the fabric on the manikin.  设计师把布料披在人体模型上。

The tailor adjusted the manikin to fit the customer's measurements.  裁缝调整了人体模型以适应顾客的尺寸。|near synonym:dummy, model, figure|opposite:giant|noun
