insconce:/ɪnˈskɒns/ /ɪnˈskɑːns/| vt.|安置；使…舒适地坐定；牢固地放置|to place or settle securely or snugly|He was insconced in a comfortable armchair by the fire. 他舒舒服服地坐在火炉旁的扶手椅上。|近义词：settle, nestle, ensconce|反义词: displace, remove|verb
