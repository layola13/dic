electees:/ɪˌlɛkˈtiːz/
n.
당선者；当选人
Those chosen by election.
The electees will begin their term next month.  当选者们将于下个月开始他们的任期。
The newly elected officials, the winners of the election.
Synonyms: winners, victors, successful candidates
Antonyms: losers, defeated candidates
