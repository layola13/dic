hypercautiousness:/ˌhaɪpərˈkɔːʃəsnəs/| n. |过度谨慎，过分小心|The state of being excessively cautious or wary.|His hypercautiousness prevented him from taking any risks. 他的过度谨慎使他不敢冒险。|近义词: overcautiousness, wariness, prudence, circumspection|反义词: recklessness, imprudence, carelessness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (meaning "over, excessive") + cautiousness (from cautious + -ness).  The root of "cautious" traces back to Latin "cautus" meaning "wary, heedful, attentive."
