anagram:/ˈænəɡræm/| n. |字谜，变形词|A word or phrase formed by rearranging the letters of another, such as *cinema*, formed from *iceman*.|例句：
"Listen" is an anagram of "silent".
“listen”是“silent”的变形词。

The word "dormitory" can be rearranged into "dirty room," a fitting anagram.
“dormitory”（宿舍）这个词可以重新排列成“dirty room”（脏房间），一个恰如其分的变形词。

|近义词：transposition, permutation, scramble|反义词: None|词性:noun
