look-and-say:/ˈlʊk ən seɪ/ | adj. n.| 看图说话;报数序列 | A sequence generated by describing the previous term.  It is also known as the Morris sequence.  | The look-and-say sequence starts with 1, then 11 (one 1), then 21 (two 1s), then 1211 (one 2, one 1), and so on. |近义词:Describe-and-generate,  Count-and-say |反义词: N/A | adjective, noun
