formulator:/ˈfɔːrmjʊleɪtər/ | n. | 配方设计师；制定者；规划者 |  A person who creates or devises something, especially a plan, system, or product formulation. | The formulator of the new policy has extensive experience in the field.  这个新政策的制定者在该领域拥有丰富的经验。| Synonyms: creator, developer, inventor, designer, planner, architect, author, originator  | Antonyms: destroyer, demolisher | noun

formulate:/ˈfɔːrmjʊleɪt/ | v. |  规划；制定；构想；确切表达 | Create or devise methodically (a strategy or a proposal). Express (something) in a clear or concise way. |  The committee formulated a plan to address the issue. 委员会制定了一个解决问题的计划。 She struggled to formulate her thoughts clearly. 她努力清晰地表达她的想法。| Synonyms: devise, conceive, frame, develop, articulate, express, specify | Antonyms: destroy, ruin, abolish | verb
