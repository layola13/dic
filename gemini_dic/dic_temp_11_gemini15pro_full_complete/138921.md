nutritionalist:/njuːˈtrɪʃənɪst/| n.|营养学家；营养师|A person who studies or is an expert in nutrition.|例句：The nutritionalist recommended a balanced diet. 营养学家建议均衡饮食。

例句：I consulted a nutritionalist about my diet. 我咨询了一位营养师关于我的饮食。|近义词：dietitian, nutrition expert|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From nutrition + -ist.  Nutrition comes from the Latin word "nutritio" meaning "nourishment".
