odium:/ˈoʊdiəm/| n.|憎恶，仇恨|Intense hatred or dislike, especially toward a person or thing regarded as contemptible, despicable, or repugnant.|The politician faced public odium after the corruption scandal.  这位政客在腐败丑闻后遭到了公众的憎恨。|Synonyms: hatred, loathing, detestation, abhorrence, aversion|Antonyms: love, admiration, respect|noun
