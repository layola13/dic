hallucinogenics:/həˌluːsɪnəˈdʒɛnɪks/
noun
致幻剂；迷幻药
Substances that cause hallucinations.
Some mushrooms contain powerful hallucinogenics.  一些蘑菇含有强效致幻剂。
Synonyms: psychedelics, hallucinogens
Antonyms: sobering agents, depressants
n.


hallucinogenic:/həˌluːsɪnəˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
致幻的
Causing hallucinations.
The plant has hallucinogenic properties.  这种植物具有致幻特性。
Synonyms: psychedelic, psychotomimetic
Antonyms: sobering, depressant
adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From hallucination + -genic.  Hallucination comes from Late Latin hallucinatio, from Latin hallucinari "to wander in mind, talk idly, dream," from alucinari "to wander in mind."  -Genic comes from Greek -genes "born, produced."
