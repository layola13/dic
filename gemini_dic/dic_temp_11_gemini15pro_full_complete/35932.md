cocurators:/ˌkoʊˈkjʊreɪtərz/| n.| 共同策展人 |A group of people who work together to curate something, typically an exhibition or collection.| The cocurators worked tirelessly to bring the exhibition to life.  共同策展人为使展览栩栩如生而不知疲倦地工作。 |synonyms: joint curators, collaborating curators| antonyms: sole curator| noun
