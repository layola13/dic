humective:/hjuːˈmɛktɪv/ | adj. | 保湿的，润湿的 |  Adding moisture; moisturizing.  Relating to or characterized by the absorption or retention of moisture.  | This humective cream is perfect for dry skin.  这款保湿霜非常适合干性皮肤。|  The scientist developed a humective material for wound dressings.  这位科学家开发了一种用于伤口敷料的保湿材料。| synonyms: moisturizing, hydrating, emollient | antonyms: drying, dehydrating | adjective

humectant:/hjuːˈmɛktənt/ | n. | 保湿剂 | A substance that promotes the retention of moisture. | Glycerin is a common humectant used in skincare products. 甘油是一种常用的护肤品保湿剂。|  The food manufacturer added humectants to keep the bread fresh. 食品制造商添加了保湿剂以保持面包新鲜。| synonyms: moisturizer, hydrating agent | antonyms: desiccant, drying agent | noun
