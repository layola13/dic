bede:/biːd/ | v. | 祈祷，恳求；（诗、文）是…的标志，预示 | To pray or make supplication; (literary) to be a sign or warning of. | He will bede the gods for forgiveness. 他将祈求神灵的宽恕。| synonyms: pray, entreat, implore, beseech, supplicate | antonyms: command, demand | verb

bede:/biːd/ | n. | (古) 祈祷，祷告 | (archaic) A prayer. | The monk offered a bede for the king's health.  这位僧侣为国王的健康做了一个祷告。 | synonyms: prayer, supplication, petition | antonyms: curse, imprecation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bēdan, from Proto-Germanic *bidaną, from Proto-Indo-European *gʷʰedʰ- (“to ask, pray”). Cognate with Old Saxon biddian, Old Frisian bidda, Old High German bitten (“to ask, beg”), Gothic 𐌱𐌹𐌳𐌾𐌰𐌽 (bidjan), Old Norse biðja, Latin fātum (“fate, destiny”), Old Irish guidim (“I pray”).
