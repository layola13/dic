nonanticipation:/nɒnˌæntɪsɪˈpeɪʃn/ | n. |不预期，不预料；无预期|The principle of nonanticipation states that current decisions should not be based on future expectations.|例句：The company's nonanticipation policy prevents them from making decisions based on projected market changes. 公司的不预料政策阻止他们根据预测的市场变化做出决策。

例句：Nonanticipation of risks can lead to project failure. 不预料风险会导致项目失败。|近义词：lack of foresight, unpreparedness|反义词：foresight, anticipation|词性: 名词
