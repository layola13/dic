hit-on:/hɪt ˈɑːn/ /hɪt ˈɒn/| phrasal verb |搭讪；突然想到；发现|To make a suggestive or flirtatious remark to someone, often a stranger. To discover something unexpectedly.|He tried to hit on me at the bar last night. 他昨晚在酒吧里试图搭讪我。

She hit on a great idea for the project. 她突然想到了一个很棒的项目创意。|近义词：flirt with, chat up, come on to, discover, find, stumble upon|反义词: ignore, disregard|词性:短语动词

hit-on:/hɪt ˈɑːn/ /hɪt ˈɒn/| n. |搭讪|An act of making a suggestive or flirtatious remark to someone.|Her constant hit-ons were getting annoying. 她不断的搭讪让人很烦。|近义词：pass, advance|反义词: rejection|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "hit" (strike) + "on" (upon). The flirtatious meaning developed in the mid-20th century.
