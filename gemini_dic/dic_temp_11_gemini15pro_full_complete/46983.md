darwinists:/ˈdɑːrwɪnɪsts/
n.
达尔文主义者
A believer in Darwinism; an advocate of the theory of evolution by natural selection.
Darwinists believe that natural selection is the primary driver of evolution. 达尔文主义者认为自然选择是进化的主要驱动力。
Many Darwinists argue against creationism. 许多达尔文主义者反对神创论。
近义词:evolutionists, proponents of natural selection
反义词:creationists, anti-evolutionists
词性: Noun

Etymology: From Charles Darwin (1809-1882), English naturalist who formulated the theory of evolution by natural selection + -ist.
