outgrossing:/ˌaʊtˈɡroʊsɪŋ/
verb
超过…的总收入
surpassing in gross revenue or earnings
The new movie is outgrossing all previous releases this year. 这部新电影的总收入超过了今年所有以前上映的电影。
近义词: outperforming, exceeding, surpassing
反义词: underperforming, lagging behind
词性: verb
