aedileship:/ˈiːdɪlʃɪp/ | n. | 市政官的职位；市政官任期 | The office or tenure of an aedile.  A position in Roman government responsible for public works, games, and markets. |  His aedileship was marked by the construction of several new aqueducts. 他的市政官任期以建造几条新的沟渠为标志。|  Synonyms: magistracy, office, position | Antonyms:  None (lack of a direct antonym for a specific Roman office) | noun
