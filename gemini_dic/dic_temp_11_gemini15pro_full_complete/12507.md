aspersion:/əˈspɜːrʒən/
n.
诽谤，中伤
an attack on the reputation or integrity of someone or something.
The politician's reputation was damaged by the aspersions cast upon his character.
这位政治家的声誉因对他品格的诽谤而受损。

近义词：slur, defamation, calumny, vilification
反义词：praise, commendation, acclaim

词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *aspersio(n-), from Latin aspergere ‘to sprinkle’, from ad- ‘to’ + spargere ‘to scatter’.
