obsessively:/əbˈsɛsɪvli/
adverb
痴迷地，着魔地
in a way that shows an obsession
He checks his phone obsessively. 他不停地查看手机。
Synonyms: compulsively, fanatically, fixatedly
Antonyms: moderately, casually, indifferently
词性: 副词
