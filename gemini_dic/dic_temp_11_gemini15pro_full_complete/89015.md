hollowly:/ˈhɒləʊli/ (UK), /ˈhɑːloʊli/ (US)| adv.|空洞地，空虚地，虚伪地|In a way that suggests a hollow space or lack of sincerity.  It describes the manner of speaking or acting when something is insincere, lacking substance, or echoing emptily.|She laughed hollowly, her eyes betraying her sadness. 她空洞地笑了笑，眼神却流露出悲伤。|Synonyms: emptily, insincerely, falsely, superficially|Antonyms: sincerely, genuinely, truthfully, heartily|adverb
