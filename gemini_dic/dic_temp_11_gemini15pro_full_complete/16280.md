baptistery: /ˈbæptɪstri/ (US), /ˈbæptɪstəri/ (UK)| n.| 洗礼堂，浸礼池|A part of a church or other religious building used for baptism.  A building standing alone, used for Christian baptism.|The baptistery of St. John in Florence is famous for its bronze doors. 佛罗伦萨的圣约翰洗礼堂以其青铜门而闻名。|synonyms: font, immersion pool|antonyms: N/A|noun
