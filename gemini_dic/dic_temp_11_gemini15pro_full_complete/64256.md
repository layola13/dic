equipollent:/ˌiːkwɪˈpɒlənt/ | adj. | 等价的，相等的，等效的 | Having equal power, force, or significance.  Equivalent in effect or value. | The two arguments are equipollent; they both lead to the same conclusion. 这两个论点是等价的；它们都得出相同的结论。 |  The equipollent forces balanced each other out.  这两种等效的力相互抵消。 | synonyms: equivalent, equal, tantamount, commensurate | antonyms: unequal, disparate, dissimilar | adjective
