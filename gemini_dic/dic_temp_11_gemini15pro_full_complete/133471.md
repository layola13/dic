nonfeeblenesses:/ˌnɑːnˈfiːblnəsɪz/
noun
非虚弱；强壮；坚强
The state or quality of not being feeble; strength; robustness.
His nonfeebleness surprised everyone after his long illness.  他久病之后展现出的强壮令所有人惊讶。
The nonfeebleness of her spirit allowed her to overcome many obstacles. 她坚强的精神使她克服了许多障碍。
Synonym: strength, robustness, vigor, power, potency
Antonym: feebleness, weakness, frailty, debility, infirmity
