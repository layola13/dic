chronogram: /ˈkrɒnəɡræm/ (UK) /ˈkrɑːnəɡræm/ (US) | n. | 年表铭文; 纪年铭 | An inscription, phrase, or other form of writing in which specific letters, when rearranged or treated as numerals, express a particular date or epoch. | The chronogram on the building's cornerstone indicated the year of its construction.  这栋建筑基石上的年表铭文标明了它的建造年份。 | Example: A chronogram carved into the archway revealed the date of the castle's completion.  拱门上雕刻的年表铭文揭示了城堡竣工的日期。| synonym: inscription, date inscription | antonym: N/A | noun
