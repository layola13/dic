digressive:/daɪˈɡrɛsɪv/ /dɪˈɡrɛsɪv/
adjective
离题的；跑题的
Tending to depart from the main point or subject; rambling.
The speaker's digressive style made it hard to follow his train of thought. 演讲者东拉西扯的风格使人难以跟上他的思路。
Synonyms: rambling, tangential, discursive, wandering
Antonyms: concise, succinct, focused, to the point
词性: adjective
