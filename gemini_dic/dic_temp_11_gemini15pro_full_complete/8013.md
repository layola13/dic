anthraciferous:/ˌænθrəˈsɪfərəs/
adjective
产无烟煤的；含无烟煤的
Containing or yielding anthracite coal.
The Pennsylvania mountains are known for their anthraciferous strata.  宾夕法尼亚的山脉以其含无烟煤的地层而闻名。
The region is rich in anthraciferous deposits.  这个地区富含无烟煤矿床。
Near synonyms: coal-bearing, carboniferous
Antonyms: barren, unproductive
词性: adjective
