faffs:/fæfs/
| n. pl. | 琐事；闲混；瞎忙| Spending time on faffs is unproductive.  把时间花在琐事上是没有效率的。|例句：He was faffing about with his phone instead of working. 他一直在摆弄手机而不是工作。|近义词：fuss, fiddle, dawdle|反义词:focus, concentrate
| v. intr. (faffs, faffing, faffed)| 闲混；瞎忙；浪费时间| Stop faffing and get on with it! 别磨蹭了，赶快做吧！|例句：She faffed around all morning and didn't get anything done. 她整个上午都在瞎忙，什么也没做成。|近义词：fuss, fiddle, potter|反义词:hurry, expedite
