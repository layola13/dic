arousing:/əˈraʊzɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人兴奋的；激起性欲的 | Exciting or stimulating, especially sexually. |  The movie had several arousing scenes. 这部电影有几个令人兴奋的场景。

The politician delivered an arousing speech.  这位政治家发表了激动人心的演讲。 | 近义词: exciting, stimulating, titillating, provocative | 反义词: dull, boring, uninteresting, soporific | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the verb "arouse," which comes from Middle English *arousen*, from Old French *areser* "to raise," ultimately from Latin *ad- "to" + *surgere* "to rise."
