ferriprussic:/ˌfɛriˈprʌsɪk/
adjective
铁氰化物的，含铁氰酸根的
Containing or derived from ferricyanide.  Relating to or containing the ferricyanide ion.
Ferriprussic acid is a deep reddish brown crystalline solid. 铁氰酸是一种深红棕色结晶固体。
Potassium ferriprussic is used in blueprints. 铁氰化钾用于蓝图制作。
Synonym: ferricyanide
Antonym: ferrocyanide (although not a direct opposite in meaning, it represents the reduced form of iron cyanide)
词性: adjective
