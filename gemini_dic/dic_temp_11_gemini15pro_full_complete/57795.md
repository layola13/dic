dredgers:/ˈdrɛdʒərz/
noun
挖泥船; 疏浚机
A machine used to remove material from the bottom of a body of water.
The dredgers were working to deepen the channel. 挖泥船正在加深河道。
synonyms: excavator, digger
antonyms: filler
