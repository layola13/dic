immure:/ɪˈmjʊər/| v. |禁闭，监禁；封闭|To enclose or confine within walls; imprison.|The king immured the traitor in a dungeon. 国王将叛徒囚禁在地牢里。|Synonyms: imprison, confine, incarcerate, enclose|Antonyms: liberate, free, release|verb
Etymology: From Latin *immurare* "to wall in," from *in-* "in" + *murus* "wall."
