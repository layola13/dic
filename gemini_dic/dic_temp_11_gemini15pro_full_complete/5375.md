alluvium:/əˈluːviəm/ | n. | 冲积层；冲积土 | Deposits of earth, sand, and other transported matter left by water flowing over land not permanently submerged. | The fertile alluvial soil of the Nile delta has supported agriculture for millennia. 尼罗河三角洲肥沃的冲积土已经支撑了数千年的农业。 | Example: The river deposited a thick layer of alluvium on the floodplain. 这条河在漫滩上沉积了厚厚的一层冲积土。 | Synonyms: sediment, deposit, silt, drift | Antonyms: bedrock | noun

alluvial:/əˈluːviəl/ | adj. | 冲积的；由冲积土形成的 | Relating to or consisting of alluvium. | The alluvial plains are rich in minerals. 冲积平原富含矿物质。 | Example: Alluvial gold is found in the riverbeds. 冲积金在河床中被发现。 | Synonyms: sedimentary | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could use "igneous" or "metamorphic" in a geological context) | adjective
