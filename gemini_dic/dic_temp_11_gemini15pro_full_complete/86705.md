helmed:/helmd/ | v. | 掌舵；领导；指挥 | to be in charge of something, especially a ship or an organization | The company is now helmed by a new CEO.  该公司现在由一位新的首席执行官领导。|  The experienced captain helmed the ship through the storm. 经验丰富的船长掌舵着船只度过了风暴。 | lead, manage, direct, control, command |  follow | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From helm (n.) + -ed. The noun "helm" comes from Old English "helma," meaning "rudder, tiller," related to Old Norse "hjálm," meaning "helm, rudder."
