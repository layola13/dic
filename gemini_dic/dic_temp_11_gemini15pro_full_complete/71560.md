flashcards:/ˈflæʃˌkɑːrdz/ | n. | 抽认卡 | Small cards with words, facts, or other information written on them, used for learning or memorizing. | I use flashcards to study vocabulary. 我用抽认卡学习词汇。| synonyms: index cards, study cards, note cards | antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "flash" (quick, brief) + "cards" (playing cards or pieces of card).  The term emerged in the late 19th century, referring to cards used for quick displays of information, often in educational settings.
