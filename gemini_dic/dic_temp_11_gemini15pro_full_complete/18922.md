bespatter:/bɪˈspætər/
verb
溅污，污蔑
To cover with drops or spots of something; to soil by splashing. Also, to damage the reputation of someone by slander or libel.
The children bespattered each other with mud. 孩子们互相溅了一身泥。
His political opponents bespattered his character with false accusations. 他的政敌用不实的指控玷污了他的名声。

近义词:splash, splatter, smear, tarnish
反义词:cleanse, purify

词性:v.
词源:From be- + spatter.
