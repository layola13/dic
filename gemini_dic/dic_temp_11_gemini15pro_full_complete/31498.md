charmless:/ˈtʃɑːrmləs/| adj.|无魅力的；不讨人喜欢的；乏味的|Lacking charm; unattractive or unpleasant.|The performance was charmless and dull.  这场演出缺乏魅力，很沉闷。|Synonyms: unattractive, unappealing, dull, insipid, tedious, boring|Antonyms: charming, attractive, appealing, captivating, enchanting|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From charm + -less.  Charm (n.) comes from Old French charme "magic charm, incantation, song," from Latin carmen "song, verse, incantation."  The suffix -less means "without."
