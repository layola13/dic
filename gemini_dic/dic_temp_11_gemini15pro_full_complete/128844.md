nigrescent:/naɪˈɡrɛsənt/
adjective
变黑的，发黑的
Becoming black or dark; darkening.
The nigrescent clouds threatened rain.  乌云密布，预示着即将下雨。
The solution turned nigrescent upon exposure to air. 溶液暴露在空气中后变黑了。
近义词: darkening, blackening, obscuring
反义词: brightening, lightening, whitening
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *nigrescēns, present participle of nigrēscere (“to grow black, become black”), from niger (“black”).
