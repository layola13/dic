chastened:/ˈtʃeɪsnd/ /ˈtʃeɪsənd/ | adj. | 惩戒的；磨炼的；受挫的； 谦恭的| Subdued and humbled, typically after a reprimand or misfortune. |  He felt chastened after his teacher scolded him for disrupting class. 他因扰乱课堂秩序被老师责骂后感到羞愧。

He returned from the meeting chastened and subdued. 他从会议回来后显得非常沮丧和顺从。 | 近义词：humbled, subdued, disciplined, corrected, reproved | 反义词: unrepentant, defiant, unashamed, emboldened | 词性: 形容词
