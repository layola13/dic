have-no-words-for:/hæv nəʊ wɜːdz fɔːr/ (British) /hæv noʊ wɜːrdz fɔːr/ (American) | idiom | speechless, at a loss for words, dumbfounded |  This idiom describes a feeling of being so overwhelmed by something (usually positive or negative to a great extent) that one cannot articulate their thoughts or feelings. It implies a strong emotional reaction. |  The beauty of the sunset was so breathtaking, I have no words for it. 夕阳的美丽令人叹为观止，我无法用言语形容。 | synonyms: speechless, dumbfounded, astonished, stunned, awestruck | antonyms: articulate, eloquent, expressive | idiom
