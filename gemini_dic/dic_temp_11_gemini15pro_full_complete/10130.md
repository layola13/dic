apologer: /əˈpɒlədʒər/ (UK) /əˈpɑːlədʒər/ (US)| n. |辩护者；辩解者； apologizer(美式)|A person who offers an argument in defense of something controversial. |She became an apologist for animal rights after witnessing the cruelty in factory farms. 她亲眼目睹了工厂化农场的残酷行为后，成为了动物权利的辩护者。| Example: He's a staunch apologist for the current regime. 他是现政权的坚定辩护者。|Synonyms: defender, advocate, supporter, proponent|Antonyms: critic, opponent, detractor|noun
