ashes:/ˈæʃɪz/| n.|灰烬，骨灰，遗骸|The ashes from the fire were still warm. 火灾后的灰烬仍然很热。|例句：The house was burned to ashes. 房子被烧成了灰烬。  Her ashes were scattered at sea. 她的骨灰撒在了海里。|近义词：remains, cinders, dust|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑新生、重建相关的词语，例如：renewal, rebirth)|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *æsc* (plural *æscu, ascan*), from Proto-Germanic *askōn* (plural), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂es- (“to burn”). Cognate with Old Frisian *asker*, Old Saxon *aska*, Old High German *asca*, Old Norse *aska*, Gothic *azgō* f.  The shift from singular *æsc* to plural *ashes* as the standard form is unusual in English.
