enticingly:/ɪnˈtaɪsɪŋli/ | adv. | 诱人地；迷人地 | In a way that attracts or tempts someone strongly; alluringly. | The aroma of freshly baked bread wafted enticingly from the kitchen.  新鲜出炉的面包的香味从厨房里诱人地飘来。| 近义词: alluringly, temptingly, seductively, attractively | 反义词: repulsively, off-puttingly, unattractively | 词性: 副词

词源: From entice + -ing + -ly.  Entice comes from Old French enticier "to set on fire," figuratively "to excite, provoke."
