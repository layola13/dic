overdescribe:/ˌōvərˌdɪˈskraɪb/| v.|过度描述，冗述|To describe something in excessive detail, often to a tedious or unnecessary extent.|She overdescribed the plot of the movie, spoiling the ending for me. 她过度描述了电影的情节，剧透了结局。|近义词：overelaborate, belabor, expatiate|反义词: understate, summarize|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From 'over-' (prefix meaning "excessive") + 'describe' (from Latin 'describere' - to write down, copy).
