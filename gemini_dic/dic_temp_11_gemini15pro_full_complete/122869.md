mollify:/ˈmɑːlɪfaɪ/ /ˈmɒlɪfaɪ/| vt.| 平息；抚慰；缓和|to make someone less angry or upset; to reduce the severity of something| I tried to mollify her by offering her a cup of tea. 我试图给她一杯茶来安抚她。| The government tried to mollify the public's fears about the economy. 政府试图缓和公众对经济的担忧。| synonyms: appease, placate, soothe, pacify | antonyms: aggravate, incite, provoke | verb
