endearment:/ɪnˈdɪərmənt/ |n.|爱慕；钟爱；爱称；亲昵的举动|Something that inspires affection.  A word or phrase expressing affection.|例句：Terms of endearment, such as "honey" or "sweetie," are common between romantic partners.  爱称，例如“亲爱的”或“甜心”，在情侣之间很常见。|例句：Her playful endearments made him feel loved and cherished. 她俏皮的亲昵举动让他感到被爱和珍惜。|近义词：affection, fondness, tenderness, caress, pet name|反义词：dislike, aversion, coldness, hostility|词性：名词

词源：Late Middle English: from endear + -ment.
