legato:/ləˈɡɑːtoʊ/ /leˈɡɑːtəʊ/
adjective
连奏的，圆滑的
smooth and flowing; without breaks between notes.
The pianist played the piece with a legato touch.  钢琴家以连奏的触感演奏了这首曲子。
smooth, flowing
detached, staccato
adjective

legato:/ləˈɡɑːtoʊ/ /leˈɡɑːtəʊ/
adverb
连奏地，圆滑地
in a smooth and flowing manner; without breaks between notes.
Play this passage legato.  请连奏地演奏这段乐句。
smoothly, flowing
detachedly, staccato
adverb


legato:/ləˈɡɑːtoʊ/ /leˈɡɑːtəʊ/
noun
连奏，连音奏法
a smooth and flowing style of playing music; the act of playing legato.
The violinist's legato was breathtaking.  小提琴家的连奏令人叹为观止。
smoothness, flow
detachment, staccato
noun

Etymology: Italian, from past participle of *legare* "to tie, bind," from Latin *ligare*.  The musical sense is from the idea of notes being "tied" together.
