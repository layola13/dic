festooning:/fɛˈstuːnɪŋ/
n.|装饰，以花彩装饰|The act of decorating with festoons. |The festooning of the hall for the wedding was extravagant. 婚礼大厅的装饰非常奢华。|decoration, adornment|removal, stripping
v.|以花彩装饰，结彩|To decorate with festoons; to adorn festively. |They festooned the streets with flags for the national holiday.  他们为国庆节用旗帜装饰街道。|decorate, adorn|strip, denude
