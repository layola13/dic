crowd-pleaser:/ˈkraʊdˌpliːzər/
n.
令人高兴的事物；取悦大众的事物
Something designed to be popular with a large audience.
The fireworks display was a real crowd-pleaser.  烟火表演真是令人高兴。

crowd-pleaser:/ˈkraʊdˌpliːzər/
n.
迎合大众的人
A person or thing that is designed to be popular with a large audience.
He's a crowd-pleaser, always telling jokes and making people laugh. 他是个迎合大众的人，总是讲笑话逗人笑。


crowd-pleaser:/ˈkraʊdˌpliːzər/
n.
讨好观众的表演
A performance or act designed to be popular with a large audience.
Her latest movie is a predictable crowd-pleaser. 她最新的电影是一部可以预见的讨好观众的作品。

近义词:popular choice, surefire hit, winner
反义词:flop, failure, dud
词性:noun

词源:  Compound of "crowd" and "pleaser". "Crowd" refers to a large group of people, and "pleaser" comes from the verb "to please," meaning to give satisfaction or enjoyment.
