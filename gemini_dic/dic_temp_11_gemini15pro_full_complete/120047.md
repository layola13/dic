millivolts:/ˈmɪlɪˌvoʊlts/| n. | 毫伏 |A unit of electromotive force or potential difference equal to one thousandth of a volt. |The reading on the EEG was only a few millivolts.  脑电图上的读数只有几毫伏。| synonym: mV | antonym: kilovolts, megavolts | noun
