aroynt:/əˈrɔɪnt/
interj.
走开，滚开
Be gone; depart.
Aroynt thee, witch!  走开，女巫！
近义词: away, begone, depart, get out
反义词: approach, come, stay, welcome
词性: interjection

Etymology:
From Middle English *aroynten, from Old English *āryntan (“to drive away, expel”), equivalent to ā- (“out”) + *ryntan (“to roar”). Cognate with dialectal Norwegian runta (“to growl, grumble”).
