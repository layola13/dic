disfigured:/dɪsˈfɪɡərd/
adjective
毁容的，丑陋的
marred or spoiled in appearance.
Her face was disfigured by the fire. 她的脸被火烧毁了容。
近义词：deformed, marred, mutilated
反义词：beautiful, attractive, unblemished
词性：adjective

disfigure:/dɪsˈfɪɡər/
verb
损毁…的外形；使变丑
to spoil the appearance of something or someone.
The accident disfigured him for life.  这场事故使他终身毁容。
近义词：mar, deform, mutilate
反义词：beautify, enhance, improve
词性：verb


词源：
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + figure (shape, form).  Ultimately from Latin figura.
