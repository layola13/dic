downright:/ˌdaʊnˈraɪt/| adj. |彻头彻尾的；十足的；坦率的；直率的|Completely; absolutely; plainly; bluntly.  It emphasizes the completeness or intensity of something, often something negative. Can also mean frank or straightforward.|That's a downright lie! 那完全是谎言！|Synonyms: absolute, utter, complete, thorough, total, outright, blunt, frank, straightforward|Antonyms: partial, incomplete, indirect, subtle|Adjective
