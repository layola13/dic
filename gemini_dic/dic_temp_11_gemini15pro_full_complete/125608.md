multiverse:/ˈmʌltɪˌvɜːrs/
noun
多元宇宙，多重宇宙
A hypothetical space or realm consisting of a vast number of universes, including our own.
Scientists have theorized about the possibility of a multiverse. 科学家们已经建立了关于多重宇宙可能性的理论。
The concept of the multiverse is explored in many science fiction stories. 多元宇宙的概念在许多科幻小说中都有探讨。
Synonyms: megaverse, omniverse
Antonyms: universe (in the singular sense)
