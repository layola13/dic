noncrime:/ˈnɒnkraɪm/ (UK) /ˈnɑːnkraɪm/ (US)| n. |非犯罪行为|An act that is not considered a crime.  Not illegal or criminal. |Example:  Parking violations are often considered noncrimes. 违章停车通常被认为是非犯罪行为。

Example:  Jaywalking, while technically an infraction, is generally treated as a noncrime. 乱穿马路虽然严格来说是违规行为，但通常被视为非犯罪行为。|近义词：non-offense, infraction (minor offense)|反义词: crime, offense|词性:名词
