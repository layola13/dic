checkmating:/ˈtʃɛkmeɪtɪŋ/
|n. / v.|将死；击败；彻底挫败|The act of putting an opponent in checkmate in chess.  The act of defeating someone utterly. |He achieved checkmating his opponent in four moves. 他四步就将死了对手。 The new law checkmated the company's plans for expansion. 新法律彻底挫败了公司扩张的计划。|近义词：defeating, conquering, vanquishing|反义词：losing, failing|词性：名词/动词 (gerund/present participle)


词源: From checkmate (n.) + -ing.  Checkmate itself comes from Old French "eschec mat" meaning "the king is dead," from Persian "shāh māt" ("the king is helpless").
