oleandrine:/ˌoʊliˈændrɪn/ |n.| 夹竹桃苷; 黄夹甙 |A toxic cardiac glycoside extracted from the oleander plant. It has similar properties to digoxin and is sometimes used in traditional medicine, but it is highly poisonous.| Oleandrine is a potent toxin found in all parts of the oleander plant. 夹竹桃苷是一种强效毒素，存在于夹竹桃植物的所有部位。| Oleander poisoning can occur from ingesting even a small amount of oleandrine. 摄入少量夹竹桃苷即可导致夹竹桃中毒。 |Synonyms: None (highly specific compound) | Antonyms:  None (highly specific compound) | noun
