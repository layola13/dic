equinumerous:/ˌiːkwɪˈnuːmərəs/| adj.|等数的，数量相等的|Having the same number of elements.  Two sets are equinumerous if they have the same cardinality, meaning a one-to-one correspondence can be established between their elements.|The set of even numbers is equinumerous with the set of natural numbers.|近义词：equal in number, equivalent, of the same size|反义词: unequal in number, different in size|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Latin *aequi-* (equal) + *numerus* (number).
