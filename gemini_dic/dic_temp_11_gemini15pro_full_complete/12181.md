as-fit-as-a-fiddle:/æz ˌfɪt əz ə ˈfɪdəl/ | adj. | 非常健康；精神饱满 |  Means to be in excellent health or condition, both physically and mentally.  The "fiddle" refers to a violin, which needs to be well-maintained to play properly. | Example: After his vacation, he returned as fit as a fiddle.  假期结束后，他回来时精神抖擞。| Synonym: healthy, well, robust, vigorous | Antonym: unwell, sick, frail, weak | Adjective
