misassert:/ˌmɪsəˈsɜːrt/
verb
错误地断言；虚报；谎称
To assert incorrectly; to state something as true when it is false.
The witness misasserted several key facts of the case.  证人在案件的几个关键事实上作了不真实的陈述。
The company misasserted its profits in the annual report.  该公司在年度报告中谎报了利润。
Synonym: misstate, misrepresent, falsify
Antonym: assert correctly, state truthfully, verify
词性: 动词
