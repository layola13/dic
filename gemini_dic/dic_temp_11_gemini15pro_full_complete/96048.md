indecentness:/ˌɪnˈdiːsntnəs/ | n. |猥亵；下流；不体面|The state or quality of being indecent; lack of decency or modesty. |The indecentness of his language shocked everyone present. 他的言语粗俗，震惊了在场的所有人。|Synonyms: impropriety, immodesty, indecency, obscenity |Antonyms: decency, modesty, propriety |noun

indecent:/ɪnˈdiːsnt/ | adj. |不体面的；猥亵的；下流的|Not conforming with generally accepted standards of propriety or good taste; improper, offensive. |His indecent behavior led to his arrest. 他的不雅行为导致他被捕。|Synonyms: improper, immodest, obscene, lewd |Antonyms: decent, modest, proper |adjective
