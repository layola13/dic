allele:/əˈliːl/ | n. |等位基因|One of two or more alternative forms of a gene that arise by mutation and are found at the same place on a chromosome.|例句：The dominant allele determines the phenotype. 显性等位基因决定表型。|例句：Individuals receive one allele from each parent for any given gene.  对于任何给定的基因，个体从每个亲本那里接受一个等位基因。|近义词：gene form, variant|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:  From Greek  ἄλλος (állos, “other”) +  -ηλος (-ēlos, diminutive suffix). Coined by William Bateson and Edith Rebecca Saunders.
