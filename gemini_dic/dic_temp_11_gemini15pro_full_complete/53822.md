disconnecting:/dɪskəˈnɛktɪŋ/
verb, adjective|断开连接，断开连接的|The act of breaking a connection, or the state of being disconnected.|He was disconnecting the phone when I called. 他正在我打电话时断开电话连接。|近义词：unplugging, detaching, separating|反义词：connecting, joining, linking
verb|断开连接的|Not connected; separated.|The disconnecting cable caused the internet outage. 断开的电缆导致了互联网中断。|近义词：detached, separated, unconnected|反义词：connected, joined, linked
