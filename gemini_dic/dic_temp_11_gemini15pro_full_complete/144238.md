outsmart:/ˌaʊtˈsmɑːrt/
verb
以智取胜，智胜
To defeat or trick (someone) by being more intelligent or clever.
She outsmarted her opponent in the chess match. 她在这场国际象棋比赛中以智取胜了她的对手。
He outsmarted the police by changing his disguise. 他通过改变伪装智胜了警方。
近义词: outwit, outfox, outmaneuver
反义词: be outsmarted, be fooled
词性: verb
