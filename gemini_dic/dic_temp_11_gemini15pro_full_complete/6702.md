anallagmatic:/ˌænəlæɡˈmætɪk/
adjective
变换的，可互换的
Relating to or involving annallagmatic curves or surfaces, which have the property of being their own inverse with respect to a certain point or circle.  These curves and surfaces remain unchanged under a specific type of transformation.
An anallagmatic curve remains unchanged by inversion.
一条非圆曲线通过反演保持不变。
The sphere is an anallagmatic surface.
球体是一个可互换的曲面。
近义词:invertible, reciprocal
反义词:non-invertible
词性:adjective
