lod:/lɒd/ | n. | 矿脉，矿藏；母岩 | A vein of metal ore; the rock enclosing such a vein. | The miners discovered a rich lod of gold in the mountain. 矿工们在山中发现了一条丰富的金矿脉。 | synonyms: vein, deposit, seam, lode | antonyms: barren rock, waste rock | noun

lod:/lɑːd/ | n. | (古语)负荷，重担 | (archaic) A load or burden. | He carried a heavy lod on his back. 他背着沉重的负担。（古语用法，现代英语中很少使用）| synonyms: burden, load, weight | antonyms: lightness, ease | noun
