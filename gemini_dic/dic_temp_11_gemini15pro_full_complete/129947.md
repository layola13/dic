non-Malthusians:/ˌnɒn mælˈθuːziənz/ | n. | 非马尔萨斯主义者 | People who do not subscribe to Malthusian theories about population growth exceeding resource growth.  These theories posit that population growth will eventually outstrip resources, leading to famine and disease. Non-Malthusians generally believe that technological advances and human ingenuity will prevent such a catastrophe. |  Non-Malthusians argue that innovation can solve resource scarcity. 非马尔萨斯主义者认为创新可以解决资源短缺问题。

Non-Malthusians often cite the Green Revolution as evidence against Malthus's predictions. 非马尔萨斯主义者经常引用绿色革命作为反驳马尔萨斯预测的证据。 | 近义词: optimists, cornucopians | 反义词: Malthusians, neo-Malthusians | 词性: noun
