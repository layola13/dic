epidermises:/ˌɛpɪˈdɜːrmɪsiːz/
n.
表皮（复数）
The plural form of epidermis, referring to the outer layers of skin on multiple organisms or multiple layers of skin on a single organism.
The epidermises of reptiles are often thick and scaly. 爬行动物的表皮通常又厚又鳞片状。
Repeated sun exposure can damage the epidermises of delicate plants. 反复的阳光照射会损害娇嫩植物的表皮。
Synonyms: skins, outer layers
Antonyms: inner layers, dermis
Noun
