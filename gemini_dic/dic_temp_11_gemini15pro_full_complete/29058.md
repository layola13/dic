caruncula:/kəˈrʌŋkjʊlə/
noun
小肉阜，肉赘
A small, fleshy excrescence; a wattle.
The surgeon removed the caruncula from the patient's eye.  外科医生切除了病人眼中的肉赘。
synonyms: wattle, papilla, nodule
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps smooth surface, or absence of growth)
n.
