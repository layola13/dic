nonscientist:/ˌnɒnˈsaɪəntɪst/ /ˌnɑːnˈsaɪəntɪst/ | n. |非科学家；门外汉|A person who is not a scientist, especially someone who does not have scientific training or expertise.|  Many nonscientists find the subject of quantum physics difficult to grasp. 许多非科学家发现量子物理学很难理解。

|Example: The book is written for nonscientists, explaining complex ideas in simple language. 这本书是为非科学家写的，用简单的语言解释复杂的思想。|Synonyms: layman, nonprofessional, amateur |Antonyms: scientist, expert, professional| noun
