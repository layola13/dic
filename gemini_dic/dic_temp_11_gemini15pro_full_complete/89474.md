honeydew:/ˈhʌniˌduː/ /ˈhʌniˌdjuː/| n. |蜜瓜；蜜露|A type of sweet, juicy melon with smooth, pale green skin and pale green flesh.  A sugary secretion produced by aphids or scale insects.|例句：We had slices of honeydew for breakfast.  我们早餐吃了蜜瓜片。

例句：The leaves were sticky with honeydew. 树叶上都是黏黏的蜜露。|近义词：melon, cantaloupe|反义词:|noun
