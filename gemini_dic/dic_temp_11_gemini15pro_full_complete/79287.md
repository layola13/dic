glaucous:/ˈɡlɔːkəs/| adj.|灰绿色的；苍白的；被白霜覆盖的|Covered with a powdery bloom that gives a frosted or grayish-green appearance.|The glaucous leaves of the succulent shimmered in the sunlight.  多肉植物灰绿色的叶子在阳光下闪闪发光。|例句：The grapes have a glaucous bloom. 这些葡萄上有一层白霜。|近义词：bluish-green, grayish-green, pale, frosted|反义词:bright green, vivid|词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *glaucus, from Ancient Greek γλαυκός (glaukós, “bluish-green, gray”).
