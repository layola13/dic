intermedium:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiəm/ | n. | 中间物，媒介物，中间阶段 |Something that acts as a means of conveying or transmitting something else; a substance formed during a chemical process before the desired product is obtained. | The software acts as an intermedium between the user and the computer hardware.  这个软件充当用户和计算机硬件之间的媒介。

|Synonyms: intermediary, medium, agent, vehicle |Antonyms: extreme, direct | noun
