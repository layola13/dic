extravert: /ˈɛkstrəvɜːrt/ (British) /ˈɛkstrəvɝːt/ (American)| n. adj.|外向的人，外向的|A person predominantly concerned with external things or objective considerations. Someone who is sociable, outgoing, and readily engages with others.|例句：She's a real extravert and loves being the center of attention. 她是一个真正的外向的人，喜欢成为关注的焦点。

例句：He's always been an extravert, making friends easily wherever he goes. 他一直都很外向，无论走到哪里都能轻易交到朋友。|近义词：outgoing, gregarious, sociable|反义词：introvert, shy, reserved|词性: 名词，形容词

词源: From Latin extra- 'outside' + vertere 'to turn'.
