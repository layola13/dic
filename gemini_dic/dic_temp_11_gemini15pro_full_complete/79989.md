go-getter:/ˈɡəʊˌɡetər/ (US) /ˈɡəʊˌɡetə(r)/ (UK)| n. |有进取心的人；积极进取的人|A go-getter is someone who is very energetic, determined to be successful, and able to deal with new or difficult situations easily.|例句：She's a real go-getter and I'm sure she'll do well in her new job. 她是一个真正的实干家，我相信她在新工作中会做得很好。

例句：He's always been a go-getter, even as a child. 他从小就是一个积极进取的人。|近义词：achiever, high-flyer, self-starter, hustler, dynamo |反义词:slacker, underachiever|词性:名词
