matoke:/məˈtoʊkeɪ/
n.|蒸熟的香蕉主食，主要在乌干达食用|A starchy variety of banana, native to East Africa, used as a staple food, especially in Uganda. It is typically steamed and mashed before being served.|Matoke is a common dish in Uganda.  马托克是乌干达的常见菜肴。|Synonym: plantain, green banana|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider dishes made from other staples like rice or potatoes)|noun
