overdiversity:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈvɜːrsɪti/ | n. | 过度多样性，过度多元化 | An excessive or arguably detrimental level of diversity, often implying an imbalance or inefficiency. | The overdiversity of species in this small area has led to intense competition for resources.  这个小区域物种的过度多样性导致了对资源的激烈竞争。| synonyms: excess diversity, hyperdiversity | antonyms: homogeneity, uniformity | noun

overdiversity:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈvɜːrsɪti/ | n. | 过度多样性，过度多元化 | The state of having too much diversity, which can sometimes be detrimental. |  The committee suffered from overdiversity of opinions, making it difficult to reach a consensus. 委员会意见过度多样化，难以达成共识。 | synonyms:  superdiversity, plethora of diversity | antonyms: lack of diversity, underdiversity | noun
