naturism:/ˈneɪtʃərɪzəm/
n.
裸体主义，自然主义
The practice of going without clothes, especially in a group or in public places, for reasons of health, freedom, or as part of a philosophy.
Naturism is practiced at designated beaches and resorts.  裸体主义在指定的沙滩和度假村实行。
Naturism emphasizes respect for the natural world. 自然主义强调尊重自然世界。
synonyms:nudism, nakedness
antonyms:prudery, conservatism
noun
