crd:/kɑːrd/ | n. | 卡片; 信用卡; 储值卡 | A small rectangular piece of stiff paper, plastic, etc., used for various purposes, such as playing games, storing information, or making payments. | Example: I used my credit card to pay for the meal. 我用信用卡支付了这顿饭。 | Example:  He pulled out a business card and handed it to me. 他掏出一张名片递给我。 | Synonyms: badge, pass, ticket, permit | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object like a card) | noun

crd:/krɛd/ | n. | 信誉; 声望; 影响力 | Belief or confidence in the power, truth, or reliability of someone or something.  This meaning is less common than the "card" meaning. | Example:  His crd with the bank was excellent. 他在银行的信誉非常好。 | Example:  The company's crd suffered after the scandal.  这家公司的声誉在丑闻后受损。| Synonyms: reputation, standing, prestige | Antonyms: disrepute, disgrace | noun
