eldar:/ˈɛldɑːr/
n.|精灵（尤指托尔金作品中的）|An elf, especially one of the immortal elves of Tolkien's mythology.|例句：The Eldar possessed great wisdom and skill. 精灵拥有伟大的智慧和技能。|例句：The Eldar queen ruled her people with grace and strength.  精灵女王以优雅和力量统治她的人民。|近义词：elf, fairy|反义词:orc, goblin|词性:名词
词源: From Quenya (one of the Elvish languages created by J.R.R. Tolkien), meaning "people of the stars".  Derived from *elda "star-person".
