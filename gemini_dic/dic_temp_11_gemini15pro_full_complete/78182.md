geoselenic:/ˌdʒiːoʊsɪˈliːnɪk/
adjective
含硒的；硒地的
Relating to or containing selenium, especially in the soil.
Selenium is found in some soils, making them geoselenic.  这些土壤中含有硒，使它们成为富硒土壤。
Geoselenic soils can affect the selenium content of crops grown in them.  富硒土壤会影响其中种植作物的硒含量。
近义词:seleniferous
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider selenium-deficient)
词性:adjective
