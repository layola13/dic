curdiness:/ˈkɜːrdɪnəs/
n. |凝乳状，凝结 |The state or quality of resembling curd; thickness; coagulation.| The milk had a slight curdiness to it. 牛奶有点凝结。|近义词：coagulation, thickness, lumpiness |反义词: smoothness, fluidity
Etymology: From curd + -iness.  "Curd" comes from the Old English "crude," meaning "coagulated milk."
