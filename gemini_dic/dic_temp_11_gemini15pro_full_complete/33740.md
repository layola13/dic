circuition:/ˌsɜːrkjuˈɪʃən/ | n. |迂回；绕行；周游；循环|The circuition of the globe took several months.  环球旅行花了几个月的时间。|例句：The blood's circuition through the body is vital. 血液在体内的循环是至关重要的。

例句：The argument's circuition avoided the central issue. 这场辩论绕开了核心问题。|近义词：detour, circumnavigation, revolution, rotation, cycle|反义词：direct route, beeline|词性：名词

词源: From Latin *circuitio*, from circuire ‘go around’, from circum ‘around’ + ire ‘go’.
