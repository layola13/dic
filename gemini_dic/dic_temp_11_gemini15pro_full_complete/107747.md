lapboard:/ˈlæpˌbɔːrd/| n.|膝上型电脑；便携式电脑桌|A lapboard is a small, portable surface that can be placed on a person's lap and used as a desk, especially for supporting a laptop computer.|She balanced the lapboard on her knees and started typing. 她把膝上型电脑桌放在膝盖上，开始打字。|Synonyms: laptop desk, portable desk|Antonyms: desk, table|noun
