edgier:/ˈɛdʒɪər/
adjective
更前卫的，更尖锐的，更急躁的
More unconventional, daring, or intense than normal; more provocative or challenging.
Her new music is edgier than her previous work. 她的新音乐比她以前的作品更前卫。
近义词: sharper, bolder, more daring, more provocative, more cutting-edge
反义词: milder, softer, more conventional, more traditional, tamer
词性: adjective

edgier:/ˈɛdʒɪər/
adjective
更急躁的，更易怒的
More irritable or tense.
He's been much edgier lately because of the stress at work.  由于工作压力，他最近变得更加急躁。
近义词: irritable, tense, on edge, jumpy
反义词: calm, relaxed, easygoing
词性: adjective
