brecciated:/ˈbrɛʃiˌeɪtɪd/
brecciated| adj.|角砾化的，碎裂的|Composed of angular fragments; like a breccia. 由角状碎片组成；像角砾岩。|The geologist examined the brecciated rock face. 地质学家检查了碎裂的岩壁。|
brecciated|近义词：fragmented, broken, shattered|
brecciated|反义词：whole, intact, solid|
brecciated|词性:形容词|


breccia:/ˈbrɛtʃə/
breccia| n.|角砾岩|A rock composed of sharp-angled fragments embedded in a finer matrix. 一种由嵌入更细基质中的尖角碎片组成的岩石。|The museum displayed a large sample of volcanic breccia. 博物馆展出了一块巨大的火山角砾岩样本。|
breccia|近义词：conglomerate|
breccia|反义词：N/A|
breccia|词性:名词|

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian "breccia" (gravel, broken stones), from Vulgar Latin *breccia, perhaps of Germanic origin.  源自意大利语“breccia”（砾石，碎石），来自俗拉丁语 *breccia，可能起源于日耳曼语。
