detesting:/dɪˈtestɪŋ/
verb
憎恶，厌恶
Feeling or expressing intense dislike or hatred for something or someone.
She was detesting the long commute to work every day. 她每天都很讨厌漫长的通勤。
He made no secret of his detesting for his rival. 他毫不掩饰对竞争对手的厌恶。
近义词：loathing, abhorring, hating, despising
反义词：loving, adoring, liking, admiring
词性: present participle of detest

detest:/dɪˈtest/
verb
憎恶，厌恶
To feel intense dislike or hatred for something or someone.
I detest hypocrisy more than anything. 我最讨厌虚伪。
She detested the smell of cigarette smoke. 她讨厌香烟的味道。
近义词：loathe, abhor, hate, despise
反义词：love, adore, like, admire
词性: verb

词源：From Latin detestari "to curse, execrate, denounce, abominate," from de- "down, away" + testari "to bear witness, call to witness," related to testis "witness."
