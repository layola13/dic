biophysicist:/ˌbaɪoʊˈfɪzɪsɪst/| n.|生物物理学家|A scientist who studies the physics of living organisms and biological processes.|例句：The biophysicist used advanced imaging techniques to study the structure of proteins.  这位生物物理学家使用先进的成像技术来研究蛋白质的结构。

例句：She is a renowned biophysicist specializing in membrane transport. 她是一位著名的生物物理学家，专攻膜转运。|近义词：biological physicist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
