nematophora:/ˌnɛməˈtɒfərə/ | n. | 线虫动物门 | A phylum or other major taxonomic group of invertebrates, including the nematodes or roundworms. |  Nematophora are a diverse group, found in almost every habitat. 线虫动物门是一个多样化的群体，几乎存在于所有栖息地。|  Many species of Nematophora are parasitic. 许多线虫动物门物种都是寄生虫。 | synonyms: Nematoda, roundworms | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a taxonomic group) | noun
