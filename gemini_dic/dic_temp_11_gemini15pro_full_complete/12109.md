artocarpeous:/ˌɑːrtəˈkɑːrpiəs/
adjective
结面包状果实的，桑科植物的
Bearing fruit that resembles a breadfruit; belonging to or characteristic of the mulberry family (Moraceae).
The artocarpeous tree provided shade and sustenance for the villagers.  这棵桑科植物为村民提供了阴凉和食物。
The botanist specialized in artocarpeous species found in the rainforest.  这位植物学家专门研究雨林中发现的桑科植物。
synonym: moraceous
antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-artocarpeous")
词性: adjective
