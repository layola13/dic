一般现在时 (Simple Present):
It's cold outside.  外面很冷。
It's important to be on time.  准时很重要。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
It's raining heavily.  正在下大雨。
It's getting late.  时间越来越晚了。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
It's been a long day. 今天过得很漫长。
It's been a pleasure meeting you. 很高兴见到你。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
It's been snowing all day.  下了一整天雪。
It's been raining for hours.  已经下了好几个小时的雨了。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
It's was a dark and stormy night. 那是一个黑暗暴风雨的夜晚。(This example demonstrates the use of "it was", the past form of "it is")
It's been a while. 好久不见了。(This example uses "it has been" contracted to "it's", but the context implies past time.)

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
(It's not typically used in past continuous as a standalone contraction. The full form "it was" would be used with a verb in the -ing form)
It was raining when I left. 我离开的时候正在下雨。
It was getting dark. 天渐渐黑了。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
(It's not typically used in past perfect as a standalone contraction.  The full form "it had" would be used.)
It had been a long week. 那是一个漫长的星期。
It had already started raining when we arrived. 我们到达时已经开始下雨了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
(It's not typically used in past perfect continuous as a standalone contraction.)
It had been raining for hours before we left. 我们离开前已经下了好几个小时的雨。
It had been snowing all night. 整夜都在下雪。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
It's going to be a hot day tomorrow. 明天天气会很热。 (This uses the "going to" future form)
It'll be fine.  会没事的。（It will contracted to It'll)

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
(It's not typically used in future continuous as a standalone contraction.)
It will be raining this time tomorrow. 明天这个时候会下雨。
It will be getting dark soon. 天很快就会黑了。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
(It's not typically used in future perfect as a standalone contraction.)
It will have been a year since we last met. 到那时我们将有一年没见面了。
It will have been snowing for days.  到那时将已经下了好几天的雪。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
(It's not typically used in future perfect continuous as a standalone contraction.)
It will have been raining for a week by then. 到那时将已经下了一周的雨。
It will have been snowing for days by the time we arrive. 到我们到达时，将已经下了好几天的雪。

虚拟语气:
It's a shame it's raining. 真可惜，下雨了。
It's time it's stopped raining.  该停雨了。


被动语态:
(It's is not typically used in the passive voice except when contracting "it is")
It's said that he is a genius.  据说他是个天才。
It’s believed that the earth is round. 人们相信地球是圆的。


疑问句:
It's raining, isn't it?  正在下雨，不是吗？
It's a nice day, isn't it?  今天天气不错，不是吗？

否定句:
It's not my fault.  这不是我的错。
It's not fair.  这不公平。
