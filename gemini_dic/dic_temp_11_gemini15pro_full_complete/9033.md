antimaterialism:/ˌænti məˈtɪəriəlɪzəm/
|n.|反物质主义|Opposition to materialism, the belief that material possessions are not the most important thing in life.|Many young people today embrace antimaterialism and focus on experiences rather than things.|近义词:asceticism, nonmaterialism, spiritualism|反义词:materialism, consumerism|词性:名词
Etymology: anti- (prefix meaning "against") + materialism (noun meaning "preoccupation with or emphasis on material objects")
