isosceles:/aɪˈsɑːsəˌliz/ | adj. |等腰的|Having two sides of equal length (referring to a triangle).| An isosceles triangle has two equal sides and two equal angles.  等腰三角形有两条边相等，并且有两个角相等。| equilateral, equiangular | scalene | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἰσοσκελής (isoskelḗs, “having equal legs”), from ἴσος (ísos, “equal”) + σκέλος (skélos, “leg”).
