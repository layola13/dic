bravenesses: /ˈbreɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
勇敢的行为；勇敢的实例（罕用，通常用bravery）
Instances or acts of bravery.  This plural form is rarely used; "bravery" is generally preferred.
He recounted several bravenesses from his time in the war, though he downplayed his own role. 他讲述了他在战争时期的一些勇敢事迹，尽管他淡化了自己的作用。
The soldiers' bravenesses in the face of danger were truly inspiring. 士兵们面对危险时的英勇行为着实鼓舞人心。
近义词：acts of bravery, courageous acts, feats of valor, heroic deeds
反义词：acts of cowardice, timidity
词性：名词

词源:
From brave + -ness + -es.  "Brave" ultimately comes from the Italian "bravo," meaning "wild" or "fierce."  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality. "-es" is the standard plural suffix.
