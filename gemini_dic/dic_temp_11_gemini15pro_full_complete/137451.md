nonthinkers:/nɒnˈθɪŋkəz/ (US) /nɒnˈθɪŋkəz/ (UK)
n.|不思考者，不动脑筋的人|People who do not engage in critical thinking or intellectual activity.  Those who accept information passively without analyzing or questioning it.|The dictator surrounded himself with nonthinkers who would not challenge his authority. 独裁者身边都是些不动脑筋、不会挑战他权威的人。|synonyms: conformists, sheep, followers|antonyms: thinkers, intellectuals, skeptics|noun
