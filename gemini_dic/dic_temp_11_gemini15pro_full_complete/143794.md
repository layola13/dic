outnumbered:/ˌaʊtˈnʌmbərd/
adjective
数量上超过；寡不敌众
being more numerous than another group
例句：They were outnumbered by the enemy. 他们在数量上不及敌人。
例句：The outnumbered soldiers fought bravely. 寡不敌众的士兵们英勇作战。
近义词：outmatched, surpassed, exceeded
反义词：outnumbering, superior
