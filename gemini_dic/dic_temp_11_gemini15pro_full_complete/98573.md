institutionalises:/ɪnˌstɪtjuːʃənəlaɪzɪz/
| v. |使制度化；使机构化|To make something become part of an established system, custom, or institution.|The government institutionalized the new policy. 政府将新政策制度化。|synonyms: establish, formalize, systematize|antonyms: abolish, discontinue|verb

institutionalizes:/ɪnˌstɪtjuːʃənəlaɪzɪz/
| v. |使…收容于机构|To place someone in an institution, such as a hospital or orphanage.|He was institutionalized for mental health issues. 他因精神健康问题而被收容于机构。|synonyms: commit, confine|antonyms: release, discharge|verb
