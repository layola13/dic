couture:/kuːˈtʊr/ | n. |高级定制服装；高级时装业|The design and manufacture of fashionable clothes to a client's specific requirements and measurements.|She works in couture. 她从事高级时装业。|Synonyms: high fashion, designer clothing|Antonyms: ready-to-wear, off-the-rack|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French, from coudre ‘to sew’, from Latin consuere .
