overinflating: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪtɪŋ/
verb
过度充气;过分膨胀
The act of inflating something beyond its safe or recommended limit.  This can apply to physical objects like tires or balloons, or to abstract concepts like prices or egos.
They ruined the raft by overinflating it. 他们把筏子充气充过头了，把它弄坏了。
The media was accused of overinflating the importance of the event. 媒体被指责过分夸大了事件的重要性。

overinflate: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪt/

近义词: overfill, exaggerate, inflate excessively
反义词: underinflate, deflate, downplay
