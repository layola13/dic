fraternal:/frəˈtɜːrnl/| adj.|兄弟般的；友爱的|Of or like a brother or brothers; showing the affection or loyalty of a brother.|例句：They share a deep fraternal bond. 他们有着深厚的兄弟情谊。|例句：The fraternal organizations provide support to their members.  这些兄弟会组织为其成员提供支持。|近义词：brotherly, amicable|反义词：hostile, antagonistic|词性:形容词

fraternal:/frəˈtɜːrnl/| n.|兄弟会成员|A member of a fraternity.|例句：He's a fraternal in the local lodge. 他是当地分会的兄弟会成员。|例句：The fraternals gathered for their annual meeting. 兄弟会成员聚集在一起举行年会。|近义词：brother, member|反义词: N/A|词性:名词 (rarely used)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fraternalis, from Latin frater (“brother”).
