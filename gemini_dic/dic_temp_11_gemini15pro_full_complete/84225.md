half-section:/ˌhæf ˈsɛkʃən/| n.|半块地，半段|A half-section of land is a parcel of land that is half the size of a standard section, typically 320 acres in the Public Land Survey System used in the United States and Canada.|He inherited a half-section of farmland from his grandfather. 他从祖父那里继承了半块农田。|Synonyms: half-quarter section,  half of a section|Antonyms: section, double section|noun

half-section:/ˌhæf ˈsɛkʃən/| n.|半部分，一半|A portion or segment that is half the size of the whole.|The first half-section of the book is more engaging than the second. 这本书的前半部分比后半部分更吸引人。|Synonyms: half, moiety|Antonyms: whole, entirety|noun
