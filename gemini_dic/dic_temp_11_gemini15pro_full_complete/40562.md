contumacious:/ˌkɒntjʊˈmeɪʃəs/ /ˌkɑːntjʊˈmeɪʃəs/| adj.| 顽固的，不服从的，抗命的|Stubbornly or willfully disobedient to authority.|The contumacious defendant refused to answer the judge's questions.  顽固的被告拒绝回答法官的问题。

The student's contumacious behavior resulted in suspension. 这个学生不服从命令的行为导致他被停学。

|近义词：rebellious, defiant, insubordinate, recalcitrant, intractable|反义词：obedient, compliant, submissive, docile|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *contumax* (stubborn, insolent), from *con-* (with, together) + *tumēre* (to swell, be swollen).  The sense evolution suggests an image of someone "swelling with pride" and thus becoming disobedient.
