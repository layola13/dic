mentions:/ˈmɛnʃənz/ | n. | 提及，说起；(通常用复数)致意，问候 | An act of mentioning or referring to something.  | He made no mention of his plans. 他没有提及他的计划。 | synonyms: references, allusions, citations | antonyms: omissions, exclusions | noun

mentions:/ˈmɛnʃənz/ | v. | 提到，谈及 |  To refer to something briefly and without going into detail. |  She mentioned that she was going to visit her family. 她提到她要去探望家人。| synonyms: notes, states, remarks | antonyms: ignores, conceals | verb
