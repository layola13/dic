entirely:/ɪnˈtaɪərli/| adv.|完全地，彻底地|Completely, totally; to the full extent.|例句：The house was entirely destroyed by the fire. 房子被大火完全烧毁了。|例句：I entirely agree with your assessment. 我完全同意你的评估。|近义词：completely, totally, fully, utterly, wholly|反义词：partially, partly|词性:副词

词源:
From Middle English *entierli, from Old French entierement, from entier (“whole”) + -ment (suffix forming adverbs).  Ultimately from Latin integer (“whole, untouched, complete”).
