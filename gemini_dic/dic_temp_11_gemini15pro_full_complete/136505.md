nonrousing:/ˌnɒnˈraʊzɪŋ/ | adj. | 不刺激的，不令人兴奋的 | Not causing excitement or arousal; not stimulating. | The lecture was rather nonrousing and I almost fell asleep.  这讲座相当枯燥，我差点睡着了。|  The medication has a nonrousing effect and is safe to take before driving. 这种药没有兴奋作用，开车前服用是安全的。 | 近义词: unexciting, unstimulating, boring, dull, bland, insipid, tedious | 反义词: exciting, stimulating, thrilling, arousing | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "rousing" (present participle of "rouse," meaning "to awaken or stir to action"). The word "rouse" ultimately comes from the Middle English word "rowsen," which in turn comes from the Old Norse word "rausa."
