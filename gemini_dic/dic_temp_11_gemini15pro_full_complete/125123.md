multi-circuited:/ˌmʌltiˈsɜːrkɪtɪd/ |adj.|多电路的，多回路的|Having multiple circuits or pathways.  It describes something that has several distinct electrical circuits.  This is often used in the context of electronics or electrical systems.|The multi-circuited system provides redundancy in case of failure.|近义词:complex, intricate, multi-path|反义词:single-circuited, simple|词性:形容词
