automated:/ˈɔːtəmeɪtɪd/
adjective
自动化的
operated by machines or computers, without needing human control
例句：The factory is now fully automated.  这家工厂现在完全自动化了。

例句：Automated systems are becoming increasingly common in manufacturing. 自动化系统在制造业中变得越来越普遍。

近义词：automatic, robotic, mechanized, self-operating
反义词：manual, hand-operated
词性：adjective
