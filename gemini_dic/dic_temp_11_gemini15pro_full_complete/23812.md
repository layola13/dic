brazier:/ˈbreɪʒər/ |n.|火盆；炭火盆|A metal container that holds burning coals, used for warming a room or cooking food.|The scent of roasting chestnuts wafted from the brazier. 从火盆里飘来烤栗子的香味。|Synonyms: fire pit, fire pan, charcoal burner|Antonyms: refrigerator, freezer|Noun

brazier:/ˈbreɪʒər/ |n.|胸罩制造商；胸罩销售商|A person who makes or sells brassieres.|She worked as a brazier in a lingerie shop. 她在一家内衣店做胸罩销售员。|Synonyms: corsetiere, lingerie maker, lingerie seller|Antonyms: N/A|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *brasier, from braise (“embers”), from Frankish *brasa (“burning coals”).
