loyalists:/ˈlɔɪəlɪsts/| n.|拥护者，忠诚者|People who remain loyal to a government, a cause, or a leader, especially during a time of revolt or change.  These individuals actively support the established order and oppose those seeking to overthrow it.|The loyalists refused to join the rebellion. 这些拥护者拒绝加入叛乱。|synonyms: adherent, supporter, devotee, patriot |antonyms: rebel, traitor, dissident, insurgent|noun
