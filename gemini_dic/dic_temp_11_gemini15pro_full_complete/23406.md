brachyuran:/bræˈkiʊrən/| n. |短尾蟹；短尾亚目动物|A crustacean of the infraorder Brachyura, having a short, broad abdomen that is usually folded under the thorax.  These crabs have five pairs of legs, the first pair of which are modified into pincers.|例句：The brachyuran scuttled across the sandy beach. 这只短尾蟹快速地穿过沙滩。

例句：Many brachyuran species are important to the fishing industry. 许多短尾蟹种类对渔业很重要。|近义词：crab|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
brachyurous:/bræˈkɪərəs/| adj. |短尾的|Having a short, broad abdomen, characteristic of brachyuran crabs.|例句：The brachyurous crab disappeared into a crevice in the rock.  这只短尾蟹消失在岩石的缝隙里。

例句：The scientist studied the brachyurous anatomy of the specimen.  科学家研究了标本的短尾解剖结构。|近义词：short-tailed|反义词:long-tailed|词性:adjective
