dishevel:/dɪˈʃevəl/| v.|使蓬乱，使头发凌乱|To make (someone's hair or clothes) untidy.|The wind disheveled her hair. 风吹乱了她的头发。|近义词：rumple, tousle, disarrange|反义词:neaten, tidy, groom|词性:动词

disheveled:/dɪˈʃevəld/| adj.|蓬乱的，凌乱的|Being in loose disarray; unkempt; messy.|He looked disheveled after his long journey.  长途旅行后，他看起来很邋遢。|近义词：untidy, messy, rumpled|反义词:neat, tidy, ordered|词性:形容词

词源: From Old French *descheveler, from des- (expressing reversal) + chevel (hair), from Latin capillus (hair).
