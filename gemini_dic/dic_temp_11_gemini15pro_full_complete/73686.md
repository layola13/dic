forging:/ˈfɔːrdʒɪŋ/| n. v.|锻造，伪造|The process of shaping metal by heating and hammering.  The act of creating something false, such as a document or signature.|The blacksmith is skilled in the art of forging.  He was accused of forging his father's signature.|synonyms: shaping, forming, fabricating, counterfeiting, faking|antonyms: casting, molding, genuine, authentic|noun, verb


forging:/ˈfɔːrdʒɪŋ/| n.|锻造，伪造物|The product of forging, either a metal object or a forgery.|The forging was so expertly done that it was almost impossible to detect.|synonyms: creation, fabrication, counterfeit|antonyms: original, genuine|noun
