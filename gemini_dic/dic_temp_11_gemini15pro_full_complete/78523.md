ghillies:/ˈɡɪliz/ | n. | 软底鞋；吉利鞋 (一种用于高地舞蹈或潜行的软底鞋) | Soft, laced shoes, often worn for Highland dancing or stalking. | The ghillies allowed the stalker to move silently through the forest.  潜行者穿着吉利鞋在森林里悄无声息地移动。|  例句：He wore ghillies while stalking deer. 他穿着吉利鞋跟踪鹿。| 近义词：soft shoes, dancing shoes, stalking shoes | 反义词: boots, hard shoes | 词性: noun

ghillie:/ˈɡɪli/ | n. | 吉利服；伪装服；穿着吉利服的人 |  A camouflage outfit, often made of burlap or netting and adorned with leaves and twigs, used for concealment; a person wearing such an outfit.  | The ghillie suit blended seamlessly with the surrounding foliage. 吉利服与周围的树叶完美地融合在一起。 | 例句：The sniper wore a ghillie suit to remain hidden. 狙击手穿着一件吉利服来隐藏自己。 | 近义词：camouflage suit, camouflage outfit | 反义词: uniform | 词性: noun
