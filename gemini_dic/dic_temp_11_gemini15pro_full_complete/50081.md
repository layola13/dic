dentately:/ˈdɛn.teɪt.li/
adv.
锯齿状地，齿状地
in a dentate manner; having a toothed margin or toothlike projections.
The leaves were dentately edged. 叶子边缘呈锯齿状。
近义词: serratedly, jaggedly
反义词: smoothly, evenly
词性: adverb

dentate:/ˈdɛn.teɪt/
adj.
锯齿状的，齿状的
having a toothed margin or toothlike projections.
The plant has dentate leaves. 这种植物有锯齿状的叶子。
近义词: serrated, toothed, jagged
反义词: smooth, even
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dentatus, past participle of dentare (“to furnish with teeth”), from dens (“tooth”).
