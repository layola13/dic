countertransports:/ˌkaʊntərˈtrænspɔːrts/
noun
反向转运，对向转运
The process in which two or more different types of molecules are transported across a cell membrane in opposite directions.
Countertransports of sodium and calcium ions help regulate intracellular calcium levels.  钠离子和钙离子的反向转运有助于调节细胞内钙水平。
synonyms: antiport, exchange diffusion
antonyms: symport, cotransport
n.


countertransport:/ˌkaʊntərˈtrænspɔːrt/
verb
反向转运，对向转运
To transport two or more different types of molecules across a cell membrane in opposite directions.
The cell membrane countertransports sodium and calcium. 细胞膜反向转运钠和钙。
synonyms: antiport, exchange
antonyms: symport, cotransport
