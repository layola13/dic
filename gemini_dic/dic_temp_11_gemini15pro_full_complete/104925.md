keld:/kɛld/ | n. | 泉水；泉源；涌泉 | A spring or well.  Specifically, a spring or well built over or otherwise protected, especially one connected with a building. |  The farmhouse had its own keld, providing fresh water year-round.  农舍有自己的泉水，一年四季提供淡水。 | synonyms: spring, well, fountain, source | antonyms: drain, sewer | noun
