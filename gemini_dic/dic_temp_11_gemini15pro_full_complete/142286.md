orsellinic:/ɔːrˈsɛlɪnɪk/ | adj. | 奥西林酸的；含有奥西林酸的 |  Relating to or containing orsellinic acid, a type of phenolic acid found in lichens and fungi. | Orsellinic acid is a precursor to many other lichen metabolites. 奥西林酸是许多其他地衣代谢物的前体。| The orsellinic acid pathway is important in the biosynthesis of certain antibiotics. 奥西林酸途径在某些抗生素的生物合成中很重要。 | 近义词: (No direct synonyms, can list related compounds like "lecanoric acid," "evernic acid") | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From *orcinol* + *-ic*.  Orcinol is named after *Orchis*, the orchid genus, from which it was first isolated. The suffix *-ic* forms adjectives meaning "relating to" or "containing."
