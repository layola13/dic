homemade:/ˌhoʊmˈmeɪd/ | adj. | 自制的，家里做的 | Made at home; not bought from a shop. |  We had a delicious homemade cake for dessert. 我们吃了一块美味的自制蛋糕作为甜点。 | home-baked, hand-crafted | shop-bought, store-bought | adjective

Etymology->
From *home* + *made*.  *Home* traces back to Old English *hām*, meaning "dwelling, house." *Made* is the past participle of *make*, from Old English *macian*, meaning "to make, form, build."
