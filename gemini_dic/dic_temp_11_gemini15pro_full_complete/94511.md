importunely:/ɪmˈpɔːrtʃənli/ | adv. | 纠缠不休地，再三恳求地 | In a persistently annoying or troublesome way; with excessive persistence. | He importunely begged for money. 他纠缠不休地乞讨钱财。|  She importunely requested a meeting with the CEO, despite being repeatedly told he was unavailable. 她不断要求与首席执行官会面，尽管一再被告知他没有空。 | 近义词: persistently, insistently, pressingly | 反义词: politely, respectfully, discreetly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin importunus "unfit, troublesome," from in- "not" + portus "harbor," thus "out of harbor, out of place, inconvenient." The sense evolution is from "inconvenient, troublesome" to "persistent in requests, insistent."
