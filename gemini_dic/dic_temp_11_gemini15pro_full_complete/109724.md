leveller:/ˈlevələ(r)/| n.| 平衡器，矫正者，使平等的人（或事物）| someone or something that makes people or things more equal | The internet has been described as a great leveller.  互联网被描述为一个伟大的平衡器。| synonyms: equalizer, leveler | antonyms: differentiator, discriminator | noun
