borrows:/ˈbɒrəʊz/ (UK) /ˈbɑːroʊz/ (US)| v.|借入，借用|Third-person singular simple present indicative form of borrow.|He borrows books from the library every week. 他每周都从图书馆借书。|近义词：takes, obtains, uses temporarily|反义词:lends, gives, donates|verb

词源:
From Middle English *borwen, from Old English borgian (“to borrow, lend, give a pledge, stand surety”), from Proto-Germanic *burgōną (“to borrow, become surety”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰerǵʰ- (“high, elevated”). Cognate with Old Frisian burga, Old Saxon borgōn, Old High German borgen, Old Norse bjarga (“to save, rescue, help”).
