mugwump:/ˈmʌɡˌwʌmp/| n. |独立人士，骑墙派|A person who remains independent, especially from political party affiliation, and often takes a critical stance.|例句：He was considered a mugwump because he refused to support either candidate. 他被认为是一个骑墙派，因为他拒绝支持任何一位候选人。

例句：The mugwumps criticized both parties for their corruption. 骑墙派批评两党腐败。|近义词：independent, nonpartisan, neutral|反义词：partisan, loyalist|词性：名词
