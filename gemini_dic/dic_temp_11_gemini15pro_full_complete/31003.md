chalkinesses:/ˈtʃɔːkinɪsɪz/
noun
白垩质；垩白色；白垩状|The chalkinesses in the paint made it difficult to apply smoothly. 油漆中的白垩质使其难以平滑地涂抹。|
chalkiness
The excessive chalkiness of the soil hindered plant growth. 土壤中过多的垩白色阻碍了植物生长。
The apples had an unpleasant chalkiness to their texture. 苹果的质地有一种令人不快的白垩状。
|Synonyms: crumbliness, friability, powderiness|Antonyms: smoothness, firmness, solidity|n.|

ETYMOLOGY->
From chalky + -ness + -es (plural suffix). Chalky originates from "chalk," which comes from Old English *cealc, from Proto-Germanic *kalkaz, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kalg- "lime, chalk."
