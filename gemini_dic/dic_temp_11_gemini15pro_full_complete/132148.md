noncumulative:/ˌnɒnˈkjuːmjələtɪv/ | adj. | 非累积的，非渐增的 | Not increasing in quantity, force, or degree by successive additions. | The penalties for late submission are noncumulative, meaning each late day incurs a fixed penalty, not an increasing one.  迟交的罚款是非累积的，这意味着每迟交一天都会受到固定的罚款，而不是递增的罚款。|  The damage to the car was noncumulative, consisting of a single dent. 汽车的损坏是非累积性的，只有一个凹痕。 |  non-additive, discrete | cumulative, additive | adjective
