astrophyton:/ˌæstrəˈfaɪtɒn/ | n. | 筐蛇尾，星蛇尾 | A type of brittle star, a marine invertebrate with long, slender, branching arms.  It belongs to the class Ophiuroidea. |  例句：The astrophyton gracefully moved its arms through the water. 筐蛇尾在水中优雅地移动着它的腕。

例句：Divers observed a vibrant astrophyton on the coral reef.  潜水员在珊瑚礁上观察到一只充满活力的筐蛇尾。 | 近义词：brittle star, serpent star | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *astron* ("star") + *phyton* ("plant").  The name refers to the star-like appearance of the animal.
