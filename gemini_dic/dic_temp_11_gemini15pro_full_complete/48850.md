deforesters:/ˌdiːˈfɔːrɪstərz/ | n. | 滥伐森林者；毁林者 | People who clear forests by cutting down trees, often on a large scale, usually for economic purposes like farming, logging, or mining. | The Amazon rainforest is being destroyed by illegal deforesters. 亚马逊雨林正被非法滥伐森林者破坏。 | The deforesters were fined heavily for their illegal activities.  这些滥伐森林者因其非法活动而被处以重罚。 | synonyms: logger, lumberjack, clear-cutter | antonyms: conservationist, environmentalist | noun
