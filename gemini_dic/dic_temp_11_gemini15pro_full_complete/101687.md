irremissible:/ˌɪrɪˈmɪsəbl/ | adj. | 不可饶恕的，不可原谅的 | Not capable of being forgiven; unpardonable. |  His actions were so cruel and heartless, they were utterly irremissible. 他的行为如此残忍无情，完全不可饶恕。

例句：

The judge deemed the crime irremissible and sentenced the defendant to life in prison. 法官认为这起罪行不可饶恕，判处被告终身监禁。


Her betrayal was irremissible, and their friendship ended that day. 她的背叛不可原谅，他们的友谊在那天结束了。


近义词：unpardonable, unforgivable, inexcusable | 反义词：forgivable, pardonable, excusable | 词性：形容词

词源：Late Middle English: from Old French irremissible, from Latin irremissibilis, from in- ‘not’ + remissibilis (from remittere ‘send back, pardon’).
