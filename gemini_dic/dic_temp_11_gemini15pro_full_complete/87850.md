hideboundnesses:/ˈhaɪdˌbaʊndnəsəz/
n.
狭隘；保守；顽固；墨守成规
The state of being hidebound, characterized by a rigid adherence to tradition and a resistance to change.
His hideboundnesses prevented him from accepting new ideas. 他的保守使他无法接受新思想。
narrow-mindedness, inflexibility, dogmatism, rigidity
open-mindedness, flexibility, adaptability
noun
