ETYMOLOGY->
From core + -ed.  "Core" comes from the Old French "cor" meaning "heart," ultimately from the Latin "cor," also meaning "heart."  The suffix "-ed" forms the past tense and past participle of regular verbs, and also adjectives from nouns.
