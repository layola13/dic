nonpresumptive:/ˌnɑːnprɪˈzʌmptɪv/
adj. |非推定的，非假定的|Not based on presumption; not presumptive. | The evidence is nonpresumptive, so we need more investigation.  证据是非推定的，所以我们需要更多的调查。|Synonyms: non-assumptive, inconclusive,  uncertain|Antonyms: presumptive, assumptive, conclusive|词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From non- + presumptive.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "presumptive" comes from the Late Latin word "praesumptivus," meaning "taken beforehand."
