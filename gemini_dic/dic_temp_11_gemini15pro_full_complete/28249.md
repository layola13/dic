captainess: /ˈkæptɪnɪs/ | n. | 女船长，女舰长 | A woman who is a captain, especially of a ship. |  She was a fearless captainess, navigating her ship through the storm. 她是一位无所畏惧的女船长，驾驶着她的船穿过风暴。

| Example: The captainess steered the ship expertly through the narrow channel. 女船长熟练地驾驶着船穿过狭窄的航道。 | Synonym: captain, skipper (more informal) | Antonym: crew, subordinate | noun
