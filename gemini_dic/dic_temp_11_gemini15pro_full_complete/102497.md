jacobinize:/ˈdʒækəbɪnaɪz/| v. |雅各宾化，激进化|To make or become radical, especially in politics, like the Jacobins of the French Revolution.|The government accused the opposition party of trying to jacobinize the country.|Synonyms: radicalize, revolutionize,  extremize|Antonyms: moderate, stabilize, conservatize|verb
jacobinization:/ˌdʒækəbɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |雅各宾化，激进化|The process of becoming jacobinized, the act of making something more radical.|The jacobinization of the political landscape led to widespread unrest.|Synonyms: radicalization, extremism |Antonyms: moderation, conservatism|noun
