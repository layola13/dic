monocephalous:/ˌmɒnəˈsɛfələs/ | adj. | 单头的，单头肌的 | Having only one head.  This term is primarily used in biological contexts, particularly in describing anatomical structures or mythical creatures. |  The monocephalous mutation in the fruit fly resulted in larvae with a single head. 果蝇的单头突变导致幼虫只有一个头。
