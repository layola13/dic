non-lucid:/nɒnˈluːsɪd/ | adj. | 不清醒的，神志不清的 | Not clear or lucid; especially not thinking or speaking clearly.  |  After the accident, he was non-lucid for several hours. 事故发生后，他几个小时都神志不清。 |  The patient's non-lucid ramblings concerned the doctors. 病人语无伦次的胡言乱语让医生们很担心。 | 近义词: unclear, confused, incoherent, delirious | 反义词: lucid, clear, coherent, rational | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From non- + lucid, from Latin lucidus "bright, shining, clear," from lucēre "to shine."
