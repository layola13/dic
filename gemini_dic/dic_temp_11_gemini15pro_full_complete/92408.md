hypogean:/ˌhaɪpəˈdʒiːən/ /ˌhaɪpoʊˈdʒiːən/
adjective
地下生活的；地下的
Living or occurring under the earth; subterranean.
例句：Hypogean animals are adapted to life in caves. 地下动物适应了洞穴生活。

例句：The hypogean environment is characterized by darkness and high humidity. 地下环境的特点是黑暗和高湿度。
近义词：subterranean, underground, chthonic
反义词：surface, epigean
词性：adjective

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ὑπόγειος (hupógeios, “underground”), from ὑπό (hupó, “under”) + γῆ (gê, “earth, land”).
