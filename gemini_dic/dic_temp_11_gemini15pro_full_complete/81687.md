greenfinch:/ˈɡriːnfɪntʃ/| n.|绿金雀|A small European finch (Chloris chloris) with bright yellow and green plumage.|I saw a greenfinch perched on the bird feeder.|近义词：finch, siskin|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From green + finch.  "Finch" comes from Old English *finc, from Proto-Germanic *finkiz, of imitative origin.
