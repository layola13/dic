abradable:/əˈbreɪdəbl/
adjective
可磨损的，耐磨的
Capable of being abraded or worn away by friction or rubbing.  Designed to wear away under controlled conditions.
The turbine blades are coated with an abradable material to prevent damage to the engine casing. 涡轮叶片涂有可磨损材料，以防止发动机外壳损坏。

The abradable seals minimize leakage and improve engine efficiency. 可磨损密封件最大限度地减少泄漏并提高发动机效率。
近义词: erodible, wearable
反义词: non-abradable, resistant
词性: adjective

词源:  From "abrade" + "-able". "Abrade" comes from Latin "abradere," from "ab-" (away) + "radere" (to scrape).
