antepast:/ˈæntɪˌpæst/ (US), /ˈæntiˌpɑːst/ (UK)| n.|开胃菜，前菜|A small dish of food served before the main meal.|例句：We started our meal with a delicious antepast of marinated olives. 我们以美味的腌橄榄开胃菜开始了用餐。

例句：The antepast consisted of various cheeses and cured meats.  开胃菜包括各种奶酪和腌制肉类。|近义词：appetizer, starter, hors d'oeuvre|反义词:dessert, afters|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *antipasto*, from *ante* (before) + *pasto* (meal, food).  Ultimately from Latin *ante* (before) and *pascere* (to feed).
