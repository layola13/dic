mendacious: /mɛnˈdeɪʃəs/ | adj. | 虚假的，撒谎的 |  Given to or characterized by deception or falsehoods.  | The politician's mendacious statements eroded public trust.  这位政客的谎言损害了公众的信任。| dishonest, lying, untruthful, deceitful | truthful, honest, sincere | adjective
