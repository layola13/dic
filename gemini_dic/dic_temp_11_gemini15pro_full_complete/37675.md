compactors:/kəmˈpæktərz/
n.
压实机；压缩机
Machines that compact materials such as trash or soil.
The city uses compactors to reduce the volume of garbage. 城市使用压实机来减少垃圾体积。
The construction crew used a soil compactor to prepare the ground for the foundation. 建筑工人使用土壤压实机来为地基做准备。
synonyms:crushers, balers, compacting machines
antonyms:expanders
