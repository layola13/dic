antievolutionistic:/ˌæntiˌɛvəˈluːʃənɪstɪk/
adjective
反进化论的
Opposed to the theory of evolution.
The politician made antievolutionistic remarks, causing an uproar among scientists.  这位政治家发表了反进化论的言论，引起了科学家的强烈抗议。
He holds antievolutionistic beliefs. 他持有反进化论的信仰。
近义词:creationist, anti-Darwinian
反义词:pro-evolution, evolutionary
词性:adjective

词源:
anti- (prefix meaning "against") + evolution (from Latin evolutio, "unrolling") + -istic (suffix forming adjectives)
