nonperspectives:/ˌnɒnpərˈspektɪvz/
n.|非透视图; 非视角; 非观点|Lacking perspectives; not representing or considering different viewpoints.|The artist's nonperspectives made the painting feel flat and unrealistic. 这位艺术家的非透视图让这幅画感觉扁平而不真实。|Near synonyms:  unconventional views, limited viewpoints, narrow perspectives|Antonyms: perspectives, viewpoints, points of view|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "perspectives" (plural of "perspective," from Latin "perspectiva," meaning "seeing through").
