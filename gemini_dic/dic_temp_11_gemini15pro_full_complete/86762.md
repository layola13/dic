helplessness:/ˈhɛlpləsnəs/| n.|无助，无力|The feeling of being unable to take care of oneself or affect a situation.|She felt a deep sense of helplessness as she watched the fire consume her home. 她看着大火吞噬了她的家，感到深深的无助。|近义词：powerlessness, inability, vulnerability|反义词:power, ability, control|词性:名词
helpless:/ˈhɛlplɪs/| adj.|无助的，无力的|Unable to take care of oneself or affect a situation.|The injured bird was helpless against the cat. 受伤的小鸟对猫无能为力。|近义词：powerless, dependent, vulnerable|反义词:powerful, capable, independent|词性:形容词

词源:  From help + -less + -ness.  Ultimately from Old English helpan.
