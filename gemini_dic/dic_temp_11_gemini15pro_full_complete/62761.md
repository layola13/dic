engraft:/ɪnˈɡræft/ /ɛnˈɡræft/| vt. |嫁接；移植；灌输|To insert or implant (something) as if by grafting, especially to establish something foreign or new in a close relationship with something existing.|They tried to engraft new ideas onto the old system. 他们试图将新思想融入旧系统中。|The surgeon successfully engrafted skin from the patient's leg onto his arm.  外科医生成功地将病人腿上的皮肤移植到他的手臂上。|近义词：implant, embed, insert, introduce, inculcate|反义词：excise, remove, uproot|v.
