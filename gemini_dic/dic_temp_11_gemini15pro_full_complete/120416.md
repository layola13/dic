minisummits:/ˈmɪniˌsʌmɪts/
noun
小型峰会，小型首脑会议
A series of small summits, often focused on a specific topic or involving a smaller group of participants than a full summit.
The president held a series of minisummits with regional leaders. 总统与区域领导人举行了一系列小型峰会。
The organization hosted several minisummits on climate change. 该组织主办了多次关于气候变化的小型峰会。
synonyms: small summits, mini-conferences, workshops
antonyms: major summits, large conferences, plenary sessions
n.
