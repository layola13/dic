at-one:/ətˈwʌn/ | n. | 和谐，一致 | agreement, harmony | The at-one between the two countries lasted for decades. 两国之间的和谐持续了几十年。| 例句:  Their at-one was short-lived, as disagreements soon arose. 他们的和谐很短暂，因为分歧很快出现。 | 近义词：unity, harmony, concord, agreement | 反义词：discord, conflict, disharmony, disagreement | 词性: 名词


词源:  "at-one" comes from the Old English phrase "æt ānum," meaning "at one," literally referring to a state of unity or agreement.
