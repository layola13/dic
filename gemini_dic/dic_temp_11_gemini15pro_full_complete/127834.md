nemean:/ˈniːmiən/| adj.|尼米亚的；凶猛的，残忍的|Relating to Nemea, a valley in ancient Greece, or to the Nemean Games held there; also, by extension, ferocious or savage.|The Nemean lion was a monstrous beast.|Synonyms: ferocious, savage, brutal, fierce|Antonyms: gentle, tame, merciful, kind|adjective

nemean:/ˈniːmiən/| n.|尼米亚运动会；尼米亚的狮子|The Nemean Games, one of the Panhellenic Games of ancient Greece, or the Nemean lion slain by Heracles as the first of his twelve labors.|Heracles' victory over the Nemean lion secured his place as a hero.|Synonyms: (for Nemean Games) Panhellenic Games, athletic competition; (for Nemean lion) monster, beast|Antonyms: (no direct antonym)|noun
