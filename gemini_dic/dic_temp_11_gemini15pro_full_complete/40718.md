convertive:/kənˈvɜːtɪv/ | adj. | 皈依的；转变的；有转换能力的 | Having the power or tendency to convert or change; pertaining to conversion. |  The preacher delivered a convertive sermon, hoping to inspire his congregation.  牧师发表了一篇劝人皈依的布道，希望激励他的会众。 | synonyms: transformative, transmuting, changing | antonyms: static, unchanging | adjective

convertive:/kənˈvɜːtɪv/ | n. | 皈依者；转换器 | One who converts or causes conversion; a device that converts. (This noun usage is less common) | The new convertive joined the monastery and dedicated his life to prayer. 新的皈依者加入了修道院，并将他的生命奉献给了祈祷。 | synonyms: convert, proselyte, transformer | antonyms: opponent, adversary | noun
