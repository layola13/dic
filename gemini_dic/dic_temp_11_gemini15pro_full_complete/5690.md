aluminate:/əˈluːmɪneɪt/
n.|铝酸盐|A salt formed by reaction of aluminum hydroxide with an alkali.|Sodium aluminate is used in water treatment.|近义词: aluminum salt|反义词: None|词性:noun
aluminate:/əˈluːmɪneɪt/
v.|形成铝酸盐|To treat or combine with alumina or an alkali.|The bauxite is aluminated in the process.|近义词: treat with alumina|反义词: None|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From alumina + -ate, ultimately from Latin alumen (“alum”).
