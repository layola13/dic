cheekiest:/ˈtʃiːkiɪst/| adj.|最厚脸皮的，最无礼的|Superlative form of cheeky: impudent or irreverent, typically in an endearing or amusing way.|例句：That was the cheekiest thing I've ever heard! 那是我听过的最厚颜无耻的话！|例句：He gave me the cheekiest grin. 他给了我一个最淘气的笑容。|近义词：impudent, brazen, bold, saucy, impertinent|反义词：respectful, polite, courteous|词性:形容词

词源: From cheek + -y + -est.  "Cheek" originally referred to the fleshy part of the face below the eye, but developed a figurative sense of "impudence" likely because a puffed-out cheek can be a sign of defiance.
