accompanist: /əˈkʌmpənɪst/ | n. |伴奏者；伴唱者| A person who plays a musical instrument or sings along with another musician, especially as a supporting role. | The pianist is a skilled accompanist who can adapt to any soloist.  这位钢琴家是一位技艺娴熟的伴奏者，可以适应任何独奏者。| Synonyms:  collaborator, partner | Antonyms: soloist, lead | noun
