cric:/krɪk/| n.|颈部僵硬；落枕|A crick in the neck.  颈部僵硬。|I woke up with a crick in my neck this morning. 我今天早上醒来落枕了。|近义词：stiff neck, spasm, twinge|反义词：(无直接反义词，可用 flexible neck 表示颈部灵活)|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crikke*, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Middle Dutch *kricke* (“crutch, staff”), or imitative of the sound of a cracking joint.
