controllables:/kənˈtroʊləbəlz/ | n. | 可控制因素，可控因素 | Factors that can be influenced or managed. | The project manager identified the key controllables in the project timeline. 项目经理确定了项目时间表中的关键可控因素。|  Synonyms: manageable factors, adjustable parameters | Antonyms: uncontrollables, unmanageable factors | Noun

controllable:/kənˈtroʊləbəl/ | adj. | 可控制的，可操纵的 | Able to be controlled or managed. |  The temperature in the room is controllable with the thermostat. 房间的温度可以用恒温器控制。| Synonyms: manageable, adjustable, governable | Antonyms: uncontrollable, unmanageable, ungovernable | Adjective
