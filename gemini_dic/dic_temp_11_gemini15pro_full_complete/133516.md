nonfeudal:/ˌnɒnˈfjuːdəl/
adjective
非封建的
Not pertaining to or characteristic of feudalism; not feudal.
The historian argued that the society was already developing nonfeudal characteristics. 这位历史学家认为该社会已经发展出非封建的特征。
The rise of a nonfeudal merchant class challenged the existing power structure. 非封建商人阶层的兴起挑战了现有的权力结构。
Near Antonyms: feudal, medieval
Antonyms: feudal
