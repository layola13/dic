anteportico:/ˌæntiˈpɔːrtɪkoʊ/ |n.|前廊，门廊|A porch, portico, or colonnade in front of a building, especially a church or temple, often forming a vestibule.|The anteportico of the cathedral was grand and imposing.  大教堂的前廊宏伟壮观。|Synonyms: porch, portico, vestibule, colonnade |Antonyms: back porch, rear entrance|noun
