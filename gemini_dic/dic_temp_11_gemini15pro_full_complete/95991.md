incurables: /ɪnˈkjʊrəblz/
noun
无法治愈的人（或事物）; 不治之症患者
People (or things) that cannot be cured; patients with incurable diseases.
The hospital has a special ward for incurables.  这家医院有一个专门收治不治之症患者的病房。
There are some social problems that seem like incurables.  有一些社会问题似乎是无法解决的。
Synonyms: terminally ill, hopeless cases
Antonyms: curables, treatable cases
n.


incurable: /ɪnˈkjʊrəbl/
adjective
无法治愈的；不可救药的
Unable to be cured; hopeless.
He has an incurable disease. 他患有不治之症。
She has an incurable optimism. 她有着不可救药的乐观精神。
Synonyms: irremediable, terminal, hopeless, irrecoverable
Antonyms: curable, treatable, remediable, recoverable
adj.
