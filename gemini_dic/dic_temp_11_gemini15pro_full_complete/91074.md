huti: /ˈhuːti/ | n. |小屋; 棚屋| A small, simple dwelling, often temporary or makeshift. |  Example: They built a huti in the woods for shelter during the storm. 他们在树林里建了一个小屋，以便在暴风雨中避难。

Example: The shepherds lived in hutis high in the mountains. 牧羊人住在高山上的小屋里。 |Synonym: hut, shack, cabin, shed | Antonym: mansion, palace | noun
