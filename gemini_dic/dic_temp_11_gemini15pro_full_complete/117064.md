me:/miː/
pron.|我（宾格）|The objective case of I, referring to the speaker or writer as the object of a verb or preposition.|She gave the book to me. 她把书给了我。|近义词: (None - pronouns don't have synonyms in the same way other words do) |反义词: (None - pronouns don't have antonyms)|词性:代词

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He sees me every day.  他每天都看见我。
They call me often.  他们经常打电话给我。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
She is helping me with my homework. 她正在帮我做作业。
They are looking at me. 他们在看着我。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has seen me before. 他以前见过我。
They have invited me to the party. 他们邀请我去参加聚会。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
She has been teaching me English. 她一直在教我英语。
They have been watching me closely. 他们一直在密切关注我。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He saw me yesterday. 他昨天看见了我。
They called me last night. 他们昨晚打电话给我了。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had seen me before the meeting. 会议前他见过我。
They had invited me to the party already. 他们已经邀请我去参加聚会了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

一般将来时 (Simple Future):

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

虚拟语气:

I wish she would help me. 我希望她能帮助我。

被动语态:

否定句:

They haven't called me yet. 他们还没给我打电话。
