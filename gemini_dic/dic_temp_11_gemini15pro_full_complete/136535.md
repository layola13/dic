nonruminative:/ˌnɒnˈruːmɪnətɪv/
| adj. | 非反刍的，不沉思的 | Not chewing the cud; not ruminating. Also, not given to contemplation or reflection. | The scientist studied the digestive system of nonruminative animals.  科学家研究了非反刍动物的消化系统。| The child's nonruminative nature meant he quickly moved on from disappointments.  这个孩子不爱沉思的性格意味着他很快就能从失望中走出来。 | Synonyms: unreflective, unthinking, impulsive | Antonyms: ruminative, reflective, contemplative | adjective
