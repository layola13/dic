moyle:/mɔɪl/ | n. | 杂交动物; 骡子(尤指母骡);  |A moyle is an offspring of a male horse and a female donkey. | The farmer used a moyle for plowing his fields. 农民用骡子耕地。 | 近义词: mule, hybrid | 反义词: purebred | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *moyle, from Old French mule, from Latin mūlus (“mule”).
