greasier:/ˈɡriːziər/ /ˈɡriːsiər/| adj.|更油腻的，更油滑的|Comparative form of greasy: more greasy.  Covered with or resembling grease; oily or fatty. Also, slippery or slimy.|The mechanic's hands were greasier than usual after working on the engine all day.  修车工整天在发动机上工作后，他的手比平时更油腻了。|The bacon is greasier than I like. 这培根比我喜欢的油腻。|近义词：oilier, fattier, slicker|反义词：cleaner, drier, less greasy|词性:形容词

词源: From Old English *grēsiġ, from grēse "grease."
