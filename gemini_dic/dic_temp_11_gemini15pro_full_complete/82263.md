groundings:/ˈɡraʊndɪŋz/
n.|（常复数）基本原则；基本知识|A strong grounding in mathematics is essential for this course.  本课程必须具备扎实的数学基础。|近义词：basics, fundamentals, principles, rudiments|反义词: ignorance, unfamiliarity|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From ground (verb) + -ing (suffix forming nouns from verbs)
From Old English "grund" meaning "bottom, foundation, surface of the earth"
