nappe:/næp/| n. |推覆体，褶皱层，台布|A sheetlike structure in which the beds dip uniformly in one direction, as in a recumbent fold. A large, generally flat or gently undulating sheet of rock that has moved over a large horizontal distance by thrust faulting or recumbent folding.  Also, tablecloth.|The geological nappe was extensive, covering hundreds of square kilometers.  这个地质推覆体范围很广，覆盖了数百平方公里。|Synonyms: sheet, layer, overthrust, fold |Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a geological formation) |noun

nappe:/næp/| n. |台布|A tablecloth.|The nappe was beautifully embroidered.  这块桌布绣得很漂亮。|Synonyms: tablecloth, cloth, cover |Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a physical object) |noun
