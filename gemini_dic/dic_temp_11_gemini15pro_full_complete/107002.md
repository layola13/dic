lacrymose: /ˈlækrɪməʊs/ (UK) /ˈlækrɪmoʊs/ (US)
adjective
泪流满面的；催人泪下的
Given to shedding tears readily; tearful. Also: suggestive of or tending to cause tears.
例句：The lacrymose film left many viewers in tears. 这部催人泪下的电影让许多观众流泪。

例句：He delivered a lacrymose speech about his lost love. 他发表了一篇关于他失去的爱情的催人泪下的演讲。

近义词：tearful, weepy, mournful, doleful
反义词：cheerful, joyful, happy
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From late Middle English: from late Latin lacrymosus, from Latin lacrima ‘tear’.
