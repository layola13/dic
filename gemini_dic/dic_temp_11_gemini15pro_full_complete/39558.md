conscientiousness:/ˌkɒnʃɪˈɛnʃəsnəs/ /ˌkɑːnʃiˈɛnʃəsnəs/| n. |认真；尽责；一丝不苟|The state of being thorough, careful, or vigilant.  It implies a desire to do a task well, and to take obligations to others seriously.|Her conscientiousness and attention to detail are remarkable. 她认真负责，注重细节，令人赞叹。|近义词：diligence, meticulousness, scrupulousness|反义词：negligence, carelessness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin conscientia (“conscience”) + -ousness.
