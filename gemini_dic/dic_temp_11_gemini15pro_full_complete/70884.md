fint:/fɪnt/| n.|鱼鳍；（尤指飞机的）垂直安定面；（尤指汽车或船的）鳍状物|A fin-like projection or appendage.  A thin projecting part of an animal, such as a fish, used for swimming or balance. |The fish uses its fins to propel itself through the water. 这条鱼用它的鳍在水中推进自己。|Synonyms: flipper, blade, wing, rudder|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly body/hull)|noun
