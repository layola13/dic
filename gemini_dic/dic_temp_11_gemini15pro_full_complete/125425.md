multiphotographies:/ˌmʌltiˌfəˈtɒɡrəfiz/ | n. | 多重摄影，多幅摄影作品 |  The process or technique of creating multiple photographic images, often combined or layered in some way.  It can also refer to the resulting artwork itself. |  Her latest exhibition showcased her innovative multiphotographies, blending images of nature and urban landscapes.  她最新的展览展示了她创新的多重摄影作品，融合了自然和城市景观的图像。 |  multiple exposures, composite photography, photomontage | N/A | noun
