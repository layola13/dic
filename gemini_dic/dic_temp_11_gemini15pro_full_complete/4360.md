alabaster: /ˈæləˌbæstər/ | n. adj. |雪花石膏；雪白的|A finely granular variety of gypsum, often white and translucent, used for carving and ornamental objects.  Also, a pale white or yellowish color resembling this material. |The sculptor carved a delicate flower from a block of alabaster.  雕塑家从一块雪花石膏上雕刻出一朵精致的花。|近义词：gypsum, marble, ivory, white |反义词:black, dark | noun

alabaster: /ˈæləˌbæstər/ | adj. |雪白的|Having a pale white or yellowish color resembling alabaster. |Her skin was alabaster smooth. 她的皮肤像雪花石膏一样光滑洁白。|近义词：pale, white, ivory |反义词:dark, tanned | adjective
