magnetic-dip:/mæɡˈnɛtɪk dɪp/ | n. | 磁倾角 | The angle between the horizontal and the direction of the Earth's magnetic field at any given point.  | The magnetic dip at the magnetic poles is 90 degrees.  磁极处的磁倾角为 90 度。|  The compass needle showed a significant magnetic dip.  指南针的指针显示出明显的磁倾角。 | inclination, dip angle |  - | noun
