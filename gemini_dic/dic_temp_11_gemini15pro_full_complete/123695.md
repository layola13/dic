moonblind:/ˈmuːnˌblaɪnd/ | adj. |夜盲的；周期性眼炎的| Affected with periodic ophthalmia (inflammation of the eye), especially in horses, recurring with each new or full moon; hence, figuratively, purblind, dim-sighted.| The old farmer believed his horse was moonblind because its vision seemed worse during certain phases of the moon. | 老农夫认为他的马得了夜盲症，因为它的视力在月亮的某些阶段似乎更差。

moonblind:/ˈmuːnˌblaɪnd/ | n. |夜盲症；周期性眼炎|Periodic ophthalmia, especially in horses.| The vet diagnosed the horse with moonblind. | 兽医诊断这匹马患有周期性眼炎。


近义词: night-blind, purblind | 反义词: sighted | 词性: adjective, noun
