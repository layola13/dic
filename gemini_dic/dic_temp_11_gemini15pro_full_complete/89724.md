hopelessly:/ˈhoʊpləsli/ /ˈhəʊpləsli/| adv.|绝望地，无望地|In a way that shows no sign of improvement or success; in a way that shows despair.|例句：She looked hopelessly at the tangled mess of wires. 她绝望地看着缠成一团糟的电线。|近义词：despairingly, desperately, forlornly|反义词: hopefully, optimistically|词性:副词
Etymology: From "hopeless" + "-ly". "Hopeless" comes from "hope" (from Old English "hopian") + "-less" (from Old English "-lēas").
