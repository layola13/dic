as regards:/əz rɪˈɡɑːrdz/
preposition
关于，至于
Concerning; in respect of.
As regards the first point, I agree completely. 关于第一点，我完全同意。
Synonyms: concerning, regarding, respecting, with respect to, with regard to
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but one might use "excluding" or "disregarding" to express an opposite concept)
