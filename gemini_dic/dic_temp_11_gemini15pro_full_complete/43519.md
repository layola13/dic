cravingness:/ˈkreɪvɪŋnɪs/| n. |渴望，热望|The state of experiencing a strong desire or craving for something.|His cravingness for chocolate was insatiable. 他对巧克力的渴望永不满足。|近义词：desire, yearning, longing, hunger, thirst|反义词：apathy, indifference, contentment|词性:名词
Etymology: From craving + -ness.  Craving comes from the Old English *crafian "to demand, ask, beg" related to the Old Norse krefja "to demand." The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
