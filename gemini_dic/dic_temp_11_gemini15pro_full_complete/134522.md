nonlibrarians:/ˌnɑːnlaɪˈbrɛəriənz/
n.|非图书馆员|People who are not librarians; those outside the library profession.  This term encompasses individuals from all other professions and walks of life.|Many nonlibrarians underestimate the skills required to manage a library effectively.|近义词:laypeople, general public|反义词:librarians|词性:名词

词源:
"non-" (prefix): Latin origin, meaning "not"
"librarian": From Latin "librarius" (copyist of books)
