grandmaster:/ˈɡrændˌmæstər/| n.|大师，特级大师|A person of the highest rank in a game, especially chess, or in a skill or art.|Magnus Carlsen is a chess grandmaster. 马格努斯·卡尔森是一位国际象棋特级大师。|The grandmaster of kung fu demonstrated his skills. 功夫大师展示了他的技艺。|synonyms: expert, master, virtuoso, prodigy, champion|antonyms: novice, beginner, amateur|noun
