haphazardnesses:/ˌhæpˈhæzərdnəsəz/ |n.|偶然性；随意性；杂乱无章|The haphazardnesses of the arrangement were apparent to everyone.  这种安排的随意性对每个人来说都很明显。| The project suffered from the haphazardnesses of its planning. 这个项目因其计划的杂乱无章而受损。|近义词：randomness, disorder, chaos, chance|反义词：order, organization, system, planning|词性:名词

Etymology: From haphazard + -ness.  "Haphazard" itself comes from hap (chance, luck) + hazard (danger, risk).  Originally, it referred to something left to chance or luck, potentially involving risk.
