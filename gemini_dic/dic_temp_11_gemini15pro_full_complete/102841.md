jasmined:/ˈdʒæzmɪnd/ | adj. | 茉莉花香味的；像茉莉花的 | Having the scent of jasmine; resembling jasmine. |  The air was jasmined and warm. 空气中弥漫着茉莉花的香味，温暖宜人。|  Her perfume was subtly jasmined. 她的香水散发着淡淡的茉莉花香。 | 近义词: fragrant, aromatic, perfumed | 反义词: odorless, unscented | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Derived from "jasmine" + "-ed".  "Jasmine" comes from the Persian word "yasmin" (یاسمن), ultimately from Arabic "yāsamīn" (ياسمين). The suffix "-ed" forms adjectives from nouns, indicating possession of the quality denoted by the noun.
