stipulation:/ˌstɪpjʊˈleɪʃn/
n.
规定，条款，约定
A stipulation is a condition or requirement that is specified or demanded as part of an agreement.
The contract included a stipulation that the work must be completed within six months.  合同中包含一项规定，即必须在六个月内完成工作。
The loan was granted with the stipulation that it be repaid within two years. 这笔贷款是在两年内偿还的条件下获得的。
synonym:condition, requirement, provision, clause, term
antonym:flexibility, leniency
