badgeringly:/ˈbædʒərɪŋli/
adv.
持续不断地烦扰地
persistently and annoyingly, like a badger harassing its prey.
He badgeringly questioned her about her whereabouts. 他一直纠缠不休地问她去过哪里。
近义词: persistently, relentlessly, harassingly, importunately
反义词: gently, mildly, politely
