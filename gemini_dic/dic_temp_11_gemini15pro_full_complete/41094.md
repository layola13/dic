coppola:/ˈkɒpələ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpələ/ (US)| n.| 男用鸭舌帽；便帽；（尤指）教父帽|A flat cap, typically with a small peak, worn by men. Often associated with Sicilian culture and the character of the Godfather.|He wore a dark coppola pulled low over his eyes. 他戴着一顶黑色的鸭舌帽，帽檐拉得很低，遮住了眼睛。|Synonyms: flat cap, driving cap, newsboy cap, Gatsby cap|Antonyms: N/A|noun

coppola:/ˈkɒpələ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpələ/ (US)| n.| 女用鸭舌帽；便帽；（尤指）教父帽|A flat cap, typically with a small peak, worn by women.  Can also refer to a similar style of hat worn by women.|She looked stylish in a tweed coppola. 她戴着一顶粗花呢鸭舌帽，看起来很时尚。|Synonyms: flat cap, newsboy cap, baker boy hat|Antonyms: N/A|noun
