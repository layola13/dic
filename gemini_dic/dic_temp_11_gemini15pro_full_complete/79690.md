gloweringly:/ˈɡlaʊərɪŋli/ | adv. | 怒目而视地 | in a way that shows anger or hostility by staring | He gloweringly stared at the intruder. 他怒视着入侵者。|例句：She gloweringly refused his offer. 她怒气冲冲地拒绝了他的提议。| 近义词：angrily, sullenly, fiercely | 反义词：happily, cheerfully | 词性: 副词

glowering:/ˈɡlaʊərɪŋ/ | adj. | 怒视的，阴沉的 | angry and hostile; showing a brooding ill humor | The sky was glowering, promising a storm. 天空阴沉沉的，预示着暴风雨即将来临。|例句：He gave me a glowering look. 他怒视着我。| 近义词：scowling, frowning, menacing | 反义词：smiling, cheerful | 词性: 形容词
