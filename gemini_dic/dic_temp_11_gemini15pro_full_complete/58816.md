duopoly:/djuːˈɒpəli/ /duˈɑːpəli/| n.| 双头垄断，双寡头垄断 |A situation in which two suppliers dominate the market for a commodity or service.|  The telecommunications duopoly in this country limits consumer choice.  这个国家的电信双寡头垄断限制了消费者的选择。|Synonym:oligopoly|Antonym:monopoly, perfect competition|noun
