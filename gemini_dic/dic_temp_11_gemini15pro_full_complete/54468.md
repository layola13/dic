disintegratively:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡrətɪvli/| adv.|瓦解地，分裂地|In a manner that causes something to break apart or disintegrate.  Describes an action or process that leads to disintegration.|The empire disintegratively collapsed after the death of the emperor.  帝国在皇帝死后瓦解崩溃了。|Synonyms: destructively, fragmentarily, ruinously|Antonyms: constructively, cohesively, unitively|词性:副词

disintegrate:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪt/| v.|瓦解，碎裂，解体|To break apart into smaller pieces or fragments; to lose cohesion or unity.|The spacecraft disintegrated upon re-entry into the Earth's atmosphere. 宇宙飞船重返地球大气层时解体了。|Synonyms: crumble, decompose, fall apart|Antonyms: unite, combine, solidify|词性:动词
