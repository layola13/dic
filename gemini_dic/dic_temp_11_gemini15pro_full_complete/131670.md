nonconceptual:/ˌnɒnkənˈsɛptʃuəl/ | adj. | 非概念的，非观念的 | Not related to or based on concepts; not involving or requiring thought or reasoning. |  Her art is nonconceptual, focusing on emotional responses rather than intellectual ideas. 她的艺术是非概念性的，专注于情感反应而不是智力思想。 |  The baby's reaction to the bright colors was purely nonconceptual, a simple sensory experience. 婴儿对鲜艳色彩的反应纯粹是非概念性的，是一种简单的感官体验。 |  non-cognitive, intuitive | conceptual, intellectual | adjective
