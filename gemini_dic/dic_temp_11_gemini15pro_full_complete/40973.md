cooptate:/koʊˈɑːpteɪt/
verb
吸收；增选；拉拢
to choose someone to become a member of a group or organization, especially by inviting someone who did not go through the usual selection process
The committee cooptated several new members last year. 委员会去年增选了几名新成员。
Synonyms: elect, appoint, select, choose
Antonyms: reject, exclude, dismiss, remove
词性: 动词


co-opt:/koʊˈɑːpt/
verb
拉拢；笼络；争取
to win or obtain the support of (someone) especially by offering special advantages
The mayor tried to co-opt his opponent by offering him a position on the city council. 市长试图通过给他提供市议会职位来争取他的对手。
Synonyms: enlist, win over, recruit
Antonyms: alienate, antagonize, repel
词性: 动词
