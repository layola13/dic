airbrush:/ˈɛərˌbrʌʃ/ (US), /ˈeəˌbrʌʃ/ (UK)| n. vt.|喷枪，气笔；用喷枪喷涂|A device that sprays paint by means of compressed air.  It is used for creating smooth gradations of color.|The artist used an airbrush to create a realistic portrait.  艺术家用喷枪创作了一幅逼真的肖像画。|synonyms: spray gun, atomizer|antonyms: brush, roller|noun, verb

airbrushes:/ˈɛərˌbrʌʃɪz/ (US), /ˈeəˌbrʌʃɪz/ (UK)| v.|用喷枪喷涂|To spray something using an airbrush.|He airbrushes custom designs onto motorcycles. 他用喷枪在摩托车上喷绘定制图案。|synonyms: sprays, atomizes|antonyms: brushes, rolls|verb
