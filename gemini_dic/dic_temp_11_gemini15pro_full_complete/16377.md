barbette:/bɑːrˈbɛt/ | n. |炮座，露天炮台|A protective circular platform, often armored, on a warship for mounting a heavy gun, allowing it to rotate and fire over a wide arc. |The battleship's main guns were mounted on barbettes.  战舰的主炮安装在炮座上。|The barbette allowed the gun to traverse a full 360 degrees.  炮座允许火炮360度旋转。| synonym: gun platform, turret mount | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "hull" in the sense that it's the opposite of a raised platform) | noun


barbette:/bɑːrˈbɛt/ | n. |(筑城)胸墙|In fortifications, a raised platform or mound of earth, often part of a parapet, from which guns could be fired. |The soldiers manned the barbette overlooking the valley. 士兵们在俯瞰山谷的胸墙上操纵火炮。| The barbette provided a clear field of fire for the artillery. 胸墙为炮兵提供了清晰的射界。 | synonym: rampart, parapet, embankment | antonym: ditch, moat | noun
