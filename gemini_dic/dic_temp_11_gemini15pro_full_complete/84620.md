hance:/hɑːns/ /hæns/ | v. | 增强，提高，增进 | To enhance something means to improve its value, quality, or attractiveness. |  例句：The new software is designed to enhance user experience.  新软件旨在提升用户体验。

例句：Regular exercise can enhance your physical and mental well-being.  规律的锻炼可以增强你的身心健康。

近义词：improve, increase, boost, augment, strengthen, heighten | 反义词: diminish, reduce, weaken, detract from | 词性:动词
