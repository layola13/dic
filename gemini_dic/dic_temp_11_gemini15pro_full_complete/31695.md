chauvinist: /ˈʃoʊvɪnɪst/ (US), /ˈʃəʊvɪnɪst/ (UK)| n. adj.| 沙文主义者，盲目爱国者；沙文主义的，盲目爱国的|A chauvinist is someone who believes that their own group (e.g., country, gender, race) is superior to all others.  As an adjective, it describes this belief or behavior.| He's such a chauvinist – he thinks men are naturally better at everything. 他真是个沙文主义者——他认为男人天生就擅长一切。|synonyms: bigot, jingoist, sexist (often)| antonyms: egalitarian, tolerant, open-minded| noun, adjective

chauvinistic: /ˌʃoʊvɪˈnɪstɪk/ (US), /ˌʃəʊvɪˈnɪstɪk/ (UK)| adj.| 沙文主义的，盲目爱国的|Showing excessive or prejudiced loyalty or support for one's own cause, group, or sex.|  Her chauvinistic remarks about women in the workplace caused outrage.  她在工作场所对女性发表的沙文主义言论引起了众怒。|synonyms: bigoted, jingoistic, prejudiced| antonyms: egalitarian, tolerant, open-minded| adjective
