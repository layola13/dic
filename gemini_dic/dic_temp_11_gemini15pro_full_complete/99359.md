interdependent:/ˌɪntərˈdɪˈpɛndənt/
adjective
相互依存的，互相依赖的
mutually reliant; dependent on each other.
The two companies have an interdependent relationship. 这两家公司有着相互依存的关系。
The global economy is becoming increasingly interdependent. 全球经济正变得越来越相互依存。
近义词:interrelated, interconnected, interactive
反义词:independent, autonomous, separate
词性:adjective
