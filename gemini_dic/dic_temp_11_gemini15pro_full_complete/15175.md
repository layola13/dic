backarack:/ˈbækəræk/| n. |军营，兵营；临时营房|A group of huts or buildings used to house soldiers or other workers.  A temporary camp, especially one for soldiers or refugees. |例句：The soldiers were housed in a backarack near the border. 士兵们被安置在靠近边界的军营里。

例句：The refugees set up a makeshift backarack until more permanent housing could be found. 难民们搭建了一个临时营房，直到找到更永久的住房为止。|近义词：barracks, camp, bivouac|反义词:home, house|词性:noun
