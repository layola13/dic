boohooing:/ˌbuːˈhuːɪŋ/
| n. | 哭诉，假哭 | A prolonged spell of crying or weeping, often exaggerated or insincere. |  She was boohooing about her lost phone, but I knew she had another one. 她哭诉着手机丢了，但我知道她还有另一部。 |  crying, weeping, sobbing, wailing | feigning, pretending | noun
boohoo:/ˌbuːˈhuː/
| int. & v. |  (表示哭泣)呜呜;  哭诉; 假哭 | Used to represent the sound of crying, or to cry noisily, often in an exaggerated or insincere way. | "Boohoo," she sobbed, "I've lost my teddy bear!"  “呜呜，”她抽泣着，“我的泰迪熊丢了！” | cry, weep, sob, wail | laugh, rejoice | interjection, verb
