australs:/ˈɔːstrəlz/| n.|南(方)人；澳大利亚人；(尤指)澳大利亚土著居民|People or things from the south, especially from Australia, particularly Aboriginal Australians.|The australs gathered for the annual festival. 南方人聚集起来参加一年一度的节日。|Synonyms: Australians, southerners|Antonyms: northerners|Noun

Etymology->
From Latin *australis*, meaning "southern," derived from auster, meaning "south wind."
