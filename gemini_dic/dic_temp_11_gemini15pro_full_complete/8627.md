anticommercially:/ˌænti kəˈmɜːʃəli/
| adv. |反商业地，非商业地| In a way that opposes or rejects commercial principles or practices. |  He lived anticommercially, refusing to buy anything that wasn't absolutely necessary. 他过着反商业的生活，拒绝购买任何并非绝对必要的东西。|
|
|He wrote poetry anticommercially, caring more about artistic expression than profit. 他进行非商业的诗歌创作，更关心艺术表达而不是利润。|
|uncommercial, noncommercial| commercial, mercenary| adverb
