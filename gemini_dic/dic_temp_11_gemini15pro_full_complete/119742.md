midwife: /ˈmɪdwaɪf/ (UK) /ˈmɪdˌwaɪf/ (US)| v.|为…接生；助产|To assist in childbirth.|The midwife midwifed her first baby last week.  上周这位助产士接生了她的第一个婴儿。|Example 2: She has midwifed hundreds of babies over her career. 在她的职业生涯中，她已经接生了数百个婴儿。|近义词：deliver, assist in childbirth|反义词: N/A|词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *midwif, from mid "with" + wif "woman."  Literally, "with-woman," referring to the woman who is with the mother during childbirth.
