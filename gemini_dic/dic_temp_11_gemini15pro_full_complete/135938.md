nonpurchase:/ˌnɑːnˈpɜːrtʃəs/ |n.| 非购买，不购买行为 |The survey tracked consumer behavior including both purchases and nonpurchases. 这项调查追踪了消费者行为，包括购买和非购买行为。| The marketing team analyzed the reasons for nonpurchase. 营销团队分析了不购买的原因。 | 近义词: nonbuying, abstention from purchase | 反义词: purchase, acquisition | 词性: 名词

Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + purchase (from Old French "purchaser" - to acquire, pursue)
