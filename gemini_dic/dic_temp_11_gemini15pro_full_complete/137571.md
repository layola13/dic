nontransparently:/ˌnɒn trænsˈpɛrəntli/ | adv. | 不透明地；不清晰地；难以理解地 | In a way that is not transparent; not clearly or openly; obscurely. | The rules were applied nontransparently, leading to confusion and distrust. 规则应用得不明确，导致混乱和不信任。| The algorithm operates nontransparently, making it difficult to understand its decisions.  该算法运作不透明，难以理解其决策。 | 近义词: obscurely, opaquely, vaguely, unclearly | 反义词: transparently, clearly, openly, plainly | 词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
transparent (adjective): from Latin transparens, present participle of transpārēre "to appear through, be visible," from trans- "through" + pārēre "to appear, be visible"
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
