albinistic:/ˌælbɪˈnɪstɪk/
|adj.|白化病的，患白化病的|Affected by albinism; lacking pigment.|The researchers studied the genetic basis of albinistic traits in mice. 研究人员研究了小鼠白化性状的遗传基础。|Synonyms: albino, depigmented|Antonyms: pigmented|adjective

albinistic:/ˌælbɪˈnɪstɪk/
|adj.|白化的，缺乏色素的|Lacking normal pigmentation, especially lacking melanin.|The albinistic rabbit stood out against the dark green foliage.  这只白化的兔子在深绿色的叶子上很显眼。|Synonyms:  leukistic, achromic|Antonyms:  pigmented|adjective
