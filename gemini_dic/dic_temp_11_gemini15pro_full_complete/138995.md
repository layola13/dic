nymphish: /ˈnɪmfɪʃ/ | adj. |  像小仙女的；淘气的；性感的 | Resembling or characteristic of a nymph, especially in being youthful, graceful, or playfully seductive. |  She had a nymphish quality that made her seem younger than her years. 她有一种小仙女般的气质，让她看起来比实际年龄更年轻。 | Example 2:  His poems were filled with nymphish imagery, evoking a sense of innocence and sensuality. 他的诗歌充满了小仙女般的意象，唤起了一种纯真和性感的感觉。| 近义词: elfin, fairylike, playful, mischievous, seductive | 反义词: matronly, mature, serious | 词性: 形容词

词源: From nymph + -ish.  Nymph comes from Latin nympha, from Greek nýmphē.
