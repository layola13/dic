nonintelligence:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns/
n.|非情报; 非智力; 无智力|Lack of intelligence; the state of not being intelligent.  The absence of the ability to learn or understand or to deal with new or trying situations.|His argument demonstrates a profound nonintelligence.|近义词：stupidity, ignorance, unintelligence|反义词：intelligence, intellect, brilliance|词性:名词
