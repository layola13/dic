outproduced:/ˌaʊtprəˈdjuːst/
verb
产量超过，生产过剩
to produce more than someone or something else.
例句：
Factory A outproduced factory B by a large margin last quarter.
A工厂上个季度的产量远超B工厂。

The smaller company surprisingly outproduced its larger rivals.
这家小公司竟然产量超过了更大的竞争对手。

近义词：surpassed, exceeded, outstripped
反义词：underproduced
