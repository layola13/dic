gauzy:/ˈɡɔːzi/ | adj. | 薄纱的；朦胧的；轻薄的 | Thin and translucent, like gauze; also, vague or indistinct. | The room was bathed in a gauzy light. 房间里沐浴着朦胧的光。| The memories were gauzy and indistinct. 记忆模糊不清。 | 近义词: sheer, transparent, filmy, hazy, vague | 反义词: opaque, thick, clear, distinct | 词性: 形容词

词源: From gauze + -y. Ultimately from Gaza, a city in Palestine where a type of fine fabric was made.
