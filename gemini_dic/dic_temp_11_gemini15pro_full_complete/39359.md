conjugately:/ˈkɒndʒʊɡɪtli/ /ˈkɑːndʒʊɡɪtli/| adv.|共轭地，联合地|In mathematics, two elements are conjugately related if they are connected by a conjugacy relation. 在数学中，如果两个元素通过共轭关系连接，则它们是共轭相关的。|The two matrices are conjugately related. 这两个矩阵是共轭相关的。| synonym: jointly, connectedly | antonym: separately, independently |词性:副词
