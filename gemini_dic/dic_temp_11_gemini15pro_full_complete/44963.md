crystaled:/ˈkrɪstəld/ | adj. |结晶的，水晶般的；清晰的，明确的 |  Having the structure and form of a crystal; transparent, clear.  | The solution slowly crystaled, forming beautiful geometric shapes. 溶液慢慢结晶，形成了美丽的几何形状。| The lawyer presented a crystaled argument, leaving no room for doubt. 律师提出了清晰的论点，不容置疑。| 近义词: crystalline, clear, transparent, distinct, lucid | 反义词: amorphous, opaque, unclear, muddled, confused | 词性: 形容词

词源: From crystal + -ed.  Ultimately from Greek *krustallos* "ice," later "rock crystal."
