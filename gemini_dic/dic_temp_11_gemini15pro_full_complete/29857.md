Simple Present:
The noise causes me headaches. 噪音让我头疼。
Stress causes many health problems. 压力会导致很多健康问题。

Present Continuous:
The construction is causing a lot of traffic. 施工造成了很多交通拥堵。
The strong winds are causing the trees to sway. 强风使树木摇摆。

Present Perfect:
The pandemic has caused a lot of disruption.  疫情造成了很多混乱。
His actions have caused a great deal of concern. 他的行为引起了很大的担忧。

Present Perfect Continuous:
The leak has been causing problems for weeks. 泄漏已经造成问题好几个星期了。
The road closures have been causing delays for commuters. 道路封闭给通勤者造成了延误。

Simple Past:
The earthquake caused widespread devastation. 地震造成了大范围的破坏。
A short circuit caused the fire. 短路引发了火灾。

Past Continuous:
The traffic was causing me to be late. 交通堵塞让我迟到了。
The loud music was causing a disturbance. 吵闹的音乐引起了骚乱。

Past Perfect:
The storm had caused significant damage before we arrived.  在我们到达之前，暴风雨已经造成了严重的破坏。
The virus had caused a panic before the vaccine was developed. 在疫苗研制出来之前，病毒已经引起了恐慌。

Past Perfect Continuous:
The dripping faucet had been causing a stain on the sink. 滴水的水龙头一直在水槽上造成污渍。
The construction noise had been causing sleep problems for months. 建筑噪音已经造成好几个月的睡眠问题。

Simple Future:
The new law will cause some controversy. 新法律会引起一些争议。
Climate change will cause more extreme weather events. 气候变化将导致更多极端天气事件。

Future Continuous:
The roadworks will be causing delays next week.  下周道路施工将造成延误。
The celebrations will be causing some disruption to traffic. 庆祝活动将对交通造成一些干扰。

Future Perfect:
By next year, the project will have caused a significant shift in the market. 到明年，该项目将引起市场上的重大转变。
The new policy will have caused a lot of changes by the time it is implemented. 到新政策实施的时候，它将已经引起了很多变化。

Future Perfect Continuous:
By the end of the decade, pollution will have been causing environmental problems for years. 到本世纪末，污染将已经造成多年的环境问题。
By next month, the construction will have been causing noise pollution for six months. 到下个月，施工噪音将已经持续了六个月。


Passive Voice:
The damage was caused by the storm.  损害是由暴风雨造成的。
The fire was caused by a faulty wire. 火灾是由电线故障引起的。

Interrogative Sentences:
What caused the accident?  是什么导致了这起事故？
What is causing the delay? 是什么导致了延误？

Negative Sentences:
The rain didn't cause the flood.  雨水没有造成洪水。
The noise doesn't cause me any problems. 噪音不会给我造成任何问题。
