gormandism: /ˈɡɔːrməndɪzəm/ | n. | 贪吃；暴食；饕餮 | Excessive eating or fondness for eating; gluttony. | He was known for his gormandism, always indulging in extravagant meals. 他以贪吃闻名，总是沉迷于奢侈的饭菜。|  His gormandism led to significant weight gain. 他的暴食导致体重显著增加。 | 近义词: gluttony, voracity, overeating | 反义词: abstemiousness, moderation | 词性: 名词

词源: From French *gourmandise*, from *gourmand* "glutton," of uncertain origin.
