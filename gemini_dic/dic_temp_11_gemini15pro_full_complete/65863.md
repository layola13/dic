evoked:/ɪˈvoʊkt/| adj.|唤起的，引起的|Brought forth or produced, especially by external stimulation.|The photograph evoked memories of her childhood. 这张照片唤起了她童年的记忆。|近义词：elicited, aroused, stimulated, provoked, inspired|反义词：suppressed, quelled, stifled|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin ēvocātus, past participle of ēvocāre (“to call out, summon”), from ex- (“out”) + vocāre (“to call”).
