frugal:/ˈfruːɡəl/| adj.|节俭的，俭朴的|Characterized by or reflecting economy in the expenditure of resources.|She lives a frugal life, rarely indulging in luxuries. 她过着俭朴的生活，很少沉迷于奢侈品。|近义词：economical, thrifty, sparing|反义词：extravagant, lavish, wasteful|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *frugalis*, from *frugi* "useful, thrifty, proper, temperate," perhaps related to *frux* "fruit, profit, value."
