demasculinizes:/diːˈmæskjʊlənaɪzɪz/
verb
使失去男子气概，消除男性化特征
To deprive of masculine characteristics or qualities; to make something less masculine.
The marketing campaign demasculinizes men by portraying them as overly sensitive and emotional. 这场营销活动将男性描绘成过度敏感和情绪化，从而削弱了他们的男子气概。
The new school uniform policy demasculinizes boys by requiring them to wear skirts. 新的校服政策要求男孩穿裙子，从而削弱了他们的男子气概。
Synonyms: emasculate, effeminize, unman
Antonyms: masculinize, virilize
