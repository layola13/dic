armatured:/ˈɑːrmətʃərd/| adj.| 装甲的，有盔甲的，配有电枢的|Equipped with armor or armature; reinforced.  Specifically referring to electrical machinery, having an armature (the rotating part of an electric motor or generator).|The robot was armatured and ready for battle.  这个机器人配备了装甲，准备战斗。|The generator was armatured with a powerful electromagnet. 这台发电机配备了一个强大的电磁铁。|近义词：armored, reinforced, protected|反义词：unprotected, vulnerable|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From armature + -ed.  "Armature" comes from the Latin "armatura," meaning "equipment, armor."
