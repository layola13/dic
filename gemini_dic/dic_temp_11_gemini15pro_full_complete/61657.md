emissivity:/ˌiːmɪˈsɪvɪti/
n.
发射率，辐射率
The ratio of the energy radiated from a material's surface to that radiated from a blackbody (a perfect emitter) at the same temperature and wavelength.  一种材料表面辐射的能量与相同温度和波长的黑体（完美发射体）辐射的能量之比。
The emissivity of polished silver is very low. 抛光银的发射率非常低。
radiation, radiant emittance
reflectivity, absorptivity
noun
