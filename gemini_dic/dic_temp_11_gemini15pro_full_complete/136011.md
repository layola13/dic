nonreaction:/ˌnɒnriˈækʃən/ /ˌnɑːnriˈækʃən/| n. |无反应，不反应|Lack of reaction or response to a stimulus or event.  The absence of a noticeable response to something. |例句：His nonreaction to the news was surprising. 他对这个消息毫无反应，令人惊讶。

例句：The teacher was concerned by the students' nonreaction to the experiment. 老师对学生们对实验的无动于衷感到担忧。|近义词：inaction, indifference, apathy, silence|反义词:reaction, response, action|词性:名词
