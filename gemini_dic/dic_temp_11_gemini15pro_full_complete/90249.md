housebound:/ˈhaʊsbaʊnd/| adj.|居家不出的，被限制在家中的|Confined to one's house, typically by illness or old age.|例句：She became housebound after breaking her hip. 她摔断了髋骨后就只能待在家里了。

例句：My grandmother has been housebound for several years due to her failing health.  由于健康状况恶化，我的祖母已经在家待了好几年了。

近义词：homebound, confined, restricted|反义词:mobile, active|词性:形容词


husband:/ˈhʌzbənd/| n.|丈夫|A married man considered in relation to his spouse.|例句：Her husband is a doctor. 她的丈夫是一位医生。

例句：They've been husband and wife for fifty years. 他们已经是五十年的夫妻了。

近义词：spouse, partner|反义词:wife|词性:名词
