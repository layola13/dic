incoherency:/ˌɪnkəʊˈhɪərənsi/ /ˌɪnkəˈhɪrənsi/| n.|不连贯，语无伦次，无条理|lack of cohesion or connection in thoughts or words; unintelligible speech or writing.|Her incoherency made it difficult to understand what she was trying to say. 她语无伦次，让人难以理解她想表达的意思。|近义词：disjointedness, rambling, inarticulateness, confusion|反义词：coherence, clarity, lucidity|词性:名词

incoherent:/ˌɪnkəʊˈhɪərənt/ /ˌɪnkəˈhɪrənt/| adj.|不连贯的，语无伦次的，无条理的|lacking cohesion or connection in thoughts or words; unintelligible.|He gave an incoherent speech, jumping from one topic to another. 他发表了一篇语无伦次的演讲，从一个话题跳到另一个话题。|近义词：disjointed, rambling, inarticulate, confused|反义词：coherent, clear, lucid|词性:形容词
