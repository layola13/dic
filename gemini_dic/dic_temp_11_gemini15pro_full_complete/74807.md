frencher:/ˈfrɛntʃər/ | n. | 沟渠挖掘机; 开沟机; |A machine for digging trenches, especially for laying pipes or cables. |The frencher dug a neat trench for the new water line.  沟渠挖掘机为新的水管线挖了一条整齐的沟渠。| 近义词: trench digger, ditch digger | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps backfiller as it fills trenches) | noun
