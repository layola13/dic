improficience:/ˌɪmprəˈfɪʃəns/ |n.|  不熟练，不精通 |lack of proficiency or skill; incompetence. |His improficience in the kitchen was evident in the burnt dinner. 他在厨房的不熟练从烧焦的晚餐中可见一斑。|近义词：ineptitude, incompetence, unskillfulness |反义词：proficiency, skill, expertise|词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *improficientia, from improficiens (present participle of improficere "to make no progress," from in- "not" + proficere "to advance, make progress"), from pro- "forward" + facere "to do, make."
