declarer:/dɪˈkleərər/| n.|申报人， déclarateur(法语)|A declarer is the player in a card game, especially bridge, who undertakes to win a specified number of tricks after the dummy hand has been laid down. |The declarer played a risky hand.|近义词: bidder, contractor|反义词: defender|Noun
