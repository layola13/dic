artworks:/ˈɑːrtwɜːrks/
n.
艺术作品，作品
Creative products with aesthetic value.
The museum has a fascinating collection of artworks from the Renaissance period.  这家博物馆收藏了文艺复兴时期令人着迷的艺术作品。
synonyms: creations, pieces, masterpieces, productions
antonyms: junk, trash, rubbish
