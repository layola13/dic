confirmedly:/kənˈfɜːrmɪdli/
adv.
确定地，无疑地
in a way that shows something is definitely true
He confirmedly stated his innocence. 他肯定地声明自己是无辜的。
近义词:definitely, certainly, undoubtedly, assuredly
反义词:doubtfully, questionably
词性:副词

词源: confirmedly 源自形容词 confirmed，加上副词后缀 -ly 构成。Confirmed 又源自动词 confirm，来自拉丁语 confirmare，意为“加强，确定”。
