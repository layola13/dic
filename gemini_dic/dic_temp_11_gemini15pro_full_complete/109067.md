legalizing:/ˈliːɡəlaɪzɪŋ/ |v. (present participle)|使合法化；将…合法化|The government is considering legalizing marijuana. 政府正在考虑将大麻合法化。|Example:  Legalizing same-sex marriage has been a significant social change. 同性婚姻合法化是一项重大的社会变革。

Example:  They are discussing legalizing gambling in the state. 他们正在讨论在本州将赌博合法化。

Synonyms: legitimizing, authorizing, decriminalizing
Antonyms: prohibiting, banning, criminalizing
Word type: Verb (present participle)

Etymology: From legalize + -ing.  "Legalize" comes from the French légaliser, from légal (legal) + -iser (to make).  The root of "legal" is the Latin word "lex," meaning "law."
