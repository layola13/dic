ephod: /ˈɛfɒd/ (UK) /ˈiːfɑːd/ (US)| n. |圣衣, 以弗得|A ceremonial vestment worn by the high priest of the ancient Israelites. It was made of richly embroidered linen and often decorated with gold and precious stones.  |The high priest's ephod was a symbol of his authority. 大祭司的以弗得是他权威的象征。

The museum displayed an intricately woven ephod. 博物馆展示了一件织工精巧的以弗得。|近义词：vestment, robe|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "everyday clothing")|词性:noun
