ganed:/ɡeɪnd/ | v. |  gained (past tense and past participle of "gain") | 获得，赢得，取得；增加；（钟表）走快 | Example: She gained a lot of experience from her internship. 她从实习中获得了丰富的经验。| Example: He gained five pounds over the holidays.  假期里他体重增加了五磅。| 近义词: acquired, obtained, earned, won, achieved, increased | 反义词: lost, forfeited, decreased | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *gaynen, *geinen, from Old North French gaainer, geigner, from Frankish *waidanjan (“to hunt, forage, gain”), from Proto-Germanic *waidjanōną (“to pasture, graze, hunt, forage”), from Proto-Indo-European *weyh₁- (“to strive after”). Cognate with Old High German weidan (“to hunt, graze”), Old English wāþ (“hunting, prey”).
