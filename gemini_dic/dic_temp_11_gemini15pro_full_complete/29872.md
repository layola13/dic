causewaying:/ˈkɔːzweɪɪŋ/
n.
筑堤,铺设堤道,铺设路面
The process of constructing or paving a causeway.
The causewaying of the road improved access to the island.  铺设堤道改善了通往岛屿的交通。
construction, paving, road building
destruction
