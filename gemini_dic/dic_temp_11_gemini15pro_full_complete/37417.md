commercialize: /kəˈmɜːʃəlaɪz/ | v. | 商业化，商品化 | To make something suitable for sale or profitable exploitation. | They commercialized the new technology quickly. 他们迅速将这项新技术商业化了。| synonyms: market, merchandise, monetize | antonyms: decommercialize | verb
