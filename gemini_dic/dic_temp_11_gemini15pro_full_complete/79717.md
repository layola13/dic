glue-like:/ˈɡluːlaɪk/
adjective
胶状的；像胶一样的
Resembling glue in consistency; sticky or adhesive.
The substance had a glue-like consistency.  这种物质具有胶状稠度。
The sap from the tree was glue-like and difficult to remove.  树上的树液像胶水一样，很难去除。
近义词:sticky, adhesive, gummy, viscous
反义词:slippery, non-adhesive, dry
词性:adjective
