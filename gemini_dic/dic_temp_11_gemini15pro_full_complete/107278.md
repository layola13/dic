lakshmi: /ˈlʌkʃmiː/ | n. | 拉克希米，财富女神 |  Lakshmi is the Hindu goddess of wealth, fortune, and prosperity. She is the wife and shakti (energy) of Vishnu, a major god in Hinduism. | Lakshmi is often depicted with four hands, holding lotus flowers, a pot of gold, and bestowing blessings. 拉克希米通常被描绘成有四只手，拿着莲花、一罐金子，并赐予祝福。|  synonyms:  Sri, prosperity, fortune | antonyms: poverty, misfortune | noun

lakshmi: /ˈlʌkʃmiː/ | n. | (l) |  (sometimes initial capital letter) good fortune; prosperity. |  He had the lakshmi to be born into a wealthy family. 他有幸出生在一个富裕的家庭。| synonyms:  luck, serendipity, prosperity | antonyms: misfortune, adversity | noun
