non-urgent:/ˌnɒnˈɜːdʒənt/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈɜːdʒənt/ (UK)| adj.|非紧急的，不紧急的|Not requiring immediate action or attention; not pressing.|例句：The email was marked as non-urgent. 这封电子邮件被标记为非紧急。|近义词：noncritical, non-essential, routine|反义词:urgent, critical, pressing|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From non- + urgent.  "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not." "Urgent" comes from the Latin "urgēns," present participle of "urgēre" meaning "to press, urge."
