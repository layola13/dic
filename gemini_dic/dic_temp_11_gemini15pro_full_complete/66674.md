exode:/ˈek.sə.dəʊd/ (UK), /ˈek.sə.doʊd/ (US)| n. | 出走；大批离去；Exodus（《圣经》中以色列人出埃及记）|A mass departure or emigration.|The Syrian refugee exode continues to strain resources in neighboring countries. 叙利亚难民的大批离去继续使邻国的资源紧张.|近义词：departure, emigration, exodus, migration|反义词：arrival, immigration, influx, return|词性:名词
词源: From French exode, from Ancient Greek ἔξοδος (éxodos, “a way out”), from ἐξ (ex, “out of”) + ὁδός (hodós, “way, road”).
