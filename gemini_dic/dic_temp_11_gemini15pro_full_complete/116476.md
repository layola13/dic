materialism:/məˈtɪəriəlɪzəm/| n. |物质主义；唯物主义|A tendency to consider material possessions and physical comfort as more important than spiritual values.|She was appalled by the crass materialism of modern society. 她对现代社会粗俗的物质主义感到震惊。|Synonyms: consumerism, acquisitiveness, worldliness|Antonyms: spiritualism, idealism|noun

materialism:/məˈtɪəriəlɪzəm/| n. |唯物论|The doctrine that nothing exists except matter and its movements and modifications.|Philosophical materialism rejects the existence of a separate spiritual realm. 哲学唯物主义否认独立精神领域的存在。|Synonyms: physicalism|Antonyms: idealism, dualism|noun
