divalent:/daɪˈveɪlənt/| adj.|二价的；两价的|Having a valence of two; capable of combining with two atoms of hydrogen or other univalent element/radical.|Calcium is a divalent metal. 钙是二价金属。|例句：
The divalent cation magnesium plays a crucial role in many biological processes. 二价阳离子镁在许多生物过程中起着至关重要的作用。

Magnesium ions, being divalent, can bind to multiple negatively charged molecules. 镁离子是二价的，可以与多个带负电荷的分子结合。|近义词：bivalent|反义词: monovalent, trivalent|词性: adjective
