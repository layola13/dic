iniquitous:/ɪˈnɪkwɪtəs/| adj.|邪恶的；不公正的|Morally wrong and unfair.|例句：The iniquitous system of apartheid was finally dismantled.  邪恶的种族隔离制度最终被废除。

例句：The judge denounced the iniquitous treatment of the prisoners. 法官谴责了对囚犯的不公正待遇。

|近义词：wicked, evil, unjust, immoral, sinful|反义词：righteous, just, virtuous, moral|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *iniquus* "uneven, unfair, unjust," from *in-* "not" + *aequus* "equal, level, just."
