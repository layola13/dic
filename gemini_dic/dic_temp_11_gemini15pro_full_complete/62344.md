endenize:/ˈɛndənaɪz/
verb
使本土化; 使地方化; 使适应本地环境
To accustom or adapt to a local environment; to make indigenous.
The company endenized its operations by hiring local staff.  这家公司通过雇佣当地员工使其运营本土化。
The settlers gradually endenized, adopting the customs of the native people.  定居者逐渐适应了当地环境，采用了当地人的习俗。
acclimatize, adapt, naturalize
alienate, denationalize
