benevolent:/bəˈnevələnt/| adj.|仁慈的；慈善的；乐善好施的|Well meaning and kindly.  Characterized by or expressing goodwill or kindly feelings.|例句：He was a benevolent dictator. 他是一位仁慈的独裁者。|例句：A benevolent smile touched his lips.  一抹仁慈的微笑掠过他的嘴角。|近义词：kind, compassionate, charitable, humane, philanthropic|反义词：malevolent, cruel, unkind, merciless|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *benevolens*, present participle of *benevolēre*  'wish well,' from *bene* 'well' + *volēre* 'wish.'
