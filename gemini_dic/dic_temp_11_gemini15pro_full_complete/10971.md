arbitrated: /ˈɑːrbɪtreɪtɪd/ | v. | 仲裁的；裁决的 | decided by a person chosen to settle a disagreement | The dispute was arbitrated by a neutral third party.  这起纠纷是由一个中立的第三方仲裁的。|  近义词: adjudicated, mediated, judged | 反义词: unmediated, unresolved | verb

arbitrate: /ˈɑːrbɪtreɪt/ | v. | 仲裁；公断 | to officially settle an argument or a disagreement between two people or groups |  They agreed to arbitrate their dispute. 他们同意将他们的争议提交仲裁。 | 近义词: mediate, adjudicate, judge | 反义词:  aggravate, escalate | verb

词源: From Late Latin *arbitratus, past participle of arbitrari (“to judge, examine, witness”), from arbiter (“judge, umpire”).
