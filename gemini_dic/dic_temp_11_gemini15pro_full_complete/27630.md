cancrinite:/ˈkæŋkrɪnaɪt/ | n. | 钙霞石 | A complex silicate mineral, typically white, yellow, orange, or pink, found in alkaline igneous rocks. | Cancrinite is often found associated with nepheline. 钙霞石通常与霞石伴生。| Cancrinite can be distinguished from nepheline by its lower hardness. 可以通过较低的硬度将钙霞石与霞石区分开来。 | synonyms: aluminosilicate | antonyms: N/A | noun
