hyperbarbarism:/ˌhaɪpərˈbɑːrbərɪzəm/
|n.|极端粗俗的词语；极端野蛮的行为|A hyperbarbarism is a barbarism formed from another barbarism, or a word or expression considered extremely barbarous.|The misuse of "irregardless" is considered by some to be a hyperbarbarism.|
Synonyms: solecism, impropriety, catachresis
|Antonyms: correctness, propriety
