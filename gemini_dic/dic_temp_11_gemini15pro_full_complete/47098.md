daubster:/ˈdɔːbstər/ | n. | 涂抹者；拙劣的画家 | A person who daubs; a clumsy painter.  | He's no artist, just a daubster. 他不是艺术家，只是一个涂抹匠。| The daubster splashed paint onto the canvas with little skill.  这个拙劣的画家笨拙地把颜料泼在画布上。 | 近义词：dabbler, amateur, incompetent painter | 反义词：artist, expert, master | 词性: noun

词源: From the verb "daub" + "-ster" (a suffix forming agent nouns).  "Daub" comes from Middle English "dauben," from Old French "dauber," ultimately of Germanic origin.
