non-carrier:/nɒnˈkæriər/ /nɑːnˈkæriər/| n. adj.|非携带者，非运营商|A person or organism that does not harbor or transmit a particular disease or genetic trait, or a company that doesn't operate a transportation network.|例句：She is a non-carrier for the cystic fibrosis gene. 她不是囊性纤维化基因的携带者。|近义词：unaffected, healthy (in some contexts)|反义词:carrier|词性:名词/形容词

non-carrier:/nɒnˈkæriər/ /nɑːnˈkæriər/|n.|非运营商|A company that doesn't operate its own transportation network, but instead leases capacity from other carriers.|例句：The non-carrier offered competitive shipping rates. 这家非运营商提供了具有竞争力的运费。|近义词：reseller, agent (in some contexts)|反义词:carrier, operator|词性:名词
