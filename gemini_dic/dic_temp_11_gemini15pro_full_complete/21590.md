bluffy:/ˈblʌfi/ | adj. | 虚张声势的；吓唬人的；风大的 |  Given to or characterized by bluffs or empty boasts; attempting to deceive or intimidate by a false display of confidence or strength; also, exposed to strong winds. | He had a bluff, hearty manner that disguised his nervousness. 他那种虚张声势的热情态度掩盖了他的紧张。| 近义词: boastful, swaggering, blustering, pretentious | 反义词: modest, humble, unassuming | 词性: 形容词

bluffy:/ˈblʌfi/ | n. |  虚张声势的人; 吓唬人的东西 | A person who bluffs; something that bluffs.  (This noun form is less common than the adjective.) | His bluff was quickly called, and he had to admit he had no ace. 他的虚张声势很快就被识破了，他不得不承认自己没有王牌。 | 近义词: braggart, swaggerer | 反义词: N/A (noun form less common, antonyms less applicable) | 词性: 名词
