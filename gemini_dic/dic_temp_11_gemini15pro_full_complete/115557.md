maqui:/ˈmɑːki/| n.|玛基莓|A dark purple berry native to Chile, known for its high antioxidant content.|The maqui berry is becoming increasingly popular as a "superfood." 玛基莓作为一种“超级食品”正变得越来越受欢迎。|Maqui berries are often added to smoothies and juices. 玛基莓经常被添加到冰沙和果汁中。|近义词：berry, acai, elderberry|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源: From Mapudungun *maki
