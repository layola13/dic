eparch:/ˈɛpɑːrk/ | n. |主教；地方行政区；管区|A bishop or other high-ranking clergyman of the Eastern Orthodox Church, often having authority over a province or diocese.  A territory or district under such jurisdiction.| The eparch issued a decree regarding church attendance.  这位主教颁布了关于教堂出席的法令。|Synonyms: bishop, diocesan, metropolitan, prelate | Antonyms: layman, parishioner | noun
