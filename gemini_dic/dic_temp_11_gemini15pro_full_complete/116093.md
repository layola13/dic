martyrize: /ˈmɑːrtəraɪz/ | vt. |折磨；使殉难；使受苦|To cause someone to suffer greatly or to die for their beliefs. |They were martyrized for their faith. 他们因信仰而殉难。| 近义词: persecute, torture, torment | 反义词: protect, comfort | verb

martyrize: /ˈmɑːrtəraɪz/ | vt. |（夸大其词地）声称自己为受害者|To portray oneself as a victim, often exaggerating one's suffering. |He's always martyrizing himself, complaining about how much work he has to do. 他总是把自己描绘成受害者，抱怨他有多少工作要做。| 近义词: dramatize, exaggerate | 反义词: downplay | verb
