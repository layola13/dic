assentors:/əˈsɛntərz/| n.|同意者，赞同者，批准者|Those who agree to or approve of something, especially formally or officially.  Those who give their assent.|The assentors to the contract signed the document. 合同的同意者在文件上签了字。|Synonyms: approvers, concurring parties, ratifiers|Antonyms: dissenters, objectors|noun
