hygrometer:/haɪˈɡrɒmɪtər/ (US) /haɪˈɡrɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |湿度计|An instrument used to measure the humidity, or amount of moisture in the air.|例句：The hygrometer showed that the humidity was very high. 湿度计显示湿度很高。

例句：He used a hygrometer to check the moisture levels in the greenhouse. 他用湿度计检查温室里的湿度水平。|近义词：moisture meter, humidistat|反义词: None|词性: noun

hygroscope:/ˈhaɪɡrəˌskoʊp/| n. |湿度计，吸湿器|An instrument that indicates variations in atmospheric humidity by the absorption of moisture, often using hair or other organic material.|例句：The hygroscope indicated an increase in humidity. 湿度计显示湿度增加。

例句：The antique hygroscope used a human hair to detect changes in moisture. 这台古董湿度计用一根人的头发来检测湿度的变化。|近义词：hygrometer|反义词: None|词性: noun


Etymology:
From French *hygromètre*, from Ancient Greek ὑγρός (*hygrós*, “wet, moist”) + μέτρον (*métron*, “measure”).
