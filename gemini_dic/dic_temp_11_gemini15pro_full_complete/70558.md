filipina:/ˌfɪlɪˈpiːnə/ | n. |菲律宾女性；菲律宾女佣|A woman or girl who is a native or inhabitant of the Philippines.  A Filipina is a female national of the Philippines.  The term can also sometimes carry a connotation of domestic worker, particularly in other Asian countries and the Middle East, which can be considered offensive depending on context.|She is a Filipina working as a nurse in the United States. 她是一位在美国做护士的菲律宾女性。|Synonyms: Filipino woman, Philippine woman|Antonyms: None (gender-specific, no direct opposite)|Noun

filipino:/ˌfɪlɪˈpiːnoʊ/ | n. adj. |菲律宾人（的）；菲律宾语（的）|A native or inhabitant of the Philippines;  relating to the Philippines or its people or language.  Can refer to both male and female people, and also to the language and culture of the Philippines.|He learned some Filipino phrases before his trip. 他在旅行前学了一些菲律宾语短语。|Synonyms: Philippine, Pinoy (informal)|Antonyms: None (nationality/language, no direct opposite)|Noun, Adjective
