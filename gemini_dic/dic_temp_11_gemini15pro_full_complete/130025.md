non-orientable:/ˌnɒnɔːriˈɛntəbəl/ | adj. | 不可定向的 | Not having a property of orientability; unable to be assigned a consistent orientation.  This is a mathematical concept often used in topology and geometry. | A Möbius strip is a classic example of a non-orientable surface.  莫比乌斯环是不可定向表面的一个典型例子。

The Klein bottle is another well-known non-orientable surface. 克莱因瓶是另一个著名的不可定向表面。 | 近义词: one-sided | 反义词: orientable | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + orientable (from orient + -able)
