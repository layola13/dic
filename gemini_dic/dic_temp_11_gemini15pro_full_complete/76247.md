futurologies:/ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɒlədʒiz/ /ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɑːlədʒiz/
noun
未来学；未来主义；对未来的预测
Studies of the future, especially systematic forecasting.
例句：His latest book delves into the futurologies of artificial intelligence.  他最新的书深入探讨了人工智能的未来。

例句：The conference explored various futurologies, from optimistic techno-utopias to dystopian warnings.  这次会议探讨了各种各样的未来预测，从乐观的技术乌托邦到反乌托邦的警告。

近义词：forecasts, predictions, projections, speculations
反义词：past, history, retrospect
