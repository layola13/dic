dropout: /ˈdrɑːpaʊt/ (US) /ˈdrɒpaʊt/ (UK)| n.|辍学; 退学; 中途退学的人|A student who leaves school before graduating.  The act of leaving school before graduating.|例句：The school has a high dropout rate.  这所学校的辍学率很高。

例句：He was a high school dropout. 他是个高中辍学生。|近义词：withdrawal, non-completion|反义词: graduate, completion|词性: noun
