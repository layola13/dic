lifequakes:/ˈlaɪfˌkweɪks/| n. |人生震荡，生活巨变|A significant disruption or upheaval in someone's life, often causing a shift in perspective or priorities.|例句：The loss of her job was a major lifequake that forced her to re-evaluate her career path. 失去工作对她来说是一次人生巨变，迫使她重新评估自己的职业道路。

例句：Experiencing a lifequake can be an opportunity for personal growth and transformation.  经历人生震荡可以成为个人成长和转变的机会。|近义词：upheaval, crisis, turning point, epiphany, sea change|反义词:stability, calm, tranquility|词性:noun
