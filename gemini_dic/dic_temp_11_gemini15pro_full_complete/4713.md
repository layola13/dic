alev:/ˈɑːlɛv/| n.|火焰; 火焰般的事物 (尤指爱情或愤怒);|A sudden outburst of strong emotion, especially love or anger, likened to a flame.|例句：The alev of their passion burned brightly.  他们的激情之火熊熊燃烧。

例句：The alev of revolution swept through the country. 革命的火焰席卷全国。|近义词：flame, blaze, fire, passion, fervor|反义词：calm, peace, tranquility|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Turkish *alev*, from Proto-Turkic *yalɯn* (“flame”). Cognate with Azerbaijani *alov*, Turkmen *alow*, Uzbek *olov*, Uyghur *ئوت* (*ot*), etc.
