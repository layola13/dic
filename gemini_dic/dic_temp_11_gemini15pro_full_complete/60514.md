elaborateness:/ɪˈlæbərɪtnəs/ | n. |  elaborateness, intricacy, complexity | the quality of being elaborate; detailed and complicated in design and planning | The elaborateness of the carving on the furniture was impressive. 家具雕刻的精细程度令人印象深刻。 | 近义词: complexity, intricacy, sophistication, refinement, detail | 反义词: simplicity, plainness, crudeness, artlessness | 词性: noun

Etymology: From elaborate + -ness.  "elaborate" comes from Late Latin elaboratus, past participle of elaborare "to work out," from ex- "out" + laborare "to labor."
