init:/ˈɪnɪt/| n. v. adj.|初始化，初始的|The init process is the parent of all other processes.|The init process starts all other processes on the system.| The init configuration file is essential for proper boot up. |近义词：initialize, initialise, boot, startup|反义词:terminate, end, shutdown|词性: 名词, 动词, 形容词 (主要做名词和动词，形容词较少用)

Etymology: Shortened form of "initialize" or "initialise," ultimately from "initial."
