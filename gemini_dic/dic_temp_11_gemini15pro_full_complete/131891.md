noncontemptibilities:/ˌnɒnkənˌtɛmptɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
noun
不被轻视的品质；不值得轻视的状态
The state or quality of not being contemptible; things or qualities that do not deserve contempt.
His quiet dignity and obvious intelligence spoke of his noncontemptibilities.  他的沉静庄重和明显的智慧表明他不值得轻视。
Near Synonyms:  respectability, merit, worthiness, value
Antonyms:  contemptibility, despicableness, worthlessness, insignificance
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + contemptibility + -es (plural suffix). Contemptibility ultimately derives from Latin "contemnere" (to despise).
