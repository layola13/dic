navigability:/ˌnævɪɡəˈbɪləti/| n. |适航性，可航性|The quality of being navigable; the degree to which a body of water is suitable for navigation. |The river's navigability was improved after the dredging project. 这条河的通航能力在疏浚工程后得到了改善。|The navigability of the Amazon River allows for extensive trade and transportation. 亚马逊河的可航性使得广泛的贸易和运输成为可能。|近义词：navigableness, seaworthiness|反义词：unnavigability, impassability|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin navigabilis, from Latin navigare "to sail, sail over, navigate," from navis "ship" + agere "to drive, lead, act."
