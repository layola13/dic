arrhizous:/əˈraɪzəs/ | adj. | 无根的，没有根的 | Lacking roots; rootless.  |  Epiphytic orchids are arrhizous plants, deriving nutrients from the air and rain.  附生兰花是无根植物，从空气和雨水中获取营养。

| Example: Some parasitic plants are arrhizous, obtaining their nutrients from their host plant.  一些寄生植物是无根的，从寄主植物中获取营养。 | Synonym: rootless | Antonym: rooted | word type: adjective
