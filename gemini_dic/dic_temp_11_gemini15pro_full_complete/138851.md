nursed:/nɜːrst/| v. |护理，照料；怀有；喂奶|cared for someone who is sick or injured, especially a baby or young child.  Breastfed a baby. Harbored (a feeling or thought).|例句：She nursed her sick mother back to health. 她护理她生病的母亲直到康复。

例句：She nursed the baby for six months. 她给婴儿哺乳了六个月。|近义词：tended, cared for, looked after, fostered, cherished, harbored|反义词：neglected, abandoned|词性:动词
