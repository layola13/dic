incisorial:/ɪnˈsaɪzəriəl/
adjective
门齿的；切牙的
Relating to or characteristic of incisors; adapted for cutting.
The rodent's incisorial teeth were remarkably long.  这啮齿动物的门齿长得惊人。
The beaver's incisorial teeth are constantly growing. 海狸的门齿不断生长。
synonyms: cutting, shearing, biting, gnawing
antonyms: grinding, crushing
adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin incisorius (“cutting”), from Latin incidere (“to cut into”), from in- (“into”) + caedere (“to cut”).
