hyped:/haɪpt/ | adj. |兴奋的，激动的；被大肆宣传的|Excited, enthusiastic, or publicized extensively, often artificially or to an excessive degree. |例句：I'm so hyped for the concert tonight! 我今晚的演唱会太兴奋了！

例句：The new phone is really hyped up.  这款新手机被大肆宣传。|近义词：excited, thrilled, enthusiastic, pumped up, promoted |反义词：bored, unenthusiastic, apathetic, underwhelmed|词性:形容词
