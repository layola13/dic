crocoite:/ˈkroʊkoʊˌaɪt/ | n. |铬铅矿 |A bright red mineral consisting of lead chromate, PbCrO4. It is a secondary mineral formed by the oxidation of lead ore deposits. | Crocoite is a highly prized mineral specimen due to its vibrant color. 铬铅矿因其鲜艳的颜色而成为一种备受珍视的矿物标本。 |Synonyms: lead chromate, mineral, ore | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
