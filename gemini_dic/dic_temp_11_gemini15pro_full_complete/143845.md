outplacement:/aʊtˈpleɪsmənt/
noun
再就业安置，转职安置
The process of helping redundant employees find new jobs, often with the assistance of a specialist consultancy.
The company offered outplacement services to all employees affected by the restructuring.  公司向所有受重组影响的员工提供再就业安置服务。
She received outplacement counseling after losing her job. 她失业后接受了转职咨询服务。
近义词:career transition services, job search assistance
反义词: recruitment, hiring
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + placement.  "Out-" signifies removal or separation, while "placement" refers to the act of finding a position or job.
