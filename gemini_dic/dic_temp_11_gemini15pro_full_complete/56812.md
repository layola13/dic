dorism: /ˈdɔːrɪzəm/ | n. | 多利斯主义；多立克式 | A style of classical Greek architecture characterized by the Doric order, known for its simple, sturdy columns.  | The temple was a perfect example of Dorism. 这座寺庙是多利斯主义的完美典范。| 近义词: Doric style, Doric order | 反义词: Ionic style, Corinthian style | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Doric + -ism.  Doric refers to the Dorian Greeks, one of the four major ethnic groups of ancient Greece.
