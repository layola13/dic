footglove: /ˈfʊtɡlʌv/
n.
足套；脚套；足部手套
A close-fitting covering for the foot, typically made of a flexible material like neoprene, often used for warmth, protection, or hygiene.
Divers often wear footgloves to protect their feet from the cold water and sharp rocks. 潜水员经常穿足套来保护他们的脚免受冷水和尖锐岩石的伤害。

She wore footgloves under her fins for extra warmth.  她在脚蹼下穿了足套以获得额外的温暖。

近义词:  footwear,  booties, socks, diving socks
反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly barefoot)
词性: noun
