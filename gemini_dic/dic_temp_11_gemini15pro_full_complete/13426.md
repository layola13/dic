atonally:/eɪˈtoʊnəli/
adverb
无调性地
Without tonality; avoiding traditional harmonies and key relationships.
The composer wrote the piece atonally, creating a dissonant and unsettling effect.  作曲家无调性地创作了这首曲子，营造出一种不和谐和令人不安的效果。
The music shifted atonally, abandoning any sense of key. 音乐无调性地变换，放弃了任何调性。
Synonyms: nontonally, in an atonal manner
Antonyms: tonally, harmonically
