counterdraw:/ˈkaʊntərˌdrɔː/ | v. | 反击；对抗；以牙还牙 | To retaliate or oppose by similar means. |  He counterdrew his opponent's attack with a swift maneuver. 他以一个迅速的策略反击了对手的进攻。

| Example: The company counterdrew the negative publicity with a series of positive advertisements.  该公司以一系列正面广告来反击负面宣传。 | Synonym: retaliate, reciprocate, counterattack | Antonym: submit, yield, concede | Verb


counterdraw:/ˈkaʊntərˌdrɔː/ | n. | 反击；对抗 | A retaliatory action or measure. | The general's counterdraw was swift and decisive. 将军的反击迅速而果断。

| Example: Her counterdraw to his accusations was a detailed account of his own misdeeds. 她对他的指控的反击是详细叙述了他自己的不当行为。| Synonym: retaliation, counterattack, riposte | Antonym: submission, surrender, concession | Noun
