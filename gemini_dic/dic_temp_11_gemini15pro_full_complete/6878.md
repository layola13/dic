ancestral:/ænˈsɛstrəl/| adj.|祖先的，祖传的|Of, relating to, or inherited from an ancestor or ancestors.  Derived from or possessed by one's ancestors.|This house contains ancestral portraits. 这栋房子里挂着祖先的画像。|Synonyms: familial, hereditary, inherited, patrimonial, avital |Antonyms: modern, contemporary, current|adjective

ancestral:/ænˈsɛstrəl/| adj.|祖传的|Belonging to or inherited from one's ancestors.|This ancestral home has been in my family for generations. 这座祖屋在我家已经传了好几代了。|Synonyms: inherited, hereditary, familial |Antonyms: acquired, modern, contemporary|adjective
