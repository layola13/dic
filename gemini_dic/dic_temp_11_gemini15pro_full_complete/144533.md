outvenoming: /ˌaʊtˈvɛnəmɪŋ/
verb
以毒胜过; 注射比…更多的毒液
injecting more venom than; surpassing in the use of venom
The spider outvenomed its prey, quickly paralyzing it.  这只蜘蛛给猎物注入了比它更多的毒液，迅速使其瘫痪。
The scorpion outvenomed its rival in the territorial dispute.  在这场领地争夺战中，蝎子用毒液战胜了它的对手。
近义词: outpoisoning, overpowering
反义词: N/A
词性: verb
