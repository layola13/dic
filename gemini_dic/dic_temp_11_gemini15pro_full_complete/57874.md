dribbler:/ˈdrɪblər/ | n. | 运球者，盘球者 | A person who dribbles a ball, especially in basketball or soccer. |  He's a skilled dribbler and can easily maneuver past defenders. 他是一个技术娴熟的运球者，可以轻松地绕过防守队员。

| 近义词: ball-handler, handler | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

dribbler:/ˈdrɪblər/ | n. | (烹飪)漏勺; (化學)滴管 | a utensil with a perforated bowl used to drain liquid from food. | Use a dribbler to remove excess oil from the fried food. 用漏勺去除油炸食品中多余的油。


| 近义词: strainer, colander | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "dribble" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Dribble" comes from the Middle English "dribilen," frequentative of "drip."
