forewoman:/ˈfɔːrwʊmən/ (US) /ˈfɔːwʊmən/ (UK)| n. |女工头；女陪审团团长|A woman who is in charge of a group of workers, especially in a factory, or the leader of a jury.|The forewoman instructed the team to start the assembly line. 女工头指示团队启动装配线。|The forewoman read the jury's verdict. 女陪审团团长宣读了陪审团的裁决。|Synonyms: supervisor, leader, head, chief, manager (especially for work contexts),  jury foreperson (specifically for jury context)|Antonyms: subordinate, employee, worker (for work contexts)|noun
