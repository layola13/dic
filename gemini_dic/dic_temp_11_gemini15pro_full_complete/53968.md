discreetly:/dɪˈskriːtli/
adjective
谨慎地；小心地；不引人注目地
In a careful and prudent manner, so as not to attract attention or give offense.
She discreetly slipped him a note. 她小心翼翼地递给他一张纸条。
近义词:carefully, cautiously, prudently, subtly, tactfully
反义词:indiscreetly, openly, publicly, carelessly
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin discretus (“separate, distinct”), past participle of discernere (“to separate, distinguish”), from dis- (“apart”) + cernere (“to sift, separate, distinguish”).
