beseek:/bɪˈsiːk/| vt.|恳求；祈求；请求|To ask earnestly for something; to implore.|例句：The refugees beseeched the government for asylum. 难民恳求政府给予庇护。

例句：She beseeched him to spare her life. 她恳求他饶她一命。|近义词：implore, entreat, beg, plead, supplicate|反义词：demand, command|词性:动词
