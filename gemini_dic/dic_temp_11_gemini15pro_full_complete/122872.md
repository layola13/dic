mollinet:/ˈmɒlɪnɛt/ | n. | 莫利内特纱; 莫利内特线 | A soft, loosely woven cotton fabric.  A type of cotton yarn used for this fabric. |  She wore a dress made of delicate mollinet. 她穿着一件精致的莫利内特纱连衣裙。 | The mollinet yarn was too fine for the knitting needles. 这莫利内特线对于编织针来说太细了。 | synonyms: muslin, gauze | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps consider "coarse fabric" or "heavy cloth") | noun
