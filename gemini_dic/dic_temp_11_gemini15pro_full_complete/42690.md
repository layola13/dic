countertendencies:/ˈkaʊntərˌtɛndənsiz/
| n. |逆势；反趋势|Trends or forces that oppose or counteract other tendencies.|The government's economic policies sparked unexpected countertendencies in the market. 政府的经济政策引发了市场上意想不到的反趋势。|Synonyms: counterforces, countervailing forces, opposing trends, reactions|Antonyms: trends, tendencies, prevailing forces|noun


countertendency:/ˈkaʊntərˌtɛndənsi/
| n. |逆势；反趋势|A trend or force that opposes or counteracts another tendency.|There's a countertendency among young people to reject traditional values. 年轻人中有一种反对传统价值观的逆势。|Synonyms: counterforce, countervailing force, opposing trend, reaction|Antonyms: trend, tendency, prevailing force|noun
