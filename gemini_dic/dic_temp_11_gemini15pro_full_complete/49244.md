deleterious:/ˌdɛlɪˈtɪəriəs/
adjective
有害的
causing harm or damage
例句：Smoking has a deleterious effect on your health. 吸烟对你的健康有害。

例句：The chemical is deleterious to the environment. 这种化学物质对环境有害。

近义词：harmful, damaging, injurious, detrimental, noxious, pernicious
反义词：beneficial, advantageous, helpful, salutary
词性：adjective
