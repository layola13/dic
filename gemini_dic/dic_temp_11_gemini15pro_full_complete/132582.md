nondiffused:/ˌnɒndɪˈfjuːzd/
adjective
未扩散的，非弥散的
Not spread or scattered; localized or concentrated.
The chemical was nondiffused, remaining concentrated in a small area.  这种化学物质未扩散，仍然集中在一个小区域。
The pain was nondiffused and focused in his lower back. 疼痛没有扩散，集中在他的下背部。
近义词: localized, concentrated, contained
反义词: diffused, spread, scattered
词性: adjective

词源:
Prefix "non-" (not) + "diffused" (past participle of "diffuse," from Latin diffusus, past participle of diffundere "to pour out, spread").
