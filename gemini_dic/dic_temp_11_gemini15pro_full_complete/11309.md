ardor:/ˈɑːrdər/ (US) /ˈɑːdə(r)/ (UK)| n.|热情，热心，激情|Great ardor for one's work. 对工作的极大热情。|She spoke with ardor about her plans. 她热情洋溢地谈论着她的计划。|近义词：passion, fervor, zeal, enthusiasm|反义词：apathy, indifference, coldness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ardor*, from Latin *ardor* "burning heat, flame; eagerness, passion," from *ardēre* "to burn."
