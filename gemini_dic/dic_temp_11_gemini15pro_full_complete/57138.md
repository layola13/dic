dovetailer:/ˈdʌvˌteɪlər/| n.|榫接者，吻合者|A person or thing that dovetails something, especially one who joins pieces of wood by means of dovetails.  A person or thing that fits together harmoniously with another.|The carpenter was a skilled dovetailer, creating seamless joints.|近义词：joiner, fitter, assembler|反义词:separator, divider|noun
