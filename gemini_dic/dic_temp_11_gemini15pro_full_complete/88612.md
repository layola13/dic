hives:/haɪvz/| n. |荨麻疹，风疹块|An eruption of itchy wheals on the skin, usually caused by an allergic reaction.|例句：The doctor diagnosed her with hives after she ate peanuts. 医生在她吃了花生后诊断她得了荨麻疹。|例句：My skin broke out in hives after I touched poison ivy. 我接触到毒葛后，皮肤上起了荨麻疹。|近义词：urticaria, nettle rash, welts|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider: clear skin)|词性:noun
