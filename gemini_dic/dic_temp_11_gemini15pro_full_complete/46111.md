cylindrical:/səˈlɪndrɪk(ə)l/
adjective
圆柱形的；圆筒状的
Having the form of a cylinder; shaped like a cylinder.
The cake was baked in a cylindrical tin.  这个蛋糕是在一个圆柱形的罐子里烤的。
The building has a cylindrical tower. 这座建筑有一个圆柱形的塔楼。
近义词:columnar, tubular, cylindrical
反义词:spherical, conical, cubical
词性:adjective
