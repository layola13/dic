myohaematin:/ˌmaɪəˈhiːmətɪn/ | n. | 肌红蛋白 | A red protein containing haem, which carries and stores oxygen in muscle cells.  It is structurally similar to a subunit of haemoglobin. | Myoglobin releases its oxygen supply when the muscle is working hard and the demand for oxygen is high. 肌红蛋白在肌肉剧烈运动且氧气需求量高时释放其氧气供应。| The athlete's muscles were rich in myohaematin.  这位运动员的肌肉富含肌红蛋白。 | synonyms: myoglobin | antonyms: N/A | noun
