loving-up:/ˈlʌvɪŋ ʌp/ | phrasal verb | 爱抚；疼爱；奉承；拍马屁 | To show someone a lot of affection, often excessively or insincerely, especially to gain their favor. |  He was loving up the boss, hoping for a promotion. 他一直在拍老板的马屁，希望能升职。|例句：She was loving him up all evening. 她整个晚上都在爱抚他。| 近义词：flattering, fawning, buttering up, sweet-talking | 反义词：criticizing, ignoring, neglecting | 词性:短语动词

loving-up:/ˈlʌvɪŋ ʌp/ | adjective (informal) |  充满爱的; 亲密的 | Characterized by or expressing strong affection; intimate. | They had a loving-up relationship. 他们之间充满了爱，关系很亲密。| 例句：They were having a loving-up weekend at a spa. 他们在水疗中心度过了一个充满爱的周末。 | 近义词: affectionate, intimate, loving | 反义词: cold, distant, unloving | 词性:形容词 (非正式)
