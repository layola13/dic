appropri:/əˈproʊpriˌeɪt/ | v. | 占用，拨出 | to take something for one's own use, typically without the owner's permission or illegally.  | The government appropriated land that was privately owned. 政府征用了私人拥有的土地。 | synonyms: seize, confiscate, commandeer, expropriate | antonyms: return, relinquish, give back | verb

appropri:/əˈproʊpriˌeɪt/ | adj. | 适当的，合适的 | suitable or proper in the circumstances. |  Her dress was appropriate for the occasion. 她的着装适合这个场合。 | synonyms: suitable, proper, fitting, apt, relevant | antonyms: inappropriate, unsuitable, improper, unfit, irrelevant | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin appropriatus, past participle of appropriare (“to make one's own”), from Latin ad (“to”) + proprius (“one's own”).
