hunas:/ˈhuːnəz/ | n. |匈人（复数）|  A nomadic people of uncertain origin who migrated westwards into Europe from Central Asia in the 4th century AD. They were feared for their military prowess and their distinctive appearance. |  The Huns invaded the Roman Empire. 匈人入侵了罗马帝国。|  nomads, barbarians | settled peoples | noun
