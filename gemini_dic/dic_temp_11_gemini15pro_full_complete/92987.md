idiolects:/ˈɪdiəlekts/| n. |个人语言; 个人方言|The way a particular person speaks, including their unique vocabulary, grammar, and pronunciation.  It is the distinctive speech habits of a particular individual.|Her idiolect included a peculiar pronunciation of the letter 'r'. 她个人的语言特点包括对字母“r”的特殊发音。|Synonyms: personal dialect, individual speech pattern|Antonyms: standard language, common dialect|noun
