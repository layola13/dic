as-a-rule:/ˌæz ə ˈruːl/
adverb
通常；一般来说
Generally; typically.
As a rule, I don't eat fast food.  我通常不吃快餐。
I try to avoid sugar, as a rule. 我一般尽量避免吃糖。
近义词:generally, usually, typically, normally, ordinarily
反义词:exceptionally, unusually, rarely, seldom
词性:adverb
