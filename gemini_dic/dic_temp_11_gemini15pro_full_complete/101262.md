invokes:/ɪnˈvoʊks/| vt.|调用，援引，祈求|calls upon (a higher power or authority) for assistance, support, or inspiration; cites or appeals to (someone or something) as an authority for an action or in support of an argument.|The shaman invokes the spirits of the ancestors.  萨满祈求祖先的灵魂。|Synonyms: appeal to, cite, conjure, evoke, implore|Antonyms: disregard, ignore|verb

invokes:/ɪnˈvoʊks/| vt.|引起，激起|causes something to happen or exist|The new law invokes strong opposition. 新法律引起了强烈的反对。|Synonyms: cause, elicit, engender, generate, provoke|Antonyms: prevent, suppress|verb
