hogslop:/ˈhɑːɡslɑːp/ /ˈhɒɡslɒp/| n.|粗劣的文章或作品|Writing or work of poor quality.|The critic dismissed the novel as sentimental hogslop.  评论家认为这部小说是多愁善感的垃圾。|Synonyms: drivel, trash, rubbish|Antonyms: masterpiece, classic|noun
