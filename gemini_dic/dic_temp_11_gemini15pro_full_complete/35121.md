cloudier:/ˈklaʊdiər/| adj.|多云的；阴沉的；模糊的|More cloudy than something else; covered with more clouds.|The sky is cloudier today than it was yesterday. 今天的天空比昨天更阴沉。|Synonyms: overcast, murky, hazy|Antonyms: clearer, brighter, sunnier|adjective
