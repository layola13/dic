lactescent:/lækˈtesənt/
adjective
乳白色的；乳汁状的；产乳的
Resembling milk; milky. Containing or yielding latex.
The plant exudes a lactescent sap when cut.  这种植物被切开时会渗出乳白色的汁液。
The mushroom had lactescent gills.  这个蘑菇有乳白色的菌褶。
synonyms: milky, lacteal, opalescent
antonyms: clear, transparent
adjective
