illusiveness:/ɪˈluːsɪvnəs/
n.
难以捉摸；难以理解；虚幻
the quality of being difficult to find or catch
The illusiveness of happiness makes it all the more precious.  幸福的难以捉摸让它更加珍贵。

近义词:elusiveness, intangibility, impalpability
反义词:tangibility, concreteness
词性:noun
