PHRASE->
gamble on something:  在某事上赌博/冒险 | He gambled on the success of his new business. 他把赌注押在了新事业的成功上。
gamble away something: 赌输某物 | She gambled away her life savings. 她把毕生的积蓄都赌输了。
take a gamble: 冒险一试 |  He decided to take a gamble and invest in the stock market. 他决定冒险一试，投资股市。
gamble with something:  用某物冒险 | He was gambling with his future. 他在拿自己的未来冒险。
a big gamble:  一次大的冒险 |  Investing in that new company was a big gamble. 投资那家新公司是一场豪赌。

词源:  From Old North French *gamboler "to leap, dance, tumble," probably of Germanic origin.  最初的意思是“跳跃、跳舞、翻滚”，可能源于日耳曼语。
