jumpstart:/ˈdʒʌmpstɑːrt/| n. v.|启动，快速启动; 刺激，推动|To jumpstart a car is to start it by connecting its battery to another car's battery with jumper cables.  Figuratively, it means to quickly revive or boost something.|I had to jumpstart my car this morning because the battery was dead. 我今天早上不得不启动我的车，因为电池没电了。|近义词：boost, revive, kickstart, restart|反义词:halt, stop|n. v.
