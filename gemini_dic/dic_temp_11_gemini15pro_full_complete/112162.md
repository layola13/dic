look-see:/ˈlʊk siː/| n.| 侦察，查看，快速查看 |A quick inspection or investigation. | I'm just going to have a quick look-see around the shop. 我只是想去商店里快速看一下。 | 近义词: glance, peek, check, inspection | 反义词: thorough examination | 词性: 名词

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I usually take a look-see before I buy anything. 我通常在买任何东西之前都会先看看。
He takes a look-see at the engine every week. 他每周都会检查一下引擎。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I'm taking a look-see at the damage now. 我现在正在查看损坏情况。
She's taking a look-see at the new house. 她在查看新房子。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I've had a look-see, and everything seems fine. 我已经看过了，一切似乎都很好。
He's taken a look-see at the report. 他已经看过报告了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I've been taking a look-see around the town. 我一直在四处看看这个城镇。
They've been taking a look-see at different options. 他们一直在查看不同的选择。
