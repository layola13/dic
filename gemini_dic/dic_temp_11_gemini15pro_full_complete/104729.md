katon: /ˈkeɪtɒn/ (British English), /ˈkeɪtɑːn/ (American English) | n. | 火遁, 火遁术 |  A fictional technique in the *Naruto* series that allows the user to create and manipulate fire.  Derived from Japanese 火遁 (katon), literally "fire release." | Naruto used Katon to create a massive fireball.  鸣人用火遁制造了一个巨大的火球。 |  Synonyms: Fire Style, Fire Release (in the context of *Naruto*) | Antonyms: Suiton (Water Release) | noun
