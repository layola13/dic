eminent: /ˈɛmɪnənt/ (US) /ˈɛmɪnənt/ (UK)| adj.| 著名的，杰出的，卓越的|Standing above others in rank, merit, or reputation.|例句：An eminent scientist made a groundbreaking discovery. 一位杰出的科学家做出了突破性的发现。|例句：She is an eminent figure in the world of literature. 她在文学界是一位杰出的人物。|近义词：distinguished, renowned, prominent, notable, outstanding|反义词：obscure, unknown, insignificant|词性: adjective

词源:
From Middle English *eminent, from Old French eminent, from Latin ēminent-, ēminēns, present participle of ēminēre (“to stand out, project”), from ex (“out of, from”) + -minēre, related to mons (“mountain”).
