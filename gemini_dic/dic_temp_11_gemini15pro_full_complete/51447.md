dethrones:/dɪˈθrəʊnz/
verb
废黜；罢免；取代
Removes from a position of authority or dominance.
The young pretender dethroned the king. 年轻的觊觎者废黜了国王。
Synonyms: oust, unseat, displace, topple, remove, supplant
Antonyms: enthrone, crown, install, appoint
verb
