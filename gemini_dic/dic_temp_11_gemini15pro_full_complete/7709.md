anomie:/ˈænəmi/ /ˈeɪnəmi/| n.|失范，社会失范状态|A condition of instability resulting from a breakdown of standards and values or from a lack of purpose or ideals.|The rapid social changes led to a state of anomie among the younger generation.|近义词：normlessness, alienation, social disintegration|反义词：social cohesion, social order|词性:noun
词源: From French anomie, from Ancient Greek ἀνομία (anomía, “lawlessness”), from ἀ- (a-, “not, without”) + νόμος (nómos, “law, custom”).
