bequeaths:/bɪˈkwiːðz/ /bɪˈkwiːθs/| v.|遗赠；把…留给；传下|To leave (personal property) to a person or other beneficiary by a will.|She bequeathed her entire estate to her children.|近义词：leave, will, bestow, grant|反义词：inherit, receive|词性:动词

词源: From Old English *becwēthan (unattested), from be- + cwēthan (“to say”). Cognate with Dutch bekwethen (“to bequeath”), German vermachen (“to bequeath”).
