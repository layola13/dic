finger-pointer:/ˈfɪŋɡərˌpɔɪntər/| n.|指责者，告密者|Someone who accuses or blames others, often without taking responsibility themselves.  A person who identifies and publicly assigns blame.| He's such a finger-pointer; he never admits his own mistakes. 他真是个爱指责别人的人，从来不承认自己的错误。|Near Synonyms: accuser, blamer, critic, faultfinder|Opposite:  self-critic,  apologist|noun
