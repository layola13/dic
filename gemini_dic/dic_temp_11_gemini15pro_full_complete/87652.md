六放珊瑚亚纲
subclass of Anthozoa including the sea anemones and stony corals, characterized by six-fold radial symmetry.
Hexactinia includes organisms like sea anemones and hard corals. 六放珊瑚亚纲包括海葵和硬珊瑚等生物。
Hexactinia are a crucial part of the coral reef ecosystem.  六放珊瑚亚纲是珊瑚礁生态系统的重要组成部分。
近义词:Zoantharia (sometimes used interchangeably, but can be broader)

词性:noun
