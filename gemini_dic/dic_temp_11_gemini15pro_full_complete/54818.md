dispersement:/dɪˈspɜːsmənt/ |n.| 分散，散布，支付，分配 | the act of dispersing or spreading something out, or the state of being dispersed; also the payment or distribution of money |The dispersement of seeds by the wind is essential for the plant's survival. 风力传播种子对植物的生存至关重要。| 近义词: distribution, scattering, dissemination, dispersal | 反义词: concentration, gathering, collection | 词性: 名词

disbursement:/dɪsˈbɜːsmənt/ |n.| 支出，支付款 | the act of paying out money | The company's disbursement of funds for research and development has increased this year. 公司今年用于研发方面的资金支出有所增加。| 近义词: expenditure, payment, outlay | 反义词: receipt, income, collection | 词性: 名词


*Note:  While "dispersement" exists, "disbursement" is much more commonly used, especially when referring to payment.  "Dispersement" emphasizes the scattering or spreading aspect, while "disbursement" focuses on the paying out or distributing, particularly of money.*
