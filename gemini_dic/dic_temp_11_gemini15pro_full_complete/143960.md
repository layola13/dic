outquestioning: /ˌaʊtˈkwɛstʃənɪŋ/
|v.| (act of) surpassing in questioning; questioning more thoroughly or persistently than another|超越提问；比另一个人更彻底或持续地提问|The lawyer's outquestioning of the witness revealed inconsistencies in his testimony.|律师对证人的反复提问揭示了他证词中的矛盾之处。|近义词: cross-examining, grilling, interrogating|反义词: acquiescing, agreeing|词性: Verb (present participle)

Etymology:  'out-' (prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing) + 'questioning' (from the verb 'question')
