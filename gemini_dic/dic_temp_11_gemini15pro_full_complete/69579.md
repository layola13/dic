felsitic:/fɛlˈsɪtɪk/| adj.|霏细岩的；长英质的|Relating to or containing felsite, a fine-grained volcanic rock composed mainly of quartz and feldspar.|The geologist identified the sample as felsitic. 地质学家将该样品鉴定为霏细岩的。|The cliff face was composed of felsitic lava flows.  悬崖表面由霏细岩熔岩流构成。|近义词：rhyolitic, aphanitic|反义词：mafic, basaltic|词性:adjective
Etymology: From felsite + -ic.  Felsite from Swedish fältspat, feldspar, from German Feldspat, from Feld (field) + Spat (spar).
