give-back:/ˈɡɪvˌbæk/ | n. | 回馈; 捐赠; 返还; | Something given back, especially a portion of profits given to employees or to the community. | The company's annual give-back to the community includes scholarships for local students.  这家公司每年都会回馈社区，其中包括为当地学生提供奖学金。|  The give-back from the fundraising event was substantial.  这次筹款活动的回馈相当可观。 | synonyms: contribution, donation, restitution, return | antonyms: taking, withholding, confiscation | noun


give back:/ɡɪv bæk/ | v. | 归还；回报；恢复 | To return something to its owner. To return something to its previous state. | Please give back my pen when you're finished.  用完后请把我的笔还给我。 |  Exercise can give you back your strength after an illness.  锻炼可以让你在生病后恢复体力。| synonyms: return, repay, restore | antonyms: keep, retain, take | verb
