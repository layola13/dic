candidate:/ˈkændɪdət/ (US), /ˈkændɪdeɪt/ (UK)| n.|候选人；应试者；求职者|A person who applies for a job or is nominated for election.|例句：She is a strong candidate for the presidency. 她是一位强有力的总统候选人。|例句：He's a candidate for the manager position. 他是经理职位的一位候选人。|近义词：applicant, nominee, contender, aspirant|反义词：incumbent|词性：名词

candidate:/ˈkændɪdət/ (US), /ˈkændɪdeɪt/ (UK)| adj.|合适的；适当的(较少使用)|Suitable or likely to be chosen.|例句：The house seemed a candidate for renovation. 这房子似乎适合翻新。|例句：This area is a candidate site for a new park.  这块区域是适合建新公园的候选地点。|近义词：suitable, appropriate, eligible, likely|反义词：unsuitable, ineligible|词性：形容词
