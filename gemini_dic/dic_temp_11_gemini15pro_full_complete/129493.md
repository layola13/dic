non-approval:/ˌnɒn əˈpruːvəl/ /ˌnɑːn əˈpruːvəl/| n.|不批准，不认可，不同意|Lack of official or formal approval; rejection.  The state of something not having been approved.|The project faced non-approval from the city council.  该项目未获得市议会的批准。|Near synonyms: rejection, disapproval, denial, refusal|Antonyms: approval, acceptance, endorsement|noun
