bespice:/bɪˈspaɪs/| vt.|给…加香料；使…增添趣味|To season or flavor with spices; to enhance or add zest to something.|She bespiced the stew with a blend of exotic herbs. 她用一种异国香草的混合物给炖菜加了香料。|The author bespiced his narrative with witty anecdotes. 作者用诙谐的轶事为他的叙述增添了趣味。|近义词：season, flavor, spice, enhance, enrich|反义词:bland, dull|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + spice.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "to make, to cause to be."  Spice comes from Old French "espice," ultimately from Latin "species."
