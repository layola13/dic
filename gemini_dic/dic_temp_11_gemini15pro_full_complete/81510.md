gravitated:/ˈɡrævɪteɪtɪd/
verb
被吸引；受吸引移动；沉淀
moved toward something; was attracted to something
The conversation gravitated towards politics.  谈话渐渐转向了政治。
The crowd gravitated toward the stage.人群向舞台靠拢。
synonym:  moved, drifted, tended, inclined
antonym: repelled, avoided, shunned
