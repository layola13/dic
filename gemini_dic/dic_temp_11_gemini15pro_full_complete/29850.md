causatively:/ˈkɔːzətɪvli/ | adv. |  起因地，因果地 | In a causative manner; so as to cause something.  | The drug causatively affects brain function.  这种药物会对大脑功能造成影响。|  近义词：directly, effectively | 反义词: indirectly, passively | 词性: 副词

causatively:/ˈkɔːzətɪvli/ | adv. |  起因地，因果地 |  By means of causation; as a cause. | The fire was causatively linked to faulty wiring. 这场火灾的起因与线路故障有关。 |  近义词： consequently, therefore | 反义词: accidentally, coincidentally | 词性: 副词
