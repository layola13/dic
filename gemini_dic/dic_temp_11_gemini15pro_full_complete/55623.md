disworkmanship:/dɪsˈwɜːkmənʃɪp/
n.
拙劣的工艺; 粗制滥造
poor quality of workmanship; faulty construction.
The disworkmanship of the cabinet was evident in the uneven drawers and poorly aligned doors.  这个橱柜的做工很差，从不平整的抽屉和错位的门就可以看出来。
synonyms: bad workmanship, poor craftsmanship, shoddy work, botched job, faulty construction
antonyms: excellent workmanship, fine craftsmanship, impeccable work, quality construction
noun
