ghosting: /ˈɡoʊstɪŋ/ (US) /ˈɡəʊstɪŋ/ (UK)
n.|鬼隐; 突然失联;|The act of ending a personal relationship with someone by suddenly and without explanation withdrawing from all communication.|例句：He was upset by her ghosting. 他对她的突然失联感到难过。|例句：Ghosting is a common phenomenon in online dating.  鬼隐在网上约会中很常见。|近义词：disappearing, vanishing, ignoring|反义词：communicating, engaging|词性:名词

词源: From the verb "ghost" (meaning to disappear like a ghost) + "-ing" (present participle suffix). 源自动词 "ghost"（意思是像鬼一样消失）+ "-ing"（现在分词后缀）。
