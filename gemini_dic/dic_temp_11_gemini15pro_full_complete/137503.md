nontractabilities:/ˌnɒntræk.təˈbɪl.ɪ.tiz/
n.|难处理性，难驾驭性|The quality or state of being nontractable; resistance to solution or management.  This refers to problems that are computationally very difficult or impossible to solve efficiently. |The nontractabilities of the problem made it impossible to solve in a reasonable timeframe.  这个问题的难处理性使得它不可能在合理的时间内得到解决。|complexity, difficulty, insolubility|tractability, solvability|noun
