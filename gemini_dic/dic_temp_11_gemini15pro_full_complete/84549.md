hammer-down:/ˈhæmər daʊn/| adj.|最终的，确定的，不可更改的；全力以赴的，坚决的|A hammer-down decision is final and cannot be changed.  A hammer-down approach means pursuing something with great determination.|The CEO made a hammer-down decision to close the factory. 首席执行官做出了关闭工厂的最终决定。| The team adopted a hammer-down approach to win the championship.  团队采取了全力以赴的方法来赢得冠军。|近义词：final, conclusive, decisive, determined, resolute|反义词：tentative, provisional, hesitant, uncertain|词性：形容词

hammer-down:/ˈhæmər daʊn/| adv.|全力以赴地，坚决地|To do something hammer-down means to do it with full force and determination.|He went hammer-down into the project, working day and night. 他全力以赴地投入到这个项目中，夜以继日地工作。|She pushed hammer-down on the accelerator and the car sped away. 她用力踩下油门，汽车飞驰而去。|近义词：vigorously, resolutely, determinedly|反义词：hesitantly, tentatively|词性：副词
