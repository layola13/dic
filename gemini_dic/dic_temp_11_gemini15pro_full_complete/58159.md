droumy: /ˈdruːmi/ | adj. | 昏昏欲睡的；催眠的 |  inducing sleepiness; soporific | The droumy afternoon made it hard to concentrate.  昏昏欲睡的下午让人难以集中注意力。| synonyms: sleepy, drowsy, soporific | antonyms: alert, awake, invigorating | adjective


Etymology:  Likely a variant or alteration of "drowsy," with influences from words like "dreamy."  The word "drowsy" comes from Old English "drusian," meaning "to become sluggish or inactive."
