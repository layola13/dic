delegatory:/ˈdɛlɪɡətɔːri/ /ˈdɛlɪɡəˌtɔri/| adj.|委托的；代表的；授权的|Relating to or involving delegation of power or authority.|The president signed a delegatory decree transferring some of his powers to the vice president. 总统签署了一项授权法令，将其部分权力移交给副总统。|Synonyms: representative, authorized, commissioned|Antonyms: personal, direct, retained|adjective

delegatory:/ˈdɛlɪɡətɔːri/ /ˈdɛlɪɡəˌtɔri/| n.|代表；代理人|A person acting as a delegate.|He served as a delegatory for his company at the conference. 他在会议上担任公司的代表。|Synonyms: delegate, representative, agent, envoy|Antonyms: principal|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *delegatorius*, from Latin delegatus (past participle of delegare "to send as a deputy, assign, entrust"), from de- "down, away" + legare "to send with a commission, depute, bequeath."
