misadapt:/ˌmɪsəˈdæpt/ |v. | 误适应，适应不良 |To adapt poorly or inappropriately to a new environment or situation.  To adjust in a way that is not helpful or effective.| The polar bear is misadapted to a warming climate. 北极熊无法适应气候变暖。 | 近义词: maladapt | 反义词: adapt |词性: 动词

misadapt:/ˌmɪsəˈdæpt/ |adj. | 适应不良的 |Poorly or inappropriately adapted to a new environment or situation. | The misadapted penguin struggled to survive in the desert. 这只适应不良的企鹅在沙漠中挣扎求生。 | 近义词: maladapted | 反义词: adapted |词性: 形容词
