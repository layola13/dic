leif:/leɪf/| n. | 叶子; (尤指) 落叶 |From Old Norse *laufi, from Proto-Germanic *laubiz, meaning "leaf." Cognates include German Laub, Dutch loof, and English leaf (though this last is from a different inflection of the same root).|The golden leif fell from the tree. 金色的叶子从树上飘落。|近义词: leaf, foliage |反义词: None |词性: noun
