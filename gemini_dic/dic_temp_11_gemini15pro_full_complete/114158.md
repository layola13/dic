mahl:/mɑːl/ | n. | 磨碎，研磨；磨碎的谷物 | The act of grinding or crushing something into small pieces, especially grain. Also refers to the ground grain itself. |  The mahl was fine and powdery. 磨碎的谷物又细又呈粉末状。 | synonyms: grind, mill, crush, pulverize | antonyms:  assemble, construct, build | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Proto-Germanic *malaną (to grind, crush), from Proto-Indo-European *melh₂- (to crush, grind). Cognates include German mahlen (to grind), Dutch malen (to grind, paint), and Old English melu (meal, flour).
