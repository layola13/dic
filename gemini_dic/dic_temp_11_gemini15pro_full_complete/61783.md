emperors:/ˈɛmpərərz/| n. |皇帝（复数）|The plural form of "emperor," referring to multiple rulers of an empire.  A male sovereign or supreme ruler of an empire.|例句：The emperors of Rome were powerful figures. 罗马的皇帝们都是强大的统治者。

例句：The museum exhibited artifacts from various emperors. 博物馆展出了不同皇帝的文物。|近义词：rulers, monarchs, sovereigns|反义词:subjects, citizens|noun
