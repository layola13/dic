dilate:/daɪˈleɪt/ (US), /daɪˈleɪt/ (UK)| v. |(使)扩大; (使)膨胀; (使)扩张|To make or become wider, larger, or more open.|The pupils of her eyes dilated in the dark.|近义词:expand, widen, enlarge|反义词:contract, constrict, shrink|verb
