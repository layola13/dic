brush-aside:/brʌʃ əˈsaɪd/
verb
漠视，不理会，置之不理
disregard something as unimportant
He brushed aside my concerns about the project. 他对我在项目上的顾虑置之不理。

The CEO brushed aside the criticism and continued with his plans. 首席执行官不理会批评，继续他的计划。

dismiss, ignore, disregard, sweep aside, shrug off
consider, acknowledge, address, heed, pay attention to
verb
