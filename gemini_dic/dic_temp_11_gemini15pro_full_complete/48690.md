defiable:/dɪˈfaɪəbl/
adj.
可反抗的，可违抗的，可蔑视的
Capable of being defied; open to challenge or resistance.
Her defiant attitude made it clear she wouldn't back down. 她不屈不挠的态度表明她不会退缩。
The castle walls, though ancient, still looked defiant. 尽管古老，城堡的城墙看起来仍然坚不可摧。

近义词:resistant, rebellious, challenging
反义词:obedient, submissive, compliant
词性:adjective


词源:
From defy + -able.  Defy comes from Old French defier "renounce faith in, challenge," from Latin diffidare "distrust, despair of," from dis- "away" + fidare "to trust," from fides "faith."
