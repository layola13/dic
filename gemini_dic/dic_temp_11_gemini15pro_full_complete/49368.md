deliquescing:/ˌdelɪˈkwesɪŋ/
adjective
潮解的，溶解的
becoming liquid by absorbing moisture from the air
The deliquescing crystals soon formed a puddle. 潮解的晶体很快就形成了一滩水。
近义词:dissolving, melting, liquefying
反义词:solidifying, hardening, drying
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin deliquescere "to melt away," from de- "down" + liquescere "to become liquid."
