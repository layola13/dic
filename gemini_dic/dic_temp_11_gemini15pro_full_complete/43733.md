creeklet: /ˈkriːklɪt/ | n. | 小溪，溪流 | A small creek; a rivulet. | The creeklet flowed gently through the meadow. 小溪缓缓流过草地。 | 近义词：rivulet, stream, brook | 反义词: river, ocean | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From creek + -let (diminutive suffix).  The word "creek" itself has uncertain origins, possibly from a Scandinavian source.
