desultoriness:/ˌdɛsəlˈtɔːrɪnəs/
n.|散漫，漫无目的，不连贯|lack of a plan, purpose, or enthusiasm|His writing suffered from desultoriness and a lack of focus.  他的写作缺乏重点，显得散漫。|近义词：randomness, aimlessness, incoherence|反义词：purposefulness, coherence, organization|词性:名词
desultory:/ˈdɛsəlˌtɔːri/
adj.|散漫的，漫无目的的，不连贯的|jumping from one thing to another; disconnected|She made a desultory attempt at conversation. 她漫不经心地尝试着与人交谈。|近义词：random, aimless, haphazard, erratic|反义词：systematic, methodical, organized|词性:形容词
