manatees:/ˈmænəˌtiz/ | n. | 海牛 | Large, aquatic mammals that inhabit shallow, coastal waters, rivers, and estuaries. They are herbivores and are known for their gentle nature. |  Manatees are often seen grazing on seagrass in Florida's coastal waters. 海牛经常在佛罗里达州的沿海水域吃海草。| synonyms: sea cows | antonyms: (no direct antonym, could consider terrestrial mammals) | noun
