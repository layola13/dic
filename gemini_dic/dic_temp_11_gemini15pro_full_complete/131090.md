nonbeneficialness:/ˌnɒnbɛnɪˈfɪʃəlnəs/
noun
无益；无用
The nonbeneficialness of the policy was clear after only a year. 这项政策的无益性仅一年后就显而易见了。
The study demonstrated the nonbeneficialness of the treatment. 这项研究证明了这种疗法的无效性。
Near synonyms: uselessness, futility, ineffectiveness
Antonyms: usefulness, benefit, effectiveness
词性: 名词
