mameluke:/ˈmæmlʊk/| n.|马穆鲁克，奴隶兵|A member of a former military caste originally composed of slave soldiers from the Near East who seized control of Egypt and ruled there for centuries.|The Mamelukes ruled Egypt for a significant period. 马穆鲁克统治埃及很长一段时间。|The Mameluke cavalry was renowned for its ferocity. 马穆鲁克骑兵以其凶猛而闻名。|近义词：slave soldier, mercenary|反义词:citizen, freeman|名词
