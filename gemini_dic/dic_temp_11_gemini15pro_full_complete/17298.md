bcc'd:/biː siː siːd/ | v. | 密送；暗送 |  Past participle of "bcc," meaning to send a blind carbon copy of an email or letter. This means the recipient of the bcc is hidden from the other recipients. | I was bcc'd on the email about the new project. 我被密送了关于新项目的电子邮件。 |  copied, included | excluded | verb
