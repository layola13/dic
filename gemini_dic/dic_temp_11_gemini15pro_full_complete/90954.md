hurr:/hɜːr/ (similar to her, but often pronounced with a more forceful or exaggerated tone)| interjection | 咳; (表示轻蔑、不耐烦等); (表示困惑、疑问等)| Used to express a variety of emotions, including scorn, impatience, disbelief, confusion, or questioning.  It is often used online and in informal settings. | Example: "Hurr, you think you're so smart."  咳，你以为你很聪明。

| Example: "Hurr, I don't get it."  咳，我不明白。 | Synonym:  huh, pfft, scoff | Antonym: (No direct antonym as it's an interjection conveying a range of emotions) | interjection
