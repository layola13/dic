lawks:/lɔːks/
interjection
哎呀；我的天哪
An exclamation of surprise, astonishment, or dismay.
Lawks, what a mess! 哎呀，真是一团糟！
Lawks-a-mercy, I've forgotten my keys! 我的天哪，我忘了带钥匙了！
近义词:goodness, heavens, my, oh my, gosh, golly
反义词: (No direct antonym for an interjection of surprise.  Consider words expressing boredom or indifference like "meh" or "whatever".)
词性: interjection
