outcavils:/ˌaʊtˈkævəl/
verb
驳倒；以诡辩胜过
to surpass or defeat in caviling; overcome by superior caviling.
He outcaviled his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中驳倒了对手。
近义词: outargue, refute, confute
反义词: concede, acquiesce
词性: verb

Etymology:
From out- + cavil.  Out- is a prefix meaning "to surpass or exceed," and cavil means "to make petty or frivolous objections."
