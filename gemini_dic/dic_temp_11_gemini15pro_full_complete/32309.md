chiggers:/ˈtʃɪɡərz/| n.|恙螨，沙蚤，秋恙|Small mites (larvae of Trombiculidae) that attach to the skin and cause intense itching.|例句：I got bitten by chiggers while hiking in the woods. 我在树林里徒步旅行时被恙螨咬了。

例句：The chiggers left itchy welts all over my legs. 恙螨在我的腿上留下了很多发痒的红肿。|近义词：harvest mites, redbugs|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源:  Likely from the regional or dialectal pronunciation of "chigoe," from Spanish "niguas," ultimately from Taino.
