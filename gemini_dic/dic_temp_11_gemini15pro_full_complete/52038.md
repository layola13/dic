dialects:/ˈdaɪəlekts/ (US) /ˈdaɪəlekts/ (UK)| n.|方言，土语|A particular form of a language which is peculiar to a specific region or social group.|She spoke a broad Yorkshire dialect. 她操着一口浓重的约克郡方言。|近义词：vernaculars, patois|反义词:standard language|noun
