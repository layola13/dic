hoful:/ˈhəʊpfʊl/| adj.|充满希望的；有希望的|Full of hope; promising success.|She felt hopeful about the future. 她对未来充满希望。|The team is hopeful of winning the championship.  这个团队对赢得冠军充满希望。|近义词:optimistic, promising, positive|反义词:hopeless, pessimistic, despairing|词性:形容词

词源:  From hope + -ful.  The suffix "-ful" means "full of" or "characterized by".  So "hopeful" literally means "full of hope".
