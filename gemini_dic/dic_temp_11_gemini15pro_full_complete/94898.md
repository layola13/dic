in-flight:/ɪn ˈflaɪt/| adj.|飞行中的；在飞行期间的|Happening or available during a flight. |例句：In-flight entertainment kept the passengers occupied.  机上娱乐设施让乘客们有事可做。

例句：In-flight meals are often served on long journeys. 长途旅行中通常会提供机上餐食。|近义词：airborne, mid-flight|反义词：grounded, landed|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in + flight.  "In" indicating location or state and "flight" referring to the act of flying.
