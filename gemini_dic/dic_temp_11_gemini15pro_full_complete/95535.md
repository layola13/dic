inclusivist:/ɪnˈkluːsɪvɪst/| n. adj.|包容主义者，包容的|Someone who believes in or practices inclusivism, a policy that emphasizes inclusion and rejects exclusionary practices based on differences like race, religion, gender, etc.  It emphasizes the importance of diversity and creating a welcoming environment for all.|例句：The inclusivist policies of the school ensured that students from all backgrounds felt welcome. 学校的包容性政策确保了来自各种背景的学生都能感受到欢迎。

例句：She is an inclusivist who believes everyone deserves a seat at the table. 她是一位包容主义者，认为每个人都应该有一席之地。|近义词：tolerant, accepting, liberal, open-minded, progressive|反义词:exclusive, intolerant, prejudiced, bigoted, discriminatory|词性: 名词，形容词
