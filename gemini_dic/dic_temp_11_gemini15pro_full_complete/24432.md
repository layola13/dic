bristle:/ˈbrɪs(ə)l/| n. v.|刚毛，鬃毛；(刷子的)毛；|A short, stiff hair, typically one of those on an animal's skin or a man's face.|The cat's fur bristled with static electricity. 猫的毛因静电竖了起来。|Synonyms: hair, quill, spine, prickle|Antonyms: smooth, soft|noun

bristle:/ˈbrɪs(ə)l/| v.|竖起；发怒；|To react in an angry or offended manner; to stand stiffly erect.|He bristled at the suggestion that he was wrong. 他对认为他错了的建议感到愤怒。|Synonyms: bridle, fume, rage|Antonyms: calm, soothe|verb
