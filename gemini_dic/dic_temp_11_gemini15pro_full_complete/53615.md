discards:/dɪˈskɑːrdz/| n.  |丢弃物，废弃物|Something that is discarded or thrown away.|The factory discards a lot of toxic waste. 这家工厂丢弃了大量的有毒废料。|近义词：rejects, waste, trash, rubbish, debris|反义词: keeps, treasures|noun

discards:/dɪˈskɑːrdz/| v.  |丢弃，抛弃|To throw something away or get rid of it because it is no longer wanted or needed.|She discarded the old newspapers. 她把旧报纸丢掉了。|近义词：jettison, dump, scrap, ditch, reject|反义词: keep, retain, save|verb
