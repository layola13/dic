ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *gorgōnia, from Ancient Greek Γοργώνεια (Gorgóneia), neuter plural of Γοργώνειος (Gorgóneios, “of or pertaining to a Gorgon”), from Γοργώ (Gorgṓ, “Gorgon”).  The corals are so named from their resemblance to the hair of the Gorgons, mythical creatures with snakes for hair.
