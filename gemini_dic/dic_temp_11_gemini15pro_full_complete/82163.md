groove:/ɡruːv/
n.|沟，槽；最佳状态；惯例|a long, narrow cut or depression in a surface; a settled routine or habit; a pronounced enjoyable rhythm in music|The record has a scratch in the groove.  唱片上的沟槽有划痕。|furrow, channel, rut|peak, height|noun
groove:/ɡruːv/
v.|开槽于；形成沟槽；(使)习惯于|to make a groove in something; to settle into a comfortable routine or habit; to enjoy and respond to the rhythm of music|He grooved the wood with a chisel. 他用凿子在木头上刻出槽。|furrow, channel, rut|disrupt, unsettle|verb
