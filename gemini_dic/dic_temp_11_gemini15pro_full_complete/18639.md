benzenoids:/ˈbɛnzənɔɪdz/ | n. | 苯型化合物 |A class of aromatic hydrocarbons based on benzene rings, often fused together.|  Polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons (PAHs) are a group of benzenoids. 多环芳烃是一组苯型化合物。|Synonyms: aromatic hydrocarbons, arenes | Antonyms:  aliphatic hydrocarbons | noun
