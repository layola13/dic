mananan: /ˌmænəˈnænən/
noun
马纳南（爱尔兰和曼岛神话中的海洋之神）
Manannan is a sea deity in Irish and Manx mythology.
Manannan mac Lir is a prominent figure in Irish mythology, often associated with the sea and magic.  马纳南·麦克·利尔是爱尔兰神话中一位杰出的人物，通常与海洋和魔法有关。
He is said to have ruled the Isle of Man. 据说他统治着马恩岛。
近义词: sea god, ocean deity
反义词: (No direct antonym for a mythological figure)
