nimbler:/ˈnɪmblər/| adj.|更灵活的，更敏捷的|Comparative form of nimble: more nimble.  Meaning quicker and lighter in movement or action.|The gymnast was nimbler than her competitors.  这个体操运动员比她的竞争对手更灵活。|Near Synonyms:  more agile, more deft, more adroit|Near Antonyms: clumsier, more awkward|adjective
