ehud:/ˈiːhʊd/| n.|埃胡德|A biblical judge who assassinated Eglon, the king of Moab.  Ehud is known for his left-handedness, which he used to his advantage in the assassination.  He is considered a hero in Jewish tradition.|例句：Ehud is a significant figure in the Book of Judges. 埃胡德是士师记中一个重要的人物。

例句：The story of Ehud's cunning plan is often recounted. 埃胡德的巧妙计划的故事经常被讲述。|近义词：judge, deliverer, assassin|反义词:king, ruler|noun
