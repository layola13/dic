laugh-out-loud:/ˌlæf aʊt ˈlaʊd/ /ˌlɑːf aʊt ˈlaʊd/ | adj.  interj. | 大声笑的；非常滑稽的 | Used to describe something extremely funny, causing uproarious laughter.  Can also be used as an interjection expressing such laughter. |  The comedian's jokes were laugh-out-loud funny. 这位喜剧演员的笑话非常滑稽。 | Synonyms: hilarious, uproarious, side-splitting | Antonyms: serious, somber | adjective, interjection

laugh-out-loud:/ˌlæf aʊt ˈlaʊd/ /ˌlɑːf aʊt ˈlaʊd/ |  interj. | 大声地笑 | An exclamation used when something is very funny. | LOL! That video was laugh-out-loud funny!  哈哈！那视频太好笑了！ | Synonyms:  ha ha,  hee hee,  ROFL | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for an interjection of laughter) | interjection
