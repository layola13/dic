exanthematous:/ˌɛksænˈθɛmətəs/ | adj. | 发疹的；出疹性的 | Relating to or characterized by an exanthem; eruptive. |  The child presented with an exanthematous rash.  这个孩子出现了发疹性皮疹。 | exanthematic, eruptive | non-eruptive | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exanthematicus, from Greek exanthema (“a breaking out; eruption; efflorescence”), from ex- (“out of”) + anthein (“to blossom; to bloom”).
