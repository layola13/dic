deprivation:/ˌdeprɪˈveɪʃn/ | n. | 剥夺，丧失，缺乏 |  The state of being deprived of something, especially something considered essential for well-being. |  The prolonged sleep deprivation had a negative impact on her health. 长时间的睡眠剥夺对她的健康产生了负面影响。 |  loss, absence, poverty, hardship |  provision, abundance, wealth, privilege | noun

deprive:/dɪˈpraɪv/ | vt. | 剥夺，使丧失 | To take something away from someone or something; to prevent someone or something from having or using something. | The city was deprived of electricity during the storm.  暴风雨期间，这座城市断电了。| dispossess, strip, rob, bereave | provide, grant, endow, enrich | verb
