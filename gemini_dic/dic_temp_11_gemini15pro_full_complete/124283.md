mothballing:/ˈmɒθˌbɔːlɪŋ/ (US) /ˈmɔːθˌbɔːlɪŋ/| n. |封存，搁置|The act of putting something into storage or preserving it for later use, often referring to equipment, plans, or projects.  It implies a temporary suspension rather than permanent abandonment.| The government is mothballing the power plant. 政府正在封存这家发电厂。|synonyms: preservation, storage, suspension, deactivation|antonyms: reactivation, recommissioning, restoration|noun


mothball:/ˈmɒθˌbɔːl/ (US) /ˈmɔːθˌbɔːl/| vt. |封存，搁置|To put something into storage or preserve it for later use, often referring to equipment, plans, or projects. It implies a temporary suspension rather than permanent abandonment.|They decided to mothball the project due to lack of funding. 由于缺乏资金，他们决定搁置该项目。|synonyms: preserve, store, suspend, deactivate|antonyms: reactivate, recommission, restore|verb
