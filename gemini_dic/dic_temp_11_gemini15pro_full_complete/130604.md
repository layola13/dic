nonadeptnesses:/ˌnɒnəˈdɛptnəsəz/
noun

lack of skill or proficiency; the state of being nonadept.
His nonadeptness in cooking was evident in the burnt meal. 他做饭的不熟练从烧焦的饭菜中可见一斑。
nonadeptness: inexperience, incompetence, unskillfulness
