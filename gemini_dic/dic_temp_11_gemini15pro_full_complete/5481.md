alongshore:/əˈlɔːŋʃɔːr/ /əˈlɑːŋʃɔːr/
adjective|沿岸的，海岸线的|situated or moving along the shore.|例句：The alongshore current carried the debris down the beach. 沿岸流把碎片带到了海滩下游。|近义词：coastal, littoral, shoreline|反义词：offshore, inland|词性:adjective
alongshore:/əˈlɔːŋʃɔːr/ /əˈlɑːŋʃɔːr/
adverb|沿着海岸|along or near the shore.|例句：They sailed alongshore for several miles. 他们沿着海岸航行了好几英里。|近义词：coastwise, coastally|反义词：offshore, inland|词性:adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From a- + long + shore.  "A-" in this context means "on" or "in". "Long" refers to following the length of something. "Shore" refers to the land bordering a body of water.
