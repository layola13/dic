baize:/beɪz/| n.|台球桌呢; 厚羊毛毯; 绿色台呢|A thick, usually green, woolen cloth used to cover billiard tables and card tables. It can also refer to a similar cloth used for other purposes.|The baize on the billiard table was worn and faded. 台球桌上的台呢已经磨损褪色了。|The magician produced a rabbit from under a piece of baize. 魔术师从一块台呢下面变出了一只兔子。|近义词:felt, cloth|反义词:None|词性:noun
