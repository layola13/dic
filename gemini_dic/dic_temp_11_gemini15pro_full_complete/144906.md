over-speeds:/ˌoʊvərˈspiːdz/| v. |超速行驶；过度加速；|The car over-speeds on the highway. 这辆车在高速公路上超速行驶。|近义词：exceeds the speed limit, drives too fast|反义词：drives slowly, under-speeds|词性:动词
over-speeds:/ˌoʊvərˈspiːdz/| n. |超速行为；过度加速|Excessive over-speeds can lead to accidents. 过度超速会导致事故。|近义词：speeding, excessive speed|反义词：slow driving, moderate speed|词性:名词


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning excessive) + "speed" (verb meaning to move quickly).  From Old English "spēd" meaning success, prosperity, then quickness, haste.
