deterrence:/dɪˈtɜːrəns/ | n. | 威慑；制止；挽留 | the action or process of deterring someone from doing something |  The country hopes its nuclear arsenal will act as a deterrence. 这个国家希望其核武库能起到威慑作用。 | synonyms: dissuasion, discouragement, prevention, inhibition | antonyms: encouragement, incitement, inducement | noun

deter:/dɪˈtɜːr/ | v. |  威慑；吓住；阻止 | discourage (someone) from doing something by instilling doubt or fear of the consequences. | The heavy fines should deter motorists from speeding.  巨额罚款应该能阻止驾车者超速行驶。 | synonyms: discourage, dissuade, prevent, stop, prohibit | antonyms: encourage, incite, induce | verb
