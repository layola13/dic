navik: /ˈnævɪk/ | n. | 领航员，航海家 | A person who navigates a ship or aircraft, especially a professional one. |  Example 1: The navik skillfully guided the ship through the treacherous waters. 领航员熟练地引导船只穿过危险的水域。

Example 2:  The experienced navik used the stars to determine their position. 经验丰富的航海家利用星星来确定他们的位置。 | Synonyms: navigator, pilot, helmsman, steersman | Antonyms: passenger | Noun
