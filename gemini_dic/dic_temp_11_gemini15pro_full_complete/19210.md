beyond-question:/biˈjɑːnd ˈkwɛstʃən/
adjective
无可置疑的；毋庸置疑的
Without any doubt; certainly.
His honesty is beyond question. 他的诚实是毋庸置疑的。
Synonyms:  indubitable, undeniable, unquestionable, indisputable, incontrovertible
Antonyms:  doubtful, questionable, debatable, disputable
词性: adjective


beyond-question:/biˈjɑːnd ˈkwɛstʃən/
adverb
无疑地；确实地
Without any doubt; certainly.
He is, beyond question, the best candidate for the job.  他无疑是这项工作的最佳人选。
Synonyms: undoubtedly, undeniably, unquestionably, indisputably, incontrovertibly
Antonyms: doubtfully, questionably, debatably, disputably
词性: adverb
