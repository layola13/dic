daub:/dɔːb/| v. n.|涂抹，涂料|To coat or smear (a surface) with a soft adhesive substance such as plaster, mud, or paint.  Also, a lump or smear of such a substance.|He daubed the wall with mud. 他用泥巴涂抹墙壁。|The child daubed paint on the paper. 孩子在纸上涂抹颜料。|近义词：smear, plaster, coat, cover|反义词:clean, wipe|词性:动词,名词
daub:/dɔːb/| n.|涂抹，涂鸦|A crude or inartistic painting.|The painting was just a daub, not a masterpiece. 这幅画只是一幅涂鸦，而不是杰作。|His early daubs showed little promise. 他早期的涂鸦作品没有什么前途。|近义词：smear, blotch, smudge|反义词:masterpiece, artwork|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *dauber* (meaning "to plaster"), probably of Germanic origin. Related to the Old High German *tūban* (meaning "to dip, immerse").
