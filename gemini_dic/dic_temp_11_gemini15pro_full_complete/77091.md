garron:/ˈɡærən/| n.|矮种马，小马|A small horse, especially one used for carrying loads.  A garron is a strong, sturdy type of horse, often found in mountainous or rugged terrain.  They are known for their endurance and surefootedness.|The crofter used his garron to carry peat down from the hills.|近义词：pony, nag, cob|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "warhorse" for a contrast in purpose and size)|noun
