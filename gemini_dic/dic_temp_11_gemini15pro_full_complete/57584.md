dramaturgist:/ˈdræmətɜːrdʒɪst/ /ˈdrɑːmətɜːrdʒɪst/| n. | 剧作家；戏剧顾问；戏剧文学顾问 | A dramaturgist is a literary editor on the staff of a theater who consults with authors and edits texts. | The dramaturgist helped the playwright develop the characters and storyline.  剧作家帮助剧作家发展人物和故事情节。 | Example: The dramaturgist's insightful notes helped to improve the pacing of the play. 戏剧顾问富有洞察力的笔记帮助改善了戏剧的节奏。| synonyms:  literary advisor, dramatic adviser, script editor | antonyms: None | Noun
