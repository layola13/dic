leis:/leɪz/ | n. | 花环; (尤指夏威夷的)花环 | A garland or wreath of flowers, leaves, or other material, especially one given as a symbol of welcome or affection in Hawaii. |  She was presented with a beautiful lei upon arriving in Honolulu. 她抵达檀香山时，获赠了一个美丽的花环。 | garland, wreath, chaplet |  (No direct antonym) | noun
