nondegenerative:/ˌnɒndɪˈdʒɛnərətɪv/
adjective
非退化的，非变性的
Not degenerating; not becoming progressively worse or less functional.
The patient was diagnosed with a nondegenerative form of arthritis.  病人被诊断出患有非退行性关节炎。
The study focused on nondegenerative diseases.  这项研究的重点是非退行性疾病。
近义词: stable, progressive (in some contexts, referring to a lack of degeneration)
反义词: degenerative, deteriorating
词性: adjective
