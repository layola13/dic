fledged:/flɛdʒd/
adjective
羽翼丰满的；成熟的
Having developed wing feathers large enough for flight. Also used figuratively to mean fully developed or mature.
The fledged bird left the nest. 这只羽翼丰满的小鸟离开了巢穴。
The company is now a fledged enterprise.  这家公司现在是一家成熟的企业。
近义词:full-fledged, mature, developed, grown
反义词:immature, undeveloped, nascent
词性:adjective


fledged:/flɛdʒd/
verb
(of a young bird) develop wing feathers that are large enough for flight.
The young eagles had fledged and left the nest.  小鹰们已经羽翼丰满，离开了巢穴。
近义词:mature, develop
反义词: (no direct antonym for the verb form)
词性:verb (past participle used as adjective)


词源: From Middle English *fleggen, from Old English *flycge (“fledged”), from *flugol (“flight, flying”), from Proto-Germanic *flugiją (“flight”), from Proto-Indo-European *plewk- (“to fly”). Cognate with Dutch vlug (“fledge, swift”), German flügge (“fledged”), Swedish flygg (“fledged”).
