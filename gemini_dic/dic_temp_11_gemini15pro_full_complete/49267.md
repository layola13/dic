deliberation:/dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 认真考虑；商议；审议 | Careful consideration or discussion of a subject. | The jury reached its verdict after hours of deliberation. 陪审团经过数小时的审议后作出了裁决。 |  Consideration, discussion, contemplation | Hastiness, impulsiveness | noun

deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/ | adj. | 故意的；蓄意的；慎重的；从容不迫的 | Done consciously and intentionally; careful and unhurried. |  The attack was a deliberate act of terrorism. 这次袭击是一起蓄意的恐怖主义行为。| Intentional, purposeful, conscious, careful, unhurried | Accidental, unintentional, hasty, impulsive | adjective

deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/ | v. | 仔细考虑；商议；讨论 | Engage in long and careful consideration. | The committee deliberated for several hours before reaching a decision. 委员会商议了几个小时才做出决定。 | Consider, discuss, contemplate, ponder | Rush, hasten | verb
