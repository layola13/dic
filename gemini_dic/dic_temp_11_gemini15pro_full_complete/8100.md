anthroposophy:/ˌænθrəˈpɒsəfi/ | n. | 人智学 | A philosophical system or method of spiritual investigation based on the teachings of Rudolf Steiner, emphasizing the spiritual world and seeking knowledge of it through inner development. | Anthroposophy is a complex system of thought. 人智学是一个复杂的思想体系。|  Many schools based on anthroposophy have been established worldwide. 世界各地建立了许多基于人智学的学校。 |  Synonyms: spiritualism, theosophy | Antonyms: materialism, empiricism | Noun
