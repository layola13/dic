irregularly: /ɪˈrɛɡjələrli/ (US), /ɪˈrɛɡjʊləli/ (UK)| adv.|不规则地，无规律地|In a manner that is not regular, consistent, or predictable; not conforming to established rules or patterns.|He eats irregularly, sometimes skipping meals altogether. 他吃饭不规律，有时甚至不吃。|The heartbeat was irregular, causing concern for the doctor. 心跳不规律，让医生很担心。|近义词：erratically, unevenly, sporadically, fitfully|反义词: regularly, evenly, consistently, methodically|词性: 副词

词源: From irregular + -ly. Irregular comes from Late Middle English: from Old French irregulier, from medieval Latin irregularis, from in- ‘not’ + regularis (see regular).
