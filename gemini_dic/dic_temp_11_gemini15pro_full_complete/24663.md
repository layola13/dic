bromide:/ˈbrəʊmaɪd/ (UK) /ˈbroʊmaɪd/ (US)| n.|溴化物；镇静剂；陈词滥调|A binary compound of bromine with another element or group, especially a salt containing the anion Br⁻. Also, a sedative drug containing a bromide. Figuratively, a platitude or cliché.|例句：He took a bromide to calm his nerves. 他服用了溴化物来镇定神经。

例句：His speech was full of bromides about the importance of hard work. 他的演讲充满了关于努力工作重要性的陈词滥调。|近义词：platitude, cliché, commonplace, banality|反义词：originality, novelty|词性:noun
