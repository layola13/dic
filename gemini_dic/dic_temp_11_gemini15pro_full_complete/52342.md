dictate:/ˈdɪkteɪt/ (US), /dɪkˈteɪt/ (UK)| v. n.|口述；命令；规定|to say words aloud for someone to write down; to give orders with total authority; to control or influence something|The doctor dictated a letter to his secretary. 医生向他的秘书口述了一封信。|近义词：order, command, prescribe, impose|反义词：obey, follow, comply|verb

dictate:/ˈdɪkteɪt/ (US), /dɪkˈteɪt/ (UK)| n.|命令；规定；要求|an order or principle that must be obeyed|The dictates of fashion. 时尚的 dictates。|近义词：order, command, decree|反义词：request, suggestion|noun
