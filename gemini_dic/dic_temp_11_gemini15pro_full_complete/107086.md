ladened:/ˈleɪdn̩d/ | adj. | 负载的，装满的 |  Carrying a heavy load; burdened.  | The ladened truck struggled up the hill.  装满货物的卡车艰难地爬上山坡。 | The ship, ladened with goods, set sail for the new world.  满载货物的船启航前往新大陆。 | 近义词: loaded, burdened, weighed down | 反义词: empty, unladen, unloaded | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the verb "laden," which is the past participle of "lade" (to load).  "Lade" comes from Old English "hladan," related to Old Norse "hlaða" and Dutch "laden."
