antiliberal:/ˌænˌtiˈlɪbərəl/ | adj. n. | 反自由主义的；反自由主义者 | Opposed to liberal principles or policies.  Characterized by opposition to liberalism. | The commentator was accused of making antiliberal remarks. 评论员被指责发表了反自由主义的言论. |  The rise of antiliberal sentiment is a cause for concern. 反自由主义情绪的抬头令人担忧. |近义词:illiberal, conservative, reactionary, right-wing |反义词: liberal, progressive, left-wing | 词性: 形容词, 名词

anti- + liberal
anti-: prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to"
liberal: from Latin liberalis "of freedom, pertaining to or befitting a free person," from liber "free"
