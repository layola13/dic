misgoverns:/ˌmɪsˈɡʌvərnz/
verb
治理不善，管理不当
To govern badly or wrongly.
The king misgoverns his kingdom, leading to widespread poverty. 国王对他的王国治理不善，导致普遍的贫困。
The CEO misgoverned the company and it went bankrupt. 首席执行官对公司管理不当，导致公司破产。
Synonyms: mismanage, misrule, mishandle
Antonyms: govern well, administer properly, manage effectively
词性: 动词
