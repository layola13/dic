juggling:/ˈdʒʌɡlɪŋ/ | n. | 杂耍；（同时做很多事情的）忙乱 | the art or skill of keeping several objects in motion in the air at the same time by repeatedly throwing and catching them, typically for entertainment. | He learned juggling when he was a child. 他小时候学会了杂耍。 | synonyms:  manipulation, sleight of hand | antonyms: idleness, inactivity | noun

juggling:/ˈdʒʌɡlɪŋ/ | adj. | （同时应付很多事情）忙乱的 | trying to do several things at the same time | She's juggling a full-time job and family responsibilities. 她既要工作又要照顾家庭，忙得不可开交。 | synonyms:  multitasking, balancing | antonyms: focused, single-tasking | adjective
