compassionateness:/kəmˈpæʃənɪtnəs/
n.
同情；慈悲；恻隐之心
The quality of being compassionate; kindness and concern for others.
Her compassionateness led her to volunteer at the homeless shelter. 她的慈悲心驱使她到流浪汉收容所做志愿者。
Synonyms:  mercy, empathy, kindness, humanity, tenderness, sympathy
Antonyms:  cruelty, ruthlessness, indifference, callousness, heartlessness
noun


compassionate:/kəmˈpæʃənɪt/
adj.
富有同情心的；表示同情的
Feeling or showing sympathy and concern for others.
He was a compassionate man who always tried to help those in need. 他是一个富有同情心的人，总是尽力帮助那些需要帮助的人。
Synonyms:  sympathetic, caring, kind, humane, tender, understanding
Antonyms:  cruel, ruthless, indifferent, callous, heartless
adjective
