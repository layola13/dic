counterpetitions:/ˌkaʊntər.pəˈtɪʃ.ənz/ | n. | 反诉，抗辩，对立的请愿 | A counterpetition is a petition that is made in response to another petition, often opposing it. |  The residents filed counterpetitions against the proposed development project. 居民们提交了反对拟议开发项目的反诉状。

The group launched a counterpetition demanding a reversal of the council's decision. 该组织发起了一项反诉，要求撤销理事会的决定。| 近义词： counterclaims, objections, appeals | 反义词: petitions, supports | 词性: 名词
