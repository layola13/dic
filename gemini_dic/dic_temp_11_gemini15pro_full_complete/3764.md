agitated:/ˈædʒɪteɪtɪd/| adj.|激动的，焦虑的，不安的|Feeling or appearing troubled and nervous.|例句：She was visibly agitated by the news. 她听到这个消息后明显很激动。|例句：The agitated crowd waited impatiently for the concert to begin.  焦躁不安的人群不耐烦地等待着音乐会的开始。|近义词：disturbed, upset, anxious, restless, nervous|反义词：calm, composed, tranquil, serene|词性:形容词
agitate:/ˈædʒɪteɪt/| v.|搅动，煽动，使…激动|To stir up; disturb.|例句：The washing machine agitates the clothes. 洗衣机搅动着衣服。|例句：His inflammatory remarks agitated the crowd. 他的煽动性言论激怒了人群。|近义词：stir, provoke, rouse, inflame|反义词：calm, soothe, pacify|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *agitatus*, past participle of *agitare* "to set in motion, drive, urge forward, rouse," frequentative of *agere* "to do, drive, act."
