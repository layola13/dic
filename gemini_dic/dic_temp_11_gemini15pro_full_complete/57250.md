downlink:/ˈdaʊnlɪŋk/| n. |下行链路|A communications link that transmits signals from a satellite or other spacecraft to a ground station.  It also refers to the act of transmitting such signals.|The satellite's downlink provided real-time data to the scientists. 卫星的下行链路向科学家们提供了实时数据。|近义词：downstream transmission|反义词: uplink|词性:名词

downlinks:/ˈdaʊnlɪŋks/| n. |下行链路（复数）|Multiple communications links that transmit signals from a satellite or other spacecraft to ground stations. It also refers to the act of transmitting such signals through multiple links.|The multiple downlinks ensured redundancy in case one failed. 多个下行链路确保了在其中一个出现故障时的冗余。|近义词：downstream transmissions|反义词: uplinks|词性:名词
