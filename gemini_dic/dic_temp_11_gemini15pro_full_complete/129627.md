non-continental:/ˌnɒnˌkɒntɪˈnɛntəl/ | adj. | 非大陆性的 | Not related to or characteristic of a continent; especially, not belonging to or originating on the main landmass of a continent. | The islands have a non-continental climate due to their isolated location. 这些岛屿由于地理位置孤立，具有非大陆性气候。| The non-continental flora and fauna of the islands are unique. 这些岛屿的非大陆动植物群非常独特。| insular, oceanic | continental | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): From Latin *non, meaning "not".
continental (adjective): From Late Middle English, from Old French continental, from Medieval Latin continentalis, from continens (continent).
