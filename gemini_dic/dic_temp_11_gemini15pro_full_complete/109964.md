liberation:/ˌlɪbəˈreɪʃn/| n.|解放，释放|The act of setting someone or something free from imprisonment, slavery, or oppression; the achievement of equal rights or status by a group or individual.|The liberation of France took place in 1944. 法国于1944年获得解放。|近义词：freedom, release, emancipation, deliverance|反义词: oppression, captivity, enslavement|词性:noun

liberate:/ˈlɪbəreɪt/| v.|解放，释放|Set someone free from imprisonment, slavery, or oppression; free (a country, city, etc.) from enemy occupation.|The army liberated the prisoners of war.  军队解放了战俘。|近义词：free, release, emancipate, deliver|反义词: imprison, enslave, oppress|词性:verb
