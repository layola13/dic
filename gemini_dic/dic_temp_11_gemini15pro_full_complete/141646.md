optimist:/ˈɒptɪmɪst/ (UK) /ˈɑːptɪmɪst/ (US)| n.|乐观主义者；乐观的人|A person who tends to be hopeful and confident about the future or the success of something.|例句：She's a born optimist and always looks on the bright side. 她天生是个乐观主义者，总是看到事物光明的一面。

例句：He's an optimist about the future of the company. 他对公司的未来很乐观。

近义词：positive thinker, hopeful person|反义词：pessimist|词性：名词

optimistic:/ˌɒptɪˈmɪstɪk/ (UK) /ˌɑːptɪˈmɪstɪk/ (US)| adj.|乐观的；乐观主义的|Feeling or showing hope and confidence about the future or the success of something.|例句：She was optimistic about the outcome of the surgery. 她对手术的结果感到乐观。

例句：He had an optimistic outlook on life. 他对人生持乐观的态度。

近义词：positive, hopeful, sanguine|反义词：pessimistic|词性：形容词

optimistically:/ˌɒptɪˈmɪstɪkli/ (UK) /ˌɑːptɪˈmɪstɪkli/ (US)| adv.|乐观地|In a way that shows hope and confidence about the future or the success of something.|例句：He optimistically predicted a quick victory. 他乐观地预测将很快取得胜利。

例句：She spoke optimistically about the company's prospects. 她乐观地谈到了公司的前景。

近义词：hopefully, positively|反义词：pessimistically|词性：副词
