accustomary:/əˈkʌstəməri/ | adj. | 习惯的，惯常的 | Usual or traditional; in accordance with the customs or practices of a particular place or group of people. | It is customary to remove your shoes before entering a Japanese home.  在进入日本人的家之前脱鞋是一种习惯。| Synonym: usual, traditional, habitual, conventional, routine, normal, regular, typical, standard, established, accepted, common, prevalent, ordinary, everyday, wonted | Antonym: unusual, uncommon, atypical, rare, exceptional, extraordinary, abnormal, irregular, unconventional, unorthodox | 词性: 形容词
