leathering: /ˈleðərɪŋ/ | n. | 鞭打，痛打 | the act of beating someone severely with a leather strap or whip  | He gave the boy a good leathering for stealing the apples. 他因为男孩偷苹果狠狠地揍了他一顿。 |  近义词：beating, flogging, thrashing, whipping | 反义词：praising, rewarding | noun


leathering: /ˈleðərɪŋ/ | v. | 鞭打，痛打 | to beat someone severely with a leather strap or whip | The coach leathered the team for their poor performance. 教练因为队员表现糟糕而痛打了他们。 | 近义词：beat, flog, thrash, whip | 反义词：praise, reward | verb


词源:  From the noun "leather" + "-ing" suffix, indicating the action of using leather (specifically a leather strap or whip) to beat someone.
