corona:/kəˈrəʊnə/ (US), /kəˈrɒnə/ (UK)| n. |日冕，冠状物，光环，电晕，冠状病毒|A circle of light seen around the sun or moon, especially during an eclipse. / A crown or crown-like structure. / A type of virus that can cause respiratory illness.|

corona:/kəˈrəʊnə/ (US), /kəˈrɒnə/ (UK)| n. |冠状病毒|A type of virus that can cause respiratory illness, specifically referring to coronaviruses including SARS-CoV-2, the virus that causes COVID-19.|
例句：The corona virus outbreak began in Wuhan, China. 冠状病毒疫情始于中国武汉。

例句：Scientists are working on a vaccine for the corona virus. 科学家们正在研制冠状病毒疫苗。

近义词：virus|反义词:|词性:noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "corona" meaning "crown, garland, wreath".  The word is ultimately derived from Proto-Indo-European *ḱer- "horn, head".
