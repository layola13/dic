co-author:/ˈkoʊˌɔːθər/ | n. | 合著者，共同作者 | A co-author is someone who writes a book, article, or other work with one or more other people. |  Example: She and her colleague were co-authors on the research paper. 她和她的同事是这篇研究论文的合著者。 | Synonyms: collaborator, joint author | Antonyms: sole author | noun

co-author:/ˈkoʊˌɔːθər/ | v. | 共同撰写 | To co-author means to write something together with one or more other people. | Example: They co-authored a series of articles on climate change. 他们共同撰写了一系列关于气候变化的文章。| Synonyms: collaborate on, jointly author | Antonyms: write alone | verb
