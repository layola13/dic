outbitched:/ˌaʊtˈbɪtʃt/
verb
胜过，比...更泼妇 (Informal)
To surpass someone in malicious or spiteful behavior, typically associated with women.
She outbitched her rival in the argument, leaving her speechless. 她在那场争论中比她的对手更泼辣，让她哑口无言。
Synonyms: outmaneuvered, outsmarted, defeated
Antonyms: lost to, succumbed to
词性: 动词
