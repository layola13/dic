bituminization:/bɪˌtʃuːmənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /bɪˌtuːmənaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
沥青化；沥青处理
The process by which organic matter is transformed into bitumen, a black, viscous substance.
Over millions of years, the organic matter underwent bituminization, forming vast deposits of oil shale.  数百万年来，有机物经历了沥青化过程，形成了大量的油页岩矿床。
The bituminization of plant matter is a key stage in the formation of coal.  植物物质的沥青化是煤炭形成的关键阶段。
synonyms: asphaltation, tarring
antonyms: N/A
noun


bituminize:/bɪˈtʃuːmənaɪz/ /bɪˈtuːmənaɪz/
vt.
用沥青处理；使成沥青状
To treat or impregnate with bitumen.
The road was bituminized to improve its surface. 这条路用沥青进行了处理，以改善其路面。
They bituminized the driveway to make it more durable.  他们在车道上铺了沥青，使其更耐用。
synonyms: asphalt, tar
antonyms: N/A
verb
