doodlesack:/ˈduːdəlsæk/ | n. | 风笛，一种乐器 | A simple wind instrument, somewhat like a bagpipe, made from a skin or bladder with a pipe attached. It is often associated with folk music. |  He played a lively tune on his doodlesack. 他用他的风笛吹奏了一首欢快的曲子。 |  The sound of the doodlesack filled the air. 风笛的声音充满了空气。 | synonyms: bagpipe, pipe | antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a musical instrument) | noun
