nonpresentability:/ˌnɑːnˌprɛzənˈtəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可展示性，无法呈现性
The nonpresentability of the evidence led to the case being dismissed.  证据的不可展示性导致案件被驳回。
The artist struggled with the nonpresentability of abstract emotions.  这位艺术家难以呈现抽象的情感。
例句：The nonpresentability of the data made it difficult to analyze.  数据的不可展示性使其难以分析。
例句：The nonpresentability of his injuries prevented him from attending the ceremony.  他伤势严重，无法出席仪式。
近义词：unrepresentability, incommunicability
反义词：presentability, communicability
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + presentability.  Presentability is derived from present + -ability.  Present ultimately comes from Latin praesentare "to show, present," from prae- "before" + esse "to be."
