oversaturates:/ˌōvərˈsætʃəˌreɪts/
|v.|过饱和，过度饱和|To cause something to become excessively saturated; to supply or fill beyond capacity or to an excessive degree.|The market is oversaturated with cheap products. 市场充满了廉价产品。|saturate, flood, glut, surfeit|desaturate, undersupply|verb
oversaturated:/ˌōvərˈsætʃəˌreɪtɪd/
|adj.|过饱和的，过度饱和的|Excessively saturated; filled beyond capacity.|The oversaturated job market makes it difficult for new graduates to find work. 过度饱和的就业市场使新毕业生很难找到工作。|saturated, glutted, surfeited|unsaturated, undersupplied|adjective
