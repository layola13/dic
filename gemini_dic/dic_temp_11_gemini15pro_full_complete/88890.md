hokiest:/ˈhoʊkiɪst/| adj.|最老套的，最俗气的|Characteristic of or resembling hokum; mawkishly sentimental or contrived.|The movie had the hokiest dialogue I've ever heard.  这部电影的对话是我听过的最老套的。| The plot was so hokie it was almost funny.  这情节太老套了，简直要笑出来了。| 近义词：corny, cheesy, clichéd, trite, banal|反义词：original, fresh, innovative|词性:形容词

词源:
From hokum (n.) "melodramatic stage play or story" (1923), perhaps from a pseudo-Native American word coined in vaudeville.
