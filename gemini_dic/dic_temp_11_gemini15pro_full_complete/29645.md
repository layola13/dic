caterwauler:/ˈkætərˌwɔːlər/| n. | 吵闹的歌手；嚎叫的人/动物（尤指猫）|A person or animal, especially a cat, that makes a loud, harsh, wailing cry. |例句：The caterwauler next door kept us awake all night. 隔壁那个嚎叫的人/动物让我们整夜没睡。

例句：The alley cats were caterwauling all night. 巷子里的野猫整夜都在嚎叫。|近义词：shrieker, screamer, howler|反义词: songbird, melodist |词性: noun
