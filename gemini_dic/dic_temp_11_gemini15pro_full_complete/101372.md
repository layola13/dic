ionization:/ˌaɪənəˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 电离；离子化 | The process by which an atom or molecule acquires a negative or positive charge by gaining or losing electrons, often in conjunction with other chemical changes. |  The ionization of the gas created a plasma.  气体的电离产生了等离子体。|  Ionization of water molecules produces hydrogen and hydroxide ions. 水分子的电离产生氢离子和氢氧根离子。 |  ion forming, charging | deionization, neutralization | noun
