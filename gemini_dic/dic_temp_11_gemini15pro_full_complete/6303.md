amortise:/ˈæmərtaɪz (US), əˈmɔːtaɪz (UK)| v. |摊销，分期偿还|To gradually reduce a debt or cost by making regular payments over a period of time.|The company is amortising the cost of new equipment over five years.  公司正在五年内摊销新设备的成本。|Example:  We are amortising the loan over 25 years. 我们在25年内分期偿还贷款。|Synonyms: depreciate, write off, expensed|Antonyms: appreciate, increase|Verb

amortise:/əˈmɔːtaɪz (UK), ˈæmərˌtaɪz (US)| n. |摊销，分期偿还 |The reduction of a debt or cost through regular payments over a period of time.|The amortisation of the loan will take ten years.  这笔贷款的摊销将需要十年时间。|Example: The amortisation schedule shows how much principal and interest is paid each month. 摊销计划表显示了每个月支付的本金和利息数额。|Synonyms: depreciation, write-off, expensing|Antonyms: appreciation, increase|Noun
