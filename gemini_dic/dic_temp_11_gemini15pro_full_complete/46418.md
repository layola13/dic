dagaa:/ˈdɑːɡɑː/
n.
银鱼科的小鱼;幼鱼
small fish of the silverfish family; young fish
Fishermen cast their nets to catch dagaa in Lake Tanganyika.  坦噶尼喀湖的渔民撒网捕捞银鱼。
The dagaa are a vital food source for the local communities. 银鱼是当地社区的重要食物来源。
synonym:silverfish, minnow, fry
antonym:large fish, adult fish
noun
