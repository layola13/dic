angustura:/ˌæŋɡəˈstʊərə/ | n. |安古斯图拉树皮, 安古斯图拉苦酒|The bark of a South American tree ( *Galipea officinalis*) or a medicinal preparation made from it, formerly used to treat fevers. Also, a kind of aromatic bitters made from this bark and used in cocktails. |An Angostura bitters is a key ingredient in a Pink Gin. 安古斯图拉苦酒是粉红金酒的关键成分。|近义词:bitters|反义词:|noun
