datiscin:/ˈdeɪtɪsɪn/ | n. |达替辛，达梯辛|A glycoside found in the bark and roots of some plants, particularly the horse chestnut. It is used in some traditional medicines.|Datiscin has been studied for its potential anti-inflammatory properties. 达替辛因其潜在的抗炎特性而被研究。|Datiscin can be extracted from the bark of horse chestnut trees. 达替辛可以从七叶树的树皮中提取。|Synonyms: esculin, fraxin|Antonyms: None|Noun
