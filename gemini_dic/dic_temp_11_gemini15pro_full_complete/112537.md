love-seat: /ˈlʌvˌsiːt/ (US), /ˈlʌvsiːt/ (UK)| n. |双人沙发，情侣沙发|A small sofa designed for two people.| We bought a new love-seat for our living room. 我们为客厅买了一张新的双人沙发。| The love-seat is perfect for cuddling up and watching a movie. 这张双人沙发非常适合依偎在一起看电影。|近义词: settee, couch, sofa |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "armchair" as it signifies single seating) |词性: noun

love-seat: 词源来自 love + seat.  "Love" 在这里指的是浪漫的爱情，"seat" 指的是座位。这个词的含义是为两个人设计的亲密座位。
