gingered:/ˈdʒɪndʒərd/| adj.|姜味的；辛辣的；活泼的；兴奋的|Flavored or spiced with ginger; having a pungent taste; lively and spirited.|例句：She enjoyed the gingery taste of the biscuits. 她喜欢饼干的姜味。

例句：The gingery beer was perfect for a hot summer day. 这款姜味啤酒非常适合炎热的夏日。|近义词：spicy, pungent, zesty, lively, spirited|反义词：bland, mild, dull|词性:形容词

词源: From ginger + -ed, meaning "having the qualities of ginger". Ultimately from Sanskrit *śṛṅgavera* meaning "horn-body," referring to the shape of the ginger root.
