kiddier:/ˈkɪdiər/| adj.|更幼稚的，更适合小孩的|More suitable for or appealing to young children than something else; more childish.|This toy is kiddier than that one, with its bright colours and simple design.  这个玩具比那个更幼稚，颜色鲜艳，设计简单。
The movie's kiddier humor fell flat with the teenage audience. 这部电影幼稚的幽默并没有引起青少年观众的共鸣。|近义词：more childish, younger, juvenile|反义词：more mature, more sophisticated, more grown-up|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From kid + -ier (comparative suffix). "Kid" meaning "child" originated in the 1590s, possibly from a Scandinavian source.  The comparative suffix "-ier" is used to form the comparative degree of adjectives.
