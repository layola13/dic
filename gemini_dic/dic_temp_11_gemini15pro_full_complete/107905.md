larrup:/ˈlærəp/| v. n.|鞭打，痛打；责骂，训斥；（非正式）痛饮；一大口（尤指酒精饮料）|To beat or thrash with a whip or strap; to scold or rebuke severely; (informal) to drink heartily; a large draft (especially of an alcoholic beverage).|He larruped the horse for disobeying. 他因为马不听话而鞭打它。|近义词：beat, thrash, flog, whip, chastise, scold, rebuke, guzzle, quaff|反义词：praise, commend|词性:动词, 名词
