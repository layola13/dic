dells:/dɛlz/
n.
小峡谷，山谷，洼地
Small, secluded valleys, often wooded.
We hiked through the shady dells. 我们徒步穿越了阴凉的小峡谷。
近义词: valleys, glens, gorges
反义词: peaks, summits
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *delle*, from Old English *dell* (“valley”), from Proto-Germanic *daliz* (“valley, dale”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰolh₁- (“valley”). Cognate with Scots *dell* (“valley”), Dutch *dal* (“valley”), German *Tal* (“valley”), Icelandic *dalur* (“valley”), Gothic *��������* (*dal*).
