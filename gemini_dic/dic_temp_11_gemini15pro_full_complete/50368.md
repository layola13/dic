depopulation:/ˌdiːˌpɒpjʊˈleɪʃn/ | n. |人口减少，人口稀少|The depopulation of rural areas is a serious problem. 农村地区人口减少是一个严重问题。|例句：The war caused widespread depopulation.  这场战争造成了大范围的人口减少。

例句：The depopulation of the island was due to famine. 这个岛屿的人口减少是由于饥荒造成的。

|近义词：decline, decrease, dwindling, emptying |反义词: population growth, increase, overpopulation |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning 'removal, reversal') + population.  The prefix "de-" comes from Latin and means "down from," "away from," "off," "out of," reversing or removing. "Population" comes from Late Middle English, from Old French populacion, from Latin populatio(n-), from populus ‘people’.
