amerceable:/əˈmɜːrsəbl/
adjective
可罚款的；应受处罚的
Subject to a pecuniary penalty; liable to be fined.
The company was found to be amerceable for violating environmental regulations.  这家公司因违反环境法规而被处以罚款。
The judge declared the defendant amerceable and imposed a heavy fine.  法官宣布被告应受罚款，并处以巨额罚款。
近义词: fineable, punishable
反义词: unpunishable, exempt

词性: adjective
