micaceous:/maɪˈkeɪʃəs/
adjective
云母的；似云母的；含云母的
Resembling mica; containing mica.
The rock is micaceous and glitters in the sunlight. 这块岩石含有云母，在阳光下闪闪发光。
Synonyms: pearly, shimmering, glistening
Antonyms: dull, matte
