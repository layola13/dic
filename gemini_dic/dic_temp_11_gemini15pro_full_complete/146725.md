overhonestnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːnɪstnɪsɪz/
noun
过度诚实
The state or quality of being excessively honest, often to the point of being blunt or tactless.
His overhonestnesses sometimes offended people. 他过分的诚实有时会冒犯别人。
Synonyms:  candor, frankness, sincerity,  bluntness, artlessness
Antonyms: dishonesty, deceptiveness,  tact, diplomacy
词性: 名词
