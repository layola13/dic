four-by-four:/ˌfɔːr baɪ ˈfɔːr/| n. adj.|四轮驱动（车）; 四轮驱动的|A four-by-four vehicle has a system that provides power to all four wheels, giving it greater traction on rough terrain.  This term refers to both the drive system itself and vehicles equipped with it.|I need a four-by-four to get up that steep mountain road. 我需要一辆四轮驱动车才能爬上那条陡峭的山路。|The four-by-four handled the muddy track with ease. 这辆四轮驱动车轻松地应对了泥泞的道路。|近义词：all-wheel drive, 4WD, SUV|反义词: two-wheel drive, 2WD|词性:名词，形容词
