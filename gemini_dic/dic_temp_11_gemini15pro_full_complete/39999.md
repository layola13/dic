consultary:/kənˈsʌltəri/ | adj. | 咨询的，顾问的 |  Relating to consultation or giving advice.  | The company offers consultary services to businesses. 这家公司向企业提供咨询服务。 | Example: He holds a consultary position with the government. 他在政府担任顾问职位。| Synonyms: advisory, consultative | Antonyms: executive, operational | adjective

consultary:/kənˈsʌltəri/ | n. | 顾问职位；咨询机构 | A position or body that provides consultation or advice. |  He works for a consultary that specializes in financial planning. 他在一家专门从事财务规划的咨询公司工作。 | Example: The government established a consultary to address the economic crisis. 政府成立了一个咨询机构来应对经济危机。 | Synonyms: advisory board, consulting firm | Antonyms: executive branch, operating company | noun
