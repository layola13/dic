nitwittedness:/ˌnɪtˈwɪtɪdnəs/| n. |愚蠢，傻气|The state of being a nitwit; stupidity or foolishness.|His nitwittedness led him to believe the obvious scam. 他的愚蠢让他相信了这个明显的骗局。|近义词：stupidity, foolishness, idiocy, silliness|反义词：intelligence, wisdom, cleverness|词性: noun
Etymology: From nitwit + -ed + -ness.  Nitwit itself likely comes from nit, meaning "egg of a louse," figuratively used to refer to a small or insignificant person, + wit, meaning "intelligence."
