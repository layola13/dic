must-see:/ˈmʌst siː/
adjective
必看的；不容错过的
Something that is described as a "must-see" is highly recommended as worth seeing.
The Grand Canyon is a must-see if you're visiting Arizona.  如果你去亚利桑那州，大峡谷是必看的景点。
The new exhibition is a must-see for art lovers.  新展览是艺术爱好者必看的。
Synonyms: essential, unmissable, not-to-be-missed
Antonyms: skippable, avoidable

noun
必看之物；不容错过的事物
A "must-see" can also refer to something that is considered essential viewing.
This documentary is a must-see for anyone interested in history.  这部纪录片是任何对历史感兴趣的人必看的。
The Eiffel Tower is a must-see in Paris.  埃菲尔铁塔是巴黎的必看景点。
Synonyms: highlight, attraction, draw
Antonyms: N/A (Noun form doesn't have a direct antonym)
