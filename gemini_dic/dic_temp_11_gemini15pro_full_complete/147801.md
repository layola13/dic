overproved:/ˌoʊvərˈpruːvd/ | adj. | 发酵过度的 |  (of dough) left to prove for too long, resulting in a coarse texture and poor rise during baking. | The bread was dense and heavy because it was overproved. 面包很硬很重，因为它发酵过度了。| The loaf was flat and didn't rise properly; I think I overproved the dough.  这块面包是扁的，没有发酵好; 我想我让面团发酵过度了。 | over-risen, over-fermented | underproved | adjective
