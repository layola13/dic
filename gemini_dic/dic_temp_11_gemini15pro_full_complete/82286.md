groundwards:/ˈɡraʊndwədz/ | adv. | 向地面；朝向地面 | Towards the ground; in a downward direction. | The apple fell groundwards from the tree. 苹果从树上掉落到地面。|  The kite plummeted groundwards after the string broke.  风筝线断了之后，风筝直坠地面。 | 近义词: downwards, earthwards | 反义词: upwards, skywards | 词性: 副词

词源:  From "ground" + "-wards" (suffix indicating direction).  "Ground" comes from Old English "grund," meaning "bottom, foundation, surface of the earth." "-wards" comes from Old English "-weardes," meaning "towards."
