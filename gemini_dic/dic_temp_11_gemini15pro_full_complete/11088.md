archaistic:/ɑːrˈkeɪɪstɪk/
adjective
古风的，过时的
marked by the characteristics of an earlier period; antiquated.
例句：The author's archaistic writing style made the novel feel like it was written centuries ago. 作者古风的写作风格使这部小说感觉像是几个世纪前写的。

例句：Using "thou" instead of "you" is an archaistic affectation.  用“thou”而不是“you”是一种刻意仿古的矫揉造作。
近义词：antique, antiquated, obsolete, old-fashioned, outdated
反义词：modern, contemporary, current
