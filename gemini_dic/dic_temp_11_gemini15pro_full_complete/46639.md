damneder:/ˈdæmədər/ | adj. | 更该死的，更可恶的 | A non-standard and intensified form of "damned," implying a greater degree of condemnation or negativity. |  "That damneder dog keeps digging up my garden!" 那只更该死的狗一直在挖我的花园！ | 近义词: more damned, more cursed | 反义词: blessed, sacred | 词性:形容词

词源:  Derived from "damned," the past participle of "damn,"  with the comparative suffix "-er." "Damn" ultimately comes from the Latin "damnare" (to condemn).
