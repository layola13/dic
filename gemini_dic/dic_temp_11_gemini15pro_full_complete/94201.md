impercipient:/ˌɪmpərˈsɪpiənt/
adjective
无感觉的；无知觉的；未察觉的
Not perceiving; unperceptive; not aware.
The impercipient driver failed to notice the pedestrian crossing the street.  这个无知觉的司机没有注意到正在过马路的行人。

The company was impercipient to the changing market trends.  该公司对不断变化的市场趋势没有察觉。

synonym: unaware, unobservant, oblivious, insensitive, inattentive
antonym: perceptive, aware, observant, sensitive, attentive
词性: adjective
