judges:/ˈdʒʌdʒɪz/| n.|法官；裁判员；评判员|A judge presides over court proceedings and makes legal decisions. 法官主持法庭程序并作出法律裁决。|The judges awarded her first prize. 评委们授予她一等奖。|synonyms: arbitrator, referee, umpire, adjudicator, assessor|antonyms: defendant, accused|noun

judge:/dʒʌdʒ/| n. vt.|法官；裁判员；评判员；判断；评价|The judge sentenced him to five years in prison. 法官判处他五年监禁。|Don't judge a book by its cover. 不要以貌取人。|synonyms:  assess, evaluate, rate, estimate, appraise, consider, deem|antonyms: misjudge|noun, verb

Etymology: From Old French juge, from Latin iudex (judge, juror), from ius (law, right) + dicere (to say, speak).
