greffier: /ɡrɛfˈjeɪ/ (UK) /ɡrɛˈfjeɪ/ (US) | n. |书记员，记录员| A clerk of court or registrar; someone who keeps records. | The greffier read the charges against the defendant. 书记员宣读了对被告的指控。| Synonyms: clerk, registrar, recorder, scribe | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "defendant" in a legal context, but not semantically opposite) | noun
