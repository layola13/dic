dimerous:/ˈdaɪmərəs/
adjective
二聚的；二基数的
Having two parts or members; specifically (Botany) having two petals, two sepals, two stamens, or two carpels in each whorl.  A flower with two petals in each whorl is described as dimerous.
例句：Dimerous flowers are relatively uncommon. 二基数的花相对少见。

例句：The botanist identified the plant as dimerous due to its two petals.  植物学家根据其两片花瓣将该植物鉴定为二基数的。
近义词:two-parted, bipartite
反义词:polymerous, trimerous, tetramerous
词性:adjective
