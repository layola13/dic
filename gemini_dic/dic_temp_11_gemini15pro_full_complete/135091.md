nonobscurities:/ˌnɒnɒbˈskjʊərətiz/ | n. | 非晦涩之处; 清晰易懂的事物 | things that are not obscure; things that are easily understood | The report was praised for its clarity and lack of nonobscurities.  这份报告因其清晰明了和通俗易懂而受到称赞。|  Example: His explanations were refreshingly free of nonobscurities. 他的解释令人耳目一新，清晰易懂。| synonyms: clarities, simplicities, plainnesses | antonyms: obscurities, complexities, intricacies | noun

词源:  Formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to "obscurities," which is the plural of "obscurity."  "Obscurity" comes from the Latin "obscuritas," meaning "darkness" or "hiddenness."
