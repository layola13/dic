cochleariform:/kɒkliˈɛərɪfɔːm/ | adj. | 耳蜗形的，勺状的 | Shaped like a spoon, specifically relating to the spoon-shaped projection of the wall of the tympanic cavity that separates the fenestra vestibuli (oval window) from the canal for the tensor tympani muscle. | The cochleariform process is a bony partition in the middle ear.  耳蜗状突是一个位于中耳的骨性隔板。| The cochleariform process acts as a pulley for the tensor tympani muscle. 耳蜗状突充当鼓膜张肌的滑车。 | spoon-shaped, spathulate | N/A | adjective
