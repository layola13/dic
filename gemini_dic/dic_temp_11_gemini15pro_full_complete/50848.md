deseeding:/diːˈsiːdɪŋ/
verb
去籽
The process of removing seeds from fruits or vegetables.
The cook was deseeding the peppers for the salsa.  厨师正在为莎莎酱去辣椒籽。
近义词:seed removal, pitting
反义词:seeding
词性:动词(gerund/present participle)

noun
去籽

反义词:seeding
