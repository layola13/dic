landman:/ˈlændmən/| n.|土地经纪人；土地测量员；（石油公司）土地权专员|A person who negotiates the purchase of land, especially on behalf of an oil or gas company, or who manages land rights.|例句：The landman secured the mineral rights for the drilling operation. 土地经纪人获得了钻井作业的矿产权。

例句：The landman surveyed the property to determine its boundaries. 土地测量员勘测了该地产以确定其边界。|近义词：surveyor, negotiator, agent|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

landman:/ˈlændmən/| n.|地主；土地所有者|A person who owns land, especially a large amount of land.|例句：The landman owned thousands of acres in the valley.  这位地主在山谷里拥有数千英亩土地。

例句：The landman leased his land to farmers for cultivation.  地主将他的土地租给农民耕种。|近义词：landowner, landlord|反义词:tenant|词性:noun
