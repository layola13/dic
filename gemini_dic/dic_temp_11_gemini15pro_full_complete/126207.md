mwananchi:/ˌmwɑːnɑːnˈtʃiː/| n.|公民;国民;平民|A citizen of a country, especially Tanzania.  A Swahili word.|例句：The mwananchi expressed his concerns about the upcoming election. 这位公民表达了他对即将到来的选举的担忧。

例句：Every mwananchi has the right to vote. 每位公民都有投票权。|近义词：citizen, national, resident, inhabitant|反义词:foreigner, alien|词性:noun
