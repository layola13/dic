nonguarantees: /nɒnˌɡærənˈtiːz/ | n. | 无担保，无保证 | Absence of guarantees; things not guaranteed. | The contract explicitly states the nonguarantees of the investment.  合同明确规定了这项投资的无担保条款。|  The company's nonguarantees regarding future performance made investors hesitant.  该公司对未来业绩的无保证承诺让投资者犹豫不决。| 近义词: uncertainties,  risks | 反义词: guarantees, assurances | 词性: 名词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "guarantees" (plural of "guarantee").  From Old French "garant" and ultimately from Germanic roots.
