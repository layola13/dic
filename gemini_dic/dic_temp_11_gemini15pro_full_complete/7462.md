anisosthenic:/ˌænˌaɪsoʊˈsθɛnɪk/
adjective
不等张的，力量不对称的
Characterized by unequal strength in corresponding muscles or limbs on both sides of the body.
Anisosthenic exercises are designed to correct muscle imbalances.  不等张运动旨在纠正肌肉失衡。
Physical therapists often work with patients on anisosthenic exercises to improve their balance.  物理治疗师经常与患者一起进行不等张运动以改善他们的平衡。
近义词: asymmetrical, imbalanced
反义词: symmetrical, balanced
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *anisos* "unequal" + *sthenos* "strength"
