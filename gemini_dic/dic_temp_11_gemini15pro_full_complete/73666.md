forgerer:/ˈfɔːrdʒərər/| n. |伪造者；造假者|A person who makes a copy of something, such as a document, painting, etc., in order to deceive people.|The forgerer was eventually caught and sent to prison.  伪造者最终被抓获并被送进监狱。|Synonyms: counterfeiter, falsifier, imitator|Antonyms: authenticator, verifier|noun
