arachnophobics:/əˌræknəˈfoʊbɪks/ | n. | 蜘蛛恐惧症患者 | People who have an extreme or irrational fear of spiders are called arachnophobics. |  Arachnophobics often avoid dark corners and basements. 蜘蛛恐惧症患者通常会避开黑暗的角落和地下室。 | My brother is such an arachnophobic that he screams if he sees even a tiny spider. 我哥哥有严重的蜘蛛恐惧症，即使看到一只小蜘蛛也会尖叫。 |  Synonyms:  spider-phobes | Antonyms:  arachnophiles | noun


Etymology:
From Greek  aráchnē  (spider) +  phóbos  (fear) + -ic (suffix forming adjectives). The plural form is created by adding -s.
