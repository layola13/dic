april-fool:/ˈeɪprəl ˈfuːl/| n.|愚人节，愚人节玩笑|A trick played on April 1st.  It's traditional to play april-fool jokes on people on April Fool's Day.|例句：He played an april-fool joke on his sister by hiding her shoes. 他把妹妹的鞋子藏起来，跟她开了个愚人节玩笑。

例句：Don't be an april-fool and believe everything you hear today! 不要做个傻瓜，今天听到的一切都不要相信！|近义词：prank, hoax|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑serious event)|词性:noun
