dysteleology:/ˌdɪsˌtiːliˈɒlədʒi/ /ˌdɪsˌtiːliˈɑːlədʒi/
noun
缺乏目的性；无目的论
The absence or denial of teleology; the philosophical doctrine that there is no design or purpose in nature or human existence.
The book explored the concept of dysteleology in the universe, suggesting that the cosmos might be a random, chaotic place. 这本书探讨了宇宙中无目的论的概念，认为宇宙可能是一个随机的、混乱的地方。
dysteleology in human existence: the feeling that life has no inherent meaning. 人类存在中的无目的论：感觉人生没有内在意义。
近义词:purposelessness, aimlessness, meaninglessness
反义词:teleology, purposefulness
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek δυσ- (dus-, “bad, difficult”) + τέλος (télos, “end, purpose”) + -λογία (-logía, “-logy”).
