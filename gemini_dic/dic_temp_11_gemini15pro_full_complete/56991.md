double-decker:/ˈdʌbəlˈdɛkər/ (US), /ˈdʌblˈdɛkə(r)/ (UK)| n. |双层公共汽车；双层甲板船；双层床|A bus or other vehicle with two decks, one on top of the other. Also, anything arranged in two tiers or layers.| I took the double-decker bus to the city center. 我乘坐双层巴士去了市中心。| synonyms: double-deck bus, two-story bus | antonyms: single-decker | noun
