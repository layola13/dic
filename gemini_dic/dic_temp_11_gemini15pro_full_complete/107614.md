landwehr:/ˈlɑːntveːr/| n. | 民兵，国土防卫队 | A military reserve force, especially in German-speaking countries, composed of men who have completed their regular military service. | The landwehr was called up to defend the city. 国土防卫队被召集起来保卫这座城市。|Synonyms: militia, reserve, territorial army | Antonyms: regular army | noun

landwehr:/ˈlɑːntveːr/| n. | 国土防卫队 |  A reserve military force, typically composed of older soldiers or those who have completed their regular service, often responsible for home defense. | The Prussian landwehr played a crucial role in the Napoleonic Wars. 普鲁士国土防卫队在拿破仑战争中发挥了至关重要的作用。| Synonyms: reserves, militia, national guard | Antonyms: standing army, active duty forces | noun
