barbel:/ˈbɑːrbəl/ | n. |触须；倒刺；杠铃；金属棒|A fleshy filament growing from the mouth or snout of certain fishes, such as the catfish, used for touch and taste.  A slender, pointed process; a barb. A barbell.|The catfish uses its barbels to locate food in murky water.  鲶鱼用它的触须在浑浊的水中寻找食物。|The fish has a prominent barbel on its chin. 这条鱼的下巴上有一个明显的触须。| synonym: whisker, feeler, barb, wattle| antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider "smooth" in the context of a fishing hook) | noun

barbel:/ˈbɑːrbəl/ | n. |杠铃；金属棒|A straight bar with weights attached to each end, used for weightlifting.|He lifted the heavy barbel with ease. 他轻松地举起了沉重的杠铃。|She added more weight to the barbel. 她在杠铃上增加了更多重量。| synonym: weight bar, barbell| antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun
