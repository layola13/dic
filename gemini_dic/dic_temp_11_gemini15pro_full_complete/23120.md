bourgeoisie: /ˌbʊərʒ.wɑːˈziː/ /ˌbʊrʒ.wɑːˈziː/
noun
中产阶级；资产阶级
The social class between the aristocracy or very wealthy and the working class, typically with reference to its perceived materialistic values or conventional attitudes.
The bourgeoisie owned the means of production during the Industrial Revolution. 工业革命时期，资产阶级拥有生产资料。
The rise of the bourgeoisie challenged the power of the aristocracy. 资产阶级的兴起挑战了贵族的权力。
middle class, capitalist class
proletariat, working class
n.
