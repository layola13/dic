groaner:/ˈɡroʊnər/| n. | 令人 groan 的事物（通常指笑话）; 烂笑话; |Something that makes you groan, typically a joke. |例句：He told a real groaner about a man walking into a bar. 他讲了一个关于一个男人走进酒吧的烂笑话。

例句：That joke was such a groaner; I could barely stifle my groan.  那个笑话真是太烂了，我差点没忍住呻吟。|近义词：corny joke, bad joke, lame joke, dad joke|反义词: witty joke, good joke, side-splitter|词性:noun
