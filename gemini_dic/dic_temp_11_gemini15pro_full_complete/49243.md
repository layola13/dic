deleterious:/ˌdɛlɪˈtɪəriəs/
adjective
有害的
causing harm or damage.
Exposure to high levels of radiation is deleterious to health.  暴露于高水平辐射对健康有害。
Smoking has a deleterious effect on the lungs. 吸烟对肺部有有害影响。

近义词: harmful, damaging, detrimental, injurious, adverse, noxious, pernicious
反义词: beneficial, advantageous, helpful, wholesome, salutary
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *dēlēteriārius, from dēlēre (“to destroy, wipe out, obliterate”) + -ius.
