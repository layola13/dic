elms:/ɛlmz/
n.|榆树|A tall deciduous tree with rough, serrated leaves, typically growing in hedgerows or woods.|The elms lining the street were magnificent.|近义词:tree,  wood|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑shrub 灌木)|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Old English *elm (attested only in compounds), from Proto-Germanic *elmaz, *alimiz (source also of West Frisian ielm, Dutch olm, German Ulme, Danish and Swedish alm), from Proto-Indo-European *h₁élemnos (source also of Latin ulmus, Russian i̇lm).
