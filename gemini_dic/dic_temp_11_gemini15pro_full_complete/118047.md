menagogue: /ˈmɛnəɡɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈmɛnəˌɡɑːɡ/ (US)| n. |催经剂；通经药|A substance that promotes menstrual flow.|Example:  The doctor prescribed a menagogue to help regulate her cycle. 医生给她开了催经药来帮助调节她的周期。

Example:  Certain herbs are considered to be natural menagogues.  某些草药被认为是天然的催经剂。|近义词:emmenagogue|反义词:emmenagogues typically don't have direct opposites, but agents that suppress menstruation could be considered functional opposites (though not true antonyms).|词性:noun
