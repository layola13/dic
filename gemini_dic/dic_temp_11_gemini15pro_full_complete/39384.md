conjunctivitis:/kənˌdʒʌŋktɪˈvaɪtɪs/
n.
结膜炎
Inflammation of the conjunctiva, the membrane that lines the eyelids and covers the exposed surface of the eyeball.
My doctor diagnosed me with conjunctivitis. 我的医生诊断我得了结膜炎。
The child's conjunctivitis caused his eyes to be red and watery.  孩子的结膜炎导致他的眼睛发红流泪。

近义词:pinkeye, red eye
反义词:(None - diseases don't typically have antonyms)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *conjunctivitis, from Latin conjunctiva + -itis.  Latin conjunctiva is the feminine of conjunctivus ("joining"), from conjunctus, past participle of conjungere ("to join together"), from com- ("together") + jungere ("to join").  The suffix -itis denotes inflammation.
