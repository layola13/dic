diphthongalize:/dɪfˈθɒŋɡəlaɪz/ /daɪˈθɔːŋɡəˌlaɪz/
verb
双元音化；变成双元音
To make or become a diphthong.  The process by which a single vowel sound changes into a diphthong.
The vowel in "name" has diphthongalized in some dialects of English.  “name”中的元音在某些英语方言中已经双元音化了。
diphthongize
No real direct synonyms.  Related terms include: monophthongize (反义词)
monophthongize
v.
