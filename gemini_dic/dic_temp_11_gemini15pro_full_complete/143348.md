outexecute:/ˌaʊtˈɛksɪˌkjut/ | v. | 胜过，在执行方面超越 | To surpass or outperform someone in the execution of a task or strategy. |  Our team outexecuted the competition and secured the contract. 我们的团队在执行方面胜过了竞争对手，并获得了合同。| Synonyms: outperform, outmaneuver, surpass | Antonyms: underperform, lag behind | verb

outexecute:/ˌaʊtˈɛksɪˌkjut/ | v. |  执行得更好 | To perform or carry out a task or plan more effectively than someone else. | They outexecuted their rivals by delivering the project ahead of schedule and under budget. 他们通过提前并在预算内交付项目，执行得比竞争对手更好。 | Synonyms: excel, exceed, outdo | Antonyms: fail, fall short | verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From out- + execute.  "Out-" is a prefix meaning "exceeding," "surpassing," or "beyond." "Execute" comes from the Latin "exsequi" meaning "to follow out," "to carry out," or "to perform."
