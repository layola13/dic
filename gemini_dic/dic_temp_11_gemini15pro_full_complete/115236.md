manikin:/ˈmænɪkɪn/ | n. |人体模型；时装模特儿；侏儒|A jointed model of the human body, used especially for teaching anatomy, demonstrating postures, or displaying clothes. | The fashion students draped the fabric over the manikin to create a new design. 时装专业的学生将布料披在人体模型上以创作新的设计。

The store used manikins to display their latest collection.  这家商店用人体模型来展示他们的最新系列。 | synonyms: dummy, model, figure, form, lay figure | antonyms: human, person | noun


manikin:/ˈmænɪkɪn/ | n. |侏儒；矮人|A person of abnormally small stature. | He felt like a manikin standing next to the basketball players.  站在篮球运动员旁边，他觉得自己像个侏儒。


In the story, the evil sorcerer turned the prince into a manikin. 在故事中，邪恶的巫师把王子变成了一个侏儒。 | synonyms: dwarf, midget | antonyms: giant | noun
