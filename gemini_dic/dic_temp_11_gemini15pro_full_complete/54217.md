disfigurer:/dɪsˈfɪɡjərər/ | n. |损毁者，毁容者|A person or thing that disfigures something, especially someone's face. |The acid was a terrible disfigurer. 这种酸是一种可怕的毁容剂。|Synonyms: deformer, marrer, mutilator |Antonyms: beautifier, enhancer| noun

disfigure:/dɪsˈfɪɡjər/ | vt. |损毁，毁容|To spoil the appearance of something, especially someone's face. |The fire disfigured her face. 大火毁了她的容貌。|Synonyms: deform, mar, mutilate |Antonyms: beautify, enhance| verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French desfigurer, from des- (expressing reversal) + figurer (to shape, represent), from Latin figura (shape, form).
