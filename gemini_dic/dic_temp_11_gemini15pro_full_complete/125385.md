multimodal:/ˌmʌltiˈməʊdl/ (UK) /ˌmʌltiˈmoʊdl/ (US)| adj.|多模态的|Involving several different modes or ways of interacting or communicating, such as combining text, images, and sound.|This museum offers a multimodal experience, combining visual art with interactive displays and audio guides. 这个博物馆提供多模态体验，将视觉艺术与互动展示和音频指南相结合。|The company is developing a multimodal AI system that can understand and respond to text, images, and voice commands. 这家公司正在开发一个能够理解和响应文本、图像和语音命令的多模态人工智能系统。|intermodal, multimedia|unimodal|adjective
