non-transferable:/ˌnɒn trænsˈfɜːrəbl/ /ˌnɑːn trænsˈfɜːrəbl/
adjective
不可转让的
Not able to be transferred or passed from one person to another.
This ticket is non-transferable.  这张票是不可转让的。
My gym membership is non-transferable. 我的健身房会员资格是不可转让的。
synonyms: non-assignable, restricted
antonyms: transferable, assignable
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
transferable (adjective): from transfer (verb) + -able (suffix)
transfer (verb): from Latin transferre "to carry across," from trans- "across" + ferre "to carry"
-able (suffix): from Latin -abilis, forming adjectives meaning "capable of being," "worthy of being"
