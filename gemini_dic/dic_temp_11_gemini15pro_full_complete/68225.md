fairings:/ˈferɪŋz/| n. |整流罩，导流罩|A structure whose primary function is to produce a smooth outline and reduce drag, as on an aircraft, boat, or motorcycle.|The motorcycle's fairings were damaged in the crash.  摩托车的整流罩在事故中损坏了。|Example: The aircraft's fairings were designed to reduce wind resistance.  飞机的整流罩旨在减少风阻。|Synonyms: cowling, cladding, shell, casing|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "exposed components")|noun
