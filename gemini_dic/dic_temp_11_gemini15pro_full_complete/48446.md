deeded:/ˈdiːdɪd/
adjective
已立契的，已转让的
transferred by deed; having been formally transferred by a deed.
The house was deeded to his daughter. 这房子已立契转让给他女儿了。
近义词：granted, conveyed, transferred
反义词：undeeded

deeded:/ˈdiːdɪd/
verb
立契转让，用契约转让(尤指土地或财产)
to transfer (something, especially real estate) by deed.
He deeded the property to his son. 他把财产立契转让给了他的儿子。
近义词：grant, convey, transfer
反义词：retain, withhold
