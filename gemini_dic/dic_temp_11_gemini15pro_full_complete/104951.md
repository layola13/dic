kelson:/ˈkɛlsən/ | n. | 龙骨 |A longitudinal timber structure in a wooden ship, lying on top of the keel and fastened to the frames, which gives internal strength, especially in the middle of the ship.| The kelson provided crucial support for the ship's hull.  龙骨为船体提供了至关重要的支撑。| synonym: keel, backbone | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "weak point" in a figurative sense) | noun
