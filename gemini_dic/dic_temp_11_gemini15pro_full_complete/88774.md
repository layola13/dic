hodgepodge:/ˈhɒdʒpɒdʒ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːdʒpɑːdʒ/ (US)| n.|大杂烩，混合物|A confused mixture or collection of different things.|The attic was a hodgepodge of old furniture, toys, and clothes.|近义词: mixture, medley, jumble, assortment|反义词: order, uniformity|noun

hodge:/hɒdʒ/ (UK) /hɑːdʒ/ (US)| n.|泥瓦匠的助手|A labourer who carries bricks and mortar for a bricklayer.|The hodge carried the heavy load up the ladder.|近义词: laborer, worker|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "supervisor" in a specific context)|noun
