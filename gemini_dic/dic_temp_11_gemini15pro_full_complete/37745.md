compartner:/kəmˈpɑːrtnər/
n.
伙伴，合伙人，配偶
One who shares or participates with another; partner; associate; spouse.
例句：He considered her his closest compartner in the business.  他认为她是他在生意上最亲密的伙伴。

例句：She found a compartner in her husband for her latest venture. 她找到了她的丈夫作为她最新事业的合伙人。
近义词：partner, associate, colleague, companion, spouse
反义词：opponent, adversary, competitor
词性：名词
