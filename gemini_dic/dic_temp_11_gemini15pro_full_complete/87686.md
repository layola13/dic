hexamerism:/ˈhɛksəmərɪzəm/
n.|六聚体化，六聚现象|The state or condition of being a hexamer; the tendency of molecules to associate in groups of six.|The protein exhibits hexamerism in solution. 这种蛋白质在溶液中呈现六聚体化现象。|oligomerization, polymerization|monomerism|noun
Etymology-> From Ancient Greek ἕξ (héx, “six”) +‎ -mer +‎ -ism.
