gold-plated:/ˈɡoʊld ˈpleɪtɪd/| adj.|镀金的，虚假的，华而不实的|Covered with a thin layer of gold; superficially attractive or valuable but lacking real worth.|The company offered a gold-plated pension plan to attract executives. 这家公司提供了一个镀金的养老金计划来吸引高管人员。

The politician made gold-plated promises he couldn't keep.  这位政客做出了他无法兑现的华而不实的承诺。|近义词：gilded, ornate, showy, ostentatious|反义词：plain, simple, unadorned|词性:形容词

gold-plate:/ˈɡoʊld ˈpleɪt/| vt.|镀金，使…表面上好看|To cover with a thin layer of gold; to make something appear more attractive or valuable than it actually is.|They gold-plated the cutlery to make it look more expensive. 他们把餐具镀金，让它看起来更贵。

The marketing team tried to gold-plate the product's features.  营销团队试图美化产品的功能。|近义词：gild, embellish|反义词：strip, expose|词性:动词
