focom:/ˈfoʊkəm/| n.| 焦点，中心 |A central point of attraction, attention, or activity.|The focom of the discussion was the impact of social media on teenagers. 讨论的焦点是社交媒体对青少年的影响。|近义词：focus, center, core|反义词: periphery, margin|词性:名词

词源: "Focom" is not a standard English word. It appears to be a misspelling or variant of "focus".  "Focus" comes from the Latin word "focus" meaning "hearth" or "fireplace,"  the central point of a home.
