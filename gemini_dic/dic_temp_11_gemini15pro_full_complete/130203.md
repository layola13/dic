non-secular:/nɒnˈsɛkjʊlə(r)/ /nɑːnˈsɛkjələr/ | adj. |非世俗的，宗教的| Relating to religion or religious beliefs; not secular. |  Example:  Non-secular education often includes religious instruction. 非世俗教育通常包括宗教指导。|  Example:  The non-secular state enforced strict religious laws.  这个非世俗国家强制执行严格的宗教法律。| 近义词: religious, sacred, spiritual, divine, holy | 反义词: secular, worldly, temporal, lay |词性: 形容词

non-secular:/nɒnˈsɛkjʊlə(r)/ /nɑːnˈsɛkjələr/ | n. | (通常用复数)宗教人士；宗教团体| A person or group related to religion or religious beliefs.
