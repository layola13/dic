inequitably:/ɪnˈɛkwɪtəbli/
adverb
不公平地，不公正地
in an unfair and unjust manner
例句：Resources are distributed inequitably across the country. 全国资源分配不均。

例句：The law was applied inequitably, favoring the wealthy.  这项法律适用不公平，偏袒富人。

近义词：unfairly, unjustly, disproportionately
反义词：fairly, justly, equitably
词性：副词

词源：
From in- (not) + equitable (fair, just) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *aequus* (equal, level, fair).
