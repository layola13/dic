acceptances:/əkˈseptənsɪz/
noun
接受; 认可; 赞同; 承认
the act of accepting something or someone, or the fact of being accepted
例句：The university sent out letters of acceptance. 大学发出了录取通知书。

例句：His acceptance of the award was greeted with applause. 他领奖时获得了掌声。

近义词：agreement, approval, consent, confirmation, admission
反义词：rejection, refusal, denial
词性：n.
