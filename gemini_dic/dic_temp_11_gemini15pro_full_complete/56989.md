double-dating:/ˈdʌbl deɪtɪŋ/
n.
双重约会；四人约会
The act of two couples going out together on a date.
例句：They went double-dating with their friends last weekend.  他们上周末和朋友们进行了双重约会。

例句：Double-dating can be a fun way to socialize. 双重约会可以是一种有趣的社交方式。

近义词：group date
反义词：single date, going out alone
词性:名词
