cephalocercal:/ˌsefələˈsɜːrkəl/ | adj. | 头尾的，从头到尾的 | Relating to or extending from the head to the tail.  |  A cephalocercal axis runs from the head to the tail of an organism.  头尾轴线从生物体的头部延伸到尾部。 | lengthwise, longitudinal | transverse | adjective

Etymology:  From Greek *kephalē* (head) +  Latin *cerca* (tail).
