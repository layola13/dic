acervate:/əˈsɜːrvət/| n. |凝聚体；凝聚层|A small droplet of coacervate, formed by the aggregation of colloidal droplets, especially in the supposed formation of the first living cells.|The scientists studied the properties of the acervate to understand the origins of life.|Synonyms: aggregate, cluster, clump|Antonyms: dispersion, solution|noun
