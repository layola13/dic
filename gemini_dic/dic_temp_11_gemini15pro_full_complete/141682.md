optometrist:/ˌɒptˈɒmɪtrɪst/ /ˌɑːptˈɑːmɪtrɪst/| n. |验光师；视力测定者|A health professional who examines the eyes for visual defects and prescribes corrective lenses or other appropriate treatment.  |例句：I made an appointment with the optometrist to check my vision. 我预约了验光师检查视力。

例句：The optometrist prescribed new glasses for me. 验光师给我开了新眼镜的处方。|近义词：eye doctor, ophthalmologist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

optician:/ɒpˈtɪʃən/ /ɑːpˈtɪʃən/| n. |眼镜商；眼镜制造者|Someone who makes or sells eyeglasses and contact lenses.|例句：The optician adjusted my glasses so they fit better. 眼镜商调整了我的眼镜，使它们更合身。

例句：I took my prescription to the optician to have it filled. 我拿着我的处方去找眼镜商配眼镜。|近义词:eyeglass maker, lens maker|反义词: N/A|词性:noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From French optométriste, from opto- + Greek μέτρον (métron, “measure”) + -ist.
