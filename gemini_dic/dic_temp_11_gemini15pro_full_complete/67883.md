fabulate:/ˈfæbjʊleɪt/ | v. | 创作故事；虚构；编造 | To invent or tell a story, often one that is not true. |  He fabulated a wild tale about being kidnapped by aliens. 他编造了一个关于被外星人绑架的离奇故事。

| Example: The author fabulated a complex plot involving time travel.  这位作者虚构了一个涉及时间旅行的复杂情节。| Synonym: invent, fabricate, concoct, make up | Antonym: report, recount, relate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fabulātus, past participle of fabulārī (“to talk, chat, tell stories”), from Latin fābula (“story, tale”).
