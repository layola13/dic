acronychal: /əˈkrɒnɪkəl/ (US) /əˈkrɒnɪkl/ (UK)| adj.| 夜晚初升的；与日落同时升起的| Relating to the rising of a star at sunset or the setting of a star at sunrise.  It describes a celestial event where a star rises above the eastern horizon at the same time the sun sets below the western horizon, making the star visible all night.| The acronychal rising of Sirius marked the beginning of the ancient Egyptian calendar.  天狼星的昏星升起标志着古埃及历法的开始。

The acronychal setting of that constellation occurs just before dawn.  那个星座的昏星落下发生在黎明前。|近义词：cosmical, heliacal|反义词：|词性: adjective
