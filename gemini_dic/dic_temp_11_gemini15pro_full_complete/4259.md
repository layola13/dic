airsick:/ˈɛərsɪk/ | adj. | 晕机的，患航空病的 | Feeling nauseous or vomiting as a result of the motion of an aircraft. |  She felt airsick during the turbulent flight.  她感觉在颠簸的飞行中晕机了。 | Example: The turbulence made him airsick.  乱流让他晕机了。| Synonyms: nauseated, queasy, sick | Antonyms: well, healthy | adjective
