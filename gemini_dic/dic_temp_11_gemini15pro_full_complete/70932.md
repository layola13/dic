firebug:/ˈfaɪərbʌɡ/| n. |纵火犯；玩火者；萤火虫；调试工具(Firebug)|A person who deliberately sets fire to property;  A person who enjoys watching fires; A type of insect, also known as a lightning bug, that emits light; A discontinued web browser extension for debugging.|例句：The police are looking for the firebug who started the blaze. 警方正在寻找引发这场大火的纵火犯。

例句：He was always a firebug as a child, fascinated by matches. 他小时候总是个玩火的孩子，对火柴很着迷。

近义词：arsonist, pyromaniac, incendiarist (for the arson meaning), lightning bug, firefly (for the insect meaning)|反义词:firefighter (for the arson meaning)|词性:noun
