industriously: /ɪnˈdʌstriəsli/ | adv. | 勤奋地，勤勉地 | In a hard-working and diligent manner. | She worked industriously on her project, determined to finish it before the deadline. 她勤奋地做着她的项目，决心在截止日期前完成。|  He studied industriously for the exam and achieved a high score. 他勤奋地学习，考试取得了高分。 | 近义词: diligently, assiduously, laboriously | 反义词: lazily, idly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *industrieux*, from Latin industrius ("active, diligent"), from indo- + -struere ("build upon").
