feats:/fiːts/| n.|功绩，壮举，技艺|An act or deed that shows daring, skill, or strength; an achievement that requires great courage, skill, or strength. |例句：The athlete's feats of strength amazed the crowd.  这位运动员的力量壮举令观众叹为观止。

例句：Climbing Mount Everest is a remarkable feat. 攀登珠穆朗玛峰是一项了不起的壮举。|近义词：achievements, accomplishments, exploits, deeds|反义词:failures, defeats|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *fait* (Modern French *fait*), from Latin *factum*, neuter past participle of *facere* "to do, make."
