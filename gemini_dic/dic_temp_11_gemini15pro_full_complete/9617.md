antisplitting:/ˌæntiˈsplɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 防分裂的，反分裂的 |  Preventing or designed to prevent splitting or fragmentation.  Refers to techniques or policies that aim to maintain unity or wholeness, often in a political or social context. | The antisplitting provisions of the treaty aimed to preserve the territorial integrity of the nation.  该条约的反分裂条款旨在维护国家的领土完整。| synonyms: unifying, cohesive, integrating | antonyms: divisive, separating, fragmenting | adjective
