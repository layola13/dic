glomming:/ˈɡlɑmɪŋ/ | n. | 依附；粘附；凝集；（尤指）过分热情地接近 | The act of glomming onto something or someone, often excessively or enthusiastically. |  She's been glomming onto me all night, asking about my job and family. 她整晚都粘着我，问我的工作和家庭情况。| 近义词：clinging, latching, attaching | 反义词：detaching, ignoring | 词性：名词

glomming:/ˈɡlɑmɪŋ/ | v. | 依附；粘附；凝集；（尤指）过分热情地接近 | To attach oneself to something or someone, often excessively or enthusiastically. | He glommed onto the idea of starting a business together. 他欣然接受了一起创业的想法。 | 近义词：cling, latch, attach | 反义词：detach, ignore | 词性：动词
