inaffable:/ɪnˈæfəbəl/ | adj. | 不友善的，难以接近的，孤傲的 | Not friendly or approachable; aloof and unsociable. |  The inaffable CEO was known for his intimidating demeanor. 这位不友善的CEO以其令人生畏的风度而闻名。

The inaffable artist refused to give interviews or discuss his work.  这位孤傲的艺术家拒绝接受采访或讨论他的作品。

| 近义词: unapproachable, aloof, standoffish, haughty, distant, reserved, unfriendly, cold, curt | 反义词: affable, approachable, friendly, sociable, warm, amiable | 词性: 形容词


词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin inaffabilis, from in- ‘not’ + affabilis (from affari ‘address, speak to’).
