muezzin:/muːˈɛzɪn/ | n. | 宣礼员，唤拜人 | A man who calls Muslims to prayer from the minaret of a mosque. |  The muezzin's call to prayer echoed through the city. 宣礼员的唤拜声响彻全城。

The muezzin climbed the minaret five times a day. 宣礼员每天爬上叫拜楼五次。| 近义词: crier, caller | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

词源: From Arabic  muʾadhdhin,  active participle of  ʾadhḏana  "to proclaim, announce," literally "to give ear, listen."
