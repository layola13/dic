depopularized:/diːˈpɒpjələraɪzd/
|v. |使不受欢迎；使人减少；降低…的声望|To make something less popular or less densely populated.|The new housing developments have depopularized the traditional village center. 新的住宅开发项目使得传统的村中心不再受欢迎。|The government's policies have depopularized the ruling party. 政府的政策使得执政党失去了民心。|Synonyms: marginalized, unpopular, ostracized|Antonyms: popularized, promoted, celebrated
|Verb
