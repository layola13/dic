chirpy:/ˈtʃɜːrpi/ (US) /ˈtʃɪːrpi/ (UK)| adj.|活泼的，快活的|Describes someone in a cheerful and lively mood, often talking a lot.|She was chirpy and optimistic despite the bad news. 她尽管听到了坏消息，仍然活泼乐观。|近义词：cheerful, lively, merry, upbeat, perky|反义词：gloomy, depressed, sullen, morose|词性:形容词
词源:  Likely imitative of bird sounds, especially those of small birds.  Relates to "chirp," meaning to make a short, sharp, high-pitched sound like a bird.
