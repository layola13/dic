creams:/kriːmz/
| n. | 奶油；乳膏；面霜；精华；(尤指烹调用的)奶油状食物；奶油色；乳白色 | A thick, smooth substance or preparation, typically of a pale yellow or white color.  It can refer to dairy cream, cosmetic creams, or other creamy substances. | She applied some hand cream. 她涂了一些护手霜。 | synonyms: lotion, ointment, moisturizer, emulsion | antonyms: liquid, solid | noun


creams:/kriːmz/
| v. |  (用奶油)覆盖；使…成奶油状；提取奶油；彻底击败 | To cover or fill with cream; to make something creamy; to remove the cream from milk; to beat thoroughly. |  She creamed the butter and sugar together. 她把黄油和糖一起打成糊状。 | synonyms: whip, blend, pulverize, thrash | antonyms: separate, thin, dilute | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *cresme, from Late Latin chrisma ("unguent, anointing oil"), from Greek χρῖσμα (khrîsma, “anointing”), from χρίω (khríō, “I anoint”).
