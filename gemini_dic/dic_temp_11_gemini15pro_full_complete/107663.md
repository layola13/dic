languishness:/ˈlæŋɡwɪʃnəs/| n. |倦怠；萎靡不振；没精打采|The state of being languid; listlessness or weakness.|Her languishness was caused by the oppressive heat. 她的倦怠是由于闷热的天气造成的。|近义词：listlessness, lethargy, lassitude, inertia, torpor|反义词：energy, vigor, vitality|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *languisshenesse, from Old French langueur + -esse, from Late Latin languor. Ultimately from Latin languēre (to be faint, weak).
