antichristianly:/ˌæntiˈkrɪstʃənli/
adverb
反基督教地；敌视基督教地
In a manner opposed to the principles of Christianity.
He acted antichristianly by denying the divinity of Christ. 他否认基督的神性，表现得反基督教。
The group spread antichristianly propaganda.  该组织散播反基督教的宣传。
近义词: impiously, irreligiously, blasphemously
反义词: Christianly, piously
词性: adverb
