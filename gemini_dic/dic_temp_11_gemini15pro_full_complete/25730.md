bunion: /ˈbʌnjən/ (US), /ˈbʌnjən/ (UK)| n. |拇囊炎，拇趾外翻|A painful swelling on the first joint of the big toe, typically caused by wearing ill-fitting shoes.|She had a bunion on her left foot that made it painful to walk. 她左脚拇趾外翻，走路很疼。|近义词: hallux valgus|反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "healthy foot/toe")|词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *bunyon, from Old French bugne (“bump, swelling”).
