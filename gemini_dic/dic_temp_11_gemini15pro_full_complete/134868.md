nonmoneyer: /nɑnˈmʌniər/ | n. | 非货币主义者 |  A person who believes that the quantity of money in circulation is not the primary determinant of economic activity.  Specifically, it often refers to those who reject the theories of monetarism. |  The nonmoneyer argued that fiscal policy was more important than monetary policy.  这位非货币主义者认为财政政策比货币政策更重要。|  The conference was attended by both monetarists and nonmoneyers.  出席会议的有货币主义者和非货币主义者。|  Near Synonyms: fiscalist, Keynesian (sometimes) | Antonyms: monetarist | Noun
