ectasia:/ekˈteɪʒə/ /ɪkˈteɪʒə/
n.
扩张，膨胀
Dilation or distention of a tubular structure, such as a blood vessel or duct.
Bronchiectasis is a condition in which the airways of the lungs become abnormally widened. 支气管扩张是一种肺部呼吸道异常扩张的疾病。
Ductal ectasia is a benign breast condition. 导管扩张症是一种良性的乳腺疾病。
dilation, distension, widening, enlargement
constriction, narrowing, shrinking
noun
