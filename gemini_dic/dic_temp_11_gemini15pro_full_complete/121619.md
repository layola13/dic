mispackaged:/ˌmɪsˈpækɪdʒd/
adjective
包装错误的，包装不当的
incorrectly or poorly packaged
The mispackaged product had to be recalled.  这个包装错误的产品不得不被召回。
The customer complained about the mispackaged items. 顾客抱怨商品包装错误。
近义词: improperly packaged, wrongly packaged
反义词: correctly packaged, properly packaged
词性: adjective

词源:
mis- (prefix): wrongly, badly  错误地，糟糕地
package (verb): to put something into a package  包装
-ed (suffix): forming the past participle of verbs  构成动词的过去分词
