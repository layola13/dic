mistranscription: /ˌmɪstrænˈskrɪpʃən/ | n. | 抄写错误，誊写错误，转录错误 | An inaccurate written record of spoken or written words.  A mistranscription can occur due to mishearing, misreading, typing errors, or technical glitches. |  The court reporter's mistranscription led to a mistrial. 法庭记录员的抄写错误导致了审判无效。

The mistranscription of the ancient text made it difficult to understand.  对古代文献的转录错误使其难以理解。 | synonyms: error, misprint, typo, inaccuracy | antonyms: accuracy, correctness | noun
