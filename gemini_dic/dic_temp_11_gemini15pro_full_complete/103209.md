jetlike: /ˈdʒɛtˌlaɪk/
adjective
像喷气式飞机的；喷射状的
Resembling a jet or its stream; moving swiftly like a jet.
The car sped down the highway with jetlike speed. 汽车以喷气式飞机般的速度在高速公路上飞驰。
The comet left a jetlike trail of dust and gas.  这颗彗星留下了一道喷射状的尘埃和气体轨迹。
近义词: swift, rapid, fast, speedy
反义词: slow, sluggish
