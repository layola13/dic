nonwatertight:/nɒnˈwɔːtətaɪt/ | adj. |不防水的，不密封的 | Not impervious to water; allowing water to pass through. | The roof was nonwatertight and leaked during the storm. 屋顶不防水，暴风雨期间漏水了。 | The seams of the tent were nonwatertight, and everything inside got wet. 帐篷的接缝不防水，里面的东西都湿透了。 | 近义词: leaky, permeable, porous | 反义词: watertight, waterproof, impermeable | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "watertight" (adjective meaning "impervious to water").  "Watertight" itself comes from "water" + "tight" (meaning "firmly or closely fixed in place").
