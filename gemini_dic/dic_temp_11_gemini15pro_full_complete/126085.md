musty's:/ˈmʌstiz/ | n. | 发霉的东西（通常用复数形式）; 霉味; | Musty's refers to things that are musty, typically used in the plural to indicate a collection of such items or the pervasive smell of mustiness. It's a less common form than simply using "musty things" or "the musty smell." |  I could smell the musty's as soon as I entered the basement. 我一进地下室就闻到一股霉味。| 近义词: mildewy, damp, fusty | 反义词: fresh, airy, clean | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From "musty" + the possessive suffix "-'s". "Musty" comes from the Old English "mustig," meaning "moldy, stale."
