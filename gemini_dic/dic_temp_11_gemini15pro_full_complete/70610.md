filminess:/ˈfɪlmɪnɪs/
noun
薄膜状；薄膜覆盖；朦胧
The state or quality of being filmy; covered with or resembling a thin film.
The filminess of the old photograph gave it a haunting quality. 这张老照片的朦胧感赋予它一种令人难忘的特质。
thinness, gauziness, transparency
opacity, thickness
n.
