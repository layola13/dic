nonconfidently:/ˌnɒnˈkɒnfɪdəntli/ | adv. |不自信地，缺乏自信地 | In a manner lacking confidence or assurance; timidly or hesitantly. | She spoke nonconfidently, her voice barely above a whisper. 她说话没有自信，声音几乎听不见。|  He nonconfidently approached the podium, unsure of his speech. 他不自信地走上讲台，对自己的演讲没有把握。 | 近义词: hesitantly, timidly, uncertainly, nervously | 反义词: confidently, assuredly, boldly | 词性: 副词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + confident (adjective) + -ly (adverbial suffix)
