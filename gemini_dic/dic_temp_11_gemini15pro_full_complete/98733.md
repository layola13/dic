insurmountableness:/ˌɪnsərˈmaʊntəbəlnəs/
n.
不可克服性，不可逾越性
The insurmountableness of the challenge made them give up.  这个挑战的不可克服性使他们放弃了。
The sheer insurmountableness of the task ahead filled him with dread. 前方任务的巨大不可逾越性使他充满了恐惧。
近义词:invincibility, impregnability, insuperability
反义词:surmountability, conquerability
词性:noun

insurmountable:/ˌɪnsərˈmaʊntəbl/
adj.
不可克服的，不可逾越的
They faced seemingly insurmountable obstacles. 他们面临着看似不可克服的障碍。
The problem proved insurmountable.  这个问题被证明是无法克服的。
近义词:insuperable, invincible, unconquerable
反义词:surmountable, conquerable, superable
词性:adjective

surmount:/sərˈmaʊnt/
v.
克服，战胜
She surmounted many obstacles to achieve her goals. 她克服了许多障碍才实现了她的目标。
He had to surmount his fear of public speaking. 他必须克服对公开演讲的恐惧。
近义词:overcome, conquer, vanquish
反义词:succumb, yield, surrender
词性:verb

mount:/maʊnt/
v.
登上，爬上；增加，上升
He mounted the stairs slowly. 他慢慢地爬上楼梯。
Tensions are mounting between the two countries. 两国之间的紧张关系正在加剧。
近义词:ascend, climb, increase
反义词:descend, decrease, decline
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + surmount (overcome) + -able (capable of being) + -ness (state of being).  Ultimately from Latin *supermontare* (to climb over).
