kinesipathy:/ˌkɪnɪˈsɪpəθi/ /ˌkaɪnɪˈsɪpəθi/
noun
运动疗法
A system of treating disease by movements or exercise.
Kinesipathy is often recommended for patients recovering from injuries.  运动疗法通常被推荐给从伤病中恢复的病人。
The doctor suggested kinesipathy as part of her rehabilitation program. 医生建议将运动疗法作为她康复计划的一部分。
Near Synonyms: physiotherapy, physical therapy, exercise therapy, movement therapy
Antonyms: inactivity, rest
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From Greek *kínēsis* "movement" + *-pátheia*  "suffering, feeling"
