gryphons:/ˈɡrɪfənz/ /ˈɡrɪfɑːnz/| n. |狮鹫|A mythical creature with the body, tail, and back legs of a lion; the head and wings of an eagle; and an eagle's talons as its front feet.  |例句：Gryphons are often depicted as guardians of treasure. 狮鹫经常被描绘成宝藏的守护者。

|例句：The gryphons guarded the entrance to the magical kingdom. 狮鹫守卫着魔法王国的入口。
|近义词：griffin, hippogriff|反义词: None (Mythical creatures generally lack direct opposites)|词性:noun
