mournfullest:/ˈmɔːrnfəlɪst/ | adj. | 最悲伤的 |  Superlative form of mournful: most mournful.  Expressing or filled with sorrow or grief to the highest degree. |  Her face was the mournfullest I had ever seen. 她的脸是我见过的最悲伤的。|  The mournfullest song played as the funeral procession began. 当葬礼队伍开始行进时，奏响了最悲伤的歌曲。| 近义词: saddest, most sorrowful, most doleful | 反义词: happiest, most joyful, most cheerful | 词性: 形容词 | 词源: From Middle English *mornfulest, from mornful + -est.  Ultimately from Old English murnful.
