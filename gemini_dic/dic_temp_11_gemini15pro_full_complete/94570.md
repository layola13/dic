impounding:/ɪmˈpaʊndɪŋ/
n.|扣押，充公|The impounding of my car was a major inconvenience. 扣押我的车给我带来了很大的不便。|
impounding:/ɪmˈpaʊndɪŋ/
v.|扣押，充公|The police impounded the vehicle after the accident.  警察在事故后扣押了车辆。|
impounding:/ɪmˈpaʊndɪŋ/
adj.(罕用，通常用impounded)|被扣押的，被充公的|The impounded vehicles were stored in a lot. 被扣押的车辆停放在一个停车场里。|Etymology: From the verb "impound," derived from the Old French "empoindre" (to seize, grasp), ultimately from Latin "in" (in) + "pondus" (weight).|
impounding:|近义词:confiscation, seizure, appropriation|反义词:release, return|n./v./adj.|中文翻译：扣押；充公；没收|The act of seizing and holding in legal custody, typically property, animals, or vehicles.  扣押是指依法扣留和保管的行为，通常指财产、动物或车辆。|例句：The city impounded several stray dogs.  该市扣押了几只流浪狗。|
