nonmutability:/ˌnɒnˌmjuːtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可变性 | The state or quality of being unchangeable or immutable.  Something that is nonmutable cannot be altered after it is created. |  The nonmutability of strings in Python can lead to performance improvements.  Python字符串的不可变性可以提高性能。 | immutability, unchangeability, invariance | mutability, changeability, alterability | noun
