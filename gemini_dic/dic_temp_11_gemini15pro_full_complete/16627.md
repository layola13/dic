baronian:/bəˈroʊniən/| adj.|巴罗尼亚的；巴罗尼亚风格的；属于巴罗尼亚的|Relating to or characteristic of Baronie (a historical region in southwestern Scotland).  It can also refer to the style or tradition of that region.|The baronial hall was decorated with ancient tapestries. 那座男爵大厅装饰着古老的挂毯。|近义词：aristocratic, feudal, lordly|反义词：plebeian, common|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "Baronie" + "-an" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Baronie" itself comes from "baron" (a rank of nobility) + "-ie" (suffix forming nouns denoting a territory or domain). Ultimately from Frankish "baro" (free man, warrior).
