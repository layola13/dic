misheard: /ˌmɪsˈhɜːrd/ (British) /ˌmɪsˈhɝːd/ (American)| v. | 误听;听错 | To hear incorrectly; to understand something differently from what was actually said. | I misheard her name and called her Mary instead of Maria. 我把她的名字听错了，叫她玛丽而不是玛利亚。 |  I misheard the directions and ended up going the wrong way. 我听错了指示，结果走错了路。| 近义词: misinterpret, misunderstand | 反义词: hear correctly, understand correctly | Verb (Past tense of mishear)
