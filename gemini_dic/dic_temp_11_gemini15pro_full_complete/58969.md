dutches:/ˈdʌtʃɪz/
n.
荷兰女人;  (尤指)荷兰女公爵;荷兰的妻子
A woman of Dutch birth or descent, especially a duchess.  Sometimes used humorously or affectionately to refer to one's wife, especially if she is of Dutch descent or has Dutch characteristics.
She’s a real Dutchess, always scrubbing the floors. 她真是个典型的荷兰女人，总是擦地板。
My dutchess made some delicious stroopwafels. 我的妻子（荷兰妻子）做了一些美味的荷兰焦糖煎饼。
近义词:Dutchwoman, wife (informal)
反义词:None (gender-specific term)
词性:noun


dutches:/ˈdʌtʃɪz/
n.
(duchess的复数) 女公爵;公爵夫人
The plural form of duchess, referring to more than one woman holding the rank of duchess.
The dutchesses attended the royal wedding. 女公爵们参加了皇室婚礼。
Several dutchesses were present at the coronation.  几位女公爵出席了加冕典礼。
近义词:peeresses (more general)
反义词:None (specific title)
词性:noun
