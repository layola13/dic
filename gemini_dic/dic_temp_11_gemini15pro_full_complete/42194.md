coumaric: /ˌkuːˈmærɪk/ | adj. |  香豆酸的；含有香豆酸的 | Relating to or containing coumaric acid, a crystalline acid found in many plants. |  Coumaric acid is a common constituent of plant cell walls. 香豆酸是植物细胞壁的常见成分。|  Coumaric acid derivatives exhibit antioxidant properties. 香豆酸衍生物具有抗氧化特性。| synonymous: hydroxycinnamic | antonymous: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "lacking coumaric acid") | adjective
