brazened:/ˈbreɪzənd/| adj.| 厚颜无耻的，肆无忌惮的 | made of or resembling brass; hardened; unrestrained by convention or propriety | He told a brazened lie to his boss. 他厚颜无耻地向老板撒谎。 | 近义词：shameless, impudent, bold, audacious | 反义词：shy, timid, modest, ashamed | 词性: 形容词

brazen:/ˈbreɪzn/| adj. v.| 黄铜制的; 厚颜无耻的;  使变得厚脸皮 | made of brass; sounding harsh and loud like a brass instrument; bold and without shame. |  She has a brazen attitude. 她态度傲慢无礼。/  He brazened out the awkward situation. 他厚着脸皮应付了尴尬的局面。 | 近义词：(adj.) bold, audacious, impudent; (v.) defy, face | 反义词：(adj.) shy, timid, modest; (v.) cower | 词性: 形容词, 动词

词源: From Middle English *brasen, from 古英语 *bræsen (“made of brass”), from *bræs (“brass”) (akin to Old High German bras (“brass”)), from Late Latin brassum, of uncertain origin.  The sense "impudent" developed from the hardness and brightness of brass.
