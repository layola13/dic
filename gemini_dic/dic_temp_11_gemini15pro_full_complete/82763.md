guillemot:/ˈɡɪləmɒt/ (UK) /ˈɡɪləˌmɑːt/ (US)| n. |海鸠|A small, short-winged seabird of northern oceans, related to the auks.|例句：The guillemot dived into the sea to catch a fish.  海鸠潜入海里去捕鱼。

|例句：Guillemots nest in large colonies on cliffs. 海鸠在悬崖上筑巢，组成庞大的群落。|近义词：auk, murre|反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *guillemot*, perhaps from Guillaume, a proper name, due to the bird's supposed cry.
