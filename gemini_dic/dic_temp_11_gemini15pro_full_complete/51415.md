deterrent:/dɪˈtɜːrənt/ (US) /dɪˈtɛrənt/ (UK)| n. adj.|威慑物，制止物；威慑的，制止的|Something that discourages or prevents a particular action or behavior by instilling fear or doubt of the consequences.|The high fines act as a deterrent to speeding. 高额罚款对超速行驶起到了威慑作用.|Synonyms: discouragement, impediment, obstacle, curb, check, restraint|Antonyms: incentive, encouragement, inducement|noun

deterrent:/dɪˈtɜːrənt/ (US) /dɪˈtɛrənt/ (UK)| adj.|威慑的，制止的|Tending to deter; discouraging.|The deterrent effect of the new law is significant. 新法律的威慑作用很明显.|Synonyms: discouraging, dissuasive, preventive|Antonyms: encouraging, promoting, inciting|adjective
