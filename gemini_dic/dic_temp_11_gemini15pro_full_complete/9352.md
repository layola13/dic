antiprohibitionist:/ˌæntiˌprəʊɪˈbɪʃənɪst/
n.
反对禁酒者；禁酒运动的反对者
A person who opposed the prohibition of alcohol.
During the Prohibition era, antiprohibitionists argued that the ban on alcohol was unconstitutional and led to increased crime.  在禁酒时期，反对禁酒者认为禁止酒精是违宪的，并且导致了犯罪的增加。
Antiprohibitionists actively campaigned for the repeal of the Eighteenth Amendment. 反对禁酒者积极争取废除第十八修正案。
Synonyms: wet, opponent of prohibition
Antonyms: prohibitionist, dry
