magnetised:/ˈmæɡnɪtaɪzd/ | adj. | 磁化的，带磁性的 | Having been made magnetic; having a magnetic field. | The magnetised needle pointed north. 磁化的指针指向北方。| The iron filings were attracted to the magnetised bar. 铁屑被吸引到磁化的棒上。 | 近义词: magnetic, charged | 反义词: demagnetised, unmagnetised | 词性:形容词

magnetize:/ˈmæɡnɪtaɪz/ | vt. | 磁化，使…磁化 | To make magnetic; to communicate magnetic properties to. |  He magnetized the needle with a strong magnet. 他用一块强磁铁磁化了指针。|  The process magnetizes the iron bar, turning it into a magnet. 这个过程使铁棒磁化，把它变成了一块磁铁。 | 近义词: charge, polarize | 反义词: demagnetize, depolarize | 词性:动词
