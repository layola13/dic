literatures:/ˈlɪtərətʃərz/ | n. | 文学作品，文献 | The body of written works produced in a particular language, country, or age, or of a particular genre, especially works of the imagination characterized by excellence of style and expression and by themes of general or enduring interest. |  Example 1:  She studied English literatures at university. 她在大学学习英语文学。

Example 2: The literatures of the Renaissance are fascinating. 文艺复兴时期的文学作品令人着迷。 |Synonym: writings, literary works, literary productions, letters, belles-lettres | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "illiteracy" in a broad sense) | noun
