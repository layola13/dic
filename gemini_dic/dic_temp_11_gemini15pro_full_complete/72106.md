flocked:/flɑːkt/ | adj. | 群集的，成群的，绒状的 | Covered with or resembling a flock; gathered or moving in a flock.  |  The hills were flocked with sheep.  山上聚集成群的绵羊。 | 近义词：crowded, gathered, flocked | 反义词：scattered, dispersed | 词性: 形容词

flocked:/flɑːkt/ | v. |  群集，涌向，用棉絮覆盖 | (past tense and past participle of flock) Moved or came together in a crowd; covered with a soft, woolly substance. | People flocked to the beach on the hot day. 炎热的天，人们涌向海滩。 | 近义词：converged, thronged, swarmed | 反义词：dispersed, scattered | 词性: 动词
