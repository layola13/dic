nibs:/nɪbz/ | n. | 笔尖；（钢笔的）尖 | pointed ends, especially of pens. |  She dipped her nibs in the inkwell. 她把笔尖蘸到墨水瓶里。| 近义词: tips, points | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "blunt ends") | 词性: Noun

Etymology: From Middle English *nib*, probably from Old English *nebb* ("beak, nose, face"), from Proto-Germanic *nebą (“nose, beak, point”). Cognate with Dutch *nib*, German *Nippe*.
