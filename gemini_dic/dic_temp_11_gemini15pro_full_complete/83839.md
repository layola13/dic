hala:/ˈhɑːlə/ | int. | 嗨，你好 (表示问候或引起注意) | An exclamation used as a greeting or to attract attention, similar to "hi" or "hey." Primarily used in Hawaii and parts of the Pacific. | Example: Hala, bra! Howzit going? 嗨，兄弟！最近怎么样？| Example: Hala, look at that beautiful sunset! 嗨，看看那美丽的日落！| 近义词: hi, hey, aloha | 反义词: goodbye, farewell | 词性:感叹词


词源: From Hawaiian *hala*.


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Hala, how's the weather today? = 嗨，今天天气怎么样？
Hala, what time is it? = 嗨，现在几点了？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Hala, what are you doing? = 嗨，你在做什么？
Hala, are you listening to me? = 嗨，你在听我说话吗？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Hala, have you seen my keys? = 嗨，你看到我的钥匙了吗？
Hala, have you finished your homework? = 嗨，你完成作业了吗？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Hala, what have you been up to lately? = 嗨，你最近在忙些什么？
Hala, have you been waiting long? = 嗨，你等很久了吗？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Hala, where did you go yesterday? = 嗨，你昨天去哪了？
Hala, did you have a good time? = 嗨，你玩得开心吗？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Hala, what were you doing when I called? = 嗨，我打电话时你在做什么？
Hala, were you sleeping when the earthquake hit? = 嗨，地震发生时你在睡觉吗？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Hala, had you eaten before you left? = 嗨，你离开前吃过饭了吗？
Hala, had you already seen that movie? = 嗨，你看过那部电影了吗？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Hala, what had you been doing before I arrived? = 嗨，我来之前你一直在做什么？
Hala, had you been working on that project for long? = 嗨，你一直在做那个项目很久了吗？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Hala, will you be at the party tomorrow? = 嗨，你明天会参加派对吗？
Hala, what will you do this weekend? = 嗨，你这周末要做什么？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Hala, what will you be doing this time next week? = 嗨，下周这个时候你会在做什么？
Hala, will you be traveling during the holidays? = 嗨，假期期间你会去旅行吗？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Hala, will you have finished the report by Friday? = 嗨，你周五前会完成报告吗？
Hala, will you have graduated by next year?  = 嗨，你明年会毕业吗？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
Hala, how long will you have been living here by next year? = 嗨，到明年你在这里住了多久了？
Hala, how long will you have been studying English by then? = 嗨，到那时你学英语多久了？


PHRASE->
Hala, what's up? = 嗨，怎么了？/ 你好吗？
Hala, howzit? = 嗨，怎么样？ (夏威夷俚语)
Hala, no ka oi! = 嗨，最好的！ (夏威夷语，表示赞赏)
Hala, and then what happened? = 嗨，然后发生了什么？
Hala, you look great! = 嗨，你看起来很棒！
