fustigation:/ˌfʌstɪˈɡeɪʃən/
|n.|鞭笞；严厉批评；抨击|The act of beating with a stick or whip; severe criticism or condemnation.|His latest novel faced fustigation from critics who found its plot convoluted and its characters underdeveloped. 他的最新小说遭到了评论家的抨击，他们认为这部小说的情节错综复杂，人物发展不充分。|Synonyms: beating, whipping, lashing, castigation, denunciation, censure|Antonyms: praise, commendation, acclaim|noun
