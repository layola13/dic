maneless:/ˈmeɪnlɪs/| adj.|无鬃毛的，没有鬃毛的|Lacking a mane (the long hair on the neck of a horse, lion, etc.).|The circus featured a maneless lion, which looked strangely vulnerable.  马戏团里有一头没有鬃毛的狮子，看起来异常脆弱。

The maneless horse was easier to groom. 这匹没有鬃毛的马更容易梳洗。|近义词：hairless, bald (in certain contexts)|反义词：maned|词性:形容词
