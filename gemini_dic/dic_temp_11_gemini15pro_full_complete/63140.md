ensober:/ɪnˈsoʊbər/
verb
使清醒；使冷静
To free from the intoxicating influence of alcohol or drugs; to make sober.
He tried to ensober his drunken friend with strong coffee. 他试图用浓咖啡让他的醉酒朋友清醒过来。
Synonyms: sober up, detoxify
Antonyms: intoxicate, inebriate
词性:动词
