earthbag:/ˈɜːrθˌbæɡ/ | n. | 土袋，沙袋 | A bag filled with earth or sand, used in building construction. |  We built a house using earthbags. 我们用土袋建造了一座房子。 | Synonyms: sandbag, burlap sack | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

Etymology:  A compound word formed from "earth" (Old English "eorðe") and "bag" (Old Norse "baggi").
