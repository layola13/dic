examp:/ɪɡˈzæmp/ | n. | 例子，样本 |  Something that serves as a pattern or model; a representative instance. | Example: This painting is an example of Impressionism.  这幅画是印象主义的一个例子。| 近义词: instance, sample, illustration, specimen | 反义词: exception, opposite | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exemplum, alteration (by influence of amplus “large”) of Latin exemplum "sample, specimen, copy; image, portrait; warning, example," from eximere "to take out, remove; select, choose," from ex "out" + emere "to take, buy, gain, procure, obtain."
