here-goes:/ˌhɪr ɪt ˈɡoʊz/ (American) /ˌhɪər ɪt ˈɡəʊz/ (British)| idiom.|开始了，就是这样，豁出去了|It is often used as an exclamation before starting something new, challenging, or risky.
