maudeline:/ˈmɔːdlɪn/ /ˈmɑːdlɪn/ | n. |玛德琳(女子名); 玛德琳蛋糕 |A small rich shell-shaped sponge cake.  A female given name.  Often associated with Proust's "Remembrance of Things Past", where the taste of a madeleine cake evokes vivid childhood memories. |例句：She bit into the delicate maudeline, savoring its buttery sweetness. 她咬了一口精致的玛德琳蛋糕，细细品味着它的奶油甜味。

例句：Maudeline is a popular name in France. 玛德琳在法国是一个很受欢迎的名字。|近义词：Madelaine, Madeleine (for the name), petit four, shell cake (for the cake) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
