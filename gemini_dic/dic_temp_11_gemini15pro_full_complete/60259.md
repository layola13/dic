efreet:/ˈiːfriːt/ (US), /ˈɛfriːt/ (UK)| n. |火神，巨灵，神怪|A powerful jinn or demon in Arabian mythology, often associated with fire.|Example: The efreet emerged from the lamp, its eyes glowing like embers. 火神从神灯中出现，它的眼睛像余烬一样发光。|Example: Legends speak of efreets guarding ancient treasures in hidden caves. 传说有火神守护着隐藏在洞穴里的古代宝藏。|近义词：jinn, genie, demon, spirit|反义词:angel, deity|词性:noun
词源: From Arabic ʻifrīt, ultimately from ʻafara “to dust, rub”
