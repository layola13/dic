idumean:/ˌaɪduːˈmiːn/ | interj. | 我的意思是；我是说；我指的是 | Used to clarify or correct a previous statement, or to soften a potentially offensive remark.  | I said I liked the color, Idumean, I liked the *shade*. 我说我喜欢这个颜色，我的意思是，我喜欢这个*色调*。 |  近义词: I mean, that is, to say, rather | 反义词: (No direct antonym for this discourse marker) | 词性: interjection
