lulz:/lʌlz/ | n. |  幸灾乐祸的笑，网络用语，表示嘲笑或取乐| An expression used online to convey amusement or mockery, often at someone else's expense. It is similar to "LOL" (laughing out loud) but often implies schadenfreude or malicious enjoyment. | Example:  The video of the cat failing to jump onto the counter got a lot of lulz.  这只猫没能跳到柜台上的视频引起了很多人的嘲笑。 | Example: "They posted the embarrassing video online for the lulz." 他们在网上发布了令人尴尬的视频，就为了幸灾乐祸地嘲笑一番。 | 近义词: LOL, ROFL, lmao | 反义词: sympathy, empathy | 词性: noun
