inro:/ˈɪnˌroʊ/
noun
开头，引子，序言
An introductory part, especially of a discourse or writing.
The inro to his speech was rather long and tedious. 他的演讲的开头冗长乏味。
近义词: introduction, preface, preamble, prologue
反义词: conclusion, epilogue, postscript
词性: 名词(n.)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *intro, from intro "into".
