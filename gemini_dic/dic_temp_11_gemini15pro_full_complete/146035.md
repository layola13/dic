overdried:/ˌoʊvərˈdraɪd/| adj.|过度干燥的，干燥过度的|Excessively dried; dried beyond the desired or optimal point.  The verb "overdry" means to dry something too much.|The laundry was overdried and felt stiff.  洗好的衣物被过度烘干，摸起来硬硬的。|The overdried herbs crumbled to dust.  干燥过度的草药碎成了粉末。|over-baked, parched, dehydrated|moist, damp, hydrated|adjective
