narrows:/ˈnæroʊz/| n. |狭窄的地方；窄路；海峡；困境|A narrow passage or channel, especially one connecting two larger bodies of water.  A difficult or restricted situation.|The boat navigated the treacherous narrows. 这艘船航行过危险的海峡。|Synonyms: straits, channel, constriction, bottleneck |Antonyms: widen, expanse, breadth|noun
