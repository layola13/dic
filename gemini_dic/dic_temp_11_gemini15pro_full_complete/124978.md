muggiest:/ˈmʌɡiɪst/ | adj. | 闷热的；潮湿的 |  Superlative form of muggy: most muggy. Characterized by oppressive humidity and warmth. | It was the muggiest day of the summer, and even the slightest movement brought on a wave of sweat. 夏天最闷热的一天，即使是最轻微的活动也会带来一阵汗水。|  sultry, sticky, humid, close, oppressive | cool, crisp, refreshing, dry | adjective
