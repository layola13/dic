fire-walls:/ˈfaɪərwɔːlz/| n.|防火墙|A network security system that monitors and controls incoming and outgoing network traffic based on predetermined security rules.|例句：The firewalls protected the company's sensitive data from unauthorized access. 防火墙保护了公司的敏感数据免受未经授权的访问。

例句：Modern firewalls can filter traffic based on application, not just port. 现代防火墙可以根据应用程序而不是仅仅端口来过滤流量。|近义词：security barrier, network barrier, network defense|反义词: (No direct antonym, but conceptually) open network, unprotected network|词性:noun
