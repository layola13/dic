fireflies:/ˈfaɪərˌflaɪz/
n.
萤火虫
Nocturnal beetles that produce light through bioluminescence, often flashing in patterns to attract mates.
The children chased fireflies in the twilight. 孩子们在暮色中追逐萤火虫。

近义词:lightning bugs
反义词:None
词性:Noun
