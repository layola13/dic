ETYMOLOGY->
From French *comatose*, from Greek *kōma* "deep sleep," *kōmat-*, stem of *koiman* "to put to sleep," related to *keimai* "to lie down."  The suffix *-osity* forms abstract nouns of quality or state, from French *-osité*, from Latin *-ositatem*.
