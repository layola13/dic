leatherette:/ˌleðəˈret/| n.|人造革，仿皮|An imitation leather, typically made by coating a fabric base with a plastic or other synthetic material.|She bought a leatherette jacket because it was cheaper than real leather. 她买了一件人造革夹克，因为它比真皮的便宜。|近义词：artificial leather, faux leather, imitation leather|反义词:genuine leather, real leather|词性:noun
