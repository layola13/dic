androgynously:/ænˈdrɒdʒɪnəsli/ | adv. |雌雄同体地，兼具两性特征地 | Having both male and female characteristics; in a manner that is neither clearly masculine nor clearly feminine. |  She dressed androgynously in loose-fitting clothes and short hair. 她穿着宽松的衣服，留着短发，打扮得很中性。| Synonyms: unisexually, neutrally, ambiguously | Antonyms: masculinely, femininely | Word type: adverb

Etymology: From androgyny + -ously. Androgyny comes from Greek androgynos, from andr- (man) + gynē (woman).
