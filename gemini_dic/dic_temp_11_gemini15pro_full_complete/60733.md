electrocardiographically: /ɪˌlɛktrəʊˌkɑːdɪəˈɡræfɪkli/ | adv. | 心电图地；用心电图记录地 |  In a manner relating to or recorded by electrocardiography.  This refers to the method of recording the electrical activity of the heart. | The patient's heart rhythm was electrocardiographically monitored during the surgery. 病人在手术期间用心电图监测心律。|  Synonyms:  ECG-wise (less formal), by electrocardiogram | Antonyms: N/A | adverb
