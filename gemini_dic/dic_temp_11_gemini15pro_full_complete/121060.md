misconnects:/ˌmɪskəˈnɛkts/
| v. |错误连接，连接失败|To fail to connect properly; to make a faulty connection. |I tried to connect the wires, but I misconnected them and caused a short circuit. 我试图连接电线，但我接错了，导致了短路。|
| | |He misconnected the cables and the internet stopped working. 他把电缆接错了，互联网停止工作了。|近义词：misjoin, mislink|反义词: connect, join|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + connect.  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-" meaning "wrongly, badly, amiss." "Connect" comes from the Latin "connectere," meaning "to bind together."
