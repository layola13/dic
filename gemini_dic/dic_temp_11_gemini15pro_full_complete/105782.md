kirsh:/kɪrʃ/ /kɜːrʃ/| n.|樱桃白兰地; 基尔酒|A clear, colorless brandy distilled from fermented cherries, especially the morello cherry.  It is typically unsweetened.  Often used in cocktails and desserts.|例句：I ordered a kirsh after dinner.  晚餐后我点了一杯樱桃白兰地。|例句：The Black Forest cake was soaked in kirsh.  这块黑森林蛋糕浸泡在樱桃白兰地里。|近义词：cherry brandy, kirschwasser|反义词:N/A|词性:名词
