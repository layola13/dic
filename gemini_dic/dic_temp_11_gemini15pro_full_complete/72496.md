fluorescein:/ˌflʊərəˈsiːɪn/ /ˌflɔːrəˈsiːɪn/| n.|荧光素|A bright yellow, crystalline, water-soluble dye that becomes intensely fluorescent in alkaline solution. It is used in dyeing and as an indicator, and as a diagnostic aid in ophthalmology.|Doctors use fluorescein to detect corneal abrasions. 医生使用荧光素来检测角膜擦伤。|Synonyms: dye, pigment, coloring, stain|Antonyms: bleach|noun

fluorescein:/ˌflʊərəˈsiːɪn/ /ˌflɔːrəˈsiːɪn/| adj.|荧光素的|Relating to or containing fluorescein.|Fluorescein angiography is a medical procedure used to examine the circulation of the retina and choroid.  荧光素血管造影术是一种用于检查视网膜和脉络膜循环的医疗程序。|Synonyms: fluorescent|Antonyms: non-fluorescent|adjective
