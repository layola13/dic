cobwork:/ˈkɒbwɜːk/| n.|泥块建筑，用泥块和稻草混合物建造的墙壁,|A building method using cob (a mixture of clay, sand, straw, and water).|The old cottage walls were made of cobwork, showing the traditional building techniques of the region. 这座古老小屋的墙壁是用泥块建造的，展现了该地区的传统建筑技术。|Synonyms: cob construction, cob walling|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could consider modern construction materials like brick, concrete)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "cob" (a lump of clay mixed with straw) + "work" (construction).  "Cob" originates from Old English.
