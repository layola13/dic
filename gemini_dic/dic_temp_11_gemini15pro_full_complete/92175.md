hyperspeculative:/ˌhaɪpərˈspɛkjʊlətɪv/
adjective
过度投机的；高度 speculative 的
Based on or involving speculation that is extreme or excessive.
The company's hyperspeculative ventures ultimately led to its downfall.  这家公司过度投机的风险投资最终导致了它的垮台。
Hyperspeculative investments are often associated with high risk. 高度投机的投资通常与高风险相关。
synonyms: excessively speculative, highly speculative, over-speculative, wildly speculative
antonyms: cautious, conservative, low-risk, safe
