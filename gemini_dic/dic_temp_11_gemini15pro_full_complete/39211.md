conglobate:/kənˈɡloʊbeɪt/ /ˈkɒŋɡləʊbeɪt/| v.|使成球状，凝聚|To form into a ball or globe; to gather or heap together. |The iron filings conglobated around the magnet. 铁屑在磁铁周围聚集成了球状。|近义词：agglomerate, aggregate, coalesce, amass|反义词：disperse, scatter, separate|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin conglobatus, past participle of conglobare (“to form into a ball”), from con- (“together”) + globare (“to make round”), from globus (“ball”).
