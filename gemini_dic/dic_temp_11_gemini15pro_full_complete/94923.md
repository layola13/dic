in-loop:/ˈɪnˌluːp/ | adj. | 内部循环的; 知情的; | Being part of a process or discussion; informed and included. | Example:  The CEO made sure all senior managers were in-loop on the new strategy. 首席执行官确保所有高级经理都了解新战略。 | Example:  She felt in-loop about the project after the briefing.  简报之后，她感觉自己对这个项目了如指掌。 | Near Synonyms: informed, involved, up-to-date, in the know | Antonyms: out-of-the-loop, uninformed, excluded | 词性: 形容词
