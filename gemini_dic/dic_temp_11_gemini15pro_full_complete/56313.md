send someone to the doghouse:  把某人打入冷宫

get out of the doghouse:  重获好感
He bought her flowers to get out of the doghouse. 他给她买了花，想让她消气。
like a dog in a doghouse: 形容不舒服，不自在
He felt like a dog in a doghouse after being scolded by his teacher. 被老师责骂后，他感到非常不自在。
