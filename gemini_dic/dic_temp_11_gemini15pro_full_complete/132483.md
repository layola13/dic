nondescriptive:/ˌnɑːndɪˈskrɪptɪv/
adjective
没有描述性的；平淡无奇的
lacking in description; not giving a clear picture of something.
The witness gave a rather nondescriptive account of the robbery.  证人对抢劫案的描述相当平淡。
synonyms:unremarkable, unexceptional, ordinary, commonplace, pedestrian, uninspired, unimaginative, uninteresting, dull, bland, vapid, characterless, featureless, undistinguished, unremarkable, run-of-the-mill, indifferent
antonyms:descriptive, vivid, detailed, graphic
