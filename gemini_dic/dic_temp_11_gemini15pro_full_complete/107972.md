laserwort:/ˈleɪzərwɜːrt/
n.|激光草，芹叶牻牛儿苗|A plant (Laserpitium latifolium) of the parsley family, having large, much-divided leaves and umbels of white flowers. It is sometimes called masterwort.|The laserwort grew wild on the rocky slopes.|近义词: masterwort, white gentian|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *laserwort*, from Old English *leásewyrt*, from *leás* (leek) + *wyrt* (wort, plant).  The connection with "laser" is purely coincidental.
