leptodactylous: /ˌlɛptəˈdæktɪləs/
adjective
细趾的，指（蛙）趾间无蹼的
Having slender, unwebbed toes, typically used in reference to certain frogs.
Leptodactylous frogs are common in South America.  细趾蛙在南美洲很常见。
Leptodactylidae is a family of frogs characterized by leptodactylous feet.  细趾蛙科是以细趾足为特征的一科蛙类。
synonyms: slender-toed, unwebbed
antonyms: webbed, palmate
adjective
