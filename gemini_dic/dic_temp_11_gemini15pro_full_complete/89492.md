honeymoon:/ˈhʌnimuːn/ |n.|蜜月；蜜月期；短暂的和谐时期|A period of harmony and happiness immediately following marriage or the beginning of a new relationship. |They went to Hawaii for their honeymoon. 他们去夏威夷度蜜月。| synonym: holiday, vacation, trip | antonym: anniversary, divorce, separation| noun

honeymoons:/ˈhʌnimuːnz/ |n.|蜜月（复数）| The plural form of "honeymoon," referring to multiple honeymoons. |They've had three honeymoons in as many years. 他们在短短三年内度过了三次蜜月。| synonym: holidays, vacations, trips | antonym: anniversaries, divorces, separations | noun
