noncontextual: /ˌnɒnkɒnˈtekstʃuəl/ | adj. | 非语境的，脱离上下文的 | Not relating to or dependent on the context; not determined by the context. | The word "run" has many noncontextual meanings.  “run”这个词有很多脱离上下文的含义。 |  The artist's noncontextual brushstrokes created a sense of abstract chaos.  这位艺术家脱离上下文的笔触营造出一种抽象的混乱感。 |  近义词:  absolute, independent, universal | 反义词: contextual, relative, dependent | 词性: adjective

词源:  From non- + contextual.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "contextual" relates to the context or surrounding information.
