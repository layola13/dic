failingly:/ˈfeɪlɪŋli/
adverb
adv. | 失败地；总是失败地；必定失败地 |  She failingly tried to bake a cake, resulting in a burnt mess. 她总是烤不好蛋糕，结果烤糊了。| synonym: unsuccessfully, constantly failing, inevitably failing | antonym: successfully, effectively, competently
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "fail" (verb) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  "Fail" ultimately comes from the Old French "faillir," meaning "to miss, be lacking, be wanting."
