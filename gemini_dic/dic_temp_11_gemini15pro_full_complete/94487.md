importance:/ɪmˈpɔːrtəns/| n.|重要性，重要，重大|The quality or state of being important; significance.|The importance of regular exercise cannot be overstated.  规律运动的重要性怎么强调都不为过。|近义词：significance, value, consequence, weight|反义词：insignificance, unimportance|noun

importances:/ɪmˈpɔːrtənsiz/| n.|重要性（复数）;  要事；重要事项|Plural form of "importance," referring to multiple things of significance.|  He juggled the importances of family, work, and health. 他兼顾家庭、工作和健康的各种重要性。|近义词：priorities, values, essentials|反义词：trivialities, insignificances|noun
