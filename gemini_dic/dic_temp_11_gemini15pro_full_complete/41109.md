coprisoners:/koʊˈprɪzənərz/
n.
同狱犯人；狱友
Fellow prisoners; people imprisoned together.
The coprisoners formed a strong bond during their time in jail.  这些狱友在狱中建立了牢固的联系。
The two coprisoners planned their escape together. 这两名狱友一起计划越狱。
synonyms: fellow prisoners, inmates, cellmates
antonyms: guard, warden
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From co- (together) + prisoner (from Old French prisonnier).
