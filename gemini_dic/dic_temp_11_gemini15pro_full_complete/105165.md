ketua:/kəˈtuːə/| n.|主席，会长，领导人|A ketua is a leader or head of an organization, particularly in Malaysia, Indonesia, Brunei, and Singapore.  It's derived from Sanskrit "kṣatriya" meaning "warrior" or "ruler".|The ketua of the student council gave a speech. 学生会主席发表了讲话。|Synonyms: chairman, president, leader, head|Antonyms: subordinate, follower|noun
