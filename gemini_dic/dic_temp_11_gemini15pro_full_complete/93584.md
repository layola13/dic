imams:/ɪˈmɑːmz/
n.
伊玛目（复数）
A Muslim religious leader, especially one who leads prayers in a mosque.
The imams led the Friday prayers. 伊玛目们带领了周五的祈祷。
近义词:mullahs,clerics,  leaders
反义词:followers, laity
词性:名词

imam:/ɪˈmɑːm/
n.
伊玛目（单数）
A Muslim religious leader, especially one who leads prayers in a mosque.
The imam delivered a powerful sermon. 伊玛目发表了一场强有力的布道。
近义词:mullah, cleric,leader
反义词:follower, layman
词性:名词


词源: From Arabic إمام (ʾimām), meaning "leader," "example," or "model."
