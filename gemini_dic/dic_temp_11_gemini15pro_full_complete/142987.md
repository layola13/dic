outbargains:/ˌaʊtˈbɑːrɡɪnz/
|v.|
胜过(某人)的讨价还价；以更低的价格成交|
To outdo someone in bargaining; to obtain a better price than someone else through negotiation.|
He outbargained the street vendor and got the rug for half the original asking price. 他在讨价还价中胜过了街头小贩，以原价的一半买到了地毯。
|
outtrade, outmaneuver|
be outbargained|
