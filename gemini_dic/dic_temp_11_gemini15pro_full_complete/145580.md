overcompresses:/ˌoʊvər kəmˈprɛsɪz/
verb
过度压缩
To compress something excessively, beyond what is necessary or desirable.
The image file was overcompressed, resulting in a noticeable loss of quality.  图像文件被过度压缩，导致明显的质量损失。
He overcompressed the video, making it blurry. 他过度压缩了视频，使其变得模糊。
synonyms: over-compress, squeeze excessively
antonyms: decompress, expand
词性: 动词
