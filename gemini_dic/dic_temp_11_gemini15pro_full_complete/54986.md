disproved:/ˌdɪsˈpruːvd/
verb
反驳；证明…是错误的

The scientist disproved the old theory.  科学家反驳了旧理论。
近义词:refuted, debunked, invalidated
反义词:proved, confirmed, verified
词性:Verb (Past Participle)
