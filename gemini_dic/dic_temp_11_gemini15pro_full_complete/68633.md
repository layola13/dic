fanzines:/ˈfænˌziːnz/| n. |同人志；爱好者杂志|A non-official magazine or journal, typically produced by fans of a particular cultural phenomenon (such as a literary or musical genre, or a television show), for other fans.|Example 1: He published his poems in several science fiction fanzines. 他在几本科幻同人志上发表了他的诗。

Example 2:  She's a regular contributor to several Star Trek fanzines. 她是几本《星际迷航》同人志的定期撰稿人。|Synonyms: fan magazine, amateur publication|Antonyms: professional publication|Noun
