nodulous:/ˈnɒdjʊləs/ (US) /ˈnɑːdʒələs/ | adj. |多节的；有结节的|Having small lumps or knots.  Characterized by nodules.|

例句：The doctor discovered nodulous growths under the patient's skin. 医生在病人皮下发现了结节状的增生。

例句：The roots of the plant were nodulous and covered in tiny bumps.  这种植物的根有很多节，上面覆盖着小疙瘩。


近义词：knobby, lumpy, bumpy, gnarled|
反义词：smooth, even|
词性：adjective|
