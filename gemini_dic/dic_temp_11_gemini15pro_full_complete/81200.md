grandly:/ˈɡrændli/| adv.|盛大地，堂皇地，宏伟地|In a grand or impressive manner.|He grandly announced his retirement. 他盛大地宣布了他的退休。|The palace was grandly decorated for the ball. 宫殿为舞会进行了盛大的装饰。|近义词：magnificently, splendidly, imposingly, stately|反义词：humbly, simply, modestly|词性:副词
词源: From grand + -ly.  "Grand" ultimately comes from the Latin "grandis" meaning "great".
