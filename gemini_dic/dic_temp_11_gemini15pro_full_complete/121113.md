misdate:/ˌmɪsˈdeɪt/ | v. n. | 写错日期；日期错误；标错日期的文件/信件 | To date (something) wrongly or with an incorrect date. |  例句：I misdated the check and had to write a new one. 我把支票的日期写错了，不得不重写一张。 | 近义词：backdate, postdate | 反义词：date correctly | 词性：动词/名词

misdate:/ˌmɪsˈdeɪt/ | n. | 标错日期的文件/信件 | A document or letter that has been dated incorrectly. | 例句：The misdate on the contract invalidated the agreement.  合同上的日期错误导致协议无效。| 近义词：anachronism | 反义词：correctly dated document | 词性：名词
