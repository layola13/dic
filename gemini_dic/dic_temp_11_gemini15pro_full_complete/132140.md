noncultivated:/ˌnɒnkʌltɪveɪtɪd/ /ˌnɑːnkʌltɪveɪtɪd/
adjective
未开垦的，未耕种的； 非人工培育的； 不雅的，粗俗的
Not cultivated; not tilled or prepared for raising crops; existing in a natural state.  Also, unrefined or uncouth.
The hikers trekked through noncultivated wilderness.  徒步旅行者徒步穿越了未开垦的荒野。
His noncultivated manners embarrassed his wife at the formal dinner. 他粗俗的举止在正式晚宴上让他的妻子感到尴尬。
近义词: wild, uncultivated, untamed, fallow, unrefined, crude, coarse
反义词: cultivated, domesticated, refined, polished, tame
