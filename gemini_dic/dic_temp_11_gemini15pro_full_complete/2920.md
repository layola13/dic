n.
顾问职位；咨询职位
The position of being an adviser or consultant.

His various adviserships keep him very busy.  他的各种顾问工作让他非常忙碌。
Synonyms:  consultancies, advisory roles, mentorships
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, consider: client relationships, subordinate roles)
