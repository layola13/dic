nonconclusively:/ˌnɒnkənˈkluːsɪvli/ | adv. | 非结论性地，没有定论地 |  without reaching a firm conclusion; in a way that does not produce definite results. | The investigation ended nonconclusively, leaving many questions unanswered. 调查没有得出结论，留下许多问题没有答案。 | The evidence was presented nonconclusively, failing to prove the defendant's guilt.  证据的呈现没有定论，未能证明被告有罪。| inconclusively, indecisively | conclusively, decisively | adverb
