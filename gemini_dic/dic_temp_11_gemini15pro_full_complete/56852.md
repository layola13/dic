dorser:/ˈdɔːrsər/| n. |背负者；背的人或物|A dorser is something or someone that carries something on its back. |The mule acted as a dorser for the heavy packs across the mountain. 骡子驮着沉重的包裹翻山越岭。|近义词：carrier, bearer, porter|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
dorsal:/ˈdɔːrsəl/| adj. |背部的，背侧的|Relating to the back or upper side of an animal, plant, or organ.|The dorsal fin of a shark is easily recognizable.  鲨鱼的背鳍很容易辨认。|近义词：posterior, back|反义词:ventral, anterior|词性:adjective
