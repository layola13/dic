kipe:/kaɪp/| v.| 偷窃，尤指小偷小摸；(苏格兰语) 弄脏，弄乱|To steal, especially in petty amounts; (Scottish) To make dirty or messy.|He kiped some sweets from the shop. 他从商店里偷了一些糖果.|近义词:pilfer, swipe, nick|反义词:return, give back|词性:动词

词源:
Likely of Scandinavian origin, related to Norwegian *kipa* "to snatch, grab."
