aphlogistic:/ˌæfləˈdʒɪstɪk/
adjective
无火焰的；不燃烧的；不发光的
Relating to or denoting combustion in which phlogiston is not released.  (historical, chemistry) Without flame; flameless.
The aphlogistic lamp, invented by Sir Humphry Davy, provided illumination in mines without the risk of igniting flammable gases. 这盏无焰灯由汉弗莱·戴维爵士发明，可在矿井中提供照明而不会点燃易燃气体。

The chemist demonstrated an aphlogistic reaction in the lab.  化学家在实验室演示了一个无焰反应。

近义词: flameless, fireless
反义词: flaming, burning
词性: adjective
