bullheadednesses:/ˌbʊlˈhɛdɪdnəsɪz/
|n.|固执，顽固，倔强|The sheer bullheadednesses of the negotiators prevented a compromise.  谈判代表纯粹的固执阻碍了妥协。|例句：His bullheadednesses cost him the promotion. 他的固执让他失去了晋升的机会。|近义词：stubbornness, obstinacy, pertinacity|反义词:flexibility, open-mindedness|词性:名词

Etymology: From bullheaded + -ness. Bullheaded itself comes from "bull" (referring to the animal's perceived stubborn nature) + "headed."
