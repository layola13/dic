evoker:/ɪˈvoʊkər/| n.|召唤者，唤起者|Someone or something that evokes a particular feeling, memory, or image.  The scent of pine needles was an evoker of happy childhood memories.|The magician, an evoker of spirits, commanded the phantom to appear.|近义词:conjurer, summoner, elicitor|反义词:suppressor, inhibitor|noun
