anti-British:/ˌæntiˈbrɪtɪʃ/
adjective
反英的
opposed to the United Kingdom, its people, or its policies
Example: Anti-British sentiment was high in some parts of Ireland during the Troubles. 在爱尔兰某些地区，“问题”期间反英情绪高涨。

Example:  His anti-British stance made him unpopular with some of his colleagues. 他的反英立场使他的一些同事不喜欢他。
Synonym: Anglophobic
Antonym: pro-British, Anglophile
词性: adjective
