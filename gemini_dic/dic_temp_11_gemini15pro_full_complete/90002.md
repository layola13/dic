horticulture:/ˈhɔːrtɪkʌltʃər/
n.
园艺；园艺学
The science and art of growing fruits, vegetables, flowers, or ornamental plants.
She studied horticulture at university and now runs her own successful gardening business. 她大学学习园艺学，现在经营着自己的园艺生意。
gardening, cultivation, floriculture
agriculture, farming
