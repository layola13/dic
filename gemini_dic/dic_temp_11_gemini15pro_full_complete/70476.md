fike:/faɪk/ | n. | 裂缝，裂口; 狭窄的小路; 小溪; (尤指) 小河 | A narrow opening or crack, especially in rock; a narrow path or track; a small stream; (especially) a small river. |  例句：The old man followed the fike up the mountain. 老人沿着小路上了山。 | 例句：Water trickled down the fike in the rock face. 水沿着岩壁上的裂缝滴下来。 | 近义词：crack, fissure, crevice, path, track, stream, brook, rivulet | 反义词: closure, blockage | 词性:名词

词源:  Likely from Middle English *fiken, perhaps related to Norwegian fika "to move nimbly, fidget," or possibly related to Old English *fic "deceit, fraud." The exact etymology is uncertain.
