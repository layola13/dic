antiskepticism:/ˌæntiˈskɛptɪsɪzəm/
|n.|反怀疑论|The philosophical position of opposing skepticism, often by defending the possibility of knowledge or certainty.|His book is a robust defense of antiskepticism.|近义词:dogmatism, foundationalism|反义词:skepticism|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + skepticism.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," derived from Greek "anti." "Skepticism" comes from the Greek "skeptikos," meaning "inquiring" or "thoughtful," later evolving to mean "doubtful."
