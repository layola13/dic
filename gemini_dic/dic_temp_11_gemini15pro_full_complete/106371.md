komtok: /ˈkɒmtɒk/ | n. | 柬埔寨高棉族传统围巾 | A traditional Cambodian Khmer scarf, often checkered, worn by both men and women. | Example: She wore a beautiful silk komtok to the ceremony. 她戴着一条漂亮的丝绸围巾去参加典礼。 | Example:  He wrapped the komtok around his neck to protect himself from the sun. 他用围巾围住脖子以防晒。| 近义词:krama (another type of Khmer scarf), scarf | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
