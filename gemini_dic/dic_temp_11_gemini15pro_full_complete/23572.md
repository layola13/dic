braise:/breɪz/| v. n.| 焖，炖；用文火炖煮；红烧肉,炖肉|To cook (meat or vegetables) by browning it in fat, then simmering it in a small amount of liquid in a closed container.|I braised the beef for three hours until it was tender. 我把牛肉炖了三个小时直到它变嫩。|近义词：stew, simmer, pot-roast|反义词：fry, roast, grill|词性:动词, 名词
braiser:/ˈbreɪzər/| n.|炖锅；焖肉锅|A heavy, covered pot used for braising.|She used her new braiser to cook the lamb shanks. 她用她的新炖锅来煮羊腿。|近义词：Dutch oven, casserole dish|反义词：frying pan, skillet|词性:名词

词源:
From French *braiser*, from Old French *braisier* "to burn, roast," from *braise* "live coals," from Frankish *brasa* or some other Germanic source akin to Old High German *brasa*.
