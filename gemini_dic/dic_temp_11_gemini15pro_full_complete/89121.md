home-free:/ˌhoʊm ˈfriː/
adjective
安全的；成功的；没有问题的
safe from danger or failure; assured of success
例句：Once we get past this checkpoint, we're home-free. 一旦我们过了这个检查站，我们就安全了。

例句：With the majority of votes counted, she's practically home-free. 大部分选票已经统计完毕，她实际上已经稳操胜券了。
近义词：safe, secure, in the clear, out of the woods
反义词：in danger, at risk, vulnerable
词性:adjective

home-free:/ˌhoʊm ˈfriː/
adverb
安全地；成功地；没有问题地
in a safe, successful, or problem-free manner (less common usage)
例句：He sailed through the exam and is home-free now. 他轻松地通过了考试，现在安全了。

例句：After securing the funding, the project is home-free. 资金到位后，项目就万事大吉了。
近义词：safely, securely, successfully
反义词：dangerously, precariously
词性:adverb

词源：This idiom originates from baseball, where a runner who reaches home base safely is "home free" and cannot be tagged out.  这个习语起源于棒球，其中安全到达本垒的跑垒员是“安全到家”的，不能被触杀出局。
