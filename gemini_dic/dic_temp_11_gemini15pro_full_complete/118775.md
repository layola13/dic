metallographic:/ˌmɛtəlˈəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 金相学的 | Relating to the microscopic study of the structure of metals and alloys. | The metallographic examination revealed the presence of micro-cracks. 金相检验 revealed the presence of micro-cracks. | 近义词: microstructural | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From "metal" + "-o-" + "graphic". "Metal" refers to the subject of study, "-o-" is a combining form, and "graphic" relates to the visual representation of the structure.  Ultimately derived from Greek "metallon" (metal) and "graphein" (to write).
