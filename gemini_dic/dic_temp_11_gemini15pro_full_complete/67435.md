extinguisher:/ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃər/| n. |灭火器|A portable device that discharges a jet of water, foam, gas, or other material to extinguish a fire.|She grabbed the fire extinguisher and put out the flames. 她抓起灭火器扑灭了火焰。|近义词：fire suppressant, fire apparatus|反义词:incendiary, accelerant|noun
