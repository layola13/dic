almanac: /ˈɔːlmənæk/ | n. | 年鉴；历书 | An annual publication containing information on a particular subject, often arranged according to the calendar, such as astronomical data, tide tables, or weather forecasts.  | I consulted the almanac to find the best time to plant my tomatoes. 我查阅了年鉴，以找到种植西红柿的最佳时间。

| Example: The farmer used the almanac to determine the best planting dates. 农民使用年鉴来确定最佳播种日期。
| Synonyms: yearbook, annual, calendar, register | Antonyms: None | Noun
