devisers:/dɪˈvaɪzərz/
noun
设计者，发明者，策划者
People who create, invent, or plan something.
The devisers of the new system claimed it would increase efficiency.  新系统的开发者声称它将提高效率。
synonyms: inventors, creators, designers, planners, architects, engineers, originators, authors, producers
antonyms: destroyers, imitators, followers
n.
