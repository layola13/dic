hexapod:/ˈhɛksəpɒd/ (US) /ˈhɛksəpɒd/ (UK)| n. |六足动物；六足机器人|An animal with six legs, especially an insect.  Also, a robot or vehicle with six legs.|例句：The ant is a hexapod. 蚂蚁是一种六足动物。

The researchers developed a hexapod robot for exploring rough terrain. 研究人员开发了一种用于探索崎岖地形的六足机器人。


|近义词：insect (for animals), six-legged|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider animals with a different number of legs like quadruped, biped)|词性:noun
