go-aheads:/ˈɡoʊ əˌhɛdz/| n.| 许可，批准；前进的信号|Permission to proceed; a signal to advance.|The project received all the necessary go-aheads.|该项目获得了所有必要的批准。|
例句：
The boss gave us the go-ahead to start the project.
老板同意我们开始这个项目。

The traffic light turned green, giving the cars the go-ahead.
交通灯变绿了，示意车辆可以通行。


近义词：approval, authorization, permission, green light|
反义词：disapproval, rejection, prohibition, red light|
词性：名词
