antiscians:/ænˈtɪʃənz/| n.|对蹠人；居住在相对纬度上的人|People who live at corresponding latitudes north and south of the equator, so that when the sun is vertically overhead for one, the other's shadow is thrown directly towards the north or south.|The ancient Greeks knew of the antiscians and their relationship to the sun.|近义词: counterpart, opposite number|反义词: None|noun

antiscian:/ænˈtɪʃən/| adj.|对蹠的；居住在相对纬度上的|Located at corresponding latitudes north and south of the equator, such that when the sun is vertically overhead for one location, the shadow of a person at the other location is cast directly north or south.|The antiscian inhabitants experienced opposite seasons.|近义词: antipodal|反义词: None|adjective
