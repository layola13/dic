columbatz: /kəˈlʌmbæts/ | n. |  鸽子（古语，现已弃用）| An archaic word for pigeon, now obsolete.  It likely derived from the Old French word "colombe," meaning dove or pigeon.| Columbatz were often used to carry messages in medieval times. (中古时期，鸽子常用来传递信息). | 近义词: pigeon, dove | 反义词:  (无直接反义词) | 词性: 名词
