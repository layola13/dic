apricot:/ˈeɪprɪkɒt/ (UK) /ˈæprɪkɑːt/ (US)| n. |杏; 杏黄色; 杏树|A small, round, soft fruit with yellow flesh and a smooth skin, or the tree that this fruit grows on.  It can also refer to a pale orange-yellow colour.|

例句：I love the taste of fresh apricots. 我喜欢新鲜杏子的味道。

例句：She wore a dress the colour of apricots. 她穿了一件杏黄色的连衣裙。


近义词：peach (somewhat), nectarine (somewhat)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
