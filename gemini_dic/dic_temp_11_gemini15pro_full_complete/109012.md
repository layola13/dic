leftist: /ˈlɛftɪst/ (US), /ˈleftist/ (UK)| n. adj.|左派，左翼分子；左派的，左翼的|A person with left-wing political views, typically advocating social equality.  Someone who supports social equality and policies that benefit the working class.|

例句：
He's considered a leftist in his political views.  他被认为政治观点左倾。

The leftist party is gaining popularity among young voters.  左翼政党正在年轻人中获得人气。

近义词：liberal, progressive, socialist, radical|
反义词：right-wing, conservative, reactionary|
词性: noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From left + -ist.  The political sense emerged in the early 20th century, referring to those who sat on the left side of the legislative assembly, traditionally associated with more radical or progressive views.
