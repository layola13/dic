codependency:/ˌkoʊdɪˈpɛndənsi/| n.|相互依赖，共同依赖|A relationship where one person supports or enables another person's addiction, poor mental health, immaturity, irresponsibility, or under-achievement.|Her codependency on her husband's approval made it difficult for her to express her own opinions.|近义词：interdependence, reliance|反义词:independence, autonomy|词性:noun

codependencies:/ˌkoʊdɪˈpɛndənsiz/| n.|相互依赖，共同依赖（复数）|Multiple relationships characterized by codependency.|The therapist helped the family members recognize their unhealthy codependencies.|近义词：interdependencies, reliances|反义词:independencies, autonomies|词性:noun

Etymology:  From co- + dependency. "Dependency" traces back to the Late Latin *dependentia, from dependēns, present participle of dependēre "to hang down, hang from."
