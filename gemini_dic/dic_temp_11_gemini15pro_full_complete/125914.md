muser:/ˈmjuːzər/ | n. | 沉思者；空想家；冥想者 |  A person who thinks deeply or dreams about something.  A person who is lost in thought, often about pleasant things. |  She was a muser, often lost in her own world. 她是一个沉思者，经常沉浸在自己的世界里。|  He sat by the window, a muser contemplating the falling leaves. 他坐在窗边，沉思着落叶。 |  contemplator, thinker, dreamer, ponderer, meditator |  doer, actor | noun
