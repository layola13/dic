adjective
伪造的，假冒的
Formed from "forge" (to create something false) + "-ive" (suffix forming adjectives).  Meaning: pertaining to forging or counterfeiting.
The expert identified the painting as a forgetive piece.  专家鉴定这幅画是伪造的。
The passport was clearly forgetive, with misspellings and a blurry photo.  这本护照明显是伪造的，拼写错误，照片也很模糊。

反义词:genuine, authentic, real
