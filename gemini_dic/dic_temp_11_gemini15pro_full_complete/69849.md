ferrite:/ˈfɛrɪt/ /ˈfɛːraɪt/ | n. |铁氧体|A ceramic-like material containing iron oxide, usually in combination with other metallic oxides, and having magnetic properties.  A ferrimagnetic material.| Ferrites are used in inductors and transformers. 铁氧体用于电感器和变压器。| synonym: ceramic magnet, ferromagnetic material | antonym: paramagnetic material | noun

ferrite:/ˈfɛrɪt/ /ˈfɛːraɪt/ | adj. |铁素体的|Made of ferrite; relating to ferrite.|  The ferrite core of the inductor improved its performance.  电感器的铁氧体磁芯提高了其性能。| synonym: ferromagnetic | antonym: paramagnetic | adjective
