crazy:/ˈkreɪzi/| adj.|疯狂的，荒唐的，着迷的|Mentally deranged; extremely enthusiastic; impractical; wildly unsound.|He drove like crazy to get to the hospital. 他疯狂地开车去医院。|Synonyms: insane, mad, wild, enthusiastic, passionate|Antonyms: sane, rational, calm, sensible|adjective

crazy:/ˈkreɪzi/| n.|疯狂的人，狂热爱好者|A person who is mentally deranged; an enthusiast.|He's a crazy for old cars. 他是个老车迷。|Synonyms: lunatic, maniac, fanatic|Antonyms: sane person|noun
