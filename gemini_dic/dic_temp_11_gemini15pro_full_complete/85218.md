harking:/ˈhɑːrkɪŋ/ (US) /ˈhɑːkɪŋ/ (UK)|
v.|
倾听，注意; 追溯到，与…有关|
The story harking back to the times of the Vikings.  这个故事可以追溯到维京时代。|
He was harking back to his childhood. 他在回忆他的童年。|
listening, attending, heeding|
ignoring, disregarding|
