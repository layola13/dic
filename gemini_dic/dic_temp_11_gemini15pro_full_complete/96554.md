indorsee:/ɪnˌdɔːrˈsiː/ /ɪnˌdɔːrˈsiː/| n.|被背书人；受让人|The indorsee is the person to whom a check or promissory note is endorsed.|被背书人是支票或期票被背书转让的对象。|
例句：The indorsee presented the check to the bank. 被背书人把支票提交给了银行。

例句：The indorsee of the bill became the lawful owner. 票据的被背书人成为了合法所有人。

近义词：assignee, transferee, recipient|
反义词:indorser|
词性:noun|

ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *endosse, past participle of endosser, from en- + dosser "to put on one's back," from dos "back."  Ultimately from Latin dorsum.
