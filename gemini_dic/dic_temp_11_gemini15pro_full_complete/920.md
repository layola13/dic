abutment:/əˈbʌtmənt/| n.|桥台，支座，支柱|A structure built to support the lateral pressure of an arch or span, e.g., at the ends of a bridge.|The abutment of the old bridge was crumbling. 老桥的桥墩正在崩塌。|Synonyms: support, pier, buttress|Antonyms: apex, peak|noun

abutment:/əˈbʌtmənt/| n.|接界，毗连|The point where two structures or pieces of land meet or join.|The property has an abutment with the river. 这处房产与河流毗邻。|Synonyms: junction, juncture, adjacency|Antonyms: separation, division|noun
