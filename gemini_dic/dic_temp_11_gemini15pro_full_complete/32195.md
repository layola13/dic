chic:/ʃiːk/| adj.|别致的，时髦的，优雅的|Elegantly and stylishly fashionable.|例句：She wore a chic little black dress. 她穿着一件别致的小黑裙。|近义词：stylish, fashionable, elegant, sophisticated|反义词：frumpy, dowdy, unfashionable, tacky|词性:形容词
Etymology: French, from German schick 'skill, fitness'
