overcarelessly: /ˌoʊvərˈkɛrlɪsli/
adverb
过于粗心地，太不小心地
Done in a way that shows a lack of attention and concern, excessively careless.
She overcarelessly tossed her keys onto the counter, nearly knocking over a vase. 她太不小心地把钥匙扔到柜台上，差点打翻一个花瓶。
Near synonyms: excessively carelessly, too carelessly, unduly carelessly
Antonyms: meticulously, carefully, cautiously
词性: 副词
