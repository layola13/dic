glary:/ˈɡlɛəri/ | adj. | 刺眼的，耀眼的，闪耀的 |  (of light) dazzlingly bright; causing temporary blindness.  | The glary headlights of the oncoming car blinded me momentarily.  迎面而来的汽车刺眼的车灯让我瞬间失明。 | 近义词：bright, dazzling, blinding, glaring | 反义词：dim, dull, soft | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From glare + -y.
