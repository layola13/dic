half-shoddy:/ˌhæfˈʃɒdi/ | adj. | 半劣质的；部分粗制滥造的 |  Made of a mixture of good and poor quality materials; partly shoddy.  It suggests something is not entirely bad, but has elements of poor quality or workmanship. | The furniture was half-shoddy, with some parts looking well-made and others clearly rushed. 这家具半劣质，有些部分看起来做工精良，而另一些则明显很粗糙。 | The coat was half-shoddy, with good quality wool on the outside but a cheap lining. 这件外套半劣质，外面是优质羊毛，但衬里很便宜。 | Near synonyms:  partly inferior, second-rate, not quite up to par | Near antonyms: high-quality, well-made, superior | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"half-" (Old English healf) + "shoddy" (from the reused wool of torn-up fabrics, from "shod," meaning to shed or cast off)
