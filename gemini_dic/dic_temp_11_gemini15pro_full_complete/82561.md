guarantee:/ˌɡærənˈtiː/ /ˈɡærənti/| n. vt.|保证，担保，保修；保证人，担保物|a formal promise or assurance (especially that certain conditions will be fulfilled relating to a product, service, or transaction); to promise or assure|The store guarantees satisfaction or your money back.  这家商店保证满意，否则退款。|近义词：assurance, pledge, warranty|反义词：insecurity, doubt|词性:noun, verb

词源: From Old French *guarantir, from garant "warrant," from Frankish *warrant.
