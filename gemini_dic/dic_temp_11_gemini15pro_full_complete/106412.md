koppel:/ˈkɒpəl/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpəl/ (US)| n. |耦合器，联轴器; 皮带; (尤指拴狗的)皮带，链条; 一对，一双|A device that connects two things, especially two parts of a machine or two railway carriages. Also, a leash, especially for a dog, or a pair of animals.|

例句：
The train koppel broke, causing a delay.
火车联轴器坏了，导致延误。

The farmer used a koppel to lead the oxen.
农夫用皮带牵着牛。

近义词：coupling, connector, link, leash, pair
反义词: (No direct antonym.  One might consider "separation" or "disconnection" in certain contexts.)
词性: noun
