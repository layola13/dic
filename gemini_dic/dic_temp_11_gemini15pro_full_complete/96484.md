indivinity:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪnɪti/
n.
lack of divinity; absence of divine qualities
The indivinity of the idols became apparent when they failed to answer the prayers of their worshippers.  偶像的神性在他们无法回应信徒的祈祷时变得显而易见。
Etymology:  From in- "not" + divinity.  "Divinity" comes from Old French divinité, from Latin divinitas "divinity, divine nature," from divinus "divine," related to divus "god."
Synonyms:  mortality, humanness, worldliness, profanity
Antonyms:  divinity, godliness, sacredness, holiness
noun
