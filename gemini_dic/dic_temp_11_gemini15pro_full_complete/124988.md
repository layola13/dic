muggletonian:/ˌmʌɡəlˈtoʊniən/ | n. | 穆格尔顿教徒 | A follower of Lodowicke Muggleton (1609–98), a London tailor who claimed to be the last of the two witnesses mentioned in the Book of Revelation and who founded a sect rejecting the doctrine of the Trinity.  |  He was a devout Muggletonian, believing in Muggleton's unique interpretation of scripture. 他是一位虔诚的穆格尔顿教徒，相信穆格尔顿对圣经的独特解释。 | synonym: sectarian, cultist | antonym: orthodox believer | noun
