incandescence:/ˌɪnkænˈdɛsəns/ | n. | 白炽，炽热，光辉 |The state of being intensely hot and emitting light, especially as a result of being heated. |The incandescence of the molten lava was mesmerizing. 熔岩的白炽令人着迷。| 近义词：glow, brilliance, luminosity, radiance | 反义词：darkness, dullness | 词性：名词

incandescent:/ˌɪnkænˈdɛsənt/ | adj. | 白炽的，炽热的，光辉的 |Emitting light as a result of being heated.  |The incandescent bulb lit up the room. 白炽灯照亮了房间。| 近义词：glowing, brilliant, luminous, radiant | 反义词：dark, dull | 词性：形容词
