outpleasing:/ˌaʊtˈpliːzɪŋ/
adjective
过分取悦的，讨好别人的
Trying too hard to please others, often to the detriment of oneself.
She exhausted herself with outpleasing her demanding boss. 她为了取悦她那苛刻的老板而精疲力竭。
近义词:fawning, subservient, obsequious
反义词:assertive, independent


Etymology:
From "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "pleasing" (from "please").
