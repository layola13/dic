brashness:/ˈbræʃnəs/| n.|鲁莽；轻率；傲慢|The brashness of youth often leads to mistakes. 年轻人的轻率常常导致错误。|例句：His brashness sometimes offends people. 他的傲慢有时会冒犯别人。|例句：She displayed a surprising brashness in challenging the CEO. 她挑战CEO的举动表现出惊人的胆量。|近义词：audacity, impudence, boldness, temerity|反义词：timidity, shyness, humility, modesty|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From brash (adjective) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Brash (adjective) likely comes from a Scandinavian source, possibly related to Old Norse *brask "crackling, crashing," suggesting a connection to noisy and impetuous behavior.
