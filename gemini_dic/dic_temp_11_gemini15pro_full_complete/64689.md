escaper:/ɪˈskeɪpər/| n.|逃脱者；逃避者；逃跑者|A person who escapes, especially from confinement or danger. |The escaper managed to flee the prison unnoticed.  逃脱者设法不为人知地逃离了监狱。

The escaper blended into the crowd and disappeared.  逃脱者混入人群中消失了。|近义词：fugitive, runaway, refugee|反义词：captive, prisoner|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From escape (v.) + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting agents).  Escape ultimately comes from the Old French word "eschaper" meaning "to escape".
