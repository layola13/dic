nonpsychologically:/ˌnɒnˌsaɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 非心理地，不从心理角度 |  Not in a psychological way; without considering psychological factors. | He approached the problem nonpsychologically, focusing solely on the physical evidence. 他非心理地处理这个问题，只关注物理证据。| She dismissed his fears nonpsychologically, attributing them to fatigue. 她不从心理角度看待他的恐惧，认为是疲劳所致。 |近义词: objectively, impersonally, materially |反义词: psychologically, subjectively, emotionally |词性: 副词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "psychologically" (adverb derived from "psychological," ultimately from Greek "psyche" (soul, mind) + "logos" (study)).
