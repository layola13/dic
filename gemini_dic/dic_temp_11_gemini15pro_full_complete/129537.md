non-bigoted:/nɒnˈbɪɡətɪd/ | adj. | 没有偏见的; 宽容的 |  Not prejudiced against any particular group or class of people, especially on the basis of race, religion, sexual orientation, etc.  Characterized by tolerance and open-mindedness. |  She raised her children in a non-bigoted environment.  她在一个没有偏见的环境中抚养她的孩子。 | Synonyms: tolerant, unprejudiced, open-minded, impartial, unbiased, fair, equitable, accepting, inclusive | Antonyms: bigoted, prejudiced, biased, intolerant, narrow-minded, discriminatory | 词性: adjective
