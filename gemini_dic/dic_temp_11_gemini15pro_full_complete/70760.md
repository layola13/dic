fineries:/ˈfaɪnəriːz/
noun
华丽的服饰；精致的物品
fine clothes and jewelry, especially those worn for a special occasion
She wore her finest fineries to the ball. 她穿着她最华丽的服饰去参加舞会。
近义词: best clothes, Sunday best, glad rags, finery
反义词: rags, tatters
