derogately: /dɪˈrɒɡətərɪli/ /dɪˈrɑːɡətɔːrɪli/
| adv. | 贬损地，轻蔑地 | In a way that shows you do not respect someone or something. | He spoke derogately about his colleagues. 他贬低他的同事们。|近义词：disparagingly, contemptuously, slightingly |反义词：respectfully, admiringly |词性: 副词
Etymology: From Late Latin *dērogātōrius, from Latin dērogō (“repeal partly, detract from, diminish”) + -ly.
