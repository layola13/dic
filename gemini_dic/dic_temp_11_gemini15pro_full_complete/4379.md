aladinist:/əˈlædɪnɪst/ | n. | 阿拉丁主义者；主张仿效阿拉丁故事中的人物，利用精灵或魔法力量来实现愿望的人 | Someone who believes in or advocates for achieving their desires through magical means, similar to the character Aladdin using a genie. |  He was accused of being an aladinist, always looking for a shortcut to success instead of hard work.  他被指责是一个阿拉丁主义者，总是想走捷径获得成功，而不是努力工作。 | Example:  Some tech entrepreneurs are seen as modern-day aladinists, believing technology can solve any problem.  一些科技企业家被视为现代阿拉丁主义者，他们相信科技可以解决任何问题。 | 近义词: dreamer, idealist, visionary | 反义词: realist, pragmatist | 词性: noun
