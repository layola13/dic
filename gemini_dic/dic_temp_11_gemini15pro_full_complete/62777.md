adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *engraven, from Old French engraver, from en- + graver (“to carve, engrave”), from Frankish *graban, from Proto-Germanic *grabananą (“to dig, carve”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰrebʰ- (“to dig, scratch, scrape”). Cognate with Old English grafan (“to dig, carve”).
