litany: /ˈlɪtəni/ (US), /ˈlɪtni/ (UK) | n. | 连祷；冗长而枯燥的陈述 | a series of petitions for use in church services or processions; a tedious recital or repetitive series | The priest recited the litany.  神父诵读了连祷文。|  The litany of complaints continued for hours.  没完没了的抱怨持续了几个小时。| synonyms:  prayer, invocation, recital, enumeration, repetition | antonyms: celebration, praise, originality, diversity | noun

litany: /ˈlɪtəni/ (US), /ˈlɪtni/ (UK) | n. | (对坏事的)长篇累牍的叙述 | a long and detailed account of something bad | a litany of failures/disasters/complaints  一连串的失败/灾难/抱怨| The report detailed a litany of abuses. 报告详细列出了一系列的虐待行为。| synonyms: list, catalogue, account, chronicle | antonyms: summary, abbreviation, concise account | noun
