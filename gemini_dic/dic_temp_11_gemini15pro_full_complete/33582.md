ciliograde:/ˈsɪliəˌɡreɪd/
adjective
纤毛运动的
Moving by means of cilia.
Ciliograde organisms, like Paramecium, use tiny hair-like structures for locomotion.  纤毛虫生物，例如草履虫，利用微小的毛状结构进行运动。
近义词:ciliary
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps amoeboid for a different type of movement)

Etymology: From Latin *cilia* (eyelashes, eyelids) + *-grade* (walking, moving)
