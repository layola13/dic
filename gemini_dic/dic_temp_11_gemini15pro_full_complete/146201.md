overexpansively: /ˌoʊvərɪkˈspænsɪvli/
adverb
过度扩张地；过分扩展地
in a way that expands or spreads too much
The company overexpansively invested in new ventures and eventually went bankrupt.  这家公司过度扩张投资于新项目，最终破产了。
The empire overexpansively stretched its resources thin, leaving it vulnerable to attack.  帝国过度扩张，资源分散，使其容易受到攻击。
近义词: excessively, immoderately, too broadly
反义词: conservatively, cautiously, moderately
词性: adverb
词源: over- (prefix meaning "too much") + expansive (adjective meaning "covering a wide area") + -ly (adverb suffix)
