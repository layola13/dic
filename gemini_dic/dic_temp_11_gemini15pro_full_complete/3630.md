agendas:/əˈdʒendəz/
n.
议程；待办事项；日程表
A list of items to be discussed at a formal meeting.
The first item on the agenda is the budget proposal.  第一个议程是预算提案。
The committee worked through the agenda quickly. 委员会迅速完成了议程。
synonyms: schedule, docket, calendar, program
antonyms:  None (Agendas are lists, so they don't have direct opposites.)
noun
