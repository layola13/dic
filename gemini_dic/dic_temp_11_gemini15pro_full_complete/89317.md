homogeneity:/ˌhɒmədʒəˈniːɪti/ /ˌhoʊmoʊdʒəˈniːɪti/ | n. | 同质性，同类，均匀性 | The homogeneity of the sample is important for accurate results.  样本的同质性对于获得准确的结果至关重要。 | The mixture lacked homogeneity, with some parts thicker than others. 这种混合物缺乏均匀性，有些部分比其他部分更稠。 | 近义词：uniformity, consistency, similarity | 反义词：heterogeneity, diversity, variety | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *homogénéité*, from Ancient Greek ὁμογενής (*homogenḗs*, “of the same kind”), from ὁμός (*homós*, “same”) + γένος (*génos*, “kind, race, stock”).
