muslin: /ˈmʌzlɪn/ | n. | 平纹细布；棉布 | A finely woven cotton fabric, often used for clothing, curtains, and other household items. |  She wore a flowing muslin dress. 她穿着一件飘逸的平纹细布连衣裙。

The curtains were made of a sheer muslin. 窗帘是用透明的平纹细布做的。

近义词: calico, gauze, voile | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps heavy cloth or canvas) | 词性: noun
