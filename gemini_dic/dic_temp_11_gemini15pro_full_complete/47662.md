debrief:/diːˈbriːf/
verb
汇报；听取汇报；盘问
To question someone, typically after a mission or project, to gain information about their experience.
The pilot was debriefed after the flight. 飞行员在飞行后接受了汇报。
The team was debriefed on the results of the market research.  团队听取了市场调研结果的汇报。
synonyms: interrogate, question, examine, interview
antonyms: brief, instruct
词性: 动词

debrief:/diːˈbriːf/
noun
汇报；听取汇报；盘问
A meeting or session for discussion and evaluation, especially after a mission or project.
The project team held a debrief to discuss the lessons learned. 项目团队举行了一次汇报会，讨论吸取的教训。
The debrief after the simulation exercise was very helpful. 模拟演习后的汇报非常有帮助。
synonyms: review, discussion, analysis, assessment
antonyms: briefing, preparation
词性: 名词
