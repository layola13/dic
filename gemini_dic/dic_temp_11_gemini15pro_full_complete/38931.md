confidant:/ˈkɒnfɪdænt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnfɪdænt/ (US)| n.| 知己；心腹；密友|A confidant is a person with whom one shares personal secrets or confidences.|She's my closest confidant – I tell her everything. 她是我最亲密的知己——我什么都告诉她。|近义词：intimate, close friend, confidante|反义词：stranger, enemy|词性: 名词
