narrowcast: /ˈnæroʊˌkæst/ (US) /ˈnærəʊˌkɑːst/ (UK) | n. v. | 窄播;定向广播; |  The transmission of information to a select group of recipients.  It contrasts with broadcast, which is the transmission of information to a wide audience. |  The company used narrowcast advertising to target specific demographics. 这家公司使用窄播广告来定位特定的人口统计数据。

The political party narrowcast their message to likely voters. 该政党将他们的信息窄播给可能的选民。 | 近义词: targetcast, pointcast | 反义词: broadcast | 词性: 名词, 动词
