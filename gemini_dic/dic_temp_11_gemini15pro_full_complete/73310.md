foreclosable:/fɔːrˈkloʊzəbl/
adjective
可取消抵押赎回权的
Subject to foreclosure; capable of being foreclosed.  A mortgage that can be foreclosed upon by the lender if the borrower defaults.
The loan agreement stipulated that the property was foreclosable if payments were not made on time.  如果未能按时付款，贷款协议规定该房产可被取消抵押品赎回权。
The bank declared the mortgage foreclosable after repeated late payments.  在多次逾期付款后，银行宣布该抵押贷款可被取消赎回权。
Synonyms: redeemable, reclaimable
Antonyms: non-redeemable, non-reclaimable
