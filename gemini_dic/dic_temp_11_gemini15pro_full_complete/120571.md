miriest:/ˈmɪəriɪst/
adjective
最欢乐的; 最愉快的
most merry; most cheerful
例句：The miriest of Christmas parties. 最欢乐的圣诞派对。
例句：It was the miriest time of the year. 这是一年中最快乐的时光。
近义词：jolliest, merriest, happiest, most cheerful
反义词：saddest, gloomiest, most depressing
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *miriest, superlative of mirie (“merry, pleasant, amusing”), from Old English myrġe (“pleasant, agreeable, sweet, delightful, merry”), from Proto-Germanic *murgijaz (“short, brief”), from Proto-Indo-European *mréǵʰus (“short, small”). More at merry.
