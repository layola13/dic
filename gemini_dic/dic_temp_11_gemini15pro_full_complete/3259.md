affinity-groups:/əˈfɪnɪti ɡruːps/| n.|亲和群体，同好团体|A group of people who share a common interest, background, or goal. These groups can provide support, networking opportunities, and a sense of belonging.|Our company has several affinity groups, including one for LGBTQ+ employees and one for working parents.|近义词：support groups, interest groups, networks, communities|反义词:  isolated individuals, opposing factions|noun

affinity:/əˈfɪnɪti/| n.|亲和力，密切关系|A spontaneous or natural liking or sympathy for someone or something.|She felt an affinity with the natural world.|近义词：attraction, rapport, empathy, fondness|反义词: aversion, dislike, antipathy|noun

group:/ɡruːp/| n. v.|组，团体，分组|A number of people or things that are located close together or are considered or classed together.|A group of tourists gathered outside the museum.|近义词：cluster, collection, assembly, gathering|反义词: individual, single|noun/verb
