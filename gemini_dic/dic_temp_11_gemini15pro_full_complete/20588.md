blackens:/ˈblækənz/| v. |使变黑；玷污；诽谤|Makes something black, literally or figuratively; tarnishes; defames.|The smoke blackens the walls. 烟把墙熏黑了。|近义词：darkens, stains, smears, defames, slanders|反义词：whitens, cleans, brightens, praises|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Old English *blacnian "to become black," from blac "black."
