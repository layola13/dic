cheaping:/ˈtʃiːpɪŋ/
verb
讨价还价；杀价
The act of bargaining for a lower price, often aggressively or persistently.
He was cheaping with the street vendor over the price of a souvenir. 他在和街边小贩就纪念品的价钱讨价还价。
She spent hours cheaping at the market, trying to get the best deals. 她在市场上花了几个小时讨价还价，试图买到最划算的东西。
近义词: bargaining, haggling, negotiating
反义词: splurging, overpaying
词性: v.

cheaping:/ˈtʃiːpɪŋ/
adjective (obsolete, rare)
低廉的；便宜的
Low in price; inexpensive.
They found some cheaping goods at the market. 他们在市场上找到了一些便宜的商品。 (This usage is archaic and rarely encountered.)
This cheaping fabric won't last long.  这种廉价的布料不会耐用。(This usage is archaic and rarely encountered.)
近义词: cheap, inexpensive, low-cost
反义词: expensive, costly, pricey
词性: adj.


词源:  From the verb "cheapen" (to make or become cheap), derived from "cheap."  Ultimately from Old English *cēap (a bargain, price, sale).
