dishallucination:/dɪsˌhəˌluːsɪˈneɪʃən/
n.
消除幻觉，清醒
The process of ceasing to hallucinate; recovery from hallucination.
After the medication, he experienced a gradual dishallucination. 服药后，他逐渐清醒了。

近义词:recovery from hallucination, becoming lucid
反义词:hallucination, delusion

词性: noun
