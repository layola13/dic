leaven:/ˈlɛvən/ (US), /ˈlɛvən/ (UK)| n. vt.|酵母，使发酵，影响|Something, such as yeast, that causes fermentation and expansion of dough. Also, an element that produces a gradual change or transformation.|例句：The baker added leaven to the dough. 面包师在面团里加了酵母。|近义词：yeast, ferment, catalyst|反义词:inhibitor|n.

leaven:/ˈlɛvən/ (US), /ˈlɛvən/ (UK)| vt.|使发酵，影响|To cause (something) to ferment or rise by adding a leavening agent. Also, to permeate or transform (something) gradually.|例句：She leavened the heavy dough with yeast. 她用酵母使沉重的面团发酵。|近义词：ferment, transform, influence|反义词:suppress|vt.
