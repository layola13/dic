nonvicariousnesses:/ˌnɒnvɪˈkɛəriəsnɪsɪz/
noun
非替代性；非代理性
The state or quality of not being vicarious; inability to be experienced or felt through another person.
His nonvicariousnesses meant he had to experience the pain himself. 他非替代性的特质意味着他必须亲自体验痛苦。
nonvicariousness (singular form is more common)


Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + vicarious (from Latin vicarius, meaning "substitute") + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
