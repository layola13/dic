disfavouring:/dɪsˈfeɪvərɪŋ/
verb
不利于，歧视，不赞成
placing or putting at a disadvantage; showing prejudice against; regarding with disfavor.
The new policy disfavouring small businesses caused widespread protests.  这项不利于小企业的新政策引起了广泛抗议。
The judge was accused of disfavouring the defendant due to his background.  法官被指控因被告的背景而歧视他。
Near Synonyms: discriminating against, prejudicing, biasing against
Antonyms: favouring, supporting, endorsing
词性: 动词
