foughten: /ˈfɔːtən/ | adj. | 已战斗过的，战斗后的 (archaic) |  This word is an archaic past participle of "fight," meaning "having fought." It's rarely used in modern English and is considered obsolete. |  He looked foughten and weary. 他看起来饱经战火，疲惫不堪。 | 近义词: battled, combatted | 反义词: peaceful, rested | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *foughten, *foghten, from Old English fohten (past participle of feohtan (“to fight”)).
