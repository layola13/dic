mispublicized: /ˌmɪspʌˈblɪsaɪzd/
adjective
被错误宣传的；宣传失当的
incorrectly or misleadingly publicized.
The event was mispublicized, leading to a poor turnout.  这个活动宣传失误，导致出席人数很少。
The product launch was mispublicized, resulting in confusion among consumers.  该产品的发布宣传失误，导致消费者感到困惑。
Synonyms: misrepresented, distorted, garbled
Antonyms: accurately publicized, well-publicized
词性: adjective
