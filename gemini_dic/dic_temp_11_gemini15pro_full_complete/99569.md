interindependence:/ˌɪntərɪndɪˈpɛndəns/
|n.|相互依存，互相依赖|A relationship between entities in which each is dependent on the other.  The state of being mutually reliant and interconnected.|The interindependence of the global economy means a crisis in one country can quickly spread to others. 全球经济的相互依存意味着一个国家的危机可以迅速蔓延到其他国家。|interdependence, interconnectedness, interrelationship, mutuality|independence, autonomy, self-reliance|noun
