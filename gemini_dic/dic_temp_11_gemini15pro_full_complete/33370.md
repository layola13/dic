chundra: /ˈtʃʌndrə/ | n. |  月神; 月亮 (主要用于印度教和佛教文化中) | A lunar deity, particularly in Hinduism and Buddhism, often associated with the moon. |  Example: Chundra, the moon god, is often depicted riding a chariot. 月神旃陀罗通常被描绘成骑着战车。

Example:  Offerings were made to Chundra during the full moon.  在满月时，人们会向月神献祭。 | Synonym: Soma (Hindu), Chandra (alternate spelling), Lunar deity | Antonym: Surya (sun god) | Noun
