novemberish:/noʊˈvɛmbəriʃ/
adjective
类似十一月的，十一月左右的
Characteristic of or appropriate to November; resembling November, especially in its weather.
The weather has been rather novemberish lately, cold and grey. 最近的天气很像十一月，阴冷灰暗。
The novemberish chill in the air reminded me to get out my winter coat. 空气中十一月般的寒意提醒我该拿出冬衣了。
Near synonyms: autumnal, fall-like, late autumn
Antonyms: summery, spring-like
词性: 形容词
