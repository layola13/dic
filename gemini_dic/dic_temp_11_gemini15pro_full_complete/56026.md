doability:/ˌduːəˈbɪləti/
noun
可行性
The quality of being doable; feasibility.
The project manager is assessing the doability of the proposed plan.  项目经理正在评估拟议计划的可行性。
The doability of the task depends on the available resources.  任务的可行性取决于可用的资源。
Synonyms: feasibility, practicability, viability
Antonyms: impossibility, impracticability, unfeasibility
n.
