edifiers:/ˈɛdɪfaɪərz/
n.
建设者；教化者；启迪者
People who build, instruct, or uplift, especially morally or intellectually.
These books are the edifiers of our nation. 这些书是我们民族的启迪者。
Synonyms: instructors, mentors, teachers, guides, builders, developers
Antonyms: destroyers, corrupters, misleaders
noun

edify:/ˈɛdɪfaɪ/
v.
教诲；启迪；陶冶；熏陶
To instruct or improve (someone) morally or intellectually.
The sermon was meant to edify the congregation. 这篇布道旨在陶冶信众。
Synonyms: instruct, enlighten, educate, uplift, improve, inspire
Antonyms: corrupt, mislead, confuse, misinform
verb
