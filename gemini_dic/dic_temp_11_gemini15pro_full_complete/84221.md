half-savage:/ˈhæf ˌsævɪdʒ/| adj.|半野蛮的，半开化的|Partly or imperfectly civilized; uncultured or barbaric to some degree.  |The explorer encountered a half-savage tribe deep in the jungle. 探险家在丛林深处遇到一个半野蛮的部落。

half-savage:/ˈhæf ˌsævɪdʒ/| n.|半野蛮人|A person who is partly or imperfectly civilized. |The half-savage glared suspiciously at the newcomers. 半野蛮人怀疑地瞪着新来的人。

half-savage:|Etymology: half (Old English healf) + savage (Middle French sauvage)|


近义词:barbaric, uncivilized, primitive, wild
反义词:civilized, cultured, refined
词性:adjective, noun
