nonbiographical: /ˌnɒnˌbaɪəˈɡræfɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːnˌbaɪoʊˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
非传记的；非自传的
Not relating to or based on a person's life.  Relating to, based on, or dealing with objective facts or fiction rather than on the life and personality of an author.
例句：The novel is nonbiographical, despite some superficial similarities to the author's life. 这部小说不是传记小说，尽管表面上与作者的生活有一些相似之处。

例句：She preferred nonbiographical novels, finding them more imaginative. 她更喜欢非传记类小说，觉得它们更有想象力。

近义词：fictional, invented, imaginary
反义词：biographical, autobiographical
词性：adjective
