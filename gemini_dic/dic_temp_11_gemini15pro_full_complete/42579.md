counterplayers:/ˈkaʊntərˌpleɪərz/
noun
对手，竞争者
Those two companies are counterplayers in the mobile phone market.  那两家公司是手机市场的竞争对手。
The team faced tough counterplayers in the championship game.  这支队伍在冠军赛中遇到了强劲的对手。
Synonyms: rivals, competitors, opponents, adversaries
Antonyms: allies, partners, collaborators
n.
