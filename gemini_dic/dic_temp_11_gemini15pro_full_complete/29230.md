cassava:/kəˈsɑːvə/| n.|木薯，树薯|A tropical plant with thick roots that are eaten as a vegetable.  A starchy tuberous root of a tropical tree, used as food in tropical countries.|Cassava is a staple food in many parts of Africa. 木薯是非洲许多地区的主食。|Synonyms: manioc, yuca|Antonyms: None|Noun
