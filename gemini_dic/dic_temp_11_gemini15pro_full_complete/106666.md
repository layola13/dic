kusov:/ˈkuːsɔf/| n.|块，片，件 (primarily used in Bulgarian and Russian)|A kusov is a Bulgarian and Russian word meaning "piece," "lump," or "chunk," often used for irregular or unspecified shapes or quantities.|例句：Два кусова захар. (Bulgarian) - Two lumps of sugar.  两个方糖。|例句：Три кусова месо. (Bulgarian) - Three pieces of meat. 三块肉。|近义词：piece, lump, chunk, bit|反义词:whole|词性:noun
