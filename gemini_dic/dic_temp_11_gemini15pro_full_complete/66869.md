expectingly:/ɪkˈspektɪŋli/| adv.|期待地，期望地|In a manner that shows anticipation or expectation.  Indicates that someone is waiting for something to happen and believes it will.|She waited expectingly for his reply. 她期待着他的回复。|近义词：hopefully, anticipatively|反义词：unexpectedly, despairingly|词性:副词

Etymology: From the verb "expect" + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverb suffix). "Expect" comes from the Latin "exspectare" (to look out for, await).
